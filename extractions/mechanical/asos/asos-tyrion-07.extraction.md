- **chapter_summary:** Tyrion dresses in darkness while Sansa sleeps, reflecting on her grief over the Red Wedding and the hollowness of their unconsummated marriage. He descends through underground passages to a cellar full of dragon skulls where he meets Shae for a secret tryst. Afterward, he recalls Varys's warning that the eunuch will not lie to Cersei about Shae's true identity, and weighs sending Shae away — to Chataya's brothel or married to Bronn or Ser Tallad. Dawn breaks on the day of Joffrey's wedding and Shae departs first.

## Events & Actions
1. **Tyrion dresses in darkness** — He dresses silently while Sansa sleeps and murmurs a name in her sleep.
2. **Tyrion reflects on Sansa's grief** — He recalls telling Sansa about Robb's death; she showed no reaction at first but sobbed behind a closed door later. He decided to shield her from the ugliest details of the Red Wedding.
3. **Tyrion reflects on the futility of his marriage** — He compares his protective cloak to the cruel jape of the crown the Freys placed on Robb's direwolf head. Sansa prays nightly in the godswood, possibly for his death.
4. **Tyrion leaves the apartments** — He pulls on boots, fastens his cloak with a lion's head brooch, and slips into the torchlit hall, passing Brella's cell where she snores.
5. **Tyrion descends through underground passages** — He lights a taper and takes the servants' steps down through still, empty floors to a gloomy cellar with vaulted stone ceiling.
6. **Tyrion finds Shae in the dragon skull cellar** — Shae stands nude within a dragon's jaws, her dress draped on a black tooth. He thinks it may be Balerion or Vhagar's skull.
7. **Shae blows out the taper and they play in darkness** — She insists he catch her, referencing "monsters and maidens." He stumbles into dragon skulls pursuing her.
8. **Tyrion and Shae have sex among the dragon skulls** — She calls him "my giant" as they have sex on the cellar floor.
9. **Post-coital conversation** — Tyrion says they should go back before dawn. Shae suggests giving Sansa dreamwine so they can use the bed. Shae notices Tyrion's tension.
10. **Tyrion enumerates his woes on his fingers** — His wife, sister, nephew, father, the Tyrells, Varys, Pycelle, Littlefinger, the Red Viper, and his own face.
11. **Shae kisses his scarred nose** — She calls it "a brave face. A kind and good face."
12. **Tyrion recalls his conversation with Varys** — Varys warned he would tell Cersei the truth about Shae if asked — that she is a camp follower brought to King's Landing against Tywin's command. Varys says he must serve Cersei to stay alive, relying on his little birds.
13. **Tyrion considers sending Shae away** — He thinks of sending her to Chataya's brothel or marrying her to Bronn or Ser Tallad.
14. **Tyrion reflects on Sansa's untrustworthiness** — She once spilled her father's plans to Cersei; he cannot risk telling her about Shae.
15. **Dawn light leaks into the cellar** — Dragon skulls emerge from darkness, black amidst grey. Tyrion notes it is a new day, new year, new century, and steels himself for Joffrey's wedding.
16. **Shae departs first** — She dresses, kisses Tyrion's brow, says "My giant of Lannister. I love you so," and leaves to help Brella with bathwater.
17. **Tyrion resolves to wed Shae to Ser Tallad** — He thinks Tallad seems decent, and tall.
