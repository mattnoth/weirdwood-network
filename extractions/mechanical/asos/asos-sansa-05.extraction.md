# ASOS — Sansa V

## Chapter Metadata

- **chapter_number:** 62

- **location_primary:** Red Keep (godswood, serpentine steps, gallery, cliff)
- **location_secondary:** Blackwater Rush, Blackwater Bay, Littlefinger's trading galley
- **approximate_timeline:** Immediately following Joffrey's death at his wedding feast; spans nighttime through first hint of dawn
- **time_markers:** Bells begin tolling at start of chapter; moonlight described in the godswood; nighttime throughout escape; "the eastern sky was vague with the first hint of dawn" when they reach the galley; events span several hours of the same night
- **chapter_summary:** Sansa flees the Red Keep after Joffrey dies choking at his wedding feast. She changes clothes in the godswood, discovers a missing stone from the silver hair net Ser Dontos gave her, and confronts him about it. Dontos guides her down through the castle and down a secret cliff-face ladder to a skiff, which rows them out to a trading galley in Blackwater Bay. Aboard the galley, Petyr Baelish reveals himself as the orchestrator of her escape and has Dontos killed by crossbowmen, explaining that Dontos was always his catspaw.

## Events & Actions
1. **Sansa hears bells tolling** — Far across the city, bells begin tolling for Joffrey's death. She tells the trees "Joffrey is dead" to see if it will wake her from the dreamlike state.
2. **Sansa recalls fleeing the throne room** — She saw Joffrey on his knees, clawing at his throat, tearing at his skin as he fought to breathe. She fled sobbing. Lady Tanda was also fleeing and praised Sansa's good heart for weeping.
3. **Sansa changes clothes in the godswood** — She undoes her gown's laces with clumsy hands, shoves the gown into the bole of an oak, and puts on the pre-hidden brown wool dress, green cloak, and sturdy shoes, as Dontos instructed.
4. **Sansa discovers the missing amethyst** — Pulling the silver hair net free from her auburn hair, she finds one black amethyst missing, with only a dark smudge in the socket. She is seized by terror, suspecting the stone was used in Joffrey's death.
5. **Ser Dontos arrives drunk** — He staggers from the trees, calls her "sweet Jonquil," and steadies himself on her arm.
6. **Sansa confronts Dontos about the hair net** — She accuses him of lying about the stones being amethysts and says "There was murder in them!" Dontos insists they are black amethysts and says Joffrey choked on his pigeon pie.
7. **Dontos reveals Tyrion has been arrested** — He tells Sansa her husband has been seized, and "she" (Cersei, implied) thinks Tyrion did it.
8. **They descend through the castle** — Sansa sometimes lends Dontos her arm to keep him upright. He stumbles on the serpentine steps and retches. They cross a sunken courtyard and enter a long gallery lined with empty dragon-scaled armor.
9. **They exit through the castle wall** — Through an oaken door banded with iron, they pass through twelve feet of wall to the cliff top outside the castle.
10. **They climb down the secret cliff ladder** — Dontos goes first. Sansa counts ten bell tolls before following. She clings to the rough, cold stone, arms trembling, telling herself "one more step." She makes it to the bottom.
11. **They meet the oarsman Oswell** — Fifty yards downriver, a man waits in a small skiff half-hidden by a burned galley wreck. He refuses to give names and demands silence.
12. **They row out through the river wreckage** — With muffled oarlocks, they thread past sunken galleys, broken masts, and burned hulls. They pass the winch tower; the great chain is lowered. They enter Blackwater Bay.
13. **They reach Littlefinger's trading galley** — A ship with a merman figurehead wearing a golden crown and blowing a seashell horn. A rope ladder is dropped.
14. **Sansa boards the galley** — Sailors help her over the rail. Someone puts a cloak around her shoulders. She recognizes the voice — Petyr Baelish, whom she thought was in the Vale. Ser Lothor Brune stands beside him with a torch.
15. **Littlefinger orders Dontos killed** — When Dontos asks for his ten thousand dragons payment, Littlefinger says "Ser Lothor, the reward." Brune dips his torch; three crossbowmen fire. One bolt punches through the left crown on Dontos's surcoat (chest), another hits his throat, a third his belly. It happens so fast neither can cry out. Brune tosses the torch onto the corpse and the boat blazes.
16. **Sansa is horrified** — She clutches the rail, retches, and says "You killed him." Littlefinger dismisses Dontos as a sot and no man's friend.
17. **Littlefinger reveals his orchestration** — He explains that Dontos sold Sansa for ten thousand dragons; that Sansa's disappearance will implicate her in Joffrey's death; that gold cloaks will hunt for her; that Dontos was always his catspaw, recruited after Sansa saved Dontos's life at Joffrey's tourney.
18. **Littlefinger reveals the note was his** — The godswood meeting was his arrangement. He explains the godswood is the only place in the Red Keep safe from Varys's "little birds" — "there are trees instead of walls."
19. **Littlefinger reveals the jousting dwarfs were his idea** — He sent to Braavos for them, hid them in a brothel, and convinced Joffrey to include them by pointing out how much Tyrion would hate it.
20. **Littlefinger leads Sansa below decks** — Shows her to a cramped cabin with a featherbed, furs, and a cedar chest of fresh clothes (wool and linen).
21. **Littlefinger speaks of Catelyn** — He tells Sansa her mother and he were "more than friends," that Cat gave him a gift "a woman can give but once." He says Family, Duty, Honor meant he could never have her hand. He calls Sansa the daughter he might have had.
22. **Littlefinger counsels strategic deception** — "Always keep your foes confused. If they are never certain who you are or what you want, they cannot know what you are like to do next." He names it "the game of thrones."

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Dreamlike dissociation and numbness ("she felt as though she were in a dream," "so numb and dreamy," "my skin has turned to porcelain, to ivory, to steel"); mixed joy and grief over Joffrey's death — she wants to dance but weeps; terror upon discovering the missing amethyst; fear of being implicated in the murder; horror at Dontos's execution; nausea (retching twice); a brief flutter of hope at potential freedom from Tyrion; despair at learning Dontos was a puppet
- **Primary preoccupation:** The missing amethyst stone and what it means — her thumb is drawn to the empty socket compulsively; secondarily, fear about what will happen to her now
- **Key decisions made:** Decides to proceed with the escape despite her fears; continues forward after Dontos is killed — "there was no going back; her only road was forward"
- **Self-deception flags:** Sansa initially tries to believe the missing stone simply fell out ("It must have been loose in the setting, that's all"); she questions whether she wished Joffrey dead, then reminds herself she's "too old to believe in grumkins"; she wonders if Tyrion truly poisoned Joffrey despite suspecting the hair net was involved
