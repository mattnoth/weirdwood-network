# ASOS — Daenerys VI

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** ASOS
- **chapter_number:** 72
- **pov_character:** Daenerys Targaryen
- **pov_chapter_number:** Daenerys VI
- **first_available:** ASOS Daenerys VI
- **location_primary:** Great Pyramid of Meereen
- **location_secondary:** Meereen (city), Astapor (discussed), Yunkai (discussed), Naath (discussed)
- **approximate_timeline:** Shortly after the fall of Meereen; Dany has been in the city long enough to establish order and hold court
- **time_markers:** "a moon's turn since the last good rain" (at time of sewer assault); fighting lasted "most of a day and well into the night"; corpse removal needed; night quiet reported that morning; dawn comes at end of chapter; Dany wakes "three times from half-remembered nightmares"; Jorah given "until dawn" to leave; "that morning she summoned her captains"
- **chapter_summary:** Daenerys holds court in the Great Pyramid of Meereen. She receives an envoy from "King Cleon" of Astapor proposing a military alliance and marriage, and a Qartheen trading captain who brings grim news from Astapor and seeks to buy slaves. Dany permits freed people to sell themselves into slavery with a tenth-part tax. She then confronts Ser Barristan and Ser Jorah about their betrayals — forgiving Barristan and restoring his sword, but banishing Jorah from her service. After a sleepless night, she decides at dawn not to march west but to stay and rule Meereen.

- **Weather:** Cool/chilly air mentioned at night; breezes both in the audience chamber and on the terrace
- **Season indicators:** None explicitly stated
- **Time of day:** Morning (breaking fast, then court); transitions through evening, nighttime (sleepless), and dawn the next morning
- **Lighting:** Morning sun on the terrace; audience chamber described as "chilly" and "echoing"; moonlight streaming through slanting windows at night; pale gold and oyster pink glow at dawn; cobalt blue sky at zenith
- **Sounds:** Dragons roaring; bird chirping in the persimmon tree; city sounds drowning out birdsong; silver bells tinkling in bloodriders' braids; doors opening and closing
- **Smells:** Scented candle driving off flies; bowels and blood from the crucified masters; city sewers; Dany imagines meat and mead in a smoky hall (Jorah's home)
- **Notable sensory details:** Flies everywhere (buzzing Dany's head, in ale, drawn by honey); grass between toes on terrace; wind ripples on bathing pool; cool breeze at night; marble floors cold underfoot; trembling after banishing Jorah

## Events & Actions
1. **Dany breakfasts on the terrace** — She eats under the persimmon tree watching her dragons chase each other around the pyramid apex, surveying her conquered city.
2. **Dany dresses for court** — Jhiqui and Missandei bathe her; she dons purple samite robe, silver sash, dragon crown, and high-heeled silver slippers.
3. **Dany recalls the fall of Meereen** — She remembers using ships' masts as battering rams, the sewer infiltration, the slave uprising, and the sack; she remembers the 163 crucified masters.
4. **Dany holds court** — Her bloodriders, Grey Worm, Brown Ben Plumm, Daario, and others attend in the audience chamber.
5. **Brown Ben reports a quiet night** — No major disturbances; Dany's harsh justice is keeping order.
6. **Dany orders corpse removal** — Grey Worm is tasked with clearing the crucified bodies from the plaza; Missandei suggests boiling bones and returning them to kin.
7. **Ghael presents himself** — Envoy from "King Cleon" of Astapor proposes a military pact against Yunkai and a marriage between Cleon and Dany.
8. **Missandei reveals Cleon's identity** — He was a butcher slave owned by Grazdan mo Ullhor.
9. **Ghael reports Yunkai's war preparations** — New levies drilling, warships being built, envoys to New Ghis and Volantis, riders to Vaes Dothrak to bring a khalasar against Dany.
10. **Dany dismisses Ghael** — She says she will "consider" but gives him chambers and moves on.
11. **Qartheen captain of Indigo Star received** — Reports Astapor is bleeding; King Cleon seizing highborn boys to make new Unsullied; city in chaos.
12. **Captain requests to buy slaves** — Holds full of ivory, ambergris, zorse hides; wants slaves to sell in Lys and Volantis.
13. **Dany permits self-sale into slavery** — People may sell themselves but not their children or wives; a tenth-part tax collected in gold, silver, or ivory.
14. **Dany orders literate freedmen found** — To keep records of slave sales; doesn't trust Stormcrows or Second Sons with gold.
15. **Dany summons Ser Barristan and Ser Jorah** — Strong Belwas brings them in under guard.
16. **Dany confronts both knights** — Reminds them she sent them into sewers hoping not to see them again; calls them "an informer and a turncloak."
17. **Ser Barristan explains his past** — Served Robert because Viserys was a child who seemed like his father's son; used a false name to observe Dany for madness before pledging his sword.
18. **Dany forgives Ser Barristan** — He kneels, swears his sword to her service; she gives him Brown Ben's longsword.
19. **Ser Jorah attempts self-defense** — Claims he stopped spying; was trying to protect her; argues the information would have come out anyway.
20. **Dany banishes Ser Jorah** — She cannot forgive him for reporting her pregnancy, which led to the assassination attempt; gives him until dawn to leave Meereen on pain of death.
21. **Jorah is dragged out by Strong Belwas** — He walks "as if drunk, stumbling and slow."
22. **Daario offers to kill Jorah** — Dany refuses: "The scales are balanced now."
23. **Dany imagines Jorah's homeland** — Pictures Bear Island with its oaks, pines, and log hall.
24. **Dany changes clothes and tries to read** — Cannot concentrate; tormented by the decision about Jorah.
25. **Dany observes her dragons on the terrace** — Rhaegal asleep by pool, Drogon atop pyramid, Viserion hunting over the river in the distance.
26. **Ser Barristan offers to share Aerys's secrets** — Dany asks if her father was truly mad; Barristan confirms some madness; quotes Jaehaerys on the Targaryen coin metaphor; Dany postpones the full conversation.
27. **Dany spends a restless night** — Takes Irri to bed; has dark dreams; wakes three times; rises and goes to terrace in moonlight.
28. **Missandei joins Dany at dawn** — They watch the sunrise together; Dany mentions looking for "a house with a red door"; asks Missandei never to lie or betray her.
29. **Dawn reveals the city** — Grey bricks become colorful; Temple of the Graces dome blazes gold; fighting pits look like bleeding sores; Unsullied helms glint bronze.
30. **Dany announces she will not march** — Summons captains to the garden; declares she will stay and rule Meereen rather than continue west, to avoid leaving another ruined city behind her.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Lonely, powerful yet uncertain; fierce anger when recalling the crucified masters and Jorah's betrayal; grief at banishing Jorah; trembling afterward; restless and sleepless at night; tormented by fear of madness; resolved by dawn
- **Primary preoccupation:** Whether she is truly a conqueror or just causing death and ruin; whom she can trust; whether she is her father's daughter (mad); the tension between marching west and staying to rule
- **Key decisions made:** (1) Permits self-sale into slavery with tenth-part tax; (2) Forgives Ser Barristan and restores his sword; (3) Banishes Ser Jorah; (4) Decides not to march west — to stay and rule Meereen
- **Self-deception flags:** "It was just. It was. I did it for the children" — rationalizing the 163 crucifixions while feeling horror; "I'm cold" — lying to Irri about trembling (actually emotional); "Harsh justice is still justice" — repeated self-justification; comparing herself to Aegon the Conqueror while also feeling like "a scared little girl"
