- **chapter_summary:** Jon Snow, imprisoned in an ice cell for four days, is pulled out and ordered by Janos Slynt and Alliser Thorne to parley with Mance Rayder — actually to assassinate him. Jon descends the Wall and walks to the wildling camp, meeting Tormund en route. In Mance's tent, Jon sees the Horn of Winter and hears Mance's terms: let the free folk pass through the Wall or the horn will be sounded in three days. Before Jon can act, Stannis Baratheon's army attacks from the east and north, routing the wildlings and overwhelming Mance's forces.

## Events & Actions
1. **Jon in the cage** — Jon rides the cage down the Wall, contemplating his likely death and his tarnished honor, flexing his sword hand as Maester Aemon taught him.
2. **Jon's imprisonment recalled** — After four days in a five-by-five ice cell, Jon was pulled out this morning and brought before Janos Slynt in the King's Tower.
3. **Slynt and Thorne give Jon his mission** — Slynt declares Maester Aemon has blocked hanging Jon; instead they send him to Mance Rayder ostensibly to parley but actually to assassinate him.
4. **Jon accepts under duress** — Jon recognizes Thorne's trap: refuse and return to the ice cell without furs, or go and likely die either way.
5. **Jon descends and walks toward camp** — Jon passes the killing ground: the splintered turtle, dead mammoth, other corpses, a dead giant with crushed head. A raven calls "Snow, snow" at him.
6. **Tormund meets Jon** — Tormund rides out alone to escort Jon, commenting on the black cloak and the battle's cost (two hundred dead, a dozen giants, Mag himself).
7. **Toast to the dead** — They drink potent mead together, toasting Donal Noye, Mag the Mighty, and Ygritte. Tormund tells of Ygritte's death and Longspear stealing his daughter Munda.
8. **Arrival at Mance's tent** — Harma Dogshead and Varamyr Sixskins (with shadowcat and wolves) are with Mance outside. They are hostile; Tormund defends Jon.
9. **Varamyr reveals he took Orell's eagle** — Varamyr explains he can see over the Wall with eagle eyes and knows the Watch's weaknesses.
10. **Mance reveals intelligence** — Mance tells Jon they know how few the Watch are, their dwindling supplies, and the loss of the stair.
11. **Jon enters the tent** — Inside, Dalla lies in labor with Val attending. Jon sees the Horn of Winter.
12. **Mance reveals the Horn of Winter** — Claims it is the horn Joramun blew to wake giants from the earth; eight feet along the curve with gold bands graven with runes.
13. **Mance presents his terms** — Let the free folk pass through the Wall; in exchange, he gives them the horn and the Wall stands. Otherwise Tormund sounds it in three days at dawn.
14. **Mance explains his reasoning** — He came south not to conquer but to flee the Others; too much blood has been shed already; he could take the Wall by force but won't waste more lives.
15. **Jon considers his options** — He thinks about destroying the horn, about reporting back, about how Slynt and Thorne would react to any message.
16. **Outrider's horn sounds** — A warhorn alert stirs the wildling camp; Varamyr's eagle spots something coming from the east.
17. **Mance organizes defense** — Orders Harma to mount raiders, Tormund to form triple line of spears, sends for his horse and armor.
18. **Attack begins** — A line of rangers (Eastwatch men) emerges from the woods; Harma charges them with thirty raiders.
19. **Main force revealed** — Three great columns of heavy horse emerge from forest (east, northeast, north); steel armor and bright surcoats; trumpets blowing.
20. **Varamyr's eagle is burned** — The eagle bursts into flame high in the sky; Varamyr screams and collapses in agony.
21. **Battle rages** — Knights smash into wildling flanks; Harma is killed (her head waved on a pole); Tormund's lines break; only giants on mammoths hold.
22. **Mance overwhelmed** — Mance rallies men but a wedge of knights hits him; his mare is speared; the steel tide washes over him.
23. **Stannis identified** — Jon sees royal standards: flaming heart on yellow, black stag on gold. Knights cry "Stannis! Stannis! STANNIS!"
24. **Jon enters the tent** — Jon turns away from the battle and goes inside the tent (where Dalla is in labor).
