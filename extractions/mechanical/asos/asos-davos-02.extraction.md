- **time_markers:** Morning (Dragonmont's smoke visible at dawn approach); the journey across Blackwater Bay and through the Gullet took place over preceding days; red world at dawn and sunset during the crossing; events of the Blackwater referenced as recent past
- **chapter_summary:** Davos arrives at Dragonstone aboard Shayala's Dance after being rescued from the sea following the Battle of the Blackwater. He meets Salladhor Saan, who tells him that Stannis sees no one but Melisandre, the fleet is destroyed, and Lord Alester Florent now serves as Hand. Davos declares his intent to kill Melisandre, believing the Mother saved him for this purpose. He walks to Dragonstone castle, encounters Edric Storm and Princess Shireen in Aegon's Garden, and is arrested by Ser Axell Florent and taken to the dungeon.

## Events & Actions
1. **Davos approaches Dragonstone** — Aboard Shayala's Dance, Davos sees Dragonmont smoking and reflects on Melisandre and his desire to kill her.
2. **Ship enters harbor** — Davos notes the harbor is nearly empty; sees Salladhor Saan's Valyrian moored; does not see Lady Marya or Wraith.
3. **Khorane insists Davos see Salladhor Saan first** — Davos wants to go to the king but is too weak to defy the captain.
4. **Davos meets Salladhor Saan** — Found counting cargo in the hold of Bountiful Harvest; Saan embraces Davos warmly, surprised he lives.
5. **Salladhor Saan reports the battle's aftermath** — Most of Stannis's ships burned or sunk; Lady Marya and Wraith lost; Dale and Allard dead; Devan alive.
6. **Salladhor Saan describes the political situation** — Stannis sees no one but Melisandre; Queen Selyse and Lord Alester Florent govern; Saan made Lord of Blackwater Bay via parchment.
7. **Davos declares intent to kill Melisandre** — Shows the Lysene dirk; believes the Mother saved him for this purpose; recounts Melisandre's crimes.
8. **Salladhor Saan warns Davos and tries to dissuade him** — Tells of Queen's men burning Lord Sunglass and Rambton's sons; suggests hiring a Faceless Man instead.
9. **Davos departs Bountiful Harvest** — Over Salladhor Saan's angry objections, Davos leaves for the castle.
10. **Davos walks through deserted streets** — Dockside streets empty; houses shuttered with marks of mourning; Davos physically struggling.
11. **Davos denied entry at castle gates** — Crossbowman initially refuses him; Davos names dead acquaintances (Jate Blackberry, Lord Chyttering, Hookface Will, Hal the Hog) before being admitted via sally port.
12. **Davos encounters Edric Storm in Aegon's Garden** — Boy knocks into him; introduces himself; discusses his father Robert and Davos's shortened fingers.
13. **Ser Axell Florent arrests Davos** — Arrives with a dozen guards bearing the fiery heart of R'hllor; orders Davos seized and his dirk taken, saying he means to use it on "our lady."

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Determined and fatalistic; burning with desire for vengeance against Melisandre; grief-stricken over his dead sons; relieved that Devan lives; physically miserable but spiritually resolved
- **Primary preoccupation:** Killing Melisandre — he believes this is his divine purpose, the reason he survived
- **Key decisions made:** Refuses Salladhor Saan's offers of rest, ships, and smuggling work; chooses to go to the castle to find Melisandre despite his weakness and warnings
- **Self-deception flags:** Davos tells himself his cough is "nothing" and the gods didn't save him just to die of flux; he dismisses his own physical weakness as irrelevant to his mission; he frames his assassination plan as divinely ordained despite no clear evidence the Mother actually spoke to him
