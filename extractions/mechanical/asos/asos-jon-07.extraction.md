# ASOS — Jon VII

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** ASOS
- **chapter_number:** 56
- **pov_character:** Jon Snow
- **pov_chapter_number:** Jon VII
- **first_available:** ASOS Jon VII
- **location_primary:** Castle Black
- **location_secondary:** Mole's Town (burning, seen from distance), the Wall, kingsroad
- **approximate_timeline:** Shortly after Jon's escape from the wildlings; battle day at Castle Black when Styr's Thenns attack from the south
- **time_markers:** Morning (smoke of Mole's Town burning wakes them); midday comes and goes with no sign of Thenns; afternoon (smoke blows away); near evenfall (Owen returns with mutton); sun low in west with shadows sharp and black; stars coming out; night (attack comes after dark)
- **chapter_summary:** Jon Snow, injured but insisting on fighting, takes position atop the King's Tower with Satin and Deaf Dick Follard as Castle Black prepares for attack by Styr's Thenns. Donal Noye commands the defense, placing scarecrow sentinels to make the castle look well-manned and building a crescent barricade around the gate. The Thenns attack at night; Jon kills several wildlings with his longbow and uses Longclaw to kill a Thenn who breaches the trapdoor. The barricade falls and the Thenns push up the wooden stair, but Noye's trap — oil-soaked steps and kindling — is ignited, destroying the staircase and killing the attackers including Styr. Afterward, Jon searches the dead and finds Ygritte dying with an arrow in her chest; she dies in his arms.

## Events & Actions
1. **Mole's Town burns** — Jon wakes to smoke rising from Mole's Town to the south, set ablaze by Styr's wildlings.
2. **Jon insists on fighting** — Despite his injured leg, Jon convinces Donal Noye to let him fight from a tower with a longbow.
3. **Castle Black prepares for defense** — Scarecrow sentinels placed everywhere; crescent barricade built of stores around the gate; Mole's Town refugees armed and assigned positions; women and children put to work.
4. **Last villagers climb the Wall** — Grenn carries a child, Pyp helps an old man; Clydas and Maester Aemon ride winch cage to safety atop the Wall.
5. **Day passes without attack** — Midday and afternoon pass; smoke blows away; no Thenns on the kingsroad; tension builds.
6. **Evening preparations** — Jon orders fire lit and kettle filled with oil as sun sets; bars the tower door; returns to roof.
7. **Night attack begins** — Horns blow in the dark; Jon directs Satin and Dick to their positions.
8. **Jon shoots wildlings in the yard** — Kills one of three runners; kills a man with a throat shot; drops an archer.
9. **Jon recognizes Big Boil** — Shoots at an axeman hacking at Hardin's Tower door; recognizes Big Boil; Mully puts an arrow through Big Boil's leg.
10. **Deaf Dick Follard killed** — Dick stands on a crenel for a better shot and is hit by an arrow from below; falls a hundred feet to his death.
11. **Jon glimpses red hair** — Sees a figure with thick red hair (possibly Ygritte) near Dick's body; cannot bring himself to release the arrow; she vanishes.
12. **Buildings burn** — Common hall and east stables catch fire; roofs collapse.
13. **Thenns storm the barricade** — Fifty Thenns charge up the kingsroad; others swarm through the castle; they assault the crescent barricade with spears and bronze axes.
14. **Thenn breaches King's Tower trapdoor** — Jon kills the first Thenn through the trapdoor with Longclaw; Satin shoots the second; they pour boiling oil down the hole on the rest.
15. **Barricade falls** — Mole's Town men flee, then Night's Watch brothers; Rast, Young Henly, Old Henly, Easy, and Dornish Dilly are killed; Kegs nearly killed but saved by Satin's bolt.
16. **Thenns take the gate and push up the stairs** — Wildlings overwhelm defenders on each landing, killing as they go; rout develops.
17. **Fire trap sprung** — Donal Noye sounds warhorn from ninth landing; Jon and other archers fire flaming arrows into kindling piled under steps (barrels of lard, lamp oil, bags of leaves, oily rags); steps drenched in oil from ninth landing to seventh are ignited.
18. **Stair destroyed** — Fire consumes the wooden stairs; wildlings trapped between fires above and below; ice cracks from heat; lower third of stair breaks off with tons of ice, killing Styr and remaining Thenns.
19. **Jon searches the dead** — After battle, Jon and Satin descend; Jon finds Quort dead, Stone Thumbs dying, Big Boil alive.
20. **Ygritte found dying** — Jon finds Ygritte beneath the Lord Commander's Tower with a black arrow (white duck feather fletching) between her breasts; she recognizes him, asks about the castle, recalls the cave, says "You know nothing, Jon Snow," and dies.
