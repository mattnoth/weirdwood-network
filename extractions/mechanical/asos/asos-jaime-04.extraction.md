- **chapter_summary:** Jaime suffers in agony from his severed hand as the Brave Companions transport him toward Harrenhal, bound to Brienne. He intervenes when Zollo, Rorge, and Shagwell attempt to rape Brienne by shouting "sapphires," invoking the lie about Tarth's wealth. They arrive at Harrenhal where Roose Bolton takes custody, shares news of the Blackwater and Karstark's execution, and sends Jaime to Qyburn for treatment of his infected stump.

## Events & Actions
1. **Jaime suffers from phantom hand pain** — Days after the amputation, he feels fire lancing up his arm; sometimes weeps, sometimes prays childhood prayers
2. **Bound face-to-face with Brienne** — After falling from the saddle twice, they are bound together on a shared horse, sometimes face-to-face
3. **Tricked into drinking horse piss** — The Companions give Jaime a cup of horse piss; he drinks it, then retches it up; they make Brienne clean him
4. **Failed escape attempt** — On a damp cold morning, Jaime grabs a Dornishman's sword with his left hand; Shagwell dances around him; Rorge kicks the sword away; Vargo threatens to take his other hand
5. **Nighttime contemplation under stars** — Jaime lies on his back staring at the beautiful night sky (King's Crown at zenith, Stallion, Swan, Moonmaid); contemplates dying
6. **Brienne urges Jaime to live** — She whispers to him, calls him "craven" when he says he's dying; tells him to live, fight, and take revenge; Rorge kicks her for talking
7. **Jaime resolves to survive** — Shocked by "craven," he decides to live for Cersei, Tyrion, and vengeance; forces himself to eat the next morning
8. **Urswyck mentions Harrenhal** — Jaime hears their destination is Harrenhal and laughs (remembering receiving his white cloak there); Timeon slashes his face with a whip
9. **Jaime tells Brienne about the tourney** — Whispers about being made a Kingsguard at Whent's tourney at age fifteen; Aerys sent him away and never let him joust
10. **Attempted rape of Brienne** — Zollo, Rorge, and Shagwell approach to rape her; argue about who goes first and which end
11. **Jaime intervenes** — Whispers to Brienne to "go far away inside"; she refuses defiantly; Rorge threatens to mutilate her
12. **Jaime shouts "SAPPHIRES"** — Draws attention; Urswyck and Vargo Hoat arrive; Hoat forbids touching Brienne ("thee'th worth a bag of thapphireth"); guards posted nightly
13. **Brienne thanks Jaime** — Two nights later she asks why he shouted; he says an honorable man would have told the truth about the Sapphire Isle; she thanks him; he cites the debt from the river
14. **Forced march to Harrenhal** — Jaime dismounts a mile from gates; roped to Hoat's saddle; stumbles in beside Brienne; a Lyseni impales a dog and calls it "Kingslayer's banner"
15. **Arrival at Harrenhal** — Thick walls; outer ward full of onlookers; Hoat presents "the Kingthlayer"; spear-jab sends Jaime sprawling onto his stump
16. **Meeting the Freys** — Jaime recognizes Ser Danwell, Ser Aenys, Ser Hosteen; tells them of Ser Cleos's death; they argue about ransoming/killing him
17. **Brienne appeals to the Freys** — Claims oath to House Stark; Ser Aenys spits at her, says Robb betrayed them
18. **Roose Bolton takes command** — Speaks softly; asserts authority over Hoat; reveals news: Karstark executed, Battle of Blackwater won by Lannisters/Tyrells, Joffrey betrothed to Tyrell, not Sansa
19. **Bolton's pregnant pause** — Says Cersei is well, and "your... nephew" — implying he knows Jaime's secret
20. **Bolton frees Brienne and orders treatment for Jaime** — Calls Brienne "my lady"; expresses displeasure at Hoat for the hand and rape attempt; sends Jaime to Qyburn via Walton
21. **Qyburn treats the stump** — Cuts away rotten flesh, pours boiling wine, applies poultice of nettle/mustard seed/bread mold; Jaime refuses milk of the poppy; screams; passes out briefly
22. **Qyburn's backstory hint** — Says "The Citadel took my chain"; treats the eye wound as well
23. **News of the Blackwater** — Qyburn tells Jaime that Stannis was caught between Tywin and fire; the Imp set the river aflame
24. **Qyburn prescribes herbs** — Offers to grind herbs for fever; says he'll put a leech on the eye tomorrow; mentions Bolton is fond of leeches

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Agony, despair giving way to grim determination; humiliation; fear and defiance mixed; feverish lightness; dark humor throughout; gratitude toward Brienne that he refuses to acknowledge openly
- **Primary preoccupation:** The pain of his lost hand; survival; reaching Cersei and King's Landing; identity crisis — "Was that all I was, a sword hand?"
- **Key decisions made:** Resolves to live (after Brienne calls him craven); resolves to eat and survive for Cersei, Tyrion, and vengeance; shouts "sapphires" to save Brienne; refuses milk of the poppy; refuses amputation of remaining arm
- **Self-deception flags:** Claims saving Brienne was merely paying a debt ("that was for the river"), minimizing his genuine concern for her; "What do I care if she does?" immediately followed by helping her; tells himself his hand is "all I was" — simplifying his identity to a single attribute
