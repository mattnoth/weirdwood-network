## Events & Actions
1. **Escape from Harrenhal** — Arya, Gendry, and Hot Pie flee Harrenhal at night on three stolen horses. Arya has stolen a map and dagger from Roose Bolton's solar, swords from the armory, and bread and cheese from the kitchens.
2. **Guard killed at postern gate** — Arya killed a guard by slitting his throat when he knelt to pick up the iron coin she dropped as a distraction (recalled, occurred just before the chapter opens).
3. **Ride north through rain** — They ride north away from the lake along a rutted farm road and into the woods. Arya takes the lead at a brisk trot.
4. **Stream diversion** — At the first stream, Arya turns off the road and follows the water for a quarter-mile before climbing out up a stony bank, hoping to throw off tracking dogs.
5. **Recitation of Syrio's words and Jaqen's** — Arya whispers "Fear cuts deeper than swords" and "valar morghulis" as she rides.
6. **Wolf encounter at fawn corpse** — They come upon three wolves devouring a fawn. Hot Pie's horse catches the scent and bolts. Two wolves flee; the third bares its teeth. Arya tells Gendry to back away slowly, then rides after Hot Pie.
7. **Burned village passage** — They pass through a burned village with blackened hovels and bones of a dozen dead men hanging from apple trees. Hot Pie prays to the Mother. Arya recites her kill list and ends with "valar morghulis," touches Jaqen's coin, and plucks an apple from the trees.
8. **Dawn without sun** — The sky lightens to grey but the sun never appears. Colors return: soldier pines in somber greens, broadleafs in russets and faded golds beginning to brown.
9. **Cold breakfast and map consultation** — They stop to water horses and eat bread and cheese. Arya shows the map and explains their route: north to the Trident, then upstream to Riverrun.
10. **Hot Pie discovers Arya can read** — He is astonished that she can read the map.
11. **Arya conceals her identity** — She does not tell Hot Pie why Riverrun is safe, hiding that it's her grandfather's castle and that Robb will be there. She reflects that Gendry knows her identity but Hot Pie does not.
12. **Day of hard riding** — Arya pushes the pace, trotting and sometimes galloping, but the terrain grows hillier. She doubles back to confuse their trail.
13. **Wolf pack sighting from ridge** — From a ridge crest, Arya spots dark shapes crossing a stream below; initially fears Bolton's riders, then recognizes a wolf pack. She howls at them; the largest wolf howls back, making her shiver.
14. **Hot Pie complains** — By midday Hot Pie says he's sore, tired, and about to fall off. Arya and Gendry warn that wolves or Mummers would find him. He stays mounted.
15. **Gendry's struggles** — Gendry rides awkwardly but stubbornly refuses to complain. Arya reflects that both boys are city-born and unused to riding.
16. **Arya's internal vow** — She thinks she'd be faster alone but won't leave them; they're her pack, her friends. She vows silently not to let them be taken, touching her sword hilt.
17. **First river reached** — Late afternoon, they emerge from trees at a river. Hot Pie thinks it's the Trident; Arya says it's too narrow (about thirty feet across) and they haven't come far enough.
18. **Map debate** — They study the map in the rain. Gendry and Arya identify possible rivers: the Little Willow (which flows into the Big Willow, then into the Trident) or Rippledown Rill (which loops back toward the lake and Harrenhal). They can't determine which river it is.
19. **Decision to cross** — Arya decides to cross and keep going north. Hot Pie worries about snakes and depth. Gendry questions their direction. Arya rides ahead; both boys follow.
20. **First river ford** — Arya finds a crossing half-mile upstream. The river is brown, fast, and swollen; the deep part comes up past the horse's belly. Water fills her boots.
21. **Second river ford** — They reach and ford a shallower, easier river without argument.
22. **Dusk rest and fire refusal** — They stop at dusk for bread and cheese. Hot Pie asks for a fire; Arya and Gendry both shout "NO!" simultaneously.
23. **Arya thinks of Jon Snow** — Gendry's speaking in unison with her reminds her of Jon; she reflects she misses Jon most of all her brothers.
24. **Night riding and exhaustion** — They ride on into darkness. Arya fights sleep, her eyes closing repeatedly. Her horse wanders and stops.
25. **Gendry wakes Arya** — Gendry shakes her awake; her horse was walking in circles. He tells her Hot Pie rode into a tree limb and fell off. He insists they stop to sleep.
26. **Camp established** — Hot Pie is already asleep with cheese in hand. Arya hobbles her horse, lies under a beech tree on hard damp ground, and unsheathes her sword beside her.
27. **Kill list recitation before sleep** — Arya whispers "Ser Gregor. Dunsen, Polliver, Raff the Sweetling. The Tickler and . . . the Tickler . . . the Hound . . ." trailing off as she falls asleep.
28. **Wolf dream** — Arya dreams she is a huge, powerful wolf. She and her pack of gaunt, wet, silent wolves attack a group of at least four Brave Companions — a pale Lyseni, a dark axeman from Ib, the scarred Dothraki Iggo, and a Dornishman. The pack kills the Lyseni, the axeman, and the Dornishman. A belled man with a curved silvery weapon fights back, his horse killing one wolf and his blade cutting another nearly in half. The wolf-Arya leaps on his back, locks her jaws on his arm through leather and wool and flesh, and rips the limb from his shoulder.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** A paradox of fear and calm — Arya acknowledges she should be more frightened but feels calmer than she ever did in Harrenhal. The rain washed away the guard's blood, she has a sword, and she feels "unafraid." Underneath this calm is anxiety about pursuit, guilt about endangering Gendry and Hot Pie, and longing for Jon Snow. Exhaustion increasingly dominates as the chapter progresses.
- **Primary preoccupation:** Evading pursuit; reaching the Trident and Riverrun; keeping the group moving despite exhaustion. Secondary: protecting Hot Pie and Gendry; navigating by moss and map.
- **Key decisions made:** (1) Turn off the road and use streams to throw off dogs. (2) Keep her identity secret from Hot Pie. (3) Cross the unknown river rather than follow it up or downstream. (4) Refuse fires. (5) Reluctantly agree to stop and sleep when Gendry insists.
- **Self-deception flags:** "I was just resting my eyes" — denying she fell asleep in the saddle. Also, she tells herself she feels calm and unafraid, but her thoughts reveal persistent fear of Bolton, Vargo Hoat, and the Mummers.
