## Events & Actions
1. **Ghost's disappearance** — Ghost is gone when the wildlings leave the cave at dawn; Jon hopes the direwolf understood about Castle Black.
2. **Scouting deployment** — The Magnar sends a dozen riders east and a dozen west to climb high hills and watch for rangers or riders on the Wall; Thenns carry bronze-banded warhorns.
3. **Approach to the Wall** — The wildlings ride to the base of the Wall at a ridge west of Castle Black where the ice sits atop a steep, forested granite ridge.
4. **Climbing teams form** — Jarl calls out names; twelve climbers (including himself) form three teams of four. All are young, lean, and hard.
5. **Climbing preparation** — The teams lace on spiked doeskin boots, wind hempen rope across shoulders and chests, equip stone hammers, stakes, and antler ice axes.
6. **Jarl's speech** — Jarl promises Mance's reward: southron swords of castle-forged steel and their names in a song for the first team up.
7. **Ascent begins** — Teams scramble up the ridge and vanish into trees. Jarl finds a sentinel tree leaning against the Wall and uses it to start higher (~300 feet before touching ice).
8. **Hours of climbing** — Jon watches as climbers hack, hammer stakes, and kick toeholds into the ice. The Magnar grows impatient. No Night's Watch patrol appears.
9. **Jarl's fall at noon** — At about 600 feet, Jarl loops his rope around a wind-carved pinnacle that crumbles; he falls but is caught by the rope; the team recovers.
10. **Grigg the Goat draws even** — After Jarl's setback, Grigg's team nearly catches up; Errok's team remains well behind at ~500 feet, moving sideways toward a chimney.
11. **Ice sheet collapse** — A sheet of ice a foot thick and fifty feet square breaks free from the Wall, sweeping Jarl and his entire team away. Chunks rain down; Jon shields Ygritte; a Thenn's nose is broken by a chunk.
12. **Discovery of the dead** — Jarl is found impaled on a splintered tree branch, still roped to three broken men below; one survivor begs for mercy and is killed by a Thenn with a stone mace.
13. **Funeral pyre** — The Magnar orders fuel gathered; a pyre is built and the dead are burned.
14. **Grigg reaches the top** — While the dead burn, Grigg the Goat reaches the summit; Errok's four join him.
15. **Ladders hauled up** — Climbers tie their ropes together, lower one end; raiders below uncask five pre-made hemp rope ladders; these are hauled up and staked to the Wall.
16. **Mass ascent begins** — Five Thenns start up the ladders at the Magnar's command; two Thenns fall to their deaths during the ascent.
17. **Jon and Ygritte reach the top** — Near midnight, Jon reaches the top; Ygritte is trembling, says the Wall tried to shake her off.
18. **Ygritte's outburst about the Horn** — Ygritte strikes Jon when he tries to comfort her; she cries not from fear but anger that they never found the Horn of Joramun to bring the Wall down.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Conflicted and torn; hopeful when Ghost leaves (maybe heading to Castle Black); grimly watchful during the climb; dread at the thought of climbing the rope; a certain grim pride in the Night's Watch's history of catching climbers
- **Primary preoccupation:** His impossible position — if he stays with Ygritte he must become a wildling; if he returns to duty the Magnar may kill Ygritte; if he takes her with him they'd be outcasts everywhere
- **Key decisions made:** None explicit — Jon remains passive, hoping circumstances (a Night's Watch patrol) will resolve his dilemma for him
- **Self-deception flags:** Jon "allowed himself to hope" that Ghost understood about Castle Black — projecting intent onto the direwolf; Jon "hoping that Styr's fears proved well founded" — wanting external forces to solve his loyalty conflict rather than acting
