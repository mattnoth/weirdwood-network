- **chapter_summary:** Jon marches south through the Gift with Styr's Thenn raiders and Jarl's climbers, maintaining his cover as a turncloak while desperately seeking a chance to escape and warn Castle Black. The column arrives at an abandoned village beside a lake with a stone tower (Queenscrown) during a rainstorm. They discover an old man sheltering in the ruined inn. Styr orders Jon to kill the captive; Jon refuses. Ygritte kills the old man instead. A direwolf (grey, not Ghost) attacks the wildlings in the chaos of a lightning strike, and Jon seizes the opportunity to fight free, mount the old man's horse, and escape. He rides north through the night with an arrow wound in his thigh.

## Events & Actions
1. **March through the Gift** — Jon and the wildling column march south through Brandon's Gift and the New Gift, passing abandoned roundtowers and avoiding inhabited villages. Grigg the Goat leads them past settlements.
2. **Jon and Ygritte debate free folk philosophy** — They argue about land ownership, raiding, the stealing of women, and whether Mance can win the war. Ygritte articulates the free folk worldview. Jon tells her Mance will fail.
3. **Ygritte declares Jon is hers** — She pushes him against a tree, kisses him publicly in the column, and declares "You're mine... Mine, as I'm yours."
4. **Jon reflects on his impossible position** — He considers the need to escape and warn Castle Black, his feelings for Ygritte, and the weak state of the garrison. He catalogs Castle Black's defenders.
5. **Storm approaches and column seeks shelter** — Lenn foretells a bad storm; Grigg tells the Magnar of a nearby village. They march to the abandoned lakeside village (Queenscrown).
6. **Jon tells Ygritte about Queenscrown** — He identifies the gold-painted merlons on the island tower and tells her the story of Queen Alysanne.
7. **Discovery of the old man** — Scouts Errok and Del find a lone old man sheltering in the ruined inn with his horse and a small fire. Thenns surround and capture him.
8. **Styr orders Jon to kill the old man** — The Magnar commands Jon to prove his loyalty by executing the captive.
9. **Jon refuses to kill** — Despite Ygritte's urging and the Magnar's pressure, Jon says "No" and turns his back.
10. **Ygritte kills the old man** — She snatches her knife, grabs the old man by the hair, and cuts his throat ear to ear. She shouts "You know nothing, Jon Snow!" and flings the bloody blade at Jon's feet.
11. **Lightning strikes the tower; direwolf attacks** — A massive blue-white lightning bolt hits the tower on the island. A large grey direwolf leaps down among the wildlings, killing a Thenn by tearing out his throat, then killing Del.
12. **Jon fights free** — In the chaos, Jon cuts down a Thenn, slashes others, hacks at Bodger's face, and mounts the old man's horse.
13. **Jon escapes on horseback** — The mare bolts through mud, rain, and darkness. A spear flies past his ear. He rides until the shouts die behind him.
14. **Jon discovers arrow in his thigh** — Hours later, the rain stops. Jon finds an arrow lodged in his right thigh. The fletching may be grey (Ygritte's arrows use pale grey goose feathers).
15. **Jon removes the arrow** — He painfully shoves the arrow through his leg, washes the wound in a stream, and binds it with cloth torn from his cloak.
16. **Jon rides north for Castle Black** — He finds the Ice Dragon constellation, turns the mare north, and rides until dawn.
