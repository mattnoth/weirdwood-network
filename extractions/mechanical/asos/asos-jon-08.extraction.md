# ASOS — Jon VIII

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** ASOS
- **chapter_number:** 65
- **pov_character:** Jon Snow
- **pov_chapter_number:** Jon VIII
- **first_available:** ASOS Jon VIII
- **location_primary:** The Wall / Castle Black
- **location_secondary:** Winterfell crypts (dream), the tunnel through the Wall
- **approximate_timeline:** Night of the major wildling assault on Castle Black from the north; follows previous battles at the Wall
- **time_markers:** Full night battle from "black of night" through dawn; morning comes with grey light then sunrise; chapter spans one full night into the following morning
- **chapter_summary:** Jon is woken by horn blasts signaling the wildling army's arrival from the north. Donal Noye commands the initial defense atop the Wall, then leaves Jon in charge while he goes to hold the tunnel. Jon commands the archers and siege engines through the night, rallies his men at dawn when they see the full wildling host, and repels the assault. Afterward, Jon finds Noye dead in the tunnel alongside the giant Mag the Mighty. Maester Aemon tells Jon he must take command of the defense.

- **Smells:** Blood and death in the tunnel (described as "suffocating"); no other smells explicitly noted above
- **Notable sensory details:** Cold hitting Jon "in the teeth like a fist"; white-hot knife of pain in his leg; frozen sweat; crabbed and stiff half-frozen fingers; fever returning; the weight of the Wall felt above his head in the tunnel; ice pressing close

## Events & Actions
1. **Jon dreams of Winterfell crypts** — Stone kings reject him; he calls for family; sees a bloody grey direwolf
2. **Jon wakes and reflects** — Remembers burning Ygritte; worries Ghost may be dead based on dream imagery; worries about Bran
3. **Horn blows twice (wildlings signal)** — Jon dresses, arms himself with Longclaw, takes crutch, descends
4. **Brothers gather at Wall base** — Wait for winch cage; Clydas serves mulled wine, Hobb gives bread
5. **Cage ascends to Wall top** — Jon and others ride up; Horse helps Jon out
6. **Donal Noye commands initial defense** — Orders trebuchets to launch burning pitch barrels for illumination
7. **Trebuchets reveal mammoth army** — First glimpse shows a dozen, second volley reveals a hundred mammoths
8. **Red Alyn sounds sentry horn** — Wildlings answer with a dozen horns, drums, and pipes
9. **Wildlings attack the gate** — Pyp spots them; Noye orders oil jars set alight and pushed over edge
10. **Noye gives Jon command of the Wall** — Selects men to defend tunnel, leaves Jon in charge
11. **Night battle atop the Wall** — Jon commands archers in darkness; arrows exchanged blindly; catapults fire rocks; right trebuchet breaks; onion broth served during fighting
12. **Dawn reveals full wildling host** — Tens of thousands visible; raiders, giants, wargs, skinchangers, Hornfoot men, ice river cannibals, dog chariots from Frozen Shore
13. **Jon rallies the defenders** — Shouts that the Wall will stop them; mocks the wildlings' inability to climb; men cheer
14. **Kegs blows warhorn for battle** — Two long blasts taken up by other horns along the Wall
15. **Jon commands focused volley on giants with ram** — Orders archers to target giants pushing the ram; fires catapults, scorpions, trebuchet with caltrops
16. **Sustained arrow fire destroys the ram** — Giants pushing ram killed; mammoths wounded and panicked
17. **Mammoth attacks gate below** — Grenn and Pyp roll burning oil barrels over edge; flaming giant seen
18. **Wildling army routs** — Mammoths flee fire, crash into those behind; center collapses; horsemen and chariots retreat
19. **Jon descends in cage** — Gives Grenn command of Wall; Pyp helps him down
20. **Jon enters the tunnel** — Pyp gets spare key from Maester Aemon; Aemon and Clydas accompany with lantern
21. **Discovery of tunnel battle aftermath** — Outer door destroyed; giant (Mag the Mighty) broke through; killed Spotted Pate (head torn off); wrenched iron bars apart
22. **Donal Noye found dead** — Sword buried in Mag's throat; Mag crushed Noye's spine; both dead
23. **Jon identifies Mag the Mighty** — King of the giants; recognizes him
24. **Jon exits tunnel to survey exterior damage** — Dead mammoth blocking way; three more giants dead outside; melted ice fallen from Wall
25. **Jon returns and suggests Ser Wynton take command** — Maester Aemon explains Wynton is too senile
26. **Maester Aemon tells Jon he must lead** — Cites Donal Noye's choice, Qhorin Halfhand's choice, Mormont making him steward, his Stark blood
