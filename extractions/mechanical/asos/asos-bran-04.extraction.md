- **chapter_summary:** Bran, the Reeds, Hodor, and Summer arrive at the Nightfort on the Wall, finding the gate sealed with stone and ice. Meera climbs the Wall's ice steps but finds no way down the other side. They explore the ruined castle and camp in the octagonal kitchen. That night, Samwell Tarly and Gilly with her baby emerge from a deep well, having come through a hidden passage called the Black Gate. Sam reveals the gate can only be opened by a Night's Watch sworn brother and that a mysterious figure called Coldhands sent him to find someone — whom Jojen identifies as Bran. The group descends the well and passes through the Black Gate, a living weirwood door that speaks and opens its mouth to let them through.

## Events & Actions
1. **Arrival at the Nightfort** — Bran, Meera, Jojen, Hodor, and Summer arrive at the Nightfort and find it a desolation of rubble, ruins, and weeds.
2. **Sealed gate discovered** — They confirm the Nightfort's gate through the Wall is sealed: iron portcullis lowered, chains removed, tunnel packed with frozen stone and rubble.
3. **Bran suppresses traumatic dream** — Bran deliberately avoids thinking about a dream he (or Summer) had involving Robb and Grey Wind, fearing that acknowledging it makes it real.
4. **Debate about route** — Bran argues they should have followed Jon to Castle Black; Jojen insists they dare not, citing wildling danger (a hundred wildlings who killed a man and wanted to kill Jon).
5. **Recap of wildling aftermath** — Bran recalls that Summer killed three or more wildlings but was shot with an arrow; the pain drove Bran out of the wolf's skin. They hid without a fire after the storm, and the wildlings departed the next morning heading north by east.
6. **Summer's return and healing** — Summer returned with an injured back leg; Meera drew out the broken arrow and treated the wound with plant juices found at the base of the tower; the direwolf is still limping but improving.
7. **Meera proposes scouting** — Meera suggests trying another castle or climbing to the top of the Wall to look around.
8. **Bran explains other castles** — Bran identifies Deep Lake and Queensgate to the east, Icemark to the west, noting all gates are sealed except Castle Black, Eastwatch, and the Shadow Tower.
9. **Meera climbs the Wall** — Meera ascends the ancient ice steps hewn into the Wall at the Nightfort, the only castle where steps were cut from the Wall itself. The steps have melted and refrozen repeatedly, becoming treacherous nubs.
10. **Bran, Jojen, and Hodor explore the Nightfort** — They explore the bell tower, rookery, brewhouse vault, library (destroyed), dungeon (rusted bars), great hall (one wall remaining), bathhouse (sinking), practice yard (overgrown), armory, and forge. Summer catches a grey rat.
11. **Meera returns from the Wall** — She reports seeing the haunted forest, wild hills, a lake, old snow, icicles, and an eagle, but no way down the other side — the drop is sheer and the ice too smooth.
12. **Camp in the kitchen** — They settle in the stone octagonal kitchen with its broken dome, noting the crooked weirwood growing through the floor beside the central well. Bran observes the well is twelve feet across with steps spiraling down into unseen depths.
13. **Hodor drops a stone into the well** — Despite Bran's protest, Hodor tosses a piece of slate into the well. Far below, the sound is "more a gulp" than a splash, and Bran hears something thrashing in the water.
14. **Meera prepares supper** — Jojen lights a fire; Meera cooks fish caught at the last stream. They eat.
15. **Bran recalls Old Nan's stories** — As night falls, Bran recalls the tales of Night's King (thirteenth Lord Commander who took a corpse queen), the Rat Cook, the seventy-nine sentinels, Lord Ryswell, Mad Axe, the thing that came in the night, and the 'prentice boys.
16. **Bran hears sounds from the well** — Unable to sleep, Bran hears footsteps, whimpering, and heavy breathing coming up the well steps. He is terrified.
17. **Bran wakes Meera** — He drags himself to Meera and silently alerts her. She takes up her frog spear and net and moves toward the well.
18. **Bran wargs into Hodor** — Bran slips into Hodor's skin, finding it much harder than warging into Summer — "like trying to pull a left boot on your right foot." He stands, walks, and grabs Hodor's longsword.
19. **Sam emerges and is netted** — A large black shape heaves up from the well. Bran panics and loses control of Hodor. Meera nets the shape and pokes it with her spear. It screams and turns out to be Samwell Tarly.
20. **Gilly and baby appear** — Gilly emerges at the lip of the well with a screaming baby, bundled in furs under an enormous black cloak.
21. **Identities revealed** — Sam identifies himself as Samwell Tarly, a steward and brother of the Night's Watch who cared for Lord Mormont's ravens. He mentions losing them at the Fist.
22. **Coldhands discussed** — Gilly and Sam explain that a figure called "Coldhands" (not his true name) brought them here on his elk with his ravens. He wears black like a brother, is pale as a wight but with different eyes, and has hands cold as ice. He saved them from dead men. Coldhands said Sam was not "the one" — someone else was.
23. **Sam reveals the Black Gate** — Sam tells them there is a hidden gate at the bottom of the well, as old as the Wall itself, called the Black Gate. Only a sworn brother of the Night's Watch can open it.
24. **Coldhands cannot pass the Wall** — Sam explains that the Wall is "more than just ice and stone" — spells are woven into it, old and strong, and Coldhands cannot pass beyond it.
25. **Jojen identifies Bran as "the one"** — Jojen tells Sam that Bran, not Jojen, is the one Coldhands sent Sam to find.
26. **Sam recognizes Bran** — Sam realizes Bran is Jon Snow's brother, "the one who fell." Jojen says "that boy is dead" and Bran asks Sam to keep his identity secret. Sam agrees.
27. **Bran shares news of Jon** — Bran tells Sam that Jon is alive, was with wildlings who killed a man, and escaped on a horse, likely heading to Castle Black.
28. **Sam meets Summer** — Summer leaps down from the broken dome. Sam recognizes the direwolf connection, mentions knowing Ghost, and lets Summer lick his hand.
29. **Bran decides to go** — Bran declares they will go with Sam through the Black Gate.
30. **Gilly left at the fire** — Sam asks if Gilly will be safe; Meera welcomes her to their fire. Sam promises to return for her.
31. **Descent into the well** — Sam leads, followed by Jojen, Summer, Hodor with Bran, and Meera in the rear. It grows darker and colder with each turn.
32. **The Black Gate revealed** — At the bottom, they find a white weirwood door with an ancient, shrunken face that glows faintly. The door opens its blind white eyes and asks "Who are you?"
33. **Sam recites the Night's Watch oath** — Sam says: "I am the sword in the darkness. I am the watcher on the walls. I am the fire that burns against the cold, the light that brings the dawn, the horn that wakes the sleepers. I am the shield that guards the realms of men."
34. **Passage through the Black Gate** — The door says "Then pass" and opens its mouth wider and wider. They pass through. A drop of water from the door falls on Bran — "strangely warm, and salty as a tear."

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Deeply afraid of the Nightfort and its legendary horrors, but forcing himself to be brave ("I'm a prince of the north, a Stark of Winterfell, almost a man grown, I have to be as brave as Robb"). Grief-stricken over Robb and Grey Wind — actively suppressing a dream he had about them ("if he never talked of it maybe he could forget he ever dreamed it, and then it wouldn't have happened"). Terrified when he hears sounds from the well. Relieved when the "monster" is only Sam. Wistful about being unable to climb.
- **Primary preoccupation:** Fear of the Nightfort and Old Nan's stories dominate his thoughts; secondarily the question of how to get through the Wall. He is repeatedly drawn to the stories of Night's King, the Rat Cook, Mad Axe, and the thing that came in the night. The well in particular unsettles him.
- **Key decisions made:** Decides the group will go with Sam through the Black Gate ("We'll go with you" / "All of you?" / Meera: "He's our prince"). Also decides to keep his identity secret from Sam. Wargs into Hodor deliberately for the first time in a threatening situation.
- **Self-deception flags:** Actively avoiding the dream about Robb and Grey Wind: "If he never talked of it maybe he could forget he ever dreamed it, and then it wouldn't have happened." Telling himself the Nightfort stories are just stories: "Maybe it is only a ruined empty castle." Recalling Sansa's advice about hiding under blankets before catching himself.
