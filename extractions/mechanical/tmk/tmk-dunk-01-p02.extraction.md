Dunk and Egg reach the lakeshore inn at sundown, learn they have missed the day's last ferry, are refused food and lodging by the innkeep, and make camp for the night among the hedge knights in the old weirwood grove while awaiting the morning ferry.
- **time_markers:** "The sun was low in the west"; "The sun's going down"; "He'll be back first thing in the morning"; "the sun was a red memory in the west"; "We will be here until midmorning"; "As dusk deepened"; "The last dragon had perished half a century ago"; Glendon knighted "Half a year when the moon turns"; Butterwell "was once the King's Hand, as his father's father was before him"; "When the Black Dragon rose"; "Both perished on the Redgrass Field"; "his youngest died in the spring"; "Quentyn Ball died ... before I was born"; "In King Daeron's day"; "the Backfire Rebellion"; Glendon "Sixteen, might be. No more than eighteen"; Egg "a boy of eleven years"
- **chapter_summary:** Dunk and Egg reach a large lakeshore inn at sundown but have missed the day's last ferry, run by a man named Ned, and the innkeep — full up with Lord Shawney's and Lord Costayne's retinues — refuses to sell them the roast boar or give them a bed. She directs them to hedge knights camped by the stumps down the shore. In an old weirwood grove Dunk and Egg join Ser Kyle the Cat of Misty Moor, Ser Maynard Plumm, and the young Ser Glendon Ball, sharing salt beef, apples, pickled eggs, onions and sour wine. Over the fire the men discuss the coming wedding of Lord Butterwell to a Frey at Whitewalls, where a dragon's egg is the champion's prize, and trade talk of the realm's troubles — the ironmen's raids, King Aerys, the princes, and the Hand, Bloodraven. Glendon reveals himself as the son of Fireball (Quentyn Ball), who fought for the Black Dragon, and vows to claim the dragon's egg at Whitewalls.
- **unit_part:** 2 of 9

## Events & Actions
1. **Arrival at the lakeshore inn** — At sundown Dunk and Egg see the lake and the inn's turrets; Dunk dons his sweaty tunic and washes the road dust from his face to look "less the wild robber knight."
   - Agent: Dunk
   - Location: The lakeshore / inn approach
2. **Egg sees to the animals** — Dunk tells Egg to water the animals but not let them drink too much; Egg tends them at the yard's well and trough.
   - Agent: Egg
   - Patient: The horses and mule
   - Instigator: Dunk
   - Location: The inn's stable yard
3. **Dunk bargains with the innkeep** — Dunk learns the day's last ferry (run by Ned) is gone until morning, that the fares have risen (three pennies each, ten per horse and mule — "six-and-thirty"), and asks to buy boar and a place to sleep.
   - Agent: Dunk
   - Patient: Innkeep
   - Location: The inn steps
   - Outcome: Refused
4. **The innkeep refuses and shoos Dunk off** — She has no bed (full of lords' retinues) and reserves the boar and ale for her guests, sweeping her broom at Dunk and directing him to the hedge knights "down by the stumps."
   - Agent: Innkeep
   - Patient: Dunk
   - Instrument: Her broom
   - Location: The inn steps
   - Outcome: Dunk sent away; door shut "with a solid thump"
5. **Egg soaks the salt beef** — Dunk finds Egg soaking his feet in the trough, slings him the greathelm, and orders him to draw well water and soak the salt beef (not in the foot-trough).
   - Agent: Egg
   - Instrument: Dunk's greathelm; the well
   - Instigator: Dunk
   - Location: The stable yard
6. **Finding the hedge knights' camp** — Egg spies the fire in the woods along the lakeshore; they lead the animals to it and reach the old weirwood grove.
   - Agent: Egg (spots the fire); Dunk and Egg (travel)
   - Location: Woods along the lakeshore / weirwood grove
7. **Introductions at the fire** — Dunk hails the armed men openly, names himself Ser Duncan the Tall and "the lad is Egg," and asks to share their fire; Ser Kyle greets them and names Ser Glendon Ball and Ser Maynard Plumm.
   - Agent: Dunk; Ser Kyle
   - Patient: Ser Kyle, Ser Maynard, Ser Glendon (met)
   - Location: The weirwood grove
   - Outcome: Welcomed to the fire
8. **Egg conceals his identity** — Asked his odd name and his baldness, Egg withholds that "Egg" is short for Aegon and gives the shaven-for-spurs story rather than the usual "rootworms" tale.
   - Agent: Egg
   - Patient: Ser Kyle (and the camp)
   - Outcome: Identity kept hidden
9. **Unsaddling and provisioning the animals** — Dunk and Egg unsaddle, feed, water, and hobble Thunder, Rain, and Maester for the night.
   - Agent: Dunk and Egg
   - Patient: Thunder, Rain, Maester
   - Location: The grove
10. **The shared meal and fireside talk** — The men pool salt beef, apples, pickled eggs, onions, and wine and discuss the Whitewalls wedding, the dragon's-egg prize, the ironmen's raids, and the realm's rulers.
    - Agent: Dunk, Egg, Ser Kyle, Ser Maynard, Ser Glendon
    - Location: The grove camp
11. **Egg speaks out about Prince Maekar; Dunk silences him** — When Kyle says Rhaegel's twins may die "of Maekar's mace," Egg protests loudly that Maekar loves his brother; Dunk growls him quiet and sets him to passing out the beef.
    - Agent: Egg (speaks); Dunk (silences)
    - Patient: Egg
    - Instigator: Dunk
    - Location: The grove camp
    - Outcome: Egg falls sullenly silent
12. **Ser Kyle names Blackfyre men "traitors"; Glendon takes offense** — Kyle calls the Blackfyre men traitors while telling of the Backfire Rebellion; Glendon, blade in hand and pock-marks reddening, declares his father fought for the Black Dragon and that Daemon was no traitor.
    - Agent: Ser Kyle (gives offense); Ser Glendon (reacts)
    - Patient: Ser Glendon
    - Instrument: Ser Glendon's drawn sword
    - Witness: Dunk
    - Location: The grove camp
    - Outcome: Tension; Dunk defuses it
13. **Glendon reveals himself as Fireball's son** — Glendon turns his shield to show the fireball sigil, says "I come from hero's blood"; Egg names him "Fireball's son," and Glendon confirms he is Quentyn Ball's son, vowing to claim the dragon's egg at Whitewalls.
    - Agent: Ser Glendon (reveals); Egg (names him)
    - Instrument: Glendon's fireball shield
    - Witness: Dunk, Ser Kyle, Ser Maynard
    - Location: The grove camp
    - Outcome: Glendon's parentage revealed; he smiles for the first time
