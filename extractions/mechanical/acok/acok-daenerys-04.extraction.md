# ACOK — Daenerys IV

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** ACOK
- **chapter_number:** 49
- **pov_character:** Daenerys Targaryen
- **pov_chapter_number:** Daenerys IV
- **first_available:** ACOK Daenerys IV
- **location_primary:** House of the Undying Ones (Palace of Dust), Qarth
- **location_secondary:** Grove of black-barked trees outside the House; various chambers and halls within; the audience chamber of the Undying
- **approximate_timeline:** During Daenerys's stay in Qarth; she has already met Xaro, Pyat Pree, and the other Qartheen factions
- **time_markers:** "morning sun" (Jhogo references how the building "drinks the morning sun"); Dany states she has walked "for hours" inside the House; she spills out "into the sun" upon exiting
- **chapter_summary:** Daenerys arrives at the House of the Undying Ones, a grey ruin in a grove of black-barked trees. Despite warnings from Jorah, Xaro, and her bloodriders, she enters alone after drinking shade of the evening. Following Pyat Pree's instructions to always take the door to the right and always climb, she navigates a labyrinth of identical chambers and a long torchlit hall, passing disturbing visions. She reaches the audience chamber of the Undying, where blue-skinned corpse-like figures sit around a floating corrupted heart and deliver cryptic prophecies. The Undying attack her physically, but Drogon destroys their heart with dragonfire and Dany escapes the burning building. Outside, Pyat Pree attacks her with a knife but is disarmed by Jhogo's whip and tackled by Rakharo.

## Events & Actions
1. **Arrival at the House of the Undying** — Daenerys emerges from her palanquin to see a grey ancient ruin coiling through a grove of black-barked trees. Drogon hisses with smoke seeping from his teeth.
2. **Companions warn Daenerys** — Jhogo calls it an evil place of ghosts and maegi. Jorah questions the warlocks' power. Xaro dismisses the warlocks and offers his pleasure barge. Aggo offers to walk with her.
3. **Pyat Pree appears** — The warlock steps out from under the trees and insists Daenerys must enter alone or not at all.
4. **Jorah warns about Mirri Maz Duur** — Jorah tells Dany to remember Mirri Maz Duur; Dany decides this motivates her to enter, since even a maegi had knowledge.
5. **Pyat Pree gives navigation instructions** — Always take the first door to the right; always climb, never go down; do not enter any room until reaching the audience chamber; ignore dwellers and servitors.
6. **Dany drinks shade of the evening** — At the oval-mouth door, a tiny dwarf servitor offers shade of the evening in a crystal glass. Dany drinks; it transforms from foul to transcendent in taste.
7. **Dany enters and navigates antechambers** — She passes through identical stone rooms, always taking the right door, then enters an oval worm-eaten wood room with six passages.
8. **Drogon attempts flight in the long hall** — Drogon flies twenty feet before crashing undignifiedly in the long torchlit hall.
9. **Dany hears disturbing sounds** — Scurrying in walls, thumping behind a closed door, dissonant piping that makes Drogon lash his tail.
10. **Vision: Woman and four dwarf-creatures** — A beautiful woman naked on the floor while four rattish creatures with pink hands savage her.
11. **Vision: Feast of corpses** — Slaughtered feasters at overturned tables; a dead man with a wolf's head wears an iron crown and holds a leg of lamb, his eyes following Dany.
12. **Vision: House with the red door** — Dany recognizes the house in Braavos with its wooden beams, carved animal faces, and lemon tree. Ser Willem Darry appears and beckons her. She nearly goes to him but remembers he is dead and flees.
13. **Vision: Dragon skull hall and the "king of ashes"** — An old man with dark eyes and silver-grey hair sits on a towering barbed throne in a hall of dragon skulls, saying "Let him be king over charred bones and cooked meat. Let him be the king of ashes."
14. **Vision: Man names baby Aegon** — A man taller than Viserys with dark indigo eyes tells a nursing woman the baby is named Aegon, calls him "the prince that was promised," says "his is the song of ice and fire" and "the dragon has three heads." He looks at Dany, says "there must be one more," then plays a harp as the vision fades.
15. **Torches going out and pursuit** — The torches behind Dany are extinguishing; she hears something shuffling toward her. The stairway ahead only goes down. She realizes "the first door on the right is the last door on the left" and flings herself through.
16. **False exit with illusory Pyat Pree** — After more right-turning rooms, Dany finds Pyat Pree apparently outside, who urges her to come to him. His face crumbles into something pale and wormlike when she refuses and takes the right-hand door instead.
17. **Stairwell ascent** — Dany climbs a stairwell despite the House having appeared to have no towers from outside.
18. **Illusory hall of wizards** — Beyond ebony-and-weirwood doors, richly dressed wizards in a sunlit hall invite Dany to "share the food of forever" and claim to be the Undying of Qarth. Drogon flies to the door and bites the wood; Dany finds a hidden plain grey door behind the grand doors.
19. **Audience chamber of the Undying** — Dany enters a gloomy chamber with a long stone table, a floating blue corrupted heart beating with indigo light, and motionless blue shadow-figures who do not breathe.
20. **The Undying deliver prophecies** — Whispered prophecies: "child of three," "three heads has the dragon," "three fires must you light," "three mounts must you ride," "three treasons will you know," each with "one for life/bed/blood, one for death/dread/gold, one to love."
21. **Rapid visions** — A sequence of indigo phantoms: Viserys dying in gold; a tall lord with copper skin under a fiery stallion banner; a dying prince with rubies in water; a blue-eyed king with a red sword and no shadow; a cloth dragon on poles; a stone beast from a smoking tower; Dany's silver riding to a stream under stars; a corpse at a ship's prow; a blue flower in a wall of ice. Then faster visions: shadows in a tent; a girl running to the red door; Mirri Maz Duur burning with a dragon from her brow; a naked man dragged behind a silver horse; a white lion; naked crones under the Mother of Mountains; ten thousand slaves crying "Mother!"
22. **The Undying attack Dany** — The visions dissolve; the Undying surround and grab Dany, pulling, stroking, biting. Her heart stops beating. A mouth descends on her eye.
23. **Drogon destroys the heart and burns the Undying** — Drogon rips the floating heart apart and breathes fire. The Undying burn — their flesh is crumbling parchment, their bones dry wood soaked in tallow. The room is ablaze.
24. **Dany escapes** — She pushes through the burning husks and runs through a serpentine passageway. The floor writhes underfoot. She finds a door shaped like an open mouth and spills into the sunlight.
25. **Pyat Pree attacks** — Outside, Pyat Pree gibbers in an unknown tongue, then draws a knife and lunges at Dany. Drogon flies at his face; Jhogo's whip disarms him; Rakharo slams him to the ground.
26. **Jorah comforts Dany** — Ser Jorah kneels beside her in the grass and puts his arm around her shoulder. The Palace of Dust smokes behind them.
