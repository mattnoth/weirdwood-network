# ACOK — Arya IV

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** ACOK
- **chapter_number:** 15
- **pov_character:** Arya Stark (disguised as "Arry")
- **pov_chapter_number:** Arya IV
- **first_available:** ACOK Arya IV
- **location_primary:** An unnamed town on the southern shore of the Gods Eye lake
- **location_secondary:** A river south of the Gods Eye; the holdfast; the Gods Eye lakeshore; the burning barn and escape tunnel
- **approximate_timeline:** Shortly after the previous Arya chapters; the group has been traveling north along the river toward the Gods Eye
- **time_markers:** "morning sun" at chapter opening; "midday" when scouting parties return; "an hour shy of evenfall" when first house spotted; "setting sun" at the lakefront; town "as dark as any forest" when Yoren returns from the towerhouse; night during the battle; "a long time ago" (Arya's reference to Harrenhal's burning); travel time "should be there before dark" (Yoren's estimate from their river position to the town)
- **chapter_summary:** Yoren's group, unable to cross a river south of the Gods Eye, follows it north to a deserted town with a stone holdfast. The townfolk, their lord, and all boats are gone. The group shelters in the holdfast for the night. After dark, Arya is awakened by howling and then Kurz's hunting horn. Ser Amory Lorch arrives with Lannister forces, burns the town, and demands entry. Yoren refuses, and the Lannisters storm the walls. After a fierce battle in which Woth, Qyle, Dobber, and others are killed, Yoren orders the survivors to escape through a tunnel under the barn. Arya retrieves an axe and throws it to the chained prisoners—Jaqen H'ghar, Rorge, and Biter—before crawling through the tunnel as the barn collapses behind her.

## Events & Actions
1. **Dead soldier found in river** — Koss spots a body caught in the reeds: a soldier in a sodden green cloak, face nibbled by fish. Dobber finds a leather purse with four coppers and blond hair tied with a red ribbon.
2. **River crossing fails** — Yoren tries to ride across but the river is too deep. He sends Koss upriver and Woth/Gerren downstream to find crossings.
3. **Burned bridge reported** — Woth reports a wooden bridge half a mile downstream has been burned. Smoke visible to the north and west.
4. **Yoren draws his plan** — Using a stick in the mud, Yoren maps the Gods Eye and river. He proposes following the river north to a town with a stone holdfast, selling goods, hiring a boat to cross the lake to Harrentown, then resupply or shelter at Harrenhal.
5. **Travel north along the river** — The group passes abandoned dwellings: a cottage (they take vegetables from its garden), a forester's cabin, a stilt-house. Fields of wheat, corn, and barley lie unharvested with no workers in sight.
6. **Arrival at the deserted town** — They find white houses, a sept, the lord's towerhouse, and a holdfast, but no people anywhere.
7. **Search for boats fails** — Arya, Gendry, Hot Pie, Lommy, and Woth search the lakefront. They find only a rotted-out rowboat abandoned on rocks. All other boats are gone.
8. **Arya at the lake** — Arya washes in the Gods Eye. The water is warm, green, unsalted, and tastes of "summer and mud and growing things." She wishes she could swim to Winterfell.
9. **Yoren reports towerhouse empty** — The lord is gone, along with all horses and pigs. Yoren notes a goose and chickens running loose, and fish in the lake.
10. **Gendry proposes building a raft** — Yoren considers it; the lake is too deep to pole across but shallows near shore might work. He decides to sleep on it.
11. **Group shelters in the holdfast** — Gates barred with iron X-brace. Yoren posts three watches, sends Tarber, Kurz, and Cutjack to the towerhouse as lookouts with the hunting horn. Gerren discovers the escape tunnel under the barn; Yoren has a wagon rolled over the trap.
12. **Supper prepared and eaten** — Gendry, Dobber, and Arya cook goose and chicken. Arya eats a chicken leg and onion. The crying girl eats goose offered by Hot Pie. Little conversation.
13. **Arya sharpens Needle and talks to Hot Pie** — Hot Pie asks about her sword. Arya says her brother gave it to her and claims to have many big brothers with longswords.
14. **Arya hears howling** — She dreams a wolf is howling and wakes. She tries to warn others but is mocked. Moments later, Kurz sounds the hunting horn.
15. **Lannister forces arrive and burn the town** — Riders with torches gallop through, setting roofs ablaze. Arya estimates a hundred or two hundred.
16. **Parley at the gates** — The knight in the spiked helm demands entry "in the name of the king." Ser Amory Lorch identifies himself as bannerman to Lord Tywin Lannister, Hand of the King. He accuses them of being Lord Beric's men. Yoren displays his black cloak and asserts Night's Watch neutrality.
17. **Woth killed** — Ser Amory raises his fist; a spear strikes Woth through the throat. He falls from the wall.
18. **Battle at the holdfast** — Ser Amory orders "Storm the walls and kill them all." Arya fights on the walls: she slashes a man's fingers with Needle, drives another back, and kills a skinny bald man with Gendry and Hot Pie. She screams "Winterfell!" as her battle cry; Hot Pie screams "Hot Pie!"
19. **Defenders fall** — Koss is killed after yielding. Qyle is killed by the knight with the wasp shield (spiked mace to the face). Dobber is stabbed in the belly. Yoren kills the knight in the spiked helm. The enemy breaches the walls and/or postern.
20. **Yoren orders retreat** — He tells Arya to herd the boys and escape through the tunnel under the barn.
21. **Escape through the barn** — Arya, Gendry, Hot Pie, and wounded Lommy head for the barn. Arya grabs the crying girl. The barn is on fire from a fallen torch.
22. **Arya frees the prisoners** — Despite the fire, Arya runs back to retrieve the axe from the woodpile. She fights off a mailed attacker (drives the axe between his legs). She re-enters the burning barn and throws the axe into the wagon. Rorge catches it and smashes the wagon apart, freeing himself, Jaqen, and Biter.
23. **Arya escapes through the tunnel** — She drops into the tunnel. The barn collapses behind her in a blast of smoke and heat. She crawls through the darkness, kisses the mud, and cries.

- **Primary preoccupation:** Survival; longing for home and her former identity; awareness of the gap between "Arya Stark the Hand's daughter" and "Arry the orphan boy"
- **Key decisions made:** (1) Warns the group about the wolf howling despite being mocked; (2) Fights fiercely on the walls rather than hiding; (3) Drags the crying girl to safety despite resistance; (4) Goes back for the axe and re-enters the burning barn to free Jaqen, Rorge, and Biter; (5) Chooses to enter the barn a second time despite remembering the Hound's burns
- **Self-deception flags:** Arya considers that Lady Whent's knights would protect her if she revealed her identity — "That was what knights did; they kept you safe, especially women" — a belief the chapter itself undermines when Ser Amory's knights attack without regard for age or innocence
