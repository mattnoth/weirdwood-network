- **chapter_summary:** Arya Stark, disguised as the orphan boy "Arry" with her head shorn, travels north on the kingsroad with Yoren's party of Night's Watch recruits. Lommy Greenhands and Hot Pie bully her about Needle until Hot Pie tries to take it; Arya beats him badly with her practice sword. Yoren punishes her with three strokes of the stick and reveals that Eddard Stark was supposed to take the black but "something went queer." Arya walks for three days too sore to ride, sees the red comet at night and imagines it as her father's sword Ice stained with his blood, then dreams of home and Jon Snow.

## Events & Actions
1. **Arya recalls her disguise** — Yoren cut her hair in an alley in King's Landing, told her she would be "Arry the orphan boy," and warned her about the dangerous company.
2. **The party departs King's Landing** — Five wagons laden with supplies pass through the gates; Lannister guardsmen are stopping everyone but Yoren calls one by name and the party is waved through.
3. **Arya looks back at King's Landing mentally** — She wishes the Rush would rise and wash the city away, but remembers Sansa is still there and wishes for Winterfell instead.
4. **Lommy and Hot Pie bully Arya** — They mock her sword, call her "Lumpyhead," and speculate it's a toy or stolen.
5. **Arya snaps back** — She tells them Needle is castle-forged steel and to shut their mouths.
6. **The Bull intervenes** — He tells them to leave Arya alone; Lommy doesn't dare mock him.
7. **Hot Pie tries to take Needle** — He rides closer and reaches for the hilt.
8. **Arya fights Hot Pie** — She hits his donkey with her practice sword, dumps him, then beats him repeatedly — breaking his nose, hitting him across the face, knees, and back of the head.
9. **The Bull warns Arya** — He shouts "Behind you" when Hot Pie picks up a rock; Arya dodges it.
10. **Arya uses water dancer's stance** — She slides into the stance Syrio taught her and lunges between Hot Pie's legs.
11. **Yoren breaks up the fight** — He pulls Arya off Hot Pie, confiscates her stick, and threatens to tie the boys behind the wagons.
12. **Yoren punishes Arya** — He drags her into the trees, makes her drop her breeches, and gives her three strokes with the stick; she bleeds from the left leg.
13. **Yoren reveals Eddard's intended fate** — He tells Arya that Lord Eddard was supposed to take the black and Yoren was waiting for him, but "something went queer."
14. **Arya names Joffrey** — She says "Joffrey" and that someone should kill him; Yoren agrees someone will but not either of them.
15. **Aftermath** — Arya walks for three days, too sore to ride; Hot Pie lies in a wagon on barley sacks, whimpering; Lommy avoids Arya, twitching whenever she looks at him.
16. **Arya sees the red comet** — Lying on the ground at night, she sees the great red comet; the Bull calls it "The Red Sword"; Arya imagines it as Ice with her father's blood on the blade.
17. **Arya dreams of home** — She falls asleep and dreams of Winterfell, her mother, Robb, Bran, Rickon, and especially Jon Snow; she imagines them saying "I missed you" at the same moment.

- **Primary preoccupation:** Survival and identity concealment; grief over her father's death; longing for home and family, especially Jon Snow
- **Key decisions made:** Decides not to go crying to Yoren for help; offers Hot Pie the practice sword to avoid a fight (then fights anyway when he reaches for Needle); decides it is safer not to talk to anyone after the incident
- **Self-deception flags:** None clearly present; Arya is remarkably clear-eyed about her situation and dangers
