- **chapter_summary:** Arya serves as Roose Bolton's cupbearer "Nan" at Harrenhal. She overhears the Frey lords argue that the war is lost and Robb must surrender, and learns that Winterfell has fallen and Bran and Rickon are reportedly dead. Bolton orders Ser Helman Tallhart to burn the castle of the Darrys and march on Duskendale. Bolton announces he will give Harrenhal to Vargo Hoat and leave Arya behind. That night, Arya steals a map and dagger from Bolton's solar, rouses Gendry and Hot Pie, obtains horses by impersonating Bolton's authority, kills the postern guard with Jaqen's coin as a lure, and escapes Harrenhal with her companions.

## Events & Actions
1. **Arya draws water at the well** — passes beneath tarred heads on the gatehouse every morning; encounters Goodwife Amabel who threatens her
2. **Amabel confronts Arya** — warns that when Tywin returns, Arya will be punished; Arya swings the water bucket at her and threatens to kill her
3. **Recall: Gendry confronts Arya about the heads** — blames her for the weasel soup that brought Bolton's regime; argues the Brave Companions are worse than Ser Amory
4. **Arya helps Elmar Frey with chainmail barrel** — they push the sand barrel; Elmar boasts about marrying a princess; Arya taunts him about leeches
5. **War council in Bolton's bedchamber** — Bolton is being leeched; Frey lords (Aenys, Jared, Hosteen, Harys Haigh, Ronel Rivers, and others) argue the war is lost and Robb must surrender
6. **Arya learns Winterfell has fallen and Bran/Rickon are reported dead** — Hosteen Frey states it; Arya is devastated but remains silent and still
7. **Bolton dismisses the Freys** — says he will "think on all you've said" with a smile
8. **Arya removes Bolton's leeches** — plucks them from his body after the Freys leave
9. **Qyburn reads Lady Walda's letter** — repetitive content about prayers and trueborn sons to replace Domeric
10. **Bolton dictates orders to Qyburn** — instructs Ser Helman Tallhart to burn castle of the Darrys, execute captives, and march with Robett Glover toward Duskendale
11. **Bolton announces he will hunt wolves** — references direwolf packs and bold southern wolves; Qyburn notes wolves attacked Septon Utt's camp three days ago
12. **Arya cleans Bolton's chambers** — sweeps rushes, changes linens, empties chamber pots, tends the solar; discovers the sheepskin map
13. **Arya discovers the map of the Trident** — notes Harrenhal's position and that Riverrun is "not so far"
14. **Arya practices swordplay in the godswood** — recites her kill list while slashing at leaves and trees; ends by saluting the heart tree with "Valar morghulis"
15. **Bolton's hunting party returns with nine dead wolves** — seven adults, two pups; orders skins made into blanket and gloves; references "winter is coming"
16. **Arya serves Bolton's supper** — Hot Pie makes spice wine; Bolton reads and burns a book; dismisses Arya
17. **Bolton tells Arya she will remain with Vargo Hoat** — refuses her request to leave with him; threatens to cut out her tongue
18. **Arya decides to escape** — goes to the godswood after dark; passes the Wailing Tower where Freys argue; finds Elmar weeping about his broken betrothal
19. **Elmar reveals his betrothal is broken** — bird from the Twins; his father says he must marry someone else or be a septon; Arya says "I hope your princess dies"
20. **Arya prays to the heart tree** — hears a wolf howl and then her father's voice: "the lone wolf dies, but the pack survives"
21. **Arya breaks the broomstick sword** — declares "I am a direwolf, and done with wooden teeth"
22. **Arya wakes Gendry at the forge** — asks him for a sword; tells him Bolton is giving Harrenhal to Vargo Hoat; lies that Hoat will cut off servants' feet
23. **Arya prepares for escape** — dresses in Bolton livery; steals the map and dagger from Bolton's solar
24. **Arya obtains three horses** — wakes a groom, uses Bolton's authority (flayed man sigil) to get horses saddled
25. **Arya leads horses to the Tower of Ghosts** — stays in curtain wall shadows; passes a grey and white cat on the godswood wall
26. **Gendry and Hot Pie arrive at the Tower of Ghosts** — Gendry in chainmail with hammer and swords; Hot Pie with bread and cheese
27. **Arya kills the postern guard** — lures him with the iron coin pretending it's silver; slits his throat when he kneels to pick it up; whispers "Valar morghulis"
28. **Escape from Harrenhal** — Arya opens the postern gate; the three ride out into the rain

- **Primary preoccupation:** The news about Winterfell and her brothers; whether to escape and how; identity crisis ("Am I still Arya, or only Nan the serving girl, for forever and forever and forever?")

- **Self-deception flags:** Tells herself "It's not true, it can't be true, it's just some Lannister lie" about Bran and Rickon; lies to Gendry about Vargo Hoat cutting off feet (deliberate manipulation, not self-deception)
