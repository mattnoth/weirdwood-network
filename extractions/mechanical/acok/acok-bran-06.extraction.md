# ACOK — Bran VI

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** ACOK
- **chapter_number:** 47
- **pov_character:** Bran Stark
- **pov_chapter_number:** Bran VI
- **first_available:** ACOK Bran VI
- **location_primary:** Winterfell
- **location_secondary:** Torrhen's Square (mentioned), Deepwood Motte (mentioned), Moat Cailin (mentioned), White Harbor (mentioned), the barrowlands (mentioned), the wolfswood (mentioned), Greywater Watch (mentioned), Iron Islands (mentioned), the Dreadfort (mentioned), Hornwood (mentioned), the Saltspear (mentioned)
- **approximate_timeline:** Eight days after Ser Rodrik departed Winterfell with the garrison; night into early morning
- **time_markers:** Night; evening rain had stopped but starts again later; "eight days past" since the garrison left; pre-dawn (Gage and Osha making morning bread when captured); four-year-old Rickon woken from sleep
- **chapter_summary:** Bran wargs into Summer and senses intruders climbing Winterfell's walls at night. He wakes to find Theon Greyjoy has captured Winterfell with a small force that swam the moat and scaled the walls with grappling hooks. Maester Luwin reports that Alebelly was killed and Hayhead wounded, and that one of two ravens he sent escaped. Bran is brought to the Great Hall where Theon, seated in the Stark high seat, announces that Balon Greyjoy has crowned himself King of the Iron Islands and claims the north. When the blacksmith Mikken defies Theon, an ironman kills him with a spear thrust through the neck. The prisoner Reek and the wildling Osha both swear fealty to Theon, while Hodor is beaten by ironmen.

## Events & Actions
1. **Summer senses intruders** — Bran, warging into Summer, hears faint sounds of steel on stone (grappling claws) and smells an unknown man-scent signaling danger
2. **Direwolves investigate the gates** — Summer and Shaggydog run to Winterfell's gates but find them locked and chained; they cannot get through or dig under
3. **Summer receives mental guidance** — A wordless whisper directs Summer toward the sentinel tree on the far side of the godswood as a way over the walls
4. **A muffled shout is heard** — A shout cut short sounds through the night (likely a guard being killed)
5. **Summer attempts to climb the sentinel tree** — In a burst of desperate fury, Summer runs at the leaning tree and scrambles partway up but slips on the wet bark and falls
6. **Bran wakes in his tower room** — He feels the ghost of Summer's fall in his shoulder; he hears dogs barking; he shouts for Hodor and others
7. **An unknown ironman enters Bran's room** — Werlag, armed with dirk and axe, enters; Bran demands he leave
8. **Theon reveals he has taken Winterfell** — Theon follows Werlag in and tells Bran he sent four men over the walls with grappling claws who opened a postern gate for the rest
9. **Theon demands Bran yield the castle** — He tells Bran to publicly announce his surrender and command the household to obey; Bran initially refuses
10. **Maester Luwin comes to Bran** — Wounded above his left eye; reports the attackers swam the moat, climbed the walls, killed Alebelly in the turret, and wounded Hayhead; he managed to send two ravens but one was shot down
11. **Bran is dressed in lordly attire** — Maester Luwin dresses Bran with cloak and wolf's-head clasp, saying he must look princely as the Stark in Winterfell
12. **Castle folk are gathered in the Great Hall** — Ironmen herd everyone in: the Reeds, the Freys, Old Nan, Hayhead (carried), Poxy Tym, Beth Cassel, Gage, Osha, Mikken (cursing), Farlen and Palla (her dress torn), Septon Chayle (knocked down), and finally Reek
13. **Reek is brought from a tower cell** — A beardless ginger-haired youth presents the prisoner; Reek identifies himself as Heke, formerly in service to the Bastard of the Dreadfort
14. **Bran yields Winterfell from the high seat** — Theon sits in the Stark high seat and has Lorren place Bran there; Bran publicly yields and tells the household to obey Theon
15. **Mikken defies Theon** — Mikken shouts insults; Stygg strikes him repeatedly with his spear butt, knocking out a tooth and bloodying him
16. **Theon announces Balon Greyjoy is King** — Declares his father has taken the crown of salt and rock; claims the north by right of conquest; threatens consequences for betrayal
17. **Mikken is killed** — After continued defiance, Stygg drives his spear point through the back of Mikken's neck and out his throat
18. **Reek swears fealty to Theon** — Given a sword, lays it at Theon's feet and swears obedience to House Greyjoy and King Balon
19. **Osha swears fealty to Theon** — Steps past Mikken's body; demands a spear; knees Stygg in the groin, takes his spear, and knocks him down; Theon accepts her and tells Stygg to find another spear
20. **Hodor is beaten** — Ironmen beat Hodor with spear butts when he won't stop shouting; he falls trying to shield himself
21. **Assembly dismissed** — The household is warned to work and make no trouble
22. **Hodor carries Bran back** — Hodor, face beaten and bloodied, carries Bran back out into the rain
