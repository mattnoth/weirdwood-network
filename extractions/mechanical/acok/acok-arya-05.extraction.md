- **chapter_summary:** Arya scouts a fishing village on Gods Eye from a treetop. In flashback, the chapter recounts how the survivors found Yoren dead, sheltered in a towerhouse, lost Kurz to infection, and were abandoned by Tarber and Cutjack. Arya reveals her true identity to Gendry after he deduces she's a girl. They scout the village at dusk and discover it held by Lannister forces under the Mountain, with corpses on a gibbet. Gendry is captured. Arya returns with Hot Pie to attempt a rescue but both are caught. The Mountain's men find Lommy and kill him when he cannot walk; Weasel escapes into the woods.

## Events & Actions
1. **Arya scouts from treetop** — Arya climbs to the highest branch of a tree and observes a fishing village on Gods Eye's shore, counting twenty-six thatched roofs and one slate roof, noting chimney smoke and a wagon.
2. **Flashback: discovery of the holdfast aftermath** — The group returned to the ruined holdfast the night after the attack and found gates broken, walls demolished, and all defenders dead including Yoren, whose skull was split by an axe.
3. **Flashback: Yoren's burial** — Arya insisted on burying Yoren despite too many dead to bury all.
4. **Flashback: towerhouse survivors** — Gendry called up to the towerhouse where Cutjack, Kurz, and Tarber had held out; they joined the group and decided to press north.
5. **Flashback: Kurz's death** — Kurz died of an infected arrow wound (taken pulling in the towerhouse ladder); flesh of throat darkened, red welts crept up jaw and down chest.
6. **Flashback: Tarber and Cutjack abandon the group** — They took Kurz's weapons and gear and left; the group waited but they never returned.
7. **Group discussion about the village** — Hot Pie wants food; Lommy wants to yield; Gendry decides he and Arya will scout at dusk.
8. **Gendry confronts Arya about her sex** — Gendry reveals he knows Arya is a girl, challenging her to prove otherwise.
9. **Arya reveals her identity** — Arya tells Gendry her real name is Arya Stark, daughter of the Hand of the King; Gendry is shocked and begins calling her "m'lady."
10. **Arya and Gendry approach the village** — They split up; Arya takes the shore route, Gendry circles west looking for a road.
11. **Arya discovers the gibbet** — She crawls through brambles and finds corpses hanging from a gibbet of raw green wood, savaged by crows and something larger.
12. **Arya observes Lannister occupation** — She sees guards in mail with spears, Lannister and Clegane banners, and hears screams from the storehouse.
13. **Gendry captured** — Gendry is shoved into view by a Lannister soldier; they take his bull's-head helm, strike him, and lock him in the storehouse with other prisoners.
14. **Arya observes the camp** — She watches the guard change, a hunting party return with a deer, cooking, and the guard routine at the storehouse.
15. **Arya returns and plans rescue** — She goes back to Lommy and Hot Pie; convinces Hot Pie to help her attempt a rescue.
16. **Arya and Hot Pie crawl toward the storehouse** — They wriggle under brambles, crawl past the gibbet in darkness.
17. **Hot Pie panics and yields** — A crow lands on Hot Pie's back; he jumps up and yields loudly, throwing away his sword.
18. **Arya captured** — She draws Needle and fights but is overpowered; multiple men grab her; one punches her with an iron-clad fist.
19. **Brought before the Mountain** — Arya and Hot Pie are brought before Gregor Clegane; Arya recognizes the three black dogs on his surcoat.
20. **Led to find the others** — The Mountain orders them to lead soldiers to the rest of the group; four armed men escort them.
21. **Lommy yields and is killed** — Lommy yields and raises his hands; when asked if he can walk and says no, a spearman drives his spear through Lommy's throat.
22. **Weasel escapes** — Lommy tells the soldiers the girl ran off when she heard them; Arya silently urges Weasel to run and hide.
