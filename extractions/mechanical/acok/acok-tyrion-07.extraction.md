- **approximate_timeline:** Well into the War of the Five Kings; Cersei has recently recovered from an illness; Pycelle is imprisoned in the black cells; food and wine shortages in King's Landing
- **time_markers:** "well past midnight" at chapter opening; Tyrion works "well into the morning" habitually; the entire chapter takes place during a single night; moonlight visible during the ride through the city
- **chapter_summary:** Lancel Lannister arrives past midnight bearing Cersei's warrant demanding Grand Maester Pycelle's release and Ser Jacelyn Bywater's arrest for treason. Tyrion confronts Lancel with knowledge of his sexual relationship with Cersei and his role in King Robert's death, turning the terrified young knight into his spy inside Cersei's inner circle. Tyrion agrees to release Pycelle but refuses to restore him to the council. After Lancel departs, Tyrion rides with Bronn through the city streets to Chataya's brothel, uses the secret passage through the turret room wardrobe, and arrives at the manse where Shae is kept under guard.

## Events & Actions
1. **Podrick Payne wakes Tyrion past midnight** — Lancel Lannister has come to visit; Tyrion tells Pod to show Lancel to the solar
2. **Tyrion deliberately delays** — Splashes water on his face, uses the garderobe, musses his hair to appear sleepy; intentionally making Lancel wait and grow anxious
3. **Lancel delivers Cersei's demands** — Presents a crimson-ribboned warrant demanding Grand Maester Pycelle's release; Tyrion waves it away
4. **Tyrion hints at Cersei's illness** — Suggests it would be a pity if Cersei suffered a relapse, revealing he was behind her illness and had hoped for more days without her interference
5. **Lancel reports Bywater's defiance** — Cersei has already tried to order Ser Jacelyn Bywater to release Pycelle and been rebuffed; she demands Bywater be arrested for treason
6. **Tyrion confronts Lancel about Cersei** — Asks if Cersei knighted him before or after taking him to bed; reads the confirmation in Lancel's eyes; credits Varys's intelligence
7. **Tyrion threatens Lancel with exposure to Joffrey** — Raises the specter of telling Joffrey that Lancel murdered his father to bed his mother
8. **Lancel confesses and begs for mercy** — Falls to his knees; admits Cersei gave him the strongwine to kill Robert; says Tywin told him to obey Cersei in everything
9. **Tyrion turns Lancel into his spy** — Orders Lancel to continue the affair, stay close to Cersei, and report everything she does; promises Lancel could earn a lordship
10. **Tyrion agrees to release Pycelle** — Will free him the next day but refuses to let him back on the council; says Cersei can keep him as a pet or send him to the Wall
11. **Tyrion instructs Lancel on contraception** — Warns Lancel that the queen must not become pregnant; Lancel reveals he practices withdrawal
12. **Tyrion reflects on Lancel's probable fate** — Believes Lancel unlikely to survive the year: Cersei will kill him if she discovers his betrayal, or Jaime will kill him upon returning to King's Landing
13. **Tyrion wakes Podrick and summons Bronn** — Orders horses saddled; they ride out through a postern gate in the north wall
14. **Tyrion and Bronn ride through the city at night** — Down Shadowblack Lane, past Aegon's High Hill, through Pigrun Alley; encounter only an old crone carrying a dead cat
15. **Tyrion reflects on previous Hands** — Considers how Ned Stark and Jon Arryn were too honest to counter Cersei and both died for it
16. **Arrival at Chataya's brothel** — Despite the late hour, the brothel is crowded; Bronn goes upstairs with a Dornish girl
17. **Dancy tries to entice Tyrion** — Sits in his lap, nibbles his ear, gropes him; Tyrion declines, saying he is fond of Alayaya's remedy
18. **Alayaya leads Tyrion to the turret room** — Tells him about Dancy's wager with Marei over black pearls; mentions Marei is teaching her to read
19. **Tyrion uses the secret passage** — Opens the wardrobe door, descends through a shaft and tunnel to emerge at a stable
20. **Tyrion hears music while riding to the manse** — Remembers Tysha singing to him; reflects that she was "a lie start to finish, only a whore his brother Jaime had hired"
21. **Tyrion arrives at the manse** — Admitted by the Braavosi daggerman; describes the deliberately ugly guards Varys assembled
22. **Tyrion has sex with Shae** — Finds her asleep, nude; they have intercourse; he reflects on his love for power, the city, and Shae
