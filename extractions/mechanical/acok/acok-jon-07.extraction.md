- **chapter_summary:** Jon and the small ranging party (Qhorin Halfhand, Squire Dalbridge, Stonesnake, Ebben) rest at the summit of the Skirling Pass. Jon confesses to Qhorin that he spared Ygritte; Qhorin reveals it was a test of character. Jon has a wolf dream in which he sees through Ghost's eyes: he perceives the wildling host gathered in the Milkwater valley with giants and mammoths, a weirwood with his brother's three-eyed face speaks to him, and an eagle attacks Ghost. Jon wakes and reports the dream; the rangers find Ghost wounded. Qhorin decides to retreat, but an eagle shadows them. At the narrowest point of the pass, Squire Dalbridge volunteers to stay behind as a rearguard. At dawn a hunting horn sounds — the wildlings are pursuing.

## Events & Actions
1. **Rangers ride through the Skirling Pass** — The party of five men and Ghost travel upward through the narrow, dark, steep pass with Squire Dalbridge taking the lead scanning heights.
2. **Rest at the summit** — Qhorin orders rest at a wind-carved stone arch marking the high point, waiting for shadows to return ("shadows are friends to men in black").
3. **Jon confesses to Qhorin about sparing Ygritte** — Jon tells Qhorin he let Ygritte go; Qhorin reveals he already knew (Stonesnake told him about the Bael the Bard story) and that it was a test of Jon's character.
4. **Qhorin discusses Mance Rayder** — Tells Jon that Mance was the best and worst ranger, wildling-born, loved the wild more than the Wall; says only fools despise the wildlings.
5. **Jon attempts to sleep; Ghost departs** — Ghost refuses to come to Jon and pads away to hunt; Jon sleeps beneath an overhang of rock.
6. **Jon has a wolf dream** — Through Ghost's eyes, he sees five direwolves scattered (should be six); he feels their loss. A weirwood with his brother's three-eyed face speaks to him silently ("Don't be afraid, I like it in the dark... first you have to open your eyes").
7. **Ghost/Jon sees the wildling host** — From a precipice, Ghost observes thousands of wildlings in the Milkwater valley: men digging, training for war, riders charging a shield wall, crude tents, earthen shelters, cookfires; also sees a mammoth with a giant riding it.
8. **Eagle attacks Ghost** — A bird plummets from the sky and attacks Ghost with talons; Jon wakes shouting.
9. **Jon reports the dream to the rangers** — Qhorin takes the wolf dream seriously; Stonesnake confirms the glacier/Milkwater geography; discussion of skinchangers and wargs.
10. **Rangers resume descent; eagle spotted** — They ride down the pass; at twilight they see an eagle perched on a rock spine watching them. Ebben wants to shoot it but Dalbridge says it's out of bowshot.
11. **Ghost found wounded** — Jon spots Ghost between boulders with bloody neck wounds from the eagle's talons; they wash and bandage him with wine and a strip of Jon's cloak.
12. **Qhorin orders retreat** — "We are seen. So now we run." The rangers turn their mounts back toward home.
13. **All-night ride back through the pass** — No fires permitted; a shadowcat screams; they reach the stone bridge at the summit and descend the other side.
14. **Squire Dalbridge volunteers as rearguard** — At the narrow point where one man could hold a hundred, Dalbridge stays behind with his longbow and extra arrows. He asks that his garron get an apple.
15. **Dawn: pursuit confirmed** — At dawn, under cloudless sky, the eagle is still visible; a hunting horn sounds behind them. "And now they come," says Qhorin.
