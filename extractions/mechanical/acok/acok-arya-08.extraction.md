- **chapter_summary:** Lord Tywin Lannister prepares to march his army from Harrenhal to confront Robb Stark. Arya, working as Weese's servant under the name "Weasel," searches for Jaqen H'ghar to use her second death. After Weese slaps her and breaks his promise about sharing a capon, she finds Jaqen in the bathhouse and whispers Weese's name. The next morning, Lord Tywin rides out with his host, and Arya realizes too late she should have named Tywin. Before she can find Jaqen to change her mind, she discovers Weese dead in the yard, his throat torn out by his own dog, and sees Jaqen signal her with two fingers against his cheek.

## Events & Actions
1. **Castle prepares for Lord Tywin's march** — Men load wagons, smiths work weapons and armor, mail is scoured, cloaks washed and mended, people crowd the sept to pray, camp outside comes down.
2. **Ser Addam Marbrand departs** — Rides out a day before the rest on a red courser with copper mane, bronze-colored trappings with the burning tree. Castle women sob.
3. **Arya runs messages and considers escape** — Weese has her running messages dawn to dusk, some beyond castle walls. She thinks she could flee with camp followers or hide in a wagon but fears Weese's threat of foot-cutting.
4. **Arya reads Weese's messages** — He doesn't know she's literate. Messages are mundane (carts to granary/armory, a gambling debt).
5. **Arya steals a drinking horn** — A knight can't read his message; tries to hit her when she tells him what it says. She snatches his silver-banded drinking horn and escapes. Gives it to Weese; he promises to share a capon with her.
6. **Arya searches for Jaqen** — Wants to whisper a second name before her targets leave. Cannot find him amid the chaos.
7. **Arya learns Ser Amory is castellan** — Gate guard tells her Lorch's men (including Jaqen) are staying; Bloody Mummers staying too. Vargo Hoat and Lorch hate each other.
8. **The Mountain's men will depart** — Dunsen, Polliver, and Raff will leave with Tywin's host, slipping beyond Arya's reach.
9. **Weese sends Arya to the armory** — For a new sword for Ser Lyonel, who rides with Ser Kevan; gives her a paper with his mark.
10. **Gendry warns Arya** — Tells her Hot Pie asked about her yelling "Winterfell" at the holdfast. Gendry told Hot Pie she yelled "Go to hell!" and instructs Arya to say the same.
11. **Arya gets the longsword from Lucan** — Heavy longsword, feels strong in her hands. She thinks "I'm not a mouse."
12. **Arya considers escape with sword and horse** — Plans to forge authorization with the paper, but doesn't know what Ser Lyonel looks like. Fears Weese.
13. **Arya overhears archers talking about Robb** — They speak of giants, wolves, Robb being "more wolf than man," and debate whether to fear him.
14. **Weese slaps Arya and takes the sword** — Says she took too long. Sends her to the brewhouse to tell Tuffleberry about barrels.
15. **Rorge confronts Arya** — Under the stone bridge between Widow's Tower and Kingspyre. With three men wearing Ser Amory's manticore badge. Threatens sexual violence, calls her "Yoren's little cunt." Halts when Arya mentions Jaqen.
16. **Arya finds Jaqen in the bathhouse** — He is soaking in a tub with a serving girl pouring water on him. He hears her approach despite her stealth. She whispers "Weese."
17. **Jaqen accepts the name** — Responds "Tell his lordship a man shall attend him at his leisure" and splashes water at her.
18. **Arya delivers message to Tuffleberry** — Brewer curses, threatens to tell Lord Tywin if barrels aren't fetched. Weese sends six men to get them.
19. **Supper — Weese eats the capon alone** — His bed-companion gets cheese and a wing. He remembers Arya only to beat her for "looking at him," threatens to spoon out her eye, shoves her to the floor tearing her shift.
20. **Arya recites her kill list** — Mending her torn shift with bone needle; whispers names including Weese, Dunsen, Polliver, Raff the Sweetling, the Tickler, the Hound, Ser Gregor, Ser Amory, Ser Ilyn, Ser Meryn, King Joffrey, Queen Cersei.
21. **Next morning — Weese wakes them, announces harder work** — Lord Tywin's host rides today; fewer hands to tend the castle.
22. **Lord Tywin's host rides out** — Arya watches from Wailing Tower. Lannister banners with golden lion; great lords and captains with colorful banners. Ser Gregor last with Polliver carrying the black dog standard and wearing Gendry's helm.
23. **Arya realizes her mistake** — Should have named Lord Tywin instead of Weese. Regrets being motivated by anger over the slap and the capon.
24. **Arya rushes to find Jaqen** — Runs down twisting steps as portcullis lowers; hears a shriek.
25. **Weese found dead** — Throat a red ruin, eyes staring; his own spotted dog stands on his chest lapping blood and ripping flesh from his face.
26. **Dog shot with crossbow** — Someone kills the dog while it worries Weese's ear.
27. **Reactions to the death** — Man says "damnedest thing"; crossbow man says "this place is cursed"; Goodwife Amabel blames Harren's ghost.
28. **Jaqen signals Arya** — Leaning against Wailing Tower, he lays two fingers against his cheek. Two deaths delivered, one remaining.
