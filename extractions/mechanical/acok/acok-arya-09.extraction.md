# ACOK — Arya IX

## Chapter Metadata

- **pov_character:** Arya Stark

- **location_primary:** Harrenhal
- **location_secondary:** Winterfell (mentioned), Deepwood Motte (mentioned), Braavos (mentioned), the Wall (mentioned)
- **approximate_timeline:** After Lord Tywin has marched from Harrenhal with his host; spans one night through the following evenfall
- **time_markers:** "the black of night"; warhorn sounds at night; "come dawn" Pinkeye reports the castle taken; "all morning" Arya watches stripping of dead; "almost evenfall" when Roose Bolton arrives; "that evening" Arya pours wine while Ser Amory is thrown to the bear
- **chapter_summary:** Vargo Hoat returns to Harrenhal with northern prisoners including Robett Glover and Ser Aenys Frey. Arya tries to convince Gendry to help free them but he refuses. In the godswood she encounters Jaqen H'ghar, names him as her third death to coerce his help, and he orchestrates a dungeon break using scalding broth, killing eight guards with Rorge and Biter. After the prisoners are freed and the castle taken, Jaqen changes his face, gives Arya an iron coin and the words "valar morghulis," and departs. Roose Bolton arrives at evenfall as the new lord of Harrenhal and makes Arya—now calling herself "Nan"—his cupbearer. Ser Amory Lorch is thrown into the bear pit.

## Events & Actions
1. **Arya visits Hot Pie at night** — She sneaks from her bed while Pinkeye is passed out drunk, finds Hot Pie baking in the kitchens, and filches a tart meant for Ser Amory.
2. **Warhorn sounds and Bloody Mummers arrive** — Two blasts of a warhorn and portcullis chains signal the arrival of Vargo Hoat with ox carts of plunder, a caged black bear, livestock, and over a hundred northern prisoners.
3. **Arya identifies the prisoners** — She recognizes sigils (mailed fist, twin towers, sunburst, bloody man, battle-axe) as belonging to northern houses loyal to Robb and her father.
4. **Ser Amory receives the prisoners** — Vargo Hoat reports he cut Roose Bolton's van to pieces and captured Glover and Ser Aenys Frey. Ser Amory dismisses Hoat's promises and orders prisoners sent to the dungeon under the Widow's Tower.
5. **Arya appeals to Gendry** — She climbs through the armory window and asks Gendry to help free the prisoners. He refuses, citing the danger and his contentment as a smith's apprentice.
6. **Arya and Gendry argue** — He mocks her status ("Would m'lady permit?") and invokes Ben Blackthumb's philosophy that it doesn't matter who you serve. She insults him and storms off.
7. **Arya practices swordplay in the godswood** — Using her hidden broomstick sword, she does Syrio's drills, climbs trees, and slashes at branches imagined as Joffrey, the queen, Ser Ilyn, Ser Meryn, and the Hound.
8. **Arya prays at the heart tree** — She kneels before the weirwood and prays to the old gods to help free the prisoners, kill Ser Amory, and bring her home. She then angrily scolds the tree for not saving her father.
9. **Jaqen H'ghar appears** — He startles Arya in the godswood. He reveals he knows her true identity ("My lady of Stark") and asks for the third name.
10. **Arya names Jaqen H'ghar** — She names Jaqen himself as the third death, coercing him to help free the prisoners. He is deeply distraught but bound by his oath.
11. **Jaqen agrees to help in exchange for unsaying his name** — After threatening with a knife and calling her "evil child," Jaqen agrees to help if she will unsay his name afterward.
12. **Arya fetches broth from the kitchens** — Following Jaqen's instructions, she tells the cooks the lord commands hot broth and waits in the kitchens.
13. **Rorge, Jaqen, and Biter collect the broth** — They arrive and intimidate the cook into giving them four kettles of not-yet-finished broth.
14. **Scalding broth assault on dungeon guards** — At the Widow's Tower dungeon, Rorge throws boiling broth across the table into the eight guards' faces. Jaqen and Biter do the same. One kettle hits the captain in the temple.
15. **Guards killed** — Rorge cuts throats, Biter snaps necks. One guard draws a blade; Jaqen duels him into a corner and kills him with a thrust to the heart. Jaqen wipes the bloody sword on Arya's shift.
16. **Prisoners freed** — Rorge takes the cell key from the wall and opens the door. Robett Glover leads the captives out. They strip dead guards of weapons and charge upstairs.
17. **Harrenhal falls** — Freed northmen and Brave Companions kill Ser Amory's garrison — some in their beds, others at table while drunk. Door guards found dead. Fighting at the gatehouse and across the ward.
18. **Arya unsays Jaqen's name** — Jaqen tells her a wolf banner will be raised. She takes back the name but asks about a third death; Jaqen says the debt is paid (three owed, more than three killed).
19. **Jaqen changes his face** — He passes a hand down his face and transforms: fuller cheeks, closer eyes, hooked nose, scar on right cheek, tight black curls, gold tooth.
20. **Jaqen gives Arya the iron coin** — He presses a hard iron coin into her palm and teaches her "valar morghulis" — the words to say to any Braavosi if she wishes to find him. He says he cannot stay and departs.
21. **Dawn — Pinkeye reports the takeover** — The new lord comes from "the wild north up where that Wall is." One servant boy was killed in the fighting.
22. **Morning — dead are stripped and burned** — Bodies dragged to the Flowstone Yard; Shagwell prances with severed heads making them joke about "hot weasel soup."
23. **Arya mops blood** — She notices people looking at her strangely; the freed men have spread the story of the dungeon break.
24. **Roose Bolton arrives at evenfall** — He enters with his host; has pale ice-colored eyes, black ringmail, spotted pink cloak, and a sigil of a man dipped in blood. His squire announces him as Lord of the Dreadfort.
25. **Bolton makes Arya his cupbearer** — He questions her age, asks if she fears leeches, and assigns her as cupbearer. She gives her name as "Nan" (short for "Nymeria").
26. **Banners changed** — Lannister lion and Lorch's black manticore hauled down; flayed man of the Dreadfort and direwolf of Stark raised.
27. **Ser Amory Lorch executed** — Stripped naked, he pleads and sobs; Rorge pulls him loose from captors; Shagwell kicks him into the bear pit. Arya notes the bear is all in black, like Yoren.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Restless and defiant at the chapter's start (sneaking around at night, spitting on tarts); desperate and determined when she sees her father's men as prisoners; frustrated and angry at Gendry's refusal; homesick while praying at the weirwood and remembering her father; exhilarated and scared during the dungeon break; reluctant sadness when Jaqen's debt is paid ("Now she was just a mouse again"); astonishment at Jaqen's face-change; resigned acceptance at becoming "Nan"
- **Primary preoccupation:** Freeing the northern prisoners and leveraging them to retake Harrenhal and escape home to Winterfell
- **Key decisions made:** Names Jaqen H'ghar as her third death to force his help; unsays the name after the prisoners are freed; chooses "Nymeria/Nan" as her new alias for Roose Bolton; conceals her identity from Rorge, Biter, and the freed men ("I'm Weasel")
- **Self-deception flags:** Tells herself she hesitated on the third name because "the last death has to count," but wonders if the real reason was that having an unused death made her feel safe and powerful; tells herself the dead guards "deserved to die" to justify the violence she participated in
