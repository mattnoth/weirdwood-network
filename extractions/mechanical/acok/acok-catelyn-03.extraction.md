# ACOK — Catelyn III

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** ACOK
- **chapter_number:** 32
- **pov_character:** Catelyn Stark
- **pov_chapter_number:** Catelyn III
- **first_available:** ACOK Catelyn III
- **location_primary:** Grassy sward between the armies near Storm's End
- **location_secondary:** Storm's End, Renly's encampment (atop a low stony ridge), royal pavilion (green silk), Catelyn's corner of camp, small sept (in nearby village — mentioned)
- **approximate_timeline:** Shortly before the battle between Stannis and Renly at Storm's End; Renly has recently raced east from the Mander/Bitterbridge area
- **time_markers:** Parley occurs during daytime ("wan sunlight"); dusk falling when Catelyn leaves pavilion; battle planned for dawn/first light; "this night" (Stannis's ultimatum); "come the dawn"; Renly was at Horn Hill when Tarly received Stannis's letter; "a year ago" Renly was scheming to make Margaery Robert's queen
- **chapter_summary:** Catelyn meets first with Stannis, then with both Baratheon brothers at a parley between their armies near Storm's End. Stannis declares Joffrey, Tommen, and Myrcella to be bastards born of Cersei and Jaime's incest, and demands Renly submit. Renly refuses, citing his vastly larger army. Catelyn fails to broker peace between them. Back at Renly's camp, a war council decides to give battle at dawn, with Ser Loras commanding the van. Catelyn is denied leave to return to Riverrun and instead goes to pray at a nearby sept.

## Events & Actions
1. **Catelyn and Hal Mollen arrive at parley site** — They rein up amidst the tree stumps between the two armies, first to arrive. Catelyn reflects on Storm's End and the legend of Durran Godsgrief.
2. **Stannis arrives with red priestess** — Two riders emerge from the camp beneath Storm's End. Catelyn notes his new banner bears a red heart in orange fire with the stag shrunken within it. The red priestess serves as his standard bearer.
3. **Catelyn and Stannis converse** — Stannis offers curt condolences for Ned's death; Catelyn reminds him Ned broke the siege of Storm's End. Stannis says he should have been Robert's Hand and promises justice for Ned's murder. Catelyn says she would sooner have her daughters back.
4. **Renly arrives with Brienne** — He arrives last deliberately (a game kings play). Brienne carries his banner in full plate armor concealing her sex.
5. **Three-way parley begins** — Renly questions Stannis's new sigil. The red priestess identifies it as the fiery heart of the Lord of Light. Catelyn urges the brothers to unite against their common Lannister foe.
6. **Stannis claims the Iron Throne by rights** — Renly dismisses his claim, saying no one wants him for king. Catelyn catalogues the Lannister threat: Tywin at Harrenhal, Kingslayer's remnants at Golden Tooth, another host at Casterly Rock, Cersei and Joffrey at King's Landing.
7. **Renly proposes Stannis bend the knee** — Stannis refuses absolutely. Renly asks why Stannis won't serve him as he served Robert.
8. **Stannis declares Joffrey, Tommen, and Myrcella are bastards** — Claims all three are abominations born of Cersei and Jaime's incest. Renly is dismissive, calls it a fable. Catelyn is speechless.
9. **Stannis reveals he brought suspicions to Jon Arryn** — Says Robert would not have listened to him; believes Cersei poisoned Jon Arryn to silence him. Renly notes the proofs died with Jon Arryn.
10. **Catelyn confirms Lysa's accusations** — Admits her sister Lysa accused the queen of killing Jon Arryn by letter, and later accused Tyrion at the Eyrie.
11. **Renly offers a peach and acknowledges Stannis's claim** — Says Stannis may have the better claim but he has the larger army. Offers a peach from Highgarden; Stannis refuses; Renly eats it.
12. **Renly offers Storm's End as a gift** — Says he has no wish to slay his brother and offers the castle. Stannis refuses, saying Storm's End is his by rights.
13. **Renly mocks Stannis's marriage and family** — Taunts him about Margaery, Stannis's lack of sons, his daughter, and his wife's appearance. Stannis roars in fury.
14. **Stannis draws Lightbringer** — The sword gleams strangely bright, shimmering with heat, colors shifting. Catelyn's horse backs away. Brienne draws her blade and steps between the brothers.
15. **Stannis delivers ultimatum** — Offers Renly Storm's End, a council seat, and designation as heir if he submits before dawn. Otherwise, destruction.
16. **Renly catalogues his military superiority** — Lists dozens of houses that support him, including House Florent (Stannis's wife's family); counts Stannis's force at ~5,000 with fewer than 400 horse; says his own foot of 100,000 is coming behind.
17. **Parley ends; Stannis departs** — Stannis gallops away disdainfully. The red priestess tells Renly to "look to your own sins" before following.
18. **Catelyn and Renly return to camp** — Renly comments he wonders where he can get a sword like Lightbringer; says Loras will make him a gift of it after battle. Admits Stannis was never cherished as a brother.
19. **Catelyn and Renly discuss succession** — Catelyn confirms that if the incest claim is true, Stannis is the lawful heir. Renly argues the best-fitted should rule, not the eldest; lists his own virtues. Catelyn supplies "humble."
20. **War council in royal pavilion** — Renly announces battle. Lord Mathis Rowan counsels bypassing Stannis to take King's Landing. Lord Randyll Tarly argues Stannis must be dealt with now or will grow stronger. Others agree with Tarly. Renly decides to fight.
21. **Battle dispositions assigned** — Lord Mathis Rowan: center of main battle. Bryce (unnamed lord): left. Renly: right. Lord Estermont: reserve. After competing claims for the van, Ser Loras is given command; he asks for Brienne to ride beside him with the banner.
22. **Brienne asks to arm Renly for battle** — She is assigned to carry Renly's banner alongside Loras in the van; she requests and is granted the honor of arming Renly. Catelyn observes Brienne loves Renly.
23. **Catelyn denied leave to return to Riverrun** — Renly insists she stay and watch the battle as a lesson for Robb. Catelyn asks leave to pray at a nearby sept instead; Renly grants it with escort.
24. **Catelyn advises Renly to pray for wisdom** — Renly asks if he should pray for victory; Catelyn says "For wisdom." Renly laughs and asks Loras to stay and help him pray.
25. **Catelyn escorted by Ser Robar Royce** — They converse; he is a second son of Bronze Yohn Royce, seeking glory away from the Vale. Catelyn thinks he cannot be older than one-and-twenty.
26. **Catelyn returns to her corner of camp** — Shadd prepares food (slicing carrots), Hal Mollen dices with three Winterfell men, Lucas Blackwood sharpens his dagger and asks if they fight or flee. Catelyn says they pray.
