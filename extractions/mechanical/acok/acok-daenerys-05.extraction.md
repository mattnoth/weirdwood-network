# ACOK — Daenerys V

- **approximate_timeline:** Late in ACOK; Dany has been in Qarth for some time following the destruction of the House of the Undying
- **time_markers:** Morning (breaking her fast); daytime (heat of the day mentioned); evening tide referenced for ship departure; the House of the Undying collapsed recently ("overnight the Qartheen had come to remember that dragons were dangerous"); Dany has "lingered in Qarth too long"
- **chapter_summary:** Daenerys, expelled from Xaro's hospitality and threatened by warlocks, rides to Qarth's waterfront seeking passage west. She is refused by every captain she approaches — some laugh, some demand too high a price, some fear dragons or Dothraki. While negotiating, she notices two men following her: a fat brown eunuch and a white-bearded old man. A Sorrowful Man attempts to assassinate her with a manticore hidden in a jewel box; the old man (Arstan Whitebeard) knocks it from her hand, saving her life. Arstan and Strong Belwas reveal they were sent by Magister Illyrio with three ships to bring Daenerys back to Pentos, and inform her that Robert is dead and four kings contest the realm. Dany accepts and orders the ships renamed Vhagar, Meraxes, and Balerion.

## Events & Actions
1. **Dany refuses Qartheen gown** — Chooses to dress in Dothraki fashion (sandsilk pants, painted vest, curved dagger) for trip to the docks.
2. **Jhiqui braids Dany's hair with silver bell** — Dany protests she has won no victories; Jhiqui credits her with burning the warlocks.
3. **Party departs Xaro's manse** — Rakharo stays to guard people and dragons; Aggo, Jhogo, and Ser Jorah accompany Dany.
4. **Ride through poorer district** — Pass modest brick houses; pale men watch without love; children, beggars, and dogs fill streets.
5. **Dany reflects on being expelled by Xaro** — Recalls conversation where Xaro warned of warlocks, proposed marriage (again), then coldly told her to leave when refused. His seneschal sent increasingly cold messages.
6. **Dany recalls Xaro's warnings about warlocks** — Glass candles burning, ghost grass in Garden of Gehane, phantom tortoises, rats chewing off tails, Mallarawan's wife gone mad, Blind Sybassion seeing again.
7. **Dany reflects on political situation** — Tourmaline Brotherhood wants her expelled, Ancient Guild of Spicers wants her dead, Thirteen barely restrained by Xaro.
8. **Dany considers destinations** — Jorah suggests going east; bloodriders want the grass sea; Dany considers Vaes Tolorro; all feel wrong.
9. **Dany recalls negotiation with Xaro over ships** — He offered ten ships for one dragon, escalated to thirty ships for one dragon; she refused. She calculated one-third of all ships in the world as fair price. Xaro called her mad.
10. **Dany ponders the three treasons prophecy** — Mirri Maz Duur was blood; wonders if Pyat Pree and Xaro are the other two; decides probably not.
11. **Party passes through warehouse district** — Streets grow emptier; Aggo rides before, Jhogo behind, Jorah at her side.
12. **Dany discusses House of the Undying visions with Jorah** — The dragon has three heads; Aegon and his sisters; Rhaegar's vision of the prince that was promised; the song of ice and fire; the mummer's dragon.
13. **Arrival at waterfront** — Jhogo smells the sea; description of Qarth's great port with its marketplace, merchants, and sailors from many nations.
14. **Aggo and Jhogo buy food** — Aggo gets honey-roasted mice, Jhogo gets cherries; they pass stalls with bronze daggers, dried squids, carved onyx, magical elixirs, fake dragon eggs.
15. **Party passes quays of the Thirteen** — See Vermillion Kiss offloading saffron/frankincense/pepper; Bride in Azure loading wine/sourleaf/hides; Sunblaze selling slaves from Astapor.
16. **Dany seeks passage at foreign ships' quay** — Rides to far end of horseshoe harbor where Summer Islands, Westeros, and Nine Free Cities ships dock.
17. **Multiple captains refuse Dany** — Ardent Friend's captain laughs; Lyseni on Trumpeteer mocks her identity; Silken Spirit says dragons too dangerous; Lord Faro's Belly won't take Dothraki; Quicksilver/Greyhound brothers' price too high; Pinchbottom Petto and Sloe-Eyed Maid too small; Bravo bound for Jade Sea; Magister Manolo not seaworthy.
18. **Jorah spots followers** — Uses brass platter as mirror to show Dany she's being followed by fat brown man and old man with staff.
19. **Dany haggles with brass merchant** — Uses the interaction as cover to observe followers; merchant drops price from thirty to two honors.
20. **Sorrowful Man assassination attempt** — Qartheen man kneels before Dany offering jewel box; contains manticore disguised as green scarab; says "I am so sorry."
21. **Arstan saves Dany** — Drives butt of staff into the manticore, knocking box from her hand; asks if her hand is broken.
22. **Chaos erupts** — Aggo rides through egg-seller's stall; Jhogo's whip cracks; Jorah hits Belwas with brass platter; bloodriders seize Arstan at knifepoint.
23. **Dany orders release** — Recognizes Arstan and Belwas as defenders, not attackers; the Sorrowful Man has escaped.
24. **Brass merchant confirmed unhurt** — Nearly stung but not; soiled himself; Dany gives him a silver coin.
25. **Arstan and Belwas introduce themselves** — Arstan from Dornish Marches, squired for knight of Lord Swann's household; Belwas from fighting pits of Meereen, never lost a fight, lets each man cut him once.
26. **They reveal Illyrio sent them** — Three ships: Saduleon, Summer Sun, Joso's Prank; Robert is dead; four kings in the land.
27. **Dany names Jorah to Queensguard** — Calls him "knight of my Queensguard" and "my true friend and good counselor."
28. **Dany accepts passage** — Orders ships renamed Vhagar, Meraxes, and Balerion with golden letters three feet high.
