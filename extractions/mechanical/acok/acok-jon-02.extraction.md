## Events & Actions
1. **Arrival at Whitetree** — The Night's Watch column arrives at Whitetree, the fourth empty wildling village they have found on their ranging.
2. **Observation of the great weirwood** — Jon, Mormont, and Thoren Smallwood examine the enormous weirwood tree with its jagged hollow mouth containing bones and skulls.
3. **Skull retrieval** — Jon reaches into the weirwood's hollow and pulls out a human skull, then discovers a second smaller skull beneath it, half-buried in ash and bone.
4. **Mormont examines the skull** — The Old Bear holds it, reflects that wildlings burn their dead, wishes he had asked them why, and tosses it back into the tree.
5. **Houses searched** — Mormont orders all houses searched in pairs; Jon is paired with Dolorous Edd.
6. **Jon and Edd search a house** — They find nothing but a packed dirt floor, ashes, straw bedding, and the faint smell of old dung; the village appears long-empty.
7. **Bedwyck climbs the weirwood** — Giant reports seeing water (a lake) to the north and flint hills to the west; nothing else visible.
8. **Mormont decides to press north** — The Old Bear rejects camping at Whitetree and orders the column to continue toward the lake with three hours of daylight remaining.
9. **Mormont writes a message** — He scrawls a note to Maester Aemon reporting the fourth empty village and that the wildlings are gone.
10. **Jon delivers message to Sam** — Sam attaches it to a raven and sends it south toward Castle Black.
11. **Sam's progress** — Sam reports he is less frightened than before, shows his steady hands, mentions working on maps and teaching ravens to talk.
12. **Column moves out in formation** — Buckwell's scouts first, Smallwood's vanguard, Mormont's main force, Locke's baggage train, Wythers's rear guard — two hundred men with half again as many mounts.
13. **The march continues** — Days of travel through game trails and streambeds; nights under stars; the men grow silent and uneasy; game has fled along with wildlings.
14. **Jon flexes his burned hand** — He thinks of Arya and worries about keeping his sword hand limber.
15. **Ghost appears** — The direwolf emerges suddenly from undergrowth; has been hunting away from the column without success.
16. **Jon rides with Mormont** — They discuss Benjen's likely discovery of the same empty villages; Mormont promises they will find the enemy, especially after Qhorin joins with his men to make three hundred.
