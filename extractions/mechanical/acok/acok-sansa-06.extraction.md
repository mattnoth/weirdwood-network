- **location_secondary:** Maegor's Holdfast, Mud Gate, King's Gate, Blackwater Rush, tourney grounds, Flea Bottom, Baelor's Sept, the Whores (trebuchets), godswood, stables
- **approximate_timeline:** During the Battle of the Blackwater; the evening of the assault on King's Landing
- **time_markers:** Night (torches lit, banquet during a nighttime battle); multiple messengers arriving at intervals throughout the evening; battle progressing from fleet engagement to ground assault over the course of the chapter
- **chapter_summary:** Sansa attends a banquet in the Queen's Ballroom during the Battle of the Blackwater, hosted by Cersei for the highborn women of the castle. Osney Kettleblack brings periodic battle updates to the queen — the fleet burning with wildfire, Stannis landing men, the Mud Gate under assault, and Tyrion leading a sortie. Cersei drinks heavily and delivers cynical monologues to Sansa about power, womanhood, loyalty through fear, and her resentment of the different fates allotted to her and Jaime. Cersei reveals that Ser Ilyn Payne — carrying Ice, stained with fresh blood — is present to execute them if the city falls, rather than let Stannis take them alive.

## Events & Actions
1. **Banquet in progress during battle** — Sansa attends a feast in the Queen's Ballroom while the Battle of the Blackwater rages outside.
2. **Osney Kettleblack delivers first report** — Fleets locked in battle, archers ashore but the Hound cutting them to pieces, chain being raised, looters in Flea Bottom, Baelor's Sept full of people praying, Joffrey walking the walls with the Hand.
3. **Cersei drinks and holds court** — The queen drinks Arbor gold heavily and makes cynical observations about the gathered women and womanhood.
4. **Salad course served** — Lollys shivering, Lord Gyles coughing, a young bride weeping and removed with dreamwine by Maester Frenken.
5. **Cersei lectures Sansa on weapons** — Quotes Jaime about battle and bed; discusses tears and sexuality as women's weapons.
6. **Two Kettleblacks return** — Osney reports the hulks ablaze and a hundred ships burning with wildfire; Joffrey at the Mud Gate.
7. **Osfryd reports internal treachery** — A groom and two maidservants caught trying to flee with three royal horses through a postern.
8. **Cersei orders executions** — Commands Ser Ilyn to behead the would-be deserters and put their heads on pikes outside the stables.
9. **Cersei lectures on fear vs. love** — Tells Sansa that loyalty comes from fear, not gentleness; Sansa internally disagrees, thinking love is better.
10. **Main courses served** — Crabclaw pies, then mutton in bread trenchers. Lollys eats too fast and vomits. Lord Gyles passes out.
11. **Osfryd reports on crowd at gates** — Merchants seeking refuge in the castle; Cersei orders them sent away or killed.
12. **Cersei's monologue on gender** — Recounts childhood with Jaime, identical appearances but divergent fates; expresses rage at being denied swords while Jaime trained.
13. **Cersei stops drinking** — Turns over her cup, saying she must keep a clear head.
14. **Final course and dire reports** — Goat cheese and baked apples served. Osney reports Stannis has landed men, Mud Gate under attack, ram at King's Gate, Tyrion gone out to drive them off.
15. **Cersei furious about Joffrey's exposure** — Learns Joffrey is at the Whores flinging Antler Men; orders Ser Osmund to bring him back immediately.
16. **Guests depart for sept** — Many ask leave to pray; Lady Tanda and daughters among those who leave.
17. **Singer performs** — Songs of Jonquil and Florian, Prince Aemon the Dragonknight, and Nymeria's ten thousand ships; women weep.
18. **Cersei accuses Sansa of treason** — Reveals she knows about Sansa's visits to the godswood; accuses her of praying for Stannis or Robb's victory.
19. **Cersei forces Sansa to drink** — Commands Sansa to drain a cup of sweet plum wine.
20. **Cersei reveals Ser Ilyn's true purpose** — Admits she lied earlier about why Ser Ilyn is present; he is there to kill them if the city falls. Ice is shown with fresh blood. Cersei declares she won't be taken alive or judged by Stannis.
21. **Cersei threatens Sansa** — Tells Sansa the Starks will have no joy from the fall of House Lannister; touches Sansa's hair, brushing it from her neck (implied threat of beheading).

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Fearful throughout; growing dread as battle reports worsen; horror at Cersei's revelations; nervous under Cersei's interrogation about the godswood; "her head was swimming" after forced wine
- **Primary preoccupation:** Survival — navigating Cersei's mercurial moods and threats; concealing her connection to Ser Dontos; absorbing the escalating danger of the battle
- **Key decisions made:** Maintains her cover story about the godswood (praying for Joffrey); does not look at Ser Dontos when Cersei mentions the godswood; internally resolves that if she's ever queen she'll rule through love not fear
- **Self-deception flags:** Reassures herself "She doesn't know, no one knows, Dontos promised me, my Florian would never fail me" — placing possibly naive trust in Dontos
