# ACOK — Jon VIII

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** ACOK
- **chapter_number:** 69
- **pov_character:** Jon Snow
- **pov_chapter_number:** Jon VIII
- **first_available:** ACOK Jon VIII
- **location_primary:** The Frostfangs (beyond the Wall)
- **location_secondary:** Cave behind waterfall, narrow defile, ridge near cave mouth
- **approximate_timeline:** Final days of the ranging party's flight from wildling pursuit; concludes with Jon joining the wildlings
- **time_markers:** Moon rising/sun sinking (evening of campfire); night ride after fire; "every night seemed colder"; break of day (morning in cave); "long hours later" emerging from cave; sun still up when wildlings arrive
- **chapter_summary:** Qhorin Halfhand and Jon Snow, the last two survivors of their five-man party, make camp and recite their Night's Watch vows. Qhorin commands Jon to yield to the wildlings if captured and infiltrate them. They attempt to evade pursuit through a hidden cave passage behind a waterfall, but the eagle finds them again. Cornered, Jon yields to Rattleshirt's band. Ygritte vouches for him, and Rattleshirt demands Jon prove himself by killing Qhorin. Jon fights and kills the Halfhand with Ghost's help, and is accepted into the wildling group.

## Events & Actions
1. **Qhorin tells Jon to gather brush for fire** — Jon knows their end is near; reflects on whether the direwolves will howl for him when he dies.
2. **Jon builds a campfire** — Strikes sparks from flint and dagger; fire grows from shavings of bark and dead pine needles.
3. **Recap of the flight from Skirling Pass** — Only Jon and Qhorin remain of five rangers; Dalbridge fell at the pass (confirmed by horn); eagle spotted repeatedly; Ebben sent east to Mormont with garrons.
4. **Stonesnake's mare breaks a leg** — A shadowcat startles the horse on a steep slope; the garron is killed; Ghost feeds on it; rangers eat blood-oat porridge and cut raw meat strips.
5. **Stonesnake departs on foot** — Qhorin sends him to make for the Fist alone and afoot across mountains; to tell Mormont about wargs, giants, and old powers waking.
6. **Continued flight by Jon and Qhorin** — Cold lonely nights; Ghost ranges but stays near; Jon senses Ghost even apart; no dreams come to Jon.
7. **Qhorin and Jon recite Night's Watch vows** — By firelight; the full oath spoken together; Ghost listens.
8. **Qhorin commands Jon to yield if captured** — Orders him to infiltrate the wildlings; to learn what they were digging for in the valley of the Milkwater; promises to tell the Old Bear Jon never broke his oath.
9. **Night ride away from fire** — Qhorin leaves fire burning as decoy; they ride back in darkness by half-moon light to a narrow defile.
10. **Discovery of cave behind waterfall** — Qhorin rides through the curtain of falling water; Jon follows; Ghost bursts through; cave has a passage through the mountain.
11. **Night in the cave** — Qhorin takes first watch, sword drawn; Jon sleeps with Ghost; Jon dreams of burning castles and dead men rising from graves; Jon takes second watch until dawn.
12. **Passage through the mountain** — By torchlight; twisted stony path; garrons squeeze through narrow spots; emerge into daylight hours later.
13. **Eagle waiting at emergence** — The eagle perches on a dead tree above; Ghost chases but it flies away; pursuit not lost.
14. **Qhorin decides to make a stand** — Positions at cave mouth for defensive advantage; they feed the horses the last oats.
15. **Wildling pursuit arrives** — Hunting horn and baying hounds; fourteen wildlings with eight dogs crest a ridge; eagle lands and screams.
16. **Standoff with Rattleshirt** — Verbal exchange; Qhorin mocks Rattleshirt; Rattleshirt threatens to make a byrnie from Qhorin's ribs.
17. **Ebben's death confirmed** — Spearwife displays Ebben's severed head; says "He died brave."
18. **Wildlings taunt Jon and Qhorin** — Mock Jon's youth; threaten Ghost; one spearwife bares her breast mockingly.
19. **Jon yields** — Shouts "We yield!" before archers can loose; Qhorin calls him craven and coward (as part of the deception).
20. **Ygritte identifies and vouches for Jon** — Reveals herself; names him "the Bastard o' Winterfell, who spared me"; argues he should live.
21. **Rattleshirt demands proof** — Ragwyle suggests Jon prove his prowess; Rattleshirt commands Jon to kill Qhorin.
22. **Duel: Jon vs. Qhorin** — Qhorin attacks; Jon defends with Longclaw; youth's quickness vs. Qhorin's left-handed strength; Ghost bites Qhorin's calf creating the opening.
23. **Jon kills Qhorin Halfhand** — Slash across throat; "a string of red tears... bright as a ruby necklace"; Qhorin says "...sharp" and dies.
24. **Jon's grief and disorientation** — Thinks "He knew"; remembers friends at Castle Black and brothers at Winterfell; wonders who he is now.
25. **Wildlings argue over Jon's fate** — Rattleshirt wants him gutted; Ygritte reminds them he yielded; short homely man notes he slew his brother; Rattleshirt calls him warg.
26. **Jon accepted into the group** — Ygritte and others support him; Rattleshirt yields grudgingly; "These are a free folk indeed."
27. **Qhorin's funeral pyre** — Burned where he fell on green and dead wood; slow smoky fire; black plume into blue sky; Rattleshirt claims charred bones; others dice for gear; Ygritte wins cloak.
28. **Ygritte reveals Mance's march** — They will not return by Skirling Pass; Mance is well down the Milkwater, marching on the Wall.
