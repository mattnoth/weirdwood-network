# ACOK — Daenerys III

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** ACOK
- **chapter_number:** 41
- **pov_character:** Daenerys Targaryen
- **pov_chapter_number:** Daenerys III
- **first_available:** ACOK Daenerys III
- **location_primary:** Qarth
- **location_secondary:** Hall of a Thousand Thrones, bazaar, Xaro's manse, Temple of Memory
- **approximate_timeline:** Mid-book; Daenerys has been in Qarth long enough to arrange audiences, receive gifts from seekers, and sell those gifts for gold
- **time_markers:** "as the sun was going down" (Ser Jorah's arrival at manse); "half a year gone" (Quaithe referring to the firemage's prior ability); "Come the morrow" (Dany's instruction to Jorah about visiting Pyat Pree)
- **chapter_summary:** Daenerys returns from the Hall of a Thousand Thrones after the Pureborn refuse her request for ships and soldiers. In Xaro's palanquin, she and the merchant prince discuss alternatives; Xaro proposes marriage again and she declines. In the bazaar, they witness a firemage climb a ladder of flame, and Quaithe appears to warn Dany she must leave Qarth, giving cryptic directions ("to go north, you must journey south"). Back at Xaro's manse, Ser Jorah reveals that Qartheen wedding custom would let Xaro claim a dragon, warns against Illyrio, and counsels going east. Dany decides Jorah should visit Pyat Pree and the warlocks the next day.

- **Lighting:** Bright daylight outside; crackling orange fire from firemage's ladder; brazier light in Dany's chambers
- **Sounds:** City murmur described as "all its myriad voices blending into one low sound like the surge of the sea"; Jhogo shouting "make way"; whip snapping; dragons hissing and squabbling; smoke hissing from dragon nostrils
- **Smells:** Not explicitly described
- **Notable sensory details:** Cool satin cushions in palanquin; silver collar chafing against throat; Rhaegal's sharp black claws digging into bare shoulder; dragon "as hot as a stone that has soaked all day in the blazing sun"; Quaithe's touch making Dany's wrist tingle

## Events & Actions
1. **Dany departs Hall of a Thousand Thrones** — Returns to Xaro's palanquin after the Pureborn refuse her plea for ships and soldiers.
2. **Dany removes enchanted collar** — Unfastens and flings aside the silver collar with amethyst that Xaro gave her to ward against poison.
3. **Dany recounts the Pureborn's refusal** — Tells Xaro that Mathos said nothing, Wendello praised her speech, and the Exquisite refused but wept.
4. **Xaro proposes marriage** — Asks Dany to marry him; she declines, recognizing his romantic disinterest and his true motive (dragons).
5. **Dany threatens to seek the warlocks** — Mentions Pyat Pree as alternative if Xaro and the Thirteen won't help; Xaro warns against warlocks.
6. **Procession halts at bazaar** — Oxen stop due to crowd blocking the way; a firemage is performing.
7. **Firemage climbs ladder of flame** — Conjures a forty-foot crackling orange ladder of fire and climbs it, dissolving each rung behind him, then vanishes.
8. **Quaithe appears and speaks to Dany** — Explains the firemage's powers have grown because of Dany's dragons; warns Dany must leave Qarth or never be permitted to leave.
9. **Quaithe delivers cryptic prophecy** — "To go north, you must journey south. To reach the west, you must go east. To go forward you must go back, and to touch the light you must pass beneath the shadow."
10. **Jhogo and Rakharo dismiss Quaithe** — Jhogo brushes away her touch; Rakharo calls her "spawn of shadows."
11. **Return to Xaro's manse** — Xaro warns against truths the Asshai'i hoard; presses wine and speaks of love.
12. **Dany feeds and observes her dragons** — Chops snake, chars it over brazier; notes they've doubled in weight since Vaes Tolorro but are years from war-readiness.
13. **Ser Jorah arrives at sunset** — Confirms Pureborn refusal was expected; reveals Qartheen wedding custom (one token of love that cannot be denied).
14. **Jorah warns about Illyrio** — Argues Illyrio sold Dany to Drogo and believes in no cause but his own; cautions against Free Cities route.
15. **Jorah counsels going east** — Warns that the longer Dany stays in one place, the easier for enemies to find her; enemies fear Targaryen name and will act when they learn of dragons.
16. **Dany decides to visit warlocks** — Tells Jorah he must go to Pyat Pree on the morrow.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Weary, disappointed, bitter, but resolute. Cycling between frustration at being treated as a beggar/curiosity and determination to find a way to Westeros. Afraid but forcing bravery.
- **Primary preoccupation:** Finding ships and an army to reclaim the Iron Throne; all avenues in Qarth seem closed.
- **Key decisions made:** Refuses Xaro's marriage proposal; decides against returning to Vaes Tolorro; chooses to send Jorah to Pyat Pree and the warlocks as a last resort.
- **Self-deception flags:** "Nothing," she said, wishing she was as certain as she sounded — explicitly acknowledges gap between her projected confidence and her inner doubt. Also reflects she is "the most splendid beggar in the world" while presenting herself as a queen.
