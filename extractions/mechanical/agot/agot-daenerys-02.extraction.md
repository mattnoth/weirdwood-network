Daenerys receives bride gifts including three handmaids, dragon eggs from Illyrio, books from Ser Jorah, and a silver-grey filly from Drogo. She rides the horse with unexpected confidence and joy. After sunset, Drogo rides out alone with Daenerys and consummates the marriage beside a stream, showing unexpected gentleness.

## Events & Actions
1. **Pre-wedding dinner** — Illyrio, Viserys, Ser Jorah, and Dany dine at Drogo's manse. Illyrio reports the city guard has been doubled due to the Dothraki presence. Viserys grows impatient about his promised crown; Illyrio and Ser Jorah counsel patience.
2. **Dany's dragon dream** — Dany dreams Viserys is hitting her; she is naked and bloody. A great fire erupts, Viserys vanishes, and a dragon appears amid columns of flame. Its molten eyes find hers and she wakes in terror, covered in sweat.
3. **Wedding ceremony begins at dawn** — Dany is seated beside Khal Drogo on a mighty earthen ramp above the Dothraki horde. She has never seen so many people.
4. **Viserys seethes at seating** — Viserys sits below Dany, below the bloodriders, and is served from portions the khal and bride refuse. His mood grows blacker through the day.
5. **Dany's isolation** — Dany has no one to talk to; she and Drogo share no common language. She nurses honeyed wine, afraid to eat.
6. **First death at the wedding** — A warrior steps into the dancing circle, takes a woman; two warriors fight over the same woman with arakhs. One is cut open from backbone to belly button. The winner takes the nearest woman.
7. **Multiple deaths at wedding** — Before the day is over, a dozen men have died at the feast, making it especially "blessed" by Dothraki standards.
8. **Bride gifts: handmaids** — Viserys presents Irri, Jhiqui, and Doreah as handmaids (actually provided by Illyrio). Each has a specific teaching role.
9. **Bride gift: books** — Ser Jorah gives Dany histories and songs of the Seven Kingdoms written in the Common Tongue, apologizing for the modest gift.
10. **Bride gift: dragon eggs** — Illyrio presents three petrified dragon eggs from the Shadow Lands beyond Asshai, nested in velvets and damasks within a bronze-bound cedar chest.
11. **Bride gifts: weapons** — Drogo's three bloodriders — Haggo, Cohollo, and Qotho — give traditional weapons. Dany performs the ritual refusal, passing them to Drogo.
12. **Numerous Dothraki gifts** — Dany receives slippers, jewels, silver hair rings, medallion belts, painted vests, soft furs, sandsilks, jars of scent, needles, feathers, tiny bottles of purple glass, and a gown of a thousand mouse skins.
13. **Bride gift: the silver filly** — Drogo brings a young grey filly with a silver-smoke mane. The entire khalasar falls silent.
14. **Dany rides the silver** — Dany rides the filly through the crowd, gaining confidence, breaking into a gallop, and leaping a firepit. She tells Illyrio to tell Drogo he has given her the wind. Drogo smiles for the first time.
15. **Viserys's threat** — As the sun sets and Drogo saddles his horse, Viserys digs his fingers into Dany's leg and threatens her to please Drogo or "see the dragon wake."
16. **Ride into the night** — Drogo and Dany ride out together as the stars come out, leaving the khalasar behind. Drogo speaks no word and rides at a hard trot.
17. **Consummation** — They stop at a grassy place beside a stream. Dany cries; Drogo wipes her tears and says "No." He unbinds his braid (Dany helps), undresses her slowly and gently, touches her with careful tenderness over a long period, and asks "No?" as a question. Dany consents, saying "Yes."

- **Primary preoccupation:** Surviving the wedding day and night; dread of the consummation
- **Key decisions made:** Chooses to ride the silver filly boldly (galloping, leaping the firepit); consents to the consummation ("Yes")
