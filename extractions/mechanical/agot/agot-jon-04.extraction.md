- **chapter_summary:** Samwell Tarly arrives at Castle Black as a new Night's Watch recruit. When Ser Alliser Thorne forces him to fight Halder and beats him mercilessly, Jon intervenes and defends Sam with help from Pyp and Grenn. Jon befriends Sam, learns his backstory — how his father Lord Randyll threatened to kill him if he did not take the black — and organizes the other recruits to protect Sam from further beatings during training. Jon reflects on Castle Black as his true home and the Night's Watch brothers as his real family.

## Events & Actions
1. **Jon trains Dareon** — Jon is showing Dareon sidestroke technique in the practice yard when Sam arrives.
2. **Samwell Tarly arrives** — The enormously fat new recruit enters the practice yard, wearing velvet and fur, announcing he was told to come for training.
3. **Ser Alliser mocks Sam** — Thorne calls Sam "Ser Piggy" and "Lord of Ham," mocking his weight and fine clothing.
4. **Sam is refitted with armor** — Donal Noye spends half the morning refitting mail with leather panels, detaching a visor, and binding leathers around Sam's legs and arms.
5. **Halder beats Sam** — On Thorne's orders, Halder fights Sam; the fight lasts less than a minute before Sam is on the ground bleeding from a shattered helm, yielding.
6. **Thorne orders continued beating** — Despite Sam's yield, Thorne orders Halder to hit Sam with the flat of his blade until he stands; Halder strikes hard enough to split leather.
7. **Jon intervenes** — Jon steps forward and tells Halder to stop, saying there's no honor in beating a fallen foe. Pyp tries to restrain him but Jon shakes him off.
8. **Thorne escalates** — Thorne orders Rast, Albett, and Halder (three against one) to fight Jon, who must defend Sam.
9. **Pyp and Grenn join Jon** — Pyp and Grenn step up to make the fight three against three.
10. **Jon defeats Halder** — Jon drives Halder backward using knowledge of his weaknesses (strong but impatient), cuts his leg from under him.
11. **Pyp defeats Rast** — Jon rings Rast's helm from behind; Pyp knocks him down and holds blade to his throat.
12. **Albett yields** — Facing two swords, Albett surrenders.
13. **Thorne ends session** — Thorne calls it "the mummer's farce" and walks away in disgust.
14. **Sam introduces himself to Jon** — After the fight, Sam removes Jon's helm for him and introduces himself as Samwell Tarly of Horn Hill.
15. **Jon works atop the Wall** — That afternoon, Jon scatters gravel over icy footpaths on top of the Wall with Ghost, thinking about Sam and Tyrion Lannister.
16. **Jon sits with Sam at supper** — Instead of joining his laughing friends, Jon sits at the far end of the bench where Sam sits alone.
17. **Jon and Sam walk outside** — Jon takes Sam outside to talk and see the Wall by moonlight.
18. **Ghost comforts Sam** — When Sam breaks down crying, Ghost licks his tears, turning sobs to laughter.
19. **Jon tells Sam his dream** — Jon describes his recurring dream of an empty Winterfell and the dark crypts with the Kings of Winter.
20. **Sam tells his story** — Sam reveals his backstory: his father's cruelty, the failed attempts to make him martial (including blood magic by Qartheen warlocks), and Lord Randyll's threat to kill him if he didn't take the black.
21. **Jon organizes recruits** — Back in the common hall, Jon persuades, cajoles, shames, and threatens the other recruits into agreeing to protect Sam in training. All agree except Rast.
22. **Jon, Grenn, and Pyp intimidate Rast** — Hours later, they visit Rast's cell at night; Grenn holds his arms, Pyp sits on his legs, Ghost nips his throat drawing blood. Jon warns "we know where you sleep."
23. **Rast covers up the incident** — Next morning, Rast claims the cut was from a shaving razor.
24. **Recruits protect Sam** — From that day, no one hurts Sam in training; they only tap him lightly despite Thorne's rage.
25. **Sam integrates with the group** — Sam joins evening meals, eventually joins conversations, laughs and teases with the others.
26. **Sam thanks Jon** — Sam visits Jon's cell, says he knows Jon did something, and says he's never had a friend before. Jon replies "We're not friends. We're brothers."
27. **Jon reflects on identity** — Jon thinks of Castle Black as his true home and the Watch recruits as his true brothers, recalling that Catelyn ensured he never belonged among the Starks.
