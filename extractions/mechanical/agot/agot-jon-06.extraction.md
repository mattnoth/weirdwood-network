Jon is assigned to the stewards rather than the rangers, which enrages him, though Sam convinces him that Mormont is grooming him for command. Jon and Sam ride beyond the Wall to a grove of nine weirwoods where they recite their vows together. Afterward, Ghost returns carrying a severed human hand.

## Events & Actions
1. **Sam announces his assignment** — Sam tells Jon he has been passed out of training and will assist Maester Aemon with the library and ravens.
2. **Assignment ceremony in the sept** — Mormont addresses all recruits, delivers a speech about leaving behind their pasts, and warns that desertion means death.
3. **Jon declares for the old gods** — Jon stands to say he keeps the old gods and will take his vows before a heart tree rather than in the sept.
4. **Sam asks to join Jon** — Sam, raised in the Faith of the Seven, asks to also take his vows before a heart tree, saying the Seven have never answered his prayers.
5. **Order assignments read** — Mormont reads the assignments: Halder and Albett to builders; Grenn, Pyp, Matthar, and Todder to rangers; Sam, Dareon, and Jon to stewards.
6. **Jon reacts with fury** — Jon is devastated at being made a steward, believing Ser Alliser arranged it to shame him.
7. **Individual steward assignments** — Bowen Marsh assigns Sam to Maester Aemon, Dareon to Eastwatch to assist Cotter Pyke with trading, and Jon as Mormont's personal steward.
8. **Maester Aemon rebukes Jon** — When Jon protests his assignment, Aemon says they took him for a man of the Night's Watch but perhaps were wrong.
9. **Sam persuades Jon** — Sam argues that Mormont is grooming Jon for command, comparing it to how his own father and Lord Eddard trained their heirs.
10. **Jon accepts his assignment** — Jon acknowledges Sam is right and agrees to take his vows.
11. **Party rides beyond the Wall** — Jon, Sam, Bowen Marsh, and a ranger escort ride through the tunnel and into the haunted forest.
12. **Night's Watch vows recited** — Jon and Sam kneel before the weirwood grove and recite the Night's Watch oath together.
13. **Ghost returns with a severed hand** — After the vows, Ghost emerges from the trees carrying a black severed human hand in his jaws.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Moves through excitement (for Sam), shock and fury (at steward assignment), shame (when Sam persuades him), and unease (in the haunted forest and at chapter's end)
- **Primary preoccupation:** Being assigned to the stewards instead of the rangers; believes Ser Alliser engineered it as a deliberate humiliation
- **Key decisions made:** Chooses to take vows before a heart tree rather than in the sept; initially wants to refuse the steward assignment but accepts it after Sam's argument; agrees to say vows with Sam
- **Self-deception flags:** Jon initially frames the steward assignment entirely as Alliser's revenge, ignoring the possibility that Mormont personally chose him for a reason — Sam has to point this out
