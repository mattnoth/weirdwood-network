# AGOT — Jon VI

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** AGOT
- **chapter_number:** 49
- **pov_character:** Jon Snow
- **pov_chapter_number:** Jon VI
- **first_available:** AGOT Jon VI
- **location_primary:** Castle Black
- **location_secondary:** The haunted forest (weirwood grove beyond the Wall)
- **approximate_timeline:** Day of the recruits' assignment to orders and swearing of Night's Watch vows
- **time_markers:** Morning (breaking fast, morning light in the sept); "at evenfall" (vows to be taken at sunset); "late that afternoon" (party sets out beyond the Wall); "the sun was sinking below the trees" (arrival at weirwood grove); "the last light faded in the west and grey day became black night" (vows spoken at twilight); "Dark's falling" (Dywen urges return)
- **chapter_summary:** Jon and the other recruits are assigned to their orders in the sept at Castle Black. Jon is assigned to the stewards rather than the rangers, which he takes as a deliberate slight by Ser Alliser Thorne. Sam convinces Jon that Lord Commander Mormont is grooming him for command by making Jon his personal steward. Jon, Sam, and a small escort ride through the tunnel in the Wall to a grove of nine weirwoods in the haunted forest, where Jon and Sam swear the Night's Watch oath together. Immediately after the vows, Ghost returns from the forest carrying a severed human hand.

- **Weather:** Warm and sunny day; snow on the ground (thin crust cracking beneath hooves)
- **Season indicators:** Warm day with melting ice on the Wall; thin crust of snow in the haunted forest
- **Time of day:** Morning (breakfast and sept ceremony); late afternoon (departure through the Wall); sunset/twilight (arrival at grove and vow-taking); nightfall (after vows)
- **Lighting:** Morning light streaming through the south-facing window of the sept, creating a rainbow on the altar via the great crystal; afternoon light on the north side of the Wall; setting sun filtering through trees; twilight in the grove; black night after vows
- **Sounds:** Whispering among recruits in the sept; wind rustling leaves in the haunted forest ("like a chilly finger tracing a path up Jon's spine"); snow crust cracking beneath hooves ("with a sound like breaking bones"); the voices of Jon and Sam filling the twilit grove; Sam's sharp intake of breath; silence after the vows
- **Smells:** Fragrant incense from Septon Celladar's censer (reminds Jon of Lady Stark's sept in Winterfell); Dywen notes "something in the smell o' the night that I mislike"
- **Notable sensory details:** Rivulets of water trickling down the Wall making the ice sparkle and shine; the cold of the tunnel through the Wall ("colder than a tomb, and more still"); the vast weight of the Wall pressing down in the tunnel; the forest feels different beyond the Wall — every shadow darker, every sound more ominous; the dried sap in the weirwood eyes "red and hard as ruby"; the forest floor "carpeted with fallen leaves, bloodred on top, black rot beneath"

## Events & Actions
1. **Jon eats breakfast** — Jon is breaking his fast on applecakes and blood sausage when Sam joins him
2. **Sam announces his assignment** — Sam reveals he has been passed out of training and will assist Maester Aemon with the library and the ravens
3. **Sept ceremony begins** — Recruits gather in the sept; Septon Celladar swings a censer; high officers arrive (Maester Aemon with Clydas, Ser Alliser, Lord Commander Mormont, Bowen Marsh, Othell Yarwyck, Ser Jaremy Rykker)
4. **Mormont's speech** — The Lord Commander addresses the recruits about beginning anew, the purpose of the Night's Watch, and the gravity of the oath; warns that desertion means death
5. **No one leaves** — Mormont offers any who wish to leave; no one moves
6. **Jon declares for the old gods** — Jon stands and says he keeps the old gods; Mormont expects he will want to say vows before a heart tree, as his uncle (Benjen) did
7. **Sam asks to join Jon** — Sam asks to say his vows before the heart tree too, forsaking the Seven because they never answered his prayers
8. **Order assignments read** — Mormont reads assignments: Halder and Albett to builders; Grenn, Pyp, Matthar, and Toad to rangers; Sam, Dareon, and Jon to stewards
9. **Jon's shock and anger** — Jon is stunned and furious at being placed with the stewards; he sees Ser Alliser's thin, happy smile and attributes the assignment to Alliser's malice
10. **Rangers and builders depart** — Ser Jaremy leads the rangers out; Othell Yarwyck leads the builders; Jon is left with Sam and Dareon
11. **Individual steward assignments** — Bowen Marsh assigns Sam to the rookery/library with Maester Aemon (Chett moving to kennels, Sam gets his cell); Dareon sent to Eastwatch to assist Cotter Pyke with trading; Jon assigned as Mormont's personal steward in the Lord Commander's tower
12. **Jon protests** — Jon sharply questions whether he is being taken for a servant
13. **Maester Aemon rebukes Jon** — Aemon tells Jon they took him for a man of the Night's Watch, but "perhaps we were wrong"
14. **Jon leaves the sept in fury** — Jon, Dareon, and Sam descend to the yard; Jon looks up at the Wall in rage
15. **Sam explains the real purpose** — Sam argues that Mormont is grooming Jon for command, drawing a parallel to how Sam's own father groomed Dickon by keeping him close during audiences; reminds Jon that Mormont asked for him personally
16. **Jon is ashamed and accepts** — Jon realizes Sam is right; recalls Benjen's words; feels ashamed of his own behavior and agrees to say his words with Sam
17. **Party departs through the Wall** — Late afternoon; Jon, Sam, Bowen Marsh, and a ranger escort lead horses through the narrow, winding tunnel with three sets of locked iron gates
18. **Ghost joins on the north side** — Jon whistles; Ghost comes loping out of the tunnel, startling the Lord Steward's horse, then races off into the forest
19. **Ride through the haunted forest** — The party rides half a league through the forest; the atmosphere feels ominous and different from the wolfswood
20. **Arrival at the weirwood grove** — They reach a clearing with nine weirwoods in a rough circle; Marsh orders horses left outside the sacred space
21. **Jon and Sam swear the Night's Watch oath** — They kneel together and recite the full oath as day turns to night
22. **Marsh declares them brothers** — "You knelt as boys. Rise now as men of the Night's Watch."
23. **Rangers congratulate them** — The escort offers smiles and congratulations, except for Dywen who urges them to head back
24. **Ghost returns with a severed hand** — Ghost appears between two weirwoods carrying something black in his jaws; it is a human hand

- **Emotional state:** Ranges dramatically — pleased for Sam at breakfast → shocked and furious at steward assignment → ashamed after Sam's reasoning → solemn/reverent during vows → disquieted by Ghost's appearance and the severed hand

- **Key decisions made:** Chooses to swear vows before the old gods rather than in the sept; initially wants to protest or reject the steward role; ultimately accepts the assignment after Sam's counsel
