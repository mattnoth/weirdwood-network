# AGOT — Arya V

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** AGOT
- **chapter_number:** 66
- **pov_character:** Arya Stark
- **pov_chapter_number:** Arya V
- **first_available:** AGOT Arya V
- **location_primary:** King's Landing — Flea Bottom, the wharfs, the plaza before the Great Sept of Baelor
- **location_secondary:** Street of Flour, Street of the Sisters, Visenya's Hill, Pig Alley (mentioned), the Red Keep (seen from a distance)
- **approximate_timeline:** After Robert's death and Eddard's arrest; Arya has been living rough in Flea Bottom for multiple days
- **chapter_summary:** Arya survives on the streets of King's Landing, catching pigeons and trading them for food in Flea Bottom pot-shops. She checks the city gates daily but finds them either closed or guarded by Lannister men. She discovers the Wind Witch — the ship her father hired — still at dock, but the guardsmen in Stark colors are strangers who do not recognize her. Bells summon the city to the Great Sept of Baelor, where Eddard publicly confesses to treason and declares Joffrey the true heir. Despite Cersei and Sansa pleading for mercy, Joffrey orders Ser Ilyn Payne to take Eddard's head. As Arya tries to fight through the crowd, Yoren of the Night's Watch seizes her, forces her not to watch, and begins cutting her hair.

## Events & Actions
1. **Arya catches a pigeon** — She knocks down a plump pigeon with her stick sword on the Street of Flour, kills it, and ties it to her belt.
2. **Failed attempt to buy a tart** — Arya tries to trade her pigeon for a tart from a pushcart man, who refuses and warns her about gold cloaks.
3. **Arya spots gold cloaks** — Two City Watch guards are nearby; she avoids them and reflects on the danger they represent.
4. **Summary of Arya's days on the street** — She has been checking all seven city gates daily, sleeping in Flea Bottom, eating pigeon and "bowl o' brown" from pot-shops, and fending off thieves.
5. **Discovery of the Wind Witch** — Arya finds the ship her father hired still docked, but the guardsmen in Stark colors are strangers who do not recognize her. She flees.
6. **Bells ring** — Summoning bells call the city to the Great Sept of Baelor.
7. **Arya learns the Hand is being taken to the sept** — A boy tells her "They'll be taking his head off."
8. **Arya falls and injures herself** — She trips in a rut, scrapes her knee, and tears off half her left thumbnail.
9. **Redwyne twins ride past** — Ser Horas and Ser Hobber Redwyne gallop through with guardsmen in checked blue-and-burgundy cloaks.
10. **Eddard's public confession** — On the pulpit of the Great Sept of Baelor, Eddard confesses to treason, names Joffrey the true heir, and renounces his earlier claims.
11. **Crowd throws stones at Eddard** — A stone gashes his forehead; more stones follow, hitting guards and knights.
12. **Joffrey orders Eddard's execution** — Despite Cersei and Sansa pleading for mercy and the expectation of Night's Watch exile, Joffrey commands Ser Ilyn Payne to take Eddard's head.
13. **Arya draws Needle and fights through the crowd** — She jumps from the statue of Baelor and tries to reach her father, slashing with Needle.
14. **Ser Ilyn Payne beheads Eddard Stark with Ice** — The execution is carried out on the pulpit steps.
15. **Yoren grabs Arya** — Yoren seizes Arya, forces her to look away, calls her "boy," and prevents her from seeing the execution.
16. **Yoren begins cutting Arya's hair** — He shoves her into a doorway and cuts her hair with a knife, beginning her disguise as a boy.
