- **chapter_summary:** Jon Snow sits among the younger squires at the lower tables during the welcoming feast for King Robert at Winterfell, having been excluded from the royal family's table at Lady Stark's direction. He watches the procession of royals and nobles, drinks heavily, feeds his direwolf Ghost under the table, and speaks with his uncle Benjen Stark about joining the Night's Watch. Benjen discourages him, saying he is too young and does not understand what he would sacrifice. Jon flees the hall in tears after an emotional outburst, and encounters Tyrion Lannister in the courtyard, who offers him blunt advice about embracing his identity as a bastard.

## Events & Actions
1. **Jon drinks at the lower tables** — Jon sits among younger squires during the fourth hour of the welcoming feast, drinking summerwine freely, having been excluded from the family table by Lady Stark.
2. **The procession observed** — Jon recalls watching the entrance procession: Eddard with the queen, Robert with Lady Stark, the children paired with royal children, Jaime and Tyrion Lannister, and finally Benjen Stark with Theon Greyjoy.
3. **Ghost fed under the table** — Jon feeds Ghost a whole honeyed chicken under the table. Ghost eats in silence.
4. **Ghost faces down a mongrel** — A black mongrel bitch three times Ghost's size challenges him for the chicken. Ghost bares his fangs silently and the dog retreats.
5. **Benjen visits Jon** — Benjen Stark comes to the lower tables, ruffles Jon's hair, tastes his summerwine, and eats a roasted onion. He observes that Ghost is a very quiet wolf.
6. **Jon asks to join the Night's Watch** — Jon asks Benjen to take him to the Wall. Benjen says Jon is too young, doesn't understand what he'd give up, and should come back after fathering bastards of his own.
7. **Jon's emotional outburst** — Jon declares he will never father a bastard. The table falls silent. Jon, on the verge of tears, flees the hall, stumbling into a serving girl and spilling a flagon of spiced wine.
8. **Jon encounters Tyrion in the courtyard** — Tyrion is sitting on a ledge above the Great Hall door. He asks about Ghost, jumps down acrobatically, and introduces himself. Ghost bares fangs at him but obeys Jon's command to sit still.
9. **Tyrion counsels Jon** — Tyrion tells Jon to make his bastardy his armor so it can never be used to hurt him. They share a moment of mutual recognition as outsiders. Tyrion returns to the feast.

- **Key decisions made:** Declares his intention to join the Night's Watch (though not yet acted upon); decides to leave the feast after his emotional outburst
- **Self-deception flags:** Tells himself he is glad to be a bastard (sitting apart lets him drink freely); tells himself his stinging eyes are from the smoke rather than tears; claims he doesn't care about what the Night's Watch oath would cost him
