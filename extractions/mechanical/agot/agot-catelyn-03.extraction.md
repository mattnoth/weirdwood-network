# AGOT — Catelyn III

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** AGOT
- **chapter_number:** 15
- **pov_character:** Catelyn Stark
- **pov_chapter_number:** Catelyn III
- **first_available:** AGOT Catelyn III
- **location_primary:** Winterfell (Bran's sickroom; Catelyn's chambers in the Great Keep)
- **location_secondary:** King's Landing (mentioned), White Harbor (mentioned), the kingsroad (mentioned), the White Knife river (mentioned)
- **approximate_timeline:** Eight days after Ned and the girls departed Winterfell for King's Landing; shortly after Bran's fall
- **chapter_summary:** Catelyn has been keeping a near-catatonic vigil at Bran's bedside for over a week since his fall. Robb confronts her about neglecting Rickon and her duties. That night, a library fire is set as a distraction; an assassin enters Bran's sickroom and attacks Catelyn, cutting her hands badly, but Bran's direwolf kills the man. After sleeping four days, Catelyn convenes Robb, Ser Rodrik, Theon Greyjoy, and Maester Luwin, reveals the assassin's Valyrian steel dagger as evidence of a wealthy patron, and declares her suspicion that Jaime Lannister threw Bran from the tower. She announces she will travel secretly by sea to King's Landing to investigate, with only Ser Rodrik as escort.

## Events & Actions
1. **Catelyn refuses to review books of account** — Maester Luwin tries to get Catelyn to tend to Winterfell business; she refuses, consumed by Bran's bedside vigil.
2. **Robb intervenes with Maester Luwin** — Robb enters and takes the list of candidates for vacant offices, tells Maester Luwin they will discuss it tomorrow, dismisses him.
3. **Robb confronts Catelyn** — Robb tells Catelyn she has not left Bran's room since the fall, did not come to the gate when Ned and the girls left, and that Rickon needs her; his voice breaks with emotion.
4. **Direwolves howl; all castle dogs begin barking** — Robb opens window; direwolves (Shaggydog and Grey Wind) howl together; then all castle dogs bark simultaneously.
5. **Fire discovered in the library tower** — Robb sees flickering reddish light from the window; identifies library tower on fire; runs to deal with it, taking guards.
6. **Assassin enters Bran's sickroom** — Small, gaunt, blond man in filthy clothes enters while everyone is distracted by the fire; says "You weren't s'posed to be here."
7. **Catelyn fights assassin** — Assassin tries to slit her throat; Catelyn grabs the blade bare-handed, cutting her fingers deeply to the bone; bites the man's hand; he pulls out a handful of her hair.
8. **Bran's direwolf kills the assassin** — The wolf enters through the open door, leaps on the assassin, takes out half his throat.
9. **Catelyn sleeps four days** — After being bathed by Old Nan and having wounds dressed by Maester Luwin, Catelyn sleeps four days under milk of the poppy.
10. **Catelyn's recovery council** — Upon waking, Catelyn meets with Robb, Ser Rodrik, Theon Greyjoy, and Maester Luwin; questions them about the dead assassin.
11. **Assassin's identity and payment discussed** — No one knows his name; he was seen around the castle for weeks; found hiding in stables; had 90 silver stags buried in straw.
12. **Catelyn reveals her theory** — She swears those present to secrecy, then discloses Lysa's suspicion that the Lannisters killed Jon Arryn, and that Jaime did not join the hunt the day Bran fell; she states she believes Bran was thrown, not that he fell.
13. **Ser Rodrik identifies the dagger** — He notes the blade is Valyrian steel with a dragonbone hilt; far too fine for the assassin, meaning someone gave it to him.
14. **Catelyn decides to go to King's Landing** — She rules out sending Robb (must stay as Stark in Winterfell) and a large escort (would alert the Lannisters); chooses to travel secretly by sea via the White Knife to White Harbor, with only Ser Rodrik.
15. **Hallis Mollen appointed captain of the guard** — Robb names Hal the new captain; Robb also orders four-guard rotation on Bran and mandates the direwolf remain in the room.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** At chapter open: grief-stricken, sleep-deprived, near-catatonic, detached from all duties. During the attack: terrified but fighting. After recovery: resolute, clear-headed, ashamed of her prior paralysis, determined.
- **Primary preoccupation:** Bran's survival and safety; then, after recovery, identifying who sent the assassin and warning Ned.
- **Key decisions made:** To travel secretly to King's Landing by sea with only Ser Rodrik; to have four guards plus the direwolf protect Bran; to leave Bran's fate in Maester Luwin's and the gods' hands.
- **Self-deception flags:** Catelyn frames her bedside vigil as "taking care of Bran," but Robb's challenge makes her acknowledge she had been paralyzed by grief rather than actively helping anyone.
