# AGOT — Arya III

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** AGOT
- **chapter_number:** 33
- **pov_character:** Arya Stark
- **pov_chapter_number:** Arya III
- **first_available:** AGOT Arya III
- **location_primary:** Red Keep, King's Landing
- **location_secondary:** Dungeons beneath the Red Keep; tunnels; sewer outlet into the river; Red Keep gatehouse; Tower of the Hand (Eddard's solar)
- **approximate_timeline:** During the Hand's Tourney period; after Arya has been training with Syrio Forel for some time; Catelyn has recently seized Tyrion Lannister
- **chapter_summary:** Arya catches the one-eared black tomcat after a long chase through the Red Keep, but is startled by Princess Myrcella and Prince Tommen, who do not recognize her. She flees from their Lannister guards, becomes lost in the castle's underground passages, and discovers a room full of enormous skulls. She overhears two men discussing political conspiracies — one mentions a bastard, a book, a threatened Hand, and the need to delay while a princess is with child. She escapes through a sewer into the river and returns to the castle, where she reports what she heard to her father, who dismisses the men as mummers. A Night's Watch brother named Yoren arrives with urgent news, and Arya is sent to her chambers before hearing it.

## Events & Actions
1. **Arya catches the one-eared black tomcat** — After a long chase through the castle, Arya corners and catches the infamous black tom, kissing him between the eyes before he can scratch her face.
2. **Encounter with Myrcella and Tommen** — The royal children, a septa, and two Lannister guards discover Arya but do not recognize her, mistaking her for a ragged boy.
3. **Arya flees the Lannister guards** — She dodges Godwyn, bowls over Tommen, evades the second guard, leaps through a narrow window, and loses pursuit.
4. **Arya discovers the dragon skull room** — Lost in the dark, she finds herself in a cavernous room filled with enormous skulls with massive teeth.
5. **Arya overhears two conspirators** — From atop a deep shaft with spiral steps, she hears two men discussing political machinations: the need to delay war, a threatened Hand, a princess with child, and multiple political actors in motion.
6. **The conspirators seal the passage** — The stout man pushes something that causes a great stone slab to drop from the ceiling, sealing the well.
7. **Arya follows the conspirators through tunnels** — She trails them at a distance, overhearing fragments about gold, "fifty birds," young people who know their letters, and risks of keeping tongues.
8. **Arya escapes through a sewer** — She emerges at a sewer mouth emptying into the river, strips, swims to clean herself, and washes her clothes.
9. **Arya is refused entry at the gatehouse** — Gold cloaks mistake her for a beggar boy and refuse her entry until she identifies herself as Arya Stark and names Jory Cassel and Vayon Poole.
10. **Arya reports to Eddard** — She tells her father what she overheard; he dismisses the men as touring mummers.
11. **Yoren arrives with urgent news** — A Night's Watch brother arrives and hints at news involving an inn, sellswords, and riders heading to Casterly Rock — news best spoken in private.
12. **Arya is sent to her chambers** — Eddard sends her away with Desmond before hearing Yoren's full report.
