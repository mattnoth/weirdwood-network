## Events & Actions
1. **Arrival at Vaes Dothrak** — Khal Drogo leads the khalasar through the Horse Gate and down the godsway into the city.
2. **Tour of the godsway** — Dany observes the plundered monuments from conquered civilizations lining the road. Viserys dismisses them as "trash of dead cities."
3. **Viserys demands his army** — Viserys presses for when Drogo will give him the promised army, growing impatient. Ser Jorah explains the Dothraki do not see the arrangement as a trade.
4. **Military discussion** — Ser Jorah analyzes Dothraki vs. Westerosi military capabilities for Dany, concluding Dothraki could defeat Robert in open battle but cannot take castles. He names Robert as brave but rash, and identifies Stannis, Tywin, and Eddard Stark as wiser counselors.
5. **Jorah reveals bitterness toward Stark** — Jorah says Stark "took from me all I loved" and spits, then quickly changes subject.
6. **Weapons surrender** — All riders including Drogo surrender weapons upon arrival; it is forbidden to carry blades or shed free blood in Vaes Dothrak.
7. **Drogo ascends Mother of Mountains** — Cohollo informs Dany that Drogo must ascend the Mother of Mountains to sacrifice to the gods. Only men are allowed; he will return at dawn.
8. **Dany prepares gifts for Viserys** — She has Dothraki-style clothing made to his measurements and invites him to supper, hoping to improve his standing among the Dothraki.
9. **Viserys strikes Doreah** — He arrives dragging Doreah by the arm, having hit her in the eye, furious that a "whore" was sent to "command" him.
10. **Viserys rejects gifts and grabs Dany** — He sneers at the clothing as "Dothraki rags," grabs her arm painfully, and threatens to "wake the dragon."
11. **Dany strikes Viserys** — She swings the bronze medallion belt into his face, cutting his cheek. She threatens to summon her khas and warns that Drogo might cut him open.
12. **Viserys retreats** — He threatens future retribution and leaves, holding his torn face.
13. **Dany cradles dragon egg** — She asks for the green dragon egg, curls up with it against her pregnant belly, and feels the child move. She whispers "You are the dragon, the true dragon" to her unborn child.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Anxious about Viserys's behavior and Drogo's patience; hurt by Viserys's rejection of her gifts; growing in confidence and willingness to assert herself; tender and protective toward her unborn child
- **Primary preoccupation:** Managing the tension between Viserys's demands and Dothraki customs; her developing identity as khaleesi rather than Viserys's subordinate
- **Key decisions made:** Decided to gift Viserys Dothraki clothing to help his standing; struck Viserys and threatened him when he grabbed her; chose the dragon egg for comfort
- **Self-deception flags:** She defends Viserys to Jorah ("It is not right to make him wait") even as she recognizes his cruelty; she still thinks of him as "her king" despite his abuse; she identifies the unborn child (not Viserys) as "the true dragon" while still outwardly deferring to Viserys's claim
