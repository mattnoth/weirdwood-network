# AGOT — Jon IX

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** AGOT
- **chapter_number:** 71
- **pov_character:** Jon Snow
- **pov_chapter_number:** Jon IX
- **first_available:** AGOT Jon IX
- **location_primary:** Castle Black, the kingsroad south of the Wall
- **location_secondary:** Mole's Town, Winterfell (recalled), King's Tower
- **approximate_timeline:** Shortly after news of Eddard Stark's death reaches the Wall; Jon attempts to desert at night and is brought back before dawn
- **time_markers:** Night (full moon); moonlight throughout the ride south; "The Old Bear was accustomed to rise at first light, so Jon had until dawn"; "Dawn was still an hour off" when they returned; "When day broke, Jon walked to the kitchens as he did every dawn"
- **chapter_summary:** Jon Snow attempts to desert the Night's Watch to join Robb's war after learning of their father's execution. He rides south on the kingsroad past Mole's Town but is intercepted by his friends — Sam, Pyp, Grenn, Halder, Toad, Matthar, and one other — who recite the Night's Watch oath to persuade him to return. Back at Castle Black, Lord Commander Mormont reveals he knew Jon would try to leave and confronts him with reports of alarming activity beyond the Wall. Mormont announces he will lead a great ranging beyond the Wall and asks Jon to commit. Jon swears he will not run again.

## Events & Actions
1. **Jon saddles a mare in the old stables at Castle Black** — preparing to desert the Night's Watch in the night, with Ghost at his side
2. **Sam confronts Jon at the stable door** — tries to prevent Jon from leaving; Jon threatens to ride him down
3. **Jon rides past Sam** — Sam jumps aside at the last moment, stumbles and falls; Jon rides out of Castle Black
4. **Jon rides south on the kingsroad** — follows the road by moonlight; no one sees him leave except Sam; men on the Wall watch north
5. **Jon reflects on his decision** — weighs honor, thinks of Winterfell, Aemon's choices, Tyrion's words about denying hard truths; resolves he is "bastard and oathbreaker" but will join Robb to avenge their father
6. **Jon recalls the beheading of the deserter** — remembers Lord Eddard's words about the vow, the execution, Theon kicking the head; wonders what Eddard would have done if it were Benjen
7. **Jon gallops recklessly** — pushes the mare hard; Ghost keeps pace for half a mile then falls behind
8. **Jon rides through Mole's Town** — village mostly still; dog barks; red lantern over the brothel
9. **Jon stops beyond Mole's Town** — dismounts, drinks snowmelt, walks the mare to cool her down
10. **Ghost disappears into the woods** — Jon calls for him repeatedly with no response
11. **Jon eats provisions under the trees** — biscuit, cheese, and apple; saves meat for later
12. **Jon hears approaching riders from the north** — hides off the road behind sentinel trees with his mare
13. **Jon's friends arrive searching for him** — Grenn, Pyp, Halder, Toad, Matthar, and others (seven total); Sam had woken them
14. **Ghost inadvertently reveals Jon's position** — bounds out of the shadows, startling Jon's mare
15. **Jon's friends surround him** — Jon draws his sword but they begin reciting the Night's Watch oath; seven against one
16. **Jon yields** — lowers his sword, unable to harm his friends; agrees to ride back
17. **The group rides back to Castle Black** — arrives with dawn still an hour off; Mole's Town brothel lantern extinguished
18. **Sam greets them at the stables** — had been waiting anxiously; says he's glad they found Jon
19. **Jon internally plans to try again** — thinks he'll bide his time and take a different route next time
20. **Jon fetches Mormont's breakfast at dawn** — Three-Finger Hobb gives him the food without comment
21. **Mormont reveals he knew about Jon's desertion attempt** — Aemon predicted it; Mormont had a watch set; Jon would have been caught regardless
22. **Mormont shares alarming reports from beyond the Wall** — elk and mammoth herds moving south, misshapen footprints near Eastwatch, abandoned wildling villages, huge fires in the mountains, Mance Rayder massing his people
23. **Mormont announces a great ranging beyond the Wall** — intends to find Benjen Stark and confront whatever threatens; will command personally; wants Jon and Ghost with him
24. **Jon swears to stay** — pledges himself to Mormont, asks forgiveness of his family in his thoughts
25. **Mormont tells Jon to go put on his sword** — referring to Longclaw

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Torn between duty and family; guilty about betraying Mormont's trust; angry at his friends for bringing him back; deeply conflicted about honor and identity; grieving his father; ultimately resolves into acceptance and commitment
- **Primary preoccupation:** Whether deserting is the right thing; his identity as bastard and outsider; fear of dying like a common deserter rather than in battle; longing for Winterfell and family
- **Key decisions made:** (1) Decides to desert the Night's Watch to join Robb; (2) Deliberately leaves Longclaw behind as a point of honor; (3) After being brought back, internally plans to try again via a different route; (4) After Mormont's speech about the threat beyond the Wall, abandons escape plans and swears himself to the Watch
- **Self-deception flags:** Jon tells himself "I warned him" about Sam to justify nearly riding him down; initially plans to desert again even after his friends bring him back, telling himself he'll "bide his time"; he cannot actually visualize Robb smiling and welcoming him, suggesting he knows the reunion would not be simple
