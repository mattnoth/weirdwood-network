- **chapter_summary:** On the last day before the Stark household departs Winterfell, Bran attempts to say his farewells but is overcome with sadness. He spends the morning in the godswood trying to train his unnamed direwolf, then goes climbing through Winterfell's rooftops one last time. At the broken tower, he overhears a man and woman (later revealed as Queen Cersei and Jaime Lannister) discussing the danger of Eddard Stark becoming Hand of the King. When Bran peers in the window and sees them naked together, they discover him. Jaime pushes Bran from the window, and he falls toward the courtyard below.

## Events & Actions
1. **Hunt departs at dawn** — King Robert leads a hunt for wild boar for the evening feast. Joffrey, Robb, Benjen, Jory, Theon, Ser Rodrik, and Tyrion ride out.
2. **Bran is left behind** — Bran remains at Winterfell with Jon, the girls, and Rickon. Jon and his wolf are nowhere to be found; Jon seems angry at everyone.
3. **Bran attempts farewells** — Bran wanders the castle with his wolf, intending to visit those staying behind (Old Nan, Gage, Mikken, Hodor, the glass gardens man), but seeing his pony in the stable makes him too sad to continue.
4. **Bran spends the morning in the godswood** — He tries to teach his unnamed direwolf to fetch a stick. The wolf understands him but shows no interest in the game.
5. **Bran considers wolf names** — He reflects on all six direwolf names: Grey Wind (Robb's), Lady (Sansa's), Nymeria (Arya's), Shaggydog (Rickon's), Ghost (Jon's). He has tried a hundred names in a fortnight for his own wolf but none fit.
6. **Bran decides to climb the broken tower** — He hasn't been up for weeks and this may be his last chance before leaving for King's Landing.
7. **Bran crosses the godswood and leaves his wolf** — He avoids the heart tree (which frightens him — trees shouldn't have eyes or hand-shaped leaves). He orders his wolf to stay at the base of the sentinel tree.
8. **The wolf howls as Bran climbs** — The wolf obeys briefly but then howls repeatedly as Bran climbs the sentinel. Bran tells him to be quiet and compares him to his mother. The howling follows him until he jumps to the armory roof.
9. **Extended narration of Bran's climbing history** — His mother's fear, his broken promise to stay on the ground, his punishment in the godswood where he climbed a tree instead, his father's permission ("you're a squirrel"), Old Nan's cautionary tale, Maester Luwin's pottery-boy demonstration, guards chasing him on rooftops.
10. **Bran traverses rooftops toward the broken tower** — He moves from the armory across the guards hall, up the blind side of the First Keep, swinging gargoyle to gargoyle toward the north side.
11. **Bran overhears voices from the First Keep** — A man and woman are arguing about the danger of Eddard Stark becoming Hand of the King. They discuss Robert, Stannis, Renly, Littlefinger, Lysa Arryn, Jon Arryn, and the fostering of Lysa's son at Casterly Rock.
12. **The conversation turns intimate** — The man tells the woman to stop talking and come to him. Bran hears a slap and laughter. The man addresses her as "sister."
13. **Bran maneuvers to see into the window** — He climbs over the gargoyle, crosses the roof, and hangs upside down from a gargoyle above the window.
14. **Bran sees a naked man and woman** — They are having a sexual encounter. He recognizes the woman's golden hair and face as the queen. He recognizes the man as her mirror image (Jaime Lannister).
15. **Bran is discovered** — Cersei sees Bran and screams. Bran tries to pull himself up but his hand slips. He falls, catches the ledge, and dangles one-handed.
16. **Jaime pulls Bran up** — Jaime reaches down, pulls Bran onto the window sill. Bran tells him he is seven years old.
17. **Jaime pushes Bran from the window** — Jaime says "The things I do for love" with loathing and shoves Bran backward out the window. Bran falls screaming toward the courtyard.
18. **Chapter ends with Bran falling** — A wolf howls in the distance. Crows circle the broken tower, waiting for corn.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Transitions from excitement about leaving → sudden sadness and loss at leaving Winterfell → joy and freedom while climbing → curiosity about the overheard voices → fear when he realizes he's hearing something dangerous → shock and terror at seeing the queen naked → relief when Jaime pulls him up → (implied) terror as he is pushed
- **Primary preoccupation:** Saying goodbye to Winterfell and everything familiar; his love of climbing; naming his direwolf
- **Key decisions made:** Decides to go climbing one last time instead of continuing farewells; decides to get closer to hear the voices; decides to look in the window
- **Self-deception flags:** "I never fall" — Bran's absolute confidence in his climbing ability; believes he is invisible when climbing ("People never looked up")
