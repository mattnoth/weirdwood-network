# AGOT — Eddard IX

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** AGOT
- **chapter_number:** 36
- **pov_character:** Eddard Stark
- **pov_chapter_number:** Eddard IX
- **first_available:** AGOT Eddard IX
- **location_primary:** King's Landing — streets
- **location_secondary:** Chataya's brothel, Red Keep
- **approximate_timeline:** Shortly after Ned has resigned as Hand of the King; Catelyn has seized Tyrion Lannister
- **time_markers:** Night (starless black sky, rain, darkness); dawn (first grey light of dawn when arriving at the Red Keep); "half a year" referenced by Barra's mother as the time Chataya would give her; "three years ago" when Robert went west for Tywin's tourney
- **chapter_summary:** Ned departs Chataya's brothel where he visited Robert's bastard daughter Barra, riding back through rainy King's Landing streets with Littlefinger, Jory Cassel, Heward, and Wyl. He recalls the young mother nursing Barra and Lyanna's warnings about Robert's nature. He questions Littlefinger about Robert's other bastard children and why Jon Arryn investigated them. Jaime Lannister ambushes the party with twenty Lannister soldiers, demanding answers about Tyrion's capture. Ned confirms Tyrion was taken at his command; Jaime orders Ned's men killed while sparing Ned. In the ensuing fight, Jory, Heward, and Wyl are slain, and Ned's horse falls on him, shattering his leg. Littlefinger returns with the City Watch to find Ned cradling Jory's body.

## Events & Actions
1. **Ned descends to the brothel common room** — Finds Littlefinger chatting with a tall, dark-skinned, elegant woman in a feathered gown, Heward playing forfeits with a buxom wench (she has unbuttoned her shift to the waist, he has lost belt, cloak, mail shirt, and right boot), and Jory standing by a rain-streaked window with a wry smile.
2. **Ned announces departure** — Pulls on his gloves at the foot of the stair and tells his men their business is done.
3. **Littlefinger makes crude jest about the Hand** — Jokes about the Hand performing all the king's functions; Ned rebukes him sharply and states he is no longer the King's Hand. Littlefinger remarks "The direwolf must be a prickly beast."
4. **Party mounts up in the warm rain** — Jory and Wyl bring horses from the stables; a barefoot whore giggles at Wyl from the stable door as he fumbles with his belt and lacings.
5. **Littlefinger praises Chataya's brothel** — Discusses buying it, comparing brothels favorably to ships as investments: "Whores seldom sink."
6. **Riding through dark, deserted streets** — Littlefinger quiets; they ride in silence through King's Landing. The warm rain beats down on Ned's head, described as "warm as blood and relentless as old guilts."
7. **Ned recalls Lyanna's words at Winterfell** — Remembers the night their father promised her hand to Robert. Lyanna said Robert had gotten a child on a girl in the Vale. Ned had held that babe. Lyanna told him: "Love is sweet, dearest Ned, but it cannot change a man's nature."
8. **Ned recalls visiting Barra and her mother** — The young red-haired, freckled mother nurses infant Barra, whose fine dark hair flows through Ned's fingers "like black silk." Ned recalls that Robert's firstborn also had fine dark hair. The mother asks Ned to tell Robert about Barra and says she's been with no one else and is waiting for him.
9. **Ned promises Barra will not go wanting** — He pledges to the mother, whose tremulous smile "cut the heart out of him." He thinks: "Robert would swear undying love and forget them before evenfall, but Ned Stark kept his vows."
10. **Ned thinks of promises to Lyanna** — Reflects on "the promises he'd made Lyanna as she lay dying, and the price he'd paid to keep them."
11. **Ned sees Jon Snow's face** — Riding through the rain, he sees Jon's face before him, "so like a younger version of his own," and thinks: "If the gods frowned so on bastards, why did they fill men with such lusts?"
12. **Ned asks Littlefinger about Robert's bastards** — Littlefinger reveals: Robert has acknowledged a boy at Storm's End, fathered the night Stannis wed on a Florent niece of Lady Selyse (a bedmaid; Renly says Robert carried her upstairs during the feast and broke in the wedding bed while Stannis and his bride still danced; Stannis shipped the boy to Renly). Whispers that Robert fathered twins on a serving wench at Casterly Rock three years ago at Tywin's tourney; Cersei had the babes killed and sold the mother to a passing slaver.
13. **Ned questions Jon Arryn's interest in the bastards** — Asks why Jon Arryn would take a sudden interest. Littlefinger dismisses it. Ned: "It had to be more than that, or why kill him?"
14. **Ned briefly thinks of Rhaegar Targaryen** — For the first time in years, wonders if Rhaegar frequented brothels; thinks not.
15. **Jaime Lannister ambushes the party** — Rain falling harder; Jory calls out in alarm. The street fills with soldiers: at least ten in front wearing ringmail over leather, golden lion-crested helms, rain-sodden cloaks, carrying longswords and iron-tipped spears. More behind, cutting off retreat.
16. **Jaime rides forward** — On a blood bay stallion, mud muffling hooves, wearing a golden breastplate with lion of Lannister. The line parts before him.
17. **Littlefinger identifies Ned as the Hand** — Jaime corrects: "He was the Hand of the King. Now, if truth be told, I'm not sure what he is."
18. **Jaime demands information about Tyrion** — Describes Tyrion: "Fair-haired, mismatched eyes, sharp of tongue. A short man." Says his father is "quite vexed."
19. **Ned confirms Tyrion's arrest** — States: "Your brother has been taken at my command, to answer for his crimes."
20. **Jaime challenges Ned to draw steel** — "Show me your steel, Lord Eddard. I'll butcher you like Aerys if I must, but I'd sooner you died with a blade in your hand." Dismisses Littlefinger with contempt.
21. **Littlefinger flees to fetch the City Watch** — The Lannister line parts for him, closes behind. He puts heels to his mare and vanishes around a corner.
22. **Ned calculates the tactical situation** — Three mounted against twenty on foot, save for Jaime. A charge might win freedom, but Ned sees a surer tactic.
23. **Ned warns Jaime** — "Kill me, and Catelyn will most certainly slay Tyrion." Jaime pokes Ned's chest with the gilded sword. Jaime doubts Catelyn would murder a hostage but is "not willing to chance my brother's life on a woman's honor."
24. **Jaime sheathes his sword and withdraws** — Tells Ned: "I suppose I'll let you run back to Robert to tell him how I frightened you. I wonder if he'll care." Pushes wet hair back, wheels his horse.
25. **Jaime orders Ned's men killed** — Tells Tregar: "See that no harm comes to Lord Stark… Still… we wouldn't want him to leave here entirely unchastened, so— kill his men."
26. **Street battle erupts** — Jaime canters away. Ned claws for his sword. Men close from both sides. Ned rides one down, cutting at soldiers in red cloaks. Jory charges; a steel-shod hoof catches a Lannister guardsman in the face with a sickening crunch; a second man reels away. Wyl is pulled from his dying horse, swords slashing in the rain.
27. **Ned strikes Tregar** — Brings his longsword down on Tregar's helm. The jolt of impact makes him grit his teeth. Tregar stumbles to his knees, lion crest sheared in half, blood running down his face.
28. **Heward killed** — A spear catches Heward in the belly while he hacks at the hands seizing his bridle.
29. **Jory fights and falls** — Jory returns among them, "a red rain flying from his sword." Ned shouts "No! Jory, away!" Lannister soldiers cut the legs from Jory's mount and drag him to the earth, swords rising and falling as they close in around him.
30. **Ned's horse falls on him** — The horse slips in the mud and comes crashing down. Blinding pain and taste of blood. Splintered bone poking through his calf. He loses consciousness.
31. **Ned wakes alone with his dead** — His horse catches the rank scent of blood and gallops away. Ned drags himself through the mud, gritting his teeth. Faces watch from candlelit windows; people emerge from alleys and doors but no one helps.
32. **Littlefinger and City Watch find Ned** — He is cradling Jory Cassel's body in his arms in the street.
33. **Ned carried to the Red Keep on a litter** — A blur of agony; loses consciousness more than once. Sees the Red Keep looming in the first grey light of dawn, its pale pink stone darkened by rain to the color of blood.
34. **Pycelle treats Ned** — Gives him milk of the poppy from a cup; tells someone to heat wine to boiling and fetch clean silk.
