# AGOT — Bran V

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** AGOT
- **chapter_number:** 38
- **pov_character:** Bran Stark
- **pov_chapter_number:** Bran V
- **first_available:** AGOT Bran V
- **location_primary:** Winterfell and the wolfswood
- **location_secondary:** Winter town, the kingsroad
- **approximate_timeline:** After news of Catelyn's capture of Tyrion; after Ned's injury in King's Landing
- **time_markers:** "the past fortnight" (Bran riding Dancer on his own); "last night" (Summer howling); "last night" (raven from King's Landing); references to the long summer ending and winter approaching
- **chapter_summary:** Bran rides outside Winterfell for the first time since his fall, using the special saddle designed by Tyrion. Robb tells him that Jory Cassel, Wyl, and Heward have been killed by the Kingslayer, and that Eddard's leg was shattered when a horse fell on him. While Bran waits alone in the wolfswood, he is ambushed by six wildlings and Night's Watch deserters who try to rob and capture him. Robb returns with the direwolves and a fight breaks out; Theon Greyjoy kills the last attacker with an arrow to save Bran, and the surviving wildling woman Osha surrenders and is taken prisoner.

## Physical Environment
- **Weather:** Light snow falling, growing heavier as the chapter progresses; flakes melting on skin and on the ground
- **Season indicators:** End of the long summer described as near; "Winter is coming"; Old Nan says when snow falls and ice winds come, farmers leave frozen fields for the winter town; Maester Luwin says the day is looming closer
- **Time of day:** Daytime (riding out from Winterfell)
- **Lighting:** Overcast/snowy daylight; natural light filtering through trees in the wolfswood
- **Sounds:** Rushing water of the stream; direwolf howling (Summer, then Grey Wind joining); soft feet on wet leaves; rustle of leaves; hoofbeats
- **Smells:** Sharp fresh tang of pine needles; earthy odor of wet rotting leaves; hints of animal musk; distant cooking fires; stench of Stiv described as smelling of fear
- **Notable sensory details:** Bran cannot feel his legs or the cold water on his foot; he feels the steel of Stiv's dagger at his throat; spray from the stream on his face; blood trickling where the knife pressed against his neck; the swaying motion of the horse initially makes Bran feel unsteady; the strap around his chest is tight and chafing; melting snow soaked through his gloves

## Events & Actions
1. **Bran rides out of Winterfell** — Bran rides through the gatehouse, over the drawbridge, and through the outer walls on Dancer, his specially trained horse, for the first time since his fall. Robb rides beside him, with Theon, four guardsmen, Joseth, and Maester Luwin following.
2. **The party rides through winter town** — They pass the deserted market square and rows of mostly unoccupied houses. Villagers bend the knee to the boys. Theon calls out to serving wenches at the Smoking Log alehouse.
3. **Bran gains confidence riding** — The swaying initially makes him unsteady, but the saddle cradles him and the straps hold him. He begins to smile.
4. **Bran and Robb gallop ahead** — They trot and then gallop, leaving the others behind. Bran catches Robb at the edge of the wolfswood, two miles beyond winter town. Bran shouts "I can ride!"
5. **Robb tells Bran about the raven** — A bird arrived the previous night from King's Landing, sent by Alyn. Jory Cassel, Wyl, and Heward have been murdered by the Kingslayer. Eddard's leg was shattered when a horse fell on him; Maester Pycelle has given him milk of the poppy; it is uncertain when he will wake.
6. **Robb discusses calling the banners** — Theon urges calling the banners ("Blood for blood"). Bran notes only the lord can call the banners. Robb speaks of the honor of the north being in his hands.
7. **Bran and Robb enter the wolfswood** — They leave the kingsroad and ride into the wolfswood to find the direwolves. Theon and the others fall behind.
8. **Bran remembers fishing at the stream** — They reach a stream where Jory once brought Bran, Robb, and Jon to fish for trout. Bran weeps remembering Jory and Jon.
9. **Robb crosses the stream with Bran** — Robb dismounts, leads his horse across, wades back, and leads Bran and Dancer across the ford. The water reaches midthigh at the deepest point.
10. **Direwolves howl** — Summer howls, then Grey Wind joins. Robb says they've made a kill and rides off to find them, leaving Bran alone.
11. **Wildlings ambush Bran** — Six ragged strangers appear: Stiv (big bald man), Wallen (grey-stubbled man in black rags), Osha (tall woman with spear and halfhelm), Hali (short woman with knife), a man with an axe, and a sixth unnamed man. They demand Bran's silver pin and horse.
12. **Bran identifies them as Night's Watch deserters** — He notices two wear faded black and remembers his father beheading a deserter.
13. **Osha suggests taking Bran to Mance Rayder** — She argues the boy is worth more alive as a hostage, being Benjen Stark's blood. Stiv dismisses this, mentioning white walkers.
14. **Stiv cuts Bran's saddle strap and wounds his leg** — The slash cuts through leather and into Bran's flesh. Blood flows but Bran feels no pain.
15. **Robb returns and demands surrender** — He appears mounted with the elk carcass and sword drawn, demanding they put down their steel.
16. **Battle at the stream** — Robb kills the axe-man with a sword blow to the face. Grey Wind drags the grey-stubbled man into the stream and kills him. Robb rides Osha down in the stream after she overextends with her spear. Summer attacks Hali, tearing at her belly and killing her. Grey Wind chases and kills the sixth man who fled.
17. **Stiv takes Bran hostage** — Stiv pulls Bran from the saddle and holds a dagger to his throat, demanding Robb call off the wolves. Robb calls the direwolves to him; Grey Wind obeys, Summer stays watching.
18. **Theon kills Stiv** — Theon shoots Stiv through the back with a broadhead arrow. The arrow explodes out of Stiv's chest. He collapses dead in the stream.
19. **Osha surrenders** — She throws down her spear and asks for mercy, offering her service in exchange for her life.
20. **Robb and Theon argue** — Robb angrily asks what would have happened if Theon had missed or hit Bran. Theon is chastened.
21. **Robb rebukes the guardsmen** — He demands to know where they were. Quent explains they waited for Maester Luwin and then Theon delayed them by hunting a turkey.
22. **Maester Luwin treats Bran's wound** — He calls it no more than a scratch and notes two of the dead wear the black.
23. **Robb orders the heads taken** — He commands the deserters' heads be hacked off and sent to the Wall, with the rest left for carrion crows.
24. **Osha is taken prisoner** — Robb decides to bring her back to Winterfell for questioning, ordering Wayn to bind her hands. She will live or die by the truths she gives.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Begins with nervous excitement and rising joy at riding again; shifts to dread and grief upon hearing news of Jory's death and Ned's injury; terror during the ambush; relief at rescue
- **Primary preoccupation:** The thrill of riding outside Winterfell again; then fear for his father and family; then survival during the ambush
- **Key decisions made:** Decides to keep riding despite Maester Luwin's advice to keep the ride short; refuses to show weakness in front of Robb
- **Self-deception flags:** Bran insists "eight isn't so much younger than fifteen" — asserting maturity he does not yet have; tells Robb "I can go as long as you can" despite the maester's warning about saddle sores
