# AGOT — Bran V

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** AGOT
- **chapter_number:** 38
- **pov_character:** Bran Stark
- **pov_chapter_number:** Bran V
- **first_available:** AGOT Bran V
- **location_primary:** Wolfswood (stream crossing near Winterfell)
- **location_secondary:** Winterfell, winter town, kingsroad
- **approximate_timeline:** After news of Catelyn taking Tyrion prisoner; after Eddard's injury in King's Landing
- **time_markers:** Bran has not been outside Winterfell since his fall; has been riding Dancer in the yard for a fortnight; the long summer is ending; Bran is eight years old; Robb is fifteen; the raven from King's Landing arrived "last night"; the direwolves were howling/restless "last night"
- **chapter_summary:** Bran rides outside Winterfell for the first time since his fall, using the specially designed saddle. Robb tells him that Jory Cassel, Wyl, and Heward have been killed by the Kingslayer, and that Eddard's leg was shattered. While Bran waits alone by a stream, six wildling deserters ambush him. Robb and the direwolves fight the attackers, killing most of them. Theon Greyjoy kills the last man, Stiv, with an arrow as he holds a knife to Bran's throat. The surviving wildling woman Osha surrenders and is taken prisoner back to Winterfell.

- **Season indicators:** End of the long summer is near; winter is coming; Old Nan says farmers leave frozen fields when snow falls and ice winds come; Maester Luwin says the day is looming closer
- **Time of day:** Daytime (riding out from Winterfell)
- **Lighting:** Overcast/snowy daylight
- **Sounds:** Rushing water of the stream; direwolf howls; hoofbeats; soft feet on wet leaves; the thrum of Theon's bowstring
- **Smells:** Sharp fresh tang of pine needles; earthy odor of wet rotting leaves; hints of animal musk; distant cooking fires; stench of Stiv (smelled of fear)
- **Notable sensory details:** Bran cannot feel his legs in the stirrups but feels the strap chafing his chest; melting snow soaks through his gloves; spray from the stream on his face; cannot feel the cold water on his foot when he falls; feels the steel of Stiv's dagger at his throat; feels blood trickle where the knife presses his neck

## Events & Actions
1. **Bran rides outside Winterfell for the first time since his fall** — He is strapped into the specially designed saddle on Dancer, with Robb, Theon, four guardsmen, Joseth, and Maester Luwin accompanying him.
2. **The party rides through the winter town** — The market square is deserted; less than one in five houses occupied; villagers bend the knee to the Stark boys.
3. **Theon calls out to serving wenches at the Smoking Log** — Kyra blushes; Theon begins telling a sexual anecdote before Robb stops him.
4. **Bran gallops Dancer for the first time** — He catches up to Robb at the edge of the wolfswood, two miles beyond winter town, leaving the others behind.
5. **Robb tells Bran about the raven from King's Landing** — Jory Cassel, Wyl, and Heward murdered by the Kingslayer; Eddard's leg shattered beneath a falling horse; given milk of the poppy by Maester Pycelle.
6. **Robb and Bran discuss calling the banners** — Theon urges it ("blood for blood"); Bran notes only the lord can call banners; Robb says the north's honor is in his hands.
7. **Robb and Bran enter the wolfswood to find the direwolves** — They leave the kingsroad; Bran observes the forest's sights and smells.
8. **They reach a stream and cross it** — Robb leads Bran and Dancer across the ford; Bran remembers fishing here with Robb and Jon.
9. **The direwolves howl from a kill** — Robb rides off to retrieve them, leaving Bran alone at the stream.
10. **Six wildling deserters ambush Bran** — They demand his silver pin, horse, and threaten him; Bran identifies himself as Brandon Stark of Winterfell.
11. **Osha suggests taking Bran hostage to Mance Rayder** — Stiv dismisses this, mentioning white walkers as a greater concern.
12. **Stiv cuts Bran's leg strap and slashes his thigh** — Bran sees flesh and blood but feels nothing due to his paralysis.
13. **Robb returns and attacks** — He kills the axe-man with a sword blow to the face; Grey Wind kills Wallen in the stream; Robb rides down Osha in midstream; Summer attacks and kills Hali; Grey Wind kills the fleeing sixth man.
14. **Stiv takes Bran hostage with a knife to his throat** — He demands Robb call off the wolves and kill them.
15. **Theon shoots Stiv through the back with a broadhead arrow** — The arrow exits through Stiv's chest; he collapses dead in the stream.
16. **Robb angrily rebukes Theon for the risky shot** — Points out Theon could have missed, hit Bran, or caused Stiv's hand to jerk.
17. **Osha surrenders and begs for her life** — Claims she broke no oaths since the Night's Watch has no place for women; Stiv and Wallen were the actual deserters.
18. **Maester Luwin advises questioning Osha** — Robb agrees; orders Wayn to bind her hands; she will return to Winterfell.
19. **Robb orders the deserters' heads hacked off and sent to the Wall** — The rest left for carrion crows.
