- **time_markers:** No specific time of day stated; travel implied over the course of the day as they descend from foothills to the camp; references to recent fighting ("there had been fighting here, and recently"); Timett "had yet to see his twentieth year"
- **chapter_summary:** Tyrion descends from the Mountains of the Moon with nearly three hundred clansmen and Bronn, arriving at Lord Tywin's army camp of twenty thousand men at the crossroads inn. He reunites with his father and uncle Ser Kevan, learning that Robert Baratheon is dead, Jaime has smashed the Tully forces and besieges Riverrun, and Ned Stark is imprisoned. When Tyrion begins requesting arms and armor for his clansmen, the clansmen burst into the common room. A messenger arrives reporting the Stark host is moving south down the causeway, and Lord Tywin orders the army to march. The clansmen agree to fight with the Lannisters, but only if Tyrion rides with them.

## Events & Actions
1. **Chella scouts ahead** — Reports an army of twenty thousand with red banners and golden lion at the crossroads.
2. **Tyrion decides to ride down** — Suggests going alone but the clansmen insist on sending representatives; five clan leaders plus Bronn accompany him.
3. **Descent through foothills** — The party passes watchtowers of unmortared stone; a raven takes wing from one. They reach a strong point with an earthen wall and crossbowmen.
4. **Recognition and escort** — The captain at the strong point recognizes Tyrion as his lord's son and provides an escort.
5. **Passage through war-torn lands** — They ride past blackened fields, burned holdfasts, and signs of recent fighting (ravens, carrion crows, no visible bodies).
6. **Arrival at the Lannister camp perimeter** — A barricade of sharpened stakes; Ser Flement Brax leads a mounted party to challenge them.
7. **Ser Flement recognizes Tyrion** — Expresses astonishment that Tyrion is alive; is uncertain about the clansmen.
8. **Entry through the camp** — Three rows of stakes removed; Tyrion's party rides through leagues of camp, passing banners of Lannister vassal houses.
9. **Arrival at the inn** — Tyrion sees the gibbet with a female corpse (the former innkeep); addresses her corpse about the hospitality she owed.
10. **Shagga reluctant to yield his horse** — Tyrion assures him the stableboy will care for it.
11. **Tyrion enters the common room** — Finds Tywin and Kevan sharing ale.
12. **Reunion with Tywin** — Cold, formal; Tywin gives Tyrion a long look; no embrace; Tyrion drinks his father's ale.
13. **War briefing** — Kevan and Tywin summarize: Jaime's victories at the Golden Tooth and Riverrun, Edmure Tully captured, Blackwood retreated, Harrenhal yielded, Gregor burned out Pipers and Brackens. Mallisters hold Seagard, Frey marshals at the Twins.
14. **Robert's death revealed** — Tywin tells Tyrion that Robert Baratheon is dead and "your nephew" reigns.
15. **Tywin offers Tyrion a command** — To deal with Marq Piper and Karyl Vance raiding across the Red Fork; also mentions Beric Dondarrion and Thoros.
16. **Tyrion requests arms for clansmen** — Begins listing: three thousand helms, hauberks, swords, pikes, spearheads, maces, battle-axes, gauntlets, gorgets, greaves, breastplates, wagons.
17. **Shagga bursts in** — Throws the guard captain across the room into the hearth, snaps his sword in two, threatens him.
18. **Clansmen enter the common room** — Bronn follows them in with a rueful shrug.
19. **Tyrion introduces the clansmen to Tywin** — Names each clan leader and their clan; introduces Bronn as a sellsword who has "changed sides twice."
20. **Tyrion introduces Tywin** — Full formal titles: Lord of Casterly Rock, Warden of the West, Shield of Lannisport, "once and future Hand of the King."
21. **Tywin addresses the clansmen** — Flatters their reputation; invites them to ride against his enemies with promises of reward.
22. **Messenger arrives** — Reports from Ser Addam that the Stark host is moving down the causeway.
23. **Tywin orders march** — Tells Ser Addam to fall back and harass the Stark flanks, drawing them south; orders drummers to beat assembly; sends word to Jaime.
24. **Tywin manipulates the clansmen** — Says the northmen are fearsome; the clansmen eagerly volunteer to prove themselves.
25. **Chella's condition** — The clansmen will ride with Tywin only if Tyrion goes with them; his life is theirs until promises are fulfilled.
26. **Tyrion accepts** — Says "Joy" with a resigned smile.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Sardonic and guarded; acutely self-conscious about his physical deformities around his father; bitter about Tywin's coldness; amused by the clansmen's behavior; resigned to his fate at the end
- **Primary preoccupation:** Managing the clansmen as allies while navigating his father's contempt; absorbing the dramatic war news (Robert's death, Jaime's victories, Ned's imprisonment, Robb's march)
- **Key decisions made:** Decides to ride down to the camp with clan representatives rather than alone; accepts (resignedly) his assignment to ride with the clansmen into battle
- **Self-deception flags:** Tyrion questions whether he is the clansmen's commander or captive ("Most of the time, it seemed to be a little of both") — unclear if he fully recognizes how little control he has; describes his planned reduction of the Vale as if confident, but never gets to present it
