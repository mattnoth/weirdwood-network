- **chapter_summary:** Catelyn waits on a wooded ridge above a valley as Robb's army prepares an ambush for Jaime Lannister's cavalry near Riverrun. She reflects on the men she has waited for throughout her life — her father Hoster, Brandon Stark, and Ned. Jaime rides into the trap with three quarters of the Lannister horse, and the battle erupts in the darkness. After the fighting ends at dawn, Robb returns unhurt, and Jaime Lannister is brought before Catelyn as a captive. Jaime killed Torrhen and Eddard Karstark and Daryn Hornwood while trying to fight his way to Robb. Catelyn reminds Robb that the war is far from over — Riverrun remains besieged and Lord Tywin is still at large.

## Events & Actions
1. **Catelyn waits on a wooded ridge** — She sits on her horse with Hallis Mollen and thirty guards, waiting for battle while the army positions itself in the Whispering Wood.
2. **Catelyn reflects on waiting** — She recalls waiting for her father Hoster Tully at Riverrun, for Brandon Stark who never returned, and for Ned Stark who rode to war and left her pregnant with Robb.
3. **Robb moves among his men** — He touches shoulders, shares jests, helps gentle anxious horses before battle, bare-headed in his armor.
4. **Robb mounts and rides down the line** — Olyvar Frey helps him mount; Robb rides to show himself to the men, citing his father's advice. Grey Wind follows him. His battle guard of thirty forms up behind him.
5. **Snow shrike calls signal the approach** — Bird calls from multiple directions alert the army that Jaime's force is approaching.
6. **Jaime Lannister rides into the valley** — Catelyn glimpses him briefly, his armor silvered by moonlight, gold hair visible, crimson cloak, unhelmeted. Long columns of Lannister horse follow.
7. **Maege Mormont's warhorn signals the trap is sprung** — A long low blast from the east confirms the last of Jaime's riders have entered the valley.
8. **Grey Wind howls** — His howl triggers the battle; the Greatjon, Mallisters, Freys, and Karstarks answer with their own horns from all directions.
9. **The ambush begins** — Bowmen hidden in trees loose arrows; riders raise lances and charge downhill. Robb shouts "Winterfell!" and leads his men down.
10. **The battle rages through the night** — Catelyn listens from the ridge, hearing the full soundscape of combat: lances, swords, battle cries, arrows, drums, screaming horses, Grey Wind fighting.
11. **Dawn breaks and the battle ends** — Grey Wind howls at red dawn; the sounds of battle dwindle to silence.
12. **Robb returns on a different horse** — He rides a piebald gelding; his shield is slashed, his glove and sleeve are black with blood, but he is unhurt. The blood belongs to others.
13. **Jaime Lannister is captured and brought before Catelyn** — Theon and the Greatjon drag him before her; he has a gash across his scalp.
14. **Jaime exchanges words with Catelyn** — He offers his sword ironically (having lost it); Catelyn demands her father, Edmure, daughters, and husband.
15. **Theon urges Robb to kill Jaime** — Robb refuses, saying Jaime is more useful alive and that his father never condoned killing prisoners after battle.
16. **Jaime is led away to be bandaged and chained** — Robb orders strong guard, noting Lord Karstark will want his head.
17. **Jaime's kills are revealed** — He killed Torrhen and Eddard Karstark and Daryn Hornwood while fighting his way toward Robb, shouting for him.
18. **Catelyn counsels Robb** — She tells him to grieve later; they have won a battle, not a war. Riverrun is still besieged.
19. **Theon boasts of the victory** — He lists captured lords and knights, claims ten-to-one kill ratio, compares it to the Field of Fire.
20. **Catelyn asks about Lord Tywin** — Theon admits Tywin was not captured. Catelyn insists the war is far from done. Robb agrees and turns attention to Riverrun.
