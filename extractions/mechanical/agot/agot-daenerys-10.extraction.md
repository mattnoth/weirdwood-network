# AGOT — Daenerys X

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** AGOT
- **chapter_number:** 73
- **pov_character:** Daenerys Targaryen
- **pov_chapter_number:** Daenerys X
- **first_available:** AGOT Daenerys X
- **location_primary:** The Dothraki sea (red waste borderlands, Drogo's funeral site)
- **location_secondary:** Vaes Dothrak (referenced), night lands (referenced), Yi Ti (referenced), Qarth (referenced), Jade Sea (referenced), Asshai by the Shadow (referenced), Free Cities (referenced)
- **approximate_timeline:** Final chapter of AGOT; follows the death of Khal Drogo
- **time_markers:** Sun creeping toward its zenith (midday); sun had begun to lower toward the west (afternoon); sun was going down (evening/dusk); first star appeared at dusk; fire burned through the night until it died
- **chapter_summary:** Daenerys oversees the construction of Khal Drogo's funeral pyre, rejecting Ser Jorah's pleas to abandon the pyre and flee east. She frees the remaining Dothraki slaves, names Jhogo, Aggo, and Rakharo as her kos, and names Ser Jorah the first of her Queensguard. She has Mirri Maz Duur bound to the pyre as a sacrifice, places the three dragon eggs on the pyre with Drogo, and lights the fire. She walks into the flames unharmed, and three living dragons hatch from the eggs. The remaining Dothraki kneel to her as the chapter — and the book — ends.

## Events & Actions
1. **Pyre construction begins** — Foragers gather gnarled cottonwoods, purple brush, and brown grass; logs are split and laid in a square, center filled with straw, brush, bark shavings, and dry grass
2. **Stallion sacrifice** — Rakharo selects a stallion; Aggo feeds it a withered apple and kills it with an axe blow between the eyes; horse carcass placed at center of pyre base
3. **Mirri Maz Duur warns Dany** — Bound and watching, she tells Dany the horse blood is not enough, she lacks the words for bloodmagic, and offers to help if freed
4. **Dany has Mirri silenced** — She tells Jhogo she is tired of the maegi's braying; Jhogo takes his whip to her
5. **Pyre platform built over horse** — Hewn logs laid east to west (sunrise to sunset); Drogo's treasures placed on the platform: tent, painted vests, saddles, harness, his father's whip, his arakh, a dragonbone bow
6. **Dany keeps her bride gifts** — Aggo would have added them but she forbids it: "Those are mine"
7. **Third level of pyre constructed** — Thin branches laid north to south (ice to fire), covered with dry leaves, twigs, cushions, and sleeping silks
8. **Ser Jorah pleads with Dany** — Calls her "princess," she corrects him; he kneels and calls her queen; offers to take her east to Yi Ti, Qarth, Asshai; begs her not to climb the pyre; she assures him she does not mean to die
9. **Dany addresses the khalasar** — Fewer than a hundred remain; she frees all slaves, invites all to stay as equals
10. **Dany names Jhogo ko** — Gives him the silver-handled whip; he hesitates, says it would shame him to be bloodrider to a woman
11. **Dany names Aggo ko** — Gives him the dragonbone bow; he says only a man can lead a khalasar or name a ko
12. **Dany names Rakharo ko** — Gives him the gold-chased arakh; he offers conditional loyalty only to Vaes Dothrak
13. **Dany names Ser Jorah first of her Queensguard** — Promises him a Valyrian steel longsword, dragon-forged; he kneels and vows to serve, obey, and die for her; she kisses him and names him first of her Queensguard
14. **Dany bathes** — Scalding hot bath; Irri helps her in, Jhiqui scented the water with oils from Vaes Dothrak market; Doreah washes and combs her hair; Irri scrubs her back; they fan her dry, scent her with spiceflower and cinnamon
15. **Dany prepares Drogo's body** — Washes him, brushes and oils his hair, braids it, slides silver rings onto his mustache, hangs his bells; dresses him in horsehair leggings, high boots, gold-and-silver medallion belt, his favorite old painted vest
16. **Drogo's body carried to pyre** — Jhogo and Aggo carry him; Dany walks behind; he is laid on cushions and silks with head toward Mother of Mountains
17. **Pyre soaked with oil** — Oil poured over the pyre until it trickles from beneath the logs
18. **Dragon eggs placed on pyre** — Black egg beside Drogo's heart under his arm; green beside his head with braid coiled around it; cream-and-gold between his legs; Dany kisses Drogo a final time, tasting oil
19. **Ser Jorah urges selling the eggs** — Suggests selling them in Asshai to buy a ship; Dany refuses: "They were not given to me to sell"
20. **Mirri Maz Duur bound to pyre** — Dany orders Jorah to bind her; he hesitates; she invokes his oath; Rakharo helps; Dany pours oil over Mirri's head; Mirri says "You will not hear me scream"; Dany replies "Only death can pay for life"
21. **The red comet appears** — Jhogo spots the first star — a comet, burning red, "bloodred; fire red; the dragon's tail"
22. **Dany lights the pyre** — Takes the torch from Aggo and thrusts it between the logs; oil catches immediately, then brush and grass
23. **Mirri Maz Duur burns** — She sings in a shrill ululating voice; the flames sweep over her; her song becomes a wail of agony, then she falls silent
24. **Drogo's body burns** — His clothing catches fire; Dany feels the urge to join him in the flames
25. **Dany approaches the fire** — The heat drives everyone else back, but Dany stands her ground and steps closer; she sees visions in the flames: firelions, serpents, unicorns, fish, foxes, wolves, birds, flowering trees, and a great grey stallion of smoke with a mane of blue flame
26. **Dany's vest smolders and burns away** — She shrugs it off; milk streams from her breasts; she glimpses Drogo mounted on a smoky stallion with a flaming whip
27. **First egg hatches** — A crack, the sound of shattering stone; a chunk of curved pale rock veined with gold lands at her feet, broken and smoking; women shriek, children cry out in wonder
28. **Second egg hatches** — A second crack, loud and sharp as thunder; logs explode; horses scream; Dothraki shout in fear; Jorah calls her name and curses
29. **Dany walks into the fire** — She steps into the firestorm calling to her children; she thinks "I am Daenerys Stormborn, daughter of dragons, bride of dragons, mother of dragons"
30. **Third egg hatches** — The third crack is "as loud and sharp as the breaking of the world"
31. **Dany found alive with three dragons** — After the fire dies and ground cools, Jorah finds her amidst the ashes, naked, soot-covered, hair burned away, but unhurt; cream-and-gold dragon suckling at left breast, green-and-bronze at right, black-and-scarlet draped across her shoulders
32. **The khalasar kneels** — Jorah falls to his knees wordlessly; Jhogo lays his arakh at her feet and says "blood of my blood"; Aggo and Rakharo echo; handmaids kneel; all the Dothraki kneel — they are hers now
33. **Dragons call into the night** — Drogon hisses with smoke from mouth and nostrils; the other two add their voices; translucent wings unfold; "for the first time in hundreds of years, the night came alive with the music of dragons"

## POV Character's Internal State

- **Self-deception flags:** None apparent — Dany acts with clarity and succeeds; her only moment of uncertainty is wondering if she is mad, quickly resolved; she tells Jorah she will not die on the pyre, which is technically true but conceals the full plan
