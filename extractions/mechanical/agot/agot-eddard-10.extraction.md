## Events & Actions
1. **Ned dreams of the Tower of Joy** — He relives the confrontation where he and six companions (Martyn Cassel, Theo Wull, Ethan Glover, Ser Mark Ryswell, Howland Reed, Lord Dustin) faced three Kingsguard (Ser Arthur Dayne, Ser Oswell Whent, Ser Gerold Hightower) before the tower where Lyanna lay.
2. **Dream dialogue exchange** — Ned and the Kingsguard discuss where they were during key events of Robert's Rebellion: the Trident, the fall of King's Landing, the siege of Storm's End, and Ser Willem Darry's flight to Dragonstone. The Kingsguard assert they swore a vow and do not flee.
3. **Dream combat begins** — Ser Arthur Dayne unsheathes Dawn and says "And now it begins." Ned responds "Now it ends." Steel clashes as Lyanna screams and rose petals blow across a blood-streaked sky.
4. **Ned wakes** — Lyanna's voice merges with a man's voice calling "Lord Eddard." Ned opens his eyes in the Tower of the Hand, moonlight streaming through windows.
5. **Vayon Poole attends Ned** — The steward tells Ned he has been unconscious for six days and seven nights, gives him water, and relays that the king has ordered Ned be sent to him immediately upon waking.
6. **Ned refuses to go to Robert** — He asks Poole to invite Robert to come to him instead, and to summon the captain of his guard.
7. **Alyn reports** — Alyn informs Ned that Jaime has fled to Casterly Rock, that the story of Catelyn seizing Tyrion is widely known, that he has posted extra guards, that Sansa and Arya have been with Ned every day, and that he sent Jory's body and the other dead north to Winterfell via the silent sisters.
8. **Ned reflects on the Tower of Joy** — He remembers that only he and Howland Reed survived the fight, that he pulled down the tower and built eight cairns from its stones, and that Rhaegar had named the place the tower of joy.
9. **Robert and Cersei arrive** — They enter Ned's bedchamber. Robert offers wine. Cersei immediately takes an aggressive stance.
10. **Confrontation over Catelyn and Tyrion** — Robert demands Ned command Catelyn to release Tyrion and make peace with Jaime. Ned refuses, pointing out Jaime killed three of his men.
11. **Cersei claims Ned's men attacked first** — She says Ned was returning drunk from a brothel and his men attacked Jaime's guards.
12. **Ned reveals Robert's bastard daughter** — He tells Robert he went to the brothel to see Robert's daughter Barra, who looks like his first bastard, fathered in the Vale. He watches Cersei's face — it is a mask, betraying nothing.
13. **Robert strikes Cersei** — After Cersei insults him by saying she should be in mail and him in skirts, Robert backhands her. She falls against the table. She says she will wear the bruise as a badge of honor. Robert has Ser Meryn Trant escort her out.
14. **Robert's vulnerable moment** — Alone with Ned, Robert expresses remorse for hitting Cersei, reflects that he was always strong but cannot fight someone he cannot hit, and says Rhaegar won despite being killed — "He has Lyanna now, and I have her."
15. **Robert reinstates Ned as Hand** — Robert refuses to discuss the Targaryen girl, announces he is hunting in the kingswood on the morrow, pulls the silver hand clasp from his cloak and tosses it on the bed, ordering Ned to remain as Hand and forbidding him to leave. He threatens to pin the badge on Jaime Lannister if Ned throws it in his face again.
