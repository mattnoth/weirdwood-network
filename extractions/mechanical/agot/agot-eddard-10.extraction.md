## Events & Actions
1. **Tower of Joy dream** — Ned dreams of riding to the Tower of Joy with six companions to face three Kingsguard. The Kingsguard explain they did not flee, did not go to the Trident, and did not sail to Dragonstone because "the Kingsguard does not flee" and they swore a vow. The fight begins. Ned hears Lyanna screaming his name from the tower. He promises her something.
2. **Ned wakes after six days unconscious** — Vayon Poole wakes him in the Tower of the Hand. Ned's leg is splinted and plastered. He drinks water and learns the king wants to see him immediately.
3. **Alyn's situation report** — Jaime Lannister has fled to Casterly Rock to join his father. The story of Catelyn seizing Tyrion is public knowledge. Extra guards have been posted. Jory and the other dead men have been given to the silent sisters to be sent to Winterfell.
4. **Ned remembers the Tower of Joy aftermath** — Only he and Howland Reed survived. He pulled the tower down and built eight cairns on the ridge.
5. **Robert and Cersei confront Ned** — Robert demands Ned command Catelyn to release Tyrion and make peace with Jaime. Cersei accuses Ned of dishonoring Robert. Ned reveals he went to the brothel to see Robert's daughter Barra.
6. **Tregar's death announced** — Cersei reports that Tregar died that morning from the blow Ned gave him, bringing the dead count to eight.
7. **Robert strikes Cersei** — After Cersei mocks Robert's masculinity, he backhands her across the face. She falls against a table. She declares she will wear the bruise as a badge of honor. Robert has Ser Meryn Trant escort her out.
8. **Robert's confession** — Robert tells Ned that Rhaegar won despite dying — Rhaegar has Lyanna while Robert has Cersei. He expresses regret for hitting Cersei and admits it was not kingly.
9. **Ned restored as Hand** — Robert tosses the silver hand clasp onto Ned's bed and forbids him to leave King's Landing. He refuses to discuss the Targaryen girl. He threatens to give the Handship to Jaime if Ned throws it away again.
