- **chapter_summary:** Tyrion Lannister reads through the night in Winterfell's library, then descends to the yard where he overhears Joffrey and Sandor Clegane discussing Bran's lingering death and the howling direwolf. Tyrion slaps Joffrey twice and orders him to pay his respects to the Starks. He then joins Jaime, Cersei, and the royal children for breakfast, where he reports that Bran may survive but will never walk again. Cersei and Jaime exchange a telling glance at this news. Tyrion announces his intention to travel to the Wall with Benjen Stark and Jon Snow rather than return south with the royal party.

## Events & Actions
1. **Tyrion reads through the night** — Tyrion stays up all night reading in Winterfell's library while a direwolf howls over the castle.
2. **Tyrion instructs Septon Chayle** — He wakes the sleeping septon and instructs him to carefully reshelve the books, especially the fragile Valyrian scrolls and the rare Engines of War.
3. **Tyrion descends the library tower** — He laboriously navigates the steep, narrow exterior steps with his short, twisted legs.
4. **Tyrion overhears Joffrey and the Hound** — From the steps, he hears Clegane say "The boy is a long time dying" and Joffrey wish the wolf would be quiet so he could sleep. Clegane offers to kill the direwolf; Joffrey delights in the idea.
5. **Tyrion confronts Joffrey** — Tyrion tells Joffrey he should be able to count past six (the Stark wolves). He orders Joffrey to pay respects to Lord and Lady Stark.
6. **Tyrion slaps Joffrey twice** — When Joffrey refuses and says the Stark boy is nothing to him, Tyrion slaps him. When Joffrey threatens to tell his mother, Tyrion slaps him again.
7. **Joffrey flees** — The prince runs from the yard holding his reddened cheek.
8. **The Hound warns Tyrion** — Clegane tells Tyrion the prince will remember the slaps. Tyrion dismisses the warning.
9. **Tyrion asks about Jaime's location** — Clegane tells him Jaime is breaking fast with the queen.
10. **Tyrion joins the Lannister breakfast** — He seats himself uninvited at the table in the Guest House morning room with Jaime, Cersei, Tommen, and Myrcella.
11. **Tyrion reports on Bran's condition** — He tells the family that Bran may survive but will never walk again; his back is broken and legs shattered. The maester thinks survival is hopeful since Bran hasn't died after four days.
12. **Jaime and Cersei exchange a glance** — When Tyrion says Bran may live, the twins share a brief look that Tyrion catches. Cersei then says the northern gods are cruel to let the child linger.
13. **Tyrion describes the direwolf's vigil** — He reports that Bran's direwolf stays outside his window howling day and night; when the window was closed, Bran weakened; when reopened, his heart beat stronger.
14. **Cersei declares direwolves dangerous** — She calls the animals unnatural and says she will not have any going south.
15. **Tyrion announces his Wall plan** — He says he will travel to the Wall with Benjen Stark and Jon Snow rather than go south with the royal party.
16. **Cersei departs with the children** — Offended by Tyrion's crude joke about celibacy and whores, Cersei takes Tommen and Myrcella and leaves.
17. **Jaime and Tyrion discuss Bran's fate** — Jaime says Stark won't leave Winterfell while Bran lingers; Tyrion says Robert will command it. Jaime suggests ending Bran's suffering would be a mercy; Tyrion advises against saying that to Eddard.
18. **Tyrion provokes Jaime** — Tyrion says he hopes Bran wakes and would be "most interested to hear what he might have to say." Jaime's smile curdles and he questions whose side Tyrion is on. Tyrion grins and professes love for his family.
