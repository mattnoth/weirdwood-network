## Events & Actions
1. **Jon visits Bran's sickroom** — Jon climbs to Bran's room to say goodbye before departing. Ghost accompanies him.
2. **Catelyn confronts Jon** — Catelyn tells Jon to leave; Jon defies her and approaches Bran's bed. Catelyn does not call the guards.
3. **Jon says goodbye to Bran** — Jon speaks to the comatose Bran, apologizes for not coming sooner, begs him not to die, then kisses him lightly on the lips.
4. **Catelyn's prayer revealed** — Catelyn tells Jon she prayed seven times to the Seven that Ned would leave Bran at Winterfell. She implies the answer to that prayer contributed to the fall.
5. **Catelyn says "It should have been you"** — Catelyn calls Jon by his name for the first time and delivers this statement before turning to weep.
6. **Jon reunites with Robb in the yard** — Robb tells Jon Benjen is looking for him; they embrace and say farewell. Jon lies to Robb, saying Catelyn was "very kind."
7. **Jon gives Needle to Arya** — Jon retrieves a package from the armory, crosses to the Keep, and presents Arya with a sword made to his specification by Mikken.
8. **Needle is named** — Jon prompts Arya to guess the name; they say "Needle" together.
9. **Jon departs Winterfell** — Jon rides north; Arya's laughter is his last memory of the departure.
