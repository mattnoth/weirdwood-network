# ADWD — Tyrion XI

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** ADWD
- **chapter_number:** 58
- **pov_character:** Tyrion Lannister
- **pov_chapter_number:** Tyrion XI
- **first_available:** ADWD Tyrion XI
- **location_primary:** Yunkish siege camp outside Meereen
- **location_secondary:** Yezzan's pavilion, the well south and west of the Harridan, Second Sons camp
- **approximate_timeline:** Two days after Daznak's Pit and Daenerys's departure on Drogon; the Volantene fleet is approaching but has not yet arrived; the siege of Meereen continues under a fragile peace
- **time_markers:** "Two days ago Nurse had been hale and healthy"; "Two days ago the fleets of Old Volantis had been two days farther off"; morning (leaving tent into "the heat of a Meereenese morning"); "Midday had come and gone" (at the well); dusk the previous evening (Nurse's body removed); "the day was growing hotter"; a dark plume of smoke rising from burning corpses
- **chapter_summary:** Tyrion and Penny tend to their dying master Yezzan zo Qaggaz, who has contracted the bloody flux (pale mare). Ordered by the slave soldier Scar to fetch water, they retrieve Ser Jorah Mormont from his iron cage and walk to the nearest well, passing through the siege camp and observing military preparations against a possible dragon return. At the well they hear conflicting rumors about Daenerys Targaryen's fate. On the return trip, Tyrion deliberately diverts their route to the Second Sons' camp, where he reveals his true identity to Captain Brown Ben Plumm and proposes an alliance, arguing his head is worth more on his shoulders than in a jar.

- **Notable sensory details:** Yezzan's excrement described as "brown slime streaked with blood"; the weight of the water pails gouging deep grooves in Tyrion's fingers; his legs cramping badly; the air outside the tent described as "a welcome relief" from the interior

## Events & Actions
1. **Healer diagnoses Yezzan with the pale mare** — enters the tent, recognizes the disease, declares his arts will not avail, advises fresh water, and flees
2. **Slaves discuss their fate if Yezzan dies** — Sweets reveals that masters sometimes kill favorite slaves to accompany them in death; Penny hopes for freedom
3. **Goat boy mentions the silver queen** — Sweets insists Daenerys is dead, carried across the river by the dragon and drowned in the Dothraki sea
4. **Penny suggests fleeing to find the queen or sail to Qarth** — Tyrion dismisses both ideas
5. **Tyrion and Penny exit the pavilion** — into the muggy heat of a Meereenese morning
6. **Tyrion internally reveals Nurse's death** — Nurse was given dogtail soup with mushroom slivers by Tyrion, who spoke the words "A Lannister always pays his debts" as Nurse died; other slaves believed it was the pale mare
7. **Tyrion tells Penny that Yezzan will not survive to sunrise** — she clutches his arm in fear; he explains the nephews may inherit them or they could be auctioned
8. **Tyrion confronts Scar about fetching water** — Scar backhands him, splitting his lip and knocking him down; Scar ultimately orders them to fetch water themselves
9. **Mormont is released from his iron cage** — Morgo brings keys; Mormont emerges bent, squinting, battered, nearly naked
10. **Tyrion reflects on Mormont's deterioration under slavery** — the knight is a shell, beaten nightly, sullen and uncooperative
11. **The three carry water pails through camp** — Tyrion observes Clanker Lords drilling slave soldiers, teams raising siege engine ramps angled skyward, crossbows everywhere
12. **Tyrion internally considers dragon vulnerabilities** — eyes are the weak point; cites Septon Barth's Unnatural History; scorpion bolts won't bring it down
13. **They observe Ghiscari legions drilling** — two legions from New Ghis with shield walls; Tyrion doubts their effectiveness compared to Unsullied
14. **They wait in line at the well** — quarter-mile queue; most Yunkish drink from the Skahazadhan river
15. **Tyrion reflects that Daenerys should have poisoned the wells** — "That was what his lord father would have done"
16. **Tyrion internally regrets his path** — thinks Griff, Duck, Haldon and the young prince should be in Westeros; blames his need for a whore and wine
17. **Rumors at the well about Daznak's Pit** — old slave claims Daenerys was killed by a crossbow bolt and crushed under a wagon; brown woman insists she flew away; Tyrion says no corpse was found
18. **Tyrion and Penny recognized as the jousting dwarfs** — the redheaded boy asks if the queen is beautiful; Tyrion recalls seeing Barristan Selmy behind Daenerys
19. **Tyrion recalls almost revealing himself to Selmy at Daznak's Pit** — stopped by caution; feared hostility due to killing Tywin
20. **Old slave boasts about his master Ghazdor** — claims bondage under a good master is better than freedom; Tyrion privately agrees slavery is insidiously easy to grow accustomed to
21. **They fill pails at the well** — one-legged slave is suspicious; Tyrion lies that six soldiers also have the flux
22. **Tyrion diverts their return route toward the Second Sons camp** — claims it's to avoid smoke from burning corpses
23. **Penny rests, Tyrion rubs his cramping legs** — she offers to help; he declines
24. **Tyrion asks Mormont if he has any fight left** — Mormont replies "Enough to crack your neck, Imp"
25. **Tyrion internally reflects on Penny** — compares her to Sansa Stark; her trusting nature troubles him; he gives her false comfort instead of truth
26. **Tyrion recalls the planned lion attack at Daznak's Pit** — they were meant to die; lions had been starved for two days; Nurse's face betrayed surprise when they returned alive; Penny never learned this
27. **Penny realizes they're going the wrong way** — Tyrion reveals he's heading for the Second Sons
28. **They are confronted by Second Sons guards** — Tyroshi spearman, stripling from Flea Bottom, and hook-handed serjeant
29. **Serjeant recognizes the dwarfs** — says "These are the dwarfs Ben tried to buy"; orders all three brought to the captain
30. **Stripling gropes Penny** — picks her up, comments on her breasts, fondles her under her tunic
31. **They are brought to Brown Ben Plumm's tent** — Kasporio the Cunning and Inkpots are also present
32. **Tyrion reveals his true identity to Brown Ben** — "That is not my name. But you know that."
33. **Mormont identifies Kasporio and Inkpots** — his voice recognized despite his battered face; Brown Ben greets him
34. **Tyrion pitches joining the Second Sons** — argues his head is worth a lordship but Cersei would cheat Brown Ben; offers alliance instead; names Bronn, Shagga, and Timett as men who prospered by his service
35. **Tyrion explains Brown Ben's dragon affinity** — speculates about Plumm lineage including Viserys Plumm and "two drops" of dragon blood
36. **Tyrion requests wine and a cyvasse game** — acknowledges he has "a deal of talking to do"

- **Key decisions made:** Poisoned Nurse (prior to chapter but revealed here); diverted the water-fetching trip to approach the Second Sons; revealed his true identity to Brown Ben; chose to pitch alliance rather than flee
- **Self-deception flags:** Tells himself his false comfort to Penny is kindness ("Every touch a lie.
