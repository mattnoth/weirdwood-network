- **chapter_summary:** Quentyn Martell and Gerris Drinkwater negotiate passage to Meereen aboard the Adventure, a foul-smelling smuggler's ship, but Quentyn suspects the captain plans to rob and murder them at sea. Having been refused by numerous ships over twenty days in Volantis, the Dornish party is running out of options. Quentyn reflects on the corsair attack that killed Cletus Yronwood, Willam Wells, and Maester Kedry during their voyage aboard the Meadowlark. At their inn, Ser Archibald Yronwood advocates traveling overland via the demon road, but Quentyn insists on speed. Gerris announces he has a new plan, risky and dishonorable, that could get them to Daenerys faster.

## Events & Actions
1. **Quentyn and Gerris board the Adventure** — They visit the smuggler's ship in Volantis harbor to negotiate passage to Meereen, enduring a horrific stench.
2. **Negotiation with the Adventure's captain** — Gerris, posing as a Dornish wine merchant, offers triple the usual fare for passage to Meereen. The captain warns that the slave cities are at war, names the sellsword companies heading to Yunkai, then accepts the offer and tells them to return an hour before first light.
3. **Quentyn and Gerris toast with the captain** — They drink ale to seal the agreement, though Quentyn privately suspects treachery.
4. **Quentyn voices suspicion** — After leaving the ship, Quentyn tells Gerris the captain accepted too quickly and likely plans to slit their throats at sea or chain them to oars.
5. **Hathay ride through Volantis** — They ride in an elephant-drawn cart through the waterfront, passing shops, temples, pillow houses, and slave-filled streets.
6. **Quentyn reflects on the corsair attack** — He recalls the ambush on the Meadowlark off the Disputed Lands at dawn, in which twelve crewmen, Cletus Yronwood, Willam Wells, and Maester Kedry were killed. The dead were sewn in canvas and given to the sea.
7. **Quentyn recalls his romantic history** — He thinks about his infatuation with Ynys Yronwood (who married Ser Ryon Allyrion), the Drinkwater twins (one gave him his first kiss), and young Gwyneth Yronwood (who wanted to marry him).
8. **Triarch's procession blocks the street** — A dozen Unsullied spearmen in tiger-skin cloaks clear the way for a triarch atop an armored elephant with a castle on its back.
9. **Quentyn and Gerris discuss Volantene politics** — Quentyn explains the freehold system, the triarchs, the voting rights of freeborn landholders including women, and the Old Blood's unbroken Valyrian descent.
10. **Arrival at Fishermonger's Square** — They pass through the fish market, observe dwarf performers staging a mock joust on a dog and a pig.
11. **Encounter with the Windblown** — Outside the Merchant's House, four Windblown sellswords at a recruiting table try to enlist them, singing a mocking song about killing "the butcher king" and bedding "the dragon queen." Quentyn and Gerris ignore them and enter the inn.
12. **Council with Archibald at the inn** — The three Dornishmen discuss their options: the Adventure (dangerous), the demon road (overland, Archibald's preference), buying a ship (impractical), the Goddess to New Ghis (too risky). Archibald favors the overland route; Quentyn insists on speed because Tywin Lannister will send assassins.
13. **Gerris proposes a new plan** — Gerris says he has just thought of a plan that is risky and dishonorable but would get them to Daenerys faster than the demon road. Quentyn asks to hear it.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Anxious, burdened by responsibility, grief-stricken over lost friends, self-conscious about his physical unattractiveness, daunted by the scale of his mission. Oscillates between determination ("he could not fail him, not whilst life remained") and self-doubt ("more and more he wondered if he was equal to the task").
- **Primary preoccupation:** Finding a ship to Meereen before Tywin Lannister's agents reach Daenerys first; fulfilling his father's mission and marrying Daenerys. Secondary: mourning Cletus, Will, and Kedry.
- **Key decisions made:** Rejects the Adventure as too dangerous (suspects the captain will murder them). Rejects the overland demon road as too slow. Rejects buying a ship (would attract attention; no one to sail it). Agrees to hear Gerris's new plan.
- **Self-deception flags:** Tells himself "I am Dorne. She will want Dorne" — rationalizing that Daenerys will accept a political marriage to a man she's never met and may not find attractive. Acknowledges "It does not mean that she will love me, though. She may not even like me" but pushes forward regardless.
