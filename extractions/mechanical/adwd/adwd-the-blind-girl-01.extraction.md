# ADWD — The Blind Girl I

## Chapter Metadata

- **chapter_number:** 46

- **approximate_timeline:** Spans multiple days of routine before focusing on one specific night of begging and the following morning; set during the period of Arya's blindness training

- **chapter_summary:** The blind girl (Arya Stark) lives in the House of Black and White, continuing her training without sight. She reports intelligence gathered from begging to the kindly man each morning. She is attacked by an unknown assailant with a stick in the vaults. She spends a night begging at Pynto's tavern where she overhears Lyseni sailors discussing the ship Goodheart, which was seized for carrying wildling slaves from Hardhome. She reports this to the kindly man and strikes him with her stick, revealing she knows he was her attacker. That morning, she regains her sight.

## Events & Actions
1. **Wolf dream** — The blind girl experiences Nymeria's hunt; the pack feasts on lamb, dog, mutton, and human flesh; game scarcer since snows began
2. **Morning routine** — She wakes on her stone shelf, washes, dresses by touch (identifying socks by stitching), recites her kill list
3. **Breakfast with the kindly man** — She eats sardines; he asks "who are you" (she says "no one"); he offers to return her eyes (she declines); she reports three things learned from begging
4. **Intelligence report** — She reports: the Sealord is still sick, Tormo Fregar rumored as next Sealord, S'vrone the whore is with child, and the Merling Queen chose a new Mermaid
5. **The kindly man catches her in a lie** — She described the new Mermaid as "lovely" but could not have seen her; he reminds her she is blind
6. **Daily training described (summary of routine)** — Poisons/potions in afternoons; language lessons at supper (Braavosi, High Valyrian, Lysene, Pentoshi); lying game in evenings; learning to navigate by sound, smell, touch
7. **Finding the dead** — She discovers two corpses in the temple: a young handsome man at the feet of the Stranger, and an old woman on a dreaming couch; she traces their features by touch
8. **Stripping the dead** — In the vault, she removes possessions from corpses, counts coins by touch (Braavosi, Volantene, Lysene, Westerosi, others); finds four golden dragons on the man, a ring on the woman
9. **Stick-fighting attack in the vault** — An unknown assailant with a harsh cold voice attacks her; she fights back but is beaten; he raps her ear and cracks her knuckles; she vows to beat him when she gets her eyes back
10. **Reflection on Cat of the Canals** — She misses her former identity and friends; recalls killing Dareon the deserter, which caused her early blinding
11. **The kindly man's teaching about killing** — Recalled conversation: the founding story of the Faceless Men; the first slave who prayed for his master's death; "we kill men, but we do not presume to judge them"; she killed Dareon and took god's power on herself
12. **Disguise application** — The waif gives her pox scars and a mummer's mole with a dark hair; she reflects on beauty and her family
13. **Journey through Braavos** — She counts steps from temple, crosses bridge to Isle of the Gods, passes temples (Starry Wisdom, R'hllor's house), navigates to Ragman's Harbor by scent and sound
14. **Begging at Pynto's** — She claims a corner near the fire; a cat joins her in her lap; Pynto gives her food and tells pirate stories; she collects coins from regulars
15. **Overhearing the Lyseni** — Three Lyseni sailors discuss the Goodheart's seizure; the ship carried wildling slaves from Hardhome; she sees them through the cat's eyes (slitted yellow eyes)
16. **Morning report to the kindly man** — She reports the Goodheart story (wildling slaves from Hardhome, the Elephant may return with more ships); then strikes his hand with her stick, revealing she knows he was her attacker
17. **The final cup** — At supper, Umma serves salt-crusted crabs; the waif's cup burns like fire; bread helps but wine does not
18. **Sight restored** — She wakes and sees a tallow candle burning; her blindness is over

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Determined, resilient, occasionally sad (missing Cat of the Canals, Jon Snow); frustrated by the stick-fighting defeats; satisfied when she identifies and strikes the kindly man; brief joy at seeing the candle
- **Primary preoccupation:** Mastering her remaining senses; maintaining her identity as "no one" while privately holding onto Arya's kill list and wolf dreams; gathering intelligence from begging
- **Key decisions made:** Refuses to ask for her eyes back (daily); chooses not to reveal she can warg into the cat; keeps the wolf dreams secret from the kindly man; decides not to tell about the snow in the riverlands
- **Self-deception flags:** Recites "I am no one" while simultaneously maintaining her kill list ("Her morning prayer. Or was it? No, not mine"); acknowledges the kill list belongs to "the night wolf" but clearly it is hers; tells herself she is "no one" while keeping secrets and private identity markers; thinks "she was not sorry" about killing Dareon while the text implies conflicted feelings
