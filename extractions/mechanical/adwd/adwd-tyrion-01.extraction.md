# ADWD — Tyrion I

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** ADWD
- **chapter_number:** 2
- **pov_character:** Tyrion Lannister
- **pov_chapter_number:** Tyrion I
- **first_available:** ADWD Tyrion I
- **location_primary:** Illyrio's manse, Pentos
- **location_secondary:** Ship crossing the narrow sea; tunnels beneath the Red Keep (recalled); the Blackwater (recalled)
- **approximate_timeline:** Shortly after Tyrion's escape from King's Landing; he has crossed the narrow sea and arrived in Pentos
- **time_markers:** Time marked by cabin boy's visits; "not half a year ago" (Magister Ordello's death); "a hundred years ago" (Braavosi treaty with Pentos); "first day of each new year" (Prince of Pentos ritual); bars of yellow sunlight between shutters (morning after arrival); autumn wind mentioned; cherry trees bare and brown
- **chapter_summary:** Tyrion drinks his way across the narrow sea hidden belowdecks on a small ship. He arrives in Pentos stuffed into a wine cask and is received by Illyrio Mopatis. He explores the manse grounds, finds poisonous mushrooms which he pockets, and encounters a washerwoman and a bedwarmer. At supper, Illyrio tests Tyrion's will to live with a mushroom dish, shares news that Cersei has put a price on Tyrion's head, informs him Stannis is at the Wall, and hints at a plan involving "a dragon with three heads" as a savior for Westeros.

## Events & Actions
1. **Tyrion drinks across the narrow sea** — Confined belowdecks in a small cabin, Tyrion drinks continuously, rarely eats, and marks time by the cabin boy's visits.
2. **Tyrion reflects on killing Tywin** — He recalls shooting his father with a crossbow and the escape through tunnels with Varys.
3. **Tyrion confesses to Varys** — At the Blackwater shore, he tells Varys he killed Shae too. Varys says "You knew what she was."
4. **Storm hits the ship** — Tyrion is thrown against a bulkhead; he retches and passes out.
5. **Arrival in port** — Tyrion is carried by a huge bald sailor and stuffed into a wine cask for transport.
6. **Cask transport** — Tyrion endures roughly half an hour of rolling, stacking, and jouncing while crammed into the cask.
7. **Illyrio opens the cask** — In a dim cellar, the fat man with a forked yellow beard opens the cask with mallet and chisel. Tyrion spills out onto earthen floor.
8. **Illyrio offers hospitality** — Declares his house is Tyrion's; promises bath, food, and soft bed.
9. **Tyrion bathes and sleeps** — Falls asleep in the bath; wakes in goose-down bed.
10. **Tyrion observes Pentos from window** — Sees cherry trees, marble pool with swordsman statue, brick rooftops, red temple, bay with ships.
11. **Illyrio departs for magisterial session** — Warns Tyrion not to leave the walls; says they will make plans in the evening.
12. **Tyrion explores the manse** — Visits kitchen (takes cheese, bread, figs), cellar (selects Lord Runceford Redwyne's strongwine), gardens.
13. **Tyrion notes the defenses** — Three gates (main with gatehouse, postern by kennels, chained garden gate); eunuch guards with spiked bronze caps; high walls with iron spikes.
14. **Tyrion speaks to washerwoman** — Rambles about Dorne, Myrcella, the Wall, Night's Watch; she walks away.
15. **Tyrion finds poisonous mushrooms** — Seven pale white mushrooms with speckles and red-ribbed undersides; wraps them in a glove and pockets them.
16. **Tyrion falls asleep on bench** — Wakes in his bedchamber; the blond bedwarmer rouses him for supper.
17. **Tyrion interacts with bedwarmer** — She was "bought to please the king" and trained in Lys; he threatens her, gets the fear he wanted.
18. **Supper with Illyrio** — Elaborate feast served; Illyrio tests Tyrion with mushroom dish; Tyrion refuses.
19. **Illyrio eats the mushrooms** — Proves they are safe; tells Tyrion "You have nothing, but we can change that."
20. **News exchanged** — Astapor and Meereen have fallen; Cersei offers a lordship for Tyrion's head; Stannis is at the Wall.
21. **Illyrio proposes a plan** — Describes Pentoshi customs regarding their prince; argues Westeros needs a savior; reveals it is "a dragon with three heads."

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Deeply depressive, self-destructive, and self-loathing. Drinking to obliterate consciousness. Oscillates between suicidal ideation (the mushrooms, wondering if he'd eat them) and survival instinct ("I have no wish to die"). Bitterness, grief, and rage at his father dominate.
- **Primary preoccupation:** "Where do whores go?" — an obsessive refrain tied to Tywin's last words and Tyrion's grief over Tysha. Also preoccupied with having killed his father and Shae.
- **Key decisions made:** Chooses not to eat the poisonous mushrooms he finds; refuses Illyrio's mushroom test; declares "I have no wish to die"; pockets the seven mushrooms (keeping the option); resolves to look for a rope to escape over the wall.
- **Self-deception flags:** Claims "I have no wish to die" but has been drinking himself toward death; pockets lethal mushrooms "just in case"; recognizes the part of himself that wanted the mushrooms "even knowing what they were" and is frightened by it; tells himself to look for escape rope but takes no action.
