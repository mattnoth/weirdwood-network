# ADWD — The Queensguard I

## Chapter Metadata

- **chapter_number:** 56

- **time_markers:** Afternoon spent in training hall; supper at sunset; meeting with Skahaz after dark/nightfall; "three days rain had threatened"; "forty-seven years" since Selmy's knighting feast; "ten years ago" Selmy muses on his declining speed; "last night" he dreamed of the pit; the gathering of corpses after Drogon's escape took "the rest of the day and most of the night"; sun set unseen as it had risen unseen that morning
- **chapter_summary:** Reznak mo Reznak informs Barristan Selmy that King Hizdahr is replacing the Queensguard with pit fighters for court sessions. Selmy reflects on how Hizdahr has systematically removed Daenerys's loyalists — Belwas poisoned, Skahaz stripped of command, Unsullied withdrawn, hostages held by Yunkai'i, Dothraki sent searching for the queen. After reassuring Missandei, training his squires, and eating a solitary supper, Selmy disguises himself and meets Skahaz mo Kandaq secretly in the stables. Skahaz reveals he has captured the poisoner (Hizdahr's confectioner), that Volantis has launched a fleet against Meereen, and proposes a pre-emptive strike against the Yunkai'i. Selmy extracts a promise that no harm will come to Hizdahr without proof and agrees to speak with Grey Worm.

## Events & Actions
1. **Selmy dismissed from court** — Reznak mo Reznak informs Selmy that Hizdahr wants Meereenese pit fighters (Goghor the Giant, Khrazz, the Spotted Cat, Belaquo Bonebreaker) as his protectors when receiving the Yunkai'i delegation, not a Westerosi knight.
2. **Selmy reflects on the systematic removal of Daenerys's loyalists** — Belwas poisoned, Skahaz stripped of command, Unsullied withdrawn, hostages held by Yunkai'i, Dothraki sent searching, Missandei replaced as herald.
3. **Selmy climbs to the royal apartments** — Notes the 800-foot climb, the aches in his body, and the empty, silent apartments. Hizdahr has not taken residence there.
4. **Selmy surveys the city from the terrace** — Observes the corpse-colored sky, Yunkai'i camps burning dead, thick greasy smoke, plague spreading both inside and outside the walls, empty streets, closed markets.
5. **Selmy reflects on his age and past** — Sees a stranger's face in still water; remembers his knighting by Aegon V, the white cloak from Jaehaerys II, serving the Mad King, rescuing Aerys at Duskendale, the deaths of Rhaegar, Elia, baby Aegon, Rhaenys, Robert.
6. **Selmy recalls Drogon's escape from the fighting pit** — Daenerys's hair aflame, mounting Drogon with a whip, the dragon wounded by spears and crossbows, loosing fire beyond the gates; 214 slain, three times as many burned or wounded; Drogon last seen flying north over the Skahazadhan.
7. **Missandei appears on the terrace** — Tells Selmy that Skahaz mo Kandaq wishes to speak with him; a Brazen Beast in an owl mask delivered the message.
8. **Selmy arranges a secret meeting** — Tells Missandei to relay that he will meet "our friend" after dark, by the stables; instructs her to verify it is the same owl.
9. **Selmy reassures Missandei about Daenerys** — Says Aggo and Rakharo know the Dothraki sea, and promises to keep the queen safe. Privately doubts: "How can I protect the queen when I am not with her?"
10. **Selmy trains his squires in the afternoon** — Works with boys aged eight to twenty in the training hall on the pyramid's third level; started with more than sixty, less than half remain; teaches sword, shield, horse, lance, and chivalry.
11. **Selmy eats supper on the terrace at sunset** — Simple meal with wine; watches fires waken in the pyramids and shadows gather in the streets.
12. **Selmy disguises himself** — Changes from white cloak to hooded brown traveler's cloak; keeps sword and dagger.
13. **Selmy descends through the pyramid** — Passes four Brazen Beasts at the great gates (boar, bear, vole, manticore); then four at the dragon pit (ape, ram, wolf, crocodile); learns dragons were fed a sheep apiece.
14. **Selmy meets Skahaz in the stables** — Skahaz wears a brass cat mask (formerly wore serpent); says "Cats go everywhere. No one ever looks at them."
15. **Skahaz reveals the poisoner** — Hizdahr's confectioner; a catspaw whose daughter was taken by the Sons of the Harpy and returned in nine pieces after Daenerys survived.
16. **Skahaz claims Hizdahr's peace is a sham** — Says the Yunkai'i feared Daenerys and her dragons, but after the pit everything changed; Yurkhaz dead, replaced by "a pack of jackals"; Bloodbeard has no taste for peace.
17. **Skahaz reveals Volantis has launched its fleet** — Says the Wise Masters, the Harpy, Reznak, and Hizdahr all know; Hizdahr will open the gates to the Volantenes; all freedmen will be re-enslaved.
18. **Skahaz proposes a pre-emptive strike** — Has spoken with Free Brothers, Mother's Men, and Stalwart Shields; wants to break the siege, kill slaver lords, turn the sellswords before the Volantene fleet arrives; needs the Unsullied and asks Selmy to talk to Grey Worm.
19. **Selmy demands conditions** — Requires Skahaz's word that no harm will come to Hizdahr until his guilt is proved; Skahaz agrees but vows to kill Hizdahr with his own hands when proof comes.
20. **Selmy agrees to speak with Grey Worm** — Prays silently to the Crone for wisdom: "For the children. For the city. For my queen."

- **Emotional state:** Deeply conflicted; exhausted and feeling old; loyal to Daenerys but uncertain how to serve her in her absence; mistrustful of Hizdahr, Reznak, and Skahaz alike; haunted by failures protecting past kings and royals; bitter about the political situation; reluctant to engage in political scheming but driven by duty to the queen's people
- **Primary preoccupation:** Whether Daenerys is alive and how to protect her city and her freedmen ("her children") in her absence; the question of where his duty lies — to Hizdahr as consort, or to Daenerys as queen
- **Key decisions made:** (1) Agrees to meet Skahaz secretly despite the risk; (2) Demands Skahaz's word to spare Hizdahr until guilt is proved; (3) Agrees to talk to Grey Worm about joining the conspiracy — a decisive turn from passive protector to active political agent
- **Self-deception flags:** Tells Missandei "I swear, I shall keep her safe" while simultaneously thinking "How can I protect the queen when I am not with her?" Frames his decision to join Skahaz as duty ("For the children. For the city. For my queen") while recognizing it may be treason against the queen's own consort. Notes he should arrest Skahaz for treason but does not.
