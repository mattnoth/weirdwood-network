# ADWD — Tyrion VII

## Chapter Metadata

- **location_secondary:** Long Bridge, Merchant's House, Fishmonger's Square, Temple of the Lord of Light
- **approximate_timeline:** Shortly after Tyrion's capture; arrival in Volantis at dusk, overnight stay, morning meeting with the widow
- **time_markers:** Purple sky to west and black to east at arrival (dusk); stars coming out; gates closing at dark; nightfires lit at sunset; waning moon at night; morning streaming through windows bright and golden; "two days from now" the Selaesori Qhoran sails; elections last ten days (third day currently); widow has resided at the Merchant's House for thirty-two years
- **chapter_summary:** Tyrion arrives in Volantis as Ser Jorah Mormont's captive, bound to a saddle. They pass through the eastern city, witness High Priest Benerro preaching at the Temple of the Lord of Light, then Mormont sells his horse and has Tyrion put in iron manacles at a smithy. They cross the Long Bridge to the western waterfront and take rooms at the Merchant's House inn. Tyrion learns Mormont intends to take him to Meereen and Daenerys Targaryen, not to Cersei. The next morning they meet the widow of the waterfront, who initially refuses aid but relents after a dwarf girl named Penny attacks Tyrion (blaming him for her brother Oppo's death at the hands of sailors who mistook Oppo for Tyrion). The widow tells them to board the cog Selaesori Qhoran and sends a message for Daenerys from the slaves of Volantis.

## Events & Actions
1. **Arrival at Volantis gate** — Tyrion and Mormont arrive at dusk, join a queue behind a wagon of limes and oranges. Guards inspect Mormont; one rubs Tyrion's head for luck.
2. **Passage through East Volantis** — They ride south along the river through declining neighborhoods; Tyrion observes the decay of Old Volantis and its sights (headless statues, naked children, bravos, slaves).
3. **Encounter with elephants** — They pass large grey elephants and white dwarf elephants pulling carts; dung carts attended by slaves with fly tattoos.
4. **Witnessing Benerro's sermon** — At the Temple of the Lord of Light, High Priest Benerro preaches to a massive crowd about Daenerys being in peril, ancient prophecy, and a coming hero. He makes flames leap from his fingers and traces Valyrian glyphs (Doom, Darkness).
5. **Tyrion's realization about Aegon** — Tyrion deduces that Benerro's prophecy of one hero makes Prince Aegon unwelcome; worries the red priest would oppose a second Targaryen without dragons.
6. **Forcing through the crowd** — Mormont draws his sword partially to clear a path through the crowd; they escape into an alley.
7. **Sale of horse** — At a stable, Mormont sells his warhorse and saddle to a slave ostler.
8. **Manacling at the smithy** — At a shuttered smithy, Mormont has Tyrion's ropes cut and replaced with heavy iron manacles and chains (wrist to wrist, wrist to ankle, ankle to ankle).
9. **Walk to the Long Bridge** — They proceed on foot; Tyrion struggles to keep up; Mormont yanks his chains when he falls behind.
10. **Crossing the Long Bridge** — They cross amid traffic jams, shops, severed hands and heads on display; Mormont elbows a pickpocket; they observe the inscriptions on the displayed heads (slave who struck mistress, accused spy for dragon queen, kinslayer).
11. **Arrival at west bank** — They pass through waterfront districts; elephant with slave girls chanting "Malaquo" passes; Tyrion nearly steps in elephant dung.
12. **Arrival at the Merchant's House** — Mormont takes a room on the fourth floor; chains Tyrion to an iron ring in the wall.
13. **Tyrion identifies his captor** — Tyrion reveals he knows who Mormont is (from the bear surcoat, arms, lost lordship); mentions Varys; tries to bargain. Mormont rebuffs him.
14. **Failed escape attempt** — Alone at night, Tyrion tries to slip manacles (tears skin, bleeds) and pull the iron ring from the wall. Fails.
15. **Overnight suffering** — Tyrion endures cramps, pain, loss of feeling; reflects on his father's death, Shae, and Tysha; sleeps fitfully.
16. **Morning release** — Mormont unchains Tyrion from the ring; Tyrion collapses, cannot feel his legs; Mormont carries him downstairs by his chain.
17. **Spotting Penny** — In the common room, Tyrion sees another dwarf (later revealed as Penny) who recognizes him; Mormont dismisses the concern.
18. **Breakfast and news** — Over breakfast, Mormont reports that an exiled lord has hired the Golden Company and captains are racing to Volon Therys. Tyrion realizes this may mean Aegon has gone west.
19. **Meeting the widow** — A serving girl brings them to the widow of the waterfront. Mormont presents gloves as a gift. The widow asks why they seek passage to Meereen.
20. **Tyrion's revelation** — Tyrion learns Mormont's destination is Meereen, not King's Landing; realizes he's being taken to Daenerys, not Cersei. Bursts into laughter.
21. **The widow's intelligence** — The widow discusses Volantene politics: war is coming against Meereen; tigers and elephants both moving toward conflict; but R'hllor worship may upend everything. She reveals Malaquo tried to hire the Golden Company against the red temple.
22. **Penny attacks Tyrion** — The dwarf girl rushes Tyrion with a knife. Tyrion smashes a flagon in her face and dodges. She stabs at the floor. Mormont grabs her and disarms her.
23. **Penny's story** — Penny reveals sailors from the Seven Kingdoms killed her brother Oppo (cut off his head) thinking he was Tyrion. They had been jousting dwarfs at Joffrey's wedding.
24. **Widow relents** — Moved by events and recognizing Tyrion's danger in Volantis, the widow agrees to help. She tells them to board the cog Selaesori Qhoran sailing in two days (bound for Qarth via New Ghis but will never reach Qarth per Benerro's visions).
25. **The widow's message** — The widow asks them to tell Daenerys that the slaves of Old Volantis are waiting and to come soon.
