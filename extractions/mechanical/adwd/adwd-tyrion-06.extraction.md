# ADWD — Tyrion VI

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** ADWD
- **chapter_number:** 23
- **pov_character:** Tyrion Lannister
- **pov_chapter_number:** Tyrion VI
- **first_available:** ADWD Tyrion VI
- **location_primary:** Selhorys (town on the eastern bank of the Rhoyne)
- **location_secondary:** Shy Maid (poleboat moored at Selhorys), the Painted Turtle (inn), a brothel in Selhorys, red temple of Selhorys
- **approximate_timeline:** Shortly after the passage through the Sorrows; Tyrion has been unconscious for an unspecified period and wakes at Selhorys; events span from his waking through nightfall
- **time_markers:** Setting sun when Tyrion first observes Selhorys; close to dusk when Yandry and Ysilla return; dusk giving way to darkness when Tyrion and Haldon go ashore; nighttime throughout Selhorys excursion; "Be back before the sun comes up" (Griff's instruction)
- **chapter_summary:** Tyrion wakes aboard the Shy Maid at Selhorys after nearly drowning in the Sorrows, and Haldon tests him for greyscale. Tyrion plays cyvasse with Prince Aegon and manipulates him with advice to go west to Westeros rather than east to Daenerys. Griff sends Haldon and Tyrion ashore to gather intelligence; they hear a red priest proclaiming Daenerys as Azor Ahai, then meet customs officer Qavo Nogarys at the Painted Turtle, learning that Volantis may join the war against Daenerys. Tyrion visits a brothel and beds a Westerosi slave whore, then is recognized and seized by a knight bearing a black bear surcoat who says he will deliver Tyrion "to the queen."

- **Notable sensory details:** Tyrion's mouth dry and rusty with the taste of blood upon waking; the broth's warmth; pike spitting and sizzling over brazier; the goat so hot it burned his fingers; the sour strong wine; Haldon's face turning green, red, purple in the light of colored lanterns; the Myrish carpet "as comforting as lies"

## Events & Actions
1. **Tyrion wakes from nightmare** — Dreams of his father merged with the Shrouded Lord giving him a grey kiss; wakes with mouth dry and bloody-tasting, heart hammering
2. **Greyscale testing** — Haldon tosses Tyrion a knife and instructs him to prick all his toes and fingers, watching for numbness, dead grey skin, or blackening nails; Tyrion complies, feeling pain at every prick
3. **Haldon warns about greyscale** — Says Tyrion may be turning to stone internally from swallowing river water, and that there is no certainty he is clean
4. **Tyrion observes Selhorys from the Shy Maid** — Notes the town's sandstone wall, towers and domes reddened by sunset, a Volantene river galley discharging soldiers at a nearby pier
5. **Lemore greets Tyrion** — Rushes to hug him, says "The Mother is merciful," calls him "Hugor"
6. **Lemore changes out of septa's robes** — Wears merchant-class garb to avoid drawing Westerosi attention; Haldon notes this
7. **Tyrion speculates about Lemore's identity** — Wonders who she really is, whether she was ever a true septa, what the prince is to her
8. **Haldon reports Dothraki and warship sightings** — Between the Sorrows and Selhorys, they thrice glimpsed Dothraki riders along the eastern shore, heard bells in braids, saw fires; also passed Volantene river galleys with slave soldiers
9. **Tyrion and Aegon play cyvasse** — Play on deck behind the cabin; Aegon uses aggressive formation with dragon, elephants, and heavy horse forward
10. **Tyrion manipulates Aegon about his identity** — Draws out Aegon's origin story: the baby swap with the tanner's son from Pisswater Bend, Varys smuggling him to Illyrio
11. **Tyrion advises Aegon to go west** — Argues that Westeros is ripe for conquest: boy king on throne, north in chaos, riverlands devastated, Cersei ruling incompetently; urges landing in Dorne and raising banners rather than going east to beg Daenerys
12. **Tyrion argues Daenerys will come to Aegon** — Says if Aegon raises the Targaryen standard in Westeros, Daenerys will fly to his side as a rescuer; they would meet as equals
13. **Tyrion defeats Aegon at cyvasse** — "Death in four"; had told Aegon not to bring out his dragon early, then used his own dragon to win; "I lied. Trust no one. And keep your dragon close."
14. **Aegon kicks over the board** — Jerks to feet, kicks cyvasse pieces across the deck, commands Tyrion to pick them up
15. **Yandry and Ysilla return with provisions** — Near dusk; porter with wheelbarrow of provisions; Yandry has wine cask, Ysilla has a pike as large as Tyrion
16. **Ysilla wards against Tyrion** — Makes three-finger stabbing gesture to ward off evil; refuses to let him touch food
17. **Yandry reports news** — Daenerys's name on every tongue in Selhorys; she still sits in Meereen beset; Old Volantis may join war against her
18. **Griff sends Haldon and Tyrion ashore** — Instructs them to find Qavo and learn truth of rumors; try the Riverman and Painted Turtle; return before sunrise or make for the Golden Company if delayed
19. **Griff gives silver** — Allows purses of silver from Illyrio's chests "to loosen tongues"
20. **Tyrion and Haldon walk through Selhorys riverfront** — Observe deserted and armed ships, parchment lanterns, colored light, foreign tongues, music, whores
21. **Tyrion observes slave whores** — Notes tears tattooed beneath right eyes indicating bondage; compares unfavorably to free whores of Lannisport and King's Landing
22. **Tiger guard rubs Tyrion's head** — At the river gate, a Volantene guard grabs Tyrion and rubs his head for good luck; Tyrion is too startled to resist
23. **Tyrion and Haldon enter the great square** — Observe nightfire, red priest preaching, cyvasse players, soldiers, brothel, a woman beating a mule, a two-wheeled cart pulled by a white dwarf elephant, the headless statue of Triarch Horonno
24. **They hear the red priest preach** — Priest calls Volantenes to fight on the side of R'hllor; says Nyessos and Malaquo have turned from the light; invokes dragons; Haldon explains Benerro has declared Daenerys is Azor Ahai returned, born from smoke and salt
25. **They enter the Painted Turtle** — Haldon leads them to the inn where Qavo Nogarys plays cyvasse with a larger man
26. **Qavo's opponent loses and departs** — After Haldon introduces Tyrion as a better player, the thin man (Qavo) defeats the big man; the big man leaves in disgust
27. **Tyrion sits to play cyvasse with Qavo** — Stakes silver coins; they eat and drink while playing
28. **Qavo reports political intelligence** — Yunkai'i envoy Grazdan mo Eraz brought gold, gems, 200 slaves to bribe triarchs; Nyessos bribed; Malaquo still a tiger; Doniphos will not be returned; the city thirsts for war; anti-Daenerys propaganda circulates; legions of New Ghis, Tolos, Elyria, and Dothraki will join Yunkai; Khal Pono outside Selhorys but manageable
29. **Qavo defeats Tyrion at cyvasse** — Takes Tyrion's dragon with his catapult; finishes the game; "The time has come for bitter tears"; scoops up Tyrion's silver
30. **Tyrion enters the brothel** — Haldon agrees to wait at the tavern by the gate; Tyrion asks for a girl who speaks Westerosi; gets one who is red-haired and freckled but speaks no Common Tongue
31. **Tyrion beds the Westerosi whore** — She looks at his noseless face with revulsion; finishes quickly; observes scars on her back; feels shame; asks about Tysha; drinks the whole flagon of wine; vomits on the Myrish carpet; takes her a second time
32. **Tyrion pays the proprietor for the ruined carpet** — Tosses a fistful of coins
33. **Knight with bear surcoat confronts Tyrion** — Calls him "Imp" from a shadowed corner; has a Lyseni-looking whore on his lap; draws his sword; declares he will deliver Tyrion "to the queen"

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Oscillates between sardonic wit, manipulative cunning, deep self-loathing, and drunken despair. Begins with disorientation from the nightmare; becomes sharp and calculating during the cyvasse game with Aegon; turns self-destructive and shame-filled in the brothel. "What a wretched creature I've become."
- **Primary preoccupation:** Multiple threads — testing whether he has greyscale; manipulating Aegon's strategic direction; gathering intelligence about Daenerys; obsessive searching for Tysha ("Selhorys may be where whores go"); processing his father's death and his own guilt
- **Key decisions made:** Advises Aegon to go west to Westeros rather than east to Daenerys (a strategic manipulation); agrees to go ashore with Haldon; chooses to visit the brothel despite Haldon's mild objection; reveals his alias as Hugor Hill to the knight
- **Self-deception flags:** Rationalizes going to the brothel ("I need to make sure my prick hasn't turned to stone") when his real motive is self-destructive despair and the hope of finding Tysha; his advice to Aegon seems altruistic but is designed to manipulate; after bedding the whore: "This girl is as good as dead. I have just fucked a corpse. Even her eyes looked dead. She does not even have the strength to loathe me."
