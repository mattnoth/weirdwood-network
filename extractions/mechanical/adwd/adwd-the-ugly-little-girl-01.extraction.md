# ADWD — The Ugly Little Girl I

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** ADWD
- **chapter_number:** 65
- **pov_character:** Arya Stark (as "the ugly little girl" / "no one")
- **pov_chapter_number:** The Ugly Little Girl I
- **first_available:** ADWD The Ugly Little Girl I
- **location_primary:** House of Black and White, Braavos
- **location_secondary:** Purple Harbor, Isle of the Gods, Ragman's Harbor, the Drowned Town, Canal of Heroes
- **approximate_timeline:** Mid-to-late ADWD; Arya has already had her eyes taken and returned, has been Cat of the Canals previously, and has been training at the House of Black and White for an extended period
- **time_markers:** "three hours of wine and words" (the servants' council); events span multiple days — she returns to Brusco "the next day," gets her first look at the target "later that morning," watches him "for a few days," takes "three more days of watching" to find the method, then "another day of practicing with her finger knife"; the kill day arrives "when at last day came to Braavos"; she waits behind crates "the best part of an hour"; "that night they gave her back the face of Arya Stark"; "on the morrow you will go to Izembaro"
- **chapter_summary:** Arya attends a secret council of eleven servants of the Many-Faced God beneath the temple. Afterward, a priest with a plague-ravaged face tests her resolve and assigns her a task: kill a specific man to earn a face. She resumes her Cat of the Canals identity to observe the target — a maritime insurance underwriter at the Purple Harbor — studying his habits, guards, and routines over several days. She is taken to a secret sanctum deep beneath the temple and given an ugly new face made from the skin of a dead girl. Wearing this face, she executes the kill by cutting a shipowner's purse, replacing a coin with a poisoned one, which the shipowner then uses to pay the old man, who bites it and dies. Arya is given back her own face, an acolyte's robe, and told she will begin an apprenticeship with Izembaro.

## Physical Environment
- **Weather:** Grey, dark, and overcast on the kill day; cold with a blustery, biting wind; no fog despite the girl's prayers for it
- **Season indicators:** Autumn referenced indirectly — "The seas are dangerous, and never more so than in autumn"
- **Time of day:** The servants' council takes place at night; subsequent events span multiple days; the kill day begins at dawn ("when at last day came to Braavos"); she reaches the Purple Harbor near noon ("it was almost noon"); that night she receives her face back
- **Lighting:** Iron lantern in the underground passages; candlelight during knife practice; torchlight behind leaded glass windows at the Lord of Harmony's hall; lantern light in the face-chamber
- **Sounds:** Soft voices speaking in Braavosi and High Valyrian; soft scuff of slippered feet on stone steps; key clicking three times softly in the lock; coins clattering on a table; shouts of "thief, thief"; Casso the seal barking and clapping flippers
- **Smells:** Musty green tunic smelling of fish; the storeroom is dusty
- **Notable sensory details:** Gooseprickles on her arms descending to the sanctum; the cut feels warm instead of cold; blood tastes of salt and copper; the tart drink tastes like biting into a lemon; the new face feels dry and stiff at first, then softens as blood soaks in; choking sensation and blinding pain during the face-donning; the wind tugs at her cloak "with ghostly fingers"

## Events & Actions
1. **Eleven servants gather** — Eleven servants of the Many-Faced God assemble beneath the temple at night; only the lordling and the fat fellow enter by the front door; the rest come by secret ways. They wear robes of black and white and reveal their chosen faces.
2. **Arya serves water** — The girl serves water while another acolyte serves wine; they stand like statues waiting on the priests.
3. **Assignments are discussed** — The priests discuss targets in Braavosi and briefly in High Valyrian. Plague face and the fat fellow say "I know this man" about a target; the handsome man volunteers to give the gift to someone he does not know; the squinter does likewise for another.
4. **Plague face interrogates Arya** — After the council, plague face questions her identity. He tells her she is "Arya of House Stark, who bites her lip and cannot tell a lie." He accuses her of wanting to kill for her own purposes.
5. **Plague face slaps her** — He strikes her when she hesitates; she accepts it as earned.
6. **Plague face tests her resolve** — He tells her the price is everything she has and ever hopes to have: her eyes, ears, legs, name, and face. She accepts.
7. **Arya is assigned to kill a stranger** — Plague face tells her she must give the gift to a man she does not know to earn a face.
8. **Arya returns to Brusco as Cat** — She resumes her Cat of the Canals identity, greeted with "Valar dohaeris" by Brusco.
9. **Arya identifies her target** — She sees the old man at the Purple Harbor while pushing her barrow; she smiles at him but he scowls and splashes through a puddle, wetting her feet.
10. **Arya observes the old man over several days** — She learns he is a maritime insurance underwriter who writes binders in the soup shop, counting coins (biting them on the left side), stamping documents with his seal, doing business with captains and shipowners.
11. **The kindly man explains the binder business** — He tells her the old man writes insurance for ships; someone who lost a claim must have prayed for his death at the House of Black and White.
12. **Arya proposes killing him directly** — She suggests waiting until the tall guard is making water and stabbing the old man through the eye. The kindly man rejects killing the guard too.
13. **Arya devises the coin-swap plan** — After three more days of watching, she finds a method; she practices with the finger knife for another day and sharpens it.
14. **Arya is taken to the sanctum** — The kindly man leads her down through vaults (18 steps), subcellar (22 more steps), through a locked iron door, and 54 more steps to the third level — the sanctum where only priests go.
15. **Arya sees the wall of faces** — A thousand face-skins hang on the walls — all ages, races, expressions. She is asked if she wants to leave; she insists on continuing.
16. **Arya is given the ugly face** — Her face is cut (warm blade, blood tasting of salt and copper), she drinks the tart potion, and a dead girl's skin is pulled over her face. She experiences the dead girl's memories: choking hands, a fat bearded brutal man beating her, blinding pain.
17. **The kindly man explains the dead girl's story** — The girl's father beat her brutally; she came to the House and asked the gift for herself, not for her father.
18. **Arya has troubled sleep** — She sees faces on the walls in her mind: her father, mother, three brothers, the black singer, the stableboy, the pimply squire, the Harrenhal guard, the Tickler. She remembers stabbing the Tickler repeatedly.
19. **Arya dresses and walks through Braavos** — She takes the long way through the Isle of the Gods, past the temples of the Weeping Lady of Lys, the Gardens of Gelenei, the Lord of Harmony's hall, the Great Shepherd, Three-headed Trios, the Silent God, the Patternmaker's Maze, Aquan the Red Bull, Semosh and Selloso, through Ragman's Harbor and the Drowned Town.
20. **Arya passes the Happy Port and sees Tagganaro** — Lysene sailors leave the Happy Port; the Ship is closed; Tagganaro tosses a ball with Casso, King of Seals. Tagganaro does not recognize her, but Casso barks.
21. **Arya reaches the Purple Harbor and waits** — She perches on a piling; the old man is in the soup shop counting coins with his guards. She watches the harbor bustle: sailors, whores, bravos, a red priest.
22. **Arya picks her mark** — A big bald shipowner she has seen deal with the old man three times arrives, walking with a cane. She follows him.
23. **Arya slits the purse and swaps the coin** — She slashes through his velvet cloak, slits his purse, and fills her fist with gold while planting a coin.
24. **Arya is caught and flees** — The shipowner turns, her arm tangles in his cloak, coins rain down. He raises his cane; she kicks his bad leg out, dodges a potbellied innkeep, runs past a laughing whore, darts into alleys.
25. **Arya hides and escapes across rooftops** — She hides in a dusty storeroom for nearly an hour, then climbs the building and crosses rooftops almost to the Canal of Heroes.
26. **Arya presents the gold dragon** — At the House of Black and White, she shows the kindly man a Westerosi gold dragon. She explains she took one of the shipowner's coins and left one of hers.
27. **The kindly man confirms the kill** — He understands: the planted coin was given to the old man in payment; "soon after that man's heart gave out." He tosses the gold dragon into the pool.
28. **Arya's face is restored** — That night they give her back the face of Arya Stark.
29. **Arya receives an acolyte's robe** — She is given the black-and-white robe and told she will go to Izembaro for her first apprenticeship; she will also need a new face since the city watch is looking for the ugly girl.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Determined and resolute but conflicted; she maintains her kill list despite training to abandon identity; she feels a flash of anger about the target living longer than her father; she is frightened in the face chamber but refuses to show it; she is pleased to receive the ugly face but does not smile outwardly; troubled by visions of her dead family and those she's killed
- **Primary preoccupation:** Completing the assigned kill to prove herself worthy and earn a face; suppressing her identity as Arya Stark while her identity keeps surfacing (the lip-biting, the kill list, the memory of lemon cakes, seeing her family's faces)
- **Key decisions made:** Accepts the price of becoming a Faceless Man; devises the coin-swap assassination method; chooses not to kill the guard (accepting the constraint); decides to take the long route through Braavos on the kill day
- **Self-deception flags:** She tells herself "Cat of the Canals had no father" while just thinking about her father; she insists "No, that was some other girl" and "I am no one" while clearly retaining all of Arya's memories, grudges, and identity; she claims "I have no heart" while demonstrating emotional investment; she tells herself the face-skins are "leather hoods" that "cannot hurt me" but they clearly disturb her; she recites her kill list while claiming to serve only the Many-Faced God
