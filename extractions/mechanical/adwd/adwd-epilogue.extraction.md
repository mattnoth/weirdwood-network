# ADWD — Kevan Lannister (Epilogue)

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** ADWD
- **chapter_number:** 73
- **pov_character:** Kevan Lannister
- **pov_chapter_number:** Epilogue
- **first_available:** ADWD Epilogue
- **location_primary:** King's Landing (Red Keep)
- **location_secondary:** Throne room, Maegor's Holdfast, Grand Maester Pycelle's chambers (beneath the rookery)
- **approximate_timeline:** After Cersei's walk of atonement; snow has been falling heavily on King's Landing; a white raven arrives from the Citadel signaling winter
- **time_markers:** "most of the night" (snow falling); supper with Cersei and Tommen (evening); "tonight" (duty at drawbridge); full moon visible after snow stops; the snow stops falling before Kevan crosses to the rookery; "five days hence" (next council meeting, after Cersei's trial)
- **chapter_summary:** Ser Kevan Lannister presides over a small council meeting where they discuss the threat of Jon Connington, the Golden Company, and Aegon's landing in the south, the crown's empty treasury, Margaery and Cersei's upcoming trials, and the political balance between Lannister and Tyrell factions. After supper with Cersei and Tommen in Maegor's Holdfast, Kevan is summoned to Grand Maester Pycelle's chambers, where he finds Pycelle murdered. Varys reveals himself, shoots Kevan with a crossbow, explains he is acting to prevent Kevan from stabilizing the realm so that Aegon can take the throne, and summons his "little birds" — children with daggers — to finish Kevan off. A white raven from the Citadel signals the arrival of winter.

## Physical Environment
- **Weather:** Heavy snowfall most of the night; drifts ankle-deep outside; snow swirling in the outer ward; the snow finally stops falling later; blowing snow around the white raven
- **Season indicators:** A white raven arrives from the Citadel to herald winter; "Autumn in King's Landing" — Kevan's thought; the change of seasons is officially declared
- **Time of day:** Begins during the day (council meeting); transitions to evening (supper); then night (summoned to Pycelle's chambers, full moon visible)
- **Lighting:** Torchlight in the throne room; Iron Throne half-shrouded in shadow; candlelight at supper (green eyes shining in candlelight); a few flickering candles in Pycelle's chambers casting pools of dim light; moonlight through the open window painting the white raven silver; pools of shadow
- **Sounds:** Steady drip-drip-drip of snowmelt; echoes of Connington's footsteps fading; wind rising, clawing at shutters; wind howling like a caged beast; soft scuffling sound of slippered feet on stone
- **Smells:** Grand Maester Pycelle "befouled himself in dying" — Varys notes the stink was "abominable"
- **Notable sensory details:** Palpable chill in the throne room; Kevan feels the Iron Throne at his back like an itch between the shoulder blades; ice crystals glittering in moonlight; an icicle long as a spear falls and shatters near Kevan's feet; the word "Winter" makes white mist in the air; Kevan grows cold as ice; every labored breath sends fresh pain through him; blood dribbles from his mouth

## Events & Actions
1. **Small council meeting** — Ser Ronnet Connington declares loyalty to Tommen and offers to fight his uncle Jon Connington; the council discusses the Golden Company's incursions along the southern coast and islands
2. **Ronnet dismissed** — Kevan orders Ronnet escorted back to his chambers (effectively confined)
3. **Sellsword threat assessed** — Pycelle presents a detailed map of incursions: Tarth, Stepstones, Estermont, and Connington reportedly moving on Storm's End
4. **Margaery's trial debated** — Mace Tyrell demands Tommen declare Margaery innocent; Kevan insists the High Septon requires a trial
5. **Financial crisis discussed** — Ser Harys reports the treasury empty (only rats and roaches); has written to Myrish bankers; Kevan suggests trying Pentos; tells Swyft he may need to go to Braavos personally
6. **Cersei's trial arrangements** — Kevan announces Cersei has chosen trial by battle with Ser Robert Strong as champion; Tyrell and Tarly express suspicion about Strong's identity
7. **Kevan outlines Cersei's containment** — Her guards replaced; attended by septa and novices; no voice in governance; to be returned to Casterly Rock after trial
8. **Myrcella and Dorne discussed** — Mace suggests a better match; Kevan warns against offending Dorne lest Martell join Connington
9. **Council adjourns** — To reconvene in five days, after Cersei's trial
10. **Kevan reflects on Tyrell threat** — Identifies Randyll Tarly as the real danger; notes the council balance (3 Lannister, 3 Tyrell when Redwyne returns, with Lady Nym as seventh)
11. **Pycelle and Swyft request guards** — Kevan suggests they hire the Mountain's men
12. **Kevan crosses to Maegor's Holdfast** — Crosses the dry moat via drawbridge; Ser Meryn Trant on guard
13. **Kevan in his solar** — Has fire built, drinks mulled wine, reflects on Cersei's walk of atonement and her past
14. **Kevan reflects on the past** — Remembers Cersei's youth, Rhaegar/Lyanna, Tytos's mistress being paraded by Tywin
15. **Supper with Cersei and Tommen** — Cersei is demure and modest; Tommen chatters about kittens; they discuss Jaime, Tyrion, the Tower of the Hand, Taena Merryweather, and the Kettleblack arrests
16. **Kevan summoned to Pycelle** — A boy messenger arrives; dark wings, dark words
17. **Kevan crosses to Pycelle's chambers** — Snow has stopped; full moon; castle alien under snow; icicle falls near him; a serving girl admits him
18. **Discovery of Pycelle's murder** — Kevan finds chambers cold, hearth dead; Pycelle's head bashed in at his table, blood and brain pooled around him
19. **White raven sighted** — An enormous white raven sits on the window seat; Kevan realizes it heralds winter
20. **Kevan shot** — A crossbow bolt slams into his chest between the ribs
21. **Varys reveals himself** — Steps from shadows with crossbow; explains his actions are for the realm and children; wants to undo Kevan's stabilizing work
22. **Varys explains his plan** — The murder will be blamed on Tyrells by Cersei, on Cersei by Tyrells, on Dornishmen by someone; doubt and division will eat the ground beneath Tommen while Aegon raises his banner
23. **Varys reveals Aegon lives** — Declares Aegon has been trained for kingship since infancy: arms, languages, history, law, poverty, labor, faith
24. **Varys's little birds emerge** — Children with daggers emerge from shadows around the dying Kevan after Varys whistles
