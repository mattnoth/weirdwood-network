Dany decides she cannot abandon Meereen and refuses Xaro's terms. Xaro threatens her and departs, leaving behind a bloodstained glove — a Qartheen declaration of war.

## Events & Actions
1. **Erotic dance performance** — Male and female dancers perform with torches and acrobatics at the feast, culminating in sexual acts set to music.
2. **Dany observes her court** — Notes Reznak's open mouth, Hizdahr watching dancers, the Shavepate's stern observation, and the Unsullied's impassivity.
3. **Dany dismisses the dancers** — Compliments them and orders Reznak to escort them to baths with food and drink.
4. **Dany and Xaro converse at the feast** — Discuss old times in Qarth; Xaro proposes marriage again (rejected); they debate slavery versus freedom.
5. **Xaro tells of a former merchant digging ditches** — Uses the example of a once-wealthy spice merchant now doing manual labor to critique Dany's policies.
6. **Xaro offers slave dancers as gift** — Dany accepts and declares she will free them.
7. **Dany takes Xaro to the terrace** — They climb the marble steps to her private terrace for frank conversation; Barristan follows.
8. **Trade negotiation fails** — Dany offers salt, wine, olives (none available — trees burned), copper; Xaro wants only slaves.
9. **Xaro warns of military threats** — Reports battle at Horns of Hazzat, Yunkai'i hiring sellswords (Company of the Cat, Long Lances, Windblown, possibly Golden Company), Tolos and Mantarys joining alliance against Dany.
10. **Xaro warns of warlocks** — Pyat Pree and three warlocks set out from Qarth to seek Dany in Pentos.
11. **Xaro offers thirteen galleys** — Condition: Dany must swear to sail immediately to Westeros.
12. **Dany consults Barristan** — He advises inspecting the ships and is eager to sail home; Dany orders Groleo to inspect at first light.
13. **Dany refuses Xaro's sexual advance** — He offers to stay; she declines, though tempted to pretend he is Daario.
14. **Next morning: Dany informs her handmaids** — Irri and Jhiqui react negatively to sea travel and the number thirteen.
15. **Lord Ghael petitions for aid** — Begs Dany to defend Astapor; she refuses to lend her Unsullied; Ghael spits in her face.
16. **Strong Belwas slams Ghael to the ground** — Breaks Ghael's teeth on the marble; Dany stops further violence.
17. **Groleo and Barristan report ships are sound** — Three named ships have issues but all are seaworthy; rowers are slaves who could be hired.
18. **Council debates leaving** — Reznak fears enslavement, Skahaz threatens to kill masters, Marselen pledges to follow, Symon doubts capacity, Rommo objects to ships, Grey Worm suggests overland march, Shavepate outlines route problems, Mollono warns of deaths on demon road.
19. **Dany decides to stay in Meereen** — Declares she will not abandon her people; Westeros must wait.
20. **Dany rejects Xaro's conditional gift** — Offers friendship and trade profits instead of immediate departure.
21. **Xaro presents ancient tapestry** — A pre-Doom map showing Valyria intact; tries one last time to persuade her.
22. **Xaro threatens Dany** — Says "I should have slain you in Qarth"; weeps; warns she will die screaming.
23. **Dany invokes guest right and expels Xaro** — Forgives his words once in memory of his hospitality, then threatens dragonfire if he doesn't leave by sunrise.
24. **Xaro departs overnight** — His galleas is gone by morning.
25. **Qartheen declaration of war** — The thirteen galleys remain with red streamers; a messenger delivers a bloodstained glove on a black satin pillow, signifying war.
