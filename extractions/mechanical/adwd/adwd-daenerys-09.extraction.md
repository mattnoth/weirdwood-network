# ADWD — Daenerys IX

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** ADWD
- **chapter_number:** 53
- **pov_character:** Daenerys Targaryen
- **pov_chapter_number:** Daenerys IX
- **first_available:** ADWD Daenerys IX
- **location_primary:** Daznak's Pit, Meereen
- **location_secondary:** Great Pyramid of Meereen (bathing pool, base), plaza before the pyramid, brick streets of Meereen, pyramid of Pahl, pyramid of Naqqan
- **approximate_timeline:** Day of the grand reopening of the fighting pits under Dany's rule; follows her marriage to Hizdahr
- **time_markers:** Rising sun shimmering on bathing pool water; sun climbing the sky; blistering heat at midday in the pit; "before the sun goes down"; heat rising from bricks in shimmering waves; shadow of persimmon tree in morning light
- **chapter_summary:** Daenerys prepares for and attends the reopening of the fighting pits at Daznak's Pit with her husband Hizdahr zo Loraq. Before departing, Ser Barristan relays Pretty Meris's claim that the Tattered Prince wants Pentos as his price for bringing the Windblown over; Dany refuses. At the pit she watches several matches with growing disgust, forbids lions from being set on jousting dwarfs, and witnesses Barsena Blackhair gored to death by a boar. Drogon descends into the arena, kills the boar and feeds on the carcass. When a spearman drives a spear into Drogon's neck, the dragon kills him and several others. Dany leaps into the pit, confronts Drogon with the pitmaster's whip, mounts him, and flies away.

## Events & Actions
1. **Dany bathes at dawn** — Jhiqui removes her robe, Irri helps her into the pool; Missandei washes her hair and questions why the queen must attend the pits in person
2. **Dany dresses for the pits** — Chooses yellow silk tokar with long red veils; Jhiqui brushes hair, Irri paints nails
3. **Missandei reports visitors** — Hizdahr bids Dany join him; Prince Quentyn and his Dornish men request an audience; Dany refuses to see Quentyn
4. **Ser Barristan reports on Pretty Meris** — Meris was released as commanded; she claims the Tattered Prince intended to bring the Windblown over from the beginning, and was betrayed by the Dornishmen before she could approach; the Tattered Prince's price is Pentos
5. **Dany refuses to give Pentos** — Cites her debt to Illyrio who arranged her marriage, gave her dragon eggs, and sent Barristan, Belwas, and Groleo
6. **Royal procession through Meereen** — Great drum, shavepate herald, Brazen Beasts marching four abreast, Strong Belwas walking, handmaids and bloodriders on horseback, Reznak in sedan chair, Barristan riding, Quentyn and companions following
7. **Crowd cheers on the plaza** — Same plaza where Dany once impaled 163 Great Masters
8. **Procession halts between pyramids of Pahl and Naqqan** — An overturned palanquin blocks the way; a bearer has collapsed from heat; Dany orders him helped and fed
9. **Ser Barristan fears a trap** — Ghiscari faces watch from terraces with cool, unsympathetic eyes; Barristan worries about the Sons of the Harpy; Hizdahr dismisses the concern
10. **Dany reflects on freed bearers** — The palanquin is no lighter, but the fallen bearer receives aid instead of a whipping
11. **Arrival at Daznak's Pit** — Pass beneath the bronze warrior archway into the great brick bowl; descend through colored tiers to the red benches and royal box
12. **Seating arrangement observed** — Graces around Galazza Galare in green; Great Masters in red and orange (Loraq in purple/indigo/lilac, Pahl in pink/white); Yunkish envoys in yellow beside the king's box with slaves and servants; freedmen and common folk in upper tiers; sellsword captains (Brown Ben, Bloodbeard) among common soldiers
13. **Hizdahr addresses the crowd** — Presents the games in Dany's name; crowd roars "Mhysa!" and stamps feet
14. **Khrazz kills the Summer Islander** — Tall fighter with red-black mohawk fights ebon-skinned spearman; slips inside the spear with shortsword and butchers him; cuts out heart, takes a bite
15. **Spotted Cat kills his opponent** — Large, slow opponent hamstrung, then throat cut ear to ear; crowd screams approval
16. **Strong Belwas finishes all the honeyed locusts** — Belches, drinks wine
17. **Diverse fighters from across the world die** — Qartheen, Summer Islanders, Dothraki, Tyroshi, Lamb Men, Jogos Nhai, Braavosi, Sothoryi half-men, and a Lysene youth with long blond hair (gutted; Dany notes he was "only a boy," Hizdahr says sixteen)
18. **Animal fights** — Elephant kills six red wolves; bull fights bear, both torn and dying; carcasses used for stew at Gates of Fate
19. **Mock battle** — Six foot soldiers with shields and longswords defeat six horsemen with Dothraki arakhs; Jhiqui disgusted, says "That was no true khalasar"
20. **Dwarf jousting folly** — Presented by a Yunkish lord; one dwarf on hound, one on sow; wooden armor painted with stag of Robert Baratheon and golden lion of Lannister; Dany's smile is forced
21. **Dany forbids lions on the dwarfs** — Hizdahr planned to release three lions; Dany furiously orders it stopped; Hizdahr shows flash of anger but complies; dwarfs herded off as crowd pelts them with stones and rotten fruit
22. **Barsena Blackhair enters the arena** — Naked save for breechclout and sandals; crowd roars approval; fights a boar
23. **Barsena is gored and killed** — After initial success cutting the boar, she leaps too late; tusk rips her leg from knee to crotch; boar gores her, roots out her entrails
24. **Dany removes her tokar and veils** — Cannot stand the smell, heat, and flies; says she is "taking off my floppy ears"
25. **Dany asks to leave** — Requests Ser Barristan escort her to her garden; Hizdahr protests, says Belaquo and Goghor remain
26. **Strong Belwas is ill** — Says he ate too many locusts, has queasy look, needs milk
27. **Drogon appears** — Shadow ripples across the pit; ten thousand voices stilled; warm wind and sound of wings
28. **Drogon kills the boar with black fire** — Lands on carcass, feeds on Barsena and the boar alike
29. **Crowd reaction** — Reznak horrified; Belwas retching; Hizdahr shows "part fear, part lust, part rapture" and licks his lips; Pahls flee; some run, some stay
30. **Unnamed spearman attacks Drogon** — Leaps onto the dragon's back, drives boar spear into base of neck; Dany and Drogon scream as one
31. **Drogon kills the spearman** — Tears his arm from his shoulder; spear remains in Drogon's back, smoking
32. **Hizdahr orders Drogon killed** — Shouts "Kill it! Kill the beast!" to the spearmen
33. **Drogon kills more attackers** — Bathes two men in black flame; breaks pitmaster in two with his tail; catches another in his jaws and tears his belly out
34. **Dany leaps into the pit** — Clears the parapet, loses a sandal, runs across hot sand; Barristan calls after her; Belwas still vomiting
35. **Dany confronts Drogon** — Dragon roars in her face, snaps at her, nearly tears her head off; she stumbles over pitmaster's corpse
36. **Dany finds the pitmaster's whip** — Touching it makes her feel braver; the leather is warm, alive
37. **Dany strikes Drogon with the whip** — Screams "No" and lashes him; barbs rake along his snout; Drogon spits black fire at her; she darts underneath the flames, swinging and shouting "Get DOWN!"
38. **Drogon submits** — Gives a last hiss and stretches flat on his belly; black blood flowing and smoking from his spear wound
39. **Dany mounts Drogon** — Vaults onto his back, seizes the spear, rips it out (point half-melted, iron glowing red-hot), flings it aside
40. **Dany flies away on Drogon** — Wings crack like thunder; scarlet sands fall away; Meereenese pour up steps and into streets below; Dany flicks whip against Drogon's neck and cries "Higher!"

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Reluctant and dutiful at the outset ("a queen belongs to her people, not to herself"); increasingly revolted and disgusted as the fights progress; horrified by Barsena's death; resolute and fearless when confronting Drogon ("I cannot let him see my fear"); exhilarated during flight ("yes, now, now, do it, take me, FLY!")
- **Primary preoccupation:** Tension between duty (attending the pits, maintaining peace) and her disgust at the violence she has sanctioned; fantasizes about the house with a red door; questions where she is being led ("one step, then the next, but where is it I'm going?"); preoccupied with betrayals (counts three treasons)
- **Key decisions made:** Refuses to see Quentyn; refuses to give Pentos to the Tattered Prince; forbids lions on the dwarfs; decides to leave the pits; leaps into the pit to confront Drogon rather than letting him be killed; mounts Drogon and flies away
- **Self-deception flags:** Counts Brown Ben Plumm as her third treason ("Brown Ben Plumm the third. Was she done with betrayals?") — the "three treasons" prophecy may not be complete or correctly identified; tells herself "Meereen needed iron, not gold" to justify paying the Windblown, but then refuses their actual price; thinks "I am not your mother" to the crowd but does not act on the thought
