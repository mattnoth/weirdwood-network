- **chapter_summary:** Tyrion and Penny perform a mummer's joust for the crew of the becalmed Selaesori Qhoran to improve morale. Tyrion provokes Jorah Mormont about his exile from Daenerys and is struck hard, losing a tooth. A wind arrives from the west bringing a catastrophic storm that destroys the ship's mast, kills nine men including Moqorro, the captain, and the cook. The survivors drift for nineteen days until a galley appears on the horizon — which Jorah identifies by smell as a slaver.

## Events & Actions
1. **Mummer's joust performed** — Tyrion rides Pretty Pig and Penny rides Crunch in a jousting show for the becalmed crew. Tyrion takes the fall as planned, biting his tongue hard enough to taste blood.
2. **Crew reaction** — Laughter is sparse and strained; some hiss in anger; one sailor insults Tyrion from the sterncastle (having wagered on him).
3. **Jorah helps Tyrion up** — Comments "You looked a fool," which was the intent.
4. **Captain orders boats out** — At midmorning, orders small boats to tow the becalmed cog, hoping to find wind to the north.
5. **Jorah warns about the pig** — Tells Tyrion to have Penny keep her door barred; too much talk about eating the pig (and a Ghiscari crew would eat the dog too).
6. **Tyrion provokes Jorah** — Needles him about being exiled by Daenerys, accuses him of spying, says his plan to present Tyrion to Daenerys is desperate. Begins comparing him to Florian the Fool.
7. **Jorah strikes Tyrion** — Punches him hard enough to crack his head around, bounce it off the deck, and break a tooth. Exiles Tyrion from their shared cabin.
8. **Penny tends Tyrion's wound in galley** — Dabs his lip; lectures him about how to behave around "big people."
9. **Tyrion reflects on love** — Thinks of Shae, Tysha, advises Penny to stay a maid. Says Jorah hit him "for love."
10. **Wind returns** — The ship begins moving again; sail comes to life; Tyrion and Penny go topside.
11. **Storm approaches** — Tyrion spots ominous cloud bank on western horizon. Moqorro confirms it as divine wrath: "God's wroth."
12. **Tyrion realizes the widow's prophecy** — Understands "the ship would never reach her destination" means literal destruction, not a course change or seizure.
13. **Storm hits** — Three hours running before the wind; seas grow rougher; lightning, thunder; Tyrion leads Penny below.
14. **Sheltering below** — Animals panicking; Penny frightened; Tyrion cleans pig shit; they tie everything down.
15. **Penny kisses Tyrion** — During the worst of the storm. Tyrion does not desire her but does not push her away. He declines further intimacy by claiming fidelity to Sansa.
16. **Eye of the storm** — Near midnight, winds die; Tyrion goes on deck; sees the storm raging in all directions with the ship in a calm center; sea like dragonglass under stars.
17. **Storm returns with fury** — Sail tears away (with two men still on lines); mast bursts; splinters hit Tyrion (one through calf, one in neck, one near eye). He clings to a line.
18. **Aftermath of storm** — Nine men lost (a mate, two fiery fingers, Moqorro, and five others); cook blinded by hot grease; captain broke both legs from being thrown; ship's mast destroyed; figurehead arm broken off; hull sprung in many places; listing ten degrees to port.
19. **Captain and cook die** — Captain dies next day; cook dies three nights later.
20. **Crew abandonment** — Mate who assumed command lowers boats; one sinks, the other crew cuts the line and rows away north.
21. **Nineteen days drifting** — Food and water dwindle; relentless sun; Penny hides in cabin; Tyrion brings her food, limps on wounded calf; fiery fingers light nightfire and keep weapons close; Jorah sharpens sword daily.
22. **Tyrion's dream** — Dreams of shooting Penny with crossbow while Tywin says "wherever whores go."
23. **Ship spotted** — Tyrion wakes to shouting; Penny spots a sail. A big galley closing on them.
24. **Tyrion kisses Penny** — In joy at rescue; once on each cheek, once on brow, once on mouth.
25. **Slaver identified** — Jorah identifies the approaching galley as a slaver by smell and draws his sword.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Shifts through humiliation (jousting), defiant humor (provoking Jorah), pain (after being hit), tenderness (toward Penny), terror (during storm), dark humor (after storm), bitter resignation (during drift), brief joy (rescue ship), then dread (it's a slaver)
- **Primary preoccupation:** Survival — keeping the crew appeased, managing Jorah, enduring the storm and its aftermath; recurring thoughts of where whores go and the people he's killed
- **Key decisions made:** Consents to perform the mummer's joust; provokes Jorah despite risk; refuses Penny's kiss by claiming fidelity to Sansa; decides to pretend his disagreement with Mormont didn't happen after the storm
- **Self-deception flags:** Claims fidelity to Sansa as a noble reason, but internally calls it a "blatant lie" and thinks Penny is "my perfect woman" for believing it; maintains dark humor as emotional armor while genuinely frightened; pretends not to be disturbed by Mormont hitting him ("One day he might want to bite someone in the leg, and for that you needed teeth")
