# ADWD — Cersei I

## Chapter Metadata

- **chapter_number:** 55
- **pov_character:** Cersei Lannister

- **first_available:** ADWD Cersei I
- **location_primary:** Great Sept of Baelor (tower cell)
- **location_secondary:** High Sparrow's sanctum (within the Great Sept)
- **approximate_timeline:** During Cersei's extended imprisonment by the Faith; she has lost track of how long she has been confined
- **time_markers:** Nights described as progressively colder; sleep broken into pieces by the hours of the owl, wolf, nightingale; moonrise and moonset, dusk and dawn referenced; one full night of sleep after confession; dawn the next morning when Kevan arrives; Cersei has lost all sense of how long she has been imprisoned
- **chapter_summary:** Cersei endures cold, sleep deprivation, and isolation in her tower cell in the Great Sept of Baelor. Desperate and exhausted, she decides to confess. She admits to fornication with Lancel Lannister and the three Kettleblack brothers before the High Sparrow, but denies the charges of murder, regicide, and incest. The High Sparrow announces she will still face trial. She is moved to a better cell and the next morning receives a visit from Ser Kevan Lannister, who tells her Jaime has disappeared with a woman (possibly Brienne), Myrcella has been attacked and maimed in Dorne by Gerold Dayne, and that Mace Tyrell is the new Hand. Cersei asks Kevan to go to Qyburn about filling an empty Kingsguard place.

## Events & Actions
1. **Cersei endures imprisonment** — Confined in a cold, tiny tower cell with no heat, minimal food, and constant sleep deprivation enforced by three septas demanding confession.
2. **Cersei reflects on her betrayers** — She catalogs all who abandoned her: Osney Kettleblack (confessed under torture), Aurane Waters (fled with fleet), the Merryweathers (fled to Longtable), Harys Swyft and Pycelle (offered realm to her enemies), Lancel (accuser), her uncle Kevan (refused to be Hand).
3. **Cersei attempts multiple strategies** — She tries threats, commands, invoking the Mother's mercy, charm, rewards, and extensive prayer; all fail.
4. **Cersei decides to confess** — Exhausted, feverish, and desperate, she resolves that confession is her only path forward.
5. **Cersei confesses to Septa Unella** — She kneels and declares she has sinned, asks to be taken to the High Septon.
6. **Cersei sleeps through the night** — First unbroken sleep since imprisonment; dreams of Jaime as her husband and their son still alive.
7. **Cersei is led to the High Sparrow's sanctum** — The three septas escort her down the tower stairs; Scolera lets slip that Margaery is still with Tommen but Unella silences her.
8. **Cersei confesses fornication** — She admits to bedding Lancel Lannister and all three Kettleblack brothers, framing it as desperate acts to protect her children.
9. **Cersei denies murder charges** — She denies ordering the murder of the previous High Septon (blaming Osney's independent action), denies conspiring to kill Robert, and denies incest.
10. **High Sparrow announces trial** — He declares she will still face trial for the charges she denies; he allows her one visitor per day.
11. **Cersei is returned to her cell** — She fantasizes about shoving Scolera down the stairs.
12. **Cersei is moved to a better cell** — Larger, with a real window, warm blankets, and proper food (roast capon, greens, mashed neeps).
13. **Ser Kevan visits at dawn** — He is angry about Lancel but forgives Cersei; delivers hard tidings.
14. **Kevan reports Jaime missing** — Jaime took Raventree and accepted Blackwood's surrender but then left with a woman, possibly Brienne of Tarth; no further word.
15. **Kevan reports sellswords in the south** — Landing at Tarth, the Stepstones, Cape Wrath; possibly hired by Stannis; Mace Tyrell refuses to act until Margaery's matter is settled.
16. **Kevan explains he cannot free her by force** — The best of their forces are in the riverlands with Jaime; High Sparrow requires atonement.
17. **Walk of atonement proposed and refused** — Kevan says the High Sparrow wants a walk before the city; Cersei flatly refuses ("I would sooner die").
18. **Kevan announces the charges** — Regicide, deicide, incest, and high treason; trial by Faith or trial by battle with a Kingsguard champion.
19. **Kevan reveals the new governance** — He is Lord Regent; Mace Tyrell is Hand; Pycelle and Swyft continue; Redwyne is lord admiral; Tarly is justiciar.
20. **Kevan reports on Margaery** — Released to Randyll Tarly's custody; case against her is weak; her alleged lovers have recanted except the half-mad Blue Bard.
21. **Kevan reports Myrcella attacked** — Gerold Dayne (Darkstar) slashed her face and cut off her ear; Arys Oakheart slain defending her.
22. **Cersei blames Tyrion** — She insists Tyrion is behind the attack on Myrcella, citing his lost nose at Blackwater as paralleling Myrcella's mutilation.
23. **Cersei requests Kevan go to Qyburn** — She asks him to bring Qyburn a white cloak and tell him "the time has come" to fill the empty Kingsguard place.

- **Emotional state:** Oscillating between desperation, rage, hatred, and calculated manipulation; beneath it all, terror about her situation and children; brief genuine tears when granted visitors
