# ADWD — The Wayward Bride I

## Chapter Metadata

- **chapter_number:** 26
- **pov_character:** Asha Greyjoy

- **first_available:** ADWD The Wayward Bride I
- **location_primary:** Deepwood Motte
- **location_secondary:** Wolfswood, surrounding forest (battle site)
- **approximate_timeline:** After Moat Cailin's fall; Stannis has raised his standards at the Wall; Euron has sailed with the Iron Fleet into the Sunset Sea
- **time_markers:** Sun sinking behind tall pines (evening); moon almost full, night clear enough to see mountains; predawn gloom during battle; shafts of pale dawn light slanting through trees at battle's end; "the sun will be up in a few hours" when they stop to rest
- **chapter_summary:** Asha Greyjoy receives a letter from Ramsay Bolton written in blood announcing the fall of Moat Cailin and threatening the ironborn. She reflects on her hopeless military position at Deepwood Motte and her options. After a conversation with Tris Botley about the precedent of Torgon the Latecomer, northmen scouts attempt to infiltrate the castle. Asha rallies her men and flees south through the wolfswood toward her ships, but the northmen catch them in the forest. After a brutal predawn battle, Asha is struck down by a northman's axe, then hears a trumpet and dreams of Stannis's sigil.

## Events & Actions
1. **Letter received** — Galbart Glover's maester brings Asha a letter sealed with pink wax from Barrowton, written by Ramsay Bolton in blood, announcing the fall of Moat Cailin and threatening ironborn with a piece of Theon's skin enclosed.
2. **Asha burns the skin** — She holds the scrap of skin to a candle and watches it burn.
3. **Council discussion** — Asha's men debate options: Cromm wants to fight, Quenton and Dagon want to join Dagmer at Torrhen's Square, Tris warns of doom.
4. **Asha retires to bedchamber** — Climbs to Galbart Glover's bedchamber at sunset, head pounding from wine.
5. **Sexual encounter with Qarl** — Qarl follows her; their violent/passionate roleplay sex scene.
6. **Asha's reflections** — She considers her situation: father dead, mother dying, brother flayed, married to Erik Ironmaker, Euron in control of the islands.
7. **Asha surveys from window** — Observes mountains, assesses Deepwood's position relative to the sea and the Wall.
8. **Detailed description of Deepwood's defenses** — Narrator describes the castle's layout and condition.
9. **Asha dresses and descends** — Goes to kitchens for food; sentry salutes her; dogs bark.
10. **Tris Botley's proposal** — In the kitchen, Tris urges escape by sea as traders; proposes voyage to Jade Sea; Asha discusses alternatives including Sea Dragon Point and returning to Pyke.
11. **Tris tells tale of Torgon the Latecomer** — Historical precedent for overturning a kingsmoot; Asha kisses him in excitement.
12. **Warhorn sounds** — Hagen blows alarm; five northmen caught attempting to scale walls.
13. **Asha interrogates the Flint** — She tortures and kills the wounded infiltrator who reveals "thousands" are coming to attack.
14. **Assembly in bailey** — Galbart Glover's folk and Asha's men gather; Lady Sybelle and maester offer terms; Asha rallies her men.
15. **Decision to flee** — Asha decides to abandon Deepwood and make for the ships rather than defend.
16. **Hagen shot** — An arrow catches Hagen in the belly as he descends the watchtower; his daughter runs to him.
17. **Escape from Deepwood** — South gate opened; ironborn ride and march into wolfswood as ram hits north gate and trumpets sound.
18. **March through forest** — Quenton's mare breaks leg in pit; Asha orders stop before dawn to rest.
19. **Rolfe the Dwarf's head thrown into camp** — Scouts killed; severed head rolled into their midst.
20. **Forest battle begins** — Northmen in camouflage attack from all sides; brutal close-quarters fight in predawn darkness.
21. **Asha fights** — Kills multiple northmen with throwing axe, dirk, shield, and acquired short sword.
22. **Quenton Greyjoy killed** — Axe driven into base of his skull by a northman.
23. **Hagen's daughter fights naked** — Emerges from trees naked with wolves chasing her; Asha saves her with throwing axe; she kills the second pursuer.
24. **Asha struck down** — A large bald bearded northman with axe drives her against a tree; axehead crunches against her temple.
25. **Trumpet sounds** — As Asha falls, a trumpet blows.
26. **Dream vision** — Asha dreams of red hearts burning and a black stag in a golden wood with flame streaming from his antlers.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Black mood deepening to despair; brief pleasure with Qarl; increasing grimness about her situation; flashes of gallows humor during battle; fatalism tempered by survival instinct; excitement at Torgon precedent; resignation mixed with defiance
- **Primary preoccupation:** Her impossible strategic position — cut off from the islands, married against her will, father dead, brother being tortured, castle indefensible, no good options
- **Key decisions made:** (1) Refuses to answer Bolton's letter; (2) Decides to flee Deepwood rather than defend it; (3) Orders march west then north to the ships; (4) Orders stop in forest to wait for dawn rather than blunder in darkness
- **Self-deception flags:** "If my father still lived, Moat Cailin would never have fallen" — idealizing Balon; "He is my blood. My father's brother" — feeble reasoning about finding Aeron; acknowledges her own self-deception about her men fleeing ("No. That is not just. They sailed home to do homage to their king. If anyone fled, it was me.")
