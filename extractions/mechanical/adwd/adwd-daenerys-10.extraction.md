- **chapter_summary:** Daenerys descends from the hill she calls Dragonstone and walks south through the Dothraki sea, following a stream toward the Skahazadhan river. Hungry, fevered, and increasingly weak, she eats poisonous berries that make her violently ill. She has visions of Quaithe, a dream-conversation with Viserys, and hears Jorah Mormont's voice in the grass urging her toward Westeros. After waking with her moon blood, she encounters a lone Dothraki scout who flees at the sight of Drogon. She mounts the dragon, flies after the scout, and Drogon kills a horse from a vast herd. Khal Jhaqo finds her beside the dragon with half a hundred mounted warriors.

## Events & Actions
1. **Daenerys descends from Dragonstone** — Takes half the morning to climb down the rocky hill; hands scraped raw, muscles aching, burns healing.
2. **Dany sets off south through the grass** — Walks briskly, tapping thigh with whip, barefoot; notes the dying autumn grass.
3. **Dany finds the stream** — Arrives in the afternoon at a tiny rivulet no wider than her arm; splashes face, drinks muddy water.
4. **Dany considers returning to Dragonstone** — Tempted by fish and safety, but resolves to keep going south; turns her back on the hill.
5. **Dany walks all afternoon** — Follows the winding stream; hears insects, sees rat, hears birds; spots Drogon three times overhead.
6. **Dany finds ruined village** — At evening, stumbles onto overgrown stone wall, old well, eight circles marking former hovels; makes a grass nest in wall corner.
7. **Dany lies awake thinking** — Contemplates whether Hizdahr is still king; suspects poison via honeyed locusts; hears a wolf howl.
8. **First dream: Quaithe** — Dreams of flying; hears Quaithe's riddle repeated ("To go north, you must journey south..."); sees her mask of starlight; Quaithe says "Remember who you are, Daenerys. The dragons know. Do you?"
9. **Dany wakes covered in ants** — Morning; finds anthill on other side of wall; brushes off hundreds of ants; remembers Viserys telling of hedge knights.
10. **Dany resumes walking south** — Relocates stream at eight paces; drinks despite cramps; continues limping along rivulet.
11. **Dany eats green berries** — Past midday, finds bush with hard green berries; eats many; an hour later begins violent cramping and retching green slime.
12. **Dany spends the day and night violently ill** — Retching, then loose stools, then shitting brown water; can barely drink; fears she is dying.
13. **Second dream: Viserys** — Extended dream-conversation with dead Viserys; he accuses her of betrayal; says she got the dragon eggs that should have been his; his jaw falls away smoking.
14. **Dany wakes with moon blood** — Thighs slick with blood; initially fears death, then realizes it's her period; notes it seems unusually heavy and the moon is wrong for her cycle.
15. **Dany hears Jorah's voice in the grass** — Hallucination/vision of Ser Jorah speaking; he urges her toward Westeros; "Dragons plant no trees"; she says "Fire and Blood."
16. **Dany senses presence in the grass** — Sees grass moving with no wind; finds a stone; hears silvery tinkling of bells.
17. **Dothraki scout appears** — Single rider with bells, arakh, whip, bow; sees Drogon's shadow and flees at a gallop.
18. **Dany mounts Drogon** — Calls him; he comes snorting smoke; she leaps onto his back; kicks him into the sky heading north by east after the scout.
19. **Drogon kills a horse** — They pass the scout; find a vast herd with twenty-plus riders who flee; Drogon descends on a lagging horse, sets it aflame, breaks its back.
20. **Dany eats raw horsemeat** — Slides off Drogon's back; rips chunks of smoking meat from dead horse with bare burned hands; eats beside the dragon.
21. **Khal Jhaqo finds Daenerys** — As western sky turns blood-bruise color, half a hundred mounted warriors emerge from drifting smoke and find her standing beside Drogon.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Loneliness, hunger, physical pain, fever; moments of strange happiness recalling flight; grief over lost companions; fear of dying; growing resolution and determination; sadness over Viserys; longing for Jorah, Daario; rising sense of purpose by chapter's end
- **Primary preoccupation:** Survival (food, water, direction); identity crisis — queen vs. dragonrider vs. Meereenese wife; whether to return to Meereen or remember who she truly is; recurring question of "who poisoned me?"
- **Key decisions made:** Decides to leave Dragonstone and walk south (already made before chapter opens); resolves not to look back; after Jorah's voice and Quaithe's dream, embraces "Fire and Blood" identity; mounts Drogon and flies toward the Dothraki rather than away; says "To go forward I must go back"
- **Self-deception flags:** Tells herself Meereen is home, then immediately undercuts it ("Meereen was not her home, and never would be"); tries to convince herself moon blood is normal despite noting the timing is wrong and the flow unusually heavy; rationalizes that Hizdahr had no reason to want her dead even while suspecting him
