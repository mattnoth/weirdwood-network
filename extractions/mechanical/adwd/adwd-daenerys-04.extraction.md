- **chapter_summary:** Daenerys receives the Green Grace Galazza Galare for a private supper, during which the priestess urges her to marry Hizdahr zo Loraq to end the Sons of the Harpy killings. Dany summons Hizdahr and gives him ninety days to stop the murders as proof of his worthiness. Ser Barristan counsels her to return to Westeros and reveals that her grandparents' marriage was commanded because a woods witch prophesied the prince that was promised would come from their line. Daario Naharis returns from Lhazar, reports success, and urges violent action against the Great Masters; Dany sends him away and orders the Stormcrows back to the field, barring Daario from her presence.

## Events & Actions
1. **Galazza Galare arrives** — The Green Grace comes to the Great Pyramid attended by a dozen White Graces
2. **Private supper begins** — Dany sends Missandei to care for the White Graces while she dines alone with the Green Grace
3. **Discussion of naval blockade** — Dany reports that Qartheen galleys sailed up the Skahazadhan last night; three New Ghis galleys and a Tolos carrack have joined the blockade
4. **Mantarys and Tolos responses revealed** — Tolos called Dany a whore and demanded she return Meereen to Great Masters; Mantarys sent back her envoys' pickled heads in a cedar chest
5. **Sons of the Harpy murders discussed** — Three freedwomen weavers were raped and killed the previous night; their loom broken
6. **Green Grace urges marriage** — Galazza Galare recommends Dany marry Hizdahr zo Loraq to reconcile the city
7. **Prophecy of united bloodlines** — Green Grace says a son of dragon and harpy will fulfill prophecies and enemies will "melt away like snow"
8. **Dany recalls Mirri Maz Duur's curse** — Thinks "when the sun rises in the west and sets in the east" — believes she cannot bear children
9. **Green Grace departs** — Leaves after finishing the meal, saying she will pray at the Temple of the Graces
10. **Hizdahr zo Loraq arrives** — Summoned up the pyramid with Unsullied assistance
11. **Hizdahr states his case** — Offers gold, friends, influence, old Ghiscari blood, and two natural children; claims he can end the killings
12. **Dany questions if Hizdahr is a Harpy's Son** — He denies it but admits he would not confess if he were
13. **The kiss** — Dany commands Hizdahr to kiss her; his kiss is light, dry, and quick; she feels nothing
14. **Ninety-day challenge issued** — Dany tells Hizdahr to stop the murders for ninety days and nights to prove his worth; he accepts
15. **Hizdahr departs** — Confident, tells Dany to have her seneschal prepare for the wedding
16. **Ser Barristan expresses disapproval** — The old knight questions the marriage decision
17. **Barristan counsels Westeros** — Argues her place is on the Iron Throne; Meereen will never fully accept her
18. **Barristan reveals Rhaegar's marriage** — Says Rhaegar was "fond" of Elia but it was not a love match
19. **Barristan reveals Aerys and Rhaella's marriage** — Commanded by Dany's grandsire because a woods witch prophesied the prince that was promised from their line
20. **Woods witch described** — Came to court with Jenny of Oldstones; "a stunted thing, grotesque to look upon"; called a dwarf; Jenny claimed she was a child of the forest; died at Summerhall
21. **Daario Naharis arrives** — Returns from Lhazar mission; enters swaggering
22. **Daario reports on mission** — Defeated the Long Lances in the Khyzai Pass; slew two of his own serjeants for plotting theft; lost nine men but gained twelve former Long Lances
23. **Daario reports Lhazar success** — Grain will come by barge down the Skahazadhan; other goods by caravan over Khyzai
24. **Dany informs Daario of river closure** — The Skahazadhan is closed; Qartheen have driven off or seized two-thirds of fishing fleet
25. **Dragon situation discussed** — Drogon not returned to city, seen north of river; Viserion snapped a chain; both pit dragons growing savage; iron doors glowed red-hot
26. **Daario reports Astapor cannibalism** — A Long Lance prisoner confirmed people eating each other in Astapor; Daario cut out his tongue and fed it to a dog to test truth
27. **Daario proposes violent solutions** — Urges attacking Great Masters; suggests massacring them at a wedding in the Temple of the Graces
28. **Dany refuses and sends Daario away** — Calls his proposal monstrous; orders him to leave
29. **Dany bars Daario from her presence** — Orders Stormcrows back to field; Daario must report to Barristan; forbidden from seeing her
30. **Sleepless night** — Dany summons Irri to her bed but pushes her away; reflects that she and Daario are both monsters

- **Emotional state:** Exhausted, burdened by responsibility, torn between desire (Daario) and duty (marriage to Hizdahr); sexually frustrated; conflicted about capacity for violence; lonely

- **Key decisions made:** (1) Gives Hizdahr 90 days to prove himself by stopping murders; (2) Bars Daario from her presence and sends Stormcrows back to the field; (3) Chooses duty over desire
