- **chapter_summary:** Imprisoned in a tower cell of Ashford Castle after striking Prince Aerion, Dunk is brought food by his squire Egg, who reveals that his true name is Aegon, that he is Prince Maekar's son and Prince Baelor's nephew, and explains how his brother Daeron shaved his head to hide him. Baelor summons Dunk to the solar and explains that he must be tried for laying hands on the blood of the dragon — a crime that could cost him a hand and a foot — but that as an accused knight he may demand trial by combat. At the hearing Aerion invokes his right to a trial of seven, and Maekar tells Dunk he must find six other knights to fight beside him. Leaving the castle, Dunk finds his horses at the Fossoway pavilion, where Ser Steffon pledges to fight and goes to recruit friends; Egg and Daeron then arrive, and Daeron warns that Maekar has commanded the three Kingsguard present to join the seven accusers.
- **unit_part:** 5 of 7

## Events & Actions
1. **Dunk is confined in a tower cell** — Lord Ashford's men hold him in a windowed chamber; they take his sword belt, sword, dagger, and silver, and forbid him to speak to anyone.
   - Agent: Lord Ashford's men
   - Patient: Dunk
   - Location: the tower cell, Ashford Castle
   - Instigator: Lord Ashford / the crown
   - Outcome: Dunk imprisoned, awaiting trial
2. **Dunk waits out the day listening to the tourney** — He sits in the window seat from sunrise to dusk, hearing the heralds' horns and the crowd, ignoring his empty belly.
   - Agent: Dunk
   - Location: the tower cell
3. **Egg brings guards, a lamp, and food; dismisses the guards** — Egg orders, "Leave the lamp and the food and go"; they obey but leave the door ajar.
   - Agent: Egg (Aegon)
   - Patient: the guards / servingman
   - Location: the tower cell
   - Outcome: Dunk fed; Egg alone with him
4. **Egg reveals his name is Aegon and explains the deception** — He tells Dunk his true name, that Aemon named him Egg, that Daeron shaved his head to hide his Targaryen coloring, and asks forgiveness at his uncle's behest.
   - Agent: Egg (Aegon)
   - Patient: Dunk
   - Location: the tower cell
   - Outcome: Dunk learns Egg is Prince Aegon
5. **Egg conducts Dunk to Baelor's solar** — After Dunk eats, Egg leads him to Lord Ashford's solar where Baelor waits.
   - Agent: Egg (Aegon)
   - Patient: Dunk
   - Location: cell → the solar
6. **Baelor offers wine; Aegon pours** — Baelor bids Dunk rise and commands Aegon to pour the sweet Dornish red.
   - Agent: Prince Baelor / Aegon (pouring)
   - Patient: Dunk
   - Instrument: the silver goblet
   - Location: the solar
7. **Baelor explains the charges and Dunk's peril, then offers trial by combat** — He dismisses Aegon, tells Dunk that Maekar has made him a target, that Aerion has filled his father's ear, that Daeron blamed a "robber knight," that the puppet show is being cast as treason, that Dunk could lose a hand and a foot, and that he may demand trial by combat.
   - Agent: Prince Baelor
   - Patient: Dunk
   - Location: the solar
   - Outcome: Dunk faces trial; offered trial by combat
8. **Aerion demands a trial of seven at the hearing** — Before the dais (Baelor, Maekar, Ashford, Leo Tyrell), Aerion invokes his right to a trial of seven so that both he and Daeron may face Dunk; Maekar challenges his motive, Daeron protests, and Leo Tyrell affirms the right.
   - Agent: Prince Aerion
   - Patient: Dunk
   - Location: the hearing (Ashford Castle)
   - Outcome: a trial of seven decreed
9. **Maekar orders Dunk to find six knights** — Maekar tells Dunk it must be seven against seven and he must find six other knights; "If you can find no champions, ser, it will be because you are guilty."
   - Agent: Prince Maekar
   - Patient: Dunk
   - Location: the hearing
   - Outcome: Dunk must recruit six champions
10. **Dunk leaves the castle; his sword and silver are returned** — The portcullis rattles down behind him; a soft rain falls; he wades across the ford past the empty lists.
    - Agent: Dunk
    - Location: Ashford Castle → the ford → the lists
    - Outcome: Dunk freed to seek champions before dawn
11. **Dunk finds his horses at the Fossoway pavilion** — He recognizes Thunder's snort and finds Thunder and Chestnut tied outside a round pavilion bearing the Fossoway apple.
    - Agent: Dunk
    - Location: outside the Fossoway pavilion
12. **Ser Steffon pledges to fight and leaves to recruit friends** — Steffon declares "I am for you," names potential champions (Leo Longthorn, the Laughing Storm, Lord Caron, the Lannisters, Ser Otho Bracken, the Blackwoods), and strides off to wake them.
    - Agent: Ser Steffon Fossoway
    - Patient: Dunk (in his defense)
    - Location: the Fossoway pavilion
    - Outcome: Steffon leaves to seek five more knights
13. **Egg and Daeron arrive at the pavilion** — A boy ducks in, followed by a thin man in a rain-sodden black cloak; Egg says he has come to squire, and Daeron reveals himself.
    - Agent: Egg (Aegon); Prince Daeron
    - Location: the Fossoway pavilion
14. **Dunk draws his knife on Daeron** — Angered by Daeron's lie about him, Dunk pulls his knife and threatens to "shove this through your belly"; Daeron sits and asks for wine instead.
    - Agent: Dunk
    - Patient: Prince Daeron
    - Instrument: Dunk's knife
    - Location: the Fossoway pavilion
    - Outcome: no violence; Daeron unruffled
15. **Daeron warns that Maekar commanded the three Kingsguard to fight as accusers** — Daeron reveals his father has ordered the three Kingsguard present (Roland Crakehall, Donnel of Duskendale, Willem Wylde) to join the seven accusers.
    - Agent: Prince Daeron
    - Patient: Dunk
    - Location: the Fossoway pavilion
    - Outcome: Dunk learns six of the seven accusers
16. **Egg recounts Aerion's childhood cruelties** — Egg tells how Aerion put a knife between his legs at night threatening to make him his sister, and threw his cat in the well.
    - Agent: Egg (Aegon)
    - Patient: Dunk (listener)
    - Location: the Fossoway pavilion
17. **Daeron asks Dunk to walk with him for a private word** — Daeron says he must steal back to the castle but wants a private word; Dunk agrees and sheathes his dagger, saying he must fetch his shield.
    - Agent: Prince Daeron
    - Patient: Dunk
    - Location: the Fossoway pavilion → outside

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Gloomy and self-mocking in the cell ("Dunk the lunk, thick as a castle wall and slow as an aurochs"); bitter ("What did I think would happen? I knocked down a prince's son and kicked him in the face"); ravenous; aghast at the threatened loss of hand and foot ("My hand?... My foot?"); "Dunk had never felt so alone as he did when he walked out the gates"; bleak and shivering in the rain; a flicker of hope at the Fossoway banner ("It looked like hope").
- **Primary preoccupation:** Finding six knights willing to fight beside him in the trial of seven before dawn — "Six knights, Dunk thought. They might as well have told him to find six thousand."
- **Key decisions made:** To choose trial by combat over judgment (inferred — the hearing that follows is a trial of seven); not to flee though he could ("Better to die a knight than live like that, he told himself stubbornly"); to accept Daeron's request to walk and talk privately.
- **Self-deception flags:** Repeatedly calls himself stupid — "Dunk the lunk, thick as a castle wall"; "Thick as a castle wall, Dunk"; "He had never been any good with words."
