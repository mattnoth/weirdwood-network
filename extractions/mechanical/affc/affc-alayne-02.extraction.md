# AFFC — Alayne II

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** AFFC
- **chapter_number:** 42
- **pov_character:** Alayne (Sansa Stark)
- **pov_chapter_number:** Alayne II
- **first_available:** AFFC Alayne II
- **location_primary:** The Eyrie; the descent path down the Giant's Lance; Gates of the Moon
- **location_secondary:** Sky (waycastle), Snow (waycastle), Stone (waycastle), Gulltown, Runestone, King's Landing (referenced)
- **approximate_timeline:** Late autumn in the Vale; the Eyrie is being closed for winter; snow and ice have set in on the mountain peaks while autumn lingers in the valley below
- **time_markers:** Pale morning light at opening; they must get below Snow before sun goes down; afternoon sun mentioned during steepest descent; dusk settling as they leave Stone; half the night gone before sighting Gates of the Moon; hour closer to dawn than dusk when they arrive; three snowstorms and an ice storm (fortnight of crystal) since Lysa's death; sweetmilk given "not three days past"; Petyr arrived at evenfall
- **chapter_summary:** Alayne coaxes the sickly, fearful Robert Arryn from bed so the last party can descend the Eyrie before winter seals the mountain. After persuading Maester Colemon to give Robert sweetsleep, she leads the descent through the three waycastles—Sky, Snow, and Stone—while befriending the shrewd Lady Myranda Royce, who shares news of the realm. At the Gates of the Moon, Alayne discovers Petyr Baelish has returned early with three hedge knights, and he reveals his plan: a betrothal between Alayne Stone and Harrold Hardyng, heir to the Vale, culminating in Sansa Stark's eventual unmasking to rally the Vale lords for Winterfell.

## Events & Actions
1. **Alayne approaches Robert's darkened bedchamber** — Gretchel warns that Robert threw his chamber pot at Maester Colemon; Alayne dismisses the servants and enters alone.
2. **Alayne coaxes Robert out of bed** — He is petulant, complains of headache, demands stories of the Winged Knight, refuses to leave; she promises lemon cakes and tales, appeals to his vanity about strength.
3. **Robert kisses Alayne** — A clumsy child's kiss; triggers her memories of Ser Loras and the Hound's kiss during the Battle of the Blackwater.
4. **Alayne orders the servants** — Instructs Maddy and Gretchel to bathe Robert; commands Terrance to lay out riding clothes; assigns Gyles to clean the broken chamber pot.
5. **Alayne discusses Robert's health with Maester Colemon** — He reports trembling, worries about sweetsleep accumulating in the flesh; she overrules him, insisting on a dose for the descent.
6. **Alayne crosses the courtyard and reflects on the Eyrie** — Notes its beauty but emptiness; no septon, no heart tree; thinks it will be a cold white prison in winter. Reflects on the danger of being unmasked below.
7. **Alayne finds Lothor Brune in the winch room** — He warns that Mya won't wait past midday; tells her Lady Myranda Royce has come up with Mya.
8. **Alayne dresses for the descent** — Dons heavy warm clothing; fastens cloak with enameled mockingbird; takes a last look at her room.
9. **Mya Stone arrives in the winch room** — Demands haste; warns it's getting colder; they need to get below Snow before sundown.
10. **Robert Arryn emerges dressed for travel** — Wears sky-blue velvet, gold and sapphire chain, white bearskin cloak; Mya asks which mule he'll ride.
11. **Descent in the oaken bucket to Sky** — Alayne and Robert ride down together in the largest bucket; a gust catches them, bucket swings and hits rock; Robert clings to Alayne; she reassures him.
12. **Arrival at Sky** — Lady Myranda Royce greets Robert with flattery and kisses; introduces herself to Alayne; twenty mules waiting.
13. **Mya organizes the descent party** — Assigns Ossy and Carrot their groups; gives an hour's lead to her own party.
14. **The party rides down the mountain** — Myranda shares news: Petyr's letter, Corbray wedding, Lady Waynwood's attendance, war news (Riverrun yielded, Dragonstone/Storm's End hold for Stannis), new High Septon, Jon Snow as Lord Commander.
15. **Alayne reacts to Jon Snow's appointment** — Blurts out his name; reflects that with Robb, Bran, and Rickon dead, Jon is her only remaining brother; remembers she must be Alayne.
16. **Myranda discusses Harry the Heir** — Explains he's Lady Waynwood's ward, Harrold Hardyng; knighted by Bronze Yohn at Runestone mêlée. She wanted to marry him herself.
17. **Myranda gossips extensively** — About her dead husband, Marillion, Mya's virginity, Mychel Redfort, Ser Lothor's feelings for Mya, whether Petyr will remarry, the size of "Littlefinger."
18. **The steep descent with worn stone steps** — Path narrows; Myranda forced to drop back; steps worn into shallow bowls of water; Alayne clings to saddle.
19. **The stone saddle crossing** — Robert begins shaking at the narrow, icy crossing; Alayne takes his hand, calls him "Ser Sweetrobin" and her "winged knight"; they cross hand in hand.
20. **Sansa thinks "a ghost wolf, big as mountains"** — The wind sounds like a wolf; she thinks as Sansa momentarily.
21. **Mya catches Robert at the other side** — Alayne warns her about his flailing; they shelter him until the shaking passes.
22. **Hot meal at Snow** — Alayne eats with Mya and Myranda; Myranda compliments her bravery; Mya insists she never falls; tells her memory of being thrown in the air by her father.
23. **Snow begins falling as they leave Stone** — Myranda suggests staying the night; Mya refuses, says snow could be five feet deep by morning.
24. **Night descent to Gates of the Moon** — Slow progress through pines and sentinels; Robert sleeps in saddle; Myranda yawns; lights of castle seen through falling snow.
25. **Arrival at Gates of the Moon** — Myranda offers her bed; Alayne accepts; a guardsman tells Alayne the Lord Protector has been waiting.
26. **Alayne meets Petyr Baelish** — He sits by crackling fire with three hedge knights (Ser Byron, Ser Morgarth, Ser Shadrich); he has her kiss him properly after a dutiful first kiss.
27. **Petyr shares political intelligence** — Cersei's incompetence; the realm beggar'd; three queens; chaos accelerating his plans.
28. **Petyr reveals the betrothal** — Alayne is to marry Harrold Hardyng, heir to the Vale; he explains the Arryn succession in detail; the plan: when Robert dies, Harry becomes Lord of the Vale, and Sansa's unmasking at their wedding will rally the Vale knights to reclaim Winterfell.
