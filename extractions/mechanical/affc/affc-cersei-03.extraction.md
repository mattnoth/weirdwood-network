# AFFC — Cersei III

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** AFFC
- **chapter_number:** 13
- **pov_character:** Cersei Lannister
- **pov_chapter_number:** Cersei III
- **first_available:** AFFC Cersei III
- **location_primary:** Red Keep, King's Landing
- **location_secondary:** Royal sept (Red Keep), Small Hall (Red Keep), Tower of the Hand, outer ward (Red Keep)
- **approximate_timeline:** Day of King Tommen's wedding to Margaery Tyrell; shortly after Tywin Lannister's murder
- **time_markers:** Morning (Cersei dressing, breaking fast); wedding ceremony during the day; feast follows; burning of the Tower of the Hand at night (hunter's moon visible); Lord Hallyne says fires will burn "all night"; Guild of Alchemists had been brewing wildfire "for a fortnight"
- **chapter_summary:** Cersei prepares for and endures the wedding of her son Tommen to Margaery Tyrell. She argues with Jaime over security measures and the burning of the Tower of the Hand. The modest ceremony takes place in the Red Keep's royal sept, followed by a seven-course feast in the Small Hall. During the feast, Lady Merryweather reveals that Cersei's maid Senelle is spying for Margaery. After the feast, Cersei orders the Tower of the Hand burned with wildfire, watching the spectacle with Ser Osmund Kettleblack while Jaime escorts Tommen and Margaery to their bedchamber.

## Physical Environment
- **Weather:** Chill in the air (settling in Cersei's bones); Jocelyn Swyft prays it will not rain on the wedding
- **Season indicators:** None explicitly stated
- **Time of day:** Morning (dressing, breakfast) → daytime (wedding ceremony) → evening (feast) → night (burning of tower under hunter's moon)
- **Lighting:** Tallow candle in the back passage; wildfire light (red, yellow, orange, green) illuminating the night; hunter's moon
- **Sounds:** Music at feast (pipers, fiddlers, lute, flute, high harp); Blue Bard singing love songs; tower groaning and cracking; crash of falling masonry; conversations and laughter
- **Smells:** Lady Merryweather's perfume (musky, moss, earth, wildflowers); Cersei recalls the smell of Maggy the Frog's tent (queer eastern spices); smoke from the burning tower
- **Notable sensory details:** Heat from the wildfire felt by the queen; Cersei feels the chill settling in her bones; the taste of wine is "bile" to Cersei; Maggy the Frog's gums described as soft when sucking blood from Cersei's finger (memory)

## Events & Actions
1. **Cersei is dressed for the wedding** — Jocelyn Swyft laces her gown; Cersei snaps at her
2. **Cersei's breakfast ruined** — She finds a bloody half-formed chick inside a boiled egg; sends food away and orders hot spiced wine
3. **Jaime outlines security for the feast** — Men in kitchens, gold cloaks escorting servants, Ser Boros tasting food, Maester Ballabar with antidotes
4. **Cersei and Jaime argue about threats** — Cersei fears Tyrion is still in the walls; Jaime dismisses her concerns
5. **Cersei commands Jaime to guard Tommen's bedchamber** — She insists he stay inside the room all night, not just post a guard outside
6. **Cersei and Jaime discuss burning the Tower of the Hand** — Jaime warns wildfire is treacherous; Cersei insists Lord Hallyne can control it; Jaime says she sounds like Aerys
7. **Cersei recalls the search of the Tower of the Hand** — Half a hundred secret passages found, a passage to the black cells, a bottomless well, skulls and bones, silver coins; one boy stuck, another broke his legs, two guardsmen vanished and were never recovered
8. **Cersei fantasizes about building a new palace and moving the court** — Jaime argues Tommen must stay on the Iron Throne to be seen as king
9. **Wedding ceremony in the royal sept** — Fewer than 100 guests; Tommen recites vows; Margaery wears same gown as Joffrey's wedding; Robert's Baratheon cloak used instead of Lannister cloak
10. **Post-ceremony congratulations** — Lyle Crakehall, Gyles Rosby, Paxter Redwyne, Mace Tyrell, Grand Maester Pycelle all greet the couple; Stokeworth women notably absent
11. **Cersei speaks with Kevan Lannister** — He tells her Hardstone has cleared Darry; Lancel's bride awaits; he mentions Beric Dondarrion hanging Freys and asks about Sandor Clegane; Cersei tells him to bring Sandor's head; Kevan rebukes her ("When a dog goes bad, the fault lies with his master")
12. **Jaime escorts Cersei to the Small Hall** — She rants about the Tyrells, questions Margaery's virginity, rails against Olenna
13. **Margaery greets Cersei at the high table** — Kisses her cheeks, calls her "second mother"
14. **Wedding feast** — Seven courses; Butterbumps and Moon Boy entertain; musicians play; Blue Bard sings love songs
15. **Olenna requests "The Rains of Castamere"** — Pointed comment made loudly
16. **Cersei remembers Maggy the Frog's prophecy** — Triggered by seeing Olenna; recalls the tent, the blood-sucking, the prophecy about a younger, more beautiful queen
17. **Mace Tyrell leads toast; Redwyne toasts both queens** — Mace toasts "the king and queen"; Redwyne toasts "the young queen and the old"
18. **Tommen coughs on wine** — Cersei panics, knocks aside a serving girl to reach him; Margaery calms him, says it was just wine going down wrong
19. **Cersei weeps in the back passage** — Cannot bear to show tears; allows herself sobs in private
20. **Lady Merryweather finds Cersei** — Offers a handkerchief; reveals Senelle is bought by Margaery; explains the intelligence goes through Margaery's cousins (Elinor, Alla, Megga) who meet Senelle in the sept pretending to pray
21. **Cersei returns to the feast** — Observes guests; refuses dances from Jaime, Mace Tyrell, and Lancel
22. **Cersei notices Aurane Waters** — Shares wine with Elinor; Cersei recalls momentarily thinking he was Rhaegar returned
23. **Cersei notices Kevan talking with Garlan** — Deepens her suspicion of her uncle
24. **Cersei spots Senelle serving Margaery's cousins** — Resists urge to throttle her
25. **Jaime suggests Cersei has had enough wine** — She rises abruptly, nearly falls; announces the burning
26. **Guests file out to watch the Tower of the Hand burn** — They pass beneath its shadow
27. **Lord Hallyne ignites the Tower of the Hand** — Signals archers who shoot flaming arrows through windows; wildfire erupts in green, red, yellow, orange flames
28. **The tower burns spectacularly** — Fifty pots of wildfire, logs, pitch, and Tyrion's possessions burn; stone cracks and upper battlements collapse
29. **Cersei reflects on former Hands** — Lists Owen Merryweather, Jon Connington, Qarlton Chelsted, Jon Arryn, Eddard Stark, Tyrion, and Tywin; savors the thought of them all burning
30. **Olenna declares it's time for bed** — Cersei sends Jaime to escort Tommen and Margaery
31. **Cersei stays to watch the fire with Ser Osmund Kettleblack** — Feels the wildfire is cleansing her; slides her arm through his
