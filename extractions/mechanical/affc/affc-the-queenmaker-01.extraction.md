# AFFC — The Queenmaker

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** AFFC
- **chapter_number:** 22
- **pov_character:** Arianne Martell
- **pov_chapter_number:** The Queenmaker
- **first_available:** AFFC The Queenmaker
- **location_primary:** Shandystone (abandoned holdfast in Dorne)
- **location_secondary:** Dornish sands (route south and west), the Greenblood river, poleboat on the Greenblood
- **approximate_timeline:** Shortly after Tywin Lannister's death; Cersei has assumed the regency; Arianne's queenmaking plot is in motion
- **time_markers:** Arrival at Shandystone as the sun is going down; departure when the moon crowns the Moonmaid; riding through the night; eastern sky turns red at dawn; sun beating down during daytime riding; reach the Greenblood by daylight
- **chapter_summary:** Arianne Martell gathers her co-conspirators — Drey, Spotted Sylva, Garin, and Darkstar — at the abandoned holdfast of Shandystone to await Ser Arys Oakheart and Princess Myrcella. Arianne plans to crown Myrcella queen under Dornish succession law to provoke conflict with the Lannisters. Ser Arys arrives with Myrcella and reports that Tywin Lannister has been murdered by the Imp. The party rides through the night toward the Greenblood, where they find their poleboat — but Areo Hotah and Doran's guardsmen are waiting in ambush. Ser Arys charges and is killed by Hotah. Myrcella is injured (blood streaming through her fingers on her face). Arianne and her companions are captured.

## Physical Environment
- **Weather:** Hot and dry; a wind gusting from the west described as "hot and dry and full of grit"; cloudless sky during daytime riding
- **Season indicators:** No explicit season statement; consistent extreme heat and desert conditions typical of Dorne
- **Time of day:** Chapter opens at sunset; transitions to full night with stars and moonlight; then through dawn (eastern sky turns red) into full daylight; the sun described as "beating down like a fiery hammer"
- **Lighting:** Sunset tapestry of gold, purple, and crimson clouds; fallen columns glimmering pinkly with red shadows; firelight from the campfire; starlight and moonlight during the night ride; full sun during day
- **Sounds:** Rattling of small green pearls on Arianne's veil; crossbow thrumming; Hotah bellowing; Myrcella shrieking; the soft splash of Arys's head landing in the Greenblood
- **Smells:** None explicitly described
- **Notable sensory details:** Sands changing color from gold to orange to purple as light fades; sweat running down Ser Arys's face in the heat; the grit of desert wind; the Greenblood's murky green waters appearing to turn gold in sunlight

## Events & Actions
1. **Arianne arrives at Shandystone** — Arianne, Drey, and Spotted Sylva arrive at the abandoned holdfast at sunset. Garin arrived a few hours earlier, Darkstar the day before.
2. **Conspirators make camp** — Garin gathers bleached wood, Drey builds a fire, and the group sits around the flames passing summerwine.
3. **Garin shares news from the Planky Town** — Reports of a slave revolt in Astapor, dragons in Qarth, grey plague in Yi Ti, a new corsair king in the Basilisk Isles, red priest riots in Qohor, and the Golden Company breaking its contract with Myr.
4. **Arianne internally connects the Golden Company to Quentyn** — She fears her brother may have the Golden Company behind him, sealed scrolls bearing the Dornish sigil found in his strongbox.
5. **Darkstar challenges Arianne's plan** — He argues crowning Myrcella is a hollow gesture, draws his sword, and suggests war should start with steel not a crown. Arianne refuses, saying she is "no murderer of children."
6. **Darkstar notes the approaching horseman** — Ser Arys Oakheart arrives on a grey palfrey with Myrcella riding pillion, her golden curls hidden in a cowled robe.
7. **Conspirators kneel to Myrcella as queen** — Drey, Spotted Sylva, and Garin each kneel and address Myrcella as "Your Grace" or "my queen." Myrcella is confused and frightened.
8. **Arianne introduces the conspirators to Myrcella** — She presents Drey (heir to Lemonwood), Spotted Sylva (heir to Spottswood), Garin of the orphans, and Ser Gerold Dayne (knight of Starfall). Myrcella asks if Darkstar is the Sword of the Morning; he says no.
9. **Ser Arys reports Tywin Lannister's death** — Privately tells Arianne that Tywin was murdered by the Imp, Tyrion has fled, Cersei has assumed the regency and offers a lordship for Tyrion's head.
10. **Ser Arys and Arianne discuss the decoy arrangements** — Rosamund impersonates Myrcella with redspot ruse to keep Maester Caleotte and Trystane away; Rolder wears Arys's armor as his decoy.
11. **The party departs Shandystone** — Seven riders set out south and west by moonlight: Arianne and Ser Arys in the lead with Myrcella between them, Garin and Spotted Sylva behind, Drey and Darkstar at the rear.
12. **Arianne reflects on her plan** — She intends to take the Greenblood to the Vaith by poleboat, crown Myrcella at the Hellholt, raise her banners, and rally Dorne.
13. **Myrcella and the group rest briefly** — They stop after several hours; Myrcella splits an orange with Sylva, Garin eats olives.
14. **Arianne explains the orphans of the Greenblood to Myrcella** — She tells the story of Nymeria burning her ships and the Rhoynar who clung to the old ways becoming the river orphans.
15. **Myrcella describes how Rosamund serves as her double** — She mentions trading places on the Seaswift with brown dye in her hair, done by Septa Eglantine.
16. **The party presses on through the desert** — Darkstar urges a faster pace to escape the daytime sun. They ride hard, veiling their faces against hot gritty wind; Ser Arys stubbornly refuses a veil.
17. **They reach the Greenblood** — Passing sandbeggars, devilgrass, olive groves, and lemon orchards, they sight the river.
18. **They find the poleboat hidden beneath a willow** — Garin dismounts and calls for the crew to come out with songs and sweetwine.
19. **Areo Hotah springs the ambush** — Hotah emerges from the poleboat door with his longaxe; a dozen guardsmen with spears and crossbows rise behind the rails and atop the cabin.
20. **Hotah demands surrender** — He tells Arianne to yield or all but the child and herself will be slain, by Prince Doran's word.
21. **Drey and Garin yield** — Garin backs away with hands up; Drey drops his swordbelt and urges Arianne to yield.
22. **Ser Arys charges** — Despite pleas to yield, he places his horse between Arianne and the crossbows, then spurs into a headlong charge at the poleboat.
23. **Ser Arys is killed** — Crossbow bolts punch through his shield and graze his temple. A thrown spear takes his horse in the flank. Horse crashes on the deck. Arys fights free, cuts down two spearmen, his horse kicks a crossbowman. Hotah's longaxe takes his right arm off at the shoulder, then removes his head. The head lands in the reeds and the Greenblood swallows it.
24. **Myrcella is injured** — She is on the ground with blood streaming through her fingers over her face. The source/cause of her injury is not specified in Arianne's confused POV.
25. **Arianne collapses and is captured** — She finds herself on hands and knees, sobbing and retching. Her hands are bound, the throwing knife from her boot is confiscated.
26. **Hotah orders pursuit of someone fleeing** — He roars "After him. He must not escape. After him!" The identity of the fleeing person is not explicitly stated in the text.
27. **Hotah tells Arianne he must bring her to Sunspear** — He says her father ordered it and adds "I am sorry, little princess." Arianne asks how Doran could have known; Hotah replies "Someone told. Someone always tells."

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Shifts dramatically through the chapter — confident and purposeful at the start, sexually aware of Darkstar's attractiveness, anxious about Quentyn's possible threat to her birthright, defiant about her claim to Dorne, briefly amused during introductions, then horrified and devastated after Arys's death. Final state is shock, grief, disbelief, and despair.
- **Primary preoccupation:** Her birthright and the queenmaking plot. She fears Quentyn is being positioned by Doran to supplant her. She wants Sunspear, Dorne, and her father's seat. Beneath the stated desire for "justice" lies ambition and resentment.
- **Key decisions made:** Rejects Darkstar's suggestion to kill Myrcella ("I am no murderer of children"); decided to include Darkstar despite others' distrust (they need his sword and castle); chose a small party of seven to reduce risk of betrayal; attempts to flee when ambush springs but is captured.
- **Self-deception flags:** Arianne tells herself the plan is for justice and Dorne's good, but her internal thoughts reveal it is fundamentally about her personal claim to Sunspear; she believes she was "so careful" but was betrayed from within; she underestimates the danger of including Darkstar; she frames Doran as feeble ("It is time he put his burdens down") when he is in fact several moves ahead of her.
