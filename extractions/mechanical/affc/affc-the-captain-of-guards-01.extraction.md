# AFFC — The Captain of Guards I

- **chapter_summary:** Areo Hotah guards Prince Doran at the Water Gardens as Obara Sand arrives demanding vengeance for Oberyn's death. Doran deflects her, then decides to return to Sunspear. After hearing all three, Doran orders Hotah to arrest all the Sand Snakes and confine them in the Spear Tower.

## Events & Actions
1. **Doran observes the blood oranges are overripe** — Sitting on the terrace at the Water Gardens in his rolling chair, he makes the observation then falls silent for hours.
2. **Obara Sand arrives demanding audience** — Hotah recognizes her stride; she approaches with Maester Caleotte trailing. Hotah blocks her with his longaxe.
3. **Obara reports popular unrest over Oberyn's death** — Thousands crossing sands, septs packed, red priests lighting temple fires, women in pillow houses refusing coin, universal demand for vengeance.
4. **Doran permits Obara to pass** — He calls out from behind Hotah to let her through.
5. **Obara proposes military campaign** — She wants command of the Prince's Pass host to march on Oldtown while Nym takes the Boneway host up the kingsroad.
6. **Doran deflects, mentions Lord Tywin's promise of the Mountain's head** — He refuses to commit to war.
7. **Obara tells the story of her father claiming her** — Oberyn threw a spear at her feet and slapped her mother; she chose the spear. Her mother drank herself to death.
8. **Doran dismisses Obara, promising to send word** — He says he will sleep on it.
9. **Doran decides to return to Sunspear** — After Caleotte warns that Obara will inflame the common people, Doran agrees he must go. Sends word to Ricasso to open his apartments in the Tower of the Sun and inform Arianne.
10. **Doran discusses the danger of being seen** — His gout has worsened dramatically in the two years at the Water Gardens; he can no longer walk.
11. **Doran spends the evening alone watching the pools** — Eats little, drinks strongwine, eventually falls asleep in his chair. Hotah rolls him to bed by moonlight.
12. **Hotah sharpens his longaxe and remembers Norvos** — Recalls the bells, wintercake, his mother, being branded by the bearded priests.
13. **Departure preparations at dawn** — Hotah selects twenty spears for escort; dons copper scales and sandsilk cloak; the cedarwood litter readied.
14. **Delayed departure until midday** — Doran bathes, breakfasts, says farewell to favorite children.
15. **Nymeria Sand intercepts the party on the road** — Appears on a dune with a dozen mounted spearmen; falls in beside Doran's litter.
16. **Nym proposes targeted assassination** — Wants Tyene's help to kill Tywin's golden twins (for Elia's children), Tywin (for Elia), and King Tommen (for Oberyn). Mentions Ser Daemon's letter that Oberyn poisoned Clegane.
17. **Doran objects that the boy king has never wronged them** — Nym invokes Stannis's claims about Cersei's children being bastards.
18. **Nym departs at gallop toward Sunspear** — Threatens that she and her sisters will not wait seventeen years for vengeance.
19. **Arrival at Sunspear through hostile crowds** — Smallfolk shout "To spears!" and throw fruit; guards shove people aside; portcullis drops behind them.
20. **Arianne and the court greet Doran** — Princess Arianne, Ricasso, Ser Manfrey, Maester Myles, Dornish knights, Myrcella, her septa, and Ser Arys are in the outer ward.
21. **Hotah carries Doran up to Tower of the Sun** — Prince gasps in pain as he is moved.
22. **Tyene Sand presents her proposal** — Crown Myrcella as queen (she is older than Tommen by Dornish law of succession); wed her to Trystane; wait for the Lannisters to attack.
23. **Doran is noncommittal about Tyene's plan** — Says "let me think on it."
24. **Tyene raises her hand toward Doran** — Hotah thumps his longaxe to intervene; Tyene backs off, kneels, asks for blessing.
25. **Maester Caleotte checks Doran's hand for poison** — Examines and sniffs; finds no scratches. Doran requests milk of the poppy.
26. **Doran questions Hotah about the guards' loyalty** — Asks if all are loyal; says he wants obedience not heads.
27. **Doran orders arrest of the Sand Snakes** — Commands Hotah to find and confine all eight of Oberyn's daughters (including the young ones) in the Spear Tower cells. Exempts Sarella who is away from Dorne. Wants it done quickly, quietly, without blood.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Dutiful, watchful, quietly troubled. Hotah is stoic and observant, not emotionally demonstrative. He feels sadness when he thinks of Ser Arys (knowing they may fight one day), tenderness toward "my little princess" Arianne, and unease about the arrest order. He is nostalgic for Norvos.
- **Primary preoccupation:** His duty to protect Doran; reading threats (Obara's spear, Tyene's needles); maintaining his longaxe; awareness of his own foreignness in Dorne after many years.
- **Key decisions made:** None — Hotah makes no independent decisions. He obeys. His role is "Serve. Obey. Protect."
- **Self-deception flags:** Hotah assesses himself as "only a captain of guards" and "a simple man" following "simple vows" — yet his observations are perceptive and his narrative role is that of a shrewd witness.
