## Chapter Metadata

- **pov_chapter_number:** Dunk I
- **first_available:** pre-agot
- **location_primary:** Coldmoat (and the stream where the trial by battle is fought)
- **location_secondary:** Standfast (mentioned), Goldengrove (mentioned), Summerhall (mentioned), the Wall / Fair Isle / Harrenhal / the Trident (named as destinations), the crossroads with the iron cage
- **approximate_timeline:** The trial by battle at the stream, then Dunk's several-day recovery at Coldmoat, the wedding "this morning," and the departure on a later rainy day
- **time_markers:** "It's too hot to die"; "It had been so long" (since clouds/rain); the wedding "this morning"; the septon visited "near every day"; "the day they took their leave"; drought giving way to steady rain
- **chapter_summary:** Dunk fights Ser Lucas Inchfield in a stream as champion for Ser Eustace Osgrey in a trial by battle, and after both horses go down and Dunk himself briefly drowns, he kills the Longinch with his dagger underwater. He wakes at Coldmoat, revived by the ironborn maester Cerrick, and learns that Ser Eustace and Lady Rohanne reconciled at her weeping over Addam's grave and were wed that morning, making Eustace lord of Coldmoat. As Dunk prepares to leave despite his injuries, Lady Rohanne offers him a captaincy of her guards, a place for Egg, and a fine blood-bay mare, all of which he refuses; he kisses her and cuts off her red braid as a keepsake. Dunk and Egg ride out, and at the crossroads they choose to turn north toward the Wall rather than south to Summerhall.
- **unit_part:** 8 of 8

## Physical Environment
- **Weather:** Hot and dry before the fight; smoke from the burned wood mostly blown away; sky dark with clouds. During recovery and departure, steady heavy rain ("a thousand cold gray whips upon his back"); the moat threatening to overflow its banks.
- **Season indicators:** Drought breaking into rain; summer heat ("It's too hot to die"; "a gown as green as summer")
- **Time of day:** Battle by day under a darkening sky; Dunk wakes to torchlight; departure in daylight rain
- **Lighting:** Torchlight against the maester's tower ceiling; the underwater world "blue and green and brown"; the sky "gray as a granite wall"
- **Sounds:** Ravens quorking and muttering in the rafters; the sound of rain (mistaken by Dunk for something else); the ringing in Dunk's ears; Egg screaming during the fight
- **Smells:** The sharp coppery smell of blood; the cloth over Dunk's eyes fragrant with unguent smelling of cloves
- **Notable sensory details:** The maester's tower "blessedly cool"; the bitter vinegar taste of the potion washing away the taste of blood; water pouring from the breath holes of Dunk's dinted helm

## Events & Actions
1. **Septon Sefton prays and pleads before the trial** — Sefton says a prayer to the Father, Warrior, and Mother, then begs Ser Eustace to withdraw the accusation and Lady Rohanne to confess, and proposes taking the matter to Lord Rowan at Goldengrove instead.
   - Agent: Septon Sefton
   - Patient: Ser Eustace, Lady Rohanne
   - Location: The stream
   - Outcome: Both refuse; the trial proceeds
2. **Ser Lucas asserts his claim to marry Rohanne** — Lucas tells Rohanne, "You will marry me when this mummer's farce is done. As your lord father wished"; she answers that her father never knew him as she does.
   - Agent: Ser Lucas Inchfield
   - Patient: Lady Rohanne
   - Location: The stream
3. **Dunk returns the signet to Egg** — Dunk kneels, puts the signet (Maekar's four three-headed dragons) back in Egg's hand, and instructs him, if Dunk dies, to go to the nearest of his father's friends to be taken back to Summerhall.
   - Agent: Dunk
   - Patient: Egg
   - Instrument: Maekar's signet
   - Location: The stream
4. **The trial by battle** — Dunk and Ser Lucas fight mounted in the stream; Lucas wields a two-handed poleax, Dunk his sword and shield. Dunk's thrust at a gap under Lucas's arm fails; Lucas's ax strikes Dunk's helm and wounds Thunder's neck.
   - Agent: Dunk / Ser Lucas
   - Patient: Ser Lucas / Dunk
   - Instrument: Longsword; poleax
   - Location: The stream
   - Outcome: Both horses go down
5. **Thunder strikes Ser Lucas and the horses fall** — Wounded, Thunder rears and lashes out, one hoof catching Lucas in the face and another on the shoulder, then comes down atop the courser; both horses go down in a tangle.
   - Agent: Thunder
   - Patient: Ser Lucas, the chestnut courser
   - Location: The stream
   - Outcome: Both riders unhorsed into the water
6. **Dunk drowns and rises** — Dunk falls face-first, one foot caught in a stirrup, and the stream floods his helm; his leg nearly pulled from its socket by Thunder's struggles, he is briefly submerged, then gathers his legs and stands, having lost his sword.
   - Agent: Dunk
   - Location: The stream
   - Outcome: Dunk survives but loses his sword and is injured
7. **Dunk kills Ser Lucas** — Lucas bursts up sword in hand and drives Dunk down, splitting his shield; Egg screams for Dunk to fight; Dunk tackles Lucas underwater, holds him down, draws his dagger, and drives it through the mail and leather beneath Lucas's arm, turning it until the strength leaves him.
   - Agent: Dunk
   - Patient: Ser Lucas Inchfield
   - Instrument: Dagger
   - Location: The stream
   - Witness: Egg
   - Outcome: Ser Lucas's death
8. **Egg pulls Dunk from the stream and guards him** — Egg (himself drowned by the water's account, revived) pulls the drowned Dunk from the stream, helps remove his armor, rides with him in the wayn to Coldmoat, and sits by his side with the sword across his lap, making the maester taste Dunk's food.
   - Agent: Egg
   - Patient: Dunk
   - Location: The stream / Coldmoat
   - Outcome: Dunk brought to Coldmoat and guarded
9. **Maester Cerrick revives and treats Dunk** — The ironborn maester, drawing on Drowned God priests' methods, brings the drowned Dunk back and treats his broken ankle, sprained knee, broken collarbone, bruising, and facial gash.
   - Agent: Maester Cerrick
   - Patient: Dunk
   - Location: Coldmoat maester's tower
   - Outcome: Dunk revived and healing
10. **Ser Eustace and Lady Rohanne reconcile and wed** — After the battle, Rohanne asks leave to cross Eustace's land to visit Addam's grave, weeps before the blackberries, and Eustace comforts her; they talk all night of Addam and her father, and are wed that morning by Septon Sefton, making Eustace lord of Coldmoat.
    - Agent: Ser Eustace / Lady Rohanne
    - Location: Addam's grave / Coldmoat
    - Instigator: (marriage officiated by Septon Sefton)
    - Outcome: Marriage; Coldmoat and Standfast reconciled
11. **Rohanne offers Dunk a place and a horse; both refused** — In the stables Rohanne offers Dunk a captaincy of her guards, a place for Egg among her squires, and the blood-bay mare Flame/"Amends"; Dunk declines all, saying the mare is too fine for a bastard.
    - Agent: Lady Rohanne
    - Patient: Dunk
    - Location: Coldmoat stables
    - Outcome: Dunk refuses
12. **Dunk kisses Rohanne and cuts off her braid** — Dunk grabs Rohanne's braid, pulls her to him, kisses her, then draws his dagger and cuts off the braid as the keepsake he chooses instead of the horse.
    - Agent: Dunk
    - Patient: Lady Rohanne
    - Instrument: Dagger
    - Location: Coldmoat stables
    - Outcome: Dunk keeps a lock of her red hair
13. **Departure and the choice of road** — At the gatehouse Egg waits mounted on a new sorrel palfrey leading the mule; the two ride out across the drawbridge, pass the iron cage of corpses at the crossroads, and choose to turn north toward the Wall rather than south to Summerhall.
    - Agent: Dunk, Egg
    - Location: Coldmoat / the crossroads
    - Outcome: They ride north
