- **chapter_summary:** Waiting in Lady Rohanne's audience chamber, Dunk, Egg, and the fat drunken Septon Sefton drink Arbor wine while Sefton discourses on the ruin of King's Landing by fire and the Great Spring Sickness, and on the realm ruled in truth by the Hand, Lord Rivers (Bloodraven). Sefton explains that Lady Rohanne must remarry before the next new moon or forfeit Coldmoat to her father's cousin Wendell Webber under Lord Wyman's will, and that the castellan Ser Lucas the Longinch drives off all suitors. Lady Rohanne enters with Maester Cerrick, settles on the dais, and negotiates: she refuses the offered blood price and demands Bennis be delivered up, then has the maester show Dunk a royal decree proving the Chequy Water is legally hers. She reveals that Ser Eustace was a Blackfyre rebel whose sons died, wife leapt from Standfast tower, and daughter was given as hostage. When Dunk appeals in the name of Eustace's dead son Addam, Rohanne makes him kneel, slaps him bloody, and expels him from Coldmoat, demanding Bennis by the morrow "else fire and sword." Sefton hurries Dunk out and tells him Rohanne had loved Addam, who died at twelve on the Redgrass Field.

## Events & Actions
1. **Septon Sefton serves the wine** — Sefton reappears with a flagon of wine, a flagon of water, and three cups, fills two with wine and one with water, gives the water to Egg, and hands Dunk a cup of Arbor vintage.
   - Agent: Septon Sefton
   - Patient: Dunk, Egg
   - Instrument: Arbor wine, water, cups
   - Location: Coldmoat audience chamber
   - Outcome: Dunk drinks warily; Egg refuses the water
2. **Sefton discourses on the realm** — Sefton recounts the burning of King's Landing, the Great Spring Sickness, the drought, Greyjoy raids, the Bracken-Blackwood feud, King Aerys's weakness, and Lord Rivers's true rule.
   - Agent: Septon Sefton
   - Patient: Dunk (listener), Egg
   - Location: Coldmoat audience chamber
   - Outcome: Dunk uneasy, thinks some of it "sounded treasonous"
3. **Sefton explains Rohanne's marriage predicament** — He lays out Lord Wyman's will, the new-moon deadline, cousin Wendell's claim, the dead husbands, and the Longinch's driving-off of suitors.
   - Agent: Septon Sefton
   - Patient: Dunk
   - Location: Coldmoat audience chamber
   - Outcome: Dunk learns the stakes of Rohanne's position
4. **Lady Rohanne enters and takes the high seat** — She appears in the doorway beside Maester Cerrick, corrects Sefton's wager about Gerold Lannister, trades barbs, and settles on the dais, pulling her braid forward.
   - Agent: Lady Rohanne Webber
   - Location: Coldmoat audience chamber (doorway → dais)
   - Outcome: The negotiation begins
5. **Rohanne refuses the blood price and demands Bennis** — Dunk offers one silver stag for Wolmer's cheek and three for her honor; she rejects it, saying "Only blood can pay for blood," and demands Bennis be delivered for chastisement (a slit nose).
   - Agent: Lady Rohanne Webber
   - Patient: Dunk (as envoy); Bennis (the man demanded)
   - Location: Coldmoat audience chamber
   - Outcome: Impasse; Rohanne threatens "fire and sword" if she must come take him
6. **Maester Cerrick shows Dunk the royal decree** — At Rohanne's command, Cerrick descends the dais and unrolls an old parchment bearing the Targaryen seal, proving the Chequy Water is hers; Dunk, unable to read, has Egg covertly confirm it.
   - Agent: Maester Cerrick
   - Patient: Dunk
   - Instrument: The royal decree (parchment)
   - Instigator: Lady Rohanne Webber ("Maester Cerrick, show him")
   - Location: Coldmoat audience chamber
   - Outcome: Dunk realizes the stream is legally hers ("It is her stream. She has a paper.")
7. **Rohanne and Cerrick reveal Eustace's treason** — They tell Dunk that Ser Eustace chose the black dragon over the red, that his sons died in the rebellion, his wife leapt from Standfast tower, and his daughter was given to the king's men as hostage.
   - Agent: Lady Rohanne Webber / Maester Cerrick
   - Patient: Dunk
   - Location: Coldmoat audience chamber
   - Outcome: Dunk stunned — "You swore your sword to a traitor, lunk."
8. **Dunk appeals in Addam Osgrey's name** — Recalling Eustace's parting words, Dunk asks Rohanne to grant water for the sake of Eustace's son Addam, who served as her father's page and squire.
   - Agent: Dunk
   - Patient: Lady Rohanne Webber
   - Location: Coldmoat audience chamber
   - Outcome: Rohanne's face turns to stone
9. **Rohanne makes Dunk kneel and slaps him** — She bids him come closer and kneel; he obeys; she slaps him with all her strength, splitting his lip and bloodying his mouth.
   - Agent: Lady Rohanne Webber
   - Patient: Dunk
   - Instrument: Her open hand
   - Location: Coldmoat audience chamber (before the dais)
   - Outcome: Dunk's cheek burns, lip broken; he suppresses an urge to strike back
10. **Rohanne expels Dunk** — She orders him from Coldmoat "at once," threatens to sew a sack for him, and demands Bennis be brought by the morrow "else I will come for him myself with fire and sword."
    - Agent: Lady Rohanne Webber
    - Patient: Dunk
    - Location: Coldmoat audience chamber
    - Outcome: Dunk expelled; ultimatum issued
11. **Sefton escorts Dunk out and explains** — Sefton pulls Dunk from the room, calls mentioning Addam "most unwise," and reveals that Rohanne loved Addam (who died at twelve on the Redgrass Field) and blames Eustace for his death.
    - Agent: Septon Sefton
    - Patient: Dunk
    - Location: Audience chamber → the steps
    - Outcome: Dunk asks Sefton to beg Rohanne's pardon; Sefton warns him to deliver Bennis quickly

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Uneasy and out of his depth; wary of the treasonous talk; ashamed at his illiteracy ("Dunk the lunk"); flushed and embarrassed; gut-punched to learn the stream is legally hers and that Eustace is a traitor; a flash of anger at the slap, quickly suppressed by fear of Rohanne's knights.
- **Primary preoccupation:** Discharging Ser Eustace's errand — paying the blood price and pleading the water dispute — without disgracing himself; not being fooled by the wine or by Rohanne's charm.
- **Key decisions made:** Sips the wine only after Sefton drinks; refuses Rohanne's offer to take service at Coldmoat; conceals that he cannot read by having Egg check the decree; appeals in Addam's name; obeys the order to kneel; leaves when expelled and asks Sefton to beg her pardon.
- **Self-deception flags:** Tells himself chicken-chasing "helps make him quicker" while knowing Rohanne would laugh; likes "the ring of twenty" though he is likely younger; is distracted by Rohanne's "snub nose and her freckles" while trying to stay on task; "I was getting to the moat" — a defensive self-justification after Egg interrupts.
