## Node Prose
### Aegon I Targaryen
Aegon took both of his sisters, Rhaenys and Visenya, to wife, and each queen bore him a son — quote: "took both of his sisters to wife". He accepted the continued independence of Dorne after his Dornish War and flew to Sunspear on Balerion for a "feast of friendship" with Deria Martell — quote: "flew to Sunspear on Balerion". In 35 AC he ordered the Aegonfort torn down to raise a stone castle, naming Lord Alyn Stokeworth and Queen Visenya to oversee it — quote: "he named the King’s Hand, Lord Alyn Stokeworth". He died of a stroke on Dragonstone in 37 AC, his grandsons Aegon and Viserys at his side — quote: "died of a stroke on Dragonstone in the 37th year After the Conquest".

### Rhaenys Targaryen
Aegon's younger sister-wife; her son Aenys was born in 7 AC — quote: "Born in 7 AC to Aegon’s younger wife, Rhaenys". She and her dragon Meraxes were slain in Dorne when Aenys was three — quote: "her dragon, Meraxes, were slain in Dorne".

### Visenya Targaryen
Aegon's elder sister-wife; she announced her pregnancy in 11 AC and bore Maegor in 12 AC — quote: "Queen Visenya suddenly announced that she was carrying the king's child". She put a sword in Maegor's hand at three and gave him Dark Sister on his thirteenth nameday — quote: "Queen Visenya put a sword into her son's hand when he was three". As Dowager Queen she whispered that Aenys was unfit and Maegor the stronger — quote: "only Maegor has the strength to rule". She presided over Maegor's second wedding on Dragonstone — quote: "under the aegis of the Dowager Queen Visenya".

### Aenys Targaryen (Aenys I)
Small and sickly at birth, Aenys began to thrive only when given the dragon Quicksilver — quote: "given the young dragon Quicksilver". He was heir apparent and a mediocre fighter but a fine singer, courteous and charming — quote: "no songs would ever be sung about his". In 22 AC he wed Lady Alyssa Velaryon, a happy and fruitful match — quote: "In 22 AC, he wed the Lady Alyssa". Crowned Aenys I in 37 AC, he gave Blackfyre to Maegor and vowed they would rule together — quote: "We shall rule this realm together, you and I". Faced with rebellions he prevaricated, and gave Maegor a choice of putting Alys aside or exile — quote: "or suffer five years of exile".

### Maegor Targaryen
Robust at birth, Maegor remained on Dragonstone with his mother and was called the Prince of Dragonstone — quote: "refer to Maegor as the Prince of Dragonstone". A quarrelsome boy of unmatched skill at arms, he was knighted at sixteen at the Riverrun tourney — quote: "the youngest knight in the Seven Kingdoms". He claimed Balerion at last above the Eyrie and executed Jonos Arryn's followers to a man — quote: "Prince Maegor executed them to a man". Named Hand after the rebellions, he later took a second wife, Alys Harroway, and chose exile to Pentos over abandoning her — quote: "In 40 AC he departed for Pentos".

### Alyssa Velaryon
Daughter of Aethan Velaryon, wed to Aenys in 22 AC at fifteen; the Velaryons were of Valyrian stock, making it a cousin match — quote: "Aethan Velaryon, King Aegon’s lord admiral and master of ships". She bore Aenys many children — Rhaena, Aegon, Viserys, Jaehaerys, Alysanne, and Vaella — quote: "Queen Alyssa gave King Aenys yet another heir, a girl she named Vaella". She once teased Maegor as afraid of dragons — quote: "my goodbrother is afraid of dragons".

### Rhaena Targaryen
Eldest daughter of Aenys and Alyssa, named in honor of Rhaenys — quote: "Prince Aenys named her Rhaena, in honor of his mother". A shy, dreamy child who loved animals, she bonded with the dragon Dreamfyre at nine and flew at twelve — quote: "the young dragon she named Dreamfyre bonded instantly". Legend says she placed dragon eggs in the cradles of Jaehaerys and Alysanne — quote: "put a dragon's egg in Princess Alysanne’s cradle". On the royal progress she reverted to shyness, loath to be parted from her favorite Melony Piper — quote: "her latest favorite, Melony Piper".

### Ceryse Hightower
Niece of the High Septon and daughter of Manfred Hightower, proposed as Maegor's bride in place of Rhaena — quote: "his own niece, Ceryse Hightower". She wed Maegor in the Starry Sept in 25 AC, ten years his senior, but never quickened — quote: "the bride ten years his senior". When Maegor declared her barren and took a second wife, she was left abandoned in King's Landing — quote: "Lady Ceryse\nwas left abandoned in King’s Landing".

### Alys Harroway
Daughter of the new Lord of Harrenhal, taken by Maegor as a second wife in 39 AC — quote: "he had therefore taken a second wife in Alys Harroway". Wed in a Valyrian rite on Dragonstone, she was denounced by the High Septon and called "Maegor's Whore" — quote: "calling the prince's new bride “this whore of Harroway”". She accompanied Maegor into exile at Pentos — quote: "taking Lady Alys, Balerion his dragon, and the sword Blackfyre".

### Aegon Targaryen (son of Aenys)
Eldest son of Aenys and Alyssa, born 26 AC and named after his grandsire, who declared he had a warrior's look — quote: "born to Lady Alyssa and fathered by Prince Aenys". He was with Aegon I at his death in the Chamber of the Painted Table — quote: "His grandsons Aegon and Viserys were with him at his death".

### Orys Baratheon
Known now as Orys One-Hand, he rode from Storm's End to smash the Dornish beneath Stonehelm — quote: "Orys Baratheon, known now as Orys One-Hand". He mutilated the captured Walter Wyl in payment for his own lost hand, calling the deed usury — quote: "Your father took my hand. | claim yours as repayment". He died of his battle wounds on the march back to Storm's End — quote: "Lord Baratheon died on the march back to Storm's End".

### Vulture King
A Dornish pretender who raised a rabble in the Red Mountains and swelled it to more than thirty thousand — quote: "more than thirty thousand men". He sliced off Harmon Dondarrion's nose and burned Blackhaven — quote: "sliced off Dondarrion’s nose". Defeated in the Vulture Hunt, he was taken alive by Savage Sam Tarly and perished of thirst and exposure — quote: "in truth he perished of thirst and exposure".

### Harren the Red
An outlaw who styled himself a grandson of Harren the Black and seized Harrenhal through an opened postern gate — quote: "claimed to be a grandson of Harren the Black". He mutilated Gargon Qoherys and declared himself Lord of Harrenhal and King of the Rivers — quote: "declared himself Lord\nof Harrenhal and King of the Rivers". Brought to bay west of the Gods Eye, he slew Alyn Stokeworth before being cut down — quote: "he slew the King’s Hand, Lord Alyn Stokeworth".

### Jonos Arryn
Ronnel Arryn's younger brother, who deposed and imprisoned him and declared himself King of Mountain and Vale — quote: "deposed and imprisoned his loyal sibling". He had Ronnel cast through the Moon Door, then was served the same way himself when the garrison surrendered to Maegor — quote: "Jonos Arryn sent his brother to them through the Moon Door".

### Goren Greyjoy
Lord Reaper of Pyke, who put down the priest king Lodos and pickled his head in brine for the king — quote: "the head of the priest king pickled in brine". As his boon he asked leave to expel the septons and septas from the Iron Islands — quote: "expel all the septons and septas".

### Samwell Tarly
Lord of Horn Hill, called Savage Sam, who fell upon the Dornish line with knights and archers — quote: "Lord Samwell Tarly of Horn Hill". He wielded the Valyrian blade Heartsbane and took the Vulture King alive — quote: "his great Valyrian steel blade Heartsbane".

### Balerion
The Black Dread, greatest of the dragons; Maegor claimed him at last above the Eyrie — quote: "claimed a dragon at last: none other than the Black Dread".

### Blackfyre
The ancestral Valyrian steel sword; Aegon pressed it on Aenys, knighted Maegor with it, and Aenys later gave it to Maegor — quote: "presented his father's sword, Blackfyre, to his brother". Maegor carried it into exile — quote: "the sword Blackfyre with him".

### Dark Sister
Visenya's own Valyrian steel blade, bestowed on Maegor at thirteen — quote: "Dark Sister, upon him".

### Aethan Velaryon
Lord of the Tides, Aegon's lord admiral and master of ships; father of Alyssa — quote: "King Aegon’s lord admiral and master of ships". He went to the Stepstones with Maegor and Osmund Strong — quote: "accompanied Osmund Strong and Aethan Velaryon to the Stepstones".

### Gargon Qoherys
Grandson of Quenton Qoherys and Lord of Harrenhal, called Gargon the Guest for claiming first night at weddings — quote: "who became known as Gargon the Guest". Harren the Red mutilated him — quote: "Harren sliced off his genitals and fed them to a dog".

### Ronnel Arryn
Lord of the Vale, once a rider who flew thrice about the Giant's Lance, deposed by his brother and cast through the Moon Door — quote: "who had flown thrice about the Giant's Lance".

### Septon Murmison
A pious cleric said to heal by the laying on of hands, made Hand after Maegor's exile — quote: "heal the sick by the laying on of hands".

### Deria Martell
Reigning Princess of Dorne; she denounced the Vulture King but was rumored to aid him — quote: "rumored to be sending them men, money, and supplies".
