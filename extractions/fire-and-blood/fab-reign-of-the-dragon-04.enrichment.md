## Node Prose
### Aegon I Targaryen
Aegon's reign ran 1 AC–37 AC and was largely peaceful, save for the Dragon's wars that preceded the Dragon's Peace — quote: "the long reign of King Aegon | Targaryen (1 AC-37 AC)". He was crowned and anointed by the High Septon in the Starry Sept of Oldtown — quote: "when Aegon was crowned and anointed by the High". He put down the ironborn chaos by descending on the islands in 2 AC riding Balerion — quote: "He descended on the islands in 2 AC, riding Balerion" — and slew the ironborn claimant himself — quote: "King Aegon slew Qhorin Volmark with Blackfyre". He led the invasion of Dorne through the Prince's Pass with an army thirty thousand strong — quote: "at the head of an army thirty thousand strong" — but never brought the Dornish to battle. After his sister Rhaenys died at the Hellholt he prosecuted the Dragon's Wroth, then read a sealed letter from Prince Nymor in open court, his hand dripping blood, burned it, and signed peace — quote: "he signed a treaty of eternal peace with Dorne". In peace he made reconciliation of the Seven Kingdoms his keystone policy — quote: "The reconciliation of the Seven Kingdoms to Targaryen rule was the keystone" — kept an endless royal progress — quote: "he devoted to an endless royal progress" — decreed the King's Peace — quote: "“The first law of the land shall be the King’s Peace,”" — and exempted the Faith from taxation — quote: "were to be exempt from taxation".

### Visenya Targaryen
Visenya was the eldest of the three heads of the dragon and outlived both siblings — quote: "The oldest of the three heads of the dragon, Visenya was to outlive both of her siblings". She rode Vhagar and wielded Dark Sister, appearing above Sisterton to break the Sistermen — quote: "the sudden appearance of Queen Visenya and Vhagar in the skies above Sisterton". She twice defended Aegon from Dornish cutthroats — quote: "Visenya twice wielded Dark Sister in Aegon's defense" — trusted no one but her brother — quote: "she trusted no one but her brother" — and once slashed him across the cheek to prove his guards too slow — quote: "drew Dark Sister and slashed him across the cheek". She founded the Kingsguard and chose its knights herself — quote: "She would choose the knights herself". According to rumor, in her later years she delved into the dark arts, mixing poisons and casting malign spells — quote: "she delved into the dark arts, mixing poisons and casting malign spells" — and some even suggest she was a kinslayer and kingslayer, though no proof was ever offered — quote: "she might have been a kinslayer and a kingslayer".

### Rhaenys Targaryen
Rhaenys rode Meraxes and opened the First Dornish War in 4 AC, burning the Planky Town — quote: "set the Planky Town ablaze". While Aegon marched, she took Lemonwood, Spottswood, and Stinkwater but found no enemy, reporting that "The Yellow / Toad has melted into the sands" — quote: "has melted into the sands". She was a patron of bards and singers — quote: "Queen Rhaenys was a great patron to the bards and singers" — took a great interest in the smallfolk and women and children — quote: "Queen Rhaenys also took a great interest in the smallfolk" — and her ruling in a wife-beating case gave the realm the "rule of six" — quote: "the “rule of six” became a part of the common law". She died at the Hellholt in 10 AC when Meraxes was killed; how she died is disputed. Some say she lost her seat and fell — quote: "Some say that she lost her seat and fell to her death" — others that she was crushed beneath Meraxes — quote: "others that she was crushed beneath Meraxes in the castle yard" — and a few accounts claim she died by torment in the dungeons of the Ullers — quote: "die a slow death by torment in the dungeons of the Ullers".

### Orys Baratheon
Orys was Aegon's bastard half-brother and first Hand — quote: "His first had been Lord Orys Baratheon, his bastard halfbrother". He led one thousand picked knights up the Boneway — quote: "Orys Baratheon led one thousand picked knights up the Boneway" — where his men were butchered and he was taken captive by Wyl of Wyl — quote: "captives of Wyl of Wyl, the savage". Ransomed in 7 AC, he returned to find his sword hand lopped off — quote: "the Widow-lover had lopped off each man’s sword hand" — and afterward asked to be relieved as Hand — quote: "“The King’s Hand should have a hand,”". In his bitter later years he argued that Princess Deria be sent home "less a hand" — quote: "argued for sending Princess Deria back to her father less a hand".

### Meria Martell
Meria was Princess of Dorne, the so-called Yellow Toad — quote: "Princess Meria Martell, the socalled Yellow Toad of Dorne". Her forces poisoned wells, burned crops, and never offered battle, and she personally threw the royal castellan from the Spear Tower — quote: "thrown from a window by none other than". When Dornish raiders burned the rainwood, she is reported to have said "Fire for fire" — quote: "Princess Meria is reported to have said". She died abed in 13 AC of age and ill health, having defeated Aegon and his dragons — quote: "she had defeated Aegon Targaryen and his dragons". Her enemies insisted she died in coitus with a stallion — quote: "whilst having intimate relations with a stallion, her enemies insisted".

### Nymor Martell
Nymor was Meria's son, sixty years old and failing, who succeeded her as Lord of Sunspear and Prince of Dorne — quote: "Her son Nymor succeeded her as Lord of Sunspear and Prince of Dorne". Wanting no more slaughter, he sent an embassy led by his daughter Deria to return the skull of Meraxes and offer peace — quote: "return the skull of the dragon Meraxes and offer King Aegon terms of peace". Whatever his sealed letter said, Aegon agreed to his terms after reading it.

### Wyl of Wyl
Wyl of Wyl was a savage mountain lord called the Widow-lover — quote: "the savage" / "mountain lord called Widow-lover". He captured Orys Baratheon on the Boneway and later maimed his ransomed captives' sword hands. In 12 AC he came uninvited to a wedding, slew Lord Oakheart and the guests, gelded the groom, and had the bride raped and sold — quote: "arrived uninvited at the wedding". He survived Aegon's dragonfire in caves and lived another twenty years — quote: "the Widow-lover lived another twenty years".

### Harlan Tyrell
Harlan Tyrell, Warden of the South, boasted his host could smash any Dornish army — quote: "Lord Harlan Tyrell, the Warden of the South". Sent against Uthor Uller, he lost a quarter of his men to poisoned wells and the sun before reaching an abandoned Hellholt — quote: "Lord Tyrell lost a quarter of his men and almost all his". Setting off across the red sands, he and his entire army vanished — quote: "Tyrell and his entire army disappeared. No man of them was ever seen again".

### Qhorin Volmark
Qhorin Volmark of Harlaw, whose grandmother was a younger sister of Harren's grandsire, declared himself rightful heir of the black line — quote: "declared himself the rightful heir". He assumed the ironborn kingship but was slain by Aegon with Blackfyre in 2 AC; his infant son was allowed to inherit his lands — quote: "allowed his infant son to inherit his father’s lands and castle".

### Lodos
Lodos was a barefoot holy man crowned on Old Wyk who proclaimed himself the living son of the Drowned God — quote: "who proclaimed himself the living son of the Drowned God" — and was said to be able to work miracles — quote: "was said to be able to work miracles". When the krakens did not rise, he filled his robes with stones and walked into the sea; thousands followed — quote: "Lodos filled his robes with stones and walked into the sea".

### Vickon Greyjoy
Vickon Greyjoy, Lord Reaper of Pyke, was chosen by the ironborn as their own lord paramount — quote: "they chose one of their own: Vickon Greyjoy". He did homage to King Aegon and renounced the mainland lands House Hoare had seized — quote: "Lord Vickon did homage to King Aegon".

### Marla Sunderland
The Three Sisters crowned Lady Marla of House Sunderland their queen during the Conquest's chaos — quote: "crown Lady Marla of House Sunderland their queen". Her people deposed her in favor of her younger brother when Visenya appeared — quote: "they promptly deposed Queen Marla in favor of her younger brother". Exiled and imprisoned, after five years her tongue was removed and she lived out her days with the silent sisters — quote: "her tongue was removed, and she spent the remainder of her life with the silent".

### Torrhen Stark
Torrhen Stark of Winterfell, Warden of the North, was commanded to end the Sistermen's Rebellion — quote: "commanded his Warden of the North, Torrhen Stark of Winterfell". He dispatched a northern army under Ser Warrick Manderly and later sent longships from Bear Island to the Iron Islands campaign.

### Osmund Strong
Ser Osmund Strong was Aegon's fourth Hand, named after Alton Celtigar's death in 17 AC — quote: "Ser Osmund was the king's fourth Hand". He and Grand Maester Gawen were charged with building King's Landing's walls — quote: "Gawen and Ser Osmund Strong, the Hand of the King".

### Edmyn Tully
Edmyn Tully, Lord of Riverrun, was Aegon's second Hand, serving 7–9 AC — quote: "Lord Edmyn served from 7-9 AC". When his wife died in childbed he begged leave to return to the riverlands — quote: "when his wife died in childbed". Quenton Qoherys of Harrenhal was made his liege man.

### Aenys Targaryen
Aenys was Aegon and Rhaenys's son and heir, six years old at the time of the Dornish peace — quote: "Queen Rhaenys’s boy, Aenys, then but six years old". Aegon told him a lord should know the land he rules — quote: "A lord should know the land he rules, the Conqueror later told his son Aenys".

### Kingsguard
Visenya conceived seven champions for the Lord of the Seven Kingdoms, and thus the Kingsguard came into being — quote: "Thus did the Kingsguard come into being". She modeled their lifelong vows on the Night's Watch — quote: "Visenya modeled their vows on those of the Night's Watch". The original seven, from the White Book, were Ser Richard Roote, Ser Addison Hill the Bastard of Cornfield, Ser Gregor Goode, Ser Griffith Goode, Ser Humfrey the Mummer, Ser Robin Darklyn (Darkrobin), and Ser Corlys Velaryon as Lord Commander — quote: "Ser Corlys Velaryon, Lord Commander". Two of them would die protecting the king — quote: "Two of her original seven would die protecting the king".

### Lord Monkeyface
Queen Visenya kept a hirsute hunchback fool called Lord Monkeyface — quote: "a hirsute hunchback called Lord Monkeyface". When he choked to death on a peach pit she dressed an ape in his clothing — quote: "When he choked to death on a peach pit".

### Ollidar
Archmaester Ollidar, keeper of histories, was the first Grand Maester, his ring and rod and mask of bronze — quote: "The first man to serve in that capacity was Archmaester Ollidar, keeper of histories". Exceptionally old, he died less than a year into the office — quote: "he passed from this world less than a year".

### Lyonce
Archmaester Lyonce, chosen after Ollidar, had a ring, rod, and mask of yellow gold — quote: "the Conclave selected Archmaester Lyonce, whose ring and rod and mask were yellow gold". He served until 12 AC, when he slipped in the mud, broke his hip, and died — quote: "serving the realm until 12 AC, when he slipped in the mud".

### Joffrey Dayne
Ser Joffrey Dayne led a Dornish army from Starfall against Oldtown — quote: "A second Dornish army under Ser Joffrey Dayne came down from Starfall" — and slew Lord Hightower's younger son Garmon — quote: "slew Lord Hightower's younger son, Garmon".

### Addam Hightower
Ser Addam was sent by his father to retake Nightsong but found it torched — quote: "Lord Hightower sent his son Addam with a strong force to retake Nightsong". On old Lord Manfred's death he succeeded as Lord of the High Tower — quote: "Ser Addam succeeded his father as the Lord of the High Tower".

### Alton Celtigar
Alton Celtigar, Lord of Claw Isle, replaced Edmyn Tully as Hand and served until his death from natural causes in 17 AC — quote: "Alton Celtigar, Lord of Claw Isle, replaced Tully".

### Jon Rosby
Lord Jon Rosby was named Castellan of Sunspear and Warden of the Sands to rule Dorne in the king's name — quote: "Lord Jon Rosby was named Castellan of Sunspear and Warden of the Sands". He was thrown to his death from the Spear Tower by Princess Meria.

### Quenton Qoherys
Ser Quenton Qoherys, Aegon's master-at-arms on Dragonstone, was granted Harrenhal — quote: "granted the ruined castle of Harrenhal and its domains to Ser Quenton Qoherys" — under Lord Edmyn Tully as his liege — quote: "required him to accept Lord Edmyn Tully of Riverrun as his liege lord". A widower, he agreed to wed one of Tully's daughters — quote: "Lord Tully's daughters as his bride".

### Theo Tyrell
Theo Tyrell, the young Lord of Highgarden and Warden of the South, was reluctant to invade Dorne again after his father's fate — quote: "Theo Tyrell, the young lord, was most reluctant to contemplate another invasion".

### Jon Cafferen
Ser Jon Cafferen, heir to Fawnton, was wed to Alys Oakheart when the Widow-lover's men gelded him at his own wedding — quote: "then made the bride look on as they gelded her husband".

### Alys Oakheart
Alys Oakheart, daughter of the Lord of Old Oak, was forced to watch her husband's gelding, then raped and sold to a Myrish slaver — quote: "then carried them off and sold them to a Myrish slaver".
