## Node Prose

### Jaehaerys I Targaryen
Jaehaerys ascended the Iron Throne in 48 AC at the age of fourteen and ruled fifty-five years until his natural death in 103 AC — quote: "ascended the Iron Throne in 48 AC at the age of fourteen". Scholars later called him "the Conciliator" and, in old age, "the Old King" — quote: "reverently as “the Conciliator.”" As the only surviving son of Aenys I, he inherited an impoverished, war-torn realm and, though still shy of his majority, insisted on a voice in all decisions made in his name — quote: "the only surviving son of King Aenys I". From the Iron Throne he forbade trials, torture, and executions of Maegor's men — quote: "There will be no trials, no torture, and no executions". He refused to reinstate the Swords and Stars but rescinded Maegor's bounties on their heads — quote: "The Faith has no need of swords".

### Aenys I
Father of Jaehaerys, Rhaena, Aegon the Uncrowned, and Viserys; his crown was later placed on Jaehaerys's head at the coronation — quote: "placed his father Aenys's crown upon his head".

### Aegon the Uncrowned
Jaehaerys's older brother, who claimed the kingship before him and died at the Battle Beneath the Gods Eye trying to unseat Maegor — quote: "his older brother Aegon had claimed the kingship before him". Before dying he wed his sister Rhaena and sired the twins Aerea and Rhaella — quote: "taking to wife his sister Rhaena and siring two daughters".

### Maegor I Targaryen
Jaehaerys's uncle, "Maegor the Cruel," whose remains were consigned to a funeral pyre after he was found dead upon the Iron Throne — quote: "By the time Maegor was found dead upon the Iron Throne". His efforts to exterminate the Poor Fellows and Warrior's Sons had set pious men against House Targaryen — quote: "King Maegor's efforts to exterminate the Poor Fellows and the Warrior's Sons".

### Rhaena Targaryen
Firstborn child of Aenys I and Alyssa, twice-widowed, and made one of Maegor's Black Brides — quote: "made her one of his Black Brides". Some suggested she held the strongest claim, and some whispered she had contrived Maegor's death after fleeing on her dragon Dreamfyre — quote: "Queen Rhaena who had somehow contrived to free the realm from Maegor". Lord Rogar dismissed her claim, and she wished only to return to Fair Isle — quote: "and Rhaena is not Nymeria." She conceded the crown to her brother rather than her daughters — quote: "the crown should go to her brother".

### Aerea Targaryen
Twin daughter of Aegon the Uncrowned and Rhaena; if Maegor were a mere usurper, she was the true king's elder daughter — quote: "the succession by rights should pass to his elder daughter, Aerea". Described as a timid child given to tears; after the coronation she went to court to serve Alysanne and became the heir to the Iron Throne — quote: "Princess Aerea... was the heir to the Iron Throne".

### Rhaella Targaryen
Twin sister of Aerea, the bolder of the pair, a novice at the Starry Sept promised to the Faith — quote: "a novice serving at the Starry Sept and promised to the Faith". It was widely believed the twins were switched during the coronation, though nothing was ever proved — quote: "used the occasion of the king's coronation to switch the twins".

### Alyssa Velaryon
Dowager Queen and mother of Jaehaerys, appointed regent for him — quote: "the Dowager Queen Alyssa, would act as regent for him". Having lost two sons to Maegor's cruelty, she urged the loyalists be put to death without trial, but yielded to her son's wishes — quote: "The Dowager Queen had lost two sons to Maegor’s cruelty". She received the heads and hands of Viserys's tormentors and pronounced herself "well pleased" — quote: "Her Grace pronounced herself “well pleased” with the tokens".

### Alysanne Targaryen
Princess and sister of Jaehaerys who stood by him at his side; at her urging the condemned Kingsguard were offered the black instead of death — quote: "at the urging of Princess Alysanne".

### Viserys Targaryen
Son of Aenys I, briefly Maegor's heir and hostage, tortured and slain — quote: "the torture and death of Prince Viserys, who had so briefly been Maegor’s heir".

### Rogar Baratheon
Lord Rogar served as Jaehaerys's Hand and Protector of the Realm — quote: "Lord Rogar served as his Hand and the Protector of the Realm". Against Alyssa's fury he urged trials over blanket execution, warning of the cost of storming the loyalists' castles — quote: "It can be done, but at what cost?". He offered to lead a host across the Reach against Moon's protectors — quote: "he would lead his own host across the Reach".

### Gyles Morrigen
Ser Gyles, a stormlands knight and nephew to Damon the Devout, was chosen as the Crown's champion — quote: "Ser Gyles Morrigen, the man chosen, was a nephew to Damon the Devout". He slew the elderly Ser Harrold and was named Lord Commander of the Kingsguard — quote: "made short work of the elderly Ser Harrold".

### Harrold Langward
The fifth surviving Kingsguard, who demanded and was granted a trial by battle, and died against Gyles Morrigen — quote: "Ser Harrold Langward, demanded a trial by battle".

### Maladon Moore
A Kingsguard knight beheaded for his part in the death of Ceryse Hightower, though he insisted the tale was a fabrication and that she died of "shrewishness" — quote: "insisted the whole tale was a fabrication". He admitted delivering Tyanna of the Tower to Maegor and witnessing her slaying — quote: "delivering Tyanna of the Tower to King Maegor's hands".

### Ceryse Hightower
Maegor's first queen, said to have died when Owen Bush removed her tongue and the blade slipped — quote: "bringing about her death".

### Tyanna of the Tower
Named as abetting the torture and death of Prince Viserys; delivered to Maegor and slain by him — quote: "standing witness as he slew her".

### Benifer
Grand Maester, newly returned from self-imposed exile in Pentos, who praised Jaehaerys as learned and pious — quote: "learned as a maester and pious as a septon". He wrote that the meeting between king and outlaw knight "set the table" for the reign — quote: "set the table" for all of Jaehaerys's reign".

### Joffrey Doggett
"The Red Dog of the Hills," self-proclaimed Grand Captain of the Warrior's Sons, who moved between the westerlands and riverlands with Lady Lucinda's support — quote: "the Red Dog of the Hills, Ser Joffrey Doggett". He came to the coronation under the king's safe conduct and was offered a place in the Kingsguard, which he accepted with tears — quote: "laid his blade at Jaehaerys's feet".

### Septon Moon
A massive man of thunderous voice, "the Poorest Fellow," whose Poor Fellows proclaimed him "the true High Septon" — quote: "his Poor Fellows had proclaimed him “the true High Septon,”". A glutton, drunkard, and lecher who began every sermon confessing sin — quote: "“| am a sinner,” were the words with which Septon Moon began every sermon". He preached daily against House Targaryen beneath the walls of Oldtown — quote: "denounce the sins of House Targaryen". A woman with a flagon of wine slit his throat in his tent — quote: "where his throat had been slit open".

### Donnel Hightower
Lord of Oldtown, who closed his gates against Moon but was slow to give battle, earning the name "Lord Donnel the Delayer" — quote: "earned him the name Lord Donnel the Delayer from the maesters of the Citadel". His knights later seized and beheaded Moon's corpse — quote: "seized Septon Moon's body, and removed his head".

### Lorcas the Learned
A would-be successor to Moon who claimed to know all of The Seven-Pointed Star and had a vision Moon would yet take Oldtown; he strapped the rotting corpse to a destrier and died attacking the walls — quote: "to storm the gates of Oldtown".

### Lord Oakheart
Lord who granted Septon Moon his protection at Oldtown and decamped the day after Moon's death — quote: "defended by Lord Oakheart and Lord Rowan and their knights".

### Lord Rowan
Lord who granted Septon Moon his protection and decamped within the hour of Moon's death; scholars suspect the killer acted at his behest — quote: "acting at the behest of either Lord Rowan or Lord Oakheart".

### Lucinda Tully
Pious wife of the Lord of Riverrun who lent Joffrey Doggett her support and connivance — quote: "the support and connivance of Lady Lucinda".

### Damon the Devout
Grand Captain of the Warrior's Sons who led them in their Trial of Seven against Maegor; uncle of Gyles Morrigen — quote: "who had led them in their Trial of Seven against Maegor".

### Rob the Starvling
A Poor Fellow and would-be successor to Moon, from whom Lorcas seized the septon's body — quote: "the Poor Fellow known as Rob the Starvling".

### Lord Rosby
A Maegor loyalist among the last to see the king alive, who drank hemlock to join his king in death — quote: "Lord Rosby drank a cup of hemlock to join his king in death".

### Owen Bush
Ser; Sworn Brother of Maladon Moore, said to have removed Ceryse Hightower's tongue — quote: "Ser Owen Bush, removed her tongue".
