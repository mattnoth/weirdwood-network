## Node Prose
### Maegor I Targaryen
King on the Iron Throne; the High Septon dissolved the Faith Militant in his name and Maegor set bounties on unrepentant members — a gold dragon per Warrior's Son, a silver stag per Poor Fellow. — quote: "a gold dragon for the head of any unrepentant Warrior's Son"
He was reconciled with his first wife Ceryse at Oldtown, sealing it with a feast and a "second consummation." — quote: "a bedding and a “second consummation,”"
Riding Balerion, he slew his nephew Aegon at the battle beneath the Gods Eye, then followed with half a year of trials and executions. — quote: "Half a year of trials and executions followed"
He destroyed House Harroway root and branch, questioned his nephew Viserys to death, massacred the Red Keep's builders, and began the Dragonpit. — quote: "put all the workmen to the sword"
He took three widows to wife in 47 AC, the "Black Brides." — quote: "took three wives in the ward of the Red Keep"
He was called "Maegor the Cruel" and "kinslayer," and died childless upon the Iron Throne after a reign of six years and sixty-six days. — quote: "lasted six years and sixty-six days"
He died childless. — quote: "He died childless, and left no heir of his body"

### Aegon Targaryen (the Uncrowned)
Emerging from Casterly Rock, he and Rhaena slipped into King's Landing hidden under corn sacks to claim Quicksilver and Dreamfyre. — quote: "entered the city concealed beneath sacks of corn"
At Pinkmaiden he denounced Maegor and raised fifteen thousand men. — quote: "Fifteen thousand men had joined the rebellion"
Still but seventeen, he charged at the Gods Eye and was slain when Balerion tore Quicksilver from the sky. — quote: "the young dragon plunged to earth, and Prince Aegon with her"

### Rhaena Targaryen
Sister-wife of Aegon; she remained at Pinkmaiden with her daughters and Dreamfyre while Aegon marched, a choice still debated. — quote: "Whether that was by his command or her own choice is still debated"
On learning of Aegon's and Melony's deaths she refused to weep. — quote: "I do not have the time for tears"
She fled to Fair Isle, hid her daughters by dyeing their hair and changing their names, then was summoned to wed Maegor. — quote: "she dyed their hair, changed their names"
When Jaehaerys rose, she flew Dreamfyre to join him, taking Aerea and the stolen Blackfyre. — quote: "mounted Dreamfyre and flew to join him"

### Tyanna of the Tower
Pentoshi mistress of whisperers; she wrote to warn Maegor of secret talks with Alyssa, and revealed that Alys's child was not his seed. — quote: "came to tell him he had been deceived"
She was given Queen Alys to torment, and later confessed under threat to poisoning the queens' children in the womb. — quote: "she had poisoned Jeyne Westerling’s child in the womb"
According to report, Maegor slew her himself with Blackfyre and fed her heart to his dogs. — quote: "cutting out her heart with Blackfyre and feeding it to his dogs"

### Alyssa Velaryon
Widow of Aenys; she remained on Dragonstone with Jaehaerys and Alysanne as prisoners in all but name. — quote: "prisoners in all but name"
After Visenya's death she escaped Dragonstone with her children, the dragons Vermithor and Silverwing, and stolen Dark Sister. — quote: "made their escape from Dragonstone, with the dragons Vermithor and Silverwing"

### Jaehaerys Targaryen
Son of Aenys and Alyssa and the rightful heir by law, he was expressly disinherited by Maegor. — quote: "the rightful heir by all the laws of the Seven Kingdoms"
At fourteen he was proclaimed king at Storm's End, unsheathed Dark Sister, and rode the dragon Vermithor. — quote: "fourteen years old when he claimed the throne"

### Alysanne Targaryen
Daughter of Aenys and Alyssa; a maid of twelve who commanded her own dragon, Silverwing. — quote: "his sister Alysanne, a maid of twelve, commanded her own dragon, Silverwing"

### Viserys Targaryen
Eldest surviving son of Aenys and Alyssa; summoned to court and made Maegor's squire under a Kingsguard shadow. — quote: "Viserys was made squire to the king"
Seized and questioned about his mother's flight, he died after nine days; he was fifteen. — quote: "he was fifteen years old when he was killed"

### Alys Harroway
Queen Alys, daughter of Lucas Harroway; in 44 AC she lost her child, a deformed monster. — quote: "the boy a monster, with twisted limbs, a huge head, and no eyes"
Her father had sent men to father her children; she was given to Tyanna and her body cut into seven parts and mounted on the city gates. — quote: "the queen’s body was cut into seven parts"

### Lucas Harroway
Hand of the King and father of Queen Alys; he sent men to his daughter's bed to get her with child. — quote: "Lucas Harroway sent men to his daughter's bed"
He was flung from the roof of the Tower of the Hand. — quote: "flung from its roof to smash upon the stones below"

### Ceryse Hightower
Maegor's first wife and sister of Lord Hightower; reconciled with him at Oldtown. — quote: "reconciled with his first wife, Queen Ceryse"
She died of a sudden illness. A rumor, which most maesters now hold a slander, said Maegor had Ser Owen remove her tongue and her throat was slit. — quote: "most maesters believe it to be a slander concocted by the king’s enemies"

### Jeyne Westerling
Lady of House Westerling and widow of Alyn Tarbeck, to whom she bore a posthumous son; taken as a Black Bride. — quote: "had been married to Alyn Tarbeck, who had died with Prince Aegon"
Three moons early she bore a monstrous stillborn child and did not long survive it. — quote: "a legless and armless creature possessed of both male and female genitalia"

### Elinor Costayne
Lady of House Costayne and widow of Ser Theo Bolling, mother of three sons; taken as a Black Bride at nineteen. — quote: "Lady Elinor had already given Bolling three sons"
She bore an eyeless winged stillbirth in 48 AC and later found Maegor dead upon the Iron Throne. — quote: "an eyeless boy born with rudimentary wings"

### Rogar Baratheon
Lord of Storm's End; he proclaimed Jaehaerys the true king and was named Protector of the Realm and Hand. — quote: "proclaimed young Jaehaerys Targaryen to be the true and lawful king"

### Septon Moon
A bearded brute crowned "High Septon" by the Poor Fellows; he roamed the realm preaching against the king and led thousands toward Oldtown. — quote: "to preach against the king"

### Ser Joffrey Doggett
The Red Dog of the Hills, self-proclaimed Grand Captain of the Warrior's Sons; he sent his men out disguised as hedge knights to slay loyalists. — quote: "the guise of hedge knights, to hunt and slay Targaryen loyalists"
He was later seen entering Riverrun as Lord Tully's guest. — quote: "not as a captive but as a guest of Lord Tully"

### Poxy Jeyne Poore
Woman leader of the Poor Fellows; betrayed by three followers, declared a witch, and burned alive by the Wendwater. — quote: "betrayed by three of her own followers"

### Melony Piper
Sister of Jon Piper and Rhaena's girlhood friend; she led the Piper levies in man's mail and died at the Gods Eye. — quote: "who donned man's mail and took up a spear"

### Ser Davos Darklyn
Kingsguard who marshalled five thousand swords for Maegor; slain by Lord Corbray with Lady Forlorn. — quote: "slain at the hands of Lord Corbray with Lady Forlorn"

### Marq Farman
New Lord of Fair Isle, whose father and elder brother died fighting for Aegon; he gave Rhaena sanctuary at Faircastle. — quote: "gave her sanctuary and swore no harm would come to her"

### Ser Walton Towers
Victor of the Harrenhal melee, named Lord of Harrenhal, dead of his wounds within the fortnight. — quote: "did not live long enough to enjoy his lordship"

### Balerion
The Black Dread, Maegor's mount; for the first time since the Doom, dragon fought dragon when he destroyed Quicksilver. — quote: "dragon contended with dragon in the sky"

### Ser Theo Bolling
Landed knight and Elinor's first husband; charged with conspiring to enthrone Jaehaerys and beheaded the same day. — quote: "he was found guilty and beheaded the same day"

### Ser Owen Bush
Kingsguard rumored to have slain Ceryse; found dead outside a brothel, his member in his mouth. — quote: "found dead outside a brothel, his member stuffed into his mouth"
