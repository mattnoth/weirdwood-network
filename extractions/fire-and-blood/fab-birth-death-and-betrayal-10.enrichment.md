## Node Prose
### Jaehaerys I Targaryen
A restless king who announced his first royal progress late in 51 AC, resolved to travel with no more than one hundred men rather than the thousand Aegon the Conqueror had used — quote: "No more than one hundred men would accompany him". He rode the dragon Vermithor and held enormous store by it — quote: "I do not need to ring myself about with swords so long as | ride Vermithor". In 52 AC he promulgated the Widow's Law and, that same year, began the rebuilding of King's Landing — quote: "King Jaehaerys in 52 AC promulgated the Widow's Law"; quote: "it was the year 52 AC when it began, by the king's command". He heeded Alysanne's influence even lacking her formal power — quote: "when she spoke, he listened".

### Alysanne Targaryen
Queen who accompanied Jaehaerys on progress and instituted the women's courts, open only to women and girls — quote: "Only women and girls were welcome at these audiences". At Maidenpool she was attacked by three sisters while undressing to bathe in Jonquil's Pool, but was unharmed — quote: "murderous blades touched the queen". She requested a female protector and gained Jonquil Darke — quote: "I need a protector of mine own". She bore a son, Aegon, who died at three days, and later a healthy daughter, Daenerys — quote: "delivered of a son, a boy she named Aegon"; quote: "a girl she named Daenerys". Though she could not enact law, she wielded great influence over the king — quote: "wield enormous influence over King Jaehaerys".

### Jonquil Darke
Bastard half-sister of the new Lord Darklyn, summoned to court at Alysanne's need — quote: "his bastard half-sister, Jonquil Darke". She had been the mystery knight of the War for the White Cloaks — quote: "the mystery knight known as the Serpent in Scarlet". She accepted appointment as the queen's sworn shield and became known as the Scarlet Shadow — quote: "gladly accepted appointment as the queen's own sworn shield"; quote: "she would be known about the realm as the Scarlet".

### Prudence Celtigar
One of the queen's Wise Women, stabbed through the shoulder defending Alysanne at Maidenpool — quote: "Prudence Celtigar stabbed through the shoulder". By Alysanne's offices she was later betrothed to Lord Grafton of Gulltown — quote: "the betrothal of Lady Prudence Celtigar to Lord Grafton of Gulltown was arranged".

### Rosamund Ball
One of the queen's Wise Women; stepped between the attackers and Alysanne and took a fatal dagger in the belly — quote: "Rosamund Ball took a dagger in the belly that, three days later, proved to be the death of her".

### Alyssa Velaryon
The Dowager Queen, thought past childbearing, whose pregnancy at forty-four was received as a miracle — quote: "At forty-four years of age, the Dowager Queen had been thought to be well beyond her childbearing years". She was fearful, recalling the babe Vaella she bore King Aenys who died in the cradle — quote: "the little girl Vaella who had died in the cradle". She bore a healthy son, Boremund, and then conceived again at forty-six — quote: "Queen Alyssa was once again with child, at the age of forty-six".

### Rogar Baratheon
Alyssa's husband, whom the prospect of a child moved to cast off his anger and repent his infidelities — quote: "he cast off his anger and repented of his infidelities". He named his son Boremund and, at the second pregnancy, foresaw no trouble — quote: "named his son Boremund"; quote: "His wife had given birth to seven children, he insisted. Why not an eighth?".

### Boremund Baratheon
Robust, healthy son of Rogar and Alyssa, born early the following year with black hair and a loud cry — quote: "a big red-faced boy born with a fuzz of jet-black hair".

### Aegon Targaryen (son of Jaehaerys I)
Son of Alysanne and Jaehaerys, named to honor the Conqueror and the uncrowned prince — quote: "a boy she named Aegon". He came too early and died at three days; Alysanne forever blamed the Maidenpool attackers — quote: "he died three days after birth"; quote: "she blamed her son's death on the women who attacked her at Maidenpool".

### Rhaena Targaryen (daughter of Aenys I)
The Queen in the East, who set up her own small court on Dragonstone and received many visitors coldly — quote: "Many of her visitors were received coldly". Her reunion with her daughter Aerea went poorly, and she denied Elissa Farman the gold for a ship — quote: "reunion with her daughter Aerea had not gone well"; quote: "Rhaena denied her request". When Elissa fled with three dragon eggs, Rhaena's fury led her to expel Ser Merrell Bullock and question her husband Androw — quote: "there was no hiding her fury". She flew Dreamfyre to King's Landing to tell Jaehaerys — quote: "did Rhaena Targaryen mount Dreamfyre to fly to the Red Keep".

### Aerea Targaryen
Daughter of Aegon the Uncrowned and Rhaena, heir to the Iron Throne, a strong-willed girl of eleven — quote: "the daughter of Aegon the Uncrowned and his sister Rhaena, was eleven years of age". Carried unwillingly to dull Dragonstone, she found her only human friend in Elissa Farman and befriended the dragons — quote: "the only one the princess warmed to was Elissa Farman of Fair Isle". Displaced as heir by newborn Daenerys, she begged Elissa to take her away — quote: "was not pleased to find herself displaced by the"; quote: "I want to sail the seas and have adventures".

### Elissa Farman
Lady of Fair Isle who told Aerea tales and promised to teach her to sail, but pined for the western seas — quote: "she missed her wide western seas". Denied incomes by her brother Franklyn and gold by Rhaena, she left Dragonstone — quote: "Denied any part of the incomes of Fair Isle by her brother Lord Franklyn". A fortnight later three dragon eggs were found missing and she was judged the thief, having sailed by way of Pentos toward Braavos — quote: "Ser Merrell was convinced that Lady Elissa had made off with them".

### Rego Draz
The Pentoshi master of coin, styled the Lord of Air, widely loathed and resented for his flaunted wealth — quote: "the growing unpopularity of the Lord of Air, Rego Draz". He was said to be corrupt, a charge he mocked, and said to be godless, worshipping a single bat-headed idol — quote: "Why should | steal from the king? | am twice as rich as he is"; quote: "a small household idol like unto a woman great with child, with swollen breasts and a bat's head". To pay for the Dragonpit and city works he imposed a lucrative gate fee — quote: "Lord Rego reluctantly imposed a new levy: a gate fee".

### Corlys Velaryon (the Sea Snake)
Handsome, healthy boy born on Driftmark to the eldest son of Daemon Velaryon, named after his great-great-uncle — quote: "The babe was named Corlys, after the great-great-uncle". He would come to be known as the Sea Snake — quote: "would come to know this new Corlys better as the Sea Snake".

### Daenerys Targaryen (daughter of Jaehaerys I)
Strong, healthy daughter of Alysanne and Jaehaerys, born in the seventh moon of 53 AC and doted on by the king — quote: "brought to bed during the seventh moon of 53 AC"; quote: "he doted on his daughter from the moment he first took her in his arms".

### Septon Oswyck
Old, failing septon who had performed the marriage rites for Jaehaerys and Alysanne when the Faith opposed it — quote: "Oswyck had agreed to perform the rites for them". He died in his sleep in 54 AC and was interred on Dragonstone — quote: "Septon Oswyck passed in his sleep".

### Merrell Bullock
Ser, commander of the Dragonstone garrison, who brought forward the grooms and concluded Elissa had stolen the eggs — quote: "still commander of the castle garrison". Rhaena relieved him of command and expelled him with his son Ser Alyn — quote: "she relieved him of his command and expelled him from Dragonstone".

### Androw Farman
Rhaena's husband and Elissa's brother, whom Rhaena summoned to demand whether he had shared in his sister's crime — quote: "went so far as to summon her husband, Androw Farman". His denials only enraged her further — quote: "His denials only goaded her to more rage".

### Grand Maester Benifer
Grand Maester who hailed Alyssa's second pregnancy as a second miracle and later witnessed Rhaena and Jaehaerys in the king's solar — quote: "Grand Maester Benifer proclaimed when he told the king". He suggested the stolen eggs might not hatch away from Dragonstone — quote: "simply turn to stone".

### Septon Barth
Septon who took on Oswyck's duties and doubted Alyssa had strength enough to carry another child to term — quote: "he questioned whether she still had strength enough to carry a child to term".

### Jennis Templeton
Lady who traveled with the king in Alysanne's place to hold women's courts at Riverrun and Stoney Sept — quote: "Lady Jennis Templeton traveled with the king to hold".

### Maegor Towers
The nine-year-old new lord of Harrenhal who hosted the king at the start of the riverlands progress — quote: "the nine-year-old Maegor Towers".

### Franklyn Farman
Lord of Fair Isle who denied his sister Elissa any part of the isle's incomes — quote: "her brother Lord Franklyn".
