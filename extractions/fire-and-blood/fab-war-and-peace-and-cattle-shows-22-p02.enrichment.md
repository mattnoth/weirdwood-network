## Node Prose
### Unwin Peake
Lord Regent, Lord of Starpike, Lord of Dunstonbury, Lord of Whitegrove, Protector of the Realm, and Hand of the King — quote: "Lord of Starpike, Lord of Dunstonbury, Lord of Whitegrove, Protector of the Realm, and Hand of the King". Gyldayn names him the only plausible culprit had Jaehaera been murdered, though there is no proof — quote: "the only truly plausible culprit: Unwin Peake, Lord Regent". He would not allow a woman to ascend the Iron Throne, having fought to prevent it — quote: "Lord Peake was not about to allow a woman to ascend the Iron Throne". He married thrice and sired seven children, of whom only Myrielle survived — quote: "Unwin Peake had married thrice and sired seven children. Only one survived". He put his daughter Myrielle forward as the king's new queen and, when opposed, staged a Maiden's Day ball to secure the match — quote: "Myrielle Peake was to be Aegon III's new queen".

### Aegon III
The young king, Jaehaera's widower, dour and gloomy — quote: "The boy was dour and gloomy, no one truly knew his nature". Told he must set aside mourning and wed again, he asked what if he did not like Myrielle — quote: "What if | do not like her?". At the ball he sat the Iron Throne in black with a golden circlet and nodded to each maid without answering them — quote: "clad in black with a golden circlet round his head". He chose the six-year-old Daenaera Velaryon, telling her she looked very pretty — quote: "Thank you for coming, my lady, you look very pretty".

### Jaehaera Targaryen
Queen of the Seven Kingdoms and last surviving child of King Aegon II, she died aged ten on the twenty-second day of the ninth moon of 133 AC — quote: "the last surviving child of King Aegon II, perished at the age of ten". She died as her mother Queen Helaena had, thrown from a window in Maegor's Holdfast onto iron spikes — quote: "throwing herself from a window in Maegor's Holdfast onto the iron spikes". Impaled, she twisted in agony for half an hour before dying — quote: "Impaled through breast and belly, she twisted in agony for half an hour". A frightened, lonely, simpleminded child, she had hidden herself away in the Red Keep — quote: "Jaehaera was a lonely child, prone to weeping and somewhat simpleminded". Whether she took her own life or was murdered was never resolved — quote: "The true tale of how Jaehaera Targaryen met her end will never be known".

### Mervyn Flowers
Ser; the Kingsguard posted at the queen's door the night she died, and Unwin Peake's bastard brother — quote: "was Mervyn Flowers, his bastard brother". Bastard-born, he was reckoned a dutiful if unheroic knight — quote: "regarded by most a dutiful, if not especially heroic, member of the Kingsguard". According to Mushroom, who drank with him, he was fond of wine and, though sworn to chastity, seldom slept alone — quote: "he was fond of wine, says Mushroom, who was known to have drunk with him". Mushroom insists he would never have thrown the child himself, but might have stood aside to admit someone like Tessario the Thumb — quote: "he might well have stood aside to allow someone else to enter her room".

### Myrielle Peake
Lady; Unwin Peake's last surviving child, put forward as Aegon III's new queen — quote: "The Hand's last surviving child was a daughter, Myrielle". After the king's turnip remark spread, she became known throughout the realm as Lady Turnips — quote: "was soon known as Lady Turnips throughout". Four-and-ten and a year older than the king, brown of hair and eye with a broad freckled face and crooked teeth — quote: "Lady Turnips was four-and-ten, one year older than Aegon". Her father brought her to court early and arranged suppers with the king, who gave her one of the little queen's dolls — quote: "he gave her one of the little queen's dolls".

### Daenaera Velaryon
An orphan of six, daughter of the late Daeron Velaryon and Hazel of House Harte, ward of Lady Baela and Alyn the Oakenfist — quote: "a ward of Lady Baela of House Targaryen and Alyn the Oakenfist of House Velaryon". Her mother was carried off by the Winter Fever and her father died when his *True Heart* went down in the Stepstones — quote: "her father had died in the Stepstones when his True Heart went down". The blood of Old Valyria was strong in her, silver hair laced with gold and eyes blue as a summer sea — quote: "The blood of Old Valyria was strong in her". Presented at the ball by the twins, she was chosen by the king as his bride — quote: "His Grace will marry".

### Baela Targaryen
Lady; half-sister of the king and twin of Rhaena, daughter of Prince Daemon, great with child on Driftmark — quote: "the child growing inside Lady Baela on Driftmark". With her twin she rode into the throne room to present Daenaera as the king's new queen — quote: "we have brought your new queen". She slashed Ser Marston Waters across the cheek with her riding crop when he blocked her path — quote: "Lady Baela slashed him across the cheek with her riding crop".

### Rhaena Targaryen
Lady; twin of Baela, half-sister of the king, wife of Ser Corwyn Corbray — quote: "Her lord husband, Ser Corwyn Corbray". She announced she was with child by Corwyn Corbray, then miscarried a moon later — quote: "Lady Rhaena announced that she was with child by Lord Corwyn Corbray". Wan and thin from her miscarriage, she rode with her twin to bring the king his new queen — quote: "Lady Rhaena wan and thin from her miscarriage".

### Mushroom
The court fool and author of the *Testimony*, much in the king's company after Jaehaera's death — quote: "The dwarf was much in His Grace's company following the death of Queen Jaehaera". He confesses he was paid handsomely to poison the king against rival maids — quote: "paid "handsomely" to poison Aegon III against these maids and others". He named the ball the "Maiden's Day Cattle Show" — quote: ""The Maiden's Day Cattle Show," Mushroom would name the ball". He says Tessario the Thumb offered him "silver or steel," and he took the purse — quote: "seized that sweet fat purse".

### Dalton Greyjoy
The Red Kraken; his longships were waiting for the betrayed Lannister crossing to Fair Isle — quote: "but someone had betrayed them, and the longships were waiting". He sent the heads of the three Lannister commanders to Casterly Rock — quote: "Dalton Greyjoy sent their heads to Casterly Rock afterward". He called it payment for his uncle, whom he dismissed as a glutton and a drunkard — quote: "payment for my uncle, though in truth he was a glutton and a drunkard".

### Johanna Lannister
Lady of Casterly Rock; after her victory at Kayce she assembled a ragtag fleet at Feastfires to retake Fair Isle — quote: "Lady Johanna Lannister hoped to follow her victory at Kayce". She later wrote the Hand putting forward her daughters Cerelle and Tyshara as brides — quote: "her daughters Cerelle and Tyshara were maidens of noble birth".

### Titus Peake
Ser; heir to Starpike and the only one of Unwin's sons to reach manhood — quote: "Ser Titus, heir to Starpike, was the only one of Lord Unwin's sons to grow to manhood". Knighted for valor after the Battle of the Honeywine by Bold Jon Roxton, he died six days later in a skirmish with broken men — quote: "Knighted for valor after the Battle of the Honeywine by Bold Jon Roxton".

### Cassandra Baratheon
Lady; eldest of the "Four Storms" and once heir to Storm's End, briefly betrothed to King Aegon II in his last year — quote: "briefly betrothed to King Aegon II during the last year of his life". Her detractors said she resented tending the child queen she blamed for her woes — quote: "bitterly resented having to care for the weepy, feeble-witted child queen". Her mother Elenda later put her forward as a bride, and the rumor she pushed the little queen to her death was revived — quote: "Cassandra Baratheon had pushed the little queen to her death".

### Steffon Connington
Ser; hunting Dornish raiders across the marches, he rode too far and was set upon by one-armed Wyland Wyl, leaving Lady Elenda widowed again — quote: "armed Wyland Wyl came down on him, and Lady Elenda found herself widowed once again".

### Daenaera's parents (Daeron Velaryon)
Daeron Velaryon, son of Ser Vaemond who was beheaded by Queen Rhaenyra, had reconciled with Lord Alyn and died fighting for him — quote: "Daeron had been reconciled with Lord Alyn and had died fighting for him".

### Samantha Tarly
Lady Samantha of Oldtown; wrote the boldest letter, putting forward her sister Sansara of House Tarly and her good-sister Bethany of House Hightower — quote: "her sister Sansara (of House Tarly)". She suggested the king marry both, as Alysanne ruled beside Jaehaerys — quote: "one to rule beside him, as Queen Alysanne did King Jaehaerys".

### Sylas the Grim
A raider who led three thousand wildlings against the Wall and overwhelmed the black brothers at Queensgate before Cregan Stark hunted him down — quote: "led three thousand wildlings against the Wall".

### Gaemon Palehair
Aegon III's young cupbearer, delighted by Mushroom's japes; given the honor of announcing the king's choice of bride — quote: "his young cupbearer, Gaemon Palehair".

### Munkun
Grand Maester; he accompanied Peake to the king, wavered on the match warning of appearances, and examined Tyshara Lannister — quote: "Grand Maester Munkun's examination revealed her maidenhead was broken".
