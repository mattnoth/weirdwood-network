## Node Prose
### Eustace Hightower
Ser Eustace Hightower was the grandson of Lord Donnel Hightower and captained the Lady Meredith on the western voyage out of Oldtown — quote: "Lord Donnel’s grandson". His ship returned to Oldtown battered after nearly three years, her crew mostly Summer Islanders hired in Sothoryos — quote: "hired on in Sothoryos". His brother captained the accompanying Autumn Moon and drowned when she foundered in a storm — quote: "under it my brother floated, dead with all his men". He refused to sail farther west and turned back at the newfound islands, then was stranded on Sothoryos for a year before reaching home — quote: "We were there for a full year".

### Elissa Farman
Born Elissa of House Farman, she is called Lady Alys Westhill and Lady Westhill in this account — quote: "born Elissa of House Farman". She captained the fast Braavosi ship Sun Chaser, seeking the lands beyond the Sunset Sea — quote: "steered her Sun Chaser south by southwest". She discovered three uninhabited western islands and named them — quote: "she named them Aegon, Rhaenys, and Visenya". When Eustace turned back she pressed on westward, and the Sun Chaser was never seen again — quote: "vanished into the west, still searching for the lands beyond the Sunset Sea".

### Corlys Velaryon
Corlys Velaryon was a boy born on Driftmark in 53 AC, a boy of six in 59 AC — quote: "the boy born on Driftmark in 53 AC". He would later sail nine great voyages aboard the Sea Snake, reaching Yi Ti, Leng, and Asshai — quote: "take his ship the Sea Snake on nine great voyages". He became the first Westerosi to reach Asshai, and House Velaryon doubled its wealth from his first voyage — quote: "became the first Westerosi ever to reach Asshai-by-theShadow". In Asshai's harbor he glimpsed a ship he swore was the Sun Chaser, though the account is hedged — quote: "if the tales be true".

### Lady Meredith
The Lady Meredith was a Hightower ship that limped back into Oldtown in 59 AC after almost three years at sea — quote: "last seen departing". A leviathan cracked her hull, forcing Eustace to make for Sothoryos — quote: "slammed into Lady Meredith of a purpose, and cracked her hull".

### Sun Chaser
The Sun Chaser was a fast Braavosi ship captained by Alys Westhill — quote: "The big Braavosi ship was very fast". Driven west by the storm, she found land unknown to any chart, then sailed on and vanished — quote: "She had found land".

### Autumn Moon
The Autumn Moon was a Hightower ship captained by Eustace's brother — quote: "the Lady Meredith and Autumn Moon had followed". She foundered in a great storm with all her men lost — quote: "saw the Autumn Moon founder with my own eyes".

### Donnel Hightower
Lord Donnel was Eustace's grandsire, in whose high hall the tale was told and recorded by his scribes — quote: "in his grandsire's high hall".

### Alaric Stark
Lord Alaric Stark had commanded that half of every harvest be preserved against the coming winter, though not all his bannermen obeyed — quote: "Lord Alaric Stark had commanded that half of every harvest be preserved".
