## Node Prose
### Jaehaerys
King Jaehaerys remade the realm's roads—roseroad, ocean road, river road, goldroad—filling ruts and bridging streams for decades. — quote: "Jaehaerys would remake them beyond all recognition, filling ruts, spreading gravel, bridging streams"
His monument at Oldtown reads that he unified the kingdoms. — quote: "He bound the land together, and made of seven kingdoms, one"
He appointed Martyn Tyrell as his new master of coin. — quote: "the king’s new master of coin, Martyn Tyrell"
He sentenced Ser Lucamore to be gelded and sent to the Wall for breaking his vows. — quote: "Ser Lucamore be gelded forthwith, then clapped in irons and sent off to the Wall"
He sent Vaegon to the Citadel at Oldtown. — quote: "he would be taking ship for Oldtown in a fortnight"

### Alysanne
Queen Alysanne bore Jaehaerys many children across these years and grieved deeply at each infant loss. — quote: "continued to bless Jaehaerys and Alysanne with children"
She took great pride in arranging marriages but struggled bitterly to match her four younger children. — quote: "never had she faced so much difficulty as she did whilst searching for mates"
She dealt with the three families of Ser Lucamore, decreeing his children bear bastard names. — quote: "from this day they would bear the bastard names Rivers, Waters, and Storm"

### Vaegon
Prince Vaegon, born 63 AC, was quiet, sour, and unlike his elder brothers, preferring books to the yard. — quote: "He preferred the library to the yard"
He publicly refused to wed his sister Daella, mocking her as unable to read. — quote: "I would never marry her,” the boy said, in front of half the court"
Grand Maester Elysar judged he had the makings of an archmaester, and he was sent to the Citadel. — quote: "he could well have the makings of an archmaester in him"

### Daella
Princess Daella, born 64 AC, was delicate, shy, tongue-tied, and easily frightened. — quote: "Princess Daella, the next oldest, was delicate and shy"
She was afraid of dragons, horses, cats, and bees, and learned to read only haltingly. — quote: "The dragons terrified her, even Silverwing"
Corlys Velaryon, Royce Blackwood, and various squires were considered for her before three great suitors were offered. — quote: "he likes his boats better than he likes me"

### Saera
Princess Saera, born about 67 AC, was tempestuous, demanding, and disobedient from the start. — quote: "Princess Saera came into the world, red-faced and squalling"
Her first word was "no" and she refused weaning past the age of four. — quote: "The first word she spoke was no"
Alysanne likened her to Aerea. — quote: "when | look at her | see Aerea"

### Viserra
Princess Viserra, born 71 AC, was described as sly and vain, and strikingly beautiful. — quote: "Sly was one word used to describe her. Vain was another."
When a squire called her a goddess, she agreed. — quote: "When one stammering young squire told her she was a goddess, she agreed"

### Aemon
Aemon, the firstborn son and Prince of Dragonstone, was betrothed to Jocelyn Baratheon in 68 AC and wed her in 70 AC. — quote: "the betrothal of their firstborn son, Aemon, Prince of Dragonstone, to Jocelyn Baratheon"
He won his knight's spurs at the Duskendale tourney in 72 AC, beating his brother Baelon in the melee. — quote: "hammering his brother into submission"
He became a dragonrider on blood-red Caraxes, the Blood Wyrm. — quote: "His mount was blood-red Caraxes"

### Baelon
Baelon the Brave, the Spring Prince, followed Aemon into knighthood in 73 AC, jousting at Old Oak as a mystery knight called the Silver Fool. — quote: "calling himself the Silver Fool"
He claimed the great she-dragon Vhagar, unridden since Visenya's death twenty-nine years before. — quote: "the great she-dragon Vhagar spread her wings"
In 75 AC he wed his sister Alyssa; in 77 AC she bore him a son, Viserys. — quote: "In 77 AC she gave her brave prince a son they named Viserys"

### Alyssa
Princess Alyssa, eldest of the remaining sisters, was destined for Baelon from childhood and wed him in 75 AC at fifteen. — quote: "Alyssa is for Baelon"
She was bawdy and boastful about the bedding. — quote: "| mounted him and took him for a ride"
She became a dragonrider on the swift scarlet she-dragon Meleys. — quote: "Princess Alyssa ascended into the sky upon Meleys"

### Jocelyn Baratheon
Lady Jocelyn Baratheon of Storm's End, one of the great beauties of the realm, wed Aemon in 70 AC. — quote: "Lady Jocelyn at sixteen years old was one of the great beauties of the realm"
She bore Rhaenys, Jaehaerys and Alysanne's first grandchild, in 74 AC. — quote: "the Lady Jocelyn, presented them with their first grandchild"
She was daughter of Rogar Baratheon and Queen Alyssa, half-sister to Jaehaerys. — quote: "Queen Alyssa, was forty-six when she gave birth to Jocelyn"

### Rhaenys
Princess Rhaenys, daughter of Aemon and Jocelyn, was born on the seventh day of the seventh moon of 74 AC. — quote: "Princess Rhaenys was born on the seventh day of the seventh moon"
Many hailed her as next in line for the Iron Throne after her father, and Alysanne called her "our queen to be." — quote: "our queen to be"

### Lucamore Strong
Ser Lucamore Strong of the Kingsguard was found to have secretly taken three wives and fathered sixteen children. — quote: "was found to have been secretly wed"
Discovered by Ser Ryam Redwyne, he confessed before the Iron Throne and was gelded and sent to the Wall. — quote: "keeping each woman ignorant of the other two"
The smallfolk mocked him as "Ser Lucamore the Lusty." — quote: "Ser Lucamore the Lusty"

### Corlys Velaryon
Corlys Velaryon, grandson to the Lord of the Tides and the future Sea Snake, was already a celebrated mariner when Daella was sent to meet him at thirteen. — quote: "the future Sea Snake was already a celebrated mariner and captain of ships"

### Royce Blackwood
Royce Blackwood, son of Lord Blackwood of Raventree, courted Daella at fifteen as a bowman, swordsman, and singer. — quote: "Royce Blackwood was a gifted bowman, a fine swordsman, and a singer"
The match failed because the Blackwoods kept the old gods. — quote: "the Blackwoods kept the old gods"

### Boremund Baratheon
Boremund Baratheon, Lord of Storm's End at eight-and-twenty, was the most imposing of Daella's three suitors. — quote: "Boremund Baratheon was the most imposing of the candidates"
As son of Lord Rogar by Queen Alyssa, he was half-brother to Jaehaerys and Alysanne. — quote: "he stood halfbrother to Alysanne and Jaehaerys"

### Tymond Lannister
Ser Tymond Lannister, heir to Casterly Rock at twenty, was the wealthiest and one of the handsomest of Daella's suitors. — quote: "Ser Tymond Lannister was the wealthiest contender, heir to Casterly Rock"

### Maegelle
Princess Maegelle joined the Faith as a novice in 73 AC at ten years of age, a quiet, studious girl. — quote: "her daughter Maegelle would be joining the Faith as a novice"

### Elysar
Grand Maester Elysar counseled Jaehaerys and Alysanne on Vaegon and later nursed the infant Gael through her first year. — quote: "he could well have the makings of an archmaester in him"

### Gaemon
Prince Gaemon, the eleventh child, was born 73 AC and named for Gaemon the Glorious; he died an infant. — quote: "Prince Gaemon died just short of the new year"

### Valerion
Prince Valerion was born 77 AC, a small and sickly babe, and died in 78 AC. — quote: "In 78 AC, Valerion died, a fortnight short of his first nameday"

### Gael
Princess Gael, born 80 AC, "the Winter Child," was frail but nursed through her first year with Septa Lyra's aid. — quote: "Called “the Winter Child” for the season of her birth"

### Alaric Stark
Alaric Stark, Lord of Winterfell, died in 72 AC; his grandson Edric succeeded him. — quote: "it fell to his grandson Edric to succeed him"
