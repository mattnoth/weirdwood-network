## Node Prose
### Jaehaerys I Targaryen
His long reign was peaceful for the most part, with few and short wars — quote: "The long reign of Jaehaerys | Targaryen was a peaceful one". He vowed to recover the stolen dragon eggs even by war across the narrow sea, saying he would not let Valyria rise again — quote: "| will not allow Valyria to rise again". He backed Septon Barth for High Septon before Barth talked him out of it, then flew to Oldtown to secure an Exceptionalist and block Septon Mattheus — quote: "the crystal crown: Septon Barth". After his mother's death he shrugged off sorrow and returned to ruling — quote: "the king shrugged off his sorrows and plunged himself into the ruling".

### Alysanne Targaryen
The queen accompanied Jaehaerys to Oldtown on Silverwing and toured the region, meeting her niece Rhaella among the novices — quote: "her niece Rhaella". She lectured the Citadel's archmaesters, urging them to admit highborn girls — quote: "Why not admit them to your Citadel?". She found solace in her daughter Daenerys after her mother's death and flew to Dragonstone to comfort Rhaena, who thrice tried to send her away — quote: "Having lost a mother, she found solace in a daughter".

### Rhaena Targaryen
Sister of the king, called the Queen in the East, seated on Dragonstone with Dreamfyre — quote: "The Queen in the East smiled then". She flew to Storm's End too late to see her mother alive and cursed Rogar Baratheon for Alyssa's death, threatening to burn Storm's End if he wed again — quote: "Her blood is on your hands". After the poisonings killed her companions she sank into black melancholy and rejected her sister's comfort — quote: "plunged her into a black melancholy". When Aerea fled, she raged, then mounted Dreamfyre to search — quote: "I need to find her".

### Queen Alyssa
Mother of Jaehaerys, Alysanne and Rhaena and wife of Rogar Baratheon, she lay dying in labor at Storm's End, too old and torn inside — quote: "their mother, Queen". Maester Kyrie cut her open to save the child and she died — quote: "Maester Kyrie opened her belly". Accounts differ on her end: in many she woke and gave her assent, saying the Crone would light her way; other accounts say she died without waking — quote: "other accounts tell us that Her Grace died without waking".

### Rogar Baratheon
Lord of Storm's End, he sat drunk and despairing outside his dying wife's chamber — quote: "seated outside the chamber door, drunk and despairing". Bidden by the king to choose, he told the maester to save his son, then fled — quote: "Save my son,". The child was a girl he named Jocelyn — quote: "Rogar Baratheon named his daughter Jocelyn". He laughed off Rhaena's curse and never wed again — quote: "Rogar Baratheon never wed again".

### Androw Farman
Husband of Rhaena, wed on Fair Isle when he was ten-and-seven, he was scorned and humiliated at her court, unable to read and mocked behind his back — quote: "the queen's husband could not read". He poisoned Rhaena's ladies with the tears of Lys, boasting of it — quote: "\| brought them cups of wine, and they drank". He knifed Maester Anselm, and when Rhaena ordered him gelded he leapt from a window to his death — quote: "Maester Anselm dead, with a dagger between his shoulder blades".

### Aerea Targaryen
Daughter of Rhaena, a willful wild child who defied her septa, mother and maesters and abused servants — quote: "her own daughter Aerea had become a terror". She begged Alysanne to take her to court, and when refused fled Dragonstone at dawn, claiming Balerion — quote: "She took Balerion, the mad child". Neither she nor the dragon was heard from again that year — quote: "Neither mother nor daughter was seen or heard from again".

### Elissa Farman
Driven from Fair Isle and disowned by her brother Franklyn, she took the bastard name Alys Westhill — quote: "calling herself Alys Westhill". As Alys Westhill she sold the dragon eggs to the Sealord of Braavos and used the gold to build the Sun Chaser — quote: "he was glad to buy the dragon eggs".

### Septon Barth
Came to oversee the Red Keep library and became a trusted advisor; too young and unorthodox to be High Septon — quote: "one of his most trusted advisors". Years later he set down a summary of the Oldtown bargain, rueing the corruption of the Most Devout — quote: "the corruption that made the Most Devout so easy to manipulate". He was later suspected of greater loyalty to the Starry Sept and was lowborn, son of a blacksmith — quote: "the son of a blacksmith".

### Rego Draz
The Pentoshi master of coin who suggested bribing the Most Devout as a jape — quote: "Spread enough gold amongst these Most Devout". He identified the Dragonstone deaths as poison, the tears of Lys — quote: "this is the tears of Lys".

### Daemon Velaryon
Lord and Hand of the King, brother of Queen Alyssa and uncle of Lianna Velaryon — quote: "Queen Alyssa, it will be recalled, had been Lord Daemon's sister". He commanded his galleys to blockade Dragonstone, then stepped down as Hand for age — quote: "his desire to step down as Hand of the King".

### Donnel Hightower
Lord of Oldtown, distrusted as a schemer, with kin among the Most Devout he hoped to see raised — quote: "Lord Donnel is a schemer and a sulker". He struck a bargain with Jaehaerys and hosted the royal couple atop the Hightower — quote: "reached an accord with the powerful Hightowers of Oldtown".

### Septon Alfyn
A Seven Speaker and fierce champion of Exceptionalism, oldest and legless, chosen High Septon on the fourth ballot though not of the Most Devout — quote: "The laurel fell to the Septon Alfyn".

### Septon Mattheus
A candidate whose King's Landing tenure left mistrust; the king worked to prevent his selection, and he led early ballots before falling — quote: "Septon Mattheus was not chosen".

### Maester Kyrie
Maester attending Alyssa; said he could not save her but might save the child by cutting her open — quote: "to cut the mother open and remove the child".

### Lianna Velaryon
Cousin of Rhaena and niece of Lord Daemon Velaryon, she died of the poison in Rhaena's arms even as her uncle's galleys pushed off — quote: "She died in Rhaena Targaryen’s arms".

### Maester Culiper
Old maester of Dragonstone, near ninety, first to die of the sickness — quote: "Culiper himself was the first to die". He wrote to the Citadel of Androw's idle, illiterate misery — quote: "the queen's husband could not read".

### Maester Anselm
Succeeded Culiper and judged the deaths a plague sparing men and Valyrians; found stabbed in the back in Sea Dragon Tower — quote: "It was her Valyrian blood that saved her".

### Ser Myles Smallwood
Lord of Acorn Hall, scarred veteran who fought beneath the Gods Eye and battled Wat the Hewer; chosen as the new Hand — quote: "the choice finally fell to Ser Myles Smallwood".

### Simon Dondarrion
Son of Lord Dondarrion of Blackhaven who played the high harp and jousted, so charming Alysanne the party lingered — quote: "his son Simon played the high harp".

### Rodrik Arryn
Lord of the Eyrie and Defender of the Vale, a boy of ten who came into his lordship after his uncle Darnold and sire Rymond were slain by wildlings — quote: "his uncle Lord Darnold and his sire Ser Rymond".

### Jocelyn Baratheon
Daughter of Rogar and Alyssa, born small and weak but survived — quote: "she would surprise many others in her time".

### Cassella Staunton
Daughter of Lord Staunton, a lady on Dragonstone aged four-and-ten, second to die of the poison — quote: "Cassella Staunton was the next to succumb".

### Samantha Stokeworth
Sam Stokeworth, boisterous companion of Rhaena who boasted she was never sick, dead in a night — quote: "big, boisterous Sam Stokeworth".
