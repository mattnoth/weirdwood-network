## Node Prose
### Rodrik Arryn
Lord of the Eyrie and Protector of the Vale, a lord since the age of ten who served twenty years on the small council as lord justiciar and master of laws — quote: "he had served on the small council as lord justiciar and master of laws". At six-and-thirty he was twenty years older than Princess Daella, short and balding, and a widower with four children by his late first wife. Princess Daella chose him as husband — quote: "Princess Daella chose Lord Rodrik to be her husband" — and they wed at Dragonstone before year's end, with no bedding at her wish. He was a kind husband who called her "my precious princess," and was later replaced as justiciar and master of laws by Prince Aemon — quote: "justiciar and master of laws in place of Rodrik Arryn".

### Daella Targaryen
Daughter of King Jaehaerys and Queen Alysanne, so delicate she feared crowds and cats and dreaded a bedding. She wed Lord Rodrik Arryn and went to the Vale, clashing at once with his eldest daughter Elys but doting on the younger children. She bore a daughter, Aemma, a fortnight early after a long and troubled labor — quote: "Aemma Arryn, the daughter of Lord Rodrik and Princess Daella". Childbed fever set in and she died at eighteen in 82 AC — quote: "By morning she was gone. She was eighteen years of age" — and Alysanne had her burned and her ashes interred on Dragonstone — quote: "interred on Dragonstone beside her sister Daenerys".

### Queen Alysanne
Wife of King Jaehaerys, mother of the royal children. She flew to the Vale on Silverwing when Daella wrote in fear — quote: "She mounted Silverwing within days" — and stayed through the confinement. After Daella's death she blamed herself, Lord Arryn, the Eyrie's maester, and most of all the king for forcing the marriage — quote: "We ought never have pushed her into marriage." In the matter of Saera she urged mercy against the king's fury — quote: "She is our daughter," she told the king — and personally questioned Perianne and Alys to extract the truth.

### Jaehaerys I Targaryen
King and husband of Queen Alysanne. He named Prince Aemon justiciar and master of laws in Rodrik Arryn's place, and led his sons Aemon and Baelon with dragons to destroy Morion Martell's fleet, having long known of the attack through spies — quote: "did Jaehaerys have spies in Morion's own court". In the Saera affair he sat in judgment from the Iron Throne, imprisoned her three lords, and after her taunts disowned her — quote: "She is no longer my daughter" — confining her to a tower cell after her escape attempt.

### Saera Targaryen
The ninthborn child of King Jaehaerys and Queen Alysanne, born 67 AC, willful and voracious where her sister Daella was timid — quote: "the matter of their ninthborn child, the Princess Saera". As a girl she tormented Daella with cats and bees, dyed the Kingsguard's white cloaks pink, stole wine, and used the fool Tom Turnip as her catspaw. She took three young lords — Jonah Mooton, Red Roy Connington, and Stinger Beesbury — as lovers and confessed she had given her maidenhead to all three — quote: "| gave it to all three." When confined she escaped in a washerwoman's robes toward the Dragonpit and was taken and confined to a tower cell — quote: "as she tried to enter the Dragonpit, she was found and taken".

### Aemon Targaryen
Prince and heir apparent to the Iron Throne, who reached his twenty-sixth nameday in 81 AC and had proved able in war and peace — quote: "As the heir apparent to the Iron Throne". King Jaehaerys named him justiciar and master of laws in place of Rodrik Arryn — quote: "justiciar and master of laws in place of Rodrik Arryn". He rode with his father and brother against the Dornish fleet.

### Baelon Targaryen
Prince called "Baelon the Brave" and the "Spring Prince," brother of Aemon and husband of Princess Alyssa. He preferred making sons to making law — quote: "I would sooner make sons." — and fathered Viserys, Daemon, and Aegon. He rode against Morion's fleet and, after Alyssa's death, took solace in his two surviving sons — quote: "the two strong sons that she had left him, Viserys and Daemon".

### Alyssa Targaryen
Princess and wife of Prince Baelon, irrepressible and bold, "with the broken nose and mismatched eyes." She bore Daemon in 81 AC and flew him aloft on Meleys within a fortnight — quote: "took the babe into the sky on Meleys". She bore a third son, Aegon, in 84 AC after a long labor, never fully recovered, and died within the year at four-and-twenty — quote: "died within the year at only four-and-twenty".

### Aemma Arryn
Daughter of Lord Rodrik Arryn and Princess Daella, born a fortnight early in 82 AC after a troubled labor — quote: "came into the world a fortnight early".

### Morion Martell
Prince of Dorne who succeeded his father in Sunspear, rash and foolish, resentful of Dornish inaction during Lord Rogar's War — quote: "his son, Morion Martell, had succeeded him in Sunspear". He planned a seaborne invasion of the stormlands, hiring pirates, Myrish sellsails, and Pepper Coast corsairs and arming every ship with scorpions to kill dragons; his fleet was burned in the Sea of Dorne in a single day.

### Braxton Beesbury
A nineteen-year-old knight called "Stinger," the finest lance in the Reach and heir to Honeyholt, one of Saera's three favorites — quote: "the finest lance in the Reach, and the heir to Honeyholt". Queen Alysanne distrusted him, having heard he sired a bastard in the Reach and another in King's Landing, the latter on a whore of the Blue Pearl. Perianne Moore said it was Stinger who "did the training for all of them."

### Alys Turnberry
A maid of Saera's own age called "Sweetberry," one of Saera's dearest friends — quote: "became her dearest friends". She confessed the truth to Queen Alysanne and revealed she was with child by an unknown father — quote: "now she was with child and she did not know who the father was".

### Perianne Moore
A maid of Saera's own age called "Pretty Peri," one of Saera's dearest friends, who confessed the whole affair to Queen Alysanne.

### Tom Turnip
The king's half-witted fool, victim and unwitting catspaw of Saera's japes; she once had him climb the Iron Throne, which cut his arms and legs, and later had him brought naked to the Blue Pearl — quote: "the fool was clumsy at the best of times and prone to tremors".

### Elys Arryn
Eldest daughter of Lord Rodrik, three years older than her stepmother Daella, with whom she clashed from the first — quote: "The two of them clashed from the first."

### Amanda Arryn
Lord Rodrik's younger daughter, who wrote most of Daella's letters home — quote: "written for her by Lord Rodrik's younger daughter, Amanda".

### Robert Redwyne
Ser Robert Redwyne, commander of the City Watch, who delivered the three lords to the king and ignored their threats and bribery — quote: "before Ser Robert Redwyne, their commander".

### Mara Martell
Became the Princess of Dorne after the Fourth Dornish War — quote: "Mara Martell became the Princess of Dorne".

### Boremund Baratheon
Lord of Storm's End, made aware of Morion's coming and waiting on Cape Wrath to meet the Dornishmen — quote: "Boremund Baratheon, Lord of Storm's End".
