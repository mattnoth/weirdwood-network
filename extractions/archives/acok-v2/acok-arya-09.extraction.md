# ACOK — Arya IX

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** ACOK
- **chapter_number:** 48
- **pov_character:** Arya Stark
- **pov_chapter_number:** Arya IX
- **first_available:** ACOK Arya IX
- **location_primary:** Harrenhal
- **location_secondary:** Godswood (within Harrenhal), Widow's Tower dungeons, armory, kitchens, Barracks Hall, Kingspyre Tower, Flowstone Yard
- **approximate_timeline:** After Lord Tywin's departure from Harrenhal; Vargo Hoat returns with northern prisoners; Roose Bolton arrives to take command
- **time_markers:** "the black of night" (chapter opening); nighttime throughout the main action; "come dawn" marks the morning after; "all morning" Arya watches cleanup; "almost evenfall" when Roose Bolton arrives; "that evening" the chapter closes with Ser Amory's death
- **chapter_summary:** Arya is awake in the kitchens at night when Vargo Hoat returns with northern prisoners including Robett Glover and Ser Aenys Frey. She tries to convince Gendry to help free them but he refuses. In the godswood, Jaqen H'ghar finds her and she manipulates him into helping by naming him as her third death, then unsaying it once he agrees. Jaqen, Rorge, and Biter use boiling broth to overwhelm the dungeon guards and free the prisoners, who then take the castle. Jaqen changes his face, gives Arya an iron coin and the words "valar morghulis," and departs. Roose Bolton arrives and takes Arya as his cupbearer under the name "Nan." Ser Amory Lorch is thrown to the bear.

## Events & Actions
1. **Arya visits Hot Pie in the kitchens at night** — She tries to convince him to spit on Ser Amory's tarts; he refuses out of fear
2. **Warhorn sounds; portcullis opens** — Two blasts announce arrivals; ox carts enter Harrenhal
3. **Vargo Hoat returns with plunder and prisoners** — Carts carry silver plate, weapons, shields, flour, livestock; a caged black bear; striped zorses visible; Bloody Mummers speak in foreign tongues
4. **Northern prisoners marched in** — Bound at wrists and roped ankle-to-ankle; many wounded; Arya recognizes sigils: mailed fist (Glover), twin towers (Frey), sunburst (Karstark), bloody man (Bolton), battle-axe (Cerwyn)
5. **Vargo Hoat reports to Ser Amory** — Claims Roose Bolton tried to cross the river; Brave Companions cut his van to pieces; killed many; sent Bolton running; identifies Glover and Ser Aenys Frey
6. **Ser Amory orders prisoners to the Widow's Tower dungeon** — Dismisses Hoat's promises of honorable treatment; threatens death for any who refuse
7. **Arya slips away from the ward** — Avoids being put to work; crosses middle bailey, around Tower of Dread, through empty mews
8. **Arya visits Gendry at the armory** — Asks him to help free the northmen; he refuses, citing the danger and arguing he's better off staying
9. **Arya argues with Gendry about his identity** — She says the queen sent gold cloaks after him; he dismisses it; she leaves angry
10. **Arya practices sword drills in the godswood** — Uses her broomstick sword; climbs trees; slashes at branches and leaves representing her enemies; sees the heart tree and thinks of Winterfell
11. **Arya prays to the old gods** — Asks for help freeing the prisoners, killing Ser Amory, returning to Winterfell; then scolds the tree for not saving her father
12. **Jaqen H'ghar appears in the godswood** — Comes to hear the third name; Arya asks him to help free the prisoners instead
13. **Jaqen refuses to exceed his debt** — Three lives for three lives; will kill one guard if named, but not help with a mass rescue
14. **Arya names Jaqen H'ghar as her third death** — He is visibly distraught; produces a knife; calls her "evil child"
15. **Jaqen agrees to help in exchange for unsaying his name** — Arya conditionally agrees; Jaqen says "Come" and acts immediately
16. **Jaqen instructs Arya to order broth from the kitchens** — She runs to the kitchens and tells the cook broth is needed
17. **Arya helps prepare and waits in the kitchens** — Stirs broth with Hot Pie; cooks prepare food for the Brave Companions
18. **Rorge, Jaqen, and Biter arrive for the broth** — Take four kettles of boiling broth; Arya helps carry one with Jaqen
19. **They pass the Widow's Tower door guards** — Two guards let them through to bring broth to prisoners
20. **Dungeon assault** — Rorge throws boiling broth in the guards' faces; Jaqen does the same; Biter throws kettles that strike the captain; eight guards killed — throats cut by Rorge, necks broken by Biter, one killed by Jaqen with a sword thrust to the heart
21. **Jaqen wipes blood on Arya's shift** — "A girl should be bloody too. This is her work."
22. **Robett Glover freed; prisoners arm themselves** — Take weapons from dead guards; rush up the stairs; comment the wounded prisoners seem less wounded than before
23. **Castle takeover** — Freed northmen and Brave Companions kill Ser Amory's garrison; fighting at the gatehouse and across the ward; door guards found dead
24. **Arya unsays Jaqen's name** — He declares the debt paid with the dead guards (three above, eight below, plus more)
25. **Jaqen changes his face** — Passes hand from forehead to chin; cheeks fuller, eyes closer, nose hooked, scar appears on right cheek, hair changes from half-red half-white to tight black curls; gold tooth revealed
26. **Jaqen gives Arya the iron coin** — Tells her to give it to any man from Braavos with the words "valar morghulis" if she ever wants to find him again
27. **Jaqen departs** — Says "Jaqen is as dead as Arry"; stalks off into darkness
28. **Arya returns to bed; whispers her names plus "valar morghulis"**
29. **Morning: Pinkeye reports Harrenhal has changed hands** — Bloody Mummers killed some of Ser Amory's men in their beds, the rest at table; one servant boy killed in the fighting
30. **Cleanup** — Dead stripped and dragged to Flowstone Yard for burning; Shagwell prances with severed heads making them joke about "hot weasel soup"
31. **Arya mops blood all morning** — People look at her strangely; Glover's men must have talked
32. **Roose Bolton arrives at evenfall** — Plain-faced, pale-eyed; black ringmail and spotted pink cloak; flayed man sigil; takes command
33. **Shagwell drags Arya to Bolton** — Bolton questions her; she gives her name as "Nymeria, called Nan"; he makes her his cupbearer
34. **Banners changed** — Lannister lion and Lorch's black manticore replaced with Bolton flayed man and Stark direwolf
35. **Ser Amory Lorch thrown to the bear** — Paraded naked; pleads and sobs; Rorge pulls him loose; Shagwell kicks him into the bear pit; Arya pours wine for Bolton and does not spill a drop

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Restless and daring at the chapter's opening; determined and desperate when she sees the northern prisoners; frustrated and angry at Gendry's refusal; nostalgic and mournful in the godswood (thinking of Winterfell and her father); bold and calculating when manipulating Jaqen; frightened but resolved during the dungeon assault; awed by Jaqen's face-change; reluctant and sad when the debt is paid ("Now she was just a mouse again"); wary and alert around Roose Bolton
- **Primary preoccupation:** Freeing the northern prisoners and using them to retake Harrenhal; getting home to Winterfell
- **Key decisions made:** Decides to use Jaqen's third death to leverage a mass rescue rather than assassinate a single target; names Jaqen himself to force his cooperation; chooses not to tell Hot Pie the plan; gives Bolton the false name "Nymeria/Nan" rather than reveal herself; does not reveal her identity to Robett Glover either
- **Self-deception flags:** Tells herself she was hesitating on the third name because "the last death has to count," but then wonders if the real reason was that having the power made her feel safe — "once she used up the last death, she would only be a mouse again"
