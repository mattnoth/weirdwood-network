- **chapter_summary:** Yoren's band finds a river too deep to cross and a burned bridge, so they follow the river north toward a lakeside town hoping to find boats. The town is deserted — no people, animals, or boats remain. They shelter in the stone holdfast for the night. After dark, Ser Amory Lorch and Lannister forces arrive, burn the town, and assault the holdfast. Yoren orders the survivors to escape through a tunnel under the barn. Arya fights on the walls, then returns to the burning barn to throw an axe to the three chained prisoners before crawling through the tunnel to safety.

## Events & Actions
1. **Dead soldier discovered** — Koss spots a swollen dead soldier in the river reeds, wearing a sodden green cloak. Dobber searches the body and finds a leather purse with four coppers and a hank of blond hair tied with red ribbon.
2. **River crossing fails** — Yoren attempts to ride across the river but finds it too deep. Woth finds a burned wooden bridge downstream. Koss finds no ford upstream.
3. **Yoren plans new route** — Drawing in the mud, Yoren explains they'll follow the river north to a lakeside town, hoping to find boats, then sail across Gods Eye to Harrentown and ultimately Harrenhal.
4. **Abandoned countryside traversed** — The group passes a deserted cottage (garden raided for onions, radishes, cabbages), a forester's cabin, a stilt-house, and ripening fields — all devoid of people.
5. **Deserted town reached** — They arrive at a town of white houses around a stone holdfast, with a sept and towerhouse. No people, animals, or boats remain except one rotted rowboat.
6. **Lake search finds no boats** — Arya, Gendry, Hot Pie, Lommy, and Woth search the lakefront. All boats are gone. They find sails, nails, tar buckets, and a mother cat with kittens, but no usable boats.
7. **Gendry suggests building a raft** — Yoren considers the idea of poling along the shallows, which would mean abandoning the wagons.
8. **Party shelters in holdfast** — They bar the gates with iron X-braces, discover the escape tunnel under the barn, roll a wagon over the trap, and set watches. Tarber, Kurz, and Cutjack go to the towerhouse as lookouts.
9. **Evening meal prepared** — Goose and chickens caught and cooked. Arya plucks fowl, Gendry splits wood. Arya eats chicken leg and onion. Crying girl eats goose eagerly.
10. **Arya sharpens Needle** — Using Yoren's whetstone, remembering Syrio's teaching about dull blades. Hot Pie asks about her sword; she says her brother gave it to her.
11. **Arya hears wolf howling** — Wakes from sleep hearing a wolf howl (or dreaming it). Others dismiss it.
12. **Kurz sounds the hunting horn** — Confirms danger approaching; everyone scrambles for weapons.
13. **Lannister forces arrive and burn town** — Riders with torches set the town ablaze. Ser Amory Lorch demands the holdfast be opened in King Joffrey's name.
14. **Yoren refuses entry** — Displays Night's Watch black cloak and asserts neutrality. Ser Amory accuses them of being Lord Beric's men or outlaws.
15. **Woth killed by spear** — Spear thrown at Yoren hits Woth instead, going through his throat. This begins the assault.
16. **Battle at the holdfast walls** — Ser Amory orders "Storm the walls and kill them all." Attackers climb the rough walls. Arya fights on the parapet — slashes fingers off one climber, drives another back. Hot Pie fights beside her. Koss shoots four axemen at the gate. Dobber fights and is stabbed in the belly. Lommy kills a man with a rock. Qyle is killed by a knight with wasp shield and spiked mace.
17. **Yoren kills the knight in spiked helm** — Tangles his banner around the spike and stabs through armor with his dirk.
18. **Skinny man killed** — Arya, Gendry, and Hot Pie take down a bald attacker. Gendry's sword shatters on the man's helm; he takes the dead man's sword.
19. **Walls breached** — Enemy forces get over the wall or through the postern gate.
20. **Yoren orders retreat through tunnel** — Tells Arya to herd the boys and escape through the trap under the barn.
21. **Koss killed after yielding** — Throws down his blade to surrender; killed where he stands.
22. **Arya gathers survivors** — Finds Gendry, Hot Pie, Lommy (wounded), and the crying girl. Gerren too badly hurt to move.
23. **Barn catches fire** — Torch falls on straw; animals trapped inside screaming.
24. **Arya saves chained prisoners** — Returns to the burning barn, retrieves the axe from the woodpile (striking a mailed attacker between the legs), and throws the axe into the wagon. Rorge catches it and smashes through the wagon floor.
25. **Escape through tunnel** — Arya rolls into the tunnel. Barn collapses behind her. She crawls through the dark, cool underground passage, kissing the mud and crying.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Progresses from cautious hope (thinking knights at Harrenhal will escort her home) → unease in deserted town (gooseprickles, fear) → dejection at finding no boats → brief peace at the lake → terror and adrenaline during battle → grim determination (returning to burning barn for the axe) → exhaustion and grief (crying in the tunnel)
- **Primary preoccupation:** Survival; awareness that the adults' plan (Night's Watch neutrality) will not protect them; getting out alive
- **Key decisions made:** (1) Warns the group they shouldn't stay, is overruled; (2) Returns to the burning barn to throw the axe to the chained prisoners despite extreme danger; (3) Drags the crying girl despite others wanting to abandon her
- **Self-deception flags:** Arya tells herself Harrenhal will have knights who "keep you safe, especially women" — a naive belief she half-recognizes as belonging to her old identity. She lies to Hot Pie about her brothers teaching her to kill, crafting a tough persona. She tells Hot Pie swordfighting is "easy" but knows it's a lie.
