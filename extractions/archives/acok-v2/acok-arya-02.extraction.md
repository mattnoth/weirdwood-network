- **chapter_summary:** Arya travels north on the kingsroad with Yoren's party, observing masses of refugees heading south toward King's Landing. Praed dies in his sleep and is buried. The party stops at an ivy-covered inn where they eat and bathe. Arya overhears talk of war—Lannister and Tully fighting, Starks marching south, and a great wolf pack near the Gods Eye led by a she-wolf. After Yoren pushes her outside, Arya encounters the three chained prisoners: Jaqen H'ghar, Rorge, and Biter. She strikes Biter with her practice sword when he grabs for her. Six gold cloaks arrive with a warrant from the queen, but they want the Bull (Gendry), not Arya. Yoren and his men face them down, and the gold cloaks withdraw. Gendry reveals his true name.

## Events & Actions
1. **Northward travel on the kingsroad** — Yoren's party travels dawn to dusk, passing through villages and fields, while masses of refugees move south toward King's Landing with weapons and belongings.
2. **Roadside encounters** — A madwoman screams warnings at them; a merchant offers to buy their wagons at a quarter their worth, citing war; Yoren refuses both.
3. **Graves along the road** — Arya notices a child's grave with a crystal; Lommy wants to take it but the Bull warns him off. More graves appear daily.
4. **Praed's death** — One night Arya wakes feeling something is wrong, noting an unusual silence. Come morning, Praed has died in his sleep. They bury him and distribute his gear. Yoren gives his longsword to the Bull. Tarber tosses acorns on the grave.
5. **Arrival at the ivy-covered inn** — The party stops at a village inn for a hot meal. Yoren decides they can afford it. Some men go to the bathhouse; Arya does not dare bathe (maintaining her disguise).
6. **Inn common room conversation** — Travelers report war between Tully and Lannister, wild men from the Mountains of the Moon, and the young Stark lord riding south. A yellow-haired man named Hod claims the boy rides a wolf big as a horse.
7. **Wolf pack discussion** — A man in a green cloak reports wolf packs grown bold around the Gods Eye, led by a she-wolf. An old woman corroborates. The man tells of Lord Mooton's failed hunt. Arya blurts out that wolves don't eat babies, and Yoren pushes her outside.
8. **Arya meets the three prisoners** — Outside, Jaqen H'ghar speaks to Arya, introducing himself and his companions Rorge and Biter. Rorge curses and throws his tankard at her. Biter lunges for her but is stopped by his chains. Arya strikes Biter between the eyes with her practice sword.
9. **Arya and Gendry spar (briefly)** — The Bull pulls Arya away from the prisoners. She challenges him to a practice fight. They draw swords but are interrupted by the arrival of the gold cloaks.
10. **Gold cloaks arrive** — Six City Watch officers ride up, led by an officer with a warrant from the queen for "a certain boy." Yoren refuses to hand anyone over, citing Night's Watch law.
11. **Standoff at the inn** — Yoren's men rally: Tarber with a pitchfork, Cutjack with his hammer, Kurz with a skinning knife, Koss with a longbow, Reysen with his staff, Dobber with a dagger, Hot Pie with a rock. Arya herself emerges with Needle, declaring she's the one they want.
12. **Gold cloaks want Gendry, not Arya** — The officer reveals they want the Bull, not Arya. Yoren puts his sword to the officer's throat, forces him to drop his weapon, and sends the gold cloaks riding off.
13. **Aftermath** — Yoren gives the officer's shortsword to Hot Pie. He tells Gendry and Arya to ride the two coursers and flee for the Wall at the first sight of gold cloaks. Gendry reveals his true name. Yoren orders the party to ride all night.
