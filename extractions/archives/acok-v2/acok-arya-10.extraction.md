- **chapter_summary:** Arya serves as Roose Bolton's cupbearer at Harrenhal, where executed heads line the gatehouse and women who consorted with Lannisters are punished in stocks. She overhears Bolton's war council with Frey lords who argue the war is lost and Robb must bend the knee; during this she learns that Winterfell has fallen and Bran and Rickon are reported dead. Bolton orders Ser Helman Tallhart to burn Castle Darry and march on Duskendale, announces he will hunt wolves, and later reveals he plans to give Harrenhal to Vargo Hoat and leave Arya behind. That night, Arya steals Bolton's map and dagger, arranges horses from the stables, recruits Gendry and Hot Pie, kills the postern guard with the dagger, and escapes Harrenhal in the rain.

## Events & Actions
1. **Arya draws water at the well** — She passes the tarred heads on the gatehouse each morning; carrion crows and ravens attend them.
2. **Goodwife Amabel confronts Arya** — Amabel grabs Arya, threatens that when Lord Tywin returns, Arya will end up in the stocks like Pia. Arya swings the water bucket at her, drenching her, and threatens to kill her.
3. **Arya recalls conversation with Gendry about the heads** — Gendry blamed Arya and her weasel soup for the current brutality; Arya defended herself, saying it was Steelshanks Walton's and the Mummers' work.
4. **Gendry criticizes the Brave Companions** — He catalogs their vices: Septon Utt likes little boys, Qyburn does black magic, Biter eats people.
5. **Arya reflects on Jaqen H'ghar's iron coin** — She once threw it away in anger but retrieved it; she considers its writing and worn face.
6. **Arya helps Elmar Frey clean Bolton's chainmail** — They roll a barrel of sand across the Flowstone Yard; she points out remaining rust; Elmar tries to order her to finish but she refuses.
7. **Bolton's war council** — Arya enters the bedchamber where Bolton is being leeched. Ser Aenys Frey argues they cannot be besieged at Harrenhal. Multiple Freys argue the war is lost and Robb must bend the knee. Bolton listens and dismisses them noncommittally.
8. **Arya learns Winterfell has fallen and Bran and Rickon are reported dead** — Ser Hosteen says "He has lost Winterfell! His brothers are dead..." Arya is devastated but stands still and silent.
9. **Arya removes Bolton's leeches** — After the Freys leave, Bolton has Arya remove the leeches from his body.
10. **Qyburn reads Lady Walda's letter** — She writes from the Twins praying for Bolton's return and promising trueborn sons to replace Domeric.
11. **Bolton orders Tallhart to burn Castle Darry and march on Duskendale** — He dictates a letter to Qyburn ordering captives put to sword, castle burned, then join Robett Glover and strike east toward Duskendale for vengeance.
12. **Arya reflects on Castle Darry** — She is glad it will burn, remembering it as where her father was made to kill Sansa's wolf.
13. **Bolton announces he will hunt wolves** — Qyburn warns of wolf attacks on Septon Utt's men; Bolton notes the unusual boldness of southern wolves.
14. **Arya cleans Bolton's chambers** — She sweeps rushes, lays fires, changes linens, empties chamber pots, brings autumn pears, then cleans the solar. She discovers the sheepskin map of the Lands of the Trident and locates Harrenhal and Riverrun.
15. **Arya practices swordwork in the godswood** — She slashes at leaves with a broken broomstick while reciting her kill list: Ser Gregor, Dunsen, Polliver, Raff the Sweetling, The Tickler, The Hound, Ser Ilyn, Ser Meryn, Queen Cersei, Joffrey. She salutes the heart tree and says "Valar morghulis."
16. **Hunting party returns with nine dead wolves** — Seven adults and two pups; Bolton orders skins sewn into a blanket, pup fur made into gloves. He quotes "winter is coming."
17. **Bolton orders supper** — Barley bread, butter, boar, and hot spice wine; sups alone.
18. **Arya fetches supper from the kitchen** — Hot Pie is sullen toward her; a guard jokes about weasel soup.
19. **Bolton reads and burns a book** — He reads a thick leatherbound book by the hearth, then deliberately places it in the fire and watches it burn.
20. **Arya asks Bolton to take her when he leaves** — He refuses; says he will give Harrenhal to Vargo Hoat and she will remain. He threatens to have her tongue out if she questions him again.
21. **Arya passes the Wailing Tower and finds Elmar weeping** — Elmar is distraught that a bird from the Twins says his betrothal to a princess is broken; Lord Walder says he must marry someone else or be a septon. Arya tells him her brothers might be dead; he dismisses her. She says "I hope your princess dies."
22. **Arya prays at the heart tree** — She asks the gods what to do. She hears a wolf howl far off and seems to hear her father's voice: "the lone wolf dies, but the pack survives." She breaks the broomstick over her knee, declaring "I am a direwolf, and done with wooden teeth."
23. **Arya lies awake listening to wolves** — A great pack howling, closer than before, calling to her.
24. **Arya escapes Kingspyre through a cellar window** — She slips out past the guarded entrance via a narrow cellar window.
25. **Arya recruits Gendry at the forge** — She wakes him quietly, asks for a sword, tells him Bolton is giving Harrenhal to Vargo Hoat. She lies that Vargo will cut off servants' feet. She tells Gendry to get swords and wake Hot Pie for food.
26. **Arya steals Bolton's map and dagger** — She re-enters Kingspyre, dresses in Bolton livery, and takes the sheepskin map and dagger from Bolton's solar.
27. **Arya procures three horses from the stables** — She impersonates Bolton's authority to a groom, demanding three fast hunting horses saddled and bridled. The boy complies, intimidated by the flayed man sigil.
28. **Arya leads horses to the Tower of Ghosts** — She stays in shadow of the curtain wall; sees a grey and white cat on the godswood wall that hisses at her, reminding her of the Red Keep and Syrio Forel.
29. **Gendry and Hot Pie arrive at rendezvous** — Gendry in oiled chainmail with hammer and swords; Hot Pie with bread and cheese.
30. **Arya kills the postern guard** — She approaches openly showing Bolton's sigil, offers a "silver piece" (actually Jaqen's iron coin), drops it so he kneels, then cuts his throat with the dagger. She whispers "Valar morghulis."
31. **Escape from Harrenhal** — Arya lifts the bar and opens the postern gate; a wolf howls outside the walls; Hot Pie and Gendry bring the horses; they ride out into the rain.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Grief-stricken and desperate upon learning of Bran and Rickon's reported deaths and the fall of Winterfell. Fluctuates between fear, anger, sorrow, and fierce resolve. Feels increasingly isolated — Hot Pie seems afraid of her, Gendry blames her, Elmar dismisses her. By chapter's end, she has hardened into cold determination.
- **Primary preoccupation:** The news about Winterfell and her brothers dominates the chapter. Secondary concern is escape — she cannot stay if Bolton is leaving and giving Harrenhal to Vargo Hoat. She wonders: "If Winterfell is truly gone, is this my home now? Am I still Arya, or only Nan the serving girl, for forever and forever and forever?"
- **Key decisions made:** (1) Decides not to report Amabel to Bolton despite knowing it would mean Amabel's death. (2) Decides to escape Harrenhal rather than remain under Vargo Hoat. (3) Breaks the broomstick, resolving to get a real weapon. (4) Plans and executes the escape, including killing the guard. (5) Decides against revealing her identity to the northman guard — "He was a northman, but not a Winterfell man. He belonged to Roose Bolton."
- **Self-deception flags:** Arya lies to Gendry about Vargo Hoat planning to cut off servants' feet — she knows it's a lie and the text says "she lied." She also tells herself "It's not true, it can't be true, it's just some Lannister lie" about Bran and Rickon, clinging to denial. She wonders if Jaqen's coin "was probably a lie too, like his name and even his face" — dismissing its value despite keeping it.
