- **chapter_summary:** Catelyn meets Stannis and then Renly at a parley between the two Baratheon brothers outside Storm's End. Stannis demands Renly bend the knee, Renly refuses, and neither brother will yield. Stannis reveals his claim that Joffrey, Tommen, and Myrcella are products of Cersei's incest with Jaime, and draws his glowing sword Lightbringer. The parley fails, and Renly returns to camp to prepare for battle at dawn, assigning Ser Loras the vanguard and refusing to let Catelyn depart. Catelyn goes to pray at a nearby sept.

## Events & Actions
1. **Catelyn and Hal Mollen arrive at the parley ground** — They are the first to reach the grassy sward between the armies, near Storm's End.
2. **Stannis arrives with the red priestess** — Two riders emerge from his camp; he bears a new banner (fiery heart of the Lord of Light) rather than the traditional Baratheon stag.
3. **Catelyn and Stannis exchange cold courtesies** — Stannis expresses condolence for Ned's death; Catelyn reminds him that Ned broke the siege of Storm's End; Stannis says he should have been Robert's Hand.
4. **Stannis promises justice for Ned's murder** — Catelyn says she would sooner have her daughters back; mentions Sansa held by Cersei and no word of Arya.
5. **Catelyn presses Stannis on his strategy** — Stannis admits he needs the southern lords Renly commands and must take them from him.
6. **Catelyn advocates for Robb** — States Robb is King in the North, bends the knee to no man, but offers friendship.
7. **Renly arrives on his palfrey** — Splendidly dressed; Brienne carries his banner in full plate armor.
8. **The brothers exchange barbs** — Renly mocks Stannis's new banner; the red priestess identifies it as the fiery heart of the Lord of Light.
9. **Catelyn urges alliance against the Lannisters** — Describes the Lannister forces arrayed against them all.
10. **Stannis declares the Iron Throne his by rights** — Renly counters that no one wants Stannis for king.
11. **Stannis reveals his claim about the royal children** — Declares Joffrey, Tommen, and Myrcella are bastards born of Cersei and Jaime's incest.
12. **Renly dismisses the claim** — Says he was at Horn Hill when Lord Tarly received Stannis's letter; asks if Stannis can prove it.
13. **Stannis explains he brought his suspicions to Jon Arryn** — Says Robert would have dismissed the accusations as self-serving if they came from Stannis directly; Jon Arryn was poisoned before he could reveal the truth.
14. **Catelyn connects Lysa's letter** — Recalls Lysa accused the queen of killing Jon Arryn and later blamed Tyrion.
15. **Renly offers Stannis a peach** — Stannis refuses; Renly eats it, noting "Life is short, Stannis. Remember what the Starks say. Winter is coming."
16. **Renly offers Stannis Storm's End as a gift** — Stannis refuses, saying it is his by rights already.
17. **Stannis taunts Renly about his marriage** — References Margaery, implying Renly schemed to make her Robert's queen; mocks Renly's lack of consummation; Renly mocks Stannis's lack of sons and his wife's appearance.
18. **Stannis draws Lightbringer** — The sword gleams strangely bright with shifting colors; air shimmers around it.
19. **Brienne intervenes** — Steps between the brothers with blade drawn, shouting at Stannis to put up his steel.
20. **Stannis issues an ultimatum** — Offers Renly Storm's End, council seat, and heir designation if he submits before dawn; threatens destruction otherwise.
21. **Renly enumerates his forces** — Lists dozens of houses supporting him and claims a hundred thousand foot coming behind; dismisses Stannis's approximately five thousand men.
22. **Stannis departs** — Gallops away; the red priestess warns Renly to look to his own sins before following.
23. **Renly and Catelyn return to camp** — Renly comments the parley was amusing if not profitable; wonders about getting a sword like Stannis's; muses whether the incest claim is true.
24. **Catelyn reminds Renly that Stannis is the lawful heir if the incest claim is true** — Renly acknowledges it but argues ability should trump birth order.
25. **Renly announces battle to his lords** — In the royal pavilion; declares Stannis must have blood.
26. **Lord Rowan counsels against battle** — Argues Storm's End can hold and they should march on King's Landing instead.
27. **Lord Tarly argues for battle** — Says leaving Stannis unblooded will let him grow stronger while Renly weakens fighting the Lannisters.
28. **Renly decides on battle** — Lords chorus agreement with Tarly.
29. **Catelyn asks leave to return to Riverrun** — Renly refuses; orders her to stay and watch.
30. **Renly assigns battle positions** — Lord Mathis center, Bryce the left, Renly the right, Lord Estermont the reserve, Ser Loras the van.
31. **Ser Loras given the van** — Multiple lords compete for it; Renly awards it to Loras; Loras asks for a knight with the king's banner; Renly assigns Brienne.
32. **Brienne asks to arm Renly for battle** — Renly grants it; Catelyn notes Brienne loves him.
33. **Renly dismisses the assembly** — Asks Loras to stay and "help me pray."
34. **Catelyn departs at dusk with Ser Robar Royce** — She learns he is Bronze Yohn's second son; they discuss why each is there.
35. **Catelyn arrives at her camp** — Shadd slices carrots, Mollen dices, Lucas Blackwood sharpens his dagger and asks if they fight or flee. Catelyn says they pray.
