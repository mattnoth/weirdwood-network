Qhorin reports to Lord Commander Mormont that Mance Rayder is gathering an enormous host and searching for some power in the Frostfangs to breach the Wall. Mormont agrees to send three scouting parties of five into the mountains. Qhorin chooses Jon Snow to accompany him, and they plan to ride at noon.

## Events & Actions
1. **Horn blast sounds** — A single long low horn blast wakes the Night's Watch camp in the night, signaling brothers returning.
2. **Camp stirs and prepares** — Men roll from blankets, reach for weapons, and wait tensely for a possible second blast. When none comes, they relax.
3. **Mormont emerges** — The Lord Commander comes out of his tent with his raven, confirms one blast, and orders hot food prepared and Qhorin brought to him.
4. **Jon recalls the debate** — Jon remembers the officers' argument about what to do: Ser Ottyn wanted retreat, Ser Mallador wanted to find Qhorin's trail, Thoren Smallwood wanted to attack Mance up the Milkwater. No decision was reached.
5. **Jon rouses Edd and Hake** — Dolorous Edd is found at the fire complaining; Hake is woken and sets brothers to cutting roots for soup.
6. **Sam approaches Jon** — Sam asks if Benjen has returned; Jon tells him it is only the Shadow Tower men.
7. **Shadow Tower rangers arrive** — Qhorin's men come up the slope, many wounded, some riding two to a horse.
8. **Jon meets Qhorin Halfhand** — Jon recognizes the legendary ranger and hails him; Qhorin notes Jon has his father's look and asks about Ghost.
9. **Jon recalls Chett's grumbling** — Jon remembers overhearing Chett, Lark the Sisterman, and others the previous night expressing discontent and hinting at possible insubordination; Jon chose not to report them.
10. **Jon examines his dragonglass dagger** — Jon studies the obsidian blade by firelight; reflects on the cache found at the Fist, the items he distributed (daggers to Grenn and Mormont, warhorn to Sam, arrowheads and spearhead to Sam and others).
11. **Jon serves food to Mormont and Qhorin** — Edd prepares bacon, eggs, and oat bread; Jon brings it into the tent.
12. **Qhorin reports on Alfyn Crowkiller** — Qhorin tells Mormont they killed Alfyn but some of his company escaped; four brothers died, a dozen wounded; they took captives and questioned one.
13. **Qhorin reports Mance's strength** — The captive claimed Mance has gathered all the wildling chiefs, wargs, mammoths, and more strength than expected; Ebben doubts the captive's claims.
14. **Discussion of Wall defense** — Mormont and Qhorin discuss garrisoning abandoned castles (Greyguard collapsed, Stonedoor better, Icemark, Deep Lake, Long Barrow) and daily patrols along the battlements.
15. **Qhorin reveals Mance plans to breach the Wall** — Qhorin states wildlings plan to break the Wall through sorcery, not climb or tunnel; Mance is seeking some power in the Frostfangs.
16. **Scouting parties decided** — Qhorin proposes three parties of five: one to the Milkwater, one to the Skirling Pass, one to the Giant's Stair, commanded by Buckwell, Smallwood, and himself.
17. **Mormont reluctantly agrees** — Mormont concedes there is no other choice; acknowledges the camp's defensive role in delaying Mance if the scouts don't return.
18. **Qhorin chooses Jon** — Qhorin selects Jon Snow for his scouting party; Mormont objects that Jon is young and his steward; Qhorin cites the old gods and the Starks; Jon volunteers to go.
19. **Dawn breaks** — Jon and Qhorin exit the tent at dawn; Qhorin tells Jon to find Ghost; they ride at noon.
