- **chapter_summary:** Catelyn waits in the Whispering Wood as Robb's forces prepare an ambush for Jaime Lannister's cavalry. She reflects on the men she has waited for throughout her life — her father, Brandon Stark, and Ned. Robb rides the line to hearten his men, and the trap is sprung when Jaime's column enters the valley. The Stark-Tully forces win a decisive victory, capturing Jaime Lannister and killing several of Robb's battle guard including Torrhen and Eddard Karstark and Daryn Hornwood. Catelyn reminds Robb that the war is not over while Tywin remains free and Riverrun remains besieged.

## Events & Actions
1. **Pre-battle waiting** — Catelyn waits on the wooded ridge with Hallis Mollen and thirty guards while Robb's forces prepare the ambush in the Whispering Wood.
2. **Catelyn's reflections** — She recalls waiting for her father Lord Hoster at Riverrun, for Brandon Stark who never returned, and for Ned who left shortly after their wedding.
3. **Brynden Tully's scouting report (recalled)** — Ser Brynden reported that Jaime does not know of their approach; his archers killed Lannister outriders. Jaime has 12,000 foot in three camps around Riverrun and 2,000-3,000 horse.
4. **Lord Jason Mallister joins the host** — He brought his forces from Seagard as Robb's army swept south around the Blue Fork headwaters. Other scattered fighters also joined.
5. **Robb rides the line** — Robb, armored with visor down, rides along the line to hearten his men before battle, quoting his father's advice.
6. **Snow shrike calls signal the enemy approach** — The bird calls, used as signals, indicate Jaime's column is entering the valley.
7. **Jaime's column enters the trap** — Jaime rides at the head of three quarters of the Lannister horse through the valley; he is unmistakable in silver armor with gold hair and crimson cloak, no helm.
8. **The trap is sprung** — Maege Mormont's warhorn signals the last rider has entered. Grey Wind howls. The Greatjon, Mallisters, Freys, and Karstarks sound their horns. Bowmen in the trees loose arrows. Robb shouts "Winterfell!" and leads the charge downhill.
9. **The Battle of the Whispering Wood** — A pitched battle in the valley; Catelyn hears but cannot see most of it. Battle cries of "Lannister," "Winterfell," and "Tully! Riverrun and Tully!" Grey Wind fights and kills.
10. **Jaime's desperate charge toward Robb** — When Jaime saw he was lost, he rallied retainers and fought up the valley to reach Robb. He killed Torrhen Karstark (cut off his hand), Eddard Karstark (sword lodged in neck), and Daryn Hornwood (skull split), shouting for Robb the entire time.
11. **Jaime's capture** — Theon Greyjoy and the Greatjon drag Jaime before Catelyn. He offers quips; Catelyn demands her father, brother, daughters, and husband. Jaime says he has "mislaid" them.
12. **Theon urges execution; Robb refuses** — Theon wants Jaime killed. Robb says Jaime is more useful alive and that his father never condoned killing prisoners after battle. Catelyn orders Jaime put in irons.
13. **Casualties reported** — Torrhen Karstark, Eddard Karstark, and Daryn Hornwood killed. Theon claims ten Lannister dead for every one of theirs. Close to a hundred knights and a dozen lords bannermen captured.
14. **Catelyn's counsel** — She tells Robb to grieve later; they have won a battle, not the war. Tywin is still free and Riverrun still besieged. Robb agrees.
