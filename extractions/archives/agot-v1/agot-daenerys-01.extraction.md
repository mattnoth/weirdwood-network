# AGOT — Daenerys I

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** AGOT
- **chapter_number:** 4
- **pov_character:** Daenerys Targaryen
- **pov_chapter_number:** Daenerys I
- **first_available:** AGOT Daenerys I
- **location_primary:** Magister Illyrio's manse, Pentos
- **location_secondary:** Khal Drogo's manse (bay-side, Pentos); the bay of Pentos; Illyrio's estate entry hall; pillared courtyard of Drogo's manse
- **approximate_timeline:** Evening of the feast arranged by Illyrio; Daenerys is thirteen years old; the Targaryens have lived in Illyrio's house for "nigh on half a year"
- **chapter_summary:** Viserys presents Daenerys with a new gown gifted by Magister Illyrio and prepares her for a feast at Khal Drogo's manse, where Drogo is expected to take her as a bride in exchange for lending Viserys his army. Daenerys is bathed and dressed by Illyrio's slaves, then escorted in Illyrio's palanquin to Drogo's manse. At the feast, Daenerys and Viserys observe the guests, including Ser Jorah Mormont identified as a knight exiled for selling poachers to slavers. Daenerys first sees Khal Drogo in person and is frightened by him. She pleads with Viserys not to make her marry Drogo; Viserys refuses and threatens her, stating he would give her to Drogo's entire khalasar if it secured him his army.

## Events & Actions
1. **Illyrio presents Daenerys's gown** — Viserys shows Daenerys a deep plum silk gown gifted by Magister Illyrio and instructs her to dress to impress Khal Drogo at the feast.
2. **Viserys threatens and gropes Daenerys** — While arranging her posture, Viserys pinches Daenerys's nipple cruelly and threatens her not to fail him, invoking "waking the dragon."
3. **Daenerys looks out over Pentos bay** — She privately wishes she could escape the feast; she reflects on Westeros as described by Viserys versus her own lack of memory of it.
4. **Daenerys recalls the Targaryen flight narrative** — She recounts internally (from Viserys's stories) the midnight flight to Dragonstone, Rhaegar's death at the Trident, the sack of King's Landing, Elia's murder, Aerys's killing, her own birth on Dragonstone in a storm, and their escape with Ser Willem Darry to Braavos.
5. **Illyrio's slaves bathe and dress Daenerys** — Two female slaves prepare Daenerys for the feast; during this the slave girl describes Drogo's wealth and qualities; Daenerys reflects that she had assumed she would marry Viserys.
6. **Viserys and Illyrio inspect Daenerys** — In the entry hall, Illyrio praises her appearance; Viserys expresses doubts about Drogo's taste but is reassured.
7. **Party travels to Drogo's manse by palanquin** — Illyrio, Viserys, and Daenerys are carried through Pentos; Viserys talks of his reconquest plans.
8. **Viserys outlines his plan** — He tells Illyrio he needs ten thousand Dothraki to retake Westeros; he names Tyrell, Redwyne, Darry, and Greyjoy as expected supporters and cites Dornish desire for revenge.
9. **Illyrio reassures Viserys** — He tells Viserys that men toast him secretly and women sew dragon banners for his return; Dany notices a faint smile suggesting he does not fully believe this.
10. **Viserys vows to kill Robert and Jaime Lannister** — He states his intention to personally kill both.
11. **Arrival at Drogo's manse** — The party is stopped at the gate by an Unsullied guardsman; Viserys is annoyed; Illyrio explains the security is for the guests' protection.
12. **Daenerys and party are announced** — A eunuch sings their full titles into the courtyard.
13. **Illyrio identifies notable guests** — He points out Drogo's bloodriders, Khal Moro and his son Rhogoro, the Archon of Tyrosh's brother, and Ser Jorah Mormont.
14. **Jorah Mormont is identified and discussed** — Illyrio explains Jorah was wanted by Robert for selling poachers to a Tyroshi slaver instead of sending them to the Night's Watch; Viserys says he wishes to speak with Jorah.
15. **Daenerys first sees Khal Drogo** — She observes him from a distance: very tall, copper-skinned, with a very long braided hair hung with bells, approximately thirty years old.
16. **Illyrio goes to fetch Drogo** — He tells Daenerys and Viserys to wait while he escorts Drogo to meet them.
17. **Viserys explains braid significance** — He tells Daenerys that Dothraki cut their braids in defeat; Drogo's long braid means he has never been defeated; Viserys calls him "Aegon the Dragonlord come again."
18. **Daenerys begs not to marry Drogo** — She says she is frightened and wants to go home; Viserys rebuffs her.
19. **Viserys threatens Daenerys with the khalasar** — He tells her he would let all forty thousand of Drogo's men and their horses have her if it secured his army; he instructs her to smile.
20. **Illyrio brings Drogo over** — Daenerys composes herself as Illyrio escorts Drogo toward them; the chapter ends.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Frightened, powerless, and compliant on the surface; internally questioning, mistrustful of Illyrio, and longing for safety and a childhood she never had
- **Primary preoccupation:** Fear of Khal Drogo and the marriage; desire for the simple life she associates with "the big house with the red door" rather than Viserys's grand ambitions
- **Key decisions made:** None — Daenerys makes no autonomous decisions; she complies with all instructions out of fear
- **Self-deception flags:** None clearly present; Daenerys appears clear-eyed about her situation, including recognizing that Viserys has never killed anyone and that Illyrio's words to Viserys may be false
