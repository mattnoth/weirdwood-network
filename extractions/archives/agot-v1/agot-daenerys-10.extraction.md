- **chapter_summary:** Daenerys prepares Khal Drogo's funeral pyre, building a platform of logs, his treasures, and cushions. She claims the title of queen, frees the remaining slaves in her khalasar, and names Jhogo, Aggo, and Rakharo as her kos, while naming Ser Jorah first of her Queensguard. She binds Mirri Maz Duur to the pyre as a blood sacrifice, places her three dragon eggs among the fuel, and lights the fire. She walks into the flames unharmed and emerges with three living dragons — the first born in hundreds of years. The Dothraki pledge themselves to her.

## Events & Actions
1. **Construction of Khal Drogo's funeral pyre** — Dany's people build a three-level platform of logs over a slain stallion's carcass, layered east-to-west and north-to-south, filled with brush, grass, and oil
2. **Mirri Maz Duur's warning** — The bound maegi tells Dany the blood magic will not work without proper words and wisdom; Dany has Jhogo whip her into silence
3. **Dany claims queenship** — She corrects Ser Jorah from "princess" to "queen," declaring herself heir to Viserys and the last blood of House Targaryen
4. **Ser Jorah pledges loyalty** — He kneels, calls her queen, offers his sword and heart, and begs her not to burn herself on the pyre
5. **Dany frees the remaining slaves** — She addresses the khalasar (fewer than a hundred), removes slave collars, and offers them freedom or a place as equals
6. **Naming of kos** — Dany names Jhogo, Aggo, and Rakharo as her kos, giving each a bride gift weapon; all three resist or limit their oaths (a woman cannot name kos per Dothraki custom)
7. **Ser Jorah named first of the Queensguard** — Dany promises him a Valyrian steel sword and kisses him; he vows to serve, obey, and die for her
8. **Dany bathes and prepares Drogo's body** — She bathes in scalding water, then washes, braids, and dresses Drogo's body for his final ride
9. **Dragon eggs placed on the pyre** — Black egg beside Drogo's heart, green beside his head with his braid coiled around it, cream-and-gold between his legs
10. **Mirri Maz Duur bound to the pyre** — At Dany's command, Jorah and Rakharo stake the maegi to the pyre; Dany pours oil over her head and thanks her for "the lessons"
11. **The red comet appears** — Jhogo spots the first star: a bloodred comet, which Dany takes as a sign
12. **Lighting of the pyre** — Dany takes a torch from Aggo and lights the pyre; Mirri sings then screams as the fire consumes her
13. **Dany walks into the fire** — She approaches the conflagration, her clothes burning away; she sees visions in the flames including a smoky stallion; she steps into the firestorm
14. **Three cracks / dragon hatching** — Three successive cracks sound as the dragon eggs shatter; the pyre collapses around Dany
15. **Dany emerges unburned with three dragons** — Ser Jorah finds her naked, unhurt, with soot but no burns, nursing two dragons and with the third on her shoulders
16. **The Dothraki pledge to Dany** — Jhogo, Aggo, and Rakharo each say "Blood of my blood," followed by handmaids and the entire khalasar kneeling
17. **Dragons cry out together** — For the first time in hundreds of years, living dragons unfurl translucent wings and call into the night
