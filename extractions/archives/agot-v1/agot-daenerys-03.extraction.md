# AGOT — Daenerys III

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** AGOT
- **chapter_number:** 24
- **pov_character:** Daenerys Targaryen
- **pov_chapter_number:** Daenerys III
- **first_available:** AGOT Daenerys III
- **location_primary:** The Dothraki Sea (open plains east of Norvos / Forest of Qohor)
- **location_secondary:** King's Landing (Dany's imagination), Dragonstone (Dany's imagination), Pentos (referenced), Asshai / Shadow Lands (referenced), Jade Sea / Lys / Qarth (referenced)
- **approximate_timeline:** Some weeks after the wedding at Pentos; the khalasar is riding east toward Vaes Dothrak and has crossed Norvos, several rivers, the Forest of Qohor, and is now crossing the open Dothraki Sea. The chapter ends when the khalasar has crossed to the far side of the Dothraki Sea, at which point Dany discovers she is pregnant.
- **chapter_summary:** Daenerys surveys the Dothraki Sea from a ridge with Ser Jorah, then rides alone onto the plain to escape her traveling companions. Viserys pursues her and physically assaults her; her khas defender Jhogo whips Viserys and offers to kill him. Dany commands that Viserys be spared but stripped of his horse and made to walk back to the khalasar. She and Ser Jorah discuss Viserys's unfitness to rule, and Dany states aloud for the first time that he will never retake the Seven Kingdoms. That evening Dany's handmaid Doreah recounts a Qartheen legend about dragons and the moon, and Dany later seduces Drogo on her own terms. The chapter closes with Dany discovering she is pregnant on her fourteenth name day.

## Events & Actions
1. **Ridge survey** — Dany and Ser Jorah ride ahead of the group and survey the Dothraki Sea from a ridge; Jorah describes the grass types and the Dothraki ghost grass legend.
2. **Dany orders the party to halt** — Dany commands everyone to stay on the ridge while she rides alone; Jorah notes she is learning to speak like a queen; she corrects him: like a khaleesi.
3. **Dany's retrospective: the journey so far** — Narrator recounts weeks of brutal riding, saddle sores, marital rape at night; Dany reached a breaking point and considered suicide. That night she had the dragon dream (black dragon, scales wet with blood, flame that cleansed rather than burned). She woke changed.
4. **Dany touches the black egg** — The morning after the dragon dream, standing over the dragon eggs, she touches the black-and-scarlet egg; feels warmth; pulls away nervously.
5. **Dany adapts to Dothraki life** — Her body hardens; she learns to ride from Irri and especially from her silver; she begins to find pleasure in riding and eventually in nights with Drogo.
6. **Viserys confronts Dany on the plain** — He rides down from the ridge in a rage, shakes her, grabs her breast, screams that she cannot command him.
7. **Dany shoves Viserys** — First physical act of defiance against her brother.
8. **Jhogo whips Viserys** — Coils the whip around Viserys's throat; drags him to the ground; offers to kill him on Dany's word.
9. **Quaro suggests taking Viserys's ear** — Irri translates; Dany declines.
10. **Dany orders Viserys's horse taken** — Commands that Viserys walk back to the khalasar; Ser Jorah complies.
11. **Viserys refuses to move; is left behind** — Sits in the dirt; does not follow immediately.
12. **Dany and Jorah discuss Viserys's unfitness** — Jorah calls Rhaegar "the last dragon"; dismisses Viserys as less than a snake's shadow; both acknowledge Viserys could never lead.
13. **Dany states Viserys will never take the Seven Kingdoms** — Says it aloud for the first time; recognizes she has known this all her life.
14. **Both express longing for "home"** — Jorah says he prays for home; Dany says she does too, but envisions King's Landing and Dragonstone burning with lights.
15. **Camp established; Dany sees dragon eggs in light** — A finger of dusty red light touches the eggs; Dany sees scarlet flame for an instant; rationalizes the warmth as sunlight.
16. **Doreah recounts the moon-egg legend** — A Qartheen trader told her the moon was an egg; one of two moons cracked near the sun and released dragons; the remaining moon will one day do the same.
17. **Dany bathes; keeps Doreah for conversation** — Sends other handmaids away; dines with Doreah; talks long into the night.
18. **Dany seduces Drogo on her own terms** — Takes him outside under the open sky (per Dothraki custom); insists on facing him; rides him; Drogo calls out her name at the moment of his pleasure.
19. **Dany's pregnancy confirmed** — On the far side of the Dothraki Sea, Jhiqui notices the swell of Dany's stomach; Dany says she already knows. It is her fourteenth name day.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Expansive and peaceful at chapter's open; exhilarated by riding alone; newly assertive after standing up to Viserys; thoughtful and questioning during the conversation with Jorah; quietly transformed by the end.
- **Primary preoccupation:** Her emerging identity as khaleesi separate from her role as Viserys's instrument; whether "home" is Westeros or the Dothraki world she now inhabits; the dragon eggs' warmth (which she rationalizes away).
- **Key decisions made:** Commands the party to halt and rides alone; shoves Viserys; orders Viserys's horse taken and that he walk; keeps Doreah to talk; initiates intimacy with Drogo on her own terms and under the open sky.
- **Self-deception flags:** Dany rationalizes the warmth of the black dragon egg as sun-warmed stone, explicitly telling herself "Stone. They are only stone." She says "I know" when Jhiqui tells her she is pregnant, suggesting awareness she had been suppressing.
