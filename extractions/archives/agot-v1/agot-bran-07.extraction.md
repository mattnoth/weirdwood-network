## Chapter Metadata

- **location_secondary:** Winterfell crypts, Maester Luwin's turret
- **approximate_timeline:** Late in AGOT; Eddard is a captive in King's Landing, Robb has marched south with the armies
- **chapter_summary:** Bran watches raw recruits training in Winterfell's yard under Ser Rodrik, lamenting his inability to fight. He tells Maester Luwin about recurring dreams of a three-eyed crow, including one where the crow led him to the crypts to find his father, who was sad. Osha carries Bran into the crypts, where they discover Rickon and Shaggydog hiding near Eddard's empty tomb — Rickon has had the same dream. Luwin lectures on the children of the forest and gives Bran obsidian arrowheads. A raven arrives bearing news that Eddard is dead.

## Events & Actions
1. **Bran observes recruit training** — Bran watches raw recruits (mostly teenagers, one past twenty) being drilled by Ser Rodrik in the yard. They fight poorly.
2. **Bran proposes fighting from Hodor's back** — Bran suggests using a poleaxe while Hodor carries him, so they could "be a knight together." Luwin dismisses this.
3. **Bran reports his crow dream** — Bran tells Luwin about dreaming of the three-eyed crow again. The crow flew into his bedchamber and led him to the crypts, where he found his father, who was sad about something involving Jon.
4. **Hodor refuses the crypts** — Bran tried to have Hodor carry him to the crypts after the dream, but Hodor refused to descend past the top step, saying only "Hodor."
5. **Descent into the crypts** — Osha carries Bran into the crypts with Luwin leading with a torch. Summer follows but stops at the bottom of the stairs.
6. **Bran recites Stark history** — Bran identifies the statues of historical Stark kings and lords to Osha as they walk through the crypts.
7. **Bran recounts Lyanna's story** — Bran tells Osha that Robert was betrothed to Lyanna, Rhaegar carried her off and raped her, Robert killed Rhaegar, and Lyanna died.
8. **Shaggydog attacks Luwin** — When Luwin reaches into Eddard's empty tomb, Shaggydog leaps out and attacks him, biting his arm. This is the third time Shaggydog has savaged someone.
9. **Summer fights Shaggydog** — Summer charges Shaggydog and the two direwolves fight, allowing Luwin to pull free.
10. **Rickon discovered in the crypts** — Rickon is found standing in the mouth of Eddard's tomb. He reveals he had the same dream as Bran — seeing their father in the crypts.
11. **Return to Luwin's turret** — Bran insists they all go to Luwin's turret. Osha supports Bran's authority as "the lordling here."
12. **Luwin lectures on the children of the forest** — Luwin gives a lengthy history of the children of the forest, the First Men, the Pact, and the Andal invasion. Osha insists the children still live beyond the Wall.
13. **Luwin gives obsidian arrowheads** — Luwin shows obsidian arrowheads from a green jar; Bran and Rickon each take some.
14. **Direwolves howl** — Summer and Shaggydog begin howling simultaneously; Bran feels dread and certainty that something terrible has happened, connecting it to his crow dream.
15. **Raven arrives with news of Eddard's death** — A blood-spattered raven arrives. Luwin reads the message and tells the boys they will need to find a stonecarver who knew Eddard's likeness — confirming Eddard is dead.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Resentful of his disability early on; stubborn and determined about his dreams; growing dread as the chapter progresses; certainty of despair when the wolves howl; grief at the end
- **Primary preoccupation:** The three-eyed crow dream and his father's appearance in the crypts; secondary preoccupation with wanting to fight despite his paralysis
- **Key decisions made:** Insists on going to the crypts to verify his dream; asserts authority as "lordling" to bring Rickon and the wolves to Luwin's turret rather than chain Shaggydog
- **Self-deception flags:** Bran "had known it since last night … but he had not believed. He had wanted Maester Luwin to be right" — acknowledges he knew the dream was prophetic but chose denial
