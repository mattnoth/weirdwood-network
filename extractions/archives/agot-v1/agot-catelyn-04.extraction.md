- **chapter_summary:** Catelyn and Ser Rodrik Cassel arrive at King's Landing aboard the Tyroshi trading galley Storm Dancer after a rough voyage from White Harbor. They take a room at an inn on Eel Alley to avoid detection, and Ser Rodrik goes ahead to the Red Keep to consult master-at-arms Ser Aron Santagar about the assassin's dagger. While Catelyn sleeps, City Watchmen arrive and escort her to Petyr Baelish (Littlefinger), who reveals she was found through Varys's spy network. Varys and Littlefinger then examine the Valyrian steel dagger, and Littlefinger identifies it as a knife he formerly owned — which he lost to Tyrion Lannister in a bet at the tourney held on Prince Joffrey's name day, when Ser Loras Tyrell unhorsed Ser Jaime Lannister in the jousting.

## Events & Actions
1. **Arrival at King's Landing** — The Storm Dancer docks at King's Landing after a voyage from White Harbor; Catelyn pays each oarman one silver stag as promised.
2. **Catelyn and Ser Rodrik take lodging** — They take rooms at an inn on Eel Alley, partway up Visenya's Hill, chosen for its anonymity.
3. **Ser Rodrik departs for the Red Keep** — He goes to consult Ser Aron Santagar in the armory about the Valyrian steel dagger.
4. **Catelyn sleeps** — She rests while Ser Rodrik is gone, falling asleep until sunset.
5. **City Watch arrives** — Watchmen in black ringmail and golden cloaks knock and present a ribbon with Littlefinger's mockingbird seal; they escort Catelyn to the Red Keep.
6. **Catelyn meets Littlefinger** — Petyr Baelish meets her alone first in a tower room before Varys arrives; they exchange guarded words.
7. **Varys enters** — Varys joins them and reveals he knows about Ser Rodrik's visit to the Red Keep and his meeting with Ser Aron Santagar; confirms Ser Rodrik is safe and drinking at the inn.
8. **Catelyn produces the dagger** — She throws it on the table; Varys cuts his thumb testing the edge.
9. **Littlefinger identifies the dagger** — He declares it was his knife; he lost it to Tyrion Lannister in a wager at the tourney held on Prince Joffrey's name day, after Loras Tyrell unhorsed Jaime Lannister and Littlefinger backed the wrong man.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Guarded, weary from the long voyage, anxious about exposure and trust; momentarily panicked when the City Watch arrives; cold and controlled in the meeting with Littlefinger and Varys; shocked at the revelation that Tyrion Lannister owned the dagger
- **Primary preoccupation:** Identifying who sent the assassin after Bran and who owns the dagger, while avoiding detection at court
- **Key decisions made:** Chooses not to go to the castle; pays the oarmen directly rather than through Moreo; decides not to share the real reason for her visit with Littlefinger or Varys; goes with the City Watch rather than resisting
- **Self-deception flags:** Catelyn trusts Littlefinger "only a little" and Varys "not at all" — yet she shows both men the dagger and learns the revelation in their company, with limited ability to control how they will use this information
