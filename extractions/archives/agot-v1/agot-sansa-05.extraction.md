# AGOT — Sansa V

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** AGOT
- **chapter_number:** 58
- **pov_character:** Sansa Stark
- **pov_chapter_number:** Sansa V
- **first_available:** AGOT Sansa V
- **location_primary:** Throne room, Red Keep, King's Landing
- **location_secondary:** Red Keep (castle grounds, yard, Myrcella's garden, sept, godswood — referenced as places Sansa has access to)
- **approximate_timeline:** Early in Joffrey's reign; first court session after his ascension; Eddard Stark is imprisoned; Robert Baratheon recently dead
- **chapter_summary:** Sansa attends the first court session of King Joffrey's reign. Grand Maester Pycelle reads decrees summoning numerous lords and ladies to swear fealty or be declared traitors — including Sansa's own family. Tywin Lannister is named Hand of the King, Cersei is seated on the small council, and Janos Slynt is raised to lordship of Harrenhal. Ser Barristan Selmy is dismissed from the Kingsguard; he strips off his armor, flings his sword at the throne, and departs with defiant words. Sandor Clegane is named to the Kingsguard in his place. Sansa steps forward to plead for mercy for her imprisoned father. Joffrey agrees — on the condition that Eddard confess and acknowledge Joffrey as king.

## Events & Actions
1. **Sansa attends first court of Joffrey's reign** — She enters the throne room and finds only about twenty lords present; courtiers avoid her.
2. **Pycelle reads the fealty decree** — A long list of lords and ladies, including Stannis, Renly, Starks, Tullys, Arryns, Tyrells, Martells, and many others are commanded to swear fealty or be declared traitors.
3. **Tywin Lannister named Hand of the King** — Replacing "the traitor Eddard Stark."
4. **Cersei Lannister seated on the small council** — Named Queen Regent, to help Joffrey "rule wisely and with justice."
5. **Janos Slynt raised to lord of Harrenhal** — Given the ancient seat with all lands and incomes, plus a seat on the small council. He enters displaying his new sigil.
6. **Ser Barristan Selmy dismissed from the Kingsguard** — Cersei announces his service is at an end. Jaime Lannister is named his replacement as Lord Commander.
7. **Barristan's defiant departure** — He refuses the offer of land near Lannisport, strips his cloak, helm, and breastplate, draws his sword (alarming the hall), flings it at the throne, declares Stannis may take Joffrey's throne, and departs.
8. **Joffrey orders Barristan seized** — Janos Slynt volunteers his gold cloaks to carry it out.
9. **Sandor Clegane named to the Kingsguard** — Accepts but refuses to take knight's vows, breaking precedent.
10. **Sansa pleads for mercy for Eddard Stark** — She approaches the throne and publicly asks Joffrey to spare her father.
11. **Joffrey conditionally grants mercy** — He agrees to show mercy if Eddard confesses his crimes and acknowledges Joffrey as king.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Nervous, frightened, but forcing herself to be brave; hopeful and naively trusting of Joffrey's love; heart goes out to Barristan; elation when Joffrey agrees to mercy
- **Primary preoccupation:** Pleading for her father's life; navigating the hostility and indifference of the court
- **Key decisions made:** Decides to step forward and publicly petition Joffrey for Eddard's life despite her fear
- **Self-deception flags:** Repeatedly tells herself Joffrey loves her and the queen does too; believes "it will all come out well"; interprets Joffrey's smile as proof of love; convinces herself Arya must be safe at Winterfell; does not question whether Joffrey's conditional mercy is genuine
