## Events & Actions
1. **Ned dreams of the Tower of Joy** — He relives the confrontation between his seven companions and three Kingsguard knights (Ser Arthur Dayne, Ser Oswell Whent, Ser Gerold Hightower) before the round tower in Dorne.
2. **Dream dialogue about loyalty and duty** — The Kingsguard explain they were not at the Trident, King's Landing, Storm's End, or Dragonstone because "the Kingsguard does not flee" and they swore a vow.
3. **The fight begins in dream** — Ser Arthur unsheathes Dawn, says "And now it begins." Ned replies "Now it ends." Steel clashes as Lyanna screams.
4. **Ned wakes** — Lyanna's voice merges with Vayon Poole's. Ned finds himself in the Tower of the Hand, leg splinted and plastered, having been unconscious six days and seven nights.
5. **Ned whispers his promise** — Before fully waking, he whispers "Lya, I promise" in response to the dream.
6. **Vayon Poole gives Ned water** — Maester Pycelle had predicted Ned would be thirsty.
7. **Poole informs Ned the king wants to see him** — Robert left orders to be notified the moment Ned woke. Ned wants to wait until morning but Poole says the king commanded immediate notification.
8. **Ned summons Alyn** — He nearly says "Jory" before remembering Jory is dead; summons the captain of his guard instead.
9. **Alyn reports** — Jaime has fled to Casterly Rock; the story of Catelyn seizing Tyrion is widely known; extra guards have been posted; Sansa prays at Ned's side while Arya is silent and furious.
10. **Ned orders his daughters kept safe** — Says "this is only the beginning."
11. **Alyn reports on the dead** — Jory and the others given to the silent sisters to be sent to Winterfell; Jory would want to lie beside his grandfather.
12. **Ned reflects on the Tower of Joy** — Recalls that Martyn Cassel (Jory's father) died there; Ned pulled down the tower and built eight cairns; Rhaegar had named it the Tower of Joy; only Ned and Howland Reed survived.
13. **Robert and Cersei arrive** — Robert is dressed formally, wine in hand; Cersei wears a jeweled tiara.
14. **Ned claims responsibility for Catelyn's actions** — Says she acted at his command.
15. **Cersei demands to know by what right Ned seizes Lannister blood** — Ned answers he is the Hand of the King.
16. **Robert demands Ned make peace** — Counts seven (then corrected to eight by Cersei, with Tregar's death) dead; demands Ned order Catelyn to release Tyrion and make peace with Jaime.
17. **Ned refuses** — Says three of his men were butchered before his eyes by Jaime.
18. **Cersei claims Ned was drunk from a brothel and his men attacked first** — Ned tells Robert to ask Lord Baelish.
19. **Robert reports Littlefinger's account** — Littlefinger says he left to fetch gold cloaks before fighting; admits Ned was returning from a whorehouse.
20. **Ned reveals he went to see Robert's daughter Barra** — The mother named her Barra; she looks like Robert's first bastard from the Vale. Cersei's face is a mask, betraying nothing.
21. **Robert is embarrassed** — Dismisses the subject as unfit for the queen's ears.
22. **Ned requests leave to bring Jaime to justice** — Robert refuses, declares it ended: Jaime killed three, Ned killed five.
23. **Cersei goads Robert** — Says no one would dare speak to a Targaryen this way; asks if Robert is less than them.
24. **Robert strikes Cersei** — A vicious backhand blow; she stumbles against the table and falls. Her cheek reddens, will bruise by morning.
25. **Cersei declares she will wear the bruise as a badge of honor** — Robert threatens to "honor" her again.
26. **Ser Meryn Trant escorts Cersei out** — Robert calls for a guard.
27. **Robert expresses remorse and confusion** — Says he should not have hit her, it was not kingly. Reflects on his strength and inability to fight someone he cannot hit.
28. **Robert speaks of Rhaegar** — Says Rhaegar won despite dying; "he has Lyanna now, and I have her" (meaning Cersei).
29. **Ned tries to discuss matters** — Robert refuses, says he is going hunting in the kingswood on the morrow.
30. **Ned says he should be gone per Robert's command to return to Winterfell** — Robert reinstates him as Hand, tossing the silver hand clasp on the bed, forbidding him to leave.
31. **Ned raises "the Targaryen girl"** — Robert cuts him off, says it is done and he will hear no more.
32. **Robert threatens** — If Ned throws the badge in his face again, Robert will pin it on Jaime Lannister.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Haunted by the Tower of Joy dream; physically weakened and in pain; grief at remembering Jory is dead (catches himself about to summon Jory); frustrated and angry at Robert's refusal to pursue justice; helpless — "felt as helpless as a child"; grim foreboding ("this is only the beginning")
- **Primary preoccupation:** The promise to Lyanna (surfaces in the dream transition); the injustice of Robert equating Jaime's losses with Ned's; protecting his daughters; the unfinished matter of the "Targaryen girl"
- **Key decisions made:** Claims Catelyn acted on his orders (taking responsibility); accepts reinstatement as Hand (given no real choice); attempts to raise Daenerys again but is cut off
- **Self-deception flags:** Ned claims Catelyn acted "at my command" — this appears to be a lie to protect her, as the text presents it as a deliberate claim rather than fact
