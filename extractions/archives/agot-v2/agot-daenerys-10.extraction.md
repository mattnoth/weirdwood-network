## Events & Actions
1. **Pyre construction begins** — Foragers gather wood (gnarled cottonwoods, purple brush, brown grass). Two straightest trees are hacked, debarked, split, and laid in a square filled with straw, brush, bark shavings, and dry grass.
2. **Stallion sacrifice** — Rakharo chooses a stallion from their remaining herd. Aggo feeds it a withered apple and kills it with an axe blow between the eyes. The horse carcass forms the base.
3. **Mirri Maz Duur warns Dany** — The bound maegi tells Dany the blood alone is nothing without the words and wisdom of bloodmagic. Calls Dany a child with a child's ignorance.
4. **Jhogo whips Mirri Maz Duur** — At Dany's command, Jhogo takes his whip to the maegi to silence her.
5. **Pyre platform constructed** — Second level of hewn logs laid east to west (sunrise to sunset). Drogo's treasures piled upon it: great tent, painted vests, saddles, harness, his father's whip, his arakh, a dragonbone bow.
6. **Dany keeps her bride gifts** — When Aggo tries to add Dany's bride gift weapons, she refuses: "Those are mine, and I mean to keep them."
7. **Ser Jorah pleads with Dany** — He pledges his sword and heart, begs her not to climb on the pyre. Offers to take her east — Yi Ti, Qarth, Jade Sea, Asshai. Asks her not to join the dosh khaleen.
8. **Dany asserts her queenship** — Corrects Jorah from calling her "princess" — Viserys was his king, Viserys is dead, she is his heir and queen. Jorah kneels and calls her "my queen."
9. **Dany reassures Jorah** — She promises she does not mean to die with Drogo, kisses his forehead.
10. **Third level of pyre completed** — Woven branches no thicker than a finger, covered with dry leaves and twigs, laid north to south (ice to fire), piled with soft cushions and sleeping silks.
11. **Dany addresses the khalasar** — Fewer than a hundred remain. She frees all slaves, tells them to remove their collars, offers them a place as brothers and sisters if they stay.
12. **Dany names Jhogo ko** — Gives him the silver-handled whip. Jhogo hesitates, says it would shame him to be bloodrider to a woman.
13. **Dany names Aggo ko** — Gives him the dragonbone bow. Aggo accepts with lowered eyes but says he cannot speak the bloodrider words — only a man can lead a khalasar or name a ko.
14. **Dany names Rakharo ko** — Gives him the gold-chased arakh. Rakharo accepts but limits his oath to riding with her to Vaes Dothrak and the dosh khaleen.
15. **Dany names Ser Jorah first of her Queensguard** — Promises him a Valyrian steel longsword, dragon-forged. Jorah kneels and swears to serve, obey, and die for her. She kisses him and names him first of her Queensguard.
16. **Dany bathes** — Scalding hot bath; does not flinch. Irri scrubs her back, Jhiqui scents the water with oils, Doreah washes and combs her hair. She is scented with spiceflower and cinnamon.
17. **Dany prepares Drogo's body** — Alone, she washes him, brushes and oils his hair, braids it, slides silver rings onto his mustache, hangs his bells. Dresses him in horsehair leggings, high boots, medallioned belt, and his favorite old painted vest.
18. **Dany dresses herself** — Chooses loose sandsilk trousers, sandals, and a vest like Drogo's.
19. **Drogo's body carried to the pyre** — Jhogo and Aggo carry him. Dany walks behind. He is laid on cushions and silks, head toward the Mother of Mountains.
20. **Pyre doused with oil** — Oil poured over the pyre, soaking silks, brush, and grass until it trickles beneath the logs.
21. **Dragon eggs placed on pyre** — Dany commands her handmaids to bring her eggs. She climbs the pyre and places them: black beside Drogo's heart under his arm; green beside his head with his braid coiled around it; cream-and-gold between his legs.
22. **Ser Jorah objects to burning the eggs** — Suggests selling them in Asshai for a ship or wealth. Dany refuses: "They were not given to me to sell."
23. **Dany kisses Drogo for the last time** — She tastes the sweetness of oil on his lips.
24. **Mirri Maz Duur bound to the pyre** — Dany commands Jorah to bind the maegi to the pyre. He hesitates. Dany invokes his oath and calls Rakharo to help. Mirri does not cry out as she is staked down amid Drogo's treasures. Dany pours oil over her head.
25. **Dany thanks Mirri Maz Duur** — "I thank you for the lessons you have taught me." Mirri says she will not scream; Dany says she wants only her life. "Only death can pay for life."
26. **The red comet appears** — Jhogo spots the first star — a comet, burning red, bloodred, fire red, "the dragon's tail." Dany takes it as a sign.
27. **Dany lights the pyre** — She takes a torch from Aggo and thrusts it between the logs. Oil catches instantly, flames race up the wood.
28. **Mirri Maz Duur sings and dies** — She sings in a shrill ululating voice as flames reach her; her song becomes a wail of agony, then she falls silent.
29. **Drogo's body burns** — His clothing catches fire; Dany watches, part of her wanting to rush into the flames.
30. **The Dothraki retreat from the heat** — The pyre roars; huge flames, thick smoke, glowing cinders. The heat drives everyone back — Dothraki coughing and retreating, even Mormont driven off — but Dany stands her ground.
31. **Dany approaches the fire** — She steps closer, feeling the truth she had sensed long ago — "the brazier had not been hot enough." She sees the flames as dancers. Her vest smolders and she shrugs it off; it bursts into flame. Milk flows from her breasts.
32. **Dany sees visions in the flames** — Crimson firelions, yellow serpents, pale blue unicorns, fish, foxes, monsters, wolves, birds, flowering trees, and a great grey stallion limned in smoke with a mane of blue flame. She addresses it: "Yes, my love, my sun-and-stars, yes, mount now, ride now."
33. **She glimpses Drogo's image** — Mounted on the smoky stallion with a flaming lash; he smiles and the whip snakes at the pyre.
34. **First egg hatches** — A crack, sound of shattering stone; the platform collapses; a chunk of curved rock, pale and veined with gold, broken and smoking, lands at Dany's feet. Women shriek and children cry in wonder.
35. **Second egg hatches** — A second crack, loud and sharp as thunder; logs explode; horses scream; Dothraki shout in fear; Ser Jorah calls her name and curses.
36. **Dany steps into the fire** — She steps forward into the firestorm, calling to her children. She thinks: "I am Daenerys Stormborn, daughter of dragons, bride of dragons, mother of dragons."
37. **Third egg hatches** — The third crack is "as loud and sharp as the breaking of the world."
38. **Dany found unburnt with three dragons** — When the fire dies and the ground cools, Ser Jorah finds her amidst the ashes, surrounded by burnt bones of man, woman, and stallion. She is naked, covered in soot, hair crisped away, but unhurt. The cream-and-gold dragon suckles at her left breast, the green-and-bronze at her right, the black-and-scarlet draped across her shoulders with its neck coiled under her chin.
39. **The khalasar kneels** — Jorah falls to his knees wordlessly. Jhogo lays his arakh at her feet and murmurs "Blood of my blood." Aggo and Rakharo echo the oath. Then handmaids, then all the Dothraki — men, women, and children — kneel. They are hers now.
40. **Dragons announce their birth** — As Dany rises, Drogon hisses with pale smoke from mouth and nostrils. The other two pull from her breasts and add their voices, translucent wings unfolding, and "for the first time in hundreds of years, the night came alive with the music of dragons."

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Resolute and calm throughout, with undercurrents of grief for Drogo. She is purposeful, almost ceremonial in her actions. She experiences moments of deep tenderness (preparing Drogo's body, inhaling his scent, asking his forgiveness) alongside iron determination. Near the fire she feels ecstasy and wonder rather than fear. The refrain "If I look back I am lost" appears twice, indicating she is suppressing doubt through forward motion.
- **Primary preoccupation:** Executing the funeral/hatching ritual — she has a clear internal plan she shares with no one. She is also consciously claiming power: correcting titles, naming kos, creating a Queensguard, freeing slaves.
- **Key decisions made:** (1) To burn the dragon eggs with Drogo rather than sell them. (2) To sacrifice Mirri Maz Duur on the pyre. (3) To walk into the fire herself. (4) To claim the title of queen and assert authority over the remnant khalasar. (5) To name Jhogo, Aggo, and Rakharo as kos despite their resistance. (6) To name Ser Jorah first of her Queensguard. (7) To free all remaining slaves.
