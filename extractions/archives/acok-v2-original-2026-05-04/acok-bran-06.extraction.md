# ACOK — Bran VI

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** ACOK
- **chapter_number:** 47
- **pov_character:** Bran Stark
- **pov_chapter_number:** Bran VI
- **first_available:** ACOK Bran VI
- **location_primary:** Winterfell
- **location_secondary:** Godswood (Winterfell), Great Hall (Winterfell)
- **approximate_timeline:** Eight days after Ser Rodrik's departure with the garrison; during the night/early morning
- **time_markers:** Night; rain has stopped then starts again; "eight days past" since the garrison left; references to "morning bread" being prepared when the ironmen arrive; Rickon is described as "cranky at being woken" suggesting pre-dawn
- **chapter_summary:** Bran experiences Summer's perspective as the direwolf detects intruders scaling Winterfell's walls at night. Summer and Shaggydog try to raise the alarm but cannot escape the godswood. Bran wakes and is confronted by Theon Greyjoy, who reveals he has seized Winterfell with a small force of ironmen who swam the moat and climbed the walls. In the Great Hall, Bran publicly yields Winterfell to Theon. Mikken defies Theon and is killed by a bald ironman. Reek and Osha both swear allegiance to Theon.

## Events & Actions
1. **Summer detects intruders** — Through the wolf-dream, Bran-as-Summer hears the clink and scrape of grappling claws on Winterfell's walls and smells an unknown man-scent.
2. **Summer and Shaggydog try to raise the alarm** — Summer howls and the brothers investigate the sounds, but they are locked inside the godswood. Gates are chained and doors are thick.
3. **Summer receives guidance from Bran's unconscious mind** — A whisper directs Summer to the sentinel tree on the far side of the godswood as a way to escape.
4. **Summer attempts to climb the sentinel tree** — In a desperate sprint, Summer runs up the slanting trunk but slips on the wet bark and falls.
5. **Bran wakes in his tower room** — He feels a phantom ache in his shoulder from Summer's fall. He realizes no one will come when he calls because the guard has been removed from his door.
6. **Bran recalls the garrison's departure** — Six hundred men left eight days ago with Ser Rodrik to relieve Torrhen's Square; Cley Cerwyn was bringing three hundred more; ravens were sent to White Harbor, the barrowlands, and the wolfswood.
7. **Bran calls for help from his window** — He shouts for Hodor, Osha, Meera, and Jojen from his window, seeing only darkness and emptiness.
8. **An unknown ironman enters Bran's room** — Werlag, armed with dirk and axe, enters first.
9. **Theon Greyjoy reveals the seizure of Winterfell** — Theon tells Bran he sent four men over the walls with grappling claws to open a postern gate. He declares himself prince and says Balon Greyjoy has crowned himself king.
10. **Theon demands Bran publicly yield Winterfell** — Theon threatens that the people's safety depends on Bran's cooperation.
11. **Maester Luwin arrives wounded** — He has a cut above his left eye. He reports Alebelly was killed at the gate, Hayhead is wounded, and he managed to send off one raven (to White Harbor) before the ironmen arrived; the second raven was shot down.
12. **Maester Luwin dresses Bran as a lord** — He garbs Bran in appropriate clothing with the wolf's-head clasp, telling him he is "the Stark in Winterfell."
13. **The household is gathered in the Great Hall** — Ironmen herd everyone together: the Reeds, the Freys, Old Nan, Hayhead (wounded), Poxy Tym, Beth Cassel, Gage, Osha, Mikken (dragged in cursing), Farlen and Palla (she has been assaulted), Septon Chayle, Hodor, and finally Reek.
14. **Reek is brought from a tower cell** — He identifies himself as "Heke," says he served the Bastard of the Dreadfort until the Starks killed the Bastard.
15. **Bran publicly yields Winterfell to Theon** — From the high seat, Bran tells the household he has yielded and commands them to obey.
16. **Mikken defies Theon and is killed** — After multiple outbursts, the bald ironman (Stygg) drives a spear through Mikken's neck.
17. **Hodor is beaten by ironmen** — Two ironmen beat him with spear butts when he shouts "Hodor."
18. **Theon declares Greyjoy dominion over the north** — He announces that Torrhen's Square and Deepwood Motte will fall, his uncle is seizing Moat Cailin, and House Greyjoy holds the north.
19. **Reek swears allegiance to Theon** — He bends the knee, lays a sword at Theon's feet, and swears obedience to House Greyjoy and King Balon.
20. **Osha swears allegiance to Theon** — She steps past Mikken's body, argues for a weapon, knees Stygg in the groin, takes his spear, and bends the knee.
21. **The household is dismissed** — They are warned to work and make no trouble.
22. **Hodor carries Bran back to bed** — His face is battered, nose swollen, one eye closed; he sobs "Hodor" through cracked lips as he carries Bran into the rain.

## POV Character's Internal State
- **Emotional state:** Fear, confusion, helplessness, and reluctant duty. Bran moves from the wild freedom and desperation of the wolf-dream to waking paralysis and political helplessness. He feels dizzy with relief when he first recognizes Theon, then sickened as the betrayal sinks in. He is hurt by Osha's apparent defection. Numb when Mikken is killed.
- **Primary preoccupation:** The seizure of Winterfell and protecting the people under his care. He also dwells on the contrast between his capabilities as Summer (running, fighting, hearing, smelling) and his waking broken body.
- **Key decisions made:** Decides to yield Winterfell to Theon publicly, following Maester Luwin's counsel that "a lord must protect his smallfolk." Tries to command Mikken to be silent to protect him.
- **Self-deception flags:** Bran does not question his warging ability itself but does not fully understand the "whisper" that guided Summer — he accepts it without examining its nature. He thinks "I thought you were a friend" about Osha, not considering she may be acting strategically.
