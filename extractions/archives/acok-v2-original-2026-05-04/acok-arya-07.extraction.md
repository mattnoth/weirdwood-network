- **chapter_summary:** Arya settles into life at Harrenhal as a servant called Weasel under the brutal overseer Weese, scrubbing and serving while absorbing castle gossip about the war. The Brave Companions, a sellsword company under Vargo Hoat, arrive and are housed in the Widow's Tower. Ser Amory Lorch returns from pursuing Beric Dondarrion, and with him come Rorge, Biter, and Jaqen H'ghar — the three prisoners Arya saved from the fire. Jaqen finds Arya in the night and offers her three deaths in payment for the three lives she saved, telling her to speak names. After hearing Chiswyck tell a horrifying story about Ser Gregor's atrocities, Arya whispers Chiswyck's name to Jaqen, and three days later Chiswyck falls from a wallwalk and dies.

## Events & Actions
1. **Arya settles into servitude** — Arya adjusts to life as "Weasel" at Harrenhal, sleeping in the vaults beneath the Wailing Tower, scrubbing floors and running errands for Weese.
2. **Hot Pie caught giving Arya food** — Hot Pie tries to slip Arya a hot apple tart but is caught by two cooks; they confiscate it and beat him with a wooden spoon.
3. **Arya adds Weese to her prayer list** — Within three days, Weese earns first place in Arya's nightly death prayer. She recites the full list: Weese, Dunsen, Chiswyck, Polliver, Raff the Sweetling, the Tickler, the Hound, Ser Gregor, Ser Amory, Ser Ilyn, Ser Meryn, King Joffrey, Queen Cersei.
4. **Weese sets his dog on a latrine boy** — Weese's ugly spotted dog tears a chunk from a boy's calf while Weese laughs.
5. **Arya overhears castle gossip** — She learns various secrets: Pretty Pia's promiscuity, the gaoler's wife's pregnancy, Lord Lefford's fear of ghosts, Jodge's bedwetting, the cooks spitting in Ser Harys Swyft's food, and a letter declaring Joffrey a bastard that Lord Tywin ordered burned.
6. **Brave Companions arrive at Harrenhal** — Vargo Hoat's sellsword company arrives with their diverse fighters and black goat standard. Weese warns Arya about them.
7. **Fight between sellswords and Lannister men** — On the night of their arrival, fighting breaks out. Ser Harys Swyft's squire is stabbed to death and two Bloody Mummers are wounded.
8. **Lord Tywin hangs three men** — The next morning, Lord Tywin hangs two Bloody Mummers and one of Lord Lydden's archers from the gatehouse walls. Afterward, Vargo Hoat and Ser Harys embrace and swear friendship while Lord Tywin looks on.
9. **News of Roose Bolton at the ruby ford** — A Bloody Mummer reports that a northern army under Roose Bolton occupies the ruby ford of the Trident. Soldiers debate whether Bolton will cross.
10. **Arya considers escape to Riverrun** — Learning Robb is at Riverrun, closer than Winterfell, Arya begins thinking about how to escape. She considers enlisting captive northmen.
11. **Captive Freys are ransomed** — Four Frey brothers (three trueborn, one bastard) who exercised in the Flowstone Yard are ransomed by two other Frey brothers arriving with gold under a peace banner. All six leave together.
12. **Arya tries to reach Lord Cerwyn** — She spends days planning how to get past guards to the wounded Lord Cerwyn, hoping he would recognize her and help fund her escape.
13. **Lord Cerwyn dies** — Arya sees silent sisters loading Lord Cerwyn's body, sewn in a fine silk cloak with a battle-axe sigil, into their wagon.
14. **Ser Amory Lorch returns** — Ser Amory rides in with fewer men, many wounded, having chased Beric Dondarrion. Rorge, Biter, and Jaqen H'ghar ride with his column.
15. **Jaqen, Rorge, and Biter pass Arya unrecognized** — They ride past Arya without recognizing her; only Jaqen glances in her direction but his eyes pass over her.
16. **Arya scrubs steps until raw** — She spends the rest of the day scrubbing inside the Wailing Tower; by evenfall her hands are raw and bleeding.
17. **Arya recites her prayer and considers adding three names** — She says her list but is too tired to decide on additions (likely Jaqen, Rorge, Biter).
18. **Jaqen visits Arya in the night** — He wakes her by clamping a hand over her mouth, reveals he knows she is a girl, and offers three deaths as payment for the three lives she saved from the fire. He tells her to speak names.
19. **Arya asks Jaqen to take her to Riverrun** — He refuses, insisting only three deaths, no more, no less.
20. **Jaqen departs, leaving scent of ginger and cloves** — He kisses her hair and vanishes; only his scent remains.
21. **Ser Gregor returns with goats** — Returns earlier than expected with goats instead of prisoners; lost four men to Lord Beric's night raids. His men take residence on the second floor of the Wailing Tower.
22. **Chiswyck tells his story** — Arya is sent to ask about mending and overhears Chiswyck recount how Ser Gregor raped a brewer's daughter, his men joined in, the brewer's son was killed, and Ser Gregor demanded change for the silver.
23. **Weese canes Arya** — When Arya returns without asking about clothes, Weese pulls down her breeches and canes her until blood runs down her thighs. She thinks of Syrio's sayings to endure it.
24. **Arya whispers "Chiswyck" to Jaqen** — Two nights later, while pouring wine in the Barracks Hall, Arya leans close and whispers "Chiswyck" in Jaqen's ear. He gives no sign of hearing.
25. **Chiswyck dies** — Three days later, Chiswyck falls from a wallwalk and breaks his neck. It is attributed to drink or Harren's ghost. Arya knows it was Jaqen.
