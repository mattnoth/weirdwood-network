## Events & Actions
1. **Arya scouts from treetop** — Arya climbs to the highest branch of a tree and observes a fishing village on Gods Eye's shore: twenty-six thatched roofs, one slate, smoking chimneys, a wagon, a pier. She hears a horse whinny.
2. **Recalled: Return to the holdfast** — After the night of the burning, the group returned to the ruined holdfast to find everyone dead, including Yoren (killed by an axe blow that split his skull). Four Lannister dead were near Yoren's body.
3. **Recalled: Burial of Yoren** — Arya insisted on burying Yoren despite too many dead to bury all.
4. **Recalled: Towerhouse survivors** — Gendry found Cutjack, Tarber, and Kurz alive in the lord's round towerhouse, which survived Lorch's attempt to burn it. They joined the group.
5. **Recalled: Death of Kurz** — Kurz died of an arrow wound that became infected (dark flesh, red welts spreading). Buried under a mound of stones.
6. **Recalled: Desertion of Cutjack and Tarber** — The two took Kurz's gear and left the group without warning.
7. **Hot Pie signals with a dog bark** — He uses the animal-sound signaling Kurz taught them to call Arya down from the tree.
8. **Group debates what to do** — Hot Pie wants food; Lommy wants to yield; Gendry wants to scout at evenfall; Arya insists on going too.
9. **Gendry confronts Arya about her sex** — During the walk to the village, Gendry tells Arya he knows she is a girl, citing her always going into the woods to urinate.
10. **Arya reveals her identity** — Arya tells Gendry her real name is Arya of House Stark, daughter of the executed Hand of the King. Gendry is stunned and begins calling her "m'lady."
11. **Arya and Gendry split to scout the village** — Gendry goes west to find the road; Arya takes the shore approach.
12. **Arya discovers the gibbet** — She finds a long gibbet of raw green wood with stripped, savaged corpses hanging in chains, pecked by crows and torn by something larger.
13. **Arya observes the Lannister camp** — Two guards with spears at the slate-roofed storehouse; banners showing Lannister lion and Clegane dogs; men in mail and boiled leather throughout.
14. **Gendry is captured** — A soldier shoves Gendry forward as a captive. Guards take his helm, smash him in the face with a spear butt, kick him, and shove him into the storehouse. A small boy tries to escape but is thrown back inside. Screams and sobbing come from inside.
15. **Arya observes the camp through the evening** — She watches the guard change, a hunting party return with a deer, cooking, choicest portions carried to a house, guards given meat, bread, and wine.
16. **Arya returns to the group** — She crawls back through the woods in full darkness, finds Lommy and Hot Pie.
17. **Arya plans a rescue** — She tells Hot Pie they will sneak up and kill the two guards to free Gendry. Hot Pie reluctantly agrees.
18. **Rescue attempt fails** — Arya and Hot Pie crawl under the briar along the gibbet. A crow lands on Hot Pie's back; he panics and yields loudly. Arya draws Needle and fights but is overpowered — slashing at one man (blocked by steel-clad arm), tackled, sword wrenched away, punched with iron-clad fist.
19. **Brought before the Mountain** — Arya and Hot Pie are brought before Gregor Clegane. Arya recognizes his sigil (three black dogs on yellow) from the tourney at King's Landing.
20. **The Mountain orders them to lead soldiers to the others** — Gregor says "You'll lead us to these others" and walks off.
21. **Soldiers find Lommy** — Four men escort Arya and Hot Pie back. They find Lommy under the oak, yielding. The soldiers ask about Dondarrion. Lommy says he doesn't know.
22. **Weasel escapes** — When asked about a girl, Lommy says she ran off. Arya thinks "Run, Weasel, run as far as you can."
23. **Lommy is killed** — A spearman asks about Lommy's wounded leg, asks if he can walk. When Lommy says no and tells them to carry him, the spearman drives his spear through Lommy's throat, killing him.
