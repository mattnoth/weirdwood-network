# ACOK — Sansa VIII

## Chapter Metadata
- **book:** ACOK
- **chapter_number:** 66
- **pov_character:** Sansa Stark
- **pov_chapter_number:** Sansa VIII
- **first_available:** ACOK Sansa VIII
- **location_primary:** Throne room, Red Keep, King's Landing
- **location_secondary:** Sansa's chambers (Red Keep); godswood (Red Keep)
- **approximate_timeline:** Shortly after the Battle of the Blackwater; a day of formal court ceremonies and rewards
- **time_markers:** The ceremonies last from daytime until "the light outside the windows was fading"; "come dark" Sansa goes to the godswood; the new knights held vigil "all through the night" in the Great Sept of Baelor and walked barefoot "that morning"; Dontos says the wedding "won't be for a moon's turn yet"
- **chapter_summary:** The Iron Throne court holds a grand ceremony to reward the victors of the Battle of the Blackwater. Lord Tywin Lannister is named Hand of the King and Savior of the City. The Tyrells receive honors and Joffrey formally sets aside his betrothal to Sansa in favor of Margaery Tyrell. Petyr Baelish is granted Harrenhal and named Lord Paramount of the Trident. Over six hundred new knights are made, captives are pardoned or executed, and Joffrey cuts himself on the Iron Throne. Sansa is privately relieved but warned by Dontos that she remains a hostage; he tells her an escape plan is set for Joffrey's wedding night and gives her a silver hair net set with dark amethysts from Asshai.

## Events & Actions
1. **Tywin Lannister rides into the throne room on his warhorse** — His destrier drops dung at the base of the Iron Throne; Joffrey steps around it to embrace his grandfather
2. **Joffrey names Tywin Savior of the City and Hand of the King** — Tywin accepts governance "until Your Grace does come of age"; Joff fastens the Hand's chain around his neck
3. **Mace Tyrell and his sons are honored** — Each receives a chain of golden roses with a lion disc; Loras asks for and receives Kingsguard appointment; Mace is given a seat on the royal council
4. **Ser Garlan proposes Margaery as Joffrey's bride** — In a scripted exchange, Joffrey feigns reluctance, Cersei and the High Septon release him from his betrothal to Sansa
5. **Joffrey sets Sansa aside and agrees to marry Margaery Tyrell** — The court cheers; Sansa privately prays he will do it and is relieved when he does
6. **Heroes of the Blackwater are rewarded** — Paxter Redwyne and sons, Mathis Rowan, Randyll Tarly, Kevan Lannister, Addam Marbrand, and western lords are honored
7. **Lesser-born heroes are rewarded** — Philip Foote made lord and given Caron lands; Lothor Brune knighted and promised riverlands holdings; Josmyn Peckledon promised knighthood and equipment; Willit given arms and his sons taken into Lannister service
8. **Ship captains and alchemists honored** — Captains of Wildwind, Prince Aemon, River Arrow, and officers from other ships honored; Hallyne raised to style of lord (without lands)
9. **Ser Lancel awarded Darry** — Lancel receives House Darry's lands, castle, and rights; he does not appear due to his wound
10. **Petyr Baelish granted Harrenhal and named Lord Paramount of the Trident** — Baelish jokes about needing sons and grandsons; Sansa reflects the honor is empty since Harrenhal is cursed and the Trident lords are sworn elsewhere
11. **Over six hundred new knights are dubbed** — They held vigil in the Great Sept and walked barefoot through the city; only three Kingsguard present to dub them (Balon Swann, Meryn Trant, Osmund Kettleblack)
12. **Captives brought before the court** — Great lords and knights who fought for Stannis: Celtigar, Bonifer, Estermont, Varner, Mullendore, Red Ronnet, and many others
13. **Defiant Florent bastard refuses to submit** — Warns the Lord of Light protects Stannis; Joffrey orders Ser Ilyn to take his head
14. **A knight with a fiery heart surcoat denounces Joffrey** — Calls him "the black worm," an abomination born of incest; names queen, dwarf, spider, and "false flowers" as corrupt
15. **Joffrey cuts himself on the Iron Throne** — Lurches to his feet, chops his hand down, arm brushes a metal fang; blood soaks through crimson samite; cries for his mother
16. **The fiery heart knight seizes a spear and cries "the throne denies him"** — Ser Meryn Trant kills him with a longsword through the chest on Tywin's signal
17. **Tywin takes control** — Seats himself on a step of the Iron Throne and continues the session; Joffrey bundled out by three maesters
18. **Sansa returns to her chambers and rejoices privately** — Hugs a pillow to muffle a squeal of joy; eats supper
19. **Sansa meets Dontos in the godswood** — Dontos warns her the queen will never let her go and Joffrey can still use her; tells her the escape is planned for Joffrey's wedding night
20. **Dontos gives Sansa the silver hair net** — Fine-spun silver with dark gems (black amethysts from Asshai); he calls it magic, justice, vengeance, and "home"
