- **chapter_summary:** Lord Tywin Lannister prepares to march his host from Harrenhal in response to Robb Stark's victories in the west. Arya, working as Weese's servant under the name "Weasel," searches for Jaqen H'ghar to whisper a second name. She learns from a gate guard that Jaqen will stay behind at Harrenhal under Ser Amory Lorch's garrison. After being slapped and abused by Weese, Arya whispers Weese's name to Jaqen at the bathhouse. The next morning, as Lord Tywin's army rides out, Arya realizes she wasted her second death on Weese instead of naming Lord Tywin or another important enemy. She discovers Weese dead in the yard, his throat torn out by his own dog, and sees Jaqen acknowledge the kill by pressing two fingers to his cheek.

## Events & Actions
1. **Lord Tywin's host prepares to march** — The castle is in chaos with wagons being loaded, smiths working, weapons and armor being prepared, and people praying in the sept.
2. **Ser Addam Marbrand departs** — Rides out a day before the rest with a double column of men, making a gallant show on his red courser.
3. **Arya runs messages for Weese** — Goes throughout the castle and beyond the walls into the camp; considers fleeing but fears Weese's threat of having Vargo Hoat cut off her feet.
4. **Arya reads Weese's unsealed messages** — Finds them mundane (cart assignments, a gambling debt demand).
5. **Arya steals a drinking horn** — Delivers a gambling debt message to an illiterate knight; when he tries to hit her, she snatches his silver-banded drinking horn and escapes; gives it to Weese.
6. **Arya searches for Jaqen H'ghar** — Cannot find him amidst the chaos; wants to whisper a second name before her targets leave.
7. **Arya learns Jaqen is staying at Harrenhal** — A gate guard tells her Ser Amory Lorch has been named castellan and his men (including Jaqen) are staying; the Bloody Mummers also remain to forage.
8. **Arya sent to the armory** — Weese sends her with a paper to get a new sword for Ser Lyonel, who rides with Ser Kevan.
9. **Gendry warns Arya** — Tells her Hot Pie heard her yell "Winterfell" at the holdfast; Gendry covered for her, saying she yelled "Go to hell."
10. **Arya receives the longsword from Lucan** — Lucan insults Ser Lyonel; Arya enjoys the weight of the sword and considers stealing a horse and riding out, but fears being caught.
11. **Arya overhears archers talking about Robb** — They describe him with giants, say he's more wolf than man, debate whether to fear him.
12. **Weese slaps Arya and takes the sword** — Catches her lingering; deals her a stinging backhand slap, making her bite her tongue.
13. **Weese sends Arya to the brewhouse** — Orders her to tell Tuffleberry about two dozen barrels.
14. **Arya encounters Rorge** — Under the stone bridge between Widow's Tower and Kingspyre; Rorge threatens her sexually and calls her "Yoren's little cunt"; she escapes by claiming to have a message for Jaqen, which makes Rorge back off.
15. **Arya whispers "Weese" to Jaqen** — Finds him in the bathhouse soaking in a tub; whispers the name; Jaqen replies cryptically and splashes water at her.
16. **Tuffleberry curses at Weese's demand** — Threatens to report to Lord Tywin; demands the barrels within the hour.
17. **Weese forces men to fetch the barrels** — Rounds up six men after receiving Tuffleberry's angry response.
18. **Weese eats the capon, breaks his promise to Arya** — Gives the cheese and wing to his bedmate; eats the rest himself; slaps Arya twice for looking at him, threatens to spoon out her eye and feed it to his dog; shoves her to the floor, ripping her shift.
19. **Arya recites her kill list** — Sews her torn shift with a bone needle while whispering the names: Weese, Dunsen, Polliver, Raff the Sweetling, the Tickler, the Hound, Ser Gregor, Ser Amory, Ser Ilyn, Ser Meryn, King Joffrey, Queen Cersei.
20. **Lord Tywin's host departs Harrenhal** — Arya watches from the Wailing Tower; sees the Lannister banners, the many lords' heraldry, and Ser Gregor Clegane last of all with Polliver carrying the black dog standard and wearing Gendry's horned helm.
21. **Arya realizes her mistake** — Understands she should have named Lord Tywin or another important enemy instead of Weese.
22. **Arya runs to find Jaqen to change the name** — Hears the portcullis lowering and then a shriek.
23. **Weese is found dead** — His throat torn out by his own spotted dog, which is lapping his blood and ripping flesh from his face; the dog is shot with a crossbow.
24. **Jaqen signals Arya** — Leaning against the Wailing Tower, he lays two fingers against his cheek when Arya looks at him.
