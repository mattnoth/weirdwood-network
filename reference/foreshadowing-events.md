# ASOIAF Major Confirmed Events — Foreshadowing Scan Targets

> **Purpose:** This is the event list for Pass 4 extraction agents. For each event, scan all prior chapters for foreshadowing, prophetic references, thematic setup, and dramatic irony. Each event includes its book/chapter location so agents know the boundary — only chapters *before* the event count as foreshadowing.

---

## A Game of Thrones (AGOT)

### 1. Jon Arryn's Murder
- **Event:** Lysa Arryn poisoned Jon Arryn at Littlefinger's instruction
- **Location:** Revealed ASOS, Sansa VII (but the death itself precedes AGOT)
- **Scan for:** Any mention of Lysa's instability, Littlefinger's influence over her, the Tears of Lys, the letter Lysa sends to Catelyn blaming the Lannisters
- **Why it matters:** The inciting event of the entire series is a misdirection — the reader (and Ned) blame the Lannisters for something Littlefinger orchestrated

### 2. Bran's Fall
- **Event:** Jaime pushes Bran from the tower after Bran witnesses Jaime and Cersei
- **Location:** AGOT, Bran II
- **Scan for:** Bran's climbing habit established in Bran I, the "broken things" motif, the three-eyed crow dream that follows

### 3. Ned's Execution
- **Event:** Joffrey orders Ned Stark beheaded despite the deal for him to take the black
- **Location:** AGOT, Arya V
- **Scan for:** Ned's honor preventing him from acting ruthlessly when he had the chance, Sansa's naivety about Joffrey, Varys's warnings, the repeated "when you play the game of thrones you win or you die," Arya's wolf dreams, the butcher's boy incident establishing Joffrey's cruelty

### 4. Robert's Death
- **Event:** Robert killed by a boar while hunting, enabled by Cersei having Lancel feed him strongwine
- **Location:** AGOT, Eddard XIII
- **Scan for:** Robert's constant drinking, Cersei's hatred of Robert, Varys's comment about who gave Robert the wine, the thematic setup of the warrior king reduced to a drunk

### 5. Drogo's Death / Dragon Birth
- **Event:** Drogo dies from an infected wound, Mirri Maz Duur's blood magic, Dany walks into the pyre and hatches three dragons
- **Location:** AGOT, Daenerys X
- **Scan for:** The dragon eggs described as stone/petrified, Dany's increasing heat resistance, "fire and blood" as Targaryen words, the stallion who mounts the world prophecy and its subversion, Mirri's warnings that Dany ignores

### 6. Catelyn Seizes Tyrion
- **Event:** Catelyn arrests Tyrion at the crossroads inn, triggering the Stark-Lannister war
- **Location:** AGOT, Catelyn V
- **Scan for:** The Valyrian steel dagger misdirection (Littlefinger's lie), Catelyn's impulsiveness masked as duty, the chain of escalation this triggers

## A Clash of Kings (ACOK)

### 7. Battle of the Blackwater
- **Event:** Stannis's fleet destroyed by wildfire, Tywin and the Tyrells arrive to save King's Landing
- **Location:** ACOK, multiple POVs (Davos III, Sansa VII, Tyrion XV)
- **Scan for:** Tyrion's wildfire preparations, the chain across the river, Cersei's escalating paranoia during the battle, the Tyrell alliance being brokered by Littlefinger, Stannis's rigid justice preventing him from compromising

### 8. Theon Takes Winterfell
- **Event:** Theon captures Winterfell with a skeleton crew, then "kills" Bran and Rickon (actually miller's sons)
- **Location:** ACOK, Theon IV-V
- **Scan for:** Theon's identity crisis between Stark and Greyjoy, the Ironborn cultural values established in his early chapters, Maester Luwin's counsel that Theon ignores, Ramsay Snow's arrival disguised as Reek

### 9. House of the Undying Visions
- **Event:** Dany enters the House of the Undying and receives prophetic visions
- **Location:** ACOK, Daenerys IV
- **Scan for:** The visions themselves are the foreshadowing — blue winter rose in a wall of ice (Jon), the cloth dragon on poles (fAegon), the red wedding vision, "three fires you must light, three mounts you must ride, three treasons you must know"

### 10. Renly's Death
- **Event:** Melisandre's shadow assassin kills Renly in his tent
- **Location:** ACOK, Catelyn IV
- **Scan for:** Melisandre's powers being established, Stannis's "I dreamed of Renly's death" comment, the shadow motif, Brienne's presence and wrongful blame setting up her entire arc

## A Storm of Swords (ASOS)

### 11. The Red Wedding
- **Event:** Walder Frey and Roose Bolton massacre Robb Stark, Catelyn, and the Northern army at Edmure's wedding
- **Location:** ASOS, Catelyn VII
- **Scan for:** (MASSIVE foreshadowing catalogue)
  - Guest right emphasized throughout the series
  - The Freys' anger at Robb breaking his marriage pact
  - Roose Bolton's quiet positioning and leech metaphor
  - Grey Wind's agitation at the Twins (the wolf senses danger)
  - Patchface: "fool's blood, king's blood, blood on the maiden's thigh"
  - Dany's House of the Undying vision of a dead man with a wolf's head at a feast
  - The Ghost of High Heart's vision: "a wolf's head on a man's shoulders"
  - Catelyn's repeated unease about the Freys
  - "Mayhaps" — the Frey weasel word for lying
  - The Rains of Castamere as a Lannister threat song

### 12. The Purple Wedding
- **Event:** Joffrey poisoned at his own wedding feast by Olenna Tyrell and Littlefinger
- **Location:** ASOS, Tyrion VIII
- **Scan for:** Sansa's hairnet (the poison delivery mechanism), Olenna touching Sansa's hair, Littlefinger's comment about "a piece that needs to be moved," Joffrey's escalating cruelty making him a liability to everyone, the "strangler" poison from the prologue

### 13. Oberyn vs. The Mountain
- **Event:** Oberyn Martell nearly kills Gregor Clegane in Tyrion's trial by combat but dies demanding a confession
- **Location:** ASOS, Tyrion X
- **Scan for:** Oberyn's obsession with Elia's murder stated from his first appearance, his fighting style described as aggressive and mobile, the thematic pattern of revenge consuming the avenger, the poisoned spear (Gregor dies slowly afterward)

### 14. Tyrion Kills Tywin
- **Event:** Tyrion murders Tywin on the privy with a crossbow, also strangles Shae
- **Location:** ASOS, Tyrion XI
- **Scan for:** The crossbow on the wall of Joffrey's chamber (literal Chekhov's gun), Tywin's repeated "wherever whores go" line, Shae's testimony at the trial, Tyrion's escalating rage and humiliation, Jaime's revelation about Tysha

### 15. Stoneheart Reveal
- **Event:** Catelyn is resurrected by Beric Dondarrion as Lady Stoneheart
- **Location:** ASOS, Epilogue
- **Scan for:** Beric's resurrection mechanics established earlier, the Brotherhood Without Banners' trajectory, Catelyn's body thrown in the river, Nymeria (Arya's wolf) pulling a body from the river in Arya's wolf dreams, "she don't speak, but she remembers"

### 16. Jon Becomes Lord Commander
- **Event:** Jon Snow elected Lord Commander of the Night's Watch
- **Location:** ASOS, Jon XII
- **Scan for:** Sam's political maneuvering (paralleling real papal elections), Jon's growing leadership through ASOS, the tension between Jon's Stark identity and his Watch vows, Mormont's raven saying "Snow" and "king"

## A Feast for Crows (AFFC)

### 17. Cersei's Arrest by the Faith
- **Event:** The High Sparrow arrests Cersei for adultery, incest, and murder
- **Location:** AFFC, Cersei X
- **Scan for:** Cersei rearming the Faith Militant (her own catastrophic decision), her paranoia about the valonqar prophecy driving increasingly erratic behavior, her affair with Lancel being known to the Faith through his confession, every chapter showing her making the same mistake — arming enemies and creating the conditions for her own downfall

### 18. Pate's Murder / Jaqen at the Citadel
- **Event:** The Alchemist (wearing Jaqen's new face) kills Pate and presumably takes his identity
- **Location:** AFFC, Prologue + Samwell V (Pate reappears alive)
- **Scan for:** The face description matching what Jaqen showed Arya at Harrenhal, the Alchemist wanting Pate's master key to the Citadel, The Death of Dragons book, the Faceless Men's historical connection to Valyria's doom

### 19. Brienne's "Death"
- **Event:** Lady Stoneheart orders Brienne hanged; Brienne shouts a word (likely "sword") to save herself
- **Location:** AFFC, Brienne VIII
- **Scan for:** The impossible choice between oaths — Brienne swore to Catelyn AND is now carrying a Lannister sword trying to save a Lannister's children. Stoneheart's corruption of Catelyn's values. The word "sword" as a callback to the choice Stoneheart gave her.

### 20. Euron's Kingsmoot Victory
- **Event:** Euron Greyjoy wins the Kingsmoot with Dragonbinder and promises to conquer Westeros with dragons
- **Location:** AFFC, The Drowned Man
- **Scan for:** Dragonbinder's description and the runes on it, the thrall who blows it dying with burned lungs, Euron's eye patch and "Crow's Eye" epithet, his claim to have sailed to Valyria, the dragon egg he claims to have thrown into the sea (paid to the Faceless Men?)

## A Dance with Dragons (ADWD)

### 21. Jon's Assassination
- **Event:** Night's Watch brothers stab Jon Snow ("for the Watch") after he announces marching on Winterfell
- **Location:** ADWD, Jon XIII
- **Scan for:** The mounting tension between Jon's Stark loyalties and his Watch neutrality, the Shieldhall speech, Bowen Marsh weeping (he doesn't want to do it), the pink letter from Ramsay, Ghost being locked away (Jon separated from his wolf right before death, unlike every other time he's in danger), Melisandre's visions of Jon as "a man, then a wolf, then a man again"

### 22. Dany Rides Drogon / Escapes Meereen
- **Event:** Drogon attacks the fighting pit, Dany mounts him and flies away to the Dothraki Sea
- **Location:** ADWD, Daenerys IX
- **Scan for:** Dany's increasing inability to control the dragons, the locusts (likely a poisoning attempt by the Shavepate or Green Grace), the pattern of Dany's story cycling through "conquer → compromise → fail → break free," her final chapter in the grass returning to "fire and blood" identity

### 23. Aegon's Landing
- **Event:** Aegon (Young Griff) lands in the Stormlands with the Golden Company and begins conquering
- **Location:** ADWD, Jon Connington chapters
- **Scan for:** Varys and Illyrio's conspiracy seeded from AGOT, the mummer's dragon prophecy from the House of the Undying, Tyrion's assessment that Aegon is being manipulated, Jon Connington's greyscale (ticking clock), the Blackfyre theory evidence — Golden Company's historical loyalty to Blackfyres, Illyrio's wife Serra, the "black or red, a dragon is still a dragon" line

### 24. Stannis Marches on Winterfell
- **Event:** Stannis's army trapped in snow marching toward Winterfell, fate unknown
- **Location:** ADWD, multiple chapters
- **Scan for:** The Night Lamp theory setup (the frozen lake, the Manderly treachery, the Karstark betrayal), the Grand Northern Conspiracy ("the North remembers" — Manderly's speech), the Frey pies at Winterfell, Theon's arc from Reek back toward agency

### 25. Varys Assassinates Kevan Lannister
- **Event:** Varys kills Kevan (and Pycelle) to prevent the realm from stabilizing under competent rule, explicitly to clear the path for Aegon
- **Location:** ADWD, Epilogue
- **Scan for:** Varys's stated philosophy about power from ACOK (the riddle of the sellsword), his disappearance from King's Landing, Kevan being established as the one competent Lannister administrator, the little birds as weapons

### 26. Quentyn's Death
- **Event:** Quentyn Martell attempts to steal a dragon and is burned alive
- **Location:** ADWD, The Dragontamer
- **Scan for:** "Oh" as his final word — the anticlimax of a heroic quest, the entire Quentyn arc as a subversion of the fantasy prince trope, Doran's long-laid plans failing at contact with reality, the Tattered Prince wanting a dragon of his own

### 27. Manderly's Frey Pies
- **Event:** Wyman Manderly kills three Freys and bakes them into pies served at Ramsay's wedding feast
- **Location:** ADWD, A Ghost in Winterfell
- **Scan for:** Manderly's "the North remembers" speech, the Rat Cook story told by Bran (guest right violation punished by the gods), the missing Freys, Manderly requesting a song about the Rat Cook at the feast, the fat lord who "ichors" when cut

## Cross-Book Thematic Events (Scan Across All Books)

### 28. R+L=J Revelation Setup
- **Event:** Jon's parentage — Rhaegar + Lyanna = Jon (confirmed in show, heavily evidenced in text)
- **Scan across all books for:** Ned's promise to Lyanna, "promise me Ned," Ned's fever dream of the Tower of Joy, the blue winter rose imagery, Ned never actually calling Jon his son in his internal monologue, the repeated association of Jon with Targaryen imagery (dark wings, fire dreams), Dany's House of the Undying vision of the blue rose in a wall of ice

### 29. The Others' True Nature
- **Event:** The Others are not simply evil monsters — their motives and nature remain unexplained
- **Scan for:** The prologue of AGOT describing them with beauty ("elegant," "alive with moonlight"), the Night's King legend (a Lord Commander who married an Other), Craster's sacrifices suggesting negotiation/pact, the obsidian/dragonglass weakness, the possible connection to the original Pact between Children and First Men

### 30. The Doom of Valyria
- **Event:** The destruction of Valyria — potentially caused by the Faceless Men
- **Scan for:** The Faceless Men origin story (slaves in Valyrian mines, the first gift of death to the masters), glass candles and their connection to Valyria, Euron's claimed visit to Valyria, the Fourteen Flames, Septon Barth's theories suppressed by the Citadel
