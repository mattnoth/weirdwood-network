## Origins

Lady Johanna was the daughter of [Roland Westerling](wiki:Roland_Westerling), [Lord of the Crag](wiki:Lord_of_the_Crag).(wiki:Johanna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

During the civil war known as the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Johanna's husband, Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister), and his twin brother, Ser [Tyland](wiki:Tyland_Lannister), supported the [greens](wiki:Greens). Jason led his [banners](wiki:Bannerman) and marched to the field in support of King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), but he was slain in the [Battle at the Red Fork](wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork) in [130 AC](wiki:130_AC).(wiki:Johanna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) After Jason's death, Johanna ruled [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) as a [regent](wiki:Regent) for their young son, Lord [Loreon Lannister](wiki:Loreon_Lannister).(wiki:Johanna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), took advantage of the situation and attacked the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands), causing much damage to the region. Lady Johanna barred the gates of the Rock, but the Red Kraken captured most of the [Lannister fleet](wiki:Lannister_fleet), burned the rest, and sacked nearby [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport).(wiki:Johanna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

As one of the [Three Widows](wiki:Three_Widows), Johanna agreed to make peace with the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) after the death of Aegon II in return for the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) ordering Dalton to return to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands), free [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle), and return all captured women.(wiki:Johanna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Johanna and her father attended the wedding of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) and [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Johanna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Dalton ignored the commands of the [regency of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III) to vacate the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands). When Johanna began building a new fleet of warships, Dalton attacked the shipyards and captured another hundred women.(wiki:Johanna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Johanna donned mail to lead the remaining Lannister forces in the struggle to drive the ironborn from their shores.(wiki:Johanna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Leading a host from Casterly Rock and Lannisport, Johanna routed the ironborn beneath [Kayce](wiki:Kayce)'s walls, with Dalton's uncle slain in the fighting. Songs claim Johanna slew twelve ironmen in the [battle at Kayce](wiki:Battle_at_Kayce), although scholars believe she only carried a banner.(wiki:Johanna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) Johanna next arranged an expedition to recover Fair Isle, but her plan was betrayed to the ironborn. The ragtag fleet sent by the regent was destroyed in an [ambush at Fair Isle](wiki:Ambush_at_Fair_Isle).(wiki:Johanna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

There was a [struggle for power](wiki:Struggle_for_power) among the ironborn after the Red Kraken was murdered at [Faircastle](wiki:Faircastle) a few years later. Since the Lannister fleet had been destroyed by the [Greyjoys](wiki:House_Greyjoy), Johanna allied with the [lord admiral](wiki:Lord_admiral) of the [Reach](wiki:Reach), Ser [Leo Costayne](wiki:Leo_Costayne), to have the westermen invade the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC). When [Rodrik Greyjoy](wiki:Rodrik_Greyjoy_(son_of_Dalton)) was taken to Casterly Rock as a captive, Johanna had him [gelded](wiki:Eunuch) and made him her son's [fool](wiki:Fool).(wiki:Johanna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_red_kraken.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Johanna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Johanna reclaimed the dwindling glory of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) and also lent gold to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to help rebuild the extensive damages caused by the previous civil war.(wiki:Johanna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)(wiki:Johanna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
