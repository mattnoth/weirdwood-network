## Appearances & Description

Grunt [lost his tongue](wiki:Tongueless) for speaking carelessly within earshot of [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton).(wiki:Grunt.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He is described as brutal.(wiki:Grunt.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Grunt enjoys touching women's breasts,(wiki:Grunt.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) although not always with permission.(wiki:Grunt.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Grunt is present in the great hall of the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort) when [Big Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Jammos)) and [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)) bring [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) before [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), who is joined by [Arnolf Karstark](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark) and [Hother Umber](wiki:Hother_Umber).(wiki:Grunt.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

[Sour Alyn](wiki:Sour_Alyn) and Grunt are on guard at the door of Ramsay's bedchamber when the [spearwives](wiki:Spearwife) go with Theon to rescue "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)" (actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)).
