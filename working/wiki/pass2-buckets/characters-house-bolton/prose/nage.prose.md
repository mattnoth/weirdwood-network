## Appearances & Description

Nage is small and round.(wiki:Nage.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Nage is a part of Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s escort from [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), under command of [Steelshanks Walton](wiki:Steelshanks_Walton). He bears the peace banner for the [guards](wiki:House_Bolton_guards), a seven-tailed rainbow banner with the staff topped by a seven-pointed star.(wiki:Nage.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Nage continues to be the banner bearer for the group of northmen when they leave the capital with "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)" (in reality [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole) in disguise).(wiki:Nage.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
