## Appearances & Description

Alyn wields a sword.(wiki:Sour_Alyn.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Sour Alyn is present in the great hall of the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort) when [Big Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Jammos)) and [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)) bring [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) before [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), who is joined by [Arnolf Karstark](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark) and [Hother Umber](wiki:Hother_Umber).(wiki:Sour_Alyn.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Alyn and [Skinner](wiki:Skinner) toss a freerider from the Battlements Gate of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) after the man angers Ramsay.(wiki:Sour_Alyn.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Sour Alyn and [Grunt](wiki:Grunt) are on guard at the door of Ramsay's bedchamber when the [spearwives](wiki:Spearwife) go with Theon to rescue "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)" (actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)).
