## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Luton is present at the wedding of [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Bolton) to "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)".(wiki:Luton.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) When several men-at-arms in the castle are murdered, suspicion eventually falls upon [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) [knights](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Luton.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) This leads to a confrontation and skirmish between the [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) and [Manderlys](wiki:House_Manderly), during which Luton is severely wounded. As he tries to push his entrails back into his wound, crying noisily for his mother, Ramsay loses patience and drives a spear through Luton's chest.(wiki:Luton.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
