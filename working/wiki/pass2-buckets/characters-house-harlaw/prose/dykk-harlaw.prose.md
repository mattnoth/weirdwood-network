## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Dykk is among the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) who participate in the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) by [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy). Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) leads a [northern](wiki:North) host to retake the castle. When Theon asks the assembled ironmen to remain and fight alongside him, Dykk spits at the ground.(wiki:Dykk_Harlaw.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Four [Harlaws](wiki:House_Harlaw) remain with Theon and are slain during the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell) by [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton);(wiki:Dykk_Harlaw.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) it is unknown if [Gevin](wiki:Gevin_Harlaw) and Dykk are among them or if they are among the ironborn who abandon Theon beforehand.
