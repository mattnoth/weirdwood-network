## Appearances & Description

According to the naming conventions for [bastards](wiki:Bastard), Robert must have been a bastard from the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn).

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

When Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard), returns to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) after months of captivity, he interrogates the new members of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) regarding their experience at arms. Ser [Osmund Kettleblack](wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack) tells Jaime he was knighted by Ser Robert on the battlefield, and that he is now dead. Jaime does not recognize this knight, and is dubious about Ser Robert's actual existence.(wiki:Robert_Stone.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
