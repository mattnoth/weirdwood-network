## Origins

Rognar II was brought down by [pirates](wiki:Pirate), [sellswords](wiki:Sellsword), and warlords, [Andals](wiki:Andals) who came to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) and allied with [Orkwoods](wiki:House_Orkwood), [Drumms](wiki:House_Drumm), [Hoares](wiki:House_Hoare), and [Greyjoys](wiki:House_Greyjoy).
