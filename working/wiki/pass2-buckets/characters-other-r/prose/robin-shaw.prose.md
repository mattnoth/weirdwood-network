## Origins

In [59 AC](wiki:59_AC) the [winter](wiki:Winter) [disease](wiki:Disease) known as the [Shivers](wiki:Shivers) killed many including Ser [Samgood of Sour Hill](wiki:Samgood_of_Sour_Hill) and Ser [Victor the Valiant](wiki:Victor_the_Valiant) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).
