## Origins

Mylenda married [Petyr Frey](wiki:Petyr_Frey), called "Petyr Pimple", when he was ten and she was three times his age.(wiki:Mylenda_Caron.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Rumors at the [Twins](wiki:Twins) claim that Mylenda is sleeping with Petyr's older brother, [Black Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Ryman)).(wiki:Mylenda_Caron.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Petyr, Mylenda's husband, wanders off with a [camp follower](wiki:Camp_follower), and is captured by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners). [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey) believes he had gone whoring because Mylenda was sleeping with his brother, [Black Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Ryman)). Mylenda becomes a widow when Petyr is hanged by the brotherhood.(wiki:Mylenda_Caron.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
