## Origins

Ser Kirby was present when Ser [Morgan Dunstable](wiki:Morgan_Dunstable) knighted Ser [Glendon Flowers](wiki:Glendon_Flowers) at the [Pussywillows](wiki:Pussywillows).(wiki:Kirby_Pimm.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

In [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), Kirby attended the [Whitewalls Tourney](wiki:Whitewalls_Tourney). He intervened when Ser Glendon Flowers was denied entry into the tourney and explained how Ser Glendon came about his knighthood to the stewards.(wiki:Kirby_Pimm.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
