## Origins

[Willam](wiki:Willam) and his brothers, Meribald, [Robin](wiki:Robin), [Owen](wiki:Owen_(brother_of_Meribald)) and Owen's friend Jon Pox all went off to fight in the War of the Ninepenny Kings. During the War, Willam and Robin died from a fever. Owen died in battle and Jon was hanged for rape. Meribald survived to become a septon.(wiki:Jon_Pox.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
