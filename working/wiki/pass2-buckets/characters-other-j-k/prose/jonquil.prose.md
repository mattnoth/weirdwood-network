## Origins

According to legend, [Florian](wiki:Florian_the_Fool) first spied the fair(wiki:Jonquil.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Jonquil bathing with her sisters in a sweetwater pool.(wiki:Jonquil.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Jonquil fell in love with Florian. [Jonquil's Pool](wiki:Jonquil's_Pool) is now a bathhouse said to have healing properties in the town of [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool),(wiki:Jonquil.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Jonquil.cite_ref-Ragot44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and the town's castle contains [Jonquil's Tower](wiki:Jonquil's_Tower).(wiki:Jonquil.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

There are songs about Florian and Jonquil; "[Six Maids in a Pool](wiki:Six_Maids_in_a_Pool)" may be one of them.(wiki:Jonquil.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Singers compared the sudden marriage of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) to the great romance of Jonquil and [Florian the Fool](wiki:Florian_the_Fool).(wiki:Jonquil.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

During the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow), Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) observed [Tanselle](wiki:Tanselle) performing a puppet show of Florian and Jonquil.(wiki:Jonquil.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Songs about Florian and Jonquil are favorites of [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark).(wiki:Jonquil.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Imprisoned in [Maegor's Holdfast](wiki:Maegor's_Holdfast), [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) reads a favorite book of legends, including Jonquil and [Florian the Fool](wiki:Florian_the_Fool).(wiki:Jonquil.cite_ref-Ragot51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Ser [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard) claims to be Florian in his promise to get Sansa out of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing),(wiki:Jonquil.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) and he calls the girl his Jonquil.(wiki:Jonquil.cite_ref-Racok52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

There is a famous song about Florian and Jonquil which Sansa knows. She offers to sing it to [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) but he declines.(wiki:Jonquil.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), a harper sings beautiful, sad songs, including one about Jonquil, in the Queen's Ballroom at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Jonquil.cite_ref-Racok60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Sandor demands the unnamed song about Florian and Jonquil when he visits Sansa's chamber, but Sansa sings him the [Mother's](wiki:Mother_(the_Seven)) hymn instead.(wiki:Jonquil.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Imprisoned in the [sky cells](wiki:Sky_cell) of the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), [Marillion](wiki:Marillion) sings of Jonquil and other sad songs.(wiki:Jonquil.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
