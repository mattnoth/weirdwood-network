## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Joss is one of the witnesses who has seen the raiders from the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) when they destroyed his town. Together with other survivors, he is sent to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to describe the events before the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Joss.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
