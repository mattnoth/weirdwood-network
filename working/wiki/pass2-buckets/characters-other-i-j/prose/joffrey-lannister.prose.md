## Origins

Ser Joffrey, a knight of [House Lydden](wiki:House_Lydden), was married to the only daughter of King [Gerold III Lannister](wiki:Gerold_III_Lannister), who was of [First Men](wiki:First_Men) ancestry. When the king died without male issue, Joffrey was crowned by a council and took the name of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Joffrey_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
