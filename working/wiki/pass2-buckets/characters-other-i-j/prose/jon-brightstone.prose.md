## Origins

Jon paid an [Andal](wiki:Andal) warlord to cross the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) in order to use his swords against [Dywen Shell](wiki:Dywen_Shell), who also contested control of the [Fingers](wiki:Fingers). The warlord eventually turned against Jon, however, and the king was tortured and beheaded. An Andal knight named [Corwyn Corbray](wiki:Corwyn_Corbray_(lord)) took Jon's daughter for his bride.(wiki:Jon_Brightstone.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
