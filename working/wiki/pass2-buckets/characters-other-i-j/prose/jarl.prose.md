## Origins

No more than twenty years old by [299 AC](wiki:299_AC), Jarl has been a raider for eight years, having gone over the [Wall](wiki:Wall) with the [Weeper](wiki:Weeper) and [Alfyn Crowkiller](wiki:Alfyn_Crowkiller). He has led his own band of raiders.(wiki:Jarl.cite_ref-Rasos26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Appearances & Description

Jarl is a dark and fierce young man. He is in a relationship with [Val](wiki:Val).(wiki:Jarl.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Jarl is present in [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder)'s tent when [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) is presented to the [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall).(wiki:Jarl.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Mance tasks him, together with [Styr](wiki:Styr), to scale the [Wall](wiki:Wall) and take [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) from the rear.(wiki:Jarl.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Styr has a hundred [Thenns](wiki:Thenns), five times as many raiders as Jarl.(wiki:Jarl.cite_ref-Rasos26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Jarl and Styr share the command, although Styr, who commands significantly more men than Jarl, resents this and often acts as the sole commander. As they travel to the Wall, Jarl keeps a suspicious eye on Jon, who accompanies him. Together with Styr, Jarl questions Jon about the patrols of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) on the Wall. Jarl specifically asks whether [Stonedoor](wiki:Stonedoor) and [Greyguard](wiki:Greyguard) are presently manned, and is quick to realize when Jon lies about the number of men stationed at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea), and the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower).(wiki:Jarl.cite_ref-Rasos26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Jarl divides his eleven men into three teams of four, being the twelfth climber himself, and leads his men up the Wall near Greyguard. During the sixth hour of their climb, Jarl and his group of three fall to their deaths when a large chunk of ice breaks off. Jarl's body is later found impaled on a tree.(wiki:Jarl.cite_ref-Rasos30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

When Jon is sent to Mance's tent amid the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), he tells Jarl's lover [Val](wiki:Val) that he is sorry for the raider's death.(wiki:Jarl.cite_ref-Rasos76.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Before entering the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest) in search of [Tormund Giantsbane](wiki:Tormund_Giantsbane), Val asks Jon about the manner of Jarl's death. The new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) explains that the Wall killed Jarl, not Jon.(wiki:Jarl.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
