## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Jommo is in [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak) at the same time as Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo). He has four wives. He was part of the procession through Vaes Dothrak that proclaims [Rhaego](wiki:Rhaego) as the [stallion who mounts the world](wiki:Stallion_who_mounts_the_world).(wiki:Jommo.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

### A Feast for Crows
