## Origins

A boy from [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle), Jace is a cobbler's son. He is one of the latest recruits of the Night's Watch brought to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) by [Conwy](wiki:Conwy).(wiki:Jace_(Night's_Watch).cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Jace trains with fellow recruits [Arron](wiki:Arron) and [Emrick](wiki:Emrick) under the tutelage of [Iron Emmett](wiki:Iron_Emmett). He is chosen by Emmett as one of his best three students when Lord Commander [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) joins the sparring session.
