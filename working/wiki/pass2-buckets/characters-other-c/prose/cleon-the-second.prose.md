## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Cleon succeeds [the Butcher King](wiki:Cleon) as ruler of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor), after the latter's death.(wiki:Cleon_the_Second.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) His reign lasts eight days, before his throat is cut. His killer claims Cleon's throne, and quickly becomes known as [King Cutthroat](wiki:King_Cutthroat). At the same time, Cleon's concubine, who becomes known as [Queen Whore](wiki:Queen_Whore), also claims his crown.(wiki:Cleon_the_Second.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Cleon_the_Second.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
