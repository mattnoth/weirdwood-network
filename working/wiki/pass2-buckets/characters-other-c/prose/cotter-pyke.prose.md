## Origins

Cotter is the [bastard](wiki:Bastardy) son of a tavern wench from the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands).(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) According to Ser [Denys Mallister](wiki:Denys_Mallister), Cotter has been violent since his childhood.(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7) He is a veteran of many battles.(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Cotter Pyke](wiki:Category:Images_of_Cotter_Pyke)*

Cotter has small, close-set eyes and a broken nose. He has a widow's peak and a sparse, rough beard to cover his [pox](wiki:Pox)-scarred face. Cotter is not handsome, but he is lean, hard, and wiry.(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Cotter wears a studded brigantine and roughspun breeches. He is more comfortable fighting at sea than on land.(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Cotter is illiterate, and Maester [Harmune](wiki:Harmune) must read and write his letters.(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) He is considered rough of speech and bold.(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Cotter enjoys [dicing](wiki:Dicing).(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

The [baseborn](wiki:Baseborn) Cotter is not impressed by nobles. He thinks of Ser [Denys Mallister](wiki:Denys_Mallister), commander of the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower), as pompous and does not consider him to be a warrior. Denys, meanwhile, considers Cotter to be uncouth and uneducated.(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) finds Cotter to be more intimidating than Denys.(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), assigns [Dareon](wiki:Dareon) to [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea) to help Cotter with visiting merchants.(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Cotter writes to Jeor that [elk](wiki:Elk) herds have been streaming toward the [Shivering Sea](wiki:Shivering_Sea) and [Bay of Seals](wiki:Bay_of_Seals), and that [mammoths](wiki:Mammoth) and [large footprints](wiki:Giants) have also been spotted near Eastwatch.(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Cotter captures [Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt) at the [Long Barrow](wiki:Long_Barrow).(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) When [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) and Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne) have [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) arrested, Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sends a letter to inform Cotter.(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

When [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) lands his men at Eastwatch, Cotter leads him and his men along hidden paths(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) which allow Stannis to take the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) unaware during the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall).(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

Cotter attends the [choosing](wiki:Choosing) for the next Lord Commander to replace Jeor, who was slain in the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep). He is vocally opposed to King Stannis naming the next lord commander, asking if he is to wipe their arses as well. Cotter is a leading candidate, along with Ser [Denys Mallister](wiki:Denys_Mallister) of the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower), collecting one hundred and eighty-seven votes by the time only seven candidates remain in the race.(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Because the support between Cotter and Denys is divided, Janos Slynt appears as a potential alternative. [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) meets with Cotter in the [Shieldhall](wiki:Shieldhall) and with Denys in the [Lance](wiki:Lance_(tower)). Each commander expresses hatred for their rival, with Cotter claiming he is only a candidate to prevent Denys from winning, but both agree that Janos would be a worse choice. Cotter also disapproves of the candidacies of [Othell Yarwyck](wiki:Othell_Yarwyck), [First Builder](wiki:First_Builder), and [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh), [Lord Steward](wiki:Lord_Steward). Sam then lies to Cotter and Denys that Stannis plans to name the other as Lord Commander, and he successfully persuades each to support [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) instead.(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-9)

### A Dance with Dragons

Cotter hosts Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) at Eastwatch,(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and he informs Jon that Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) and [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan) have sailed for [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor).(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Cotter is aware that Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) is building war galleys.(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) Cotter requests reinforcements from Jon, but he dislikes the wildlings sent by Jon to help. Eastwatch galleys spot increasing numbers of wildlings near the shore.(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Captaining *[Talon](wiki:Talon)*, Cotter sails from Eastwatch towards [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome) with eleven ships by the command of Lord Commander Snow.(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) He is to rescue the wildlings brought there by the vision of the woods witch [Mother Mole](wiki:Mother_Mole), who saw in her vision that ships would come to take the wildlings south.(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Eastwatch is commanded by Ser [Glendon Hewett](wiki:Glendon_Hewett) in Cotter's absence.(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

By the time Cotter arrives at Hardhome, he has lost five ships and the wildlings are eating their own dead. The undead are lurking in the woods and in the waters. Cotter sends a raven to Jon Snow, pleading him to send help by land, since the weather was harsh on the seas.(wiki:Cotter_Pyke.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
