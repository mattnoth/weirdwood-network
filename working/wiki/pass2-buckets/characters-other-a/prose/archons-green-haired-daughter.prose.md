## Origins

[Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), planned to send his young daughter, [Arianne](wiki:Arianne_Martell), to [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) to serve as a [cupbearer](wiki:Cupbearer) to the [Archon of Tyrosh](wiki:Archon_of_Tyrosh), and to [secretly meet with](wiki:Secret_marriage_pact) her [betrothed](wiki:Betrothal), Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen). The Archon's green-haired daughter was sent to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) in exchange for Arianne. However, Doran abandoned the plan due to suicidal threats from Arianne's mother [Mellario](wiki:Mellario), who was already deeply upset that her son [Quentyn](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) had been sent away to be [fostered](wiki:Fostered).(wiki:Archon's_green-haired_daughter.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Arianne was left ignorant of the plan and of her secret betrothal, and played with the Archon's green-haired daughter in the pools of the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens), though she did not know the girl's parentage. When they played the shoulder-riding game, if Arianne rode the shoulders of her friend [Garin](wiki:Garin_(orphan)), nobody could defeat them, not even [Nymeria Sand](wiki:Nymeria_Sand) and the Archon's green-haired daughter.(wiki:Archon's_green-haired_daughter.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Appearances & Description

The Archon's daughter has [dyed](wiki:Hair_dye) green hair. As a child, she was good at the [shoulder-riding game](wiki:Shoulder-riding_game) in the pools of the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens).(wiki:Archon's_green-haired_daughter.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell) reminisces to his daughter [Arianne](wiki:Arianne_Martell) about how she used to play in the pools of the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens) when she was a child. When Arianne mentions she used to play with a [green-haired](wiki:Hair_dye) [Tyroshi](wiki:Tyrosh) girl, Doran says that she was the [Archon's](wiki:Archon_of_Tyrosh) daughter, and that Arianne was supposed to have been sent to Tyrosh in her place, to meet with her betrothed. Arianne, confused, asks if her missing brother [Quentyn](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) has gone to Tyrosh to court the Archon's daughter. Doran then confesses the truth of the [secret marriage pact](wiki:Secret_marriage_pact) between Arianne and [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen); and that due to Viserys's death, Quentyn has gone to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay) to court [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen).(wiki:Archon's_green-haired_daughter.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
