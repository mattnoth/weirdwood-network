## Origins

Arlan married one of the daughters of Lord [Roderick Blackwood](wiki:Roderick_Blackwood), wedding her beneath [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall)'s great [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood). When Roderick rebelled against King [Humfrey I Teague](wiki:Humfrey_I_Teague), Arlan supported Roderick and intended to restore the riverlands to the Blackwoods. Arlan's [stormlanders](wiki:Stormlanders) crushed the Teagues in the [Battle of Six Kings](wiki:Battle_of_Six_Kings), but Roderick was slain in battle. Roderick's heir was a boy of only eight and Arlan distrusted Roderick's brothers. He suggested crowning Roderik's eldest child, [Shiera Blackwood](wiki:Shiera_Blackwood), who was wed to his own son, but the riverlords rejected this suggestion. Arlan then decided to add the riverlands to the realm of the Storm Kings.(wiki:Arlan_III_Durrandon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Arlan was able to plant the banner of the Durrandons on the shores of the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea). Arlan's successors were not able to hold his gains, however.(wiki:Arlan_III_Durrandon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
