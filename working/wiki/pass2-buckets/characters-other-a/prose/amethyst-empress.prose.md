## Origins

She succeeded her father, the [Opal Emperor](wiki:Opal_Emperor) but was cast down and slain by her envious younger brother, who crowned himself the [Bloodstone Emperor](wiki:Bloodstone_Emperor). According to the annals of the [Further East](wiki:Further_East), it was the [Blood Betrayal](wiki:Blood_Betrayal), as his usurpation was named, that ushered in the [Long Night](wiki:Long_Night).(wiki:Amethyst_Empress.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
