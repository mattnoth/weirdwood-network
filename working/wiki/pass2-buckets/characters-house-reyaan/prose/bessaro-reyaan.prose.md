## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter

Prior to [the Gate](wiki:The_Gate)'s performance of *[The Bloody Hand](wiki:The_Bloody_Hand)* [Mercy](wiki:Arya_Stark) and [Daena](wiki:Daena_(mummer)) peek out into the house and see Bessaro in the audience. Mercy giggles that Bessaro is so fat you ought to count him twice. The keyholder is so big that he needs a special seat, three times the size of a common chair. Daena notes that all Reyaans are fat.(wiki:Bessaro_Reyaan.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Bmercy_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Cmercy.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
