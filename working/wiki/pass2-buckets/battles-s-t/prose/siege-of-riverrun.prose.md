## Origins

[Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr) tells [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) he has seen a vision in his flames of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) besieged by [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

After the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) and the death of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident), the river lords offer terms to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) and support for the [Stark](wiki:House_Stark)-[Tully](wiki:House_Tully) kingdom slips away. House Lannister, supported by their new allies, [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey), besiege Riverrun in an effort to remove the final vestiges of resistance in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) and install Ser [Emmon Frey](wiki:Emmon_Frey) as the new Lord of Riverrun, as the terms of the alliance between the Freys and Lannisters dictate. Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister), the new [Warden of the West](wiki:Warden_of_the_West), is sent to command the siege.(wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) Ser [Ryman Frey](wiki:Ryman_Frey) marches two thousand Freys to Riverrun.(wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully), Robb's [Warden of the Southern Marches](wiki:Warden_of_the_Southern_Marches), anticipates the siege. He gathers all the foodstuffs and goods he can within the castle, expels all the unnecessary mouths, and prepares for a long siege.(wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-13)

## Aftermath

Edmure is sent to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), accompanied by [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling), the wife and queen of the late [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), who had been within Riverrun when it was surrendered. Brynden escapes, presumably swimming down the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork). The Tully garrison is disarmed and released, although Ser [Desmond Grell](wiki:Desmond_Grell) and Ser [Robin Ryger](wiki:Robin_Ryger) choose to [take the black](wiki:Take_the_black). Ryman is found hanged near [Fairmarket](wiki:Fairmarket), his attackers unknown, though his son [Edwyn](wiki:Edwyn_Frey) has several ideas as to who was behind his father's murder. [House Frey of Riverrun](wiki:House_Frey_of_Riverrun) is established and Jaime sets out to end the [siege of Raventree](wiki:Siege_of_Raventree), seat of [House Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood), by [House Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken).(wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)
