## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Garrison is among the lords of the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) who escort the corpse of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) back to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock). He wears the [ermine](wiki:Ermine) of [House Prester](wiki:House_Prester) with a cloak of crimson [silk](wiki:Silk) in honor of Tywin.(wiki:Garrison_Prester.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
