## Origins

When [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) first named himself [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands) at the [Grey King's Hall](wiki:Grey_King's_Hall), Tarle the Thrice-Drowned placed a driftwood crown upon his head.(wiki:Tarle_the_Thrice-Drowned.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

[Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) informs her uncle, [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy), that Tarle has been overheard saying that Lord [Maron Volmark](wiki:Maron_Volmark) is the true heir to the "[black line](wiki:House_Hoare)".(wiki:Tarle_the_Thrice-Drowned.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
