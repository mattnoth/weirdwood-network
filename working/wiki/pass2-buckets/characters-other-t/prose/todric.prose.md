## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Todric participates in the [harrying of the Stony Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore) under the command of [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy). After they ambush the [Wild Hares](wiki:Wild_Hares), Todric clads himself in a blood-stained cloak of white foxfur taken from a dead [northman](wiki:Northman). Theon has two rules, no drinking and no fighting over the plunder. Todric fails to obey both, drunkenly fighting with one of [Maron Botley](wiki:Maron_Botley)'s sons. Theon tries to shoot an arrow into Todric's drinking hand, but hits his belly when Todric lurches. Theon orders the Botleys to silence him, which they do while stripping the corpse of anything of value.(wiki:Todric.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
