## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Tim is part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) that leaves [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) and the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) in search of [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark) and [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder). He survives the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist).(wiki:Tim_Stone.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

Tim stays loyal to the Watch during the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep) and is one of the few survivors of the ranging to make it all the way back to Castle Black.(wiki:Tim_Stone.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
