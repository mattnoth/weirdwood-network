## Origins

Ser Garibald led a group of [rivermen](wiki:Rivermen) in the [Battle by the Lakeshore](wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore).(wiki:Garibald_Grey.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Garibald refused the challenge of Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) before the [Butcher's Ball](wiki:Butcher's_Ball) began, and Criston was instead slain by arrows.(wiki:Garibald_Grey.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

During the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton), Garibald commanded six thousand [blacks](wiki:Blacks) who faced the army of Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower). Garibald's men were forced to retreat after a rain of arrows and a cavalry charge, however. When the [Two Betrayers](wiki:Two_Betrayers) began attacking the blacks, Garibald was struck by a crossbow bolt and then burned by the [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame) of [Tessarion](wiki:Tessarion).(wiki:Garibald_Grey.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
