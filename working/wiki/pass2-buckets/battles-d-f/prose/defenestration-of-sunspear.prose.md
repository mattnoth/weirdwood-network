## Origins

Soon after the [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen) returned to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), the Dornish swarmed from the [shadow city](wiki:Sunspear) to retake Sunspear. Lord [Jon Rosby](wiki:Jon_Rosby), who had been named [Castellan](wiki:Castellan) of Sunspear and [Warden of the Sands](wiki:Warden_of_the_Sands) by King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), was bound hand and foot, dragged to the top of the Spear Tower and defenestrated from a window by none other than the blind [Princess](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne) [Meria Martell](wiki:Meria_Martell) herself.(wiki:Defenestration_of_Sunspear.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_dorne:_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)(wiki:Defenestration_of_Sunspear.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon:_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
