## Origins

After the [battle at the Mummer's Ford](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford), Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) leads his 20,000 men northeast toward the [ruby ford](wiki:Ruby_ford). Tywin's son, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), captures Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) in a [battle under the walls of Riverrun](wiki:Battle_under_the_walls_of_Riverrun). [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood), [Lord of Raventree Hall](wiki:Lord_of_Raventree_Hall), holds [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) while it is besieged by Jaime.(wiki:Fall_of_Raventree_Hall.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Aftermath

Tywin leaves Raventree under the control of a Lannister garrison. Later, after [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s victories at the [Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood) and the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps) and Tywin's retreat to [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Raventree Hall is [liberated](wiki:Liberation_of_Raventree_Hall) by Lord Tytos.(wiki:Fall_of_Raventree_Hall.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
