## Origins

[Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands) and [Lord Reaper of Pyke](wiki:Lord_Reaper_of_Pyke), seized [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Fair_Isle_rebellion.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

In the wake of Dalton's death in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), many [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) captains raced back to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands), as a [struggle for succession](wiki:Struggle_for_power_in_the_Iron_Islands) was inevitable due to the Red Kraken's only heirs being [salt sons](wiki:Salt_son). The [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) of the isle and the few [knights](wiki:Knight) who remained on the island rose in rebellion against their ironmen oppressors. In the span of three days, hundreds of reavers were hacked to death or set upon on the docks, with their [ships](wiki:Watercraft) set ablaze.(wiki:Fair_Isle_rebellion.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Ultimately, only [Faircastle](wiki:Faircastle) remained in the hands of the ironborn, with the garrison at the castle mostly composed of close companions of the late Dalton, led by [Alester Wynch](wiki:Alester_Wynch) and [Gunthor Goodbrother](wiki:Gunthor_Goodbrother). The former was eventually killed by the latter in a quarrel over [Lysa Farman](wiki:Lysa_Farman), one of the [salt widows](wiki:Salt_wife) of the Red Kraken.(wiki:Fair_Isle_rebellion.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

By the time Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) arrived in the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) with the [Velaryon fleet](wiki:Velaryon_fleet), Fair Isle was already free with the surviving ironborn having fled back to the Iron Islands in their longships.(wiki:Fair_Isle_rebellion.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
