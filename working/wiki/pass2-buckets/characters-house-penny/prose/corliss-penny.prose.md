## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Ser Corliss is sworn to King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon). He is among the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) who go north to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) with Stannis.(wiki:Corliss_Penny.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

After the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), Corliss commands the guards when [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) are allowed to pass through the Wall to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black). Those who cross are required to throw pieces of [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) into a fire. After a belligerent wilding is killed by [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men), Corliss tosses the body into the fire pit.(wiki:Corliss_Penny.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

When a blizzard slows Stannis's army during the [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell), a fearful Corliss blames it on the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods) of the [north](wiki:North). As conditions worsen, Ser [Richard Horpe](wiki:Richard_Horpe) tells Corliss that the weakest men are beyond saving. [Hugo Wull](wiki:Hugo_Wull) is dismissive of the storm, however, and Corliss is incredulous at the [clan chief](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans)'s willingness to die in battle.(wiki:Corliss_Penny.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Stannis's host becomes snowbound at a [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village), with Corliss blaming the storm on the [northmen](wiki:Northmen) and their [weirwoods](wiki:Weirwood). Four [Peasebury](wiki:House_Peasebury) men are caught committing [cannibalism](wiki:Cannibalism), so they are to be [sacrificed](wiki:Blood_sacrifice) to [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor). One of the condemned, a serjeant desiring a quicker death, insults the queen's men, thereby goading Ser [Clayton Suggs](wiki:Clayton_Suggs) into cutting his throat. Corliss then lights the pyres with his torch, burning the three other condemned men and the body of the serjeant.(wiki:Corliss_Penny.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Corliss wants Stannis to sacrifice [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy).(wiki:Corliss_Penny.cite_ref-6)
