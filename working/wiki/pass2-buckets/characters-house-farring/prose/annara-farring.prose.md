## Origins

Though invited to the wedding of Annara and Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey), Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully) of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) did not attend.(wiki:Annara_Farring.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Rumors around the [Twins](wiki:Twins) claim that Annara may have been sleeping with Walder's great-grandson, [Black Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Ryman)), casting doubts on her children's parentage.(wiki:Annara_Farring.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
