## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter

Andrew attends the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) to select the members of the newly-created [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights). During the feast, Andrew is among the young knights who dance with [Alayne Stone](wiki:Sansa_Stark).(wiki:Andrew_Tollett.cite_ref-Winds_1-2)
