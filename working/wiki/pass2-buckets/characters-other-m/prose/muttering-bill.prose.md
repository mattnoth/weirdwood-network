### A Feast for Crows

Muttering Bill remains at [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep) after the [betrayal of its owner](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep), along with a few other former members of the Night's Watch.(wiki:Muttering_Bill.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Dance With Dragon

Five betrayers wander in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest), planning to head south over the [Wall](wiki:Wall). However, the traitors are killed by [Coldhands](wiki:Coldhands) and his ravens. [Summer](wiki:Summer), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark)'s [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), finds the bodies and eats parts of the meat.(wiki:Muttering_Bill.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) It is unknown if Bill is among them.
