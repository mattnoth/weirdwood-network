## Appearances & Description

Mully has greasy orange hair(wiki:Mully.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and a tangled orange beard.(wiki:Mully.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) thinks of Mully as old on one occasion(wiki:Mully.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) and as being in his prime on another.(wiki:Mully.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Mully is one of the brothers left behind by [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) to defend [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black). From the roof of the [Flint Barracks](wiki:Flint_Barracks), he shoots an arrow into the leg of [Big Boil](wiki:Big_Boil) during the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black).(wiki:Mully.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Mully is sent by [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), acting commander of Castle Black, to find out why [Zei](wiki:Zei) has not yet returned from a mission to request help from [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town), and he finds the village and its [brothel](wiki:Mole's_Town_brothel) deserted.(wiki:Mully.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) When [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) try to move the bodies of dead [giants](wiki:Giants) from before the gate, Mully and [Horse](wiki:Hareth_(Mole's_Town)) drops rocks on them from above.(wiki:Mully.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Mully and [Owen the Oaf](wiki:Owen_the_Oaf) help Jon push a barrel to destroy the wildlings' [turtle](wiki:Turtle_(siege_engine)).(wiki:Mully.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

Mully congratulates Jon after he is chosen [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch).(wiki:Mully.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Mully stands guard outside of the armory of Castle Black.(wiki:Mully.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Mully is one of the men brought along as muscle to assist Jon in escorting [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) out of Castle Black to carry out his duties as garrison commander of [Greyguard](wiki:Greyguard).(wiki:Mully.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Mully is among the black brothers who gather at the gate of Castle Black after word spreads that the heads of three rangers were found impaled on spears outside the gate on the north side of the Wall. When Jon and his party return through the gate, Mully is informed that [Jack Bulwer](wiki:Jack_Bulwer), [Hairy Hal](wiki:Hairy_Hal), and [Garth Greyfeather](wiki:Garth_Greyfeather) have been slain by the [Weeper](wiki:Weeper).(wiki:Mully.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Mully and [Eddison Tollett](wiki:Eddison_Tollett) escort [Val](wiki:Val) to the gate through the Wall and provide her with a horse on the orders of Jon, who wants Val to find [Tormund Giantsbane](wiki:Tormund_Giantsbane) and offer him and his people sanctuary behind the Wall in exchange for fighting the [Others](wiki:Others) when they attack. As Val rides off, Mully raises his concerns about what the other men of the Night's Watch will say, but Jon brushes off his concerns, noting that many of those who would doubt his motives already think him to be half-wildling anyway.(wiki:Mully.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

When [Ty](wiki:Ty) and [Dannel](wiki:Dannel) return to Castle Black with [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark), whom they found on the road riding to Castle Black seeking Jon, Mully wakes the Lord Commander and informs him of the situation before escorting him to the girl.(wiki:Mully.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Wearing his best attire, Mully attends [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark) of Alys to [Sigorn](wiki:Sigorn), the [Magnar](wiki:Magnar) of [Thenn](wiki:Thenn_(valley)), and he complains of the cold. The steward eagerly attends the wedding feast prepared by [Three-Finger Hobb](wiki:Three-Finger_Hobb). When two horn blasts are heard coming from the Wall, Mully listens nervously with the other wedding guests, and is relieved when no third blast is heard.(wiki:Mully.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

When Tormund leads his band of wildlings to the Wall after negotiating a peace with the Night's Watch, Mully is a member of the honor guard which protects Jon as he oversees their passage through the Wall.(wiki:Mully.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Some time later, an angry [Ghost](wiki:Ghost) bites at Mully, but Jon attributes his agitation to the [boar](wiki:Boar) of [Borroq](wiki:Borroq), a [skinchanger](wiki:Skinchanger).(wiki:Mully.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

After Jon is informed of [the letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) from [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), Jon has Mully and [Satin](wiki:Satin) escort [Clydas](wiki:Clydas) back to his chambers. [Fulk the Flea](wiki:Fulk_the_Flea) and Mully stand guard when Jon addresses the [Shieldhall](wiki:Shieldhall), and the two guards are replaced with Horse and Rory at the changing of the watch.(wiki:Mully.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)
