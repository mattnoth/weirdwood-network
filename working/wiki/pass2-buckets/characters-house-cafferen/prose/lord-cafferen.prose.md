## Origins

During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Lord Cafferen, faithful to [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen), was captured by his liege lord, [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), in the [battles at Summerhall](wiki:Battles_at_Summerhall). He became a loyal friend of Robert until he was slain by Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) at the [battle of Ashford](wiki:Battle_of_Ashford).
