## Origins

Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly)'s son (and [Leona Woolfield](wiki:Leona_Woolfield)'s husband) [Wylis Manderly](wiki:Wylis_Manderly) is being held captive by the [Lannisters](wiki:Lannister). The Lord's other son, [Wendel Manderly](wiki:Wendel_Manderly), was killed at the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), but three Freys have returned his bones and attend Wyman's court.

## Narrative Arc

[Davos](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) is held for eighteen days in a large, airy, handsomely furnished chamber with guards at the door and a view of the city's streets and the harbor in the [New Castle](wiki:New_Castle) at [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor). The *[Merry Midwife](wiki:Merry_Midwife)* left after four days.

Ser [Marlon Manderly](wiki:Marlon_Manderly) takes Davos to treat with Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) and his [Merman's Court](wiki:Merman's_Court). Davos asks Lord Wyman to ally with King [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).

He is resisted, however, by envoys of [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey) ([Rhaegar Frey](wiki:Rhaegar_Frey), Ser [Jared Frey](wiki:Jared_Frey) and [Symond Frey](wiki:Symond_Frey)), who have come with the remains of one of Lord Wyman Manderly's sons, [Wendel Manderly](wiki:Wendel_Manderly). The Frey envoys have lied to Wyman, saying that Robb Stark transformed into a wolf at the Red Wedding, and killed Wendel and several Freys. Davos expresses skepticism of the Freys’ claim. Ser Jared challenges Davos to a fight after the accusation, but Wyman prevents violence. The Freys continue to besmirch Robb's name, and although Wyman and the other non-Freys appear to disagree with their words, they do not prevent them from speaking.

A young girl, [Wylla Manderly](wiki:Wylla_Manderly), Lord Wyman’s granddaughter, vocally supports Stannis, and appears to sway the court. However, Lord Manderly calls for Davos’ head and has Davos dragged off to prison in the [Wolf's Den](wiki:Wolf's_Den) as a traitor in the presence of the Freys.
