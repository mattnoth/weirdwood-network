## Origins

Theo was a [landed knight](wiki:Landed_knight) during the reign of King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen). He was married to [Elinor Costayne](wiki:Elinor_Costayne), with who he had three sons. Ser Theo participated in one of King Maegor I's [campaigns](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising) against the [Poor Fellows](wiki:Poor_Fellows). In [47 AC](wiki:47_AC), Theo was arrested by knights of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) and accused of conspiring with Dowager Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) to place Prince [Jaehaerys Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) on the throne. Although he declared his innocence, Theo was found guilty and beheaded that same day.(wiki:Theo_Bolling.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)(wiki:Theo_Bolling.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Seven days after Theo's execution, King Maegor I announced that he would wed Theo's widow, Lady Elinor. After the wedding, Maegor dispatched one of Theo's sons to be fostered at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) by [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) and the other at [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) by Lord [Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell), while the youngest was given to a [wetnurse](wiki:Wetnurse).(wiki:Theo_Bolling.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)
