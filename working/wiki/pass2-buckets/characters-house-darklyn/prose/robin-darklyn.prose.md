## Origins

Robin was chosen by Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) to be one of the first [knights](wiki:Knight) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) when the order was founded in [10 AC](wiki:10_AC) by King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Robin_Darklyn.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Robin_Darklyn.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Robin was the first of the eventual seven Darklyns who served in the Kingsguard.(wiki:Robin_Darklyn.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Appearances & Description

Ser Robin is remembered for being quick, strong, observant, and famed for his prowess with [sword](wiki:Sword) and shield. He was devoted to his king.(wiki:Robin_Darklyn.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
