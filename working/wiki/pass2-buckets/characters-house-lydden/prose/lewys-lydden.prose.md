## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lewys is part of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s army invading the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), riding alongside Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) in the center of Tywin's army during the [battle of the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_of_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:Lewys_Lydden.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Lewys is with Lord Tywin's army when they [take Harrenhal](wiki:Yielding_of_Harrenhal). During their time there, one of Lewys's archers is hanged on Tywin's orders for starting an argument over the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions)'s inability to kill [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) which led to a fight with some Lannister men.(wiki:Lewys_Lydden.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Lewys is among the lords present for King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_I_Baratheon)'s first audience after the [battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Lewys_Lydden.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
