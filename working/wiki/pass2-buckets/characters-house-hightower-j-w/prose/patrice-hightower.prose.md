## Origins

Patrice is considered one of the suspects in the death of the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aenys_I)) in [43 AC](wiki:43_AC),(wiki:Patrice_Hightower.cite_ref-3) as she had sought an audience with His High Holiness at the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) on the day of his death. However, he was known to be alive when she departed. According to Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel), the suspicion only rests in the belief that [poison](wiki:Poison) is a woman's weapon.(wiki:Patrice_Hightower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Patrice_Hightower.cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
