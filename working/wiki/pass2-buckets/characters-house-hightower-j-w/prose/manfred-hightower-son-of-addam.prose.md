## Origins

In [23 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), when Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) suggested a betrothal between her son [Maegor](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) and her newborn niece [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)), the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aegon_I)) objected to the match and suggested instead his own niece, Manfred's daughter [Ceryse](wiki:Ceryse_Hightower).(wiki:Manfred_Hightower_(son_of_Addam).cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) She and Maegor were wed in [25 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Manfred_Hightower_(son_of_Addam).cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

In [39 AC](wiki:39_AC), when Maegor took a second bride by wedding [Alys Harroway](wiki:Alys_Harroway), Lord Manfred made protest to King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen), demanding that Lady Alys be put aside.(wiki:Manfred_Hightower_(son_of_Addam).cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)

Manfred died in [41 AC](wiki:41_AC) and was succeeded by his son [Martyn](wiki:Martyn_Hightower).(wiki:Manfred_Hightower_(son_of_Addam).cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8)
