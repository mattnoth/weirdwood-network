## Origins

Martyn was born to Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower) and his first wife, who died in childbed at some point. He had an elder brother, [Lyonel](wiki:Lyonel_Hightower), a younger brother, [Garmund](wiki:Garmund_Hightower), and a sister, [Bethany](wiki:Bethany_Hightower).
