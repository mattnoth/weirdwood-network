## Origins

When Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) was a boy, the [grey plague](wiki:Grey_plague) hit [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). Lord Quenton had the city gates closed, burned every [ship](wiki:Watercraft) in the port, and ordered that anyone trying to flee was to be killed. Once the plague burned itself out, Quenton rescinded those orders. On the day he did so, however, he and his young son were pulled from their [horses](wiki:Horse) by the survivors and had their throats slit.(wiki:Quenton_Hightower.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
