## Origins

In [56 AC](wiki:56_AC), Norman and his brother, Ser [Eustace](wiki:Eustace_Hightower), accompanied Lady [Elissa Farman](wiki:Elissa_Farman), under the alias Alys Westhill, on her voyage to discover new lands. Lord [Donnel Hightower](wiki:Donnel_Hightower) received a [raven](wiki:Raven) from the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) that Elissa was to be arrested and brought back to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in chains, but by the time Donnel acted, Norman and his brother had already set sail with her.(wiki:Norman_Hightower.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Sailing west and south of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) into the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea), the expedition began suffering from storms during their second week of sailing. Norman's *[Autumn Moon](wiki:Autumn_Moon)* foundered during one storm and sank under a wave. Some of the men on Eustace's ship, *[Lady Meredith](wiki:Lady_Meredith)*, claimed that a [kraken](wiki:Kraken) had brought down Norman's ship.(wiki:Norman_Hightower.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
