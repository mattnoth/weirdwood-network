## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) recalls that Richard was a member of the host gathered by [Renly I](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) beneath the walls of [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden). There, he joined a wager as to who could take the maidenhead of Brienne. In his efforts to woo her he played love songs on his [lute](wiki:Lute) outside her pavilion.(wiki:Richard_Farrow.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) After the bet was discovered and ended, Brienne sought out and defeated Richard during the [melee at Bitterbridge](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge).(wiki:Richard_Farrow.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He was killed at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Richard_Farrow.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
