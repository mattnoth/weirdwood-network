## Origins

Thirty years before the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Ser Wynton came within a dozen votes of being named [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch).(wiki:Wynton_Stout.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He has been a ranger in the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) for nearly eighty years.(wiki:Wynton_Stout.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) His mental state has significantly deteriorated within the last ten years.(wiki:Wynton_Stout.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Wynton is among those left behind at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) when Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) goes [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) in search of [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark) and [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder).(wiki:Wynton_Stout.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) As the last knight in the castle, he is made castellan of Castle Black by Lord Steward [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) when Bowen leaves to investigate reports of [wildling](wiki:Free_folk) incursions. Wynton seems to have forgotten his appointment, however, and it is the [smith](wiki:Smith) [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye) and later [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) who command the defense against the wildling assaults on [Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) and the [gates of the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall).(wiki:Wynton_Stout.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Wynton_Stout.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
