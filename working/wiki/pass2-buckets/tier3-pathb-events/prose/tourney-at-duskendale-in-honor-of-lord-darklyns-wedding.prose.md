## Origins

The [tourney](wiki:Tourney) celebrated the marriage of [Lord Darklyn](wiki:House_Darklyn) to a daughter of Lord [Theomore Manderly](wiki:Theomore_Manderly). The tourney included a squire's melee in which Prince [Aemon Targaryen](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) was the victor, defeating his younger brother Prince [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)). Aemon also distinguished himself in the lists, and he was subsequently [knighted](wiki:Knight) for his jousting skills. After returning to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), the newly-made knight took to the skies as a [dragonrider](wiki:Dragonrider) on [Caraxes](wiki:Caraxes).(wiki:Tourney_at_Duskendale_in_honor_of_Lord_Darklyn's_wedding.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
