## Origins

The [Opal Emperor](wiki:Opal_Emperor) was the seventh ruler of a line that began with the [God-on-Earth](wiki:God-on-Earth), the son of the [Maiden-Made-of-Light](wiki:Maiden-Made-of-Light) and the [Lion of Night](wiki:Lion_of_Night). He was succeeded by his daughter, the [Amethyst Empress](wiki:Amethyst_Empress), but her younger brother, envious, cast her down and [slew her](wiki:Kinslaying), taking the crown and proclaiming himself the [Bloodstone Emperor](wiki:Bloodstone_Emperor). He turned his back on the gods of Yi Ti and began worshiping a [black stone](wiki:Black_stone) that had fallen from the skies, took a [tiger-woman](wiki:Tiger-men) as wife, practiced [cannibalism](wiki:Cannibalism) and [necromancy](wiki:Necromancy), and [enslaved](wiki:Slavery) his own people. Horrified at the Blood Betrayal, the Maiden-Made-of-Light turned her back upon the world and the [Lion of Night](wiki:Lion_of_Night) came forth in all his fury to punish the wickedness of man.(wiki:Blood_Betrayal.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
