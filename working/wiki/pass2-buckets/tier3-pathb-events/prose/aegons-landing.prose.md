## Origins

[Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), [Lord of Dragonstone](wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone), and his sisters, [Visenya](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) and [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen), met with their allies at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), and on the seventh day [ravens](wiki:Raven) were sent through the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) announcing that henceforth there would be only one king in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), Aegon.(wiki:Aegon's_Landing.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The three siblings prayed in the [sept at Dragonstone](wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone) the night before they sailed for the mainland.(wiki:Aegon's_Landing.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Aegon's_Landing.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
The Targaryens sailed from the [isle of Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)) with a host said to have numbered between some hundreds and three thousand men. They arrived at the undefended mouth of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) with the intention of conquering the continent.(wiki:Aegon's_Landing.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Aegon landed on the same day that [Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare), [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers), took residence in the finally completed [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Aegon's_Landing.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) When the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aegon's_Conquest)) learned of Aegon's landing in Westeros, he locked himself in the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) for a week to pray.(wiki:Aegon's_Landing.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Aegon ordered the construction of the [Aegonfort](wiki:Aegonfort) on the highest of the hills at the Blackwater's mouth, and Rhaenys and Visenya were sent to [Rosby](wiki:Yielding_of_Rosby) and [Stokeworth](wiki:Submission_of_Stokeworth), respectively. The would-be king was then victorious in [Aegon's first test](wiki:Aegon's_first_test).(wiki:Aegon's_Landing.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) After securing the mouth of the Blackwater, Aegon was crowned at the Aegonfort as "Aegon, First of His Name, [King of All Westeros](wiki:King_of_All_Westeros), and [Shield of His People](wiki:Shield_of_His_People)". The new king then departed the Aegonfort to confront Harren the Black in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Aegon's_Landing.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
[Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), the first of the [Wars of Conquest](wiki:Wars_of_Conquest), lasted for more than two years and ended with Aegon I anointed king by the High Septon in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown).(wiki:Aegon's_Landing.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Six of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) were consolidated under his rule, with only [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) remaining independent.(wiki:Aegon's_Landing.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
