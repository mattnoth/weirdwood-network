## Origins

The [tourney for the completion of the Dragonpit](wiki:Tourney_for_the_completion_of_the_Dragonpit) was held in [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), with lists raised in the fields found between the [Lion Gate](wiki:Lion_Gate) and the [King's Gate](wiki:King's_Gate).(wiki:Tourney_grounds_(King's_Landing).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
A tourney was held at the capital in [98 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) to celebrate the fiftieth year of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen)'s reign.(wiki:Tourney_grounds_(King's_Landing).cite_ref-Rfabfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
To commemorate the fifth anniversary of the marriage between King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower), a great [tourney](wiki:Tourney) was held in [111 AC](wiki:111_AC). Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen), returning from the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones), circled three times above the tourney grounds on [Caraxes](wiki:Caraxes) before landing and reconciling with Viserys to the cheers of both [lords](wiki:Lord) and [commons](wiki:Smallfolk).(wiki:Tourney_grounds_(King's_Landing).cite_ref-Rfabfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
A tourney was held in [114 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) to celebrate the marriage of Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and Ser [Laenor Velaryon](wiki:Laenor_Velaryon).(wiki:Tourney_grounds_(King's_Landing).cite_ref-Rfabfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)
Tourneys were held at the capital in [193 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) and [206 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Tourney_grounds_(King's_Landing).cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) The [winter tourney at King's Landing](wiki:Winter_tourney_at_King's_Landing) took place in [253 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Tourney_grounds_(King's_Landing).cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) hosted the [Anniversary Tourney](wiki:Anniversary_Tourney) in honor of King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) in [272 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Tourney_grounds_(King's_Landing).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
A tourney was held at King's Landing to celebrate the [wedding of Robert I Baratheon and Cersei Lannister](wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister) in [284 AC](wiki:284_AC).(wiki:Tourney_grounds_(King's_Landing).cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) was champion at a tourney in King's Landing in [294 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Tourney_grounds_(King's_Landing).cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2)
The [tourney on Prince Joffrey's name day](wiki:Tourney_on_Prince_Joffrey's_name_day) took place in [298 AC](wiki:298_AC).(wiki:Tourney_grounds_(King's_Landing).cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

The [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) is held at the tourney grounds in honor of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s appointment as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Tourney_grounds_(King's_Landing).cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) lands hundreds of men on the tourney grounds and bring a ram to the [King's Gate](wiki:King's_Gate).(wiki:Tourney_grounds_(King's_Landing).cite_ref-Racok59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) leads a detachment of defenders along the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) and sees three galleys beached on the tourney grounds.(wiki:Tourney_grounds_(King's_Landing).cite_ref-Racok61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Tyrion, condemned for [the murder](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), is to be beheaded on the old tourney grounds. Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) help Tyrion escape the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) the night before, however.(wiki:Tourney_grounds_(King's_Landing).cite_ref-Rasos77.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
