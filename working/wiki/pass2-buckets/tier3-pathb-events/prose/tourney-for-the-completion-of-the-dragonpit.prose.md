## Origins

Lord [Manfryd Redwyne](wiki:Manfryd_Redwyne), [master of ships](wiki:Master_of_ships), proposed that the completion of the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit) be celebrated with a great [tourney](wiki:Tourney), to which King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen). Lists were raised in the fields west of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing)'s city walls between the [Lion Gate](wiki:Lion_Gate) and the [King's Gate](wiki:King's_Gate).(wiki:Tourney_for_the_completion_of_the_Dragonpit.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
After winning the squire's tourney, [Rickard Redwyne](wiki:Rickard_Redwyne) was knighted by Jaehaerys himself. The champion's laurels went to Ser [Simon Dondarrion](wiki:Simon_Dondarrion) of [Blackhaven](wiki:Blackhaven), who won the love of the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) when he crowned Princess [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)) as his [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty).(wiki:Tourney_for_the_completion_of_the_Dragonpit.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
As no [dragons](wiki:Dragon) had been settled in the Dragonpit as yet, the huge building edifice was chosento host the tourney's grand melee, which involved seventy-seven knights divided into eleven teams. The participants began the melee on their [horses](wiki:Horse), but continued fighting on foot with blunted weaponws after being unhorsed. When only one team remained, its members turned on one another until only a single champion remained. By melee's end, two men had been killed and more than twoscore were wounded. Queen Alysanne forbade her favorites, [Jonquil Darke](wiki:Jonquil_Darke) and [Tom the Strummer](wiki:Tom_the_Strummer), from taking part in the melee.(wiki:Tourney_for_the_completion_of_the_Dragonpit.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Aftermath

Soon after the conclusion of the tourney, Queen Alysanne left King's Landing for [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), there to await the birth of her child. Alysanne gave birth to Prince [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) in late [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Tourney_for_the_completion_of_the_Dragonpit.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
