## Origins

After the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood) and the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), Robb Stark, the new [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), sends Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) as an envoy to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with a peace offer for Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister). In case the offer is rejected, Robb sends [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) to treat with his father [Balon](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), for an alliance to assault the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) from both land and sea. Meanwhile, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) tasks his cousin, Ser [Stafford Lannister](wiki:Stafford_Lannister), with training a new Lannister army at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock). With the main Lannister army in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), this new army could be used to attack Robb's host from two fronts.(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Robb marches to deal with the forming threat as Stafford trains his fresh recruits, believed to be [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords), [freeriders](wiki:Freeriders), and boys from the stews of [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport).(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) Also among them are many veterans of Ser [Forley Prester](wiki:Forley_Prester)'s 4,000-strong force at the Golden Tooth, survivors of the Camps.(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-11) As Robb marches west from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), he sends his mother, [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), to forge an alliance with King [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon).(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) This is done with the aim that Renly will pose a more immediate threat to Tywin and force him to leave [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2)
Robb's host invades the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) by avoiding the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth), the strong castle at the border, through a previously-unknown goat track found by his direwolf, [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind).(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

## Narrative Arc

Stafford trains the inexperienced recruits near the village of [Oxcross](wiki:Oxcross), within three days ride from [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport). Thinking himself safe in his own lands, Stafford does not bother to set sentries. During the night, Robb sends men to cut the lines that hold Stafford's horses. When Robb sets loose Grey Wind, the horses are maddened by the scent and trample tents and men alike while trying to flee. With confusion sending Lannister men fleeing everywhere, Robb sends his own cavalry. The battle is more a rout then a real encounter, as the untrained and surprised Lannister army fail to put up much resistance.(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
Stafford is slain by Lord [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark) while running after his horse.(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) Ser [Stevron Frey](wiki:Stevron_Frey), Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey)'s eldest son and heir, suffers a minor wound during the battle, but dies shortly thereafter.(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Among those reported dead, missing and wounded are Ser [Lymond Vikary](wiki:Lymond_Vikary), Lord [Roland Crakehall](wiki:Roland_Crakehall_(lord)), and Lord [Antario Jast](wiki:Antario_Jast).(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) These men are actually captured by the Stark forces, along with [Martyn Lannister](wiki:Martyn_Lannister), Lord Jast's sons, and half a hundred other highborn hostages.(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

## Aftermath

The Lannister army's defeat is so great that, according to rumor, Robb cut out Stafford's heart and fed it to Grey Wind. Remnants fall back to Lannisport, where Stafford's son, Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister), reforms them.(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Robb continues attacking the westerlands, capturing [Ashemark](wiki:Taking_of_Ashemark), and marches on to take the [Crag](wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag).(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) [Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover) and [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark) raid along [the coast](wiki:Raiding_along_the_westerlands_coast). Lady [Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont) captures thousands of livestock and begins driving them back towards the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). Lord [Greatjon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber) captures [the gold mines](wiki:Seizure_of_westerlands_gold_mines) at [Nunn's Deep](wiki:Nunn's_Deep), [Castamere](wiki:Castamere), and in the [Pendric Hills](wiki:Pendric_Hills).(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7)
[Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister) proclaims in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) that Robb used "vile sorcery" to win, after which [northmen](wiki:Northmen) ate [enemy flesh](wiki:Cannibalism).(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9) Lord Tywin, having heard of the defeat at Oxcross, and feeling confident that [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) laying [siege to Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)) means a secure southern front,(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) marches west to face Robb's host.(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
The singer [Rymund the Rhymer](wiki:Rymund_the_Rhymer) composes a tune commemorating the northern victory, "[Wolf in the Night](wiki:Wolf_in_the_Night)".(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)
Remembering his uncle Stafford's mistake, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) sets sentries himself while at [Hayford](wiki:Hayford).(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Daven vows not to cut his hair until he avenges his father's death by slaying Lord Karstark.(wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
