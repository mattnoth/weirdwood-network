## Origins

Whever he took part in tourneys, Ser [Uthor Underleaf](wiki:Uthor_Underleaf) would bribe the master of the games to get paired up so that he would face the opponents he preferred—big men, older champions past their prime, and "village heroes"—where the odds would appear to be against him.(wiki:Master_of_the_games.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
During the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow), the position was occupied by [Plummer](wiki:Plummer), the steward for [Lord Ashford](wiki:Lord_Ashford).(wiki:Master_of_the_games.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
During the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls), the position was occupied by [Cosgrove](wiki:Cosgrove), the steward for Lord [Ambrose Butterwell](wiki:Ambrose_Butterwell).(wiki:Master_of_the_games.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
