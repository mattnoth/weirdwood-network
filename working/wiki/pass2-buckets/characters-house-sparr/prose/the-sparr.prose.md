## Origins

The Sparr was ritually drowned in service to the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God) as a boy.(wiki:The_Sparr.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Appearances & Description

He is a hatchet-faced old man with watery eyes.(wiki:The_Sparr.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

The Sparr, his son [Steffarion](wiki:Steffarion_Sparr), and [Gormond Goodbrother](wiki:Gormond_Goodbrother) meet [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) to give him the news that [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) has died.(wiki:The_Sparr.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
