## Origins

Lorcas has been an acolyte for fifty years,(wiki:Lorcas.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) forging nine links in his [maester's chain](wiki:Maester).(wiki:Lorcas.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Appearances & Description

Lorcas has a pinched face. He hates [novices](wiki:Maester), particularly those of [noble birth](wiki:Feudalism).(wiki:Lorcas.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He is in service to the [Seneschal of the Citadel](wiki:Seneschal_of_the_Citadel).(wiki:Lorcas.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Lorcas is the gatekeeper when [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) arrives at the [Seneschal's Court](wiki:Seneschal's_Court) and asks to speak with the [Seneschal](wiki:Seneschal_of_the_Citadel). Lorcas makes Samwell wait for hours. [Alleras](wiki:Alleras) intervenes and explains Lorcas will continue to make Sam wait if he does not bride him with a [penny](wiki:Currency). Sam and Alleras depart the hall for the [Ravenry](wiki:Ravenry).(wiki:Lorcas.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
