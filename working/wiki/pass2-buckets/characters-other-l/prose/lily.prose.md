## Origins

Lily's mother Megette was the mistress of Prince Aegon for four years. Lily was the second of four daughters. After Prince [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen) discovered this, Megette was returned to her husband in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), while Lily and her three sisters were given to the Faith to be trained as a [septa](wiki:Septa).(wiki:Lily.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
