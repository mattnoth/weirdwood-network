## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser Perkin stays with Queen [Selyse](wiki:Selyse_Florent) at [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea) when King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) goes to take [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte). When Selyse leaves Eastwatch, Perkin accompanies her to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), where she introduces him to [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch).(wiki:Perkin_Follard.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
