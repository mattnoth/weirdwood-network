## Origins

Lord [Alyn Tarbeck](wiki:Alyn_Tarbeck) fought for Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) during the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye) in [43 AC](wiki:43_AC). He was slain during the battle and his posthumous [son](wiki:Lord_Tarbeck_(son_of_Alyn)) succeeded him.(wiki:Lord_of_Tarbeck_Hall.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Lord Tarbeck died from the [Shivers](wiki:Shivers) in [59 AC](wiki:59_AC).(wiki:Lord_of_Tarbeck_Hall.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Lord Tarbeck was slain by Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy)'s men in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC) while trying to retake [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle).(wiki:Lord_of_Tarbeck_Hall.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

During the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), Lord Tarbeck supported both sides.(wiki:Lord_of_Tarbeck_Hall.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Lord [Walderan Tarbeck](wiki:Walderan_Tarbeck) and his kin were killed in the [Reyne-Tarbeck revolt](wiki:Reyne-Tarbeck_revolt) against [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) in [261 AC](wiki:261_AC).(wiki:Lord_of_Tarbeck_Hall.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Lord_of_Tarbeck_Hall.cite_ref-sample_7-0)
