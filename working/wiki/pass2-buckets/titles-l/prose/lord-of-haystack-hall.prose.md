## Origins

During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Lords Buckler, [Errol](wiki:House_Errol), and [Fell](wiki:House_Fell) opposed [Orys Baratheon](wiki:Orys_Baratheon) during his march from the [Aegonfort](wiki:Aegonfort) toward [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), but the storm lords were forced to retreat when [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) had [Meraxes](wiki:Meraxes) set [afire](wiki:Dragonfire) parts of what is now the southern [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood).(wiki:Lord_of_Haystack_Hall.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
