## Origins

When [Johanna Swann](wiki:Johanna_Swann) was captured by [Lysene](wiki:Lysene) captains of the [Triarchy](wiki:Triarchy), her niggardly uncle, the Lord of [Stonehelm](wiki:Stonehelm), refused to pay her ransom.(wiki:Lord_of_Stonehelm.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Lord [Gawen Swann](wiki:Gawen_Swann) competed in the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC).(wiki:Lord_of_Stonehelm.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Before becoming a lord, Ser [Manfred Swann](wiki:Manfred_Swann) had a young [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) as his squire.(wiki:Lord_of_Stonehelm.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Stonehelm.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Lord [Gulian Swann](wiki:Gulian_Swann) agrees to meet Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) but refuses to join the cause of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord_of_Stonehelm.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) During the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Gulian remains in his castle pleading [illness](wiki:Illness).(wiki:Lord_of_Stonehelm.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Clifford Swann](wiki:Clifford_Swann) is listed as Lord of Stonehelm.(wiki:Lord_of_Stonehelm.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) It is unknown if this is a mistake or whether Clifford has succeeded Gulian over Gulian's eldest son, Ser [Donnel](wiki:Donnel_Swann).
