## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser [Philip Foote](wiki:Philip_Foote) slays [Bryce Caron](wiki:Bryce_Caron), Lord of [Nightsong](wiki:Nightsong), during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). The [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) rewards him with Bryce's lands and titles, and Philip founds [House Foote of Nightsong](wiki:House_Foote_of_Nightsong).(wiki:Lord_of_Nightsong.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Bryce's [bastard](wiki:Bastard) half-brother, Ser [Rolland Storm](wiki:Rolland_Storm), is a pretender to Nightsong, laying claim under [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s banner.(wiki:Lord_of_Nightsong.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
