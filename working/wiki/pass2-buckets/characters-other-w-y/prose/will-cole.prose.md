## Origins

Will is a [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) exile. His surname Cole is of a house that was once prominent in the histories of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Will_Cole.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) [House Cole](wiki:House_Cole) served as [stewards](wiki:Steward) to [House Dondarrion](wiki:House_Dondarrion) in the years preceding the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), and their most prominent member was [Ser](wiki:Ser) [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole),(wiki:Will_Cole.cite_ref-Rfab12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) under [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) and [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Will_Cole.cite_ref-Rfab13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) By the present day, their arms are obscure,(wiki:Will_Cole.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and they may be extinct.(wiki:Will_Cole.cite_ref-Citadel_6-0) Will may or may not belong to House Cole, as in the free companies a man can call himself whatever he chooses, and not all are genuine. He might be related to another serjeant in the company, [Dick Cole](wiki:Dick_Cole).(wiki:Will_Cole.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Appearances & Description

He wears his worldly wealth upon his person and a lord’s ransom in golden arms rings.(wiki:Will_Cole.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Will is present during [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington)’s rendezvous with the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) three miles south of [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys).(wiki:Will_Cole.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Like the rest of the company, he swears his allegiance to [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)) before they [invade Westeros](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company).
