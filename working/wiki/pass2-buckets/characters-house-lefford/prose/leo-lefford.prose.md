## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) is taken captive by Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), Lord Tywin calls his banners and raids the [Riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). Leo joins his liege lord in the campaign. He is given charge of Lord Tywin's stores and supplies.(wiki:Leo_Lefford.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

When Tyrion joins with the Lannister camp on the east bank of the [Trident](wiki:Trident), Leo provides him with a tent and is charged with supplying weapons to the [mountain clans](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) Tyrion has recruited to join the army. He reluctantly hands over his arms to what he calls savages, being particularly displeased when [Shagga](wiki:Shagga) demands two axes.(wiki:Leo_Lefford.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) He later rides in the center of the Lannister force during the [battle of the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_of_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:Leo_Lefford.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

When a messenger arrives with news of the defeat of Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s forces in the [battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), Lord Leo suggests forming a truce and exchanging prisoners with the [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) army, pointing out that Queen [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) has [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) in her hands. When Ser [Addam Marbrand](wiki:Addam_Marbrand) says that [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) would be foolish to accept exchanging his sisters for Jaime, Leo then suggests ransoming Jaime.(wiki:Leo_Lefford.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Lord Leo mocks the ghosts of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) while encamped within the castle with Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s army. However, he always sleeps with a candle next to his bed.(wiki:Leo_Lefford.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

He drowns at the [Stone Mill](wiki:Stone_Mill) during the [battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords) in [299 AC](wiki:299_AC).(wiki:Leo_Lefford.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Leo_Lefford.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
