## Origins

Humfrey was chosen by Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) to be one of the first sworn brothers of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) when the order was founded in [10 AC](wiki:10_AC) by King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Humfrey_the_Mummer.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Humfrey_the_Mummer.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Appearances & Description

Humfrey is remembered for being quick, strong, observant, and famed for his prowess with sword and shield. He was devoted to his king.(wiki:Humfrey_the_Mummer.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
