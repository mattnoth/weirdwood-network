## Origins

Hot Pie's mother was a baker in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), and when she was alive the boy would push her [cart](wiki:Cart) through the streets yelling, "Hot pies! Hot pies!"(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Racok1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Appearances & Description

Hot Pie is plump with straw-colored hair(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Racok1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and big eyes.(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He is a poor [horse](wiki:Horse) rider(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Rasos3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) but an accomplished baker,(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) thinks him stupid and craven.(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Hot Pie and others are collected by [Yoren](wiki:Yoren) and bound for the [Wall](wiki:Wall) as recruits for the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). Hot Pie lies about kicking another boy to death.(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Racok1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Racok9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Unaware that [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) is a girl, let alone the daughter of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), he and [Lommy Greenhands](wiki:Lommy_Greenhands) taunt Arya mercilessly until she beats Hot Pie savagely with a wooden practice sword.(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Racok1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

When Yoren's band is [attacked](wiki:Storming_of_the_Gods_Eye_town_holdfast) by Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch) at the [Gods Eye town](wiki:Gods_Eye_town), Hot Pie and Arya are among the survivors along with [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) and Lommy.(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) The youths are eventually captured by the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men), however, and Lommy is killed.(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Hot Pie is surprised when Arya reveals herself to be a girl.(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Like Arya, Hot Pie is made a servant at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal). He is sent to work in the kitchens and believes the castle is haunted by ghosts.(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Hot Pie is beaten with a spoon after trying to give Arya a tart from the kitchen.(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) After the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Hot Pie escapes Harrenhal alongside Arya and Gendry.(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Having seen her kill a [Bolton guard](wiki:House_Bolton_guards) at Harrenhal, Hot Pie is frightened by Arya.(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Rasos3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) They are captured by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) and brought to the [Inn of the Kneeling Man](wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man).(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) En route Hot Pie sings "[The Bear and the Maiden Fair](wiki:The_Bear_and_the_Maiden_Fair)" with [Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams).(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) The boy is shocked when [Harwin](wiki:Harwin) reveals Arya's identity. He decides to stay on at the inn and make bread for [Sharna](wiki:Sharna) and [Husband](wiki:Husband), hoping that his friend Arya will return after the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Training in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), Arya thinks that Hot Pie would enjoy the kitchens of the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White).(wiki:Hot_Pie.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
