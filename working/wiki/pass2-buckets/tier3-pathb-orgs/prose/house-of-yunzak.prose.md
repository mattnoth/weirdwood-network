## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Yurkhaz zo Yunzak](wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak) hires the sellsword company the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown) during the war against [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), signing their contract on behalf of the city of [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai).(wiki:House_of_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
Yurkhaz is named the [Supreme Commander of the Armies and Allies of Yunkai](wiki:Supreme_Commander_of_the_Armies_and_Allies_of_Yunkai),(wiki:House_of_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and is one of the seven commanders who enter Meereen to sign the peace treaty and witness the opening of the [fighting pits of Meereen](wiki:Fighting_pits_of_Meereen).(wiki:House_of_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He dies in the panic that ensues after [Drogon](wiki:Drogon)'s arrival in [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit), the greatest [fighting pit](wiki:Fighting_pit) of Meereen.(wiki:House_of_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The Yunkai'i try to use Yurkhaz's death to their advantage and claim that his death is a breach of the peace.(wiki:House_of_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen) is resumed, and command over the Yunkish armies is given over to a new commander every day.
