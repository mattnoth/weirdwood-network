## Origins

The origin of this branch of House Fossoway is from [209 AC](wiki:209_AC), when [Raymun Fossoway](wiki:Raymun_Fossoway) participated in the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow). Raymun took a different side than his cousin, Ser [Steffon](wiki:Steffon_Fossoway), the heir to [Cider Hall](wiki:Cider_Hall), in a [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven) at [Ashford](wiki:Ashford).(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_New_Barrel.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
Raymun, who was [knighted](wiki:Knight) minutes before the battle by [Lyonel Baratheon](wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon), [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End), fought for his friend. Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall). To differentiate himself from his cousin fighting for the other side, Raymun painted the [apple](wiki:Apple) on his shield in green, playing at Steffon's oft-repeated jape that he was "still green". Raymun stated that he preferred to be green than rotten.(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_New_Barrel.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser [Arnell](wiki:Arnell), a knight of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), is the son of an unnamed green-apple Fossoway.(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_New_Barrel.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
The banners of the green-apple Fossoways are spotted beneath the walls of [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge) by [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark).(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_New_Barrel.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) During the feast with [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)'s army at Bitterbridge, Catelyn is placed between Lord [Mathis Rowan](wiki:Mathis_Rowan) and Ser [Jon Fossoway](wiki:Jon_Fossoway), whom she considers genial.(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_New_Barrel.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
Both branches of House Fossoway turn to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) after Renly's death. Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose) acknowledges Ser Jon of the green-apple Fossoways and Ser [Bryan Fossoway](wiki:Bryan_Fossoway) of the red during his parley with Stannis. During [Stannis's siege](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)) of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), Cortnay's lieutenant is Lord [Elwood Meadows](wiki:Elwood_Meadows), a green boy of twenty years and cousin to the Fossoways. Elwood immediately surrenders the castle after Cortnay's death.(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_New_Barrel.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
The banner of the green-apple Fossoways is spotted in Stannis's van as it approaches [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_New_Barrel.cite_ref-Racok52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), [Lothor Brune](wiki:Lothor_Brune) cuts his way through half a hundred Fossoway men-at-arms to capture Ser Jon from the green apple and slay Ser Bryan and Ser [Edwyd](wiki:Edwyd_Fossoway) of the red, earning the name Lothor Apple-Eater. Jon is one of the highborn prisoners who bend the knee to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) after the Blackwater.(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_New_Barrel.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) observes a Fossoway and his pregnant wife at Joffrey's [royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding). The man often kisses her and caresses her stomach.(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_New_Barrel.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lady [Janna Fossoway](wiki:Janna_Fossoway), the wife of Ser [Jon Fossoway](wiki:Jon_Fossoway), is one of the companions of Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) at her court in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), and often goes [hawking](wiki:Hawk) with her and [Meredyth Crane](wiki:Meredyth_Crane).(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_New_Barrel.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) spots the green-apple badge of House Fossoway among those that have been collected from the dead after the [battle at Duskendale](wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale).(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_New_Barrel.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
