## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Kraznys mo Nakloz](wiki:Kraznys_mo_Nakloz), one of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor)'s [Good Masters](wiki:Good_Masters) and a dealer in [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied), receives [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) when she arrives in the city to purchase slave soldiers.(wiki:House_of_Nakloz.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Kraznys is present for the negotiations about the price of the Unsullied, and later also when Daenerys arrives to take possession of her army. He takes over the largest of her [dragons](wiki:Dragon), [Drogon](wiki:Drogon), in exchange for the army of Unsullied, but later dies in the [fall of Astapor](wiki:Fall_of_Astapor) when Daenerys sets Drogon upon him.(wiki:House_of_Nakloz.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

During the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor) by the [Yunkai'i](wiki:Yunkai'i), the [pyramid of Nakloz](wiki:Pyramid_of_Nakloz) is set aflame by the people of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor) because they blame the late Kraznys for their woes.(wiki:House_of_Nakloz.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
