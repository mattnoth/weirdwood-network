## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After entering the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White) for the first time, [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) notices the statue of a hooded man leaning on a staff.(wiki:Hooded_Wayfarer.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) She later learns the statue is a favorite of poor men, and that the Hooded Wayfarer is considered by the [Faceless Men](wiki:Faceless_Men) to be an aspect of the [Many-Faced God](wiki:Many-Faced_God).(wiki:Hooded_Wayfarer.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
