## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Manderly guards escort Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) to the [Merman's Court](wiki:Merman's_Court) to meet Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly). Ser [Marlon Manderly](wiki:Marlon_Manderly) is ordered to have Davos executed,(wiki:House_Manderly_guards.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) but there is a secret ploy to spare Davos's life and enlist him for Wyman's cause.(wiki:House_Manderly_guards.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
