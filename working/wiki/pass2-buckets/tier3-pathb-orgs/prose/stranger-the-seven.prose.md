## Culture

The Stranger represents death and the unknown, and he leads the dead to the other world.(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Whilst referred to as male, he is neither male nor female.(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) The Stranger's face has been described as half-human, concealed beneath a hooded mantle.(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The wooden statue of the Stranger in the sept of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) is carved to look more [animal](wiki:Animal) than human.(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Worshippers rarely seek favor from the Stranger or light candles at his altars.(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) The Stranger is also rarely sung to in songs, such as the "[The Song of the Seven](wiki:The_Song_of_the_Seven)".(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Rasos46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
[Silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters) are sometimes referred to as the Stranger's wives(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) or his handmaidens.(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) They clothe themselves in grey and keep their faces cowled except for their eyes.(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Silent sisters traverse the Stranger's Steps within the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor).(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
The [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White) in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) contains a public shrine with idols of many death gods, though the the shrine of the Stranger is rarely visited.(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) The [Faceless Men](wiki:Faceless_Men) believe that the Stranger is actually the [Many-Faced God](wiki:Many-Faced_God).(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
[Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane)'s horse is named [Stranger](wiki:Stranger_(horse)).(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Rasos47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

## Origins

Queen [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) decided upon the [rule of six](wiki:Rule_of_six) as a form of punishment, omitting a seventh blow because of the Stranger.(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
The year [54 AC](wiki:54_AC) became known as the Year of the Stranger due to the deaths and tragedies that befell [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) and the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_y.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the [Shepherd](wiki:Shepherd) preached about the Stranger to a crowd before the [Storming of the Dragonpit](wiki:Storming_of_the_Dragonpit).(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
During the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion), Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) slew Ser [Tommard Heddle](wiki:Tommard_Heddle) before the altar of the Stranger within the [sept](wiki:Sept) of [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls).(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) prays at a modest [sept](wiki:Sept) in a village near [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End). The sept has no statues of [the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), only rough charcoal drawings to represent them. The Stranger's face is a black oval, a shadow with stars for eyes which makes Catelyn uneasy.(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
In the [royal sept](wiki:Royal_sept) of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) lights a candle at the altar of the [Warrior](wiki:Warrior_(the_Seven)) for his brother, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), and then a candle at the Stranger's altar for himself.(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
Upon entering the royal sept before the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) sees that there are even a few candle flames dancing below the Stranger’s half-human face. Sansa visits each of the Seven in turn, lighting a candle at each altar.(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After the death of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), his funeral bier is laid out the Stranger in the royal sept.(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

In the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) finds a dead young man at the feet of the Stranger.(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
During her confinement beneath the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor), [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) prays to all the aspects of the godhead, even the Stranger.(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

As the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen) is resuming, Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) prays to all of the Seven, even the Stranger.(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Barristan_23-0) [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) thinks to himself that the Stranger has mounted his [pale mare](wiki:Bloody_flux) and is riding toward them with his sword in hand.(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-24)
In [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), [Marro](wiki:Marro) portrays the Stranger in a performance of a new play, [The Bloody Hand](wiki:The_Bloody_Hand).(wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven).cite_ref-25)
