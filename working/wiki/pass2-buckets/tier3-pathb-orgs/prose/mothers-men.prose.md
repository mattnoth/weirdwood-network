## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

A boy of the the slaver class petitions Daenerys to have one of his father's former slaves, who had joined the Mother's Men, hanged for the rape and murder of his mother. Daenerys refuses as she had decreed a full pardon for all crimes committed during the [sack of Meereen](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen).(wiki:Mother's_Men.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
When [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) and a number of [pit fighters](wiki:Pit_fighter) petition Daenerys to reopen the [fighting pits](wiki:Fighting_pits), Daenerys proposes the fighters to swear their swords to one of the companies of freedmen, including the Mother's Men. The pit fighters refuse.(wiki:Mother's_Men.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
To aid her in ruling Meereen, Daenerys assembles a [council](wiki:Ruling_council_of_Meereen) formed by the leaders of each of the factions supporting her. Marselen is the representative of the Mother's Men.(wiki:Mother's_Men.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

When the [Second Siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_Siege_of_Meereen) turns into battle, the Mother's Men soon break the [Long Lances](wiki:Long_Lances) like a rotten stick and drag the [Ghost of Astapor](wiki:Six_sisters) trebuchet over with chains.(wiki:Mother's_Men.cite_ref-6)
