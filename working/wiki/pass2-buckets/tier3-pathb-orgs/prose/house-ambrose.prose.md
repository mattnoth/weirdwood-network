## Origins

In [42 AC](wiki:42_AC) Ser [Aegon Ambrose](wiki:Aegon_Ambrose), a knight of the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons), took part in the [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven) in which seven of the Warrior's Sons faced King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) and his six champions. He died during the battle.(wiki:House_Ambrose.cite_ref-Rtsotd.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
During the civil war known as the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), [Marq Ambrose](wiki:Marq_Ambrose) was loyal to King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen). He was a member of the [Caltrops](wiki:Caltrops), supporters of Aegon II who plotted to dispose of [Ulf the White](wiki:Ulf_the_White) and [Hugh Hammer](wiki:Hugh_Hammer). Marq suggested that, since both Ulf the White and Hugh Hammer were [dragonriders](wiki:Dragonrider), they would kill the men only after taking [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) from the forces of [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), but [Richard Rodden](wiki:Richard_Rodden) insisted that such a plan would be dishonorable.(wiki:House_Ambrose.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Marq was later slain during the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton).(wiki:House_Ambrose.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
Ser [Aubrey Ambrose](wiki:Aubrey_Ambrose) was named by [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey) as one of the outstanding supporters of the [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre)'s cause.(wiki:House_Ambrose.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

House Ambrose follow their [Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) overlords in declaring for [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) on the outset of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). While on the march with Renly's army, Ser [Edmund Ambrose](wiki:Edmund_Ambrose) is one of the knights who start the wager on the maidenhood of [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth), bringing her flowers and offering to ride with her.(wiki:House_Ambrose.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Brienne eventually discovers the wager, after which she seeks Edmund out during the melee at [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge) and defeats him.(wiki:House_Ambrose.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
After Renly's death, the Ambroses again follow the Tyrells, marching with the Tyrell-[Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) army at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). The squire [Alyn Ambrose](wiki:Alyn_Ambrose) slays a [Myrish](wiki:Myr) crossbowman and a [Mullendore](wiki:House_Mullendore) man-at-arms during the fighting.(wiki:House_Ambrose.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Elinor Tyrell](wiki:Elinor_Tyrell) is betrothed to [Alyn Ambrose](wiki:Alyn_Ambrose), a son of Lord [Arthur Ambrose](wiki:Arthur_Ambrose).(wiki:House_Ambrose.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
Ambrose men march with Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) to pacify the [crownlands](wiki:Crownlands) after the victory at the Blackwater.(wiki:House_Ambrose.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) spots horse trappings bearing the ants of Ambrose among the gear collected from the dead after the [battle at Duskendale](wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale).(wiki:House_Ambrose.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
