## Origins

Lord Goodbrook supported [Harwyn Hardhand](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), when he warred against the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Arrec Durrandon](wiki:Arrec_Durrandon).(wiki:House_Goodbrook.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Lord Goodbrook was one of the lords from the riverlands that stayed loyal to King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), instead of following the banner of their liege lord. For this, Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully) descended upon the Goodbrooks and destroyed at least one village. After the war, his son made peace with King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) and Lord Hoster, but the wealth of the Goodbrooks had been substantially reduced from the fighting.(wiki:House_Goodbrook.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Traveling with the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) visits a village that was Lord Goodbrook's, and [Notch](wiki:Notch) tells of the Goodbrooks' history.(wiki:House_Goodbrook.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
During the march to the [Twins](wiki:Twins) for his wedding to [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey), Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) enjoys the company of Ser [Marq Piper](wiki:Marq_Piper), Lord [Lymond Goodbrook](wiki:Lymond_Goodbrook), [Patrek Mallister](wiki:Patrek_Mallister), and the young [Vances](wiki:House_Vance).(wiki:House_Goodbrook.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
