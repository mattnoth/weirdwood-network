## Origins

As some point in the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes), [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) had grown so numerous that [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) could not contain all of them. Instead of tunneling out new passages and halls in the Rock, the members of the lesser branches left to make their home in a village a scant mile away. This village eventually grew into Lannisport.(wiki:House_Lanny.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
