## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The company is under contract from [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) and is the first to be dispatched to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay).(wiki:Long_Lances.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) They later participate at the [siege](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor) of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor) and later [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Long_Lances.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
During the [Stormcrows](wiki:Stormcrows)' mission to [Lhazar](wiki:Lhazar) to secure an alliance with Meereen, the Yunkish send the Long Lances to close the [Khyzai Pass](wiki:Khyzai_Pass). The Stormcrows [descend](wiki:Fighting_on_the_Khyzai_Pass) on the Long Lances in the night and defeat them, losing nine men in the process. Twelve Longe Lances go over to the Stormcrows.(wiki:Long_Lances.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

As the [Battle of Slaver's Bay](wiki:Battle_of_Slaver's_Bay) begins, the Long Lances are defending the [Ghost of Astapor](wiki:Six_sisters). The [Mother's Men](wiki:Mother's_Men) break the Long Lances easily and drag the [trebuchet](wiki:Trebuchet) over with chains.(wiki:Long_Lances.cite_ref-5)
