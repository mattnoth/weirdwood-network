## Origins

After the defeat at the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field), those lords and knights who went into exile with Ser [Aegor Rivers](wiki:Aegor_Rivers) began to sign on to sellsword companies, such as the Ragged Standard. This would cause Ser Aegor to make his own company, the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company).(wiki:Ragged_Standard.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
