## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser [Gerald Gower](wiki:Gerald_Gower) remains in the service of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) after the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). One of the [king's men](wiki:King's_men), Gerald questions Stannis's new faith in [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor) after the defeat.(wiki:House_Gower.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Gerald is among the conspirators who smuggle [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm) out of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) on behalf of Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth).(wiki:House_Gower.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Gerald helps protect Edric as the boy hides in [Lys](wiki:Lys).(wiki:House_Gower.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
