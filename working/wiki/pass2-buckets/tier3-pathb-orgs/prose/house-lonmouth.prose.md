## Origins

Ser [Joffrey Lonmouth](wiki:Joffrey_Lonmouth), a close friend of Ser [Laenor Velaryon](wiki:Laenor_Velaryon), was mortally wounded by Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) at the wedding tourney of Laenor and Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen). Laenor named his son, Prince [Joffrey Velaryon](wiki:Joffrey_Velaryon), after his late friend.(wiki:House_Lonmouth.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:House_Lonmouth.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_viserys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Ser [Richard Lonmouth](wiki:Richard_Lonmouth) was a former [squire](wiki:Squire) and close personal friend of Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen).(wiki:House_Lonmouth.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) At a feast at the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal), Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) outdrank a member of House Lonmouth, most likely Richard.(wiki:House_Lonmouth.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
