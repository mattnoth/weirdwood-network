## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

When news reaches the [Twins](wiki:Twins) of the [raid on Saltpans](wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans), Ser [Arwood Frey](wiki:Arwood_Frey), Ser [Donnel Haigh](wiki:Donnel_Haigh), and Ser [Harys Haigh](wiki:Harys_Haigh) take a small force to investigate. They find the town in ruins and Ser [Quincy Cox](wiki:Quincy_Cox) locked within his castle.(wiki:House_Cox.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He did not come out to help the smallfolk during the sack.(wiki:House_Cox.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
At the [Quiet Isle](wiki:Quiet_Isle), Septon [Meribald](wiki:Meribald) defends Quincy by saying that his sons and good-sons are dead or off fighting, his grandsons are still too young, and he has two daughters. He had nothing to face the outlaws with.(wiki:House_Cox.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
