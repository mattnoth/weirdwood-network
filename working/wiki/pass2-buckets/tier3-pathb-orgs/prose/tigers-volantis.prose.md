## Origins

After the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria), during the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood), the Volantenes considered themselves the heirs to the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) and rightful rulers of the world.(wiki:Tigers_(Volantis).cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_doom_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Thus the militaristic tigers, supported by the [Old Blood](wiki:Old_Blood), held sway for almost a century, and led Volantis into conflict with the other Free Cities. This conflict would eventually involve all of the Free Cities except for (as far as is known) [Lorath](wiki:Lorath). At first, Volantis succeeded in their conquest. But an alliance of [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) and [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) resisted Volantis, and [Myr](wiki:Myr) and [Lys](wiki:Lys) rebelled. Volantis was further defeated by an alliance of [Qohor](wiki:Qohor) and [Norvos](wiki:Norvos), by [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki), by [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Argilac Durrandon](wiki:Argilac_Durrandon), and by Lord [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). In the end, the elephants - the faction favoring peace - took power from the tigers, and the fighting was at an end.(wiki:Tigers_(Volantis).cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_doom_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Tigers_(Volantis).cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Their followers went on a rampage, knocking the heads from the statues of those they blamed for all the wars and deaths.(wiki:Tigers_(Volantis).cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
For the last three centuries, since the tigers lost power to the elephants, no more than one tiger has been named triarch at a time. At some times no tiger has been elected at all.(wiki:Tigers_(Volantis).cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Tigers_(Volantis).cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
