## Origins

It is suggested that the [Night's King](wiki:Night's_King) was a Flint, among the many possibilities. Lord Commander [Rodrik Flint](wiki:Rodrik_Flint) is regarded as one of the worst commanders of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), for trying to make himself [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall). One of the dark legends surrounding the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort) is the rape and murder of brave young [Danny Flint](wiki:Danny_Flint).(wiki:House_Flint_of_Widow's_Watch.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:House_Flint_of_Widow's_Watch.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
A branch of House Flint held the [Wolf's Den](wiki:Wolf's_Den) for a century after the extinction of the [Greystarks](wiki:House_Greystark).(wiki:House_Flint_of_Widow's_Watch.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

After [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls his banners, Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin) explains that while some houses (such as the [Karstarks](wiki:House_Karstark), [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton), [Hornwoods](wiki:House_Hornwood) and [Umbers](wiki:House_Umber)) gather at the [winter town](wiki:Winter_town), others such as the [Reeds](wiki:House_Reed), the [Ryswells](wiki:House_Ryswell) and [Dustins](wiki:House_Dustin) and also Lords [Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) and Flint are waiting to join him along the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad). Since House Flint of [Widow's Watch](wiki:Widow's_Watch) is ruled by Lady [Lyessa Flint](wiki:Lyessa_Flint), the passage probably refers to the Lord of [Flint's Finger](wiki:Flint's_Finger).(wiki:House_Flint_of_Widow's_Watch.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
[Robin Flint](wiki:Robin_Flint) is one of the [thirty sons of nobles](wiki:Robb's_personal_guard) that ride besides the Young Wolf during the war. He and Ser [Wendel Manderly](wiki:Wendel_Manderly) are among the older men in the group.(wiki:House_Flint_of_Widow's_Watch.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The Flints are reported to have ties to House Hornwood through the female line according to [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Luwin](wiki:Luwin), although he does not specify which branch. Because of these ties, Luwin expects them to be one of several houses vying for a claim to [House Hornwood](wiki:House_Hornwood)'s lands.(wiki:House_Flint_of_Widow's_Watch.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
The Flints of Widow Watch excuse themselves for not attending the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) because Lady [Lyessa Flint](wiki:Lyessa_Flint) is heavy with child and there is sickness at [Widow's Watch](wiki:Widow's_Watch).(wiki:House_Flint_of_Widow's_Watch.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) Robin accompanies Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) on her voyage south to deal with [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) and [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:House_Flint_of_Widow's_Watch.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
When [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) studies the banners from the northern lords preparing to [attack Winterfell](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell) through Maester Luwin's [Myrish lens tube](wiki:Far-eye), he sees some of House Flint, though not as many as from other houses.(wiki:House_Flint_of_Widow's_Watch.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Robin commands the rearguard during Robb's march to the [Twins](wiki:Twins) for the [wedding](wiki:Wedding) of [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully).(wiki:House_Flint_of_Widow's_Watch.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Robin is one of Robb's bodyguards during the wedding feast, sitting near him with the [Smalljon](wiki:Jon_Umber_(Smalljon)) without drinking. During the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Robin is last seen ringed by [Freys](wiki:House_Frey), their daggers rising and falling.(wiki:House_Flint_of_Widow's_Watch.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sends a raven to [Widow's Watch](wiki:Widow's_Watch) in a plea for help to defend [Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) from [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings).(wiki:House_Flint_of_Widow's_Watch.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The fleet of [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan) is spotted by the Flints of Widow's Watch.(wiki:House_Flint_of_Widow's_Watch.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
According to Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly), should he declare for King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), the Flints of Widow's Watch will take their lead from him.(wiki:House_Flint_of_Widow's_Watch.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
