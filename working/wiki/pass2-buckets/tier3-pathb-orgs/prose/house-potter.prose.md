## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) recalls that [Robin Potter](wiki:Robin_Potter) participated in a bet beneath the walls of [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) to see who would be the first to claim her maidenhead. After the bet was discovered and ended, Brienne sought out and defeated Robin during the [melee at Bitterbridge](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge), leaving him with a scar on his head.(wiki:House_Potter.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He survived the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).
