## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister) is granted [Castle Darry](wiki:Darry) and its lands after the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), as [House Darry](wiki:House_Darry)'s child lord, [Lyman Darry](wiki:Lyman_Darry), died during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:House_Lannister_of_Darry.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lancel is betrothed to [Amerei Frey](wiki:Amerei_Frey), daughter of [Mariya Darry](wiki:Mariya_Darry), per the alliance between [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) and [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey). Mariya's younger sister, [Jeyne Darry](wiki:Jeyne_Darry), is dismayed that the lordship was granted to Lancel rather than one of her own young sons. Despite the house's banners combining the sigils of Lannister and Darry, an idea of [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), the house functions more as a branch of the Freys due to their numbers and Lancel's disinterest in his lordly duties.(wiki:House_Lannister_of_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
[Harwyn Plumm](wiki:Harwyn_Plumm) leads some men to root out outlaws on the Darry lands, to little effect.(wiki:House_Lannister_of_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Later [Lyle Crakehall](wiki:Lyle_Crakehall), charmed by Amerei, leaves [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) to conclude the campaign.(wiki:House_Lannister_of_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Lord Lancel renounces both lordship and his unconsummated marriage to join the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons).(wiki:House_Lannister_of_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)
