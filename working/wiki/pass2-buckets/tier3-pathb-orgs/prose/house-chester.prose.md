## Origins

The [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands) are a considerable naval power and were the first line of defense during the days when the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) pirates tried to sail up the [Mander](wiki:Mander) to sack the wealth of the [Reach](wiki:Reach).(wiki:House_Chester.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
In [89 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) approved of his granddaughter's, Princess [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aemon)), choice to marry Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) while being the host of Lord Chester in [Greenshield](wiki:Greenshield).(wiki:House_Chester.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
