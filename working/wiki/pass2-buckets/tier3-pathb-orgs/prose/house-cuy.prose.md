## Origins

The Cuys trace their ancestry back to [Andal](wiki:Andal) adventurers given lands and wives by the [Three Sage Kings](wiki:Three_Sage_Kings) of [House Gardener](wiki:House_Gardener) during the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to the [Reach](wiki:Reach).(wiki:House_Cuy.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) spotted a blue pavilion covered with sunflowers, possibly a use of the colors and sigil of House Cuy, while attending the [Whitewalls tourney](wiki:Whitewalls_tourney).(wiki:House_Cuy.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

House Cuy supports [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) in the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:House_Cuy.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Ser [Emmon Cuy](wiki:Emmon_Cuy) is chosen for Renly's [Rainbow Guard](wiki:Rainbow_Guard), being known as Ser Emmon the Yellow afterwards. Emmon, Ser [Robar Royce](wiki:Robar_Royce), and a third guard are slain by Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) in a fit of rage after the death of Renly.(wiki:House_Cuy.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
