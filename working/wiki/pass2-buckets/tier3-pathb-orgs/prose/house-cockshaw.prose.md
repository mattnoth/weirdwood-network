## Origins

Lord [Alyn Cockshaw](wiki:Alyn_Cockshaw) took part in the [Whitewalls Tourney](wiki:Whitewalls_Tourney) and was defeated by Ser [Glendon Flowers](wiki:Glendon_Flowers) in the tourney. Alyn intended to kill Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Dunk) by forcing him into a well, however, through a turn of events, Alyn ended up in the well.
