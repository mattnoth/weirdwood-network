## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Hotho Humpback](wiki:Hotho_Harlaw) goes to [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers) to support [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy)'s bid to become queen after the passing of her father, King [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy). He gives his cousin, Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw), a copy of Archmaester [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn)'s [The Book of Lost Books](wiki:The_Book_of_Lost_Books) in the hope it might convince Rodrik to marry [his daughter](wiki:Hotho_Harlaw's_daughter).(wiki:House_Harlaw_of_the_Tower_of_Glimmering.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
At the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), Hotho offers his support to [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) if he agrees to marry his daughter. Victarion says that Hotho is Lord Rodrik's heir, though Rodrik himself had designated their cousin [Harras the Knight](wiki:Harras_Harlaw).(wiki:House_Harlaw_of_the_Tower_of_Glimmering.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Hotho switches to [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) after hearing his speech.(wiki:House_Harlaw_of_the_Tower_of_Glimmering.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Hotho reports Ser Harras's capture of [Greyshield](wiki:Greyshield) to Victarion at the feast celebrating their victory after the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields). With Harras named [Lord of Greyshield](wiki:Lord_of_Greyshield) by Euron Crow's Eye, Hotho Humpback is the new heir to Ten Towers and the titles [Lord of the Ten Towers](wiki:Lord_of_the_Ten_Towers), [Lord of Harlaw](wiki:Lord_of_Harlaw), and [Harlaw of Harlaw](wiki:Harlaw_of_Harlaw).(wiki:House_Harlaw_of_the_Tower_of_Glimmering.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
