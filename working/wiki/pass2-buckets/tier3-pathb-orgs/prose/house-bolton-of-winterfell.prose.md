## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), the Bastard of [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton), leads the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort)'s garrison in betraying Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel)'s army of [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) loyalists in a [battle at Winterfell](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell). Although [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) hide during the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell) and later escape,(wiki:House_Bolton_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)(wiki:House_Bolton_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) they are presumed dead.(wiki:House_Bolton_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) weds his son [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing),(wiki:House_Bolton_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) hoping that Tyrion can one day claim [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:House_Bolton_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), Lord of the Dreadfort and Ramsay's father, betrays [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:House_Bolton_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) names Roose the new [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North) and legitimizes Ramsay. [Steelshanks Walton](wiki:Steelshanks_Walton) departs King's Landing with a girl claimed to be [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark); she is to marry Ramsay Bolton and cement [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton)'s claim to Winterfell.(wiki:House_Bolton_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:House_Bolton_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:House_Bolton_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Tywin, however, hopes the Boltons will exhaust themselves fighting the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), allowing Tyrion to later claim Winterfell.(wiki:House_Bolton_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ramsay signs himself as Lord of Winterfell in a letter sent to [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:House_Bolton_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Roose gathers many northern bannermen to Winterfell, the ruins of which are partially repaired. Ramsay weds the fake Arya, actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole), in Winterfell's [godswood](wiki:Godswood).(wiki:House_Bolton_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
During the fierce blizzard, [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) and Jeyne escape Winterfell with the aid of [Abel](wiki:Abel)'s [spearwives](wiki:Spearwives)(wiki:House_Bolton_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) and are found by [Mors Umber](wiki:Mors_Umber).(wiki:House_Bolton_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
