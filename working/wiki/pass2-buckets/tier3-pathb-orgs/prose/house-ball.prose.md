## Origins

### Origins[ ]

House Ball is an ancient house of [First Men](wiki:First_Men) origin that can trace its lineage back to the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes). According to legend, they descend from one of the three husbands of [Florys the Fox](wiki:Florys_the_Fox), a daughter of [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand).(wiki:House_Ball.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### Targaryen dynasty[ ]

Lady [Rosamund Ball](wiki:Rosamund_Ball) was selected as a [lady-in-waiting](wiki:Lady-in-waiting) for Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen). She died defending the queen from an assassination attempt at [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) in [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:House_Ball.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### Blackfyre Rebellions[ ]

Ser [Quentyn Ball](wiki:Quentyn_Ball), better known as Fireball, was the master-at-arms at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) during the reign of King [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen). Aegon promised to name Quentyn to the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), but he died before he could fulfill that promise. When a spot opened during the reign of his son, King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen), Quentyn was passed over. This was the major contributing factor to Quentyn siding with [Daemon Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) during the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion).
Quentyn slew Lord [Lefford](wiki:House_Lefford) outside of [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) and then defeated Lord [Damon Lannister](wiki:Damon_Lannister_(Lord)) in turn. Fireball rescued Daemon when Daeron sent the Kingsguard to arrest him. Quentyn slew all the sons of Lady [Penrose](wiki:House_Penrose) save for the youngest, sparing him out of respect for his mother. Fireball died on the eve of the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field).(wiki:House_Ball.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
