## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Lord Lucias is present at the [Twins](wiki:Twins) for the [wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) and Lady [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey). At the Twins, he is introduced to Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) while he was sitting with his brothers-in-law the evening before the wedding.(wiki:Lucias_Vypren.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Feast for Crows

After [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey) has been killed by the [Brotherhood without Banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_Banners),(wiki:Lucias_Vypren.cite_ref-Rasos81.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Lucias tracks some of the brotherhood's members to [Fairmarket](wiki:Fairmarket), where he loses them.(wiki:Lucias_Vypren.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
