## Origins

During the [Andal Invasion](wiki:Andal_Invasion), he was one of the many warlords that conquered the [Riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). At the [Widow's Ford](wiki:Widow's_Ford), Vypren and his [knights](wiki:Knight) were held back by three sons of Lord [Darry](wiki:House_Darry) for a day and a night before being victorious.(wiki:Vorian_Vypren.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
