## Appearances & Description

According to Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), Lewys resembles his father [Clement](wiki:Clement_Piper), who is short, fat, bowlegged and has red hair that is bushy and wild.(wiki:Lewys_Piper.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Lewys is named as a squire to Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) when Jaime takes command of a force to aid in the [Siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun). He names Jaime's horses [Honor](wiki:Honor_(horse)) and [Glory](wiki:Glory_(horse)). Although Jaime usually does not name his horses, Lewys's choice of names makes him laugh and he accepts them. On route to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), Lewys collects blackberries and offers them to Jaime, who tells him to give some to his other squires, [Josmyn Peckledon](wiki:Josmyn_Peckledon) and [Garrett Paege](wiki:Garrett_Paege), as well as Ser [Ilyn Payne](wiki:Ilyn_Payne).(wiki:Lewys_Piper.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Lewys is the only person who dares ask Jaime where his bruises, which are a result from his secret nightly sword training with Ilyn Payne, come from. Jaime tells him that they are love bites from the lusty wenches at Castle [Hayford](wiki:Hayford).(wiki:Lewys_Piper.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

At [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister) recognizes Lewys from his looks as a son of [Clement Piper](wiki:Clement_Piper). Daven tells Lewys that he beat his brother [Marq](wiki:Marq_Piper) bloody in a [mêlée](wiki:Mêlée) once, after Marq had taken offense at Daven's question whether the woman dancing naked on his shield was his sister. Lewys tells him that it is the [sigil](wiki:Heraldry) of their house and that he does not have a sister. Jaime tells Daven to leave Lewys be after Daven has suggested to Lewys that it is improper to hide behind a naked woman as it makes the attacker feel unchivalrous. When Jaime and Devan later discuss the lynching of [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) foragers and Devan suggests that some lords of the [Trident](wiki:Trident) might be responsible, Jaime think to himself that he would hate to have to execute Lewys, the son of a riverlord, as he has grown fond of the boy.(wiki:Lewys_Piper.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Lewys is present when Jaime threatens Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) in an attempt to convince him to yield Riverrun peacefully.(wiki:Lewys_Piper.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
