## Origins

She was the sister of Lord [Jon Piper](wiki:Jon_Piper) of [Pinkmaiden](wiki:Pinkmaiden), and had at least two other brothers.(wiki:Melony_Piper.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

She became a favorite of Princess [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)). It was only after Queen [Alyssa](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) invited Melony,(wiki:Melony_Piper.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Rhaena's closest companion at the time,(wiki:Melony_Piper.cite_ref-Rtrotd41.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) to join the royal party on King [Aenys's](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen) coronation [progress](wiki:Progress), that Rhaena finally put aside her sullenness to join the celebrations.(wiki:Melony_Piper.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

During Prince [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) and Princess [Rhaena's](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) progress in the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands), Melony arrived at [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) with her brothers to swear the loyalty of House Piper. When Prince Aegon [rose](wiki:Aegon_the_Uncrowned's_rebellion) against King [Maegor](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), it was widely believed that Lord Jon joined due to Rhaena's childhood friend Melony winning him over to the cause.(wiki:Melony_Piper.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

Melony donned man's mail, with a spear, and led the Piper levies against Maegor. She was slain in the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye) in [43 AC](wiki:43_AC) along with three brothers.(wiki:Melony_Piper.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)
