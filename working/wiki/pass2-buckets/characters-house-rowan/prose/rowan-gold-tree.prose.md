## Origins

According to legend, Rowan was so bereft when her lover left her for a rich rival that she wrapped an [apple](wiki:Apple) in her golden hair and planted it upon a hill. From the apple grew a tree whose bark, leaves and fruit were yellow [gold](wiki:Gold).(wiki:Rowan_Gold-Tree.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

[House Rowan](wiki:House_Rowan) traces their roots to Rowan's daughters. In some tales of the [Reach](wiki:Reach), [Lann the Clever](wiki:Lann_the_Clever) was a [bastard](wiki:Bastard) born to Rowan or her sister, [Florys the Fox](wiki:Florys_the_Fox).(wiki:Rowan_Gold-Tree.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
