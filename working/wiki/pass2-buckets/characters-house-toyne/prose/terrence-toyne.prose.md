## Origins

Ser Terrence Toyne was found abed with King [Aegon the Unworthy](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen)'s mistress, Lady [Bethany Bracken](wiki:Bethany_Bracken).(wiki:Terrence_Toyne.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Even though they proclaimed their love, Aegon had them executed.(wiki:Terrence_Toyne.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Terrence_Toyne.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Terrence was dismembered piece by piece, while Bethany was forced to watch, before meeting her own death.(wiki:Terrence_Toyne.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Ser Terrence's brothers attempted to avenge him by killing King Aegon, but were stopped by Prince [Aemon the Dragonknight](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_II)), who died defending him.(wiki:Terrence_Toyne.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Terrence_Toyne.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The brothers paid for their treason with their lives; this event was key in the downfall of [House Toyne](wiki:House_Toyne).(wiki:Terrence_Toyne.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)(wiki:Terrence_Toyne.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
