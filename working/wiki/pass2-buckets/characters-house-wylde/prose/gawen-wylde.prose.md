## Origins

During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), when the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End) was at its worst, Ser Gawen and three other [knights](wiki:Knight) tried to sneak out of Storm's End through a postern gate to surrender. However, the four knights were intercepted and captured. [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), who had command of the castle, ordered them flung from the walls using the catapults. As they were being strapped down, [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Cressen](wiki:Cressen) suggested that they not send them to their deaths, as the remaining rations were low and there was no need to waste good meat.(wiki:Gawen_Wylde.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Gawen was sent to the cells where he later died shortly before Storm's End received food from the smuggler [Davos](wiki:Davos_Seaworth).(wiki:Gawen_Wylde.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
