## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

The Bastard of Bracken is killed by the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings),(wiki:Harry_Rivers.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) presumably during the [burning of Stone Hedge](wiki:Burning_of_Stone_Hedge) or the [castle's liberation](wiki:Liberation_of_Stone_Hedge).

### A Dance of Dragons

Justifying his demand for territory from [House Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood), Lord [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken) tells Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) that his natural son was killed by Lannisters. Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood) alleges to Jaime that Jonos was not Harry's father, as there was little physical resemblance.(wiki:Harry_Rivers.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
