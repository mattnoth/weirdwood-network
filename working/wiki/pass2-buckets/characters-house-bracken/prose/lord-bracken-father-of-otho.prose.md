## Origins

During the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), a Lord Bracken (presumably, the father of Ser [Otho](wiki:Otho_Bracken)) declared for [House Blackfyre](wiki:House_Blackfyre). He sailed to [Myr](wiki:Myr) to hire crossbowmen to aid [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) and [Bittersteel](wiki:Bittersteel), but was delayed by storms.(wiki:Lord_Bracken_(father_of_Otho).cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

In [211 AC](wiki:211_AC), Lord Bracken is dying. As he outlived his eldest son, who died during the [Great Spring Sickness](wiki:Great_Spring_Sickness), his second son, the belligerent Ser Otho, was set to inherit [Stone Hedge](wiki:Stone_Hedge).(wiki:Lord_Bracken_(father_of_Otho).cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

It is unclear if he died by [212 AC](wiki:212_AC) or not. The conspirators of the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion) hoped for Otho to join the forces of [House Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken) to their own, which may imply Otho had succeeded his father.(wiki:Lord_Bracken_(father_of_Otho).cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
