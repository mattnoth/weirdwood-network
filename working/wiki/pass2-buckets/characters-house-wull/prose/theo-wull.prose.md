## Origins

Theo was one of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s companions, fighting alongside him during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). He was slain at the [tower of joy](wiki:Tower_of_joy) in the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) [during a fight](wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy) with three of [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen)'s [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). Afterwards, Eddard had the tower pulled down, and used its stones for cairns for the fallen.(wiki:Theo_Wull.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Appearances & Description

Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) considers Theo to have been faithful.(wiki:Theo_Wull.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

During a fever dream, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) recalls Theo and his other former companions at the tower of joy.(wiki:Theo_Wull.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Storm of Swords

[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) and his companions travel through the [northern mountains](wiki:Northern_mountains). They see almost no-one on their journey, and [Meera Reed](wiki:Meera_Reed) asks if anyone lives there. Bran mentions the [northern mountain clans](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans), including the [Wulls](wiki:House_Wull), and Meera asks her brother [Jojen](wiki:Jojen_Reed) if she recalls correctly that a Wull rode with [their father](wiki:Howland_Reed) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). Jojen replies that it was Theo Wull, called Buckets, and Bran adds that buckets are on House Wull's [sigil](wiki:Sigil).(wiki:Theo_Wull.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
