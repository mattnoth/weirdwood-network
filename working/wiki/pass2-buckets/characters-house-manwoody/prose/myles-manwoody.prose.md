## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Myles accompanies his brother, Lord [Dagos](wiki:Dagos_Manwoody), and his two nephews [Mors](wiki:Mors_Manwoody) and [Dickon](wiki:Dickon_Manwoody) as part of the entourage of Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) and travels to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for the wedding of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Myles_Manwoody.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
