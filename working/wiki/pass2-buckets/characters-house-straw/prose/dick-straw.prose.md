## Origins

The [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince) has had Dick whipped more than any man in the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown).(wiki:Dick_Straw.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Dick has had sex with Pretty [Meris](wiki:Meris) a few times.(wiki:Dick_Straw.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Appearances & Description

Dick has cornflower blue eyes, an unsettling smile and hair as white as flax. His back bears numerous cris-crossed scars from the whippings he receives for poor discipline.(wiki:Dick_Straw.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Dick Straw was part of the [Siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor) and was wary of later marching on [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) and facing [Daenerys Targaryens](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) real [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) and her [Dragons](wiki:Dragon). He was later sent by the [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince) along with the other [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) Windblown to go over to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen).(wiki:Dick_Straw.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) He is currently languishing in the dungeons of the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) with some of the [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) [Windblown](wiki:Windblown).(wiki:Dick_Straw.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
