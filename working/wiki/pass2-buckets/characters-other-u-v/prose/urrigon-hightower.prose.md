## Origins

Urrigon's brother [Peremore](wiki:Peremore_Hightower) had an insatiable thirst for knowledge and therefore surrounded himself with scholars, healers, priests and sorcerers. After Peremore died, King Urrigon granted a tract of land beside the [Honeywine](wiki:Honeywine) to "Peremore's pets" so they might establish and continue their quest for knowledge. Thus the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) was established and the Order of [Maesters](wiki:Maesters) was born.(wiki:Urrigon_Hightower.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
