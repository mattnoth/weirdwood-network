## Origins

Urswyck killed his own wife.(wiki:Urswyck.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Urswyck](wiki:Category:Images_of_Urswyck)*

Urswyck resembles a cadaver with red-rimmed eyes and dark veins showing in the pallid skin on his face and hands.(wiki:Urswyck.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) He has thin, dry hair.(wiki:Urswyck.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) The sellsword has a sly smile and a dry, papery laugh.(wiki:Urswyck.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

Urswyck wears a tattered, ragged leather cloak(wiki:Urswyck.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) fringed with long blond hair.(wiki:Urswyck.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

When the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) enter [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) sees a sickly sellsword whose leather cloak is fringed with long blond hair.(wiki:Urswyck.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Arya observes Faithful Urswyck sitting with [Rorge](wiki:Rorge) during meals.(wiki:Urswyck.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

### A Storm of Swords

Urswyck leads a party of Brave Companions which encounters Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) fighting each other near [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool). Urswyck recognizes Jaime immediately; when Jaime offers him a reward from the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) for his safe return and suggests that Brienne could be ransomed, he remains unmoved. Urswyck informs Jaime that the Brave Companions have changed allegiance and are now serving Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) and his [northmen](wiki:Northmen). Urswyck has Jaime beaten for mocking the Brave Companions and he orders his new captives to be taken to [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat).(wiki:Urswyck.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)

During the journey, Jaime proposes to Urswyck that he should betray Vargo and bring Jaime to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) instead, where [gold](wiki:Gold), a royal pardon, and even [knighthood](wiki:Knight) would await him. Urswyck remains unmoved, slapping Jaime and telling him that he would be a fool to trust the word of an oathbreaker.(wiki:Urswyck.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8)

Urswyck's group finds Vargo sacking a small [sept](wiki:Sept), and [Rorge](wiki:Rorge) begins kicking Brienne when she tries to speak with the captain. Urswyck tells Rorge to not break any of her bones, because "the horse-faced bitch" is worth her price in [sapphires](wiki:Sapphire), as suggested to him by Jaime. Urswyck assists when Vargo has [Zollo](wiki:Zollo) cut off Jaime's sword hand;(wiki:Urswyck.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-9) Urswyck later hangs it on a cord around Jaime's neck.(wiki:Urswyck.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Urswyck remains close to Jaime as they ride to [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), where Jaime and Brienne are given to Lord Bolton.(wiki:Urswyck.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

Almost all of the Brave Companions abandon a fevered Vargo before the [capture of Harrenhal](wiki:Capture_of_Harrenhal) by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane).(wiki:Urswyck.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Feast for Crows

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) comes across three former [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions)—[Timeon](wiki:Timeon), [Shagwell](wiki:Shagwell), and [Pyg](wiki:Pyg)—at the [Whispers](wiki:Whispers). Timeon explains that a wound Vargo received from Brienne biting his ear festered and he became half-mad with fever. Urswyck and [Rorge](wiki:Rorge) argued for leaving [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), but Vargo insisted his men must hold his castle. The Brave Companions eventually abandoned their leader and dispersed, with Urswyck heading towards [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) with a small band.(wiki:Urswyck.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Urswyck, like many other Brave Companions, is still on the run.(wiki:Urswyck.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) instructs Ser [Bonifer Hasty](wiki:Bonifer_Hasty), the new castellan of Harrenhal, to send word if any of the outlaws are found.(wiki:Urswyck.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
