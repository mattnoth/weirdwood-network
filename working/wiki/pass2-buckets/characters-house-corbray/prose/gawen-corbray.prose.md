## Origins

On the account of his prowess at arms and reputation, Ser Gawen was chosen by Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) as the first [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms) for her son, Prince [Maegor Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). Under Corbray's tutelage, Prince Maegor took to swordplay at once.(wiki:Gawen_Corbray.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
