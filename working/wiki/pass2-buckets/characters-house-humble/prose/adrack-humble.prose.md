## Origins

Adrack lost one of his arms in battle. He reportedly has a [rock wife](wiki:Rock_wife) and three [salt wives](wiki:Salt_wife), three of whom were big with child when Adrack left the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands).(wiki:Adrack_Humble.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

## Appearances & Description

Adrack is one-armed.(wiki:Adrack_Humble.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

During the [siege of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin), [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) sends [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) to give peace terms to the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) garrison, promising them food and safe passage. Adrack wants to surrender and kills [Dagon Codd](wiki:Dagon_Codd) with a throwing axe when the latter objects. When he and the other ironborn march unarmed to the Bolton camp, Adrack tells Theon he will name a son after him in gratitude once he returns to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands). That night, Theon hears Adrack screaming along with the other ironborn. The next day, Theon notices that, among the sixty-three flayed bodies of the ironmen displayed on pikes along the [causeway](wiki:Causeway), one of the bodies is one-armed.(wiki:Adrack_Humble.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)
