## Appearances & Description

A stocky youth in his early twenties, Ser Donnel has a wide nose and a shock of thick brown hair. Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) describes him as earnest and homely.(wiki:Donnel_Waynwood.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Like most [Waynwoods](wiki:House_Waynwood), Donnel was always one for ceremony and tradition. He speaks with the solemn courtesy of a youth.(wiki:Donnel_Waynwood.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

After the death of Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), the [mountain clans](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) of the [Vale](wiki:Vale) grew bolder. Ser Donnel desires to lead a hundred men to root out the mountain clans, but Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) forbids it, wanting to keep the [swords of the Vale](wiki:Knights_of_the_Vale) close to home.(wiki:Donnel_Waynwood.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Ser Donnel leads the sortie from the [Bloody Gate](wiki:Bloody_Gate) that saves Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark)'s party from the clans in the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon). After escorting her party to the Bloody Gate, Donnel allows the wounded to recuperate within the battlements. He also sends [ravens](wiki:Raven) to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) and [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon), informing Lady Arryn of her sister's arrival.(wiki:Donnel_Waynwood.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

Lady [Anya Waynwood](wiki:Anya_Waynwood) is present in the [High Hall](wiki:High_Hall) of the Eyrie, surrounded by her sons, when [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) demands a [trial by battle](wiki:Trial_by_battle) to prove his innocence of the claims against him.(wiki:Donnel_Waynwood.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Donnel has replaced Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) as the new [Knight of the Gate](wiki:Knight_of_the_Gate) at the Bloody Gate after Brynden's resignation.(wiki:Donnel_Waynwood.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)(wiki:Donnel_Waynwood.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows

When Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) teaches her some [Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) genealogy and their relation to the [Waynwoods](wiki:House_Waynwood), [Alayne Stone](wiki:Sansa_Stark) recalls that Donnel serves as the Knight of the Gate.(wiki:Donnel_Waynwood.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)(wiki:Donnel_Waynwood.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Donnel_Waynwood.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
