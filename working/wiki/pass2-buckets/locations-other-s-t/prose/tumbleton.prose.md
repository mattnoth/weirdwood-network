## Origins

The prosperous market town of Tumbleton was the site of two battles during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). In the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton), the [Two Betrayers](wiki:Two_Betrayers), Ser [Hugh Hammer](wiki:Hugh_Hammer) and Ser [Ulf White](wiki:Ulf_White), betrayed the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) and unleashed their [dragons](wiki:Dragon), [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor) and [Silverwing](wiki:Silverwing), upon the town. [Lord Footly](wiki:Lord_Footly)'s men surrendered, but were slain. Much of Tumbleton was burned to ash by [dragonfire](wiki:Dragonfire). Tumbleton was savagely sacked by the [greens](wiki:Greens) who encamped outside the walls. Ser [Jon Roxton](wiki:Jon_Roxton) killed Lord Footly and claimed his wife, Lady [Sharis Footly](wiki:Sharis_Footly).(wiki:Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

The leaders of the greens squabbled for control, as their commander, Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower) had been killed in the battle.(wiki:Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) [Thirteen greens](wiki:Caltrops) met in the [Bloody Caltrops](wiki:Bloody_Caltrops) to conspire against the ambitious Hugh and Ulf. The dragons Vermithor, Silverwing, and [Seasmoke](wiki:Seasmoke) fought during the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton), in which the town was further damaged.(wiki:Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Tumbleton was famously restored by Lady Sharis, who pulled down burned buildings and had the town's walls rebuilt. The heads of Seasmoke and Vermithor were put on display in the town square, with travelers able to pay a [penny](wiki:Currency) to look at them and a star to touch them.(wiki:Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Later [Footlys](wiki:House_Footly) followed Sharis's example, but [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) believed the ground to be haunted and the "new town" would never be a tenth the size of the old one.(wiki:Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

## Appearances & Description

Prior to the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Tumbleton was a flourishing market town with shops, [septs](wiki:Sept), and inns,(wiki:Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) including the [Bloody Caltrops](wiki:Bloody_Caltrops) and the [Bawdy Badger](wiki:Bawdy_Badger).(wiki:Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Tumbleton is now much smaller than its size from before the civil war, however.(wiki:Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

A stout but small castle overlooks the town; during the Dance it had a garrison of forty men.(wiki:Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
