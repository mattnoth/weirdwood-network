## Origins

Prior to [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), the [Osgreys](wiki:House_Osgrey) controlled four castles, the greatest of them [Coldmoat](wiki:Coldmoat), and watchtowers on hills. The house's fortunes declined after the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire), however, and the Osgreys were eventually reduced to [landed knights](wiki:Landed_knight) controlling only Standfast and its vicinity.(wiki:Standfast.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Standfast was originally built of black stone. A few centuries before the reign of King [Aerys I Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_I_Targaryen), the castle suffered a partial collapse. The Osgreys rebuilt the north and west faces in pale grey stone above the windows, and they also added turrets above the roofline on the rebuilt sides. At the time of Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey), [grotesques](wiki:Grotesque) decorated the rooftop corners which had not been rebuilt.(wiki:Standfast.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

Eustace supported the pretender king [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) during the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), but the rebellion failed and Eustace's three sons were killed in the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field). Afterwards, King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen) pardoned Eustace, although much of his remaining lands were taken by the crown and his daughter [Alysanne](wiki:Alysanne_Osgrey) was sent to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) as a [hostage](wiki:Hostage). Eustace was allowed to keep Standfast, though it would revert to the crown if he died without a male heir of his body.(wiki:Standfast.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

After Eustace brought home the bones of his sons and allowed Alysanne to be taken away, his wife committed suicide by throwing herself from the top of Standfast tower. Eustace buried his wife and sons among the blackberries they had loved to pick.(wiki:Standfast.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

Eustace's servants included [Sam Stoops](wiki:Sam_Stoops) and [his wife](wiki:Sam_Stoops'_wife).(wiki:Standfast.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9) Because of a conflict with [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber), [Lady of Coldmoat](wiki:Lady_of_Coldmoat), Eustace hired the [sworn swords](wiki:Sworn_sword) Ser [Bennis of the Brown Shield](wiki:Bennis_of_the_Brown_Shield) and Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall), and Duncan's [squire](wiki:Squire), [Egg](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen).(wiki:Standfast.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)

## Appearances & Description

Standfast stands upon a hill and can be seen for leagues around. A single path leads to the tower, which can only be ridden single file. A daub-and-wattle stable sits at the base of the tower.(wiki:Standfast.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Standfast has one entrance, an [oak](wiki:Oak) and [iron](wiki:Iron) door twenty feet above the ground, the stairs leading up to it consist of bottom steps made of blocks of smooth black stone, so worn that they are bowl-shaped in the middle. Higher up, they give way to a steep wooden stair that can be swung up like a drawbridge in times of trouble.(wiki:Standfast.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Standfast's size is deceptive. It is only four stories tall above ground, with the two upper floors having windows and balconies and the two lower floors having only arrow slits. The rebuilt north and west faces of the building are of pale grey stone above and old black stone below. Turrets were added during the rebuild, and two ancient [grotesques](wiki:Grotesque) whose shape had been lost long ago stand on the other two corners. Its pinewood roof is flat, though warped and leaky from time. Underground, however, there is a complex of deep vaults and cellars cut into the hill that Standfast sits upon. The castle has its own well in the undercellar.(wiki:Standfast.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

There is a blackberry thicket around back of Standfast's hill. The [west way](wiki:West_way) runs between Standfast and [Coldmoat](wiki:Coldmoat).(wiki:Standfast.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
