## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

When peace is made with [Tormund Giantsbane](wiki:Tormund_Giantsbane) and his band of [free folk](wiki:Free_folk), Big Liddle is selected by [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), to be one of the members of the honor guard who accompany him to oversee the wildlings through the [Wall](wiki:Wall). When one hundred wildling boys are turned over as hostages to the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), Big Liddle and [Rory](wiki:Rory) remove two wildling girls from the group.(wiki:Duncan_Liddle.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
