## Appearances & Description

Gerold is a wide man with thick arms and shoulders, but he is not tall. His hair is a dirty blond mop and he has a booming voice.(wiki:Gerold_Grafton.cite_ref-TWOW_2-0)

Lord Grafton is a courteous man.(wiki:Gerold_Grafton.cite_ref-TWOW_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) asks Lord Gerold to send him one of his sons to [ward](wiki:Ward) at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) with Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn).(wiki:Gerold_Grafton.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Gerold sends his youngest son, [Gyles](wiki:Gyles_Grafton).(wiki:Gerold_Grafton.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Gerold attends [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Lyonel_Corbray_and_Lady_Corbray) of Lord [Lyonel Corbray](wiki:Lyonel_Corbray) at [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown).(wiki:Gerold_Grafton.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### The Winds of Winter

Gerold and Lord [Benedar Belmore](wiki:Benedar_Belmore) tour the granaries of Lord [Nestor Royce](wiki:Nestor_Royce) at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon). Petyr instructs him to keep grain in reserve instead of selling to Gulltown merchants.(wiki:Gerold_Grafton.cite_ref-TWOW_2-2)
