## Appearances & Description

Dennis is a big man with a thick neck and a red face.(wiki:Dennis_Plumm.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Like his brother [Peter](wiki:Peter_Plumm), Dennis is boisterous, quick to laugh, quick to anger, and quick to forgive.(wiki:Dennis_Plumm.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Dennis and his brothers, Ser [Peter](wiki:Peter_Plumm) and Ser [Harwyn](wiki:Harwyn_Plumm), are among the men sent by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) to the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) in search of his son, after Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) has been released from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) by Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark).(wiki:Dennis_Plumm.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
