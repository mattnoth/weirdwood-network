## Origins

Ossifer was a wealthy but aged man when he wed Princess [Elaena Targaryen](wiki:Elaena_Targaryen) in [176 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). He is said to have died during the [bedding](wiki:Bedding), after seeing his wife naked for the first time.(wiki:Ossifer_Plumm.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i_.28the_sisters_of_baelor_i.29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Elaena became pregnant, and gave birth to a child, [Viserys Plumm](wiki:Viserys_Plumm). However, rumors later suggested that Lord Ossifer had died before he was able to consummate the marriage, and that the child instead had been fathered by King [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen).(wiki:Ossifer_Plumm.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i_.28the_sisters_of_baelor_i.29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Ossifer_Plumm.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The claim that Ossifer fathered a child after his death led to the saying that he had had "a cock six feet long".(wiki:Ossifer_Plumm.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Ossifer_Plumm.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Regardless of his true paternity, Viserys Plumm inherited Ossifer's lands and titles.(wiki:Ossifer_Plumm.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

When [Viserion](wiki:Viserion), one of the [dragons](wiki:Dragon) of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), acts friendly with [Brown Ben Plumm](wiki:Ben_Plumm), commander of the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons), Ben tells Daenerys it is because he has "a drop of [the dragon blood](wiki:House_Targaryen)", which startles her. Ben says that his [Plumm](wiki:House_Plumm) ancestor was a lord who married "[a dragon princess](wiki:Elaena_Targaryen)", and was famous throughout the land, due to having a six-foot-long penis.(wiki:Ossifer_Plumm.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Feast for Crows

When Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) tells Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) that there is no worry that her son, King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), could consummate his marriage with [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), as Tommen is "much too young", Cersei replies that Ossifer Plumm "was much too dead", but that did not stop him from fathering a child. She intends to explain that any child of Margaery's could be recognized as being fathered by Tommen, even if it was physically impossible for him to impregnate her. However, Jaime becomes confused, not recognizing Ossifer's name, so Cersei tells Jaime to forget him and simply guard Tommen until sunrise.(wiki:Ossifer_Plumm.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) encounters Brown Ben, and speculates he is descended from a younger son of [Viserys Plumm](wiki:Viserys_Plumm). He guesses that Daenerys's dragons were fond of Ben, which amuses Ben as it is true, and that he knows why. When Ben says his mother said his father had "a drop of dragon blood", Tyrion replies, "Two drops. That, or a cock six foot long",(wiki:Ossifer_Plumm.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) suggesting that Tyrion believes the rumors about Ossifer's posthumous child being fathered by [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen).
