## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Philip is among the lords of the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) who escort the corpse of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) back to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock). He wears the colors of his house with a cloak of crimson silk in honor of Tywin. Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) thinks Lord Plumm could deal with the outlaws [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) and [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion), but he would not be a good [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:Philip_Plumm.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
