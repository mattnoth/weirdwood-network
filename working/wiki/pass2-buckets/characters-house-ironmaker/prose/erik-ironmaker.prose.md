## Origins

In his youth, Erik was a fearsome reaver who sailed with Lord [Dagon Greyjoy](wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy).(wiki:Erik_Ironmaker.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Appearances & Description

Erik is eighty-eight years old and weighs around twenty stone. He has white hair, and his long, massive beard is white too. Erik has to be carried about in a chair.(wiki:Erik_Ironmaker.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Erik's weapon of choice is a monstrous hammer.(wiki:Erik_Ironmaker.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Erik puts himself forth at the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), where his champions are his grandsons, [Urek](wiki:Urek_Ironmaker), [Thormor](wiki:Thormor_Ironmaker), and [Dagon](wiki:Dagon_Ironmaker). He speaks of his many battles, and of justice meted out by smashing thieves' hands with his hammer, and equates his age with wisdom and size with strength. His gifts are of [silver](wiki:Silver), [bronze](wiki:Bronze), and [steel](wiki:Steel). Erik's speech gathers some support, but he is unable to stand up when [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) demands that he do so. After that, his support crumbles and his claim is soon dismissed. [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) later sees Erik shouting his support for [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), who is chosen king by the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) captains.(wiki:Erik_Ironmaker.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

### A Dance with Dragons

In Euron's absence while at war, Erik rules as [Lord Steward of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_Steward_of_the_Iron_Islands) and castellan of [Pyke](wiki:Pyke).(wiki:Erik_Ironmaker.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

At [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers), [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley) reveals to Asha that her uncle, Euron Crow's Eye, had arranged a marriage between her and Erik. Since Asha fled [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk) after the kingsmoot, a [seal](wiki:Seal) was used to stand in her place for the ceremony.(wiki:Erik_Ironmaker.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Asha has no desire to return to her new husband.(wiki:Erik_Ironmaker.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) After she is captured by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), Asha does not believe Erik could afford to ransom her.(wiki:Erik_Ironmaker.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
