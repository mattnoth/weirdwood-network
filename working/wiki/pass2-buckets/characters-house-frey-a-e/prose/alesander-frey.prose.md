## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

When [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) asks [Ryman Frey](wiki:Ryman_Frey) about Alesander's whereabouts at the wedding of [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) and [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey), Ryman squints at her before telling her Alesander is away. When Catelyn later feels the chainmail underneath the clothing of [Edwyn Frey](wiki:Edwyn_Frey), she thinks of the absence of Alesander, [Olyvar](wiki:Olyvar_Frey), and [Perwyn Frey](wiki:Perwyn_Frey), and Roslin's crying, and realizes [the reason for it](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Alesander_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
