## Origins

Cleos was born at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) to [Genna Lannister](wiki:Genna_Lannister) and Ser [Emmon Frey](wiki:Emmon_Frey).(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Rawoiafcleos_frey.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Appearances & Description

Cleos has a thin face with a weak chin, and thinning stringy brown hair.(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) He is cowardly and not particularly bright.(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

The surcoat worn by Cleos quarters the arms of Houses [Frey](wiki:House_Frey) and [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) He wields a [sword](wiki:Sword)(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and a knife.(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Due to his [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) blood, Cleos fights for the Lannisters when fighting breaks out in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms),(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) although [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey) joins [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark).(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Cleos is captured along with Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) at the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

### A Clash of Kings

[Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), sends Cleos from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with his peace offer. The captive knight is alarmed by [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind), Robb's [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf). Cleos promises to resume his captivity at Riverrun after completing his mission.(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3)

Despite carrying a peace banner, Cleos's party is attacked twice by [broken men](wiki:Broken_men), suffering three dead and six wounded.(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), and his sister, Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), reject Robb's offer at the capital. Tyrion sends Cleos back to Riverrun with the Lannister counter offer, along with an escort of a hundred [red cloaks](wiki:House_Lannister_guards) led by [Vylarr](wiki:Vylarr).(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

Cleos brings the bones of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) to Riverrun.(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Despite arriving under Cleos's peace banner, four false envoys attempt to free Jaime from his Riverrun captivity in a scheme devised by Tyrion. After the plot fails, Jaime is moved to Riverrun's dungeon and an unknowing Cleos is imprisoned in the tower cell previously occupied by Jaime.(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) has a flagon of [wine](wiki:Wine) sent to Cleos so that he is inebriated before she questions him.(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Catelyn releases Jaime from the dungeon, tasking [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) with escorting him to King's Landing in exchange for her daughters, [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark). Cleos is released as well, with Catelyn giving him new terms to bring to Cersei.(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-4) Cleos promises to send a [raven](wiki:Raven) after Tyrion releases the girls.(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Jaime has his head shaved by Cleos, his cousin. Cleos is present for the river chase by Ser [Robin Ryger](wiki:Robin_Ryger),(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-5) and he complains when Brienne insists they cannot remain at the [Inn of the Kneeling Man](wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man).(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Cleos rides a [palfrey](wiki:Horse) as they depart from the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork).(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1)

On [the road](wiki:Duskendale_road) between [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) and [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale),(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) the trio is ambushed by [outlaw](wiki:Outlaw) archers. Cleos is hit by arrows, and his horse spooks and drags him across the ground, killing him. Jaime uses Cleos's sword to fight Brienne before the two are captured by the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions), who then divvy up Cleos's possessions.(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Frustrated by Brienne's stubbornness while riding from [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) to King's Landing, Jaime finds himself unexpectedly missing Cleos.(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Feast for Crows

During the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Jaime speaks of Cleos's death with his parents, [Genna Lannister](wiki:Genna_Lannister) and Ser [Emmon Frey](wiki:Emmon_Frey). Jaime lies by claiming that Cleos was buried by a stream after scattering outlaws.(wiki:Cleos_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
