Benfrey resembles his sister [Roslin](wiki:Roslin_Frey).(wiki:Benfrey_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) He is a younger [knight](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Benfrey_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

When Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) arrives at the [Twins](wiki:Twins) for his wedding, Benfrey is ordered by Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) to lead his sister [Roslin](wiki:Roslin_Frey) into the hall. After Roslin and Edmure meet for the first time, Walder has Benfrey return his sister to her chambers.(wiki:Benfrey_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

During the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Benfrey grabs [Dacey Mormont](wiki:Dacey_Mormont) by the arm, but she smashes a flagon full of [wine](wiki:Wine) in his face and wrenches free.(wiki:Benfrey_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Benfrey dies of a wound he receives at the wedding.(wiki:Benfrey_Frey.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
