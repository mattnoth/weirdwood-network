## Origins

Ser Rayford was present at [Visenya's Hill](wiki:Visenya's_Hill) when King Maegor was challenged by Ser [Damon Morrigen](wiki:Damon_Morrigen), [Grand Captain](wiki:Grand_Captain) of the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons), to a [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven). He kept quiet when Maegor called upon those who would stand beside him but was shamed by the example of [Dick Bean](wiki:Dick_Bean), a simple man-at-arms, and the appeal of Ser [Bernarr Brune](wiki:Bernarr_Brune) and became one of the king's champions.(wiki:Rayford_Rosby.cite_ref-Rtsotd.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Rayford_Rosby.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

During the fight against the Warrior's Sons, Rayford was killed.(wiki:Rayford_Rosby.cite_ref-Rtsotd.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Rayford_Rosby.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
