## Origins

[Daemon III Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_III_Blackfyre) and the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) of [Aegor Rivers](wiki:Aegor_Rivers) landed at [Massey's Hook](wiki:Massey's_Hook). The rebellion had little support, however, as people thought the [Blackfyres](wiki:House_Blackfyre) to be done, as tattered as their banners. Accompanied by his three sons, Princes [Duncan](wiki:Duncan_Targaryen), [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_II_Targaryen), and [Daeron](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_V)), King [Aegon V](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) led the loyalists of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen).

The Blackfyres suffered a shattering defeat in the Battle of Wendwater Bridge, with their casualties filling the river and Daemon slain by Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) of Aegon's [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). The loyalists lost less than a hundred men, but they included Ser [Tion Lannister](wiki:Tion_Lannister).
