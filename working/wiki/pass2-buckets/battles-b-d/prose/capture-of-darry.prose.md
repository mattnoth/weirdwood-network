## Narrative Arc

Many of the [riverlords](wiki:Riverlands) return to their lands after the [battle under the walls of Riverrun](wiki:Battle_under_the_walls_of_Riverrun). The [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) army led by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) and Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) marches on each stronghold in turn.(wiki:Capture_of_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Darry is captured by Lannister soldiers at some point.(wiki:Capture_of_Darry.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
