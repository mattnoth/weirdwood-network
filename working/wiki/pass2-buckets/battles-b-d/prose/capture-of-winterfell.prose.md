## Origins

As [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) planned, after [Dagmer Cleftjaw](wiki:Dagmer) laid siege to [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Torrhen's_Square) as a diversion, Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) raises Winterfell's levies and marches with six hundred men, leaving Winterfell minimally defended.(wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Theon travels inland with about thirty [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) to Winterfell, planning to take it by surprise. His followers include [Aggar](wiki:Aggar), [Black Lorren](wiki:Black_Lorren), [Cadwyl](wiki:Cadwyl), [Drennan](wiki:Drennan), [Endehar](wiki:Endehar), [Gelmarr](wiki:Gelmarr), [Gynir](wiki:Gynir), [Kenned the Whale](wiki:Kenned_the_Whale), [Kromm](wiki:Kromm), [Wex Pyke](wiki:Wex_Pyke), [Red Rolfe](wiki:Red_Rolfe), [Squint](wiki:Squint), [Stygg](wiki:Stygg), [Tymor](wiki:Tymor) and his brothers, [Ulf the Ill](wiki:Ulf_the_Ill), [Urzen](wiki:Urzen), and [Werlag](wiki:Werlag).(wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)(wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Nobles who accompany Theon to Winterfell include [Harrag Sharp](wiki:Harrag_Sharp),(wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) at least four [Harlaws](wiki:House_Harlaw) (including [Dykk](wiki:Dykk_Harlaw) and possibly [Gevin](wiki:Gevin_Harlaw)), and at least two [Botleys](wiki:House_Botley).(wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Aftermath

The grief over the alleged deaths of Bran and Rickon causes [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), the [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), to seek solace in [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling)'s arms and then marry her at the [Crag](wiki:Crag), breaking his marriage alliance with [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey) of the [Twins](wiki:Twins).(wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Realizing that he is in danger of losing the [north](wiki:North), Robb seeks to mend his alliance with the Freys(wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) and gain their aid for the [siege of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin).(wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

The boys' mother, [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), frees Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) in hopes that he will be exchanged at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for Catelyn's daughters, [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark).(wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)(wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Rodrik gathers a host to [Castle Cerwyn](wiki:Castle_Cerwyn) to free Winterfell from Theon's ironborn.(wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

When [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) travels from [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) to Winterfell, Theon refuses his sister's advice to abandon Winterfell.(wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
