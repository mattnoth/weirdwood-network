## Origins

Following [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) defeats at the [Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood) and the [Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) orders Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch) to burn territory in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) from the [Green Fork](wiki:Green_Fork) down to the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye).

## Narrative Arc

[Westermen](wiki:Westerlands) led by Amory attempt to burn [Sow's Horn](wiki:Sow's_Horn) in the [crownlands](wiki:Crownlands), despite [House Hogg](wiki:House_Hogg) being sworn to [House Hayford](wiki:House_Hayford) and not the rebel [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully). The burning fails as a result of Sow's Horn's thick stone walls, however, and Amory retreats northwards back into the riverlands after slaughtering half of Ser [Roger Hogg](wiki:Roger_Hogg)'s sheep and several goats.(wiki:Burning_of_Sow's_Horn.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Aftermath

There is further [fighting at Sow's Horn](wiki:Fighting_at_Sow's_Horn) between Hoggs and [Karstarks](wiki:House_Karstark). Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) passes through Sow's Horn on his way to resolve the sieges of [Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun) and [Raventree Hall](wiki:Siege_of_Raventree) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). Roger is angry that Amory attempted to burn Sow's Horn despite Houses Hogg and Lannister both having been supporters of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Burning_of_Sow's_Horn.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
