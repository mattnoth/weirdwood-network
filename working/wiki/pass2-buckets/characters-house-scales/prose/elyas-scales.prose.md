## Origins

When young King Jaehaerys was on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) during his [regency](wiki:Regency), he spent every morning until midday in the castle yard training with sword, shield, and any other [armament](wiki:Armament). Ser Elyas, along with Ser [Merrell Bullock](wiki:Merrell_Bullock) the commander of the castle garrison, Ser Merrell's [two](wiki:Alyn_Bullock) [sons](wiki:Howard_Bullock), and [knights](wiki:Knight) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), would spar with the king.(wiki:Elyas_Scales.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Appearances & Description
