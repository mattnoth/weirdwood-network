## Origins

Sansara was the daughter of Lord [Donald Tarly](wiki:Donald_Tarly) and Lady [Jeyne Rowan](wiki:Jeyne_Rowan).(wiki:Sansara_Tarly.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Sansara_Tarly.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Her sister, [Samantha](wiki:Samantha_Tarly), married Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower) and then his son, Lord [Lyonel](wiki:Lyonel_Hightower).(wiki:Sansara_Tarly.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

After the death of Queen [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Lady Sam boldly wrote a letter proposing that King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) should marry both her sister Sansara and her step-daughter, [Bethany Hightower](wiki:Bethany_Hightower).(wiki:Sansara_Tarly.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Appearances & Description
