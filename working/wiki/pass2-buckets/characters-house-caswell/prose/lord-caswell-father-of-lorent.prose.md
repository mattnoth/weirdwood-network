## Origins

Old Lord Caswell was [Lord of Bitterbridge](wiki:Lord_of_Bitterbridge) when his son, [Lorent](wiki:Lorent_Caswell), took a [longsword](wiki:Longsword) made by the castle's [armorer](wiki:Armorer) for his own son, [Rolly](wiki:Rolly_Duckfield). After taking revenge by wounding Lorent, Rolly fled the [Reach](wiki:Reach) for the exiles of the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company).
