## Origins

[Bear Island](wiki:Bear_Island) has historically been raided by [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) from the Frozen Shore.(wiki:Men_of_the_Frozen_Shore.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Before [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) negotiated a peace, the men of the Frozen Shore, particularly the walrus men, warred with the cannibal [ice-river clans](wiki:Ice-river_clans).(wiki:Men_of_the_Frozen_Shore.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Clan members are among [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder)'s army as it [marches on and later attacks the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall).(wiki:Men_of_the_Frozen_Shore.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)(wiki:Men_of_the_Frozen_Shore.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The men of the Frozen Shore are among the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) who accompany [Tormund](wiki:Tormund) to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) in declaring a truce with the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) and passing through the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Men_of_the_Frozen_Shore.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
