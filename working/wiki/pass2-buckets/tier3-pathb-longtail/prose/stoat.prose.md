## Culture

Members of [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey) are often compared to stoats on account of their facial features.(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Ermine fur is often included in luxurious garments.(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Ermine spots can be a heraldic tincture; ermine is depicted in the arms of Houses [Florent](wiki:House_Florent), [Gower](wiki:House_Gower), [Prester](wiki:House_Prester), [Rosby](wiki:House_Rosby), [Ruthermont](wiki:House_Ruthermont), and [Varner](wiki:House_Varner), as well as the arms of Ser [Illifer the Penniless](wiki:Illifer_the_Penniless).(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Origins

Prince [Maekar Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen) wore a heavy black cloak trimmed with ermine during the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow).(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle)'s red velvet robe has an ermine collar.(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) wears an ermine cloak when touring the gates of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) wears an ermine mantle during the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast_(299)) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) wears a thick ermine cloak when departing [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
While in the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) sees [wizards](wiki:Wizard) wearing robes of ermine, ruby velvet, and cloth of gold.(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey)'s lap is covered with an ermine robe at the [Twins](wiki:Twins).(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Cersei wears an old gown of black velvet lined with ermine for the wake of her father, Lord [Tywin](wiki:Tywin).(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Lord [Garrison Prester](wiki:Garrison_Prester) wears ermine when the lords of the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) depart [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with Tywin's body.(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Lord [William Mooton](wiki:William_Mooton) pins his ermine cloak with a [salmon](wiki:Salmon)-shaped brooch,(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) and Lord [Gilwood Hunter](wiki:Gilwood_Hunter) uses a brooch with five arrows for his own ermine cloak.(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and his cousin, Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister), mock [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey) for resembling stoats.(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Val](wiki:Val) wears an ermine cloak at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) [Selyse Baratheon](wiki:Selyse_Baratheon) dons an ermine mantle during the [wedding of Sigorn and Alys Karstark](wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark),(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) and [Gerrick Kingsblood](wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood) is given an ermine half-cape by Selyse's retinue.(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
[Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole), pretending to be [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), wears an ermine cloak clasped with a silver [wolf](wiki:Wolf)'s head.(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) pins his ermine mantle with a golden trident.(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
[Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris) has robes trimmed with ermine.(wiki:Stoat.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
