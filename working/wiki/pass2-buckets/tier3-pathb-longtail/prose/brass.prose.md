## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

While [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) visits the [port of Qarth](wiki:Port_of_Qarth) to seek passage west, she meets a [brass-seller](wiki:Brass-seller) who tries to sell her a large polished platter of brass. However, she refuses due to its unreasonably high price (thirty [honors](wiki:Currency)). While the merchant persists and negotiates a lower price with Daenerys, Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) warns her that two strangers have been following them. They use their reflection in the metal to observe the [fat brown man](wiki:Belwas) and the [old man](wiki:Barristan_Selmy). In the end, Daenerys buys the platter for two honors but she is attacked by a [Sorrowful Man](wiki:Sorrowful_Men) before she can leave the brass-seller. The old man saves her, and in the confusion Jorah slams the platter on the fat brown man's head which leaves the metal dented.(wiki:Brass.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

During the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) uses Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I))'s Myrish eye from atop the [Wall](wiki:Wall) to observe the camp of [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder).(wiki:Brass.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After [Dove](wiki:Dove) is added to the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet), [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) pays the [iron price](wiki:Iron_price) by taking the brass-made Myrish eye from its captain.(wiki:Brass.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
