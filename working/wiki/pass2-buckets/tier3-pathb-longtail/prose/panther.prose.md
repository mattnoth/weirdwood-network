## Culture

Panthers have been depicted on [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) banners.(wiki:Panther.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Origins

After actions taken by [Malthar Xaq](wiki:Malthar_Xaq) and other princes of the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles) began a new era of exploration and trade for their people, a fashion developed amongst the [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlord) of [Valyria](wiki:Valyria) for panther cubs from the islands.(wiki:Panther.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_summer_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

When first laying eyes on Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) thinks him as graceful as the panther in the [menagerie](wiki:Menagerie) of [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis).(wiki:Panther.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Similarly, [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) thinks [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) moves like a panther during a fight.(wiki:Panther.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

When [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) takes his leave of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), one of the banners [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) spies traveling with his lords and captains bears a panther on it.(wiki:Panther.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Daenerys thinks [Barsena Blackhair](wiki:Barsena_Blackhair), a [pit fighter](wiki:Pit_fighter) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), moves with the feral grace of a panther.(wiki:Panther.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
