## Origins

Stannis nursed Proudwing back to health, but it never flew higher than the treetops thereafter. His older brother, [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), nicknamed it Weakwing. Stannis's great-uncle, Ser [Harbert](wiki:Harbert), convinced him to abandon Proudwing, which he did.(wiki:Proudwing.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
