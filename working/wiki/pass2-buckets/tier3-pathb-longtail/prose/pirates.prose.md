## Culture

Pirates make their dens upon the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) and venture forth to prey on honest men.(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Some pirates of the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) and the Stepstones can be hired as [sellsail](wiki:Sellsail) companies.(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf824_2-0)
While the [Qohorik](wiki:Qohor) rule the [Qhoyne](wiki:Qhoyne) and the [Darkwash](wiki:Darkwash) north of [Ar Noy](wiki:Ar_Noy), pirates are thickest on the rivers between Ar Noy and the [Sorrows](wiki:Sorrows). There are pirates on [Dagger Lake](wiki:Dagger_Lake), which is full of islands where pirates lurk in hidden caves and secret strongholds. Pirates do not sail into the Sorrows, and the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne) south of the Sarrows is held by [Volantene](wiki:Volantis) galleys.(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
The [Basilisk Isles](wiki:Basilisk_Isles) off [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos) are a base for corsairs who raid shipping in the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea) for captives to sell as [slaves](wiki:Slaves) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai), and [Astapor](wiki:Astapor).(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The northern coast of Sothoryos is navigable by the corsair's road.(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Origins

### Older History[ ]

The [Ravenry](wiki:Ravenry) at the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) was the stronghold of a pirate lord during the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes).(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
[Justin Milk-Eye](wiki:Justin_Massey_(Milk-Eye)) was a pirate king who fought with [Erich VII Durrandon](wiki:Erich_VII_Durrandon) over [Massey's Hook](wiki:Massey's_Hook).(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf783_8-0)
The [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters) have been a favorite haunt of smugglers for hundreds of years, and were pirate nests before that. The ancestors of [House Borrell](wiki:House_Borrell) of [Sweetsister](wiki:Sweetsister) were pirate kings until the [Starks](wiki:House_Stark) came down on them with fire and sword in the [Rape of the Three Sisters](wiki:Rape_of_the_Three_Sisters).(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### Recent History[ ]

Pirates from the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) attacked [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath) while King [Argilac Durrandon](wiki:Argilac_Durrandon) was preoccupied with [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Rfab1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) In [8 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), a pirate king from the Stepstones transported [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) raiders to attack the cape.(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Rfab2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Pirates sacked [Tall Trees Town](wiki:Tall_Trees_Town) in [19 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Rfab3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
From [29](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)–[30 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Prince [Maegor Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), Ser [Osmund Strong](wiki:Osmund_Strong), and Lord [Aethan Velaryon](wiki:Aethan_Velaryon) led campaigns in the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) to root out [Sargoso Saan](wiki:Sargoso_Saan).(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
The author of [A Caution for Young Girls](wiki:A_Caution_for_Young_Girls) claims to have spent time as the plaything of a corsair queen in the [Basilisk Isles](wiki:Basilisk_Isles).(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Rfab7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
Corsairs from the [Pepper Coast](wiki:Pepper_Coast) and pirates from the Stepstones supported [Morion Martell](wiki:Morion_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), in the ill-fated [Fourth Dornish War](wiki:Fourth_Dornish_War) in [83 AC](wiki:83_AC).(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) After the [Myrish Bloodbath](wiki:Myrish_Bloodbath) of [91](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)–[92 AC](wiki:92_AC), the [Archon of Tyrosh](wiki:Archon_of_Tyrosh) allied with pirate kings to force [Myrish](wiki:Myr) rogues from the Stepstones. The defeated Myrmen then established themselves as pirates on [Tarth](wiki:Tarth).(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)
In [96 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), the [Kingdom of the Three Daughters](wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Three_Daughters) conquered the Stepstones and killed hundreds of pirates. Ten years later, Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) and Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) began their own [war for the Stepstones](wiki:War_for_the_Stepstones) against the Triarchy. Daemon called himself [King of the Stepstones and the Narrow Sea](wiki:King_of_the_Stepstones_and_the_Narrow_Sea).(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Rfab12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
[Racallio Ryndoon](wiki:Racallio_Ryndoon) claimed the Stepstones for himself during the [Daughters' War](wiki:Daughters'_War).(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Rfab20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) At the end of the war, Racallio fled his pirate kingdom(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Rfab21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) for the Basilisk Isles.(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Rfab23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
Corsairs from the Basilisks captured highborn maidens from [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) sailing to Westeros to attend King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen)'s [Maiden's Day Ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball).(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Rfab21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1)
[Bellegere Otherys](wiki:Bellegere_Otherys), the first Black Pearl of [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) and the fourth of [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen)'s mistresses, was a pirate queen.(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf411.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
[Ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) engaged in piracy during the era of [Dagon Greyjoy](wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
The [Old Mother](wiki:Old_Mother) and [Samarro Saan](wiki:Samarro_Saan) were pirates among the [Ninepenny Kings](wiki:Ninepenny_Kings).(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf416.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
The Braavosi innkeeper [Pynto](wiki:Pynto) was formerly a pirate.(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) hires [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan), a [Lysene](wiki:Lys) pirate self-styled as the [Prince of the Narrow Sea](wiki:Prince_of_the_Narrow_Sea), to provide him with more ships to aid in his battle for the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Racok0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)
According to [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos), the [Tourmaline Brotherhood](wiki:Tourmaline_Brotherhood) of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) is full of pirates.(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Three days before [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) inspects [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) in [Astapor](wiki:Astapor), a [corsair king](wiki:Corsair_king) was interested in purchasing one hundred of the slave soldiers.(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)
Ser [Denys Mallister](wiki:Denys_Mallister) considers the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) to be pirates and thieves.(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

A corsair king has risen in the [Basilisk Isles](wiki:Basilisk_Isles) and raided [Tall Trees Town](wiki:Tall_Trees_Town) in the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles).(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)
The [Cinnamon Wind](wiki:Cinnamon_Wind) is attacked by pirates off the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones).(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)
[Aurane Waters](wiki:Aurane_Waters) flees [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with the [royal dromonds](wiki:Cersei's_dromonds) after the arrest of Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister). [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) believes that Aurane is sailing to the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) to set himself up as a pirate.(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) sails incognito on the [Meadowlark](wiki:Meadowlark), pirates come aboard when the ship is anchored off the coast of the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands); the crew fends off the pirates at the cost of twelve lives.(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)
Two [Lysene](wiki:Lys) pirate ships, [Goodheart](wiki:Goodheart) and [Elephant](wiki:Elephant_(ship)), capture [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) as slaves at [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome).(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

Lady [Nymella Toland](wiki:Nymella_Toland) tells Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) that a new pirate king, calling himself [Lord of the Waters](wiki:Lord_of_the_Waters), has set up on [Torturer's Deep](wiki:Torturer's_Deep).(wiki:Pirates.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Barianne_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Carianne_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)
