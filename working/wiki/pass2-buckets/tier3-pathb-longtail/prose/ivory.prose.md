## Culture

Ivory is prized in the [Slaver Cities](wiki:Slaver_Cities) and can be used in some cases instead of [gold or silver](wiki:Currency).(wiki:Ivory.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Some women of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor) use ivory combs.(wiki:Ivory.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) The bronze [harpy](wiki:Harpies) at the city's [Plaza of Pride](wiki:Plaza_of_Pride) has eyes and pointed teeth of ivory.(wiki:Ivory.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
According to [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon), the [shan](wiki:Shan) of the [Isle of Elephants](wiki:Isle_of_Elephants) has an ivory palace.(wiki:Ivory.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_leng.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
The [Prince of Pentos](wiki:Prince_of_Pentos) rides in a [palanquin](wiki:Palanquin) of ivory and gold.(wiki:Ivory.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
[Cyvasse](wiki:Cyvasse) pieces can be carved of ivory and [onyx](wiki:Onyx)(wiki:Ivory.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) or ivory and [jade](wiki:Jade).(wiki:Ivory.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Origins

Adventurers from the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles) and from [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) have sought gems, gold, and ivory from the western and eastern coasts of [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos), respectively.(wiki:Ivory.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_sothoryos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
According to Maester [Norren](wiki:Norren_(House_Mooton)) of [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool), Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) gave [Nettles](wiki:Nettles) a hairbrush with an ivory handle.(wiki:Ivory.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Ivory from [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos) was popular in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) during the [Lysene Spring](wiki:Lysene_Spring).(wiki:Ivory.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
[Shiera Seastar](wiki:Shiera_Seastar) was fond of ivory.(wiki:Ivory.cite_ref-10)
After Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey) wed [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber), [Lady of Coldmoat](wiki:Lady_of_Coldmoat), Eustace gifted his [sworn sword](wiki:Sworn_sword), Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall), a Webber [spider](wiki:Spider) sigil brooch made of ivory, with [garnet](wiki:Garnet) spots and silver legs.(wiki:Ivory.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
When [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) wed King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), her uncle [Gerion](wiki:Gerion_Lannister) gave Robert a gilded dagger with an ivory grip and a [sapphire](wiki:Sapphire) pommel.(wiki:Ivory.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
[Roro Uhoris](wiki:Roro_Uhoris) sailed [Cobblecat](wiki:Cobblecat) into the [Shivering Sea](wiki:Shivering_Sea) to trade steel to the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) for ivory, furs, [amber](wiki:Amber), and [obsidian](wiki:Obsidian).(wiki:Ivory.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The [Tourmaline Brotherhood](wiki:Tourmaline_Brotherhood) gives [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) a dragon crown with three heads carved from ivory, [jade](wiki:Jade), and [onyx](wiki:Onyx).(wiki:Ivory.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ivory tusks are among the plundered items used by [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) to bribe [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn).(wiki:Ivory.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
A [Tyroshi](wiki:Tyrosh) brings the head of a [dwarf](wiki:Dwarf) to [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) in a cedar chest inlaid with ivory flowers and vines, but Cersei insists the dead dwarf is not her disgraced brother, [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Ivory.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) clasps his [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) cloak with a silver brooch with battling ivory and onyx swans.(wiki:Ivory.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
[Graces](wiki:Graces) provide an ivory chair for Daenerys to sit upon when she weds [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Ivory.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
Among the tribute collected from the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) passing through the [Wall](wiki:Wall) is an ivory phallus.(wiki:Ivory.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
