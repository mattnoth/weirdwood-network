## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sees wolfsbane in [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle)'s collection of [medicines](wiki:Medicine) and [poisons](wiki:Poison) in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Wolfsbane.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Pycele is a witness during Tyrion's trial for [the death](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). Wolfsbane is among the many small jars of poison which the [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) identifies for the prosecution, with Pycelle claiming that Tyrion stole the poisons from his collection.(wiki:Wolfsbane.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
