## Culture

Many headsmen use axes to dispatch their victims,(wiki:Headsman.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)(wiki:Headsman.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Headsman.cite_ref-Rasos12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Headsman.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) although Ser [Ilyn Payne](wiki:Ilyn_Payne) uses a greatsword.(wiki:Headsman.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Headsman.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The head of the condemned can be placed on a headsman's block during the execution.(wiki:Headsman.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
The [King's Justice](wiki:King's_Justice) is the royal executioner for the [King of the Andals, the Rhoynar, and the First Men](wiki:King_of_the_Andals,_the_Rhoynar,_and_the_First_Men), the monarch who sits the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Headsman.cite_ref-Ragot16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Headsman.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Instead of using a headsman, the [Lords of Winterfell](wiki:Lords_of_Winterfell) from [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) choose to execute condemned people themselves, in the manner of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men).(wiki:Headsman.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
The [Lords of the Eyrie](wiki:Lords_of_the_Eyrie) from [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) throw the condemned out of the [Moon Door](wiki:Moon_Door) instead of employing a headsman.(wiki:Headsman.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
