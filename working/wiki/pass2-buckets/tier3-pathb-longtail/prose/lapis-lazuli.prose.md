## Culture

Altars in [septs](wiki:Sept) can be inlaid with [onyx](wiki:Onyx), [mother-of-pearl](wiki:Mother-of-pearl), and lapis lazuli.(wiki:Lapis_lazuli.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
The massive chairs of the [Pureborn](wiki:Pureborn) of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) are decorated with [goldwork](wiki:Gold) and studded with [amber](wiki:Amber), onyx, lapis, and [jade](wiki:Jade).(wiki:Lapis_lazuli.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) A bronze arch in the city depicts two snakes mating, and their scales are made of delicate flakes of jade, [obsidian](wiki:Obsidian), and lapis.(wiki:Lapis_lazuli.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent) has inlaid lapis flowers on his breastplate.(wiki:Lapis_lazuli.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Lapis_lazuli.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The doublet of Ser [Harys Swyft](wiki:Harys_Swyft) has a bantom rooster depicted in lapis beads.(wiki:Lapis_lazuli.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Some women of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor) wear blue linen decorated with flakes of lapis.(wiki:Lapis_lazuli.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
[Cyvasse](wiki:Cyvasse) boards can have squares of [carnelian](wiki:Carnelian), jade, and lapis.(wiki:Lapis_lazuli.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
