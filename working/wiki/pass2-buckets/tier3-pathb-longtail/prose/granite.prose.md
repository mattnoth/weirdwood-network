## Culture

[Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) is built with grey granite. The castle's massive double walls(wiki:Granite.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and its [Great Keep](wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell))(wiki:Granite.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) are made of granite, as are the pillars,(wiki:Granite.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) tombs,(wiki:Granite.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and statues(wiki:Granite.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) of the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell).
Granite can be found on both sides of the [Wall](wiki:Wall) in the [Frostfangs](wiki:Frostfangs)(wiki:Granite.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) and the [Northern mountains](wiki:Northern_mountains),(wiki:Granite.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) as well as on the shores of [Great Wyk](wiki:Great_Wyk).(wiki:Granite.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
The walls and the triple towers of the main keep of [the seat](wiki:Hollard_castle) of [House Hollard](wiki:House_Hollard) were made of grey granite.(wiki:Granite.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
The monuments and houses of [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) are made of grey granite, whereas the legs and lower torso of the [Titan](wiki:Titan_of_Braavos) and the islands surrounding the city are made of black granite.(wiki:Granite.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
The middle wall of the [triple walls](wiki:Triple_walls_of_Qarth) of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) is made of grey granite and decorated with scenes of war.(wiki:Granite.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

A stonemason chisels the likeness of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) for his granite tomb in the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Granite.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) and his companions pass a granite upthrust as large as Winterfell in the [Northern mountains](wiki:Northern_mountains).(wiki:Granite.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) A group of [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) led by [Jarl](wiki:Jarl) climb the [Wall](wiki:Wall) from a long granite ridgeline in between [Greyguard](wiki:Greyguard) and [Stonedoor](wiki:Stonedoor).(wiki:Granite.cite_ref-Rasos30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) revives [Emmond](wiki:Emmond) with the [kiss of life](wiki:Kiss_of_life) next to a rounded granite boulder on the shores of [Great Wyk](wiki:Great_Wyk).(wiki:Granite.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
