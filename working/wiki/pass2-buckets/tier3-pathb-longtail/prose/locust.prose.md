## Culture

Locusts are eaten in different locations in [Essos](wiki:Essos) in a number of recipes, such as locust pie in the Eastern Market of [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak),(wiki:Locust.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) fried locust in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis),(wiki:Locust.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) or honeyed locusts in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Locust.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

During her time in Vaes Dothrak, [khaleesi](wiki:Khaleesi) [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) tastes locust pie at the [Eastern Market](wiki:Eastern_Market).(wiki:Locust.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Daenerys Targaryen compares the hosts of [freedmen](wiki:Freedmen) that have followed her from [Astapor](wiki:Astapor) to the outskirts of [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) to locusts with sandals, for they have eaten the land bare.(wiki:Locust.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

At the [Merchant's House](wiki:Merchant's_House), [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and his captor [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) break their fast with fried locusts, among other dishes.(wiki:Locust.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
During the celebrations for her marriage to [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) at [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit), Queen Daenerys is served [honeyed locusts](wiki:Poisoned_locusts), which she refuses despite Hizdahr urging her to taste them. Her [Queensguard](wiki:Queensguard), [Strong Belwas](wiki:Strong_Belwas), gorges on them, only to retch them minutes later.(wiki:Locust.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) The locusts turn out to have been [poisoned](wiki:Poison).(wiki:Locust.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
As part of the plot devised by [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) and Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) to overthrow King Hizdahr, the [Brazen Beasts](wiki:Brazen_Beasts) who are part of the plan put on masks in the likeness of locusts.(wiki:Locust.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
