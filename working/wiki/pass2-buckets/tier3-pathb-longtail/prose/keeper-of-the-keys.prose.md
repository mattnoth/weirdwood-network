## Origins

After reaching majority and beggining to rule in his own right, King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) replaced the Keeper of the Keys, among other lesser offices.(wiki:Keeper_of_the_Keys.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) notes that several key offices of the royal bureaucracy are filled by Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish)'s men, among them all four Keepers of the Keys.(wiki:Keeper_of_the_Keys.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord [Gyles Rosby](wiki:Gyles_Rosby), the new [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin), intends to replace one of the keepers who had been appointed by Petyr.(wiki:Keeper_of_the_Keys.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
