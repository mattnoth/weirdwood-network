## Origins

Ebony from the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles) was popular in the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold).(wiki:Ebony.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_summer_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
According to [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom), [Sandoq the Shadow](wiki:Sandoq_the_Shadow) played a stringed instrument of ebony and [goldenheart](wiki:Goldenheart) for [Larra Rogare](wiki:Larra_Rogare).(wiki:Ebony.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Maester [Caleotte](wiki:Caleotte) designed a rolling chair with wheels of ebony and iron for [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne).(wiki:Ebony.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos) gives [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) a palanquin of ebony and gold.(wiki:Ebony.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Within the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying), Daenerys sees doors of ebony and [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood). [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) flies to the top of a door and bites at the carved wood.(wiki:Ebony.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Following the [siege of Meereen](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen), Daenerys replaces the [harpy](wiki:Harpies) throne in the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid)'s audience chamber with a simple bench of polished ebony.(wiki:Ebony.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Ebony.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) delivers the [Mountain's skull](wiki:Mountain's_skull) to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) in a box of carved ebony.(wiki:Ebony.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
