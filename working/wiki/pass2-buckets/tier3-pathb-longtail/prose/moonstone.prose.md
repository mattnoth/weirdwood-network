## Culture

Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) has a necklace of [sapphires](wiki:Sapphire) and moonstones.(wiki:Moonstone.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)(wiki:Moonstone.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Lord [Guncer Sunglass](wiki:Guncer_Sunglass) wears moonstones on his neck, wrists, and fingers.(wiki:Moonstone.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
[Nightfall](wiki:Nightfall), the [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) sword of Ser [Harras Harlaw](wiki:Harras_Harlaw), has a moonstone pommel.(wiki:Moonstone.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Within the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor), a chamber has carvings of [the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) with eyes of [onyx](wiki:Onyx), [jade](wiki:Jade), [malachite](wiki:Malachite), and yellow moonstone.(wiki:Moonstone.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

At the [tourney](wiki:Tourney_on_King_Joffrey's_name_day) for King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon)'s [name day](wiki:Name_day), [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) wears a moonstone hair net that had been a gift from him.(wiki:Moonstone.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Sansa also wears the moonstone hair net when the court rides to the harbor to see Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) off to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Moonstone.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

When Sansa is forced to wed [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) has her wear the moonstone necklace and earrings that Joffrey had given her.(wiki:Moonstone.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) gives [Marillion](wiki:Marillion) a belt studded with moonstones.(wiki:Moonstone.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Tyrion Lannister meets [Sweets](wiki:Sweets), the beloved [slave](wiki:Slave) of [Yezzan zo Qaggaz](wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz), who dresses in moonstones and [Myrish](wiki:Myr) lace.(wiki:Moonstone.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
