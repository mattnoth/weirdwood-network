## Appearances & Description

The woods walkers were a small, shy, and gentle folk.(wiki:Ifequevron.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Many [maesters](wiki:Maester) believe the Ifequevron were kin to the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Ifequevron.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
[Bryan of Oldtown](wiki:Bryan_of_Oldtown) encountered [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibbenese) who said they had never seen any woods walkers, but claimed that they blessed households that left overnight offerings of leaf, stone, and water.(wiki:Ifequevron.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Origins

Some believe the Ifequevron were extinguished by the [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibbenese), when they conquered a large swath of the territory directly south of the island of [Ib](wiki:Ib). Others believe the Ifequevron went into hiding or fled to other lands.(wiki:Ifequevron.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
While the Ifequevron still lived in the northern forests, the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) shunned those lands, either out of reverence for the woods walkers or for fear of their powers. However, after they vanished, the Dothraki began to attack the Ibbenese territories.(wiki:Ifequevron.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
The Dothraki still refer to the great forest along the northern coast of central Essos as the [Kingdom of the Ifequevron](wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Ifequevron).(wiki:Ifequevron.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) The Ifequevron left behind one settlement in the forest, now called by the Dothraki [Vaes Leisi](wiki:Vaes_Leisi), or "City of Ghosts".(wiki:Ifequevron.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_vaes_leisi.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon), the first [Westerosi](wiki:Westerosi) to visit the Kingdom of the Ifequevron, wrote of carved trees, haunted grottoes, and strange silences.(wiki:Ifequevron.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
