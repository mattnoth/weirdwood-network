## Culture

A khal leads a [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) and its various [khas](wiki:Khas). The khal is usually considered the strongest or fiercest of a khalasar; he must be a ruthless and cunning warrior to deal with any who challenge his authority or get in his way. It is said that if a khal cannot ride, he cannot lead.(wiki:Khal.cite_ref-Ragot68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Khals keep trusted companions and guards known as [bloodriders](wiki:Bloodrider), who follow the khal everywhere, even in death. If their khal falls in battle, the bloodriders live only long enough to avenge his death. They refer to each other as "blood of my blood" as a sign of respect and the closeness of their bond.(wiki:Khal.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
A khal's wife is called a [khaleesi](wiki:Khaleesi), while his heir to a khalasar is named the [khalakka](wiki:Khalakka).(wiki:Khal.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) When a khal dies, his khaleesi joins the [dosh khaleen](wiki:Dosh_khaleen) in [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak). Even the mightiest khals bow down before the authority and wisdom of the dosh khaleen.(wiki:Khal.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Khals bring their captives to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay) to be sold into [slavery](wiki:Slavery).(wiki:Khal.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The [Slaver Cities](wiki:Slaver_Cities)(wiki:Khal.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_rise_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Khal.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Pentos](wiki:Pentos),(wiki:Khal.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_pentos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Qohor](wiki:Qohor),(wiki:Khal.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_qohor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and [Selhorys](wiki:Selhorys)(wiki:Khal.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) have been known to pay lavish gifts to avoid conflict with the Dothraki.

## Origins

Led by their khals, the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) conquered what is now known as the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea) during the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood) which followed the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria).(wiki:Khal.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) marries Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) in [a ceremony](wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen) outside [Pentos](wiki:Pentos).(wiki:Khal.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Drogo slays Khal [Ogo](wiki:Ogo) when their [khalasars](wiki:Khalasar) clash near a town of the [Lhazareen](wiki:Lhazareen).(wiki:Khal.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Drogo's [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) breaks apart after he collapses.(wiki:Khal.cite_ref-Ragot68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Instead of joining the [dosh khaleen](wiki:Dosh_khaleen) in [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak) after Drogo's death, Daenerys hatches three [dragons](wiki:Dragon) from his funeral pyre and leads her own khalasar as their ruling [khaleesi](wiki:Khaleesi).(wiki:Khal.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
