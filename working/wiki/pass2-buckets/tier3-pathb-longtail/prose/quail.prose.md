## Origins

A [black tomcat](wiki:Balerion_(cat)) once snatched a roast quail from the hands of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) during dinner at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Quail.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) takes the finest cook in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) into his service after becoming [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), and he enjoys quails drowned in butter during his meeting with [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt).(wiki:Quail.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
[Koss](wiki:Koss) and [Kurz](wiki:Kurz) hunt deer and quail for [Yoren](wiki:Yoren)'s band of recruits.(wiki:Quail.cite_ref-Racok9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Ser [Wendel Manderly](wiki:Wendel_Manderly) offers to hunt quail for [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) near [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge).(wiki:Quail.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Quail is served at the [wedding of Petyr Baelish and Lysa Arryn](wiki:Wedding_of_Petyr_Baelish_and_Lysa_Arryn).(wiki:Quail.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Tyrion is served quails in honey at [Illyrio's manse](wiki:Illyrio's_manse) in [Pentos](wiki:Pentos).(wiki:Quail.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) has a brace of quail at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Quail.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
