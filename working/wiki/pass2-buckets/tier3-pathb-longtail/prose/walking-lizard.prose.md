## Appearances & Description

Walking lizards appear to be [tattooed](wiki:Tattoo), and chase their prey on their powerful hind legs. They also have long curved claws on their hind legs, which they use to rip their prey apart.(wiki:Walking_lizard.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf834_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Syrio Forel](wiki:Syrio_Forel) tells [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) about the rare live specimens of walking lizards kept in the exotic [menagerie](wiki:Sealord's_menagerie) of the [Sealord](wiki:Sealord) of [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Walking_lizard.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Dance With Dragons[ ]

[Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) wonders if the rumors about [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) and her [dragons](wiki:Dragon) are true, or if they are only tattooed lizards from the swamps of [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos).(wiki:Walking_lizard.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
