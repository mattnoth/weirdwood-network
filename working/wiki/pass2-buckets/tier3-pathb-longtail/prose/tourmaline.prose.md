## Culture

[Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) has tourmaline among his various rings.(wiki:Tourmaline.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
The [Tourmaline Brotherhood](wiki:Tourmaline_Brotherhood) is a faction of merchant princes in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth).(wiki:Tourmaline.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Origins

The [Tourmaline Emperor](wiki:Tourmaline_Emperor) was a legendary ruler of the [Great Empire of the Dawn](wiki:Great_Empire_of_the_Dawn).(wiki:Tourmaline.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
