## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) reminisces to [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) about King [Maekar Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen)'s summer, which occurred when Pycelle was a young man still forging his chain. It was hotter than the current one, and nearly as long. Pycelle says that there were fools, even in the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel), who took that to mean that the Great Summer had come at last, the summer that never ends. But in the seventh year it broke suddenly, and there was a short [autumn](wiki:Autumn) and a terrible long [winter](wiki:Winter).(wiki:Great_Summer.cite_ref-Ragot25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

In [299 AC](wiki:299_AC), the longest summer in living memory finally ends. It lasted [ten years, two turns, and sixteen days](wiki:Measurement).(wiki:Great_Summer.cite_ref-Racok0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

In [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), High Priest [Benerro](wiki:Benerro) makes a speech declaring [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) as [Azor Ahai](wiki:Azor_Ahai) reborn, saying:(wiki:Great_Summer.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
and her triumph over darkness will bring a summer that will never end...
