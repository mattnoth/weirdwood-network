## Culture

The arms of [House Mollen](wiki:House_Mollen) depict a pine tree covered with snow, those of [House Hoare](wiki:House_Hoare) include a dark green pine, and [House Orkwood](wiki:House_Orkwood) has dark green pines strewn closely together. The arms of [House Liddle](wiki:House_Liddle) include three pinecones.
Seasoned water is better than green pine for water casks to prevent spoilage.(wiki:Pine.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)
Pinewood can be used in shipbuilding,(wiki:Pine.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)(wiki:Pine.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)(wiki:Pine.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) tables(wiki:Pine.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0),(wiki:Pine.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0) and staves.(wiki:Pine.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0) Pine shields are light and more likely to catch the weapon of an enemy, but heavier oak shields can be more protective.(wiki:Pine.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) carries a pine shield.(wiki:Pine.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0) [Standfast](wiki:Standfast) has a pinewood roof.(wiki:Pine.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1)
"[Black Pines](wiki:Black_Pines)" is a song of the [northern mountain clans](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans).(wiki:Pine.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_mountain_clans.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)

## Origins

When [Joffrey Caswell](wiki:Joffrey_Caswell) was three, Ser [Kyle the Cat](wiki:Kyle_the_Cat) carved him a sword out of pine.(wiki:Pine.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0) Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) wielded a shield of pine and [iron](wiki:Iron) during the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls).(wiki:Pine.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) advises [Hother](wiki:Hother_Umber) and [Mors Umber](wiki:Mors_Umber) to cooperate with Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) in building longships from [House Umber](wiki:House_Umber)'s pine and [oak](wiki:Oak).(wiki:Pine.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[The Liddle](wiki:The_Liddle) gives [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) and his companions oatcakes baked with pinenuts or blackberries.(wiki:Pine.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
During the [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding) for King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), the second course is a pastry coffyn filled with pork, pine nuts, and eggs.(wiki:Pine.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

During the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) has a chest of pinecones upended to display the so-called wealth of [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Pine.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Clansmen](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans) from the [northern mountains](wiki:Northern_mountains) cover themselves in pine boughs before the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Pine.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-1)
Pinewood poles are used as stakes when [House Peasebury](wiki:House_Peasebury) men are burned for [cannibalism](wiki:Cannibalism) during the [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell).(wiki:Pine.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0)
