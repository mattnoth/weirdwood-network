## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), while traveling with [Yoren](wiki:Yoren)'s [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) recruits in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), encounters a prisoner, the Lorathi [Jaqen H'ghar](wiki:Jaqen_H'ghar). Jaqen speaks the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue), but refers to himself only as "a man" or "this man", and never refers to Arya by any direct pronoun such as "you", instead calling her "a boy" (while she is disguised as a boy)(wiki:Lorathi_language.cite_ref-Racok5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) or later "a girl" or "this girl".(wiki:Lorathi_language.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) After Jaqen reveals an association with the [Faceless Men](wiki:Faceless_Men) of [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), and shifts his face to another appearance while claiming Jaqen is dead, he no longer uses the Lorathi speaking habits, calling himself "I" and "me" and calling Arya "you".(wiki:Lorathi_language.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
