## Origins

According to [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) legend, [Nagga](wiki:Nagga) was the first and mightiest sea dragon, a creature which fed on [krakens](wiki:Kraken) and [leviathans](wiki:Leviathan). She was slain by the [Grey King](wiki:Grey_King), however, and her bones formed part of the [Grey King's Hall](wiki:Grey_King's_Hall).(wiki:Sea_dragon.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
