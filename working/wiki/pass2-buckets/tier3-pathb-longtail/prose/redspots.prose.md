## Origins

Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) had redspots when she was eight, while Prince [Trystane Martell](wiki:Trystane_Martell) had the disease when he was four.(wiki:Redspots.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Princess Arianne Martell plots to have Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) crowned as queen. Myrcella's [handmaid](wiki:Handmaid) [Rosamund Lannister](wiki:Rosamund_Lannister) is to serve as a decoy while Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) smuggles the real princess out of [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear). To make the disguise convincing, Myrcella curls Rosamund's hair, dresses the handmaid in her own bedrobe, and paints red dots on the girl's face so it appears she has redspots. Ser Arys tells [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Caleotte](wiki:Caleotte) about the supposed redspots, and the maester provides a jar of salve, but stays away because he never had the disease as a child.(wiki:Redspots.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
