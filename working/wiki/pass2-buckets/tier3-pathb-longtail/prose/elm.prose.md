## Culture

Elms can be found in the [godswoods](wiki:Godswood) of the [Red Keep](wiki:Godswood_of_the_Red_Keep),(wiki:Elm.cite_ref-Ragot25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) [Riverrun](wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun),(wiki:Elm.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) [White Harbor](wiki:Godswood_of_White_Harbor),(wiki:Elm.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) and [Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Elm.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Elms line a street in [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) which leads to [Barrow Hall](wiki:Barrow_Hall).(wiki:Elm.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) included an elm and a shooting star in his sigil.(wiki:Elm.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[Crossed Elms](wiki:Crossed_Elms) is a village near the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Elm.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Origins

Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) set up his camp by a tall, leafy elm during the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow).(wiki:Elm.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
Lord [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers)'s pavilion was raised by a spreading elm tree near [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) during his suppression of the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion).(wiki:Elm.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
When he was a child, [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) broke his arm in by falling from an elm in the [godswood of Riverrun](wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun).(wiki:Elm.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) takes his evening meal at a trestle table set up underneath an elm near the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad) in the northern [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Elm.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) sleeps in the branches of an elm while traveling near the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye).(wiki:Elm.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Arya sees mossy elms in the riverlands.(wiki:Elm.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
The [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) hang Septon [Utt](wiki:Utt) beneath a tall elm following the [battle at the burning septry](wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry).(wiki:Elm.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
[Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey) passes a skeletal elm near [Oldstones](wiki:Oldstones).(wiki:Elm.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Brienne Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth) finds wild elms growing amidst the ruins of [Hollard castle](wiki:Hollard_castle).(wiki:Elm.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
The brotherhood without banners hang outlaws from elms and other trees,(wiki:Elm.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) and they attempt to hang Ser [Hyle Hunt](wiki:Hyle_Hunt) and [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne) from elms.(wiki:Elm.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sees bare brown elms along the [Valyrian road](wiki:Valyrian_road) between [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) and [Ghoyan Drohe](wiki:Ghoyan_Drohe).(wiki:Elm.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
