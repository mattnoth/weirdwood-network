## Culture

Turtles are worshipped by the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar).(wiki:Turtle.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Turtle.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf28.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The sigil of [House Estermont](wiki:House_Estermont) includes a turtle,(wiki:Turtle.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and the sigil of [House Tudbury](wiki:House_Tudbury) includes a tortoise.(wiki:Turtle.cite_ref-6)

## Origins

According to legend, the [First Turtle War](wiki:First_Turtle_War), the first of the [Rhoynish Wars](wiki:Rhoynish_Wars), began when [Valyrians](wiki:Valyria) butchered a gigantic turtle known to the [Rhoynish](wiki:Rhoynar) as the [Old Men of the River](wiki:Old_Men_of_the_River) and held as consorts of [Mother Rhoyne](wiki:Mother_Rhoyne).(wiki:Turtle.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf28.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

While traveling along the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne), [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sees a multitude of turtles, including a giant one which [Yandry](wiki:Yandry) and [Ysilla](wiki:Ysilla) believe to be the [Old Man of the River](wiki:Old_Man_of_the_River).(wiki:Turtle.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
