## Origins

Some Shadow Men have engaged in [piracy](wiki:Piracy) and reaving. The [maroon emperors](wiki:Maroon_emperors) of [Yi Ti](wiki:Yi_Ti) kept their court in [Jinqi](wiki:Jinqi) to better guard the borders of the [Golden Empire](wiki:Golden_Empire_of_Yi_Ti) from reavers from the [Shadow Lands](wiki:Shadow_Lands).(wiki:Shadow_Men.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf841_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

During her stay at [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak) to be presented to the [dosh khaleen](wiki:Dosh_khaleen), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) often spends her mornings in the Eastern Market. She enjoys watching the people there, including dour and frightening masked Shadow Men.(wiki:Shadow_Men.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
