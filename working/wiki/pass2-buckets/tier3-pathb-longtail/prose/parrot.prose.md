## Origins

[Monkeys](wiki:Monkey), [apes](wiki:Ape), [panther](wiki:Panther) cubs, and parrots from the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles) were popular among the [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlords) of the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold).(wiki:Parrot.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_summer_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
[Roggerio Rogare](wiki:Roggerio_Rogare) filled the [Mermaid](wiki:Mermaid_(pillow_house)), his [pillow house](wiki:Pillow_house) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), with parrots from the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles) and [monkeys](wiki:Monkey) from [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos). After Roggerio was sentenced to seven lashes by Lord [Torrhen Manderly](wiki:Torrhen_Manderly), he sold everything in the Mermaid so he could purchase a cog which he named [Mermaid's Daughter](wiki:Mermaid's_Daughter).(wiki:Parrot.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
A great red parrot was part of the immense pie at the [wedding at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls).(wiki:Parrot.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) mocks [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) by comparing her to the talking birds of the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles).(wiki:Parrot.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
In the Western Market of [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak), a birdseller teaches a green-and-red parrot to say the name of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), but she refuses to buy the bird.(wiki:Parrot.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
