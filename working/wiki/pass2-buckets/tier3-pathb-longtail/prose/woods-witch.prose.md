## Origins

### Ancient History[ ]

A beautiful woods witch married both legendary founders of [House Tarly](wiki:House_Tarly), the twin brothers [Harlon the Hunter](wiki:Harlon_the_Hunter) and [Herndon of the Horn](wiki:Herndon_of_the_Horn).(wiki:Woods_witch.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
The [Green Queen](wiki:Green_Queen) was a woods witch who challenged the rule of King [Durwald I Durrandon](wiki:Durwald_I_Durrandon).(wiki:Woods_witch.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_durrandon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
King [Mern III Gardener](wiki:Mern_III_Gardener) gave [gold](wiki:Gold) and honors to a woods witch who claimed she could raise armies of the [dead](wiki:Wights) to fight off [Andals](wiki:Andals) if they invaded the [Reach](wiki:Reach).(wiki:Woods_witch.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
The wife of Ser [Clarence Crabb](wiki:Clarence_Crabb) was a woods witch. Stories about the [Whispers](wiki:Whispers) claim that she could bring back to life with a kiss the heads of those killed by Clarence.(wiki:Woods_witch.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
In [Hardhome: An Account of Three Years Spent Beyond-the-Wall among Savages, Raiders, and Woods-witches](wiki:Hardhome:_An_Account_of_Three_Years_Spent_Beyond-the-Wall_among_Savages,_Raiders,_and_Woods-witches), Maester [Wyllis](wiki:Wyllis) wrote about time he spent among the healers of [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome).(wiki:Woods_witch.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_wall_and_beyond:_the_wildlings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### Targaryen Era[ ]

According to Septon [Eustace](wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)), [Alys Rivers](wiki:Alys_Rivers) was a woods witch.(wiki:Woods_witch.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
At the command of their father, King [Jaehaerys II](wiki:Jaehaerys_II_Targaryen), [Rhaella Targaryen](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen) and her brother [Aerys](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) were married. Jaehaerys was influenced by [a certain woods witch](wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart) brought to court by [Jenny of Oldstones](wiki:Jenny_of_Oldstones), who prophesied that the [prince that was promised](wiki:Prince_that_was_promised) would be born from their line.(wiki:Woods_witch.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
[Varamyr Sixskins](wiki:Varamyr) knew a woods witch [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) when he was a child.(wiki:Woods_witch.cite_ref-Radwdprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
[Lenn](wiki:Lenn)'s mother was a woods witch.(wiki:Woods_witch.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
A woods witch in the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) taught [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) how to brew [moon tea](wiki:Moon_tea) to prevent pregnancy after she lost her [maidenhead](wiki:Maiden) to a [Lyseni](wiki:Lys).(wiki:Woods_witch.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Storms[ ]

Travelling with the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) meets a woods witch known as the [ghost of High Heart](wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart).(wiki:Woods_witch.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After the defeat of [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) at the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), [Mother Mole](wiki:Mother_Mole) claims to have had a vision of a fleet of ships coming to carry the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) away to safety across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea). Thousands of those who fled the battle are desperate enough to believe her. Mother Mole leads them all to [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome) to pray and await salvation from across the sea.(wiki:Woods_witch.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Women and children among the refugees end up captured by [Lysene](wiki:Lys) slavers, however.(wiki:Woods_witch.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
In [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) is astonished to learn from Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) that her grandsire, King [Jaehaerys II Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_II_Targaryen), commanded that [her father](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) and [her mother](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen) be wed after the king heard a prophecy from [a woods witch](wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart).(wiki:Woods_witch.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
