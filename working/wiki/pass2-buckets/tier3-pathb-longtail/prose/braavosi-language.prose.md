## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) learns some Braavosi words while sailing on the [Titan's Daughter](wiki:Titan's_Daughter) from [Saltpans](wiki:Saltpans) to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), such as the words for please, thank you, sea, star, and fire wine.(wiki:Braavosi_language.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
The [waif](wiki:Waif) teaches Arya the tongue of Braavos at the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White), while Arya teaches the waif the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue).(wiki:Braavosi_language.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Arya learns more of the Braavosi tongue while acting as Cat of the Canals.(wiki:Braavosi_language.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) knows some Braavosi.(wiki:Braavosi_language.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Maester [Kedry](wiki:Kedry) is fluent in the tongues of all of the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities).(wiki:Braavosi_language.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Arya hears priests of the [Faceless Men](wiki:Faceless_Men) speaking Braavosi and [High Valyrian](wiki:High_Valyrian) during their meeting at the House of Black and White.(wiki:Braavosi_language.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
