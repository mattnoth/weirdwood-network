## Culture

[Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) sigils depicting snakes include those of Houses [Gargalen](wiki:House_Gargalen), [Lynderly](wiki:House_Lynderly), [Paege](wiki:House_Paege), [Saltcliffe](wiki:House_Saltcliffe), [Wydman](wiki:House_Wydman), and [Wyl](wiki:House_Wyl).(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-11)
[Snakewood](wiki:Snakewood) is the seat of House Lynderly in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn), named for the nearby [forest](wiki:Snakewood_(forest)).(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Rtloiafwesteros.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
The [Milk Snakes](wiki:Milk_Snakes) are one of the [Vale mountain clans](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) of the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon).(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
A serpent [god](wiki:Religion) is worshipped in the lost city of [Lyber](wiki:Lyber).(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
[Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) are fond of adding snake venom to their [wine](wiki:Wine)(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) and [food](wiki:Food).(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) As a result, they are often compared to snakes by other Westerosi.(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2)
Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) owns an ornate snake bracelet with [copper](wiki:Copper) and [gold](wiki:Gold) scales.(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1)
Slender pole boats, known as serpent boats, are used to move through [Braavos](wiki:Braavos)'s canals.(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
A [mystery knight](wiki:Mystery_knight) known as The Serpent in Scalet entered the tourney at the [Golden Wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) and was ultimitely revealed to be [Jonquil Darke](wiki:Jonquil_Darke), the [bastard](wiki:Bastard) daughter of [Lord Darklyn](wiki:Lord_Darklyn_(father_of_Jonquil_Darke)).(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) was the greatest seafarer the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) had ever known.(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) He was best known as the Sea Snake, a nickname he got from his [ship](wiki:Sea_Snake).(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)
Lady [Serala of Myr](wiki:Serala_of_Myr) was known as The Lace Serpent.(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) earned his nickname The Red Viper after using [poisoned](wiki:Poison) [blades](wiki:Armament) during a duel to first blood with Lord [Edgar Yronwood](wiki:Edgar_Yronwood), killing the old lord in the process.(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) Oberyn's eight bastard daughters are collectively known as the [Sand Snakes](wiki:Sand_Snakes).(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-2)
[Stonesnake](wiki:Stonesnake) is a veteran [ranger](wiki:Ranger) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)
[Senerra She-Snake](wiki:Senerra_She-Snake) is a [pit fighter](wiki:Pit_fighter) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-28)

## Origins

After being captured by [Lord Wyl](wiki:Lord_Wyl_(conquest_of_Dorne)), Ser [Aemon Targaryen](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_II)) was put naked in a [crow cage](wiki:Crow_cage) above a pit filled with vipers.(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)
Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) taught his daughter, [Tyene Sand](wiki:Tyene_Sand), how to milk viper venom.(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

After fleeing [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) repeats to herself phrases [Syrio Forel](wiki:Syrio_Forel) taught her. "Quick as a snake" is one of them.(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

In the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), Arya Stark and the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) camp overnight at [High Heart](wiki:High_Heart). They meet with the [ghost of High Heart](wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart) to hear her tell the future. The old woman says she [dreamed](wiki:Vision) of a [maid](wiki:Sansa_Stark) at a [feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding) with [purple serpents](wiki:Silver_hair_net) in her hair, [venom](wiki:Strangler) dripping from their fangs.(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)
Lady [Olenna Tyrell](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell) refers to Oberyn Martell's longtime [paramour](wiki:Paramour), [Ellaria Sand](wiki:Ellaria_Sand), as the serpent's whore.(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

During her [walk of atonement](wiki:Walk_of_atonement), [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) sees a [whore](wiki:Whore) with a huge striped snake draped about her breasts and shoulders.(wiki:Snake.cite_ref-Radwd65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
