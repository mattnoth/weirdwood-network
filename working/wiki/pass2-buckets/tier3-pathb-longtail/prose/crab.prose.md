## Culture

Red crabs are depicted in the arms of [House Celtigar](wiki:House_Celtigar) of [Claw Isle](wiki:Claw_Isle),(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Racok61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-21) with Lord [Ardrian Celtigar](wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar) known as the Red Crab.(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) A white spider crab is included in the arms of [House Borrell](wiki:House_Borrell), and varieties of crab are included in the [sister's stew](wiki:Sister's_stew) popular at the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters).(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) [House Crabb](wiki:House_Crabb) is a noble family from [Crackclaw Point](wiki:Crackclaw_Point).(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
The [Crab King](wiki:Crab_King) is a lesser god of the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne).(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_long_night.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) Crab gods are said to be worshipped by [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) at [Storrold's Point](wiki:Storrold's_Point) in the lands [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_wall_and_beyond:_the_wildligns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

## Origins

After suppressing a rebellion by [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown), King [Gerold Grafton](wiki:Gerold_Grafton_(king)) threw the dead into the city's bay for the crabs.(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)
The crab-eaten bodies of the followers of [Lodos](wiki:Lodos) washed up on the shores of [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk) after walking into the sea.(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)
[Craghas Drahar](wiki:Craghas_Drahar), an admiral of [Myr](wiki:Myr), was known as Craghas Crabfeeder for his habit of staking captured pirates on the shore to drown.(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)
Crabs and seagulls feasted on the head and neck of [Arrax](wiki:Arrax) after the [dragon](wiki:Dragon) died near [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End).(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
[Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) gave the body of his [third wife](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy's_third_wife) to the crabs after killing her.(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) enjoys crabs delivered from [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea) while dining with the high officers of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Ragot21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)
The ailing Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully) refers to his stomach pain as crabs pinching in his belly.(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Hot crab pie is one of the courses served during Tyrion's meal with Lord [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt).(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)
Crabs are among the seafood brought by Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) to the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)
Crabclaw pies are served in the [Queen's Ballroom](wiki:Queen's_Ballroom) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) eats tiny crabs while shipwrecked on a [spear of the Merling King](wiki:Spear_of_the_Merling_King) after the battle.(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Rasos5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)
Crabs reportedly eat King [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy)'s eyes after he falls to his death from [Pyke](wiki:Pyke).(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) sees a wrecked galley on the rocks of [Skagos](wiki:Skagos), with bodies of crewmen being consumed by [rooks](wiki:Rooks) and crabs.(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) buys hot crab stew for a [pious dwarf](wiki:Pious_dwarf) at the [Seven Swords](wiki:Seven_Swords) in [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale).(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Crabs are included in the thick seafood stew served at the [Quiet Isle](wiki:Quiet_Isle). [Dog](wiki:Dog_(Meribald)) catches a crab in a tidal pool near the isle's [septry](wiki:Septry).(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)
[Cat of the Canals](wiki:Arya_Stark) sells crabs in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0)
[Ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) feast on buckets of fresh crabs at [Lord Hewett's castle](wiki:Lord_Hewett's_castle) after the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields).(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Manderly brings crates of crabs to [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) for the [wedding of Ramsay Bolton and Arya Stark](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark).(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)
[Umma](wiki:Umma) serves salt-crusted crabs for supper at the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White).(wiki:Crab.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0)
