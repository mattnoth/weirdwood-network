## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Blindeye is one of the several medicines, poisons and potions [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) notices on Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle)'s cabinet.(wiki:Blindeye.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

During [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s trial for the [death](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), Pycelle shows a number of poisons and medicines from his collection and claims that Tyrion stole a several of them, including a sample of blindeye, during the time Tyrion had him imprisoned.(wiki:Blindeye.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
