## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Having arrived in [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) for the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast_(299)), [the steward](wiki:Glover_steward) of [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) informs Prince [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) and his councilors that he is setting aside only a tenth of his harvest. A hedge wizard had told him that there would be a bountiful spirit summer before the cold set in. Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin) expresses his disapproval of hedge wizards, and Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) tells the steward to set aside a fifth of the harvest instead.(wiki:Hedge_wizard.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

In the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel), rumors say that Archmaester [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn) keeps the company of hedge wizards, among other social outcasts.(wiki:Hedge_wizard.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
In [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), one of the men trying to pass the head of a [murdered dwarf](wiki:Pious_dwarf) as that of [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)—to collect the reward offered by Queen [Regent](wiki:Regent) [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)—argues that a hedge wizard had regrown Tyrion's missing nose.(wiki:Hedge_wizard.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) arrives at [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor), he visits [Fishfoot Yard](wiki:Fishfoot_Yard) and he finds it teeming with people. Beneath the arches of the peddler's colonnade, he sees that a hedge wizard has set up for business.(wiki:Hedge_wizard.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
