## Culture

The massive chairs of the [Pureborn](wiki:Pureborn) of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) are decorated with [goldwork](wiki:Gold) and studded with amber, [onyx](wiki:Onyx), [lapis](wiki:Lapis), and [jade](wiki:Jade).(wiki:Amber.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
Amber can be found [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:Amber.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Amber.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Amber.cite_ref-Radwdprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
The [rainwood](wiki:Rainwood) is known for its fur, wood, and amber.(wiki:Amber.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_the_men_of_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) According to a semi-canon source, the castle of [Amberly](wiki:Amberly), seat of [House Rogers](wiki:House_Rogers), may be named for having large deposits of amber nearby.(wiki:Amber.cite_ref-6)
Gold and amber flow from the [Axe](wiki:Axe) in the [Qhoyne](wiki:Qhoyne) to [Dagger Lake](wiki:Dagger_Lake).(wiki:Amber.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) The [Forest of Qohor](wiki:Forest_of_Qohor) has [silver](wiki:Silver), [tin](wiki:Tin), and amber.(wiki:Amber.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_qohor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Amber can also be found on [Ib](wiki:Ib).(wiki:Amber.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Origins

[House Amber](wiki:House_Amber) was a family of the [north](wiki:North) extinguished by the [Kings of Winter](wiki:Kings_of_Winter).(wiki:Amber.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
[Roro Uhoris](wiki:Roro_Uhoris) sailed [Cobblecat](wiki:Cobblecat) into the [Shivering Sea](wiki:Shivering_Sea) to trade steel to the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) for [ivory](wiki:Ivory), furs, amber, and [obsidian](wiki:Obsidian).(wiki:Amber.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) compares the voice of [Chataya](wiki:Chataya) to flowing amber.(wiki:Amber.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Among the gifts brought by trader captains to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) are amber and [dragonglass](wiki:Dragonglass) from [Asshai](wiki:Asshai).(wiki:Amber.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos) gives Daenerys a thousand miniature [knights](wiki:Knight) carved of amber, [amethyst](wiki:Amethyst), [beryl](wiki:Beryl), [jade](wiki:Jade), [onyx](wiki:Onyx), [opal](wiki:Opal), and [tourmaline](wiki:Tourmaline), with armor of [silver](wiki:Silver) and [gold](wiki:Gold).(wiki:Amber.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Daenerys compares the skin color of the people of [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay) to amber.(wiki:Amber.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Among the tribute collected from the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) passing through the [Wall](wiki:Wall) are amber pendants.(wiki:Amber.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
