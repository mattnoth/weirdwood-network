## Quotes

Hear me. The [ships](wiki:Ships) of [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) sail as far as the winds blow, to lands strange and wonderful, and when they return, their captains fetch queer animals to the [Sealord](wiki:Sealord)'s menagerie. Such animals as you have never seen, [striped horses](wiki:Zorse), great spotted things with necks as long as stilts, hairy mouse-pigs as big as cows, stinging [manticores](wiki:Manticore), tigers that carry their cubs in a pouch, terrible [walking lizards](wiki:Walking_lizard) with scythes for claws. Syrio Forel has seen these things.(wiki:Pouch_tiger.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
