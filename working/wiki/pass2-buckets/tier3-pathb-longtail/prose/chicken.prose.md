## Culture

Male and female chickens are known as roosters and hens, respectively. Chickens are commonly used as poultry. Capons are roosters which have been castrated and raised for meat.
A blue bantam rooster is depicted in the arms of [House Swyft](wiki:House_Swyft),(wiki:Chicken.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) and a yellow rooster's head is included in the arms of [House Herston](wiki:House_Herston).(wiki:Chicken.cite_ref-2)
A [cockatrice](wiki:Cockatrice) has the head of a rooster.(wiki:Chicken.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Origins

Since [Sunfyre](wiki:Sunfyre), the [dragon](wiki:Dragon) of King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), healed awkwardly after the [battle at Rook's Rest](wiki:Battle_at_Rook's_Rest), [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom) compared the beast to "a great golden [fire-breathing](wiki:Dragonflame) chicken, hopping and fluttering from hill to hill".(wiki:Chicken.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Hens wandered the yard at [Standfast](wiki:Standfast). Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) sometimes had his squire, [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen), chase chickens to improve his speed.(wiki:Chicken.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
While those seated at the high table at the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls) enjoyed a [peacock](wiki:Peacock) roasted in its plumage, lowerborn guests were served capon stuffed with onions, herbs, mushrooms, and roasted chestnuts.(wiki:Chicken.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and [Tysha](wiki:Tysha) ate two whole chickens and part of a third at an inn while waiting for [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) to return from [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Chicken.cite_ref-Ragot42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
[Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) grew to hate blood and wept at even the slaughter of chickens.(wiki:Chicken.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Every year [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) sent piglets, chickens, and a bag of [stars](wiki:Currency) to [the mother](wiki:Miller's_wife) of [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) to keep the boy's parentage a secret.(wiki:Chicken.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
