## Origins

After reaching majority and beginning to rule in his own right, King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) replaced the Warden of the King's Mint, among other lesser offices.(wiki:Warden_of_the_King's_Mint.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
At the time of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) the office is possibly defunct, with there now being three royal mints each managed by its own officer.(wiki:Warden_of_the_King's_Mint.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
