## Culture

There are ancient tales of [Hairy Men](wiki:Hairy_Men) in [Essos](wiki:Essos) riding unicorns to battle,(wiki:Unicorn.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf835_5-0) and [Skagosi](wiki:Skagosi) lords are also said to ride unicorns.(wiki:Unicorn.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf713_4-1)
Stone sentries outside of the shop of [Tobho Mott](wiki:Tobho_Mott) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) are decorated as [griffin](wiki:Griffin) and unicorn knights.(wiki:Unicorn.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Several [Westerosi](wiki:Westerosi) houses feature heraldic unicorns in their sigils, including Houses [Brax](wiki:House_Brax),(wiki:Unicorn.cite_ref-west_7-0) [Doggett](wiki:House_Doggett),(wiki:Unicorn.cite_ref-west_7-1) [Rogers](wiki:House_Rogers),(wiki:Unicorn.cite_ref-8) and [Wydman](wiki:House_Wydman).(wiki:Unicorn.cite_ref-9) Ser [Flement Brax](wiki:Flement_Brax) decorates his horsehead helm with a long spiral horn.(wiki:Unicorn.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Dishonest traders sometimes sell "unicorn horns" which are actually the tusks of narwhals hunted by the [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibbenese).(wiki:Unicorn.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf713_4-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) recalls having read that the Skagosi ride great shaggy unicorns to war.(wiki:Unicorn.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Unicorn horns are amongst plunder brought from distant lands by [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy).(wiki:Unicorn.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), in a dream as his [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), [Ghost](wiki:Ghost), sees that [Shaggydog](wiki:Shaggydog) has fought and killed what may be a unicorn.(wiki:Unicorn.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
In the [cave of the three-eyed crow](wiki:Cave_of_the_three-eyed_crow) [Leaf](wiki:Leaf) tells [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) that the unicorns are all but gone.(wiki:Unicorn.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
Among some of the tribute collected from the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) passing through the [Wall](wiki:Wall) is a helm made with a unicorn’s head.(wiki:Unicorn.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
