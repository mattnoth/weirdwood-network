## Origins

The [greenseers](wiki:Greenseer) of the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) are said to have called upon cave lions to fight on their behalf against the encroaching [First Men](wiki:First_Men).(wiki:Cave_lion.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf22.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

[Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) [dreams](wiki:Dreams_and_prophecies) of being trapped beneath [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) alongside [Brienne Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth). During it, she asks if a cave lion or other large wildlife is kept there, but Jaime tells her there is only doom.(wiki:Cave_lion.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
