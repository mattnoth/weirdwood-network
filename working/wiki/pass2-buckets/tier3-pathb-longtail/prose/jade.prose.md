## Culture

The [Jade Gates](wiki:Jade_Gates) separate the [Jade Sea](wiki:Jade_Sea) from the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea).(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) [Jade Compendium](wiki:Jade_Compendium) is a book about the lands of the Jade Sea.(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Only the [god-emperor](wiki:God-emperor) is allowed to wear the traditional gowns of [cloth-of-gold](wiki:Cloth-of-gold), green [pearls](wiki:Pearl), and jade in [Yi Ti](wiki:Yi_Ti).(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
The massive chairs of the [Pureborn](wiki:Pureborn) of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) are decorated with [goldwork](wiki:Gold) and studded with [amber](wiki:Amber), [onyx](wiki:Onyx), [lapis lazuli](wiki:Lapis_lazuli), and jade.(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) A [bronze](wiki:Bronze) arch in the city depicts two [snakes](wiki:Snake) mating, and their scales are made of delicate flakes of jade, [obsidian](wiki:Obsidian), and lapis.(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Within the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor), a chamber has carvings of [the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) with eyes of [onyx](wiki:Onyx), jade, [malachite](wiki:Malachite), and yellow [moonstone](wiki:Moonstone).(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
The buttons on [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan)'s coat are carved jade monkeys.(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos) decorates his nose with [rubies](wiki:Ruby), [opals](wiki:Opal), and flakes of jade,(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) and he owns goblets of jade and gold.(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) [Leo Tyrell](wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn)) pins his half cape with a jade rose.(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Garin](wiki:Garin_(orphan)) of the [orphans](wiki:Orphans_of_the_Greenblood) wears a jade stud in his ear.(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) has jade among his various rings.(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) The beard of the [merling king](wiki:Merling_King_(god)) on the helm of Ser [Marlon Manderly](wiki:Marlon_Manderly) is decorated in [jet](wiki:Jet) and jade.(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Squares on [cyvasse](wiki:Cyvasse) boards can be made of jade, [carnelian](wiki:Carnelian), and lapis lazuli,(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) and game pieces can be made of [ivory](wiki:Ivory) and jade.(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
[Wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) is also known as the jade [demon](wiki:Demon).(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Rasos5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

## Origins

The [Jade Emperor](wiki:Jade_Emperor) was a legendary ruler of the [Great Empire of the Dawn](wiki:Great_Empire_of_the_Dawn).(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) The [jade-green emperors](wiki:Jade-green_emperors) were a dynasty in [Yi Ti](wiki:Yi_Ti).(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
The [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon) presented [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen) with a [golden](wiki:Gold) crown inlaid in jade and [pearl](wiki:Pearl) with the faces of [the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
[House Velaryon](wiki:House_Velaryon) became rich when [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) returned from the [Jade Sea](wiki:Jade_Sea) with jade, [spice](wiki:Spice), and [silk](wiki:Silk).(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) gave to Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) a jade tiara alleged to have belonged to the [Empress of Leng](wiki:Empress_of_Leng).(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
Jade from [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) was popular in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) during the [Lysene Spring](wiki:Lysene_Spring).(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey) owned an old [silver](wiki:Silver) cup which displayed the lion of [House Osgrey](wiki:House_Osgrey) in flakes of gold and jade.(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) has a vision of ghosts with eyes of [amethyst](wiki:Amethyst), jade, [opal](wiki:Opal), and [tourmaline](wiki:Tourmaline).(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Ragot68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)'s [crown](wiki:Crown) is a ring of [gold](wiki:Gold) roses with a [stag](wiki:Stag)'s head made of jade.(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
The [Tourmaline Brotherhood](wiki:Tourmaline_Brotherhood) gives Daenerys a crown with three [dragon](wiki:Dragon) heads carved from [ivory](wiki:Ivory), jade, and [onyx](wiki:Onyx).(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos) gives her a thousand miniature [knights](wiki:Knight) carved of [amber](wiki:Amber), amethyst, [beryl](wiki:Beryl), jade, onyx, opal, and tourmaline, with armor of silver and gold.(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell), having joined the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), clasps his cloak with a golden rose brooch with jade leaves.(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sends [Alayaya](wiki:Alayaya) a necklace of silver and jade and a pair of matching bracelets to make amends for the girl's beating by order of [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister).(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Rasos12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
Twelve jade monkeys are among the trade goods that Daenerys Targaryen exchanges with the [Good Masters](wiki:Good_Masters) for the [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) in [Astapor](wiki:Astapor).(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)
The seven-sided golden [chalice](wiki:Wedding_chalice) given by Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) has a blue jade [falcon](wiki:Falcon) to represent [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn).(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Young [Ermesande Hayford](wiki:Ermesande_Hayford)'s cloth-of-gold gown is decorated with the sigil of [House Hayford](wiki:House_Hayford) done in tiny beads of jade.(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)
Jade [manticores](wiki:Manticore) are among the plundered items used by [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) to bribe [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn).(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) visit the [widow of the waterfront](wiki:Widow_of_the_waterfront) at the [Merchant's House](wiki:Merchant's_House) in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), they see she has been given a gift of an ornate fan carved of jade leaves so thin they are translucent.(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)
Among the tributes collected from the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) passing through the [Wall](wiki:Wall) is a green jade comb.(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)
One of the [Wise Masters](wiki:Wise_Masters) of [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) that meets with [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) has an ornate breastplate inlaid with erotic scenes done in jet and jade and [mother-of-pearl](wiki:Mother-of-pearl).(wiki:Jade.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)
