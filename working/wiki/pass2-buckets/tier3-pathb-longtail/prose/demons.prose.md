## Origins

[Maegi](wiki:Maegi) are said by the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) to lay with demons.(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Legends of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) describe [Ygg](wiki:Ygg) as a demon tree who fed on human flesh.(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
It is said demons haunt the remaining lands of the [Valyrian peninsula](wiki:Valyrian_peninsula),(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) the [Smoking Sea](wiki:Smoking_Sea),(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0), the ruins of [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome),(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) the island of [Leng](wiki:Leng),(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) the vicinity of [Stygai](wiki:Stygai),(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_asshai-by-the-shadow.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) and the caves and cliffs of the [Mountains of the Morn](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Morn).(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_asshai-by-the-shadow.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) The forests of [Mossovy](wiki:Mossovy) are said to be a land of [demon hunters](wiki:Demon_hunter).(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_east_of_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
The [Five Forts](wiki:Five_Forts) are said to have been erected by the [Pearl Emperor](wiki:Pearl_Emperor) of the [Great Empire of the Dawn](wiki:Great_Empire_of_the_Dawn) to keep the [Lion of Night](wiki:Lion_of_Night) and his demons away from the realms of men.(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
According to reports, it is possible to fornicate with demons in [Asshai](wiki:Asshai).(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_asshai-by-the-shadow.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2) Demons, [dragons](wiki:Dragon), and other creatures are said to inhabit the cliff caves above the [Ash](wiki:Ash_(river)).(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_asshai-by-the-shadow.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-3)
The demon mother of [ice giants](wiki:Ice_giants) is said to dance eternally through the night in the northern waters of the [Shivering Sea](wiki:Shivering_Sea).(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_shivering_sea.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

## Culture

[Slavers](wiki:Slaver) will brand a slave's cheek with a demon's mask to mark them as a dangerous and disobedient slave.(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
The [bearded priests](wiki:Bearded_priests) of [Norvos](wiki:Norvos) consider the [Black Goat](wiki:Black_Goat) of [Qohor](wiki:Qohor) to be a demon.(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_qohor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
"Demon" is also used as a term to describe some great fighters, such as "the demon of the [Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident)",(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), or the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) from [House Darry](wiki:House_Darry) known as the [Demon of Darry](wiki:Demon_of_Darry).(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) Archmaester [Mancaster](wiki:Mancaster) wrote [Sea Demons: A History of the Children of the Drowned God of the Isles](wiki:Sea_Demons:_A_History_of_the_Children_of_the_Drowned_God_of_the_Isles) about the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn).(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

## Origins

[Ghiscari](wiki:Ghiscari) referred to [Walano](wiki:Walano) as the Demon Isle after first coming into contact with the dark-skinned inhabitants of the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles).(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_summer_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)
During [their coming](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), conquering [Andals](wiki:Andals) considered the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) and their [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) trees to be little more than demons.(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) Tales of the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons) claim they were [demonhunters](wiki:Demonhunter).(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)
[Priests of the Drowned God](wiki:Priests_of_the_Drowned_God) denounced [Othgar Hoare](wiki:Othgar_Hoare_(Demonlover)), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), as Othgar Demonlover, possibly because of [House Hoare](wiki:House_Hoare)'s support of the Faith.(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)
[Dragonglass](wiki:Dragonglass) and demonic black blood are said to have rained down from red clouds during the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria).(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
[Alys Rivers](wiki:Alys_Rivers) was feared to be a witch who lay with demons.(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)
The [Shepherd](wiki:Shepherd) claimed that [dragons](wiki:Dragons) were demons summoned from the [seven hells](wiki:Seven_hells) by [Valyrian](wiki:Valyria) sorcerers.(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)
[Sam Stoops' wife](wiki:Sam_Stoops'_wife) believed the [Lord of the Seven Hells](wiki:Lord_of_the_Seven_Hells) would send a demon to collect the child whenever [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber), [Lady of Coldmoat](wiki:Lady_of_Coldmoat), gave birth.(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)
Serving girls at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) believed that [Maggy the Frog](wiki:Maggy_the_Frog) could summon demons.(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Dywen](wiki:Dywen) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) tells [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) that the [wildling](wiki:Wildling) [Craster](wiki:Craster) deals with [slavers](wiki:Slavery) and demons.(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)
[Salloreon](wiki:Salloreon) offers to craft for [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) a helm decorated as a demon's head with tall golden horns, though Tyrion declines.(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) A [begging brother](wiki:Begging_brother) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) refers to Tyrion, a [dwarf](wiki:Dwarf), as a "little monkey demon".(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0) When revealing to Tyrion the story of his mutilation, [Varys](wiki:Varys) wonders if the voice heard from the flames in which his sexual organs were burned was a god, a demon, or a trick.(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

When [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) sees the elaborate hairstyles of [Astapori](wiki:Astapor) guards, she compares them to a troupe of demons from the seventh hell.(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0)
[Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) describes the [Others](wiki:Others) as "demons made of snow and ice and cold".(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Alarmed by the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) and Stannis's support of [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor), [sparrows](wiki:Sparrows) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) warn of doom and demon worship.(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0)(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0)(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Leona Woolfield](wiki:Leona_Woolfield) refers to R'hllor as a fire demon,(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0) though the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) in Stannis's company believe the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God) and [weirwoods](wiki:Weirwood) to be demons.(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0)(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0) [Wulfe One-Ear](wiki:Wulfe_One-Ear) considers the black-skinned [Moqorro](wiki:Moqorro) to be a demon priest,(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0) while the [red priest](wiki:Red_priest) considers the Drowned God to be a demon in thrall to [the Other](wiki:Great_Other).(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-0)
[Slavers](wiki:Slavers) brand Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont)'s face with a demon's mask to mark him as dangerous and disobedient.(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1)(wiki:Demons.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1)
