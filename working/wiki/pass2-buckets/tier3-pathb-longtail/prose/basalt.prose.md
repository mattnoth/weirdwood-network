## Culture

### Westeros[ ]

The curtain wall of [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin) was made of enormous blocks of black basalt.(wiki:Basalt.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) When wet, the remaining stones appear to be coated in fine black oil.(wiki:Basalt.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
[Blackhaven](wiki:Blackhaven), seat of [House Dondarrion](wiki:House_Dondarrion), has forbidding walls of black basalt.(wiki:Basalt.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_the_men_of_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
The bridge at [Breakwater Castle](wiki:Breakwater_Castle) is made of black basalt.(wiki:Basalt.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### Essos[ ]

In eastern [Essos](wiki:Essos), [Kayakayanaya](wiki:Kayakayanaya) has walls of black basalt, black [iron](wiki:Iron), and yellow [bone](wiki:Bone).(wiki:Basalt.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
