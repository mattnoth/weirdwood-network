## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

While the retinue of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) travels south through the [Neck](wiki:Neck) along the [causeway](wiki:Causeway), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) receives a rash from poison kisses after gathering flowers for her father, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark). [Mycah](wiki:Mycah) tells her that mud will stop the itching.(wiki:Poison_kisses.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]
