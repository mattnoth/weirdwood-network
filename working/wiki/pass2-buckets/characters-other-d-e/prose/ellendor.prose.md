## Origins

Ellendor was elected Grand Maester when he was close to 90 years old and was sent to serve the court of King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) to replace the late Grand Maester [Kaeth](wiki:Kaeth). Ellendor died within a year of his appointment and was succeeded by Grand Maester [Merion](wiki:Merion).(wiki:Ellendor.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
