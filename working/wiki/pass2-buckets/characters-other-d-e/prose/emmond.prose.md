## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Emmond is one of the boys that [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) drowns near the lands of [House Sparr](wiki:House_Sparr). Emmond has a difficult time accepting his drowning, and he fights against Aeron while underwater. Aeron successfully brings Emmond back to life, after which Emmond is accepted as one of the [drowned men](wiki:Drowned_men).(wiki:Emmond.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
