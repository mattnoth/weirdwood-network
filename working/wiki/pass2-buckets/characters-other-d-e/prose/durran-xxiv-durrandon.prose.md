## Origins

Durran was born during the late [Andal invasion](wiki:Andal_invasion) of the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands), from the union of his father [Maldon IV Durrandon](wiki:Maldon_IV_Durrandon) with an [Andal](wiki:Andal) maiden he took as his wife, thus earning Durran his nickname. He followed the example of his father and also took a wife of pure Andal blood. During his reign, and the following ones, Andal warlords became lords and petty kings and married the daughters of Stormlords and gave them their daughters in return, swearing fealty and their swords for lands.(wiki:Durran_XXIV_Durrandon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_andals_in_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
