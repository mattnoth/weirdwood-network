## Origins

Edgerran was the [Seneschal of the Citadel](wiki:Seneschal_of_the_Citadel) in [272 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). He wrote a treatise on the swaddling of infants and tested his theories on the infant Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel).(wiki:Edgerran.cite_ref-Rtwoiafpreface.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Appearances & Description

Edgerran's ring, rod, and mask were made of [silver](wiki:Silver), which meant he was an expert in [medicine](wiki:Medicine).(wiki:Edgerran.cite_ref-Rtwoiafpreface.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
