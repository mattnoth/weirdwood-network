## Origins

Osney's father, [Oswell Kettleblack](wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack), has been in the service of Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) for a long time.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Osney and his brothers have known Petyr since they were children.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rawoiafosmund_kettleblack.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rawoiafosfryd_kettleblack.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

## Appearances & Description

Osney is a tall man with a hooked nose and a shaven face,(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) though he is not as tall as his brother Ser [Osfryd](wiki:Osfryd_Kettleblack).(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) His dark hair(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) reaches his collar.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) [Osmund](wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack) thinks that he and Osfryd are stronger than Osney, but that Osney is still quick to kill.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Like his two brothers, Osney is illiterate.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Osney and his brothers are amiable, popular with both [knights](wiki:Knight) and servants, and amorous with serving women.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) considers all three brothers to be deceitful and unsavory,(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) but none as dangerous as [Bronn](wiki:Bronn).(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) also considers the brothers to be treacherous.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Osney receives four long thin scratches on his cheek,(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) which eventually become three(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) faint(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) scars, that flush bright red when he smiles.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

In order to match her brother [Tyrion's](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) personal power base of [sellswords](wiki:Sellsword), Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) calls on the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Fat_One)) at the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) as cover for secret meetings with the Kettleblack brothers, who are supposed to hire other sellswords for her. Unbeknownst to Cersei, however, the Kettlebacks are already being paid by [Bronn](wiki:Bronn), Tyrion's captain.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

Cersei has Osney and Osfryd abduct [Alayaya](wiki:Alayaya) in order to threaten Tyrion, as the queen believes the [prostitute](wiki:Prostitute) is Tyrion's [paramour](wiki:Paramour). Alayaya scratches Osney's face in the process,(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) and the four long thin scratches on his cheek become crusted with scabs.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

Osney and his brothers become great favorites in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), getting along with both servants and [highborns](wiki:Highborn) with their easygoing manner and their humor, and it is rumored that they get best along with the serving wenches. However, [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) wonders why she has never heard of them before Osmund was named to the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Osney brings reports to Cersei,(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) and he aids Osfryd.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Osmund tells Osney of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s defeat in the battle, and Osney informs Ser [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard).(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4)

### A Storm of Swords

Osney and Osfryd are given their [knighthoods](wiki:Knight) after the Battle of the Blackwater, though they took no part in the actual fighting,(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and they serve as Cersei's [sworn swords](wiki:Sworn_sword).(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) The Kettleblacks bring Prince [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) back from [Rosby](wiki:Rosby) after the death of Ser [Jacelyn Bywater](wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater).(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Sansa Stark sees Osney training against Ser [Kennos of Kayce](wiki:Kennos_of_Kayce).(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

All three of the Kettleblack brothers give evidence against Tyrion in his trial for the [the murder](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King Joffrey. Osfryd and Osney mention Tyrion's threats against Cersei, although they omit that Tyrion had said them after Alayaya was harmed.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

Osney, Osfryd, and Ser [Garlan Tyrell](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell) guard King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) when Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) meets with the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) in the [White Sword Tower](wiki:White_Sword_Tower).(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) reveals to Sansa that he had Oswell send his sons to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) when Bronn was hiring sellswords for Tyrion, and thus the Kettleblack brothers have been secretly in his employ all along, unbeknownst to Cersei and Tyrion. However, Petyr believes the brothers are too treacherous to have been trusted with his scheme to kill Joffrey.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

### A Feast for Crows

After Tyrion escapes from the [black cells](wiki:Black_cells) and murders Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) and [Shae](wiki:Shae) in the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand), Queen Cersei orders Ser Osney and his brothers to secretly dispose of Shae's corpse. Cersei tells them that what they do with the corpse is of no concern, as long as nobody knows she was ever there.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Cersei also orders Ser [Boros Blount](wiki:Boros_Blount) and the Kettleblacks to kill two sleeping gaolers, which upsets Jaime.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

Osney is given a black [stallion](wiki:Stallion) by his brother, Osfryd, which he names [Midnight](wiki:Midnight_(horse)).(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) Cersei notices that the three scars on Osney's cheek flush bright red when he smiles.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

Cersei has Osney kill the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Tyrion's)), fearing what [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister) might have told the man. Osney sneaks into the [Great Sept](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) and smothers the High Septon with a pillow while he sleeps. As a reward Cersei beds him.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Cersei tasks Osney with seducing and bedding Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), with the pair to be discovered by Osney's brother, Ser Osmund of the Kingsguard. Tommen would then send Osney to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) as punishment, where he can kill the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow). Cersei promises Osney a pardon and [lordship](wiki:Lord) when both these tasks are completed.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

Cersei names Osney as the [sworn shield](wiki:Sworn_shield) of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) so that the knight can be close to Margaery.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) The young queen responds to Osney's advances with nothing but teasing,(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1) however, so Cersei hatches a new scheme. She tells Osney to go the new [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon) (known as the [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow)) and falsely confess to bedding Margaery and two of her three cousins. Cersei then lets him bed her again to make sure he obeys.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

As part of Cersei's plan, she has the [Blue Bard](wiki:Blue_Bard) [tortured](wiki:Torture) until he is forced to list those he claims are Queen Margaery's secret lovers, including Osney.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1) Osney confesses to the High Sparrow, who has Margaery and her cousins arrested. The High Sparrow is suspicious, however, as he has never heard a confession from a man so pleased to be guilty. The High Sparrow then has Osney [whipped](wiki:Whipping) to verify his confession. Under that torture, Osney tells a different story, which implicates Cersei in the death of the previous High Septon and in having sexual relations with Osney. This news allows the High Sparrow to arrest Cersei on similar charges to those of Margaery.(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

### A Dance with Dragons

Osney remains a prisoner of the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven). He is to be executed for the murder of the [previous High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Tyrion's)).(wiki:Osney_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
