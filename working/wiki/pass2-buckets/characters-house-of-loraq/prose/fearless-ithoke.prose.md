## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Fearless Ithoke, along with his fellow pit fighters [Barsena Blackhair](wiki:Barsena_Blackhair), [Camarron of the Count](wiki:Camarron_of_the_Count), [Goghor the Giant](wiki:Goghor_the_Giant), [Khrazz](wiki:Khrazz), the [Spotted Cat](wiki:Spotted_Cat), and [Belaquo Bonebreaker](wiki:Belaquo_Bonebreaker), accompanies [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) when he petitions [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) for the seventh time for the [fighting pits of Meereen](wiki:Fighting_pits_of_Meereen) to be reopened.(wiki:Fearless_Ithoke.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### The Winds of Winter

Shortly before the resumption of the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen), Ithoke stands beside the [Spotted Cat](wiki:Spotted_Cat) among the several hundred pit fighters who have gathered for the battle near the [Spire of Skulls](wiki:Spire_of_Skulls).(wiki:Fearless_Ithoke.cite_ref-barristan1_2-0)
