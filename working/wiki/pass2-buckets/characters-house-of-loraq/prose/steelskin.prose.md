## Appearances & Description

Steelskin is a lean and wiry youth whose cheeks, brow, chest, and arms are covered with intricate [tattoos](wiki:Tattoo) in green and black. These [sorcerer](wiki:Sorcerer) signs of ancient [Valyria](wiki:Valyria) supposedly make his skin and flesh as hard as [steel](wiki:Steel).(wiki:Steelskin.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

The pit fighter uses a longaxe.(wiki:Steelskin.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
