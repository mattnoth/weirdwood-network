## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Symon Silver Tongue](wiki:Symon_Silver_Tongue) informs [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) that Alaric is one of the seven singers chosen to sing at the [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_wedding) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and Lady [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), where he will compete for a [gilded](wiki:Gold) [lute](wiki:Lute) with [silver](wiki:Silver) strings.(wiki:Alaric_of_Eysen.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) While Alaric is waiting to start singing, King Joffrey and Queen Margaery cut the wedding pie, and Joffrey [chokes to death](wiki:Strangler) while eating pie and drinking [wine](wiki:Wine).(wiki:Alaric_of_Eysen.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Alaric remains in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) and occasionally plays for Queen Margaery and her entourage.(wiki:Alaric_of_Eysen.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
