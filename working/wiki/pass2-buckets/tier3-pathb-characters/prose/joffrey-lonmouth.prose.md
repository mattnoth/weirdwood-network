## Appearances & Description

Joffrey was a rumored [lover](wiki:Paramour) to Ser [Laenor Velaryon](wiki:Laenor_Velaryon), the heir to [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark).(wiki:Joffrey_Lonmouth.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Origins

Ser Joffrey was [Laenor Velaryon](wiki:Laenor_Velaryon)'s favorite knight and companion. At Laenor's [wedding](wiki:Wedding) to Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), Joffrey rode as Laenor's champion in the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) while Ser [Harwin Strong](wiki:Harwin_Strong) rode for the princess. During the tourney, Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) attacked him with a morningstar and cracked his helm. He died six days later without recovering consciousness; Laenor never left his side and wept at his death.(wiki:Joffrey_Lonmouth.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
Ser Laenor wanted to [name](wiki:Named_after) his first son, [Jacaerys](wiki:Jacaerys_Velaryon), after him, but was overruled by his father, Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon). Instead, Laenor's third son, [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey_Velaryon), bore his name.(wiki:Joffrey_Lonmouth.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
