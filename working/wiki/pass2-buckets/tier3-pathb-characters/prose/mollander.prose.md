## Appearances & Description

Mollander has thick arms and broad shoulders. He has a clubfoot, which prevents him from becoming a [knight](wiki:Knight), like his father.(wiki:Mollander.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After Mollander heard his father was killed in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), he began [drinking](wiki:Alcoholic_beverages) heavily. Because of his drinking, he is not a very good student.(wiki:Mollander.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
Mollander is one of the group that discusses the discovery of [dragons](wiki:Dragon). He gets very drunk and [Roone](wiki:Roone) has to help him walk back to the Citadel.(wiki:Mollander.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
