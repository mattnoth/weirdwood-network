## Origins

During the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to the [Kingdom of the Reach](wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Reach), the [Three Sage Kings](wiki:Three_Sage_Kings) took [Andals](wiki:Andals) into their service as household knights, such as Ser [Alester Tyrell](wiki:Alester_Tyrell).(wiki:Household_knight.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Ser [Walton Towers](wiki:Walton_Towers) was rewarded with [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) by King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) after winning a melee between twenty-three of Maegor's knights in the streets of [Harroway](wiki:Harroway).(wiki:Household_knight.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
A daring [wildling](wiki:Wildling) girl was fostered by one of the household knights of Lord [Theomore Manderly](wiki:Theomore_Manderly).(wiki:Household_knight.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Ser [Laenor Velaryon](wiki:Laenor_Velaryon) was slain by one of his favorites, the household knight Ser [Qarl Correy](wiki:Qarl_Correy).(wiki:Household_knight.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
A score of knights in the household of Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) volunteered during the [Sowing of the Seeds](wiki:Sowing_of_the_Seeds).(wiki:Household_knight.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) According to [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom), the crippled King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) would watch his household knights couple with servants and ladies of his court.(wiki:Household_knight.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
The [second mistress](wiki:Tytos_Lannister's_second_mistress) of Lord [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister) outraged his heir, [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), by giving orders to household knights.(wiki:Household_knight.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) During the [Reyne-Tarbeck revolt](wiki:Reyne-Tarbeck_revolt), Ser Tywin crushed Lord [Walderan Tarbeck](wiki:Walderan_Tarbeck) and his household knights with a much larger force.(wiki:Household_knight.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
[Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) took as mistress the pretty young daughter of one of his household knights. Following the death of his son [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aerys_II)), Aerys accused his mistress of poisoning the child and had her and her family tortured to death.(wiki:Household_knight.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
Ser [Colin Florent](wiki:Colin_Florent) wed his daughter, [Delena Florent](wiki:Delena_Florent), to Ser [Hosman Norcross](wiki:Hosman_Norcross), one of his household knights, after the girl was deflowered by King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Household_knight.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) has a dozen knights and a hundred [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms) under her command in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Household_knight.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Storm of Storms[ ]

A dozen household knights escort Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) to the [Fingers](wiki:Drearfort).(wiki:Household_knight.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Now Queen Regent, Cersei demeans her uncle, Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), for having been a household knight in service to her late father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), but Kevan explains he is still powerful because of his own incomes, accumulated wealth and base of support.(wiki:Household_knight.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
Ser [Balman Byrch](wiki:Balman_Byrch) considers four knights taken into the household of Ser [Bronn of the Blackwater](wiki:Bronn_of_the_Blackwater) to be no more than upjumped [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords).(wiki:Household_knight.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
Ser [Hyle Hunt](wiki:Hyle_Hunt) is judged by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) for being a household knight in service of [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly).(wiki:Household_knight.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) thinks that Ser [Justin Massey](wiki:Justin_Massey), who has lost access to his ancestral lands through the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), hopes to wed her and thereby improve his station, otherwise he will be no more than a household knight in the service of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Household_knight.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
