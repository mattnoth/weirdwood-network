## Appearances & Description

Halder is tall and muscular.(wiki:Halder.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) thinks him incredibly strong, but easy to anticipate.(wiki:Halder.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Halder sometime sings.(wiki:Halder.cite_ref-Rasos46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Origins

Sixteen years old in [298 AC](wiki:298_AC), Halder is the son of a quarryman and apprenticed as a stonemason.(wiki:Halder.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Halder is in the [same class of recruits](wiki:Jon_Snow's_Night's_Watch_recruitment_group) as [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), where his ability to fight with a sword is dismissed with disdain by Jon.(wiki:Halder.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) When [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) arrives at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne) sends Halder to be his first practice partner. Alliser tells Halder to hurt Sam, but he is stopped by Jon's intervention. Alliser has Halder, [Rast](wiki:Rast), and [Albett](wiki:Albett) attack Jon, but they are soundly defeated when [Pypar](wiki:Pypar) and [Grenn](wiki:Grenn) aid Jon.(wiki:Halder.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
The recruits celebrate when Alliser believes them ready to man the [Wall](wiki:Wall). When Pyp squirts a [wineskin](wiki:Wine) into Jon's face, Halder objects to the waste of the good sour red.(wiki:Halder.cite_ref-Ragot41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) When Halder is named to the [builders](wiki:Builder), he gives a stiff nod of approval.(wiki:Halder.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Halder helps [Pate](wiki:Pate_(ranger)) carve the [wolf](wiki:Wolf)'s head for the new pommel of [Longclaw](wiki:Longclaw).(wiki:Halder.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Halder is among the group of friends who stop Jon from abandoning the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) and forswearing himself. Jon hides, hoping they will pass him by, but Halder is able to hear Jon's [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), [Ghost](wiki:Ghost).(wiki:Halder.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Before Grenn leaves with the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), Pyp, [Todder](wiki:Todder), and Halder bring him to [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town).(wiki:Halder.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

When Jon becomes distracted while training with [Iron Emmett](wiki:Emmett), Halder and [Horse](wiki:Hareth_(Mole's_Town)) protect Emmett from Jon. Halder congratulates Jon after he is chosen as the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch).(wiki:Halder.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Halder is still stationed at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Halder.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Halder is one of the friends present in the new common hall in the cellar below the armory who try to invite Lord Commander Snow to sit with them, to no avail. Jon later decides to separate his friends and transfers Halder and Toad west to the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower).(wiki:Halder.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
