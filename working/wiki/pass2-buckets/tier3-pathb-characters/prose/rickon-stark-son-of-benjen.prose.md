## Origins

Rickon succeeded his father, Lord [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark_(lord)), as the [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell) and [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North).(wiki:Rickon_Stark_(son_of_Benjen).cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_stark_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Rickon_Stark_(son_of_Benjen).cite_ref-5)
After Lord Rickon passed away in [121 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), he was succeeded as Lord of Winterfell by his thirteen-year-old son and heir, [Cregan](wiki:Cregan_Stark). Rickon's brother, [Bennard](wiki:Bennard_Stark), served as [regent](wiki:Regent) during Cregan's [minority](wiki:Minority) until [126 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), when Cregan rose against his uncle for he was slow to surrender power.(wiki:Rickon_Stark_(son_of_Benjen).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
