## Origins

At the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord Caswell was arrested and put in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep)'s dungeons by [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen)'s supporters for supporting his half-sister, Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen). When given the choice between swearing his allegiance to Aegon II or being killed by the [King's Justice](wiki:King's_Justice), Lord Caswell was one of the nobles who chose to keep their sworn words and were beheaded for it.
