## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) travels to [Astapor](wiki:Astapor) to view the [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied). The slaver [Kraznys mo Nakloz](wiki:Kraznys_mo_Nakloz) has his translator, [Missandei](wiki:Missandei), tell Daenerys that three days earlier a corsair king was interested in purchasing a thousand of the troops. Missandei corrects the [Good Master](wiki:Good_Master), and says that the unidentified corsair was interested in only one hundred Unsullied.(wiki:Corsair_king.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Garin](wiki:Garin_(orphan)) tells [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) that a corsair king has risen in the [Basilisk Isles](wiki:Basilisk_Isles) and raided [Tall Trees Town](wiki:Tall_Trees_Town) in the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles).(wiki:Corsair_king.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
