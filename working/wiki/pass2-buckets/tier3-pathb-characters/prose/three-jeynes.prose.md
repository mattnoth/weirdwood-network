## Origins

The three Jeynes were among the many [maidens](wiki:Maiden) who suffered what was called the "[Maiden's Day curse](wiki:Maiden's_Day_curse)" prior to the [Maiden's Day Ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball) of [133 AC](wiki:133_AC). Rumors spread saying they liked to dress in [squire](wiki:Squire)'s garb, visit [brothels](wiki:Brothel) along the [Street of Silk](wiki:Street_of_Silk) and kiss and fondle women as if they were boys.(wiki:Three_Jeynes.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
