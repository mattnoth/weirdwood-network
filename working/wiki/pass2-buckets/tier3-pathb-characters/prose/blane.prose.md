## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Blane is part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) led by Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), where he is [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand)'s second.(wiki:Blane.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He is the only one of Jeor's advisors who is not highborn.(wiki:Blane.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Blane is chosen to die by the conspirators who wish to leave the Night's Watch due to his position as the leader of the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) men in [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand)'s absence.(wiki:Blane.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) [Chett](wiki:Chett) tasks [Dirk](wiki:Dirk) with killing Blane, but their plans are interrupted by [wights](wiki:Wights) attacking the Night's Watch.(wiki:Blane.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Blane commands a group of Shadow Tower men during the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist);(wiki:Blane.cite_ref-Rasos18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) it is unknown if he survives the battle.
