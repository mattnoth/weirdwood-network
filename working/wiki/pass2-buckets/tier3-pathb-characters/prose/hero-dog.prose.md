## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

While [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) and her companions are travelling with Septon [Meribald](wiki:Meribald) through the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), Podrick asks what [Dog](wiki:Dog_(Meribald))’s name is. Meribald replies that he has not told him his name so he calls him Dog. Pod thinks about that for a while and then mentions that he used to have a dog when he was little and he called him Hero.
