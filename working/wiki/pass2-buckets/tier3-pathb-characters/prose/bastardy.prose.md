## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Prince [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) is believed by King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) to be his trueborn son, and is therefore the heir to [House Baratheon of King's Landing](wiki:House_Baratheon_of_King's_Landing), and heir to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). However, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) discovers that Joffrey, as well as his siblings [Myrcella](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) and [Tommen](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), are actually the bastards of Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) and her twin brother, [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Bastardy.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_64-0) After Robert's death, King Joffrey has Lord Stark executed after he admits to treason.(wiki:Bastardy.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_65-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The revelation of Joffrey's bastardy, as the product of adultery on the part of Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), causes Joffrey's claim to the Iron Throne to be disputed after Robert's death by Robert's eldest brother, [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Bastardy.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_66-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow)'s official bastard status is removed by a royal decree, as a reward for the [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton) betraying the [Starks](wiki:House_Stark) in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) and bowing to the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister), and he becomes the heir of House Bolton.(wiki:Bastardy.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_58-1)
[Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), as [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), planned to legitimize his bastard half-brother [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) and proclaim him as his heir.(wiki:Bastardy.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_53-4) Following Robb's death in the Red Wedding, Stannis, having proclaimed himself [King of Westeros](wiki:King_of_Westeros), also proposes to legitimize Jon and make him [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Bastardy.cite_ref-Rasos76.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_67-0) Jon declines Stannis's offer, however, agreeing to instead serve as [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) after being elected by his Sworn Brothers.(wiki:Bastardy.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-1)
Having fled [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with the aid of Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) poses as his supposed natural daughter, "Alayne Stone".(wiki:Bastardy.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Sansa continues posing as Alayne Stone, the bastard-born daughter of Littlefinger, while in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn).(wiki:Bastardy.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_68-0)(wiki:Bastardy.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_69-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) uses the name Hugor Hill while traveling through [Essos](wiki:Essos), pretending to be a bastard from [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport).(wiki:Bastardy.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_70-0)(wiki:Bastardy.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_71-0)
The legitimized Ramsay becomes enraged whenever he is called Snow instead of Bolton.(wiki:Bastardy.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-1)
