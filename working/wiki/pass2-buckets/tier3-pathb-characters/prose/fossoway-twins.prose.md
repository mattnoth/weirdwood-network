## Origins

In [73 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), the Fossoway twins entered the list in a [tourney at Old Oak](wiki:Tourney_at_Old_Oak) hosted by Lord [Oakheart](wiki:House_Oakheart) in honor of the birth of a son. Both of them were defeated by a [mystery knight](wiki:Mystery_knight) known as the "Silver Fool", which was later revealed to be Prince [Baelon Targaryen](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)).(wiki:Fossoway_twins.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
