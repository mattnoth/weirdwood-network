## Origins

Otho had the wooden palisades and ditch protecting [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) replaced with massive stone walls, thicker and higher than any seen in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) in that time, as the city had previously been sacked three times in a single century. Otho's efforts beggared the city for three generations, but succeeded in their purpose of turning back would-be invaders and raiders.(wiki:Otho_II_Hightower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
