## Origins

Among fifty [lords](wiki:Lord), Argoth won Maris's hand in the first [tourney](wiki:Tourney) held in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). Maris wed King [Uthor of the High Tower](wiki:Uthor_of_the_High_Tower) before Argoth could claim her, however, and Argoth spent the rest his life roaring for Maris outside the walls of [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown).(wiki:Argoth_Stone-Skin.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) It is unconfirmed if Argoth was a man or a [giant](wiki:Giant).
