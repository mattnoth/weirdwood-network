## Appearances & Description

The beautiful Merling Queen is never seen without [her Mermaids](wiki:Mermaids_(Merling_Queen's)), young [maidens](wiki:Maiden) in the blush of their first [flowering](wiki:Flowering), to hold her hair and veil.(wiki:Merling_Queen.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Like every Braavosi courtesan, she has her own [barge](wiki:Watercraft), and servants to pole her through the canals of Braavos to her trysts.(wiki:Merling_Queen.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Origins

One of the Merling Queen's Mermaids drowned at some point.(wiki:Merling_Queen.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) informs the [kindly man](wiki:Kindly_man) that the Merling Queen has chosen a new Mermaid to take the place of the one that drowned. She is the daughter of a [Prestayn](wiki:Prestayn) serving maid, thirteen and penniless, but lovely.(wiki:Merling_Queen.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
