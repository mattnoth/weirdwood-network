## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Maslyn is part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), the force that leaves [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) in search of [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings). He is part of the conspiracy to kill Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont).(wiki:Maslyn.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) He pleads for mercy during the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist), but is slain by a [wight](wiki:Wight).(wiki:Maslyn.cite_ref-Rasos18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
