## Origins

The disguised [Rickard Thorne](wiki:Rickard_Thorne) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) and his charge, Prince [Maelor Targaryen](wiki:Maelor_Targaryen), tried to stay overnight in the [Hogs Head](wiki:Hogs_Head) while journeying to the safety of Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower)'s host at the nearby [siege of Longtable](wiki:Siege_of_Longtable). Ben Buttercakes instructed his stableboy, [Sly](wiki:Sly_(stableboy)), to search for coins in Rickard's belongings, but Sly found Maelor's [dragon egg](wiki:Dragon_egg) wrapped in Rickard's white cloak instead. When he informed the inn's common room of his discovery, Rickard fled the inn with Maelor, slaying Ben on his way out.(wiki:Ben_Buttercakes.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
