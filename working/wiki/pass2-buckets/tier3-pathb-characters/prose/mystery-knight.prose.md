## Culture

Mystery knights choose to conceal their names for a variety of reasons, and they may or may not reveal their identity later.(wiki:Mystery_knight.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) They are often popular with [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk).(wiki:Mystery_knight.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Origins

[Jonquil Darke](wiki:Jonquil_Darke) competed in the [War for the White Cloaks](wiki:War_for_the_White_Cloaks) as the Serpent in Scarlet.(wiki:Mystery_knight.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Prince [Baelon Targaryen](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) participated in the [tourney at Old Oak](wiki:Tourney_at_Old_Oak) as the Silver Fool.(wiki:Mystery_knight.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Prince [Aemon Targaryen](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_II)) competed as the Knight of Tears in a tourney so he could name his sister, [Naerys Targaryen](wiki:Naerys_Targaryen), the tournament's [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty).(wiki:Mystery_knight.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) competed as the Gallows Knight during the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls).(wiki:Mystery_knight.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) competed as a mystery knight at the age of ten in a [tourney at Blackhaven](wiki:History_of_Tourneys_in_Westeros),(wiki:Mystery_knight.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) and he was knighted for again doing so at the age of sixteen in the [winter tourney at King's Landing](wiki:History_of_Tourneys_in_Westeros).(wiki:Mystery_knight.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The Bastard of Uplands competed in the [Oldtown tourney](wiki:History_of_Tourneys_in_Westeros) as [Blackshield](wiki:Blackshield), but he was defeated and exposed by Barristan.(wiki:Mystery_knight.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
According to [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), [Simon Toyne](wiki:Simon_Toyne), the infamous leader of the [Kingswood Brotherhood](wiki:Kingswood_Brotherhood), competed in [Lord Steffon's tourney](wiki:Lord_Steffon's_tourney) at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) as a mystery knight, where was defeated by Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen);(wiki:Mystery_knight.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) though, according to a semi-canon source, Barristan may have confused two separate tourneys.(wiki:Mystery_knight.cite_ref-7)
The [Knight of the Laughing Tree](wiki:Knight_of_the_Laughing_Tree) participated in the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) in [281 AC](wiki:281_AC). King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) tasked his son, Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), with uncovering the mystery knight's identity, but all that was found was the knight's shield.(wiki:Mystery_knight.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Quotes
