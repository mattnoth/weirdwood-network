## Narrative Arc

### A Dance With Dragons[ ]

[Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) provides the horse to [Val](wiki:Val) when she leaves on her mission to find [Tormund Giantsbane](wiki:Tormund_Giantsbane). When [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) goes to see Val off he sees the shaggy garron and is incredulous that Val has been given a horse that is blind, but [Mully](wiki:Mully) remarks that he is only half-blind and sound enough.
Val herself is unconcerned that the horse is half blind, telling Jon that she knows where she must go. After Val and her escort pass underneath the [Wall](wiki:Wall) Jon Snow bids goodbye to her. She then mounts the garron and wheels him towards the north.
At [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark) and [Sigorn](wiki:Sigorn)'s wedding feast a horn is sounded twice, announcing [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings). Jon knows that Val has succeeded in her mission, and that Tormund Giantsbane has come at last. Val returns to Castle Black alongside Jon on the back of the garron.(wiki:Half-blind_garron.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
