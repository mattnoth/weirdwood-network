## Origins

Septon Mattheus claimed to be a descendant of [House Gardener](wiki:House_Gardener), the extinguished family who once ruled as [Kings of the Reach](wiki:Kings_of_the_Reach). In [49 AC](wiki:49_AC) many felt he was almost guaranteed to be the next [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon). He became a part of the court of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) during the [regency](wiki:Regent) of the young King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen). Mattheus accompanied Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) and [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) when the [small council](wiki:Small_council) learned that King Jaehaerys and his younger sister, [Alysanne](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), had fled to the island fortress to wed. The [incestuous](wiki:Incest) marriage enraged the septon, who threw insults at the couple. Jaehaerys would not allow his bride to be insulted, however, and ordered Mattheus to be silent or he would have his lips sewn shut.(wiki:Mattheus.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
In [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), Septon Mattheus was dismissed from court by Jaehaerys and replaced with Septon [Barth](wiki:Barth), who came from [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden). According to legend, Mattheus passed Barth coming in the other direction whilst crossing the [Mander](wiki:Mander) at [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge).(wiki:Mattheus.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
After the death of the [High Lickspittle](wiki:High_Lickspittle), Mattheus became a likely candidate to become his successor as High Septon. During the first of the four ballots required for the election, Mattheus was the lead candidate, but his supporters dwindled thereafter with each ballot. On the fourth ballot, Septon [Alfyn](wiki:Alfyn_(septon)) was elected.(wiki:Mattheus.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
