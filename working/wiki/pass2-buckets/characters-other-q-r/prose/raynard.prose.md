## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Raynard informs the [small council](wiki:Small_council) that there are upwards of two thousand [sparrows](wiki:Sparrows) within King's Landing and more arriving each day. He is among the two leading possibilities to replace the [deceased High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Tyrion's)).(wiki:Raynard.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) visits Septon Raynard and Septon [Torbert](wiki:Torbert) at the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) and finds Raynard on his knees cleaning the floor. The [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow), the new High Septon, claims that doing work is pleasing to [the Smith](wiki:Smith_(the_Seven)).(wiki:Raynard.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

When the High Sparrow begins preaching against the brothels in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Cersei summons him to court to stop as brothels are a valued source of income for the crown. Instead the High Sparrow sends Raynard with an escort of six [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons). Raynard repeats the High Septon's words on the sinfulness of brothels which Cersei finds hypocritical, since she knows Raynard has "special friends" in half the brothels on the [Street of Silk](wiki:Street_of_Silk).(wiki:Raynard.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
