## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Along with Ser [Shadrich](wiki:Shadrich) and Ser [Morgarth the Merry](wiki:Morgarth_the_Merry), Ser Byron enters the service of Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon). He compliments [Alayne Stone](wiki:Sansa_Stark) on her beauty when introduced, and he kisses her hand when he leaves the room.(wiki:Byron_the_Beautiful.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
