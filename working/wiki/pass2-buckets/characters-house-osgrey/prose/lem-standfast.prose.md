## Origins

After being trained by Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey)'s sons with other peasants from his lands, Lem went off to fight for [House Osgrey](wiki:House_Osgrey) during the [Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Blackfyre_Rebellion). He was one of the only three surviving men that returned with Ser Eustace, along with [Dake](wiki:Dake) and [Pate](wiki:Pate_(Standfast)). Lem brought back a knife and a fine pair of boots from the war. The boots were too small to fit him, so he hung them on his wall for display.(wiki:Lem_(Standfast).cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
