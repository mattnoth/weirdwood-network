## Origins

Lord Reynard was the lord of Coldmoat during the time of Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey)'s father. When Eustace Osgrey's father took him to call on old Reynard Webber, he showed his son the chequy lion of Osgrey carved above the gates of the castle.(wiki:Reynard_Webber.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) His relation to Lord [Wyman Webber](wiki:Wyman_Webber) is unknown.
