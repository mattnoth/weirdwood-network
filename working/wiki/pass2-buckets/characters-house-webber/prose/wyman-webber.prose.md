## Origins

Wyman was good friends with Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey) until the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion). He fought at the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field) for [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen).(wiki:Wyman_Webber.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Lord Wyman died in the [Great Spring Sickness](wiki:Great_Spring_Sickness).(wiki:Wyman_Webber.cite_ref-Rawoiafwyman_webber.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) He dictated, in his will, that his daughter [Rohanne](wiki:Rohanne_Webber) would have to be married by the second anniversary of his death, or else the lands and income of [Coldmoat](wiki:Coldmoat) would pass to his cousin, Ser [Wendell Webber](wiki:Wendell_Webber).(wiki:Wyman_Webber.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
