## Origins

Jorgen was a peasant in service to the [Lady of Coldmoat](wiki:Lord_of_Coldmoat), [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber), during the reign of King [Aerys I Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_I_Targaryen). He was one of the peasants building a dam across the [Chequy Water](wiki:Chequy_Water). After Ser [Bennis of the Brown Shield](wiki:Bennis_of_the_Brown_Shield) slashed the face of another peasant, [Wolmer](wiki:Wolmer), Jorgen voiced his belief that the peasants could take Bennis and his fellow [knight](wiki:Knight), [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall). Wolmer convinced him that it would be best to go to Lady Webber.(wiki:Jorgen.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
