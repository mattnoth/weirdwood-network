## Origins

Lord [Wyman Webber](wiki:Wyman_Webber) dictated in his will that his daughter, Lady [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber), must have married before the second anniversary of his death or [Coldmoat](wiki:Coldmoat) and its lands would go to his cousin, Ser Wendell.(wiki:Wendell_Webber.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Appearances & Description

Wendell was a short, bald man with a goiter on his neck. He had a problem with flatulence.(wiki:Wendell_Webber.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

According to Septon [Sefton](wiki:Sefton_Staunton), Wendell was grasping and stupid.(wiki:Wendell_Webber.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
