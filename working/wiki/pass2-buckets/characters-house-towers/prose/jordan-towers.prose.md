## Origins

Ser [Walton Towers](wiki:Walton_Towers), a [household knight](wiki:Household_knight) of the king, was granted [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) by King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) in [44 AC](wiki:44_AC) after winning a melee in [Lord Harroway's Town](wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town), but Walton died a fortnight later from his wounds. Harrenhal thus passed to Jordan, Walton's eldest son. The Harrenhal holdings that passed to Jordan were much diminished as the holdings of [House Harroway](wiki:House_Harroway) were granted to Lord [Alton Butterwell](wiki:Alton_Butterwell) and Lord [Darnold Darry](wiki:Darnold_Darry).(wiki:Jordan_Towers.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Jordan_Towers.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Over the course of the king's wars, Lord Towers remained staunchly loyal to Maegor, though his elder sons all died fighting in the king's wars.(wiki:Jordan_Towers.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) In [48 AC](wiki:48_AC), Prince [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) [pressed his claim](wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen's_uprising) to the throne against his uncle Maegor, and high [lords](wiki:Lord) of the realm flocked to his cause. Maegor commanded a score of lesser lords, including Lord Towers, to gather at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), but their numbers were few and many soon lost heart. Shortly afterwards, Maegor was found dead upon the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne); Lords Towers and [Rosby](wiki:House_Rosby) were the last to see the king alive.(wiki:Jordan_Towers.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Along with Lords [Darklyn](wiki:Lord_Darklyn_(father_of_Jonquil_Darke)) and [Staunton](wiki:Lord_Staunton_(father_of_Cassella)), Jordan was the only one of Maegor's supporters to have the courage to yield the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) to Prince Jaehaerys and sisters, Princess [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) and Princess [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen). The three [lords](wiki:Lord) were sent to the [black cells](wiki:Black_cells) in chains, but were eventually pardoned by King Jaehaerys I after surrendering certain lands and sending a son to court to serve as a hostage.(wiki:Jordan_Towers.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Jordan eventually died of a chest congestion in [53 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). Harrenhal passed to Jordan's last surviving son, the sickly and impoverished [Maegor Towers](wiki:Maegor_Towers).(wiki:Jordan_Towers.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
