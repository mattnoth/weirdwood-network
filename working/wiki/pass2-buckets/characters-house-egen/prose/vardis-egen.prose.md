## Origins

During Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn)'s tenure as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Vardis served as his [captain of the guards](wiki:House_Arryn_guards).(wiki:Vardis_Egen.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn) and his mother, Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn), returned to the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) after Jon's death in the capital. Robert played with Vardis's son and the sons of the steward of the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), but Lysa sent them away, judging them too rough with her son.(wiki:Vardis_Egen.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Vardis Egen](wiki:Category:Images_of_Vardis_Egen)*

Vardis is stocky and silver haired.(wiki:Vardis_Egen.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) He has a square, plain face.(wiki:Vardis_Egen.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) considers Vardis to be humorless.(wiki:Vardis_Egen.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

Vardis wears a sky-blue cloak and hammered moon-and-falcon breastplate while on guard duty.(wiki:Vardis_Egen.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) When prepared for combat, however, the knight wears heavier steel, including plate over mail and padded surcoat. Vardis also dons a skirt of lobstered metal, gauntlets, and a solid gorget. His rondels are enameled cream-and-blue in the moon-and-falcon sigil of [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn). The temples of Vardis's helm are decorated with [falcon](wiki:Falcon)'s wings, and his visor is a pointed metal beak. The knight's triangular shield, near four feet tall, is heavy [oak](wiki:Oak) studded with [iron](wiki:Iron). He wields a [sword](wiki:Sword).(wiki:Vardis_Egen.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Vardis greets Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) upon her arrival at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), and Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) has him take [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to the [sky cells](wiki:Sky_cells).(wiki:Vardis_Egen.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

The captain is present when Tyrion demands a [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat) to demonstrate his innocence in the death of Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn). Vardis is chosen as the champion for [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) by Jon's widow, Lysa. He wields Jon's silver [falcon](wiki:Falcon) sword and is dressed in plate over mail. The heavy armor makes Vardis slow and clumsy, however, and [Bronn](wiki:Bronn), Tyrion's champion, tires him during their duel in the [godswood of the Eyrie](wiki:Godswood_of_the_Eyrie). After Bronn pushes the statue of [Alyssa Arryn](wiki:Alyssa_Arryn) on top of Vardis, the [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) kills the defenseless knight with his sword.(wiki:Vardis_Egen.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Lysa reluctantly allows Tyrion and Bronn to depart the Vale on the [high road](wiki:High_road).(wiki:Vardis_Egen.cite_ref-Ragot42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Lysa appoints Ser [Marwyn Belmore](wiki:Marwyn_Belmore) to replace Vardis.(wiki:Vardis_Egen.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Tyrion mentions Vardis when introducing Bronn to Ser [Mandon Moore](wiki:Mandon_Moore) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Vardis_Egen.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
