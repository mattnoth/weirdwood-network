## Appearances & Description

Like her father and her ancestor, the [Red Raven](wiki:Red_Raven_(free_folk)), she is [kissed by fire](wiki:Kissed_by_fire). [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) describes her hair as being of the same flame-red color as [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte)'s but hung long and straight. She is sweet looking and she is younger and more cowed than [Val](wiki:Val).(wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood's_eldest_daughter.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Gerrick and his family come through [the Wall](wiki:The_Wall) with the [Free Folk](wiki:Free_Folk) following [Tormund](wiki:Tormund). The so-called "King of the Wildlings" presents his three daughters with him when he swears his oath to [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), who agrees to present them to Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent).(wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood's_eldest_daughter.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Selyse arranges the betrothal of Gerrick's eldest daughter to Ser [Axell Florent](wiki:Axell_Florent), her [Hand](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) and uncle.(wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood's_eldest_daughter.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
