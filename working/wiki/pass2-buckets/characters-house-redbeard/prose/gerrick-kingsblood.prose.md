## Origins

Gerrick boasts of descent from [Raymun Redbeard](wiki:Raymun_Redbeard), a [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall) who invaded the [north](wiki:North) but was killed along with his sons in the [battle at Long Lake](wiki:Battle_at_Long_Lake). [Tormund](wiki:Tormund) claims Gerrick actually descends from Raymun's younger brother, the [Red Raven](wiki:Red_Raven_(free_folk)).

## Appearances & Description

Gerrick is tall with long legs, broad shoulders, long red hair and a red beard.(wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

According to [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), Gerrick seems a fool.(wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Gerrick comes through the [Wall](wiki:Wall) with the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk), or wildlings, following [Tormund](wiki:Tormund). His [red-haired son](wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood's_son) is one of the hostages that [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) demands. Gerrick brings his three daughters with him when he swears his oath to Jon, who agrees to present them to Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent).(wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Selyse attires Gerrick in clothes which once belonged to her husband, [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) She arranges the betrothals of [Gerrick's](wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood's_eldest_daughter) [three](wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood's_second_daughter) [daughters](wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood's_youngest_daughter) to Ser [Axell Florent](wiki:Axell_Florent), her [Hand](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), and to Ser [Brus Buckler](wiki:Brus_Buckler) and Ser [Malegorn of Redpool](wiki:Malegorn_of_Redpool), two of the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men). Tormund doubts(wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Selyse's claim that Gerrick is the true King of the Wildlings, descended in an unbroken male line from their great king, [Raymun Redbeard](wiki:Raymun_Redbeard).(wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
