## Appearances & Description

According to the [steward of Deepwood Motte](wiki:Glover_steward), Larence is smart and courageous.(wiki:Larence_Snow.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

After the death of Lord [Halys Hornwood](wiki:Halys_Hornwood) and his trueborn son, [Daryn](wiki:Daryn_Hornwood), many parties have started competing to inherit the title of Lord of [Hornwood](wiki:Hornwood). In discussions at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) during the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast), Larence is seen as a possible candidate, an idea supported by [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) as Larence reminds Bran of his own half-brother, [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow). Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) thinks this would please the [Glovers](wiki:House_Glover) while Lady [Donella Hornwood](wiki:Donella_Hornwood) might resent her husband's [bastard](wiki:Bastard) being made heir. However, [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Luwin](wiki:Luwin) thinks the scenario cannot be ruled out, as no new marriage of Lady Donella could produce an heir because she is too old to bear children.(wiki:Larence_Snow.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Deepwood Motte is [captured](wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte) by [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) and the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn).(wiki:Larence_Snow.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Now thirteen years old, Larence is kept captive at Deepwood Motte by Asha Greyjoy.(wiki:Larence_Snow.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Larence is presumably freed from captivity by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) after Asha's ironmen are defeated in the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Larence_Snow.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
