## Origins

The [septon](wiki:Septon) at [Horn Hill](wiki:Horn_Hill) sang "The Song of the Seven" to [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) and his sisters when they were young to help them sleep. When Samwell and his mother [Melessa](wiki:Melessa_Florent) sang the song to to the infant [Dickon](wiki:Dickon_Tarly), however, Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) reprimanded his wife and heir, as he believed that singing had made Sam weak.(wiki:The_Song_of_the_Seven.cite_ref-Rasos46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
