## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) listens to a performance of the song by a troupe of singers during the feast ending the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney).(wiki:The_Dance_of_the_Dragons.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

It is sung by [Collio Quaynis](wiki:Collio_Quaynis) at [the wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell); [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) does not particularly enjoy Collio's solo performance.(wiki:The_Dance_of_the_Dragons.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
