## Origins

As Mushroom was the court [fool](wiki:Fool) and a [dwarf](wiki:Dwarf), people believed him to be a [lackwit](wiki:Lackwit). This caused the highborns to speak freely around him. Mushroom's testimony was written down by an unknown writer. The account is filled with Mushroom's tales of plots, murders, trysts, and debaucheries, amongst other things. The stories are in the most explicit detail.(wiki:The_Testimony_of_Mushroom.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_viserys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
[Septon](wiki:Septon) [Eustace](wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)) wrote an account, [The Reign of King Viserys](wiki:The_Reign_of_King_Viserys,_First_of_His_Name,_and_the_Dance_of_the_Dragons_That_Came_After), covering many of the same events that are described in Mushroom's account, and while often the two disagree on how events occurred, at times they do agree.(wiki:The_Testimony_of_Mushroom.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_viserys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Copies of The Testimony of Mushroom were burned on orders of King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen), but a few copies escaped Baelor's flames and survived for [Archmaester](wiki:Archmaester) [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn) to reference in his own [history](wiki:Fire_&_Blood,_Being_a_History_of_the_Targaryen_Kings_of_Westeros).(wiki:The_Testimony_of_Mushroom.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
