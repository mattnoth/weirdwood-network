## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw) is reading about Signs and Portents when [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) meets him in the Book Tower of [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers). [Hotho Harlaw](wiki:Hotho_Harlaw) brought him a copy of Archmaester [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn)'s [Book of Lost Books](wiki:Book_of_Lost_Books) from [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). Rodrik tells Asha that Marwyn claims to have found three pages of Signs and Portents.(wiki:Signs_and_Portents.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
