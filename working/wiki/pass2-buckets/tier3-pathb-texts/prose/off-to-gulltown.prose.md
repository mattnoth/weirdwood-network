## Origins

Ser [Arlan of Pennytree](wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree) sang the song while he rode with [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) to the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC).
