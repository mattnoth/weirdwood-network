## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams) recalls a time when he was forced to sing "The King Without Courage" and "[The Name Day Boy](wiki:The_Name_Day_Boy)" in order to gain passage when he arrived naked at the [Bloody Gate](wiki:Bloody_Gate), having had all his possessions except his harp stolen by [Vale mountain clansmen](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans). The incident is still spoken of in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn), to which Tom has never returned, and he refuses to sing the song any more.(wiki:The_King_Without_Courage.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
