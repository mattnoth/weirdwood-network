## Origins

Andrey has been a close friend of Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell), daughter of [Doran](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), since childhood.(wiki:Andrey_Dalt.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Andrey_Dalt.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Both he and his brother [Deziel](wiki:Deziel_Dalt) had desired to marry her.(wiki:Andrey_Dalt.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Andrey experimented sexually with Arianne and [Tyene Sand](wiki:Tyene_Sand) in their youth, though he got too excited too early.(wiki:Andrey_Dalt.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Appearances & Description

Andrey has an open face and an easy smile.(wiki:Andrey_Dalt.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Andrey is part of Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell)'s conspiracy aiming to crown Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) as Queen of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Andrey_Dalt.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) After the conspiracy is exposed, Andrey is arrested by [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah) and his men and sent to [Ghaston Grey](wiki:Ghaston_Grey). As punishment for committing treason, Andrey is sent to the Free City of [Norvos](wiki:Norvos) for three years, to join the service of Lady [Mellario](wiki:Mellario), the estranged wife of [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne).(wiki:Andrey_Dalt.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
