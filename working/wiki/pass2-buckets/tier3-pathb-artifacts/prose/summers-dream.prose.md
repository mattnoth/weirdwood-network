## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The Summer's Dream was hired by [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) while he was in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). It took [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) and six others to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) via [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown), the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters), [Skagos](wiki:Skagos) and then [Eastwatch-by-the-sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-sea). The master of the ship was told to throw [Allar Deem](wiki:Allar_Deem) over the side when they got out to sea.(wiki:Summer's_Dream.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
