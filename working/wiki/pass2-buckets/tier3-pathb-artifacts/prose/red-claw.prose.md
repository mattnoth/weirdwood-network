## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Red Claw is spotted by [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) in the harbor of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) when [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) gathers his sworn swords in preparation for the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Red_Claw.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) It is part of Stannis's [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet) commanded by Ser [Imry Florent](wiki:Imry_Florent) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Red_Claw.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
