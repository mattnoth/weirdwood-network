## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Dagmer and the Foamdrinker are part of the small force under the command of [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) during the [harrying of the Stony Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore).(wiki:Foamdrinker.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Deviating from the plan of his father [Balon](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), Theon grants six longships to [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) to continue raiding the shore but keeps the Foamdrinker and the [Sea Bitch](wiki:Sea_Bitch).(wiki:Foamdrinker.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
Dagmer threatens [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Torrhen's_Square),(wiki:Foamdrinker.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) but Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) defeats him in the [fight at Torrhen's Square](wiki:Fight_at_Torrhen's_Square).(wiki:Foamdrinker.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Dagmer's distraction allows Theon to capture [Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell), however.(wiki:Foamdrinker.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell), Dagmer and his men [capture](wiki:Capture_of_Torrhen's_Square) Torrhen's Square.(wiki:Foamdrinker.cite_ref-Rasos76.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
