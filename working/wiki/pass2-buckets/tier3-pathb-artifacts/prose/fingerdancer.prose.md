## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The Fingerdancer is part of the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) contingent dispatched to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay). En route to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), near [Ghozai](wiki:Ghozai), she participes along with [Iron Victory](wiki:Iron_Victory), [Grief](wiki:Grief), and [Sparrowhawk](wiki:Sparrowhawk) in the capture of the merchant cog named [Ghiscari Dawn](wiki:Ghiscari_Dawn).(wiki:Fingerdancer.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
