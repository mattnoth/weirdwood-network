## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Godsgrace is part of King [Joffrey I Baratheon's](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) fleet at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). It is engulfed and destroyed by [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) after the explosion of the [Swordfish](wiki:Swordfish_(ship)).(wiki:Godsgrace_(ship).cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Some of its under officers survive the battle and are honored by the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Godsgrace_(ship).cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
