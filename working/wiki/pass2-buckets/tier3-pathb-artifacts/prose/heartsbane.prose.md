## Origins

For the last five centuries, the sword has been an heirloom of [House Tarly](wiki:House_Tarly) passed down from one [Lord of Horn Hill](wiki:Lord_of_Horn_Hill) to the next.(wiki:Heartsbane.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Heartsbane.cite_ref-Rasos18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
[Savage Sam Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly_(lord)) cut down dozens of [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) with Heartsbane during the [Vulture Hunt](wiki:Vulture_Hunt).(wiki:Heartsbane.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) let his son and heir, [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), hold Heartsbane a few times, but the boy was afraid he would hurt his sisters.(wiki:Heartsbane.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Randyll eventually disowned his pacific son, forcing Sam to [take the black](wiki:Take_the_black).(wiki:Heartsbane.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) wears Heartsbane while with [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)'s army.(wiki:Heartsbane.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

While serving [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) with the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) dreams of a feast at [Horn Hill](wiki:Horn_Hill) during which he cuts a roast with Heartsbane.(wiki:Heartsbane.cite_ref-Rasos46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Randyll carries Heartsbane while bringing order to [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool).(wiki:Heartsbane.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
[Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) anticipates claiming Heartsbane from Randyll when the [Reach](wiki:Reach) retaliates for the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields) by the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn).(wiki:Heartsbane.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
