## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The Lionstar is part of the fleet [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sends to bring [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos). The captains have orders to run off or destroy any lone ship they spot on the way. Should an enemy fleet attack, the [Bold Wind](wiki:Bold_Wind) would escort the [Seaswift](wiki:Seaswift) while the rest of the fleet, consisting of [King Robert's Hammer](wiki:King_Robert's_Hammer), Lionstar, and [Lady Lyanna](wiki:Lady_Lyanna), would give battle.(wiki:Lionstar.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
When the fleet of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) enters the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) before the start of the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) notes the absence of the Lionstar, King Robert's Hammer and the Lady Lyanna, making him suspect a trap, as these ships should lead any defense of the enemy.(wiki:Lionstar.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The Lionstar transports [Rhaegar](wiki:Rhaegar_Frey), Ser [Jared](wiki:Jared_Frey), and [Symond Frey](wiki:Symond_Frey) to [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) to deliver the remains of Ser [Wendel Manderly](wiki:Wendel_Manderly), who was killed at the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), and to negotiate [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) abandoning the [northern](wiki:North) cause and bending the knee to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) and King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon). On the way, the ship makes a stop at [Sisterton](wiki:Sisterton) to fill its water casks and the [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) are guests of Lord [Godric Borrell](wiki:Godric_Borrell). Godric informs Davos of this when the Onion Lord arrives in Sisterton twelve days later.(wiki:Lionstar.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Davos hopes that the ship sank in the same storms that destroyed [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan)'s fleet. However, when he arrives in White Harbor on board the [Merry Midwife](wiki:Merry_Midwife) to negotiate a possible alliance between Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) and Stannis, Davos spots the Lionstar lying in the harbor. Its banner displays the [Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon_of_King's_Landing) and [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) arms of King [Tommen I](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon). This increases Davos's doubts whether he can succeed in his mission.(wiki:Lionstar.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
