## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

While in [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool), [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) and [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne) pay a few pennies to stay in one of the galley's cabins for a night. The Lady of Myr has lost her mast and half of her crew in a storm, so her captain is appreciative of the two visitors.(wiki:Lady_of_Myr.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
