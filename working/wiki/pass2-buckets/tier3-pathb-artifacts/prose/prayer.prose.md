## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Prayer is seized by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) when Lord [Guncer Sunglass](wiki:Guncer_Sunglass) refuses to acknowledge Stannis as his liege, since Stannis destroyed the [sept](wiki:Sept) at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Prayer.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Prayer is part of the fleet commanded by Ser [Imry Florent](wiki:Imry_Florent) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Prayer.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) During the battle Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) recognizes the dog's head helm of [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane). Davos sees Sandor's [white cloak](wiki:Kingsguard) streaming from his shoulders as he rides [his horse](wiki:Stranger_(horse)) up the plank and on to the deck of Prayer, hacking down anyone who blunders within reach.(wiki:Prayer.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
