## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The Bold Laughter is part of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet) commanded by Ser [Imry Florent](wiki:Imry_Florent) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). It is destroyed when a boulder thrown from trebuchets in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) tears through its hull during the battle.(wiki:Bold_Laughter.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
