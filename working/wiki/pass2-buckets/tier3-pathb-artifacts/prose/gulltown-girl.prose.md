## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) and [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne) attempt to book passage on the Gulltown Girl at [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool). They are refused because the master of the ship believes Brienne to be a [whore](wiki:Whore) and he claims his ship is no bawdy house.(wiki:Gulltown_Girl.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
