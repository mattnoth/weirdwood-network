## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

The Wind Witch is contracted by Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), to take his daughters, [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark), from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), as Ned intends to inform King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) of Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s deceit when the king returns from hunting.(wiki:Wind_Witch.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Ned instructs [Tomard](wiki:Tomard), the captain of the [Stark guards](wiki:House_Stark_guards), to personally deliver a letter to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), Lord of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), during the journey to the [north](wiki:North).(wiki:Wind_Witch.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) betrays Ned, however, and Tom is killed in the throne room before the ship can depart King's Landing.(wiki:Wind_Witch.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
When Arya is living on the streets of the city, she goes to the river front to investigate stowing away aboard a ship and sees the Wind Witch with guards in the colors of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) guarding it. She is about to go to them when she realizes she knows none of the guards. When the unknown guards notice her, she pretends to be a boy trying to sell them a pigeon she had killed and they tell her to scram.(wiki:Wind_Witch.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
