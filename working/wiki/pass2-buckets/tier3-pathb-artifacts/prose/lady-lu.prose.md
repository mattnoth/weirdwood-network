## Appearances & Description

When [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) sees the axe, he thinks to himself that Lady Lu is the biggest axe he has ever seen, with the sharpest edge Davos has ever seen. The other keepers at the [Wolf's Den](wiki:Wolf's_Den) claim that Garth spends his days honing the axe.(wiki:Lady_Lu.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

While in captivity, [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) is shown Lady Lu by [Garth](wiki:Garth_(Wolf's_Den)), one of the keepers of the [Wolf's Den](wiki:Wolf's_Den). Garth tells Davos that when Davos's hands and head will be cut off, it will be done using Lady Lu.(wiki:Lady_Lu.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
