## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos) sails from [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) on the Silken Cloud, along with thirteen galleys. He tries to persuade [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) to leave [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay). When she refuses he returns to Qarth.(wiki:Silken_Cloud.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
