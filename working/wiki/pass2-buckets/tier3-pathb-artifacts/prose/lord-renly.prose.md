## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord Renly is among the ships being built as part of Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s plan to increase the size of the [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet), instead of relying on the traditional sea powers to protect the crown's interests.(wiki:Lord_Renly.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
During her imprisonment by the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), Cersei is visited by [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn), who tells the queen that [Aurane Waters](wiki:Aurane_Waters) fled King's Landing with the new ships after her arrest.(wiki:Lord_Renly.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
