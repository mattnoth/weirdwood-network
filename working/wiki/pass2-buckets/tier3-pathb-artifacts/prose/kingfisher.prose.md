## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

While passing through the [Sorrows](wiki:Sorrows), the [Shy Maid](wiki:Shy_Maid) crosses paths with the Kingfisher, whose crew claims to transport knives, needles, lace, linen, and spice [wine](wiki:Wine). The Kingfisher informs the Shy Maid that [Triarchs](wiki:Triarch_of_Volantis) [Malaquo Maegyr](wiki:Malaquo_Maegyr) and [Nyessos Vhassar](wiki:Nyessos_Vhassar) want to take [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) to war.(wiki:Kingfisher.cite_ref-Radwd18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
