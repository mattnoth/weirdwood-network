## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

The Peregrine takes Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) and her party from [Ghost Hill](wiki:Ghost_Hill) across the [Sea of Dorne](wiki:Sea_of_Dorne) to meet [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) in the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands). The crossing takes a day and a night, and [Jayne Ladybright](wiki:Jayne_Ladybright) becomes seasick. Arianne loses to [Daemon Sand](wiki:Daemon_Sand) and [Garibald Shells](wiki:Garibald_Shells) in games of [cyvasse](wiki:Cyvasse).(wiki:Peregrine.cite_ref-1) The party disembarks at the [Weeping Town](wiki:Weeping_Town).(wiki:Peregrine.cite_ref-2)
