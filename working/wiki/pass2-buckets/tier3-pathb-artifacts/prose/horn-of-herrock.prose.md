## Origins

[Herrock the Whoreson](wiki:Herrock_the_Whoreson) used the horn as a signal for the whores of [Kayce](wiki:Kayce) to open a postern gate, allowing his men to take the town from the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) for [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Horn_of_Herrock.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) orders Ser [Kennos of Kayce](wiki:Kennos_of_Kayce) to blow the Horn of Herrock to announce his arrival before the barred gates of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal),(wiki:Horn_of_Herrock.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) and does so again when they arrive at [Darry](wiki:Darry).(wiki:Horn_of_Herrock.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Kennos sounds it a third time upon their arrival at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Horn_of_Herrock.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
