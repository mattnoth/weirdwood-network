## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Courageous is part of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s fleet commanded by Ser [Imry Florent](wiki:Imry_Florent) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). It is among the first ships to be hit by burning pitch. She eventually sinks and her survivors are brought aboard the [Cat](wiki:Cat_(galley)). Her hulk is engulfed by wildfire after the explosion of the [Swordfish](wiki:Swordfish).(wiki:Courageous.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
