## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[The master](wiki:Master_of_the_White_Hart) of the White Hart plans to slip anchor from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) and go over to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), but his plan is uncovered by Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys). [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) orders for him to be put to trial by King [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey) to send a message to the other captains.(wiki:White_Hart.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The White Hart is a part of King [Joffrey I Baratheon's](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) fleet at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). During the battle, the White Hart is successfully boarded by the [Black Betha](wiki:Black_Betha). After the [Swordfish](wiki:Swordfish_(ship)) rams a hulk filled with [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire), the White Hart is engulfed by [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) and destroyed.(wiki:White_Hart.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
