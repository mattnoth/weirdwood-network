## Origins

When Lord [Marq Farman](wiki:Marq_Farman) of [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle) died in [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), his eldest son [Franklyn Farman](wiki:Franklyn_Farman) became the new [lord](wiki:Lord). He ordered [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)), who had been a guest of his late father, to leave [Faircastle](wiki:Faircastle).
