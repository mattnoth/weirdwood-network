## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Faithful is part of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s fleet commanded by Ser [Imry Florent](wiki:Imry_Florent) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). It engages the [Kingslander](wiki:Kingslander) with the aid of the [Sceptre](wiki:Sceptre). Rammed during the battle, the Faithful is engulfed and destroyed by [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) after the explosion of the [Swordfish](wiki:Swordfish_(ship)).(wiki:Faithful.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
