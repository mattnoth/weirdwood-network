## Origins

Lord [Steffon Baratheon](wiki:Steffon_Baratheon) and his wife, Lady [Cassana Estermont](wiki:Cassana_Estermont), took Windproud to the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) to search for a wife for Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), the son of King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen). On their return trip, however, the ship broke up during a storm in [Shipbreaker Bay](wiki:Shipbreaker_Bay). [Robert](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) and [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) watched the destruction of their parents' ship from [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End). All lives were lost with the exception of [Patchface](wiki:Patchface), who washed up on shore three days later, half-mad.(wiki:Windproud.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
