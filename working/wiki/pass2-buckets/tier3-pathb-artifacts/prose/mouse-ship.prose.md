## Origins

After her father passed away, [Marilda of Hull](wiki:Marilda_of_Hull) sold his shipyard and bought the cog she [named](wiki:Named_after) Mouse, after her childhood nickname. Within a decade she had become a canny trader who owned seven ships.(wiki:Mouse_(ship).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
When Marilda's son, [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon), agreed to transport King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) from [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) back to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), the king sailed with Marilda on her battered Mouse, rather than a prestigious [Velaryon](wiki:House_Velaryon) warship.(wiki:Mouse_(ship).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
