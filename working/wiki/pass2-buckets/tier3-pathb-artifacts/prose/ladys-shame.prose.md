## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lady's Shame is a part of King [Joffrey I Baratheon's](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) fleet at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). It engages [Queen Alysanne](wiki:Queen_Alysanne) with the aid of its sister ship, [Lady of Silk](wiki:Lady_of_Silk). Lady's Shame is rammed simultaneously by [Black Betha](wiki:Black_Betha) and [Lady Marya](wiki:Lady_Marya).
