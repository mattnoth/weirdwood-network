## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The Shark is part of the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) contingent dispatched to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay). Violent storms east of the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) leave the Shark little more than a hulk, so [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) decides to leave her behind at the rendezvous point, off the southern tip of the [Isle of Cedars](wiki:Isle_of_Cedars), to make repairs and to alert stragglers to the current plan.
