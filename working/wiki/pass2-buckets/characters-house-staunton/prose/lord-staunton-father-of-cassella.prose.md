## Origins

In [48 AC](wiki:48_AC), Prince [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) [pressed his claim to the throne](wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen's_uprising) against his [usurping](wiki:Usurper) uncle, King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), and high [lords](wiki:Lord) of the realm flocked to his cause. Maegor commanded a score of lesser lords, including Lord Staunton, to gather at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), but their numbers were few and many soon lost heart. Shortly afterwards, Maegor was found dead upon the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), and the prince ascended as King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.(wiki:Lord_Staunton_(father_of_Cassella).cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) In the chaos that followed Maegor's death, many lords fled the capital, but Lord Staunton remained.(wiki:Lord_Staunton_(father_of_Cassella).cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Along with Lord [Jordan Towers](wiki:Jordan_Towers) and [Lord Darklyn](wiki:Lord_Darklyn_(father_of_Jonquil_Darke)), he yielded the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) to Prince Jaehaerys, Princess [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)), and Princess [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen). The three lords were sent to the [black cells](wiki:Black_cells), but were eventually pardoned by King Jaehaerys I after surrendering some of their land and sending a child to court to serve as a [hostage](wiki:Hostage).(wiki:Lord_Staunton_(father_of_Cassella).cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

In [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), Lord Staunton was among the lords who visited Jaehaerys at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). This displeased Jaehaerys' [Hand](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon), who referred to them as the "lords lickspittle".(wiki:Lord_Staunton_(father_of_Cassella).cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Lord Staunton's daughter, [Cassella](wiki:Cassella_Staunton), became a companion to Princess Rhaena on Dragonstone in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC). She died of [poison](wiki:Poison) that same year, however.(wiki:Lord_Staunton_(father_of_Cassella).cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Five years later, Lord Staunton, his lady wife, and his remaining children all died of the [Shivers](wiki:Shivers) at [Rook's Rest](wiki:Rook's_Rest).(wiki:Lord_Staunton_(father_of_Cassella).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
