## Origins

When [riots](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)) broke out in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Arthor was in the manse of his kinsman, Lord [Bartimos Celtigar](wiki:Bartimos_Celtigar), who served as [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin) to Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen). The building was overrun by a mob, and Arthor made a brave stand in a doorway, holding the mob at bay briefly with a sword, until a treacherous serving girl let rioters in from a back way. Arthor was slain when he was stabbed in the back with a spear wielded by one of the throng.(wiki:Arthor_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
