## Origins

Alton was named [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) by King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), following the resignation of Lord [Edmyn Tully](wiki:Edmyn_Tully) in [9 AC](wiki:9_AC). Alton served ably as Hand until he died from natural causes in [17 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).
