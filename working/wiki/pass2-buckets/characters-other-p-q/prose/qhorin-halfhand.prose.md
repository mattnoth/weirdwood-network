## Origins

Qhorin lost all the fingers on his right hand except his thumb and forefinger after catching a [wildling](wiki:Wildling) axe before it would strike his skull. Qhorin then thrust his maimed hand into his adversary's face and slew the man while he was blinded by blood.(wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-9) The Halfhand trained how to fight left-handed and became just as proficient with his left hand as he had been with his right.(wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand.cite_ref-Racok68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-5) Qhorin has explored deep into the lands [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-10)

Qhorin was a good friend to [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) before Mance deserted the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand.cite_ref-Racok53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3) The Halfhand also knows [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell), and his late father, Lord [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark).(wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-11)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Qhorin is placed in command of a hundred men from the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) charged to meet up with [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), during the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) in search of [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark). On the way to the rendezvous, Qhorin's men run into a wildling scouting party led by [Alfyn Crowkiller](wiki:Alfyn_Crowkiller), which they practically destroys at the cost of four dead and a dozen wounded. A prisoner taken in the battle reveals that [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) was in the [Frostfangs](wiki:Frostfangs) searching for some magic to bring down the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-12)

When Qhorin meets Lord Commander Mormont at the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men), he advises sending three scouting parties into the mountains to discover what Mance was looking for, one of which he leads personally.(wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-13) Qhorin's party is composed of [Dalbridge](wiki:Dalbridge), [Ebben](wiki:Ebben), [Stonesnake](wiki:Stonesnake), and [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow).(wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Qhorin personally requests the participation of Jon, who had been serving as Jeor's steward.(wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-14)

Qhorin's rangers enter the [Skirling Pass](wiki:Skirling_Pass). Stonesnake and Jon kill a small party of wildlings with the exception of [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte), whom Jon takes captive after she yields. Qhorin allows Jon to choose Ygritte's fate, and Jon eventually decides to let her go free;(wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) Qhorin is not surprised by Jon's decision.(wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand.cite_ref-Racok53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-4)

When the rangers are spotted by the [eagle](wiki:Eagle) of [Orell](wiki:Orell), Qhorin decides that they must retreat. Qhorin leaves Dalbridge at a strong position,(wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand.cite_ref-Racok53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-5) but the skilled bowman is eventually killed by wildlings. Qhorin sends [Ebben](wiki:Ebben) to ride with all haste back to the Fist, while the other rangers draw off the pursuing wildlings. Qhorin sends Stonesnake to climb the Frostfangs by himself.(wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand.cite_ref-Racok68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-6)

Realizing that he and Jon will eventually be overtaken, Qhorin has Jon recite the oath of the Night's Watch together with him. Qhorin orders Jon to yield if they are caught, in order to act as a spy. [Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt)'s band catches up with them, and a [spearwife](wiki:Spearwife) shows them Ebben's head. When Jon yields as previously ordered, Ygritte vouches for him. Rattleshirt tells Jon to prove his sincerity by killing Qhorin, and the Halfhand immediately attacks the younger man. [Ghost](wiki:Ghost) distracts Qhorin by biting his calf, and Jon slashes the ranger's throat with [Longclaw](wiki:Longclaw); Rattleshirt is displeased by Jon's [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) having aided him in the fight. The wildlings burn Qhorin's body, with Rattleshirt taking some bones for himself.(wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand.cite_ref-Racok68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-7)

### A Storm of Swords

[Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte) wears Qhorin's cloak as her own, while [Lenyl](wiki:Lenyl) takes his hauberk, [Ragwyle](wiki:Ragwyle) his gloves, and a bowman his boots. [Longspear Ryk](wiki:Longspear_Ryk) gives Qhorin's helm to Ygritte since it does not fit him.(wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) [Styr](wiki:Styr) is unhappy after learning of Qhorin's death; like Rattleshirt, he had wanted to kill the Halfhand himself. [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) tells Jon that he was once Qhorin's friend. While Mance admits that he himself is easily tempted by the charms of women, he compares Qhorin, a man loyal to the vows of the Night's Watch, to carved old oak.(wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2)

After the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black), the captured [Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt) tells Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne) and [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) that Jon had begged for his life in the Frostfangs. When Qhorin tried to kill Jon as a craven, Ghost attacked the Halfhand and Jon then opened his throat. Alliser and Janos disbelieve Jon's story of Qhorin's last command. (wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) does believe Jon after the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), however.(wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand.cite_ref-Rasos76.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) While gathering support for Jon to be chosen as the new Lord Commander, [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) reminds Ser [Denys Mallister](wiki:Denys_Mallister) that Qhorin had trusted Jon.(wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

When Jon suffers a nightmare, Qhorin is among the men cut down by the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch).(wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
