## Appearances & Description

Quhuru is black and wears a cape of green and yellow feathers.(wiki:Quhuru_Mo.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) brings Quhuru from the docks of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) to meet with [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen). He comes to see her [dragons](wiki:Dragon). He in turn informs her that King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) has died. After leaving Daenerys he is off to do the traders' circle in the [Jade Sea](wiki:Jade_Sea).(wiki:Quhuru_Mo.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Feast for Crows

In [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) and Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) meet [Xhondo](wiki:Xhondo), a mate of Quhuru's crew. Xhondo explains to them that he has heard about dragons and tells them about Daenerys. Xhondo's stories make Aemon strong enough to try the voyage with the *[Cinnamon Wind](wiki:Cinnamon_Wind)*. In payment for the passage Captain Quhuru asks for the books Sam had brought with him. Quhuru knows he can sell the books for a good price to the [maesters](wiki:Maesters) of the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel). Quhuru also wants Aemon's chain. Sam refuses to give the chain and after some haggling Quhuru is satisfied with some of their clothes instead.(wiki:Quhuru_Mo.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

During the trip Aemon dies. Normally the [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen) burn their dead but Quhuru does not allow this because of the danger for the ship.(wiki:Quhuru_Mo.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
