## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

She claimed the crown along with [King Cutthroat](wiki:King_Cutthroat) when [Cleon the Second](wiki:Cleon_the_Second) was slain.(wiki:Queen_Whore.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Queen_Whore.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) She is killed along with most of the population of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor) when the city is [besieged](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor) by the [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai). She reportedly died fighting against the enemies, while King Cutthroat surrendered.(wiki:Queen_Whore.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
