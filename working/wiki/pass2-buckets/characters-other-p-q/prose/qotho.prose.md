## Appearances & Description

Qotho is known as the most sadistic of Drogo's bloodriders. He has cruel eyes and quick hands that like to hurt. He leaves bruises on [Doreah](wiki:Doreah)’s pale skin, and sometimes makes [Irri](wiki:Irri) sob in the night. Even his [horses](wiki:Horse) react to him with fear.(wiki:Qotho.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

As a wedding gift to the newly-wed [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) and [Khal](wiki:Khal) [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), Qotho gives them a double curved [dragonbone](wiki:Dragonbone) bow.(wiki:Qotho.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

When [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) bares [steel](wiki:Steel) during a feast in [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak) and threatens Daenerys, Drogo orders his bloodriders to seize him. Qotho pins Viserys while the others disarm him.(wiki:Qotho.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

After Drogo's injury, Daenerys intends to take her husband to the *[maegi](wiki:Maegi)* [Mirri Maz Duur](wiki:Mirri_Maz_Duur) to heal him, but Drogo's bloodriders, including Qotho, attempt to stop this from happening. Qotho kills [Quaro](wiki:Quaro) in the ensuing fight and is in turn killed by Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont).(wiki:Qotho.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
