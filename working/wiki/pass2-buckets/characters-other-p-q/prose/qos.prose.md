## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Captain Qos and the *[Wind Witch](wiki:Wind_Witch)* are hired by Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) to take his daughters, [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark), home to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Qos.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Before Qos can sail, however, Lord Eddard [is seized](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark) during Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish)'s coup.(wiki:Qos.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Arya later sees the *[Wind Witch](wiki:Wind_Witch)* still at harbor in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Qos.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
