## Origins

One of the girls gave Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) his first kiss. Ser [Cletus Yronwood](wiki:Cletus_Yronwood) suggested that Quentyn take one or both of them as [paramours](wiki:Paramour), but the prince instead avoided the pair afterwards.(wiki:Drinkwater_twins.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Appearances & Description

The Drinkwater twins are tawny young [maidens](wiki:Maiden), who enjoy [hawking](wiki:Hawk), hunting, and climbing rocks.(wiki:Drinkwater_twins.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

While plotting to tame one of the [dragons](wiki:Dragon) of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) regrets not having spent more time with the Drinkwater twins.(wiki:Drinkwater_twins.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
