## Appearances & Description

Tytos wears a pale grey doublet slashed with cloth-of-silver, with the [amethyst](wiki:Amethyst) unicorn of [House Brax](wiki:House_Brax) pinned above his heart.(wiki:Tytos_Brax.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Tytos is a member of the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) host besieging [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), but he is captured at the [Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Tytos_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) His father, Lord [Andros Brax](wiki:Andros_Brax), dies during the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps).(wiki:Tytos_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Clash of Kings

A Brax is among the western lords present at King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon)'s first audience in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) after the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Tytos_Brax.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) It is unknown if this is Tytos, who may have been ransomed by [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) or managed to escape captivity.

### A Storm of Swords

Previously a captive at the [Twins](wiki:Twins), Tytos has become the new Lord of [Hornvale](wiki:Hornvale).(wiki:Tytos_Brax.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) His younger brother, Ser [Flement](wiki:Flement_Brax), is his heir.(wiki:Tytos_Brax.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Tytos is among the gathered nobility that escorts the corpse of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) back to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock). Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) thinks Lord Brax could deal with the [outlaws](wiki:Outlaws) [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) and [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion), but he would not be a good [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:Tytos_Brax.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
