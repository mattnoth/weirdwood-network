## Appearances & Description

Franklyn is one of the most powerful [lords](wiki:Lord) in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). He shares his family's ancestral enmity against [House Yronwood](wiki:House_Yronwood) and dislikes their current ruler, Lord [Anders Yronwood](wiki:Anders_Yronwood).(wiki:Franklyn_Fowler.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Franklyn_Fowler.cite_ref-Rawoiaffranklyn_fowler.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) decides to contact Lord Franklyn for aid, for he is strong enough to threaten her father, Prince [Doran](wiki:Doran_Martell), and dislikes [House Yronwood](wiki:House_Yronwood). She sends a letter with [Cedra](wiki:Cedra), but it is intercepted before it can get to Franklyn.(wiki:Franklyn_Fowler.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

### The Winds of Winter

Lord Franklyn commands the Dornish host assembled at the [Prince's Pass](wiki:Prince's_Pass).(wiki:Franklyn_Fowler.cite_ref-twow-arianne_4-0)
