## Origins

Roggerio was the son of Lysandro Rogare. He had five trueborn brothers, [Lysaro](wiki:Lysaro_Rogare), [Moredo](wiki:Moredo_Rogare), [Lotho](wiki:Lotho_Rogare), [Drako](wiki:Drako_Rogare), and [Fredo](wiki:Fredo_Rogare), and three trueborn sisters, [Larra](wiki:Larra_Rogare), [Lysara](wiki:Lysara_Rogare), and [Marra](wiki:Marra_Rogare), as well as sixteen [bastard](wiki:Bastard) siblings.(wiki:Roggerio_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

When his sister Larra came to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) as the wife of Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen), Roggerio, Moredo, and Lotho came with her. Roggerio and Lotho were fluent in the [common tongue](wiki:Common_tongue), although Moredo was not.(wiki:Roggerio_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Roggerio opened a high-class [pleasure house](wiki:Pleasure_house) which he called the [Mermaid](wiki:Mermaid_(pillow_house)) near the [River Gate](wiki:River_Gate) in the capital. The Mermaid was no common brothel, but an opulent establishment, full of exotic animals such as [parrots](wiki:Parrot) and [monkeys](wiki:Monkey). Its [prostitutes](wiki:Prostitutes) were exotic too, from every corner of the world and cost ten times more than common whores. Despite this Roggerio never lacked for customers, mainly high [lords](wiki:Lord), [knights](wiki:Knight) and rich merchants. Roggerio banned [Tessario the Tiger](wiki:Tessario_the_Tiger) from the Mermaid when the sellsword struck one of his girls.(wiki:Roggerio_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

In [135 AC](wiki:135_AC) Roggerio was arrested at the Mermaid by Ser [Marston Waters](wiki:Marston_Waters) when [House Rogare](wiki:House_Rogare) was accused of treason. Although the treason against King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) was later proven unfounded, the new [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Lord [Torrhen Manderly](wiki:Torrhen_Manderly) still sentenced Roggerio to seven lashes, when Roggerio asked *"for what?"*, Lord Manderly replied *"for being a thrice damned Lyseni"*.(wiki:Roggerio_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

After his punishment and release, he sold his brothel and everything in it to purchase a great [cog](wiki:Ships) which he named the [Mermaid's Daughter](wiki:Mermaid's_Daughter), which effectively was the pillowhouse reborn with sails. Roggerio departed King's Landing and sailed the sea, trading in [wines](wiki:Wine) and carnal pleasures at every port he visited.(wiki:Roggerio_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)
