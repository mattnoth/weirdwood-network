## Origins

Marra was the daughter of Lysandro Rogare. She had six brothers, [Lysaro](wiki:Lysaro_Rogare), [Moredo](wiki:Moredo_Rogare), [Lotho](wiki:Lotho_Rogare), [Drako](wiki:Drako_Rogare), [Fredo](wiki:Fredo_Rogare), and [Roggerio](wiki:Roggerio_Rogare), and two sisters, [Larra](wiki:Larra_Rogare) and [Lysara](wiki:Lysara_Rogare), as well as sixteen bastard siblings.(wiki:Marra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

After the death of her father, [Lysandro "the Magnificent"](wiki:Lysandro_Rogare), Marra inherited his library. When the [Rogare Bank](wiki:Rogare_Bank) ran dry in [135 AC](wiki:135_AC), Lysaro Rogare's brothers and sisters claimed to have no part in it, but many doubted their claims of innocence. She claimed sanctuary at the Temple of [Yndros](wiki:Yndros_of_the_Twilight) in man's garb. When the Rogares were tried, Marra was deprived of the books she had inherited.(wiki:Marra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
