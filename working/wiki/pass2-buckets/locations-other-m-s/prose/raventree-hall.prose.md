## Origins

The [Blackwoods](wiki:House_Blackwood) made Raventree their seat in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) after they were driven from the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) by the [Kings of Winter](wiki:Kings_of_Winter).(wiki:Raventree_Hall.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

The Blackwoods claim that Raventree's great [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) was poisoned by the [Brackens](wiki:House_Bracken).(wiki:Raventree_Hall.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

The [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Arlan III Durrandon](wiki:Arlan_III_Durrandon) married a daughter of Lord [Roderick Blackwood](wiki:Roderick_Blackwood) beneath Raventree's weirwood. When Roderick rebelled against [House Teague](wiki:House_Teague), Arlan relieved King [Humfrey I Teague](wiki:Humfrey_I_Teague)'s ensuing siege of the castle.(wiki:Raventree_Hall.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Lord Blackwood hosted Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) and Princess [Daella Targaryen](wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)) when the princess was fifteen. Daella was charmed by [Royce Blackwood](wiki:Royce_Blackwood), but refused to marry him after learning the Blackwoods followed the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods).(wiki:Raventree_Hall.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

[Widow Fairs](wiki:Widow_Fairs) were held at Raventree after the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Raventree_Hall.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) The bones of Ser [Addam Velaryon](wiki:Addam_Velaryon) were stored by Lord [Benjicot Blackwood](wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood) at Raventree for eight years after the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton), until Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) had his brother's remains interred at [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark) in [138 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Raventree_Hall.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Raventree Hall](wiki:Category:Images_of_Raventree_Hall)*

Raventree has high, ancient stone walls with moss climbing up them. There are two huge, square towers flanking the gate and a square tower at each angle in the wall. It is also defended by a deep moat lined with stone. Inside the walls are a muddy outer ward and a cavernous timber keep.(wiki:Raventree_Hall.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Lord Blackwood's [solar](wiki:Solar) in the keep is large and airy, with great beams of dark [oak](wiki:Oak). Wool tapestries adorn its walls, while latticework doors with panes of diamond-shaped yellow glass overlook the [godswood of Raventree Hall](wiki:Godswood_of_Raventree_Hall). It contains a high-backed chair.(wiki:Raventree_Hall.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

The godswood contains a dead, ancient [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) of colossal size. Every evening at dusk hundreds of [ravens](wiki:Raven) come and roost all night on the dead weirwood as they have for thousands of years.(wiki:Raventree_Hall.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Near the castle, a rise overlooking a stream gives a view of two of Raventree's gates.(wiki:Raventree_Hall.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

During the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), the stronghold is [taken](wiki:Fall_of_Raventree_Hall) by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) and Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) due to the absence of Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood), who is at [Riverrun](wiki:Battle_under_the_walls_of_Riverrun).(wiki:Raventree_Hall.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Clash of Kings

Following the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), Tytos is allowed to depart Riverrun by [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident). Tytos leads the [liberation of Raventree Hall](wiki:Liberation_of_Raventree_Hall).(wiki:Raventree_Hall.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Ser [Garth Greenfield](wiki:Garth_Greenfield) is held captive in the castle.(wiki:Raventree_Hall.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Raventree is [besieged](wiki:Siege_of_Raventree) by Lord [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken) after the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding). Lord [Karyl Vance](wiki:Karyl_Vance) implores Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) to intervene, knowing that Lord Blackwood would never surrender to Lord Bracken.(wiki:Raventree_Hall.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

In the wake of the Red Wedding and the submissions of [Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun) and [Seagard](wiki:Siege_of_Seagard), Raventree Hall is the last castle in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) refusing to declare for King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon). Jaime Lannister goes to Raventree and negotiates the surrender of Lord Tytos, taking his son [Hoster](wiki:Hoster_Blackwood) as a hostage to ensure good behavior.(wiki:Raventree_Hall.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) Tytos intends to have the bones of [Lucas Blackwood](wiki:Lucas_Blackwood), a son who was killed at the Red Wedding, buried in [the godswood](wiki:Godswood_of_Raventree_Hall) beneath the great [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood).(wiki:Raventree_Hall.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)
