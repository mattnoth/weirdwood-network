## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

At the start of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) in [298 AC](wiki:298_AC), [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) declares himself [Lord of the Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Lord_of_the_Seven_Kingdoms) after the death of his brother, King [Robert](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). To reflect his status, King Renly creates the Rainbow Guard, his own version of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell), Renly's lover, is the first appointee and [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard). Except for the Lord Commander, each of the members are given a color of the rainbow.(wiki:Rainbow_Guard.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

The last position is held open, in the hope of awarding it to Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), should he join Renly's cause. Barristan the Bold never arrives in Renly's camp, however, and the cloak is granted to [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth), who is awarded the position after winning the great [melee at Bitterbridge](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge) involving over a hundred [knights](wiki:Knight), including Loras.(wiki:Rainbow_Guard.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Following the death of Renly at [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)) in [299 AC](wiki:299_AC),(wiki:Rainbow_Guard.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) the Rainbow Guard disintegrates, with Loras Tyrell joining his family in declaring for King [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), two killed by Loras, three switching allegiance to King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), and one fleeing and becoming the sworn sword of Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark).
