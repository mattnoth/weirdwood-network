## Origins

During the Faith Militant uprising, Jon remained loyal to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). He fought in the [battle at the Great Fork](wiki:Battle_at_the_Great_Fork), under the command of King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen). Early in the battle, he was blinded by a sword slash, yet he managed to rally his men and led a charge that broke through the lines of the [Faith Militant](wiki:Faith_Militant) and put the [Poor Fellows](wiki:Poor_Fellows) to flight.
