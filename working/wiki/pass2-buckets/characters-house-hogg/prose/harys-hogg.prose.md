## Origins

Harys was regarded as an upjumped [squire](wiki:Squire) in the writings of [Archmaester](wiki:Archmaester) [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn). In [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Harys became a favorite of the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) during the [great melee](wiki:Tourney_for_the_completion_of_the_Dragonpit) celebrating the completion of the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit). In [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Harys took part in a [tourney](wiki:Tourney) celebrating King [Jaehaerys I's](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) tenth year on the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), and was unhorsed by the young Ser [Ryam Redwyne](wiki:Ryam_Redwyne) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Harys_Hogg.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Appearances & Description

Harys wore a pig's head helm at [tourneys](wiki:Tourney), which along with his House name, contributed to people calling him "Harry the Ham".(wiki:Harys_Hogg.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
