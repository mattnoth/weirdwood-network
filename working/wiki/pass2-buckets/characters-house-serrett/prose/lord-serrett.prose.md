## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord Serrett is part of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s army invading the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), riding alongside Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) in the center of Tywin's army during the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:Lord_Serrett.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) When Tywin makes his son, [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Lord Serrett is among the alternatives that Tyrion suggests.(wiki:Lord_Serrett.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
