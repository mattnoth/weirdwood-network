## Origins

Clayton was a [hedge knight](wiki:Hedge_knight)(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) of [lowborn](wiki:Lowborn) origin,(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_clayton_suggs.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) hailing from [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). According to Ser [Justin Massey](wiki:Justin_Massey), Clayton gambled with [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone)'s [torturers](wiki:Torture), and aided them in the questioning of prisoners, especially young women.(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)

## Appearances & Description

Clayton is a short, burly man, with a balding head(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-TWOW_3-1) and a mouthful of broken brown teeth.(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He has pig eyes, and blackheads on his nose.(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Clayton has a deep voice.(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) He is older than Lord [Harwood Fell](wiki:Harwood_Fell).(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Clayton is considered a crony(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) and the strong right hand of Ser [Godry Farring](wiki:Godry_Farring).(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) He is strong and courageous, but also crude and brutish.(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Clayton is cruel and enjoys [torturing](wiki:Torture), and his love for the [Lord of Light](wiki:R'hllor) may only be love of human sacrifice.(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Ser Clayton is sworn to King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon). He is among the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) who goes north to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) with Stannis.(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Clayton is part of Stannis's honor guard when [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) pass through the [Wall](wiki:Wall) to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) after the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall).(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) During Stannis's war council, Clayton states that Houses [Hornwood](wiki:House_Hornwood), [Cerwyn](wiki:House_Cerwyn), [Tallhart](wiki:House_Tallhart), [Ryswell](wiki:House_Ryswell) and [Dustin](wiki:House_Dustin) are all [traitors](wiki:Traitor) and lapdogs of the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister). When Stannis dismisses his men during a meeting with [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), the [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), Clayton mutters something to [Harwood Fell](wiki:Harwood_Fell) that makes him laugh.(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

When Stannis's army is slowed during the [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell), Clayton agrees with Ser [Godry Farring](wiki:Godry_Farring) they should burn someone to appease [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor) and end the blizzard, but the king refuses.(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) fears Clayton will burn her because of her king's blood, as her father [Balon](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) was [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North).(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5)

When four [Peasebury](wiki:House_Peasebury) men are caught committing [cannibalism](wiki:Cannibalism) at a [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village), they are to be burned alive as a sacrifice to R'hllor. Desiring a quick death, their serjeant insults the queen's men. The serjeant calls Clayton a "bleeding [bastard](wiki:Bastard)", goading him into quickly killing the condemned man with a knife.(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-6) Clayton insults Asha after the sacrifices are burned, but he is interrupted by [Alysane Mormont](wiki:Alysane_Mormont) and Ser [Justin Massey](wiki:Justin_Massey).(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-7)

When Asha later reminds Clayton that Stannis [gelds](wiki:Eunuch) men for rape, he laughs and says he would sooner see her burn.(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-8) When they hear riders approaching the village, Clayton tells Asha to run and warn the king, as he assumes it is Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton)'s host upon them. However, the riders turn out to be [Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris) of the [Iron Bank of Braavos](wiki:Iron_Bank_of_Braavos), and his escort, some of whom are Asha's [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) men from [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte).

### The Winds of Winter

Clayton is present along with Ser [Godry Farring](wiki:Godry_Farring) and Ser [Richard Horpe](wiki:Richard_Horpe) when Stannis interrogates Maester [Tybald](wiki:Tybald) about his loyalties. Stannis has Clayton threaten violence on the [maester](wiki:Maester) to make Tybald tell him everything. Clayton wants Stannis to burn [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy).(wiki:Clayton_Suggs.cite_ref-TWOW_3-2)
