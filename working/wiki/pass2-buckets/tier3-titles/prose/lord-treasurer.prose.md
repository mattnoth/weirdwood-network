## Origins

Lord [Edwell Celtigar](wiki:Edwell_Celtigar) was named master of coin and lord treasurer by Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) during the regency of Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) for King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord treasurer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
The [Pentoshi](wiki:Pentoshi) [Rego Draz](wiki:Rego_Draz) was named master of coin and lord treasurer by King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.(wiki:Lord treasurer.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Lord [Lyman Beesbury](wiki:Lyman_Beesbury), [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin) for both [Jaehaerys I](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and [Viserys I](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen), also held the title of lord treasurer.(wiki:Lord treasurer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lady [Alyse Ladybright](wiki:Alyse_Ladybright) serves as the lord treasurer at [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear).(wiki:Lord treasurer.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), leaves Alyse in charge of the accounts and finances of Sunspear when he travels to the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens).(wiki:Lord treasurer.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), the [Queen Regent](wiki:Regent) for King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), renames the office of [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin) as "lord treasurer" in the style of the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities), and she grants the post to Lord [Gyles Rosby](wiki:Gyles_Rosby).(wiki:Lord treasurer.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Gyles serves until his death and is then replaced by Ser [Harys Swyft](wiki:Harys_Swyft), who until that point had served as Tommen's [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:Lord treasurer.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), the new Hand of the King, wants to replace Harys with his uncle [Garth Tyrell](wiki:Garth_Tyrell), the [Lord Seneschal of Highgarden](wiki:Lord_Seneschal_of_Highgarden).(wiki:Lord treasurer.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
