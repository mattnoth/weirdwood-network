### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Around half of the Iron Fleet contingent dispatched to Slaver's Bay is missing after violent storms beset it east of the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones). The Lord Quellon is one of the ships that make it to the rendez-vous point off the southern tip of the [Isle of Cedars](wiki:Isle_of_Cedars).(wiki:Lord Quellon.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
