## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord [Meldred Merlyn](wiki:Meldred_Merlyn) is blessed with seawater by [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy), and he is among the first to hear Aeron call for a [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot).(wiki:Lord of Pebbleton.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) He attends the kingsmoot at [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk), where he is among the supporters of [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy).(wiki:Lord of Pebbleton.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
