## Origins

Lord [Owen Costayne](wiki:Owen_Costayne) declared for the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). He was killed by [Bold Jon Roxton](wiki:Jon_Roxton) at the [Battle of the Honeywine](wiki:Battle_of_the_Honeywine) in [130 AC](wiki:130_AC).(wiki:Lord of the Three Towers.cite_ref-Rfab_the_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
An unnamed [Lord Costayne](wiki:Lord_Costayne_(Daeron_II)) fought for [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) in the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), while his [son](wiki:Lord_Costayne_(Aerys_I)) and successor supported [Daemon II Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre) in the [second rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion).(wiki:Lord of the Three Towers.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
