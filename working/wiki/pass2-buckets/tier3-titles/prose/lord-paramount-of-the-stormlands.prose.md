## Origins

[Argilac Durrandon](wiki:Argilac_Durrandon), the last [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King), was killed by [Orys Baratheon](wiki:Orys_Baratheon) in the [Last Storm](wiki:Last_Storm) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest). [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) named Orys the Lord Paramount of the Stormlands and [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King). Argilac's seat, [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), was also granted to [House Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord Paramount of the Stormlands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
After [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) angered his younger brother [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) by giving him [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) and granting [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) to their youngest brother, [Renly](wiki:Renly_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord Paramount of the Stormlands.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Mortally wounded by a [boar](wiki:Boar), King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) dies and is succeeded by his supposed son, King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord Paramount of the Stormlands.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Robert's youngest brother [Renly](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End), flees [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) and is crowned king at [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden).(wiki:Lord Paramount of the Stormlands.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Renly is [assassinated](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings),(wiki:Lord Paramount of the Stormlands.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) takes [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) after the death of its [castellan](wiki:Castellan), Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose).(wiki:Lord Paramount of the Stormlands.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

King Joffrey chokes to death at his [royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding)(wiki:Lord Paramount of the Stormlands.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and is succeeded by his younger brother, King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord Paramount of the Stormlands.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Stannis has been attainted by the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Lord Paramount of the Stormlands.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
