## Origins

The [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) tasked Ser [Olyver Bracken](wiki:Olyver_Bracken) and Ser [Raymund Mallery](wiki:Raymund_Mallery) with restoring the crumbling [Rimegate](wiki:Rimegate) and Sable Hall, respectively, but the two former [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) rebelled from the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) in [50 AC](wiki:50_AC) with the intention to make the castles their seats as [lords](wiki:Lord). After loyal brothers of the Night's Watch recaptured Rimegate with the aid of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), Raymund and his followers fled from Sable Hall [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall). Lord [Walton Stark](wiki:Walton_Stark_(son_of_Brandon)) pursued the rebels into the haunted forest, but he was overwhelmed by [giants](wiki:Giants). A [wildling](wiki:Wildling) chieftain eventually delivered Raymund's head to [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea), claiming that his clan had eaten Raymund's men.(wiki:Sable Hall.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
