## Origins

Lord Smallwood died fighting in King [Arlan III Durrandon](wiki:Arlan_III_Durrandon)'s campaign against King [Humfrey I Teague](wiki:Humfrey_I_Teague), which included the [Battle of Six Kings](wiki:Battle_of_Six_Kings).(wiki:Lord of Acorn Hall.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
During the reign of King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen), Lord [Myles Smallwood](wiki:Myles_Smallwood) fought with Lord [Alyn Stokeworth](wiki:Alyn_Stokeworth) in his campaign against [Harren the Red](wiki:Harren_the_Red). During the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising), Lord Myles battled [Wat the Hewer](wiki:Wat_the_Hewer) in the [battle at Stonebridge](wiki:Battle_at_Stonebridge). He later sided with Prince [Aegon the Uncrowned](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) in the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye) against his uncle King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord of Acorn Hall.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death_and_betrayal_under_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
In [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), Lord Myles was raised to [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), favored for his loyalty and prowess in battle.(wiki:Lord of Acorn Hall.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death_and_betrayal_under_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Though he was renowned for his courage and undoubtedly well-intentioned, he was ill-suited to the office and was dismissed by Jaehaerys in [57 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Lord of Acorn Hall.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Lord of Acorn Hall.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord [Joseth Smallwood](wiki:Joseth_Smallwood) supported the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) and [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen). He briefly halted the [westermen](wiki:Westermen) host under Ser [Adrian Tarbeck](wiki:Adrian_Tarbeck) in a [battle](wiki:Battle_at_Acorn_Hall) near Acorn Hall.(wiki:Lord of Acorn Hall.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Lord Joseth later scraped together fresh levies for the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton).(wiki:Lord of Acorn Hall.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
In [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), Lord and Lady Smallwood traveled to [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) to take part in a [tourney](wiki:Whitewalls_tourney). [Daemon II Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre) issued commands to Lord Smallwood during the tourney, suggesting he was aware of the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion).(wiki:Lord of Acorn Hall.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
