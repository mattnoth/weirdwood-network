## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Following the hatching of her [dragons](wiki:Dragon)—[Drogon](wiki:Drogon), [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal), and [Viserion](wiki:Viserion)—[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) is known as Mother of Dragons by her [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)(wiki:Mother of Dragons.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) and the people of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth).(wiki:Mother of Dragons.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) [Quhuru Mo](wiki:Quhuru_Mo) refers to her as Dragonmother.(wiki:Mother of Dragons.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Daenerys uses the title when she meets with [Kraznys mo Nakloz](wiki:Kraznys_mo_Nakloz) in [Astapor](wiki:Astapor).(wiki:Mother of Dragons.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Daenerys uses Mother of Dragons as part of her style as [Queen of Meereen](wiki:Queen_of_Meereen).(wiki:Mother of Dragons.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
