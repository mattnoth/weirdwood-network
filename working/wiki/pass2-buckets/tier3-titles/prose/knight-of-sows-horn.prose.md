### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Jaime advises him to plant and pray for one last harvest.(wiki:Knight of Sow's Horn.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
