## Origins

A [Stane](wiki:First_Ranger_Stane) of [Skagos](wiki:Skagos) once briefly served as First Ranger.(wiki:First Ranger.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_north:_the_stoneborn_of_skagos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
[Benton Glover](wiki:Benton_Glover) was First Ranger when Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) visited the [Wall](wiki:Wall) in [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:First Ranger.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

With First Ranger [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark) missing during a ranging [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall), Ser [Jaremy Rykker](wiki:Jaremy_Rykker) commands the rangers in Benjen's absence.(wiki:First Ranger.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) However, Jaremy is slain by a [wight](wiki:Wight).(wiki:First Ranger.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Thoren Smallwood](wiki:Thoren_Smallwood) declares he is First Ranger, but Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) insists that Benjen will remain First Ranger until his fate is confirmed.(wiki:First Ranger.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Thoren is killed during the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist).(wiki:First Ranger.cite_ref-Rasos18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) [Ronnel Harclay](wiki:Ronnel_Harclay) commands the rangers after the death of Thoren's death.(wiki:First Ranger.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Black Jack Bulwer](wiki:Jack_Bulwer) is officially appointed as First Ranger,(wiki:First Ranger.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) but he is slain by the [Weeper](wiki:Weeper) while ranging north of the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:First Ranger.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
