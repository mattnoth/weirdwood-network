## Origins

According to Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), it has been "a thousand years" since [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) last had a king.(wiki:King of Meereen.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:King of Meereen.cite_ref-date_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

In [300 AC](wiki:300_AC), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) [takes Meereen](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen) from the [Great Masters](wiki:Great_Masters), becoming [the city](wiki:Meereen)'s new ruler.(wiki:King of Meereen.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Daenerys is introduced as "Queen of Meereen" as she holds court in the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid).(wiki:King of Meereen.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Daenerys weds [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq), through which he becomes Meereen's [king](wiki:King).(wiki:King of Meereen.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) After Daenerys disappears from [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit) on [Drogon](wiki:Drogon), Hizdahr rules over the city as King of Meereen.(wiki:King of Meereen.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
[Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) convinces Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) that Hizdahr played a part in the attempt on Daenerys's life.(wiki:King of Meereen.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Together with [Grey Worm](wiki:Grey_Worm), Barristan and Skahaz arrest Hizdahr and install a council to rule the city in Daenerys's absence.(wiki:King of Meereen.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
