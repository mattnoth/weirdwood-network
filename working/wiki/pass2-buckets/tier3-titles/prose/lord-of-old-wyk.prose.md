## Origins

Lord Drumm was killed by [westermen](wiki:Westerlands) during Lady [Johanna Lannister](wiki:Johanna_Lannister)'s invasion of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC).(wiki:Lord of Old Wyk.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord [Dunstan Drumm](wiki:Dunstan_Drumm) puts forth his name for consideration at the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot). His two sons, [Denys](wiki:Denys_Drumm) and [Donnel](wiki:Donnel_Drumm), as well as [Andrik the Unsmiling](wiki:Andrik_the_Unsmiling), a famed ironborn warrior in his service, champion him. [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) is proclaimed [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North) instead.(wiki:Lord of Old Wyk.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), Lord Dunstan along side with Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw) and Lord [Gorold Goodbrother](wiki:Gorold_Goodbrother) discuss their fears that the gains from the battle are little compared to the enmity of the [Reach](wiki:Reach) they have earned.(wiki:Lord of Old Wyk.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
