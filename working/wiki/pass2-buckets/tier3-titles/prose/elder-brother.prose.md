## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

A [septry in the riverlands](wiki:Burning_septry) is sacked repeatedly during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). Thinking its Elder Brother has hidden [gold](wiki:Gold), a [monstrous attacker](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) orders [his men](wiki:Mountain's_men) to kill the contemplative brothers one by one.(wiki:Elder Brother.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

A [septry](wiki:Septry) near [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) is attacked first by [northmen](wiki:Northmen) and then by [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). Its elder brother hides some [coins](wiki:Coin), and a [dwarf brother](wiki:Dwarf_brother) takes the coins after the rest of his brothers are killed.(wiki:Elder Brother.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) travels to the [Quiet Isle](wiki:Quiet_Isle) and meets [its Elder Brother](wiki:Elder_Brother_(Quiet_Isle)), a former [knight](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Elder Brother.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
