## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

The [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) led by King [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) capture [Southshield](wiki:Southshield) during the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields). Lord [Osbert Serry](wiki:Osbert_Serry) is reported to have fled the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands) for the safety of [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden).(wiki:Lord of Southshield.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Euron names [Andrik the Unsmiling](wiki:Andrik_the_Unsmiling) as the new Lord of Southshield.(wiki:Lord of Southshield.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
