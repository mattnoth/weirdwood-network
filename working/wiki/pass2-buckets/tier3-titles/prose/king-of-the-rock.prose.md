## Origins

The [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) trace their descent back to [Lann the Clever](wiki:Lann_the_Clever), a trickster from the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes) who allegedly tricked the [Casterlys](wiki:House_Casterly) out of [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:King of the Rock.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) There is no record of Lann calling himself king, although some have granted him the title posthumously. The first recorded King of the Rock was [Loreon the Lion](wiki:Loreon_I_Lannister), who added the lands of [House Reyne](wiki:House_Reyne) to his land and defeated [House Banefort](wiki:House_Banefort).(wiki:King of the Rock.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
While King [Tybolt Lannister](wiki:Tybolt_Lannister_(king)) defended against the initial [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to the westerlands, Kings [Tyrion III](wiki:Tyrion_III_Lannister) and [Gerold II](wiki:Gerold_II_Lannister) allowed the [Andals](wiki:Andals) to marry into westermen nobility. An Andal knight, Joffrey Lydden, married into the Lannisters and, as King [Joffrey Lannister](wiki:Joffrey_Lannister), was the first Andal King of the Rock.(wiki:King of the Rock.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
The Kings of the Rock expanded their realm with Andal support. King [Cerion](wiki:Cerion_Lannister) conquered the land near the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth), and King [Tommen I](wiki:Tommen_I_Lannister) added [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle) to the kingdom. Many Kings of the Rock, including [Lancel I](wiki:Lancel_I_Lannister), [Gerold the Great](wiki:Gerold_Lannister_(king)), and [Lancel IV](wiki:Lancel_IV_Lannister), warred with neighboring realms, including the [Kingdom of the Reach](wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Reach), the [ironborn kings](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands) of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands), the [river kings](wiki:River_kings) of the [Trident](wiki:Trident),(wiki:King of the Rock.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) and the [Kings of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:Kings_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers).(wiki:King of the Rock.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
The last King of the Rock was [Loren I](wiki:Loren_I_Lannister), who bent the knee to [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) after being defeated at the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire).(wiki:King of the Rock.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) While [House Gardener](wiki:House_Gardener) was extinguished and replaced with [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), the Lannisters remained in control of the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) and became [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen)'s [Wardens of the West](wiki:Wardens_of_the_West).(wiki:King of the Rock.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
