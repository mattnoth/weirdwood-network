## Origins

Since the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters) had declared [Marla Sunderland](wiki:Marla_Sunderland) their queen during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) tasked Lord [Torrhen Stark](wiki:Torrhen_Stark), his new [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), with ending the rebellion. Torrhen granted command of a northern army to Warrick, who sailed from [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) to the [Bite](wiki:Bite) on [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) galleys. The [Sistermen's Rebellion](wiki:Sistermen's_Rebellion) ended when [Sisterton](wiki:Sisterton) spotted Warrick's fleet and Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) on her [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar). Marla's younger brother, [Steffon](wiki:Steffon_Sunderland), became the new [Lord of the Three Sisters](wiki:Lord_of_the_Three_Sisters) and sent one of his sons to be fostered by the Manderlys at White Harbor for his good behavior.(wiki:Warrick_Manderly.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
