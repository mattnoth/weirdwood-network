## Origins

Ser Steffon died a few years before the [Defiance of Duskendale](wiki:Defiance_of_Duskendale) and therefore took no part in it. This played a large role in King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) sparing the life of young [Dontos](wiki:Dontos_Hollard) in the aftermath of the defiance.(wiki:Steffon_Hollard.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
