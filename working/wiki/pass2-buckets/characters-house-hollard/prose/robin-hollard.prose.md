## Origins

When King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) was a captive in the [Defiance of Duskendale](wiki:Defiance_of_Duskendale), Robin would dance around the king and pull on his beard. After the Defiance had been put down, Robin died [on the rack](wiki:Torture).(wiki:Robin_Hollard.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
