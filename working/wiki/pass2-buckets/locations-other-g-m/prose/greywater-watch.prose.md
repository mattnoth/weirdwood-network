## Origins

[Ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) and [Andal](wiki:Andal) warriors as well as [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) have attempted to conquer Greywater Watch, but none have been able to find it. Many of the invaders rode into bogs and sank because of their armor.(wiki:Greywater_Watch.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) has sent letters to his friend, Lord [Howland Reed](wiki:Howland_Reed), at Greywater over the years, but [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) cannot recall any [crannogmen](wiki:Crannogmen) visiting [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) in response.(wiki:Greywater_Watch.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) sends word to Lord [Howland Reed](wiki:Howland_Reed) at Greywater Watch to have the [crannogmen](wiki:Crannogmen) defend the [Neck](wiki:Neck) if Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) marches up the [causeway](wiki:Causeway).(wiki:Greywater_Watch.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Clash of Kings

At Greywater, [Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed) dreams of black waves of salt water overcoming [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Greywater_Watch.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) The youth also has a [green dream](wiki:Green_dream) of a winged wolf, so Howland sends his children to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) to aid [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark).(wiki:Greywater_Watch.cite_ref-Racok28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Jojen and his older sister, [Meera Reed](wiki:Meera_Reed), attend the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast) at Winterfell and pledge the faith of Greywater.(wiki:Greywater_Watch.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
