## Origins

Last Hearth was the royal seat of the [Umbers](wiki:House_Umber) when they were petty kings in the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes).(wiki:Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

In [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) flew on [Silverwing](wiki:Silverwing) to the Last Hearth and other local keeps, surprising and delighting [northern](wiki:North) lords.(wiki:Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

In the great [mêlée at Last Hearth](wiki:Mêlée_at_Last_Hearth) in [170 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), it is said that no fewer than eighteen men died, and half again that number were sorely maimed before the day was done.(wiki:Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord [Greatjon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber) and his son, the [Smalljon](wiki:Smalljon_Umber), lead most of the Umber fighting men south alongside [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark).(wiki:Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Clash of Kings

In the absence of its lord, Last Hearth is administered by joint castellans, [Mors Crowfood](wiki:Mors_Umber) and [Hother Whoresbane](wiki:Hother_Umber), uncles of the Greatjon. During the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), Mors and Hother request assistance from [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) because the Umbers lack the manpower to harvest their land and repel [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) encroaching from across the Bay of Seals. The castellans are tasked by Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) to build longships made of Umber lumber with [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor)'s assistance.(wiki:Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Bran and his companions consider stealing horses and riding to Last Hearth, but Bran decides they should find the [three-eyed crow](wiki:Three-eyed_crow) [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) instead.(wiki:Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Rasos9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sends a raven to Last Hearth in a plea for help to defend [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) from [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings).(wiki:Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Because Greatjon Umber is held prisoner at the [Twins](wiki:Twins) after the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Hother reluctantly supports Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) as [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North). Mors decides to support [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) as [King of Westeros](wiki:King_of_Westeros) if he is given the skull of [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) and if Hother is pardoned for supporting Roose.(wiki:Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) It is unknown who holds Last Hearth in the brothers' absence.

At [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), Ser [Godry Farring](wiki:Godry_Farring) suggests that Stannis intimidate the Umbers by killing Mors and burning Last Hearth, but [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) scoffs at the idea.(wiki:Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

### The Winds of Winter

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) tells Stannis that Mors took green boys to support Stannis, while Hother took greybeards to support Roose.(wiki:Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btheon_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctheon.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
