## Origins

Lord Blacktyde died during the [Greyjoy Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy_Rebellion), fighting for [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy). After the rebellion was over, [his son](wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde) was made a hostage to ensure the Blacktydes' loyalty to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), and taken to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown).(wiki:Lord_Blacktyde_(father_of_Baelor).cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
