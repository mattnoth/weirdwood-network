## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Torrhen accompanies his father and his two brothers [Harrion](wiki:Harrion_Karstark) and [Eddard](wiki:Eddard_Karstark) to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) when [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls his banners.(wiki:Torrhen_Karstark.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He is a member of [Robb's personal guard](wiki:Robb's_personal_guard) during the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood). Torrhen and Eddard are both slain by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) during the battle.(wiki:Torrhen_Karstark.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
