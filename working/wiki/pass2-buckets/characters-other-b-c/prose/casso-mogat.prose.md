## Appearances & Description

Casso is only five feet tall. He is very hairy, and [dyes](wiki:Hair_dye) his hair and whiskers a mossy green.(wiki:Casso_Mogat.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) He may be sterile.(wiki:Casso_Mogat.cite_ref-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Casso takes [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) from Sisterton to [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor). When Davos disembarks at White Harbor, Casso tells him that the *Midwife* could stay for three days. Davos replies that if things go well he could be back in one day. When Casso asks if things do not go well, Davos replies that then he need not wait.(wiki:Casso_Mogat.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Casso waits four days before leaving.(wiki:Casso_Mogat.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
