## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Six [gold cloaks](wiki:Gold_cloaks) search for [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) at a village in the [crownlands](wiki:Crownlands). One of them, a big lout with a broken nose, bares his steel but is challenged by a number of [Yoren](wiki:Yoren)'s recruits. Broken Nose laughs at [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) when she enters a [water dancer](wiki:Water_dance)'s pose with [Needle](wiki:Needle). Yoren disarms the officer leading the gold cloaks and sends them into retreat down the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad).(wiki:Broken_Nose.cite_ref-Racok5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
