## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) is sent to work for Brusco the fishmonger who sells his stock below the wharves of the Drowned Town. She is sent to him to learn the Braavosi tongue.(wiki:Brusco.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Brusco arrives at the fishmarket with Arya in tow, going through the daily catch and choosing which to buy. While Brusco sleeps, his daughter Brea visits with a boy on the rooftop.(wiki:Brusco.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) returns to work for Brusco, after having been training at the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White), as if she had never been away in order to carry out an assassination.(wiki:Brusco.cite_ref-Radwd64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
