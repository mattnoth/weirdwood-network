## Appearances & Description

Byan is a small man with weather-beaten skin, and he wears a splendid [blue-dyed](wiki:Hair_dye) mustache extending from one ear to the other.(wiki:Byan_Votyris.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Byan_Votyris.cite_ref-2)

Byan's caravan is composed of four hundred [horses](wiki:Horse).(wiki:Byan_Votyris.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Byan's caravan travelled from [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) to [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak) by way of [Norvos](wiki:Norvos) and [Qohor](wiki:Qohor),(wiki:Byan_Votyris.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) just like Princess [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) did with *Khal* [Drogo](wiki:Drogo)'s *[Khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)*.(wiki:Byan_Votyris.cite_ref-Rtloiafjourneys.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Once arrived, they encampe in the Western Market. Byan is the bearer of a letter from Magister [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) addressed to Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont). Daenerys is trading in the market when she is nearly [poisoned](wiki:Poison) by a [wine trader](wiki:Wine_merchant) of the caravan. Aghast, Byan gives the poisoner into the custody of the princess's [guards](wiki:Ko) and his goods to Daenerys as a token of regret.(wiki:Byan_Votyris.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
