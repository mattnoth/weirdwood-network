## Appearances & Description

She has fine dark hair and heavy breasts. She is a shade plump for [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy)'s taste. Her skin is as splotchy as oatmeal. Theon thinks she looks stupid when she smiles.(wiki:Captain's_daughter.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

During his journey from [Seagard](wiki:Seagard) to [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport), [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) sails on the *Myraham*. After she willingly comes to him, Theon sleeps with the captain's daughter for the remainder of the voyage. The captain disapproves of this, but doesn't dare to speak out against Theon. The girl was a [maiden](wiki:Maiden) the first time Theon bedded her, which surprised him due to her age. The girl falls in love with Theon, and offers to become his [salt wife](wiki:Salt_wife), an offer Theon refuses. He tells her that her place is on the *Myraham*, and his on [Pyke](wiki:Pyke). When she tells him that her father will call her names and hurt her for having been with Theon, he tells her that he bedded her so often, that there's a chance she's pregnant, and that her father should be pleased; not every man has the honor of raising a king's [bastard](wiki:Bastard). Theon then leaves her.(wiki:Captain's_daughter.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

As Theon is disembarking, the captain's daughter brings his pack of clothing, and to Theon's mortification tries to embrace him, but he turns deftly aside. When she tells him that she loves him, Theon replies that he must go, and hurries after [his uncle](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy).(wiki:Captain's_daughter.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

The *Myraham* is forbidden to set sail again by Lord [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), and remains in Lordsport. The captain's daughter is forbidden to leave the ship, and wanders the deck of the ship forlornly during the time the ship remains. When Theon Greyjoy walks past, she calls out to him, but he ignores her.(wiki:Captain's_daughter.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
