## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

After the deaths of her father, [Helman](wiki:Helman_Tallhart), and elder brother, [Benfred](wiki:Benfred_Tallhart), Eddara becomes the Lady of [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Torrhen's_Square).(wiki:Eddara_Tallhart.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Eddara is held captive by the [ironmen](wiki:Ironmen) at Torrhen's Square, following its [capture](wiki:Sack_of_Torrhen’s_Square) by [Dagmer](wiki:Dagmer).(wiki:Eddara_Tallhart.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
