## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

He is five feet tall, and mocked by the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown) as the "Little Pigeon". His [slave](wiki:Slave) soldiers range from seven to almost eight feet tall and also wear stilts built into their ornate armor. Their armor consists of pink-enameled scales on their torsos, and elongated helms with pointed steel beaks and crests of pink feathers, which earn them the nickname the [Herons](wiki:Herons) by the Windblown. He was present at the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor).(wiki:Paezhar_zo_Myraq.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Paezhar is among the seven commanders who enter Meereen to sign the peace and witness the opening of the fighting pits. During the feast held in the Great Pyramid to celebrate the signing of the peace.(wiki:Paezhar_zo_Myraq.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
