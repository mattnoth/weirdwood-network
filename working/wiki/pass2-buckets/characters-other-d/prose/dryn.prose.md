## Appearances & Description

Dryn is a chunky boy, with short legs, thick arms, a wide red face, and a shock of dark brown hair. He looks like a miniature version of his father.(wiki:Dryn.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

When [Longspear Ryk](wiki:Ryk) comes to 'steal' [Munda](wiki:Munda), [Dormund](wiki:Dormund) and Dryn give Ryk a fight to defend their sister's honor.(wiki:Dryn.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Tormund](wiki:Tormund) and over three thousand [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) declare a truce with the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). Dryn is with his father Tormund, his brother [Toregg](wiki:Toregg), and [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) as the wildlings pass the [Wall](wiki:Wall) at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black). Tormund gives Dryn to Jon as a hostage. Jon, the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), promises that Dryn will serve as his own [page](wiki:Page).(wiki:Dryn.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
