## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Dornish Dilly is among the men that [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) leaves behind to defend [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black). He dies during the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) when a [Thenn](wiki:Thenns) stabs him between the shoulder blades with a spear after he slips.(wiki:Dornish_Dilly.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
