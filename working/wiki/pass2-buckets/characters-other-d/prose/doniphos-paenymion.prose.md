## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Doniphos, like the other two triarchs, is up for re-election and many believe he is unlikely to be re-elected. Doniphos is one of the few who does not wish war between [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) and [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), Queen of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). According to [Qavo Nogarys](wiki:Qavo_Nogarys), Doniphos will not be returned as triarch as the city of Volantis thirsts for war.(wiki:Doniphos_Paenymion.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Possible candidates for his replacement are [Alios Qhaedar](wiki:Alios_Qhaedar), [Parquello Vaelaros](wiki:Parquello_Vaelaros), and [Belicho Staegone](wiki:Belicho_Staegone).(wiki:Doniphos_Paenymion.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
