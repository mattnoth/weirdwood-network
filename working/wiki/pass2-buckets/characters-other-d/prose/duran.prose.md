## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) purchases [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) from the [Good Masters](wiki:Good_Masters) of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor). After freeing the soldiers, she turns them against the [slavers](wiki:Slavers) with the [fall of Astapor](wiki:Fall_of_Astapor).(wiki:Duran.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

As Daenerys told the Unsullied to abolish the practice of choosing a new name every day,(wiki:Duran.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Duran presumably chose to go back to his birth name.

[Grey Worm](wiki:Grey_Worm) informs Daenerys that Duran, while on patrol with another Unsullied named [Mossador](wiki:Mossador), was ambushed and killed. Both were crushed by falling stones beneath the river wall of the [Skahazadhan](wiki:Skahazadhan), dropped on them by the [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy).(wiki:Duran.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
