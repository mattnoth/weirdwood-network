## Origins

During the Faith Militant uprising, Lord Jon took up arms on behalf of the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aenys_I)) and the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) against the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). He fought in the [battle at the Great Fork](wiki:Battle_at_the_Great_Fork) alongside Lord [Rupert Falwell](wiki:Rupert_Falwell) and other puissant [knights](wiki:Knight) from the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) and [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), as well as hundreds of [Poor Fellows](wiki:Poor_Fellows), against the royal army led by King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) himself.(wiki:Jon_Lychester.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) It is unknown if he survived.
