## Narrative Arc

Aware that [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) is trying to rid herself of him, [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) argues that his place is beside the king, not off laying siege to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun). But his sister responds that she can't rely on [Daven](wiki:Daven_Lannister), and needs Jaime to defeat [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully), as well as find out why [Gregor's men](wiki:The_Mountain's_men) at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) have not released [Wylis Manderly](wiki:Wylis_Manderly) as the crown requested. She states that [Osmund Kettleblack](wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack) will command the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) in his absence, although Jaime dislikes the idea.

Jaime's command consists of [Kennos](wiki:Kennos_of_Kayce), [Dermot](wiki:Dermot), [Strongboar](wiki:Lyle_Crakehall), [Red Ronnet](wiki:Ronnet_Connington), as well as [Ilyn Payne](wiki:Ilyn_Payne) and [Addam Marbrand](wiki:Addam_Marbrand), whom Jaime demanded as concessions from his sister.

On the first night, they guest at Castle [Hayford](wiki:Hayford), and Jaime considers the fate of his cousin [Tyrek](wiki:Tyrek_Lannister), who would be lord of the castle were he not missing. He thinks it possible the boy is still alive, perhaps having been secreted off by command of [Varys](wiki:Varys), who very well could have known that the riot would occur.

Starting that night, Ser Jaime begins training every night with Ilyn Payne, in the hopes that he can become a proficient swordsman with his left hand. The company also visits [Sow's Horn](wiki:Sow's_Horn) and meets with Ser [Roger Hogg](wiki:Roger_Hogg).

Arriving at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Jaime has Gregor Clegane's men release all prisoners, including Wylis Manderly, and then leaves Ser [Bonifer Hasty](wiki:Bonifer_Hasty) and the [Holy Hundred](wiki:Holy_Hundred) to hold the castle until [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) takes up its lordship. Jaime commands Bonifer to kill [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) or [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr) if either are captured, but requests he send him any [Bloody Mummers](wiki:Bloody_Mummers) or [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) should they fall into his hands.

Outside, Jaime comes across [Ronnet Connington](wiki:Ronnet_Connington), who mentions how he once was betrothed to [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth). When Ronnet mocks Brienne, Jaime hits him in the face with his golden hand and insists he call her by name.
