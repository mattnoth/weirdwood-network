## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Lord Donnor is a bannerman of [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Donnor_Saltcliffe.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Balon declares himself [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Donnor_Saltcliffe.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Donnor is a bannerman of [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy),(wiki:Donnor_Saltcliffe.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) who was chosen as Balon's successor at the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Donnor_Saltcliffe.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
