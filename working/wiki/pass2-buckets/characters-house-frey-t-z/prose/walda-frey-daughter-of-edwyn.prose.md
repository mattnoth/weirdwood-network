## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

On his way to [Oldstones](wiki:Oldstones) to ransom his nephew [Petyr Frey](wiki:Petyr_Frey) from the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey) thinks that Petyr may be [Lord of the Crossing](wiki:Lord_of_the_Crossing) one day, as Petyr's older brother [Edwyn](wiki:Edwyn_Frey) has no sons, and his other brother [Black Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Ryman)) only has [bastards](wiki:Bastard). However, when Merrett arrives at the rendezvous, he is shown Petyr's hanged body, and is himself hanged soon afterwards.(wiki:Walda_Frey_(daughter_of_Edwyn).cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Merrett also thinks that it is common knowledge in the [Twins](wiki:Twins) that Black Walder has slept with Edwyn's wife and Walda's mother, [Janyce Hunter](wiki:Janyce_Hunter), among others.(wiki:Walda_Frey_(daughter_of_Edwyn).cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Black Walder's illicit relationships may cast doubt over the paternity of many of the younger Freys, including Walda.
