## Identity

The Assault on Dragonstone was a naval assault in 284 AC at the end of Robert's Rebellion, in which Stannis Baratheon led the royal fleet to seize the Targaryen island fortress of Dragonstone. (wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone)

## Origins

After Robert Baratheon won the Iron Throne at the Battle of the Trident and the Sack of King's Landing, the remaining Targaryen loyalists held Dragonstone. Queen Rhaella Targaryen and the young Prince Viserys had fled there. Robert ordered Stannis to build a fleet and take the island. (wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone)

## Narrative Arc

Stannis Baratheon constructed a fleet and sailed against Dragonstone. A great storm struck the island the night before the assault — the same storm in which Daenerys Targaryen was born, earning her the epithet "Stormborn." The Targaryen fleet was destroyed by the storm. Before Stannis's forces landed, loyalist retainers smuggled the infant Daenerys and young Viserys across the narrow sea to the Free Cities. Stannis took Dragonstone with little resistance after the storm had broken the garrison's strength. Robert was furious that the Targaryen children had escaped. Robert granted Dragonstone to Stannis rather than the more prestigious Storm's End, which went to the younger Renly — a slight Stannis never forgave. (wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone) (agot-catelyn-01)
