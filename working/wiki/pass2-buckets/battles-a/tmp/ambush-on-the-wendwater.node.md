## Identity

The Ambush on the Wendwater was a skirmish during Aegon's Conquest in 2 BC, in which stormlander lords ambushed a Targaryen host marching through the kingswood near the Wendwater river. (wiki:Ambush_on_the_Wendwater)

## Origins

After Aegon's initial victories in the crownlands, stormlords loyal to Argilac the Arrogant, the last Storm King, harried the Targaryen forces as they advanced southward. Houses Fell, Buckler, and Errol organized the ambush. (wiki:Ambush_on_the_Wendwater)

## Narrative Arc

The stormlander forces under Lords Fell, Buckler, and Errol set an ambush along the Wendwater for the advancing Targaryen army led by Orys Baratheon. The engagement was inconclusive — the ambushers inflicted casualties but failed to destroy the Targaryen column, and the Targaryen host continued its march toward Storm's End. The skirmish was one of several guerrilla actions by stormlands lords attempting to slow the Conquest's advance. (wiki:Ambush_on_the_Wendwater)
