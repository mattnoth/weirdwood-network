## Identity

The Assault on Harrenhal was a military engagement during the Dance of the Dragons in 129 AC, in which Prince Daemon Targaryen seized Harrenhal for the black faction. (wiki:Assault_on_Harrenhal)

## Origins

At the outbreak of the Dance, Harrenhal was held by Ser Simon Strong, castellan for the imprisoned Lord Larys Strong. The blacks needed the castle as a strategic strongpoint in the riverlands to anchor their position against the greens in King's Landing. (wiki:Assault_on_Harrenhal)

## Narrative Arc

Prince Daemon Targaryen flew to Harrenhal atop his dragon Caraxes. The garrison, faced with a dragon, yielded the castle without significant resistance. Ser Simon Strong surrendered and Daemon took control of the fortress, establishing it as the blacks' base of operations in the riverlands. Daemon used Harrenhal as a rallying point for riverlords loyal to Queen Rhaenyra. The seizure gave the blacks a critical strategic position between King's Landing and the loyal riverlands houses. Daemon would later abandon Harrenhal voluntarily as part of a strategic maneuver before Aemond's march. (wiki:Assault_on_Harrenhal)
