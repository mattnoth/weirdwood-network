## Identity

The Arrest of Eddard Stark was a pivotal event in 298 AC at the Red Keep in King's Landing, in which Lord Eddard Stark, Hand of the King, was seized by Lannister forces after attempting to depose King Joffrey Baratheon. The event is a key trigger of the War of the Five Kings. (wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark)

## Origins

After the death of King Robert I Baratheon, Eddard Stark attempted to enforce Robert's will naming him Lord Protector and to reveal the truth that Joffrey, Myrcella, and Tommen were not Robert's children but born of Cersei Lannister's incest with her brother Jaime. Stark expected the City Watch, commanded by Janos Slynt, to support him based on assurances from Petyr Baelish. (wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark)

## Narrative Arc

Eddard confronted Cersei in the throne room, presenting Robert's letter naming him Protector of the Realm. Cersei tore the letter and ordered Eddard seized. When the City Watch turned their gold cloaks against Stark's household guard rather than supporting him, Eddard's men were killed or captured. Littlefinger's betrayal was the decisive factor — he had promised Stark the gold cloaks' loyalty but secretly aligned with the Lannisters. Stark's household guard, including Jory Cassel's surviving men, were slaughtered. Eddard was imprisoned and later publicly executed on the steps of the Great Sept of Baelor by order of King Joffrey, directly sparking Robb Stark's rebellion and the broader War of the Five Kings. (wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark)
