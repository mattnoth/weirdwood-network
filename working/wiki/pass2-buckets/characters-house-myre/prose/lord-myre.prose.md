## Narrative Arc

### A Feast For Crows

Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw) summons his banners to support the claim of his niece, [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), to the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair). The Myres are divided in their answer as some Myres have answered Rodrik's call and came to [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers), whilst [Pinchface Jon Myre](wiki:Jon_Myre) sailed to [Pyke](wiki:Pyke) with a dozen longships to support [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy).(wiki:Lord_Myre.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) It is unknown which claimant Lord Myre supports.
