## Origins

Dale was married. He and his wife were praying for a child.(wiki:Dale_Seaworth.cite_ref-Rasos5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Appearances & Description

Dale has a square plain face, forthright brown eyes, and thin brown flyaway hair.(wiki:Dale_Seaworth.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) When Dale was an adolescent, he had a peach-fuzz "beard", which he was proud of.(wiki:Dale_Seaworth.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

During [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre)'s burning of the [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) [sept](wiki:Sept)'s statues of the [Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), [Allard Seaworth](wiki:Allard_Seaworth) quietly expresses his disapproval, and his brother Dale agrees with him. King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) orders Dale to distribute a letter accusing Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) of an incestuous relationship with her brother, Ser [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), denying the legitimacy of her children and asserting Stannis's claim to the throne of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms). Dale is sent to travel south in *[Wraith](wiki:Wraith)*, past [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath) and the [Broken Arm](wiki:Broken_Arm), all along the coast of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) as far as the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor).(wiki:Dale_Seaworth.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Planning for the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s admiral, [Lord High Captain](wiki:Lord_High_Captain) Ser [Imry Florent](wiki:Imry_Florent), assigns Davos Seaworth and his sons' ships a place in the second line of battle, on the dangerous starboard wing. While Allard voices his belief that it is a place of honor, Davos states it is a place of peril. Dale and his other sons look pityingly at him for his nervousness.(wiki:Dale_Seaworth.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Dale battles on *Wraith* during the Battle of the Blackwater, fighting in the naval combat on the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush). During the fight, the *Wraith* is caught in an explosion of [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire), destroying it and killing Dale.(wiki:Dale_Seaworth.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)(wiki:Dale_Seaworth.cite_ref-Rasos5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Storm of Swords

Davos, a survivor of the Battle of the Blackwater, remembers his four sons who died during the battle. He thinks that Dale and his wife will never have the child they had prayed for.(wiki:Dale_Seaworth.cite_ref-Rasos5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Davos asks [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan) if *Wraith* had managed to escape the destruction of the battle. Salladhor tells him no, and comforts him, saying Dale was a good man.(wiki:Dale_Seaworth.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Davos tells Stannis he planned to murder [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) because he believed she sent his sons to the flames, but Stannis tells him the fires were not her work.(wiki:Dale_Seaworth.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

When Davos looks at his eleven-year-old son [Devan](wiki:Devan_Seaworth), he thinks how much he looked like Dale at that age.(wiki:Dale_Seaworth.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
