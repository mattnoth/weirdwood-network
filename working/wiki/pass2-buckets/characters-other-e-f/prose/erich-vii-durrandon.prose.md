## Origins

Erich took little note of the invaders, declaring he had no interest in "the quarrels of strangers in a land far away", as he was embroiled in his own war to reconquer [Massey's Hook](wiki:Massey's_Hook) from pirate king [Justin Milk-Eye](wiki:Justin_Massey_(Milk-Eye)) and whilst fending off incursions of King [Olyvar Yronwood](wiki:Olyvar_Yronwood). He died while the Andals conquered the [Vale](wiki:Vale).
