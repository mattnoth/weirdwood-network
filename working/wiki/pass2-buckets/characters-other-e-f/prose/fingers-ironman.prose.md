## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Fingers sails to [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) with Asha Greyjoy as she contemplates her next move after the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk). He is captured along with Asha, [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley), and several [other ironborn](wiki:Ragged_nine) during the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte) by the army of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Fingers_(ironman).cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Fingers is later freed and ransomed along with the other surviving ironborn by [Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris). The [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) banker needs an escort to get him to Stannis, whose army is [marching](wiki:March_on_Winterfell) on [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Fingers_(ironman).cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
