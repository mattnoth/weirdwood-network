## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

The settlement where Eroeh lives is attacked by the *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* of Khal [Ogo](wiki:Ogo). As the attack is ongoing, the *khalasar* of Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) falls upon them. Eroeh is captured during the raid. [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), Drogo's *[khaleesi](wiki:Khaleesi)*, comes across Eroeh as she is being raped by several men waiting their turn, and Daenerys claims the girl to protect her. The rapers laugh when [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) intervenes, and the young guard beheads a raper who shouts back at him. Most of the rapers angrily depart, with the exception of the man already atop Eroeh, the second man to have raped her. Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) pulls the raper from atop Eroeh, and [Aggo](wiki:Aggo) then kills him with an arrow. Daenerys has [Doreah](wiki:Doreah) treat Eroeh's wounds.(wiki:Eroeh.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

[Mago](wiki:Mago), one of the rapists, complains to Drogo that Daenerys took a girl from him as a spoil of war. Drogo defends his wife's claiming of Eroeh and orders Mago to look elsewhere.(wiki:Eroeh.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

When Drogo falls ill and falls from his horse, Eroeh helps Daenerys and her handmaidens to care for Drogo. When she sees Drogo's condition she whispers that the *khal* will die, for which Daenerys slaps her. When Jorah arrives at the tent, Eroeh is sent away.(wiki:Eroeh.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Eroeh is later present when [Mirri Maz Duur](wiki:Mirri_Maz_Duur) prepares to treat the dying Drogo, but she flees sobbing from the tent.(wiki:Eroeh.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

When Drogo dies, his horde breaks into several smaller *khalasars*. Eroeh is seized by Mago, now a [bloodrider](wiki:Bloodrider) to the new Khal [Jhaqo](wiki:Jhaqo). After Mago is finished with Eroeh, he gifts her to his *khal*, who gives her to his other six bloodriders. After they finish raping her, they cut her throat. Daenerys swears that she will avenge the girl.(wiki:Eroeh.cite_ref-Ragot68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords

The day before she is set to exchange her black [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Drogon](wiki:Drogon), for eight thousand six hundred [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) in [Astapor](wiki:Astapor), Daenerys asks Jorah if he can still recall Eroeh.(wiki:Eroeh.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Daenerys recalls Eroeh again when she learns that the new Astapori king, [Cleon](wiki:Cleon), has begun training new [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied), even though Daenerys had ended the slave trade with the [fall of Astapor](wiki:Fall_of_Astapor). She compares the situation to Eroeh.(wiki:Eroeh.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

When Daenerys learns that the forces from [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) have taken Astapor, she compares the city once more to Eroeh, whom she had attempted to save from rape and death, only to have her suffer a much worse fate later on. Daenerys states to her councilors that she made "ten thousand Eroehs" in Astapor.(wiki:Eroeh.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

The day before [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit) is to open, Daenerys recalls the innocents who have died in her service, including Eroeh, in order to justify to herself that she has made the right decision by allowing a few to voluntarily risk their lives in the [fighting pits of Meereen](wiki:Fighting_pits_of_Meereen), than to have thousands die in war and conflict.(wiki:Eroeh.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
