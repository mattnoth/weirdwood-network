## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Ezzara treats the first victim of the [bloody flux](wiki:Bloody_flux) at [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), although the man dies within an hour of arrival. Ezzara accompanies [Galazza Galare](wiki:Galazza_Galare), the [Green Grace](wiki:Green_Grace), along with two other [Blue Graces](wiki:Blue_Graces), to the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) of Meereen to explain to Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) the symptoms of the disease and its possible spread in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Ezzara.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
