## Narrative Arc

### A Feast For Crows

Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw) summons his banners to support the claim of his niece, [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), to the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair). Lord Stonetree answers the call and comes to [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers).(wiki:Lord_Stonetree.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) later sees several Stonetrees at the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot),(wiki:Lord_Stonetree.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) including [Sigfry Stonetree](wiki:Sigfry_Stonetree).(wiki:Lord_Stonetree.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
