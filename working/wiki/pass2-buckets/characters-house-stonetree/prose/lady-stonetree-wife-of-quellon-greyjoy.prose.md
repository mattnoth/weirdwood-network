## Origins

Lady Stonetree was wed by a [priest](wiki:Priest_of_the_Drowned_God) of the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God) to Lord [Quellon Greyjoy](wiki:Quellon_Greyjoy).(wiki:Lady_Stonetree_(wife_of_Quellon_Greyjoy).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

She was the mother of [Harlon](wiki:Harlon_Greyjoy), [Quenton](wiki:Quenton_Greyjoy) and [Donel Greyjoy](wiki:Donel_Greyjoy). After her death, Lord Quellon took a new rock wife, [Lady Sunderly](wiki:Lady_Sunderly_(wife_of_Quellon_Greyjoy))(wiki:Lady_Stonetree_(wife_of_Quellon_Greyjoy).cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
