## Origins

Lord Estermont's daughter was [Cassana Estermont](wiki:Cassana_Estermont), who married [Steffon Baratheon](wiki:Steffon_Baratheon), [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End). Lord Estermont is thus grandfather to [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), the [King of the Andals, the Rhoynar, and the First Men](wiki:King_of_the_Andals,_the_Rhoynar,_and_the_First_Men); [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), the [Lord of Dragonstone](wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone); and [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), the Lord of Storm's End.(wiki:Lord_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Lord_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)(wiki:Lord_Estermont.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Appearances & Description

Lord Estermont is older than Lord [Ardrian Celtigar](wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar);(wiki:Lord_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) finds him to be an ancient, old man.(wiki:Lord_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

The cautious Lord Estermont follows the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:Lord_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose) considers Lord Estermont to be a great lord.(wiki:Lord_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) recognizes Lord Estermont's standard in the camp of [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) when she arrives at the [melee at Bitterbridge](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge) as an envoy for her son, [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark).(wiki:Lord_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Renly gives command of the reserve to Lord Estermont in the coming battle at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) against his elder brother, [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Renly is assassinated by a [shadow](wiki:Shadow_child) before the battle begins, however.(wiki:Lord_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Lord Estermont joins Stannis's cause after Renly's death. He dislikes [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre), the [red priestess](wiki:Red_priest) now in Stannis's service, and he prefers to fight under the crowned stag of [House Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon) instead of the flaming heart of [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor).(wiki:Lord_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) Lord Estermont approves of the fate of Storm's End being decided by single combat between one of Stannis's followers and Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose), the [castellan](wiki:Castellan) of Storm's End who continues to hold the castle in Renly's name, but Stannis refuses the suggestion. Stannis thinks that Lord Estermont would advise him to starve out Cortnay's garrison, which could take a year.(wiki:Lord_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6) Melisandre instead sends a shadow to kill Cortnay,(wiki:Lord_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7) and Lord [Elwood Meadows](wiki:Elwood_Meadows) quickly surrenders Storm's End to Stannis.(wiki:Lord_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

The turtle of [House Estermont](wiki:House_Estermont) is seen in Stannis's vanguard as it marches north from Storm's End toward [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Lord_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Lord Estermont publicly bends the knee to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) after Stannis's defeat in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Lord_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Lord Estermont attends [the wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of Joffrey and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell);(wiki:Lord_Estermont.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Joffrey chokes to death during the feast.(wiki:Lord_Estermont.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) During [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s trial for murder, old Estermont testifies that he saw Tyrion pour out [wine](wiki:Wine) from the [wedding chalice](wiki:Wedding_chalice) as the king was dying.(wiki:Lord_Estermont.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

Although Lord Estermont has bent the knee to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), other Estermonts continue to support Stannis as he travels to the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Lord_Estermont.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Lord Estermont has apparently passed away, as Ser [Eldon Estermont](wiki:Eldon_Estermont) is now [Lord of Greenstone](wiki:Lord_of_Greenstone).(wiki:Lord_Estermont.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
