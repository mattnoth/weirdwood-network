## Origins

In her childhood, she played with the young [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Estermont_(cousin_of_Robert).cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Both her husband and father died at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) when the castle was [besieged](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End) during the [rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) that made her cousin king. In the beginning of his reign, Robert stayed at [Greenstone](wiki:Greenstone) for a fortnight. During his visit, he consoled the young widow and [bedded](wiki:Bedding) her.(wiki:Estermont_(cousin_of_Robert).cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
