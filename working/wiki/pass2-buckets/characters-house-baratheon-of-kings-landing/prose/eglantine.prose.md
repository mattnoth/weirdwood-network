## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Septa Eglantine accompanies Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) when she is sent to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) to be a ward of Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell) and betrothed to Prince [Trystane Martell](wiki:Trystane_Martell). Because of the risk of the *[Seaswift](wiki:Seaswift)* being taken by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s forces on the way to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), Septa Eglantine puts brown [dye](wiki:Hair_dye) in Myrcella's hair to disguise her, and has the princess's handmaid [Rosamund Lannister](wiki:Rosamund_Lannister) wear her clothes. The septa tells Myrcella it is a game, but Myrcella understands the truth.(wiki:Eglantine.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
