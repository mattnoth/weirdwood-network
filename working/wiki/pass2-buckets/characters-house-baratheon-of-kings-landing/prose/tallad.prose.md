## Appearances & Description

Big and strapping,(wiki:Tallad.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) the handsome Tallad is considered a promising young [knight](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Tallad.cite_ref-Rasos58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Tallad.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) considers him an oaf.(wiki:Tallad.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Tallad pledges his sword to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) observe him training at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Tallad.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Storm of Swords

After the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) spies Tallad beating one of the [Redwyne](wiki:House_Redwyne) twins in the yard.(wiki:Tallad.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Tallad dances with Sansa at the feast when [she weds Tyrion](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark).(wiki:Tallad.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Tyrion considers having his lover, [Shae](wiki:Shae), who is disguised as a serving girl, wed Tallad in order to protect her from his sister, Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister).(wiki:Tallad.cite_ref-Rasos58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Tallad dances with [Megga Tyrell](wiki:Megga_Tyrell) at [the wedding feast](wiki:Wedding_of_Tommen_I_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) of [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) and King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Tallad.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) spots Tallad practicing at [quintain](wiki:Quintain) in the yard of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), and the sandbag thumps him in the head.(wiki:Tallad.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) On another occasion young Tommen asks to have a turn when Tallad is practicing.(wiki:Tallad.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Cersei, the Queen Regent, briefly considers naming him [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms) for the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Tallad.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

Ser [Osmund Kettleblack](wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack) tells Cersei that Tallad is interested in [Elinor](wiki:Elinor_Tyrell) and [Alla Tyrell](wiki:Alla_Tyrell).(wiki:Tallad.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) Tallad, now a [household knight](wiki:Household_knight), shows the party of Margaery and Tommen how to fight with a staff during a trip to the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood).(wiki:Tallad.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) When the party returns to the capital, Cersei spots Tallad accompanying Alla.(wiki:Tallad.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)

During his torture, the [Blue Bard](wiki:Blue_Bard) is forced to list those he claims to have been Queen Margaery's secret lovers, including Ser Tallad.(wiki:Tallad.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Cersei has an arrest warrant signed for Tallad as part of her plans to remove Margaery. The knight wounds three [gold cloaks](wiki:Gold_cloaks) when they come to arrest him, but he is overwhelmed and consigned to the dungeons.(wiki:Tallad.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser Tallad is held with the rest of Margaery's falsely accused lovers in a dungeon under the charge of [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn).(wiki:Tallad.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
