## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

After [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sends [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) to the [Wall](wiki:Wall), [Varys](wiki:Varys) reveals to Tyrion that the captain is plotting to slip anchor three days hence in order to offer his sword and ship to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon). Tyrion contemplates having Ser [Jacelyn Bywater](wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater) arrange for his dissappereance but concludes that a trial before King [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey) would assure the loyalty of the other captains.(wiki:Master_of_the_White_Hart.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
