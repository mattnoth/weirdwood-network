## Origins

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) squired for Sumner for four years;(wiki:Sumner_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey) was also sent to [Crakehall](wiki:Crakehall) as a page and made a squire by Lord Crakehall.(wiki:Sumner_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

During the campaign against the outlaws of the [Kingswood Brotherhood](wiki:Kingswood_Brotherhood), Sumner had to ransom Merrett back after he was captured by [Wenda the White Fawn](wiki:Wenda_the_White_Fawn). After receiving a hard blow to the head from a mace in the next battle, Merrett was no longer able to fight. Unwilling to grant [knighthood](wiki:Knight) to Merrett, Sumner kindly sent Merrett back to the [Twins](wiki:Twins).(wiki:Sumner_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Jaime stopped [Big Belly Ben](wiki:Big_Belly_Ben) from killing the Lord of Crakehall in battle.(wiki:Sumner_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Sumner's fate is unknown. Lord [Roland Crakehall](wiki:Roland_Crakehall_(lord)) led the house during the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:Sumner_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
