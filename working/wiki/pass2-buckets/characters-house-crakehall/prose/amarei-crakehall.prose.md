## Origins

Amarei was born as a daughter of [House Crakehall](wiki:House_Crakehall). She was married to Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) as his third wife, after his second wife, [Cyrenna Swann](wiki:Cyrenna_Swann), had died. Amarei gave her husband seven children: Ser [Hosteen](wiki:Hosteen_Frey), [Lythene](wiki:Lythene_Frey), [Symond](wiki:Symond_Frey), Ser [Danwell](wiki:Danwell_Frey), [Merrett](wiki:Merrett_Frey), Ser [Geremy](wiki:Geremy_Frey), and Ser [Raymund Frey](wiki:Raymund_Frey).(wiki:Amarei_Crakehall.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8)

Amarei's children and grandchildren often maintain the Crakehall look, tending to be big-boned and strong. Her granddaughters are strong, with wide hips and large breasts.(wiki:Amarei_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Merrett was sent to [Crakehall](wiki:Crakehall) to serve his mother's family as a [page](wiki:Page), and later [squired](wiki:Squire) for Lord [Sumner Crakehall](wiki:Sumner_Crakehall).(wiki:Amarei_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord Walder recalls when Ser [Hosteen](wiki:Hosteen_Frey) and Ser [Danwell](wiki:Danwell_Frey) were unhorsed at a [tourney](wiki:Tourney) held in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in [298 AC](wiki:298_AC). Disappointed by their performance, Walder tells Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) that he sometimes ponders if they are his actual sons, for "all of the Crakehall women are sluts".(wiki:Amarei_Crakehall.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

### A Storm of Swords

Lord Walder selects [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey) to wed Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully), though Catelyn prefers Amarei's granddaughters, for their wide hips to bear children, big breasts to nurse them, and strong arms to carry them.(wiki:Amarei_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
