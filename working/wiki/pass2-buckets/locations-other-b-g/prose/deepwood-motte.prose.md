## Origins

Deepwood Motte was the royal seat of the [Glovers](wiki:House_Glover) when they were kings in the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes).(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) has visited Deepwood several times with Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark).(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Deepwood Motte](wiki:Category:Images_of_Deepwood_Motte)*

An old but not particularly strong castle, Deepwood Motte is located in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) at a wide, rounded hill with a flattened top. The castle's cavernous longhall sits atop the hill and has at one end a watchtower rising fifty feet higher, the tallest object this side of the [northern mountains](wiki:Northern_mountains). Wooden steps lead to the bedchamber of the [Master of Deepwood Motte](wiki:Master_of_Deepwood_Motte), and the keep has an exterior stair.(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Below the hill is a bailey containing stables, paddock, smithy, well, and sheepfold. These are defended by deep ditches, a sloping earthen dike, and a log palisade. The kitchens are located in Deepwood's inner yard, and the castle also has a [godswood](wiki:Godswood) and a cellar. Apple [wine](wiki:Wine) is available at Deepwood.(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) The castle also has dungeons.(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Deepwood's mossy, wooden outer walls make an oval about the hill and are protected by wallwalks. Gates located to the north and south each have two square wooden towers, with moss growing on the southern towers and palisade.(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) Deepwood has at least one postern gate, located on the eastern side.(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

East and west of Deepwood are fields of oat and barley.(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) North of the castle Motte lie tidal flats(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) leading to the [Bay of Ice](wiki:Bay_of_Ice). Deepwood is five leagues south of the [Bay of Ice](wiki:Bay_of_Ice)(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6) and one hundred leagues (three hundred miles) northwest of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) The dense forest by Deepwood includes [soldier pines](wiki:Soldier_pine), [sentinels](wiki:Sentinel), [beech](wiki:Beech), [ash](wiki:Ash), ancient [oaks](wiki:Oak), [chestnut trees](wiki:Chestnut_tree), [ironwoods](wiki:Ironwood), and firs.(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Larence Snow](wiki:Larence_Snow), the Bastard of [Hornwood](wiki:House_Hornwood), is a ward of [Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover) at Deepwood.(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) With Galbart and [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover) fighting south of the [Neck](wiki:Neck) in the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Lady [Sybelle Glover](wiki:Sybelle_Glover) remains at Deepwood. The [castle's steward](wiki:Glover_steward) visits [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) for the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast).(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

[Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) is sent by her father [Balon](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), to attack Deepwood Motte from the north,(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) and she [captures it](wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte) with a thousand [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) after a moon's turn.(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Balon declares himself first [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands)(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and then [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North).(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sends a raven to Deepwood Motte and other northern seats in a plea for help to defend [Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) from [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings).(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Asha brings Lady Glover and her children, [Gawen](wiki:Gawen_Glover) and [Erena](wiki:Erena_Glover), from Deepwood to [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers) on [Harlaw](wiki:Harlaw) in the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands).(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Following the selection of [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) as [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North) at the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), Asha returns to Deepwood Motte with Lady Glover, whose children remain at Ten Towers. Sybelle spends most of her time in the castle's [godswood](wiki:Godswood).(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8)

Asha and her followers abandon Deepwood Motte when [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) and the [northern mountain clans](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans) loyal to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) appear to retake the seat of the Glovers.(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-9) Asha is captured in the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte),(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) and Stannis restores the castle to the Glovers.(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

[Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) desires to march on Deepwood from [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) to confront Stannis, but Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) insists his son wait until after he is married.(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) Instead of Barrowton, Roose has [Ramsay's wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark) moved to Winterfell.(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Lady Sybelle sends trackers and hunters to guide Stannis's army for their [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell).(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) She later releases six [surviving ironborn](wiki:Ragged_nine) to serve as escorts for [Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris).(wiki:Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
