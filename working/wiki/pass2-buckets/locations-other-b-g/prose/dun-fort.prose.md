## Origins

The Dun Fort was the seat of [House Darklyn](wiki:House_Darklyn). It was the site of the [Defiance of Duskendale](wiki:Defiance_of_Duskendale), when Lord [Denys Darklyn](wiki:Denys_Darklyn) captured and imprisoned King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Dun_Fort.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) visits the Dun Fort during her search for [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark). Because Lord [Renfred Rykker](wiki:Renfred_Rykker) is with Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) at [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool), he left Ser [Rufus Leek](wiki:Rufus_Leek) as the castle's castellan. Brienne also meets the Dun Fort's bald maester, who has a freckled scalp and red mustache.(wiki:Dun_Fort.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
