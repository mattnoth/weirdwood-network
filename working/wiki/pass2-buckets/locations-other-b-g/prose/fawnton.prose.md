## Origins

In [12 AC](wiki:12_AC) during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), the [Wyl of Wyl](wiki:Wyl_of_Wyl) infiltrated the [wedding](wiki:Wedding) of Ser [Jon Cafferen](wiki:Jon_Cafferen) and [Alys Oakheart](wiki:Alys_Oakheart). After [gelding](wiki:Eunuch) Jon and killing Alys's father, Lord [Oakheart](wiki:House_Oakheart), and most of the wedding guests, the Wyls raped the bride and her handmaids and sold the women to [slavers](wiki:Slavery) from [Myr](wiki:Myr).(wiki:Fawnton.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The Wyl of Wyl is still remembered in Fawnton and [Old Oak](wiki:Old_Oak).(wiki:Fawnton.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
