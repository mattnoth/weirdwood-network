## Origins

Documents of the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort) indicate that the Warg King ruled [Sea Dragon Point](wiki:Sea_Dragon_Point). He was allied with the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest), but they were defeated by the [Kings of Winter](wiki:Kings_of_Winter) of [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark). The Starks killed his sons, [beasts](wiki:Bestiary), and [greenseers](wiki:Greenseer), but took his daughters as prizes.(wiki:Warg_King.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
