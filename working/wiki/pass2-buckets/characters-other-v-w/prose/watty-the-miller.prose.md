## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

The brotherhood engages the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) in the [battle at the burning septry](wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry). Watty the Miller, positioned as a bowman, is injured by an opposing archer. When they leave the septry, he serves as a scout for the brotherhood.(wiki:Watty_the_Miller.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Watty catches a cold from heavy rainfall.(wiki:Watty_the_Miller.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows

After Beric's final death, a part of the brotherhood, including Watty, apparently go their separate way from those who chose to follow the resurrected, vengeful [Lady Stoneheart](wiki:Lady_Stoneheart).(wiki:Watty_the_Miller.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
