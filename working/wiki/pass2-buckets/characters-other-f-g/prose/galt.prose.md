## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Galt is among the Stone Crows who accompany [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). While standing guard outside Tyrion's door, Galt brings Tyrion word that [Varys](wiki:Varys) wants to see him, part of Tyrion's plan to find the identity of the spy on the [small council](wiki:Small_council).(wiki:Galt.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
