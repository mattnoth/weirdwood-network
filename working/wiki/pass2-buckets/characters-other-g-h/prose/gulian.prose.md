## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Maester Gulian accompanies the force sent from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to end the [siege](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun) of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun). He brings four cages full of [ravens](wiki:Raven) with him. He remains at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) after Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) leaves for Riverrun.(wiki:Gulian.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
