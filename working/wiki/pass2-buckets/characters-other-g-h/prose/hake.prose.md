## Appearances & Description

Hake knows some [herblore](wiki:Medicine),(wiki:Hake.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and he carries a dirk.(wiki:Hake.cite_ref-Racok34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Hake accompanies [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) when the [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) inspects the corpses of [Othor](wiki:Othor) and [Jafer Flowers](wiki:Jafer_Flowers) in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest) north of the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Hake.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [Pyp](wiki:Pypar) overhears Hake saying that the two men had been part of [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark)'s ranging party.(wiki:Hake.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Hake and Dywen unsuccessfully search for Benjen.(wiki:Hake.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Hake journeys [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) as part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging),(wiki:Hake.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and he prepares stews(wiki:Hake.cite_ref-Racok34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) and soups(wiki:Hake.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) for the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). When [Dolorous Edd](wiki:Eddison_Tollett) disparages the smell of Hake's stew, the cook pats his dirk in response.(wiki:Hake.cite_ref-Racok34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

### A Storm of Swords

Hake prepares hardbread and a bowl of bean and bacon soup for the Watch at the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men).(wiki:Hake.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Following the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist), [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) recalls that Hake has been lost.(wiki:Hake.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

When [wights](wiki:Wights) attack Samwell and [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) at an [abandoned wildling village](wiki:Whitetree), Sam thinks that one of the undead may be Hake, although it is difficult to tell because half of the walking corpse's head is missing. The survivors only manage to escape with the help of [Coldhands](wiki:Coldhands); Sam sees [ravens](wiki:Raven) plucking flesh from Hake's shattered head.(wiki:Hake.cite_ref-Rasos46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
