## Origins

According to legend, Ser [Artys Arryn](wiki:Artys_Arryn), the [Winged Knight](wiki:Winged_Knight), flew to the top of [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance) on the back of a giant [falcon](wiki:Falcon) to slay the Griffin King and thereby claim the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn).(wiki:Griffin_King.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)(wiki:Griffin_King.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale:_house_arryn.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) [Maesters](wiki:Maesters) are skeptical, however, and believe that the actual Artys Arryn, an [Andal](wiki:Andal) warlord, became conflated with the legends of a [First Men](wiki:First_Men) hero who may have lived thousands of years before the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals).(wiki:Griffin_King.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale:_house_arryn.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
