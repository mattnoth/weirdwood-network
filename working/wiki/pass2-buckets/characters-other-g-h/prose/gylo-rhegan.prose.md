### A Dance with Dragons

Rhegan accepts [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai)'s contract and is present at the sieges of [Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor) and [Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen). Daenerys secretly sends Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) to try and persuade Rhegan to turn his cloak and bring the [Long Lances](wiki:Long_Lances) over to her.(wiki:Gylo_Rhegan.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
