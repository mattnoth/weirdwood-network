## Origins

Ser Morgil is renowned for slandering Queen [Naerys Targaryen](wiki:Naerys_Targaryen) and being killed by her brother, Prince [Aemon the Dragonknight](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_II)), in [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat).(wiki:Morgil_Hastwyck.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Morgil_Hastwyck.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryens_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) According to [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Kaeth](wiki:Kaeth)'s *[Lives of Four Kings](wiki:Lives_of_Four_Kings)*, these accusations were instigated by Aegon IV himself, who was at the time quarreling with his son and heir, Prince [Daeron](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Morgil_Hastwyck.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryens_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
