## Origins

Valysar was founded as a colony of the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold). It was taken by [Garin the Great](wiki:Garin_the_Great) and the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar) during the [Second Spice War](wiki:Second_Spice_War).(wiki:Valysar.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

The town has been under the authority of [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) since the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood), the years of expansion under the [tigers](wiki:Tigers_(Volantis)).(wiki:Valysar.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_volantis.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Appearances & Description

Although it is larger than the cities of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) and [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), Valysar is considered a town.(wiki:Valysar.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_volantis.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Timber from the [Forest of Qohor](wiki:Forest_of_Qohor) is sold in the markets of Valysar.(wiki:Valysar.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_qohor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
