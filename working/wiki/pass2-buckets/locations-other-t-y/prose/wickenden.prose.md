## Origins

King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) planned to visit Wickenden in [51 AC](wiki:51_AC) during his first [royal progress](wiki:Royal_progress). After Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) was attacked at [Jonquil's Pool](wiki:Jonquil's_Pool) in [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool), however, the king postponed his visit to the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) and instead returned to [Maegor's Holdfast](wiki:Maegor's_Holdfast).(wiki:Wickenden.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
