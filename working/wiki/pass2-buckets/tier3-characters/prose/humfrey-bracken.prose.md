## Origins

Humfrey hosted Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) when she [visited](wiki:Royal_progress) the [Trident](wiki:Trident) in [112 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Humfrey_Bracken.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
[House Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken) supported the [greens](wiki:Greens) when the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) began, while [House Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood) declared for the [blacks](wiki:Blacks). Humfrey's son, Ser [Amos Bracken](wiki:Amos_Bracken), and Lord [Samwell Blackwood](wiki:Samwell_Blackwood) both died during the [Battle of the Burning Mill](wiki:Battle_of_the_Burning_Mill), after which Amos's half-brother, Ser [Raylon Rivers](wiki:Raylon_Rivers), led the Bracken survivors back to [Stone Hedge](wiki:Stone_Hedge). In the meantime, however, Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) had [stormed Stone Hedge](wiki:Storming_of_Stone_Hedge) and captured Humfrey, his remaining children, his third wife, and his lowborn [paramour](wiki:Paramour). Raylon surrendered to Daemon to spare his kin's lives.(wiki:Humfrey_Bracken.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
When King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) began punishing [lords](wiki:Lord) after the death of his rival, Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), Humfrey joined the host of blacks led by Lord [Elmo Tully](wiki:Elmo_Tully).(wiki:Humfrey_Bracken.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
