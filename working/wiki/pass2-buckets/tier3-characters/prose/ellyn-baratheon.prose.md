## Appearances & Description

Ellyn was less pretty than her sister [Floris](wiki:Floris_Baratheon), and less clever than her sister [Maris](wiki:Maris_Baratheon). She and her sisters would sometimes fight, but their father always made them make peace afterwards.(wiki:Ellyn_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfab14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Origins

At the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) arrived at Storm's End. Lord Borros offered the hand of any of his daughters. Prince Aemond agreed to a betrothal, though the identity of the daughter is not known, save it was not Maris. The lord and prince were haggling over dates and dowries the morning Prince [Lucerys](wiki:Lucerys_Velaryon) arrived, who claimed he could not marry Ellyn or any of her sisters due to his betrothal to [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daemon)), and was sent away by Lord Borros.(wiki:Ellyn_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfab14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
Lady Elenda sent Cassandra, Ellyn, and Floris to represent House Baratheon at the coronation of [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) and his wedding to Princess [Jaehaera](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen). Ellyn remained at court as a companion of Queen [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen) while her mother departed the capital.(wiki:Ellyn_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfab20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
After Queen Jaehaera died, Lady Elenda put forward Ellyn and Cassandra as potential brides for Aegon III. She attended the [Maiden's Day Ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball) and Ellyn asked the king if he liked her gown. Her sister later put it about that her question was if he liked her breasts.(wiki:Ellyn_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfab21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
