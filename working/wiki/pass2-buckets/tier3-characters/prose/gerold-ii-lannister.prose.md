## Origins

Like his father, [Tyrion III Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_III_Lannister), Gerold decided to arrange [marriages](wiki:Marriage) for the more powerful [Andal](wiki:Andal) warchiefs that were entering the [Westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) with the daughters of the great local [Houses](wiki:List_of_Houses). Aware of Andal betrayals in the [Vale](wiki:Vale), he also took the sons and daughters of the new Andal [lords](wiki:Lord) as [wards and fosterlings](wiki:Fosterage) to serve as [pages](wiki:Page) and [cupbearers](wiki:Cupbearer), as well as hostages, at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Gerold_II_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
