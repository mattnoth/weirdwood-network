## Origins

The woman worked in an [alehouse](wiki:Ale)(wiki:Gendry's_mother.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) and would bring home [drunks](wiki:Alcohol) from the establishment.(wiki:Gendry's_mother.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) She had a black-haired [bastard](wiki:Bastard) named [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) with King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon),(wiki:Gendry's_mother.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) though she never revealed to the boy the identity of his father.(wiki:Gendry's_mother.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) She sometimes sang to Gendry but died when the boy was little.(wiki:Gendry's_mother.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) asked Gendry about his mother during the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King)'s investigation into the parentage of Robert's various children.(wiki:Gendry's_mother.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), the new [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), learns from [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) that Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) had asked about the apprentice's mother.(wiki:Gendry's_mother.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Gendry has become a blacksmith at the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). Since the youth resembles the late King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) and the late Lord [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) asks him about his parents.(wiki:Gendry's_mother.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
