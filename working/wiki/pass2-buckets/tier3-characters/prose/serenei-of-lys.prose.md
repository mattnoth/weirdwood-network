## Appearances & Description

Serenei was considered by many to be the most lovely of Aegon's mistresses.(wiki:Serenei_of_Lys.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) She was cold and haughty towards others at court.(wiki:Serenei_of_Lys.cite_ref-SSM_4-0)

## Origins

Lady Serenei was the last daughter from an ancient but impoverished [Valyrian](wiki:Valyria) noble family of [Lys](wiki:Lys). She was brought to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) by Lord [Jon Hightower](wiki:Jon_Hightower), the new [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King). Rumors spread that Serenei was much older than the king, practicing [dark arts](wiki:Magic) to retain her youth and beauty.(wiki:Serenei_of_Lys.cite_ref-SSM_4-1) She died giving birth to the last of the king’s bastard children, a girl called [Shiera Seastar](wiki:Shiera_Seastar).(wiki:Serenei_of_Lys.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
