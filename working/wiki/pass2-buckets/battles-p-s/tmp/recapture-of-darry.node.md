## Identity

The recapture of Darry was a battle during the War of the Five Kings in 299 AC, in which House Darry retook Castle Darry from a Lannister garrison (wiki:Recapture_of_Darry.cite_ref-Racok7).

## Allegiances

Part of the `FIGHTS_IN` War of the Five Kings. Fought between Iron Throne/House Lannister forces and the Kingdom of the North and Trident/House Darry (wiki:Recapture_of_Darry).

## Narrative Arc

After the Battle of the Camps saved Riverrun from the Lannister siege, King Robb Stark gave the riverlords leave to liberate their own seats. The forces of House Darry marched to Castle Darry, which had been captured during the Lannister occupation of the riverlands, and reclaimed it in the name of their lord, the child Lyman Darry. The recapture proved short-lived — a fortnight later Ser Gregor Clegane descended upon the castle and put it to the sword (wiki:Recapture_of_Darry.cite_ref-Racok7).
