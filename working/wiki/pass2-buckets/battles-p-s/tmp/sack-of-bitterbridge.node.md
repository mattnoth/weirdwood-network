## Identity

The sack of Bitterbridge was a battle during the Dance of the Dragons in 130 AC, in which green forces under Lord Ormund Hightower and Prince Daeron Targaryen destroyed the town and castle of Bitterbridge (wiki:Sack_of_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant).

## Allegiances

Part of the `FIGHTS_IN` Dance of the Dragons. Fought between the blacks (House Caswell) and the greens (House Hightower, House Peake, House Targaryen) (wiki:Sack_of_Bitterbridge).

## Narrative Arc

The sack was provoked by the death of Prince Maelor Targaryen in Bitterbridge. Ser Rickard Thorne of the Kingsguard had been escorting the young prince through the town when his identity was revealed, leading to a mob that killed both Rickard and Maelor. Lady Caswell hanged the guilty parties and sent Maelor's head to Queen Rhaenyra and his dragon egg to Lord Ormund. When Ormund's army arrived, Prince Daeron refused mercy and used Tessarion's dragonflame to burn the town, starting with the Hogs Head inn. Hundreds died in the sept and townspeople were cut down or drowned in the Mander. Lady Caswell yielded her castle, begging mercy for her children, then hanged herself. Ormund spared her children, sending them as prisoners to Oldtown, but ordered the garrison put to the sword (wiki:Sack_of_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant).
