## Identity

The raid on Saltpans was a devastating attack on the town of Saltpans in the riverlands during the War of the Five Kings in 300 AC, conducted by former Brave Companions led by Rorge wearing the Hound's distinctive helmet (wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans.cite_ref-Raffc25).

## Allegiances

Part of the `FIGHTS_IN` War of the Five Kings. Fought between House Cox and the townfolk of Saltpans versus former Brave Companions led by Rorge (wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans).

## Narrative Arc

After the Brave Companions fragmented following the capture of Harrenhal by Gregor Clegane, Rorge led a splinter band toward Saltpans seeking a ship to flee Westeros. Along the way they found the Hound's helmet, left by the Elder Brother who had buried the gravely wounded Sandor Clegane on the Quiet Isle (wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans.cite_ref-Raffc31). Finding no ship at Saltpans, the outlaws turned on the town — burning buildings, slaughtering smallfolk, raping, and mutilating. Ser Quincy Cox, Knight of Saltpans, refused to open his holdfast to the desperate townsfolk, leaving them to their fate (wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans.cite_ref-Raffc16)(wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans.cite_ref-Raffc31). Septon Bennet was killed and Brother Clement had his tongue cut out before dying of his wounds. Survivors were brought to the Quiet Isle by fishermen (wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans.cite_ref-Raffc31).

The raid's aftermath was far-reaching. Because Rorge wore the Hound's helmet, Sandor Clegane was widely blamed. Lord Randyll Tarly spread rumors blaming the brotherhood without banners. The term "Mad Dog of Saltpans" circulated widely (wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans.cite_ref-Raffc25). The High Sparrow cited the atrocities — particularly the killing of Septon Bennet and rape of a girl promised to the Faith — as justification for restoring the Faith Militant, which Cersei agreed to in exchange for the Faith's political support (wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans.cite_ref-Raffc28). Eventually Brienne of Tarth killed Rorge at the crossroads inn, and Biter was slain by Gendry. Lem Lemoncloak claimed the hound helm (wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans.cite_ref-Raffc37)(wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans.cite_ref-Raffc42).
