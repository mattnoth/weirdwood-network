## Identity

The riot of King's Landing was a massive popular uprising during the Dance of the Dragons in 130 AC, triggered by the suicide of Queen Helaena Targaryen and the growing fear that the Hightower army would burn the city. The riots led to the Storming of the Dragonpit, the death of five dragons, and Queen Rhaenyra's flight from King's Landing (wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown).

## Allegiances

Part of the `FIGHTS_IN` Dance of the Dragons. Fought between the smallfolk of King's Landing (led by the Shepherd, Perkin the Flea, Wat the Tanner) and Rhaenyra's loyalist forces (Queensguard, City Watch, House Manderly) (wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)).

## Narrative Arc

After the First Battle of Tumbleton, terror gripped King's Landing as citizens feared the approaching green army. The Shepherd preached against the Targaryens and their dragons at Cobbler's Square. When Rhaenyra ordered the arrest of Addam Velaryon and imprisoned Corlys Velaryon, the Velaryon forces — more than half her army — abandoned her. Helaena Targaryen's suicide was blamed on Rhaenyra, and the city erupted (wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown).

The first night saw Flea Bottom pour out in riot. Ser Luthor Largent and his gold cloaks were slaughtered at Cobbler's Square. Perkin the Flea crowned his squire Trystane Truefyre and gathered gutter knights. On the second day, the Shepherd called on the crowd to kill the dragons, leading to the Storming of the Dragonpit and the deaths of Syrax, Morghul, Shrykos, Dreamfyre, and Tyraxes. Prince Joffrey Velaryon died attempting to fly Syrax to the Dragonpit. Rhaenyra fled King's Landing the next morning, and the city entered the anarchy of the Moon of the Three Kings (wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown)(wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rtpatq).
