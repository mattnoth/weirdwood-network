## Identity

The raiding along the westerlands coast was a northern military campaign in 299 AC during the War of the Five Kings, in which Lord Rickard Karstark and Galbart Glover raided the shores of the Sunset Sea in the westerlands (wiki:Raiding_along_the_westerlands_coast.cite_ref-Racok39).

## Allegiances

Part of the `FIGHTS_IN` War of the Five Kings. Fought between the Iron Throne/House Lannister and the Kingdom of the North and the Trident (House Glover, House Karstark) (wiki:Raiding_along_the_westerlands_coast).

## Narrative Arc

After Robb Stark's victory at the Battle of Oxcross, he divided his forces to strike multiple targets across the westerlands. Lord Rickard Karstark and Galbart Glover were sent to raid along the coastline of the Sunset Sea, while Lady Maege Mormont captured thousands of cattle and Lord Greatjon Umber seized the gold mines (wiki:Raiding_along_the_westerlands_coast.cite_ref-Racok39)(wiki:Raiding_along_the_westerlands_coast.cite_ref-Rasos19). Robb later revealed to Ser Edmure Tully that he had remained in the westerlands after Oxcross in hopes of drawing Lord Tywin Lannister back to defend his homeland (wiki:Raiding_along_the_westerlands_coast.cite_ref-Rasos14).
