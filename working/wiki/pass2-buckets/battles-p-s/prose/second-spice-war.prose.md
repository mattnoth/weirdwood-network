## Origins

The war began a thousand years ago when three [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlord) from the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) joined [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) in destroying the Rhoynish port [Sarhoy](wiki:Sarhoy) on the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea). The Rhoynar responded by uniting under Prince [Garin](wiki:Garin_(prince)) of [Chroyane](wiki:Chroyane), the wonder of the Rhoyne who temporarily made Valyria tremble. Garin led a Rhoynish army of 250,000, which conquered [Selhorys](wiki:Selhorys), [Valysar](wiki:Valysar), and [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys), where they defeated three [dragons](wiki:Dragon) with Rhoynish [water wizards](wiki:Water_wizard). The alarmed Volantenes sought help from Valyria itself, and the dragonlords responded by sending 300 [dragons](wiki:Dragon). The Valyrians crushed Garin's army with their dragons,(wiki:Second_Spice_War.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) then destroyed [Sar Mell](wiki:Sar_Mell) and Chroyane. According to legend, the captive Garin called down a curse upon the Valyrians at Chroyane which resulted in the [Sorrows](wiki:Sorrows).(wiki:Second_Spice_War.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Princess [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria) of [Ny Sar](wiki:Ny_Sar), seeing the fate of Garin and his army, gathered the surviving Rhoynar, most of whom were women and children and old men, aboard their remaining watercraft and fled from [Essos](wiki:Essos) aboard [ten thousand ships](wiki:Ten_thousand_ships).(wiki:Second_Spice_War.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) The city of [Ghoyan Drohe](wiki:Ghoyan_Drohe) on the [Little Rhoyne](wiki:Little_Rhoyne) was turned into a ruin by the Valyrian [dragons](wiki:Dragon).(wiki:Second_Spice_War.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Other cities destroyed by the Valyrians were [Ar Noy](wiki:Ar_Noy) and Nymeria's Ny Sar.
