## Narrative Arc

With the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps) saving [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) from the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) army besieging it, King Robb gives the riverlords leave to liberate their own seats and lands from Lannister occupation. The forces of [House Darry](wiki:House_Darry) march to [Castle Darry](wiki:Darry), which was [captured](wiki:Capture_of_Darry) during the Lannister occupation of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), and reclaim it in the name of their lord, the child [Lyman Darry](wiki:Lyman_Darry).(wiki:Recapture_of_Darry.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Aftermath

The recapture of Darry proves short-lived, as a fortnight later Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) descends upon the castle and [puts it to the sword](wiki:Sack_of_Darry).(wiki:Recapture_of_Darry.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
