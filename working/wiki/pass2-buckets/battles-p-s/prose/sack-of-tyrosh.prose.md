## Origins

The [Band of Nine](wiki:Band_of_Nine) was a group of merchants, [mercenaries](wiki:Mercenaries) and [pirates](wiki:Pirates) from the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) who banded together to carve out kingdoms for themselves. They began their conquests by overrunning the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands).(wiki:Sack_of_Tyrosh.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Aftermath

Afterwards, the Band of Nine conquered the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones), poised to invade the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) on behalf of [Maelys I Blackfyre](wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre). However, the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) launched a preemptive attack on the Band of Nine, which became known as the [War of the Ninepenny Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Ninepenny_Kings). The Band of Nine was defeated, but Adarys remained as the ruler of Tyrosh until he was [poisoned](wiki:Poison) by his queen in [266 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Sack_of_Tyrosh.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
