## Origins

Ryger's mother was a [peasant](wiki:Peasant) who milked goats before Lord Walder got her pregnant with Ryger.(wiki:Ryger_Rivers.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) comes to discuss terms with Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) over the crossing of the [northern](wiki:North) army at the [Twins](wiki:Twins), he is rude to her. Ryger attempts to chastise his father for his discourtesy. Ryger is rudely told off, however.(wiki:Ryger_Rivers.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
