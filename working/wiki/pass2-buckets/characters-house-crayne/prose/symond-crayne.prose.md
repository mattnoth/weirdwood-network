## Origins

Ser Symond served in the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) during Maegor's reign. After Maegor's mysterious death, the new young king [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) offered Maegor's surviving Kingsguard a choice between execution or [taking the black](wiki:Night's_Watch).
