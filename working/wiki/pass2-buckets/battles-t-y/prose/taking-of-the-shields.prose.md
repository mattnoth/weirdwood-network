## Origins

When [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) is chosen at the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), he promises to conquer the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) and return the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) as a significant power.(wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) His first strike will be a surprise attack on the unguarded [Reach](wiki:Reach), whose armies are campaigning in the [crownlands](wiki:Crownlands), [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), and [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) whilst their main naval force (the [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet)) is besieging [Dragonstone](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone) and [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(300)).(wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-12)

## Narrative Arc

[Torwold Browntooth](wiki:Torwold_Browntooth) and the [Red Oarsman](wiki:Red_Oarsman) lead twelve ironborn longships up the river [Mander](wiki:Mander) to draw out the defenses of the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands). Instead of sailing along the [Reach](wiki:Reach)'s coastline, [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) leads the other ironborn ships into the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea) and attacks the Shields from the west, with the setting sun hiding their approach.(wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-13)

Ser [Harras Harlaw](wiki:Harras_Harlaw) lands at [Grimston](wiki:Grimston) and plants his standard beneath the castle walls. Daring the defenders to face him, he kills five and forces two to yield. After the seventh man is defeated the septon in the castle declares the gods have spoken and surrenders the castle. The islands of [Greyshield](wiki:Greyshield), [Greenshield](wiki:Greenshield), and [Southshield](wiki:Southshield) are captured before the next morning, while [Oakenshield](wiki:Oakenshield) lasts half a day. Euron uses [Oakenshield Castle](wiki:Oakenshield_Castle) at [Lord Hewett's Town](wiki:Lord_Hewett's_Town) as his base, with Lord [Humfrey Hewett](wiki:Humfrey_Hewett) and his family captured.(wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-14)

When the Shields' ships abandon Torwold and the Red Oarsmen in the Mander and return to the islands, they are trapped by the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) at the river's mouth. Lord Captain [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) slays Ser [Talbert Serry](wiki:Talbert_Serry) during the boarding of a [Serry](wiki:House_Serry) vessel when Talbert falls overboard. Victarion loses only six ironborn ships, including *[Hardhand](wiki:Hardhand)*, but captures thirty-eight. Victarion allows a dozen [southron](wiki:Southron) ships to escape to the Mander.(wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-15)

## Aftermath

[Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) attributes the success of his brother [Euron's](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) attack from the west to [blood sacrifice](wiki:Magic) and the assistance of [warlocks](wiki:Warlocks). King Euron raises four new [lords](wiki:Lord), granting [Oakenshield](wiki:Oakenshield) to [Nute the Barber](wiki:Nute), [Southshield](wiki:Southshield) to [Andrik the Unsmiling](wiki:Andrik), [Greenshield](wiki:Greenshield) to [Maron Volmark](wiki:Maron_Volmark), and [Greyshield](wiki:Greyshield) to Ser [Harras Harlaw](wiki:Harras_Harlaw), men who previously were supporters of potential rivals to Euron.(wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-16)

The lords who escaped destruction on the islands or the Mander retreat and wait for reinforcement from [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) of [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden). Instead being taken as [thralls](wiki:Thrall), the [peasant](wiki:Peasant) women and children whose husbands and fathers died during the battle are to be sold into [slavery](wiki:Slavery) at [Lys](wiki:Lys), which disturbs Victarion.(wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-17)(wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) According to the tidings that reach [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Lord [Moribald Chester](wiki:Moribald_Chester) and Lord [Humfrey Hewett](wiki:Humfrey_Hewett) are slain, Lord [Guthor Grimm](wiki:Guthor_Grimm) is held captive in his own castle, and Lord [Osbert Serry](wiki:Osbert_Serry) flees to Highgarden with the remnants of his fleet. Ser [Willas Tyrell](wiki:Willas_Tyrell) sends word to [House Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) at [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), and Ser [Garlan Tyrell](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell) begins raising men to retake the Shields.(wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Later, after capturing his brother [Aeron Damphair](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy), Euron admits he does not intend to hold the Shields. Attacking them opened the Mander to their raiding ships, and giving them away allows Euron to appear open handed. The ironborn will remember his victory taking the islands, but will blame their loss on the new Houses that try to hold them.(wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields.cite_ref-6)
