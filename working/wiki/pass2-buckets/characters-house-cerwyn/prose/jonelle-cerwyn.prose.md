## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Jonelle accompanies her father to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) when [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls his banners. Robb tells his brother [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) that Lord [Medger](wiki:Medger_Cerwyn) plans to take her south with Robb's host. Although Medger claims this is so she can cook for him, [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) believes that Robb will one night find her in his bed.(wiki:Jonelle_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Storm of Swords

After the deaths of her father, [Medger](wiki:Medger_Cerwyn),(wiki:Jonelle_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and her brother, [Cley](wiki:Cley_Cerwyn),(wiki:Jonelle_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Jonelle has become the Lady of [Cerwyn](wiki:Castle_Cerwyn).(wiki:Jonelle_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
