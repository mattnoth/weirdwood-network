## Narrative Arc

[Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) is once again dreaming through the eyes of [Summer](wiki:Summer), who is alerted by a faint clink, and he and [his brother](wiki:Shaggydog) can smell something wrong in the air. The direwolf desperately tries to escape from the godswood, but the gate is locked, so tries to climb a tree to reach the wall, following Bran’s thoughts. Summer falls from the tree, and Bran awakes with a sore shoulder to find a strange man in his room, followed by [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy). Theon, calling himself a prince, tells him that the castle is his now, that his [ironmen](wiki:Ironmen) swam the moat and unlocked the postern gate. His father’s former ward informs him that he must make the people of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) aware that Theon is in command, and that no one will be hurt if they concede. Bran watches as all those whom [Jojen](wiki:Jojen_Reed) saw in his dream die, and all of the crannogman’s words have come true. [The sea has come to Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell), and Mikken drowns on his own blood as he refuses to bend the knee.(wiki:A_Clash_of_Kings-Chapter_46.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Most in the castle defy Theon, but [Reek](wiki:Reek) and [Osha](wiki:Osha) join his service. Bran is disappointed about the latter, but manages to convince the rest that fighting is not the answer.
