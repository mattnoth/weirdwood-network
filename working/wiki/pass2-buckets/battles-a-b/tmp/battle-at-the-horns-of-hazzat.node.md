## Identity

The Battle at the Horns of Hazzat was fought between the forces of Astapor and a Yunkish coalition in 300 AC, during the Targaryen campaign in Slaver's Bay. (wiki:Battle_at_the_Horns_of_Hazzat)

## Allegiances

Part of `FIGHTS_IN` Targaryen campaign in Slaver's Bay. (track_b: Conflict)

## Narrative Arc

After Daenerys Targaryen left Astapor, Cleon the butcher took control by killing the ruling council and began training new Unsullied from enslaved Astapori youths. Meanwhile, the Wise Masters of Yunkai resumed slaving and raised a new army of slave levies and sellswords, joined by iron legions from New Ghis. Cleon sent an envoy to Daenerys proposing marriage and alliance; she refused. Without her aid, Cleon met the Yunkish host at the Horns of Hazzat. His inexperienced Unsullied fled in defeat back to Astapor. Daenerys later regretted not aiding him. Cleon was eventually killed by his own men during the subsequent siege of Astapor.
