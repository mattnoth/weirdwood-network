## Identity

The Battle Above the Gods Eye was an aerial duel fought over the Gods Eye lake in the riverlands in 130 AC during the Dance of the Dragons. (wiki:Battle_Above_the_Gods_Eye)

## Allegiances

Part of `FIGHTS_IN` Dance of the Dragons. (track_b: Conflict)

## Narrative Arc

Prince Aemond Targaryen had been terrorizing the riverlands astride Vhagar, the largest living dragon. Prince Daemon Targaryen flew from Maidenpool on Caraxes to meet him. They found each other above the Gods Eye near Harrenhal. Despite Vhagar's vastly greater size, Daemon drove Caraxes directly into Vhagar. In the clash, Daemon leapt from Caraxes and drove the Valyrian steel sword Dark Sister through Aemond's remaining eye. All four — both riders and both dragons — perished. Vhagar and Aemond sank into the lake; Vhagar's corpse was found years later with Aemond's bones still chained to the saddle and Dark Sister through his eye socket. Daemon's body was never recovered, though singers claim he survived. Caraxes, mortally wounded, managed to crawl ashore before dying. The battle is one of the most famous single combats in Westerosi history. (wiki:Battle_Above_the_Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rfabtpq) (wiki:Battle_Above_the_Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings_-_aegon_ii)
