## Identity

The battle beneath the Wall was a prolonged engagement fought at Castle Black in early 300 AC between the free folk and the Night's Watch, with the decisive intervention of Stannis Baratheon's forces. (wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall)

## Narrative Arc

Mance Rayder's host of 30,000–100,000 free folk, including giants and mammoths, assaulted Castle Black from both sides. The Thenns attacked from the south while the main host pressed against the Wall's gate from the north. Donal Noye led the defense of the tunnel gate, killing the giant king Mag Mar Tun Doh Weg in single combat before dying himself. Jon Snow assumed command of the Wall's defense with barely three dozen brothers, holding for days and destroying the wildlings' turtle siege engine. Jon was arrested by Janos Slynt and Alliser Thorne, then sent to parley with Mance under a peace banner — secretly to assassinate him. During the parley, Stannis Baratheon's cavalry arrived from Eastwatch-by-the-Sea and routed the wildlings. Mance was captured, Harma Dogshead was killed, and the free folk scattered.
