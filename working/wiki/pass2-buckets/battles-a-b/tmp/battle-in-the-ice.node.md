## Identity

The battle in the ice is a pending engagement between Stannis Baratheon's forces and the Bolton-held Winterfell garrison in 300 AC, during the War of the Five Kings. (wiki:Battle_in_the_ice)

## Allegiances

Part of `FIGHTS_IN` War of the Five Kings. (track_b: Conflict)

## Narrative Arc

Stannis Baratheon marched from Deepwood Motte toward Winterfell through brutal winter conditions, taking 33 days to cover what should have been a shorter distance. His army of ~5,400 — southron knights and northern mountain clansmen — became trapped in a crofters' village three days from Winterfell. Jon Snow warned Stannis via raven that Arnolf Karstark was a Bolton agent; Stannis arrested Arnolf and his men before they could betray him from within. Theon Greyjoy and Jeyne Poole (posing as Arya Stark) escaped Winterfell and were brought to Stannis's camp. At Winterfell, tensions between the Freys and Manderlys erupted after Little Walder Frey was found dead; Roose Bolton sent the Freys and Manderlys out to attack Stannis, partly to remove them from Winterfell. Mors Umber dug snow-covered pit traps outside Winterfell's gates — Ser Aenys Frey fell into one and broke his neck. The battle's outcome remains unresolved in published text; the Pink Letter claims Stannis was defeated, but its reliability is uncertain. (wiki:Battle_in_the_ice.cite_ref-Radwd42) (wiki:Battle_in_the_ice.cite_ref-Radwd62) (wiki:Battle_in_the_ice.cite_ref-Radwd69)

## Notes

The battle has not yet occurred in published ASOIAF novels. Details are drawn from ADWD setup chapters and TWOW preview material. The Pink Letter's claim that Stannis died may be unreliable.
