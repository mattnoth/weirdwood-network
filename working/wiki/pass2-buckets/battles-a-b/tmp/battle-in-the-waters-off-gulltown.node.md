## Identity

The Battle in the waters off Gulltown was a naval engagement in 2 BC during Aegon's Conquest between the Targaryen fleet and the Arryn fleet of the Vale. (wiki:Battle_in_the_waters_off_Gulltown)

## Allegiances

Part of `FIGHTS_IN` Aegon's Conquest. (track_b: Conflict)

## Narrative Arc

During Aegon's Conquest, the Targaryen fleet sailed to take Gulltown and the Vale. Daemon Velaryon, master of ships for Aegon, commanded the Targaryen fleet. The Arryn fleet met them in the waters off Gulltown. The naval battle was fierce; Daemon Velaryon was slain in the fighting. Visenya Targaryen arrived on Vhagar and burned the Arryn fleet, but the Targaryen fleet was also destroyed. Both fleets were effectively annihilated. The result was a strategic setback for the Targaryens — they could not take Gulltown by sea, and the Vale remained unconquered until the Arryn boy king's mother flew him on Vhagar at his own request and he yielded the Vale peacefully.
