## Identity

The Battle at Black Bog was fought during the Coming of the Andals to the stormlands. The Weirwood Alliance between the First Men and the children of the forest defeated the Andals at Black Bog. (wiki:Battle_at_Black_Bog)

## Allegiances

## Narrative Arc

The Weirwood Alliance — a pact between House Durrandon's Storm Kings and the children of the forest — dealt the Andals a stinging defeat at Black Bog. The battle was one of a series of victories for House Durrandon under King Durran XXI that helped check the decline of the Kingdom of the Storm for a time.
