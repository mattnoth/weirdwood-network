## Identity

The Battle for the Dawn was a legendary battle against the Others which marked the end of the Long Night during the Age of Heroes. Not to be confused with the War for the Dawn. (wiki:Battle_for_the_Dawn)

## Allegiances

Part of `FIGHTS_IN` The Long Night. (track_b: Conflict)

## Narrative Arc

During the Long Night, the Others emerged from the far north wielding ice swords and raising the dead to fight the living. The children of the forest and their allies the First Men were driven south until dragonglass weapons were discovered to be effective against the Others. (wiki:Battle_for_the_Dawn.cite_ref-Ragot24) (wiki:Battle_for_the_Dawn.cite_ref-Rasos33) According to the song "The Night That Ended," the first members of the Night's Watch rode against the Others and drove them back in the Battle for the Dawn. (wiki:Battle_for_the_Dawn.cite_ref-Racok21) Maesters believe the defeat occurred six thousand years ago, though True History states eight thousand. (wiki:Battle_for_the_Dawn.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history)
