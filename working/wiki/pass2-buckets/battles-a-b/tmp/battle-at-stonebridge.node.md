## Identity

## Narrative Arc

Led by Wat the Hewer, nine thousand Poor Fellows marched against the forces of King Maegor I Targaryen. As they attempted to cross the Mander, they were caught between six royalist hosts. The Poor Fellows were untrained, armed largely with woodsmen's axes, sharpened sticks, and farm implements. With Wat's forces divided by the river, they were cut to pieces. The carnage made the Mander run red for twenty leagues, and the bridge and castle were thereafter known as Bitterbridge. Wat fought Lord Myles Smallwood in single combat, both surviving. Wat killed half a dozen lords including Lord Meadows, the royal commander, before being taken alive and sent to King's Landing in chains.
