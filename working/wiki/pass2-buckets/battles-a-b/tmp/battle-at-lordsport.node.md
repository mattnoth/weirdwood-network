## Narrative Arc

After the Iron Fleet's defeat at the sea battle off Fair Isle, royal forces crossed Ironman's Bay. King Robert led a host of thousands against Lordsport on the isle of Pyke. The fighting left longships and galleys wrecked on the strand, buildings broken and burned including the sept and Otter Gimpknee's inn. Robert razed House Botley's timber-and-wattle castle. After conquering Lordsport, Robert continued to the final battle at Pyke. The townspeople later rebuilt — Otter's inn doubled in size, Sawane Botley rebuilt in stone — but the sept was never restored. (wiki:Battle_at_Lordsport.cite_ref-Racok11) (wiki:Battle_at_Lordsport.cite_ref-Racok12)
