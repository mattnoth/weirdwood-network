## Identity

The Battle at the Great Fork was fought in 42 AC during the Faith Militant uprising between King Maegor I Targaryen and a host of the Faith Militant and rebel lords. (wiki:Battle_at_the_Great_Fork)

## Allegiances

Part of `FIGHTS_IN` Faith Militant uprising. (track_b: Conflict)

## Narrative Arc

The Warrior's Sons and Poor Fellows, joined by several rebel lords, mustered an army of some 20,000 men near the Great Fork of the Trident. Maegor rode Balerion the Black Dread against them. Lord Rupert Falwell, a renowned warrior who had proclaimed he would slay the dragon, led the vanguard; he killed two Kingsguard before being cut down. Big Jon Hogg, blinded during the fighting, led a mounted charge by sound alone. Despite their numbers, the Faith Militant were devastated by Balerion's dragonfire and routed. The victory confirmed Maegor's dominance over the Faith, though guerrilla resistance continued for years.
