## Identity

The Battle by the Lakeshore, also called the Fishfeed, was the bloodiest land battle of the Dance of the Dragons, fought on the western shore of the Gods Eye in 130 AC. (wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore)

## Allegiances

Part of `FIGHTS_IN` Dance of the Dragons. (track_b: Conflict)

## Narrative Arc

After the death of Lord Jason Lannister at the Red Fork, the slow-moving Lannister army under Lord Humfrey Lefford was cornered against the Gods Eye by converging black forces. Lord Roderick Dustin led the Winter Wolves, joined by Frey knights under Lord Forrest Frey, Blackwood archers under Lord Benjicot Blackwood, and other rivermen. Red Robb Rivers shot down Lefford's ravens to prevent word reaching Aemond at Harrenhal. Attacked from three sides, the Lannister spearmen were driven into the lake. The Winter Wolves charged the green lines five times. The green army was completely annihilated — Lords Lefford, Swyft, and Reyne were killed along with Ser Clarent Crakehall. The blacks suffered heavily too: Lord Forrest Frey, Lord Bigglestone, and Lord Jon Charlton were slain, and two-thirds of the Winter Wolves were killed or wounded. (wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_)
