## Identity

The Battle at Kayce occurred in 132 AC during Dalton Greyjoy's war against the westerlands, in the aftermath of the Dance of the Dragons. The title is conjectural. (wiki:Battle_at_Kayce)

## Allegiances

## Narrative Arc

Dalton Greyjoy, Lord of the Iron Islands, had attacked the westerlands during the Dance, raiding Lannisport and invading Fair Isle. Lady Johanna Lannister led the westermen's resistance after her husband Lord Jason Lannister's death. Since Ser Tyland Lannister, Hand of the King, had been unable to aid the west, Johanna donned mail and marched from Lannisport and Casterly Rock to protect Kayce. The ironborn raiders were routed; Dalton's favorite uncle was among the slain. Singers claim Johanna killed a dozen ironborn, though Archmaester Gyldayn writes she instead carried a banner and inspired her men.
