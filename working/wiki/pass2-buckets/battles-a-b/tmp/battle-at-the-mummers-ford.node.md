## Identity

The Battle at the Mummer's Ford was fought in 298 or 299 AC during the War of the Five Kings. It was Beric Dondarrion's first engagement against Gregor Clegane and the origin of the Brotherhood without Banners. The title is conjectural. (wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford)

## Allegiances

Part of `FIGHTS_IN` War of the Five Kings. (track_b: Conflict)

## Narrative Arc

Eddard Stark, as Hand of the King, sent Lord Beric Dondarrion with a force of 120 men to bring Gregor Clegane to the king's justice for raiding the riverlands. Beric's party included Thoros of Myr, Ser Gladden Wylde, Lord Lothar Mallery, and Ser Raymun Darry. Clegane ambushed them at the Mummer's Ford. The battle was a rout: Clegane killed Beric with a lance thrust through the chest, and most of Beric's force was destroyed. Thoros of Myr unknowingly performed his first resurrection, bringing Beric back to life. The survivors, rather than returning to King's Landing, reorganized as the Brotherhood without Banners and began waging guerrilla war against those preying on the smallfolk.
