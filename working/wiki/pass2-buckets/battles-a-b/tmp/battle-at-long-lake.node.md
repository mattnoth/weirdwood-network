## Identity

The Battle at Long Lake was fought between free folk and northern forces of Houses Stark and Umber in 226 AC. The title is conjectural. (wiki:Battle_at_Long_Lake)

## Narrative Arc

Raymun Redbeard united the free folk and took the title King-Beyond-the-Wall. The Night's Watch let his reavers get atop the Wall and throw down ladders. Lord Willam Stark marched north from Winterfell, calling Lord Harmond Umber's banners. They met Raymun on the shores of Long Lake, with the free folk forced to fight with the lake at their back. Although Raymun beheaded Willam, he was in turn slain by Willam's younger brother Artos the Implacable, and the wildling force was defeated. Raymun's sons were also killed, but his brother Red Raven fled beyond the Wall. The Night's Watch arrived too late; Lord Commander Jack Musgood was called Sleepy Jack afterwards. (wiki:Battle_at_Long_Lake.cite_ref-Radwd7) (wiki:Battle_at_Long_Lake.cite_ref-Radwd69)
