## Identity

The battle near Yunkai was fought between the forces of Daenerys Targaryen and Yunkai in Slaver's Bay in 299 AC. The title is conjectural. (wiki:Battle_near_Yunkai)

## Allegiances

Part of `FIGHTS_IN` Targaryen campaign in Slaver's Bay. (track_b: Conflict)

## Narrative Arc

Following the fall of Astapor, Daenerys marched north toward Yunkai. The Yunkish host fielded 4,000 slave infantry in the center with sellsword companies on the flanks: the Stormcrows (500) on the left and Second Sons (500) on the right. Daenerys had ~10,000 Unsullied plus freedmen. She met separately with the sellsword captains and the Wise Masters, trying to turn the sellswords. Prendahl na Ghezn of the Stormcrows refused; Mero of the Second Sons took a wagon of wine back to his men. Near midnight, Daario Naharis arrived having killed Prendahl and Sallor, surrendering the Stormcrows. The attack was launched hours later: the Stormcrows hit the Yunkai'i rear, the Second Sons were too drunk to fight, and the slave army broke. Around 200 Yunkish were killed; Daenerys lost only a dozen men. Yunkai surrendered and freed its slaves. (wiki:Battle_near_Yunkai.cite_ref-Rasos42)
