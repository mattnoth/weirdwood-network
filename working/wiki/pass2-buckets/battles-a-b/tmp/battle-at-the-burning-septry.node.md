## Identity

The battle at the burning septry occurred in the riverlands during the War of the Five Kings in 299 AC. The title is conjectural. (wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry)

## Narrative Arc

A detachment of Brave Companions occupied a septry settlement in the riverlands. The Brotherhood without Banners, led by Beric Dondarrion, attacked before dawn. Archers Anguy, Kyle, and Notch slew three Mummer guards. When fire arrows set the septry ablaze, the Brave Companions charged out and were met by Beric's outlaws. Thoros of Myr and Beric wielded flaming swords; young Lord Edric Dayne fought at Beric's side. Kyle was killed but quickly avenged by Lem. Arya Stark and Gendry observed from a ridge. The Mummers surrendered after a short fight. Septon Utt was found hiding and hanged with his fellows, though Beric allowed two Dothraki to escape as messengers to Harrenhal. (wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry.cite_ref-Rasos39)
