## Origins

After the [fall of Astapor](wiki:Fall_of_Astapor), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) leaves a council to rule the city when she continues on to [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai). [Cleon](wiki:Cleon), a freed [slave](wiki:Slave) who worked as a butcher, takes control of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor), however, after killing the council.(wiki:Battle_at_the_Horns_of_Hazzat.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Cleon begins training new [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) after enslaving highborn Astapori youths.(wiki:Battle_at_the_Horns_of_Hazzat.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Although Daenerys forces Yunkai to surrender its [slaves](wiki:Slaves), the [Wise Masters](wiki:Wise_Masters) of Yunkai resume slaving after Daenerys continues north to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). Yunkai begins raising a new army of slave levies and [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords).(wiki:Battle_at_the_Horns_of_Hazzat.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) The Yunkai'i are joined by [iron legions](wiki:Iron_legions) from [New Ghis](wiki:New_Ghis).(wiki:Battle_at_the_Horns_of_Hazzat.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The Yunkish host is presumably led by [Yurkhaz zo Yunzak](wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak),(wiki:Battle_at_the_Horns_of_Hazzat.cite_ref-4) the richest man of Yunkai.(wiki:Battle_at_the_Horns_of_Hazzat.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Cleon the Great sends Lord [Ghael](wiki:Ghael), a former slave, as an envoy to Daenerys, now Queen of Meereen. She rejects Cleon's offer of marriage and an alliance against Yunkai.(wiki:Battle_at_the_Horns_of_Hazzat.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

Without the assistance of Daenerys's soldiers, Cleon meets the Yunkish host in battle at the [Horns of Hazzat](wiki:Horns_of_Hazzat). Cleon and his inexperienced Unsullied flee in defeat back to his palace in Astapor.(wiki:Battle_at_the_Horns_of_Hazzat.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Aftermath

After hearing of Cleon's defeat, Daenerys regrets not coming to his aid at Hazzat. However, [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) reminds her that the [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy) would try to take over Meereen if she left her city.(wiki:Battle_at_the_Horns_of_Hazzat.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Cleon is eventually killed by his own men when he orders them to attack the Yunkish host during the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor). He is briefly succeeded by [Cleon the Second](wiki:Cleon_the_Second).(wiki:Battle_at_the_Horns_of_Hazzat.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
