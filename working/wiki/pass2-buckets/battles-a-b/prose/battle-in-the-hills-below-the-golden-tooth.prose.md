## Origins

Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) finds out that the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) are raising a host at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock). With Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully) ill, Edmure sends riders to the Rock, demanding that Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) proclaim his intent. Meanwhile, he starts raising a host at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) and sends [Lord Vance](wiki:Lord_Vance) of [Wayfarer's Rest](wiki:Wayfarer's_Rest) and Lord [Clement Piper](wiki:Clement_Piper) of [Pinkmaiden](wiki:Pinkmaiden) to guard the pass below the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth), to prevent any Lannister armies from entering the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Battle_in_the_hills_below_the_Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Tywin dispatches Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) to raid the villages along the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork), and Gregor circumvents the [Tully](wiki:House_Tully) forces below the Golden Tooth. In response Edmure further divides his forces, sending men to every village and holdfast within a day's ride of the border. Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), fears this will weaken the defense of the riverlands.(wiki:Battle_in_the_hills_below_the_Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), commanding the Lannister forces, descends on the outnumbered [rivermen](wiki:Rivermen) and breaks them. Lord Vance is slain during the fight and Lord Piper retreats back to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) with the remnants of his forces, with Jaime following on his heels. Meanwhile, Tywin brings another Lannister army up from the south.(wiki:Battle_in_the_hills_below_the_Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Aftermath

The rout of the rivermen beneath the Golden Tooth allows Jaime to then fight the [battle under the walls of Riverrun](wiki:Battle_under_the_walls_of_Riverrun).(wiki:Battle_in_the_hills_below_the_Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Ser [Marq Piper](wiki:Marq_Piper) and [Karyl Vance](wiki:Karyl_Vance), now a lord, raid behind the Lannisters' lines.(wiki:Battle_in_the_hills_below_the_Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Jaime later thinks of the battle as a glorious victory.(wiki:Battle_in_the_hills_below_the_Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
