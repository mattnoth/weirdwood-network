## Origins

Born in [291 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest),(wiki:Alysanne_Bulwer.cite_ref-Calculation_2-1) Alysanne is the daughter of Lord [Jon Bulwer](wiki:Jon_Bulwer) and Lady [Victaria Tyrell](wiki:Victaria_Tyrell), and she is a distant cousin of Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell). She succeeded her father after he died of summer fever.(wiki:Alysanne_Bulwer.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Alysanne is a [lady attendant](wiki:Handmaid) of [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Alysanne_Bulwer.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Lady Alysanne and Queen Margaery play [come-into-my-castle](wiki:Come-into-my-castle) together. It is one of the things Ser [Osney Kettleblack](wiki:Osney_Kettleblack) claims is preventing him from seducing Margaery.(wiki:Alysanne_Bulwer.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
