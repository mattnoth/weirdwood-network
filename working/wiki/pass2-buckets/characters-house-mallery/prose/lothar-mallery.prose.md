## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord Lothar is assigned, along with others, the task of bringing Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) back to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to face justice for the crimes he committed in the [Riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) at the beginning of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Lothar_Mallery.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Storm of Swords

It is reported by [Harwin](wiki:Harwin) that Lord Lothar was ridden down at the [Battle at the Mummer's Ford](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford) and drowned.(wiki:Lothar_Mallery.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Harwin later accuses [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) of killing Lord Lothar Mallery, though Sandor was in King's Landing at the time of the battle.(wiki:Lothar_Mallery.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
