## Origins

During the [Andal Invasion](wiki:Andal_Invasion) of the [Vale](wiki:Vale), Lord Ruthermont was part of the [Andal](wiki:Andal) coalition led by Ser [Artys Arryn](wiki:Artys_I_Arryn). He fought in the [Battle of the Seven Stars](wiki:Battle_of_the_Seven_Stars) and some named him as the killer of King [Robar II Royce](wiki:Robar_II_Royce), the leader of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) coalition.(wiki:Lord_Ruthermont.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
