## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

When [Saltpans](wiki:Saltpans) is raided by a group reportedly led by [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) (actually [Rorge](wiki:Rorge) wearing Sandor's signature [Hound](wiki:Hound) helmet), Ser Quincy orders his castle gates shut and never attempts to help the inhabitants of the town. [Saltpans is burned](wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans) and the smallfolk scatter.(wiki:Quincy_Cox.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) A dying woman curses Quincy for not preventing her rape by [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) and mutilation by [Biter](wiki:Biter).(wiki:Quincy_Cox.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
