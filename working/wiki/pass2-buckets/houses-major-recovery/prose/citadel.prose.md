## Origins

### Origin

In the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes), the [Ravenry](wiki:Ravenry) was supposedly the stronghold of a [pirate](wiki:Pirate) lord who picked off [ships](wiki:Ship) as they came down the [Honeywine](wiki:Honeywine).(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

The origin of the Citadel is disputed, but [House Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) is generally considered to have played an integral role in its foundation.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Most accounts on the Citadel's origins credit its foundation to Prince [Peremore the Twisted](wiki:Peremore_Hightower), the second son of King [Uthor of the High Tower](wiki:Uthor_of_the_High_Tower). The curious Peremore invited numerous scholars, including wise men, teachers, priests, healers, singers, [wizards](wiki:Wizard), [alchemists](wiki:Alchemy), and [sorcerers](wiki:Sorcerer), to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). After Peremore's death, his brother, King [Urrigon](wiki:Urrigon_Hightower), granted land alongside the Honeywine to "Peremore's pets", who developed the tract into the [maesters](wiki:Maesters)' Citadel.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

House Hightower continues to be a strong patron of learning.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

### Iron Throne

There are reports of [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) and [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) visiting the Citadel in their youth. During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Lord [Manfred Hightower](wiki:Manfred_Hightower) submitted to the Conqueror to prevent the destruction of [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) by [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame).(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) visited the Citadel in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), spending three days in the Library and attending lectures.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Alysanne and her husband, King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), sent their son [Vaegon](wiki:Vaegon_Targaryen) to the Citadel to become an archmaester.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign.E2.80.94jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

[Lyonel Strong](wiki:Lyonel_Strong), [Lord of Harrenhal](wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal), studied at the Citadel in his youth, earning six links.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) While sailing to the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) visited the Citadel and studied ancient charts and [Valyrian](wiki:Valyria) treatises about warships and maritime tactics.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Ser [Michael Manwoody](wiki:Michael_Manwoody) studied at the Citadel when he was young.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen) sent his grandson [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) to the Citadel to become a maester(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) when he was nine or ten years old.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Circa [210](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)–[211 AC](wiki:211_AC), Aemon's younger brother [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) visited him at the Citadel, along with Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall).(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) achieved six links while studying at the Citadel in his youth.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Ser [Gunthor Hightower](wiki:Gunthor_Hightower) has also studied at the Citadel.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9) When [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) mentioned to his father that he would like to become a maester at the Citadel, Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) had him chained to a wall for three days to discourage him.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

Bones of [giants](wiki:Giants) found in the [north](wiki:North) have been sent to the Citadel for study.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_dawn_age.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Archmaester [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn)'s history of Aegon's Conquest was recently found in the papers of Archmaester [Gerold](wiki:Gerold_(archmaester)) in the vaults of the Citadel.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

## Appearances & Description

The Citadel lies on the [Honeywine](wiki:Honeywine), where its towers and domes are connected with arching stone bridges. Houses and stalls sit on the bridges. It lies upriver compared to the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) and [Battle Island](wiki:Battle_Island).(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) The gates are flanked by a pair of tall green [sphinxes](wiki:Sphinx) with the bodies of [lions](wiki:Lion), the wings of [eagles](wiki:Eagle) and the tails of [serpents](wiki:Serpent). One has the face of a man, the other a woman.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### Scribe's Hearth

Just inside the gates of the Citadel lies the [Scribe's Hearth](wiki:Scribe's_Hearth). Here, the citizens of Oldtown come to hire scribes, usually acolytes, to write and read letters for them. The scribes wait in open stalls for their custom. Other stalls situated at the Scribe's Hearth include those where books are bought and sold. Others offer maps.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

The path divides at a statue of King [Daeron I Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen) atop a tall stone horse; the king's sword is pointed toward [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) The Citadel also has a monument of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen); its plinth reads "He bound the land together, and made of [seven kingdoms, one](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms)."(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### Weeping Dock

From this dock, one can get a boat for a short voyage to the [Bloody Isle](wiki:Bloody_Isle).(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Near the dock is a [septry](wiki:Septry).(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

### Seneschal's Court

Outside the [Seneschal's Court](wiki:Seneschal's_Court) lies the stocks where acolytes and novices are punished for minor crimes, such as petty thievery. Inside the doors is a hall with a stone floor and high, arched windows. At the far end of the hall there is a raised dais where a gatekeeper greets all those who wish to make an appointment to see the [Seneschal](wiki:Seneschal). Behind the dais is a turnpike stair.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

### Isle of Ravens

The [Isle of Ravens](wiki:Isle_of_Ravens) is linked to the eastern bank of the Honeywine by a weathered wooden drawbridge. On the island is located the [Ravenry](wiki:Ravenry), the oldest building of the Citadel. The walls of the Ravenry are covered in moss and vines, and within its yard sits a [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) tree on which the [ravens](wiki:Raven) like to perch. The [rookery](wiki:Rookery) for [white ravens](wiki:White_raven) is located in the west tower, and that of black ravens is found in the north tower.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

### Library and Vaults

The vast(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) [Library](wiki:Library_(Citadel)) of the Citadel is known to be a great repository of knowledge.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

The Citadel has four [glass candles](wiki:Glass_candle), one green in color and three black. Before an acolyte says his vows, he spends the night prior in the darkness of the Citadel's vault. The acolyte can attempt to light an [obsidian](wiki:Obsidian) candle, but none have succeeded.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) The Citadel also has a stuffed [Little Valyrian](wiki:Little_Valyrian) on display, but its fur fell out as students patted it for luck over the years.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_qohor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

The [Conclave](wiki:Conclave) meets to choose the new [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) after [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle)'s removal from the position. Although the deliberations are supposed to be secret, Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) learns from spies at the Citadel that Maester [Gormon](wiki:Gormon_Tyrell), born a [Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) of [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden), has been selected. The [master of whisperers](wiki:Master_of_whisperers) informs the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), who quickly reinstates Pycelle.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Rasos12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sends a [raven](wiki:Raven) to the Citadel in a plea for help to defend [Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) from [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings).(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Half a year earlier, Archmaester [Walgrave](wiki:Walgrave) was found weeping in the [Library](wiki:Library_(Citadel)) by acolytes after being unable to find his way back to his chambers.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

[Pate](wiki:Pate_(novice)), a novice at the Citadel, gives Walgrave's [archmaester's key](wiki:Personal_key_of_Walgrave) to a man calling himself an [alchemist](wiki:Alchemist), and is killed by him afterwards.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), sends [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) to the Citadel to study and become a maester, and sends Maester Aemon with him to protect the former prince from [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre).(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) Maester Aemon brings a chest of rare books from the vault of Castle Black to gift to the Citadel,(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4) but Sam gives them to [Quhuru Mo](wiki:Quhuru_Mo) to pay for passage on the *[Cinnamon Wind](wiki:Cinnamon_Wind)* at [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) and the aged Aemon dies en route.(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) After Sam arrives at Oldtown, he is forced by the gatekeeper [Lorcas](wiki:Lorcas) to wait for the [Seneschal](wiki:Seneschal), Archmaester [Theobald](wiki:Theobald). [Alleras](wiki:Alleras) brings Sam to meet Archmaester [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn) at the [Ravenry](wiki:Ravenry). Marwyn the Mage alleges that maesters of the Citadel helped to extinguish [dragons](wiki:Dragon), and the archmaester departs the Citadel to find [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen).(wiki:Citadel.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)
