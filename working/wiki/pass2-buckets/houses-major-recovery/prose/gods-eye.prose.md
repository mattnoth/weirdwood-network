## Origins

### Age of Heroes

The [Pact](wiki:Pact) between the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) and the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) was signed on the [Isle of Faces](wiki:Isle_of_Faces) in the middle of the Gods Eye,(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) and the order of the [green men](wiki:Green_men) was formed to protect the [weirwoods](wiki:Weirwood) on the island.(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_coming_of_the_first_men.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

Singers and storytellers tell the tale of the [Green King of the Gods Eye](wiki:Green_King_of_the_Gods_Eye).(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

The [Andals](wiki:Andals) were unable to conquer the Isle of Faces.(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_arrival_of_the_andals.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### Targaryen Era

[Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare), [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers), constructed [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) on the northern shore of the Gods Eye. During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), the [Battle of the Reeds](wiki:Battle_of_the_Reeds) and the [Wailing Willows](wiki:Wailing_Willows) were fought along the southern shore of the lake. Two sons of Harren the Black were slain by [Balerion](wiki:Balerion) on the lake while returning in their longboats to Harrenhal. King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) conquered the region through the [burning of Harrenhal](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal).(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Aegon then marched from his camp besides the Gods Eye to face the [Two Kings](wiki:Two_Kings).(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

[Bernarr Brune](wiki:Bernarr_Brune) slew the rebel [Harren the Red](wiki:Harren_the_Red) in a village west of the Gods Eye.(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)), the son and heir to King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen), was slain by King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) in the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye) in [43 AC](wiki:43_AC).(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the [Battle by the Lakeshore](wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore) was fought along the western shore of the Gods Eye, while the [Butcher's Ball](wiki:Butcher's_Ball) occurred south of the lake.(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Princes [Aemond](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) and [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) and their dragons, [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) and [Caraxes](wiki:Caraxes), fought to their mutual deaths in the [Battle Above the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Above_the_Gods_Eye).(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Ser [Addam Velaryon](wiki:Addam_Velaryon) flew above the lake on [Seasmoke](wiki:Seasmoke) to visit the [Isle of Faces](wiki:Isle_of_Faces).(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Aemond's body was found years later with [Dark Sister](wiki:Dark_Sister) in his eye socket, although Daemon's remains were never found.(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

[Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) was constructed by [House Butterwell](wiki:House_Butterwell) near the Gods Eye.(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rawoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) During the reign of [Aerys I Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_I_Targaryen), there was an [old timbered inn](wiki:Old_inn_by_the_lakeshore) with turrets along the lake, and Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) and Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) used [Ned's](wiki:Ned_(ferryman)) nearby ferry to cross to the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls). In the aftermath of the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion), Whitewalls was destroyed.(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Lord [Walter Whent](wiki:Walter_Whent)'s great [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) was hosted along the northern shore of the lake.(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Amid the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), [wolf](wiki:Wolf) packs are reportedly bolder than ever near the Gods Eye.(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Racok5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

[Yoren](wiki:Yoren) brings his recruits for the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), including a disguised [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), to [an abandoned town](wiki:Gods_Eye_town) on the southern shore of the Gods Eye. They are set upon by [westermen](wiki:Westermen) led by Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch), however, and [only a few survive](wiki:Fight_at_the_holdfast).(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Trying to avoid men-at-arms, Arya observes that all settlements they pass, whether villages or castles, have been burned by the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) She and [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) are eventually captured by the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) at a village by the Gods Eye(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) and taken to [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Arya learns that Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) was hanged by Amory at [Rushing Falls](wiki:Rushing_Falls) near the Gods Eye.(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-1)(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

[Meera Reed](wiki:Meera_Reed) tells [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) the story of the [Knight of the Laughing Tree](wiki:Knight_of_the_Laughing_Tree), which includes [a crannogman](wiki:Howland_Reed) who traveled to the [Isle of Faces](wiki:Isle_of_Faces) within the Gods Eye.(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

When leaving Harrenhal, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) takes the lake road to avoid a segment of the busier [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad).(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Jaime returns to Harrenhal while pacifying the riverlands. When the Mountain's men present him with the head of [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat), Jaime has [Josmyn Peckledon](wiki:Josmyn_Peckledon) throw it in the lake.(wiki:Gods_Eye.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
