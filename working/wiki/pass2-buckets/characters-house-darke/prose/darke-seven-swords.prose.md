## Appearances & Description

She has a liver-colored birthmark on her face. As a Darke by birth, she is a descendant of the [Darklyn](wiki:House_Darklyn) kings of old, and she is proud of it.(wiki:Darke_(Seven_Swords).cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

While she helps [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) with her bath, Brienne asks her about the [Darklyns](wiki:House_Darklyn). She explains that the lordly Darklyns are all gone since the [Defiance of Duskendale](wiki:Defiance_of_Duskendale), but [Darkwoods](wiki:House_Darkwood), [Dargoods](wiki:House_Dargood) and [Darkes](wiki:House_Darke) still live in the town, such as herself.(wiki:Darke_(Seven_Swords).cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
