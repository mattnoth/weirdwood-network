## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) erroneously informs [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) that Ser Lymond, as a member of the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) force being formed at [Oxcross](wiki:Oxcross) by Ser [Stafford Lannister](wiki:Stafford_Lannister), was killed in the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross) by the northern force.(wiki:Lymond_Vikary.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Lymond is being held captive at [Wayfarer's Rest](wiki:Wayfarer's_Rest).(wiki:Lymond_Vikary.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Lord [Karyl Vance](wiki:Karyl_Vance) of Wayfarer's Rest bends the knee to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) after the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) and joins [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) in the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun).(wiki:Lymond_Vikary.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Ser Lymond is presumably released from his imprisonment at Karyl's castle.
