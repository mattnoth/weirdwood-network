## Origins

In [35 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Larissa became the first true friend of her cousin, Princess [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)). For a time the two girls were inseparable, until Larissa was suddenly recalled to [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark) to be wed to the second son of the [Evenstar](wiki:Evenstar) of [Tarth](wiki:House_Tarth).(wiki:Larissa_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

By [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Lady Larissa's husband had died, and their daughter had recently been wed to the elderly Lord [Estermont](wiki:House_Estermont). Rather than remain on [Tarth](wiki:Tarth) or return to Driftmark, the widowed Larissa chose to stay with her daughter at [Greenstone](wiki:Greenstone) after the wedding. Her presence on Greenstone drew her cousin Rhaena, whose companions had recently been murdered, and whose daughter [Aerea](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen) had recently disappeared on [Balerion](wiki:Balerion). Rhaena lingered on [Estermont](wiki:Estermont) for a year, seeking the solace of her childhood companion Larissa.(wiki:Larissa_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

After Aerea's return, death, and funeral in [56 AC](wiki:56_AC), Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) asked Rhaena if she wished to return to Greenstone. Rhaena dismissed the idea, referring to Larissa as a ghost, though a kind one.(wiki:Larissa_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
