## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Ryon is among the group of nobles that accompanies Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for the wedding of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Ryon_Allyrion.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ryon is present in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), along with his bastard son, Ser [Daemon Sand](wiki:Daemon_Sand), at the feast when Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) arrives in [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) to deliver the [Mountain's skull](wiki:Mountain's_skull). He drinks when [Ricasso](wiki:Ricasso) raises a toast to King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Ryon_Allyrion.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
