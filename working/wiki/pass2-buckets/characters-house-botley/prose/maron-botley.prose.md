## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Maron becomes part of the crew of the *[Sea Bitch](wiki:Sea_Bitch)*.(wiki:Maron_Botley.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He takes part in the [harrying of the Stony Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore), with his sons in accompaniment. One of his sons gets into an argument with [Todric](wiki:Todric) over plunder and loses, leading [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) to put an arrow in the belly of Todric. Theon then orders Maron and his sons to finish Todric. They slash his throat and loot his corpse as well.(wiki:Maron_Botley.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Two [Botleys](wiki:House_Botley) remain with [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) after the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) and are slain during the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell) by [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton);(wiki:Maron_Botley.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) it is unknown if Maron or his sons are among them or if they are among the ironborn who abandon Theon beforehand.

### A Storm of Swords

Maron is listed among the crew of *[Sea Bitch](wiki:Sea_Bitch)*.(wiki:Maron_Botley.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
