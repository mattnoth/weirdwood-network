## Origins

Bellonara had been the [Black Pearl of Braavos](wiki:Black_Pearl_of_Braavos) before her daughter, [Bellegere](wiki:Bellegere_Otherys_(Courtesan)). Bellonara had succeeded the position from her own mother, who had been a Black Pearl as well, and whose own mother, in turn, had been a Black Pearl too.(wiki:Bellonara_Otherys.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
