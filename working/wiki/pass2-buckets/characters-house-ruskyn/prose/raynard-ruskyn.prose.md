## Origins

By [135 AC](wiki:135_AC), Ser Raynard was a sworn brother of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) defied an attempted coup against the [Rogare family](wiki:House_Rogare) and the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Lord [Thaddeus Rowan](wiki:Thaddeus_Rowan), beginning a [secret siege](wiki:Secret_siege). Raynard was imprisoned in [Maegor's Holdfast](wiki:Maegor's_Holdfast) after being overwhelmed and wounded by the [Lysene](wiki:Lys) guards of [Larra Rogare](wiki:Larra_Rogare).(wiki:Raynard_Ruskyn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

In the aftermath of the coup, Raynard was released from captivity, and Ser [Marston Waters](wiki:Marston_Waters), [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard), was mortally wounded when he confronted Ser [Mervyn Flowers](wiki:Mervyn_Flowers), a fellow Kingsguard and one of the conspirators. At some point, Marston was heard to say to Raynard about Mervyn, "We are the both of us [bastards](wiki:Bastard) and [Sworn Brothers](wiki:Sworn_Brother) besides".(wiki:Raynard_Ruskyn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Several royal offices were filled at the [Great Council of 136 AC](wiki:Great_Council_of_136_AC), including the promotion of Ser Raynard to Lord Commander, left vacant by Marston's death.(wiki:Raynard_Ruskyn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
