## Origins

Beatrice was the closest friend of Princess [Viserra Targaryen](wiki:Viserra_Targaryen). In [87 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), the princess complained to Beatrice that her brother, Prince [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)), showed no desire in [marrying](wiki:Marriage) her after the death of his wife, their sister Princess [Alyssa](wiki:Alyssa_Targaryen), and told Beatrice how she believed herself to be far more beautiful than Alyssa.(wiki:Beatrice_Butterwell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
