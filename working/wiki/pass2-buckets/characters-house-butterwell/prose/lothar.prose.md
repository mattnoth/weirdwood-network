## Origins

During the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls), Lothar took care of the parade of wounded, among them Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall). While in his care he let Duncan know of his disdain for the martial arts.(wiki:Lothar.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

After Duncan lost his armor and horse to Ser [Uthor Underleaf](wiki:Uthor_Underleaf), Duncan's [squire](wiki:Squire), Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen), went to Lothar and showed him the dragon ring that had been given to him by his father, Prince [Maekar Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen), hoping to enlist Lothar's help in sending a raven for help. However, Lothar delivered the Targaryen prince to Lord [Ambrose Butterwell](wiki:Ambrose_Butterwell) upon seeing the ring.(wiki:Lothar.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Appearances & Description
