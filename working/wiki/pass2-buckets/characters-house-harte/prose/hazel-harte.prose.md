## Origins

Hazel was married to [Daeron Velaryon](wiki:Daeron_Velaryon). They had one child together, Lady [Daenaera Velaryon](wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon), born in [127 AC](wiki:127_AC).(wiki:Hazel_Harte.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Hazel was one of the casualties of the [Winter Fever](wiki:Winter_Fever). In [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), her husband Daeron drowned in the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) during the [Daughters' War](wiki:Daughters'_War). Daenaera was left an orphan at the age of six, and was taken in as a [ward](wiki:Ward) by Lady [Baela Targaryen](wiki:Baela_Targaryen) and her husband, Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon).(wiki:Hazel_Harte.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Daenaera would become the queen consort of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen),(wiki:Hazel_Harte.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)(wiki:Hazel_Harte.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) and the mother of his five children.(wiki:Hazel_Harte.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_targaryen_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
