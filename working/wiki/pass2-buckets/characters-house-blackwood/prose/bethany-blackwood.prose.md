## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Sent to resolve the [siege of Raventree](wiki:Siege_of_Raventree), Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) intends to take a member of [House Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood) as a hostage to ensure that Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood) remains loyal to King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon). [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken), [Lord of the Stone Hedge](wiki:Lord_of_the_Stone_Hedge) and Tytos's rival, suggests to Jaime that he take Bethany as her father dotes on her. Jaime takes [Hoster Blackwood](wiki:Hoster_Blackwood) as a hostage instead, and he also orders Jonos to send one of his own daughters to attend Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) at court in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Bethany_Blackwood.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
