## Origins

Ser Mortimer attended the [wedding tourney](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls) at [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) in [212 AC](wiki:212_AC). He was defeated in [the lists](wiki:The_lists) by Ser [Glendon Flowers](wiki:Glendon_Flowers), and had to forfeit his [horse](wiki:Horse) and [armor](wiki:Armament) for his loss. However, because Glendon was a [bastard](wiki:Bastard), Ser Mortimer refused to send over his horse, and the armor he sent over had holes hammered through it.(wiki:Mortimer_Boggs.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Appearances & Description

Mortimer was a knight of some repute as a jouster.(wiki:Mortimer_Boggs.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
