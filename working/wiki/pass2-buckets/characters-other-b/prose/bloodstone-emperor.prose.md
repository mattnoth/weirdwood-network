## Origins

The Bloodstone Emperor's reign was a reign of terror. His usurpation became known as the [Blood Betrayal](wiki:Blood_Betrayal) in the annals of the [Further East](wiki:Further_East), which claim that the act of usurping his sister's throne ushered in the [Long Night](wiki:Long_Night). He practiced torture, dark arts, and [necromancy](wiki:Necromancy). He [enslaved](wiki:Slavery) his own people, took a [tiger-woman](wiki:Tiger-men) for wife, feasted on [human flesh](wiki:Cannibalism) and cast down the true gods of [Yi Ti](wiki:Yi_Ti) to worship a [black stone](wiki:Black_stone) that fell from the sky.(wiki:Bloodstone_Emperor.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Some scholars believe him to have been the first [High Priest](wiki:High_Priest_(Church_of_Starry_Wisdom)) of the sinister [Church of Starry Wisdom](wiki:Church_of_Starry_Wisdom).(wiki:Bloodstone_Emperor.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
