## Appearances & Description

Books is a notorious reader; he always seems to have his nose poked in some crumbly scroll. He considers [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) to be both murderous and [mad](wiki:Targaryen_madness).(wiki:Books.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Books is part of the [Siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor). He plays [dice](wiki:Dice) with [Old Bill Bone](wiki:Old_Bill_Bone), [Beans](wiki:Beans) and [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood) after the siege.(wiki:Books.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
