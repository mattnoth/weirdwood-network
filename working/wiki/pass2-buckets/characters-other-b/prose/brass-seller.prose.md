## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

The brass-seller desires to sell [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) a large polished platter of [brass](wiki:Brass). He boasts of its quality and asks thirty [honors](wiki:Currency) for it, which Daenerys considers too high. The merchant persists and negotiates a lower price with her. In the end Daenerys buys the metal for two honors even though she does not know what to do with it. Before leaving its shop, a [Sorrowful Man](wiki:Sorrowful_Men) tries to kill her by using a [manticore](wiki:Manticore). The attempt on her life fails thanks to the intervention of [Arstan Whitebeard](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) who uses his staff to flung the [poisonous](wiki:Poison) insect away. The manticore touches the brass-seller but does not sting him, the man takes fright, shriekes and soils himself.
