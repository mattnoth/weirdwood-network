## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

After the Golden Company has taken [Griffin's Roost](wiki:Griffin's_Roost), Ser Brendel is part of the war council called by [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington). He raises objections against Connington's plan to march on [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) because he sees the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) as the main enemy.(wiki:Brendel_Byrne.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
