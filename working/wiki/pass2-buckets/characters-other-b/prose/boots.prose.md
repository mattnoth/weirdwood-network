## Appearances & Description

Boots is a black kitten.(wiki:Boots.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) gives her young husband King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) three black kittens,(wiki:Boots.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) whom he names Boots, [Lady Whiskers](wiki:Lady_Whiskers), and [Ser Pounce](wiki:Ser_Pounce).(wiki:Boots.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The servant [Dorcas](wiki:Dorcas) makes Tommen a toy mouse from scraps of fur and ties it on a fishing pole. The kittens love to chase it, and Tommen likes nothing better than jerking it across the floor as the cats pounce on it.(wiki:Boots.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

When King Tommen dines with his mother, Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), and Lord Regent [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), he brings his kittens. Tommen serves morsels of [pike](wiki:Pike) to his pets. After dinner, the king and his kittens are escorted to the royal bedchamber by Ser [Boros Blount](wiki:Boros_Blount).(wiki:Boots.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
