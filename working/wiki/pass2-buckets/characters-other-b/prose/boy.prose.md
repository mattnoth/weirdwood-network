He is a chunky boy of fifteen years.(wiki:Boy.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Boy is present when [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth), Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), and Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) arrive at the [Inn of the Kneeling Man](wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man). He points a crossbow at Jaime until the three of them drop their weapons.(wiki:Boy.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

He is present when the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) arrives at the inn with the captive [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie), and [Gendry](wiki:Gendry). He serves food and drink to them, and laughs at Arya when she offers to sell her three horses for a skiff and a lesson in how to sail it. He witnesses Arya's attempt to escape, and her subsequent revelation of her true identity to [Harwin](wiki:Harwin).(wiki:Boy.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
