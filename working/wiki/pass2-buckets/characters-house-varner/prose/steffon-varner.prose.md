## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Lord Steffon is part of [Renly's](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) force beneath the walls of [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge). He takes part in the feast.(wiki:Steffon_Varner.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) After Renly's death he goes over to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) and is present beneath the walls of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) for the parley with Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose).(wiki:Steffon_Varner.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

He fights in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), having his knee shattered and getting captured by forces loyal to King [Joffrey I](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). He bends the knee to Joffrey.(wiki:Steffon_Varner.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
