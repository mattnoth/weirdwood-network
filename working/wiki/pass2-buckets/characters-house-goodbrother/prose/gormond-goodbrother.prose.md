## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Gormond brings the news of the death of King [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) first to the [Sparrs](wiki:House_Sparr) and then [Aeron Damphair](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy). Aeron takes his horse when he sets off for [Hammerhorn](wiki:Hammerhorn).(wiki:Gormond_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
