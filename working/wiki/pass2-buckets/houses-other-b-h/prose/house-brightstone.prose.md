## Origins

King [Jon Brightstone](wiki:Jon_Brightstone) paid an [Andal](wiki:Andal) warlord to help him eliminate his foes, but instead the warlord turned his swords upon him and within a year Jon had been taken, tortured, and beheaded. An Andal [knight](wiki:Knight) named [Corwyn Corbray](wiki:Corwyn_Corbray_(lord)) took Jon's daughter as wife, establishing [House Corbray](wiki:House_Corbray) in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:House_Brightstone.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
