## Origins

The ancestors of House Borrell were [pirate](wiki:Pirate) kings until the [Starks](wiki:House_Stark) came down on them with fire and sword.(wiki:House_Borrell.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6) For five thousand years the Borrells have had "the mark", a sort of webbing between the three middle fingers of their hands.(wiki:House_Borrell.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)(wiki:House_Borrell.cite_ref-7)

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Prince [Jacaerys Velaryon](wiki:Jacaerys_Velaryon) gained Houses Borrell and [Sunderland](wiki:House_Sunderland) for the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) during his visit to Sisterton.(wiki:House_Borrell.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)(wiki:House_Borrell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) The Sunderlands dragged the Three Sisters into two of the [Blackfyre Rebellions](wiki:Blackfyre_Rebellions), for which the Sistermen suffered grievously.(wiki:House_Borrell.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8)

At the start of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), [Lord Borrell](wiki:Lord_Borrell_(father_of_Godric)) let Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) sail for [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor), telling him to remain silent about his stay in Sisterton in case the rebellion failed.(wiki:House_Borrell.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-9)
