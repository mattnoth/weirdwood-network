## Origins

The Brunes of [Brownhollow](wiki:Brownhollow) are an old [knightly](wiki:Landed_knight) family from [Crackclaw Point](wiki:Crackclaw_Point).(wiki:House_Brune_of_Brownhollow.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

The [Brothers Brune](wiki:Brothers_Brune) were legendary champions who brought peace to Crackclaw.(wiki:House_Brune_of_Brownhollow.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

[Bernarr Brune](wiki:Bernarr_Brune) was squire to Lord [Alyn Stokeworth](wiki:Alyn_Stokeworth), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to King [Aegon I](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) and [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen). When Lord Alyn was slain by the rebel [Harren the Red](wiki:Harren_the_Red), Bernarr cut down the [outlaw](wiki:Outlaw) and was rewarded with [knighthood](wiki:Knight). Bernarr was later one of the champions in [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen)'s [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven).(wiki:House_Brune_of_Brownhollow.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the Brunes declared for [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen)'s [blacks](wiki:Blacks). Brune forces joined Lord [Walys Mooton](wiki:Walys_Mooton) to [liberate Rook's Rest](wiki:Retaking_of_Rook's_Rest) from the control of [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen)'s [greens](wiki:Greens).(wiki:House_Brune_of_Brownhollow.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Brunes were with [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone), at the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:House_Brune_of_Brownhollow.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

[Lothor Brune](wiki:Lothor_Brune), distant kin to the Brunes of [Brownhollow](wiki:Brownhollow), visited the family after his father died. The Brunes sent Lothor away, however, claiming he was not of their blood.(wiki:House_Brune_of_Brownhollow.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Lothor Brune](wiki:Lothor_Brune) enters the service of Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish).(wiki:House_Brune_of_Brownhollow.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Lothor is [knighted](wiki:Knight) for his feats during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), and he is to be granted a keep in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) once the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) ends.(wiki:House_Brune_of_Brownhollow.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Lothor's men slay [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard) after the [fool](wiki:Fool) leads [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) to Petyr's ship, *[Merling King](wiki:Merling_King)*, in [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay).(wiki:House_Brune_of_Brownhollow.cite_ref-Rasos61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Lothor accompanies Petyr and Sansa to the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn).(wiki:House_Brune_of_Brownhollow.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Lothor becomes captain of the [House Arryn guards](wiki:House_Arryn_guards).(wiki:House_Brune_of_Brownhollow.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
