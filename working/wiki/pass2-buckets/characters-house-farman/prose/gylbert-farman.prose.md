## Origins

Though the [Farmans](wiki:House_Farman) had ruled as petty kings of [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle),(wiki:Gylbert_Farman.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) the island had fallen to the rule of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands). As the power of the [driftwood kings](wiki:Driftwood_king) dwindled and the strength of the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) grew, Fair Isle was the first to break free from the Iron Islands when Gylbert Farman led the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) of Fair Isle in an uprising to expel their ironborn overlords from their island.(wiki:Gylbert_Farman.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
