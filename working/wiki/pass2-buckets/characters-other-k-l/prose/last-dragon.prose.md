## Origins

The last dragon was born to [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) during the reign of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen).(wiki:Last_dragon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Although she was sickly, she laid a clutch of five [eggs](wiki:Dragon_egg),(wiki:Last_dragon.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) which never hatched.(wiki:Last_dragon.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

In [152 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Ser [Arlan of Pennytree](wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree), then a young boy, was taken to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) by his grandfather, where he saw the last dragon,(wiki:Last_dragon.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) as well as her eggs, which he later described as *"hard as stone, but beautiful."*(wiki:Last_dragon.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

The last dragon died in [153 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) in King's Landing.(wiki:Last_dragon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Last_dragon.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Some rumors claimed that Aegon III [poisoned](wiki:Poison) her, as he had been afraid of dragons since [the day](wiki:Flight_to_Dragonstone) he had seen [Sunfyre](wiki:Sunfyre), the dragon of his uncle, King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), devour his mother, Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen).(wiki:Last_dragon.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)

In the final years of the Targaryen dynasty, nineteen [dragon skulls](wiki:Skulls_of_the_Targaryen_dragons) hung on the walls of the [throne room](wiki:Red_Keep). Two of these skulls were from the last hatchlings born on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), and were no bigger than a [mastiff's](wiki:Dog) skull, and oddly misshapen.(wiki:Last_dragon.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Due to the description of the skulls, one of them may be the skull of the last Targaryen dragon.

It is thought by some that [magic](wiki:Magic) began to leave the [world](wiki:Known_world) the day the last dragon died.(wiki:Last_dragon.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Ser Arlan believed that [summers](wiki:Seasons) became shorter after the last dragon died, and winters longer and crueler.(wiki:Last_dragon.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5)

## Appearances & Description

The last dragon was a green female, who lived in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). She was small and stunted, with withered wings.(wiki:Last_dragon.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Her name is not known.

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

In the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) recalls how her brother [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) had told her that the last [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) dragons had died a century and a half earlier, during the reign of King [Aegon III](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), the Dragonbane.(wiki:Last_dragon.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Clash of Kings

In [299 AC](wiki:299_AC), in King's Landing, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) meets with [Wisdom](wiki:Wisdom) [Hallyne](wiki:Hallyne) the [pyromancer](wiki:Pyromancer), who tells him that the alchemists' production of [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) is ahead of schedule. Hallyne says that their spells are more effective than usual, and wonders if there are any dragons around, as Wisdom [Pollitor](wiki:Pollitor) had once told him that [magic](wiki:Magic) had begun to go out of the world the day the last dragon died.(wiki:Last_dragon.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

### A Feast for Crows

In [300 AC](wiki:300_AC), in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) [novices](wiki:Novice) and [acolytes](wiki:Acolyte) [Mollander](wiki:Mollander), [Alleras](wiki:Alleras), [Roone](wiki:Roone), [Armen](wiki:Armen), and [Leo Tyrell](wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn)) debate the rumors that [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) has hatched three dragons. Armen says the last dragon died in the reign of Aegon III, and insists that the last Targaryen dragon was the last dragon in the world.(wiki:Last_dragon.cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

In [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), the feverish and rambling [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) tells [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) that he remembers dragons. Sam wonders how that could be possible, since the last dragon died before Aemon was born, and Aemon explains that he saw them in his [dreams](wiki:Dreams).(wiki:Last_dragon.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

At the Citadel, [Archmaester](wiki:Archmaester) [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn) tells Samwell Tarly that the [maesters](wiki:Maester) killed the dragons.(wiki:Last_dragon.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) recalls that as a child, his uncles once asked him what he wanted for a [nameday](wiki:Nameday) present. When he asked for a dragon, his uncle [Tygett](wiki:Tygett_Lannister) replied that the last dragon had died a century before, which so upset young Tyrion that he cried himself to sleep that night.(wiki:Last_dragon.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
