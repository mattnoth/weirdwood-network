## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Kyle is present when Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) gives [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) a [trial by battle](wiki:Trial_by_battle). Kyle takes part in the [battle at the burning septry](wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry). He is killed during the battle when he attempts to loose an arrow at a [Bloody Mummer](wiki:Brave_Companions), only to be impaled by a spear thrown from a man wearing black mail. Kyle is quickly avenged by [Lem](wiki:Lem).(wiki:Kyle_(brotherhood).cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
