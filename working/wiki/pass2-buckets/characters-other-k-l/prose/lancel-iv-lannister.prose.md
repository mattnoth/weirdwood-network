## Origins

Lancel IV is said to have beheaded the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) king [Harrald Halfdrowned](wiki:Harrald_Halfdrowned) and his heir during the [Battle of Lann's Point](wiki:Battle_of_Lann's_Point).(wiki:Lancel_IV_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0), with a single stroke of [Brightroar](wiki:Brightroar), the [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) greatsword of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister)(wiki:Lancel_IV_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) (however, it was probably a common steel sword of the same name, which has been conflated with the Valyrian blade).(wiki:Lancel_IV_Lannister.cite_ref-6)

When [Gyles III Gardener](wiki:Gyles_III_Gardener), [King of the Reach](wiki:King_of_the_Reach), was away battling the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) in the east, Lancel attempted to cut a piece out of the [Reach](wiki:Reach) for himself. According to Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey), his ancestor [Wilbert](wiki:Wilbert_Osgrey) fought against Lancel as the [Osgreys](wiki:House_Osgrey) were [Marshalls of the Northmarch](wiki:Marshalls_of_the_Northmarch). Although Lancel nearly cut Ser Wilbert in half with Brightroar, the dying Little Lion was able to stab his dagger through a gap in Lancel's armor to kill the king.(wiki:Lancel_IV_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Eustace claimed Lancel's men returned to the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) after Lancel's death, as they did not want to fight on after the death of their king.(wiki:Lancel_IV_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

According to Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel), Lancel IV died at [Red Lake](wiki:Red_Lake) during his invasion of the Reach.(wiki:Lancel_IV_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Yandel writes that Gyles halted his own conquest and returned home to the Reach to deal with Lancel's westermen.(wiki:Lancel_IV_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Had Lancel not invaded the Reach, Gyles might have been able to complete his conquest in the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands).(wiki:Lancel_IV_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
