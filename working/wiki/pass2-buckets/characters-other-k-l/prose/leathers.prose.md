## Appearances & Description

Well past forty, Leathers has sons and grandsons.(wiki:Leathers.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Despite his age, Lord Commander [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) considers him to still be in his prime.(wiki:Leathers.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) A savage warrior, Leathers is as dangerous with a stone axe as most [knights](wiki:Knight) are with castle-forged [steel](wiki:Steel). Not a patient tutor, he scares some of the younger recruit of the Night's Watch.(wiki:Leathers.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Leathers speaks both the [Old Tongue](wiki:Old_Tongue) and the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue).(wiki:Leathers.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Leathers is part of [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder)'s host,(wiki:Leathers.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) but they are defeated in the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall). He is one of the sixty-three [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) that [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) takes to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) from [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town). He and [Jax](wiki:Jax) are the first of them to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). They swear their vows with four other recruits ([Horse](wiki:Hareth_(Mole's_Town)), [Satin](wiki:Satin), [Arron](wiki:Arron), and [Emrick](wiki:Emrick)) at the grove of [weirwoods](wiki:Weirwood) north of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), inside the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest). They are escorted by Jon and ten rangers.(wiki:Leathers.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

Scouting ahead, [Tom Barleycorn](wiki:Tom_Barleycorn) finds nine wildlings in the [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) grove, including the [giant](wiki:Giants) [Wun Weg Wun Dar Wun](wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun). Leathers helps Jon calm them by talking to the giant in the [Old Tongue](wiki:Old_Tongue). Jon, [Iron Emmett](wiki:Iron_Emmett), and [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) weigh each recruit and assigned them to an order, with Leathers assigned to the rangers.(wiki:Leathers.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

Jon appoints Leathers as [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms) at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), over the objection of the [Lord Steward](wiki:Lord_Steward), [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh).(wiki:Leathers.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Leathers serves as a translator for Jon and Wun Wun, teaching the Old Tongue to Jon and the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue) to the giant.(wiki:Leathers.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
