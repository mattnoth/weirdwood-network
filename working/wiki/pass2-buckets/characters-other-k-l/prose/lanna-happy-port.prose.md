## Appearances & Description

Lanna has fine long golden hair. She is the daughter of another prostitute, the [Sailor's Wife](wiki:Sailor's_Wife). [Merry](wiki:Meralyn), the proprietor of the Happy Port, charges three times as much for Lanna as other whores.(wiki:Lanna_(Happy_Port).cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
