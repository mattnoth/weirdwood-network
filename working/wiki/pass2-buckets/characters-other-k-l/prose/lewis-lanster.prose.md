## Appearances & Description

Lewis is of Westerosi origin. He has golden hair.(wiki:Lewis_Lanster.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Lewis is among the sellswords of [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) origin in the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown) who are sent by the [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince) to go over to Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen). In order to gain the trust of Daenerys, Lewis is to tell her that the [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince) killed a boy that he was fond of.(wiki:Lewis_Lanster.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) After Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) reveals this plot to Queen Daenerys after the group arrives at [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), Lewis and the other Windblown are imprisoned beneath the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid).(wiki:Lewis_Lanster.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
