## Origins

Ser Tom became known as 'Long Tom' while serving in the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) for sixty years.(wiki:Tom_Costayne.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

He may have been [Tommen Costayne](wiki:Tommen_Costayne_(knight)), a knight who participated in the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow).(wiki:Tom_Costayne.cite_ref-Rthk.5B.5Bthe_hedge_knight_.28comics.29.7Cgraphic_novel.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) In this case, he would have been appointed to the white cloaks after [209 AC](wiki:209_AC) and died before Ser [Harlan Grandison](wiki:Harlan_Grandison) in [281 AC](wiki:281_AC).
