## Origins

Tommen participated in the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC).(wiki:Tommen_Costayne_(knight).cite_ref-Rthk.5B.5Bthe_hedge_knight_.28comics.29.7Cgraphic_novel.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

He may have been [Tom Costayne](wiki:Tom_Costayne), a knight of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) in an unknown period. In this case, he would have been appointed to the white cloaks after [209 AC](wiki:209_AC) and died before Ser [Harlan Grandison](wiki:Harlan_Grandison) in [281 AC](wiki:281_AC).
