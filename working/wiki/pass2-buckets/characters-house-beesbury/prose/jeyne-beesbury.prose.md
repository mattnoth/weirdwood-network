### A Dance with Dragons

Jeyne's widower, [Rhaegar Frey](wiki:Rhaegar_Frey), is betrothed to [Wynafryd Manderly](wiki:Wynafryd_Manderly), granddaughter of [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly), the [Lord of White Harbor](wiki:Lord_of_White_Harbor).(wiki:Jeyne_Beesbury.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Rhaegar mysteriously disappears on his way to [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) and is presumed dead, however. Thus making Jeyne's three children orphans.(wiki:Jeyne_Beesbury.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
