## Origins

According to semi-canon sources, Alton was the only son of [Ben Beesbury](wiki:Ben_Beesbury), the [Lord of Honeyholt](wiki:Lord_of_Honeyholt). Alton joined the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) to become a [maester](wiki:Maester) and renounced his inheritance despite his father's protests. Upon Ben's death, he was succeeded by Alton's cousin, [Warryn Beesbury](wiki:Warryn_Beesbury).(wiki:Alton_Beesbury.cite_ref-Alton_1-1)
