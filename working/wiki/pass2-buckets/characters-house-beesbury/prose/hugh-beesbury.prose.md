## Origins

Ser Hugh participated in the wager to take [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth)'s maidenhead beneath the walls of [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden). He brought Brienne a pot of honey, claiming that [Tarth](wiki:Tarth) maids were as sweet as honey. The wager was discovered by Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly), who put a stop to it.(wiki:Hugh_Beesbury.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
