## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[House Morrigen](wiki:House_Morrigen) supports [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Lester_Morrigen.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) After [Renly's death](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Lester allies with [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Lester_Morrigen.cite_ref-Rawoiafcrow.27s_nest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Lester's youngest brother, Ser [Guyard Morrigen](wiki:Guyard_Morrigen), is slain in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Lester_Morrigen.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Stannis tells Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) that Lester is among the [stormlords](wiki:Stormlords) who still remain loyal to him following his loss at the Blackwater.(wiki:Lester_Morrigen.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
