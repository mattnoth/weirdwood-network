## Appearances & Description

Robin is of mature age.(wiki:Robin_Flint.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Robin joins the host of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) after he calls the [northern](wiki:North) banners to rescue his father, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), and Robin remains with Robb after the split of the northern host at the [Twins](wiki:Twins). Robin is named to [Robb's personal guard](wiki:Robb's_personal_guard) before the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood). He is one of the older members of the group, along with Ser [Wendel Manderly](wiki:Wendel_Manderly).(wiki:Robin_Flint.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Clash of Kings

Robin is one of the lordlings who go south with Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) to meet with [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon). When a half-day's ride from Renly's camp near [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge), Robin ranges ahead to scout and spots a lookout on a distant windmill. By the time, Catelyn's party arrives at the mill, the scout is gone and Renly's outriders escort them to his camp.(wiki:Robin_Flint.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) After Renly's sudden death near [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Robin and the rest of Catelyn's party return to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Robin_Flint.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Robin, one of Robb's [sworn swords](wiki:Sworn_sword),(wiki:Robin_Flint.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) commands the rearguard for the [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) as his column marches to the Twins for Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully)'s wedding,(wiki:Robin_Flint.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and he attends Robb's meeting with Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton).(wiki:Robin_Flint.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Robin remains sober during the wedding feast as he is serving as one of Robb's guards, and he dances with [Alyx Frey](wiki:Alyx_Frey). During the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), however, Robin is stabbed to death by a group of [Freys](wiki:House_Frey).(wiki:Robin_Flint.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
