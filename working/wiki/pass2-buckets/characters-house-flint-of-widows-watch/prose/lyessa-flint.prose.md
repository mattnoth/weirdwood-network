## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Lady Lyessa does not attend the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). She is pregnant and there is a sickness at [Widow's Watch](wiki:Widow's_Watch).(wiki:Lyessa_Flint.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Lyessa's son, [Robin Flint](wiki:Robin_Flint),(wiki:Lyessa_Flint.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) is killed at the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) by members of [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey).(wiki:Lyessa_Flint.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Lady Lyessa survived the sickness at Widow's Watch and she is still the head of the house.(wiki:Lyessa_Flint.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) However, it is unknown if her baby survived.

According to Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly), should he declared for King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), the Flints of Widow's Watch will take their lead from him.(wiki:Lyessa_Flint.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
