## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Arron is part of the escort accompanying Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for the wedding of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and Lady [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Arron_Qorgyle.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Ser Arron helps Prince Oberyn donning his battle [armor](wiki:Armor) for [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat).(wiki:Arron_Qorgyle.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
