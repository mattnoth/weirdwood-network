## Origins

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Ser Glendon sided with the [blacks](wiki:Blacks). After Rhaenyra [seized](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing) King's Landing, he was made one of her Queensguard, at twenty years of age. A month later, he became [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard) after Ser [Lorent Marbrand](wiki:Lorent_Marbrand), whose body Glendon recovered, was killed during the first day of the [Riot of King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)).(wiki:Glendon_Goode.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

He passed into history as one of the [Seven Who Rode](wiki:Seven_Who_Rode), seven knights that went after Prince [Joffrey Velaryon](wiki:Joffrey_Velaryon) when the prince mounted the [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [Syrax](wiki:Syrax) to fly to the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit) to protect it from the [rioting mob](wiki:Storming_of_the_Dragonpit). Glendon and his fellow knights managed to reclaim the body of the fallen prince, but he was attacked from behind by a man who set his white cloak afire with a torch. He was then thrown out of the saddle by his [horse](wiki:Horse) and swarmed by a mob, which tore him to pieces. He had been Lord Commander for less than a day.(wiki:Glendon_Goode.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
