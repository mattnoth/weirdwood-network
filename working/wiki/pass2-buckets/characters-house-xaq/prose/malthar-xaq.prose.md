## Origins

After the first contact with people from beyond the [seas](wiki:Summer_Sea), the princes of the Summer Isles began the construction of large, strong [ships](wiki:Watercraft) that could cross long stretches of ocean and resist the fiercest storms. Prince Malthar was the greatest of these shipbuilders and is remembered as "Malthar the Windrider" and "Malthar the Mapmaker".(wiki:Malthar_Xaq.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_summer_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
