## Origins

Lord Cressey was one of the six [lords](wiki:Lord) who accompanied Lord [Manfryd Mooton](wiki:Manfryd_Mooton) to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC). As the Crown's envoy, Manfryd was tasked to make peace with the [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos) after the recent [conflict](wiki:Lord_Alyn's_attack) between the [Free City](wiki:Free_City) and Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon).
