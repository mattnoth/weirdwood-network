## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Lady Nymella is ordered by Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell) to delay Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) at [Ghost Hill](wiki:Ghost_Hill) when he arrives. She plans to outdo Lady [Jordayne](wiki:House_Jordayne), who had arranged games in his honor.(wiki:Nymella_Toland.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Nymella is present at the feast when [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) arrives in [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) to deliver the head of Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane). She drinks when [Ricasso](wiki:Ricasso) raises a toast to King [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Nymella_Toland.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Along with her daughters, Nymella hosts [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) and her companions when they arrive at [Ghost Hill](wiki:Ghost_Hill).(wiki:Nymella_Toland.cite_ref-3)
