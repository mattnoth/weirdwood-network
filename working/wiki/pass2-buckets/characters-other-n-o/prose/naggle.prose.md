## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Naggle refuses to let Ser [Creighton Longbough](wiki:Creighton_Longbough) stay in his inn because Creighton still owes him seven silver stags. [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) offers to pay Naggle for lodgings of both Ser Creighton and Ser [Illifer](wiki:Illifer), which Naggle accepts.(wiki:Naggle.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
