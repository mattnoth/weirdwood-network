## Appearances & Description

Beans is [Myrish](wiki:Myr), squint-eyed and skilled with a crossbow.(wiki:Beans.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

He relishes the tales of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s promiscuity.(wiki:Beans.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Beans is part of the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor). Beans plays [dice](wiki:Dice) with [Old Bill Bone](wiki:Bill_Bone), [Books](wiki:Books) and [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood) after the siege.(wiki:Beans.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
