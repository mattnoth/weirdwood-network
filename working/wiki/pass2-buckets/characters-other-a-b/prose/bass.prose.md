## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) is appointed to aid Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)), [Chett](wiki:Chett) is reassigned to the kennels to help Bass.(wiki:Bass.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

[Ghost](wiki:Ghost) brings a severed hand to [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) when he swears his vows in a [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) grove in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest).(wiki:Bass.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Bass tries to have his pack of hounds track the scent of the hand, but they fight to get away. Ghost instead leads the search party, which finds the corpses of [Othor](wiki:Othor) and [Jafer Flowers](wiki:Jafer_Flowers). The dogs continue to be frightened of the bodies.(wiki:Bass.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
