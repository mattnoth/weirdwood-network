## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Stalker, One Eye, and [Sly](wiki:Sly) chase down and kill a party of [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) for food. Varamyr considers which of his animals he will enter into when he dies, and thinks the younger Stalker might suit him, but prefers One Eye. Varamyr dies, and enters One Eye.(wiki:Stalker.cite_ref-Radwdprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark)'s [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) [Summer](wiki:Summer) encounters the wolf pack eating the remains of the dead [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) deserters. Summer recognizes that the head wolf, One Eye, has a [warg](wiki:Warg) within him, and they fight. After defeating One Eye and making him submit, Summer makes Stalker and Sly submit as well, and takes over leadership of the pack.(wiki:Stalker.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Near the [cave of the last greenseer](wiki:Cave_of_the_last_greenseer), Summer and the pack find a wight [snow bear](wiki:Snow_bear), and tear it to pieces and devour it, even though the rotten meat still moves while they eat it.(wiki:Stalker.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
