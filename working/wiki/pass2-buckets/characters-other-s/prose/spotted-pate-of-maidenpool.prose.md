## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Halder](wiki:Halder) informs [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) that he helped Pate carve the stone for the new pommel of [Longclaw](wiki:Longclaw).(wiki:Spotted_Pate_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Spotted Pate is among the men that [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) leaves behind to defend [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Spotted_Pate_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) After the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black), Spotted Pate, [Kegs](wiki:Kegs), and some of the other [builders](wiki:Builders) argue that the debris of the burned stairs and broken ice should be left where it is, thereby creating a new obstacle for [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder). As the suggestion would mean abandoning the defense of the tunnel, [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye) decides against it. Pate fights in the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), where he accompanies Noye down into the tunnel. There, he is killed by a [giant](wiki:Giant), [Mag Mar Tun Doh Weg](wiki:Mag_Mar_Tun_Doh_Weg), who twists the builder's head off.(wiki:Spotted_Pate_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
