## Appearances & Description

Smiler is a stallion, smaller than a destrier, but larger than a courser. He is black, and has a black temper to match. Smiler has "fire in his eyes", and has been described as a "hellhorse".(wiki:Smiler.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

A year before, Lord [Sawane Botley](wiki:Sawane_Botley) had bought the black stallion in [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport), but he proved to be too much horse for him. Theon Greyjoy, who wishes to find a quality horse to ride in battle, is pleased to find Lord Botley's horse on the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands), where good horses are rare. Part of Lord Botley's price for the horse is that Theon must take his [bastard](wiki:Bastard) nephew, [Wex Pyke](wiki:Wex_Pyke), as his [squire](wiki:Squire). The horse's size suits Theon, as does his temper. When the stallion first meets his new owner, he tries to bite his face off. Theon names the horse "Smiler" because someone Theon once knew said he smiled at all the wrong things.(wiki:Smiler.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

In [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport), Theon meets a woman calling herself Esgred, who Theon can tell is impressed with Smiler. He rides double with her to [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), flirting while Esgred asks him questions about his life and family. Theon is shocked when Esgred is revealed as his sister, [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy).(wiki:Smiler.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Theon brings Smiler to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) with him when he [captures it](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell). He rides Smiler while hunting his escaped [hostages](wiki:Hostage), [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark).(wiki:Smiler.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Theon also rides Smiler when parleying with [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) outside the gates of Winterfell. When [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Luwin](wiki:Luwin) advises Theon to [take the black](wiki:Take_the_black), Theon considers it, thinking that he has plenty of black clothing, and even his horse is black.(wiki:Smiler.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

During the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell), Theon briefly recovers consciousness after his cheekbone is shattered by [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), and the last thing he sees is Smiler, kicking free of the burning stables with his mane on fire, screaming and rearing.(wiki:Smiler.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) After Bran and Rickon and their companions emerge from where they were hiding in the [crypts of Winterfell](wiki:Crypts_of_Winterfell), [Osha](wiki:Osha) points out to Bran the arrows in Theon's fallen warhorse.(wiki:Smiler.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Theon, who has been [tortured](wiki:Torture) by Ramsay for months, is sent by him to [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin) to end [its siege](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin). Before giving the [gift of mercy](wiki:Gift_of_mercy) to [Ralf Kenning](wiki:Ralf_Kenning), Theon remembers he once had a horse. He remembers that Smiler's screams had sounded almost human; that with his mane afire, he had reared up on his hind legs, blind with pain, lashing out with his hooves. However, Theon rejects the memory, denying that [Reek](wiki:Reek) ever had a horse, and slits Ralf's throat.(wiki:Smiler.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Much later, in [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell)'s yard, Theon remembers the day that Ramsay had set his horse afire, recalling again the manner of Smiler's death, and noting that the horse burning was the last sight he had seen on the day the castle fell.(wiki:Smiler.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
