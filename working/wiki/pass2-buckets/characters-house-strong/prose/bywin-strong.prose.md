## Origins

In [73 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), after the death of the widowed Queen [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)), who had resided at Harrenhal, her brother King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) granted the castle to Bywin Strong.(wiki:Bywin_Strong.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

At the end of the year, it was discovered that Bywin's brother, Ser [Lucamore](wiki:Lucamore_Strong) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), had disgraced his vows by marrying three times and fathering sixteen children. When matters were brought up to the king, Jaehaerys put Lucamore's first wife and their children in Lord Bywin's charge.(wiki:Bywin_Strong.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

By [105 AC](wiki:105_AC), the lordship of Harrenhal had passed to [Lyonel Strong](wiki:Lyonel_Strong).(wiki:Bywin_Strong.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
