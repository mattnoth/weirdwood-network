## Origins

Ser Alyn Terrick, alongside Lord [Rupert Falwell](wiki:Rupert_Falwell) and other knights of the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) and [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), fought on behalf of the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) and the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aenys_I)) against [Maegor I](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen)'s royal army at the [battle at the Great Fork](wiki:Battle_at_the_Great_Fork).
