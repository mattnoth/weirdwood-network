## Appearances & Description

Uther is a skinny, ginger-haired young man no more than eighteen years old. His face is covered in pimples, which he attempts to hide behind his whiskers.(wiki:Uther_Shett.cite_ref-alayneI_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter

Ser Uther Shett arrives at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) to compete in Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn)'s tourney for a place in the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights). While flirting with [Myranda Royce](wiki:Myranda_Royce) along Ser [Ossifer Lipps](wiki:Ossifer_Lipps), he is interrupted by [Alayne Stone](wiki:Sansa_Stark), who rescues Myranda from their unwanted advances. During the feast on the eve of the tourney, Uther treads upon Alayne's feet when the two dances.(wiki:Uther_Shett.cite_ref-alayneI_2-1)
