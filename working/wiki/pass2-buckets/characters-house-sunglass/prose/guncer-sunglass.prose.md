## Appearances & Description

Guncer wears [moonstones](wiki:Moonstone) at his throat, wrists, and fingers.(wiki:Guncer_Sunglass.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He is pious in his [faith](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) in [the Seven](wiki:The_Seven),(wiki:Guncer_Sunglass.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) praying three times a day.(wiki:Guncer_Sunglass.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

As [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s vassal, Lord Guncer supports his claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). Stannis complains about Guncer's constant muttering about the will of [the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:Guncer_Sunglass.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

After [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) destroy the [sept at Dragonstone](wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone), Guncer approaches Stannis and tells him he can no longer support his claim. Stannis has him promptly thrown in the cells beneath [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) with the two surviving sons of Ser [Hubard Rambton](wiki:Hubard_Rambton).(wiki:Guncer_Sunglass.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords

On the orders of Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent), Lord Guncer and [Hubard Rambton](wiki:Hubard_Rambton)'s surviving sons are [burned alive](wiki:Blood_sacrifice) for treason by [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) as sacrifices to [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor), while Stannis is fighting the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Guncer_Sunglass.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Guncer's brother](wiki:Lord_Sunglass) succeeds him as head of [House Sunglass](wiki:House_Sunglass), and flees to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis).(wiki:Guncer_Sunglass.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Ser [Triston of Tally Hill](wiki:Triston_of_Tally_Hill), who was one of Guncer's men, aids Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) with smuggling [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm) from Dragonstone.(wiki:Guncer_Sunglass.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
