## Origins

Garlan was plump in his youth. His elder brother, [Willas](wiki:Willas_Tyrell), shrewdly dubbed him "the Gallant" to save him from the indignity that befell an uncle, nicknamed [Garth the Gross](wiki:Garth_Tyrell).(wiki:Garlan_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Garlan Tyrell](wiki:Category:Images_of_Garlan_Tyrell)*

Garlan is similar in appearance to his younger brother, Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell), but he is taller,(wiki:Garlan_Tyrell.cite_ref-11) more broadly built, and has a beard. Garlan is five years older than Loras.(wiki:Garlan_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Garlan is an exceptionally skilled swordsman, preferring to train against three or four swordsmen, so as to better prepare himself for actual battle,(wiki:Garlan_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-3) but his lack of interest in gaining glory makes him less famous than Loras.

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser Garlan takes part in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), playing the part of the late [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) by dressing in his armor, to scare the ignorant men-at-arms among [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s army.(wiki:Garlan_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) "Renly's ghost", actually Garlan,(wiki:Garlan_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) leads the [Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell)-[Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) army's vanguard and slays his opposite number, Ser [Guyard Morrigen](wiki:Guyard_Morrigen), in single combat.(wiki:Garlan_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

In the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) following the battle, Garlan introduces [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), his sister and Renly's widow, to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). Joffrey agrees to set aside his betrothal to [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and instead marry Margaery.(wiki:Garlan_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Garlan is raised to [Lord of Brightwater Keep](wiki:Lord_of_Brightwater_Keep), the seat of attainted [House Florent](wiki:House_Florent), as a reward for the Battle of the Blackwater.(wiki:Garlan_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

Garlan speaks and dances with Sansa during [her wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark) to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister). He tries to assuage Sansa's fears about marrying Tyrion, despite the fact that most of the Tyrells are avoiding any interaction with Sansa in the wake of their attempts to marry Sansa off to [Willas Tyrell](wiki:Willas_Tyrell) having been foiled. Garlan remarks that Tyrion is a "bigger man than he seems" and states that she would be happier with him than Loras, which implies that he knows of his brother's sexual orientation.(wiki:Garlan_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)

Garlan and his wife, [Leonette Fossoway](wiki:Leonette_Fossoway), attend the [royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of Joffrey to Margaery. While there, he speaks to Tyrion, praising his plans which helped save [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) from Stannis Baratheon's forces, and tells Tyrion that he is "made to do great deeds". He also rebukes Joffrey for publicly shaming Tyrion and destroying Tyrion's wedding gift, *[Lives of Four Kings](wiki:Lives_of_Four_Kings)*. Garlan unsuccessfully tries to save the boy king when he chokes to death.(wiki:Garlan_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

While the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) meet in the [White Sword Tower](wiki:White_Sword_Tower), Garlan guards the new king, [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Garlan_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)

### A Feast for Crows

From King's Landing, Garlan brings half of the Tyrell strength back to the [Reach](wiki:Reach) to press his claim on [Brightwater Keep](wiki:Brightwater_Keep),(wiki:Garlan_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) which is held by Ser [Colin Florent](wiki:Colin_Florent).(wiki:Garlan_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) He is preparing to take Brightwater when news of the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields) by [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) reaches him. Garlan begins to gather men from across the Reach to retake the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands).(wiki:Garlan_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

After Queen Margaery is imprisoned by the [Faith Militant](wiki:Faith_Militant), she tells Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) that she would want Garlan to represent her in a possible [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat). Cersei explains that Garlan would not be able to represent a queen because he is not a member of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Garlan_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
