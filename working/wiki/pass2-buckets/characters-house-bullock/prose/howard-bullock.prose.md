## Origins

When King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) was on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) during his regency, he spent every morning until midday in the castle yard training with sword, shield, and any other [armament](wiki:Armament). The young king sparred with Ser Howard, his father [Merrell](wiki:Merrell_Bullock), his brother [Alyn](wiki:Alyn_Bullock), Ser [Elyas Scales](wiki:Elyas_Scales), and [knights](wiki:Knight) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Howard_Bullock.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

In [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), Howard fled Dragonstone with Lady [Coryanne Wylde](wiki:Coryanne_Wylde), taking most of his wife's [jewelry](wiki:Jewelry) with them. A fishing boat took them to [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark), where they took ship for [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) and then made their way to the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands), where Howard joined the [Free Company](wiki:Free_Company_(sellsword_company)). He died in [Myr](wiki:Myr) in [53 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) after falling from his [horse](wiki:Horse) after a night of [drinking](wiki:Alcoholic_beverages).(wiki:Howard_Bullock.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)
