## Origins

Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) hoped for his son and heir, [Samwell](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), to be taken as Lord [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne)'s page at the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor) and in due time be betrothed to Desmera. Her brothers [Horas](wiki:Horas_Redwyne) and [Hobber](wiki:Hobber_Redwyne) bullied Sam during his visit, however, and Randyll brought his son back to [Horn Hill](wiki:Horn_Hill).(wiki:Desmera_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister) mentions how his father, Ser [Stafford](wiki:Stafford_Lannister), had been negotiating with Lord [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne) about a marriage between the nicely-[dowered](wiki:Dowry) Desmera to Daven. Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), however, promised a [marriage](wiki:Marriage) between Daven and an unspecified female member of [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey), after Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) changes sides in the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) and joins [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Desmera_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
