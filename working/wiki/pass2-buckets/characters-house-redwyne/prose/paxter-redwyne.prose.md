## Origins

Following the lead of his liege lord, [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), Paxter remained loyal to the [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). During the year-long [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End), the [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet) blockaded [Shipbreaker Bay](wiki:Shipbreaker_Bay) to prevent [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) from being resupplied by sea. Paxter and Mace feasted outside Storm's End while the [Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon) garrison starved.(wiki:Paxter_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The siege was nearly successful, but Storm's End was resupplied when the smuggler [Davos](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) slipped through the Redwyne blockade.(wiki:Paxter_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Following the defeat of the Targaryens through the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident) and the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing), Paxter swore fealty to the new king, [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), when Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) arrived to break the siege.(wiki:Paxter_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

During [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion), warships from the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor) supported Stannis, Robert's [master of ships](wiki:Master_of_ships), in the [sea battle off Fair Isle](wiki:Sea_battle_off_Fair_Isle).(wiki:Paxter_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

When [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) was ten,(wiki:Paxter_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rawoiafsamwell_tarly.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) brought his son to the Arbor on Paxter's ship, *[Arbor Queen](wiki:Arbor_Queen)*. Paxter's twins [Horas](wiki:Horas_Redwyne) and [Hobber](wiki:Hobber_Redwyne) bullied Sam, howevever, and Randyll brought him home to [Horn Hill](wiki:Horn_Hill). Sam's mother [Melessa](wiki:Melessa_Florent) revealed that Randyll had meant for him to remain at the Arbor as Lord Redwyne's page and squire. Had Sam pleased Paxter, he would have been [betrothed](wiki:Betrothal) to his daughter, [Desmera](wiki:Desmera_Redwyne).(wiki:Paxter_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Ser [Stafford Lannister](wiki:Stafford_Lannister) has been negotiating to betroth his son, Ser [Daven](wiki:Daven_Lannister), to Desmera, although an agreement has not been reached with her father.(wiki:Paxter_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

## Appearances & Description

Paxter is thin, with stooped shoulders. He is balding, with only a few tufts of orange hair remaining.(wiki:Paxter_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

With [Horas](wiki:Horas_Redwyne) and [Hobber](wiki:Hobber_Redwyne) held [hostage](wiki:Hostage) by Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), Lord Paxter fears for his sons' lives and thus is the only [Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) bannerman who does not declare for [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon). Following Renly's death, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sends Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) to [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) to court the Tyrells to the side of King [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), and Petyr suggests they send Hobber to Paxter as a gesture of goodwill. Petyr believes Paxter would not want [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) on the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), considering Paxter besieged him at [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End) during the [War of the Usurper](wiki:War_of_the_Usurper).(wiki:Paxter_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

Paxter participates in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), when House Tyrell joins with [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) to smash Stannis's army.(wiki:Paxter_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Paxter serves on the [small council](wiki:Small_council) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Paxter_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Lord Redwyne dances with Cersei at the [wedding of Tyrion Lannister and Sansa Stark](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark).(wiki:Paxter_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

At Joffrey's royal wedding, Paxter gives him a scale model of a ship being built on the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor) that will be named *[King Joffrey's Valor](wiki:King_Joffrey's_Valor)* after the king.(wiki:Paxter_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Following [Joffrey's assassination](wiki:Purple_Wedding), Paxter is called as a witness for the prosecution in the trial of Tyrion. He tells of how he heard Tyrion threaten the king.(wiki:Paxter_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Paxter attends [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Tommen_I_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Paxter_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

Lord Redwyne is sent to command Tommen's forces at the [siege of Dragonstone](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone). Paxter intends to take the castle bloodlessly by either starving out the defenders or creating a breach in the walls through mining, forcing the defenders to surrender and reducing Stannis's last stronghold in the south. Queen Cersei later sends Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) to take command,(wiki:Paxter_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) who leads a reckless storming of the stronghold in an effort to free up the [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet) to fight the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) in the [Reach](wiki:Reach).(wiki:Paxter_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

After the arrest of Queen Cersei by the [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow), Lord Paxter is given a seat at the small council and made [master of ships](wiki:Master_of_ships) and [lord admiral](wiki:Lord_admiral) by Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), the new [Lord Regent](wiki:Regent).(wiki:Paxter_Redwyne.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) Paxter and his Redwyne fleet are last reported as passing [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) during their voyage back to the Arbor.(wiki:Paxter_Redwyne.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
