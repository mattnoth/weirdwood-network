## Origins

In [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Torreo sent his daughter [Myrmadora](wiki:Myrmadora_Haen) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to be presented to King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) at the [Maiden's Day Ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball).(wiki:Torreo_Haen.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

After the fall of the [Rogare family](wiki:House_Rogare) in [135 AC](wiki:135_AC), a bloody struggle ensued in the resulting power vacuum. Torreo was elevated to first magister, but was poisoned with his wife, [mistress](wiki:Paramour), daughters (including Myrmadora), siblings, and supporters at the feast he held to celebrate his new position.(wiki:Torreo_Haen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
