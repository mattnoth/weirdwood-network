## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser Edwyd fights for King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) after the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)). During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), [Lothor Brune](wiki:Lothor_Brune) cuts his way through Fossoway men-at-arms to kill Edwyd and Ser [Bryan Fossoway](wiki:Bryan_Fossoway).(wiki:Edwyd_Fossoway.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
