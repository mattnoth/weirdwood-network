## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser Tanton supports the claim of [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). While marching to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), he is amongst the noblemen who are camped with Renly beneath the walls of [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge). During the feast he swears to slay [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) in single combat, though the vow is not taken seriously as he mistakenly steps in a gravy boat.(wiki:Tanton_Fossoway.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Tanton, a survivor of the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), has bent the knee to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). He is a [sworn sword](wiki:Sworn_sword) in the service of Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell).(wiki:Tanton_Fossoway.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
