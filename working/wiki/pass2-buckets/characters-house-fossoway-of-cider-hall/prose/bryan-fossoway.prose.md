## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser Bryan supports [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) at the start of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), but he goes over to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) after Renly's death at [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)). Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose) acknowledges Bryan during the parley beneath the walls of Storm's End when Stannis demands the surrender of the castle.(wiki:Bryan_Fossoway.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), [Lothor Brune](wiki:Lothor_Brune) cuts his way through Fossoway men-at-arms to kill Bryan and Ser [Edwyd Fossoway](wiki:Edwyd_Fossoway).(wiki:Bryan_Fossoway.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
