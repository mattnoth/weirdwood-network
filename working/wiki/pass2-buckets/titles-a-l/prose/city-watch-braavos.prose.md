## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), tasked by the [Faceless Men](wiki:Faceless_Men) with assassinating an [old man](wiki:Old_man), is disguised with the face of an ugly little girl. After the man is killed at the [Purple Harbor](wiki:Purple_Harbor), the city watch of [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) hunt for the ugly girl. Arya, however, returns to the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White) and has her original face restored.(wiki:City_watch_(Braavos).cite_ref-Radwd64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
