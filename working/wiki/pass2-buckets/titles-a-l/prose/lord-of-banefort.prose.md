## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When [war breaks out](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Lord [Quenten Banefort](wiki:Quenten_Banefort) supports King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). He is part of the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) host led by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) which besieges [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), but he is taken prisoner in the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Lord_of_Banefort.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Lord Banefort is a captive of Lord [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken) at [Stone Hedge](wiki:Stone_Hedge).(wiki:Lord_of_Banefort.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Freed from his captivity, Quenten is among the gathered nobility at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) which escort the corpse of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) back to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Lord_of_Banefort.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
