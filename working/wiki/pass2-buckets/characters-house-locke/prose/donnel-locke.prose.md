## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

At some point, Donnel is captured by [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) forces. He is offered along with Lord [Medger Cerwyn](wiki:Medger_Cerwyn) in a trade for [Tion Frey](wiki:Tion_Frey) by [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Donnel_Locke.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Storm of Swords

At some point, Donnel is released from captivity. One of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s [sworn swords](wiki:Sworn_sword),(wiki:Donnel_Locke.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Donnel attends the wedding of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) to Lady [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey) at the [Twins](wiki:Twins). He is among those nobles killed during the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), getting hit with crossbow bolts.(wiki:Donnel_Locke.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
