## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Benjicot is one of the men of the wolfswood that Lady [Sybelle Glover](wiki:Sybelle_Glover) sends with King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) as hunters and scouts to lead his army to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). He kills a scrawny hart that is made into venison stew for Stannis's table.(wiki:Benjicot_Branch.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He insists they are three days from Winterfell, but a blizzard strands the army at a [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village) for weeks.(wiki:Benjicot_Branch.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
