## Origins

Clegane was originally the [kennelmaster](wiki:Kennelmaster) at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock). One [autumn](wiki:Autumn) year, Lord [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister) came between a [lioness](wiki:Lion) and her prey. The lioness killed Tytos's horse and was about to attack him as well, when the kennelmaster came to his rescue with his [hounds](wiki:Hound), losing his leg and three dogs in the effort. As a reward, Tytos granted the kennelmaster a [knighthood](wiki:Knighthood), lands and a [towerhouse](wiki:Clegane's_Keep), and took [his son](wiki:Clegane_(father_of_Gregor_and_Sandor)) as his [squire](wiki:Squire), thereby creating [House Clegane](wiki:House_Clegane). For his [sigil](wiki:Sigil), Ser Clegane chose the three dogs he lost saving Tytos, on a yellow field like autumn grass.(wiki:Clegane_(founder).cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
