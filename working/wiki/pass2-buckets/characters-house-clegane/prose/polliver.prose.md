## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Polliver](wiki:Category:Images_of_Polliver)*

Polliver is near as tall as [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane), but not as heavily muscled.(wiki:Polliver.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He has a thick, black, spade-shaped beard that is neatly trimmed, covering his jaws and jowls. His head is more bald than not.(wiki:Polliver.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Polliver is a grim and methodical fighter,(wiki:Polliver.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) but [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) does not believe him to be as evil as his more violent comrades in the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men).(wiki:Polliver.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Polliver serves with Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) and is among the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) who capture [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) and [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie) near the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye). He takes [Needle](wiki:Needle) from Arya.(wiki:Polliver.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) For this, Arya adds him to her [prayer kill list](wiki:Arya_Stark).(wiki:Polliver.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

Polliver later departs [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) alongside Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) as part of Gregor's vanguard, unknowingly putting himself out of Arya's reach to choose him as her next target for [Jaqen H'ghar](wiki:Jaqen_H'ghar).(wiki:Polliver.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He is the standard bearer of Gregor and is seen wearing Gendry's bull helm when leaving, supposedly having taken it from [Dunsen](wiki:Dunsen).(wiki:Polliver.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Polliver participates in the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords) under Gregor's command, managing to cross the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) as one of his best men. He survives the battle at [Stone Mill](wiki:Stone_Mill) and successfully retreats when Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) counter-attacks with his reserve.(wiki:Polliver.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Polliver's atrocities are recalled by Arya, who informs [Harwin](wiki:Harwin) and the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners).(wiki:Polliver.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) He continues to be included in Arya's list, though as time goes on she struggles to remember his face as well as the other marked members of Gregor's men-at-arms.(wiki:Polliver.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

After the [capture of Harrenhal](wiki:Capture_of_Harrenhal), Polliver, the [Tickler](wiki:Tickler) and a [Sarsfield squire](wiki:Pimply_squire) go [whoring](wiki:Whoring) at the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads), where they encounter [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) and Arya.(wiki:Polliver.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) A fight ensues after Sandor refuses to accompany Polliver to Harrenhal and the three Mountain's men are killed, with Polliver being slain by Sandor. Arya retrieves Needle from his corpse.(wiki:Polliver.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

### A Feast for Crows

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) discovers that Gregor had named Polliver as Harrenhal's [castellan](wiki:Castellan) after his departure, but Polliver was killed by Sandor, leaving no one in charge at the castle.(wiki:Polliver.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
