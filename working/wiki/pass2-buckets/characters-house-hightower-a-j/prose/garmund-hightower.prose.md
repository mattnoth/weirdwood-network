## Origins

Shortly before the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Garmund's father, Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower), married a second time to [Samantha Tarly](wiki:Samantha_Tarly), known as Lady Sam, who would later marry his brother [Lyonel](wiki:Lyonel_Hightower).(wiki:Garmund_Hightower.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) During the war, Garmund served as a [page](wiki:Page) and [cupbearer](wiki:Cupbearer) to [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) at [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden). In [131 AC](wiki:131_AC), Lord Lyonel accepted Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon)'s peace terms for the end of the conflict, despite initially resisting them. According to [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Munkun](wiki:Munkun)'s *[True Telling](wiki:True_Telling)*, this is because the Tyrells forbade him to raise a [host](wiki:Host) or go to war without their leave, with the implicit threat that Garmund would pay for Lyonel's disobedience with his life, as every [ward](wiki:Ward) is also a hostage.(wiki:Garmund_Hightower.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Garmund later married Lady [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daemon)), and had six daughters by her.(wiki:Garmund_Hightower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_targaryen_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
