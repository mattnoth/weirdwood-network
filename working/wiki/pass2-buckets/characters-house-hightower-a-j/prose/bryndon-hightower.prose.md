## Origins

During the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton) in the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Ser Bryndon put himself between his [liege lord](wiki:Feudalism) and cousin, [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower), and the advancing [Winter Wolves](wiki:Winter_Wolves), lead by [Roderick Dustin](wiki:Roderick_Dustin), the [Lord of Barrowton](wiki:Lord_of_Barrowton). Bryndon took off Roddy the Ruin's shield arm with his longaxe, but the northman still killed both Hightowers.(wiki:Bryndon_Hightower.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

## Appearances & Description
