## Origins

Ser Gunthor studied at the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) for several years, during which time he learned to speak the [Summer Tongue](wiki:Summer_Tongue).(wiki:Gunthor_Hightower.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Appearances & Description

Gunthor owns a cloth-of-[silver](wiki:Silver) cloak and a [suit](wiki:Armament) of grey enameled scales.(wiki:Gunthor_Hightower.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

After the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) attack on the [Reach](wiki:Reach), Ser Gunthor is given command of the harbor at [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), and prepares its defenses.(wiki:Gunthor_Hightower.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Approaching Oldtown, the *[Cinnamon Wind](wiki:Cinnamon_Wind)* is inspected in [Whispering Sound](wiki:Whispering_Sound) by the captain of the *[Huntress](wiki:Huntress)*, and then inspected a second time in the harbor of Oldtown by Gunthor himself. Because Gunthor knows the Summer Tongue, he and Captain [Quhuru Mo](wiki:Quhuru_Mo) speak privately in his cabin for a while. Afterwards, Gunthor orders the harbor's chain boom to be opened so the [swan ship](wiki:Swan_ship) can dock.(wiki:Gunthor_Hightower.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
