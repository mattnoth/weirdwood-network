## Origins

In [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) arranged a marriage between Lord Uther Peake and Lady [Prunella Celtigar](wiki:Prunella_Celtigar), a lady-in-waiting to the queen and the daughter of Lord [Edwell Celtigar](wiki:Edwell_Celtigar).
