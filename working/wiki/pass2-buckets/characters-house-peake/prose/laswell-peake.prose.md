## Origins

Laswell has an unspecified familiar relationship to the main branch of [House Peake](wiki:House_Peake) of [Starpike](wiki:Starpike).(wiki:Laswell_Peake.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

In the appendix of *[A Dance with Dragons](wiki:A_Dance_with_Dragons-Appendix)*, Laswell is described as an "exile lord". Given that the Peakes were stripped of two of their three castles ([Dunstonbury](wiki:Dunstonbury) and [Whitegrove](wiki:Whitegrove)) by the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) for their support to the pretender [Daemon Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) during the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion),(wiki:Laswell_Peake.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) it is possible Laswell's ancestor was the lord of one of these castles who chose to follow [Bittersteel](wiki:Aegor_Rivers) in exile at the end of the war.

## Appearances & Description

Laswell wears his worldly [wealth](wiki:Currency) upon his person and a lord’s ransom in golden arm rings. Each ring signifies one year's service with the Golden Company.(wiki:Laswell_Peake.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Laswell is present during [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington)'s rendezvous with the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) three miles south of [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys). He, like the rest of the company, swears his allegiance to [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)) before they [invade Westeros](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company).(wiki:Laswell_Peake.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

While a quarter of the company's available strength is [taking Griffin's Roost](wiki:Taking_of_Griffin's_Roost), Ser [Tristan Rivers](wiki:Tristan_Rivers) sets off simultaneously for the seat of [House Morrigen](wiki:House_Morrigen) at [Crow's Nest](wiki:Crow's_Nest), and Laswell for [Rain House](wiki:Rain_House), the stronghold of [House Wylde](wiki:House_Wylde), each with a formidable force of comparable size. If Rivers and Peake are successful, [Harry Strickland](wiki:Harry_Strickland) argues that the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) will control the better part of [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath).(wiki:Laswell_Peake.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Laswell successfully [takes](wiki:Taking_of_Rain_House) Rain House.(wiki:Laswell_Peake.cite_ref-Rawoiafrain_house.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
