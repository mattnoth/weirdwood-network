## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Lady Leonette is among [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell)'s retinue when she comes to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Margaery introduces Leonette to [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) over dinner in the [Maidenvault](wiki:Maidenvault).(wiki:Leonette_Fossoway.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) For his valor during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Garlan is granted the lordship of [Brightwater Keep](wiki:Brightwater_Keep), making Leonette the Lady of Brightwater Keep.(wiki:Leonette_Fossoway.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) She later teaches the high harp to [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark).(wiki:Leonette_Fossoway.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Leonette and Garlan attend Sansa's wedding to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister). Noticing Sansa's deep sadness over the marriage, Leonette expresses her concerns to her husband, who tries to assuage Sansa's fears about marrying Tyrion.(wiki:Leonette_Fossoway.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Her and Garlan later attend the [royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and Margaery, seated at the high table next to Sansa and Tyrion. Leonette jests with Garlan and Tyrion at the expense of the singers.(wiki:Leonette_Fossoway.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
