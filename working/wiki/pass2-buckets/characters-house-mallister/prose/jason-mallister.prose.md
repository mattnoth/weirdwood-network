## Origins

Jason is known to have been the [Lord of Seagard](wiki:Lord_of_Seagard) since about [278 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-9) Lord Jason is a renowned [tourney](wiki:Tourney) knight, participating in several tourneys over the years. He was defeated in [Lord Steffon's tourney](wiki:Lord_Steffon's_tourney) at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) by Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy).(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-12)

During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Jason attended the [weddings of Catelyn and Lysa Tully](wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), where his gifts were lavish. Jason jested with Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) during the wedding feast.(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) Lord Mallister fought in the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident), slaying three of Rhaegar's bannermen.(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

During [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion) in [289 AC](wiki:289_AC), Jason killed [Rodrik Greyjoy](wiki:Rodrik_Greyjoy) beneath the walls of [Seagard](wiki:Storming_of_Seagard), throwing the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) reavers back into the sea.(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) After the defeat of the rebellion, King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) ordered a [tourney at Lannisport](wiki:Tourney_at_Lannisport) to celebrate his victory. There, Lord Jason was defeated by the eventual champion, Lord [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont).(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

## Appearances & Description

Lord Jason is a tall and lean man. His brown hair is mixed with white, and he has fierce blue-grey eyes.(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He has a gaunt, chiseled face, with high cheekbones. He is clean-shaven.(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Jason's armor is indigo chased with [silver](wiki:Silver),(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and his helm is decorated with the wings of an [eagle](wiki:Eagle).(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Jason is known for his courtesy(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) and his honor.(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) thinks he is still handsome(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) and fearless.(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord Jason travels to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), accompanied by his son [Patrek](wiki:Patrek_Mallister) and a small party of knights and squires, to participate in the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney). They travel from [Seagard](wiki:Seagard) by the way of the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad) and pass [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) and Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) near the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads), but do not recognize them.(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) During the tourney, Jason defeats Ser [Aron Santagar](wiki:Aron_Santagar) in a joust.(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

In the opening weeks of [war](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) after the death of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) identifies Lord Jason and [House Mallister](wiki:House_Mallister) as one of the only standing threats to the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) campaign in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) but Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) disregards his brother, believing Jason is not strong enough to fight alone.(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) sends [ravens](wiki:Raven) demanding that Jason and other riverlords swear allegiance to the new king, [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

Once [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) marches past the [Twins](wiki:Twins), Lord Mallister adds his strength to Robb's and takes part in the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood),(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) during which he captures Lord [Gawen Westerling](wiki:Gawen_Westerling).(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) The Mallister eagle is one of the banners spotted during the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps).(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) After their victory, Jason sits at Robb's war council, where he suggests fortifying the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) and cutting off Tywin from supplies and fresh men. Robb is proclaimed [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) at the council.(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Clash of Kings

When Robb announces his intention to send his friend, [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy), back to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) as an envoy, Catelyn unsuccessfully suggests sending Jason instead. Jason returns to [Seagard](wiki:Seagard), accompanied by his son, [Patrek Mallister](wiki:Patrek_Mallister), and Theon.(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) When he thinks his son is growing too close to the young ironman, Jason reminds Patrek whom Seagard was built to protect against.(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) As no ships from the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) await Theon on his arrival in Seagard, Jason believes the ravens Robb had sent from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) to announce Theon's return had been lost and thus he sends his own birds.(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-2)

At the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords), Jason commands the defense of four fords along the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork), including the one closest to Riverrun. Catelyn watches Jason's men defeat [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) riders, and Jason later sends a messenger to inform Riverrun of Lord [Flement Brax](wiki:Flement_Brax)'s defeat.(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

### A Storm of Swords

When Robb returns to Riverrun from the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands), Jason is cool but courteous toward Catelyn, who released Robb's captive, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), without the king's permission.(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) Jason is one of the seven [nobles](wiki:Nobility) who are chosen to launch the funeral boat of Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully) at Riverrun.(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

Jason meets Robb at [Hag's Mire](wiki:Hag's_Mire) on his march north to the [Twins](wiki:Twins), bringing with him the [captain](wiki:Captain_of_the_Myraham) of the *[Myraham](wiki:Myraham)*, who brings news of [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy)'s death. Jason is ordered by Robb to send two [Mallister](wiki:House_Mallister) longships into the [Neck](wiki:Neck), one carrying [Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover) and the other [Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont), to find [Greywater Watch](wiki:Greywater_Watch) and inform Lord [Howland Reed](wiki:Howland_Reed) of Robb's plan to retake [Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin).(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Robb intends for Catelyn to spend the rest of the war in safety with Lord Mallister at Seagard after Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) weds [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey).(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) However, Robb and Catelyn are betrayed by Houses [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) and [Frey](wiki:House_Frey) and murdered during the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Lord Jason [surrenders his castle](wiki:Siege_of_Seagard) at Seagard to [Black Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Ryman)) after he threatens to hang Jason's son, Patrek, who had been taken captive at the Red Wedding.(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) Black Walder keeps Jason as a prisoner inside his own castle, alongside his son.(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Jason and Patrek remain imprisoned at Seagard.(wiki:Jason_Mallister.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)
