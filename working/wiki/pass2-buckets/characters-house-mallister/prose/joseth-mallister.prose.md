## Origins

In [209 AC](wiki:209_AC), Ser Joseth participated in the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow). He challenged Ser [Humfrey Hardyng](wiki:Humfrey_Hardyng) in the second tilt, but was defeated and carried from the field unconscious.(wiki:Joseth_Mallister.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

## Appearances & Description

Joseth wore a winged helm. His shield bore a [silver](wiki:Silver) [eagle](wiki:Eagle) flying across an indigo sky, the arms of [House Mallister](wiki:House_Mallister).(wiki:Joseth_Mallister.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
