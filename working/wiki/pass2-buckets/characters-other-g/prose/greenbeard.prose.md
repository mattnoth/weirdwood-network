## Origins

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) learned some of [the tongue](wiki:Bastard_Valyrian) of [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) from a [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Greenbeard.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) It is unknown if he is related to Greenbeard or [the commander of Ser Forley Prester's freeriders](wiki:Tyroshi_sellsword).(wiki:Greenbeard.cite_ref-SSM_9-0)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Greenbeard](wiki:Category:Images_of_Greenbeard)*

Pello's beard is [colored](wiki:Hair_dye) bright green, but it is starting to grow out grey. He is taller and bigger than [Lem Lemoncloak](wiki:Lem).(wiki:Greenbeard.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

It is probable that Greenbeard is a deserter from the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings)(wiki:Greenbeard.cite_ref-SSM_9-1)

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) meets Greenbeard when he visits the [Inn of the Kneeling Man](wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man).(wiki:Greenbeard.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) He is among the members of the brotherhood that escort Arya to see Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion), rather than to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) as she desires.(wiki:Greenbeard.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) He speaks with Lady [Ravella Smallwood](wiki:Ravella_Smallwood) at [Acorn Hall](wiki:Acorn_Hall) and with [the ghost](wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart) at [High Heart](wiki:High_Heart).(wiki:Greenbeard.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Greenbeard is abed with [Tansy](wiki:Tansy_(innkeep)) at the [Peach](wiki:Peach) when the [Mad Huntsman](wiki:Mad_Huntsman) returns to [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept) with [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane).(wiki:Greenbeard.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Greenbeard.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) After meeting up with Lord Beric in the [hollow hill](wiki:Hollow_hill) and witnessing the [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat) of Sandor, Pello is sent with the Mad Huntsman to buy grain south of the [Mander](wiki:Mander) with the gold confiscated from the [Hound](wiki:Hound).(wiki:Greenbeard.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Greenbeard is included among the followers of the late [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion), rather than the supporters of [Lady Stoneheart](wiki:Lady_Stoneheart).(wiki:Greenbeard.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
