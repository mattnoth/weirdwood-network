## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When [war breaks out](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Lord [Lewys Lydden](wiki:Lewys_Lydden) supports King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Deep_Den.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) He is part of the army of his liege, [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), the [Lord of Casterly Rock](wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Deep_Den.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Lord_of_the_Deep_Den.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Lewys is positioned at the center of the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) host during the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork), where he fights under the command of Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Deep_Den.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Lewys is one of the great western [lords](wiki:Lord) honored at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) after the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Deep_Den.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
