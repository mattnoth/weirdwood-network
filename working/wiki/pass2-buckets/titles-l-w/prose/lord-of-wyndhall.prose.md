## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When [war breaks out](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Lord [Regenard Estren](wiki:Regenard_Estren) supports King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). He is a part of Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s force besieging [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), but is captured at the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Lord_of_Wyndhall.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Lord Estren is held captive at the [Twins](wiki:Twins).(wiki:Lord_of_Wyndhall.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Feast for Crows

Regenard is released following the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Lord_of_Wyndhall.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
