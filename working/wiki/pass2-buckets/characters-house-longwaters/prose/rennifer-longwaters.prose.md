## Origins

Rennifer first came to work in the Red Keep in [288 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Rennifer_Longwaters.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Appearances & Description

Rennifer is bentbacked and old, with spots on his head and white hairs on his chin.(wiki:Rennifer_Longwaters.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) As [chief undergaoler](wiki:Chief_undergaoler), it is his duty to keep the counts of the prisoners in the cells of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Rennifer_Longwaters.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) enquires into Rennifer's role in the escape of his brother, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister). Rennifer explains how the cells work, and boasts of having royal blood.(wiki:Rennifer_Longwaters.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) When Ser Jaime seeks out Ser [Ilyn Payne](wiki:Ilyn_Payne) before departing for the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), Rennifer lets Ser Jaime into Ser Ilyn's quarters.(wiki:Rennifer_Longwaters.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
