## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Mag Mar Tun Doh Weg](wiki:Category:Images_of_Mag_Mar_Tun_Doh_Weg)*

Mag the Mighty has a grey pelt streaked with white. His mouth is full of huge square teeth. His laughter sounds like a rumbling belch.(wiki:Mag_Mar_Tun_Doh_Weg.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The giant speaks the [Old Tongue](wiki:Old_Tongue).(wiki:Mag_Mar_Tun_Doh_Weg.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Mag is smaller than [Wun Weg Wun Dar Wun](wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun).(wiki:Mag_Mar_Tun_Doh_Weg.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Mag's [mammoth](wiki:Mammoth) has the same coloring and is larger than other mammoths, denoting its older age.(wiki:Mag_Mar_Tun_Doh_Weg.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Mag leads the [giants](wiki:Giants) who are allied with [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder)'s host of [free folk](wiki:Free_folk). [Tormund](wiki:Tormund) explains to [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) that Mag is not a king.(wiki:Mag_Mar_Tun_Doh_Weg.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

During the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), Mag the Mighty leads the assault on the gate at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), tearing off the outer door of studded [oak](wiki:Oak). Within the tunnel, Mag is struck by a dozen crossbow quarrels and stabbed by spears. Despite the defending [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), Mag is able to wrench the bars and chains of the iron gate and tear off the head of [Spotted Pate of Maidenpool](wiki:Spotted_Pate_of_Maidenpool). Although Mag crushes the spine of the last defender, [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye), the one-armed [armorer](wiki:Armorer) drives his sword into Mag's throat. It is unknown who dies first.(wiki:Mag_Mar_Tun_Doh_Weg.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Despite Tormund's explanation, Jon thinks of the late Mag as a [king of the giants](wiki:King_of_the_giants).(wiki:Mag_Mar_Tun_Doh_Weg.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Mag_Mar_Tun_Doh_Weg.cite_ref-Rasos76.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Jon and Tormund drink [mead](wiki:Mead) to honor Mag and Donal during a parley.(wiki:Mag_Mar_Tun_Doh_Weg.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
