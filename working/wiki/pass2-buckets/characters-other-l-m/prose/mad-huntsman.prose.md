## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of the Mad Huntsman](wiki:Category:Images_of_the_Mad_Huntsman)*

The Mad Huntsman is stocky, balding, and weak-chinned. He wears patched tan leathers and is considered quarrelsome.(wiki:Mad_Huntsman.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He has a wife and a sister.(wiki:Mad_Huntsman.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

The Huntsman's pack includes black mastiffs, lean wolfhands, black-and-white sheepdogs, and shaggy brindled dogs.(wiki:Mad_Huntsman.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

During the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept) is attacked by [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister). The attackers rape the Mad Huntsman's wife and his sister, torch his crops, eat half his sheep and kill the rest for spite, and slay six of his [dogs](wiki:Dog). The dog carcasses are thrown into his well.(wiki:Mad_Huntsman.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

The Mad Huntsman becomes a leader of Stoney Sept after the death of Ser [Wilbert](wiki:Wilbert) at the hands of the Lannisters. A member of the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners),(wiki:Mad_Huntsman.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) he somewhat alleviates the famine around Stoney Sept by bringing in a flock of sheep.(wiki:Mad_Huntsman.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

After Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) escapes from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), a group of [northmen](wiki:Northmen) rape and murder after failing to find him at [Tumbler's Falls](wiki:Tumbler's_Falls). The Mad Huntsman captures these northmen and imprisons them in [crow cages](wiki:Crow_cage) at Stoney Sept.(wiki:Mad_Huntsman.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

One of the sons of [Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams) joins the Mad Huntsman in search of Jaime.(wiki:Mad_Huntsman.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8) The dozen-strong party catches [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) instead, and the Huntsman intends to put the [Hound](wiki:Hound) in a crow cage. His dogs feast on the corpse of a fat northman. After a tense confrontation with [Lem](wiki:Lem), [Greenbeard](wiki:Greenbeard), and [Anguy](wiki:Anguy), however, the Mad Huntsman reluctantly agrees to take Sandor before his sometime ally,(wiki:Mad_Huntsman.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion).(wiki:Mad_Huntsman.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

After the unhappy Huntsman brings Sandor before Beric in the [hollow hill](wiki:Hollow_hill), Sandor denies the Huntsman's claims of having participated in Lannister crimes at [Sherrer](wiki:Sherrer) and [Mummer's Ford](wiki:Mummer's_Ford).(wiki:Mad_Huntsman.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) The Hound defeats the lightning lord in a [trial by battle](wiki:Trial_by_battle), but Beric is revived by [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr).(wiki:Mad_Huntsman.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

Beric sends the Mad Huntsman and Greenbeard to buy grain south of the [Mander](wiki:Mander) with gold confiscated from Sandor.(wiki:Mad_Huntsman.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows

The Huntsman is included among the followers of the late [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion), rather than the supporters of [Lady Stoneheart](wiki:Lady_Stoneheart).(wiki:Mad_Huntsman.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
