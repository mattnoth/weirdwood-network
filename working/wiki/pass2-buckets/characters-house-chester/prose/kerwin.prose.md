## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Kerwin](wiki:Category:Images_of_Kerwin)*

[Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) considers Kerwin to be girlish, with soft hands, soft cheeks and long brown curled hair. Victarion estimates him to be about twenty-two in [300 AC](wiki:300_AC).(wiki:Kerwin.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), Kerwin is [forced](wiki:Thrall) to serve [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) by his brother, King [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy). During the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet)'s voyage to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay), Kerwin is raped by four sailors. When he complains of this to Victarion, Kerwin is given a dagger to seek revenge against his abusers, though he is too timid to use it.(wiki:Kerwin.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Victarion suspects Kerwin of [poisoning](wiki:Poison) him as the maester's attempts to heal Victarion's wounded hand have all failed. [Moqorro](wiki:Moqorro) succeeds where the maester failed, so Victarion has Kerwin killed and thrown into the sea under Moqorro's direction so the fleet would gain favorable winds.(wiki:Kerwin.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
