## Origins

Ser Steffon was the second son of Lord [Connington](wiki:House_Connington) of [Griffin's Roost](wiki:Griffin's_Roost).(wiki:Steffon_Connington.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

With Lord [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon) dead after the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) and his son, Lord [Royce Baratheon](wiki:Royce_Baratheon) a mere infant, attacks from [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) raiders in the [Marches](wiki:Dornish_Marches) became increasingly common. Lady [Elenda](wiki:Elenda_Caron), [regent](wiki:Regent) of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), felt she needed a strong man's hand to keep the peace, and thus married Steffon, who was twenty years her junior.(wiki:Steffon_Connington.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

In [133 AC](wiki:133_AC) he was slain by [Wyland Wyl](wiki:Wyland_Wyl) in the [Dornish Marches](wiki:Dornish_Marches) while pursuing raiders across the marches, and Elenda was widowed once more.(wiki:Steffon_Connington.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
