## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), newly Lord Commander, looks at the seat of the Lord Commander of the Kingsguard in the [Round Room](wiki:White_Sword_Tower) and wonders how he, the Kingslayer, could belong in the company of Lords Commander past, including Alyn Connington.(wiki:Alyn_Connington.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Jaime speaks with Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) and tells him that he would do well to learn about the Kingsguard that came before then. Alyn Connington is among those he expect Loras to have already heard of.(wiki:Alyn_Connington.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
