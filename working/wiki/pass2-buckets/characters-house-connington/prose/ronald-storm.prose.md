## Appearances & Description

Ronald takes after his father in appearance. He is red-haired, and fierce.(wiki:Ronald_Storm.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Ronald is made a captive when [Griffin's Roost](wiki:Griffin's_Roost) is taken by [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) and the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company), along with [Ronnet Connington](wiki:Ronnet_Connington)'s younger siblings, [Raymund](wiki:Raymund_Connington) and [Alynne](wiki:Alynne_Connington). Jon feels that the three will make useful hostages should Ronnet attempt to retake the castle. When Jon orders that Ronald and the other young Conningtons be confined under guard, Ronald tries to bite the spearman closest to him. That night, Jon has him and the other young Conningtons as guests at the victory feast, in hopes of getting to know his relatives. However, when Ronald tells Jon that his father will kill him, Jon sends the children back to their cells.(wiki:Ronald_Storm.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
