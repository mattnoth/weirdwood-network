## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

During the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), Eldred dances the [finger dance](wiki:Finger_dance) with [Qarl the Maid](wiki:Qarl_the_Maid). One of his fingers is cut off and lands in [Ralf the Limper](wiki:Ralf_the_Limper)'s drink.(wiki:Eldred_Codd.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) After [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) and [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) confront each other, Eldred departs the tent with his bloodied hand.(wiki:Eldred_Codd.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
