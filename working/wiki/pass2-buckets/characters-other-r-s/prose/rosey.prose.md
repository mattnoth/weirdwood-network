## Narrative Arc

### A Feast For Crows

Early in [300 AC](wiki:300_AC), Rosey is fifteen years old and freshly [flowered](wiki:Flowered). Her mother, [Emma](wiki:Emma), is demanding a [gold dragon](wiki:Currency) for her [maidenhead](wiki:Maiden). [Pate](wiki:Pate_(novice)), a novice at the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel), is infatuated with her and desires to pay for Emma's price but he does not have enough money. Rosey introduces Pate to the [Alchemist](wiki:Alchemist).(wiki:Rosey.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
