## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Ryles is part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) which leaves [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) and the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) in search of [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark) and [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder). At some point he dies and is turned into a [wight](wiki:Wight). His body attacks [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) and [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) at an abandoned [wildling](wiki:Wildling) village, and they only escape with the help of [Coldhands](wiki:Coldhands).(wiki:Ryles.cite_ref-Rasos46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
