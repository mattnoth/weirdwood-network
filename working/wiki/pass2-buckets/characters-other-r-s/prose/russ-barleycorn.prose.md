## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Russ sails aboard his *Storm Crow* to rescue the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) at [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome). During the voyage, his crew sees the wreckage of a galley along the coast of [Skagos](wiki:Skagos).(wiki:Russ_Barleycorn.cite_ref-Radwd21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Russ arrives at destination but the desperate wildlings try to take his ship by force. They are successfully repelled at the cost of the lives of six black brothers, however.(wiki:Russ_Barleycorn.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) It is unknown if Russ is among the dead.
