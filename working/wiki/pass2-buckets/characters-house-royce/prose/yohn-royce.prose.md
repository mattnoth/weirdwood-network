## Origins

Yohn took part in the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal), where he was defeated by Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen).(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Yohn also participated in the [tourney at Lannisport](wiki:Tourney_at_Lannisport), where he was defeated by the eventual champion, Lord [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont).(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) He defeated [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr) in the melee at the [tourney on Prince Joffrey's name day](wiki:Tourney_on_Prince_Joffrey's_name_day) with a common mace, after the [fire](wiki:Wildfire) of Thoros's sword had guttered out.(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) offered his son [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) for marriage within House Royce, but was rejected.(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

When Yohn's youngest son, Ser [Waymar Royce](wiki:Waymar_Royce), joined the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) in [296 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), his father escorted him north. They stayed in [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) on their way, where Yohn participated in hunts, and spoke with Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark). Bronze Yohn practiced sparring with Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) and Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel), defeating one and then the other.(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)

## Appearances & Description

Yohn's face is lined, and he has grey hair, slate-grey eyes, and bushy eyebrows. He is as tall as [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane), and has large, gnarled hands.(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Bronze Yohn is a proud(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and formidable(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) man, with a booming voice,(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) but the noble lord can also be solemn and quiet.(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

Lord Royce is a tourney [knight](wiki:Knight)(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) who dons a set of [bronze](wiki:Bronze) armor while competing. The armor is said to be thousands of years old, and its inscribed [runes](wiki:Runes) can allegedly ward its owner from harm.(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

During a ranging [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall), Yohn's youngest son, Ser [Waymar Royce](wiki:Waymar_Royce), is killed by [Others](wiki:Others) in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest).(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Ragotprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Yohn attends the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), participating with his sons, Ser [Andar](wiki:Andar_Royce) and Ser [Robar](wiki:Robar_Royce).(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2) He remains at court, with a retinue of [knights](wiki:Knight) and [sworn swords](wiki:Sworn_swords).(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Yohn participates in King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s hunt in the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood) for a white hart.(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), sends Robar to the kingswood to inform his father and Robert of Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane)'s raids on the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) After the hart's carcass is found, the Royces return to the capital, but Robert remains in the forest to hunt a monstrous [boar](wiki:Boar).(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

As Robert lies dying in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) after being gored by the boar, Bronze Yohn is named by Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin), as a possible ally for Eddard against Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) in the matter of the succession, though ultimately Ned chooses to rely on the [gold cloaks](wiki:Gold_cloaks).(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Petyr betrays the plan to Cersei, however.(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

During King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon)'s first court session after Ned's imprisonment, Lord Royce and his sons are listed by [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) among those required to swear fealty to the new king.(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Robar is slain by an enraged Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) after [the murder](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)) of King [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon).(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Yohn is mentioned as a suitor of Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) by Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister).(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) According to Lysa herself, Yohn is pressuring her into participating in the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings)(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) to support [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-18)

### A Feast for Crows

After the death of Lysa, Lord Yohn brings five other nobles of the Vale—[Benedar Belmore](wiki:Benedar_Belmore), [Gilwood Hunter](wiki:Gilwood_Hunter), [Horton Redfort](wiki:Horton_Redfort), [Symond Templeton](wiki:Symond_Templeton), and [Anya Waynwood](wiki:Anya_Waynwood)—to [Runestone](wiki:Runestone), where they sign a document forming the [Lords Declarant](wiki:Lords_Declarant). Yohn's cousin, Lord [Nestor Royce](wiki:Nestor_Royce), informs Petyr Baelish about Yohn's intent to question [Marillion](wiki:Marillion) about the murder of Lysa, and to remove Petyr from his position as [Lord Protector of the Vale](wiki:Lord_Protector_of_the_Vale).(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

The Lords Declarant surround the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) with their hosts. Along with Nestor and Ser [Lyn Corbray](wiki:Lyn_Corbray), they ascend the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance) to meet with Lord Baelish at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie). [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) worries that Yohn may recognize her from his visit to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) a few years earlier, but Nestor tells his cousin that she is Petyr's [natural](wiki:Bastardy) daughter, Alayne Stone. Yohn declares his intent to foster the young [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn), [Lord of the Eyrie](wiki:Lord_of_the_Eyrie), at [Runestone](wiki:Runestone) as his [ward](wiki:Ward) and [squire](wiki:Squire), which Petyr is reluctant to support. When Lyn draws [Lady Forlorn](wiki:Lady_Forlorn) against Ser [Lothor Brune](wiki:Lothor_Brune), an angry Yohn intervenes to prevent bloodshed. Still, Petyr blames the Lords Declarant for Lyn baring steel during a parley. When the other Lords Declarant allow Lord Baelish a year more to prove himself as Lord Protector, Yohn departs the Eyrie in anger. Afterwards, Petyr reveals to Sansa that Lyn is secretly in his employ. Petyr believes Yohn will be the only member of the Lords Declarant remaining opposed to his plans, but Lord Royce should not be a threat by himself.(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5)

Bronze Yohn holds [a small melee](wiki:Mêlée_at_Runestone) at Runestone only for squires. [Harrold Hardyng](wiki:Harrold_Hardyng), Lord Robert's cousin and heir, wins the melee, and Yohn [knights](wiki:Knight) him as his reward. Lord Royce's daughter, [Ysilla](wiki:Ysilla_Royce), marries Ser [Mychel Redfort](wiki:Mychel_Redfort), which upsets [Mya Stone](wiki:Mya_Stone).(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

News reaches [Sisterton](wiki:Sisterton) and [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) about Lord Yohn vowing to remove Petyr from power in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn).(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Lord Baelish is convinced Yohn warned Ser Harrold against him before the young knight travels to the Gates of the Moon to participate in the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) for the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights).(wiki:Yohn_Royce.cite_ref-twow_21-0)
