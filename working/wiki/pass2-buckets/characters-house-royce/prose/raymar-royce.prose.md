## Origins

One of his younger sons, [Benedict Royce](wiki:Benedict_Royce), married [Jocelyn Stark](wiki:Jocelyn_Stark), a sister of [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s grandfather Lord [Edwyle](wiki:Edwyle_Stark). They had three daughters, one of which married a [Corbray](wiki:House_Corbray), another a [Waynwood](wiki:House_Waynwood) and the third might have married a [Templeton](wiki:House_Templeton).(wiki:Raymar_Royce.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
