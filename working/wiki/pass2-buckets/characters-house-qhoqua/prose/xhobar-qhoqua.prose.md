## Origins

The [Band of Nine](wiki:Band_of_Nine) were a group of merchants, sellswords and pirates who joined together to carve kingdoms out for themselves. After conquering the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands), they took over [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh).(wiki:Xhobar_Qhoqua.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Next, they seized the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones), from where they stood poised to attack [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). The Band of Nine was defeated during the [War of the Ninepenny Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Ninepenny_Kings).(wiki:Xhobar_Qhoqua.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
