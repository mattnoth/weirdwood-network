## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

According to [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn), Septon Luceon hosted a supper for thirty of the [Most Devout](wiki:Most_Devout) in an effort to buy their votes in the election of a new [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon).(wiki:Luceon_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He becomes the leading candidate after the [sparrows](wiki:Sparrows) follow Septon [Ollidor](wiki:Ollidor) to a [brothel](wiki:Brothel) and drag him out into the street naked.(wiki:Luceon_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Qyburn's whisperers later report that Luceon was nine votes short of being chosen High Septon when the sparrows overran the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) with axes in hand and their leader on their shoulders. They forced the Most Devout to elect the sparrows' leader, an as yet unnamed individual who became the new High Septon, mockingly called the [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow) by the higher echelons of society in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Luceon_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
