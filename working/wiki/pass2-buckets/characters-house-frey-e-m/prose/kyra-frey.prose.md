## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

During the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) kills Kyra's brother, Ser [Tytos Frey](wiki:Tytos_Frey), by putting an axe through his head.(wiki:Kyra_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Kyra_Frey.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Kyra_Frey.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Kyra becomes a widow that night as her husband, Ser [Garse Goodbrook](wiki:Garse_Goodbrook), dies outside of the [Twins](wiki:Twins) during the fighting. Sandor may be Garse's killer as well.(wiki:Kyra_Frey.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Kyra_Frey.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Kyra loses her last remaining parent when her father, Ser [Jared Frey](wiki:Jared_Frey), disappears in the [North](wiki:North) under mysterious circumstances on his way from [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) to the wedding of "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)" to [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow).(wiki:Kyra_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Kyra_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
