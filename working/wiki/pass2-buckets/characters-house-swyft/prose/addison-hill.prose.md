## Origins

In [10 AC](wiki:10_AC), Ser Addison was chosen by Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) to be one of the first [knights](wiki:Knight) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) when the order was founded by King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Addison_Hill.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) He would serve his king with honor and valor until the end of his life.(wiki:Addison_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Despite his [bastard](wiki:Bastard) origin, Addison eventually rose to become [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard).(wiki:Addison_Hill.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) It is unknown when it happened, however. It could have been during the reign of Aegon I or during one of his sons's, [Aenys I](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen) and [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen).

## Appearances & Description

Addison is remembered for being quick, strong, observant, and famed for his prowess with [sword](wiki:Sword) and shield. He was devoted to his king.(wiki:Addison_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
