## Origins

After Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) arrives at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to deliver [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s terms for peace, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) has his agent, [Bronn](wiki:Bronn), search for a [mummer](wiki:Mummer), a [poisoner](wiki:Poison), a murderer, and a thief in the low places of the capital. Tyrion has them disguised as [red cloaks](wiki:House_Lannister_guards) and places them along Cleos's honor guard, which comes from Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s guard and are led by [Vylarr](wiki:Vylarr). Tyrion has [Varys](wiki:Varys) convince the queen of going along with Tyrion's plan—which leaves her without men under her direct command—by telling her that the four infiltrators will be unnoticed among the hundred men.(wiki:Plot_to_free_Jaime_Lannister.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Aftermath

Edmure has Tyrion's recruits and the red cloaks that aided Jaime hanged from the walls of Riverrun. The rest of the honor guard are thrown in the dungeons. Jaime is also imprisoned in a dark cell, chained hand and foot and bolted to a dungeon wall. Cleos Frey, who was ignorant of the plot, is imprisoned in Jaime's old tower cell.(wiki:Plot_to_free_Jaime_Lannister.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) and her escorts see the hanged plotters when they return to Riverrun from [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)).(wiki:Plot_to_free_Jaime_Lannister.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
