## Origins

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) is sent to [Pyke](wiki:Pyke) as an envoy of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), offering his father [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), an alliance and a strike against the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister). Balon rejects the offer, however, and instead plans to strike at the vulnerable north.(wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) As part of the plan, Theon, accompanied by [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) and [Dagmer Cleftjaw](wiki:Dagmer), is to take eight longships and harry the [Stony Shore](wiki:Stony_Shore), sinking any ship chance met.(wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Theon and his party attack a fishing village in the dark of the early morning, slaying most of the men and taking the women for [salt wives](wiki:Salt_wives) or [thralls](wiki:Thrall). A few men are allowed to escape and inform [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Torrhen's_Square).(wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Aftermath

After defeating the Wild Hares, Theon gives six of his eight longships to Aeron so he can continue harrying the coast, while the crew of Theon's *[Sea Bitch](wiki:Sea_Bitch)* and [Dagmer](wiki:Dagmer)'s *[Foamdrinker](wiki:Foamdrinker)* advance inland.(wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)(wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

By killing Benfred's party, the true size of the assault by the ironmen is kept secret a little longer, which allows the [taking of Deepwood Motte](wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte) and an [attack on Torrhen's Square](wiki:Fight_at_Torrhen's_Square).
