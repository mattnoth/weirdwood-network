## Origins

The [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) submitted to [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), thus losing their title of [King of Mountain and Vale](wiki:King_of_Mountain_and_Vale). The Targaryens allowed the boy king [Ronnel Arryn](wiki:Ronnel_Arryn_(king)) to remain the ruler of the region henceforth as [Lord of the Eyrie](wiki:Lord_of_the_Eyrie) and [Warden of the East](wiki:Warden_of_the_East).(wiki:Jonos_Arryn's_rebellion.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Aftermath

[Hubert Arryn](wiki:Hubert_Arryn), a cousin of Ronnel and Jonos, was installed as the new Lord of the Eyrie and Warden of the East. Lord Allard approved of this choice as Hubert had married a [Royce of Runestone](wiki:House_Royce) and had already six sons by her. For his efforts to put down the rebellion, Lord Royce was rewarded with [gold](wiki:Gold), titles, and honors by King Aenys.(wiki:Jonos_Arryn's_rebellion.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
