## Origins

Oppo was the son of [Hop-Bean](wiki:Hop-Bean). Oppo and his sister [Penny](wiki:Penny) make their living as entertainers who ride atop a pig and a dog while mock fighting each other. Oppo rides the Pig. During these fights they used the names Groat and Penny after [the two smallest coins](wiki:Currency).

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Oppo and [Penny](wiki:Penny) were hired by a man known as [Oswell](wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack) to entertain the guests in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) during [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon)'s wedding to [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) by riding a pig and a dog while in painted wooden armor.(wiki:Oppo.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Unknown to the pair this stunt is arranged by [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) in order to provoke a quarrel between [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and Joffrey in order to add further false evidence against the dwarf when he would be accused of [Joffrey's murder](wiki:Purple_Wedding).

### A Dance with Dragons

[Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) sees Oppo and his sister perform whilst in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis).(wiki:Oppo.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Oppo is later killed by sailors from [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) who believe him to be [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Oppo.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### The Winds of Winter

At the start of the [Battle of Slaver's Bay](wiki:Second_Siege_of_Meereen), as they ready themselves, [Penny](wiki:Penny) tells Tyrion Oppo thought [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) was beautiful when he saw her when they jousted for King [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). Tyrion thinks privately to himself that Groat was a stunted little lickspittle with a stupid name.(wiki:Oppo.cite_ref-5)
