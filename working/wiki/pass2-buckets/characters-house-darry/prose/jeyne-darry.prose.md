## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Jeyne's relative [Lyman Darry](wiki:Lyman_Darry) is killed by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Jeyne_Darry.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) His death extinguishes the male line of House Darry.(wiki:Jeyne_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Jeyne's husband, Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey), is killed while escorting his cousin Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Jeyne_Darry.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Feast for Crows

According to [Genna Lannister](wiki:Genna_Lannister) Jeyne is furious that the lordship of [Darry](wiki:Darry) was not given to her sons but to [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister) and his wife [Amerei Frey](wiki:Amerei_Frey), the daughter of Jeyne's sister [Mariya Darry](wiki:Mariya_Darry). As the Darry male line is extinct the Darry lordship is seen as safe. As heirs of Cleos Frey and grandsons of [Emmon Frey](wiki:Emmon_Frey), Jeyne's sons are first in line to inherit [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun). The [Tullys](wiki:House_Tully), although dispossessed of Riverrun in favor of Emmon, are still alive and thus remain a threat to the [new owners](wiki:House_Frey_of_Riverrun).(wiki:Jeyne_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
