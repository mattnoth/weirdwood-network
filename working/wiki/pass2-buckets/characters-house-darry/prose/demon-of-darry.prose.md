## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

When looking around the [White Sword Tower](wiki:White_Sword_Tower) after his ascension to the position of Lord Commander, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) studied the black [oak](wiki:Oak) seat of the Lord Commander and wondered how he, the Kingslayer, could be counted among such exalted company as the Demon of Darry and several other famed Lords Commander.(wiki:Demon_of_Darry.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Feast for Crows

When discussing with the young Kingsguard knight Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) what he knows of those who served before him, Jaime mentions the Demon of Darry among the most famed past members of the Kingsguard before turning to those of more ignominious or unremarkable service.(wiki:Demon_of_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
