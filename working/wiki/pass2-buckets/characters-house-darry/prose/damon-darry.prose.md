## Origins

During the [regency of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III), Ser [Tyland Lannister](wiki:Tyland_Lannister), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), sent fifty men with Ser [Regis Groves](wiki:Regis_Groves) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) to suppress the rabble of [Alys Rivers](wiki:Alys_Rivers) at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal). Regis was joined at [Castle Darry](wiki:Castle_Darry) with another fifty men led by Ser Damon.(wiki:Damon_Darry.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Alys refused to surrender Harrenhal, however, and Regis was killed in the fighting which ensued. Only thirty-two of the king's men reached the safety of Darry. Alys sent another survivor as a messenger to Castle Darry, claiming he was cursed and doomed to die if anyone laughed at his message. Although Damon reassured the messenger, one of Lord Darry's men did laugh at the messenger's talk of [dragons](wiki:Dragon) and the envoy is said to have abruptly choked to death. Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake), one of the [regents](wiki:Regent), discounted Damon's talk of [sorcery](wiki:Sorcery).(wiki:Damon_Darry.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
