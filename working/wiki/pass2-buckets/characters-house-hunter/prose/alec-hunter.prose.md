## Origins

Alec travelled to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to attend the [Golden Wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC). King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) took the opportunity to entertain the assembled lords in the intimacy of his [solar](wiki:Solar), encouraging them to speak freely and share their views on the problems of the [realm](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) and how they might best be overcome.
