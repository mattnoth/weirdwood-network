## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

After Lord [Eon Hunter](wiki:Eon_Hunter) dies suddenly, Harlan and his brother Ser [Eustace](wiki:Eustace_Hunter) accuse their eldest brother, [Gilwood](wiki:Gilwood_Hunter), of murder.(wiki:Harlan_Hunter.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Feast for Crows

According to [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), Harlan was responsible for the death of his father and believes Lord Gilwood will soon be murdered by his brothers, most likely Ser Harlan.(wiki:Harlan_Hunter.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
