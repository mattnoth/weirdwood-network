## Origins

After the death of his son, Prince [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aerys_II)), King Aerys fasted for a fortnight and made a walk of repentance across [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor), where he prayed with the High Septon. From that point on, Aerys vowed to remain faithful to his sister-wife, Queen [Rhaella](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen).
