## Origins

During the reign of King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen), the [old High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Baelor_I)) died, and King Baelor informed the [Most Devout](wiki:Most_Devout) that [the Seven](wiki:The_Seven) had revealed the new High Septon's identity to him. They promptly elected Baelor's choice to the office, a [common-born](wiki:Smallfolk) stonemason named Pate.(wiki:High_Septon_(stonemason).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Pate could carve stonework so beautifully that Baelor believed him to be [the Smith](wiki:Smith_(the_Seven)) in human form.(wiki:High_Septon_(stonemason).cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) While a fantastic stonecarver, this High Septon was [simple](wiki:Simple),(wiki:High_Septon_(stonemason).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) and could not read or write, nor remember or recite any prayers, and thus was ill suited to his new vocation as High Septon.(wiki:High_Septon_(stonemason).cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)(wiki:High_Septon_(stonemason).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

He served as High Septon only for a year before a fever took him.(wiki:High_Septon_(stonemason).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) It was rumored that Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen) used his position as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to have him [poisoned](wiki:Poison), thereby sparing the realm the embarrassment of an ineffectual High Septon.(wiki:High_Septon_(stonemason).cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
