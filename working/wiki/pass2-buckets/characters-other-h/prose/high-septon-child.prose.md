## Origins

His appointment was made by King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen) who informed the [Most Devout](wiki:Most_Devout) that the Seven had revealed the new High Septon's identity to him after the sudden death of his [predecessor](wiki:High_Septon_(stonemason)). The boy was said to be a street urchin or the son of a draper, but Baelor claimed he saw the boy perform miracles, that the boy spoke to doves and they answered him back in the voice of the gods. Despite their misgivings the [Most Devout](wiki:Most_Devout) agreed to the King's request since Baelor did so much for the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).
