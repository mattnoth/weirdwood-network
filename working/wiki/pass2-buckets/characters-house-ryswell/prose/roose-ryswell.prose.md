## Appearances & Description

Roose and his brothers [Roger](wiki:Roger_Ryswell) and [Rickard](wiki:Rickard_Ryswell) are said to be quarrelsome. They each take a horsehead of a different color for their sigil, the jape being that the [Ryswells](wiki:House_Ryswell) cannot even agree upon the color of their arms.(wiki:Roose_Ryswell.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Along with the rest of [House Ryswell](wiki:House_Ryswell), Roose attends [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark) of Ramsay to "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)" (actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)) in [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) mentions to [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) that Roose Ryswell is his [namesake](wiki:Named_after).(wiki:Roose_Ryswell.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Roose sends scouts out the [Hunter's Gate](wiki:Hunter's_Gate) of Winterfell to locate the army of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood), but none have returned yet.(wiki:Roose_Ryswell.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
