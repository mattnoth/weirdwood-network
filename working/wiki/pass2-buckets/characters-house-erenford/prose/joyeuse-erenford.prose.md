## Appearances & Description

Joyeuse is a pale and wispy young woman.(wiki:Joyeuse_Erenford.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Joyeuse_Erenford.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

The sixteen-year-old Joyeuse marries [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey), [Lord of the Crossing](wiki:Lord_of_the_Crossing) and seven times a widower, on his ninetieth [name day](wiki:Name_day).(wiki:Joyeuse_Erenford.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Though invited to [Joyeuse and Walder's wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Walder_Frey_and_Joyeuse_Erenford), the ailing Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), [Lord of Riverrun](wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun), does not attend.(wiki:Joyeuse_Erenford.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Joyeuse is present when [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) and [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident), presents himself at the [Twins](wiki:Twins) before Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully)'s wedding to [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey), Walder's fifth daughter. When Lord Walder has his kinswomen present themselves so that Robb can make amends after having married [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling) instead of a Frey, Joyeuse prevents [Jinglebell](wiki:Aegon_Frey_(son_of_Stevron)) from joining the women.(wiki:Joyeuse_Erenford.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Joyeuse dances with Robb during the wedding feast.(wiki:Joyeuse_Erenford.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The Freys then betray [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) with the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Joyeuse_Erenford.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Joyeuse is pregnant.(wiki:Joyeuse_Erenford.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
