## Origins

King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) visited Ashford on multiple occasions during his [royal progresses](wiki:Royal_progress).(wiki:Ashford.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Septon [Alfyn](wiki:Alfyn) was at Ashford when he learned he had been selected as [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon).(wiki:Ashford.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

In [209 AC](wiki:209_AC) the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) was held at the nearby [grassy field](wiki:Ashford_Meadow) south of the [Cockleswent](wiki:Cockleswent).(wiki:Ashford.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7) [Lord Ashford](wiki:Lord_Ashford) turned his chambers over to [Baelor Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)), [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone), during the tourney. After being killed in a [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven), Baelor Breakspear was cremated in the yard of Ashford Castle.(wiki:Ashford.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8)

The [Battle of Ashford](wiki:Battle_of_Ashford) in [282 AC](wiki:282_AC) was the only defeat of [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End), during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). Commanding the vanguard, [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly), [Lord of Horn Hill](wiki:Lord_of_Horn_Hill), slew [Lord Cafferen](wiki:Lord_Cafferen) at Ashford Castle(wiki:Ashford.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) and forced Robert to retreat before [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), [Lord of Highgarden](wiki:Lord_of_Highgarden), arrived with the main force. Despite Randyll's leadership, his liege Mace took credit for the indecisive victory.(wiki:Ashford.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Ashford.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Appearances & Description

Ashford is an appealing market town whose whitewashed houses have thatched roofs.(wiki:Ashford.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Ashford Castle is a small castle built in the shape of a triangle, with round towers thirty feet tall at each point. Between the towers run thick crenelated walls. The orange banners of [House Ashford](wiki:House_Ashford) fly from its battlements.(wiki:Ashford.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) Wool tapestries of fruits and flowers hang in its [Great Hall](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Ashford). A tower cell has a view of town, field, and forest.(wiki:Ashford.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

Nearby [Ashford Meadow](wiki:Ashford_Meadow) is used as a commons for the townspeople.(wiki:Ashford.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) During the reign of King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen), [an inn](wiki:Inn_near_Ashford) was found a day's ride to the south past a burned mill.(wiki:Ashford.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)
