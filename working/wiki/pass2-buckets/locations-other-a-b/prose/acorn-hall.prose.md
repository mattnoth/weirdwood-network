## Origins

King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) visited Acorn Hall atop [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor) in [53 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Acorn_Hall.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

A [battle at Acorn Hall](wiki:Battle_at_Acorn_Hall) occurred in [130 AC](wiki:130_AC) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Acorn_Hall.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Appearances & Description

Acorn Hall is a small castle with stone curtain walls and a large [oak](wiki:Oak) keep. Near the kennels is a smithy whose forge has not been used for a considerable amount of time.(wiki:Acorn_Hall.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

During the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Lord [Theomar Smallwood](wiki:Theomar_Smallwood) is away fighting for his liege lord, Lord Vance, and in his absence, the gates of the keep are closed and barred and few men remain to hold it.(wiki:Acorn_Hall.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) and the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners)—including [Tom Sevenstrings](wiki:Tom_Sevenstrings), who had once been Lady [Ravella Smallwood](wiki:Ravella_Smallwood)'s lover—travel through Acorn Hall where Lady Smallwood welcomes them, opening the keep's gates for them to rest there. Ravella notices Arya's dirtiness and forces her to take a bath and clean herself, without knowing Arya's identity. Afterwards, Ravella chats with Arya discussing tastes and the courtesies of a lady after discovering that Arya is highborn.(wiki:Acorn_Hall.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

The brotherhood feast with Arya and Lady Smallwood in the keep's hall. They ask the lady if she has word of Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) and soon everyone begins chatting with each other. Ravella mentions that Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) escaped [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) and some [Karstark](wiki:House_Karstark) men had gone through Acorn Hall looking for Jaime.

After the feast, [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) and Arya go to the forge where they talk about [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr), and Gendry compliments Arya for her new, more highborn looks. The day afterwards, Lady Smallwood makes Arya take another bath before parting ways with the little lady and the brotherhood without banners, who proceed with their journey to find Lord Beric.(wiki:Acorn_Hall.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
