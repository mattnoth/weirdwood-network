## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Shadrich](wiki:Category:Images_of_Shadrich)*

Ser Shadrich is a wiry, fox-faced man, with a sharp nose and bristly orange hair.(wiki:Shadrich.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Though he is not more than five foot two,(wiki:Shadrich.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) short enough to be mistaken for a boy, he has the face of an older man, with wrinkles by his mouth, and hard eyes. He also has a scar beneath his ear.(wiki:Shadrich.cite_ref-twow_5-0)

Shadrich is not a [tourney](wiki:Tourney) knight, instead saving his valor for the battlefield. He has a cocksure manner, and is sharp-witted.(wiki:Shadrich.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Shadrich's shield depicts his [sigil](wiki:Heraldry), a large white [mouse](wiki:Mouse) with red eyes on bendy brown and blue.(wiki:Shadrich.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) The hedge knight rides a rangy chestnut [courser](wiki:Horse).(wiki:Shadrich.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Ser Shadrich fought for [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). After Stannis's defeat, Shadrich was forced to pay a ransom he could not afford, meaning that he was ruined financially. Shadrich becomes a hired knight for the merchant [Hibald](wiki:Hibald) and his serving men.(wiki:Shadrich.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

While Shadrich is escorting Hibald's party to [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale), they are joined by [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth), Ser [Creighton Longbough](wiki:Creighton_Longbough), and Ser [Illifer the Penniless](wiki:Illifer_the_Penniless). When Brienne says she is searching for her lost sister, Shadrich realizes that it is [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) whom Brienne seeks. Shadrich explains [Varys](wiki:Varys) is offering a plump bag of [gold](wiki:Gold) for Sansa, which would return him to the status he enjoyed before the Blackwater.(wiki:Shadrich.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8) Shadrich offers to split the reward, but Brienne carries on pretending she is seeking her sister. Shadrich becomes sarcastic, as he knows she is lying.(wiki:Shadrich.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)

After Brienne abandons her traveling companions at the [Old Stone Bridge](wiki:Old_Stone_Bridge),(wiki:Shadrich.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10) she worries that Shadrich might be following her. It is actually [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne) who follows Brienne after Duskendale, however.(wiki:Shadrich.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Ser Shadrich eventually turns up at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon), as a newly-hired hedge knight in service to Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish). Along with Ser [Byron the Beautiful](wiki:Byron_the_Beautiful) and Ser [Morgarth the Merry](wiki:Morgarth_the_Merry), Shadrich is introduced to Alayne Stone (a disguised Sansa Stark) in the west tower of the Gates.(wiki:Shadrich.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### The Winds of Winter

When Alayne is greeting guests the day before the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) for the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights) is to be held at the Gates of the Moon, she bumps into Ser Shadrich, but he prevents her from falling. He tells her and [Myranda Royce](wiki:Myranda_Royce) that he does not intend to participate in the [joust](wiki:Joust). When Alayne asks if he might try the [melee](wiki:Melee) instead, he replies that hedge knights hope for melees, if they can't find a bag of [dragons](wiki:Currency). Later, at the feast, Alayne dances with Shadrich, among others.(wiki:Shadrich.cite_ref-twow_5-1)
