## Appearances & Description

Porridge is broad and squat, with thick shoulders and large, strong hands. He is strong as a bull.(wiki:Porridge.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He is kinder than his fellow gaoler, [Lamprey](wiki:Lamprey_(gaoler)).(wiki:Porridge.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

While Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) is imprisoned in the dungeon beneath [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), Porridge is one of his gaolers. He brings Davos a bowl of oaten porridge for breakfast, for which Davos gives him his nickname. Davos suspects he feeds the rats in the dungeon. He thinks he hears Porridge talking to the rats as if they were children, but may have only dreamed that.(wiki:Porridge.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
