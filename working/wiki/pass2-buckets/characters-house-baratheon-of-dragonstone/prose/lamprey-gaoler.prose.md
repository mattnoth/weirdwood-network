## Appearances & Description

Lamprey is stooped and has yellowish skin. He has greasy unwashed hair and pebbled skin. He is a brusque gaoler.(wiki:Lamprey_(gaoler).cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Lamprey wears a doublet of white velvet with a ring of golden stars upon it.(wiki:Lamprey_(gaoler).cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) He may thus have been a servant of [House Sunglass](wiki:House_Sunglass) or took it from someone affiliated with that House, as Lord [Guncer Sunglass](wiki:Guncer_Sunglass) was burned by [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre).(wiki:Lamprey_(gaoler).cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

While Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) is imprisoned beneath [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), one of his gaolers is Lamprey. Though not his real name, he receives the nickname Lamprey because he once brings Davos half a [lamprey](wiki:Lamprey) pie. Davos finds the other gaoler, [Porridge](wiki:Porridge), to be more friendly than Lamprey.(wiki:Lamprey_(gaoler).cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
