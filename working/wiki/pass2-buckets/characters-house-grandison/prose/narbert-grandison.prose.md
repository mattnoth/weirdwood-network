## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

As one of the queen's sworn swords,(wiki:Narbert_Grandison.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Ser Narbert travels with Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) from [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea) to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Narbert_Grandison.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Narbert yawns during the [wedding of Sigorn and Alys Karstark](wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark), and he dances with each of Selyse's lady companions at the feast afterwards.(wiki:Narbert_Grandison.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

The knight is present when Selyse refuses [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), aid in rescuing the [wildlings](wiki:Free_folk) stranded at [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome). When Jon informs the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) and the free folk about his plans for Hardhome and [the letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) he has received from [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), Narbet and Ser [Benethon Scales](wiki:Benethon_Scales) stand near the hall's door.(wiki:Narbert_Grandison.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
