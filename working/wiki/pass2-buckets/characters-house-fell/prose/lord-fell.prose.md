## Origins

When Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) returned to [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) after the [taking of Gulltown](wiki:Taking_of_Gulltown) and called his banners to rebel against King [Aerys II](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), Lord Fell remained loyal to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) and marched against his liege lord. He was to be joined by [Lord Cafferen](wiki:Lord_Cafferen) and [Lord Grandison](wiki:Lord_Grandison) at [Summerhall](wiki:Summerhall), but Robert, having caught wind of their plan, marched to meet them separately. In one of the ensuing [battles at Summerhall](wiki:Battles_at_Summerhall), Robert slew Lord Fell in single combat and took his son [Silveraxe](wiki:Silveraxe_Fell) as prisoner.(wiki:Lord_Fell.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
