## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) asks Lord Lynderly to send a son to ward at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) and act as Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn)'s squire, so Jon sends Terrance. On the day of departure from the Eyrie for the descent to the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon), Terrance is given the duties of laying out the clothes for Robert.(wiki:Terrance_Lynderly.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
