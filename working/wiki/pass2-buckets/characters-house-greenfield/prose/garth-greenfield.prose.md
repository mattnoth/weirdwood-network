## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Garth is part of the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) host besieging [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun). He is taken captive during the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Garth_Greenfield.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Garth is held captive at [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall).(wiki:Garth_Greenfield.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) goes to Raventree Hall and negotiates the surrender of Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood).(wiki:Garth_Greenfield.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Garth is presumably freed from captivity.
