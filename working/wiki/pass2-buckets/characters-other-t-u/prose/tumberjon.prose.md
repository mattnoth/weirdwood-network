## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Tumberjon is part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) that leaves [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) to search for [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings). He is one of the scouts commanded by [Thoren Smallwood](wiki:Thoren_Smallwood) who are sent up the [Frostfangs](wiki:Frostfangs) to find the wildlings. Tumberjon wants to put an arrow into [Harma the Dogshead](wiki:Harma), but is ordered not to by Thoren.(wiki:Tumberjon.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

Tumberjon possibly returns to Castle Black and then departs with [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) to confront the [Weeper](wiki:Weeper). The brothers of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) left to defend the [Wall](wiki:Wall) from [the wildling assault](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall) name straw dummies after missing brothers, including [Eddison Tollett](wiki:Eddison_Tollett), [Othell Yarwyck](wiki:Othell_Yarwyck), [Watt of Long Lake](wiki:Watt_of_Long_Lake), and Tumberjon.(wiki:Tumberjon.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
