## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter

Two Swords takes part in the Golden Company's [invasion](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company) of the [Stormlands](wiki:Stormlands).(wiki:Two_Swords_(sellsword).cite_ref-Ar2_1-2) While accompanying Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell)'s embassy to [Griffin's Roost](wiki:Griffin's_Roost), [Chain](wiki:Chain) reveals some details of his fellow sellswords to her, including Two Sword's taste in women.(wiki:Two_Swords_(sellsword).cite_ref-Ar2_1-3)
