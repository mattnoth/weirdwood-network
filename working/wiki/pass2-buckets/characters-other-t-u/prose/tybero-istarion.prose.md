## Appearances & Description

Inkpots is plump and balding, with ink stains on his fingers.(wiki:Tybero_Istarion.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He usually speaks in a mild tone.(wiki:Tybero_Istarion.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btyrion_ii_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctyrion_ii.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

The [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons) go over to the [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai)'i when [Brown Ben Plumm](wiki:Ben_Plumm) thinks [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s cause is lost.(wiki:Tybero_Istarion.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) meet Tybero when they join the Second Sons outside [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). When Tyrion writes out notes promising large sums of gold to several high ranking members in the company, Inkpots is promised ten thousand [golden dragons](wiki:Currency).(wiki:Tybero_Istarion.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Ben assigns Tyrion to work with Tybero.(wiki:Tybero_Istarion.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### The Winds of Winter

As the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen) resumes, Inkpots urinates himself watching [Viserion](wiki:Viserion) snatch corpses in mid-air being flung by the [Wicked Sister](wiki:Wicked_Sister). A Yunkish officer rides up to them and orders Inkpots to bring the company to the shores of the bay, but Inkpots refuses to obey the order on account of Brown Ben not being present.(wiki:Tybero_Istarion.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btyrion_ii_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctyrion_ii.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
