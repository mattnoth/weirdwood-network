## Appearances & Description

Theomore is a fat, rose-cheeked man, with thick lips, and a head of golden curls.(wiki:Theomore_Lannister.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He is said to be "all head and no heart".(wiki:Theomore_Lannister.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Theomore is present in the [Merman's Court](wiki:Merman's_Court) when Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) comes to treat with Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly). Theomore dismisses Davos' claim that King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) is a [bastard](wiki:Bastard) born of [incest](wiki:Incest), stating it is a lie created by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) to lay claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Theomore_Lannister.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Lord Wyman later reveals that he does not trust Theomore with his messages, due to his [relationship](wiki:House_Lannister_of_Lannisport) to [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) of [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Theomore_Lannister.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
