## Narrative Arc

[Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) has learned from [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) the fate of her [brother](wiki:Robb_Stark) and [mother](wiki:Catelyn_Tully) at the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding). She and her husband join many other guests at the Queen’s Ballroom to give [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) his wedding gifts. It is a new year, the 300th since [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:War_of_Conquest), and Joffrey’s wedding is that night. Tyrion presents his nephew with a copy of Grand Maester [Kaeth](wiki:Kaeth)’s [Lives of Four Kings](wiki:Lives_of_Four_Kings), one of only four copies in existence written by Kaeth himself. [Kevan](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) tells the King it is "A book every king should read." Joffrey is disappointed with the gift, and hacks the book in half with the [Valyrian sword](wiki:Widow's_Wail) his [grandfather](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) had just given him. Joffrey remarks that he is no stranger to [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel), and also that Tyrion owes him another gift. Tyrion suggests a Valyrian dagger with a [dragonbone](wiki:Dragonbone) hilt, which seems to unsettle Joffrey for a moment before he says it is a good idea but wants one with a gold hilt instead.

Later, back in their rooms, Sansa tells Tyrion that she would rather not know how Robb and her mother had perished, and Tyrion promises that he will say no more.
