## Narrative Arc

[Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) is being fitted for a new gown provided by Queen [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister). [Margaery](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) tells Sansa that she does not fear [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), because [her father](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) only conceded her hand in marriage with the provision that [Loras](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) was named to the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). Her brother will protect her from Joffrey.

When Sansa tells [Dontos](wiki:Dontos_Hollard) about the [Queen of Thorns](wiki:Olenna_Redwyne)'s plan to marry her to [Willas](wiki:Willas_Tyrell), the drunk man warns her that the Tyrells wish to wed her for her claim to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), stating, "These Tyrells are only [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) with flowers." Dontos tells her that they will escape, as promised, on Joffrey's wedding day.
