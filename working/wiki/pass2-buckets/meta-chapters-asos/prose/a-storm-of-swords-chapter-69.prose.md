## Narrative Arc

[Mance](wiki:Mance_Rayder)’s raiders advance under the protection of a giant fortified [turtle](wiki:Turtle_(siege_engine)) but the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) is able to destroy it. Afterwards, [Jon](wiki:Jon_Snow) tries to get some sleep, but is pulled out of bed by some men from [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea). They bring him before [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne), and his new friend [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt). The two accuse Jon of being a turncloak and oathbreaker, and they even use the captured [Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt) to support their accusations. Even Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) cannot persuade Slynt to relent, and the former Commander of the City Watch has Jon thrown into an ice cell, relishing the thought of punishing one of [Ned Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)’s sons.
