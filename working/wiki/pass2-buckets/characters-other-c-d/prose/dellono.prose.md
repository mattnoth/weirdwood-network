## Appearances & Description

Dellono has a pinched white face and mottled purple hands. He is one of the regulars at the [Gate](wiki:Gate_(playhouse)).(wiki:Dellono.cite_ref-winds_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter

Prior to the performance of *[The Bloody Hand](wiki:The_Bloody_Hand)*, [Daena](wiki:Daena_(mummer)) recognizes some of the [Gate](wiki:Gate_(playhouse)) regulars in the crowd and points them out for [Mercy](wiki:Arya_Stark), including Dellono.(wiki:Dellono.cite_ref-winds_1-1)
