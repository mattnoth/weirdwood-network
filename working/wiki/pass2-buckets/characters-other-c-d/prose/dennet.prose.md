## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Dennet is present when [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) is given a [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat) by Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) in the [hollow hill](wiki:Hollow_hill). Dennet, [Anguy](wiki:Anguy), and [Kyle](wiki:Kyle_(brotherhood)) are ordered to feather the [Hound](wiki:Hound) at the first sign of treachery, and only after all three bowmen have their shafts notched does [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr) hand Sandor a belt.(wiki:Dennet.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
