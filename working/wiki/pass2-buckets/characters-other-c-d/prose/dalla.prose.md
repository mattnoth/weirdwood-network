## Appearances & Description

According to [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), Dalla looks so heavily pregnant that it's surprising she can move.(wiki:Dalla.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

[Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) thinks his new queen is good(wiki:Dalla.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and wise.(wiki:Dalla.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) agrees that Dalla's words are wise.(wiki:Dalla.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

The pregnant Dalla is cooking a brace of hens when [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) is brought to [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder). When Jon wonders if Mance had abandoned the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) for Dalla, the [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall) informs him that he met Dalla upon his return to the lands [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall), after secretly attending King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s visit to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Dalla.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Mance instructs [Varamyr](wiki:Varamyr) to protect Dalla during the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall),(wiki:Dalla.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) but the [skinchanger](wiki:Skinchanger) is incapacitated when [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) [magically](wiki:Magic) burns [Orell](wiki:Orell)'s eagle.(wiki:Dalla.cite_ref-Rasos76.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Jon protects the tent during the battle, tasking [Val](wiki:Val) to act as midwife in the chaos.(wiki:Dalla.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Dalla gives birth to a son, but dies in childbirth.(wiki:Dalla.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Dalla.cite_ref-Rasos76.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

After the battle, Dalla's son, the so-called "[wildling prince](wiki:Aemon_Steelsong)", is kept at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), and fed goats' milk and potions made by [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)). When [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) arrives at Castle Black, he is given over to her care, to [nurse](wiki:Wet_nurse) alongside [her own infant son](wiki:Monster).(wiki:Dalla.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Jon Snow, now the [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), hears that Melisandre plans to sacrifice Dalla's son for his king's blood, in order to wake a [dragon](wiki:Dragon).(wiki:Dalla.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) To protect the baby, Jon arranges for him to be secretly switched with Gilly's son.(wiki:Dalla.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Dalla.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Dalla's baby is sent with Gilly, [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), and Maester Aemon to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown),(wiki:Dalla.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) while Gilly's babe remains at Castle Black. For much of the journey, Gilly is overcome with grief at being separated from her child,(wiki:Dalla.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)(wiki:Dalla.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) but eventually comes to love Dalla's child as much as she loves her own.(wiki:Dalla.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) At Maester Aemon's funeral aboard the *[Cinnamon Wind](wiki:Cinnamon_Wind)*, Gilly suggests that when the baby turns two years old, he should be named Aemon Battleborn or Aemon Steelsong, [in honor of](wiki:Named_after) the late maester and because the boy was born during the battle beneath the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Dalla.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Jon Snow recalls how he convinced Gilly to secretly take Dalla's baby and leave her own child behind.(wiki:Dalla.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

Jon speaks with Melisandre about [sorcery](wiki:Sorcery), and mentions what Dalla had told him about it.(wiki:Dalla.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
