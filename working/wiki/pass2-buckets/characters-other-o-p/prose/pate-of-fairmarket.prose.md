## Origins

Pate claimed the title of [river king](wiki:River_king) or [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident) in the generations after the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) were conquered by the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Arlan III Durrandon](wiki:Arlan_III_Durrandon).
