## Appearances & Description

The [godswood](wiki:Godswood) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) is an airy garden with streams(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) and thick stands of great old elms and tall redwoods.(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The godswood's [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree) is a slender [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) with a sad-looking face.(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Grass,(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) wildflowers,(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) and mint(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) also grow in the garden. Songbirds have hidden nests in the godswood.(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The shortest way from the [Great Hall of Riverrun](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Riverrun) to the castle's central keep is through the godswood.(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) Wind from the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) blows through the higher branches of the godswood trees, and the ivy-covered Wheel Tower is nearby.(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
Since [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully) prays in their [sept](wiki:Sept) as followers of the [Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), they use their godswood for relaxation instead of worship of the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods).(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Origins

[Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), [Lord of Riverrun](wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun), taught his eldest child, [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), how to ride in the [godswood](wiki:Godswood).(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) His son and heir, [Edmure](wiki:Edmure_Tully), broke his arm once after falling from an elm.(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) Edmure would chase Catelyn through the damp godswood when it rained.(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Catelyn and her sister [Lysa](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) fed mud pies to [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), their father [Hoster's](wiki:Hoster_Tully) ward.(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Petyr liked to chew the mint which grows in the godswood, and on one occasion he kissed the Tully sisters beneath a bower.(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

After their victory in the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), followers of the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods)—including [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), Lord [Greatjon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber), Lord [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark), Lady [Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont), [Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover), and Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood)—pray in the [godswood](wiki:Godswood) of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7) During the war council held in the [Great Hall](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Riverrun), Robb is proclaimed [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) by the assembled nobles.(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

After Robb's mother, [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), returns to Riverrun from [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), she meets with her brother, Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully), in the castle's godswood.(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Catelyn considers praying in the godswood during the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords), as her late husband [Eddard](wiki:Eddard_Stark) followed the old gods.(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Karstark commits treason by murdering two of Robb's prisoners, [Willem Lannister](wiki:Willem_Lannister) and [Tion Frey](wiki:Tion_Frey), after being denied his vengeance against Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister). Robb responds by publicly beheading Rickard before the godswood's [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree) in the rain the next morning.(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
While walking through the godswood, Robb informs Catelyn that his sister, [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark), has been wed to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister). They argue about Robb possibly making peace with the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Before departing Riverrun for the [Twins](wiki:Twins), Robb bids farewell to his wife, Queen [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling), before the heart tree.(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Robb and Catelyn are killed in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding),(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) but Riverrun remains under the control of Catelyn's uncle, Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully).(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) is sent to resolve the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun) and force the surrender of Brynden the Blackfish. Threatening the captive Edmure, Jaime says he will fell Riverrun's godswood if the besiegers are forced to assault the castle.(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Edmure negotiates the castle's surrender, but Brynden is able to escape the Lannister siege.(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) With winter arriving in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), Jaime sees snow piling up in the godswood.(wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)
