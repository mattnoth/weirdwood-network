## Origins

During the [Dawn Age](wiki:Dawn_Age), the stony spine of the Hook was inhabited by [giants](wiki:Giant) that lived in the defiles and ridges of the peninsula.(wiki:Massey's_Hook.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_the_coming_of_the_first_men.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
[Stonedance](wiki:Stonedance), the seat of [House Massey](wiki:House_Massey), was built on Massey's Hook by Lord [Maldon Massey](wiki:Maldon_Massey), a vassal of the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Durran the Ravenfriend](wiki:Durran_Durrandon_(the_Ravenfriend)). The Masseys broke away during the reign of [Durwald the Fat](wiki:Durwald_I_Durrandon), however.(wiki:Massey's_Hook.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_house_durrandon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
At the time of the [Andal invasion](wiki:Andal_invasion), the Storm King [Erich the Unready](wiki:Erich_VII_Durrandon) battled the pirate king [Justin Milk-Eye](wiki:Justin_Massey_(Milk-Eye)) for control of Massey's Hook; this was eventually resolved when [Qarlton II Durrandon](wiki:Qarlton_II_Durrandon) defeated [Josua Massey](wiki:Josua_Massey). Soon after the [Andal](wiki:Andals) warlord [Togarion Bar Emmon](wiki:Togarion_Bar_Emmon) traveled south of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) and established a new kingdom on Massey's Hook with [Sharp Point](wiki:Sharp_Point) as his seat. The peninsula was eventually reclaimed by the Storm Kings, however.(wiki:Massey's_Hook.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_andals_in_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Some Storm Kings kept war fleets at Massey's Hook.(wiki:Massey's_Hook.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_men_of_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Prior to the [War of Conquest](wiki:War_of_Conquest), Massey's Hook was part of the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) owing allegiance to the Durrandon Storm Kings of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End). The [Bar Emmons](wiki:House_Bar_Emmon) and Masseys had developed closer ties with the [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen) of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), however, and allied with [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) against the Storm King [Argilac the Arrogant](wiki:Argilac_Durrandon).(wiki:Massey's_Hook.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) After Aegon the Conqueror's victory, Massey's Hook became part of the new [crownlands](wiki:Crownlands) owing allegiance to the Targaryens reigning in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).
The [Fourth Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Fourth_Blackfyre_Rebellion) began when [Daemon III Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_III_Blackfyre) and [Aegor Rivers](wiki:Aegor_Rivers) landed on Massey's Hook, but they were defeated in the [Battle of Wendwater Bridge](wiki:Battle_of_Wendwater_Bridge).(wiki:Massey's_Hook.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

While sailing to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s fleet regroups along Massey's Hook after being scattered across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) by a storm.(wiki:Massey's_Hook.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
