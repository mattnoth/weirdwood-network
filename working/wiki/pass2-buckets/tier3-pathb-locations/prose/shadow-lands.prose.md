## Origins

Ancient tales from [Asshai](wiki:Asshai) claim that [dragons](wiki:Dragon) originated in the Shadow and were first tamed by a people so ancient they had no name.(wiki:Shadow_Lands.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf25.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
The [maroon emperors](wiki:Maroon_emperors) of [Yi Ti](wiki:Yi_Ti) kept their court in [Jinqi](wiki:Jinqi) to better guard the borders of the [Golden Empire](wiki:Golden_Empire_of_Yi_Ti) against reavers from the Shadow Lands.(wiki:Shadow_Lands.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf841_8-0)
While studying in Asshai, [Mirri Maz Duur](wiki:Mirri_Maz_Duur) learned from a [bloodmage](wiki:Bloodmage) from the Shadow Lands.(wiki:Shadow_Lands.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Magister [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) tells [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) that the three [dragon eggs](wiki:Dragon_egg) he gives her as wedding gifts come from the Shadow Lands beyond [Asshai](wiki:Asshai).(wiki:Shadow_Lands.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Daenerys recalls hearing that the first dragons had come from the east, from the Shadow Lands beyond Asshai and the islands of the [Jade Sea](wiki:Jade_Sea).(wiki:Shadow_Lands.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
After entering [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak), Daenerys sees plundered statues she considers misshapen and terrible. Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) says they likely come from the Shadow Lands beyond Asshai.(wiki:Shadow_Lands.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Daenerys meets [Quaithe](wiki:Quaithe) of the Shadow.(wiki:Shadow_Lands.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

During the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) claims to have visited Asshai by the Shadow.(wiki:Shadow_Lands.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
