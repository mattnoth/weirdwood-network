## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

In the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), just before he begins his long descent alone to meet [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq), Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) recalls that it had been built as an echo to the Great Pyramid of Ghis. He also remembers that [Lomas Longstrider](wiki:Lomas_Longstrider) had once visited its colossal ruins.(wiki:Great_Pyramid_of_Ghis.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)
