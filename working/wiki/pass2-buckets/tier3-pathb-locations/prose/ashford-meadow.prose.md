## Quotes

In normal times the meadow served as a commons for the folk of [Ashford](wiki:Ashford) town across [the river](wiki:Cockleswent), but now it was transformed. A second town had sprung up overnight, a town of [silk](wiki:Silk) instead of stone, larger and fairer than its elder sister. Dozens of merchants had erected their stalls along the edge of the field, selling felts and fruits, belts and boots, hides and [hawks](wiki:Hawk), earthenware, [gemstones](wiki:Gemstone), pewterwork, [spices](wiki:Spice), feathers, and all manner of other goods.
