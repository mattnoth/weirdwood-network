## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) tells Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) that her husband, Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), has talked of leading his [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) east after [Rhaego](wiki:Rhaego) is born, to plunder the lands around the Jade Sea.(wiki:Jade_Sea.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Before Drogo's body is burnt on his funeral pyre, Jorah begs Daenerys to come east with him, to [Yi Ti](wiki:Yi_Ti), [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), the Jade Sea and [Asshai](wiki:Asshai) by the shadow, to see all the wonders yet unseen.(wiki:Jade_Sea.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

In [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos) tries to entreat Daenerys to accompany him on his pleasure barge to go in search of a golden vintage of wine that is made across the Jade Sea. He also suggests that they journey around Yi Ti to search for the dreaming city of poets, to sip the [wine](wiki:Wine) of wisdom from a dead man's skull.(wiki:Jade_Sea.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

In [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), Daenerys reminds Jorah that after Drogo's death he had asked her to go with him to Yi Ti and the Jade Sea.(wiki:Jade_Sea.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
During a [small council](wiki:Small_council) meeting, Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) reports that sailors back from the Jade Sea report that a three-headed [dragon](wiki:Dragon) has hatched in Qarth.(wiki:Jade_Sea.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Before departing the [Wall](wiki:Wall), [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) overhears Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) tell [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) that he left a book for him in his chambers, the [Jade Compendium](wiki:Jade_Compendium). This was written by the [Volantene](wiki:Volantene) adventurer [Colloquo Votar](wiki:Colloquo_Votar), who traveled east and visited all the lands of the Jade Sea. Aemon tells Jon there is a passage he might find interesting.(wiki:Jade_Sea.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) tells [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) of the [Treasure](wiki:Treasure), a [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) trading galley that once returned from the Jade Sea with cloves and saffron, jet and jade, scarlet samite and green silk … and the grey death.(wiki:Jade_Sea.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
In [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), when discussing how to find a Westerosi ship heading to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) remarks that few come as far as Volantis and those who do fill their holds with [silk](wiki:Silk), and [spices](wiki:Spices) from the Jade Sea, then bend their oars for home.(wiki:Jade_Sea.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
In [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte), [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley) suggests to [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) that they make new lives upon the sea as traders. They can voyage east and come back with silks and spices, as one voyage to the Jade Sea would make them wealthy.(wiki:Jade_Sea.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
