## Culture

The [Jogos Nhai](wiki:Jogos_Nhai), a race of nomadic mounted warriors, live in small bands on windswept plains and rolling hills. The plains are drier and less fertile than the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea), having sparse grass. The [zorses](wiki:Zorse) of the Jogos Nhai eat weeds and [devilgrass](wiki:Devilgrass).(wiki:Plains_of_the_Jogos_Nhai.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_the_plains_of_the_jogos_nhai.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Origins

Before the [Dry Times](wiki:Dry_Times), the [Jogos Nhai](wiki:Jogos_Nhai) warred with the [Patrimony of Hyrkoon](wiki:Patrimony_of_Hyrkoon) and brought thousands of Hyrkoon to the plains as [slaves](wiki:Slaves). [Yi Ti](wiki:Yi_Ti) has invaded the plains in multiple campaigns.(wiki:Plains_of_the_Jogos_Nhai.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_the_plains_of_the_jogos_nhai.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
