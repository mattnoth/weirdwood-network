## Origins

The caves within the hills were inhabited by [giants](wiki:Giants) and men who painted on walls in antiquity.(wiki:Hills_of_Norvos.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_norvos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
After winning two victories over [Norvoshi](wiki:Norvos) hosts in the hills, [Qarlon the Great](wiki:Qarlon_the_Great) of [Lorath](wiki:Lorath) decided to attack Great Norvos. The [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) came to the aid of Norvos, however, and defeated Qarlon with [dragonfire](wiki:Dragonfire).(wiki:Hills_of_Norvos.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_lorath.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
