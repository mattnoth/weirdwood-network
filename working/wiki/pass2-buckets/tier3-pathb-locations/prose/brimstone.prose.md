## Origins

During the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals), the [Ullers](wiki:House_Uller) raised the [Hellholt](wiki:Hellholt), a grim and stinking keep, on the banks of the Brimstone.(wiki:Brimstone.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_the_andals_arrive.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) [House Dryland](wiki:House_Dryland) ruled as [Kings of the Brimstone](wiki:King_of_the_Brimstone) from [Hellgate Hall](wiki:Hellgate_Hall) until the last of their line, [Lucifer Dryland](wiki:Lucifer_Dryland), was sent to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) by [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria).(wiki:Brimstone.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_the_coming_of_the_rhoynar.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
During the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) had Lord [Harlan Tyrell](wiki:Harlan_Tyrell) garrison his invading host at the abandoned Hellholt, for it was a strong castle on the Brimstone, and thought to be well situated to deal with any revolts against [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen). However, the fish taken from the sulphurous river made Lord Tyrell's men sicken.(wiki:Brimstone.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
When Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) sailed past Dorne towards Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), a sudden storm briefly scattered [his fleet](wiki:Velaryon_fleet). Near the mouth of the Brimstone, a damaged [galley](wiki:Galley) put in to take aboard fresh water and make repairs, but was attacked under the cover of darkness by bandits, who slaughtered her crew and looted her supplies.(wiki:Brimstone.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
