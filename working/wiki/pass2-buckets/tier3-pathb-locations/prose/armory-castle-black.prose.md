## Appearances & Description

The armory is warmed by chunks of coal which burn in [iron](wiki:Iron) braziers at each end of the long room. Equipment like swords and scabbards can be hung from hooks in the stone wall,(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and spears are stored on racks.(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The room also contains benches.(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) The armory has a whetstone and bellows for the armorer, [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye).(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
A steep flight of stone steps descends from the back of the armory to the wormways, subterranean tunnels which connect the towers and keeps of Castle Black.(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Below the armory is one of the [vaults of Castle Black](wiki:Vaults_of_Castle_Black), a cavernous stone cellar divided by two rows of square stone pillars. The hall has barrel-vaulted ceilings, tables, and great casks of [wine](wiki:Wine) and [ale](wiki:Ale) along the walls. A potbellied kettle can be found by a hearth in the corner.(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Donel has a pair of modest rooms(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) behind the armory,(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) past the forge.(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Fellow recruits of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), angry at [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow)'s treatment of them, attack him in the armory. [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye), the armorer of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), breaks up the fighting and then explains to Jon that he had been acting like a bully.(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Jon then sometimes assists one-armed Donal in the armory, spinning the whetstone or pumping the bellows.(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
When [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) arrives at Castle Black, Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne), the [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms), has Sam don black equipment from the armory.(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
After Alliser allows his recruits to become brothers of the Night's Watch, [Dareon](wiki:Dareon) retrieves a skin of sour red [wine](wiki:Wine) so the youths can celebrate.(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Ragot41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser [Endrew Tarth](wiki:Endrew_Tarth), the new master-at-arms, trains recruits outside of the armory, with Donal watching from the doorway.(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Seeing smoke rising from the armory's chimney when he returns to Castle Black from the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), Jon meets with Donal in the building.(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) In preparation for the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) by [Styr](wiki:Styr) and his [Thenns](wiki:Thenns), Donal empties the armory to arm the people of [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town).(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Jon sees [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) atop the armory's roof during the fighting.(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Donal is slain fighting [Mag Mar Tun Doh Weg](wiki:Mag_Mar_Tun_Doh_Weg) during the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall).(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Jon spends most of his time at the armory and in the practice yard.(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) In the aftermath of the fighting, the [election](wiki:Election) of a new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) is held in the vault below the armory.(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Jon, the new Lord Commander, takes up residence in Donal's old chambers.(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Since the [common hall](wiki:Common_hall) was burned by wildlings, the Night's Watch take their meals in the cellar below the armory.(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
Jon realizes that [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) intends to clean out the armory for his campaigns. Jon offers some older equipment, but mentions that Castle Black has lost its armorer.(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
Jon meets with his officers and then with [Tormund Giantsbane](wiki:Tormund_Giantsbane) in his chambers.(wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black).cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
