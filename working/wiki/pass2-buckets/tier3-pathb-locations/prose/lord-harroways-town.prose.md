## Appearances & Description

Harroway includes a seven-sided [sept](wiki:Sept), a two-story inn, a stone roundtower, and many smaller buildings with chimneys.(wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town.cite_ref-Rasos47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) A wide, flat-bottomed boat, called [Old King Andahar's](wiki:Andahar) two-headed water horse by some, is found at the ferry. It has a dozen oarlocks, a wooden house on its deck, and is decorated with two carved [horse](wiki:Horse) heads fore and aft.(wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town.cite_ref-Rasos47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) A two-headed horse is included in the arms of [House Roote](wiki:House_Roote) of Harroway.(wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town.cite_ref-4)

## Origins

The [river kings](wiki:River_king) of old refused charters which would have allowed towns like [Fairmarket](wiki:Fairmarket), [Saltpans](wiki:Saltpans), and Harroway to expand and potentially become cities.(wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Because of his inability to conceive a son with Queen [Alys Harroway](wiki:Alys_Harroway), King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) killed Alys and her all of her kin from [House Harroway](wiki:House_Harroway) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), and Lord Harroway's Town.(wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Afterward, Maegor decreed that the strongest of his [knights](wiki:Knight) could win Harrenhal. Ser [Walton Towers](wiki:Walton_Towers) was the victor of twenty-three household knights who fought in the blood-soaked streets of Harroway for the honor. Walton died of his wounds shortly after, with Harrenhal passing to his son [Jordan Towers](wiki:Jordan_Towers). Meanwhile, Maegor granted Harroway town to Lord [Alton Butterwell](wiki:Alton_Butterwell) and the remaining Harroway lands to Lord [Darnold Darry](wiki:Darnold_Darry).(wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
Harroway was in the possession of [House Roote](wiki:House_Roote) by the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons),(wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and the town was burned by [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) during the civil war of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Before the river moved some seventy to eighty years before the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), one could take a ferry across the [Trident](wiki:Trident) from the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads) to the road that led to Lord Harroway's Town and [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls).(wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) and [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) find Lord Harroway's Town flooded when the [Trident](wiki:Trident) overflows its banks. They ferry across aboard the two-headed horse boat, but the [Hound](wiki:Hound) refuses to pay [golden dragons](wiki:Currency) to the [ferrymen](wiki:Bent-backed_man).(wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town.cite_ref-Rasos47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After stopping at the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads) with [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth), Septon [Meribald](wiki:Meribald) intends to travel on to [Nutten](wiki:Nutten), [Riverbend](wiki:Riverbend), and Lord Harroway's Town.(wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
