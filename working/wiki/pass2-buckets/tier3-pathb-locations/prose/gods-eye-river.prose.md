## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Yoren](wiki:Yoren) leads his group of recruits for the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), including [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark). He hopes to travel west of the Gods Eye to avoid traffic on the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad).(wiki:Gods_Eye_river.cite_ref-Racok9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Their route west is blocked by the river, however, and [Woth](wiki:Woth) reports that a wooden bridge has been burned up. Yoren instead leads them north to the [unnamed town](wiki:Gods_Eye_town).(wiki:Gods_Eye_river.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
