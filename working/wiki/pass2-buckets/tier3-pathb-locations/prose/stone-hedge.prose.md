## Origins

When Lord [Lothar Bracken](wiki:Lothar_Bracken) rebelled against King [Harwyn Hoare](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) sacked and destroyed Stone Hedge, and starved Lothar in a [crow cage](wiki:Crow_cage).(wiki:Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Early in the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Stone Hedge was [taken](wiki:Taking_of_Stone_Hedge) by Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) after the [Battle of the Burning Mill](wiki:Battle_of_the_Burning_Mill), with Daemon using [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) as a base of operations.(wiki:Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Although born in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), [Aegor Rivers](wiki:Aegor_Rivers) was raised at Stone Hedge, the ancestral home of his mother, [Barba Bracken](wiki:Barba_Bracken).(wiki:Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

After the onset of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Stone Hedge is [burned](wiki:Burning_of_Stone_Hedge) by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane).(wiki:Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

After [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) is proclaimed [king](wiki:King_of_the_Trident), Lord [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken) returns to [retake](wiki:Liberation_of_Stone_Hedge) Stone Hedge from [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister). He is injured, and his nephew [Hendry](wiki:Hendry_Bracken) is killed.(wiki:Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Jonos tells Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) that Gregor and the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) killed his natural son, [Harry Rivers](wiki:Harry_Rivers), and his nephew, [Hendry Bracken](wiki:Hendry_Bracken), and that Gregor raped one of his daughters. After this event, [Lady Bracken](wiki:Lady_Bracken) decided the [gods](wiki:Gods) were punishing them, and now spends her time praying with her [septon](wiki:Septon).(wiki:Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
