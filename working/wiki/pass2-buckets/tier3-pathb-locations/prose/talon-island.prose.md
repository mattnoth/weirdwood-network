## Origins

Some of [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria)'s [ten thousand ships](wiki:Ten_thousand_ships) were attacked by corsairs from Talon. Nymeria then refused the corsairs' offer to allow the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar) to settle upon the [Isle of Toads](wiki:Isle_of_Toads), since the corsairs insisted the Rhoynar give up their boats and send each corsair king thirty virgin girls and pretty boys per year, but Nymeria refused.(wiki:Talon_(island).cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
