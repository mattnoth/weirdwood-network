## Appearances & Description

The sept is located across the yard from the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep)'s stables,(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) above the castle's serpentine steps.(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) The [Maidenvault](wiki:Maidenvault) is found behind the sept.(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
Fragrant candles burn inside the sept,(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) which can smell of incense.(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Crystals in its high windows turn sunlight into rainbows.(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) There are rows(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) of benches for worshipers.(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2) It has altars of pale [marble](wiki:Marble) for each of the [Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven),(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) and the marriage altar is located between the [Mother](wiki:Mother_(the_Seven)) and the [Father](wiki:Father_(the_Seven)).(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Origins

Hundreds of people crowded into the royal sept to pray for Princess [Aerea Targaryen](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen) after the sick girl returned to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
By the age of twelve, Princess [Saera Targaryen](wiki:Saera_Targaryen) often arrived for prayers drunk from stolen [wine](wiki:Wine) and [ale](wiki:Ale).(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Septon [Eustace](wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)) served in the royal sept during the reign of King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) According to Eustace, Ser [Arryk Cargyll](wiki:Arryk_Cargyll) prayed in the sept before sailing to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) Some of Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen)'s companions prayed in the sept for deliverance amid the [Storming of the Dragonpit](wiki:Storming_of_the_Dragonpit).(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) After refusing to abdicate in a [small council](wiki:Small_council) meeting, a fatigued King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) asked Ser [Gyles Belgrave](wiki:Gyles_Belgrave) to bring him to the castle sept; Aegon died of [poison](wiki:Poison) en route.(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) During the [Lysene Spring](wiki:Lysene_Spring), Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen) was anointed by Septon [Bernard](wiki:Bernard) in the royal sept.(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Septa [Mordane](wiki:Mordane) prays in the sept after [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) goes missing while exploring the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
After the death of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) and downfall of her father, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), Arya steals two candles from the empty sept when she makes her escape from the castle.(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) The captive [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) is allowed to visit the sept to pray for her imprisoned father.(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

In the darkness of the sept, Ser [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister) reveals to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) that Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) intends for Prince [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) to be taken to the safety of [Rosby](wiki:Rosby). After Lancel leaves, Tyrion lights candles at the altars of the [Warrior](wiki:Warrior_(the_Seven)) and the [Stranger](wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven)).(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) awards the white cloaks of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) to Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) and Ser [Osmund Kettleblack](wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack) within the crowded castle sept, and the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Tyrion's)) leads them through their vows.(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)
Once the [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet) of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) is spotted before the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), songs begin to be sung in the packed sept. [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) lights a candle at each altar of the [Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), and she sings hymns with the gathered attendees. Sansa departs for the Queen's Ballroom when a [septon](wiki:Septon) prays for Joffrey's protection.(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-3) After Cersei holds a feast in the Queen's Ballroom, many guests depart for the sept.(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Racok60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Sansa sings in the sept with her new friends, [Margaery](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), [Megga](wiki:Megga_Tyrell), [Alla](wiki:Alla_Tyrell), and [Elinor Tyrell](wiki:Elinor_Tyrell).(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
Sansa is forced by the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) to [wed Tyrion](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark) in the sept.(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)
Sansa often prays in the royal sept, lighting candles to the [Mother](wiki:Mother_(the_Seven)), the [Maid](wiki:Maid_(the_Seven)), and the [Crone](wiki:Crone_(the_Seven)).(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
The body of King Joffrey rests on a bier within the sept after [his death](wiki:Purple_Wedding). Osmund is on guard at the door when Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) arrives. Jaime and Cersei have sex on the Mother's altar.(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

The [wedding of Tommen I Baratheon and Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Wedding_of_Tommen_I_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) is a modest ceremony in the castle's sept.(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) [Taena Merryweather](wiki:Taena_Merryweather) tells Cersei that her maid, [Senelle](wiki:Senelle), reveals secrets about Cersei to Margaery's cousins while pretending to pray in the sept.(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1)
Margaery brings Tommen to pray daily in the sept after Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) is wounded in the [siege of Dragonstone](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone).(wiki:Royal_sept.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
