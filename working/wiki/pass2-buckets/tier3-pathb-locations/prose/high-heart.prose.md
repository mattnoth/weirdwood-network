## Origins

According to song, the [Andal](wiki:Andal) king [Erreg](wiki:Erreg) slaughtered [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest), their animals, and their [First Men](wiki:First_Men) allies at High Heart, after which his men cut down the [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) grove. According to [True History](wiki:True_History), however, the children had left the region long before the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals).(wiki:High_Heart.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
The nearby [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) avoid the area as it is said to be haunted by [ghosts](wiki:Ghosts) of the children of the forest slaughtered by Erreg.(wiki:High_Heart.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) and the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) rest here one night while travelling south, in order to meet with the [ghost of High Heart](wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart) and to gather information on the whereabouts of Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion).(wiki:High_Heart.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) After having found Beric and heading back north, the brotherhood returns to High Heart and again meets with the ghost of High Heart. This time the dwarf woman meets Arya but becomes distressed, believing the girl to be associated with death.(wiki:High_Heart.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) receives a report that her brother [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) has become a hermit in the riverlands, living on some haunted hill.(wiki:High_Heart.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

While aboard the [Selaesori Qhoran](wiki:Selaesori_Qhoran), Tyrion recalls what he considers to be a less reliable tale that had reached his ears, that of a [dwarf witch](wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart) who haunts a hill in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:High_Heart.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
