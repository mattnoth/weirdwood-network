## Origins

After giving birth to a [bastard](wiki:Bastard) boy in [48 AC](wiki:48_AC), [Coryanne Wylde](wiki:Coryanne_Wylde) was closely watched within the walls of the Rain House. She was eventually summoned to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to serve Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen).(wiki:Rain_House.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
