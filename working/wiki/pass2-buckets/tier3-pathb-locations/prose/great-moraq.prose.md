## Origins

Great Moraq was once conquered by [Jar Joq](wiki:Jar_Joq), one of the sea-green [god-emperors](wiki:God-emperor) of [Yi Ti](wiki:Yi_Ti).(wiki:Great_Moraq.cite_ref-Rtloiaf_the_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Because of the prevailing winds, the eastern shore of Great Moraq is lightly settled and rarely visited. The western shore is more populous and is connected with the Jade Sea trade routes.(wiki:Great_Moraq.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_great_moraq.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
