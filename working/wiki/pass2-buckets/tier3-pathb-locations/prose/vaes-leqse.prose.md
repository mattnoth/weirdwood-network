## Origins

Gornath was known for its canals.(wiki:Vaes_Leqse.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
During the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood), the kings of Gornath and [Kasath](wiki:Kasath) fought over the spoils of [Sathar](wiki:Sathar), after that city's destruction by [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki). When Khal [Moro](wiki:Moro) and his [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) attacked Kasath, the king of Gornath gave the Dothraki his aid, going as far as to take one of Moro's daughters as his wife. Gornath fell twelve years later to Khal [Horro](wiki:Horro), while the King of Gornath was killed by his own Dothraki wife.(wiki:Vaes_Leqse.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
