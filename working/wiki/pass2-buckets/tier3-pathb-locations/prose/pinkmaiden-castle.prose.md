## Origins

Supporters of Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) gathered at Pinkmaiden Castle prior to the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye). After Aegon's death, [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) flew on [Dreamfyre](wiki:Dreamfyre) from Pinkmaiden to the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) to escape King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Pinkmaiden_Castle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Rhaena briefly returned to Pinkmaiden during the reign of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Pinkmaiden_Castle.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The king visited Pinkmaiden atop [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor) in [53 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Pinkmaiden_Castle.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[Quellon Greyjoy](wiki:Quellon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), was married to his third wife, a daughter of Lord [Piper](wiki:House_Piper), by a [septon](wiki:Septon) in Pinkmaiden's hall.(wiki:Pinkmaiden_Castle.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
