## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Penny](wiki:Penny) tells [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) that a [dwarf](wiki:Dwarf) [juggler](wiki:Dwarf_juggler) that she and her brother [Oppo](wiki:Oppo) knew had been found murdered and dismembered at the Temple of Trios. He had been cut into three parts, each part placed into one of the three mouths of the statue of Trios. However, when the parts were put back together, the juggler's head was missing. Penny and Tyrion feel certain that the dwarf's head was taken to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), who has put a bounty on Tyrion's head.(wiki:Temple_of_Trios.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
