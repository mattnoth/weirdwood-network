## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) drowns Lord [Sawane Botley](wiki:Sawane_Botley), Lord [Waldon Wynch](wiki:Waldon_Wynch) is the first man to recognize Euron as [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North).
