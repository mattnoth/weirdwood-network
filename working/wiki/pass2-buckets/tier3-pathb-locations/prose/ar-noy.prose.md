## Origins

Ar Noy was a great city of the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar), famed for its green [marble](wiki:Marble) halls. It was likely destroyed following the [Rhoynish Wars](wiki:Rhoynish_Wars), however.(wiki:Ar_Noy.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
The [Free City](wiki:Free_City) of [Qohor](wiki:Qohor) now rules the rivers north of Ar Noy, while [river pirates](wiki:Pirates) are most common on the rivers between Ar Noy and the [Sorrows](wiki:Sorrows).(wiki:Ar_Noy.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
