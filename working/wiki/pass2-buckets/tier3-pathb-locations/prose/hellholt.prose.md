## Origins

The Hellholt was founded during the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) by the [Ullers](wiki:House_Uller), who were [Andal](wiki:Andal) adventurers.(wiki:Hellholt.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_the_andals_arrive.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) The castle is named after an event in which Lord Uller invited his rivals to a feast and then had them burned to death after locking them within his hall.(wiki:Hellholt.cite_ref-Rawoiafhellholt.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
During the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), Lord [Harlan Tyrell](wiki:Harlan_Tyrell) lost a quarter of his men and most of his horses while traveling through the sands to Hellholt, which he found abandoned upon arrival. His soldiers were sickened by fish taken from the sulfurous [Brimstone](wiki:Brimstone).(wiki:Hellholt.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Harlan marched east after learning of the [Defenestration of Sunspear](wiki:Defenestration_of_Sunspear), but his host disappeared in the red sands between Hellholt and [Vaith](wiki:Vaith).(wiki:Hellholt.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) and [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) burned the Hellholt in [9 AC](wiki:9_AC).
[Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) and [Meraxes](wiki:Meraxes) were lost in battle in [10 AC](wiki:10_AC) when the dragon was struck in the right eye by a scorpion bolt fired from the castle's tallest tower. The fall of Meraxes destroyed the tower and part of the curtain wall.(wiki:Hellholt.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) During the ensuing [Dragon's Wroth](wiki:Dragon's_Wroth), some of the nearby sand was fused into glass through the [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame) of [Balerion](wiki:Balerion) and [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar). The sun-mottled bones of Meraxes remained on display at the Hellholt,(wiki:Hellholt.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) but the body of Rhaenys was never recovered. Some allege she was tortured within the Hellholt's dungeon.(wiki:Hellholt.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) intends to crown Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) as queen at Hellholt. Before she can do so, however, her party is intercepted and captured at the [Greenblood](wiki:Greenblood).(wiki:Hellholt.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

[Ellaria Sand](wiki:Ellaria_Sand) returns to Hellholt from [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear).(wiki:Hellholt.cite_ref-8)
