## Culture

According to [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) legends, the first man emerged from the depths of the Womb of the World, riding on the back of the first [horse](wiki:Horse) a thousand thousand years ago.(wiki:Womb_of_the_World.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) Merchant caravans are allowed to visit the Western Market of [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak) as long as they do not profane the [Mother of Mountains](wiki:Mother_of_Mountains) or the Womb of the World.(wiki:Womb_of_the_World.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

After eating the [heart of a stallion](wiki:Stallion_heart) as part of a ceremony for [her child](wiki:Rhaego), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) rides to the lake and bathes naked to remove the blood. Afterwards she has sex with Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) on the grassy shore.(wiki:Womb_of_the_World.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Within the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying) in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), Daenerys has a vision of a line of naked [crones](wiki:Dosh_khaleen) creeping from a great lake beneath the [Mother of Mountains](wiki:Mother_of_Mountains) and kneeling before her.(wiki:Womb_of_the_World.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
