## Origins

Vaes Leisi is a [Dothraki language](wiki:Dothraki_language) name, meaning "City of Ghosts". Consisting of carved trees and haunted grottoes, the settlement was inhabited by a vanished people the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) called the [Ifequevron](wiki:Ifequevron), or "[woods walkers](wiki:Woods_walkers)".(wiki:Vaes_Leisi.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_vaes_leisi.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
