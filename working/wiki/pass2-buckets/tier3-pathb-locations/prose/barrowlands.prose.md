## Origins

The largest hill of the region, the [Great Barrow](wiki:Great_Barrow) in [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton), is said to contain the grave of the [First King](wiki:First_King) of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) or a [King of the Giants](wiki:King_of_the_Giants).(wiki:Barrowlands.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) [House Dustin](wiki:House_Dustin) of [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) has ruled the barrowlands on behalf of [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) since the defeat of the [Barrow Kings](wiki:Barrow_Kings).(wiki:Barrowlands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
In the story of the [seventy-nine sentinels](wiki:Seventy-nine_sentinels), deserters of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) fled to the barrowlands and sought refuge in the castle of [Lord Ryswell](wiki:Lord_Ryswell_(Night's_Watch)), but the [Lord of the Rills](wiki:Lord_of_the_Rills) returned them to the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Barrowlands.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
There were reports that [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) and her dragon, [Dreamfyre](wiki:Dreamfyre), were seen as far north as the barrowlands and the [Fever River](wiki:Fever_River) during her search for the missing [Aerea Targaryen](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen).(wiki:Barrowlands.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Attendees of the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) included barrowdown men.(wiki:Barrowlands.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

After accepting the position of [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) travels with King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) south from [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). While in the barrowlands, the longtime friends temporarily depart from the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad) to ride amongst the grassy plains.(wiki:Barrowlands.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
En route to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) sees snow for the first time while passing through the barrowlands .(wiki:Barrowlands.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin) tells [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) that barrow [knights](wiki:Knight) wait to join [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s army as it travels south from Winterfell.(wiki:Barrowlands.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Luwin sends a raven to the barrowlands requesting a levy to assist in the [fight at Torrhen's Square](wiki:Fight_at_Torrhen's_Square).(wiki:Barrowlands.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Led by Lady [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin) of [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton), the barrowlands support Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), the new [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), after the death of Robb, the [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Barrowlands.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
Using Barrowton as a base, [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) and the [Bastard's Boys](wiki:Bastard's_Boys) unsuccessfully search the barrowlands for the [Frey](wiki:House_Frey) envoys, [Rhaegar](wiki:Rhaegar_Frey), [Symond](wiki:Symond_Frey), and [Jared](wiki:Jared_Frey), who have gone missing after leaving [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor).(wiki:Barrowlands.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)
