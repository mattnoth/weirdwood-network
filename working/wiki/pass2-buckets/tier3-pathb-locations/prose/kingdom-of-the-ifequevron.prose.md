## Origins

[Ifequevron](wiki:Ifequevron), meaning "wood walkers", is the [Dothraki language](wiki:Dothraki_language) name for a people who once lived in the forest. Located on a small western peninsula, [Vaes Leisi](wiki:Vaes_Leisi) is a ruined settlement of carved trees and haunted grottoes.(wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Ifequevron.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_vaes_leisi.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
The Dothraki are reported to have shunned the Kingdom of the Ifequevron out of reverence for the wood walkers or fear of their powers.(wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Ifequevron.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
[Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon), after his return from the [Thousand Islands](wiki:Thousand_Islands), wrote of carved trees, haunted grottoes, and strange silences at the Kingdom of the Ifequevron.(wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Ifequevron.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
