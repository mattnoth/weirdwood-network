## Appearances & Description

Located atop a hill, Ghost Hill has chalk-white walls that shine against the deep blue of the [Sea of Dorne](wiki:Sea_of_Dorne). There are square towers at the corners of the castle, which has a great central keep. The castle's maester is [Toman](wiki:Toman). There is a village with dusty lanes situated at the bottom of the hill.(wiki:Ghost_Hill.cite_ref-TWOW_2-0)

## Origins

During the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), Lord [Toland](wiki:House_Toland) sent a champion to face King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) in single combat. After slaying his opponent, Aegon discovered the champion was the [fool](wiki:Fool) of Ghost Hill, and that Lord Toland had disappeared.(wiki:Ghost_Hill.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Ghost Hill was quickly recaptured by [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) after Aegon returned to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Ghost_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) unleashed the [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame) of [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) on Ghost Hill after Dornishmen attacked [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath).(wiki:Ghost_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
[Morion Martell](wiki:Morion_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), gathered his army to the [Tor](wiki:Tor) and Ghost Hill in preparation for the [Fourth Dornish War](wiki:Fourth_Dornish_War), but King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) destroyed Morion's fleet as it crossed the [Sea of Dorne](wiki:Sea_of_Dorne).(wiki:Ghost_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) departs [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) on a mission from her father, [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), to discover the truth about the supposed [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)). [Elia Sand](wiki:Elia_Sand) defeats Arianne and [Valena Toland](wiki:Valena_Toland) in a horse race from the village beneath Ghost Hill to the castle's gates. Arianne and her knights sup with Lady [Nymella Toland](wiki:Nymella_Toland) and her daughters in the great hall of Ghost Hill. Arianne and Ser [Daemon Sand](wiki:Daemon_Sand) explore the battlements and parapets at night. The princess's party departs via the [Peregrine](wiki:Peregrine) for the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands).(wiki:Ghost_Hill.cite_ref-TWOW_2-1)
