## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After [Viserion](wiki:Viserion) and [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal) are unleashed upon the city the [ruling council of Meereen](wiki:Ruling_council_of_Meereen) fill the Pit of Ghrazz with bullocks to feed the dragons and keep them sated. The dragons however prove to have a taste for mutton and instead keep returning to [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit), which is filled with sheep, whenever they grow hungry.
