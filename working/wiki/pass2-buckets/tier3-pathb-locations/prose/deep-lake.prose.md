## Origins

Deep Lake was built during the reign of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) as a smaller, cheaper replacement for the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort). Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) convinced [Lothor Burley](wiki:Lothor_Burley), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), to replace the older castle. Deep Lake was paid for jewels donated by Alysanne and built over eight years by the men Jaehaerys sent north. A statue of Alysanne is outside the main hall.(wiki:Deep_Lake.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Deep_Lake.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand) suggests that the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) re-garrison Deep Lake in an effort to extend the Watch's defense of the Wall.(wiki:Deep_Lake.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Commander [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) considers re-garrisoning Deep Lake or [Sable Hall](wiki:Sable_Hall) with [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) under the command of [Halleck](wiki:Halleck), although neither castle is ready for human habitation yet.(wiki:Deep_Lake.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Jon later tells [Torghen Flint](wiki:Torghen_Flint) that Deep Lake is garrisoned, although undermanned.(wiki:Deep_Lake.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
