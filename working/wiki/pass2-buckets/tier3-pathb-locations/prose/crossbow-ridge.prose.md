## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken) wants the east bank of the Widow's Wash from Crossbow Ridge to [Rutting Meadow](wiki:Rutting_Meadow) for subduing Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood). Tytos surrenders Crossbow Ridge as part of the price to submit to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Crossbow_Ridge.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
