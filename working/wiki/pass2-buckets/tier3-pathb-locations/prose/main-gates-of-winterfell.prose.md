## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

The entourage of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) arrives at Winterfell through the castle gates, though the [wheelhouse](wiki:Wheelhouse) of Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) is too wide to pass through.(wiki:Main_gates_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) rides through the gates to the [winter town](wiki:Winter_town) and then the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) during his first ride on [Dancer](wiki:Dancer) outside of Winterfell.(wiki:Main_gates_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
Bran watches [northmen](wiki:Northmen) arrive at Winterfell after [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls the banners after the [arrest of Eddard Stark](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), and the boy watches as Robb's host departs the castle to march to Eddard's rescue.(wiki:Main_gates_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) presents the bodies of two [miller's sons](wiki:Miller's_sons) as those of the missing Bran and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) and has their heads displayed on the wall by the main gate.(wiki:Main_gates_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Main_gates_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Theon passes through the gatehouse to meet with Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) and Lord [Cley Cerwyn](wiki:Cley_Cerwyn) in the market of the [winter town](wiki:Winter_town). After the [battle outside the gates of Winterfell](wiki:Battle_outside_the_gates_of_Winterfell), Theon allows [Red Helm](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) to enter the castle.(wiki:Main_gates_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
In the aftermath of the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell) by [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton), the massive oak-and-iron gates are charred and askew and the drawbridge is a tangle of broken chains and missing planks.(wiki:Main_gates_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), the new [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North) after the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), holds [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark) of his son [Ramsay](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) to "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)" at Winterfell. Roose has squatters raise stout new gates to replace those which had burned, and then he has them all hanged.(wiki:Main_gates_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
The great main gates become choked with ice and snow during a blizzard.(wiki:Main_gates_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) When Roose decides to send the feuding [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) and [Manderlys](wiki:House_Manderly) against [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), he orders the Freys to gather by the main gates and the Manderlys by the [east gate](wiki:East_gate).(wiki:Main_gates_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

[Mors Umber](wiki:Mors_Umber) has his green boys dig a pit trap outside Winterfell. When the Freys ride out of Winterfell's main gate, several fall into the trap. Ser [Hosteen Frey](wiki:Hosteen_Frey) loses his horse and Ser [Aenys Frey](wiki:Aenys_Frey) breaks his neck and dies.(wiki:Main_gates_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-TWOW_3-1)
