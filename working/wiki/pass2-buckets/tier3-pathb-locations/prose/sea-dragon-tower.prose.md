## Appearances & Description

Sea Dragon Tower is shaped like a dragon calmly gazing across the water.(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) The tower has apartments for nobles.(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Tower.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Tower.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
The [maester](wiki:Maester)'s chambers are located high atop Sea Dragon Tower.(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The room has a long wooden table.(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Steep iron steps lead up to the [rookery](wiki:Rookery), and beneath the rookery stair is a workroom with the maester's potions.(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Tower.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [Gargoyles](wiki:Gargoyle) of a [hellhound](wiki:Hellhound) and a [wyvern](wiki:Wyvern) are found at the balcony outside the chamber.(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Tower.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
The turnpike stairs of the tower are narrow and twisting.(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Tower.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Noble prisoners can be held comfortably in Sea Dragon Tower's cells.(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Tower.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The tower also has a cellar.(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Origins

Dowager Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) kept her chambers in Sea Dragon Tower during the reign of her nephew, King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen). (wiki:Sea_Dragon_Tower.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
[Androw Farman](wiki:Androw_Farman) killed Maester [Anselm](wiki:Anselm) in the tower's rookery.(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Tower.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
After three days of labor in her apartments in Sea Dragon Tower, [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) gave birth to the stillborn [Visenya](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Rhaenyra)).(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Tower.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) After King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) fed Rhaenyra to [Sunfyre](wiki:Sunfyre) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), her ladies-in-waiting were confined to the tower's cells to await ransom.(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Tower.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Maesters [Cressen](wiki:Cressen) and [Pylos](wiki:Pylos) show a [white raven](wiki:White_raven) to Princess [Shireen Baratheon](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon) and [Patchface](wiki:Patchface).(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Tower.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Pylos teaches Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) how to read in the maester's chambers. On one occasion, Davos reads a letter from Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) warning about [wildling](wiki:Wildling) threats to the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
While Pylos is teaching sums to [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm), Davos arrives at Sea Dragon Tower with Ser [Rolland Storm](wiki:Rolland_Storm), Ser [Gerald Gower](wiki:Gerald_Gower), Ser [Andrew Estermont](wiki:Andrew_Estermont), Ser [Triston of Tally Hill](wiki:Triston_of_Tally_Hill), and [Lewys the Fishwife](wiki:Lewys_the_Fishwife). The [king's men](wiki:King's_men) take Edric from Dragonstone so that he cannot be given to [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre)'s fires.(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
