## Appearances & Description

The Peach is located at the eastern side of [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept)'s main market square. It has white-washed walls, and its sign is a wooden shingle painted like a peach with a bite taken out of it. Next to the inn are stables.(wiki:Peach.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The inn is run by [Tansy](wiki:Tansy_(innkeep)), while [Alyce](wiki:Alyce), [Bella](wiki:Bella), [Cass](wiki:Cass), [Helly](wiki:Helly), [Jyzene](wiki:Jyzene), and [Lanna](wiki:Lanna_(Peach)) work there as [prostitutes](wiki:Prostitution), or "peaches".(wiki:Peach.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Origins

During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) hid at the Peach before the [Battle of the Bells](wiki:Battle_of_the_Bells). According to [Leslyn](wiki:Leslyn), Robert laid with all of the inn's prostitutes, but his favorite was [Bella](wiki:Bella)'s mother.(wiki:Peach.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) An alleged son of [Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams) also resides at the inn.(wiki:Peach.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

The Peach is damaged when [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept) is attacked during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). When [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) and [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) arrive in the town with a group of the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), however, they see that half of the inn's roof has been patched over. After the visitors are bathed, [Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams) performs in the common room, singing "[Six Maids in a Pool](wiki:Six_Maids_in_a_Pool)", "[The Maids that Bloom in Spring](wiki:The_Maids_that_Bloom_in_Spring)", and "[Two Hearts That Beat as One](wiki:Two_Hearts_That_Beat_as_One)".(wiki:Peach.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
[Bella](wiki:Bella) flirts with [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) and [Jack-Be-Lucky](wiki:Jack-Be-Lucky), and she reveals that she does not charge friends of [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr) and [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion). [Greenbeard](wiki:Greenbeard) flirts with two girls, while [Anguy](wiki:Anguy) and [Lem](wiki:Lem) disappear into private rooms with their own women. Later, [Tansy](wiki:Tansy_(innkeep)) reveals to Lem and [Harwin](wiki:Harwin) of the release of Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) from captivity at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) by [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) and [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth), which Arya disbelieves. An old man propositions young Arya, but [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) sends him away by protecting his "sister".
Arya and her companions sleep on a large mattress under eaves at the top of the stairs. The next morning, they awake to find the [Mad Huntsman](wiki:Mad_Huntsman) has returned to Stoney Sept with [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) in custody.(wiki:Peach.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)(wiki:Peach.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
