## Appearances & Description

The tower is lopsided beneath the weight of slagged stone, the result of [dragonfire](wiki:Dragonfire).(wiki:Kingspyre_Tower.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) There is an arching stone bridge that connects it to the [Widow's Tower](wiki:Widow's_Tower).(wiki:Kingspyre_Tower.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) The castellan's chambers are within this tower, and are as spacious as any lords.(wiki:Kingspyre_Tower.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
The lord's bedchamber contains a hearth and a featherbad. Half a flight of stairs below is the [solar](wiki:Solar), a spare, drafty room that is as large as a hall in a smaller castle. The solar contains a huge oaken table.(wiki:Kingspyre_Tower.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Origins

The original name for the tower is unknown, and it received its new name after the [burning of Harrenhal](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest). King [Harren the Black](wiki:Harren_Hoare) and his sons burned alive in the tower when King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) had his dragon, [Balerion](wiki:Balerion), attack the castle with [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame).(wiki:Kingspyre_Tower.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) led the [assault on Harrenhal](wiki:Assault_on_Harrenhal) at the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). The castellan of Harrenhal, the elderly Ser [Simon Strong](wiki:Simon_Strong), was quick to strike his banners when the prince lighted atop Kingspyre on [Caraxes](wiki:Caraxes).(wiki:Kingspyre_Tower.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Later in the war, [Alys Rivers](wiki:Alys_Rivers) watched the [Battle Above the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Above_the_Gods_Eye) between Caraxes and [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) from the top of Kingspyre Tower. When the two dragons crashed into the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye), a gout of water as tall as Kingspyre is said to have splashed up.(wiki:Kingspyre_Tower.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

When Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) holds Harrenhal during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), he keeps his apartments in Kingspyre Tower.(wiki:Kingspyre_Tower.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch), the castellan after Tywin leaves, resides in the tower. The [Wailing Tower](wiki:Wailing_Tower) is closed off, so servants such as the incognito [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) move to cellars beneath Kingspyre.(wiki:Kingspyre_Tower.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) takes up residence in Kingspyre after the [fall of Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal) to [northmen](wiki:North), and he leeches himself in the lord's bedchamber. After bringing Lord Bolton his supper, Arya observes him throw a book into a hearth. That night, Arya sneaks through a narrow cellar window during her escape from Harrenhal, and she steals a dagger and a sheepskin map of the [Trident](wiki:Trident) from Roose's table.(wiki:Kingspyre_Tower.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
