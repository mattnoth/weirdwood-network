## Origins

Tolos was founded by or was subject to the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold). In the aftermath of the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria), Tolos and [Elyria](wiki:Elyria) formed ties with the [Ghiscari](wiki:Ghiscari) cities of [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay).(wiki:Tolos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_doom_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ships from Tolos are spotted by [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) in the harbors of [Volantis](wiki:Volantis).(wiki:Tolos.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) sends missions to Tolos hoping to forge an alliance to balance out the enmity of [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) in the south, but Tolos joins [Mantarys](wiki:Mantarys) in allying with Yunkai.(wiki:Tolos.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) The envoys sent to Tolos return and report the city declares her a [whore](wiki:Whore) and demands she return Meereen to its [Great Masters](wiki:Great_Masters).(wiki:Tolos.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) A carrack out of Tolos is soon spotted among the naval fleet that closes Meereen from the rest of [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay).(wiki:Tolos.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
Tolosi slingers are spotted among the Yunkish army marching up the coast road to Meereen.(wiki:Tolos.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) witnesses a group of Tolosi torture and kill three slaves, noting the extreme damage the slinger's soft [lead](wiki:Lead) balls do to the body.(wiki:Tolos.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
The [bloody flux](wiki:Bloody_flux) is later reported to have taken hold among the Tolosi forces within the [Yunkish](wiki:Yunkish) host outside of Meereen, spreading the disease across the [Skahazadhan](wiki:Skahazadhan) river to the third [Ghiscari legion](wiki:Ghiscari_legions).(wiki:Tolos.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
