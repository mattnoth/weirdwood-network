## Appearances & Description

A covered bridge connects the fourth floor of the bell tower with the second floor of the [rookery](wiki:Rookery).(wiki:Bell_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) sees the [red comet](wiki:Red_comet) in the sky above the Bell Tower and the [Guards Hall](wiki:Guards_Hall).(wiki:Bell_Tower.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
After his [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell), [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) sees a half-[moon](wiki:Moon) above the Bell Tower.(wiki:Bell_Tower.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The bridge between the Bell Tower and the rookery collapses into the yard as a result of the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell) by [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton).(wiki:Bell_Tower.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) includes the bell tower and its covered bridge while making a snow castle of Winterfell at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie).(wiki:Bell_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
