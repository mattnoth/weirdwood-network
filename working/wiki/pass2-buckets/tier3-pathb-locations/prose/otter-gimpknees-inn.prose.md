## Origins

Most of [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport) was destroyed during [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion) by the host of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) in a [battle at Lordsport](wiki:Battle_at_Lordsport). Rebuilt after the war, the waterside inn of [Otter Gimpknee](wiki:Otter_Gimpknee) is twice the size of its predecessor. Its lower story is made of cut stone, while the two upper floors are timber.(wiki:Otter_Gimpknee's_inn.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

When [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) returns to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands), he sees a [prostitute](wiki:Prostitute) call to [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibben) sailors from the inn.(wiki:Otter_Gimpknee's_inn.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) stays overnight(wiki:Otter_Gimpknee's_inn.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) at the inn after her journey to [Great Wyk](wiki:Great_Wyk).(wiki:Otter_Gimpknee's_inn.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) With Lord [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) calling his bannermen to [Pyke](wiki:Pyke_(island)), the inn is crowded and boys from [House Goodbrother](wiki:House_Goodbrother) patronize the inn's prostitutes.(wiki:Otter_Gimpknee's_inn.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
