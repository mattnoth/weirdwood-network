## Appearances & Description

The godsway is a broad grassy road which begins at the bronze [Horse Gate](wiki:Horse_Gate) and ends at the [Mother of Mountains](wiki:Mother_of_Mountains).(wiki:Godsway.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) It is lined with artifacts and monuments plundered by the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki), including statues of [gods](wiki:Gods), kings, heroes, maidens, and monsters.(wiki:Godsway.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Those displayed include:(wiki:Godsway.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
deities with broken thunderbolts
stone kings on thrones
maidens dancing on [marble](wiki:Marble) plinths
maidens draped in flowers
maidens pouring from empty jars
[dragons](wiki:Dragon) of black [iron](wiki:Iron) with jeweled eyes
roaring [griffins](wiki:Griffin)
[manticores](wiki:Manticore) ready to strike with barbed tails
a moss-covered stone monolith fifty feet tall
a woman with six breasts and the head of a [ferret](wiki:Ferret_(mammal))

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

The [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) of Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) rides the godsway after arriving at [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak). [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) is dismissive of most of the statues. [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) finds some beautiful and others ugly; Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) assumes the latter came from the [Shadow Lands](wiki:Shadow_Lands) beyond [Asshai](wiki:Asshai).(wiki:Godsway.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
After Daenerys ceremonially eats a [stallion heart](wiki:Stallion_heart), a procession takes the road to the [Womb of the World](wiki:Womb_of_the_World) so that she can cleanse herself in its waters. She and Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) have sex by the lakeside, and they then ride on their horses along the godsway to Drogo's hall in Vaes Dothrak.(wiki:Godsway.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Daenerys takes the godsway when she visits the [Western Market](wiki:Western_Market).(wiki:Godsway.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
