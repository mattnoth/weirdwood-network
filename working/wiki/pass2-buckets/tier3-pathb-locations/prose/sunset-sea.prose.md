## Origins

In [Songs the Drowned Men Sing](wiki:Songs_the_Drowned_Men_Sing), Maester [Kirth](wiki:Kirth) suggested that the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair) was created by a people from west of the Sunset Sea,(wiki:Sunset_Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_dawn_age.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) and Archmaester [Haereg](wiki:Haereg) proposed that the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) descend from these visitors.(wiki:Sunset_Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
The ironborn of antiquity used their longships to dominate the western coast of Westeros, initially raiding the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) of the [green lands](wiki:Green_lands)(wiki:Sunset_Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) but eventually turning to conquest in the era of [driftwood kings](wiki:Driftwood_king).(wiki:Sunset_Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) During the reign of [Qhored Hoare](wiki:Qhored_Hoare) the ironborn controlled the western shore of Westeros from the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor) to [Bear Island](wiki:Bear_Island), but his gains were gradually lost by his descendants.(wiki:Sunset_Sea.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) The First Men of the continent eventually fought back with longships of their own and used stone fortifications to defend against the raiders,(wiki:Sunset_Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2) and skilled [Andal](wiki:Andal_invasion) sailors accelerated the ironborn's fall.(wiki:Sunset_Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_iron_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
Thousands of years ago, [Brandon the Shipwright](wiki:Brandon_Stark_(Shipwright)), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), tried to sail across the Sunset Sea but was never heard from again. His son, [Brandon the Burner](wiki:Brandon_Stark_(Burner)), destroyed the northern shipyards in his grief.(wiki:Sunset_Sea.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)(wiki:Sunset_Sea.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
Claiming the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), King [Arlan III Durrandon](wiki:Arlan_III_Durrandon) extended the borders of the [Kingdom of the Storm](wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Storm) from the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) in the east to the Sunset Sea in the west. However, his descendant, [Arrec](wiki:Arrec_Durrandon), lost the riverlands to [Harwyn Hoare](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers).(wiki:Sunset_Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_andals_in_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
[Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) once considered flying atop [Meraxes](wiki:Meraxes) to see what was beyond the Sunset Sea,(wiki:Sunset_Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) but she was killed during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War).(wiki:Sunset_Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
In [56 AC](wiki:56_AC) [Elissa Farman](wiki:Elissa_Farman), Ser [Eustace Hightower](wiki:Eustace_Hightower), and Ser [Norman Hightower](wiki:Norman_Hightower) sailed west from [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) to discover what is beyond the Sunset Sea.(wiki:Sunset_Sea.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) [Autumn Moon](wiki:Autumn_Moon) sank during a storm, with some on Eustace's [Lady Meredith](wiki:Lady_Meredith) claiming they saw a [kraken](wiki:Kraken) attack Norman's ship. Elissa's [Sun Chaser](wiki:Sun_Chaser) discovered the uninhabited islands Aegon, Rhaenys, and Visenya. While Eustace chose to return to Westeros, Elissa continued exploring west but was never seen again. Years later, [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) believed that he saw Sun Chaser in [Asshai](wiki:Asshai).(wiki:Sunset_Sea.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
Lord [Alton Greyjoy](wiki:Alton_Greyjoy), known as the Holy Fool, attempted to conquer lands beyond the [Lonely Light](wiki:Lonely_Light).(wiki:Sunset_Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), the Red Kraken, ruled much of the Sunset Sea during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) until his murder at [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle).(wiki:Sunset_Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_red_kraken.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Lord [Dagon Greyjoy](wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy) also attempted to make the lands along the sea subject to the ironborn again, but was eventually defeated by [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen).(wiki:Sunset_Sea.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) invade the [north](wiki:North) by attacking the [Stony Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore), the [Fever River](wiki:Fever_River), and [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte) near the [Bay of Ice](wiki:Bay_of_Ice).

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

During the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot), [Gylbert Farwynd](wiki:Gylbert_Farwynd), Lord of the [Lonely Light](wiki:Lonely_Light), puts his name forth for [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), supported by three champions who are his sons. He promises to lead the ironborn to lands beyond the Sunset Sea where every man would be a king, but his claims are quickly dismissed.(wiki:Sunset_Sea.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
The ironborn under [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North), attack the [Shield Islands](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields) and the [Arbor](wiki:Invasion_of_the_Arbor).(wiki:Sunset_Sea.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
