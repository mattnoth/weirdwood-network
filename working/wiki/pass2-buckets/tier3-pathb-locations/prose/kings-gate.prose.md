## Appearances & Description

The King's Gate is by the southern corner of the southwest wall of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), connecting the western end of the [River Row](wiki:River_Row) to the [tourney grounds](wiki:Tourney_grounds_(King's_Landing)) outside the city by the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush). There is a sally port in it,(wiki:King's_Gate.cite_ref-Racok59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) and a stable near it.(wiki:King's_Gate.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Heads of the condemned can be mounted on spikes above the King's Gate.(wiki:King's_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:King's_Gate.cite_ref-Ragot21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Origins

In [19 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) decreed that new city walls with seven gates, each with a massive gatehouse and defensive towers, be built to protect [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Grand Maester [Gawen](wiki:Gawen) and Ser [Osmund Strong](wiki:Osmund_Strong), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), headed the construction, which took place between [20](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)–[26 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:King's_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) had his own wife and queen, [Alys Harroway](wiki:Alys_Harroway), tortured to death and executed. Following her death, Alys's body was cut into seven parts and mounted on spikes above each of the seven gates of the city, one of which was above the King's Gate.(wiki:King's_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:King's_Gate.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Princess [Aerea Targaryen](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen) spent part of the regency of [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) working in a stable near the King's Gate, which she considered to be the happiest time of her life.(wiki:King's_Gate.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the [gold cloaks](wiki:Gold_cloaks) at the King's Gate fled amid the [riot of King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)). Ser [Perkin the Flea](wiki:Perkin_the_Flea) recruited scores of former gold cloaks during the [Moon of the Three Kings](wiki:Moon_of_the_Three_Kings), and he gained control over the King's Gate, the [Dragon Gate](wiki:Dragon_Gate), and the [Lion Gate](wiki:Lion_Gate) for [Trystane Truefyre](wiki:Trystane_Truefyre).(wiki:King's_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
During the [regency of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III), the King's Hand, Ser [Tyland Lannister](wiki:Tyland_Lannister) ordered the city gates to be strengthened so they could better resist attacks from within the city walls.(wiki:King's_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) led his [northmen](wiki:Northmen) through the King's Gate during the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:King's_Gate.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

After leaving a riverside evening feast during the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney), [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) climb in a cart near the King's Gate and are taken through [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:King's_Gate.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Following the death of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) and ascension of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), travelers are only allowed to depart [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) from the [Iron Gate](wiki:Iron_Gate) or the King's Gate, both of which are manned by [red cloaks](wiki:Red_cloaks). Spying from the rooftop of an inn by the King's Gate, [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) watches the guards search wagons.(wiki:King's_Gate.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) prevents the [northmen](wiki:Northmen) who escort Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) to King's Landing from entering the city.(wiki:King's_Gate.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) When Tyrion sends Cleos back to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), the northmen rejoin him at the King's Gate for the journey west.(wiki:King's_Gate.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
After ships land by the [tourney grounds](wiki:Tourney_grounds_(King's_Landing)), the gate is assaulted with a battering ram by the army of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) rides to the King's Gate, where the gatehouse square is littered with wounded. Sandor refuses to lead a sortie and instead deserts from the battle. Tyrion and Ser [Mandon Moore](wiki:Mandon_Moore) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) instead lead a detachment out of a sally port. After ending the threat of the ram, they ride toward the [River Gate](wiki:River_Gate).(wiki:King's_Gate.cite_ref-Racok59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Ser [Osney Kettleblack](wiki:Osney_Kettleblack) later reports that the King's Gate is again being rammed.(wiki:King's_Gate.cite_ref-Racok61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) meets his betrothed, [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), at the King's Gate when she arrives at King's Landing.(wiki:King's_Gate.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
Tyrion finds mud, ash, and bones when he investigates the land outside of the King's Gate. He reports to his father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), that the gate is splintered and should be replaced.(wiki:King's_Gate.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) is sent by his sister, Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), to inspect the city gates. According to him the King's Gate needs to be replaced.(wiki:King's_Gate.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
