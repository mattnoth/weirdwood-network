## Origins

Stonehelm was built to oversee the [Slayne](wiki:Slayne).(wiki:Stonehelm.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_the_men_of_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) During the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to the stormlands, a [battle on the river Slayne](wiki:Battle_on_the_river_Slayne) occurred near the castle.(wiki:Stonehelm.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_andals_in_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
During the [Second Dornish War](wiki:Second_Dornish_War), the [Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Aenys_I)) divided his forces and sent Lord [Walter Wyl](wiki:Walter_Wyl) to attack Stonehelm. Lord [Orys Baratheon](wiki:Orys_Baratheon) smashed the [Dornish](wiki:Dornishmen) host beneath the walls of Stonehelm, however, and captured Walter.(wiki:Stonehelm.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Stonehelm.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
[Fletcher Dick](wiki:Fletcher_Dick) was raised in a village near Stonehelm.(wiki:Stonehelm.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_the_men_of_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
