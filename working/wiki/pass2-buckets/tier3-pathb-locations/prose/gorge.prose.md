## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

A wildling captured in the Gorge by [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand) claims that [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) is gathering the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) to a secret stronghold [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:Gorge.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) commands the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) against the wildlings of the [Weeper](wiki:Weeper) in a [fight at the Bridge of Skulls](wiki:Fight_at_the_Bridge_of_Skulls) and the gloomy Gorge.(wiki:Gorge.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The commander of the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower), Ser [Denys Mallister](wiki:Denys_Mallister), writes to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) to report sightings of fires in the night north of the Gorge.(wiki:Gorge.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Denys believes wildlings are amassing to force the Bridge of Skulls once more.(wiki:Gorge.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
