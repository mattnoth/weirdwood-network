## Appearances & Description

The main building sits along the shore where the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) of the [Trident](wiki:Trident) bends. It has long, low wings that stretch out along the river. The lower story is made of grey stone, the upper one of whitewashed wood, and the roof is [slate](wiki:Slate).(wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The building has thick, diamond-shaped windowpanes and a cellar.(wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) The common room has tables, a hearth, along with the kitchen.(wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
The inn is surrounded by outbuildings, including stables. Behind it is an arbor with apples trees and a small garden.(wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) The inn brews a fine [ale](wiki:Ale), and [cider](wiki:Cider) is also available.(wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
Swinging from an iron post at the end of the inn's wooden dock is a flaking shingle painted to depict [Torrhen Stark](wiki:Torrhen_Stark) kneeling with his hands together in submission.(wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) A painted sign of the king is also located above the inn's door.(wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
A cottage and a village are located about two miles and six miles east of the inn, respectively.(wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)(wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Ser [Warren](wiki:Warren)'s towerhouse is located south of the village.(wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

## Origins

[Torrhen Stark](wiki:Torrhen_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), came south with his forces during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest) to oppose [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen). After receiving the submission of [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden), [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) led his host north to confront the [northmen](wiki:Northmen). Torrhen's scouts had seen the aftermath of the [burning of Harrenhal](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal), and the King in the North had heard of Aegon's victory at the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire).(wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Once at the [Trident](wiki:Trident), Torrhen saw Aegon's larger host and the three [dragons](wiki:Dragon) of the Targaryens, [Balerion](wiki:Balerion), [Meraxes](wiki:Meraxes), and [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar). Instead of fighting, the [Starks](wiki:House_Stark) and Targaryens negotiated.(wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) The inn is said to be built where Torrhen submitted(wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8) on the south bank of the Trident.(wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth), Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey), and Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) stop at the inn on their way from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). They meet a man, [Husband](wiki:Husband), who has taken over duties at the inn, although he denies being an innkeep. The trio also meet a crossbow-wielding [boy](wiki:Boy) adopted by the man. The man says that he and his wife had buried the previous innkeep and his family out back. The man's wife, [Sharna](wiki:Sharna), is not there at that time. The travelers are served horse steak, fried onions, stale oatcakes, [ale](wiki:Ale), and [cider](wiki:Cider). After the meal, Brienne overpays the man for three horses but refuses to stay the night. She also disregards his advice on choice of roads, suspecting him to be in league with bandits and leading them to ambush.(wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)
After [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) is captured by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), [Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams) says that Sharna is the new innkeep, and the outlaws bring "Squab" to the inn. [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie) is pleased to have ale and rabbit. Alarmed when [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) announces that soldiers are approaching, Arya breaks [Lem](wiki:Lem)'s nose with her tankard of ale. Her true identity is revealed when she is recognized by [Harwin](wiki:Harwin), one of the returning outlaws and a former [Stark guardsman](wiki:House_Stark_guards).(wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) Hot Pie remains at the inn to help Sharna bake; the youth asks Arya to visit once the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) has ended.(wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
