## Appearances & Description

The Lion Gate is located near the center of the southwest wall, with the [King's Gate](wiki:King's_Gate) and the [tourney grounds](wiki:Tourney_grounds_(King's_Landing)) to the south. The [Goldroad](wiki:Goldroad) to [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) in the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) starts at the Lion Gate.(wiki:Lion_Gate.cite_ref-Rtloiafking.27s_landing.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
The Lion Gate is narrower and less splendid than the [Gate of the Gods](wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods).(wiki:Lion_Gate.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) The [gold cloaks](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing) stationed at the Lion Gate are called "[lions](wiki:Lions)".(wiki:Lion_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Origins

In [19 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) decreed that new city walls with seven gates, each with a massive gatehouse and defensive towers, be built to protect [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Grand Maester [Gawen](wiki:Gawen) and Ser [Osmund Strong](wiki:Osmund_Strong), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), headed the construction, which took place between [20](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)–[26 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Lion_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
In the reign of [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), Maegor had his own wife and queen, [Alys Harroway](wiki:Alys_Harroway), tortured to death and executed. Following her death, Alys's body was cut into seven parts and mounted on spikes above each of the seven gates of the city.(wiki:Lion_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Lion_Gate.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
The [tourney for the completion of the Dragonpit](wiki:Tourney_for_the_completion_of_the_Dragonpit) in [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) was held in [the fields](wiki:Tourney_grounds_(King's_Landing)) west of the Lion Gate and the [King's Gate](wiki:King's_Gate).(wiki:Lion_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Amid the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the [lions](wiki:Lions), the [gold cloaks](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing) at the Lion Gate, joined the mobs during the [riot of King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)).(wiki:Lion_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
During the [regency of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III), the King's Hand, Ser [Tyland Lannister](wiki:Tyland_Lannister), ordered the city gates to be strengthened so they could better resist attacks from within the city walls.(wiki:Lion_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Ser [Adrian Thorne](wiki:Adrian_Thorne) was the captain of the Lion Gate before becoming the [Commander of the City Watch](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing) in [135 AC](wiki:135_AC).(wiki:Lion_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

After the [arrest of Eddard Stark](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark), his daughter [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark) finds that the [Dragon Gate](wiki:Dragon_Gate), the Lion Gate, and the [Old Gate](wiki:Old_Gate) are closed and barred.(wiki:Lion_Gate.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After the funeral of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), Lord [Gyles Rosby](wiki:Gyles_Rosby) tells Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) that he has hired a master stonecarver to sculpt a statue of her father to stand by the Lion Gate.(wiki:Lion_Gate.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) thinks it would have been more appropriate if the procession returning Tywin's body to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) had departed through the Lion Gate rather than the [Gate of the Gods](wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods).(wiki:Lion_Gate.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Jaime is sent by his sister, Cersei, to inspect the city gates.(wiki:Lion_Gate.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
