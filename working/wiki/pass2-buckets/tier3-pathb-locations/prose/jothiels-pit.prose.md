## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

In Astapor, when [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) is inspecting the [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) soldiers that she is considering purchasing, she remarks that whole men are stronger than [eunuchs](wiki:Eunuchs). [Kraznys mo Nakloz](wiki:Kraznys_mo_Nakloz) replies that a bull is strong too, but bulls die every day in the fighting pits.
