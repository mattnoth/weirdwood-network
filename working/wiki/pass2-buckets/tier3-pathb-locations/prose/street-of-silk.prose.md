## Origins

### Early History[ ]

[Rego Draz](wiki:Rego_Draz), the [Pentoshi](wiki:Pentos) [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin) for King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), resided in his manse on the Street of Silk rather than at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Street_of_Silk.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
The whores of the Street of Silk and the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) of [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom) started to refer to Ser [Lucamore Strong](wiki:Lucamore_Strong) as "Ser Lucamore the Lusty" once his three marriages became known to the public.(wiki:Street_of_Silk.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
Before departing [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to marry old Lord [Theomore Manderly](wiki:Theomore_Manderly) in [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor), Princess [Viserra Targaryen](wiki:Viserra_Targaryen) visited the brothels of the Street of Silk with her companions for "one last night of laughter". She died that night in a drunken horse race, however.(wiki:Street_of_Silk.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

### Viserys I[ ]

Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) was a known patron of the brothels of the Street of Silk, and he was in one of them when he referred to his short-lived nephew, [Baelon Targaryen](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)), as the "heir for a day".(wiki:Street_of_Silk.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom) claims Daemon smuggled his niece, Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), to go observe the sex workers.(wiki:Street_of_Silk.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Daemon's [paramour](wiki:Paramour), [Mysaria](wiki:Mysaria), was known to have been a dancing girl in King's Landing,(wiki:Street_of_Silk.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) so she may have worked in one of the brothels along the Street of Silk.
According to Mushroom, Prince [Aegon the Elder](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) fathered a bastard boy on a girl whose maidenhood he won at auction on the Street of Silk in [123 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Street_of_Silk.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3)

### Dance of the Dragons[ ]

After the murder of Prince [Jaehaerys Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_II)) amid the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Ser [Luthor Largent](wiki:Luthor_Largent) and his [gold cloaks](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing) were sent to search after Mysaria and [Cheese](wiki:Cheese_(the_rat-catcher)) in the Street of Silk.(wiki:Street_of_Silk.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
According to Mushroom, Ser [Hugh Hammer](wiki:Hugh_Hammer) celebrated his knighthood by visiting the brothels of the Street of Silk. He beat to death one of Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen)'s [household knights](wiki:Household_knight) after quarreling over the [maidenhood](wiki:Maiden) of a woman.(wiki:Street_of_Silk.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Amid the [riots of King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)), [Gaemon Palehair](wiki:Gaemon_Palehair) was raised up as king by whores at a brothel on the Street of Silk. Gaemon's supporters were based out of the [House of Kisses](wiki:House_of_Kisses) atop [Rhaenys's Hill](wiki:Rhaenys's_Hill).(wiki:Street_of_Silk.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### Aegon III[ ]

During the [Hour of the Wolf](wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf), a brothel on the Street of Silk burned down when a quarrel began over a whore between one of Lord [Elmo Tully](wiki:Elmo_Tully)'s men and one of Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark)'s.(wiki:Street_of_Silk.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
The former [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester), [Orwyle](wiki:Orwyle), who had escaped his imprisonment, hid and worked in a brothel of the Street of Silk named [Mother's](wiki:Mother's_(brothel)) during the [regency of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III) under the alias of "Old Wyl".(wiki:Street_of_Silk.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
According to the rumors of the [Maiden's Day curse](wiki:Maiden's_Day_curse), the "three Jeynes" ([Jeyne Smallwood](wiki:Jeyne_Smallwood), [Jeyne Mooton](wiki:Jeyne_Mooton) and [Jeyne Merryweather](wiki:Jeyne_Merryweather)) visited the brothels along the Street of Silk.(wiki:Street_of_Silk.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) The following year, other rumors claimed Lady [Larra Rogare](wiki:Larra_Rogare) also visited the brothels.(wiki:Street_of_Silk.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_end_of_the_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) commands Ser [Addam Marbrand](wiki:Addam_Marbrand) to have the [gold cloaks](wiki:Gold_cloaks) search the Street of Silk for his brother, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), who has gone missing after the murder of their father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister).(wiki:Street_of_Silk.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) recalls that Ser [Boros Blount](wiki:Boros_Blount) visits the Street of Silk despite the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) vows of chastity.(wiki:Street_of_Silk.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
According to Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), Septon [Raynard](wiki:Raynard) is known for frequenting the brothels of the Street of Silk.(wiki:Street_of_Silk.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Brothel keepers complain that the [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow) is preaching against [prostitution](wiki:Prostitution).(wiki:Street_of_Silk.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
