## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

During the resolution of the [siege of Raventree](wiki:Siege_of_Raventree), Lord [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken) asks Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) for the east bank of the Widow's Wash from [Crossbow Ridge](wiki:Crossbow_Ridge) to [Rutting Meadow](wiki:Rutting_Meadow) and all the islands in the stream for subduing Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood). Jaime agrees to the transfer of Crossbow Ridge but not the rest of the shore.(wiki:Widow's_Wash.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
After leaving Raventree, Jaime's [column](wiki:Column) follows the Widow's Wash until dusk, when they cross a shallow ford and take shelter in nearby [Pennytree](wiki:Pennytree).(wiki:Widow's_Wash.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
