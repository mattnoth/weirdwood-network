## Origins

According to [The Seven-Pointed Star](wiki:The_Seven-Pointed_Star), [Hugor of the Hill](wiki:Hugor_of_the_Hill) had a vision of a "golden land amidst towering mountains".(wiki:Vale_of_Arryn_(valley).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) During the [Andal invasion](wiki:Andal_invasion), King [Gerold Grafton](wiki:Gerold_Grafton_(king)) began the development of [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown) into the only city of the Vale. King [Robar II Royce](wiki:Robar_II_Royce) united many of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) of the Vale against the [Andals](wiki:Andals), and he defeated the [Hammer of the Hills](wiki:Hammer_of_the_Hills), who had held the eastern Vale. Robar's alliance was crushed by [Artys I Arryn](wiki:Artys_I_Arryn) at the [Battle of Seven Stars](wiki:Battle_of_Seven_Stars), however.(wiki:Vale_of_Arryn_(valley).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Some First Men who refused to submit to [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) fled the Vale for the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon), becoming the [mountain clans](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans).(wiki:Vale_of_Arryn_(valley).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Queen Regent [Sharra Arryn](wiki:Sharra_Arryn) fortified the [Bloody Gate](wiki:Bloody_Gate) to block access to the Vale of Arryn. [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) flew on [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), however, and secured the submission of the Vale.(wiki:Vale_of_Arryn_(valley).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
