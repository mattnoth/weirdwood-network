## Origins

The head of House Strickland rose in rebellion with [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) during the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion). After the defeat of the rebels at the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field), he fled across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) with Ser [Aegor Rivers](wiki:Aegor_Rivers) and the other exiles. Four generations of Stricklands have served in the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company).(wiki:House_Strickland.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
