## Origins

The [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands) are a considerable sea strength and were the first line of defense during the days when the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) raiders tried to sail up the [Mander](wiki:Mander) to sack the wealth of the [Reach](wiki:Reach).(wiki:House_Serry.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Ser [Talbert Serry](wiki:Talbert_Serry) defends the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands) against the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), and dies in a duel with [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy). After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields) and the death of his son and heir, Lord [Osbert Serry](wiki:Osbert_Serry) is reported to have fled to [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden).(wiki:House_Serry.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
