# Edmure yields Riverrun

The resolution of the inherited [Siege of Riverrun](siege-of-riverrun). [Jaime](jaime-lannister),
forbidden by his vow to Catelyn from taking arms against the Tullys, ends the siege by coercion
rather than assault: in the bath-tent parley he tells [Edmure](edmure-tully) that if he does not
yield, Jaime will storm the castle, put everyone to the sword, raze it, and divert the Tumblestone
over the ruins — and, his unborn child by Roslin Frey notwithstanding, *"I'll send him to you when
he's born. With a trebuchet."* Broken, Edmure waits most of a day, then hauls down the direwolf of
Stark in token of surrender, and Riverrun passes bloodlessly to [Emmon Frey](emmon-frey) and the
crown. The yielding also creates the confusion that masks
[the Blackfish's escape](blackfish-escapes-riverrun).
