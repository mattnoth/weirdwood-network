## NEW NODES NEEDED

### A-NEW-01: `appointment-of-davos-as-hand`
- **type:** event.incident
- **container:** wo5k (this is Stannis's political recovery from the Blackwater, directly part of the War of the Five Kings)
- **summary:** After releasing Davos from Dragonstone's dungeon, Stannis Baratheon formally elevates him in the Chamber of the Painted Table: knight of House Seaworth becomes Lord of the Rainwood, Admiral of the Narrow Sea, and Hand of the King. The scene includes the oath (ASOS Davos IV) and is the formal turning point in Davos's arc — from prisoner to the king's most trusted counselor. Melisandre witnesses, arriving just after the appointment.
- **key quote:** "Rise again, Davos Seaworth, and rise as Lord of the Rainwood, Admiral of the Narrow Sea, and Hand of the King." (asos-davos-04.md:203)
- **judgment:** Merits a hub rather than just dyadic edges because it has multiple participants (Stannis, Davos, Melisandre as arriving witness) and an outgoing causal seam: the appointment ENABLES Davos's later actions as Hand (reading the NW letter to Stannis, smuggling Edric). However this is a LOW-PRIORITY new node — the load-bearing content is already partially covered by the MOTIVATES(davos-seaworth → stannis-baratheon) edge from s155 and the stannis-retreats arc. Only mint if the hub would own a unique causal edge.

### A-NEW-02: `davos-smuggles-edric-storm-to-safety`
- **type:** event.incident
- **container:** wo5k
- **summary:** During the nightly R'hllor prayer service at Dragonstone (ASOS Davos VI), Davos leads a small cell of king's men — Ser Andrew Estermont, Ser Gerald Gower, the Bastard of Nightsong, Lewys the Fishwife, and Omer Blackberry — to spirit Edric Storm off the island while Melisandre and the queen's men are occupied at the nightfire. Edric is taken to the postern gate and rowed out to the Lyseni galley Mad Prendos. Davos then returns alone to face Stannis's judgment.
- **key quote:** "He is aboard a Lyseni galley, safely out to sea." (asos-davos-06.md:176)
- **judgment:** This event hub OWNS a unique causal edge: it ENABLES Stannis's decision to march north (davos-seaworth MOTIVATES stannis-baratheon is already built, but the smuggling event is the concrete action that removes the sacrifice option, forcing Stannis to find another way). Also has multiple participants not yet reified against this event. MINT this hub.

### A-NEW-03: `davos-sent-to-fetch-rickon-from-skagos`
- **type:** event.incident
- **container:** north (the commission is about Rickon Stark, the North's succession, and White Harbor's allegiance — squarely [north] container)
- **summary:** In a private chamber in New Castle, White Harbor, Lord Wyman Manderly reveals his deception to Davos and names his price for Manderly allegiance to Stannis: Davos must sail to Skagos (the "isle of unicorns and cannibals") and retrieve Rickon Stark, the last known legitimate male heir of House Stark, along with his direwolf Shaggydog. Wex Pyke's intelligence (communicated by chalk and gesture, relayed by Robett Glover) is the source of the knowledge that Rickon fled to Skagos. The scene closes with Wex's dagger quivering in the sheepskin map — implicitly marking Skagos — and Davos contemplating the cannibals who break their fast on human flesh.
- **key quote:** "That is my price, Lord Davos. Smuggle me back my liege lord, and I will take Stannis Baratheon as my king." (adwd-davos-04.md:211)
- **judgment:** THIS IS THE PRIMARY MISSING HUB. Mint unconditionally. The SEEKS dyads for Davos and Wyman already exist; this hub provides the event-level anchor for the commission and its causal seams. Three key outgoing edges: ENABLES(→ stannis gains white harbor allegiance / grand-northern-conspiracy ENABLES stannis-march-on-winterfell), the MOTIVATES of Wyman from Rickon-vs-Bolton logic, and a TRAVELS_TO(davos → skagos) that is implied but off-page.
