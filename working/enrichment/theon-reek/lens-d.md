## Notes

### Seams considered and REJECTED (agency-collapse / over-distal)

1. **`balon-declares-himself-king` CAUSES `capture-of-winterfell`** — rejected as over-distal. Balon declares himself king and launches the invasion, but Theon's independent decision to abandon the Stony Shore assignment and seize Winterfell is unauthorized by Balon ("Your lord father commanded us to harry the coast, no more," acok-theon-03.md:89). There is an active human actor (Theon) breaking the causal chain. Better: `balon-declares-himself-king` CAUSES `ironborn-invasion-of-the-north` (already exists), and `ironborn-invasion-of-the-north` ENABLES `capture-of-winterfell` (already exists per baseline). The Theon-divergence is captured by MOTIVATES on the person.

2. **`theon-greyjoy-taken-as-ward` CAUSES `capture-of-winterfell`** — rejected as over-distal agency-collapse. The hostage years shape Theon's psychology but do not proximately cause the capture — two decades and many intervening decisions separate them. MOTIVATES is correct; CAUSES is not.

3. **`robb-receives-false-news-of-brans-death` MOTIVATES Red Wedding chain** — rejected as too far removed and speculative. The fake deaths contribute to Robb's grief and fractured state, but attributing the Red Wedding directly to this event skips Robb's broken vow to Frey, Jaime's release, Tywin's plotting, etc. Too many proximate actors intervene. VERIFY would be required and this is theory-adjacent territory.

4. **`sack-of-winterfell` ENABLES `pink-letter-delivered`** — rejected as over-distal. The letter is proximate to the ESCAPE, not the sack. The sack → Theon-as-captive → Dreadfort-breaking → Moat Cailin → Winterfell-wedding → escape → letter is too long a causal chain to wire directly. Use `theon-carries-jeyne-up-battlements-stairs` TRIGGERS `pink-letter-delivered` (row 14) as the proximate link.

5. **`wedding-of-ramsay-bolton-and-arya-stark` CAUSES `battle-outside-winterfell` (future Stannis battle)** — rejected as over-distal. The battle is contingent on many factors not yet resolved in ADWD and the node `battle-outside-winterfell` may not yet be built. Theory-gated territory. Blocked.

6. **`theon-greyjoy-taken-as-ward` ENABLES `capture-of-winterfell`** — rejected. ENABLES implies structural possibility-creation; the hostage years do not enable the capture, they shape Theon's motives. MOTIVATES (row 1) is correct.

### Blocked on unbuilt nodes

- **`theon-fakes-the-deaths-of-bran-and-rickon`** — the moral nadir event node is a Mint Candidate per the baseline. Rows 4, 5, and 20 above all require it. Once Lens A or synthesis mints it, those three edges fire immediately.

- **`the-shieldhall-speech`** — assumed to exist (baseline says pink-letter ENABLES it) but slug not confirmed in baseline dedup map. Row 15 may already be wired; verify before proposing.

- **`theon-and-jeyne-escape-winterfell`** — if a broader escape event node replaces/supplements `theon-carries-jeyne-up-battlements-stairs`, row 14's source_slug changes. Per baseline, lens A/C own the escape node decision; this lens wires from whatever slug they settle on.
