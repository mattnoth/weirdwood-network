## Identity

In [Volantis](volantis), five drunken sailors from the Seven Kingdoms follow the dwarf jousters
[Oppo](oppo) ("Groat") and his sister [Penny](penny) from their square performance and behead Oppo,
mistaking him for [Tyrion Lannister](tyrion-lannister) — chasing the bounty [Cersei](cersei-lannister)
has set on her brother's head. Penny is spared when they realize she is a girl. The killing fuels Penny's
grief and her initial hatred of Tyrion, and is one of a chain of misidentification dwarf-killings (a
juggler in Tyrosh, too) that the published text traces to Cersei's bounty without naming who sent the men.
