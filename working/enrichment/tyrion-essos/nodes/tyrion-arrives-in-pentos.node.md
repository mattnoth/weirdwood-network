## Identity

Smuggled across the narrow sea inside a wine cask by [Varys](varys)'s agents after the murder of
[Tywin](assassination-of-tywin-lannister), [Tyrion Lannister](tyrion-lannister) is decanted in the
cellar of [Illyrio Mopatis](illyrio-mopatis)'s manse in [Pentos](pentos). The sojourn — baths, lavish
feasts, the dragon-revelation pitch, the mushroom test — launches Tyrion's Essos exile and is the
forward terminus that makes [the smuggling out of King's Landing](varys-smuggles-tyrion-out-of-kings-landing)
no longer causally islanded.
