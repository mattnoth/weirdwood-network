## Identity

In a brothel at [Selhorys](selhorys) on the upper [Rhoyne](rhoyne), the exiled knight
[Jorah Mormont](jorah-mormont) — desperate to win back [Daenerys](daenerys-targaryen)'s favour — seizes the
drunken [Tyrion Lannister](tyrion-lannister), meaning to deliver him to the queen at Meereen as a gift.
Tyrion, too drunk to resist, is chained and marched downriver to Volantis. The dyad
`jorah-mormont CAPTURES tyrion-lannister` is reified here as an event with its participant roles and motive.
