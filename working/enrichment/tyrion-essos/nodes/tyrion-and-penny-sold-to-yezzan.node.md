## Identity

The slavers who board the wrecked [Selaesori Qhoran](shipwreck-of-the-selaesori-qhoran) carry the
survivors to the auction block outside [Meereen](meereen), where the gross Yunkish lord
[Yezzan zo Qaggaz](yezzan-zo-qaggaz) buys [Tyrion](tyrion-lannister) and [Penny](penny) for his
grotesquerie and [Jorah Mormont](jorah-mormont) as a dangerous slave to be beaten nightly. Tyrion becomes a
treasure in a gilded, belled collar; the overseer [Nurse](nurse) runs the slaves.
