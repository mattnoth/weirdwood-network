## Identity

The Spears of the Merling King are a hazardous maritime feature — a group of rocks or sea stacks — located in the waters near the Blackwater Bay region of Westeros. After the Battle of the Blackwater, Davos Seaworth was thrown into the water when his ships were destroyed by wildfire. He survived but was stranded on the Spears of the Merling King for many days, during which he hallucinated conversations with the Seven. He was eventually rescued when Khorane Sathmantes brought his ship Shayala's Dance into the Spears to retrieve him and deliver him to Salladhor Saan at Dragonstone.
