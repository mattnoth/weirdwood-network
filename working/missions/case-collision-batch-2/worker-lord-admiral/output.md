## Identity

Lord Admiral is a noble military title in the Seven Kingdoms denoting command over a royal or regional fleet. The title is typically held alongside or in close association with the position of Master of Ships on the small council, which is the official naval advisory role to the crown. Lord Admiral appears to be a distinct command rank rather than strictly a council seat, though in practice the same individual often holds both.

Numerous figures across Westerosi history have held the title. During the reign of Aegon I Targaryen, Lord Aethan Velaryon served as lord admiral and master of ships, leading Stepstones campaigns in 29 and 30 AC alongside Ser Osmund Strong. Manfryd Redwyne was named both master of ships and lord admiral by Jaehaerys I Targaryen — the first holder of that combined post who was not a member of House Velaryon. In 135 AC, Gedmund Peake was named lord admiral and master of ships as a political concession to House Peake following instability at court. During the reign of Joffrey I, Lord Paxter Redwyne was appointed to both roles by Lord Regent Kevan Lannister after Queen Cersei's arrest. In 134 AC, Ser Leo Costayne held the title of lord admiral of the Reach, and was slain on Great Wyk by Arthur Goodbrother during Johanna Lannister's invasion of the Iron Islands.
