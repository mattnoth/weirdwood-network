## Identity

The River Road is a major road in the Seven Kingdoms created or expanded by King Jaehaerys I Targaryen during his long reign, as part of his program of road building that also produced the Kingsroad, the Roseroad, the Ocean Road, and the Goldroad. The River Road runs through the Riverlands; it crosses the Kingsroad near the Ruby Ford at the chief crossing of the Trident, where the Battle of the Trident was fought during Robert's Rebellion. The crossroads of the river road and the kingsroad, near the Ruby Ford, was a strategically significant point — it was where Rhaegar Targaryen attempted to cross the Trident before Robert Baratheon's forces met him in battle.
