## Identity

The drowned men are the acolytes of Aeron Greyjoy, a priest of the Drowned God of the Iron Islands. They wear mottled robes and carry driftwood cudgels as marks of their devotion to the Drowned God. It is uncertain whether acolytes to other priests of the Drowned God are also referred to as "drowned men," or whether the term applies specifically to Aeron's followers.

The drowned men are initiated through a literal drowning ritual: Aeron performs the "kiss of life" on initiates after submerging them. Emmond, one of the boys Aeron drowned near the lands of House Sparr, fought against Aeron during the drowning but was successfully brought back to life and accepted as one of the drowned men. After Balon Greyjoy's death, the drowned men called for Aeron himself to claim the Seastone Chair, but Aeron rejected this demand, instead summoning the lords and captains of the Iron Islands to Old Wyk for a kingsmoot.
