## Identity

The Seventy-nine Sentinels were a legendary group of Night's Watch deserters who abandoned their posts at the Nightfort and fled south of the Wall. They were led by the youngest son of Lord Ryswell, who brought them to the lands of House Ryswell hoping his father would shelter them. Instead, Lord Ryswell had them captured and returned to the Nightfort, where the Lord Commander had all seventy-nine sealed alive within the Wall as punishment for their desertion. The name "Sentinels" derives from their fate — entombed in the ice, they stand watch forever in death. The story became legendary in the North; Lord Ryswell himself, wracked with guilt over condemning his son, eventually joined the Night's Watch as a dying old man so he could stand beside the son he had sent to his death.
