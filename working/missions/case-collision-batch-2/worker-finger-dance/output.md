## Identity

The finger dance is a traditional ironborn game or contest of skill involving axes or blades, in which participants risk losing fingers. The activity is associated with ironborn culture and appears in both historical legend and contemporary settings within the narrative.

In the contemporary story, the finger dance appears as a pastime played by ironborn at gatherings. During the kingsmoot on Old Wyk, Eldred Codd dances the finger dance with Qarl the Maid; Eldred loses a finger during the contest, which lands in Ralf the Limper's drink. At Pyke during Balon Greyjoy's mustering of his bannermen, a man named Rolfe plays the finger dance when Asha Greyjoy calls for him to throw his axe to her. Lady Piper's account records that Urrigon Greyjoy lost half his hand playing the finger dance with his younger brother Aeron; the wound became infected and Urrigon died, prompting Balon Greyjoy to command the same fatal treatment be applied to the maester who had tried to save the hand.

In legend, Harras Hoare is said to have won the crown of the Iron Islands through a contest of the finger dance after the defeat of Rognar II Greyiron, though Archmaester Haereg offers an alternative account that Harras became king by marrying an Andal warlord's daughter.
