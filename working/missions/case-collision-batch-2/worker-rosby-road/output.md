## Identity

The Rosby Road is the road connecting King's Landing to the town of Rosby and continuing northeast to Duskendale on the coast of the narrow sea. It runs through terrain that includes the outskirts of the Crownlands east of King's Landing.

During the period following the Battle of the Blackwater, the Rosby Road was reported safe as far as Duskendale, but the woods beyond were dangerous with outlaws and broken men. The road plays a key role in an incident during the reign of Joffrey I: when Queen Cersei secretly attempted to move Prince Tommen to Rosby for safekeeping following the riot of King's Landing, Tyrion Lannister learned of the plan through Ser Lancel Lannister and ordered Ser Jacelyn Bywater to intercept the party. Bywater and his mounted lancers seized Tommen on the Rosby Road, imprisoning Lord Gyles Rosby and Ser Boros Blount in the process. Blount was subsequently stripped of his white Kingsguard cloak for cowardice during the incident, which led to Cersei appointing Osmund Kettleblack to fill the vacancy.
