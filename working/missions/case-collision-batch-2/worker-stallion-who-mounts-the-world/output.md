## Identity

The Stallion Who Mounts the World is a messianic figure in Dothraki prophecy — a destined leader foretold to unite all khalasars into a single great horde and conquer the known world, burning cities and trampling nations into the dust. The prophecy is held and administered by the dosh khaleen, the council of widowed khaleesis who reside permanently in Vaes Dothrak.

When Daenerys Targaryen performed the stallion heart ceremony in Vaes Dothrak while pregnant with Rhaego, the dosh khaleen proclaimed that her unborn son would be the stallion who mounts the world. The assembled khalasars, including those of Khal Fogo and Khal Jommo, were part of the procession that witnessed and ratified this proclamation. Khal Drogo publicly invoked the prophecy to defend Daenerys from his riders, declaring that their son's fire already filled her.

The prophecy was unfulfilled when Rhaego was stillborn following Mirri Maz Duur's blood magic. Mirri explicitly framed Rhaego's death as preventing the stallion who mounts the world from burning cities and trampling nations. In the House of the Undying, Daenerys later saw a vision of a tall copper-skinned, silver-haired lord beneath a banner of a fiery stallion before a burning city — interpreted as a glimpse of what might have been, had Rhaego survived to fulfill the prophecy.
