## Identity

"Two Hearts That Beat as One" is a song performed in Westeros. It is one of the songs in Tom of Sevenstreams's repertoire, performed by him at the Peach in Stoney Sept alongside "Six Maids in a Pool" and "The Maids that Bloom in Spring." The song is also performed by Mance Rayder, disguised as the bard Abel, during the wedding feast of Ramsay Bolton at Winterfell and the subsequent feasts held there.
