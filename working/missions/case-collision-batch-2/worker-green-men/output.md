## Identity

The green men are a mysterious order said to guard the Isle of Faces in the Gods Eye lake. The order was formed following the Pact between the First Men and the Children of the Forest, which was signed on the Isle of Faces, specifically to protect the ancient weirwood grove on the island.

The green men are associated with the preservation of the Children's sacred places. When the Andals invaded Westeros and drove out the Children, they failed to conquer the Isle of Faces; legend holds that some Children of the Forest survived on the Isle with the aid of the green men. The order remains obscure and rarely mentioned. Howland Reed, after reaching his majority, boldly sought out the Isle of Faces in search of the green men, staying there all winter. Ser Addam Velaryon is said to have sought counsel from the green men on the Isle of Faces before gathering his army to retake Tumbleton during the Dance of the Dragons.
