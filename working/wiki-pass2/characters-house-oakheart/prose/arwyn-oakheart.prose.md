## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Although one of her sons, Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart), is a member of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) of [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Lady Arwyn follows her liege lord, [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), in supporting King [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon). Arwyn is present with the forces of [House Oakheart](wiki:House_Oakheart) in Renly's army that slowly progresses along the [roseroad](wiki:Roseroad) toward the capital, and she attends the [melee at Bitterbridge](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge).(wiki:Arwyn_Oakheart.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

After the death of Renly at [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Arwyn does not support [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Arwyn_Oakheart.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Instead, the Oakhearts follow the [Tyrells](wiki:House_Tyrell) in supporting Joffrey in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Arwyn_Oakheart.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords

The [small council](wiki:Small_council) of King Joffrey grants Lady Oakheart land as a reward for her support in the war.(wiki:Arwyn_Oakheart.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
