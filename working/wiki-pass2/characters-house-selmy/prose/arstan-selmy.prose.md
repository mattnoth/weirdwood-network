## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Lord Arstan sends men to support King [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Arstan_Selmy.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) tells [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) he briefly considered returning to Harvest Hall after his dismissal from the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) by King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_I_Baratheon) as he knew he would be welcomed by his relatives. Not wanting to inflict Joffrey's displeasure on them, he chose to find his true king and die in his service instead.(wiki:Arstan_Selmy.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
