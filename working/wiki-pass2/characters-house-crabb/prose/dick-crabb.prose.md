## Origins

Dick was born and bred at [Crackclaw Point](wiki:Crackclaw_Point) and knows the terrain well, as well as much of its folklore and history. According to him, he carries the blood of Ser [Clarence Crabb](wiki:Clarence_Crabb). He has a sister who was [bedded](wiki:Bedded) by a [knight](wiki:Knight)'s son and then went off to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to take a job as a [whore](wiki:Whore).(wiki:Dick_Crabb.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

Nimble Dick deserted the service of an unnamed [lord](wiki:Lord).(wiki:Dick_Crabb.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)(wiki:Dick_Crabb.cite_ref-Rawoiafdick_crabb.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Dick_Crabb.cite_ref-4)

## Appearances & Description

Dick is scrawny and ill-fed.(wiki:Dick_Crabb.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He has a sharp face with dirty brown hair.(wiki:Dick_Crabb.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) The man can be duplicitous.(wiki:Dick_Crabb.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)(wiki:Dick_Crabb.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Nimble Dick wears a faded, torn doublet where some [lord](wiki:Lord)'s badge had been ripped off.(wiki:Dick_Crabb.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) His [armor](wiki:Armor) is a dinted halfhelm spotted with rust.(wiki:Dick_Crabb.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) In place of a [sword](wiki:Sword), he carries an old nicked dagger.(wiki:Dick_Crabb.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) Dick's cloak is lined with [squirrelskin](wiki:Squirrel).(wiki:Dick_Crabb.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

Dick is a frequent patron of the [Stinking Goose](wiki:Stinking_Goose) in [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool).(wiki:Dick_Crabb.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) He sings songs with a scratchy voice.(wiki:Dick_Crabb.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) speaks with a [dwarf brother](wiki:Dwarf_brother) in [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) during her search for [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), who is believed to be accompanied by the [fool](wiki:Fool) [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard). The dwarf tells Brienne that a drunken Dick had bought [ale](wiki:Ale) for everyone at the [Stinking Goose](wiki:Stinking_Goose) in [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) while boasting of having fooled a fool.(wiki:Dick_Crabb.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Brienne travels to Maidenpool and waits for Dick in the Stinking Goose, offering to buy his [wine](wiki:Wine) after he arrives. In return for two [silver](wiki:Silver) [stags](wiki:Currency), Dick admits that he had seen a fool with "two girls" and that the fool had been seeking passage across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea). After Brienne gives him a [golden](wiki:Gold) dragon, Dick admits that he sent the fool and his two companions to a [smuggler](wiki:Smuggler)'s cove in [Crackclaw Point](wiki:Crackclaw_Point), but he did not tell them that [ships](wiki:Ships) have not landed there in thirty years. Dick offers to take Brienne there, and she promises him six gold dragons if they find her "[sister](wiki:Sansa_Stark)" or two if they only find the fool.(wiki:Dick_Crabb.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

Brienne and [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne) leave Maidenpool with Dick as their guide. During their travels, Brienne is suspicious of him, and he once attempts to search her saddlebags for [coin](wiki:Coin) but is thwarted by flour Brienne has placed in them. He sings parts of songs and regales Pod and Brienne with legends and tales of Crackclaw Point, including that of Ser [Clarence Crabb](wiki:Clarence_Crabb) and of [squishers](wiki:Squishers). When Brienne remarks outside the [Dyre Den](wiki:Dyre_Den) that Dick speaks of Lord [Eustace Brune](wiki:Eustace_Brune) as if he knows him, Dick answers that he might have once.(wiki:Dick_Crabb.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8)

Dick leads Brienne to the ruined castle called the [Whispers](wiki:Whispers). Before entering the castle, Brienne gives Dick her [longsword](wiki:Longsword), and she unsheathes her [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) sword, [Oathkeeper](wiki:Oathkeeper). The fool that Dick had sold the map to turns out to be [Shagwell](wiki:Shagwell), once of the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions), with two [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) companions, [Timeon](wiki:Timeon) and [Pyg](wiki:Pyg). Dick points out that Shagwell was the fool he sold the map to. Angry at having been previously misled by Dick, however, Shagwell kills an unsuspecting Dick by breaking his knee with a morningstar and then smashing his face. A fight ensues and Brienne kills Pyg and Timeon. After Shagwell yields, Brienne makes him dig a grave for Dick beneath a [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood). Once the grave is finished, as she anticipated, Shagwell attempts to kill Brienne with a jagged chunk of rock, but fails and she slays him with a dagger, avenging Dick. Brienne then tosses two gold dragons into Dick's grave, telling Podrick it was the reward promised for finding the fool. Ser [Hyle Hunt](wiki:Hyle_Hunt) helps Brienne bury Dick.(wiki:Dick_Crabb.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-9)

While in the company of the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), Brienne has fevered nightmares and visions which include Dick.(wiki:Dick_Crabb.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
