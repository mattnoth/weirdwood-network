### A Storm of Swords

Amid the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep), [Ferny](wiki:Ferny) and Gilly's mother approach [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) and ask him to flee with [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) and her [newborn son](wiki:Monster).(wiki:Gilly's_mother.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Feast for Crows

When Samwell is upset after breaking his [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) vows by sleeping with Gilly on the *[Cinnamon Wind](wiki:Cinnamon_Wind)*, [Kojja Mo](wiki:Kojja_Mo) reminds him that Gilly has recently lost all of her family.(wiki:Gilly's_mother.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
