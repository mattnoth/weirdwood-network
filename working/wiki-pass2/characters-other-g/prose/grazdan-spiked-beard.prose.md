## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Grazdan is among the Good Masters who bargain with [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) over how many [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) she could purchase. He offers her two thousand. He is present when Daenerys buys her Unsullied, and is the one who demands [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) to be turned over.
