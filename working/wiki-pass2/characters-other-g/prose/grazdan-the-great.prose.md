## Origins

According to legend, Grazdan founded the [lockstep legions](wiki:Lockstep_legions), with which Old Ghis colonized its surroundings and subjugated its neighbors, forming the [first empire](wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis).(wiki:Grazdan_the_Great.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf25.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Grazdan is also credited with raising the ancient brick walls of Old Ghis.(wiki:Grazdan_the_Great.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf25.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
