## Origins

In [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), Lord Dondarrion hosted King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) at [Blackhaven](wiki:Blackhaven) during their return from a [royal progress](wiki:Royal_progress) in the [Reach](wiki:Reach). The royal couple stayed longer than they had intended but left immediately on [dragonback](wiki:Dragon) when they learned their mother, Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon), was dying at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End).(wiki:Lord_Dondarrion_(father_of_Simon).cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
