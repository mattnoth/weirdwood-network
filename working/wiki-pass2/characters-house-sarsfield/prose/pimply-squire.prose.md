## Appearances & Description

The youth is chunky and has brown hair.(wiki:Pimply_squire.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) He is pimpled, with a large white one on one side of his nose, as well as red ones on his forehead.(wiki:Pimply_squire.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) thinks the boy is stupid(wiki:Pimply_squire.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and has a mocking grin.(wiki:Pimply_squire.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) The boy dresses like a [squire](wiki:Squire). He carries a [sword](wiki:Sword) and a knife.(wiki:Pimply_squire.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

In [300 AC](wiki:300_AC), [Polliver](wiki:Polliver) and the [Tickler](wiki:Tickler) bring the squire to the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads) so the youth can lose his [virginity](wiki:Virgin) to one of the [whores](wiki:Whore) there. When [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) and [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) arrive at the inn for [wine](wiki:Wine) and information, Sandor converses with the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men). The drunken squire insults the [Hound](wiki:Hound) for fleeing [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater) and will not be quiet until the Tickler twists his ear.(wiki:Pimply_squire.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

When Sandor refuses to go to [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) or King's Landing with his brother's men, a fight ensues. The squire tries to join in, but Arya throws a wine cup at his white pimple and knocks him down. The squire interrupts Arya when she tries to aid Sandor, so she stabs him in the belly with his own knife. After the Tickler and Polliver are killed, the mortally-wounded squire cries and begs for mercy. The boy mistakenly refers to Sandor as a [knight](wiki:Knight), so the Hound strikes his face. Since the squire will take a long time dying, Sandor tells Arya to [give him mercy](wiki:Gift_of_mercy) and asks if she remembers where the heart is. Arya takes [Needle](wiki:Needle), which she had recovered from Polliver's body, and kills the squire. She claims eight [silvers](wiki:Currency) from his body.(wiki:Pimply_squire.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

### A Feast for Crows

Arya remembers how the pimply squire had cried after he was stabbed.(wiki:Pimply_squire.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

The [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) tell Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) that according to the innkeeper and his [whores](wiki:Whore), Sandor and "a ragged peasant lad" had killed Polliver, the Tickler, and "that Sarsfield boy" at the crossroads inn.(wiki:Pimply_squire.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

After Arya receives a new face from the [Faceless Men](wiki:Faceless_Men), she dreams of the people she has killed, including the pimply squire.(wiki:Pimply_squire.cite_ref-Radwd64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
