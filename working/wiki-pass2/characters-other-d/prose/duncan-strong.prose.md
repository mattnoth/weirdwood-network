## Origins

Duncan is a [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) exile. His surname Strong is of a house that had once loomed large in the histories of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Duncan_Strong.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) [House Strong](wiki:House_Strong) once held the infamous castle of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) until they were extinguished during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Duncan_Strong.cite_ref-Rfab16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Duncan_Strong.cite_ref-Rfab19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He may or may not be of House Strong, as in the free companies a man can call himself whatever he chooses, though not all are genuine.(wiki:Duncan_Strong.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) He may be related to another serjeant in the company, [Denys Strong](wiki:Denys_Strong).

## Appearances & Description

Duncan wears his worldly wealth upon his person and a [lord](wiki:Lord)'s ransom in golden arm rings. Each ring signifies one year's service with the Golden Company.(wiki:Duncan_Strong.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Duncan, like the rest of the company, swears his allegiance to [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)) in [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys) before they [invade Westeros](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company).(wiki:Duncan_Strong.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)(wiki:Duncan_Strong.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
