## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Dormund, together with his father [Tormund](wiki:Tormund), brother [Toregg](wiki:Toregg), and sister [Munda](wiki:Munda), and others join [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte) when she sings "[The Last of the Giants](wiki:The_Last_of_the_Giants)".(wiki:Dormund.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) When [Longspear Ryk](wiki:Ryk) comes to 'steal' Munda, [Dryn](wiki:Dryn) and Dormund give Ryk a fight to defend their sister's honor.(wiki:Dormund.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
