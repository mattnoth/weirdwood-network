## Origins

Durran dammed the river [Slayne](wiki:Slayne) with [Dornish](wiki:Dornish) corpses after turning back [Yoren Yronwood](wiki:Yoren_Yronwood) and the warrior maid [Wylla of Wyl](wiki:Wylla_of_Wyl) in the [Battle by the Bloody Pool](wiki:Battle_by_the_Bloody_Pool).
