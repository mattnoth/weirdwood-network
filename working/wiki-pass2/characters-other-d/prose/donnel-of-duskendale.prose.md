## Origins

At the [tourney at Maidenpool](wiki:Tourney_at_Maidenpool) in [208 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Ser Donnel of [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) was among those overthrown by Ser [Humfrey Hardyng](wiki:Humfrey_Hardyng) in the lists.(wiki:Donnel_of_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

The following year, Donnel, along with his two fellow brothers of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), Ser [Roland Crakehall](wiki:Roland_Crakehall_(Kingsguard)) and Ser [Willem Wylde](wiki:Willem_Wylde), escorted the [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) princes to the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow). As the princes participated in the tourney, the Kingsguard knights did not join in the lists, as it would not be fitting to have them ride against those they are sworn to protect.(wiki:Donnel_of_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) After the princes [Daeron](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) and [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) disappeared, Donnel accompanied Prince [Maekar Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen) in searching for them.(wiki:Donnel_of_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rawoiafdonnel_of_duskendale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) The three of them fought on the side of Prince [Aerion Targaryen](wiki:Aerion_Targaryen) during the [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven) of Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall). In the first charge, Donnel slew Ser [Humfrey Beesbury](wiki:Humfrey_Beesbury).(wiki:Donnel_of_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

After King Daeron II passed away during the [Great Spring Sickness](wiki:Great_Spring_Sickness), Ser Donnel continued to serve in the Kingsguard of his successor, King [Aerys I Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Donnel_of_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rawoiafdonnel_of_duskendale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

## Appearances & Description

Ser Donnel was red of hair and red of beard, and shorter than his sworn brother, Ser [Roland Crakehall](wiki:Roland_Crakehall_(Kingsguard)).(wiki:Donnel_of_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Donnel was from the port town of [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) in the [crownlands](wiki:Crownlands).(wiki:Donnel_of_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rawoiafdonnel_of_duskendale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) He was not a nobleman of the ruling [House Darklyn](wiki:House_Darklyn), though he may have been inspired by their long tradition of knights in the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) to become a member of the order.(wiki:Donnel_of_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rssm1196.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) considered Donnel and his fellow sworn brothers to be the most puissant knights in [the realm](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), aside from perhaps Prince [Baelor Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)).(wiki:Donnel_of_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
