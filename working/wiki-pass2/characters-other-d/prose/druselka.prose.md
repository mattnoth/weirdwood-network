## Origins

When Nymeria had her people abandon the [Abulu](wiki:Isle_of_Women), one of the Summer Isles, Druselka and her followers abandoned Nymeria and returned to [Essos](wiki:Essos), as Druselka claimed to have heard [Mother Rhoyne](wiki:Mother_Rhoyne) calling her children home.
