## Origins

After losing three sons in the cradle, Lord Theomore decided that the dampness and constant flooding of the ancient seat of House Harlaw, [Harlaw Hall](wiki:Harlaw_Hall), was to blame. He ordered the construction of the [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers), a better sited castle as his seat.(wiki:Theomore_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
