## Origins

Gwynesse's husband died in the [sea battle off Fair Isle](wiki:Sea_battle_off_Fair_Isle) during [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion) in [289 AC](wiki:289_AC).(wiki:Gwynesse_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Following his death, Gwynesse returned to [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers), where she has remained since. One of the towers of the castle was [renamed](wiki:Named_after) the [Widow's Tower](wiki:Widow's_Tower_(Ten_Towers)) in her honor. She believes that the castle should be hers since she is older than Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw), and constantly reminds her brother of this.(wiki:Gwynesse_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Appearances & Description

Gwynesse is seven years older than her brother [Rodrik](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw).(wiki:Gwynesse_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) She still grieves the loss of her husband in [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion), and has become forgetful.(wiki:Gwynesse_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Alannys Harlaw](wiki:Alannys_Harlaw), wife of Lord [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), has moved to [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers) to live with her sister Gwynesse, as [Harlaw](wiki:Harlaw) is less raw than [Pyke](wiki:Pyke_(island)).(wiki:Gwynesse_Harlaw.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw) tells his niece, [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), that his sister Gwynesse continues to mourn her husband but has become forgetful.(wiki:Gwynesse_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

### A Dance with Dragons

After fleeing the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), Asha briefly visits Gwynesse and her mother, [Alannys Harlaw](wiki:Alannys_Harlaw), at [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers) before sailing for [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte). Gwynesse maintains that Ten Towers should belong to her.(wiki:Gwynesse_Harlaw.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
