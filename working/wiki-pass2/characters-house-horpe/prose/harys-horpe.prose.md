## Origins

In [42 AC](wiki:42_AC), Horpe was stationed at the [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) chapter of the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons), based in the [Sept of Remembrance](wiki:Sept_of_Remembrance). He fought in the [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven) in which seven of the Warrior's Sons faced King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) and his six champions.(wiki:Harys_Horpe.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

According to several chronicles of the event, Ser Harys lost his arm to Lord [Lucifer Massey](wiki:Lucifer_Massey), one of the king's champions. One of these accounts state that he then tossed his battle-axe into his remaining hand and buried it between Lord Lucifer's eyes. Other chroniclers suggest Death's Head Harry simply died.(wiki:Harys_Horpe.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
