## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Left-Hand Lucas joins with [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) when Euron declares himself [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North).(wiki:Lucas_Codd.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Lucas and [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) trade insults at the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk),(wiki:Lucas_Codd.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and he takes the [dragon horn](wiki:Dragonbinder) from [Cragorn](wiki:Cragorn) after the horn is blown.(wiki:Lucas_Codd.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

While feasting in the hall of [the castle](wiki:Oakenshield_Castle) overlooking [Lord Hewett's Town](wiki:Lord_Hewett's_Town), Left-Hand Lucas tears tapestries down from the walls and wears one when [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) enters the hall. After the feast, Lucas rapes one of Lord [Humfrey Hewett](wiki:Humfrey_Hewett)'s daughters.(wiki:Lucas_Codd.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

After learning she has been wed by proxy to [Erik Ironmaker](wiki:Erik_Ironmaker), Asha thinks Erik is at least better than Lucas or the [Red Oarsman](wiki:Red_Oarsman).(wiki:Lucas_Codd.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Lucas feasts in the ruins of a castle near the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor) with some of Euron's men, and he takes part in mocking [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) when the latter is brought up from the hold of the *[Silence](wiki:Silence)*.(wiki:Lucas_Codd.cite_ref-8)
