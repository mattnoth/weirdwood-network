## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

When the [Lords Declarant](wiki:Lords_Declarant) of the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) met at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) intending to extract the young [Lord](wiki:Lord) [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn) from the influence of his deceased [mother's](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) husband Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), Lady [Anya Waynwood](wiki:Anya_Waynwood) cited Septon Lucos' presence at [Runestone](wiki:Runestone) as a reason why Robert should be sent there to become a [ward](wiki:Fosterage). In this plan, he would serve as an instructor in spiritual matters to the boy lord.(wiki:Lucos.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
