## Origins

By [49 AC](wiki:49_AC) the young king [Jaehaerys I](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) still had five vacant spots in the Kingsguard to fill. At the [wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) of his mother [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) and Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon), it was decided to fill the remaining five slots by a test of arms since knights from all over the realms had come to attend the wedding. Jaehaerys agreed, but with a twist - the knights must fight on foot, not the joust, for foes seldom strike a king from horseback.(wiki:Victor_the_Valiant.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

The wild [melees](wiki:Melee) and bloody duels that followed were dubbed by [maesters](wiki:Maesters) as the [War for the White Cloaks](wiki:War_for_the_White_Cloaks) with hundreds of knights competing for the honor of serving in the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). Ser Victor the Valiant was one of the five champions and was given a white cloak. Prior to this event, Victor was in the employ of the [Royces](wiki:House_Royce) of [Runestone](wiki:Runestone).(wiki:Victor_the_Valiant.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

In [59 AC](wiki:59_AC) when the [winter](wiki:Winter) [disease](wiki:Disease) known as the [Shivers](wiki:Shivers) came to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Victor and his sworn brother Ser [Samgood of Sour Hill](wiki:Samgood_of_Sour_Hill) were among those carried off by the disease.(wiki:Victor_the_Valiant.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
