## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Jarman is the leader of the scouts on the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:Jarman_Buckwell.cite_ref-Racok13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand) suggests himself, Jarman, and [Thoren Smallwood](wiki:Thoren_Smallwood) as leaders of small scouting groups sent into the [Frostfangs](wiki:Frostfangs) to seek out the [wildling](wiki:Wildling) camp.(wiki:Jarman_Buckwell.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Jarman leads the scouting mission to climb the [Giant's Stair](wiki:Giant's_Stair) in search of the wildlings. By the time they return, the disastrous [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist) has already happened and Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont)'s party evacuates for [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep). Jarman's team remains in the wilderness, tracking [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder)'s host, where they spot [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) walking freely amongst the wildlings. Jarman's team is able to return to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), after the main party arrives but before Jon Snow does. Jarman reports to Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) and [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye) that Jon may be a [turncloak](wiki:Turncloak).(wiki:Jarman_Buckwell.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Jon thinks to himself that the Night's Watch has lost too many of its best men, including Jarman,(wiki:Jarman_Buckwell.cite_ref-Radwd21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) indicating the scout is now deceased.
