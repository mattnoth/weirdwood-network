## Origins

In her youth, Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) played in the pools of the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens) with Jeyne and [Jennelyn](wiki:Jennelyn_Fowler), [riding one of the twins' shoulders](wiki:Shoulder-riding_game).(wiki:Jeyne_Fowler.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Appearances & Description

Jeyne is a tall woman with wispy yellow hair.(wiki:Jeyne_Fowler.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Nymeria Sand](wiki:Nymeria_Sand) was abed with Jeyne and her sister [Jennelyn](wiki:Jennelyn_Fowler) when news of Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell)'s death reached them.(wiki:Jeyne_Fowler.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), feels Nymeria is too close to the Fowler twins.(wiki:Jeyne_Fowler.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

### A Dance with Dragons

Jeyne and her sister are present at the feast at [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) when Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) arrives to deliver the [Mountain's skull](wiki:Mountain's_skull). The Fowler twins refuse to drink when [Ricasso](wiki:Ricasso) raises a toast to King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Jeyne_Fowler.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
