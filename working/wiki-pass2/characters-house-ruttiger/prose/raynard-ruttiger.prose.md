## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Raynard is part of Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister)'s force at the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun). Raynard and Ser [Manfryd Yew](wiki:Manfryd_Yew) are given the command of the boom across the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) that prevent any escape from the castle. They are also supplied with nets to fish from the river.(wiki:Raynard_Ruttiger.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Despite their efforts, Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) manages to escape [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) by swimming downriver during a moonless night.(wiki:Raynard_Ruttiger.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
