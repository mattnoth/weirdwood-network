## Appearances & Description

William is fleshy with pale skin and he looks soft.(wiki:William_Mooton.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) He is cowardly and timid.(wiki:William_Mooton.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Lord Mooton pins his ermine cloak at his shoulder with a red-gold brooch in the shape of a [salmon](wiki:Salmon).(wiki:William_Mooton.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

According to rumor, when Lord Mooton heard about the dangerous [wolf](wiki:Wolf) pack led by [a huge she-wolf](wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf)) around the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye), he set out with his sons and his [dogs](wiki:Dog) to hunt her down. When they found her lair, they were attacked and they just managed to escape, losing all their dogs in the process.(wiki:William_Mooton.cite_ref-Racok5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords

After the [battle at Duskendale](wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale), Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) marches on Maidenpool, takes the town, and locks William in a tower cell.(wiki:William_Mooton.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) signs a pardon for Lord Mooton and welcomes him back into the [king's peace](wiki:King's_peace).(wiki:William_Mooton.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) learns that during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Lord William remained safely behind his walls while he sent soldiers off to fight and let Maidenpool burn.(wiki:William_Mooton.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

William is present when Lord Tarly is dispensing justice; he fears Randyll and does not intervene in any of his decisions.(wiki:William_Mooton.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

William agrees to marry his eldest daughter and heir,(wiki:William_Mooton.cite_ref-7) [Eleanor](wiki:Eleanor_Mooton), to Randyll's son and heir, [Dickon Tarly](wiki:Dickon_Tarly).(wiki:William_Mooton.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6) After a short [betrothal](wiki:Betrothal), the two children marry.(wiki:William_Mooton.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
