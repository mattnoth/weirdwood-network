## Origins

Petyr is only a year or two older than [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), but he was married at the age of ten to [Mylenda Caron](wiki:Mylenda_Caron), a woman three times his age.(wiki:Petyr_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

## Appearances & Description

Petyr's face is covered in pimples, the reason for his nickname.(wiki:Petyr_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He was nicknamed "Petyr Pimple" by his brothers,(wiki:Petyr_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) and his uncles and cousins address him that way as well.(wiki:Petyr_Frey.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Petyr_Frey.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Petyr, together with his brother ["Black" Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Ryman)), and his father, Ser [Ryman Frey](wiki:Ryman_Frey), are sent to greet [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s arrival at the [Twins](wiki:Twins) for the wedding of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) and [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey). Robb's [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind), tries to attack them, scaring the greeters' [horses](wiki:Horse) and dropping Petyr into the mud.(wiki:Petyr_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) During the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Petyr passes out drunk trying to compete with [Greatjon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber_(Greatjon)).(wiki:Petyr_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

After the Red Wedding, Petyr wanders off with a [camp follower](wiki:Camp_follower), and is captured by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners). The brotherhood offer a ransom of one hundred [gold pieces](wiki:Currency). [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey) volunteers to pay the ransom at [Oldstones](wiki:Oldstones), hoping to gain favor with Ryman's branch of House Frey, so that his family will not be thrown out of the Twins when Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) finally dies. However, when Merrett arrives at the rendezvous, he is shown Petyr's hanged body. Merrett is himself hanged soon afterwards, for his own participation in the Red Wedding.(wiki:Petyr_Frey.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
