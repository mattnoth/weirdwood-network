## Origins

Lady Merryweather became the head of her house after the execution of [Lord Merryweather](wiki:Lord_Merryweather) at the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Lady_Merryweather.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

In [130 AC](wiki:130_AC), she was [besieged](wiki:Siege_of_Longtable) in her own castle by the [green](wiki:Greens) army led by Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower). Lady Merryweather eventually yielded [Longtable](wiki:Longtable) to Ormund, who claimed the castle's wealth and food, but did no harm to the Merryweathers.(wiki:Lady_Merryweather.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Later, during the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton), Lady Merryweather sent levies from Longtable to join the [blacks](wiki:Blacks)'s host, fortifying [Tumbleton](wiki:Tumbleton) against the [Hightowers](wiki:House_Hightower).(wiki:Lady_Merryweather.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

By [136 AC](wiki:136_AC), [Marq Merryweather](wiki:Marq_Merryweather) had succeeded the lordship of Longtable.(wiki:Lady_Merryweather.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
