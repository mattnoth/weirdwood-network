## Origins

Russell was born at [Longtable](wiki:Longtable).(wiki:Russell_Merryweather.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

While Russell remains at [Longtable](wiki:Longtable), his father, Lord [Orton Merryweather](wiki:Orton_Merryweather), becomes [justiciar](wiki:Justiciar) on the [small council](wiki:Small_council) of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Russell_Merryweather.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Russell's mother, [Taena of Myr](wiki:Taena_of_Myr), becomes a confidante of Tommen's mother, Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister).(wiki:Russell_Merryweather.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Russell_Merryweather.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Cersei invites Taena to bring Russell to court as the queen's [fosterling](wiki:Fosterage). Taena wishes to think about the offer before committing herself to it.(wiki:Russell_Merryweather.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Cersei appoints Orton as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) after demoting Ser [Harys Swyft](wiki:Harys_Swyft).(wiki:Russell_Merryweather.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) After Cersei is arrested by the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), Orton resigns and departs with Taena for [Longtable](wiki:Longtable).(wiki:Russell_Merryweather.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

After her release from imprisonment in the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor), Cersei asks her uncle, Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), Tommen's new regent, to send word to Longtable asking for Taena and Russell to come to court. Kevan, who wants to send his niece to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), agrees, thinking he still can send Taena to the Rock with Cersei and foster young Russell himself at King's Landing. Kevan is [assassinated](wiki:Assassinations_of_Pycelle_and_Kevan_Lannister) before he can send a letter to Longtable, however.(wiki:Russell_Merryweather.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
