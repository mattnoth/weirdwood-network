## Origins

Bellegere was born of the union between the son of a [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos) and a [Summer Islander](wiki:Summer_Isles) princess.(wiki:Bellegere_Otherys.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf411.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Bellegere_Otherys.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Bmercy_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Cmercy.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Bellegere_Otherys.cite_ref-4)

After Princess [Naerys Targaryen](wiki:Naerys_Targaryen) almost died in [161 AC](wiki:161_AC) after giving birth, King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen) sent her husband, Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen), to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) on a diplomatic mission. Aegon met Bellegere in the [Free City](wiki:Free_City). The [Black Pearl](wiki:Black_Pearl)'s ensuing affair with the prince continued for ten years, though it was said that she had a husband in every port and that Aegon was one of many.(wiki:Bellegere_Otherys.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf411.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
