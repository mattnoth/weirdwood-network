## Origins

Maester Smike served Lord [Marq Farman](wiki:Marq_Farman) of [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle), as well as his eldest son, Ser [Franklyn Farman](wiki:Franklyn_Farman), who succeeded his father as [lord](wiki:Lord) in [50 AC](wiki:50_AC).

Smike wrote to the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) after Lord Franklyn brusquely commanded his late father's guest, Princess [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)), to depart Fair Isle. In the letter, Smike states that Franklyn did not realize how close he came to death, for the maester could see the fire in the princess's eyes, who took his lordship's words and tone as a great slight against her.(wiki:Smike.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
