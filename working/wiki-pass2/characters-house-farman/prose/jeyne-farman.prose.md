## Origins

Jeyne was a childhood friend and [bedmaid](wiki:Bedmaid) of [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), along with another bedmaid and friend, [Melara Hetherspoon](wiki:Melara_Hetherspoon). In [276 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), the girls attended the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) hosted by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) in [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) [in honor of Prince Viserys's birth](wiki:Tournament_in_honor_of_Viserys's_birth). The first night of the tourney, Cersei decided to visit the [witch woman](wiki:Woods_witch) [Maggy the Frog](wiki:Maggy_the_Frog) with Melara and Jeyne to have their fortunes told,(wiki:Jeyne_Farman.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) after Melara told them tales of Maggy's powers she had heard from the Casterly Rock serving girls,(wiki:Jeyne_Farman.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) and because Cersei wanted to show her friends that a queen and a [lioness](wiki:House_Lannister) had no fears.(wiki:Jeyne_Farman.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

That night, Cersei and her friends sneaked out of their beds to seek Maggy's tent on the tourney grounds, with the timid Jeyne hanging back behind them. They entered the tent to find that the sorceress was sleeping, and Cersei kicked her bed and demanded she wake up and tell their fortunes. However, when Maggy opened her yellow and crusted eyes, the sight of them caused Jeyne to flee into the night, shrieking with fear, and she never heard her future.(wiki:Jeyne_Farman.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Jeyne_Farman.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Later, Jeyne married one of her brother Lord [Sebaston](wiki:Sebaston_Farman)'s [bannermen](wiki:Bannermen),(wiki:Jeyne_Farman.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) Ser [Gareth Clifton](wiki:Gareth_Clifton),(wiki:Jeyne_Farman.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and had a dozen children.(wiki:Jeyne_Farman.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

## Appearances & Description

As a child, Jeyne was plump and pasty-faced. She was timid, and always hung back or hid behind her friends. [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) considered Jeyne to be stupid and fat, and afraid of every shadow.(wiki:Jeyne_Farman.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) She recalls Jeyne as a friend who betrayed her.(wiki:Jeyne_Farman.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Jeyne still lives on [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle) with her husband and children.(wiki:Jeyne_Farman.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) has a nightmare about the night when she, Jeyne, and [Melara Hetherspoon](wiki:Melara_Hetherspoon) went together to visit [Maggy the Frog](wiki:Maggy_the_Frog) in [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport). Cersei thinks that perhaps Jeyne was the wisest of all of them when she fled without hearing her future, since afterwards she had a pleasant life, unlike Melara who died soon afterwards.(wiki:Jeyne_Farman.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)
