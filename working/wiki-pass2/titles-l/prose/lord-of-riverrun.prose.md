## Origins

After having supported King [Tristifer IV Mudd](wiki:Tristifer_IV_Mudd) in many of his battles, Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully_(father_of_Axel)) went over to the [Andal](wiki:Andal) [Armistead Vance](wiki:Armistead_Vance). Vance granted Edmure's son [Axel](wiki:Axel_Tully) lands at the juncture of the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) and the [Tumblestone](wiki:Tumblestone), where Axel build his seat of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

After years of [illness](wiki:Illness), Lord Hoster Tully dies, and his son Edmure inherits [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Edmure travels to the [Twins](wiki:Twins) to marry [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey), but [the wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) turns out to be a trap, as Edmure is taken captive after the [bedding](wiki:Bedding), and his sister [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) and king [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) are slain.(wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

The lordship of Riverrun is granted to Ser [Emmon Frey](wiki:Emmon_Frey) by King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), whilst Edmure remains a captive at the Twins.(wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Edmure's wife [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey) is pregnant, and prays for a daughter, since giving birth to a son could mean Edmure's execution.(wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Riverrun is still being held by Edmure's uncle, Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully), and soon finds itself besieged. Ser [Ryman Frey](wiki:Ryman_Frey) threatens to hang Edmure if Riverrun is not surrendered, and makes Edmure stand at the gallows every day, but fails to make good on his threats.(wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) After months of siege, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) arrives, giving Edmure the chance to peacefully surrender the castle,(wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) which he eventually does.(wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Following Riverrun's surrender, [Emmon Frey](wiki:Emmon_Frey) occupies Riverrun as its new Lord, while Edmure is sent to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) to live as a prisoner for the remainder of his days.(wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
