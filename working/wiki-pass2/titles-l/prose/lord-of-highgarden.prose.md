## Origins

[Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) had been the seat of [House Gardener](wiki:House_Gardener) for thousands of years. During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), King [Mern IX Gardener](wiki:Mern_IX_Gardener) faced Aegon's army during the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire). There, he died with all his sons. Aegon marched his army to Highgarden, where [Harlan Tyrell](wiki:Harlan_Tyrell), the [High Steward](wiki:High_Steward), opened the gates of the castle at Aegon's approach, pledging the loyalty of his family to [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen). For this, Aegon named Harlan the Lord of Highgarden, [Warden of the South](wiki:Warden_of_the_South), and [Lord Paramount of the Reach](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Reach), despite the fact that other houses in the [Reach](wiki:Reach) had closer blood ties to House Gardener.(wiki:Lord_of_Highgarden.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_house_tyrell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Highgarden.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Lord Harlan took part in the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), during which he disappeared with his army in the desert of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). Harlan's son, Lord [Theo Tyrell](wiki:Theo_Tyrell), consolidated the power of House Tyrell after the First Dornish War had ended, by arranging a council of [septons](wiki:Septon) and [maesters](wiki:Maester) to examine and dismiss some of the more persistent claims to Highgarden put forth by other families.(wiki:Lord_of_Highgarden.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_house_tyrell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord [Lyonel Tyrell](wiki:Lyonel_Tyrell_(lord)) was still a young child, and regency fell to his mother, who, together with Highgarden's castellan, decided to keep House Tyrell out of the war. During the reign of [Daeron I Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen), he fought beneath the Targaryen banner, and eventually remained in Dorne to keep the peace and chase rebels. He was died at [Sandstone](wiki:Sandstone) in the infamous bed of scorpions.(wiki:Lord_of_Highgarden.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_house_tyrell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) During the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), Lord [Leo Tyrell](wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(Longthorn)) won victories against [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) in the Reach. And nearly a century later, Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) remained loyal to [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), and [besieged Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End) in his name for the better part of a year.

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Following his coronation, King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) commands Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) and his sons to swear fealty to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Lord_of_Highgarden.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Instead of proclaiming his loyalty to Joffrey, Mace marries his daughter [Margaery](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) to Lord [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), who claims the Iron Throne a fortnight later.(wiki:Lord_of_Highgarden.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Mace bends the knee to Renly(wiki:Lord_of_Highgarden.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) and becomes his [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:Lord_of_Highgarden.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Clash of Kings

After Renly's death, King Joffrey's [small council](wiki:Small_council) offers Mace an alliance with the Iron Throne,(wiki:Lord_of_Highgarden.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and Mace agrees to betroth his daughter to King Joffrey in return for his support. Mace's forces prove decisive for the Iron Throne in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Lord_of_Highgarden.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Lord Mace refuses to leave King's Landing with his army to continue the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(300)) until his daughter has been declared innocent by the [Faith](wiki:Faith).(wiki:Lord_of_Highgarden.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
