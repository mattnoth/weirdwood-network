## Origins

Lord Morrigen had a territorial dispute with Lord [Armond Connington](wiki:Armond_Connington) a few years before [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Lord_of_Crow's_Nest.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

At the start of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Lord [Lester Morrigen](wiki:Lester_Morrigen) supports [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord_of_Crow's_Nest.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Lester turns to King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) after [Renly's death](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)).(wiki:Lord_of_Crow's_Nest.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Lord Lester remains loyal to Stannis after his defeat at the [battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Lord_of_Crow's_Nest.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Crow's_Nest.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
