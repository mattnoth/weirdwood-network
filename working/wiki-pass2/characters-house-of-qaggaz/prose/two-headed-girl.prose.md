## Appearances & Description

The girl is [feebleminded](wiki:Lackwit). One head is no bigger than an orange and does not speak at all, the other has filed teeth and growls at anyone that comes near her cage.(wiki:Two-headed_girl.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Along with the rest of Yezzan's special treasures, the two-headed girl accompanies her master to the Yunkai encampment outside of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Two-headed_girl.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

When Yezzan contracts the [pale mare](wiki:Pale_mare), it falls to Yezzan's special treasures to attend to him as he is dying. [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and [Penny](wiki:Penny) escape before they can learn of the fate of Yezzan's grotesquerie. The fate of the two-headed girl is unknown.(wiki:Two-headed_girl.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
