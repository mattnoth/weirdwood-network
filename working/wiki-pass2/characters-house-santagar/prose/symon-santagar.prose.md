## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) rules out contacting Ser Symon for aid because she believes he is too dutiful and not powerful enough to intimidate Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell).(wiki:Symon_Santagar.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
