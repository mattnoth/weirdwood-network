## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Lady Ermesande attends the [tourney on King Joffrey's name day](wiki:Tourney_on_King_Joffrey's_name_day) with her [wet nurse](wiki:Wet_nurse). She is rumored to soon wed one of Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s cousins, so the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) might claim her lands.(wiki:Ermesande_Hayford.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Ermesande is [married](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrek_Lannister_and_Ermesande_Hayford) to [Tyrek Lannister](wiki:Tyrek_Lannister),(wiki:Ermesande_Hayford.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) but he disappears soon after during the [riots in King's Landing](wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing).(wiki:Ermesande_Hayford.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Ermesande nurses during [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark) of [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark).(wiki:Ermesande_Hayford.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Wearing a [jade](wiki:Jade)-decorated gown, Ermesande is brought before Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and his retinue when their host stops at [Hayford](wiki:Hayford) while en route to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun). The crying girl is taken to bed by her wet nurse, however.(wiki:Ermesande_Hayford.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
