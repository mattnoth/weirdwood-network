## Origins

Pate served as a [squire](wiki:Squire) in the [Battle at the Red Fork](wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork), during which he mortally wounded Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister) in the third attack by the [greens](wiki:Greens). Lord [Petyr Piper](wiki:Petyr_Piper) granted [knighthood](wiki:Knight) to Pate afterward.(wiki:Pate_of_Longleaf.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Longleaf the Lionslayer led a ragged band of survivors from earlier battles to join Lords [Bigglestone](wiki:Lord_Bigglestone), [Chambers](wiki:Lord_Chambers), and [Perryn](wiki:Lord_Perryn) at the [Battle by the Lakeshore](wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore).(wiki:Pate_of_Longleaf.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) offered single combat to Pate, Lord [Roderick Dustin](wiki:Roderick_Dustin), and Ser [Garibald Grey](wiki:Garibald_Grey) at the [Butcher's Ball](wiki:Butcher's_Ball), but all refused him. Criston was instead slain by [Robb Rivers](wiki:Robb_Rivers)' arrows and his host was destroyed.(wiki:Pate_of_Longleaf.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

When [Tessarion](wiki:Tessarion) began attacking during the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton), Pate was unhorsed and trampled.(wiki:Pate_of_Longleaf.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
