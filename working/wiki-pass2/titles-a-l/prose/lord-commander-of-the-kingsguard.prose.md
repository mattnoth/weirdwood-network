## Origins

Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) modeled the vows of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) upon those of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), and Ser [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon_(son_of_Daemon)) was chosen as the first leader of the new order.(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) The Sea Snake, Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon), was named after his famous great-great-uncle.(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) once chose his Lord Commander, Ser [Ryam Redwyne](wiki:Ryam_Redwyne), to be [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), but the famed [knight](wiki:Knight) turned out to be a poor advisor.(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole), the Lord Commander for King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen), disobeyed the king's will after his passing and crowned [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) instead of [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) The Kingmaker was slain in the ensuing [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

During [the regency](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III) of the young King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), Ser [Robin Massey](wiki:Robin_Massey) was named Lord Commander by Aegon but the appointment was later set aside by the [Lord Regent](wiki:Regent) and [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake), in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC).(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) Aegon denied the appointment of Ser [Marston Waters](wiki:Marston_Waters) as Hand during the [secret siege](wiki:Secret_siege).(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

During the [War of the Ninepenny Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Ninepenny_Kings), Ser [Gerold Hightower](wiki:Gerold_Hightower) took command of the royal forces after the death of the Hand, Lord [Ormund Baratheon](wiki:Ormund_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Gerold was slain in [combat at the tower of joy](wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) is mortally wounded by a boar while hunting in the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood), and Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) is unable to save him.(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Robert's heir, King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), and the [small council](wiki:Small_council) dismiss Barristan as Lord Commander, and the council replaces him with Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister),(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) who is on campaign in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) serves as the Lord Commander of [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)'s short-lived [Rainbow Guard](wiki:Rainbow_Guard).(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Storm of Swords

[Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), [Lord of Casterly Rock](wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock), expects his eldest son to resign from the Kingsguard and again be his heir, but Jaime remains true to his vows.(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Feast for Crows

[Garrett Paege](wiki:Garrett_Paege), [Josmyn Peckledon](wiki:Josmyn_Peckledon), and [Lewys Piper](wiki:Lewys_Piper) squire for the Lord Commander when Jaime marches to restore the [king's peace](wiki:King's_peace) to the riverlands after the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Jaime wants Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) to command the Kingsguard in his absence from the capital, although Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) prefers Ser [Osmund Kettleblack](wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack).(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Barristan, having traveled across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea), becomes the Lord Commander of the [Queensguard](wiki:Queensguard) of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), [Queen of Meereen](wiki:Queen_of_Meereen).(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
