## Origins

After a messenger delivered a crucial message in time to a [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) of [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon), the king raised him to [lordship](wiki:Lord) as the first Lord [Dondarrion](wiki:House_Dondarrion).(wiki:Lord_of_Blackhaven.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

In [37 AC](wiki:37_AC), Lord [Harmon Dondarrion](wiki:Harmon_Dondarrion) helped Lady [Ellyn Caron](wiki:Ellyn_Caron) and Lord [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly_(lord)) defeat the [Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Aenys_I)), who had previously cut off his nose. King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen) rewarded the lords for restoring the [King's Peace](wiki:King's_Peace) in the realm.(wiki:Lord_of_Blackhaven.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Lord Dondarrion, [Selmy](wiki:House_Selmy), [Tarly](wiki:House_Tarly) represented the [Dornish Marches](wiki:Dornish_Marches) at the [Golden Wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) between Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) and Queen Regent [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC).(wiki:Lord_of_Blackhaven.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Six years later, [Lord Dondarrion](wiki:Lord_Dondarrion_(father_of_Simon)) hosted King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) at Blackhaven.(wiki:Lord_of_Blackhaven.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Lord [Simon Dondarrion](wiki:Simon_Dondarrion) was a noted musician and warrior who helped to defeat a [Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Jaehaerys_I)) in the [Third Dornish War](wiki:Third_Dornish_War).(wiki:Lord_of_Blackhaven.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

During the reign of King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen), Lords [Dondarrion](wiki:Lord_Dondarrion_(father_of_Manfred)) and [Caron](wiki:House_Caron) burned a [Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Daeron_II)) out of the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains).(wiki:Lord_of_Blackhaven.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) was dubbed "the Bold" during a [tourney](wiki:Tourney) held at Blackhaven by Lord Dondarrion.(wiki:Lord_of_Blackhaven.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) was betrothed to [Allyria Dayne](wiki:Allyria_Dayne) of [Starfall](wiki:Starfall).(wiki:Lord_of_Blackhaven.cite_ref-Rawoiafberic_dondarrion.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) comes to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to participate in the tournament celebrating [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s appointment as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:Lord_of_Blackhaven.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) After the conclusion of the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney), Beric remains in the capital. Eddard later appoints him the leader of an expedition to kill Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane), who has been raiding the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) after the capture of [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Lord_of_Blackhaven.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Beric's party is ambushed in the [battle at the Mummer's Ford](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford), however, with many killed.(wiki:Lord_of_Blackhaven.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Lord Dondarrion is named among the nobles King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) insists must do homage to him or be named traitors.(wiki:Lord_of_Blackhaven.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Rumors have Beric as missing or perhaps dead,(wiki:Lord_of_Blackhaven.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Blackhaven.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) yet he still raids Lannister foraging parties(wiki:Lord_of_Blackhaven.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) and the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) actively seek him.(wiki:Lord_of_Blackhaven.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Storm of Swords

When [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) and [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie) are brought to Beric by members of the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), they learn Beric is now a follower of the [Lord of Light](wiki:Lord_of_Light). It is revealed that Beric has been resurrected several times by [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr) through the [last kiss](wiki:Last_kiss).(wiki:Lord_of_Blackhaven.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

Three days after the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Lord Beric performs the last kiss on [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark)'s corpse, leading to her resurrection as "Lady Stoneheart" and Beric's final death.(wiki:Lord_of_Blackhaven.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) The name of Lord Beric's heir and successor has not been revealed.
