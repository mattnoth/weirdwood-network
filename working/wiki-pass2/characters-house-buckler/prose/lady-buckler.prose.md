## Origins

In [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), the [Maiden's Day Ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball) was held at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) to find a second wife and new queen for King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen). Lady Buckler came to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with her two daughters, hoping that one of the two [maidens](wiki:Maiden) would be chosen as Aegon III's new queen. However, the three ladies did not reach the capital, as they drowned when the [boat](wiki:Watercraft) that was carrying them across the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) foundered and sank.(wiki:Lady_Buckler.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Some considered their deaths to be part of the "[Maiden's Day curse](wiki:Maiden's_Day_curse)". It is possible that this accident was orchestrated by Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), who wished to promote his [own daughter](wiki:Myrielle_Peake) as Aegon III's second queen.(wiki:Lady_Buckler.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
