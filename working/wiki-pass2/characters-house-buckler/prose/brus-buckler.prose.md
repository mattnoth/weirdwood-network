## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Brus is one of the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) in the service of King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Brus_Buckler.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) When Stannis rides to [Castle Black](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall) and then [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte), Brus stays with Queen [Selyse](wiki:Selyse_Florent) at [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea) as one of her protectors. When Selyse leaves Eastwatch to go to the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort), he accompanies her to Castle Black.(wiki:Brus_Buckler.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Brus is nervous after hearing that [Wun Wun](wiki:Wun_Wun) is a guest of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Brus_Buckler.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Brus appears half drunk during the [wedding of Sigorn and Alys Karstark](wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark), and he gives [Shireen Baratheon](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon) her first dance during the celebration.(wiki:Brus_Buckler.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Selyse arranges for Brus to wed the [second daughter](wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood's_second_daughter) of [Gerrick Kingsblood](wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood).(wiki:Brus_Buckler.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
