## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Lord Buckler hosts Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) and his entourage when they are on their way to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for the wedding of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Ralph_Buckler.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Ralph offers a toast to the royal couple during the wedding feast at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep). However, Joffrey is [poisoned](wiki:Poison) moments later.(wiki:Ralph_Buckler.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
