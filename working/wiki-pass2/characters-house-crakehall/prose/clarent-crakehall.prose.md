## Origins

Ser Clarent joined Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister)'s host in support of King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). Clarent later participated in the [Battle by the Lakeshore](wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore) under Lord [Humfrey Lefford](wiki:Humfrey_Lefford), where he was slain in [130 AC](wiki:130_AC).(wiki:Clarent_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
