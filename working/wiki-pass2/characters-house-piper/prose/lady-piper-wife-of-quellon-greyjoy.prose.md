## Origins

Lady Piper was wed by a [septon](wiki:Septon) to Lord [Quellon Greyjoy](wiki:Quellon_Greyjoy) at [Pinkmaiden Castle](wiki:Pinkmaiden_Castle).(wiki:Lady_Piper_(wife_of_Quellon_Greyjoy).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) She brought with her a [maester](wiki:Maester) to [Pyke](wiki:Pyke).(wiki:Lady_Piper_(wife_of_Quellon_Greyjoy).cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

While Lord Quellon and his three oldest sons, [Balon](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Euron](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) and [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy), were away fighting in [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), [Urrigon Greyjoy](wiki:Urrigon_Greyjoy) lost half his hand playing the [finger dance](wiki:Finger_dance) with his younger brother, [Aeron](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy). Lady Piper sent her maester to sew the missing fingers back but the wound became infected and Urrigon died.(wiki:Lady_Piper_(wife_of_Quellon_Greyjoy).cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

After his return to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands), Balon heard what had happened to his brother. As an act of revenge, he cut three of the maester's fingers and forced Lady Piper to sew them back. Lady Piper died not long after while giving birth to a stillborn daughter.(wiki:Lady_Piper_(wife_of_Quellon_Greyjoy).cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)
