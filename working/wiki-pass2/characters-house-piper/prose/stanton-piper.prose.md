## Origins

Lord Stanton succeeded Lord [Petyr Piper](wiki:Petyr_Piper) after he died in battle against [westermen](wiki:Westermen) at [Acorn Hall](wiki:Acorn_Hall) in [130 AC](wiki:130_AC).(wiki:Stanton_Piper.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

During the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton), Lord Stanton scraped together fresh levies of greybeards and green boys, though had suffered grievous losses in the [autumn](wiki:Autumn) battles. Seated atop a low rise, Lord Stanton and Lord [Lyonel Deddings](wiki:Lyonel_Deddings) were burned to death by the riderless [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor) along with their squires, servants, and sworn shields.(wiki:Stanton_Piper.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
