## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Benethon stays with Queen [Selyse](wiki:Selyse_Florent) at [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea), when Stannis goes to take [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte). When Selyse leaves Eastwatch to go to the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort), he accompanies her to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Benethon_Scales.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Benethon is present when Selyse refuses [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) aid in rescuing the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) stranded at [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome). He is later in the [Shieldhall](wiki:Shieldhall) when Jon Snow informs his men about his plans for Hardhome and the [letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) he has received from [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Bolton).(wiki:Benethon_Scales.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
