## Identity

The Ambush at Fair Isle was a naval engagement in 133 AC, during the aftermath of the Dance of the Dragons, in which ironborn forces ambushed a westermen expedition near Fair Isle. (wiki:Ambush_at_Fair_Isle)

## Origins

Following the Dance of the Dragons, the ironborn under the Red Kraken, Dalton Greyjoy, had raided the western coast extensively. A punitive expedition was dispatched to end the ironborn depredations. (wiki:Ambush_at_Fair_Isle)

## Narrative Arc

The westermen force, including ships from Houses Lannister, Prester, and Tarbeck, sailed to confront the ironborn near Fair Isle. The ironborn, experienced raiders and sailors, set an ambush and caught the western fleet at a disadvantage. The engagement resulted in an ironborn victory, humiliating the western lords and demonstrating that the Iron Islands remained a potent naval threat even after the Dance. (wiki:Ambush_at_Fair_Isle)
