## Identity

Aegon's first test was the first battle of Aegon's Conquest, fought near the mouth of the Blackwater Rush in 2 BC. It pitted the Targaryen host against a combined force from Duskendale and Maidenpool. (wiki:Aegon's_first_test)

## Origins

When Lord Darklyn of Duskendale and Lord Mooton of Maidenpool learned of Aegon's landing, they joined forces to oppose the Targaryen invaders before they could establish a foothold on the mainland. (wiki:Aegon's_first_test)

## Narrative Arc

The combined Darklyn-Mooton army marched against the Targaryen landing force near the Blackwater Rush. The allied lords had numerical superiority, but Aegon unleashed Balerion the Black Dread upon the field. The dragon's fire routed the host and secured a decisive Targaryen victory. Both Lord Darklyn and Lord Mooton survived the battle and eventually bent the knee to Aegon. The engagement demonstrated that conventional armies could not stand against dragonfire and set the pattern for the remainder of the Conquest. (wiki:Aegon's_first_test)
