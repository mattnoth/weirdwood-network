## Identity

Aemond's march on Harrenhal was a military operation during the Dance of the Dragons in 130 AC, in which Prince Aemond Targaryen and Ser Criston Cole led the green faction's army from King's Landing to seize Harrenhal. (wiki:Aemond's_march_on_Harrenhal)

## Origins

After the greens consolidated power in King's Landing following the death of King Viserys I, they sought to take Harrenhal, a strategically vital castle in the riverlands that was held by the black faction. (wiki:Aemond's_march_on_Harrenhal)

## Narrative Arc

Prince Aemond rode the dragon Vhagar while Ser Criston Cole commanded the ground forces. The march proceeded through the crownlands into the riverlands. When the green army arrived at Harrenhal, they found the castle abandoned — Prince Daemon Targaryen had withdrawn the black garrison before the greens' arrival, likely as a deliberate strategic choice. The greens occupied Harrenhal without battle, but the maneuver drew Aemond and Cole's forces away from King's Landing, which was the blacks' true objective. The march is generally considered a strategic victory for the blacks despite their loss of the castle, as it enabled Rhaenyra's subsequent seizure of King's Landing. (wiki:Aemond's_march_on_Harrenhal)
