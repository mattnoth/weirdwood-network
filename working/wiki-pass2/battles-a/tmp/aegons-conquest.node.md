## Identity

Aegon's Conquest was the war of conquest waged by Aegon I Targaryen and his sister-wives Visenya and Rhaenys from 2 BC to 1 AC, in which they unified six of the Seven Kingdoms of Westeros under Targaryen rule using three dragons: Balerion, Vhagar, and Meraxes. (wiki:Aegon's_Conquest)

## Origins

Aegon launched the invasion from Dragonstone, landing at the mouth of the Blackwater Rush and establishing what would become King's Landing. The Targaryens had resided on Dragonstone since the Doom of Valyria. (wiki:Aegon's_Conquest)

## Allegiances

Aegon allied with houses that bent the knee early, including `SWORN_TO` House Baratheon (through Orys Baratheon), `SWORN_TO` House Tully, and `SWORN_TO` House Tyrell, who were elevated for their submission. Opponents included Houses Hoare, Gardener, Durrandon, Lannister, Arryn, and Martell. (wiki:Aegon's_Conquest)

## Narrative Arc

The Conquest comprised multiple major engagements. Aegon's first test near the Blackwater Rush destroyed the combined Darklyn-Mooton host. The Burning of Harrenhal ended House Hoare and won the riverlands. The Field of Fire, the only battle where all three dragons fought together, annihilated the combined Gardener-Lannister army and ended House Gardener. The Last Storm broke the Durrandon stormlands. Orys Baratheon slew the last Storm King, Argilac the Arrogant. Torrhen Stark, the King Who Knelt, submitted without battle. Only Dorne resisted successfully, repelling the Targaryen forces. The Conquest resulted in the creation of the Iron Throne and the unification of Westeros under a single crown. (wiki:Aegon's_Conquest) (agot-jon-08) (affc-brienne-04)
