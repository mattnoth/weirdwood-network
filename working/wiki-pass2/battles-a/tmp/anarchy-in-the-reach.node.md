## Identity

The Anarchy in the Reach was a succession crisis and multi-sided civil war in the Reach during the pre-Conquest era, triggered by the death of King Garth X Gardener without a clear heir. (wiki:Anarchy_in_the_Reach)

## Origins

When Garth X Gardener died, multiple claimants from cadet branches and allied houses vied for the Oakenseat and control of Highgarden. The lack of a clear line of succession plunged the Reach into a period of internal warfare. (wiki:Anarchy_in_the_Reach)

## Narrative Arc

The succession crisis drew in lords from across the Reach. Houses Peake, Manderly, Hightower, and Tyrell were among the factions contending for power. External powers exploited the disorder: the Storm King and the King of the Rock both intervened to press territorial claims or back favored candidates. The anarchy eventually resolved with Mern VI Gardener claiming the throne and restoring Gardener rule. The conflict weakened the Reach's internal cohesion and resulted in lasting feuds between its great houses. House Manderly was ultimately expelled from the Reach during this period, eventually settling in White Harbor under Stark protection. (wiki:Anarchy_in_the_Reach)
