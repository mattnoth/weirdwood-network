## Origins

By [299 AC](wiki:299_AC), Axell had been castellan of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) for ten years while Lord [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) has been away in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to serve on the [small council](wiki:Small_council) of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Axell Florent](wiki:Category:Images_of_Axell_Florent)*

Axell is a short and muscular stout man, round with thick arms and bandy legs.(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He has the prominent ears for which [House Florent](wiki:House_Florent) is known.(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Axell's nose is broad, the eyes are close set, and he has a double chin which is saggy.(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) The brows are beetled and both his nose and ears are hairy.(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) He is homely(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Racok0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) and ugly.(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Axell's voice rings like a [trumpet](wiki:Trumpet).(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) During his time in the [north](wiki:North), he grows a ragged brush of a beard.(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Axell wears russet and fox fur.(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Racok0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) He carries a dagger.(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

Axell is threatening,(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) discourteous,(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and quick to anger.(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Axell supports [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). He emerges as the foremost of the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) and a fervent believer in [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor). Despite Axell being more courteous to Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) than other lords supporting Stannis, Davos does not trust him because the others of [House Florent](wiki:House_Florent) declares for [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon).(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

During a [small council](wiki:Small_council) meeting, Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) suggests creating a rumor that Stannis's daughter, Princess [Shireen Baratheon](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon), was actually fathered by Axell.(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

When Stannis sails for [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), Axell and his niece, Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent), are left behind on Dragonstone.(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Axell arrests Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) in Aegon's Garden on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) after [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) predicts Davos's plot to kill her.(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Axell brings his own brother, Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent), to the dungeon when the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) is accused of treason.(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Axell wants Davos to be burned, but Melisandre instead advises [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) to meet with him. Axell wishes to be named as the new King's Hand. Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) has urged for his appointment, and Axell himself has struck a deal with [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan) for his support. While escorting Davos to Stannis in the [Chamber of the Painted Table](wiki:Chamber_of_the_Painted_Table), he attempts to intimidate Davos into recommending him as Hand to Stannis as well, threatening to have Davos killed if he refuses.(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4) During the meeting with Stannis, Axell urges Stannis to attack [Claw Isle](wiki:Claw_Isle), put the island population to the sword, and loot the wealth rumored to reside there, as Lord [Ardrian Celtigar](wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar) abandoned Stannis after the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). Davos argues in favor of sparing the people of Claw Isle, and recalls to Stannis that he had abandoned his rightful king during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). When Axell declares Davos a traitor for saying this, he is sent away. Stannis next names Davos as Hand instead of Axell.(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5)

Axell and Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) advise Stannis to sacrifice [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm) to Melisandre's flames,(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) but Davos has the boy smuggled from Dragonstone.(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Axell claims to be able to see visions in flames, but Melisandre is skeptical of his ability.(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

### A Feast for Crows

When Stannis goes [north](wiki:North) to the [Wall](wiki:Wall), he leaves Axell at [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea) with Selyse.(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser Axell takes charge of the household of his niece, Queen [Selyse](wiki:Selyse_Florent), at Eastwatch. He mocks Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) during a feast with [Cotter Pyke](wiki:Cotter_Pyke) and officers of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), comparing the Onion Lord to an ape.(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Axell uses the title [Queen's Hand](wiki:Hand_of_the_King). When Cotter sends letters to [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), he disparages the Florent knight.(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

Axell accompanies Selyse to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) after she sets out for the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort). He is disgusted with [Wun Wun](wiki:Wun_Wun) and desires to meet the beautiful [Val](wiki:Val).(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) During the wedding feast of [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark) and [Sigorn](wiki:Sigorn), Axell gives a toast and dances with Selyse.(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3) The queen arranges Axell's betrothal to the [eldest daughter](wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood's_eldest_daughter) of [Gerrick Kingsblood](wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood).(wiki:Axell_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
