## Origins

During the wedding of his niece [Selyse](wiki:Selyse_Florent) and Lord [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), Colin's daughter [Delena](wiki:Delena_Florent) bedded King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). The match led to the birth of [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm), whom Robert acknowledged due to Delena's highbirth.(wiki:Colin_Florent.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) once offered to betroth his son [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to Delena, though Colin instead married her to Ser [Hosman Norcross](wiki:Hosman_Norcross), one of his [household knights](wiki:Household_knight).(wiki:Colin_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
