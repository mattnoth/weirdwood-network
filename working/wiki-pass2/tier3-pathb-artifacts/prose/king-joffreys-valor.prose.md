## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne), Lord of the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor), begins construction of King Joffrey's Valor [in honor of](wiki:Named_after) King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). Prior to [Joffrey's wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding), Paxter gives the king a model of the ship. Joffrey plans to use it as his flagship for a possible [siege of Dragonstone](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone).(wiki:King_Joffrey's_Valor.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
