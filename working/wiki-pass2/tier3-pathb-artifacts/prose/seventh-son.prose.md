## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

On the first day of their stay in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell), Ser [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater), and Ser [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood) seek passage on the Seventh Son to sail to Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).
