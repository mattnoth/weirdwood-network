## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The letter is delivered to Lord Commander [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) by the steward [Clydas](wiki:Clydas) at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black). It is addressed to "[Bastard](wiki:Bastardy)" and is sealed with a smear of pink sealing [wax](wiki:Wax), which is used by [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton). The letter states:
Your [false king](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) is dead, bastard. He and all his host were smashed in seven days of battle. I have his [magic sword](wiki:Lightbringer). Tell his [red whore](wiki:Melisandre).
Your false king's friends are dead. Their heads upon the walls of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). Come see them, bastard. Your false king lied, and so did you. You told the world you burned the [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall). Instead you sent him to Winterfell to steal [my bride](wiki:Jeyne_Poole) from me.
I will have my bride back. If you want [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) back, come and get him. I have him in a cage for all the [north](wiki:North) to see, proof of your lies. The cage is cold, but I have made him a warm cloak from the skins of the [six whores](wiki:Spearwife) who came with him to Winterfell.
I want my bride back. I want the false king's [queen](wiki:Selyse_Florent). I want his [daughter](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon) and his red witch. I want this [wildling princess](wiki:Val). I want his [little prince](wiki:Monster), the wildling babe. And I want my [Reek](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy). Send them to me, bastard, and I will not trouble you or your [black crows](wiki:Night's_Watch). Keep them from me, and I will cut out your bastard's heart and eat it.
[Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Bolton),
Jon reads the letter to the illiterate [Tormund](wiki:Tormund), who is skeptical of its contents. Convinced some of the letter is accurate, Jon announces in the [Shieldhall](wiki:Shieldhall) he will march to Winterfell, which leads many [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) to volunteer to accompany him. After leaving the hall, however, Jon is stabbed in the [mutiny at Castle Black](wiki:Mutiny_at_Castle_Black).(wiki:Bastard_Letter.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
