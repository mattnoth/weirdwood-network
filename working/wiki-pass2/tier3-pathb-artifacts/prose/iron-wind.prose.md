## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Iron Wind is among the ships that make the voyage to the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Iron_Wind.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) keeps Iron Wind, [Lord Dagon](wiki:Lord_Dagon), [Grief](wiki:Grief_(ship)), and [Maiden's Bane](wiki:Maiden's_Bane) as part of his rearguard, commanding from his own ship, [Iron Victory](wiki:Iron_Victory).(wiki:Iron_Wind.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
