## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) is denied passage for her [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) on the Trumpeteer from the [port of Qarth](wiki:Port_of_Qarth). A [Lyseni](wiki:Lys) crew member does not believe Daenerys when she says she is Daenerys Stormborn, Queen of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), replying, "Aye, and I'm Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) and shit gold every night."(wiki:Trumpeteer.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
