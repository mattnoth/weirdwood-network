## Appearances & Description

Longclaw is a [bastard sword](wiki:Bastard_sword) or hand and a half sword, which means it is a good half a foot longer than a standard [long sword](wiki:Long_sword), tapered to thrust as well as slash. With three fullers incised into the blade to reduce weight. There are ripples in the dark steel indicating it is of [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel). It has a [bear](wiki:Bear)'s head for the pommel.(wiki:Longclaw.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Origins

The [Mormonts](wiki:House_Mormont) have carried Longclaw for five hundred years. Lord [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) gave the blade to his son, [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont), when Jeor joined the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). Lord Jorah later brought dishonor to the House by selling poachers to a slaver, however, but he had the grace to leave the sword behind before he fled [Bear Island](wiki:Bear_Island). His aunt, Lady [Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont), returned the sword to her brother Jeor, by then [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), who put it aside at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) since the sight of it reminded him of his son's shame.(wiki:Longclaw.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

While saving the life of [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), from a [wight](wiki:Wight) attack at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) sets a fire in the [Lord Commander's Tower](wiki:Lord_Commander's_Tower) that damages Longclaw's hilt, melting [silver](wiki:Silver) on the bear's head pommel and burning the crossguard and grip.(wiki:Longclaw.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
After finding the blade in the ashes of his bedchamber, Jeor has brothers of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) repair the sword. The Lord Commander brings the sword to [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye) and [Rudge](wiki:Rudge) in the forge. [Halder](wiki:Halder) helps [Spotted Pate of Maidenpool](wiki:Spotted_Pate_of_Maidenpool) carve stone for the pommel, which becomes a snarling [wolf](wiki:Wolf)'s head of pale stone to reflect Jon's [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) heritage. [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) purchases [garnets](wiki:Garnet) in [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town) so chips can be used for the wolf's eyes. The pommel is also weighted with [lead](wiki:Lead) to give better balance.(wiki:Longclaw.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) The sword has a soft leather grip.(wiki:Longclaw.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Jeor gives the blade to Jon, who notes the appropriateness of a [bastard](wiki:Bastardy) wielding a bastard sword. Jon's friends celebrate after he receives the blade.(wiki:Longclaw.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Jon uses Longclaw in combat against [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings)(wiki:Longclaw.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) in the [Frostfangs](wiki:Frostfangs) and later against [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand).(wiki:Longclaw.cite_ref-Racok68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Jon uses Longclaw when he escapes from [Thenns](wiki:Thenns) at [Queenscrown](wiki:Queenscrown).(wiki:Longclaw.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) He also wields the blade during the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black).(wiki:Longclaw.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Jon, now [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), beheads [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) with the bastard sword for insubordination.(wiki:Longclaw.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
