## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

When the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) gather to elect a new [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North) at the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot) on [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk), [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) displays Dragonbinder, a dragon horn. Euron claims to have sailed the [Smoking Sea](wiki:Smoking_Sea), and found it amongst the smoking ruins that were [Valyria](wiki:Valyria).(wiki:Dragonbinder.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) However, according to a semi-canon source, Euron took the horn from four [warlocks](wiki:Warlocks) from [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), whose ship he seized when they went in search of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) after the destruction of their [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying).(wiki:Dragonbinder.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_qarth.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
The horn's noise silences all at the kingsmoot, and ends the possible fight between the supporters of Euron's brother, [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy), and his niece, [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy).(wiki:Dragonbinder.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The horn's noise sounds like the screaming of a thousand souls, and it seems to listeners as if their very bones are aflame and searing their flesh from within.(wiki:Dragonbinder.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) [Cragorn](wiki:Cragorn), the man who blows the horn for Euron, collapses with blisters on his lips, and the [tattoo](wiki:Tattoo) he has of a bird on his chest is bleeding.(wiki:Dragonbinder.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Euron wins the kingsmoot by promising the ironmen that they will conquer [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) with [dragons](wiki:Dragon). When Asha argues that there are no more dragons, Euron tells her that there are three, and he knows where to find them. He does not mention that the dragons belong to Daenerys.(wiki:Dragonbinder.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
Later, Euron tells Victarion that Cragorn died. Euron says that when a [maester](wiki:Maester) cut him open to examine the cause of death, it was discovered his lungs were charred black as soot.(wiki:Dragonbinder.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

At [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte), Asha reflects that on [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk), her uncle's hellhorn had blown a death knell for her dreams.(wiki:Dragonbinder.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Euron sends Victarion to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) to bring [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) back to him, and gives Victarion the horn. Victarion has the horn with him aboard the [Iron Victory](wiki:Iron_Victory). According to [Moqorro](wiki:Moqorro), a [red priest](wiki:Red_priest), the Valyrian glyphs on the horn read, "I am Dragonbinder ... No mortal man shall sound me and live ... Blood for fire, fire for blood."(wiki:Dragonbinder.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
Moqorro says whoever blows the horn will die, but any dragons that hear will obey the horn's master. The red priest says Victarion must be made the horn's master, and he must claim the horn with blood.(wiki:Dragonbinder.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

When Victarion and the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) arrive at [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay), Victarion still has the horn with him in his cabin.(wiki:Dragonbinder.cite_ref-8) Some of the Iron Fleet arrives at the bay of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), just as the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen) is about to resume.(wiki:Dragonbinder.cite_ref-9)
