## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Captained by [Old Tattersalt](wiki:Old_Tattersalt), Blackbird carries [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), [Dareon](wiki:Dareon), Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)), and [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).
