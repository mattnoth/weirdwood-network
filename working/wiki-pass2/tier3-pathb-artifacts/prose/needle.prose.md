## Appearances & Description

Needle is a slender(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) sword with a deep blue sheen of [steel](wiki:Steel)(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Ragot10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and a smooth pommel.(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [Mikken](wiki:Mikken)'s mark is on the blade.(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Its scabbard is soft grey leather.(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Ragot10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Needle is suitable to [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)'s slight build and the [water dance](wiki:Water_dance) style used by [bravos](wiki:Bravo) of the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities).(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Ragot10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Before leaving for the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) commissions [Mikken](wiki:Mikken) to make the sword for his sister, [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark). The girl names it Needle as an ironic reference to her hatred of needlework.(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Ragot10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
In [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) hires [Syrio Forel](wiki:Syrio_Forel), a [water dancer](wiki:Water_dancer) from [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), to train Arya.(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
When [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) purges the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) of [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) after [Eddard's arrest](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark), Arya kills [a stableboy](wiki:Stableboy_at_King's_Landing) with Needle while fleeing.(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Arya uses Needle against a mob gathered at the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) to witness Eddard's public confession. She drops the sword when [Yoren](wiki:Yoren) interrupts and grabs her, but the [wandering crow](wiki:Wandering_crow) returns the blade to her.(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

While travelling northward in Yoren's company of recruits for the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie) and [Lommy](wiki:Lommy) make fun of Needle and attempt to take it from Arya, but she successfully defends herself with a wooden practice sword.(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Racok1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) She offers to spar with [Gendry](wiki:Gendry), she with Needle and he with [Praed](wiki:Praed)'s longsword, but they are interrupted by the arrival of [gold cloaks](wiki:Gold_cloaks) who are searching for Gendry. When Arya enters a [water dancer](wiki:Water_dancer)'s pose wielding Needle, [Broken Nose](wiki:Broken_Nose) laughs at her.(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Racok5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Arya's fingers become calloused from long hours of practicing with Needle,(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and she always keeps the blade close at hand.(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Racok9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) While standing watch at the [Gods Eye town](wiki:Gods_Eye_town), she uses Yoren's whetstone to hone Needle. The town comes under attack by Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch), and Arya cuts off the fingers of an attacking [westerman](wiki:Westerman) with Needle.(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Arya loses Needle when she is captured by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) and the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands);(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Arya adds [Polliver](wiki:Polliver), the man-at-arms who took Needle, to her prayer kill list.(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
Practicing with a broken broomstick while at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), she refers to her swordplay as needlework.(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Arya arrives at the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads) in the custody of [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane). There she spots Polliver carrying Needle, accompanied by the [Tickler](wiki:Tickler). Arya and the [Hound](wiki:Hound) cross these two names off her list, and Arya retrieves her sword. Arya uses Needle to kill the [pimply squire](wiki:Pimply_squire) from [House Sarsfield](wiki:House_Sarsfield).(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Arya pats Needle's hilt for luck when she reaches the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White) in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos),(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) where she begins training to become a servant of the [Many-Faced God](wiki:Many-Faced_God).(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) She secretly practices her swordplay, dueling with her shadow by the light of a blue candle, but is spotted by the [waif](wiki:Waif) one night. The [kindly man](wiki:Kindly_man) tells Arya she must up all her possessions to train, so the girl drops her belongings into the lagoon outside the temple. Arya cannot bring herself to give up Needle, however, and she instead hides the sword and sheath in a crack in the stairs leading to the House of Black and White.(wiki:Needle.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
