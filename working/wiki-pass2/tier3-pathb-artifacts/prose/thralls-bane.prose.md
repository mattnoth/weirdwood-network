## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Thrall's Bane is part of the Iron Fleet contingent dispatched to Slaver's Bay. Thrall's Bane and [Seven Skulls](wiki:Seven_Skulls) capture a fishing ketch, whose master tells [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) that [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) has flown away on her dragon, beyond the [Dothraki Sea](wiki:Dothraki_Sea).(wiki:Thrall's_Bane.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
