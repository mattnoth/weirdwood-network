## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Swift Sword is part of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s fleet commanded by Ser [Imry Florent](wiki:Imry_Florent) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). During the battle it manages to break through and make it upriver alongside the [Lord Steffon](wiki:Lord_Steffon) and the [Ragged Jenna](wiki:Ragged_Jenna).(wiki:Swift_Sword.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Swift Sword is among the members of the fleet that survived the Blackwater only to be sunk by its crew in the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) to prevent it from falling into the hands of [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Swift_Sword.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
