## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Dragonsbane is part of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s fleet commanded by Ser [Imry Florent](wiki:Imry_Florent) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). It is among the first ships to be hit by burning pitch. its captain drives it between two quays and the crew joins the fight ashore.(wiki:Dragonsbane.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) During the fight, the burning hulks of both fleets jam the mouth of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush), forming a quasi-bridge from one bank to the other. At the base of the bridge is Dragonsbane.(wiki:Dragonsbane.cite_ref-Racok61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
