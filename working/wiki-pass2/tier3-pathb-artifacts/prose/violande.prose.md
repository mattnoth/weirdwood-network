## Origins

The Violande sailed Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and her party to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) after she fled [King’s Landing](wiki:King’s_Landing). Having no coin Rhaenyra was forced to sell her crown to pay for passage.(wiki:Violande.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
During the voyage strong winds pushed the Violande closer to the shores of [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark) than the queen would have liked. It passed three times within hailing distance of Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon)'s warships, but Rhaenyra took care to keep out of sight.(wiki:Violande.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The Violande put to harbor below the [Dragonmont](wiki:Dragonmont) on the eventide. The queen disembarked with her son, Prince [Aegon the Younger](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), her ladies, and three Queensguard knights, Ser [Harrold Darke](wiki:Harrold_Darke), Ser [Adrian Redfort](wiki:Adrian_Redfort), and Ser [Loreth Lansdale](wiki:Loreth_Lansdale). At the port she found a forty strong escort commanded by Ser [Alfred Broome](wiki:Alfred_Broome) waiting for her, completing the [fall of Dragonstone](wiki:Fall_of_Dragonstone).(wiki:Violande.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
