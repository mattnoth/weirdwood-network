## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The cargomaster refuses [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) and her [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) passage from [Qarth](wiki:Qarth). He opines that [dragons](wiki:Dragon) are too dangerous at sea, where any stray breath of their [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame) might set the ship's rigging afire.(wiki:Silken_Spirit.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
