## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

The captain of the [Huntress](wiki:Huntress) tells [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) that [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) captured a [Tyroshi](wiki:Tyrosh) trader in the [Redwyne Straits](wiki:Redwyne_Straits), impersonated its crew, and tried to sneak into [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). However, they met the Lady of the Tower, whose oarmaster has a Tyroshi wife. When the oarmaster hailed the ship in Tyroshi, no one responded and the masquerade was over.(wiki:Lady_of_the_Tower.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
