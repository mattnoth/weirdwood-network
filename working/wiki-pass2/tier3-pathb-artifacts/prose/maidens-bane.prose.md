### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) keeps Maiden's Bane, [Lord Dagon](wiki:Lord_Dagon), [Iron Wind](wiki:Iron_Wind), and [Grief](wiki:Grief_(ship)) as part of his rearguard, commanding from his own ship, [Iron Victory](wiki:Iron_Victory).(wiki:Maiden's_Bane.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
