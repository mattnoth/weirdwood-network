## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord Steffon is seen berthed next to the [Fury](wiki:Fury) on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) by Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth).(wiki:Lord_Steffon.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) It is part of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s fleet commanded by Ser [Imry Florent](wiki:Imry_Florent) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). It flanks Fury with [Stag of the Sea](wiki:Stag_of_the_Sea) in the first line of battle. During the battle, it manages to break through and make it upriver with the [Ragged Jenna](wiki:Ragged_Jenna) and the [Swift Sword](wiki:Swift_Sword).(wiki:Lord_Steffon.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Steffon survives the Blackwater but is one of the ships sunk by its crew in the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) after the battle to prevent them from falling into [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) hands.(wiki:Lord_Steffon.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
