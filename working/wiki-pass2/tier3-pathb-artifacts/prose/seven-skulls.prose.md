## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Seven Skulls is part of the Iron Fleet contingent dispatched to Slaver's Bay. Seven Skulls and [Thrall's Bane](wiki:Thrall's_Bane) capture a fishing ketch, whose master tells [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) that [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) has flown away on her dragon, beyond the [Dothraki Sea](wiki:Dothraki_Sea).(wiki:Seven_Skulls.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
