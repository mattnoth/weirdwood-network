## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

The [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) use a crude turtle to attack the gates leading through the [Wall](wiki:Wall) at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) in the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall). It is described as a "a hull turned upside down and opened fore and aft; a longhall on wheels".(wiki:Turtle_(siege_engine).cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) The turtle has a rounded top and eight huge wheels with a stout wooden frame underneath and a number of layers of raw [mammoth](wiki:Mammoth) skins on top of sheepskins and pelts. It is eventually destroyed by the men of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), who drop barrels of frozen gravel on it from atop the [Wall](wiki:Wall).
A great wooden turtle is used to protect [Joso's Cock](wiki:Joso's_Cock) during the [siege of Meereen](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen).(wiki:Turtle_(siege_engine).cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
