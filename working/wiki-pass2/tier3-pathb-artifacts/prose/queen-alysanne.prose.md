## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Queen Alysanne is part of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s fleet commanded by Ser [Imry Florent](wiki:Imry_Florent) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). It is hit by burning pitch during the battle. It engages both [Lady of Silk](wiki:Lady_of_Silk) and [Lady's Shame](wiki:Lady's_Shame). After Lady's Shame is sunk, Queen Alysanne is hit by [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire), destroying her.(wiki:Queen_Alysanne.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
