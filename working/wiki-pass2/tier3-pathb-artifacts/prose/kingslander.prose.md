## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The Kingslander is part of King [Joffrey I Baratheon's](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon)'s [fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet) at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). It attacks the [Faithful](wiki:Faithful) and the [Sceptre](wiki:Sceptre) simultaneously. The Kingslander is engulfed and destroyed by [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) after the explosion of the [Swordfish](wiki:Swordfish_(ship)).(wiki:Kingslander.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
