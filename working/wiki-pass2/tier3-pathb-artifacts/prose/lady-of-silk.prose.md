## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lady of Silk is a part of King [Joffrey I Baratheon's](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) fleet at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). It engages [Queen Alysanne](wiki:Queen_Alysanne) with the aid of its sister ship, [Lady's Shame](wiki:Lady's_Shame). During the fight, Queen Alysanne is engulfed in [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire), which quickly spreads to the Lady of Silk, destroying both ships.(wiki:Lady_of_Silk.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Some of its under officers survive the battle and are honored by the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Lady_of_Silk.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
