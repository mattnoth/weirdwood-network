## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The River Arrow is part of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon)'s fleet at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). The ship survives the battle and its [captain](wiki:Captain_of_the_River_Arrow) is honored by the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:River_Arrow.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
