## Appearances & Description

Lord Tywin is to be twice as large as [King Robert's Hammer](wiki:King_Robert's_Hammer), the flagship of the [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet).(wiki:Lord_Tywin.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord Tywin is being built at the dockyards in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) as part of Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s plan to have a large fleet of powerful ships independent of the historic naval powers of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) and the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor). It is the first of the new ships for the fleet to commence building.(wiki:Lord_Tywin.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Lord [Aurane Waters](wiki:Aurane_Waters), the new [grand admiral](wiki:Grand_admiral), pleases Cersei by asking to name her flagship after her late father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister). According to Aurane, Lord Tywin will be twice the size as [King Robert's Hammer](wiki:King_Robert's_Hammer) or [Sweet Cersei](wiki:Sweet_Cersei).(wiki:Lord_Tywin.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
During her imprisonment by the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), Cersei is visited by [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn), who tells the queen that Aurane fled King's Landing with the new ships after her arrest.(wiki:Lord_Tywin.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
