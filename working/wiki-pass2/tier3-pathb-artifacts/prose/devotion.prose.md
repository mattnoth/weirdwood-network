## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Devotion is seized by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) when Lord [Guncer Sunglass](wiki:Guncer_Sunglass) refuses to acknowledge Stannis as his liege, since Stannis destroyed the [sept](wiki:Sept) at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).
