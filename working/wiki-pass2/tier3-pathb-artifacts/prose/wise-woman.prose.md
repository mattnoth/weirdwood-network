## Origins

On the seventh day of the second moon of [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), the Wise Woman carried the women chosen to become Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen)'s ladies and companions across [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay) from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).
