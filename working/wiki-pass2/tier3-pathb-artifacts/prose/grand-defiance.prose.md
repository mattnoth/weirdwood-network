## Origins

During [Lord Alyn's attack](wiki:Lord_Alyn's_attack) on the [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) fleet during the [Daughters' War](wiki:Daughters'_War), Grand Defiance fought past six [Velaryon](wiki:House_Velaryon) ships to gain the open sea beyond the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones). Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon)'s flagship, [Queen Rhaenys](wiki:Queen_Rhaenys), struck the dromond's side with its prow "like a great oaken fist", however, cutting it in two. Grand Defiance quickly sank when Queen Rhaenys rowed away.
