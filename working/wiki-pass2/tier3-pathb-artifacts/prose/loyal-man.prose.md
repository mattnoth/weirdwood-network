## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The Loyal Man is part of King [Joffrey I Baratheon's](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) fleet during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). Captured by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s fleet during the battle, it is destroyed by [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) after the explosion of the [Swordfish](wiki:Swordfish_(ship)).(wiki:Loyal_Man.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
