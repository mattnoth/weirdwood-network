## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) spots the Valyrian anchored in the bay of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) when [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) is gathering his swords.(wiki:Valyrian_(ship).cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The Valyrian is Salladhor's flagship during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Valyrian_(ship).cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Davos sees the Valyrian in the harbor of Dragonstone when he returns from [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay).(wiki:Valyrian_(ship).cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Salladhor Saan's pirate fleet, including his [Old Mother's Son](wiki:Old_Mother's_Son) and Valyrian, tries to board a galley from [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) near the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones). They kill the captain, but the ship gets away. In [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), a mate from the galley tells [Cat of the Canals](wiki:Cat_of_the_Canals) about the incident.(wiki:Valyrian_(ship).cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Salladhor Saan and his fleet set sail from [Eastwatch](wiki:Eastwatch) for [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor), to take Davos Seaworth as an envoy from King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) to Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly). As a show of strength, Davos was to arrive on the great Valyrian, with the rest of the splendid fleet behind her. However, when much of his fleet is destroyed by storms, Salladhor loses heart and decides to abandon Stannis, sick of waiting for payment from the king. He refuses to take Davos to White Harbor, and instead sets him ashore on [Sweetsister](wiki:Sweetsister) by rowboat.(wiki:Valyrian_(ship).cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Valyrian_(ship).cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
