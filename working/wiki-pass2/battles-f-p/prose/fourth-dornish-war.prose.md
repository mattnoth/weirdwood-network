## Origins

During the [Third Dornish War](wiki:Third_Dornish_War), the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) took up arms against the [Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Jaehaerys_I)), who had been raiding the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands). The Vulture King was eventually defeated by a force led by King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon), [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End).

The [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne) had kept his armies out of the conflict. Regardless, his son, [Morion Martell](wiki:Morion_Martell), regarded it as a slight against [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) that [knights](wiki:Knight) of the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) had marched into the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) of Dorne without being harmed, whilst the armies of Dorne left the Vulture King to his own fate. When he succeeded his father, Prince Morion decided to rectify this, and began to plan an invasion of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Fourth_Dornish_War.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Morion planned to invade the stormlands by ship. As Dorne lacked strength at sea, he hired pirates from the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones), [sellsails](wiki:Sellsail) from [Myr](wiki:Myr), and [corsairs](wiki:Corsair) from the [Pepper Coast](wiki:Pepper_Coast). This took him the best part of a year. Although Morion had hoped to take Jaehaerys unaware, the king on the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) had spies in Morion's own court, and friends among some of the Dornish lords, and was therefore warned far in advance of the plans made by the Dornish prince.(wiki:Fourth_Dornish_War.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

In [83 AC](wiki:83_AC), Prince Morion was ready to attack and sailed his spearmen to [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath) to begin his invasion. The Fourth Dornish War consisted of a single battle, and lasted a single day. King Jaehaerys and his two eldest sons, Prince [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) and Prince [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)), awaited the Dornish on [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor), [Caraxes](wiki:Caraxes), and [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar), whilst Lord [Boremund Baratheon](wiki:Boremund_Baratheon) awaited the Dornish with his army at Cape Wrath.(wiki:Fourth_Dornish_War.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

As the Dornish fleet was sailing north on the [Sea of Dorne](wiki:Sea_of_Dorne), Jaehaerys and his sons struck first by descending from the skies with their [dragons](wiki:Dragon). Though the Dornish responded by filling the air with scorpion and crossbow bolts, none of the projectiles pierced the dragons' scales. [Dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame) destroyed the Dornish fleet.(wiki:Fourth_Dornish_War.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Aftermath

For half a year following the battle, burned bodies of the Dornish crew washed up on [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath). Jaehaerys, Aemon, and Baelon received a riotous welcome on their return to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), as they had won a war without losing a single man, something not even [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) could boast.(wiki:Fourth_Dornish_War.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Morion's fate is as yet unknown, although [Mara Martell](wiki:Mara_Martell) became the Princess of Dorne in [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) in the aftermath of the failed invasion.(wiki:Fourth_Dornish_War.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

Additionally, the foreign pirates, sellsails, and corsairs were less troublesome to the Seven Kingdoms for a time.(wiki:Fourth_Dornish_War.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)
