## Origins

Reports of the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) taking the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands) reach [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). The ironborn are said to have a thousand ships, although this is most likely an exaggeration. Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) demands that the [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet), which is besieging [Storm’s End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(300)) and [Dragonstone](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone), be allowed to leave the stormlands and the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) in order to defend the Reach, but Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) refuses, stating that the two castles remain a danger to King's Landing and King [Tommen I Baratheon's](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) rule as they remain loyal to a rival king, [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Invasion_of_the_Arbor.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Margaery's brother, Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell), offers to lead a charge on the walls of Dragonstone in order to quickly end the siege, which Cersei accepts.(wiki:Invasion_of_the_Arbor.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) A fortnight later it is reported that Loras has successfully taken the castle, being grievously wounded in the process, which allows for the Redwyne fleet to make for the Reach.(wiki:Invasion_of_the_Arbor.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

In the meantime, [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) has ordered his fleet to carry out attacks on settlements of the [Mander](wiki:Mander), the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor), and the Whispering Sound. King's Landing is informed of the presence of ironborn longships in the [Redwyne Straits](wiki:Redwyne_Straits).(wiki:Invasion_of_the_Arbor.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
