## Origins

Late in [45 AC](wiki:45_AC), the forces of King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) hunted down scores of [Poor Fellows](wiki:Poor_Fellows) that had taken refuge in the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood). Many were sent to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) while those who refused to take the black were hanged.(wiki:Hunt_of_the_Poor_Fellows.cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

The Poor Fellows were being led by [Poxy Jeyne Poore](wiki:Poxy_Jeyne_Poore), a woman considered a [witch](wiki:Witch) by the three [septons](wiki:Septon) of Maegor's retinue. She constantly eluded the king until she was eventually betrayed by three of her followers. The betrayers were pardoned and granted them [knighthoods](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Hunt_of_the_Poor_Fellows.cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Maegor the Cruel ordered that Jeyne be [burned alive](wiki:Burning_of_Jeyne_Poore) in a field by the [Wendwater](wiki:Wendwater).(wiki:Hunt_of_the_Poor_Fellows.cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
