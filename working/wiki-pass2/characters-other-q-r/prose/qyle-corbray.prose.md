## Origins

King Qyle was the first of the [Andal](wiki:Andal) kings to fall to [Robar II Royce](wiki:Robar_II_Royce), after uniting the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) of the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn). According to legend, King Robar II personally slew King Qyle after striking Lady Forlorn from his hand. Afterwards, Robar claimed Lady Forlorn for himself.(wiki:Qyle_Corbray.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
