## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Quort is of the raiders from the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) who goes over the [Wall](wiki:Wall) with [Jarl](wiki:Jarl) and [Styr](wiki:Styr).(wiki:Quort.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He fights in the assault on [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), but is killed in [battle](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black). His body is later found by [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow).(wiki:Quort.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
