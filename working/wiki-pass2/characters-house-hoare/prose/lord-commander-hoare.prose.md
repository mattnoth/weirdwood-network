## Origins

Lord Commander Hoare led the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) when [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) invaded [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) with his [dragons](wiki:Dragon). The Lord Commander's brother was [Harren the Black](wiki:Harren_Hoare), [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers). Although Lord Commander Hoare commanded ten thousand swords at the [Wall](wiki:Wall), he remained loyal to the Watch and did not intervene when Harren and his sons were slain by Aegon's [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Balerion](wiki:Balerion), during the [burning of Harrenhal](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal).(wiki:Lord_Commander_Hoare.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
