## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Grigg is a part of [Jarl](wiki:Jarl)'s party that scales the [Wall](wiki:Wall) as part of the rear assault on [Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black). He leads a team of four climbers to the top of the Wall. He tells [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) that he hopes to visit the [Isle of Faces](wiki:Isle_of_Faces) someday.(wiki:Grigg_the_Goat.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
