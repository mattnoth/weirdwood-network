## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Hairy Hal is one of the men that [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) leaves behind at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Hairy_Hal.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Hairy Hal stands guard duty outside of Lord Commander [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow)'s chambers.(wiki:Hairy_Hal.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Hal accompanies [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) to [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town) where the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) distributes food among the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) who have surrendered to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Hairy_Hal.cite_ref-Radwd21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Jon sends three groups of three rangers [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) to scout and explore, with [Garth Greyfeather](wiki:Garth_Greyfeather), [Black Jack Bulwer](wiki:Black_Jack_Bulwer), and Hairy Hal in one group.(wiki:Hairy_Hal.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) They are caught and killed by the [wildling](wiki:Wildling) known as the [Weeper](wiki:Weeper), however, and their heads are removed with their eyes plucked out and are placed on spears north of Castle Black.(wiki:Hairy_Hal.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
