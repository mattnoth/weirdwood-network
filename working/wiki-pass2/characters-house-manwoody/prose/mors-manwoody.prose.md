## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Mors, accompanied by his brother, [Dickon](wiki:Dickon_Manwoody), his father Lord [Dagos](wiki:Dagos_Manwoody), and his uncle Ser [Myles](wiki:Myles_Manwoody), are part of the entourage of Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) when he comes to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to claim the seat on the [small council](wiki:Small_council) that [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) had offered the [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell).(wiki:Mors_Manwoody.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
