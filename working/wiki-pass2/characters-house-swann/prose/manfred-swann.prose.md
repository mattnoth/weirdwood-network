## Origins

Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) [squired](wiki:Squire) for Ser Manfred in his youth.(wiki:Manfred_Swann.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Manfred_Swann.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Manfred Swann became the [Lord of Stonehelm](wiki:Lord_of_Stonehelm) no earlier than [260 AC](wiki:260_AC).(wiki:Manfred_Swann.cite_ref-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

After first meeting [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) at the [port of Qarth](wiki:Port_of_Qarth), Ser Barristan Selmy, presenting himself as the squire "Arstan Whitebeard," claims to have squired for a knight of Lord Swann's household in his youth.(wiki:Manfred_Swann.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords

When Barristan Selmy confesses his true identity to Daenerys, he amends his story and says he squired for Lord Swann himself in his youth.(wiki:Manfred_Swann.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) His service with Swann is recorded at the beginning of his long entry in the [White Book](wiki:White_Book) of the Kingsguard by [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard) [Gerold Hightower](wiki:Gerold_Hightower), where Hightower refers to Manfred Swann as "Ser" rather than "Lord."(wiki:Manfred_Swann.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
