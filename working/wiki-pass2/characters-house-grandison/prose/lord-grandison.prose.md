## Origins

During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Lord Grandison remained faithful to [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) but was captured by his liege lord, [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), in the [battles at Summerhall](wiki:Battles_at_Summerhall). He became a loyal ally of Robert and fought for him during the rest of the war. He was wounded at the [battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident) and died of his wounds a year later.(wiki:Lord_Grandison.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
