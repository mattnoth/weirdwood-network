## Origins

To restore the [Old Way](wiki:Old_Way),(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), declared himself [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Rawoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion) began with the surprise [burning of the Lannister fleet](wiki:Burning_of_the_Lannister_fleet) at [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport).(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The [storming of Seagard](wiki:Storming_of_Seagard) by Balon's [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) was a failure, however, with Balon's heir, [Rodrik Greyjoy](wiki:Rodrik_Greyjoy), killed by Lord [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister).(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Lord [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s victory over the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) in the [sea battle off Fair Isle](wiki:Sea_battle_off_Fair_Isle) allowed royal forces to land in the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands). Stannis subdued [Great Wyk](wiki:Great_Wyk),(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) led the invasion of [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk),(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and forces of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) also landed on [Harlaw](wiki:Harlaw) and [Orkmont](wiki:Orkmont).(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Rawoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), [Warden of the West](wiki:Warden_of_the_West), also supported the invasions.(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Rawoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

With Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North) and his childhood friend, beside him, Robert landed on the [isle of Pyke](wiki:Pyke_(island)). Balon fell back to his own [castle of Pyke](wiki:Pyke).(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Rawoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) The castle of [House Botley](wiki:House_Botley) was destroyed by Robert in a [battle at Lordsport](wiki:Battle_at_Lordsport) before the main attack on the castle of Pyke was launched.(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Narrative Arc

Thousands of men were with Robert and Eddard on Pyke.(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) They assaulted Pyke's walls with [catapults](wiki:Catapult),(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) and [Maron Greyjoy](wiki:Maron_Greyjoy), the second of Balon's three sons, was killed on the walls(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) when the south tower collapsed.(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) Balon's youngest son, [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy), watched the fighting from the safety of the [Sea Tower](wiki:Sea_Tower).(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

[Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr), a [red priest](wiki:Red_priest) in Robert's host, was first through the breach, wielding a sword coated in [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire).(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Lord [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) was not far behind.(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Wielding [his warhammer](wiki:Robert's_warhammer), Robert entered the breach with Ned Stark by his side.(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6) The grandson of [Old Nan](wiki:Old_Nan) was killed on the walls.(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Ironborn resistance collapsed once the wall fell.(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Rawoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) Robert and Ned stood side by side for Balon's surrender in his fallen stronghold.(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Balon reminded the [Lord of the Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Lord_of_the_Seven_Kingdoms) that he had never sworn him an oath after [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). In return for his allegiance now, Balon's life was spared by the generous Robert.(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Rawoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) Young Theon was given into the care of Lord Stark as a hostage and [ward](wiki:Ward) to ensure his defeated father's good behavior.(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

### A Clash of Kings

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) recalls the fighting at [Pyke](wiki:Pyke) when he returns to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) to treat with his father, [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8) Balon declares independence again during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) After his [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell), Theon defends his supposed killings of [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) to his sister, [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), claiming vengeance for [Rodrik](wiki:Rodrik_Greyjoy) and [Maron Greyjoy](wiki:Maron_Greyjoy).(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) tells [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) how he earned his [knighthood](wiki:Knight) at Pyke and met [Lynesse Hightower](wiki:Lynesse_Hightower).(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) [Arstan Whitebeard](wiki:Arstan_Whitebeard) later tells Jorah that he recalls having seen him at Pyke.(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

## Aftermath

Theon was taken away from Pyke on Robert's war galley and was raised alongside Eddard's children at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7) Jorah was rewarded with [knighthood](wiki:Knight) by Robert for his bravery,(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) and Ser [Jacelyn Bywater](wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater) also received his knighthood at Pyke after losing his hand in the battle.(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Robert celebrated his victory by holding a [tourney at Lannisport](wiki:Tourney_at_Lannisport).(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Jorah, victorious in the lists, won the hand of [Lynesse Hightower](wiki:Lynesse_Hightower) during the festivities.(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Age and grief led Balon to become bitter.(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) With Theon away on the mainland, Balon began to see his daughter, [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), as his heir, treating her like he would a son.(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Balon's wife, [Alannys](wiki:Alannys_Harlaw), declined at Pyke and often forgot that her sons were gone.(wiki:Battle_at_Pyke.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
