## Identity

The Battle in the hills below the Golden Tooth was the opening land battle of the War of the Five Kings in 298 AC. (wiki:Battle_in_the_hills_below_the_Golden_Tooth)

## Allegiances

Part of `FIGHTS_IN` War of the Five Kings. (track_b: Conflict)

## Narrative Arc

After Gregor Clegane's raids on the riverlands, Edmure Tully sent Lord Vance of Wayfarer's Rest and Lord Clement Piper to hold the pass below the Golden Tooth against the Lannister army. The rivermen were vastly outnumbered — Jaime Lannister descended on them with roughly 15,000 men. The rivermen were broken and put to flight. Lord Vance was slain in the battle. Lord Piper retreated toward Riverrun with Jaime in pursuit. The victory opened the riverlands to the Lannister invasion and led directly to Jaime's siege of Riverrun. (wiki:Battle_in_the_hills_below_the_Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Ragot55) (wiki:Battle_in_the_hills_below_the_Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Ragot56)
