## Identity

The Attack on Old Wyk was an assault on the isle of Old Wyk by forces loyal to King Robert I Baratheon during Greyjoy's Rebellion. The title is conjectural. (wiki:Attack_on_Old_Wyk)

## Allegiances

Part of `FIGHTS_IN` Greyjoy's Rebellion. (track_b: Conflict)

## Narrative Arc

The invasion of Old Wyk was led by Ser Barristan Selmy, Lord Commander of the Kingsguard, as part of the royal campaign to suppress Balon Greyjoy's rebellion in 289 AC. The royal fleet attacked the ironborn-held island, with the Iron Fleet opposing them. The battle resulted in a royal victory. (wiki:Attack_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Rasos67)
