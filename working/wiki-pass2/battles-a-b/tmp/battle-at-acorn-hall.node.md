## Identity

The Battle at Acorn Hall was fought during the Dance of the Dragons in 130 AC between westermen loyal to the greens and rivermen loyal to the blacks. The title is conjectural. (wiki:Battle_at_Acorn_Hall)

## Allegiances

Part of `FIGHTS_IN` Dance of the Dragons. (track_b: Conflict)

## Narrative Arc

After the Battle at the Red Fork, Ser Adrian Tarbeck led the remaining westermen eastward through the riverlands. They were confronted near Acorn Hall by a force under Lord Joseth Smallwood and Lord Petyr Piper. The blacks fought to stop the greens' advance but were defeated. Lord Petyr Piper was slain in the battle. The westermen continued their march, though their numbers had been reduced across the fighting in the riverlands. (wiki:Battle_at_Acorn_Hall.cite_ref-Rfab)
