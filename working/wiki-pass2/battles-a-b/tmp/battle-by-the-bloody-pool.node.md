## Identity

The Battle by the Bloody Pool was fought in the stormlands between the Storm Kings and Dornish invaders. (wiki:Battle_by_the_Bloody_Pool)

## Narrative Arc

Storm King Durran the Young dammed the river Slayne with Dornish corpses after turning back Yoren Yronwood and Wylla of Wyl. The battle took place on the banks of the Slayne. (wiki:Battle_by_the_Bloody_Pool.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands)
