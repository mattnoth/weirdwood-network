## Identity

The Battle at the Red Fork was fought in 130 AC during the Dance of the Dragons between a westermen army loyal to the greens and rivermen loyal to the blacks. (wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork)

## Allegiances

Part of `FIGHTS_IN` Dance of the Dragons. (track_b: Conflict)

## Narrative Arc

Lord Jason Lannister led a host of westermen eastward into the riverlands to support the greens. The rivermen defended the crossings of the Red Fork. Jason led three attempts to force a crossing; on the third attempt he was slain by a common squire, Pate of Longleaf, who drove a spear through his neck. Despite Jason's death, Ser Adrian Tarbeck found a crossing upstream and flanked the rivermen, routing them. Lord Tristan Vance was killed in the rout. The westermen won the crossing but lost their lord. Adrian Tarbeck took command and marched the army east, where they would fight again at Acorn Hall and later the Battle by the Lakeshore. (wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork.cite_ref-Rfab)
