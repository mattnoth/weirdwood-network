## Identity

The Battle in the Whispering Wood was fought in 299 AC during the War of the Five Kings. It was Robb Stark's decisive ambush of Jaime Lannister's cavalry force near Riverrun. (wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood)

## Allegiances

Part of `FIGHTS_IN` War of the Five Kings. (track_b: Conflict)

## Narrative Arc

Robb Stark lured Jaime Lannister's cavalry into a forested valley near Riverrun using a feint force carrying Tully banners. Ser Brynden Tully's outriders killed Lannister scouts and shot down ravens to prevent word reaching Tywin. Once Jaime's ~2,000 horsemen entered the valley, forces under Maege Mormont, Jason Mallister, Rickard Karstark, Stevron Frey, and the Greatjon closed the trap from all sides. Grey Wind terrified the Lannister horses. The Lannisters suffered losses at a ten-to-one ratio. When Jaime saw the battle was lost, he tried to cut through to kill Robb personally, slaying Daryn Hornwood, Eddard Karstark, and Torrhen Karstark before being pulled from his horse and captured. The victory led directly to the Battle of the Camps and the relief of Riverrun. (wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Ragot63) (wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Ragot55)
