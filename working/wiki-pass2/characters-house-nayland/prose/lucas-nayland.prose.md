## Origins

In [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), Lucas came from [Hag's Mire](wiki:Hag's_Mire) to [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) to attend the [wedding](wiki:Wedding) of Lord [Ambrose Butterwell](wiki:Ambrose_Butterwell) and the [daughter](wiki:Lady_Frey_(wife_of_Ambrose_Butterwell)) of [Lord Frey](wiki:Lord_Frey). He participated in the [wedding tourney](wiki:Whitewalls_Tourney), and was considered one of the better jousters.(wiki:Lucas_Nayland.cite_ref-Rawoiaflucas_nayland.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) During the event, he also played a drinking game with Ser [Kirby Pimm](wiki:Kirby_Pimm).(wiki:Lucas_Nayland.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
