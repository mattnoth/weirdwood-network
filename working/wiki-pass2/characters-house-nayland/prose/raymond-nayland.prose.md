## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Ser Raymond joins [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)'s host when the latter declares himself King. [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) recalls that Raymond participated in a bet beneath the walls of [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) to see who would be the first to claim her [maidenhead](wiki:Maidenhead). After the bet was discovered and ended, Brienne sought out and defeated Raymond during the [melee at Bitterbridge](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge).(wiki:Raymond_Nayland.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) His whereabouts afterwards are unknown.
