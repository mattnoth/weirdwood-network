## Origins

Robert participated in the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC). Along with his older brother, Ser [Androw](wiki:Androw_Ashford), he was one of [his sister's](wiki:Lord_Ashford's_daughter) champions in the tourney. In the first tilt he was challenged by Ser [Lyonel Baratheon](wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon), who beat him after ten lances and a fight afoot.(wiki:Robert_Ashford.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
