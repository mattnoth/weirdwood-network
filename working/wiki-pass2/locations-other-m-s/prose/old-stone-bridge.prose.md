## Appearances & Description

The inn is a tall, timbered building next to an old stone bridge. It has diamond-shaped window panes. The plank floor of the inn's common room is covered in sawdust. One of the rooms for guests has a low ceiling and a wide bed. Nearby are stables.(wiki:Old_Stone_Bridge.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) stays here during her search for [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), as one of her traveling companions, Ser [Creighton Longbough](wiki:Creighton_Longbough), is friends with the innkeep. Goat, goat's milk, bread, and tankards of [ale](wiki:Ale) are served in the common room. Brienne pays for a room for herself and another for Creighton and Ser [Illifer the Penniless](wiki:Illifer_the_Penniless) to share. The merchant [Hibald](wiki:Hibald) pays for a room for himself and Ser [Shadrich](wiki:Shadrich), while his serving men sleep in the stables. Brienne departs in the middle of the night, leaving Creighton and Illifer behind.(wiki:Old_Stone_Bridge.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
