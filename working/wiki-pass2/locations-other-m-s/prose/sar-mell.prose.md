## Origins

During the [Rhoynish Wars](wiki:Rhoynish_Wars), Sar Mell, a pale city known for its flowers, fought with nearby [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys), a colony of the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold). Sar Mell was burned during the [First Turtle War](wiki:First_Turtle_War), while Volon Therys was flooded with Rhoynish [water magic](wiki:Water_magic).
