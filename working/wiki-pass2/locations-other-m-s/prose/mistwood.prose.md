## Origins

Lord [Mertyns](wiki:House_Mertyns) and his household at Mistwood were [poisoned](wiki:Poison) by a cask of [wine](wiki:Wine) during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War).(wiki:Mistwood.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Appearances & Description

Mistwood is protected by walls, a gatehouse, and a keep. The [lord](wiki:Lord)'s [solar](wiki:Solar) is found high in the [Tower of Owls](wiki:Tower_of_Owls).(wiki:Mistwood.cite_ref-Ar2_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter

[Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) visits Mistwood while traveling to find [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)). They find it [held](wiki:Fall_of_Mistwood) by [John Mudd](wiki:Young_John_Mudd) and [Chain](wiki:Chain) of the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company), who keep the dowager Lady [Mary Mertyns](wiki:Mary_Mertyns) under house arrest. John has the castle's [maester](wiki:Maester) send a [raven](wiki:Raven) to [Haldon](wiki:Haldon) at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End).(wiki:Mistwood.cite_ref-Ar2_2-2)
