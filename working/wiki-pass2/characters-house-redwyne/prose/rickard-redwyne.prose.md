## Origins

While still a squire, he came to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with his father and brothers, [Robert](wiki:Robert_Redwyne) and [Ryam](wiki:Ryam_Redwyne), when Lord Manfryd was appointed to the [small council](wiki:Small_council) in [50 AC](wiki:50_AC).(wiki:Rickard_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

In [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) Rickard took part in the squire's tourney held as part of the celebrations for the completion of the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit). He was subsequently knighted on the field by King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Rickard_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_thir_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

In [73 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Rickard took part at a [tourney at Old Oak](wiki:Tourney_at_Old_Oak), where he unhorsed the [mystery knight](wiki:Mystery_knight) called the *Silver Fool*. Rickard unmasked the mystery knight and revealed him to be Prince [Baelon Targaryen](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)). He then knighted Prince Baelon.(wiki:Rickard_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
