## Origins

When Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), sought to make his daughter [Myrielle](wiki:Myrielle_Peake) the queen to King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), Lady [Samantha Hightower](wiki:Samantha_Hightower) nominated nubile [maidens](wiki:Maiden) from House Redwyne as potential queens for Aegon.(wiki:Patricia_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Patricia was later presented to the king at the [Maiden's Day Ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball). When [Alyssa Royce](wiki:Alyssa_Royce) told the king she had come all the way from [Runestone](wiki:Runestone), Lady Patricia went her one better by declaring that her party had traveled from the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor), had thrice been forced to beat back attacks by [outlaws](wiki:Outlaws), and that she shot one in the rear.(wiki:Patricia_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
