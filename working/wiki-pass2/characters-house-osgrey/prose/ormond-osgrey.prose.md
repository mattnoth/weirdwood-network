## Origins

During the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising), Lord Ormond spoke out against [King Maegor's laws](wiki:King_Maegor's_laws) that suppressed the [Poor Fellows](wiki:Poor_Fellows) and the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons), the [militant orders](wiki:Faith_Militant) of the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).
