## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Following the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), during which [House Florent](wiki:House_Florent) fought for [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), Brightwater Keep and all of its lands and incomes are granted to Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell)'s second son, Ser [Garlan Tyrell](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell), thus establishing [House Tyrell of Brightwater Keep](wiki:House_Tyrell_of_Brightwater_Keep).(wiki:Brightwater_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Seeking peace with [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) and hoping for the return of Brightwater, Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) for Stannis, attempts negotiations with Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), the Hand for King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). Alester does so without Stannis's approval, however, and is imprisoned in the dungeon of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) when Stannis finds out.(wiki:Brightwater_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Feast for Crows

From [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Garlan brings half of the Tyrell strength back to the [Reach](wiki:Reach) to press his claim on Brightwater,(wiki:Brightwater_Keep.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) which is held by its castellan, Ser [Colin Florent](wiki:Colin_Florent).(wiki:Brightwater_Keep.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) When news comes of the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), Garlan begins to gather men from across the Reach to retake the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands) from the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn).(wiki:Brightwater_Keep.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) recalls that when Stannis decided to aid the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) at the [Wall](wiki:Wall), Alester, the former Lord of Brightwater Keep, was [burned alive](wiki:Blood_sacrifice) by [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) in order to obtain favorable winds for their expedition to [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea).(wiki:Brightwater_Keep.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
