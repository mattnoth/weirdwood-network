## Origins

Gai's seat is located at the city of [Yin](wiki:Yin), in a palace said to be larger than all of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). His rule is challenged by imperial general [Pol Qo](wiki:Pol_Qo) in [Trader Town](wiki:Trader_Town), who claims to be the first of the [orange emperors](wiki:Orange_emperors). Another pretender is a [sorcerer lord](wiki:Sorcerer_lord), claiming to be the sixty-ninth [yellow emperor](wiki:Yellow_emperors) despite the fall of that dynasty a thousand years ago, who is exiled to the city of [Carcosa](wiki:Carcosa), far to the east of Yi Ti.(wiki:Bu_Gai.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
