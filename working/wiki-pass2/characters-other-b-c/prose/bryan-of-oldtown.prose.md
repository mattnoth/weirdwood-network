## Origins

Hailing from [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) in the [Reach](wiki:Reach), Bryan was the captain of the cog *[Spearshaker](wiki:Spearshaker)*.(wiki:Bryan_of_Oldtown.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

Bryan reported that the [Dothraki language](wiki:Dothraki_language) name for the lost race that inhabited the [Kingdom of the Ifequevron](wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Ifequevron) means "those who walk in the woods". He also reported that the [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibbenese) he met claimed these [woods walkers](wiki:Woods_walkers) bless households that leave offerings of leaf, stone, and water.(wiki:Bryan_of_Oldtown.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
