## Appearances & Description

Bernarr knows some [herblore](wiki:Medicine)(wiki:Brown_Bernarr.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and snores loudly.(wiki:Brown_Bernarr.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Brown Bernarr is part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall), that sets out from [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black). He is one of the chosen few who sleep within [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep).(wiki:Brown_Bernarr.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Brown Bernarr is charged with carrying the medicinal supplies from Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) during the ranging. He is killed during the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist), however.(wiki:Brown_Bernarr.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
