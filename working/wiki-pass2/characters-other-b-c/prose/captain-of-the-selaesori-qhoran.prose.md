## Appearances & Description

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) considers him a mean-mouthed, flinty, kettle-bellied man with close-set, greedy eyes, as well as a bad [cyvasse](wiki:Cyvasse) player and a worse loser.(wiki:Captain_of_the_Selaesori_Qhoran.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) He is a bookish man and keeps a collection of nautical poetry, a well-thumbed tome about the erotic adventures of a young [slave](wiki:Slavery) girl in a [Lysene](wiki:Lys) [pillow house](wiki:Pleasure_house), and the fourth and final volume of *[The Life of the Triarch Belicho](wiki:The_Life_of_the_Triarch_Belicho)*.(wiki:Captain_of_the_Selaesori_Qhoran.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

It is unclear whether the captain is a worshipper of [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor).(wiki:Captain_of_the_Selaesori_Qhoran.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

During the voyage of [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) and [Penny](wiki:Penny) from [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) to [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) aboard the *Selaesoi Qhoran*, the captain always emerges for the evening prayers led by the [red priest](wiki:Red_priest) [Moqorro](wiki:Moqorro), but takes no other part in them. Even though the captain would prefer to be fifty leagues out to sea, Moqorro commands the captain to steer the shortest course to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay), forcing the ship to sail near the coast of [Valyria](wiki:Valyria).(wiki:Captain_of_the_Selaesori_Qhoran.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

After twelve days floating in the [Gulf of Grief](wiki:Gulf_of_Grief), the captain orders the *Selaesori Qhoran'*s boats to be put out to tow the cog. However, a storm strikes and the captain is thrown from the stern-castle to the main deck, breaking both legs as a result. The following day, the captain dies from his injuries.(wiki:Captain_of_the_Selaesori_Qhoran.cite_ref-Radwd40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
