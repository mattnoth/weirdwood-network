## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

As the proctor whose day it is to speak, Brother Narbert is one of the three brothers who greet Septon [Meribald](wiki:Meribald), [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth), Ser [Hyle Hunt](wiki:Hyle_Hunt), and [Podrick](wiki:Podrick_Payne) when they first arrive at the Quiet Isle.
