## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ogo is in [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak) when Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) arrives with his *khalasar*. He has a son, the *[khalakka](wiki:Khalakka)* [Fogo](wiki:Fogo). He takes part in the procession that proclaims Drogo's son to be the [stallion who mounts the world](wiki:Stallion_who_mounts_the_world).(wiki:Ogo.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

He is slain in the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea) by Drogo near a [Lhazareen](wiki:Lhazareen) settlement.(wiki:Ogo.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
