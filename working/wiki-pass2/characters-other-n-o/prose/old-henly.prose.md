## Appearances & Description

He is well past seventy years old. He is called "Old Henly" by his black brothers to differentiate him from [Young Henly](wiki:Young_Henly), who is called that despite being well past fifty years old.(wiki:Old_Henly.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Old Henly is among the men that [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) leaves behind to defend [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black). He dies during the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black).(wiki:Old_Henly.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
