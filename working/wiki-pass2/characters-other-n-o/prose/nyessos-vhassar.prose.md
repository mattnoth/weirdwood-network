## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Nyessos, like the other two triarchs, is up for reelection. [Griff](wiki:Griff) says that [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) has paid Nyessos enough to own him eight times over. He owes much of his wealth to the [slave](wiki:Slave) trade. It is said in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) that Nyessos has been bought by [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) and plans to have the Volantene fleet sail against [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) upon reelection.(wiki:Nyessos_Vhassar.cite_ref-Radwd18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
