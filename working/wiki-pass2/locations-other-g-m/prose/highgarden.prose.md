## Origins

### House Gardener

Highgarden is said to have been built by [Garth the Gardener](wiki:Garth_the_Gardener) atop a hill overlooking the [Mander](wiki:Mander).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Highgarden was the center of the [Reach proper](wiki:Reach_proper), and the [Kings of the Reach](wiki:Kings_of_the_Reach) from [House Gardener](wiki:House_Gardener) grew to acquire what has become the [Reach](wiki:Reach).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Many members of [House Peake](wiki:House_Peake) served as counselors at Highgarden.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

During the [Andal invasion](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), King [Mern II Gardener](wiki:Mern_II_Gardener) built a curtain wall about the castle. The [Andals](wiki:Andals) were ultimately welcomed into the Reach, however, and King [Garth IX Gardener](wiki:Garth_IX_Gardener) built the first [sept](wiki:Sept) at Highgarden, although he continued to worship in its [godswood](wiki:Godswood). [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell), a family of Andal origin, became the hereditary stewards of the castle.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Highgarden was [sacked](wiki:Anarchy_in_the_Reach) by [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) during the reign of King [Garth X Gardener](wiki:Garth_X_Gardener), and the Gardeners' [Oakenseat](wiki:Oakenseat) was destroyed. The castle was rebuilt by Garth's cousin, King [Mern VI Gardener](wiki:Mern_VI_Gardener).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

The peaceful [Harmund II Hoare](wiki:Harmund_II_Hoare), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), once visited Highgarden and [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

The Gardener kings hosted [tourneys](wiki:Tourney) of [love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty), and the greatest champions were allowed to join the [Order of the Green Hand](wiki:Order_of_the_Green_Hand).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_highgarden.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

### House Tyrell

Highgarden was the seat of House Gardener until [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) defeated King [Mern IX Gardener](wiki:Mern_IX_Gardener) and his host on the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire), ending their line during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest). When Aegon then marched his host to Highgarden, its steward, [Harlan Tyrell](wiki:Harlan_Tyrell), surrendered the castle without a fight. For this, Aegon granted him the castle and gave [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) dominion over the [Reach](wiki:Reach) as the [Lord Paramount of the Mander](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Mander).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) However, other houses, such as the [Florents](wiki:House_Florent), [Rowans](wiki:House_Rowan), and [Oakhearts](wiki:House_Oakheart), also claim blood ties to the Gardeners and covet Highgarden.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) During the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), Aegon I took counsel at Highgarden with Lord [Theo Tyrell](wiki:Theo_Tyrell).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Aegon visited Highgarden during many of his royal progresses.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) When Aegon died at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) in [37 AC](wiki:37_AC), his eldest son flew from Highgarden, which he had been visiting as part of a progress, to Dragonstone to be crowned King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) The new king visited Highgarden after being anointed at the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) of [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1) After [Elinor Costayne](wiki:Elinor_Costayne) was forced to wed King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), her second son was sent to Highgarden for fostering.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-2)

[Florence Fossoway](wiki:Florence_Fossoway) ably kept the accounts at Highgarden for her husband, Lord [Martyn Tyrell](wiki:Martyn_Tyrell).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) The Tyrells hosted the famed [Tourney of the Field of Roses](wiki:Tourney_of_the_Field_of_Roses) during the reign of the Old King.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_highgarden.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) Jaehaerys and his granddaughter, Princess [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aemon)), visited Highgarden in [89 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1)

### Dance of the Dragons

After the passing of King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen), the [green council](wiki:Green_council) sent [ravens](wiki:Raven) to Highgarden asking for [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) to support King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) Many lords of the [Reach](wiki:Reach) declared for [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), however, and the regents for young Lord [Lyonel Tyrell](wiki:Lyonel_Tyrell_(lord)) decided that Highgarden should not take part in the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

Because the Tyrells were neutral, Ser [Ulf White](wiki:Ulf_White) declared after the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton) that they should be attainted as traitors and he should receive Highgarden.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) He was poisoned after [Second Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton), however.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

Highgarden was slow to answer Aegon II after his restoration to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) Young Lord [Lyonel Hightower](wiki:Lyonel_Hightower) wanted to support Aegon, but he was dissuaded from doing by the Tyrells, his liege lords. Lord Hightower's younger brother, [Garmund Hightower](wiki:Garmund_Hightower), was a page and companion to Lord Tyrell at Highgarden, so declaring for Aegon would put Garmund's life in danger.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Highgarden](wiki:Category:Images_of_Highgarden)*

Highgarden is located on a broad verdant hill overlooking the [Mander](wiki:Mander). The castle is surrounded by three rings of white stone whose crenellated curtain walls increase in height. Between the outer and middle walls is a famous briar labyrinth which entertains residents and their guests but also slows intruders.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_highgarden.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The oldest towers, squat and square, date from the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes). Newer towers are tall and slender,(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_highgarden.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) round fortifications dating from after the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_winterfell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

The castle [sept](wiki:Sept), which has rows of stained-glass windows honoring [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand) and the [Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), is matched only by the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) and the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept). The castle's [godswood](wiki:Godswood) contains three [weirwoods](wiki:Weirwood) known as the [Three Singers](wiki:Three_Singers). The palatial keep is decorated with statues and colonnades. Also within Highgarden's walls are groves, fountains, and courtyards. The castle's structures are covered in ivy, grapes, and climbing [roses](wiki:Rose).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_highgarden.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Highgarden is filled with flowers, singers, pipers, fiddlers and harpers. The stables have a fine selection of [horseflesh](wiki:Horse), and there are pleasure boats to sail along the Mander.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) There are fields of golden roses that stretch as far as the eye can see. Fruits grown nearby include melons, peaches, and [fireplums](wiki:Fireplum).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Following the death of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), his younger brother, Lord [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), and Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) flee from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to Highgarden.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0) Renly [weds](wiki:Wedding_of_Renly_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) Loras's sister, [Margaery](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), at Highgarden and claims the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) with the support of [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell). Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) calls the banners to Highgarden.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)

### A Clash of Kings

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) travels to Highgarden,(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0) where she is falsely courted by some of Renly's men.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0) Renly marches from Highgarden on the [roseroad](wiki:Roseroad) toward [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing),(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0) but he diverts at [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge) for [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0) Mace remains at Highgarden with ten thousand reinforcements.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-1)

During the parley at Storm's End, Renly offers his brother [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) a [peach from Highgarden](wiki:Renly's_peach).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0) After the [death of Renly](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), the Tyrells do not follow Stannis. Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) travels to Bitterbridge(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0) and then Highgarden, where he meets Mace and Lady [Olenna Tyrell](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0) Petyr negotiates an alliance between the Tyrells and the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister), with the widowed Margaery agreeing to marry King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0)

Mace's men prove decisive in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-1) The Tyrells in King's Landing send for Margaery, who had returned to Highgarden from Bitterbridge.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-2)

### A Storm of Swords

After having caused famine in King's Landing by closing the roseroad while supporting Renly,(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0) the Tyrells send carts of food from Higharden to the royal capital and are thus beloved by the Kingslanders.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0) Many of the Tyrells travel to King's Landing, but [Willas Tyrell](wiki:Willas_Tyrell), Mace's heir, remains at Highgarden.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

The Tyrells plot to marry [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) to Willas,(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0) but the Lannisters instead engineer Sansa's marriage to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0)

After [Joffrey's death](wiki:Purple_Wedding), Littlefinger reveals to Sansa that while he was at Highgarden negotiating with Mace and Olenna, he ordered his men to spread disturbing tales of Joffrey to discredit the boy king. Petyr's men also bribed Highgarden's musicians into singing songs of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), thereby influencing Loras into joining the knightly order.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Ser [Garlan Tyrell](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell) takes half the Tyrell strength back to Highgarden,(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-0) and Lady [Olenna Tyrell](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell) returns as well.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-0) After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields) by the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), Lord [Osbert Serry](wiki:Osbert_Serry) flees to Highgarden. Garlan begins to gather men from across the Reach to retake [the islands](wiki:Shield_Islands).(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_48-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Laswell Peake](wiki:Laswell_Peake) tells [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)) that the power of Highgarden may not be what Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) imagines, as some members of the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) still have friends in the Reach.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_49-0)

Since the Tyrells are occupied with the ironborn and politics in King's Landing, [Obara Sand](wiki:Obara_Sand) wants the [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) to attack the Reach.(wiki:Highgarden.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_50-0)
