## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

A frightened Jocelyn is present when Dowager Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) receives news of the death of her father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister).(wiki:Jocelyn_Swyft.cite_ref-Raffc3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

As Jocelyn helps Queen Regent Cersei prepare for the wedding of her son, King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), to Lady [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), Cersei takes out her anger on her companion.(wiki:Jocelyn_Swyft.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Cersei is presented with the head of a [dwarf](wiki:Dwarf) falsely claimed to be that of her disgraced brother, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), and Jocelyn throws up when she sees the head covered with maggots.(wiki:Jocelyn_Swyft.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Jocelyn spends time in the queen's apartments with Lady [Taena Merryweather](wiki:Taena_of_Myr) and a handmaid, [Dorcas](wiki:Dorcas), while Cersei is away for a [small council](wiki:Small_council) meeting.(wiki:Jocelyn_Swyft.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Jocelyn and Dorcas continue to serve Cersei.(wiki:Jocelyn_Swyft.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Jocelyn_Swyft.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Jocelyn_Swyft.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) When Cersei dismisses Tommen from her presence, she often sends the boy off with Jocelyn.(wiki:Jocelyn_Swyft.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)(wiki:Jocelyn_Swyft.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) The girl snores at night.(wiki:Jocelyn_Swyft.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

### A Dance with Dragons

When Cersei completes her [walk of atonement](wiki:Walk_of_atonement) through the streets of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) and enters the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), Jocelyn provides a blanket when the [Lord Regent](wiki:Lord_Regent), Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), commands the naked Cersei to be covered up.(wiki:Jocelyn_Swyft.cite_ref-Radwd65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
