## Appearances & Description

Eustace is an old man with sons and grandsons.(wiki:Eustace_Brune.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He is the cousin of Ser [Bennard Brune](wiki:Bennard_Brune), the [Knight of Brownhollow](wiki:Knight_of_Brownhollow).(wiki:Eustace_Brune.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Dick Crabb](wiki:Dick_Crabb) claims to have some knowledge of Lord Eustace as he guides [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) and [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne) past the [Dyre Den](wiki:Dyre_Den). Dick claims Lord Eustace's sons and grandsons had gone off to [war](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Eustace_Brune.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
