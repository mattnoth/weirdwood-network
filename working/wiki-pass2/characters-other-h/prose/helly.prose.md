## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Helly is one of the two serving [wenches](wiki:Wench) who carries [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) up the stairs when she arrives at the [Peach](wiki:Peach), in order to bathe her. She argues with the other wench about whether Arya is a boy or a girl, and eventually wins the argument.(wiki:Helly.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
