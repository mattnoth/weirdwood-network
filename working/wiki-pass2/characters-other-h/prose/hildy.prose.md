## Appearances & Description

Hildy has a pug nose and a shaggy mane of hair, and [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) thinks her nipples are darker and thrice the size of his sister [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s. She has a playful personality.(wiki:Hildy.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Lord [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken) of [Stone Hedge](wiki:Stone_Hedge) claims Hildy for himself after killing her lover during the [siege of Raventree](wiki:Siege_of_Raventree).(wiki:Hildy.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Hildy and Jonos are caught [abed](wiki:Bedding) by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) when he strolls unannounced into [Bracken's](wiki:House_Bracken) tent outside [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall). She flirts with and teases Jaime before Jonos yells at her to leave, which she does gladly.(wiki:Hildy.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
