## Origins

### Youth

In his youth Harwyn raided the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) and visited [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) such as [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh), [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), and [Lys](wiki:Lys). He was a captive in the [Basilisk Isles](wiki:Basilisk_Isles) for two years, served as a [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) in the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands), and joined the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons), with whom he fought in several battles.(wiki:Harwyn_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Harwyn succeeded his ailing father, King [Qhorwyn the Cunning](wiki:Qhorwyn_Hoare), although Harwyn had only been third in line to the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair). His eldest brother had already died of [greyscale](wiki:Greyscale) two years prior, but the middle brother, Prince [Harlan](wiki:Harlan_Hoare), died after falling from a horse. Though it was said to be an accident, the death was considered suspicious by some, especially when King Qhorwyn passed six days later, allowing Harwyn to claim the throne as [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Harwyn_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

### Reign

Qhorwyn, a peaceful king who focused on trade, tripled the fleets of [House Hoare](wiki:House_Hoare) and expanded the [armament](wiki:Armament) of his forces to deter adversaries from attacking the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands). Harwyn, however, saw their strengthened forces as a weapon to be used.(wiki:Harwyn_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Harwyn planned the invasion of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), which were weakly held by the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Arrec Durrandon](wiki:Arrec_Durrandon). Led by Harwyn, one hundred [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) longships landed south of [Seagard](wiki:Seagard) and then carried the ships overland to the [Blue Fork](wiki:Blue_Fork). Ser [Samwell Rivers](wiki:Samwell_Rivers)' small host was shattered by Harwyn's charge at the [Tumblestone](wiki:Tumblestone). Harwyn then defeated the army of Lady [Agnes Blackwood](wiki:Agnes_Blackwood), who was betrayed in the battle by Lord [Lothar Bracken](wiki:Lothar_Bracken). Harwyn was impressed with Agnes's defiance and offered her to become his [salt wife](wiki:Salt_wife), but the king killed her when she refused.(wiki:Harwyn_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

The Storm King Arrec then met Harwyn in a [battle at Fairmarket](wiki:Battle_at_Fairmarket). Aided by many river lords, including Lothar Bracken, [Theo Charlton](wiki:Theo_Charlton), and Lords [Goodbrook](wiki:House_Goodbrook), [Paege](wiki:House_Paege), and [Vypren](wiki:House_Vypren), the Hoares crushed the [stormlanders](wiki:Stormlanders) and Arrec's rule over the riverlands collapsed. The rivermen had hoped to regain their independence and celebrated at first, but Harwyn retained control for himself. Lord Bracken had desired to be [river king](wiki:River_king); he rose in rebellion six months later, which resulted in him starving to death in a [crow cage](wiki:Crow_cage).(wiki:Harwyn_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Harwyn spent most of his reign ruling and suppressing rebellions in the riverlands rather than the Iron Islands. Harwyn died in bed with a salt wife at the age of sixty-four, and he was succeeded as [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers) by his son, [Halleck Hoare](wiki:Halleck_Hoare).(wiki:Harwyn_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) Harwyn was the grandfather of [Harren the Black](wiki:Harren_Hoare)(wiki:Harwyn_Hoare.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and [Lord Commander Hoare](wiki:Lord_Commander_Hoare).(wiki:Harwyn_Hoare.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Harwyn's younger sister was the grandmother of [Qhorin Volmark](wiki:Qhorin_Volmark).(wiki:Harwyn_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_greyjoys_of_pyke.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Appearances & Description

Harwyn was belligerent in his youth and bold and demanding as king. While most [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) were only considered fearsome at sea, Harwyn was also a skilled leader on land from his time in the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands).(wiki:Harwyn_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

According to the *[Iron Chronicle](wiki:Iron_Chronicle)*, Harwyn pit his [bannermen](wiki:Bannermen) against each other to keep them weak.(wiki:Harwyn_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
