## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Hibald travels from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) with his serving men and a [hedge knight](wiki:Hedge_knight), Ser [Shadrich](wiki:Shadrich). On the road, he comes upon [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth) and her two companions, Ser [Illifer](wiki:Illifer) and Ser [Creighton Longbough](wiki:Creighton_Longbough). After Shadrich declares the three are no threat, Hibald agrees they can travel together. They all spend the night at the [Old Stone Bridge](wiki:Old_Stone_Bridge), where Hibald confirms to one of the locals that [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) has lost his sword hand.(wiki:Hibald.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
