## Origins

When Prince Baelor became king after the death of his brother, King [Daeron I Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen), he convinced the High Septon to dissolve his [marriage](wiki:Marriage) to his sister, Princess [Daena Targaryen](wiki:Daena_Targaryen). The High Septon supported Baelor's choice of taking a [septon](wiki:Septon)'s vows, and grew increasingly influential in the kingdom.(wiki:High_Septon_(Baelor_I).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

After his death, Baelor convinced the [Most Devout](wiki:Most_Devout) to elect a [lackwit](wiki:Lackwit) stonemason, [Pate](wiki:High_Septon_(stonemason)), to the position of High Septon.(wiki:High_Septon_(Baelor_I).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
