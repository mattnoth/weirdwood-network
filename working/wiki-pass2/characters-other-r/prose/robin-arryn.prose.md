## Origins

Robin was the younger brother of King [Roland II Arryn](wiki:Roland_II_Arryn). When his older brother was killed by King [Tristifer IV Mudd](wiki:Tristifer_IV_Mudd), Robin took the [Falcon Crown](wiki:Falcon_Crown) and resumed work on the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie).(wiki:Robin_Arryn.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
