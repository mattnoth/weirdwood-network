## Origins

Following the death of [Septon](wiki:Septon) [Moon](wiki:Moon_(septon)), Rob the Starvling became one of his would-be successors. He conflicted with [Lorcas the Learned](wiki:Lorcas_the_Learned) over the possession of Septon Moon's corpse. Rob was not among the Poor Fellows killed when they attacked [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), nor those one of those killed when [Donnel Hightower](wiki:Donnel_Hightower) bestirred himself to slaughter the survivors.(wiki:Rob_the_Starvling.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

During the time the newly crowned King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) journeyed from Oldtown to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Rob the Starvling was among the Poor Fellows who presented themselves to the king to beg for clemency. They were pardoned, on the condition that they agreed to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Rob_the_Starvling.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
