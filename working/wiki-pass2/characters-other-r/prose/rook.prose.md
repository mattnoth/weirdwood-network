## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Rook sails to [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) with [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) as she contemplates her next move. He is captured along with Asha, [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley), and several [other ironborn](wiki:Ragged_nine) by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) after the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Rook.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

Rook is later freed and ransomed along with the other ironborn by [Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris), who needs an escort to get him to Stannis during his [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell).(wiki:Rook.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
