## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

When Hotho arrives at the [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers), he gives Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw) a copy of Archmaester [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn)'s *[Book of Lost Books](wiki:Book_of_Lost_Books)* from [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) in the hope it might persuade Rodrik to marry [his daughter](wiki:Hotho_Harlaw's_daughter).(wiki:Hotho_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Before the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), Hotho offers his support to [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) and offers him a daughter for his queen. Victarion promises Hotho he will take a look at the girl after he has been crowned.(wiki:Hotho_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) At the kingsmoot, [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) spots Hotho calling out his support for [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) following Euron's speech.(wiki:Hotho_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

At the feast to celebrate the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), Hotho reports to Victarion that Ser [Harras Harlaw](wiki:Harras_Harlaw) has captured [Greyshield](wiki:Greyshield). As Harras is named the new [Lord of Greyshield](wiki:Lord_of_Greyshield) for his actions, Hotho believes this makes him the new heir to the [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers).(wiki:Hotho_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
