## Appearances & Description

According to [Hotho Humpback](wiki:Hotho_Harlaw), his daughter is a fair girl, newly [flowered](wiki:Flowering). She has blonde hair, small breasts, and good hips. Unlike her father, she does not have a hump.(wiki:Hotho_Harlaw's_daughter.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Hotho Harlaw](wiki:Hotho_Harlaw) goes to [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers) hoping to persuade his widowed cousin and liege lord, [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw), to marry his daughter, but Rodrik refuses.(wiki:Hotho_Harlaw's_daughter.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Before the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), Hotho offers his support to [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) and offers the [Lord Captain](wiki:Lord_Captain_of_the_Iron_Fleet) his daughter for his queen. Victarion promises Hotho he will take a look at the girl after he has been crowned.(wiki:Hotho_Harlaw's_daughter.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) wins the election, however.(wiki:Hotho_Harlaw's_daughter.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
