## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When called upon for loyalty to [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully) by Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), along with [Lharys](wiki:Lharys) and [Mohor](wiki:Mohor), Kurleket assists in the capture of [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) at the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn). He is among the party that escorts Lady Stark into the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon). He is slain during their first battle with [Vale mountain clans](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans), his face smashed by a mace.
