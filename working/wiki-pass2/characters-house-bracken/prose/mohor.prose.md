## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Along with [Lharys](wiki:Lharys) and [Kurleket](wiki:Kurleket), Mohor is one of three Bracken men-at-arms at the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads) who assist Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) in taking captive [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and bringing him to the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn). He is slain during their first fight with members of the [Vale mountain clans](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans).(wiki:Mohor.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
