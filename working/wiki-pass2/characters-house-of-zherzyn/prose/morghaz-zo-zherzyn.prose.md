## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

The [Windblown](wiki:Windblown), a [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) company hired by [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai), mock him as the "Drunken Conqueror", as he is always drunk.(wiki:Morghaz_zo_Zherzyn.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Morghaz_zo_Zherzyn.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Morghaz is among the seven commanders who enter [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) to sign the peace and witness the opening of the [fighting pits](wiki:Fighting_pits). During the feast held in the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) to celebrate the signing of the peace.(wiki:Morghaz_zo_Zherzyn.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### The Winds of Winter

Morghaz becomes supreme commander of the Yunkai'i on the eve of battle during the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen) due to the death of [Gorzhak zo Eraz](wiki:Gorzhak_zo_Eraz), who was slain by the [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince). Morghaz swears that the [turncloak](wiki:Turncloak) who names himself the Prince of Tatters shall die screaming for this infamy.(wiki:Morghaz_zo_Zherzyn.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btyrion_ii_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctyrion_ii.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Due to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) advancing toward the [Harpy's Daughter](wiki:Harpy's_Daughter), Morghaz gives orders that while [Bloodbeard](wiki:Bloodbeard) and two [Ghiscari](wiki:Ghiscari) legions stand against them and hold the line, the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons) are to sweep around behind the [eunuchs](wiki:Eunuch) and take them in the rear, sparing none. A Yunkish nobleman passes this command on to the Second Sons while they are meeting in [Brown Ben Plumm](wiki:Brown_Ben_Plumm)'s tent. They ignore Morghaz's command, and slay his messenger.(wiki:Morghaz_zo_Zherzyn.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btyrion_ii_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctyrion_ii.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
