## Origins

About thousand years ago (possibly before 700 BC)(wiki:Karlon_Stark.cite_ref-date_2-0) after putting down a rebel lord, Karlon was granted lands for his success and bravery. Originally the castle he built was named Karl's Hold but eventually the name changed to [Karhold](wiki:Karhold) and the Karhold Starks became the [Karstarks](wiki:House_Karstark).(wiki:Karlon_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
