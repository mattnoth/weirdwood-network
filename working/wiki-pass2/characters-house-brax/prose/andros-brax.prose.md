## Origins

Lord Andros once visited [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) in hopes of making an offer for the hand of [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) or [Lysa Tully](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) for one of his sons.(wiki:Andros_Brax.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

During Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s siege of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), Andros commands the western camp which directly faces the fortress between two rivers, the [Tumblestone](wiki:Tumblestone) and the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) of the [Trident](wiki:Trident). When Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) leads a surprise attack in the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), Lord Brax tries to cross the Tumblestone on rafts with his men in an attempt to defend the northern camp. Catapults fire at them from atop Riverrun, causing Andros's raft to overturn. Being fully encased in his plate armor, Andros drowns.(wiki:Andros_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Andros has been succeeded by his eldest son, Lord [Tytos Brax](wiki:Tytos_Brax).(wiki:Andros_Brax.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)
