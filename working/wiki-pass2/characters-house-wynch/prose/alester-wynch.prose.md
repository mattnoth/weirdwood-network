## Origins

After Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy) was killed by his own [salt wife](wiki:Salt_wife) [Tess](wiki:Tess) at [Faircastle](wiki:Faircastle) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) and remaining [knights](wiki:Knight) of [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle) rose up in rebellion against the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn). Alongside [Gunthor Goodbrother](wiki:Gunthor_Goodbrother), Alester was one of the commanders of the [Greyjoy](wiki:House_Greyjoy) garrison at Faircastle, composed of the Red Kraken's close companions and brothers-in-battle. While the ironborn fled the island or were otherwise murdered by its people, the garrison at Faircastle continued to stubbornly hold out, until a quarrel over one of the Red Kraken's salt widows, [Lysa Farman](wiki:Lysa_Farman), led to Gunthor slaying Alester.(wiki:Alester_Wynch.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
