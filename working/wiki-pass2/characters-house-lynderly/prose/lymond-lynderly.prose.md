## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter

Ser Lymond attends the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) to select the members of the newly-created [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights). During the feast, after musicians begin playing for the dance, he explains to [Ben Coldwater](wiki:Ben_Coldwater) that Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn) cannot abide singers because of [Marillion](wiki:Marillion).(wiki:Lymond_Lynderly.cite_ref-Winds_1-2)
