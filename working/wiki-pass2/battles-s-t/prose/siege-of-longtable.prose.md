## Narrative Arc

After the [Battle on the Honeywine](wiki:Battle_on_the_Honeywine), Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower) and Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)) forced the [Oakhearts](wiki:House_Oakheart), [Rowans](wiki:House_Rowan), and the lords of the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands) to submit to King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen). While Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) took [King's Landing](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing), Ormund marched on [Longtable](wiki:Longtable), seat of [House Merryweather](wiki:House_Merryweather).(wiki:Siege_of_Longtable.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Ormund's siege of Longtable forced numerous refugees to flee before his host to [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge). Ser [Rickard Thorne](wiki:Rickard_Thorne) tried to bring Prince [Maelor Targaryen](wiki:Maelor_Targaryen) and his [dragon egg](wiki:Dragon_egg) to the safety of Ormund's camp outside Longtable, but the pair were killed at Bitterbridge. [Lady Caswell](wiki:Lady_Caswell) sent Maelor's dragon egg to Ormund.(wiki:Siege_of_Longtable.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

[Lady Merryweather](wiki:Lady_Merryweather) eventually yielded Longtable to Ormund, who claimed the castle's wealth and food but did no harm to the Merryweathers. Lord Hightower then marched on [Bitterbridge](wiki:Sack_of_Bitterbridge) and [Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton).(wiki:Siege_of_Longtable.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
