## Origins

[Castle Darry](wiki:Darry) is [recaptured](wiki:Recapture_of_Darry) from the [Lannister](wiki:Lannister) occupation by the forces of [House Darry](wiki:House_Darry). However, a fortnight later, Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) falls upon the castle and [puts it to the sword](wiki:Sack_of_Darry), killing the eight-year-old Lord [Lyman Darry](wiki:Lyman_Darry) and extinguishing [House Darry](wiki:House_Darry) in the male line.(wiki:Siege_of_Darry.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Siege_of_Darry.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

Ser [Helman Tallhart](wiki:Helman_Tallhart) leads [Tallhart](wiki:House_Tallhart) men in a brief siege of Castle Darry that results in the surrender of the Lannister garrison.(wiki:Siege_of_Darry.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Aftermath

Darry is once again free of Lannister occupation. At [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) sends word to Ser Helman to put the castle to the torch and the Lannister captives to the sword in the name of King [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark). Helman is to then join forces with [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover) and [attack Duskendale](wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale).(wiki:Siege_of_Darry.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Following the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) grants Darry to Ser [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister).(wiki:Siege_of_Darry.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) At the end of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), the Lannisters claim the Darry lands and marry Lancel to [Amerei Frey](wiki:Amerei_Frey), the daughter of [Mariya Darry](wiki:Mariya_Darry).
