## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

[Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) leads a search for [Jared](wiki:Jared_Frey), [Rhaegar](wiki:Rhaegar_Frey), and [Symond Frey](wiki:Symond_Frey), envoys who went missing after departing [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor). [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin), [Lady of Barrowton](wiki:Lady_of_Barrowton), bars Ramsay from her keep of [Barrow Hall](wiki:Barrow_Hall), so Ramsay stays instead at the more modest [Goldgrass](wiki:Goldgrass) when he returns to [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton). When Ramsay insists that Lord Stout hold a feast to welcome him back, Harwood's servants quietly worry that their winter stores will be exhausted. [Grey Jeyne](wiki:Grey_Jeyne) and [Maude](wiki:Maude), two of [Ramsay's dogs](wiki:Bastard's_girls), kill one of Harwood's old hounds over a meaty bone tossed to them by [Will Short](wiki:Will_Short). Harwood departs his hall when [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), [Lord of the Dreadfort](wiki:Lord_of_the_Dreadfort) and the new [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), arrives at the feast to speak with his son.(wiki:Harwood_Stout.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Harwood attends [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark) of Ramsay to "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)", actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole), at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Harwood_Stout.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) During a later breakfast, Harwood quietly converses with [Hother Umber](wiki:Hother_Umber). Among the snowmen that squires build along the battlements of Winterfell, one lacks an arm to depict Harwood.(wiki:Harwood_Stout.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Lord Stout reacts incredulously when Ser [Hosteen Frey](wiki:Hosteen_Frey) suggests taking the fight to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Harwood_Stout.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) He later jokes with [Roger Ryswell](wiki:Roger_Ryswell).(wiki:Harwood_Stout.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
