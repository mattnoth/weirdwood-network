## Origins

Deziel had once aspired to marry Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell), as had his younger brother, Ser [Andrey Dalt](wiki:Andrey_Dalt).(wiki:Deziel_Dalt.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Appearances & Description

Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) believes that Ser Deziel is dutiful to his liege, Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell).(wiki:Deziel_Dalt.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Deziel is one of the [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) nobles who are part of the entourage of Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) when he comes to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to claim the seat [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) had offered the [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell).(wiki:Deziel_Dalt.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Feast for Crows

While imprisoned by her father, Doran, Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) considers contacting Deziel for aid. She decides against it, however, considering him too dutiful to his liege lord to be willing to act against him, and not nearly powerful enough to be successful.(wiki:Deziel_Dalt.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

### A Dance with Dragons

Deziel is present at the feast when Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) arrives in [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) to deliver the [Mountain's skull](wiki:Mountain's_skull). He drinks when [Ricasso](wiki:Ricasso) raises a toast to King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Deziel_Dalt.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
