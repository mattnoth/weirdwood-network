## Origins

[Maesters](wiki:Maesters) believe that men have lived at the mouth of the Honeywine since the [Dawn Age](wiki:Dawn_Age).(wiki:Honeywine.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The city of [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) grew near [Battle Isle](wiki:Battle_Isle) and the [Hightower](wiki:Hightower), and the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) developed from a tract of land beside the Honeywine.(wiki:Honeywine.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) A [pirate](wiki:Pirate) lord raided river traffic from the [Isle of Ravens](wiki:Isle_of_Ravens) during the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes).(wiki:Honeywine.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the river was the site of the [Battle of the Honeywine](wiki:Battle_of_the_Honeywine), which resulted in a victory for the [greens](wiki:Greens) over the [blacks](wiki:Blacks).(wiki:Honeywine.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
[Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) walked in the gardens by the river in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) during the sweltering heat of King [Maekar's](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen) summer.(wiki:Honeywine.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

At the [Quill and Tankard](wiki:Quill_and_Tankard), [Mollander](wiki:Mollander) throws apples into the air and [Alleras](wiki:Alleras) attempts to shoot them with arrows before they fall into the Honeywine.(wiki:Honeywine.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
After arriving in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) walks the river road along the Honeywine and passes the Weeping Dock.(wiki:Honeywine.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)
