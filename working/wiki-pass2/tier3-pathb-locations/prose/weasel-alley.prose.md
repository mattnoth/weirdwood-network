## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) tries to influence her brother, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), to become [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) for [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) by using their memory of the night spent together in the inn on [Eel Alley](wiki:Eel_Alley) years ago.
