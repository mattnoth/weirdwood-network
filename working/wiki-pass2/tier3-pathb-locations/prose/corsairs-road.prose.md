## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) divides the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) into three squadrons during their journey to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay). [Red Ralf Stonehouse](wiki:Ralf_Stonehouse) leads the fastest ships on the corsair's road. Only nine of Red Ralf's ships rejoin Victarion at the [Isle of Cedars](wiki:Isle_of_Cedars), with Ralf himself among the missing.(wiki:Corsair's_road.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
