## Origins

There were once extensive forests on Harlaw, but they were exhausted by the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) of old for use in shipbuilding.(wiki:Harlaw.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Ships from Harlaw began to trade at [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport), [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), and the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) during the dynasty of [House Hoare](wiki:House_Hoare), [Kings of the Iron Islands](wiki:Kings_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Harlaw.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Qhorin Volmark](wiki:Qhorin_Volmark) of Harlaw tried to succeed [Harren the Black](wiki:Harren_Hoare) after the [burning of Harrenhal](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal), but he was cut down by King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) during the [invasion of the Iron Islands](wiki:Invasion_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Harlaw.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_greyjoys_of_pyke.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
After the death of Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), [Blacktyde](wiki:Blacktyde) and Harlaw supported [Rodrik Greyjoy](wiki:Rodrik_Greyjoy_(son_of_Dalton)) against [Toron Greyjoy](wiki:Toron_Greyjoy).(wiki:Harlaw.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Westermen](wiki:Westermen) landed on Harlaw when Ser [Leo Costayne](wiki:Leo_Costayne) invaded the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC) on behalf of Lady [Johanna Lannister](wiki:Johanna_Lannister).(wiki:Harlaw.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
[Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) brought [Alannys Harlaw](wiki:Alannys_Harlaw) from Harlaw for her wedding to his brother, [Balon](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy).(wiki:Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Harlaw was conquered by King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s supporters during [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion).(wiki:Harlaw.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Archmaester [Hake](wiki:Hake_(archmaester)) came from the island.(wiki:Harlaw.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

The [Old Grey Gull](wiki:Old_Grey_Gull) informs the island of [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy)'s calling for a [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
[Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) visits her uncle, Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw), at [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers) prior to the kingsmoot. Rodrik mentions that Lord [Baelor Blacktyde](wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde) briefly visited Harlaw before continuing to [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk).(wiki:Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) was crowned [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North) at the kingsmoot, Asha fled Old Wyk for [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte), bidding farewell to her mother [Alannys Harlaw](wiki:Alannys_Harlaw) at Ten Towers during her flight. While at Ten Towers she received word that Euron had married her by proxy to [Erik Ironmaker](wiki:Erik_Ironmaker).(wiki:Harlaw.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
