## Origins

The hills and their surrounding lands have frequently changed hands in the ongoing conflict between [House Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken) and [House Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood). The [Battle of Six Kings](wiki:Battle_of_Six_Kings), in which the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Arlan III Durrandon](wiki:Arlan_III_Durrandon) defeated five kings from [House Teague](wiki:House_Teague), was fought between the Mother's Teats.(wiki:Teats.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
In [172 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), King [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen) dubbed the hills Barba's Teats in honor of his buxom mistress, Lady [Barba Bracken](wiki:Barba_Bracken). Later that year Aegon took up a new mistress, Lady [Melissa Blackwood](wiki:Melissa_Blackwood), who was slender. After it was heard that Barba had insulted the size of Melissa's breasts, Aegon renamed the hills Missy's Teats,(wiki:Teats.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) and granted them from the Brackens to the Blackwoods.(wiki:Teats.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Each rival family still calls the hills by their preferred name.(wiki:Teats.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
