## Origins

The [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlords) of [Valyria](wiki:Valyria) used [dragonfire](wiki:Dragonfire) to [fuse stone](wiki:Dragonstone_(material)), like the [Black Walls](wiki:Black_Walls) of [Volantis](wiki:Volantis).(wiki:Valyrian_roads.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The only comparable roads in the [known world](wiki:Known_world) are the stone roads built by the [Eunuch Emperors](wiki:Eunuch_Emperors) of [Yi Ti](wiki:Yi_Ti).(wiki:Valyrian_roads.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) travels with the [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) of Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) along Valyrian roads between [Norvos](wiki:Norvos) and [Qohor](wiki:Qohor) in the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities).(wiki:Valyrian_roads.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

At the start of his journey from [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) looks at the Valyrian road on which he and [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) travel. It runs as straight as a spear to the horizon. It is a ribbon of fused stone raised half a foot above the ground to allow rainfall and snowmelt to run off its shoulders. Tyrion muses that unlike the muddy tracks that pass for roads in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), the Valyrian roads are wide enough for three wagons to pass abreast, and neither time nor traffic mar them. They still endure, unchanging, four centuries after [Valyria](wiki:Valyria) met its [Doom](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria).(wiki:Valyrian_roads.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
[Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) does not want to take the [demon road](wiki:Demon_road) from Volantis to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), as it is too dangerous and slow.(wiki:Valyrian_roads.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He and his companions instead join the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown) and sail from Volantis.(wiki:Valyrian_roads.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
