## Origins

The [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) tried to settle colonies at Basilisk Point, but they failed due to [Brindled Men](wiki:Brindled_Men), plague, and abandonment during the [Fourth Ghiscari War](wiki:Fourth_Ghiscari_War).(wiki:Basilisk_Point.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_sothoryos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
During the travels of [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria)'s [ten thousand ships](wiki:Ten_thousand_ships), some of her [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar) settled on Basilisk Point. Two towns were raided by [slavers](wiki:Slavers), however, and the Rhoynar eventually abandoned [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos).(wiki:Basilisk_Point.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
