## Origins

According to legend, [Owen Oakenshield](wiki:Owen_Oakenshield) drove [selkies](wiki:Selkies) and [merlings](wiki:Merlings) from the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands) into the sea.(wiki:Oakenshield.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
[Garth VII Gardener](wiki:Garth_VII_Gardener), [King of the Reach](wiki:King_of_the_Reach), expelled the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) from the islands and resettled them with warriors to help defend the [Mander](wiki:Mander).(wiki:Oakenshield.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_the_gardener_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) visited all of the Shield Islands in [89 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Oakenshield.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)) forced the submission of the Lords of the Shield Islands to the [greens](wiki:Greens).(wiki:Oakenshield.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Six galleys from Oakenshield joined the fleet of [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon), [Lord of the Tides](wiki:Lord_of_the_Tides), when he sailed to face [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Oakenshield.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Oakenshield is captured by the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) during the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields). After the battle, [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North), raises [Nute the Barber](wiki:Nute_the_Barber) as [Lord of Oakenshield](wiki:Lord_of_Oakenshield).(wiki:Oakenshield.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
