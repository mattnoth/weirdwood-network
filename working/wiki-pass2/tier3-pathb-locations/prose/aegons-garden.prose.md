## Appearances & Description

Aegon's Garden is located near the arch of the Dragon's Tail. Tall pine trees grow in the garden, which also has wild roses, towering thorny hedges, and a small cranberry bog.(wiki:Aegon's_Garden.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Origins

[Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen) unsuccessfully searched in Aegon's Garden for her husband, [Androw Farman](wiki:Androw_Farman), after being accused of [poisoning](wiki:Poison) her allies.(wiki:Aegon's_Garden.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Upon returning to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) after the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) is escorted Aegon's Garden instead of the castle's Stone Drum. The Onion Knight finds Princess [Shireen Baratheon](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon) at play in the garden with [Patchface](wiki:Patchface) and [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm). Ser [Axell Florent](wiki:Axell_Florent) then has Davos arrested for plotting against [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre).(wiki:Aegon's_Garden.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Shireen continues to play with Edric in Aegon's Garden every day.(wiki:Aegon's_Garden.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
