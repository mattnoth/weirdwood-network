## Origins

Goldengrove is part of the [Reach proper](wiki:Reach_proper)(wiki:Goldengrove.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and was added to the [Kingdom of the Reach](wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Reach) by [Garth III Gardener](wiki:Garth_III_Gardener) through pacts of friendship and mutual defense.(wiki:Goldengrove.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_the_gardener_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
During the [War of Conquest](wiki:War_of_Conquest), the [King of the Rock](wiki:King_of_the_Rock), [Loren I Lannister](wiki:Loren_I_Lannister), and the [King of the Reach](wiki:King_of_the_Reach), [Mern IX Gardener](wiki:Mern_IX_Gardener), joined their mighty hosts at Goldengrove before marching against [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen)'s army.(wiki:Goldengrove.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the Rowans submitted their castle to the [Greens](wiki:Greens) host led by Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower) fearing that Prince [Daeron the Daring](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)) would burn Goldengrove with his [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Tessarion](wiki:Tessarion).(wiki:Goldengrove.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Lord [Thaddeus Rowan](wiki:Thaddeus_Rowan) died on his way back to Goldengrove in [135 AC](wiki:135_AC).(wiki:Goldengrove.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
In [136 AC](wiki:136_AC), King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) canceled the [royal progress](wiki:Royal_progress) prepared to mark his [coming of age](wiki:Customs). Goldengrove was one of the castles planned to be visited by the king.(wiki:Goldengrove.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)
