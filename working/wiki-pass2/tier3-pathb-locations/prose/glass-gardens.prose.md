## Appearances & Description

The greenhouse has a glass roof of green and yellow panes(wiki:Glass_Gardens.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) locked in frames.(wiki:Glass_Gardens.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The natural hot springs beneath [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) fill the glass gardens with a moist warmth and prevent the ground from freezing.(wiki:Glass_Gardens.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Vegetables,(wiki:Glass_Gardens.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Glass_Gardens.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) fruits,(wiki:Glass_Gardens.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) and flowers(wiki:Glass_Gardens.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) are grown in the garden.

## Origins

In the legend of [Bael the Bard](wiki:Bael_the_Bard), Lord [Brandon Stark](wiki:Brandon_Stark_(the_daughterless)) asked Sygerrik of [Skagos](wiki:Skagos) (Bael) what he wanted as a reward for his singing. The bard requested only the most beautiful flower blooming in Winterfell's [gardens](wiki:Garden), so Lord Stark presented him with a [winter rose](wiki:Winter_rose). The following morning, the [maiden](wiki:Maiden) daughter of Lord Stark had disappeared, and in her bed was the blue rose.(wiki:Glass_Gardens.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
A man in the glass gardens gives a blackberry to [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) whenever the boy visits.(wiki:Glass_Gardens.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) From atop the [First Keep](wiki:First_Keep), Bran likes to watch cooks tend vegetables in the glass gardens.(wiki:Glass_Gardens.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The glass panes of the gardens are turned into shards during the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Glass_Gardens.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) wishes he could give one of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell)'s flowers to [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte).(wiki:Glass_Gardens.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) includes the glass gardens while making a snow castle of Winterfell while at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie).(wiki:Glass_Gardens.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) recalls the warm, earthy smell of the glass gardens.(wiki:Glass_Gardens.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The ruins of Winterfell are covered in snow. On the broken panes of the Glass Gardens, [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) sees hoarfrost sparkling in the moonlight. The fruits and vegetables within are black and frozen.(wiki:Glass_Gardens.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
