## Appearances & Description

Fishermonger's Square is found near the western end of the [Long Bridge](wiki:Long_Bridge),(wiki:Fishermonger's_Square.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) with the [Merchant's House](wiki:Merchant's_House) overlooking the market.(wiki:Fishermonger's_Square.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) The nearby waterside of the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne) has warehouses, [brothels](wiki:Brothel), and taverns.(wiki:Fishermonger's_Square.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
A cracked and headless statue of a dead [triarch of Volantis](wiki:Triarch_of_Volantis) is found in the center of the square.(wiki:Fishermonger's_Square.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
There is a tangle of wayns, [palanquins](wiki:Palanquin), and foot traffic in Fishermonger's Square,(wiki:Fishermonger's_Square.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) which can also be busy at night with sailors, [whores](wiki:Whores), and merchants.(wiki:Fishermonger's_Square.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell), Ser [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater), and Ser [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood), travel on a [hathay](wiki:Hathay) through Fishermonger's Square while journeying to the [Merchant's House](wiki:Merchant's_House). They see a pair of [dwarfs](wiki:Dwarf) putting on a show in the square, with [one on a dog](wiki:Penny) and [the other on a pig](wiki:Oppo).(wiki:Fishermonger's_Square.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) brings the captive [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) through the square to the Merchant's House.(wiki:Fishermonger's_Square.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) While chained to a wall in the inn, Tyrion looks out a window at the crowded square. A crowd has gathered about a pair of jugglers throwing flaming torches at each other, and he notices a [cyvasse](wiki:Cyvasse) game and hears a woman winging a sad song.(wiki:Fishermonger's_Square.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

The fishmongers were out in strength, crying the morning catch. Quentyn understood one word in two at best, but he did not need to know the words to know the fish. He saw [cod](wiki:Cod) and sailfish and sardines, barrels of mussels and clams. [Eels](wiki:Eel) hung along the front of one stall. Another displayed a gigantic [turtle](wiki:Turtle), strung up by its legs on [iron](wiki:Iron) chains, heavy as a [horse](wiki:Horse). Crabs scrabbled inside casks of brine and seaweed. Several of the vendors were frying chunks of fish with onions and beets, or selling peppery fish stew out of small iron kettles.(wiki:Fishermonger's_Square.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
