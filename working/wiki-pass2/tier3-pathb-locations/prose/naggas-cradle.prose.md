## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) calls for a [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot) after the death of his eldest brother, [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North).(wiki:Nagga's_Cradle.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Lord Captain [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) leads the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) into Nagga's Cradle and sets anchor in the bay. Feasting tents are raised between the tideline and Nagga's Hill in preparation of the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Nagga's_Cradle.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) is chosen by the assembled [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) as Balon's successor.(wiki:Nagga's_Cradle.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
King Euron has the Iron Fleet close the bay after being crowned. When Lord [Baelor Blacktyde](wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde) tries to sail back to [Blacktyde](wiki:Blacktyde), his [Nightflyer](wiki:Nightflyer) is seized and Euron has him executed.(wiki:Nagga's_Cradle.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
