## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

The [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) sends scouts to watch for [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) coming up the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad). When the wildlings show, the scouts light a beacon atop the ridge to warn the defenders back at Castle Black of the [impending attack](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black).(wiki:Weatherback_Ridge.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
