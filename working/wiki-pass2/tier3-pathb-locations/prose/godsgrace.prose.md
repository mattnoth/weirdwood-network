## Origins

During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) found Godsgrace to be deserted during her flight on [Meraxes](wiki:Meraxes) toward [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear).(wiki:Godsgrace.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
According to Archmaester [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn), each Dornish castle, with the exception of Sunspear, was burned in the Dragon's Wroth during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War).(wiki:Godsgrace.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
