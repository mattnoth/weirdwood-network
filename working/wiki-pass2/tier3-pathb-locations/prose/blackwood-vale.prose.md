## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood) sweeps the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) from his lands, but the invaders leave him with nothing but [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall) and a scorched desert.(wiki:Blackwood_Vale.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) travels to Raventree to negotiate the surrender of Lord Tytos and to end the [siege of Raventree](wiki:Siege_of_Raventree). Jaime sees that little remains of the fields, farms, and orchards of the valley, which he attributes to the campaign of his father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Blackwood_Vale.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
The besieger, Lord [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken), desires to gain the east bank of the [Widow's Wash](wiki:Widow's_Wash), from [Crossbow Ridge](wiki:Crossbow_Ridge) to [Rutting Meadow](wiki:Rutting_Meadow) and all the islands in the stream, [Grindcorn Mill](wiki:Grindcorn_Mill), [Lord's Mill](wiki:Lord's_Mill), the ruins of [Muddy Hall](wiki:Muddy_Hall), the [Ravishment](wiki:Ravishment), [Battle Valley](wiki:Battle_Valley), [Oldforge](wiki:Oldforge), the villages of [Buckle](wiki:Buckle), [Blackbuckle](wiki:Blackbuckle), [Cairns](wiki:Cairns) and [Claypool](wiki:Claypool), and the market town at [Mudgrave](wiki:Mudgrave), [Waspwood](wiki:Waspwood), [Lorgen's Wood](wiki:Lorgen's_Wood), [Greenhill](wiki:Greenhill), the [Teats](wiki:Teats), and [Honeytree](wiki:Honeytree) and all the hives. Jonos omits [Pennytree](wiki:Pennytree), as it is a [royal](wiki:Iron_Throne) fief. However, Tytos only agrees to transfer [Woodhedge](wiki:Woodhedge), Crossbow Ridge, Buckle, Lord's Mill, and Honeytree.(wiki:Blackwood_Vale.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
