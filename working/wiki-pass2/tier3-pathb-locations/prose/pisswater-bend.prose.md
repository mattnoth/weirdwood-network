## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser [Jacelyn Bywater](wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater) reports to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) that the [bloody flux](wiki:Bloody_flux) is spreading in the [stews](wiki:Bowl_of_brown) along Pisswater Bend.(wiki:Pisswater_Bend.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Young Griff](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(Young_Griff)) explains how he survived the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing) to Tyrion. The story involves a baby swap with a [tanner's son](wiki:Pisswater_prince) from Pisswater Bend.(wiki:Pisswater_Bend.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) reflects on the [walk of atonement](wiki:Walk_of_atonement) of his niece, [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), and he is irritated that [people](wiki:Smallfolk) from Pisswater Bend saw her naked.(wiki:Pisswater_Bend.cite_ref-Radwd72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
