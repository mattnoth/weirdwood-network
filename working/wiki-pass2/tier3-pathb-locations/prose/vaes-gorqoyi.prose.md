## Origins

During the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood), Mardosh defended itself against various [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) [khalasars](wiki:Khalasar) for six years. When the Mardoshi were driven to the point of starvation, their warriors slew their women and children before riding out of the city walls to certain defeat. The fall of Mardosh led to the remaining Sarnori to unite and march against the Dothraki at the [Field of Crows](wiki:Field_of_Crows).
