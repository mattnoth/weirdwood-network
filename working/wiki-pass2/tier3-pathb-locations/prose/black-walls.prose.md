## Appearances & Description

The Black Walls consist of a great oval of fused black [dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(material)) built two hundred feet high in the eastern half of [Volantis](wiki:Volantis). The wall is so thick that six four-horse [chariots](wiki:Chariot) can race around its top abreast, which is done annually to celebrate the founding of the city.(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_volantis.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Armored guardsmen are stationed on the Wall's parapets.(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
Within the Black Walls are a large labyrinth of palaces, courtyards, towers, temples, cloisters, and cellars. The [Old Blood](wiki:Old_Blood) in their ancient palaces are attended to by armies of [slaves](wiki:Slaves).(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_volantis.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
No outlander, [freedman](wiki:Freedman), slave, or foreigner is allowed inside the Black Wall unless they are invited by the [Old Blood](wiki:Old_Blood).(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_volantis.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) Women of good birth who dwell behind the Black Walls are allowed to vote.(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)
Many inside the Black Walls keep the old gods of [Valyria](wiki:Valyria), but [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor) is favored outside the Black Walls, both by slaves and freedmen.(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_volantis.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) The [Temple of the Lord of Light](wiki:Temple_of_the_Lord_of_Light) is found in a plaza outside of the Black Walls, near the river road of the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne).(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

## Origins

When [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) was no more than a military outpost of the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold), the Black Walls were raised by the [Valyrians](wiki:Valyrians) to protect the new settlement's garrison of soldiers.(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_volantis.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4) Volantis began to expand as taverns, [brothels](wiki:Brothel), and stables were raised outside of the walls and merchant ships came to the natural harbor at the mouth of the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne).(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_volantis.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)
After [Garin the Great](wiki:Garin_the_Great)'s victories at [Selhorys](wiki:Selhorys), [Valysar](wiki:Valysar), and [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys), the Volantenes retreated to their Black Walls and appealed to the Freehold for assistance against the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar).(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
For two generations during the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood) which followed the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria), [Lys](wiki:Lys), [Myr](wiki:Myr), and Volantis were [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) ruled from within the Black Wall by [Volantene triarchs](wiki:Triarch_of_Volantis). That endeavor ended when the [tigers](wiki:Tigers_(Volantis)) tried to conquer [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) and were defeated by a coalition, however.(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Thirty-three years ago the [widow of the waterfront](wiki:Widow_of_the_waterfront) was compelled to sell the manse of her late husband, [Vogarro](wiki:Vogarro), as no freedman may dwell within the Black Wall.(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) espies the Black Wall across the wide blue expanse of the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne) while he is heading toward the western end of the [Long Bridge](wiki:Long_Bridge).(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5)
Writing [his dragonlore](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister's_dragonlore) on the [Shy Maid](wiki:Shy_Maid), [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) wishes for [Galendro](wiki:Galendro)'s [The Fires of the Freehold](wiki:The_Fires_of_the_Freehold) and hopes that Volantis has a copy in a library. He thinks he may find a copy if he can find a way inside the Black Walls to the city's heart.(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
While playing [cyvasse](wiki:Cyvasse) with Tyrion in [Selhorys](wiki:Selhorys), [Qavo Nogarys](wiki:Qavo_Nogarys) reveals that [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s smashing of the slave trade in [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay) has deeply affected those that dwell behind the Black Walls and beyond.(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
During his sermon [Benerro](wiki:Benerro) points at the Black Wall behind the [Temple of the Lord of Light](wiki:Temple_of_the_Lord_of_Light_(Volantis)), gesturing up at its parapets, where a handful of armored guardsmen stand gazing down.(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)
While chained to a wall in a room in the [Merchant's House](wiki:Merchant's_House), Tyrion sees that one of the two windows looks towards the [Long Bridge](wiki:Long_Bridge) and the black-walled heart of Old Volantis across the river.(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7) The [widow of the waterfront](wiki:Widow_of_the_waterfront) tells Tyrion that the Old Blood will have their dogs and children voting before any freedman.(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-8)

Even the vilest beggar stands higher than a slave. This dragon queen would rob him of that consolation.(wiki:Black_Walls.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)
