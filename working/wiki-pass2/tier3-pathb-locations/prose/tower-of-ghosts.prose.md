## Origins

The tower's original name was lost with the death of King [Harren the Black](wiki:Harren_Hoare) and his sons in the [burning of Harrenhal](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest). It has since been named the Tower of Ghosts, with the castle believed to be [haunted](wiki:Ghosts).(wiki:Tower_of_Ghosts.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), [Gendry](wiki:Gendry), and [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie) meet near the Tower of Ghosts for their escape from [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), with Arya killing a [Bolton guard](wiki:House_Bolton) at the postern gate behind the tower.(wiki:Tower_of_Ghosts.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
