## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms) in the host of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) take their meals in the Barracks Hall while staying at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal). [Weese](wiki:Weese) has the disguised [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) sometimes serve at the hall's tables.(wiki:Barracks_Hall.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) While [Jaqen H'ghar](wiki:Jaqen_H'ghar) takes his meal one night, Arya whispers [Chiswyck](wiki:Chiswyck)'s name into his ear as a target for the assassin.(wiki:Barracks_Hall.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
On the night of the [fall of Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal), Arya hears the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) and the men of Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch)'s garrison feasting in the Barracks Hall. When fighting breaks out, Arya sees a wounded man stagger out of the hall and then be cut down by three others.(wiki:Barracks_Hall.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
