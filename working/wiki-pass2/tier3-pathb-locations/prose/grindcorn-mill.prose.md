## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Grindcorn Mill is disputed between [House Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken) and [House Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood). Lord [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken) unsuccessfully asks Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) for Grindcorn Mill for subduing Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood).(wiki:Grindcorn_Mill.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
