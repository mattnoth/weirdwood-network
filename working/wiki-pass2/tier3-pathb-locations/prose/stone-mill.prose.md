## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Stone Mill is the scene of the fiercest fighting during the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords). There, Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) manages to cross the Trident with his best men, but only after heavy losses. He is routed when Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) counter-attacks with his reserve.(wiki:Stone_Mill.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Lord [Leo Lefford](wiki:Leo_Lefford) drowns during the fighting.(wiki:Stone_Mill.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Harwin](wiki:Harwin) tells [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) that Gregor reportedly lost half of his men at Stone Mill.(wiki:Stone_Mill.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
[Rymund the Rhymer](wiki:Rymund_the_Rhymer) entertains Edmure, Ser [Marq Piper](wiki:Marq_Piper), and [Patrek Mallister](wiki:Patrek_Mallister) with verses about the battle at the Stone Mill.(wiki:Stone_Mill.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
