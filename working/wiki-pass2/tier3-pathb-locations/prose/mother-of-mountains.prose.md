## Culture

The Mother of Mountains is considered a holy place for the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) people. Only men can set foot on it, which some do to make sacrifices to their gods.(wiki:Mother_of_Mountains.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Feuding [khalasars](wiki:Khalasar) cease their fighting when near the Mother,(wiki:Mother_of_Mountains.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) and merchant caravans are allowed to visit [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak) as long as they do not profane the Mother of Mountains or the [Womb of the World](wiki:Womb_of_the_World).(wiki:Mother_of_Mountains.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Cohollo](wiki:Cohollo) informs [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) that her husband, Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), will ascend the Mother of Mountains.(wiki:Mother_of_Mountains.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
After a [wineseller](wiki:Wineseller) attempts to [poison](wiki:Poison) Daenerys, Drogo swears before the Mother of Mountains that he will invade the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Mother_of_Mountains.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
After learning of [Eroeh](wiki:Eroeh)'s death at the hands of [Mago](wiki:Mago) and [Jhaqo](wiki:Jhaqo), Daenerys swears by the Mother of Mountains that she will avenge the girl.(wiki:Mother_of_Mountains.cite_ref-Ragot68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
When Drogo's head is placed on a funeral pyre in the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea), his head is oriented toward the Mother of Mountains.(wiki:Mother_of_Mountains.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Among her [visions](wiki:Visions) in the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying), Daenerys sees a line of naked crones creeping from a [great lake](wiki:Womb_of_the_World) beneath the Mother of Mountains and kneeling before her.(wiki:Mother_of_Mountains.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
