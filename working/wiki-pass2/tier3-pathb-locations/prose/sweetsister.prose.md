## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The [Lionstar](wiki:Lionstar) stops at [Sisterton](wiki:Sisterton) and [Godric Borrell](wiki:Godric_Borrell), Lord of Sweetsister, hosts Ser [Jared](wiki:Jared_Frey), [Rhaegar](wiki:Rhaegar_Frey), and [Symond Frey](wiki:Symond_Frey) at [Breakwater](wiki:Breakwater).(wiki:Sweetsister.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
[Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan) loses heart and decides to abandon [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), sick of waiting for payment from the king. His fleet is off the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters) when this happens. He refuses to take Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) to [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) but does not throw him overboard as he threatened. Instead, Davos is set ashore on Sweetsister by rowboat.(wiki:Sweetsister.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Davos is caught in the [Belly of the Whale](wiki:Belly_of_the_Whale) trying to buy his way off island, and he is brought before Lord Godric. Rather than turn Davos over to Lord [Triston Sunderland](wiki:Triston_Sunderland), Godric allows him to continue on to White Harbor.(wiki:Sweetsister.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
