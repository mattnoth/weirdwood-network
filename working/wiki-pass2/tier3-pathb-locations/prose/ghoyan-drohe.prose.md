## Origins

Ghoyan Drohe was a [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar) city that was destroyed by the [dragons](wiki:Dragons) of [Valyria](wiki:Valyria) during the [Rhoynish Wars](wiki:Rhoynish_Wars). According to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), until the wars the city was "a fair place, green and flowering, a city of canals and fountains."(wiki:Ghoyan_Drohe.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) passes Ghoyan Drohe during the journey of [Khal](wiki:Khal) [Drogo](wiki:Drogo)'s [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) from [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) to [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak).(wiki:Ghoyan_Drohe.cite_ref-Rtloiaf_journeys.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) meets the crew of the [Shy Maid](wiki:Shy_Maid) at Ghoyan Drohe. He notes that "the canals were choked with reeds and mud, and pools of stagnant water gave birth to swarms of flies. The broken stones of temples and palaces were sinking back into the earth, and gnarled old willows grew thick along the riverbanks."(wiki:Ghoyan_Drohe.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
