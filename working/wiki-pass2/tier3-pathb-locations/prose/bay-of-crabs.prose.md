## Origins

During the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn), [Gerold Grafton](wiki:Gerold_Grafton_(king)) put down a rebellion of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) of [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown), with the dead thrown into the bay to feed crabs. Gulltown developed into a city under the rule of [House Grafton](wiki:House_Grafton).(wiki:Bay_of_Crabs.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The [Storm Kings](wiki:Storm_King) from [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon) once controlled land from [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath) to the Bay of Crabs, but they gradually lost much of it over the centuries.(wiki:Bay_of_Crabs.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
The [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen) and [Arryns](wiki:House_Arryn) fought a [battle in the waters off Gulltown](wiki:Battle_in_the_waters_off_Gulltown) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Bay_of_Crabs.cite_ref-Rfabaeong.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
After fleeing [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the dragonrider [Nettles](wiki:Nettles) was last seen flying [Sheepstealer](wiki:Sheepstealer) in the morning mists of the Bay of Crabs.(wiki:Bay_of_Crabs.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
