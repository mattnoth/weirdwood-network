## Origins

The [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) constructed [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) beneath the Dragonmont.(wiki:Dragonmont.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_doom_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
At the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), six wild and semi-wild [dragons](wiki:Dragon)—the [Cannibal](wiki:Cannibal), [Grey Ghost](wiki:Grey_Ghost), [Seasmoke](wiki:Seasmoke), [Sheepstealer](wiki:Sheepstealer), [Silverwing](wiki:Silverwing), and [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor)—made their [lairs](wiki:Dragon_lair) in the smokey caverns of the volcano.(wiki:Dragonmont.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Grey Ghost resided in a smoking vent on the volcano's eastern side.(wiki:Dragonmont.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) and [Sunfyre](wiki:Sunfyre) recuperated in a hidden lair on the eastern slopes.(wiki:Dragonmont.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
