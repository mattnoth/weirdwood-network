## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

The [Moonshadow](wiki:Moonshadow), a Braavosi courtesan, rewards the [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) singer [Dareon](wiki:Dareon) with a kiss when she hears him perform at the Moon Pool.(wiki:Moon_Pool.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
[Brusco](wiki:Brusco)'s daughter, [Talea](wiki:Talea), sometimes sells shellfish at the Moon Pool, as does [Cat of the Canals](wiki:Arya_Stark).(wiki:Moon_Pool.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
