## Appearances & Description

A hundred feet tall, the King's Tower is a round tower with merlons atop it.(wiki:King's_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) It has an [oak](wiki:Oak) door studded with [iron](wiki:Iron)(wiki:King's_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and spiral steps.(wiki:King's_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
The building overlooks the gate of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) and the foot of the wooden stair along the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:King's_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Origins

Though named for kings, no king has visited the tower in over a hundred years.(wiki:King's_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) stays in the King's Tower when he visits [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:King's_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
[Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), moves his quarters to the King's Tower after the [Lord Commander's Tower](wiki:Lord_Commander's_Tower) burns.(wiki:King's_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

From atop the King's Tower, [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) sees smoke from the burning of [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town) by [Styr](wiki:Styr).(wiki:King's_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
Jon, [Satin](wiki:Satin), and [Dick Follard](wiki:Dick_Follard) defend the tower against Styr's [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) during the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black).(wiki:King's_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Jon rests in the King's Tower amid [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder)'s assault on the Wall.(wiki:King's_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) occupies the King's Tower after his victory over the wildlings in the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall).(wiki:King's_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Since Stannis has claimed the King's Tower, Jon, the new Lord Commander, makes his residence in [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye)'s modest chambers behind the armory.(wiki:King's_Tower.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Stannis has [Val](wiki:Val) placed in rooms above his own in the King's Tower, though he allows the captive to walk the battlements. Jon sees Val staring at the Wall from the tower's roof.(wiki:King's_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Stannis watches the beheading of [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) from the steps of the King's Tower.(wiki:King's_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Stannis meets with his captains in the tower's solar.(wiki:King's_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
After Stannis departs for the [northern mountains](wiki:Northern_mountains), [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) urges Jon to move into Jeor's old chambers in the King's Tower. Jon declines, however, and keeps his chambers near the armory.(wiki:King's_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
[Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) invites Jon to her chambers in the King's Tower, where "[Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt)" is revealed to be Mance Rayder.(wiki:King's_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
The royal chambers in the King's Tower are prepared for Stannis's wife, [Selyse Baratheon](wiki:Selyse_Baratheon), when she arrives from [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea).(wiki:King's_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
When Val returns to Castle Black after finding [Tormund](wiki:Tormund) in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest), Lord Commander Snow has her quarters moved from the King's Tower to [Hardin's Tower](wiki:Hardin's_Tower).(wiki:King's_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
