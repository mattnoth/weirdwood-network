## Origins

Some of [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria)'s [ten thousand ships](wiki:Ten_thousand_ships) were attacked by corsairs from the Howling Mountain. Afterwards, the corsairs offered the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar) to settle upon the [Isle of Toads](wiki:Isle_of_Toads), on the condition that they give up their boats and that they'd send each king thirty virgin girls and pretty boys per year, but Nymeria refused.(wiki:Howling_Mountain.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
