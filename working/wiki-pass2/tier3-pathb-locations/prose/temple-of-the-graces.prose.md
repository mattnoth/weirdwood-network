## Appearances & Description

The Temple of the Graces is a huge structure topped with golden domes.(wiki:Temple_of_the_Graces.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) It lies to the west of the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) and is visible from the [pyramid's terrace](wiki:Apex_of_the_Great_Pyramid).(wiki:Temple_of_the_Graces.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The air inside the Temple of the Graces is thick with incense. Statues of the gods of [Ghis](wiki:Ghis) in alcoves stand cloaked in shadows.(wiki:Temple_of_the_Graces.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
[Red Graces](wiki:Red_Graces) wait to be chosen at [pleasure gardens](wiki:Pleasure_gardens).(wiki:Temple_of_the_Graces.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [White Graces](wiki:White_Graces) are girls of noble birth too young to serve their year in the temple's pleasure gardens.(wiki:Temple_of_the_Graces.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

From the [apex of the Great Pyramid](wiki:Apex_of_the_Great_Pyramid), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) sees the golden dome of the Temple of the Graces blazing brightly at sunrise.(wiki:Temple_of_the_Graces.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After the murder of [Stalwart Shield](wiki:Stalwart_Shield), Daenerys, ruling as [Queen of Meereen](wiki:Queen_of_Meereen), gives an order to send men to the Temple of the Graces to ask if any man has come to the [Blue Graces](wiki:Blue_Graces) with a sword wound.(wiki:Temple_of_the_Graces.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
When numerous claimants come to Daenerys to tell of animals they have lost to her [dragons](wiki:Dragon), Daenerys decides that future claimants must present themselves at the Temple of the Graces and swear a holy oath before the gods of Ghis.(wiki:Temple_of_the_Graces.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) After [Hazzea](wiki:Hazzea) is said to have been killed by [Drogon](wiki:Drogon), Daenerys declares that the child's bones should be laid to rest in the temple, with a hundred candles burned in her memory.(wiki:Temple_of_the_Graces.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[Daario Naharis](wiki:Daario_Naharis) suggests that Daenerys could end the threat of the [Great Masters](wiki:Great_Masters) by marrying [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) and then having the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons) butcher the [slavers](wiki:Slavers) when they gather to celebrate in the temple.(wiki:Temple_of_the_Graces.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
[Blue Graces](wiki:Blue_Graces) at the temple treat a sick man who arrived at Meereen riding a pale horse.(wiki:Temple_of_the_Graces.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
The [wedding of Hizdahr and Daenerys](wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen) is held in the Temple of the Graces.(wiki:Temple_of_the_Graces.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
The Blue Graces treat [Strong Belwas](wiki:Strong_Belwas) in their temple after he eats [poisoned locusts](wiki:Poisoned_locusts).(wiki:Temple_of_the_Graces.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) The [pale mare](wiki:Pale_mare) spreads throughout the city, and Meereenese begin to avoid the Temple of the Graces.(wiki:Temple_of_the_Graces.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
