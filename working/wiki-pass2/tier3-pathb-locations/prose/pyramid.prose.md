## Culture

The [Good Masters](wiki:Good_Masters) of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor) light [silk](wiki:Silk) lanterns on the terraces of their pyramids at night.(wiki:Pyramid.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
The [coins](wiki:Coin) of [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) are stamped with a stepped pyramid on one side and the [harpy](wiki:Harpy) of [Ghis](wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis) on the other.(wiki:Pyramid.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Tree, vines, and flowers can be grown on the terraces of pyramids,(wiki:Pyramid.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) including figs, dates, and olives on the terraces of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen)'s buildings.(wiki:Pyramid.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Origins

The [Great Pyramid of Ghis](wiki:Great_Pyramid_of_Ghis) was destroyed by the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) during the [Ghiscari wars](wiki:Ghiscari_wars).(wiki:Pyramid.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
The stepped pyramids of [Ghozai](wiki:Ghozai) were drowned by the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria).(wiki:Pyramid.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) sees stepped pyramids among the buildings of [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak).(wiki:Pyramid.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Daenerys takes the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) as her abode after the successful [siege of Meereen](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen). The master of the [Indigo Star](wiki:Indigo_Star) informs her that each pyramid in [Astapor](wiki:Astapor) hosts an armed camp in the aftermath of [that city's fall](wiki:Fall_of_Astapor).(wiki:Pyramid.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy) plot against Daenerys from within [Meereen](wiki:Meereen)'s pyramids in response to her abolition of [slavery](wiki:Slavery).(wiki:Pyramid.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Daenerys, now [Queen of Meereen](wiki:Queen_of_Meereen), imposes a tax of a hundred pieces of [gold](wiki:Gold) from every pyramid for every [freedman](wiki:Freedman) killed in the city, and she demands that each pyramid send her a boy and a girl to serve as [her cupbearers](wiki:Cupbearers_of_Daenerys_Targaryen).(wiki:Pyramid.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Many pyramids in Astapor are despoiled amid the city's [capture by Yunkai'i](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor).(wiki:Pyramid.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:Pyramid.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
