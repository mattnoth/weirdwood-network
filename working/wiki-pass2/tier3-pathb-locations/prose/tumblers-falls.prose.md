## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

At Tumbler's Falls, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) joins Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), who has brought barges and a large host with him. From there, they sail down the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush), and land a half day's ride outside of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). They take [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s army in the rear during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Tumbler's_Falls.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Not finding Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) in Tumbler's Falls after his escape from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), a group of [northmen](wiki:North) proceed to rape and murder, killing eight people. They are apprehended by the [Mad Huntsman](wiki:Mad_Huntsman), however, and put in [crow cages](wiki:Crow_cage) in [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept) to die. [Anguy](wiki:Anguy) eventually puts three surviving northmen out of their misery.(wiki:Tumbler's_Falls.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
