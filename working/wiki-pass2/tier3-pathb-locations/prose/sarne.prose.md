## Origins

The ancient [Kingdom of Sarnor](wiki:Kingdom_of_Sarnor) developed around the Sarne and its tributaries. [Sarnath](wiki:Sarnath), the seat of the High King of the Sarnori, was located along the southwestern branch of the Sarne. However, the grassland kingdom and its cities were ruined by the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) in the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood) following the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria).(wiki:Sarne.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
