## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) sends [Daario Naharis](wiki:Daario_Naharis) along with the [Stormcrows](wiki:Stormcrows) to [Lhazar](wiki:Lhazar) in the hopes of reestablishing the land trade route through the Khyzai Pass.(wiki:Khyzai_Pass.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The [Long Lances](wiki:Long_Lances), under contract with [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai), attempt to close the Khyzai Pass. The Stormcrows descend on them in the night and [defeat them](wiki:Fighting_on_the_Khyzai_Pass).(wiki:Khyzai_Pass.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
