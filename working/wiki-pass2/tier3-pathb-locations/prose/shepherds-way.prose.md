## Origins

On the last day of [130 AC](wiki:130_AC), the [Shepherd](wiki:Shepherd) and his remaining two hundred forty-one followers were covered with pitch and chained to poles along Hill Street. As King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) advanced up the street in his litter, his [knights](wiki:Knight) lit the captives on fire with torches. At the end, Aegon set the Shepherd aflame himself.
