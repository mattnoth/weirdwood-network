## Appearances & Description

Saath is a small port with white walls.(wiki:Saath.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_shivering_sea.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) The people of Saath still call themselves Tagaez Fen, or [Tall Men](wiki:Tall_Men), after the Sarnori of old. The city has managed to survive with the support of [Ib](wiki:Ib) and [Lorath](wiki:Lorath).(wiki:Saath.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The people of Saath often trade with the Lorathi colony of [Morosh](wiki:Morosh).(wiki:Saath.cite_ref-Rawoiafmorosh.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Origins

Saath and its sister city, [Sarys](wiki:Sarys), fought the [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibbenese) for control over the mouth of the [Sarne](wiki:Sarne) on several occasions.(wiki:Saath.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Saath is the last surviving remnant of the [Kingdom of Sarnor](wiki:Kingdom_of_Sarnor), as the other Sarnori cities, including Sarys, were destroyed by the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) during the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood).(wiki:Saath.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
