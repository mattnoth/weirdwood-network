## Origins

The hill became known as Visenya's Hill as [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) grew after [Aegon's Landing](wiki:Aegon's_Landing); its original name is now forgotten.(wiki:Visenya's_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) A grand sept was built on Visenya's Hill with funding from one of the [High Septons](wiki:High_Septon) during the reign of King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Visenya's_Hill.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen) lived in a [manse](wiki:Manse) atop Visenya's Hill while the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) was under construction, but he relocated to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) amid tensions with the [Faith Militant](wiki:Faith_Militant).(wiki:Visenya's_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) During the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising), King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) raised the red dragon of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) atop Visenya's Hill to rally his supporters.(wiki:Visenya's_Hill.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), [Sheepstealer](wiki:Sheepstealer) landed on Visenya's Hill as part of the [fall of King's Landing](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:Visenya's_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) The child [Gaemon Palehair](wiki:Gaemon_Palehair), said to be a bastard of [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), was briefly acclaimed a king during the civil war and made his seat at the hill's [House of Kisses](wiki:House_of_Kisses).(wiki:Visenya's_Hill.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
The [wedding of Aegon III Targaryen and Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Wedding_of_Aegon_III_Targaryen_and_Jaehaera_Targaryen) was held atop Visenya's Hill after the Dance ended, with towering grandstands built for noble guests.(wiki:Visenya's_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) A branch of the [Rogare Bank](wiki:Rogare_Bank) was opened on Visenya's Hill during the [Lysene Spring](wiki:Lysene_Spring).(wiki:Visenya's_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_an_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Construction of the [Great Sept](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) began during the reign of [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Visenya's_Hill.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
