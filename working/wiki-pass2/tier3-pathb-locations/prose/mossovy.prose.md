## Culture

Mossovy is said to be a cold and dark land of [shapechangers](wiki:Shapechanger) and [demon hunters](wiki:Demonhunter). It is unknown what lies beyond, although some [septons](wiki:Septon) claim the world ends beyond the forest.(wiki:Mossovy.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_east_of_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Origins

[Sarnori](wiki:Sarnori) ships sailed as east as Far Mossovy in the height of their power.(wiki:Mossovy.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Upon revealing to [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) and [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) that she had wed her brother, King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC), [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) remarked that even if she was sent to the ends of the earth and forced to marry "the King of Mossovy or the Lord of the [Grey Waste](wiki:Grey_Waste)", she would still find her way back to her new husband.(wiki:Mossovy.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
On his third voyage on the [Sea Snake](wiki:Sea_Snake), [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) became the first [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) to navigate the [Thousand Islands](wiki:Thousand_Islands) and visit the shores of [N'ghai](wiki:N'ghai) and Mossovy.(wiki:Mossovy.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
