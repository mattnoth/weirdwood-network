## Appearances & Description

Skyreach is carved into the stone which overlooks the [Prince's Pass](wiki:Prince's_Pass), the shortest and easiest route through the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) from [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) to the [Reach](wiki:Reach). The castle is known for its lofty perch and soaring towers.(wiki:Skyreach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_kingdoms_of_the_first_men.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Origins

The ancient castle of Skyreach was home to the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) lords of [House Fowler](wiki:House_Fowler) who called themselves [Kings of Stone and Sky](wiki:Kings_of_Stone_and_Sky).(wiki:Skyreach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_kingdoms_of_the_first_men.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
When King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) invaded Dorne during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), he took Skyreach by storm. After Aegon returned to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), however, Skyreach was retaken by Dornish spearmen. After Lord Fowler burned [Nightsong](wiki:Nightsong), Aegon vowed to make Skyreach a "[second Harrenhal](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal)" and used the [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame) of [Balerion](wiki:Balerion) against the castle.(wiki:Skyreach.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
While growing up, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) heard reports of a [dwarf](wiki:Dwarf) [jester](wiki:Jester) at Skyreach.(wiki:Skyreach.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
