## Organization

The forts' walls are slabs of fused [black stone](wiki:Black_stone) almost a thousand feet high. Some think the walls are [Valyrian](wiki:Valyria) in origin, although there is no record of Valyrians having visited the area and they even predate the rise of the [Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold). Each fort can house ten thousand men.(wiki:Five_Forts.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Origins

The Five Forts predate the [Golden Empire of Yi Ti](wiki:Golden_Empire_of_Yi_Ti). Some suggest they were built by the [Pearl Emperor](wiki:Pearl_Emperor) at the time of the [Great Empire of the Dawn](wiki:Great_Empire_of_the_Dawn) to defend against the [demons](wiki:Demon) of the [Lion of Night](wiki:Lion_of_Night). They currently guard the Golden Empire from raiders out of the Grey Waste.(wiki:Five_Forts.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
