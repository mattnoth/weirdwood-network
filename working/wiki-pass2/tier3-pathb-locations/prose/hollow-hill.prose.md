## Appearances & Description

The walls of the cave consist of stone and soil interrupted by the huge roots of [weirwoods](wiki:Weirwood). The hollow hill has numerous tunnels, crannies, and crevices. The brotherhood build a firepit which has stained the ceiling of the largest cavern. One section of the weirwood roots seems to form a stairway leading to where the brotherhood's leader, Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion), rests in a tangle of roots. Men, women, and little children live within the cave. Its defenders call themselves the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), the knights of the hollow hill, and the forgotten fellowship.(wiki:Hollow_hill.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Harwin](wiki:Harwin) and other outlaws bring the blindfolded [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), [Gendry](wiki:Gendry), and [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) to the hollow hill to meet Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion). Arya is unable to tell how far the cave's tunnels extend. She is in attendance when Sandor kills Beric in a [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat), and then she witnesses Beric rise from the dead after [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr) provides the [kiss of life](wiki:Last_kiss).(wiki:Hollow_hill.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) The outlaws release Sandor a few miles from the hollow hill.(wiki:Hollow_hill.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
While resting in a brewhouse after dispatching members of the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions), Gendry tells Beric that he would like to [smith](wiki:Smith) for the brotherhood. Beric decides to [knight](wiki:Knight) him as Ser Gendry, knight of the hollow hill.(wiki:Hollow_hill.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After rescuing [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) from [Biter](wiki:Biter), the brotherhood bring her to meet [Lady Stoneheart](wiki:Lady_Stoneheart) in a cave whose description resembles the hollow hill.(wiki:Hollow_hill.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
