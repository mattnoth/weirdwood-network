## Origins

[Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) lacks a safe anchorage because of wild waters and dangerous rocks in Shipbreaker Bay. Instead, the [Storm Kings](wiki:Storm_King) of [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon) kept fleets at [Massey's Hook](wiki:Massey's_Hook), [Estermont](wiki:Estermont), [Tarth](wiki:Tarth), or the [Sea of Dorne](wiki:Sea_of_Dorne).(wiki:Shipbreaker_Bay.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_stormlands:_the_men_of_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
At the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) and [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) killed Prince [Lucerys Velaryon](wiki:Lucerys_Velaryon) and [Arrax](wiki:Arrax) above the bay.(wiki:Shipbreaker_Bay.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Lord [Steffon Baratheon](wiki:Steffon_Baratheon) and his wife, [Cassana Estermont](wiki:Cassana_Estermont), died when the [Windproud](wiki:Windproud) broke up within sight of Storm's End during a sudden storm.(wiki:Shipbreaker_Bay.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
During the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End), the smuggler [Davos](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) navigated the rocks of Shipbreaker Bay to bring supplies to the besieged [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Shipbreaker_Bay.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet) of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) sails from [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)) to Shipbreaker Bay for the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)).(wiki:Shipbreaker_Bay.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) sails across Shipbreaker Bay to smuggle [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) below [the castle](wiki:Storm's_End).(wiki:Shipbreaker_Bay.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Two cogs from Stannis's fleet are lost on the rocks of Shipbreaker Bay on the day his fleet sets sail for [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Shipbreaker_Bay.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lords [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) and [Mathis Rowan](wiki:Mathis_Rowan) command another [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(300)), which still supports Stannis after the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). Lord [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne) has part of the [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet) patrol Shipbreaker Bay to prevent the castle from being resupplied by sea.(wiki:Shipbreaker_Bay.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
