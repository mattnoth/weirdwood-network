## Origins

The ancient [mazemakers](wiki:Mazemakers) constructed a great labyrinth at Lorassyon, covering three quarters of the island's surface and having four subterranean levels.(wiki:Lorassyon.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_lorath.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The seat of [Qarlon the Great](wiki:Qarlon_the_Great) was a wooden keep at the center of Lorassyon's maze. The keep was destroyed by [Valyrian](wiki:Valyria) [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlord) during the [Scouring of Lorath](wiki:Scouring_of_Lorath).(wiki:Lorassyon.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_lorath.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
When the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) of the isles overthrew the disciples of [Boash](wiki:Boash), the only surviving acolytes were those who fled to Lorassyon. They died out almost a century later.(wiki:Lorassyon.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_lorath.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
