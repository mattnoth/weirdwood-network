## Origins

During the [Reyne-Tarbeck revolt](wiki:Reyne-Tarbeck_revolt) against [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister), the [Reynes](wiki:House_Reyne) retreated to their largely-underground castle, [Castamere](wiki:Castamere), after the defeat of the [Tarbecks](wiki:House_Tarbeck). To prevent the Reynes from escaping, Ser [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) had his host destroy the gates of their castle. The Lannisters then dammed the stream which fed the pool of Castamere, diverting the stream into House Reyne's mines.
