## Origins

The city, located on the north end of the [Isle of Cedars](wiki:Isle_of_Cedars), vanished in a heartbeat during the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria). The ancient brick walls and stepped [pyramids](wiki:Pyramid) of the slaver port of Ghozai suffered the same fate as [Velos](wiki:Velos) on the south end of the island - a wall of water 300 feet high descended on the city, drowning hundreds of thousands of men, women, and children, leaving none to tell the tale.(wiki:Ghozai.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) The broken pyramids are currently infested with [monkeys](wiki:Monkey).(wiki:Ghozai.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) orders the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) to chase a trading galley, the [Ghiscari Dawn](wiki:Ghiscari_Dawn), which tries to escape by drawing close to the Isle of Cedars. By the time [Lamentation](wiki:Lamentation_(ship)) comes alongside its prey, both ships are so close to the haunted ruins of Ghozai that they can hear [monkeys](wiki:Monkey) chattering as the first light of dawn washes over the city's broken [pyramids](wiki:Pyramid).(wiki:Ghozai.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
