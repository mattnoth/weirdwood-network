## Origins

The [Ghiscari](wiki:Ghiscari_Empire) hill city Hazdahn Mo was once a thriving slave market.(wiki:Vaes_Diaf.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
After the fall of [Sathar](wiki:Sathar), at the hands of the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) during the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood), the women and children of the city were carried off on a grueling march to Hazdahn Mo, though three-quarters of them died.(wiki:Vaes_Diaf.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Hazdahn Mo was in turn eventually destroyed by the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) during the same century.(wiki:Vaes_Diaf.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_vaes_diaf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
