## Appearances & Description

The Quill and Tankard is located on an island in the [Honeywine](wiki:Honeywine). It is a tall, timbered building that leans toward the south and has a common room and a torchlit terrace. There is an apple tree by the river. The island connects to the rest of the city with an old plank bridge.(wiki:Quill_and_Tankard.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
The inn is renowned for its fearsomely strong [cider](wiki:Cider). Novices from the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) go there to drink. Some serving wenches also entertain men. [Emma](wiki:Emma) is the oldest of the inn's wenches, who also include her daughter, [Rosey](wiki:Rosey).(wiki:Quill_and_Tankard.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Origins

In six hundred years, the Quill and Tankard has never closed.(wiki:Quill_and_Tankard.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
When Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) was a young novice he used to go to the Quill and Tankard.(wiki:Quill_and_Tankard.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) When his younger brother, Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen), and Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall), the knight Aegon squired for, came to visit Aemon, they also went to the inn.(wiki:Quill_and_Tankard.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast For Crows[ ]

[Alleras](wiki:Alleras) buys [Mollander](wiki:Mollander), [Roone](wiki:Roone) and [Pate](wiki:Pate_(novice)) a round of [cider](wiki:Cider) at the Quill and Tankard to celebrate his [copper](wiki:Copper) link. There, they talk of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) and the rumors of her three [dragons](wiki:Dragon) emanating from [Qarth](wiki:Qarth). Pate desires to pay [Emma](wiki:Emma) a [golden dragon](wiki:Currency) for the [maidenhead](wiki:Maiden) of her daughter, [Rosey](wiki:Rosey).(wiki:Quill_and_Tankard.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
