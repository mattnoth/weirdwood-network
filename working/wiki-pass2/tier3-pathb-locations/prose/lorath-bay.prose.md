## Culture

[Leviathans](wiki:Leviathan), [seals](wiki:Seal), and [walruses](wiki:Walrus) live in Lorath Bay. While the city of [Lorath](wiki:Lorath) claims the bay, they lack the strength to prevent [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) fishing fleets and [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibbenese) whalers and sealers from entering the waters. The western shore of the bay is controlled by Braavos.(wiki:Lorath_Bay.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_lorath.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Origins

The shores and isles of Lorath Bay were inhabited in antiquity by [mazemakers](wiki:Mazemakers), [hairy men](wiki:Hairy_men), and [Andals](wiki:Andals), the latter of whom used longships. The Andals were defeated in the [Scouring of Lorath](wiki:Scouring_of_Lorath), however, and the region was eventually colonized by the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold).(wiki:Lorath_Bay.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_lorath.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
