## Appearances & Description

Torrhen's Square has stone walls thirty feet high with square towers at each corner. Its square keep is considered strong.(wiki:Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Origins

King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) visited Torrhen's Square while traveling from [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) to [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) in [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) accompanied Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) during a visit to Torrhen's Square for a fortnight circa [296 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

With Ser [Helman Tallhart](wiki:Helman_Tallhart), the Master of Torrhen's Square, and the best Tallhart men on campaign with [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) in the south, the castle is left in the hands of its castellan, Helman's brother [Leobald](wiki:Leobald_Tallhart). He brings a large party from Torrhen's Square to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) for the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast).(wiki:Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
During his [harrying of the Stony Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore), [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) allows some villagers to escape and warn Torrhen's Square.(wiki:Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Leobald sends Helman's son [Benfred](wiki:Benfred_Tallhart) and his [Wild Hares](wiki:Wild_Hares) to deal with the raiders,(wiki:Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) but they are slain by Theon's [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn).(wiki:Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
When Torrhen's Square comes under attack from ironborn led by [Dagmer Cleftjaw](wiki:Dagmer_Cleftjaw), Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) takes the better part of the Winterfell garrison to respond to the threat. Once there, he breaks Dagmer's shield wall in the [fight at Torrhen's Square](wiki:Fight_at_Torrhen's_Square) and sends the ironborn retreating back to the [Stony Shore](wiki:Stony_Shore).(wiki:Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser Helman is slain in the [battle at Duskendale](wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale).(wiki:Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sends a raven to Torrhen's Square in a plea for help to defend [Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) from [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings).(wiki:Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Dagmer returns to [capture the castle](wiki:Capture_of_Torrhen's_Square), as Leobald was slain and the Tallhart forces were largely destroyed by the [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton) in the [battle at Winterfell](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell).(wiki:Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Rasos76.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_torrhen.27s_square.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Dagmer holds captive the surviving Tallharts at Torrhen's Square.(wiki:Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) offers to help [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) recover Torrhen's Square, but he refuses and focuses on Winterfell instead.(wiki:Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
