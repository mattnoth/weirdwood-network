## Origins

After [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) ascended the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) in [48 AC](wiki:48_AC), [Elinor Costayne](wiki:Elinor_Costayne), a widow of the late King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), returned to her father's seat at Three Towers.(wiki:Three_Towers.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) In [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), Jaehaerys and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) traveled to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) after the death of the [High Septon](wiki:High_Lickspittle). While awaiting the arrival of [Alfyn](wiki:Alfyn), the king visited [Bandallon](wiki:Bandallon), Three Towers, [Uplands](wiki:Uplands), [Honeyholt](wiki:Honeyholt), and the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor).(wiki:Three_Towers.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Three Towers declared for [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), but Lord [Owen Costayne](wiki:Owen_Costayne) was slain in the [Battle on the Honeywine](wiki:Battle_on_the_Honeywine).(wiki:Three_Towers.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
