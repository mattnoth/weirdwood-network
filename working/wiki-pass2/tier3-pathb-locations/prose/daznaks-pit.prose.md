## Appearances & Description

At the pit's entrance are the [Gates of Fate](wiki:Gates_of_Fate),(wiki:Daznak's_Pit.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) where the names of all gladiators who have died in the arena are engraved.(wiki:Daznak's_Pit.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The circular brick arena has descending tiers of benches, each in a different color. The colors are:
black
purple
blue
green
white
yellow
orange
red
The bottom tiers, the red and orange, are for the highest ranking of Meereen. The black and purple benches, the highest from the arena, are for the lowest of Meereen, usually [slaves](wiki:Slave) or the poorer Meereenese citizens.(wiki:Daznak's_Pit.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After the [siege of Meereen](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) has 163 of the [Great Masters](wiki:Great_Masters) impaled on spikes in Daznak's Pit in retaliation for the 163 slave children the masters killed to spite her.(wiki:Daznak's_Pit.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Daznak's_Pit.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

In celebration of the marriage between Daenerys and [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) - who had bought all fighting pits in the city after it was conquered - and the peace between [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) and [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), the fighting pit is reopened. Several fights and spectacles take place but the noise and smell of blood draws [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) to the pit, where he causes death and chaos and leaves with Daenerys on his back.(wiki:Daznak's_Pit.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
After [Viserion](wiki:Viserion) and [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal) are accidentally unleashed upon the city, the [ruling council of Meereen](wiki:Ruling_council_of_Meereen) herd 1,000 sheep into the pit to feed them and keep them sated. The dragons prove to have a taste for mutton, returning to the pit whenever they grow hungry.(wiki:Daznak's_Pit.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
