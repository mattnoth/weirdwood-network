## Appearances & Description

The [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad) enters [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) from the north at the Gate of the Gods,(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Rtloiafking.27s_landing.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) which is wider and more splendid than the [Lion Gate](wiki:Lion_Gate).(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) There are detailed carvings on the gatehouse and over the portcullis with eyes that seem to follow as one enters the gates.(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The gatehouse has a windowless guard room.(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
The Gate of the Gods can be seen from the [Broken Anvil](wiki:Broken_Anvil).(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Farmers sell vegetables at a market square inside the gate.(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) A barracks of the [City Watch of King's Landing](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing) is found by the gate.(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Origins

In [19 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) decreed that new city walls with seven gates, each with a massive gatehouse and defensive towers, be built to protect [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Grand Maester [Gawen](wiki:Gawen) and Ser [Osmund Strong](wiki:Osmund_Strong), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), headed the construction, which took place between [20](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)–[26 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
In the reign of [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), Maegor had his own wife and queen, [Alys Harroway](wiki:Alys_Harroway), tortured to death and executed. Following her death, Alys's body was cut into seven parts and mounted on spikes above each of the seven gates of the city, one of which was above the Gate of the Gods.(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), [Blood](wiki:Blood) was taken into custody at the Gate of the Gods.(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) When he departed for [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) rode forth with his host from the gate.(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) The captain of the Gate of the Gods and a third of his gold cloaks died alongside Ser [Luthor Largent](wiki:Luthor_Largent) while trying to disperse [rioters](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)) at [Cobbler's Square](wiki:Cobbler's_Square). [Wat the Tanner](wiki:Wat_the_Tanner) led a mob of ruffians against the Gate of the Gods, and they streamed into the countryside after overwhelming the weak garrison which remained.(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) [Mysaria](wiki:Mysaria) died from being whipped naked while trying to walk from the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) to the Gate of the Gods.(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) After their victory in the [Battle of the Kingsroad](wiki:Battle_of_the_Kingsroad), the [Lads](wiki:Lads) had a parley with Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) and Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) outside of the Gate of the Gods before entering the city.(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
During the [regency of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III), the King's Hand, Ser [Tyland Lannister](wiki:Tyland_Lannister) ordered the city gates to be strengthened so they could better resist attacks from within the city walls.(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) In [135 AC](wiki:135_AC), the [Commander of the City Watch](wiki:Commander_of_the_City_Watch), Ser [Lucas Leygood](wiki:Lucas_Leygood) was implicated in a plot against [Larra Rogare](wiki:Larra_Rogare), the wife of Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen). When men came to arrest him, Lucas refused to go meekly and was killed along with eight others in a savage fight at the Gate of the Gods.(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

In the aftermath of the [arrest of Eddard Stark](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark), the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) close up all the gates of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), except for the [Mud Gate](wiki:Mud_Gate) and the Gate of the Gods where people can enter the city but not leave it.(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) arrives at the city through the Gate of the Gods.(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
Ser [Jacelyn Bywater](wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater), [Commander of the City Watch](wiki:Commander_of_the_City_Watch), summons Tyrion to the Gate of the Gods when Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) arrives. Tyrion meets with Cleos in the windowless guardroom.(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) The farmers' market by the gate is empty because of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)
During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), mobs fight to escape the city at the [Iron Gate](wiki:Iron_Gate) and the Gate of the Gods.(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

The Gate of the Gods is open when Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and [Brienne Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth) arrive at King's Landing. Jaime sees two dozen wagons waiting to enter; a [gold cloak](wiki:Gold_cloak) tells [Steelshanks Walton](wiki:Steelshanks_Walton) that each merchant must pay a toll for the right to sell in the city.(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

The procession returning Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s body to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) leaves King's Landing through the Gate of the Gods. Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) feels it would have been more fitting and appropriate if they left via the [Lion Gate](wiki:Lion_Gate) instead.(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Jaime is sent by his sister, Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), to inspect the city gates.(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

In [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) tells Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) that he escaped through the [Mud Gate](wiki:Mud_Gate) after being dismissed from the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). The knight then reentered King's Landing though the Gate of the Gods while disguised as an old man.(wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
