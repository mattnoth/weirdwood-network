## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) has a [freerider](wiki:Freerider) brought through the Battlements Gate's bridge, up stairs to the outer wall, and tossed by the [Bastard's Boys](wiki:Bastard's_Boys) to the snowy ground outside [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Battlements_Gate.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy), allowed to walk around the castle, passes through the Battlements Gate and considers jumping to his death from the walls.(wiki:Battlements_Gate.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
When Theon and [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole) escape Winterfell, [Holly](wiki:Holly) and [Frenya](wiki:Frenya) kill two guards at the Battlements Gate. They use the drawbridge to cross the frozen moat and then climb the stair to the top of the outer wall. Theon grabs Jeyne and jumps from the battlements as guardsmen try to stop them.(wiki:Battlements_Gate.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
