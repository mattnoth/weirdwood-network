## Origins

The town of [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) is sacked [first by "lions"](wiki:First_sack_of_Maidenpool), [again by "wolves"](wiki:Second_sack_of_Maidenpool), and [finally by outlaws](wiki:Third_sack_of_Maidenpool). [William Mooton](wiki:William_Mooton), Lord of Maidenpool, remains in his castle and does not defend the town each time it is attacked.(wiki:Taking_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The town's gates are destroyed, half of the buildings are burned or plundered, and its famous pool is filled with corpses.(wiki:Taking_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
