## Origins

The war was the result of the [Rape of the Three Sisters](wiki:Rape_of_the_Three_Sisters) two thousand years ago, when [northmen](wiki:Northmen) occupied the previously-independent islands known as the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters). In response, the [pirate](wiki:Pirate) kings of the [Sistermen](wiki:Sistermen) bent the knee to King [Mathos II Arryn](wiki:Mathos_II_Arryn) in exchange for the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn)'s assistance in driving out the northmen.(wiki:War_Across_the_Water.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:War_Across_the_Water.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Mathos sailed for [Sisterton](wiki:Sisterton) with a hundred warships. Although he never returned from war, his sons and descendants continued the fight against the northmen for a thousand years.(wiki:War_Across_the_Water.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Control of the Three Sisters changed more than twelve times. Northmen landed on the [Fingers](wiki:Fingers) three times;(wiki:War_Across_the_Water.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) King [Theon Stark](wiki:Theon_Stark) once conquered the islands and landed an army on the Fingers,(wiki:War_Across_the_Water.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) though it is unknown if this was part of the Worthless War or during a different time. A northern castle, the [Wolf's Den](wiki:Wolf's_Den) at the mouth of the [White Knife](wiki:White_Knife), was besieged by King [Osgood Arryn](wiki:Osgood_Arryn) and burned by his son, [Oswin the Talon](wiki:Oswin_Arryn).(wiki:War_Across_the_Water.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The [Starks](wiki:House_Stark) attacked [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown) in response and, unable to overcome its walls, burned hundreds of ships in the city's harbor.(wiki:War_Across_the_Water.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

The war eventually ended when House Stark simply lost interest in controlling the Three Sisters.(wiki:War_Across_the_Water.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

## Aftermath

The war is described in Archmaester [Perestan](wiki:Perestan)'s *[A Consideration of History](wiki:A_Consideration_of_History)*.(wiki:War_Across_the_Water.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

Rule by the triumphant [Arryns](wiki:House_Arryn) over the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters) has remained tenuous.(wiki:War_Across_the_Water.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Some [Sistermen](wiki:Sistermen) still resent [northmen](wiki:Northmen) for the invasion.(wiki:War_Across_the_Water.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
