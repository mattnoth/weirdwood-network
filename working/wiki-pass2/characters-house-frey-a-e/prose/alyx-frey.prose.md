## Appearances & Description

[Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) considers Alyx to be one of the more nubile Frey maidens.(wiki:Alyx_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Alyx is one of the Frey women presented to [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) when he arrives at the [Twins](wiki:Twins) for Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully)'s [wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) to Lady [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey).(wiki:Alyx_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) During the wedding feast Alyx is seated between Robb Stark and [Robin Flint](wiki:Robin_Flint). She later dances with Robin.(wiki:Alyx_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
