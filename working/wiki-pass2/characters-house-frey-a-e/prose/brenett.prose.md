## Appearances & Description

Brenett is bald, fat and double-chinned. His robes are stained with [raven](wiki:Raven) droppings and he has an amiable personality.(wiki:Brenett.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Maester Brenett shaves the head of [Marissa Frey](wiki:Marissa_Frey) but swears the girl's hair will grow back.(wiki:Brenett.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

[Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) visits with Brenett to inquire into the family history of [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey). Her fears are somewhat allayed by this conversation, since the maester explains that [Bethany Rosby](wiki:Bethany_Rosby) was a fertile wife for Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey).(wiki:Brenett.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
