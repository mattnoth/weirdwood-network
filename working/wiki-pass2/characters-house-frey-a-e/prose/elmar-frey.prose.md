## Origins

There is a rumor in the [Twins](wiki:Twins) that Lady [Annara Farring](wiki:Annara_Farring) slept with [Black Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Ryman)), a great-grandson of Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey), casting doubts on Elmar's parentage.(wiki:Elmar_Frey.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

As part of the pact sealed during the parley between Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) and Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey), Elmar is promised to [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark).(wiki:Elmar_Frey.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Elmar squires for Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) and is part of his army when it [takes Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal).(wiki:Elmar_Frey.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) There, he frequently interacts with his betrothed, [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), who has assumed the identity "Nan", leaving Elmar unaware of the fact. When Elmar learns that his betrothal to Arya, his "princess", has been called off due to [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s marriage to [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling), he cries about losing his princess.(wiki:Elmar_Frey.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Elmar remains at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) to serve Roose Bolton. He serves as Bolton's cupbearer when Roose hosts Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth).(wiki:Elmar_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
