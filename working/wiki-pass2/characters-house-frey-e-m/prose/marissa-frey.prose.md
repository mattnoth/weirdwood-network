## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Maester [Brenett](wiki:Brenett) shaves Marissa's head but swears the girl's hair will grow back.(wiki:Marissa_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Marissa is one of the Frey women presented to [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) when he comes to the [Twins](wiki:Twins) for Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully)'s wedding.(wiki:Marissa_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Feast for Crows

After the marriage of her sister, [Amerei](wiki:Amerei_Frey), to Lord [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister), Marissa moves to [Castle Darry](wiki:Darry) with her mother, [Mariya Darry](wiki:Mariya_Darry).(wiki:Marissa_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
