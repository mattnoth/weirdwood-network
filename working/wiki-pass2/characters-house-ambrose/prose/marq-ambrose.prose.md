## Origins

During the civil war known as the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Marq was loyal to the [greens](wiki:Greens). When the [Caltrops](wiki:Caltrops) assembled to conspire against the [Two Betrayers](wiki:Two_Betrayers), Marq was of the opinion to take [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) before killing them.
