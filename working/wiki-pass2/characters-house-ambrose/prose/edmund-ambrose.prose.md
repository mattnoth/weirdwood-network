## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

While [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth) waits for [Dick Crabb](wiki:Dick_Crabb), she reflects on the events at [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) when a group of knights made a wager on who would be the first to take her [maidenhead](wiki:Maiden). To woo her Edmund brought flowers and offered to ride with her.(wiki:Edmund_Ambrose.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) After the bet was discovered Brienne sought out and defeated Edmund during the [melee at Bitterbridge](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge).(wiki:Edmund_Ambrose.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
