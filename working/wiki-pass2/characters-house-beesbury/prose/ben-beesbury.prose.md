## Origins

[Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), attempted to betroth his daughter, Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell), to Ben, a minor lordling who was blind, toothless, and eighty years old at the time. To Arianne's relief, Ben died a few years later before the betrothal was completed.(wiki:Ben_Beesbury.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

According to semi-canon sources, Ben had one son named [Alton](wiki:Alton_Beesbury). Alton joined the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) to become a [maester](wiki:Maester) and renounced his inheritance despite Ben's protests. Upon Ben's death, he was succeeded by his nephew, [Warryn Beesbury](wiki:Warryn_Beesbury).(wiki:Ben_Beesbury.cite_ref-3)

## Appearances & Description

An aged lord, Ben Beesbury was blind and toothless as he met his eighties, earning him the name "Blind Ben".(wiki:Ben_Beesbury.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
