## Origins

Alan's grandsire, Lord [Lyman Beesbury](wiki:Lyman_Beesbury), was the first to die during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), although his cause of death is disputed.(wiki:Alan_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Alan was unaware of his grandfather's death. Instead, he believed him to languish in the [black cells](wiki:Black_cells), and loudly called for his grandfather's release. Alan fought during the war as a supporter of Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and the [blacks](wiki:Blacks).(wiki:Alan_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Alan and Lord [Alan Tarly](wiki:Alan_Tarly) (the "[Two Alans](wiki:Two_Alans)") harassed the host of Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower) after it departed [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). Along with Lord [Owen Costayne](wiki:Owen_Costayne), the two Alans fought Ormund's army along the banks of the [Honeywine](wiki:Honeywine), trapping the greens with a second army of blacks led by Lord [Thaddeus Rowan](wiki:Thaddeus_Rowan) and [Tom Flowers](wiki:Tom_Flowers). Lord Hightower's lines started to crumble and defeat seemed all but a foregone conclusion. However, Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)) joined the [Battle on the Honeywine](wiki:Battle_on_the_Honeywine) upon his [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Tessarion](wiki:Tessarion), turning the battle for the greens. Both Alans were captured after the battle.(wiki:Alan_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
