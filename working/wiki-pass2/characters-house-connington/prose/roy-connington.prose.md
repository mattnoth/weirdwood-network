## Origins

Lord Roy, [Jonah Mooton](wiki:Jonah_Mooton), and Ser [Braxton Beesbury](wiki:Braxton_Beesbury) became the three male favorites of Princess [Saera Targaryen](wiki:Saera_Targaryen) in [82 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). Joined by [Alys Turnberry](wiki:Alys_Turnberry) and [Perianne Moore](wiki:Perianne_Moore), the young men and women became inseparable at every feast and ball, hunted and hawked together, and once sailed across [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay) to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Roy_Connington.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

In [84 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Roy, Jonah, and Braxton were found drunk at the [Blue Pearl](wiki:Blue_Pearl), a [brothel](wiki:Brothel) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), laughing as the king's [fool](wiki:Fool), [Tom Turnip](wiki:Tom_Turnip), was surrounded by [whores](wiki:Prostitution). When asked by men of the [City Watch](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing), Roy admitted they thought it would be funny to see the fool do the deed, and Jonah admitted it was Princess Saera's idea.(wiki:Roy_Connington.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

When Perianne and Alys were questioned about the incident, it was revealed that Alys was pregnant outside of wedlock, and that Saera had coerced them into sexual acts with her male favorites. Roy, Jonah, and Braxton were then imprisoned in the [dungeons](wiki:Black_cells) for one night.(wiki:Roy_Connington.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

When questioned by King [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), Saera defended herself by saying Roy would marry her. She confessed that she had [bedded](wiki:Bedded) all three of her male favorites, with each of them thinking he was the first to take her [maidenhead](wiki:Maidenhead). She proposed a solution of redemption by [marrying all three](wiki:Polygamy) of her favorites, like King [Maegor](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) and his six wives, at which the king grew furious.(wiki:Roy_Connington.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

A hasty marriage was proposed between Lord Roy and the pregnant Alys Turnberry, but he refused, claiming the child was Braxton's [bastard](wiki:Bastard) whom he would not make heir to Griffin's Roost. Nevertheless, the child born to Alys was a girl with red hair, signifying she was likely fathered by Lord Roy.(wiki:Roy_Connington.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

King Jaehaerys gave Roy the choice of his punishment: joining the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) or ten years of exile. Roy chose exile, going first to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) and then [Myr](wiki:Myr). He never returned to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), as he was stabbed to death by a whore only half a year before the end of his exile.(wiki:Roy_Connington.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)

## Appearances & Description

Presumably, the lord was known as Red Roy due to the characteristic red hair of [House Connington](wiki:House_Connington), as Archmaester [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn) notes red hair as an important quality in a possible child of his.(wiki:Roy_Connington.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
