## Origins

During the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), Quickfinger sided with the rebels, supporting [Daemon Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre). Quickfinger was caught with stolen [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [eggs](wiki:Dragon_egg).
