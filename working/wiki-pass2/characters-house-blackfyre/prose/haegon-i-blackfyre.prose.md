## Origins

After the [battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field) and the failure of the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), Haegon was taken to [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) by his uncle, [Bittersteel](wiki:Aegor_Rivers).(wiki:Haegon_I_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) In exile he had at least two sons, the eldest of which he named [Daemon](wiki:Daemon_III_Blackfyre). In [219 AC](wiki:219_AC) Haegon launched the [Third Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Third_Blackfyre_Rebellion), which ended in failure. He was slain treacherously after he gave up his sword.(wiki:Haegon_I_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf413.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
