## Origins

[Sigrin](wiki:Sigrin) named *[Esgred](wiki:Esgred_(ship))*, the first ship that he built, after his mother.(wiki:Esgred.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) returns to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) after nearly a decade of absence since [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion). To see what kind of man her brother has become, [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) claims to be "Esgred", the wife of the shipwright [Sigrin](wiki:Sigrin), when she encounters Theon at [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport). When Asha reveals her true identity at [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), Theon feels he has been made a [fool](wiki:Fool).(wiki:Esgred.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Asha later explains who Esgred actually is to Theon during dinner.(wiki:Esgred.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

### The Winds of Winter

Traumatized by his experiences with [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), Theon is able to remember that Asha had once gone by the name of Esgred when he reunites with his sister at the [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village) in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood).(wiki:Esgred.cite_ref-TheonI_2-0)
