## Origins

After the death of his father, [Urras Greyiron](wiki:Urras_Greyiron), Erich seized his driftwood crown and proclaimed himself [High King of the Iron Islands](wiki:High_King_of_the_Iron_Islands). However, the priest [Galon Whitestaff](wiki:Galon_Whitestaff), despite being half-blind and feeble, arose in fury and declared Erich an unlawful king, as he had not been chosen at a [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot). He assembled all [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) "captains and kings" to [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk) and Erich was quickly unmade and condemned to death. He avoided this fate by casting his father's crown to the sea, a sign of his submission to the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God).
