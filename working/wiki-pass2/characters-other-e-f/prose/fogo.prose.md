## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Fogo is with his father in the procession in [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak) that proclaims [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s son the [stallion who mounts the world](wiki:Stallion_who_mounts_the_world).(wiki:Fogo.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Upon his father's death at the hands of Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), Fogo becomes *khal*, but he is in turn killed by Drogo in the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea) near a [Lhazareen](wiki:Lhazareen) settlement.(wiki:Fogo.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
