## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) spots the banner of Sigfryd among those that have assembled for her at the [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers). When Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw) offers her the Ten Towers, Asha believes that Sigfryd would be opposed for he intends to follow his great nephew as Lord of the Ten Towers.(wiki:Sigfryd_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)
