## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Joss squires for Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) during the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Joss_Stilwood.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) When Gregor is unhorsed by Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell), he calls for his squire to bring his sword, which the Mountain then uses to kill the horse.(wiki:Joss_Stilwood.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Clash of Kings

According to [Chiswyck](wiki:Chiswyck), an overflowing river detoured the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) to a nearby alehouse while they were returning from the tourney. [Rafford](wiki:Rafford) urged Joss to loss his virginity to the brewer's young daughter, [Layna](wiki:Layna). When the brewer objected, Gregor and his men raped the girl. Gregor also stabbed the brewer's son in the belly when he tried to intervene.(wiki:Joss_Stilwood.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Feast for Crows

[Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn) mentions to Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) that he questioned Gregor's squire, who informed him that Gregor constantly suffers from extreme headaches and consumes vast quantities of [milk of the poppy](wiki:Milk_of_the_poppy) to try and dull the pain.(wiki:Joss_Stilwood.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
