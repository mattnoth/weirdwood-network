## Origins

Adrian was the captain of the [Lion Gate](wiki:Lion_Gate) during the [regency](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III) of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen).

In [135 AC](wiki:135_AC), he was the only one of [Commander](wiki:Commander_of_the_City_Watch) [Lucas Leygood](wiki:Lucas_Leygood)'s seven captains not accused of involvement in the plot against [House Rogare](wiki:House_Rogare).
