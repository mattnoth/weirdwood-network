### A Clash of Kings

After Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) takes over [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Harra continues in service to the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Harra.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) When a captive [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) arrives at Harrenhal, Harra and [Amabel](wiki:Amabel) inspect her as they do the other captives, and eventually assign her to [Weese](wiki:Weese).(wiki:Harra.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) After Lord Tywin departs Harrenhal and the castle falls to Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), Harra is executed for telling the servants of Lady [Shella Whent](wiki:Shella_Whent) to serve the Lannisters.(wiki:Harra.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
