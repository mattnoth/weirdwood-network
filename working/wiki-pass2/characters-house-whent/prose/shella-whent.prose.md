## Origins

According to a semi-canon response from [George R. R. Martin](wiki:George_R._R._Martin), Shella was married to Lord [Walter Whent](wiki:Walter_Whent), the brother of Ser [Oswell Whent](wiki:Oswell_Whent), and she was the mother of the fair maid of the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal). In *[A Game of Thrones](wiki:A_Game_of_Thrones-Chapter_28)* and *[A Clash of Kings](wiki:A_Clash_of_Kings-Chapter_47)* it is stated that Shella is from House Whent, and that her father and grandfather held Harrenhal before her.(wiki:Shella_Whent.cite_ref-W1_5-0)(wiki:Shella_Whent.cite_ref-W2_6-0)(wiki:Shella_Whent.cite_ref-W3_7-0)

## Appearances & Description

Shella is an old lady.(wiki:Shella_Whent.cite_ref-Rasos50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) She is considered a friend of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Shella_Whent.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

While traveling near the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads), [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) thinks of Lady Whent, the last of her line, living with her ghosts in the halls of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Shella_Whent.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Lady Shella [yields Harrenhal](wiki:Yielding_of_Harrenhal) to forces belonging to [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) because she lacks the men to defend it during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Shella_Whent.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) grants Harrenhal to [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) and demands that Shella swear fealty to the new king.(wiki:Shella_Whent.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Following instruction from his father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), who is aghast at a once-royal seat being granted to the upjumped son of a butcher, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) strips Lord Slynt of Harrenhal, but it is not returned to Shella.(wiki:Shella_Whent.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Instead, the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) grants the castle to Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) after the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater) and names him [Lord Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Shella_Whent.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) serves incognito at Harrenhal, where Shella's household had used only the lower thirds of two of Harrenhal's five towers.(wiki:Shella_Whent.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) speaks of [Ben Blackthumb](wiki:Ben_Blackthumb), who had smithed for Shella, her father, her grandfather, and [House Lothston](wiki:House_Lothston).(wiki:Shella_Whent.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Prior to the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), an incognito [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) tells Ser [Donnel Haigh](wiki:Donnel_Haigh) that he serves Lady Whent.(wiki:Shella_Whent.cite_ref-Rasos50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) tells [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) that Lady Shella has reportedly died, but her whereabouts and manner of death are unknown.(wiki:Shella_Whent.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Shella is considered the dispossessed Lady of Harrenhal.(wiki:Shella_Whent.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
