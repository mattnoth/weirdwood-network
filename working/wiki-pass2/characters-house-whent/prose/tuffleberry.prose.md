### A Feast for Crows

On his way to lifting the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) returns to [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) and puts the castle back to order. [Ben Blackthumb](wiki:Ben_Blackthumb), [Pia](wiki:Pia), and a cook are the only servants from Lady [Shella Whent](wiki:Shella_Whent)'s time who are still around.(wiki:Tuffleberry.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Tuffleberry's fate is unknown.
