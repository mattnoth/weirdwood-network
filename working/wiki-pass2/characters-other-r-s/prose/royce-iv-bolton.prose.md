## Origins

During one of the wars between Houses Bolton and [Stark](wiki:House_Stark), King Royce succeeded in taking and burning [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) just like his [namesake](wiki:Named_after) King [Royce II Bolton](wiki:Royce_II_Bolton) had done three hundred years earlier.(wiki:Royce_IV_Bolton.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
