## Origins

Rylona was enslaved and trained in [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) to play music for many of the [noble families](wiki:Wise_Masters) of the city.(wiki:Rylona_Rhee.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Appearances & Description

Rylona plays the [harp](wiki:Harp) beautifully.(wiki:Rylona_Rhee.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Rylona becomes a leader for the freeman of [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) and often speaks for them on Dany's council on [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). She plays her harp while Dany is entertaining guests. The [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy) break into her house and cut off her fingers before killing her.(wiki:Rylona_Rhee.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
