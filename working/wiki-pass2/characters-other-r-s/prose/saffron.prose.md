## Appearances & Description

Saffron is described as tall and slim, with big brown eyes and hair like honey. Her father considers her more precious than gold.(wiki:Saffron.cite_ref-twow_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), who is masquerading as [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish)'s [bastard](wiki:Bastard) daughter, "Alayne Stone", hears gossip from [Myranda Royce](wiki:Myranda_Royce) that, despite his youth, Ser [Harrold Hardyng](wiki:Harrold_Hardyng) has already fathered at least one baseborn child, with another on the way. Later, Petyr tells Alayne that he has arranged to betroth her to Harrold.(wiki:Saffron.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### The Winds of Winter

At the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon), during the feast for the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) for the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights), Alayne dances with Harrold, and inquires about his bastards, to which he answers in all honesty. He tells her of his two-year-old daughter [Alys Stone](wiki:Alys_Stone), fathered on a [House Waynwood](wiki:House_Waynwood) servant, [Cissy](wiki:Cissy). According to Harrold, childbirth left Cissy "fat as a cow", so Lady [Anya Waynwood](wiki:Anya_Waynwood) arranged for her to be married off to one of her [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms). However, Harrold says it is different with Saffron, who he has impregnated with his second child. Alayne is amused by Saffron's name, and Harrold explains that her father is a spice merchant in [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown), the richest man in the city, who considers his daughter more precious than gold. Alayne jokingly asks Harrold if he plans to name the child "Cinnamon" if it's a girl, or "Cloves" if it's a boy. Harrold is impressed with Alayne's cleverness, and Alayne tells him if they marry, he will have to send Saffron back to her father.(wiki:Saffron.cite_ref-twow_1-3)
