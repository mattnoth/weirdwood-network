## Appearances & Description

Rowan is tall and skinny, too lean and leathery to be called pretty. She has long legs and red-brown hair.(wiki:Rowan.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Rowan accompanies [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) and five other [spearwives](wiki:Spearwife) to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) on a mission to free "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)" (actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)), who is to wed [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow). Mance disguises himself as "Abel", a singer,(wiki:Rowan.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and the six spearwives pretend to be his mother, wife, two daughters, and two sisters, who perform the instruments with him. Abel and the spearwives play at the wedding feast.(wiki:Rowan.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Rowan is the first of the washerwomen to contact [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) about how he [took Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Rowan.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

After several men-at-arms and squires are found dead at Winterfell, Theon disbelieves the spearwives' claims of innocence.(wiki:Rowan.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Rowan.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Rowan explains that [Yellow Dick](wiki:Yellow_Dick) stank and was a pig of a man, but denies responsibility for the murder of [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Little_Walder_Frey).(wiki:Rowan.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) The spearwives enlist the help of a reluctant and fearful Theon in freeing Jeyne.(wiki:Rowan.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Rowan.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) The six spearwives, disguised as maids and accompanied by Theon, walk into Jeyne's room under the pretext of bringing hot water so Jeyne can have a bath. As they fight their way out of the castle, Rowan and the other spearwives are taken down one by one by the [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) men.(wiki:Rowan.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

According to [a letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) received by [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), Ramsay has captured Mance and has him wearing a cloak made from the skins of the six women who accompanied him to Winterfell.(wiki:Rowan.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
