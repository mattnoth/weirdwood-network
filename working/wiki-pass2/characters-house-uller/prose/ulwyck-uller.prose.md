## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Ulwyck is one of the nobles who accompany Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for the wedding of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Ulwyck_Uller.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ulwyck is present along with [his brother](wiki:Harmen_Uller) at the feast when Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) arrives in [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) to deliver the head of Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane). He refuses to drink when [Ricasso](wiki:Ricasso) raises a toast to King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Ulwyck_Uller.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
