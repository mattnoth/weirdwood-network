## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord Cerwyn comes to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) when [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls his banners. He is accompanied by his daughter, [Jonelle](wiki:Jonelle_Cerwyn).(wiki:Medger_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) One of Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton)'s men knifes one of Medger's at the [Smoking Log](wiki:Smoking_Log).(wiki:Medger_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) Lord [Jon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber) objects to being placed behind the [Cerwyns](wiki:House_Cerwyn) or the [Hornwoods](wiki:House_Hornwood) in the order of march.(wiki:Medger_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3)

Medger is placed under the command of Lord Bolton when the northern army splits at the [Twins](wiki:Twins). He is captured at the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:Medger_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Clash of Kings

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), unsuccessfully offers to trade Lord Medger and Ser [Donnel Locke](wiki:Donnel_Locke) for [Tion Frey](wiki:Tion_Frey) in an exchange of prisoners.(wiki:Medger_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) holds captives from the Green Fork at the [Tower of Dread](wiki:Tower_of_Dread) in [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Medger_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), disguised as a servant in the castle, hopes to sneak past the guards and ask Lord Cerwyn for assistance. Medger suffers from a serious wound taken at the battle, however, and never leaves his tower cell. Medger eventually dies of his wounds. Arya sees [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters) take his remains from Harrenhal, with his body in a cloak of silk decorated with the battle-axe of House Cerwyn.(wiki:Medger_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)
