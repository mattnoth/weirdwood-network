## Origins

According to a semi-canon source, Rorge owned a [pot shop](wiki:Pot_shop) or tavern in [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom), that also served as a [rat pit](wiki:Rat_pit) where he would stage rat fights, [dog](wiki:Dog) fights, and [bear](wiki:Bear) cub fights for his profit. At some point, he found the orphan child [Biter](wiki:Biter), and raised him ferally to compete in these fights.(wiki:Rorge.cite_ref-Rssm1400.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Rorge.cite_ref-Rssm1401.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Appearances & Description

Rorge is described as squat, bulky, and hairy, with black hair covering most of his body. He is also described as thick and powerful.(wiki:Rorge.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He is missing his nose, and the nasal bar of the black iron half-helm he comes to wear does not completely conceal the hole.(wiki:Rorge.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Rorge is one of three known criminals imprisoned in the [black cells](wiki:Black_cells) of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Rorge.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Yoren recruits him for the Night's Watch, but considers him so dangerous that he keeps him chained in a [wagon](wiki:Wagon), along with [Jaqen H'ghar](wiki:Jaqen_H'ghar) and [Biter](wiki:Biter), for the journey to the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Rorge.cite_ref-Racok1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

When Yoren's band is [attacked](wiki:Storming_of_the_Gods_Eye_town_holdfast) at the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye_town) by raiders led by Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch), Rorge and his companions find themselves trapped in the wagon in the middle of a fire, but [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), before escaping herself, throws an axe into the wagon so that they could break free and save themselves.(wiki:Rorge.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Rorge and his companions manage to escape and join Ser Amory's raiding party for a time, eventually arriving at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Rorge.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

The three men remain in the garrison holding Harrenhal after [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) departs the castle. They help Arya free the captive [northmen](wiki:North) during the [fall of Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal), though Rorge and Biter do it reluctantly and only because Jaqen requested their help. With Lorch's men slaughtered, Rorge and Biter join the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions).(wiki:Rorge.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Rorge is part of the foraging company who captures [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) near [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool). He immediately threatens Brienne with rape and severely beats Jaime into submission.(wiki:Rorge.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) He later claims the bloodstained surcoat off the corpse of [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey).(wiki:Rorge.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

On the way back to [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Rorge, [Shagwell](wiki:Shagwell), and [Zollo](wiki:Zollo) attempt to rape Brienne. He argues with Zollo over who would rape Brienne first, but Jaime thwarts them. Jaime considers Rorge one of the three worst members of the Brave Companions.(wiki:Rorge.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Rorge is one of the many Brave Companions who remain behind at Harrenhal after several failed foraging runs. He is present when Brienne is thrown into the bear pit and threatens Jaime once more when he comes back to save her, but [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat) orders him to stand down.(wiki:Rorge.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Feast for Crows

After the [capture of Harrenhal](wiki:Capture_of_Harrenhal) and Vargo's death, Rorge went to the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads) with [Shagwell](wiki:Shagwell), [Timeon](wiki:Timeon), [Pyg](wiki:Pyg) and other former [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions). Rorge killed the innkeeper. Then they parted: Shagwell, Timeon, and Pyg went to [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool), while Rorge travelled toward the town of [Saltpans](wiki:Saltpans) with [Biter](wiki:Biter) and other former Brave Companions. (wiki:Rorge.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Along the way Rorge finds and dons the [hound](wiki:Hound) [helm](wiki:Helm) once worn by [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane). He wears the helm during the brutal [raid on Saltpans](wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans), which results in Sandor being blamed for the attack.(wiki:Rorge.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Rorge.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

Rorge encounters [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) at the inn at the crossroads. Brienne goads him by stating, "[Shagwell](wiki:Shagwell) said they [cut your manhood off](wiki:Eunuch) when they took your nose." After Brienne slays Rorge in their duel, she is attacked by the savage Biter and then rescued by [Gendry](wiki:Gendry).(wiki:Rorge.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)

When the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) returns to the crossroads inn, the dog helm is taken from Rorge's body by [Lem Lemoncloak](wiki:Lem), who becomes the new Hound.(wiki:Rorge.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
