## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter

Valena greets Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) and her companions when they arrive at [Ghost Hill](wiki:Ghost_Hill). At the welcoming feast, when her younger sister, [Teora](wiki:Teora_Toland), says [dragons](wiki:Dragon) are coming, Valena scornfully asks if Teora knows that from one of her "little [dreams](wiki:Dreams)."(wiki:Valena_Toland.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Barianne_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Carianne_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
