## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Arnell is sent by [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) as an ambassador to [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon). Lord Mormont considered sending [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), on account of his father [Lord](wiki:Lord) [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly)'s high position in Renly's councils. He sent Arnell because he was considerably more steady than Sam and was kin to the [green-apple Fossoways](wiki:House_Fossoway_of_New_Barrel).(wiki:Arnell.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) It is not known if he reaches his destination before Renly's death, nor what has happened to him since.
