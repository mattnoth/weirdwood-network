## Origins

Anson wrote *[Truth](wiki:Truth_(book))*, in which he expressed the belief that the idea that [dragons](wiki:Dragons) can change sex at need is erroneous. According to Anson, this is a misunderstanding of the metaphor which was used by [Septon](wiki:Septon) [Barth](wiki:Barth), when discussing the [higher mysteries](wiki:Higher_mysteries).(wiki:Anson.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_winterfell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
