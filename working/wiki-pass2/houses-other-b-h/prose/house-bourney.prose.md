## Origins

Lord [Owain Bourney](wiki:Owain_Bourney) was a turncloak during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons); he initially declared for the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) but switched side to the [greens](wiki:Greens) during the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton).
