## Appearances & Description

Bolton guardsmen usually wear fur cloaks over chainmail,(wiki:House_Bolton_guards.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) while their sergeants wear pale pink cloaks decorated with red teardrops.(wiki:House_Bolton_guards.cite_ref-Rasos50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

After learning that Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) plans to leave her with [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat) when he departs [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) kills a tall, thin Bolton guard at one of the castle's postern gates when she escapes with [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) and [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie).(wiki:House_Bolton_guards.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

According to Lady [Donella Hornwood](wiki:Donella_Hornwood), Roose's bastard, [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), is massing men at the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort).(wiki:House_Bolton_guards.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Ramsay later brings six hundred men, whom he calls his father's garrison, to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), where they [betray](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell) the host of [northmen](wiki:Northmen) assembled by Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel).(wiki:House_Bolton_guards.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Storm of Swords

When Lord Bolton hosts Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) at Harrenhal, the castle's Hall of a Hundred Hearths is guarded by a dozen spearmen. Roose tasks [Steelshanks Walton](wiki:Steelshanks_Walton) with escorting Jaime to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:House_Bolton_guards.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)" is later escorted by Walton and his men from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to Lord Bolton at the [Twins](wiki:Twins).(wiki:House_Bolton_guards.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

[Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) brings [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) to the Twins during [the wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) to [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey). Outside the castles, they are stopped by a Bolton sergeant who is more wary than Ser [Donnel Haigh](wiki:Donnel_Haigh).(wiki:House_Bolton_guards.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

The [Bastard's Boys](wiki:Bastard's_Boys) are men-at-arms(wiki:House_Bolton_guards.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) who follow [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow)(wiki:House_Bolton_guards.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) and guard his new wife, "Arya", actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole).(wiki:House_Bolton_guards.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Walton commands Roose's guards while in [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton)(wiki:House_Bolton_guards.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:House_Bolton_guards.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) Bolton spearmen are forced to intervene when [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) clash with [Manderlys](wiki:House_Manderly).(wiki:House_Bolton_guards.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2)
