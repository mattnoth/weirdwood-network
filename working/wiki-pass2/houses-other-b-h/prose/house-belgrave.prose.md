## Origins

Ser [Gyles Belgrave](wiki:Gyles_Belgrave), a [knight](wiki:Knight) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), was accused of treason against the late King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) during the [Hour of the Wolf](wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf). He chose death over [taking the black](wiki:Night's_Watch) and was executed by Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:House_Belgrave.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen) ordered proud [Lord Belgrave](wiki:Lord_Belgrave) to wash the ulcerous feet of beggars.(wiki:House_Belgrave.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
