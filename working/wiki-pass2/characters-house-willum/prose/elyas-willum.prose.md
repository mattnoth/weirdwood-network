## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Elyas is part of [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)'s army encamped beneath the walls of [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge). During the feast he argues with his brother, Josua, over who will be the first to scale the walls of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Elyas_Willum.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

[House Willum](wiki:House_Willum) goes over to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) after Renly's death. Lord Willum and both his sons fight for Stannis at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), but are captured, and later bend the knee to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Elyas_Willum.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
