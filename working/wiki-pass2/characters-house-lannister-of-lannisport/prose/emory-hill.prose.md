## Origins

Emory joined Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister)'s host during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), in support of King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) and the [greens](wiki:Greens).
