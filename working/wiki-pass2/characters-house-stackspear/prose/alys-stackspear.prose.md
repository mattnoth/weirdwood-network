## Origins

According to a semi-canon source, the fifteen-year-old [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold)) deflowered the [maiden](wiki:Maiden) Alys in [244 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), getting her pregnant.
