## Appearances & Description

Shitmouth has grey hair and is grizzled, with a thick beard.(wiki:Shitmouth.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

The man-at-arms is called Shitmouth because of the foul language he uses. He is not as cruel compared to the rest of the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men).(wiki:Shitmouth.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Shitmouth is one of Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane)'s men-at-arms and is part of the group that captures [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), [Gendry](wiki:Gendry), and [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). Arya does not add him to her [prayer kill list](wiki:Arya_Stark) as he displays a lack of cruelty in comparison to his comrades.(wiki:Shitmouth.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Feast for Crows

When Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and his force arrives at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), he encounters Shitmouth after emerging from the gates. Jaime is amused by the drunk man with crumbs in his beard.(wiki:Shitmouth.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

[Rafford](wiki:Rafford) informs Jaime that some time ago [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane), accompanied by [a boy](wiki:Arya_Stark), killed [Polliver](wiki:Polliver), the [Tickler](wiki:Tickler), and a [pimply squire](wiki:Pimply_squire) at the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn). When Jaime asks if they sent men went after Sandor. Shitmouth replies he never liked Polliver and that Sandor is the brother of their commander, Gregor.(wiki:Shitmouth.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Shitmouth declines Ser [Bonifer Hasty](wiki:Bonifer_Hasty)'s offer of land in return for service, as Gregor had promised rich rewards to his followers.(wiki:Shitmouth.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) According to a semi-canon source, Shitmouth joins Jaime's retinue as it heads for the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun).(wiki:Shitmouth.cite_ref-Rawoiafshitmouth.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
