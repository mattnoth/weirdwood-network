## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The House is hostile to the rule of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) and its household is suspected of being members of the [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy).(wiki:House_of_Yherizan.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
When the [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal) escapes from its imprisonment, he makes his new lair in the great black [pyramid of Yherizan](wiki:Pyramid_of_Yherizan). The pyramid is now a smouldering ruin and its former occupants are either dead or in flight.(wiki:House_of_Yherizan.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
