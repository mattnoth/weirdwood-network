## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The shavepates have shaved off their traditional Ghiscari hairstyles, which represents leaving the old Meereen behind and accepting the new Meereen. They are considered traitors by the [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy) who kill them along with [freedmen](wiki:Freedmen) and [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied). Their leader is [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq), who is known as "the Shavepate".(wiki:Shavepates.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
