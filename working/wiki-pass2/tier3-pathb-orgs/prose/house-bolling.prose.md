## Origins

The upper left side of the arms shows the [Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon) or [Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon) colors reversed beneath a bend sinister, an indication that the founder of the house was either a [bastard](wiki:Bastard) or a descendant of a bastard of one of those lines.(wiki:House_Bolling.cite_ref-citadel_1-3)
Ser [Theo Bolling](wiki:Theo_Bolling) was a [landed knight](wiki:Landed_knight) married to Lady [Elinor Costayne](wiki:Elinor_Costayne). During the reign of King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), Ser Theo fought for King Maegar in his [campaign](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising) against the [Poor Fellows](wiki:Poor_Fellows). In [47 AC](wiki:47_AC), Maegor had Ser Theo arrested, charged with treason, and executed on the same day, allowing Maegor to marry Elinor. Ser Theo's eldest sons were fostered at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) and [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden), while the youngest was given over to a [wet nurse](wiki:Wet_nurse).(wiki:House_Bolling.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
