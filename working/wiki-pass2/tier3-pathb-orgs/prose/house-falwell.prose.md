## Origins

Lord [Rupert Falwell](wiki:Rupert_Falwell), the Fighting Fool, participated in the [battle at the Great Fork](wiki:Battle_at_the_Great_Fork) in [42 AC](wiki:42_AC) during the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising). He slew two members of [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen)'s [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) before being cut down by Lord [Mooton](wiki:House_Mooton) of [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool).(wiki:House_Falwell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Maegor I later used [Balerion](wiki:Balerion) to set [aflame](wiki:Dragonflame) the seat of the Falwells.(wiki:House_Falwell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
During the misrule of Lord [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister), Lords Falwell and [Jast](wiki:House_Jast) became embroiled in a private quarrel. Rather than seek a ruling from [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), the lords decided to settle the matter with a melee. Nine men were killed, twenty-seven maimed and wounded, and still the quarrel raged on.(wiki:House_Falwell.cite_ref-sample_4-0)
