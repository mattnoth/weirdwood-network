## Organization

The Pureborn rule the city of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) from the [Hall of a Thousand Thrones](wiki:Hall_of_a_Thousand_Thrones). They command the [Civic Guard](wiki:Civic_Guard), which includes [camelry](wiki:Camel), and a fleet of ornate galleys which control the [Jade Gates](wiki:Jade_Gates).(wiki:Pureborn.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Pureborn.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
To receive an audience with the Pureborn, a petitioner should make a traditional sacrifice in the [Temple of Memory](wiki:Temple_of_Memory), offer a traditional bribe to the [Keeper of the Long List](wiki:Keeper_of_the_Long_List), and send a traditional persimmon to the [Opener of the Door](wiki:Opener_of_the_Door). If all goes well, the petitioner will be sent a pair of blue slippers, signifying the granting of an audience in the Hall of a Thousand Thrones.(wiki:Pureborn.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
The Pureborn are courteous and respond to flattery.(wiki:Pureborn.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Known Pureborn include [Wendello Qar Deeth](wiki:Wendello_Qar_Deeth), [Egon Emeros](wiki:Egon_Emeros), and [Mathos Mallarawan](wiki:Mathos_Mallarawan). The calculating Pureborn are notorious for offering [poisoned wine](wiki:Poison) to those they consider dangerous, and their avarice is equally well-known.(wiki:Pureborn.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

## Origins

The Pureborn are descendants of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth)'s ancient [kings](wiki:King_of_Qarth) and queens.(wiki:Pureborn.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) When the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) destroyed other [Qaathi](wiki:Qaathi) cities in the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood), the Qartheen were forced to look to the sea. The Pureborn built a fleet to control the [Jade Gates](wiki:Jade_Gates), allowing their city to prosper from trade between the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea) to the west and the [Jade Sea](wiki:Jade_Sea) to the east.(wiki:Pureborn.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

While residing in [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos)'s palace in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) seeks an audience with the Pureborn to plead for their ships and soldiers. Despite her bribes, the Pureborn refuse her, as Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) predicted.(wiki:Pureborn.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) Their rejection is adorned with great courtesy.(wiki:Pureborn.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

In Meereen, Xaro tells Daenerys that, following the destruction of the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying), he went to the [Hall of a Thousand Thrones](wiki:Hall_of_a_Thousand_Thrones) to beg the Pureborn for her life. When Xaro told them that Daenerys was no more than a child, [Egon Emeros](wiki:Egon_Emeros) replied she was a foolish child, mad and heedless and too dangerous to live.(wiki:Pureborn.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
