## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Ghazdor zo Ahlaq](wiki:Ghazdor_zo_Ahlaq), a Yunkish noble and slaver, is present at the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor).(wiki:House_of_Ahlaq.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) After the death of [Yurkhaz zo Yunzak](wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak) in the [fighting pits of Meereen](wiki:Fighting_pits_of_Meereen), the Yunkai'i start to rotate the duty of supreme commander amongst them. Ghazdor is among the Yunkish nobles who take part in this rotation.(wiki:House_of_Ahlaq.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
