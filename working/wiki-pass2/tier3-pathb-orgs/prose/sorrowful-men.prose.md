## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

In [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) a member of the Sorrowful Men makes an attempt on [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s life using a [manticore](wiki:Manticore), only to be thwarted by [Arstan Whitebeard](wiki:Barristan_Selmy).(wiki:Sorrowful_Men.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Aboard the [Balerion](wiki:Balerion_(ship)) [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) dwells upon her enemies and remembers that the [warlock](wiki:Warlock) [Pyat Pree](wiki:Pyat_Pree) had sent a Sorrowful Man after her to avenge the [Undying](wiki:Undying_Ones) she had burned in their [House of Dust](wiki:House_of_the_Undying). She remembers that it is said that Sorrowful Men never failed to kill.(wiki:Sorrowful_Men.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
