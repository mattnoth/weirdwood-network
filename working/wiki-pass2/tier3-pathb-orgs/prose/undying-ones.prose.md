## Appearances & Description

The Undying Ones present themselves as beautiful wizards at the prime of their lives and power, with some wearing sumptuous robes, elaborate armor, or tall pointed hats. They include gown-clad women.(wiki:Undying_Ones.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) In actuality, however, the Undying Ones are ancient and withered. Through their drinking of the [shade of the evening](wiki:Shade_of_the_evening), their flesh, nails, and even the whites of their eyes have turned shades of blue and violet-blue.(wiki:Undying_Ones.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Pyat Pree](wiki:Pyat_Pree), one of the [warlocks](wiki:Warlocks) of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), informs [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) that he will ask the Undying Ones to grant her an audience.(wiki:Undying_Ones.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) After being rejected by the [Pureborn](wiki:Pureborn),(wiki:Undying_Ones.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Daenerys decides to meet the Undying Ones.(wiki:Undying_Ones.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
In a great hall within the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying), Daenerys finds a splendor of wizards who offer her knowledge and magic weapons. [Drogon](wiki:Drogon), one of Daenerys's [dragons](wiki:Dragon), points her to another door, however, so she abandons these wizards who call on her with sweet voices.(wiki:Undying_Ones.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
In a gloomy chamber, Daenerys finds a blue and rotting human heart floating above a stone table, and in the darkness she perceives the true Undying Ones. Although they do not move and do not breathe, Daenerys hears them whisper. The Undying Ones show Daenerys a number of [visions](wiki:Visions) of past and future events, but as they do so they attack her and attempt to drain her life. Drogon rescues Daenerys, however, burning the Undying Ones with [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame) and ripping apart the dark heart. Pyat Pree tries to attack Daenerys when she escapes from the building, but he is stopped by [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) and [Rakharo](wiki:Rakharo).(wiki:Undying_Ones.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

According to [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos), the Undying are all dead.(wiki:Undying_Ones.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
