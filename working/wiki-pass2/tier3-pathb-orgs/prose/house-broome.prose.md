## Origins

House Broome was among the pious noble houses who openly sided with the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising) against King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen). Eventually Maegor the Cruel used his [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [Balerion](wiki:Balerion) to burn out their castle, along with the castles of other houses in the westerlands such as the [Falwells](wiki:House_Falwell), [Lorches](wiki:House_Lorch), and [Doggetts](wiki:House_Doggett).(wiki:House_Broome.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
[Prentys Tully](wiki:Prentys_Tully), who was [Lord of Riverrun](wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun) during the uprising, was married to [Lucinda Broome](wiki:Lucinda_Broome), who was noted for her piety.(wiki:House_Broome.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
