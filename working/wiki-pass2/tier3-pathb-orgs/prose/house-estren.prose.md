## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord [Regenard Estren](wiki:Regenard_Estren) is part of Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s army besieging [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) and is captured at the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:House_Estren.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Estren spends the rest of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) captive at the [Twins](wiki:Twins),(wiki:House_Estren.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) suggesting he had been captured by a [Frey](wiki:House_Frey).

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Regenard is released following the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:House_Estren.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
