## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The Tourmaline Brotherhood gives [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) a crown wrought in the shape of a three-headed [dragon](wiki:Dragon), the symbol of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen). The coils are made of yellow gold, the wings are made of silver, and the heads consist of jade, ivory, and onyx.(wiki:Tourmaline_Brotherhood.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
After the destruction of the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying), the Brotherhood calls openly for Daenerys's expulsion from [Qarth](wiki:Qarth).(wiki:Tourmaline_Brotherhood.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After the [siege of Meereen](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen), Daenerys wears the crown she received from the Tourmaline Brotherhood.(wiki:Tourmaline_Brotherhood.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
