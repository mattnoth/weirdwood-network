## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) recalls there are some Liddles living in the [high places](wiki:Northern_mountains) during his journey to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) with [Hodor](wiki:Hodor), [Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed), and [Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed). They meet a man from the mountain clans whom Bran identifies as a Liddle because of the pinecone-shaped gold and bronze clasp fastening his cloak. [The Liddle](wiki:The_Liddle) shares oatcakes, blood sausage, and [ale](wiki:Ale), without asking their names nor giving his.(wiki:House_Liddle.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sends [ravens](wiki:Raven) to most of the noble houses from the north asking for help to fight the [wildlings](wiki:Free_folk) at [Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black), including the Liddles.(wiki:House_Liddle.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Morgan Liddle](wiki:Morgan_Liddle) joins the army of King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) and participates in the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:House_Liddle.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) He later apologizes to [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) for cursing at her during the battle.(wiki:House_Liddle.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
