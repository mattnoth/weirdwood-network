## Origins

In some historical accounts, [Qhored I Hoare](wiki:Qhored_I_Hoare), one of the greatest [High King of the Iron Islands](wiki:High_King_of_the_Iron_Islands), is said to have been a Blacktyde.(wiki:House_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
High King [Joron I Blacktyde](wiki:Joron_I_Blacktyde) captured [Gyles II Gardener](wiki:Gyles_II_Gardener), [King of the Reach](wiki:King_of_the_Reach), when their fleets clashed off the [Misty Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands). After torturing the king to death, Joron cut Gyles's corpse to pieces for bait. He also raided the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor), carrying off many of its women.(wiki:House_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
After the death of [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands) at [Faircastle](wiki:Faircastle), the Blacktydes and the [Harlaws](wiki:House_Harlaw) joined the Red Kraken's cousins to raise up [Rodrik Greyjoy](wiki:Rodrik_Greyjoy), a younger [salt son](wiki:Salt_son) of Dalton, as the Red Kraken's successor. The resulting bloody [struggle for power in the Iron Islands](wiki:Struggle_for_power_in_the_Iron_Islands) lasted for half a year.(wiki:House_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_the_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
[Lord Blacktyde](wiki:Lord_Blacktyde) was killed in battle during [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion).(wiki:House_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) After the war, his son [Baelor](wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde) was taken as a hostage for eight years in the city of [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) in the [Reach](wiki:Reach).(wiki:House_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Upon his return, Baelor had converted to the mainland's [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:House_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
Also after the rebellion, the young [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley) was sent away to be fostered with the Blacktydes. Tris had been fostered at [Pyke](wiki:Pyke) with the [Greyjoys](wiki:House_Greyjoy), but was sent away by Lord [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) when Maester [Qalen](wiki:Qalen) told him the boy intended to marry Balon's daughter, [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy). At Blacktyde, Maester [Joseran](wiki:Joseran) refused to send letters from Tris to Asha.(wiki:House_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

War breaks out on mainland [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) after the death of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), and [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), calls the banners to his seat of [Pyke](wiki:Pyke). The recently-crowned [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), assigns [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) to travel to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) and seek an alliance with his father, Balon, against the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:House_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) In the [Great Hall of Pyke](wiki:Great_Keep_of_Pyke), Theon notices his father has gathered hundreds of his captains, with the Blacktydes from [Blacktyde](wiki:Blacktyde) amongst them.(wiki:House_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
During the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Balon declares himself first [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands)(wiki:House_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) and then [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North).(wiki:House_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After Balon falls to his death at Pyke, his youngest brother [Aeron](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) demands a [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot) be held to elect a new king.(wiki:House_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) [Blind Beron Blacktyde](wiki:Beron_Blacktyde) is one of the [priests of the Drowned God](wiki:Priests_of_the_Drowned_God) who add their voices to the Damphair's call.(wiki:House_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2)
Visiting Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw) at [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers), Lord [Baelor Blacktyde](wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde) fears that [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), who has already claimed the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair), will descend upon the other candidates and kill them all, just like [Urron Greyiron](wiki:Urron_Greyiron) had done at the last kingsmoot thousands of years before.(wiki:House_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-3)
[Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) sees Baelor's ship, [Nightflyer](wiki:Nightflyer), when he arrives at [Nagga's Cradle](wiki:Nagga's_Cradle) with the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet). Remembering that [his father](wiki:Lord_Blacktyde) had been killed in [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion), Baelor is cautious about supporting the bellicose Victarion.(wiki:House_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) Baelor decides to follow [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) instead after she announces that if she were to win, she would bring about the conflict's ending.(wiki:House_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Against Aeron's intentions, his hated brother Euron is chosen during the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:House_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)
After Euron is crowned, he commands the Iron Fleet to close Nagga's Cradle, blocking all ships from leaving. Baelor's Nightflyer is seized and Lord Blacktyde is delivered to Euron in chains for refusing to accept him as king. Euron orders his men to kill Baelor and cut him in seven pieces in ironic tribute to his worship of the [seven mainland gods](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:House_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), Euron wears Baelor's sable cloak.(wiki:House_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)
