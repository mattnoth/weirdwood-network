## Origins

Because the Harltons were loyal to [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Lord Harlton was executed for treason by Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully). The late Lord Harlton's daughter, [Marianne](wiki:Marianne_Harlton), was then raised by his heir and younger brother, the childless [Arwood Harlton](wiki:Arwood_Harlton). Lord Arwood became a friend of Lord [Raynald Sarwyck](wiki:Raynald_Sarwyck).(wiki:House_Harlton.cite_ref-VG_1-1)
