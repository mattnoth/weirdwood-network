## Origins

According to legend the house was founded by [Rose of Red Lake](wiki:Rose_of_Red_Lake), a daughter of [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand). It is rumored that some female members of House Crane are able to [transform](wiki:Shapechanger) into [cranes](wiki:Crane).(wiki:House_Crane.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The Crane castle of [Red Lake](wiki:Red_Lake) is located at a lake of the same name.(wiki:House_Crane.cite_ref-Rawoiafred_lake.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Red Lake and the Cranes joined the [Kingdom of the Reach](wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Reach) through pacts of friendship and mutual defense with King [Garth III Gardener](wiki:Garth_III_Gardener).(wiki:House_Crane.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_the_gardener_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) [Lancel IV Lannister](wiki:Lancel_IV_Lannister), [King of the Rock](wiki:King_of_the_Rock), and Ser [Wilbert Osgrey](wiki:Wilbert_Osgrey), [Marshall of the Northmarch](wiki:Marshall_of_the_Northmarch), slew each other at Red Lake when Lancel invaded the Reach.(wiki:House_Crane.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:House_Crane.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
[Ellard Crane](wiki:Ellard_Crane) was a young [squire](wiki:Squire) in the court of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen). He was a brief companion of Princess [Daella Targaryen](wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)), but Ellard fell out with the princess when he tried to kiss her.(wiki:House_Crane.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The banners of House Crane are seen beneath the walls of [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge) in [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)'s encampment.(wiki:House_Crane.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Ser [Parmen Crane](wiki:Parmen_Crane), known as Parmen the Purple, is a member of Renly's [Rainbow Guard](wiki:Rainbow_Guard).(wiki:House_Crane.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) After Renly's [death at Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Parmen joins [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s cause. His new king sends him to Bitterbridge with Ser [Erren Florent](wiki:Erren_Florent) to retrieve Renly's foot soldiers.(wiki:House_Crane.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
