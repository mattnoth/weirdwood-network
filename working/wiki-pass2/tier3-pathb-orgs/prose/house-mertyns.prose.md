## Origins

During the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), Lord Mertyns and his entire household were [poisoned](wiki:Poison) by means of a cask of [Dornish wine](wiki:Wine) by [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) assassins.(wiki:House_Mertyns.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Lord [Jon Mertyns](wiki:Jon_Mertyns) traveled to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for the [wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) of Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) to Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon), where he spoke to the young King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and approved of his good-humored nature.(wiki:House_Mertyns.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Ser [Michael Mertyns](wiki:Michael_Mertyns), better known as the White Owl, was a member of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:House_Mertyns.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Among the gathered knights at the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) is one with the horned owl of House Mertyns.(wiki:House_Mertyns.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

The sons and grandsons of Lady [Mary Mertyns](wiki:Mary_Mertyns) initially support [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), with a grandson wounded in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). Since the Mertyns men are away, [Mistwood](wiki:Mistwood) falls to the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) during their [invasion of the stormlands](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company). [Young John Mudd](wiki:Young_John_Mudd) and [Chain](wiki:Chain) keep Mary under house arrest and host [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) for a night.(wiki:House_Mertyns.cite_ref-Ar2_7-0)
