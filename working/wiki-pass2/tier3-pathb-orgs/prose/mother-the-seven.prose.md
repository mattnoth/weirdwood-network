## Culture

The Mother represents motherhood and nurturing. She is prayed to for fertility or compassion,(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and is depicted as smiling with love, embodying the concept of mercy.(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Like the other aspects of the Seven, there is an holy day dedicated to the Mother.(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-4)
It is also said that the Mother could be fiercer than the [Warrior](wiki:Warrior_(the_Seven)) when her children were in danger.(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
A passage in [The Seven-Pointed Star](wiki:The_Seven-Pointed_Star) says that the [Maiden](wiki:Maiden_(the_Seven)) brought [Hugor of the Hill](wiki:Hugor_of_the_Hill) a bride, so the Mother made her fertile.(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Weddings](wiki:Wedding) are conducted standing between the altars of the [Father](wiki:Father_(the_Seven)) and the Mother.(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
The senior member at [motherhouses](wiki:Motherhouse) is refered to as "[Mother](wiki:Mother)".(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
The [Teats](wiki:Teats), a pair of grassy hills in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), were once named the Mother's Teats.(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
The Mother is represented in the [coat-of-arms](wiki:Heraldry) of [House Graceford](wiki:House_Graceford) of [Holyhall](wiki:Holyhall).(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-9)
Tradional words recited during an adoubement include: "In the name of the Mother I charge you to defend the young and innocent".(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Origins

Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) remained devoted to the Mother all her life, even after the deaths of some of her children.(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen) was found collapsed before the altar of the Mother after a fasting for forty one days.(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) considered meeting [Egg](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) as a sign from the Mother telling him to repay his debts to his late mentor, Ser [Arlan of Pennytree](wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree), by taking in and trusting the boy the same way Ser Arlan did with him.(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)
Queen [Aelinor Penrose](wiki:Aelinor_Penrose) was said to pray daily to the Mother to bless her with a child.(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) burns the wooden statues of the Seven from the sept at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) as a sacrifice to [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor). A longsword is thrust through the heart of the Mother's statue, and during the ceremony [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) pulls it free to reveal [Lightbringer](wiki:Lightbringer).(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
[Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) prays in a nameless village's sept. The sept is modest and has no statues of the Seven, only rough charcoal drawings to represent them. A crack runs down through the Mother's left eye. It makes her look as if she were crying. Catelyn sees [her own mother](wiki:Minisa_Whent)'s face in the face of the Mother, as well as her sister [Lysa](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) and [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister).(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Upon entering the sept of [Maegor's Holdfast](wiki:Red_Keep), on the eve of the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) sees that the Mother's altar is swimming in candlelight. Sansa visits each of the Seven in turn, lighting a candle at each altar, then sings the Mother's Hymn with the people in the sept.(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) She silently prays to the Mother for [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane):
'He is no [true knight](wiki:True_knight) but he saved me all the same. Save him if you can, and gentle the rage inside him.(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2)
Sansa Stark sings the Mother's Hymn again that night to [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) when he [comes to her room](wiki:UnKiss).(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After being taken captive by the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions), [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) are taken to [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat) who is sacking a small [sept](wiki:Sept) with some of his men. The [sellswords](wiki:Sellsword) have dragged the sept's carved wooden gods outside. Jaime sees [Zollo](wiki:Zollo) sitting on the Mother's chest prying out her chalcedony eyes with the point of his knife.(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
[Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) sings [The Song of the Seven](wiki:The_Song_of_the_Seven), which includes a verse for the Mother, as a lullabye to [Gilly](wiki:Gilly)'s [son](wiki:Monster).(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Rasos46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister) tells [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) that the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon) told him that the Mother spared him for some holy purpose, so he might atone for his sins. Cersei wonders how he intends to atone for her.(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) prays to the Mother to show him mercy should he die in the [upcoming battle](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen).(wiki:Mother_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Bbarristan_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Cbarristan_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
