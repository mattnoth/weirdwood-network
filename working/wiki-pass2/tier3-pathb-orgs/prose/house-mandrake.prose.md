## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser [Marq Mandrake](wiki:Marq_Mandrake), like the rest of the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company), swears his allegiance to Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(Young_Griff)) before they [invade Westeros](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company).(wiki:House_Mandrake.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Marq may not be a genuine Mandrake, as a man in the [free companies](wiki:Free_companies) can call himself whatever he chooses.(wiki:House_Mandrake.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
