## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

In [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), [Garin](wiki:Garin_(orphan)) explains the history of the [orphans of the Greenblood](wiki:Orphans_of_the_Greenblood) to Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon), including their devotion to [Mother Rhoyne](wiki:Mother_Rhoyne). When Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) says that he had heard the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar) worshipped a turtle god, Garin explains that the Old Man of the River is a lesser god, the child of Mother Rhoyne.(wiki:Old_Man_of_the_River.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When the [Shy Maid](wiki:Shy_Maid) sails down the Rhoyne at [Ny Sar](wiki:Ny_Sar), they see a [huge horned turtle](wiki:Old_Men_of_the_River), which [Yandry](wiki:Yandry) claims is the Old Man of the River himself.(wiki:Old_Man_of_the_River.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
