## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser [Manfryd Yew](wiki:Manfryd_Yew) and Ser [Raynard Ruttiger](wiki:Raynard_Ruttiger) are entrusted by Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister) to see no boats escape downstream during the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun). [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) escapes by swimming from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) on a moonless night. Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) thinks that if Ruttiger, Yew or their men heard a splash, they would have believed it was a turtle or a trout.
