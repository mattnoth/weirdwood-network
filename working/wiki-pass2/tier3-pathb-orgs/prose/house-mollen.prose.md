## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Hallis Mollen](wiki:Hallis_Mollen) is made the new [captain of the guards](wiki:House_Stark_guards) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) after the departure of [Jory Cassel](wiki:Jory_Cassel). Hallis guards [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) during the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:House_Mollen.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Hallis travels south with Catelyn to meet [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon). Catelyn sends Hallis north with the bones of her dead husband, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark).(wiki:House_Mollen.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) and her companions see the corpses of the perpetrators of the [raid on Saltpans](wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans) hanged from trees. Among the dead is a man with a pine tree badge sewn on his breast, presumably a Mollen man-at-arms who became a [broken man](wiki:Broken_men) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:House_Mollen.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
