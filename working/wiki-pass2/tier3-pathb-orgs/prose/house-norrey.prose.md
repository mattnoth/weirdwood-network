## Origins

The Norreys have historically been friendly with the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:House_Norrey.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) It is suggested that the [Night's King](wiki:Night's_King) was a Norrey, among the many possibilities.(wiki:House_Norrey.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Lady [Arra Norrey](wiki:Arra_Norrey) was the first wife of Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark).(wiki:House_Norrey.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The Norreys aided Cregan in hunting down the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) raiders of [Sylas the Grim](wiki:Sylas_the_Grim).(wiki:House_Norrey.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) recalls there are Norreys living in the [high places](wiki:Northern_mountains) during his trip to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) with [Hodor](wiki:Hodor), [Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed), and [Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed).(wiki:House_Norrey.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
While going to the [Twins](wiki:Twins) to attend Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully)'s [wedding](wiki:Wedding), Norrey men are caught on the wrong side of the [Trident](wiki:Trident). They suffer heavy losses when attacked by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) in the [fighting at the fords of the Trident](wiki:Fighting_at_the_fords_of_the_Trident).(wiki:House_Norrey.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) During the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) sees [Owen Norrey](wiki:Owen_Norrey) killed by crossbow bolts.(wiki:House_Norrey.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sends [ravens](wiki:Raven) to most noble houses from the north asking for help to fight [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) at [Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black), including the Norreys.(wiki:House_Norrey.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) gains the Norreys for his cause.(wiki:House_Norrey.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Lord [Brandon Norrey](wiki:Brandon_Norrey) travels to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) and meets with [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow),(wiki:House_Norrey.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) while [Brandon the Younger](wiki:Brandon_Norrey_(son_of_Brandon)) is part of Stannis's [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell).(wiki:House_Norrey.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)
