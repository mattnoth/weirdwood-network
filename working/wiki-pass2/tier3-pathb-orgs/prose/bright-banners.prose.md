## Origins

Shortly after the doom of [Valyria](wiki:Valyria) the Bright Banners and [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons) were hired by the [Qohorik](wiki:Qohor) to defend against Khal [Temmo](wiki:Temmo)'s khalasar. The [Battle of Qohor](wiki:Battle_of_Qohor) ensued, and in the face of hopeless odds both companies fled.(wiki:Bright_Banners.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
