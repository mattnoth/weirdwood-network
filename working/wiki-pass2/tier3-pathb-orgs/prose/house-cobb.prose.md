## Origins

When Lady [Samantha Tarly](wiki:Samantha_Tarly) famously wrote a letter suggesting potential brides for King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), she included [maidens](wiki:Maiden) from House Cobb, among other houses from the [Reach](wiki:Reach).(wiki:House_Cobb.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Ser [Edmund Warrick](wiki:Edmund_Warrick), Ser [Dennis Whitfield](wiki:Dennis_Whitfield), and Ser [Agramore Cobb](wiki:Agramore_Cobb) were appointed to Aegon's [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) to replace Ser [Marston Waters](wiki:Marston_Waters), Ser [Mervyn Flowers](wiki:Mervyn_Flowers), and Ser [Amaury Peake](wiki:Amaury_Peake).(wiki:House_Cobb.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
