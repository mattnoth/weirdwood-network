## Origins

Jeyne was brought to court by her mother, [Falena Stokeworth](wiki:Falena_Stokeworth), in [178 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), when she was fourteen. King [Aegon the Unworthy](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen) made Lord [Lucas Lothston](wiki:Lucas_Lothston_(husband_of_Falena)) of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) his new [Hand](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), and it was said (but never proved) that he enjoyed mother and daughter together in the same bed. Aegon soon gave Jeyne a [pox](wiki:Pox) he had caught from the [whores](wiki:Whores) he had seen after Lady [Bethany Bracken](wiki:Bethany_Bracken)'s execution, and the Lothstons were then all sent from court.(wiki:Jeyne_Lothston.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
