## Origins

Lord Robert arranged the betrothal of his daughter [Ellyn](wiki:Ellyn_Reyne) to Lord [Gerold Lannister](wiki:Gerold_Lannister)'s son and heir, [Tywald](wiki:Tywald_Lannister). Tywald served Robert as a squire.(wiki:Robert_Reyne.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

During the [Peake Uprising](wiki:Peake_Uprising) both Robert and Tywald were killed during the [Storming of Starpike](wiki:Storming_of_Starpike).(wiki:Robert_Reyne.cite_ref-sample_2-0) Upon Robert's death, his son [Roger](wiki:Roger_Reyne) became the new [Lord of Castamere](wiki:Lord_of_Castamere).(wiki:Robert_Reyne.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) In the aftermath of Starpike, the vengeful Roger executed seven captive [Peakes](wiki:House_Peake) before Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) prevented him from harming others.(wiki:Robert_Reyne.cite_ref-sample_2-1)
