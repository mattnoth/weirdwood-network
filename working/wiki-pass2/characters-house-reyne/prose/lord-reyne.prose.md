## Origins

Lord Reyne declared for the [greens](wiki:Greens) during the Dance of the Dragons. He was part of the westermen host led by Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister) then by Ser [Adrian Tarbeck](wiki:Adrian_Tarbeck) and Lord [Humfrey Lefford](wiki:Humfrey_Lefford).
