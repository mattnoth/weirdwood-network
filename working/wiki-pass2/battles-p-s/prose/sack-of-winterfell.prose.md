## Origins

[House Greyjoy](wiki:House_Greyjoy) invades the [north](wiki:North) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), leading Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) to take Winterfell's garrison to assist in the defense of [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Battle_of_Torrhen's_Square). Since Rodrik is away, [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) is able to [capture lightly-defended Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) by stealth. While Rodrik is gathering an army to retake Winterfell, Theon allows a previously-imprisoned [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) serving man, "[Reek](wiki:Reek)", to leave Winterfell in search of aid when he promises to find a few hundred reinforcements for Theon.(wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

When Rodrik's scouts approach Winterfell, Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin) warns Theon his [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) will not hold the castle against an assault. Theon gathers the ironmen to the yard and offers them the choice of departing or remaining with him to hold the castle. [Urzen](wiki:Urzen), [Stygg](wiki:Stygg), and all ten men previously brought by [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) are among those who abandon Theon's cause. He is left with only seventeen followers, including his squire [Wex Pyke](wiki:Wex_Pyke), [Black Lorren](wiki:Black_Lorren), [Kenned the Whale](wiki:Kenned_the_Whale), [Kromm](wiki:Kromm), [Red Rolfe](wiki:Red_Rolfe), [Ulf the Ill](wiki:Ulf_the_Ill), [Werlag](wiki:Werlag), and [Tymor](wiki:Tymor) and his two brothers.(wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Nobles who remain with Theon include [Harrag Sharp](wiki:Harrag_Sharp),(wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) four [Harlaws](wiki:House_Harlaw) (possibly including [Dykk](wiki:Dykk_Harlaw) and [Gevin](wiki:Gevin_Harlaw)), and two [Botleys](wiki:House_Botley).(wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

When the ironborn-held Winterfell is besieged by Rodrik's [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) army of near two thousand, Luwin tries to persuade Theon to surrender and join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). They are interrupted by the arrival of [House Bolton's garrison](wiki:House_Bolton_guards) from the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort), however, which betrays Rodrik's army and routs it in a [battle outside the gates of Winterfell](wiki:Battle_outside_the_gates_of_Winterfell) and in the [winter town](wiki:Winter_town). The Boltons lose only twenty or thirty men from their force of near six hundred.(wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

## Aftermath

At [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), [Lothar Frey](wiki:Lothar_Frey) tells [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), the [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), of a letter the Frey wards wrote to their grandfather, Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey). The youths claim that Theon's ironmen had burnt Winterfell and put its men to the sword, but that Ramsay had led the surviving women and children to safety at the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort).(wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) At the [Twins](wiki:Twins) prior to the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) tells Robb that Ramsay has captured Theon and indicates Ramsay is torturing his captive.(wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Theon's sister, [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), visits Winterfell before returning to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands). Finding only [wolves](wiki:Wolves) eating the corpses and unable to identify Theon's body, Asha thinks the northmen were fighting each other.(wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

[Wex Pyke](wiki:Wex_Pyke), Theon's mute squire, eventually falls into the custody of [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly). By asking yes and no questions, Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) and [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover) learn that Ramsay was responsible for the destruction instead of Theon, and that Ramsay took Theon and the women of Winterfell back to the Dreadfort.(wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4) They also learn from Wex that Osha took Rickon to [Skagos](wiki:Skagos),(wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rawoiafskagos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) so Wyman tasks Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) with retrieving Rickon from that isle.(wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5)

After being tortured by Ramsay at the Dreadfort,(wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Theon attends [Ramsay's wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark) to "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)" at Winterfell. Most of the towers and keeps are roofless, and some have collapsed. Roose has squatters build stout new gates and a new roof for the Great Hall, after which he has the squatters hanged. The kitchens, barracks keep, and stables are also rebuilt, although the quality is questionable.(wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
