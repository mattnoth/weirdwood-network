## Origins

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)) fought for the [greens](wiki:Greens) and supported his older brother, King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen). Daeron the Daring died at the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton) in [130 AC](wiki:130_AC), however. The manner of his death is disputed, with sources claiming he may have been killed by [Black Trombo](wiki:Black_Trombo)'s morningstar, or killed by a [man-at-arms](wiki:Man-at-arms) with a sword, or burned to death after his tent caught fire.(wiki:Pretenders_claiming_to_be_Prince_Daeron_the_Daring.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Aegon II died in the aftermath of the [Battle of the Kingsroad](wiki:Battle_of_the_Kingsroad), and he was succeeded by King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen).(wiki:Pretenders_claiming_to_be_Prince_Daeron_the_Daring.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
