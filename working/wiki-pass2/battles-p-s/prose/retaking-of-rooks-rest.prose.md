## Origins

[Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aemon)) and her [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Meleys](wiki:Meleys), were killed by King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) atop [Sunfyre](wiki:Sunfyre) and Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) atop [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) during the first [battle at Rook's Rest](wiki:Battle_at_Rook's_Rest). Aemond and Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) took [Rook's Rest](wiki:Rook's_Rest), beheading [Lord Staunton](wiki:Lord_Staunton) and slaying his garrison. Wounded and unable to fly away, Sunfyre remained near the castle. The garrison left by Criston fed the dragon calves and sheep.(wiki:Retaking_of_Rook's_Rest.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

Lord [Walys Mooton](wiki:Walys_Mooton) led a hundred knights from [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) to [Crackclaw Point](wiki:Crackclaw_Point), where they joined a host of [Brunes](wiki:House_Brune), [Celtigars](wiki:House_Celtigar), and [Crabbs](wiki:House_Crabb). The [blacks](wiki:Blacks) surprised the garrison of greens left behind at Rook's Rest by Criston Cole. After retaking the castle, Walys and his bravest men slew Sunfyre's guards and then tried to end the wounded dragon while he slept. Sunfyre killed threescore men, including Lord Mooton, however, and forced the rest to flee.(wiki:Retaking_of_Rook's_Rest.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Aftermath

A fortnight later Lord [Manfryd Mooton](wiki:Manfryd_Mooton) found the charred body of his brother, Walys, but Sunfyre had mysteriously disappeared.(wiki:Retaking_of_Rook's_Rest.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
