## Identity

The seizure of westerlands gold mines was a northern military action during the War of the Five Kings in 299 AC, in which Lord Greatjon Umber captured the gold mines at Castamere, Nunn's Deep, and the Pendric Hills to strike at House Lannister's wealth (wiki:Seizure_of_westerlands_gold_mines.cite_ref-Racok39).

## Allegiances

Part of the `FIGHTS_IN` War of the Five Kings. Fought between the Iron Throne/House Lannister and the Kingdom of the North and the Trident (House Umber) (wiki:Seizure_of_westerlands_gold_mines).

## Narrative Arc

After Robb Stark's victory at Oxcross, he divided his forces across the westerlands. Lord Greatjon Umber was sent to capture several gold mines to hurt House Lannister's wealth. Castamere, Nunn's Deep, and the Pendric Hills were seized and held until Robb's withdrawal from the westerlands (wiki:Seizure_of_westerlands_gold_mines.cite_ref-Racok39)(wiki:Seizure_of_westerlands_gold_mines.cite_ref-Rasos19). Following the Red Wedding, Tywin Lannister granted Castamere to Ser Rolph Spicer (wiki:Seizure_of_westerlands_gold_mines.cite_ref-Rasos72).
