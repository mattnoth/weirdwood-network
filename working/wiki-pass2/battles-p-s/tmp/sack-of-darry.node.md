## Identity

The sack of Darry was a battle during the War of the Five Kings in 299 AC, in which Ser Gregor Clegane retook Castle Darry and killed its lord, the eight-year-old Lyman Darry — the last male heir of House Darry (wiki:Sack_of_Darry.cite_ref-Racok7).

## Allegiances

Part of the `FIGHTS_IN` War of the Five Kings. Fought between Iron Throne/House Lannister (House Clegane) and Kingdom of the North and the Trident/House Darry (wiki:Sack_of_Darry).

## Narrative Arc

A fortnight after House Darry recaptured their castle following the Battle of the Camps, Ser Gregor Clegane descended on Darry, taking it quickly and putting all defenders to the sword, including the child Lord Lyman Darry. Gregor left a Lannister garrison behind (wiki:Sack_of_Darry.cite_ref-Racok7)(wiki:Sack_of_Darry.cite_ref-Racok64). Gregor had previously killed Ser Raymun Darry at the Battle at the Mummer's Ford, making him directly responsible for the extinction of the male line of House Darry (wiki:Sack_of_Darry.cite_ref-Racok7). Later, Ser Helman Tallhart received orders from Lord Roose Bolton to besiege Darry, put the Lannister garrison to the sword, and torch the castle (wiki:Sack_of_Darry.cite_ref-Racok64).
