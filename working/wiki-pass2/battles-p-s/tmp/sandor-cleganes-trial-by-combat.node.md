## Identity

Sandor Clegane's trial by combat was a judicial duel during the War of the Five Kings, held at the hollow hill (wiki:Sandor_Clegane's_trial_by_combat).

## Allegiances

Part of the `FIGHTS_IN` War of the Five Kings (track_b: Conflict).

## Narrative Arc

Arya Stark accused Sandor Clegane of murdering the butcher's boy Mycah. Lord Beric Dondarrion served as champion for the accusers. Beric was killed during the fight but Sandor was acquitted by the gods' judgment. Beric was subsequently resurrected by Thoros of Myr (wiki:Sandor_Clegane's_trial_by_combat).
