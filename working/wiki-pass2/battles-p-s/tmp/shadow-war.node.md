## Identity

The shadow war is the armed resistance of the Sons of the Harpy against the rule of Daenerys Targaryen over Meereen and the abolition of slavery (wiki:Shadow_war.cite_ref-Radwd70).

## Allegiances

Part of the `FIGHTS_IN` Targaryen campaign in Slaver's Bay (track_b: Conflict). The Harpy and the Great Masters are suspected orchestrators. The Sons of the Harpy are the perpetrators, targeting freedmen, Unsullied, Shavepates, and Brazen Beasts (wiki:Shadow_war).

## Narrative Arc

In 300 AC, the Sons of the Harpy waged a campaign of assassinations and terror in Meereen against Daenerys's supporters. Notable victims included Stalwart Shield, Rylona Rhee, Mossador, and Duran. The conflict was temporarily halted but later resumed (wiki:Shadow_war.cite_ref-Radwd70).
