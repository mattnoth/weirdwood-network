## Identity

The sack of Tyrosh was the conquest of the Free City of Tyrosh by the Band of Nine, occurring in 258, 259, or 260 AC, prior to the War of the Ninepenny Kings (wiki:Sack_of_Tyrosh.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii).

## Narrative Arc

The Band of Nine — a group of merchants, mercenaries, and pirates from the Free Cities — banded together to carve out kingdoms for themselves, beginning by overrunning the Disputed Lands. They then turned to Tyrosh, sacking the city and deposing the ruling Archon. The merchant Alequo Adarys was installed as the new ruler, becoming known as the "Tyrant of Tyrosh." Afterwards the Band of Nine conquered the Stepstones, leading to the War of the Ninepenny Kings when the Iron Throne launched a preemptive attack. Adarys remained ruler of Tyrosh until he was poisoned by his queen in 266 AC (wiki:Sack_of_Tyrosh.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii).
