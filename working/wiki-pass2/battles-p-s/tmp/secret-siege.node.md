## Identity

The secret siege occurred in 135 AC during the regency of Aegon III. King Aegon III Targaryen and his brother Prince Viserys Targaryen defied the council of regents by barricading themselves inside Maegor's Holdfast for eighteen days to protect Viserys's wife Larra Rogare from arrest (wiki:Secret_siege.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency).

## Narrative Arc

During the Lysene Spring, Larra Rogare and House Rogare were accused of plotting to overthrow King Aegon. Ser Marston Waters, the new Hand of the King, ordered Larra's arrest. When Ser Amaury Peake went to Maegor's Holdfast with men-at-arms, Prince Viserys planted an axe on the drawbridge as warning. Sandoq the Shadow emerged and slew Peake and all his guards. The king commanded the portcullis lowered and drawbridge raised. On the twelfth day, the tortured Lord Thaddeus Rowan was brought before the holdfast, but his forced confession fell apart under questioning by Viserys and Mushroom. King Aegon ordered Ser Marston to arrest those responsible, and Marston switched sides. Lord George Graceford implicated a dozen conspirators including Ser Mervyn Flowers, Tessario the Tiger, and Ser Lucas Leygood. Ser Marston was stabbed during arrests; Mervyn was beaten to death; Lucas was killed at the Gate of the Gods. Six days after the conspiracy unraveled, the king and his companions emerged, weak from hunger (wiki:Secret_siege.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency).
