## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Septon Torbert is among the leading possibilities to become the new High Septon after [the old one](wiki:High_Septon_(Tyrion's)) died.(wiki:Torbert.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Torbert kisses Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s feet whenever they meet. Cersei looks for him and Septon [Raynard](wiki:Raynard) when she visits the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor), but Torbert cannot be located.(wiki:Torbert.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) According to the [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow), Torbert is confined to a penance cell on nothing but bread and water as the High Sparrow believes it is sinful for any man to be as plump as Torbert with half the realm starving.(wiki:Torbert.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
