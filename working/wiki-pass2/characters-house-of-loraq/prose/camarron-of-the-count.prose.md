## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

The seventh time [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) petitions Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) to have the [fighting pits of Meereen](wiki:Fighting_pits_of_Meereen) reopened, he brings seven [pit fighters](wiki:Pit_fighters) with him, including Camarron of the Count.(wiki:Camarron_of_the_Count.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Following the disappearance of Daenerys upon [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) and the arrest of Hizdahr, Barristan insists on including Camarron in the [ruling council of Meereen](wiki:Ruling_council_of_Meereen) along with [Belaquo Bonebreaker](wiki:Belaquo_Bonebreaker), [Goghor the Giant](wiki:Goghor_the_Giant), and the [Spotted Cat](wiki:Spotted_Cat), despite the objections of [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq).(wiki:Camarron_of_the_Count.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) When discussing how best to deal with the forces from [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai), Camarron suggests seizing the ships tied up along the riverfront and bring three hundred pit fighters around the Yunkish rear using the [Skahazadhan](wiki:Skahazadhan).(wiki:Camarron_of_the_Count.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### The Winds of Winter

Shortly before the resumption of the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen), Camarron is among the several hundred pit fighters who have gathered for the battle near the [Spire of Skulls](wiki:Spire_of_Skulls). Camarron, together with [Goghor](wiki:Goghor) and the [Spotted Cat](wiki:Spotted_Cat), is to lead the pit fighters in the battle. Camarron and his pit fighters are to follow the [Stormcrows](wiki:Stormcrows) into battle and follow the horsemen into the breach of the Yunkish lines in order to slaughter as much as they can.(wiki:Camarron_of_the_Count.cite_ref-barristan1_3-0)
