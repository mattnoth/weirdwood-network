## Origins

Khrazz is of humble [Meereenese](wiki:Meereen) birth.(wiki:Khrazz.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

## Appearances & Description

Khrazz is a tall man(wiki:Khrazz.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) with cold,(wiki:Khrazz.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) dead eyes and pale skin.(wiki:Khrazz.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) He has a crest of bristly red-black hair that runs down the center of his head from his brow to the base of his neck.(wiki:Khrazz.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Khrazz.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

Khrazz is a pit fighter of great skill and speed(wiki:Khrazz.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) who believes that eating the hearts of his foes makes him stronger.(wiki:Khrazz.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) Khrazz is able to wield a shortsword in the [fighting pits of Meereen](wiki:Fighting_pits_of_Meereen),(wiki:Khrazz.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) but his weapon of choice is a [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) *[arakh](wiki:Arakh)* which he wields like a blur.(wiki:Khrazz.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Khrazz, along with his fellow pit fighters [Barsena Blackhair](wiki:Barsena_Blackhair), [Camarron of the Count](wiki:Camarron_of_the_Count), [Goghor the Giant](wiki:Goghor_the_Giant), the [Spotted Cat](wiki:Spotted_Cat), [Fearless Ithoke](wiki:Fearless_Ithoke), and [Belaquo Bonebreaker](wiki:Belaquo_Bonebreaker), accompanies [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) when he petitions [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) for the seventh time for the [fighting pits of Meereen](wiki:Fighting_pits_of_Meereen) to be reopened.(wiki:Khrazz.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Khrazz fights the first match in [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit) on the day the fighting pits are reopened. After using a shortsword to kill a [Summer Islander](wiki:Summer_Isles) armed with a trident, Khrazz cuts the man's heart out and bites it.(wiki:Khrazz.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

After Daenerys has disappeared on [Drogon](wiki:Drogon)'s back, Khrazz is one of several pit fighters who become bodyguards to King Hizdahr.(wiki:Khrazz.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

When Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) goes to arrest Hizdahr, Khrazz comes to the defense of his king. Although he is both younger and heavier than Barristan, he is not used to fighting a man in armor, which protects Barristan from Khrazz's *[arakh](wiki:Arakh)*. Despite taking several wounds, Khrazz refuses to yield. Barristan disembowels the pit fighter and stabs him in the heart.(wiki:Khrazz.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4)
