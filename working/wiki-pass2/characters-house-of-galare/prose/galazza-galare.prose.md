## Appearances & Description

Galazza is a tall, graceful-looking woman, despite being past eighty years old. Her hair is white and her skin parchment thin, but the years have not dimmed her piercing green eyes. [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) thinks they are sad eyes, full of wisdom.(wiki:Galazza_Galare.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

As the [Green Grace](wiki:Green_Grace), Galazza Galare wears a shimmering green silk veil that covers her whole face, except for her eyes. She has a soft and kindly voice.(wiki:Galazza_Galare.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Galazza's counsel proves invaluable to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), [Queen of Meereen](wiki:Queen_of_Meereen), as she is a voice for peace and tolerance.(wiki:Galazza_Galare.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Two of Galazza's cousins, [Grazhar](wiki:Grazhar) and [Qezza](wiki:Qezza), are made the queen's [cupbearers and hostages](wiki:Cupbearers_of_Daenerys_Targaryen).(wiki:Galazza_Galare.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) The Green Grace is accompanied by a dozen [White Graces](wiki:White_Graces) when she arrives at the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) for a private supper with the queen. Galazza suggests Daenerys marry [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) to achieve the peace she desires.(wiki:Galazza_Galare.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Three [Blue Graces](wiki:Blue_Graces) accompany the Green Grace to inform Daenerys about a rider bringing [disease](wiki:Bloody_flux) from [Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor) to Meereen.(wiki:Galazza_Galare.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

While discussing Daenerys's upcoming wedding to Hizdahr, the Green Grace says that the queen should re-open the [fighting pits of Meereen](wiki:Fighting_pits_of_Meereen), which would be pleasing to the gods.(wiki:Galazza_Galare.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) On the day of [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen), Galazza and her fellow [Graces](wiki:Graces) wait for the bride and groom outside their temple.(wiki:Galazza_Galare.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Galazza and many of her Graces attend the reopening of [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit), but Daenerys disappears from the pit on [Drogon](wiki:Drogon).(wiki:Galazza_Galare.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), [Lord Commander of the Queensguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Queensguard), seizes control of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) in his queen's absence, and he sends Galazza to the [Yunkai'i](wiki:Yunkai'i) to offer his peace terms and gold for the three hostages held by the Yunkai'i. [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) distrusts Galazza.(wiki:Galazza_Galare.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Galaza returns to inform Barristan that the Yunkai'i have refused his offer, and that the only price they will accept is the death of the [dragons](wiki:Dragon). She tells him that Daenerys is dead, and that he should let her dragons die as well.(wiki:Galazza_Galare.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)
