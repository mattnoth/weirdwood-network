## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

After the death of his father, Lord [Sawane](wiki:Sawane_Botley), and eldest brother, [Harren](wiki:Harren_Botley), Symond would become the heir of [House Botley](wiki:House_Botley). King [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) dispossesses Symond's elder brother [Tristifer](wiki:Tristifer_Botley), however, and gives the title to his uncle, [Germund Botley](wiki:Germund_Botley).(wiki:Symond_Botley.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
