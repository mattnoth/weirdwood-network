## Origins

Tristifer was among the five boys brought to [Pyke](wiki:Pyke) to become the foster sons of Lady [Alannys Harlaw](wiki:Alannys_Harlaw) after [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion). [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) knew him since they were boys.(wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-TWOW_5-0) Tris became friends with [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), but he was sent away to [Blacktyde](wiki:Blacktyde) after he and Asha were found touching each other by Maester [Qalen](wiki:Qalen). Tris wrote letters to Asha from Blacktyde, but Maester [Joseran](wiki:Joseran) refused to send them.(wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Tristifer Botley](wiki:Category:Images_of_Tristifer_Botley)*

Tristifer has messy brown hair and large eyes. He was pimpled as a boy. Although now handsome, Tris has an unrequited love for [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy).(wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Tris wears a [sealskin](wiki:Seal) cloak.(wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Tristifer is the titular [Lord of Lordsport](wiki:Lord_of_Lordsport) after his father, [Sawane](wiki:Sawane_Botley), is killed by [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) and his elder brother, [Harren](wiki:Harren_Botley), dies at [Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin). Euron gives half of the Botley lands to [House Wynch](wiki:House_Wynch) of [Iron Holt](wiki:Iron_Holt), however, and Tris's uncle [Germund](wiki:Germund_Botley) claims to be lord.(wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Tris meets with Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw) at the [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers). He declares his love for [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) at that castle, but Asha spurns him.(wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

Tris is one of the champions for Asha at the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

After Euron is proclaimed [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North) at the kingsmoot, Tristifer sails on his longship to [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers) and [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) with three others of Asha's as she contemplates her next move. He grows a thick brown beard, wears furs, and rides a tall roan stallion.(wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Asha kisses Tris after he tells her of [Torgon the Latecomer](wiki:Torgon_Greyiron).(wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

Tris proposes that the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) under Asha abandon [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) and try to make their fortune as traders. When [northern mountain clansmen](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans) approach Deepwood, Asha abandons the castle and leads her ironborn toward the [Bay of Ice](wiki:Bay_of_Ice). Traveling in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood), Tris refuses to sleep with [Hagen's daughter](wiki:Hagen's_daughter). They are defeated in the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte) by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s men, however. Despite not being a warrior Tristifer fights bravely in the battle.(wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) While Asha is taken by Stannis for his [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell), Tris and the [other ironborn survivors](wiki:Ragged_nine) are imprisoned at Deepwood.(wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Asha's four longships are captured or burned by [Alysane Mormont](wiki:Alysane_Mormont).(wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

The ironborn at Deepwood are eventually ransomed from Lady [Sybelle Glover](wiki:Sybelle_Glover) by [Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris). They escort this envoy of the [Iron Bank of Braavos](wiki:Iron_Bank_of_Braavos) to Stannis, whose army is encamped at a [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village).(wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Tristifer and [Qarl the Maid](wiki:Qarl_the_Maid) accompany Asha to her meeting with Stannis Baratheon, when she asks about the fate of her captive brother [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy). Theon considers Tris and Qarl to be Asha's pets.(wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-TWOW_5-1)
