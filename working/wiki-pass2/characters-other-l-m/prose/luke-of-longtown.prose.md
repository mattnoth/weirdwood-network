## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Luke accompanies Lord Commander [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) when he travels to the [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) grove north of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black). Jon sent Luke to scout to the west of the column and warn of any approach. He was armed with [obsidian](wiki:Obsidian) and steel plus a warhorn to call for help.(wiki:Luke_of_Longtown.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
