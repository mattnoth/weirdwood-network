## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo)'s *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* destroys a [Lhazareen](wiki:Lhazareen) settlement, Mago begins raping a woman, [Eroeh](wiki:Eroeh), until she is rescued by [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen).(wiki:Mago.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

After Drogo falls ill, [Jhaqo](wiki:Jhaqo) declares himself *khal* and names Mago a [bloodrider](wiki:Bloodrider). Mago then steals Eroeh back and rapes her. He gives her to Jhaqo and she is raped again by him and his other bloodriders. When they are done, they cut her throat. Upon hearing of Eroeh's fate, Daenerys promises that Mago's fate will be more cruel, and swears that Mago and Jhaqo will plead for the mercy they showed Eroeh before she is done with them.(wiki:Mago.cite_ref-Ragot68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Storm of Swords

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) recalls how Mago raped and killed [Eroeh](wiki:Eroeh) after Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo)'s death.(wiki:Mago.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

While in the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea), Daenerys recalls her promise to kill both Jhaqo and Mago before being discovered by their *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)*.(wiki:Mago.cite_ref-Radwd71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
