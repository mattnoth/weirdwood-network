## Appearances & Description

Lysono is a [Lyseni](wiki:Lys), with pale [lilac eyes](wiki:Purple_eyes) and long white-gold hair.(wiki:Lysono_Maar.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Lysono_Maar.cite_ref-Ar2_2-0) His lips are full, giving him an almost feminine appearance at first glance. His fingernails are [painted](wiki:Cosmetics) purple, and his earlobes drip with [pearls](wiki:Pearl) and [amethysts](wiki:Amethyst).(wiki:Lysono_Maar.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Lysono is glib but guarded in conversation.(wiki:Lysono_Maar.cite_ref-Ar2_2-1) He speaks the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue) of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) very well.(wiki:Lysono_Maar.cite_ref-Ar2_2-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

When the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) arrives in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), a disguised Lysono tries to arrange passage from the harbor to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). No trade ships are en route to the city, however, because of the [Yunkish](wiki:Yunkai) blockade during the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen).(wiki:Lysono_Maar.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Lysono swears his allegiance to [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(Young_Griff)) and is supportive of the plan to invade [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) without the assistance of Daenerys and her [dragons](wiki:Dragon).(wiki:Lysono_Maar.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

During the [landing of the Golden Company](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company), the cautious [Harry Strickland](wiki:Harry_Strickland) suggests using Lysono's spies to gather information.(wiki:Lysono_Maar.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Near [Griffin's Roost](wiki:Griffin's_Roost), Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) and her party encounter Lysono with a [column](wiki:Column) of sellswords from the Golden Company. His appearance reminds her of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen); while Lysono thinks that a compliment, Arianne is unnerved.(wiki:Lysono_Maar.cite_ref-Ar2_2-3)
