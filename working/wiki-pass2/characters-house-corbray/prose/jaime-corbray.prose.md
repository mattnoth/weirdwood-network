## Origins

Ser Jaime Corbray participated in the [Battle of Seven Stars](wiki:Battle_of_Seven_Stars), fighting under the banner of Ser [Artys Arryn](wiki:Artys_Arryn). House Corbray insists he was the one who slew King [Robar II Royce](wiki:Robar_II_Royce), citing the recovery of the Corbray sword [Lady Forlorn](wiki:Lady_Forlorn), which King Robar had taken from the hands of King [Qyle Corbray](wiki:Qyle_Corbray).(wiki:Jaime_Corbray.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
