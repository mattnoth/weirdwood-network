## Origins

Lady Corbray is the sixteen-year-old daughter of a rich [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown) merchant of [lowborn](wiki:Smallfolk) birth.(wiki:Lady_Corbray.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

## Appearances & Description

The merchant's daughter is described as a tall, strapping, healthy girl, with big breasts and wide hips.(wiki:Lady_Corbray.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)(wiki:Lady_Corbray.cite_ref-TWOW_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), a former customs officer at the port of Gulltown, brokers a [marriage](wiki:Marriage) between the merchant's daughter and the childless [Lyonel Corbray](wiki:Lyonel_Corbray), [Lord of Heart's Home](wiki:Lord_of_Heart's_Home), after the passing of Lyonel's first wife.(wiki:Lady_Corbray.cite_ref-TWOW_1-3) Lyonel, the head of impoverished [House Corbray](wiki:House_Corbray),(wiki:Lady_Corbray.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) receives a significant [dowry](wiki:Dowry) for marrying a [commoner](wiki:Smallfolk). It is [a well-attended wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Lyonel_Corbray_and_Lady_Corbray), with Lord [Benedar Belmore](wiki:Benedar_Belmore), Lord [Gerold Grafton](wiki:Gerold_Grafton), Lord [Jon Lynderly](wiki:Jon_Lynderly), Ser [Symond Templeton](wiki:Symond_Templeton), Lord [Waxley](wiki:House_Waxley), and Lady [Anya Waynwood](wiki:Anya_Waynwood) present.(wiki:Lady_Corbray.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

Petyr reports that Lyonel is pleased with his wife and with her dowry.(wiki:Lady_Corbray.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

### The Winds of Winter

Lord Lyonel and the new Lady Corbray do not attend the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) for the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights) at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon), remaining at Heart's Home instead where they are expecting a baby. The prospect of a child angers Lyonel's brother and heir, Ser [Lyn Corbray](wiki:Lyn_Corbray), as this pushes him down in the line of succession.(wiki:Lady_Corbray.cite_ref-TWOW_1-4)
