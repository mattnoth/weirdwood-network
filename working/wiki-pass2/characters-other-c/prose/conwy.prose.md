## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Conwy brings a new group of recruits to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), consisting of a greybeard, two blond youths with the look of brothers ([Arron](wiki:Arron) and [Emrick](wiki:Emrick)), a young man in soiled satin ([Satin](wiki:Satin)), a man with a clubfoot ([Hop-Robin](wiki:Hop-Robin)), and a man [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) describes as a "grinning loon". He found them in a dungeon of a [lord](wiki:Lord) who resides near [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown).(wiki:Conwy.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
