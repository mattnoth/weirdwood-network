## Appearances & Description

Like the other children of the forest, Coals is smaller than humans, but not childlike. Coals has nut-brown skin, dappled like a [deer](wiki:Deer)'s with paler spots. Coals's hands have only three fingers and a thumb, with sharp black claws instead of nails. Coals has large ears that can hear things that no man can hear.(wiki:Coals.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Coals has large golden eyes slitted like those of a [cat](wiki:Cat),(wiki:Coals.cite_ref-Radwd13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) allowing them to see in dark passages.(wiki:Coals.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Coals does not speak the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue).(wiki:Coals.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

In the [cave of the three-eyed crow](wiki:Cave_of_the_three-eyed_crow) [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) and his companions [Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed) and [Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed) meet a remnant of the children of the forest, who serve the [last greenseer](wiki:Last_greenseer), Lord Brynden. Their names are too long for the human tongue, according to [Leaf](wiki:Leaf), so Bran and Meera give them names. Coals is one of them. Bran does not know what Coals thinks about their new name.(wiki:Coals.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
