## Origins

When [Dunk](wiki:Dunk) became Ser Arlan's [squire](wiki:Squire), he would ride the [hedge knight](wiki:Hedge_knight)'s stot Chestnut,(wiki:Chestnut.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) while Arlan rode his palfrey Sweetfoot. (Arlan's destrier, [Thunder](wiki:Thunder), was not ridden except in [tourneys](wiki:Tourney) and battle.) Arlan frequently told Dunk that a [knight](wiki:Knight) should never love a horse, since they might die under him, but he loved his horses anyway. Arlan often spent his last [copper](wiki:Currency) on an apple for Chestnut, or oats for Sweetfoot and Thunder.(wiki:Chestnut.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

In [209 AC](wiki:209_AC), the aged Arlan died from a chill on the way to [Ashford Meadow](wiki:Ashford_Meadow), and Dunk took ownership of Chestnut, along with Thunder and Sweetfoot. At first Dunk debated selling Thunder and Chestnut, and keeping Sweetfoot, but then he decided to attend the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) as the knight Ser Duncan the Tall. To afford a suit of [armor](wiki:Armor), Dunk reluctantly decided to sell Sweetfoot, as Chestnut was not worth much, and Thunder would be needed for the [joust](wiki:Joust).(wiki:Chestnut.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

Dunk rode Sweetfoot to [Ashford](wiki:Ashford), finding her gait easier than Chestnut's, but still tiring. On the way, he stopped at an inn, where Dunk first met Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen), disguised as "Egg", when he asked the (assumed) stableboy to rub down Sweetfoot and feed his horses. On the tourney grounds, Dunk was arrested when he attacked Prince [Aerion Targaryen](wiki:Aerion_Targaryen), who was assaulting [a girl](wiki:Tanselle) that Dunk had met earlier. While Dunk was imprisoned, Egg and their friend [Raymun Fossoway](wiki:Raymun_Fossoway) took care of Chestnut and Thunder.(wiki:Chestnut.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

After Prince [Maekar Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_Targaryen) allowed his son Aegon to officially become Dunk's [squire](wiki:Squire), Egg rode Chestnut when they traveled, while Duncan rode Thunder.(wiki:Chestnut.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)

Chestnut died in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), during the desert crossing between the [Prince's Pass](wiki:Prince's_Pass) and [Vaith](wiki:Vaith). Dunk wanted to bury his old friend, but their Dornish companions would not wait, and so Chestnut's corpse was left to the [sand dogs](wiki:Dog). Afterwards, Dunk and Egg rode double on Thunder, until Egg's brother gave them [Maester](wiki:Maester_(mule)) the [mule](wiki:Mule).(wiki:Chestnut.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Later, at [Standfast](wiki:Standfast), Dunk dreamed of burying Chestnut in the sands while crying, as the Dornish [knights](wiki:Knight) and others stood by and mocked him.(wiki:Chestnut.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
