## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) becomes attached to the war band of Munda's father, [Tormund](wiki:Tormund). She sings along during the song "[The Last of the Giants](wiki:The_Last_of_the_Giants)".(wiki:Munda.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Munda fights back when [Longspear Ryk](wiki:Ryk) comes to "steal" her.(wiki:Munda.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Tormund mentions to Jon that Munda and Ryk are now married, and she is well treated.(wiki:Munda.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
