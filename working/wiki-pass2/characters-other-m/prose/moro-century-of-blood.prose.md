## Origins

Moro led the Dothraki to the gates of [Sathar](wiki:Sathar), the "Waterfall City" of the [Sarnori](wiki:Tall_Men). All men were put to the sword and their women and children carried off as slaves to the marts of the hill city of [Hazdahn Mo](wiki:Hazdahn_Mo), a colony of the [Ghiscari Empire](wiki:Ghiscari_Empire). Sathar was burned to the ground, and it is written that Moro gave the ruins a new name: Yalli Qamayi, the place of Wailing Children.(wiki:Moro_(Century_of_Blood).cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Six years later, Moro razed [Kasath](wiki:Kasath) to the ground, with the aid of the King of [Gornath](wiki:Gornath), who had taken one of Moro's daughters as wife and made common cause with him and sent aid to Moro's riders. Twelve years later, however, Moro was slain by Khal [Horro](wiki:Horro).(wiki:Moro_(Century_of_Blood).cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
