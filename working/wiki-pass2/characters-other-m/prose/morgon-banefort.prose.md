## Origins

King Morgon and his [thralls](wiki:Thrall) were defeated in a twenty-years-long war with King [Loreon I](wiki:Loreon_I_Lannister) of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Morgon_Banefort.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) According to a semi-canon source, Morgon was said to have been a powerful [necromancer](wiki:Necromancy) who cursed his vanquishers as he died, claiming he would return from the grave. In response, Loreon had Morgon's body cut into pieces and fed to his [lions](wiki:Lion). Two years later the lions broke free within [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), slaying three of Loreon's sons.(wiki:Morgon_Banefort.cite_ref-2)
