## Origins

Melissa Blackwood "reigned" for five years as the mistress of King [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen), during which she gave birth to three children: Mya, [Gwenys](wiki:Gwenys_Rivers), and [Brynden](wiki:Brynden_Rivers).
