## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Morna White Mask is among the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) leaders who accompany [Tormund Giantsbane](wiki:Tormund) to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) in declaring a truce with the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Morna_White_Mask.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) She is given the abandoned castle [Queensgate](wiki:Queensgate) by [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), to garrison and settle her own people.(wiki:Morna_White_Mask.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
