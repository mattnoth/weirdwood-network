## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Lord [Godric Borrell](wiki:Godric_Borrell) of [Sweetsister](wiki:Sweetsister) tells [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) that Lord Triston Sunderland would require him to hand over [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) if he was aware that Davos is on Sweetsister.(wiki:Triston_Sunderland.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Godric says Triston would sell Davos to the [queen](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) for a pot of [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) gold; with seven sons all determined to be [knights](wiki:Knight) the poor man needs every [dragon](wiki:Currency). Godric has heard Triston bemoaning the cost of [destriers](wiki:Horse) and tells Davos it takes a lot of fish to buy a decent suit of plate and mail.(wiki:Triston_Sunderland.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
