## Origins

The [Red Kings](wiki:Red_Kings) of the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort) from [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) eventually bent the knee to the [Kings of Winter](wiki:Kings_of_Winter), [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Dreadfort.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) leads the [northern](wiki:North) foot in the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Dreadfort.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Roose helps orchestrate the [fall of Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Dreadfort.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Lord Roose betrays [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Dreadfort.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The [small council](wiki:Small_council), on the authority of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), grants Roose the title of [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Dreadfort.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Roose's heir, [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), claims the titles of [Lord of the Hornwood](wiki:Lord_of_the_Hornwood) and [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Dreadfort.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
