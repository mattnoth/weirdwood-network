## Origins

When King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) left Dorne to return to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), he named Lord [Jon Rosby](wiki:Jon_Rosby) the [Castellan](wiki:Castellan) of [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) and the Warden of the Sands. The [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) soon rebelled, however, and Rosby was killed in the [Defenestration of Sunspear](wiki:Defenestration_of_Sunspear).(wiki:Warden_of_the_Sands.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_dorne:_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
