## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

While inhabiting his eagle to search for a party of [rangers](wiki:Rangers), Orell's human body and another [wildling](wiki:Wildling) are killed in the [Skirling Pass](wiki:Skirling_Pass) by [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow). Stonesnake and Jon toss the bodies into the depths below, and Jon takes [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte) prisoner.(wiki:Orell.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Due to the magic of [skinchanging](wiki:Skinchanging), a portion of Orell's consciousness remains in the eagle, which develops a fierce hatred for Jon. He is instrumental in helping [Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt) track down and capture Jon and [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand).(wiki:Orell.cite_ref-Racok68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Orell's eagle attacks the captive Jon and scars his face with its talons.(wiki:Orell.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Another skinchanger, [Varamyr Sixskins](wiki:Varamyr_Sixskins), takes control of Orell's eagle.(wiki:Orell.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Varamyr uses the eagle to scout [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) and spots [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s arrival at the Wall.(wiki:Orell.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) The eagle bursts into flames during the attack on Castle Black with [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) claiming she was responsible. The incident greatly affects Varamyr and supposedly kills the remnants of Orell inside the eagle.(wiki:Orell.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)(wiki:Orell.cite_ref-Rasos76.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
