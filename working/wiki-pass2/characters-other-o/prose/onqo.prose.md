## Origins

Onqo led a particularly huge *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)*.(wiki:Onqo.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Onqo began to make incursions into the [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibbenese) territories in [Essos](wiki:Essos), only to be killed by the Ibbenese in an epic battle that destroyed his *khalasar*.
