## Appearances & Description

The Old Tattersalt earned his nickname for the stained and faded black clothes he wears. He has a large belly and seldom talks.(wiki:Old_Tattersalt.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Tattersalt captains the *Blackbird* on its voyage to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).

### A Dance with Dragons

Again captaining the *Blackbird*, Tattersalt is second-in-command during [Cotter Pyke](wiki:Cotter_Pyke)'s expedition to [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome).(wiki:Old_Tattersalt.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Maester [Harmune](wiki:Harmune) reports to Lord Commander [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) from Hardhome that the *Blackbird* and all hands have been lost.(wiki:Old_Tattersalt.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
