## Origins

At some time the [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince) dispatched Orson's brother to the [Sorrows](wiki:Sorrows).(wiki:Orson_Stone.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Appearances & Description

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) describes Orson as a red-faced man.(wiki:Orson_Stone.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He is known as *the Bastard Knight* by the rest of the Windblown.(wiki:Orson_Stone.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) His surname implies that he is probably a [bastard](wiki:Bastardy) born or raised in [the Vale](wiki:The_Vale).

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Orson Stone takes part of the [Siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor). He is later sent by the [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince) along with the other [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) Windblown to go over to Daenerys Targaryen.(wiki:Orson_Stone.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Daenerys has Orson and the other Westerosi Windblown imprisoned after she learns from Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) that they have turned their cloaks on the orders of the Tattered Prince. Daenerys sends the only woman of the lot, [Pretty Meris](wiki:Meris), back to the Tattered Prince(wiki:Orson_Stone.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) but Orson and the others remain imprisoned in the [dungeons](wiki:Dungeons_(Great_Pyramid)) of the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Orson_Stone.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
