## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Harmund is a supporter of [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy).(wiki:Harmund_Sharp.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) During the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot), Asha explains that his son [Harrag](wiki:Harrag_Sharp) died at [Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell) for turnips.(wiki:Harmund_Sharp.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
