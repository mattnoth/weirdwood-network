## Appearances & Description

Godry is a tall man with a deep, booming voice.(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Godry is broad shouldered, brawny, and muscled.(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Winds_7-0)

Godry is boastful and condescending,(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) but fierce in his dedication to [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor).(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) His crony is Ser [Clayton Suggs](wiki:Clayton_Suggs).(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Godry wears plate and mail.(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) His breastplate is silvered steel inlaid with niello,(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Winds_7-1) as is the armor of his courser.(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He fastens his cloak with the burning heart of R'hllor.(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Winds_7-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Godry earns the nickname *Giantslayer* from the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), during which he chases after a fleeing [giant](wiki:Giants), puts a lance in his back, and then hacks off his small head.(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

### A Feast for Crows

Godry is at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) with [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

In the practice yard of Castle Black, Godry attempts to goad [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), the young [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), into a fight, but the knight is ignored.(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) He is among the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) in the common room who witness Jon's confrontation with [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt), and he laughs when Janos refuses to obey the lord commander's orders.(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

During the execution of "[Mance Rayder](wiki:Lord_of_Bones)", Godry rides the horse Mance is tied to, and drags him to the cage in which he is burned by [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre).(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) He ridicules [Mors Crowfood](wiki:Mors_Umber) and the [Umbers](wiki:House_Umber) during Stannis's war council.(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) Jon worries that Stannis will marry [Val](wiki:Val) to Godry, Ser [Richard Horpe](wiki:Richard_Horpe), or Ser [Justin Massey](wiki:Justin_Massey).(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Godry is among those who march with Stannis on [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte). He leads the vanguard of Stannis's army during the [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell), but they are passed by swifter [northmen](wiki:Northmen) wearing [bear-paws](wiki:Bear-paws).(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4) Godry leads the prayers to [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor) at the nightfires, and he urges Stannis to sacrifice an unbeliever to stop the blizzard slowing the march of the army.(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5) He gets his wish when four [Peasebury](wiki:House_Peasebury) men are caught in the act of [cannibalism](wiki:Cannibalism), and are subsequently staked and burned at the [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village).(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

### The Winds of Winter

Godry has been urging Stannis to sacrifice [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) to R'hllor. Along with Ser [Clayton Suggs](wiki:Clayton_Suggs) and Ser [Richard Horpe](wiki:Richard_Horpe), Godry brings Maester [Tybald](wiki:Tybald) in for questioning by the king. Stannis has Godry confine the [maester](wiki:Maester) to a hut.(wiki:Godry_Farring.cite_ref-Winds_7-3)
