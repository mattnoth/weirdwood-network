## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser [Deziel Dalt](wiki:Deziel_Dalt) is among the Dornish nobles that accompany [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Knight of Lemonwood.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

According to Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell), Ser Deziel is too dutiful and not powerful enough to fight or defy her [father](wiki:Doran_Martell), the [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne).(wiki:Knight of Lemonwood.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
