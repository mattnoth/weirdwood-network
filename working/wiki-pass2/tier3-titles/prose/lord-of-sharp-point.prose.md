## Origins

Prior to the [War of Conquest](wiki:War_of_Conquest), House Bar Emmon was sworn to [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon), the Storm Kings from [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), but had closer ties to [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) of nearby [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). When [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) launched his [invasion](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), Lord Bar Emmon supported him instead of the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King), [Argilac the Arrogant](wiki:Argilac_Durrandon). With the creation of the [crownlands](wiki:Crownlands) after the Conquest, the Bar Emmons became sworn directly to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), instead of [House Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon) of Storm's End, who had replaced the Durrandons.(wiki:Lord of Sharp Point.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
When the realm began to turn against King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) for his nephew Prince [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), Lord Bar Emmon was among the score of lesser lords who gathered at his command.(wiki:Lord of Sharp Point.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) However, when Maegor mysteriously died upon the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), Lord Bar Emmon fled back to Sharp Point.(wiki:Lord of Sharp Point.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Lord Bar Emmon was one of the first lords who traveled to Dragonstone to speak with Jaehaerys I during his minority.(wiki:Lord of Sharp Point.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
A Lord Bar Emmon died of the [Shivers](wiki:Shivers) in the winter of [59 AC](wiki:59_AC).(wiki:Lord of Sharp Point.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Lord Bar Emmon ruling in [101 AC](wiki:101_AC) supported [Laenor Velaryon](wiki:Laenor_Velaryon)'s claim in the [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council).(wiki:Lord of Sharp Point.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Lord of Sharp Point.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Lord of Sharp Point.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord Bar Emmon was a member of Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen)'s [black council](wiki:Black_council).(wiki:Lord of Sharp Point.cite_ref-Rfabthe_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) After the [greens](wiki:Greens)' victory at the [Battle of the Honeywine](wiki:Battle_of_the_Honeywine), he suggested for Rhaenyra to bend the knees to King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), but the queen dismissed his advice.(wiki:Lord of Sharp Point.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)(wiki:Lord of Sharp Point.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
