## Origins

### House Yronwood[ ]

The lords of [House Yronwood](wiki:House_Yronwood) styled themselves High Kings of Dorne when they ruled northern Dorne, from the domains of [House Wyl](wiki:House_Wyl) in the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) to the headwaters of the [Greenblood](wiki:Greenblood).

### Houses of the Greenblood[ ]

The title was also claimed by a ruler seated on a great wooden motte-and-bailey castle on the south bank of the [Greenblood](wiki:Greenblood) near [Lemonwood](wiki:Lemonwood). This king was chosen by election from a dozen noble houses settled along the river or its eastern shores. These included Houses [Briar](wiki:House_Briar), [Brook](wiki:House_Brook), [Brownhill](wiki:House_Brownhill), [Holt](wiki:House_Holt_(Dorne)), [Lake](wiki:House_Lake_(Dorne)), [Shell](wiki:House_Shell_(Dorne)), and [Wade](wiki:House_Wade). This powerful river realm ended when a disputed election led to warfare.
