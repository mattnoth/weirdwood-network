## Origins

Prior to bending the knee to [House Martell](wiki:House_Martell), [House Fowler](wiki:House_Fowler) held the title of [Lord of the Wide Way](wiki:Lord_of_the_Wide_Way), referring to the older name of the [Prince's Pass](wiki:Prince's_Pass).(wiki:Warden of the Prince's Pass.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_kingdoms_of_the_first_men.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
