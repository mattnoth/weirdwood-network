## Origins

In [131 AC](wiki:131_AC), during the final months of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord [Leowyn Corbray](wiki:Leowyn_Corbray) led the ten thousand men of Lady [Jeyne Arryn](wiki:Jeyne_Arryn) from the [Vale](wiki:Vale) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), together with his brother, Ser [Corwyn Corbray](wiki:Corwyn_Corbray).(wiki:Lord of Heart's Home.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) At the start of the [regency of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III), Leowyn was named [Protector of the Realm](wiki:Protector_of_the_Realm), a position he held until his death in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), when he succumbed to the [Winter Fever](wiki:Winter_Fever).(wiki:Lord of Heart's Home.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
