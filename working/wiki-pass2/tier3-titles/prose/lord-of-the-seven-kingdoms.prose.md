## Origins

After [his landing](wiki:Aegon's_Landing) at the mouth of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush), [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) was crowned by his sister [Visenya](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) as "Aegon, First of His Name, [King of All Westeros](wiki:King_of_All_Westeros), [Shield of His People](wiki:Shield_of_His_People)".(wiki:Lord of the Seven Kingdoms.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) When Aegon was anointed by the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(War_of_Conquest)) at [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown)'s [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) after [the Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), he was named "Aegon of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen), the First of His Name, [King of the Andals, the Rhoynar, and the First Men](wiki:King_of_the_Andals,_the_Rhoynar,_and_the_First_Men), Lord of the Seven Kingdoms, and [Protector of the Realm](wiki:Protector_of_the_Realm)".(wiki:Lord of the Seven Kingdoms.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Queen Visenya decided that the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) should consist of seven members because her husband, King Aegon, was Lord of the Seven Kingdoms.(wiki:Lord of the Seven Kingdoms.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
While [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) was declared Lord of the Seven Kingdoms in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), his rival, his half sister [Rhaenyra](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), did not claim a corresponding title when she was crowned as queen at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Lord of the Seven Kingdoms.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Both were killed during the civil war, and Rhaenyra's son, [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), succeeded to the throne.(wiki:Lord of the Seven Kingdoms.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Despite claiming authority over the [Andals](wiki:Andals), the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar), and the [First Men](wiki:First_Men), the rulers on the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) did not rule all [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) until [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) joined the realm under King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord of the Seven Kingdoms.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
[House Blackfyre](wiki:House_Blackfyre) claimed the title during the unsuccessful [Blackfyre Rebellions](wiki:Blackfyre_Rebellions).(wiki:Lord of the Seven Kingdoms.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
The title passed from [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) to [House Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon) as a result of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Lord of the Seven Kingdoms.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_end.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

The exiled [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) claims the title,(wiki:Lord of the Seven Kingdoms.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) but he is slain by Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) in [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak).(wiki:Lord of the Seven Kingdoms.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), Lord of the Seven Kingdoms, is succeeded by his son, [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), after being mortally wounded by a boar.(wiki:Lord of the Seven Kingdoms.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Joffrey's mother, [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), serves as [Queen Regent](wiki:Queen_Regent).(wiki:Lord of the Seven Kingdoms.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
At [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden), Robert's youngest brother, [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), is declared king with the support of [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell).(wiki:Lord of the Seven Kingdoms.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
