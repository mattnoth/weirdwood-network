## Origins

The first king to unite most of the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) was the last [Bronze King](wiki:Bronze_King), [Robar II Royce](wiki:Robar_II_Royce), during the [Andal invasion](wiki:Andal_invasion) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). With [Andals](wiki:Andals) having conquered three-quarters of the Vale, Robar united the houses and clans of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) in resistance and became [High King of the Vale, the Fingers, and the Mountains of the Moon](wiki:High_King_of_the_Vale,_the_Fingers,_and_the_Mountains_of_the_Moon). However, Robar and the First Men were defeated by Ser [Artys Arryn](wiki:Artys_Arryn), a young Andal called the Falcon Knight, in the [Battle of Seven Stars](wiki:Battle_of_Seven_Stars). The Vale was renamed the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) in Artys's honor.(wiki:King of Mountain and Vale.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He became the first King of Mountain and Vale, and his [Falcon Crown](wiki:Falcon_Crown) was passed down from Arryn to Arryn.(wiki:King of Mountain and Vale.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_vale:_house_arryn.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
The Arryn kings fostered trade, and [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown) grew into one of the largest cities in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). King [Roland I Arryn](wiki:Roland_I_Arryn) began construction of the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie).(wiki:King of Mountain and Vale.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_vale:_the_eyrie.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) King [Hugh Arryn](wiki:Hugh_Arryn) took [Pebble](wiki:Pebble), while King [Hugo Arryn](wiki:Hugo_Arryn) conquered the [Paps](wiki:Paps). King [Alester II Arryn](wiki:Alester_II_Arryn) married [Arwen Upcliff](wiki:Arwen_Upcliff), adding the [Witch Isle](wiki:Witch_Isle) to the Arryn kingdom. In the [War Across the Water](wiki:War_Across_the_Water) after the [Rape of the Three Sisters](wiki:Rape_of_the_Three_Sisters), the Arryns warred with the [Kings of Winter](wiki:Kings_of_Winter) for a thousand years.(wiki:King of Mountain and Vale.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:King of Mountain and Vale.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Some Arryns warred with the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands); King [Roland II Arryn](wiki:Roland_II_Arryn) was ultimately beheaded by King [Tristifer IV Mudd](wiki:Tristifer_IV_Mudd).(wiki:King of Mountain and Vale.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_eyrie.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The [Bloody Gate](wiki:Bloody_Gate) protected the Vale from three failed invasions launched from the riverlands by [Halleck Hoare](wiki:Halleck_Hoare), [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers).(wiki:King of Mountain and Vale.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
The last King of Mountain and Vale was [Ronnel Arryn](wiki:Ronnel_Arryn_(king)), a boy-king whose mother, [Sharra](wiki:Sharra_Arryn), submitted to [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest). The Arryns have since served the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) as Protectors of the Vale and [Wardens of the East](wiki:Warden_of_the_East).(wiki:King of Mountain and Vale.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
