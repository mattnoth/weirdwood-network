## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Ser [Raymun Darry](wiki:Raymun_Darry) hosts King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) at [Darry](wiki:Darry). Raymun is not formally mentioned as "Lord of Darry", but Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) thinks of him as the castle's lord.(wiki:Lord of Darry.cite_ref-Ragot16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) It is possible that [Raymun's father](wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun)) was still alive, having retired from active rule in the years after the rebellion, with Raymun being the de facto lord.(wiki:Lord of Darry.cite_ref-retirement_3-0)
Raymun is killed by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) in the [battle at the Mummer's Ford](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford).(wiki:Lord of Darry.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) According to a semi-canon source, his father, the current lord, dies in the battle as well.(wiki:Lord of Darry.cite_ref-RPG_5-0) His son, [Lyman Darry](wiki:Lyman_Darry), is among the nobles assembled at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Lord of Darry.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The child lord(wiki:Lord of Darry.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Lyman is killed by Gregor in the [sack of Darry](wiki:Sack_of_Darry).(wiki:Lord of Darry.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
After the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Darry is granted to Ser [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister).(wiki:Lord of Darry.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

When the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) lists victims of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), [Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams) mentions "Ser Raymun Darry, Lord Darry, young Lord Darry."(wiki:Lord of Darry.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) It is possible that "Lord Darry" and "young Lord Darry" refer respectively to Raymun's father and son.(wiki:Lord of Darry.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
