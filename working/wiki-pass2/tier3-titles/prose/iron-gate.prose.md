## Origins

In [19 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) decreed that new city walls with seven gates, each with a massive gatehouse and defensive towers, be built to protect [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Grand Maester [Gawen](wiki:Gawen) and Ser [Osmund Strong](wiki:Osmund_Strong), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), headed the construction, which took place between [20](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)–[26 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Iron Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
In the reign of [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), the king had his wife, Queen [Alys Harroway](wiki:Alys_Harroway), tortured to death and executed. Following her death, Alys's body was cut into seven parts and mounted on spikes above each of the seven gates of the city, including the Iron Gate.(wiki:Iron Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Iron Gate.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), a [massive riot](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)) broke out in the city. Ser [Medrick Manderly](wiki:Medrick_Manderly) sallied out and restored some order to the area northeast of [Aegon's Hill](wiki:Aegon's_Hill), to the Iron Gate.(wiki:Iron Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) In the [regency of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III), the King's Hand, Ser [Tyland Lannister](wiki:Tyland_Lannister), ordered the city gates to be strengthened so they could better resist attacks from within the city walls.(wiki:Iron Gate.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt), the commander of the Iron Gate, was promoted by Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) to commander of the [City Watch of King's Landing](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing) after the passing of [Manly Stokeworth](wiki:Manly_Stokeworth).(wiki:Iron Gate.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Following the death of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) and ascension of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), travelers are only allowed to depart [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) from the Iron Gate or the [King's Gate](wiki:King's_Gate), both of which are manned by [red cloaks](wiki:Red_cloaks).(wiki:Iron Gate.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) has [Shae](wiki:Shae) hidden in a [manse near the Iron Gate](wiki:Manse_near_the_Iron_Gate).(wiki:Iron Gate.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), mobs fight to escape the city at the Iron Gate and the [Gate of the Gods](wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods). After deserting the battle, [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) considers leaving from the Iron Gate.(wiki:Iron Gate.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) is sent by his sister, Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), to inspect the city gates. According to him the Iron Gate's hinges are rusted.(wiki:Iron Gate.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
