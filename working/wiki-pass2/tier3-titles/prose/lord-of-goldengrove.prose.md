## Origins

In [48 AC](wiki:48_AC), Lords [Rickard Rowan](wiki:Rickard_Rowan) and [Torgen Oakheart](wiki:Torgen_Oakheart) supported [Septon](wiki:Septon) [Moon](wiki:Moon_(septon)) and marched with him towards [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). When Moon was assassinated, Lord Rickard decamped within the hour.(wiki:Lord of Goldengrove.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_.E2.80.93_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) The following year, Lord Rickard attended [the wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) of [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) and [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), where he met King [Jaehaerys I](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), pronouncing him gentle and soft-spoken.(wiki:Lord of Goldengrove.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_.E2.80.93_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
In [73 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Lord Rowan was defeated by Prince [Baelon Targaryen](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) in a [tourney](wiki:Tourney) at [Old Oak](wiki:Old_Oak).(wiki:Lord of Goldengrove.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
At the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord [Thaddeus Rowan](wiki:Thaddeus_Rowan) declared for the [blacks](wiki:Blacks), fighting in the [battle on the Honeywine](wiki:Battle_on_the_Honeywine).(wiki:Lord of Goldengrove.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) He later became a [regent](wiki:Regent) and [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) during the [regency](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III) of [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord of Goldengrove.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
In [211 AC](wiki:211_AC), Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey) feared Lord Rowan would side with Lady [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber) over their quarrel due to his distant family ties to [House Webber](wiki:House_Webber).(wiki:Lord of Goldengrove.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
In the early 230s AC, Lord Rowan arranged a [betrothal](wiki:Betrothal) for his daughter to [Tion Lannister](wiki:Tion_Lannister), the second son of the [Lord of Casterly Rock](wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock), [Gerold the Golden](wiki:Gerold_Lannister).(wiki:Lord of Goldengrove.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord [Mathis Rowan](wiki:Mathis_Rowan) rides with King [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Lord of Goldengrove.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) After Renly's death, Mathis joins his liege lord, Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), and the [Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell)-[Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) alliance at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Lord of Goldengrove.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Lord Mathis is among the lords presented to King [Joffrey I](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) at the award ceremony after the battle.(wiki:Lord of Goldengrove.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) grants him an advisory seat on the [small council](wiki:Small_council).(wiki:Lord of Goldengrove.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Mathis dance with [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) at the feast after her wedding to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Lord of Goldengrove.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Mathis gives Joffrey a red silk pavilion as a wedding gift.(wiki:Lord of Goldengrove.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After the death of her father, Queen [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) revokes Mathis's seat at the small council.(wiki:Lord of Goldengrove.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Mathis attends the wedding of King [Tommen I](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Lord of Goldengrove.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) Mathis later marches with Lord Tyrell to lay siege to [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End).(wiki:Lord of Goldengrove.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
