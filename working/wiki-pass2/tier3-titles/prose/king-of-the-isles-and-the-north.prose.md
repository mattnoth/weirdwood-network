## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Rejecting the authority of the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), Lord [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) declares himself [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:King of the Isles and the North.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:King of the Isles and the North.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Since [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), the [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), is fighting south of the [Neck](wiki:Neck), the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) invade the [north](wiki:North) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). Balon claims the region by right of conquest and styles himself King of the Isles and the North.(wiki:King of the Isles and the North.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Balon styles himself King of the Iron Islands and the North, [King of Salt and Rock](wiki:King_of_Salt_and_Rock), [Son of the Sea Wind](wiki:Son_of_the_Sea_Wind), and [Lord Reaper of Pyke](wiki:Lord_Reaper_of_Pyke).(wiki:King of the Isles and the North.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) He dies after falling from [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), however.(wiki:King of the Isles and the North.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Most of the ironborn withdraw from the north to attend a [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk) to choose Balon's successor.(wiki:King of the Isles and the North.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) His brother, [Euron Crow's Eye](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), is chosen after announcing he intends to conquer all of Westeros.(wiki:King of the Isles and the North.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Euron styles himself King of the Isles and the North.(wiki:King of the Isles and the North.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), the new [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), recovers [Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin) from its ironborn garrison(wiki:King of the Isles and the North.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte) is freed by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon),(wiki:King of the Isles and the North.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) leaving the [Dagmer Cleftjaw](wiki:Dagmer_Cleftjaw)-held [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Capture_of_Torrhen's_Square) as the only remaining ironborn possession in the north.(wiki:King of the Isles and the North.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
