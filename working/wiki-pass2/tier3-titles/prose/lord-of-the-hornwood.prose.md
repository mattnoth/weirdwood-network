## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lord [Halys Hornwood](wiki:Halys_Hornwood) and his son and heir, [Daryn](wiki:Daryn_Hornwood), are killed at the [Battle of the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_of_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:Lord of the Hornwood.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord Hornwood's widow, Lady [Donella](wiki:Donella_Hornwood), becomes Lady of the Hornwood after the deaths of her husband and son. The Hornwood succession is discussed at the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) considers marrying himself or his son [Wendel](wiki:Wendel_Manderly) to Lady Donella, and claim her lands. Lord [Leobald Tallhart](wiki:Leobald_Tallhart) proposes that his younger son, [Beren](wiki:Beren_Tallhart), be made Lord of the Hornwood and adopt the Hornwood name, as his wife [Berena](wiki:Berena_Hornwood) is the sister of Lord Halys. The [Flints](wiki:House_Flint) and [Karstarks](wiki:House_Karstark) are said to have ties to the Hornwood in the female line, as well. Prince [Brandon Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) considers [Larence Snow](wiki:Larence_Snow) as a possible candidate, the [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son of the late Lord Hornwood.(wiki:Lord of the Hornwood.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
[Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), the Bastard of [Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), later claims the lands of House Hornwood after forcibly marrying Lady Donella. Ramsay then starves Donella to death in a locked tower.(wiki:Lord of the Hornwood.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The now-legitimized Ramsay Bolton styles himself Lord of the Hornwood during his marriage to "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)" (actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Lord of the Hornwood.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Lord of the Hornwood.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
