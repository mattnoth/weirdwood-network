## Origins

The Bower was built as a vast timber castle, presumably in the [Dawn Age](wiki:Dawn_Age), since it was said to be made entirely from [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood).(wiki:Greenfield.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Greenfield.cite_ref-4) At some point, the name of the castle was changed to Greenfield.(wiki:Greenfield.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
