## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) boards the Titan's Daughter in [Saltpans](wiki:Saltpans) intending to go to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Titan's Daughter.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

The Titan's Daughter brings Arya to Braavos. Many of the crew, including Captain [Ternesio Terys](wiki:Ternesio_Terys) and his son [Yorko](wiki:Yorko_Terys), ask her the favor of remembering their names.(wiki:Titan's Daughter.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) The Titan's Daughter is spotted casting off at [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) by [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth).(wiki:Titan's Daughter.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
