## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord [Dagos Manwoody](wiki:Dagos_Manwoody) accompanies Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Lord of Kingsgrave.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Dagos is present when Tyrion comes to see Oberyn on the day of his [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat), helping the prince don his battle armor.(wiki:Lord of Kingsgrave.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Dagos is present at the feast when Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) arrives in [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) to deliver the [Mountain's skull](wiki:Mountain's_skull). He refuses to drink when [Ricasso](wiki:Ricasso) raises a toast to King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord of Kingsgrave.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
