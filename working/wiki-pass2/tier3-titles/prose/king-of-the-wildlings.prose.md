## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

In the aftermath of the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) and later his wife, [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent), attempt to integrate defeated [free folk](wiki:Free_folk), also called wildings, into the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms). Since [Gerrick Kingsblood](wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood) claims descent from [Raymun Redbeard](wiki:Raymun_Redbeard), a historical [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall),(wiki:King of the Wildlings.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Ser [Axell Florent](wiki:Axell_Florent) introduces Gerrick as the King of the Wildlings and Selyse agrees to have Gerrick's daughters marry her [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men).(wiki:King of the Wildlings.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), thinks the title is meaningless to the free folk, however, as Gerrick actually descends from Raymun's younger brother.(wiki:King of the Wildlings.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
