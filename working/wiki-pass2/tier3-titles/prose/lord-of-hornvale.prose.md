## Origins

Lord [Andros Brax](wiki:Andros_Brax) once visited [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) attempting to arrange a [marriage](wiki:Marriage) for one of his three sons to [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Tully) or [Lysa](wiki:Lysa_Tully) of [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully), the daughters of Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), [Lord Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Lord of Hornvale.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

At the start of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Lord Andros supports King [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). He is part of the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) host commanded by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) [besieging Riverrun](wiki:Battle_under_the_walls_of_Riverrun). He commands the western camp during the siege.(wiki:Lord of Hornvale.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Lord Andros drowns in the [Tumblestone](wiki:Tumblestone) during the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps) after his raft overturns and the weight of his armor takes him under. His eldest son, Ser [Tytos](wiki:Tytos_Brax), becomes Lord of Hornvale after his death, but he spends the war in captivity at the [Twins](wiki:Twins) after his capture during the [Battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Lord of Hornvale.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Freed from his captivity, Lord Tytos comes to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to escort the corpse of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) back to the [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Lord of Hornvale.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
