## Origins

During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), together with his [bastard](wiki:Bastard) half-brother [Orys Baratheon](wiki:Orys_Baratheon), defeated Lord Darklyn of Duskendale and Lord [Mooton](wiki:House_Mooton) of [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) in his [first test](wiki:Aegon's_first_test). Lord Darklyn died during the battle and was succeeded by his son, who yielded to Aegon.(wiki:Lord of Duskendale.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
When the realm began to turn against King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) in favor of his nephew, Prince [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), [Lord Darklyn](wiki:Lord_Darklyn_(father_of_Jonquil_Darke)) was among the score of lesser lords who gathered at the King's command in [48 AC](wiki:48_AC).(wiki:Lord of Duskendale.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Along with Lords [Towers](wiki:Jordan_Towers) and [Staunton](wiki:House_Staunton), Lord Darklyn yielded the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) to Jaehaerys, Princess [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)), and Princess [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen). The three lords were sent to the [black cells](wiki:Black_cells), but were eventually pardoned by King Jaehaerys I after surrendering some of their land.(wiki:Lord of Duskendale.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Several lords formerly loyal to Maegor traveled to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)) to talk to the King while he resided there during his minority, including Lord Darklyn.(wiki:Lord of Duskendale.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
By [51 AC](wiki:51_AC), Lord Darklyn had died and had been succeeded by his son. This new Lord Darklyn hosted King Jaehaerys I and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) during their [first royal progress](wiki:Royal_progress). After some angry [Septas](wiki:Septa) tried to assassinate the queen in [Jonquil's Pool](wiki:Jonquil's_Pool) at Maidenpool, it was decided that the queen needed a female [sworn shield](wiki:Sworn_shield) to protect her in places where men were not allowed to go. Lord Darklyn sent his bastard half-sister, [Jonquil Darke](wiki:Jonquil_Darke), to court as per the king's request.(wiki:Lord of Duskendale.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
In [72 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), a young Lord Darklyn married a daughter of Lord [Theomore Manderly](wiki:Theomore_Manderly) of [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor).(wiki:Lord of Duskendale.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord [Gunthor Darklyn](wiki:Gunthor_Darklyn) attended [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen)'s coronation and sat on her [black council](wiki:Black_council); in retaliation [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen)'s [Hand](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole), beheaded Gunthor after the [sack of Duskendale](wiki:Sack_of_Duskendale).(wiki:Lord of Duskendale.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) After the death of her husband, Lady [Meredyth Darklyn](wiki:Meredyth_Darklyn), led the family for the rest of the war.(wiki:Lord of Duskendale.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) She briefly sheltered Queen Rhaenyra after she fled [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Lord of Duskendale.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
When Lord [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers) marched on [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) to quash the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion), among the lords who came in support was Lord Darklyn.(wiki:Lord of Duskendale.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
In [277 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Lord [Denys Darklyn](wiki:Denys_Darklyn) asked King [Aerys II](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) for certain rights for his citizens and a new town charter for Duskendale. The king refused and Denys refused to pay taxes to the crown. This led to an escalation of events that saw the capture of Aerys.(wiki:Lord of Duskendale.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)(wiki:Lord of Duskendale.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Denys remained defiant despite the army led by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), Aerys's Hand, camped outside his walls. The Defiance ended when Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) sneaked into the Dun Fort, and rescued his king. Denys opened his gates and begged for mercy but his entire family was executed on Aerys's orders.(wiki:Lord of Duskendale.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)(wiki:Lord of Duskendale.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
During a feast thrown by Aerys II when [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) first arrived at court, Lord Rykker made fun of Tywin.(wiki:Lord of Duskendale.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
