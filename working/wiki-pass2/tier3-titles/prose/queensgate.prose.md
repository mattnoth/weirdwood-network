## Origins

Six hundred years ago, the commanders of the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort) and Snowgate went to war with each other.(wiki:Queensgate.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
During Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen)'s visit with [Silverwing](wiki:Silverwing)(wiki:Queensgate.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) in [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), [Benton Glover](wiki:Benton_Glover) escorted her from [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) past Snowgate to the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort). Alysanne provided jewels to pay for the construction of [Deep Lake](wiki:Deep_Lake), so [Lothor Burley](wiki:Lothor_Burley), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), renamed Snowgate to Queensgate in her honor.(wiki:Queensgate.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Queensgate was overwhelmed by three thousand [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) led by [Sylas the Grim](wiki:Sylas_the_Grim) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC). Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark) hunted down the wildlings with the aid of [Flints](wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains), [Glovers](wiki:House_Glover), [Norreys](wiki:House_Norrey), and [rangers](wiki:Rangers).(wiki:Queensgate.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
