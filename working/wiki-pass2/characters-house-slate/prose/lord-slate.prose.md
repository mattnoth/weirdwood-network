## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Lord Slate sent men south with [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) at the start of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Lord_Slate.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) It is unknown if he went with them or not.
