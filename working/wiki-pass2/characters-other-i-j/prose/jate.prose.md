## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Jate serves with the force gathered to besiege [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun). After Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) escapes Riverrun, Jate is sent north under the command of Ser [Dermot of the Rainwood](wiki:Dermot) to search for him. While Jate is on picket duty, the camp is attacked by [wolves](wiki:Wolves). Jate falls victim to the wolves, but informes Ser Dermot of seeing a [giant she-wolf](wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf)) leading the pack before dying.(wiki:Jate.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
