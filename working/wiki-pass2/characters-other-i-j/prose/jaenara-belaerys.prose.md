## Origins

Jaenara flew her [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Terrax](wiki:Terrax), farther south in [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos) than anyone had gone before. She returned to the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) after three years, having found only endless jungle, deserts, and mountains.
