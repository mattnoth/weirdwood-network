## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Jhaqo is the second member of [Drogo](wiki:Drogo)'s [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) to declare himself *[khal](wiki:Khal)* after Drogo's [illness](wiki:Illness). His *khalasar* consists of twenty thousand riders. [Mago](wiki:Mago) becomes one of his [bloodriders](wiki:Bloodrider).(wiki:Jhaqo.cite_ref-Ragot68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Before departing, Mago steals [Eroeh](wiki:Eroeh), a girl [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) had protected from him. Mago rapes her and gives her to Jhaqo to rape as well. The girl is then raped by six of Jhaqo's other men before having her throat slit. When Daenerys learns of Eroeh's fate, she promises that Jhaqo and Mago will pay for their crime.(wiki:Jhaqo.cite_ref-Ragot68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Jhaqo's *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* discovers [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) and her [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) in the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea).(wiki:Jhaqo.cite_ref-Radwd71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
