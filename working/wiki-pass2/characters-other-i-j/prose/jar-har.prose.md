## Origins

Under Har's rule, the Golden Empire began the apex of its power with the conquest of [Leng](wiki:Leng). He had at least two sons: [Jar Joq](wiki:Jar_Joq), his successor, and [Jar Han](wiki:Jar_Han).(wiki:Jar_Har.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

The periodic slaughter of foreigners on Leng was ended by Jar Har. He also sent warriors to the subterranean ruins of Leng, though they returned mad, or not at all. Thus he finally decreed the vast underground ruins of the island should be sealed up and forgotten and entering such places be punished with torture and death.(wiki:Jar_Har.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_leng.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
