## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Jax is one of the sixty-three [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) that [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) takes to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) from [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town). He and [Leathers](wiki:Leathers) are the first of them to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). They swear their vows with four other recruits ([Horse](wiki:Hareth_(Mole's_Town)), [Satin](wiki:Satin), [Arron](wiki:Arron), and [Emrick](wiki:Emrick)) at the grove of [weirwoods](wiki:Weirwood) north of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), inside the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest). They are escorted by Jon and ten rangers. Jon, [Iron Emmett](wiki:Iron_Emmett), and [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) weigh each recruit and assign them to an order, with Jax assigned to the rangers.(wiki:Jax.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
