## Narrative Arc

The wildling band led by [Styr](wiki:Styr) and [Jarl](wiki:Jarl) stay in a [cave near Greyguard](wiki:Cave_near_Greyguard) the night before the planned attempt to scale the [Wall](wiki:Wall). [Jon](wiki:Jon_Snow) tells [Ghost](wiki:Ghost) that he cannot come with him, and that they will meet again at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).
[Styr](wiki:Styr) and [Jarl](wiki:Jarl) force Jon to tell themmore about the patrols the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) use along the Wall. [Jarl](wiki:Jarl) and Styr plan to climb the Wall between [Stonedoor](wiki:Stonedoor) and [Greyguard](wiki:Greyguard), where a hill rests against the Wall, making for a shorter climb.
Later, Jon and [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte) spend time in a cavern which supposedly leads from the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest) all the way beneath the Wall and beyond, but no one since [Gendel](wiki:Gendel) and [Gorne](wiki:Gorne), the legendary [wildling](wiki:Wildling) brother kings, knows the way. As they make love, Ygritte asks Jon to stay with her and hide in this cavern forever.
