## Origins

[Lord Ashford](wiki:Lord_Ashford) staged the tourney to celebrate [his daughter's](wiki:Lord_Ashford's_daughter) thirteenth [name day](wiki:Name_day). The fair maid was the [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty) and would have five champions to defend her honor. All other entrants were the challengers, and if anyone defeated a champion, they would take their place as the new champion. After three days of jousting, the champions would determine if Lord Ashford's daughter retained her title or if another would wear it.(wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) According to a semi-canon source, Lord Ashford hoped to curry favor with Prince [Baelor Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)) by hosting the tourney.(wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow.cite_ref-4)
Instead of competing in the tourney, Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) escaped his escort and hid with his younger brother, Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen), at an [inn near Ashford](wiki:Inn_near_Ashford). Aegon became acquainted with Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) at the inn.(wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### The Trial of Seven[ ]

An altercation occurred between Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) and Prince [Aerion Targaryen](wiki:Aerion_Targaryen) when the prince assaulted a girl, [Tanselle](wiki:Tanselle). This resulted in Duncan striking the prince, claiming that he was only following the [knight](wiki:Knight)'s vows to defend the weak. Aerion demanded a [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven) to clear his name. Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) also claimed to have been injured by Duncan, so he made a claim as well. The two sides met on [Ashford Meadow](wiki:Ashford_Meadow).(wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
Duncan made Aerion and Daeron withdraw their claims, proving his innocence. However, Humfrey Beesbury was slain by Donnel during the fight, and Willem had to be carried from the field. Baelor died from a blow to his head, likely given by his brother, Maekar. Humfrey Hardyng also died afterwards from wounds received in the skirmish.(wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
Maekar deeply regretted having caused Baelor's death, and each year he observed the anniversary of his brother's passing.(wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
