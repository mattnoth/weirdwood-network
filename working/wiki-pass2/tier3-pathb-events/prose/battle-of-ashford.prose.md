## Origins

With the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) united behind him following the [battles at Summerhall](wiki:Battles_at_Summerhall), Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_Baratheon) left his brother [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) to hold [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) and advanced west to [Ashford](wiki:Ashford).(wiki:Battle_of_Ashford.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Ashford, located within the [Reach](wiki:Reach), was sworn to [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) and was likely a loyalist holding. It is possible that Robert tried to take Ashford to secure the stormlands' western flank against the threat of the [Tyrells](wiki:House_Tyrell) (who could muster the largest army in the realm), or he may have been outmaneuvered by Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly).

## Narrative Arc

The battle occurred when the van of the Tyrell army under command of Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) ran into Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_Baratheon)'s forces. Randyll's forces overran Robert's army, and Robert was forced to withdraw from the field before the main force of the Tyrell host had joined the battle.(wiki:Battle_of_Ashford.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
[Lord Cafferen](wiki:Lord_Cafferen), a former [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) loyalist who had joined the rebels after his defeat at [Summerhall](wiki:Battles_at_Summerhall), was cut down by Randyll whilst fighting for Robert. Randyll had his head sent to King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Battle_of_Ashford.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) On the Reach side, one casualty of note was Lord Mace's cousin, Ser [Quentin Tyrell](wiki:Quentin_Tyrell).

## Aftermath

Faced with the large numbers of the main Tyrell force, Robert had no choice but to withdraw from the south and attempt to link up with his allies in the north. To this end, he force-marched his troops north towards the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). Robert's withdrawal from the stormlands allowed the victorious Tyrell army to invade the stormlands and lay [siege to Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End). However, the result of the battle was marked by [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) as indecisive.(wiki:Battle_of_Ashford.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
