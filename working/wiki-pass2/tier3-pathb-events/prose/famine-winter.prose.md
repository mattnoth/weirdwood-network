## Origins

King [Harmund III Hoare](wiki:Harmund_III_Hoare) and his mother, [Lelia Lannister](wiki:Lelia_Lannister), were mutilated when the [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands) was deposed by the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn). This led to twelve years of warfare between the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) and the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands), which left the islands defeated and impoverished.
