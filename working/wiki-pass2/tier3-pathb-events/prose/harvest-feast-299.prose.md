## Aftermath

All of the lords and knights depart Winterfell within a day or two after the feast, though the Reed siblings remain at the castle with Bran.(wiki:Harvest_feast_(299).cite_ref-Racok28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
While returning to [Hornwood](wiki:Hornwood), Lady Hornwood is abducted by Ramsay Snow, leading to [fighting in the Hornwood](wiki:Fighting_in_the_Hornwood) between Houses Bolton and Manderly.(wiki:Harvest_feast_(299).cite_ref-Racok28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
