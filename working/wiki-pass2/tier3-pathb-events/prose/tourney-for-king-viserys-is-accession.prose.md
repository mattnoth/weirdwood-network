## Origins

The twenty-two-year-old Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) defeated Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen), the brother of Viserys, in the [melee](wiki:Melee) by knocking aside [Dark Sister](wiki:Dark_Sister) with his [morningstar](wiki:Armament). He then gave the [victor's laurel](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty) to the seven-year-old Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and asked to wear her favor in the lists. Criston subsequently became a favorite at court and joined the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) the following year taking the slot left vacant by the death of Ser [Ryam Redwyne](wiki:Ryam_Redwyne).(wiki:Tourney_for_King_Viserys_I's_accession.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
