## Origins

Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark), [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), marched south during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) in support of first Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and then her son, King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen). Cregan's army, which is said to have numbered between eight thousand and twenty thousand men,(wiki:Widow_Fairs.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) arrived at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) after the death of King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) and the end of fighting.(wiki:Widow_Fairs.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Many of the northerners were childless and homeless men, unwed men, old men, and younger sons,(wiki:Widow_Fairs.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) people who might become burdens in the upcoming [winter](wiki:Winter) if they returned north. [Alysanne Blackwood](wiki:Alysanne_Blackwood), who became [betrothed](wiki:Marriage) to Lord Cregan during the [Hour of the Wolf](wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf), suggested that northmen remain in the south to help rebuild the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), as many widows had lost their husbands during the war.(wiki:Widow_Fairs.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
More than a thousand northmen returned to the riverlands with Cregan and Alysanne. Hundreds of women met their husbands in Widow Fairs held at [Fairmarket](wiki:Fairmarket), [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall), [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept), and the [Twins](wiki:Twins). Many men who did not want to marry at that time took service as guards and [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms), while some became criminals. Houses [Blackwood](wiki:Blackwood) and [Tully](wiki:House_Tully) in particular benefited from the northmen, who revived worship of the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods) in the south. Other northmen helped found two [free companies](wiki:Free_companies) in [Essos](wiki:Essos), the [Wolf Pack](wiki:Wolf_Pack) and [Stormbreakers](wiki:Stormbreakers).(wiki:Widow_Fairs.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
