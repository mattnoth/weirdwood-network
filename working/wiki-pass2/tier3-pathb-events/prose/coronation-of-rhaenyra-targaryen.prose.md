## Origins

King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) chose Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), his daughter by his first queen, [Aemma Arryn](wiki:Aemma_Arryn), as his preferred heir over Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), the king's eldest son by his second queen, [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower).(wiki:Coronation_of_Rhaenyra_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) After Viserys died in his sleep within the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) in [129 AC](wiki:129_AC), Alicent concealed the king's death from the public and gathered support in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for her son, Aegon the Elder, against the claim of Rhaenyra, who was pregnant at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). Aegon II was soon [crowned at the Dragonpit](wiki:Coronation_of_Aegon_II_Targaryen) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with [the crown](wiki:Crown) of King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Coronation_of_Rhaenyra_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
When [Rhaenyra](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) learned about the death of her father and the betrayal of her siblings, her anger made her go into labor which lasted for three days and brought forth a stillborn, deformed girl, whom she named [Visenya](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daemon)). In answer to Aegon's betrayal and the death of her daughter, Rhaenyra swore revenge. She met with her chief supporters, the so-called [black council](wiki:Black_council), and made plans for her own coronation. Ser [Steffon Darklyn](wiki:Steffon_Darklyn) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), choosing Rhaenyra over Aegon, fled to Dragonstone to give Rhaenyra [the crown](wiki:Crown) of her father.(wiki:Coronation_of_Rhaenyra_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Aftermath

Aegon II became outraged after learning of his half sister's coronation, but was convinced to allow Grand Maester [Orwyle](wiki:Orwyle) to travel under a peace banner to meet with Rhaenyra. Orwyle's mission was unsuccessful, however, and civil war broke out in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) with the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Coronation_of_Rhaenyra_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) Lord [Larys Strong](wiki:Larys_Strong) gathered a list of lords who attended Rhaenyra's coronation and sat on her council.(wiki:Coronation_of_Rhaenyra_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
