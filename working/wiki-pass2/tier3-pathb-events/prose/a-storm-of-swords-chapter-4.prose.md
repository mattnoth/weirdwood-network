## Narrative Arc

[Bronn](wiki:Bronn) visits [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), who is recovering from his wounds. He finds that much has changed in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) under [his father](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)’s rule. He also realizes that his father has seen to it that Tyrion received little of the credit for saving the city. Bronn has been knighted, and now goes by Ser Bronn of the Blackwater. Lord [Jacelyn Bywater](wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater) was killed by his own men during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), and Ser [Addam Marbrand](wiki:Addam_Marbrand) was named Commander of the [City Watch](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing). [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) has returned [Tommen](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) to the city, and the [Kettleblacks](wiki:House_Kettleblack), now all [knights](wiki:Knight), are no longer working for Tyrion. His cousin [Tyrek](wiki:Tyrek_Lannister) still has not been found, and is feared dead. Additionally, Tyrion's [mountain clansmen](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) have been chased out of the city. Some remain in the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood), but others return to the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn). Tyrion asks Bronn to learn more about Ser [Mandon Moore](wiki:Mandon_Moore), trying to discover if his sister sent the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) to kill him.
Tyrion forces himself to dress and visit his father, despite his near captivity in [Maegor's Holdfast](wiki:Maegor's_Holdfast) by his sister. Lord Tywin reveals that some battles are won by quills and ravens, and is in the process of composing letters. The [Hand](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) tells his son that Ser [Helman Tallhart](wiki:Helman_Tallhart) and [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover) are marching on [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale), but he has sent Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) and Ser [Gregor](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) to deal with them. Tyrion seeks a little gratitude from his father, but Lord Tywin tells him, "[Mummers](wiki:Mummer) and [monkeys](wiki:Monkey) require applause." Tyrion states that he wants [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), as he is heir by rights since [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) is a sworn brother of the Kingsguard. Tywin will never let Tyrion have the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister)’s ancestral seat, however, and warns him that he will hang the next whore Tyrion takes to bed.
