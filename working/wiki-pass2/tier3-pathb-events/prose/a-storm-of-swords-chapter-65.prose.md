## Narrative Arc

[Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark) dreams that she is running with a pack of wolves every night, but by day she is still [Sandor](wiki:Sandor_Clegane)’s captive. The [Hound](wiki:Hound) hit her with the flat of his axe to keep her from entering the [Twins](wiki:Twins). Sandor now intends to take her to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) and ransom her to Lady [Lysa](wiki:Lysa_Arryn). That night, Arya dreams the wolf dream again, and she watches as the other wolves feast on corpses by the river. She follows a scent that leads her to [her mother](wiki:Catelyn_Stark)’s body, and pulls it from the river. Looking at the corpse, Arya’s wolf-self thinks "Rise. Rise and eat and run with us." Then, the pack notices men approaching and flees. Arya wakes up. At some point they encounter a survivor from the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), a bowman in service to Ser [Marq Piper](wiki:Marq_Piper). The man is injured, and they give him water, then Sandor gives him [the gift of mercy](wiki:The_gift_of_mercy). Later, she and Sandor take on work in a village in the foothill of the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon), and learn that the [Vale mountain clans](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) have grown bolder and now carry steel. Realizing they will never reach the Eyrie, Clegane decides to head back to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) and attempt to ransom Arya to the [Blackfish](wiki:Brynden_Tully). Arya tells the Hound that he has lost his belly for fighting since he ran from the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), but he just laughs at her.
