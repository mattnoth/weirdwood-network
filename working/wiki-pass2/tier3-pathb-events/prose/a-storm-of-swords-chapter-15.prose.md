## Narrative Arc

[Jon](wiki:Jon_Snow) is observing [Mance](wiki:Mance_Rayder)'s host, taking note of all the [giants](wiki:Giants) and mammoths that make up the army. [Tormund](wiki:Tormund) is telling Jon some tall tales about himself, when the eagle that was once [Orell](wiki:Orell) rakes Jon's face. [Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt) has arrived to bring Jon before Mance, this time at the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men). The king confronts Jon about how many men were at the Fist, and [who](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) led. Jon, seeing how many of the [Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) died here and realizing Mance may kill him for lying, tells the truth. The situation is still tense. Jon feels that Mance may still have him killed because Jon lied to him previously. Briefly he thinks about attacking Mance but [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte) saves him by telling them that they are lovers. The [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) respect any man who steals his woman, and Rayder informs Jon that he will be leaving with [Styr](wiki:Styr) and [Jarl](wiki:Jarl) on the morrow to climb the [Wall](wiki:Wall). That night, Jon and Ygritte share a bed together.
