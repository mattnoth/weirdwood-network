## Origins

The title derives from a great tournament held in [111 AC](wiki:111_AC) at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) on the fifth anniversary of King [Viserys I's](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) marriage to Queen [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower), his second wife.(wiki:Blacks.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
At the opening feast, Queen Alicent wore a green gown, whilst Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), Viserys's daughter by his first wife, dressed dramatically in Targaryen red and black. Note was taken, and thereafter it became the custom to refer to "[greens](wiki:Greens)" and "blacks" when talking of the queen's party and the party of the princess.(wiki:Blacks.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
