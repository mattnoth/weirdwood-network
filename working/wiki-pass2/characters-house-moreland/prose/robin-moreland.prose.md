## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Robin is among the lords of the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) who escort the corpse of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) back to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).
