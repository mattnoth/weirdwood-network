## Origins

[House Stokeworth](wiki:House_Stokeworth) [surrendered](wiki:Submission_of_Stokeworth) their castle during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest) after [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) burned its roofs with [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar).(wiki:Stokeworth.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Lord Stokeworth fled back to his castle after the death of King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Stokeworth.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) began his first [progress](wiki:Progress) in [51 AC](wiki:51_AC) with visits to Stokeworth, [Rosby](wiki:Rosby), and [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale).(wiki:Stokeworth.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) ordered the execution of Lord Stokeworth for repeatedly changing his loyalty between herself and her rival, King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen). Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) suggested marrying Lord Stokeworth's daughter to [Ulf White](wiki:Ulf_White), but Rhaenyra decided to award the inheritance to Lord Stokeworth's son.(wiki:Stokeworth.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) When Rhaenyra fled the capital after the [Storming of the Dragonpit](wiki:Storming_of_the_Dragonpit), she was allowed to stay at Stokeworth for one night by the castle's castellan.(wiki:Stokeworth.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Lord [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon) forced Stokeworth to submit after Aegon II regained the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). Stokeworth levies joined Borros's host, but they abandoned him during the [Battle of the Kingsroad](wiki:Battle_of_the_Kingsroad).(wiki:Stokeworth.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Stokeworth raised the banner of [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) after the death of Aegon II.(wiki:Stokeworth.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Stokeworth is among the castles which receive letters from [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Stokeworth.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Together with [Rosby](wiki:Rosby), the lands of Stokeworth supply half of the food to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Stokeworth.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) [Falyse Stokeworth](wiki:Falyse_Stokeworth) arrives at the capital with a small troop from Stokeworth.(wiki:Stokeworth.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Falyse's younger sister, the pregnant [Lollys Stokeworth](wiki:Lollys_Stokeworth), weds Ser [Bronn of the Blackwater](wiki:Bronn).(wiki:Stokeworth.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Lollys's [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son, [Tyrion Tanner](wiki:Tyrion_Tanner), is born at Stokeworth.(wiki:Stokeworth.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) convinces Falyse and her husband, Ser [Balman Byrch](wiki:Balman_Byrch), to arrange Bronn's death.(wiki:Stokeworth.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) After killing Balman in a duel, Bronn threatens to give Falyse to Stokeworth's garrison. She flees to King's Landing and asks Cersei for aid in retaking Stokeworth, but the queen instead sends Falyse to [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn) in the [black cells](wiki:Black_cells).(wiki:Stokeworth.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) When Lady [Tanda Stokeworth](wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth) dies from a chill, her daughter Lollys becomes Lady Stokeworth with Bronn as her lord.(wiki:Stokeworth.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
