## Origins

Lord Alyn was named [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) by King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) after the death of Ser [Osmund Strong](wiki:Osmund_Strong), the previous Hand, in [34 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). When Aegon ordered the [Aegonfort](wiki:Aegonfort) torn down and a stone castle to be built in its place in [35 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), he assigned the task to Alyn and Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen), while he moved to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) with the rest of his court.(wiki:Alyn_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Lord Alyn brought his daughter [Samantha](wiki:Samantha_Stokeworth) to court, who became a close friend and companion of Princess [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)).(wiki:Alyn_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Aegon the Conqueror died at Dragonstone in [37 AC](wiki:37_AC) and Alyn remained as Hand for his son and heir, King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen). Upon Aenys' coronation, rebellions rose throughout the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms). The first of these challenges was by a bandit [outlaw](wiki:Outlaw) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) known as [Harren the Red](wiki:Harren_the_Red), who claimed to be a grandson of King [Harren the Black](wiki:Harren_Hoare). First, Aenys commanded Lord Stokeworth to take a fleet and army to the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) to put down a rebellion instigated by [Jonos Arryn](wiki:Jonos_Arryn) and restore his brother [Ronnel](wiki:Ronnel_Arryn_(king)) to his lordship.(wiki:Alyn_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

As Alyn was about to set sail to the Vale, Aenys countermanded the order, fearing that Stokeworth's departure would leave [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) undefended. Instead, Alyn was sent to the riverlands with a few hundred men to hunt down Harren the Red. Alyn personally led the campaign against Red Harren and successfully caught and cornered the outlaw king in a village west of the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye). In the final fight, Harren killed Alyn but was subsequently slain by Alyn's squire, [Bernarr Brune](wiki:Bernarr_Brune). A grateful King Aenys conferred [knighthood](wiki:Knight) on Brune.(wiki:Alyn_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)(wiki:Alyn_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aenys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
