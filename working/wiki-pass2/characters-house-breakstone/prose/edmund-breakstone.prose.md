## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter

Ser Edmund has traveled to the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) to participate in the tourney to select the membership of the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights). During the feast, in light of the splendid gifts for all competitors - as well as their attending relatives and friends - Ser Edmund comments that their host, Lord [Nestor Royce](wiki:Nestor_Royce), has an open hand.(wiki:Edmund_Breakstone.cite_ref-Winds_1-1)
