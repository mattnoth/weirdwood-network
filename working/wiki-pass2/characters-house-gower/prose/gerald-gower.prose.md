## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

After the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), where Ser Gerald fought well, Gerald is among those who question [the new faith](wiki:R'hllor) that [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) has joined. [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) seeks Gerald out and befriends him.(wiki:Gerald_Gower.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Gerald is among Lord Davos's men when he ships [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm) out of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Gerald_Gower.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
