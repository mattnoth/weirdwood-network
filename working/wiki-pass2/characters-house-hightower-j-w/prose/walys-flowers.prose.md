## Origins

Walys was born a [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son of a [Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) girl and an [archmaester](wiki:Archmaester) of the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel). After forging his chain, he served at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) when Lord [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark) was head of [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark).(wiki:Walys_Flowers.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
