## Origins

When [Damon Hightower](wiki:Damon_Hightower), [Lord of the Hightower](wiki:Lord_of_the_Hightower), died of a bad belly, Robeson became [regent](wiki:Regent) for Damon's newborn son, [Triston](wiki:Triston_Hightower). Robeson ruled Oldtown in all but name for twenty years and ultimately became the first [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon).
