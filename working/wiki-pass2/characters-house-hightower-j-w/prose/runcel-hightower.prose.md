## Origins

Some five hundred years ago,(wiki:Runcel_Hightower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_wall_and_beyond:_the_night.27s_watch.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Lord Commander Runcel forgot his vows and attempted to bequeath [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) and the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) to his [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son. Runcel's ambitions almost destroyed the Night's Watch,(wiki:Runcel_Hightower.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) but his efforts to subvert the traditional electoral system of the Night's Watch ultimately failed.(wiki:Runcel_Hightower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_wall_and_beyond:_the_night.27s_watch.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
