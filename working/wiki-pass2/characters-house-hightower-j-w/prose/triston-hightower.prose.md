## Origins

Triston was the son of Lord [Damon Hightower](wiki:Damon_Hightower), who died when Triston was newly born. Triston was raised and trained by Septon [Robeson](wiki:Robeson), who ruled in all but name for twenty years and ultimately became the first [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon). Lord Triston raised the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) in Robeson's honor after his passing.
