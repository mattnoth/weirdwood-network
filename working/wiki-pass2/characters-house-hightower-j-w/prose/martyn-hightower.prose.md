## Origins

Martyn inherited the [Hightower](wiki:Hightower) in [41 AC](wiki:41_AC), following the death of his father, Lord [Manfred Hightower](wiki:Manfred_Hightower_(son_of_Addam)).(wiki:Martyn_Hightower.cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

In [43 AC](wiki:43_AC)(wiki:Martyn_Hightower.cite_ref-5) when King Maegor, Queen Dowager [Visenya](wiki:Visenya), their [dragons](wiki:Dragon), and the royal army made their way to Oldtown during the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising), Lord Martyn called his banners with a fire atop the Hightower. By the morning of their arrival, however, the city gates were open, and the banners of Houses [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen), [Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell), and [Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) flew atop the city walls. After word of the sudden death of the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aenys_I)) reached him, Martyn at once dispatched his own [knights](wiki:Knight) to arrest and disarm all of the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons), including his brother Ser [Morgan](wiki:Morgan_Hightower). Martyn sent his men to rouse the [Most Devout](wiki:Most_Devout) from their beds and march them at spearpoint to the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) to choose a new High Septon.(wiki:Martyn_Hightower.cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Through unspecified circumstances, by the end of Maegor's reign, Martyn died and was succeeded by Lord [Donnel Hightower](wiki:Donnel_Hightower), whose relationship to Martyn has not been specified.(wiki:Martyn_Hightower.cite_ref-Rfab5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
