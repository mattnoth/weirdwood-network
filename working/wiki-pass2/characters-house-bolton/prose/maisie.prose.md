## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

[Abel](wiki:Mance_Rayder)'s [spearwives](wiki:Spearwife) begin serving at Winterfell after the wedding of [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) to "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)", whom they are intending to rescue. When a [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) guard asks [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) what happened to the usual servants like [Jez](wiki:Jez_(serving_girl)) and Maisie, Theon says they are not attending Lady Arya because they did not keep her water warm.(wiki:Maisie.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
