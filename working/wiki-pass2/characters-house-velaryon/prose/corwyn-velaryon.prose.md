## Origins

When King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) flew their [dragons](wiki:Dragon) to [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark) and toured the shipyards, they were escorted by Corwyn and his brothers [Jorgen](wiki:Jorgen_Velaryon) and [Victor](wiki:Victor_Velaryon).(wiki:Corwyn_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

As Corwyn predeceased his father, his eldest son, Ser [Corlys](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon),(wiki:Corwyn_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rtrotd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) succeeded Lord Daemon as the [Lord of the Tides](wiki:Lord_of_the_Tides) and [Master of Driftmark](wiki:Master_of_Driftmark).(wiki:Corwyn_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Corwyn_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
