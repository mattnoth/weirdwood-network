## Appearances & Description

Wylla has blond hair, longer than her sister [Wynafryd's](wiki:Wynafryd_Manderly). She [dyes](wiki:Hair_dye) it a garish green and wears it in a braid, but leaves her eyebrows blond. She has a thin and high voice.(wiki:Wylla_Manderly.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) She is brave.(wiki:Wylla_Manderly.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

As part of the terms of [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) being pardoned and returning to the king's peace of [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), Wylla is betrothed to [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)),(wiki:Wylla_Manderly.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) a squire of [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow).(wiki:Wylla_Manderly.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

When Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) appears in the [Merman's Court](wiki:Merman's_Court) before her grandfather, Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly), Wylla stands behind him with her sister, [Wynafryd](wiki:Wynafryd_Manderly). While Wyman is skeptical of Davos's call for vengeance for the death of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Wylla pipes out her agreement. When she announces her refusal to marry Little Walder, her grandfather threatens to send her to the [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters). Wylla goes on to tell of the promises of loyalty [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) made to [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) when they were exiled from the [Reach](wiki:Reach) centuries ago. Finally, her mother, Lady [Leona](wiki:Leona_Woolfield), has to drag her from the hall.(wiki:Wylla_Manderly.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

After faking the execution of Davos and releasing him from imprisonment in the [Wolf's Den](wiki:Wolf's_Den), the scheming Wyman reveals his true feelings about the [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) and calls Wylla brave.(wiki:Wylla_Manderly.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Wylla's unwanted betrothal ends with the death of Little Walder at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Wylla_Manderly.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
