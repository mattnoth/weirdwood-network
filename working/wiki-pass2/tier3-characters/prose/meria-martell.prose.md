## Appearances & Description

By the time of the [War of Conquest](wiki:War_of_Conquest) Meria was around eighty years old, fat, blind, and almost bald. The [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King), [Argilac Durrandon](wiki:Argilac_Durrandon), called her the Yellow Toad of Dorne.(wiki:Meria_Martell.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
Despite her age and blindness, Meria's wits were not diminished, and she met the invasion of Aegon, [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen), and [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) with steadfast opposition.(wiki:Meria_Martell.cite_ref-Rfab1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) So firm was her resolve that one [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) [knight](wiki:Knight) claimed she would sooner see her people dead than alive as [slaves](wiki:Slavery) to [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen). Though her tactics successfully repelled the invading forces, some considered her cowardly and felt she fought through lies and treachery, an approach they attributed to her gender.(wiki:Meria_Martell.cite_ref-Rfab2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
