## Origins

Like his father and [grandfather](wiki:Qarlton_II_Durrandon), Monfryd V reigned in times of war against the [Andals](wiki:Andals). He defeated the [Holy Brotherhood of the Andals](wiki:Holy_Brotherhood_of_the_Andals), an alliance of seven petty kings and warlords, at the [Battle of Bronzegate](wiki:Battle_of_Bronzegate), though he lost his life in the process.(wiki:Monfryd_V_Durrandon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafandals_in_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
