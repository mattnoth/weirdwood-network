## Origins

Monfryd crossed the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) and defeated the petty kings of [House Darklyn](wiki:House_Darklyn) and [House Mooton](wiki:House_Mooton) in a series of wars and seized the port towns of [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) and [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool). He was succeeded by [Durran XI Durrandon](wiki:Durran_XI_Durrandon).(wiki:Monfryd_I_Durrandon.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf782_1-0)
