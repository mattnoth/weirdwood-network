## Origins

[Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) fathered [Mya Stone](wiki:Mya_Stone) on a common woman(wiki:Mya_Stone's_mother.cite_ref-Rawoiafmya_stone.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) while he and [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) were living at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) as [wards](wiki:Ward) of Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn). Robert made daily visits to the babe Mya, sometimes with Eddard, even after he lost interest in her mother.(wiki:Mya_Stone's_mother.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Robert never publicly acknowledged Mya, however.(wiki:Mya_Stone's_mother.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Mya has been told stories about the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance) by her mother.(wiki:Mya_Stone's_mother.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
