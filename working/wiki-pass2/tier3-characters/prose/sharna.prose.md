## Appearances & Description

Sharna is very tall, and ugly with a knobby chin.(wiki:Sharna.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) She is goodhearted, but with a sharp tongue and a cynical view of strangers.(wiki:Sharna.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Origins

Sharna and her family were displaced during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). She had two sons, one of whom was killed by [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) men, whilst the other died of the flux. She and her [husband](wiki:Husband) later adopted an orphaned [boy](wiki:Boy) whose mother had been killed by the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions). They came upon the Inn of the Kneeling Man and found the previous owners dead, so buried them and took over running the establishment.(wiki:Sharna.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Sharna is off delivering [Fern](wiki:Fern)'s baby in [Lambswold](wiki:Lambswold) when [Brienne](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth), [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), and [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) pass through. She is present though when [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), who had recently been captured by members of the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), is brought to the inn.(wiki:Sharna.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
