## Origins

During the [Andal invasion](wiki:Andal_invasion) in the [Westerlands](wiki:Westerlands), the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) [Kings of the Rock](wiki:Kings_of_the_Rock) ennobled the more powerful of the [Andal](wiki:Andal) war chiefs, but took their children as [wards](wiki:Ward), to serve as squires and pages and cupbearers in [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), and as hostages against potential treachery.(wiki:Cupbearer.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
In [105 AC](wiki:105_AC), eight-year-old Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) became the cupbearer for her father, King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen). Thereafter, the king was seldom seen without his daughter by his side.(wiki:Cupbearer.cite_ref-Rtrp.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
In [126 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), King Viserys I's youngest son Prince [Daeron](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)) was sent to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) to serve as cupbearer and squire to Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower).(wiki:Cupbearer.cite_ref-Rtrp.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) civil war, King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) offered his rival Queen [Rhaenyra](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) terms of parlay, including that her son Prince [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen) would be given a place of honor in his court as his cupbearer, but she rejected the offer.(wiki:Cupbearer.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
After Queen Rhaenyra [took King's Landing](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing), she made her son Prince [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) her cupbearer, so he might never be far from her side.(wiki:Cupbearer.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
After Prince [Duncan Targaryen](wiki:Duncan_Targaryen) broke his betrothal with the daughter of Lord [Lyonel Baratheon](wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon) in [239 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), a short rebellion ensued due to the Storm Lord's anger at his wounded pride. It ended only when Lord Lyonel was defeated in single combat, and King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) promised that his youngest daughter, [Rhaelle](wiki:Rhaelle_Targaryen), would wed Lord Lyonel's heir [Ormund](wiki:Ormund_Baratheon). To seal the bargain, Princess Rhaelle was sent to [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) to serve as Lord Lyonel's cupbearer and companion to his lady wife.(wiki:Cupbearer.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Around [252 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) was sent by his father Lord [Tytos](wiki:Tytos_Lannister) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), to serve as a cupbearer in the court of King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen).(wiki:Cupbearer.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), incognito as "Nan", is given the honor of becoming Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton)'s cupbearer for her role in freeing the northern prisoners at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Cupbearer.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) As a cupbearer, Arya dresses as a squire in Bolton livery, and performs such duties as fetching water and food and clothing, removing leeches from Roose's body, and cleaning his chambers.(wiki:Cupbearer.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Since Arya Stark has fled Harrenhal, Roose Bolton has his squire [Elmar Frey](wiki:Elmar_Frey) serve as his cupbearer at dinner with [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth).(wiki:Cupbearer.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
To mock him, King [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) has his uncle [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) serve as his cupbearer at his [wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) to [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Cupbearer.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) After Joffrey dies of [poison](wiki:Poison), this is used as evidence against Tyrion in his trial for the king's murder.(wiki:Cupbearer.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) recalls that when was he no more than ten, he was to be a [ward](wiki:Ward) of Lord [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne) at the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor), to serve as his page and cupbearer, but his cravenly behavior when faced with the bullying of Redwyne's sons and [fool](wiki:Fool) caused [his father](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) to bring him home in shame instead.(wiki:Cupbearer.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
[Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) learns that when she was a girl, she was to have been sent to [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) to be the [Archon](wiki:Archon)'s ward and cupbearer, to [secretly meet with her betrothed](wiki:Secret_marriage_pact) [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen). However, her father [Doran](wiki:Doran_Martell) abandoned that plan due to suicidal threats from his wife [Mellario](wiki:Mellario), who was already deeply upset that their son [Quentyn](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) had been sent away to be fostered.(wiki:Cupbearer.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

To stymie the nightly killings of the [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) has every noble family of Meereen who is of dubious loyalty send her a child as a hostage.(wiki:Cupbearer.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The children are made her [cupbearers](wiki:Cupbearers_of_Daenerys_Targaryen), and serve in [her chambers](wiki:Queen's_apartments_(Great_Pyramid)), serving wine and food and clothing and standing at guard at her door. Daenerys hopes having noble children as hostages will halt the killings, but it does not. However, Daenerys refuses to allow any harm to come to the children. She grows fond of them all, and ignores [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq)'s counsel to kill one for every death done by the Sons of the Harpy. Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) instructs the boys in the ways of [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) [chivalry](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Cupbearer.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
When Daenerys disappears on [Drogon](wiki:Drogon)'s back, her cupbearers continue to serve her husband, King [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq).(wiki:Cupbearer.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) After Barristan seizes control of Meereen in the queen's name, he again rejects Skahaz's advice to kill the young hostages.(wiki:Cupbearer.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) The cupbearers obey Barristan's commands, as [Hand of the Queen](wiki:Hand_of_the_Queen).(wiki:Cupbearer.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
When [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) and ["Young Griff"](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)) meet [Harry Strickland](wiki:Harry_Strickland) and the rest of the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) at [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), Harry tells his squire [Watkyn](wiki:Watkyn) to serve them wine. Watkyn pats Harry's feet dry after he has finished soaking them to treat blisters.(wiki:Cupbearer.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

[Obella Sand](wiki:Obella_Sand) is sent to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear), to serve as a cupbearer for the wife of Ser [Manfrey Martell](wiki:Manfrey_Martell), castellan of the castle.(wiki:Cupbearer.cite_ref-19)
