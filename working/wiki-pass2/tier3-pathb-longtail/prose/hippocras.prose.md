## Origins

It was King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen)'s custom to take a cup of hippocras at the [hour of the bat](wiki:Named_hours).(wiki:Hippocras.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) drank hippocras during the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls).(wiki:Hippocras.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) drinks hippocras when he hosts Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Hippocras.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Roose sips hippocras prior to the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Hippocras.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Feast For Crows[ ]

Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) has [Falyse Stokeworth](wiki:Falyse_Stokeworth) and her husband, Ser [Balman Byrch](wiki:Balman_Byrch), as guests for dinner. By the time she joins them in her [solar](wiki:Solar) a flagon of hippocras is already half empty.(wiki:Hippocras.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Cersei and [Taena Merryweather](wiki:Taena_Merryweather) celebrate with hippocras after the queen comes to an arrangement with the [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow).(wiki:Hippocras.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) brings casks of hippocras with him from [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) to [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton).(wiki:Hippocras.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
