## Origins

[Westeros](wiki:Westeros) was ruled by a multitude of kings during the [Age of the Hundred Kingdoms](wiki:Age_of_the_Hundred_Kingdoms).(wiki:Kingship.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)
[House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) ruled the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) as [Kings of the Andals, the Rhoynar, and the First Men](wiki:Kings_of_the_Andals,_the_Rhoynar,_and_the_First_Men) after [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Kingship.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2) [House Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon_of_King's_Landing) succeeded the Targaryens through [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Kingship.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_robert.27s_rebellion.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), [King of the Andals, the Rhoynar, and the First Men](wiki:King_of_the_Andals,_the_Rhoynar,_and_the_First_Men), is mortally wounded by a boar,(wiki:Kingship.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) and he is succeeded by his son, King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Kingship.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End) is crowned king at [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) in opposition to his nephew.(wiki:Kingship.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Following the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) is declared [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Kingship.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
The exiled [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) dies at [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak),(wiki:Kingship.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) and his younger sister, [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), acknowledges herself as queen and the last of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen).(wiki:Kingship.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Rejecting the claims of Joffrey and Renly, [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), [Lord of Dragonstone](wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone), declares himself king.(wiki:Kingship.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) Renly is [assassinated near Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)).(wiki:Kingship.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), secedes from the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) and declares himself [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North).(wiki:Kingship.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Amid the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Balon falls to his death at [Pyke](wiki:Pyke)(wiki:Kingship.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) and Robb is killed in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Kingship.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) Joffrey dies at his [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding) and is succeeded by his younger brother, [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Kingship.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Since [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) claimed the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair) after Balon's death, [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) calls for a [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk) to decide succession in the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands).(wiki:Kingship.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Despite the efforts of Aeron, [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy), and [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), Euron is chosen king after announcing he intends to conquer all of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Kingship.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)
A new [corsair king](wiki:Corsair_king) has risen in the [Basilisk Isles](wiki:Basilisk_Isles).(wiki:Kingship.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)
Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) fails in an attempt to crown Tommen's older sister, Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon), according to [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) customs.(wiki:Kingship.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-1)(wiki:Kingship.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)
