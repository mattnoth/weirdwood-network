## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Among the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki)-pillaged statues of deities at [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) sees statues of manticores, with their barbed tails poised to strike.(wiki:Manticore.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
[Syrio Forel](wiki:Syrio_Forel) tells [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) that the menagerie of the [Sealord](wiki:Sealord) of [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) includes manticores.(wiki:Manticore.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)
In the Eastern Market of Vaes Dothrak, Daenerys sees manticores in silver cages.(wiki:Manticore.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Daenerys sees that [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) has many elaborate fountains, including those wrought in the shapes of manticores.(wiki:Manticore.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)
A [Sorrowful Man](wiki:Sorrowful_Men) attempts to kill Daenerys at the [Port of Qarth](wiki:Port_of_Qarth) using a manticore, initially presented to her as a jeweled scarab. He is thwarted by [Arstan Whitebeard](wiki:Barristan_Selmy).(wiki:Manticore.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) sees manticores among the gargoyles that adorn the fortress of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Manticore.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Feast of Crows[ ]

[Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn) claims that the poison [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) coated his spear with and used against [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) was [manticore venom](wiki:Manticore_venom), but thickened somehow, possibly with [sorcery](wiki:Sorcery). Instead of killing instantly when it reaches the heart, the modified venom leaves Clegane to die slowly and in extreme agony.(wiki:Manticore.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
[Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy)'s plunder from his reavings throughout Essos includes jade manticores.(wiki:Manticore.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sees that the gateway to the [Long Bridge](wiki:Long_Bridge) of [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) is carved with all kinds of strange creatures, including manticores.(wiki:Manticore.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
In the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), a [Brazen Beast](wiki:Brazen_Beast) guarding the gates wears a manticore mask.(wiki:Manticore.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
