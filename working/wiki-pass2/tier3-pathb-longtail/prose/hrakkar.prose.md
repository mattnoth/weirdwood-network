## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Khal](wiki:Khal) [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) and his [bloodriders](wiki:Bloodrider) hunt a hrakkar. It rakes Drogo through his leggings, scarring his leg. He returns to [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak) with the carcass slung across a packhorse.(wiki:Hrakkar.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) takes to wearing the pelt of the hrakkar that Drogo had slain.(wiki:Hrakkar.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Within the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying) in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), Daenerys has a series of visions, one of which features a white lion.(wiki:Hrakkar.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

The hrakkar pelt made by Drogo remains a favorite attire of Daenerys, its musty smell reminding her of her dead husband.(wiki:Hrakkar.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Hrakkar.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
