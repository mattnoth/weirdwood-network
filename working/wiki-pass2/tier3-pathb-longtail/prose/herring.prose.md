## Culture

Shoals of herring and great [codfish](wiki:Cod) are among the painted decorations of the [Merman's Court](wiki:Merman's_Court) at the [New Castle](wiki:New_Castle) of [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor).(wiki:Herring.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Origins

The [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos) served [ale](wiki:Ale) and herring to [Elissa Farman](wiki:Elissa_Farman) before she departed [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Herring.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Herring from [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) is served at the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast_(299)) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Herring.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), acting at Cat of the Canals, sees herring being sold at the fishmarket in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Herring.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) is served creamed herring while at [Illyrio's manse](wiki:Illyrio's_manse) in [Pentos](wiki:Pentos).(wiki:Herring.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) sees a woman offering herring with chopped onions at [Fishfoot Yard](wiki:Fishfoot_Yard) in [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor).(wiki:Herring.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Pickled herring is among the stores of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Herring.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
The great cog [Noble Lady](wiki:Noble_Lady), carrying salt [cod](wiki:Cod), [whale](wiki:Whale) oil, and pickled herring, is captured by [Iron Victory](wiki:Iron_Victory) in the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones).(wiki:Herring.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
