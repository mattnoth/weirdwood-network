## Culture

Medicinal leeches can be used by [maesters](wiki:Maesters)(wiki:Leech.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) to thin the blood and remove "bad blood".(wiki:Leech.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Maesters learn how to leech fevers at the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel).(wiki:Leech.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Healers at the [House of the Red Hands](wiki:House_of_the_Red_Hands) in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) also use leeches in their treatments.(wiki:Leech.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), [Lord of the Dreadfort](wiki:Lord_of_the_Dreadfort), is convinced that regular leechings improve a person's health, and as such frequently has himself leeched, which has led to some calling him the Leech Lord, though never to his face.(wiki:Leech.cite_ref-Rasos50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Origins

Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) attended a lecture about leechcraft at the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) during her visit to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC).(wiki:Leech.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Grand Maester [Mellos](wiki:Mellos) treated [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) with leeches after the king cut himself on the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) and lost two fingers. Grand Maester [Orwyle](wiki:Orwyle) appeared to have more success using potions and tinctures, but Viserys's improvement was short-lived.(wiki:Leech.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
[Chett](wiki:Chett) grew up in [Hag's Mire](wiki:Hag's_Mire) as the son of a leechman who sold leeches to [maesters](wiki:Maesters).(wiki:Leech.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) has himself leeched at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), which frightens his [squire](wiki:Squire), [Elmar Frey](wiki:Elmar_Frey). However, the leechings do not scare Roose's [cupbearer](wiki:Cupbearer) "Nan", the incognito [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), and he has the girl remove leeches from his chest, arms, and legs.(wiki:Leech.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn) leeches the bad blood from Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s eye as part of his treatment at Harrenhal.(wiki:Leech.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Leech.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Maester [Pylos](wiki:Pylos) leeches the bad blood from Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) after the Onion Knight is returned to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) from [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay).(wiki:Leech.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Pylos also leeches [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm), a [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son of the late King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), when the boy becomes sick with a chill.(wiki:Leech.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre), a [red priestess](wiki:Red_priest) of [Asshai](wiki:Asshai), tells [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) she can wake a [stone dragon](wiki:Stone_dragon) with Edric's [king's blood](wiki:Bloodmagic). Stannis refuses to allow Melisandre to sacrifice the boy, however, and the king instead throws leeches filled with Edric's blood into flames while stating the names of the "three false kings:" [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), and [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Leech.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) Balon falls to his death at [Pyke](wiki:Pyke),(wiki:Leech.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Robb is killed in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding),(wiki:Leech.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) and Joffrey chokes to death at his [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding).(wiki:Leech.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Davos has Edric smuggled from Dragonstone to save him from Melisandre.(wiki:Leech.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) is [poisoned](wiki:Poison) by Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) during their [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat). Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) uses leeches on the Mountain in an attempt to heal him, but all of the leeches die.(wiki:Leech.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
Maester [Colemon](wiki:Colemon) uses leeches to treat the [shaking sickness](wiki:Shaking_sickness) of Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn), but the boy dislikes the treatment.(wiki:Leech.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

According to [Aurane Waters](wiki:Aurane_Waters), Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) has lost so much blood from the [siege of Dragonstone](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone) that maesters dare not leech him.(wiki:Leech.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
