## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

As [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and the crew of the [Shy Maid](wiki:Shy_Maid) travel through the ruins of [Chroyane](wiki:Chroyane) in the Sorrows, something very peculiar happens. They sail unscathed beneath the [Bridge of Dream](wiki:Bridge_of_Dream), but despite sailing with the current of the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne) and rivers only running one way, they appear to approach the Bridge of Dream again shortly afterward.(wiki:Garin's_Curse.cite_ref-Radwd18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) If this occurs due to the lingering effects of Garin's Curse is not known. According to [Yandry](wiki:Yandry) the [Mother Rhoyne](wiki:Mother_Rhoyne) runs how she will.
