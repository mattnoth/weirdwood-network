## Origins

The [rat](wiki:Rat) pits of Flea Bottom date back to the founding of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Rat_pits.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
During Princess [Viserra Targaryen](wiki:Viserra_Targaryen)'s "one last night of laughter" before her marriage, she visited the rat pits with her companions, among other unsavory locations in King's Landing, before her death in a [horse](wiki:Horse) racing accident that night.(wiki:Rat_pits.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)
Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) spent time with his friends and his [gold cloaks](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing) in the seedy places of King's Landing, which earned him such nicknames as the "Prince of the City" and "Lord Flea Bottom".(wiki:Rat_pits.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Daemon knew the rat pits well.(wiki:Rat_pits.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
If the [Testimony](wiki:The_Testimony_of_Mushroom) of [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom) can be believed, when Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) searched for Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) to inform him of the death of his father, King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen), he eventually found the prince drunk and naked in a Flea Bottom rat pit, watching two children with filed teeth fight each other for his entertainment while another young girl fellated him. [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Munkun](wiki:Munkun)'s [account](wiki:The_Dance_of_the_Dragons,_A_True_Telling) corroborates that Aegon was "at his revels", but does not specify the details.(wiki:Rat_pits.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the [riot of King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)) started in the rat pits, among other seedy places in Flea Bottom.(wiki:Rat_pits.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)
Just like her father, Daemon, before her, Princess [Baela Targaryen](wiki:Baela_Targaryen) had a wild side to her and enjoyed spending time in the low places of King's Landing. She was known to wager [coin](wiki:Currency) and sometimes clothing in the rat pits of Flea Bottom.(wiki:Rat_pits.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2)
According to a semi-canon source, [Rorge](wiki:Rorge) owned a [pot shop](wiki:Pot_shop) or tavern in Flea Bottom that also served as a rat pit where he would stage rat fights, dog fights, and bear cub fights for his profit. At some point, he found the orphan child [Biter](wiki:Biter), and raised him ferally to compete in these fights.(wiki:Rat_pits.cite_ref-Rssm1400.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)(wiki:Rat_pits.cite_ref-Rssm1401.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) The adult Biter has teeth filed into points,(wiki:Rat_pits.cite_ref-Racok5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) and following Mushroom's account of the Flea Bottom rat pits in a previous era, this was likely done when he was a child competitor in the pits.
