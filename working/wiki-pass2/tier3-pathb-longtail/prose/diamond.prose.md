## Culture

Magister [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) has diamonds among his various rings.(wiki:Diamond.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Black diamonds are among the gems adorning the nose of [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos).(wiki:Diamond.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Origins

Many women at court dazzled in [pearls](wiki:Pearl) and diamonds during the era of King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen) and his wife, Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon).(wiki:Diamond.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

After the death of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), his heir, King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), wears a [golden](wiki:Gold) crown crusted with [rubies](wiki:Ruby) and black diamonds.(wiki:Diamond.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Joffrey's mother, Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), accompanies her black mourning gown with a veil of diamonds in her hair.(wiki:Diamond.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

King [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) wears a swordbelt studded with jagged chunks of black diamond.(wiki:Diamond.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
After the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell), [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) claims the title [Prince of Winterfell](wiki:Prince_of_Winterfell) and wears an [iron](wiki:Iron) crown with chunks of black diamond and nuggets of [gold](wiki:Gold).(wiki:Diamond.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Cersei wears a necklace of diamonds and [emeralds](wiki:Emerald) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Diamond.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) dons a golden chain studded with black diamonds for the feast at [Darry](wiki:Darry).(wiki:Diamond.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
