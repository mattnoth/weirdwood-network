## Culture

The throne room of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) is decorated with tapestries depicting hunting and battle,(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) and tapestries from Lys, Norvos, and Qohor decorate the chambers of the [small council](wiki:Small_council).(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3) The gloomy halls of the castle have faded tapestries.(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) The walls of [White Sword Tower](wiki:White_Sword_Tower) are hung with white woolen tapestries.(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Tapestries also hang in the castle's bedchambers.(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Raffc3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
The [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) is decorated with tapestries, including a dozen splendid tapestries in an arcade.(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
A tapestry at [Old Oak](wiki:Old_Oak) depicts Lord [Edgerran Oakheart](wiki:Edgerran_Oakheart) sitting with the heads of a hundred [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) piled around his feet. The castle also has tapestries depicting Ser [Olyvar Oakheart](wiki:Olyvar_Oakheart), dying beside King [Daeron I Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen) in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), and [Alester Oakheart](wiki:Alester_Oakheart) in the [Prince's Pass](wiki:Prince's_Pass), blowing a warhorn.(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
The solar of Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood) at [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall) has woolen tapestries.(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
[House Connington](wiki:House_Connington) has faded tapestries on the walls of the great hall at [Griffin's Roost](wiki:Griffin's_Roost).(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
Wool tapestries of fruits and flowers are located in [Ashford Castle](wiki:Ashford).(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
The audience chamber at [Coldmoat](wiki:Coldmoat) has tapestries depicting [tourney](wiki:Tourney) scenes and battles.(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
Tapestries are on the walls of the Hermit's Hole at the [Quiet Isle](wiki:Quiet_Isle).(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
Archmaester [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn) has faded tapestries in his chambers at the [Ravenry](wiki:Ravenry).(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

## Origins

According to legend, the [Grey King's Hall](wiki:Grey_King's_Hall) had been warmed by [Nagga](wiki:Nagga)'s living fire and on the walls hung tapestries made of silver seaweed.(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) bought tapestries and murals from the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) to decorate the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Rfab8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
Ser [Bennis of the Brown Shield](wiki:Bennis_of_the_Brown_Shield) stole coins which Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey) had hidden behind a mildewed tapestry at [Standfast](wiki:Standfast).(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1)
King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) replaced the [skulls of the Targaryen dragons](wiki:Skulls_of_the_Targaryen_dragons) in the Red Keep's throne room with hunting tapestries vivid with greens, browns, and blues.(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s hunting tapestries in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep)'s throne room are taken down after his death.(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) recalls the tapestries at [Old Oak](wiki:Old_Oak).(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) While discretely meeting Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) in the [shadow city](wiki:Shadow_city) of [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear), Arys sees a tapestry of [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria) and her [ten thousand ships](wiki:Ten_thousand_ships).(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)
After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), [Lucas Codd](wiki:Lucas_Codd) and [Quellon Humble](wiki:Quellon_Humble) tear Lord [Humfrey Hewett](wiki:Humfrey_Hewett)'s tapestries from [his hall's](wiki:Lord_Hewett's_castle) wall to serve as cloaks.(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)
Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) mentions that she received a letter from [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) beseeching her to ship him some old tapestries of Robert's.(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Later Petyr mentions to [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) that Cersei is sending him some splendid tapestries.(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)
While traveling to the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Jaime stops at [Castle Darry](wiki:Darry). He recalls that when Robert's entourage had traveled north to offer the position of [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), they had stayed at Darry both en route and returning. On the northbound stop, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) had discovered tapestries depicting all of the kings from [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen), from [Aegon I](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) to [Aerys II](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), hidden in the cellar. Tyrion joked that Robert would have made him [Lord of Darry](wiki:Lord_of_Darry) if he had revealed their existence to the king, as the Targaryens had been overthrown in [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Jaime observes that the castle's walls are still bare after it has been given to his disinterested cousin, Lord [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister).(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

While visiting [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), [Queen of Meereen](wiki:Queen_of_Meereen), the merchant prince [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos) gifts her with a beautiful tapestry of [Essos](wiki:Essos) that has been in his family vaults since before the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria). Xaro uses it in his attempt to persuade her to abandon [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) and travel by sea to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) [Freedwomen](wiki:Freedwomen) give Daenerys a tapestry which she hangs over her bed.(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)
The chambers of King [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) within Meereen's [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) are decorated with priceless tapestries depicting the glory of the [Old Empire of Ghis](wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis). The largest shows the last survivors of a defeated [Valyrian](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) army passing beneath the yoke and being chained.(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) After slaying the pit fighter [Khrazz](wiki:Khrazz), Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) finds Hizdahr hiding behind one of these tapestries, begging to be spared.(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

At the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon), Lord [Nestor Royce](wiki:Nestor_Royce) shows Lady [Waxley](wiki:House_Waxley) his prize tapestries which had belonged to King Robert and had been given to him by Lord Baelish.(wiki:Tapestry.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Balayne_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Calayne_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)
