## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) sees [spotted tigers](wiki:Spotted_tiger) during her journey through the [Forest of Qohor](wiki:Forest_of_Qohor).(wiki:Tiger.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
[Syrio Forel](wiki:Syrio_Forel) tells [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) that in the [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos)'s menagerie, he saw [tigers that carry their cubs in a pouch](wiki:Pouch_tiger).(wiki:Tiger.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Tiger.cite_ref-6)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Daenerys sees [Qartheen](wiki:Qarth) wearing tiger skins.(wiki:Tiger.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) The [Balerion](wiki:Balerion_(ship)), [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar_(ship)), and [Meraxes](wiki:Meraxes_(ship)) take on trade goods in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), including bales of costly tiger skins.(wiki:Tiger.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis)'s tiger skins are part of Daenerys's payment for the [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) in [Astapor](wiki:Astapor).(wiki:Tiger.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) claims that his brother, [Euron](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), seduced many [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) captains with valuable gifts, such as tiger pelts.(wiki:Tiger.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Some of the [Brazen Beasts](wiki:Brazen_Beasts) who escort Daenerys Targaryen to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen)'s fighting pits wear tiger masks.(wiki:Tiger.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
