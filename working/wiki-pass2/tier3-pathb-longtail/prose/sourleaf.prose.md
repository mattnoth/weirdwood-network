## Culture

Sourleaf can be chewed in a similar fashion to chewing tobacco. Chewing the leaves can dull pain,(wiki:Sourleaf.cite_ref-Racok1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) but it turns spit red(wiki:Sourleaf.cite_ref-Racok9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) or pinkish(wiki:Sourleaf.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) and can stain teeth(wiki:Sourleaf.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Sourleaf.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and fingers red.(wiki:Sourleaf.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Origins

Ser [Bennis of the Brown Shield](wiki:Bennis_of_the_Brown_Shield) was fond of chewing sourleaf.(wiki:Sourleaf.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Masha Heddle](wiki:Masha_Heddle) chews sourleaf while working at the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads).(wiki:Sourleaf.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Yoren](wiki:Yoren) has a bale of sourleaf in his wagons when he departs [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for the [Wall](wiki:Wall),(wiki:Sourleaf.cite_ref-Racok1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and the [wandering crow](wiki:Wandering_crow) often chews leaves during the journey.(wiki:Sourleaf.cite_ref-Racok9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Sourleaf.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) After Yoren strikes [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)'s thighs with a stick as punishment for her having fought with [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie), he advises her to chew sourleaf to reduce the pain. It helps Arya feel better, though she thinks the plant tastes foul.(wiki:Sourleaf.cite_ref-Racok1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) sees bales of sourleaf being loaded onto [Bride in Azure](wiki:Bride_in_Azure) at the [port of Qarth](wiki:Port_of_Qarth).

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Chett](wiki:Chett) chews sourleaf at the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men) and share some with guards on duty.(wiki:Sourleaf.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Brienne Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth) meets a [dwarf brother](wiki:Dwarf_brother) at the [Seven Swords](wiki:Seven_Swords) whose teeth have been stained red.(wiki:Sourleaf.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) sees pinkish froth on the lips of Ser [Emmon Frey](wiki:Emmon_Frey) from his sourleaf chewing.(wiki:Sourleaf.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Sourleaf.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Archmaester [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn) chews sourleaf when he meets [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly).(wiki:Sourleaf.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Snatch](wiki:Snatch), a member of the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown), has had his teeth and fingers stained red from sourleaf juice.(wiki:Sourleaf.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
