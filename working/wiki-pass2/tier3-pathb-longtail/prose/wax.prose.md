## Culture

[Wickenden](wiki:Wickenden), the seat of [House Waxley](wiki:House_Waxley) in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn), is known for their scented beeswax candles.(wiki:Wax.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[Ghiscari](wiki:Ghiscari) men shape their hair using wax, combs, and [irons](wiki:Iron).(wiki:Wax.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Sealing waxes can be found in different colors, [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) [nobles](wiki:Nobility) usually use wax the same color as their [coat of arms](wiki:Coat_of_arms). Examples include:
Black wax, used by the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch)(wiki:Wax.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Wax.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) (the [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) has a personal seal but it is not stated in the books what it looks like(wiki:Wax.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)). Black wax is also used by [House Karstark](wiki:House_Karstark).(wiki:Wax.cite_ref-TWOW_11-0)
Blue wax with a [moon](wiki:Moon)-and-[falcon](wiki:Falcon) seal, used by [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn)(wiki:Wax.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

## Origins

Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) recognized the three-headed [dragon](wiki:Dragon) seal of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) in the wax of the royal decree showned to him by Maester [Cerrick](wiki:Cerrick) and Lady [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber).(wiki:Wax.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)
During the "trade wars" between the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) of [Lys](wiki:Lys), [Myr](wiki:Myr), and [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh), the cities often grant combattants licenses to prey on ships of the other cities. Grand Maester [Merion](wiki:Merion) referred to this practise as "piracy with a wax seal".(wiki:Wax.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_the_quarrelsome_daughters:_myr.2C_lys.2C_and_tyrosh.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The officer of the [City Watch](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing) in charge of retreiving [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) from [Yoren](wiki:Yoren)'s group of recruits has a warrant ribbon with golden wax from Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister).(wiki:Wax.cite_ref-Racok5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)
Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle)'s medicines are stored in dozens of pots sealed with wax.(wiki:Wax.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) The [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) pots and jars of [Aerys's](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) day are sealed with wax and stored in stone cells beneath the [guildhall](wiki:Guildhall_of_the_Alchemists) of the [Alchemists' Guild](wiki:Alchemists'_Guild).(wiki:Wax.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)(wiki:Wax.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)
One of [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)'s task as Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton)'s [cupbearer](wiki:Cupbearer) at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) include putting the quills, inks and sealing wax in order in Roose's [solar](wiki:Solar).(wiki:Wax.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Maester [Vyman](wiki:Vyman) provides parchment, quills and sealing wax to [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Tully) so she can write to her sister, Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn).(wiki:Wax.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)
Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) finds [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan) and two [eunuchs](wiki:Eunuch) in the hold of the [Bountiful Harvest](wiki:Bountiful_Harvest) as they are making its inventory and writting it on a wax tablet with stylus.(wiki:Wax.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Ayra sees four archers outside of the [Twins](wiki:Twins) slipping waxed strings over the notches of their [longbows](wiki:Armament) as she makes her way towards the castle.(wiki:Wax.cite_ref-Rasos50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)
The new boy king, [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), enjoys putting the royal seal into the hot wax.(wiki:Wax.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)(wiki:Wax.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)

The [maester](wiki:Maester) at the [Dun Fort](wiki:Dun_Fort) inspects carefully the seal on [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth)'s royal letter stating she is on a royal mission.(wiki:Wax.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-1) Once satisfied with the authenticity of the document, he answers Brienne's questions.(wiki:Wax.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0)
