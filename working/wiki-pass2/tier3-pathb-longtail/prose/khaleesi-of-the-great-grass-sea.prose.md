## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) weds Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), becoming his [khaleesi](wiki:Khaleesi).(wiki:Khaleesi_of_the_Great_Grass_Sea.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) After the [khal](wiki:Khal) dies and [dragons](wiki:Dragon) are hatched amidst his funeral pyre, Dany becomes the leader of a remnant of his [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar).(wiki:Khaleesi_of_the_Great_Grass_Sea.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Daenerys thinks of the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea) of central [Essos](wiki:Essos) as a great grass sea.(wiki:Khaleesi_of_the_Great_Grass_Sea.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Missandei](wiki:Missandei) includes Khaleesi of Great Grass Sea among the various titles of Dany, who now rules the city of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) along [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay).(wiki:Khaleesi_of_the_Great_Grass_Sea.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
