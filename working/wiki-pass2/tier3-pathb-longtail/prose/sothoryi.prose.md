## Appearances & Description

Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel) describes the Sothoryi as massively-muscled, big-boned creatures with long arms and coarse black hair. He writes they have sloped foreheads, huge square teeth, and heavy jaws. Sothoryi have broad, flat noses that suggest snouts and thick skins brindled in patterns of brown and white, making them seem more like hogs than humans.(wiki:Sothoryi.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf834_1-0)
Sothoryi women are only able to breed with their own males. Only stillbirths, many deformed, have resulted when Sothoryi women have mated with men from [Essos](wiki:Essos) or [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Sothoryi.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf834_1-1)

## Culture

Sothoryi who live near the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea) have learned the [Trade Talk](wiki:Trade_Talk). Although they are fierce fighters, [Ghiscari](wiki:Ghiscari) find them too dim to make effective [slaves](wiki:Slaves).(wiki:Sothoryi.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf834_1-2)
The more savage and barbaric Brindled Men found further south supposedly worship dark [gods](wiki:Gods) and perform obscene rites. Many are [cannibals](wiki:Cannibalism) who eat the flesh of enemies and strangers, and more are said to be [ghouls](wiki:Ghouls) who devour their own dead.(wiki:Sothoryi.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf834_1-3)

## Origins

There are stories of other races and forgotten peoples in [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos) that were driven out, destroyed, or devoured by the Brindled Men. There are also tales of lizard men and eyeless cave-dwellers. [Maesters](wiki:Maesters) have no proof of these stories, however.(wiki:Sothoryi.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf834_1-4)
The Brindled Men destroyed the first colony of the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) in [Basilisk Point](wiki:Basilisk_Point).(wiki:Sothoryi.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf834_1-5) Similarly, during the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar) attempt to settle in [Yeen](wiki:Yeen), they faced frequent attacks from "brindled ghouls" of the jungle deeps.(wiki:Sothoryi.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf28.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Ser [Eustace Hightower](wiki:Eustace_Hightower) landed on the coast of Sothoryos after a [leviathan](wiki:Leviathan) cracked the hull of his ship, [Lady Meredith](wiki:Lady_Meredith). During the crew's year-long stay on the southern continent, they observed men that howled like [monkeys](wiki:Monkey).(wiki:Sothoryi.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) is reopened, Sothoryi are among those who are set to fight.(wiki:Sothoryi.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

The [Brindled Butcher](wiki:Brindled_Butcher) is among the Meereenese pit fighters who participate in [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy)'s attack on the [Yunkish](wiki:Yunkish) army besieging [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Sothoryi.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Bbarristan_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Cbarristan_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
