## Origins

Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel) writes that the [giants](wiki:Giants) of the [Dawn Age](wiki:Dawn_Age) had no [kings](wiki:King) or [lords](wiki:Lord).(wiki:King_of_the_Giants.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_dawn_age.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Tales of the [north](wiki:North) claim the [Great Barrow](wiki:Great_Barrow) at [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) contains the grave of the [First King](wiki:First_King) of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) or, because of the hill's size, the grave of a King of the Giants.(wiki:King_of_the_Giants.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Songs claim that [Durran the Dour](wiki:Durran_Durrandon_(the_Dour)) slew [Lun the Last](wiki:Lun_the_Last), King of the Giants, at the [Battle of Crookwater](wiki:Battle_of_Crookwater) in the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands).(wiki:King_of_the_Giants.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_durrandon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

When [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) asks if [Mag Mar Tun Doh Weg](wiki:Mag_Mar_Tun_Doh_Weg) is a king, [Tormund](wiki:Tormund) explains that [giants](wiki:Giants) have no kings.(wiki:King_of_the_Giants.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) After Mag the Mighty and [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye) slay each other during the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), Jon continues to think of Mag as the king of the giants.(wiki:King_of_the_Giants.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:King_of_the_Giants.cite_ref-Rasos76.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
