## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Benfred Tallhart](wiki:Benfred_Tallhart) raises a company of young lancers during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). After [Leobald Tallhart](wiki:Leobald_Tallhart) calls them young [rabbits](wiki:Rabbit), they dub themselves the [Wild Hares](wiki:Wild_Hares) and tie rabbitskins to the ends of their lances.(wiki:Hare.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) shoots at hares with a crossbow in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep); some are killed but others escape.(wiki:Hare.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Summer](wiki:Summer), the [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) of [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark), hunts [squirrels](wiki:Squirrel) and hares as Bran's party travels toward the [northern mountains](wiki:Northern_mountains).(wiki:Hare.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
[Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) kills and cooks a hare near the [Trident](wiki:Trident).(wiki:Hare.cite_ref-Rasos65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Three crossbowmen at the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun) offer a share of a hare they caught to Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Hare.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Varamyr](wiki:Varamyr) skinchanges into a hare in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest).(wiki:Hare.cite_ref-Radwdprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Potted hare is found in one of the storerooms of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Hare.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
