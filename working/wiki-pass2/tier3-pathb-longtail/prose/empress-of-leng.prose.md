## Origins

According to legends, the empresses of [Leng](wiki:Leng) were known to have congress with the [Old Ones](wiki:Old_Ones), who, from time to time, ordered them to put all foreigners on the island to death.(wiki:Empress_of_Leng.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_leng.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
[Khiara the Great](wiki:Khiara_the_Great) began the tradition of the god-empress taking both a Lengii husband and a [YiTish](wiki:Yi_Ti) one.(wiki:Empress_of_Leng.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_leng.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) gave Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) a [jade](wiki:Jade) tiara said to have belonged to an Empress of Leng.(wiki:Empress_of_Leng.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
