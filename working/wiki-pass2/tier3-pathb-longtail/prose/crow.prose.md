## Culture

[Iron](wiki:Iron) cages hanging from posts care called [crow cages](wiki:Crow_cage), as carrion crows can peck at prisoners held inside.(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Humans use scarecrows to ward off the birds.(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Members of the black-clad [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) are sometimes called crows,(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) and their recruiters can be referred to as [wandering crows](wiki:Wandering_crow).(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) [Free folk](wiki:Free_folk) often denigrate the Watch as black crows,(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) and the [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) can be called Lord Crow by them.(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1)(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
A [Goodbrother branch](wiki:House_Goodbrother_of_Crow_Spike_Keep) resides at [Crow Spike Keep](wiki:Crow_Spike_Keep). Groups named after the bird include the [Stone Crows](wiki:Stone_Crows) and the [Stormcrows](wiki:Stormcrows).
The crow's nest is a lookout atop a ship's mast,(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) and the lookout sailor can be called a crow's eye.(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) [Storm Crow](wiki:Storm_Crow) is a ship at [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea).
[Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) is known as the Crow's Eye.(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) [Mors Umber](wiki:Mors_Umber) has been called Crowfood after a crow pecked out his eye and he responded by biting its head off.(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2)

## Origins

The defeat of the [Sarnori](wiki:Sarnori) by the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) became known as the [Field of Crows](wiki:Field_of_Crows).(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf835_6-1)
Some tales of [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers) claimed that carrion crows were spies who whispered secrets in his ears.(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
Crows nest atop the [broken tower](wiki:Broken_tower) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell)(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) and the common hall of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) enjoys climbing the broken tower to feed corn to its crows.(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Comatose after falling from the [First Keep](wiki:First_Keep), Bran dreams of a [three-eyed crow](wiki:Three-eyed_crow).(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Ragot17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) sees carrion crows during her travels in the wartorn [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Racok9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) observes crows arriving at dusk after the [battle at Winterfell](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell),(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0) and Bran sees numerous crows feasting on bodies after the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

The [ghost of High Heart](wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart) says she dreamed of a [man without a face](wiki:Faceless_Men) with a [drowned crow](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) on his shoulder.(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) sees crows on bodies floating in [Whispering Sound](wiki:Whispering_Sound).(wiki:Crow.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-1)
