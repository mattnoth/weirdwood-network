## Culture

[Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte) wears a rabbitskin vest.(wiki:Rabbit.cite_ref-Rasos26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
The [Windblown](wiki:Windblown) mockingly refer to [Faezhar zo Faez](wiki:Faezhar_zo_Faez) as "the Rabbit".(wiki:Rabbit.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Origins

During the [Moon of the Three Kings](wiki:Moon_of_the_Three_Kings), [Trystane Truefyre](wiki:Trystane_Truefyre) granted the poor the right to take rabbits, [hares](wiki:Hare), and [deer](wiki:Deer) from the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood).(wiki:Rabbit.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
After the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen) granted the right to hunt red deer, [boar](wiki:Boar), and rabbits in [Wat's Wood](wiki:Wat's_Wood) to [Wyman Webber](wiki:Wyman_Webber), [Lord of Coldmoat](wiki:Lord_of_Coldmoat), and his descendants.(wiki:Rabbit.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), disguised as "Arry" while part of [Yoren](wiki:Yoren)'s band of recruits for the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), kills a rabbit as they travel north from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Yoren stews the rabbit and gives a leg to Arya, who shares it with [Gendry](wiki:Gendry). [Rorge](wiki:Rorge) calls mocks her as "Lumpyface Lumpyhead Rabbitkiller".(wiki:Rabbit.cite_ref-Racok9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Benfred Tallhart](wiki:Benfred_Tallhart) raises a company of young lancers during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). After [Leobald Tallhart](wiki:Leobald_Tallhart) calls them young rabbits, they dub themselves the [Wild Hares](wiki:Wild_Hares) and tie rabbitskins to the ends of their lances.(wiki:Rabbit.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[Gilly](wiki:Gilly) plans to breed rabbits in a rabbit hutch at [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep),(wiki:Rabbit.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) but [Ghost](wiki:Ghost), the [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) of [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), gets into the rabbits.(wiki:Rabbit.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Rabbit.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Ghost eats one of the rabbits, and Jon cooks the other.(wiki:Rabbit.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
Rabbits are turned on spits and basted with [honey](wiki:Honey) in the kitchen of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Rabbit.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Three rabbits are flushed from the underbrush when [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy)'s hunting party searches for the missing [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood).(wiki:Rabbit.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Sharna](wiki:Sharna) serves rabbits to Arya and the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) at the [Inn of the Kneeling Man](wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man).(wiki:Rabbit.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Patchface](wiki:Patchface) is given a motley cloak of [beaver](wiki:Beaver) pelts, [sheepskins](wiki:Sheep), and rabbit fur while at [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea).(wiki:Rabbit.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
