## Culture

[Maesters](wiki:Maesters) train in how to set bones.(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) The [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters) strip the flesh from the bones of the deceased.(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Racok67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Many peoples of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) bury the bones of their dead in tombs,(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) crypts,(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) or [lichyards](wiki:Lichyard).(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
[House Drumm](wiki:House_Drumm)'s arms depict a bone hand, and the sigil of [House Willum](wiki:House_Willum) includes a [dragonbone](wiki:Dragonbone) skeleton. The sigil of [House Manwoody](wiki:House_Manwoody) depicts a crowned skull,(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) and skulls are also included in the arms of [House Lonmouth](wiki:House_Lonmouth) and [House Qoherys](wiki:House_Qoherys). [House Bulwer](wiki:House_Bulwer)'s symbol presents the skull of a bull.
The [Boneway](wiki:Boneway) is one of the passes through the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Rawoiafboneway.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) The [Bridge of Skulls](wiki:Bridge_of_Skulls) crosses the [Gorge](wiki:Gorge) near the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
The [Grey King's Hall](wiki:Grey_King's_Hall) on [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk) is said to have been made from the bones of [Nagga](wiki:Nagga).(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) [Seven Skulls](wiki:Seven_Skulls) is a longship in the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet).(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
Captains-general of the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) have their skulls [gilded after they die](wiki:Golden_skulls).(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)
Some [slaves](wiki:Slaves) in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) have skull tattoos.(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
[Meereen](wiki:Meereen) has a monument called the [Spire of Skulls](wiki:Spire_of_Skulls),(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-26) and [Belaquo Bonebreaker](wiki:Belaquo_Bonebreaker) is a famed [pit fighter](wiki:Pit_fighter) in the city.(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) One of the [tokars](wiki:Tokar) of [Zahrina](wiki:Zahrina) is fringed with tiny golden skulls.(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) A slave of [Yurkhaz zo Yunzak](wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak) plays a [bone flute](wiki:Bone_flute).(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)
The [Bone Mountains](wiki:Bone_Mountains), the tallest range in the [known world](wiki:Known_world), are named after the multitude of bones left behind by people and animals.(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) [Bonetown](wiki:Bonetown) in the [Further East](wiki:Further_East) of [Essos](wiki:Essos) is said to be made of bones.(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0) Some of the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles) are called the [Bones](wiki:Bones),(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Rtloiafthe_known_world.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0) and [Skull Island](wiki:Skull_Island) is one of the [Basilisk Isles](wiki:Basilisk_Isles).(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Rawoiafskull_island.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)

## Origins

The [Brotherhood of Bones](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Bones) were a group of [corsairs](wiki:Corsairs) active in the [Basilisk Isles](wiki:Basilisk_Isles).(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_basilisk_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)
The gates of [Ibbish](wiki:Ibbish) were known as the Whalebone Gates.(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)
In [46 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) during the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising), King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) displayed two thousand skulls before the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). They were claimed to be those of [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons) and [Poor Fellows](wiki:Poor_Fellows) killed during the king's campaign, but many believed the slain had been innocent [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk).(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)
Ser [Harwin Strong](wiki:Harwin_Strong), one of the strongest men in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), was called Breakbones.(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0)
The [skulls of the Targaryen dragons](wiki:Skulls_of_the_Targaryen_dragons) were kept in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep)'s throne room until their removal by King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0)
Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) keeps his finger bones, which were cut off by Lord [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), in a pouch about his neck.(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Rasos5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) finds the [skulls of the Targaryen dragons](wiki:Skulls_of_the_Targaryen_dragons) in a cellar of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) gives the name "[Vaes Tolorro](wiki:Vaes_Tolorro)", or City of Bones, to an abandoned city they find in the [red waste](wiki:Red_waste).(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0)(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

The [kindly man](wiki:Kindly_man) initially greets Arya with the visage of a yellowed skull.(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0)
During the search for [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) in the [secret passages](wiki:Secret_passages) of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), a chamber full of skulls and yellow bones is found.(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0)
The [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk) is held among [Nagga](wiki:Nagga)'s bones at the [Grey King's Hall](wiki:Grey_King's_Hall).(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0)
[Sparrows](wiki:Sparrows) bring to the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) the bones of pious people who were killed in the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After [Hazzea](wiki:Hazzea) is killed by [Drogon](wiki:Drogon), her father brings her burnt bones before Daenerys, [Queen of Meereen](wiki:Queen_of_Meereen).(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-0)
[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) finds bones littered throughout a passage within the [cave of the three-eyed crow](wiki:Cave_of_the_three-eyed_crow).(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Radwd13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_48-0)
[Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) sees a thousand skulls in one of her visions.(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_49-0)
After the death of Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane), the [Mountain's skull](wiki:Mountain's_skull) is sent to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear).(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_50-0)
Arya discovers that one of the tunnels beneath the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White) has walls lined with human bones and columns of skulls.(wiki:Bone.cite_ref-Radwd64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_51-0)
