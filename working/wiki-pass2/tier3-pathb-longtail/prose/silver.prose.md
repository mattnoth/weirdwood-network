## Origins

Warriors of the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar) wore armor with silver scales.(wiki:Silver.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-0)

Houses of the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands), such as the [Reynes](wiki:House_Reyne), grew wealthy from mining gold and silver.(wiki:Silver.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_49-0) [House Yronwood](wiki:House_Yronwood) also grew powerful from mining [iron](wiki:Iron), [tin](wiki:Tin), and silver in their lands.(wiki:Silver.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_kingdoms_of_the_first_men.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_50-0) Silver, tin, and [amber](wiki:Amber) have been found in the [Forest of Qohor](wiki:Forest_of_Qohor).(wiki:Silver.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_qohor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_51-0)
