## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

While waiting in a forest between the [Blue Fork](wiki:Blue_Fork) and the [Tumblestone](wiki:Tumblestone) within the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) hears the high, sharp trilling of snow shrikes. It is, however, unknown if they are actually bird calls or instead signaling by [northmen](wiki:Northmen) to begin the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Snow_shrike.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
