## Origins

Old Ghiscari developed from the ancient city of [Ghis](wiki:Old_Ghis) in [Ghiscar](wiki:Ghiscar). The region was conquered by the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) in five [Ghiscari wars](wiki:Ghiscari_wars), however, and the [Ghiscari people](wiki:Ghiscari) became [slaves](wiki:Slaves) of [Valyria](wiki:Valyria). The [Slaver Cities](wiki:Slaver_Cities) instead came to speak [bastard Valyrian](wiki:Bastard_Valyrian), [High Valyrian](wiki:High_Valyrian) corrupted and flavored with Ghiscari(wiki:Old_Ghiscari.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)(wiki:Old_Ghiscari.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Old_Ghiscari.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_rise_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) to form a [mongrel tongue](wiki:Ghiscari_language).(wiki:Old_Ghiscari.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Old_Ghiscari.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Old_Ghiscari.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:Old_Ghiscari.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Mhysa](wiki:Mhysa), an Old Ghiscari word meaning "mother", is used by many of the freed people of [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) to refer to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) when she enters the city after the [battle near Yunkai](wiki:Battle_near_Yunkai).(wiki:Old_Ghiscari.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) holds a feast in the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), singers from [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) sing love songs in Old Ghiscari.(wiki:Old_Ghiscari.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
[Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq),(wiki:Old_Ghiscari.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq),(wiki:Old_Ghiscari.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) and [Yurkhaz zo Yunzak](wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak)(wiki:Old_Ghiscari.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) speak "Ghiscari" at times, though it is unclear if this refers to Old Ghiscari or [mongrel Ghiscari](wiki:Mongrel_Ghiscari).
