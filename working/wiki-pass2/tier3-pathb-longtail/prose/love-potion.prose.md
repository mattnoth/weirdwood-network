## Origins

[Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom) suggests that [Alys Rivers](wiki:Alys_Rivers) used love potions and philtres on Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) and Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole), causing them to be rivals for her affections.(wiki:Love_potion.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) spit out a love potion put in his drink by Princess [Rhae Targaryen](wiki:Rhae_Targaryen), who wanted her brother to marry her instead of their sibling, Princess [Daella Targaryen](wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Maekar_I)).(wiki:Love_potion.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
[Smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) of [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) went to [Maggy the Frog](wiki:Maggy_the_Frog) for fortunetelling, charms, cures, love potions, and the like.(wiki:Love_potion.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Love_potion.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
