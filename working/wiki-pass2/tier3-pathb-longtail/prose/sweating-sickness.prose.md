## Origins

The sweating sickness broke out on the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters) in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC).(wiki:Sweating_sickness.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) When people close to [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) began dying at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) that same year, Maester [Culiper](wiki:Culiper) determined they did not suffer from the sweating sickness, the [shaking sickness](wiki:Shaking_sickness), or [greyscale](wiki:Greyscale). It was eventually discovered that the victims had been [poisoned](wiki:Poison) by [Androw Farman](wiki:Androw_Farman), Rhaena's husband.(wiki:Sweating_sickness.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
