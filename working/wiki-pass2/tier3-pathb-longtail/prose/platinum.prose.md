## Culture

Platinum is one of the metals used by [maesters](wiki:Maesters) for their chains;(wiki:Platinum.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) the subject it represents is unknown. The chain of Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) has more links of precious metals such as [gold](wiki:Gold), [silver](wiki:Silver), and platinum than baser metals.(wiki:Platinum.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
