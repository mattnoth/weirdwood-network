## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) trains as a [greenseer](wiki:Greenseer) with Lord [Brynden](wiki:Brynden_Rivers), the [three-eyed crow](wiki:Three-eyed_crow), in a [cave beyond the Wall](wiki:Cave_of_the_three-eyed_crow). To enhance Bran's gift, [Leaf](wiki:Leaf) and [Snowylocks](wiki:Snowylocks) give the youth a bowl of thick [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) paste to eat. The bowl has a dozen faces carved like a [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree). The white paste has red veins, which Bran supposes are only weirwood sap, though in the torchlight they look like blood.(wiki:Weirwood_paste.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Bran's reaction to the paste's taste is similar to the reaction [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) had upon drinking [shade of the evening](wiki:Shade_of_the_evening) in the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying).(wiki:Weirwood_paste.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) The weirwood paste at first tastes bitter, then better, then almost sweet, tasting of honey, new-fallen snow, pepper and cinnamon and the last kiss Bran's mother [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) ever gave him.(wiki:Weirwood_paste.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
With the cave's torches extinguished by the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest), Brynden has Bran [slip his skin](wiki:Skinchanger) into the weirwood roots instead of [Summer](wiki:Summer), [Hodor](wiki:Hodor), or [ravens](wiki:Raven). Bran then has [visions of past events](wiki:A_Dance_with_Dragons-Chapter_34) which occurred at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Weirwood_paste.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
