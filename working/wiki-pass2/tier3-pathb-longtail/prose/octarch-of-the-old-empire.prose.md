## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Following the disappearance of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), [Queen of Meereen](wiki:Queen_of_Meereen), from [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit),(wiki:Octarch_of_the_Old_Empire.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) her new husband, [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq), claims the throne and includes "Octarch of the Old Empire" among his many titles.(wiki:Octarch_of_the_Old_Empire.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
