## Origins

The [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlords) of the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) often traded for goldenheart from the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles).(wiki:Goldenheart.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_summer_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) The princes of the islands have forbidden export of the wood since the Slavers' Wars.(wiki:Goldenheart.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_summer_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
According to [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom), [Sandoq the Shadow](wiki:Sandoq_the_Shadow) played a stringed instrument of goldenheart and [ebony](wiki:Ebony) for [Larra Rogare](wiki:Larra_Rogare).(wiki:Goldenheart.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

During the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney), Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) uses a [lance](wiki:Lance) made from the golden wood of the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles).(wiki:Goldenheart.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

The Summer Islander [Jalabhar Xho](wiki:Jalabhar_Xho) gifts King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) with a golden wood great bow for his [royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding).(wiki:Goldenheart.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Alleras](wiki:Alleras) uses arrows of golden wood with his goldenheart longbow.(wiki:Goldenheart.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) [Kojja Mo](wiki:Kojja_Mo) wields a double-curved goldenheart bow on the [Cinnamon Wind](wiki:Cinnamon_Wind).(wiki:Goldenheart.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Led by [Black Balaq](wiki:Black_Balaq), the fifty Summer Islanders of the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) use goldenheart bows.(wiki:Goldenheart.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
