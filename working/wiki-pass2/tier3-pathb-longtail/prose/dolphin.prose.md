## Culture

A silver dolphin is depicted in the arms of [House Lowther](wiki:House_Lowther).(wiki:Dolphin.cite_ref-3)

## Origins

Some dolphins swam near [Sun Chaser](wiki:Sun_Chaser), [Lady Meredith](wiki:Lady_Meredith), and [Autumn Moon](wiki:Autumn_Moon) as [Alys Westhill](wiki:Alys_Westhill) attempted to cross the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea).(wiki:Dolphin.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
