## Origins

According to legend, the first of the [Rhoynish Wars](wiki:Rhoynish_Wars), the [First Turtle War](wiki:First_Turtle_War), began when [Valyrians](wiki:Valyria) killed one of these gigantic turtles.(wiki:Old_Men_of_the_River.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf28.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When the [Shy Maid](wiki:Shy_Maid) sails down the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne) at [Ny Sar](wiki:Ny_Sar), they see an exceptionally huge horned turtle, which comes to the surface and bellows at them. Tyrion judges it to be bigger than their boat. [Ysilla](wiki:Ysilla) says they are blessed to see it, and [Yandry](wiki:Yandry) declares it to be the [Old Man of the River](wiki:Old_Man_of_the_River), not just any turtle but the actual god that gives its name of the turtles.(wiki:Old_Men_of_the_River.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sees a large turtle shell, painted in different colors, decorating a stone inn at [Selhorys](wiki:Selhorys).(wiki:Old_Men_of_the_River.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
