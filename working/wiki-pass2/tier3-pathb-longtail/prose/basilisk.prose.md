## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sees basilisk venom in [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle)'s collection of [medicines](wiki:Medicine) and [poisons](wiki:Poison).(wiki:Basilisk.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
When [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) is visiting the docks at the [port of Qarth](wiki:Port_of_Qarth), she dismounts from [her silver](wiki:Her_silver) beside a gaming pit where a basilisk is tearing a big red dog to pieces amidst a shouting ring of sailors.(wiki:Basilisk.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) sees basilisks among the gargoyles that adorn the fortress of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Basilisk.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) learns of the properties of basilisk blood paste from the [Waif](wiki:Waif) of the [Faceless Men](wiki:Faceless_Men), and suspects it may be how [Jaqen H'ghar](wiki:Jaqen_H'ghar) caused [Weese](wiki:Weese)'s [spotted dog](wiki:Spotted_dog) to go mad and kill him.(wiki:Basilisk.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
[Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) dreams of her visit to the tent of [Maggy the Frog](wiki:Maggy_the_Frog), which was lit by an iron brazier shaped like a basilisk's head.(wiki:Basilisk.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

In [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), the animal-headed [brass](wiki:Brass) masks of the [Brazen Beasts](wiki:Brazen_Beasts) include the heads of basilisks.(wiki:Basilisk.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Basilisk.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
