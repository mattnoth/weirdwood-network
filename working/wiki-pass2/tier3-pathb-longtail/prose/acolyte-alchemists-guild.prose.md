## Origins

During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), the acolytes of the Alchemists' Guild were unaware of the [wildfire plot](wiki:Wildfire_plot). [Hallyne](wiki:Hallyne) was an acolyte in those days.(wiki:Acolyte_(Alchemists'_Guild).cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

After a cache of wildfire from King Aerys' days is discovered in a storeroom beneath the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor), [Wisdom](wiki:Wisdom) Hallyne dispatches the most able acolytes with a [cart](wiki:Cart) filled with sand to transport the jars of wildfire to the Guildhall.(wiki:Acolyte_(Alchemists'_Guild).cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
