### A Storm of Swords

Easy is among the men that [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) leaves behind to defend [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black). He laughs while spinning and fighting during the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black), but is killed by a [Thenn](wiki:Thenns).(wiki:Easy.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
