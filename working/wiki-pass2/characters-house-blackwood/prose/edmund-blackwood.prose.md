## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

When Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) arrives to relieve the [siege of Raventree](wiki:Siege_of_Raventree), he insists on a hostage. Lord Tytos, fearing to lose his only daughter, [Bethany](wiki:Bethany_Blackwood), offers his son Edmund instead. He suggests that Edmund could be a [squire](wiki:Squire) for Ser Jaime, but Jaime refuses, insisting he has too many squires already ([Lewys Piper](wiki:Lewys_Piper), [Garrett Paege](wiki:Garrett_Paege), and [Josmyn Peckledon](wiki:Josmyn_Peckledon)). Both Edmund and Bethany eventually remain at their father's castle, as one of their other brothers, [Hoster](wiki:Hoster_Blackwood), is taken as a hostage instead.(wiki:Edmund_Blackwood.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
