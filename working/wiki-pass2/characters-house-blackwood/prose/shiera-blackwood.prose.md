## Origins

Shiera was the eldest child of Lord [Roderick Blackwood](wiki:Roderick_Blackwood), who was allied with the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Arlan III Durrandon](wiki:Arlan_III_Durrandon). Arlan had married one of Roderick's daughters, while Shiera was herself married to Arlan's son.(wiki:Shiera_Blackwood.cite_ref-1) Arlan intended to restore the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) to House Blackwood, but Roderick was slain in battle.

After the [Battle of Six Kings](wiki:Battle_of_Six_Kings), Roderick's heir was a boy of only eight and Arlan distrusted Roderick's brothers. Arlan considered granting the riverlands to his good-daughter Shiera and having his own son rule by her side, but the other river lords opposed being ruled by a woman. Arlan instead added the riverlands to the domains of [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon).(wiki:Shiera_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
