## Origins

Tom fought for the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) and [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) in the civil war known as the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). During the [Battle on the Honeywine](wiki:Battle_on_the_Honeywine), the Bastard of Bitterbridge and Lord [Thaddeus Rowan](wiki:Thaddeus_Rowan) led a great host of mounted [knights](wiki:Knight) from the northeast, bearing down on the [green](wiki:Greens) army of Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower). With another army cutting off Hightower's retreat, a victory for the blacks seemed imminent until Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)) arrived on his [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Tessarion](wiki:Tessarion), turning the tide of battle.
