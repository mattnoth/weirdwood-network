## Appearances & Description

Lenn is the son of a [woods witch](wiki:Woods_witch). People believe he has a gift for foretelling the weather.(wiki:Lenn.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Lenn is one of the raiders who is ordered by [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) to climb the [Wall](wiki:Wall) and attack [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) from the south. He survives the climb and arrives at [Queenscrown](wiki:Queenscrown) with the others.(wiki:Lenn.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Lenn dies during the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black).(wiki:Lenn.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
