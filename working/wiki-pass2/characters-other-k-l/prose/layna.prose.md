## Appearances & Description

Layna is a fat young girl. She has a brother.(wiki:Layna.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Chiswyck](wiki:Chiswyck) tells a story at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) about how Layna was gang raped by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) and [his men](wiki:Mountain's_men) after the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney). Her brother tried to stop the assault but he was stabbed in the belly by [Raff the Sweetling](wiki:Rafford).(wiki:Layna.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Chiswyck estimated her to have been 18-years-old at the time of the event. Raff corrected him, stating that she was 13-years-old.(wiki:Layna.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
