## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Lew is part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), the force that leaves [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) and the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) in search of [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark) and [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder). He survives the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist). Lew stays loyal to the Watch during the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep) and is one of the few survivors of the ranging to make it all the way back to Castle Black.(wiki:Left_Hand_Lew.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Lew is stationed at Castle Black.(wiki:Left_Hand_Lew.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He accompanies [Black Jack Bulwer](wiki:Black_Jack_Bulwer) in escorting [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)), and [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) to [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea).(wiki:Left_Hand_Lew.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Lew, a man in his prime, guards [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) when [Tormund](wiki:Tormund)'s [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) pass through the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Left_Hand_Lew.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) He later is present when Jon Snow presents his plans for the rescue mission at [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome), before telling those present about the [letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) he received, purportedly from [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Bolton).(wiki:Left_Hand_Lew.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
