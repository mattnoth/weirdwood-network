## Appearances & Description

Robin is a stocky man with a bald head, bushy grey eyebrows, and brawny arms.(wiki:Robin_Ryger.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Like Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), Ser [Desmond Grell](wiki:Desmond_Grell), and [Utherydes Wayn](wiki:Utherydes_Wayn), he is an old man.(wiki:Robin_Ryger.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Robin was known as a tenacious fighter in his prime.(wiki:Robin_Ryger.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Robin wears mail under his white surcoat, which is decorated with a weeping willow embroidered in pale green. He fastens his cloak with a [silver](wiki:Silver) [trout](wiki:Trout).(wiki:Robin_Ryger.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Having served years as [Knight of the Gate](wiki:Knight_of_the_Gate), Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) speaks with Robin when he returns to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Robin_Ryger.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Ser Robin brings Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) to the [Great Hall of Riverrun](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Riverrun) to hear [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s terms for his release and for peace between the [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) and King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). Robb orders Robin to make sure that Cleos has a good meal and clean clothing before the envoy departs for [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Robin_Ryger.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Robin is introduced to [Brienne Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth) when she and Catelyn return from [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)). Robin asks about the manner of [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)'s death.(wiki:Robin_Ryger.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Robin is sent by Ser [Desmond Grell](wiki:Desmond_Grell)(wiki:Robin_Ryger.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) to re-capture Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) after [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) sets him free, chasing after the Kingslayer in a river galley. After Robin refuses Jaime's offer of single combat, [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth), Jaime's protector, sinks Robin's ship with a rock in the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork).(wiki:Robin_Ryger.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) Robin and his men walk back to Riverrun footweary and drenched.(wiki:Robin_Ryger.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Feast for Crows

After the [surrender of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Robin and Ser [Desmond Grell](wiki:Desmond_Grell) ask to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). Both are to be escorted to [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) by a dozen of the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) under the command of [Raff the Sweetling](wiki:Rafford).(wiki:Robin_Ryger.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
