## Origins

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord Lyonel Deddings sided with the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) and [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen).(wiki:Lyonel_Deddings.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

At the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton), Lord Deddings joined the host led by Ser [Addam Velaryon](wiki:Addam_Velaryon) and scraped together fresh levies of greybeards and green boys, despite the grievous losses he had suffered in the [autumn](wiki:Autumn) battles. During the battle, Lyonel and Lord [Stanton Piper](wiki:Stanton_Piper) seated together atop a low rise and burned with their squires, servants, and [sworn shields](wiki:Sworn_shield) when the riderless [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor) took note of them.(wiki:Lyonel_Deddings.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
