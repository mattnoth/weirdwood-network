## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Doss is among the wildling leaders who accompany [Tormund Giantsbane](wiki:Tormund) to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) in declaring a truce with the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). He is present in the [Shieldhall](wiki:Shieldhall) when [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) announces the [letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) purportedly from [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) entitled 'Bastard', and he stands when Jon asks for volunteers.(wiki:Blind_Doss.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
