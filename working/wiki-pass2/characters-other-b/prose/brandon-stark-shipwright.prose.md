## Origins

King Brandon was known as Brandon the Shipwright due to his love of the sea. Thousands of years before [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest),(wiki:Brandon_Stark_(Shipwright).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) King Brandon attempted to sail across the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea), but was never seen again.(wiki:Brandon_Stark_(Shipwright).cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) It is known that he never visited [Asshai](wiki:Asshai).(wiki:Brandon_Stark_(Shipwright).cite_ref-4) His tomb in the [crypts of Winterfell](wiki:Crypts_of_Winterfell) is empty. His son burned the remaining ships in his grief, and is thus known as King [Brandon the Burner](wiki:Brandon_Stark_(Burner)).(wiki:Brandon_Stark_(Shipwright).cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
