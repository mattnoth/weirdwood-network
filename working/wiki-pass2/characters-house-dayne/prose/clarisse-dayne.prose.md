## Origins

When King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) decided to take another wife, [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Benifer](wiki:Benifer) suggested Clarisse as a possible match in the hopes of detaching [House Dayne](wiki:House_Dayne) and their lands from [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). Maegor dismissed his advice, however.(wiki:Clarisse_Dayne.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
