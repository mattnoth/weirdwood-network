## Origins

Wylla was the wet nurse of [Edric Dayne](wiki:Edric_Dayne). She worked at [Starfall](wiki:Starfall) for many years.(wiki:Wylla.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

While [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) is out on a ride with King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), the king asks for the name of his [bastard](wiki:Bastard)'s mother, and Eddard replies that her name is Wylla. Eddard then states he does not want to talk about it. Robert then adds that she must have been quite a woman, if she was able to make Eddard Stark forget his honor even for an hour.(wiki:Wylla.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Lord [Edric Dayne](wiki:Edric_Dayne) tells [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) that he and [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) are "[milk brothers](wiki:Milk_brother)". He elaborates that one of the servants of his house, called Wylla, was Jon Snow's mother. When his own mother had no milk, Wylla became Edric's wet-nurse. Arya, who has never heard the name and is aware that Jon doesn't know anything about his mother, first thinks that Edric might be mocking her, but when he swears on the honor of his [house](wiki:House_Dayne) that he's telling the truth, she decides to remember the name, so that she can tell Jon when they meet again.(wiki:Wylla.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
