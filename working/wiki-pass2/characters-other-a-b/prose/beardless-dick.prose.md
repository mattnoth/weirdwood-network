## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Beardless Dick is part of the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), under the leadership of Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion). During the [battle at the burning septry](wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry), Dick and [Mudge](wiki:Mudge_(brotherhood)) stay behind to guard [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) and [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) against the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions).(wiki:Beardless_Dick.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
