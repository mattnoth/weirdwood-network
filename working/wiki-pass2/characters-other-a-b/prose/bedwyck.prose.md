## Origins

Bedwyck is the son of a crofter. He was sent to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) for poaching, and has been a [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) ranger for over a dozen years.(wiki:Bedwyck.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Appearances & Description

Bedwyck is a "hair and a half" over five feet tall, and has streaks of grey in his hair, showing his age.(wiki:Bedwyck.cite_ref-Racok13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He cannot read, but can write his own name on a good day.(wiki:Bedwyck.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Bedwyck is part of the [Great Ranging](wiki:Great_Ranging) [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall), led by [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont). He is a scout under the command of [Thoren Smallwood](wiki:Thoren_Smallwood). Due to his small stature, agility, and excellent climbing ability, Bedwyck is used as a look-out by Lord Commander Mormont at [Whitetree](wiki:Whitetree).(wiki:Bedwyck.cite_ref-Racok13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Storm of Swords

Bedwyck survives the assault by the [Others](wiki:Others) at the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist), and makes his way to [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep) with the other survivors.(wiki:Bedwyck.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) Bedwyck stays loyal to the Watch during the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep), and was one of the few survivors of the ranging to make it all the way back to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Bedwyck.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Bedwyck is part of the force that [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) takes when he heads off to fight the [Weeper](wiki:Weeper) at the [Bridge of Skulls](wiki:Bridge_of_Skulls).(wiki:Bedwyck.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

The new [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) of the Night's Watch, [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), puts Bedwyck in command of the abandoned way-castle [Icemark](wiki:Icemark), and gives him a garrison of thirty men. Bedwyck's mission is to restore the way-castle, to allow patrols to move about, and put eyes on the wall for climbers.(wiki:Bedwyck.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)
