## Origins

According to histories of [Qohor](wiki:Qohor), he raised forces from Qohorik colonists and declared himself first [Emperor of Valyria](wiki:Emperor_of_Valyria). Atop his [dragon](wiki:Dragon), he flew south to lay claim to what remained of [Valyria](wiki:Valyria) and reestablish the [Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) along with his thirty thousand men.
