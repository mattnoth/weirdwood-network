## Culture

[Smithyton](wiki:Smithyton) is the seat of [House Shermer](wiki:House_Shermer) in the [Reach](wiki:Reach).(wiki:Smith.cite_ref-6) [House Ironsmith](wiki:House_Ironsmith) is a noble house from the [north](wiki:North).(wiki:Smith.cite_ref-7)
[Coppersmith's Wynd](wiki:Coppersmith's_Wynd) is a street in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Smith.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Most smiths of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) have their forges on the [Street of Steel](wiki:Street_of_Steel).(wiki:Smith.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
[Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) is known for its goldsmiths(wiki:Smith.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) while [Bear Island](wiki:Bear_Island) have none.(wiki:Smith.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) is known for its silversmiths.(wiki:Smith.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Goldsmiths and jewelers of [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) shower the [courtesans](wiki:Courtesan) of the city with gifts.(wiki:Smith.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
The goldsmiths on the [Long Bridge](wiki:Long_Bridge) of [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) have guards at their door.(wiki:Smith.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

## Origins

The [Three Sage Kings](wiki:Three_Sage_Kings) sought after [Andal](wiki:Andal) craftsmen. Blacksmiths and stonemasons in particular were handsomely rewarded.(wiki:Smith.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
Blacksmiths, goldsmiths and silversmiths benefited from the prosperity and the developping trade under the [Old King](wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen).(wiki:Smith.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) meets with the master smiths of the capital to build a monstrous chain in preparation for the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). They considered this work beneath them at first but obliged the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) in the end.(wiki:Smith.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
The silversmiths of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) gift rings and chains to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen).(wiki:Smith.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) A wealthy goldsmmith is killed during the [riots in King's Landing](wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing) and his manse is burned.(wiki:Smith.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Just like Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) before her,(wiki:Smith.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) is well-known amongst the [city's](wiki:King's_Landing) goldsmiths.(wiki:Smith.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
