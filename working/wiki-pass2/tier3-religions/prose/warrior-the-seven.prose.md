## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) prays in a nameless village’s sept. The sept is modest and has no statues of the Seven, only rough charcoal drawings to represent them. She asks the Warrior to keep [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) strong and shield him in his battles.(wiki:Warrior_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
In the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying) [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) says a quick prayer, begging the Warrior for courage and the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) [horse god](wiki:Horse_god) for strength.(wiki:Warrior_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
When [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) chooses Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) to go with [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), Arys lights a candle to the Warrior in thanks.(wiki:Warrior_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

While standing vigil over his father’s body, it is revealed that the Warrior has been [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)’s god since he was old enough to hold a sword. While other men may be fathers, sons and husbands, Jaime thinks to himself that he is a warrior, and that it is all he will ever be.(wiki:Warrior_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
[Meribald](wiki:Meribald) tells [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne) that he has never known a boy who did not love the Warrior. He remarks that he is old though, and he loves [the Smith](wiki:Smith_(the_Seven)).(wiki:Warrior_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

During her imprisonment, [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) prays to all the gods, the Warrior included, stating "Any god in a storm".(wiki:Warrior_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
In his bedchamber in the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Queen’s_apartments_(Great_Pyramid)) [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) keeps a beeswax candle and a small carving of the Warrior on his bedside table. Though he is not a pious man, the carving makes him feel less alone in the unfamiliar city that is [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Warrior_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
When approaching the dragons [Viserion](wiki:Viserion) and [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal), Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) prays to the Warrior for courage.(wiki:Warrior_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
