## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

When Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and his army arrive outside [Darry](wiki:Darry), Lady [Amerei Frey](wiki:Amerei_Frey) sends Maester Ottomore to greet them.(wiki:Ottomore.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) It is unknown if Ottomore served at Darry before the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) or if he was assigned to the castle after its transfer from [House Darry](wiki:House_Darry) to [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister_of_Darry).
