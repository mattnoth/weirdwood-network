## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Four men sent with the [red cloaks](wiki:Red_cloaks) of Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) attempt to free Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) from his cell at Riverrun. They are partially successful, getting Jaime out of his cell. However, the [Tully guards](wiki:House_Tully_guards) catch on and attempt to stop him. Jaime slays Poul and [Myles](wiki:Myles_(squire)), the squire of Ser [Desmond Grell](wiki:Desmond_Grell), before being recaptured.(wiki:Poul_Pemford.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
