## Origins

Ser Osmund was named [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) by [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) in [17 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), following the death of Lord [Alton Celtigar](wiki:Alton_Celtigar) in office.(wiki:Osmund_Strong.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

After Aegon heard of an attack on [Tall Trees Town](wiki:Tall_Trees_Town) in the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles), Ser Osmund and [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Gawen](wiki:Gawen) were charged with the overseeing and raising of walls around [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Osmund_Strong.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) Work on the walls commenced in [20 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) and continued until [26 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Osmund_Strong.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) In [29 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) and again [30 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Ser Osmund and Lord [Aethan Velaryon](wiki:Aethan_Velaryon), [lord admiral](wiki:Lord_admiral) and [master of ships](wiki:Master_of_ships), led campaigns in the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) to root out the [Lysene](wiki:Lys) [pirate](wiki:Pirate) king [Sargoso Saan](wiki:Sargoso_Saan). They were accompanied by the young Prince [Maegor Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Osmund_Strong.cite_ref-Rtsotd.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

In [34 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Ser Osmund died in office and was replaced as Hand by Lord [Alyn Stokeworth](wiki:Alyn_Stokeworth).(wiki:Osmund_Strong.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Osmund_Strong.cite_ref-Rtsotd.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
