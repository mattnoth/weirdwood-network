## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

When [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) is disguised as Cat of the Canals, she encounters the mummers of the [Ship](wiki:Ship_(playhouse)), including Quill. The playwright offers to teach her how to kiss like a woman, but [Tagganaro](wiki:Tagganaro) puts an end to that when he hits him with a codfish.(wiki:Quill.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### The Winds of Winter

Prior to the first performance of *[The Bloody Hand](wiki:The_Bloody_Hand)* at the [Gate](wiki:Gate_(playhouse)), [Mercy](wiki:Arya_Stark) spies Quill standing in the back of the playhall. She thinks he has come to see what he can steal from the performance for one of his own plays.(wiki:Quill.cite_ref-Rtwow_.5B.5Bmercy_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Cmercy.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
