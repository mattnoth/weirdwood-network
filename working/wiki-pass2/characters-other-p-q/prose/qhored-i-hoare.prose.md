## Origins

The young Qhored sacked [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), taking thousands of women as captives.(wiki:Qhored_I_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) When he was thirty, Qhored defeated King [Bernarr II Justman](wiki:Bernarr_II_Justman) and took his three sons as hostages. Three years later he murdered the boys in the [Bloody Keep](wiki:Bloody_Keep) of [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), crushed Bernarr's vengeful army, and drowned the [river king](wiki:River_king) as a sacrifice to the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God), putting an end to [House Justman](wiki:House_Justman).(wiki:Qhored_I_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Qhored_I_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

During the reign of King Qhored, the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) could rightfully claim that his writ ran "wherever men can smell salt water or hear the crash of waves." The ironborn were at their peak of expansion, ruling territory along the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea) from the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor) to [Bear Island](wiki:Bear_Island).(wiki:Qhored_I_Hoare.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Many men abandoned the shores for fear of Qhored's reavers, while those who remained were forced to pay tribute.(wiki:Qhored_I_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) The [Kingdom of the Reach](wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Reach) called him Qhored the Cruel.(wiki:Qhored_I_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_the_gardener_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) King Qhored died at the age of ninety, having ruled for seventy five years.(wiki:Qhored_I_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) Qhored's conquests were gradually lost by his successors and descendants, however.(wiki:Qhored_I_Hoare.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

According to a semi-canon source, after the reign of Qhored the Cruel there were other kings from [House Hoare](wiki:House_Hoare) that were named Qhored, both in the [driftwood king](wiki:Driftwood_king) era and the [iron king](wiki:Iron_king) era.(wiki:Qhored_I_Hoare.cite_ref-5)
