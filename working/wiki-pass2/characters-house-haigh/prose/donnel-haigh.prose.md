## Origins

Donnel has been beaten many times in tourneys by [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane), who almost killed him in a [mêlée](wiki:Mêlée).(wiki:Donnel_Haigh.cite_ref-Rasos50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Donnel is introduced to Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) at the [Twins](wiki:Twins) on the eve of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully)'s [wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Donnel_Haigh.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

While traveling to the Twins, Sandor Clegane and [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) encounter Donnel and his outriders.(wiki:Donnel_Haigh.cite_ref-Rasos50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) They manage to trick Donnel with their [peasant](wiki:Peasant) disguises, claiming they serve Lady [Shella Whent](wiki:Shella_Whent). When Arya asks Sandor why Donnel did not recognize him, Sandor tells her it is because [knights](wiki:Knight) are fools and it would be beneath him to look twice at some poxy peasants.(wiki:Donnel_Haigh.cite_ref-Rasos50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

### A Feast for Crows

When news of the [raid on Saltpans](wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans) reaches the Twins, Donnel rides with his brother, Ser [Harys Haigh](wiki:Harys_Haigh), and their cousin, Ser [Arwood Frey](wiki:Arwood_Frey), to the town with a small force to investigate. There they find Ser [Quincy Cox](wiki:Quincy_Cox) sitting behind his walls and [Saltpans](wiki:Saltpans) in ashes.(wiki:Donnel_Haigh.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
