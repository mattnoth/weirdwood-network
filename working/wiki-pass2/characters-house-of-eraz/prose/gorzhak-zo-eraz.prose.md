## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

The [Windblown](wiki:Windblown), a [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) company hired by [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai), mock Gorzhak as "Pudding Face".(wiki:Gorzhak_zo_Eraz.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Since the death of [Yurkhaz zo Yunzak](wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak) in the [fighting pits](wiki:Fighting_pits) of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), the Yunkish leaders, including Gorzhak, keep a daily rotation for the duty of [supreme commander](wiki:Supreme_Commander_of_the_Armies_and_Allies_of_Yunkai).(wiki:Gorzhak_zo_Eraz.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### The Winds of Winter

On the eve of battle during the [Second Siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_Siege_of_Meereen), Gorzhak is supposed to become the Supreme Commander according to the rotation chart but is slain by the [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince), who turns the Windblown's cloak to join Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen).(wiki:Gorzhak_zo_Eraz.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btyrion_ii_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctyrion_ii.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
