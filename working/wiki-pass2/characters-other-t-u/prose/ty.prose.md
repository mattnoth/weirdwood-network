## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

When Ty and [Dannel](wiki:Dannel) search for wildlings along the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad), they find [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark), who seeks [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow). Ty brings the girl to [Clydas](wiki:Clydas) for treatment.(wiki:Ty.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) When [Cregan Karstark](wiki:Cregan_Karstark) searches for Alys with a small group of men, one is killed by the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) after shooting a crossbow quarrel at Ty.(wiki:Ty.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Ty stands sentry at the top of the [Wall](wiki:Wall) alongside [Owen the Oaf](wiki:Owen_the_Oaf) when Jon, [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), arrives upon the elevator with [Leathers](wiki:Leathers) for a meeting with other officers of the Night's Watch.(wiki:Ty.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
