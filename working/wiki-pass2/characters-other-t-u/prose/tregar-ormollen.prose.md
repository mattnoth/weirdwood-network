## Origins

A native of [Lys](wiki:Lys), Tregar is a wealthy merchant prince with a wife and several concubines.(wiki:Tregar_Ormollen.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

In [293 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest),(wiki:Tregar_Ormollen.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) and his wife, [Lynesse Hightower](wiki:Lynesse_Hightower), arrived in [Lys](wiki:Lys), seeking an escape from Jorah's [crimes](wiki:Slavery) in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). In order to keep up with his wife's demands of wealth and luxury, Jorah sold his service as a [sellsword](wiki:Mercenary). However, while Jorah was fighting the [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) on the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne), Lynesse met Tregar. Soon, he had taken her as one of his concubines and moved her into his [manse](wiki:Manse).(wiki:Tregar_Ormollen.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

When Jorah returned to Lys, Tregar informed him that he would be enslaved for his debts, unless he gave up Lynesse and left Lys. Heartbroken, Jorah went to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis).(wiki:Tregar_Ormollen.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) As Tregar's chief concubine, it is rumored that even his wife goes in fear of Lynesse.(wiki:Tregar_Ormollen.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

After [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy)'s [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) attack the [Reach](wiki:Reach), Ser [Humfrey Hightower](wiki:Humfrey_Hightower) travels to [Lys](wiki:Lys) in hopes of hiring [sellsails](wiki:Sellsail) with the aid of his sister, [Lynesse](wiki:Lynesse_Hightower).(wiki:Tregar_Ormollen.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) tells [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) of Tregar's threats of enslavement if he did not give up Lynesse.(wiki:Tregar_Ormollen.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
