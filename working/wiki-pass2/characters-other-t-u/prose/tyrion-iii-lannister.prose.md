## Origins

As the [Andals](wiki:Andals) began moving into the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) in larger numbers than in the time of [Tybolt the Thunderbolt](wiki:Tybolt_Lannister_(king)), Tyrion decided to arrange [marriages](wiki:Marriage) for the more powerful Andal warchiefs with the daughters of the great local [Houses](wiki:List_of_Houses). Aware of Andal betrayals in the [Vale](wiki:Vale), he also took the sons and daughters of the new Andal [lords](wiki:Lord) as [wards and fosterlings](wiki:Fosterage) to serve as [pages](wiki:Page) and [cupbearers](wiki:Cupbearer), as well as hostages, at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock). He was succeeded by his son, [Gerold II Lannister](wiki:Gerold_II_Lannister), who continued Tyrion's policies.(wiki:Tyrion_III_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
