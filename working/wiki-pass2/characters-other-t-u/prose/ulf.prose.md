## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ulf is the leader of the [Moon Brothers](wiki:Moon_Brothers) who accompany [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to his father [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s encampment on the promise of weapons and armor.(wiki:Ulf.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Lord [Leo Lefford](wiki:Leo_Lefford) is displeased when Tyrion requests arms and armor from him for Ulf's men.(wiki:Ulf.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Ulf demands blood money when three [Stone Crows](wiki:Stone_Crows) kill a Moon Brother, but [Conn](wiki:Conn) and [Shagga](wiki:Shagga) refuse to pay.(wiki:Ulf.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Ulf is slain during the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork). After the battle, Tyrion finds Ulf with his arm gone at the elbow and a dozen Moon Brothers dead around him.(wiki:Ulf.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
