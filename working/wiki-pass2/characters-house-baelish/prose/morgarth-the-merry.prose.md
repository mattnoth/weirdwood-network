## Appearances & Description

Morgarth is burly with a thick salt-and-pepper beard. He has a red, bulbous nose with broken veins and large, gnarled hands.(wiki:Morgarth_the_Merry.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Along with Ser [Shadrich](wiki:Shadrich) and Ser [Byron the Beautiful](wiki:Byron_the_Beautiful), Ser Morgarth enters the service of Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon). He remarks that Petyr did not mention that his daughter, [Alayne Stone](wiki:Sansa_Stark), is beautiful.(wiki:Morgarth_the_Merry.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
