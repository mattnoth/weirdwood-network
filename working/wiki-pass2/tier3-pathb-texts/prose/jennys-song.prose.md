## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) hears the song when the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) takes her to [High Heart](wiki:High_Heart) for the first time. The outlaws meet with the [ghost of High Heart](wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart), who requests a song after telling them of her recent dreams.(wiki:Jenny's_song.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Jenny's_song.cite_ref-5) [Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams) sings, accompanying the words with his woodharp. The song is so soft and sad that Arya can only hear snatches of the words, though the tune is half familiar to her.(wiki:Jenny's_song.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
When the brotherhood go to High Heart a second time,(wiki:Jenny's_song.cite_ref-6) the dwarf woman requests the same song, which she associates with a "Jenny". As Tom sings she closes her eyes and rocks slowly back and forth, murmuring the lyrics and crying.(wiki:Jenny's_song.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
When [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), stops at [Oldstones](wiki:Oldstones) while en route to the [Twins](wiki:Twins), Robb recalls a song about [Jenny of Oldstones](wiki:Jenny_of_Oldstones) to his mother, [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark).(wiki:Jenny's_song.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
After the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), the brotherhood waits for [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey) to arrive at Oldstones with the ransom for his captured kin, [Petyr Frey](wiki:Petyr_Frey). When Merrett arrives, he hears Tom softly and sadly singing, "High in the halls of the kings who are gone, Jenny would dance with her ghosts..." Merrett is familiar with the song.(wiki:Jenny's_song.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
