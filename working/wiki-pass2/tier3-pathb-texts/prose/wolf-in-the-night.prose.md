## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Rymund the Rhymer](wiki:Rymund_the_Rhymer) writes the song about the victory of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), at the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross).(wiki:Wolf_in_the_Night.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) He performs it for Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) and [Riverrun's garrison](wiki:House_Tully_guards) while they eat in the [Great Hall](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Riverrun).(wiki:Wolf_in_the_Night.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Quotes
