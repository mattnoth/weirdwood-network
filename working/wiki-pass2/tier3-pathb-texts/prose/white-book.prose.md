## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), the [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard), observes that Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) took time to update the White Book before he fled [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). He feels that his own page is scant by comparison.(wiki:White_Book.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
The one-handed Jaime later awkwardly updates his own entry.(wiki:White_Book.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]
