## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

When Eustace's father, Lord [Eon](wiki:Eon_Hunter), dies suddenly, Eustace and his younger brother [Harlan](wiki:Harlan_Hunter) accuse their older brother [Gilwood](wiki:Gilwood_Hunter) of having murdered their father.(wiki:Eustace_Hunter.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Feast for Crows

According to Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), Eustace or Harlan will kill Gilwood, although he feels that Harlan is most likely to do it.(wiki:Eustace_Hunter.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
