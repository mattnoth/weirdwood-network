Lord Costayne supported Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and the [blacks](wiki:Blacks).(wiki:Owen_Costayne.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Owen_Costayne.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Owen, along with Lord [Alan Tarly](wiki:Alan_Tarly) and Ser [Alan Beesbury](wiki:Alan_Beesbury), trapped Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower)'s forces between them and Lord [Thaddeus Rowan](wiki:Thaddeus_Rowan) and the [Bastard of Bitterbridge](wiki:Tom_Flowers) and the banks of the [Honeywine](wiki:Honeywine). Lord Hightower's lines were starting to crumble and defeat seemed all but a forgone conclusion. Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)) joined the [Battle of the Honeywine](wiki:Battle_of_the_Honeywine) on his dragon [Tessarion](wiki:Tessarion), however, turning the battle for the [greens](wiki:Greens).(wiki:Owen_Costayne.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Owen_Costayne.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
