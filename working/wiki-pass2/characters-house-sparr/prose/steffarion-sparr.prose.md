### A Feast for Crows

Steffarion accompanies his father, [the Sparr](wiki:The_Sparr), and [Gormond Goodbrother](wiki:Gormond_Goodbrother) to meet [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) to give him the news that [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) has died.(wiki:Steffarion_Sparr.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
