## Origins

Julian fought for the [greens](wiki:Greens) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). After [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) was [taken back](wiki:Moon_of_the_Three_Kings) by the greens in [130 AC](wiki:130_AC), Ser [Tyland Lannister](wiki:Tyland_Lannister), Ser [Marston Waters](wiki:Marston_Waters) and Ser Julian were sent to the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) of [Pentos](wiki:Pentos), [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh), and [Myr](wiki:Myr) to hire [sellswords](wiki:Sellsword) to fight for King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Julian_Wormwood.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

By the time Ser Tyland's party returned empty handed to King's Landing, Aegon II was dead.(wiki:Julian_Wormwood.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
