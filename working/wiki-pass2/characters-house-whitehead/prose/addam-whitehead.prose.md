## Origins

### The Winds of Winter

[Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell)'s embassy to [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) and the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) lands at the [Weeping Town](wiki:Weeping_Town), heading north to meet them at [Griffin's Roost](wiki:Griffin's_Roost). While staying there, Ser [Joss Hood](wiki:Joss_Hood) overhears at the [Loon](wiki:Loon) inn that half a hundred men and boys from the Weeping Town already left to join Jon at Griffin's Roost, including Ser Addam, old Lord Whitehead's son and heir.(wiki:Addam_Whitehead.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Barianne_ii_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Carianne_ii.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
