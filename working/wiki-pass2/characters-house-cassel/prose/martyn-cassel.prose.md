## Origins

Martyn was one of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s companions, fighting alongside him during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). He was slain at the [tower of joy](wiki:Tower_of_joy) in the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) [during a fight](wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy) with three of King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen)'s [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). Afterwards, Eddard had the tower pulled down, and used its stones for cairns for the fallen.(wiki:Martyn_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

During a fever dream, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) recalls Martyn and his other former companions at the tower of joy.(wiki:Martyn_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

### A Clash of Kings

[Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) mentions his brother Martyn when explaining to [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) that nothing in the future is certain.(wiki:Martyn_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
