## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser Lomas is listed as supporting [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) at the onset of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Lomas_Estermont.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Lomas is among those who remain loyal to Stannis after the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Lomas_Estermont.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Ser Lomas is part of the force [defending](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(300)) [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) from the forces of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Lomas_Estermont.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
