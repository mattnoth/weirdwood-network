## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser Alyn is listed as supporting [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) at the beginning of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Alyn_Estermont.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Alyn is among the Estermonts that swear fealty to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Alyn_Estermont.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Ser Alyn is currently in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with his father, [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Estermont), supporting King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Alyn_Estermont.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
