## Origins

### Dance of the Dragons

Samantha was the daughter of [Donald Tarly](wiki:Donald_Tarly), [Lord of Horn Hill](wiki:Lord_of_Horn_Hill), and Lady [Jeyne Rowan](wiki:Jeyne_Rowan). She was the second wife of Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower), head of [House Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) and a supporter of the [greens](wiki:Greens) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Samantha_Tarly.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

Ormund, [Lord of the Hightower](wiki:Lord_of_the_Hightower), died at the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton) in [130 AC](wiki:130_AC). According to [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom), the fifteen-year-old Lord [Lyonel Hightower](wiki:Lyonel_Hightower) was infatuated with his father's widow, who was only two years older than the young lord, but Samantha refused his advances.(wiki:Samantha_Tarly.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) [House Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) declined King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen)'s demand for new armies after the [Moon of the Three Kings](wiki:Moon_of_the_Three_Kings).(wiki:Samantha_Tarly.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

During the [False Dawn](wiki:False_Dawn), Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) attempted to make peace between the warring factions. Lord Lyonel initially reacted angrily to the Sea Snake's offer, but Samantha allegedly persuaded her step-son to agree to Corlys's terms by allowing Lyonel to seduce her and promising to marry him if he made peace. Sam was related to the [Tarlys](wiki:House_Tarly) and [Rowans](wiki:House_Rowan), houses who supported the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) of Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen). Grand Maester [Munkun](wiki:Munkun) believes that Lord Hightower accepted the terms because of the end to [Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) neutrality, however.(wiki:Samantha_Tarly.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

### Regency of Aegon III

Lord Lyonel announced his intention to marry his father's widow, but the reigning [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Regency_of_Aegon_III)) forbade the union as a form of [incest](wiki:Incest). However, Lyonel kept Samantha as his [paramour](wiki:Paramour) for thirteen years and fathered six children on her.(wiki:Samantha_Tarly.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9) Lyonel and Lady Sam attended the coronation of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) and [his wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Aegon_III_Targaryen_and_Jaehaera_Targaryen) to Queen [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen), with their presence being so great a scandal that the High Septon refused to travel with them.(wiki:Samantha_Tarly.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Relations between the Hightowers and the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) soured when the High Septon commanded Lady Sam to remove herself from her step-son's bed and take vows as a [silent sister](wiki:Silent_sisters) as penance. When she refused, the High Septon condemned her as a shameless fornicator and forbade her to set foot in the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) until she repented. Instead, Samantha mounted a warhorse and burst into the Starry Sept as the High Septon was leading prayer, claiming that while she could not set foot there, the High Septon had said nothing of her horse's hooves. Sam commanded her men to close the doors, as the sept should be closed to all if it was closed for her. Though the High Septon grew furious, he had no choice but to relent.(wiki:Samantha_Tarly.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

After the death of Queen Jaehaera in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Lady Sam wrote a letter proposing that Aegon III take two wives: her sister [Sansara](wiki:Sansara_Tarly), and Lord Lyonel's sister, [Bethany](wiki:Bethany_Hightower). Samantha also appended the names of thirty-one other maidens from Houses [Ambrose](wiki:House_Ambrose), [Beesbury](wiki:House_Beesbury), [Cobb](wiki:House_Cobb), [Costayne](wiki:House_Costayne), [Florent](wiki:House_Florent), [Grimm](wiki:House_Grimm), [Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower), [Redwyne](wiki:House_Redwyne), [Tarly](wiki:House_Tarly) and [Varner](wiki:House_Varner) who might be suitable as queens. According to [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom), Sam offered to suggest the names of boys as well if Aegon were interested, but no other chronicles mention this postscript, and the letter has been lost to history.(wiki:Samantha_Tarly.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Lyonel and Sam hosted Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) when he stopped at [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) during his voyage toward the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC). Alyn became a close friend of the pair due to the courtesy he showed Sam.(wiki:Samantha_Tarly.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Lady Sam protected [Lotho Rogare](wiki:Lotho_Rogare) after the fall of [House Rogare](wiki:House_Rogare). The Hightowers remained extremely wealthy as they had not entrusted any of their gold to [Lys](wiki:Lys) before the [Rogare Bank](wiki:Rogare_Bank) ran dry. Lotho helped Sam found the prosperous [Bank of Oldtown](wiki:Bank_of_Oldtown).(wiki:Samantha_Tarly.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Lord Lyonel and Lady Sam made their way to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with six hundred men in [136 AC](wiki:136_AC) when the [new regents](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III) were to be chosen by lot.(wiki:Samantha_Tarly.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) Lord [Torrhen Manderly](wiki:Torrhen_Manderly) and the regents planned for Aegon III to visit Lyonel and Samantha in Oldtown during a [royal progress](wiki:Royal_progress) to mark his coming of age, but the king abruptly cancelled the progress and dismissed his regents.(wiki:Samantha_Tarly.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

### Later Life

A [new High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aegon_III)) eventually reversed the position of his predecessor, allowing Lyonel to marry Sam, and by the same occasion [legitimized](wiki:Bastardy) their six chidren.(wiki:Samantha_Tarly.cite_ref-8)(wiki:Samantha_Tarly.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)

Along with [Johanna Lannister](wiki:Johanna_Lannister), [Sharis Footly](wiki:Sharis_Footly), and [Alys Rivers](wiki:Alys_Rivers), Samantha is featured in Archmaester [Abelon](wiki:Abelon)'s *[When Women Ruled: Ladies of the Aftermath](wiki:When_Women_Ruled:_Ladies_of_the_Aftermath)*.(wiki:Samantha_Tarly.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
