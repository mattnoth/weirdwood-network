## Origins

Alan supported Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and the [blacks](wiki:Blacks). Joined by Lord [Owen Costayne](wiki:Owen_Costayne) and Ser [Alan Beesbury](wiki:Alan_Beesbury), Lord Tarly trapped Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower)'s forces along the banks of the [Honeywine](wiki:Honeywine) between them and another army of blacks led by Lord [Thaddeus Rowan](wiki:Thaddeus_Rowan) and [Tom Flowers](wiki:Tom_Flowers). Ormund's lines started to crumble and defeat seemed all but a forgone conclusion. However, Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)) attacked on his dragon, [Tessarion](wiki:Tessarion), turning the battle for the [greens](wiki:Greens).
