## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Gladden is chosen by Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to be part of a force sent into the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) to apprehend Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) and bring him to justice.(wiki:Gladden_Wylde.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) In the [battle at the Mummer's Ford](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford), however, that force is ambushed and Gladden is amongst those slain.(wiki:Gladden_Wylde.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Storm of Swords

The [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) include Gladden among the victims of the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Gladden_Wylde.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
