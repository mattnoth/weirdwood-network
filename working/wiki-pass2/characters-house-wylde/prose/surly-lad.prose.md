## Origins

He [deflowered](wiki:Flowering) and impregnated [Coryanne Wylde](wiki:Coryanne_Wylde), the younger daughter of Lord [Morgan Wylde](wiki:Morgan_Wylde). He was [gelded](wiki:Eunuch) shortly after, and sent to the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Surly_lad.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

## Appearances & Description

According to *[A Caution for Young Girls](wiki:A_Caution_for_Young_Girls)*, he was a handsome boy of a similar age to [Coryanne Wylde](wiki:Coryanne_Wylde). However, the [maester](wiki:Maester) at the [Rain House](wiki:Rain_House) describes him as a pox-scarred man of thirty with a large penis.(wiki:Surly_lad.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
