## Origins

The young Princess [Aliandra Martell](wiki:Aliandra_Martell) encouraged Dornish noblemen to raid the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands).(wiki:Wyland_Wyl.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) In [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Ser [Steffon Connington](wiki:Steffon_Connington) pursued a small band of [Dornish](wiki:Dornish) raiders across the [Dornish Marches](wiki:Dornish_Marches), but rode too far. Ignorant of what lied ahead, Steffon was ambushed and slain by Wyland Wyl.(wiki:Wyland_Wyl.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
