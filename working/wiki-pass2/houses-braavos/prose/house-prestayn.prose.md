## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

The [kindly man](wiki:Kindly_man) offers to find [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) passage on [Luco Prestayn](wiki:Luco_Prestayn)'s ship, *[Lady Bright](wiki:Lady_Bright)*, which is leaving for [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown), [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale), and [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) the next day, but she refuses.(wiki:House_Prestayn.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Arya learns that [Lotho Lornel](wiki:Lotho_Lornel) sleeps in [Moredo Prestayn](wiki:Moredo_Prestayn)'s home when the captain is away.(wiki:House_Prestayn.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Arya learns that the thirteen-year-old daughter of a poor Prestayn serving maid has become the [Merling Queen](wiki:Merling_Queen)'s [new mermaid](wiki:Mermaids_(Merling_Queen's)).(wiki:House_Prestayn.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### The Winds of Winter

An ancient Prestayn sits alone in a private box at the [Gate](wiki:Gate_(playhouse)) during a performance of *[The Bloody Hand](wiki:The_Bloody_Hand)*.(wiki:House_Prestayn.cite_ref-5)
