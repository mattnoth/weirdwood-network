## Origins

When Queen [Alys Harroway](wiki:Alys_Harroway) was accused of infidelity and arrested, every family member of hers present in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) was executed. Horas was thrown onto the spikes of the dry moat around [Maegor's Holdfast](wiki:Maegor's_Holdfast).
