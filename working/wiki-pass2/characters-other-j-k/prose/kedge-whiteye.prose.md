## Appearances & Description

Kedge is a forty-odd year old ranger who has been at the [Wall](wiki:Wall) since he was ten years old. His left eye is blind, his right eye mean. [Beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) he is as a good as any ranger alone with an axe and garron, but he has never gotten along well with other men.(wiki:Kedge_Whiteye.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Kedge is part of [Thoren Smallwood](wiki:Thoren_Smallwood)'s scouting expedition in the [Frostfangs](wiki:Frostfangs) during the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging). When they return to the encampment of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) on the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men), tells assorted members of the Night's Watch of the march of [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder)'s host.(wiki:Kedge_Whiteye.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He survives the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist). After reaching [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep), Kedge chops off [Bannen](wiki:Bannen)'s mangled foot in an attempt to save the injured scout, but Bannen eventually dies.(wiki:Kedge_Whiteye.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Kedge stays loyal to the Watch during the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep) and is one of the few survivors of the ranging to make it back to Castle Black.(wiki:Kedge_Whiteye.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Kedge is one of the rangers sent to escort [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)), and [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) to [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea). He is angry when Samwell shows up on his own, as it means he lost a bet that Samwell would have to be dragged from his bed by the [rangers](wiki:Ranger).(wiki:Kedge_Whiteye.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Kedge guards the [Wall](wiki:Wall) when [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) makes his inspection. Kedge and [Dywen](wiki:Dywen) report that two of the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men), Ser [Richard Horpe](wiki:Richard_Horpe) and Ser [Justin Massey](wiki:Justin_Massey), rode south along the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad).(wiki:Kedge_Whiteye.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Jon appoints Kedge as the leader of one of three groups of three rangers sent ranging.(wiki:Kedge_Whiteye.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) warns Jon soon after their departure that in her visions she has seen three of the nine rangers return eyeless, weeping blood.(wiki:Kedge_Whiteye.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

When the decapitated heads of the group led by [Black Jack Bulwer](wiki:Black_Jack_Bulwer) turn up north of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), Jon asks Melisandre if she has also seen the surviving six rangers, including Kedge, in her visions. Melisandre reveals she has not seen their fates just yet.(wiki:Kedge_Whiteye.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
