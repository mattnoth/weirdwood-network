## Origins

About [257 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) Kaeth was made Grand Maester at the age of eighty and sent to serve the court of King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). He died within a year of his appointment and was succeeded by Grand Maester [Ellendor](wiki:Ellendor).(wiki:Kaeth.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Grand Maester Kaeth wrote the well-regarded work *[Lives of Four Kings](wiki:Lives_of_Four_Kings)*, a detailed history of the reigns of [Daeron I](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen), [Baelor I](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen), [Aegon IV](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen), and [Daeron II](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen). Kaeth scants [Viserys II](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen) terribly, however, as his short reign as king came after Baelor's.(wiki:Kaeth.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

The book is considered to be an excellent treatise of the art of rulership. Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister)(wiki:Kaeth.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) and his nephew [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)(wiki:Kaeth.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) are amongst its appreciators, as is Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell).(wiki:Kaeth.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
