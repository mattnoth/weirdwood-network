## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

King Cutthroat is killed, along with most of the population of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor), when the city is [besieged](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor) by the [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai). He surrendered to their forces but was later thrown in to a pit to be killed by a pack of dogs.(wiki:King_Cutthroat.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) During the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown)'s march to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell), one of the besiegers, recalls "the last free king of Astapor (King Cutthroat), screaming naked in the pit as he was set on by a score of starving dogs."(wiki:King_Cutthroat.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
