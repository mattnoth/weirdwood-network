## Origins

Khorane has spent many years trading with the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Khorane_Sathmantes.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Appearances & Description

Khorane has blue eyes and a bony face worn from weather.(wiki:Khorane_Sathmantes.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) The captain is a kindly [Lyseni](wiki:Lys) who enjoys rich foods like [snails](wiki:Snail) and [lampreys](wiki:Lamprey).(wiki:Khorane_Sathmantes.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Khorane takes *Shayala's Dance* into the [Spears of the Merling King](wiki:Spears_of_the_Merling_King) to rescue Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth). The captain gives Davos the use of his own cabin and his own clothes, as well as a dirk and a new pair of boots. Khorane tells Davos about the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), and he delivers Davos to [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan) at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Khorane_Sathmantes.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
