## Origins

In [50 AC](wiki:50_AC) Ser [Orryn Baratheon](wiki:Orryn_Baratheon) arrived at the [motherhouse](wiki:Motherhouse) demanding Princess [Rhaella](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon)), a novice of the [Faith](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), be given into his custody. The ploy might well have worked but for Septa Karolyn, who had the door of the motherhouse that day. Whilst placating Orryn with the pretext of sending for the girl, she sent instead to the [High Septon](wiki:High_Lickspittle). The High Septon sent his steward, [Casper Straw](wiki:Casper_Straw) with thirty armed [septons](wiki:Septons) to the motherhouse and Ser Orryn and his men were captured.(wiki:Karolyn.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
