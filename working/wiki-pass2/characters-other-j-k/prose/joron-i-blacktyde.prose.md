## Origins

Joron I captured [Gyles II Gardener](wiki:Gyles_II_Gardener), [King of the Reach](wiki:King_of_the_Reach), when their fleets clashed off the [Misty Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands). After torturing him to death, Joron had Gyles's corpse cut to pieces and used them to bait his fishhooks with "a chunk of king".(wiki:Joron_I_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Later in his reign, Joron raided the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor) and supposedly carried off every woman under thirty, earning the name "Maidensbane", by which he is best remembered.(wiki:Joron_I_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
