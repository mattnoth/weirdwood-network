## Origins

Jon Waters was the bastard son of the seafarer Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon), called "the Oakenfist", and Princess [Elaena Targaryen](wiki:Elaena_Targaryen). He had a twin sister, [Jeyne Waters](wiki:Jeyne_Waters). Elaena had hoped to marry Alyn, but he was lost at sea.(wiki:Jon_Waters.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_the_sisters_of_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Jon's [trueborn son](wiki:Longwaters), also a great knight, changed the family name to [Longwaters](wiki:House_Longwaters) to remove the taint of bastardy. His line continues until [the present times](wiki:Rennifer_Longwaters).(wiki:Jon_Waters.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
