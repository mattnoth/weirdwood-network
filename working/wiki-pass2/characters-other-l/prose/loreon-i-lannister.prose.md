## Origins

Loreon made [House Reyne](wiki:House_Reyne) of [Castamere](wiki:Castamere) his vassals by [wedding](wiki:Marriage) a daughter of that house.(wiki:Loreon_I_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

King Loreon fought and defeated [Morgon Banefort](wiki:Morgon_Banefort), the Hooded King, and his [thralls](wiki:Thrall), in a war that lasted twenty years.(wiki:Loreon_I_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) According to a semi-canon source, Morgon was said to have been a powerful [necromancer](wiki:Necromancy) who cursed his vanquishers as he died, claiming he would return from the grave. In response, Loreon had Morgon's body cut into pieces and fed to his [lions](wiki:Lion). Two years later the lions broke free within [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), slaying three of Loreon's sons.(wiki:Loreon_I_Lannister.cite_ref-2)
