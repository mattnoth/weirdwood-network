## Origins

In [158 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), after the [Submission of Sunspear](wiki:Submission_of_Sunspear) during the [conquest of Dorne](wiki:Conquest_of_Dorne), Cassella was one of the [hostages](wiki:Hostage) sent to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), as she was the daughter of [Lord Vaith](wiki:Lord_Vaith_(father_of_Cassella)). She was escorted by Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen), who kept Cassella a "hostage" in his own chambers.(wiki:Cassella_Vaith.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

After the [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) revolted and killed King [Daeron I Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen), all the hostages were to be killed. Aegon, by then bored of her, returned Cassella to her place with the other prisoners. The new king, [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen), pardoned all the hostages and personally took them back to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Cassella_Vaith.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Cassella never wed or had children. In her old age, the deluded Lady Vaith believed she had been Aegon's one true love and that he would soon send for her.(wiki:Cassella_Vaith.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

At Vaith circa [209](wiki:209_AC)–[210 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) met an aged Lady Vaith, and spoke to her with such lack of grace that he put his own life and [Egg's](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen), his [squire](wiki:Squire), in danger. According to Egg, Lady Vaith was mad, so it is unclear if that danger could have been avoided, but that Duncan could have been more gallant.(wiki:Cassella_Vaith.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Cassella_Vaith.cite_ref-4)

For a brief time before he met [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber), [Lady of Coldmoat](wiki:Lady_of_Coldmoat), Duncan worried that she was as old as Lady Vaith.(wiki:Cassella_Vaith.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

After taking a blow to the head during a [joust](wiki:Jousting) with Ser [Uthor Underleaf](wiki:Uthor_Underleaf), Dunk sees the faces of many people he'd met on his travels when he wakes up, including Lady Vaith.(wiki:Cassella_Vaith.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Appearances & Description

When she was young, Cassella was a willowy maid with green eyes and pale white-blond hair.(wiki:Cassella_Vaith.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) and his squire, Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen), considered the older Lady Vaith to be mad,(wiki:Cassella_Vaith.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and Duncan also found her to be sad.(wiki:Cassella_Vaith.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
