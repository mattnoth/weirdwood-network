## Origins

Dacks was the squire of a [Redfort](wiki:House_Redfort) knight, possibly Lord [Horton Redfort](wiki:Horton_Redfort), during the time when [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) and [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) were [fostering](wiki:Fostering) at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) with Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn). Robert once threw a rotten [orange](wiki:Orange) at Dacks, beginning a food fight in the castle's [High Hall](wiki:High_Hall).(wiki:Dacks.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
