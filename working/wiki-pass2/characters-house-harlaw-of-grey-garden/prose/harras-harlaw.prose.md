## Origins

In his youth Harras was a good friend of [Rodrik Greyjoy](wiki:Rodrik_Greyjoy). [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) enjoyed thrashing him when they were younger.(wiki:Harras_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Appearances & Description

Harras is tall with a long face and is austere. He bears a [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) sword, [Nightfall](wiki:Nightfall).(wiki:Harras_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Harras's personal sigil is quartered: a silver scythe on black, a peacock on cream.(wiki:Harras_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Harras_Harlaw.cite_ref-5) The scythe is from his [Harlaw](wiki:House_Harlaw) father and the peacock, the blazon of [House Serrett](wiki:House_Serrett), is from his mother.(wiki:Harras_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

The banner of Ser Harras is spotted by [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) at [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers).(wiki:Harras_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

Harras is spotted by [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) at the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot) on [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk). He is one of Asha's champions at the kingsmoot.(wiki:Harras_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

[Hotho Harlaw](wiki:Hotho_Harlaw) reports to [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) that during the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), Harras planted his standard beneath [Grimston](wiki:Grimston), the [Grimm](wiki:House_Grimm) castle on [Greyshield](wiki:Greyshield), and fought and defeated seven men, slaying five in the process. The septon in the castle believed the [gods](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) had spoken and surrendered the castle to Harras. He is then named the new Lord of Greyshield by [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North). Hotho believes that this makes himself the new heir to Ten Towers.(wiki:Harras_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
