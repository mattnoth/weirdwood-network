## Origins

[Dywen Shell](wiki:Dywen_Shell), King of the [Fingers](wiki:Fingers), paid an [Andal](wiki:Andal) warlord to help him eliminate his foes, but instead the warlord turned his swords upon him and Shell was roasted alive inside his wooden longhall. An Andal [knight](wiki:Knight) named [Corwyn Corbray](wiki:Corwyn_Corbray_(lord)) took Dywen's wife as his bedwarmer.
