## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) and his mother [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) arrive at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) after the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), they are greeted by Cat's brother, Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully), and Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood). One of Edmure's guards splashes into the water when [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind) bounds from the [boat](wiki:Boat).(wiki:House_Tully_guards.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Clash of Kings

Ser [Robin Ryger](wiki:Robin_Ryger), [captain of the Tully guard](wiki:Captain_of_the_guards), announces the prisoner Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) in Riverrun's [Great Hall](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Riverrun). Robb, the new [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), sends Cleos to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) as an envoy.(wiki:House_Tully_guards.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

When Cleos returns to Riverrun with the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne)'s counteroffer, his escort of [red cloaks](wiki:Red_cloaks) includes four men—a mummer, a murderer, a poisoner, and a thief—tasked by [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to rescue his brother, Ser [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), from Tully captivity when Edmure is away.(wiki:House_Tully_guards.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The murderer kills two guards while the thief opens Jaime's cell. The mummer impersonates Edmure's voice to make their escape from Riverrun, but [Long Lew](wiki:Long_Lew) prevents the River Gate from opening when he sees Edmure returning to the castle. The Tullys hang the false envoys and some red cloaks who came to their aid, and Jaime and the rest of the Lannister guards are imprisoned.(wiki:House_Tully_guards.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

While the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords) is being fought, Riverrun is left with a garrison of green boys.(wiki:House_Tully_guards.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Catelyn frees Jaime from the dungeon and sends him with Cleos to King's Landing to be exchanged for her daughters, [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark). Ser [Desmond Grell](wiki:Desmond_Grell), the castellan while Edmure is in the field, sends Robin to recapture Jaime.(wiki:House_Tully_guards.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Jaime's escort, [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth), sinks Robin's galley, however, halting the pursuit.(wiki:House_Tully_guards.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

The guards [Delp](wiki:Delp) and [Elwood](wiki:Elwood) are killed by Lord [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark)'s men when Rickard has the captives [Tion Frey](wiki:Tion_Frey) and [Willem Lannister](wiki:Willem_Lannister) murdered.(wiki:House_Tully_guards.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Long Lew is ready to execute Rickard the next morning, but Robb chooses to behead Lord Karstark himself.(wiki:House_Tully_guards.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Robb names Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) the castellan of Riverrun(wiki:House_Tully_guards.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and [Warden of the Southern Marches](wiki:Warden_of_the_Southern_Marches)(wiki:House_Tully_guards.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) when he and Edmure depart for the [Twins](wiki:Twins). Robb and Catelyn are then killed and Edmure is captured in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:House_Tully_guards.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:House_Tully_guards.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Feast for Crows

During the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun) by forces sworn to King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), Jaime explains that the garrison will be allowed to [take the black](wiki:Night's_Watch) if Edmure yields the castle.(wiki:House_Tully_guards.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Although Edmure negotiates the castle's surrender, Brynden the Blackfish escapes in the night. Jaime allows the garrison to safely depart after they relinquish their weapons and armor. Robin chooses to take the black along with Desmond, however.(wiki:House_Tully_guards.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
