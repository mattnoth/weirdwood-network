## Origins

Ser [Elyas Scales](wiki:Elyas_Scales) was the [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms) at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) during the [regency](wiki:Regency) of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen). Ser Elyas was known to spar with the young king during his training.(wiki:House_Scales.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser [Benethon Scales](wiki:Benethon_Scales) is in service to King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), and is among the knights known as the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men).(wiki:House_Scales.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) When Stannis goes to take [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte), Benethon remains with Queen [Selyse](wiki:Selyse_Florent), later accompanying her to the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort) when she travels from [Eastwatch](wiki:Eastwatch) to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:House_Scales.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Benethon is present when Selyse refuses [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) aid in rescuing the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings), and later appears in the [Shieldhall](wiki:Shieldhall) when Jon Snow informs his men about his plans for Hardhome and the [letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) he has received from [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Bolton).(wiki:House_Scales.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
