## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Lord Tremond is among the [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) nobles who accompany Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for the wedding of [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Tremond_Gargalen.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Tremond is present at the feast when Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) arrives in [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) to deliver the [Mountain's skull](wiki:Mountain's_skull). He refuses to drink when [Ricasso](wiki:Ricasso) raises a toast to King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Tremond_Gargalen.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
