## Origins

According to Roose, Bethany never made a sound in bed.(wiki:Bethany_Bolton.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Bethany had one son with Roose, [Domeric](wiki:Domeric_Bolton).(wiki:Bethany_Bolton.cite_ref-Rawoiafbethany_ryswell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) During his youth, Domeric served as a page at [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) with Bethany's sister, Lady [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin). [Reek](wiki:Reek), a servant at the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort), was once caught stealing perfume from Bethany's bedchamber in order to his mask his unpleasant scent; the following year Reek was caught drinking the perfume.(wiki:Bethany_Bolton.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Bethany died of a fever.(wiki:Bethany_Bolton.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Domeric died in [297 AC](wiki:297_AC),(wiki:Bethany_Bolton.cite_ref-5) allegedly [poisoned](wiki:Poison) by her husband's bastard, [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow).(wiki:Bethany_Bolton.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)
