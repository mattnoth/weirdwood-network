## Origins

Lord Ryswell's [youngest son](wiki:Lord_Ryswell's_youngest_son) was one of the legendary [seventy-nine sentinels](wiki:Seventy-nine_sentinels) who deserted their posts at the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort) and went south to the lands of [House Ryswell](wiki:House_Ryswell). They hoped that Lord Ryswell would aid them, but instead they were captured and imprisoned within the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Lord_Ryswell_(Night's_Watch).cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Lord Ryswell, as he was dying, had himself carried to the Wall to take the black of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), so he could stand beside the son that he had condemned to certain death.(wiki:Lord_Ryswell_(Night's_Watch).cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
