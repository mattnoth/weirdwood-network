## Origins

Ser Adrian was inducted into the Queensguard after Rhaenyra [seized](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing) [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) and commanded [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard) [Lorent Marbrand](wiki:Lorent_Marbrand) to recruit six knights to serve alongside him.(wiki:Adrian_Redfort.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Ser Adrian was killed in defense of Rhaenyra by the men of Ser [Alfred Broome](wiki:Alfred_Broome) at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). He was stabbed in the back by a spear.(wiki:Adrian_Redfort.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
