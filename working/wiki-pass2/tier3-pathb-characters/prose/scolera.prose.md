## Appearances & Description

Scolera is short, thick-waisted with heavy breasts and olive skin. [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) believes Scolera has a sour smell.(wiki:Scolera.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Scolera is among the septas who arrest Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) at the Great Sept of Baelor.(wiki:Scolera.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Scolera continues to be one of the holy septas who visit Cersei every hour of the day or night and asking her to confess, sometimes reading from [The Seven-Pointed Star](wiki:The_Seven-Pointed_Star). When Cersei relents, Scolera is one of the septas who bring her before the [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow).(wiki:Scolera.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Septas Scolera, [Moelle](wiki:Moelle), and [Unella](wiki:Unella) are part of the escort for Cersei as she completes her [walk of atonement](wiki:Walk_of_atonement) through the streets of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Scolera.cite_ref-Radwd65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
