## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

Marro plays the part of the [Stranger](wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven)) in [The Bloody Hand](wiki:The_Bloody_Hand). As the troupe prepares for the start of the play, he tells [Mercy](wiki:Mercy) to bring paste as his horn is coming loose. Mercy fetches the sticky paste and fastens the Stranger’s left horn back onto his forehead. Marro is in the opening [lichyard](wiki:Lichyard) scene with [Bobono](wiki:Bobono).(wiki:Marro.cite_ref-winds_1-1)
