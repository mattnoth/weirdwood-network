## Origins

Benedict II was the son and heir of the founder of [House Justman](wiki:House_Justman), King [Benedict I](wiki:Benedict_I_Justman). Benedict II ruled for sixty years, adding [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale), [Rosby](wiki:Rosby), and the mouth of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) to the Justman realm.(wiki:Benedict_II_Justman.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
