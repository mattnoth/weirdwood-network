## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Meizo was the elder of the two eunuchs employed by Salladhor to count the cargoes of the [Bountiful Harvest](wiki:Bountiful_Harvest) after it had been seized. He takes Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) to the owner's cabin and serves him some hot [wine](wiki:Wine) with cloves.(wiki:Meizo_Mahr.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
