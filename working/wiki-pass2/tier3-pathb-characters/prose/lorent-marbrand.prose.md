## Origins

When the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) began in [129 AC](wiki:129_AC), Ser Lorent and Ser [Erryk Cargyll](wiki:Erryk_Cargyll) were on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). They chose to side with Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and the [blacks](wiki:Blacks), joining her [Queensguard](wiki:Queensguard) under Lord Commander Ser [Steffon Darklyn](wiki:Steffon_Darklyn).(wiki:Lorent_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rfab13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Ser Steffon died during the [Sowing of the Seeds](wiki:Sowing),(wiki:Lorent_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rfab15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and Queen Rhaenyra named Lorent the [Lord Commander of the Queensguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard) after she ascended to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) following the [fall of King's Landing](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing). Ser Lorent was tasked with finding six worthy [knights](wiki:Knights) to serve beside him in Rhaenyra's Queensguard.(wiki:Lorent_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rfab16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Lorent_Marbrand.cite_ref-5) After the [Treasons of Tumbleton](wiki:Treasons_of_Tumbleton) by the [Two Betrayers](wiki:Two_Betrayers), Lorent was among those who counseled Queen Rhaenyra to arrest the other two [baseborn](wiki:Baseborn) [dragonriders](wiki:Dragonrider), Ser [Addam Velaryon](wiki:Addam_Velaryon) and [Nettles](wiki:Nettles).(wiki:Lorent_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rfab16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
Lorent died during the [riot of King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)), leading a hundred knights and [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms) into [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom), of which only sixteen returned. According to [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom), Rhaenyra wept when she was told of the manner of Lorent's death. His body was retrieved by his sworn brother Ser [Glendon Goode](wiki:Glendon_Goode), to prevent the rioters from despoiling it. Ser Glendon succeeded Lorent as Lord Commander, but less than a day later, the young knight also died in the riot.(wiki:Lorent_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rfab17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
