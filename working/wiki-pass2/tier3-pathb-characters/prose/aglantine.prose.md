## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Septas Aglantine, [Moelle](wiki:Moelle), [Melicent](wiki:Melicent), and [Nysterica](wiki:Nysterica) examine [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) to see if her [maidenhead](wiki:Maiden) is still intact, but they find it is not. This is the grounds for Margaery's arrest by the Faith on charges of infidelity.(wiki:Aglantine.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
