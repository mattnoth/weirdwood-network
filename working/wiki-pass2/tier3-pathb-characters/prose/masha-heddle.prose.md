## Appearances & Description

Masha is a fat and kind middle-aged woman. Her teeth have turned blood-red due to her fondness of chewing [sourleaf](wiki:Sourleaf). She also enjoys honey cakes.(wiki:Masha_Heddle.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Masha_Heddle.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Origins

Masha let Septon [Meribald](wiki:Meribald) sleep beside the hearth when she did not have room for him, and gave him [food](wiki:Food) when he left the inn. At some point, Masha told him the history of the inn.(wiki:Masha_Heddle.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
When [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) was a child, Masha was kind to her, and gave her and other children sweet cakes.(wiki:Masha_Heddle.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Upon her arrival at the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn), [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) remembers that when she was a child, Masha always had a smile and a sweet cake for the children.(wiki:Masha_Heddle.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
Masha does not want to get involved when Catelyn arrests [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), so she lets Catelyn take him.(wiki:Masha_Heddle.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
When the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) invade the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), Tyrion's father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), hangs Masha on a gibbet in the inn's yard as punishment. Tyrion sees her body when he arrives at the inn to meet with his father. [Ravens](wiki:Raven) have eaten her lips and eyes and most of her cheeks, baring her stained red teeth in a hideous smile.(wiki:Masha_Heddle.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Masha's nephew takes over the inn.(wiki:Masha_Heddle.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Masha's nephew is killed by an unspecified nobleman. Afterwards, his daughters, Masha's great-nieces [Willow](wiki:Willow_Heddle) and [Jeyne](wiki:Jeyne_Heddle), manage the inn, and become associates of the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners).(wiki:Masha_Heddle.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
