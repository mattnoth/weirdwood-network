## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

A [coin](wiki:Currency) that had been minted during the reign of Garth the Twelfth is found beneath the chamber pot in [Rugen](wiki:Rugen)'s cell by [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn). This leads Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) to believe that the [Tyrells](wiki:House_Tyrell) have something to do with [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s escape.(wiki:Garth_XII_Gardener.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
