## Appearances & Description

According to [Tormund](wiki:Tormund), he and his brother [Harle the Handsome](wiki:Harle_the_Handsome), who both had a son with the same woman, hate each other.(wiki:Harle_the_Huntsman.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Harle is among the wildling leaders who accompany Tormund to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) and declare a truce with the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). He presents [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) with a bear-claw necklace as payment for crossing the Wall. As agreed upon in the truce, his son and his nephew become hostages of the Night's Watch.(wiki:Harle_the_Huntsman.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) He is present in the [Shieldhall](wiki:Shieldhall) when Jon Snow reads aloud the [letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) purportedly from [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Bolton) entitled 'Bastard'. When Jon is done reading the letter, Harle is among those who stand up to show Jon his support.(wiki:Harle_the_Huntsman.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
