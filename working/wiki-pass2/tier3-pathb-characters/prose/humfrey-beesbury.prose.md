## Appearances & Description

Ser Humfrey was a young bold knight. In the lists, he wore yellow and black stripes clothing with three beehives depicted on his shield.(wiki:Humfrey_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Origins

Ser Humfrey participated in the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC) where he jousted against Ser [Humfrey Hardyng](wiki:Humfrey_Hardyng). They broke twelve lances in what was considered the best fought battle of the day, which the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) soon began calling "the Battle of Humfrey". Humfrey Hardyng won the struggle and remained in the competition as a champion.(wiki:Humfrey_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
The two Humfreys joined the side of Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) in the [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven). Humfrey Beesbury was slain by Ser [Donnel of Duskendale](wiki:Donnel_of_Duskendale) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) in the first pass.(wiki:Humfrey_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
Three years after the events at Ashford Meadow, Duncan remained troubled by what happened there and the deaths of both Humfreys and Prince [Baelor Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)).(wiki:Humfrey_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
