## Origins

Bernarr's three sons were taken as hostages after the river lords were defeated by [Qhored Hoare](wiki:Qhored_Hoare), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands). Three years later Qhored murdered Bernarr's sons, whilst they were held captive in a keep of [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), known afterward as the [Bloody Keep](wiki:Bloody_Keep),(wiki:Bernarr_II_Justman.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) because Bernarr was slow to pay tribute. Qhored's [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) crushed Bernarr's hopeless war for vengeance, and Bernarr was drowned as a sacrifice to the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God). The fall of the Justmans led the riverlands into a century of anarchy.(wiki:Bernarr_II_Justman.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Bernarr_II_Justman.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
