## Origins

During the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands), the [Hoares](wiki:House_Hoare) joined other [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) houses and [Andals](wiki:Andals) in bringing down King [Rognar II Greyiron](wiki:Rognar_II_Greyiron). According to legend, they performed the [finger dance](wiki:Finger_dance) afterward to choose a new king. Harras was chosen king, although he lost two fingers in the process and became known as Harras Stump-hand. Archmaester [Haereg](wiki:Haereg) instead suggested that Harras came to power by marrying the daughter of an Andal warlord.
