## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Following the arrest of her father, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) is returned to her room in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) after promising to support King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). She loses herself in a collection of stories, one of which is the story of Lady Shella and the Rainbow Knight.(wiki:Shella.cite_ref-Ragot51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
