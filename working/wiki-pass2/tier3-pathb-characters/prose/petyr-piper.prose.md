## Origins

In [105 AC](wiki:105_AC), Lord Petyr swore his fealty to Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) as the heir of King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen). At the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord Petyr stayed true to his word and supported the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) and Rhaenyra's claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Petyr_Piper.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
At the [Battle at the Red Fork](wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork), Lord Petyr and Lord [Tristan Vance](wiki:Tristan_Vance) faced the [westermen](wiki:Westermen) army commanded by [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister), [Lord of Casterly Rock](wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock). In the aftermath of the battle Petyr knighted [Pate of Longleaf](wiki:Pate_of_Longleaf), a squire who killed Lord Jason, dubbing him "Longleaf the Lionslayer".(wiki:Petyr_Piper.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Lord Piper died in a [battle at Acorn Hall](wiki:Battle_at_Acorn_Hall) against the invading [westermen](wiki:Westermen). According to [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom), his heart burst at the sight of his favorite grandson's head upon a spear. Petyr was succeeded by Lord [Stanton Piper](wiki:Stanton_Piper).(wiki:Petyr_Piper.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Petyr_Piper.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
