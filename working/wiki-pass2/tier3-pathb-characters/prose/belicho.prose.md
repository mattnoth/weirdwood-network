## Origins

Belicho was a renowned Volantene patriot whose famous exploits are recorded in the series [The Life of the Triarch Belicho](wiki:The_Life_of_the_Triarch_Belicho). His unbroken succession of conquests and triumphs ended rather abruptly when he was eaten by [giants](wiki:Giants).(wiki:Belicho.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
