## Origins

Maester Harmune has read and written the correspondence for [Cotter Pyke](wiki:Cotter_Pyke), the illiterate [commander](wiki:Commander_(Night's_Watch)) of [Eastwatch](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea),(wiki:Harmune.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) for years.(wiki:Harmune.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Harmune accompanies [Cotter Pyke](wiki:Cotter_Pyke) when he sails from [Eastwatch](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea) with eleven ships to rescue [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) at [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome).(wiki:Harmune.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
From [Talon](wiki:Talon), Harmune sends [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), a message that most of the ships are lost and that he, Cotter, and most of the crew are stranded and they need rescue. His report mentions that the [living dead](wiki:Wights) are all over the area.(wiki:Harmune.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
