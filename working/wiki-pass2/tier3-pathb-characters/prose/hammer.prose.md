## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Hammer, along with the rest of the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons), went over to the [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai)'i when [Brown Ben Plumm](wiki:Ben_Plumm) thought [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s cause was lost.(wiki:Hammer.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
According to [Kem](wiki:Kem), Hammer is drunk more often than not. He tells [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) that Brown Ben lets it go, but that one day they'd get a real armorer. He is sleeping off a drunk when Tyrion and [Penny](wiki:Penny) arrive at the forge to pick out some company [steel](wiki:Steel) after signing with the company, just as Kem had predicted. [Nail](wiki:Nail) is there to admit them to the wagons to choose weapons and armor. [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) was already choosing his armor from the wagons when they arrived.(wiki:Hammer.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
