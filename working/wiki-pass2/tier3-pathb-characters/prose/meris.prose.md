## Appearances & Description

Meris is a thumb under six feet tall.(wiki:Meris.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) She has blond hair.(wiki:Meris.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) She is earless, and has a slit nose and scars crossing both her cheeks.(wiki:Meris.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) She is said to have cold dead eyes,(wiki:Meris.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) like two grey stones.(wiki:Meris.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Meris dresses all in [mail](wiki:Mail)(wiki:Meris.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) and carries a [crossbow](wiki:Crossbow).(wiki:Meris.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) There is talk that underneath her shirt there are only scars, left by the men who cut off her breasts.(wiki:Meris.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
[Old Bill Bone](wiki:Old_Bill_Bone) says that Meris can stretch out a man's dying for a [moon](wiki:Moon)'s turn.(wiki:Meris.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

## Origins

Originally from [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), Meris has been with the [free companies](wiki:Free_companies) for twenty years.(wiki:Meris.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) She was reportedly raped by half the members of a sellsword company.(wiki:Meris.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
Meris eventually joined the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown),(wiki:Meris.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) and now serves as the company's [torturer](wiki:Torturer).(wiki:Meris.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)(wiki:Meris.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Meris and the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown) participate in the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor). The [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince) sends her along with several dozen [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) members of the company to secretly go over to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen); the captain does not have faith in the [Yunkish](wiki:Yunkai) commanders and wants to ensure the Windblown will be on the winning side. Meris is displeased when [Dick Straw](wiki:Dick_Straw) mentions he has slept with her several times.(wiki:Meris.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)
Meris is arrested with the other Windblown when Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) reveals to Daenerys the intentions of the Tattered Prince. Daenerys eventually orders that Meris be released as a gesture of her regard and brought back to the Tattered Prince, stating to Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) that if the Tattered Prince is a clever man he will understand her gesture.(wiki:Meris.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Meris tells Barristan that the Tattered Prince has wanted to betray Yunkai from the beginning, and the captain decided to join Daenerys in the hopes she will grant him [Pentos](wiki:Pentos).(wiki:Meris.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Meris is present when the Tattered Prince meets with Quentyn and his two companions, Ser [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater) and Ser [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood), at the [Purple Lotus](wiki:Purple_Lotus).(wiki:Meris.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5)
Quentyn hires the Windblown to try and capture one of Daenerys's [dragons](wiki:Dragon), and Meris forces some [Brazen Beasts](wiki:Brazen_Beasts) to give them their daily code word.(wiki:Meris.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Meris and the rest of the men flee when they see how dangerous [Viserion](wiki:Viserion) and [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal) are and that they cannot be reliably controlled.(wiki:Meris.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)(wiki:Meris.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
