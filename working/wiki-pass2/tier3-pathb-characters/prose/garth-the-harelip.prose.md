## Origins

During the [riots of King's landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)), Garth led the men of the [Dragon Gate](wiki:Dragon_Gate) garrison against the rioters and managed to restore some semblance of order to the streets north and east of Rhaenys's Hill.(wiki:Garth_the_Harelip.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
When a mob marched for the [storming of the Dragonpit](wiki:Storming_of_the_Dragonpit), Rhaenyra sent riders to Garth and Ser [Balon Byrch](wiki:Balon_Byrch), commanding them to lead their respective garrisons to disperse the rioters, seize the [Shepherd](wiki:Shepherd), and protect [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen)'s [dragons](wiki:Dragon). It is unknown if the riders reached their destination.(wiki:Garth_the_Harelip.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
After Rhaenyra fled the capital, Garth was charged with defense of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep). However, not half a day passed before he struck Rhaenyra's banners and opened his gates to Ser [Perkin the Flea](wiki:Perkin_the_Flea) and his gutter knights, trusting to their mercy. Garth was dragged before the Flea and beheaded, however, along with twenty knights still loyal to the queen.(wiki:Garth_the_Harelip.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
