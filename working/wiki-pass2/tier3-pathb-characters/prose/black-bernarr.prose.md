## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Black Bernarr is part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) by the Night's Watch and survives the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist). He breaks his ankle in a deep hole on the march south from the Fist, however, and is then carried on a horse.(wiki:Black_Bernarr.cite_ref-Rasos18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He is skeptical of [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) having slain an [Other](wiki:Other) with a [dragonglass](wiki:Dragonglass) dagger.(wiki:Black_Bernarr.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Black Bernarr stays loyal to the Watch during the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep) and is one of the few survivors of the ranging to make it all the way back to Castle Black.(wiki:Black_Bernarr.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Black_Bernarr.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
