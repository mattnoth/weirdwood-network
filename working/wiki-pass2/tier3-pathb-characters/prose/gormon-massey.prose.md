## Origins

As predicted by Ser [Otto Hightower](wiki:Otto_Hightower), upon the death of King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen), Lord Gormon Massey sided with Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), sitting among the lesser lords of the [black council](wiki:Black_council) at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). Lord Massey counted himself among Rhaenyra's loyalists, well-aware that King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) would name him a traitor, having already received a summons from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) demanding an oath of loyalty from him.(wiki:Gormon_Massey.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
When Prince [Jacaerys Velaryon](wiki:Jacaerys_Velaryon) [called](wiki:Sowing_of_the_Seeds) for [dragonriders](wiki:Dragonrider), Gormon made an attempt to ride [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor), but was burned to death on approach.(wiki:Gormon_Massey.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
