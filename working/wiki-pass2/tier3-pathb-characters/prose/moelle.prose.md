## Appearances & Description

Moelle has stiff white hair, a wrinkled face as sharp as an axe with lips pursed in perpetual disapproval, and small eyes that look constantly crinkled in suspicion. She has a stern expression.(wiki:Moelle.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Moelle.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Septa Moelle is escorted by six of the [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow)'s [knights](wiki:Warrior's_Sons) when she comes before the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) to discuss the causes of Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell)'s arrest. She explains that Margaery and her ladies cannot be released until their innocence has been proven, and reveals that she examined Margaery herself, and found the girl's [maidenhead](wiki:Maiden) to no longer be intact.(wiki:Moelle.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
When Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) is arrested, it is Moelle who brings her watery grey gruel on the first morning, and fish and bread that evening.(wiki:Moelle.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Moelle is one of the septas who visit Cersei Lannister regularly. Cersei is awakened often with a demand of a confession. Moelle tells her it is her sins which are keeping her sleepless. She is present when Cersei declares she is ready to confess, and escorts her to the [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Septon). Moelle is later amongst the septas who escort Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) to Cersei's cell.(wiki:Moelle.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
Moelle, together with Septas [Unella](wiki:Unella) and [Scolera](wiki:Scolera), are part of the escort for Cersei as she completes her [walk of atonement](wiki:Walk_of_atonement) through the streets of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Moelle explains Cersei must walk it barefoot.(wiki:Moelle.cite_ref-Radwd65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
