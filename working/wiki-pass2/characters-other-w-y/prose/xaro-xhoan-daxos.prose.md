## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Category:Images_of_Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos)*

Xaro is a languid, elegant man, pale and lean.(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He has a bald head, and a great beak of a nose which is crusted with [rubies](wiki:Ruby), [opals](wiki:Opal), and flakes of [jade](wiki:Jade),(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) giving him the aspect of some strange glittery bird.(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) His fingers are long and elegant.(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Xaro can cry at will, a skill typical of [Qartheen](wiki:Qartheen) men.(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) It is implied he has a homosexual orientation.(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Xaro wears robes of maroon [silk](wiki:Silk) and cloth-of-gold.(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

[Xaro's palatial home](wiki:Xaro's_home) is larger than some market towns.(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) He owns eighty-four vessels, including *[Vermillion Kiss](wiki:Vermillion_Kiss)*, the galleas *[Silken Cloud](wiki:Silken_Cloud)*, and his pleasure barge.(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) The merchant prince travels in a [palanquin](wiki:Palanquin) with satin cushions(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) and also rides a [camel](wiki:Camel) with an ornate horned saddle.(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) He has [slaves](wiki:Slaves) in his service.(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

When [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) returns to [Vaes Tolorro](wiki:Vaes_Tolorro) after discovering [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), he is accompanied by three representatives of the city, one of them being Xaro.(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Having seen her [dragons](wiki:Dragon), Xaro escorts [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) to Qarth, where he offers her use of [his palace](wiki:Xaro's_home) during her stay.(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) The palace easily accommodates Daenerys's little *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* and their horses. An entire wing is given over to her, and she has [slaves](wiki:Slave) to tend to her every need.(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

Xaro gives Daenerys many gifts, including:

- a [silver](wiki:Silver) collar with an enchanted [amethyst](wiki:Amethyst) to protect against [poison](wiki:Poison);
- perfume and pomegranates;
- tumbling [monkeys](wiki:Monkey) and spitting snakes;
- scrolls from lost [Valyria](wiki:Valyria);
- an idol's head and a [serpent](wiki:Serpent)'s foot;
- a [palanquin](wiki:Palanquin) of [ebony](wiki:Ebony) and gold, and a matched set of bullocks to bear it, one white as [ivory](wiki:Ivory) and one black as [jet](wiki:Jet), with horns inlaid with jewels;
- a thousand [knights](wiki:Knight), each in shining armor. The armor is made of silver and [gold](wiki:Gold), the knights of [jade](wiki:Jade) and [beryl](wiki:Beryl) and [onyx](wiki:Onyx) and [tourmaline](wiki:Tourmaline), of [amber](wiki:Amber) and [opal](wiki:Opal) and amethyst, each as tall as Daenerys's little finger.(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

Xaro offers Daenerys his knowledge, telling her which members of the [Pureborn](wiki:Pureborn) to bribe, the ways of reaching the Pureborn, and who to go to next after she is rejected by them. Xaro also seeks to marry her, though Daenerys later learns he wishes to marry her so he can claim one of her dragons via a Qartheen marriage custom and use it to rule the city. It is also revealed that he does not seem interested in women, as Daenerys can tell that Xaro hardly deigns to notice her bare breast when she is wearing a [Qartheen gown](wiki:Qartheen_gown). Daenerys notices the beautiful boys who surround the merchant prince, flitting through his palace halls in wisps of silk.(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)

After the destruction of the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying), Xaro prevents the Thirteen from opposing Daenerys. Xaro offers to take her sailing around the [Jade Sea](wiki:Jade_Sea), but Daenerys again refuses his offer of marriage. Daenerys then refuses to trade him one of her dragons for ships, so Xaro tells her to leave the city.(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Aboard the *[Balerion](wiki:Saduleon)*, Daenerys contemplates the threats to her and thinks of Xaro. She hopes he is not among her enemies, but remembers that the Qartheen merchant had coveted her dragons.(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Xaro arrives on his ship *[Silken Cloud](wiki:Silken_Cloud)* in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) to visit Daenerys. He claims to have defended Daenerys before the Pureborn. Xaro brings forth a gift of thirteen ships—including *[Banded Lizard](wiki:Banded_Lizard)*, *[Narraqqa](wiki:Narraqqa)*, and *[Pureborn Princess](wiki:Pureborn_Princess)*—stating that they are hers on the condition she sails from [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay) to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) as her presence has disrupted the [slave](wiki:Slave) trade which many cities of [Essos](wiki:Essos), such as [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) and [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), depend on for their economies. Daenerys thinks the galleys were provided by all of the [Thirteen](wiki:Thirteen) and not specifically by Xaro. Xaro also provides Daenerys with a tapestry of the lands of [Valyria](wiki:Valyria) from before the [Doom](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria).(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6)

During Xaro's visit Daenerys again dons a Qartheen gown, leaving one breast bare in his honor. She notes that half of the men in the hall have stolen glances at her, but not Xaro. He warns her of many threats to her, including Pyat Pree. Daenerys has the ships he brought inspected by [Groleo](wiki:Groleo), but she later refuses the offer. Xaro's reply is to leave a bloodstained glove on a satin pillow, a message that Qarth has declared war on Daenerys.(wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7)
