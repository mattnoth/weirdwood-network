## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

When [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), in the guise of the blind girl "Blind Beth", goes to the [Inn of the Green Eel](wiki:Inn_of_the_Green_Eel), the patrons state the [Sealord](wiki:Sealord), [Ferrego Antaryon](wiki:Ferrego_Antaryon), is ill and when he dies Tormo Fregar will be the new Sealord. Later, when she tells the [Kindly Man](wiki:Kindly_Man) as a fact that Tormo Fregar will be the new Sealord, he reminds her that she merely overheard men drinking at the Inn of the Green Eel say that Tormo Fregar will be the new Sealord.(wiki:Tormo_Fregar.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
