## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Bayard is named by [Taena of Myr](wiki:Taena_of_Myr) as being among the group of young knights who give their attentions to Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Bayard_Norcross.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) had an arrest warrant signed for Bayard as part of her plans to remove Margaery. Bayard is subsequently arrested and sent to the dungeons.(wiki:Bayard_Norcross.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser Bayard remains imprisoned with the rest of Margaery's falsely accused lovers in a dungeon under the charge of [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn).(wiki:Bayard_Norcross.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
