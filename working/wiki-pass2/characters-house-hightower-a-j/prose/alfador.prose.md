## Origins

After Grand Maester [Mellos](wiki:Mellos) passed away, Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) recommended that her own maester, [Gerardys](wiki:Gerardys), take his place as Grand Maester. Queen [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower), on the other hand, wanted Maester Alfador named to the office. Beset from both sides, King Viserys chose neither, reminding both his wife and daughter that the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) would decide who filled the office.
