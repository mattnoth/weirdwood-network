## Origins

In [277 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), when King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) [accepted the invitation](wiki:Defiance_of_Duskendale) of Lord [Denys Darklyn](wiki:Denys_Darklyn) and traveled to [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale), Ser Gwayne accompanied him as part of the king's small escort. Upon arrival, however, the king was taken captive, and Gwayne was slain by Ser [Symon Hollard](wiki:Symon_Hollard), the [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms) to Lord Denys, in an attempt to defend his king. His death was later avenged by his sworn brother, Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy).(wiki:Gwayne_Gaunt.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Gwayne_Gaunt.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
