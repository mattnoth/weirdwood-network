## Origins

Alarra was married to Lord [Aethan Velaryon](wiki:Aethan_Velaryon) of [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark). During the marriage, she gave birth to several sons, including [Daemon](wiki:Daemon_Velaryon_(son_of_Aethan)), and at least one daughter, [Alyssa](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon), in [7 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Alarra_Massey.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
