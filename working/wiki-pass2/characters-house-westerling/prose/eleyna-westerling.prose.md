## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Eleyna becomes part of King [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s retinue when he marries her elder sister, [Jeyne](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling). The sisters stay at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) with their mother, [Sybell Spicer](wiki:Sybell_Spicer), and their younger brother, [Rollam](wiki:Rollam_Westerling), when Robb goes to the [Twins](wiki:Twins) for the [marriage](wiki:Red_Wedding) of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully).(wiki:Eleyna_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Eleyna is confined in Riverrun during its [siege](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun). She is freed and reunited with her father, Lord [Gawen](wiki:Gawen_Westerling), when Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) successfully ends the siege. For her actions that undermined Robb’s cause, Lady Sybell receives the promise of good marriage prospects for her two daughters by the Lannister regime. Eleyna returns home with her family among the [column](wiki:Column) led by Ser [Forley Prester](wiki:Forley_Prester).(wiki:Eleyna_Westerling.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
