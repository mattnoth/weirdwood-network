## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

During the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot), Fralegg offers [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) his support.(wiki:Fralegg_the_Strong.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) He is listed as one of Victarion's supporters at the kingsmoot.(wiki:Fralegg_the_Strong.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

At the feast celebrating the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), Fralegg is one of those who shout down King [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy)'s plans to sail to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay), proposing to raid [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) instead.(wiki:Fralegg_the_Strong.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
