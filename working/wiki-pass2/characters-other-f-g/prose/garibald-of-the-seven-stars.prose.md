## Origins

In [42 AC](wiki:42_AC), Ser Garibald was stationed at the [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) chapter of the Warrior's Sons, based at the [Sept of Remembrance](wiki:Sept_of_Remembrance). He fought in the [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven) in which seven of the Warrior's Sons faced King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) and his six champions. According to one chronicler, Ser Garibald sang a paean as he fought.
