## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Garth is part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) which leaves [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) and the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) in search of [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark) and [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder). He is one of the survivors of the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist).(wiki:Garth_Greyfeather.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Garth stays loyal to the Watch during the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep) and was one of the few survivors of the ranging to make it all the way back to Castle Black.(wiki:Garth_Greyfeather.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

When [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) is burning "[Mance Rayder](wiki:Rattleshirt)" at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), Lord Commander [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) has Garth, [Donnel Hill](wiki:Donnel_Hill), [Ulmer](wiki:Ulmer), and [Bearded Ben](wiki:Bearded_Ben) shoot arrows at the burning man to end his suffering.(wiki:Garth_Greyfeather.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Jon sends three groups each comprising three rangers [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) to scout and explore. Garth Greyfeather, [Black Jack Bulwer](wiki:Black_Jack_Bulwer), and [Hairy Hal](wiki:Hairy_Hal) are one such group.(wiki:Garth_Greyfeather.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) They are caught and killed by the [wildling](wiki:Wildling) known as the [Weeper](wiki:Weeper). Their heads are removed with their eyes plucked out and are placed on spears north of Castle Black.(wiki:Garth_Greyfeather.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
