## Origins

Hother Umber is the youngest son of [Hoarfrost Umber](wiki:Hoarfrost_Umber), former Lord of Last Hearth. His father believed that Hother had the making of a [maester](wiki:Maester) and therefore sent him to study at the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) around [250 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). While in Oldtown a [whore](wiki:Whore) tried to rob him, so Hother disemboweled the whore, earning him the nickname "Whoresbane." The story is told in whispers because the whore was probably a man.(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Hother appears to be a widower.(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

## Appearances & Description

Hother is old and gaunt with [flinty](wiki:Flint) eyes and a long white beard. His face is hard as winter frost.(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) considers Hother and his brother [Mors](wiki:Mors_Umber) to be hoary old brigands.(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Hother is made joint [castellan](wiki:Castellan) of [Last Hearth](wiki:Last_Hearth), together with his brother, [Mors Crowfood](wiki:Mors_Umber), by Lord [Greatjon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber) when the Greatjon marches south to answer the call to arms of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark).(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Clash of Kings

Hother attends the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) with his brother, Mors. He asks for ships to defend the [Bay of Seals](wiki:Bay_of_Seals) from the [wildlings](wiki:Free_folk) slipping past the [Wall](wiki:Wall) and the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). He is ordered to work with Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) to build a fleet. Hother reports that the Greatjon took so many of the Umber men south with Robb, that they lost half of their harvest because they have too few men to swing the scythes.(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) During the feast, Hother blows a silver warhorn during a performance of "[The Night That Ended](wiki:The_Night_That_Ended)".(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Storm of Swords

At [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), the Greatjon tells Robb that his uncles wish to remarry. Robb considers the possibility of Hother marrying a daughter of Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey).(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2) Robb is killed and the Greatjon is captured during the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) at the [Twins](wiki:Twins).(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Hother begrudgingly swears allegiance to the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort) because his nephew, Lord [Greatjon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber), is a captive of the [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) at the Twins.(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) However, [Mors Umber](wiki:Mors_Umber) offers to join [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) on the condition that Stannis pardon Hother and pledge that the Umbers will not fight against one another.(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

[Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), the new [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), does not trust Hother's allegiance and suspects that he plots betrayal.(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Hother and [Arnolf Karstark](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark) are seated next to [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) during the feast held at the Dreadfort before Ramsay marches on [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin).(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btheon_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctheon.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Hother recognizes "Reek" as [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) when he is brought before them and recommends to Ramsay that he slit Reek's throat, as he will most likely betray Ramsay as he did [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) with the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) Hother leads three hundred spears and a hundred archers south for the [siege of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin).(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Hother is present at [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark) of the [fake Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole) to Ramsay at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) He remains at Winterfell during the storms that follow the wedding. During a breakfast, Theon spots him talking quietly with Lord [Harwood Stout](wiki:Harwood_Stout). Among the snowmen that squires built along the battlements of Winterfell, one has an icy beard to depict Hother.(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Whoresbane predicts that a freerider thrown over the wall will join Stannis.(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) According to Lady [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin), Hother would love to kill Ser [Hosteen](wiki:Hosteen_Frey) and Ser [Aenys Frey](wiki:Aenys_Frey), who are also attending the wedding, but will not do so while the Greatjon remains a captive at the Twins.(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### The Winds of Winter

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) reveals to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) that the majority of Hother's forces are old men and Mors has green boys, as the main Umber strength went south with the [Greatjon](wiki:Jon_Umber) and likely perished in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Hother_Umber.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btheon_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctheon.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)
