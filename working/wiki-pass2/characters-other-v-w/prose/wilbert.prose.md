## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

When the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) see that the people of [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept) are keeping captured [northmen](wiki:Northmen) in [crow cages](wiki:Crow_cage), [Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams) asks if this was done on the orders of Ser Wilbert. A resident of the town replies that Ser Wilbert was killed by forces of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) the year before, during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). His sons are away in the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) with [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) and [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident), and the people of Stoney Sept believe that they do not care about the town. The [Mad Huntsman](wiki:Mad_Huntsman) has been serving justice in their absence.(wiki:Wilbert.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
