## Origins

When he was sixty-five, Humfrey was betrothed to a sixteen-year-old [Brienne Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth), her third and final match. On the day of their [betrothal](wiki:Betrothal), Humfrey told Brienne that he would expect her to wear only women's clothes after they [married](wiki:Married), warning her that he would chastise her if she would not obey him. Brienne, however, told Humfrey that she would accept punishment only from a man who could beat her in a fight. The [castellan](wiki:Castellan) turned purple with anger, yet agreed to put on his armor and fight her to teach her a woman's proper place. They fought with blunted weapons, with Brienne wielding a spike-less mace. The girl broke Humfrey's collarbone and two ribs, causing the betrothal to be called off.(wiki:Humfrey_Wagstaff.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
