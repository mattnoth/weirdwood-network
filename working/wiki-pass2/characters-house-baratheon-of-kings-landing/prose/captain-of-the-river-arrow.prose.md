## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

The *[River Arrow](wiki:River_Arrow)* is part of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon)'s fleet at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).
