## Origins

In [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), Ser Clarence took part in [the tourney](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls) held by Lord [Ambrose Butterwell](wiki:Ambrose_Butterwell) at his castle of [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) to celebrate his second marriage to [the daughter](wiki:Lady_Butterwell) of [Lord Frey](wiki:Lord_Frey). Clarence was soundly defeated in the lists by Ambrose's [commander](wiki:Captain_of_the_guards) and son-in-law, Ser [Tommard Heddle](wiki:Tommard_Heddle).(wiki:Clarence_Charlton.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
