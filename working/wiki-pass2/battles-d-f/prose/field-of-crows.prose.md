## Origins

The [Kingdom of Sarnor](wiki:Kingdom_of_Sarnor) was a confederation of cities, each with their own king, which was ruled by a High King. Many of the Sarnori, or [Tall Men](wiki:Tall_Men), sought to use the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) nomads for their own benefit, not recognizing the threat the horselords posed to their cities. Although several Sarnori cities were sacked and burned by the Dothraki, it was the fall of [Mardosh](wiki:Mardosh) that led the Sarnori to unite behind High King [Mazor Alexi](wiki:Mazor_Alexi).(wiki:Field_of_Crows.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf835_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

The Sarnori army contained six thousand scythed [chariots](wiki:Chariot), ten thousand heavy horse, ten thousand light horse, and one hundred thousand foot. They met four *[khalasars](wiki:Khalasar)*, nearly eighty thousand Dothraki, halfway between [Sarnath](wiki:Sarnath) and [Kasath](wiki:Kasath). The van of Sarnori chariots cut through the Dothraki, including Khal [Haro](wiki:Haro), causing his *khalasar* to rout. Mazor Alexi led the Sarnori in pursuit, but quickly fell into a trap. While Haro's survivors fired arrows at the Sarnori, Khal [Qano](wiki:Qano) attacked from the north and Khal [Zhako](wiki:Zhako) from the south. Khal [Loso](wiki:Loso)'s mobile *khalasar* attacked from the rear, preventing the Sarnori from retreating. Mazor Alexi and his great host were destroyed by the four *khalasars*. A hundred thousand men may have died in the battle.(wiki:Field_of_Crows.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf835_1-2)

## Aftermath

The Dothraki quickly conquered [Sarnath](wiki:Sarnath), which lacked defenders, and then gradually conquered the remaining Sarnori cities, with the exception of [Saath](wiki:Saath).(wiki:Field_of_Crows.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf835_1-3)
