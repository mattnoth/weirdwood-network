## Origins

[Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), who had captured [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fall_of_Deepwood_Motte) when [House Greyjoy](wiki:House_Greyjoy) entered the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), returns to Deepwood after the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot). She has four longships and near two hundred men at Deepwood and the strand of the [Bay of Ice](wiki:Bay_of_Ice).(wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

[Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), under the advice of [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), goes west from [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) to free Deepwood. While moving through the [northern mountains](wiki:Northern_mountains), Stannis wins the support of the [mountain clans](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans) [Flint](wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains), [Wull](wiki:House_Wull), [Norrey](wiki:House_Norrey) and [Liddle](wiki:House_Liddle), who remain loyal to the legacies of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) and [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), the [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)(wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-10)

## Aftermath

[Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) frees Lady [Sybelle Glover](wiki:Sybelle_Glover) and returns Deepwood Motte to [House Glover](wiki:House_Glover). This wins Stannis much popular support in the [north](wiki:North), with House Glover and its vassal houses as well as [House Mormont](wiki:House_Mormont) declaring for him afterwards.(wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-12) Some men sworn to [House Hornwood](wiki:House_Hornwood) also join Stannis' army.(wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Stannis ransoms many of the most notable ironmen,(wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) and only [nine](wiki:Ragged_nine) remain when he departs Deepwood.(wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-13) Stannis brings [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) with him during the king's [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell), leaving the other captives behind.(wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-14) [Morgan Liddle](wiki:Morgan_Liddle) apologizes to Asha for having cursed at her during the fighting.(wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-15)

[Cromm](wiki:Cromm) eventually dies of his wounds.(wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) [Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris), an envoy of the [Iron Bank of Braavos](wiki:Iron_Bank_of_Braavos), later ransoms [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley), [Fingers](wiki:Fingers_(ironman)), [Grimtongue](wiki:Grimtongue), [Qarl the Maid](wiki:Qarl_the_Maid), [Roggon Rustbeard](wiki:Roggon_Rustbeard), and [Rook](wiki:Rook) from Sybelle's dungeon. Tycho has the six remaining ironmen escort him to Stannis at the [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village).(wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)
