## Origins

Horro slew Khal [Moro](wiki:Moro_(Century_of_Blood)) and took control of his *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)*. He also took Moro's daughter, who had married the King of [Gornath](wiki:Vaes_Leqse) in an alliance between him and Moro, for himself.
