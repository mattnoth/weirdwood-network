## Origins

According to legend, he was born of the last of the [Fisher Queens](wiki:Fisher_Queens) and took to wife the daughters of the greatest lords and kings of the [Gipps](wiki:Gipps), the [Cymmeri](wiki:Cymmeri), and the [Zoqora](wiki:Zoqora). His Zoqora wife drove his [chariot](wiki:Chariot), and his Cymmeri wife made his armor. He also wore about his shoulders a great cloak made from the pelt of a king of the [Hairy Men](wiki:Hairy_Men).(wiki:Huzhor_Amai.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
