## Origins

When his grandsire, [Quenton Qoherys](wiki:Quenton_Qoherys), received the [lordship of Harrenhal](wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal) in [2 AC](wiki:2_AC) from King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), the succession of the newly-created [House Qoherys](wiki:House_Qoherys) was assured as Quenton had two strong sons and one plump grandson, Gargon.(wiki:Gargon_Qoherys.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) However, when Lord Quenton died from a fall from his horse in [9 AC](wiki:9_AC), Gargon directly succeeded him.(wiki:Gargon_Qoherys.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

During the rebellion of [Harren the Red](wiki:Harren_the_Red) in [37 AC](wiki:37_AC), the father of a [maid](wiki:Maiden) deflowered by Gargon allowed Harren to enter [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal). Harren and his outlaws dragged Gargon from his bed to the [castle godswood](wiki:Godswood_of_Harrenhal), where he sliced off Gargon's genitals, and fed them to a dog while Gargon bled to death. As Gargon was the sole living [Qoherys](wiki:House_Qoherys), the house became extinct and Harrenhal was then granted to [House Harroway](wiki:House_Harroway).(wiki:Gargon_Qoherys.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Gargon_Qoherys.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

## Appearances & Description

Gargon was fat and considered foolish.(wiki:Gargon_Qoherys.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He had a lusty appetite and was known to attend each [wedding](wiki:Wedding) in his domain to take advantage of the lord's right to the [first night](wiki:First_night), giving him the nickname Gargon the Guest. He also made free with the wives and daughters of his own servants.(wiki:Gargon_Qoherys.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Gargon_Qoherys.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aenys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
