## Origins

Vaellyn gave Archmaester [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn) the nickname "Marwyn the Mage" when Marwyn returned from spending eight years in the [east](wiki:Essos). However, he was annoyed when the nickname soon spread all over of Oldtown.(wiki:Vaellyn.cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) The first time the novice [Pate](wiki:Pate_(novice)) tried out for a link of [his chain](wiki:Maesters), he went before Vaellyn, who criticized him harshly. When Pate was later accused of theft, Vaellyn was among the archmaesters who interrogated him.(wiki:Vaellyn.cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Appearances & Description

Vaellyn teaches and judges the subject of the heavens,(wiki:Vaellyn.cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) presumably [astronomy](wiki:Astronomy), which has led people to call him "the Stargazer".(wiki:Vaellyn.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) He is renowned for his acid tongue, which has earned him his nickname "Vinegar Vaellyn". His ring, rod, and mask are [bronze](wiki:Bronze).(wiki:Vaellyn.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
