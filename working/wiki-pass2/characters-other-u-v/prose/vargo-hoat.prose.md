## Origins

Vargo was born in [Qohor](wiki:Qohor).(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Rawoiafvargo_hoat.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Vargo and the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) were brought across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister).(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Rasos77.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

After having caught a venereal [disease](wiki:Disease), Vargo began sending women to be examined by [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn) as a precautionary measure, before raping them.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

## Appearances & Description

Vargo is a tall and gaunt man.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) He has a two-feet-long ropey black goatee dangling from a pointed chin.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) A large tongue causes Vargo to lisp and slobber when he speaks.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Vargo is more cunning than he seems.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) He is a sadist who frequently tortures his prisoners by chopping off their hands and feet, earning him an additional nickname, "the Crippler".(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Vargo wears a chain of linked [coins](wiki:Coin) from all the places where he has fought. The coins are of every shape and size, cast and hammered, bearing the likenesses of kings, wizards, gods, demons, and all manner of fanciful beasts. Vargo rides a [zorse](wiki:Zorse). His coat of arms feature the [Black Goat of Qohor](wiki:Black_Goat_of_Qohor) and he wears a helm shaped as a goat's head.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

As war breaks out in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) orders his brother, Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), to have Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane), Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch), and Vargo raid the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) with three hundred horsemen each.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Tywin offers to add [Vale mountain clansmen](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) to Vargo's party, but [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) declines so that the clansmen can accompany him to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)

### A Clash of Kings

The [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) and the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) forage and raid near [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) and the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye). Vargo's sellswords are said to have slain Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion), although he has since been reported as alive.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

There is tension between the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) forces and Vargo's men, leading to occasional violence. One incident starts with an archer from Lord [Lewys Lydden](wiki:Lewys_Lydden)'s men taunting some Brave Companions over their failure to capture the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) and their leader, Beric, and leads to the death of Ser [Harys Swyft](wiki:Harys_Swyft)'s squire. After Tywin has the archer and two Brave Companions hanged, Vargo and Harys complete their reconciliation with a kiss.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

When Tywin marches his troops out of Harrenhal to pursue [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), who has invaded the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands), he leaves Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch) behind as castellan of the castle. However, Amory and Vargo hate each other and Vargo soon decides to turn cloak. He leads the Brave Companions to Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton)'s camp, where Vargo and Roose reach a deal: the Brave Companions arrange for Harrenhal to fall into the hands of the [northmen](wiki:Northmen), in exchange for which Vargo is promised the title of [Lord of Harrenhal](wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal) once Roose and his men leave the castle.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

As a result of the agreement, together with some plunder that includes a huge caged bear, Vargo brings a group of northmen to Harrenhal, led by [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover) and Ser [Aenys Frey](wiki:Aenys_Frey). He claims they are captives the Brave Companions took when they repelled an attempt by Lord Bolton's forces to cross the [Trident](wiki:Trident). As part of the feint, the northerners are chained, in ragged clothes and many fake injuries. Despite his displeasure with Vargo's assumed big catch, Amory falls for the trick and orders that the captives be brought to a dungeon, ignoring a protest by Robett that they had been promised honorable treatment by Vargo.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Before the Brave Companions can keep their side of the bargain of releasing the prisoners so that they can overpower the small force left to defend Harrenhal, [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), not realizing what is going on, organizes a successful liberation of the captives with the assistance of [Jaqen H'ghar](wiki:Jaqen_H'ghar), [Biter](wiki:Biter), and [Rorge](wiki:Rorge). The northmen and the Brave Companions, now joined by Biter and Rorge, then [bring the castle under their control](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal) and the following evening Roose arrives to take his seat at Harrenhal. Vargo and Lord Bolton watch on as the Brave Companions parade a naked Amory through the yard and throw him into the bear pit.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

The Brave Companions keep foraging for Lord Bolton with the additional assignment of rooting out Lannister forces they come across. Vargo splits up his group in four parties, the largest led by him, the others by his most trusted captains, so that they can visit as many villages as possible. In order to identify traitors, Vargo seizes people who had helped him while he was employed by Lord Tywin and confiscates the money they had received for their prior assistance. The Brave Companions often return to Harrenhal with bags full of silver.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Arya, serving incognito as the cupbearer Nan to Roose Bolton, finds out that he is going to eventually hand Harrenhal to Vargo and that she will stay behind when Roose leaves the castle. Arya flees from Harrenhal, taking [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie) and [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) along.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)

### A Storm of Swords

At least four [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) are sent in pursuit of [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), but the sellswords are killed by [a wolfpack](wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf)).(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Rasos3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

In the aftermath of the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Vargo fears the vengeance of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) and worries that Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) will switch from [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) to [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2) After Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) are captured near [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) by his men, Vargo has [Zollo](wiki:Zollo) cut off Jaime's sword hand(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) as a message to his father and to dishonor Roose in the eyes of the Lannisters.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-3) Vargo also hopes to gain in marriage the hand of [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark), offered by Lord [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark) to whomever brings him the Kingslayer, which would give Vargo the chance to become Lord of [Karhold](wiki:Karhold).(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) A displeased Roose still intends to leave [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) to Vargo, albeit knowing that Tywin is bound to retake the castle and chastise Vargo eventually.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-4) Vargo refuses to accept Lord [Selwyn Tarth](wiki:Selwyn_Tarth)'s offer of ransom for Brienne, as he expects to be rewarded with [sapphires](wiki:Sapphire).(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

After sending Jaime on to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in the company of [Steelshanks Walton](wiki:Steelshanks_Walton), Roose marches north to the [Twins](wiki:Twins) for the wedding of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) and [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey).(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Vargo tries to force himself on Brienne at Harrenhal, but she bites him in the left ear. Vargo throws her into the bear pit and has her fight his bear with a tourney sword and no armor. When Jaime returns to rescue Brienne from expected rape at the hands of the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions), he offers to ransom Brienne, but Vargo says that if he wants her, he should go and get her. After Jaime leaps into the bear pit to rescue Brienne, Walton's crossbowmen kill the bear to protect Jaime. Vargo reluctantly allows them to leave Harrenhal, stating he is 'merthiful'.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2)

Tywin intends to offer generous terms to rebels in the aftermath of the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), with the exception of the Brave Companions.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) After the effortless [capture of Harrenhal](wiki:Capture_of_Harrenhal) by the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men), Vargo's hands and feet are cut off.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Feast for Crows

At the [Whispers](wiki:Whispers), [Timeon](wiki:Timeon) explains to [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) that Vargo's ear became infected, turning black and leaking pus. [Urswyck](wiki:Urswyck) and [Rorge](wiki:Rorge) argued that the Brave Companions should leave [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), but Vargo, still believing he could be [Lord of Harrenhal](wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal), ordered his men to defend it for him. Almost all Brave Companions abandoned their leader and dispersed, however, allowing Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) to easily capture the castle. Before leaving for [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to fight at [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat) for the death of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), Gregor finished Vargo off.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) [Rafford](wiki:Rafford) took possession of Vargo's chain of linked coins.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

When Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) returns to Harrenhal on his way to ending the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), he commands that Vargo's head be shown to him. Although most of the flesh is gone and the lips, nose and ears have been sliced off, Jaime recognizes that it is Vargo's because of the distinctive goatee.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3) [Shitmouth](wiki:Shitmouth) and Raff inform Jaime that Gregor found Vargo sitting alone in the Hall of a Hundred Hearths, half mad with pain and fever from his festering wound. In a mockery of Vargo's preferred way of torturing his prisoners, Gregor cut off Vargo's limbs piece by piece over an extended period of time while he had the wounds bandaged so that Vargo stayed alive. Vargo first lost his hands and feet, then his arms and legs. The extremities as well as other parts of Vargo's body were then fed as "roast goat" to [northern](wiki:North) prisoners that Gregor brought to Harrenhal, including Ser [Wylis Manderly](wiki:Wylis_Manderly), as well as to Vargo himself.(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-4) Jaime orders his squire, [Josmyn Peckledon](wiki:Josmyn_Peckledon), to throw Vargo's head into the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye).(wiki:Vargo_Hoat.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-5)
