## Appearances & Description

The majority of Viserion's scales are cream, but his horns, wing bones and spinal crest are gold colored.(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) His teeth are shining black daggers.(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) His claws are black and sharp.(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) His eyes are two pools of molten gold,(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) and his flame is pale gold shot through with red and orange.(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Viserion has a roar that would send a hundred [lions](wiki:Lion) running.(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) Although his true color is cream,(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) he is often referred to as "the white dragon".(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He knows his name.(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4) Wisps of smoke emit from his nostrils.(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5) When wounded, his blood glows gold and red.(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-6)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

At the wedding of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) to *[Khal](wiki:Khal)* [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), she receives multiple bride gifts, including three petrified [dragon eggs](wiki:Dragon_egg) from [Magister](wiki:Magister) [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis). Viserion's egg is colored pale cream streaked with gold.(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

The *[maegi](wiki:Maegi)* [Mirri Maz Duur](wiki:Mirri_Maz_Duur) proclaims that in order to bring new life one has to give life. Daenerys burns [Drogo](wiki:Drogo)'s body, along with Mirri Maz Duur herself and the three eggs, birthing Viserion, along with his brothers, [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) and [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal).(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Clash of Kings

When Daenerys calls her people together and mounts [her silver](wiki:Her_silver) mare, she is wearing her [lion pelt](wiki:Hrakkar), and has Viserion on her shoulder. He sinks his sharp black claws into the lion's mane, and coils his tail around her arm, as she gives the order for her [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) to follow the [Red Comet](wiki:Red_Comet).(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

When Daenerys arrives in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), Viserion and the other dragons are a marvel in the ancient city.(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

When Daenerys receives the news of the death of King [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_Baratheon), the [Usurper](wiki:Usurper), Viserion is on her shoulder. He flaps wings the color of cream, stirring the air.(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Viserion is about the size of a small [dog](wiki:Dog). At [Astapor](wiki:Astapor), when Daenerys has the [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) [attack their former masters](wiki:Sack_of_Astapor), [Irri](wiki:Irri) frees Viserion to attack the [Good Masters](wiki:Good_Masters).(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Viserion is fond of the [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) captain [Ben Plumm](wiki:Ben_Plumm), and sometimes lands on Plumm's shoulder.(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Tales of dragons start to reach [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

While in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), Viserion and the other dragons misbehave and start to grow wild. Viserion sets [Reznak mo Reznak](wiki:Reznak_mo_Reznak)'s *[tokar](wiki:Tokar)* on fire.(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

As Daenerys walks out onto the terrace of the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid), Viserion senses her disquiet, as she is concerned about the [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy). She finds him lying coiled around a pear tree, with his head resting on his tail.(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) She watches Viserion take off and fly in widening circles, until he is lost to her sight beyond the muddy waters of the [Skahazadhan](wiki:Skahazadhan).(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2)

When [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) kills [a young girl](wiki:Hazzea) outside Meereen, Daenerys has Viserion and [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal) confined and chained in the Great Pyramid in a [makeshift "dragon pit"](wiki:Dragon_pit_(Great_Pyramid)) beneath the city.(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Daenerys leads Viserion down to the pit herself and shuts him up inside with several oxen. After gorging himself and falling asleep, he is chained.(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Daenerys is later escorted by Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) to the Great Pyramid’s pit to visit the dragons. She recalls that not long ago he had ridden on her shoulder, with his tail coiled around her shoulder, and she had fed him morsels of charred meat from her hand. When speaking with [Daario Naharis](wiki:Daario_Naharis) later, she thinks to herself that Viserion and Rhaegal are growing more savage every day.(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

When Daenerys takes Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) to see her dragons she notices that Viserion has shattered one chain and melted the others. He clings to the roof of the pit like some huge white [bat](wiki:Bat), his claws dug deep into the burnt and crumbling bricks.(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

After Daenerys disappears on Drogon's back, Prince Quentyn attempts to steal a dragon after enlisting the help of the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown). Quentyn realizes that Viserion is in the process of making a cave in the brick big enough to sleep in. Viserion appears and is first attracted to Pretty [Meris](wiki:Meris), as he is looking for Daenerys. Quentyn tries to tame him, but a crossbowman of the [Brazen Beasts](wiki:Brazen_Beasts) fires a quarrel at Viserion and is then killed by the dragon. Quentyn attempts to dominate Viserion with a whip. Unbeknownst to Quentyn, however, [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal) is behind him. The green dragon looses his fire and fatally burns Quentyn.(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-7)

In the aftermath the iron doors are left open and the two dragons escape. Viserion makes his lair in the [pyramid of Uhlez](wiki:Pyramid_of_Uhlez). Sometime thereafter, while on the terrace of the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid), Ser Barristan espies pale wings moving above a distant line of eastern hills beyond the city walls.(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### The Winds of Winter

As the [Second Siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_Siege_of_Meereen) begins, Viserion and Rhaegal are both drawn to the battle. Viserion flies above the battle being fought beneath the city. After donning his armor, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) steps out of his tent and sees some of the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons) gawking at the white dragon above. The [Wicked Sister](wiki:Wicked_Sister) is sending corpses through the sky, and some are bursting into flame as Viserion seeks to devour them. Some of the aflame corpses fall to the ground, causing [Yunkish](wiki:Yunkish) horsemen to catch fire, spreading terror and panic. By the time [Ben Plumm](wiki:Ben_Plumm) comes back from his meeting with [Malazza](wiki:Malazza), the Girl General, Viserion has flown back to its lair atop the [pyramid of Uhlez](wiki:Pyramid_of_Uhlez).(wiki:Viserion.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btyrion_ii_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctyrion.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
