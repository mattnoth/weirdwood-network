## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Whalen and his half brother, Ser [Danwell](wiki:Danwell_Frey), attend Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) during the parley with Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) at the [Twins](wiki:Twins), helping their father to his chair.(wiki:Whalen_Frey.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Whalen passes out while drinking with Lord [Greatjon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber) during the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Whalen_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Feast for Crows

[Walder Rivers](wiki:Walder_Rivers) tells Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) that Whalen participated in the slaying of [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind). Ser [Raynald Westerling](wiki:Raynald_Westerling) grabbed Whalen's axe in an unsuccessful attempt to save the [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), and Whalen claimed the wounded Raynald vanished into the [Green Fork](wiki:Green_Fork).(wiki:Whalen_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
