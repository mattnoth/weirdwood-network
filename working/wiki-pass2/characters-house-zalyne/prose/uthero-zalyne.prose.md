## Origins

Uthero dispatched envoys of the [Iron Bank](wiki:Iron_Bank) to [Valyria](wiki:Valyria) to pay settlements to the grandchildren of the owners of the [ships](wiki:Ship) Braavos' founders had seized - but not the value of the escaped [slaves](wiki:Slave). The [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlords) turned out to have little interest in the descendants of slaves who had escaped a century before. Several years later, Uthero sent forth ships throughout the [world](wiki:Known_world), announcing the existence(wiki:Uthero_Zalyne.cite_ref-4) of Braavos, and inviting the men of all nations to celebrate the 111th anniversary of the city's founding.(wiki:Uthero_Zalyne.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_braavos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) However, even after it unveiled itself, Braavos continued to be known as the Secret City,(wiki:Uthero_Zalyne.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_valyria.27s_children.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) with its exact location hidden for another two centuries.(wiki:Uthero_Zalyne.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

The anniversary of the [Uncloaking of Uthero](wiki:Uncloaking_of_Uthero) is celebrated every year, in a ten day festival of masks and revelry. At midnight on the tenth day, the [Titan of Braavos](wiki:Titan_of_Braavos) roars, and all celebrants and revelers remove their masks as one.(wiki:Uthero_Zalyne.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_braavos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
