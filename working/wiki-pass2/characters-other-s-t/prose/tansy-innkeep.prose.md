## Appearances & Description

Tansy is a large-breasted redhead. She enjoys teasing and has a throaty chuckle.(wiki:Tansy_(innkeep).cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Tansy greets [Greenbeard](wiki:Greenbeard), [Lem](wiki:Lem), and [Anguy](wiki:Anguy) when they arrive at the Peach. She makes fun of [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) when he appears. Tansy is abed with Greenbeard when the [Mad Huntsman](wiki:Mad_Huntsman) returns to [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept) with the captured [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane).(wiki:Tansy_(innkeep).cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Tansy_(innkeep).cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
