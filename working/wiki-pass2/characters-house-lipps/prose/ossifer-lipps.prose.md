## Appearances & Description

Ossifer is a large, grizzled man with a round belly.(wiki:Ossifer_Lipps.cite_ref-TWOW_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter

Summoned by Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn), Ser Ossifer arrives at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) to compete in the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) for the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights). While flirting with [Myranda Royce](wiki:Myranda_Royce), he is interrupted by [Alayne Stone](wiki:Sansa_Stark), who rescues her friend from his unwanted advances.(wiki:Ossifer_Lipps.cite_ref-TWOW_1-2)
