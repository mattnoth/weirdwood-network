## Origins

In [135 AC](wiki:135_AC), Priscella was accused of a [poisoning](wiki:Poison) attempt against members of the royal household. She had conceived the notion that Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen) would marry her if only [Larra of Lys](wiki:Larra_Rogare) were dead. She told the court that he smiled whenever he saw her, and his shoulder once brushed against her bosom when he passed her on the steps.(wiki:Priscella_Hogg.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Priscella was deemed to have only played a minor role in the plot, and on account of her [sex](wiki:Gender_and_sexuality), Lord [Torrhen Manderly](wiki:Torrhen_Manderly), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), gave her a minor punishment. She was condemned to have her nose cut off, with the understanding that the punishment would be put aside should she give herself to [the Faith](wiki:The_Faith) and so long as she remained true to her vows.(wiki:Priscella_Hogg.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Appearances & Description
