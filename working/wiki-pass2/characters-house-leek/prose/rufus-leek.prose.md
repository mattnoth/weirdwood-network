## Appearances & Description

Rufus is short, stout with a grey beard and an amputated left leg. He cannot read.(wiki:Rufus_Leek.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) visits the [Dun Fort](wiki:Dun_Fort) in an effort to find more information on Ser [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard). She is presented to Ser Rufus. When she offers him her letter of authorizations, he tells her that he cannot read and instead sends her to the [maester](wiki:Maester).(wiki:Rufus_Leek.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
