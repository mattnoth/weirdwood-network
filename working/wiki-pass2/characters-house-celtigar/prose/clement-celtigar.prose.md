## Origins

Clement became the Lord of [Claw Isle](wiki:Claw_Isle) after the death of his father during the [riot in King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)) in [130 AC](wiki:130_AC).(wiki:Clement_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

After the ascension of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), Lord Clement rode to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) from [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) with Ser [Corwyn Corbray](wiki:Corwyn_Corbray) and [Lady Staunton](wiki:Lady_Staunton).(wiki:Clement_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
