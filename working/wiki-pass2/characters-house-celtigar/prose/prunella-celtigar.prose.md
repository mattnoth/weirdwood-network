## Origins

Prunella was the daughter of Lord [Edwell Celtigar](wiki:Edwell_Celtigar) of [Claw Isle](wiki:Claw_Isle).(wiki:Prunella_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) She had at least one sister, [Prudence](wiki:Prudence_Celtigar),(wiki:Prunella_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) and a brother.(wiki:Prunella_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

In [47 AC](wiki:47_AC), her father urged King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) to marry either Prunella or Prudence, or both if he so desired. The king did not choose Prunella or her sister, instead marrying the [Black Brides](wiki:Black_Brides), [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)), [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling_(wife_of_Maegor_I)) and [Elinor Costayne](wiki:Elinor_Costayne).(wiki:Prunella_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

In [49 AC](wiki:49_AC), her father made the same offer to Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) and Queen Regent [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon), that King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) marry either Prunella and Prudence; Lord Baratheon rebuffed this offer.(wiki:Prunella_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Prunella became a lady companion to Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) during her time on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Prunella_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) During King Jaehaerys and Queen Alysanne's second [wedding](wiki:Wedding), she disrobed the king during the [bedding](wiki:Bedding) ceremony.(wiki:Prunella_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

In [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Alysanne arranged a marriage between Prunella and Lord [Uther Peake](wiki:Uther_Peake). It was considered an exceptional match for her, and a triumph for the queen.(wiki:Prunella_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Appearances & Description
