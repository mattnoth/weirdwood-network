## Origins

Once the greatest of the four castles of [House Osgrey](wiki:House_Osgrey), Coldmoat was raised by [Lord](wiki:Lord_of_Coldmoat) [Perwyn the Proud](wiki:Perwyn_Osgrey). However, the castle was taken from the family during the reign of [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) because Lord [Ormond Osgrey](wiki:Ormond_Osgrey) opposed [King Maegor's laws](wiki:King_Maegor's_laws).(wiki:Coldmoat.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8) At some point, [House Webber](wiki:House_Webber) became the Lords of Coldmoat.

[Addam Osgrey](wiki:Addam_Osgrey) served at Coldmoat as a page and a [squire](wiki:Squire). His father, Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey) of [Standfast](wiki:Standfast), once visited Coldmoat to suggest a marriage between Addam and [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber), the daughter of [Wyman Webber](wiki:Wyman_Webber), but the Lord of Coldmoat refused Eustace.(wiki:Coldmoat.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9) In return for House Osgrey's support in the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) promised to give Coldmoat to Eustace. Addam and Daemon were slain in the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field), however.(wiki:Coldmoat.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)

When Rohanne was Lady of Coldmoat, an Osgrey man named [Dake](wiki:Dake) stole sheep from Webber land. When Eustace did nothing in response to her complaints, Rohanne's [knights](wiki:Knight) abducted Dake and drowned him in a sack in the castle's moat.(wiki:Coldmoat.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-11)

Rohanne's men later dammed the [Chequy Water](wiki:Chequy_Water) and diverted the stream to fill her castle's moat, angering Eustace. Eustace's [sworn sword](wiki:Sworn_sword), Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall), and his squire, [Egg](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen), visited Coldmoat as Eustace's envoys. However, Rohanne revealed a royal decree granting the Webbers rights to the Chequy Water. After Duncan defeated Ser [Lucas Inchfield](wiki:Lucas_Inchfield) in a [trial by battle](wiki:Trial_by_battle), Rohanne agreed to wed Eustace, Ser Eustace is called the [Lord of Coldmoat](wiki:Lord_of_Coldmoat) by [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Cerrick](wiki:Cerrick) afterwards, but whether this is as co-ruler or as Lord Consort is unknown. As such, it is uncertain if Coldmoat is now under the joint control of House Webber and House Osgrey, or is still only under the rule of House Webber.(wiki:Coldmoat.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-12)

## Appearances & Description

Coldmoat has crenellated outer walls standing thirty feet high, with towers at each of the corners. It is surrounded by a moat. The [lion](wiki:Lion) [sigil](wiki:Sigil) of [House Osgrey](wiki:House_Osgrey) is carved in checkered squares in the stone above Coldmoat's gates, although it has been worn down by the elements.(wiki:Coldmoat.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Within Coldmoat's yard are kennels, stables, a smithy, and a seven-sided wooden [sept](wiki:Sept) with windows of leaded glass. The audience chamber of the [Webbers](wiki:House_Webber) is decorated with [tapestries](wiki:Tapestries) of battles and [tourneys](wiki:Tourney), and the stone floors are covered with rushes. Coldmoat also contains a [maester](wiki:Maester)'s tower.(wiki:Coldmoat.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

The castle can support twenty times as many [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) as [Standfast](wiki:Standfast).(wiki:Coldmoat.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) The lands near Coldmoat contain fields of barley, wheat, and corn, as well as six orchards of apples, apricots, and pears. The Webbers are proud of their [horse](wiki:Horse) breeding as well.(wiki:Coldmoat.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

During the rule of Lady [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber), Coldmoat's household included Maester [Cerrick](wiki:Cerrick), [Septon](wiki:Septon) [Sefton](wiki:Sefton_Staunton), and the [castellan](wiki:Castellan), Ser [Lucas Inchfield](wiki:Lucas_Inchfield).(wiki:Coldmoat.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)
