## Appearances & Description

Craster's Keep sits in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest) atop a low hill with an earthen dike around it. There is at least one gate on the southwest side of the compound and a stream runs around the north end of the hill. The gate is decorated with the skulls of a [bear](wiki:Bear) and a ram. Inside the dike, there is also a midden heap, a pigsty and a sheepfold.(wiki:Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

The keep itself is a daub-and-wattle hall that is long and low, made with logs and roofed with sod and big enough to hold thirty to fifty men at best. The door to the hall is made of two flaps of [deer](wiki:Deer) hide. The windowless hall is a single room, with a sleeping loft above reached by a pair of splintery ladders. Only Craster has a chair inside, and all others sit on the benches. There is a firepit in the center of the dirt floor, and dogs run around.(wiki:Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) There is also a cellar.(wiki:Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

The [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) stays at Craster's Keep during their [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) north of the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) [Craster](wiki:Craster) informs [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), that he had been visited by Ser [Waymar Royce](wiki:Waymar_Royce), [Gared](wiki:Gared), and [Will](wiki:Will) during their search for [wildling](wiki:Wildling) raiders. Waymar refused to stay overnight, however. Craster claims he has not been visited by [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark).(wiki:Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

[Ghost](wiki:Ghost), the direwolf of [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), scares the pregnant [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) when he kills a rabbit.(wiki:Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

### A Storm of Swords

Plotting to mutiny against the Lord Commander, [Chett](wiki:Chett) decides he will claim Craster's Keep and wives for himself. His plans are interrupted by the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist), however, and forty-four survivors of the Fist reach Craster's Keep.(wiki:Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Four die from their wounds, however, including [Bannen](wiki:Bannen), whose body is burned. There are no attacks by [wights](wiki:Wights) or [Others](wiki:Others) during their stay, and a number of black brothers practice archery in Craster's yard.(wiki:Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

[Clubfoot Karl](wiki:Clubfoot_Karl) and [Garth of Oldtown](wiki:Garth_of_Oldtown) believe that Craster is keeping food in a secret larder. During the feast before the Night's Watch are to leave, Craster and Lord Commander Mormont are killed in the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep). The mutineers gorge in Craster's cellar and rape his wives.(wiki:Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

### A Feast for Crows

The betrayers from the Night's Watch reside at Craster's Keep.(wiki:Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

During [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark)'s journey north through the haunted forest to the [cave of the three-eyed crow](wiki:Cave_of_the_three-eyed_crow), [Coldhands](wiki:Coldhands) kills a small group of foes from the Night's Watch.(wiki:Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
