## Origins

Oswell has been in the service of Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) for a long time.(wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Appearances & Description

Oswell is a grizzled(wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) old man, who is tall and gangling.(wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) He has a windburnt face, crooked teeth, and huge knuckled hands.(wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Oswell has long white hair and a hooked nose;(wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) his sons share his nose and smile.(wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Oswell is stronger than he looks, and has a fierce voice.(wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) His speech sounds [lowborn](wiki:Lowborn).(wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

In [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), King [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) dies at his own [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding), and [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) escapes the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) with Ser [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard). Dontos brings Sansa down to the shore of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush), where a cowled Oswell waits for them in a small [skiff](wiki:Skiff). Oswell rows them to the *[Merling King](wiki:Merling_King)* in [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay), where Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) and Ser [Lothor Brune](wiki:Lothor_Brune) await. After Sansa and Oswell board the galley, Petyr has his men shoot Dontos with [crossbows](wiki:Crossbow) and set the skiff afire.(wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

*Merling King* sails to the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn). When they arrive at the [Fingers](wiki:Drearfort), Oswell and Lothor row Sansa and Petyr ashore. Peter talks with Sansa about his plan to [poison](wiki:Poison) Joffrey, and reveals that he secretly hired [dwarf](wiki:Dwarf) entertainers to antagonize [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and frame him for the murder. Petyr further reveals that he has secret friends in King's Landing; he has Sansa examine Oswell, and she eventually recognizes him as a [Kettleblack](wiki:House_Kettleblack). Petyr reveals that when he learned Tyrion's man [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) was seeking [hired swords](wiki:Sellsword), he advised Oswell to send his sons [Osmund](wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack), [Osfryd](wiki:Osfryd_Kettleblack), and [Osney](wiki:Osney_Kettleblack) to King's Landing.(wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) Bronn secretly paid the brothers to work for Tyrion, and Ser [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister) hired the brothers to serve Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister).(wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) However, neither Cersei or Tyrion were aware of the Kettleblack brothers' connection to Petyr.(wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

### A Feast for Crows

When Lord [Nestor Royce](wiki:Nestor_Royce) arrives at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), Petyr has Oswell escort Nestor's retinue to the Lower Hall.(wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Petyr confirms that Oswell is aware of Sansa's identity.(wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

At the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon), Petyr tells Sansa that Oswell has heard interesting news from the crew of the *Merling King*, which has returned to [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown)(wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) after sailing to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

### A Dance with Dragons

Aboard the *[Selaesori Qhoran](wiki:Selaesori_Qhoran)* in the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea), the dwarf entertainer [Penny](wiki:Penny) mentions to Tyrion Lannister that she and her brother [Oppo](wiki:Oppo) had been hired in [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) to [joust](wiki:Joust) at King Joffrey's wedding feast, by a man whose name was something like Osmund or Oswald.(wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### The Winds of Winter

At the Gates of the Moon, the night before the start of the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) to choose the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights), Oswell arrives from Gulltown on a lathered [horse](wiki:Horse). He breaks his fast with Petyr Baelish, talking of what he learned in Gulltown.(wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Winds_9-0)
