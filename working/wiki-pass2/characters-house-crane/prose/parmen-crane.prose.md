## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser Parmen is part of King [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)'s army encamped beneath [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge) and a member of the [Rainbow Guard](wiki:Rainbow_Guard), Renly's version of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Parmen_Crane.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Parmen stands guard with Ser [Emmon Cuy](wiki:Emmon_Cuy) outside Renly's tent when Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) comes to see Renly before the battle at [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)) with his brother, [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon). When Renly is murdered within his tent a short while later, Parmen is not present, replaced by Ser [Robar Royce](wiki:Robar_Royce).(wiki:Parmen_Crane.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Upon seeing Renly's corpse, Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) kills Emmon and Robar in a blood rage, supposing they must have aided the murder. Somehow, only Parmen gets away from Loras's rage, despite being on duty as Renly's guard alongside Emmon.(wiki:Parmen_Crane.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

After Renly's death, Parmen defects to Stannis. Parmen and Ser [Erren Florent](wiki:Erren_Florent) are sent to retrieve Renly's foot soldiers encamped at Bitterbridge.(wiki:Parmen_Crane.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Parmen is imprisoned at [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) after being [captured](wiki:Fighting_at_Bitterbridge) by Ser Loras.(wiki:Parmen_Crane.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
