## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Burton accompanies [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) when the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) sails to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay). When the fleet sails past the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones), Burton knocks out four of Maester [Kerwin](wiki:Kerwin)'s teeth for smiling at him. When [Moqorro](wiki:Moqorro) is brought aboard the *[Iron Victory](wiki:Iron_Victory)*, Burton suggests sacrificing the [red priest](wiki:Red_priest) to the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God) before he can call a curse down on them.(wiki:Burton_Humble.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
