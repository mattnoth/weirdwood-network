## Origins

Cissy is the mother of [Alys Stone](wiki:Alys_Stone), the eldest [bastard](wiki:Bastard) daughter of Ser [Harrold Hardyng](wiki:Harrold_Hardyng). Cissy gained weight from her pregnancy, causing Harrold to lose interest in her. Lady [Anya Waynwood](wiki:Anya_Waynwood) arranged a [marriage](wiki:Marriage) between Cissy and one of the Waynwood men-at-arms.(wiki:Cissy.cite_ref-twow_2-1)
