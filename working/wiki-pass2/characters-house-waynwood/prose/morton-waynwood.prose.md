## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Morton is one of Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn)'s suitors after the death of Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn).(wiki:Morton_Waynwood.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Lady [Anya Waynwood](wiki:Anya_Waynwood) is present in the [High Hall](wiki:High_Hall) of the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), surrounded by her sons, when [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) demands a [trial by battle](wiki:Trial_by_battle) to prove his innocence of the claims against him.(wiki:Morton_Waynwood.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Morton attempts to convince [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) that Ser [Vardis Egen](wiki:Vardis_Egen), a [knight](wiki:Knight), will make short work of [Bronn](wiki:Bronn), a mere [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword), during the duel. However Bronn unexpectedly wins and slays Ser Vardis.(wiki:Morton_Waynwood.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
