## Origins

While Lord [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) was performing his duties in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) as [master of laws](wiki:Master_of_laws), Ser Cortnay was named the [castellan](wiki:Castellan) of Storm's End. He took a part in raising [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm), the acknowledged [bastard](wiki:Bastard) of Renly's brother, King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Cortnay_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Cortnay first met [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) when she was a child while visiting [Evenfall Hall](wiki:Evenfall_Hall), and again when Lord [Selwyn Tarth](wiki:Selwyn_Tarth) sent her to Storm's End.(wiki:Cortnay_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

Lord [Mathis Rowan](wiki:Mathis_Rowan) considers Ser Cortnay to be a seasoned battle commander.(wiki:Cortnay_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

## Appearances & Description

Cortnay is completely bald with a red, spade-shaped beard and a weathered face.(wiki:Cortnay_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

An older man,(wiki:Cortnay_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Cortnay is a seasoned commander(wiki:Cortnay_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and stubborn.(wiki:Cortnay_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)(wiki:Cortnay_Penrose.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Cortnay_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) and Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) consider Cortnay to be a good and brave man.(wiki:Cortnay_Penrose.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)(wiki:Cortnay_Penrose.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Cortnay is fond of [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm), [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son and his own [ward](wiki:Ward).(wiki:Cortnay_Penrose.cite_ref-Rawoiafcortnay_penrose.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Cortnay_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

As [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) marches towards [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) lays [siege](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)) to [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), held by Cortnay.(wiki:Cortnay_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) As the castle has a strong garrison, is well-provisioned, and held by the seasoned Cortnay, Renly's lords encourage him to continue towards the capital, though Renly refuses to decline Stannis's challenge.(wiki:Cortnay_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4) However, Renly dies before he can give his brother battle. Cortnay sends out ravens with a plea for help in breaking Stannis's siege, offering to bend the knee to whomever would send him aid.(wiki:Cortnay_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) He demands to see Renly's corpse before opening Storm's End's gates.(wiki:Cortnay_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

When Cortnay parleys with Stannis and his lords beneath the walls, Stannis demands Cortnay turn over the castle and [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s bastard, [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm), but Cortnay refuses, instead tossing his glove at Stannis and challenging him to a single combat duel. Stannis refuses this, and Cortnay tells him to elect a champion, insulting those who have joined Stannis after having sworn allegiance to Renly. Rather than risk losing the duel, Stannis warns Cortnay that he will take the castle only by storm—a threat to which Cortnay replies by reminding Stannis of Storm's End's name.(wiki:Cortnay_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

After the parley, Stannis tells Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) that [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) has seen Cortnay's death in the flames of the future. That night, Stannis sends Davos and Melisandre to have the castellan assassinated by one of Melisandre's [shadows](wiki:Shadow_assassin).(wiki:Cortnay_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)(wiki:Cortnay_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Cortnay's lieutenant, Lord [Elwood Meadows](wiki:Elwood_Meadows), immediately gives up the castle and Edric Storm.(wiki:Cortnay_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
