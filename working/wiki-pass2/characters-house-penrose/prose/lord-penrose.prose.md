## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Although he has raised his banners for King [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), Lord Penrose breaks bread with Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) when he comes as a messenger from his liege lord, [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon),(wiki:Lord_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) treating the Onion Knight courteously.(wiki:Lord_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) The banners of House Penrose are seen in Renly's camp beneath [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge).(wiki:Lord_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

During the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent) urges Stannis to bring Lord Penrose to the castle and hang him in order to force the surrender of Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose), the castellan of Storm's End and old Penrose's son.(wiki:Lord_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) instead sends a [shadow](wiki:Shadow_assassin) to assassinate Cortnay.(wiki:Lord_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
