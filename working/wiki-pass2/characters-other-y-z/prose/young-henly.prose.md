## Appearances & Description

Despite being well past fifty years old, he is called "Young Henly" by his black brothers to differentiate him from [Old Henly](wiki:Old_Henly).(wiki:Young_Henly.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Henly is among the men that [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) leaves behind to defend [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black). He dies during the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black).(wiki:Young_Henly.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
