## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Norbert is among the [river lords](wiki:Riverlands) who bend the knee to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) after the death of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident), at the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding). He is among the reluctant lords who join the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), sent as a representative to the war council for the recalcitrant rivermen. Norbert offers to talk with Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully), the castellan of Riverrun, in an effort to end the siege, but his suggestion is dismissed by [Clement Piper](wiki:Clement_Piper), [Lord of Pinkmaiden Castle](wiki:Lord_of_Pinkmaiden_Castle).(wiki:Norbert_Vance.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
