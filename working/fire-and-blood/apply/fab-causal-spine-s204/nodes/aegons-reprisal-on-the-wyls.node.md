## Identity

In retaliation for the maiming of the ransomed captives, King Aegon personally descends on the mountain fastnesses of House Wyl with Balerion, reducing half a dozen of their keeps and watchtowers to molten stone; the Wyls survive by hiding in caves and tunnels.
