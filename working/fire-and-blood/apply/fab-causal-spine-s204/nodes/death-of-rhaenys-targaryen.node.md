## Identity

Queen Rhaenys Targaryen perishes at the Hellholt in 10 AC when her dragon Meraxes is shot down; whether she lost her seat and fell, was crushed beneath the dragon, or survived only to be tortured to death by the Ullers is disputed by the sources, and her body was never recovered.
