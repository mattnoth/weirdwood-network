## Identity

In Lord Borros Baratheon's war camp outside King's Landing, Larys Strong and Queen Alicent strike a restoration pact with Borros, witnessed by Grand Maester Orwyle: pardons for Ser Perkin's men, Aegon II's marriage to Lady Cassandra Baratheon, and a betrothal for Larys, in exchange for stormlander muscle to retake the city and restore Aegon II to the Iron Throne.
