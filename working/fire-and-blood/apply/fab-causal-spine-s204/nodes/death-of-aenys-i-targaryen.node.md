## Identity

Worn down by the Faith Militant's rebellion against his rule, King Aenys I collapses and dies at Dragonstone three days after learning that Poor Fellows have surrounded his son and daughter at Crakehall Castle, leaving the throne vacant and his half-brother Maegor free to claim it.
