## Identity

During Aegon's renewed assault on the Hellholt, a Dornish defender's scorpion bolt catches the dragon Meraxes in the right eye; the dragon does not die at once but crashes to earth in mortal agony, destroying the castle's highest tower and part of its curtain wall.
