## Identity

With his support collapsed to a handful of crownland lords and his nephew Jaehaerys mustering an army at Storm's End, Maegor I Targaryen is found dead upon the Iron Throne at dawn, his arms slashed on the throne's blades and a blade through his neck; murder, suicide, and death-by-throne are all argued by the maesters, and he leaves no heir of his body.
