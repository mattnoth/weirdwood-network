## Identity

King Aegon II's younger son Maelor, fleeing with Ser Rickard Thorne, is seized by a mob at Bitterbridge after Thorne is shot down on the bridge; chroniclers disagree on the manner of his death (crushed by Willow Pound-Stone per Mushroom, hacked apart by a butcher per Eustace, or torn limb from limb by the mob per Munkun).
