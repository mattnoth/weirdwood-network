## Identity

At his own coronation on Dragonstone in 37 AC, newly-crowned King Aenys I tells his half-brother Maegor 'we shall rule this realm together, you and I' and gifts him their father's Valyrian steel blade Blackfyre — a bequest Gyldayn explicitly calls unwise, since it fed Maegor's later claim that the throne belonged to whoever had the strength to seize it.
