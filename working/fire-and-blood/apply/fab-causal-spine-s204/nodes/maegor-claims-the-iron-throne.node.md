## Identity

Within hours of Aenys's death, Dowager Queen Visenya fetches Maegor from exile in Pentos; he lands on Dragonstone and has her crown him with Aegon the Conqueror's iron crown, bypassing Aenys's son and heir Aegon and beheading Grand Maester Gawen for objecting.
