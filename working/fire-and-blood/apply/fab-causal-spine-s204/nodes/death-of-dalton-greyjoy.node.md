## Identity

Lord Dalton Greyjoy, the Red Kraken, is slain in his bed at Faircastle by his salt wife Tess, who cuts his throat with his own dagger and then drowns herself; his death instantly dissolves the ironborn fleet massed against Alyn Oakenfist and opens a succession crisis on the Iron Islands.
