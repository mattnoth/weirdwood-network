## Identity

The regency council -- Alyssa, Rogar, and their advisors -- settles swiftly on Orryn Baratheon, Lord Rogar's youngest brother, as husband for the thirteen-year-old Princess Alysanne, deciding the match without consulting her or the king; word of the plan reaches Alysanne before any formal announcement and spurs her and Jaehaerys to flee to Dragonstone and marry in secret instead.
