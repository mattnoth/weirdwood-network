## Identity

Ser Regis Groves of the Kingsguard, sent to reclaim Harrenhal from the self-styled 'witch queen' Alys Rivers, is killed before her gates within an instant of refusing to kneel to her infant son; chroniclers dispute whether by sorcery, crossbow, or sling.
