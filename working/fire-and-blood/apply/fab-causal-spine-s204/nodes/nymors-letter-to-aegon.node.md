## Identity

Princess Deria delivers a sealed letter from her father, Prince Nymor of Dorne, to King Aegon on the Iron Throne in open court; the letter's contents were never revealed, but reading it visibly shakes the king and leads him, after a night alone on Dragonstone, to accept Nymor's peace terms.
