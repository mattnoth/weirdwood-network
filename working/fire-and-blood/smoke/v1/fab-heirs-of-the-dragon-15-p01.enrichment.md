## Node Prose
### Jaehaerys I Targaryen
The Old King, called the Conciliator, whose reign was the longest and most peaceful any of the Conqueror's descendants enjoyed — quote: "the longest and most peaceful reign that any of the Conqueror's". He and Queen Alysanne produced thirteen children, of whom four grew to maturity and married — quote: "produced thirteen children." Twice he was estranged from Alysanne, in the First and Second Quarrels, the second over his passing Rhaenys over for the succession — quote: "the period of estrangement recorded in the histories as the Second Quarrel." After his heirs Aemon and Baelon both died, he summoned Vaegon and convened the Great Council of 101 — quote: "summoning his last son to King's Landing." He died in his bed in 103 AC at nine-and-sixty, and his remains were burned in the Dragonpit — quote: "His remains were burned in the Dragonpit."

### Alysanne Targaryen
Good Queen Alysanne ruled beside Jaehaerys for many years and died of a wasting illness in 100 AC at four-and-sixty — quote: "The queen died of a wasting illness in 100 AC." She was the most prominent dissenter against passing over Rhaenys, telling the king a cock is not essential to rule — quote: "A cock is not essential." She then flew to Dragonstone on Silverwing, beginning the Second Quarrel — quote: "flew to Dragonstone on her dragon Silverwing." To her death she insisted her granddaughter Rhaenys had been cheated — quote: "had been unfairly cheated of their rights."

### Aemon Targaryen
Eldest son of Jaehaerys I, anointed Prince of Dragonstone at seven in 62 AC and heir to the Iron Throne — quote: "Aemon was formally anointed Prince of Dragonstone and heir." He was knighted at seventeen, a tourney champion at twenty, and his father's justiciar and master of laws at six-and-twenty — quote: "he became his father's justiciar and master of laws at six-and-twenty." He was slain on Tarth in 92 AC by a Myrish crossbow bolt meant for the man beside him — quote: "was slain on Tarth by a Myrish crossbow bolt." His daughter Rhaenys was his only child — quote: "his daughter, Rhaenys, born in 74 AC."

### Baelon Targaryen
Younger son of Jaehaerys I, "Baelon the Brave," the Spring Prince; he earned knighthood at sixteen and wed at eighteen — quote: "The younger prince earned his knighthood at sixteen." After Aemon's death he avenged him by driving the Myrmen into the sea and was named Prince of Dragonstone and heir — quote: "avenged his brother by driving the Myrmen into the sea." By his sister Alyssa he sired three sons; two, Viserys and Daemon, lived — quote: "Baelon had sired three sons by his sister Alyssa." He became the King's Hand in 99 AC and died in 101 AC of a burst belly at four-and-forty — quote: "Prince Baelon died in his bedchamber in the Tower of the Hand."

### Rhaenys Targaryen
Daughter of Prince Aemon, born 74 AC, described as clever, capable, and beautiful, and a dragonrider — quote: "grown into a clever, capable, and beautiful young woman." In 90 AC at sixteen she wed Corlys Velaryon and was with child when her father died — quote: "she had wed the king's admiral and master of ships, Corlys of House Velaryon." She was the first to object to being passed over, telling the king he would rob her son of his birthright — quote: "You would rob my son of his birthright." Her claim and her daughter Laena's were set aside at the Great Council on account of their sex — quote: "Princess Rhaenys and her daughter on account of their sex."

### Corlys Velaryon
The Sea Snake, Lord of the Tides and Master of Driftmark, called one of the most extraordinary figures of the age — quote: "renowned in song and story as the Sea Snake." He first crossed the narrow sea at six and made nine famous voyages on the Sea Snake, the first Westerosi past Qarth to Yi Ti and Asshai — quote: "In the end the Sea Snake made nine voyages." His trade made the Velaryons briefly the wealthiest house in the realm — quote: "the Velaryons became the wealthiest house in the Seven Kingdoms." He built the castle High Tide, moved the Driftwood Throne there, and tripled the royal fleet as master of ships — quote: "Lord Corlys raised a new castle on the far side of the island." When Rhaenys was passed over he gave up his admiralty and took her to Driftmark, and later pressed Laenor's claim at Harrenhal — quote: "he gave up his admiralty and his place on the small council."

### Viserys I Targaryen
Eldest son of Baelon and Alyssa, chosen heir by the Great Council of 101 and crowned King Viserys I at twenty-six in 103 AC — quote: "the lords assembled chose Viserys Targaryen as the rightful heir." He was the last Targaryen to ride Balerion but never mounted another dragon after the Black Dread died in 94 AC — quote: "the last Targaryen to ride Balerion." He wed Lady Aemma of House Arryn; of their children only Rhaenyra survived — quote: "she had also given birth to a healthy daughter, Rhaenyra." Generous and well loved, he grew soft and plump and left the tedium of rule to his Hand — quote: "The king had grown soft and plump over the years."

### Daemon Targaryen
Second son of Baelon and Alyssa, ambitious, impetuous, and hot-tempered, given Dark Sister by the Old King — quote: "had been given Dark Sister by the Old King himself." He wed Lady Rhea Royce in 97 AC but scorned her and the Vale, calling her his "bronze bitch" — quote: "whom he called “my bronze bitch”." He served briefly as master of coin and master of laws, then as Commander of the City Watch, whom he outfitted with golden cloaks — quote: "King Viserys made him Commander of the City Watch." He regarded himself as rightful heir, coveted the title Prince of Dragonstone, and took the Lysene dancer Mysaria as his favorite — quote: "Daemon regarded himself as the rightful heir to the Iron Throne."

### Rhaenyra Targaryen
Only surviving child of Viserys I and Queen Aemma, born 97 AC, dubbed "the Realm's Delight" by court singers — quote: "the little girl the court singers dubbed “the Realm’s Delight.”" She became a dragonrider at seven on Syrax and a cupbearer to her own father at eight — quote: "At seven, she became a dragonrider, taking to the sky." Her uncle Daemon was ever attentive, bringing her exotic gifts from across the narrow sea — quote: "he brought her some exotic gift on his return."

### Vaegon Targaryen
Jaehaerys's surviving son, an archmaester at the Citadel known as Vaegon the Dragonless, pale, frail, and bookish — quote: "Known to history as Vaegon the Dragonless." Largely forgotten and never well-liked, few thought him fit to rule — quote: "his very existence had been largely forgotten." Summoned by the Old King, he proposed the Great Council; what passed between them is disputed — some say the king offered him the throne and was refused, others that he only sought counsel — quote: "Some say the king offered Vaegon the throne and was refused."

### Aemma Arryn
Lady of House Arryn, daughter of the late Princess Daella and thus a granddaughter of the Old King, wed to Viserys in 93 AC as an eleven-year-old — quote: "Lady Aemma of House Arryn, the eleven-year-old child of the late Princess Daella." She suffered several miscarriages and a son who died in the cradle before bearing the healthy Rhaenyra — quote: "Lady Aemma had suffered several miscarriages."

### Rhea Royce
Lady of House Royce, heir to Runestone in the Vale, wed to Daemon in 97 AC in a marriage that was not a success — quote: "Lady Rhea of House Royce, heir to the ancient castle of Runestone."

### Otto Hightower
Younger brother of Lord Hightower of Oldtown, chosen by Jaehaerys as Hand and continuing to serve Viserys I — quote: "he called upon Ser Otto Hightower, younger brother to Lord Hightower of Oldtown." Able but proud and haughty, his manner and access to the throne bred resentment — quote: "many found him proud, brusque, and haughty."

### Alicent Hightower
Otto's precocious fifteen-year-old daughter, who became the failing Old King's constant companion and was reading to him when he died — quote: "Alicent, became his constant companion." Near the end the king mistook her for his daughter Saera — quote: "he grew certain she was his daughter Saera."

### Laenor Velaryon
Son of Rhaenys and Corlys, a boy of seven at the Great Council, whose claim through the male line of Aemon was pressed by his powerful parents but rejected — quote: "Laenor Velaryon was male, and could claim descent from." He had a grey-and-white dragon named Seasmoke — quote: "a splendid grey-and-white beast he named Seasmoke."

### Laena Velaryon
Daughter of Rhaenys and Corlys, the "boy in the belly" whose birth was so debated but who proved a girl, born late 92 AC — quote: "proved to be a girl when born late in 92 AC."

### Septon Barth
The Old King's most trusted friend, "companion of my labors," who served as Hand of the King for forty-one years and wrote the Unnatural History — quote: "serving ably as Hand of the King for forty-one years." He died peacefully in his sleep a fortnight after the 98 AC tourney — quote: "Septon Barth died peacefully in his." 

### Mushroom
The court fool whose verbal account became The Testimony of Mushroom, a three-foot dwarf thought feeble-minded before whom lords did not guard their secrets — quote: "The Testimony of Mushroom, based upon the verbal account of the court fool." His ribald chronicle was ordered burned by King Baelor the Blessed — quote: "every copy of Mushroom’s chronicle should be burned."

### Septon Eustace
Of the royal sept in the Red Keep, a confidant of King Viserys, he set down the most detailed sober history of the period — quote: "set down the most detailed history of this period."

### Saera Targaryen
Daughter of Jaehaerys, alive in Volantis at thirty-four; three of her bastard sons came to press claims though she declined to press her own — quote: "her own claim was clearly superior to those of any of her bastard sons." She said she had her own kingdom in Volantis — quote: "“| have my own kingdom here,” she said."

### Runciter
Grand Maester newly arrived from the Citadel who tended the dying Baelon and recorded the cause of death as a burst belly — quote: "put down the cause of death as a burst belly."

### Ryam Redwyne
Kingsguard Lord Commander and co-champion of the 98 AC tourney, made Hand after Barth but removed within a year as unfit — quote: "remove Ser Ryam after only a year in office."

### Boremund Baratheon
Lord of Storm's End, brother of Jocelyn and uncle to Rhaenys, angered by her being passed over and later a supporter of Laenor's claim — quote: "Boremund Baratheon (uncle to Rhaenys and greatuncle to the boy Laenor)."
