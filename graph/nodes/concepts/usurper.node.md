## Origins

Throughout history, people have rebelled against the regime in place and been denounced as usurpers, such as:

- The [Bloodstone Emperor](wiki:Bloodstone_Emperor), who usurped his sister the [Amethyst Empress](wiki:Amethyst_Empress) in a event remember as the [Blood Betrayal](wiki:Blood_Betrayal).(wiki:Usurper.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
- [Ronard Storm](wiki:Ronard_Storm), who usurped his half-brother King [Morden II Durrandon](wiki:Morden_II_Durrandon).(wiki:Usurper.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_durrandon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
- [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), who was denounced an usurper by his nephew Prince [Aegon the Uncrowned](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)).(wiki:Usurper.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
- [Jonos Arryn](wiki:Jonos_Arryn), who usurped his older brother Lord [Ronnel Arryn](wiki:Ronnel_Arryn_(king)).(wiki:Usurper.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aenys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
- During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the half-siblings [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) and [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) both denounced the other as the usurper.(wiki:Usurper.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
- [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen), called Daeron the Falseborn by the [Blackfyre](wiki:House_Blackfyre) supporters.(wiki:Usurper.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
- [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre), who [rebelled](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion) against his half-brother King Daeron II Targaryen. [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) judges him as a rebel and usurper.(wiki:Usurper.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btheon_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctheon_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), the daughter of the late [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), often thinks of the Usurper, [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), and how he betrayed [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen).(wiki:Usurper.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Her brother [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) believes there are houses still loyal to the Targaryens who will rise up against the Usurper when he and Dany cross the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea).(wiki:Usurper.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Robert admits to his friend, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), that he is wary of Daenerys marrying Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) and Viserys allying with a [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)*, since some in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) still call him Usurper.(wiki:Usurper.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Clash of Kings

In [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), [Quhuru Mo](wiki:Quhuru_Mo) informs Daenerys that Robert died after hunting in the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood), and she is happy to learn that [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) and [Starks](wiki:House_Stark) are fighting amongst themselves.(wiki:Usurper.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Dany desires to use Robert's skull as a drinking vessel.(wiki:Usurper.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

During the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) considers rivals of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) to be usurpers.(wiki:Usurper.cite_ref-Racok0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Stannis calls his younger brother, [Renly](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), a usurper at the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)),(wiki:Usurper.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) and Melisandre [assassinates](wiki:Shadow_assassin) Renly.(wiki:Usurper.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Following Melisandre's instructions, Stannis tosses leeches, which have fed on the blood of Robert's bastard [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm), into a fire, an attempt to kill the usurpers [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), and [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark).(wiki:Usurper.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

Daenerys has a dream of the [Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident), in which she burns the ice-armored host of the Usurper with [dragonfire](wiki:Dragonfire).(wiki:Usurper.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) She is wary of Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), since he served Robert after the [War of the Usurper](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Usurper.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Daenerys refers to Joffrey as the Young Usurper,(wiki:Usurper.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) and [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) tells the [Merman's Court](wiki:Merman's_Court) that [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) is a usurper of Stannis.(wiki:Usurper.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

[Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) considers [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) to have been a usurper of [Gerrick Kingsblood](wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood), a descendant of [Raymun Redbeard](wiki:Raymun_Redbeard), although Gerrick actually descends from Raymun's brother.(wiki:Usurper.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
