## Origins

During the [First Turtle War](wiki:First_Turtle_War), the Rhoynar emerged victorious over the [Valyrians](wiki:Valyria) when their water wizards called up the power of the river [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne) and proceeded to flood [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys). If the tales can be believed, half the city was washed away.(wiki:Water_magic.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
In the [Second Spice War](wiki:Second_Spice_War), the Rhoynish Prince [Garin](wiki:Garin_(prince)) raised an army a quarter of a million strong to fight the [Volantenes](wiki:Volantis) and Valyrians. So long as the army remained beside Mother Rhoyne, the prince declared, they need not fear the [dragons](wiki:Dragon) of Valyria; their own water wizards would protect them from their fires. In the battle at [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys), the water wizards raised enormous waterspouts against the Valyrians' three dragons, and the Rhoyne flooded the city.(wiki:Water_magic.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) The Valyrians returned with 300 dragons, destroyed the Rhoynar army, captured Garin, and destroyed his city, [Chroyane](wiki:Chroyane). The captive prince called down a [curse](wiki:Garin's_Curse), and that night the Rhoyne flooded out of season, with greater force than known in living memory. A fog fell upon the city, and the Valyrian conquerors began to die of [greyscale](wiki:Greyscale).(wiki:Water_magic.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) To this day, the ruins of Chroyane and that part of the river Rhoyne are known as the [Sorrows](wiki:Sorrows), where the [stone men](wiki:Stone_men) live and travelers can still be infected with greyscale.(wiki:Water_magic.cite_ref-Radwd18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Princess [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria) has been called a "witch queen",(wiki:Water_magic.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) though it is not true that she practiced water magic herself.(wiki:Water_magic.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_dorne:_the_coming_of_the_rhoynar.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) But when she brought her people to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), it is said that the Rhoynish water witches knew secret spells that made dry streams flow again and deserts bloom.(wiki:Water_magic.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
