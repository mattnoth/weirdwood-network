## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Early in the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) refers to the marriage of [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) as the mortar that holds together the "great southron alliance" between the [Reach](wiki:Reach) and the [Stormlands](wiki:Stormlands).(wiki:Southron.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin) tells [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) that [Brandon Stark](wiki:Brandon_Stark) was enamored with her, but [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark) arranged the betrothal of his eldest son to [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Tully), a match that served the lord's "southron ambitions".(wiki:Southron.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
