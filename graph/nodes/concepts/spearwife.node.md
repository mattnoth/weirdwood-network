### A Dance with Dragons

[Varamyr Sixskins](wiki:Varamyr_Sixskins), a [skinchanger](wiki:Skinchanger), attempts to take over a spearwife, [Thistle](wiki:Thistle).(wiki:Spearwife.cite_ref-Radwdprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
