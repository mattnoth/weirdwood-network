## Origins

Bloodmages were prominent in the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold).(wiki:Bloodmage.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_basilisk_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Bloodmage.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_sothoryos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) They were very powerful, and not all bloodmages came from [dragonlord](wiki:Dragonlord) families, although there was overlap between the two groups.(wiki:Bloodmage.cite_ref-5)
Septon [Barth](wiki:Barth), in his [Dragons, Wyrms, and Wyverns: Their Unnatural History](wiki:Dragons,_Wyrms,_and_Wyverns:_Their_Unnatural_History), speculated that the bloodmages of [Valyria](wiki:Valyria) employed [wyvern](wiki:Wyvern) stock to create [dragons](wiki:Dragons).(wiki:Bloodmage.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_sothoryos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) In the flesh pits of [Gogossos](wiki:Gogossos), bloodmages mated beast to [slave](wiki:Slave)-women to bring forth twisted half-human children.(wiki:Bloodmage.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_basilisk_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Even though magic is said to have died out in the west after the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria),(wiki:Bloodmage.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) it is believed that bloodmagic is still practiced in [Qohor](wiki:Qohor), one of the daughters of Valyria, to this day.(wiki:Bloodmage.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_qohor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[Mirri Maz Duur](wiki:Mirri_Maz_Duur), a [maegi](wiki:Maegi), was taught by a bloodmage from the [Shadow Lands](wiki:Shadow_Lands).(wiki:Bloodmage.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
