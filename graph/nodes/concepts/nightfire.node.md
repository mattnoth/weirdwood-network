## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) burns statues of the [Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) taken from the [sept at Dragonstone](wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone) in a nightfire. [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) draws [Lightbringer](wiki:Lightbringer) from the statue of [the Mother](wiki:Mother_(the_Seven)).(wiki:Nightfire.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) sees [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) praying at a huge nightfire in Dragonstone's yard.(wiki:Nightfire.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
After the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), Melisandre and the queen's men light nightfires at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Nightfire.cite_ref-Rasos76.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Nightfire.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Stannis intends for nightfires to burn at each castle of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) along the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Nightfire.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

In [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) thinks a light in the distance is a nightfire at the city's [red temple](wiki:Red_temple).(wiki:Nightfire.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sees a nightfire at the red temple in [Selhorys](wiki:Selhorys),(wiki:Nightfire.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) and then huge nightfires at the [Temple of the Lord of Light](wiki:Temple_of_the_Lord_of_Light) in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis).(wiki:Nightfire.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)
Melisandre sets nightfires each evening at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Nightfire.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Stannis and the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) with him light nightfires during their [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell).(wiki:Nightfire.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
[Moqorro](wiki:Moqorro) lights nightfires in a great iron brazier while sailing on [Selaesori Qhoran ](wiki:Selaesori_Qhoran).(wiki:Nightfire.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) After being rescued from the [Gulf of Grief](wiki:Gulf_of_Grief) by the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet), he sets a nightfire while with [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) on [Iron Victory](wiki:Iron_Victory).(wiki:Nightfire.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
