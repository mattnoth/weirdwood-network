## Origins

During the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes), Prince [Peremore the Twisted](wiki:Peremore_Hightower), the second son of King [Uthor of the High Tower](wiki:Uthor_of_the_High_Tower), invited numerous scholars, including wise men, teachers, priests, healers, singers, [wizards](wiki:Wizard), [sorcerers](wiki:Sorcerer), and alchemists to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). After Peremore's death, his brother, King [Urrigon Hightower](wiki:Urrigon_Hightower), granted land alongside the [Honeywine](wiki:Honeywine) to "Peremore's pets", who developed the tract into the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) of the [maesters](wiki:Maesters).(wiki:Alchemy.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen) dabbled in alchemy, [astronomy](wiki:Astronomy), and astrology.(wiki:Alchemy.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Queen [Tyanna of the Tower](wiki:Tyanna_of_the_Tower), wife of [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), was said to practice alchemy and [sorcery](wiki:Magic).(wiki:Alchemy.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Archmaester [Vaegon](wiki:Vaegon_Targaryen), born to [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen), was devoted to alchemy, astronomy, mathematics, and other arcane arts.(wiki:Alchemy.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon:_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) included the [Alchemists' Guild](wiki:Alchemists'_Guild) of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in his [wildfire plot](wiki:Wildfire_plot).(wiki:Alchemy.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The [Alchemists' Guild](wiki:Alchemists'_Guild) is commissioned to provide ten thousand jars of [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) for the defense of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Alchemy.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

In [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), the novice [Pate](wiki:Pate_(novice)) meets [an alchemist](wiki:Alchemist) who claims to be able to change [iron](wiki:Iron) into [gold](wiki:Gold).(wiki:Alchemy.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) considers alchemists and [pyromancers](wiki:Pyromancers) to be practitioners of feeble tricks.(wiki:Alchemy.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
