### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) recalls stories she has heard of shadowbinders working terrible sorceries at night.(wiki:Shadowbinder.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) enlists the aid of the [red priestess](wiki:Red_priest) and shadowbinder [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre),(wiki:Shadowbinder.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) who claims that shadows are the servants of [light](wiki:R'hllor).(wiki:Shadowbinder.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) She gives birth to [shadow assassins](wiki:Shadow_assassin) that kill [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)(wiki:Shadowbinder.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) and [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose).(wiki:Shadowbinder.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) meets [Quaithe](wiki:Quaithe) for the first time;(wiki:Shadowbinder.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) the shadowbinder proceeds to give the young queen cryptic information during her journeys.
