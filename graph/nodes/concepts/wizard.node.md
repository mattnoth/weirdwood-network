## Culture

Wizards are viewed skeptically by some [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
In the stories of [Old Nan](wiki:Old_Nan), wizards are associated with long white beards, and tall pointed hats speckled with stars.(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Images of wizards are on some of the [coins](wiki:Currency) draped around the neck of [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat).(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Wizards study in [Asshai](wiki:Asshai), where no knowledge is forbidden.(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_asshai-by-the-shadow.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Origins

[Greenseers](wiki:Greenseer) are sometimes called the wizards of the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest).(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Wizards were active in the [Golden Empire of Yi Ti](wiki:Golden_Empire_of_Yi_Ti).(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[Valyrian](wiki:Valyria) wizards are said to have shaped stone with fire and [magic](wiki:Magic).(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Rhoynish](wiki:Rhoynar) [water wizards](wiki:Water_magic) fought against the [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlords) during the [Rhoynish Wars](wiki:Rhoynish_Wars).(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Wizards, [alchemists](wiki:Alchemists), and [sorcerers](wiki:Sorcerer) were among the wise men and scholars gathered by [Peremore Hightower](wiki:Peremore_Hightower).(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
In tales of Ser [Clarence Crabb](wiki:Clarence_Crabb), the [knight](wiki:Knight) brought the heads of wizards to the [Whispers](wiki:Whispers).(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
When [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) was a child, he used to pretend to be a wizard.(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

In a [secret passage](wiki:Red_Keep) near the cellar of the [skulls of the Targaryen dragons](wiki:Skulls_of_the_Targaryen_dragons), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) overhears a conversation between two unknown men — actually [Varys](wiki:Varys) and [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) — in which Illyrio jokingly calls Varys a wizard. When she tries to tell her father, [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), about the conversation, which also mentioned his potential death, she mentions the "monsters" and how one man called the other a wizard, and Eddard dismisses the story as [mummery](wiki:Mummer). Arya later asks [Desmond](wiki:Desmond), one of her father's [household guards](wiki:House_Stark_guards), if he could protect her father from a wizard, and Desmond replies that wizards die the same as other men.(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Arya Stark wonders if [Jaqen H'ghar](wiki:Jaqen_H'ghar) could be a wizard, and if so, then [Rorge](wiki:Rorge) and [Biter](wiki:Biter) could be [demons](wiki:Demons) that he conjured up from some [hell](wiki:Seven_hells).(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
While in the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) has a [vision](wiki:Vision) of a splendor of sumptuously-dressed wizards in a hall. They wear robes of ermine, [ruby](wiki:Ruby) velvet, and cloth of [gold](wiki:Gold), or elaborate armor studded with gemstones, or tall pointed hats speckled with stars.(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[The cook](wiki:Husband) at the [Inn of the Kneeling Man](wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man) refers to [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr) as "the red wizard".(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
[Greenbeard](wiki:Greenbeard) also calls Thoros a wizard, and Arya thinks he does not look much like the ones in [Old Nan](wiki:Old_Nan)'s stories.(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
When [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) manages to get [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) elected as [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), [Pyp](wiki:Pyp) calls Sam a wizard.(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) refers to the [warlocks](wiki:Warlocks) in the company of [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) as wizards.(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Euron tells his brother, [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy), that he slew a [Myrish](wiki:Myr) wizard who was unable to hatch a [dragon egg](wiki:Dragon_egg) for him.(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Coldhands](wiki:Coldhands) says that the [three-eyed crow](wiki:Three-eyed_crow) could be called a wizard.(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
Ironborn in the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) consider [Moqorro](wiki:Moqorro) to be a wizard.(wiki:Wizard.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
