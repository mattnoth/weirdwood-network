## Identity

Come-into-my-castle is a children's game played in Westeros. It is attested as a game played by the children of Winterfell — Arya Stark played it with other Winterfell children in the courtyards and among the crypts. When Big Walder Frey and Little Walder Frey came to Winterfell as wards, they played come-into-my-castle alongside monsters-and-maidens, rats-and-cats, and lord-of-the-crossing with Rickon Stark and other children. The game is also played among noble ladies: Queen Margaery Tyrell and Lady Alysanne Bulwer played come-into-my-castle together, which Ser Osney Kettleblack cited as one of the distractions preventing him from seducing Margaery. The game appears to be a common pastime across multiple social classes in the Seven Kingdoms.
