## Origins

As a child, [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) played monsters-and-maidens, [hide-the-treasure](wiki:Hide-the-treasure), and [come-into-my-castle](wiki:Come-into-my-castle) with the children of the people of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Monsters-and-maidens.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) She also played come-into-my-castle and monsters-and-maidens amongst the stone kings in the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Monsters-and-maidens.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) plays monsters-and-maidens, [lord of the crossing](wiki:Lord_of_the_crossing), [rats and cats](wiki:Rats_and_cats), and [come-into-my-castle](wiki:Come-into-my-castle) with [Big Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Jammos)) and [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)).(wiki:Monsters-and-maidens.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords

[Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm) plays monsters-and-maidens with [Shireen Baratheon](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon) and [Patchface](wiki:Patchface) at Aegon's Garden in [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), with Edric pretending to be a monster.(wiki:Monsters-and-maidens.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

[Shae](wiki:Shae) and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) pretend to play the game during a tryst amongst the [dragon skulls](wiki:Dragon_skulls_(Targaryen)) within the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Monsters-and-maidens.cite_ref-Rasos58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
