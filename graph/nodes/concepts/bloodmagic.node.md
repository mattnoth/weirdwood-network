## Origins

### Myth[ ]

According to legend, [Azor Ahai](wiki:Azor_Ahai) succeeded in forging [Lightbringer](wiki:Lightbringer) by working on it for a hundred days and a hundred nights, and tempering the [steel](wiki:Steel) by plunging the blade into the heart of his beloved wife, [Nissa Nissa](wiki:Nissa_Nissa).(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### Valyria[ ]

All magic practiced by the [Valyrians](wiki:Valyrians) was rooted in blood and [fire magic](wiki:Fire_magic).(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Not all [bloodmages](wiki:Bloodmage) came from [dragonlord](wiki:Dragonlord) families, although there was overlap between the two groups.(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-10)
Septon [Barth](wiki:Barth), in his [Dragons, Wyrms, and Wyverns: Their Unnatural History](wiki:Dragons,_Wyrms,_and_Wyverns:_Their_Unnatural_History), speculated that the bloodmages of the [Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) employed [wyvern](wiki:Wyvern) stock to create [dragons](wiki:Dragons).(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_sothoryos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Meanwhile, in the flesh pits of [Gogossos](wiki:Gogossos), bloodmages mated beasts to [slave](wiki:Slavery) women to bring forth twisted half-human children.(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_basilisk_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

### Qohor[ ]

Bloodmagic, along with [necromancy](wiki:Necromancy) and divination, is rumored to be practiced in [Qohor](wiki:Qohor).(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_qohor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) According to Maester [Pol](wiki:Pol), the spells required to rework [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) involve blood sacrifice, including that of infant slaves.(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_qohor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

### Asshai[ ]

Bloodmagic is practiced freely in [Asshai](wiki:Asshai), where no practice is forbidden, however dark or depraved.(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_asshai-by-the-shadow.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### Westeros[ ]

According to [Old Nan](wiki:Old_Nan), [Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare), [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers), included human blood in the mortar of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal),(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) which is now said to be cursed.(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) [Danelle Lothston](wiki:Danelle_Lothston), the mad [Lady of Harrenhal](wiki:Lady_of_Harrenhal), is said to have bathed in blood,(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) held [feasts of flesh](wiki:Cannibalism),(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1) and practiced dark arts during the reign of King [Maekar I Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) According to tales of [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk), giant [bats](wiki:Bat) from Harrenhal would bring bad children to her.(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
Some [septons](wiki:Septon) believed that the letter from [Nymor Martell](wiki:Nymor_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), to King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War) was actually an ensorceled missive written by Nymor's mother, Princess [Meria Martell](wiki:Meria_Martell), before her death, and that Meria had used the blood of Queen [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) as ink.(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon:_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
[Shiera Seastar](wiki:Shiera_Seastar), the [bastard](wiki:Bastard) daughter of King [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen), was rumored to bathe in blood to retain her beauty.(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
[Maggy the Frog](wiki:Maggy_the_Frog) was a [witch woman](wiki:Woods_witch) in [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) and reputed sorceress(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) or priestess from [Essos](wiki:Essos), who sold fortunes, [cures](wiki:Medicine), and [love potions](wiki:Love_potion) to half the city.(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) In [276 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Maggy tasted the blood of young [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) and [Melara Hetherspoon](wiki:Melara_Hetherspoon), and [foretold their fates](wiki:Prophecy).(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) tells [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) that [warlocks](wiki:Warlocks) from [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) bathed him in [aurochs](wiki:Aurochs) blood in an attempt to make him brave.(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)
[Mirri Maz Duur](wiki:Mirri_Maz_Duur) tells [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) that she knows spells capable of saving a man from death, but warns her that only death may pay for life, and death may be cleaner. To save the life of the dying Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), Daenerys's husband, Mirri sacrifices [Drogo's stallion](wiki:Drogo's_stallion)(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and, unbeknownst to Daenerys, her unborn son [Rhaego](wiki:Rhaego). However, the spell only restores Drogo's body, leaving him in a catatonic state.(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Ragot68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
Daenerys has a funeral pyre built for Drogo, and orders a horse killed. Mirri believes Daenerys is trying to resurrect Drogo through bloodmagic, and insists that without the spells the blood is nothing. She even offers assistance in exchange for release. Daenerys has the horse, Drogo's body, her three [dragon eggs](wiki:Dragon_eggs), and Mirri Maz Duur placed on the pyre. After the pyre is lit and Mirri dies in flames, Daenerys walks into the inferno. After the fire dies down to ashes, not only is Daenerys found alive, with only her clothes and hair burned away, but also the eggs have hatched, and the first three [dragons](wiki:Dragon) in more than a hundred years have been born.(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) According to [George R.R. Martin](wiki:George_R.R._Martin), Daenerys's use of magic was instinctive, made up as she went along.(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-23)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

While King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) is fighting the [battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), his wife Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) has the [red priestess](wiki:Red_priestess) [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) burn alive Lord [Guncer Sunglass](wiki:Guncer_Sunglass) and the sons of [Hubard Rambton](wiki:Hubard_Rambton), as a sacrifice to [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor).(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)
Melisandre pleads to Stannis to give her [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm), [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son of the late King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), as there is power in king's blood, and she believes Edric's sacrifice will "wake the stone dragon." Stannis refuses, but Melisandre is able to leech some of Edric's blood. She has Stannis cast the leeches into the fire of a brazier, while stating the names of the "three false kings", [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), and [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) In time, Balon falls from a bridge at [Pyke](wiki:Pyke),(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) Robb is killed at the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding),(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) and Joffrey is poisoned at his own [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding).(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) recalls that during her youth, she, [Jeyne Farman](wiki:Jeyne_Farman), and [Melara Hetherspoon](wiki:Melara_Hetherspoon) visited [Maggy the Frog](wiki:Maggy_the_Frog) outside a [tournament](wiki:Tournament_in_honor_of_Viserys's_birth) in [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport). Although Jeyne fled in terror, Maggy tasted the blood of Cersei and Melara, and told the girls their futures.(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
[Yna](wiki:Yna), a one-eyed [prostitute](wiki:Prostitute), is reputed to be able to tell your fortune by tasting a drop of your blood. She has tasted the blood of the [Sailor's Wife](wiki:Sailor's_Wife), and knows that her first husband is dead.(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When Stannis's army sails for the [Wall](wiki:Wall), Melisandre sacrifices Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent) to [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor) to gain favorable winds for the voyage north.(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)
During Stannis's [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell), his army is trapped by a blizzard in a [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village) a few days from Winterfell. In an attempt to end the storm, [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) sacrifice four [Peasebury](wiki:House_Peasebury) men accused of [cannibalism](wiki:Cannibalism).(wiki:Bloodmagic.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)
