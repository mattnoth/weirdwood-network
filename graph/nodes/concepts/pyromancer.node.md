## Culture

## Origins

Mages are said to have renewed and maintained the rituals that tempered the [Fourteen Flames](wiki:Fourteen_Flames) of the [Valyrian peninsula](wiki:Valyrian_peninsula), with the assassinations of too many of them being a possible cause for the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria).(wiki:Pyromancer.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_doom_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
During the [Great Spring Sickness](wiki:Great_Spring_Sickness), pyromancers burned the dead of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in the ruins of the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit).(wiki:Pyromancer.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Pyromancers are mentioned in the fragments of Archmaester [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn)'s writings which survived the [tragedy at Summerhall](wiki:Tragedy_at_Summerhall).(wiki:Pyromancer.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Pyromancers were often at the court of the fire-obsessed King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Pyromancer.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Lord [Rossart](wiki:Rossart), [Garigus](wiki:Garigus), and [Belis](wiki:Belis) were master pyromancers of the [Alchemists' Guild](wiki:Alchemists'_Guild) who conspired with the Mad King for his [wildfire plot](wiki:Wildfire_plot).(wiki:Pyromancer.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), meets with Wisdom [Hallyne the Pyromancer](wiki:Hallyne) in the [Guildhall of the Alchemists](wiki:Guildhall_of_the_Alchemists), with his [alchemists](wiki:Alchemy) producing [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) for the defense of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Pyromancer.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Hallyne is raised to [lord](wiki:Lord) after the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Pyromancer.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
During their stay in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) and [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) see the performance of a firemage who conjures a ladder of swirling flame in the air, rising unsupported from the floor of a bazaar. The mage urges the flames higher with sweeps of his arms until it rises forty feet high and then climbs it, with each touched rung dissolving after him. After he reaches the top, the ladder disappears along with the mage. Meanwhile, [cutpurses](wiki:Cutpurse) steal from the watching crowd. Jhogo considers the mage's actions a trick, but [Quaithe](wiki:Quaithe) states it is not. The [shadowbinder](wiki:Shadowbinder) explains that while half a year before the mage had meager skills manipulating fire, his powers have grown thanks to Daenerys hatching her [dragons](wiki:Dragons).(wiki:Pyromancer.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

During the [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), four master pyromancers entertain by conjuring beasts of living flame which tear at each other with fiery claws.(wiki:Pyromancer.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre), a [red priestess](wiki:Red_priest) of [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor), has little regard for alchemists and pyromancers.(wiki:Pyromancer.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
