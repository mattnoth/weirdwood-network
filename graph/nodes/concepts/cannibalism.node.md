## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Upon returning to Lady [Danelle Lothston](wiki:Danelle_Lothston)'s former home of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) recalls hearing stories about her bathing in blood and presiding over feasts of human flesh.(wiki:Cannibalism.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Once in Harrenhal [Shitmouth](wiki:Shitmouth) and [Raff the Sweetling](wiki:Rafford) inform Jaime that Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane), in a mockery of [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat)'s preferred way of torturing his prisoners, cut off Vargo's limbs piece by piece over an extended period of time while he had the wounds bandaged so that Vargo stayed alive. Vargo first lost his hands and feet, then his arms and legs. The extremities as well as other parts of Vargo's body were then fed as "roast goat" to [northern](wiki:North) prisoners that Gregor brought to Harrenhal, including Ser [Wylis Manderly](wiki:Wylis_Manderly), as well as to Vargo himself.(wiki:Cannibalism.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

[Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) tells his brother [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) that he captured four [warlocks](wiki:Warlocks) in a galleas out of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth). One threatened the corsair so Euron killed the warlock and fed him to the other three.(wiki:Cannibalism.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Thousands of survivors from the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor) flee to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). Some starving refugees resort to cannibalism of the diseased.(wiki:Cannibalism.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

[Coldhands](wiki:Coldhands) slays [deserters from the Night's Watch](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep) in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest). The ranger provides meat to [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark), [Hodor](wiki:Hodor), and [Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed) and [Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed), claiming to have found a sow in the wilderness.(wiki:Cannibalism.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

[Rhaegar Frey](wiki:Rhaegar_Frey), [Jared Frey](wiki:Jared_Frey), and [Symond Frey](wiki:Symond_Frey) go missing after departing [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor).(wiki:Cannibalism.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) During the wedding of "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)" to [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) presents three huge pork pies at the wedding feast while requesting a song from the singer [Abel](wiki:Mance_Rayder) about the [Rat Cook](wiki:Rat_Cook), implying the [three Freys were actually present at the wedding after all](wiki:Frey_Pies/Theories). Wyman serves the first two portions to [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) and [Walda Frey](wiki:Fat_Walda_Frey), the next to [Hosteen](wiki:Hosteen_Frey) and [Aenys Frey](wiki:Aenys_Frey) and then eats two slices from each himself.(wiki:Cannibalism.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

During King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s harsh winter [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell), four [House Peasebury](wiki:House_Peasebury) soldiers are staked and burned by [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) at a [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village) for cannibalism. It is rumored that Lord [Robin Peasebury](wiki:Robin_Peasebury) may also have eaten human flesh.(wiki:Cannibalism.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
