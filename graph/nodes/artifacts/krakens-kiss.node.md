### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The Kraken's Kiss is part of the Iron Fleet contingent dispatched to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay). Kraken's Kiss, [Iron Wing](wiki:Iron_Wing), and [Sparrowhawk](wiki:Sparrowhawk) are the three fastest ships in [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy)'s fleet and are tasked with running down and capturing two unladen [Ghiscari](wiki:Ghis) galleys. The captured ships are subsequently renamed [Ghost](wiki:Ghost_(ship)) and [Shade](wiki:Shade).(wiki:Kraken's_Kiss.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
