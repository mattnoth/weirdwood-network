## Origins

Legend says the Seastone Chair was found on the shores of [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk) by [First Men](wiki:First_Men) when they came to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands).(wiki:Seastone_Chair.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Archmaester [Haereg](wiki:Haereg) pondered that the chair was created by a people who inhabited the isles before the First Men, though he eventually dismissed the idea.(wiki:Seastone_Chair.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Maester [Kirth](wiki:Kirth) speculated in *[Songs the Drowned Men Sing](wiki:Songs_the_Drowned_Men_Sing)* that the chair was left by a people from across the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea).(wiki:Seastone_Chair.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_dawn_age.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Noting similarities between the Seastone Chair and the fused [black stone](wiki:Black_stone) at the base of the [Hightower](wiki:Hightower), Maester [Theron](wiki:Theron) suggested in *[Strange Stone](wiki:Strange_Stone)* that they were the work of so-called [Deep Ones](wiki:Deep_Ones).(wiki:Seastone_Chair.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

[House Greyjoy](wiki:House_Greyjoy) of [Pyke](wiki:Pyke) has ruled the Iron Islands since [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Seastone_Chair.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) It is unknown if extinct [iron king](wiki:Iron_king) dynasties, such as the [Greyirons](wiki:House_Greyiron) or [Hoares](wiki:House_Hoare), used the Seastone Chair as their throne.

After Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy) was killed by [Tess](wiki:Tess) at [Faircastle](wiki:Faircastle) without an heir, the Seastone Chair passed by law to his oldest [salt son](wiki:Salt_son), [Toron Greyjoy](wiki:Toron_Greyjoy).(wiki:Seastone_Chair.cite_ref-Rtwoiafunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

The Seastone Chair is now located on a dais in the Great Hall of the [Great Keep of Pyke](wiki:Great_Keep_of_Pyke), the seat of Lord [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy). His younger brother [Aeron Damphair](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy), a [priest of the Drowned God](wiki:Priest_of_the_Drowned_God), claims that only a pious ironman can sit the throne, not an ungodly man or a woman.(wiki:Seastone_Chair.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Appearances & Description

See also: Images of the Seastone Chair

The Seastone Chair is made of an immense block of oily [black stone](wiki:Black_stone) carved into the shape of a great [kraken](wiki:Kraken).(wiki:Seastone_Chair.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Seastone_Chair.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Lord [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) declares himself [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Seastone_Chair.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords

The day after Balon falls to his death while crossing [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), his brother [Euron](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) returns to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) and claims the Seastone Chair. When Lord [Sawane Botley](wiki:Sawane_Botley) objects, Euron Crow's Eye has him drowned.(wiki:Seastone_Chair.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Opposed to his hated brother Euron succeeding Balon, [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) insists that a [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot) be held to determine the new king,(wiki:Seastone_Chair.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) but the [gathering on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk) confirms Euron as king.(wiki:Seastone_Chair.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Euron offers to give the Seastone Chair to his brother [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy), [Lord Captain of the Iron Fleet](wiki:Lord_Captain_of_the_Iron_Fleet), once Euron claims the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Seastone_Chair.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
