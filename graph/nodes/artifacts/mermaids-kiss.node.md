## Origins

After the passing of Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), his body was sailed from the capital to [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark) aboard the Mermaid's Kiss by [Marilda of Hull](wiki:Marilda_of_Hull) and her surviving son, [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon), Corlys's designated heir. Corlys's body was then buried at sea aboard the [Sea Snake](wiki:Sea_Snake) near [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)).(wiki:Mermaid's_Kiss.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

On the first day of their stay in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell), Ser [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater), and Ser [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood) seek passage on the Mermaid's Kiss to sail to Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). The captain refuses them, however.(wiki:Mermaid's_Kiss.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) It is unknown if this is the same ship once sailed by [Marilda of Hull](wiki:Marilda_of_Hull).
