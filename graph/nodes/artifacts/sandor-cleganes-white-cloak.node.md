## Identity

The white Kingsguard cloak Sandor Clegane abandoned when he deserted during the Battle of the Blackwater. Left bloodstained and fire-scorched in Sansa Stark's chamber, it becomes a marker of his defection from the crown's service — and of the fraught final encounter between Sandor and Sansa. Sansa wraps herself in it through the night.
