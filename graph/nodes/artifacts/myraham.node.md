## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The Myraham is hired by [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) to take him from [Seagard](wiki:Seagard) to [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport) to treat with his father, [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands). Theon takes [the captain's daughter](wiki:Daughter_of_the_captain_of_the_Myraham) to his bed.(wiki:Myraham.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

The Myraham is held for half a year at Lordsport by Balon, who now styles himself [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North). In the chaos resulting from the death of Balon at [Pyke](wiki:Pyke) and the return of [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), the captain slips anchor and returns to Seagard. The captain is brought by Lord [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister) to tell his story to [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), at [Hag's Mire](wiki:Hag's_Mire).(wiki:Myraham.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
