## Origins

While returning from [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), the Nessaria was battered by a storm and driven off course to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). Its sailors witnessed [Sunfyre](wiki:Sunfyre) fighting [Grey Ghost](wiki:Grey_Ghost) near the [Dragonmont](wiki:Dragonmont), and the tale soon spread throughout the island. Remembering the golden scales of Sunfyre, [Tom Tangletongue](wiki:Tom_Tangletongue) led a group in search of [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen)'s dragon.(wiki:Nessaria.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
