## Origins

Three or four years ago, during the reign of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), Asha traded at various ports, including [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle), [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport), and the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor). She slept with [Qarl the Maid](wiki:Qarl_the_Maid) during the voyage.(wiki:Black_Wind.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Asha was raiding [Lyseni](wiki:Lys) pirates at the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) when her uncle [Euron](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) was exiled by her father [Balon](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Black_Wind.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Asha takes the *Black Wind* to [Great Wyk](wiki:Great_Wyk) with messages from her father, Lord [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy).(wiki:Black_Wind.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) When the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) enter the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Asha leads thirty longships around [Sea Dragon Point](wiki:Sea_Dragon_Point) to the [Bay of Ice](wiki:Bay_of_Ice),(wiki:Black_Wind.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) where she besieges and captures [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fall_of_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Black_Wind.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Feast for Crows

After Balon's death at [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), Asha returns to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) from the [north](wiki:North) and visits her uncle, Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw), at [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers). During the journey, the longship stops at the [Stony Shore](wiki:Stony_Shore) in search of a wet nurse for young [Erena Glover](wiki:Erena_Glover). A goat is used for milk instead.(wiki:Black_Wind.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Arriving at [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk) for the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot), Asha beaches the *Black Wind* near the castle of Lord [Norne Goodbrother](wiki:Norne_Goodbrother).(wiki:Black_Wind.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) During the kingsmoot, her crew present the meager resources captured from the north, pebbles, pinecones, and turnips.(wiki:Black_Wind.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

After [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) is chosen [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North) at the kingsmoot, Asha flees Old Wyk, sailing first for Ten Towers on [Harlaw](wiki:Harlaw) and then returning to Deepwood in the north. She has only four longships left, including *Black Wind* and [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley)'s ship.(wiki:Black_Wind.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

When Deepwood comes under attack by [northern mountain clans](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans), Asha abandons the castle and leads her men through the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) toward the strand. They are defeated by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) in the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte), however.(wiki:Black_Wind.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Meanwhile, Asha's four longships are burned or taken by [Alysane Mormont](wiki:Alysane_Mormont) as they lay off the strand.(wiki:Black_Wind.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
