### A Feast for Crows[ ]

The Lady Ushanora was hired by [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) to take himself, [Gilly](wiki:Gilly), [Dareon](wiki:Dareon), and Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). However, Aemon fell sick and the captain refused to wait longer than three days. He also refused passage to Aemon, whom the captain believed would die on his ship. The Lady Ushanora sailed without the party.(wiki:Lady_Ushanora.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
