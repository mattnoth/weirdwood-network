## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

In the Velvet Hills, Illyrio and Tyrion meet Haldon Halfmaester and Ser Rolly Duckfield, who take on Tyrion, supplies from Illyrio, and the six oaken chests. Haldon believes Illyrio's baggage [mules](wiki:Mule) would be too slow, so has Duck shift them to three [pack horses](wiki:Horse). Before they depart, Illyrio tells them that there is a gift for [Young Griff](wiki:Young_Griff) in one of the chests, some candied ginger.(wiki:Illyrio's_chests.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
When they halt for supper, Tyrion questions Duck and Haldon as to the contents of the chests. He tells them that he thought they contained [gold](wiki:Gold) for the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company), but after seeing Duck hoist a chest onto one shoulder, he realized that if it were full of [coin](wiki:Coin), he could never have lifted it so easily. Duck and Haldon tell him that the chests contain armor and fine clothing, as well as gifts for Queen Daenaerys.(wiki:Illyrio's_chests.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)
The three reach the [Little Rhoyne](wiki:Little_Rhoyne) at [Ghoyan Drohe](wiki:Ghoyan_Drohe), where a [poleboat](wiki:Poleboat), the [Shy Maid](wiki:Shy_Maid), is waiting for them. The chests are stored on the poleboat.(wiki:Illyrio's_chests.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8) Later on the Shy Maid, [Septa](wiki:Septa) [Lemore](wiki:Lemore) takes child's clothing from one of the chests, and uses them to make an outfit of motley for Tyrion.(wiki:Illyrio's_chests.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
At [Selhorys](wiki:Selhorys), [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) sends Haldon and Tyrion ashore to learn news and rumors concerning Daenerys Targaryen. Griff gives them each a purse of silver from Illyrio's chests, to loosen tongues.(wiki:Illyrio's_chests.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
After meeting with the Golden Company in [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys), Jon Connington tells Haldon to ride back to the Shy Maid and return with Illyrio's chests.(wiki:Illyrio's_chests.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Not long afterwards, the Golden Company [land in Westeros](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company) in multiple parties.(wiki:Illyrio's_chests.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Illyrio's chests may be with Jon Connington's party, or one of the others.
