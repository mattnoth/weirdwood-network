## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The Seaswift is part of the fleet [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sends to bring Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos). As the fastest ship, it is to carry Myrcella. If they spot any lone ship they are to run it off or destroy it. Should an enemy fleet give battle the [Bold Wind](wiki:Bold_Wind) will escort the Seaswift to safety while the rest of the fleet, consisting of the [King Robert's Hammer](wiki:King_Robert's_Hammer), [Lionstar](wiki:Lionstar) and [Lady Lyanna](wiki:Lady_Lyanna), gives battle.(wiki:Seaswift.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
