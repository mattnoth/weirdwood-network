## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

While anchored in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), Ser [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater) and Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) attempt to hire the ship to take them to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). The [captain](wiki:Captain_of_the_Adventure) agrees but only at three times the usual fee. Gerris and Quentyn realize that the captain will only take them far enough out to sea to kill them and make them disappear while keeping their gold.(wiki:Adventure.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) instead join the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown).(wiki:Adventure.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
