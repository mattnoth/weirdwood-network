## Appearances & Description

The blade has red and black ripples through the steel. Its scabbard is garishly decorated with gold, cherrywood, and red leather, with [golden lions'](wiki:House_Lannister) heads. The eyes of the [lions](wiki:Lion) are [rubies](wiki:Rubies).(wiki:Widow's_Wail.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) has the [armorer](wiki:Armorer) [Tobho Mott](wiki:Tobho_Mott) melt down [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) [greatsword](wiki:Greatsword) [Ice](wiki:Ice), to make a sword for [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister). Mott has enough metal to make two swords, [one longsword](wiki:Oathkeeper) and a slightly smaller sword. He attempts to tint the swords crimson for the color of House Lannister, but the metal absorbs the color, resulting in ripples of nearly-black grey and red almost as dark as the grey.(wiki:Widow's_Wail.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Tywin gives one of the swords to Joffrey before his wedding, replacing [Hearteater](wiki:Hearteater).(wiki:Widow's_Wail.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Accepting the name Widow's Wail when it is shouted by a guest, Joffrey uses the blade to destroy [Lives of Four Kings](wiki:Lives_of_Four_Kings), a gift from [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Widow's_Wail.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
Upon [Joffrey's death](wiki:Purple_Wedding), Widow's Wail passes to his younger brother, [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon). The sword is held at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) until the boy king is old enough to wield it.(wiki:Widow's_Wail.cite_ref-3)
