## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The Banded Lizard is one of the thirteen galleys [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos) sails with from [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). He tries to persuade [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) to leave [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay) and go to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) by offering the ships to her; Daenerys thinks the galleys were provided by the [Thirteen](wiki:Thirteen). [Groleo](wiki:Groleo) determines the ship has some cracked oars but it would serve. Daenerys declines Xaro's offer.(wiki:Banded_Lizard.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
