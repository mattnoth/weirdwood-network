## Origins

[Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower), [Lord of the Hightower](wiki:Lord_of_the_Hightower), wielded the blade during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), in which he fought for King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen). After the [Battle on the Honeywine](wiki:Battle_on_the_Honeywine), he used it to knight Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)), his squire and the king's brother, dubbing him "Ser Daeron the Daring" for turning the battle in their favor with Daeron's [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Tessarion](wiki:Tessarion).(wiki:Vigilance.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Lord Ormund was killed by [Roderick Dustin](wiki:Roderick_Dustin), [Lord of Barrowton](wiki:Lord_of_Barrowton), during the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton).(wiki:Vigilance.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) It is unknown what happened to Vigilance afterwards.
