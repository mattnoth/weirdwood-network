## Appearances & Description

The pommel of Hearteater is a [ruby](wiki:Ruby) cut in the shape of a heart, set between a [lion](wiki:Lion)'s jaws. Three fullers are incised deeply in the blade.(wiki:Hearteater.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
