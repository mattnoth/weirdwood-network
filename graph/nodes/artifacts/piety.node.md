### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Piety is seized by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) when pious Lord [Guncer Sunglass](wiki:Guncer_Sunglass) refuses to acknowledge Stannis as his liege, since Stannis destroyed the [sept](wiki:Sept) at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). It is part of the fleet commanded by Ser [Imry Florent](wiki:Imry_Florent) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Piety.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) During the battle Piety is engulfed and destroyed by [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) after the explosion of the [Swordfish](wiki:Swordfish_(ship)).(wiki:Piety.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
