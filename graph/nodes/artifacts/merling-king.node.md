## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

The Merling King comes to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) after the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). There it is hired by Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) to take him to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) to wed the widowed Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn).(wiki:Merling_King.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Petyr and [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) flee on the galley after the death of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) at [his royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding).(wiki:Merling_King.cite_ref-Rasos61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Their voyage north is dangerous, as two men are swept overboard and a third breaks his neck.(wiki:Merling_King.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) After putting Petyr and [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) ashore at the [Fingers](wiki:Fingers), the Merling King sets sail for [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Merling_King.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
