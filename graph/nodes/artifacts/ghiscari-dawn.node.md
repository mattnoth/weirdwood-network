## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ghiscari Dawn trades at [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) and [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai). While returning to [New Ghis](wiki:New_Ghis), it encounters the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) in the strait between the [Isle of Cedars](wiki:Isle_of_Cedars) and [Astapor](wiki:Astapor)'s hinterland. Ghiscari Dawn is captured by [Lamentation](wiki:Lamentation_(ship)) near [Ghozai](wiki:Ghozai) and [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) has its captain drowned as a sacrifice to the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God). The ship is renamed Red God's Wroth by Victarion in honor of [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor), the god worshiped by the [red priest](wiki:Red_priest) [Moqorro](wiki:Moqorro).(wiki:Ghiscari_Dawn.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
