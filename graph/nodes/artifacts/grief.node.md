## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Grief is among the ships that make the voyage to the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk). During the kingsmoot, Lord Captain [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) orders Grief and [Iron Vengeance](wiki:Iron_Vengeance) to lie between [Silence](wiki:Silence) and the open sea.(wiki:Grief.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), Lord Victarion keeps Grief, [Lord Dagon](wiki:Lord_Dagon), [Iron Wind](wiki:Iron_Wind), and [Maiden's Bane](wiki:Maiden's_Bane) as part of his rearguard, commanding from his own ship, [Iron Victory](wiki:Iron_Victory).(wiki:Grief.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Grief is the fifty-fourth and last ironborn ship to arrive at the [Isle of Cedars](wiki:Isle_of_Cedars) while the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) regroups on their way to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay). The crew has rescued [Moqorro](wiki:Moqorro) from the water, and [the Vole](wiki:Rodrik_Sparr) presents the [red priest](wiki:Red_priest) to Victarion on Iron Victory.(wiki:Grief.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
