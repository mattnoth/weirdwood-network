## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin) receives a new lens from [Myr](wiki:Myr) for his observatory at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Myrish_eye.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) After [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls the banners, [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) watches the [Karstarks](wiki:House_Karstark) arrive through Luwin's bronze far-eye.(wiki:Myrish_eye.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Luwin uses it to study the [red comet](wiki:Red_comet), and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) uses the device to look at the [gargoyles](wiki:Gargoyle) on the [First Keep](wiki:First_Keep).(wiki:Myrish_eye.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) observes the host of Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) outside Winterfell through Luwin's Myrish lens tube.(wiki:Myrish_eye.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

During the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) uses Maester [Aemon's](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) lens tube on a heavy tripod from atop the [Wall](wiki:Wall) to observe the camp of [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder).(wiki:Myrish_eye.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) captures the Myrish cog [Dove](wiki:Dove), he takes its captain's Myrish eye.(wiki:Myrish_eye.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
