## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After trading in [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale), [Pentos](wiki:Pentos), [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh), and [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), the Seastrider stops in [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool), where it is encountered by [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth). She buys an orange for [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne), but rejects the captain's offer of heading north to [Sisterton](wiki:Sisterton) and [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor).(wiki:Seastrider.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
