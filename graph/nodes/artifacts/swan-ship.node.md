## Origins

Swan ships are believed to have been developed by [Xanda Qo](wiki:Xanda_Qo), and most swan ships are built on the isle of [Koj](wiki:Koj). Each ship is protected by archers wielding [goldenheart](wiki:Goldenheart) bows, so they are rarely attacked by [slavers](wiki:Slavers).(wiki:Swan_ship.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_summer_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) sees a swan ship when the [Storm Dancer](wiki:Storm_Dancer) arrives at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Swan_ship.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

There are swan ships in the [Ragman's Harbor](wiki:Ragman's_Harbor) when [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) arrives at [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) aboard the [Titan's Daughter](wiki:Titan's_Daughter).(wiki:Swan_ship.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) sails aboard the [Cinnamon Wind](wiki:Cinnamon_Wind), a swan ship captained by [Quhuru Mo](wiki:Quhuru_Mo), when he travels from Braavos to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown).(wiki:Swan_ship.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Archmaester [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn) intends to take the same ship to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) to meet [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen).(wiki:Swan_ship.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

[Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) notices swan ships at [Volantis](wiki:Volantis).(wiki:Swan_ship.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
