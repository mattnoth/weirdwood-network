## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

While crossing [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay), the Bountiful Harvest is seized by [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan), ostensibly for evading the new duties and customs he had imposed after being named [Lord of Blackwater Bay](wiki:Lord_of_Blackwater_Bay) by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent).(wiki:Bountiful_Harvest.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
In [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone)'s harbor, Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) finds his friend Salladhor in the seized ship's hold counting cargo with two eunuchs. The Harvest was supposed to be carrying forty-three jars of pepper but was carrying only thirty-nine. Davos is taken to the owner's cabin where he and Salladhor discuss recent events. When Davos talks of seeking vengeance, Salladhor becomes angry and Davos leaves.(wiki:Bountiful_Harvest.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The Bountiful Harvest is part of the fleet that carries Stannis's army north to the [Wall](wiki:Wall). The fleet encounters violent storms on its way back south toward [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) and Bountiful Harvest is one of many ships that are lost. A gale in the [Shivering Sea](wiki:Shivering_Sea) rips away Harvest's rigging, forcing Salladhor to have her towed. Ten leagues north of [Widow's Watch](wiki:Widow's_Watch), heavy seas slam Harvest into one of the galleys towing her, sinking both ships.(wiki:Bountiful_Harvest.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
