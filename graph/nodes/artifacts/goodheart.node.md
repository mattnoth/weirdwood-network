## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The Goodheart and the [Elephant](wiki:Elephant_(ship)) are driven north by a storm. They drop anchor off [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome) to make repairs and see the thousands of [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) that had been led there by [Mother Mole](wiki:Mother_Mole) after the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall). The desperate wildlings want to be taken away. The two ships do not have room for them all, so they agree to take the women and children. The wildlings have nothing to eat, so the men send out their wives and daughters, hundreds of them, but as soon as they are out to sea the pirates put them below and rope them up, meaning to sell them all as [slaves](wiki:Slave) in [Lys](wiki:Lys).
The pirates' plans are foiled when another storm parts the two ships and badly damages the Goodheart. Her captain has no choice but to put in at [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) where the [Sealord](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos) seizes the ship as slaving is forbidden in Braavos.
