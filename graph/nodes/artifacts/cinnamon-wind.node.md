## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

The *Cinnamon Wind* is at the [port of Qarth](wiki:Port_of_Qarth), having traveled there from [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). [Quhuru Mo](wiki:Quhuru_Mo) is brought to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), sees her [dragons](wiki:Dragons), and gives her news of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), including the death of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). Daenerys asks if the ship is returning to Westeros soon, but the captain says it will not be for a year or more, as the *Cinnamon Wind* will be sailing east, to make the trader's circle around the [Jade Sea](wiki:Jade_Sea).(wiki:Cinnamon_Wind.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Feast for Crows

The *Cinnamon Wind* is in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), and the ship's mate [Xhondo Dhoru](wiki:Xhondo_Dhoru) encounters [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly).(wiki:Cinnamon_Wind.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Sam, Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)), and [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) take passage to Oldtown, with Sam paying for the transport by working as a crewmember since the ship is shorthanded. Aemon dies while aboard, and his funeral is held on the *Cinnamon Wind* while it is becalmed in the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea) off the shore of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). Although [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen)'s funeral custom is cremation, Quhuru Mo will not allow a pyre aboard the ship, so Aemon's body is placed in a cask of rum to preserve it until they reach Oldtown.(wiki:Cinnamon_Wind.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

The ship is attacked by [pirates](wiki:Pirates) off the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones), but [Kojja Mo](wiki:Kojja_Mo) and her red archers slay them and drive them off.(wiki:Cinnamon_Wind.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) As the ship approaches Oldtown, they are menaced by three [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) [longships](wiki:Longship), but the *Cinnamon Wind* outdistances the first two, and the third is driven away by the ship's archers.(wiki:Cinnamon_Wind.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) In the [Whispering Sound](wiki:Whispering_Sound), the ship is inspected by the captain of the *[Huntress](wiki:Huntress)*, and then by Ser [Gunthor Hightower](wiki:Gunthor_Hightower) as it approaches the dock of Oldtown.(wiki:Cinnamon_Wind.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

The *Cinnamon Wind* arrives at Oldtown. After hearing all the information Samwell Tarly has for him, [Archmaester](wiki:Archmaester) [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn) leaves the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel), intending to take the ship to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) to serve Daenerys.(wiki:Cinnamon_Wind.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
