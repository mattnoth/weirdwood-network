## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Allard sails Lady Marya to the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) to distribute [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s claim against the legitimacy of [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s children with King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Lady_Marya.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Lady Marya is among Stannis's fleet that sails into [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). It rams and sinks [Lady's Shame](wiki:Lady's_Shame) with the aid of [Black Betha](wiki:Black_Betha), which is commanded by Allard's father, [Davos](wiki:Davos_Seaworth).(wiki:Lady_Marya.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Lady Marya does not survive the battle.(wiki:Lady_Marya.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
