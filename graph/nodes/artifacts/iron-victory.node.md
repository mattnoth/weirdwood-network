## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

During the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) leads the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) to the [Fever River](wiki:Fever_River) before the [fall of Moat Cailin](wiki:Fall_of_Moat_Cailin).(wiki:Iron_Victory.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Victarion sails back to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) after learning of the death of his oldest brother, [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North).(wiki:Iron_Victory.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

After Victarion loses to his elder brother [Euron](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) at the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot) on [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk),(wiki:Iron_Victory.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) the *Iron Victory* takes part in the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn)'s [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields). It rakes and boards at least one ship, a smaller one belonging to [House Serry](wiki:House_Serry), capturing it.(wiki:Iron_Victory.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

*Iron Victory* leads part of the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay) to retrieve [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) and her [dragons](wiki:Dragons). The *Iron Victory* is boarded by [monkeys](wiki:Monkey) from the [Isle of Cedars](wiki:Isle_of_Cedars), while the [dusky woman](wiki:Dusky_woman) stays in Victarion's cabin.(wiki:Iron_Victory.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The ship captures a merchant vessel, *[Noble Lady](wiki:Noble_Lady)*, off the coast of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne),(wiki:Iron_Victory.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) and joins *[Grief](wiki:Grief)* and *[Warrior Wench](wiki:Warrior_Wench)* in capturing the *[Willing Maiden](wiki:Willing_Maiden)* north of [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai).(wiki:Iron_Victory.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Victarion spares the life of the shipwrecked [Moqorro](wiki:Moqorro), and the [red priest](wiki:Red_priest) promises to heal the captain's wounded hand. The crew report wild laughter and Maester [Kerwin](wiki:Kerwin) describes [High Valyrian](wiki:High_Valyrian) singing while Moqorro performs a ritual in Victarion's cabin. Afterward, Victarion orders his crew to kill the ineffectual Kerwin and toss his body from *Iron Victory*.(wiki:Iron_Victory.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Moqorro kindles fires on the forecastle each night.(wiki:Iron_Victory.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
