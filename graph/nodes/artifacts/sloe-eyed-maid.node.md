## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) finds the Sloe-Eyed Maid in the [port of Qarth](wiki:Port_of_Qarth). She considers the ship too small for her purposes.(wiki:Sloe-Eyed_Maid.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

### A Dance With Dragons[ ]

[Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) is served [sister's stew](wiki:Sister's_stew) while at [Breakwater](wiki:Breakwater). Lord [Godric Borrell](wiki:Godric_Borrell) tells him the stew's [spices](wiki:Spices) were from a sloe-eyed maid. Godric explains a ship sailing to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) was blown from the [Bite](wiki:Bite) into the rocks of [Sweetsister](wiki:Sweetsister).(wiki:Sloe-Eyed_Maid.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
While Davos is at the [Lazy Eel](wiki:Lazy_Eel) in [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor), a Braavosi oarsman recounts a tale he heard from the captain's steward of the Sloe-Eyed Maid while the ship was in [Pentos](wiki:Pentos). The captain of the Sloe-Eyed Maid had refused to transport [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) from [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), claiming that spices were more profitable and less dangerous than [dragons](wiki:Dragons).(wiki:Sloe-Eyed_Maid.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Instead, the ship may have eventually been lured to its doom by a false light of the [Sistermen](wiki:Sistermen).
