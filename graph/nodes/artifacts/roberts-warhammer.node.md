## Identity

Robert's warhammer is an artifact (named weapon, ship, or object) from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges


## Appearances & Description

The spiked [iron](wiki:Iron) warhammer is a heavy weapon which [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) can barely lift.(wiki:Robert's_warhammer.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Origins

The warhammer was forged for [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) by [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye) at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End).(wiki:Robert's_warhammer.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Robert wielded his warhammer during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). The [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End) used its spike(wiki:Robert's_warhammer.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_robert.27s_rebellion.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) to deal a crushing blow against [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone), in the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident), smashing his chest, sending [rubies](wiki:Ruby) from Rhaegar's armor into [the river](wiki:Ruby_ford).(wiki:Robert's_warhammer.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
King Robert used his hammer during the [siege of Pyke](wiki:Siege_of_Pyke).(wiki:Robert's_warhammer.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
On [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm)'s last [name day](wiki:Name_day), Robert sent his [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son a smaller version of his warhammer.(wiki:Robert's_warhammer.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

During the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney), Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) sees the warhammer alongside an immense [iron](wiki:Iron) shield outside of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s pavilion.(wiki:Robert's_warhammer.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[King Robert's Hammer](wiki:King_Robert's_Hammer) is a war galley in the [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet) of Robert's son, King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Robert's_warhammer.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
