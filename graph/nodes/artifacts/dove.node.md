## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Sailing out of [Myr](wiki:Myr), Dove and its cargo of Myrish lace, carpets, and sweet green [wines](wiki:Wine) are captured by the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) near [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay). The Dove reports of things [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) already knows, such as [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) horsemen along the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne) and the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) upon the march.(wiki:Dove.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
The ship is added to the fleet and renamed Shrike by Victarion, who also takes the captain's [Myrish eye](wiki:Myrish_eye). Victarion opts to keep its crew for ransom, reasoning that they are neither [slaves](wiki:Slave) nor slavers, but valuable free Myrmen and seasoned sailors.(wiki:Dove.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
