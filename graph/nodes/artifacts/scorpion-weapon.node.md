## Origins

During the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), a scorpion bolt fired from the highest tower of the [Hellholt](wiki:Hellholt) struck [Meraxes](wiki:Meraxes) in the eye.(wiki:Scorpion_(weapon).cite_ref-Rfabthe_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Prince [Morion Martell](wiki:Morion_Martell) loaded his ships with massive scorpions for the [Fourth Dornish War](wiki:Fourth_Dornish_War), but the fleet was destroyed by the [dragons](wiki:Dragon) of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) while crossing the [Sea of Dorne](wiki:Sea_of_Dorne).(wiki:Scorpion_(weapon).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), multiple iron bolts fired from scorpions struck [Meleys](wiki:Meleys) during the [battle at Rook's Rest](wiki:Battle_at_Rook's_Rest), but were unable to bring down the dragon.(wiki:Scorpion_(weapon).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) [Stormcloud](wiki:Stormcloud) was mortally wounded by a scorpion bolt.(wiki:Scorpion_(weapon).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)
[Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), loaded the decks of his new [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) with scorpions and [spitfires](wiki:Spitfire) before [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion).(wiki:Scorpion_(weapon).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
