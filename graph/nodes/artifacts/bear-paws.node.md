## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

During the [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell), [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) sees the [northmen](wiki:Northmen) putting on bear-paws. At first she judges them as strange things, but she quickly realizes how useful they are when she sees the northmen easily outpassing [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s [southron](wiki:Southron) [knights](wiki:Knight). The knights try to put bear-paws on their horses, but the palfreys and destriers refuse to move, or try to shake them off their feet. One destrier breaks an ankle trying to walk in them.(wiki:Bear-paws.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
The bear-paws make the march much easier for the northmen, which allows them to remain more healthy than the southerners once they reach the [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village).(wiki:Bear-paws.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) sees lads of the free folk wearing bear-paws as they pass through the [Wall](wiki:Wall), walking atop the snowdrifts without sinking through.(wiki:Bear-paws.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
