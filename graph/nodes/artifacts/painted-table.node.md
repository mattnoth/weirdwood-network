## Appearances & Description

The Painted Table is a large table, carved from a block of wood and painted in the form of a detailed map of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) as it was at the start of [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Painted_Table.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) It depicts the landscape and settlements of the continent, but does not include borders.(wiki:Painted_Table.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The Painted Table is more than fifty feet long: roughly twenty-five feet wide at its widest point and four feet at its thinnest. Positioned near [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone)'s spot on the map is a raised seat that allows the occupant to view the entire table. It is covered by over three hundred years of varnish.(wiki:Painted_Table.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Near the table is an iron brazier,(wiki:Painted_Table.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) and the chamber contains a hearth.(wiki:Painted_Table.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Origins

Commissioned by [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), [Lord of Dragonstone](wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone), years before [the Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest),(wiki:Painted_Table.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) the Painted Table was where Aegon the Conqueror planned his invasion of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Painted_Table.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) While showing the Painted Table to his grandsons, Princes [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) and [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)), in [37 AC](wiki:37_AC), King Aegon I suffered a stroke and died soon after.(wiki:Painted_Table.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) sent letters from the Painted Table after his coronation.(wiki:Painted_Table.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) received visitors in the chamber when he resided at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Painted_Table.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Androw Farman](wiki:Androw_Farman) often spent days in the Chamber of the Painted Table, pushing wooden solders on the great map. After admitting to having [poisoned](wiki:Poison) the companions of his wife, [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)), Androw leapt from the chamber's window to his death.(wiki:Painted_Table.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Maester [Cressen](wiki:Cressen) speaks with [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), [Lord of Dragonstone](wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone), in the Chamber of the Painted Table.(wiki:Painted_Table.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Stannis later speaks with Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) and Maester [Pylos](wiki:Pylos) about his claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) at the table.(wiki:Painted_Table.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Meeting with Davos and [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) at the table, Stannis names Davos as his [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) and [Lord of the Rainwood](wiki:Lord_of_the_Rainwood).(wiki:Painted_Table.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Davos waits for Stannis in the Chamber of the Painted Table after helping [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm) escape Dragonstone.(wiki:Painted_Table.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
