## Origins

[Roro Uhoris](wiki:Roro_Uhoris), the Blind Bastard, once took the Cobblecat [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) and traded weapons and armor to the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) for furs and other precious goods. When he set sail south again, he was intercepted in the [Bay of Seals](wiki:Bay_of_Seals) by three ships of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) out of [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea). The Cobblecat was brought into port, the sailors lost their cargo, and Roro was beheaded for having traded arms to the wildlings.(wiki:Cobblecat.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
