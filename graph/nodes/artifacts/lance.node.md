## Origins

Lord [Leo Tyrell](wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(Longthorn)) was called "Longthorn" for his prowess with a lance. He is considered by many to be the finest jouster in history.(wiki:Lance.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_house_tyrell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
At the [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven) of Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall), Duncan's team used tourney lances to counter the war lances used by Prince [Aerion](wiki:Aerion_Targaryen)'s team, as tourney lances are longer.(wiki:Lance.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
[Lormelle Long Lance](wiki:Lormelle_Long_Lance) was once defeated by Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy).(wiki:Lance.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
[Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) is better with a lance than his half brother, [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow).(wiki:Lance.cite_ref-Ragot5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Similarly Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) is a better lance than his older brother, Ser [Garlan Tyrell](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell).(wiki:Lance.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

At the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney), [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) uses a lance fashioned from [goldenheart](wiki:Goldenheart).(wiki:Lance.cite_ref-Ragot25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) offers to supply the [Vale mountain clans](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) with lances, among other weapons.(wiki:Lance.cite_ref-Ragot42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) is killed by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane)'s lance at the [Mummer's Ford](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford) and dies for the first time before being [resurrected](wiki:Last_kiss) by his friend, [Thoros](wiki:Thoros) of [Myr](wiki:Myr).(wiki:Lance.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)(wiki:Lance.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)(wiki:Lance.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Instead of banners, the [Wild Hares](wiki:Wild_Hares) attach rabbit skins to their lances.(wiki:Lance.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Oznak zo Pahl](wiki:Oznak_zo_Pahl) uses a fourteen foot long lance, swirled in pink and white.(wiki:Lance.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
At the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), the [wildlings](wiki:Free_folk) use fire-hardened lances.(wiki:Lance.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

The lance is the weapon of choice of [Elia Sand](wiki:Elia_Sand) which led [her father](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) to call her Lady Lance.(wiki:Lance.cite_ref-twowarianne1_27-0)
