## Identity

A Valyrian steel dagger with a dragonbone handle, sent by an unknown master to kill Bran Stark while he lay comatose at Winterfell. The assassination attempt — and the mystery of its ownership — triggers the chain of suspicion that fractures the realm. Later identified as having passed through Littlefinger's hands.
