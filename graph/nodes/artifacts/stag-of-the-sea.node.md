## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Stag of the Sea is seen berthed alongside the [Fury](wiki:Fury) at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) by Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth).(wiki:Stag_of_the_Sea.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Stag of the Sea is part of Stannis's fleet commanded by Ser [Imry Florent](wiki:Imry_Florent) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). It flanks Fury with [Lord Steffon](wiki:Lord_Steffon) in the first line of battle. It splits one of the enemy galleys in two, but later catches fire and is boarded during the fighting.(wiki:Stag_of_the_Sea.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
