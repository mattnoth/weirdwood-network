### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Swordfish is seen by Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) in the harbor of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Swordfish_(ship).cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) It has the largest ram in [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s fleet.(wiki:Swordfish_(ship).cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
It is part of the fleet commanded by Ser [Imry Florent](wiki:Imry_Florent) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), though its captain is not considered capable by Davos. Its sails are left up and are hit by pitch, but the Swordfish keeps sailing until she rams a hulk filled with [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire), destroying her almost instantly.(wiki:Swordfish_(ship).cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
