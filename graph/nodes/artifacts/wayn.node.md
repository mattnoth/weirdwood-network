## Culture

The [Free Folk](wiki:Free_Folk) from [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) uses wayns, [carts](wiki:Cart), and [sleds](wiki:Sled) to transport their goods.(wiki:Wayn.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Wayns, carts, [hathays](wiki:Hathay), and [palanquins](wiki:Palanquin) are common in the streets of [Volantis](wiki:Volantis).(wiki:Wayn.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

[House Wayn](wiki:House_Wayn) of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) and [House Waynwood](wiki:House_Waynwood) of the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) both depict wheels on their sigil.(wiki:Wayn.cite_ref-6)(wiki:Wayn.cite_ref-7)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

After the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) brings one hundred wayns laden with [food](wiki:Food) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) each day.(wiki:Wayn.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

[Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) gives the command of the baggage train of his army to Ser [Wendel Manderly](wiki:Wendel_Manderly) when they depart from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) for the [Twins](wiki:Twins) to attend the [wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) of his uncle, Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully), and [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey). The train is composed of a procession of wayns laden with food, fodder, camp supplies, and wedding gifts. The heavy rain renders the journey very slow. Half the wayns are abandoned to the muck, and their loads distributed amongst mules and draft [horses](wiki:Horse).(wiki:Wayn.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

[Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) steals a wayn from a farmer, and uses it to infiltrate the Twins with [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) during the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Wayn.cite_ref-Rasos50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Two wayns are used to transport [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Aemon](wiki:Aemon), [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) and her [son](wiki:Monster), as well as provisions for the journey from [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) to [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea).(wiki:Wayn.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) takes forty wayns laden with food when he leaves [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) to attend the wedding of [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Bolton) and "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)" at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Wayn.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

The wayns and wagons of the baggage train of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s army have difficulty keeping up the pace of the [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell).(wiki:Wayn.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
