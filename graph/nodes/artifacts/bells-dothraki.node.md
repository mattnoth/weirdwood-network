## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

When [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) first sees her future husband [Khal](wiki:Khal) [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), she notes that his heavy black braid is hung with tiny bells that ring softly as he moves.(wiki:Bells_(Dothraki).cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) After their wedding, before they consummate the marriage, Drogo removes all his bells and unbraids his hair, with Dany's help.(wiki:Bells_(Dothraki).cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) When Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo)'s khalasar defeats [Ogo](wiki:Ogo)'s khalasar, he adds Ogo's and his son [Fogo](wiki:Fogo)'s bells to his hair.(wiki:Bells_(Dothraki).cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) When Daenerys prepares Drogo for his funeral pyre, she puts all of his bells in his hair.(wiki:Bells_(Dothraki).cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Jhiqui](wiki:Jhiqui) follows [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) custom, and fastens a silver bell in [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s hair to mark her victory over the [Undying](wiki:Undying). Daenerys tries to tell her that she has won no victories, but Jhiqui disagrees, telling Dany that she burned the [maegi](wiki:Maegi) in their house of dust and sent their souls to hell. Daenerys decides to hold her tongue, thinking that the Dothraki will esteem her all the more for a few bells in her hair. When she goes to mount [her silver](wiki:Her_silver) she chimes, and again with every stride, but neither Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) or her [bloodriders](wiki:Bloodrider) make mention of it.(wiki:Bells_(Dothraki).cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Upon entering [Astapor](wiki:Astapor) to purchase her [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied), Dany is garbed in Dothraki fashion. [Irri](wiki:Irri) and [Jhiqui](wiki:Jhiqui) have braided her hair and hung it with a tiny silver bell whose chime sings of the [Undying](wiki:Undying) of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), burned in their [Palace of Dust](wiki:Palace_of_Dust).(wiki:Bells_(Dothraki).cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
After her victory over the [Good Masters](wiki:Good_Masters) of Astapor, Daenerys wears two bells in her braid.(wiki:Bells_(Dothraki).cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) A third bell is added after her defeat of the [Wise Masters](wiki:Wise_Masters) of [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai).(wiki:Bells_(Dothraki).cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Presumably a fourth bell was added after her defeat of the [Great Masters](wiki:Great_Masters) of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).
After conquering Meereen, silver bells tinkle in Daenerys's bloodriders' oiled braids, and they wear the gold and jewels of dead men.(wiki:Bells_(Dothraki).cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

As Queen of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), Dany adopts the Meereenese [style of dress](wiki:Tokar), and forgoes her Dothraki bells.(wiki:Bells_(Dothraki).cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Daenerys's bloodrider [Rakharo](wiki:Rakharo) returns from subduing the hinterlands of Meereen with four bells in his hair.(wiki:Bells_(Dothraki).cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
On the [Shy Maid](wiki:Shy_Maid), [Haldon](wiki:Haldon) Halfmaeaster explains to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) that on the way down from the [Sorrows](wiki:Sorrows) to [Selhorys](wiki:Selhorys), the boat's crew thrice glimpsed Dothraki riders moving south along the river's eastern shore. Once they were so close, they could hear the bells tinkling in their braids.(wiki:Bells_(Dothraki).cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
As Dany wanders the [Dothraki Sea](wiki:Dothraki_Sea), starving and alone, she hears through the grass a soft silvery tinkling. At first she smiles, remembering Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), then she thinks that her bloodriders have found her, but then the green sea opens and a unknown Dothraki rider appears, bells singing in his hair. Dany realises that he is a [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) scout, and she is in great danger. The scout does not see her. When he gallops away, Dany manages to call [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) down to her.(wiki:Bells_(Dothraki).cite_ref-Radwd71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
