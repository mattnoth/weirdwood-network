## Origins

Ser [Willam Royce](wiki:Willam_Royce) wielded the blade during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). He was one of the [Seven Who Rode](wiki:Seven_Who_Rode) to rescue Prince [Joffrey Velaryon](wiki:Joffrey_Velaryon) from the [riot](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)) of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), but was killed when a man leaped down from a rooftop to land on his back. Lamentation was torn from Willam's hand and carried off, never to be found again.(wiki:Lamentation_(sword).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Lamentation_(sword).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
However, some rumors claim that Ser [Warrick Wheaton](wiki:Warrick_Wheaton) slashed a wing off of [Syrax](wiki:Syrax) with a Valyrian steel sword, likely Lamentation, during the [Storming of the Dragonpit](wiki:Storming_of_the_Dragonpit).(wiki:Lamentation_(sword).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
