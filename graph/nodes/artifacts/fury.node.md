## Origins

Stannis commanded the [assault on Dragonstone](wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone) from the decks of the Fury after [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Fury.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Presumably Stannis also made Fury his flagship for the [sea battle off Fair Isle](wiki:Sea_battle_off_Fair_Isle) during [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion), where the ship sank [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy)'s [Golden Storm](wiki:Golden_Storm).(wiki:Fury.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Fury is seen berthed between the [Stag of the Sea](wiki:Stag_of_the_Sea) and [Lord Steffon](wiki:Lord_Steffon) in the harbor at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) by Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth).(wiki:Fury.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Ser [Imry Florent](wiki:Imry_Florent) commands Stannis's fleet from the Fury, which is flanked by Stag of the Sea and Lord Steffon in the first line of battle. It engages the [Godsgrace](wiki:Godsgrace_(ship)) during the battle. Fury is engulfed and destroyed by [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) after the explosion of the [Swordfish](wiki:Swordfish_(ship)).(wiki:Fury.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) The oarmaster, Davos's son [Maric](wiki:Maric_Seaworth), dies in the battle.
