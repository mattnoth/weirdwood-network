## Edges


## Origins

Dark Sister has been in the possession of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) since before [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) It may have been forged for a [woman warrior](wiki:Woman_warrior) originally, as its slender blade is designed for a woman's hand.(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-SSM_2-0)

### Visenya[ ]

Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) was skilled in the use of Dark Sister, having trained beside her younger brother, King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), since childhood. During the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), the Targaryens were attacked on multiple occasions by assassins sent by [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Visenya was attacked one night in King's Landing, and the queen slew the last assassin with Dark Sister after two of her escorts were killed.(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Visenya also twice wielded her longsword against Dornish assassins in Aegon's defense,(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) with one such attack taking place in [10 AC](wiki:10_AC).(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) When Aegon proclaimed he still felt sufficiently protected by [his guards](wiki:House_Targaryen_guards), Visenya quickly drew Dark Sister and cut Aegon's cheek before the king's defenders could react. Aegon then entrusted Visenya with establishing the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### Maegor and Jaehaerys[ ]

Visenya bestowed Dark Sister on her thirteen-year-old son, Prince [Maegor Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), in [25 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) When his brother [Aenys I](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen) became king in [37 AC](wiki:37_AC), Maegor was given [Blackfyre](wiki:Blackfyre). Since that day, he hung Dark Sister on the walls of his chambers on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), while wielding Blackfyre in battle.(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
After Dowager Queen Visenya died on Dragonstone in [44 AC](wiki:44_AC), the hostage Dowager Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon), Aenys's widow, fled from with her children [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and [Alysanne](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), taking Dark Sister from Visenya's chamber.(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) In [48 AC](wiki:48_AC), Jaehaerys carried Dark Sister when he announced his claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End). The prince became king after Maegor died on the throne.(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

### Baelon the Brave[ ]

Prince [Baelon Targaryen](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)), one of King Jaehaerys's sons, wielded Dark Sister in [92 AC](wiki:92_AC) when avenging the death of his brother, Prince [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)), who had been slain by [Myrish](wiki:Myr) exiles who had invaded and occupied [Tarth](wiki:Tarth). Baelon the Brave slew countless Myrmen with the blade.(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### The Rogue Prince[ ]

Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) was given Dark Sister by King Jaehaerys I for his prowess.(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_viserys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-11) Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) defeated Daemon in the [tourney for King Viserys I's accession](wiki:Tourney_for_King_Viserys_I's_accession), knocking Dark Sister from the prince's hand with a morningstar.(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Daemon wielded Dark Sister during his [war for the Stepstones](wiki:War_for_the_Stepstones), using it to behead [Craghas Drahar](wiki:Craghas_Drahar) in [108 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) In [115 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), he defeated the [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) betrothed of [Laena Velaryon](wiki:Laena_Velaryon) with the blade in a duel.(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Daemon fought against [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) with the blade in the [Battle Above the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Above_the_Gods_Eye), leaping from the back of his [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Caraxes](wiki:Caraxes), to plunge Dark Sister into Aemond's eye. When Aemond's bones were found some years later in the waters of the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye), Dark Sister was thrust hilt-deep through his eye socket.(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### The Dragonknight[ ]

Prince [Aemon Targaryen](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_II)) was deemed worthy of Dark Sister. The Dragonknight was considered the greatest jouster and swordsman of his era.(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_viserys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### Bloodraven[ ]

Dark Sister was eventually bestowed upon Lord [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers), though his favorite weapon was a [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) longbow.(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-SSM_2-1) In [205 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), a young [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) saw Lord Bloodraven wearing Dark Sister on his hip while riding in the [Street of Steel](wiki:Street_of_Steel) with his [Raven's Teeth](wiki:Raven's_Teeth).(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)
According to a semi-canon source, Brynden took Dark Sister with him when he went to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-16)(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-17) Bloodraven rose to become [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) in [239 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), but he disappeared [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) in [252 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Dark_Sister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) The sword's current whereabouts are unknown.
