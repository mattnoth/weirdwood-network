## Appearances & Description

It has grey-black and red ripples through the steel, with the red almost as dark as the grey. Its scabbard glitters gold, and is decorated with a row of [lion](wiki:Lion)'s heads and smoldering red [rubies](wiki:Rubies).(wiki:Oathkeeper.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) The pommel has a [golden lion's](wiki:House_Lannister) head, with ruby eyes that shine like two red stars.(wiki:Oathkeeper.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) has the [armorer](wiki:Armorer) [Tobho Mott](wiki:Tobho_Mott) melt down [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) [greatsword](wiki:Greatsword) [Ice](wiki:Ice), to make a sword for [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister). Mott has enough metal to make two swords, one [longsword](wiki:Longsword) and [a slightly smaller sword](wiki:Widow's_Wail). He attempts to tint the swords crimson for the color of House Lannister, but the metal absorbs the color, resulting in ripples of nearly-black grey and red almost as dark as the grey.(wiki:Oathkeeper.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Tywin presents the sword as a gift for his elder son, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Oathkeeper.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) tells Jaime that the gift is heartfelt, but Jaime thinks his father and uncle are mocking his missing hand.(wiki:Oathkeeper.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Jaime instead gives the sword to [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth), charging her with finding the whereabouts of [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), asking her to name the sword "Oathkeeper".(wiki:Oathkeeper.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Jaime notes the appropriateness of protecting [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s daughter with his own steel.(wiki:Oathkeeper.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) is initially reluctant to use Oathkeeper, considering it almost a "magic sword", but wields it to slay [Pyg](wiki:Pyg) and [Timeon](wiki:Timeon) at the [Whispers](wiki:Whispers),(wiki:Oathkeeper.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and [Rorge](wiki:Rorge) at the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads).(wiki:Oathkeeper.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) When she is captured by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), they use the sword's appearance as evidence that Brienne serves [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister), and [Lady Stoneheart](wiki:Lady_Stoneheart) says it should be better named "Oathbreaker". She demands that Brienne take up the sword to keep her oath to [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) and slay Jaime Lannister, or be hanged. Brienne initially refuses, but when she is being hanged and sees her friends dying, she screams "sword".(wiki:Oathkeeper.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Oathkeeper.cite_ref-8)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Brienne appears at [Pennytree](wiki:Pennytree) while Jaime is camped there. She bears Oathkeeper, and tells Jaime that to fulfill her quest, Jaime must come alone with her to save [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) from the [Hound](wiki:Hound), claiming they are a day's ride away.(wiki:Oathkeeper.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
