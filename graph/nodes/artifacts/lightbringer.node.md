## Origins

Lightbringer is associated with a great warrior—most commonly called [Azor Ahai](wiki:Azor_Ahai), but also Hyrkoon the Hero, Yin Tar, Neferion, and Eldric Shadowchaser in other cultures—who led the virtuous in battle when darkness lay over the [known world](wiki:Known_world), possibly the [Long Night](wiki:Long_Night) recalled by [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
According to the legend of Azor Ahai, he labored for thirty days and thirty nights to create a hero's sword. However, when he went to temper it in water, the sword broke. Azor Ahai was not one to give up easily, so he started over. The second time he took fifty days and fifty nights to make the sword, even better than the first. To temper it this time, he captured a [lion](wiki:Lion) and drove the sword into its heart, but once more the [steel](wiki:Steel) shattered. The third time, with a heavy heart, for he knew beforehand what he must do to finish the blade, he worked for a hundred days and nights until it was finished. This time, Azor Ahai called for his wife, [Nissa Nissa](wiki:Nissa_Nissa), and asked her to bare her breast. He drove his sword into her breast, her soul combining with the steel of the sword, creating Lightbringer, while her cry of anguish and ecstasy left a crack across the face of the [moon](wiki:Moon).(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
According to the [Jade Compendium](wiki:Jade_Compendium) by [Colloquo Votar](wiki:Colloquo_Votar), Lightbringer was henceforth always as warm as Nissa Nissa had been, and that the blade burned fiery hot during battles.(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Lightbringer was able to boil the blood of a monster when Azor Ahai thrust the sword through the belly of the beast. After steam poured from the beast's mouth and its eyes melted, its body burst into flame.(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Followers of [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor) believe that Azor Ahai used the red sword to end the darkness.(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_long_night.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
It has been foretold in books from [Asshai](wiki:Asshai) that when the stars bleed and the cold winds blow, Azor Ahai will be reborn as a warrior who draws the burning sword Lightbringer from a fire.(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre), a [red priestess](wiki:Red_priest), burns wooden statues of the [Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) from the [sept at Dragonstone](wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone) in a pyre outside of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone)'s gates; [her ruby](wiki:Melisandre's_ruby) glows during the ceremony. Melisandre proclaims that [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) is [Azor Ahai](wiki:Azor_Ahai) come again, and she has Stannis withdraw a longsword which had been thrust into the breast of the [Mother's](wiki:Mother_(the_Seven)) statue. The cherry-red steel of the blade, which has a leather grip, is covered with jade-green flames. After Stannis thrusts the sword into the ground, [Bryen Farring](wiki:Bryen_Farring) and [Devan Seaworth](wiki:Devan_Seaworth) cover the burnt and blackened sword with Stannis's cloak.(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan) tells Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) that the sword is not actually Lightbringer, and the sellsail tells the Onion Knight about the legend of Azor Ahai.(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
When Stannis meets with his younger brother, [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), near [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) notices that his sword has a great square-cut [ruby](wiki:Ruby) set in the hilt. Stannis draws Lightbringer after being mocked by Renly, and the longsword brightly shimmers red, yellow, and blazing white.(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) After Renly is assassinated by [a shadow](wiki:Shadow_assassin), one of the rumors about his death claims that Stannis killed his brother with a magic sword.(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Stannis declines Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose)'s offer to resolve the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)) through single combat.(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Because stories have spread about Stannis and his magic sword, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) decides to give his grandson, King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), a [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) longsword made from [Ice](wiki:Ice) as a wedding gift.(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Joffrey eventually names his blade [Widow's Wail](wiki:Widow's_Wail).(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Stannis touches Lightbringer to Davos's shoulder when appointing him as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King). Davos sees the longsword glowing orange, yellow, and red, and air shimmers around it. Davos does not notice heat, however.(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Melisandre tells Stannis she has had [visions](wiki:Vision) in her flames of Stannis wielding Lightbringer against the dark.(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Stannis draws a glowing Lightbringer in anger after Davos has [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm) smuggled to safety from Dragonstone. Davos reads a letter from the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch),(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) however, and Stannis travels north with his remaining followers to save [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) from [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder).(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
Following the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), Melisandre tells the officers of the Night's Watch that Stannis wields the red sword of heroes. Stannis draws Lightbringer upon Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I))'s request, and [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) explains to the blind maester that the sword prettily glows without flame. [Grenn](wiki:Grenn) compares the glowing Lightbringer to the [sun](wiki:Sun).(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

While sailing from [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), a fevered and dying Aemon tells Sam that the real Lightbringer both shone with light and radiated heat.(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Stannis angrily draws Lightbringer when [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), refuses to give him more castles along the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
When Melisandre burns "[Mance Rayder](wiki:Rattleshirt)" and the [Horn of Joramun](wiki:Horn_of_Joramun), Stannis displays a brilliant Lightbringer before the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) at Castle Black.
Stannis's possession of the enchanted blade is among the reasons why [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) continue to support him during the [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell).(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) sends [a letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) to Jon in which he claims that he has defeated Stannis in battle and taken the magic sword.(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

Instead of having [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) burned at the [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village), [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) advises Stannis to behead her brother with his sorcerous sword before a [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) on a nearby islet.(wiki:Lightbringer.cite_ref-19)
