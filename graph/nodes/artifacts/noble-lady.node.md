## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The Noble Lady is en route to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) by way of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) and [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown) with a cargo of salt cod, whale oil, and pickled herring, when it is captured off the coast of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) by the [Iron Victory](wiki:Iron_Victory). The ship is added to the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet).(wiki:Noble_Lady.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

The Noble Lady and the Iron Victory part ways in [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay). [Wulfe One-Ear](wiki:Wulfe_One-Ear) captains the Noble Lady, and is given command of the fleet accompanying her to the bay of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) has packed the Noble Lady's huge holds with armed men, who are to remain hidden until they have come upon the enemy. With the Noble Lady also sail the other, lesser prizes that the Iron Fleet had taken on its long voyage to Slaver's Bay, a lubberly assortment of cogs, great cogs, carracks, and trading galleys salted here and there with fishing boats.(wiki:Noble_Lady.cite_ref-twow_2-1) The Noble Lady and her fleet arrive as the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen) is starting, and begin attacking the enemy ships in the bay.(wiki:Noble_Lady.cite_ref-3)
