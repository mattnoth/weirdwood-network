## Origins

[Brightroar](wiki:Brightroar) was lost by [Tommen II Lannister](wiki:Tommen_II_Lannister), [King of the Rock](wiki:King_of_the_Rock), when he went in search of plunder during the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood) after the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria).(wiki:Laughing_Lion.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Centuries later, [Gerion Lannister](wiki:Gerion_Lannister) hoped to return Brightroar with any other treasures that might have survived the Doom. The Laughing Lion left [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) in [292 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) and almost nothing is known about its fate afterwards.
Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) sent men to search for the Laughing Lion and his brother. The only trace they could find was in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), where the crew deserted and Gerion had been forced to buy [slaves](wiki:Slaves), since no free man would willingly sign aboard a ship sailing to the [Smoking Sea](wiki:Smoking_Sea).(wiki:Laughing_Lion.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) What happened afterwards is unknown. The ship with Gerion and the crew are presumed lost at sea.(wiki:Laughing_Lion.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
