## Appearances & Description

Saduleon is a large, broad-beamed cog with immense holds and huge sails.(wiki:Saduleon.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Magister [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) sends Saduleon, [Joso's Prank](wiki:Joso's_Prank), and [Summer Sun](wiki:Summer_Sun) to [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) so they can bring Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) back to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) in preparation for her return to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). Daenerys accepts, but insists on renaming the ships [in homage of](wiki:Named_after) the three [dragons](wiki:Dragon) from [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), [Balerion the Black Dread](wiki:Balerion), [Meraxes](wiki:Meraxes), and [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar).(wiki:Saduleon.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) She tasks [Arstan Whitebeard](wiki:Arstan_Whitebeard) with having the new names painted on the hulls in golden letters three feet high.(wiki:Saduleon.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Daenerys travels aboard Balerion with her [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar), her [handmaids](wiki:Handmaid) [Irri](wiki:Irri) and [Jhiqui](wiki:Jhiqui), and her three young dragons, [Drogon](wiki:Drogon), [Viserion](wiki:Viserion), and [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal). The number of rats on Balerion drastically decline, and her initially-wary crew become proud of the dragons. [Dolphins](wiki:Dolphin) sometimes swim alongside the ship.(wiki:Saduleon.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
While enroute in the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea) from Qarth to Pentos, Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) convinces Daenerys to change course for [Astapor](wiki:Astapor), buy an army of [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied), and continue to Pentos overland.(wiki:Saduleon.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Daenerys has Jorah remain aboard Balerion with her dragons when she first visits Astapor.(wiki:Saduleon.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
On the shores of [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay), Daenerys has Balerion broken up so as to provide her army with the wood it needs to make instruments of war for the [siege of Meereen](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen).(wiki:Saduleon.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
