## Origins

Late in the year [233 AC](wiki:233_AC) following the [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council),(wiki:Golden_Dragon.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sailed north from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) aboard the *Golden Dragon*. He was escorted by Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) to [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea). Among those aboard who had been sent to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) by King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) to join the Night's Watch alongside Aemon was [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers),(wiki:Golden_Dragon.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and many of the [Raven's Teeth](wiki:Raven's_Teeth).(wiki:Golden_Dragon.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
