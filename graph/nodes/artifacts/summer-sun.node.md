## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Magister [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) sends [Saduleon](wiki:Saduleon), [Joso's Prank](wiki:Joso's_Prank), and Summer Sun to [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) to bring [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) back to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) in preparation for her return to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). Daenerys accepts, but renaming the ships as Balerion, Meraxes, and Vhagar in homage of the three powerful [dragons](wiki:Dragon) of [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Summer_Sun.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) She tasks [Arstan Whitebeard](wiki:Arstan_Whitebeard) with having the new names painted on the hulls in golden letters three feet high.(wiki:Summer_Sun.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Daenerys orders her small fleet to head for [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay). At one point, she offers all three ships as payment to the [Good Masters](wiki:Good_Masters) of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor) for her [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied).(wiki:Summer_Sun.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
On the shores of Slaver's Bay, Daenerys orders her fleet broken up to provide the wood needed by her army for instruments of war for the [siege of Meereen](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen).(wiki:Summer_Sun.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
