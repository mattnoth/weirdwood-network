## Appearances & Description

Sea Bitch has a lean, black hull a hundred feet long, a single mast and fifty oars. The longship has a deck large enough to hold a hundred men and an iron ram shaped like an arrowhead.(wiki:Sea_Bitch.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) is given command of a newly-built longship at [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport) after he returns to [Pyke](wiki:Pyke_(island)). He [names](wiki:Named_after) it Sea Bitch after Esgred (actually his sister [Asha](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy)) claims the longship is a sea bitch instead of a fair maiden.(wiki:Sea_Bitch.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) loans his own steersman, [Rymolf Stormdrunk](wiki:Rymolf_Stormdrunk), to his nephew,(wiki:Sea_Bitch.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) and [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy), another of Theon's uncles, blesses the new ship.(wiki:Sea_Bitch.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
Theon sails the Sea Bitch as the flagship of his force during his [harrying of the Stony Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore).(wiki:Sea_Bitch.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Deviating from the plan of his father [Balon](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), Theon grants six longships to [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) to continue raiding the shore but keeps the Sea Bitch and [Dagmer](wiki:Dagmer)'s [Foamdrinker](wiki:Foamdrinker).(wiki:Sea_Bitch.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
While Dagmer threatens [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Torrhen's_Square) and distracts Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel), Theon is able to capture [capture Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Sea_Bitch.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Crewmembers [Stygg](wiki:Stygg) and [Urzen](wiki:Urzen) are among those who abandon Theon before [battle outside the gates of Winterfell](wiki:Battle_outside_the_gates_of_Winterfell) and the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell) by [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton).(wiki:Sea_Bitch.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
