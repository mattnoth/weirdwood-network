### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Pate](wiki:Pate_(novice)) steals the personal key of Walgrave, in exchange for a [golden dragon](wiki:Currency) from a person who calls himself the [Alchemist](wiki:Alchemist). Pate's steals the key due to needing a gold coin to claim the maidenhood of his beloved [Rosey](wiki:Rosey), the youngest serving girl of the [Quill and Tankard](wiki:Quill_and_Tankard) inn.(wiki:Personal_key_of_Walgrave.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Before Pate hands over the key in a narrow alley in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), something makes him hesitate, and he asks what the man wants with the key, as some of the old [Valyrian](wiki:Valyria) scrolls down in the locked vaults were said to be the only surviving copies in the world. The Alchemist replies that what he wants is none of Pate's concern.(wiki:Personal_key_of_Walgrave.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
After he hands over the key, Pate dies of [poison](wiki:Poison),(wiki:Personal_key_of_Walgrave.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) and the Alchemist takes his face and place.(wiki:Personal_key_of_Walgrave.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
