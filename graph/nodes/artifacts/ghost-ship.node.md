## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Two sleek galleys sail from [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) and [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) south toward [New Ghis](wiki:New_Ghis) for supplies and [legionary](wiki:Ghiscari_legions) reinforcements, but encounter the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) in [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay). The galleys evade [Woe](wiki:Woe) and [Forlorn Hope](wiki:Forlorn_Hope), but are captured by [Iron Wing](wiki:Iron_Wing), [Sparrowhawk](wiki:Sparrowhawk), and [Kraken's Kiss](wiki:Kraken's_Kiss). [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) beheads their captains because they said that [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) is dead. Victarion kills their crew aside from the enslaved rowers, who are forced to join the Iron Fleet. Victarion renames the ships Ghost and [Shade](wiki:Shade), believing they will return to haunt [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai).(wiki:Ghost_(ship).cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
