## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

King Robert's Hammer is part of the escort [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sends to bring [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos). If the small fleet spots any lone ship they are to run it off or destroy it. Should an enemy fleet give battle the [Bold Wind](wiki:Bold_Wind) is to escort the [Seaswift](wiki:Seaswift) while the rest of the fleet, consisting of Rob's Hammer, [Lionstar](wiki:Lionstar) and [Lady Lyanna](wiki:Lady_Lyanna), are to give battle.(wiki:King_Robert's_Hammer.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) notices the absence of Rob's Hammer from the ships defending [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) against [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:King_Robert's_Hammer.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After the Blackwater there are less than a dozen warships left in King's Landing, so Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) orders the construction of [ten new dromonds](wiki:Cersei's_dromonds).(wiki:King_Robert's_Hammer.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [Sweet Cersei](wiki:Sweet_Cersei) will be the equal of Rob's Hammer, while [Lord Tywin](wiki:Lord_Tywin) will have twice as many oars.(wiki:King_Robert's_Hammer.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:King_Robert's_Hammer.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
