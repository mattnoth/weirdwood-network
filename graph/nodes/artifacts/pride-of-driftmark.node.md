## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The Pride of Driftmark is spotted by [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) in the harbor of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) when [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) gathers his sworn swords in preparation for the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Pride_of_Driftmark.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) It is part of Stannis's [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet) commanded by Ser [Imry Florent](wiki:Imry_Florent) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), and the ship crashes through two [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) river runners. Pride of Driftmark tries to flee from [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) during the battle, but green flame races up its oars.(wiki:Pride_of_Driftmark.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]
