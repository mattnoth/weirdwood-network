## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Magister [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) sends [Saduleon](wiki:Saduleon), Joso's Prank, and [Summer Sun](wiki:Summer_Sun) to [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) to bring [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) back to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) in preparation for her return to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). Daenerys accepts, but insists in renaming the ships as Balerion, Meraxes, and Vhagar in homage of the three powerful [dragons](wiki:Dragon) from [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Joso's_Prank.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) She tasks [Arstan Whitebeard](wiki:Arstan_Whitebeard) with having the new names painted on the hulls in golden letters three feet high.(wiki:Joso's_Prank.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Meraxes is broken up along with the others on the shores near [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) so that Daenerys's army will have the wood to build rams, shields and other items useful in a siege. They use the mainmast of Meraxes, which they nickname Joso's Cock, to break down the [eastern gate](wiki:Eastern_gate) of the city during the [siege of Meereen](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen).(wiki:Joso's_Prank.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
