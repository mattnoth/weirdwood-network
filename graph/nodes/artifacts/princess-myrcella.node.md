### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Princess Myrcella is among the ships being built as part of Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s plan to increase the size of the [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet) instead of relying on the traditional sea powers to protect the crown's interests.(wiki:Princess_Myrcella.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) intends to name it Moon Boy after [the fool](wiki:Moon_Boy) of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), but [Aurane Waters](wiki:Aurane_Waters) convinces the king to [name](wiki:Named_after) the ship after [his sister](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon).(wiki:Princess_Myrcella.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
During her imprisonment by the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), Cersei is visited by [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn), who tells the queen that Aurane fled [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with the new ships after her arrest.(wiki:Princess_Myrcella.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
