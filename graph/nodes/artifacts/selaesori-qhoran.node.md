## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The Selaesori Qhoran is set to sail from [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) to [Qarth](wiki:Qarth). However, the high priest of [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor) in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), [Benerro](wiki:Benerro), sees in his fires that the ship will not reach her destination.(wiki:Selaesori_Qhoran.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) This information reaches the [widow of the waterfront](wiki:Widow_of_the_waterfront), who instructs [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), who are trying to find a passage to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), to board the Selaesori Qhoran.(wiki:Selaesori_Qhoran.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Jorah Mormont and Tyrion Lannister board the Selaesori Qhoran, and are accompanied by the dwarf [Penny](wiki:Penny) and her pets [Pretty Pig](wiki:Pretty_Pig) and [Crunch](wiki:Crunch). The [red priest](wiki:Red_priest) [Moqorro](wiki:Moqorro) travels on the ship as well, and also five warriors of the [Fiery Hand](wiki:Fiery_Hand).(wiki:Selaesori_Qhoran.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) The Selaesori Qhoran carries tin and iron, bales of wool and lace, fifty [Myrish](wiki:Myr) carpets, a corpse pickled in brine, and twenty jars of [dragon peppers](wiki:Dragon_pepper).(wiki:Selaesori_Qhoran.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
As predicted,(wiki:Selaesori_Qhoran.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) the ship does not reach its destination. After being becalmed in the [Gulf of Grief](wiki:Gulf_of_Grief) for twelve days, it is first damaged in a violent storm, which causes the ship to end up floating low in the water, listing ten degrees to port, with her hull sprung in half a hundred places and her hold awash with seawater. The scroll-carrying arm of the figurehead breaks off also.(wiki:Selaesori_Qhoran.cite_ref-Radwd40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Several people are lost at sea during the storm, including the priest Moqorro. A few people die during the following days, including the ship's captain, and several of the slaves escape in one of the smaller boats. The damaged and helpless Selaesori Qhoran drifts around for nineteen days, until it is boarded by slavers, who take the survivors captive.(wiki:Selaesori_Qhoran.cite_ref-Radwd40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
