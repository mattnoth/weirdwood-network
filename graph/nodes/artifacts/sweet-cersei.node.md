## Appearances & Description

Sweet Cersei is [named after](wiki:Named_after) Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) and has a gilded figurehead carved in her likeness, with a spear, mail and [lion](wiki:Lion) helm.(wiki:Sweet_Cersei.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Sweet Cersei is one of the ships being built in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) as part of Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s plan to augment the [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet) instead of relying on the traditional sea powers within the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) to defend the crown's interests.(wiki:Sweet_Cersei.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) According to Lord [Aurane Waters](wiki:Aurane_Waters), the new [grand admiral](wiki:Grand_admiral), Sweet Cersei is to be the equal of [King Robert's Hammer](wiki:King_Robert's_Hammer). Sweet Cersei is assigned the duty of taking Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Sweet_Cersei.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Aurane later returns to King's Landing on Sweet Cersei and reports on the capture of Dragonstone and Loras's injuries.(wiki:Sweet_Cersei.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
During her imprisonment by the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), Cersei is visited by [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn), who tells the queen that Aurane fled King's Landing with the new ships after her arrest.(wiki:Sweet_Cersei.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
