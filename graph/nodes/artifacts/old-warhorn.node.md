## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

While the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) is encamped at the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men) during the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), [Ghost](wiki:Ghost) finds a bundle of black wool buried in a mound of soft earth behind a fallen tree. The [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) informs [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow). A length of rope binds together the bundle, which is perhaps two feet across. It contains the horn and [dragonglass](wiki:Dragonglass) [weapons](wiki:Armament), including a dozen knives, leaf-shaped spearheads, and numerous arrowheads.(wiki:Old_warhorn.cite_ref-Racok34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Jon is unable to get any sound from the warhorn, so he gives it to [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), reasoning that Sam likes old things. He suggests to Sam that he make a drinking horn out of it to celebrate his part in mission [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall), Jon also gives Sam a spearhead and a dozen arrowheads, passing the rest out among his other friends for luck.(wiki:Old_warhorn.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Feast For Crows[ ]

While staying at an inn in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) with Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)), [Dareon](wiki:Dareon), and [Gilly](wiki:Gilly), Sam still has the horn.(wiki:Old_warhorn.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) On the [Cinnamon Wind](wiki:Cinnamon_Wind), Sam trades away almost all that they own in order to pay [Quhuru Mo](wiki:Quhuru_Mo) for their passage south from Braavos to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). By the time the dealing is done, Sam is only left with his boots, black clothing, smallclothes, and the broken horn Jon had found on the Fist of the First Men.(wiki:Old_warhorn.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
