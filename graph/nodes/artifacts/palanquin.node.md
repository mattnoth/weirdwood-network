## Origins

According to legend, the [God-on-Earth](wiki:God-on-Earth) traveled in a palanquin carved from a single pearl and carried by his hundred wives.(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Septon [Alfyn](wiki:Alfyn) was carried in a litter because of his missing legs while he toured the realm spreading the [Doctrine of Exceptionalism](wiki:Doctrine_of_Exceptionalism).(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Rtrotd62.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

[Rego Draz](wiki:Rego_Draz), a native of [Pentos](wiki:Pentos), was resented for flaunting his wealth by riding in a gilded palanquin in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) In [59 AC](wiki:59_AC), Rego was murdered after his palanquin was overwhelmed in [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom).(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Culture

The [Prince of Pentos](wiki:Prince_of_Pentos) rides about [the city](wiki:Pentos) in a palanquin of [ivory](wiki:Ivory) and gold. Three heralds go before him.(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Those who dwell in [Asshai](wiki:Asshai) often ride in palanquins of [ebony](wiki:Ebony) and iron, hidden behind dark curtains and carried upon the backs of [slaves](wiki:Slaves).(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Rtwoiafasshai-by-the-shadow.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

In [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) it is said that travelling afoot can taint visitors in the eyes of foreign captains and the Volantenes alike, therefore persons of quality travel by palanquin, or in the back of a *[hathay](wiki:Hathay)*.(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

The people of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) seldom ride within their city walls, preferring instead palanquins, litters, and sedan chairs borne upon the shoulders of their slaves. The Meereenese believe that [horses](wiki:Horse) befoul their streets, but slaves do not.(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Palanquins are uncommon in the poorer neighborhoods of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth).(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), her brother [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen), and Magister [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) travel in Illyrio's elaborately carved palanquin on the way to present Daenerys to her prospective husband, Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo). The streets of [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) are pitch-dark when they set out so servants go ahead to light the way, carrying ornate oil lanterns with panes of pale blue glass, while a dozen strong men hoist the poles on their shoulders.(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

While in [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak), a pregnant Daenerys opts to be carried in a litter to visit the Western Market. If Drogo were accompanying her she would have ridden [her silver](wiki:Her_silver) not wanting to appear weak in his eyes. With her husband off hunting, however, Daenerys finds it pleasant to lie back on soft cushions and be carried with red silk curtains to shield her from the sun.(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Clash of Kings

[Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos) gives Daenerys a palanquin of [ebony](wiki:Ebony) and gold with a matched set of bullocks to bear it.(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) After being refused by the [Pureborn](wiki:Pureborn) of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), Daenerys rides with [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal) and Xaro as [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) clears a path. The drapes keep out the dust and heat of the streets.(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Daenerys rides in her palanquin to visit the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying).(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

Daenerys chooses to ride [the silver](wiki:The_silver) for her visit to [port of Qarth](wiki:Port_of_Qarth). She rejects going by palanquin, feeling she had reclined too long on its satin cushions. (wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Feast for Crows

At the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot), [Erik Ironmaker](wiki:Erik_Ironmaker) is carried on a palanquin by three of his grandsons.(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Yurkhaz zo Yunzak](wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak) of [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) comes and goes in a palanquin so huge it requires forty slaves to carry it.(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) is smuggled out of [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) inside the litter of [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis), and they travel in it on their way to the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne). The palanquin(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-16) is suspended between eight draft horses on heavy leather straps. Four [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) pace beside the horses. Inside it is pleasantly warm, despite the [autumn](wiki:Autumn) chill.(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

A matron on an antique palanquin bids on Tyrion and [Penny](wiki:Penny) when they are up for sale on the slave auction block outside of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

[Reznak mo Reznak](wiki:Reznak_mo_Reznak) orders that Daenerys's palanquin be made ready for her wedding procession in Meereen, but she instead chooses a sedan chair.(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Daenerys and her new husband, [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq), ride on two thrones side by side in an ornate open palanquin, surrounded by [Brazen Beasts](wiki:Brazen_Beasts), on their way to the reopening of [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit).(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) After Daenerys disappears from Meereen atop [Drogon](wiki:Drogon), a woman complains to Reznak about damage to her palanquin.(wiki:Palanquin.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
