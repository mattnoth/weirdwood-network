## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ragged Jenna is part of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s fleet commanded by Ser [Imry Florent](wiki:Imry_Florent) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). During the battle it manages to break through and make it upriver along with the [Swift Sword](wiki:Swift_Sword) and [Lord Steffon](wiki:Lord_Steffon).(wiki:Ragged_Jenna.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ragged Jenna is among the warships that survived the Blackwater. [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan) tells Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) that Ragged Jenna and [Laughing Lord](wiki:Laughing_Lord) are playing [pirate](wiki:Pirate) on the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush).(wiki:Ragged_Jenna.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
