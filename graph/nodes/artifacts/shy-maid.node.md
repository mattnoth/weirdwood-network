## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The boat is used by [Griff](wiki:Griff) to transport his party down the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne) to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis). Much to Griff’s consternation [Yandry](wiki:Yandry) and [Ysilla](wiki:Ysilla) refuse to risk their pole boat in the dark and will not continue downstream at night. The [Upper Rhoyne](wiki:Upper_Rhoyne) is full of snags and sawyers, anyone of which could rip out the Shy Maid's hull.
[Yandry](wiki:Yandry) and [Ysilla](wiki:Ysilla) share one cabin, Griff and [Young Griff](wiki:Young_Griff) another. Septa [Lemore](wiki:Lemore) has a cabin to herself, as does [Haldon](wiki:Haldon), who has the largest. [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), going by the names Hugor Hill and Yollo, makes a bed atop the roof of one of the Shy Maid's cabins, with a coil of hempen rope for a pillow. He likes it better there than the boat's cramped hold. Ser [Rolly Duckfield](wiki:Rolly_Duckfield) possibly sleeps in the hold.(wiki:Shy_Maid.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
