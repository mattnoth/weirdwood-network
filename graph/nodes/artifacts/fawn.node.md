## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

On the first day of their stay in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell), Ser [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater), and Ser [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood) seek passage on the Fawn to sail to Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). The captain of the Fawn is the only captain to give reasons for refusing them passage. Speaking with the three [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) over watered wine, the captain explains he will be sailing on an already dangerous voyage past [Valyria](wiki:Valyria) to [New Ghis](wiki:New_Ghis), [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), and the [Jade Sea](wiki:Jade_Sea). It would be too much of a risk for him to take the Fawn to wartorn [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay).(wiki:Fawn.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
