## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) sails Black Betha north from [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown) to [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) to distribute [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s claim against the legitimacy of [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s children with King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Black_Betha.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) It returns to the fleet while it is [blockading Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)).(wiki:Black_Betha.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Black Betha is part of Stannis's fleet that sails into [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). It rams and sinks [Lady's Shame](wiki:Lady's_Shame) with the aid of [Lady Marya](wiki:Lady_Marya), which is commanded by Davos's son [Allard](wiki:Allard_Seaworth). Black Betha successfully boards [White Hart](wiki:White_Hart). After [Swordfish](wiki:Swordfish) rams the hulk filled with [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire), Black Betha is engulfed by flame and destroyed.(wiki:Black_Betha.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Black_Betha.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
