## Culture

*Arakhs* can be sharpened with whetsones(wiki:Arakh.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and rubbed down with red oil.(wiki:Arakh.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) There are a handful of *arakhs* made of [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel).(wiki:Arakh.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Blades like the *arakh* are forbidden in [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak).(wiki:Arakh.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

During her wedding celebration, [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) watches two [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) duel with *arakhs* over a woman.(wiki:Arakh.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) [Cohollo](wiki:Cohollo) gives Daenerys a magnificent *arakh* chased in gold.(wiki:Arakh.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

After a Dothraki warrior insults Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) for wearing [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) armor, the pair fight with longsword and *arakh*; the [knight](wiki:Knight) slays the Dothraki.(wiki:Arakh.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The *[khalasars](wiki:Khalasar)* of Khals [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) and [Ogo](wiki:Ogo) clash, and Drogo loses a nipple to an *arakh* slice by one of Ogo's [bloodriders](wiki:Bloodrider).(wiki:Arakh.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Daenerys asks [Mirri Maz Duur](wiki:Mirri_Maz_Duur) to save Drogo's life, but Drogo's bloodriders attempt to halt the *[maegi](wiki:Maegi)'s* ceremony. [Qotho](wiki:Qotho)'s *arakh* cuts through Jorah's chainmail to his hip but catches on the knight's bone, allowing Jorah to slay Qotho with his longsword.(wiki:Arakh.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

After Drogo dies, Daenerys includes his *arakh* among his possessions on a funeral pyre.(wiki:Arakh.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Daenerys gives to [Rakharo](wiki:Rakharo) the *arakh* she had received from Cohollo.(wiki:Arakh.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) Daenerys emerges from the pyre with three hatched [dragons](wiki:Dragon), and [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) lays his *arakh* before her in homage.(wiki:Arakh.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

### A Clash of Kings

[Strong Belwas](wiki:Strong_Belwas) carries a long curving *arakh*.(wiki:Arakh.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords

[Zollo](wiki:Zollo), one of the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions), uses his *arakh* to chop off the sword hand of Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Arakh.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) kills an *arakh*-wielding Dothraki during the [battle at the burning septry](wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry).(wiki:Arakh.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

[Daario Naharis](wiki:Daario_Naharis) wields an *arakh* and a [Myrish](wiki:Myr) stiletto, the hilts of which are golden naked women.(wiki:Arakh.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Belwas kills [Oznak zo Pahl](wiki:Oznak_zo_Pahl) with his *arakh* in single combat.(wiki:Arakh.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

During the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor), [Caggo](wiki:Caggo) strikes down the corpse of [Cleon the Great](wiki:Cleon_the_Great) with his black *arakh* of [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel).(wiki:Arakh.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), armored in plate and mail, defeats the *arakh*-wielding [Khrazz](wiki:Khrazz).(wiki:Arakh.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
