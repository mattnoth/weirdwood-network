## Identity

Eel pie is a Braavosi prepared dish — eel baked in a pastry crust. Distinct from raw or stewed eel; the pie form appears in Arya's Braavos storyline as charity food distributed by street-level figures like Pynto. It represents the working-class food culture of Braavos's canals.

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

- (The Blind Girl I, ADWD — `sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-the-blind-girl-01.md:175`) Pynto's charity to the blind girl: watered wine, a chunk of stinky cheese, and half an eel pie — a named Braavosi dish, confirming eel pie as a standard street food of the city.
