## Identity

Biscuits appear in two contrasting forms on Tyrion's ADWD river/sea journey: freshly cooked biscuits dripping with honey and butter from Ysilla's brazier on the Shy Maid (comfortable barge-life); and the famously inedible ship's biscuits on the Selaesori Qhoran that "could have been used to drive nails" (harsh sea-rations). The contrast marks the shift from river luxury to sea hardship.

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

- (Tyrion IV, ADWD — `sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-tyrion-04.md:65`) Ysilla's Shy Maid cooking: "Some days she cooked biscuits and bacon; some days bacon and biscuits. Once every fortnight there might be a fish." Tyrion steals a hot biscuit "dripping with honey and butter" off the brazier and darts away from Ysilla's wooden spoon.
- (Tyrion VIII, ADWD — `sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-tyrion-08.md:117`) Selaesori Qhoran shipboard supper: "biscuits 'that could have been used to drive nails'" served alongside buttered beets and cold fish stew — the quintessential sea-ration biscuit complaint.
