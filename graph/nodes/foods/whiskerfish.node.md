### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

A whiskerfish from the bottom of the [Greenblood](wiki:Greenblood) is served during the feast welcoming Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). It is so large it takes four servants to carry it to the table.(wiki:Whiskerfish.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

- (The Watcher I, ADWD — `sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-the-watcher-01.md:51`) The 7-course feast for Ser Balon Swann at Sunspear: the whiskerfish "was so big it took four serving men to carry it to table" — a spectacle course, arriving after the lamprey pies and before the savory snake stew. Its size signals Dornish abundance and the court's capacity to impress a distinguished guest.
