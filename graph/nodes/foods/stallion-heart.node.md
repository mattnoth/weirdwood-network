## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Since steel is not permitted within the sacred confounds of Vaes Dothrak, beneath the Shadow of the [Mother of Mountains](wiki:Mother_of_Mountains), Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) and his [bloodriders](wiki:Bloodrider) kill the wild stallion using stone knives.(wiki:Stallion_heart.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The heart is steaming as Drogo sets it in front of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen). The heart is tough and stringy. However, Daenerys had prepared herself for the ceremony by dining on bowls of half clotted blood to accustom herself to the taste, despite her tender mother's stomach, and starved herself for a day and a night before the ceremony, hoping that the hunger would help her keep down the raw meat.(wiki:Stallion_heart.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
The taste still threatens to gag her, and because the heart is all muscle, she has to worry it with her teeth a long time each mouthful. Although her stomach roils and heaves, she continues.(wiki:Stallion_heart.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
After she has finished the entire heart, Daenerys turns to the old women of the [dosh khaleen](wiki:Dosh_khaleen), and proclaims in her best Dothraki "Khalakka dothrae mr’anha!", meaning "A prince rides inside me!". The oldest of the crones, raises her arms on high and shrieks "Khalakka dothrae!", meaning "The prince is riding!" The other women answer "He is riding", proclaiming "Rakh! Rakh! Rakh haj!" ("A boy, a boy, a strong boy."). The oldest of the crones then delivers the prophecy that [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s son will be the [Stallion Who Mounts the World](wiki:Stallion_Who_Mounts_the_World).(wiki:Stallion_heart.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
Afterwards Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) leads her from the chalk pit and the procession rides to the [Womb of the World](wiki:Womb_of_the_World) where Daenerys cleanses herself in its waters.(wiki:Stallion_heart.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
