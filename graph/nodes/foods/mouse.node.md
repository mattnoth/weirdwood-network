## Origins

In some stories about [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister)'s acquisition of [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), [Lann the Clever](wiki:Lann_the_Clever) filled a cleft in the castle with mice, rats, and other vermin to chase out [House Casterly](wiki:House_Casterly).(wiki:Mouse.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
[Marilda of Hull](wiki:Marilda_of_Hull) was known as Mouse when she was young because she was "small, quick, and always underfoot." Later in life, Marilda acquired a trading cog which she named [Mouse](wiki:Mouse_(ship)).(wiki:Mouse.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom) referred to Marilda's palatial manse at [Hull](wiki:Hull) as the [Mouse House](wiki:Mouse_House).(wiki:Mouse.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) The tomb at Hull of Ser [Addam Velaryon](wiki:Addam_Velaryon), one of Marilda's sons, has carvings of a mouse and the [seahorse](wiki:Seahorse) of [House Velaryon](wiki:House_Velaryon).(wiki:Mouse.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
When they worked together in [Pentos](wiki:Pentos), [Varys](wiki:Varys) and [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) referred to Varys's [network of informants](wiki:Little_birds) as mice.(wiki:Mouse.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

One of the gifts which [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) receives at [her wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen) is a gown made from the skin of a thousand mice, which Magister [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) considers to be a handsome gift.(wiki:Mouse.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Serving incognito as a servant at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) thinks of herself as a mouse while trying not to bring attention to herself.(wiki:Mouse.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
[Aggo](wiki:Aggo) purchases a skewer of honey-roasted mice at the [port of Qarth](wiki:Port_of_Qarth).(wiki:Mouse.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) finds a mouse in the library of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Mouse.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) hears [soft sounds](wiki:Little_birds) in the walls of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) and tells herself that they are mice.(wiki:Mouse.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) informs his mother that [Ser Pounce](wiki:Ser_Pounce) caught a mouse but had it stolen away by [Lady Whiskers](wiki:Lady_Whiskers).(wiki:Mouse.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) [Dorcas](wiki:Dorcas) makes a toy mouse with scraps of fur and ties it on a long string at the end of an old fishing pole so that Tommen can play with his three kittens.(wiki:Mouse.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
