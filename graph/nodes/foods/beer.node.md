## Origins

The big-bellied Ser [Willam Stafford](wiki:Willam_Stafford) was called "the Keg o' Ale" by [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk).(wiki:Beer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)
Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) praised a strong [northern](wiki:North) ale when she visited [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) in [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). [Lothor Burley](wiki:Lothor_Burley) served stout and [mead](wiki:Mead) when the queen arrived at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Beer.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)
After the [fall of King's Landing](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing) to [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) amid the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord [Bartimos Celtigar](wiki:Bartimos_Celtigar) doubled taxes on wine and ale to raise funds for the queen.(wiki:Beer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0) [Tom Tangletongue](wiki:Tom_Tangletongue) gave ale to the crew of [Nessaria](wiki:Nessaria) while gathering information about [Sunfyre](wiki:Sunfyre).(wiki:Beer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0) During the [Moon of the Three Kings](wiki:Moon_of_the_Three_Kings), [Gaemon Palehair](wiki:Gaemon_Palehair) decreed that the poor should receive bread and beer during famine.(wiki:Beer.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_dorne:_queer_customs_of_the_south.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0) [Tom Tanglebeard](wiki:Tom_Tanglebeard) was drowned in a cask of ale when King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) was assassinated.(wiki:Beer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0) Lord [Torrhen Manderly](wiki:Torrhen_Manderly) preferred thick northern ale over the beer served at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Beer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0)
"[A Cask of Ale](wiki:A_Cask_of_Ale)" is a favorite song of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Beer.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

During the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney), King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) drinks strong black beer from a polished horn.(wiki:Beer.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0)
[Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), believes that drinking [lemon](wiki:Lemon) with his beer keeps his teeth strong.(wiki:Beer.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Chilled [autumn](wiki:Autumn) ale is served during the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Beer.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0)
[Shagga](wiki:Shagga) dislikes the weaker ale served in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Beer.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0)

- (Davos I, ACOK — `sources/chapters/acok/acok-davos-01.md:91`) Salladhor Saan eating grapes from a wooden bowl, urging Davos to eat with him; Davos orders ale instead of partaking in the grapes — the characterization contrast (Lyseni luxury vs. Westerosi austerity) expressed through their drink choices.
- (Arya II, ACOK — `sources/chapters/acok/acok-arya-02.md:33`) Inn at the crossroads: the innkeeper gives Yoren's Watch column a round of beer "on the house" for a brother who took the black (loved pepper) — an instance of the old guest-custom for black brothers, presented as increasingly rare under war conditions.

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Sybell Spicer](wiki:Sybell_Spicer) makes a posset of herbs, milk, and ale for her daughter, [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling).(wiki:Beer.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0)
[Hobb](wiki:Hobb) cooks mutton in a broth of ale and onions at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Beer.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) supplies black stout and yellow beer for the wedding of [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) to "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)" at Winterfell.(wiki:Beer.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

- (The Prince of Winterfell I, ADWD — `sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-the-prince-of-winterfell-01.md:129`) Ramsay/Arya wedding feast: "stout" and "beer" alongside Arbor wines — the contrast between Manderly's stout/beer and the Arbor wines maps class/origin distinctions at the feast table.
- (Jon XIII, ADWD — `sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-jon-13.md:195`) Tormund arrives at Castle Black roaring for "a horn of ale and something hot to eat" — the simplest possible demand for hospitality, from the Mead-King of Ruddy Hall.
During the [siege of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin), the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) garrison consume ale because water in the nearby [Neck](wiki:Neck) is unsafe to drink.(wiki:Beer.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-0)
