## Culture

Lemon cakes are a favorite of [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark).(wiki:Lemon_cake.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)(wiki:Lemon_cake.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Lemon_cake.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn) also loves lemon cakes, after [Alayne Stone](wiki:Alayne_Stone) told him they were her favorites.(wiki:Lemon_cake.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Lemon_cake.cite_ref-twow_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lemon cakes are served in Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s [wheelhouse](wiki:Wheelhouse),(wiki:Lemon_cake.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and at the feast for the [Hand's Tourney](wiki:Hand's_Tourney).(wiki:Lemon_cake.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lemon cakes in the shape of roses are served at King [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)'s feast for the [Melee at Bitterbridge](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge).(wiki:Lemon_cake.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark)'s love for lemon cakes is so well known that [Varys](wiki:Varys) informs the [Tyrell](wiki:Tyrell) party, who prepare the delicacy for her first meeting with [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) and her family.(wiki:Lemon_cake.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

At the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon), when [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) tells Sansa Stark he has brought her a gift, something better than gowns or jewels, she asks him if he has brought lemons, as she has promised young Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn) lemon cake.(wiki:Lemon_cake.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

In the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White), the [kindly man](wiki:Kindly_man) gives [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) a drink that is so tart, she compares it to biting into a lemon, which reminds her of [a girl](wiki:Sansa_Stark) she once knew who loved lemon cakes.(wiki:Lemon_cake.cite_ref-Radwd64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

[Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) acquires every [lemon](wiki:Lemon) in the [Vale](wiki:Vale) for a twelve-foot-tall lemon cake in the shape of the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance) for the feast before the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) to select the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights), to please Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn) and [Alayne Stone](wiki:Alayne_Stone).(wiki:Lemon_cake.cite_ref-twow_5-1)
