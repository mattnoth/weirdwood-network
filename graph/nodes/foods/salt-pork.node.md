## Identity

Salt pork is cured/preserved pork used as traveling and shipboard rations. It appears in contrast to rich noble fare — Tyrion explicitly finds the plain fare on the Shy Maid "a pleasant change from all the rich food" at Illyrio's manse.

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

- (Tyrion III, ADWD — `sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-tyrion-03.md:147`) First supper on the Shy Maid: "salt pork and cold white beans washed down with ale" — Tyrion notes it "a pleasant change from all the rich food" of Illyrio's manse; the contrast of humble river-barge fare vs. the Pentoshi feast.
