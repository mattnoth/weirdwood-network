## Identity

Black sausage is a smoked/cured sausage used as a march ration and a Night's Watch staple. In ADWD it appears as one of the most basic rations distributed to common soldiers on Stannis's Winterfell march — a finger's-length chunk washed down with the last of Galbart Glover's ale, contrasting with the king's tent venison stew. The Castle Black stores census also lists "three thousand long black sausages" hanging from ceiling beams — a key winter provision.

### A Feast for Crows

- (Jaime IV, AFFC — `sources/chapters/affc/affc-jaime-04.md:213`) Sparrows at Darry gather round a dozen cookfires to "watch fat sausages spit and sizzle above the flames" — the smallfolk pilgrims' humble meal in the castle yard while Jaime dines at the high table inside; Jaime reflects these "useless mouths" will be eating rats by winter.

### A Dance with Dragons

- (Jon IV, ADWD — `sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-jon-04.md:23`) Castle Black stores census: "Three hundred hams and three thousand long black sausages hung from ceiling beams below the smokehouse" — the scale of winter provisions at the Wall.
