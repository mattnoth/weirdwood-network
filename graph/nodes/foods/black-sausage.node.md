## Identity

Black sausage is a smoked/cured sausage used as a march ration and a Night's Watch staple. In ADWD it appears as one of the most basic rations distributed to common soldiers on Stannis's Winterfell march — a finger's-length chunk washed down with the last of Galbart Glover's ale, contrasting with the king's tent venison stew. The Castle Black stores census also lists "three thousand long black sausages" hanging from ceiling beams — a key winter provision.
