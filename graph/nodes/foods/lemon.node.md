## Culture

[Dorne](wiki:Dorne) has goods which are uncommon in the rest of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), such as lemons.(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Some Dornish lemon orchards are watered by a network of old canals.(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) There are lemon trees in the lower terrace of the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Lemons are used in cuisine, including lemon cream,(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) lemon pies,(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and [lemon cakes](wiki:Lemon_cake).(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Ragot44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Lemonsweet is a popular beverage available.(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) and [Gerold Dayne](wiki:Gerold_Dayne) drink lemon water, with the latter taking his unsweetened.(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) adds lemon to his morning [ale](wiki:Ale).(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Racok34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) [Galazza Galare](wiki:Galazza_Galare) enjoys lemon juice sweetened with [honey](wiki:Honey).(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
Lemons can be used as fragrances and in perfumes.(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Rasos12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Some individuals bathe in lemon water,(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) which can also be used to freshen breath.(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Raffc3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
The arms of [House Dalt](wiki:House_Dalt) of [Lemonwood](wiki:Lemonwood) are a purple field strewn with lemons.(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-23)

## Origins

The title [High King of Dorne](wiki:High_King_of_Dorne) was claimed by some [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) kings who ruled from a high hall set among lemon trees along the [Greenblood](wiki:Greenblood).(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_kingdoms_of_the_first_men.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)
[Duck](wiki:Duck) cooked with cherries and lemons was served during the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls).(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
A Dornish girl once cooked duck with lemons for [Anguy](wiki:Anguy).(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) thinks longingly of the [house with the red door](wiki:House_with_the_red_door) in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), where she had a lemon tree outside her bedroom window.(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) The interior of [Drogo's manse](wiki:Drogo's_manse) in [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) is scented with lemon and [spices](wiki:Spices).(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-2)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark)'s love for [lemon cakes](wiki:Lemon_cake) is so well known that [Varys](wiki:Varys) informs the [Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) party, who prepare the delicacy for her.(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)
At the [Inn of the Kneeling Man](wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man), [Anguy](wiki:Anguy) asks [Sharna](wiki:Sharna) if they can eat duck with lemons. Sharna is annoyed, as lemons can be grown in Dorne instead of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

The air of the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens) is sharp with the smell of lemons.(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Arriving in [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear), Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell)'s retinue is accosted by townspeople calling for vengeance for his slain brother, Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell). The townsfolk pelt lemons, [limes](wiki:Lime), and [oranges](wiki:Orange) at Doran's litter, and one of his guards is hit in the eye with a lemon.(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
Ser [Gerold Dayne](wiki:Gerold_Dayne) drinks unsweetened lemonwater during Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell)'s rendezvous at [Shandystone](wiki:Shandystone).(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) One of the midday meals served to Arianne when she is imprisoned atop the [Spear Tower](wiki:Spear_Tower) is [kid](wiki:Goat) roasted with lemon and honey.(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)
At the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon), when Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) tells [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) he has brought her a gift, she asks him if he has brought lemons, as she has promised [lemon cake](wiki:Lemon_cake) to young Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn).(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Aboard the [Shy Maid](wiki:Shy_Maid), traveling along the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne), [Ysilla](wiki:Ysilla) cooks a [pike](wiki:Pike) on a brazier, squeezing a lemon over it as it sizzles.(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)
At the feast held for Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) in [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear), among the seven courses served is a soup made of eggs and lemons.(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
In the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White), the [kindly man](wiki:Kindly_man) gives [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) a drink that is so tart, she compares it to biting into a lemon.(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-Radwd64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

Petyr acquires every lemon in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) for a twelve-foot-tall lemon cake in the shape of the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance) for the feast before a [tourney](wiki:Tourney) to select the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights). He promises Sansa he will send away to Dorne for more.(wiki:Lemon.cite_ref-twow_31-0)
