## Culture

Apples can be eaten by themselves or be prepared in cuisine, including baked apples,(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) dried apples,(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) apple crisps,(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) applecakes,(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) apple tarts,(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) stewed apples,(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) applesauce,(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) buns,(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) oatbread,(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and in salads.(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Racok54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Racok60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Apples are sometimes included in [bowls of brown](wiki:Bowls_of_brown).(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Cider is a popular [alcoholic](wiki:Alcohol) beverage made from fermented apples.(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) [Wine](wiki:Wine) can be made from green apples.(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
Apples are included in the sigils of [House Fossoway of Cider Hall](wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall) and [House Fossoway of New Barrel](wiki:House_Fossoway_of_New_Barrel).(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Ser [Derrick Fossoway](wiki:Derrick_Fossoway), one of the [Ninepenny Kings](wiki:Ninepenny_Kings), was known as the Bad Apple.(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

## Origins

[Foss the Archer](wiki:Foss_the_Archer), a legendary son of [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand), is remembered for shooting apples off the heads of [maids](wiki:Maid) he fancied.(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) [Rowan Gold-Tree](wiki:Rowan_Gold-Tree), one of Garth's legendary daughters, is said to grown a tree from an apple wrapped in her golden hair.(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1)
According to Septon [Barth](wiki:Barth), Lady [Florence Fossoway](wiki:Florence_Fossoway)'s bookkeeping skills came from "counting apples since she learned to walk".(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
Queen [Daenaera Velaryon](wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon) was the target of an assassination attempt when the crusts of her apple tarts were [poisoned](wiki:Poison).(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
Ser [Arlan of Pennytree](wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree) often gave apples to [Chestnut](wiki:Chestnut). When Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) sold [Sweetfoot](wiki:Sweetfoot) to [Henly](wiki:Henly_(Ashford)), he gave a few [[currency|coppers] back so that the horse could have some oats and an apple.(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) forbade his son [Samwell](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) from eating apples while at the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor) after Lord [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne) compared the youth to a [pig](wiki:Pig) with an apple in its mouth.(wiki:Apple.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
