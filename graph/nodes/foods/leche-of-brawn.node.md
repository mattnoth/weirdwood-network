## Identity

Leche of brawn is a molded, sliced dish of pressed pork (brawn), spiced with cinnamon, cloves, sugar, and almond milk — a rich, sweet-savory feast dish. It appears twice in the ASOIAF feast inventory: the Red Wedding opens with "a leche of stringy beef" (the poor-quality Frey version); the Purple Wedding serves the superior brawn variant. The name "leche" refers to the sliced/molded presentation form.
