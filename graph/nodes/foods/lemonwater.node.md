## Identity

Lemonwater (and its sweetened variant, lemonsweet) is a Dornish beverage — tart, citrus-based, and refreshing in the desert heat. Darkstar's preference for "unsweetened lemonwater" at the Shandystone fire while others share summerwine is a character marker: austere, contrary, refusing even the social comfort of a shared skin. The sweetened form (lemonsweet) appears as a travel provision for Myrcella in the desert.
