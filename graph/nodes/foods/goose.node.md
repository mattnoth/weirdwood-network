[Dunk](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) and [Egg](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) had goose eggs for breakfast on the first day of the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow).(wiki:Goose.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

During the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney), [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) sees a serving man carrying a goose which honks and and pecks at the man's fingers.(wiki:Goose.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Boiled goose eggs are served at King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s breakfast.(wiki:Goose.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Yoren](wiki:Yoren) sees a goose running loose in an abandoned [Gods Eye town](wiki:Gods_Eye_town), so [Koss](wiki:Koss) retrieves the bird.(wiki:Goose.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
Lady [Tanda Stokeworth](wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth) invites [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to a dinner which includes a brace of stuffed geese sauced with mulberries.(wiki:Goose.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
Goose-in-berries is served during the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast_(299)) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Goose.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Anguy](wiki:Anguy) brings down a goose for the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) to eat.(wiki:Goose.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) [Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams) sings a silly song about [Big Belly Ben](wiki:Big_Belly_Ben) and the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon)'s goose.(wiki:Goose.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

Tyrion is served goose livers drowned in [wine](wiki:Wine) at [Illyrio's manse](wiki:Illyrio's_manse) in [Pentos](wiki:Pentos).(wiki:Goose.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)
