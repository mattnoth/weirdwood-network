## Identity

Nahsa is fermented goat's milk "served in an iron cup and laced with honey," a traditional Norvoshi drink. For Areo Hotah it is inseparable from the taste of wintercake and the memory of his one annual festival day in Norvos. The iron cup and honey-lacing are distinctive; nahsa's sourness contrasts with its sweetening.

### A Feast for Crows

- (The Captain of Guards I, AFFC — `sources/chapters/affc/affc-the-captain-of-guards-01.md:157`) Hotah's Norvoshi memory while sharpening his axe: "The taste of wintercake filled his mouth again, rich with ginger and pine nuts and bits of cherry, with nahsa to wash it down, fermented goat's milk served in an iron cup and laced with honey." The pair (wintercake + nahsa) constitutes the taste of home for the transplanted captain.
