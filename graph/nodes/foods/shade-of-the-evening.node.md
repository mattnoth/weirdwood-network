## Appearances & Description

Shade of the evening is deep blue and viscous, flowing like honey.(wiki:Shade_of_the_evening.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) It has an unappetizing smell and initial taste, having been compared to ink, spoiled meat, and rotten flesh,(wiki:Shade_of_the_evening.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Shade_of_the_evening.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) but upon swallowing tastes like all things its imbiber has ever tasted, and more.(wiki:Shade_of_the_evening.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
The liquid is used heavily by the [warlocks](wiki:Warlocks) of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), whose lips have been turned blue by its extended use.(wiki:Shade_of_the_evening.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Shade_of_the_evening.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The [Undying Ones](wiki:Undying_Ones) have drank so much of it not only their lips have turned blue, but their entire bodies as well.(wiki:Shade_of_the_evening.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos) tells [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) that the [warlocks](wiki:Warlocks) of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) drink shade-of-the-evening until their lips turn blue.(wiki:Shade_of_the_evening.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
[Pyat Pree](wiki:Pyat_Pree) has a [dwarf servitor](wiki:House_of_the_Undying_servitor) give Daenerys a glass of shade to prepare her for her entrance into the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying).(wiki:Shade_of_the_evening.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
While strolling the [port of Qarth](wiki:Port_of_Qarth), Daenerys sees for sale a potent [magical](wiki:Magic) elixir made of virgin's milk and shade of the evening.(wiki:Shade_of_the_evening.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), the new [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North), gives his brother [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) a cup of shade of the evening, claiming to have taken possession of a cask from a captured galleas out of Qarth which was carrying warlocks. Victarion tries a small amount and promptly spits it out, referring to it as foul.(wiki:Shade_of_the_evening.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

- (The Reaver I, AFFC — `sources/chapters/affc/affc-the-reaver-01.md:255`) Euron at the Shields victory feast: "The Crow's Eye filled two cups with a strange black wine that flowed as thick as honey." Victarion takes the cup Euron had not offered, sniffs it: "Seen up close, it looked more blue than black. It was thick and oily, with a smell like rotted flesh. He tried a small swallow, and spit it out at once. 'Foul stuff. Do you mean to poison me?'" Euron drinks deep and smiles — "Shade-of-the-evening, the wine of the warlocks." Euron claims he captured a cask from a Qartheen galleas along with four warlocks, one of whom he killed and fed to the other three.

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

Euron forces his captive brother [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) to drink the liquid. Aeron has several nightmarish [visions](wiki:Dreams) afterwards.(wiki:Shade_of_the_evening.cite_ref-Forsaken_4-1)
