## Identity

Clam broth (made with seaweed, cooked above a driftwood fire) is Aeron Damphair's characteristic austere breakfast — the ironborn drowned-priest's equivalent of a monk's fast-breaking gruel. It encodes his ascetic devotion to the Drowned God: shore-gathered, sea-flavored, consumed before dawn.

### A Feast for Crows

- (The Prophet I, AFFC — `sources/chapters/affc/affc-the-prophet-01.md:185`) Aeron Greyjoy on the shore of Old Wyk: "When he woke the day was bright and windy. Aeron broke his fast on a broth of clams and seaweed cooked above a driftwood fire. No sooner had he finished than the Merlyn descended from his towerhouse." The meal precedes his calling of the kingsmoot.
