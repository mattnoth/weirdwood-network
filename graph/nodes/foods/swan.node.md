## Culture

Swans are known for their beauty and serenity.(wiki:Swan.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)(wiki:Swan.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Battling swans are found in the arms of [House Swann](wiki:House_Swann) of [Stonehelm](wiki:Stonehelm).(wiki:Swan.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) has wide white wings ornamenting his greathelm, and black and white swans fight on his shield.(wiki:Swan.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Balon also clasps his cloak with a [silver](wiki:Silver) brooch depicting two fighting swans, one [ivory](wiki:Ivory) and the other [onyx](wiki:Onyx).(wiki:Swan.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
[Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) refer to the ships of the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles) as [swan ships](wiki:Swan_ship) because of their billowing white sails, their figureheads, and their tall, curved prows.(wiki:Swan.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_summer_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Swan.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Origins

Legends from [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) claim that seven [swan maidens](wiki:Swan_maidens) once preyed on travelers in the [Velvet Hills](wiki:Velvet_Hills).(wiki:Swan.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_arrival_of_the_andals.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Lady [Johanna Swann](wiki:Johanna_Swann), known as the Black Swan, was a [courtesan](wiki:Courtesan) who rose to become the ruler of [Lys](wiki:Lys) in all but name.(wiki:Swan.cite_ref-Rfabhers_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) sees black swans swimming in the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye).(wiki:Swan.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
[Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) have roast swan stuffed with [mushrooms](wiki:Mushroom) and [oysters](wiki:Oyster) during a meal at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Swan.cite_ref-Racok54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Anguy](wiki:Anguy) reveals that he spent much of his winnings from the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) on the prostitutes [Dancy](wiki:Dancy), [Jayde](wiki:Jayde), and [Alayaya](wiki:Alayaya), enjoying roast swan and [wine](wiki:Wine) from the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor) with the girls.(wiki:Swan.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Slivers of swan poached in a sauce of saffron and peaches are served at [the wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Swan.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Tyrion refuses black swan when it is served to him at [Illyrio's manse](wiki:Illyrio's_manse) in [Pentos](wiki:Pentos).(wiki:Swan.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

- (Tyrion I, ADWD — `sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-tyrion-01.md:213`) Illyrio's second-course feast: "a black swan in her plumage" served as the centerpiece — Tyrion declines it; "The swan was too rich for his taste."

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

Swans in almond milk are served at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon).(wiki:Swan.cite_ref-12)
