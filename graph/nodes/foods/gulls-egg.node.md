## Identity

Gull's eggs are a Dornish breakfast ingredient, diced and cooked with bits of ham and fiery peppers for Prince Doran Martell's morning meal. They represent coastal-Dornish provisioning — seabirds' eggs as a practical protein source in the sun-baked south.
