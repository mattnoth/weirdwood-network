## Origins

Sea snails found only near [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) were used to make a deep dark reddish dye popular in [Valyria](wiki:Valyria), bringing thousands of merchants to the young city. By varying the diet of the snails, dyers from Tyrosh were also able to create scarlet, crimson, and deep indigo dyes.(wiki:Snail.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_the_quarrelsome_daughters:_myr.2C_lys.2C_and_tyrosh.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
A sea snail akin to those of Tyrosh was discovered near [Braavos](wiki:Braavos). Its dark purple dye was used by Braavosi to change the color of their sails and disguise their stolen ships.(wiki:Snail.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_braavos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
