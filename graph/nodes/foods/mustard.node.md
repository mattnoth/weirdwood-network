## Identity

Mustard appears in two forms: prepared mustard served as a condiment (the "big stone jar from Oldtown" at Ten Towers), and mustard seeds as an ingredient in Dornish snake sauce. Both uses mark it as a flavoring agent rather than a primary food, but it appears specifically enough to deserve a node.
