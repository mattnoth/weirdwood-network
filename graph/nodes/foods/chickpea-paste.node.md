## Identity

Chickpea paste is a Dornish condiment/dip appearing alongside olives, flatbread, and cheese at Doran Martell's private evening meal at the Water Gardens. It represents the simple, unpretentious side of Dornish cuisine — the prince's own table, in private, without the elaboration of a feast.
