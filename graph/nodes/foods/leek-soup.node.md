## Identity

Thin leek soup — the deliberately meager first course served at the Red Wedding feast at the Twins. Lord Walder Frey's niggardly table is pointedly contrasted with the solemnity of the occasion; this soup sets the tone for the insult-laden feast that precedes the massacre.
