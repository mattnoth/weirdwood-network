## Culture

Frogs are included in the arms of [House Marsh](wiki:House_Marsh), and a toad appears in the arms of [House Vypren](wiki:House_Vypren).
Frogs are hunted by [crannogmen](wiki:Crannogmen) in the [Neck](wiki:Neck),(wiki:Frog.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) and [Meera Reed](wiki:Meera_Reed) wields a slender three-pronged frog spear.(wiki:Frog.cite_ref-Racok28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) claim that eating frogs causes crannogmen to have green teeth and moss growing under their arms.(wiki:Frog.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Frog.cite_ref-Rasos9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Some [rivermen](wiki:Rivermen) believe that crannogmen have webbed hands and feet like frogs.(wiki:Frog.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_crannogmen_of_the_neck.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
The [Toad Stone](wiki:Toad_Stone), an ancient idol carved from greasy [black stone](wiki:Black_stone) in the shape of a a gigantic toad, can be found on the the [Isle of Toads](wiki:Isle_of_Toads).(wiki:Frog.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_basilisk_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Origins

[Meria Martell](wiki:Meria_Martell), [Princess of Dorne](wiki:Princess_of_Dorne), was called the "the Yellow Toad of Dorne" by [Argilac Durrandon](wiki:Argilac_Durrandon), the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King).(wiki:Frog.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[Lord Toad](wiki:Lord_Toad) was a [fool](wiki:Fool) at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) during the rule of Lord [Gerold Lannister](wiki:Gerold_Lannister).(wiki:Frog.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
[Maggy the Frog](wiki:Maggy_the_Frog) was a reputed sorceress at [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport).(wiki:Frog.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne) gives the nickname "Toad" to [Todder](wiki:Todder).(wiki:Frog.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed) and [Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed) bring gifts of fish, frog, and fowl to [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) for the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast_(299)) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Frog.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Meera hunts frogs while Bran and his companions travel through the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) north of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), but Bran grows tired of eating them.(wiki:Frog.cite_ref-Rasos9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), disguised as Cat of the Canals, overhears sailors in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) speaking about rains of toads.(wiki:Frog.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) takes the name Frog while disguised with the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown).(wiki:Frog.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
As part of the plot devised by [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) and Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) to overthrow King [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq), some of the [Brazen Beasts](wiki:Brazen_Beasts) who are part of the plan put on masks in the likeness of toads.(wiki:Frog.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
