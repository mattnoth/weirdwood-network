## Culture

Aurochs once dwelled in the majority of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). Because of hunting and domestication, however, they have dwindled in the wild and are not often seen.(wiki:Aurochs.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Aurochs.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
One place reportedly still inhabited by wild aurochs is near the juncture of the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad) and the [Green Fork](wiki:Green_Fork) of the [Trident](wiki:Trident) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Aurochs.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) They still dwell [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall), where the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) employ them to drag sledges.(wiki:Aurochs.cite_ref-Rasos0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Aurochs are frequently used as a symbol of size, strength, stubbornness, and stupidity.(wiki:Aurochs.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Origins

An aurochs is said to have been used as a mount by the mythical [Clarence Crabb](wiki:Clarence_Crabb), as horses could not bear his weight.(wiki:Aurochs.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Aurochs were seen in [Wat's Wood](wiki:Wat's_Wood) before [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Aurochs.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Lord [Theomore Manderly](wiki:Theomore_Manderly) served an aurochs when Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) visited [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) in [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Aurochs.cite_ref-Rfab10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
After [Daeron the Daring](wiki:Daeron_the_Daring) helped turn the tide of the [Battle of the Honeywine](wiki:Battle_of_the_Honeywine) in favor of the [greens](wiki:Greens), Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower) is recorded to have feasted the prince on aurochs and [strongwine](wiki:Strongwine).(wiki:Aurochs.cite_ref-Rfab15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Grenn](wiki:Grenn), a member of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), is given the nickname "Aurochs" by Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne).(wiki:Aurochs.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
[Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) recounts how two [warlocks](wiki:Warlocks) from [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) slaughtered a bull aurochs and made him bathe in the [animal](wiki:Animal)'s hot blood in an attempt to make him brave.(wiki:Aurochs.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Six aurochs are roasted whole for a feast during the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney).(wiki:Aurochs.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

During the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), one of the dishes is joints of aurochs roasted with leeks. [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) has a slice of it served to [Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed) and [Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed).(wiki:Aurochs.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Aurochs bones are part of the armor worn by [Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt) and his mount.(wiki:Aurochs.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Aurochs are employed in the rearguard of [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder)'s host of [free folk](wiki:Free_folk).(wiki:Aurochs.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

During the feast prior to the tourney for a place among the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights), three huge aurochs are roasted whole.(wiki:Aurochs.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Balayne_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Calayne_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
