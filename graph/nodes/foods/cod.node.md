## Origins

When Lord [Franklyn Farman](wiki:Franklyn_Farman) attempted to prevent his sister [Elissa](wiki:Elissa_Farman) from sailing away from [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle), onlookers threw him into a ship's hold filled with fresh-caught cod.(wiki:Cod.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
[Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) sailed the [Cod Queen](wiki:Cod_Queen) from [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark) to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) when he was sixteen.(wiki:Cod.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord [Larys Strong](wiki:Larys_Strong) helped King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) escape the [fall of King's Landing](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing). Ser [Marston Waters](wiki:Marston_Waters) smuggled the king to Dragonstone on a fishing skiff loaded with codfish.(wiki:Cod.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Salted cod is served at the feast hosted by [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) during the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Cod.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) sees cod wives selling the fish at the fishmarket of [Braavos](wiki:Braavos). When [Quill](wiki:Quill) offers to teach her how a woman kisses, [Tagganaro](wiki:Tagganaro) smacks him with a codfish.(wiki:Cod.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) sees cod sold at [Fishermonger's Square](wiki:Fishermonger's_Square) in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis).(wiki:Cod.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
Lord Manderly brings a monstrous codfish for the [wedding of Ramsay Bolton and Arya Stark](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark).(wiki:Cod.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) Cod cakes are served during the wedding feast in the [Great Hall](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell) of Winterfell.(wiki:Cod.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

- (The Prince of Winterfell I, ADWD — `sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-the-prince-of-winterfell-01.md:129`) Ramsay Bolton / "Arya Stark" wedding feast: "cod cakes" as one of the feast dishes alongside winter squash, hills of neeps, wheels of cheese, mutton, beef ribs, and three wagon-wheel wedding pies.
- (The Prince of Winterfell I, ADWD — `sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-the-prince-of-winterfell-01.md:137`) Theon Greyjoy at the wedding feast: "You do not eat." "No." Ramsay had left him with so many broken teeth that chewing was an agony; he can only drink, grasping the cup with both hands. The feast is designed to be eaten and he cannot eat — a subtle extension of Reek's dehumanization, expressed through the inability to participate in the feast's hospitality.
- (The Prince of Winterfell I, ADWD — `sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-the-prince-of-winterfell-01.md:130`) Wyman Manderly devours six portions of the wedding pies — two from each of the three pies — "smacking his lips and slapping his belly," then calls for "a song about the Rat Cook." The pies (q686 above) contain what the reader infers are Freys; Wyman's gluttony is a performance of innocence over the act of revenge. The cod cakes at this feast were furnished by Manderly's fleet — the same man now loudly enjoying what he brought.
- (Cat of the Canals I, AFFC — `sources/chapters/affc/affc-cat-of-the-canals-01.md:181`) Umma's kitchen: after the lemonwater de-personing bath, Umma has saved "fried cod" and mashed yellow turnips for Arya — comfort food as domestic care in the Faceless Men's kitchen.
- (Brienne V, AFFC — `sources/chapters/affc/affc-brienne-05.md:175`) Septon Meribald's donkey relief-food inventory includes "salt cod" among seeds/nuts/dried fruit, oaten porridge, flour, barley bread, three wheels of yellow cheese, salt mutton, onions, carrots, turnips, beans, barley, and oranges.
The great cog [Noble Lady](wiki:Noble_Lady), carrying salt cod, [whale](wiki:Whale) oil, and pickled [herring](wiki:Herring), is captured by [Iron Victory](wiki:Iron_Victory) in the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones).(wiki:Cod.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)
