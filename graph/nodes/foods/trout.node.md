## Culture

A leaping silver trout is included in the arms of [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully) of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), the [Lords Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lords_Paramount_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Trout.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) The pillars of Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully)'s bed are carved in the shapes of leaping trout.(wiki:Trout.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) A trout scaled in [silver](wiki:Silver) and [bronze](wiki:Bronze) crowns the crest of Hoster's greathelm,(wiki:Trout.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) and a silver trout ornaments the crest of the greathelm of his son, Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully).(wiki:Trout.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully), known as the Blackfish, has a black trout on his own helm.(wiki:Trout.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Ser [Robin Ryger](wiki:Robin_Ryger), the captain of [Riverrun's guards](wiki:House_Tully_guards), clasps his cloak with a silver trout.(wiki:Trout.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
A statue of a leaping trout spouts water into a shallow pool in the market square of [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept).(wiki:Trout.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
