## Identity

Onion soup appears in ADWD as the dish cooling before Stannis during the Winterfell march — "a dish of onion soup cooled before him, hardly tasted, staring at the flame of the nearest candle" while his men urged sacrifice. It encodes Stannis's ascetic obstinacy: even when food is placed before him, he does not eat. The soup's gradual cooling mirrors the king's psychological withdrawal.
