## Culture

[White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) has an inn famous for its lamprey pies.(wiki:Lamprey.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) According to [Mors Umber](wiki:Mors_Umber), Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) is mocked by some of his own people as Lord Lamprey.(wiki:Lamprey.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Origins

According to Septon [Eustace](wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)), Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) grew stout from eating sweetmeats, cakes, and lamprey pie while ruling [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Lamprey.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[Rhaegel Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegel_Targaryen) choked to death on lamprey pie.(wiki:Lamprey.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lady [Tanda Stokeworth](wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth) serves lamprey pie and roast suckling pig to Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish).(wiki:Lamprey.cite_ref-Ragot33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Tanda repeatedly offers lamprey pie to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Lamprey.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) A begging brother in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) preaches against Tyrion, claiming the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) has grown fat on [lark](wiki:Lark_(bird)) and lamprey while the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) starve.(wiki:Lamprey.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Lamprey pie is one of the courses in a supper enjoyed by Tyrion and his sister, Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister).(wiki:Lamprey.cite_ref-Racok54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Lampreys are included in the seafood brought by Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) for the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast).(wiki:Lamprey.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After he is rescued from [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay), Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth)'s stomach cannot tolerate the rich lampreys and [snails](wiki:Snail) provided by [Khorane Sathmantes](wiki:Khorane_Sathmantes).(wiki:Lamprey.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) While imprisoned in [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone)'s dungeon, Davos is served half a lamprey pie by one of his unnamed gaolers, whom he names [Lamprey](wiki:Lamprey_(gaoler)).(wiki:Lamprey.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Disguised in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) helps [Umma](wiki:Umma) prepare lampreys taken from the city's lagoon. Some nights Umma cooks the eels with chopped garlic.(wiki:Lamprey.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lamprey pie is served at the feast welcoming Ser [Wylis Manderly](wiki:Wylis_Manderly) back to [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor).(wiki:Lamprey.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Lord Wyman brings barrels of fresh-caught lampreys with him to [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) in preparation for the [wedding of Ramsay Bolton and Arya Stark](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark).(wiki:Lamprey.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
Lamprey pies are served at the feast welcoming Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear).(wiki:Lamprey.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
