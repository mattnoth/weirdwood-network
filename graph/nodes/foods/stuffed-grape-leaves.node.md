## Identity

Stuffed grape leaves are a specific Dornish dish: grape leaves filled with a mélange of raisins, onions, mushrooms, and fiery dragon peppers, served as part of Arianne Martell's prison meal. The dish exemplifies the Dornish fusion of sweet (raisins), savory (onions, mushrooms), and fiery (dragon peppers) — a complexity that contrasts with Westerosi cooking.
