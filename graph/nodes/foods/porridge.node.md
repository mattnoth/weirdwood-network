## Identity

Porridge is a plain grain-based breakfast staple, typically served with fruit or honey. In ASOS it marks Tyrion's diminished, imprisoned circumstances during his trial — reduced from feast food to the simplest morning fare, and eventually flung at the wall in helpless rage.
