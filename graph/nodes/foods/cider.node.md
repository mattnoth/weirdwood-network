## Culture

[Cider Hall](wiki:Cider_Hall) is the seat of the [elder branch](wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall) of House Fossoway,(wiki:Cider.cite_ref-Rawoiafcider_hall.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) while the [younger branch](wiki:House_Fossoway_of_New_Barrel) resides at [New Barrel](wiki:New_Barrel).(wiki:Cider.cite_ref-4)
The [Quill and Tankard](wiki:Quill_and_Tankard) in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) is renowned for its fearsomely strong cider.(wiki:Cider.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Cider.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The [septry](wiki:Septry) on the [Quiet Isle](wiki:Quiet_Isle) is also far famed for its sweet cider.(wiki:Cider.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Origins

A dozen [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) villages paid homage to [Varamyr Sixskins](wiki:Varamyr_Sixskins) with bread, salt, and cider.(wiki:Cider.cite_ref-Radwdprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
