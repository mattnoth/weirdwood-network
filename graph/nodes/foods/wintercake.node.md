## Identity

Wintercake is a rich Norvoshi festive cake eaten once a year when the city celebrates the bears dancing down the Sinner's Steps — "rich with ginger and pine nuts and bits of cherry." For Areo Hotah, its taste is one of his most cherished childhood memories of Norvos, recalled while sharpening his axe on duty in Dorne.
