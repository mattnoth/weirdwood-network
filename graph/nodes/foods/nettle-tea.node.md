## Identity

Nettle tea is a plain herbal infusion appearing as part of Melisandre's ordered breakfast at Castle Black — "nettle tea, a boiled egg, and bread with butter. Fresh bread, if you please, not fried." The order is notable because Melisandre then forgets to eat; the wildling Mance (in glamour) consumes it instead, buttering the warm brown bread with his dagger while Jon Snow refuses to break bread with him.
