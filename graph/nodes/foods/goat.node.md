## Culture

The [Black Goat](wiki:Black_Goat) is a god worshiped in [Qohor](wiki:Qohor).(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_qohor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) The standard of the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions), a [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) company, depicts a black goat with bloody horns.(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Their leader is [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat), a Qohorik who wears a helm in the shape of a goat's head (wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) and is called "the goat"(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) (though not to his face).(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
[Grigg the Goat](wiki:Grigg_the_Goat) is a [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) raider.(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Rasos30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Goat bones are part of the armor worn by [Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt) and his mount.(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Racok68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
A slave owned by [Yezzan zo Qaggaz](wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz) is known as [goat boy](wiki:Goat_boy) because of his twisted legs.(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
The [Goodbrothers](wiki:House_Goodbrother) of [Great Wyk](wiki:Great_Wyk) wear striped goat's hair sashes.(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
[Nahsa](wiki:Nahsa) is a fermented goat's milk laced with [honey](wiki:Honey) which is popular in [Norvos](wiki:Norvos).(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Goat cheese from the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters) is bought by [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor).(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
The so-called "sandy [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen)" raise goats and [horses](wiki:Horse) in the sands of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_queer_customs_of_the_south.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) The [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) drive great herds of goats and horses across the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea).(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Goats are also herded by [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibbenese)(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) and [Jogos Nhai](wiki:Jogos_Nhai).(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_the_plains_of_the_jogos_nhai.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) [Vaes Jini](wiki:Vaes_Jini) means "City of Goats" in the [Dothraki language](wiki:Dothraki_language).(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_vaes_jini_.28yinishar.29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

## Origins

Ser [Artys Arryn](wiki:Artys_Arryn) used a goat track at the foot of the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance) to surprise the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) during the [Battle of the Seven Stars](wiki:Battle_of_the_Seven_Stars).(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
The followers of the [Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Aenys_I)) entered the [Reach](wiki:Reach) from goat tracks in the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains).(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
During the [Third Dornish War](wiki:Third_Dornish_War), the host of Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) proceeded on foot along goat tracks while hunting the [second Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Jaehaerys_I)).(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
[Caraxes](wiki:Caraxes) devoured six goats at [Tarth](wiki:Tarth) when Prince [Aemon Targaryen](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) flew to aid Lord [Cameron Tarth](wiki:Cameron_Tarth).(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1)
King [Daeron I Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen) used a goat track to bypass watchtowers on the [Boneway](wiki:Boneway) during his [conquest of Dorne](wiki:Conquest_of_Dorne).(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
During the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow), servants at the tent of [House Fossoway](wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall) prepared goat with honey and herbs over a small cookfire.(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
After Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully) called his brother, Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully), "the black goat of the [Tully](wiki:House_Tully) flock", Brynden took a black fish as his personal emblem.(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
[Mychel Redfort](wiki:Mychel_Redfort) remarked that the father of [Mya Stone](wiki:Mya_Stone) must have been a goat because of her ability to navigate the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance).(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1)
[Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) once successfully wagered his ship against a flock of goats that he could douse a hearth-fire by urinating on it, and subsequently outraged his brother, Lord [Balon](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), by naming the ship [Golden Storm](wiki:Golden_Storm).(wiki:Goat.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
