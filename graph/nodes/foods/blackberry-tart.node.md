## Identity

Blackberry tarts served as the sweet course at the pre-Blackwater supper shared by Tyrion and Cersei Lannister in ACOK. The dessert's arrival marks the end of the formal meal just as the military crisis (Stannis's assault on King's Landing) reaches its climax.
