## Identity

Dog meat appears in ADWD as siege-starvation food in the Yunkish camp and in the form of "dogtail soup" — the vehicle for Tyrion's poisoning of Nurse. Both appearances mark the Meereen siege's extremity: dog meat is the final resort before human cannibalism. The poisoned dogtail soup is load-bearing (the slug `tyrions-mushrooms` in the poison-weapon context).

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

- (Tyrion X, ADWD — `sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-tyrion-10.md:199`) Yunkish siege camp: "one man skinning a dog for his stewpot" — a single observed detail amid the catalogue of slave-camp misery; dog meat as a signal of the besiegers' food collapse even before the plague arrives.
- (Tyrion XI, ADWD — `sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-tyrion-11.md:69`) Nurse's death: Tyrion brings Nurse "dogtail soup, with slivers of mushroom in the broth" as the ostensible comfort food for the pale-mare sick; the mushrooms are the poison vehicle. Last words Nurse hears: "A Lannister always pays his debts."
