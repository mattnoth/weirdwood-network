## Origins

In [131 AC](wiki:131_AC), Grand Maester [Orwyle](wiki:Orwyle), disguised as "Old Wyl", mixed moon tea and potions of [tansy](wiki:Tansy_(plant)) and [pennyroyal](wiki:Pennyroyal) for the prostitutes from [Mother's](wiki:Mother's_(brothel)), a brothel in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), to abort unwanted children.(wiki:Moon_tea.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
In [282 AC](wiki:282_AC), [Lysa Tully](wiki:Lysa_Tully) became pregnant by [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish). Lysa revealed her pregnancy to her father, Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), hoping that he would let her wed Petyr. Hoster, who had always felt Petyr was too lowborn,(wiki:Moon_tea.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) instead forced Lysa to abort the child with moon tea provided by Maester [Kym](wiki:Kym).(wiki:Moon_tea.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Lysa nearly died from the effects of the tea, and never forgave her father for it.(wiki:Moon_tea.cite_ref-Rawoiaflysa_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Moon_tea.cite_ref-Rawoiafpetyr_baelish.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
A [woods witch](wiki:Woods_witch) showed [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) how to brew moon tea after she lost her virginity to a [Lysene](wiki:Lys) sailor.(wiki:Moon_tea.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

As Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully) lies dying, he repeats the words "tansy", "blood", and asks for forgiveness. His daughter [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) initially thinks her father is referring to a woman by the name of Tansy, but then deduces that he had forced her sister [Lysa](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) to abort a pregnancy.(wiki:Moon_tea.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Lady [Ravella Smallwood](wiki:Ravella_Smallwood) indicates that milkmaids across the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) have been drinking tansy tea to avoid becoming pregnant by [Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams).(wiki:Moon_tea.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) overhears Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) pleading her love to her new husband, Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), telling him that she gave him her [maidenhead](wiki:Maidenhead) and would have given him a son as well, but the pregnancy had been ended by moon tea. Lysa tells Petyr that it wasn't her choice, she never knew what substance her father had given her to drink, and Petyr reassures her that both Hoster and his maester are dead.(wiki:Moon_tea.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) consumes moon tea during her romance with Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart).(wiki:Moon_tea.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) believes that Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell)'s requesting of moon tea from Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) is evidence that Margaery has a secret lover.(wiki:Moon_tea.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
When Margaery is arrested by the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), the [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow) lists multiple crimes/sins she has committed, including murdering "the fruit of her fornications" by using moon tea.(wiki:Moon_tea.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Grand Maester Pycelle feels that Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) dislikes him because of the moon tea he provided to Margaery, and says he would not have said anything about it if Queen Cersei had not commanded him.(wiki:Moon_tea.cite_ref-Radwd72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
