## Identity

Beef is a common meat across Westeros, consumed in multiple forms: salt beef (cured for long marches and provisioning), boiled beef (domestic supper staple), and fresh beef. Salt beef appears as a campaign ration alongside hardbread; boiled beef appears as humble household supper fare.
