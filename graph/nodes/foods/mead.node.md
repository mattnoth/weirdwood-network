## Culture

The sharing of "meat and mead" is a common custom in the [known world](wiki:Known_world), including in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros),(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) by the [free companies](wiki:Free_companies),(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and at [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak).(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) The [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) prefer clotted mare's milk over mead, however.(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
The mead and cider of the [Quiet Isle](wiki:Quiet_Isle) are famous.(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
[Tormund](wiki:Tormund)'s titles include [Mead-king of Ruddy Hall](wiki:Mead-king_of_Ruddy_Hall).(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Origins

The party of Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) celebrated with meat and mead when they reached [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) in [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). [Lothor Burley](wiki:Lothor_Burley) served [mammoth meat](wiki:Mammoth), mead, and [stout](wiki:Beer) to the queen when she arrived at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Rfab10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) often drank mead with [Farlen](wiki:Farlen) while discussing hounds and hunting.(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
[Tormund](wiki:Tormund) claims to have slept with a [bear](wiki:Bear) after consuming a cask of mead at [Ruddy Hall](wiki:Ruddy_Hall).(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
[Gilly](wiki:Gilly) drank sweet mead on the day she wed her father, [Craster](wiki:Craster).(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Rasos46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Val](wiki:Val) and [Jarl](wiki:Jarl) share a horn of mead when [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) is introduced to [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder). Jon accepts the offer of mead from the [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall).(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) After [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye), [Mag Mar Tun Doh Weg](wiki:Mag_Mar_Tun_Doh_Weg), and [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte) are slain during the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black), Jon and [Tormund](wiki:Tormund) share a skin of strong mead to honor their fallen comrades.(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
[Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) tells [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) that [Dareon](wiki:Dareon)'s singing is as sweet as mead.(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Rasos46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Casks and wagons of [ale](wiki:Ale), mead, and [wine](wiki:Wine) are provided by [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey) during the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Rasos50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Flagons of mead, milk, and golden wine are served at the breakfast feast when King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) receives wedding gifts.(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) A fine light mead is served for the wedding of [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) to [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) at the [Fingers](wiki:Drearfort).(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Brother [Clement](wiki:Clement) sells mead from the [Quiet Isle](wiki:Quiet_Isle) at [Saltpans](wiki:Saltpans), but he is killed when the town is [raided by outlaws](wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans).(wiki:Mead.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
