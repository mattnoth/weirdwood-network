## Identity

Lamprey pie is a rich, high-status delicacy eaten at feasts throughout Westeros — associated with wealthy households and noble tables. Distinct from raw or stewed lamprey; the pie form appears repeatedly in feast and celebratory contexts. Notable for its association with gluttony, excess, and ironic tragedy (Rhaegel Targaryen choked to death on one).
