## Culture

A red salmon is included in the arms of [House Mooton](wiki:House_Mooton) of [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool).(wiki:Salmon.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Lord [William Mooton](wiki:William_Mooton) pins his cloak with a red-gold brooch in the shape of a salmon.(wiki:Salmon.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Origins

Lord [Torrhen Manderly](wiki:Torrhen_Manderly), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), suggested that King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) could fish for salmon in the [White Knife](wiki:White_Knife) if he were to visit the [north](wiki:North). Aegon cancelled the planned [royal progress](wiki:Royal_progress) when he came of age, however, and dismissed Lord Manderly from his service.(wiki:Salmon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Salmon is among the seafood brought by Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) from [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) sends salmon to Lady [Donella Hornwood](wiki:Donella_Hornwood) during the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast).(wiki:Salmon.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Salmon is served at [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea) the night before [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan)'s fleet departs for White Harbor.(wiki:Salmon.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
The stores of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) have planks of dry smoked salmon.(wiki:Salmon.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

[Pike](wiki:Pike), [trout](wiki:Trout), and salmon are served at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon).(wiki:Salmon.cite_ref-Alayne_9-0)
