## Origins

Since doves and pigeons can carry messages, the peaceful King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen) attempted to have the [maesters](wiki:Maesters) of the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) replace their messenger [ravens](wiki:Raven) with doves;(wiki:Pigeon.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) the debacle is included in Archmaester [Walgrave](wiki:Walgrave)'s [Black Wings, Swift Words](wiki:Black_Wings,_Swift_Words).(wiki:Pigeon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Baelor also claimed to have seen [a child](wiki:High_Septon_(child)) speaking with doves that answered him in the voice of men and women. Baelor believd the birds spoke with the voices of [the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), so the king convinced the [Most Devout](wiki:Most_Devout) to choose the child as the new [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon).(wiki:Pigeon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)
Pigeons and doves were part of the immense pie at the [wedding at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls).(wiki:Pigeon.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), living on the streets of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) after escaping the [arrest of Eddard Stark](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark), learns to knock down pigeons with a stick. She eats some raw and sells others to shops in [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom).(wiki:Pigeon.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Skewers of pigeon and capon are served during the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast_(299)) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Pigeon.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

A hundred doves are baked into a great pie for [the wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Pigeon.cite_ref-Rasos12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) The royal couple cut the pie with a greatsword, allowing the birds to escape during the wedding feast.(wiki:Pigeon.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) Cooked and spiced pigeon pies are served during the celebration.(wiki:Pigeon.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Paezhar zo Myraq](wiki:Paezhar_zo_Myraq) is mocked as the "Little Pigeon" by the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown).(wiki:Pigeon.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
The [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) captures a [Myrish](wiki:Myr) cog named [Dove](wiki:Dove) in [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay).(wiki:Pigeon.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
