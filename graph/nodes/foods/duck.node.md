## Origins

[Dunk](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) enjoyed duck at an inn near [Ashford Meadow](wiki:Ashford_Meadow).(wiki:Duck.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
Ser [Rolly Duckfield](wiki:Rolly_Duckfield) chose his surname because he was in a field with ducks nearby when he was knighted by [Griff](wiki:Jon_Connington), earning him the nickname "Duck".(wiki:Duck.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]
