## Origins

Legend claims that [Corlos](wiki:Corlos) discovered [gold](wiki:Gold) in [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) after tracking a [lion](wiki:Lion) which had been hunting sheep.(wiki:Sheep.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)
During the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), the vanguard of King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) was barred in the high passes of the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) by a wall of rotting sheep carcasses.(wiki:Sheep.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)
[Sheepstealer](wiki:Sheepstealer) was a [dragon](wiki:Dragon) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) with a taste for mutton.(wiki:Sheep.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0) [Nettles](wiki:Nettles) left out a freshly-killed sheep for the dragon every morning, causing Sheepstealer to grow accustomed to her presence and eventually allow her to mount him.(wiki:Sheep.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-1) When Nettles and Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) departed each other, the girl slew a black ram and fed the animal to Sheepstealer before flying away from [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool).(wiki:Sheep.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)
[Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber), [Lady of Coldmoat](wiki:Lady_of_Coldmoat), had five hundred head of black-nosed sheep.(wiki:Sheep.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)
Ser [Goodwin](wiki:Goodwin) had [Brienne Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth) slaughter lambs and suckling pigs in an effort to toughen the girl.(wiki:Sheep.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)
