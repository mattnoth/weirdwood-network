## Identity

Sourleaf is a leaf chewed as a stimulant and mild analgesic across Westeros. It has a foul taste and makes the chewer's spit look like blood. It is associated with common folk, Night's Watch members, and rough travel — Yoren chews it habitually while sharpening his blade. Its mild pain-dulling properties make it useful for beatings and injuries.
