## Identity

Buttermilk is a soured dairy drink used in Westeros as a stomach-settler. Sansa drinks a glass of buttermilk with sweet biscuits before being escorted to the throne room by Ser Meryn Trant — a small act of self-care before facing Joffrey's cruelties.
