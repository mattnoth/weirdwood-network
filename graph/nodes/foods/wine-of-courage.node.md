## Identity

Wine of courage is a potion fed to Unsullied slave soldiers in Astapor from the day they are cut, with every meal, throughout their years of training. Despite its name it is not true wine. It is made from deadly nightshade, bloodfly larva, black lotus root, and many secret ingredients. The potion progressively dulls and eventually eliminates pain sensation, which is part of what makes the Unsullied fearless in battle. The recipe is a trade secret of the Good Masters of Astapor.
