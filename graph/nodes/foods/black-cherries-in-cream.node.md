## Identity

Black cherries in sweet cream served as the dessert course at Illyrio Mopatis's manse in Pentos during Tyrion Lannister's first dinner there. The dish accompanies a lavish multi-course feast; its presence marks the meal's wealth and the host's calculated generosity.
