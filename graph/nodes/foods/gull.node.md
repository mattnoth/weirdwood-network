## Culture

[Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown) is a port city found in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn).(wiki:Gull.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) According to a semi-canon source, [House Shett of Gulltown](wiki:House_Shett_of_Gulltown) include nine white seagulls in their arms.(wiki:Gull.cite_ref-2) Ser [Damon Shett](wiki:Damon_Shett) is the Knight of [Gull Tower](wiki:Gull_Tower).(wiki:Gull.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
One of the [priests of the Drowned God](wiki:Priests_of_the_Drowned_God) is called the [Old Grey Gull](wiki:Old_Grey_Gull).(wiki:Gull.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Origins

The remains of [Arrax](wiki:Arrax) were feasted upon by [crabs](wiki:Crab) and seagulls after washing up beneath [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Gull.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The butchered people of [Spicetown](wiki:Spicetown) were left for gulls, rats, and carrion [crows](wiki:Crow) after the [Battle of the Gullet](wiki:Battle_of_the_Gullet).(wiki:Gull.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) once put an arrow through a seagull on the wing while training with [Dagmer Cleftjaw](wiki:Dagmer_Cleftjaw) in his youth.(wiki:Gull.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan) has roasted gull stuffed with mushrooms and fennel and onion prepared for him on [Valyrian](wiki:Valyrian_(ship)).(wiki:Gull.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

- (Davos I, ACOK — `sources/chapters/acok/acok-davos-01.md:147`) Salladhor's dinner menu aboard Valyrian as he bids Davos farewell: "Minced lamb with pepper and roasted gull stuffed with mushrooms and fennel and onion" — Lyseni feast cooking; the stuffed gull is a show-piece dish for a pirate who styles himself a prince.

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell)'s breakfast at the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens) includes a plate of gull's eggs diced with bits of ham and fiery peppers.(wiki:Gull.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
A raucous seagull watches the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk) from one of [Nagga](wiki:Nagga)'s ribs.(wiki:Gull.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
