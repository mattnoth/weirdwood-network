## Identity

Candied ginger appears as a characterization detail: Illyrio Mopatis packs it as a parting gift for Young Griff (Aegon Targaryen), noting "he was always fond of it." The small detail indicates Illyrio's personal familiarity with Aegon from childhood and implies years of contact.
