## Identity

Acorn paste is a survival food made by grinding acorns on rocks, described as tasting "awful." It appears in ACOK as the nadir of Arya's starvation arc during the flight from Harrenhal, consumed alongside bugs, worms, frogs, and blackberries when no other food could be found. The recipe came from the poacher Kurz before he died of his wound.
