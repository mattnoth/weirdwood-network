## Origins

Great elk have gone extinct south of the [Wall](wiki:Wall), just like [giants](wiki:Giants), [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest), and [direwolves](wiki:Direwolves).(wiki:Deer.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
By [royal](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen) decree, Lord [Wyman Webber](wiki:Wyman_Webber) and his descendants were granted the right to hunt red deer in the [Osgrey](wiki:House_Osgrey) owned [wood](wiki:Wat's_Wood).(wiki:Deer.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)
[Wenda the White Fawn](wiki:Wenda_the_White_Fawn), an [outlaw](wiki:Outlaw) of the [Kingswood Brotherhood](wiki:Kingswood_Brotherhood), would brand a fawn on the buttocks of her [highborn](wiki:Highborn) captives, such as [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey).(wiki:Deer.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)(wiki:Deer.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) granted the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) of the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood) the right to hunt a few deer during the [autumn](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Deer.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1)
[Will](wiki:Will) was sent to the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) after being caught skinning a buck in [House Mallister](wiki:House_Mallister)'s woods.(wiki:Deer.cite_ref-Ragot0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)
Lord [Yohn Royce](wiki:Yohn_Royce) brought back a buck to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) after a hunt in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) with Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark).(wiki:Deer.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)
While skinning a deer, Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) confronted his son, [Samwell](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), about his view on the inheritance of [their house](wiki:House_Tarly), and forced him to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Deer.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

When a [white hart](wiki:White_hart) is sighted in the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood), King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) goes to hunt it with his royal retinue, including his brother [Renly](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) and Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy).(wiki:Deer.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) wishes that [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) would be the one to kill the deer, noting that white harts are believed to be rare and [magical](wiki:Magic).(wiki:Deer.cite_ref-Ragot44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Robert does not find the hart until it is too late; wolves kill and devour it before the hunters arrive, leaving behind little more than hooves and horns. Robert is infuriated, until scouts bring word that a monstrous [boar](wiki:Boar) was sighted deeper in the forest, so Robert goes off to pursue it.(wiki:Deer.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)
[Syrio Forel](wiki:Syrio_Forel) teaches [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) multiple phrases of animals she should try to be like, including "Swift as a deer."(wiki:Deer.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) After [the arrest](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark) of her father, [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), Arya has to flee [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), and she frequently repeats the phrases to strengthen herself.(wiki:Deer.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)(wiki:Deer.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)(wiki:Deer.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Patchface](wiki:Patchface) wears a mock helm fashioned from an old [tin](wiki:Tin) bucket strapped with a rack of deer antlers.(wiki:Deer.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)
[Yoren](wiki:Yoren) commands the former poachers, [Koss](wiki:Koss) and [Kurz](wiki:Kurz), to go hunt in the woods to feed their group marching towards the Wall. They come back with deer or quails.(wiki:Deer.cite_ref-Racok9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1)
The lands on [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath) given to Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) by Lord [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) include woods containing red deer.(wiki:Deer.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)
[Summer](wiki:Summer) and [Shaggydog](wiki:Shaggydog) kill and eat a young elk in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood).(wiki:Deer.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
The [White Hart](wiki:White_Hart) and [Stag of the Sea](wiki:Stag_of_the_Sea) are both destroyed by [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Deer.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)
