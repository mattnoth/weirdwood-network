## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Maester [Cressen](wiki:Cressen) attempts to assassinate [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) using the strangler. She survives after drinking almost the whole poisoned cup of wine, claiming her faith cleanses her. When Cressen drinks the remaining half a swallow from the same cup, he dies.(wiki:Strangler.cite_ref-Racok0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
The [fool](wiki:Fool) Ser [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard) provides [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) with a [silver hair net](wiki:Silver_hair_net), decorated with what he claims to be black [amethysts](wiki:Amethyst) from [Asshai](wiki:Asshai). He claims the net is [magic](wiki:Magic) that will give Sansa justice, vengeance for King [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon)'s execution of her father [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), and take her home to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Strangler.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Sansa Stark wears the silver hair net to the [royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), per Dontos Hollard's orders.(wiki:Strangler.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Strangler.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) During the wedding, Lady [Olenna Redwyne](wiki:Olenna_Redwyne) secretly takes one of the crystals from the hair net and uses it to poison the wine in King Joffrey's goblet. Joffrey appears to choke on a piece of his wedding pie, and dies, unable to breathe. The wine, originally red, has turned purple.(wiki:Strangler.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
Sansa, fleeing the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) in the chaos of the assassination, discovers that one of the amethysts in her hair net is missing,(wiki:Strangler.cite_ref-Rasos61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and later learns that Dontos was given the hair net by Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish). Petyr tells Sansa that he and Lady Olenna conspired to murder Joffrey and frame [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Strangler.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
At Tyrion's trial for [kingslaying](wiki:Kingslaying), Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) tells the court that Joffrey was killed by the strangler, and that Tyrion stole it and other poisons from his chambers.(wiki:Strangler.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
