## Origins

Grey plague hit the city of [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) when [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) was young. It wiped out half the city and three-quarters of the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) during its run. To prevent its spread, Lord [Quenton Hightower](wiki:Quenton_Hightower) ordered the gates barred, all the ships in harbor burned, and anyone trying to leave killed. These orders made him extremely unpopular, but the measures were successful. The day he rescinded the orders, after the plague had burnt itself out, the survivors pulled Quenton and his young son from their horses and slit their throats.(wiki:Grey_plague.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Some years before to the death of [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_Baratheon), grey plague came to the city of [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) when the [Treasure](wiki:Treasure), a [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) trading galley, landed there on her way back from the [Jade Sea](wiki:Jade_Sea). The Pentoshi slew the ship's oarsmen as they came ashore and burned the ship at anchor, but the rats crept down the oars and paddled to the quay on cold stone feet. The plague killed over two thousand people in the city before it ran its course, including [Serra](wiki:Serra), the wife of [Magister](wiki:Magister) [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis).(wiki:Grey_plague.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
