## Appearances & Description

The hair net looks like a silvery spiderweb. Its fine-spun silver wire strands are so thin and delicate, that the net seems to weigh no more than a breath of air. Small gems are set where any two strands cross. The gems, so dark they drink the [moonlight](wiki:Moon), are supposedly rare black amethysts from [Asshai](wiki:Asshai), which are a deep true purple in daylight.(wiki:Silver_hair_net.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Silver_hair_net.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) However, the "amethysts" are in fact the [strangler](wiki:Strangler), a choking [poison](wiki:Poison) in the form of deep purple crystals.(wiki:Silver_hair_net.cite_ref-Racok0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

After the award ceremony for the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Ser [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard) meets [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) in the [godswood](wiki:Godswood) of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep). Dontos gives Sansa a silver hair net, set with gems he claims are black [amethysts](wiki:Amethyst) from [Asshai](wiki:Asshai). He says that the hair net is [magic](wiki:Magic), that it will give Sansa justice, vengeance for King [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon)'s execution of her father, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), and will take her home to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Silver_hair_net.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Dontos reminds Sansa that she must wear the silver hair net the night of King Joffrey's upcoming wedding to [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), and afterwards they will escape.(wiki:Silver_hair_net.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
In the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) and the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) camp overnight at [High Heart](wiki:High_Heart). They meet with the [ghost of High Heart](wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart) to hear her tell the future. The old woman's dreams says she [dreamed](wiki:Vision) of a maid at a feast with purple [serpents](wiki:Snake) in her hair, [venom](wiki:Poison) dripping from their fangs.(wiki:Silver_hair_net.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
As instructed, Sansa wears the silver hair net for the [royal wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding), with a matching gown of silvery satin and purple felt. [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), Sansa's husband, notices that her [handmaid](wiki:Handmaid), Tyrion's secret lover [Shae](wiki:Shae), has arranged the girl's hair artfully within the silver and purple [gemstone](wiki:Gemstone) net. When they arrive at the feast, Lady [Olenna Tyrell](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell) says that the wind has disturbed Sansa's hair, and she tucks the loose strands back into place and straightens the hair net.(wiki:Silver_hair_net.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
During the wedding feast, King Joffrey begins choking while eating pigeon pie and drinking [wine](wiki:Wine) from his [wedding chalice](wiki:Wedding_chalice). Bystanders and the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) are unable to help, and the boy king dies, unable to breathe. Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) has her brother Tyrion arrested for the crime of [kingslaying](wiki:Kingslaying).(wiki:Silver_hair_net.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
In the chaos of Joffrey's death, Sansa flees to the godswood as she had been instructed by Dontos, and changes into the darker, simpler clothing she had previously hidden there. When she removes her hair net, she notices that one of the black amethysts is missing, with a dark smudge in the silver socket where the stone had fallen out. Terrified, Sansa realizes that the "magic" of the hair net must be related to Joffrey's murder, that the amethysts must not truly be amethysts. When she hears Dontos arriving in the godswood, she hides the hair net in the pocket of her cloak. Sansa confronts Dontos, saying he must have taken the amethyst from her hair net and poisoned Joffrey, but he denies it. They then escape the Red Keep to [a ship](wiki:Merling_King) in [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay), where Dontos is killed by order of Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish).(wiki:Silver_hair_net.cite_ref-Rasos61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
At [the tower](wiki:Drearfort) of [House Baelish](wiki:House_Baelish) in the [Fingers](wiki:Fingers), Sansa confronts Petyr about Joffrey's death, accusing him of commanding Dontos to poison the king. Littlefinger laughs, and says that the alcoholic Dontos's role was to make sure Sansa wore the silver hair net to the wedding feast and help her escape the castle. Petyr says that someone must have straightened her hair net that night, and Sansa realizes it must have been Lady Olenna who took the "amethyst" from the net and used it to poison Joffrey during the celebration.(wiki:Silver_hair_net.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
