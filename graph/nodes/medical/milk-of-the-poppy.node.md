## Identity

Milk of the poppy is a medicine — a poppy-derived sedative and painkiller used throughout the Seven Kingdoms by maesters to dull pain and induce sleep. It is one of architecture.md's canonical `concept.medical` examples.

## Edges


## Origins

Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) was treated with milk of the poppy after the stillbirth of her daughter [Visenya](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daemon)).(wiki:Milk_of_the_poppy.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), at the [Battle of Rook's Rest](wiki:Battle_of_Rook's_Rest), King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) suffered broken ribs, a broken hip, and [dragonfire](wiki:Dragonfire) burns that covered half his body. He was treated with milk of the poppy, and was "lost in poppy dreams", bedridden for the rest of the year. When the [greens](wiki:Greens) took [Dragonstone](wiki:Fall_of_Dragonstone), Aegon was further injured with two shattered legs, but refused milk of the poppy, living the rest of his life in great pain.(wiki:Milk_of_the_poppy.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-4)
In his old age, Lord [Quellon Greyjoy](wiki:Quellon_Greyjoy) suffered from excruciating stomach pains, and had to take a draught of milk of the poppy every night to sleep.(wiki:Milk_of_the_poppy.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Hoster Tully grows even more deathly ill, until Maester Vyman is afraid to make the milk of the poppy any stronger.(wiki:Milk_of_the_poppy.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell) is treated by Maester [Caleotte](wiki:Caleotte) with milk of the poppy for the pain of his gout.(wiki:Milk_of_the_poppy.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
[Joss Stilwood](wiki:Joss_Stilwood), Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane)'s squire, tells Qyburn that Gregor drinks milk of the poppy often, like normal men drink ale, to self-treat his blinding headaches. Gregor's tolerance of and dependency on milk of the poppy has the effect that both Grand Maester Pycelle and Qyburn are unable to moderate his pain from his spear wounds and [manticore venom](wiki:Manticore_venom) poisoning.(wiki:Milk_of_the_poppy.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)
Maester [Colemon](wiki:Colemon) treats young Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn)'s shaking sickness with milk of the poppy, to help him sleep.(wiki:Milk_of_the_poppy.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) Later, he suggests that the boy be drugged with milk of the poppy so that he will sleep on the dangerous journey from the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) to the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon), but [Alayne Stone](wiki:Alayne_Stone) rejects the idea, as the Lord of the Eyrie should not arrive looking like a sack of grain tied to a mule.(wiki:Milk_of_the_poppy.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

At a feast in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), Doran Martell drinks wine with poppy juice, made by Maester [Myles](wiki:Myles), to ease the pain in his joints. Later, however, when he meets with the [Sand Snakes](wiki:Sand_Snakes), he rejects Maester Caleotte's offer of milk of the poppy because he wants to keep his head clear, despite the fact that he thinks he would need a bucketful for his pain.(wiki:Milk_of_the_poppy.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2)
After Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) is mortally wounded by [dragonfire](wiki:Dragonfire) all over his body, [Missandei](wiki:Missandei) gives him milk of the poppy during the three days he lays dying.(wiki:Milk_of_the_poppy.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)
