## Origins

Long ago, multiple apprentice boys at the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort) claimed to have seen something which came in the night, but their descriptions of it differed when they informed their [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch). Three boys died within a year and a fourth went mad.(wiki:Thing_that_came_in_the_night.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The thing is said to have returned a century later, this time with the apprentices shambling behind it in chains.(wiki:Thing_that_came_in_the_night.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
[Old Nan](wiki:Old_Nan) told the children of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) about the thing that came in the night, among other scary stories.(wiki:Thing_that_came_in_the_night.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) The legend may be included in [Harmune](wiki:Harmune)'s [Watchers on the Wall](wiki:Watchers_on_the_Wall).(wiki:Thing_that_came_in_the_night.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_wall_and_beyond:_the_night.27s_watch.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
