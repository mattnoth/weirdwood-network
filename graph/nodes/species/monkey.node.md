## Culture

Monkeys can be kept as pets.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf831_9-1)(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Rfab21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Monkeys can be purchased on the [Long Bridge](wiki:Long_Bridge) in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis).(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)

[Jade](wiki:Jade) statues carved in the shape of monkeys are a trade good.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan) wears a flamboyant tunic with jade buttons carved in the shape of monkeys.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
[Mummers](wiki:Mummer) often have monkeys in their farces and plays.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Ragot41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
There is a small carrack called the [Brazen Monkey](wiki:Brazen_Monkey).(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

## Origins

A legend from [Yi Ti](wiki:Yi_Ti) says that the [Long Night](wiki:Long_Night) was averted by the deeds of a woman with a monkey's tail.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf24.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
Monkeys were among the many goods traded between the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles) and the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold), as there was a fashion among the [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlord) for pet monkeys.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf831_9-2)
The hunchbacked [fool](wiki:Fool) in the service of Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) was known as [Lord Monkeyface](wiki:Lord_Monkeyface).(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Rfab3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
Among the strange creatures observed by the crew of [Lady Meredith](wiki:Lady_Meredith) during their year-long stay in [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos) were monkeys that walked like men.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)
Lady [Baela Targaryen](wiki:Baela_Targaryen) had a pet monkey.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Rfab21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)
[Roggerio Rogare](wiki:Roggerio_Rogare) kept monkeys from Sothoryos at the [Mermaid](wiki:Mermaid_(pillow_house)).(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Rfab23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)
[Gerion Lannister](wiki:Gerion_Lannister) taught his young nephew [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) some tumbling tricks, after which he practiced tumbling for six months, until his father [Tywin](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) told him to stop, telling him he was born a [lion](wiki:House_Lannister), not a monkey.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
The [widow of the waterfront](wiki:Widow_of_the_waterfront) once had a monkey which could perform all sorts of clever tricks.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne) derisively nicknames [Pypar](wiki:Pypar), a recruit of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), as "Monkey" and "the [Mummer](wiki:Mummer)'s Monkey".(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Ragot41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

In [Cobbler's Square](wiki:Cobbler's_Square) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), a [begging brother](wiki:Begging_brother) rants about corruption in the city, calling [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) a "twisted little monkey [demon](wiki:Demon)" who controls the [abomination](wiki:Incest), King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) Tyrion is hurt by the title, and often uses it mockingly in reference to himself thereafter.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)
Ser [Mark Mullendore](wiki:Mark_Mullendore) has a pet monkey, a black-and-white creature from the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles),(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) and he joins [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) beneath the walls of [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge). The knight feeds his monkey morsels at the feast.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) Mark fights at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), losing part of his left arm and his monkey before getting captured.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)
[Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos) gives [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) many gifts, including tumbling monkeys.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Leo Tyrell](wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn)) mocks [Alleras](wiki:Alleras) by referring to his mother, a trader from the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles), as a monkey.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)
[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) recalls that Ser [Mark Mullendore](wiki:Mark_Mullendore) was a part of the wager over who would claim her [maidenhead](wiki:Maidenhead). He used his monkey to make her laugh in his attempt to woo her.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) When she learns of Mark's fate, she remembers him sitting outside his pavilion with his monkey on his shoulder in a little suit of chainmail, the two of them making faces at each other.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3)
[Megga Tyrell](wiki:Megga_Tyrell) asks Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) for help in finding Mark a new monkey.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

In [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) sees a number of [animal](wiki:Animal) sellers clustered together; some of the animals on offer are agile little monkeys with striped tails and clever pink hands. Ser [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater) suggests to Quentyn that [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) might like a monkey.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0) When attempting to help Quentyn steal one of Daenerys's [dragons](wiki:Dragon) from the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), Ser [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater) wears an ape mask and says his disguise "makes a monkey out of me."(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)
When [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) meet with some of the magister's cohorts, Illyrio tells them Tyrion is called "Yollo". Tyrion thinks that Yollo sounds like something you might name a monkey, and says his mother called him Hugor Hill.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)
Anchored near the [Isle of Cedars](wiki:Isle_of_Cedars), half of the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) becomes infested with monkeys from the isle, even [Iron Victory](wiki:Iron_Victory). [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) thinks that the isle should have been called the "Isle of Monkeys". He sends men up into the rigging to deal with the animals, but they prove themselves more agile than his crew. The antics of the monkeys amuse the crew, but they do not amuse Victarion, who dislikes laughter.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) The monkeys aboard Iron Victory do not seem to like [Moqorro](wiki:Moqorro), screaming and throwing their feces at the [red priest](wiki:Red_priest) and the ship's crew. As Moqorro heals Victarion's hand, he sings a strange high wailing song in [High Valyrian](wiki:High_Valyrian), which appears to make the monkeys leave the ship, screeching as they leap into the water.(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) When [Lamentation](wiki:Lamentation_(ship)) chases down [Ghiscari Dawn](wiki:Ghiscari_Dawn), both ships can hear monkeys chattering from the ruins of [Ghozai](wiki:Ghozai).(wiki:Monkey.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
