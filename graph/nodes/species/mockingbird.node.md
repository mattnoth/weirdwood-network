## Culture

Rather than the [Titan's head](wiki:Titan_of_Braavos) used in the traditional arms of [House Baelish](wiki:House_Baelish), Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) has adopted mockingbird imagery for himself.(wiki:Mockingbird.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Petyr uses a mockingbird seal,(wiki:Mockingbird.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and he clasps his cloak with a [silver](wiki:Silver) mockingbird.(wiki:Mockingbird.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Mockingbird.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Petyr owns a plum-colored doublet with a mockingbird embroidered on the breast in black thread,(wiki:Mockingbird.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and he also has a silvery cape patterned with mockingbirds.(wiki:Mockingbird.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) According to a semi-canon source, Petyr's arms are a field of silver mockingbirds on green.(wiki:Mockingbird.cite_ref-6)
