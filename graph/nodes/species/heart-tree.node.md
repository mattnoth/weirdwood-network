## Origins

After [Ice Eyes](wiki:Brandon_Stark_(Ice_Eyes)) retook the [Wolf's Den](wiki:Wolf's_Den) from slavers from the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones), the freed slaves were said to have hung the entrails of the slavers from a heart tree.(wiki:Heart_tree.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
The [Knight of the Laughing Tree](wiki:Knight_of_the_Laughing_Tree) carried a shield depicting a weirwood heart tree with a laughing face.(wiki:Heart_tree.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
