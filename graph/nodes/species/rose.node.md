## Culture

A golden rose is included in the arms of [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell). Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) clasps his cloak with a golden rose(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) or a jade rose,(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) his belt is a linked chain of golden roses,(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) and a crest of golden roses runs down the center of his helm.(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) The sword of the Knight of Flowers has a rose in alabaster as its pommel.(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Loras's shield is emblazoned with three golden roses,(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) while that of his elder brother, Ser [Garlan Tyrell](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell), has a pair of golden roses instead.(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)
Besides the Tyrells, other families with roses in their arms include the [Costayne](wiki:House_Costayne) with black roses(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) and the [Serrys](wiki:House_Serry) with a white rose.(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Roses can also be included the arms of [House Meadows](wiki:House_Meadows), who blazon their shield with a border of flowers of many colors and varieties on green.(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-18)
Lady [Olenna Tyrell](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell),(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-2) Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys),(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) and the [Blue Bard](wiki:Blue_Bard)(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) scent themselves with rosewater, and [Racallio Ryndoon](wiki:Racallio_Ryndoon) was known to bathe in [lavender](wiki:Lavender) or rosewater.(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
The [roseroad](wiki:Roseroad) runs through the [Reach](wiki:Reach) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Rtloiafwesteros.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) The [Rosewood](wiki:Rosewood) is also found in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
A [firemage](wiki:Firemage) in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) can make burning roses bloom in the air.(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

## Origins

Wild roses have grown over the sepulcher of King [Tristifer IV Mudd](wiki:Tristifer_IV_Mudd) at [Oldstones](wiki:Oldstones).(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
The [Company of the Rose](wiki:Company_of_the_Rose) was founded by [northmen](wiki:Northmen) who refused to submit to King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) during [the Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest) and instead traveled across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) to [Essos](wiki:Essos) to become [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords).(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
The [Tourney of the Field of Roses](wiki:Tourney_of_the_Field_of_Roses) was a magnificent tourney at held by [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) during the reign of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_highgarden.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
[Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark) loved the scent of [winter roses](wiki:Winter_rose).(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) During the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal), Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) named Lyanna the [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty) instead of his wife, [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell), giving Lyanna a garland of the blue roses.(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-1)(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)
[Chett](wiki:Chett) picked wild roses, [Tansy](wiki:Tansy_(plant)), and [goldencups](wiki:Goldencup) in his failed attempt to woo [Bessa](wiki:Bessa_(Hag's_Mire)).(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)
[Ronnet Connington](wiki:Ronnet_Connington) was betrothed to [Brienne Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth), but he had no real interest in marrying her. When Red Ronnet was introduced to the girl, he handed her a single rose, saying it was all Brienne would ever have from him.(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

During the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney), the stallion of Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) is draped in a blanket of red and white roses. After each victory, the Knight of Flowers gives a white rose to a [maiden](wiki:Maiden) in the audience. After defeating Ser [Robar Royce](wiki:Robar_Royce), however, Loras gives a red rose to [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark).(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The [crown](wiki:Crown) of [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) is a ring of roses made of soft [gold](wiki:Gold).(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2) While hosting Renly and Loras at [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge), [House Caswell](wiki:House_Caswell) serves [lemon](wiki:Lemon) cakes in the shape of roses.(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-3)
After the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) rewards Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) and his sons [Garlan](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell) and Loras with chains of roses wrought in soft yellow gold, from which hang golden discs with the [lion](wiki:Lion) of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) picked out in [rubies](wiki:Rubies).(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Near the lands of [House Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken), Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) sees an abandoned watchtower overlooking the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) overgrown with climbing roses.(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
Rose petals are strewn between the sheets of their bed when [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) is forced to marry [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)
The [wedding chalice](wiki:Wedding_chalice) given by Mace to Joffrey includes an [emerald](wiki:Emerald) rose to represent [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell).(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0) When [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) marries Joffrey, her [maiden's cloak](wiki:Maiden's_cloak) is made of a hundred cloth-of-gold roses sewn to green velvet. Prince [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) scatters rose petals from a basket during the procession. [Hamish the Harper](wiki:Hamish_the_Harper) sings "[A Rose of Gold](wiki:A_Rose_of_Gold)" at the [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding), during which Joffrey chokes to death.(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)
[Daario Naharis](wiki:Daario_Naharis) picks dusky roses, [waspwillow](wiki:Waspwillow), wild mint, [lady's lace](wiki:Lady's_lace), [daggerleaf](wiki:Daggerleaf), broom, [prickly ben](wiki:Prickly_ben), and [harpy's gold](wiki:Harpy's_gold) for [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) on the road from [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Queen Margaery places a great bouquet of golden roses at the bier of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) during his funeral.(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)
King Tommen gives the name [Golden Rose](wiki:Golden_Rose) to one of the [Cersei's dromonds](wiki:Cersei's_dromonds).(wiki:Rose.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)
