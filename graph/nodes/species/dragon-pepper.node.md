## Origins

At the [winter tourney](wiki:Winter_tourney_at_King's_Landing) at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in [253 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), sixteen-year-old [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) received his his [knighthood](wiki:Knight) from King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen).(wiki:Dragon_pepper.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Dragon_pepper.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) That night at the feast, Ser Barristan ate ribs of wild [boar](wiki:Boar) prepared with dragon peppers in the Dornish style, so hot they burned his mouth.(wiki:Dragon_pepper.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

In the shadow city of [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear), Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) watches a man grilling snakes with such pungent sauces that they bring tears to Arys's eyes.(wiki:Dragon_pepper.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
In one of Sunspear's towers, the captive Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) is served a midday meal of goat kid roasted with [lemons](wiki:Lemon) and honey, and grape leaves stuffed with raisins, onions, [mushrooms](wiki:Mushroom_(fungi)), and dragon peppers.(wiki:Dragon_pepper.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The [Selaesori Qhoran](wiki:Selaesori_Qhoran) carries twenty jars of dragon peppers.(wiki:Dragon_pepper.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
At a feast in Sunspear to honor the arrival of Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann), one course includes savory snake stew containing chunks of seven kinds of snakes simmered with dragon peppers, [blood oranges](wiki:Blood_orange), and a dash of venom.(wiki:Dragon_pepper.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
In [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), forty-seven years after he was knighted, Barristan Selmy still recalls the taste of the boar with dragon peppers.(wiki:Dragon_pepper.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
