## Appearances & Description

Shadowcats have thick black fur with white stripes.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) In size, they are somewhere in between real world tigers and mountain lions.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Rssm3196.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Their claws are known to be very sharp;(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Racok54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) a shadowcat can disembowel a man with one paw.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Shadowcats have sharp curved front teeth and a lashing tail.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) Shadowcats have deep, rumbling growls(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and furious screams.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Racok53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Their eyes appear to glow at night.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Racok53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) Shadowskin,(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) the pelts of shadowcats, are prized(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2) as warm and thick fur cloaks.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
Shadowcats are fearsome predators, and also scavenge.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Silent and agile hunters, it is said that shadowcats can smell blood six miles away. They will eat every shred of meat from a kill, and will crack bones to get at marrow.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Shadowcats tend to not attack living men unless starved.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Racok53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-2)(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Racok68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

## Origins

Some believe that in ancient days, the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) resided in "tree towns" high above the ground to avoid predators such as shadowcats, which their stone weapons and [greenseers](wiki:Greenseer) were not proof against.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf21.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
[Matteno Orthys](wiki:Matteno_Orthys) was a [Lyseni](wiki:Lys) who was a fervent worshipper of the cat-goddess [Pantera](wiki:Pantera) and kept a prized shadowcat. In [135 AC](wiki:135_AC), during the bloody power struggle in Lys after the downfall of the [Rogare family](wiki:House_Rogare), his shadowcat's cage was unaccountably left open one night, and the cat mauled and partly devoured Matteno.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Rfab23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
[Briar](wiki:Briar) was a [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) [skinchanger](wiki:Skinchanger) who controlled a shadowcat and inspired [Varamyr Sixskins](wiki:Varamyr_Sixskins) to control his own shadowcat, along with several other animals.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) The shadowcat fought Varaymyr wildly before being subdued.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Radwdprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) He then used his shadowcat to stalk women he wished to have sex with, forcing them to come to him.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Radwdprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1) Varamyr wears a shadowskin cloak.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-3)
When [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) was still a member of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), he and his brothers once hunted an elk, and the smell of blood attracted a shadowcat. The shadowcat attacked Mance, shredding his cloak, his arm, and his back. The Watch brought him to a local wildling village where a [wisewoman's daughter](wiki:Wisewoman's_daughter) healed him, and sewed up the tears in his cloak with red silk from [Asshai](wiki:Asshai). After Mance returned to the [Wall](wiki:Wall), Ser [Denys Mallister](wiki:Denys_Mallister) told him to burn his old cloak and replace it with an entirely black one. This infringement of his freedom caused Mance to abandon the Night's Watch.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

While riding along the [Trident](wiki:Trident), [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) track a shadowcat to its lair.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
Along the [high road](wiki:High_road) in the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon), [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) and her party (including the captive [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)) are worried about the shadowcats that live in the passes.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) The party is attacked by [Vale mountain clansmen](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans), led by a man wearing a shadowskin cloak.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6) After the battle with the clansmen, the survivors leave the corpses to be scavenged by shadowcats, despite Catelyn's objections.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7)
The singer [Marillion](wiki:Marillion) somehow acquires the dead chief's shadowskin cloak,(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8) but Tyrion wins the cloak from him at [dice](wiki:Dice).(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) In the [sky cells](wiki:Sky_cells) of the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), the gaoler [Mord](wiki:Mord) takes the cloak from Tyrion; but once Tyrion wins his freedom through [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat), he takes it back.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) Tyrion wears it for warmth while in the mountains after he leaves the Eyrie.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Ragot42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

When Tyrion arrives in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to take the role of acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), he is still wearing his shadowskin cloak.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) He wears it for warmth in the underground vaults of the [Guildhall of the Alchemists](wiki:Guildhall_of_the_Alchemists).(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
While making his way through the [Skirling Pass](wiki:Skirling_Pass) with [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand), [Dalbridge](wiki:Dalbridge), [Ebben](wiki:Ebben), and [Stonesnake](wiki:Stonesnake), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) watches a shadowcat stalk a ram.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) After Jon and Stonesnake kill two wildlings and capture [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte), the bodies of the slain are left for shadowcats to eat.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) Jon thinks a shadowcat would be a threat to his direwolf, [Ghost](wiki:Ghost).(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Racok53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-3) A half-starved shadowcat comes snarling from its lair and spooks Stonesnake's horse; she breaks a leg, and the [rangers](wiki:Rangers) have to kill her. [Ghost](wiki:Ghost) eats most of the horse, the men take some of the meat and leave the rest for the shadowcats.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Racok68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1) Later, Qhorin and Jon hide in a cave behind a waterfall. Qhorin tells Jon that when he was young, a brother of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) told him how he had followed a shadowcat through the falls.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Racok68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-2)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ghost becomes aggressive whenever he sees [Varamyr Sixskins](wiki:Varamyr_Sixskins)'s shadowcat.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2)
From the top of the [Wall](wiki:Wall), looking down into the wildlings' camp with Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I))'s [Myrish eye](wiki:Myrish_eye), Jon sees Varamyr walking through the trees with his shadowcat.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) When the wildling camp is attacked by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s army in the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), Varamyr's shadowcat prevents Jon from entering [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder)'s tent to search for the [Horn of Winter](wiki:Horn_of_Winter).(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-4) In the agony of the death of [his eagle](wiki:Orell), Varamyr loses control of his shadowcat, which races into the woods.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-5)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Varamyr Sixskins](wiki:Varamyr_Sixskins) remembers [Briar](wiki:Briar) and her shadowcat, as well as how he had used his own shadowcat to intimidate women. Varamyr discovers it is even more difficult to take control of a [human being](wiki:Thistle) than a shadowcat.(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Radwdprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-2)
Jon Snow remembers how Mance Rayder had compared some of the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) to shadowcats, saying they prowl "where they please and will tear your dogs to pieces."(wiki:Shadowcat.cite_ref-Radwd21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)
