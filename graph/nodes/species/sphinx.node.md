## Culture

In tales, sphinxes speak in riddles,(wiki:Sphinx.cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) including far-fetched stories of [dragons](wiki:Dragons) riddling with them.(wiki:Sphinx.cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Sphinx.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
The gates of the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) are flanked by a pair of towering green sphinxes(wiki:Sphinx.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) with eyes of [onyx](wiki:Onyx).(wiki:Sphinx.cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) They have the bodies of [lions](wiki:Lion), the wings of [eagles](wiki:Eagle), and the tails of [serpents](wiki:Serpent). One has a man's face, and the other a woman's.(wiki:Sphinx.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
The gateway to the [Long Bridge](wiki:Long_Bridge) in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) is a black stone arch carved with sphinxes, [manticores](wiki:Manticore), dragons, and other creatures.(wiki:Sphinx.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Statues of [Valyrian](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) sphinxes in the [Velvet Hills](wiki:Velvet_Hills) have human faces and the bodies of dragons.(wiki:Sphinx.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
The [small council](wiki:Small_council) chamber door in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) is flanked by a pair of Valyrian sphinxes with eyes of polished [garnets](wiki:Garnet) smoldering in black [marble](wiki:Marble) faces.(wiki:Sphinx.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Sphinx.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

As [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) enters the chambers of the [small council](wiki:Small_council) in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), he pauses to admire the [Valyrian](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) sphinxes at its entrance, and he rests his hand on the head of one of them.(wiki:Sphinx.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Alleras](wiki:Alleras), also called the Sphinx, is a novice studying at the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel).(wiki:Sphinx.cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) His dark eyes remind [Pate](wiki:Pate_(novice)) of the [onyx](wiki:Onyx)-eyed sphinxes at the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) gates,(wiki:Sphinx.cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) and Archmaester [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn) refers to him as a [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) sphinx.(wiki:Sphinx.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
[Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) uses plunder from half a hundred distant lands to bind the [ironmen](wiki:Ironmen) to him. Among the plunder are ancient Valyrian sphinxes.(wiki:Sphinx.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

While traveling in the [Velvet Hills](wiki:Velvet_Hills), Tyrion sees a huge Valyrian sphinx with a [dragon](wiki:Dragon)'s body and a woman's face crouched beside the road. [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) explains that the accompanying male sphinx was dragged off its nearby plinth and taken by the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) on wooden wheels to [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak).(wiki:Sphinx.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

In visions seen by [Aeron Damphair](wiki:Aeron_Damphair), after being forced by [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) to drink [shade of the evening](wiki:Shade_of_the_evening), Aeron sees Euron blow a [great horn](wiki:Dragonbinder), causing [dragons](wiki:Dragon), [krakens](wiki:Kraken), and sphinxes to come at his command and bow before him.(wiki:Sphinx.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Bthe_forsaken_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Cthe_forsaken.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
