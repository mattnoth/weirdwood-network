## Origins

[Old Nan](wiki:Old_Nan) has told stories about grumkins to the children of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark).(wiki:Grumkin.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Since [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) looks more like her half brother [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) than her trueborn siblings, her sister [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) once asked their mother, Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), if the real Arya had been taken by grumkins.(wiki:Grumkin.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
