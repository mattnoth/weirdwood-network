## Origins

The [greenseers](wiki:Greenseer) of the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) are said to have called upon snow bears to fight on their behalf.(wiki:Snow_bear.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf22.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
One of the animals controlled by the [skinchanger](wiki:Skinchanger) [Varamyr](wiki:Varamyr) is a female snow bear which he rides into battle.(wiki:Snow_bear.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:Snow_bear.cite_ref-Radwd0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) The bear hates Varamyr and rages each time he wears her skin or climbs upon her back. The bear used to fight him and had gone half mad for a time after he first skinchanged into her, snapping at trees and rocks and empty air.(wiki:Snow_bear.cite_ref-Radwd0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Varamyr Sixskins](wiki:Varamyr_Sixskins)'s steed is a savage white female snow bear that stands 13 feet tall on its hind legs. [Varamyr](wiki:Varamyr) rides past [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) on her, and Jon's [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) [Ghost](wiki:Ghost) is bothered by the presence of the bear.(wiki:Snow_bear.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)
During the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), [Varamyr](wiki:Varamyr) loses control over his beasts while in agony from the death of [Orell](wiki:Orell)'s [eagle](wiki:Eagle).(wiki:Snow_bear.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) The snow bear turns her claws on those around her, ripping apart four men before falling to a spear. She would have slain Varamyr had he come within her reach.(wiki:Snow_bear.cite_ref-Radwd0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After being defeated at the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), a ruined Varamyr Sixskins rues to himself that it was [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) who brought him to this place and that he should not have listened to him. Before Mance he had been a lord of sorts; he thinks that he should have slipped inside his snow bear and torn Mance to pieces.(wiki:Snow_bear.cite_ref-Radwd0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)
Outside the [cave of the last greenseer](wiki:Cave_of_the_last_greenseer), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) sees a snow bear crash through the brush, huge and [skeletal](wiki:Wight), half its head sloughed away to reveal the skull beneath. [Summer](wiki:Summer) and his pack fall upon it and tear it into pieces. Afterwards they gorge, though the meat is rotten, half-frozen, and moves even as they eat it.(wiki:Snow_bear.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
