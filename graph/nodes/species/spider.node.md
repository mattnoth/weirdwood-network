## Culture

The arms of [House Webber](wiki:House_Webber) of [Coldmoat](wiki:Coldmoat) depict a spotted spider on a silver web.(wiki:Spider.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Lady [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber) wore a cloak of silvery silk which resembled a spider's web, and her banner bearer carried a staff crowned by an [iron](wiki:Iron) spider painted white and red.(wiki:Spider.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Ser [Lucas Inchfield](wiki:Lucas_Inchfield), Rohanne's castellan, wore a helm decorated with an [onyx](wiki:Onyx) spider.(wiki:Spider.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
[Webber](wiki:Webber), a sellsword of the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown), has spiders [tattooed](wiki:Tattoo) across his head, chest, and arms.(wiki:Spider.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Some [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) warriors decorate their shields with painted images of [snakes](wiki:Snake) and spiders.(wiki:Spider.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Acolytes of a spider goddess are said to have lived in the lost city of [Lyber](wiki:Lyber).(wiki:Spider.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
[Spiderwood](wiki:Spiderwood) is a settlement in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Spider.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Origins

During the [Long Night](wiki:Long_Night), the [Others](wiki:Others) are said to have hunted the [last hero](wiki:Last_hero) with packs of [giant ice spiders](wiki:Giant_ice_spiders).(wiki:Spider.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) In another telling, the Others rode the ice spiders as mounts.(wiki:Spider.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_long_night.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Spider silk was worn by the riders of the [Kingdom of Sarnor](wiki:Kingdom_of_Sarnor).(wiki:Spider.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
[Tyanna of the Tower](wiki:Tyanna_of_the_Tower) is said to have been a [witch](wiki:Witch) who spoke with rats and spiders.(wiki:Spider.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Lady [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber) gave Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) an [ivory](wiki:Ivory) spider brooch with [silver](wiki:Silver) legs as a gift after he helped resolve her conflict with Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey).(wiki:Spider.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) recalls that the [First Keep](wiki:First_Keep) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) is now inhabited only by rats and spiders.(wiki:Spider.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) [Old Nan](wiki:Old_Nan) tells him a tale of the [Long Night](wiki:Long_Night), in which the [Others](wiki:Others) hunted the [last hero](wiki:Last_hero) with packs of [spiders big as hounds](wiki:Giant_ice_spiders).(wiki:Spider.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) While riding in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood), Bran sees the silvery web of an empress spider.(wiki:Spider.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) recalls Old Nan telling her that spiders live in the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Spider.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Bran finds cobwebs in the armory and the forge of the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort).(wiki:Spider.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Bran sees spiderwebs in the [cave of the three-eyed crow](wiki:Cave_of_the_three-eyed_crow).(wiki:Spider.cite_ref-Radwd13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) has heard that the abandoned castles of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) are full of rats and spiders, but [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), thinks that the cold has killed the spiders.(wiki:Spider.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) The rafters of the [Shieldhall](wiki:Shieldhall) have become festooned with cobwebs.(wiki:Spider.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
