## Culture

Some [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) consider ghouls to be rotting corpses;(wiki:Ghouls.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) it is unknown if they are similar to [wights](wiki:Wights).
According to [Old Nan](wiki:Old_Nan), [wildlings](wiki:Free_folk) consort with [giants](wiki:Giants) and ghouls [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:Ghouls.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Origins

[Yeen](wiki:Yeen) in [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos) is threatened by ghouls. When [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria) led the [Rhoynar](wiki:Ten_thousand_ships) to Sothoryos, the Rhoynish colony at Yeen was attacked by brindled ghouls.(wiki:Ghouls.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
[Rangers](wiki:Rangers) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) claim that the ruins of [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome) are haunted by ghouls, [demons](wiki:Demons), and bloodthirsty burning ghosts.(wiki:Ghouls.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
