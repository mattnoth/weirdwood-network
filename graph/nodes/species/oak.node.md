## Culture

Oak is used by multiple [Westerosi Houses](wiki:List_of_Houses) on their coat-of-arms; such as the leaves of [House Oakheart](wiki:House_Oakheart), the spoon of [House Hetherspoon](wiki:House_Hetherspoon), the [escutcheon](wiki:Escutcheon) of [House Hewett](wiki:House_Hewett), the barrels of [House Lolliston](wiki:House_Lolliston), the doors of [House Rhysling](wiki:House_Rhysling), the acorns of [House Smallwood](wiki:House_Smallwood), the saltire of [House Sparr](wiki:House_Sparr), the paly of [House Uffering](wiki:House_Uffering), and the field of [House Woodright](wiki:House_Woodright).(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-32)
[Owen Oakenshield](wiki:Owen_Oakenshield) and [John the Oak](wiki:John_the_Oak) were two sons of the mythological [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand). The [Oakhearts](wiki:House_Oakheart) of [Old Oak](wiki:Old_Oak) claim descent from the latter.(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0) Oakenshield is the name of an [abandoned castle](wiki:Oakenshield_(Wall)) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) along the [Wall](wiki:Wall), as well as the [castle](wiki:Oakenshield_Castle) of [House Hewett](wiki:House_Hewett) located in [Lord Hewett's Town](wiki:Lord_Hewett's_Town) on the [Oakenshield island](wiki:Oakenshield).(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)
[House Yew](wiki:House_Yew) in the [Westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) claim descent from [Alan o' the Oak](wiki:Alan_o'_the_Oak), a legendary figure from the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes).(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)
[Ironoaks](wiki:Ironoaks) and [Acorn Hall](wiki:Acorn_Hall) are the seats of Houses [Waynwood](wiki:House_Waynwood) and [Smallwood](wiki:House_Smallwood) respectively.(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0)(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0) The [Acorn Water](wiki:Acorn_Water) is a small river near Winterfell.(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0)
Outside of the [North](wiki:North), great oaks can be used in place of [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) as [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree) in [godswoods](wiki:Godswood), such as at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Ragot25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0)
Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) owns a tunic with a spreading oak tree worked in shining gold thread.(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The lands of [House Umber](wiki:House_Umber) are known for their forests of tall pine and old oak.(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0)
The tree that gives the village of [Pennytree](wiki:Pennytree) its name is an ancient and tall oak.(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-1)

## Origins

According to legend, the [Oakenseat](wiki:Oakenseat) was carved from an oak planted by [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand) himself.(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_the_gardener_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0) The [Gardener](wiki:House_Gardener) kings used it as a throne until its destruction by [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) invaders who sacked [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) late in the reign of King [Garth X Gardener](wiki:Garth_X_Gardener).(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0)
[First Men](wiki:First_Men) made their homes beneath ancient and tall trees such as oaks.(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_the_coming_of_the_first_men.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0)
Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) earned the nickname Oakenfist after he [rammed and sank](wiki:Lord_Alyn's_attack) the [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) dromond Grand Defiance strucking its side "like a great oaken fist".(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0) The Oakenfist's [squire](wiki:Russell_Stillman) later wrote a biography on him titled [Hard as Oak](wiki:Hard_as_Oak).(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0)
Ser [Olyvar Oakheart](wiki:Olyvar_Oakheart) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) is remembered as the Green Oak.(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-0)
Ser [Arlan of Pennytree](wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree) was buried on a hill near an oak tree.(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

During the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) sits in the oak chair reserved for the [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-1)
The [three great trebuchets](wiki:Three_Whores) built inside the [Mud Gate](wiki:Mud_Gate) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) prior to the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater) are made of old oaks.(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lady [Ravella Smallwood](wiki:Ravella_Smallwood) gives a dress paterned with the acorns of her house to [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark). Arya first complains she looks like an oak tree and inadvertently tears the dress but she later apologizes to Lady Smallwood and compliments her gift.(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_48-0)
During the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), the brother of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) throw big oak barrels full of frozen gravel from atop the [Wall](wiki:Wall) onto the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk)'s [turtle](wiki:Turtle_(siege_engine)).(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_49-0)
[Petyr](wiki:Petyr_Frey) and [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey) are hanged from an oak tree by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners).(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Rasos81.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_50-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

At the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) presents oak chests full of pebbles, pinecones, and turnips to the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) captains to show them how little they have gained from the raidings in the north.(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_51-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) settled near [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town) carves angry faces on oak trees.(wiki:Oak.cite_ref-Radwd21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_52-0)
