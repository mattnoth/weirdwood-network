## Identity

Ice dragons are creatures of legend told in Old Nan's stories at Winterfell. They are described as enormous creatures of living ice, exhaling cold rather than fire. The Wall at Castle Black is described as having tunnels "cold as the belly of an ice dragon." Old Nan used to tell tales of ice dragons to the Stark children. Whether they are mythical or once-real creatures is ambiguous in the text.
