## Culture

Three black scorpions are included in the arms of [House Qorgyle](wiki:House_Qorgyle) of [Sandstone](wiki:Sandstone).(wiki:Scorpion.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Origins

After the [Submission of Sunspear](wiki:Submission_of_Sunspear) during his [conquest of Dorne](wiki:Conquest_of_Dorne), King [Daeron I Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen) appointed Lord [Lyonel Tyrell](wiki:Lyonel_Tyrell_(lord)) to rule [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) in his absence. Lyonel was killed at [Sandstone](wiki:Sandstone) when a hundred red scorpions were dropped on him from a canopy above his bed, and his assassination was the signal for Dorne to rise in rebellion against the Young Dragon.(wiki:Scorpion.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_house_tyrell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Scorpion.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
