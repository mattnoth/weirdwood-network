## Appearances & Description

Wyverns have great leathery wings, "cruel" beaks, and an insatiable hunger. They are more ferocious than [dragons](wiki:Dragon), if smaller in size. Wyverns are called "the tyrants of the southern skies", and are one of the reasons why [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos) is thinly populated.(wiki:Wyvern.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf834_2-1)
Varieties of wyverns include brindled wyverns, swamp wyverns, brownbellies, and shadow-wings. Brindled wyverns have green-and-white scales, and grow up to thirty feet long. Swamp wyverns may grow larger than that, but they are mostly inactive and rarely fly far from their lairs. Brownbellies, though not much bigger than [monkeys](wiki:Monkey), hunt in packs of a hundred or more, and so are far more dangerous than larger breeds. The shadow-wing wyvern is nocturnal, with black scales and wings that make it invisible in the dark, and therefore is the most feared.(wiki:Wyvern.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf834_2-2)

## Culture

[Wyvern Point](wiki:Wyvern_Point) in northern [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos) is named after the creature, which hunts above its swamps and jungles.(wiki:Wyvern.cite_ref-Rawoiafwyvern_point.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
In [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), the sigil of [House Vyrwel](wiki:House_Vyrwel) of [Darkdell](wiki:Darkdell) depicts a wyvern.(wiki:Wyvern.cite_ref-4) The castle of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) includes wyverns among its stone [gargoyles](wiki:Gargoyle).(wiki:Wyvern.cite_ref-Racok0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Wyvern.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Origins

Septon [Barth](wiki:Barth) wrote about wyverns in [Dragons, Wyrms, and Wyverns: Their Unnatural History](wiki:Dragons,_Wyrms,_and_Wyverns:_Their_Unnatural_History),(wiki:Wyvern.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and he speculated that [bloodmages](wiki:Bloodmages) of [Valyria](wiki:Valyria) may have created [dragons](wiki:Dragons) using wyvern stock.(wiki:Wyvern.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf834_2-3)
In [56 AC](wiki:56_AC) a report came to the [small council](wiki:Small_council) of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) from one of Lord [Rego Draz](wiki:Rego_Draz)'s agents, which claimed that a dragon was being displayed in the fighting pits of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor) on [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay). It was at the same time when King Jaehaerys was looking for [Balerion](wiki:Balerion), on whom [Aerea Targaryen](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen) had escaped [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) two years earlier. Barth dismissed these claims, however, declaring that it was without a doubt a wyvern mistaken for a [dragon](wiki:Dragon) by people who had never seen a dragon.(wiki:Wyvern.cite_ref-Rfab10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
