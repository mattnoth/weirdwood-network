## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

When [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) returns to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) and her khalasar in [Vaes Tolorro](wiki:Vaes_Tolorro), he brings [Pyat Pree](wiki:Pyat_Pree), [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos), and [Quaithe](wiki:Quaithe). The three strangers are all riding camels, creatures Daenerys does not recognize.(wiki:Camel.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
A [column](wiki:Column) of camelry emerge from [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) as Daenerys's honor guards upon her arrival outside of the city of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth). The camelry riders wear scaled copper armor and snouted helms with [copper](wiki:Copper) tusks and long black silk plumes. They sit high on saddles inlaid with [rubies](wiki:Rubies) and [garnets](wiki:Garnet). Their camels are dressed in blankets of a hundred different hues.(wiki:Camel.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Daenerys discovers that horses cannot abide the close presence of camels. As she rides [her silver](wiki:Her_silver) down a great arcade her mare shies when Xaro and his camel approach her.(wiki:Camel.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Envoys from [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) come to parlay with Daenerys when she comes to conquer their city. Fifty men arrive on magnificent black horses while [Grazdan mo Eraz](wiki:Grazdan_mo_Eraz) rides a great white camel.(wiki:Camel.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

During the wedding feast of Daenerys and [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) in the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), a dozen sorts of meat and fish is served, amongst them camel.(wiki:Camel.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
A corps of Qartheen camelry join the Yunkai'i marching up the coast road to [lay siege to Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen).(wiki:Camel.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
