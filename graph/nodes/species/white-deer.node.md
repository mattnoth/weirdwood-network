## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

After a white hart is sighted in the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood), a royal hunt is organized, with King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) being accompanied by Lord [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), Prince [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane), Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann), and half the court.(wiki:White_deer.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [Wolves](wiki:Wolf) get to the white hart first, however, which angers Robert. The king then ventures deeper into the kingswood after hearing word of a monstrous [boar](wiki:Boar).(wiki:White_deer.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
