## Origins

According to legend, [Serwyn of the Mirror Shield](wiki:Serwyn_of_the_Mirror_Shield) killed Urrax. Serwyn sneaked up on Urrax, hiding behind his shield which was a mirror, so the dragon only saw its reflection. Serwyn then killed him by plunging a spear though his eye.(wiki:Urrax.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

When trying to get the measure of [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), [Haldon](wiki:Haldon) questions him as to how [Serwyn](wiki:Serwyn) of the Mirror Shield slew the dragon Urrax. He is unimpressed with Tyrion's correct answer, and asserts that even [Rolly Duckfield](wiki:Rolly_Duckfield) knows that tale.(wiki:Urrax.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
