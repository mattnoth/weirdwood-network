## Origins

[Chestnut](wiki:Chestnut) was the name of a horse owned by first Ser [Arlan of Pennytree](wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree) and then by Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall).(wiki:Chestnut_tree.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Ser [Glendon Ball](wiki:Glendon_Ball) was sitting against a chestnut tree when he first met Duncan.(wiki:Chestnut_tree.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Roasted chestnuts were served during the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls).(wiki:Chestnut_tree.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
[Three-Finger Hobb](wiki:Three-Finger_Hobb) once chopped up prunes, carrots, and chestnuts and cooked them in a hen.(wiki:Chestnut_tree.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) found secret hiding places among the chestnuts, [elms](wiki:Elm), and [soldier pines](wiki:Soldier_pine) of the [godswood of Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Chestnut_tree.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Bronn](wiki:Bronn) hones the edge of his sword beneath a chestnut tree prior to the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:Chestnut_tree.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

During the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) sees a lightning-blasted chestnut tree overgrown with wild white [roses](wiki:Rose) in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest).(wiki:Chestnut_tree.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) finds [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard) by a chestnut tree in the [godswood of the Red Keep](wiki:Godswood_of_the_Red_Keep).(wiki:Chestnut_tree.cite_ref-Racok52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
A creamy chestnut soup is part of the meal enjoyed by [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), and Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Chestnut_tree.cite_ref-Racok54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) sees stands of [beech](wiki:Beech), [oak](wiki:Oak), and chestnut on bluffs overlooking the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork).(wiki:Chestnut_tree.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
While sacking a small [sept](wiki:Sept), the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) hang a [septon](wiki:Septon) upside down from a spreading chestnut tree and use him for target practice.(wiki:Chestnut_tree.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Leo Tyrell](wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn)) has suckling [pig](wiki:Pig) in [plum](wiki:Plum) sauce, stuffed with chestnuts and white truffles, at the [Checkered Hazard](wiki:Checkered_Hazard).(wiki:Chestnut_tree.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) and her retinue roast chestnuts in the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood).(wiki:Chestnut_tree.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Stately chestnuts are among the trees near the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn) from which the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) hang their victims.(wiki:Chestnut_tree.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) While [Brienne Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth) is being brought by the brotherhood to meet with [Lady Stoneheart](wiki:Lady_Stoneheart), Brienne sees a horse rooting for chestnuts.(wiki:Chestnut_tree.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
