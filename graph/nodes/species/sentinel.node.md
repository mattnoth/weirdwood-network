## Culture

Three green sentinel trees are used on the coat-of-arms of [House Tallhart](wiki:House_Tallhart).(wiki:Sentinel.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) [Sentinel Stand](wiki:Sentinel_Stand) is an abandoned castle of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) along the [Wall](wiki:Wall); it is unknown if it is named after the tree.

## Origins

[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) once slept overnight in the tallest sentinel in the [godswood of Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Sentinel.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) lost his virginity when he slept with a woman beneath the tree.(wiki:Sentinel.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

While ranging [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall), [Will](wiki:Will) climbs a sentinel and observes Ser [Waymar Royce](wiki:Waymar_Royce) being slain by [Others](wiki:Others).(wiki:Sentinel.cite_ref-Ragotprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

During the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell), [Summer](wiki:Summer) climbs a tall sentinel in a failed attempt to escape the [castle's godswood](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Sentinel.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) passes sentinels in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) during his search for the missing [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark).(wiki:Sentinel.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Jarl](wiki:Jarl) begins his attempted ascent of the [Wall](wiki:Wall) by climbing a sentinel.(wiki:Sentinel.cite_ref-Rasos30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
