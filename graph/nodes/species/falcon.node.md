## Culture

Falcons are especially prevalent near the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) and the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance).(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf742_3-0)(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The sigil of [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn), [Lords of the Eyrie](wiki:Lords_of_the_Eyrie), is a sky-blue falcon outlined against a white [moon](wiki:Moon) on a sky-blue field.(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf741_6-0)(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Rssm1023.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn)'s favorite bird was a falcon gifted to him by King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon),(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) and his longsword has a falcon-head pommel.(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Ser [Vardis Egen](wiki:Vardis_Egen), captain of the [House Arryn guards](wiki:House_Arryn_guards), has falcon's wings on his helm, the visor of which resembles a beak.(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) The Falcon Tower is one of the towers of the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon).(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Balayne_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Calayne_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
The mews of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) are said to be haunted by the spirits of dead falcons.(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Origins

According to legend, the [Winged Knight](wiki:Winged_Knight) flew on a huge falcon to the top of the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance), where he then defeated the [Griffin King](wiki:Griffin_King).(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Archmaester [Perestan](wiki:Perestan) suggests this to be a distorted memory of [dragonriders](wiki:Dragonrider), however.(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf741_6-1)
Falcon's wings decorated the silver warhelm of Ser [Artys Arryn](wiki:Artys_Arryn), the Falcon Knight.(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf74.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) The [Kings of Mountain and Vale](wiki:Kings_of_Mountain_and_Vale) from [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) wore the [Falcon Crown](wiki:Falcon_Crown).(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) King [Osgood Arryn](wiki:Osgood_Arryn) was known as the Old Falcon.(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
[Isembard Arryn](wiki:Isembard_Arryn), a merchant from [House Arryn of Gulltown](wiki:House_Arryn_of_Gulltown), became known as the Gilded Falcon after men joked that the falcon on his sigil was made of [gold](wiki:Gold).(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Rfab23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
Young [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) kept a gyrfalcon named [Thunderclap](wiki:Thunderclap).(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

A potboy tells [Jory Cassel](wiki:Jory_Cassel) that Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) had been considering commissioning plate wrought in pale [silver](wiki:Silver) with a blue [jasper](wiki:Jasper) falcon and a [mother-of-pearl](wiki:Mother-of-pearl) [moon](wiki:Moon) on the breast. When Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) asks [Tobho Mott](wiki:Tobho_Mott) if he made a falcon helm for Lord Arryn, the [smith](wiki:Smith) explains that Jon and Lord [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) did not patronize his work, but instead were interested in one of his apprentices.(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
In mourning for the death of her husband, Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) wears black silk with a moon-and-falcon sewn in pearls.(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

When they go hawking in the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood), [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark)'s merlin catches three ducks, and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell)'s peregrine takes a heron in full flight.(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
The [wedding chalice](wiki:Wedding_chalice) given by Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) has a falcon of blue [jade](wiki:Jade) to represent [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn).(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
Lysa gives Jon's favorite falcon to [Marillion](wiki:Marillion).(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

While looking upon the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance) from the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), Sansa sees a blue-winged falcon soaring above the frozen waterfall of [Alyssa's Tears](wiki:Alyssa's_Tears).(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
After the court of Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn), the Young Falcon, departs the Eyrie for the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon), [Mord](wiki:Mord) butchers the winch oxen and leaves them for the falcons.(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

Each competitor in the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) to choose the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights) receives a cloak of [cloth-of-silver](wiki:Cloth-of-silver) and a [lapis](wiki:Lapis) brooch in the shape of a pair of falcon's wings. The eight victors are to receive warhelms decorated with falcon's wings.(wiki:Falcon.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Balayne_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Calayne_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
