## Culture

Eagles are one of the birds of prey employed in falconry.(wiki:Eagle.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
[House Mallister](wiki:House_Mallister) has an eagle in their sigil, and the Mallisters also control the [Cape of Eagles](wiki:Cape_of_Eagles). [House Condon](wiki:House_Condon) includes an eagle's head in their sigil, while double-headed eagles are included in the sigils of Houses [Estren](wiki:House_Estren) and [Graves](wiki:House_Graves).
Lord [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister) decorates his helm with eagle wings.(wiki:Eagle.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Some [skinchangers](wiki:Skinchangers) can enter eagles.(wiki:Eagle.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) For instance, [Orell](wiki:Orell) controls an eagle with great blue-grey wings(wiki:Eagle.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and golden eyes.(wiki:Eagle.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Origins

The [wood dancers](wiki:Wood_dancer) of the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) are said to have called upon eagles to fight on their behalf.(wiki:Eagle.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf22.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

While traveling through the [Skirling Pass](wiki:Skirling_Pass), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) sees eagles nesting in the heights and hunting in the valleys of the [Frostfangs](wiki:Frostfangs).(wiki:Eagle.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) [Orell](wiki:Orell), a [skinchanger](wiki:Skinchanger) of the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk), spies with an eagle, but he is killed by Jon.(wiki:Eagle.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) The bird remains with [Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt)'s band(wiki:Eagle.cite_ref-Racok68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and gashes Jon's [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), [Ghost](wiki:Ghost).(wiki:Eagle.cite_ref-Racok53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Part of Orell's consciousness lives on within his eagle, which attacks Jon.(wiki:Eagle.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) The [skinchanger](wiki:Skinchanger) [Varamyr](wiki:Varamyr) takes control of Orell's eagle, but he is later almost driven mad when [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) kills the bird during the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall) by burning it.(wiki:Eagle.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Eagle.cite_ref-Radwd0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Lord [Ardrian Celtigar](wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar) is said to have a trained sea eagle.(wiki:Eagle.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
