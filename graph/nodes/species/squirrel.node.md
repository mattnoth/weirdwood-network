## Culture

A squirrel is included in the arms of [House Wythers](wiki:House_Wythers).(wiki:Squirrel.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Squirrel fur can be used in clothing. Squirrel fur sewn together in a grey-and-white pattern is called vair, which also is used as a tincture in [heraldry](wiki:Heraldry).
[A Liddle](wiki:The_Liddle) wears a squirrelskin cloak,(wiki:Squirrel.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and [Dick Crabb](wiki:Dick_Crabb) wears a cloak lined with squirrel skin.(wiki:Squirrel.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Maester [Colemon](wiki:Colemon) wears a threadbare grey cloak lined with squirrel fur.(wiki:Squirrel.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
The mother of [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah) has a dress with a squirrel collar which she wears annually when [bears](wiki:Bear) dance down the [Sinner's Steps](wiki:Sinner's_Steps) of [Norvos](wiki:Norvos).(wiki:Squirrel.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
One of the [spearwives](wiki:Spearwives) of the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) is called [Squirrel](wiki:Squirrel_(free_folk)) because of her ability to climb.(wiki:Squirrel.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
[Geoff](wiki:Geoff), a [ranger](wiki:Ranger) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), is called the Squirrel.(wiki:Squirrel.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Origins

[Giants](wiki:Giants) referred to the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) as woh dak nag gran, the squirrel people, because they were small and quick and fond of trees.(wiki:Squirrel.cite_ref-Radwd13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) has stalked squirrels in the [godswood of Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell) with a bow he made himself.(wiki:Squirrel.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) once promised his mother [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) that he would no longer climb the walls of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) because she feared for his safety, but this only lasted two weeks before he was climbing again. His father, [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), had Bran stay in the godswood overnight to contemplate his misbehavior, but the next morning Bran was found fast asleep in the tallest [sentinel](wiki:Sentinel) tree. However, Eddard only laughed and compared Bran to a squirrel, and allowed him to climb if he must, but to try not to be seen by his mother.(wiki:Squirrel.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
