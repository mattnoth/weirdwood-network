## Appearances & Description

[Naath](wiki:Naath), the Isle of Butterflies in the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea), is said to have a hundred varieties of the insect. Some believe that [butterfly fever](wiki:Butterfly_fever) is caused by a specific type of butterfly, with Archmaester [Ebrose](wiki:Ebrose) suggesting a large black-and-white variety with wings as large as a man's hand.(wiki:Butterfly.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_naath.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Butterflies are active in the day, enjoying the morning dew and the afternoon sun.(wiki:Butterfly.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_naath.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Culture

A cloud of black-and-orange butterflies appear in the arms of [House Mullendore](wiki:House_Mullendore);(wiki:Butterfly.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Ser [Mark Mullendore](wiki:Mark_Mullendore) has been called the butterfly knight.(wiki:Butterfly.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
The [Naathi](wiki:Naathi) worship the [Lord of Harmony](wiki:Lord_of_Harmony), who is said to be attended by swarms of slender maidens with butterfly wings.(wiki:Butterfly.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The wooden hall of the Lord of Harmony in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) has windows of leaded glass which show half a hundred kinds of brightly-colored butterflies.(wiki:Butterfly.cite_ref-Radwd64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
A [pillow house](wiki:Pillow_house) in [Lys](wiki:Lys) has Naathi girls who wear diaphanous silken gowns and gaily-painted butterfly wings.(wiki:Butterfly.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_naath.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Origins

Before their abduction from [Naath](wiki:Naath), [Missandei](wiki:Missandei) once found her brother [Mossador](wiki:Mossador) asleep in their garden with a hundred butterflies crawling on him.(wiki:Butterfly.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
