## Origins

In [56 AC](wiki:56_AC), when planning to travel west of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) to discover new lands, [Alys Westhill](wiki:Alys_Westhill) chose to avoid the northern Sunset Sea and its many dangers, including island-sized leviathans, and decided on a more southerly route for the journey of her [Sun Chaser](wiki:Sun_Chaser).(wiki:Leviathan.cite_ref-Rfab10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)
Around [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), while returning to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) from accompanying Alys Westhill's journey in the Sunset Sea, Ser [Eustace Hightower](wiki:Eustace_Hightower)'s [Lady Meredith](wiki:Lady_Meredith) encountered a pod of leviathans. One of them, a huge white bull larger than a ship, deliberately slammed into the Lady Meredith and cracked her hull. Eustace redirected for the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles), but was further south than he realized, and the ship ended up in [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos).(wiki:Leviathan.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
