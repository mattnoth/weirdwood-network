## Culture

The Jogos Nhai drink [fermented zorse milk](wiki:Fermented_milk).(wiki:Zorse.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf842_2-3) The outermost of the triple walls of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) is decorated with pictures of animals, including zorses.(wiki:Zorse.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Zorse hides are considered to be a fine trade good.(wiki:Zorse.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

In the Eastern Market of [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) sees the striped black and white horses of the [Jogos Nhai](wiki:Jogos_Nhai).(wiki:Zorse.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

While at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) witnesses the arrival of the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) and sees that some of them ride strange black-and-white horses, including their captain, [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat).(wiki:Zorse.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
In [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), a pair of Jogos Nhai present Daenerys with one of their striped zorses, as a token to the Mother of Dragons. Dany sells the zorse, along with other gifts received in Qarth, in order to gather wealth needed to petition the [Pureborn](wiki:Pureborn).(wiki:Zorse.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

A mile from the gates of Harrenhal, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) are tied to the pommel of Vargo Hoat's saddle so that he can parade Jaime into the castle. Jaime and Brienne stumble along side by side behind the Qohorik's striped zorse.(wiki:Zorse.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
