## Culture

A domesticated species, cats can be kept as pets by humans.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Quiet, quick, and graceful,(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) they are often used to hunt mice(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and keep buildings free of vermin.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Some cultures believe that cats bring good luck,(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) although [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) may believe that black cats bring bad luck.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Scrawny cats skulk about [Illyrio's manse](wiki:Illyrio's_manse) in [Pentos](wiki:Pentos).(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) is full of cats,(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) with many congregating at [Pynto](wiki:Pynto)'s.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) One of the brothels in Braavosi is named the [Cattery](wiki:Cattery).(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
[Pantera](wiki:Pantera) is a six-breasted cat goddess worshiped in [Lys](wiki:Lys).(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
According to a semi-canon source, a cat's face is included in the arms of [House Lyberr](wiki:House_Lyberr).(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-11)
[Rats and cats](wiki:Rats_and_cats) is a game played by children.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
[Cat](wiki:Cat_(galley)) is a galley in the [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet).(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
The [Company of the Cat](wiki:Company_of_the_Cat) is a [free company](wiki:Free_company) in [Essos](wiki:Essos).(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) The [Spotted Cat](wiki:Spotted_Cat) is a famed [pit fighter](wiki:Pit_fighter) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully) refers to his eldest daughter, [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), as his little cat.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) is fond of kittens.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

## Origins

The notorious [Maegor the Cruel](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) supposedly butchered one of the cats at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) when his mother, Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen), first gave him a sword when he was three.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) His niece, Princess [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)), however, enjoyed feeding the castle's cats.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1)
Because the [Shivers](wiki:Shivers) was believed to have been spread by rats, cats became popular throughout the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
Princess [Saera Targaryen](wiki:Saera_Targaryen) was able to persuade her father, King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), to give her a variety of animals, including a kitten. Saera often sneaked cats into the bedchamber of Princess [Daella Targaryen](wiki:Daella_Targaryen), since her sister was afraid of the animals after a beloved kitten scratched her.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1)
After King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) ordered the hanging of all ratcatchers in King's Landing in reaction to [Blood and Cheese](wiki:Blood_and_Cheese), Ser [Otto Hightower](wiki:Otto_Hightower) brought a hundred cats to the Red Keep to serve as mousers.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) The lonely Queen [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen) enjoyed kittens.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) The contradictory [Racallio Ryndoon](wiki:Racallio_Ryndoon) is said to have loved kittens as well, while hating cats.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) Because cats were often seen entering and exiting the chambers of [Larra Rogare](wiki:Larra_Rogare), a worshiper of [Pantera](wiki:Pantera), it was rumored that they were spies who purred secrets to her in soft voices. Some tales claimed that Larra could even transform into a cat.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)
King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) believed that his older brother, Prince [Aerion Targaryen](wiki:Aerion_Targaryen), threw his cat down a well when they were young.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
Ser [Kyle](wiki:Kyle) called himself the Cat of Misty Moor.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)
The [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos) tested [Syrio Forel](wiki:Syrio_Forel) by asking the [water dancer](wiki:Water_dancer) about his pet cat, which the Sealord claimed to have come from a distant island. The observant Syrio, however, noticed that it was not an exotic female cat as the Sealord claimed, but just a common male tomcat.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Princess [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Rhaegar)) had a black kitten she named [Balerion](wiki:Balerion_(cat)), after the [fearsome dragon](wiki:Balerion).(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
Prince [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) once used his dagger to open the belly of a pregnant kitchen cat to show the kittens within to his father, King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon); the angry king responded by striking his son so hard that two baby teeth were knocked out.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) A [black tomcat](wiki:Balerion_(cat)) once stole a roast quail served to Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) at a dinner with King Robert.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) The tom also once clawed Joffrey's hand.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Syrio Forel](wiki:Syrio_Forel) tasks [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) with catching cats in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) as part of her training in the style of the [water dance](wiki:Water_dance).(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Arya finds a mother cat with a litter of kittens in the [Gods Eye town](wiki:Gods_Eye_town).(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) She later sees a grey and white cat during her escape from [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)
The [black tomcat](wiki:Balerion_(cat)) of the Red Keep rubs against [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark)'s leg as she makes her way to the [godswood](wiki:Godswood).(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
Angry after learning of the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross), King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) shoots a yellow cat with a crossbow.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

With her mother, [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), in mind, as well as her experience chasing cats in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Arya takes the name Cat of the Canals while learning from the [Faceless Men](wiki:Faceless_Men) in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) Arya enjoys feeding the cats of the city.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)
Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) gives her new husband, King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), three black kittens,(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) which the boy names [Boots](wiki:Boots), [Lady Whiskers](wiki:Lady_Whiskers), and [Ser Pounce](wiki:Ser_Pounce).
Striped [tiger](wiki:Tiger) pelts and the skins of spotted cats are among the treasures offered by [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) at the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

A cat follows the blind Arya from [Pynto's](wiki:Pynto's) back to the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White), and she is able to see through its eyes as a [skinchanger](wiki:Skinchanger).(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)
[Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) wears the cat mask of a [Brazen Beast](wiki:Brazen_Beast) while meeting with Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy).(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)
Someone throws a dead cat at [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) during her [walk of atonement](wiki:Walk_of_atonement) through the streets of King's Landing.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Radwd65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)
Tommen feeds morsels from his plate to his kittens. The black tomcat visits his chamber's window at night, but Ser Pounce hisses and scares the tom away.(wiki:Cat.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-1)
