## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) likes the tiny sparrows which nest between the cracks of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Sparrow.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

When [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) tries to escape [Harwin](wiki:Harwin) on horseback in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), sparrows fly from the branches of an alder.(wiki:Sparrow.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lowly members of the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) begin calling themselves [sparrows](wiki:Sparrows) as they march toward [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing),(wiki:Sparrow.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and they force the [Most Devout](wiki:Most_Devout) to choose their leader, the so-called [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow), as the next [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon).(wiki:Sparrow.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Varamyr](wiki:Varamyr), a [skinchanger](wiki:Skinchanger), briefly takes possession of a sparrow before ending up in [One Eye](wiki:One_Eye).(wiki:Sparrow.cite_ref-Radwd0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
