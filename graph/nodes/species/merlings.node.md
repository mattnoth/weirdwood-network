## Origins

Legends hold that the [Grey King](wiki:Grey_King) of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) wed a mermaid and became king of the western isles and all [the sea beyond](wiki:Sunset_Sea).(wiki:Merlings.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) The [ironmen](wiki:Ironmen) believe that in the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God)'s watery halls there are mermaids to attend to every want. The priests of the Drowned God claim the ironmen are closer kin to fish and merlings than other humans.(wiki:Merlings.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
The [Merling King](wiki:Merling_King_(god)) is worshipped as a god; there is a statue of the deity in the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White) in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos). The merlings are said to hail their lord by blowing into seashells.(wiki:Merlings.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) [Ursula Upcliff](wiki:Ursula_Upcliff) called herself the bride of the Merling King.(wiki:Merlings.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) According to legends of [House Velaryon](wiki:House_Velaryon), they received their [Driftwood Throne](wiki:Driftwood_Throne) from the Merling King to conclude a pact.(wiki:Merlings.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
The [Winged Knight](wiki:Winged_Knight) is said to have been friends with merlings.(wiki:Merlings.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale:_house_arryn.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) The legend of [Owen Oakenshield](wiki:Owen_Oakenshield) claims he drove the merlings into the sea.(wiki:Merlings.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Maester [Theron](wiki:Theron) argues that the [Deep Ones](wiki:Deep_Ones) are the origin of merling legends.(wiki:Merlings.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) According to legend, the [mazemakers](wiki:Mazemakers) of [Lorath](wiki:Lorath) were wiped out by a maritime enemy, possibly merlings.(wiki:Merlings.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_lorath.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
[Patchface](wiki:Patchface) mentions merfolk in some of his cryptic jingles that may have prophetic meaning. According to him the merwives wear nennymoans in their hair and weave gowns of silver seaweed. The mermaids of the [Shivering Sea](wiki:Shivering_Sea) are said to have pale skin and black-scaled tails, and to be far more dangerous than mermaids of southern seas.(wiki:Merlings.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_shivering_sea.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

## Origins

Some claimed that the [Mermaid](wiki:Mermaid_(pillow_house)), the [pillow house](wiki:Pillow_house) of [Roggerio Rogare](wiki:Roggerio_Rogare) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), contained an actual mermaid.(wiki:Merlings.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
[Roro Uhoris](wiki:Roro_Uhoris) claimed that he could tell one port from another just by the way they smelled. Cities were like women, he insisted, as each one had its own unique scent. [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) was sharp, salty, and a little fishy like a mermaid.(wiki:Merlings.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
What happened to [Patchface](wiki:Patchface) during the two days after the [Windproud](wiki:Windproud) had broken up is unknown, but the fisherfolk like to say that "a mermaid had taught him to breathe water in return for his seed."(wiki:Merlings.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

When [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), tells [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) of fisherfolk having seen [white walkers](wiki:Others) near [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea), a disbelieving Tyrion replies that the fisherfolk of [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) often claim of having glimpsed merlings.(wiki:Merlings.cite_ref-Ragot21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

In [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), [Armen](wiki:Armen) wagers with [Mollander](wiki:Mollander) that down at the docks they can find sailors who tell of mermaids they have bedded.(wiki:Merlings.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)
The [Merling Queen](wiki:Merling_Queen) is a [courtesan](wiki:Courtesan) in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos). She is never seen without her "[Mermaids](wiki:Mermaids_(Merling_Queen's))" to hold her train and do her hair.(wiki:Merlings.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
