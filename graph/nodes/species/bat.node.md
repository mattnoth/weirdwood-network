## Culture

The sigil of [House Lothston](wiki:House_Lothston) of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) depicted a black bat. More than half a century after the Lothston's downfall, their arms are still considerate to be of ill repute. The Lothston's successor, [House Whent](wiki:House_Whent), also depicts bats on their sigil.(wiki:Bat.cite_ref-5)(wiki:Bat.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
In Westeros, the first [hour of the night](wiki:Named_hours) after the sun is set is called the hour of the bat.(wiki:Bat.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Ser [Raymond Rychard](wiki:Raymond_Rychard) depicted a red bat on his [heraldry](wiki:Heraldry).(wiki:Bat.cite_ref-Rthk.5B.5Bthe_hedge_knight_.28comics.29.7Cgraphic_novel.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Ser [Oswell Whent](wiki:Oswell_Whent) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) wore a black bat spreading its wings on his white helm.(wiki:Bat.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
A deity looking like a pregnant woman with swollen breasts and a bat's head is among the many gods [worshipped](wiki:Religion) in [Pentos](wiki:Pentos).(wiki:Bat.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
[Harpies](wiki:Harpies) have wings of a bat or a [dragon](wiki:Dragon) instead of arms.(wiki:Bat.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Origins

Notable events that occurred during the hour of the bat include Septon [Barth](wiki:Barth) announcing the death of Princess [Aerea Targaryen](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen) in [56 AC](wiki:56_AC),(wiki:Bat.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) and King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) being found dead by a servant in [129 AC](wiki:129_AC). King Viserys was fond of drinking [hippocras](wiki:Hippocras) at this hour.(wiki:Bat.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
[Danelle Lothston](wiki:Danelle_Lothston), [Lady of Harrenhal](wiki:Lady_of_Harrenhal), was rumored to practice the [black arts](wiki:Magic). One story claims she sent giant bats out of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) on [moonless](wiki:Moon) night to take the bad children back to her.(wiki:Bat.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Grenn](wiki:Grenn) mocks [Pyp](wiki:Pypar) comparing his large ears to those of bats.(wiki:Bat.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) According to [Mirri Maz Duur](wiki:Mirri_Maz_Duur), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s stillborn son, [Rhaego](wiki:Rhaego), is born with leather wings reminiscent of a bat.(wiki:Bat.cite_ref-Ragot68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After the death of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) at his [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding), a rumor spreads that the missing [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) used magic to transform herself into a [wolf](wiki:Wolf) with bat wings in order to escape [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Bat.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When meeting with Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) in secret, [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) wears a blood bat mask of the [Brazen Beasts](wiki:Brazen_Beasts).(wiki:Bat.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
