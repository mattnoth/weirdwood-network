## Origins

According to legend, the Oakenseat was a living throne carved unto an [oak](wiki:Oak) planted by [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand) himself.(wiki:Oakenseat.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_the_gardener_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) King [Garth VII Gardener](wiki:Garth_VII_Gardener), the Goldenhand, died upon the Oakenseat at the age of ninety-three.(wiki:Oakenseat.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_the_gardener_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
The Oakenseat was [chopped into pieces and burned](wiki:Anarchy_in_the_Reach) by the forces of a [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) king who sacked [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) late into the reign of the then-senile King [Garth X Gardener](wiki:Garth_X_Gardener).(wiki:Oakenseat.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
