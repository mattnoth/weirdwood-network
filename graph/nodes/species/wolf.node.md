## Origins

### Pre-Conquest[ ]

In antiquity, wolves made their lairs within the [Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf762_33-0)
According to songs and legend, the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) controlled armies of wolves and sent them against [Andal](wiki:Andal) invaders.(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf73.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
King [Jon Stark](wiki:Jon_Stark) raised the [Wolf's Den](wiki:Wolf's_Den) to defend the mouth of the [White Knife](wiki:White_Knife) from raiders from the sea.(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-1)
The [War of the Wolves](wiki:War_of_the_Wolves) was a conflict between a [King of Winter](wiki:King_of_Winter) from [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) and [Gaven Greywolf](wiki:Gaven_Greywolf).(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf711_34-0)
[Gorm the Wolf](wiki:Gorm_the_Wolf) was a chieftain of the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) who ruled [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome).(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf722_35-0)

### Targaryen Era[ ]

[The wife](wiki:Lady_Mormont_(wife_of_Alaric_Stark)) of Lord [Alaric Stark](wiki:Alaric_Stark) killed two wolves with her axe in her youth and used their skins to sew a cloak.(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Rfab10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
Ser [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) captained the [Ice Wolf](wiki:Ice_Wolf) when he sailed the [Shivering Sea](wiki:Shivering_Sea).(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Rfab12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord [Roderick Dustin](wiki:Roderick_Dustin) led a host of two thousand northmen who called themselves the [Winter Wolves](wiki:Winter_Wolves).(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Rfab16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1) At the end of the war, Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark) took control of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) during a period remembered as the [Hour of the Wolf](wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf).(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf47.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0) In the aftermath of the conflict, a [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) company consisting exclusively of northmen, called the [Wolf Pack](wiki:Wolf_Pack), was founded by [Hallis Hornwood](wiki:Hallis_Hornwood) and [Timotty Snow](wiki:Timotty_Snow).(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Rfab20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0)
[Sandoq the Shadow](wiki:Sandoq_the_Shadow) was a veteran [pit fighter](wiki:Pit_fighter) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), who fought against men and animals, including wolves.(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Rfab23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0)
[Varamyr](wiki:Varamyr), a [skinchanger](wiki:Skinchanger), was able to gain control of wolves. His master [Haggon](wiki:Haggon), a [warg](wiki:Warg), died and was going to have a [second life](wiki:Second_life) in his wolf [Greyskin](wiki:Greyskin), but Varamyr resentfully forced him out and took Greyskin for himself. Varamyr later bonded with three wolves, [One Eye](wiki:One_Eye), the head of the pack, [Sly](wiki:Sly), the female, and [Stalker](wiki:Stalker), the "tail".(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Radwd0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

When [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) rides to war, [his guards](wiki:Robb_Stark's_battle_guard) insist upon calling him the Young Wolf.(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0) Robb is proclaimed [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) after the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps).(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The direwolf [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf)) takes control over a pack of common wolves.(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Racok5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0) Lord [William Mooton](wiki:William_Mooton) and his sons track the pack to their lair, but the Mootons are forced to flee.(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Racok5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-1)
After the [fall of Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal) to Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) and the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions),(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0) Septon [Utt](wiki:Utt)'s men are attacked by bold wolves.(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-1) Roose is irritated by the howling of the wolf pack, so goes hunting and manages to kill nine wolves. He orders his men to sew the skins of the adults into a blanket for his bed, and to make gloves with the softer fur of the pups.(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-2)
[Rymund the Rhymer](wiki:Rymund_the_Rhymer) writes a song called "[Wolf in the Night](wiki:Wolf_in_the_Night)" about Robb's victory at the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross).(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0)
[Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed) tells [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) he had a [green dream](wiki:Green_dream) of a winged wolf, which he claims is Bran.(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Racok28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Jojen and his sister [Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed) tell Bran about the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) and the [Knight of the Laughing Tree](wiki:Knight_of_the_Laughing_Tree). The Reeds refer to a [wild wolf](wiki:Brandon_Stark), a [quiet wolf](wiki:Eddard_Stark), a [she-wolf](wiki:Lyanna_Stark), and a [wolf pup](wiki:Benjen_Stark).(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Nymeria's pack has grown to a great pack of hundreds of wolves who prowls the [Trident](wiki:Trident).(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-0) Some are still afraid of men,(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_48-0) but others have become more aggressive under the leadership of the female direwolf and have killed [horses](wiki:Horse) and men.(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Rasos3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_49-0)(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Varamyr](wiki:Varamyr)'s wolves [One Eye](wiki:One_Eye), [Stalker](wiki:Stalker), and [Sly](wiki:Sly) chase down and kill a party of [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) for food. Varamyr considers which of his animals he will enter into when he dies, and thinks the younger Stalker might suit him, but prefers One Eye. Varamyr dies, and enters One Eye for his [second life](wiki:Second_life).(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Radwd0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2)
[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark)'s direwolf [Summer](wiki:Summer) encounters Varamyr's wolf pack eating the remains of the dead [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) deserters. Summer sees that One Eye is not afraid of him even though he is twice the wolf's size. He realizes that One Eye has a [warg](wiki:Warg) within him, and they fight. After defeating One Eye and making him submit, Summer makes Stalker and Sly submit as well, and takes over leadership of the pack.(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)
Near the [cave of the last greenseer](wiki:Cave_of_the_last_greenseer), Summer and his wolf pack find a [wight](wiki:Wight) [snow bear](wiki:Snow_bear), and tear it to pieces and devour it, even though the rotten meat still moves while they eat it.(wiki:Wolf.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_50-0)
