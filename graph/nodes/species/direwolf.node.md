## Appearances & Description

Direwolves can grow larger than ponies and twice the size of large hounds.(wiki:Direwolf.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Direwolves are not simply oversized wolves, however, as they have slightly different body proportions: their legs are longer and their heads are larger than normal wolves. Their muzzles are also longer and more pronounced.(wiki:Direwolf.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Possible colorations include grey with yellow eyes and black with green eyes.(wiki:Direwolf.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Albino direwolves have white fur and red eyes.(wiki:Direwolf.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Origins

Although it is believed that direwolves and [shadowcats](wiki:Shadowcat) hunted the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest),(wiki:Direwolf.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf21.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) the [wood dancers](wiki:Wood_dancer) of the children are said to have called upon direwolves to fight on their behalf.(wiki:Direwolf.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf22.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) The [Others](wiki:Others) are said to have ridden dead animals, including direwolves.(wiki:Direwolf.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
Direwolves once roamed the [north](wiki:North) in large packs.(wiki:Direwolf.cite_ref-Racok53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) According to a semi-canon source, direwolves have been hunted by people because they are large and dangerous predators.(wiki:Direwolf.cite_ref-15) According to [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy), direwolves have not been sighted south of the Wall for two hundred years.(wiki:Direwolf.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) [Rangers](wiki:Rangers) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) hear direwolves [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:Direwolf.cite_ref-Ragot5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
Within the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell), great stone direwolves sit at the feet of statues depicting the Lords of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) and [Kings of Winter](wiki:Kings_of_Winter) from [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark).(wiki:Direwolf.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)(wiki:Direwolf.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) finds a dead female direwolf with a litter of six pups on the path between [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) and the holdfast where [Gared](wiki:Gared) was executed. Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) allows his children to adopt the pups.(wiki:Direwolf.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
