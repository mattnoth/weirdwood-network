## Culture

[House Volmark](wiki:House_Volmark) includes a [leviathan](wiki:Leviathan) in their sigil.(wiki:Whale.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
The savage spotted whales are known as "the [wolves](wiki:Wolf) of the wild sea" because they hunt in packs.(wiki:Whale.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf836_2-7)
Sailing on huge, fat-bellied whalers,(wiki:Whale.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Whale.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) the [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibbenese) hunt whales for their bone, blubber, and oil.(wiki:Whale.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf837_9-0) According to a semi-canon source, the Ibbenese chew blubber.(wiki:Whale.cite_ref-Rssm1226.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Some horns claimed to be those of [unicorns](wiki:Unicorn) are actually the horn of a whale hunted by Ibbenese,(wiki:Whale.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf713_11-0) presumably the [narwhal](wiki:Narwhal).(wiki:Whale.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf836_2-8)
Whale oil is used to light lamps in the [Port of Ibben](wiki:Port_of_Ibben)(wiki:Whale.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf837_9-1) and [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor).(wiki:Whale.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Some stories claim that [House Farwynd of the Lonely Light](wiki:House_Farwynd_of_the_Lonely_Light) and their [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) are [skinchangers](wiki:Skinchangers) who take the forms of [sea lions](wiki:Sea_lion), [walruses](wiki:Walrus), and [spotted whales](wiki:Spotted_whale).(wiki:Whale.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf75.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Whale.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) [Ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) named after the mammal include [Jorl the Whale](wiki:Jorl_the_Whale)(wiki:Whale.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf75.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) and [Kenned the Whale](wiki:Kenned_the_Whale).(wiki:Whale.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
The [Belly of the Whale](wiki:Belly_of_the_Whale) is an establishment frequented by [smugglers](wiki:Smuggler) in [Sisterton](wiki:Sisterton).(wiki:Whale.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

## Origins

The Whalebone Gates protected the city of [Ibbish](wiki:Ibbish) before its destruction by [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki).(wiki:Whale.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf837_9-2)
While sailing west into the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea), the [House Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) ships [Sun Chaser](wiki:Sun_Chaser), [Lady Meredith](wiki:Lady_Meredith), and [Autumn Moon](wiki:Autumn_Moon) spotted a great dark ship which they assumed to be an Ibbenese whaler.(wiki:Whale.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

During the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), Lord [Gylbert Farwynd](wiki:Gylbert_Farwynd) includes arm rings made of whalebone among the treasures he offers to the assembled [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn). The captains turn away from his gifts, leaving less prestigious men to scramble after them.(wiki:Whale.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
[Leo Tyrell](wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn)) mocks [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) by referring to him as a black-clad whale.(wiki:Whale.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The [Windblown](wiki:Windblown) refer to the grotesquely large [Yezzan zo Qaggaz](wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz) as the Yellow Whale.(wiki:Whale.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
[Iron Victory](wiki:Iron_Victory) captures [Noble Lady](wiki:Noble_Lady) with a cargo of salt cod, whale oil, and pickled herring.(wiki:Whale.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
