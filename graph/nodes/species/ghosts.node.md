## Culture

To keep vengeful spirits within the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell), [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) places an [iron](wiki:Iron) [longsword](wiki:Longsword) across the lap of each stone statue of the [Lords of Winterfell](wiki:Lords_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
The hour of ghosts is one of the [named hours](wiki:Named_hours).(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
[Ghostskin](wiki:Ghostskin) is a white moss found in the [Neck](wiki:Neck).(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
The [ghost grass](wiki:Ghost_grass) of the [Shadow Lands](wiki:Shadow_Lands) is said to glow in the dark with the spirits of the damned.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
When [summer](wiki:Summer_(season)) is warm and humid before the arrival of a colder autumn, old men refer to it as a "spirit summer", giving up its ghosts at last.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Ghost Hill](wiki:Ghost_Hill) is the seat of [House Toland](wiki:House_Toland) in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne),(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and they once had a ghost on their [banner](wiki:Heraldry).(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
[Vaes Leisi](wiki:Vaes_Leisi) is a ruined settlement in the forests of northern [Essos](wiki:Essos). Its name in the [Dothraki language](wiki:Dothraki_language) means "City of Ghosts", and it was the home of a vanished people the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) called the [Ifequevron](wiki:Ifequevron), or "woods walkers".(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Rawoiafvaes_leisi.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
[Grey Ghost](wiki:Grey_Ghost_(ship)) is a [longship](wiki:Longship) of the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet).(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
[Jenny's song](wiki:Jenny's_song) includes the lyrics "High in the halls of the kings who are gone, [Jenny](wiki:Jenny_of_Oldstones) would dance with her ghosts..."(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Origins

Legends of the [Shivering Sea](wiki:Shivering_Sea) claim that drowned spirits rise at night to drag the living down into grey-green depths.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_shivering_sea.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
The ancient cities of [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos) are said to be full of ghosts.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_sothoryos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
It is said that [northern](wiki:North) ghosts, hungry for [southron](wiki:Southron) blood, appear at the ruins of [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin) at night.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
[Rangers](wiki:Rangers) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) claim that [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome) is haunted by [ghouls](wiki:Ghouls), [demons](wiki:Demons), and burning ghosts with an unhealthy taste for blood.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) [Old Nan](wiki:Old_Nan) refers to the abandoned castles of the Watch along the [Wall](wiki:Wall) as ghost castles;(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Rasos40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort) is especially said to be haunted by ghosts.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
[Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) is said to be haunted by fiery spirits(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) after [its burning](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1) The [Tower of Ghosts](wiki:Tower_of_Ghosts) is one of the ruined castle's towers,(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) the spirits of [Harren the Black](wiki:Harren_Hoare) and his sons are said to haunt the cellar of the [Wailing Tower](wiki:Wailing_Tower), and some believe that the spirits of [falcons](wiki:Falcon) are found in the castle's mews.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1)
The ghosts of [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) are said to haunt [High Heart](wiki:High_Heart) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), and the [ghost of High Heart](wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart) is a [dwarf](wiki:Dwarf) woman who lives at the hill.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
Some claim that [Lann the Clever](wiki:Lann_the_Clever) haunts [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) as a ghost.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
[Serwyn of the Mirror Shield](wiki:Serwyn_of_the_Mirror_Shield) is said to have been haunted by the ghosts of the [knights](wiki:Knight) he killed.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
The [Grey Ghost](wiki:Grey_Ghost) was a reclusive [dragon](wiki:Dragon) who lived at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)).(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
Old Nan has told the children of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) that the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) has ghosts,(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) and she has referred to sunrise wisps of pale mist as "morning ghosts" returning to their graves.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)
[Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) and [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) once pranked their younger siblings in the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell). Robb brought [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark), [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark), and [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) down to the end of the crypts where their own tombs would be, and when Sansa worried about [spiders](wiki:Spider) and rats, Robb told her that there were worse things in the crypts, saying "this is where the dead walk". A pale white ghost then emerged from an open tomb, moaning for blood, and Sansa ran shrieking while Bran grabbed onto Robb and sobbed. However, Arya punched the spirit and discovered that it was only Jon, covered in flour.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Will](wiki:Will) sees that the crystal swords of the [Others](wiki:Others) shimmer with a "ghost-light" around their edges.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Ragot0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)
[Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) visits the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell) with King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), and notes that the swords on the laps of the most ancient [Lords of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell) have long ago rusted away to nothing. He hopes this does not mean their ghosts are free to roam [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) gives the name [Ghost](wiki:Ghost) to his albino [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) because the pup is mute and white.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Ragot5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)
During the journey of [Khal](wiki:Khal) [Drogo](wiki:Drogo)'s [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) from [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) to [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak), they skirt the tumbled ruins of a vast dead city (possibly [Vaes Khadokh](wiki:Vaes_Khadokh) or [Vaes Khewo](wiki:Vaes_Khewo))(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Rtloiafjourneys.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) where ghosts are said to moan among blackened [marble](wiki:Marble) columns.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
[Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) tells her uncle, Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully), of the tales of the ghosts that haunt [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin) at night.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) [dreams](wiki:Dream) that she is traveling down a long hall towards a [red door](wiki:House_with_the_red_door). As she runs, she sees the hall is lined with ghosts wearing the faded raiment of kings, with [silver](wiki:Silver), [gold](wiki:Gold), and [platinum](wiki:Platinum) hair and jeweled eyes, wielding swords of pale fire. They urge her to move faster, and as they scream "Faster!" in unison, she launches into the air with [dragon](wiki:Dragon) wings bursting from her back.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Ragot68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

Daenerys replies that she is not afraid of ghosts, and that dragons are stronger than ghosts.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)
Lord [Leo Lefford](wiki:Leo_Lefford) mocks the idea of ghosts at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), but he always keeps a candle burning by his bed.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-2)
After [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) secretly has targets in Harrenhal [assassinated](wiki:Assassin) by [Jaqen H'ghar](wiki:Jaqen_H'ghar), some nervous residents blame the deaths on the ghost of [Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare). Arya wants to tell them it isn't Harren, that she is "the ghost in Harrenhal", and begins to think of herself by that title.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-3)(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)
When [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) offers to move [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) into the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand) under his protection, she fears that will make it more difficult for Ser [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard) to help her escape the castle. She tells Tyrion that since the Tower was where her father [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s [men](wiki:House_Stark_guards) [were killed](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark), their ghosts would give her nightmares, and Tyrion accepts the excuse.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)

After [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand) commands Jon Snow to execute the [spearwife](wiki:Spearwife) [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte), she tells Jon to strike hard and fast with his sword, [Longclaw](wiki:Longclaw), or she will come back and haunt him.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)

"Renly" kills Ser [Guyard Morrigen](wiki:Guyard_Morrigen) and a dozen other knights,(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0) causing many of the men in Stannis's host who had once been Renly's to again change sides.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0) Maester [Ballabar](wiki:Ballabar) reports to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) that [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) are claiming that Renly's ghost saved [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) from Stannis.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Racok67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Tyrion asks [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) about the rumors of Renly's ghost. Allegedly his armor had a ghostly glow from [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire), and his antlered helm was covered in golden flames.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0)
Ygritte tells Jon Snow that [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) and the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) have been searching for the [Horn of Winter](wiki:Horn_of_Winter). She says that they opened dozens graves of heroes and kings, letting out all their shades into the world, but were unable to find the horn.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Rasos30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0)
After leaving Harrenhal, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) has a nightmare where he and [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) are in a cavern below [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), and are confronted by the ghosts of Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) and the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) [knights](wiki:Knight) [Oswell Whent](wiki:Oswell_Whent), [Jon Darry](wiki:Jon_Darry), [Lewyn Martell](wiki:Lewyn_Martell), [Gerold Hightower](wiki:Gerold_Hightower), and [Arthur Dayne](wiki:Arthur_Dayne). When Jaime wakes, he asks the former [maester](wiki:Maester), [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn), if he believes in ghosts, and Qyburn replies that he believes that some part of a human soul must remain in this world after death. He further states that the [archmaesters](wiki:Archmaester) of the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) did not care for his theories, except for [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn).(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0)
At the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) recalls all the ghost stories that [Old Nan](wiki:Old_Nan) used to tell about the haunted castle. When Bran hears something coming out of the kitchens' well, he thinks it is a ghost or monster, but it is only [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) and [Gilly](wiki:Gilly), climbing a secret staircase.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)
Following King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_I_Baratheon)'s wedding to [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), [Hamish the Harper](wiki:Hamish_the_Harper) performs "[Lord Renly's Ride](wiki:Lord_Renly's_Ride)" during the [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding). Hamish sings of Renly's ghost repenting from his attempt to usurp his nephew Joffrey, returning to this world to defend the realm from Stannis, and then returning to [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) to see his true love's face one last time.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0)
After Joffrey's death at his wedding, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) is accused of his murder.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-1) [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) uses the opportunity to secretly spirit Tyrion's wife, [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), out of King's Landing.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Rasos61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0) On Jaime Lannister's journey to the city, he hears of many rumors of Joffrey's murder, including from a [House Rowan](wiki:House_Rowan) archer who claims that Sansa did the murder with Tyrion, and afterwards vanished in a puff of brimstone, whereafter a ghostly direwolf was seen prowling the Red Keep, blood dripping from his jaws.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-0)

Loras reluctantly reveals that it was his brother, Ser [Garlan Tyrell](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell), who fit the armor better than Loras did. He tells Jaime that the masquerade was done per the suggestion of Petyr Baelish, who said the appearance of Renly's ghost, returned for vengeance, would frighten Stannis's men.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Having returned to Winterfell for [Ramsay Bolton's wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark), a regretful Theon feels that the castle is full of ghosts for him.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_50-0)(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_51-0)(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_52-0) He begins to think of himself as one of the ghosts in Winterfell.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_51-1) In the [godswood of Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell), Theon sees Bran's face in the [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) and momentarily thinks he is being haunted by his ghost, before recalling that they had not actually killed Bran and Rickon Stark, but instead only [miller's sons](wiki:Miller's_sons). He hears the tree whispering his name and Bran's, and believes the ghosts of Winterfell are speaking to him.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_52-1)
[Ellaria Sand](wiki:Ellaria_Sand) asks the vengeful [Sand Snakes](wiki:Sand_Snakes) if they believe [Myrcella](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) and [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) must die before the shades of [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Rhaegar)) and [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)) can rest.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_53-0)
One of the [six trebuchets](wiki:Six_sisters) built by [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) for the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen) is called Ghost of Astapor.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_54-0)
[Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) gives the names [Ghost](wiki:Ghost_(ship)) and [Shade](wiki:Shade) to two [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) galleys he captures in [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay), since he intends for them to return to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) to haunt the Yunkishmen.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_55-0)
In the camp of the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons) outside Meereen, Tyrion Lannister encounters a young [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword), [Kem](wiki:Kem), who tells Tyrion he was part of Stannis's army at the battle of the Blackwater, and fled when Renly's ghost attacked.(wiki:Ghosts.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_56-0)
