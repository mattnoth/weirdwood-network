## Culture

[Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) [sigils](wiki:Sigil) depicting lions or lion heads include those of Houses [Casterly](wiki:House_Casterly),(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-1) [Grandison](wiki:House_Grandison), [Jast](wiki:House_Jast), [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister), [Osgrey](wiki:House_Osgrey), [Parren](wiki:House_Parren), [Polander](wiki:House_Polander), [Reyne](wiki:House_Reyne), [Vikary](wiki:House_Vikary), and [Wydman](wiki:House_Wydman).
Because of the Lannisters' sigil, men of the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) can be called lions.(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Rfab16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Rfab20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The [Lion Gate](wiki:Lion_Gate) at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) opens to the [Goldroad](wiki:Goldroad), which leads to the westerlands.(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The garrison of the [City Watch of King's Landing](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing) stationed at the Lion Gate is called the "[Lions](wiki:Lions)".(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Rfab17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) At sunset, [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) is believed to resemble a lion in repose.(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf762_7-0)
The [Lion of Night](wiki:Lion_of_Night) is a [deity](wiki:Deity) of [Yi Ti](wiki:Yi_Ti),(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf841_8-0)(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) also worshipped in other parts of [Essos](wiki:Essos), including [Braavos](wiki:Braavos)(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) and [Asshai](wiki:Asshai).(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf844_11-0) In the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White) in Braavos, there is an [ebony](wiki:Ebony) statue of the Lion of Night, depicted as a man with a lion's head seated on a throne.(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
The [Prince of Pentos](wiki:Prince_of_Pentos) keeps a pride of lions in his menagerie.(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

## Origins

The [wood dancers](wiki:Wood_dancer) of the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) are said to have called upon cave lions to fight on their behalf.(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf22.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
Lions inhabited the [western hills](wiki:Westerlands) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). In antiquity, lions made dens in [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) itself.(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf76.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Rssm1379.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) According to legend, the founder of House Casterly was the huntsman [Corlos](wiki:Corlos), son of [Caster](wiki:Caster), who lived in a village near where [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) eventually sprang up. When a lion began preying upon the village's sheep, Corlos tracked him back to his den, a cave in the base of the [nearby mountain](wiki:Casterly_Rock). Armed only with a spear, Corlos slew the lion and his mate, but spared her newborn cubs. This act of mercy pleased the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods), who took favor on Corlos and sent a shaft of sunlight deep into the cave, which shone upon a huge vein of [gold](wiki:Gold).(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf76.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)
Later, lions were owned by the [Kings of the Rock](wiki:Kings_of_the_Rock). According to a semi-canon source, some of King [Loreon I Lannister](wiki:Loreon_I_Lannister)'s lions broke free and slew three of his sons, some say as a curse from [Morgon Banefort](wiki:Morgon_Banefort).(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-17)
[Sandoq the Shadow](wiki:Sandoq_the_Shadow) is said to have slain lions, [bears](wiki:Bear), [wolves](wiki:Wolves), and [wyverns](wiki:Wyvern) in the [fighting pits of Meereen](wiki:Fighting_pits_of_Meereen).(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Rfab23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
Wild lions were seen during the reign of Lord [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister), as the first [knight](wiki:Knight) of [House Clegane](wiki:House_Clegane), [the kennelmaster](wiki:Clegane_(founder)) at the Rock, saved Tytos from a lioness and lost a leg and three [dogs](wiki:Dog) in the effort.(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Several caged lions were kept in the bowels of Casterly Rock during the childhood of [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), a legacy of their grandfather Tytos's time.(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Radwd65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The [handmaids](wiki:Handmaid) of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) make her a cloak from the hrakkar's pelt, to cover her bald head.(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
Within the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying) in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), Daenerys has a series of [visions](wiki:Vision), one of which features a white lion.(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

In [Pentos](wiki:Pentos), [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) refers to himself as a lion, which amuses Illyrio Mopatis. He offers to take Tyrion to the [Prince of Pentos](wiki:Prince_of_Pentos)'s menagerie so that he can share a cage with a pride of real lions, but Tyrion demurs.(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)
In [300 AC](wiki:300_AC), [Leaf](wiki:Leaf), one of the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest), tells [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) that the great lions of the western hills have all been slain.(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) According to a semi-canon source, a few lions survive in the outlying hills of the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands), but they mostly have been hunted to extinction.(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Rssm1379.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1)
[Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) remembers the restless caged lions of her youth, kept in the bowels of [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), and how she worked up the courage to touch one, while her brother [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) pulled her away.(wiki:Lion.cite_ref-Radwd65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1)
