## Origins

The inhabitants of [Mardosh](wiki:Mardosh) were forced to eat their dogs when their city was besieged by [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) [khalasars](wiki:Khalasar).(wiki:Dog.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf835_33-0)
The feelings of [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) princelings towards dogs varied from fear ([Aerea](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen) at first)(wiki:Dog.cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-1) to love ([Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)),(wiki:Dog.cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-2) [Viserra](wiki:Viserra_Targaryen),(wiki:Dog.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0) and [Saera](wiki:Saera_Targaryen))(wiki:Dog.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-1) to indifference ([Maegor](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen)).(wiki:Dog.cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-3)
[Harren the Red](wiki:Harren_the_Red) fed the genitals of Lord [Gargon Qoherys](wiki:Gargon_Qoherys) to a dog before killing him.(wiki:Dog.cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-4)
After the death of Queen [Tyanna of the Tower](wiki:Tyanna_of_the_Tower), rumors spread of King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) feeding her heart to his dogs.(wiki:Dog.cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-5)
The [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [Sheepstealer](wiki:Sheepstealer) occasionally ate sheep dogs on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)).(wiki:Dog.cite_ref-Rfab15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)
Prior to the [Maiden's Day Ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball), rumors spread of [Rosamund Darry](wiki:Rosamund_Darry) having six nipples because her mother had supposedly lain with a dog.(wiki:Dog.cite_ref-Rfab21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0)
During the winter of [134 AC](wiki:134_AC) northmen in the [winter town](wiki:Winter_town) ate dogs and [horses](wiki:Horse) to avoid starvation.(wiki:Dog.cite_ref-Rfab23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)
Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) buried his old stot, [Chestnut](wiki:Chestnut), deep in the sand of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) to avoid it being found and eaten by sand dogs.(wiki:Dog.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Some rumors about [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers) claimed he could use [magic](wiki:Magic) to put on the likeness of a one-eyed dog.(wiki:Dog.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
Lord [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister) was saved from a [lioness](wiki:Lion) by the first [knight](wiki:Knight) of [House Clegane](wiki:House_Clegane), [the kennelmaster](wiki:Clegane_(founder)) at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), who lost a leg and three dogs in the effort.(wiki:Dog.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1)
[Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) was interested in [horses](wiki:Horse), swords, and dogs as a boy.(wiki:Dog.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0)
Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) took a great interest in the breeding of hunting hounds while investigating the parentage of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s children.(wiki:Dog.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0)
