## Culture

A vulture carrying away an infant is depicted in the arms of [House Blackmont](wiki:House_Blackmont).(wiki:Vulture.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
Several outlaws from the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) have styled themselves as [Vulture Kings](wiki:Vulture_King).(wiki:Vulture.cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Origins

King [Benedict Blackmont](wiki:Benedict_Blackmont) is said to have been been able to change into a vulture of enormous size.(wiki:Vulture.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf794_3-0)
Singers claim that the [first Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Aenys_I)) was torn apart by vultures after being caught by Lord [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly_(lord)), though the outlaw actually died of thirst and exposure before being eaten.(wiki:Vulture.cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) thinks the nose of his sister [Asha](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) resembles the beak of a vulture.(wiki:Vulture.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

During the [wedding of Ramsay Bolton and Arya Stark](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark), Theon thinks that Lord [Ondrew Locke](wiki:Ondrew_Locke) resembles a vulture in the mists of the [godswood of Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Vulture.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
