## Culture

Foxes are known for being sly.(wiki:Fox.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) A red gold fox is included in the sigil of [House Florent](wiki:House_Florent).(wiki:Fox.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [House Foxglove](wiki:House_Foxglove) is a noble house from [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Fox.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Expensive cloaks are often lined with foxfur. Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) owns a grey silk cloak trimmed with black fox,(wiki:Fox.cite_ref-Ragot33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and [Salloreon](wiki:Salloreon) also owns a foxfur cloak.(wiki:Fox.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) has a blue velvet robe trimmed with fox fur.(wiki:Fox.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Lord [Meldred Merlyn](wiki:Meldred_Merlyn) wears a fox-fur mantle.(wiki:Fox.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
A sly red fox is included on the painted doors of the [Seven Swords](wiki:Seven_Swords) in [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale).(wiki:Fox.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
[Ronnel Harclay](wiki:Ronnel_Harclay),(wiki:Fox.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) [Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams),(wiki:Fox.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Ser [Shadrich of the Shady Glen](wiki:Shadrich_of_the_Shady_Glen),(wiki:Fox.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) [Big Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)),(wiki:Fox.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) and [Brandon Norrey](wiki:Brandon_Norrey)(wiki:Fox.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) are said to resemble foxes.

## Origins

[Florys the Fox](wiki:Florys_the_Fox) is said to have been the cleverest of the children of [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand), and Houses [Florent](wiki:House_Florent), [Ball](wiki:House_Ball), and [Peake](wiki:House_Peake) claim descent from her.(wiki:Fox.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) was unsuccessfully hunting a fox on the [Weeping Water](wiki:Weeping_Water) on the day he raped the mother of [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow).(wiki:Fox.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Todric](wiki:Todric) claims a white foxfur cloak from one of the [Wild Hares](wiki:Wild_Hares).(wiki:Fox.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) often sings a song about a [chicken](wiki:Chicken) which dressed like a fox to her son, Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn).(wiki:Fox.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) wears a hooded cloak of soft white fox fur at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie).(wiki:Fox.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) thinks she might be hearing [owls](wiki:Owl) and prowling foxes in the woods at night.(wiki:Fox.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) [Meribald](wiki:Meribald)'s [dog](wiki:Dog_(Meribald)) barks at a fox while Brienne's party travel along the [Bay of Crabs](wiki:Bay_of_Crabs) toward [Saltpans](wiki:Saltpans).(wiki:Fox.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Varamyr](wiki:Varamyr) detects the faint smells of foxes and other animals in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest).(wiki:Fox.cite_ref-Radwdprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
