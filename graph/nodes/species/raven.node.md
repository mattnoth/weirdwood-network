## Origins

The [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) taught the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) to use ravens to communicate over long distances. The [greenseers](wiki:Greenseer) of the children could change their skins and speak through the birds. Unlike humans, ravens can speak the [True Tongue](wiki:True_Tongue), the language of the children.(wiki:Raven.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) Some priests of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) believe that ravens are servants of the [Storm God](wiki:Storm_God).(wiki:Raven.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) In the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), the first raven is said to have been let into the world when the [Crone](wiki:Crone_(the_Seven)) peered through the door of death.(wiki:Raven.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)
There is a dead [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) of colossal size in the [godswood](wiki:Godswood) at [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall) in [Blackwood Vale](wiki:Blackwood_Vale). Every evening at dusk hundreds of ravens come and roost on the tree all night, as they have for thousands of years.(wiki:Raven.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)
[Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) sent a cloud of ravens from [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) at the start of [the Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Raven.cite_ref-Rfab1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)
King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen) attempted to replace messenger ravens with doves.(wiki:Raven.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) suggests that [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) aid Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) because the youth is good with animals.(wiki:Raven.cite_ref-Ragot41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)
Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) has ravens from the [Twins](wiki:Twins) shot down to prevent Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) from potentially informing Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) of the movements of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark).(wiki:Raven.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Since [Cressen](wiki:Cressen) is no longer able to climb to the [rookery](wiki:Rookery), [Pylos](wiki:Pylos) attends the ravens at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Raven.cite_ref-Racok0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2) When he announces his claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) has Pylos send forth all one hundred and seventeen ravens from Dragonstone.(wiki:Raven.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)
Ravens are sent to [Pyke](wiki:Pyke) from Robb at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) and from Lord [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister) at [Seagard](wiki:Seagard), but Lord [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) does not respond.(wiki:Raven.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)

Ravens from [Luwin](wiki:Luwin)'s rookery and [crows](wiki:Crow) from the [broken tower](wiki:Broken_tower) feast on corpses after the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Raven.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Sam releases the caged ravens during the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist), but he forgets to attach messages to them.(wiki:Raven.cite_ref-Rasos18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0) While retreating to the [Wall](wiki:Wall), he and [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) are attacked by [wights](wiki:Wights) at [a village](wiki:Whitetree). Hundreds of ravens fly from the village's [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) and attack the wights, however, and the pair are rescued by [Coldhands](wiki:Coldhands).(wiki:Raven.cite_ref-Rasos46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0)(wiki:Raven.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0)
During the election of the new [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), [Mormont's raven](wiki:Mormont's_raven) flies from a kettle, lands by [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), and says the word "snow". The inspired men of the Night's Watch soon select Jon as their new leader.(wiki:Raven.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]
