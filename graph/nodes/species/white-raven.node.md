## Appearances & Description

White ravens are not albino, but a separate species from black [ravens](wiki:Ravens). This is known because they have bright black eyes. White ravens are larger and more intelligent than their black counterparts, and can be larger than hawks.(wiki:White_raven.cite_ref-Racok0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Like black ravens, white ravens can croak words.(wiki:White_raven.cite_ref-Racok0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:White_raven.cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Origins

Because white ravens were released two days before his birth in [57 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), [Baelon Targaryen](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) was known as the Spring Prince.(wiki:White_raven.cite_ref-Rfab10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
In [130 AC](wiki:130_AC) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), three hundred white ravens were sent from the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) on [Maiden's Day](wiki:Maiden's_Day) to announce the arrival of winter.(wiki:White_raven.cite_ref-Rfab16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The [Conclave](wiki:Conclave) released white ravens in [135 AC](wiki:135_AC) to announce the end of the cruel winter.(wiki:White_raven.cite_ref-Rfab23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

A white raven arrives at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) from the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) with a message declaring the end of summer. Maester [Cressen](wiki:Cressen) shows the bird to Princess [Shireen Baratheon](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon).(wiki:White_raven.cite_ref-Racok0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
White ravens bring word of [autumn](wiki:Autumn) to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell),(wiki:White_raven.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black),(wiki:White_raven.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:White_raven.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

The Citadel novice [Pate](wiki:Pate_(novice)) helps tend the ravens as an assistant to Archmaester [Walgrave](wiki:Walgrave) at the [Ravenry](wiki:Ravenry). The white ravens know Pate's name, and often mutter it to each other whenever they see him. The white ravens are Walgrave's pride, and he wants them to eat him when he dies.(wiki:White_raven.cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) arrives at Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle)'s chambers at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) and finds a white raven sitting on the window ledge, a sign from the Citadel that winter has come.(wiki:White_raven.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
