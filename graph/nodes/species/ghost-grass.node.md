## Identity

Ghost grass is a plant found in the Shadow Lands beyond Asshai. It grows taller than a man on horseback, with stalks as pale as milkglass that glow in the dark with what some say are the spirits of the damned. It kills all other grasses and plants it touches. The Dothraki believe ghost grass will someday spread across the entire world, covering the Dothraki Sea and ending all life — a grass-based eschatology. Ser Jorah Mormont describes it to Daenerys when she surveys the Dothraki Sea.
