## Culture

Three beehives are included in the arms of [House Beesbury](wiki:House_Beesbury) of [Honeyholt](wiki:Honeyholt).(wiki:Bee.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Origins

[Ellyn Ever Sweet](wiki:Ellyn_Ever_Sweet), the legendary founder of [House Beesbury](wiki:House_Beesbury), is said to have loved honey so much that she sought out the [King of the Bees](wiki:King_of_the_Bees) in his vast mountain hive, and made a pact to care for his children and his children's children for all time, becoming the first beekeeper.(wiki:Bee.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Princess [Daella Targaryen](wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)) loved flowers, but was afraid of gardens after a bee almost stung her once. Daella's sister, Princess [Saera](wiki:Saera_Targaryen), once played a prank on her by filling her chamberpot with bees.(wiki:Bee.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[A septry](wiki:Burning_septry) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) has a hundred beehives, but their [honey](wiki:Honey) is taken by [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Bee.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

While staying at the [Quiet Isle](wiki:Quiet_Isle), [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) and her companions are served [honey](wiki:Honey) from the [septry](wiki:Septry)'s hives.(wiki:Bee.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken) asks for [Honeytree](wiki:Honeytree) and its hives as part of negotiations during the [siege of Raventree](wiki:Siege_of_Raventree).(wiki:Bee.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
