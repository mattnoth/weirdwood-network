## Narrative Arc

The town of [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) is sacked [first by "lions"](wiki:First_sack_of_Maidenpool) and [again by "wolves"](wiki:Second_sack_of_Maidenpool). [William Mooton](wiki:William_Mooton), Lord of Maidenpool, remains in his castle and does not defend the town when it suffers a third sack, this time by [outlaws](wiki:Outlaws).(wiki:Third_sack_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Half of Maidenpool's buildings have been burned or plundered and its famous pool is filled with corpses.(wiki:Third_sack_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Aftermath

The sack of Maidenpool by outlaws may have been perpetrated by the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions).(wiki:Third_sack_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) and Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) are taken captive by Brave Companions shortly after leaving a deserted Maidenpool,(wiki:Third_sack_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) although the sellswords are based out of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) with Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) at that time. Brienne later recalls that the Brave Companions used a [septon](wiki:Septon) for archery practice near Maidenpool.(wiki:Third_sack_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) was killed near Maidenpool by outlaw bowmen not connected with the Brave Companions.(wiki:Third_sack_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Following the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) [takes Maidenpool](wiki:Taking_of_Maidenpool)(wiki:Third_sack_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and begins to rebuild the town.(wiki:Third_sack_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
