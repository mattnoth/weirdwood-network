## Origins

The younger sister of [Harwyn Hoare](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers), had married into [House Volmark](wiki:House_Volmark). After the death of Harwyn's grandson, King [Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare), in the [burning of Harrenhal](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), [Qhorin Volmark](wiki:Qhorin_Volmark) claimed to be the heir of the black line of [House Hoare](wiki:House_Hoare). He was the first of many rival claimants to the title [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands). However, he was defeated during the [invasion of the Iron Islands](wiki:Invasion_of_the_Iron_Islands) and beheaded by [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) instead. The [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) then elected [Vickon Greyjoy](wiki:Vickon_Greyjoy), the [Lord Reaper of Pyke](wiki:Lord_Reaper_of_Pyke), as the [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands)(wiki:Volmark_conspiracy.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands_-_the_greyjoys_of_pyke.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)(wiki:Volmark_conspiracy.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
