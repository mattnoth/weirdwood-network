## Origins

[House Peake](wiki:House_Peake) of [Starpike](wiki:Starpike), supporters of the [Blackfyre Pretenders](wiki:Blackfyre_Pretenders), rose in rebellion against [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen). King [Maekar I Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen) led a host to the [Dornish Marches](wiki:Dornish_Marches) to suppress the rebellious Lord Peake.(wiki:Storming_of_Starpike.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_maekar_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

While leading the attack against the gates of Starpike, King Maekar died when a rock dropped from the castle's battlements crushed his crowned helm.(wiki:Storming_of_Starpike.cite_ref-sample_1-1) [Tywald Lannister](wiki:Tywald_Lannister), the heir to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) and the squire of Lord [Robert Reyne](wiki:Robert_Reyne), was mortally wounded shortly after while fighting through the gates. Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen), Maekar's youngest son, [knighted](wiki:Knight) Tywald before the youth died in the arms of [Tion Lannister](wiki:Tion_Lannister), Tywald's twin and Aegon's squire. Robert, [Lord of Castamere](wiki:Lord_of_Castamere), was also killed during the fighting.(wiki:Storming_of_Starpike.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Storming_of_Starpike.cite_ref-sample_1-2)

## Aftermath

[Roger Reyne](wiki:Roger_Reyne), the Red Lion, took vengeance for his father Robert's death by executing seven captive Peakes. Prince Aegon prevented further killings, however.(wiki:Storming_of_Starpike.cite_ref-sample_1-3)

Maekar's death led to chaos.(wiki:Storming_of_Starpike.cite_ref-sample_1-4) The succession crisis was resolved with the selection of his youngest son as King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) after a [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council).(wiki:Storming_of_Starpike.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_maekar_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
