## Identity

Riots in King's Landing is a event.battle from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges

- FIGHTS_IN: War of the Five Kings (track_b: Conflict)
- DEFEATS: Curfew under penalty of death ordered (track_b: Result)

## Origins

The riots are caused by lack of [food](wiki:Food) and a sense of dissatisfaction among the general populace during the preparation of [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), for the eventual assault on King's Landing by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), a claimant to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). With Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) cutting off supplies from the [Reach](wiki:Reach) coming up the [roseroad](wiki:Roseroad), and the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) awash in blood, King's Landing is supplied mainly by [House Rosby](wiki:House_Rosby) and [House Stokeworth](wiki:House_Stokeworth), whose lands have been largely untouched by the war. This leads to shortages in most basic foods, and most of the incoming provisions are allocated to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) and the garrison. A baker is killed in his oven for allegedly charging too much for his bread.(wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Aftermath

The [small council](wiki:Small_council) extends the curfew, bringing more peace to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) Food remains scarce, however, and the price of new seafood soars.(wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Ser [Jacelyn Bywater](wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater) is unsure of the discipline and loyalty of the [gold cloaks](wiki:Gold_cloaks) under his command, and he reveals to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) that the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) mostly blame Tyrion, the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), for the city's troubles instead of young King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-10) Tyrion has a [new High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Tyrion's)) named, and Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) fills Ser [Preston Greenfield](wiki:Preston_Greenfield)'s place in the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) has nightmares of the riot.(wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Racok52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) plans for Prince [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) to be discretely moved to [Rosby](wiki:Rosby) for his own safety.(wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) [Tyrek Lannister](wiki:Tyrek_Lannister) remains missing, despite the efforts of Jacelyn, Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys), and Ser [Addam Marbrand](wiki:Addam_Marbrand) to find him.(wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) notes that Varys was not present to see Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) off to Dorne, thereby avoiding the riot, and he wonders if Varys had agents abduct Tyrek because the youth had squired for King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

While Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) meets with the [Tyrells](wiki:House_Tyrell) in [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden), Petyr's men spread tales of the mob killing Preston and raping [Lollys Stokeworth](wiki:Lollys_Stokeworth), a way to discretely encourage Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) to join the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) and thereby defend his sister, [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), who is betrothed to Joffrey.(wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Lollys eventually has a son who is named [Tyrion Tanner](wiki:Tyrion_Tanner).(wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

After becoming Hand, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) replaces the stolen and smashed crystal [crown](wiki:Crown) of the High Septon with an even grander one of crystal and spun [gold](wiki:Gold).(wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

During Tyrion's trial for [Joffrey's death](wiki:Purple_Wedding), Balon verifies that Tyrion had kicked Joffrey on the day of the riot.(wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

The night before her [walk of atonement](wiki:Walk_of_atonement) through the streets of the capital, a fearful Cersei recalls the bread riots.(wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Radwd65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
