## Origins

In his campaign to place [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)) on the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) joins the [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) in invading the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands). Their [Volantene](wiki:Volantis) fleet is scattered by a storm after [Lys](wiki:Lys), and only six of the sellswords' ten ships arrive at the rendezvous near the [rainwood](wiki:Rainwood) in [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath), land formerly owned by [House Connington](wiki:House_Connington). The gathered force is not quite half the Golden Company's full strength of ten thousand.(wiki:Taking_of_Rain_House.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Jon leads a quarter of the available strength to [Griffin's Roost](wiki:Taking_of_Griffin's_Roost), the ancestral seat of the Conningtons, of which he was lord before being exiled during the [War of the Usurper](wiki:War_of_the_Usurper). Simultaneously, Ser [Tristan Rivers](wiki:Tristan_Rivers) sets off to take [Crow's Nest](wiki:Taking_of_Crow's_Nest), seat of [House Morrigen](wiki:House_Morrigen), while [Laswell Peake](wiki:Laswell_Peake) marches for [Rain House](wiki:Rain_House), stronghold of [House Wylde](wiki:House_Wylde).(wiki:Taking_of_Rain_House.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Aftermath

During her journey to meet Aegon, Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) learns that Rain House has fallen to the Golden Company.(wiki:Taking_of_Rain_House.cite_ref-arianne1_1-1)
