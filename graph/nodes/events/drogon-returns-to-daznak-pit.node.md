## Identity

At the reopening of [Daznak's Pit](daznaks-pit) — the fighting games [Daenerys](daenerys-targaryen) grudgingly permitted as a concession sealed by her [marriage to Hizdahr zo Loraq](wedding-of-hizdahr-zo-loraq-and-daenerys-targaryen) — a vast shadow falls across the arena. [Drogon](drogon), grown enormous in his self-exile, descends from the sky, kills the boar in mid-fight, and devours Barsena Blackhair along with it. His arrival stills ten thousand voices, then breaks the games into panic. A lone spearman [leaps onto his back and wounds him](unnamed-spearman-attacks-drogon); [Hizdahr shouts for the dragon to be killed](hizdahr-orders-drogon-killed); the spearmen close in and [Drogon burns and breaks them](drogon-kills-more-attackers). The chaos is the precipitating incident of the Meereen arc's end: Drogon's presence in the pit is what makes Daenerys's flight from the city possible.
