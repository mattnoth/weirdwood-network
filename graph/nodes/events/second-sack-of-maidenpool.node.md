## Narrative Arc

The town of [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) is [sacked](wiki:First_sack_of_Maidenpool) by [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) soldiers. At some point after the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), the town is sacked by "wolves", [northern](wiki:North) soldiers sworn to [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark). [William Mooton](wiki:William_Mooton), Lord of Maidenpool, again remains in his castle and does not defend the town.(wiki:Second_sack_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) [House Mooton](wiki:House_Mooton) is sworn to [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully), who are themselves sworn to [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident). It is unknown if there was a Lannister garrison in the town, or if the northmen sack the town for plunder.

## Aftermath

Maidenpool is later [sacked by outlaws](wiki:Third_sack_of_Maidenpool).(wiki:Second_sack_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
