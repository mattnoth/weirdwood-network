## Narrative Arc

[Jon](wiki:Jon_Snow) is with [the Magnar](wiki:Styr)’s men nearing [a village](wiki:Queenscrown) alongside a lake. He realizes that he must somehow escape and reach [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) before the raiders do, in order to warn them. The [Thenns](wiki:Thenns) captured an old man camped in one of the ruined houses, and Styr commands Jon to kill the man. Jon balks, but [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte) slits the man’s throat with her dagger. As Styr begins to command his men in the Old Tongue, a crash of thunder sounds, and suddenly a [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) is attacking the Thenns. Jon mistakes the wolf for [Ghost](wiki:Ghost) or [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind) for a moment, wondering if [Robb](wiki:Robb_Stark) returned to the North, but he never realizes that it is [Summer](wiki:Summer_(direwolf)). The direwolf slaughters several [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings), and in the confusion, Jon mounts the old man’s horse and begins to flee. He is hit with an arrow in the calf, believing the shot was fired by Ygritte. Jon gets away, forcing the horse to gallop for hours without rest, heading for Castle Black.
