## Narrative Arc

Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) and the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) burn [Stone Hedge](wiki:Stone_Hedge) and the [Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken) lands under orders from Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister).(wiki:Burning_of_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) The Mountain rapes a daughter of Lord [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken) and steals or burns the Bracken harvest.(wiki:Burning_of_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [Harry Rivers](wiki:Harry_Rivers), the Bastard of Bracken, is killed by [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) at some point during the war.(wiki:Burning_of_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Aftermath

Following the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), Jonos travels from ruined Stone Hedge to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Burning_of_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Lord Bracken [liberates his castle](wiki:Liberation_of_Stone_Hedge) from the Lannisters after [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) is crowned [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Burning_of_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

During the [siege of Raventree](wiki:Siege_of_Raventree) after the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Jonos requests land and castles from [House Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood) as recompense for the damage inflicted by the Mountain's men.(wiki:Burning_of_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

After the burning, [Lady Bracken](wiki:Lady_Bracken) spends most of her time praying with her [septon](wiki:Septon).(wiki:Burning_of_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
