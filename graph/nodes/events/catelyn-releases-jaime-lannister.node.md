## Identity

At Riverrun, grieving the reported deaths of Bran and Rickon and desperate to recover her daughters, Lady Catelyn Stark frees Jaime Lannister from his cell without Robb's knowledge or consent. She sends him south toward King's Landing under the escort of Brienne of Tarth, who has sworn to deliver the Kingslayer in exchange for Sansa and Arya. Ser Desmond Grell confines Catelyn for the act; she confesses it openly as treason and hers alone. The release is the spark of two consequence-chains: Lord Rickard Karstark's vengeance (he blames Jaime for his sons' deaths), and the launch of the Jaime–Brienne journey. This beat is the upstream cause of the Karstark crisis that costs Robb half his northern strength.
