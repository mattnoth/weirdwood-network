## Origins

[Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), attacked the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) in [130 AC](wiki:130_AC) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), raiding [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport)(wiki:Ambush_at_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) and invading [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle).(wiki:Ambush_at_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) After conquering [Faircastle](wiki:Faircastle), the Red Kraken ransomed Lord [Farman](wiki:House_Farman) and sons back to [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) of [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) while keeping Lord Farman's daughters as [salt wives](wiki:Salt_wives) for himself and his brother, [Veron Greyjoy](wiki:Veron_Greyjoy).(wiki:Ambush_at_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Lady [Johanna Lannister](wiki:Johanna_Lannister), one of the [Three Widows](wiki:Three_Widows), led the [westermen](wiki:Westermen)'s resistance to the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) after the death of her husband, Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister), in the [Battle of the Red Fork](wiki:Battle_of_the_Red_Fork). During the [Hour of the Wolf](wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf) in [131 AC](wiki:131_AC), Johanna agreed to make peace with the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) in return for the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) commanding Dalton to relinquish Fair Isle, surrender captured women, and return to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands).(wiki:Ambush_at_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Dalton ignored the [regency of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III), however, and attacked Johanna's shipyards when she attempted to build a new fleet.(wiki:Ambush_at_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

After her westermen [defended Kayce](wiki:Battle_at_Kayce) and slew Dalton's favorite uncle,(wiki:Ambush_at_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Lady Johanna planned an expedition to recover Fair Isle in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC).(wiki:Ambush_at_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) She discretely assembled a fleet of fishing boats and cogs by [Feastfires](wiki:Feastfires), seat of [House Prester](wiki:House_Prester) on the mainland, and a host of a hundred [knights](wiki:Knight) and three thousand [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms). Commanded by Ser [Erwin Lannister](wiki:Erwin_Lannister), Lord Prester, and Lord [Tarbeck](wiki:House_Tarbeck), their plan was to sail across the [Straits of Fair Isle](wiki:Straits_of_Fair_Isle) at night and land on the southern coast of the island undetected.(wiki:Ambush_at_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Someone betrayed the plan to the ironborn, however, so Dalton's longships were waiting when the westermen set sail. After destroying the ragtag fleet, Dalton sent the heads of the three commanders to Casterly Rock as vengeance for his slain uncle.(wiki:Ambush_at_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
