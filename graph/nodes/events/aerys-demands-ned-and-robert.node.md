## Identity

After executing Rickard and Brandon Stark, King Aerys II Targaryen demanded that Lord Jon Arryn surrender his wards Eddard Stark and Robert Baratheon for execution, 282 AC. Jon Arryn refused and called his banners instead — the act that opened Robert's Rebellion as armed conflict. This is the direct trigger of the war.
