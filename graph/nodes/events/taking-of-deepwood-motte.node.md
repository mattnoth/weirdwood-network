## Origins

[Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover), [Master of Deepwood Motte](wiki:Master_of_Deepwood_Motte), and his brother, [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover), march south with [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) after the arrest of his father, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark).(wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) After Eddard's execution, Robb is proclaimed [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Seeking allies, Robb sends his friend, [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy), as an envoy to his father, [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands). Balon rejects Robb's offer, however, and instead intends to strike at the [north](wiki:North), which is weakly defended with northmen away fighting in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) and the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands). Balon sends his son Theon, his brother [Aeron Damphair](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy), and [Dagmer Cleftjaw](wiki:Dagmer) to [harry the Stony Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore) in hopes that the remaining northern lords will march away from their castles. Balon sends his daughter [Asha](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) to seize [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte), and he sends his brother [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) with the majority of the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) to seize [Moat Cailin](wiki:Fall_of_Moat_Cailin), thereby preventing the northern lords in the south from returning to their seats.(wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

[Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) takes thirty longships of picked men and sails north around [Sea Dragon Point](wiki:Sea_Dragon_Point). She lands on the tidal flats to the north of Deepwood and seizes the castle after a month long siege with a thousand [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn).(wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)(wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) [Deepwood's steward](wiki:Glover_steward) loses a leg during the fighting.(wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Asha takes hostage Robett's family, including Lady [Sybelle Glover](wiki:Sybelle_Glover) and her children, [Gawen](wiki:Gawen_Glover) and [Erena](wiki:Erena_Glover), as well [Larence Snow](wiki:Larence_Snow), the Bastard of [Hornwood](wiki:House_Hornwood).(wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Aftermath

Rather than follow Balon's instructions to continue raiding the Stony Shore, Theon instead [seizes Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) in an attempt to compete with Asha. Having few men to hold the large castle, Theon has [Stygg](wiki:Stygg) ride to Deepwood to request aid from Asha.(wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) She visits Theon at Winterfell, but he refuses to give up his new seat and Asha leaves him only with ten reinforcements.(wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) All ten abandon Theon when Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) approaches the castle, and Theon is soon captured by [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) during the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

After the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) orders Robett Glover to attack [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale). Following the [battle at Duskendale](wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale), Roose tells Robb Stark that Robett attacked out of grief and fear for the fall of Deepwood.(wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Asha is eventually taken prisoner by King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) during the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)(wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
