## Origins

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) smashes the river lords' forces at the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Battle_in_the_hills_below_the_Golden_Tooth) and [Riverrun](wiki:Battle_under_the_walls_of_Riverrun), capturing the heir to the riverlands, Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully), and many of his [knights](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Jaime besieges [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun),(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) the capital of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), while the host of his father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), closes the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad) to the east(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) and methodically captures the castles of river lords.(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-2)

Unbeknownst to Jaime, [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) has forged an alliance with [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey), [Lord of the Crossing](wiki:Lord_of_the_Crossing), securing passage over the [Green Fork](wiki:Green_Fork) of the [Trident](wiki:Trident).(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Joined by additional soldiers from [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey) of the [Twins](wiki:Twins) and [House Mallister](wiki:House_Mallister) of [Seagard](wiki:Seagard), Robb moves on Riverrun from the north in an attempt to outflank the unsuspecting Lannister forces and relieve the besieged forces at Riverrun. Robb manages to keep his march secret, thanks to the efforts of his great uncle, Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully), and three hundred picked outriders, who shoot down Lannister [ravens](wiki:Raven) and slay Jaime's scouts.(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-22) Ser [Marq Piper](wiki:Marq_Piper) separately harries the Lannister supply trains with half a hundred men.(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Jaime leaves his camp at Riverrun three times to deal with raiders or stubborn holdfasts.(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-23)

## Narrative Arc

Robb sends Brynden with a few hundred men carrying [Tully](wiki:House_Tully) banners to raid and draw Jaime from his camp at Riverrun. Expecting to crush a small force, Jaime and most of his cavalry are lured north of the [Tumblestone](wiki:Tumblestone) to a [forested valley](wiki:Whispering_Wood) where the rest of Robb's men are hidden.(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-24) Lady [Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont) and Lord [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister) wait east of the valley, while Lord [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark) is to the north. West is Ser [Stevron Frey](wiki:Stevron_Frey), and across a far ridge Lord [Greatjon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber). Robb's mother, Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), waits high on a ridge under the protection of thirty guards led by [Hallis Mollen](wiki:Hallis_Mollen).(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-25)

Once all of Jaime's unsuspecting men have entered the valley, Catelyn hears the sound of [snow shrikes](wiki:Snow_shrike), possibly alerts by the waiting northmen. The [Mormonts](wiki:House_Mormont) sound horns to signal the moonlit trap has been sprung, followed by [Umbers](wiki:House_Umber), [Mallisters](wiki:House_Mallister), Freys, and [Karstarks](wiki:House_Karstark). Jaime's horsemen are attacked by bowmen hidden in the trees by Robb, and the Young Wolf and northern bannermen lead their own cavalry against the ambushed [westermen](wiki:Westermen). Robb is joined in the fighting by his [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind).(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-26) [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy), Robb's friend, kills men in the battle.(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

When Jaime sees the battle is lost, he rallies his retainers and attempts to cut his way through the host to slay Robb in single combat. He is halted, but not before slaying [Daryn Hornwood](wiki:Daryn_Hornwood), [Eddard Karstark](wiki:Eddard_Karstark), and [Torrhen Karstark](wiki:Torrhen_Karstark) of [Robb's personal guard](wiki:Robb's_personal_guard).(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-27) Grey Wind howls as dawn breaks and the fighting winds down.(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-28)

## Aftermath

According to Theon, the Lannisters lose ten men for every one of Robb's who fall.(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-29) Jaime is captured, along with near a hundred knights and a dozen lords bannermen including Lord [Gawen Westerling](wiki:Gawen_Westerling), Lord [Quenten Banefort](wiki:Quenten_Banefort), Ser [Garth Greenfield](wiki:Garth_Greenfield), Lord [Regenard Estren](wiki:Regenard_Estren), Ser [Tytos Brax](wiki:Tytos_Brax), [Mallor the Dornishman](wiki:Mallor_the_Dornishman), [Willem Lannister](wiki:Willem_Lannister), and the Lannister cousins [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) and [Tion Frey](wiki:Tion_Frey).(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-30)

The capture of Jaime leaves the Lannister army surrounding Riverrun leaderless, leading directly to its disintegration at the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps) and leaving Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) and Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) despondent.(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1)(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Rumors reach [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) that [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind) tore out Jaime's throat in the battle.(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

Having Jaime as a hostage, and recognizing his value as a prisoner, the Starks gain enormous bargaining power. The deaths of Eddard and Torrhen Karstark by Jaime's hand plant a seed of vengeance in the heart of their father, Lord [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark), however.(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-2)(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) Gawen is taken to [Seagard](wiki:Seagard), Quenten to [Stone Hedge](wiki:Stone_Hedge), Garth to [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall), and Regenard and Tytos to the [Twins](wiki:Twins). Willem, Cleos, and Tion are held hostage at Riverrun.(wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
