## Narrative Arc

Many of the [riverlords](wiki:Riverlords) return to their lands after the [battle under the walls of Riverrun](wiki:Battle_under_the_walls_of_Riverrun). The [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) army, led by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) and Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), marches on each stronghold in turn.(wiki:First_sack_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) At some point, the town of Maidenpool is sacked by "lions", [westermen](wiki:Westermen) soldiers sworn to the Lannisters. Lord [William Mooton](wiki:William_Mooton) remains in his castle and does not defend the town.(wiki:First_sack_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
