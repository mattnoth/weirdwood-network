## Origins

Reports of the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) taking the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands) reach [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). The ironborn are said to have a thousand ships, although this is most likely an exaggeration. Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) demands that the [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet), which is besieging [Storm’s End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(300)) and [Dragonstone](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone), be allowed to leave the stormlands and the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) in order to defend the Reach, but Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) refuses, stating that the two castles remain a danger to King's Landing and King [Tommen I Baratheon's](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) rule as they remain loyal to a rival king, [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).

Margaery's brother, Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell), offers to lead a charge on the walls of Dragonstone in order to quickly end the siege, which Cersei accepts.(wiki:Battle_on_Whispering_Sound.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) A fortnight later it is reported that Loras has successfully taken the castle, being grievously wounded in the process, which allows for the Redwyne fleet to make for the Reach.(wiki:Battle_on_Whispering_Sound.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

In the meantime, [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) has ordered his fleet to carry out attacks on settlements of the [Mander](wiki:Mander), the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor), and Whispering Sound. King's Landing is informed of ironborn raids up Whispering Sound, threatening [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), one of the largest cities in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Battle_on_Whispering_Sound.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

Fighting takes place between ironborn longships and ships of the Reach.(wiki:Battle_on_Whispering_Sound.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) The victor of the battle is unknown, although [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) spots burnt villages and fields on the banks of Whispering Sound, suggesting the ironborn were successful in raiding some settlements along the inlet.(wiki:Battle_on_Whispering_Sound.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Many destroyed merchant and fishing boats are spotted on shallows and sandbars, as well as longships and two dromonds;(wiki:Battle_on_Whispering_Sound.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) it is unknown if the dromonds belong to the [Hightowers](wiki:House_Hightower), [Bulwers](wiki:House_Bulwer), or [Costaynes](wiki:House_Costayne), families with lands along Whispering Sound. One half-sunken longship bearing the flag of the [Crow’s Eye](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) is spotted.(wiki:Battle_on_Whispering_Sound.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
