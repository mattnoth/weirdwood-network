## Identity

While climbing the abandoned First Keep at Winterfell during King Robert's visit, Bran Stark stumbles on Queen Cersei Lannister and her twin brother Ser Jaime coupling in a tower room. Bran recognizes them both. The discovery is the spark of the whole chain: it is *because* Bran has seen them that Jaime pushes him from the window moments later.
