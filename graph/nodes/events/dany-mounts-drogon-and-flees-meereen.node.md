## Identity

In the bloody chaos after [Drogon descends on Daznak's Pit](drogon-returns-to-daznak-pit), [Daenerys](daenerys-targaryen) runs into the arena rather than flee it. She takes up the dead pitmaster's whip, forces the wounded [Drogon](drogon) to submit through will and lash ("I cannot let him see my fear"), then vaults onto his back, seizes the half-melted spear lodged in his neck, and rips it free. Drogon's wings crack like thunder and the scarlet sands fall away beneath her; she cries "Higher!" and the dragon climbs out of the pit and over the pyramids of Meereen. The flight is unsteady and unsaddled — Drogon is wounded, she cannot steer him — and it carries her out of the city for good, ending her reign as Queen of Meereen and beginning the [Dothraki-sea sojourn](dany-lost-on-dothraki-sea).
