## Identity

After [Jaime Lannister](jaime-lannister) [pushes him from the tower](jaime-pushes-bran-from-the-tower), [Bran](bran-stark) falls into a long coma. In the coma he dreams of a three-eyed crow — in fact [Brynden Rivers](brynden-rivers), the last greenseer, reaching through the weirwood net — who urges him to *choose: fly or die*. Bran chooses to fly, and wakes to find his direwolf [Summer](summer) on his bed. This is the origin of the warg/greenseer gift that drives the whole of Bran's arc, from the tower window to the weirwood throne. (The waking is folded into this beat as its terminus — it owns no distinct outgoing causal edge.)
