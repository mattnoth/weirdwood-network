## Identity

The killing of Lord Rickard Stark and his heir Brandon Stark by King Aerys II Targaryen in the throne room of the Red Keep, 282 AC, after Brandon rode to King's Landing demanding Rhaegar answer for Lyanna's abduction. Aerys roasted Rickard in his armor while Brandon strangled himself trying to reach a sword to save his father. The atrocity turned the Starks, Arryns, and Tullys decisively against the crown — a direct cause of Robert's Rebellion.
