## Origins

The town of [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge) became crowded from refugees fleeing the host of [greens](wiki:Greens) led by Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower). A disguised Ser [Rickard Thorne](wiki:Rickard_Thorne) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) and his charge, Prince [Maelor Targaryen](wiki:Maelor_Targaryen), tried to stay overnight in the [Hogs Head](wiki:Hogs_Head), a Bitterbridge inn, while journeying to the safety of Ormund's host at the nearby [siege of Longtable](wiki:Siege_of_Longtable). [Sly](wiki:Sly_(stableboy)), a stableboy for the inn's proprietor, [Ben Buttercakes](wiki:Ben_Buttercakes), informed the inn's common room that he had discovered Maelor's [dragon egg](wiki:Dragon_egg). After slaying Ben, Rickard rode for the southern shore of the [Mander](wiki:Mander), but he was killed by crossbowmen at the bridge. [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom) claims [Willow Pound-Stone](wiki:Willow_Pound-Stone) accidentally crushed Maelor to death, [Eustace](wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)) says he was chopped into six pieces by a butcher, and [Munkun](wiki:Munkun) writes that Maelor was torn apart by a mob.(wiki:Sack_of_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

[Lady Caswell](wiki:Lady_Caswell), whose [lord husband](wiki:Lord_Caswell_(Dance)) had earlier been executed by Maelor's father, King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), was aghast at the violence. She hanged Willow, Sly, and the man whose horse had been stolen by Rickard. Then, she sent Rickard's corpse and Maelor's head to Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), while Maelor's dragon egg to was sent to Lord Ormund Hightower at [Longtable](wiki:Longtable). Ormund was generous after Longtable was yielded, taking its wealth and grains but doing no harm to the Merryweathers.(wiki:Sack_of_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Aftermath

Refugees from Bitterbridge fled to [Tumbleton](wiki:Tumbleton). Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)) later named [Ulf the White](wiki:Ulf_the_White) as [Lord of Bitterbridge](wiki:Lord_of_Bitterbridge) after the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton).(wiki:Sack_of_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
