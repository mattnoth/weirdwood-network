## Origins

After King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) executed Lord [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark) and his heir, [Brandon Stark](wiki:Brandon_Stark), in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), he sent word to Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) to execute his wards, [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), the new Lord of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), and [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), the Lord of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End). Instead, Jon sent back only defiance.(wiki:Taking_of_Gulltown.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_robert.27s_rebellion.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Ned Stark left the Eyrie to gather his [bannermen](wiki:Bannermen) from the [north](wiki:North), while Lord Arryn began to call his own vassals from the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) in rebellion to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). The city of [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown) remained loyal to the [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) dynasty, however, and rather than sailing from there Ned returned to the north via the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon), the [Fingers](wiki:Fingers), and [Sisterton](wiki:Sisterton).(wiki:Taking_of_Gulltown.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Jon Arryn also faced opposition from loyalists attempting to bring him down.(wiki:Taking_of_Gulltown.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_robert.27s_rebellion.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

While Ned was making his way north, Lord Arryn led the rebel bannermen from the Vale against Gulltown, which was defended by [House Grafton](wiki:House_Grafton). Robert, a close friend of Ned, was the first of the rebels to gain the walls of Gulltown. Robert slew Lord [Marq Grafton](wiki:Marq_Grafton) with his own hand.(wiki:Taking_of_Gulltown.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Aftermath

The taking of Gulltown by [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) allowed the Defender of the Vale to consolidate rebel control in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn). [Lyn Corbray](wiki:Lyn_Corbray) won his [knighthood](wiki:Knight) fighting alongside [House Grafton](wiki:House_Grafton) at the gates of Gulltown, but he joined Lord Arryn after the battle.(wiki:Taking_of_Gulltown.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) returned to the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) to raise his own bannermen, which led to the [battles at Summerhall](wiki:Battles_at_Summerhall).(wiki:Taking_of_Gulltown.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [Ned Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) survived the journey across the [Bite](wiki:Bite) and raised his [northern](wiki:North) bannermen.(wiki:Taking_of_Gulltown.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
