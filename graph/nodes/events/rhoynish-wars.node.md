## Origins

Although the expansion of the Valyrians into the western coastal regions of [Essos](wiki:Essos) was welcomed by the Rhoynar at first, the Valyrian outposts eventually became a threat to the great city-states of the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne). What began as disputes between the citizens of rivaling Valyrian and Rhoynish cities, grew into a series of short-lived and bloody wars. The first war was the [First Turtle War](wiki:First_Turtle_War), fought between [Sar Mell](wiki:Sar_Mell) and [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys). Over the course of two and a half centuries, other wars were fought, including the [War of Three Princes](wiki:War_of_Three_Princes), the [Second Turtle War](wiki:Second_Turtle_War), the [Fisherman's War](wiki:Fisherman's_War), the [Salt War](wiki:Salt_War), the [Third Turtle War](wiki:Third_Turtle_War), the [War on Dagger Lake](wiki:War_on_Dagger_Lake), and the [Spice War](wiki:Spice_War), among many others.(wiki:Rhoynish_Wars.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The conflict escalated when [Sarhoy](wiki:Sarhoy) was destroyed by the Valyrian [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlord) in the [Second Spice War](wiki:Second_Spice_War). The remaining five Rhoynish city-states were united by Prince [Garin](wiki:Garin) of [Chroyane](wiki:Chroyane), to end the threat of the Valyrians once and for all. Garin achieved early successes at [Selhorys](wiki:Selhorys), [Valysar](wiki:Valysar) and Volon Therys, which caused the dragonlords in Valyria to concentrate their full attention and power on the Rhoynar as never before. After a massive battle outside [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) involving three hundred [dragons](wiki:Dragon), Garin's army was crushed and he himself was captured. The cities of Sar Mell and Chroyane were sacked and their people enslaved.(wiki:Rhoynish_Wars.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
In response, Princess [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria), the ruler of [Ny Sar](wiki:Ny_Sar), quickly led her people out of Essos using whatever ships and barges they could find. The story of their flight became known as the [ten thousand ships](wiki:Ten_thousand_ships). Their refugee fleet wandered the lands of the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea) for many years before eventually arriving in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), the desert land in southern [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Rhoynish_Wars.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
