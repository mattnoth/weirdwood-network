## Origins

After the death of King [Daeron I Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen) in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) under a peace banner, Prince [Aemon the Dragonknight](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_II)) was imprisoned by Lord Wyl in a [cage](wiki:Crow_cage). King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen) tried to obtain Aemon's release during his journey to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear), but Lord Wyl refused. After negotiating peace with the [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne_(father_of_Maron)), Baelor found that Aemon's cage was now suspended over a pit of [vipers](wiki:Snake). Lord Wyl obeyed the king's command to release the Dragonknight from captivity, but gave the key to Baelor instead of having his men open the cage.
