## Identity

Summoned before the Iron Throne, Rogar Baratheon kneels and submits himself to the king's judgment; Jaehaerys pardons his 'foolish words' on the condition that Rogar champion Queen Alysanne and honor Queen Alyssa, remarries his mother and former Hand to each other, and sentences Orryn to ten years' exile rather than death -- closing the regency's final crisis without bloodshed.
