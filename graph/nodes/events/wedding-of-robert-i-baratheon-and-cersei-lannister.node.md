## Origins

### Prelude[ ]

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), tried to betroth his daughter [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) to [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone), at the [tourney in Lannisport](wiki:Tournament_in_honor_of_Viserys's_birth) in honor of [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen)'s birth in [276 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), but King Aerys refused to marry "his heir to his servant's daughter".(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Rhaegar instead married Princess [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell) of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) in [279 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
In [282 AC](wiki:282_AC), Rhaegar abducted [Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark), who was betrothed to [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End). During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) against [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen), Rhaegar was slain by Robert in the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) In order to prove his loyalty to Robert,(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Tywin led the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing), during which Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), his son and Cersei's twin, slew Aerys in the [throne room](wiki:Throne_room).(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) According to a semi-canon source, Lyanna died at the [tower of joy](wiki:Tower_of_joy) in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Rawoiaflyanna_stark.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Robert claimed the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) and [succeeded Aerys as king](wiki:Coronation_of_Robert_I_Baratheon) in [283 AC](wiki:283_AC).(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_reign_of_the_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), the new Hand, advised Robert to marry Cersei to bind [House Baratheon of King's Landing](wiki:House_Baratheon_of_King's_Landing) with powerful [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### Wedding[ ]

According to Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel), [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s first act as king was to marry [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister).(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_glorious_reign.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), having been pardoned by Robert and allowed to remain in the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), served in the honor guard which brought his sister to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing);(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Robert was unaware the twins had an [incestuous](wiki:Incest) relationship.(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Robert replaced the [skulls of the Targaryen dragons](wiki:Skulls_of_the_Targaryen_dragons) in the [throne room](wiki:Great_Hall_(Red_Keep)) of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) with banners and tapestries. Hoping to see the skulls when he arrived for the wedding, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), Cersei's youngest brother, eventually found them in a cellar beneath the castle.(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
Cersei and Jaime had sex on the morning of the wedding.(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Robert and Cersei were wed later in the day in the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor).(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) During the changing of the cloaks, Robert draped Cersei with a heavy [cloth-of-gold](wiki:Cloth-of-gold) cloak with the [stag](wiki:Stag) of [House Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon) depicted with beads of [onyx](wiki:Onyx).(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Thousands cheered at the plaza outside when the couple left the sept, though Cersei noticed that her brother Jaime was unhappy.(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) When Robert and Cersei slept together for the first time the night of the wedding feast, the drunken king whispered [Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark)'s name instead of his bride's.(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
Jaime was champion in the tourney held at the capital for the wedding.(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) Cersei's uncle, [Gerion Lannister](wiki:Gerion_Lannister), gave King Robert a gilded dagger with an [ivory](wiki:Ivory) grip and a [sapphire](wiki:Sapphire) pommel as a wedding gift.(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Robert appointed Ser [Ilyn Payne](wiki:Ilyn_Payne), whose tongue had been removed by the late King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), as the [King's Justice](wiki:King's_Justice) as his own gift for Tywin.(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### Aftermath[ ]

Two of Robert's [Estermont](wiki:House_Estermont) uncles remained at court in King's Landing for half a year following the wedding. Robert insisted on repaying the visit, with Queen Cersei and members of his court accompanying him for a two-week stay at [Greenstone](wiki:Greenstone). While there, Cersei suspected that Robert was bedding a [widowed cousin](wiki:Estermont_(cousin_of_Robert)) he used to play with as a child, and the queen had Jaime follow Robert to confirm her suspicions. Cersei and Jaime slept together at Greenstone, and Cersei likes to believe that was the night their eldest child, Prince [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), was conceived.(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
The marriage between Robert and Cersei continued to deteriorate over the years,(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) and Cersei and Jaime continued their clandestine incest.(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) Robert believes himself to be the father of Joffrey and the prince's siblings, Princess [Myrcella](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) and Prince [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) tells his childhood friend, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), about his resentment towards Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) and his yearning for Ned's late sister, [Lyanna](wiki:Lyanna_Stark).(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

For the wedding of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) in the [royal sept](wiki:Royal_sept), Cersei, now the Queen Regent, wants Tommen to use the red silk cloak which her father, Lord [Tywin](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), had used when he married her mother, [Joanna Lannister](wiki:Joanna_Lannister). Lady [Olenna Tyrell](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell) objects, however, and Tommen instead uses the heavy gold-and-black cloak from his parents' wedding.(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1)
Cersei recalls her regret that Robert survived the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident) instead of Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen).(wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1)
