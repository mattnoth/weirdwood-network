## Identity

The Andal Invasion was the migration and conquest of Westeros by the Andals, a people originating from Essos (the hills of Andalos, in the eastern part of the continent). It occurred approximately 6,000 years before the main series. The Andals crossed the Narrow Sea and gradually conquered the southern Six Kingdoms, displacing or absorbing the First Men, destroying weirwood trees, and spreading the Faith of the Seven. The North (ruled by the Kings of Winter, ancestors of House Stark) successfully resisted Andal conquest. The Andal Invasion fundamentally transformed Westerosi culture, religion, and bloodlines throughout the south.
