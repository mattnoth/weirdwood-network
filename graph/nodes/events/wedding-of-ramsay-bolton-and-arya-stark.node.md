## Origins

[Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell) and [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), brings his daughters [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark) with him to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) after being named [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) by [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) During [Ned's arrest](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark) after King Robert's death, his daughter Arya evade captures and escapes the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Sansa and her friend [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole) are apprehended by [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister), however, and Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) offers to find a place for Jeyne as her father [Vayon](wiki:Vayon_Poole) has been killed.(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
The [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) breaks out in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), with [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) declared [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun)(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) after King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) orders the execution of his father, Lord Eddard.(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The Lannisters claim to hold captive both Sansa and Arya in the capital, but Robb and his mother [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) fear the worst for Arya after receiving word only from Sansa.(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Unbeknownst to the warring parties, Arya has fled the capital and is traveling incognito through the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Racok1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
[Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), the [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son of [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), [Lord of the Dreadfort](wiki:Lord_of_the_Dreadfort), abducts Lady [Donella Hornwood](wiki:Donella_Hornwood) and forces her to marry him. Ramsay claims the title [Lord of the Hornwood](wiki:Lord_of_the_Hornwood) and leaves Donella to die in a tower.(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy), once a ward of [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark), leads the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) by [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn),(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) and he announces the supposed deaths of [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) after they disappear from [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Ramsay orders the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell) after betraying Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel)'s host of Stark loyalists.(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) At [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Roose tells Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) that Arya has been found and he will be bringing her back to the [north](wiki:North).(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Robb and Catelyn are both slain at the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), with Roose personally killing the King in the North.(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Sansa disappears from King's Landing after Joffrey's death in the [Purple Wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding),(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) with Petyr secreting her to the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn).(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
As a reward for [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton)'s aid in the downfall of [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark), Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) has young King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) sign documents naming Roose as the new Warden of the North and legitimizing his son as Ramsay Bolton.(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) With the Starks believed dead or disappeared, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), the new Hand, has [Steelshanks Walton](wiki:Steelshanks_Walton), Roose's captain, escort a northern girl claimed to be Arya away from King's Landing, and the girl tells Jaime in passing that the she is to wed Ramsay. Jaime thinks that the girl is older than Arya should have been, and he later tells [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth) about the deceit.(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1)
At the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort), Ramsay tells his tortured captive Theon that he will become Lord of Winterfell with Arya as his lady,(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) When Roose Bolton returns to the north with "Arya" after Theon resolves the [siege of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin), Theon recognizes that the girl with him is actually Jeyne Poole, the steward's daughter.(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

## Aftermath

[Ramsay](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) confines [Jeyne](wiki:Jeyne_Poole) to her bedchamber after their wedding night, and he continues to bruise and mistreat her.(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0) Lady [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin) and Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) worry that the sobbing of "Arya" threatens Ramsay's hold on [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-1)
[Northmen](wiki:Northmen) who have joined [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)—including [Morgan Liddle](wiki:Morgan_Liddle), [Brandon Norrey](wiki:Brandon_Norrey), [Hugo Wull](wiki:Hugo_Wull), [Alysane Mormont](wiki:Alysane_Mormont), and the [Flints](wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains)—support his [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell) so they can rescue "[the Ned's](wiki:Eddard_Stark) girl".(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)
Rather than Arya coming to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) as prophesied by [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre), [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark) arrives at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) seeking aid from [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) after fleeing a potential marriage with her cousin [Cregan](wiki:Cregan_Karstark) at [Karhold](wiki:Karhold).(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)
[Abel](wiki:Mance_Rayder) and his [spearwives](wiki:Spearwives) enlist Theon in their mission to free "Arya", and Theon helps Jeyne escape Winterfell.(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0) They eventually encounter Theon's sister [Asha](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) at a [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village) in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood).(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)
At Castle Black, Jon receives [a letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) from Ramsay in which he demands the return of his bride.(wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)
