## Identity

Wedding of Stannis Baratheon and Selyse Florent is a battle/event from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges


## Origins

### Wedding[ ]

While Lord [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) and Lady [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) were dancing at their wedding feast, Stannis's elder brother, King [Robert](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), [deflowered](wiki:Maiden) [Delena Florent](wiki:Delena_Florent), Selyse's cousin and one of her [bedmaids](wiki:Bedmaid), in the wedding bed.(wiki:Wedding_of_Stannis_Baratheon_and_Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Robert later told Stannis he had not recognized the bedchamber because he was drunk, and had not meant to cause shame.(wiki:Wedding_of_Stannis_Baratheon_and_Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) However, Stannis felt his brother's actions were an insult to [House Florent](wiki:House_Florent).(wiki:Wedding_of_Stannis_Baratheon_and_Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### Aftermath[ ]

When Delena gave birth to Robert's [bastard](wiki:Bastard), [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm), Stannis had the infant sent to [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) to be [fostered](wiki:Fostered) by his younger brother, Lord [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon).(wiki:Wedding_of_Stannis_Baratheon_and_Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) offered to betroth his son [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to Delena, but her father, Ser [Colin Florent](wiki:Colin_Florent), refused, and instead married her to one of his [household knights](wiki:Household_knight), Ser [Hosman Norcross](wiki:Hosman_Norcross).(wiki:Wedding_of_Stannis_Baratheon_and_Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Stannis only does his duty in the marriage bed once or twice a year, taking no joy in it.(wiki:Wedding_of_Stannis_Baratheon_and_Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Though he once hoped for sons, Stannis has only had a daughter, [Shireen](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon), with Selyse.(wiki:Wedding_of_Stannis_Baratheon_and_Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Selyse believes that Robert and Delena's dalliance cursed her marriage and made her unable to birth a son.(wiki:Wedding_of_Stannis_Baratheon_and_Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Some years before the start of [A Song of Ice and Fire](wiki:A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire), Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) informed Robert's [small council](wiki:Small_council) that Selyse had taken up with a [red priestess](wiki:Red_priest), [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) of [Asshai](wiki:Asshai), at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Wedding_of_Stannis_Baratheon_and_Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), [Lord of Dragonstone](wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone), announces his claim for the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), with [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) as his queen.(wiki:Wedding_of_Stannis_Baratheon_and_Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)(wiki:Wedding_of_Stannis_Baratheon_and_Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) of [Asshai](wiki:Asshai) advises Stannis to sacrifice [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm), his [bastard](wiki:Bastard) nephew, so that his [king's blood](wiki:Bloodmagic) can be used to awaken a stone [dragon](wiki:Dragon) at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Wedding_of_Stannis_Baratheon_and_Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) To save Edric, Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) has the boy smuggled across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea).(wiki:Wedding_of_Stannis_Baratheon_and_Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Selyse has often asked Stannis that they marry again, this time in a ceremony under the [Lord of Light](wiki:R'hllor).(wiki:Wedding_of_Stannis_Baratheon_and_Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) believes that Melisandre is Stannis's true queen,(wiki:Wedding_of_Stannis_Baratheon_and_Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) and that the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) might attend Selyse but actually serve the [red priestess](wiki:Red_priest).(wiki:Wedding_of_Stannis_Baratheon_and_Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
