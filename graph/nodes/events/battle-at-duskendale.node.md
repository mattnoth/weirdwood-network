## Origins

While at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) learns of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s defeat in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater) and the [siege of Darry](wiki:Siege_of_Darry) by Ser [Helman Tallhart](wiki:Helman_Tallhart). He orders Helman to kill his [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) captives and put Darry to the torch, and then march with [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover) to [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) in the mostly-unharmed [crownlands](wiki:Crownlands). With [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Taking_of_Torrhen's_Square) and [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte), their strongholds in the north, having been seized by the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), Roose states both men are keen for vengeance.(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Roose also sends [Harrion Karstark](wiki:Harrion_Karstark) with the remaining [Karstarks](wiki:House_Karstark) at Harrenhal.(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The army, a third of the foot(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), burns its way towards Duskendale.(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Meanwhile, after the victory over Stannis at the Blackwater and the new alliance between [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) and [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell), the two houses loyal to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) have combined their forces. The [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), sends Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) with a sizeable army, composed of men from the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) and the [Reach](wiki:Reach), to catch the northern force at Duskendale and throw them back. Tywin also sends Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) with [westermen](wiki:Westermen) to cut off their retreat on the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad).(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

## Narrative Arc

Northern participants include Houses [Cerwyn](wiki:House_Cerwyn), [Glover](wiki:House_Glover), [Hornwood](wiki:House_Hornwood), [Karstark](wiki:House_Karstark), and [Tallhart](wiki:House_Tallhart).(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

The army loyal to [House Baratheon of King's Landing](wiki:House_Baratheon_of_King's_Landing) includes men from the [Reach](wiki:Reach), the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands), and the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands). Named participants include Houses [Tarly](wiki:House_Tarly), [Leygood](wiki:House_Leygood), [Ambrose](wiki:House_Ambrose), [Fossoway of Cider Hall](wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall), [Fossoway of New Barrel](wiki:House_Fossoway_of_New_Barrel), and [Tarly](wiki:House_Tarly).(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) catches [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover) outside of Duskendale and traps him against [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay). Ser [Helman Tallhart](wiki:Helman_Tallhart) is killed during the fierce battle,(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) which takes places in the fields and farms outside Duskendale.(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) There are heavy losses on both sides, including possibly a thousand dead northmen. As Robett falls back toward Harrenhal he encounters Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) near the kingsroad,(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) and the northern host is broken.(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Aftermath

[Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover) is taken prisoner by [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane),(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) while [Gregor's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) hunt stragglers.(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Gregor is reportedly wounded in the fighting.(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Robett eventually sails from Duskendale in an exchange for [Martyn Lannister](wiki:Martyn_Lannister),(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) but [Harrion Karstark](wiki:Harrion_Karstark) is held in captivity at [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool).(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

The northern foot is destroyed, which hurts [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s ability to retake the north. It is later revealed that [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) had secretly defected to the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) side and in his attempt to diminish the northern lords' ability to attack him, he had sent much of the northern army to its destruction.(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_robett_glover.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

The northmen are buried in a common grave by the sea marked only by a wooden sign reading "HERE LIE THE WOLVES", while [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly)'s less numerous dead are buried in heroes' tombs beneath the [septs](wiki:Sept) of Duskendale. [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) notices many used weapons, armor, and clothing from the battle available for sale at Duskendale's market square.(wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3)
