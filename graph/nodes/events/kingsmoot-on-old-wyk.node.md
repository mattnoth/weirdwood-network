## Identity

The Kingsmoot on Old Wyk is the assembly of ironborn captains and kings convened on the holy isle of [Old Wyk](old-wyk) at [Nagga's Hill](naggas-hill) to choose a successor after [Balon Greyjoy's death](death-of-balon-greyjoy) — the first true kingsmoot held in thousands of years. [Aeron Damphair](aeron-greyjoy) summons it in the name of the Drowned God to deny the godless [Euron Greyjoy](euron-greyjoy), who has already [seized the Seastone Chair](euron-seizes-the-seastone-chair). [Asha Greyjoy](asha-greyjoy) (offering peace and the north) and [Victarion Greyjoy](victarion-greyjoy) (offering to finish Balon's war) both stake claims, but Euron wins the driftwood crown by sounding the Valyrian dragon-horn [Dragonbinder](dragonbinder) and promising to conquer all of Westeros. Retyped from `event.battle` to `event.ceremony` (S116) — it is an election/assembly, not an armed engagement.

## Origins

[Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), seeks independence for the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). Declaring himself [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands),(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Balon sends the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) captains and the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) to attack the [north](wiki:North), as [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), is away fighting in southern [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Balon styles himself [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North) after parts of the north are conquered by his captains.(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy), Balon's surviving son, styles himself [Prince of Winterfell](wiki:Prince_of_Winterfell),(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) but he disappears after the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Theon is feared dead after his sister, [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), unsuccessfully searches for him in the ruins of Winterfell.(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Unbeknownst to [House Greyjoy](wiki:House_Greyjoy), Theon has been taken by [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) to the dungeons of the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort).(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
King Balon falls to his death while crossing one of the bridges of [Pyke](wiki:Pyke).(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Shortly afterward, his younger brother, [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), returns from exile and claims the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair) in Pyke. Lord [Sawane Botley](wiki:Sawane_Botley) objects to Euron's actions, so Euron has Sawane drowned in a cask of seawater.(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) The [Myraham](wiki:Myraham) escapes from [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport) and returns to the mainland. After hearing of Balon's death from the [ship's captain](wiki:Captain_of_the_Myraham), Robb Stark plans a [siege of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin).(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)
Balon's youngest brother, [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy), learns of the king's death while initiating [drowned men](wiki:Drowned_men) on [Great Wyk](wiki:Great_Wyk). Considering Euron to be a godless man unworthy of sitting the Seastone Chair, Aeron Damphair calls for a [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot) to choose the new king to be held on the holy isle of [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk),(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) as [Nagga's Hill](wiki:Nagga's_Hill) was where the [High Kings of the Iron Islands](wiki:High_Kings_of_the_Iron_Islands) were chosen in antiquity.(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Aeron's fellow [priests of the Drowned God](wiki:Priests_of_the_Drowned_God) spread word across the Iron Islands.(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
At [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers), [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw), [Lord of Harlaw](wiki:Lord_of_Harlaw), tells Asha that the last kingsmoot was held four thousand years according to Archmaester [Haereg](wiki:Haereg) or two thousand years according to Maester [Denestan](wiki:Denestan).(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) Rodrik the Reader does not want to sail to Old Wyk, but he ultimately agrees to in order to support his niece in her claim.(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Asha spends four days meeting with ironborn as they arrive at Old Wyk and realizes her support is not as strong as she needs.(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
[Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) sails to [Nagga's Cradle](wiki:Nagga's_Cradle) with most of the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet), leaving a small force behind at the [Fever River](wiki:Fever_River).(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) Men from every isle have sailed to Old Wyk for the kingsmoot; Victarion sees a multitude of ships along the strand. Victarion provides a feast for half a hundred famous captains on the night before the kingsmoot, with roasted kid, salted [cod](wiki:Cod), [lobster](wiki:Lobster), and [ale](wiki:Ale) served to the guests. Asha offers to share rule with her uncle and serve as his [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), but Victarion declines.(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2)

## Aftermath

As [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy)'s followers celebrate his selection, [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) flees [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk) on [Black Wind](wiki:Black_Wind) as her uncle, [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw), fears that Euron will kill her or force her to wed.(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) While at [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers), Asha learns that Euron has wed her to [Erik Ironmaker](wiki:Erik_Ironmaker), with a seal standing in as her substitute.(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) At [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte), [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley) tells Asha the tale of [Torgon Greyiron](wiki:Torgon_Greyiron), explaining that the absence of Torgon the Latecomer was used to denounce the kingsmoot of [Urrathon IV Goodbrother](wiki:Urrathon_IV_Goodbrother) as unlawful.(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-2)
[Baelor Blacktyde](wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde) refuses to recognize Euron as king. When he attempts to return to [Blacktyde](wiki:Blacktyde), however, Baelor is caught by the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) in [Nagga's Cradle](wiki:Nagga's_Cradle) and delivered to Euron. The new king has the [Lord of Blacktyde](wiki:Lord_of_Blacktyde) cut into seven body parts.(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)
Upset at their brother's victory, [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) tells [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) that he will raise the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) against the new king. The Damphair disappears from Old Wyk,(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2) however, and Erik persecutes [drowned men](wiki:Drowned_men) and [priests of the Drowned God](wiki:Priests_of_the_Drowned_God).(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-3)
Euron sails with the loyal [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) and the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) against the [Reach](wiki:Reach), beginning with the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields).(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-3) Erik remains behind to serve as [Lord Steward of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_Steward_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
