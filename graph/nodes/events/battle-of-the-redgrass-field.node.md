## Origins

King [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen)'s trueborn son and heir, [Daeron](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen), was born to his sister-wife [Naerys](wiki:Naerys_Targaryen) in [153 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). Seventeen years later, Aegon's cousin, Princess [Daena Targaryen](wiki:Daena_Targaryen), gave birth to the king's eldest known [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son, [Daemon Waters](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) King Aegon IV publicly acknowledged Daemon in [182 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) and gave him the [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) sword [Blackfyre](wiki:Blackfyre),(wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)(wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field.cite_ref-greatbastards_8-0) which had previously been passed on from king to king ever since King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), the Conqueror. Because of this, there were some who felt that the sword symbolized the monarchy,(wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field.cite_ref-9) and that Aegon's public gift of the sword was an indication that he wanted Daemon to succeed him. Aegon's gift would eventually start the first talks that Daemon should be king.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field.cite_ref-targaryenhistory_10-0) Following the acquisition of the sword, Daemon took the name "Blackfyre" for himself.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) On his deathbed in [184 AC](wiki:184_AC), King Aegon IV legitimized all of his bastard children, including Daemon.

In [196 AC](wiki:196_AC), Daemon claimed the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), thereby challenging his half-brother, King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) The war that followed was known as the Blackfyre Rebellion (and decades later as the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion)). It lasted nigh on a year, during which time battles were fought between the rebels and loyalists in the [Vale](wiki:Vale), the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands), the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) and the [Reach](wiki:Reach), among other places.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

The war came to an end after nearly a year at the [Redgrass Field](wiki:Redgrass_Field). On the eve of the battle, Ser [Quentyn Ball](wiki:Quentyn_Ball), one of Daemon's key generals, was slain by a common archer.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Meanwhile, Lord [Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken) had gone across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) to hire forces, but storms delayed the return of him and the [Myrish](wiki:Myr) crossbowmen he had hired.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Aftermath

Ten thousand men died on what would later be known as the [Redgrass Field](wiki:Redgrass_Field), presumably for all the blood sucked red.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6) [Daemon Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) and both of his eldest twin sons, [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Blackfyre) and [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Blackfyre), were slain in the battle, effectively ending the rebellion and confirming King [Daeron II's](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen) rule of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms). However, [Bittersteel](wiki:Aegor_Rivers) fled to the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) with many others (including the five surviving sons and several daughters of Daemon)(wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4) and the [Blackfyre Pretenders](wiki:Blackfyre_Pretenders) continued to plague the [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) dynasty until [Maelys the Monstrous](wiki:Maelys_Blackfyre) was slain in the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones).

It is suggested that the Redgrass Field was a close thing and if Daemon had not stopped for Gwayne, he might have broken Maekar's left before Bloodraven had gained the ridge and Baelor arrived.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7) Then, with the Hand slain and the road to King's Landing open, Daemon would have had little opposition.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-8)

Amongst the dead were Lady [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber)'s first husband, squire to Lord [Wyman Webber](wiki:Wyman_Webber);(wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-9) [Roger of Pennytree](wiki:Roger_of_Pennytree), Ser [Arlan of Pennytree](wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree)'s squire;(wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-10)(wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5) and [Lord Hayford](wiki:Lord_Hayford)(wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-11) slain by Lord [Gormon Peake](wiki:Gormon_Peake).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-6)
