## Origins

[Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), the new [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), takes his army from the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) into the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) while Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) is encamped at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal). Circumventing the border castles near the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth), Robb soundly defeats the new army being trained by Ser [Stafford Lannister](wiki:Stafford_Lannister) in the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross).(wiki:Taking_of_Ashemark.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

In the aftermath of Oxcross, Robb takes several castles of the westermen, including the castle of [Ashemark](wiki:Ashemark), the seat of [House Marbrand](wiki:House_Marbrand).(wiki:Taking_of_Ashemark.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind), Robb's [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), slays a man during the taking of the castle.(wiki:Taking_of_Ashemark.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) It is unknown if Lord [Damon Marbrand](wiki:Damon_Marbrand) is taken captive.

## Aftermath

Robb Stark continues plundering the westerlands,(wiki:Taking_of_Ashemark.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) moving onto [the Crag](wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag). After learning that [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) now [holds](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)) [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) has Maester [Vyman](wiki:Vyman) send a [raven](wiki:Raven) from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) to Ashemark, hoping that a rider can then be sent to inform Robb of Stannis.(wiki:Taking_of_Ashemark.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
