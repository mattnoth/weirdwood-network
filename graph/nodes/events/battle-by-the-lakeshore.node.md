## Origins

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) of the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) supported the [greens](wiki:Greens), while [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) of the [north](wiki:North) and most of the disorganized lords of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) supported the [blacks](wiki:Blacks). Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister) led an army eastward in support of [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), but Jason was killed by [Pate of Longleaf](wiki:Pate_of_Longleaf) in the [Battle at the Red Fork](wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork)(wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and Ser [Adrian Tarbeck](wiki:Adrian_Tarbeck) was later slain by Ser [Harry Penny](wiki:Harry_Penny).(wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The Lannister army continued slowly because of the age and infirmity of its wounded new commander, Lord [Humfrey Lefford](wiki:Humfrey_Lefford).(wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

When the [westermen](wiki:Westermen) reached the western shore of the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye), Lord Lefford discovered he was opposed by Lord [Roderick Dustin](wiki:Roderick_Dustin) with two thousand [Winter Wolves](wiki:Winter_Wolves) (all of them mounted), Lord [Forrest Frey](wiki:Forrest_Frey) with two hundred [knights](wiki:Knight) and six hundred infantrymen, and [Robb Rivers](wiki:Robb_Rivers) with three hundred [archers](wiki:Archer) from [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall). The westermen were quickly faced with another threat from the south, when Ser [Pate of Longleaf](wiki:Pate_of_Longleaf) arrived with survivors from previous battles in the war (possibly from the [Pipers](wiki:House_Piper) and [Vances](wiki:House_Vance)),(wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore.cite_ref-Combatant_3-0) as well as Lords [Bigglestone](wiki:Lord_Bigglestone), [Chambers](wiki:Lord_Chambers), and [Perryn](wiki:Lord_Perryn). The cautious Humfrey did not rush to face either enemy force, and thus be caught out of position in the field, but took up a defensive position with his back to the lakeshore. Humfrey then sent [ravens](wiki:Raven) to nearby [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) to seek assistance from Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen), but Red Robb shot down the dozen ravens. The blacks received further reinforcement with the arrival of more [riverlords](wiki:Riverlords) the following day, including Ser [Garibald Grey](wiki:Garibald_Grey), Lord [Jon Charlton](wiki:Jon_Charlton), and young Lord [Benjicot Blackwood](wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood).(wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Despite cornering the Lannister army, the black commanders now faced a difficult decision: the Lannisters had pulled back into a defensive position behind columns of disciplined pikemen, so a direct assault would still result in heavy losses. Nonetheless, they could not simply wait to starve out the Lannister army, because Aemond's host would eventually realize that communications had ceased, and rush to the aid of the Lannisters - bringing Aemond's [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar). Lord Dustin and the other black commanders therefore chose to rush the Lannister position while they still could.(wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Narrative Arc

The army of westermen were attacked at dawn the next morning. The Winter Wolves led the attack, their cavalry charging the Lannister spearmen five times, but suffering grievous losses in the attempts. Attacked by the blacks from three sides, the Lannister soldiers were gradually forced back into the Gods Eye. Hundreds died fighting amongst the lake's reeds, and hundreds of others drowned while trying to escape.(wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

The battle degenerated into carnage, and was ultimately the bloodiest land battle of the entire war; the high number of casualties led to it being called the "Fishfeed" by its participants. The blacks lost Lords Frey, Bigglestone, and Charlton, and two-thirds of the northern soldiers were killed or wounded. The greens lost their commander, Lord Lefford, as well as Lords [Swyft](wiki:Lord_Swyft) and [Reyne](wiki:Lord_Reyne) and the knights [Clarent Crakehall](wiki:Clarent_Crakehall) and [Emory Hill](wiki:Emory_Hill). Young Lord Blackwood wept when he saw all of the dead.(wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

## Aftermath

The Lannisters' main army was totally destroyed in the battle, the flower of knighthood from all the westerlands cut down in a single day. With only skeleton forces left to defend their home territory, [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy) opportunistically allied with Rhaenyra so the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) could plunder the coasts of the westerlands, sacking [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) but failing to take [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) itself. Despite their disproportionate victory, the black armies in the riverlands were also badly mauled in the bloodbath.

In response to the greens' crushing defeat, Prince Aemond and Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) abandoned Harrenhal. Aemond waged a guerilla war atop [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar), and Criston headed south to find Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower). The victorious rivermen avoided facing Criston's host directly, but wore him down with ambushes and scorched earth tactics, which included arranging the bodies of fallen soldiers from the Fishfeed in gruesome displays to frighten Criston's host (causing many to desert). The rivermen (and few surviving Winter Wolves) eventually cornered Criston's depleted army in the [Butcher's Ball](wiki:Butcher's_Ball).(wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)
