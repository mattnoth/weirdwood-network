## Origins

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) is betrothed to Prince [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) when her father, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), becomes the new [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) for Joffrey's father, King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Joffrey becomes king after Robert dies,(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) and he soon orders the execution of Lord Stark for treason.(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) King Joffrey I begins mistreating Sansa,(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and he intensifies his cruelty after her brother, [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), is declared [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
As part of an alliance between Houses [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) and [Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) negotiated by Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), Joffrey breaks his betrothal with Sansa and agrees to instead wed Lady [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Margaery's grandmother, Lady [Olenna Tyrell](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell), plots to wed Sansa to Margaery's brother [Willas](wiki:Willas_Tyrell), the heir to [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden).(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Sansa confides in her friend [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard), but the [fool](wiki:Fool) advises her against trusting the Tyrells.(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Unbeknownst to Sansa, Dontos is secretly in Petyr's employ,(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) and Petyr informs Joffrey's grandfather, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) of the Tyrells' intentions.(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) To forestall the Tyrells, Tywin decides that his own son [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) will marry Sansa instead. Tyrion can then claim Sansa's ancestral home, [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) in the [north](wiki:North).(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

## Aftermath

Lord [Gyles Rosby](wiki:Gyles_Rosby) is dispossessed of his spacious apartments atop the [Kitchen Keep](wiki:Kitchen_Keep) so that Tyrion and his household can move into them from [Maegor's Holdfast](wiki:Maegor's_Holdfast).(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Tyrion and Sansa leave their marriage unconsummated, which upsets Lord Tywin. The Hand's plan to have his widowed daughter, Queen Cersei, marry Willas, is refused by Lord Tyrell.(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Tyrion continues to sleep with [Shae](wiki:Shae), his secret lover, and he takes the [prostitute](wiki:Prostitute) into his household as one of Sansa's maids.(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) Sansa often goes to the [godswood of the Red Keep](wiki:Godswood_of_the_Red_Keep), where she conspires with Dontos about escaping [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), or to the royal sept.(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
When Joffrey chokes to death during his [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding), Cersei accuses Tyrion of having murdered the boy king.(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Sansa flees the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) with Dontos, and Lord Baelish has Dontos killed out in [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay).(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) Petyr later reveals that he had conspired with Olenna Tyrell to assassinate Joffrey.(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) During Tyrion's trial for murder, Shae lies by saying that Tyrion and Sansa had plotted Joffrey's death together.(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
