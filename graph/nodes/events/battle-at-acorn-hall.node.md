## Origins

Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister) was slain during the [Battle at the Red Fork](wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork), with Ser [Adrian Tarbeck](wiki:Adrian_Tarbeck) taking command of the victorious [greens](wiki:Greens) and Lord [Petyr Piper](wiki:Petyr_Piper) leading the surviving [blacks](wiki:Blacks) in retreat. As the [westermen](wiki:Westermen) marched east toward [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), they were confronted at [Acorn Hall](wiki:Acorn_Hall) by blacks led by Lord [Joseth Smallwood](wiki:Joseth_Smallwood), the lord of the castle, and Lord Piper. Petyr died during the battle, however, and Joseth retreated into Acorn Hall. According to [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom), Lord Piper died of a burst heart when he saw his grandson's head on a spear.(wiki:Battle_at_Acorn_Hall.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Three days after the battle at Acorn Hall, Adrian and Ser [Harry Penny](wiki:Harry_Penny) slew each other in renewed fighting after the [rivermen](wiki:Rivermen) regrouped. Lord [Humfrey Lefford](wiki:Humfrey_Lefford) took command of the remaining greens.(wiki:Battle_at_Acorn_Hall.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Rather than besiege the few survivors in Acorn Hall, the Lannister host bypassed it and continued their advance. Thus Stanton Piper and Joseth Smallwood survived, and later in the war they scraped up new levies from old men and green boys to fight in the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton).(wiki:Battle_at_Acorn_Hall.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
