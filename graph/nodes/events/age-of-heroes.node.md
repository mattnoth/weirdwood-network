## Identity

The Age of Heroes was a legendary era in the history of Westeros, occurring approximately 10,000 years before the events of the main series. It was the period following the Pact of the Isle of Faces between the First Men and the Children of the Forest, and preceding the Long Night. The great heroes and founders of Westerosi noble houses were said to have lived in this age — including Bran the Builder (House Stark), Lann the Clever (House Lannister), Garth Greenhand (House Gardener/Tyrell), and others. The age ended with the arrival of the Long Night and the War for the Dawn.
