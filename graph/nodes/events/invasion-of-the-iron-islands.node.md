## Origins

[House Hoare](wiki:House_Hoare) had ruled the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) as [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands) since the downfall of [House Greyiron](wiki:House_Greyiron),(wiki:Invasion_of_the_Iron_Islands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) and they then ruled as [Kings of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:Kings_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers) after conquering the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) from [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon).(wiki:Invasion_of_the_Iron_Islands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), however, the Hoares were extinguished in the [burning of Harrenhal](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal) by King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) and his [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Balerion the Black Dread](wiki:Balerion).(wiki:Invasion_of_the_Iron_Islands.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Aegon continued with his conquest of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), and he was eventually crowned at the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) of [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) in [1 AC](wiki:1_AC).(wiki:Invasion_of_the_Iron_Islands.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Aftermath

After achieving the submission of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands), King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) continued the [Wars of Conquest](wiki:Wars_of_Conquest) with the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War).(wiki:Invasion_of_the_Iron_Islands.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragons_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-7)

In [33 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), [Goren Greyjoy](wiki:Goren_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), suppressed an attempt to crown the son of Lord [Qhorin Volmark](wiki:Qhorin_Volmark).(wiki:Invasion_of_the_Iron_Islands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_greyjoys_of_pyke.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6) In [37 AC](wiki:37_AC), the priest-king [Lodos the Twice-Drowned](wiki:Lodos_the_Twice-Drowned) claimed to be Lodos reborn from the sea. Lord Goren, however, invaded [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk) and [Great Wyk](wiki:Great_Wyk) with a hundred longships, defeated the rebels, and sent the head of the second Lodos to King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Invasion_of_the_Iron_Islands.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Invasion_of_the_Iron_Islands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_greyjoys_of_pyke.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7)
