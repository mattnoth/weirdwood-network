## Identity

As Tywin's host took King's Landing, the Mad King Aerys II — who had long had pyromancers cache wildfire beneath the city — gave the order to ignite it rather than yield, sending the alchemist Rossart to fire the caches and burn the capital to "naught but ashes." This is the Mad King's in-Sack decision to execute the standing [wildfire plot](wildfire-plot), distinct from the plot's earlier preparation: it is the pivotal command that drives Jaime Lannister to slay him. Modeled as a discrete decision-beat per the agency-collapse principle (the order is a human choice that the bare event chain would otherwise hide).
