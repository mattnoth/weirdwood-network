# Catelyn seizes the moment and arrests Tyrion

At the inn at the crossroads, Catelyn Stark recognizes Tyrion Lannister and — believing Littlefinger's claim that the Valyrian-steel dagger left by Bran's would-be assassin was Tyrion's — calls on the assembled lords to seize him and bring him to Winterfell for the king's justice. The capture is the pivot from the assassination attempt into open Stark–Lannister conflict.
