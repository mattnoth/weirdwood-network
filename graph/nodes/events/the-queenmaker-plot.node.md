# The Queenmaker Plot

Princess Arianne Martell's conspiracy to spirit Myrcella Baratheon out of Sunspear
and crown her Queen of the Seven Kingdoms — invoking Dornish law of equal
inheritance, under which a king's elder daughter outranks his younger son — and so
raise Dorne (and ideally the realm) in rebellion against Myrcella's brother King
Tommen. Arianne's stated aims are vengeance for Oberyn and Elia, the freeing of the
imprisoned Sand Snakes, and above all her own birthright; her deepest motive is a
years-old fear, taken from a half-read letter, that her father Doran means to
disinherit her in favour of her brother Quentyn.

Over half a year she seduces Ser Arys Oakheart, Myrcella's sworn shield of the
Kingsguard, and binds him to the scheme. Her fellow conspirators are her cousins
Ser Andrey "Drey" Dalt and Garin of the orphans, her friend Lady Sylva "Spotted
Sylva" Santagar, and the dangerous Ser Gerold Dayne, called **Darkstar** — recruited,
against her better judgement, for his sword and his castle. The plot is betrayed to
Doran before it can crown anyone; it ends in the Greenblood ambush, Arys's death,
Myrcella's maiming by Darkstar, and Arianne's capture and imprisonment.
