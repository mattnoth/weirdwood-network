## Identity

During [Theon and Ramsay's sack of Winterfell](sack-of-winterfell), [Bran](bran-stark) and [Rickon](rickon-stark) escape death by hiding in the Winterfell crypts — laying a false wolf-trail to mislead pursuers, then doubling back to shelter in their father's tomb. The hiding is revealed retrospectively (Bran discloses it to the dying [Maester Luwin](luwin)); it is not shown on-page as a scene. They emerge to find the castle burned and Luwin dying beneath the heart tree. [Osha](osha)'s earlier feigned defection is the precondition that makes the hiding possible — she becomes their armed guide. Theon mounts two miller's boys' burned bodies as Bran and Rickon, the deception behind the WO5K beat [Robb receives false news of Bran's death](robb-receives-false-news-of-brans-death). This beat opens the entire flight-and-journey spine (Spine 2): emerging from the crypts is the precondition for the [party-split](bran-s-party-splits-from-rickon) that Osha and the Reeds then decide over Luwin's body.
