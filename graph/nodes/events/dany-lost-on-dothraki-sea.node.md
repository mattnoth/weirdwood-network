## Identity

[Drogon](drogon) carries [Daenerys](daenerys-targaryen) north beyond the Skahazadhan and sets down on a volcanic hill she names Dragonstone, stranding her far out on the Dothraki sea — alone, barefoot (one sandal lost in the flight, the other left at the dragon's cave), dressed in rags, burned, and without supplies. She passes days eating Drogon's scorched leavings and failing to ride him back to Meereen; the wild dragon returns to his lair instead of obeying her. Sick and starving, she walks the grass searching for the river and the road home, reciting "Fire and Blood" to the swaying grass as she fixes on who she is rather than the city she lost. The chapter — and Daenerys's whole published arc — closes when Khal Jhaqo's outriders emerge from the drifting smoke and find her.
