## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Yohn Royce](wiki:Yohn_Royce), [Lord of Runestone](wiki:Lord_of_Runestone), hosts a small mêlée with squires being the only participants. Bronze Yohn intends for [Harrold Hardyng](wiki:Harrold_Hardyng), the [ward](wiki:Ward) of Lady [Anya Waynwood](wiki:Anya_Waynwood) and the heir of Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn), to be the victor. After Harry the Heir wins the competition, he receives [knighthood](wiki:Knight) from Lord Royce.(wiki:Mêlée_at_Runestone.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

Ser Harrold upsets [Alayne Stone](wiki:Sansa_Stark) when they meet at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon). When Alayne encounters Ser [Lothor Brune](wiki:Lothor_Brune), Lothor refers to Harry as "some upjumped squire".(wiki:Mêlée_at_Runestone.cite_ref-2)
