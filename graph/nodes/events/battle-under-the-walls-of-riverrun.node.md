## Identity

Battle under the walls of Riverrun is a event.battle from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges

- FIGHTS_IN: War of the Five Kings (track_b: Conflict)
- DEFEATS: Lannister (track_b: Result)

## Origins

After Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) defeats the [Pipers](wiki:House_Piper) and [Vances](wiki:House_Vance_of_Wayfarer's_Rest) in a [battle in the hills below the Golden Tooth](wiki:Battle_in_the_hills_below_the_Golden_Tooth), Lord [Clement Piper](wiki:Clement_Piper) falls back to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Battle_under_the_walls_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Jaime and his father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), invade the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) with separate hosts. Jaime marches directly on Riverrun, where Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) has assembled many of his father [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully)'s bannermen and knights. Edmure's force is diminished on account of the men he has sent to guard the borders and the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth) in response to Lannister raiding. The forces of Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) are also not present.

## Narrative Arc

Jaime meets the Tullys under the walls of Riverrun. Securing a victory, Jaime puts the [rivermen](wiki:Rivermen) to rout. Edmure and many of his knights and bannermen are taken captive. Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood) manages to lead some of the surviving men back within Riverrun, where Jaime places them under siege.(wiki:Battle_under_the_walls_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Aftermath

Tywin leads a second, larger Lannister army against the companies of men Edmure had dispersed along his borders to guard against Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane)'s raiding. Tywin and Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) are able to destroy them piecemeal before they can regroup. They then attack several castles of the river lords, including [Raventree Hall](wiki:Fall_of_Raventree_Hall), [Pinkmaiden Castle](wiki:Burning_of_Pinkmaiden_Castle), [Stone Hedge](wiki:Burning_of_Stone_Hedge), [Darry](wiki:Capture_of_Darry), and [Harrenhal](wiki:Yielding_of_Harrenhal). The [westermen](wiki:Westermen) assume a position at the [ruby ford](wiki:Ruby_ford), where they can both block any [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) or [Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) attempt to relieve Riverrun and can maintain contact with [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Battle_under_the_walls_of_Riverrun.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
