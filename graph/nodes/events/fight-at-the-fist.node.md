## Origins

Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) leads three hundred men [on an expedition](wiki:Great_ranging) beyond the Wall, with two hundred men from [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) and the rest from the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower), in search of the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) and, if possible, to discover the whereabouts of [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark). Finding neither, Lord Commander Mormont choses the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men), an ancient ringfort, as his base of operations while he sends out scouting parties in search of information.

From the Fist, Mormont hopes to stop any march on the Wall. He strengthens their defenses with spikes and pits, caltrops scattered on the slopes, and a ring of watchers around the camp and along the river to warn of any approach.

When the men arrive at the Fist they make camp. [Dywen](wiki:Dywen) feels that it smells cold, while [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) thinks it smells like death.(wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist.cite_ref-Racok34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

While waiting for the last of the search parties to return to decide on a plan of action regarding the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings), the Watch are attacked by hundreds of [wights](wiki:Wights), both human and [animal](wiki:Animal). Their volleys of arrows have no effect against the undead attackers and Mormont orders them to use fire arrows, slowing the wights but not stopping them. The Watch is quickly overrun, but Mormont is able to form up about fifty survivors to break out of the Fist.(wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist.cite_ref-Rasos18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Aftermath

After a crushing defeat, the majority of the Night's Watch who fight on the Fist are slain by the insurmountable odds. Those who survive are led by Jeor Mormont on a grim march back to [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep) where the next [disaster](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep) occurs.(wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
