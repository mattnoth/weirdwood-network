## Origins

The eldest son of [Haegon I Blackfyre](wiki:Haegon_I_Blackfyre), [Daemon III Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_III_Blackfyre), had been crowned by [Aegor "Bittersteel" Rivers](wiki:Aegor_Rivers) in [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh), following Haegon's death during the end of the [Third Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Third_Blackfyre_Rebellion).(wiki:Fourth_Blackfyre_Rebellion.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) In [233 AC](wiki:233_AC), during the [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council) in [233 AC](wiki:233_AC), Daemon's uncle, Haegon's younger brother, [Aenys Blackfyre](wiki:Aenys_Blackfyre), had tried to make his claim, and travelled to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), but he was arrested and killed on the orders of [Brynden "Bloodraven" Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers).(wiki:Fourth_Blackfyre_Rebellion.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_maekar_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) This caused the enmity of the exiles in [Essos](wiki:Essos) to harden, instead of lessen. As a six-year, cruel, winter was ending, Daemon decided to make the crossing.(wiki:Fourth_Blackfyre_Rebellion.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Aftermath

Aegor Rivers emerged in the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands) a few years after the rebellion, fighting with his sellswords in a skirmish between [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) and [Myr](wiki:Myr). Despite Aegor's death a few years after the Fourth Blackfyre Rebellion, the Blackfyre cause would remain until [260 AC](wiki:260_AC).(wiki:Fourth_Blackfyre_Rebellion.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)(wiki:Fourth_Blackfyre_Rebellion.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
