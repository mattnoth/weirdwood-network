## Edges


## Origins

Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), dies abruptly, so King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) decides to offer the position to his childhood friend, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark).(wiki:Feast_in_honor_of_King_Robert's_visit_to_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Eddard has preparations made for a feast welcoming the king to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Feast_in_honor_of_King_Robert's_visit_to_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Robert is accompanied from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to the [north](wiki:North) by a large retinue of three hundred, including [bannermen](wiki:Bannermen), [knights](wiki:Knight), [sworn swords](wiki:Sworn_sword), and [freeriders](wiki:Freeriders). His wife, Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), travels in a large [wheelhouse](wiki:Wheelhouse).(wiki:Feast_in_honor_of_King_Robert's_visit_to_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
[Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder), [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall), learns of Robert's visit and decides to attend the feast so he can evaluate King Robert and Eddard's brother [Benjen](wiki:Benjen_Stark), the [First Ranger](wiki:First_Ranger) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). After scaling the [Wall](wiki:Wall) near [Long Barrow](wiki:Long_Barrow), Mance infiltrates Robert's retinue disguised as a musician. (wiki:Feast_in_honor_of_King_Robert's_visit_to_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
When the royal retinue arrives at Winterfell, the children of Eddard and Robert are introduced to each other. Robert then insists that Eddard bring him to the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell) so the king can pass his respects at the tomb of Eddard late sister [Lyanna](wiki:Lyanna_Stark), to whom Robert was once betrothed.(wiki:Feast_in_honor_of_King_Robert's_visit_to_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Aftermath

[Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark) informs Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin) of [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow)'s desire to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Feast_in_honor_of_King_Robert's_visit_to_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Septa [Mordane](wiki:Mordane) sits with girls at Winterfell as they practice needlework. [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole) informs [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) that Prince [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) likes her sister, [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), and had told her she is very beautiful.(wiki:Feast_in_honor_of_King_Robert's_visit_to_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
[Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder), [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall), meets [Dalla](wiki:Dalla) upon his return to the lands [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:Feast_in_honor_of_King_Robert's_visit_to_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)
Jon is captured by [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) during the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) of the Night's Watch north of the [Wall](wiki:Wall). Following the instructions of [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand), Jon lies to the wildlings and tells them he wishes to join them.(wiki:Feast_in_honor_of_King_Robert's_visit_to_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) When Jon is brought before Mance reveals to the youth that he had secretly attended the feast welcoming King [Robert](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). When Mance asks Jon why he has deserted the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), Jon lies by claiming it was because he was seated away from the rest of his family at the feast because of being a [bastard](wiki:Bastard).(wiki:Feast_in_honor_of_King_Robert's_visit_to_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)
