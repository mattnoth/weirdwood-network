# Davos sent to fetch Rickon from Skagos

In a private chamber at the Wolf's Den (New Castle, White Harbor), [Lord Wyman Manderly](wyman-manderly)
reveals his deception to [Davos Seaworth](davos-seaworth) and names his price for House Manderly's
allegiance to [Stannis Baratheon](stannis-baratheon): Davos must sail to **Skagos** — the isle of
unicorns and cannibals — and bring home [Rickon Stark](rickon-stark), the last known trueborn male heir
of House Stark, along with his direwolf Shaggydog. The intelligence that Rickon fled to Skagos came from
the mute boy [Wex Pyke](wex-pyke), who survived the sack of Winterfell hidden in a tree and communicated
what he saw — by chalk-drawn tree-with-a-face and by writing his name — through [Robett Glover](robett-glover).
Wyman's motive is to counter Roose Bolton's gambit: "Roose Bolton has Lord Eddard's daughter. To thwart
him White Harbor must have Ned's son … and the direwolf."

The commission is a beat inside the [Grand Northern Conspiracy](grand-northern-conspiracy), and it is the
[staged fake execution of Davos](wyman-manderly-stages-fake-execution-of-davos) that makes it possible:
a man "supposed to be dead" can slip unseen past dangers as no living envoy could. The quest is left
unresolved in the published books.
