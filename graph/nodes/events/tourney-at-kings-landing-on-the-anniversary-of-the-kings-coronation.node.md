## Origins

The tourney was held at King's Landing in celebration of the first decade of [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen)'s reign. King Jaehaerys watched the jousts in the royal box with his wife, Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), and their children, [Daenerys](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)), [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) and the newborn [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)). The young knight, Ser [Ryam Redwyne](wiki:Ryam_Redwyne), was the champion, and crowned Queen Alysanne as the [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty).(wiki:Tourney_at_King's_Landing_on_the_anniversary_of_the_king's_coronation.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
