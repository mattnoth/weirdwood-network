## Identity

Queen Helaena Targaryen, sister-wife of King Aegon II, throws herself from her window in Maegor's Holdfast to die impaled on the dry moat's iron spikes, at one-and-twenty, after half a year of captivity at Rhaenyra's court. The false rumor that Rhaenyra ordered her murder ignites the bloody riot of King's Landing that same night.
