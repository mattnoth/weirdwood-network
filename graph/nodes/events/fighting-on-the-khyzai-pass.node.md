## Identity

Fighting on the Khyzai Pass is a event.battle from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges

- FIGHTS_IN: Targaryen campaign in Slaver's Bay (track_b: Conflict)

## Origins

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) forces the [Wise Masters](wiki:Wise_Masters) of [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) to surrender their [slaves](wiki:Slaves), but after she leaves for [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) the Wise Masters resume slaving and begin raising new forces against her.(wiki:Fighting_on_the_Khyzai_Pass.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) The Yunkai'i send the [Long Lances](wiki:Long_Lances), a sellsword company, to hold the [Khyzai Pass](wiki:Khyzai_Pass) between Meereen and [Lhazar](wiki:Lhazar).(wiki:Fighting_on_the_Khyzai_Pass.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Daenerys, the new Queen of Meereen, sends [Daario Naharis](wiki:Daario_Naharis) and his sellsword [Stormcrows](wiki:Stormcrows) to negotiate with the [Lhazareen](wiki:Lhazareen) to ensure land trade through Khyzai Pass.(wiki:Fighting_on_the_Khyzai_Pass.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

Daario's Stormcrows attack the Long Lances in the night, killing a few of Yunkai's sellswords. The Stormcrows lose nine men, but Daario convinces twelve of the Long Lances to join Daenerys's cause. Daario kills one Long Lance who says that Meereen will suffer the same fate as starving [Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor).(wiki:Fighting_on_the_Khyzai_Pass.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Aftermath

The Lamb Men of Lhazar agree to trade with Meereen, but the [Skahazadhan](wiki:Skahazadhan) is closed by the Yunkai'i.(wiki:Fighting_on_the_Khyzai_Pass.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) During the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen), two [lockstep legions](wiki:Lockstep_legions) from [New Ghis](wiki:New_Ghis) close the road from Meereen to the Khyzai Pass.(wiki:Fighting_on_the_Khyzai_Pass.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
