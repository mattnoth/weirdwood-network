# Arianne collapses and is captured

As Hotah's ambush breaks the Queenmaker plot, Arianne Martell falls from her horse,
shaking and retching in the sand, and offers no resistance when the prince's guardsmen
bind her hands and take from her boot the throwing knife her cousin Nymeria gave her.
Hotah tells her the prince has commanded he bring her back to Sunspear, where she will
be held a prisoner in the Spear Tower.
