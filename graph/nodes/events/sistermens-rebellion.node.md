## Origins

[Aegon's Landing](wiki:Aegon's_Landing) and the coronation of King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) at the [Aegonfort](wiki:Aegonfort) threw the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) into disarray. Aegon's sister, Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen), destroyed the fleet of [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn), the [Kings of Mountain and Vale](wiki:Kings_of_Mountain_and_Vale), during the [battle in the waters off Gulltown](wiki:Battle_in_the_waters_off_Gulltown).(wiki:Sistermen's_Rebellion.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) The lords of the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters), who had sworn allegiance to the Arryns centuries earlier in the [War Across the Water](wiki:War_Across_the_Water),(wiki:Sistermen's_Rebellion.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) renounced King [Ronnel Arryn](wiki:Ronnel_Arryn_(king)) and named [Marla Sunderland](wiki:Marla_Sunderland) as queen.(wiki:Sistermen's_Rebellion.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The Arryns eventually submitted to [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) when Visenya landed with [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), with Ronnel becoming the [Warden of the East](wiki:Warden_of_the_East) for King Aegon.(wiki:Sistermen's_Rebellion.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

After being anointed by the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aegon's_Conquest)) in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), Aegon ordered Lord [Torrhen Stark](wiki:Torrhen_Stark), his new [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), to end the Sistermen's Rebellion. Since the Arryn fleet had not yet been rebuilt after Gulltown, Ser [Warrick Manderly](wiki:Warrick_Manderly) led the [northern](wiki:North) army to the [Bite](wiki:Bite) on galleys hired from [Braavos](wiki:Braavos). The [Sistermen](wiki:Sistermen) surrendered instead of fighting when they saw Warrick's fleet and Visenya on her [dragon](wiki:Dragon). They deposed Marla for her younger brother, Lord [Steffon Sunderland](wiki:Steffon_Sunderland), who yielded the Three Sisters to the Targaryens and Arryns.(wiki:Sistermen's_Rebellion.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Sistermen's_Rebellion.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

[House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) took one of Steffon's sons as a hostage, while House Arryn took the other. After five years of imprisoned exile, Marla had her tongue removed and joined the [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters).(wiki:Sistermen's_Rebellion.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
