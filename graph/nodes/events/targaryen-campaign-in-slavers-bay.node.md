## Origins

Following the death of her husband, [Khal](wiki:Khal) [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) manages to hatch her three [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [eggs](wiki:Dragon_egg) on his funeral pyre.(wiki:Targaryen_campaign_in_Slaver's_Bay.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) As Drogo's [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) has disbanded into several smaller factions, Daenerys sees herself forced to travel through the [Red Waste](wiki:Red_Waste), as she considers the chance of encountering an enemy too large when traveling in any other direction.(wiki:Targaryen_campaign_in_Slaver's_Bay.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Daenerys eventually arrives in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth),(wiki:Targaryen_campaign_in_Slaver's_Bay.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) but following the destruction of the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying) and the resulting hostility of the Qartheen, finds herself in need of a new destination.(wiki:Targaryen_campaign_in_Slaver's_Bay.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Whilst looking for a passage on a ship at the docks of Qarth, Daenerys is approached by the eunuch [Belwas](wiki:Belwas) and the squire [Arstan Whitebeard](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) (secretly Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) from [Westeros](wiki:Westeros)), who were sent to [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) by [magister](wiki:Magister) [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis). Illyrio wishes her to return to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos), and has sent three ships to Qarth for Daenerys to use.(wiki:Targaryen_campaign_in_Slaver's_Bay.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

However, whilst on route to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) with her dragons and the remnants of her khalasar, [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) convinces Daenerys to change course to [Astapor](wiki:Astapor) instead, arguing that she will need an army to conquer Westeros. Jorah believes she should buy [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied), slave soldiers trained and sold in [Astapor](wiki:Astapor) with an extraordinary reputation.(wiki:Targaryen_campaign_in_Slaver's_Bay.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) In Astapor, Daenerys inspects the Unsullied, but is sickened by the misery of the slave trade.(wiki:Targaryen_campaign_in_Slaver's_Bay.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) However, she agrees to acquire all the Unsullied available - eight thousand six hundred - as well as the boys still in training - an additional five thousand - in exchange for her three ships, all trading goods on board, and the strongest of her [dragons](wiki:Dragons): [Drogon](wiki:Drogon).(wiki:Targaryen_campaign_in_Slaver's_Bay.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
