## Origins

### Aegonfort[ ]

After securing the mouth of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) on both sides, [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) commanded the lords that had submitted to him to come to the [Aegonfort](wiki:Aegonfort). In front of their new subjects, Aegon's sister-wives proclaimed him king; [Visenya](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) placed a [crown](wiki:Crown) on Aegon's head and [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) hailed him as "Aegon, First of His Name, [King of All Westeros](wiki:King_of_All_Westeros) and [Shield of His People](wiki:Shield_of_His_People)".(wiki:Aegon's_coronations.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
A few days after the coronation, Aegon's armies marched out of the Aegonfort to continue his Conquest, whilst the other monarchs of Westeros prepared to face the [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen).(wiki:Aegon's_coronations.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### Oldtown[ ]

After the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire) and the submission of the North, Aegon marched south towards [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). Lord [Manfred Hightower](wiki:Manfred_Hightower_(Aegon's_Conquest)) of the [Hightower](wiki:Hightower) rode outside of the city walls and personally submitted his sword and city to Aegon on the advice of the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aegon's_Conquest)), who had received a vision from the [Crone](wiki:Crone_(the_Seven)) of Oldtown being burned and destroyed should they resist the Conqueror.(wiki:Aegon's_coronations.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
Three days after his arrival, the High Septon anointed Aegon with the seven oils in the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept), crowned him, and proclaiming him "Aegon of House Targaryen, the First of His Name, [King of the Andals, the Rhoynar, and the First Men](wiki:King_of_the_Andals,_the_Rhoynar,_and_the_First_Men), [Lord of the Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Lord_of_the_Seven_Kingdoms), and [Protector of the Realm](wiki:Protector_of_the_Realm)". This day marked the official start of Aegon's reign.(wiki:Aegon's_coronations.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Quotes

Only a handful of [lords](wiki:Lord) had been present for [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen)'s first coronation at the mouth of the [Blackwater](wiki:Blackwater), but hundreds were on hand to witness his second, and tens of thousands cheered him afterward in the streets of [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) as he [rode](wiki:Dragonrider) through the city on [Balerion](wiki:Balerion)'s back. Amongst those at Aegon's second coronation were the [maesters](wiki:Maesters) and archmaesters of the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel). Perhaps for that reason, it was this coronation, rather than the [Aegonfort](wiki:Aegonfort) crowning on the day of [Aegon's landing](wiki:Aegon's_Landing), that became fixed as the start of Aegon's reign.(wiki:Aegon's_coronations.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
