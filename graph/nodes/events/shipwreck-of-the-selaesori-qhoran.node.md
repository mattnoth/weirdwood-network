## Identity

A storm in [Slaver's Bay](slavers-bay) shatters the cog [Selaesori Qhoran](selaesori-qhoran), bound for
Qarth with [Tyrion](tyrion-lannister), [Penny](penny), [Jorah Mormont](jorah-mormont) and the red priest
[Moqorro](moqorro) aboard. Nine are lost, Moqorro among them (he later resurfaces with
[Victarion](iron-fleet-departs-for-slavers-bay)). The broken hulk drifts until slavers board the
crippled cog — the precondition that delivers the survivors into [bondage](tyrion-and-penny-sold-to-yezzan).
