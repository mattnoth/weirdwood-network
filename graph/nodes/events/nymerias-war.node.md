## Origins

After the defeat of the Rhoynish army by the [Valyrian](wiki:Valyria) [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlord) in the [Second Spice War](wiki:Second_Spice_War), Princess [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria) led the Rhoynar in exile from the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne) aboard [ten thousand ships](wiki:Ten_thousand_ships). After years of wandering, the surviving Rhoynar, mostly women, children, and the elderly, landed at the mouth of the [Greenblood](wiki:Greenblood) in southeastern Dorne.

Nymeria made common cause with [Mors Martell](wiki:Mors_Martell), Lord of the [Sandship](wiki:Sandship), whose lands were dwarfed by those of powerful kings such as [House Yronwood](wiki:House_Yronwood).(wiki:Nymeria's_War.cite_ref-3) On the day she wed Mors, forming [House Nymeros Martell](wiki:House_Martell), Nymeria had her fleet burned to affirm that the Rhoynar could not return to Essos.(wiki:Nymeria's_War.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The people of the Martell lands also intermarried with the Rhoynar, and the addition of the Rhoynar increased the strength of the Martells tenfold. Nymeria declared Mors to be the [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), using the Rhoynish title of prince instead of the Westerosi "king". Equal primogeniture was also introduced.(wiki:Nymeria's_War.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
