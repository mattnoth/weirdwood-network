## Identity

After his catastrophic defeat at the [Battle of the Blackwater](battle-of-the-blackwater) in 299 AC — his fleet burned by wildfire and broken by the great chain, his bannermen abandoning him by the hundreds, and Tywin Lannister's relief host striking him from the flank — Stannis Baratheon escaped King's Landing with only a fraction of his army aboard Salladhor Saan's Lysene ships. He withdrew to his seat at [Dragonstone](dragonstone), where he fell into brooding isolation in the Stone Drum, seeing almost no one while Queen Selyse and her uncle Lord Alester Florent kept a hollow court in his name. The retreat removed Stannis's threat to the capital for years and left him a reduced king clinging to a poor, rocky island — the nadir from which the R'hllor-fueled northern gambit of ASOS/ADWD would later be launched.
