## Origins

After defeating Houses [Piper](wiki:House_Piper) and [Vance](wiki:House_Vance_of_Wayfarer's_Rest) below the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Battle_in_the_hills_below_the_Golden_Tooth), Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) leads his host of [westermen](wiki:Westermen) against [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), where he [routs another rivermen force](wiki:Battle_under_the_walls_of_Riverrun) and captures Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully). The rivermen who do not retreat to their own castles hole up inside Riverrun, commanded by Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood) in place of the ailing [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), [Lord of Riverrun](wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

Beginning a siege, Jaime is harassed by raiders led by Ser [Marq Piper](wiki:Marq_Piper) and Lord [Karyl Vance](wiki:Karyl_Vance). While dealing with what he thinks is another such attack, Jaime is ambushed and captured by [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s cavalry in the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5) Having had their outriders and scouts eliminated, the Lannister force is unaware of their commander's defeat or of Robb's army's approach, and is still divided in three camps in order to besiege Riverrun properly.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7)

## Narrative Arc

Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully), leading Robb's van, attacks the Lannister camp north of the [Tumblestone](wiki:Tumblestone) at night. His force clears palisades and finishes off any remaining scouts, and the sleeping, leaderless camp is overrun. Men from the other camps, roused by the noise, soon attempt to come to the aid of the camp under attack. Lord [Andros Brax](wiki:Andros_Brax) leads his men from the middle camp to the rafts and attempts to make a crossing. Riverrun begins pelting the rafts with thrown rocks, however, and soon the rafts are flipping and sinking. Among those drowned is Lord Brax.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8)

Meanwhile, Robb leads two columns of armored horse, including [Umbers](wiki:House_Umber) and [Mallisters](wiki:House_Mallister), from the west in overrunning the camp between the rivers. [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind), Robb's [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), allegedly kills four men and a dozen horses. The Lannisters in the middle camp form a shieldwall and repel one charge, but they are then taken in the rear by a sortie from Riverrun led by Tytos Blackwood. Lord [Jon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber) burns the siege towers which were under construction, and Lord Blackwood releases the prisoners taken in the earlier [battle under the walls of Riverrun](wiki:Battle_under_the_walls_of_Riverrun), including Edmure.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-9) Despite his condition, Lord Hoster watches the fighting from the battlements of Riverrun.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-5)

In the southern camp of the Lannister force, the [Tyroshi sellsword](wiki:Tyroshi_sellsword) who leads the [freeriders](wiki:Freerider) of Ser [Forley Prester](wiki:Forley_Prester) strikes his banners and switches sides. Forley leads the retreat of two thousand spears and two thousand bowmen when he sees the battle is lost.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-10)

## Aftermath

Robb takes unspecified captives in the battle(wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) and enters [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) during daylight. While [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) greets her father, Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), in his solar, Robb prays in the [godswood of Riverrun](wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun) with [Jon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber), [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark), [Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont), [Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover), [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood), and others.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-6) Word spreads through the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) of the victory, and the northmen and rivermen who assemble in the [Great Hall of Riverrun](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Riverrun) for Robb's war council proclaim him as [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-7)

Ser [Forley Prester](wiki:Forley_Prester) retreats with his men from Riverrun to the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)(wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Meanwhile, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) leads his own host south in a forced march after learning that Robb did not participate in the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork). At the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn), however, his war council is informed of the [Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood) and the Battle of the Camps by a messenger from [House Crakehall](wiki:House_Crakehall). Ser [Harys Swyft](wiki:Harys_Swyft) describes the Lannister defeats as catastrophic.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-11) Tywin tasks Ser [Stafford Lannister](wiki:Stafford_Lannister) with raising another host at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1)
