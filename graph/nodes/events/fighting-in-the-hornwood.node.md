## Identity

Fighting in the Hornwood is a event.battle from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges

- FIGHTS_IN: War of the Five Kings (track_b: Conflict)
- DEFEATS: House Manderly captures Hornwood but later withdraws, resulting in a Bolton victory (track_b: Result)
- DEFEATS: Ramsay Snow believed dead after intervention of Winterfell (track_b: Result)

## Aftermath

Despite the supposed death of Ramsay, the northerners of Winterfell still fear his father, Roose Bolton, will press a claim on the Hornwood lands. Rodrik also states that fighting is ongoing between Bolton and Manderly men as he lacks a strong enough force of men to stop them.(wiki:Fighting_in_the_Hornwood.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Rodrik imprisons "Reek"—actually Ramsay—in Winterfell's dungeon.(wiki:Fighting_in_the_Hornwood.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) "Reek" is taken into the service of [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) during the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Fighting_in_the_Hornwood.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Theon eventually sends him to gather reinforcements, resulting in Ramsay and Bolton men slaying Rodrik, White Harbor knights and Hornwoods, along with other northern [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) loyalists, in a [battle at Winterfell](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell). Theon allows Ramsay into Winterfell, resulting in the slaughtering of the ironborn garrison of Winterfell and [sacking](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell) the [winter town](wiki:Winter_town).(wiki:Fighting_in_the_Hornwood.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Following the betrayal of House Stark by the Boltons and Freys at the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) legitimizes Ramsay and names his father, Lord Roose Bolton, [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North).(wiki:Fighting_in_the_Hornwood.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Ramsay presents himself from this point onwards as [Lord of the Hornwood](wiki:Lord_of_the_Hornwood) and heir to the Dreadfort.(wiki:Fighting_in_the_Hornwood.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:Fighting_in_the_Hornwood.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Fighting_in_the_Hornwood.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Fighting_in_the_Hornwood.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Because Wyman Manderly's son and heir, Ser [Wylis](wiki:Wylis_Manderly), was captured at the [Trident](wiki:Fighting_at_the_fords_of_the_Trident), Wyman agrees to a deal with the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister). In exchange for Wyman executing the imprisoned Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) and envoy of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), as well as Wyman withdrawing from the Hornwood lands and castles and submitting to Roose, Wylis will be released from [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool).(wiki:Fighting_in_the_Hornwood.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)(wiki:Fighting_in_the_Hornwood.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)(wiki:Fighting_in_the_Hornwood.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)(wiki:Fighting_in_the_Hornwood.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Sometime after this agreement is made the Manderlys withdraw from Hornwood territory, allowing for the Boltons to take control of the region.

Whilst some Hornwoods submit to Ramsay and attend his wedding to "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)",(wiki:Fighting_in_the_Hornwood.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) other Hornwood survivors of the Bolton betrayal at Winterfell join Stannis following his victorious [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Fighting_in_the_Hornwood.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
