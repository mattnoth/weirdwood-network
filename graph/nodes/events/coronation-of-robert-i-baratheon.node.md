## Origins

### Robert's Rebellion[ ]

In [282 AC](wiki:282_AC), [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone), abducted [Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark), who was betrothed to [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End).(wiki:Coronation_of_Robert_I_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Lyanna's brother, [Brandon Stark](wiki:Brandon_Stark), was imprisoned by King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) when he rode to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) to confront Rhaegar, and the Mad King then executed Brandon and his father, Lord [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark).(wiki:Coronation_of_Robert_I_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Aerys next sent an order to [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), [Lord of the Eyrie](wiki:Lord_of_the_Eyrie), to behead Robert and [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), now [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell) after the death of his father Rickard. Jon refused to kill his former wards, however, and the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) plunged into civil war with [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Coronation_of_Robert_I_Baratheon.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Robert announced his intention to claim the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne),(wiki:Coronation_of_Robert_I_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rawoiafrobert_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Coronation_of_Robert_I_Baratheon.cite_ref-6) and he slew Rhaegar in the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Coronation_of_Robert_I_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) led a host to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to ostensibly aid King Aerys, but he had his men attack the Targaryen loyalists after being admitted to the capital. Tywin's eldest son, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), killed Aerys, the king he was sworn to protect, during the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing)(wiki:Coronation_of_Robert_I_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) in [283 AC](wiki:283_AC).(wiki:Coronation_of_Robert_I_Baratheon.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Tywin presented the bodies of Rhaegar's wife, [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell), and children, Princess [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Rhaegar)) and Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)), to Robert as tokens of fealty by [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister). While Robert accepted their deaths as consequences of war, the murders outraged Lord Stark,(wiki:Coronation_of_Robert_I_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) and Eddard departed King's Landing to lift the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End) to the south.(wiki:Coronation_of_Robert_I_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Their shared grief at [Lyanna's passing](wiki:Tower_of_joy) later reconciled Robert and Eddard.(wiki:Coronation_of_Robert_I_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

### Coronation[ ]

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys), and Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) knelt at King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s feet during his coronation so they could be pardoned for having served the Mad King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) and return to royal service.(wiki:Coronation_of_Robert_I_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
During his coronation feast, Robert spoke to Jaime about his being known as "Kingslayer" for having killed Aerys, joking it should not become a habit.(wiki:Coronation_of_Robert_I_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Robert's first act as king was to take as his queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), Jaime's twin and the daughter of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister),(wiki:Coronation_of_Robert_I_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_glorious_reign.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) and he had the [skulls of the Targaryen dragons](wiki:Skulls_of_the_Targaryen_dragons) removed from the [throne room](wiki:Great_Hall_(Red_Keep)) before the wedding.(wiki:Coronation_of_Robert_I_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Coronation_of_Robert_I_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
