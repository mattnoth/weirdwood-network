## Origins

[Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), Lord of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), made known his intentions of conquering [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) and unifying the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) under his rule. [Sharra Arryn](wiki:Sharra_Arryn), Queen Regent for her son, [Ronnel Arryn](wiki:Ronnel_Arryn_(king)), the [King of Mountain and Vale](wiki:King_of_Mountain_and_Vale), sent a portrait of herself to Aegon and offered her hand in marriage with the condition Aegon would make Ronnel his heir, but Aegon refused.(wiki:Battle_in_the_waters_off_Gulltown.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

After being crowned at the mouth of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush), King Aegon sent his fleet commanded by [Daemon Velaryon](wiki:Daemon_Velaryon) to take [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown), the city of the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn). Daemon was accompanied by Aegon's sister, [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen).(wiki:Battle_in_the_waters_off_Gulltown.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

The Arryns hastily assembled a fleet to meet the Targaryens. Their fleet was augmented by a dozen [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) warships.(wiki:Battle_in_the_waters_off_Gulltown.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

The Targaryen and Arryn fleets met in the waters off Gulltown. The Arryns were able to destroy the Targaryen fleet and Daemon was killed. Visenya's [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar), burned the Arryn fleet in response. The battle was a tactical draw since both fleets were destroyed, but a strategic defeat for the Targaryens since they were unable to take Gulltown.(wiki:Battle_in_the_waters_off_Gulltown.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Aftermath

Though the Targaryens lost the battle, the Sistermen on the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters) revolted against [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) after the destruction of its fleet and named [Marla Sunderland](wiki:Marla_Sunderland) their queen.(wiki:Battle_in_the_waters_off_Gulltown.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
