## Identity

[Daenerys Targaryen](daenerys-targaryen) builds a great funeral pyre for [Khal Drogo](drogo), lays his body atop it with her three petrified dragon eggs, binds the maegi [Mirri Maz Duur](mirri-maz-duur) to the wood as a blood-price, and against [Jorah Mormont](jorah-mormont)'s pleading walks into the flames herself — "I am Daenerys Stormborn, daughter of dragons, bride of dragons, mother of dragons." Three cracks like the breaking of the world sound from the eggs. When the fire dies, Jorah finds her amid the ashes, naked and unburnt, with three newborn dragons — [Drogon](drogon), [Rhaegal](rhaegal), and [Viserion](viserion) — clinging to her body. The Dothraki of her khas fall to their knees crying "Blood of my blood." This is the rebirth of dragons into the world after a century and a half — the magical engine for everything Daenerys does thereafter, and the prime mover of the entire Essos arc (the Slaver's Bay campaign, Meereen, and the long road back toward Westeros).
