## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

On [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) speaks of a great battle is the coming war between the [Great Other](wiki:Great_Other) and [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor), the Lord of Light,(wiki:War_for_the_dawn.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) which has been waged since time began.(wiki:War_for_the_dawn.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Melisandre states the Great Other is marshaling his power, a power fell and evil and strong beyond measure, and that the battle has begun.(wiki:War_for_the_dawn.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

After King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) makes [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) the Lord of the [Rainwood](wiki:Rainwood) and his [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), he tells Davos that it is he whom he wants beside him for the battle. Davos thinks Stannis means another battle against the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister), but [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) explains to Davos that it is the great battle Stannis is speaking of. Stannis then tells Davos that he saw the great battle in the flames. Davos is astonished, as it is not like Stannis to lie about such a thing. Stannis tells him that after the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater) he was lost in despair but Melisandre bid him to gaze into the hearth fire; he describes to Davos what he saw in the flames,

> The ashes were white, rising in the updraft, yet all at once it seemed as if they were falling. Snow, I thought. Then the sparks in the air seemed to circle, to become a ring of torches, and I was looking **through** the fire down on some [high hill in a forest](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men). The cinders had become [men in black behind the torches](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fist_of_the_First_Men), and there were [shapes moving through the snow](wiki:Wights). For all the heat of the fire, [I felt a cold so terrible](wiki:Others) I shivered, and when I did the sight was gone, the fire but a fire once again. But what I saw was real, I’d stake my kingdom on it.(wiki:War_for_the_dawn.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

After the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall) Melisandre states that the war they have come to fight is a war for life itself. Melisandre tells Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) that [the prince that was promised](wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised) is standing before him, King Stannis Baratheon, who is [Azor Ahai](wiki:Azor_Ahai) reborn and that in him the prophecies are fulfilled.(wiki:War_for_the_dawn.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
