## Origins

At the start of the civil war, Prince [Jacaerys Velaryon](wiki:Jacaerys_Velaryon) flew to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) to gain [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) and the [North](wiki:North) for the cause of his mother, Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen).(wiki:Pact_of_Ice_and_Fire.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)(wiki:Pact_of_Ice_and_Fire.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) According to [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom), Prince Jacaerys secretly married Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark)'s [bastard](wiki:Bastardy) sister, [Sara Snow](wiki:Sara_Snow), which led to the pact. It was agreed that the firstborn daughter of Prince Jacaerys would marry Lord Cregan's son and heir, [Rickon](wiki:Rickon_Stark_(son_of_Cregan)).(wiki:Pact_of_Ice_and_Fire.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Lord [Roderick Dustin](wiki:Roderick_Dustin) led the [Winter Wolves](wiki:Winter_Wolves) to join the [blacks](wiki:Blacks), while [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) also contributed [knights](wiki:Knight) to Rhaenyra's side.(wiki:Pact_of_Ice_and_Fire.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Lord Cregan himself led a host to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) near the end of the war, but they arrived after the death of King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Pact_of_Ice_and_Fire.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Cregan briefly governed in the capital as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) during the [Hour of the Wolf](wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf).(wiki:Pact_of_Ice_and_Fire.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Although a royal princess never did marry into the Starks, Cregan gained many rewards for his support of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen).(wiki:Pact_of_Ice_and_Fire.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_north:_the_lords_of_winterfell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
