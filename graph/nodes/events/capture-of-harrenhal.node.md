## Origins

Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), having decided to switch his allegiance from [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) to [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister), sends a third of the [northern](wiki:North) foot to [defeat at Duskendale](wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale). Roose leaves Harrenhal to its new lord, [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat) of the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions), as he goes north to the [Twins](wiki:Twins) for the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) with the remaining northmen. There is [fighting at the Trident](wiki:Fighting_at_the_fords_of_the_Trident) while Roose is on his way to the wedding, as he allows Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) to overwhelm his rearguard at the [ruby ford](wiki:Ruby_ford).(wiki:Capture_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Vargo suffers from infection after his ear is bitten off by [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth).(wiki:Capture_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Aftermath

[Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) is again in the hands of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister). Members of the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) are on the run throughout the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). When Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) returns to Harrenhal after the death of Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane), the only servants remaining of those who had once served Lady [Shella Whent](wiki:Shella_Whent) are the maimed cook, [Ben Blackthumb](wiki:Ben_Blackthumb), and [Pia](wiki:Pia).(wiki:Capture_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)
