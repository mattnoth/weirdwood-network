## Identity

On his sixteenth nameday, King Aegon III enters the small council chamber, declares the regency at an end, dismisses lot-chosen regents Stackspear, Merryweather, and Grandison, and curtly sends Hand of the King Torrhen Manderly back to White Harbor, taking the governance of the Seven Kingdoms into his own hands.
