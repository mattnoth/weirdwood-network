## Origins

### Prelude

During the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood) after the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria), the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) first rode out east of the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea).(wiki:Three_Thousand_of_Qohor.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Khal [Temmo](wiki:Temmo)'s *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* consisted of at least fifty thousand Dothraki, half of whom were braided warriors with bells in their hair. Temmo led these twenty-five thousand screamers in sacking and burning every town and city in his path.(wiki:Three_Thousand_of_Qohor.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Aware of the *khalasar's* advance, the Qohorik strengthened their walls, doubled their garrison, and hired two [mercenary](wiki:Mercenary) companies, the [Bright Banners](wiki:Bright_Banners) and the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons). They also sent an envoy to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay) to purchase three thousand [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied), the eunuch slave soldiers of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor).(wiki:Three_Thousand_of_Qohor.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

### Battle

The Dothraki prevailed in the first day of fighting. Though the Qohorik forces inflicted over five thousand losses on the Dothraki, their garrison and heavy horse were shattered and nearly destroyed.(wiki:Three_Thousand_of_Qohor.cite_ref-4) The [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons) and the [Bright Banners](wiki:Bright_Banners) had fled. The victorious Dothraki retired to their camps to drink and feast, and it was expected that they would return the following day to sack the city.(wiki:Three_Thousand_of_Qohor.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) That evening, the reinforcement of three thousand Unsullied reached the city. Come dawn, they awaited the Dothraki horde, arrayed in battle formation before the city gates.(wiki:Three_Thousand_of_Qohor.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

According to [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont), the Dothraki could have outflanked such a small force, but in their contempt for infantry, the Dothraki riders launched a direct frontal assault instead, in an attempt to simply ride down the heavy infantry.(wiki:Three_Thousand_of_Qohor.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) In total, the Unsullied repelled eighteen Dothraki charges and three attacks by Dothraki archers.(wiki:Three_Thousand_of_Qohor.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)(wiki:Three_Thousand_of_Qohor.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_qohor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Three_Thousand_of_Qohor.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) The Dothraki halted their attacks after more than twelve thousand of their fighters had been killed, including Khal Temmo, his sons, and his [bloodriders](wiki:Bloodrider). Out of the three thousand Unsullied, only six hundred remained. Four days after the fighting began, the new *[khal](wiki:Khal)* led his remaining *khalasar* past the city gates of Qohor in a stately procession. Each man cut off his braid and threw it down before the Unsullied.(wiki:Three_Thousand_of_Qohor.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)(wiki:Three_Thousand_of_Qohor.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_qohor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

### Aftermath

Since the victory of the Three Thousand of Qohor, the city guard of Qohor has been made up entirely of Unsullied. Each soldier carries a spear with a braid made of human hair attached to it.(wiki:Three_Thousand_of_Qohor.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)
