## Origins

[Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) holds a great [melee at Bitterbridge](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge) during his march toward [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Fighting_at_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) After hearing that his older brother [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) has [besieged Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Renly leaves his foot at Bitterbridge with his queen, [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell),(wiki:Fighting_at_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) and rides east with his horse to confront Stannis.(wiki:Fighting_at_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) assassinates Renly with a [shadow](wiki:Shadow_child),(wiki:Fighting_at_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Fighting_at_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) however, and Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell), kills two of his fellow [Rainbow Guard](wiki:Rainbow_Guard), [Robar Royce](wiki:Robar_Royce) and [Emmon Cuy](wiki:Emmon_Cuy), and another guard in his rage.(wiki:Fighting_at_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent) bends the knee to Stannis, the first of several of Renly's followers to join their late king's brother.(wiki:Fighting_at_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) Loras, however, returns to [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge) from Storm's End with a fifth of Renly's knights.(wiki:Fighting_at_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4) Lords [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) and [Mathis Rowan](wiki:Mathis_Rowan), who had also accompanied Renly,(wiki:Fighting_at_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) leave the camp as well.(wiki:Fighting_at_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Aftermath

Parmen and Erren are taken to [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) as hostages.(wiki:Fighting_at_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) The [Rowans](wiki:House_Rowan) and [Oakhearts](wiki:House_Oakheart) are spotted north of the [Mander](wiki:Mander).(wiki:Fighting_at_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) rides from the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) to Bitterbridge and then Highgarden,(wiki:Fighting_at_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) negotiating an alliance between the Tyrells, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), and King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). Most of the men at Bitterbridge join Joffrey,(wiki:Fighting_at_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-SSM_10-2) leading to the defeat of Stannis in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Fighting_at_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Fighting_at_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
