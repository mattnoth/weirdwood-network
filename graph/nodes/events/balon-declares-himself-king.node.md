## Identity

When [Robb Stark](robb-stark) sent his former ward [Theon Greyjoy](theon-greyjoy) to Pyke as his [envoy](theon-greyjoy-taken-as-ward) — bearing a letter offering alliance and a crown won together against the Lannisters — [Balon Greyjoy](balon-greyjoy) refused it. He would not be *given* a crown like a vassal; he would pay the iron price and take one, as Urron Redhand had five thousand years before. Styling himself "the Greyjoy, Lord Reaper of Pyke, King of Salt and Rock, Son of the Sea Wind," Balon chose war on his own terms — striking not west at the strong Lannisters but north at the ripe, undefended kingdom Robb had stripped of its swords. His refusal-and-declaration is the decision that launches the [ironborn invasion of the North](ironborn-invasion-of-the-north).
