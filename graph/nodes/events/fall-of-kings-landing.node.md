## Origins

Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) and Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) gathered their [green](wiki:Greens) forces to [march](wiki:Aemond's_march_on_Harrenhal) on [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), which left [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) largely defenseless. This created an opportunity that the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) seized.(wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) on [Caraxes](wiki:Caraxes) and Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) on [Syrax](wiki:Syrax) flew over the capital and circled their dragons above [Aegon's High Hill](wiki:Aegon's_High_Hill). The [fleet](wiki:Velaryon_fleet) of the Sea Snake, Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon), then arrived to the east of [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay).(wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

The sight of Daemon on Caraxes and Rhaenyra on Syrax over King's Landing sent the city into a panic. The Queen Dowager, [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower), tried to send riders to bring back Aemond and ravens to leal lords, but her efforts were quickly stopped. Unknown to Alicent was that the gold cloaks, the [City Watch of King's Landing](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing), had turned. The gold cloaks were loyal to Daemon who had once been their leader. When he went to dispatch ravens, Grand Maester [Orwyle](wiki:Orwyle) was beaten and bound by gold cloaks as he arrived in his chambers. The seven commanders of the city gates who had been chosen for their loyalty to King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) were either imprisoned or killed the moment Caraxes was spotted over the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep). Ser [Gwayne Hightower](wiki:Gwayne_Hightower), the second in command of the City Watch, was captured when he tried to sound the alarm. He was brought before Ser [Luthor Largent](wiki:Luthor_Largent), the commander of the gold cloaks. Gwayne denounced him as a turncloak to which Luthor replied:

> Daemon gave us these cloaks and they're gold no matter how you turn them.(wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Luthor then murdered Gwayne and had the city gates opened to the army pouring in from the Sea Snake's ships. Thirteen [House Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) knights and a hundred men-at-arms managed to hold the [River Gate](wiki:River_Gate) for eight hours from both attacks without and within the city. The other six gates, however, fell swiftly and the blacks' army entered the city unmolested through those gates. The city fell in less then a day. Seeing hope was lost, Queen Alicent ordered her forces to surrender.(wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Aftermath

King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) escaped the fall of King's Landing. He was smuggled out by the [master of whisperers](wiki:Master_of_whisperers), Lord [Larys Strong](wiki:Larys_Strong), via [secret passages](wiki:Secret_passages). Aegon was protected by two of his [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) knights, Ser [Willis Fell](wiki:Willis_Fell) and Ser [Rickard Thorne](wiki:Rickard_Thorne), and was accompanied by his children, Princess [Jaehaera](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen) and Prince [Maelor](wiki:Maelor_Targaryen).(wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)(wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Lord [Otto Hightower](wiki:Otto_Hightower) and Lord [Jasper Wylde](wiki:Jasper_Wylde) were executed for treason. Lords [Rosby](wiki:Lord_Rosby) and [Stokeworth](wiki:Lord_Stokeworth) tried to switch their allegiances back to Rhaenyra, but she refused, judging them to be "faithless friends" and ordered their executions. Queen [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower) and Princess [Helaena Targaryen](wiki:Helaena_Targaryen) were held as captives. Ser [Tyland Lannister](wiki:Tyland_Lannister) was [tortured](wiki:Torture) for information on where he had hidden the crown's treasure. Grand Maester Orwyle was confined in the [black cells](wiki:Black_cells).(wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Queen Rhaenyra Targaryen seized the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), though Septon [Eustace](wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)), who favored Aegon II,(wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing.cite_ref-3) wrote that she cut herself upon the throne, as if the throne had rejected her. At first the population of the city had welcomed Rhaenyra's ascent to the Iron Throne as Aegon II and Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) had not been much loved. In time, however, with more executions happening daily and more taxes being raised to fund the wars, the population began to loathe Rhaenyra, calling her "King [Maegor](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) with teats".(wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

When Queen Alicent heard of Rhaenyra's planned campaigns against Prince Aemond and Prince [Daeron](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)), she made a new offer of peace. This time, she proposed that the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) would be divided between the two claimants, with Rhaenyra keeping the [crownlands](wiki:Crownlands), [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn), the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) and the [North](wiki:North) and ruling from King's Landing, while Aegon II would rule a new realm from [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) that consisted of the [Reach](wiki:Reach), the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) and the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands). Rhaenyra rejected this offer, and noted that her brothers had given up positions of honor that they may have had when they stole her birthright and took the lives of her sons.(wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
