## Origins

In his campaign to place [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)) on the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) joins the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) in invading the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands). Their [Volantene](wiki:Volantis) fleet is scattered by a storm after [Lys](wiki:Lys), and only six of the [sellswords](wiki:Sellsword)' ten ships arrive at the rendezvous near the [rainwood](wiki:Rainwood) in [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath), land formerly owned by [House Connington](wiki:House_Connington). The gathered force is not quite half the Golden Company's full strength of ten thousand.(wiki:Taking_of_Greenstone.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Meanwhile, the Volantenes put Ser [Marq Mandrake](wiki:Marq_Mandrake) ashore on Estermont, with close to five hundred men. Although the island was never one of their objectives, Marq and his men take Greenstone, the seat of [House Estermont](wiki:House_Estermont),(wiki:Taking_of_Greenstone.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) but not before the Estermonts are able to send ravens to [King’s Landing](wiki:King’s_Landing) notifying the [small council](wiki:Small_council) of the attack as none of Mandrake's men are archers.(wiki:Taking_of_Greenstone.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Taking_of_Greenstone.cite_ref-Rawoiafestermont.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

During her journey to meet Aegon, Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) learns that Greenstone has fallen to the Golden Company. She wonders what happened to her friend [Spotted Sylva](wiki:Sylva_Santagar), the Lady of Greenstone.(wiki:Taking_of_Greenstone.cite_ref-arianne1_4-0)
