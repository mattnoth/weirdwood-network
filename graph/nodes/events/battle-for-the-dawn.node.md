## Origins

In the midst of the darkness of the [Long Night](wiki:Long_Night), the [Others](wiki:Others) emerged from the [far north](wiki:Lands_of_Always_Winter), wielding razor-thin swords of ice and raising [the dead](wiki:Wight) to fight the living. The [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) and their allies, the [First Men](wiki:First_Men), fought against them, but were driven southwards by their advance.(wiki:Battle_for_the_Dawn.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) The Others were eventually checked when it was discovered that weapons made of [dragonglass](wiki:Dragonglass) could kill them.(wiki:Battle_for_the_Dawn.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

According to the song "[The Night That Ended](wiki:The_Night_That_Ended)", the first members of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) rode against the Others and drove them back north in the Battle for the Dawn.(wiki:Battle_for_the_Dawn.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [Maesters](wiki:Maesters) generally believe the defeat of the Others and the construction of the [Wall](wiki:Wall) occurred six thousand years ago, although *[True History](wiki:True_History)* states these events were eight thousand years ago.(wiki:Battle_for_the_Dawn.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_the_long_night.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
