## Origins

After the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps) and the proclaiming of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) as [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) and [of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident), Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) allows his river lords to return to their lands and clear them of [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) intruders. Engagements are fought at [Stone Hedge](wiki:Liberation_of_Stone_Hedge), [Raventree Hall](wiki:Liberation_of_Raventree_Hall) and at [Darry](wiki:Darry), where Lord [Lyman Darry](wiki:Lyman_Darry)'s men [recapture his castle](wiki:Recapture_of_Darry) from the [westermen](wiki:Westerlands).(wiki:Sack_of_Darry.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Aftermath

Later in the war, Darry falls to [northern](wiki:North) forces after a [brief siege](wiki:Siege_of_Darry). Ser [Helman Tallhart](wiki:Helman_Tallhart) receives orders from Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) to put the Lannister garrison to the sword and torch the castle in the name of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark).(wiki:Sack_of_Darry.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
