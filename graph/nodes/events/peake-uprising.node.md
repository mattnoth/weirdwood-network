## Identity

## Edges

## Origins

[House Peake](wiki:House_Peake) were loyal supporters of the [Blackfyre pretenders](wiki:Blackfyre_pretenders). They were stripped of their castles [Dunstonbury](wiki:Dunstonbury) and [Whitegrove](wiki:Whitegrove) in the aftermath of the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion) in [196 AC](wiki:196_AC), and Lord [Gormon Peake](wiki:Gormon_Peake) was executed in the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion) in [212 AC](wiki:212_AC).(wiki:Peake_Uprising.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Aftermath

King Maekar's death led to political chaos. A [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council) was held [later that year](wiki:Great_Council) to determine his heir, and his fourth son, Prince Aegon, was crowned as King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen).(wiki:Peake_Uprising.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf414.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Tywald Lannister had long been [betrothed](wiki:Betrothal) to the Red Lion's sister, Lady [Ellyn Reyne](wiki:Ellyn_Reyne), who was unwilling to give up her ambition of becoming the Lady of Casterly Rock. After Tywald's death, Ellyn Reyne convinced his brother Tion to set aside his own betrothal for her. Lord [Gerold Lannister](wiki:Gerold_Lannister), the twins' father, opposed this match, but the ailing lord gave way(wiki:Peake_Uprising.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf761_2-4) after Tion revealed that Tywald's last words were begging him to "take care of Lady Ellyn".(wiki:Peake_Uprising.cite_ref-sample_4-4)
