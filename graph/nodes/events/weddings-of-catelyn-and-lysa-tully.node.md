## Origins

[Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark), [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell), betrothed [Brandon](wiki:Brandon_Stark), his eldest son and heir, to [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), the eldest daughter of [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), [Lord of Riverrun](wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun). [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), Hoster's lower-born [ward](wiki:Ward), later challenged Brandon over Catelyn's hand, and Brandon easily defeated Petyr in the ensuing duel at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) While recovering from his wounds, Petyr slept with Catelyn's younger sister, [Lysa](wiki:Lysa_Arryn), and impregnated her. Lord Tully sent Petyr back to the [Fingers](wiki:Fingers) to complete his healing(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) and, after learning of Lysa's pregnancy, forced her to end it by drinking [moon tea](wiki:Moon_tea).(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
[Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark), Lord Rickard's daughter, was betrothed to [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End). During the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) in [281 AC](wiki:281_AC), [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone), caused a scandal by naming Lyanna as [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty) instead of his own wife, [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell).(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Shortly before Catelyn and Brandon were to wed at Riverrun in [282 AC](wiki:282_AC),(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Rhaegar abducted Lyanna near Harrenhal at the start of the new year.(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons_-_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Telling Catelyn that they would be wed when he returned to Riverrun, Brandon rode to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), calling for Rhaegar to come out and die after reaching the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep). Rhaegar was not in the capital, however, and his father, King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), had Brandon and his companions imprisoned for treason.(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Summoning their fathers to court, the Mad King then had fathers and sons all executed, with the exception of [Ethan Glover](wiki:Ethan_Glover). In the [throne room](wiki:Great_Hall_(Red_Keep)) of the Red Keep, Aerys had Rickard burned to death while Brandon strangled nearby.(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) King Aerys next called for the heads of Robert Baratheon and Rickard's second son, [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), who had grown up in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) as wards of [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), [Lord of the Eyrie](wiki:Lord_of_the_Eyrie).(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Jon's refusal to surrender his former wards began [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons_-_robert.27s_rebellion.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Eddard (now Lord of Winterfell), Robert, and Jon rallied their [bannermen](wiki:Bannermen) against [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen). Lord Tully eventually joined the rebels as well, helping Eddard rescue Robert at the [Battle of the Bells](wiki:Battle_of_the_Bells).(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) As part of the alliance, Eddard agreed to marry Catelyn in Brandon's place.(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) The widowed Jon, meanwhile, had been left heirless after the deaths of his nephew, [Elbert](wiki:Elbert_Arryn), and cousin, Ser [Denys](wiki:Denys_Arryn).(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Jon, who was even older than Hoster, agreed to marry his younger daughter Lysa, as she was known to be fertile.(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Aftermath

Eddard stayed at Riverrun for a fortnight, during which Catelyn became pregnant with their first child, [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark).(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Catelyn and Lysa remained at Riverrun with their father Hoster when their new husbands left to rejoin the rebellion against [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen).(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1)(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Lysa hoped she had become pregnant as well, but her moon's blood was only delayed at the time.(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
Catelyn and Eddard came to have a loving relationship with five children, though Catelyn dislikes that Eddard brought his [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son, [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), to live at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) after the war.(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) Lysa and Jon Arryn, meanwhile, have had five miscarriages, two stillborn children and one living child, [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn).(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Lysa has grown to resent her marriage with Jon,(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) who is old enough to be her grandfather,(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) and she has secretly pined for Petyr Baelish,(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1) who has become [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin) for King Robert I Baratheon.

And one day fifteen years ago, this second father had become a brother as well, as he and Ned stood together in the [sept](wiki:Sept) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) to wed two sisters, the daughters of Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully).(wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-6)
