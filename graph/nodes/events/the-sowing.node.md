## Identity

Prince Jacaerys Velaryon's call for any commoner or dragonseed who could master one of Dragonstone's masterless dragons, following the deaths at Rook's Rest; also called the Red Sowing or the Sowing of the Seeds. Produced four new dragonriders — Hugh Hammer (Vermithor), Ulf White (Silverwing), Addam of Hull (Seasmoke), and Nettles (Sheepstealer) — at heavy cost in dead and maimed seekers.
