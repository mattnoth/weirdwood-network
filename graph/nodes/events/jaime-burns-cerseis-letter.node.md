# Jaime burns Cersei's letter

The marquee [Jaime](jaime-lannister)↔[Cersei](cersei-lannister) rupture. At Riverrun, on the first
snowfall of winter, a raven brings Jaime a letter from King's Landing — [Qyburn](qyburn)'s terse cover
note over Cersei's fevered plea, written from her imprisonment by the Faith: *"Come at once… Help me.
Save me. I need you now as I have never needed you before. I love you. I love you. I love you."*
Jaime reads it in the window seat as a snowflake melts on the ink, rolls it up one-handed, hands it to
[Peck](peck), and says only, *"No… Put this in the fire."* He sends no reply and does not ride to her
aid — the decisive break in the Kingsguard-twin bond.
