## Origins

Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) and Lord [Cley Cerwyn](wiki:Cley_Cerwyn) lead levies from [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) and [Castle Cerwyn](wiki:Castle_Cerwyn) to defend [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Torrhen's_Square), the seat of [House Tallhart](wiki:House_Tallhart) in the [north](wiki:North). The besieging [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) led by [Dagmer Cleftjaw](wiki:Dagmer) are forced to retreat to the [Stony Shore](wiki:Stony_Shore) following the [fight at Torrhen's Square](wiki:Fight_at_Torrhen's_Square).(wiki:Capture_of_Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Capture_of_Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Rodrik, Cley, and [Leobald Tallhart](wiki:Leobald_Tallhart), the castellan of Torrhen's Square, depart to retake Winterfell from [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy). However, they are betrayed by [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) and killed by [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton) in the [battle at Winterfell](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell).(wiki:Capture_of_Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
