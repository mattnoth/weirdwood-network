## Identity

Grieving the deaths of Queen Aemma and the infant Prince Baelon in childbed, King Viserys I breaks with the precedents of 92 and 101 AC and declares his daughter Rhaenyra his heir, naming her Princess of Dragonstone; hundreds of lords do obeisance and swear to defend her succession.
