## Origins

Thirteen thousand [Poor Fellows](wiki:Poor_Fellows) and two hundred [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons), led by Ser [Horys Hill](wiki:Horys_Hill), joined nearly seven thousand household knights and feudal levies of a dozen rebel lords from the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) and [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands), led by Lord [Rupert Falwell](wiki:Rupert_Falwell), Alongside Rupert the Fighting Fool rode Ser [Lyonel Lorch](wiki:Lyonel_Lorch), Ser [Alyn Terrick](wiki:Alyn_Terrick), Lord [Tristifer Wayn](wiki:Tristifer_Wayn), Lord [Jon Lychester](wiki:Jon_Lychester), and many other puissant knights.(wiki:Battle_at_the_Great_Fork.cite_ref-Rtsotd.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Maegor had a similar number of men, although he had near twice as many armored horse. The king also had numerous longbowmen and his [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Balerion](wiki:Balerion).(wiki:Battle_at_the_Great_Fork.cite_ref-Rtsotd.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

The two armies met at the Great Fork of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush). During the battle, Lord Rupert killed two knights of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), but was himself slain by Lord [Mooton](wiki:House_Mooton) of [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool). Despite having been blinded by a sword early on in the battle, [Big Jon Hogg](wiki:Jon_Hogg) led a charge which put the Poor Fellows to flight. King Maegor flew upon Balerion's back during the battle, and despite the rain, the dragon caused many deaths with its flames. The savage battle lasted until nightfall, ending in victory for Maegor.(wiki:Battle_at_the_Great_Fork.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Battle_at_the_Great_Fork.cite_ref-Rtsotd.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) The king returned to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) and mutilated [Wat the Hewer](wiki:Wat_the_Hewer), who had been captured in the earlier [battle at Stonebridge](wiki:Battle_at_Stonebridge).(wiki:Battle_at_the_Great_Fork.cite_ref-Rtsotd.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
