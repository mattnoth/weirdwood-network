## Identity

Over the dying [Maester Luwin](luwin) at the burned Winterfell godswood, the survivors divide. [Osha](osha) takes [Rickon](rickon-stark) (and Shaggydog) one way; [Bran](bran-stark) with [Hodor](hodor), [Jojen](jojen-reed), [Meera](meera-reed) (and [Summer](summer)) strikes north — *"Our road is north," Jojen announced.* The northward course is driven by Jojen's green dreams, not by Luwin (whose dying words are a dismissal and a request for mercy, not counsel for the split). This is the connective beat that launches the journey beyond the Wall; Rickon's onward thread (Skagos) is deferred/unwritten.
