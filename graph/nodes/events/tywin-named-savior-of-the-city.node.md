## Identity

In the days after the [Battle of the Blackwater](battle-of-the-blackwater), King Joffrey held a grand throne-room ceremony to honor the victory's heroes. Tywin Lannister — whose relief force, marching with the [Tyrell](house-tyrell) host, had arrived at dusk and shattered Stannis's flank — rode into the hall on his destrier. Joffrey descended the Iron Throne to embrace his grandfather and proclaim him **Savior of the City**, then fastened the Hand's chain of office around his neck, restoring him as [Hand of the King](hand-of-the-king) after Tyrion's tenure. The ceremony consolidated Tywin's political dominance over the Iron Throne and immediately preceded the small council's request that Joffrey set Sansa Stark aside for Margaery Tyrell. (The honorific itself is the [Savior of the City](savior-of-the-city) title node; this node is the event of its conferral.)
