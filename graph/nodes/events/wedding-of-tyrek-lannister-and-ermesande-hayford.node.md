## Identity

Wedding of Tyrek Lannister and Ermesande Hayford is a battle/event from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges


## Origins

The thirteen-year-old(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrek_Lannister_and_Ermesande_Hayford.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) [Tyrek Lannister](wiki:Tyrek_Lannister), once a squire of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), is betrothed to [Ermesande Hayford](wiki:Ermesande_Hayford), the infant [Lady of Hayford](wiki:Lady_of_Hayford), so that [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) can claim her lands.(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrek_Lannister_and_Ermesande_Hayford.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
When hungry [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) hear about preparations for the wedding feast, they gather at the gates of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) to demand food. King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) leads a sortie of [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) and [red cloaks](wiki:House_Lannister_guards) which disperses the crowd.(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrek_Lannister_and_Ermesande_Hayford.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Aftermath

Tyrek is mocked as "Wet Nurse" by other squires for having married Ermesande.(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrek_Lannister_and_Ermesande_Hayford.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrek_Lannister_and_Ermesande_Hayford.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Tyrek goes missing during the [riots in King's Landing](wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing).(wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrek_Lannister_and_Ermesande_Hayford.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
