## Origins

After 89 years on the [Oakenseat](wiki:Oakenseat), King Garth Greybeard was senile and had an insecure succession, having sired only daughters, one of whom was married to Lord [Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) and another to Lord [Peake](wiki:House_Peake), both lords pushing for their own wives to succeed the king. The rivalry between Manderly and Peake was marked by betrayal, conspiracy, and murder, eventually escalating into an open war of succession.(wiki:Anarchy_in_the_Reach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Aftermath

The new king spent his reign rebuilding Highgarden and restoring the power of [House Gardener](wiki:House_Gardener) and the Reach. His son and successor, [Garth XI](wiki:Garth_XI_Gardener), took vengeance upon the [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen), earning the epithet Garth the Painter after Lord [Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) said that the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) had been green until the king painted them with Dornish blood.(wiki:Anarchy_in_the_Reach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
