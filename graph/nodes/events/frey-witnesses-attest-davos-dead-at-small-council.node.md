# Frey witnesses attest to Davos's death at Cersei's small council

Beat 4 (terminal beat) of the Wyman Manderly deception arc (`wyman-manderly-stages-fake-execution-of-davos`). King Tommen I's small council in King's Landing is informed that Wyman Manderly has had Davos Seaworth executed. Members of House Frey testify they have seen Davos's head — the tarred, onion-mouthed prop mounted above White Harbor's Seal Gate (beat 3) — and Cersei Lannister accepts the testimony.

This is the beat that closes the deception's audience-loop: Wyman's false belief lands where it was aimed. The payoff follows immediately — in return for the apparent execution, the Iron Throne returns Wylis Manderly from captivity at Maidenpool to White Harbor. Wylis's homecoming is what the whole farce was *for*; the deception's entire purpose was to ransom Wyman's heir without ever appearing to bargain.

Wyman names the closure himself a chapter later (ADWD Davos IV): "the mummer's farce is almost done. My son is home."
