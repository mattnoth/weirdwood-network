## Origins

Then ten years old, [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) was infatuated when she saw the seventeen-year-old crown prince, [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), who was newly [knighted](wiki:Knight). Rhaegar defeated two of Cersei's uncles and a dozen of Lord Tywin's finest knights in the joust before falling to Ser [Arthur Dayne](wiki:Arthur_Dayne) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). That night, Rhaegar's harp playing made Cersei cry.
Lady [Genna Lannister](wiki:Genna_Lannister) confided to her niece, Cersei, that the young girl would be betrothed to Prince Rhaegar in the feast ending the tourney. The happy Cersei then visited the tent of [Maggy the Frog](wiki:Maggy_the_Frog) with her friends, [Jeyne Farman](wiki:Jeyne_Farman) and [Melara Hetherspoon](wiki:Melara_Hetherspoon). There was no celebratory feast, however; Aerys rejected Tywin's offer, as he considered Tywin, his [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), a servant. Cersei was left in tears, while the tension between Aerys and Tywin increased.(wiki:Tournament_in_honor_of_Viserys's_birth.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
