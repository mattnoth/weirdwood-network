## Origins

[House Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood) declared for Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) when war broke out, while [House Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken) supported her half-brother, King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen). Lord [Samwell Blackwood](wiki:Samwell_Blackwood) sent raiders into Bracken lands, causing Ser [Amos Bracken](wiki:Amos_Bracken) to march on Blackwood territory in response. The Blackwoods surprised the Brackens while they were camped by [a mill](wiki:Burning_Mill) near a river, possibly the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Burning_Mill.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Amos slew Lord Blackwood in single combat during the ensuing battle. The Bracken [knight](wiki:Knight) was himself slain by a [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) arrow said to have been fired by Samwell's sister, [Alysanne](wiki:Alysanne_Blackwood). The mill was put to the torch during the hours of fighting. Ser [Raylon Rivers](wiki:Raylon_Rivers) eventually led the Bracken survivors in retreat to [Stone Hedge](wiki:Stone_Hedge), but the Bracken castle had been [taken](wiki:Taking_of_Stone_Hedge) in the meantime by Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Burning_Mill.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Battle_of_the_Burning_Mill.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Battle_of_the_Burning_Mill.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
