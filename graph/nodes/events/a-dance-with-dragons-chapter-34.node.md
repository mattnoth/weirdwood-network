## Narrative Arc

[Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark), [Hodor](wiki:Hodor), [Jojen](wiki:Jojen_Reed), and [Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed) are still dwelling in the [cave](wiki:Cave_of_the_three-eyed_crow) of the [children of the Forest](wiki:Children_of_the_Forest) and exploring its chambers. Bran uses his ability as a [warg and skinchanger](wiki:Skinchanger) to enter [Summer](wiki:Summer) and [Hodor](wiki:Hodor). They begin to name some of the "children" as their own names are unpronounceable by the human tongue. Bran names one of the females [Leaf](wiki:Leaf), as she is always adorned with leaves. She is one of the few children who can speak the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue). Others are called [Ash](wiki:Ash), [Scales](wiki:Scales), [Black Knife](wiki:Black_Knife), [Snowylocks](wiki:Snowylocks), and [Coals](wiki:Coals).
[Jojen](wiki:Jojen_Reed) is growing extremely sad and solemn, and still has fits of shaking. His sister [Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed) does her best to keep up the spirits of both her brother and Bran.
The [three-eyed crow](wiki:Three-eyed_crow) teaches [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) how to enter into ravens. He learns that all of the ravens around the three-eyed crow have children who have passed away and remain in the skins of these animals. He also learns to use [weirwoods](wiki:Weirwood) to look into the past. The three-eyed crow tells Bran that he was once a lord called Brynden.
The children feed Bran a bowl of [weirwood paste](wiki:Weirwood_paste) to awaken his green seeing gifts. Bran sees his father, [Eddard](wiki:Eddard_Stark), through [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell)'s heart tree, and learns that he can view the past, present and future through the weirwoods. The last greenseer tells Bran that he is haunted by his own ghosts, a brother that he loved, a brother that he hated, and a woman that he desired,(wiki:A_Dance_with_Dragons-Chapter_34.cite_ref-1) but knows from experience that you cannot change the past. When he gets tired, he sends Bran away. Hodor carries Bran to his bed.
In his sleep, Bran sees the following visions through Winterfell's heart tree:
His young [father](wiki:Ned_Stark) praying with a bowed head "…let them grow up close as brothers, with only love between them, and let my [lady wife](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) find it in her heart to forgive.";(wiki:A_Dance_with_Dragons-Chapter_34.cite_ref-2)
A girl and a younger boy play fighting with branches;(wiki:A_Dance_with_Dragons-Chapter_34.cite_ref-3)
A pregnant woman coming out of the black pool praying for a son to avenge her;
A slender girl on her toes kissing a [knight](wiki:Knight) as tall as Hodor;(wiki:A_Dance_with_Dragons-Chapter_34.cite_ref-4)
A pale, dark-eyed youth cutting three branches from the weirwood and shaping them into arrows;
Other lords of Winterfell: tall, hard, stern men in fur and chain mail;
A bearded man forcing a captive down on his knees, and a white-haired woman killing the captive with a bronze sickle.
