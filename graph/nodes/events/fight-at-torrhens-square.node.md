## Origins

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) and [Dagmer Cleftjaw](wiki:Dagmer) plot to take [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). They plan to besiege Torrhen's Square, the seat of [House Tallhart](wiki:House_Tallhart), as a diversion, drawing away Winterfell's garrison and leaving it defenceless. Theon sends Dagmer with most of their [ironmen](wiki:Ironmen) to Torrhen's Square,(wiki:Fight_at_Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) whose castellan is [Leobald Tallhart](wiki:Leobald_Tallhart).

In response Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) raises Winterfell's levies and marches with six hundred [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) men to relieve Torrhen's Square, where he is joined by three hundred [Cerwyns](wiki:House_Cerwyn) led by Lord [Cley Cerwyn](wiki:Cley_Cerwyn). Dagmer is still besieging Torrhen's Square when the northmen arrive.(wiki:Fight_at_Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

Rodrik knows the land intimately, while Dagmer does not. Rodrik leads the gathered northmen to victory. The ironborn are not disciplined enough to stand a charge by mounted horsemen and their shield wall breaks. Dagmer leads the remnants of his force back to the [Stony Shore](wiki:Stony_Shore).(wiki:Fight_at_Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Aftermath

With Winterfell's levies away at Torrhen's Square, Theon Greyjoy is able to [capture the undefended Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Fight_at_Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) After defeating Dagmer's attack on Torrhen's Square, Rodrik redirects his levies to retake Winterfell from Theon and his ironborn.(wiki:Fight_at_Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) However, [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) and the [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton) betray Rodrik's army in a [battle at Winterfell](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell), during which Leobald, Rodrik, and Cley are slain.(wiki:Fight_at_Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Dagmer then returns to Torrhen's Square and is able to [capture the lightly-defended castle](wiki:Capture_of_Torrhen's_Square).(wiki:Fight_at_Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
