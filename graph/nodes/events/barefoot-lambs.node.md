## Origins

During the [Moon of the Three Kings](wiki:Moon_of_the_Three_Kings), the [Shepherd](wiki:Shepherd) and his followers controlled the [Hill of Rhaenys](wiki:Hill_of_Rhaenys) and the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit). At the height of his power, the Shepherd's "barefoot army" counted in the tens of thousands.(wiki:Barefoot_lambs.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) However, by the time [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) was taken by Lord [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon)'s army in late [130 AC](wiki:130_AC), their numbers had dwindled to three hundred. On the last day of the year, the Shepherd and two hundred forty-one of his lambs were executed.(wiki:Barefoot_lambs.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
