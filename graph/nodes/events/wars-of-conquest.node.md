## Origins

Six of the kingdoms submitted to [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), the first [Lord of the Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Lord_of_the_Seven_Kingdoms), during the initial two years of war, which became known as [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest). Though the Wars of Conquest are usually said to have ended when Aegon was [crowned in Oldtown](wiki:Aegon's_coronations), according to Archmaester [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn) and Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel) there is no consensus ending for the conflicts, since [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) remained independent.(wiki:Wars_of_Conquest.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)(wiki:Wars_of_Conquest.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
After Aegon's coronation, Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) suppressed the [Sistermen's Rebellion](wiki:Sistermen's_Rebellion) and Aegon led an [invasion of the Iron Islands](wiki:Invasion_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Wars_of_Conquest.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0); afterwards Aegon invaded Dorne in the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War)(wiki:Wars_of_Conquest.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) This period is known as the [Dragon's wars](wiki:Dragon's_wars). Subsequent kings—[Aenys I](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen), [Maegor I](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), [Jaehaerys I](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and [Daeron I](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen)—also fought [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen).(wiki:Wars_of_Conquest.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
There are no known attempts by Aegon's family to conquer [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall). Generations later, King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) considered claiming all land one hundred leagues north of the [Wall](wiki:Wall), however.(wiki:Wars_of_Conquest.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
