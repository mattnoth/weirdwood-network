## Origins

King [Harmund III Hoare](wiki:Harmund_III_Hoare) and his mother, [Lelia Lannister](wiki:Lelia_Lannister), were mutilated when the [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands) was deposed by the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn). This led to twelve years of warfare between the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) and the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands), which left the islands defeated and impoverished. Archmaester [Hake](wiki:Hake_(archmaester)) states that during the Famine Winter which followed, three times as many islanders died from famine than from the previous warfare.(wiki:Famine_Winter.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
