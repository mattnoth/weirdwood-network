Wedding of Roose Bolton and Walda Frey is a battle/event from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges


## Origins

[Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), [Lord of the Dreadfort](wiki:Lord_of_the_Dreadfort), retreats to the [Twins](wiki:Twins)(wiki:Wedding_of_Roose_Bolton_and_Walda_Frey.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) after being defeated by [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), [Lord of Casterly Rock](wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock), at the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:Wedding_of_Roose_Bolton_and_Walda_Frey.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Roose, twice a widower,(wiki:Wedding_of_Roose_Bolton_and_Walda_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) agrees to marry a member of [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey). [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey), [Lord of the Crossing](wiki:Lord_of_the_Crossing), offers the bride's weight in [silver](wiki:Silver) for a dowry, so Roose chooses Walder's fifteen-year-old granddaughter [Walda](wiki:Walda_Frey_(daughter_of_Merrett)), who is known as "Fat Walda" because of her weight.(wiki:Wedding_of_Roose_Bolton_and_Walda_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Aftermath

After Tywin retreats to [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Roose takes the [ruby ford](wiki:Ruby_ford) and the [crossroads](wiki:Crossroads).(wiki:Wedding_of_Roose_Bolton_and_Walda_Frey.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) When Tywin marches west to confront [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) sends word for Ser [Helman Tallhart](wiki:Helman_Tallhart) to depart the Twins and join Roose in a [campaign against Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal).(wiki:Wedding_of_Roose_Bolton_and_Walda_Frey.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
Walda sends frequent letters from the Twins to Roose while he is encamped at Harrenhal.(wiki:Wedding_of_Roose_Bolton_and_Walda_Frey.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Roose hopes to have a trueborn son with Walda,(wiki:Wedding_of_Roose_Bolton_and_Walda_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) as he fears his [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son, [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), murdered his heir, [Domeric Bolton](wiki:Domeric_Bolton).(wiki:Wedding_of_Roose_Bolton_and_Walda_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
In the aftermath of the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) names Roose as [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North) and legitimizes Ramsay.(wiki:Wedding_of_Roose_Bolton_and_Walda_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) While at [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton), Roose informs the captive [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) that he has become fond of Walda, though he expects that Ramsay will kill any sons she births.(wiki:Wedding_of_Roose_Bolton_and_Walda_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) At [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), Theon notices that Walda is pregnant.(wiki:Wedding_of_Roose_Bolton_and_Walda_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
