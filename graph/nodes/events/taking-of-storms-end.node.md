## Origins

During the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) takes [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)) after the death of its castellan, Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose).(wiki:Taking_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He appoints Ser [Gilbert Farring](wiki:Gilbert_Farring) as the new castellan, with Lord [Elwood Meadows](wiki:Elwood_Meadows) as Gilbert's second.(wiki:Taking_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) After his defeat in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Stannis sails for the [Wall](wiki:Wall) to aid the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) against [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder),(wiki:Taking_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) leaving Storm's End and [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) lightly held in the south.(wiki:Taking_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

After [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Tommen_I_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), the bride's father, Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), travels south with half of [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell)'s strength to [besiege Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(300)).(wiki:Taking_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) After learning of Margaery's imprisonment by the [Faith](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), however, Mace abandons his siege and returns to the capital.(wiki:Taking_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Mace leaves a token force(wiki:Taking_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) under the leadership of Lord [Mathis Rowan](wiki:Mathis_Rowan) to maintain their siege of Storm's End.(wiki:Taking_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Rawoiafmathis_rowan.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

The [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) sails on [Volantene](wiki:Volantis) ships from [Essos](wiki:Essos) to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) as part of their plan to crown Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(Young_Griff)), widely thought to have been killed during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), in opposition to Tommen.(wiki:Taking_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) At [Griffin's Roost](wiki:Griffin's_Roost), [Haldon Halfmaester](wiki:Haldon) informs Lord [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) that Lord Tyrell has returned to King's Landing. Jon tells the captains of the company that capturing Storm's End would give them a secure base and boost Aegon's prestige. When Jon informs Aegon four days later, the prince agrees with the plan but surprises Jon by stating he wants to lead the attack himself.(wiki:Taking_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

The [small council](wiki:Small_council) of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) hears reports of the Golden Company marching on the castle with Jon and the pretender Aegon.(wiki:Taking_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Narrative Arc

While Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) travels from [Mistwood](wiki:Mistwood) to Griffin's Roost, she overhears [Chain](wiki:Chain) talking about Storm's End. Once at Griffin's Roost, she is told by Haldon that Storm's End has fallen and that Lord Connington awaits her there.(wiki:Taking_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Arianne_II_1-1)'

## Aftermath

An army from King's Landing, assumed by Ser [Daemon Sand](wiki:Daemon_Sand) to be led by Lord Tyrell, is descending on Storm's End. Haldon claims that Aegon means to meet this army in open battle. Daemon privately advises Arianne against traveling to Storm's End to meet with Jon, but she insists upon going.(wiki:Taking_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Arianne_II_1-2)
