## Origins

[Argilac Durrandon](wiki:Argilac_Durrandon), the aged [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King), sought to use [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), [Lord of Dragonstone](wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone), as a shield against the threat of [Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare), [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers). Argilac clumsily offered Aegon the hand of his [maiden](wiki:Maiden) daughter, [Argella](wiki:Argella_Durrandon), with a [dowry](wiki:Dowry) of consisting of the lands east of the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye) between the [Trident](wiki:Trident) and the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush). Although once controlled by the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands), this territory in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) was now ruled by [House Hoare](wiki:House_Hoare).(wiki:Last_Storm.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Last_Storm.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Aegon refused and instead made his own counter-offer, asking for the additional cession of [Massey's Hook](wiki:Massey's_Hook) and lands between the Blackwater, the [Wendwater](wiki:Wendwater), and the [Mander](wiki:Mander), territory long held by the stormlands.(wiki:Last_Storm.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) This would be sealed with the [marriage](wiki:Marriage) of Argella to [Orys Baratheon](wiki:Orys_Baratheon), Aegon's childhood friend and champion, who was also rumored to be Aegon's [bastard](wiki:Bastard) half-brother. Enraged, Argilac had the hands of Aegon's envoy cut off and sent to Aegon with a message: "These are the only hands your bastard shall have of me."(wiki:Last_Storm.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Artilac's response sparked Aegon's intentions to conquer [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) and unify the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) under his rule. After [landing at the mouth of the Blackwater](wiki:Aegon's_Landing), Aegon was crowned by one of his sister-wives, [Visenya](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen). He then received the allegiance of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) by slaying the Hoares through the [burning of Harrenhal](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal). Meanwhile, Aegon sent Orys and his other sister-wife, [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen), to conquer the stormlands. Argilac refused to die in the same manner as Harren and called his banners to [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End).(wiki:Last_Storm.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Lords [Buckler](wiki:House_Buckler), [Errol](wiki:House_Errol), and [Fell](wiki:House_Fell) attacked Orys's van in an [ambush on the Wendwater](wiki:Ambush_on_the_Wendwater) and then fled into the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood). Rhaenys responded by burning the forest with the [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame) of [Meraxes](wiki:Meraxes).(wiki:Last_Storm.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) The stormlands were also troubled by [pirates](wiki:Pirates) attacking [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath) from the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) and [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) raiding the [Dornish Marches](wiki:Dornish_Marches) from the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains).(wiki:Last_Storm.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Narrative Arc

Atop [Meraxes](wiki:Meraxes), [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) scouted [Argilac Durrandon](wiki:Argilac_Durrandon)'s movements while [Orys Baratheon](wiki:Orys_Baratheon) fortified a strong defensive position in the hills south of [Bronzegate](wiki:Bronzegate). As the two armies closed on each other, a massive storm broke out, with the rain blowing against the Targaryen host. Argilac, knowing he had almost twice as many men as the Targaryens and four times as many [knights](wiki:Knight) and heavy horse, decided to press the attack.(wiki:Last_Storm.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Initial charges by Argilac's horsemen were slowed by muddy ground and steep slopes. His spearmen had greater success on foot, however, and the Durrandon host conquered two hills.(wiki:Last_Storm.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

Argilac's fourth charge broke through the enemy center only to meet Rhaenys atop Meraxes, with the [dragon](wiki:Dragon) grounded because of the storm. The queen unleashed [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame) on [Dickon Morrigen](wiki:Dickon_Morrigen) and the [Bastard of Blackhaven](wiki:Bastard_of_Blackhaven), who commanded the Durrandon van. Argilac's personal guard were burned as well, and the Storm King was thrown from his saddle in the confusion. Argilac slew six men and held off another six as Orys rode down the hill and dismounted. When Argilac refused Orys's offer to yield, the pair began single combat. Each took a wound, but soon Argilac would get his wish and die fighting. His death ended the battle, as the stormlanders yielded or fled.(wiki:Last_Storm.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

## Aftermath

After her father's death, Princess [Argella Durrandon](wiki:Argella_Durrandon) barred [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) and declared herself [Storm Queen](wiki:Storm_King). Her garrison, however, feared to share [the fate of Harren](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal) and revolted against Argella after a few days, delivering her to Orys, chained and naked. Orys treated Argella gently, removing the chains, covering her with his cloak, and giving her food and [wine](wiki:Wine). Afterward, Orys married Argella and became [Lord Paramount of the Stormlands](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Stormlands). He took the sigil and words of [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon) for his own family, the newly-founded [House Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon).(wiki:Last_Storm.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)(wiki:Last_Storm.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

After the submission of the stormlands, Rhaenys flew from Storm's End to support Aegon at the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire).(wiki:Last_Storm.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
