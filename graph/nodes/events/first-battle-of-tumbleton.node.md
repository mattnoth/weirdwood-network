## Origins

Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower) led an army of [greens](wiki:Greens) from the [Reach](wiki:Reach) toward [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), which at the time was held by Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and the [blacks](wiki:Blacks). Ser [Ulf White](wiki:Ulf_White) and Ser [Hard Hugh Hammer](wiki:Hugh_Hammer) were sent with their dragons, [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor) and [Silverwing](wiki:Silverwing), to [Tumbleton](wiki:Tumbleton), the seat of [House Footly](wiki:House_Footly) and the last stronghold of Rhaenyra's between the capital and Ormund's advancing army.(wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

The blacks defending the market town including thousands from [House Caswell](wiki:House_Caswell) of [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge), [House Merryweather](wiki:House_Merryweather) of [Longtable](wiki:Longtable), and lands farther south. Ser [Garibald Grey](wiki:Garibald_Grey), Ser [Pate of Longleaf](wiki:Pate_of_Longleaf), [Red Robb Rivers](wiki:Robb_Rivers), and the [Winter Wolves](wiki:Winter_Wolves) of Lord [Roderick Dustin](wiki:Roderick_Dustin) arrived victorious from the [Butcher's Ball](wiki:Butcher's_Ball). Other defenders came from the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush), including [Moslander of Yore](wiki:Moslander_of_Yore), Ser [Garrick Hall](wiki:Garrick_Hall), Ser [Merrell the Bold](wiki:Merrell_the_Bold), Ser [Roger Corne](wiki:Roger_Corne), and Lord [Owain Bourney](wiki:Owain_Bourney). Total numbers vary between six thousand and twelve thousand, with *[True Telling](wiki:True_Telling)* using nearly nine thousand.(wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

The greens greatly outnumbered the blacks. Some greens infiltrated the town as refugees, and Roger and Owain were secretly loyal to King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen).(wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Narrative Arc

At the start of the battle, Ser [Garibald Grey](wiki:Garibald_Grey) led six thousand blacks against the troops of Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower), whose archers thinned Garibald's ranks. The bowmen of [Robb Rivers](wiki:Robb_Rivers) defended Garibald's men as they retreated from Ormund's heavy horse. Lord [Roderick Dustin](wiki:Roderick_Dustin) then lead the [Winter Wolves](wiki:Winter_Wolves) out a postern gate against the Hightowers. Although the [northmen](wiki:Northmen) were outnumbered ten-to-one, the Winter Wolves managed to reach the commander of the greens. Roderick slew Ormund and his cousin, Ser [Bryndon Hightower](wiki:Bryndon_Hightower), before succumbing to his wounds.(wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

The appearance of the dragon [Tessarion](wiki:Tessarion), mount of Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)), across the field did not dismay Tumbleton's defenders, as Tessarion was smaller than [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor) and [Silverwing](wiki:Silverwing). The defenders of Tumbleton thought the battle was won when the Hightowers were killed, but the [Two Betrayers](wiki:Two_Betrayers), Ser [Hugh Hammer](wiki:Hugh_Hammer) and Ser [Ulf White](wiki:Ulf_White), turned their dragons upon the town.(wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

Buildings throughout Tumbleton burned to ash, and thousands burned to death or drowned trying to cross the [Mander](wiki:Mander).(wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6) Outside of the walls, Ser [Pate of Longleaf](wiki:Pate_of_Longleaf) was unhorsed and trampled, while Garibald was burned by Tessarion's [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame). Ser [Roger Corne](wiki:Roger_Corne)'s men opened Tumbleton's gates to the greens, and Lord [Owain Bourney](wiki:Owain_Bourney) betrayed Ser [Merrell the Bold](wiki:Merrell_the_Bold) within the town's castle. Lord [Footly's](wiki:House_Footly) men yielded, but they were then bound and beheaded by the greens.(wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)

## Aftermath

After hearing of the Battle of Tumbleton and the blacks' defeat, Dowager Queen [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower) is said to have laughed, promising, "All they have sowed, now shall they reap."(wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-12)

Many in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) tried to flee, fearing that the fate of Tumbleton would happen to the capital, but Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) had the [gold cloaks](wiki:Gold_cloaks) seal the city's gates.(wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-13)

Remaining at Tumbleton instead of continuing on to King's Landing, the green army diminished daily as men deserted with their plunder.(wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-14) Disapproval of the Two Betrayers led to the formation of the [Caltrops](wiki:Caltrops) by highborn nobles,(wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and the greens then faced Ser [Addam Velaryon](wiki:Addam_Velaryon) and an army of rivermen in the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton).(wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
