## Identity

Queen Jaehaera, ten years old and the last surviving child of Aegon II, dies on the twenty-second day of the ninth moon of 133 AC after falling (or being thrown) from a window in Maegor's Holdfast onto the spikes of the dry moat below — officially ruled a suicide like her mother Helaena's death, but widely suspected as murder at Unwin Peake's instigation.
