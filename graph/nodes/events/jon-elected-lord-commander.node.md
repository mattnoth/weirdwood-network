## Identity

[Jon Snow](jon-snow) is chosen the 998th Lord Commander of the Night's Watch in the choosing held at Castle Black in the aftermath of the [battle beneath the Wall](battle-beneath-the-wall), with King [Stannis Baratheon](stannis-baratheon) confining the brothers until they elect a leader. [Samwell Tarly](samwell-tarly) engineers the vote against Lord [Janos Slynt](janos-slynt) — Stannis's and Lord Tywin's favored candidate — by persuading the rival captains Cotter Pyke and Ser Denys Mallister to stand aside, while Dolorous Edd puts Jon's name forward. During the count, Lord Mormont's old raven bursts from the kettle, circles the vault, and lands on Jon's shoulder, and a flood of arrowhead tokens carries the election. The office establishes the authority Jon then wields to order the [execution of Janos Slynt](execution-of-janos-slynt) for insubordination and, later, to admit the free folk through the Wall.
