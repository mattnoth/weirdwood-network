## Identity

Failed harvests across the North, riverlands, westerlands, Vale, and Reach following the cruel winter of 59-60 AC; larders and granaries emptied, some old folk went out into the snow to die so their kin might live, and hoarding drove up the price of bread across the Seven Kingdoms.
