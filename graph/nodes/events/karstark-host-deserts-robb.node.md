## Identity

The night [Robb Stark](robb-stark) condemned [Lord Rickard Karstark](rickard-karstark) to death for [murdering the Lannister and Frey captives](karstark-murders-prisoners-at-riverrun), the Karstark host melted away from [Riverrun](riverrun) — "near three hundred riders and twice as many mounts," all the mounted strength of Karhold, stealing off into the rain in ones and twos and then in larger groups. The Blackfish rode out after them but expected to bring back few. With the Karstark foot away under [Roose Bolton](roose-bolton) likely to turn at the news of their liege's [execution](execution-of-rickard-karstark) as well, Robb's army was gutted of a major contingent at the worst possible moment. The loss deepened his dependence on a renewed alliance with [House Frey](house-frey) — the reconciliation that would draw him to the Twins and the [Red Wedding](red-wedding).
