## Origins

Lyonel Baratheon, [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End), had always been one of King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen)'s most loyal supporters. They had such a strong friendship, that in [237 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), the king gladly agreed to [betroth](wiki:Betrothal) his eldest son and heir, [Duncan Targaryen](wiki:Duncan_Targaryen), [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone), to Lyonel's daughter.(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon's_rebellion.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon's_rebellion.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) However, in [239 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Duncan was traveling in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) when he met and became smitten with a mysterious [peasant woman](wiki:Smallfolk) named [Jenny of Oldstones](wiki:Jenny_of_Oldstones). Setting aside his betrothal, Prince Duncan married Jenny then and there. The king did all he could to have the marriage undone, but the prince stubbornly refused him, even when the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aegon_V)), [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester), and [small council](wiki:Small_council) joined together to insist King Aegon force his son to choose between the Iron Throne and his new wife. Refusing to give up Jenny, Duncan renounced his inheritance in favor of his younger brother [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_II_Targaryen), and abdicated as Prince of Dragonstone.(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon's_rebellion.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon's_rebellion.cite_ref-4)

## Aftermath

To seal the bargain, Princess Rhaelle was sent to Storm's End, to serve as Lord Lyonel's [cupbearer](wiki:Cupbearer) and [companion](wiki:Handmaid) to his lady wife.(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon's_rebellion.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6) Rhaelle married Lord Ormund Baratheon in [245 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon's_rebellion.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8) Jenny of Oldstones, now called Lady Jenny by courtesy, was eventually accepted at the court in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). She and her husband, now known as the Prince of Dragonflies, were beloved of the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk), and a favorite subject of singers for many years.(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon's_rebellion.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon's_rebellion.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)

The match that King Aegon V Targaryen agreed to in order to bring peace, ultimately led to the end of [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) rule in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), when Rhaelle and Ormund's grandson, [Robert](wiki:Robert_Baratheon), [rebelled](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) against Jaehaerys's son, King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), and took the throne as King Robert I Baratheon in [282 AC](wiki:282_AC).(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon's_rebellion.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)
