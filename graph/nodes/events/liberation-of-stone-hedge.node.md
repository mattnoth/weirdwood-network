## Origins

The [burning of Stone Hedge](wiki:Burning_of_Stone_Hedge) by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) and the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) takes place near the start of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Liberation_of_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

With the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps) saving [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) from the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) army besieging it, [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) is proclaimed [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) and [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident). Robb gives the [riverlords](wiki:Riverlands) leave to liberate their own seats and lands from Lannister occupation.(wiki:Liberation_of_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

Lord [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken) leaves Riverrun with the intent to recover and rebuild [Stone Hedge](wiki:Stone_Hedge) from Lannisters. He is successful but is injured in the fight, while his nephew, [Hendry Bracken](wiki:Hendry_Bracken) is slain.(wiki:Liberation_of_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
