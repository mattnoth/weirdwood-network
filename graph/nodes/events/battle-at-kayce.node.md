## Origins

[Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), attacked the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) in [130 AC](wiki:130_AC) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), raiding [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport)(wiki:Battle_at_Kayce.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) and invading [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle).(wiki:Battle_at_Kayce.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Dalton nearly captured [Kayce](wiki:Kayce),(wiki:Battle_at_Kayce.cite_ref-3) and the Red Kraken took the widow of the [Knight of Kayce](wiki:Knight_of_Kayce) as a [salt wife](wiki:Salt_wife).(wiki:Battle_at_Kayce.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Lady [Johanna Lannister](wiki:Johanna_Lannister), one of the [Three Widows](wiki:Three_Widows), led the [westermen](wiki:Westermen)'s resistance to the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) after the death of her husband, Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister), in the [Battle of the Red Fork](wiki:Battle_of_the_Red_Fork). According to Archmaester [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn), Johanna agreed to make peace with the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) during the [Hour of the Wolf](wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf) in [131 AC](wiki:131_AC) out of concern for the defense of Kayce. In return, Johanna asked for the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) to command Dalton to relinquish captured women and return to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands).(wiki:Battle_at_Kayce.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Dalton ignored the [regency of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III), however, and attacked Johanna's shipyards when she attempted to build a new [Lannister fleet](wiki:Lannister_fleet).(wiki:Battle_at_Kayce.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

Since Ser [Tyland Lannister](wiki:Tyland_Lannister), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) and the late Lord Jason's twin, had been unable to aid the westermen against the ironborn, Lady Johanna donned mail and marched with a host from [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) and [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) to protect Kayce. Singers claim that Johanna killed a dozen ironborn beneath the town's walls, though Gyldayn writes that Johanna instead carried a banner and inspired her men to victory. The ironborn raiders were routed in the battle, with Dalton's favorite uncle among the slain, and Kayce was saved.(wiki:Battle_at_Kayce.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

## Aftermath

Victorious at Kayce, Lady Johanna next planned to recover Fair Isle from the ironborn. Her plan was betrayed to the enemy, however, and the ragtag fleet she sent was defeated in an [ambush at Fair Isle](wiki:Ambush_at_Fair_Isle). As vengeance for his slain uncle, Dalton sent the heads of Johanna's three commanders to Casterly Rock.(wiki:Battle_at_Kayce.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
