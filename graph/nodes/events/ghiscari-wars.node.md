## Identity

The Ghiscari Wars were a series of five great conflicts fought between the Old Empire of Ghis and the rising Valyrian Freehold, occurring approximately five thousand years before the events of the main series. The wars were sparked by the Ghiscari desire to acquire dragons after the Valyrians discovered and bonded with them. The Tall Men of the Kingdom of Sarnor participated in three of the five conflicts. Despite winning individual battles — a tapestry in the Great Pyramid of Meereen commemorates a Ghiscari victory in which a defeated Valyrian army passed beneath the yoke in chains — the Ghiscari ultimately lost each of the five wars. The Fifth and final Ghiscari War ended in total Ghiscari defeat: the Valyrians smashed the Ghiscari legions, used their dragons to burn Old Ghis and its pyramids to ash, and sowed the fields with salt, sulfur, and skulls. This destruction ended the Old Empire of Ghis and allowed the Valyrian Freehold to absorb the Ghiscari colonies of Slaver's Bay, adopting slavery as an institution in the process.
