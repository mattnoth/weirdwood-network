## Origins

King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) impregnated Queen [Rhaella Targaryen](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), his ardor raised after burning his [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Lord [Qarlton Chelsted](wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted), for opposing the [wildfire plot](wiki:Wildfire_plot).(wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) After the Mad King learned that Crown Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) had been killed by Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) in the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident), he sent Rhaella with Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)), the ancient island stronghold of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen).(wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Years later, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) remembers Rhaella traveling from [Aegon's High Hill](wiki:Aegon's_High_Hill) to a ship in the morning,(wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) while Viserys tells his sister [Daenerys](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) tales of a midnight flight to Dragonstone.(wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

The royal family who remained in King's Landing, including Aerys and Rhaegar's children, were slain in the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing) in [283 AC](wiki:283_AC). With Rhaella and Viserys at Dragonstone, Robert Baratheon succeeded Aerys on the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) as [Lord of the Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Lord_of_the_Seven_Kingdoms). King Robert tasked his oldest brother, [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), who had successfully withstood the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End) during the rebellion, with building a new fleet for [House Baratheon of King's Landing](wiki:House_Baratheon_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Aerys's widow, Rhaella, died giving birth to Daenerys nine months later at the castle of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), the last stronghold held by the Targaryens. A ferocious storm raged that night and destroyed the Targaryen fleet while it lay at anchor.(wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

Commanding from the decks of the war galley *[Fury](wiki:Fury)*,(wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Stannis set sail with the new [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet) to capture Dragonstone. The garrison of its castle was ready to sell Viserys and the infant Daenerys to the Baratheons. However, just before Stannis sailed, Ser [Willem Darry](wiki:Willem_Darry), the former [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms) of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), led four other loyalists in smuggling Viserys, Daenerys, and a [wet nurse](wiki:Wet_nurse) from Dragonstone under cover of night. They sailed for the [Braavosian Coastland](wiki:Braavosian_Coastland) across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea).(wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Stannis's assault on Dragonstone was successful,(wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) and he conquered the island and its castle in the name of King Robert I Baratheon.(wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Aftermath

With the conquest of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), the [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen) lost their last holding in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) and [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) lived in exile with Ser [Willem Darry](wiki:Willem_Darry) at the [house with the red door](wiki:House_with_the_red_door) in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos),(wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) until Darry's death.(wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) granted Dragonstone to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), making him Lord of Dragonstone. The island castle was traditionally the seat of the crown prince, the [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone), so Robert honored Stannis by granting him the seat of the heir to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)(wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone.cite_ref-8) However, Robert was also furious that the Targaryen children had escaped before Stannis arrived, and he granted the ancestral [Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon) seat of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) to the youngest brother, [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), which Stannis resented as an intentional slight.(wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)(wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
