## Identity

For the murder of Willem Lannister and Tion Frey, Robb Stark condemns Lord Rickard Karstark to death for murder and high treason and beheads him personally before the heart tree in the godswood at Riverrun, against the counsel of those who urge leniency. Following his father's teaching that the man who passes the sentence should swing the sword, Robb takes Rickard's head himself. Rickard's dying words curse Robb as no true king. The execution costs Robb the loyalty and swords of House Karstark — a strategic wound that leaves him desperate for the Frey alliance he will later break.
