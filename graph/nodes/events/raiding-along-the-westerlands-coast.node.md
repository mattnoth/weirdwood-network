## Origins

In order to defeat a host being trained by Ser [Stafford Lannister](wiki:Stafford_Lannister), [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) leaves [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) and marches for the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands). Bypassing the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth), the new [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) [smashes](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross) the new westermen host at [Oxcross](wiki:Oxcross). Following his victory Robb divides his force to launch further attacks upon the westerlands.(wiki:Raiding_along_the_westerlands_coast.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Raiding_along_the_westerlands_coast.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Raiding_along_the_westerlands_coast.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Aftermath

Returning to Riverrun after the [storming of the Crag](wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag), Robb tells Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) he remained in the westerlands after Oxcross in hopes of drawing Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) back to his homeland.(wiki:Raiding_along_the_westerlands_coast.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
