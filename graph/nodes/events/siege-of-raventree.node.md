## Origins

After the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), most of the river lords, such as Lord [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken), choose to submit to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). However, Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood) resists for honor's sake. Jonos is persuaded by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) to attack Tytos's seat, [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall), in return for lands historically claimed by [Stone Hedge](wiki:Stone_Hedge).(wiki:Siege_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Siege_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Siege_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

The rivalry between the Blackwoods and Brackens goes back thousands of years so neither side asks or offers quarter. While there are skirmishes at the start of the siege, it develops into a stalemate. Jonos lacks siege towers, battering rams, and catapults, preferring instead to starve out Tytos.(wiki:Siege_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Young [Robert Blackwood](wiki:Robert_Blackwood) dies from a looseness of the bowels.(wiki:Siege_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

After resolving the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) is asked by Lord [Karyl Vance](wiki:Karyl_Vance) to assist in the resolution of the siege of Raventree, as Tytos will never yield to Jonos. Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister) advises Jaime to bring the siege equipment at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) to Raventree, but Jaime is confident he can resolve the conflict personally and has the equipment put to the torch.(wiki:Siege_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Aftermath

The surrender of Raventree represents the last remnant of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s brief [river kingdom](wiki:King_of_the_Trident), as the entire nobility of the riverlands have now submitted to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). After resolving the siege, Jaime travels to [Pennytree](wiki:Pennytree).(wiki:Siege_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)
