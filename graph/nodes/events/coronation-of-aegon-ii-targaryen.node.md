## Origins

King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) chose Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), his daughter by his first queen, [Aemma Arryn](wiki:Aemma_Arryn), as his preferred heir over Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), the king's eldest son by his second queen, [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower).(wiki:Coronation_of_Aegon_II_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) After Viserys died in his sleep within the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) in [129 AC](wiki:129_AC), Alicent concealed the king's death from the public and gathered support in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for her son, Aegon the Elder, against the claim of Rhaenyra, who was pregnant at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). After the death of Lord [Lyman Beesbury](wiki:Lyman_Beesbury), the [small council](wiki:Small_council) began making plans for Aegon's coronation. According to Septon [Eustace](wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)), Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole), [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard), convinced a reluctant Prince Aegon to accept the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) because his mother, brothers, sister and children would otherwise be killed by Rhaenyra.(wiki:Coronation_of_Aegon_II_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Aegon chose to don [the crown](wiki:Crown) of King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) for his reign. Alicent, the dowager queen, ordered that [Viserys's crown](wiki:Crown) be locked away, but the steward carrying it disappeared instead. Ser [Steffon Darklyn](wiki:Steffon_Darklyn) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), choosing Rhaenyra over Aegon, fled to Dragonstone to give Rhaenyra the crown of her father.(wiki:Coronation_of_Aegon_II_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Aftermath
