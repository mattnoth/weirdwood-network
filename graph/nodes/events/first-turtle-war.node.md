## Origins

The First Turtle War was a bloody, but short war, lasting less than a moon's turn.(wiki:First_Turtle_War.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

The war was fought between Volon Therys and Sar Mell. According to legend, the war began when the [Valyrians](wiki:Valyria) killed a gigantic [turtle](wiki:Turtle), one of those called the [Old Men of the River](wiki:Old_Men_of_the_River) by the Rhoynar. The Rhoynar hold these turtles sacred, viewing them as the consorts of [Mother Rhoyne](wiki:Mother_Rhoyne) herself.(wiki:First_Turtle_War.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

During the course of the war, Sar Mell was raided and burned. They emerged victorious when Rhoynish [water wizards](wiki:Water_wizard) called up the power of the river, and proceeded to flood Volon Therys. If the tales can be believed, half the city was washed away.(wiki:First_Turtle_War.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
