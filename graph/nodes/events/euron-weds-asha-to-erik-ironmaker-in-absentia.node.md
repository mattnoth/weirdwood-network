## Identity

After [Asha Greyjoy](asha-greyjoy) loses her claim at the [kingsmoot](kingsmoot-on-old-wyk) and flees Old Wyk, [Euron Greyjoy](euron-greyjoy) — now king — weds her **in absentia** to [Erik Ironmaker](erik-ironmaker), the aged "Anvil-Breaker," using a seal to stand in for the bride at the ceremony, and names Erik to rule the Iron Islands while Euron is away chasing dragons. The marriage is a single calculated stroke: it turns a rival (Erik, a kingsmoot candidate) into a supporter, secures the isles in Euron's absence, and removes Asha as a threat by binding her to an old man loyal to the Crow's Eye. Asha learns of it by raven while at Ten Towers; she is, in her own words, "a woman wed … wedded and bedded, though not by the same man." This terminal beat closes Asha's AFFC sub-arc and opens her ADWD exile at Deepwood Motte. The terminus is the consequence of [Asha's failed claim](asha-claims-the-kingsmoot); its downstream (her ADWD arc) is left dark for a later Essos/ADWD pass.
