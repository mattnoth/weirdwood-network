## Narrative Arc

[Aeron Damphair](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) awakens in a grim dungeon and is forced to drink [shade of the evening](wiki:Shade_of_the_evening) by his brother, [Euron](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy). Aeron dreams of his dead brother [Urri](wiki:Urrigon_Greyjoy), of Euron on a throne of skulls, and of [gods](wiki:Religion) impaled on the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).

Aeron remembers that after the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot) he intended to raise the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands), but he was quickly abducted by Euron's mutes. After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), he was held in the dungeon of [Oakenshield Castle](wiki:Oakenshield_Castle) and visited by [Falia Flowers](wiki:Falia_Flowers), the bastard daughter of Lord [Humfrey Hewett](wiki:Humfrey_Hewett). Despite Aeron's warning, Falia insisted she will become Euron's [salt wife](wiki:Salt_wife) and a sister to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), who is to be Euron's [rock wife](wiki:Rock_wife). While sailing on the *[Silence](wiki:Silence)* to the current dungeon, Euron admits to having caused the deaths of [Harlon](wiki:Harlon_Greyjoy), [Robin](wiki:Robin_Greyjoy), and King [Balon](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy).

At the second dungeon, Aeron is joined by mutilated priests, including [septons](wiki:Septons), a burned [red priest](wiki:Red_priest) who quickly dies, and [warlocks](wiki:Warlocks), one of whom repeats the word "[Pree](wiki:Pyat_Pree)". In another vision, Aeron sees Euron as a [kraken](wiki:Kraken)-like figure on the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), accompanied by a shadow in a woman's form. Aeron and the other priests are eventually brought from the second dungeon to the stone hall above. It is filled with hanging corpses, men who lived on an isle near the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor).

Euron's men prepare for the arrivals of the [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet) and ships of [House Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower). On the deck of the *Silence*, Aeron sees Euron armored in [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) and believes Euron's claim at the kingsmoot to have visited [Valyria](wiki:Valyria). Euron orders the priests to be bound to the prows of [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) longships, with Aeron on the *Silence*. Once at sea, Aeron is joined by Falia, now pregnant and without tongue. Aeron tastes salt on his lips.
