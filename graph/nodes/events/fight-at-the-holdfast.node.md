## Origins

As the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) begins, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) sends Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane), [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat), and Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch) to raid the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), each with three hundred horse.(wiki:Fight_at_the_holdfast.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Amory's men then hunt [the outlaws](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) of Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion).(wiki:Fight_at_the_holdfast.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

[Yoren](wiki:Yoren), a [wandering crow](wiki:Wandering_crow) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), leads a group of recruits from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) toward the [Wall](wiki:Wall). After his party is stopped by [gold cloaks](wiki:Gold_cloaks) in search of [Gendry](wiki:Gendry), Yoren decides to avoid the busy [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad).(wiki:Fight_at_the_holdfast.cite_ref-Racok9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The recruits rest at an [abandoned town](wiki:Gods_Eye_town) on the southern shore of the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye).(wiki:Fight_at_the_holdfast.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

## Narrative Arc

While the recruits are sheltering in the unnamed town's holdfast, Amory's raiders arrive during the night and begin burning the town's houses. Suspicious that the holdfast is protecting Beric's outlaws, Amory commands that the gates be opened in the name of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). Yoren refuses, however, as the Night's Watch is sworn to take no part in the wars of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Fight_at_the_holdfast.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

Amory orders his men to storm the walls, and [Woth](wiki:Woth) is quickly killed by a thrown spear. [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), wielding [Needle](wiki:Needle) and disguised as "Arry", and her friend [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie) hack and slash at [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) soldiers who climb the parapets. [Koss](wiki:Koss) shoots arrows at four men attacking the gate, [Lommy](wiki:Lommy) smashes the head of a soldier who stabs [Dobber](wiki:Dobber), and a [knight](wiki:Knight) kills [Qyle](wiki:Qyle) with a mace. Arya shouts "[Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell)!" and Hot Pie screams "Hot Pie!" while attacking a soldier who has gained the wall, and Gendry claims the soldier's sword after his own breaks.(wiki:Fight_at_the_holdfast.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

As the [westermen](wiki:Westermen) advance into the holdfast, Yoren orders his recruits to retreat. Koss is killed while attempting to yield. Gendry rescues [Weasel](wiki:Weasel) as the youths head toward a tunnel hidden within a barn. Since the barn has been set on fire, Arya decides to save [Jaqen H'ghar](wiki:Jaqen_H'ghar), [Rorge](wiki:Rorge), and [Biter](wiki:Biter), dangerous recruits kept within a wagon in the barn. After striking an attacking soldier between his legs with an axe, Arya throws the weapon into the wagon, allowing Rorge to break through the bottom of the prison. Arya and her friends escape into the tunnel.(wiki:Fight_at_the_holdfast.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

[Kurz](wiki:Kurz), [Tarber](wiki:Tarber), and [Cutjack](wiki:Cutjack) defend the lord's towerhouse(wiki:Fight_at_the_holdfast.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6) and pull up the ladder to its second-story door.(wiki:Fight_at_the_holdfast.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7) The Lannisters attempt to burn the tower, but they give up since the stone does not burn.(wiki:Fight_at_the_holdfast.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Aftermath

Arya and her companions return to the town the next night, finding it burned and abandoned. Corpses of Night's Watch recruits and Lannister [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms) lie unburied. Arya finds that Yoren was killed by an axe blow to the head, with the corpses of four men-at-arms lying near his body. Arya insists that at least Yoren be buried.(wiki:Fight_at_the_holdfast.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) The three men who defended the towerhouse join the youths as they depart the town. After Kurz dies from his wounds, however, Tarber and Cutjack loot his possessions and then abandon the orphans.(wiki:Fight_at_the_holdfast.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Arya's party is eventually captured by the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) and taken to [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Fight_at_the_holdfast.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) When Amory arrives at the castle, Arya discovers that Rorge, Biter, and Jaqen have joined his column of raiders.(wiki:Fight_at_the_holdfast.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
