## Origins

King [Robar II Royce](wiki:Robar_II_Royce) won the alliance of Lord [Hunter](wiki:House_Hunter) by outshooting him in an archery contest. Legend claims that he cheated, however.(wiki:Martial_games.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Princess [Saera Targaryen](wiki:Saera_Targaryen), and her friends [Perianne Moore](wiki:Perianne_Moore) and [Alys Turnberry](wiki:Alys_Turnberry), cheered her male favorites—[Jonah Mooton](wiki:Jonah_Mooton), [Roy Connington](wiki:Roy_Connington), and [Braxton Beesbury](wiki:Braxton_Beesbury)—when they rode at rings or crossed swords in the yards.(wiki:Martial_games.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Princess [Daena Targaryen](wiki:Daena_Targaryen) was an expert horsewoman. She practiced at riding at rings, but was never allowed to ride in a [tourney](wiki:Tourney).(wiki:Martial_games.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
In the great [mêlée at Last Hearth](wiki:Mêlée_at_Last_Hearth), held by [House Umber](wiki:House_Umber) at [Last Hearth](wiki:Last_Hearth) in [170 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), at least eighteen participants were killed, and at least twenty-seven men were maimed during the contest.(wiki:Martial_games.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
In [200 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Lord [Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon) held a hastilude at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) to celebrate the birth of a grandson. In the joust, Prince [Baelor Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_Targaryen) defeated Ser [Arlan of Pennytree](wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree).(wiki:Martial_games.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
Ser Arlan of Pennytree taught [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) how to tilt at rings and the quintain.(wiki:Martial_games.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
When Duncan the Tall first arrived at [Coldmoat](wiki:Coldmoat), he saw Lady [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber) and a squire practicing archery, and knights taking turn at a quintain.(wiki:Martial_games.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
The [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) of [281 AC](wiki:281_AC) was the greatest tourney of its time, with five days of jousting, a great seven-sided mêlée, archery and axe-throwing competitions, a horse race, and a tourney of singers.(wiki:Martial_games.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
[Harwin](wiki:Harwin) rode at quintain with [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) and [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Martial_games.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
[Edric Dayne](wiki:Edric_Dayne) once won a prize riding at rings.(wiki:Martial_games.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

The [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) consists of a joust, melee, and archery contest.(wiki:Martial_games.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

At the [tourney on King Joffrey's name day](wiki:Tourney_on_King_Joffrey's_name_day), Prince [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) strikes a quintain so hard that it spins around and knocks him off his pony.(wiki:Martial_games.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) watches squires training in the yard of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), and notices [Big Walder](wiki:Big_Walder) and [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Little_Walder_Frey) riding at the quintains.(wiki:Martial_games.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Jon Snow, [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), institutes a daily archery drill for the entire garrison of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Martial_games.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
After the loss of his right hand, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) realizes his days of jousting and riding at quintains are over.(wiki:Martial_games.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) disapproves of Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) training King Tommen at riding at the quintain.(wiki:Martial_games.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

For the purpose of gaining the favor of the [Meereenese](wiki:Meereenese) people without re-opening the [fighting pits](wiki:Fighting_pits), Ser [Barristan](wiki:Barristan) proposes to Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) to hold a [tourney](wiki:Tourney), where his orphan squires could ride at rings and fight a mêlée with blunted [weapons](wiki:Armament).(wiki:Martial_games.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
