## Origins

Prior to Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully)'s marriage to [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey), [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), the [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident), sends Lord [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister) back to his seat of [Seagard](wiki:Seagard). Jason is tasked with using [Mallister](wiki:House_Mallister) longships to take [Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover) and [Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont) into the [Neck](wiki:Neck).(wiki:Siege_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

[House Frey](wiki:House_Frey) betrays Robb in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), however, and Jason's son, [Patrek](wiki:Patrek_Mallister), is taken captive at the [Twins](wiki:Twins).

## Narrative Arc

While the Freys are now supporters of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), the Mallisters remain loyal to [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark). ["Black" Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Ryman)) commands a Frey siege of Seagard.(wiki:Siege_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Although Jason resists at first, he yields the castle when Black Walder threatens to hang Patrek before the walls of Seagard.(wiki:Siege_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Aftermath

House Mallister submits to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne),(wiki:Siege_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) but Lord Jason and Patrek are held captive by the Freys within Seagard.(wiki:Siege_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
