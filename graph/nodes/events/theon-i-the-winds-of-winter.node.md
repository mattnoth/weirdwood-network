## Narrative Arc

Though reportedly isolated and secluded at the [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village), [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) is actively preparing for the looming battle against the [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton). Stannis received the [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) [banker](wiki:Iron_Bank_of_Braavos) [Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris) and the two signed a contract, Stannis is planning on sending the banker back to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) so he does not get caught up in the fighting.

Stannis is made aware of [Arnolf Karstark](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark)'s planned treachery due to the message [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) gave the banker. Stannis has Arnolf, his son [Arthor](wiki:Arthor_Karstark) and three grandsons arrested and plans to execute them, though whether their death will be quick beheadings or by fire depends on their willingness to confess. Stannis is preparing his position to battle the coming vanguard of [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton)'s army led by [Hosteen Frey](wiki:Hosteen_Frey). Stannis is sending Ser [Justin Massey](wiki:Justin_Massey) to escort "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)" (actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole), though this is unknown to Stannis) back to the Wall to reunite the girl with her brother [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) in gratitude for Snow warning him to amass the [northern mountain clans](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans) rather than march straight into Arnolf's trap.

Stannis also orders Massey to go with Tycho Nestoris to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), where Massey will use the money given to him by the [Iron Bank of Braavos](wiki:Iron_Bank_of_Braavos) to hire sellsword companies until he has a force no less than twenty thousand strong, and then sail back to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). Stannis also gives orders that if he is slain in the coming battle, Massey is still to do as instructed, with the intention of using the army to place [Shireen](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon) on the Iron Throne.

Stannis also plans to have [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) executed, hoping to gain favor with his northern allies by exacting justice for the murders of [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark). [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) tries to convince Stannis to execute Theon before a [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree).(wiki:Theon_I_(The_Winds_of_Winter).cite_ref-1)
