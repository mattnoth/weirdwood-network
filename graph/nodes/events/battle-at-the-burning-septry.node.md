## Origins

A detachment of [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions), a [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) company pejoratively called the Bloody Mummers, occupy a settlement in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) containing [a septry](wiki:Burning_septry), mill, brewhouse, and stables. The septry by a small river had previously been looted by other combatants, including [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) troops.(wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

The lightning lord, [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion), leads the [outlaw](wiki:Outlaw) [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) in reprisals against those who harm [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk).

## Narrative Arc

Guarded by [Beardless Dick](wiki:Beardless_Dick) and [Mudge](wiki:Mudge_(brotherhood)), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) and [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) observe the battle from a forested ridge overlooking the septry.

The archers [Anguy](wiki:Anguy), [Kyle](wiki:Kyle_(brotherhood)), and [Notch](wiki:Notch) begin the fighting by slaying three Mummer guards shortly before dawn. The Brave Companions inside the septry are multinational, including a [Myrish](wiki:Myr) crossbowman, two [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibben), [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki), and [Volantenes](wiki:Volantis), among others. When the septry is set ablaze by fire arrows, the Mummers charge forth but are met by [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion)'s brotherhood of outlaws. There is also fighting in the stables. Although Kyle is killed, he is quickly avenged by [Lem](wiki:Lem). [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr) and Beric both wield flaming swords against the Mummers, and the young Lord [Edric Dayne](wiki:Edric_Dayne) fights by Beric's side. The Brave Companions surrender after a short battle.(wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Aftermath

[Jack-Be-Lucky](wiki:Jack-Be-Lucky), [Harwin](wiki:Harwin) and [Merrit o' Moontown](wiki:Merrit_o'_Moontown) rescue eight captive brown brothers from the burning septry before it collapses. The surviving Mummers are taken prisoner, with the exception of two Dothraki whom Beric allows to escape. [Utt](wiki:Utt), the disgraced septon of the Brave Companions, is found hiding beneath the cellar steps. Despite their protests and excuses, Utt and his fellow sellswords are hanged by Beric's outlaws.(wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

The rescued brown brothers host Beric's outlaws at the nearby brewhouse that evening, since the septry has been destroyed.(wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
