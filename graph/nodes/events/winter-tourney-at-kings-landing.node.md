## Origins

The sixteen-year-old [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) participated in the winter tourney as a [mystery knight](wiki:Mystery_knight). He defeated both Prince [Duncan Targaryen](wiki:Duncan_Targaryen) and Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall), [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard). King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) rewarded Barristan for his feats of prowess by granting him [knighthood](wiki:Knighthood).(wiki:Winter_tourney_at_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) At the feast that night, ribs of wild [boar](wiki:Boar) were served, prepared the [Dornish](wiki:Dornish) way with [dragon peppers](wiki:Dragon_pepper).(wiki:Winter_tourney_at_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) reads Barristan Selmy's entry in the [White Book](wiki:White_Book) of the Kingsguard, which includes his accomplishments at the winter tourney.(wiki:Winter_tourney_at_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser Barristan Selmy recalls how King Aegon's [sword](wiki:Sword) felt on his shoulders when he was dubbed, and what he ate that night at the feast. He thinks he still recalls its taste far better than what he had eaten a few days ago.(wiki:Winter_tourney_at_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
