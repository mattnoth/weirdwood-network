# Sam kills the Other

During the retreat from the [Fight at the Fist](fight-at-the-fist), [Samwell Tarly](samwell-tarly), left
straggling in the snow, is set upon by an [Other](others). Weeping and half-blind with terror, Sam stabs
it through the throat with the **obsidian (dragonglass) dagger** [Jon Snow](jon-snow) had given him from
the cache buried beneath the Fist. The Other's armor and flesh dissolve into a fine white mist, leaving
bones like milkglass. It is the first Other slain by a man in living memory — and the pivotal pay-off of
the dragonglass Chekhov's gun. Sam's brothers mockingly dub him "Sam the Slayer." The kill is the textual
proof that [dragonglass](dragonglass) destroys the Others, intelligence Sam carries to Lord Commander
[Jeor Mormont](jeor-mormont).
