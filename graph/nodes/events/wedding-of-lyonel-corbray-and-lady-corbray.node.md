## Identity

Wedding of Lyonel Corbray and Lady Corbray is a battle/event from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges


## Origins

Lord [Lyonel Corbray](wiki:Lyonel_Corbray)'s wife bore a sickly child who died in infancy, so Lyonel's heir is his brother, Ser [Lyn](wiki:Lyn_Corbray).(wiki:Wedding_of_Lyonel_Corbray_and_Lady_Corbray.cite_ref-TWOW_3-0)
After Lyonel's wife finally dies,(wiki:Wedding_of_Lyonel_Corbray_and_Lady_Corbray.cite_ref-TWOW_3-1) [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), [Lord Protector of the Vale](wiki:Lord_Protector_of_the_Vale), arranges for Lyonel to marry the [sixteen-year-old daughter](wiki:Lady_Corbray) of a merchant from [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown) in [300 AC](wiki:300_AC).(wiki:Wedding_of_Lyonel_Corbray_and_Lady_Corbray.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Since the bride is of [common birth](wiki:Smallfolk), a staggering dowry is provided by her father.(wiki:Wedding_of_Lyonel_Corbray_and_Lady_Corbray.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Aftermath

Lyonel is reported to be pleased with his new bride.(wiki:Wedding_of_Lyonel_Corbray_and_Lady_Corbray.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7) Lord Corbray remains with his pregnant wife at [Heart's Home](wiki:Heart's_Home) instead of traveling to the Gates of the Moon to attend the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) for the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights).(wiki:Wedding_of_Lyonel_Corbray_and_Lady_Corbray.cite_ref-TWOW_3-2) Lyonel's landless brother, Ser Lyn, is resentful.(wiki:Wedding_of_Lyonel_Corbray_and_Lady_Corbray.cite_ref-TWOW_3-3)
