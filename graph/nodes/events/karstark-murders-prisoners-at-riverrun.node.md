## Identity

Enraged by Catelyn's release of Jaime Lannister — whom he blames for the deaths of his sons Torrhen and Eddard at the Whispering Wood — Lord Rickard Karstark leads a group of his men into the dungeons of Riverrun and murders two of Robb's highborn captives, Willem Lannister and Tion Frey, asleep and unarmed in their cells. Rickard frames the killing as blood-for-blood vengeance and open defiance of his king. The murders force Robb's hand: he condemns Rickard for murder and treason, and the rupture costs Robb the Karstark bannermen at the worst possible moment.
