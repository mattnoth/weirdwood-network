## Origins

After [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) takes prisoner [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) at the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn), Tyrion's father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), retaliates by sending Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) to raid the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). Without displaying their banners, the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) attack the [Mummer's Ford](wiki:Mummer's_Ford), [Sherrer](wiki:Sherrer), and [Wendish Town](wiki:Wendish_Town).(wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), Catelyn's father, responds by sending Ser [Marq Piper](wiki:Marq_Piper), Ser [Karyl Vance](wiki:Karyl_Vance), and Ser [Raymun Darry](wiki:Raymun_Darry) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to demand justice for the towns that have been pillaged.(wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Tywin hopes that Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), will personally lead the force which will respond to Gregor, and an ambush is planned so that Tywin can capture Eddard as a hostage to trade for Tyrion.(wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

However, Eddard's leg is broken by an altercation with Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) in the streets of King's Landing and he cannot lead himself. Lord Stark passes over the impetuous Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell), instead granting command to [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) and tasking the Lord of [Blackhaven](wiki:Blackhaven) with killing Gregor in the name of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). Beric, [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr), Ser [Gladden Wylde](wiki:Gladden_Wylde), and Lord [Lothar Mallery](wiki:Lothar_Mallery) are each to raise twenty men-at-arms, and Eddard provides twenty [House Stark guards](wiki:House_Stark_guards) (including [Alyn](wiki:Alyn) and [Harwin](wiki:Harwin)) to fight alongside them.(wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Raymun Darry joins the royal party as well.(wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Narrative Arc

The force under Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) is intercepted at the [Mummer's Ford](wiki:Mummer's_Ford) by forces from [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister). They make a fighting retreat only to be taken in the rear by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) and the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men). Gregor kills Ser [Raymun Darry](wiki:Raymun_Darry) and his horse with a single blow, and Lord [Lothar Mallery](wiki:Lothar_Mallery) drowns in the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork). Ser [Gladden Wylde](wiki:Gladden_Wylde) is killed, as are most of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s men.(wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Beric is mortally wounded by the Mountain's lance, but his young squire [Edric Dayne](wiki:Edric_Dayne), [Lord of Starfall](wiki:Lord_of_Starfall), pulls him out of the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) and stands over Beric while the battle rages around them.(wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) [Alyn of Winterfell](wiki:Alyn) rallies the survivors into a retreat. Thoros reflexively says the words of [his faith](wiki:R'hllor) and administers the [last kiss](wiki:Last_kiss) to Beric after he passes, but the young lord unexpectedly comes back to life.(wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Aftermath

At [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin), [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) tells his mother, [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), that the Lannisters have won victories at the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Battle_in_the_hills_below_the_Golden_Tooth) and the [Mummer's Ford](wiki:Mummer's_Ford), and that Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) has closed off the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad) and is marching north towards [Harrenhal](wiki:Yielding_of_Harrenhal).(wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

The survivors of Beric's company form the core of the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners). Later at the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn), after hearing of the [Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood) and the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), Tywin tells [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) that [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr) and Beric continue to plague their foraging parties.(wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
