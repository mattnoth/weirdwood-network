Wedding of Petyr Baelish and Lysa Arryn is a battle/event from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges


## Origins

Wounded after dueling [Brandon Stark](wiki:Brandon_Stark) for the hand of [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) impregnated Catelyn's younger sister [Lysa](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) while she helped him to recover. Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully) forced Lysa to take [tansy tea](wiki:Tansy_tea) to abort the child,(wiki:Wedding_of_Petyr_Baelish_and_Lysa_Arryn.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and he arranged for her to [wed](wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully) Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Wedding_of_Petyr_Baelish_and_Lysa_Arryn.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Under Lysa's recommendations, Jon first gave customs appointments to Petyr at the port of [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown)(wiki:Wedding_of_Petyr_Baelish_and_Lysa_Arryn.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and later at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Wedding_of_Petyr_Baelish_and_Lysa_Arryn.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) According to a semi-canon source, Petyr and Lysa began a secret affair.(wiki:Wedding_of_Petyr_Baelish_and_Lysa_Arryn.cite_ref-Rawoiafpetyr_baelish.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Wedding_of_Petyr_Baelish_and_Lysa_Arryn.cite_ref-Rawoiaflysa_arryn.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
When Lysa learns that Jon planned to send their son, [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn), away to be fostered at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) with Lord [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), Petyr provides her with [poison](wiki:Tears_of_Lys) she uses to murder her husband.(wiki:Wedding_of_Petyr_Baelish_and_Lysa_Arryn.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) The widowed Lysa is pursued by many suitors at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie).(wiki:Wedding_of_Petyr_Baelish_and_Lysa_Arryn.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Wedding_of_Petyr_Baelish_and_Lysa_Arryn.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
During the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Petyr, now [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin) and [Lord of Harrenhal](wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal), offers to marry Lysa and thereby bring the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) to the side of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Wedding_of_Petyr_Baelish_and_Lysa_Arryn.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Petyr and Lady [Olenna Tyrell](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell) conspire to have King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) choke to death at his [royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding).(wiki:Wedding_of_Petyr_Baelish_and_Lysa_Arryn.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Petyr also arranges for [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), daughter of his childhood infatuation Catelyn, to escape the [crownlands](wiki:Crownlands) with him.(wiki:Wedding_of_Petyr_Baelish_and_Lysa_Arryn.cite_ref-Rasos61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Petyr and Sansa sail on the [Merling King](wiki:Merling_King) to the [Fingers](wiki:Fingers), where they wait for Lysa's arrival at Petyr's [unnamed tower](wiki:Drearfort). To protect Sansa's identity, Petyr claims her to be his [bastard](wiki:Bastard) daughter, "Alayne Stone".(wiki:Wedding_of_Petyr_Baelish_and_Lysa_Arryn.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Aftermath

The travelers visit the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) during the journey to the Eyrie,(wiki:Wedding_of_Petyr_Baelish_and_Lysa_Arryn.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and Sansa meets Lord [Nestor Royce](wiki:Nestor_Royce).(wiki:Wedding_of_Petyr_Baelish_and_Lysa_Arryn.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) The [bannermen](wiki:Bannermen) of the Vale resent Lysa's marriage and that she has named Petyr as [Lord Protector of the Vale](wiki:Lord_Protector_of_the_Vale).(wiki:Wedding_of_Petyr_Baelish_and_Lysa_Arryn.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
While helping Sansa build a snow castle of her family's ancestral home, [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), Petyr kisses the girl in the [godswood of the Eyrie](wiki:Godswood_of_the_Eyrie). A jealous Lysa accuses Sansa of initiating the encounter, however, and threatens her niece by the [Moon Door](wiki:Moon_Door). Petyr temporarily soothes Lysa, assuring her that he has only ever loved one woman. He then adds "only Cat" and shoves Lysa out the Moon Door to her death. The killing is then blamed on the singer Marillion.(wiki:Wedding_of_Petyr_Baelish_and_Lysa_Arryn.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
