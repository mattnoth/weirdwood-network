## Origins

### Background[ ]

The tourney was announced in [280 AC](wiki:280_AC) by Lord [Walter Whent](wiki:Walter_Whent) shortly after he was visited by his brother, Ser [Oswell Whent](wiki:Oswell_Whent) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). The rewards offered by Walter were three times greater than those offered by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) in the [tourney in honor of Prince Viserys's birth](wiki:Tournament_in_honor_of_Viserys's_birth) at [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) in [276 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-6) The lavish prizes offered by Lord Whent brought hundreds of challengers to the tourney.(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Walter also wanted to present his massive castle and his fine sons.(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
While most believed [House Whent](wiki:House_Whent) was displaying their wealth and splendor, some believed the Whents lacked the funding for the prizes and were instead supported by a "shadow host"; Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel) suggests [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), the [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone).(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) attended the tournament only because Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) warned him that Rhaegar, his son and the crown prince, arranged the tournament as a pretext to meet with several high lords to discuss removing his father as king in an informal [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council).(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Many in attendance were shocked by the king's physical decline.(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) Lord Tywin was not at Harrenhal because of a quarrel with King Aerys, although many [westermen](wiki:Westerlands) attended.(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### Festivities[ ]

During the tourney's opening ceremonies, fifteen-year-old Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) was admitted into the order of the Kingsguard. King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) is said to have believed the cheering of the crowd was for himself instead of Jaime,(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) and a young [Pia](wiki:Pia) was impressed with the handsome knight.(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Although Jaime wanted to compete in the tourney the next day, Aerys commanded him to return to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) that night to guard Queen [Rhaella](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen) and Prince [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen).(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Held that first night within the Hall of a Hundred Hearths(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) was a great feast for lords from throughout the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) Besides [Starks](wiki:House_Stark) and [Howland Reed](wiki:Howland_Reed), other [northmen](wiki:North) in attendance were [Dustins](wiki:House_Dustin), [Hornwoods](wiki:House_Hornwood), [Manderlys](wiki:House_Manderly), and [Mormonts](wiki:House_Mormont).(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2) Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) drank [wine](wiki:Wine) with Ser [Richard Lonmouth](wiki:Richard_Lonmouth), and a [wandering crow](wiki:Wandering_crow) sought recruits for the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-3) [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark) teased his sister, [Lyanna](wiki:Lyanna_Stark), for crying at the singing of Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), so Lyanna poured wine over her younger brother's head.(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-4) Lady [Ashara Dayne](wiki:Ashara_Dayne) danced with a knight of the Kingsguard, Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell), and Lord [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington). [Brandon Stark](wiki:Brandon_Stark) convinced Ashara to dance with his shy brother, [Eddard](wiki:Eddard_Stark).(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-5) According to Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), Ashara was dishonored by a man at Harrenhal.(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

### Tourney[ ]

The tourney took place over ten days,(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-tourneyrules_5-1) of which the competition lasted seven days,(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6) with five days allotted to the joust.(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-6) There was a seven-sided melee in the ancient style,(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-tourneyrules_5-2)(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-7) in which Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) unhorsed many.(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Besides the melee and the joust, the tourney included an archery contest, an axe-throwing contest, a horse race, and a tourney of singers.(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-8) There was also a [mummer](wiki:Mummer) show.(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
Lord [Walter Whent](wiki:Walter_Whent)'s fair daughter was the [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty) when the tourney began. Her honor was defended by her brothers and her uncle, Ser [Oswell Whent](wiki:Oswell_Whent) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), but they were all defeated in the tourney.(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-9)
A [mystery knight](wiki:Mystery_knight), the "[Knight of the Laughing Tree](wiki:Knight_of_the_Laughing_Tree)", appeared in the lists to fight for the honor of [a crannogman](wiki:Howland_Reed).(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-10) King [Aerys](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) sent men, including Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), to learn the identity of the mystery knight, who appeared and disappeared mid tourney, but they were unsuccessful.(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)
Prince Rhaegar was champion of the joust, defeating four knights of the Kingsguard,(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8) and in the end he chose [Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark) as the new queen of love and beauty, placing a crown of [blue winter roses](wiki:Blue_winter_rose) in her lap. This was scandalous in that, not only was Rhaegar already married to [Elia of Dorne](wiki:Elia_Martell), who was present at the tourney, but Lyanna herself was betrothed to Robert Baratheon.(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-11)

### Aftermath[ ]

When winter returned at the coming of the new year, [282 AC](wiki:282_AC),(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-10) mere days before the planned wedding of [Brandon Stark](wiki:Brandon_Stark) to [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun),(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) and six confidants abducted Brandon's sister, [Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark), less than ten leagues from [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-11) This led to [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), also called the War of the Usurper, and the end of [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) rule over the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Imprisoned in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep)'s [black cells](wiki:Black_cells), [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) vividly remembers the tourney at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), which took place when he was eighteen.(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-19)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

With his sword hand having been removed by the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions), Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) is brought as a captive to Harrenhal. He observes that many men have recently encamped outside of the castle, beside the lakeshore, where Lord [Walter Whent](wiki:Walter_Whent) had staged his greatest tourney in the [year of the false spring](wiki:Year_of_the_false_spring). Jaime notices someone dug a privy trench where he had once knelt before King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) to say his [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) vows.(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
Encamped outside of [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai), [Arstan Whitebeard](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) tells Rhaegar's sister, [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), about the tourney staged by Lord Whent at Harrenhal beside the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye).(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) [Meera Reed](wiki:Meera_Reed) tells [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) the story of the [Knight of the Laughing Tree](wiki:Knight_of_the_Laughing_Tree).(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-27)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

In [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), alone and melancholic on [the terrace of the Great Pyramid](wiki:Queen's_apartments_(Great_Pyramid)), Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) remembers Old Lord Whent's tourney at Harrenhal and finds the memory of it still bitter.(wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)
