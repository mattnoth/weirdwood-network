## Origins

After being defeated in the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork), Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) withdraws his forces to the [causeway](wiki:Causeway).(wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) When the victorious Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) camps at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Roose takes the [ruby ford](wiki:Ruby_ford) and [crossroads](wiki:Crossroads).(wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

The incognito [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) travels north with a group of [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) recruits led by [Yoren](wiki:Yoren). Three recruits — [Jaqen H'ghar](wiki:Jaqen_H'ghar), [Rorge](wiki:Rorge), and [Biter](wiki:Biter) — were taken from the [black cells](wiki:Black_cells) of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) and are traveling fettered in a cage.(wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Racok5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [The company is attacked](wiki:Fight_at_the_holdfast) by raiders under the command of Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch), a [knight](wiki:Knight) in Tywin's service. Yoren is slain, but Arya helps the three prisoners escape a fire before escaping herself with her friends.(wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Arya and her companions are eventually captured by the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) and taken to Harrenhal, where they are made to work as servants.(wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Amory arrives at the castle, and Arya discovers that Rorge, Biter, and Jaqen have joined his column of raiders.(wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

Roose Bolton's liege, King [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), attacks the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands), drawing Tywin out of Harrenhal. Tywin names Ser Amory Lorch as [castellan](wiki:Castellan) and marches out, leaving as a garrison only two hundred men: one hundred [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms) and a hundred [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions), an unsavory [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) company from [Essos](wiki:Essos) under the command of [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat).(wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

## Narrative Arc

Vargo returns to Harrenhal with at least one hundred captives—led by [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover) and [Aenys Frey](wiki:Aenys_Frey), and including men from Houses [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton), [Cerwyn](wiki:House_Cerwyn), [Frey](wiki:House_Frey), [Glover](wiki:House_Glover), and [Karstark](wiki:House_Karstark)—claiming to have beaten Roose when he tried to cross the [Trident](wiki:Trident). [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), incognito as a servant in Harrenhal called "Weasel", sees an opportunity and blackmails [Jaqen H'ghar](wiki:Jaqen_H'ghar) into helping her free the [northern](wiki:North) prisoners in the [Widow's Tower](wiki:Widow's_Tower). Jaqen enlists the aid of [Rorge](wiki:Rorge) and [Biter](wiki:Biter). Under the pretense of bringing onion soup to a group of Amory's guards, the four kill the guards (some with scalding hot soup) and free the northern prisoners.

Unknown to Ser Amory Lorch and independently of Arya and Jaqen, Vargo Hoat had previously approached Roose's encampment and struck a deal to turn over the castle to the [Starks](wiki:House_Stark). While the freed [northmen](wiki:Northmen) and [rivermen](wiki:Rivermen) are escaping, the Brave Companions simultaneously turn on their former Lannister allies, killing them in their beds or while they are celebrating in the [Barracks Hall](wiki:Barracks_Hall).(wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

## Aftermath

The bodies of the slain Lannisters are burned in a pyre within Harrenhal's [Flowstone Yard](wiki:Flowstone_Yard). Jaqen [assumes a new persona](wiki:Faceless_Men) and leaves Harrenhal, while Rorge and Biter join the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions). Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) marches on Harrenhal, and [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat) turns the castle over to him. As a reward for [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)'s actions with the "weasel soup", Roose elevates her from a servant drudge to his own [cupbearer](wiki:Cupbearer), and she begins to call herself "Nan". As sport for Vargo's Brave Companions, Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch) is killed by a large black [bear](wiki:Bear) in Harrenhal's bear pit.(wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)

The loss of Harrenhal traps Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) between the forces of Roose at Harrenhal and Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), culminating in the next major battle of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords).
