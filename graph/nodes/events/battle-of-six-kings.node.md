## Origins

When [Humfrey I Teague](wiki:Humfrey_I_Teague), the pious [King of the Rivers and the Hills](wiki:King_of_the_Rivers_and_the_Hills), began to repress worship of the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods), Lord [Roderick Blackwood](wiki:Roderick_Blackwood) of [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall) rose in rebellion, followed by [House Vance](wiki:House_Vance_of_Atranta) of [Atranta](wiki:Atranta) and [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully) of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun). Supported by the [Faith Militant](wiki:Faith_Militant), Humfrey's loyalists were on the verge of defeating the rebels. Roderick sought help from the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Arlan III Durrandon](wiki:Arlan_III_Durrandon), with whom he was related by marriage. Arlan desired to restore the riverlands to the Blackwoods, who had ruled as kings in the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes).(wiki:Battle_of_Six_Kings.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Leading a great host of [stormlanders](wiki:Stormlanders) across the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush), Arlan defeated the loyalists in several battles and was able to relieve the siege of Raventree. The bloody final battle took place in an afternoon between the [Mother's Teats](wiki:Teats), during which the stormlanders extinguished the line of [House Teague](wiki:House_Teague). Humfrey died first, followed by his sons [Humfrey II](wiki:Humfrey_II_Teague), [Hollis](wiki:Hollis_Teague), and [Tyler](wiki:Tyler_Teague), and finally his brother, [Damon](wiki:Damon_Teague), all of whom briefly ruled as a river king. Other casualties were Roderick Blackwood, [Elston Tully](wiki:Elston_Tully), and Lords [Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken), [Darry](wiki:House_Darry), [Smallwood](wiki:House_Smallwood), [Vance of Atranta](wiki:House_Vance_of_Atranta), and [Vance of Wayfarer's Rest](wiki:House_Vance_of_Wayfarer's_Rest),(wiki:Battle_of_Six_Kings.cite_ref-2) although it is unknown if they died in the final battle or earlier in the campaign.(wiki:Battle_of_Six_Kings.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
The battle became known as the Battle of Six Kings, for the five kings from House Teague and Arlan the Storm King. Roderick Blackwood's heir was a boy of only eight, and Arlan distrusted Roderick's brothers. He considered crowning Roderick's eldest child, [Shiera Blackwood](wiki:Shiera_Blackwood), and having her rule with his own son from [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon), but the river lords refused to be ruled by a woman. Instead, Arlan chose to add the riverlands to the realm of the Storm Kings.(wiki:Battle_of_Six_Kings.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
