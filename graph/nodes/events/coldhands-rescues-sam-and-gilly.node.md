# Coldhands rescues Sam and Gilly

Fleeing south after the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](mutiny-at-crasters-keep), [Samwell Tarly](samwell-tarly)
and [Gilly](gilly) (with her newborn son) are cornered at an abandoned wildling village by a score of
wights, among them Sam's dead brothers of the Watch. After Sam burns the wight of Small Paul, a hooded
rider in mottled blacks and greys — [Coldhands](coldhands), astride a great elk — appears and looses a
swarm of ravens that drives the wights off. He pulls Gilly and Sam onto the elk and begins guiding them
toward the Wall. This is **distinct** from [Bran's later meeting with Coldhands](bran-meets-coldhands)
(ADWD, beyond the Wall): the Sam rescue happens first, in ASOS, before Coldhands ever encounters Bran's
party.
