## Identity

Wedding of Drogo and Daenerys Targaryen is a battle/event from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges


## Origins

[Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) and his sister [Daenerys](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) have lived in exile in the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) in the aftermath of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). While the exiles are staying with [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) in [Pentos](wiki:Pentos), the [magister](wiki:Magister) negotiates a [betrothal](wiki:Betrothal) between Daenerys and [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), the greatest [khal](wiki:Khal) of the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea). In return, Drogo will help Viserys recover the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) from the [Usurper](wiki:Usurper), King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Daenerys is to meet Drogo for the first time at a feast held at [his manse](wiki:Drogo's_manse). Illyrio has the girl wear a [silk](wiki:Silk) gown of deep plum, gilded sandals, a tiara, [golden](wiki:Gold) bracelets crusted with [amethysts](wiki:Amethyst), and a heavy golden torc emblazoned with [Valyrian](wiki:Valyria) [glyphs](wiki:High_Valyrian), to emphasize the fact that she is a princess.(wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Other notables in attendance for the meeting are Khal [Moro](wiki:Moro) and his son [Rhogoro](wiki:Rhogoro), the brother of the [Archon of Tyrosh](wiki:Archon_of_Tyrosh), and the exiled [knight](wiki:Knight) Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont).(wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Viserys accepts Jorah's offer of [service](wiki:Sworn_sword) that night.(wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
After Drogo agrees to marry Daenerys, he allows the siblings to live in his manse while he joins his [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) outside of Pentos.(wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) The city's magisters double the [city guard](wiki:City_watch_(Pentos)) while the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) are nearby.(wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
Viserys, while willing to wed Daenerys to Drogo to achieve his goals, is resentful of having to give her up, and the night before the wedding tries to enter her room and claim her [maidenhead](wiki:Maidenhead). Illyrio, however, has the foresight to post guards on her room and ensure the impetuous Viserys does not undo "[years of planning](wiki:Illyrio's_plans)".(wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Aftermath

After the wedding, Drogo's khalasar departs Pentos for [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak) so that Daenerys can be presented to the [dosh khaleen](wiki:Dosh_khaleen).(wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)(wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Illyrio invites Viserys to remain with him in Pentos, but Viserys insists upon accompanying Drogo to ensure the khal honors their alliance.(wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) Drogo initially ignores Daenerys during the day, but every night, contrary to his behavior at the wedding's consummation, he couples with her roughly, uncaring of her bruises and sores from riding and ignoring her cries of pain, until Daenerys considers suicide. However, Daenerys has a [dragon dream](wiki:Dragon_dream) that gives her new confidence and strength. Eventually, Daenerys initiates sex with Drogo, marking the beginning of a loving relationship between them. On the far end of the Dothraki sea, on her fourteenth nameday, Daenerys reveals that she is pregnant.(wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)
