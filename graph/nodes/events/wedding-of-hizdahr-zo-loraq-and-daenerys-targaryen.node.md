Wedding of Hizdahr zo Loraq and Daenerys Targaryen is a battle/event from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges


## Origins

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) is [wed](wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen) to [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) as part of her brother [Viserys's](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) alliance with the [khal](wiki:Khal).(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Drogo later kills Viserys at [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak),(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) however, and Drogo himself dies from wounds suffered in the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea).(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Daenerys hatches three [dragons](wiki:Dragon) from [dragon eggs](wiki:Dragon_egg) place in Drogo's funeral pyre,(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and she eventually embarks on [a campaign](wiki:Targaryen_campaign_in_Slaver's_Bay) to end [slavery](wiki:Slavery) in [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay).(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Daenerys chooses to rule as [Queen of Meereen](wiki:Queen_of_Meereen) after [conquering the city](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen),(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) and the widow refuses marriage offers from [Cleon the Great](wiki:Cleon_the_Great) of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor)(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) and from [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos) of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth).(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Meanwhile, the [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy) resist Daenerys's rule over Meereen by engaging in a [shadow war](wiki:Shadow_war) against her supporters.(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) To bring peace to the city, [Galazza Galare](wiki:Galazza_Galare) advises Daenerys to marry a highborn Meereenese man of noble blood, suggesting [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq). Daenerys tells Hizdahr she might be willing to marry him if he can prevent any murders in the city for ninety days and nights.(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), [Lord Commander of the Queensguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Queensguard), is wary of the idea, telling Daenerys that the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) would never accept Hizdahr as king.(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) [Daario Naharis](wiki:Daario_Naharis) suggests himself as a groom instead but is refused. After the [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) suggests that Daenerys massacre the [Great Masters](wiki:Great_Masters) at the wedding if she marries Hizdahr, the queen decides to send Daario back into the field.(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)
[Reznak mo Reznak](wiki:Reznak_mo_Reznak) and Galazza, the [Green Grace](wiki:Green_Grace), discuss wedding preparations and customs with Daenerys. The queen rejects the custom of the bride being examined by the husband's female family members to test for fertility, as well as the custom of the bride washing the groom's feet. Daenerys would prefer to marry using Westerosi rites, but Galazza explains that would be unacceptable to the [Ghiscari](wiki:Ghiscari). Daenerys learns that the bride is expected to wear dark red veils above a [tokar](wiki:Tokar) of white [silk](wiki:Silk) fringed with baby [pearls](wiki:Pearl).(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) Hizdahr affirms for Daenerys that her enemies, including [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) and [New Ghis](wiki:New_Ghis), require her to wed as part of any peace treaty to end the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen).(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2)
Daenerys sleeps with Daario when he returns to Meereen,(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-3) but continues to refuse his own marriage offers.(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) arrives in Meereen to offer [his own hand in marriage](wiki:Secret_marriage_pact) as well, but he is refused by Daenerys as well.(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) The queen commits instead to marrying Hizdahr the next day, though she sleeps with Daario one last time that night.(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-2)

## Aftermath

King Hizdahr decides to reopen the [fighting pits of Meereen](wiki:Fighting_pits_of_Meereen) to celebrate the city's peace with Yunkai, and he hosts a feast in the Great Pyramid for the Yunkishmen. Daenerys is displeased that a slave market has been opened by the [Wise Masters](wiki:Wise_Masters) south of the [Skahazadhan](wiki:Skahazadhan). Daario is angered by the marriage, so for his own protection Daenerys includes the sellsword among the hostages sent over the Yunkai'i.(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
Queen Daenerys attends the reopening of [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit). [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) appears during the competition, and the queen flies away on his back while attempting to control the [dragon](wiki:Dragon).(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) With Daenerys vanished from Meereen, Hizdahr assumes rule over the city.(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Quentyn attempts to tame one of the dragons confined by the queen to the [pit below the Great Pyramid](wiki:Pit_(Great_Pyramid)),(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) but he is mortally wounded in the attempt and [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal) and [Viserion](wiki:Viserion) are freed.(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
[Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) sails with the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) toward Meereen, having been sent by his brother [Euron](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) to bring Daenerys to Westeros as the Crow's Eye's bride. Victarion intends to marry Daenerys himself, however, and after learning of her marriage he finds himself willing to kill Hizdahr to make Daenerys a widow again.(wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

## Quotes

Galazza: Oft have I heard that yours is the blood of [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), [Jaehaerys the Wise](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), and [Daeron the Dragon](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen). The noble [Hizdahr](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) is of the blood of [Mazdhan the Magnificent](wiki:Mazdhan_zo_Loraq), [Hazrak the Handsome](wiki:Hazrak_zo_Loraq), and [Zharaq the Liberator](wiki:Zharaq_zo_Loraq).
Daenerys: His forebears are as dead as mine. Will Hizdahr raise their shades to defend [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) against [its enemies](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen)? I need a man with ships and swords. You offer me ancestors.
Reznak: Magnificence, you do not understand. The washing of the feet is hallowed by tradition. It signifies that you shall be [your husband's](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) handmaid. The [wedding](wiki:Wedding) garb is fraught with meaning too. The bride is dressed in dark red veils above a [tokar](wiki:Tokar) of white [silk](wiki:Silk), fringed with baby [pearls](wiki:Pearl).
Daenerys: All those pearls will make me rattle when I walk.
Hizdahr: Words are wind, as you yourself have so oft said. No words of yours will secure this peace for [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). [Your foes](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen) require deeds. They would see us wed, and they would see me crowned as [king](wiki:King_of_Meereen), to rule beside you.
Daenerys: Marriage or carnage. A wedding or a war. Are those my choices?
