## Identity

[Jojen Reed](jojen-reed), a greendreamer himself, names [Bran](bran-stark)'s wolf-dreams for what they are: *"A warg is what you are … you are the winged wolf, but you will never fly. Unless you open your eye."* This crystallizes the latent gift and orients Bran toward the three-eyed crow in the north. It is a **recognition, not a cause** — the gift predates Jojen's naming (Bran already turns into a wolf in his sleep, ACOK Bran I), so the naming names a thing that already exists rather than creating it.
