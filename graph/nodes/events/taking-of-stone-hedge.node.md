## Origins

[House Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood) declared for Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and her [blacks](wiki:Blacks) when war broke out, while [House Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken) supported her half-brother, King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) and the [greens](wiki:Greens). Lord [Samwell Blackwood](wiki:Samwell_Blackwood) sent raiders into Bracken lands, so Lord [Humfrey Bracken](wiki:Humfrey_Bracken) sent his son, Ser [Amos Bracken](wiki:Amos_Bracken), to retaliate with a strong force. However, Amos was slain in the [Battle of the Burning Mill](wiki:Battle_of_the_Burning_Mill), and Ser [Raylon Rivers](wiki:Raylon_Rivers) led the green survivors back to Bracken territory.(wiki:Taking_of_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

They returned to [Stone Hedge](wiki:Stone_Hedge) only to find that it had been captured in their absence: Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen), riding his [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [Caraxes](wiki:Caraxes), had led a strong host of [Darrys](wiki:House_Darry), [Freys](wiki:House_Frey), [Pipers](wiki:House_Piper), and [Rootes](wiki:House_Roote) to the Brackens' castle. With most of the Bracken forces away with Amos and Raylon, the blacks quickly stormed the castle. Humfrey was captured, along with his remaining children, his third wife, and his lowborn [paramour](wiki:Paramour). When Raylon returned to Stone Hedge, he yielded to Daemon, ending any green strength in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Taking_of_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
