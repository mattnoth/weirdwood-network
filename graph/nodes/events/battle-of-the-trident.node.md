## Origins

After Lord [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington)'s defeat in the [Battle of the Bells](wiki:Battle_of_the_Bells), King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) at last realized that the rebellion was a major threat to [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen), perhaps the greatest threat to the dynasty since [Daemon Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) left [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept) for [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), where Lords [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) and [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) married the daughters of Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully) in a [double wedding](wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully). Around the time of the Trident, Robert announced his claim for the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne)(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rssm1384.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and led the combined [Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn), [Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon), [Stark](wiki:House_Stark), and [Tully](wiki:House_Tully) host of rebels.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rawoiafrobert_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

King Aerys's heir, the crown prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), returned from [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) to take command of the royalist army.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rawoiafrhaegar_targaryen.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Aerys gracelessly reminded the [Martells](wiki:House_Martell) that Princess [Elia](wiki:Elia_Martell) was in his power and dispatched her uncle, Prince [Lewyn Martell](wiki:Lewyn_Martell) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), to assume command of ten thousand [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) marching up the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad), and joined them to Rhaegar's royalist army en route to the Trident. At the same time, Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) and Ser [Jonothor Darry](wiki:Jonothor_Darry), also of the Kingsguard, were sent to rally the scattered forces of Lord Connington. Additionally, Rhaegar persuaded his father to ask for help from his old [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Led by the loyalist lords [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) and [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne), the [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet) and most levies from the [Reach](wiki:Reach) were at the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Some of Mace's men joined Rhaegar's host, however, according to a semi-canon source.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rssm1043.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

The battle took place at [the chief crossing](wiki:Ruby_ford) of the [Trident](wiki:Trident), near the [crossroads](wiki:Crossroads) of the [river road](wiki:River_road) and the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad),(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rawoiafruby_ford.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) when [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) tried to cross the river.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) used his booming voice to command during the fighting.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

[Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) under the command of Prince [Lewyn Martell](wiki:Lewyn_Martell) threatened Robert's left flank. When [Lord Corbray](wiki:Lord_Corbray_(father_of_Lyonel)) fell wounded, Ser [Lyn Corbray](wiki:Lyn_Corbray) picked up his father's [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) sword, [Lady Forlorn](wiki:Lady_Forlorn), and led a charge which broke the Dornishmen. During the charge Lyn killed the already-injured Lewyn.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)

Elsewhere during the battle, Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) killed a dozen friends of Robert and [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark),(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Ragot33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) and Lord [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister) slew three of Rhaegar's bannermen.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)

Though many great lords and knights either lost their lives or made names for themselves in the fighting, the battle was decided when Robert and Rhaegar met in single combat on horseback in the stream. Robert eventually crushed Rhaegar's chest with a blow of [his warhammer](wiki:Robert's_warhammer), which had been made by [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) The blow was so hard that the [rubies](wiki:Ruby) adorning Rhaegar's armor were shattered free to litter the river, giving the [ruby ford](wiki:Ruby_ford) its name.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) Leaderless, the royal army broke and fled(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) back to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

Wounded by arrow, spear, and sword,(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) Barristan was close to death when he was brought before Robert. Although he was counseled to kill him by Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), Robert spared Barristan's life and sent his own [maester](wiki:Maester) to tend to his adversary's wounds.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Ragot33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) granted the [Wolf's Den](wiki:Wolf's_Den) to Ser [Bartimus](wiki:Bartimus) as a reward for saving his life during the battle.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) The sons of [Mors Umber](wiki:Mors_Umber) were killed at the Trident,(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) and [Lord Grandison](wiki:Lord_Grandison) died a year later from wounds sustained in the fighting.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

The son of Lord [Goodbrook](wiki:House_Goodbrook) made peace with Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully) and Robert after the battle.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1) Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) arrived after the fighting was over, so Hoster has since called him "the Late Lord Frey" and questioned his bannerman's loyalty.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1)(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)

### A Game of Thrones

Recalling the stories told to her by her brother [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) sometimes imagines how their late brother [Rhaegar](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) died for the woman he loved. Viserys desires to avenge Rhaegar's death by killing King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)

Unhappy as king, Robert admits to his childhood friend, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), that he sometimes regrets the outcome of the Trident and that he was unable to save Eddard's sister, [Lyanna](wiki:Lyanna_Stark), during [the war](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-2)

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) and [Mycah](wiki:Mycah) leave the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads)(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0) to look for any of Rhaegar's [rubies](wiki:Rubies) remaining in the [ruby ford](wiki:Ruby_ford).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)

A feverish Eddard has a fever dream of the [tower of joy](wiki:Tower_of_joy) in which he tells Ser [Arthur Dayne](wiki:Arthur_Dayne), Ser [Oswell Whent](wiki:Oswell_Whent), and Ser [Gerold Hightower](wiki:Gerold_Hightower) that he had unsuccessfully looked for the three [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) on the Trident.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-1)

## Aftermath

The battle was the decisive engagement of the war. When [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) learned of Prince [Rhaegar's](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) death, King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) sent the pregnant Queen [Rhaella](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen) with Prince [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). Aerys kept [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell) and her children at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) as a threat against [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), however, since the Mad King thought he had been betrayed by Doran's uncle, Prince [Lewyn](wiki:Lewyn_Martell), at the Trident.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

The way to King's Landing was now open for the rebels, with the only significant loyalist force left invested in the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End). The rebels also learned that Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) was heading toward King's Landing. Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) had been wounded during the battle, so Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) led Robert's vanguard down the kingsroad towards the capital, racing to reach it before the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-1) Tywin arrived first, however, and Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) convinced the Mad King to open the city gates, resulting in the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Robert succeeded Aerys on the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne),(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1) with Barristan included among those pardoned by the new king.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) yielded to Lord Stark after the Trident, ending the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End),(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) and Eddard continued south to the [tower of joy](wiki:Tower_of_joy).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0) Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), Robert's new wife, came to believe that the wrong man died at the Trident.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)
