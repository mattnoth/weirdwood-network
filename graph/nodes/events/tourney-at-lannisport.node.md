## Identity

Tourney at Lannisport is a tournament/tourney from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges


## Origins

The victor of the joust was Lord [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont), who wore the favor of [Lynesse Hightower](wiki:Lynesse_Hightower) and was awarded the win by King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) after breaking nine lances against Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister). Jorah crowned Lynesse his [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty), requesting and receiving her hand in marriage from her father, Lord [Leyton Hightower](wiki:Leyton_Hightower), that night. The pair then wed in [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport).(wiki:Tourney_at_Lannisport.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
