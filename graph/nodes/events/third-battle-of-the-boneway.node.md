## Origins

[Rhoynish](wiki:Rhoyne) warrior Queen [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria) made common cause with [Mors Martell](wiki:Mors_Martell), Lord of the [Sandship](wiki:Sandship), whose lands were dwarfed by those of powerful kings such as [House Yronwood](wiki:House_Yronwood).(wiki:Third_Battle_of_the_Boneway.cite_ref-2) She wed Mors, forming [House Nymeros Martell](wiki:House_Martell), and on the day of their wedding, Nymeria had her fleet burned to affirm that the Rhoynar could not return to Essos.(wiki:Third_Battle_of_the_Boneway.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The people of the Martell lands also intermarried with the Rhoynar, and the addition of the Rhoynar increased the strength of the Martells tenfold. Nymeria declared Mors to be the [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), using the Rhoynish title of prince instead the Westerosi king. Equal primogeniture was also introduced.(wiki:Third_Battle_of_the_Boneway.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Such supremacy was easier to declare than to achieve, however, and years of war followed. The Martells and their Rhoynar partners met and subdued one petty king after another. In total, six conquered kings were sent to the Wall in golden fetters by Nymeria and Mors. The sixth and last of these kings was the greatest of their foes: [Yorick Yronwood](wiki:Yorick_Yronwood), [the Bloodroyal](wiki:The_Bloodroyal), Fifth of His Name, Lord of [Yronwood](wiki:Yronwood), [Warden of the Stone Way](wiki:Warden_of_the_Stone_Way), [Knight of the Wells](wiki:Knight_of_the_Wells), [King of Redmarch](wiki:King_of_Redmarch), [King of the Greenbelt](wiki:King_of_the_Greenbelt), and [King of the Dornish](wiki:King_of_the_Dornish).(wiki:Third_Battle_of_the_Boneway.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

Mors Martell fought, with his allies, against the Yronwoods and their bannermen for nine years, in numerous battles. Mors and Yorick met in battle during the Third Battle of the [Boneway](wiki:Boneway), where Yorick personally slew Mors.(wiki:Third_Battle_of_the_Boneway.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Aftermath

After Mors's death, the fighting continued for two more years, until Yorick Yronwood bent the knee to Nymeria. Yorick was send to the [Wall](wiki:Wall), to serve in the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), and Nymeria ruled Dorne from [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) thereafter.(wiki:Third_Battle_of_the_Boneway.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)(wiki:Third_Battle_of_the_Boneway.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_dorne:_the_coming_of_the_rhoynar.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
