## Origins

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), hosted the tourney to celebrate the tenth year of King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen)'s reign. During its proceedings the king drunkenly insulted Tywin's wife, Lady [Joanna Lannister](wiki:Joanna_Lannister), by asking if her breasts had been ruined by breastfeeding her children, [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) and [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister). Aerys refused to allow Tywin to resign as Hand the following day.(wiki:Anniversary_Tourney.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
It was oft believed that the lavish prizes offered in the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) in [281 AC](wiki:281_AC) was an attempt by Lord [Walter Whent](wiki:Walter_Whent) to outdo Tywin's tourney in 272.(wiki:Anniversary_Tourney.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
