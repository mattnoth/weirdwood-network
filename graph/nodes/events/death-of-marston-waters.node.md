## Identity

Ser Marston Waters, Lord Commander of the Kingsguard and King's Hand, is fatally stabbed in the belly by his sworn brother Ser Mervyn Flowers when he goes to arrest him as a conspirator in the plot against King Aegon III; he dies of the wound that same night.
