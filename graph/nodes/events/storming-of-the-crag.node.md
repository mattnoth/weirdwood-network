## Identity

Storming of the Crag is a event.battle from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges

- FIGHTS_IN: War of the Five Kings (track_b: Conflict)
- DEFEATS: Stark (track_b: Result)

## Origins

After the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross), the host of King [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) takes castles of the [westermen](wiki:Westermen), including [Ashemark](wiki:Taking_of_Ashemark), and [three gold mines](wiki:Capture_of_Westerland_gold_mines).(wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The [northmen](wiki:Northmen) move on to the [Crag](wiki:Crag)(wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) as part of Robb's plan to draw Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) west and out of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) The [castellan](wiki:Castellan) of the weakly-garrisoned Crag is Ser [Rolph Spicer](wiki:Rolph_Spicer).(wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

After learning that [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) has taken [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) has Maester [Vyman](wiki:Vyman) send a [raven](wiki:Raven) from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) to Ashemark, hoping that a rider can then catch up to Robb as he advances on the Crag.(wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

## Narrative Arc

The Crag is stormed at night. [Smalljon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber_(son_of_Jon)) and [Black Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Ryman)) lead scaling parties over the walls. Meanwhile, Robb breaks through the main gate with a ram but takes an arrow wound. [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind), Robb's [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), kills a man during the assault.(wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

## Aftermath

Robb is nursed back to health by Lady [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling). After hearing of the supposed deaths at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) of his brothers, [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon](wiki:Rickon_Stark), Robb deflowers Jeyne, and, to spare her dishonor, marries her shortly thereafter. As a consequence of that, the [Westerlings](wiki:House_Westerling) join with [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark).(wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8) However, by marrying Jeyne, Robb breaks his alliance with [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey).(wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

[Smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) at Riverrun celebrate Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully)'s imminent return after the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords) and Robb's victory at the Crag with nut-brown [ale](wiki:Ale).(wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
