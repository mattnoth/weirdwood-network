## Origins

[Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) and his brother, Lord Captain [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy), led the [burning of the Lannister fleet](wiki:Burning_of_the_Lannister_fleet) anchored at [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) to begin [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion) on behalf of their elder brother, [Balon](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy). In response, King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) brought the [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet) commanded by Lord [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), supported by warships from the [Arbor](wiki:Redwyne_fleet), [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), and the [Reach](wiki:Reach), to deal with the longship threat of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands).(wiki:Sea_battle_off_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

Led by Victarion, the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) found itself in the [Straits of Fair Isle](wiki:Straits_of_Fair_Isle), with the island of [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle) to their west and the mainland of the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) to their east. Stannis's ships trapped them from the north and the south, smashing the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) fleet off Fair Isle.(wiki:Sea_battle_off_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Sea_battle_off_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

*[Golden Storm](wiki:Golden_Storm)*, the ship of the youngest Greyjoy brother, [Aeron](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy), was destroyed by Stannis's flagship, *[Fury](wiki:Fury)*.(wiki:Sea_battle_off_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Lady [Gwynesse Harlaw](wiki:Gwynesse_Harlaw)'s husband died in the battle, as did the two sons of Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw).(wiki:Sea_battle_off_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Recollection of his naval defeat bothers [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy), [Lord Captain of the Iron Fleet](wiki:Lord_Captain_of_the_Iron_Fleet).(wiki:Sea_battle_off_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Aftermath

With the threat of the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) diminished, King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) was able to land in the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) and put down [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion).(wiki:Sea_battle_off_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) was held prisoner by [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Sea_battle_off_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
