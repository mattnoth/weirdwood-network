## Narrative Arc

The remaining members of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) from [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) and several people from [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town) man the [Wall](wiki:Wall) as [Mance](wiki:Mance_Rayder)’s army approaches. As the Watch repels attack after attack from their great vantage point, some of the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) have reach the gate. To his surprise, [Jon](wiki:Jon_Snow) is left in command as [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye) leaves to defend the gate. Jon orders fire arrows and oil casks dropped on the [giants](wiki:Giants) and [mammoths](wiki:Mammoth), and the defenders once again repel the [wildling attack](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall). In the aftermath of the fighting, Jon goes down into the gate tunnel, to find Donal Noye dead alongside [Mag Mar Tun Doh Weg](wiki:Mag_Mar_Tun_Doh_Weg). When Jon tells Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) the news, the ancient [maester](wiki:Maester) tells him that he must lead the Watch now.
