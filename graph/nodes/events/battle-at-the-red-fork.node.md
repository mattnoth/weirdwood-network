## Origins

A [green](wiki:Greens) ally of King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister) led the [westermen](wiki:Westermen) in attacking the [rivermen](wiki:Rivermen), many of whom were [black](wiki:Blacks) supporters of Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen).

## Narrative Arc

The westermen met the outnumbered rivermen at a crossing of the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork). The rivermen threw back three attempts of the greens to cross the river, with Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister) being slain in the third attack by a [squire](wiki:Squire), [Pate of Longleaf](wiki:Pate_of_Longleaf). Command of the Lannister host was assumed by Ser [Adrian Tarbeck](wiki:Adrian_Tarbeck).(wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Adrian and a hundred [knights](wiki:Knight) shed their heavy armor, swam the Red Fork upstream of the fighting, and surprised Lord [Tristan Vance](wiki:Tristan_Vance)'s men from the rear. The rivermen resistance collapsed after Adrian slew Tristan, allowing the remaining westermen to cross the Red Fork.(wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Aftermath

Lord [Petyr Piper](wiki:Petyr_Piper) knighted [Pate of Longleaf](wiki:Pate_of_Longleaf), who became known as Longleaf the Lionslayer.(wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

The Lannister host continued to march east towards [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal),(wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) winning victories under Adrian's command.(wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The westermen were briefly checked at [Acorn Hall](wiki:Acorn_Hall) by Lord [Joseth Smallwood](wiki:Joseth_Smallwood) and Lord Petyr Piper, but Lord Piper died [in battle](wiki:Battle_at_Acorn_Hall) and Lord Smallwood retreated back to his castle. Three days later, the rivermen regrouped under a [hedge knight](wiki:Hedge_knight), Ser [Harry Penny](wiki:Harry_Penny), and gave battle to the westermen again. While the Lannister host once again prevailed, Ser Adrian and Ser Harry killed each other in the fighting.(wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

With Adrian's death, command of the westermen passed to the aged and wounded Lord [Humfrey Lefford](wiki:Humfrey_Lefford). The Lannister host continued east into the riverlands, but they were eventually crushed in the [Battle by the Lakeshore](wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore). With Jason dead and [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) in disarray,(wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0), Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), the Red Kraken, then attacked the westerlands. Jason's widow, Lady [Johanna Lannister](wiki:Johanna_Lannister), barred the gates of Casterly Rock, but was unable to prevent the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) from sacking [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) and raiding the coast.(wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_red_kraken.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
