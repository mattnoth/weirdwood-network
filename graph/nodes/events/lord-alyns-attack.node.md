## Origins

Because the [Daughters' War](wiki:Daughters'_War) was hampering trade in the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), desired to defeat [Racallio Ryndoon](wiki:Racallio_Ryndoon) and establish a presence on [Bloodstone](wiki:Bloodstone), one of the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones). Unwin granted command of the rebuilt [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet) to his inexperienced uncle, Ser [Gedmund Peake](wiki:Gedmund_Peake), and named [Ned Bean](wiki:Ned_Bean) as Gedmund's second. The Hand instructed the eight warships and twenty cogs and galleys of Gedmund to be joined by [House Velaryon](wiki:House_Velaryon). Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) brought sixty war galleys, thirty longships, and more than a hundred cogs to Gedmund's fleet.(wiki:Lord_Alyn's_attack.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

The [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) fleet was joined by twelve longships led by Lord [Bryndemere Tarth](wiki:Bryndemere_Tarth). While at [Tarth](wiki:Tarth), however, they learned that Racallio had allied with the [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos) and the [Archon of Tyrosh](wiki:Archon_of_Tyrosh). Since [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) was the most powerful of the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities), Gedmund Great-Axe and Blackbean waited for further instructions from Lord Peake. Disregarding orders, Alyn chose not to wait and instead led the Velaryon ships south toward the Stepstones.(wiki:Lord_Alyn's_attack.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Narrative Arc

While the [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) grand captain and twoscore of his captains were feasting with [Racallio Ryndoon](wiki:Racallio_Ryndoon) and the [Tyroshi](wiki:Tyrosh) envoys at Bloodstone, Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) attacked the unsuspecting Braavosi ships among the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones). The [Velaryons](wiki:House_Velaryon) captured, burned, or sank half of the Braavosi ships while they were anchored, and they fought Braavosi ships which attempted to sail. The great dromond *[Grand Defiance](wiki:Grand_Defiance)* was able to fight past six Velaryon ships, but it sank in open sea after being struck by Alyn's flagship, *[Queen Rhaenys](wiki:Queen_Rhaenys)*.(wiki:Lord_Alyn's_attack.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Alyn lost only three ships in his attack, including the *[True Heart](wiki:True_Heart)* of his cousin, [Daeron Velaryon](wiki:Daeron_Velaryon), and he sank more than thirty Braavosi ships. Alyn captured six galleys, eleven cogs, and eighty-nine hostages. He also claimed food, drink, weapons, currency, and an [elephant](wiki:Elephant) meant for the [Sealord](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos).(wiki:Lord_Alyn's_attack.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

## Aftermath

Alyn, called Oakenfist for his sinking of *[Grand Defiance](wiki:Grand_Defiance)*, was celebrated by thousands of [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) when he returned to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). During a lavish ceremony in the throne room, Alyn Oakenfist was [knighted](wiki:Knight) by Ser [Marston Waters](wiki:Marston_Waters), the [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard). Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake) reluctantly draped a gold admiral's chain around Alyn's neck and gave him a silver replica of *[Queen Rhaenys](wiki:Queen_Rhaenys)*. King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) appointed Alyn as [master of ships](wiki:Master_of_ships).(wiki:Lord_Alyn's_attack.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

Lord Peake had been outraged at Alyn's attack, since the Westerosi soldiers meant to take [Bloodstone](wiki:Bloodstone) had been left behind with Ser [Gedmund Peake](wiki:Gedmund_Peake)'s ships at [Tarth](wiki:Tarth). [Racallio Ryndoon](wiki:Racallio_Ryndoon) remained in power and Alyn had initiated hostilities with powerful [Braavos](wiki:Braavos). Desiring to be rid of Alyn, Unwin orchestrated for the Oakenfist to sail to the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea) and end the predations of Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), the Red Kraken. Alyn set sail for the west on *[Lady Baela](wiki:Lady_Baela)*, a captured Braavosi galley which he named after his wife, [Baela Targaryen](wiki:Baela_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord_Alyn's_attack.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

Lord Peake sent Lord [Manfryd Mooton](wiki:Manfryd_Mooton) with a delegation to negotiate with the Sealord and prevent open war. Manfryd returned the elephant to the Sealord, who forgave Alyn's "unwarranted transgression" and ended the alliance with Tyrosh and Racallio. In return, the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) paid a large indemnity to Braavos.(wiki:Lord_Alyn's_attack.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)
