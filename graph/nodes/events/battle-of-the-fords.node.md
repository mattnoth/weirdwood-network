## Origins

When [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), the new [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), marches west from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), he leaves his uncle, Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully), to hold Riverrun and guard his rear.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-8) Edmure commands the [rivermen](wiki:Rivermen), as his father, Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), is ill.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Robb's [northmen](wiki:Northmen) and [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) bypass the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth) using a goat track found by [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind), Robb's [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-7) Near [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport), Robb surprises the newly-raised host of [Stafford Lannister](wiki:Stafford_Lannister), who neglected to post enough sentries, in the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-9) The northmen [capture Ashmark](wiki:Taking_of_Ashemark) and pay the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) back in kind for the devastation inflicted on the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-10)(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-8)

Expecting [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) to be preoccupied for months with the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) risks leaving [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) to stop Robb's raiding of his homeland.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-15) In response, Edmure orders Ser [Helman Tallhart](wiki:Helman_Tallhart) to leave the [Twins](wiki:Twins) and aid Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), who is at the [crossroads](wiki:Crossroads), with recovering Harrenhal. Edmure thinks that once Robb returns from the west, Tywin can be trapped between Riverrun and Harrenhal and then defeated. Edmure's plan is supported by Lords [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood) and [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken),(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-11) fierce rivals who usually disagree.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)

In order to return to the westerlands, Tywin must cross the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) of the [Trident](wiki:Trident). Edmure calls his banners, approximately eight thousand foot and three thousand horse, to prevent Tywin, who has near twice as many, from crossing the river.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-12) While returning to Riverrun, [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) spots the tents of Lord Blackwood and Ser [Marq Piper](wiki:Marq_Piper), as well as [Darrys](wiki:House_Darry) and [Paeges](wiki:House_Paege).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-13) Edmure allows [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) to seek refuge in Riverun.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-14)

## Aftermath

[Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) simultaneously celebrates Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully)'s victory in the Battle of the Fords and [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s [storming of the Crag](wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) The [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) is wounded at the Crag, however, and treated by [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling). After being told that his brothers, [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon](wiki:Rickon_Stark), have [been killed](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell), the grieving Robb sleeps with Jeyne, and then marries her to preserve her honor.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2) Since Robb has broken his betrothal to a daughter of Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey), the [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) at the Crag and Riverrun angrily depart.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-3) The Freys at Harrenhal are also outraged.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

After Robb returns to Riverrun, he and Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) criticize Edmure for having confronted Tywin. They explain that Brynden's scouts had found an advantageous position on the [Goldroad](wiki:Goldroad). Robb had hoped to draw Tywin into the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) and then either defeat Tywin on the Goldroad or lead the westermen on a chase along the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea)'s coast. Robb explains [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) would have been able to conquer [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-4) Edmure's defense of the fords delayed Tywin long enough for riders from [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge) to inform him of happenings in the east, however. With Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) having negotiated an alliance between [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) and [House Baratheon of King's Landing](wiki:House_Baratheon_of_King's_Landing),(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Tywin turned back east, rendezvousing with Lords [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), [Mathis Rowan](wiki:Mathis_Rowan), and [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly). The combined army then defeated Stannis in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

To make amends, Edmure offers to lead Robb's van in the next battle.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-5) After the passing of Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), Edmure, now Lord of Riverrun, is convinced by Robb, Brynden, and [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) that he should wed one of Walder's daughters, so that the Freys will again support Robb.(wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
