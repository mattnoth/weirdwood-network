## Origins

During [the Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) slew [Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare), [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers), in the [burning of Harrenhal](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal).(wiki:Harren_the_Red's_rebellion.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) The realm of Harren the Black was then divided between the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands), ruled by [House Greyjoy](wiki:House_Greyjoy), and the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), ruled by [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully). Within the riverlands, Aegon granted rule over [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) to [House Qoherys](wiki:House_Qoherys).(wiki:Harren_the_Red's_rebellion.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

The lecherous [Gargon Qoherys](wiki:Gargon_Qoherys), [Lord of Harrenhal](wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal), was disliked for practicing the [first night](wiki:First_night) within his domains and for taking advantage of his servants. While King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen) was at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) during a [royal progress](wiki:Royal_progress) after succeeding Aegon on the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), the father of a maid dishonored by Gargon opened a postern gate at Harrenhal to the outlaw [Harren the Red](wiki:Harren_the_Red), who claimed to be Harren the Black's grandson. Gargon the Guest was killed by Red Harren in the [godswood of Harrenhal](wiki:Godswood_of_Harrenhal), and most of Harrenhal's men-at-arms joined the brigands. Harren called himself Lord of Harrenhal and [King of the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Rivers).(wiki:Harren_the_Red's_rebellion.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

[Lord Tully](wiki:House_Tully) urged King Aenys to fly to Harrenhal on [Quicksilver](wiki:Quicksilver), his [dragon](wiki:Dragon), and quickly end Harren's rebellion, but Aenys instead ordered Lord Tully to call his banners. By the time Aenys eventually marched on Harrenhal with a thousand men, the castle had been abandoned with Harren fled to the woods.(wiki:Harren_the_Red's_rebellion.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Aenys's reign was also troubled by [Jonos Arryn's rebellion](wiki:Jonos_Arryn's_rebellion) in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn), [Lodos the Twice-Drowned's revolt](wiki:Lodos_the_Twice-Drowned's_revolt) in the Iron islands, and the [Second Dornish War](wiki:Second_Dornish_War).(wiki:Harren_the_Red's_rebellion.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Worried that Red Harren might threaten [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), King Aenys sent Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) and their younger children to the safety of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Harren_the_Red's_rebellion.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Lord [Alyn Stokeworth](wiki:Alyn_Stokeworth), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), led a few hundred men,(wiki:Harren_the_Red's_rebellion.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) including [Myles Smallwood](wiki:Myles_Smallwood),(wiki:Harren_the_Red's_rebellion.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) to hunt down Harren. The royal host eventually caught the river rebels in a village west of the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye). Harren slew Lord Stokeworth and was in turn slain by Alyn's squire, [Bernarr Brune](wiki:Bernarr_Brune). Aenys rewarded Bernarr with [knighthood](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Harren_the_Red's_rebellion.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)
