## Origins

Inseparable after their victory in the [Battle of the Kingsroad](wiki:Battle_of_the_Kingsroad), the young Kermit, Oscar, and Benjicot began to be called "the Lads" by their men.(wiki:Lads.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
With the Lads' forces approaching [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) and Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark) also marching south on the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad), Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) advised King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) to abdicate and join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) but was refused. The king was found [poisoned](wiki:Poison) later that day, however, and was succeeded by King [Aegon III](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen).(wiki:Lads.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Lads.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Lads.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The Lads were welcomed by [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk), including pretty women showering them with kisses, and the [gold cloaks](wiki:Gold_cloaks) when they arrived in the capital, although [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom) suspects their reception was organized by Lord [Larys Strong](wiki:Larys_Strong).(wiki:Lads.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
The arrival of Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark) and his host of [northmen](wiki:North) dampened enthusiasm, however, and the Lads deferred to the imposing [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North) during the [Hour of the Wolf](wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf).(wiki:Lads.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
