## Origins

[Beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall), [Raymun Redbeard](wiki:Raymun_Redbeard) united the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) and took the title of [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall). The [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), whose guard was down, let Raymun's reavers get atop the [Wall](wiki:Wall) and throw down ladders for the army waiting beneath. The army of wildlings attacked south of the Wall, causing Lord [Willam Stark](wiki:Willam_Stark) to leave [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) and march north, calling Lord [Harmond Umber](wiki:Harmond_Umber)'s banners too.(wiki:Battle_at_Long_Lake.cite_ref-RPG_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

Lords Willam and Harmond met Raymun on the shores of [Long Lake](wiki:Long_Lake) in the [north](wiki:North), with the free folk forced to fight with the lake at their back.(wiki:Battle_at_Long_Lake.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_wall_and_beyond:_the_wildlings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Although Raymun beheaded Willam, he was in turn slain by Willam's younger brother, [Artos the Implacable](wiki:Artos_Stark), and the wildling force was defeated.(wiki:Battle_at_Long_Lake.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Battle_at_Long_Lake.cite_ref-RPG_2-1) The sons of Raymun Redbeard were also killed, but his younger brother, [Red Raven](wiki:Red_Raven_(free_folk)), fled back [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:Battle_at_Long_Lake.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Aftermath

The Night's Watch arrived too late to join the battle. Artos ordered them to dispose of the dead. The [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), [Jack Musgood](wiki:Jack_Musgood), was known as Sleepy Jack afterwards.(wiki:Battle_at_Long_Lake.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Battle_at_Long_Lake.cite_ref-RPG_2-2) Raymun's invasion was the last major wildling threat until the rise of [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder), which coincided with the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).
