## Narrative Arc

[Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) is lying in bed beside [his wife](wiki:Sansa_Stark). Dreadful details of the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) have reached [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), about how the [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) mutilated the corpses of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) and [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Tully). Tyrion has tried to keep those stories from Sansa and wants to comfort her but he knows she will never take comfort from a [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister). He leaves to visit [Shae](wiki:Shae) in the room where the dragon skulls are kept, but he remembers his recent conversation with [Varys](wiki:Varys). The eunuch warned him that if confronted by [the Queen](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), he would tell her the truth about Shae. Tyrion realizes that if he is to save Shae, he must either send her away or marry her off. After she pleases him, he decides that he will marry her to Ser [Tallad](wiki:Tallad), and Cersei will never think to find out who she is.
