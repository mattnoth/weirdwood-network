# Death of Maester Aemon

Maester [Aemon Targaryen](aemon-targaryen-son-of-maekar-i) — son of Maekar I, a hundred and two years
old — dies aboard the swan ship *Cinnamon Wind* somewhere off the sun-scorched southern coast of Dorne,
of a chill caught during the storm-wracked voyage from the Wall. [Jon Snow](jon-snow) had sent him south
because Aemon's Targaryen blood made him a target for Melisandre's king's-blood magic at the Wall (the
precedent set by the [leeching of Edric Storm](leeching-of-edric-storm)). On his deathbed Aemon, briefly
lucid after [Samwell Tarly](samwell-tarly) brings him talk of Daenerys's dragons, declares his belief
that Daenerys — not Rhaegar — is the prince that was promised, and charges Sam to carry the warnings to
the archmaesters of the Citadel. With no septon aboard, Sam says the funeral words; Aemon's body is
preserved in a cask of rum to be burned at Oldtown. His death drives the rest of Sam's Citadel mission.
