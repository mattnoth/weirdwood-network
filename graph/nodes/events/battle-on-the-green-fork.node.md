## Origins

War breaks out after the death of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) and the arrest of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) invades the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), scattering the riverlords and placing [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) under siege. Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) occupies the [crossroads](wiki:Crossroads) near the [ruby ford](wiki:Ruby_ford) and waits to see how Houses [Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) and [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) will respond. Tywin expects [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey), who control the crossing over the [Green Fork](wiki:Green_Fork), to remain neutral.(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

[Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls the [northern](wiki:North) banners and marches south from [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) to rescue his father.(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) When Ser [Addam Marbrand](wiki:Addam_Marbrand)'s outriders report that Robb is on the [causeway](wiki:Causeway), Tywin orders for Addam to draw the northmen further south while Tywin leads his own host north on the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad) east of the Green Fork to meet them.(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1)

Once south of the [Neck](wiki:Neck), Robb's mother, [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), persuades Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) to ally with Robb, opening the way over the Green Fork at the [Twins](wiki:Twins). Robb, who does not wish to stake everything on one battle with Tywin, splits his host in two. The foot and one-tenth of the horse under Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) continue marching south on the kingsroad to engage Tywin, while Robb himself commands the rest of the cavalry and crosses west of the river, in the hope of lifting the siege of Riverrun by taking Tywin's son, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), unawares.(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-1)(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1)

Addam's outriders inform Tywin that the Freys have joined the Starks and their combined host is marching south on the kingsroad.(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-19) Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully)'s men kill some of Addam's scouts, however, preventing the Lannisters from knowing that Robb's host split at the Twins.(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-2)

## Narrative Arc

When the [westermen](wiki:Westerlands) think the [northmen](wiki:North) are still a day's march away, the [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) army, commanded by Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), marches throughout the night in an effort to take the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) army by surprise, arriving before dawn.(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-20)

However, the Lannister army arrays itself in time to meet the northmen. The westermen's order of battle includes:

- Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) commands ten thousand men in the center, with Lords [Lefford](wiki:Leo_Lefford), [Lydden](wiki:Lewys_Lydden) and [Serrett](wiki:House_Serrett) at his side with three hundred heavy horse. His foot archers are arranged in three long lines east and west of the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad). Between the archers are squares of pikemen, and behind them are ranks of [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms) wielding axes, swords, and spears.(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-21)
- On the right, Ser [Addam Marbrand](wiki:Addam_Marbrand) commands four thousand knights and other heavy lancers, among them Ser [Flement Brax](wiki:Flement_Brax) and members of Houses [Crakehall](wiki:House_Crakehall), [Swyft](wiki:House_Swyft), and other families.(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-22)
- Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) commands the vanguard on the left. At one thousand men, the van is entirely mounted, but consists of freeriders, [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords), and raw fieldhands and smallfolk from [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport). The van also includes [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), his sellsword [Bronn](wiki:Bronn), and his three hundred [Vale mountain clansmen](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans),(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-23) who are considered savages by the nobles.(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-2)
- From the high ground of a hilltop, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) commands the reserve of five thousand men, half mounted, half afoot.(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-24)

Unbeknownst to Tyrion, Tywin has placed his undisciplined men on the left to tempt the Stark commander, whom Tywin believes is the untested [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), to overcommit there. Tywin hopes the Lannister van will rout and the Starks will plunge into the gap, eager to flank him. Tywin then intends for Kevan and the center to wheel to the left and take the northerners in the flank, driving them into the Green Fork.(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-25) Before the fighting begins, Gregor orders Tyrion to hold the van's left, along the riverfront.(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-26)

Kevan's archers fire volleys of arrows from the kingsroad as the northmen march forward; Tyrion observes the banners of Houses [Cerwyn](wiki:House_Cerwyn), [Frey](wiki:House_Frey), [Glover](wiki:House_Glover), [Hornwood](wiki:House_Hornwood), [Karstark](wiki:House_Karstark), and Stark. The armored veterans with Gregor break a shield wall of Karstark spearmen, and Tyrion's clansmen follow through the breach. Tyrion notices a volley of arrows land amongst the northmen and Lannister van, although he does not know from where they were fired. Tyrion kills a northern swordsman who attacks him, and then forces a northern knight to yield after the man becomes trapped beneath his horse.(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-27)

Meanwhile, Kevan sends in his pikemen to push the Stark lines against the hills behind them, and Kevan's archers fire against the northmen behind shield walls. Tywin leads his reserve behind the van, along the river, to break the remnants of the Stark line.(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-28) Tywin feels the northmen were led cautiously during the battle, which he did not expect from young Robb.(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-29)

## Aftermath

[Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton)'s host loses several thousand men in the battle, including Lord [Halys Hornwood](wiki:Halys_Hornwood).(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-30) Captives taken include Lord [Medger Cerwyn](wiki:Medger_Cerwyn), [Harrion Karstark](wiki:Harrion_Karstark), Ser [Wylis Manderly](wiki:Wylis_Manderly), Ser [Donnel Locke](wiki:Donnel_Locke), Ser [Jared Frey](wiki:Jared_Frey), Ser [Hosteen Frey](wiki:Hosteen_Frey), Ser [Danwell Frey](wiki:Danwell_Frey), and [Ronel Rivers](wiki:Ronel_Rivers).(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) leaves the northern knight he captured in the care of [Bronn](wiki:Bronn).(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-31) About half of Tyrion's [clansmen](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) are killed in the fighting, with [Conn](wiki:Conn) of the [Stone Crows](wiki:Stone_Crows) and [Ulf](wiki:Ulf) of the [Moon Brothers](wiki:Moon_Brothers) among the slain.(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-32)

The Lannisters learn from their captives that the northern host had split at the [Twins](wiki:Twins), and that [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) is rushing toward [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-33) Instead of pursuing Roose's host as it retreats northward, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) leads his own host on a forced march south to aid his son, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) Robb captures the Kingslayer at the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood),(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) however, and he then saves Riverrun with the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps).(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)

After learning of Jaime's defeat while at the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn), Tywin decides to march to [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4) Three dozen captives from the Green Fork are housed in the [Tower of Dread](wiki:Tower_of_Dread), and Lord Cerwyn dies at Harrenhal from a wound taken on the Green Fork.(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

Roose reforms his host at the mouth of the [causeway](wiki:Causeway)(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-1) and returns to the [Twins](wiki:Twins).(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-1) The deaths of Lord Hornwood at the Green Fork and his son [Daryn](wiki:Daryn_Hornwood) in the Whispering Wood cause a [succession crisis to Hornwood](wiki:Fighting_in_the_Hornwood) back in the north.(wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)
