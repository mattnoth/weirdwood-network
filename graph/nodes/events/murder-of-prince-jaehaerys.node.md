## Identity

Acting on Prince Daemon's "an eye for an eye, a son for a son" vow of vengeance for Lucerys's death, the hired killers Blood and Cheese infiltrate the Red Keep and behead Aegon II's young heir, Prince Jaehaerys, before Queen Helaena, escalating the Dance into open atrocity.
