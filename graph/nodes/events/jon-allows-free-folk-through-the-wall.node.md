## Identity

In ADWD Jon XII, [Jon Snow](jon-snow), as Lord Commander of the Night's Watch, opens the gate at [Castle Black](castle-black) and admits roughly four thousand [free folk](free-folk) south of the Wall — the price he extracted from [Tormund](tormund) for sanctuary, and the keystone of his policy of settling the wildlings to garrison the abandoned castles along the Wall. Jon himself names it "Madness" even as he commits to it, knowing "once the gate was opened there would be no turning back." The decision crystallizes the bone-deep disapproval of his conservative officers, above all [Bowen Marsh](bowen-marsh). It is one of the accumulating provocations that move the conspirators toward the mutiny that ends in [Jon's stabbing](jon-is-stabbed-repeatedly).
