## Identity

On the last night before the [Iron Fleet](iron-fleet) reaches Meereenese waters, [Victarion Greyjoy](victarion-greyjoy) brings forth [Dragonbinder](dragonbinder) — Euron's hellhorn, found amongst the smoking wastes of Valyria — for the red priest [Moqorro](moqorro) to read. Moqorro translates the three Valyrian glyphs banded into its steel: the horn names itself ("I am Dragonbinder"), warns that "No mortal man shall sound me and live," and instructs that "Blood for fire, fire for blood" — whoever masters the horn must claim it *with blood*, and the dragons will come to the horn's master regardless of who blows it. The revelation gives Victarion the mechanism by which he means to bind a dragon to his will, and seeds his eventual plan to wield the horn against both his enemies and his brother Euron. The in-saga terminus of Dragonbinder's story in the published text (the actual sounding is TWOW).
