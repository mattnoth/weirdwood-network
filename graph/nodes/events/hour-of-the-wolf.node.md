## Origins

Despite the [death](wiki:Flight_to_Dragonstone) of Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) at [Dragonstone](wiki:Flight_to_Dragonstone) in the tenth moon of [130 AC](wiki:130_AC), and the [greens](wiki:Greens) retaking [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) after the [Moon of the Three Kings](wiki:Moon_of_the_Three_Kings), Rhaenyra's supporters, the [blacks](wiki:Blacks), continued to fight in her name against King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Indeed in [131 AC](wiki:131_AC), three blacks armies were marching on King's Landing: from [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) an army of northmen led by Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark), from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) an army of rivermen led by Lord [Elmo Tully](wiki:Elmo_Tully) and from [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown) an army of Valemen commanded by Lord [Leowyn Corbray](wiki:Leowyn_Corbray) and his brother Ser [Corwyn Corbray](wiki:Corwyn_Corbray) in the name of Lady [Jeyne Arryn](wiki:Jeyne_Arryn).(wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Lord Elmo died during the march. Command of the army fell to his sons, Lord [Kermit](wiki:Kermit_Tully) and [Oscar Tully](wiki:Oscar_Tully), as well as the young Lord [Benjicot Blackwood](wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood). After their victory in the [Battle of the Kingsroad](wiki:Battle_of_the_Kingsroad) against Lord [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon), Oscar was [knighted](wiki:Knight) and the three young lordlings were called the "[Lads](wiki:Lads)" by their men.(wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

As the Lads' [rivermen](wiki:Rivermen) and Cregan's [northmen](wiki:North) approached [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), who had been urged by his councillors to surrender and [take the black](wiki:Night's_Watch), was found [poisoned](wiki:Poison).(wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

## Aftermath

Lord Cregan resigned the day after the executions, having served officially as Hand for only a day. He returned to the North, although not all of his army did. Many were surplus men whose return would cause their families to starve during winter. [Widow Fairs](wiki:Widow_Fairs) in the riverlands helped hundreds of northerners meet women who had lost husbands during the Dance, while others sailed across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) with [Hallis Hornwood](wiki:Hallis_Hornwood) and [Timotty Snow](wiki:Timotty_Snow) and founded the [Wolf Pack](wiki:Wolf_Pack), a [free company](wiki:Free_companies).(wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6)(wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Lords and ladies came from all of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) to attend the [wedding](wiki:Wedding) and coronation of Prince Aegon. On the seventh day of the seventh moon of [131 AC](wiki:131_AC), the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Regency_of_Aegon_III)) wed Prince Aegon to Princess [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen) in a ceremony at the top of [Visenya's Hill](wiki:Visenya's_Hill). Later that same day, Aegon was coronated at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) and proclaimed "Aegon of House Targaryen, the Third of His Name, [King of the Andals, the Rhoynar, and the First Men](wiki:King_of_the_Andals,_the_Rhoynar,_and_the_First_Men) and [Lord of the Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Lord_of_the_Seven_Kingdoms)".(wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Though officially crowned, Aegon was eleven years old at the start of his reign. A [council of regents](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III) was established to rule in his place during his [minority](wiki:Customs).(wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)
