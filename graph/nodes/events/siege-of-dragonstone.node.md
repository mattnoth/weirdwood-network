## Narrative Arc

Lord [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne) leads the [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet) in transporting two thousand [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) men(wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) across [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay) to the [isle of Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)). Paxter has his men encamp before the walls of the [castle of Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) He intends to take the castle bloodlessly by either starving the defenders out or creating a breach in the walls through mining and forcing the defenders to surrender.

With the development of [ironmen](wiki:Ironmen) raiding the [Reach](wiki:Reach) after the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), there is more pressure on the besiegers to end the conflict quickly to free up the Redwyne fleet. Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) successfully asks Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) for the command, as he believes Paxter is taking too long. Cersei agrees, hoping that Loras will die in the battle.(wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Aftermath

Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) is reportedly gravely wounded(wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) and dying on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)).(wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) According to Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), his son's men found no wealth or [dragon eggs](wiki:Dragon_egg) on the isle.(wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

With Dragonstone taken, Lord [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne) (now [grand admiral](wiki:Master_of_ships)) is free to return to the [Reach](wiki:Reach) with the [Arbor fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet) to defend against King [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy)'s ironborn. It also removes the last direct threat to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) and causes further damage to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s already failing cause.
