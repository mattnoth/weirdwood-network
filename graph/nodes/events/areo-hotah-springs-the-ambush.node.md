# Areo Hotah springs the ambush

Acting on Doran Martell's foreknowledge of the Queenmaker plot, Captain Areo Hotah and
a dozen of the prince's guardsmen lie in wait aboard a poleboat at the Greenblood and
spring an ambush on Arianne's party as they flee to crown Myrcella. Hotah calls on
Arianne to yield "by your father's word"; Ser Arys Oakheart charges to his death rather
than surrender; and in the confusion Darkstar maims the princess. Arianne and her
fellow conspirators are taken.
