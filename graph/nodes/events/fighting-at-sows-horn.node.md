## Origins

Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch) leads the attempted [burning of Sow's Horn](wiki:Burning_of_Sow's_Horn). Following [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark)'s release of Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), Lord [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark), who had two of his sons killed by Jaime, offers the hand of his maiden daughter [Alys](wiki:Alys_Karstark) in marriage to anyone who can find the Kingslayer. Karstark men raid across the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) and [crownlands](wiki:Crownlands) looking for Jaime.(wiki:Fighting_at_Sow's_Horn.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

Karstark men looking for Jaime around Sow's Horn are defeated by the men of House Hogg, resulting in three Karstarks being killed and buried.(wiki:Fighting_at_Sow's_Horn.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
