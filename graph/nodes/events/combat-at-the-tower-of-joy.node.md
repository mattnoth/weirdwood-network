## Origins

According to a semi-canon source,(wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_lyanna_stark.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_rhaegar_targaryen.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) kept [Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark) at the tower of joy after her abduction near [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) sent [Gerold Hightower](wiki:Gerold_Hightower), [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard), to retrieve Rhaegar. The prince returned to the [crownlands](wiki:Crownlands), but left Gerold, Ser [Arthur Dayne](wiki:Arthur_Dayne), and Ser [Oswell Whent](wiki:Oswell_Whent) to guard Lyanna at the tower.(wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_rhaegar_targaryen.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

After the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing), Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) rode south to lift the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End). Lords [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) and [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne) quickly dipped their banners.(wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) After [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), Eddard rode south to the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).
