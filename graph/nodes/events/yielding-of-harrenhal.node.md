## Narrative Arc

With the rivermen defeated in the [battle under the walls of Riverrun](wiki:Battle_under_the_walls_of_Riverrun), Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) and Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) march on the various castles of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). After controlling the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad), Tywin heads to [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), with his men burning the land while they march north.(wiki:Yielding_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Lady [Shella Whent](wiki:Shella_Whent) surrenders her great castle to the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) because she lacks the men to hold it.(wiki:Yielding_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Aftermath

Harrenhal is granted by the Iron Throne to [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) and the newly-made [House Slynt](wiki:House_Slynt) for his role in the [arrest of Eddard Stark](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark).(wiki:Yielding_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Tywin uses Harrenhal as his base during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Yielding_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
