## Identity

Reading Grand Maester Malleon's lineage tome, Eddard Stark works out the secret Jon Arryn died for: King Robert's three children by Cersei — Joffrey, Tommen, and Myrcella — are not Robert's at all but the incestuous issue of Cersei and her brother Jaime ("the gold yielding before the coal"). Ned confronts Cersei in the godswood, where she confirms it, and — out of misplaced honor — warns her to flee with the children before he lays the truth before Robert. This is the ROOT engine of the AGOT endgame: the discovery is what makes Ned a mortal threat to Cersei and drives both his fatal moves and her decision to destroy him. The graph carried the consequences (arrest, execution) but not the cause; this beat anchors it.
