## Identity

In the hours after [Renly Baratheon was killed by the shadow](shadow-assassination-of-renly) outside [Storm's End](storms-end), the bulk of his great host — the stormlords and the lords who had ridden to Storm's End — went over to his brother [Stannis](stannis-baratheon) "banner-and-blade," seeing no other Baratheon to turn to. Overnight Stannis gained "all the power of Storm's End and Highgarden, the power that had been Renly's an hour ago," and with it the numbers he had wholly lacked to threaten [King's Landing](kings-landing). Not all submitted — [Loras Tyrell](loras-tyrell), Randyll Tarly, and Mathis Rowan led a fifth of the knights away rather than kneel — but the absorbed host is what put Stannis on the road to the [Blackwater](battle-of-the-blackwater). It is the military substance Renly's murder transferred to Stannis "with a single evil stroke."
