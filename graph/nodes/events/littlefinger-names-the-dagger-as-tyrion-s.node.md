## Identity

In King's Landing, Petyr "Littlefinger" Baelish tells Catelyn Stark that the assassin's Valyrian-steel dagger was won from him by Tyrion Lannister in a wager on Prince Joffrey's name-day tourney. This is a lie (the dagger's true provenance is later shown to be Littlefinger's own fabrication), and it is the **actual causal hinge** of Tyrion's arrest: without the false attribution, Catelyn has a suspicious blade but no named suspect. The catspaw's failed attack supplied the dagger; Littlefinger's lie aimed it at Tyrion.
