## Origins

The [Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Aenys_I)) was a [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) [outlaw](wiki:Outlaw) who gathered thousands of followers to rise against King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen); the outlaw wished to avenge [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) for the destruction wrought upon her by [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War). Although [Deria Martell](wiki:Deria_Martell), the [Princess of Dorne](wiki:Princess_of_Dorne), denounced the Vulture King, she did not take to the field. The Vulture King managed to become the largest of the four rebellions King Aenys I faced during the first year of his reign.(wiki:Second_Dornish_War.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

The Vulture King scored a number of early victories against the [Marcher lords](wiki:Marcher_lords), and his support swelled to more than thirty thousand men, most of them [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk). With such a large host, the Vulture King decided to divide his forces in two, sending one half to besiege [Stonehelm](wiki:Stonehelm) under the command of Lord [Walter Wyl](wiki:Walter_Wyl) while marching ahead the other half against [Nightsong](wiki:Nightsong) and [Horn Hill](wiki:Horn_Hill). Unable to capture Nightsong, the Vulture King halted his siege and began to march west.(wiki:Second_Dornish_War.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Aftermath

To show his gratitude for their services, King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen) rewarded [Davos Baratheon](wiki:Davos_Baratheon), Lord [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly_(lord)), Lady [Ellyn Caron](wiki:Ellyn_Caron), and Lord [Harmon Dondarrion](wiki:Harmon_Dondarrion) with gold, offices and honors.(wiki:Second_Dornish_War.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)
