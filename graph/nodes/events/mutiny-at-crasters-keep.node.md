## Origins

The [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) has been losing strength for years with fewer volunteers every season, so it increasingly relies on the dungeons of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) to fill the ranks. Many disreputable individuals are selected for the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), and the forced march further [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) makes them even more disgruntled.(wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

[Chett](wiki:Chett) and a few other brothers plan to betray [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), the [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), and his senior commanders so they can escape the Watch, but the combination of the heavy weather and the assault from [wights](wiki:Wights) at the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men) make this plan redundant.(wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Around sixty men(wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) retreat from the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist), but some die from wounds, wander off, or are attacked by wights or [Others](wiki:Others).(wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Forty-four men reach Craster's Keep, but their morale is low and certain brothers start being more vocal with their criticism. After several days, [Bannen](wiki:Bannen) and three others have died, leaving thirty-two men capable of fighting and eight incapacitated by wounds or [illness](wiki:Illness).(wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Aftermath

Fearing they will be killed by mutineers, [Grenn](wiki:Grenn), [Dolorous Edd](wiki:Dolorous_Edd), [Bedwyck](wiki:Bedwyck), [Dywen](wiki:Dywen) and other outnumbered loyalists admit defeat and retreat back to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) to report on the disastrous expedition. [Grenn](wiki:Grenn) urges [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) to come with them but he does not move. Of note, [Sweet Donnel Hill](wiki:Donnel_Hill) was complicit in [Chett](wiki:Chett)'s plan to betray their brothers on the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men), but it appears after the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist) he decided to remain loyal to the Watch. Approximately a dozen men return to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) before the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black).(wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Other men, such as [Jarman Buckwell](wiki:Jarman_Buckwell), also return to Castle Black from [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

[Rolley of Sisterton](wiki:Rolley_of_Sisterton) breaks his neck after falling from the loft while chasing after [Craster's wives](wiki:Craster's_wives), and [Garth of Greenaway](wiki:Garth_of_Greenaway) kills [Garth of Oldtown](wiki:Garth_of_Oldtown), though Sam does not remember why.(wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) [Dirk](wiki:Dirk) and [Ollo Lophand](wiki:Ollo_Lophand) tear up the walls of Craster's Keep looking for food,(wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) and Ollo rapes one of the wives on a table.(wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) While mutineers gorge in Craster's cellar or rape his younger wives, the shocked Sam sits with the dying [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont)'s head in his lap. Jeor tells Sam to make for the [Wall](wiki:Wall), instructing him to inform Castle Black of what happened. The Lord Commander's dying wish is for his exiled son, Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont), to take the black. Encouraged to escape by [Ferny](wiki:Ferny) and [Gilly's mother](wiki:Gilly's_mother), Sam flees [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep) with [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) and [her baby](wiki:Monster).(wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

Nine mutineers remain at Craster's Keep with his food and wives.(wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Five betrayers later wander in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest), planning to head south over the [Wall](wiki:Wall) for a warmer climate. However, they are killed by [Coldhands](wiki:Coldhands) and his [ravens](wiki:Raven). [Summer](wiki:Summer), the [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) of [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark), finds the bodies and eats parts of the meat.(wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
