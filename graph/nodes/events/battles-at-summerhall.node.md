## Origins

After the [taking of Gulltown](wiki:Taking_of_Gulltown), Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) returned to [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) and called his bannermen in rebellion against King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen). However, some of the storm lords remained loyal to Aerys and, encouraged by Lord [Owen Merryweather](wiki:Owen_Merryweather),(wiki:Battles_at_Summerhall.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_fall_of_the_dragons:_robert.27s_rebellion.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), they rejected Robert's commands. Lords [Cafferen](wiki:Lord_Cafferen), [Fell](wiki:Lord_Fell), and [Grandison](wiki:Lord_Grandison) were going to join forces at [Summerhall](wiki:Summerhall), a ruined castle of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) in the [Dornish Marches](wiki:Dornish_Marches), and then march on Storm's End to confront their liege lord. Robert, catching wind of it, raced with his forces to meet them.

## Narrative Arc

Not much is known about the battle, only that Robert managed to arrive first and won three battles in a single day, defeating the forces of the three loyalist houses in turn as they arrived. Additionally, Robert killed [Lord Fell](wiki:Lord_Fell) in single combat.(wiki:Battles_at_Summerhall.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Aftermath

Robert consolidated his power in the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands), winning the support of the rest of the storm lords, which allowed him to march towards the [Reach](wiki:Reach) with no enemy forces loose in his rear.

The late Lord Fell's son, [Silveraxe](wiki:Silveraxe), was captured, as were [Lord Grandison](wiki:Lord_Grandison) and [Lord Cafferen](wiki:Lord_Cafferen) were captured. Taken to Storm's End as prisoners, the three joined the rebel cause after some time spent in captivity. Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) killed Cafferen in the [battle of Ashford](wiki:Battle_of_Ashford), while Grandison died after the war from wounds taken in the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Battles_at_Summerhall.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Battles_at_Summerhall.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
