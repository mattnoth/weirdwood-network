## Origins

[Styr](wiki:Styr), [Magnar of Thenn](wiki:Magnar_of_Thenn), dies during his [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black),(wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) host led by [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder), [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall), is defeated in the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall).(wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Styr's son [Sigorn](wiki:Sigorn), the new leader of the [Thenns](wiki:Thenns), submits to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Harrion Karstark](wiki:Harrion_Karstark), heir to [Karhold](wiki:Karhold) after the death of his father, Lord [Rickard](wiki:Rickard_Karstark), is held captive at [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) on behalf of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Rickard's uncle [Arnolf](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark), the [castellan](wiki:Castellan) of Karhold, declares for Stannis instead of Tommen, hoping that when the Iron Throne learns of this they will execute Harrion, thus making Harrion's sister [Alys](wiki:Alys_Karstark) the new heir to Karhold.(wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Arnolf's plan is to wed his own son, [Cregan Karstark](wiki:Cregan_Karstark), to Alys and thereby claim Karhold.(wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Arnolf's declaration for Stannis is also a plot orchestrated alongside Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton); their plan is to turn against Stannis once [his forces reach Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell).(wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Roose's legitimized son, [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), is to wed a girl claimed to be "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)", though his bride-to-be is actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole).(wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
[Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) has a [vision](wiki:Vision) of a girl fleeing a marriage on a dying horse. She tells [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), that it is his half-sister Arya coming to him at Castle Black.(wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Rather than Arya Stark, the girl fleeing toward Castle Black is actually Alys Karstark. [Ty](wiki:Ty) and [Dannel](wiki:Dannel) find her south of [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town) and escort her to the [Wall](wiki:Wall). Alys tells her story to Jon, explaining that she fled Karhold before she could be forcefully wed to Cregan.(wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) Cregan has been pursuing Alys, but his party is intercepted by Lord Commander Snow and the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) the day after Alys's arrival at the Wall.(wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) To ensure Alys's safety and to help integrate the two hundred Thenns into the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), Jon arranges for Alys to wed Sigorn. Ser [Patrek of King's Mountain](wiki:Patrek_of_King's_Mountain) is displeased, as he hoped to wed Alys himself.(wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
