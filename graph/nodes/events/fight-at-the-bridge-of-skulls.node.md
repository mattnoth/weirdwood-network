## Origins

[Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder), the [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall), leads the gathered [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) south, hoping to lead them through the gates at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) after having learned that the bulk of the fighting force of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) has been wiped out in the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist) by the [Others](wiki:Others). In order to do so, he first plans to weaken the strength of the brothers of the Night's Watch who still remain at the castle.(wiki:Fight_at_the_Bridge_of_Skulls.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Fight_at_the_Bridge_of_Skulls.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Mance sends several small raiding parties to attack various points along the [Wall](wiki:Wall). This prompts [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh), the [Lord Steward](wiki:Lord_Steward) and [castellan](wiki:Castellan) of Castle Black, to march with the garrison, taking most of the fighting men still remaining there. Bowen leaves only the old, the green, and the sickly behind to defend Castle Black.(wiki:Fight_at_the_Bridge_of_Skulls.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Bowen chases wildlings to the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) and beyond, into the [Gorge](wiki:Gorge).(wiki:Fight_at_the_Bridge_of_Skulls.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) As Castle Black prepares for the [attack from the south](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) by [Styr](wiki:Styr) and his men, [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye) sends riders after Bowen to fetch them back.(wiki:Fight_at_the_Bridge_of_Skulls.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

When the [Weeper](wiki:Weeper) tries to cut his way across the [Gorge](wiki:Gorge)(wiki:Fight_at_the_Bridge_of_Skulls.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) with three hundred [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) raiders,(wiki:Fight_at_the_Bridge_of_Skulls.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) and the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) confront them on the [Bridge of Skulls](wiki:Bridge_of_Skulls). It is a bloody battle, with more than a hundred brothers of the Watch slain in the engagement, among them Ser [Endrew Tarth](wiki:Endrew_Tarth) and Ser [Aladale Wynch](wiki:Aladale_Wynch). Bowen is seriously wounded in the battle.(wiki:Fight_at_the_Bridge_of_Skulls.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

## Aftermath

The wounded [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) has to be carried back to the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower). Ser [Denys Mallister](wiki:Denys_Mallister), the Commander of the Shadow Tower, sends a [raven](wiki:Raven) to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) to inform its defenders that it will take some time before Bowen is fit enough to return. This news arrives as Castle Black is [under assault](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall) from the main wildling force of [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder).(wiki:Fight_at_the_Bridge_of_Skulls.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

When Denys rides east toward Castle Black with Bowen and other survivors from the [Bridge of Skulls](wiki:Bridge_of_Skulls), their column encounters [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) and [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) beneath the Wall.(wiki:Fight_at_the_Bridge_of_Skulls.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

The [Weeper](wiki:Weeper) survives the Bridge of Skulls and remains [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall). [Tormund](wiki:Tormund) later believes that the Weeping Man will attempt to cross the bridge again.(wiki:Fight_at_the_Bridge_of_Skulls.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
