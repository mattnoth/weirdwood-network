## Appearances & Description

The Stone Drum received its name from the booming and rumbling its walls make during during storms.(wiki:Stone_Drum.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) A high stone bridge arches over the yard to the massive tower.(wiki:Stone_Drum.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Located atop the Stone Drum is the Chamber of the Painted Table, a large round room with black stone walls. Its four windows, tall and narrow, look out upon the points of the compass.(wiki:Stone_Drum.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Each window has wooden shutters and heavy velvet curtains, and the room has a hearth as well.(wiki:Stone_Drum.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The [Painted Table](wiki:Painted_Table) of [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) is found in the center of the chamber.(wiki:Stone_Drum.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
While Dragonstone has a dragon-shaped [Great Hall](wiki:Great_Hall_(Dragonstone)) for large gatherings,(wiki:Stone_Drum.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) the Stone Drum has its own great hall as well.(wiki:Stone_Drum.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Origins

[Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) commissioned the [Painted Table](wiki:Painted_Table) years before [the Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Stone_Drum.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) In [37 AC](wiki:37_AC), King Aegon I died of a stroke in the Chamber of the Painted Table while explaining his victories to his grandsons, [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) and [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)).(wiki:Stone_Drum.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
[Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) claimed the crown after the death of his elder brother, King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen). Aenys's widow, [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon), fled to [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark) with their children, and Maegor responded by dictating letters in the Chamber of the Painted Table.(wiki:Stone_Drum.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and his sister-wife, Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), received visitors in the Chamber of the Painted Table when the king resided at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Stone_Drum.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
According to Maester [Culiper](wiki:Culiper), [Androw Farman](wiki:Androw_Farman) would spend entire days moving wooden soldiers about the Painted Table. After admitting to several murders at Dragonstone, Androw leapt to his death from the chamber.(wiki:Stone_Drum.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) kept her chambers in the Stone Drum. When she argued with her daughter, [Aerea Targaryen](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen), their quarrel rang throughout the tower.(wiki:Stone_Drum.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the crippled King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) rested in the great hall of the Stone Drum after feeding Grand Maester [Gerardys](wiki:Gerardys) to [Sunfyre](wiki:Sunfyre).(wiki:Stone_Drum.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Maester [Cressen](wiki:Cressen) meets with [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) in the Chamber of the Painted Table.(wiki:Stone_Drum.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
After burning statues of the [Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) on the shore of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)), Stannis holds a war council atop the Stone Drum. After meeting with his bannermen, Stannis tasks Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) with spreading his letters along the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea).(wiki:Stone_Drum.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan) informs Davos that Stannis has been brooding in the Stone Drum after being defeated in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Stone_Drum.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
After being released from Dragonstone's dungeon, Davos is brought before Stannis in the Chamber of the Painted Table. The Onion Knight advises against Ser [Axell Florent](wiki:Axell_Florent)'s plan to attack [Claw Isle](wiki:Claw_Isle), and Stannis decides to name Davos as his [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:Stone_Drum.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Stannis later discusses the deaths of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) and [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) in the chamber.(wiki:Stone_Drum.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Davos waits for Stannis in the Chamber of the Painted Table after helping [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm) escape Dragonstone without the king's knowledge.(wiki:Stone_Drum.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
