## Origins

### Ironmen and Northmen[ ]

Bear Island has long been disputed by the [north](wiki:North) and the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands).(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_north:_the_mountain_clans.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_iron_islands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Thousands of years ago,(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-8) Bear Island was ruled by [House Woodfoot](wiki:House_Woodfoot) prior to its conquest by the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn).(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) The ironmen of antiquity controlled the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea) from Bear Island and the [Frozen Shore](wiki:Frozen_Shore) as far south as the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor).(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_iron_islands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
The island was used as a reaving base by [Ravos Hoare](wiki:Ravos_Hoare), the son of [Harrag Hoare](wiki:Harrag_Hoare), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands). [Theon Stark](wiki:Theon_Stark), a [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), drove the ironborn from Bear Island during his reign, slaying Ravos himself.(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
The driftwood king [Loron Greyjoy](wiki:Loron_Greyjoy) retook Bear Island, but it was again lost to the ironborn after the Old Kraken's death. King [Rodrik Stark](wiki:Rodrik_Stark) is said to have won the island in a wrestling match and granted it to [House Mormont](wiki:House_Mormont),(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) although some scholars are skeptical of the story.(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_north:_the_mountain_clans.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) This presumably occurred in the century after [Gerold Lannister](wiki:Gerold_Lannister_(king)), [King of the Rock](wiki:King_of_the_Rock), raided the Iron Islands, when weaker driftwood kings lost most ironborn possessions along western [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Lord [Torrhen Stark](wiki:Torrhen_Stark) sent a few longships from Bear Island to aid King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) in his campaign against the Iron Islands.(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragons_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
[Elissa Farman](wiki:Elissa_Farman) sailed as far as Bear Island and the Arbor by the age of twenty.(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_or_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### Jorah Mormont[ ]

Lord [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) joined the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) to allow his son, [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont), to rule Bear Island. Jorah ruled the island with his wife, [Lynesse Hightower](wiki:Lynesse_Hightower), but she decided that the remote isle was no place for a [Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) of [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). After Jorah was caught selling poachers to a [slaver](wiki:Slavery) to support Lynesse's lifestyle,(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), the liege lord of the north, traveled to Bear Island to execute Jorah or force him to take the black. However, the disgraced Jorah had already taken ship into exile with his wife by the time Ned arrived.(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Bear Island thus passed to Jeor's sister, Lady [Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont).(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-Ragot21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lady [Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont) leads the levy from Bear Island that joins the army of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark).(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) She is accompanied by three of her daughters, [Dacey](wiki:Dacey_Mormont), [Lyra](wiki:Lyra_Mormont), and [Jorelle](wiki:Jorelle_Mormont).(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) intends to restore Bear Island to the exiled Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) if she gains the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Robb, [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), sends Maege into the [Neck](wiki:Neck) to find Lord [Howland Reed](wiki:Howland_Reed). In case of capture, Maege is to claim to be returning to Bear Island.(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Dacey, the heir to Bear Island, is killed alongside Robb in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sends a [raven](wiki:Raven) to Bear Island, in a plea for help defending [Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) from [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings).(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) demands allegiance from the northmen, ten-year-old [Lyanna Mormont](wiki:Lyanna_Mormont), Maege's youngest daughter, reaffirms Bear Island's support for [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark).(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) However, [Alysane Mormont](wiki:Alysane_Mormont), the new heir to Bear Island, joins Stannis's army after the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte) and the defeat of [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy)'s longships.(wiki:Bear_Island.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
