## Origins

### Foundation

Pentos was founded by merchants, traders, seafarers, and farmers from the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) as a trading outpost. Many were [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) who bred with local [Andals](wiki:Andals), so the Pentoshi are less protective of [Valyrian](wiki:Valyria) blood than other [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_pentos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-9) There are some who claim Pentos existed before the colonization and that the original inhabitants decided to pay homage to the Freehold, citing [Gessio Haratis](wiki:Gessio_Haratis)'s *[Before the Dragons](wiki:Before_the_Dragons)* as their source.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_valyria.27s_children.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

Pentos grew to control almost all of [Andalos](wiki:Andalos), with domains stretching from the [Velvet Hills](wiki:Velvet_Hills) and the [Little Rhoyne](wiki:Little_Rhoyne) to the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_pentos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-10)

### Century of Blood

As the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood) slowly came to an end, Pentos joined [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) in their resistance to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), which had conquered [Lys](wiki:Lys) and [Myr](wiki:Myr) in a Volantene attempt to seize control of all Old Valyria had left behind.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), Lord of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), was invited to Pentos, where he agreed to support Pentos and Tyrosh. Atop [Balerion](wiki:Balerion), Aegon used [dragonfire](wiki:Dragonfire) to destroy a Volantene fleet at Lys.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_doom_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2) He later became the first [Lord of the Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Lord_of_the_Seven_Kingdoms) through [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### Targaryen Politics

In [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), in [39 AC](wiki:39_AC), Prince [Maegor Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) was exiled by his brother, King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aenys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) to Pentos.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) When Aenys died in [41 AC](wiki:41_AC), Dowager Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) flew to Pentos to retrieve her exiled son. Maegor's second wife, Queen [Alys Harroway](wiki:Alys_Harroway), remained in Pentos, arriving in Westeros after his [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven). With her came a Pentoshi beauty called [Tyanna](wiki:Tyanna), who was rumored to have been the bastard daughter of a magister, and who would become Maegor's third wife.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1) Lords [Buckwell](wiki:House_Buckwell) and [Rollingford](wiki:House_Rollingford) sailed to Pentos after Maegor's death in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Grand Maester [Benifer](wiki:Benifer) also sought exile in Pentos for a time.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

Twenty-two Pentoshi [magisters](wiki:Magisters) attended the [Golden Wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) Although he was the richest man of Pentos, the lowborn [Rego Draz](wiki:Rego_Draz) was unable to join the council of magisters. Rego instead became the [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin) for King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) the following year.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) In [54 AC](wiki:54_AC) [Elissa Farman](wiki:Elissa_Farman) discreetly sailed from [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark) for Pentos and traveled on land to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

Pentos and [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) began a war in [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) which significantly disrupted trade in the narrow sea. Ser [Willam the Wasp](wiki:Willam_the_Wasp) searched for [Balerion](wiki:Balerion) in the [Velvet Hills](wiki:Velvet_Hills), but his party was ambushed by brigands in [56 AC](wiki:56_AC). Jaehaerys refused Lord [Myles Smallwood](wiki:Myles_Smallwood)'s recommendation to punish Pentos.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) In [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) the Tyroshi and Pentoshi requested that Jaehaerys mediate the negotiations to end their dispute. Both the [Prince of Pentos](wiki:Prince_of_Pentos) and the [Archon of Tyrosh](wiki:Archon_of_Tyrosh) traveled to King's Landing to conduct negotiations in person. They concluded "A Treaty of Eternal Peace", but the treaty was not well received and the magisters sacrificed the Prince of Pentos to their gods.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-1) [Roy Connington](wiki:Roy_Connington) fled in exile to Pentos and then Myr after being scandalized with Princess [Saera Targaryen](wiki:Saera_Targaryen).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

The Sea Snake, [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon), sailed to Pentos with his uncle when he was six years old, and he returned to the Free City on his own ship in later years.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

In [115 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) married Lady [Laena Velaryon](wiki:Laena_Velaryon). Since Daemon realized his brother, King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen), would not be pleased when he heard about the marriage, the newlyweds left Westeros and travelled to Pentos instead. The Prince of Pentos entertained them, as the Pentoshi feared the increasing strength of the [Triarchy](wiki:Triarchy), and Pentos saw Daemon as a valuable ally. Though Daemon and Laena travelled elsewhere as well, they had returned to Pentos by the time Laena learned she was pregnant. They remained at a manse outside the city walls as guests of a Pentoshi magister, and their twin daughters, [Baela](wiki:Baela_Targaryen) and [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daemon)), were born in Pentos in [116 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). Daemon and Laena remained for another half a year, after which they returned to [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-1)

Since Daemon had many Pentoshi friends, Prince [Jacaerys Velaryon](wiki:Jacaerys_Velaryon) decided that his younger half-brothers, [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) and [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen), would be fostered with the Prince of Pentos until their mother, Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), had secured the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). The young princes departed on the Pentoshi cog *[Gay Abandon](wiki:Gay_Abandon)* towards the end of [129 AC](wiki:129_AC), but the ship was intercepted shortly before the [Battle of the Gullet](wiki:Battle_of_the_Gullet).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

Representatives from Pentos attended the coronation of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) in [131 AC](wiki:131_AC).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) Pentos joined an alliance with [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) and [Lorath](wiki:Lorath) in order to bring down the Triarchy in the [Daughters' War](wiki:Daughters'_War).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-1) When Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake) sent Ser [Gedmund Peake](wiki:Gedmund_Peake) and Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) to conquer the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones), the islands were occupied by [Racallio Ryndoon](wiki:Racallio_Ryndoon) and Pentoshi [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords). Racallio eventually allied with the [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos) and the Archon of Tyrosh, however, disregarding any Pentoshi claims. The Archon later hired the Pentoshi sellswords for himself.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) A daughter of the Prince of Pentos was the first woman presented during the [Maiden's Day Ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball) in King's Landing.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-1)

During the reign of King [Daeron I Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen), Pentos and [Lys](wiki:Lys) were at war with [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-29) [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Kaeth](wiki:Kaeth) discusses at length in his book, *[Lives of Four Kings](wiki:Lives_of_Four_Kings)*, that because Daeron had entered talks regarding a marriage alliance with Braavos,(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_the_quarrelsome_daughters:_myr.2C_lys.2C_and_tyrosh.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0) concerning the [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos) and one of Daeron's sisters,(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_the_quarrelsome_daughters:_myr.2C_lys.2C_and_tyrosh.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-1) this emboldened the other Free Cities to lend aid to the [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) rebels.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_the_quarrelsome_daughters:_myr.2C_lys.2C_and_tyrosh.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-2)

### Wars with Braavos

During the past two centuries, Pentos lost four of six wars with [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), over [slavery](wiki:Slavery) and control of the lands and waters between the two cities. Four princes were elected and sacrificed within a year during the last war, which ended in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-31) After achieving a victory rumored to have been purchased through bribery, [Nevio Narratys](wiki:Nevio_Narratys), the fifth [Prince of Pentos](wiki:Prince_of_Pentos) within a year, convinced the [magisters](wiki:Magister) to sue for peace. Braavos forced Pentos to abolish slavery and withdraw from the slave trade. Braavos has since limited the Pentoshi military forces and prevented the city from hiring [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_pentos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-11) The magisters have become friendly with Dothraki *[khals](wiki:Khal)*, showering them with gifts whenever they are near the city.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-9)(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_pentos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-12)

### Recent History

In [262 AC](wiki:262_AC), a man now known as the [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince), twenty-three years old at the time, was chosen by the magisters of Pentos to be their new Prince of Pentos, shortly after the beheading of the previous one.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_pentos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-13)(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0) Instead of accepting their offer, the Tattered Prince fled to the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands), later forming the free company called the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_pentos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-14)

While a [bravo](wiki:Bravo) in his youth, [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) protected the despised [eunuch](wiki:Eunuch) [Varys](wiki:Varys) in Pentos. They began to work together,(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) Varys stealing from Pentoshi and Illyrio returning the goods for a fee. As the friends became more influential, Varys began training "[mice](wiki:Little_birds)" to acquire secrets and information. Illyrio became so respected that a cousin of the prince offered him the hand of his daughter in marriage.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4) King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) sent across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) to Pentos for Varys, whom he named [spymaster](wiki:Spymaster), sometime after the death of Lord [Steffon Baratheon](wiki:Steffon_Baratheon) in [278 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5)(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)

Varys, now the [master of whisperers](wiki:Master_of_whisperers) for King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), employs Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) as a spy in Pentos.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0) Illyrio has hosted the exiled [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) and [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) at [his manse](wiki:Illyrio's_manse) for almost six months, but the Pentoshi refer to Viserys as "the beggar king".(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-10) The eunuch slave [Belwas](wiki:Belwas) was sold from [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) to [Qohor](wiki:Qohor), and afterwards to Pentos.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)

## Appearances & Description

### Location

Pentos is a large port city, more populous than [Astapor](wiki:Astapor) on [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Located closest of all the Free Cities to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in Westeros, Pentos is one of the westernmost of the Free Cities.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtloiaf_the_free_cities.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_pentos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The city lies on the [Bay of Pentos](wiki:Bay_of_Pentos) off the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea), with the [Flatlands](wiki:Flatlands) and [Velvet Hills](wiki:Velvet_Hills) to the east.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtloiaf_the_free_cities.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### Layout

Even though Pentos has massive, high walls,(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_pentos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) it is often regarded as the most vulnerable of the Free Cities.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_pentos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) The city has many square brick towers.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Most of the roofing is done in tiles. Pentos has walled estates, such as [manses](wiki:Manse) belonging to Magister [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio's_manse) and Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo's_manse).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) The Sunrise Gate allows the traveller to exit the city to the east, in the direction of the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) The fastest way to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) from Pentos is by sea.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

A large [red temple](wiki:Red_temple) is located in the city,(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) and the [red priests](wiki:Red_priests) are known to sing and light their night fires.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

### People

The Pentoshi descend from [Andals](wiki:Andals), [Valyrians](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold), [slaves](wiki:Slaves), and ancient peoples.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Like the [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) and [Lyseni](wiki:Lys), Pentoshi are great lovers of song, and generous with those who please them.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Some of their men [dye](wiki:Hair_dye), oil, and fork their beards.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rrpgguardiansorder.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Pentos has a much higher population than [Astapor](wiki:Astapor).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The [Pentoshi language](wiki:Pentoshi_language) is a form of [bastard Valyrian](wiki:Bastard_Valyrian).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Numerous gods are worshipped in Pentos,(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) including [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

Pentos is a city where wealth equals power, ruled over by [a prince](wiki:Prince_of_Pentos) with [a council](wiki:Council_of_magisters_(Pentos)) of rich [magisters](wiki:Magister). The prince has a mostly ceremonial function, however, while the magisters rule. The prince, who is chosen from forty families, presides chiefly over balls and feasts.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) He is carried from place to place in a rich palanquin with a handsome guard. Each new year he deflowers two maidens, the maid of the sea and the maid of the fields, to ensure prosperity on land and sea. If there is famine or a war is lost, the magisters [sacrifice](wiki:Blood_sacrifice) the prince and slit his throat to appease the gods, then choose a new prince.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_pentos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4)

[Slavery](wiki:Slavery) was heavily practiced for most of Pentoshi history, but [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) imposed abolition after several wars. Pentoshi ships flout these laws, however, by running Lysene or [Myrish](wiki:Myr) banners when challenged. Pentos is full of "free bond servants", who are collared and branded like slaves in Lys, Myr, or [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh). Although they are free men and women by law, the cost of their food, clothing, and shelter is higher than the value of their service and they become indebted to their masters.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_pentos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5)

Pentos is not allowed by Braavos to have more than twenty warships, nor are they allowed to hire [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords), make contracts with free companies, or maintain an army. As a consequence, Pentoshi are less belligerent than Lyseni, Myrmen, or Tyroshi. The Pentoshi are friendly with the other Free Cities(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_pentos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) and generous with [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) horselords.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_pentos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6) Pentos is known for its [bravos](wiki:Bravo).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Ragot10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### Economy

The Pentoshi are involved in trade, with ships daily passing forth between Pentos and [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_pentos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7) One such galley is the *[Storm Dancer](wiki:Storm_Dancer)*.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Pentos produces a pale amber wine.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-6) Teeming bazaars can be found in Pentos as well.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Slavery has been forbidden in Pentos since its last war with [Braavos](wiki:Braavos). Nonetheless, there are many servants that are slaves in all but name.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_pentos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8)(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7)(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-7) One Pentoshi merchant is Magister [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis), who deals in [spices](wiki:Spices), [gemstones](wiki:Gemstone), and [dragonbone](wiki:Dragonbone), as well as other, less savory things.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-8)

According to a semi-canon source, the square brick towers are controlled by the spice traders of Pentos, who send off their ships to distant locations to trade for saffron and peppers. The poorer population of Pentos, however, struggles, and many become performers, training as singers or tumblers, for multiple generations, perfecting their art.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rrpgguardiansorder.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

A [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* led by Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) arrives at the city, leading the [magisters](wiki:Magister) of Pentos to double the city guard.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0) Magister [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) brokers a marriage between his guest, Princess [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), and Khal Drogo. Daenerys is presented to Drogo at the [nine-towered manse](wiki:Drogo's_manse) Drogo has been given in the city.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-11) [Their wedding ceremony](wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen) is held in a field outside the city.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-1) Drogo, his new bride, and his *khalasar* depart the city shortly after the ceremony, heading for [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)

### A Clash of Kings

[Arstan Whitebeard](wiki:Arstan_Whitebeard) and [Strong Belwas](wiki:Belwas) sail from Pentos to [New Ghis](wiki:New_Ghis) and then on to [Qarth](wiki:Qarth),(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rtloiaf_journeys.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0) where they meet Daenerys with the intent to give her transportation back to Pentos.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-1) Less than a fortnight after her departure from Qarth [Pyat Pree](wiki:Pyat_Pree) and three of his fellow [warlocks](wiki:Warlocks) set out for Pentos seeking Daenerys.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Led by [Groleo](wiki:Groleo), Illyrio's three ships sail from Qarth for Pentos with Daenerys, her people, and her three dragons aboard them. During their journey, however, Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) counsels Daenerys to sail for [Astapor](wiki:Astapor) and buy [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied). Daenerys heeds his counsel, turning away from the original plan to sail to Pentos.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

While in Pentos, [Oppo](wiki:Oppo) and [Penny](wiki:Penny) are approached by [a man](wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack) who wishes to hire them for the wedding of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and Lady [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), on the orders of Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0)

### A Feast for Crows

*[Seastrider](wiki:Seastrider)* sails from [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) to [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh), Pentos, [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale), and [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0) [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) spots two battered Pentoshi cogs in the harbor of [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0) [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) describes Pentos to Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) when *[Cinnamon Wind](wiki:Cinnamon_Wind)* stops at the city.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0)

[Varys](wiki:Varys) smuggles [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea), and the [dwarf](wiki:Dwarf) stays in [Illyrio's manse](wiki:Illyrio's_manse) during his brief sojourn in Pentos.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-9) Illyrio explains he can serve Daenerys best in Pentos, smoothing the way for her return, sending Tyrion to her instead.(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-6) Illyrio had planned for the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) to meet Daenerys and her dragons in Pentos and then invade [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), but that plan went awry when the dragon queen instead sailed for [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0) [Penny](wiki:Penny) later tells Tyrion she and [Oppo](wiki:Oppo) had been hired in Pentos by [a man](wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack) to perform for [Joffrey's wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0)

A trading galley out of Pentos travelling in the [Bay of Seals](wiki:Bay_of_Seals) is commandeered by [Cotter Pyke](wiki:Cotter_Pyke), the commander of [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea), on the orders of [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch).(wiki:Pentos.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-0)

Quentyn meets with the Tattered Prince after Daenerys disappears from Meereen, since Quentyn requires assistance in stealing the remaining two [dragons](wiki:Dragon) locked in the [dragon pit](wiki:Dragon_pit_(Great_Pyramid)).
