## Origins

[Redwyn](wiki:Redwyn), a [ranger](wiki:Ranger) who lived during the reign of King [Dorren Stark](wiki:Dorren_Stark), wrote an account of a ranging from the Shadow Tower to [Lorn Point](wiki:Lorn_Point) on the [Frozen Shore](wiki:Frozen_Shore). During that ranging, they fought [giants](wiki:Giants) and traded with the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest).(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_dawn_age.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Ser [Denys Mallister](wiki:Denys_Mallister) has commanded the Shadow Tower since [267 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) served at the Shadow Tower before he abandoned the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). Mance's black cloak was torn when he was attacked by [shadowcat](wiki:Shadowcat) during a ranging [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall), so a [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) woman mended it with red [silk](wiki:Silk). When Denys ordered Mance to wear an all-black cloak once the ranger returned to the Shadow Tower, Mance deserted the next morning.(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

[Lord Commander Qorgyle](wiki:Lord_Commander_Qorgyle) sent patrols from [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) to the Shadow Tower every second day.(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

A cook at the Shadow Tower raped dozens of [septas](wiki:Septa) before he was caught and sent to the Night's Watch for his crimes. He burned a seven-pointed star into his flesh for each one. His left arm is stars from wrist to elbow, and stars mark his calves as well.(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser [Denys Mallister](wiki:Denys_Mallister) writes that increasing numbers of [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) are slipping past the Shadow Tower,(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) and his rangers find abandoned villages [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

[Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand) ventures forth from the castle in search of [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark), but is unable to find the [First Ranger](wiki:First_Ranger).(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Ser [Endrew Tarth](wiki:Endrew_Tarth) travels from the Shadow Tower to become the new [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms) at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), after Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne) leaves for [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Clash of Kings

[Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), considers re-garrisoning [Stonedoor](wiki:Stonedoor) using men from the Shadow Tower.(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

One hundred men from the Shadow Tower—including lead rangers Qhorin Halfhand, [Ebben](wiki:Ebben), [Dalbridge](wiki:Dalbridge), and [Stonesnake](wiki:Stonesnake)—arrive at the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men) to augment the main force from Castle Black.(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

### A Storm of Swords

Qhorin's second-in-command, [Blane](wiki:Blane), commands the Shadow Tower men in his absence during the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist).(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sends [ravens](wiki:Raven) to [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea) and the Shadow Tower to warn of [Thenns](wiki:Thenns).(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Men from the Shadow Tower make up the bulk of the Night's Watch who [fight at the Bridge of Skulls](wiki:Fight_at_the_Bridge_of_Skulls).(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Maester [Mullin](wiki:Mullin) treats [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) after the battle.(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1)

Denys, Bowen, and other men from the Shadow Tower ride toward Castle Black, picking up [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) and [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) en route.(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

After the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), Shadow Tower men are housed in Castle Black's [Lance](wiki:Lance_(tower)) tower.(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

The [Weeper](wiki:Weeper) is said to be gathering free folk to cross the [Bridge of Skulls](wiki:Bridge_of_Skulls) and threaten the Shadow Tower.(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Radwdprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

A grizzled [steward](wiki:Steward) from the Shadow Tower is re-assigned as commander of the newly-formed garrison at [Greyguard](wiki:Greyguard).(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

Denys sends regular ravens to Castle Black requesting additional men for the Shadow Tower.(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) Lord Commander [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) re-assigns [Halder](wiki:Halder) and [Toad](wiki:Toad) to meet his demands, as well as ten untrained men from [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town).(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1)(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

The garrison of the Shadow Tower spot multiple fires in the night north of the Gorge. Denys believes [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) are amassing to force the Bridge of Skulls once more.(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) When free folk are allowed to cross the Wall at Castle Black, sixty [hostages](wiki:Hostage) are sent to the Shadow Tower and Eastwatch.(wiki:Shadow_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
