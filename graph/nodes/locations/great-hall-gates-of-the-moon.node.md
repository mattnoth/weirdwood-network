## Appearances & Description

The back of the Great Hall has a pillared gallery which connects to tower stairs. A staircase built into the thick western wall leads from the gallery to the inner ward of the castle.(wiki:Great_Hall_(Gates_of_the_Moon).cite_ref-TWOW_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

The [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) hosts a [tourney](wiki:Tourney) to determine the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights). Serving men set up trestle tables for the evening feast, and their wives and daughters sweep up old rushes and scatter fresh ones. [Alayne Stone](wiki:Sansa_Stark) sees Lord [Nestor Royce](wiki:Nestor_Royce) showing to Lady [Waxley](wiki:House_Waxley) tapestries which once belonged to the late King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Great_Hall_(Gates_of_the_Moon).cite_ref-TWOW_1-2)
Since sixty-four men are to compete in the upcoming tourney, sixty-four dishes are served in the feast. After the meal is completed, the trestle tables are removed so that the guests can dance in the hall.(wiki:Great_Hall_(Gates_of_the_Moon).cite_ref-TWOW_1-3)

## Quotes

Sixty-four dishes were served, in honor of the sixty-four competitors who had come so far to contest for [silver wings](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights) before [their lord](wiki:Robert_Arryn). From the rivers and the lakes came [pike](wiki:Pike) and [trout](wiki:Trout) and [salmon](wiki:Salmon), from the seas [crabs](wiki:Crab) and [cod](wiki:Cod) and [herring](wiki:Herring). [Ducks](wiki:Duck) there were, and [capons](wiki:Capon), [peacocks](wiki:Peacock) in their plumage and [swans](wiki:Swan) in almond milk. Suckling pigs were served up crackling with apples in their mouths, and three huge [aurochs](wiki:Aurochs) were roasted whole above firepits in the castle yard, since they were too big to get through the kitchen doors. Loaves of hot bread filled the trestle tables in Lord [Nestor's](wiki:Nestor_Royce) hall, and massive wheels of [cheese](wiki:Cheese) were brought up from the vaults. The butter was fresh-churned, and there were leeks and carrots, roasted onions, beets, turnips, parsnips. And best of all, Lord Nestor's cooks prepared a splendid subtlety, a [lemon cake](wiki:Lemon_cake) in the shape of the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance), twelve feet tall and adorned with an [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) made of sugar.(wiki:Great_Hall_(Gates_of_the_Moon).cite_ref-TWOW_1-4)
