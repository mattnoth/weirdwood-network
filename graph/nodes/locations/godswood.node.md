## Origins

When the First Men took up the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods) of the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest), they created godswoods, groves within their castles and villages where a single [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood), known as a "[heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree)" and carved with a face, was planted so the gods could be worshipped. In the [north](wiki:North) every castle has a weirwood heart tree with a face. South of the [Neck](wiki:Neck), however, most weirwoods were cut down or burnt years ago.(wiki:Godswood.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) There is a significant number on the [Isle of Faces](wiki:Isle_of_Faces), however, and many [southron](wiki:Southron) castles still have weirwoods within their godswoods.(wiki:Godswood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_arrival_of_the_andals.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
