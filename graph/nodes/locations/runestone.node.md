## Origins

Runestone was the ancient seat of the [Bronze Kings](wiki:Bronze_King) of old from [House Royce](wiki:House_Royce). After defeating seven [Andal](wiki:Andal) longships during the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals), King [Yorwyck VI Royce](wiki:Yorwyck_VI_Royce) placed the heads of his slain foes along the walls of his castle. The Royces submitted to [Artys Arryn](wiki:Artys_Arryn) after the death of King [Robar II Royce](wiki:Robar_II_Royce) in the [Battle of the Seven Stars](wiki:Battle_of_the_Seven_Stars).(wiki:Runestone.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
During the reign of King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen), Princess [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) was seen flying [Dreamfyre](wiki:Dreamfyre) as far as Runestone.(wiki:Runestone.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) visited Runestone during a [royal progress](wiki:Royal_progress) in [52 AC](wiki:52_AC).(wiki:Runestone.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Ser [Victor the Valiant](wiki:Victor_the_Valiant) was part of the household of Runestone before being named to the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) through the [War for the White Cloaks](wiki:War_for_the_White_Cloaks).(wiki:Runestone.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) disliked living at Runestone with his first wife, Lady [Rhea Royce](wiki:Rhea_Royce). He tried to claim Runestone after Rhea's death, however, but his claim was rejected by Lady [Jeyne Arryn](wiki:Jeyne_Arryn). Daemon instead married Lady [Laena Velaryon](wiki:Laena_Velaryon) and fled with her to the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities).(wiki:Runestone.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
[Alyssa Royce](wiki:Alyssa_Royce) traveled from Runestone to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for the [Maiden's Day Cattle Show](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Cattle_Show).(wiki:Runestone.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Ser [Arnold Arryn](wiki:Arnold_Arryn), who had squired at Runestone in his youth, fled to the castle when war broke out in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) after the passing of Lady [Jeyne Arryn](wiki:Jeyne_Arryn).(wiki:Runestone.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Ser [Corwyn Corbray](wiki:Corwyn_Corbray), one of the [regents of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III), was killed by a crossbowman on the walls of Runestone in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC).(wiki:Runestone.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Runestone.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

At Runestone, the [Lords Declarant](wiki:Lords_Declarant) write and sign a document containing their demands for the removal of Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) as [Lord Protector of the Vale](wiki:Lord_Protector_of_the_Vale).(wiki:Runestone.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
During a parley at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), the Lords Declarant attempt to have Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn) become Lord [Yohn Royce](wiki:Yohn_Royce)'s ward at Runestone. Petyr manipulates them into granting him a year to prove his worth, however.(wiki:Runestone.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Bronze Yohn holds [a small mêlée](wiki:Mêlée_at_Runestone) for squires only at Runestone. [Harrold Hardyng](wiki:Harrold_Hardyng) wins and is knighted by Yohn as his reward.(wiki:Runestone.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
