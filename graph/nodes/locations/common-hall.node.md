## Appearances & Description

The immense longhall(wiki:Common_hall.cite_ref-Rasos46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) is drafty even when a fire burns in its great hearth.(wiki:Common_hall.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) [Crows](wiki:Crow) nest in the lofty ceiling timbers.(wiki:Common_hall.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) [Hobb](wiki:Hobb) prepares meals for the members of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch),(wiki:Common_hall.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and black brothers often drink mulled [wine](wiki:Wine) in the hall.(wiki:Common_hall.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Common_hall.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) takes his meals in the common hall.(wiki:Common_hall.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Common_hall.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Jon introduces [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) to [Ghost](wiki:Ghost) in the hall.(wiki:Common_hall.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)
Jon learns from [Pypar](wiki:Pypar) about the death of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). When Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne) mocks Jon about the [arrest of Eddard Stark](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark), Jon leaps onto a table and has to be restrained from attacking Alliser by his friends.(wiki:Common_hall.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

During the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) by [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings), Jon sees the common hall's doors on fire, and then the timbered keep itself burns.(wiki:Common_hall.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Since the common hall has burned to the ground, the Night's Watch take their meals in one of the [vaults of Castle Black](wiki:Vaults_of_Castle_Black), a stone cellar below the [armory](wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black)).(wiki:Common_hall.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
