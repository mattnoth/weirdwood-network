## Origins

As Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole)'s [greens](wiki:Greens) traveled south along the western shore of the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye), the river lords allied with the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) practiced scorched earth tactics, burning their forests and villages. In order to frighten the greens, the blacks arranged dead men in grotesque mockeries of feasts. Prior to the [Butcher's Ball](wiki:Butcher's_Ball), Criston's outriders were attacked at Crossed Elms by men disguised as feasting corpses, losing twelve men before realizing [Black Trombo](wiki:Black_Trombo)'s ruse.(wiki:Crossed_Elms.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)(wiki:Crossed_Elms.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
