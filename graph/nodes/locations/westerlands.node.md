## Origins

### Before the Andals

Little is known of the time before the coming of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men). Like the rest of Westeros, the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) and [giants](wiki:Giants) lived in the westerlands and fought with the First Men when they came. After the [Pact](wiki:Pact) at the [Isle of Faces](wiki:Isle_of_Faces), the children only kept the forests.(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7) The hills of the west were once inhabited by [lions](wiki:Lion), but they are believed to all have been slain.(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

First Men lords, some of whom were petty kings, included the [Baneforts](wiki:House_Banefort), [Brooms](wiki:House_Broom), [Crakehalls](wiki:House_Crakehall), [Farmans](wiki:House_Farman), [Footes](wiki:House_Foote), [Greenfields](wiki:House_Greenfield), [Hawthornes](wiki:House_Hawthorne), [Morelands](wiki:House_Moreland), [Plumms](wiki:House_Plumm), [Reynes](wiki:House_Reyne), [Westerlings](wiki:House_Westerling), and [Yews](wiki:House_Yew).(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8) The coastal First Men were often raided by the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) from the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands).(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

During the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes), the legendary hero and trickster [Lann the Clever](wiki:Lann_the_Clever) acquired [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) in some manner from [House Casterly](wiki:House_Casterly). According to legend he is the ancestor of the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister), who became the [Kings of the Rock](wiki:Kings_of_the_Rock) with Casterly Rock as their seat.(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-9) Westermen believe that Lann made off with some of the inheritance of [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand) by posing as one of his sons.(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

### Kingdom of the Rock

The Lannister Kings of the Rock gradually conquered or acquired neighboring territory, including the lands of the Reynes and the Baneforts. They initially resisted the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals), but later Lannister kings allowed the [Andals](wiki:Andals) to marry into the westermen nobility. New houses formed by the intermarriages included Houses [Brax](wiki:House_Brax), [Drox](wiki:House_Drox), [Jast](wiki:House_Jast), [Kyndall](wiki:House_Kyndall), [Lefford](wiki:House_Lefford), [Marbrand](wiki:House_Marbrand), [Parren](wiki:House_Parren), [Sarsfield](wiki:House_Sarsfield), and [Serrett](wiki:House_Serrett). In contrast to most kings of First Men origin, the support of the Andals allowed the Kings of the Rock to expand their power. When King [Gerold III Lannister](wiki:Gerold_III_Lannister) died without male issue, a council crowned his daughter's husband, Ser Joffrey Lydden, who became the first Andal King of the Rock, [Joffrey Lannister](wiki:Joffrey_Lannister).(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-10)

With Andal support the Lannisters extended their rule to the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth) and [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle) and engaged in border wars with the [Kings of the Trident](wiki:Kings_of_the_Trident) and [Kings of the Reach](wiki:Kings_of_the_Reach).(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-11) The growing strength of House Lannister allowed them to expel the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) from the western coasts, and King [Gerold the Great](wiki:Gerold_Lannister_(king)) raided the Iron Isles and brought hostages back to the Rock.(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

Some of the eastern hills of what is now the westerlands were historically ruled by [Kings of the Rivers and the Hills](wiki:Kings_of_the_Rivers_and_the_Hills), such as the [Mudds](wiki:House_Mudd) and the [Teagues](wiki:House_Teague).(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) Lord [Torrence Teague](wiki:Torrence_Teague) used his spoils after raiding the west in his successful campaign to become river king.(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-1)

Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey) mentioned that a famous relative of his defeated an attack by the Kingdom of the Rock by killing a Lannister king, [Lancel V](wiki:Lancel_V_Lannister).(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

Westermen fended off an attack by [Halleck Hoare](wiki:Halleck_Hoare), [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers).(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

### Targaryen Era

[Loren I Lannister](wiki:Loren_I_Lannister), King of the Rock, mustered his armies to fight [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), [Lord of Dragonstone](wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone), during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest). Allied with [Mern IX Gardener](wiki:Mern_IX_Gardener), King of the Reach, they assembled the enormous Host of the Two Kings. However, they were no match for Targaryen [dragons](wiki:Dragon). King Loren escaped the so-called [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire) and submitted to Aegon. His primacy in the westerlands as [Warden of the West](wiki:Warden_of_the_West) was confirmed by the [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen). Since this time the westerlands have been part of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) controlled by the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)

During the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising), Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) and Princess [Rhaena ](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) were besieged by the [Poor Fellows](wiki:Poor_Fellows) at [Crakehall](wiki:Crakehall). Lord [Lyman Lannister](wiki:Lyman_Lannister) later gave sanctuary and lent Aegon limited support when he challenged his uncle, King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen). Several houses from the westerlands fought for Aegon at the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye), including houses [Farman](wiki:House_Farman), [Tarbeck](wiki:House_Tarbeck), [Parren](wiki:House_Parren) and [Westerling](wiki:House_Westerling). When Prince [Jaehaerys Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) announced his claim to the throne in 48 AC, Lord Lyman was one of the great lords who supported him. (wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the westerlands under Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister) declared for [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen). Jason led his armies into the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), where they fought several battles against the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) until the host was obliterated during the [Battle by the Lakeshore](wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore). Meanwhile, the undefended westerlands were attacked by Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), the Red Kraken, who declared for Queen [Rhaenyra](wiki:Rhaenyra). Dalton subsequently sacked [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) and took [Kayce](wiki:Kayce) and [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle). (wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

After the conclusion of the Dance, the regency council of [Aegon III](wiki:Aegon_III) ordered Dalton to cease the raiding of the westerlands, but the order was ignored.(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_red_kraken.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) The regents sent a fleet under the command of Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) to deal with this rebellion, but Dalton was slain by one of his [salt wives](wiki:Tess) before a battle could be joined. (wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) The ironborn were subsequently driven out from the westerlands, and Lady [Johanna Lannister](wiki:Johanna_Lannister) led a [campaign](wiki:Struggle_for_power_in_the_Iron_Islands) into the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) in retaliation. (wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

During the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), many battles were fought in the west.(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0) [House Tarbeck](wiki:House_Tarbeck) kept a foot in both camps, trying to support [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen) and his rival, [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre).(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1) Ser [Quentyn Ball](wiki:Quentyn_Ball) killed Lord [Lefford](wiki:House_Lefford) and forced Lord [Damon Lannister](wiki:Damon_Lannister_(lord)) into retreat.(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3)

During the [Great Spring Sickness](wiki:Great_Spring_Sickness) the coast of the westerlands suffered under the attacks [Dagon Greyjoy](wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy),(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-2)(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0) the Last Reaver.(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)

### Reyne-Tarbeck revolt

During the rule of Lord [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister), the power of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) was nominal. Tytos was held in low esteem and was largely ignored by his vassals. Lord [Roger Reyne](wiki:Roger_Reyne) took command of the Lannister army after Ser [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold)) was killed in the [War of the Ninepenny Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Ninepenny_Kings).(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-4)

Houses [Reyne](wiki:House_Reyne) and [Tarbeck](wiki:House_Tarbeck) eventually rebelled against the rule of Tytos, but his son, Ser [Tywin](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), led the campaign against the [Reyne-Tarbeck revolt](wiki:Reyne-Tarbeck_revolt). The two families were extinguished and their respective seats of [Castamere](wiki:Castamere) and [Tarbeck Hall](wiki:Tarbeck_Hall) were destroyed. Since this time no House has dared oppose House Lannister in the westerlands. The prestigious Tywin served for a time as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) for King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-5)

### Robert's Rebellion

[Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark), who was betrothed to Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), was apparently abducted by Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen). After the executions of her father, Lord [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark), and brother, [Brandon](wiki:Brandon_Stark), by King Aerys II, [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) broke out against [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen), with Houses [Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn), [Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon), [Stark](wiki:House_Stark), and [Tully](wiki:House_Tully) opposing [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen).

For the better part of the war the westerlands remained neutral. After the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident) and the death of Rhaeger, however, Tywin chose the side of the rebels instead of his one-time friend, Aerys. Tywin ordered the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing) and the killing of the Targaryen heirs to prove House Lannister's support. After being crowned king, Robert I married [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) to bind the Lannisters to his cause.(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)

The [burning of the Lannister fleet](wiki:Burning_of_the_Lannister_fleet) at [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) began [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion).(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1) After the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) failed in the [storming of Seagard](wiki:Storming_of_Seagard), King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) invaded the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) along with his [Warden of the West](wiki:Warden_of_the_West), Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), and his [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark).(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0)

### A Storm of Swords

### A Feast for Crows

Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), the [Lady of Casterly Rock](wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock), succeeds Tywin as the liege lord of the westerlands.(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_56-0) The lords of the west lead a procession of hundreds of soldiers which accompanies Tywin's corpse on its return to the Rock from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Two thousand westermen participate in the [siege of Dragonstone](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone).(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_57-0)

When King [Euron](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) and Lord Captain [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) set out to attack mainland Westeros after Balon's death, the westerlands escapes their wrath, as the Iron Fleet sails past and attacks the [Shield Islands](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields) and the [Reach](wiki:Reach) instead.(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_58-0)

Jaime joins Daven, the new [Warden of the West](wiki:Warden_of_the_West), and other westermen at the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun).(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_59-0) Forley commands the escort of Edmure and Jeyne as they travel from Riverrun to the west.(wiki:Westerlands.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_60-0)
