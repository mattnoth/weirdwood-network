## Appearances & Description

The hall has a stone floor and high, arched windows. There are benches along the wall and a raised dais at the far end. A door behind the dais leads to a turnpike stair.(wiki:Seneschal's_Court.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
Outside of the Seneschal's Court are stocks in which novices can be locked by rectors and then pelted with rotting vegetables by acolytes.(wiki:Seneschal's_Court.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After arriving at the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel), [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) looks at a map at [Scribe's Hearth](wiki:Scribe's_Hearth) for the fastest way to the Seneschal's Court so he can see speak with the Seneschal, who is now Archmaester [Theobald](wiki:Theobald). Once at the hall, Sam is forced to wait for hours by [Lorcas](wiki:Lorcas). [Alleras](wiki:Alleras) arrives and brings Sam to the [Ravenry](wiki:Ravenry).(wiki:Seneschal's_Court.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
