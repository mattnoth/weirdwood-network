## Appearances & Description

Faircastle is located above the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea). The castle has a great hall, tall white towers, and dungeons.(wiki:Faircastle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Faircastle.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Origins

[House Farman](wiki:House_Farman) of Faircastle ruled [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle) as kings during the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes).(wiki:Faircastle.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Following the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye) in [43 AC](wiki:43_AC), [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) fled from King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) to the safety of [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle), where Lord [Marq Farman](wiki:Marq_Farman) granted them hospitality at Faircastle.(wiki:Faircastle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) After hiding her daughters, [Aerea](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen) and [Rhaella](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon)), with trusted allies, Rhaena departed Faircastle in [47 AC](wiki:47_AC) to become one of Maegor the Cruel's [Black Brides](wiki:Black_Brides).(wiki:Faircastle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
Rhaena returned to Faircastle after the coronation of her brother, King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen),(wiki:Faircastle.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and she [wed](wiki:Wedding_of_Rhaena_Targaryen_and_Androw_Farman) [Androw Farman](wiki:Androw_Farman).(wiki:Faircastle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Maester [Smike](wiki:Smike) observed that Rhaena, the so-called Queen in the West, and her friends, the [Four-Headed Beast](wiki:Four-Headed_Beast), held court at Faircastle for visiting [lords](wiki:Lord).(wiki:Faircastle.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) After [Dreamfyre](wiki:Dreamfyre) laid a clutch of [dragon eggs](wiki:Dragon_egg), a [begging brother](wiki:Begging_brother) warned that Fair Isle would be overwhelmed by [dragons](wiki:Dragon). The preacher died in Faircastle's dungeon after being imprisoned by Lord Marq.(wiki:Faircastle.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
After becoming the castle's lord, Ser [Franklyn Farman](wiki:Franklyn_Farman) ordered Rhaena to depart Faircastle, with Androw, [Samantha Stokeworth](wiki:Samantha_Stokeworth), and [Alayne Royce](wiki:Alayne_Royce) following her into exile. [Elissa Farman](wiki:Elissa_Farman) disobeyed her brother's instructions by also departing her home.(wiki:Faircastle.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) Rhaena visited Faircastle on Dreamfyre after Aerea disappeared from [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Faircastle.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Because of Franklyn's poor treatment of Rhaena, King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) did not visit Faircastle during his royal progress to the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) in [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Faircastle.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) The king eventually visited in [88 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), after Lord Franklyn had died.(wiki:Faircastle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Faircastle was captured by [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Faircastle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) The Red Kraken was murdered by [Tess](wiki:Tess) within Lord Farman's bedchamber in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC).(wiki:Faircastle.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_red_kraken.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Although most of Fair Isle was recovered by smallfolk in a bloody rebellion, [Alester Wynch](wiki:Alester_Wynch) and [Gunthor Goodbrother](wiki:Gunthor_Goodbrother) continued to hold Faircastle with an ironborn garrison. The castle fell after Gunthor slew Alester in a feud over [Lysa Farman](wiki:Lysa_Farman).(wiki:Faircastle.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) and his squire, Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen), considered visiting Faircastle and aiding Lord Farman against the ironborn of Lord [Dagon Greyjoy](wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy).(wiki:Faircastle.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Some years after the [Reyne-Tarbeck revolt](wiki:Reyne-Tarbeck_revolt), Lord Farman grew truculent. In response, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) sent an envoy to Faircastle with the instruction to perform "[The Rains of Castamere](wiki:The_Rains_of_Castamere)". The Farmans acquiesced after being reminded of the fate of [Castamere](wiki:Castamere).(wiki:Faircastle.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
