## Culture

Hides and timber from the [north](wiki:North) are brought down the river for trade in White Harbor.(wiki:White_Knife.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) There is ice fishing on the northern rivers.(wiki:White_Knife.cite_ref-8) People fish [salmon](wiki:Salmon) from the White Knife.(wiki:White_Knife.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) counts [Warden of the White Knife](wiki:Warden_of_the_White_Knife) among his titles.(wiki:White_Knife.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Origins

The [Red Kings](wiki:Red_King) of the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort) once controlled land stretching from the [Last River](wiki:Last_River) to the White Knife.(wiki:White_Knife.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
King [Jon Stark](wiki:Jon_Stark) had the [Wolf's Den](wiki:Wolf's_Den) built to defend the mouth of the White Knife against raiders and [slavers](wiki:Slavers).(wiki:White_Knife.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) sailed up the river when they captured the Wolf's Den during the [War Across the Water](wiki:War_Across_the_Water).(wiki:White_Knife.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) King [Brandon Stark](wiki:Brandon_Stark_(Ice_Eyes)) reclaimed the Wolf's Den from slavers from the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) when the White Knife froze during a particularly harsh winter.(wiki:White_Knife.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
Approximately one thousand years before [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) received the Wolf's Den and land at the mouth of the White Knife from [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), which they developed into the city of [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor).(wiki:White_Knife.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)(wiki:White_Knife.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2)
In [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen)'s party sailed from White Harbor to the river's rapids, then continued on through trackless bogs and summer snows to Winterfell.(wiki:White_Knife.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) and Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) travel down the White Knife to then sail from [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:White_Knife.cite_ref-Ragot14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Lord [Halys Hornwood](wiki:Halys_Hornwood) gives gifts to [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) in hopes that Robb will allow [House Hornwood](wiki:House_Hornwood) to dam part of the White Knife.(wiki:White_Knife.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Septon [Chayle](wiki:Chayle) recalls to [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) that he is a strong swimmer after having grown up along the banks of the river.(wiki:White_Knife.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
To help Rodrik recover [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) from [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy), [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly), Lord of White Harbor, has reportedly sent upriver a dozen barges with [knights](wiki:Knight), warhorses, and siege engines.(wiki:White_Knife.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Bran and his companions consider stealing a boat and sailing down the White Knife to seek refuge in White Harbor, but Bran decides they should find the [three-eyed crow](wiki:Three-eyed_crow) [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) instead.(wiki:White_Knife.cite_ref-Rasos9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) includes spearmen from the White Knife in his defense of the [Trident](wiki:Trident).(wiki:White_Knife.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) learns that [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) from along the White Knife and [Hornwood](wiki:Hornwood) have been seeking protection at White Harbor with [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly).(wiki:White_Knife.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) Lord Wyman reveals to Davos that he has warships hidden up the White Knife.(wiki:White_Knife.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)
