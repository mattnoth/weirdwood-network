## Culture

In wintertime, stones in the icy Weeping Water become half frozen.(wiki:Weeping_Water.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) There is ice fishing on the northern rivers.(wiki:Weeping_Water.cite_ref-3)

## Origins

During the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals), [Theon Stark](wiki:Theon_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), allied with the [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton) to crush [Argos Sevenstar](wiki:Argos_Sevenstar) and his [Andals](wiki:Andals) in the [Battle of the Weeping Water](wiki:Battle_of_the_Weeping_Water).(wiki:Weeping_Water.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
While foxhunting along the Weeping Water, Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) raped and impregnated a [miller's wife](wiki:Miller's_wife); the child of the rape was [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow). After returning from the [Redfort](wiki:Redfort), [Domeric Bolton](wiki:Domeric_Bolton) rode up the river to befriend Ramsay. Domeric eventually died of [poison](wiki:Poison).(wiki:Weeping_Water.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) remembers escaping from the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort) with [Kyra](wiki:Kyra), stumbling over stones in the icy river, and kissing her after they reached the other side. The escape turned out to be a hunting game devised by [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), however.(wiki:Weeping_Water.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
