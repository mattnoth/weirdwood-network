## Origins

Blackhaven was burned by the [first Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Aenys_I)), who mutilated Lord [Harmon Dondarrion](wiki:Harmon_Dondarrion). Harmon participated in the [Vulture Hunt](wiki:Vulture_Hunt) which defeated the Vulture King and his rebels.(wiki:Blackhaven.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) greatly enjoyed Blackhaven when [Lord Dondarrion](wiki:Lord_Dondarrion_(father_of_Simon)) and his son, Ser [Simon Dondarrion](wiki:Simon_Dondarrion), hosted her and King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC). Simon captivated the queen with songs of star-crossed lovers and the fall of kings.(wiki:Blackhaven.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) was the son of the steward of Blackhaven.(wiki:Blackhaven.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen) was treated by the castle's [maester](wiki:Maester) during his return from [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Blackhaven.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

When Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) was ten years old, he borrowed armor and entered as a [mystery knight](wiki:Mystery_knight) a tourney that took place at Blackhaven, where he was eventually defeated and unmasked by Prince [Duncan Targaryen](wiki:Duncan_Targaryen). For that, Barristan earned the nickname "the Bold".(wiki:Blackhaven.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Appearances & Description

Blackhaven has black [basalt](wiki:Basalt) walls and a dry moat said to be bottomless.(wiki:Blackhaven.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_the_men_of_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) It is small and modest compared to the great castles of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Blackhaven.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion), Lord of Blackhaven, is reported slain by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) in the [battle at the Mummer's Ford](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford).(wiki:Blackhaven.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Beric is revealed to be the still-living leader of the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), as he was revived by [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr).(wiki:Blackhaven.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Thoros tells [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) that Lord Beric finally died when he, rather than Thoros, revived the murdered [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark).(wiki:Blackhaven.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) The heir to Blackhaven is unknown.
