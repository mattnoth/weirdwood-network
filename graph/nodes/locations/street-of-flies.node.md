## Origins

On the [Feast Day of Our Father Above](wiki:Feast_Day_of_Our_Father_Above) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) for King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), had the dungeons of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) emptied and all the offenders punished publicly. There were so many prisoners that the [King's Justice](wiki:King's_Justice), Ser [Victor Risley](wiki:Victor_Risley), was aided in his task by [Tessario](wiki:Tessario) and his [Fingers](wiki:Fingers_(guards)). The court fool [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom) described the event as looking more like a butchery than execution, and noted that Lord Unwin should have sent for the butchers of the Street of Flies instead of the Hand's Fingers.(wiki:Street_of_Flies.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
