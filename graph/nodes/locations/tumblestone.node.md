## Origins

[Axel Tully](wiki:Axel_Tully) constructed [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) at the juncture of the Tumblestone and the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) on land granted to him by [Armistead Vance](wiki:Armistead_Vance).(wiki:Tumblestone.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
During his conquest of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), [Harwyn Hoare](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), shattered the lines of Ser [Samwell Rivers](wiki:Samwell_Rivers) on the Tumblestone, with hundreds drowning.(wiki:Tumblestone.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) establishes three camps during his siege of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun). During the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) camp on the northern shore of the Tumblestone is attacked by Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully). Lord [Andros Brax](wiki:Andros_Brax) drowns while trying to cross the river to aid the Lannister camp.(wiki:Tumblestone.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

In preparation for the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords),(wiki:Tumblestone.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) the [Darrys](wiki:House_Darry), [Paeges](wiki:House_Paege), and [Pipers](wiki:House_Piper) set up camp north of the Tumblestone, while the [Blackwoods](wiki:House_Blackwood) establish themselves between the Tumblestone and the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork).(wiki:Tumblestone.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) During the [plot to free Jaime Lannister](wiki:Plot_to_free_Jaime_Lannister) from captivity at Riverrun, Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) is wenching north of the river.(wiki:Tumblestone.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

A boat carrying the body of Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully) is released into the Tumblestone from Riverrun and then sails into the Red Fork.(wiki:Tumblestone.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
[Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling) rides an hour north of the Tumblestone in an effort to join her new husband, [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), in his journey to the [Twins](wiki:Twins), but the [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) sends her back to Riverrun.(wiki:Tumblestone.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser [Ryman Frey](wiki:Ryman_Frey) establishes his large and disorderly camp north of the Tumblestone during the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun).(wiki:Tumblestone.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Jaime, the [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard), threatens Edmure that he will destroy Riverrun and divert the Tumblestone over its ruins if the castle is not yielded.(wiki:Tumblestone.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)
