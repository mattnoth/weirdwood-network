## Identity

Walls of Qarth is a location from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges

## Origins

While other [Qaathi](wiki:Qaathi) settlements were destroyed by [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) during the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood), the triple wall protected [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) from attack.(wiki:Walls_of_Qarth.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Appearances & Description

The outer wall, red sandstone thirty feet high, is decorated with carved animals, including [snakes](wiki:Snake), [kites](wiki:Kite), fish, [wolves](wiki:Wolves), [zorses](wiki:Zorse), and [elephants](wiki:Elephants).(wiki:Walls_of_Qarth.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) The middle wall, grey granite forty feet high, depicts scenes of war instead of animals.(wiki:Walls_of_Qarth.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) The innermost wall is made of black [marble](wiki:Marble) fifty feet high and is carved with scenes of lovemaking.(wiki:Walls_of_Qarth.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

The city's outer and middle gates are banded with [copper](wiki:Copper) and [iron](wiki:Iron), respectively. The innermost gates are studded with [golden](wiki:Gold) eyes.(wiki:Walls_of_Qarth.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

All three walls open for [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) when she arrives at [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) from the [red waste](wiki:Red_waste). Men beat gongs from atop the walls to herald her coming.(wiki:Walls_of_Qarth.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

### A Dance with Dragons

As [Yezzan zo Qaggaz](wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz) lies dying during the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen), [Penny](wiki:Penny) suggests sailing to Qarth and tells [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) of the city walls. However, Tyrion informs her that he has no intention of going any further east.(wiki:Walls_of_Qarth.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
