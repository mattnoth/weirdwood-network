## Origins

### Antiquity

The [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) of old raided the [Mander](wiki:Mander) as far as Stonebridge in their longships.(wiki:Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### House Targaryen

A [battle at Stonebridge](wiki:Battle_at_Stonebridge) was one of many battles during the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising) against King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen). The savagery of that particular battle made the Mander run red with blood for twenty leagues. The bridge and castle that commanded it was forever known as Bitterbridge thereafter.(wiki:Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

According to legend, Septon [Mattheus](wiki:Mattheus)'s opulent [wheelhouse](wiki:Wheelhouse) passed Septon [Barth](wiki:Barth), who was riding a [donkey](wiki:Donkey), at Bitterbridge in [50 AC](wiki:50_AC).(wiki:Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) landed at Bitterbridge with their [dragons](wiki:Dragon), [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor) and [Silverwing](wiki:Silverwing), during their journey to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC).(wiki:Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### The Dance of the Dragons

At the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Ser [Otto Hightower](wiki:Otto_Hightower), dispatched ravens all over Westeros to gain support for his grandson, King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen). One of these ravens was sent to Bitterbridge.(wiki:Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Tom Flowers](wiki:Tom_Flowers), the Bastard of Bitterbridge, was one of the [black](wiki:Blacks) commanders at the [Battle of the Honeywine](wiki:Battle_of_the_Honeywine) and died during the battle.(wiki:Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

Later during the war, Ser [Rickard Thorne](wiki:Rickard_Thorne) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) and Prince [Maelor Targaryen](wiki:Maelor_Targaryen) were killed by a mob in Bitterbridge while trying to make their way to the [siege of Longtable](wiki:Siege_of_Longtable). Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)) responded by burning the town with [Tessarion](wiki:Tessarion) in the [sack of Bitterbridge](wiki:Sack_of_Bitterbridge).(wiki:Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

[Ulf the White](wiki:Ulf_the_White) was named [Lord of Bitterbridge](wiki:Lord_of_Bitterbridge) by Daeron for his service in the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton),(wiki:Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) but he was [poisoned](wiki:Poison) by Ser [Hobert Hightower](wiki:Hobert_Hightower) after the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton).(wiki:Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### Recent History

[Rolly's](wiki:Rolly_Duckfield) father was an [armorer](wiki:Armorer) at Bitterbridge. Rolly fled Bitterbridge after attacking Ser [Lorent Caswell](wiki:Lorent_Caswell).(wiki:Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Appearances & Description

Bitterbridge is situated where the river [Mander](wiki:Mander) meets the [Roseroad](wiki:Roseroad). It takes its name from an ancient stone bridge that crosses the river.(wiki:Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Bitterbridge's small castle has stone-and-timber battlements and a modest [Great Hall](wiki:Great_Hall_(Bitterbridge)).(wiki:Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) The keep is not that tall, but the land is low and flat and makes it seem taller.(wiki:Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

The [Hogs Head](wiki:Hogs_Head), a disreputable inn, was the first building destroyed in the [sack of Bitterbridge](wiki:Sack_of_Bitterbridge). The town's [sept](wiki:Sept) was also destroyed by [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame).(wiki:Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) passes Bitterbridge while traveling from [Tarth](wiki:Tarth) to [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden).(wiki:Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rawoiafjourneys.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

King [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)'s army is camped beneath the walls of Bitterbridge when Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) comes to meet with him. A [great melee](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge) is hosted beneath the castle.(wiki:Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) Renly takes his horse to [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)) to deal with King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), leaving his foot behind at Bitterbridge with his bride, [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Following Renly's death near Storm's End, Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) hurries from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to Bitterbridge to meet with Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell),(wiki:Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) continuing on to Highgarden to negotiate with Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell).(wiki:Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) returns to Bitterbridge, seizes Renly's stores, and [puts many of the foot to death](wiki:Fighting_at_Bitterbridge), especially those of [House Florent](wiki:House_Florent). Lord [Lorent Caswell](wiki:Lorent_Caswell) shuts himself within the walls of his castle.(wiki:Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Riders from Bitterbridge inform Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) of the alliance between [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) and [House Baratheon of King's Landing](wiki:House_Baratheon_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
