## Origins

### Ancient History[ ]

[Andal invaders](wiki:Andals) tried to conquer the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) of Crackclaw Point during the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), but the newcomers were defeated by their lack of knowledge of the area. Eventually, the Andals learned that what they could not conquer with swords they could through marriage. The [Darklyn](wiki:House_Darklyn) kings of [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) tried to impose themselves on the houses of the Point, as did the [Mootons](wiki:House_Mooton) of [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) and the [Celtigars](wiki:House_Celtigar) of [Claw Isle](wiki:Claw_Isle), but all were repelled.(wiki:Crackclaw_Point.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-14)
When not fighting would-be conquerors, internecine fighting between the houses was commonplace. Champions rising from local noble families, such as Ser [Clarence Crabb](wiki:Clarence_Crabb), [Crackbones](wiki:Crackbones), the [Brothers Brune](wiki:Brothers_Brune), and Ser [Lucifer Hardy](wiki:Lucifer_Hardy), imposed peace on Crackclaw but it never survived their lifetimes.(wiki:Crackclaw_Point.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-15)

### House Targaryen[ ]

Having heard of the destruction of [House Hoare](wiki:House_Hoare) in the [burning of Harrenhal](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal)(wiki:Crackclaw_Point.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-16), the lords of Crackclaw quickly swore allegiance to Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Crackclaw_Point.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Because they surrendered peacefully, they were sworn directly to [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). The region has since been known as Targaryen loyalists.(wiki:Crackclaw_Point.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-17)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the [Crabbs](wiki:House_Crabb) and [Brunes](wiki:House_Brune_of_the_Dyre_Den) joined the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) of Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and assisted [Walys Mooton](wiki:Walys_Mooton), [Lord of Maidenpool](wiki:Lord_of_Maidenpool), in retaking [Rook's Rest](wiki:Rook's_Rest).(wiki:Crackclaw_Point.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) The inhabitants of the peninsula say that following Lord Mooton's failed attempt to kill [Sunfyre](wiki:Sunfyre), the dragon sheltered amongst their dark and piney woods and caves for some time before traveling to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Crackclaw_Point.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons.3B_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Some thought that [Nettles](wiki:Nettles) and [Sheepstealer](wiki:Sheepstealer) sought refuge in Crackclaw Point or the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon) after disappearing from Maidenpool.(wiki:Crackclaw_Point.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Ser [Mortimer Boggs](wiki:Mortimer_Boggs) was a well-regarded hero from Crackclaw who attended the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls).(wiki:Crackclaw_Point.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Many of Crackclaw Point's lords and champions fought beside [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone), in the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Crackclaw_Point.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-18) The Celtigars sometimes send men to collect taxes, but they have limited success.(wiki:Crackclaw_Point.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-19)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Transporting Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon), [Seaswift](wiki:Seaswift) and its escorts follow the coast from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to Crackclaw, avoiding [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)). After reaching the peninsula, the ships sail for [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Crackclaw_Point.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

In [Saltpans](wiki:Saltpans), [Ternesio Terys](wiki:Ternesio_Terys) tells [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) that his crew saw a dozen [pirate](wiki:Pirate) ships heading north as his [Titan's Daughter](wiki:Titan's_Daughter) rounded Crackclaw Point.(wiki:Crackclaw_Point.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne), [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth), and [Dick Crabb](wiki:Dick_Crabb) pass through Crackclaw Point to get to the [Whispers](wiki:Whispers). Dick regales them with the history and folklore of the peninsula. [Pyg](wiki:Pyg), [Timeon](wiki:Timeon), and [Shagwell](wiki:Shagwell), once members of the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions), kill Dick at the Whispers, but Brienne avenges her companion by slaying the three sellswords.(wiki:Crackclaw_Point.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-20) Joined by Ser [Hyle Hunt](wiki:Hyle_Hunt), Brienne and Podrick retrace their steps and return to [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool).(wiki:Crackclaw_Point.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
