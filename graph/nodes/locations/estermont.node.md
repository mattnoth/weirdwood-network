## Origins

Estermont and the islands to its south were added to the realm of the [Storm Kings](wiki:Storm_King) of [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon) during the reign of [Erich the Sailmaker](wiki:Erich_Durrandon_(the_Sailmaker)).(wiki:Estermont.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_durrandon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Estermont was conquered shortly after [Tarth](wiki:Tarth) during the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands). Peace in the region was eventually achieved when the Durrandons and their [First Men](wiki:First_Men) bannermen intermarried with the [Andals](wiki:Andals).(wiki:Estermont.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_andals_in_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Some Storm Kings kept war fleets at Estermont.(wiki:Estermont.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_the_men_of_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Near the end of [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) spent time at [Greenstone](wiki:Greenstone) with her cousin and childhood friend, [Larissa Velaryon](wiki:Larissa_Velaryon).(wiki:Estermont.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

During the [landing of the Golden Company](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company) in the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands), five hundred men are put ashore on Estermont by the [Volantene](wiki:Volantis) fleet transporting the sellswords. Ser [Marq Mandrake](wiki:Marq_Mandrake) leads the [taking of Greenstone](wiki:Taking_of_Greenstone).(wiki:Estermont.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
