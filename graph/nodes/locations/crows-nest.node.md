### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) are invaded with the [landing of the Golden Company](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company). While a quarter of the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company)'s available strength is taking [Griffin's Roost](wiki:Taking_of_Griffin's_Roost), Ser [Tristan Rivers](wiki:Tristan_Rivers) sets off to [seize](wiki:Taking_of_Crow's_Nest) the seat of [House Morrigen](wiki:House_Morrigen) at Crow's Nest, and [Laswell Peake](wiki:Laswell_Peake) for [Rain House](wiki:Taking_of_Rain_House), the stronghold of [House Wylde](wiki:House_Wylde), simultaneously. [Harry Strickland](wiki:Harry_Strickland) argues that if Rivers and Peake are successful the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) will control a better part of [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath).(wiki:Crow's_Nest.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The seat falls quickly.(wiki:Crow's_Nest.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_crow.27s_nest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
