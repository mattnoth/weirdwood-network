## Appearances & Description

The broad street is pebbled with white stones and has steps that lead up from the [Wolf's Den](wiki:Wolf's_Den) by the [harbors](wiki:Bite) to the [New Castle](wiki:New_Castle) on top of the city's hill. [Marble](wiki:Marble) [mermaids](wiki:Mermaid) light the way, bowls of burning whale oil cradled in their arms. From the top there is a view down to both the inner and outer harbor.(wiki:Castle_Stair.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) There is a long tunnel that secretly runs below the Castle Stair connecting the Wolf's Den and the New Castle.(wiki:Castle_Stair.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
