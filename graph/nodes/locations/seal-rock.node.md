## Origins

The [First Men](wiki:First_Men) constructed an ancient [ringfort](wiki:Ringfort) atop Seal Rock. The ruins have been abandoned for centuries.(wiki:Seal_Rock.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Whenever [Cobblecat](wiki:Cobblecat) departed [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor), [Roro Uhoris](wiki:Roro_Uhoris) would have [Davos](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) count the [seals](wiki:Seal) below Seal Rock.(wiki:Seal_Rock.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) returns to [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) as an envoy of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), he observes that the previously-abandoned Seal Rock has been occupied because of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). He sees [scorpions](wiki:Scorpion_(weapon)), spitfires, and crossbowmen amidst the [ringfort](wiki:Ringfort)'s ruins, and peat smoke rises from the rock.(wiki:Seal_Rock.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
