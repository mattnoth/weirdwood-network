## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), plans an invasion of the [north](wiki:North) and declares himself [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Saltspear.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) His younger brother, Lord Captain [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy), sails the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) up the Saltspear and the [Fever River](wiki:Fever_River) to [take Moat Cailin](wiki:Fall_of_Moat_Cailin), which lies near the river.(wiki:Saltspear.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Saltspear.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Balon styles himself [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Saltspear.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After King Balon's death at [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), Victarion returns to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) with nine-tenths of the Iron Fleet for the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Saltspear.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
