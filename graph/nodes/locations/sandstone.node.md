## Origins

Sandstone was founded during the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). The [Qorgyles](wiki:House_Qorgyle) were [Andal](wiki:Andals) adventurers who fortified the only well around for fifty leagues in the deep dunes.(wiki:Sandstone.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_the_andals_arrive.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
The Qorgyles attacked the foraging parties of Lord [Harlan Tyrell](wiki:Harlan_Tyrell) during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War). Sandstone was burned in [9 AC](wiki:9_AC) by King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen).(wiki:Sandstone.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
In the aftermath of the [conquest of Dorne](wiki:Conquest_of_Dorne) by King [Daeron I Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen), it was rumored that Lord Qorgyle arranged the murder by scorpion of Lord [Lyonel Tyrell](wiki:Lyonel_Tyrell_(lord)) at Sandstone.(wiki:Sandstone.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) was [fostered](wiki:Fosterage) at Sandstone in his youth.(wiki:Sandstone.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) expects that Sandstone will aid her in her plot to crown Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) as queen. The conspiracy falters at the [Greenblood](wiki:Greenblood), however.(wiki:Sandstone.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
