## Origins

During the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), [Axel Tully](wiki:Axel_Tully) constructed [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) at the confluence of the [Tumblestone](wiki:Tumblestone) and the Red Fork on land granted to him by [Armistead Vance](wiki:Armistead_Vance), the mightiest of the [Andal](wiki:Andal) warlords.(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
According to a semi-canon source, the red in the sigil of [House Strong](wiki:House_Strong) referred to the Red Fork.(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-15)
During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), [Torrhen Stark](wiki:Torrhen_Stark), the [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), submitted to [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) along the shores of the Trident.(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) The [Inn of the Kneeling Man](wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man) on the southern shore of the Red Fork is said to be where Aegon accepted Torrhen's surrender.(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister) was slain by [Pate of Longleaf](wiki:Pate_of_Longleaf) in the [Battle at the Red Fork](wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork).(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) [Sallydance](wiki:Sallydance) on the Red Fork was attacked by Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) and his dragon [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) during the civil war.(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) raids [Wendish Town](wiki:Wendish_Town), [Sherrer](wiki:Sherrer), and [Mummer's Ford](wiki:Mummer's_Ford), and he retreats across the Red Fork back to the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) before Ser [Raymun Darry](wiki:Raymun_Darry) can respond. The [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), sends Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) to bring Gregor to justice.(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
After crossing the Red Fork, Beric's force is attacked by [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister). While retreating, they are then ambushed by the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) in the [battle at the Mummer's Ford](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford).(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) As the Lannisters invade the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), Gregor burns out [Pinkmaiden](wiki:Burning_of_Pinkmaiden_Castle).(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) divides his host into three camps while besieging [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Ser [Marq Piper](wiki:Marq_Piper) and Lord [Karyl Vance](wiki:Karyl_Vance) retaliate by raiding lands of the westermen across the Red Fork.(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1)
After capturing Jaime in the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood),(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) the army of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) continues south and defeats the westermen outside Riverrun in the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps).(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Ser [Forley Prester](wiki:Forley_Prester) retreats from the camp south of the Red Fork.(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Returning from [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) crosses the Red Fork at a ford south of Riverrun. Her brother, Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully), fortifies the river to prevent Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) from crossing.(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) The rivermen defeat Tywin's attempted crossing in the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords), and Lord [Leo Lefford](wiki:Leo_Lefford) drowns during the fighting.(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth), Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey), and Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) travel along the Red Fork east from Riverrun in a skiff. While in the lands of [House Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken), they see a burned inn and tavern wenches hanged for having slept with Lannister soldiers.(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3) The trio are pursued from Riverrun by Ser [Robin Ryger](wiki:Robin_Ryger), but Brienne sinks his river galley with a boulder.(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-4)
At the [Inn of the Kneeling Man](wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man), the [inn's cook](wiki:Husband) explains that outlaws have been preying where the channel has been silting up by a couple of sunken boats, as well as further downstream around the [Skipping Stones](wiki:Skipping_Stones) and [Red Deer Island](wiki:Red_Deer_Island). Ser [Warren](wiki:Warren) has died, and the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) are active in the area, crossing the Red Fork at will. Brienne's party departs the river and heads inland.(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-3)
[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), [Gendry](wiki:Gendry), and [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie) are caught by the brotherhood at a burned cottage along the Red Fork, and the outlaws bring them to the Inn of the Kneeling Man about two miles away.(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)
After Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully) dies, his body is burned in a funeral pyre in the Red Fork.(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

While traveling toward Riverrun, Jaime practices his swordsmanship with Ser [Ilyn Payne](wiki:Ilyn_Payne) in a burned village along the Red Fork. Jaime slips on mud and lands in reeds by the riverside.(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
Ser [Emmon Frey](wiki:Emmon_Frey) and Forley Prester are encamped south of the Red Fork during the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun). To prevent escape, the besiegers throw a boom across the river downstream of the castle, with Ser [Manfryd Yew](wiki:Manfryd_Yew) and Ser [Raynard Ruttiger](wiki:Raynard_Ruttiger) defending the boom.(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1) Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) swims under Riverrun's Water Gate in the night and presumably escapes down the Red Fork.(wiki:Red_Fork.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
