## Origins

Zamettar was raised by the [Ghiscari](wiki:Ghiscari) in the days of the [Old Empire](wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis). The walled city was captured by the [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlords) of the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) during the [Fourth Ghiscari War](wiki:Fourth_Ghiscari_War).(wiki:Zamettar.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_sothoryos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
After refusing the offer of the corsair kings of the [Basilisk Isles](wiki:Basilisk_Isles) to settle at the [Isle of Toads](wiki:Isle_of_Toads) in exchange of a yearly tribute of thirty virgin girls and pretty boys, Princess [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria) and the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar) settled on Zamettar, which had been abandoned already for a thousand years. Nymeria remained there with the ships while some of her people made their way upriver to [Yeen](wiki:Yeen). Despite the riches to be found on Sothoryos, many Rhoynar fell to disease, parasites, predators, and the heat over the following year. Nymeria abandoned Zamettar and returned to the sea when a ship sent to Yeen found the ancient city suddenly vacant.(wiki:Zamettar.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Zamettar remains abandoned and the jungle is slowly creeping in.(wiki:Zamettar.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_sothoryos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
