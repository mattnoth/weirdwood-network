## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

After Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) and her party disembark from the [Peregrine](wiki:Peregrine) at Weeping Town Arianne sends Ser [Daemon Sand](wiki:Daemon_Sand) to the Broken Shield in order to gather intelligence, as she knows its common room is rife with rumors.
In the Broken Shield, Ser Daemon is told that the great [septry](wiki:Septry) on the Holf of Men has been burned and looted by raiders from the sea, and a hundred young novices from the motherhouse on [Maiden Isle](wiki:Maiden_Isle) were carried off into [slavery](wiki:Slavery).(wiki:Broken_Shield.cite_ref-Ar2_1-0)
That night, Arianne dispatches the first of her [ravens](wiki:Raven) back to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), reporting to [her father](wiki:Doran_Martell) on all they'd seen and heard.(wiki:Broken_Shield.cite_ref-Ar2_1-1)
