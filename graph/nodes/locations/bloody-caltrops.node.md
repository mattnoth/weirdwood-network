## Origins

The Bloody Caltrops survived the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). Thirteen nobles from the [Reach](wiki:Reach) met in its cellar to discuss killing the [Two Betrayers](wiki:Two_Betrayers), Ser [Ulf White](wiki:Ulf_White) and Ser [Hugh Hammer](wiki:Hugh_Hammer), and claiming their [dragons](wiki:Dragon), [Silverwing](wiki:Silverwing) and [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor).
