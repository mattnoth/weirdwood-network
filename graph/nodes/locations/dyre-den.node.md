## Appearances & Description

A small castle with three crooked towers, the Dyre Den lies on a wind-carved cliff above the end of the bayside road from [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool). The castle is accessible from below by a steep and narrow cleft in the cliff, with the stony path leading up to the castle walls. Pine forests and limestone hills lie beyond the castle.(wiki:Dyre_Den.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Dick Crabb](wiki:Dick_Crabb), [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth), and [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne) pass by the castle in their search for the [fool](wiki:Fool)(wiki:Dyre_Den.cite_ref-3) who was looking for passage aboard a ship from the [Whispers](wiki:Whispers). Dick does not think they will receive a warm reception at the castle, so the three travelers continue on through the forest to the Whispers.(wiki:Dyre_Den.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
