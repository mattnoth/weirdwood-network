## Origins

New Ghis is the newest and smallest of the [Ghiscari](wiki:Ghiscari) cities, but also the most dynamic.(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_rise_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Its [iron legions](wiki:Ghiscari_legions) are inspired by the lockstep legions of the [Old Empire of Ghis](wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis).(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_the_rise_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) They are armed and trained in the fashion of the [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied), the [eunuch](wiki:Eunuch) soldiers of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor).(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The Unsullied are [slaves](wiki:Slavery), however, while the legionaries of New Ghis are free men who serve terms of three years.(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_the_rise_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

[Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) is said to wield some influence in New Ghis.(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Arstan Whitebeard](wiki:Arstan_Whitebeard) and [Strong Belwas](wiki:Strong_Belwas) sail from [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) to New Ghis and then on to [Qarth](wiki:Qarth).(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Rtloiaf_journeys.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords

After [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) leaves [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai), the [Wise Masters](wiki:Wise_Masters) sends envoys to New Ghis seeking allies against the liberator of the [Slaver Cities](wiki:Slaver_Cities).(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

The [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai)'i stir up enmity in New Ghis against Daenerys and her rule in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Two [iron legions](wiki:Ghiscari_legions) fight in the [battle at the Horns of Hazzat](wiki:Battle_at_the_Horns_of_Hazzat),(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) and three galleys from New Ghis join Qartheen galleys and a [Tolosi](wiki:Tolos) carrack in blocking the [Skahazadhan](wiki:Skahazadhan).(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Ghiscari legions also fight in the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor) and the city's ensuing sack.(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Four legions are part of the Yunkish host which marches for the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen), while another two legions sail from Astapor(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) and encamp north of the Skahazadhan.(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) thinks the legions of New Ghis look more formidable than the Yunkish slave soldiers, but he thinks the [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) in Daenerys's service are more dedicated than the iron legionaries.(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) The [bloody flux](wiki:Bloody_flux) spreads from Tolosi slingers to a Ghiscari legion.(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)

The *[Goddess](wiki:Goddess)* and the *[Fawn](wiki:Fawn)* sail from [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) for New Ghis, the latter ship continuing on to Qarth.(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) The *[Selaesori Qhoran](wiki:Selaesori_Qhoran)* is to sail for Qarth by way of New Ghis,(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) but the ship is overtaken by [slavers](wiki:Slavery) en route.(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Radwd40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) The [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) intercepts the *[Ghiscari Dawn](wiki:Ghiscari_Dawn)* of New Ghis, as well as two galleys returning to the island city for supplies and reinforcements.(wiki:New_Ghis.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
