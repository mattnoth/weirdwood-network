## Appearances & Description

The [godswood](wiki:Godswood) is a small circle surrounded by the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie)'s tall white towers,(wiki:Godswood_of_the_Eyrie.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) which protect it from the worst of the mountain winds.(wiki:Godswood_of_the_Eyrie.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) It contains grass, blue flowers, and low, flowering shrubs,(wiki:Godswood_of_the_Eyrie.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and there are some thin trees as well.(wiki:Godswood_of_the_Eyrie.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
The garden contains scattered statuary, including a [marble](wiki:Marble) statue of [Alyssa Arryn](wiki:Alyssa_Arryn) in its center.(wiki:Godswood_of_the_Eyrie.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) The ground is uneven and has moss-covered stones.(wiki:Godswood_of_the_Eyrie.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
Overlooking the godswood are the apartments of Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn), with a terrace and a balustrade.(wiki:Godswood_of_the_Eyrie.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) There is a balcony across the garden,(wiki:Godswood_of_the_Eyrie.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) and the [rookery](wiki:Rookery) of Maester [Colemon](wiki:Colemon) is also nearby.(wiki:Godswood_of_the_Eyrie.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Origins

The [Lords of the Eyrie](wiki:Lords_of_the_Eyrie) tried to plant a [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) in their [godswood](wiki:Godswood), but none would grow in the hard stone of the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance).(wiki:Godswood_of_the_Eyrie.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) The garden thus lacks a [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree).(wiki:Godswood_of_the_Eyrie.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), accused by Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) of having murdered Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), calls for [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat).(wiki:Godswood_of_the_Eyrie.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Their respective champions, the sellsword [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) and Ser [Vardis Egen](wiki:Vardis_Egen), captain of the [Arryn guards](wiki:House_Arryn_guards), are to face each other in the [godswood](wiki:Godswood) garden. The agile Bronn wears down the heavily-armored knight in their duel, and Bronn eventually kills Vardis after pushing the statue of [Alyssa Arryn](wiki:Alyssa_Arryn) atop him.(wiki:Godswood_of_the_Eyrie.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Snow falls upon the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), so [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) tries to recreate [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) out of snow in the garden. Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), Lysa's new husband, helps Sansa add more buildings, and he then surprises her with a kiss. Lysa's son, young Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn), arrives and begins demolishing the snow castle with his doll, and Sansa ruins the doll while trying to stop him. Lysa, having observed the kiss from her balcony, later summons Sansa to the [High Hall](wiki:High_Hall). Accusing the girl of initiating the kiss with Petyr, Lysa threatens Sansa with the [Moon Door](wiki:Moon_Door). Petyr interrupts Lysa, however, pushes her through the Moon Door, and accuses [Marillion](wiki:Marillion) of having murdered Lysa.(wiki:Godswood_of_the_Eyrie.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
