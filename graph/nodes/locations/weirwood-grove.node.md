## Origins

[Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark) took his vows in front of a [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree),(wiki:Weirwood_grove.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) presumably at the weirwood grove.

## Appearances & Description

The grove is located in the southern portion of the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest) half a league north of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Weirwood_grove.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) A stream and a game trail are found nearby.(wiki:Weirwood_grove.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

The clearing in the dense forest contains nine great(wiki:Weirwood_grove.cite_ref-Rasos26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [weirwoods](wiki:Weirwood) growing in a rough circle. The trees' smooth and wide trunks are pale as bone,(wiki:Weirwood_grove.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and the faces of the [heart trees](wiki:Heart_tree), all different in expression,(wiki:Weirwood_grove.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) face inward.(wiki:Weirwood_grove.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

[Free folk](wiki:Free_folk) visit the grove of weirwoods.(wiki:Weirwood_grove.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Since Castle Black has a [sept](wiki:Sept) but not a [godswood](wiki:Godswood), members of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) who keep to the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods) also travel to the weirwood grove.(wiki:Weirwood_grove.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) and [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), recruits of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), choose to recite their vows at the grove north of the [Wall](wiki:Wall) instead of stating them in the [sept](wiki:Sept) of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black). [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) escorts them to the grove, and has the youths leave their [horses](wiki:Horse) outside of the circle. After Jon and Sam state their vows, [Ghost](wiki:Ghost) emerges from the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest) with a hand.(wiki:Weirwood_grove.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) The Night's Watch eventually find the bodies of [Othor](wiki:Othor) and [Jafer Flowers](wiki:Jafer_Flowers).(wiki:Weirwood_grove.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Jon recalls reciting vows at the grove when he sleeps with [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte).(wiki:Weirwood_grove.cite_ref-Rasos26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Jon, now [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), leads six new recruits—[Horse](wiki:Hareth_(Mole's_Town)), [Arron](wiki:Arron), [Emrick](wiki:Emrick), [Satin](wiki:Satin), [Leathers](wiki:Leathers), and [Jax](wiki:Jax)—to the grove so they can recite their vows. Although snowfall has been heavy, Jon estimates it will take them less than two hours to reach the grove. When the Night's Watch nears the grove, [Tom Barleycorn](wiki:Tom_Barleycorn) detects a group of weakened wildlings, including a [giant](wiki:Giant), in the clearing. The Night's Watch surprise the encamped wildlings, and Leathers calms the giant, [Wun Weg Wun Dar Wun](wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun). After the recruits recite their vows, the black brothers return to Castle Black with the surviving wildlings.(wiki:Weirwood_grove.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
