## Appearances & Description

Fishmonger's Square is located inside the [River Gate](wiki:River_Gate) in the south of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). The [Street of Steel](wiki:Street_of_Steel) begins at the market square beside the gate(wiki:Fishmonger's_Square.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and runs west to [Visenya's Hill](wiki:Visenya's_Hill).(wiki:Fishmonger's_Square.cite_ref-6) The [Muddy Way](wiki:Muddy_Way) heads north from the square to the center of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), while the [River Row](wiki:River_Row) runs along the southern wall of the city.(wiki:Fishmonger's_Square.cite_ref-Rtloiafking.27s_landing.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
The [fish market](wiki:Fish_market) is located nearby outside of the walls.(wiki:Fishmonger's_Square.cite_ref-Rawoiaffishmarket.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Origins

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), [violent riots](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)) occurred in the capital. Ser [Torrhen Manderly](wiki:Torrhen_Manderly) led a sortie from the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) down the [Hook](wiki:Hook) with his [northmen](wiki:Northmen) to try to stop the rioters. When they arrived at the Fishermonger's Square and the [River Row](wiki:River_Row), the place was swarming with the [gutter knights](wiki:Gutter_knights) of Ser [Perkin the Flea](wiki:Perkin_the_Flea). Torrhen lost a quarter of his men fighting his way back to the Red Keep.(wiki:Fishmonger's_Square.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), rides from the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand) and sees crowds at the market as he travels toward the [River Gate](wiki:River_Gate) and the [Street of Steel](wiki:Street_of_Steel).(wiki:Fishmonger's_Square.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

On the way back to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) from the docks, the party of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) crosses Fishmonger's Square.(wiki:Fishmonger's_Square.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
[Three catapults](wiki:Three_Whores) are built in the market square by the Mud Gate in preparation for the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Fishmonger's_Square.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
