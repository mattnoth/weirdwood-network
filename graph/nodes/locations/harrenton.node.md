## Origins

Harrenton is named after [Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare), the [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers) who constructed the great castle of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) at the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye).(wiki:Harrenton.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
The multitude of [lords](wiki:Lord), retainers, and [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) who came to Harrenhal for the [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council) of [101 AC](wiki:101_AC) led to nearby Harrenton briefly being considered the fourth largest city of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), behind only [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), and [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport).(wiki:Harrenton.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Harrenton.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Yoren](wiki:Yoren) is hopeful his [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) recruits will be able to sail from [a town](wiki:Gods_Eye_town) on the southern shore of the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye) north to Harrentown, where they can find new horses.(wiki:Harrenton.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
When [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) is brought to [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) by the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), she sees the host of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) encamped in the burned remnants of the town.(wiki:Harrenton.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

When the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) bring Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) to Harrenhal, Jaime notices that the town outside the castle walls has been burned to ash and blackened stone.(wiki:Harrenton.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
