## Origins

The Summer Sea was once separated from the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) by the [Arm of Dorne](wiki:Arm_of_Dorne). The [Breaking](wiki:Breaking) led to the [Broken Arm](wiki:Broken_Arm), the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones), and the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands).(wiki:Summer_Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_the_breaking.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
The [Old Empire of Ghis](wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis) founded colonies in the [Basilisk Isles](wiki:Basilisk_Isles) and [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos), but the [Ghiscari](wiki:Ghiscari) were conquered by the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) during the [Ghiscari wars](wiki:Ghiscari_wars).(wiki:Summer_Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_basilisk_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Summer_Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_sothoryos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) was founded after a fleet with [slaves](wiki:Slaves), taken from the Summer and [Jade Seas](wiki:Jade_Sea) and intended to work a Valyrian colony in Sothoryos, was taken in an uprising.(wiki:Summer_Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_valyria.27s_children.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Summer_Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_braavos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
[Sarhoy](wiki:Sarhoy) on the Summer Sea was destroyed by [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) and Valyrian [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlord) during the [Second Spice War](wiki:Second_Spice_War). According to legend, [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria) then led the exodus of the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar) on [ten thousand ships](wiki:Ten_thousand_ships). They traveled to the Basilisk Isles, Sothoryos, [Naath](wiki:Naath), the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles), and finally [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Summer_Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
In the aftermath of the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria), the [Free City](wiki:Free_City) of Volantis considers itself the mistress of the Summer Sea.(wiki:Summer_Sea.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
In [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), [Elissa Farman](wiki:Elissa_Farman), Ser [Eustace Hightower](wiki:Eustace_Hightower), and Ser [Norman Hightower](wiki:Norman_Hightower), sailed from [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) to explore the Summer and Sunset Seas. Norman's [Autumn Moon](wiki:Autumn_Moon) went down in a storm, but Elissa's [Sun Chaser](wiki:Sun_Chaser) found [three islands](wiki:Aegon,_Rhaenys,_and_Visenya_(islands)). Elissa continued west to an unknown fate. Eustace's [Lady Meredith](wiki:Lady_Meredith) was damaged by a pod of [leviathans](wiki:Leviathan) while returning home. The ship headed for the Summer Isles, but sailed off course and landed at Sothoryos. Eustace eventually reached [Tall Trees Town](wiki:Tall_Trees_Town) and then Oldtown with the aid of Summer Islanders.(wiki:Summer_Sea.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)(wiki:Summer_Sea.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
