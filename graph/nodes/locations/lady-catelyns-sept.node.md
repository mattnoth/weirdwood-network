## Appearances & Description

The [sept](wiki:Sept) is a small building with fragrant incense.(wiki:Lady_Catelyn's_sept.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Septon [Chayle](wiki:Chayle) hangs carved masks of [the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) on the sept's seven walls.(wiki:Lady_Catelyn's_sept.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Septa [Mordane](wiki:Mordane) tutors [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark).(wiki:Lady_Catelyn's_sept.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Origins

[Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell), had the [sept](wiki:Sept) built after [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) so that his new [southron](wiki:Southron) wife, [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), could pray to the [Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) after moving to the [north](wiki:North).(wiki:Lady_Catelyn's_sept.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) has prayed Arya with her mother in the sept and with her father in the [godswood of Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Lady_Catelyn's_sept.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

After Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) decides to bring [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) with him to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) prays seven times that Ned would change his mind and leave their son at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). When Bran becomes crippled and comatose after falling from the [First Keep](wiki:First_Keep), Catelyn wishes that it had instead been [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), Eddard's [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son.(wiki:Lady_Catelyn's_sept.cite_ref-Ragot10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Chayle is drowned in a well after the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) by [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn).(wiki:Lady_Catelyn's_sept.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) The sept is burned to an ashen shell during the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell) by [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton).(wiki:Lady_Catelyn's_sept.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) visits the ruins of [Mikken](wiki:Mikken)'s forge and Lady Catelyn's sept.(wiki:Lady_Catelyn's_sept.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
