## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

The [dwarf brother](wiki:Dwarf_brother) encounters [a fool](wiki:Shagwell) at the Stinking Goose. One night some of Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly)'s men visit the tavern, looking for the [fool](wiki:Fool). A few days later, the dwarf brother overhears a drunken man boasting that he'd "fooled a fool". Later, in the common room of the [Seven Swords](wiki:Seven_Swords) inn in [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale), the brother encounters [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth), who is searching for [a fool](wiki:Dontos_Hollard). He gives her this information, and tells her that the man's name was [Nimble Dick](wiki:Dick_Crabb).(wiki:Stinking_Goose.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Brienne visits the Stinking Goose to look for Nimble Dick. Upon entering, Brienne does not dare to ask for water in such a place, but instead buys a cup of wine, and asks the proprietor about Nimble Dick. When Dick enters the premises, the proprietor nods at Brienne. Brienne offers to buy him wine, for information about the fool he had fooled.(wiki:Stinking_Goose.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
Later, after traveling to the [Whispers](wiki:Whispers) with Dick acting as a guide, Brienne returns to [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) and arranges to meet up with Ser [Hyle Hunt](wiki:Hyle_Hunt) at the tavern. She and [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne) have breakfast there. The proprietor remembers her and asks about Nimble Dick. When Brienne tells her that he was slain by [outlaws](wiki:Bloody_Mummers), she remarks that she always figured Dick would hang, or get sent off the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Stinking_Goose.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Ser Hyle eventually arrives. He is aghast that they have eaten at the tavern. He provides Brienne with information about [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) garnered from his cousin, Ser [Alyn Hunt](wiki:Alyn_Hunt).(wiki:Stinking_Goose.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
