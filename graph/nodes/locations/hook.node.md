## Origins

[Violent riots](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)) occurred in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). Ser [Torrhen Manderly](wiki:Torrhen_Manderly) led a sortie from the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) down the Hook with his [northmen](wiki:Northmen) to try to stop the rioters. When they arrived at the [Fishmonger's Square](wiki:Fishmonger's_Square) and the [River Row](wiki:River_Row), the region was swarming with Ser [Perkin the Flea](wiki:Perkin_the_Flea)'s gutter [knights](wiki:Knight). Torrhen lost a quarter of his men fighting his way back to the Red Keep.(wiki:Hook.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

King [Joffrey I Baratheon's](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) party returns to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) after saying goodbye to Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) at the docks. While they are riding up the Hook, a woman walks out of the crowd of [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) with a dead baby and blocks the way. [Riots in King's Landing](wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing) ensue after an altercation between the royal party and the crowd.(wiki:Hook.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) passes by the Hook while traveling to meet [Symon Silver Tongue](wiki:Symon_Silver_Tongue) at a [winesink](wiki:Winesink) near [Visenya's Hill](wiki:Visenya's_Hill).(wiki:Hook.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
