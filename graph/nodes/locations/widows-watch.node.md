## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lady [Lyessa Flint](wiki:Lyessa_Flint) declines the invitation to the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) because she is pregnant and there is a sickness at Widow's Watch.(wiki:Widow's_Watch.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sends a raven to Widow's Watch in a plea for help to defend [Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) from [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings).(wiki:Widow's_Watch.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The fleet of [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan) is spotted from Widow's Watch. [Bountiful Harvest](wiki:Bountiful_Harvest) sinks ten leagues north of the castle after colliding with a galley.(wiki:Widow's_Watch.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
According to [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly), [Lord of White Harbor](wiki:Lord_of_White_Harbor), the [Flints](wiki:House_Flint_of_Widow's_Watch) of Widow's Watch will take their lead from him.(wiki:Widow's_Watch.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
