## Culture

[Lys](wiki:Lys) is full of pleasure houses catering to every taste, no matter how peculiar.(wiki:Pleasure_house.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_the_quarrelsome_daughters:_myr.2C_lys.2C_and_tyrosh.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) One in particular is famed for its [Naathi](wiki:Naath) girls, who are clad in diaphanous [silken](wiki:Silk) gowns and adorned with gaily painted [butterfly](wiki:Butterfly) wings.(wiki:Pleasure_house.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_naath.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Summer Islanders](wiki:Summer_Islands) held the belief that no shame can be found in pillow houses.(wiki:Pleasure_house.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
In [Ghiscari](wiki:Ghiscari) culture, [Red Graces](wiki:Red_Graces) wait for patrons at snuggeries in pleasure gardens at the [Temple of the Graces](wiki:Temple_of_the_Graces).(wiki:Pleasure_house.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Girls of noble birth too young to serve their year in the pleasure gardens serve instead as [White Graces](wiki:White_Graces).(wiki:Pleasure_house.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Origins

Sometime after fleeing [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), Princess [Saera Targaryen](wiki:Saera_Targaryen) became the owner of a famous pleasure house in [Old Volantis](wiki:Old_Volantis).(wiki:Pleasure_house.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Pleasure_house.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
During the tilme of the [Triarchy](wiki:Triarchy), Lysene captains took off women, girls, and comely young boys to serve in their pleasure gardens and pillow houses. One of these unfortunate women was the fifteen-year-old [Johanna Swann](wiki:Johanna_Swann), niece of the [Lord of Stonehelm](wiki:Lord_of_Stonehelm).(wiki:Pleasure_house.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
The wealthy [Rogare family](wiki:House_Rogare) owned a famous pleasure house, the [Perfumed Garden](wiki:Perfumed_Garden), before their downfall in [135 AC](wiki:135_AC).(wiki:Pleasure_house.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
[Doreah](wiki:Doreah) and [Serra](wiki:Serra) were both bought by Magister [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) in Lysene pleasure houses.(wiki:Pleasure_house.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Pleasure_house.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Obara Sand](wiki:Obara_Sand) claims the whores in the pillow houses of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) mourn the death of Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) so much that they are working for free.(wiki:Pleasure_house.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The [Yunkaii](wiki:Yunkai) galley [Willing Maiden](wiki:Willing_Maiden) contains twenty perfumed boy and eighty girl [slaves](wiki:Slaves) to be trained in the pleasure houses of Lys when the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) captures it.(wiki:Pleasure_house.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
