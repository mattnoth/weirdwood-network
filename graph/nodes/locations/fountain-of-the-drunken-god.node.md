## Origins

For years an elderly [dwarf](wiki:Dwarf) [juggler](wiki:Dwarf_juggler) known to [Penny](wiki:Penny) and [Oppo](wiki:Oppo) juggled by the fountain each day. His hands were not as deft as they had been, and sometimes he dropped the balls and had to chase them across the square. The [Tyroshi](wiki:Tyrosh) laughed and threw him [coins](wiki:Coin) all the same.(wiki:Fountain_of_the_Drunken_God.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Penny](wiki:Penny) tells [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) that one morning she and her brother Oppo learned that the juggler had been found beheaded and dismembered at the [Temple of Trios](wiki:Temple_of_Trios). Penny and Tyrion feel certain that the juggler's head was taken to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), who has put a bounty on Tyrion's head.(wiki:Fountain_of_the_Drunken_God.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
