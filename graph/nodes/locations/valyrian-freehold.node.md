## Origins

### Origin

Ancient Valyria was a civilization sheltered in the [Fourteen Flames](wiki:Fourteen_Flames), a ring of volcanoes on the [Valyrian peninsula](wiki:Valyrian_peninsula).(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_the_rise_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) [Valyrian](wiki:Valyrians) stories claim that [dragons](wiki:Dragon) originated in their lands, although tales from [Asshai](wiki:Asshai) claim the beasts were brought to Valyria from the [Shadow Lands](wiki:Shadow_Lands).(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_the_rise_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) The Valyrians tamed the dragons with [magic](wiki:Magic)—taught by a vanished people from the Shadow Lands according to the Asshai'i(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_the_rise_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)—and mastered the technique of raising and training the dragons into devastating weapons of war.(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

They began expanding their influence, establishing the Freehold with the city of [Valyria](wiki:Valyria) as its capital. Magic flowered, topless towers(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) rose toward the heavens where dragons soared, stone [Valyrian sphinxes](wiki:Valyrian_sphinx) gazed down through eyes of garnet,(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and smiths used spells to forge [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) weapons of legendary strength and sharpness.(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### Rise to Power and the Fall of Ghis

Some five thousand years ago, in the early days of Valyria, the [Old Empire of Ghis](wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis) dominated and controlled much of [Essos](wiki:Essos). The Ghiscari attempted to stop Valyria's expansion and the burgeoning freehold was involved in a series of great wars against the Old Empire. The Ghiscari [lockstep legions](wiki:Lockstep_legions) attacked Valyria five times, but they could never defeat them - with the help of [dragons](wiki:Dragon), Valyria was able to defend and emerge victorious each time.

Finally, in the last of the [Ghiscari wars](wiki:Ghiscari_wars), the Valyrians marched on their capital, [Old Ghis](wiki:Old_Ghis), razed it to the ground, and sowed its fields with salt, sulfur, and skulls,(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) obliterating it and thereby destroying the Old Empire of Ghis. Adopting [slavery](wiki:Slavery) from Ghis, Valyria expanded its influence over the surviving Ghiscari colonies of [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay), and continued to conquer and colonize further.

### Western Expansion

The Valyrian Freehold continued to expand and conquer further west, capturing many [slaves](wiki:Slave) from conquered lands and using them to mine great wealth from the Fourteen Flames, as well as build great cities and [roadways](wiki:Valyrian_roads) that led to Valyria.

For many years the Valyrians were at peace with the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar) civilization of the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne), west of the Valyrian peninsula. From the colonial freehold of [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), the Valyrians instead crossed the Rhoyne and marched west to wage war on the [Andals](wiki:Andals) of [Andalos](wiki:Andalos). Rather than be enslaved by Valyria, the Andals crossed the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) and [invaded Westeros](wiki:Andal_invasion).(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_the_arrival_of_the_andals.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) The Valyrians overwhelmed the remaining Andals of western Essos and established colonies west of their peninsula.

The Valyrians are believed to have traveled as far as [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), predating the arrival of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) and trading with the [elder](wiki:Giants) [races](wiki:Children_of_the_forest), according to [Jellicoe](wiki:Jellicoe). [Septon](wiki:Septon) [Barth](wiki:Barth) also claimed Valyrians came to Westeros because their priests prophesied that the Doom of Man would come out of the land beyond the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea).(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0). [Archmaester](wiki:Archmaester) [Perestan](wiki:Perestan) speculates some tragedy or mishap fell upon them and led them to shun Westeros ever after.(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### Rhoynish Wars

The [Rhoynish Wars](wiki:Rhoynish_Wars) were a series of wars fought between the city-states of the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar) and [colonies](wiki:Free_Cities) of the Valyrian Freehold between ~[950 BC](wiki:950_BC) and [700 BC](wiki:700_BC).(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-15) They are described in [Beldecar](wiki:Beldecar)'s *[History of the Rhoynish Wars](wiki:History_of_the_Rhoynish_Wars)*.(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rasos12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

The wars were concluded in the [Second Spice War](wiki:Second_Spice_War), when the Valyrian colony [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) turned its eyes to the ancient and high civilization of the Rhoynar. Prince [Garin](wiki:Garin_(prince)) of [Chroyane](wiki:Chroyane) led an army of a 250,000 men and defeated the Valyrian armies at [Selhorys](wiki:Selhorys), [Valysar](wiki:Valysar), and [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys), where they won their greatest victory by defeating an army of a hundred thousand men and killing two dragons in the process.(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)

However, the Valyrians responded with an overwhelming force of three hundred dragons, capturing Garin the Great and burning the Rhoynish armies. Princess [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria) of [Ny Sar](wiki:Ny_Sar) led the exodus of the remaining Rhoynar out of Essos, eventually arriving in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). The singers say her [ten thousand ships](wiki:Ten_thousand_ships) were filled with women and children, suggesting most of the men of fighting age had died in the conflict with the Freehold.(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2)

### Valyrian Imperialism

At the height of its power, the Freehold stretched over most of [Essos](wiki:Essos) west of the [Bone Mountains](wiki:Bone_Mountains). Many of the numerous cities were connected by [dragon roads](wiki:Valyrian_road).(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtloiaf_central_essos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

The [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities), the self-ruling daughters of [Valyria](wiki:Valyria), are spread throughout western Essos. [Volantis](wiki:Volantis)(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_volantis.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) and [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh)(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_the_quarrelsome_daughters:_myr.2C_lys.2C_and_tyrosh.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) began as military outposts, while [Myr](wiki:Myr)(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_the_quarrelsome_daughters:_myr.2C_lys.2C_and_tyrosh.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1) and [Pentos](wiki:Pentos)(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_pentos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) were founded by merchants in [Andal](wiki:Andals) territory. [Dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlords) established [Lys](wiki:Lys) as a pleasure retreat.(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_the_quarrelsome_daughters:_myr.2C_lys.2C_and_tyrosh.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-2) Religious refugees founded [Lorath](wiki:Lorath),(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_lorath.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) [Norvos](wiki:Norvos),(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_norvos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) and [Qohor](wiki:Qohor).(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_qohor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) [Essaria](wiki:Essaria) was founded as a colony near the [Kingdom of Sarnor](wiki:Kingdom_of_Sarnor).(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) was founded by slaves who rebelled against a Valyrian slaving fleet, and this Secret City did not accept the rule of Valyria.(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_braavos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

Other cities and towns founded or conquered by the Freehold were governed by [archons](wiki:Archon) from Valyria. The [Valyrian peninsula](wiki:Valyrian_peninsula) contained [Oros](wiki:Oros) and [Tyria](wiki:Tyria).(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtloiaf_central_essos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1) Near Volantis were built [Selhorys](wiki:Selhorys), [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys), and [Valysar](wiki:Valysar).(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Radwd18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) South of the [Painted Mountains](wiki:Painted_Mountains) were [Bhorash](wiki:Bhorash), [Mantarys](wiki:Mantarys), and [Tolos](wiki:Tolos), while located in [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay) were [Velos](wiki:Velos) on the [Isle of Cedars](wiki:Isle_of_Cedars) and [Elyria](wiki:Elyria).

The greatest remnants of the [Old Empire of Ghis](wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis) were [Astapor](wiki:Astapor), [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), and [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai). Other [Ghiscari](wiki:Ghiscari) settlements were [Ghozai](wiki:Ghozai) on the Isle of Cedars, [Ghardaq](wiki:Ghardaq), and what are now [Vaes Efe](wiki:Vaes_Efe) and [Vaes Mejhah](wiki:Vaes_Mejhah).(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rawoiaf.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) It is unknown if [New Ghis](wiki:New_Ghis), located south of ruined [Old Ghis](wiki:Old_Ghis), owed allegiance to Valyria.

South of the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea), the Freehold controlled [Gogossos](wiki:Gogossos) on the [Isle of Tears](wiki:Isle_of_Tears)(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_the_basilisk_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) and also had three failed colonies at [Basilisk Point](wiki:Basilisk_Point) in [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos).(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_sothoryos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

Valyrian cities of unknown location included [Aquos Dhaen](wiki:Aquos_Dhaen), [Draconys](wiki:Draconys), [Mhysa Faer](wiki:Mhysa_Faer), and [Rhyos](wiki:Rhyos).(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0) Among the ruined cities of the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar) were [Ar Noy](wiki:Ar_Noy), [Chroyane](wiki:Chroyane), [Ghoyan Drohe](wiki:Ghoyan_Drohe), [Ny Sar](wiki:Ny_Sar), [Sar Mell](wiki:Sar_Mell),(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtloiaf_the_free_cities.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0) and [Sarhoy](wiki:Sarhoy).(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-3)

### Dragonstone

Some two centuries before the [Doom](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria), the Valyrian Freehold colonized the island of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)) in the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) and established [a citadel](wiki:Dragonstone) there, with ineffective resistance from the local lords of [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay). The island was the westernmost outpost of the Freehold.(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_the_doom_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)

In [114 BC](wiki:Years_before_Aegon's_Conquest), [Daenys Targaryen](wiki:Daenys_Targaryen), the maiden daughter of [Aenar Targaryen](wiki:Aenar_Targaryen), had a vision of the destruction of Valyria. [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) abandoned their homeland for Dragonstone, an act seen by other [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlords) as cowardice.(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Nearby [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark) had already been settled by [House Velaryon](wiki:House_Velaryon), a lesser Valyrian family,(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0) and [Claw Isle](wiki:Claw_Isle) became ruled by the Valyrian [Celtigars](wiki:House_Celtigar).(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### Doom of Valyria

The [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria) was a cataclysmic event in [102 BC](wiki:Years_before_Aegon's_Conquest), after which the city Valyria was utterly destroyed and the Valyrian Freehold crumbled and was no more. The Doom fragmented the land surrounding the city itself into numerous smaller islands, creating the [Smoking Sea](wiki:Smoking_Sea) between them.(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Ragot41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-36) The area is now described as "demon-haunted", and most people are afraid to go there, as all known expeditions to the shattered peninsula has ended up in failure. Hence the quote, "The Doom still rules in Valyria."(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0)

With Valyrian leadership destroyed, the lands outside of the peninsula fell into chaos in the ensuing [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood), which saw the rise of the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities). [Essaria](wiki:Essaria), the "Lost Free City", was reduced to ruins by the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki),(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1) while [Gogossos](wiki:Gogossos), the "Tenth Free City", was abandoned after the [Red Death](wiki:Red_Death) plague.(wiki:Valyrian_Freehold.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_the_basilisk_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-1)
