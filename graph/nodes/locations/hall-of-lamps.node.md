## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After the death of her father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) meets with mourners in the Hall of Lamps.(wiki:Hall_of_Lamps.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
During Tywin's funeral service, King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) flees from his grandfather's funeral bier out of disgust while trying to pray. When Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) catches up to the boy in the Hall of Lamps, Tommen promises he will do better in the morning.(wiki:Hall_of_Lamps.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
The newly-chosen [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon), the so-called [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow), has a score of [septons](wiki:Septon), including [Most Devout](wiki:Most_Devout) like Septon [Raynard](wiki:Raynard), clean the floor of the hall.(wiki:Hall_of_Lamps.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Cersei is met in the Hall of Lamps by three [knights](wiki:Knights) from the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons) when she returns to the Great Sept to meet with her imprisoned rival, Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Hall_of_Lamps.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

A dozen Warrior's Sons escort Cersei from the Hall of Lamps when she begins her [walk of atonement](wiki:Walk_of_atonement).(wiki:Hall_of_Lamps.cite_ref-Radwd65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
