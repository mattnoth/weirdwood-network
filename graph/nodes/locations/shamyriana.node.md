## Appearances & Description

Samyriana is a grey stone city carved into the rock of the mountains it defends.(wiki:Shamyriana.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) The [Stone Road](wiki:Stone_Road), which penetrates the [Bone Mountains](wiki:Bone_Mountains), leads from [Vaes Jini](wiki:Vaes_Jini) to Samyriana, passing beneath its walls.

## Origins

Samyriana is one of the three fortress cities that were once part of the [Patrimony of Hyrkoon](wiki:Patrimony_of_Hyrkoon) and were founded by the [Patriarchs of Hyrkoon](wiki:Patriarchs_of_Hyrkoon).(wiki:Shamyriana.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)
