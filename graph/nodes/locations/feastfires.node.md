## Origins

[Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), occupied [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), so Lady [Johanna Lannister](wiki:Johanna_Lannister) gathered a small fleet of cogs and fishing boats beneath the walls of Feastfires. [Ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) longships [ambushed the westermen](wiki:Ambush_at_Fair_Isle) when they sailed to retake Fair Isle, however, and Lord [Prester](wiki:House_Prester), Lord [Tarbeck](wiki:House_Tarbeck), and Ser [Erwin Lannister](wiki:Erwin_Lannister) were killed. (wiki:Feastfires.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Dalton gathered longships to Feastfires and other ports to await the fleet of Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon), but the Red Kraken was assassinated at [Faircastle](wiki:Faircastle) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC) before the Oakenfist arrived in the west.(wiki:Feastfires.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
According to a semi-canon source,(wiki:Feastfires.cite_ref-4) Ser [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold)) resided at Feastfires after marrying Lady [Marla Prester](wiki:Marla_Prester).
