## Appearances & Description

The winter town has rows of small and neat houses built of log and undressed stone. Its streets are muddy.(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Located by the [main gates of Winterfell](wiki:Main_gates_of_Winterfell), the market square has wooden stalls for produce and goods(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) and a well at its center.(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The local inn(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and alehouse is the [Smoking Log](wiki:Smoking_Log).(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
The [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad) lies past the sprawl of castle and town,(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) with the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) two miles away.(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) Hunting parties from Winterfell can avoid the winter town by taking the [Hunter's Gate](wiki:Hunter's_Gate) instead of the main gate.(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
During spring and summer, four-fifths of the winter town's homes are empty.(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) During autumn and winter, however, the town becomes "full to bursting" with northmen from throughout the region, including farmers,(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) villagers,(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_north.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and [clansmen](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans) from the [northern mountains](wiki:Northern_mountains).(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) When full, the winter town might have a population of around fifteen thousand.(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-9)
Some townfolk claim that the hot springs of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) are warmed by a [dragon](wiki:Dragon) that sleeps below the castle.(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_winterfell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Origins

During the winter of [134 AC](wiki:134_AC), northmen in the winter town ate dogs and horses to avoid starvation.(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
When [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) was little, he went to the market square with his mother, [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), and Septa [Mordane](wiki:Mordane). [Hodor](wiki:Hodor) accompanied them to carry their purchases, but the stableboy wandered off during the excursion. The [simple](wiki:Simple) Hodor was eventually found in an alley with boys poking him with sticks.(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) once had an amorous encounter with [Bessa](wiki:Bessa_(Winterfell)) and [Kyra](wiki:Kyra) of the winter town.(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

After receiving a frosty greeting from [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) in [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) decides to stay in an inn in the winter town, rather than in the castle.(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
Robb, [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark), and [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) ride through the winter town on their way to the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood). Most townspeople have become accustomed to their [direwolves](wiki:Direwolves), [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind) and [Summer](wiki:Summer).(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8)
The winter town becomes full when Robb calls the [northern](wiki:North) banners in response to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne)'s imprisonment of his father, [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell). The townspeople cheer Robb when he leads the soldiers south along the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad).(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Some [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) from the town attend the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast) in Winterfell.(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
After the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) by Theon's [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings),(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) [Kyra](wiki:Kyra), a tavern wench who has lived her entire life in the winter town, becomes Theon's bedmate in the castle.(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
Theon unsuccessfully parleys with Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) in the market square after Rodrik gathers an army of [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) loyalists to retake Winterfell. The square is defended by Rodrik's men, including spearmen, archers atop the houses, and knights from [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor).(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Later that day, however, Rodrik's men are betrayed by the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort) garrison brought by [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow). The Stark men are unable to form up between the houses, which [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton) put to the torch.(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Following the scattering of Rodrik's men in this [battle at Winterfell](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell), Theon admits the Boltons into Winterfell, which they [quickly sack](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) [Summer](wiki:Summer) and [Shaggydog](wiki:Shaggydog) find no survivors when they pass through the ruins.(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The remnants of the winter town are buried by the blizzard striking the north,(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1) so the army of Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), the new [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), instead quarters in Winterfell.(wiki:Winter_town.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
