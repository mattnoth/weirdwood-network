## Origins

### Older History

[Tristifer IV Mudd](wiki:Tristifer_IV_Mudd), [King of the Rivers and the Hills](wiki:King_of_the_Rivers_and_the_Hills), ruled from the Trident to the [Neck](wiki:Neck).(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) During the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), the [newcomers](wiki:Andals) used longships to sail up the Trident and its tributaries to attack the kingdoms of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men).(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

The [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Arlan III Durrandon](wiki:Arlan_III_Durrandon) conquered the Trident from the [Teagues](wiki:House_Teague), the last of the [Kings of the Rivers and Hills](wiki:Kings_of_the_Rivers_and_Hills).(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) [Harwyn Hoare](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), conquered the Trident from [Arrec Durrandon](wiki:Arrec_Durrandon) by using [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) longships to quickly move about the territory.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

### House Targaryen

During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Lord [Edmyn Tully](wiki:Edmyn_Tully) of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) declared for [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) and led the river lords of the Trident in rebellion against [Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare), [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers). After the [burning of Harrenhal](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal), Aegon the Conqueror named Edmyn his [Lord Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Later during the war, [Torrhen Stark](wiki:Torrhen_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), marched a great [northern](wiki:North) host to the banks of the Trident. Instead of fighting, Torrhen chose to submit to Aegon on the south bank.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) The [Inn of the Kneeling Man](wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man) is allegedly located where the two kings met.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

Prince [Maegor Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) hunted down the [Giant of the Trident](wiki:Giant_of_the_Trident), a robber knight, in [31 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Thousands of [Poor Fellows](wiki:Poor_Fellows) haunted the Trident during the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising).(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1)

Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) visited the Trident in [112 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Most of the river lords supported Rhaenyra during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), although the ruling [Tullys](wiki:House_Tully) were divided in intention.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) used [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) to become the terror of the Trident, attacking [Old Willow](wiki:Old_Willow), [White Willow](wiki:White_Willow), [Hogg Hall](wiki:Hogg_Hall), [Merrydown Dell](wiki:Merrydown_Dell), [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), [Stonyhead](wiki:Stonyhead), [Sweetwillow](wiki:Sweetwillow), [Sallydance](wiki:Sallydance), [Bowshot Bridge](wiki:Bowshot_Bridge), [Old Ferry](wiki:Old_Ferry), [Crone's Mill](wiki:Crone's_Mill), and [Bechester](wiki:Bechester).(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Led by the [Lads](wiki:Lads), however, the Lords of the Trident hastened the downfall of [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) and the rise of [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen).(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) Once the civil war ended, [Alysanne Blackwood](wiki:Alysanne_Blackwood) suggested that some of Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark)'s host of [northmen](wiki:Northmen) could marry widows and help rebuild the war-torn Trident.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

The [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads) once was located next to the Trident. It became known as the River Inn, since at that time the river flowed beneath its back door and half the rooms were built out over the water. It was said that the inn's guests could throw a line out their window and catch fish. There was also a ferry landing at the inn, where travelers could cross to [Lord Harroway's Town](wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town) and [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls). However, c.[220 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)-[230 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), the course of the river moved, flowing miles south of the inn.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

### Recent History

During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End), defeated [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone), in the decisive [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident). The site of Rhaegar's death became known as the [ruby ford](wiki:Ruby_ford).(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

Archmaester [Laurent](wiki:Laurent) wrote *[Old Places of the Trident](wiki:Old_Places_of_the_Trident)*.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) *[Trident Three](wiki:Trident_Three)* is a war galley of the [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet).(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

## Culture

The forks of the Trident are the easiest way to move goods and men across the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), with common sights including fisherfolk on skiffs, grain barges, floating shops, [mummers](wiki:Mummer)' boats,(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) and river galleys.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2) The rivers can have lines of poleboats stretching for a mile.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The [ruby ford](wiki:Ruby_ford) and the [Twins](wiki:Twins) are two of the major crossings over the rivers. The Trident and its branches can be effective barriers during wartime, but they can also facilitate quick travel by longship.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

The colors of the Trident's branches are represented in the arms of Houses [Shawney](wiki:House_Shawney) and [Strong](wiki:House_Strong), and the [river kings](wiki:River_king) from [House Teague](wiki:House_Teague) used a trident in their sigil.

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When the retinue of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) stops at the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn), [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and Prince [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) range along the woods on the northern bank of the Trident. The youths explore riverbank caves, track a [shadowcat](wiki:Shadowcat) to its lair, and eat fresh [trout](wiki:Trout) at a nearby holdfast.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) Near the [ruby ford](wiki:Ruby_ford), however, [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) tosses Joffrey's sword, [Lion's Tooth](wiki:Lion's_Tooth), into the Trident after an altercation.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-1)

Comatose after falling from the [First Keep](wiki:First_Keep), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) sees the rushing blue-green waters of the Trident in a dream.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Ragot17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)

The [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork) takes place along the eastern bank of the Green Fork.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) At [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) is declared [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) by the lords of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) and the [north](wiki:North).(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Since her brother [Rhaegar](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) was slain on the green banks of the Trident, [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) gives the name [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal) to her green [dragon](wiki:Dragon).(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

Fighting spreads north of the Trident almost to the [Twins](wiki:Twins) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0) A lordling from the Trident comes to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) to seek recompense from King Joffrey after his lands are despoiled by Lannisters.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0) Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) occupies the [ruby ford](wiki:Ruby_ford).(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)

For his service in allying [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) with [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) and [House Baratheon of King's Landing](wiki:House_Baratheon_of_King's_Landing), Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) receives the title [Lord Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident), replacing Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully) as liege lord of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) according to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Robb Stark includes [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident) amongst his titles.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0) Robb names Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) as [Warden of the Southern Marches](wiki:Warden_of_the_Southern_Marches) when he leaves Riverrun.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4)

Daenerys has a dream of the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident) in which she melts icy enemies with [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame), turning the river into a torrent.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)

Heavy rain make the Trident and its forks overflow their banks, slowing travel for Robb and Roose as they march to the [Twins](wiki:Twins).(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5)(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0) [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) defeats a group of [northmen](wiki:Northmen) in the [fighting at the fords of the Trident](wiki:Fighting_at_the_fords_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-1)(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0) The green and brown torrent of the Trident looks a mile wide when it is reached by Arya and [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) on their way to the Twins, and they find [Lord Harroway's Town](wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town) to be flooded.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0)

In the aftermath of the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Gregor crosses the Trident and seizes the ruby ford.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0) Sandor and Arya head for [Saltpans](wiki:Saltpans) after fighting the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) at the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn), and Arya notices that the once-raging Trident has died down. Arya eventually abandons the wounded Sandor to die along the banks of the river, and she continues on to Saltpans alone.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-3)

### A Feast for Crows

The [Hound](wiki:Hound) is said to have crossed the Trident with a hundred outlaws.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0) Because of the raiding, Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) wants Sandor's head.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0)

Searching for [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) is told by [Timeon](wiki:Timeon) that [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) is seeking a Stark girl who was with Sandor all along the Trident, but she does not realize that [Beric's outlaws](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) are seeking Arya instead of Sansa.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0) At the [Quiet Isle](wiki:Quiet_Isle), the [Elder Brother](wiki:Elder_Brother_(Quiet_Isle)) tells Brienne that the Hound died in his arms along the Trident and that Sandor is at rest.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0)

Outlaws roam freely on both sides of the Trident,(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-0) and hundreds of wolves hunt in a great pack near the river.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) Ser [Harwyn Plumm](wiki:Harwyn_Plumm) leads a detachment from [Darry](wiki:Darry) to hunt outlaws.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

During his visits to [Sisterton](wiki:Sisterton) and [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor), Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) hears that [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) is plundering and killing in the lands along the Trident.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_48-0)

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) expects that his work pacifying the Trident will be done once the [siege of Raventree](wiki:Siege_of_Raventree) ends.(wiki:Trident.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_49-0)
