## Appearances & Description

The [manse](wiki:Manse) has brick walls twelve feet high with [iron](wiki:Iron) spikes atop. There are three gates, the main gatehouse being the chief way in and out of the manse. There is one gate in the [garden](wiki:Garden) that is hidden by ivy. The third gate is a postern by the kennels. The main and postern gates are guarded by some of Illyrio's [eunuchs](wiki:Eunuch), while the garden gate is chained. The manse has pillared galleries, pointed arches, and a tiled courtyard in which [mushrooms](wiki:Tyrion's_mushrooms) grow from cracks.(wiki:Illyrio's_manse.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Illyrio decorates one of his bedchambers with soft, patterned [Myrish](wiki:Myr) carpets.(wiki:Illyrio's_manse.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
There is a [marble](wiki:Marble) pool with a statue of a naked youth in its center. The statue is lithe and handsome, made of painted marble so that the hair is blond and shoulder-length. It is poised to duel, with a [bravo](wiki:Bravo)'s blade in hand.(wiki:Illyrio's_manse.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) According to Illyrio, it was carved when he was sixteen by [Pytho Malanon](wiki:Pytho_Malanon).(wiki:Illyrio's_manse.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The pool is surrounded by six cherry trees. The gardens behind the manse are more extensive than the one by the pool.(wiki:Illyrio's_manse.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) Illyrio serves [snails](wiki:Snail) with garlic from his own gardens.(wiki:Illyrio's_manse.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Illyrio's cellar, long and dim, has barrel-vaulted ceilings and stone walls spotted with nitre. The cellar contains casks of [ale](wiki:Ale) and [wine](wiki:Wine), including sweet blackberry, sweet reds from the [Reach](wiki:Reach), sour [Dornish reds](wiki:Dornish_red), pale ambers from [Pentos](wiki:Pentos), green nectars and fire wines from [Myr](wiki:Myr), and [Arbor gold](wiki:Arbor_gold).(wiki:Illyrio's_manse.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)(wiki:Illyrio's_manse.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Illyrio also has wines from the [Further East](wiki:Further_East), including [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), [Yi Ti](wiki:Yi_Ti), and [Asshai](wiki:Asshai), as well as [strongwine](wiki:Strongwine) from the private stock of [Runceford Redwyne](wiki:Runceford_Redwyne), a former [Lord of the Arbor](wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor).(wiki:Illyrio's_manse.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)
The manse is guarded by plump [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied), eunuch soldiers from [Astapor](wiki:Astapor). Other [slaves](wiki:Slave) include two fat female cooks, young and old; a potboy; a freckled washerwoman; a [blond girl](wiki:Girl_bought_to_please_the_king) trained in [Lys](wiki:Lys); and a mousy older woman.(wiki:Illyrio's_manse.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Illyrio's_manse.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)
Illyrio reportedly keeps the [petrified hands](wiki:Grey_plague) of his second wife, [Serra](wiki:Serra), in his bedchamber.(wiki:Illyrio's_manse.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Daenerys](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) and [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) stay at Illyrio's manse in [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) for half a year while they await Daenerys's marriage to Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo). Before she is presented to Drogo for the first time, Daenerys is bathed by Illyrio's servants.(wiki:Illyrio's_manse.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos) offers Daenerys the hospitality of his home while she is in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth). She thinks that Xaro's palace, which appears larger than a market town, makes Illyrio's manse look like a swineherd's hovel.(wiki:Illyrio's_manse.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After crossing the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea), [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) is hosted by Illyrio in his manse. He finds clean fine clothes, made for a small boy, in a [cedar](wiki:Cedar) chest inlaid with [lapis](wiki:Lapis) and [mother-of-pearl](wiki:Mother-of-pearl). Tyrion explores the manse until Illyrio convinces him to accompany him to meet some friends to the east. Tyrion takes some [poisonous mushrooms](wiki:Tyrion's_mushrooms) from the courtyard.(wiki:Illyrio's_manse.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)
