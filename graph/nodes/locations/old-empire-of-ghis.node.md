## Origins

### Rise

The Old Empire rose from the ancient [Old Ghis](wiki:Old_Ghis) in [Ghiscar](wiki:Ghiscar), a city founded by [Grazdan the Great](wiki:Grazdan_the_Great). Old Ghis fielded highly-disciplined [lockstep legions](wiki:Ghiscari_legions) of free soldiers reputed to have been nearly unbeatable on the battlefield. The architecture of Old Ghis was dominated by massive brick towers and pyramids built using [slave](wiki:Slave) labor.(wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Since the Ghiscari hills were rich in [copper](wiki:Copper), the Old Empire used [bronze](wiki:Bronze) weapons.(wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

After colonizing nearby land, Old Ghis began subjugating its neighbors.(wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf25.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) The Ghiscari expanded south to [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos) and the [Basilisk Isles](wiki:Basilisk_Isles), building [Gorosh](wiki:Gorosh) as a penal colony at [Wyvern Point](wiki:Wyvern_Point), [Zamettar](wiki:Zamettar) at the mouth of the [Zamoyos](wiki:Zamoyos), and [Gorgai](wiki:Gogossos) on the [Isle of Tears](wiki:Isle_of_Tears).(wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis.cite_ref-Rawoiafgorosh.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### Ghiscari Wars

After the [Valyrians](wiki:Valyrians) discovered [dragons](wiki:Dragons) and quickly rose to power, the Ghiscari wanted dragons for their own. [Five great wars](wiki:Ghiscari_wars) were fought between Ghiscar and the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold),(wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) with the [Tall Men](wiki:Tall_Men) of the [Kingdom of Sarnor](wiki:Kingdom_of_Sarnor) participating in three of the conflicts.(wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf835_7-0) The Ghiscari won some battles, as there is a large ancient [tapestry](wiki:Tapestry) in the [heart of the Great Pyramid](wiki:Heart_(Great_Pyramid)) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) showing the last survivors of a defeated Valyrian army passing beneath the yoke and being chained.(wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) However, the Ghiscari eventually lost each war.(wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

The Old Empire fell five thousand years ago.(wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) In the [Fifth Ghiscari War](wiki:Fifth_Ghiscari_War) the Valyrians smashed the Ghiscari legions and used their dragons to burn Old Ghis and its pyramids to the ground. The Valyrians sowed the fields with salt and sulfur to prevent any surviving inhabitants from returning.(wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

### Aftermath

With the destruction of [Old Ghis](wiki:Old_Ghis), the Old Empire collapsed and was conquered by the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold). The Valyrians learned [slavery](wiki:Slavery) from the Ghiscari and applied it to other lands.(wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf26.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

After the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria), the [Slaver Cities](wiki:Slaver_Cities) of [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay) and [New Ghis](wiki:New_Ghis) overthrew their Valyrian overlords and reestablished control of the slave trade.(wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf25.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) During the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood), the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) destroyed northern Ghiscari colonies, whose ruins are now called [Krazaaj Has](wiki:Krazaaj_Has),(wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis.cite_ref-Rawoiafkrazaaj_has.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) [Vaes Efe](wiki:Vaes_Efe),(wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis.cite_ref-Rawoiafvaes_efe.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) [Vaes Diaf](wiki:Vaes_Diaf),(wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis.cite_ref-Rawoiafvaes_diaf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) and [Vaes Mejhah](wiki:Vaes_Mejhah).(wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis.cite_ref-Rawoiafvaes_mejhah.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
