### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Since the Kingsroad Gate has not been used recently, ice freezes its drawbridge chains.(wiki:East_Gate.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) When Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), decides to send the feuding [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) and [Manderlys](wiki:House_Manderly) against [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), he orders the Freys to gather by Winterfell's main gates and the Manderlys by the east gate.(wiki:East_Gate.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
