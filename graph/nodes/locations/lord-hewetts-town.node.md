## Appearances & Description

Twice as big as [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport) on [Pyke](wiki:Pyke_(island)), Lord Hewett's Town contains a stone quay, a pier-lined harbor, and a [sept](wiki:Sept). [Oakenshield Castle](wiki:Oakenshield_Castle) sits above the town's harbor.(wiki:Lord_Hewett's_Town.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Lord Hewett's Town is captured by the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) during their [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields). Though shops and houses are looted, only the [sept](wiki:Sept) is burned.(wiki:Lord_Hewett's_Town.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

[Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North), uses the town as his seat of power while he remains in the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands). Euron decrees that widows and orphans from the battle are to be sold as [slaves](wiki:Slaves) instead of being taken back to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) as [thralls](wiki:Thrall), which disturbs [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy).(wiki:Lord_Hewett's_Town.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
