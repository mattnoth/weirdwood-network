## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Moqorro](wiki:Moqorro) tells [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) that three missing ships will rejoin the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) at Yaros. The [Lord Captain of the Iron Fleet](wiki:Lord_Captain_of_the_Iron_Fleet) rewards the [red priest](wiki:Red_priest) with a golden torque when the missing ships are found waiting near the cliffs of Yaros.
