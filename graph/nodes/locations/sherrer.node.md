## Appearances & Description

There is an [alehouse](wiki:Ale) located next to the stone bridge, owned by a man named [Joss](wiki:Joss). Also located in Sherrer are the stables of Ser [Willum](wiki:Willum), who may be the Knight of Sherrer.(wiki:Sherrer.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

At the beginning of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), the population of Sherrer is decimated, and their houses burned by [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms) posing as brigands. The people find shelter in the stone holdfast of the village, and the raiders consider setting a fire to smoke them out, before deciding to attack Mummer's Ford instead.(wiki:Sherrer.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
Afterwards, the surviving villagers are brought to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) by Ser [Karyl Vance](wiki:Karyl_Vance), Ser [Raymun Darry](wiki:Raymun_Darry), and Ser [Marq Piper](wiki:Marq_Piper), as witnesses against the raiders. The former owner of the destroyed alehouse, [Joss](wiki:Joss), tells of the attack to the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), and his story is corroborated by an old man who used to work in Ser [Willum](wiki:Willum)'s stables. In response, Eddard sends a force led by Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) to bring Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) to justice.(wiki:Sherrer.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

The [Mad Huntsman](wiki:Mad_Huntsman) accuses [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) of crimes against the population of Sherrer, stating that girls of six and seven were raped and babies were killed. Sandor replies that he was not at Sherrer, nor at the [Mummer's Ford](wiki:Mummer's_Ford).(wiki:Sherrer.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
