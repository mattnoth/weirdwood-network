## Appearances & Description

Located near the [broken tower](wiki:Broken_tower), the First Keep is a squad and round drum tower(wiki:First_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) taller than it appears. It is decorated with weather-worn [gargoyles](wiki:Gargoyle).(wiki:First_Keep.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:First_Keep.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The First Keep's blind side is near the guards hall.(wiki:First_Keep.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) In the fortress's shadow is an ancient [lichyard](wiki:Lichyard) containing the graves of servants for the [Kings of Winter](wiki:Kings_of_Winter).(wiki:First_Keep.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The entrance to the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell) is also near the First Keep.(wiki:First_Keep.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Origins

The First Keep has been rebuilt numerous times, so its origin is uncertain.(wiki:First_Keep.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_stormlands:_storm.27s_end.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Some believe it was built by the [First Men](wiki:First_Men), but Maester [Kennet](wiki:Kennet_(maester)) determined the round tower in its current form was built after the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals), since the First Men and early [Andals](wiki:Andals) constructed square fortifications.(wiki:First_Keep.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_north:_winterfell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
The fortress is empty(wiki:First_Keep.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) and has not been used for several centuries.(wiki:First_Keep.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:First_Keep.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) enjoys climbing the First Keep and swinging from its [gargoyles](wiki:Gargoyle). Sometimes he climbs from the First Keep to the nearby [broken tower](wiki:Broken_tower) so he can feed [crows](wiki:Crow).(wiki:First_Keep.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

While climbing the [gargoyles](wiki:Gargoyle), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) discovers Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) and her brother, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), cavorting in the First Keep. Jaime tosses Bran from a window,(wiki:First_Keep.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) crippling the boy.(wiki:First_Keep.cite_ref-Ragot9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
[Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) looks at the gargoyles using the [far-eye](wiki:Far-eye) in Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin)'s turret.(wiki:First_Keep.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Bran has trouble remembering his fall, but he is disconcerted when he sees the First Keep's gargoyles.(wiki:First_Keep.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Following the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell), Bran discovers that one side of the First Keep has fallen away, shattering gargoyles and crushing at least one body. Its floors and beams have burned away, allowing Bran to see the privy.(wiki:First_Keep.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) includes the First Keep when she builds a snow castle of Winterfell at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie).(wiki:First_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When they visit the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell), [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) and [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin) pass the ruins of the First Keep, finding it filling with snow.(wiki:First_Keep.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Ser [Aenys Frey](wiki:Aenys_Frey)'s squire is found naked and frozen in the castle's lichyard,(wiki:First_Keep.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) and later the bloodied body of [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)) is found near the ruined keep.(wiki:First_Keep.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
