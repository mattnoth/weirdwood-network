## Appearances & Description

The Round Hall is found in the single massive tower of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), located below bedchambers, the [maester](wiki:Maester)'s cell, and the [rookery](wiki:Rookery).(wiki:Round_Hall.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Nearby is the castle yard.(wiki:Round_Hall.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The throne of the old [Storm Kings](wiki:Storm_Kings) from [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon), now used by the [Lords Paramount of the Stormlands](wiki:Lords_Paramount_of_the_Stormlands) from [House Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon), is located on a dais in the Round Hall.(wiki:Round_Hall.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Origins

[Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon), [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End) and [Lord Paramount of the Stormlands](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Stormlands), was in the Round Hall with his young son [Boremund](wiki:Boremund_Baratheon) on his lap when Princess [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) blamed him for the death of her mother, [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon).(wiki:Round_Hall.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) and the [greens](wiki:Greens) both sought the support of Lord [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon). Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) and Prince [Lucerys Velaryon](wiki:Lucerys_Velaryon) were sent to represent their respective factions and were received by Borros in the Round Hall.(wiki:Round_Hall.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
