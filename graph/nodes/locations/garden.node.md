## Culture

All sectors of society are known to keep gardens, such as the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) who keep homely and functional cottage gardens,(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) and nobles who may have more extravagant gardens. Some gardens are kept for pleasure, sustenance, or both, with a mixture of ornamental and edible plants.(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Attractive fruit trees, such as cherry(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) and lemon,(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) are also sometimes a feature of gardens.(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Food can be grown in a glass garden (real-world greenhouse) in [winter](wiki:Winter).(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Many individuals draw comfort from spending time in gardens.(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
[Godswoods](wiki:Godswood) have garden features. Many godswoods still found in the south now serve as secular gardens.(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

In [Vaes Tolorro](wiki:Vaes_Tolorro), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) puts [Rakharo](wiki:Rakharo) in charge of pulling up the plaza in an effort to turn it into a garden.(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0) Later at the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying) in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), [Pyat Pree](wiki:Pyat_Pree) falsely tells Daenerys her visit is over, and to follow him into a garden.(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-1) After the destruction of the House of the Undying, [ghost grass](wiki:Ghost_grass) is found growing in the [Garden of Gehane](wiki:Garden_of_Gehane).(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-1)
North along the eastern side of [the river](wiki:Gods_Eye_river) running south from the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye), [Yoren](wiki:Yoren) and his men come across an abandoned cottage. [Tarber](wiki:Tarber) finds a garden in the backyard where they pull some onions and radishes.(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), [Gendry](wiki:Gendry), and [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie) encounter the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) at the cottage of dead [Old Pate](wiki:Old_Pate). His cottage is burned and his garden overgrown.(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0)
[Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm) plays [monsters-and-maidens](wiki:Monsters-and-maidens) with [Shireen Baratheon](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon) and [Patchface](wiki:Patchface) at [Aegon's Garden](wiki:Aegon's_Garden) in [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)
Missandei finds Daenerys in the terrace garden of the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). Dany has the girl promise to never lie to her, to never betray her.(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Daenerys draws great solace from her garden.(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) states that after the war she means to build a new palace beyond the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush); in a dream she sees the palace surrounded by woods and gardens.(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)
Cersei is appalled to hear and see that hundreds of [sparrows](wiki:Sparrows) are encamped in the gardens of the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor).(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Varamyr Sixskins](wiki:Varamyr_Sixskins) recalls that a dozen villages [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) paid him a kind of homage; offering, among other things, fruit from their orchards and vegetables from their gardens.(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Radwdprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1)
Daenerys entertains the representatives from [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) in the [audience chamber](wiki:Audience_chamber_(Great_Pyramid)) of the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) and its terrace.(wiki:Garden.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
