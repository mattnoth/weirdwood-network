## Origins

According to Archmaester [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn), Greenstone is an ancient castle.(wiki:Greenstone.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
In [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), the elderly Lord Estermont had been wed to a lady of [House Tarth](wiki:House_Tarth). His wife's widowed mother, [Larissa Velaryon](wiki:Larissa_Velaryon), chose to stay with her daughter at Greenstone after the [wedding](wiki:Wedding). Princess [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) visited Larissa, her cousin and the first of her favorites, at the castle.(wiki:Greenstone.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) along with Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) and some of the court from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), including Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), spent a fortnight at Greenstone. Robert [bedded](wiki:Bedded) a widowed [Estermont cousin](wiki:Estermont_(cousin_of_Robert)) of his and Cersei believes that it was there that she and Jaime conceived their eldest child, Prince [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). Jaime gave the castle the nickname "Greenshit".(wiki:Greenstone.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
