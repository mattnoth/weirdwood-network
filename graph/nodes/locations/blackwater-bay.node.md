## Origins

The shores of Blackwater Bay were historically disputed by [river kings](wiki:River_kings),(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) [Storm Kings](wiki:Storm_King),(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) [Kings of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:Kings_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers),(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) and kings from [House Darklyn](wiki:House_Darklyn) of [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) and [House Massey](wiki:House_Massey) of [Stonedance](wiki:Stonedance).(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
Prior to [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)) and [House Velaryon](wiki:House_Velaryon) of [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark) prospered from their control of the [Gullet](wiki:Gullet), which divided the bay from the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea).(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
[King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), the new capital of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), quickly developed at the mouth of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) along Blackwater Bay.(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
A mock sea battle was held in Blackwater Bay during the [Golden Wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding), and Princess [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) sailed on the bay with visiting noblewomen.(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
Princess [Daella Targaryen](wiki:Daella_Targaryen) became seasick while sailing to [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark) to meet [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon).(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Princess [Gael Targaryen](wiki:Gael_Targaryen) drowned herself in the bay after giving birth to a stillborn son.(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) blockaded Blackwater Bay by closing the Gullet with the [Velaryon fleet](wiki:Velaryon_fleet) of Corlys, now [Lord of the Tides](wiki:Lord_of_the_Tides). The [greens](wiki:Greens) tried in vain to break the [blacks](wiki:Blacks)' naval superiority in the [Battle of the Gullet](wiki:Battle_of_the_Gullet), but sacked [Spicetown](wiki:Spicetown) so severely it was never rebuilt.(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
Rather than wed Lord [Thaddeus Rowan](wiki:Thaddeus_Rowan), Lady [Baela Targaryen](wiki:Baela_Targaryen) fled King's Landing and had a fisherman bring her to Driftmark, where she wed Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon).(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Prior to the [Maiden's Day Cattle Show](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Cattle_Show), [Lucinda Penrose](wiki:Lucinda_Penrose) was attacked by outlaws while [hawking](wiki:Hawk) along the bay.(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) is well familiar with the waters around King's Landing from his years of smuggling.(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Rasos5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) and Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) sail on [Storm Dancer](wiki:Storm_Dancer) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Seaswift](wiki:Seaswift) transports Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) from King's Landing to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
The [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater) occurs at the mouth of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) where it meets the bay. This battle pits the forces loyal to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) against those loyal to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), devises a system with a chain boom pulled across where the river widens into the bay. This chain is raised once the majority of Stannis's [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet) is up river, and [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) is deployed to ignite many of the ships. The current pulls these burning ships towards the mouth of the river and against the chain, which results in the destruction of much of the fleet. [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan)'s galleys remain outside of the chain in the bay.(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Salladhor's ships rescue as many of Stannis's men as they can from the shores of the bay and retreat with the king to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)).(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) Salladhor is named the [Lord of Blackwater Bay](wiki:Lord_of_Blackwater_Bay) by [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent), the Hand of Stannis.(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
After the death of King Joffrey at [his royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding), [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) escape King's Landing by sailing aboard the [Merling King](wiki:Merling_King) into the fog of Blackwater Bay.(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Rasos61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

With the aftermath of the Battle of the Blackwater hampering trade at King's Landing, the port of [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) is busier than ever.(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
The [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet) transports two thousand [westermen](wiki:Westermen) across Blackwater Bay for the [siege of Dragonstone](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone).(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)
Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) sometimes eats shells along the bay while traveling the [Rosby road](wiki:Rosby_road).(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Varys](wiki:Varys) has a ship smuggle [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) from King's Landing to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos).(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)
Salladhor, titular lord of the bay, abandons the cause of Stannis to return to the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones).(wiki:Blackwater_Bay.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)
