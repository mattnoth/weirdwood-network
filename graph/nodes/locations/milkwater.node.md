## Origins

Kings and heroes of the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) are buried along the valley of the Milkwater.(wiki:Milkwater.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

When the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) camps at the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men), [Jarman Buckwell](wiki:Jarman_Buckwell) has his sharpest-eyed men hide along the Milkwater. [Thoren Smallwood](wiki:Thoren_Smallwood)'s [rangers](wiki:Rangers) scouts along the eastern shore.(wiki:Milkwater.cite_ref-Racok34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
After [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand) arrives at the First, Thoren is sent with a group of rangers to scout the Milkwater for [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder)'s host of [free folk](wiki:Free_folk).(wiki:Milkwater.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), part of Qhorin's group sent to scout the [Skirling Pass](wiki:Skirling_Pass), has a vision through [Ghost](wiki:Ghost) of Mance's encampment at the source of the Milkwater.(wiki:Milkwater.cite_ref-Racok53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Mance's host digs through hundreds of graves along the valley of the Milkwater in search of the [Horn of Winter](wiki:Horn_of_Winter).(wiki:Milkwater.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
[Kedge Whiteye](wiki:Kedge_Whiteye) reports that he and [Bannen](wiki:Bannen) observed Mance's great host slowly marching along the Milkwater toward the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Milkwater.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), plans to confront the wildlings at the Fist of the First Men, but the great ranging is overwhelmed first by [wights](wiki:Wights) during the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist).(wiki:Milkwater.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Milkwater.cite_ref-Rasos18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Having been captured by the [Lord of Bones](wiki:Lord_of_Bones), Jon is brought to Mance's camp in the valley of the Milkwater.(wiki:Milkwater.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Jon observes snow and pale mists swirling along the river.(wiki:Milkwater.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Men are able to cross from the western bank to the eastern at shallows near the Fist.(wiki:Milkwater.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), a wise woman respected by the Milkwater free folk is one of the lesser leaders who kneel to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Milkwater.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
As free folk slowly pass through the [Wall](wiki:Wall) at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), [Tormund](wiki:Tormund) compares the tunnel through the ice to sucking the Milkwater through a reed.(wiki:Milkwater.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
