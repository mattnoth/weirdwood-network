## Origins

After Lord Staunton and his wife died of the [Shivers](wiki:Shivers) in [59 AC](wiki:59_AC), their children locked themselves in their bedchambers but still died of the [illness](wiki:Illness).(wiki:Rook's_Rest.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aemon)) and her dragon, [Meleys](wiki:Meleys), were killed in the [battle at Rook's Rest](wiki:Battle_at_Rook's_Rest). Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) and Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) executed [Lord Staunton](wiki:Lord_Staunton) after the battle. Lord [Walys Mooton](wiki:Walys_Mooton) led the [retaking of Rook's Rest](wiki:Retaking_of_Rook's_Rest), but he died in an attempt to slay [Sunfyre](wiki:Sunfyre).(wiki:Rook's_Rest.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
