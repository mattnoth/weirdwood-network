## Appearances & Description

Horseshoe-shaped and several miles long, the port of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) is situated in a sheltered harbor.(wiki:Port_of_Qarth.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Ships from the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles), [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), and the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) are permitted to dock at the far side of the harbor.(wiki:Port_of_Qarth.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
The port's streets are lined with [winesinks](wiki:Winesink), warehouses, gaming dens, [brothels](wiki:Prostitution), and temples. Winesinks, warehouses, and gaming dens line the streets, cheek by jowl with cheap brothels and the temples of peculiar [gods](wiki:God). Buying and selling goes on all day and night at the waterfront marketplace, and goods might be had for a fraction of what they cost at the city's bazaar.(wiki:Port_of_Qarth.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
Cutpurses, cutthroats, spellsellers, and moneychangers mingle in the crowd. Old women sell flavored waters and goat's milk from glazed ceramic jugs strapped to their shoulders. Seamen from half a hundred nations wander amongst the stalls, drinking spiced liquors and trading jokes in their languages. The air smells of salt and frying fish, of hot tar and honey, of incense and oil and sperm.(wiki:Port_of_Qarth.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
Items for sale include beautiful [bronze](wiki:Bronze) daggers, [brass](wiki:Brass) platters, dried squids, carved [onyx](wiki:Onyx), and a magical elixir made of [virgin's](wiki:Maiden) milk and [shade of the evening](wiki:Shade_of_the_evening). [Dragon eggs](wiki:Dragon_egg) look suspiciously like painted rocks. Food includes honey-roasted mice and fat white cherries.(wiki:Port_of_Qarth.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

When [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) decides to flee [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) she goes down to the docks to attempt to book ship passage to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). As she and her companions pass the long stone quays reserved for the ships of the [Thirteen](wiki:Thirteen), she sees chests of saffron, frankincense, and pepper being off-loaded from [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos)'s ornate [Vermillion Kiss](wiki:Vermillion_Kiss).(wiki:Port_of_Qarth.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) Casks of [wine](wiki:Wine), bales of [sourleaf](wiki:Sourleaf), and pallets of striped hides are trundled up the gangplank onto the [Bride in Azure](wiki:Bride_in_Azure), to sail on the evening tide. A crowd has gathered around the Spicer galley [Sunblaze](wiki:Sunblaze) to bid on [slaves](wiki:Slave) from [Astapor](wiki:Astapor). At the far end of the harbor, Daenerys dismounts beside a gaming pit where a [basilisk](wiki:Basilisk) is tearing a dog to pieces amidst a shouting ring of sailors.(wiki:Port_of_Qarth.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)
