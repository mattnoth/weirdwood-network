## Origins

Prior to [Nymeria's War](wiki:Nymeria's_War), the Tor was the seat of kings from [House Jordayne](wiki:House_Jordayne).(wiki:Tor.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_the_andals_arrive.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
During the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), the steward of the Tor surrendered the castle to King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) since Lord [Jordayne](wiki:House_Jordayne) had recently died. The Tor was quickly recaptured by [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) after Aegon returned to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), however. [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) unleashed the [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame) of [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) on the Tor after Dornishmen attacked [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath).(wiki:Tor.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
[Morion Martell](wiki:Morion_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), gathered his army to the Tor and [Ghost Hill](wiki:Ghost_Hill) in preparation for the [Fourth Dornish War](wiki:Fourth_Dornish_War), but King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) destroyed Morion's fleet as it crossed the [Sea of Dorne](wiki:Sea_of_Dorne).(wiki:Tor.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
