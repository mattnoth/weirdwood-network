## Origins

Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel) describes Ny Sar as a "city of fountains, alive with song".(wiki:Ny_Sar.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

[Garris the Grey](wiki:Garris_the_Grey), Prince of Ny Sar, expelled [hairy men](wiki:Hairy_men) who lived where the city of [Norvos](wiki:Norvos) would later develop. Garris's [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar) did not remain in the area, however.(wiki:Ny_Sar.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_norvos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Prince [Garin](wiki:Garin_(prince)) of [Chroyane](wiki:Chroyane) led a combined host of the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar), but they were defeated by the [Volantenes](wiki:Volantis) and their [Valyrian](wiki:Valyria) kin at Volantis during the [Second Spice War](wiki:Second_Spice_War). Princess [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria) of Ny Sar, fearing the enslavement of the people of her own city, gathered every ship that remained upon the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne) and filled them full of as many women and children as they could carry. Nymeria led these [ten thousand ships](wiki:Ten_thousand_ships) down the Rhoyne past [Sarhoy](wiki:Sarhoy) and into the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea), eventually arriving in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Ny_Sar.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

The ruins of Nymeria's palace are all that remains, a colossal palace of pink and green [marble](wiki:Marble), its collapsed domes and broken spires looming large above a row of covered archways.(wiki:Ny_Sar.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) The [Noyne](wiki:Noyne) as far south as Ny Sar is now patrolled by galleys from Norvos.(wiki:Ny_Sar.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_norvos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

[Griff](wiki:Jon_Connington), [Young Griff](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)), and their company pass Ny Sar on their way to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis). [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sees the ruins on the eastern bank of the Rhoyne: "crooked walls and fallen towers, broken domes and rows of rotted wooden pillars, streets choked by mud and overgrown with purple moss."(wiki:Ny_Sar.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) According to Tyrion, the city is ten times as large as [Ghoyan Drohe](wiki:Ghoyan_Drohe). Tyrion notices big bonesnapper [turtles](wiki:Turtle) basking in the sun. Shortly thereafter a [horned turtle of enormous size](wiki:Old_Men_of_the_River) appears not six yards from the *[Shy Maid](wiki:Shy_Maid)*. [Ysilla](wiki:Ysilla) exclaims they are blessed for having seen the creature, while [Yandry](wiki:Yandry) says it is the [Old Man of the River](wiki:Old_Man_of_the_River).(wiki:Ny_Sar.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
