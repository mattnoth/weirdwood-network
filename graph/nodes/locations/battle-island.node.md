## Origins

It is unknown how the island came to be named. Some tales claim [dragons](wiki:Dragons) once roosted on Battle Isle until the first member of [House Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) put an end to them.(wiki:Battle_Island.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
A large square fortress of [black stone](wiki:Black_stone) was constructed on Battle Island by unknown builders.(wiki:Battle_Island.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) The Hightowers then ruled as kings from the isle, using the stone fortress as a foundation as they built the [Hightower](wiki:Hightower) atop it.(wiki:Battle_Island.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) [Gyles I Gardener](wiki:Gyles_I_Gardener), [King of the Reach](wiki:King_of_the_Reach), once sacked [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) but was unable to conquer the Hightower on Battle Isle.(wiki:Battle_Island.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)
