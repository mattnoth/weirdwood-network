## Origins

According to a semi-canon source, there was once only a strait in that location, north of the city of [Valyria](wiki:Valyria).(wiki:Smoking_Sea.cite_ref-5)
In [102 BC](wiki:Years_before_Aegon's_Conquest), every hill surrounding Valyria for five hundred miles exploded, filling the air with ash and smoke and fire. Huge fissures opened in the ground, and earthquakes destroyed palaces, temples, and towns, while lakes boiled or turned to acid. The [Fourteen Flames](wiki:Fourteen_Flames), the volcanoes of Valyria, sent molten rock a thousand feet into the air, and red clouds rained down [dragonglass](wiki:Dragonglass). To the north, the ground splintered and collapsed, and the surrounding [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea) rushed in. The cataclysmic [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria) fragmented the [Valyrian peninsula](wiki:Valyrian_peninsula) into numerous smaller islands, creating the Smoking Sea between them.(wiki:Smoking_Sea.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
After the Doom, the [tigers](wiki:Tigers_(Volantis)), a political party in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), sent a fleet to reclaim [Valyria](wiki:Valyria), but the ships vanished in the Smoking Sea.(wiki:Smoking_Sea.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Smoking_Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_doom_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
[Brightroar](wiki:Brightroar), the ancient [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) [greatsword](wiki:Greatsword) of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister), was lost after the Doom, when King [Tommen II Lannister](wiki:Tommen_II_Lannister) carried it with him when he sailed to Valyria and never returned.(wiki:Smoking_Sea.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Circa [292 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest),(wiki:Smoking_Sea.cite_ref-9) [Gerion Lannister](wiki:Gerion_Lannister) went on a quest to Valyria to find Brightroar, and any other treasures that might have survived the Doom, and also never returned.(wiki:Smoking_Sea.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) sent men to look for his lost brother, who traced the course of the [Laughing Lion](wiki:Laughing_Lion) as far as Volantis, where half of Gerion's crew had deserted him because of his intent to sail into the Smoking Sea. He was forced to buy [slaves](wiki:Slave) to replace them.(wiki:Smoking_Sea.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

While traveling on the [Balerion](wiki:Balerion_(ship)) in the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea) from [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos), Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) convinces Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) to instead sail for [Astapor](wiki:Astapor), buy an army of [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied), and continue to Pentos overland. When she points out that there are dangers on such a long march, he tells her that there are dangers at sea as well, such as storms, [krakens](wiki:Kraken), [corsairs](wiki:Corsairs) and [pirates](wiki:Pirates) in the southern route, or the demon-haunted Smoking Sea in the northern route.(wiki:Smoking_Sea.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

King [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) claims to have sailed the Smoking Sea and seen [Valyria](wiki:Valyria), but Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw) is skeptical.(wiki:Smoking_Sea.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

In [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) examines [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos)'s old [tapestry](wiki:Tapestry), which was made before the Doom that took Valyria. Dany notices that there is no Smoking Sea depicted, and that [Valyria](wiki:Valyria) is not yet an island.(wiki:Smoking_Sea.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Aboard the [Selaesori Qhoran](wiki:Selaesori_Qhoran), [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) remembers his Uncle [Gerion Lannister](wiki:Gerion_Lannister)'s quest to Valyria, recalling that Gerion had bought slaves to crew his ship, as his intent to sail the Smoking Sea had caused half his original crew to desert him.(wiki:Smoking_Sea.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
[Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) remembers that, among other exploits, his brother Euron braved the Smoking Sea and lived to tell of it.(wiki:Smoking_Sea.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
