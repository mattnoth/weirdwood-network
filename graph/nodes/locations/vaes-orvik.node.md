## Origins

Vaes Orvik was a small [Qaathi](wiki:Qaathi) city destroyed by the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) during the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood). Because of the large number of [slaves](wiki:Slaves) taken during its conquest, its [Dothraki language](wiki:Dothraki_language) name means City of the Whip.(wiki:Vaes_Orvik.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_vaes_orvik.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Vaes_Orvik.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
