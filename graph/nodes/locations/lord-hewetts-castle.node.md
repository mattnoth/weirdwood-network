## Appearances & Description

Sitting above the harbor of [Lord Hewett's Town](wiki:Lord_Hewett's_Town), the castle is small but strong. Its thick walls and studded oaken gates are reminiscent of the [iron](wiki:Iron)-and-[oak](wiki:Oak) sigil of [House Hewett](wiki:House_Hewett). The castle has green-roofed towers. Lord Hewett's bedchamber is accessible from the hall by a winding stone stair.(wiki:Lord_Hewett's_castle.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast of Crows[ ]

During the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) storm the castle and break and burn its great gates. After the battle, [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) speaks with Lords [Gorold Goodbrother](wiki:Gorold_Goodbrother), [Dunstan Drumm](wiki:Dunstan_Drumm), and [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw) in the castle's yard.(wiki:Lord_Hewett's_castle.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
[Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North), holds his victory feast in the castle's hall. He makes the noble ladies, daughters, and daughters-in-law of Lord [Humfrey Hewett](wiki:Humfrey_Hewett) serve the ironborn, while Lord Hewett is tied up and forced to watch. Euron humiliates the women by ordering them to disrobe and continue serving his men while nude.(wiki:Lord_Hewett's_castle.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Euron sleeps with [Falia Flowers](wiki:Falia_Flowers), Humfrey's bastard daughter and a former serving girl of the castle, in Humfrey's bedchamber.(wiki:Lord_Hewett's_castle.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
