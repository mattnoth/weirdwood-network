## Origins

Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel) was a foundling in an empty stall of the Scribe's Hearth in [272 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), where he was found by an [acolyte](wiki:Acolyte).(wiki:Scribe's_Hearth.cite_ref-Rtwoiafpreface.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Appearances & Description

Scribe's Hearth is found just past the gates of the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel). [Acolytes](wiki:Acolyte) there can be hired by the people of [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) to write wills and read letters. Books and maps are sold at other stalls.(wiki:Scribe's_Hearth.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) stops at Scribe's Hearth after arriving at the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) and looks at a map for the fastest way to the [Seneschal's Court](wiki:Seneschal's_Court).(wiki:Scribe's_Hearth.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
