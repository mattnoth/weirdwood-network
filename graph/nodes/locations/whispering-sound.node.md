## Origins

### Ancient History[ ]

The oldest [runic](wiki:Runes) records of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men), as well as records from [maesters](wiki:Maesters) that claim to have lived among the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest), confirm men have lived at the mouth of the [Honeywine](wiki:Honeywine) since the [Dawn Age](wiki:Dawn_Age). Maester [Jellicoe](wiki:Jellicoe) suggested the settlement at Whispering Sound began as a trading post where ships from [Valyria](wiki:Valyria), [Old Ghis](wiki:Old_Ghis), and the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles) could resupply and trade with so-called "elder races".(wiki:Whispering_Sound.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

### Recent History[ ]

The [Velaryon fleet](wiki:Velaryon_fleet) lingered in Whispering Sound when its commander, Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon), waited for the promised reinforcement from the [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet) for his expedition to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) to deal with the [Red Kraken](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy).(wiki:Whispering_Sound.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

With the destruction of the small fleet that [House Redwyne](wiki:House_Redwyne) keeps to defend the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor), the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) are able to send their longships into Whispering Sound and threaten Oldtown.(wiki:Whispering_Sound.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The [Cinnamon Wind](wiki:Cinnamon_Wind) fights off ironborn raiders as it sails along Whispering Sound towards Oldtown before being inspected by the crew of the [Huntress](wiki:Huntress).(wiki:Whispering_Sound.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
