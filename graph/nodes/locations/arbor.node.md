## Culture

The Arbor is among the richest places of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The island is known for making the best [wine](wiki:Wine) in Westeros,(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) including [Arbor reds](wiki:Arbor_red)(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and [Arbor golds](wiki:Arbor_gold).(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Wine from the Arbor is usually sweeter than wine from [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Peaches from the Arbor are huge and sweet.(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Traders from the Arbor have often sailed to [Qarth](wiki:Qarth).(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Origins

### Legend[ ]

[Gilbert of the Vines](wiki:Gilbert_of_the_Vines), the legendary founder of [House Redwyne](wiki:House_Redwyne), is said to have taught the Arbor's people to make wine.(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### Ironborn[ ]

In the era of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men), [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) rule under [driftwood kings](wiki:Driftwood_king) such as [Qhored Hoare](wiki:Qhored_Hoare) extended as far south as the Arbor.(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) [House Hoare](wiki:House_Hoare)'s sigil included a grape cluster to represent the Arbor.(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-14) Weaker ironborn kings eventually lost the Arbor, however, although [Joron I Blacktyde](wiki:Joron_I_Blacktyde) is said to have raided the island and carried off its younger women.(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### Kingdom of the Reach[ ]

In the aftermath of the ironborn's decline, the Arbor was an independent kingdom.(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) It was incorporated into the realm of [Meryn III Gardener](wiki:Meryn_III_Gardener), the [King of the Reach](wiki:King_of_the_Reach), when his cousin, the last [King of the Arbor](wiki:King_of_the_Arbor), was lost at sea.(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Fleets from the Arbor and [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) protected the Reach from naval attacks during the [Andal invasion](wiki:Andal_invasion).(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### Targaryen Era[ ]

There were reliable reports that Lord [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) [hawked](wiki:Hawk) at the Arbor as a guest of Lord [Redwyne](wiki:House_Redwyne) before [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Once king, Aegon I visited the Arbor on multiple occasions during his [royal progresses](wiki:Royal_progress).(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
[Elissa Farman](wiki:Elissa_Farman) sailed to [Bear Island](wiki:Bear_Island) and the Arbor by the time she was twenty.(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) sampled the Arbor's [wines](wiki:Wine) during a visit on [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor) in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC).(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) During her search for the missing [Aerea Targaryen](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen), [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) and her dragon, [Dreamfyre](wiki:Dreamfyre), were seen flying over the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands) and the Arbor.(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the Arbor supported King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) [Martyn Hightower](wiki:Martyn_Hightower_(son_of_Ormund)) squired at the Arbor with [Lord Redwyne](wiki:Lord_Redwyne).(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) The second son of Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake) was also [fostered](wiki:Fosterage) on the island by Lord Redwyne, but the youth died in a sailing accident.(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) While traveling from the Arbor to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for the [Maiden's Day Ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball), the party of [Patricia Redwyne](wiki:Patricia_Redwyne) was attacked three times by [outlaws](wiki:Outlaws).(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-1) Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) briefly visited the Arbor while returning from [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle).(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)
During the reign of King [Aerys I Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_I_Targaryen), the Arbor suffered from a drought and its grapes turned to raisins on the vine. The island was also raided by the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) of [Dagon Greyjoy](wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), around that time.(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)
Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) and Princess [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell) visited the Arbor on their way to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) where [their mother](wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran)) was to broker their [marriage](wiki:Marriage) to the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) twins, [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) and [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) [Horas](wiki:Horas_Redwyne) and [Hobber Redwyne](wiki:Hobber_Redwyne) bullied [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) when the youth came to the Arbor.(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) has traded at the Arbor(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) and sailed [Black Wind](wiki:Black_Wind) around the island to the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones).(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) sends [Dale Seaworth](wiki:Dale_Seaworth) to sail [Wraith](wiki:Wraith) along [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath), the [Broken Arm](wiki:Broken_Arm), the coast of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), and the Arbor, informing the populace of Stannis's claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne) states that [King Joffrey's Valor](wiki:King_Joffrey's_Valor) is being constructed at the Arbor in honor of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) spots big-bellied wine cogs from the Arbor at the [Ragman's Harbor](wiki:Ragman's_Harbor) of [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)
The Arbor is attacked by the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) after the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields). Most of the [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet) is away at the [siege of Dragonstone](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone), and the small fleet left to defend the island is [overwhelmed and destroyed](wiki:Invasion_of_the_Arbor). This allows the ironborn to send their ships into the [Whispering Sound](wiki:Whispering_Sound) and threaten [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown).(wiki:Arbor.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)
