## Appearances & Description

Fairmarket is located mostly on the southern shore of the [Blue Fork](wiki:Blue_Fork).(wiki:Fairmarket.cite_ref-Rtloiafwesteros.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) The wooden bridge which crosses the river at Fairmarket is newer and larger than another bridge upstream near [Oldstones](wiki:Oldstones).(wiki:Fairmarket.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Origins

The [river kings](wiki:River_king) of old refused charters which would have allowed towns like [Lord Harroway's Town](wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town), [Saltpans](wiki:Saltpans), and Fairmarket to expand and potentially become cities.(wiki:Fairmarket.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
[Pate of Fairmarket](wiki:Pate_of_Fairmarket) was a [peasant](wiki:Peasant) who unsuccessfully claimed to be a river king after the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) were conquered by the [Storm Kings](wiki:Storm_King) of [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon).(wiki:Fairmarket.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
The Storm King [Arrec Durrandon](wiki:Arrec_Durrandon) was defeated in a [battle at Fairmarket](wiki:Battle_at_Fairmarket) by [Harwyn Hoare](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), allowing Harwyn to conquer the riverlands from the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands).(wiki:Fairmarket.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) Rather than rule from the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands), Harwyn's son, [Halleck Hoare](wiki:Halleck_Hoare), made his seat a modest tower house at Fairmarket. Halleck's son [Harren the Black](wiki:Harren_Hoare), the last [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers), desired a grander seat and constructed [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) on the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye).(wiki:Fairmarket.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Widow Fairs](wiki:Widow_Fairs) were held at Fairmarket after the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Fairmarket.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen) met one of his mistresses, [Megette](wiki:Megette), after his horse threw a shoe while riding near Fairmarket in [155 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Fairmarket.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

While traveling from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) to the [Twins](wiki:Twins), [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) and [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident), sends scouts to Fairmarket to see if he can cross the [Blue Fork](wiki:Blue_Fork) there, but its wooden bridge has washed away from heavy rainfall and flooding.(wiki:Fairmarket.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord [Lucias Vypren](wiki:Lucias_Vypren) tracks a band of outlaws who may have been involved in the hanging of [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey) to Fairmarket but loses them there.(wiki:Fairmarket.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
While returning to the [Twins](wiki:Twins) from the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Ser [Ryman Frey](wiki:Ryman_Frey) and his party are attacked and killed two leagues south of Fairmarket.(wiki:Fairmarket.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
