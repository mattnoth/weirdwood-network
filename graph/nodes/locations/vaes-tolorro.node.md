## Appearances & Description

The city has large white walls encircling it, though they are cracked and crumbling. There are also wall towers. The city gates are broken.(wiki:Vaes_Tolorro.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The city is a maze of narrow, crooked alleys. The buildings are built close together, their façades blank and chalky. All buildings are windowless. Some of the houses have long fallen into rubble, and other buildings seem damaged by fire. The remnants of a gutted palace stand before a windswept plaza. [Devilgrass](wiki:Devilgrass) grows between the paving stones.(wiki:Vaes_Tolorro.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
A [marble](wiki:Marble) plinth stands at the crossroads between six alleys, but no statue stands on it. The [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) are presumed to have visited the city, taking its statues to [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak).(wiki:Vaes_Tolorro.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
There are grapevines, fig trees, peach trees, and other fruit trees in the abandoned gardens, hidden behind closed doors. There is enough grass to sustain a small herd of [horses](wiki:Horse). Its wells contain pure and cold water.(wiki:Vaes_Tolorro.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) There are also small animals in the city, most notably flies and red scorpions.(wiki:Vaes_Tolorro.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
There are remnants of the previous inhabitants in the city: the skulls of the unburied dead, bleached and broken. The twisty alleys contain old [bronze](wiki:Bronze) coins, bits of purple glass, and stone flagons with handles carved like snakes.(wiki:Vaes_Tolorro.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

While wandering the [red waste](wiki:Red_waste), the [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) stumbles upon the abandoned city. They make camp within its walls to recover from their travels while sending out scouts to discover what is near.(wiki:Vaes_Tolorro.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10) Daenerys presumes that Vaes Tolorro and the cities of its vicinity were destroyed "in ancient days" by enemies who crossed the red waste.(wiki:Vaes_Tolorro.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-11)
While inhabiting the city, Daenerys's [bloodriders](wiki:Bloodrider) are sent to explore its vicinity. [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) reaches [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) and returns to Vaes Tolorro with three new companions: [Pyat Pree](wiki:Pyat_Pree), [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos), and [Quaithe](wiki:Quaithe).(wiki:Vaes_Tolorro.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-12) The khalasar soon leaves Vaes Tolorro, heading for Qarth.(wiki:Vaes_Tolorro.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Planning to leave Qarth, Daenerys briefly considers travelling back to Vaes Tolorro to settle with her people, but thinks the journey back would be too difficult.(wiki:Vaes_Tolorro.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
