## Origins

Ibbish began as an [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibbenese) fishing village, but the colony developed into a port with a deep harbor. High white walls and the Whalebone Gates protected temples and treasuries.(wiki:Vaes_Aresak.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Vaes_Aresak.cite_ref-3)
[Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) began raiding from the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea) during the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood). Khal [Scoro](wiki:Scoro) took loot from Ibbish back to [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak), while Khal [Rogo](wiki:Rogo) burned the rebuilt city and forced ten thousand women into [slavery](wiki:Slavery). When Khal [Dhako](wiki:Dhako) approached Ibbish, the inhabitants retreated from his [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) back to [Ib](wiki:Ib). [Terrio Erastes](wiki:Terrio_Erastes) observed the angry Dhako burning Ibbish and its vicinity, and the Dothraki have since called it Vaes Aresak, meaning City of Cowards.(wiki:Vaes_Aresak.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) The Ibbenese have built [New Ibbish](wiki:New_Ibbish) to the northwest, but it is a much smaller town.(wiki:Vaes_Aresak.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
