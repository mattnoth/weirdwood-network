## Origins

King [Benedict II Justman](wiki:Benedict_II_Justman) added Rosby to the lands of the [river kings](wiki:River_king) during his reign.(wiki:Rosby.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

King [Halleck Hoare](wiki:Halleck_Hoare) conquered as far as [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) and Rosby, making [House Rosby](wiki:House_Rosby) vassals of the [Kings of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:Kings_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers).(wiki:Rosby.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Rosby [yielded](wiki:Yielding_of_Rosby) peacefully to [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Rosby.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) visited Rosby during their [royal progress](wiki:Royal_progress) in [51 AC](wiki:51_AC).(wiki:Rosby.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Rosby initially supported the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), but the castle was taken by Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) during the fighting.(wiki:Rosby.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) ordered the execution of Lord Rosby for treason after the [fall of King's Landing](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:Rosby.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) During her flight later in the war, Rhaenyra was refused admittance to the castle of Rosby by the late lord's daughter.(wiki:Rosby.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) After Rhaenyra's death, King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) sent his men with Lord [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon) to force the submissions of Rosby, [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale), and [Stokeworth](wiki:Stokeworth).(wiki:Rosby.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Rosby then raised the flag of [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) after the death of Aegon II.(wiki:Rosby.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Appearances & Description

Rosby sits just northeast of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) along the [Rosby road](wiki:Rosby_road), the shortest road between the capital and [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale). Lying around the small castle is a village with daub-and-wattle huts, a [sept](wiki:Sept), apple orchards, and fields of barley.(wiki:Rosby.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Rosby's maester is [Melwys](wiki:Melwys_Rivers).(wiki:Rosby.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

The majority of the supply of food for [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) comes from Rosby and [Stokeworth](wiki:Stokeworth), both as yet untouched by the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Rosby.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Prince [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) is held at Rosby on [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s command to keep the royal family spread apart and make it harder for [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) to end the claims of Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s children to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Rosby.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Ser [Boros Blount](wiki:Boros_Blount) is held in Rosby's dungeon for surrendering Tommen to Ser [Jacelyn Bywater](wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater) without a fight.(wiki:Rosby.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Prior to the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Jacelyn brings a fresh levy of spearmen from the Rosby estates to reinforce the defense of King's Landing.(wiki:Rosby.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Cersei sends the [Kettleblack](wiki:House_Kettleblack) brothers to Rosby to recover Tommen and free Boros.(wiki:Rosby.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

[Alan of Rosby](wiki:Alan_of_Rosby) participates in the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep).(wiki:Rosby.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Feast for Crows

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) visits Rosby in her search for [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark).(wiki:Rosby.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

[The ward](wiki:Ward_of_Lord_Gyles_Rosby) of Lord [Gyles Rosby](wiki:Gyles_Rosby) refuses to let [Falyse Stokeworth](wiki:Falyse_Stokeworth) and her husband, Ser [Balman Byrch](wiki:Balman_Byrch), stay the night when they travel to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Rosby.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

Rosby's inheritance is unresolved after Gyles dies in King's Landing as he has no trueborn sons or daughters, although he does leave the unidentified ward. Cersei considers granting the castle to [Aurane Waters](wiki:Aurane_Waters).(wiki:Rosby.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

The Rosby inheritance is brought up in a [small council](wiki:Small_council) meeting. According to Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle), six claims have been put forward, but the council decides to settle the issue at a later date as they have more pressing matters to deal with.(wiki:Rosby.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
