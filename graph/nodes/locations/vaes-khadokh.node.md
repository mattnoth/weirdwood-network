## Origins

Essaria was a colony of the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold), but it was destroyed by the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) during the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood) following the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria).(wiki:Vaes_Khadokh.cite_ref-Rawoiafvaes_khadokh_.28essaria.29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Essaria is remembered as the "Lost Free City". Like the other [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities), it had a contract from the Freehold for limited self-governance in local affairs. In its time it prospered from trade with [Sarnor](wiki:Sarnor) to the east.(wiki:Vaes_Khadokh.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
