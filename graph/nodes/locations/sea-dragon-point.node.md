## Origins

The [First Men](wiki:First_Men) of old built strongholds in Sea Dragon Point, which was more heavily populated in antiquity.(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Point.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
At some point after the [Long Night](wiki:Long_Night), the [Kings of Winter](wiki:Kings_of_Winter), [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), conquered Sea Dragon Point from the [Warg King](wiki:Warg_King) and his allies, the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest).(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Point.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
The [ironmen](wiki:Ironmen) of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) have held the peninsula at times,(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Point.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_mountain_clans.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) but all of their possessions on the [mainland](wiki:Green_lands) were eventually lost by the [High Kings of the Iron Islands](wiki:High_Kings_of_the_Iron_Islands)(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Point.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and [Kings of the Iron Islands](wiki:Kings_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Point.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_iron_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), tasks his daughter [Asha](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) with sailing past Sea Dragon Point and [attacking Deepwood Motte](wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Point.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Balon declares himself [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings),(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Point.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and he then styles himself [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North).(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Point.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After speaking with Lady [Sybelle Glover](wiki:Sybelle_Glover), the captured wife of [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover), Asha believes that [northmen](wiki:Northmen) would cede Sea Dragon Point and the [Stony Shore](wiki:Stony_Shore) if the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) return [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte), [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Torrhen's_Square), and [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin).(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Point.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
In her candidacy to succeed her late father during the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), Asha tries to convince the ironborn to make peace with the [north](wiki:North). However, the assembled ironmen instead choose [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy)'s plan to expand their war.(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Point.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Asha flees Old Wyk for [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers) and then Deepwood. She hopes she can hold Sea Dragon Point and develop it into a kingdom of her own, thinking the pine trees would be useful for shipbuilding, but [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley) is skeptical of her plans.(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Point.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) Asha and Tris are captured by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) in the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Sea_Dragon_Point.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
