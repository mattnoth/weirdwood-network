## Identity

The Broken Tower, also known as the Burned Tower, is one of the structures within Winterfell, the seat of House Stark. It was once the tallest watchtower in Winterfell. Over 140 years before the events of the main narrative, a lightning strike set the tower afire and the top third collapsed inward; no one subsequently rebuilt it. The tower stands behind the old inner ward, and crows nest atop its ruins. The archery butts of the old inner ward are located near the broken tower.

The Broken Tower is narratively significant as the destination Bran Stark was climbing toward when he came upon Ser Jaime Lannister and Queen Cersei Lannister in an incestuous embrace in the First Keep, after which Jaime pushed Bran from the window. During the events at Winterfell in A Dance with Dragons, the Burned Tower is also mentioned as a landmark near which Theon Greyjoy spots one of Mance Rayder's spearwives (likely Frenya) with Rickard Ryswell.
