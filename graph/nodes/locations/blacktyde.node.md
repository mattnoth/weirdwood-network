## Origins

After the death of [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), the lords of Blacktyde and [Harlaw](wiki:Harlaw) supported [Rodrik Greyjoy](wiki:Rodrik_Greyjoy_(son_of_Dalton)) against [Toron Greyjoy](wiki:Toron_Greyjoy) in a [struggle for power in the Iron Islands](wiki:Struggle_for_power_in_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Blacktyde.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
After [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley) was found with [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) in [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), he was sent away to Blacktyde to be a ward of [Baelor Blacktyde](wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde), [Lord of Blacktyde](wiki:Lord_of_Blacktyde).(wiki:Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Members of [House Blacktyde](wiki:House_Blacktyde) travel from Blacktyde to [Pyke](wiki:Pyke) when [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), summons his bannermen.(wiki:Blacktyde.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Baelor Blacktyde](wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde), [Lord of Blacktyde](wiki:Lord_of_Blacktyde), travels from his isle to visit [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw), [Lord of Harlaw](wiki:Lord_of_Harlaw), at the [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers).(wiki:Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) has Baelor executed after the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
