## Identity

The Demon Road is an overland route in Essos that runs toward Meereen. It is considered extremely dangerous — when Quentyn Martell's party was stranded in Volantis and unable to hire a ship to carry them to Meereen, Archibald Yronwood proposed taking the Demon Road as an alternative, but the suggestion was dismissed in favor of joining the Windblown sellsword company. Harry Strickland of the Golden Company also rejected the route outright, calling the demon road "death" when it was floated as a means for the Golden Company to march overland to meet Daenerys Targaryen in Meereen. Cletus Yronwood, killed before reaching Meereen, had called the journey "demon roads and stormy seas" — framing it as a grand adventure, not knowing his own death lay ahead. The road's dangers are treated as common knowledge among Westerosi travelers and mercenaries operating in Essos.
