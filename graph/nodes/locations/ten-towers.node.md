## Origins

The newest castle in the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands), Ten Towers was built by Lord [Theomore Harlaw](wiki:Theomore_Harlaw), who blamed the deaths of three infant sons on the dampness of ancient [Harlaw Hall](wiki:Harlaw_Hall), which often flooded. The new castle was built at a better site, but the mercurial Theomore constructed ten towers different in appearance.(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-10)

While growing up [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) felt more at home at Ten Towers than at the [Greyjoy](wiki:House_Greyjoy) seat of [Pyke](wiki:Pyke). She enjoyed racing amongst Ten Towers' walks and bridges and reading in Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw)'s library.(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-11) When the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) was tolerated in the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands), Rodrik kept [septons](wiki:Septon) at the castle to maintain his library.(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-12)

Rodrik's older sister, Lady [Gwynesse Harlaw](wiki:Gwynesse_Harlaw), came back to Ten Towers after the death of her husband in the [sea battle off Fair Isle](wiki:Sea_battle_off_Fair_Isle) during [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion) in [289 AC](wiki:289_AC), saying she would remain until her grief had passed. Her residence is now known as the Widow's Tower, and the still-grieving Gwynesse believes that the castle should belong to her instead of the younger Rodrik.(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-13)

[Alannys Harlaw](wiki:Alannys_Harlaw), the wife of [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), moved to Ten Towers to live with her sister Gwynesse,(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-14) as it is less raw on [Harlaw](wiki:Harlaw) than on the [isle of Pyke](wiki:Pyke_(island)).(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Appearances & Description

The castle consists of ten towers all shaped differently, with wallwalks, covered bridges, kitchens, and dungeons.(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

The hall of Ten Towers contains the high seat of [House Harlaw](wiki:House_Harlaw), with two giant scythes of beaten [silver](wiki:Silver) hanging crossed above the seat.(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

The [Book Tower](wiki:Book_Tower), the fattest of the castle's towers, is octagonal and made of large block stones.(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw) does not allow food or drink in his library.(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) The yard below contains a well.(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

The [Widow's Tower](wiki:Widow's_Tower_(Ten_Towers)) is so called due to the presence of Rodrik's sister, Lady [Gwynesse Harlaw](wiki:Gwynesse_Harlaw).(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)

A village lies near Ten Towers.(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8) There is good fishing off the [Long Stone Quay](wiki:Long_Stone_Quay).(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-9)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Rival claimants arise in the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) after the death of [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North). Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw) announces that Balon intended to be succeeded by [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), Balon's daughter and Rodrik's niece, and Lord Harlaw calls her supporters to Ten Towers.(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) calls for a [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot) to be held to choose Balon's successor.(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Lord [Baelor Blacktyde](wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde) briefly visits Rodrik at Ten Towers before continuing on to [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk).(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-15) Potential supporters of [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) who assemble at Ten Towers include [Botleys](wiki:House_Botley), [Myres](wiki:House_Myre), [Stonetrees](wiki:House_Stonetree), and [Volmarks](wiki:House_Volmark). Harlaw cousins include [Boremund](wiki:Boremund_Harlaw), [Hotho Humpback](wiki:Hotho_Harlaw), Ser [Harras](wiki:Harras_Harlaw), and [Sigfryd Silverhair](wiki:Sigfryd_Harlaw).(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-16)

Upon arriving at Ten Towers, Asha orders the steward [Three-Tooth](wiki:Three-Tooth) to have the captive Lady [Sybelle Glover](wiki:Sybelle_Glover) and her children, [Gawen](wiki:Gawen_Glover) and [Erena](wiki:Erena_Glover), comfortably quartered in one of the towers instead of the dungeons, and to have warm food served to the crew of *[Black Wind](wiki:Black_Wind)*. Asha delays seeing her mother Alannys in the [Widow's Tower](wiki:Widow's_Tower_(Ten_Towers)) until the next morning, and instead meets her uncle, Lord Rodrik, within the [Book Tower](wiki:Book_Tower). Worried that Asha will lose to her uncles [Euron](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) or [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) at the kingsmoot, Rodrik offers to name her heir to Ten Towers if she remains on [Harlaw](wiki:Harlaw), though Harras would remain heir to [House Harlaw](wiki:House_Harlaw) itself. Asha refuses and wants the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair) instead.(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-17)

Euron ends up chosen by the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) who assemble at [Nagga's hill](wiki:Nagga's_hill) for the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

When Euron Crow's Eye is chosen, Asha flees from Old Wyk back to Ten Towers on Harlaw to bid farewell to her mother. While taking on provisions, she learns that Euron has had her wed to [Erik Ironmaker](wiki:Erik_Ironmaker) using a [seal](wiki:Seal) as her proxy.(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Asha leaves Gawen and Erena Glover at Ten Towers in the care of Alannys and her aunt, [Gwynesse Harlaw](wiki:Gwynesse_Harlaw), but continues on to [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) with the captive Lady Glover.(wiki:Ten_Towers.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
