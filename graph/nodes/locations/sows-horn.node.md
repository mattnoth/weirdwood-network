## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) leads his force past Sow's Horn. He meets with Ser [Roger Hogg](wiki:Roger_Hogg), the head of the household, who tells him that during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) he was attacked by lions ([Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) men under the command of Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch)) who attempted to [burn Sow's Horn](wiki:Burning_of_Sow's_Horn). There was further [fighting at Sow's Horn](wiki:Fighting_at_Sow's_Horn) when the Hoggs were attacked by white star wolves ([Karstark](wiki:House_Karstark) men in search of Jaime). Roger shelters six men-at-arms, four crossbowmen, and a score of peasants in his towerhouse.(wiki:Sow's_Horn.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
