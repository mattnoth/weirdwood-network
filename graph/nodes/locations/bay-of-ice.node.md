## Culture

[Clansmen](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans) from [House Wull](wiki:House_Wull) live west of the [northern mountains](wiki:Northern_mountains) along the Bay of Ice(wiki:Bay_of_Ice.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and fish in its waters.(wiki:Bay_of_Ice.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Fishing sloops from [Bear Island](wiki:Bear_Island) also sail in the bay.(wiki:Bay_of_Ice.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Origins

The [Wulls](wiki:House_Wull) hold animosity toward [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) who have crossed the [Wall](wiki:Wall) to raid their lands, as well as toward sailors from the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) who have attacked the shores of the Bay of Ice.(wiki:Bay_of_Ice.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf712_8-0) The [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) have also historically fought [northmen](wiki:North) for control of [Bear Island](wiki:Bear_Island) within the bay.(wiki:Bay_of_Ice.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf711_9-0)(wiki:Bay_of_Ice.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf75.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Bay_of_Ice.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

When the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) enter the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) leads thirty longships(wiki:Bay_of_Ice.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) between [Bear Island](wiki:Bear_Island) and the mainland(wiki:Bay_of_Ice.cite_ref-Rtloiafjourneys.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) for her [siege of Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fall_of_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Bay_of_Ice.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Asha returns to the Iron Islands to attend the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Bay_of_Ice.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Fleeing [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk) after the election of her uncle [Euron Crow's Eye](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), Asha returns to Deepwood with only four longships, including her own [Black Wind](wiki:Black_Wind), the [Salty Wench](wiki:Salty_Wench) of [Quenton Greyjoy](wiki:Quenton_Greyjoy_(cousin)), and the ship of [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley). Asha keeps two in the bay and beaches two on the strand by the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood).(wiki:Bay_of_Ice.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
Leading fighters hidden in fishing sloops, [Alysane Mormont](wiki:Alysane_Mormont) burns or captures Asha's ships,(wiki:Bay_of_Ice.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) and [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) captures Asha in the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Bay_of_Ice.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)
