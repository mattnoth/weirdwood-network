## Appearances & Description

The Tower of the Hand has crenellated battlements.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) The tower's private audience chamber is not as large as the king's, but it has [Myrish](wiki:Myr) rugs, wall hangings, and a golden-tinted round window that give it a sense of intimacy.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
The bedchamber of the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) has a canopied bed and sconces on the walls and rushes on the floor.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Racok54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Inside its hearth is a small door opening on a secret passageway known to Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys), the [master of whisperers](wiki:Master_of_whisperers). From this door one can climb down two hundred and thirty rungs which lead to the [chamber of the dragon mosaic](wiki:Chamber_of_the_dragon_mosaic), where half a dozen tunnels meet.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Rasos77.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Down a hallway from the bedchamber is the privy tower.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Rasos77.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
The tower's [Small Hall](wiki:Small_Hall), named in contrast to the [Great Hall](wiki:Great_Hall_(Red_Keep)) of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), is a long room with a high-vaulted ceiling and bench space for two hundred. It has trestled tables(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and great wooden doors.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
The Tower of the Hand also has a [solar](wiki:Solar) and a garderobe. The tower has tall windows(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) that overlook the Great Yard.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Origins

Construction of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) began in [35 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) during the reign of King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), and the castle was completed in [45 AC](wiki:45_AC) during the era of [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen). While inspecting the Tower of the Hand in [44 AC](wiki:44_AC), Lord [Lucas Harroway](wiki:Lucas_Harroway) was thrown to his death from its top by Maegor's [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Princess [Viserra Targaryen](wiki:Viserra_Targaryen) enjoyed persuading admiring squires to climb the tower's walls, among other tasks.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
The Hand of King [Jaehaerys I](wiki:Jaehaerys_I), Prince [Baelon Targaryen](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)), died of a burst belly in the Tower of the Hand in [101 AC](wiki:101_AC).(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-12)
Queen [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower) took up residence in the Tower of the Hand during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), as her father, Ser [Otto](wiki:Otto_Hightower), was Hand of the King to [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen). [Blood and Cheese](wiki:Blood_and_Cheese) infiltrated the tower and assassinated Prince [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_II)).(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) The Red Keep was occupied by Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), Aegon's rival, after the [fall of King's Landing](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing). Rhaenyra ordered the arrest of Ser [Addam Velaryon](wiki:Addam_Velaryon) in reaction to the [Two Betrayers](wiki:Two_Betrayers) and [First Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton), but Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) forewarned his kin. Corlys, Rhaenyra's Hand, was then arrested by Ser [Luthor Largent](wiki:Luthor_Largent) in the tower.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Ser [Tyland Lannister](wiki:Tyland_Lannister) resided in the Tower of the Hand when the [regency of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III) began.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) [Lotho](wiki:Lotho_Rogare) and [Roggerio Rogare](wiki:Roggerio_Rogare) were imprisoned in the tower after the [Lysene Spring](wiki:Lysene_Spring).(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) is appointed [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) by King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) and moves into the Tower of the Hand.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Ned eats with [his guards](wiki:House_Stark_guards) in the [Small Hall](wiki:Small_Hall), which is also where his daughter [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark) trains with [Syrio Forel](wiki:Syrio_Forel).(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) During the [coup against Lord Stark](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark), Syrio protects Arya from Ser [Meryn Trant](wiki:Meryn_Trant) and [House Lannister guards](wiki:House_Lannister_guards) in the Small Hall.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) names his son [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) the acting Hand in his absence. Tyrion takes up residence in the Tower of the Hand.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Tyrion has Lord [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) arrested in the Small Hall.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) sneaks [Shae](wiki:Shae) into Tyrion's bedchamber through the secret passageway.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Racok54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Wounded by Ser [Mandon Moore](wiki:Mandon_Moore) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Tyrion is moved from the Tower of the Hand into [Maegor's Holdfast](wiki:Maegor's_Holdfast).(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Racok67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Tywin takes over as Hand and moves into the Tower of the Hand.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) After Tyrion weds [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), [their wedding feast](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark) is held in the Small Hall.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
Before fleeing the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), Tyrion visits his father in the tower, gaining access through the secret passageway. There, in the Hand's bedchamber, he discovers his lover Shae in Tywin's bed. Tyrion strangles Shae, arms himself with a crossbow hanging on the wall, and then confronts his father in the privy. Tyrion slays Tywin in fury and escapes the same way he came in.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Rasos77.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord Tywin is discovered murdered in the Tower of the Hand. As Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) is about to view her father's body, which has been moved to his bed, she notices the secret door that she was told about when the news of Tywin's death was delivered. The door gapes open behind the ashes of the hearth, no bigger than an oven. She thinks to herself that a man would need to crawl through it.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Raffc3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and his men reduce the Tower of the Hand to a shell in their unsuccessful search for Tyrion.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Cersei decides to burn the building.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
After [the wedding feast](wiki:Wedding_of_Tommen_I_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) is held in the Small Hall, Cersei leads the guests outside to watch as the [pyromancers](wiki:Pyromancer) set the Tower of the Hand on fire. Fifty pots of [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) placed inside the tower along with logs and casks of pitch are set alight. The Tower of the Hand is ruined. The greater part of Tyrion's possessions, which were still in the tower, are also destroyed.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
During the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Lady [Genna Lannister](wiki:Genna_Lannister) asks Jaime if it is true that Cersei burned the Red Keep but Jaime tells her that it was only the Tower of the Hand. Genna replies that Cersei would have done better to leave the tower and instead burn her Hand, Ser [Harys Swyft](wiki:Harys_Swyft).(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Cersei tells Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) that Tommen told her that Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) intends on rebuilding the Tower of the Hand. Kevan concurs telling her that the new tower is to be twice as tall as the one she burned.(wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)
