## Origins

During the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), after burning the [Planky Town](wiki:Planky_Town), Queen [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) descended upon Spottswood with her [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Meraxes](wiki:Meraxes), but as with all other Dornish seats of power great and small, the Targaryens found only old women and children.
