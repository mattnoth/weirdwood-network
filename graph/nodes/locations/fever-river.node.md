## Origins

There were reports that [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) and her dragon, [Dreamfyre](wiki:Dreamfyre), were seen as far north as the [barrowlands](wiki:Barrowlands) and the banks of the Fever River during her search for the missing [Aerea Targaryen](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen).(wiki:Fever_River.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), plans an invasion of the [north](wiki:North) and declares himself [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Fever_River.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) His younger brother, Lord Captain [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy), sails the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) up the [Saltspear](wiki:Saltspear) and the Fever River to [take Moat Cailin](wiki:Fall_of_Moat_Cailin), which lies nearby.(wiki:Fever_River.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Fever_River.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Balon styles himself [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Fever_River.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

The Iron Fleet lays among the reeds and willows of the Fever River. After King Balon's death at [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), Victarion departs with nine-tenths of the Iron Fleet for the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Fever_River.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The [Ryswells](wiki:House_Ryswell) and [Dustins](wiki:House_Dustin) surprise the remaining [ironmen](wiki:Ironmen) on the Fever River and burn their ten longships.(wiki:Fever_River.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
