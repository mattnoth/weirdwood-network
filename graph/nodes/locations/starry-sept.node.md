## Appearances & Description

Just like the newer [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), the Starry Sept is shaped as a dome.(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rtrotd62.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The building has black [marble](wiki:Marble) walls and arched windows.(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) A [motherhouse](wiki:Motherhouse) is attached to the sept.(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
The Starry Sept was once the residence of the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon), who had bedchambers in the structure.(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Crypts below the sept contain the bodies of deceased High Septons, and members of the [Most Devout](wiki:Most_Devout) would gather in the sept to choose his successor.(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Before being anointed and consecrated, new High Septons stood vigil in the Starry Sept.(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)
A wide marble [plaza](wiki:Plaza) is located outside of the Starry Sept,(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) and [manses](wiki:Manse) of the pious are clustered near the building.(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
According to Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel), the Starry Sept of Oldtown is rivaled only by the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) and the castle sept of [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden).(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_highgarden.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Origins

### Origin[ ]

The Starry Sept was built by Lord [Triston Hightower](wiki:Triston_Hightower), in honor of his mentor and earlier regent, Septon [Robeson](wiki:Robeson).(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) It was the seat of the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon) for "a thousand years prior" to the [Wars of Conquest](wiki:Wars_of_Conquest).(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Pate_9-0)

### Aegon's Conquest[ ]

When [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) landed at the mouth of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush), the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aegon's_Conquest)) in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) locked himself inside the Starry Sept and prayed for seven whole days while refusing to eat or drink. At the end of the seventh day he emerged from his fast and declared that the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) would not fight [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), proclaiming that if they did Oldtown would burn and the Starry Sept would be destroyed.(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Lord [Manfred Hightower](wiki:Manfred_Hightower_(Aegon's_Conquest)) heeded the prophecy and welcomed the Conqueror when he appeared before Oldtown's gates later in the war. Three days later, Aegon was anointed in the Faith when he was proclaimed [Lord of the Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Lord_of_the_Seven_Kingdoms); Aegon's reign is dated from his anointment and coronation in the Starry Sept.(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) The new king visited the High Septon within the Starry Sept during each of his progresses throughout the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### Faith Militant Uprising[ ]

Prince [Maegor Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) wed [Ceryse Hightower](wiki:Ceryse_Hightower) in the Starry Sept in [25 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) After Aegon's death in [37 AC](wiki:37_AC), King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen) was anointed in the sept.(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) The [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aenys_I)) denounced Prince Maegor after he wed [Alys Harroway](wiki:Alys_Harroway) in [39 AC](wiki:39_AC).(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2)
In [41 AC](wiki:41_AC) during the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising), Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) unsuccessfully advised her nephew, King Aenys, to destroy the Starry Sept and the [Sept of Remembrance](wiki:Sept_of_Remembrance).(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aenys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-3) When Maegor I threatened to destroy the building after becoming king, Ser [Morgan Hightower](wiki:Morgan_Hightower) led two hundred men from Oldtown's chapter of the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons) in protecting the sept. The High Septon opposing Maegor abruptly died, however, and Oldtown's gates were opened to the Targaryen forces. The [Most Devout](wiki:Most_Devout) then chose [Pater](wiki:Pater) as his replacement.(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-4)
In [48 AC](wiki:48_AC) Septon [Moon](wiki:Moon_(septon)) led thousands of followers to Oldtown to force Pater's successor, the so-called [High Lickspittle](wiki:High_Lickspittle), to denounce Maegor,(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-5) and this new High Septon eventually confined himself within the Starry Sept for protection.(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) After Moon's death and the defeat of his followers, the rebel septon's head was presented to the High Septon as a gift.(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1)

### Jaehaerys I[ ]

The High Lickspittle crowned King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) in the Starry Sept in [48 AC](wiki:48_AC).(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-2)
Septa [Rhaella](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon)), the king's niece, trained as a novice at the Starry Sept.(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-3) In [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) sent his brother, Ser [Orryn](wiki:Orryn_Baratheon), to take the girl into custody. Septa [Karolyn](wiki:Karolyn) prevented Orryn's men from entering the [motherhouse](wiki:Motherhouse), however, and [Casper Straw](wiki:Casper_Straw), the High Septon's steward, gathered thirty armed septons to help protect the girl.(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
The author of [A Caution for Young Girls](wiki:A_Caution_for_Young_Girls) claims to have become a [septa](wiki:Septa) at the Starry Sept at the end of her life.(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
In [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), the High Lickspittle died while climbing the stairs to his chambers, and Jaehaerys gave a eulogy in the sept's plaza.(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4)

### Regency of Aegon III[ ]

When Lord [Lyonel Hightower](wiki:Lyonel_Hightower) took as his paramour Lady [Samantha Tarly](wiki:Samantha_Tarly), who had briefly been married to his father [Ormund](wiki:Ormund_Hightower), the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Regency_of_Aegon_III)) accused her of [incest](wiki:Incest) and forbade her from setting foot in the Starry Sept until she repented. Sam responded by riding on horseback into the sept during the High Septon's prayers and having her knights bar anyone else from entering. The outraged High Septon eventually backed down.(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) visited Oldtown during his voyage to confront Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC). The High Septon blessed him at the Starry Sept and called for the [Warrior](wiki:Warrior_(the_Seven)) to lead him against Dalton's [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), followers of the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God).(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
As part of his punishment for [plotting against Aegon III](wiki:Secret_siege), Septon [Bernard](wiki:Bernard) was forced by the High Septon to take a vow of silence and spend the remainder of his days copying texts in the Starry Sept.(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### Later History[ ]

The Starry Sept was eventually replaced as the seat of the High Septon by the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Starry_Sept.cite_ref-Spring_20-0)
