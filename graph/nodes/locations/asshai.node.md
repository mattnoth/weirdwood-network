## Origins

The origins of Asshai are lost in history. Even the Asshai'i do not claim to know who built it, stating only a city stood there since the world began and will stand there until it ends.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf844_5-9) [Maesters](wiki:Maesters) find it difficult to discern the truth about legendary Asshai.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf25.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

Texts from Asshai speak of the [Dawn Age](wiki:Dawn_Age).(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf21.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Annals from the city tell of [Azor Ahai](wiki:Azor_Ahai) during the [Long Night](wiki:Long_Night),(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf24.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) and ancient books claim he will come again.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2) Texts also claim that [dragons](wiki:Dragon) first appeared in the Shadow.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf25.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)

Asshai has been a thriving port for centuries.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf844_5-10) Before the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood), merchants from the [Kingdom of Sarnor](wiki:Kingdom_of_Sarnor) traded at Asshai.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf835_18-0)

[Jar Han](wiki:Jar_Han), one of the sea-green [god-emperors](wiki:God-emperor) of [Yi Ti](wiki:Yi_Ti), extracted tribute from Asshai during his reign.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf841_19-0)

On the second of his nine [great voyages](wiki:Great_voyages) on *[Sea Snake](wiki:Sea_Snake)*, [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) sailed to Asshai, becoming the first [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) to visit the city. Tales claim that Corlys lost his love and half his crew there, and he claimed to have seen the *[Sun Chaser](wiki:Sun_Chaser)* of [Elissa Farman](wiki:Elissa_Farman) in the harbor of Asshai.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) commissioned journeys in search of [dragonlore](wiki:Dragonlore) from distant places, including Asshai.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf415.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

[Mirri Maz Duur](wiki:Mirri_Maz_Duur) studied in Asshai, learning from a [moonsinger](wiki:Moonsinger) of the [Jogos Nhai](wiki:Jogos_Nhai), a [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) woman, Maester [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn),(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) and a [bloodmage](wiki:Bloodmage) [from](wiki:Shadow_Men) the [Shadow Lands](wiki:Shadow_Lands).(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

When a wrecked trading [cog](wiki:Ship) washed up on the [Frozen Shore](wiki:Frozen_Shore), a woman of the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) found scarlet silk from Asshai in the wreckage. [Her granddaughter](wiki:Wisewoman's_daughter) later used the silk to mend the cloak of [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder).(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

## Appearances & Description

### Layout

Asshai sprawls for leagues across both sides of the [Ash](wiki:Ash_(river)), and its great walls allegedly could contain [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), and [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) combined. The population of Asshai, however, is no more than that of a good-sized market town. By night only one building in ten shows a light.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf844_5-1)

The buildings, streets, and walls of Asshai are made out of [black stone](wiki:Black_stone) that is greasy to the touch and seems to drink the light, making the city appear to be a dark and gloomy place. Structures include bazaars, halls, hovels, palaces, and temples.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf844_5-2)

### Trade

Asshai is a popular trading destination for ships.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf844_5-3) According to a semi-canon source, it is not as important to traders as [Yi Ti](wiki:Yi_Ti) and [Leng](wiki:Leng), which are more easily reached from [Qarth](wiki:Qarth).(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-7) Asshai exports goods such as [amber](wiki:Amber) and [dragonglass](wiki:Dragonglass).(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) [Gold](wiki:Gold) and gems are also common exports, though some say the gold of the [Shadow Lands](wiki:Shadow_Lands) is as unhealthy as the fruits that grow there.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf844_5-4) The city is usually traveled to by ship, but it can also be reached overland by caravans.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Trading ships from [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) and the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) bound for Asshai go east through the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea) and the [Jade Sea](wiki:Jade_Sea), which are connected by the straits at Qarth, the [Jade Gates](wiki:Jade_Gates).(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rssm1122.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Caravans from Asshai traverse the [Bone Mountains](wiki:Bone_Mountains) to reach [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak).(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Aside from traders, few from Westeros visit Asshai and little is known about it. The famous scribe and traveler [Lomas Longstrider](wiki:Lomas_Longstrider) never visited Asshai,(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf844_5-5) though he apparently heard from merchants in Yi Ti who did trade with Asshai that the vast gold of [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) was famous even there.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf76.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

All foodstuff and freshwater is brought in by ship, as animals brought into the city soon die, and the waters of the [Ash](wiki:Ash_(river)) glisten black beneath the noonday sun and glimmer with green phosphorescence by night. The only fish that dwell in its water are blind and deformed, and only fools and [shadowbinders](wiki:Shadowbinder) dare eat their flesh.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf844_5-6)

### People

Asshai and its few people have an ominous reputation in other lands. Asshai'i are described as having a dark and solemn appearance.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) The [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) believe that Asshai'i are "spawn of shadows" and hold them in contempt and fear.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

There are no children in Asshai. All who dwell there wear masks or veils, and often walk alone or ride in [palanquins](wiki:Palanquin) of [ebony](wiki:Ebony) and [iron](wiki:Iron), hidden behind dark curtains and carried upon the backs of slaves. Only the [shadowbinders](wiki:Shadowbinder) of Asshai dare to travel up the [Ash](wiki:Ash_(river)), and even they fear to tread before the doors of the [Stygai](wiki:Stygai), the corpse city northeast of Asshai.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf844_5-7)

[Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) prays and sings in the [tongue of Asshai](wiki:Tongue_of_Asshai).(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### Esoteric Knowledge

Asshai holds a significant amount of arcane knowledge. Of any current location, it is the most likely to have [dragonlore](wiki:Dragonlore).(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rssm1085.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Ancient books of Asshai also record the [Azor Ahai](wiki:Azor_Ahai) prophecy followed by members of the [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor) faith.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

It is rumored that [spellsingers](wiki:Spellsinger), [aeromancers](wiki:Aeromancer), and [warlocks](wiki:Warlocks) practice their arts openly in Asshai.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Asshai is open to all sorts of practitioners of the "higher mysteries" such as [godswives](wiki:Godswife), [alchemists](wiki:Alchemy), [necromancers](wiki:Necromancer), night-walkers, [pyromancers](wiki:Pyromancer), [bloodmages](wiki:Bloodmage), as well as inquisitors, [torturers](wiki:Torture) and [poisoners](wiki:Poison), and even worshippers of the [Black Goat](wiki:Black_Goat_of_Qohor) and [Bakkalon](wiki:Bakkalon) and the [Lion of Night](wiki:Lion_of_Night). No practice is forbidden in the city, however depraved.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf844_5-8)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) is gifted three [dragon eggs](wiki:Dragon_egg) by [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis), reportedly from the [Shadow Lands](wiki:Shadow_Lands) beyond Asshai.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) In the Eastern Market of [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak), Daenerys spies dark and solemn Asshai'i people, as well as [Shadow Men](wiki:Shadow_Men).(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) [Mirri Maz Duur](wiki:Mirri_Maz_Duur) tells Daenerys she once studied in Asshai, and there met a [maester](wiki:Maester), [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn).(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1)

Once Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) realizes that Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) is going to die he suggests to Daenerys that they leave the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea) for Asshai, where they can find a ship to take them back to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos).(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-1) Before Drogo's body is burnt on his pyre Jorah begs Daenerys to come east with him. When Daenerys commands her handmaids to bring her dragon eggs to place on Drogo's funeral pyre, Jorah tells her that it would be better to sell them in Asshai. However, in the pyre, the eggs hatch into [dragons](wiki:Dragon), and Daenerys then walks into the pyre, but both remain unscathed.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) has a vision of many lands, including Asshai, where he sees dragons stirring.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Ragot17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

During Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s council meeting, talk eventually turns to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon). Among other reports he has received from [Varys](wiki:Varys) of Stannis's activities on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)), Tywin mentions that Stannis is bringing [a shadowbinder](wiki:Melisandre) from Asshai.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

### A Clash of Kings

[Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) of Asshai is on Dragonstone to serve Stannis. She has converted Stannis's wife, [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent), to the faith of R'hllor.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Racok0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)

[Quaithe](wiki:Quaithe) of the Shadow is one of the three representatives from [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) who return to [Vaes Tolorro](wiki:Vaes_Tolorro) with [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) in search of [dragons](wiki:Dragon). Later in Qarth, trader captains bring Daenerys amber and [dragonglass](wiki:Dragonglass) from Asshai. Quaithe urges Daenerys to leave the city as soon as possible and to "pass beneath the shadow".(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

[Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard) gifts [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) with a hairnet of black [amethysts](wiki:Amethyst) from Asshai.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

*[Silence](wiki:Silence)*, the ship of [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), is said to be infamous from Asshai to [Ibben](wiki:Ibben).(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

### A Storm of Swords

[Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) tells [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) of his black cloak that had been repaired with scarlet silk from Asshai.(wiki:Asshai.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1)
