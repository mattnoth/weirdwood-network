## Origins

According to some legends, [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand) is said to have led the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) from [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) through the Red Mountains to the [Reach](wiki:Reach).(wiki:Red_Mountains.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2) The rugged terrain kept many mountain communities isolated.(wiki:Red_Mountains.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_kingdoms_of_the_first_men.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-2) The most powerful dynasties which developed were the [Kings of the Torrentine](wiki:Kings_of_the_Torrentine) from [House Dayne](wiki:House_Dayne), the [Kings of Stone and Sky](wiki:Kings_of_Stone_and_Sky) from [House Fowler](wiki:House_Fowler), and the [High Kings of Dorne](wiki:High_Kings_of_Dorne) from [House Yronwood](wiki:House_Yronwood).(wiki:Red_Mountains.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_kingdoms_of_the_first_men.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-3)
[Garth XI Gardener](wiki:Garth_XI_Gardener), [King of the Reach](wiki:King_of_the_Reach), avenged [Garth X](wiki:Garth_X_Gardener) by attacking Dorne; Lord [Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) claimed that Garth XI painted the mountains from green to red with Dornish blood.(wiki:Red_Mountains.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
King [Albin Manwoody](wiki:Albin_Manwoody) was a madman who claimed dominion over the Red Mountains, but he was defeated and sent to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) during [Nymeria's War](wiki:Nymeria's_War).(wiki:Red_Mountains.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_the_coming_of_the_rhoynar.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
Dornishmen raided the [Dornish Marches](wiki:Dornish_Marches) from the Red Mountains during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Red_Mountains.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) The Dornish abandoned their watchtowers in the mountains during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), and the [Wyls](wiki:House_Wyl) ambushed the host of Lord [Orys Baratheon](wiki:Orys_Baratheon) in the [Boneway](wiki:Boneway).(wiki:Red_Mountains.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1)
The [first Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Aenys_I)) rose in the Red Mountains during the reign of King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen), beginning the [Second Dornish War](wiki:Second_Dornish_War).(wiki:Red_Mountains.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) A [second Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Jaehaerys_I)) fought against King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), but he was defeated in the [Third Dornish War](wiki:Third_Dornish_War).(wiki:Red_Mountains.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) [Morion Martell](wiki:Morion_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), desired to ravage the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands), but the [Fourth Dornish War](wiki:Fourth_Dornish_War) was swiftly crushed by Jaehaerys.(wiki:Red_Mountains.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-1)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon) entered the Red Mountains with six thousand men in response to Dornish incursions.(wiki:Red_Mountains.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)
During the [conquest of Dorne](wiki:Conquest_of_Dorne), Lord [Lyonel Tyrell](wiki:Lyonel_Tyrell_(lord)) led a host through the [Prince's Pass](wiki:Prince's_Pass) and King [Daeron I Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen) traveled the Boneway.(wiki:Red_Mountains.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
Lords [Caron](wiki:House_Caron) and [Dondarrion](wiki:House_Dondarrion) burned a [Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Daeron_II)) out of the Red Mountains.(wiki:Red_Mountains.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater) once led [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) and other friends to find the lair of one of the [Vulture Kings](wiki:Vulture_King).(wiki:Red_Mountains.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) requests that [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), send his armies to the [Boneway](wiki:Boneway) and the [Prince's Pass](wiki:Prince's_Pass) to keep the [marcher lords](wiki:Marcher_lords) in their castles as part of the political betrothal of [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) and [Trystane Martell](wiki:Trystane_Martell).(wiki:Red_Mountains.cite_ref-Rawoiafdornish_marches.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) Doran promises to move his banners into the high passes once Myrcella arrives safely at [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear).(wiki:Red_Mountains.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

Two [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) hosts have massed in the two passes through the Red Mountains. The hosts sit encamped, waiting for Doran to loose them on the enemies of [House Martell](wiki:House_Martell).(wiki:Red_Mountains.cite_ref-28)
