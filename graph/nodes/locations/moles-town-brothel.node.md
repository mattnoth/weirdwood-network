## Appearances & Description

Like most of [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town), the brothel is located in the village's cellars. Above ground it has only a wooden shack no bigger than a privy, with a red lantern hanging over its door.(wiki:Mole's_Town_brothel.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) A stable adjoins the shed.(wiki:Mole's_Town_brothel.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
The brothel is frequented by members of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). It is said that brothers go to Mole's Town to dig for "buried treasure", a euphemism for engaging the services of the [prostitutes](wiki:Prostitutes) in the cellars. Although the brothers are [breaking their vows](wiki:Night's_Watch), [Lord Commanders](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) know some men find it impossible to be celibate and after they are done with the whores the men return to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) and their duties.(wiki:Mole's_Town_brothel.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

On the [Wall](wiki:Wall), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) hears men refer to the [whores](wiki:Whore) in [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town) as "buried treasures".(wiki:Mole's_Town_brothel.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
While trying to desert the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) and join his brother [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) in secret, Jon sees the light of the Mole's Town brothel and wonders if any black brothers are there breaking their vows that night.(wiki:Mole's_Town_brothel.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Prior to the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), the grounds of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) seem deserted with many [rangers](wiki:Rangers) off drinking and whoring in Mole's Town.(wiki:Mole's_Town_brothel.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [Pyp](wiki:Pyp), [Halder](wiki:Halder) and [Todder](wiki:Todder) buy [Grenn](wiki:Grenn) his first woman to celebrate his first ranging. They invite [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) and Jon as well, but Sam is frightened of the whores and Jon remains loyal to his vow of celibacy.(wiki:Mole's_Town_brothel.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Having escaped [Styr](wiki:Styr)'s raiding party, a wounded Jon receives a fresh mount at the stable by the brothel before continuing on to Castle Black.(wiki:Mole's_Town_brothel.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
[Zei](wiki:Zei), a whore from the brothel, helps to defend against the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) by [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings).(wiki:Mole's_Town_brothel.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
