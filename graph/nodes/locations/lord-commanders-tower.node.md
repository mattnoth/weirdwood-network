## Appearances & Description

The Lord Commander's Tower is a formidable stone tower. The top two floors contain the chambers of the [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch). The tower's [solar](wiki:Solar) includes a table and windows with heavy drapes. An inner door leads to the sleeping cell of [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont).(wiki:Lord_Commander's_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Below the Lord Commander's chambers are cells for other black brothers.(wiki:Lord_Commander's_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Its subcellars include an undervault.(wiki:Lord_Commander's_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

After being named as the steward to [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) moves from [Hardin's Tower](wiki:Hardin's_Tower)(wiki:Lord_Commander's_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) to a cell below Jeor's chambers.(wiki:Lord_Commander's_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) When [Othor](wiki:Othor) rises as a [wight](wiki:Wight) and attempts to assassinate Jeor, Jon saves the Lord Commander's life after being awakened by [Ghost](wiki:Ghost). Jon burns the wight after drapes are set afire from an oil lamp.(wiki:Lord_Commander's_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) The interior of the tower's top two floors are gutted from the fire, so Jeor makes his residence in the [King's Tower](wiki:King's_Tower).(wiki:Lord_Commander's_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

During the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black), Jon sees the empty shell of the Lord Commander's Keep. [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte) is killed near the tower during the fighting.(wiki:Lord_Commander's_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Jon, now Lord Commander, resides in [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye)'s old quarters behind the armory, since the Lord Commander's Tower is a shell and [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) has occupied the King's Tower.(wiki:Lord_Commander's_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Jon orders that the captive [Cregan Karstark](wiki:Cregan_Karstark) be moved from the ice cells to the undervault of the Lord Commander's Tower.(wiki:Lord_Commander's_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
