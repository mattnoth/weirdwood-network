## Origins

Flint's Finger was once held by [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn). A weak [High King of the Iron Islands](wiki:High_King_of_the_Iron_Islands) lost Flint's Finger in the century after [Gerold Lannister](wiki:Gerold_Lannister_(king)), [King of the Rock](wiki:King_of_the_Rock), raided the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands).(wiki:Flint's_Finger.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
[Timotty Snow](wiki:Timotty_Snow), the Bastard of Flint's Finger, helped form the [Wolf Pack](wiki:Wolf_Pack) with [Hallis Hornwood](wiki:Hallis_Hornwood) after the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Flint's_Finger.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
