## Appearances & Description

The [sept](wiki:Sept) has altars, statues, and stained glass.(wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) The statues of [the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) were carved from the masts of the ships which brought [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) from [Valyria](wiki:Valyria) to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)).(wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) The statues have been repainted and decorated over the centuries.(wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

## Origins

Prior to beginning [their invasion](wiki:Aegon's_Landing) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), Lord [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) and his sister-wives, [Visenya](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) and [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen), prayed in the castle sept to [the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
The castle septon refused to officiate the wedding of Prince [Maegor Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) and his second wife, [Alys Harroway](wiki:Alys_Harroway) in [39 AC](wiki:39_AC), so the ceremony was instead performed by the Dowager Queen Visenya.(wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Septon [Oswyck](wiki:Oswyck) of Dragonstone officiated the marriage of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and his sister-wife, Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), in the castle's great yard in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC).(wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
Lord [Rodrik Arryn](wiki:Rodrik_Arryn) wed Princess [Daella Targaryen](wiki:Daella_Targaryen) in a small ceremony in the sept at Dragonstone in [80 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) married Lady [Baela Targaryen](wiki:Baela_Targaryen) in the sept on Dragonstone in [132 AC](wiki:132_AC).(wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), [Lord of Dragonstone](wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone), announces his claim for the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) amid the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre), a [red priestess](wiki:Red_priest) from [Asshai](wiki:Asshai), convinces him to have the statues of [the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) in the sept burned as sacrifices to [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor), the Lord of Light.(wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)
Septon [Barre](wiki:Barre) curses the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) when they ruin the sept and pull down the statues. Ser [Hubard Rambton](wiki:Hubard_Rambton) and his three sons try to defend the sept by force, slaying four of the queen's men before being overwhelmed. Barre and the two surviving Rambton sons are subsequently imprisoned in Dragonstone's dungeons.(wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) When pious Lord [Guncer Sunglass](wiki:Guncer_Sunglass) withdraws his support for Stannis's cause in response, the [Lord of Sweetport Sound](wiki:Lord_of_Sweetport_Sound) is imprisoned as well.(wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6)
Melisandre publicly burns the statues of the Seven outside of the castle gates. Stannis draws [Lightbringer](wiki:Lightbringer) from the ruins of [the Mother](wiki:Mother_(the_Seven)).(wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

While Stannis is away fighting the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Melisandre and Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) have Lord Sunglass and the two Rambtons burned at Dragonstone for treason.(wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8)
