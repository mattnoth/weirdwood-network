## Origins

Some believe the Thousand Islands are the remnants of a drowned kingdom whose buildings were submerged by the rising sea thousands of years ago.(wiki:Thousand_Islands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_east_of_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) Few mariners land on the islands, due to their inhabitants.(wiki:Thousand_Islands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_east_of_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

## Origins

[Sarnori](wiki:Sarnor) ships sailed the Thousand Islands in the height of their power.(wiki:Thousand_Islands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) There is evidence of historical [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibben) settlements in the Thousand Islands.(wiki:Thousand_Islands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
On his third voyage on the [Sea Snake](wiki:Sea_Snake), [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) became the first [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) to navigate the Thousand Islands and visit [N'ghai](wiki:N'ghai) and [Mossovy](wiki:Mossovy).(wiki:Thousand_Islands.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Corlys turned back after reaching the isles,(wiki:Thousand_Islands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_east_of_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7) with no man from the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) sailing further east in the [Shivering Sea](wiki:Shivering_Sea).(wiki:Thousand_Islands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_shivering_sea.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
