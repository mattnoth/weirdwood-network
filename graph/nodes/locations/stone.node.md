## Appearances & Description

The path to Stone on the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance) is surrounded by forest, including tall [pines](wiki:Pine) and grey-green [sentinels](wiki:Sentinel).(wiki:Stone.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) The waycastle has a massive [ironbound](wiki:Iron) gate and a yard. The stone walls are crowned with iron spikes and its two large round towers rise above a keep. Stone's stables have fresh mules for travelers. The trail then becomes steeper as it continues on to the smaller waycastles [Snow](wiki:Snow_(waycastle)) and [Sky](wiki:Sky).(wiki:Stone.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Origins

Queen [Sharra Arryn](wiki:Sharra_Arryn) tripled the size of the garrisons in Stone, [Snow](wiki:Snow_(waycastle)), and [Sky](wiki:Sky) in preparation for the imminent march of the [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) forces against the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest). Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) evaded them, however, by flying [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie)'s inner courtyard.(wiki:Stone.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) stops at Stone while traveling to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie). It is commanded by a portly [knight](wiki:Knight) who greets [Mya Stone](wiki:Mya_Stone) and offers charred meat and onions to the travelers.(wiki:Stone.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn) stop at Stone while descending the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance). [Myranda Royce](wiki:Myranda_Royce) suggests staying overnight at the waycastle, but Mya advises them to continue on to the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) because of the possibility of heavy snowfall.(wiki:Stone.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
