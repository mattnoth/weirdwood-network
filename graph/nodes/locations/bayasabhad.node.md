## Origins

Bayasabhad is one of three fortress cities that were once part of the [Patrimony of Hyrkoon](wiki:Patrimony_of_Hyrkoon) and were founded by the [Patriarchs of Hyrkoon](wiki:Patriarchs_of_Hyrkoon).(wiki:Bayasabhad.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Bayasabhad guards the eastern end of the Sand Road, which crosses the southern Bone Mountains, known as the [Dry Bones](wiki:Dry_Bones) for the scarcity of water.(wiki:Bayasabhad.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
