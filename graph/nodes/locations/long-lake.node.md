## Origins

In [226 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), the shore was the site of a [battle at Long Lake](wiki:Battle_at_Long_Lake), which saw [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) and [Umber](wiki:House_Umber) forces repel [Raymun Redbeard](wiki:Raymun_Redbeard), a [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall) who managed to bypass the Wall. Brothers of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) buried the dead of the battle.(wiki:Long_Lake.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) tells [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) she had a vision of a girl—believed to be [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)—in grey on a dying horse near a large body of water, Mance assumes she is speaking of Long Lake.(wiki:Long_Lake.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Instead of Arya, [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark) eventually arrives at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Long_Lake.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
