## Origins

### Ancient History[ ]

Some [maesters](wiki:Maesters), such as [Cassander](wiki:Cassander), believe the Sea of Dorne was once a smaller inland sea separated from the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) by salt marshes, and that the narrow sea eventually flooded into this once-freshwater sea through the [Song of the Sea](wiki:Song_of_the_Sea).(wiki:Sea_of_Dorne.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_the_breaking.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
The [Storm Kings](wiki:Storm_King) of old sometimes kept war fleets at the towns and village situated on the northern shore of the sea.(wiki:Sea_of_Dorne.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_the_men_of_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### Dornish Wars[ ]

During the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), a pirate king from the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) transported [Dornish](wiki:Dornishmen) raiders across the sea to attack [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath) and the [rainwood](wiki:Rainwood).(wiki:Sea_of_Dorne.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragons_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and his sons, Princes [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) and [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)), used their [dragons](wiki:Dragon) to destroy the fleet of [Morion Martell](wiki:Morion_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), as it crossed the Sea of Dorne in the [Fourth Dornish War](wiki:Fourth_Dornish_War).(wiki:Sea_of_Dorne.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) sails across the Sea of Dorne from [Ghost Hill](wiki:Ghost_Hill)(wiki:Sea_of_Dorne.cite_ref-TWOW1_5-1) to the [Weeping Town](wiki:Weeping_Town) aboard the [Peregrine](wiki:Peregrine).(wiki:Sea_of_Dorne.cite_ref-11) It is reported that strange sails have been seen entering the sea and landing on [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath).(wiki:Sea_of_Dorne.cite_ref-TWOW1_5-2)
