## Appearances & Description

The Crag is more of a ruin than a castle as the [Westerlings](wiki:House_Westerling) no longer have the funds to maintain it.(wiki:Crag.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Origins

King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) visited the castle in [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Crag.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Gawen Westerling](wiki:Gawen_Westerling), [Lord of the Crag](wiki:Lord_of_the_Crag), is captured in the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Crag.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Gawen is held at [Seagard](wiki:Seagard) by [House Mallister](wiki:House_Mallister).(wiki:Crag.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), raids the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). He marches on the Crag around the same time as the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords).(wiki:Crag.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) News is received at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) that Robb has taken the castle.(wiki:Crag.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[The Crag was stormed](wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag), with [Smalljon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber_(son_of_Jon)) and [Black Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Ryman)) scaling the walls and Robb breaking through the main gate. Robb took an arrow to the arm, however, and was nursed back to health by [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling).(wiki:Crag.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Jeyne comforted Robb when he learned of the alleged deaths of his younger brothers, [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon](wiki:Rickon_Stark), at [Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell). To preserve her honor after they slept together, Robb married Jeyne shortly thereafter. As a consequence of that, the [Westerlings](wiki:House_Westerling) have joined with [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark), though the marriage has broken Robb's alliance with [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey).(wiki:Crag.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
In the aftermath of Robb's death at the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding),(wiki:Crag.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Ser [Rolph Spicer](wiki:Rolph_Spicer), the [castellan](wiki:Castellan) of the Crag, is named [Lord of Castamere](wiki:Lord_of_Castamere) by the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Crag.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) resolves the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Lord Gawen and his family begin their journey back to the Crag.(wiki:Crag.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
