## Origins

Ancient(wiki:Claw_Isle.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Claw Isle was settled by [House Celtigar](wiki:House_Celtigar), a family of [Valyrian](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) ancestry. Prior to [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), the Celtigars and the [Velaryons](wiki:House_Velaryon) of [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark) controlled the middle portions of the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) with their ships.(wiki:Claw_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Celtigars from Claw Isle attempted to conquer [Crackclaw Point](wiki:Crackclaw_Point) prior to the Conquest, and some have sent tax collectors to the mainland even after the peninsula was made part of the [crownlands](wiki:Crownlands).(wiki:Claw_Isle.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Lord [Edwell Celtigar](wiki:Edwell_Celtigar) resigned as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) and returned to Claw Isle in [48 AC](wiki:48_AC) after King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) declined to marry one of his daughters.(wiki:Claw_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Edwell later served King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) as [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin), but he again returned to his island when the king reached his majority in [50 AC](wiki:50_AC).(wiki:Claw_Isle.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_resting_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord [Walys Mooton](wiki:Walys_Mooton)'s force to retake [Rook's Rest](wiki:Rook's_Rest) from [the greens](wiki:The_greens) included men from Claw Isle.(wiki:Claw_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Though Lord [Ardrian Celtigar](wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar) presents himself as stingy, Claw Isle's castle is reputedly stuffed with [Myrish](wiki:Myr) carpets, [Volantene](wiki:Volantis) glass, [gold](wiki:Gold) and [silver](wiki:Silver) plate, jeweled cups, magnificent [hawks](wiki:Hawk), a trained [sea eagle](wiki:Eagle), an axe of [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel), a [magic](wiki:Magic) horn said to summon [krakens](wiki:Kraken) from the deep, chests of [rubies](wiki:Ruby), and numerous [wines](wiki:Wine).(wiki:Claw_Isle.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Claw_Isle.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Ardrian Celtigar](wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar), [Lord of Claw Isle](wiki:Lord_of_Claw_Isle), takes most of the able-bodied men with him when [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) calls his banners during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Claw_Isle.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Captured in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Ardrian bends the knee to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Claw_Isle.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Because Lord Celtigar submitted to Joffrey, Ser [Axell Florent](wiki:Axell_Florent) plots with [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan) to attack Claw Isle as a reprisal, for the island is lightly garrisoned. Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) advises Stannis that it is a poor option, reminding him that Claw Isle is only held by women, children, and old men because the warriors of the island were killed fighting for Stannis on the Blackwater. Stannis decides not to attack Claw Isle. For his counsel and loyalty, Stannis names Davos [Lord of the Rainwood](wiki:Lord_of_the_Rainwood) and [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:Claw_Isle.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
