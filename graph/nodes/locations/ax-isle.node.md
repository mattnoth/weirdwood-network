## Origins

There are ruins from a possibly ancient civilization on the island.(wiki:Ax_Isle.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_basilisk_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Some of [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria)'s [ten thousand ships](wiki:Ten_thousand_ships) were attacked by corsairs from Ax Isle. Afterwards, the corsairs offered the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar) to settle upon the [Isle of Toads](wiki:Isle_of_Toads), on the condition that they give up their boats and that they send each king thirty virgin girls and pretty boys per year, but Nymeria refused.(wiki:Ax_Isle.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
