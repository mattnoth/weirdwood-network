## Appearances & Description

A sept is a single building with seven walls. Some septs are small and humble, but others are large and magnificent, such as the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor). Some septs have crystal(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) or leaded windows.(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Every sept houses representational art portraying each of the seven aspects of the [Faith](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven). Wealthy septs in cities have stone statues and altars for each of the Seven.(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Crystals spread seven-colored rainbows onto the altars,(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) which may be inlaid with precious metals and stones, such as [mother-of-pearl](wiki:Mother-of-pearl), [onyx](wiki:Onyx), and [lapis lazuli](wiki:Lapis_lazuli).(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) In more isolated or rural septs, however, the depictions of the Seven may be merely carved masks or simple charcoal drawings hanging on each wall.(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
Septs can be fortified for greater protection.(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Origins

In the aftermath of the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals), kings such as [Merle I Gardener](wiki:Merle_I_Gardener),(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf773_18-0) [Humfrey I Teague](wiki:Humfrey_I_Teague),(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf73.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) and the [Arryns](wiki:House_Arryn)(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf741_20-0) founded septs, [septries](wiki:Septry) and [motherhouses](wiki:Motherhouse) throughout southern [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). The [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) was the seat of the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon) for a thousand years.(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) The [Sept-Beyond-the-Sea](wiki:Sept-Beyond-the-Sea) is a sept in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) for [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Raffc3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
King [Theon Stark](wiki:Theon_Stark), a follower of the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods), repelled an attack of [Andals](wiki:Andals) on the [north](wiki:North) and retaliated by raiding [Andalos](wiki:Andalos) in western [Essos](wiki:Essos), burning a score of villages, capturing three towerhouses and a fortified sept, and killing hundreds.(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf711_23-0) Septs in the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) have periodically been destroyed by worshippers of the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God).(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf753_24-0)
[Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) is said to have prayed at the sept of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) before beginning [the Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) A sept was destroyed during Aegon's [burning of Harrenhal](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal).(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)
Soon after Aegon founded [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in [2 BC](wiki:Years_before_Aegon's_Conquest), a makeshift sept was constructed out of the hulk of a [cog](wiki:Cog) on the [Blackwater](wiki:Blackwater) to serve the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk), and a much grander sept was raised on [Visenya's Hill](wiki:Visenya's_Hill) with money sent by the High Septon.(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf41.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) After [10 AC](wiki:10_AC), the [Sept of Remembrance](wiki:Sept_of_Remembrance) was built on [Rhaenys's Hill](wiki:Rhaenys's_Hill) in memory of the late Queen [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen).(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf41.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-1) That sept was used as the base of the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons) during the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising), and was [destroyed](wiki:Burning_of_the_Sept_of_Remembrance) in the conflict by [Maegor the Cruel](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) in [42 AC](wiki:42_AC).(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf43.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)
Inspired by a vision, King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen) ordered the construction of a great sept atop Visenya's Hill; the fate of the grand sept atop the hill from Aegon the Conqueror's reign is unknown. The [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) was completed years after the death of Baelor the Blessed,(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf49.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) and the High Septon moved there, replacing the Starry Sept of Oldtown as the center of the Faith in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1)
In [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) fought and killed Ser [Tommard Heddle](wiki:Tommard_Heddle) in the sept of [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls), in defense of Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen).(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-2)
Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) constructed [a small sept](wiki:Lady_Catelyn's_sept) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) for his [southron](wiki:Southron) wife, [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Stark).(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) The sept at [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport) was not rebuilt after its destruction during [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion).(wiki:Sept.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)
