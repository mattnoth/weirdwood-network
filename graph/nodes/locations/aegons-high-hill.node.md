## Origins

When Lord [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) sailed from [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) at the start of [the Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), he chose the mouth of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) as the site of his first base of operations. The [Aegonfort](wiki:Aegonfort), a crude wood-and-earth fortifications, was built on the tall hill that would later be given his name.(wiki:Aegon's_High_Hill.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
The city of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) developed haphazardly around the Aegonfort,(wiki:Aegon's_High_Hill.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) which itself came to spread halfway down Aegon's High Hill. In [35 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), the king had the Aegonfort torn down to begin the construction of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) as a more durable and fitting seat for [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen).(wiki:Aegon's_High_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Aegon's_High_Hill.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
[Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) ordered secret passages and tunnels throughout the depths of the hill to connect with the Red Keep.(wiki:Aegon's_High_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)(wiki:Aegon's_High_Hill.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
Princess [Viserra Targaryen](wiki:Viserra_Targaryen) died from a [horseracing](wiki:Horse) accident in an alley at the foot of Aegon's High Hill.(wiki:Aegon's_High_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
At the end of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), thousands climbed Aegon's High Hill to hear Prince [Aegon the Younger](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) proclaim an end to the fighting.(wiki:Aegon's_High_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath:_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) sees Aegon's high hill when she arrives at the [harbor of King's Landing](wiki:Harbor_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:Aegon's_High_Hill.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sees the [red comet](wiki:Red_comet) in the skies above the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) atop Aegon's High Hill.(wiki:Aegon's_High_Hill.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) keeps her focus on Aegon's High Hill during her [walk of atonement](wiki:Walk_of_atonement) through the streets of King's Landing. The [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) of [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom) crowd the lower slopes of the hill during her journey.(wiki:Aegon's_High_Hill.cite_ref-Radwd65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
