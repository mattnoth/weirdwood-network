## Appearances & Description

The [manse](wiki:Manse) has nine towers and high brick walls overgrown with pale ivy.(wiki:Drogo's_manse.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Its gates are guarded by [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied), including at least one of [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) origin.(wiki:Drogo's_manse.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
The manse's interior is scented with [spices](wiki:Spices), [pinchfire](wiki:Pinchfire), [lemon](wiki:Lemon), and cinnamon. A colored glass mosaic in the entry hall displays the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria). The manse has an arch of twining stone leaves and a pillared courtyard overgrown with ivy.(wiki:Drogo's_manse.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Betrothed to Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) travels from [Illyrio's manse](wiki:Illyrio's_manse) to attend a feast at Drogo's manse.(wiki:Drogo's_manse.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) Drogo gives his estates to Daenerys and her brother, [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen), for their residence until her wedding. One night they eat honey duck and orange snap peppers.(wiki:Drogo's_manse.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) The day after the wedding, the Targaryen siblings accompany Drogo's [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) on a journey to [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak).(wiki:Drogo's_manse.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
