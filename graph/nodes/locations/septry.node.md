## Origins

The ancient septry at [Peasedale](wiki:Peasedale) contains fragments of *[Annals of the Rivers](wiki:Annals_of_the_Rivers)*.(wiki:Septry.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn), the [Kings of Mountain and Vale](wiki:Kings_of_Mountain_and_Vale), encouraged learning at the [septs](wiki:Sept) and septries in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn).(wiki:Septry.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_vale:_house_arryn.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Likewise, [Merle I Gardener](wiki:Merle_I_Gardener), [King of the Reach](wiki:King_of_the_Reach), encouraged construction of septries, septs and [motherhouses](wiki:Motherhouse) in the [Reach](wiki:Reach).(wiki:Septry.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Prior to the reconciliation of the Faith through the efforts of Septon [Barth](wiki:Barth), greedy septries and septons took advantage of [lords](wiki:Lord) and neighbors during the reign of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Septry.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

The journey of King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen) to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) is remembered in songs popularized by septries and motherhouses.(wiki:Septry.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Septon [Meribald](wiki:Meribald) often stays at septries during his travels.(wiki:Septry.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Appearances & Description

Forty-four brothers live at a [prosperous septry](wiki:Burning_septry) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). They tend to a dozen milk cows and a bull, a hundred beehives, a vineyard, an apple arbor, a mill, and a brewhouse. The septry has wooden walls and a heavy slate roof.(wiki:Septry.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

The septry at the [Quiet Isle](wiki:Quiet_Isle) contains terraced fields, an apple grove, a stable, a windmill, cloisters, a common hall, and a wooden [sept](wiki:Sept). Modest cottages are set aside for female visitors. Sheep graze on the island.(wiki:Septry.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

The [battle at the burning septry](wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) involves the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) and the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions).(wiki:Septry.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Feast for Crows

During the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), [northmen](wiki:Northmen) burn a holy house near [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool). [Sellswords](wiki:Sellsword) later kill its brothers, aside from a [dwarf brother](wiki:Dwarf_brother) who hides.(wiki:Septry.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) visits the septry at the [Quiet Isle](wiki:Quiet_Isle) and meets [its Elder Brother](wiki:Elder_Brother_(Quiet_Isle)).(wiki:Septry.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
