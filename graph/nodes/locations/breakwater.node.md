## Appearances & Description

Breakwater is built partially on huge, stone arches that stand as bulwarks against the [Bite](wiki:Bite).(wiki:Breakwater.cite_ref-Rawoiafsisterton.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Entry to Breakwater is by a bridge of black [basalt](wiki:Basalt) and a rusty [iron](wiki:Iron) portcullis. Its defenses include a deep salt moat and a drawbridge supported by two massive chains, followed by a larger gatehouse of algae-covered stones. Steps lead to Breakwater's cavernous stone keep. Its entrance is covered by threadbare [Myrish](wiki:Myr) carpet.(wiki:Breakwater.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Breakwater's hall is gloomy and has a leaky roof. Only four of its twenty wall-mounted sconces hold torches. A white spider crab on a grey-green field, the sigil of [House Borrell](wiki:House_Borrell), is embroidered on a banner above the hall's hearth. Beneath the castle are dungeons.(wiki:Breakwater.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
The [Night Lamp](wiki:Night_Lamp), the black tower lighthouse of Sisterton,(wiki:Breakwater.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) is possibly located at Breakwater.

## Origins

Near the start of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) washed up on Sweetsister while trying to sail from the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) to the [north](wiki:North) to raise his banners. Lord Borrell, the father of [Godric Borrell](wiki:Godric_Borrell), could have turned Eddard over to King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) for a reward, but instead freed him, saying, "If you lose, you were never here."(wiki:Breakwater.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The [Lionstar](wiki:Lionstar) stops at [Sisterton](wiki:Sisterton) and Lord [Godric Borrell](wiki:Godric_Borrell) hosts Ser [Jared](wiki:Jared_Frey), [Rhaegar](wiki:Rhaegar_Frey), and [Symond Frey](wiki:Symond_Frey).(wiki:Breakwater.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
After being caught at the [Belly of the Whale](wiki:Belly_of_the_Whale), [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) is taken to Godric inside Breakwater. After hosting Davos with brown [beer](wiki:Beer), black bread, and [sister's stew](wiki:Sister's_stew) made by [Gella](wiki:Gella), Godric allows him to travel on to [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor).(wiki:Breakwater.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
