## Origins

Before [Elissa Farman](wiki:Elissa_Farman) departed [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) on [Sun Chaser](wiki:Sun_Chaser), the [Sealord](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos) called her to his palace and advised her to sail away quickly and cautiously.(wiki:Sealord's_Palace.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
