## Origins

Ser [Lucas Nayland](wiki:Lucas_Nayland) rode from Hag's Mire to attend the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls).(wiki:Hag's_Mire.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
[Chett](wiki:Chett) grew up in Hag's Mire helping his father collect [leeches](wiki:Leech). Chett fled the village after killing [Bessa](wiki:Bessa_(Hag's_Mire)), but he was apprehended near [Sevenstreams](wiki:Sevenstreams).(wiki:Hag's_Mire.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

With the fords of the [Blue Fork](wiki:Blue_Fork) overflowing and the bridges at [Fairmarket](wiki:Fairmarket) and [Oldstones](wiki:Oldstones) washed away, King [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s thirty-five hundred men are forced to ride through Hag's Mire and [Sevenstreams](wiki:Sevenstreams) on their way from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) to the [Twins](wiki:Twins). While the host is encamped at Hag's Mire, Robb explains his plan to [retake Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin) from the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) to [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) and [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully), [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister), [Greatjon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber), [Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover), and [Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont). The attendees are also witnesses to Robb's will.(wiki:Hag's_Mire.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Black Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Ryman)) leads a group of men and dogs into the mire chasing after a [band of outlaws](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) who may have had some involvement in the hangings of [Merrett](wiki:Merrett_Frey) and [Petyr Frey](wiki:Petyr_Frey).(wiki:Hag's_Mire.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
