## Origins

[Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Benifer](wiki:Benifer) was in his rookery when Princess [Aerea Targaryen](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen) and [Balerion](wiki:Balerion) returned to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in [56 AC](wiki:56_AC).(wiki:Rookery.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

Princess [Viserra Targaryen](wiki:Viserra_Targaryen) liked to manipulate the boys who courted her, playing them off one against the other. To win her favor for a [joust](wiki:Joust), she would send them on foolish quests, such as setting free all the ravens in the Red Keep rookery.(wiki:Rookery.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

During the [fall of Dragonstone](wiki:Fall_of_Dragonstone), Ser [Marston Waters](wiki:Marston_Waters) seized the rookery to prevent Grand Maester [Gerardys](wiki:Gerardys) from sending ravens.(wiki:Rookery.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

The cat [Balerion](wiki:Balerion_(cat)) has been known to attack ravens in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) rookery.(wiki:Rookery.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

During the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell), the maester's turret and the bridge connecting the [Bell Tower](wiki:Bell_Tower) to the rookery are destroyed. The maester's ravens are loosed, and eat the flesh of the slain, such as [Poxy Tym](wiki:Poxy_Tym).(wiki:Rookery.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Cleaning the rookery is among [Chett](wiki:Chett)'s duties at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Rookery.cite_ref-Rasos0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) builds a replica of Winterfell out of snow at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie). [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) helps her make the bridge in between the rookery and the Bell Tower.(wiki:Rookery.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)

### A Feast for Crows

After the death of Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn), ravens come and go from the Eyrie's rookery.(wiki:Rookery.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

During the [taking of Griffin's Roost](wiki:Taking_of_Griffin's_Roost) by the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company), the castle's maester is thrown from the rookery. Later, [Haldon Halfmaester](wiki:Haldon) is commanded by Lord [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) to take charge of the rookery in his stead.(wiki:Rookery.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)
