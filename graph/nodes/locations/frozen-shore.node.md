## Origins

The [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) of antiquity controlled the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea) from the Frozen Shore to the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor).(wiki:Frozen_Shore.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

[Wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) from the Frozen Shore have raided [Bear Island](wiki:Bear_Island).(wiki:Frozen_Shore.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

During the reign of King [Dorren Stark](wiki:Dorren_Stark), [Redwyn](wiki:Redwyn) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) claimed he encountered [giants](wiki:Giants) and [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) while ranging from the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) to [Lorn Point](wiki:Lorn_Point).(wiki:Frozen_Shore.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Frozen_Shore.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_dawn_age.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Before [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) negotiated a peace, the [walrus men](wiki:Men_of_the_Frozen_Shore) fought with those of the [ice-river clans](wiki:Ice-river_clans).(wiki:Frozen_Shore.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

[Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder), the [King-beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-beyond-the-Wall), tells [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) that his cloak of black wool and scarlet silk was a gift from a wildling woman who patched him up. The scarlet silk was her grandmother's most prized possession, which she found in the wreck of a cog from [Asshai](wiki:Asshai) off the Frozen Shore.(wiki:Frozen_Shore.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

[Men of the Frozen Shore](wiki:Men_of_the_Frozen_Shore) are found in Mance's army as it [attacks the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall).(wiki:Frozen_Shore.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
