## Appearances & Description

The wormways are a maze of tunnels which link the keeps and towers of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Wormways.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Wormways.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) The tunnels are lit by candles placed in wall niches.(wiki:Wormways.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) The wormwalks have steep stone steps which reach the surface.(wiki:Wormways.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) The [vaults of Castle Black](wiki:Vaults_of_Castle_Black) open onto wormwalks.(wiki:Wormways.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Wormwalks are used infrequently during summertime, but they become invaluable during winter when snowfall makes travel difficult above ground.(wiki:Wormways.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)(wiki:Wormways.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) and [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) use the wormwalks when visiting the [vaults of Castle Black](wiki:Vaults_of_Castle_Black).(wiki:Wormways.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

### A Storm of Swords

Jon takes a wormway from the [armory](wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black)) to the bathhouse.(wiki:Wormways.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Sam uses a wormway when returning from the vaults.(wiki:Wormways.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Dance with Dragons

Jon and [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) take the wormways to check the storerooms of the castle.(wiki:Wormways.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

The men of Castle Black increasingly use the wormways as winter intensifies.(wiki:Wormways.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
