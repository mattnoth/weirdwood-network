## Origins

The [Vaiths](wiki:House_Vaith) built their castle during the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Vaith.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_the_andals_arrive.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) discovered that the Vaiths had abandoned the castle.(wiki:Vaith.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
The Vaiths attacked the foraging parties of Lord [Harlan Tyrell](wiki:Harlan_Tyrell) during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War). After hearing of the [Defenestration of Sunspear](wiki:Defenestration_of_Sunspear), Lord Tyrell intended to capture Vaith and continue east along [the river](wiki:Vaith_(river)). His army disappeared in the red sands between the [Hellholt](wiki:Hellholt) and Vaith, however. King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) and [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) burned Vaith in [9 AC](wiki:9_AC).(wiki:Vaith.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
At some point between the ending of [The Hedge Knight](wiki:The_Hedge_Knight) and the start of [The Sworn Sword](wiki:The_Sworn_Sword), Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) and Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) reached the [Prince's Pass](wiki:Prince's_Pass) in Dorne, probably seeking [Tanselle Too-Tall](wiki:Tanselle) as hinted by the last few words of The Hedge Knight. Dunk's horse, [Chestnut](wiki:Chestnut), died on the crossing from the Prince's Pass to Vaith.(wiki:Vaith.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Dunk and Egg rode double on [Thunder](wiki:Thunder_(horse)) for an unspecified period of time until one of Egg's brothers gave them [Maester](wiki:Maester_(mule)).(wiki:Vaith.cite_ref-8) Once in Vaith, Dunk met the aged Lady [Cassella Vaith](wiki:Cassella_Vaith) and spoke to her with such lack of grace as to put his own life and Egg's in danger.(wiki:Vaith.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) According to Egg, however, Lady Vaith was mad,(wiki:Vaith.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) so it is unclear if that danger could have been avoided.
Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) fled [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) with [Tyene Sand](wiki:Tyene_Sand) to try to marry [Willas Tyrell](wiki:Willas_Tyrell) at [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden), but they were caught by [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) at Vaith.(wiki:Vaith.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) plans to take Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) across the sands of Dorne via Vaith, the [Hellholt](wiki:Hellholt), and [Sandstone](wiki:Sandstone), but they are intercepted by [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah) on the [Greenblood](wiki:Greenblood).(wiki:Vaith.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
