## Origins

The Golden Tooth is the seat of the [Leffords](wiki:House_Lefford), a family formed by the union of [First Men](wiki:First_Men) and [Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals).(wiki:Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) [Cerion Lannister](wiki:Cerion_Lannister), a [King of the Rock](wiki:King_of_the_Rock), conquered the hilly vicinity of the Tooth by defeating three petty kings.(wiki:Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) The [gold](wiki:Gold) from the Tooth helped establish [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) as one of the richest houses in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Ser [Joffrey Doggett](wiki:Joffrey_Doggett) controlled much of the land north of the Golden Tooth during the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising).(wiki:Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) and her dragon, [Dreamfyre](wiki:Dreamfyre), briefly stayed at the Golden Tooth with the Leffords in [51 AC](wiki:51_AC).(wiki:Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) visited the castle in [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

During the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), the supporters of [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) won victories at [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) and the Golden Tooth.(wiki:Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of the Golden Tooth](wiki:Category:Images_of_the_Golden_Tooth)*

Although the Golden Tooth is a small castle,(wiki:Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-3) it is a strong keep commanding the hill road between the westerlands and the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) It is generally held that in order to attack the westerlands from the east, one must take the Tooth to have a secure passage.(wiki:Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) sends Lords [Piper](wiki:Clement_Piper) and [Vance](wiki:House_Vance_of_Wayfarer's_Rest) to prevent any [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) army from entering the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) from the Golden Tooth.(wiki:Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) A force under the command of Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) breaks them in a [battle in the hills below the Golden Tooth](wiki:Battle_in_the_hills_below_the_Golden_Tooth), however.(wiki:Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) After their own defeat in the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), Ser [Forley Prester](wiki:Forley_Prester) leads the remnants of Jaime's host back to the Golden Tooth.(wiki:Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Clash of Kings

During the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), an army under the command of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), circumvents the Golden Tooth and the passage it blocks by using a narrow goat path found by his [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind). Robb descends upon Ser [Stafford Lannister](wiki:Stafford_Lannister)'s host, annihilating them at [Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross), three days ride from [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport),(wiki:Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) and the king then raids the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands).(wiki:Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Oxcross shocks King [Joffrey I Baratheon's](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) court in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), since the Tooth remains in Lannister control.(wiki:Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

### A Storm of Swords

In return for the release of [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover) from [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale), Robb tasks Ser [Rolph Spicer](wiki:Rolph_Spicer) with bringing [Martyn Lannister](wiki:Martyn_Lannister) to the Golden Tooth.(wiki:Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Following the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister) and Forley march from the Golden Tooth toward [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Feast for Crows

After Jaime resolves the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Forley commands the escort bringing Edmure to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock). Jaime worries the group will be attacked by the [outlaws](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) of Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) before they reach the Golden Tooth.(wiki:Golden_Tooth.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
