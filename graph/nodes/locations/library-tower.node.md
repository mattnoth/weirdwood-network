## Appearances & Description

The Library Tower is located across the bailey from the [Great Keep](wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell)).(wiki:Library_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Snug and warm, the Library Tower has high windows.(wiki:Library_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) The tower contains shelves, benches, and a table. [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell)'s library contains more than a hundred books(wiki:Library_Tower.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and includes rare volumes, including a complete copy of [Engines of War](wiki:Engines_of_War) by [Ayrmidon](wiki:Ayrmidon).(wiki:Library_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) Curling around the tower's exterior are steep stone steps.(wiki:Library_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

## Origins

The library of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) has been collected by [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) for centuries.(wiki:Library_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) When Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) visited Winterfell in [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Lord [Alaric Stark](wiki:Alaric_Stark) allowed her to browse the modest castle library.(wiki:Library_Tower.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) explores the library during his visit to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) and reads a book about the changing of the seasons. Meanwhile, Septon [Chayle](wiki:Chayle) falls asleep while reading a history of Grand Maester [Aethelmure](wiki:Aethelmure).(wiki:Library_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) allows Tyrion to borrow a few rare volumes for his journey north to the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Library_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
[An assassin](wiki:Catspaw) sets the Library Tower on fire as a distraction for his attempt to kill [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark).(wiki:Library_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Bran and Chayle sort through scrolls rescued from the library fire.(wiki:Library_Tower.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
After the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell), Bran sees a shallow steaming lake beneath the Library Tower and a crack in its side.(wiki:Library_Tower.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) includes the Library Tower while making a snow castle of Winterfell while at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie).(wiki:Library_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
