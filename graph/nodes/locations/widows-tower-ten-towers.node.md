## Appearances & Description

## Origins

[Gwynesse Harlaw](wiki:Gwynesse_Harlaw) returned to [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers) to mourn the death of her husband in the [sea battle off Fair Isle](wiki:Sea_battle_off_Fair_Isle) in [289 AC](wiki:289_AC). She claimed she would leave once her grief had passed, but the still-grieving widow has remained at Ten Towers, believing she should have inherited the castle instead of her younger brother, Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw).(wiki:Widow's_Tower_(Ten_Towers).cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Alannys Harlaw](wiki:Alannys_Harlaw), the ailing wife of [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), has moved to [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers) to live with her widowed sister [Gwynesse](wiki:Gwynesse_Harlaw),(wiki:Widow's_Tower_(Ten_Towers).cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) as it is less raw on [Harlaw](wiki:Harlaw) than on [Pyke](wiki:Pyke_(island)).(wiki:Widow's_Tower_(Ten_Towers).cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) visited her mother before sailing against [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fall_of_Deepwood_Motte), finding Alannys in the Widow's Tower staring out to sea under a pile of furs. Alannys only asks about her missing son, [Theon](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy).(wiki:Widow's_Tower_(Ten_Towers).cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
Asha returns to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) after learning of the death of her father, Lord [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy). When she arrives at [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers), Asha delays reuniting with her mother until the morning, choosing to meet first with her uncle, Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw), in the [Book Tower](wiki:Book_Tower).(wiki:Widow's_Tower_(Ten_Towers).cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Asha flees the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk) after [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) is chosen instead of her. She returns to Ten Towers to bid farewell to her mother, but Alannys does not understand and again asks for Theon.(wiki:Widow's_Tower_(Ten_Towers).cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
