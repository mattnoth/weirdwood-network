## Origins

[King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) rapidly grew without any planning during the first century following [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), so much so that by the reign of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) it became a problem; the streets were close, dark and filthy. In [52 AC](wiki:52_AC) Jaehaerys ordered the start of works to widen, straighten the streets, and cobble them where possible. Jaehaerys also built a great central square carved out and planted with trees, with markets and arcades beneath. From this square sprung Blackwater Way, which was quickly renamed the Muddy Way by [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk).(wiki:Muddy_Way.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

On the way back to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) from the docks, King [Joffrey I Baratheon's](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) party rides along the Muddy Way before arriving at the [Hook](wiki:Hook), where the [riots in King's Landing](wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing) begin.(wiki:Muddy_Way.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sees the Muddy Way crowded when he goes with [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) to the [winesink](wiki:Winesink) where he meets with [Symon Silver Tongue](wiki:Symon_Silver_Tongue). Tyrion tosses to children a fistful of [coppers](wiki:Currency).(wiki:Muddy_Way.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
