## Origins

Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel) states the [Carons](wiki:House_Caron) have held Nightsong for many centuries and the castle guarded the western border of the domain of the [Storm Kings](wiki:Storm_Kings) of [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon), but also that the [Kingdom of the Reach](wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Reach) once included the [western marches](wiki:Western_marches) from [Horn Hill](wiki:Horn_Hill) to Nightsong.(wiki:Nightsong.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Nightsong.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Records indicate the castle has been besieged at least thirty-seven times in the last millenium.(wiki:Nightsong.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_the_men_of_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Nightsong was burned by Lord [Fowler](wiki:House_Fowler) in [10 AC](wiki:10_AC) during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War). Ser [Addam Hightower](wiki:Addam_Hightower) recovered the castle, only to find that Lord Caron and his family had been taken to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) as captives.(wiki:Nightsong.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

The [Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Aenys_I)) led one of his hosts of [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) to attack Nightsong and Horn Hill. He failed to capture Nightsong, however, and while marching west was defeated by Lady [Ellyn Caron](wiki:Ellyn_Caron), Lord [Harmon Dondarrion](wiki:Harmon_Dondarrion), and Lord [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly_(lord)) during the [Vulture Hunt](wiki:Vulture_Hunt).(wiki:Nightsong.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

The Carons hosted King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) at Nightsong in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC).(wiki:Nightsong.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Lord [Royce Caron](wiki:Royce_Caron), part of the [regency of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III), returned to Nightsong in [132 AC](wiki:132_AC) to deal with Dornish incursions.(wiki:Nightsong.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser [Philip Foote](wiki:Philip_Foote) kills Lord [Bryce Caron](wiki:Bryce_Caron) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). The [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) awards Nightsong to Philip after the battle, forming [House Foote of Nightsong](wiki:House_Foote_of_Nightsong).(wiki:Nightsong.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

The Bastard of Nightsong, Bryce's half-brother Ser [Rolland Storm](wiki:Rolland_Storm), is the pretender [Lord of Nightsong](wiki:Lord_of_Nightsong).(wiki:Nightsong.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
