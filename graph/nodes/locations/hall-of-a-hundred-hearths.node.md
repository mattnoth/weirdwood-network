## Appearances & Description

The Hall of a Hundred Hearths is a cavernous and drafty room at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), larger than even the [throne room](wiki:Great_Hall_(Red_Keep)) of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Hall_of_a_Hundred_Hearths.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
Huge hearths are arranged along the walls every ten feet or so.(wiki:Hall_of_a_Hundred_Hearths.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Despite its name, it only contains thirty-some hearths.(wiki:Hall_of_a_Hundred_Hearths.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) The hall has a smooth [slate](wiki:Slate) floor and two galleries.(wiki:Hall_of_a_Hundred_Hearths.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

While serving incognito at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) tries to count the number of hearths in the great hall, counting thirty-three on one occasion and then thirty-five on another. Arya thinks the Hall of a Hundred Hearths could host all of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s army if he so desired. Tywin's captives eat at their own table in the hall.(wiki:Hall_of_a_Hundred_Hearths.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Harrenhal is left nearly empty once Tywin's army marches west to confront [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), so Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch) closes off the Hall of a Hundred Hearths.(wiki:Hall_of_a_Hundred_Hearths.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The castle then [falls](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal) to Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton).(wiki:Hall_of_a_Hundred_Hearths.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Roose, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), and [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) eat a meal at a lone trestle table in the center of the empty hall.(wiki:Hall_of_a_Hundred_Hearths.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
During the [capture of Harrenhal](wiki:Capture_of_Harrenhal) by the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men), Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) finds a fevered [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat) sitting alone in the Hall of a Hundred Hearths.(wiki:Hall_of_a_Hundred_Hearths.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Jaime is tasked with restoring order to the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). When he returns to Harrenhal, he has [Rafford](wiki:Rafford) gather the castle's occupants, including the garrison and captives, to the Hall of a Hundred Hearths for him to review. Jaime has fires lit in the hall so that his own men can eat, and he then has a private meal with Ser [Bonifer Hasty](wiki:Bonifer_Hasty) in the [Hunter's Hall](wiki:Hunter's_Hall).(wiki:Hall_of_a_Hundred_Hearths.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
