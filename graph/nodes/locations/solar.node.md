## Origins

During the [Golden Wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding), the young [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) granted audience to any lord or knight who wished a private word with him, agreeing to speak with them in his solar.(wiki:Solar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
In [78 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), King Jaehaerys I summoned his son, Prince [Vaegon Targaryen](wiki:Vaegon_Targaryen), in his solar and announced him he was sent to the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel).(wiki:Solar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) used to supper in the tranquility of his solar with his wife, Queen [Daenaera Velaryon](wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon), and his friend, [Gaemon Palehair](wiki:Gaemon_Palehair). It was during one of these meal that Gaemon a was [poisoned](wiki:Poison).(wiki:Solar.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

In [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) and his daughters [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark) usually eat meals in the solar of the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand).(wiki:Solar.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)
The [wight](wiki:Wight) [Othor](wiki:Othor) attacks [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) in his solar, but [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) defeats it by setting it and the room on fire.(wiki:Solar.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) frequently does business in the solar of the Tower of the Hand.(wiki:Solar.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) has come to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) and taken up the position of Hand of the King, and Tyrion is reluctant to consider the Hand's solar as no longer his.(wiki:Solar.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)
Jon Snow is accused of treachery by Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne) in the solar in [King's Tower](wiki:King's_Tower) of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Solar.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) Later, [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) demonstrates the fiery glow of [Lightbringer](wiki:Lightbringer) to [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) and [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Aemon](wiki:Aemon) in that solar, radiance filling the room.(wiki:Solar.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) meets with [Nestor Royce](wiki:Nestor_Royce) and the [Lords Declarant](wiki:Lords_Declarant) in the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie)'s solar.(wiki:Solar.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)(wiki:Solar.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)
