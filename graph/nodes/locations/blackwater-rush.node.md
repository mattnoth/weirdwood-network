## Origins

### Antiquity

In the days of the [Hundred Kingdoms](wiki:Hundred_Kingdoms), the mouth of the Blackwater was claimed by the [Darklyns](wiki:House_Darklyn) of [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale), the [Masseys](wiki:House_Massey) of [Stonedance](wiki:Stonedance), and the [river kings](wiki:River_kings) of Houses [Mudd](wiki:House_Mudd), [Fisher](wiki:House_Fisher), [Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken), [Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood), and [Hook](wiki:House_Hook).(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) The river's mouth was successfully claimed by King [Benedict II Justman](wiki:Benedict_II_Justman).(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

### The Conquest

Prior to [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), the southern shore of the Blackwater was claimed by [Argilac Durrandon](wiki:Argilac_Durrandon), the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King), and the northern shore by [Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare), the [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers).(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1) After [landing at the mouth of the river](wiki:Aegon's_Landing), [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) founded the [Aegonfort](wiki:Aegonfort). The new king had the swords of those slain in the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire) sent downriver as material for the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

A new town developed at the mouth of the Blackwater Rush during the Conquest. After being anointed by the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aegon's_Conquest)) at [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), Aegon I chose to make the court of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) at this new settlement, which would become known as [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1)

### House Targaryen

A cog on the Blackwater was used as a temporary [sept](wiki:Sept) for [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), and then a stouter building of daub-and-wattle was built on the shore. The High Septon then sponsored the building of a more impressive permanent sept at [Visenya's Hill](wiki:Visenya's_Hill).(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) achieved a great victory over the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons) in a [battle at the Great Fork](wiki:Battle_at_the_Great_Fork) of the Blackwater during the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising).(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

In [57 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Septon [Barth](wiki:Barth) proposed a system of wells and pipes to deliver fresh water to King's Landing, as the Blackwater had muddy river water which was also polluted by sewers.(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

Princess [Gael Targaryen](wiki:Gael_Targaryen) drowned herself in the river in [99 AC](wiki:99_AC).(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)

After the [yielding of Harrenhal](wiki:Yielding_of_Harrenhal) in the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) and [Caraxes](wiki:Caraxes) followed the Blackwater downstream to participate in the participate in the [fall of King's Landing](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) [Moslander of Yore](wiki:Moslander_of_Yore), Ser [Garrick Hall](wiki:Garrick_Hall) of [Middleton](wiki:Middleton), Ser [Merrell the Bold](wiki:Merrell_the_Bold), Ser [Roger Corne](wiki:Roger_Corne), and Lord [Owain Bourney](wiki:Owain_Bourney) were men of the Blackwater who participated in the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton).(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

Baela Targaryen enjoyed moonlight swims in the powerful river.(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1) [Lady Buckler](wiki:Lady_Buckler) and her daughters drowned en route to the [Maiden's Day Cattle Show](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Cattle_Show) of [133 AC](wiki:133_AC) when their boat sank in the Blackwater.(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

Prior to the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen) granted land near the Blackwater to his half-brother, [Daemon Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre).(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)

During the terrible summer drought of [211 AC](wiki:211_AC) the Blackwater Rush and the [Mander](wiki:Mander) were running low,(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0) while the river froze at the end of [281 AC](wiki:281_AC), the [year of the false spring](wiki:Year_of_the_false_spring).(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)

Displeased with King's Landing in [265 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) once considered building a new city of [marble](wiki:Marble) on the southern shore of the river.(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-1)

## Culture

[Trout](wiki:Trout)(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) and river [pike](wiki:Pike)(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) are fished from the Blackwater.

Swimmers have been known to drown in the Blackwater's powerful current.(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) According to King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), women swim naked in the river.(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) find sleeping places along the riverside while trying to deal with the heat at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Ragot25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)

A hundred pavilions are raised alongside the Blackwater Rush for the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) at the capital.(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1)

### A Clash of Kings

The party of [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) crosses the Blackwater while en route from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) to [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge).(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0)

After being stopped at the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) in the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords), Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) joins Lord [Mathis Rowan](wiki:Mathis_Rowan) and Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) near the headwaters of the Blackwater. At [Tumbler's Falls](wiki:Tumbler's_Falls) they meet Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), who has brought barges and a large host with him. From there they float down the Blackwater and land a half day's ride outside of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), many of the ships of [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and his uncle [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) are set ablaze by [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) after a great chain divides the river from [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay).(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-3) The Rush appears to be on fire,(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Racok59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0) and King Joffrey has [Antler Men](wiki:Antler_Men) flung into the river.(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Racok60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0) [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne) knocks the armored Ser [Mandon Moore](wiki:Mandon_Moore) into the water to save Tyrion.(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Racok61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0) The Lannisters and Tyrells attack Stannis's army in the rear and force him to retreat.(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2)

### A Storm of Swords

While the vicinity of [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept) has suffered greatly from the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), the harvest has not yet been burned south of the river and trading is still possible.(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0)

In the aftermath of the Battle of the Blackwater, *[Ragged Jenna](wiki:Ragged_Jenna)* and *[Laughing Lord](wiki:Laughing_Lord)* become pirates on the river.(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0) [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) is rewarded for his role in the battle by being [knighted](wiki:Knight) as Ser Bronn of the Blackwater.(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0)

Before the port of King's Landing can be reopened, the Blackwater must be dredged of sunken ships. Three out of every four quays are damaged.(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0) A flat-bottomed ferry is one of the few ships crossing the river at the capital.(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) desires to have a new palace built across the Blackwater from King's Landing once the war has ended.(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0) She approves the construction of [new dromonds](wiki:Cersei's_dromonds) on the river.(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-0)

The entourage of Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) enjoys sailing up and down the Blackwater Rush and crossing the river to go hawking.(wiki:Blackwater_Rush.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-1)
