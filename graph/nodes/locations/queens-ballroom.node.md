## Appearances & Description

The Queen's Ballroom, which can seat a hundred, is much smaller than the [Great Hall](wiki:Great_Hall_(Red_Keep)) and the [Small Hall](wiki:Small_Hall).(wiki:Queen's_Ballroom.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Its walls are paneled in richly carved wood, and every wall sconce has beaten [silver](wiki:Silver) mirrors which reflect torchlight. Musicians can perform in the gallery above the floor, which is covered with sweet-smelling rushes. Arched windows on the south wall can be covered with thick velvet draperies. The ballroom has long trestle tables and a dais.(wiki:Queen's_Ballroom.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), highborn women of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) and some older men and young boys seek refuge in the Queen's Ballroom. [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) accompanies Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) to the hall.(wiki:Queen's_Ballroom.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Queen's_Ballroom.cite_ref-Racok60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Maester [Ballabar](wiki:Ballabar) treats [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), wounded during the battle, in a chamber over the Queen's Ballroom. [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne), Tyrion's squire, is reluctant to enter the [sickroom](wiki:Sickroom).(wiki:Queen's_Ballroom.cite_ref-Racok67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

On the wedding day of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), breakfast is held in the Queen's Ballroom, and guests from the [Reach](wiki:Reach) give gifts to the young king.(wiki:Queen's_Ballroom.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
