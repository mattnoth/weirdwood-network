## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

The forest takes its name from the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood), in which the army of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) must whisper as they wait to ambush Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

While journeying north from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) to the [Twins](wiki:Twins) for the wedding of Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) and [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey), Robb's army passes again through the Whispering Wood. Most of the leaves have changed from green to dull gold, brown, and red, but spruce and soldier pines still show green. [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) sees remnants of the battle from early in the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), including bones, weapons, armor, and stone cairns.(wiki:Whispering_Wood.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
