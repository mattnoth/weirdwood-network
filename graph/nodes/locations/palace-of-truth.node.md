### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), disguised as the Braavos street urchin and fish peddler Cat of the Canals, travels south on the [Long Canal](wiki:Long_Canal) to the fishmarket in southern Braavos. [Brusco](wiki:Brusco)'s boat passes beneath the Palace of Truth on the way.(wiki:Palace_of_Truth.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Cat sells shellfish on the steps of the Palace of Truth. When another peddler tries to run her off, she upends his oyster [cart](wiki:Cart) and flees.(wiki:Palace_of_Truth.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]
