## Origins

After the death of [Scarb](wiki:Scarb), [Ben Plumm](wiki:Ben_Plumm) fled [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) by escaping through the sewers which empty into the Skahazadhan.(wiki:Skahazadhan.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

To resolve the [siege of Meereen](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) sends a group into the [the city](wiki:Meereen) by infiltrating the sewers.(wiki:Skahazadhan.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

During the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen), galleys from [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) and [New Ghis](wiki:New_Ghis) and a carrack from [Tolos](wiki:Tolos) seal off the Skahazadhan,(wiki:Skahazadhan.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and two [iron legions](wiki:Iron_legions) are to land north of the river to block Meereenese access to the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea).(wiki:Skahazadhan.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
The [Wise Masters](wiki:Wise_Masters) of [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) set up [slave](wiki:Slave) pens and an auction block on the southern shore of the river, where it meets [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay).(wiki:Skahazadhan.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
After Daenerys, now [Queen of Meereen](wiki:Queen_of_Meereen), disappears from [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit), her [bloodriders](wiki:Bloodrider) are sent in search of her north of the Skahazadhan.(wiki:Skahazadhan.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) King [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) styles himself [Master of the Skahazadhan](wiki:Master_of_the_Skahazadhan).(wiki:Skahazadhan.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) declines the ancient office of [Warden of the River](wiki:Warden_of_the_River).(wiki:Skahazadhan.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
