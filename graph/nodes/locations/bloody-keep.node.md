## Appearances & Description

Located on its own island, the Bloody Keep is one of the larger buildings of [Pyke](wiki:Pyke). Although its halls are larger and better furnished than those of the [Sea Tower](wiki:Sea_Tower), it is still cold and damp.(wiki:Bloody_Keep.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The gloomy suite which inspires the Bloody Keep's name has high ceilings.(wiki:Bloody_Keep.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) A covered stone walkway connects the Bloody Keep and the [Great Keep](wiki:Great_Keep_of_Pyke).(wiki:Bloody_Keep.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Origins

King [Qhored I Hoare](wiki:Qhored_I_Hoare) defeated King [Bernarr II Justman](wiki:Bernarr_II_Justman) in battle and took his three sons back to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) as hostages. When Bernarr was slow in paying tribute three years later, Qhored hacked the boys to pieces in their beds at [Pyke](wiki:Pyke) and cut their hearts out.(wiki:Bloody_Keep.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Bloody_Keep.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The keep where the boys were slaughtered has since been known as the Bloody Keep.(wiki:Bloody_Keep.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
