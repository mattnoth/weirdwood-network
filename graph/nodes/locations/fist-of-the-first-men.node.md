## Identity

## Edges

## Origins

The ancient [ringfort](wiki:Ringfort) on the Fist was built by the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) during the [Dawn Age](wiki:Dawn_Age). Some of its stone ringwall still remains.(wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men.cite_ref-Racok34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

The [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) make their camp at the Fist of the First Men while searching for the [wildling](wiki:Wildling) masses during the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging). From the Fist, they hope to stop any march on the [Wall](wiki:Wall). When they arrive at the top of the hill, they have to circle some distance west before they find a gap in the ringwall large enough to admit the horses. The Watch discovers the site is in poor repair. [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) has the brothers fill breaches in the low ringwall with timber, and protect them with rows of stakes. They booby-trap the slopes with caltrops.(wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men.cite_ref-Racok34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow)'s [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), [Ghost](wiki:Ghost), is unnerved by the ringfort, and refuses to enter it. Jon joins [Dywen](wiki:Dywen), [Grenn](wiki:Grenn), [Dolorous Edd](wiki:Eddison_Tollett), and [Hake](wiki:Hake) for dinner. Dywen states there is a smell he does not like, a smell of cold. Jon hears the howling of [wolves](wiki:Wolves), and then Ghost appears, seeming preoccupied and sniffing the air. Jon is alarmed, since Ghost had known when [Othor](wiki:Othor) and [Jafer Flowers](wiki:Jafer_Flowers) rose as [wights](wiki:Wight), and Jon recalls they smelled of cold and death. Ghost starts to leave but looks back, so Jon follows, leaving the protection of the ringwall. Ghost stops at the brook, and Jon follows him, circling around the Fist. When he catches up with Ghost again, the direwolf is digging at a grave that has been dug only recently. Jon expects to find a body, but instead finds a bundle two feet across. Inside the bundle, made from a Night's Watch black cloak, Jon finds [dragonglass](wiki:Dragonglass) weapons and an [old, cracked warhorn](wiki:Old_warhorn).(wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men.cite_ref-Racok34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

[Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand) arrives at the Fist, leading one hundred men from the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower).(wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Qhorin tells Jeor Mormont the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) plan to breach the Wall, not climb it or burrow beneath it. Qhorin says that [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) is seeking something in the high, cold places of the [Frostfangs](wiki:Frostfangs), some power or artifact. Qhorin advises the Lord Commander to send scouts into the mountains, and Mormont agrees. [Jarmen Buckwell](wiki:Jarmen_Buckwell) will take four men to climb the [Giant's Stair](wiki:Giant's_Stair), [Thoren Smallwood](wiki:Thoren_Smallwood) will lead a party to probe the [Milkwater](wiki:Milkwater), while Qhorin himself will lead a third party into the [Skirling Pass](wiki:Skirling_Pass). Qhorin immediately chooses Jon Snow as one of his four men.(wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Storm of Swords

In spite of the Watch's best efforts to mount a defense, they are attacked and easily swept by [wights](wiki:Wights) during the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist).(wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men.cite_ref-Rasos18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

In the aftermath of the battle, [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) and [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) discover the bodies of at least a hundred horses, but none of men.(wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
