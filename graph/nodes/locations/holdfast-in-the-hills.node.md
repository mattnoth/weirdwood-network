## Appearances & Description

The [holdfast](wiki:Holdfast) is a small fortification with a gate and an [ironwood](wiki:Ironwood) stump in the center of its square.(wiki:Holdfast_in_the_hills.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Captured in the [north](wiki:North) after deserting the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), [Gared](wiki:Gared) is taken outside the small holdfast and bound hand and foot to its wall. [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell) and [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), rides to the holdfast from [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) to administer the [king's justice](wiki:King's_justice_(concept)). After speaking with the condemned man, Lord Stark beheads him with [Ice](wiki:Ice) at the [ironwood](wiki:Ironwood) stump. [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy), Eddard's ward, then kicks Gared's head away. While riding back to Winterfell, Eddard's party finds six [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) pups near a bridge across a river.(wiki:Holdfast_in_the_hills.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
