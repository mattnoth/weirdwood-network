## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) leads the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) by a small group of [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn).(wiki:Hunter's_Gate.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [Drennan](wiki:Drennan) and [Squint](wiki:Squint) stand guard at the Hunter's Gate when [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) and his companions escape the castle. [Osha](wiki:Osha) kills Drennan in the gatehouse, and the direwolves [Summer](wiki:Summer) and [Shaggydog](wiki:Shaggydog) kill Squint on the wallwalk; the latter's body is then found in the moat.(wiki:Hunter's_Gate.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Theon assembles a search party by the Hunter's Gate for an unsuccessful search in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood).(wiki:Hunter's_Gate.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
Bran and his companions hide in the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell) and thus survive the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell). Bran, [Hodor](wiki:Hodor), and [Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed) and [Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed) later depart Winterfell through the Hunter's Gate. Its iron portcullis has been warped by heat so it cannot be raised more than a foot, so the travelers have to squeeze beneath its spikes.(wiki:Hunter's_Gate.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After observing that [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) has slowed during his [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell), two scouts of Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) return to the castle through the Hunter's Gate.(wiki:Hunter's_Gate.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Heavy snowfall accumulates at the Hunter's Gate, and scouts sent through the gate do not return.(wiki:Hunter's_Gate.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) hears drumming from the wolfswood beyond the Hunter's Gate.(wiki:Hunter's_Gate.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
