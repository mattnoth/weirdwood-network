## Origins

After dissolving his marriage with his sister-wife, [Daena Targaryen](wiki:Daena_Targaryen), King [Baelor the Blessed](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen) confined Daena and her younger sisters, [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon_III)) and [Elaena](wiki:Elaena_Targaryen), into their own "Court of Beauty" to avoid them tempting him with carnal thoughts.(wiki:Maidenvault.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

The sisters were comfortably confined(wiki:Maidenvault.cite_ref-3) in what has become known as the Maidenvault.(wiki:Maidenvault.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) On one occasion Elaena cut her beautiful hair in an entreaty to be freed, hoping that she would now be considered ugly.(wiki:Maidenvault.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Daena escaped three times while dressed as a servant or [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk),(wiki:Maidenvault.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) and she became pregnant with a child, [Daemon Waters](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre), who was secretly fathered by her cousin, Prince [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen).(wiki:Maidenvault.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [Barba Bracken](wiki:Barba_Bracken) was one of the noble companions for the sisters during their decade of confinement.(wiki:Maidenvault.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Unlike her sisters, Rhaena did not mind the confinement.(wiki:Maidenvault.cite_ref-5) The princesses were released from the Maidenvault after Baelor's death.(wiki:Maidenvault.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_viserys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Appearances & Description

Located behind the [royal sept](wiki:Royal_sept), the Maidenvault is a long keep with a [slate](wiki:Slate) roof. It has tall carved doors.(wiki:Maidenvault.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

After the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) and his entourage stay in the Maidenvault. [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) has supper with Mace's mother, [Olenna Tyrell](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell), and daughter, [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Maidenvault.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Feast for Crows

Margaery stays in the Maidenvault even after her wedding to King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Maidenvault.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) The [Blue Bard](wiki:Blue_Bard) performs for the young queen in the keep.(wiki:Maidenvault.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Margaery and her cousins—[Megga](wiki:Megga_Tyrell), [Alla](wiki:Alla_Tyrell), and [Elinor](wiki:Elinor_Tyrell)—attempt to learn a new game from [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), presumably *[cyvasse](wiki:Cyvasse)*.(wiki:Maidenvault.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
