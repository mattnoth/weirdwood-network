## Origins

A [Pentoshi](wiki:Pentos) legend says that the hero [Hukko](wiki:Hukko) killed seven [swan maidens](wiki:Swan_maidens), mythical [maidens](wiki:Maiden) that lured travelers in the Velvet Hills to their deaths.(wiki:Velvet_Hills.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_arrival_of_the_andals.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Ser [Willam the Wasp](wiki:Willam_the_Wasp) and his party searched for [Balerion](wiki:Balerion) in the Velvet Hills, but they were slain by outlaws.(wiki:Velvet_Hills.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
