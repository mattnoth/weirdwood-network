## Origins

According to [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) have crossed the Bay of Seals to raid the lands of [House Umber](wiki:House_Umber) since the [Dawn of Days](wiki:Dawn_of_Days).(wiki:Bay_of_Seals.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
In the [Feast of Skane](wiki:Feast_of_Skane), stoneborn from [Skagos](wiki:Skagos) raided [Skane](wiki:Skane), allegedly rendering the isle uninhabited.(wiki:Bay_of_Seals.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)(wiki:Bay_of_Seals.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_stoneborn_of_skagos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Brandon IX Stark](wiki:Brandon_IX_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), stopped Skagosi from raiding the mainland by destroying their ships and denying them the sea.(wiki:Bay_of_Seals.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_stoneborn_of_skagos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
Galleys from [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea) caught [Cobblecat](wiki:Cobblecat) in the Bay of Seals.(wiki:Bay_of_Seals.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Free folk](wiki:Free_folk) in little boats cross the Bay of Seals and wash up on [Umber](wiki:House_Umber) lands, causing [Hother Umber](wiki:Hother_Umber) to demand that a fleet be built to stop the wildlings.(wiki:Bay_of_Seals.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) sails from [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) to [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea).(wiki:Bay_of_Seals.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), [Gilly](wiki:Gilly), and Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sail on [Blackbird](wiki:Blackbird) from Eastwatch across the Bay of Seals to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Bay_of_Seals.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When the fleet of [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan) sails south from Eastwatch, [Oledo](wiki:Oledo) and [Old Mother's Son](wiki:Old_Mother's_Son) are driven onto [Skagos](wiki:Skagos).(wiki:Bay_of_Seals.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
A savage storm rages on the Bay of Seals.(wiki:Bay_of_Seals.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
