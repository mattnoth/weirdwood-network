## Origins

The Lands of the Long Summer were once part of the fabled [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold).(wiki:Lands_of_the_Long_Summer.cite_ref-Rawoiaflands_of_the_long_summer.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) According to a semi-canon source, there were forty families of [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlords) who stayed close to the Freehold and the Lands of the Long Summer.(wiki:Lands_of_the_Long_Summer.cite_ref-3)
All [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities), except for [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), looked to the Lands of the Long Summer for leadership in times of crisis.(wiki:Lands_of_the_Long_Summer.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) In the centuries after the fall of the [Old Empire of Ghis](wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis), the adventurers, exiles and traders from the Freehold of Valyria began to expand beyond the Lands of the Long Summer, towards the lands of the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar).(wiki:Lands_of_the_Long_Summer.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Some Valyrians of the Freehold, like Lord [Aenar Targaryen](wiki:Aenar_Targaryen), had holdings in the Lands of the Long Summer. When [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) moved to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)) in [114 BC](wiki:Years_before_Aegon's_Conquest), Lord Aenar sold his holdings.(wiki:Lands_of_the_Long_Summer.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
The Lands of the Long Summer were once the most fertile in all the world.(wiki:Lands_of_the_Long_Summer.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_doom_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) However, in [102 BC](wiki:Years_before_Aegon's_Conquest), the [Doom](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria) fell on the Lands of the Long Summer,(wiki:Lands_of_the_Long_Summer.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Lands_of_the_Long_Summer.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_volantis.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) during which they became scorched, drowned and blighted.(wiki:Lands_of_the_Long_Summer.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Lands_of_the_Long_Summer.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_doom_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Now, the way across these once so fertile lands is considered to be "long and grueling".(wiki:Lands_of_the_Long_Summer.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
