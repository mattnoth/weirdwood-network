## Origins

[Chett](wiki:Chett), a native of [Hag's Mire](wiki:Hag's_Mire), was caught near Sevenstreams after killing [Bessa](wiki:Bessa_(Hag's_Mire)). After being judged by [Walder Rivers](wiki:Walder_Rivers), Chett was taken by [Yoren](wiki:Yoren) to the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
