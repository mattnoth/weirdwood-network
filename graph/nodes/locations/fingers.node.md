## Origins

### First Men and Andals[ ]

Thousands of years ago, the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) was inhabited by petty kings of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men). Two such monarchs, [Dywen Shell](wiki:Dywen_Shell) and [Jon Brightstone](wiki:Jon_Brightstone), each claimed to be [King of the Fingers](wiki:King_of_the_Fingers). They invited [Andals](wiki:Andals) to cross the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) to aid them against their rivals,(wiki:Fingers.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) so the Fingers were where Andals first arrived when they [invaded Westeros](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals).(wiki:Fingers.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_arrival_of_the_andals.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) They chiseled the seven-pointed star of the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) onto boulders to mark where they landed.(wiki:Fingers.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Dywen and Jon both were slain when Andals turned against them. Taking Jon's daughter as his wife and Dywen's wife as his bedwarmer, the Andal [Corwyn Corbray](wiki:Corwyn_Corbray_(lord)) proclaimed himself [Lord of the Five Fingers](wiki:Lord_of_the_Five_Fingers).(wiki:Fingers.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
[Robar II Royce](wiki:Robar_II_Royce), a [Bronze King](wiki:Bronze_King), was elevated by the First Men to [High King of the Vale, the Fingers, and the Mountains of the Moon](wiki:High_King_of_the_Vale,_the_Fingers,_and_the_Mountains_of_the_Moon), and Robar slew [Qyle Corbray](wiki:Qyle_Corbray), King of the Fingers. Robar eventually fell to [Artys I Arryn](wiki:Artys_I_Arryn) in the [Battle of the Seven Stars](wiki:Battle_of_the_Seven_Stars).(wiki:Fingers.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

### Kings of Mountain and Vale[ ]

The [Kings of Mountain and Vale](wiki:Kings_of_Mountain_and_Vale) from [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) constructed numerous watchtowers with beacons to defend the Fingers from raiders.(wiki:Fingers.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
King [Theon Stark](wiki:Theon_Stark), the Hungry Wolf, once landed an army on the Fingers, but the [Kings of Winter](wiki:Kings_of_Winter) were not able to hold onto the conquest.(wiki:Fingers.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) During the [War Across the Water](wiki:War_Across_the_Water), the [Starks](wiki:House_Stark) landed on the Fingers three times.(wiki:Fingers.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

### Targaryen Era[ ]

During her search for the missing [Aerea Targaryen](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen), [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) and her dragon, [Dreamfyre](wiki:Dreamfyre), were seen flying over the Fingers.(wiki:Fingers.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Prince [Jacaerys Velaryon](wiki:Jacaerys_Velaryon) flew [Vermax](wiki:Vermax) above the Fingers to [Sisterton](wiki:Sisterton) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Fingers.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) When the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) broke out into civil war after the passing of Lady [Jeyne Arryn](wiki:Jeyne_Arryn), the lords of the Fingers supported Ser [Arnold Arryn](wiki:Arnold_Arryn).(wiki:Fingers.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
After [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) was wounded in a duel with [Brandon Stark](wiki:Brandon_Stark) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully) sent Petyr to heal at the Fingers once he was strong enough to move.(wiki:Fingers.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
At the start of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) crossed the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon) and found a fisherman on the Fingers to sail him across the [Bite](wiki:Bite).(wiki:Fingers.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Two [peasant](wiki:Peasant) boys from the Fingers, accused of rape, are among [Yoren](wiki:Yoren)'s band of recruits for the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Fingers.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) [Hedge knights](wiki:Hedge_knight) from the Fingers participate in [the Hand's tourney](wiki:The_Hand's_tourney).(wiki:Fingers.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) is sent by King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) with a chest of letters to spread word of King [Joffrey I Baratheon's](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) illegitimacy. His route is to sail to the Fingers and other harbors along the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea).(wiki:Fingers.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Varys](wiki:Varys) reports to the [small council](wiki:Small_council) that a [kraken](wiki:Kraken) was seen off the Fingers pulling under an [Ibbenense](wiki:Ibben) whaler.(wiki:Fingers.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) and [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) sail aboard the [Merling King](wiki:Merling_King) from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to [his family's tower](wiki:Drearfort) in the Fingers, where Petyr weds the widowed Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn).(wiki:Fingers.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) recalls he once heard of a [dwarf](wiki:Dwarf) [maester](wiki:Maester) who served on the Fingers.(wiki:Fingers.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
