## Origins

According to the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel), the gloomy Forest of Qohor has never been fully mapped.(wiki:Forest_of_Qohor.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_qohor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) The earliest known settlement at what is now [Qohor](wiki:Qohor) was a lumber camp.(wiki:Forest_of_Qohor.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_qohor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

When [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) is newly wedded, Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo)'s [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) rides through the Forest of Qohor for half a [moon](wiki:Moon). The animals all flee at the approach of the khalasar and Daenerys has no glimpse of them.(wiki:Forest_of_Qohor.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The khalasar of Khal [Zekko](wiki:Zekko) is said to be moving through the Forest of Qohor.(wiki:Forest_of_Qohor.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
