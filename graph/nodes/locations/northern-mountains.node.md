## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) executes [Gared](wiki:Gared) at a [holdfast in the hills](wiki:Holdfast_in_the_hills) near [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Northern_mountains.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
The party of [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) travels on the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad) from Winterfell to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black). West of the road they see grey and rugged flint hills guarded with tall watchtowers. Five days north of Winterfell, they see jagged, snow-covered mountains, blue and grey in color, forming a wall to the west.(wiki:Northern_mountains.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Rather than take the kingsroad to the [Wall](wiki:Wall), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) and his companions travel through the northern mountains, which have no roads among their twisted valleys. They pass a granite upthrust as large as Winterfell. Bran recalls that [his great-grandmother](wiki:Arya_Flint) was a [Flint of the mountains](wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains), and that [Old Nan](wiki:Old_Nan) used to claim that it was her blood that gave Bran his interest in climbing. Bran explains to his companions that the clansmen know of their presence, but will not bother them unless they try to take their goats or [horses](wiki:Horse). The only person they meet is [a Liddle](wiki:House_Liddle) taking shelter from freezing rain in a shallow cave. The Liddle warns them about [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) and [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton) on the kingsroad, and he leaves them with sausage and oatcakes.(wiki:Northern_mountains.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-8)
Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sends [ravens](wiki:Raven) to the mountain fastnesses of the clans to request aid against [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder), the [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall), and his [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings).(wiki:Northern_mountains.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Following the advice of [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), who provides sure-footed [garrons](wiki:Garron),(wiki:Northern_mountains.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6) [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) travels to the mountains, gains the northern mountain clans for his cause, and [frees Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte) in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) from the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) of [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy).(wiki:Northern_mountains.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
[Old Flint](wiki:Torghen_Flint) and [the Norrey](wiki:Brandon_Norrey) tell Jon they do not want [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) to settle on their lands.(wiki:Northern_mountains.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)
