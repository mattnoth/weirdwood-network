## Origins

According to legend, [Rose of Red Lake](wiki:Rose_of_Red_Lake) was a daughter of [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand) and the founder of [House Crane](wiki:House_Crane). [Brandon of the Bloody Blade](wiki:Brandon_of_the_Bloody_Blade), one of Garth's sons, is said to have slain so many [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) that what had been called Blue Lake was renamed Red Lake because of their blood.(wiki:Red_Lake.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Red Lake was part of the [Reach proper](wiki:Reach_proper) of old.(wiki:Red_Lake.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The lake was added to the [Kingdom of the Reach](wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Reach) by [Garth III Gardener](wiki:Garth_III_Gardener) through pacts of friendship and mutual defense.(wiki:Red_Lake.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_the_gardener_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
[Lancel IV Lannister](wiki:Lancel_IV_Lannister), [King of the Rock](wiki:King_of_the_Rock), was killed in battle at Red Lake(wiki:Red_Lake.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) by Ser [Wilbert Osgrey](wiki:Wilbert_Osgrey), [Marshall of the Northmarch](wiki:Marshall_of_the_Northmarch).(wiki:Red_Lake.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Following [Second Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton) in the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), [Silverwing](wiki:Silverwing) became wild and the [dragon](wiki:Dragon) made a lair on a small, stony isle in the lake.(wiki:Red_Lake.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Red_Lake.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) When arranging a [royal progress](wiki:Royal_progress) for King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) after the war, Lord [Torrhen Manderly](wiki:Torrhen_Manderly) planned for the dragon-hating king to avoid Red Lake.(wiki:Red_Lake.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
