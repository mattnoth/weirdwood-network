## Origins

Most of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) who settled in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) after crossing the [Arm of Dorne](wiki:Arm_of_Dorne) settled along the banks of the Greenblood, digging ditches and canals.(wiki:Greenblood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) A dozen houses of the Greenblood—including Houses [Briar](wiki:House_Briar), [Brook](wiki:House_Brook), [Brownhill](wiki:House_Brownhill), [Holt](wiki:House_Holt_(Dorne)), [Lake](wiki:House_Lake_(Dorne)), [Shell](wiki:House_Shell_(Dorne)), and [Wade](wiki:House_Wade)—chose a [High King of Dorne](wiki:High_King_of_Dorne) from amongst their number. Three of the houses were destroyed after a generation of warfare following a disputed election, however.(wiki:Greenblood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_kingdoms_of_the_first_men.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The remaining houses were conquered during the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals), with the Shells and Wades defeated by [House Martell](wiki:House_Martell).(wiki:Greenblood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_the_andals_arrive.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[House Yronwood](wiki:House_Yronwood)'s dominion once reached from the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) to the headwaters of the Greenblood.(wiki:Greenblood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_kingdoms_of_the_first_men.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) The Yronwoods submitted to the Martells during [Nymeria's War](wiki:Nymeria's_War), however.(wiki:Greenblood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_the_coming_of_the_rhoynar.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
[Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) flew [Meraxes](wiki:Meraxes) over the Greenblood and the Planky Town when she traveled to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) to treat with [Meria Martell](wiki:Meria_Martell) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Greenblood.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Rhaenys burned the Planky Town at the start of the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War) in [4 AC](wiki:4_AC), choking the mouth of the river. She then took [Lemonwood](wiki:Lemonwood), [Spottswood](wiki:Spottswood), and [Stinkwater](wiki:Stinkwater).(wiki:Greenblood.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) In reaction to the [Defenestration of Sunspear](wiki:Defenestration_of_Sunspear), Lord [Harlan Tyrell](wiki:Harlan_Tyrell) planned to march from the [Hellholt](wiki:Hellholt) to [Vaith](wiki:Vaith) and then follow the rivers to Sunspear. His army disappeared in the deep sands of Dorne, however.(wiki:Greenblood.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
During the [conquest of Dorne](wiki:Conquest_of_Dorne), Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) broke the Planky Town and sailed up the Greenblood.(wiki:Greenblood.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Alyn Oakenfist returned again in [160 AC](wiki:160_AC).(wiki:Greenblood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) and Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) took a poleboat down the Greenblood to the Planky Town when they visited Dorne.(wiki:Greenblood.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
The [orphans of the Greenblood](wiki:Orphans_of_the_Greenblood) spread the game of [cyvasse](wiki:Cyvasse) up and down the river.(wiki:Greenblood.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser [Gascoyne of the Greenblood](wiki:Gascoyne_of_the_Greenblood) is the sworn shield of Prince [Trystane Martell](wiki:Trystane_Martell).(wiki:Greenblood.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)
[Obara Sand](wiki:Obara_Sand) claims that people along the Greenblood are outraged at the death of her father, Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell).(wiki:Greenblood.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
After gathering her conspirators to [Shandystone](wiki:Shandystone), Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) plans to meet [orphans of the Greenblood](wiki:Orphans_of_the_Greenblood) along the river. Once they reach the Greenblood, however, Arianne realizes her plot to crown [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) has been betrayed. Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) is decapitated by [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah), with his head splashing into the river.(wiki:Greenblood.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Whiskerfish](wiki:Whiskerfish) from the Greenblood is served during the feast welcoming Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear).(wiki:Greenblood.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
