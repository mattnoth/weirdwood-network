## Appearances & Description

Ringforts are rough-hewn, unmortared walls(wiki:Ringfort.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) of circular construction; the ancient ringfort atop [Seal Rock](wiki:Seal_Rock) at [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor), for instance, is a circle of weathered stones.(wiki:Ringfort.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) The ringwall at the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men) crowns the top of the steep stony hill.(wiki:Ringfort.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Origins

As the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) spread throughout [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) building farms and villages, they ruled from ringforts from the [Land of Always Winter](wiki:Land_of_Always_Winter) to the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea).(wiki:Ringfort.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_coming_of_the_first_men.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Ringfort.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_age_of_heroes.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men) is said to have been a ringfort built during the [Dawn Age](wiki:Dawn_Age).(wiki:Ringfort.cite_ref-Racok34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Some believe that [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) was once a complex of linked ringforts, though little evidence remains.(wiki:Ringfort.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_winterfell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Old ruins of the First Men are found in the hills and bogs of [Sea Dragon Point](wiki:Sea_Dragon_Point).(wiki:Ringfort.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
[House Casterly](wiki:House_Casterly) built a ringfort atop [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Ringfort.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_casterly_rock.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
The [Bloody Gate](wiki:Bloody_Gate) was once a rough-hewn, unmortared wall like the First Men's ringforts.(wiki:Ringfort.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Instead of ringforts, the First Men eventually began building stout motte-and-bailey forts and then stone castles.(wiki:Ringfort.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Many stout stone-and-timber castles were built during the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to Westeros.(wiki:Ringfort.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_iron_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
The older ringforts have been abandoned(wiki:Ringfort.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) and left in ruins.(wiki:Ringfort.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_storm.27s_end.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) The ringfort at [Seal Rock](wiki:Seal_Rock), for instance, has been abandoned for hundreds of years.(wiki:Ringfort.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

During the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) fortify the ringfort at the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men).(wiki:Ringfort.cite_ref-Racok34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Wights](wiki:Wights) overwhelm the ringwall and defeat the Watch in the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist).(wiki:Ringfort.cite_ref-Rasos18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) fortifies the ruins of the ringfort atop [Seal Rock](wiki:Seal_Rock), placing [scorpions](wiki:Scorpion_(weapon)) and spitfires behind the standing stones and garrisoning it with crossbowmen.(wiki:Ringfort.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
