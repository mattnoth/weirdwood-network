### A Feast for Crows[ ]

The Redwyne Straits are filled with [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) warships preying on defenseless trade vessels since the [Redwyne](wiki:House_Redwyne) fleet is at [Dragonstone](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone). After capturing a [Tyroshi](wiki:Tyrosh) merchantmen in the straits, some ironborn tried to sneak into [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) but were stopped by the [Lady of the Tower](wiki:Lady_of_the_Tower).(wiki:Redwyne_Straits.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
