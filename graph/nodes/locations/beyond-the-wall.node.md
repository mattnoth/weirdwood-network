## Origins

### Older History

[Redwyn](wiki:Redwyn), a [ranger](wiki:Ranger) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), explored from the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) to [Lorn Point](wiki:Lorn_Point) during the reign of [Dorren Stark](wiki:Dorren_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf21.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Another king, [Theon Stark](wiki:Theon_Stark), joined the Night's Watch in a venture north of the [Wall](wiki:Wall) which weakened the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) for a generation.(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf711_16-0)

[Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome) was mysteriously destroyed six hundred years ago.(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### Targaryen Era

During the suppression of the [Night's Watch rebellion of 50 AC](wiki:Night's_Watch_rebellion_of_50_AC), Ser [Raymund Mallery](wiki:Raymund_Mallery) and other rebels fled from [Sable Hall](wiki:Sable_Hall) to the lands north of the [Wall](wiki:Wall). [Walton Stark](wiki:Walton_Stark), [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell), pursued them but was slain by [giants](wiki:Giants) in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest). The rebels were eventually slain by [cannibal](wiki:Cannibalism) wildlings who delivered Raymund's head to [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea).(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

[Lothor Burley](wiki:Lothor_Burley), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), brought Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) atop the Wall in [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) so she could see the haunted forest. The queen attempted to fly [Silverwing](wiki:Silverwing) north of the Wall three times, but the [dragon](wiki:Dragon) refused each time and veered away.(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

Lord Comamnder [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers) vanished during a ranging beyond the Wall in [252 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf415.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### Recent History

[Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) was attacked by a [shadowcat](wiki:Shadowcat) while ranging, and was healed by a wildling woman, a [wisewoman's daughter](wiki:Wisewoman's_daughter). While he recuperated, she mended his torn cloak with swatches of red silk. Upon returning to the Wall, Ser [Denys Mallister](wiki:Denys_Mallister) required Mance to replace his mended cloak with one of uniform black. This infringement of freedom caused Mance to abandon the Shadow Tower(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Racok53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) and live with the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) in the way he wished.(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

Mance spent years gaining gathering various tribes into one host, seeking support from clan mothers and [magnars](wiki:Magnar). He made peace between [Harma Dogshead](wiki:Harma_Dogshead) and the [Lord of Bones](wiki:Lord_of_Bones), the [Hornfoots](wiki:Hornfoots) and the [Nightrunners](wiki:Nightrunners), and the [men of the Frozen Shore](wiki:Men_of_the_Frozen_Shore) and the [ice-river clans](wiki:Ice-river_clans).(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) Opposed by five other would-be [Kings-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:Kings-Beyond-the-Wall), Mance gained the support of [Tormund](wiki:Tormund) of [Ruddy Hall](wiki:Ruddy_Hall) and [Styr](wiki:Styr) of [Thenn](wiki:Thenn_(valley)) and slew the three rivals who refused to submit.(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

Texts about the region include *[History of the Kings-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:History_of_the_Kings-Beyond-the-Wall)* by Maester [Herryk](wiki:Herryk)(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf21.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) and *[Hardhome: An Account of Three Years Spent Beyond-the-Wall among Savages, Raiders, and Woods-witches](wiki:Hardhome:_An_Account_of_Three_Years_Spent_Beyond-the-Wall_among_Savages,_Raiders,_and_Woods-witches)* by Maester [Wyllis](wiki:Wyllis).(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf722_23-0)

It is sometimes suggested that the lords of the [north](wiki:North) be allowed to extend their power beyond the Wall to end the threat of wildling raids.(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf721_24-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser [Waymar Royce](wiki:Waymar_Royce) leads a ranging party in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest), but he is slain by [Others](wiki:Others) and rises as a [wight](wiki:Wight).(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Ragot0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark), the [First Ranger](wiki:First_Ranger), searches for Waymar beyond the Wall, but Benjen also disappears.(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) Ser [Jaremy Rykker](wiki:Jaremy_Rykker) and [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand) search for Benjen to no avail.(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) view the northern lands from atop the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Ragot21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) Jon says his vows for the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) in a grove of [weirwoods](wiki:Weirwood) within the haunted forest.(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

The bodies of [Othor](wiki:Othor) and [Jafer Flowers](wiki:Jafer_Flowers) are found in the haunted forest. When they are brought back to Castle Black, the bodies rise as [wights](wiki:Wights), attacking [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont)(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) and killing Jaremy.(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Jon Snow participates in the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) to investigate the [conflict beyond the Wall](wiki:Conflict_beyond_the_Wall). The Night's Watch discover that [Whitetree](wiki:Whitetree) has been abandoned.(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Racok13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Jon is captured by [Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt) in the [Skirling Pass](wiki:Skirling_Pass) and fights [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand) to the death.(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Racok68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

### A Storm of Swords

[Wights](wiki:Wights) overwhelm the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) in the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist).(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Rasos0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Rasos18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0) After the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep), [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), [Gilly](wiki:Gilly), and other survivors flee south for the Wall.(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)

[Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder), the [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall), leads the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) into attacking the gate through the Wall at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black). They are defeated by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) in the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), however.(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

With Mance a captive of Stannis, the free folk host is broken, with some submitting to Stannis and the Night's Watch and others scattering.(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Radwd0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Jon Snow, now [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), frees [Val](wiki:Val) so she can find [Tormund](wiki:Tormund) in the wilderness and negotiate the passage of his followers through the Wall.(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0) Jon sends [Cotter Pyke](wiki:Cotter_Pyke) with ships to rescue free folk at [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome), but the free folk do not trust the Night's Watch and Maester [Harmune](wiki:Harmune) writes of dead things in the water.(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)

[Coldhands](wiki:Coldhands) leads [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark), [Hodor](wiki:Hodor), [Meera Reed](wiki:Meera_Reed), and [Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed) through the haunted forest to the [cave of the three-eyed crow](wiki:Cave_of_the_three-eyed_crow).(wiki:Beyond_the_Wall.cite_ref-Radwd13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)
