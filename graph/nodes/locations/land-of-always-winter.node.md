## Origins

According to Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel), the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) and [giants](wiki:Giants) lived in the Land of Always Winter during the [Dawn Age](wiki:Dawn_Age).(wiki:Land_of_Always_Winter.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_the_dawn_age.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) In the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes) following the [Pact](wiki:Pact), [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) was settled by petty kingdoms of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) from the Land of Always Winter to the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea).(wiki:Land_of_Always_Winter.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_the_age_of_heroes.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) According to legend, the [Others](wiki:Others) came from the Land of Always Winter during the [Long Night](wiki:Long_Night).(wiki:Land_of_Always_Winter.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_long_night.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

While comatose, [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) has a vision in which he looks past the [Wall](wiki:Wall) far to the north. After passing the enormous [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest) and then dead plains without vegetation, he becomes frightened after looking deep into the [heart of winter](wiki:Heart_of_winter).(wiki:Land_of_Always_Winter.cite_ref-Ragot17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
