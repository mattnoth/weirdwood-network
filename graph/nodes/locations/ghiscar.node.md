## Origins

Ghiscar was the foundation of the [Old Empire of Ghis](wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis). The [lockstep legions](wiki:Lockstep_legions) of [Old Ghis](wiki:Old_Ghis) allowed the Ghiscari to conquer and [enslave](wiki:Slavery) nearby nations and colonize their surroundings.(wiki:Ghiscar.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_the_rise_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Ghiscar was conquered by the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) during five [Ghiscari wars](wiki:Ghiscari_wars), however. The Ghiscari were reduced to [High Valyrian](wiki:High_Valyrian)-speaking slaves of the Freehold. The Ghiscari regained control of the slave trade after the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria).(wiki:Ghiscar.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_the_rise_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
