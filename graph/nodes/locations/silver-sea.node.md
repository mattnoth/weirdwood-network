## Origins

The Silver Sea of antiquity was ruled by the legendary [Fisher Queens](wiki:Fisher_Queens), and some theories claim the [Andals](wiki:Andals) originated in the fertile land south of the sea. The [Kingdom of Sarnor](wiki:Kingdom_of_Sarnor) later included the three great lakes of the former Silver Sea. Nearby cities of the [Tall Men](wiki:Tall_Men) included [Gornath by the Lake](wiki:Gornath) and [Sallosh by the Silver Shore](wiki:Sallosh), but they were sacked by the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) during the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood).(wiki:Silver_Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
