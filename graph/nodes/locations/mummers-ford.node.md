## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

The town is destroyed at the beginning of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane)'s raiders from the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands), who have already attacked [Sherrer](wiki:Sherrer). All of the inhabitants are killed.(wiki:Mummer's_Ford.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Ser [Karyl Vance](wiki:Karyl_Vance), Ser [Raymun Darry](wiki:Raymun_Darry), and Ser [Marq Piper](wiki:Marq_Piper) ride to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to inform Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:Mummer's_Ford.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Eddard sends Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) to arrest Gregor, but Beric's detachment is ambushed by the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) in a [battle at the Mummer's Ford](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford).(wiki:Mummer's_Ford.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
