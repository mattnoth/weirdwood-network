## Origins

Princess [Viserra Targaryen](wiki:Viserra_Targaryen) visited the inns of Eel Alley and [River Row](wiki:River_Row) with her companions for "one last night of laughter" before being sent to [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) to marry old Lord [Theomore Manderly](wiki:Theomore_Manderly). She died that night in a drunken horse race in an alley at the foot of [Aegon's High Hill](wiki:Aegon's_High_Hill), however.(wiki:Eel_Alley.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
[Tom Tanglebeard](wiki:Tom_Tanglebeard) was in a tavern of Eel Alley when agents of the conspirators who killed King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) found him. They drowned him in a cask of ale.(wiki:Eel_Alley.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) stays at an [old rambling place](wiki:Old_inn_on_Eel_Alley) on Eel Alley suggested by Captain [Moreo Tumitis](wiki:Moreo_Tumitis).(wiki:Eel_Alley.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) recalls the time he spent the night at an old inn on Eel Alley with his sister [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) when he visited her after the defeat of the [Kingswood Brotherhood](wiki:Kingswood_Brotherhood).(wiki:Eel_Alley.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Cersei tries to influence Jaime to become King [Tommen](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon)'s [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) by using their memory of the night spent together in the inn years ago. She fails, however, and Jaime rejects her.(wiki:Eel_Alley.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
