## Origins

The origins of the ancient city of [Yeen](wiki:Yeen) are an enigma, the greatest one in Sothoryos, as it is a ruin "older than time". It is built of the same [black stone](wiki:Black_stone) as the [Toad Stone](wiki:Toad_Stone) in the [Isle of Toads](wiki:Isle_of_Toads), in massive blocks, and though it has been abandoned for thousands of years, the surrounding jungle has barely touched it. Every attempt to rebuild or resettle Yeen has ended in horror.(wiki:Sothoryos.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf834_3-11)(wiki:Sothoryos.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf833_7-1)
The [Old Empire of Ghis](wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis) established outposts on the northern shores of the continent. They raised the walled city of [Zamettar](wiki:Zamettar) at the mouth of the [Zamoyos](wiki:Zamoyos), and the grim penal colony of [Gorosh](wiki:Gorosh) on [Wyvern Point](wiki:Wyvern_Point).(wiki:Sothoryos.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf834_3-12) The [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) established three colonies on [Basilisk Point](wiki:Basilisk_Point). The first was destroyed by [Brindled Men](wiki:Brindled_Men), the second was lost to plague, and the third was abandoned when the [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlords) took Zamettar during the [Fourth Ghiscari War](wiki:Fourth_Ghiscari_War).(wiki:Sothoryos.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf834_3-13)
A large convoy of slave ships sailing from Valyria to a colony in Sothoryos rose in rebellion, and the free slaves instead sailed north to found [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Sothoryos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_braavos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
During their [their migration](wiki:Ten_thousand_ships) from the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne), Princess [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria)'s [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar) settled on Sothoryos, mainly on Zamettar (abandoned for a thousand years), established towns on Basilisk Point, and even settled on the ruins of Yeen. However, weather, disease, parasites, and predators took their toll on them. Two towns on Basilisk Point were raided, their populations carried off in chains or put to the sword, whilst those in Yeen had to face attacks from "[brindled ghouls](wiki:Sothoryi)" from the jungle's depths. The Rhoynar struggled to survive for more than a year, until a boat sent from Zamettar to Yeen found that all of its inhabitants - men, women, and children - had vanished overnight. Afterward, the Rhoynar abandoned Sothoryos entirely.(wiki:Sothoryos.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf28.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)
After [Lady Meredith](wiki:Lady_Meredith), the ship of the adventurer Ser [Eustace Hightower](wiki:Eustace_Hightower)', was damaged by a [leviathan](wiki:Leviathan) in the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea), he sailed for the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles). Since he was further south than he realized, Eustace landed on the coast of Sothoryos. He and his crew were stuck there for a year, trying to repair the ship and discovering treasure, but the crew dwindled from disease, attacks of deadly animals, and unexplained vanishings. Luckily, Eustace eventually was found by a passing Summer Islander ship, and with their help, was able to sail for [Tall Trees Town](wiki:Tall_Trees_Town), hire a new crew, and return to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) in [59 AC](wiki:59_AC).(wiki:Sothoryos.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
[Yezzan zo Qaggaz](wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz) contracted an unidentified disease while in Sothoryos.(wiki:Sothoryos.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Brindle-skinned half-men](wiki:Sothoryi) from the jungles of Sothoryos are among those who come to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) to battle in [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit).(wiki:Sothoryos.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
[Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) divides the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) into three squadrons during their journey to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay). [Ralf Stonehouse](wiki:Ralf_Stonehouse) leads the fastest ships on the [corsair's road](wiki:Corsair's_road) off the northern coast of Sothoryos. Only nine of Red Ralf's ships rejoin Victarion at the [Isle of Cedars](wiki:Isle_of_Cedars), with Ralf himself among the missing.(wiki:Sothoryos.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
