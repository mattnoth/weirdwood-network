## Origins

A thousand years after the time of King [Brandon the Burner](wiki:Brandon_Stark_(Burner)), [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) sailing out of [Great Wyk](wiki:Great_Wyk) were blown off course to a cluster of islands eight days sail from the closest shore. Their captain built a tower and a beacon there, took the name "[Farwynd](wiki:House_Farwynd_of_the_Lonely_Light)", and declared the Lonely Light his seat.(wiki:Lonely_Light.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Quotes

Aeron knew some [Farwynds](wiki:House_Farwynd), a queer folk who held lands on the westernmost shores of [Great Wyk](wiki:Great_Wyk) and the scattered isles beyond, rocks so small that most could support but a single household. Of those, the Lonely Light was the most distant, eight days' sail to the northwest amongst rookeries of [seals](wiki:Seal) and [sea lions](wiki:Sea_lion) and the boundless grey oceans. The [Farwynds there](wiki:House_Farwynd_of_the_Lonely_Light) were even queerer than the rest. Some said they were [skinchangers](wiki:Skinchanger), unholy creatures who could take on the forms of [sea lions](wiki:Sea_lion), [walruses](wiki:Walrus), even [spotted whales](wiki:Whale), the wolves of [the wild sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea).(wiki:Lonely_Light.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
