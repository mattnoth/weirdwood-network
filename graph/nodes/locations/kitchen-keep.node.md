## Appearances & Description

The Kitchen Keep is located across the courtyard from the main kitchen of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep),(wiki:Kitchen_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) and occupants can see and smell [food](wiki:Food) being prepared.(wiki:Kitchen_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Below the Kitchen Keep is a gloomy cellar with a vaulted stone ceiling, and the keep connects to other buildings through underground passageways.(wiki:Kitchen_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Lord [Gyles Rosby](wiki:Gyles_Rosby) has spacious apartments atop the Kitchen Keep, including a large bedchamber and [solar](wiki:Solar), a bath and dressing room, and small adjoining chambers for servants.(wiki:Kitchen_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord [Gyles Rosby](wiki:Gyles_Rosby) is dispossessed of his apartments in the Kitchen Keep by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) so that Tywin's son [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) can move in with his household.(wiki:Kitchen_Keep.cite_ref-Rasos58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
