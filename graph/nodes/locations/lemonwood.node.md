## Origins

Prior to the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals), some [High Kings of Dorne](wiki:High_Kings_of_Dorne) resided at a wooden motte-and-bailey castle near Lemonwood, but their river kingdom eventually splintered.(wiki:Lemonwood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_kingdoms_of_the_first_men.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
During the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), Lemonwood was captured by [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen). Her sister, [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen), unleashed the [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame) of [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) on Lemonwood after Dornishmen attacked [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath).(wiki:Lemonwood.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
