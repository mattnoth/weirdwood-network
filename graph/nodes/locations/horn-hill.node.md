## Origins

According to legend, the castle was built on Horn Hill by the brothers [Harlon the Hunter](wiki:Harlon_the_Hunter) and [Herndon of the Horn](wiki:Herndon_of_the_Horn), twin sons of [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand).(wiki:Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) In the [Age of the Hundred Kingdoms](wiki:Age_of_the_Hundred_Kingdoms), before the Reach was united under the [Kings of the Reach](wiki:Kings_of_the_Reach), the kingdom of the [western marches](wiki:Western_marches) extended from Horn Hill to [Nightsong](wiki:Nightsong).(wiki:Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) visited Horn Hill during his reign.(wiki:Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

The [Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Aenys_I)) led one of his [Dornish](wiki:Dornishmen) hosts to attack Nightsong and Horn Hill, but he was defeated in the [Vulture Hunt](wiki:Vulture_Hunt).(wiki:Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

[House Tarly](wiki:House_Tarly) hosted King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) at Horn Hill in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC).(wiki:Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) made [a tour](wiki:Royal_progress) of the [Riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), the [Westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) and the [Reach](wiki:Reach) in [112 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) which included a stop at Horn Hill.(wiki:Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

Two [warlocks](wiki:Warlock) from [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) sacrificed a bull aurochs at Horn Hill and had young [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) bathe in its blood. When the act did not make his son stronger, Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) had the warlocks scourged.(wiki:Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Samwell rode a [mare](wiki:Horse) from Horn Hill to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

## Appearances & Description

Horn Hill has a [sept](wiki:Sept)(wiki:Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and a library.(wiki:Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Ragot41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) According to [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), Horn Hill's walls are less formidable than the city walls of [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown).(wiki:Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) A pond lies below the castle,(wiki:Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and the woods around Horn Hill are teeming with game. The Tarlys employ huntsmen and [hounds](wiki:Dog) during their hunts.(wiki:Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Rasos46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) is camped at Horn Hill when Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) receives [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s letter claiming that the children of the late King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) are [bastards](wiki:Bastard).(wiki:Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Feast for Crows

[Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) intends to send [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) and the infant [wildling prince](wiki:Wildling_prince) from [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) to live at Horn Hill with his mother, [Melessa Florent](wiki:Melessa_Florent).(wiki:Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
