## Origins

Tiqui is one of the ancient capitals of [Yi Ti](wiki:Yi_Ti), and was the origin of some of the nation's past [god-emperors](wiki:God-Emperor).(wiki:Tiqui.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_tiqui.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The dynasty of the [purple emperors](wiki:Purple_emperors) had their seat located at Tiqui.(wiki:Tiqui.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Tiqui is currently a market city, and is connected to [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) by the [Sand Road](wiki:Sand_Road).(wiki:Tiqui.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
