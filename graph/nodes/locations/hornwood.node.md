## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

After [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) kidnaps and forcibly marries the widowed Lady [Donella Hornwood](wiki:Donella_Hornwood), Hornwood is [seized](wiki:Fighting_in_the_Hornwood) by Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) to prevent it from falling into Ramsay's hands.(wiki:Hornwood.cite_ref-Racok28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) learns that [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) from along the [White Knife](wiki:White_Knife) and Hornwood have been seeking protection at [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) with the [Manderlys](wiki:House_Manderly). Some Hornwood men are also reported to have joined Ramsay's force marching on [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin).(wiki:Hornwood.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
As a condition for peace with the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), Wyman Manderly relinquishes his claim on Hornwood lands and castles.(wiki:Hornwood.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) During the wedding of "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)" to Ramsay, now legitimized as Ramsay Bolton, in [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), Ramsay is introduced as [Lord of the Hornwood](wiki:Lord_of_the_Hornwood).(wiki:Hornwood.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
