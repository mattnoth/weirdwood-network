## Origins

The tower's original name was lost with the death of [Harren the Black](wiki:Harren_Hoare), the builder of Harrenhal, in the [burning of Harrenhal](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest). It has since been renamed the Tower of Dread.(wiki:Tower_of_Dread.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
The sickly and impoverished Lord [Maegor Towers](wiki:Maegor_Towers) only utilized the Tower of Dread. Here he lived with his household of a cook and three men-at-arms until his death in [61 AC](wiki:61_AC). Queen [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)), who lived in the [Widow's Tower](wiki:Widow's_Tower), took care of Maegor's servants after he died.(wiki:Tower_of_Dread.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

When Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) holds [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), three dozen [northmen](wiki:Northmen) and [rivermen](wiki:Rivermen) captured in the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork) are held in the upper chambers of the Tower of Dread.(wiki:Tower_of_Dread.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) These include Lord [Medger Cerwyn](wiki:Medger_Cerwyn), [Harrion Karstark](wiki:Harrion_Karstark), Ser [Wylis Manderly](wiki:Wylis_Manderly), Ser [Jared Frey](wiki:Jared_Frey), Ser [Hosteen Frey](wiki:Hosteen_Frey), Ser [Danwell Frey](wiki:Danwell_Frey), and [Ronel Rivers](wiki:Ronel_Rivers).(wiki:Tower_of_Dread.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Tower_of_Dread.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
