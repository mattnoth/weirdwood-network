## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

In Astapor, [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) goes to the Plaza of Pride to inspect the [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) soldiers that she is considering purchasing. A thousand Unsullied have been marched out of their barracks for her inspection and drawn up in ten ranks of one hundred before the fountain and its great bronze harpy. [Arstan Whitebeard](wiki:Arstan_Whitebeard) taps the end of his staff in displeasure on the red bricks as he listens to [Kraznys mo Nakloz](wiki:Kraznys_mo_Nakloz) boast to Daenerys how the Unsullied have been standing in the Plaza of Pride for a day and a night with no food and no water.(wiki:Plaza_of_Pride.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) When Dany returns to buy the Unsullied she and her [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) head to the [Plaza of Punishment](wiki:Plaza_of_Punishment).(wiki:Plaza_of_Pride.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
