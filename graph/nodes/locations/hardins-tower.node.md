## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow)'s cells are located in Hardin's Tower when he begins training with the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Hardin's_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) After swearing his vows and becoming a [steward](wiki:Steward), he moves to the [Lord Commander's Tower](wiki:Lord_Commander's_Tower) to aid [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont). After that tower is burned, however, Jon returns to his old cells in Hardin's Tower.(wiki:Hardin's_Tower.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Jon sees old snow laying in drifts against Hardin's Tower when he returns to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) after being [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:Hardin's_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
[Big Boil](wiki:Big_Boil) attacks the door of Hardin's Tower with his axe during the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black), but [Mully](wiki:Mully) fires an arrow into his leg and forces the [wildling](wiki:Wildling) to retreat.(wiki:Hardin's_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Jon, now [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) after Jeor's death, moves into [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye)'s rooms behind the armory.(wiki:Hardin's_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Hardin's Tower becomes the residence of nineteen wildling women and girls who accept Jon's call. Some of the men of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) attempt to take advantage of the women there; although a dozen [spearwives](wiki:Spearwives) turn them away, the men hostilely refer to the building as "Harlot's Tower".(wiki:Hardin's_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Jon has his own men guard the tower, and the giant [Wun Weg Wun Dar Wun](wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun) sleeps in its entryway.(wiki:Hardin's_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Jon has the top floor of Hardin's Tower prepared for [Val](wiki:Val).(wiki:Hardin's_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
Wun Wun kills Ser [Patrek of King's Mountain](wiki:Patrek_of_King's_Mountain), who was smitten with Val,(wiki:Hardin's_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) outside Hardin's Tower. When Jon comes to investigate the commotion, he is betrayed in the [mutiny at Castle Black](wiki:Mutiny_at_Castle_Black).(wiki:Hardin's_Tower.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
