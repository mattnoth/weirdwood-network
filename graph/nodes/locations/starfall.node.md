## Origins

### Older History

According to legend, Starfall was built where the first [Dayne](wiki:House_Dayne) found a [magic stone](wiki:Dawn) after he followed the path of a shooting star. The Daynes grew in power to become the [Kings of the Torrentine](wiki:King_of_the_Torrentine) and one of the strongest houses of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Starfall.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_kingdoms_of_the_first_men.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

The Daynes submitted to [House Nymeros Martell](wiki:House_Martell) during [Nymeria's War](wiki:Nymeria's_War).(wiki:Starfall.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### Targaryen Era

After Ser [Joffrey Dayne](wiki:Joffrey_Dayne) attacked [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) burned Starfall with her dragon, [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar).(wiki:Starfall.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Starfall.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) and Princess [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell) visited Starfall on their way to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) where their mother was to broker their marriage to the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) twins, [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) and [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Starfall.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

In the aftermath of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Ser [Arthur Dayne](wiki:Arthur_Dayne), the [Sword of the Morning](wiki:Sword_of_the_Morning), perished at the [tower of joy](wiki:Tower_of_joy). When Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) returned Arthur's sword, the ancestral blade [Dawn](wiki:Dawn), to Starfall, Arthur's sister [Ashara](wiki:Ashara_Dayne) threw herself from the castle's Palestone Sword tower.(wiki:Starfall.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Starfall](wiki:Category:Images_of_Starfall)*

Starfall is located in the western [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains)(wiki:Starfall.cite_ref-Rtloiafwesteros.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) on an island in the [Torentine](wiki:Torentine) where it pours into the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea).(wiki:Starfall.cite_ref-Rawoiafstarfall.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The castle includes a tower called the Palestone Sword.(wiki:Starfall.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Northeast of Starfall is [High Hermitage](wiki:High_Hermitage),(wiki:Starfall.cite_ref-5) the seat of a [lesser branch](wiki:House_Dayne_of_High_Hermitage) of House Dayne.(wiki:Starfall.cite_ref-6)(wiki:Starfall.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
