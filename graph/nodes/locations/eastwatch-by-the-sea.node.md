## Edges

## Origins

Maester [Balder](wiki:Balder) wrote *[The Edge of the World](wiki:The_Edge_of_the_World)* while serving at Eastwatch.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_stoneborn_of_skagos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Maester [Wyllis](wiki:Wyllis), who studied the customs of the free folk and lived among them, was last seen at the docks of [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) hoping to find transport to Eastwatch.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_wall_and_beyond:_the_wildlings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Half a year after the [Night's Watch rebellion](wiki:Night's_Watch_rebellion_of_50_AC) of [50 AC](wiki:50_AC) was put down and Ser [Raymund Mallery](wiki:Raymund_Mallery) and his men fled [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall), his head was delivered to Eastwatch by a [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) chieftain, who claimed that Raymund's rebels had been [eaten](wiki:Cannibalism).(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

[Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) visited Eastwatch on the *[Ice Wolf](wiki:Ice_Wolf)*.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

In [219 AC](wiki:219_AC), [Aegor Rivers](wiki:Aegor_Rivers) was captured at the end of the [Third Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Third_Blackfyre_Rebellion), and King [Aerys I Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_I_Targaryen) decided to sentence him to join the Night's Watch. However, the ship taking Aegor north to Eastwatch was intercepted en route, and he returned to the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) and his support of [House Blackfyre](wiki:House_Blackfyre) in exile.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

When Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) took the black, Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) accompanied him on *[Golden Dragon](wiki:Golden_Dragon)* to see him safely to Eastwatch.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Among those aboard who had been sent to the Wall by King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) to join the Night's Watch alongside Aemon, was [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers)(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) and many of the [Raven's Teeth](wiki:Raven's_Teeth).(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

Before [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), when [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) was a [smuggler](wiki:Smuggler), he traded at Eastwatch. In his youth, he served on the *[Cobblecat](wiki:Cobblecat)*, captained by [Roro Uhoris](wiki:Roro_Uhoris). The ship was taken by galleys from Eastwatch for the crime of trading weapons to the free folk, and Roro was beheaded at the castle.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

When [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye) joined the Night's Watch after the Rebellion, some [Skagossons](wiki:Skagosi) were serving at Eastwatch.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

One brother at Eastwatch is a man who set his father's house on fire, and barred the door. All nine members of his family burned to death.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

There are fewer than two hundred men stationed at Eastwatch under the command of [Cotter Pyke](wiki:Cotter_Pyke). [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and the officers of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) enjoy succulent [crabs](wiki:Crab) shipped from Eastwatch in a barrel of snow. [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), tells Tyrion there are reports that fisherfolk near Eastwatch have seen [white walkers](wiki:White_walkers).(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Ragot21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

[Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) sends [Dareon](wiki:Dareon) to serve with [Borcas](wiki:Borcas) at Eastwatch.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Cotter writes to Jeor that large, misshapen footprints have been seen north of Eastwatch.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Tyrion, the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), sends [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) and [Allar Deem](wiki:Allar_Deem) to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), discreetly arranging for Allar to be tossed from *[Summer's Dream](wiki:Summer's_Dream)* while the carrack sails for Eastwatch.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

Lord Commander Mormont plans to re-garrison the [Long Barrow](wiki:Long_Barrow) with men from Eastwatch.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne) becomes the new [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms) of Eastwatch.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

### A Storm of Swords

[Free folk](wiki:Free_folk) are said to be massing near Eastwatch,(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1) and Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sends [ravens](wiki:Raven) from Castle Black to warn Eastwatch and the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) that [Thenn](wiki:Thenn) raiders led by [Styr](wiki:Styr) are south of the Wall.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-2)

Cotter captures [Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt) at Long Barrow,(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) and the wildling reveals that [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) killed [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand).(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) Alliser Thorne and Ser [Glendon Hewett](wiki:Glendon_Hewett) are among a small group of Eastwatch men who escort Janos Slynt to Castle Black. Janos takes command of Castle Black in the absence of Bowen Marsh, and he has Jon arrested.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-1)

King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) and his forces sail to Eastwatch, then ride west with Cotter's scouts to defeat [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder)'s host in the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall).(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-1)(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent), Princess [Shireen Baratheon](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon), and Stannis's fleet remain at Eastwatch.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-1) Vindicated by Stannis, Jon trains with the promising [Iron Emmett](wiki:Iron_Emmett), one of Eastwatch's best rangers. Instead of Cotter or Ser [Denys Mallister](wiki:Denys_Mallister), Jon is chosen as the new Lord Commander after Jeor's death.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Aboard *[Blackbird](wiki:Blackbird)*, [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), Maester Aemon, and Dareon sail from Eastwatch to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-2)

### A Dance with Dragons

Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) and [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan) sail from Eastwatch for [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor),(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) while Selyse and her [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) travel from Eastwatch to Castle Black.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) Lord Commander Snow sends his friends [Grenn](wiki:Grenn) and [Pypar](wiki:Pypar) from Castle Black to Eastwatch.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0) When [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) has a vision of submerged towers by the sea, Jon ponders if it is Eastwatch.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

In order to save thousands of wildlings trapped at [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome), Cotter Pyke leads eleven ships in a rescue mission, with [Glendon Hewett](wiki:Glendon_Hewett) remaining to hold Eastwatch.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0) Bowen Marsh is tasked with collecting the treasures of wildlings crossing the Wall south, and having the tolls sent to Eastwatch. Because their [mammoths](wiki:Mammoth) are too big to travel through the Wall at Castle Black, the [giants](wiki:Giants) are sent east to round the Wall at Eastwatch.(wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)
