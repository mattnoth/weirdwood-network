## Appearances & Description

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), [Lord of Dragonstone](wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone), holds a quiet feast with his followers in the Great Hall. The fool [Patchface](wiki:Patchface) collides with [Cressen](wiki:Cressen) when the elderly [maester](wiki:Maester) arrives late. [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) places Patchface's bucket helm atop Cressen, leading the attendees to laugh at him. Stannis dismisses Cressen in favor of the younger Maester [Pylos](wiki:Pylos), but Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) allows Cressen to eat with him at a lower table. Cressen attempts to poison Melisandre with the [strangler](wiki:Strangler) while leading a toast to her [Lord of Light](wiki:R'hllor). The [red priestess](wiki:Red_priestess) survives the assassination attempt, however, and the maester chokes to death instead.(wiki:Great_Hall_(Dragonstone).cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
