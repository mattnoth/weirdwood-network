## Culture

According to folklore the Great Barrow contains the grave of the [First King](wiki:First_King) of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) or a [King of the Giants](wiki:King_of_the_Giants).(wiki:Great_Barrow.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Maester [Kennet's](wiki:Kennet_(maester)) [Passages of the Dead](wiki:Passages_of_the_Dead) mentions a curse was supposedly placed on the Great Barrow allowing none to rival the First King. The curse is said to have weakened and made corpselike any living man who pretended to the title, such as the [Barrow Kings](wiki:Barrow_Kings). [Maesters](wiki:Maesters) doubt the legend.(wiki:Great_Barrow.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_north.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
