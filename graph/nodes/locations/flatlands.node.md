## Origins

The Flatlands used to be part of the southern marches of [Andalos](wiki:Andalos), the kingdom of the [Andals](wiki:Andals), before [their migration](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) several millennia ago.(wiki:Flatlands.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
