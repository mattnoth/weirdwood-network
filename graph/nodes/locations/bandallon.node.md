## Origins

After the election of [Septon](wiki:Septon) [Alfyn](wiki:Alfyn) to the seat of [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon), King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) visited Bandallon, [Three Towers](wiki:Three_Towers), [Uplands](wiki:Uplands), and [Honeyholt](wiki:Honeyholt) while awaiting the arrival of the ailing Alfyn to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown).(wiki:Bandallon.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

In [89 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), King Jaehaerys I stopped at Bandallon during one of his [progress](wiki:Royal_progress) around the [Reach](wiki:Reach). As Jaehaerys was [estranged](wiki:First_Quarrel) from his sister-wife, Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), at the time, Lord [Blackbar](wiki:House_Blackbar)'s youngest daughter boldly tried to seduce the king. Jaehaerys refused her advances, however.(wiki:Bandallon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

While sailing against Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy) in the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon)'s [fleet](wiki:Velaryon_fleet) passed Bandallon.(wiki:Bandallon.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
