## Origins

The castle was built by [Karlon Stark](wiki:Karlon_Stark), a younger son of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), who was awarded land after defeating a rebel lord "a thousand years ago".(wiki:Karhold.cite_ref-date_1-1) The castle was named **Karl's Hold** after Karlon, but over centuries the name developed into Karhold and the Karhold Starks became the [Karstarks](wiki:House_Karstark).(wiki:Karhold.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Similar to the [northern mountain clans](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans), older men of Karhold leave to hunt during winter to ease the burden on younger generations.(wiki:Karhold.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark) and his sons [Harrion](wiki:Harrion_Karstark), [Eddard](wiki:Eddard_Karstark), and [Torrhen](wiki:Torrhen_Karstark) lead three hundred horsemen and near two thousand foot from Karhold to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) when [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls the northern banners.(wiki:Karhold.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Eddard and Torrhen are slain by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) during the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Karhold.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords

After Jaime escapes from captivity at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), the vengeful Lord Rickard promises his daughter [Alys's](wiki:Alys_Karstark) hand to whomever would bring him the Kingslayer.(wiki:Karhold.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

[Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat) maims and captures Jaime with the hopes of gaining Alys in marriage, which would give him the chance to become Lord of Karhold.(wiki:Karhold.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Because Lord Rickard murders two prisoners, [Willem Lannister](wiki:Willem_Lannister) and [Tion Frey](wiki:Tion_Frey), at Riverrun, he is executed by Robb, now [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Karhold.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sends a raven to Karhold in a plea for help to [defend Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) from [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings).(wiki:Karhold.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Feast for Crows

[Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) has ravens sent from Castle Black with messages seeking allegiance from the northern lords. Karhold is the only seat to pledge support.(wiki:Karhold.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Karhold's harvest is less than expected because so many men went south with Lord Rickard, leaving women, children, the elderly, and the crippled to work the fields. Crops wither or are ruined by storms and snow.(wiki:Karhold.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

[Arnolf Karstark](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark), the castellan of Karhold and uncle of late Lord [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark), declares for Stannis. [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), the [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), thinks Arnolf's support is only because [House Karstark](wiki:House_Karstark) has damaged its relations too badly with [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) and [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Karhold.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) However, Arnolf plots to make his son, [Cregan](wiki:Cregan_Karstark), the new Lord of Karhold by forcing Alys to marry Cregan. Arnolf also declares for Stannis to goad the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) to execute the rightful heir of Karhold, captive [Harrion](wiki:Harrion_Karstark).(wiki:Karhold.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

To avoid the planned marriage, Alys flees from Karhold and seeks protection from Jon at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Karhold.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) The Lord Commander arranges for [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) to marry Alys to [Sigorn](wiki:Sigorn), [Magnar](wiki:Magnar) of [Thenn](wiki:Thenn_(valley)), thus creating a new [noble house](wiki:House_Thenn) that would claim Karhold should Harrion perish.(wiki:Karhold.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) Cregan is imprisoned by Jon at Castle Black. Alys thinks that Karhold will open its gates to her and Sigorn's [Thenns](wiki:Thenns). Jon advises her to send to the Night's Watch spare boys or any older men who would otherwise leave to hunt in winter.(wiki:Karhold.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3)

### The Winds of Winter

After receiving a message from Jon Snow that Arnolf plans to betray the king, [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) has the castellan of Karhold arrested.(wiki:Karhold.cite_ref-15)
