## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After having fled [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) atop the [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [Drogon](wiki:Drogon), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) ends up in the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea). She stays in the dragon's lair, a cave in a hill that she names Dragonstone. She lives on local edible vegetation, and on meat from kills that Drogon brings back, and catches one fish from the pool.(wiki:Dragonstone_(hill).cite_ref-Radwd71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Daenerys eventually decides to return to Meereen, but is unable to get Drogon to follow her commands, leading to her traveling on foot. She follows a stream that she thinks will flow into the [Skahazadhan](wiki:Skahazadhan).(wiki:Dragonstone_(hill).cite_ref-Radwd71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
