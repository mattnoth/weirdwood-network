## Appearances & Description

The town is sunny, but shaded by the enormous [Talking Trees](wiki:Talking_Trees), on the trunks of which is recorded the entire history of the Summer Islanders.(wiki:Tall_Trees_Town.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_summer_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Origins

In [19 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Tall Trees Town was sacked by a [pirate](wiki:Pirate) fleet, who stole a fortune and carried off thousands into [slavery](wiki:Slavery). When King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) heard of this event, he commanded that walls be raised around [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Tall_Trees_Town.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
[Summer Islanders](wiki:Summer_Isles) who visited [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos) helped Ser [Eustace Hightower](wiki:Eustace_Hightower) sail his damaged [Lady Meredith](wiki:Lady_Meredith) to Tall Trees Town. Eustace hired a new crew and continued on to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown).(wiki:Tall_Trees_Town.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
