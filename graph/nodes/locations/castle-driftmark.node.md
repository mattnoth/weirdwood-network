## Origins

Shortly after the death of her husband, King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen), Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) brought her children to the castle of Driftmark.(wiki:Castle_Driftmark.cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

The island prospered when [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) was [Lord of the Tides](wiki:Lord_of_the_Tides), and three fishing villages near Castle Driftmark developed into [Hull](wiki:Hull).(wiki:Castle_Driftmark.cite_ref-Rfab12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Because Driftmark was a damp and cramped castle, Corlys the Sea Snake raised a new castle to house the [Driftwood Throne](wiki:Driftwood_Throne) on the far side of the island. During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), this new seat, [High Tide](wiki:High_Tide), was burned by the [greens](wiki:Greens) amid the [Battle in the Gullet](wiki:Battle_in_the_Gullet), however.(wiki:Castle_Driftmark.cite_ref-Rfab15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

The succession of [House Velaryon](wiki:House_Velaryon) was disputed after the passing of Lord Corlys. While [Daemion](wiki:Daemion_Velaryon) and [Daeron Velaryon](wiki:Daeron_Velaryon) were eventually reconciled with Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon), [Malentine](wiki:Malentine_Velaryon) and [Rhogar Velaryon](wiki:Rhogar_Velaryon) attempted to assassinate Alyn at Driftmark. Loyal guards alerted Alyn, however, and Malentine was slain and Rhogar chose to [take the black](wiki:Take_the_black).(wiki:Castle_Driftmark.cite_ref-Rfab20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Appearances & Description

Castle Driftmark is located on the south side of the island,(wiki:Castle_Driftmark.cite_ref-2) with the port town of [Hull](wiki:Hull) located beneath its walls.(wiki:Castle_Driftmark.cite_ref-Rfab12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Driftmark is a grim-looking castle, often damp and flooded. It has dark, salt-stained walls.(wiki:Castle_Driftmark.cite_ref-Rfab12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
