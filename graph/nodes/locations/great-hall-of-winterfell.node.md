## Appearances & Description

The Great Hall has grey stone walls and tall narrow windows.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Its wide doors made of [oak](wiki:Oak) and [iron](wiki:Iron)(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) open to the castle yard,(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and the lord's door at the rear leads to a dimly-lit gallery.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) The hall has a pitched roof,(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and its rafters have been stained by centuries of smoke.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
The Great Hall of Winterfell contains the high seat of the old [Kings in the North](wiki:Kings_in_the_North).(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The seat's cold stone has been polished by the many [lords](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell) who have sat upon it, and its massive arms are decorated with the carved heads of snarling [direwolves](wiki:Direwolves).(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
The Great Hall can seat five hundred people.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) One arrangement is for eight long rows of trestle tables, four to each side of the central aisle.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) The immense castle of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) has kitchens as large as Winterfell's Great Hall.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Origins

The family of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) often eats in the Great Hall, and Eddard always keeps an extra seat at his table for a servant or adviser to join him.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Eddard's goblet has the snarling head of a silver [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) raised on its side.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) holds a welcoming feast for King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) in the Great Hall. Eddard and Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) host Robert and Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) on a raised platform, with all of their children seated nearby. Eddard's bastard, [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), is seated on benches with the lower born, however. When an upset Jon leaves the banquet to go outside, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) jumps down from the door above the hall.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
When [Yoren](wiki:Yoren) eats in the Great Hall with [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), a long trestle table is set up near the hearth.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
After [Eddard's arrest](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls the [northern](wiki:North) banners to Winterfell. The Great Hall is not large enough to seat all of the visitors at the same time, so Robb hosts them in turn. When Lord [Jon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber) challenges Robb's authority, the boy's [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind), bites off two of the Greatjon's fingers. A laughing Lord Umber then becomes Robb's strongest supporter.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
Jon Snow, fleeing from [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), recalls the Great Hall's smells of smoke and dog and roasting meat.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast) is held in Winterfell's Great Hall. After the attendees eat, the tables are clearing to allow space for dancing.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)
Calling himself [Prince of Winterfell](wiki:Prince_of_Winterfell), [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) claims the high seat during the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) by his [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn). Theon has the servants gathered to the Great Hall so they can hear [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) relinquish the castle.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
After [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) arrives to visit her brother, Theon finds her having breakfast with her men in the Great Hall.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
During the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell) by [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton), [Ulf the Ill](wiki:Ulf_the_Ill) is killed by crossbow while running for the Great Hall.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) After the Boltons burn the castle and depart, Bran emerges from the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell) to find the Great Hall's doors charred and smoldering. Its rafters have given way, causing the roof to crash down.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Jon wishes he could show Winterfell to [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte) and feast with her in the Great Hall.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Jon later has a dream in which he is not welcome at a feast in the hall.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), hiding at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), includes the Great Hall while making a snow castle of Winterfell.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), the new [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North) after the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), holds [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark) of his son [Ramsay](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) to "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)" at Winterfell. Roose has squatters quickly build a new roof for the Great Hall, and then he has them all hanged.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Raw planks are raised to replaced the original burned doors.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) The wedding feast is held in the Great Hall, with [Abel](wiki:Abel) performing music and Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) providing [food and drink](wiki:Frey_pies).(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)
As the army of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) slowly marches on Winterfell during [a blizzard](wiki:March_on_Winterfell), Roose's supporters enjoy the comforts of the Great Hall. It becomes overcrowded with hundreds of horses, dogs, and men, however.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Theon, a captive of the Boltons, prefers the darkened rear of the hall.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
After several men are found dead at Winterfell, the nobles begin quarreling during their meals in the Great Hall.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1) When [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)) is found dead, [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) and [Manderlys](wiki:House_Manderly) begin fighting each other in the hall.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1)

I betook of your lord father's meat and mead, had a look at [Kingslayer](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and [Imp](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) ... and made passing note of Lord [Eddard's](wiki:Eddard_Stark) children and the [wolf pups](wiki:Direwolves) that ran at their heels.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
