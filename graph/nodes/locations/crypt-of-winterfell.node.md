## Identity

The Crypt of Winterfell is the ancient underground burial vault beneath Winterfell, seat of House Stark. It is one of the oldest parts of the castle. The crypt contains stone sarcophagi and statues of past Lords of Winterfell and Kings in the North, each with an iron sword laid across their lap to keep their spirit at rest. The deepest and oldest levels are said to date back thousands of years. Bran Stark has visions in the crypts, and they figure prominently in Jon Snow's dreams. Many Starks are interred here.
