## Origins

### High Kings[ ]

[Ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) believe that the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair) was found on Old Wyk when their [First Men](wiki:First_Men) ancestors first arrived on the uninhabited isle.(wiki:Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_dawn_age.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
The ironborn consider Old Wyk to be the holiest of all the Iron Islands.(wiki:Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The [Grey King's Hall](wiki:Grey_King's_Hall) is said to have been made of the bones of [Nagga](wiki:Nagga) after the [sea dragon](wiki:Sea_dragon) was slain by the legendary [Grey King](wiki:Grey_King). His hall was where [kingsmoots](wiki:Kingsmoot) to choose the [High King of the Iron Islands](wiki:High_King_of_the_Iron_Islands) were held for thousand of years, beginning with the crowning of [Urras Greyiron](wiki:Urras_Greyiron) by [Galon Whitestaff](wiki:Galon_Whitestaff). Urras Ironfoot's son, [Erich I Greyiron](wiki:Erich_I_Greyiron), later tried to claim the crown without a kingsmoot, but opposition from Galon led to Erich renouncing his claim and the selection by kingsmoot of Old Wyk's [rock king](wiki:Rock_king), [Regnar Drumm](wiki:Regnar_Drumm).(wiki:Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
[Ragged Ralf](wiki:Ragged_Ralf) was a famous reaver from the island.(wiki:Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) King [Harrag Hoare](wiki:Harrag_Hoare) included longships from Old Wyk in his forces when he attacked the [north](wiki:North) of [Theon Stark](wiki:Theon_Stark).(wiki:Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### Iron Kings[ ]

The selection of high kings through the kingsmoot ended when [Urron Greyiron](wiki:Urron_Greyiron) gathered his rivals to Old Wyk and slaughtered them amidst Nagga's ribs. Old Wyk's rock and [salt kings](wiki:Salt_king) were reduced to [lords](wiki:Lord) sworn to hereditary dynasties of [Kings of the Iron Islands](wiki:Kings_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_iron_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
When a [sept](wiki:Sept) was built on Old Wyk during the reign of King [Horgan Hoare](wiki:Horgan_Hoare), the [priests of the Drowned God](wiki:Priests_of_the_Drowned_God) burned the sept, killed the septon, and drowned its congregation. The king reacted by slaughtering the priests, earning him the sobriquet Horgan Priestkiller.(wiki:Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### Iron Throne[ ]

After [the death](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal) of King [Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), priests of the Drowned God crowned the priest-king [Lodos](wiki:Lodos) at [Nagga's Hill](wiki:Nagga's_Hill). During the [invasion of the Iron Islands](wiki:Invasion_of_the_Iron_Islands) by King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), the new [Lord of the Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Lord_of_the_Seven_Kingdoms) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), in [2 AC](wiki:2_AC), Lodos called upon [krakens](wiki:Kraken) to destroy Aegon's ships. When that did not occur, a stone-laden Lodos urged his followers to walk into the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea) and seek counsel with the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God). Thousands of corpses washed up on the island's shores, although the body of Lodos was never found.(wiki:Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_island:_the_greyjoys_of_pyke.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
When [Lodos the Twice-Drowned](wiki:Lodos_the_Twice-Drowned) rose in rebellion, [Goren Greyjoy](wiki:Goren_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), led a hundred longships to Great Wyk and Old Wyk to defeat the second Lodos on behalf of King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
After the death of Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), there were battles on Old Wyk and [Pyke](wiki:Pyke_(island)) in the resulting [struggle for power in the Iron Islands](wiki:Struggle_for_power_in_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_red_kraken.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) led the attack on Old Wyk on behalf of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) during [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion).(wiki:Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), sends [Dagmer Cleftjaw](wiki:Dagmer) to roust the [Stonehouses](wiki:House_Stonehouse) and the [Drumms](wiki:House_Drumm) on Old Wyk.(wiki:Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Balon declares himself first [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands)(wiki:Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) and then [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North)(wiki:Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

At [Pebbleton](wiki:Pebbleton), [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) preaches that a [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot) should be held on [Nagga's hill](wiki:Nagga's_hill) on Old Wyk to choose the new king after Balon's death.(wiki:Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
Longships from throughout the Iron Islands sail to the shore of Old Wyk along [Nagga's Cradle](wiki:Nagga's_Cradle), and most of the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) returns from the [north](wiki:North). The [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) celebrate in feast tents set up along the holy strand.(wiki:Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) [Dunstan Drumm](wiki:Dunstan_Drumm), [Lord of Old Wyk](wiki:Lord_of_Old_Wyk), is a candidate during the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), but [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) is chosen as king by the assembled ironborn.(wiki:Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) disappears from Old Wyk after the kingsmoot.(wiki:Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Following Euron's election as king, [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) flees the small island for [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers) and then [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Old_Wyk.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)
