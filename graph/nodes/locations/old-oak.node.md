## Origins

The petty kings of [House Oakheart](wiki:House_Oakheart) joined the [Kingdom of the Reach](wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Reach), the realm of King [Garth III Gardener](wiki:Garth_III_Gardener), through a pact of friendship and mutual defense.(wiki:Old_Oak.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_the_gardener_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) [Lancel I Lannister](wiki:Lancel_I_Lannister), a [King of the Rock](wiki:King_of_the_Rock), conquered as far south as Old Oak before being killed in combat, and his gains were lost by his son, King [Loreon III Lannister](wiki:Loreon_III_Lannister).(wiki:Old_Oak.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
There are [tapestries](wiki:Tapestries) at Old Oak that show historical conflicts between the Oakhearts and [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen).(wiki:Old_Oak.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) In [12 AC](wiki:12_AC) during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), the [Wyl of Wyl](wiki:Wyl_of_Wyl) infiltrated the wedding of Ser [Jon Cafferen](wiki:Jon_Cafferen) and [Alys Oakheart](wiki:Alys_Oakheart). After gelding Jon and killing Alys's father, Lord Oakheart, and most of the wedding guests, the Wyls raped the bride and her handmaids and sold the women to [slavers](wiki:Slavery) from [Myr](wiki:Myr).(wiki:Old_Oak.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) When Princess [Deria Martell](wiki:Deria_Martell) visited [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) as an envoy in [13 AC](wiki:13_AC), the new Lord Oakheart suggested to King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) that Deria be sold to a brothel in retaliation for the Wyl of Wyl,(wiki:Old_Oak.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) who is still remembered in [Fawnton](wiki:Fawnton) and Old Oak.(wiki:Old_Oak.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Aegon visited Old Oak on multiple occasions during his [royal progresses](wiki:Royal_progress).(wiki:Old_Oak.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
When Lord Oakheart held a tourney to celebrate the birth of a son in [73 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Prince [Baelon Targaryen](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) participated as a [mystery knight](wiki:Mystery_knight) and received [knighthood](wiki:Knight) from Ser [Rickard Redwyne](wiki:Rickard_Redwyne).(wiki:Old_Oak.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) visited Old Oak atop [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor) in [88 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Old_Oak.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)
Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)) forced the submission of Old Oak to King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Old_Oak.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
