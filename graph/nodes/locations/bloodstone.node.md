## Origins

Bloodstone was the seat of [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) when he was the self-declared [King of the Stepstones and the Narrow Sea](wiki:King_of_the_Stepstones_and_the_Narrow_Sea) amid the [war for the Stepstones](wiki:War_for_the_Stepstones).(wiki:Bloodstone.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
[Racallio Ryndoon](wiki:Racallio_Ryndoon) controlled a [pirate](wiki:Pirate) kingdom from a sprawling wooden fortress on Bloodstone during the [Daughters' War](wiki:Daughters'_War).(wiki:Bloodstone.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), sent Ser [Gedmund Peake](wiki:Gedmund_Peake) and Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) to establish a [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) presence on Bloodstone. While Racallion was hosting many [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) and [Tyroshi](wiki:Tyrosh) captains and envoys at Bloodstone, the Braavosi fleet in the Stepstones was defeated in [Lord Alyn's attack](wiki:Lord_Alyn's_attack). Alyn lacked the men to conquer Bloodstone from Racallio, however.(wiki:Bloodstone.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
When Unwin tasked Lord Velaryon with sailing to the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea) to confront Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), Alyn Oakenfist sailed into Bloodstone under a parley banner, as the Stepstones lay between the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) and the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea). Racallio eventually allowed the Velaryons to pass the Stepstones and continue west.(wiki:Bloodstone.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Ser [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold)) died on Bloodstone in [260 AC](wiki:260_AC) during the [War of the Ninepenny Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Ninepenny_Kings).(wiki:Bloodstone.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
