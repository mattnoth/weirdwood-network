## Appearances & Description

Snow consists of a single fortified tower, a timber keep, and a stable placed behind a low wall of unmortared rock. It is nestled into the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance) so as to command the entire pathway to it from [Stone](wiki:Stone) below, and the trail to Snow is steeper than the trail from the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) to Stone. The trail then continues up the mountain from Snow to [Sky](wiki:Sky).(wiki:Snow_(waycastle).cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Origins

According to [Mya Stone's mother](wiki:Mya_Stone's_mother), the waycastle Snow was where snow began on the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance) hundreds of years ago.(wiki:Snow_(waycastle).cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Queen [Sharra Arryn](wiki:Sharra_Arryn) tripled the size of the garrisons in [Stone](wiki:Stone), Snow, and [Sky](wiki:Sky) in preparation for the imminent march of the [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) forces against the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest). Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) evaded them, however, by flying [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie)'s inner courtyard.(wiki:Snow_(waycastle).cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) stops at Snow while traveling to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie). The waycastle is commanded by a young [knight](wiki:Knight) with a pockmarked face who offers bread and cheese, but [Mya Stone](wiki:Mya_Stone) has them continue on to [Sky](wiki:Sky). Catelyn rides [Whitey](wiki:Whitey) from Snow.(wiki:Snow_(waycastle).cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn) stop at Snow while descending from the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) to the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon). They enjoy a hot meal of stewed goat and onions before receiving fresh mules for the journey to [Stone](wiki:Stone).(wiki:Snow_(waycastle).cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
