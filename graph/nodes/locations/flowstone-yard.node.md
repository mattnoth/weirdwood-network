## Appearances & Description

Located near [Hunter's Hall](wiki:Hunter's_Hall) and a covered bridge,(wiki:Flowstone_Yard.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) the Flowstone Yard has a lumpy surface.(wiki:Flowstone_Yard.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) To scour them clean, mail shirts can be tossed in barrels of sand and rolled across the uneven ground.(wiki:Flowstone_Yard.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Flowstone_Yard.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) A covered gallery overlooks the yard.(wiki:Flowstone_Yard.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Also located near the Flowstone Yard is the [bear](wiki:Bear) pit of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) and the [Wailing Tower](wiki:Wailing_Tower).(wiki:Flowstone_Yard.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) sees three [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey) knights and their bastard brother ([Jared](wiki:Jared_Frey), [Hosteen](wiki:Hosteen_Frey), [Danwell](wiki:Danwell_Frey), and [Ronel](wiki:Ronel_Rivers)), all captives of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister),(wiki:Flowstone_Yard.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) practice with staves and wooden shields in the Flowstone Yard. The four are soon ransomed by other kin from House Frey.(wiki:Flowstone_Yard.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
After the [fall of Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal), [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) bring the slain to the Flowstone Yard and burn them bodies in a pyre.(wiki:Flowstone_Yard.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Elmar Frey](wiki:Elmar_Frey) asks Arya for help rolling a barrel across the yard.(wiki:Flowstone_Yard.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) gallops through the Flowstone Yard to rescue [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) from the [bear](wiki:Bear) pit.(wiki:Flowstone_Yard.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After having supper with Ser [Bonifer Hasty](wiki:Bonifer_Hasty) in [Hunter's Hall](wiki:Hunter's_Hall), Jaime passes through the Flowstone Yard and meets Ser [Ronnet Connington](wiki:Ronnet_Connington) by the bear pit.(wiki:Flowstone_Yard.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
