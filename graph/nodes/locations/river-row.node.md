## Appearances & Description

The River Row runs from the [King's Gate](wiki:King's_Gate) past the [River Gate](wiki:River_Gate) toward [Aegon's High Hill](wiki:Aegon's_High_Hill)(wiki:River_Row.cite_ref-2) and [Eel Alley](wiki:Eel_Alley), with the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) nearby.(wiki:River_Row.cite_ref-Rtloiafking.27s_landing.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Inns can be found on the River Row,(wiki:River_Row.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) and sea captains, fishmongers, and others with interest in the harbor live along the street.(wiki:River_Row.cite_ref-Rawoiafriver_row.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Origins

Princess [Viserra Targaryen](wiki:Viserra_Targaryen) visited the inns of [Eel Alley](wiki:Eel_Alley) and River Row with her companions for "one last night of laughter" before being sent to [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) to marry old Lord [Theomore Manderly](wiki:Theomore_Manderly). She died later that night at the foot of [Aegon's High Hill](wiki:Aegon's_High_Hill) in a drunken horse race.(wiki:River_Row.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), violent [riots](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)) occurred in the capital. Ser [Torrhen Manderly](wiki:Torrhen_Manderly) led a sortie from the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) down the [Hook](wiki:Hook) with his northmen to try to stop the rioters. When they arrived at [Fishmonger's Square](wiki:Fishmonger's_Square) and the River Row, the place was swarming with Ser [Perkin the Flea](wiki:Perkin_the_Flea)'s gutter [knights](wiki:Knight). Torrhen lost a quarter of his men fighting his way back to the Red Keep.(wiki:River_Row.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
