## Origins

The Pussywillows was the place where [Penny Jenny](wiki:Penny_Jenny) came to live and work after the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field). There she raised her son, [Glendon Flowers](wiki:Glendon_Flowers), and her daughter. It was within the establishment that Glendon was eventually [knighted](wiki:Knight) by Ser [Morgan Dunstable](wiki:Morgan_Dunstable).(wiki:Pussywillows.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Glendon described Pussywillows as an inn to Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall), when mentioning where he had grown up, in order to prevent having to admit he had grown up in a brothel.(wiki:Pussywillows.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
