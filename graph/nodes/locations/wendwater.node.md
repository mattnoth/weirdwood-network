## Origins

[Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen), [Lord of Dragonstone](wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone), angered the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King), [Argilac Durrandon](wiki:Argilac_Durrandon), by asking for lands between the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush), the Wendwater, and the [Mander](wiki:Mander).(wiki:Wendwater.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Prior to the [Last Storm](wiki:Last_Storm), the host of [Orys Baratheon](wiki:Orys_Baratheon) was attacked by Lords [Errol](wiki:House_Errol), [Fell](wiki:House_Fell), and [Buckler](wiki:House_Buckler) in an [ambush on the Wendwater](wiki:Ambush_on_the_Wendwater).(wiki:Wendwater.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
During the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising), Ser [Horys Hill](wiki:Horys_Hill) was killed while trying to prevent the [burning of Jeyne Poore](wiki:Burning_of_Jeyne_Poore) in a field by the Wendwater.(wiki:Wendwater.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
The dragon [Sheepstealer](wiki:Sheepstealer) of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)) was known for grabbing sheep from flocks in [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay) from [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark) to as far as the Wendwater.(wiki:Wendwater.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
The [Fourth Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Fourth_Blackfyre_Rebellion) was crushed in the [Battle of Wendwater Bridge](wiki:Battle_of_Wendwater_Bridge).(wiki:Wendwater.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

En route to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s fleet anchors at the mouth of the Wendwater. Ser [Imry Florent](wiki:Imry_Florent), Lord High Captain of Stannis's fleet, calls for a meeting of captains to instruct them in the battle plan once they reach the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush). Three days later they attack King's Landing in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Wendwater.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
