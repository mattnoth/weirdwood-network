## Origins

During the [Rhoynish Wars](wiki:Rhoynish_Wars), Sar Mell, a pale city known for its flowers, fought with nearby [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys), a colony of the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold). Sar Mell was burned during the [First Turtle War](wiki:First_Turtle_War), while Volon Therys was flooded with Rhoynish [water magic](wiki:Water_magic). Sar Mell was ruined during the [Second Spice War](wiki:Second_Spice_War) and its people were enslaved by Valyria.(wiki:Sar_Mell.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
