## Origins

The Old Palace was built after the union of the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar) with the [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen), as the [Sandship](wiki:Sandship) had been the seat of [House Martell](wiki:House_Martell) prior to [Nymeria's War](wiki:Nymeria's_War). The Old Palace's towers are built in the Rhoynish fashion.(wiki:Old_Palace.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_sunspear.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) flew to abandoned Sunspear, where her demand for submission was rejected by [Meria Martell](wiki:Meria_Martell), [Princess of Dorne](wiki:Princess_of_Dorne).(wiki:Old_Palace.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

In the [Defenestration of Sunspear](wiki:Defenestration_of_Sunspear) during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), Lord [Jon Rosby](wiki:Jon_Rosby) was thrown from the top of the Spear Tower.(wiki:Old_Palace.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

When King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen) visited Dorne after the death of his predecessor, King [Daeron I Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen), he was hosted by the Prince of Dorne in the Old Palace.(wiki:Old_Palace.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Appearances & Description

Rising from the Old Palace is the slender Spear Tower, a massive tower of stone. It thrusts up from the surrounding community, stretching one hundred and fifty feet tall, with a spear of gilded steel atop that adds a further thirty feet. Highborn prisoners can be confined in cells at the top of the Spear Tower.(wiki:Old_Palace.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Old_Palace.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

The Tower of the Sun has a dome of gold and leaded glass. Beneath the dome sits the throne room, a large round room with thick windows and many colored glass. The floors are made of pale [marble](wiki:Marble). Within the throne room there are two seats on a dais, near twins to one another, the only difference being that one is inlaid with the Martell spear on its back and the other features the blazing [Rhoynish](wiki:Rhoynar) sun that flew from the masts of [Nymeria's ships](wiki:Ten_thousand_ships). The spear seat is used by the ruling [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne).(wiki:Old_Palace.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

A long gallery with a rough-cut stone floor connects the feast hall with the solar of the prince.(wiki:Old_Palace.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

While at the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens), [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), the [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), tasks his seneschal, [Ricasso](wiki:Ricasso), with opening his apartments in the Tower of the Sun.(wiki:Old_Palace.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Because of his gout, Doran must be carried by [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah) up the long stone stairs of the Tower of the Sun when he returns to Sunspear. Doran has Areo imprison the older [Sand Snakes](wiki:Sand_Snakes) in the cells atop the Spear Tower.(wiki:Old_Palace.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

While a guest at Sunspear, [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) has chambers in the Tower of the Sun.(wiki:Old_Palace.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Following her failed attempt to crown Myrcella, [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) is imprisoned in the Spear Tower.(wiki:Old_Palace.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Dance with Dragons

Doran hosts a feast for Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) in the Old Palace's feast hall, after which he meets with the Sand Snakes and Arianne in his solar.(wiki:Old_Palace.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
