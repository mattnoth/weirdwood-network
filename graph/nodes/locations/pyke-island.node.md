## Origins

### Antiquity[ ]

The ancient [castle of Pyke](wiki:Pyke) was once part of mainland Pyke, but is now located on small islands and stone stacks after segments of land fell into the sea.(wiki:Pyke_(island).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf757_3-1) [Hrothgar](wiki:Hrothgar) of Pyke was an [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) warrior of old.(wiki:Pyke_(island).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf75.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
King [Theon Stark](wiki:Theon_Stark) defended the [north](wiki:North) against raiders from Pyke.(wiki:Pyke_(island).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf711_10-0)
The island was once ruled by [rock kings](wiki:Rock_king) and [salt kings](wiki:Salt_king), but they became [lords](wiki:Lord) when [Urron Greyiron](wiki:Urron_Greyiron) established a hereditary dynasty of [Kings of the Iron Islands](wiki:Kings_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Pyke_(island).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf752_11-0)
[Iron](wiki:Iron), [lead](wiki:Lead), and [tin](wiki:Tin) from Pyke was traded by ironborn merchants during the dynasty of [House Hoare](wiki:House_Hoare), the successors of [House Greyiron](wiki:House_Greyiron).(wiki:Pyke_(island).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf753_12-0)

### Iron Throne[ ]

Pretenders arose on [Great Wyk](wiki:Great_Wyk), [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk), [Orkmont](wiki:Orkmont), and Pyke after the death of [Harren the Black](wiki:Harren_Hoare) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest). [House Greyjoy](wiki:House_Greyjoy) ultimately became the new [Lords of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lords_of_the_Iron_Islands) after submitting to King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Pyke_(island).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf754_4-1)(wiki:Pyke_(island).cite_ref-Rfab2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Aegon visited Pyke during two of his royal progresses.(wiki:Pyke_(island).cite_ref-Rfab3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
After the death of the Red Kraken, Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), battles were fought on Pyke by rival [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) claimants and then by vengeful [westermen](wiki:Westermen).(wiki:Pyke_(island).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf755_15-0)(wiki:Pyke_(island).cite_ref-Rfab23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
Lord [Quellon Greyjoy](wiki:Quellon_Greyjoy) led fifty longships from Pyke after the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident), but he died in the [battle at the Mander](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mander).(wiki:Pyke_(island).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf756_17-0)
During [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion), Pyke was invaded by the forces of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) and the castle of Pyke [was besieged](wiki:Siege_of_Pyke).

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

In preparation for his plan to attack the [north](wiki:North), [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), the [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), gathers confiscated merchant ships, dozens of longships, and countless fishing ships to the harbor of [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport).(wiki:Pyke_(island).cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) sails from [Seagard](wiki:Seagard) to Pyke. His uncle, [Aeron Damphair](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy), escorts him from [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport) to the [castle of Pyke](wiki:Pyke).(wiki:Pyke_(island).cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
After viewing [Sea Bitch](wiki:Sea_Bitch), Theon rides from Lordsport to the castle of Pyke with "Esgred", not knowing she is actually his sister, [Asha](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy).(wiki:Pyke_(island).cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Balon, [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North), falls to his death from the castle of Pyke. The [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair) is claimed by Balon's brother, [Euron Crow's Eye](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), who drowns [Sawane Botley](wiki:Sawane_Botley) after the Lord of Lordsport Sawane objects.(wiki:Pyke_(island).cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Euron has Maester [Wendamyr](wiki:Wendamyr) send ravens demanding the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) pay homage to him at Pyke.(wiki:Pyke_(island).cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Euron's supporters on Pyke include [Alyn Orkwood](wiki:Alyn_Orkwood), [Jon Myre](wiki:Jon_Myre), [Lucas Codd](wiki:Lucas_Codd), [Harren Half-Hoare](wiki:Harren_Half-Hoare), the [Red Oarsman](wiki:Red_Oarsman), [Kemmett Pyke](wiki:Kemmett_Pyke), [Rodrik Freeborn](wiki:Rodrik_Freeborn), and [Torwold Browntooth](wiki:Torwold_Browntooth).(wiki:Pyke_(island).cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
Aeron Damphair calls for a [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot) on [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk).(wiki:Pyke_(island).cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1) While Sawane's heir, [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley), advocates for [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), Euron supports [Germund Botley](wiki:Germund_Botley) and gives some Botley land to Lord [Waldon Wynch](wiki:Waldon_Wynch).(wiki:Pyke_(island).cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) The kingsmoot selects Euron at Old Wyk.(wiki:Pyke_(island).cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) expects Euron will retreat back to Pyke to protect his spoils of war.(wiki:Pyke_(island).cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
