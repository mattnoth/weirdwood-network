## Origins

House Hoare was originally from [Orkmont](wiki:Orkmont).(wiki:Hoare_Castle.cite_ref-2) It is unknown when Hoare Castle was built on Great Wyk.
King [Harmund III Hoare](wiki:Harmund_III_Hoare) was overthrown by the priests of the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God), who imprisoned the king in Hoare Castle, crowned his brother, [Hagon](wiki:Hagon_Hoare), and mutilated their mother, Dowager Queen [Lelia Lannister](wiki:Lelia_Lannister). In retaliation, [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) invaded the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands). The Lannister commander, Ser [Aubrey Crakehall](wiki:Aubrey_Crakehall), defeated King Hagon the Heartless, gave the [gift of mercy](wiki:Gift_of_mercy) to Harmund in the dungeon, and ordered that the castle be razed to the ground.(wiki:Hoare_Castle.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
