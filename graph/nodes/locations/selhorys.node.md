## Origins

During the [Second Spice War](wiki:Second_Spice_War), [Garin the Great](wiki:Garin_the_Great) achieved his first victory over the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) at Selhorys.(wiki:Selhorys.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

The town has been under the authority of [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) since the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood), the years of expansion under the [tigers](wiki:Tigers_(Volantis)).(wiki:Selhorys.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_volantis.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Appearances & Description

Although it is larger than the cities of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) and [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros),(wiki:Selhorys.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_volantis.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Selhorys is still considered a town, not an independent city.(wiki:Selhorys.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Alone among the major towns, Selhorys stands upon the eastern bank of the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne), making it more vulnerable to [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) than its sister towns west of the river.(wiki:Selhorys.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Timber from the [Forest of Qohor](wiki:Forest_of_Qohor) is sold in the markets of Selhorys.(wiki:Selhorys.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_qohor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Selhorys has towers, domes, and cobbled paths. The city's sandstone walls shelter shops, stalls, and storehouses along the piers. The river gate is guarded by [Volantene](wiki:Volantis) slave soldiers with tiger stripes, while [prostitutes](wiki:Prostitution) in its brothels are also slaves. Volantene river galleys loaded with slave soldiers protect the waterway.(wiki:Selhorys.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) Lanterns within the city are made of colored glass, unlike the parchment lanterns outside of the city walls.(wiki:Selhorys.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

The main square in Selhorys contains an ornate but headless statue of [Triarch](wiki:Triarch) [Horonno](wiki:Horonno) and a stone [red temple](wiki:Red_temple) of [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor). [Pleasure houses](wiki:Pleasure_house) and inns front the square, including a large stone inn with an [immense turtle](wiki:Old_Men_of_the_River) hanging above its door.(wiki:Selhorys.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

A [Yunkish](wiki:Yunkai) envoy comes to Selhorys with chests of gold and gems and two hundred slaves, seeking support for their war against [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Selhorys.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

The *[Shy Maid](wiki:Shy_Maid)* stops at Selhorys, and [Haldon Halfmaester](wiki:Haldon_Halfmaester) and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) listen to a [red priest](wiki:Red_priest) calling for [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) to support [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen). Tyrion plays *[cyvasse](wiki:Cyvasse)* with [Qavo Nogarys](wiki:Qavo_Nogarys), a customs officer, within the stone inn. After fornicating with the [sunset girl](wiki:Sunset_girl) at a modest brothel, Tyrion is kidnapped by Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont)(wiki:Selhorys.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6) and taken by him to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) on horseback.(wiki:Selhorys.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
