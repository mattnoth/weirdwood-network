## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Cley Cerwyn](wiki:Cley_Cerwyn) rides to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) for the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast).(wiki:Castle_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
[Medger Cerwyn](wiki:Medger_Cerwyn), [Lord of Cerwyn](wiki:Lord_of_Cerwyn), succumbs at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) to wounds he suffered in the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:Castle_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
After the [fight at Torrhen's Square](wiki:Fight_at_Torrhen's_Square), Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) sends a [raven](wiki:Raven) from Castle Cerwyn to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) informing [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) that her sons, [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon](wiki:Rickon_Stark), have been killed by [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy). Rodrik marches from Cerwyn to Winterfell to retake Winterfell from Theon's [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn).(wiki:Castle_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lady [Jonelle Cerwyn](wiki:Jonelle_Cerwyn) has become the head of [House Cerwyn](wiki:House_Cerwyn) as her younger brother, Lord Cley, was killed in [battle at Winterfell](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell).(wiki:Castle_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Castle_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sends a raven to Castle Cerwyn in a plea for help to defend [Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) from [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings).(wiki:Castle_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Soldiers from Cerwyn serve in the army of Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), the new [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), but he does not trust their loyalty.(wiki:Castle_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) [Rhodry](wiki:Rhodry) once served as the maester to the Cerwyns.(wiki:Castle_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
