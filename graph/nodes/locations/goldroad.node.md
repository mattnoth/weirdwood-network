## Origins

The goldroad was made during the reign of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), who was aided by his [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin), Lord [Martyn Tyrell](wiki:Martyn_Tyrell).(wiki:Goldroad.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Its route passes near the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire).(wiki:Goldroad.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

After Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) is wounded in the streets of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) flees the capital(wiki:Goldroad.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) and takes the Goldroad to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Goldroad.cite_ref-Rawoiafjourneys_map_-_jaime_lannister.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) tells his nephew, Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully), that Brynden and [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), had wanted to fortify a position on the gold road against Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister). Edmure's defeat of Tywin in the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords) nullified their plan, however.(wiki:Goldroad.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) takes the Goldroad from Casterly Rock to King's Landing to become the [regent](wiki:Regent) for King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Goldroad.cite_ref-Rawoiafjourneys_map_-_kevan_lannister.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
