## Origins

According to legend, a land bridge called the [Arm of Dorne](wiki:Arm_of_Dorne) once connected [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) and [Essos](wiki:Essos). The Broken Arm and the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) were created when the Arm of Dorne was shattered by the [Breaking](wiki:Breaking),(wiki:Broken_Arm.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_the_breaking.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) the usage of the [hammer of the waters](wiki:Hammer_of_the_waters) by the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest).(wiki:Broken_Arm.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
During the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals), [Morgan Martell](wiki:Morgan_Martell) established [House Martell](wiki:House_Martell) between the Broken Arm and the [Greenblood](wiki:Greenblood).(wiki:Broken_Arm.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_the_andals_arrive.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) sends [Dale Seaworth](wiki:Dale_Seaworth) past the Broken Arm to spread word of his claim on the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) to the [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen).(wiki:Broken_Arm.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Obara Sand](wiki:Obara_Sand) claims the people of the Broken Arm are outraged at the death of her father, Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell).(wiki:Broken_Arm.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne) leads the [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet) past the Broken Arm toward the [Reach](wiki:Reach).(wiki:Broken_Arm.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

[Krakens](wiki:Kraken) have been sighted attacking crippled galleys off the Broken Arm.(wiki:Broken_Arm.cite_ref-9)
