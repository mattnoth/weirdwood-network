## Appearances & Description

The grey, gloomy Port of Ibben has cobbled alleys, steep hills, and teeming docks and shipyards. The city is lit by whale-oil lamps suspended on iron chains. Its main structure is the ruins of the [God-King](wiki:God-King)'s castle. Beyond the Port of Ibben, foreigners are only allowed with an [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibbenese) host.(wiki:Port_of_Ibben.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Origins

The Port of Ibben has ruled over [Ib](wiki:Ib) and the lesser isles since the [Dawn Age](wiki:Dawn_Age).(wiki:Port_of_Ibben.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
The [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) raiders of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) have sailed as far as the Port of Ibben.(wiki:Port_of_Ibben.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
