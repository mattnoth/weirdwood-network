## Appearances & Description

The town is a cluster of white houses around the walls of a [holdfast](wiki:Holdfast). It contains a large [sept](wiki:Sept) with a shingled wooden roof and a [lord](wiki:Lord)'s towerhouse on a small hill to the west. There is an inn built atop the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye), as well as a long pier and other docks.
The holdfast is sturdy, with rough unmortared stone walls ten feet high and a wooden catwalk inside the battlements. There is a hidden tunnel leading from a ramshackle barn to the lake. The town's haven is low, long, and built of stone.(wiki:Gods_Eye_town.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Yoren](wiki:Yoren) brings his recruits for the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), including [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), to the town, planning to sail to [Harrentown](wiki:Harrentown) on the northern shore of the Gods Eye. They find the town completely abandoned amidst the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), and the only remaining boat is rotted out.(wiki:Gods_Eye_town.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The group decides to spend the night in the holdfast, but they are woken by the arrival of Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch), who is burning the town in his search for the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners). When Yoren refuses to open the gates, Amory has his men [storm the holdfast](wiki:Fight_at_the_holdfast). Before escaping through the barn's tunnel, Arya frees [Jaqen H'ghar](wiki:Jaqen_H'ghar), [Rorge](wiki:Rorge), and [Biter](wiki:Biter).(wiki:Gods_Eye_town.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
When Arya, [Lommy](wiki:Lommy), [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie), and [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) return the next night they find blackened stones, shells of houses, the sacked holdfast, and dead bodies. After burying Yoren in a grave, they discover that [Cutjack](wiki:Cutjack), [Kurz](wiki:Kurz), and [Tarber](wiki:Tarber) survived in the lord's towerhouse.(wiki:Gods_Eye_town.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
