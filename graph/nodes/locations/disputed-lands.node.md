## Origins

### Valyria and the Doom[ ]

The region was once connected to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) by the [Arm of Dorne](wiki:Arm_of_Dorne), but the [Breaking](wiki:Breaking) shattered the land bridge and left behind the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) in the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea).(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_the_quarrelsome_daughters:_myr.2C_lys.2C_and_tyrosh.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) The region was a rich land during the era of the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold).(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_the_quarrelsome_daughters:_myr.2C_lys.2C_and_tyrosh.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
What is now called the Disputed Lands became a waste during the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood) after the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria).(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) conquered [Lys](wiki:Lys) and [Myr](wiki:Myr), ruling the cities for two generations. [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) supported [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) when that city came under attack from Volantis, however, and Lys and Myr rose in rebellion. The [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King), [Argilac Durrandon](wiki:Argilac_Durrandon), led a host from the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) to the Disputed Lands and defeated the Volantene army threatening Myr. The [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos) supported a Lysene exile with a hundred ships. [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), [Lord of Dragonstone](wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone), joined the alliance against Volantis and burned the Volantene fleet threatening Lys with his dragon, [Balerion](wiki:Balerion). Volantis was forced to retreat.(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_doom_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### Disputed Lands[ ]

The [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) of [Lys](wiki:Lys), [Myr](wiki:Myr), and [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) have continued to contest the Disputed Lands, usually fighting with [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords). More [free companies](wiki:Free_companies) have originated in the Disputed Lands than the rest of the [known world](wiki:Known_world), and twoscore companies are currently active in the region. Some captains initiate their own wars when not contracted to a Free City.(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_the_quarrelsome_daughters:_myr.2C_lys.2C_and_tyrosh.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
[Ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) from the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) have occasionally reaved along the Disputed Lands or served as sellswords or sellsails, such as [Harwyn Hoare](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), for the Free Cities.(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
According to some tales, [Coryanne Wylde](wiki:Coryanne_Wylde) became a camp follower in the Disputed Lands after following Ser [Howard Bullock](wiki:Howard_Bullock) to the region.(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Ser [Orryn Baratheon](wiki:Orryn_Baratheon) died in the Disputed Lands when the [Maiden's Men](wiki:Maiden's_Men) fought the [Men of Valor](wiki:Men_of_Valor).(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Six villages were burned in [56 AC](wiki:56_AC) when dry grasses led to a great fire in the Disputed Lands. The [Adventurers](wiki:Adventurers) were destroyed when they were trapped between the fire and the [Archon of Tyrosh](wiki:Archon_of_Tyrosh)'s host. Ser [Myles Smallwood](wiki:Myles_Smallwood) suggested that [Balerion](wiki:Balerion) had caused the fire, but King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) was skeptical.(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
In [96 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) the [Triarchy](wiki:Triarchy) of Lys, Myr, and Tyrosh defeated [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) in the [Battle of the Borderland](wiki:Battle_of_the_Borderland) and forced the Volantenes to withdraw from the Disputed Lands. The Triarchy then conquered the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones), leading to Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen)'s [war for the Stepstones](wiki:War_for_the_Stepstones).(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) After abandoning his claim as [King of the Stepstones and the Narrow Sea](wiki:King_of_the_Stepstones_and_the_Narrow_Sea), Dameon and Lady [Laena Velaryon](wiki:Laena_Velaryon) flew from [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) across the Disputed Lands to Volantis.(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
In the aftermath of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Ser [Oscar Tully](wiki:Oscar_Tully) led the [Stormbreakers](wiki:Stormbreakers) to Tyrosh and the Disputed Lands in [131 AC](wiki:131_AC).(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) The Triarchy collapsed into the [Daughters' War](wiki:Daughters'_War), with fighting occurring throughout the Stepstones and the Disputed Lands.(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) The death of [Bambarro Bazanne](wiki:Bambarro_Bazanne) in the region in [132 AC](wiki:132_AC) led to [Lysandro Rogare](wiki:Lysandro_Rogare) acquiring the captive Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) The end of the Daughters' War in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC) saw Lys, Myr, and Tyrosh divide the Disputed Lands among themselves.(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
The [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company), formed in [212 AC](wiki:212_AC) in the aftermath of the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion),(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) have often fought in the Disputed Lands.(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Ser [Aegor Rivers](wiki:Aegor_Rivers) died fighting in the region when Myr and Tyrosh fought.(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
The [Band of Nine](wiki:Band_of_Nine), including [Xhobar Qhoqua](wiki:Xhobar_Qhoqua) and [Spotted Tom](wiki:Spotted_Tom), allied at the [Tree of Crowns](wiki:Tree_of_Crowns) in the Disputed Lands in [258 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) and conquered the region as the first step in their campaign to conquer the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), leading to the [War of the Ninepenny Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Ninepenny_Kings). The Disputed Lands were eventually lost once the Band of Nine's efforts began to fail.(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-15)(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2)(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
The [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince) fled for the Disputed Lands rather than become [Prince of Pentos](wiki:Prince_of_Pentos) in [262 AC](wiki:262_AC).(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_pentos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) rode with the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons) in the Disputed Lands before forming his own company.(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Lord [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington), the exiled [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), is said to have drank himself to death in the Disputed Lands.(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-20)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser [Osmund Kettleblack](wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack) claims to have served with the [Gallant Men](wiki:Gallant_Men) in the Disputed Lands, fighting first for [Lys](wiki:Lys) and then [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh).(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

While anchored off the coast of the Disputed Lands, the [Meadowlark](wiki:Meadowlark) is attacked by [corsairs](wiki:Corsairs).(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
The [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) is contracted to fight for [Myr](wiki:Myr) in the Disputed Lands, but they break the contract to support [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)).(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
The [Company of the Cat](wiki:Company_of_the_Cat) and the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown) fought against each other in the Disputed Lands a year ago, but they are both hired by [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) for the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen).(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1)
[Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) leads a portion of the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) along the Disputed Lands before resupplying in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis).(wiki:Disputed_Lands.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)
