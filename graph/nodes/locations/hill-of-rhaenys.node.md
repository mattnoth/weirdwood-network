## Appearances & Description

Atop the hill lies the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit), a cavernous ruin which once housed the [dragons](wiki:Dragon) of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen).(wiki:Hill_of_Rhaenys.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Sunk deep into the hillside are long brick-lined tunnels and undervaults fashioned like caves.(wiki:Hill_of_Rhaenys.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Hill_of_Rhaenys.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
The [Street of the Sisters](wiki:Street_of_the_Sisters) runs between the Hill of Rhaenys and Visenya's Hill.(wiki:Hill_of_Rhaenys.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
Many brothels are found on the [Street of Silk](wiki:Street_of_Silk), which runs northwest from Rhaenys's Hill.(wiki:Hill_of_Rhaenys.cite_ref-Rtloiafking.27s_landing.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) A secret tunnel beneath the hill connects [Chataya's brothel](wiki:Chataya's_brothel) with a stable a few blocks away.(wiki:Hill_of_Rhaenys.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Origins

The Hill of Rhaenys was named after Queen [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen), one of the sister-queens of King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen); the hill's name prior to [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest) has been forgotten.(wiki:Hill_of_Rhaenys.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) The [Sept of Remembrance](wiki:Sept_of_Remembrance) was raised on the hill as a memorial to Rhaenys,(wiki:Hill_of_Rhaenys.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) who was lost during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War).(wiki:Hill_of_Rhaenys.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
In [42 AC](wiki:42_AC) during the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising), King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) destroyed the sept with the [dragonfire](wiki:Dragonfire) of [Balerion](wiki:Balerion),(wiki:Hill_of_Rhaenys.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) as the building had been fortified by [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons) during the reign of King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Hill_of_Rhaenys.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aenys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Hill_of_Rhaenys.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Maegor decreed that a large domed structure, the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit), would be built on the hill for [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen) to house its royal [dragons](wiki:Dragons).(wiki:Hill_of_Rhaenys.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
The [Golden Wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) of Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) and Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) was held in the Dragonpit atop the hill in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC).(wiki:Hill_of_Rhaenys.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
The [Storming of the Dragonpit](wiki:Storming_of_the_Dragonpit) let to the structure's ruin during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Hill_of_Rhaenys.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) The [Shepherd](wiki:Shepherd) attempted to fortify Rhaenys's Hill with his followers, but Lord [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon) scattered his rabble with [knights](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Hill_of_Rhaenys.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
