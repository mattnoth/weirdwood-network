## Origins

### Ancient History

According to a semi-canon source, Kayce was founded by [Gundor Goodbrother](wiki:Gundor_Goodbrother), an [ironman](wiki:Ironman), when [House Casterly](wiki:House_Casterly) held [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Kayce.cite_ref-Gundor_1-2)

Kayce was held by ironborn during the era of the [driftwood kings](wiki:Driftwood_king). The town changed hands when [Herrock the Whoreson](wiki:Herrock_the_Whoreson), a [Kenning of Harlaw](wiki:House_Kenning_of_Harlaw),(wiki:Kayce.cite_ref-Rawoiafkayce.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) used [his horn](wiki:Horn_of_Herrock) to signal the [whores](wiki:Whores) of the town to open a postern gate, allowing Herrock's men to enter the town. The region was then ruled by [House Kenning of Kayce](wiki:House_Kenning_of_Kayce) as part of the dominion of the [Kings of the Rock](wiki:Kings_of_the_Rock) from [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister). Three ironborn kings failed in their attempts to reclaim the town, with Herrock killing two of them.(wiki:Kayce.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### House Targaryen

King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) visited Kayce in [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Kayce.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

According to a semi-canon source, [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), nearly captured Kayce during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons),(wiki:Kayce.cite_ref-Gundor_1-3) and the widow of the [Knight of Kayce](wiki:Knight_of_Kayce) was taken by the Red Kraken as a [salt wife](wiki:Salt_wife).(wiki:Kayce.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Lady [Johanna Lannister](wiki:Johanna_Lannister) was focused on defending Kayce instead of involving the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) further in [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen)'s civil war.(wiki:Kayce.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

At the time of the [regency of Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III), Johanna led a host from [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) and [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport), routing the ironborn beneath the town's walls, with Dalton's uncle slain in the [battle at Kayce](wiki:Battle_at_Kayce). Songs claim Johanna slew twelve ironmen in the fighting, although scholars believe she only carried a banner.(wiki:Kayce.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) When Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) sailed to confront Dalton, ironborn longships gathered along the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea), including Kayce. Dalton was assassinated at [Faircastle](wiki:Faircastle) before Alyn arrived in the west, however.(wiki:Kayce.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

### Recent History

Merchants from Kayce sought loans from [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister) after the [Lord of Casterly Rock](wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock) began to extend and forgive debts.(wiki:Kayce.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
