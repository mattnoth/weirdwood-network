## Appearances & Description

Built on the hill [Great Barrow](wiki:Great_Barrow),(wiki:Barrow_Hall.cite_ref-Rawoiafbarrowton.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Barrow Hall has wooden walls and square towers. There is an old windmill and a grassy courtyard located near the gatehouse. Wide and wooden stairs lead up the hill to Barrow Hall's keep.(wiki:Barrow_Hall.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

[Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) is upset that a feast is held for him by [Harwood Stout](wiki:Harwood_Stout) at [Goldgrass](wiki:Goldgrass) instead of Lady [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin) at Barrow Hall, as Barbrey has banned Ramsay from her castle.(wiki:Barrow_Hall.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), who has called the northern bannermen to [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton), rides through the town to Barrow Hall with [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy). Flapping in the wind above its towers are the banners of Houses [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton), [Cerwyn](wiki:House_Cerwyn), [Tallhart](wiki:House_Tallhart), [Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly), [Locke](wiki:House_Locke), [Umber](wiki:House_Umber), [Flint](wiki:House_Flint_of_Flint's_Finger), [Hornwood](wiki:House_Hornwood), [Stout](wiki:House_Stout), and [Slate](wiki:House_Slate), in addition to four different [Ryswell](wiki:House_Ryswell) banners. Above the northern banners is that of [House Baratheon of King's Landing](wiki:House_Baratheon_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:Barrow_Hall.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Once they reach the keep of Barrow Hall Theon sees the arms of [House Dustin](wiki:House_Dustin) and the quartered arms of Lady Barbrey, whom Theon meets before the keep's hearth.(wiki:Barrow_Hall.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
