## Appearances & Description

The River Gate is near the center of the southeast wall and connects [Fishmonger's Square](wiki:Fishmonger's_Square) and [the wharfs](wiki:Harbor_of_King's_Landing) on the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush).(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Rtloiafking.27s_landing.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) It has a portcullis.(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The [Street of Steel](wiki:Street_of_Steel) begins at the market square beside the River Gate,(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) and the [River Row](wiki:River_Row) runs east and west from the River Gate.(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Rtloiafking.27s_landing.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) The [fish market](wiki:Fish_market) is found outside of the gate.(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Rawoiaffishmarket.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Opposite the River Gate on the southern shore of the Blackwater is the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad), which leads to [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End).(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Rtloiafking.27s_landing.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Origins

### Construction[ ]

In [19 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) decreed that new city walls with seven gates, each with a massive gatehouse and defensive towers, be built to protect [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Grand Maester [Gawen](wiki:Gawen) and Ser [Osmund Strong](wiki:Osmund_Strong), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), headed the construction, which took place between [20](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)–[26 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### Maegor the Cruel[ ]

In the reign of [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), Maegor had his own wife and queen, [Alys Harroway](wiki:Alys_Harroway), tortured to death by [Tyanna of the Tower](wiki:Tyanna_of_the_Tower). Following her death, Alys's body was cut into seven parts and mounted on spikes above each of the seven gates of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), including the River Gate.(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### Dance of the Dragons[ ]

During the [fall of King's Landing](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing), part of the civil war known as the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), thirteen [knights](wiki:Knight) from [House Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) and a hundred [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms) held the River Gate for eight hours from attacks without and within the city. Despite this impressive resistance, the other six gates fell swiftly to the [blacks](wiki:Blacks).(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
When a [massive riot](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)) broke out in King's Landing, sailors unable to return to their ships attacked the River Gate. Ser [Luthor Largent](wiki:Luthor_Largent) and four hundred spearmen were eventually able to disperse the rioters, through the gate was badly damaged and a hundred men were slain, including twenty-five gold cloaks.(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) As the riot intensified, the banner of King [Trystane](wiki:Trystane_Truefyre) was raised above the River Gate's battlements, and the bodies of its captain and three of his serjeants were hung from the gatehouse.(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) Ser [Perkin the Flea](wiki:Perkin_the_Flea) feasted his gutter knights on stolen food at the River Gate and then led them to loot along the riverfront.(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) Since the Mud gate was held by her foes, Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) escaped the capital through the [Dragon Gate](wiki:Dragon_Gate).(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3)
The crippled King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) returned to King's Landing through the River Gate in a closed litter.(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### Aegon III[ ]

During the [regency of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III), Ser [Tyland Lannister](wiki:Tyland_Lannister), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), ordered the city gates to be strengthened so they could better resist attacks from within the city walls.(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
When Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) returned to the capital after his [victory in the Stepstones](wiki:Lord_Alyn's_attack), he rode through the River Gate on an [elephant](wiki:Elephant) intended for the [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos).(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Later, thousands rushed through the River Gate to greet Alyn when he returned from his voyage to the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea).(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfeast.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
[Roggerio Rogare](wiki:Roggerio_Rogare) opened a [pillow house](wiki:Pillow_house) known as the [Mermaid](wiki:Mermaid_(pillow_house)) beside the River Gate. During the end of the [Lysene Spring](wiki:Lysene_Spring), [Tessario the Tiger](wiki:Tessario_the_Tiger) was arrested in a dockside tavern near the River Gate.(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### Recent History[ ]

[Gendry](wiki:Gendry) was once almost rode down near the Mud Gate by King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) returning from a hunt.(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

In the aftermath of the [arrest of Eddard Stark](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark), the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) close up all the gates of the city, except for the Mud Gate and the [Gate of the Gods](wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods) where people can enter the city but not leave it.(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
After being dismissed from the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) flees from the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) and escapes from the capital through the Mud Gate, slaying two [gold cloaks](wiki:Gold_cloaks) in the process.(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The commander of the River Gate, Ser [Jacelyn Bywater](wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater), is made the new commander of the [City Watch of King's Landing](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
In preparation for [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s attack on King's Landing, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), tours the riverfront. Carpenters extend wooden hoardings from the battlements of the Mud Gate.(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) [Three catapults](wiki:Three_Whores) are raised in the market square by the gate.(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1)(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
The Lannisters burn the riverfront and retreat to the Mud Gate during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) is at the gate with Tyrion and the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), with the king encouraging the archers on the hoardings.(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Racok60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) The River Gate is assaulted with a battering ram by the army of Stannis.(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Racok60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1)(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) When Ser [Osmund Kettleblack](wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack) informs Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) that the gate is under attack, Cersei orders that Joffrey be brought back to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Racok60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-2) Tyrion and Ser [Mandon Moore](wiki:Mandon_Moore) ride for the Mud Gate after defending the [King's Gate](wiki:King's_Gate).(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Racok61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Chella](wiki:Chella) and a dozen [Black Ears](wiki:Black_Ears) return to King's Landing after the Blackwater, but [red cloaks](wiki:Red_cloaks) chase them away while Kingslanders cheer and throw dung at the [clansmen](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans).(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
[Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) brings [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) to the waterfront, and they find the Mud Gate scarred and splintered from the Blackwater.(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)
Charged with inspecting the damages from the battle, Tyrion judges that both the River Gate and the King's Gate should be replaced.(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard), inspects the city walls and affirms that the King's Gate and the Mud Gate need to be replaced.(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)
Thousands cheer for Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) at the Mud Gate as he leaves King's Landing with his host for [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(300)).(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)
Three thousand [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk), mostly women, crowd through the Mud Gate to see Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) as he sails of [Sweet Cersei](wiki:Sweet_Cersei) for [Dragonstone](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone).(wiki:River_Gate.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)
