## Origins

### Gardener Era[ ]

Some say the river is named after [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly).(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rawoiafmander.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
According to legend, [Garth the Gardener](wiki:Garth_the_Gardener) built [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) atop a hill along the Mander.(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) [John II Gardener](wiki:John_II_Gardener), [King of the Reach](wiki:King_of_the_Reach), sailed his barge to the river's headwaters, receiving homage from lords and petty kings during his voyage.(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_the_gardener_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) The Mander was considered part of the [Reach proper](wiki:Reach_proper).(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Historically, the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) raided up the river Mander all the way to [Stonebridge](wiki:Bitterbridge).(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) [Torgon Greyiron](wiki:Torgon_Greyiron) was raiding in the river when his father, King [Urragon III Greyiron](wiki:Urragon_III_Greyiron), died.(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
King [Garth VII Gardener](wiki:Garth_VII_Gardener) fortified the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands), which sit near the mouth of the river, and armed its [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) to guard the approaches and warn those further inland of coming attacks.(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_iron_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
The headwaters of the Mander were conquered by [Arlan I Durrandon](wiki:Arlan_I_Durrandon), a [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King), but they were eventually lost by the descendants of his grandson, [Arlan III](wiki:Arlan_III_Durrandon).(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_andals_in_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) A [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) king once sacked Highgarden after crossing the Mander.(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
[House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) once lived at [Dunstonbury](wiki:Dunstonbury)(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) and held rich lands along the Mander,(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) but they were driven away(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) by Lord [Lorimar Peake](wiki:Lorimar_Peake) at the behest of King [Perceon III Gardener](wiki:Perceon_III_Gardener).(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1) Despite being exiled, the Manderlys still claim the title [Lord Marshal of the Mander](wiki:Lord_Marshal_of_the_Mander).(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1)

### Targaryen Era[ ]

After the downfall of [House Gardener](wiki:House_Gardener) in the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire), [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) granted [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) to [Harlan Tyrell](wiki:Harlan_Tyrell) and named him [Lord Paramount of the Mander](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Mander).(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
The Mander was said to have been red with blood for twenty leagues after the [battle at Stonebridge](wiki:Battle_at_Stonebridge), part of the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising).(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) According to legend, Septon [Mattheus](wiki:Mattheus) encountered Septon [Barth](wiki:Barth) at [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge) in [50 AC](wiki:50_AC) during their respective journeys to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) and [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1)
Ser [Rickard Thorne](wiki:Rickard_Thorne) was slain while crossing the Mander at Bitterbridge during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), and Prince [Maelor Targaryen](wiki:Maelor_Targaryen) was killed shortly after.(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) Many townspeople drowned in the river during the retaliatory [sack of Bitterbridge](wiki:Sack_of_Bitterbridge) by the [greens](wiki:Greens).(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-1)
At a crossing of the Mander during the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), Ser [Quentyn Ball](wiki:Quentyn_Ball) slew all of Lady [Penrose](wiki:House_Penrose)'s sons except for the youngest, whom he spared as a favor to the lady.(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) The [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) and the Mander ran low during the terrible summer drought of [211 AC](wiki:211_AC).(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2)
During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), the [Greyjoys](wiki:House_Greyjoy) and Shield Islanders fought a [battle at the Mander](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mander)'s mouth. Lord [Quellon Greyjoy](wiki:Quellon_Greyjoy) was killed in the fighting.(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

### Baratheon Era[ ]

Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) once crossed the Mander with three [Sand Snakes](wiki:Sand_Snakes) to visit the mother of [Tyene Sand](wiki:Tyene_Sand).(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

During the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) sets his sprawling camp(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) where the [roseroad](wiki:Roseroad) crosses the Mander near [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge).(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Men of the [Reach](wiki:Reach) set up their pavilions north of the river, and the [storm lords](wiki:Stormlands) establish themselves on the southern bank.(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) sees the muddy waters of the Mander when she arrives at the [melee at Bitterbridge](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge).(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
Following the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), forces of Houses [Oakheart](wiki:House_Oakheart) and [Rowan](wiki:House_Rowan) are seen north of the Mander.(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Hoping to marry [Willas Tyrell](wiki:Willas_Tyrell), [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) imagines drifting down the Mander with him on a pleasure barge.(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)
Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) sends [Greenbeard](wiki:Greenbeard) and the [Mad Huntsman](wiki:Mad_Huntsman) south of the Mander to buy provisions for the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners).(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), the new [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North), sends [Torwold Browntooth](wiki:Torwold_Browntooth) and the [Red Oarsman](wiki:Red_Oarsman) up the Mander with a dozen longships to draw away the lords of the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands). The [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) is then victorious in the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields),(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4) allowing the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) to sail up the Mander in strength and threaten the [Reach](wiki:Reach).(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

Ironborn raiders sail up the Mander deep into the heart of the Reach.(wiki:Mander.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)
