## Origins

### Old Empire

Old Ghis was founded by [Grazdan the Great](wiki:Grazdan_the_Great).(wiki:Old_Ghis.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Its emblem was a [harpy](wiki:Harpies), which had a woman's torso, the wings of a [bat](wiki:Bat) instead of arms, the legs of an [eagle](wiki:Eagle), and a scorpion's tail. In its claws rested a thunderbolt.(wiki:Old_Ghis.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

In the dawn of days Old Ghis opened its gates and poured forth its [lockstep legions](wiki:Lockstep_legions) to control [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay) and the [Gulf of Grief](wiki:Gulf_of_Grief). With their lockstep discipline and absolute obedience the [Old Empire of Ghis](wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis) ground nations beneath their boots, destroying that which they did not enslave.

### Fall

Old Ghis was brought to ruin following a [series of wars](wiki:Ghiscari_wars) with the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold).(wiki:Old_Ghis.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Old_Ghis.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Ghiscar fought with Valyria five times, losing each war because of Valyria's [dragons](wiki:Dragon). Ghis's [lockstep legions](wiki:Lockstep_legions) were shattered in the final war five thousand years ago. The city's brick walls were pulled down, its streets and buildings were turned to ash and cinder by [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame), and its fields were sown with salt, sulfur, and skulls.(wiki:Old_Ghis.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

### Aftermath

The city of [New Ghis](wiki:New_Ghis), located on an island south of [Ghaen](wiki:Ghaen) and the [Ghiscari Strait](wiki:Ghiscari_Strait),(wiki:Old_Ghis.cite_ref-Rtloiafcentral_essos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) claims to be Old Ghis come again.(wiki:Old_Ghis.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_rise_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

[Lomas Longstrider](wiki:Lomas_Longstrider) once visited the colossal ruins of the [Great Pyramid of Ghis](wiki:Great_Pyramid_of_Ghis).(wiki:Old_Ghis.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy) sailed to Old Ghis by the age of fourteen.(wiki:Old_Ghis.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_red_kraken.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
