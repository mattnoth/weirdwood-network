## Origins

Development of the Street of the Sisters began in [52 AC](wiki:52_AC) during the reign of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen). A great central square was made in the center of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), and the Street of the Sisters was one of four long and wide streets which sprung from it.(wiki:Street_of_the_Sisters.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Lady [Baela Targaryen](wiki:Baela_Targaryen) took part in drunken horse races along the Street of the Sisters.(wiki:Street_of_the_Sisters.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

During the frenzy preceding the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney), a drunken horse race takes place in the Street of the Sisters.(wiki:Street_of_the_Sisters.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) is in the crowd marching up the Street of Sisters to the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) to go see Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s confession and unexpected execution.(wiki:Street_of_the_Sisters.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) rides along the Street of the Sisters to go see [Hallyne](wiki:Hallyne) in the [Guildhall of the Alchemists](wiki:Guildhall_of_the_Alchemists).(wiki:Street_of_the_Sisters.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
