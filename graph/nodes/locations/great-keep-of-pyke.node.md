## Appearances & Description

The Great Keep is a massive building which dominates the largest islet of [Pyke](wiki:Pyke). A wide stone bridge connects it to the clifftop gatehouse on [the mainland](wiki:Pyke_(island)), and other bridges lead to the Kitchen Keep and the [Bloody Keep](wiki:Bloody_Keep) on their own islands.(wiki:Great_Keep_of_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The Great Hall is long and smoky.(wiki:Great_Keep_of_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair) is located on a dais in the hall.(wiki:Great_Keep_of_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), hosts a feast in the Great Hall for the captains and [bannermen](wiki:Bannermen) who have mustered for his invasion of the [north](wiki:North).(wiki:Great_Keep_of_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) The gathered guests laugh at [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) after he is mocked by his sister, [Asha](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy).(wiki:Great_Keep_of_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
