## Culture

Fisherfolk from [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) sail the Bite,(wiki:Bite.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and [Sistermen](wiki:Sistermen) have a reputation for smuggling and wrecking.(wiki:Bite.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Origins

[Pirate kings](wiki:King_of_the_Three_Sisters) of the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters) controlled the Bite and neighboring waters in antiquity.(wiki:Bite.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn), the [Kings of Mountain and Vale](wiki:Kings_of_Mountain_and_Vale), gained control of the Sisters through the [War Across the Water](wiki:War_Across_the_Water).(wiki:Bite.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Prince [Jacaerys Velaryon](wiki:Jacaerys_Velaryon) flew on [Vermax](wiki:Vermax) over the Bite to [Sisterton](wiki:Sisterton) and [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor).(wiki:Bite.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) In [132 AC](wiki:132_AC), [Winter Fever](wiki:Winter_Fever) crossed the Bite from the Sisters to White Harbor.(wiki:Bite.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
After the outbreak of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) attempted to return to the [north](wiki:North) with the aid of a fisherman from the [Fingers](wiki:Fingers). The fisherman drowned during a storm, but his daughter was able to transport Ned to the Three Sisters. Some say Lord Stark impregnated the daughter, who gave birth to [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow).(wiki:Bite.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) and Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) sail on [Storm Dancer](wiki:Storm_Dancer) from [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Strong winds in the Bite contribute to Rodrik becoming seasick.(wiki:Bite.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) During his coma, [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) sees his mother and Rodrik sailing across the Bite during one of his [dreams](wiki:Dream).(wiki:Bite.cite_ref-Ragot17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) tasks Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) with sailing on [Black Betha](wiki:Black_Betha) north from [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) to the Bite, delivering the king's letters to settlements along the way.(wiki:Bite.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

While sailing for [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), [Sloe-Eyed Maid](wiki:Sloe-Eyed_Maid) is blown by a gale into the Bite, and the ship is wrecked at [Sweetsister](wiki:Sweetsister).(wiki:Bite.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)
The remnants of [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan)'s fleet sails toward White Harbor with Davos. Tired of storms and Stannis's inability to pay, Salladhor sets Davos loose in a rowboat near [Sisterton](wiki:Sisterton) and sails for the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones).(wiki:Bite.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)
[Lionstar](wiki:Lionstar) transports three [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) to Sisterton and then White Harbor.(wiki:Bite.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Ser [Wylis Manderly](wiki:Wylis_Manderly) returns to White Harbor from [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool).(wiki:Bite.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

## Quotes

For thousands of years, these islands had boasted their own [cruel kings](wiki:King_of_the_Three_Sisters), pirates and raiders whose longships sailed the Bite, the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea), and even the [Shivering Sea](wiki:Shivering_Sea) with impunity, plundering and reaving as they would and returning to the [Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters) laden with gold and slaves. These depredations finally led the [Kings of Winter](wiki:Kings_of_Winter) to send their own war fleets to seek dominion over the Sisters—for whoever holds the Three Sisters holds the Bite.(wiki:Bite.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)
The [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters) were islands in the Bite. Dunk had heard [septons](wiki:Septon) say that the isles were sinks of sin and avarice.(wiki:Bite.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
There's ships that go between the [Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters) and [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) all the time. We sell them crabs and fish and goat [cheese](wiki:Cheese), they sell us wood and wool and hides.(wiki:Bite.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5)
East of [the road](wiki:Causeway) lay a bleak and barren shore and a cold salt sea, to the west the swamps and bogs of the [Neck](wiki:Neck), infested with [serpents](wiki:Serpent), [lizard lions](wiki:Lizard_lions), and [bog devils](wiki:Bog_devils) with their [poisoned](wiki:Poison) arrows.(wiki:Bite.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
