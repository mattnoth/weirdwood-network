## Origins

The [Red Kings](wiki:Red_King) from [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) of the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort) once ruled lands stretching from the Last River to the [White Knife](wiki:White_Knife).(wiki:Last_River.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Umbers](wiki:House_Umber) led by [Hother](wiki:Hother_Umber) and [Mors](wiki:Mors_Umber) come from beyond the Last River to attend the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast) in [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Last_River.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
The Umbers are reported as gathering men beyond the Last River to assist in the recovery of Winterfell from [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy)'s [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn).(wiki:Last_River.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) considers marching from [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) to the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) warns him that Umbers led by Mors would cut his host to pieces when Stannis travels past the Last River and the [Lonely Hills](wiki:Lonely_Hills). Stannis instead travels west to the [northern mountain clans](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans) and [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Retaking_of_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Last_River.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Last_River.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
