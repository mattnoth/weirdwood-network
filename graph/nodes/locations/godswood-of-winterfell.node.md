## Origins

The [godswood](wiki:Godswood) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) may date back ten thousand years.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) According to legend, the face on its [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) was carved by [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest).(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) [Brandon the Builder](wiki:Brandon_the_Builder) is said to have built the castle around the grove(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) and prayed in the ancient godswood.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_winterfell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

According to [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom), Prince [Jacaerys Velaryon](wiki:Jacaerys_Velaryon) married [Sara Snow](wiki:Sara_Snow) before the godswood's [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark), [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell), wed [Alysanne Blackwood](wiki:Alysanne_Blackwood) in the icy godswood in [132 AC](wiki:132_AC).(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) always visits the godswood after taking a man's life.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8) Eddard once ordered one of his sons, [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark), to spend a night cleansing himself in the godswood after climbing Winterfell's walls without permission.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy), a ward of Eddard, skipped stones across the black pool and hunted [squirrels](wiki:Squirrel) with a self-made bow in his youth, and the older Theon often recuperates in the hot springs after sparring with [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) and [Jory Cassel](wiki:Jory_Cassel).(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6) Theon also had some of his first romantic experiences in the grove.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7) [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) once prayed in the sept and in the godswood for more singers to visit Winterfell after enjoying the songs of a traveling bard, but no more came.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) [Hodor](wiki:Hodor) enjoys soaking in the warmest pool.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

## Appearances & Description

The godswood's multitude of trees create a dense canopy over old, packed earth and humus and moss. Common trees includes ash,(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) [chestnut](wiki:Chestnut_tree),(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [elm](wiki:Elm),(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) hawthorn,(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [ironwood](wiki:Ironwood),(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) [oak](wiki:Oak),(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) [sentinel](wiki:Sentinel),(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) and [soldier pine](wiki:Soldier_pine),(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and the wood also has blackberry bushes.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) The godswood contains a meandering footpath of cracked stones.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

At the center of the grove stands an ancient [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) with a melancholy face carved into it, a [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree) standing over a pool of cold black water.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) [Snow shrikes](wiki:Snow_shrike) can be seen when the godswood is covered in snow,(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and [squirrels](wiki:Squirrel) live in the oak trees.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

Across the godswood from the heart tree, beneath the windows of the [Guest House](wiki:Guest_House), an underground hot spring feeds three small pools, with a moss-covered wall looming above them.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

The godswood is enclosed by walls, and is accessed by a main iron gate,(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) wooden doors,(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) and an arched stone door.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) There is a tall sentinel near the armory wall.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

On the night of the Bolton-"Arya" wedding the godswood is nearly unrecognizable to Theon, who "had never seen the godswood like this... grey and ghostly, filled with warm mists and floating lights and whispered voices that seemed to come from everywhere and nowhere," the hot springs shrouding the trees in steam and drawing "grey curtains across the watching windows" (Theon POV, adwd-the-prince-of-winterfell-01 — `sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-the-prince-of-winterfell-01.md:55`).

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Following the execution of the deserter [Gared](wiki:Gared), Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) finds her husband, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), cleaning [Ice](wiki:Ice) by the [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood). Catelyn, a [southron](wiki:Southron), is unnerved by Winterfell's forest.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9) [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) tries to teach [his direwolf pup](wiki:Summer) how to fetch a stick in the godswood.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2)

While Bran contemplates in the godswood, [Hodor](wiki:Hodor) relaxes in the heated pools. [Osha](wiki:Osha) tells the crippled boy about life [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

### A Clash of Kings

Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) confines the direwolves [Summer](wiki:Summer) and [Shaggydog](wiki:Shaggydog) to the godswood after the latter bites [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)). The children later play [lord of the crossing](wiki:Lord_of_the_crossing) at one of the pools. When [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)) knocks [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) into the water, Shaggydog leaps at Walder.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) The direwolves try to escape the godswood by digging under the iron gate, but large flat stones underneath prevent them from doing so.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

Bran has several [visions](wiki:Skinchanger) of Summer in the godswood.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5) The boy's favorite spot is by the weirwood, and on one occasion he finds Osha swimming in the cold pool beneath the [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree).(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

[Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed) and [Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed) come to Winterfell for the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast), and they visit the direwolves in the godswood after Bran goes to bed.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) Meera later practices her skills with the net against Summer in the godswood.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

During the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) by [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) and his [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), Summer unsuccessfully tries to climb a falling tree to escape.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6) Theon keeps the direwolves in the godswood after declaring himself [Prince of Winterfell](wiki:Prince_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7)(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Osha and Hodor bring the direwolves with them when they help Bran and Rickon escape Winterfell.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

[Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) orders the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell) by [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton), but the damp soil and green wood of the trees leads to only a few pines being scorched in the godswood. After emerging from the [crypts of Winterfell](wiki:Crypts_of_Winterfell), Bran find Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin) dying beneath the heart tree. Osha gives him the [gift of mercy](wiki:Gift_of_mercy).(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Storm of Swords

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), hiding at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), uses twigs and fallen branches to represent the godswood while making a snow castle of Winterfell.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

After the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) offers to name [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) as [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos76.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Jon declines, however, as [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) would require him to burn the godswood's heart tree.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Wex Pyke](wiki:Wex_Pyke), taken into the custody of [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly), reveals to Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) and [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover) that he survived the sack of Winterfell by hiding in the leaves of the godswood's [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree), and that he saw Bran and his companions alive as well.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

The [three-eyed crow](wiki:Three-eyed_crow) trains Bran in [a cave](wiki:Cave_of_the_three-eyed_crow) found [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall). Looking through the eyes of the heart tree, Bran has visions of events which occurred in the godswood in the past.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

In the aftermath of the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) and [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) being named [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), the [wedding of Ramsay Bolton and Arya Stark](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark) (actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)) is held in Winterfell's misty, candle-lit godswood. The hot springs prevent the ground from freezing.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8)

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy), [Ramsay's](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) prisoner, is allowed to wander the castle. He frequently visits the godswood and observes it is often full of praying [northmen](wiki:Northmen) during daylight.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) During one visit, Theon thinks he hears the weirwood's leaves whisper his name as well as that of Bran.(wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1)
