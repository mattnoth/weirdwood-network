## Origins

After the [Rhoynish Wars](wiki:Rhoynish_Wars), [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria) led the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar) in their [ten thousand ships](wiki:Ten_thousand_ships) to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), where she wed the Lord of the Sandship, [Mors Martell](wiki:Mors_Martell), a lesser lord of Dorne. The alliance of the Martells and the Rhoynar allowed Nymeria and Mors to conquer the rest of Dorne in [Nymeria's War](wiki:Nymeria's_War), however. The Princes and Princesses of Dorne made their seat [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear), the great castle which developed around the Sandship.(wiki:Sandship.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_sunspear.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
