## Origins

The [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar) built Sarhoy as a great port city made of pink stone with canals and saltwater gardens. During the [Second Spice War](wiki:Second_Spice_War), three [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlords) and men from [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) sacked the city. The city's warriors were slaughtered and its children sold into [slavery](wiki:Slavery). The Volantenes burned the city and salted the earth. After further defeats, Princess [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria) led her [ten thousand ships](wiki:Ten_thousand_ships) down the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne) past Sarhoy and into the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea).(wiki:Sarhoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

According to Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel), Sarhoy "remains in ruins to this day, a desolate and haunted place."(wiki:Sarhoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_volantis.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
