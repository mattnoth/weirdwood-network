## Appearances & Description

The village is inhabited by crofters and is located between two lakes. It consists of a few huts, a longhall, and a watchtower by the lakeshore.(wiki:Crofters'_village.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) The larger lake is dotted with small wooded islands, one of which has an ancient [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree) growing on it. The longhall has space to comfortably seat fifty men.(wiki:Crofters'_village.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Slowed by a blizzard while [traveling](wiki:March_on_Winterfell) through the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) from [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), the army of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) stops at the crofters' village. They find it abandoned, a mean and meagre place.(wiki:Crofters'_village.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) They make camp there, but the snowstorm buries the huts below mounds of snow.(wiki:Crofters'_village.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Crofters'_village.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Though [Benjicot Branch](wiki:Benjicot_Branch) thought they were about three days away from Winterfell,(wiki:Crofters'_village.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) the blizzard immobilizes Stannis's army. They have limited success ice-fishing from the frozen lakes. On the eleventh day of the standstill, Stannis's army is bolstered by the arrival of [Arnolf Karstark](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark) and his men, but Arnolf only brings enough supplies for his own. On the fifteenth day, Stannis's young squire [Bryen Farring](wiki:Bryen_Farring) succumbs to the cold and hunger, and his body is burnt in a funeral pyre.(wiki:Crofters'_village.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
On the nineteenth day, the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) build a pinewood pyre on the village green to burn four soldiers from [House Peasebury](wiki:House_Peasebury) who were caught [eating human flesh](wiki:Cannibalism). Later that day, six [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) and two brothers of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) bring [Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris), [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole), and [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) to the village, much to the surprise of Theon's sister, [Asha](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy).(wiki:Crofters'_village.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

Theon is kept prisoner by Stannis inside the watchtower. Informed by a letter from [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), that Arnolf plots betrayal, Stannis has the [Karstarks](wiki:House_Karstark) taken into custody. Rather than have Theon burned as a sacrifice to the [Lord of Light](wiki:Lord_of_Light), Asha suggests to Stannis that he instead have Theon beheaded at the island's [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) as a sacrifice to the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods).(wiki:Crofters'_village.cite_ref-4)
