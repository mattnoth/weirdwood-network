## Origins

The founder of [House Dayne](wiki:House_Dayne) is said to have raised [Starfall](wiki:Starfall) on an island at the river's mouth, having tracked a falling star there. The Daynes ruled as [Kings of the Torrentine](wiki:Kings_of_the_Torrentine) from the days of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men)(wiki:Torentine.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_kingdoms_of_the_first_men.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) until the defeat of King [Vorian Dayne](wiki:Vorian_Dayne) in [Nymeria's War](wiki:Nymeria's_War).(wiki:Torentine.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_the_coming_of_the_rhoynar.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
There were reports that [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) and her dragon, [Dreamfyre](wiki:Dreamfyre), were seen as far south as the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) and the canyons of the Torrentine during her search for the missing [Aerea Targaryen](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen).(wiki:Torentine.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
