## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

It is at Rushing Falls that Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch) claims to have killed Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion).(wiki:Rushing_Falls.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Lem](wiki:Lem) tells the [maester](wiki:Maester) of [Lord Lychester's keep](wiki:Lord_Lychester's_keep) that [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr) cut down the hanged Beric before he died.(wiki:Rushing_Falls.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Beric recalls that Amory had captured a pair of innocent beekeepers at Rushing Falls and told Beric that he intended to kill them if Beric did not surrender himself. Beric did and Amory hanged him between the beekeepers, but Beric was revived by Thoros.(wiki:Rushing_Falls.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
