## Origins

Skagos is known as the isle of unicorns and cannibals, with treacherous waters: the galleys *Oledo* and *Old Mother's Son* were driven onto its rocks, "the isle of unicorns and cannibals where even the Blind Bastard had feared to land." (adwd-davos-01:49)

According to [The Edge of the World](wiki:The_Edge_of_the_World) by Maester [Balder](wiki:Balder), in ancient days men of Skagos sailed to the nearby island [Skane](wiki:Skane), seizing all the women, killing all the men, and [feasting on their flesh](wiki:Cannibalism) for a fortnight. The smaller isle has reportedly been uninhabited since the [Feast of Skane](wiki:Feast_of_Skane).(wiki:Skagos.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-9)(wiki:Skagos.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Some attribute the destruction of [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome) to cannibals from Skagos.(wiki:Skagos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_wall_and_beyond:_the_wildlings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
The people of Skagos tended to cross the [Bay of Seals](wiki:Bay_of_Seals) to trade and raid on the mainland until [Brandon IX Stark](wiki:Brandon_IX_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), broke their power, destroying their ships and forbidding them the sea.(wiki:Skagos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_stoneborn_of_skagos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
It is not known when Skagos ultimately became subject to the supremacy of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), but they rose in rebellion against [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) during the rule of King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen). The rebellion lasted for years and claimed the lives of thousands, among them Lord [Barthogan Stark](wiki:Barthogan_Stark), before being put down.(wiki:Skagos.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_stoneborn_of_skagos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

With [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) aboard, the carrack [Summer's Dream](wiki:Summer's_Dream) sails from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown), the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters), Skagos, and [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea).(wiki:Skagos.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Sailing from Eastwatch to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), [Blackbird](wiki:Blackbird) passes the shores of Skagos. [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) sees a wrecked galley on the rocks, with bodies of crewmen being consumed by [rooks](wiki:Rook_(bird)) and [crabs](wiki:Crab).(wiki:Skagos.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-10)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The galleys [Oledo](wiki:Oledo) and [Old Mother's Son](wiki:Old_Mother's_Son) are driven onto the rocks of Skagos after setting sail from the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Skagos.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) [Cotter Pyke](wiki:Cotter_Pyke) writes that [Storm Crow](wiki:Storm_Crow) spotted the wreckage of a galley along the coast of the island.(wiki:Skagos.cite_ref-Radwd21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) learns from [Wex Pyke](wiki:Wex_Pyke) that [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) is alive and has been taken to Skagos by [Osha](wiki:Osha). Wyman tasks Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) with retrieving the boy.(wiki:Skagos.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Skagos.cite_ref-Rawoiafrickon_stark.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
