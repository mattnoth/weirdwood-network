## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

After the [melee at Bitterbridge](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge), Lord [Lorent Caswell](wiki:Lorent_Caswell) hosts a farewell feast in the Great Hall for the departing [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon). [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) is seated on the dais between Lord [Mathis Rowan](wiki:Mathis_Rowan) and Ser [Jon Fossoway](wiki:Jon_Fossoway), and Catelyn's retinue are squeezed onto the crowded benches with Renly's knights. A plump [fool](wiki:Fool) and a [dwarf](wiki:Dwarf) entertain the guests by pretending to be Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Great_Hall_(Bitterbridge).cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
