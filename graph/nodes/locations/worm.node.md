### A Storm of Swords[ ]

On her way to the [Plaza of Punishment](wiki:Plaza_of_Punishment) to collect the [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) she has agreed to purchase, [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) leads her tattered band up along the Worm. She glimpses children playing on one of the wooded islands, darting amongst elegant [marble](wiki:Marble) statues. On another island two lovers kiss in the shade of tall green trees, with no more shame than [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) at a wedding. Without clothing Daenerys cannot tell if they are [slaves](wiki:Slave) or free.(wiki:Worm.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
