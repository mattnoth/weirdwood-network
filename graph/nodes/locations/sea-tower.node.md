## Appearances & Description

The oldest and outmost building at [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), the round and tall Sea Tower is located on a crooked pillar. Its base has turned white from salt spray, and its upper stories are green with lichen. The nightly watchfire has turned its jagged crown black with soot. The banner of [House Greyjoy](wiki:House_Greyjoy) flies over the building.(wiki:Sea_Tower.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The Sea Tower is accessible from the [Great Keep](wiki:Great_Keep_of_Pyke) through three increasingly narrow bridges.(wiki:Sea_Tower.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Its door is grey wood studded with [iron](wiki:Iron).(wiki:Sea_Tower.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) The solar of the [Lord Reaper of Pyke](wiki:Lord_Reaper_of_Pyke) is found in the Sea Tower.(wiki:Sea_Tower.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) The halls of the [Bloody Keep](wiki:Bloody_Keep) are larger and better furnished than those of the Sea Tower, but both buildings are still cold and damp.(wiki:Sea_Tower.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) Howling wind and crashing waves can be heard from the Sea Tower.(wiki:Sea_Tower.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Origins

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) had a snug bedchamber in the Sea Tower in his youth.(wiki:Sea_Tower.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) During [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion), Theon watched the [siege of Pyke](wiki:Siege_of_Pyke) from the tower.(wiki:Sea_Tower.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)
Before moving to [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers), Lady [Alannys Harlaw](wiki:Alannys_Harlaw) would often get splinters in her feet by walking barefoot the swaying plank bridge which leads to the Sea Tower.(wiki:Sea_Tower.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Instead of staying in the Sea Tower upon his return to [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) is given a suite in the [Bloody Keep](wiki:Bloody_Keep). Theon then meets his father [Balon](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), in his solar within the Sea Tower. Balon burns the letter from [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) in a brazier.(wiki:Sea_Tower.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)
Balon holds a war council in his solar after feasting with his bannermen in the [Great Keep](wiki:Great_Keep_of_Pyke).(wiki:Sea_Tower.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Balon is reported to have fallen to his death from one of Pyke's bridges.(wiki:Sea_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Sea_Tower.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) recalls having spoken with Balon in the Sea Tower about Theon before his brother's death.(wiki:Sea_Tower.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
