## Appearances & Description

The heart of Stoney Sept is a market square with a fountain in the shape of a leaping [trout](wiki:Trout). A [brothel](wiki:Brothel) known as the [Peach](wiki:Peach) is located on the east side of the market.(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
The town's namesake old [sept](wiki:Sept) sits on a hill. Below it is a stout [holdfast](wiki:Holdfast) made of grey stone.(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) There are many bells in Stoney Sept, including [bronze](wiki:Bronze) bells and [silver](wiki:Silver) chimes.(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Origins

The bells of Stoney Sept rang in celebration after King [Harwyn Hoare](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare)'s victory over King [Arrec Durrandon](wiki:Arrec_Durrandon) in the [battle at Fairmarket](wiki:Battle_at_Fairmarket), but instead of granting the rivermen independence, Harwyn subsequently claimed the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) for himself.(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Stoney Sept was the gathering place for [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) and his sisters, [Visenya](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) and [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen), prior to the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire).(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Hundreds of [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons) from Stoney Sept fought against King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) at the Great Fork of the [Blackwater](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) during the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising).(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) While in the town, Septon [Moon](wiki:Moon_(septon)) denounced Maegor's plans to wed three wives at the same time.(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
When King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) visited Stoney Sept in [53 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), [Jennis Templeton](wiki:Jennis_Templeton) held a [women's court](wiki:Women's_court) in the town on behalf of Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen).(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
[Widow Fairs](wiki:Widow_Fairs) were held at Stoney Sept after the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Septon [Eustace](wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)) was born in Stoney Sept, and returned to the town after being dismissed by Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake).(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
It was rumored that Ser [Bennis of the Brown Shield](wiki:Bennis_of_the_Brown_Shield) once killed a knight near Stoney Sept.(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) and Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) were in the town prior to the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion). A [hunchbacked septon](wiki:Hunchbacked_septon) spoke [treason](wiki:Treason) in the town square about Lord [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers), and his head was displayed above the town gates after his execution.(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) was wounded in the [Battle of Ashford](wiki:Battle_of_Ashford), and sheltered in Stoney Sept, hiding from a royal army led by Lord [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Jon's men searched from house to house, and hostages were hung in [crow cages](wiki:Crow_cage),(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) but the townsfolk moved Robert from one hiding place to another, including the [Peach](wiki:Peach).(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) When Robert's allies, Lords [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) and [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), arrived at Stoney Sept, Robert defeated Jon in the [Battle of the Bells](wiki:Battle_of_the_Bells), forcing the Hand to retreat.(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2) After the battle, Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) and Ser [Jonothor Darry](wiki:Jonothor_Darry) rode to Stoney Sept to rally surviving royalists.(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser [Wilbert](wiki:Wilbert) is slain by [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) raiders early in the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) Wilbert's sons ride to the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) with [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) Lannisters attack the household of the [Mad Huntsman](wiki:Mad_Huntsman), raping his wife and his sister.(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6) Besides Lannisters, Stoney Sept is also attacked by [northmen](wiki:North) and [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions).(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7)
The town is supportive of the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners). Lady [Ravella Smallwood](wiki:Ravella_Smallwood) tells [Harwin](wiki:Harwin) there is famine around Stoney Sept and the [Threepenny Wood](wiki:Threepenny_Wood).(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) When [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) visits the town with the brotherhood, she finds its gates have been rebuilt with new wood and every third house is burned and empty. An alehouse has been reduced to rubble, and the [Peach](wiki:Peach)'s roof is rebuilt.(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8)
After the Mad Huntsman captures northmen accused of crimes committed at [Tumbler's Falls](wiki:Tumbler's_Falls), he imprisons them in [crow cages](wiki:Crow_cage) in Stoney Sept's square. [Anguy](wiki:Anguy) puts the surviving prisoners out of their misery.(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-9) Arya and her [outlaw](wiki:Outlaw) companions stay overnight at the Peach, and the next day the Mad Huntsman returns with a captured [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane).(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-10)(wiki:Stoney_Sept.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
