## Appearances & Description

From the outside the Purple Lotus is a low brick hovel squatting amidst a row of similar hovels in the shadow of the great yellow-and-green [pyramid of Rhazdar](wiki:Pyramid_of_Rhazdar). A purple lotus is painted on its weathered wooden door.(wiki:Purple_Lotus.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Within, the Purple Lotus is actually a large space consisting of one long hall stretching left and right past adjoining hovels. At the far end of the room is a [fighting pit](wiki:Fighting_pit) in which naked [pit fighters](wiki:Pit_fighters) duel with knives while watchers cheer them on.(wiki:Purple_Lotus.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
The undercellar of the Purple Lotus is a brick vault located below a flight of steep and twisting wooden steps. Wooden [wine](wiki:Wine) vats line the vaults of the cellar, which is three times as large as the winesink above. An overturned barrel can serve as a table. The vault is lit by a red lantern and a black candle.(wiki:Purple_Lotus.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The clandestine meeting between the [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince) and [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) takes place at the Purple Lotus, with Quentyn providing the password "freedom" in order to gain admittance to the winesink. [Zahrina](wiki:Zahrina) has them descend to the undercellar. Quentyn is accompanied by Ser [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater) and Ser [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood), while the Tattered Prince is joined by [Caggo](wiki:Caggo), [Meris](wiki:Meris), and [Denzo D'han](wiki:Denzo_D'han). After apologizing for having previously misled the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown), Quentyn asks the captain for aid in stealing one of the [dragons](wiki:Dragon) of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen).(wiki:Purple_Lotus.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
