## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Kraznys mo Nakloz](wiki:Kraznys_mo_Nakloz) agrees to trade [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) for one of the [dragons](wiki:Dragon) of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), but he is killed by [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) during the [fall of Astapor](wiki:Fall_of_Astapor).(wiki:Pyramid_of_Nakloz.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

### A Dace with Dragons[ ]

During the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor) by [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai), the pyramid of Nakloz is sacked and burned by those blaming [Astapor](wiki:Astapor)'s troubles on the late Kraznys.(wiki:Pyramid_of_Nakloz.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
