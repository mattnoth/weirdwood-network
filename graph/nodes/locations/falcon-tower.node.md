### The Winds of Winter[ ]

When Ser [Harrold Hardyng](wiki:Harrold_Hardyng) arrives at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) for the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) which will determine the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights), he is given chambers in the Falcon Tower, far from those of Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn). [Alayne Stone](wiki:Sansa_Stark) offers to escort Harrold to his rooms, but he coldly refuses the girl.(wiki:Falcon_Tower.cite_ref-TWOW_1-1)
