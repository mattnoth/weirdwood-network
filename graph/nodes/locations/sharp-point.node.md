## Origins

Sharp Point was founded during the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) by the warlord [Togarion Bar Emmon](wiki:Togarion_Bar_Emmon), who established a new kingdom on [Massey's Hook](wiki:Massey's_Hook). The peninsula was eventually recovered by the [Storm Kings](wiki:Storm_King) of [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon), however, and included within the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands).(wiki:Sharp_Point.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_andals_in_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Although sworn to King [Argilac the Arrogant](wiki:Argilac_Durrandon) of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), Lord Bar Emmon had stronger ties to Lord [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), and the Bar Emmons supported the [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Sharp_Point.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The castle became part of the newly-created [crownlands](wiki:Crownlands) and are now sworn to Dragonstone.(wiki:Sharp_Point.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
