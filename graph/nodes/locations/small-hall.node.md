## Appearances & Description

The Small Hall is a long room with a high-vaulted ceiling and bench space for two hundred.(wiki:Small_Hall.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) It has trestled tables(wiki:Small_Hall.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) and great wooden doors.(wiki:Small_Hall.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
The [Queen's Ballroom](wiki:Queen's_Ballroom) is half as big as the Small Hall.(wiki:Small_Hall.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) resides in the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand) after becoming [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). Eddard, his daughters [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark), and his retinue take their meals in the Small Hall.(wiki:Small_Hall.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
[Syrio Forel](wiki:Syrio_Forel) trains Arya in the [water dance](wiki:Water_dance) in the cleared Small Hall.(wiki:Small_Hall.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)(wiki:Small_Hall.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Ser [Meryn Trant](wiki:Meryn_Trant) and [House Lannister guards](wiki:House_Lannister_guards) attempt to seize Arya during the [arrest of Eddard Stark](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark), but Syrio defends the girl and allows her to escape the hall.(wiki:Small_Hall.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), acting Hand of the King to [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), has Lord [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) seized by [gold cloaks](wiki:Gold_cloaks) during dinner in the Small Hall. Tyrion then meets with Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) in the hall. (wiki:Small_Hall.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Tyrion takes a meal in the Small Hall with some of his protectors, [Stone Crows](wiki:Stone_Crows) and [Moon Brothers](wiki:Moon_Brothers).(wiki:Small_Hall.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After Tyrion marries Sansa in the [royal sept](wiki:Royal_sept), [their wedding feast](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark) is held in the Small Hall.(wiki:Small_Hall.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[The wedding feast](wiki:Wedding_of_Tommen_I_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) for King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) is held in the Small Hall. Afterward, Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) has the Tower of the Hand ruined with [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire).(wiki:Small_Hall.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
