## Appearances & Description

Forty-four brothers live at the prosperous [septry](wiki:Septry) beneath a wooded ridge. They tend to a dozen milk cows and a bull, a hundred [beehives](wiki:Bee), a vineyard, an apple arbor, a mill, a [brewhouse](wiki:Beer), and stables. The septry has wooden walls and a heavy [slate](wiki:Slate) roof.(wiki:Burning_septry.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

The septry is sacked during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) by [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister), who take [wine](wiki:Wine) and honey, slaughter the cows, and torch the vineyard. The septry is then visited by other armed bands. Thinking that the [Elder Brother](wiki:Elder_Brother) has hidden gold, a [monstrous attacker](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) has [his men](wiki:Mountain's_men) kill the brothers one by one. By the time the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) defeat the latest invaders, the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions), in the [battle at the burning septry](wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry), only eight brothers remain alive. Because the septry is set afire and collapses beneath the weight of its roof, the brotherhood shelters in the brewhouse, where the contemplative brothers had hidden food.(wiki:Burning_septry.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
