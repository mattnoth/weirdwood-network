## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

After Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) and her party disembark from the [Peregrine](wiki:Peregrine) at Weeping Town, Arianne sends [Feathers](wiki:Feathers) to the Drunken Dornishman in order to gather intelligence, as she knows its common room is rife with rumors.
In the Drunken Dornishman, Feathers hears men muttering that [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) put Ser [Ronnet Connington](wiki:Ronnet_Connington)'s brother, [Raymund Connington](wiki:Raymund_Connington), to death, and raped his maiden sister, [Alynne Connington](wiki:Alynne_Connington). Ser Ronnet is said to be rushing south to avenge his brother's death and his sister's dishonor.
That night, Arianne dispatches the first of her [ravens](wiki:Raven) back to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), reporting to [her father](wiki:Doran_Martell) on all they'd seen and heard.(wiki:Drunken_Dornishman.cite_ref-Ar2_1-1)
