## Origins

### Antiquity[ ]

During the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes), the [Farman](wiki:House_Farman) kings used their longships to guard Fair Isle and the west coast of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) against [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) reavers from the nearby [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands).(wiki:Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
At some point [Gylbert Farman](wiki:Gylbert_Farman) successfully led the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) of Fair Isle in rebellion against the ironborn. [Erich V Harlaw](wiki:Erich_V_Harlaw), [High King of the Iron Islands](wiki:High_King_of_the_Iron_Islands), eventually reconquered Fair Isle, but lost it when he grew old.(wiki:Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
During the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals), Fair Isle was added to the [Kingdom of the Rock](wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Rock) when [Tommen I Lannister](wiki:Tommen_I_Lannister) raised a great fleet and married the last Farman king's daughter.(wiki:Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### Rhaena Targaryen[ ]

Princess [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) sought refuge from King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) with Lord [Marq Farman](wiki:Marq_Farman),(wiki:Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) but she eventually returned to the [crownlands](wiki:Crownlands) to wed the king.(wiki:Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Rhaena returned to Fair Isle after the coronation of her brother, King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Rhaena wed Marq's son, [Androw Farman](wiki:Androw_Farman), in a [small ceremony](wiki:Wedding_of_Rhaena_Targaryen_and_Androw_Farman) in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC).(wiki:Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Elissa Farman](wiki:Elissa_Farman), Androw's sister and a close friend of Rhaena, had sailed around Fair Isle when she was fourteen.(wiki:Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Once lord, Ser [Franklyn Farman](wiki:Franklyn_Farman) forced Rhaena and her companions to leave his island.(wiki:Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
Rhaena returned to Fair Isle during her search for the missing [Aerea Targaryen](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen). Because of Franklyn's poor treatment of Rhaena, King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) did not visit Fair Isle during his royal progress to the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) in [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) The king eventually visited the island in [88 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), after Lord Franklyn had died.(wiki:Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### The Red Kraken[ ]

[Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), captured Fair Isle during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Lady [Johanna Lannister](wiki:Johanna_Lannister) began construction of a fleet to retake the isle, but Dalton burned them in her shipyards.(wiki:Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) After a victory at [Kayce](wiki:Kayce), Johanna sent Lord [Prester](wiki:House_Prester), Lord [Tarbeck](wiki:House_Tarbeck), and Ser [Erwin Lannister](wiki:Erwin_Lannister) with a host to Fair Isle, but Dalton killed them after the westermen's fleet was [ambushed by longships](wiki:Ambush_at_Fair_Isle).(wiki:Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
Following [Lord Alyn's attack](wiki:Lord_Alyn's_attack) during the [Daughters' War](wiki:Daughters'_War), Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake) ordered Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) to sail west and retake Fair Isle.(wiki:Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) Dalton gathered hundreds of longships to Fair Isle and western ports in anticipation of Alyn Oakenfist's fleet.(wiki:Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) After the Red Kraken was murdered by [Tess](wiki:Tess) at [Faircastle](wiki:Faircastle), the ironborn fleet collapsed and returned to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands). Fair Isle's [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) and remaining [knights](wiki:Knight) rose in [rebellion](wiki:Fair_Isle_rebellion), slaying the remaining ironborn.(wiki:Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_red_kraken.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)(wiki:Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)

### Recent History[ ]

During the reign of [Aerys I Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_I_Targaryen), the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) raided Fair Isle, making off with half its worth. Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) and his squire, Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen), considered aiding Lord Farman against Lord [Dagon Greyjoy](wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy).(wiki:Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
[Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) defeated [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy), [Lord Captain of the Iron Fleet](wiki:Lord_Captain_of_the_Iron_Fleet), in a [sea battle off Fair Isle](wiki:Sea_battle_off_Fair_Isle) during [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion).(wiki:Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
Sailing on [Black Wind](wiki:Black_Wind), [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) and [Qarl the Maid](wiki:Qarl_the_Maid) visited Fair Isle and [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) during their voyage to the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor).(wiki:Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
