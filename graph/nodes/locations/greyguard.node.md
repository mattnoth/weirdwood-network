## Appearances & Description

Greyguard has largely collapsed into ruin.(wiki:Greyguard.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Huge stone steps lead to the top of the [Wall](wiki:Wall) above.(wiki:Greyguard.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Origins

Greyguard has been abandoned for two hundred years, and a section of the steps collapsed a century ago.(wiki:Greyguard.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), plans to re-garrison the castle with men from the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower), but on the advice of [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand), they instead choose [Stonedoor](wiki:Stonedoor) due to Greyguard's dire straits.(wiki:Greyguard.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

The party of [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) that [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) is attached to stays overnight in [a cave](wiki:Cave_near_Greyguard) between Stonedoor and Greyguard.(wiki:Greyguard.cite_ref-Rasos26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) They climb the [Wall](wiki:Wall) the following day,(wiki:Greyguard.cite_ref-Rasos30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and they descend at Greyguard.(wiki:Greyguard.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Once at the castle, [Styr](wiki:Styr) warns his raiders that they will need to kill any [kneelers](wiki:Kneelers) they meet.(wiki:Greyguard.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
Prior to their assaults on [Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) and the [Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), wildlings hack at the gates of Greyguard.(wiki:Greyguard.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Jon Snow, now Lord Commander, decides to re-garrison Greyguard with thirty men—ten from Castle Black, ten from the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) and ten from King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)—under the command of [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt). However, Janos refuses to follow Jon's orders, the second time publicly, which leads Jon to execute him at Castle Black.(wiki:Greyguard.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Jon later considers Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne) as Janos's replacement, but he instead orders Greyguard to be re-garrisoned with a grizzled [steward](wiki:Steward) from the Shadow Tower in command.(wiki:Greyguard.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
