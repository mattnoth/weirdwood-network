## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

After Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) and her party disembark from the [Peregrine](wiki:Peregrine) at Weeping Town Arianne sends [Joss Hood](wiki:Joss_Hood) to the Loon in order to gather intelligence, as she knows the common room is rife with rumors.
In the Loon, Joss learns that half a hundred men and boys from the Weeping Town have set off north to join [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) at [Griffin's Roost](wiki:Griffin's_Roost), including young Ser [Addam Whitehead](wiki:Addam_Whitehead), old Lord Whitehead’s son and heir.(wiki:Loon.cite_ref-Ar2_1-0)
That night, Arianne dispatches the first of her [ravens](wiki:Raven) back to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), reporting to [her father](wiki:Doran_Martell) on all they'd seen and heard.(wiki:Loon.cite_ref-Ar2_1-1)
