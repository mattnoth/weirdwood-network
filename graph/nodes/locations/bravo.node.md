## Culture

Bravos use slender swords that are edged and balanced for the thrust.(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Ragot10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) They dress in colorful finery and some put scented oil in their hair.(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) They often go about in pairs.(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Radwd64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Bravos swagger through [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) looking to pick fights and prove their skills.(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Most will fight with little cause or no cause. Anyone carrying a sword after dark in Braavos can be challenged to a duel. Simply placing a finger on the hilt of a sword while facing a bravo is akin to challenging them.(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Bravos never bother anyone without a sword.(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) Dying bravos often find their way to the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White) to hasten their ends.(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Braavosi bravos are known to fight duels in the names of [courtesans](wiki:Courtesan), such as the [Nightingale](wiki:Nightingale_(courtesan)).(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Bravos prefer the cheapest seats in the fourth and highest balcony of the [Gate](wiki:Gate_(playhouse)).(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-8)
The greatest bravos in Braavos practice a style of sword fighting known as the [water dance](wiki:Water_dance).(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_braavos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) They are known to duel by the [Moon Pool](wiki:Moon_Pool) at night.(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
The most skilled among the bravos of Braavos is by tradition the [First Sword](wiki:First_Sword), the personal guard of the [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos).(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_braavos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
During elections for the [triarchs of Volantis](wiki:Triarchs_of_Volantis), bravos duel each other to the death to defend the honor of candidates.(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Origins

Ser [Herman Rollingford](wiki:Herman_Rollingford), part of Lord [Manfryd Mooton](wiki:Manfryd_Mooton)'s diplomatic mission to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), was slain in a duel by a bravo who was offended by the color of Herman's doublet.(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

While at [Drogo's manse](wiki:Drogo's_manse) in [Pentos](wiki:Pentos), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) spots bravos from [Myr](wiki:Myr), Pentos, and [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh).(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) has [Mikken](wiki:Mikken), the blacksmith of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), make a thin sword in the style of those used by bravos for [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark). Arya names the blade [Needle](wiki:Needle).(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Ragot10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) is confronted by two bravos, [Orbelo](wiki:Orbelo) and [Terro](wiki:Terro), in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), but Arya—disguised as Cat of the Canals—warns them away.(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4) Bravos in striped satin are among the customers for Cat's seafood.(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) spots bravos before the doors of [wineshops](wiki:Wine) in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis).(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
The [kindly man](wiki:Kindly_man) tells Arya that bravos are swaggering and puffed up with pride.(wiki:Bravo.cite_ref-Radwd64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
