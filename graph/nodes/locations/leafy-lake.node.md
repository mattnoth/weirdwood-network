## Origins

Leafy Lake was once under the domain of [House Osgrey](wiki:House_Osgrey). Since Lady [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber) married Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey) it may or may not now be back under the control of House Osgrey. There was once a cadet branch of the Osgreys, called [House Osgrey of Leafy Lake](wiki:House_Osgrey_of_Leafy_Lake). Ser [Clifford Conklyn](wiki:Clifford_Conklyn) also had a claim on it.
