## Origins

In the [Dawn Age](wiki:Dawn_Age) the kingswood was part of a vast primeval forest which also included the current [rainwood](wiki:Rainwood).(wiki:Kingswood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_the_coming_of_the_first_men.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
The forest as far as the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) was once within the domain of the [Storm Kings](wiki:Storm_King) of [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon). In the aftermath of [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), however, the lands of [House Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon) of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) were bounded in the north by the south bank of the [Wendwater](wiki:Wendwater) and the southern kingswood.(wiki:Kingswood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) The northern portion of the kingswood became part of the [crownlands](wiki:Crownlands) of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).
During the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising), [Pooxy Jeyne Poore](wiki:Jeyne_Poore) led [Poor Fellows](wiki:Poor_Fellows) against King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen)’s forces in the kingswood.(wiki:Kingswood.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Prince [Baelon Targaryen](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) fell ill while hunting in the kingswood in [101 AC](wiki:101_AC), and he died five days later.(wiki:Kingswood.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), [Trystane Truefyre](wiki:Trystane_Truefyre) promised that starving [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) could take [deer](wiki:Deer), hares, and rabbits from the kingswood, but not [boar](wiki:Boar) or elk. (wiki:Kingswood.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Lord [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon) seized the capital and ended the [Moon of the Three Kings](wiki:Moon_of_the_Three_Kings), however.(wiki:Kingswood.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Outlaws in the forest proved troublesome during the [regency of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III).(wiki:Kingswood.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
King [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen) sent siege engines in the shape of dragons to attack [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), but almost a quarter of the kingswood burned when the seven mechanical [dragons](wiki:Dragons_(mechanical)) caught fire.(wiki:Kingswood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
The kingswood suffered several fires during the drought of [211 AC](wiki:211_AC).(wiki:Kingswood.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
The forest was the hideout of an infamous band of outlaws, the [Kingswood Brotherhood](wiki:Kingswood_Brotherhood). In order to turn the smallfolk against the outlaws, Ser [Arthur Dayne](wiki:Arthur_Dayne) negotiated for villagers to have expanded grazing lands, the right to fell some trees, and the right to hunt a few of the king's [deer](wiki:Deer) in [autumn](wiki:Autumn).(wiki:Kingswood.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) was hunting in the kingswood when Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) gave birth to Prince [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Kingswood.cite_ref-Racok52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) is hunting a white hart in the kingswood(wiki:Kingswood.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) when he is mortally wounded by a boar.(wiki:Kingswood.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), opens the kingswood to any hunter willing to cross the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush).(wiki:Kingswood.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
Tyrion sends expert woodsmen and the [Vale mountain clansmen](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) who had accompanied him to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to the kingswood in preparation for [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s imminent arrival. There they prey on Stannis's baggage train and harry his flanks.(wiki:Kingswood.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Leading Stannis's vanguard, Ser [Guyard Morrigen](wiki:Guyard_Morrigen) burns part of the kingswood to smoke out the clansmen;(wiki:Kingswood.cite_ref-Rawoiafkingswood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) can see the fire and smoke from the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Kingswood.cite_ref-Rasos52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), the [Stone Crows](wiki:Stone_Crows) remain in the kingswood.(wiki:Kingswood.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) cross the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) and find its northern edge to be a wilderness of ash and charcoal and dead trees.(wiki:Kingswood.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell)'s party picks autumn flowers and roast [chestnuts](wiki:Chestnut_tree) in the kingswood.(wiki:Kingswood.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), has been informed of scheming by [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister). His son [Trystane](wiki:Trystane_Martell) is to be ambushed by supposed outlaws in the kingswood while traveling in the company of Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Kingswood.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
