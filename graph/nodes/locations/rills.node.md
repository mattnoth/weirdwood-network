## Origins

[House Ryder](wiki:House_Ryder) ruled the Rills as [First Men](wiki:First_Men) kings after the [Long Night](wiki:Long_Night), but they eventually submitted to the [Kings of Winter](wiki:Kings_of_Winter) and became vassals of [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). It is unknown what led the Rills to eventually be ruled by [House Ryswell](wiki:House_Ryswell) instead of House Ryder, but King [Theon Stark](wiki:Theon_Stark) put down a rebellion in the Rills during his reign.(wiki:Rills.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
In the story of the [seventy-nine sentinels](wiki:Seventy-nine_sentinels), deserters of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) fled to the [barrowlands](wiki:Barrowlands) and sought refuge in the castle of [Lord Ryswell](wiki:Lord_Ryswell_(Night's_Watch)), but he returned them to the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Rills.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[Brandon Stark](wiki:Brandon_Stark) enjoyed riding in the Rills, where he met [Barbrey Ryswell](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin).(wiki:Rills.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) includes spearmen from the Rills as part of the force he leaves behind to defend the [ruby ford](wiki:Fighting_at_the_fords_of_the_Trident) from the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men).(wiki:Rills.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sends a [raven](wiki:Raven) to the Rills requesting aid against [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder)'s [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) who are approaching the [Wall](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black).(wiki:Rills.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The [Ryswells](wiki:House_Ryswell) lead men from the Rills in attacking the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) on the [Fever River](wiki:Fever_River)(wiki:Rills.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) and besieging the ironborn-held [Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin).(wiki:Rills.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
