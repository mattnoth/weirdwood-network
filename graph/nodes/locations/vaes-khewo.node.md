## Origins

The greatest of the [Tall Men](wiki:Tall_Men) cities, Sarnath was the seat of the [High King of Sarnor](wiki:High_King_of_Sarnor), who dwelled in the fabled [Palace With a Thousand Rooms](wiki:Palace_With_a_Thousand_Rooms).(wiki:Vaes_Khewo.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
The [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) invaded the [Kingdom of Sarnor](wiki:Kingdom_of_Sarnor) during the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood) which followed the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria). [Mazor Alexi](wiki:Mazor_Alexi) gathered an army of Tall Men to Sarnath after the fall of [Mardosh](wiki:Mardosh), but his host was destroyed by four [khalasars](wiki:Khalasar) at the [Field of Crows](wiki:Field_of_Crows). Within a fortnight, Khal [Loso](wiki:Loso) conquered Sarnath and put the city to the torch.(wiki:Vaes_Khewo.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
