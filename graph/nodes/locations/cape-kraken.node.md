## Origins

In antiquity, [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) lived in the primeval woodland which stretched from Cape Kraken to [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath).(wiki:Cape_Kraken.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_the_coming_of_the_first_men.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Cape Kraken has long been disputed by the [north](wiki:North) and the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands).(wiki:Cape_Kraken.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_mountain_clans.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) [Theon Stark](wiki:Theon_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), drove the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) from Cape Kraken, but [Loron Greyjoy](wiki:Loron_Greyjoy), [High King of the Iron Islands](wiki:High_King_of_the_Iron_Islands), later retook the cape. The sons and grandsons of King [Rodrik Stark](wiki:Rodrik_Stark) then battled the ironmen for the region.(wiki:Cape_Kraken.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The forces of [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) sail past Cape Kraken when they attack the [north](wiki:North) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Cape_Kraken.cite_ref-Rtloiafjourneys.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) leads the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) around the cape toward the [Saltspear](wiki:Saltspear) for his attack against [Moat Cailin](wiki:Fall_of_Moat_Cailin).(wiki:Cape_Kraken.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
