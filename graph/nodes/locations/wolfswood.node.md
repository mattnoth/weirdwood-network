## Appearances & Description

The wolfswood stretches across the northwestern part of the north.(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-2) It extends as far west as [Sea Dragon Point](wiki:Sea_Dragon_Point) and nearly as far south as [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Torrhen's_Square). It is bounded to the north by the [Bay of Ice](wiki:Bay_of_Ice) and to the northeast by the lands of the [northern mountain clans](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans). To the east the wolfswood reaches [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) and extends north and south of the seat of [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark). North of Winterfell the forest meets the foothills of the [northern mountains](wiki:Northern_mountains). In the southeastern corner of the wolfswood south of Winterfell is a lake and a tributary of the [White Knife](wiki:White_Knife). The [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad) also travels north through the eastern wolfswood.(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Rtloiafwesteros.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The edge of the wolfswood is two miles away from the [winter town](wiki:Winter_town) near Winterfell.(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
The wolfswood is made up of [oak](wiki:Oak), evergreen, and black brier,(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) as well as [sentinels](wiki:Sentinel), [soldier pines](wiki:Soldier_pine), and hawthorn,(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) beech, ash, [chestnuts](wiki:Chestnut_tree), [ironwoods](wiki:Ironwood), firs, and many tall [pines](wiki:Pine).(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) [Weirwoods](wiki:Weirwood) are occasionally found isolated or in pairs.(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) The forest contains abundant timber(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) and stone.(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) The wolfswood has rushing streams and blue lakes.(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Rasos9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Besides [wolves](wiki:Wolves), other [animals](wiki:Bestiary) living in the wolfswood include [badgers](wiki:Badger),(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) [bears](wiki:Bear),(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) [boars](wiki:Boar),(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) [deer](wiki:Deer),(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) elk,(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) fish,(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) [foxes](wiki:Fox),(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4) [rabbits](wiki:Rabbit),(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5) [treecats](wiki:Treecat),(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) black [squirrels](wiki:Squirrel),(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) empress [spiders](wiki:Spider), turkeys,(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) and [kites](wiki:Kite).(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Rasos9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

## Origins

According to the traditions of [House Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood), the family once controlled most of what is now the wolfswood before being driven from the [north](wiki:North) by the [Kings of Winter](wiki:Kings_of_Winter), the [Starks](wiki:House_Stark) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). The Blackwoods then settled at [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), where they ruled as kings before the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals).(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) The [Glovers](wiki:House_Glover) of [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) in the western wolfswood were also once kings during the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes), but they were reduced to vassals of Winterfell.(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1)
Parts of the wolfswood were reduced to ashes by [Harrag Hoare](wiki:Harrag_Hoare), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-2)
When Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) visited the north in [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), she hunted elk and wild boar in the wolfswood with Lord [Alaric Stark](wiki:Alaric_Stark).(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
[Jory Cassel](wiki:Jory_Cassel) once brought [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark), and [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) into the wolfswood to fish for [trout](wiki:Trout) in a rushing stream near [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) leads a party to a [holdfast](wiki:Holdfast) in [the hills](wiki:Northern_mountains) north of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) to execute [a deserter](wiki:Gared) from the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). While returning south, they find a dead [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) with six pups near a bridge in the woods.(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
En route to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) travel on the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad) through the eastern wolfswood. They are met by [Yoren](wiki:Yoren) at a wooden holdfast near the edge of the forest.(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) joins his brother [Robb](wiki:Robb_Stark) and [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) when a hunting party ventures into the wolfswood near Winterfell. After Bran becomes separated from the main party, he is attacked by six deserters from the Night's Watch and [wildlings](wiki:Free_folk). Robb, Theon, and the direwolves [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind) and [Summer](wiki:Summer) save the crippled Bran from the outlaws, however.(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

After hearing of the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) threat to [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Torrhen's_Square), Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin) sends [ravens](wiki:Raven) to [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor), the [barrowlands](wiki:Barrowlands), and the deepest parts of the wolfswood with instructions to summon their levies.(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) The ironborn [capture Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) and [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte), however.(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1)(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
After Bran and his companions, [Osha](wiki:Osha), [Hodor](wiki:Hodor), and [Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed) and [Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed), vanish from Winterfell, [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) follows a trail that leads north by northwest into the misty wolfswood. His hunting party passes near treacherous gullies, stony hills, a deserted crofter's cottage, and a flooded quarry.(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-6) Unbeknownst to Theon, however, Bran and his friends have hidden in the [crypts of Winterfell](wiki:Crypts_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2)
[Summer](wiki:Summer) and [Shaggydog](wiki:Shaggydog) hunt down an [elk](wiki:Elk) in the wolfswood and defend their catch from a [treecat](wiki:Treecat).(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-3)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Bran and his companions travel north to the [Wall](wiki:Wall). Rather than take the kingsroad from Winterfell, they detour around the western edge of the hills found north of the castle.(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Rtloiafjourneys.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) Bran is protected within [Tumbledown Tower](wiki:Tumbledown_Tower) while [Summer](wiki:Summer) hunts in the eastern wolfswood. While in Summer's mind, Bran considers himself to be "prince of the green, prince of the wolfswood".(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Rasos9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-3)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

At Deepwood Mootte, [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) is unable to see the [Bay of Ice](wiki:Bay_of_Ice) only five leagues away because of the thick forest.(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) learns in a letter from [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) that crofters from the wolfswood have joined his army after the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) Because of a blizzard, the host of Stannis travels slowly in their [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell), where Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) has his own army. Stannis's men are forced to make camp at a [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village) in the eastern forest.(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)(wiki:Wolfswood.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-2)
