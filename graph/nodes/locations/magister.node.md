## Origins

The [Triarchy](wiki:Triarchy) was ruled by a council of thirty-three magisters,(wiki:Magister.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) with [Lys](wiki:Lys), [Myr](wiki:Myr), and [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) each supplying eleven members.(wiki:Magister.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Magisters, [archons](wiki:Archon), and merchant princes of the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) initially welcomed [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) and [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) after they departed the [house with the red door](wiki:House_with_the_red_door) in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos). The exiles ended up being hosted by [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) in [Pentos](wiki:Pentos).(wiki:Magister.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
