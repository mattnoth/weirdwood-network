## Appearances & Description

Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) keeps his apartments in a stout timber keep below a [rookery](wiki:Rookery).(wiki:Maester_Aemon's_tower.cite_ref-Ragot41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Maester_Aemon's_tower.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The tower contains a library.(wiki:Maester_Aemon's_tower.cite_ref-Ragot41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

The elderly Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) shares his chambers with two younger [stewards](wiki:Stewards), [Clydas](wiki:Clydas) and [Chett](wiki:Chett). Concerned about [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) visits Aemon the middle of the night to discuss his friend's future.(wiki:Maester_Aemon's_tower.cite_ref-Ragot41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Sam is chosen to assist Aemon with the library and with the [ravens](wiki:Raven). Sam is given the cell of Chett, who is reassigned to the kennels.(wiki:Maester_Aemon's_tower.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
The right hand of [Jafer Flowers](wiki:Jafer_Flowers) is stored in a jar of vinegar in Maester Aemon's tower after it is found in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest).(wiki:Maester_Aemon's_tower.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
After [war breaks out](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) in the south, Aemon summons Jon to explain that the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) takes no part in the politics of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms). Aemon has Jon help Clydas with feeding meat to the ravens.(wiki:Maester_Aemon's_tower.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

While on the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), Chett is resentful toward Sam.(wiki:Maester_Aemon's_tower.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
During the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black), three [Thenns](wiki:Thenns) hack their way into Aemon's apartments.(wiki:Maester_Aemon's_tower.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Wounded from the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall)— including black brothers, [king's men](wiki:King's_men), and [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men)—are tended to behind the maester's door.(wiki:Maester_Aemon's_tower.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Sam goes to the rookery to tend the ravens; he has taught them how to say the word Snow.(wiki:Maester_Aemon's_tower.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Jon Snow, now [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), tasks Aemon and Sam with traveling from Castle Black to the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). Sam tells Aemon his fears at the rookery, but Aemon reassures the youth.(wiki:Maester_Aemon's_tower.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Jon visits Clydas, who has remained in the rooms below the rookery.(wiki:Maester_Aemon's_tower.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark) is taken to Aemon's chambers after she arrives at Castle Black, having fled [Karhold](wiki:Karhold).(wiki:Maester_Aemon's_tower.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
