## Origins

The young [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) once saw Lord [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers) and his [Raven's Teeth](wiki:Raven's_Teeth) up the Street of Steel.(wiki:Street_of_Steel.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), and Lord [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) visited [the shop](wiki:Shop_of_Tobho_Mott) of [Tobho Mott](wiki:Tobho_Mott), where they met [Gendry](wiki:Gendry), one of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s [bastards](wiki:Bastard).(wiki:Street_of_Steel.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Investigating Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn)'s death, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) visits [Tobho's shop](wiki:Shop_of_Tobho_Mott) like his predecessor.(wiki:Street_of_Steel.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) commands the blacksmiths of the Street of Steel to build him a great chain he will later use in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Street_of_Steel.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
