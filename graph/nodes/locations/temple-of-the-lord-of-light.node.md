## Appearances & Description

The Temple of the Lord of Light is found along the river road on the eastern shore of the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne), beneath the [Black Walls](wiki:Black_Walls). The enormous temple has numerous pillars, steps, buttresses, bridges, domes, and towers which flow into one another, and its walls are colored in hues of red, yellow, gold, and orange.(wiki:Temple_of_the_Lord_of_Light.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) According to Archmaester [Gramyon](wiki:Gramyon) in [Remnants of the Dragonlords](wiki:Remnants_of_the_Dragonlords), the temple is three times the size of the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Temple_of_the_Lord_of_Light.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_volantis.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
The [High Priest](wiki:High_Priest_(R'hllor)) preaches from a red stone pillar in the great plaza found before the temple. A slender stone bridge connects the pillar to a lofty terrace of the temple. Huge [nightfires](wiki:Nightfire) burn besides the temple's steps, with the pillar standing between them. Crowds can gather in the torchlit plaza to listen to the priests.(wiki:Temple_of_the_Lord_of_Light.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Origins

Triarch [Malaquo Maegyr](wiki:Malaquo_Maegyr) tried to hire the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) to clean out the [red temple](wiki:Red_temple) and have [Benerro](wiki:Benerro) put to the sword, but the [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords) refused.(wiki:Temple_of_the_Lord_of_Light.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

In [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), thousands of [slaves](wiki:Slaves) and [freedmen](wiki:Freedmen) crowd the temple plaza to hear [Benerro](wiki:Benerro) shriek of [bleeding stars](wiki:Red_comet) and a [sword of fire](wiki:Lightbringer) that will cleanse the world.(wiki:Temple_of_the_Lord_of_Light.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) pass through the crowded temple plaza as Benerro preaches one night. Shouts erupt from the crowd as Benerro speaks, with women weeping and men shaking their fists. The atmosphere reminds Tyrion of the day his niece, Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon), sailed for [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) and [the riot that boiled up](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing) as the royal retinue made their way back to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Temple_of_the_Lord_of_Light.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
