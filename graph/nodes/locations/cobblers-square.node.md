## Origins

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the one-handed prophet known as the [Shepherd](wiki:Shepherd) made his first of many speeches against Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and the [dragons](wiki:Dragon) at Cobbler's Square.(wiki:Cobbler's_Square.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The square hosted rallies for the Shepherd's followers (known as his "lambs"). Even some of [House Velaryon](wiki:House_Velaryon)'s sworn swords joined the Shepherd's cause after the news of Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon)'s imprisonment became public. At the high of his power, the Shepherd's lambs numbered as many as 10,000. Eventually, Ser [Luthor Largent](wiki:Luthor_Largent), the [Commander of the City Watch](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing), led five hundred gold cloaks to disperse the Shepherd's lambs. What ensued was [a bloodbath](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)), with Luthor and the captain of the [Gate of the Gods](wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods) among the dead.(wiki:Cobbler's_Square.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) After King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) returned to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), he had 241 of the Shepherd's followers burned along [Hill Street](wiki:Shepherd's_Way) between Cobbler's Square and the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit).(wiki:Cobbler's_Square.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sees a [begging brother](wiki:Begging_brother) preaching on Cobbler's Square against him and his siblings.(wiki:Cobbler's_Square.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) rides through Cobbler's Square along with [Steelshanks Walton](wiki:Steelshanks_Walton), [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn), and two hundred [northmen](wiki:Northmen) from [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) on his way to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Cobbler's_Square.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

When Jaime leaves [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), he sees [sparrows](wiki:Sparrows) gathering up in the square.(wiki:Cobbler's_Square.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
