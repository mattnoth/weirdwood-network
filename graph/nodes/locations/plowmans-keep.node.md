## Appearances & Description

The lord's bedchamber, which contains [Myrish](wiki:Myr) carpet, sits at the top of the keep and has a window overlooking the [godswood](wiki:Godswood). Ser [Raymun Darry](wiki:Raymun_Darry)'s bed has brown velvet draperies and [oakwood](wiki:Oak) posts carved with vines and leaves. The lady's bedchamber adjoins the lord's with a servant's cell between.(wiki:Plowman's_Keep.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
The keep has a gallery.(wiki:Plowman's_Keep.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Its walls were once decorated with tapestries of [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) kings, but they were hidden in the cellar before a visit by King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Plowman's_Keep.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
There are vaults beneath the Plowman's Keep.(wiki:Plowman's_Keep.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Origins

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lady Darry and her younger children hid in the keep's vaults when Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) attacked [Darry](wiki:Darry) with [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar).(wiki:Plowman's_Keep.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

As [Darry](wiki:Darry) has been burned and sacked during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), the steps to the keep and the shutters on its windows are replaced after Ser [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister) takes possession of the castle.(wiki:Plowman's_Keep.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) Old stale rushes have replaced the [Myrish](wiki:Myr) carpet, and the new furnishings crudely made. Ser [Raymun Darry](wiki:Raymun_Darry)'s spacious bed is gone, with Lancel sleeping instead on a lumpy straw pallet by the window.(wiki:Plowman's_Keep.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)
Lancel begins sleeping in the castle's [sept](wiki:Sept), so Maester [Ottomore](wiki:Ottomore) places Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) in the bedchamber atop the Plowman's Keep when the [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard) visits the castle. Jaime observes that the keep's walls are still undecorated since King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s visits. Jaime descends the tower to attend supper in the castle's great hall. While practicing in the [godswood](wiki:Godswood), Jaime recounts to the mute Ser [Ilyn Payne](wiki:Ilyn_Payne) that Robert had stayed in Raymun's bedchamber when the royal retinue was returning south from [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). After Robert passed out on the Myrish carpet, Jaime slept with his twin, Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), in Raymun's bed.(wiki:Plowman's_Keep.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)
