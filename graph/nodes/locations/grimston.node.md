### A Feast for Crows[ ]

During the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), Grimston falls to Ser [Harras Harlaw](wiki:Harras_Harlaw), who planted his standard beneath its walls and challenged the defenders to face him in single combat. After defeating seven men, two of whom yielded, a [septon](wiki:Septon) declared [the gods](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) had spoken and surrendered the castle to Harras.(wiki:Grimston.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Its previous lord, [Guthor Grimm](wiki:Guthor_Grimm), is kept prisoner in Grimston.(wiki:Grimston.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) King [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) names Harras [Lord of Greyshield](wiki:Lord_of_Greyshield).(wiki:Grimston.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
