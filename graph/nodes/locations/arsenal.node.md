## Appearances & Description

The Arsenal is located at the entrance to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos)'s lagoon, just beyond the [Titan](wiki:Titan_of_Braavos). It sits upon a knob of rock, fortified with stone battlements and bristling with scorpions, trebuchets, and spitfires.(wiki:Arsenal.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
The Arsenal houses the defense fleet of Braavos and is the center of shipbuilding for the city. Along its shores are innumerable quays, docks, and wooden sheds holding galleys.(wiki:Arsenal.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The Arsenal's war galleys are all constructed with the same design, allowing shipbuilders to work on separate parts at the same time and thereby create a galley in a single day.(wiki:Arsenal.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_braavos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Origins

During the [Daughters' War](wiki:Daughters'_War), Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) defeated a [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) fleet in the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones), sinking more than thirty galleys and capturing six galleys and eleven cogs. When Lord [Manfryd Mooton](wiki:Manfryd_Mooton) negotiated with the [Sealord](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos) to prevent war after [Lord Alyn's attack](wiki:Lord_Alyn's_attack), the Sealord showed the famed Arsenal to the regent and boasted that Braavos had already replaced their lost ships.(wiki:Arsenal.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
