## Origins

### Antiquity

Originally a rough-hewn unmortared wall built in the fashion of the [ringforts](wiki:Ringfort) of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men), the Bloody Gate was constructed anew during the rule of [Osric V Arryn](wiki:Osric_V_Arryn), [King of Mountain and Vale](wiki:King_of_Mountain_and_Vale).(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [Andals](wiki:Andals) poured through the Bloody Gate into the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) after securing the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn_(valley)).(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_arrival_of_the_andals.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

During the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes), a dozen armies smashed themselves to bits upon the Bloody Gate.(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6) [Halleck Hoare](wiki:Halleck_Hoare), [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers), failed to conquer the fortification three times.(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### House Targaryen

During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) was charged with subduing the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) for her brother, King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen). [Sharra Arryn](wiki:Sharra_Arryn), Queen Regent of the Vale, who ruled in her son's name, the boy-king [Ronnel Arryn](wiki:Ronnel_Arryn_(king)), amassed an army at the Bloody Gate. Visenya, however, simply bypassed the gate and flew on her dragon [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) straight up to the courtyard of the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) to obtain the surrender of [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn).(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

When Lady [Jeyne Arryn](wiki:Jeyne_Arryn) died in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC), her chosen heir was her cousin, Ser [Joffrey Arryn](wiki:Joffrey_Arryn), who had served as [Knight of the Gate](wiki:Knight_of_the_Gate) for a decade. Joffrey was opposed by Ser [Arnold Arryn](wiki:Arnold_Arryn) and [Isembard Arryn](wiki:Isembard_Arryn), however, and a war of succession broke out. Ser [Robert Rowan](wiki:Robert_Rowan) led a royal army of seven thousand or more in support of Joffrey, but they were weakened by [clansmen](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) attacks, cold, and hunger while traveling through the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon). By the time the royal army reached the safety of the Bloody Gate, they had lost a third of their strength, including Robert.(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

### Recent History

At [the wedding](wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully) of Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) and [Lysa Tully](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) informed his brother, Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), that he was going to serve Jon and his niece, Lysa.(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7) Lord Arryn named Brynden as [Knight of the Gate](wiki:Knight_of_the_Gate) when King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) died and [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) became king.(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Rawoiafbrynden_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

[Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams), after being sent up the [high road](wiki:High_road) by Lysa, had his gold, horse, and clothing stolen by clansmen, leaving him only his harp. When he reached the Bloody Gate, the nude bard was forced by the garrison to sing "[The Name Day Boy](wiki:The_Name_Day_Boy)" and "[The King Without Courage](wiki:The_King_Without_Courage)".(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Appearances & Description

The Bloody Gate is located southwest of the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) and northwest of the [Redfort](wiki:Redfort).(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Rtloiafwesteros.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) There are two long parapets built into the stone of the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon). The [high road](wiki:High_road), so narrow where it meets the gate that only four riders can pass, is watched over by twin watchtowers, which are joined by a covered bridge of grey stone that arches above the road. Defenders of the gate can fire through arrow slits in the towers, bridge, and battlements. Beyond the fortification is a steep, rocky trail which descends to the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn_(valley)).(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

[Ravens](wiki:Raven) are kept at the gate, as are shaggy, sure-footed mountain [horses](wiki:Horse) in its stables.(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) The Bloody Gate can provide respite for at least a few thousand men.(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

The commander of the fortification, the [Knight of the Gate](wiki:Knight_of_the_Gate), traditionally asks the question, "Who would pass the Bloody Gate?" to all who would pass through.(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) A [septon](wiki:Septon) is also in attendance.(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) brings [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn), Ser [Donnel Waynwood](wiki:Donnel_Waynwood) leads twoscore [knights](wiki:Knight) and [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms) from the Bloody Gate's garrison to protect them from [Vale mountain clans](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) and escort them to the fortification. Catelyn's uncle, Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully), the [Knight of the Gate](wiki:Knight_of_the_Gate), greets the newcomers when they arrive. While Catelyn continues on to the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) with Tyrion and Brynden, the wounded Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) and Ser [Willis Wode](wiki:Willis_Wode) remain at the Bloody Gate to recover with the aid of a [septon](wiki:Septon).(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8)

Because Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) refuses to send [knights of the Vale](wiki:Knights_of_the_Vale) to aid [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully) when [war threatens](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Brynden resigns as Knight of the Gate to return to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Following [Bronn](wiki:Bronn)'s victory in Tyrion's [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat),(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) Ser [Lyn Corbray](wiki:Lyn_Corbray) escorts Tyrion and Bronn from the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) to the Bloody Gate, where he commands them never to return to the Vale.(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Ragot42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Donnel has replaced Brynden as the Knight of the Gate.(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Storm of Swords

[Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) and [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident), asks that his aunt, Lady Lysa, allow his army to pass the Bloody Gate and take ship from [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown) for the [north](wiki:North), but Lysa refuses.(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

After the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) intends to take [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) along the high road to the Bloody Gate so he ransom the girl to Lysa. However, they turn back in the foothills of the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon) because of frost and snow in the high passes above, as well as the threat of mountain clansmen.(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-Rasos65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Men from the Bloody Gate travel to the Gates of the Moon to compete in the tourney determining the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights).(wiki:Bloody_Gate.cite_ref-15)
