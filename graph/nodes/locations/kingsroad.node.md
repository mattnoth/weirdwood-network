## Origins

In late [62 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) began a project to connect [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with the great settlements of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), with what [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) would call the "kingsroad" being the first of his roads. Construction began in the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood) south of the capital, allowing King's Landing to connect to [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End). Construction continued north of King's Landing to connect the city with the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) and the [north](wiki:North).(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

During winter, food has been brought to the north from the south via the kingsroad.(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

The [Battle of the Kingsroad](wiki:Battle_of_the_Kingsroad) between Lord [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon) and the [Lads](wiki:Lads) was the last battle of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark) led a great host of northmen to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), leading to the [Hour of the Wolf](wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf).(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Since many northmen remained to attend [Widow Fairs](wiki:Widow_Fairs), Cregan returned north on the kingsroad with a much smaller host.(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Ser [Robert Rowan](wiki:Robert_Rowan) led a host up the kingsroad to the [high road](wiki:High_road) to end the war of succession in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn).(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Prince [Lewyn Martell](wiki:Lewyn_Martell) led ten thousand [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) on the kingsroad to the [Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s retinue travels the kingsroad to offer the position of [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), Lord of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell),(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) and they return to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) the same way.(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) accompanies [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark) and [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) north to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

Traffic on the kingsroad south of the [Neck](wiki:Neck) intensifies in anticipation of the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney). Blaming Tyrion for [an attempt](wiki:Catspaw) on her son [Bran's life](wiki:Bran_Stark), Catelyn abducts the returning Tyrion at the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn).(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

With Ned Stark arrested by the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) after the death of Robert, [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) gathers [northmen](wiki:North) to Winterfell. His host is joined by other bannermen as they travel south along the kingsroad.(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) With the [outbreak of war](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), Tyrion's father, closes off the kingsroad in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) Commanding Robb's foot, Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) marches south on the kingsroad but is defeated in the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

### A Clash of Kings

[Yoren](wiki:Yoren) brings a group of recruits for the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), including [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) and [Gendry](wiki:Gendry), from King's Landing.(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Racok1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) They depart from the kingsroad for the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye), however, because of the numerous soldiers and [gold cloaks](wiki:Gold_cloaks) along the road.(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Racok5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Racok9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The kingsroad and Gods Eye become ravaged by warfare between [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) and rivermen.(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

### A Storm of Swords

In response to [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover) and Ser [Helman Tallhart](wiki:Helman_Tallhart) marching on [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale), Tywin sends Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) to cut off their retreat on the kingsroad.(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0) After the [battle at Duskendale](wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale), Robett is captured by Gregor near the kingsroad.(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

Following the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell), soldiers from [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) search the kingsroad for the missing Bran and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark).(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)

Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) leads nine of the most powerful lords of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) to King's Landing along the kingsroad.(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)

A hundred [Thenns](wiki:Thenn_(people)) and sixteen raiders of the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) travel up the kingsroad to [attack](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) from the south.(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)

In the aftermath of the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) controls the kingsroad in the riverlands and [crownlands](wiki:Crownlands).(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) leads a host from King's Landing north to [Hayford](wiki:Hayford) and [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Traveling from Castle Black to [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town), Jon Snow observes newly-carved [heart trees](wiki:Heart_tree) overlooking the kingsroad.(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Radwd21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0) [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark) is found two leagues south of the village,(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0) after which Jon intercepts [Cregan Karstark](wiki:Cregan_Karstark)'s party on the kingsroad before they can claim [guest right](wiki:Guest_right).(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0)

[Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) has the executed [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) garrison nailed to posts on the kingsroad after the [siege of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin) is resolved.(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0) The surrender of the ruined castle allows Roose Bolton to march his host from the riverlands to Winterfell.(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0)

The kingsroad near Winterfell is blanketed by snowfall from a fierce blizzard,(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0) making it impassable.(wiki:Kingsroad.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-1)
