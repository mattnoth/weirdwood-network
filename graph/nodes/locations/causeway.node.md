## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

When the retinue of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) returns to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) from [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) dislikes traveling on the causeway.(wiki:Causeway.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
The [northern](wiki:North) army of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) marches south along the causeway to rescue Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark).(wiki:Causeway.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Robb then divides his forces at the [Twins](wiki:Twins).(wiki:Causeway.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Following the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork), Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) reforms his host of northmen at the mouth of the causeway.(wiki:Causeway.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

While Robb is fighting in southern [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Lord [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) sends his brother [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) with the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) to [capture Moat Cailin](wiki:Fall_of_Moat_Cailin) and block Robb from retreating on the causeway.(wiki:Causeway.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[House Frey](wiki:House_Frey) abandons Robb's cause after he weds [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling) instead of a daughter of Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey). Robb fears that if he marches north on the causeway, he will be caught between Freys and Victarion's [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn).(wiki:Causeway.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
For his planned [siege of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin), Robb intends to have Lord Bolton and the [Greatjon](wiki:Jon_Umber) lead two hosts up the causeway. Meanwhile, Robb would lead a third northern host through the swamps of the [Neck](wiki:Neck) with the aid of the [crannogmen](wiki:Crannogmen), and then reappear north of the Moat to surpise the ironborn.(wiki:Causeway.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Robb is betrayed in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) and killed at the Twins before he can put his plan into effect, however.(wiki:Causeway.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Bolton leads an army of mostly [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) and [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton) north on the causeway toward Moat Cailin.(wiki:Causeway.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) After [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) negotiates the surrender of the ruined castle, [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) has the ironborn garrison flayed and their corpses displayed along the causeway. Ramsay sends riders south on the causeway to inform his father that it is safe to reach Moat Cailin.(wiki:Causeway.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)
