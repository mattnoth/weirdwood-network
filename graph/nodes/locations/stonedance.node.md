## Origins

Stonedance was founded by Lord [Maldon Massey](wiki:Maldon_Massey) during the reign of the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Durran the Ravenfriend](wiki:Durran_Durrandon_(the_Ravenfriend)).(wiki:Stonedance.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_house_durrandon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Stonedance was the seat of Massey kings after they rebelled against the [Durrandons](wiki:House_Durrandon). The Storm King [Qarlton the Conqueror](wiki:Qarlton_II_Durrandon) captured Stonedance after a year-long siege, during which King [Josua Massey](wiki:Josua_Massey) was killed. The [Andal](wiki:Andals) warlord [Togarion Bar Emmon](wiki:Togarion_Bar_Emmon) expelled Qarlton's men from Stonedance and established his own kingdom on [Massey's Hook](wiki:Massey's_Hook), but the region was eventually recovered by the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands).(wiki:Stonedance.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_andals_in_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Although sworn to King [Argilac the Arrogant](wiki:Argilac_Durrandon) of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), Lord [Triston Massey](wiki:Triston_Massey) had stronger ties to Lord [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), and the Masseys supported the [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen) during Aegon's [War of Conquest](wiki:War_of_Conquest).(wiki:Stonedance.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The castle became part of the newly-created [crownlands](wiki:Crownlands) owing allegiance to the Targaryens of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).
