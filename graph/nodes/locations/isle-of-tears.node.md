## Origins

The [Old Empire of Ghis](wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis) founded Gorgai on the southern coast of the Isle of Tears. The [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlord) of the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) captured the city during the Third Ghiscari War and, renaming it [Gogossos](wiki:Gogossos), used it as a penal colony. Gogossos grew powerful during the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood), but the [Red Death](wiki:Red_Death) eventually spread from the city's [slave pens](wiki:Slavery) across the Isle of Tears and other Basilisks.(wiki:Isle_of_Tears.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_basilisk_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
