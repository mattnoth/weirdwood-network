## Origins

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower) led the [siege of Longtable](wiki:Siege_of_Longtable). Lady [Caswell](wiki:House_Caswell) of Bitterbridge sent him the [dragon egg](wiki:Dragon_egg) of Prince [Maelor Targaryen](wiki:Maelor_Targaryen), who had just been murdered in the town, in hopes it might appease him. The siege of Longtable eventually ended when the widowed Lady [Merryweather](wiki:House_Merryweather) yielded. Lord Ormund took the castle's wealth and food before marching for Bitterbridge to [sack the town](wiki:Sack_of_Bitterbridge) and revenged Maelor's death.(wiki:Longtable.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) [Blacks](wiki:Blacks) from Longtable helped to fortify [Tumbleton](wiki:Tumbleton) before the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton).(wiki:Longtable.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

At the outbreak of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) attained Lord [Owen Merryweather](wiki:Owen_Merryweather), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), of Longtable, his titles, and lands for his failure to contain the rebellion. When the rebellion ended, Owen's grandson [Orton](wiki:Orton_Merryweather) persuaded King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) to restore the lordship of Longtable to him,(wiki:Longtable.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Longtable.cite_ref-Rawoiaflongtable.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) but only some of the lands were returned and Robert emptied the Merryweather treasury in compensation.(wiki:Longtable.cite_ref-Rawoiaflongtable.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Orton's son [Russell Merryweather](wiki:Russell_Merryweather) was born at Longtable(wiki:Longtable.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) in [293 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) or [294 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Longtable.cite_ref-6)
