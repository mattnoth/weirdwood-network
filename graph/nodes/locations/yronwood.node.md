## Origins

Yronwood was the seat of the powerful kings from [House Yronwood](wiki:House_Yronwood) in antiquity.(wiki:Yronwood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_kingdoms_of_the_first_men.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) The [Kings of Yronwood](wiki:Kings_of_Yronwood)(wiki:Yronwood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_queer_customs_of_the_south.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) eventually submitted to [House Martell](wiki:House_Martell) during [Nymeria's War](wiki:Nymeria's_War).(wiki:Yronwood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_the_coming_of_the_rhoynar.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

During the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) led a brief siege of Yronwood.(wiki:Yronwood.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) The castle was defended by old men, boys, and women, however, as the fighting men had fled according to the strategy of [Meria Martell](wiki:Meria_Martell), [Princess of Dorne](wiki:Princess_of_Dorne).(wiki:Yronwood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Yronwood was quickly recaptured by [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) after Aegon returned to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Yronwood.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) has been fostered at Yronwood by Lord [Anders Yronwood](wiki:Anders_Yronwood) from a young age.(wiki:Yronwood.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Quentyn was smitten with [Ynys Yronwood](wiki:Ynys_Yronwood) when he first arrived at the castle, but the girl eventually wed [Ryon Allyrion](wiki:Ryon_Allyrion). Ynys's younger sister [Gwyneth](wiki:Gwyneth_Yronwood) is interested in the prince, however.(wiki:Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) [Cletus Yronwood](wiki:Cletus_Yronwood) became Quentyn's closest friend.(wiki:Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
