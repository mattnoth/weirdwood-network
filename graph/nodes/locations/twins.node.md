## Origins

[House Frey](wiki:House_Frey) has held the crossing of the [Green Fork](wiki:Green_Fork) for six hundred years, beginning when a petty lord built a rickety wooden bridge over the narrowest portion of the river.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) It took the family three generations to complete the bridge in stone, after which they built timber keeps on each bank of the river.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-10)

The Freys have grown wealthy by charging a heavy toll on all those who need to cross—a fact which irks many older or more powerful houses. The timber keeps have since been replaced by stone. These castles are what give the stronghold its name, for they are identical.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-11) Given their strong castle, strategic location, wealth, and numbers, the Freys are one of the most powerful [bannermen](wiki:Bannermen) sworn to [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully), now able to raise about four thousand men.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord [Roderick Dustin](wiki:Roderick_Dustin) and the [Winter Wolves](wiki:Winter_Wolves) stopped by the Twins during their journey south to support Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen).(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Lady [Sabitha Frey](wiki:Sabitha_Frey) fled back to the Twins after Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) attacked [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) with [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar).(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) Sabitha, widowed after the death of Lord [Forrest Frey](wiki:Forrest_Frey) at the [Fishfeed](wiki:Fishfeed), joined the host of Ser [Addam Velaryon](wiki:Addam_Velaryon) for the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton).(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) [Widow Fairs](wiki:Widow_Fairs) were held at the Twins after the war.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

In the story of the [Knight of the Laughing Tree](wiki:Knight_of_the_Laughing_Tree), a [crannogman](wiki:Crannogman) passed the Twins by night to avoid being attacked by the Freys.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

[Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey), [Lord of the Crossing](wiki:Lord_of_the_Crossing), claims to have hosted three [kings](wiki:Iron_Throne) during his life.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-12)(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-12) Walder offered to host young [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn) as a [ward](wiki:Ward) at the Twins, but the suggestion was declined by Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) and Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn).(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-13)

## Appearances & Description

The Twins consist of two identical stone castles standing on a margin of the [Green Fork](wiki:Green_Fork) of the [Trident](wiki:Trident), where the river runs swift and deep.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) The castles have high curtain walls, deep moats, and a barbican and portcullis in each.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Channels have been dug to form moats, turning each castle into an island.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) Scorpions can be placed on the battlements.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) Each castle has a courtyard, and the Freys can slide plank bridges from sally ports to cross their moats.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) The castles have high tower windows.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rasos50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

A bridge of smooth grey rock arches between the castles, with the bridge footings rising from within the inner keeps. This bridge is wide enough for two wagons to cross abreast. It is the only crossing point over the Green Fork for hundreds of miles in either direction, from the [north](wiki:North) to the western riverlands. It lies directly athwart the main route from [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

The bridge is guarded in the middle by the Water Tower, which also has command over the Green Fork. This structure has arrow slits, murder holes, and portcullises for defense.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6) Guests of honor can reside in its richly-furnished chambers and at least one suite, and their featherbeds have corner posts carved in the shape of towers. The tower has curved steps.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

The seat of the [Lord of the Crossing](wiki:Lord_of_the_Crossing), located in the great hall of the east castle,(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7) is a chair of black [oak](wiki:Oak), tall and massive. Its back is carved in the shape of two towers joined by an arched bridge.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8)(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) The chambers of the [maester](wiki:Maester) are found in a turret accessible from Lord Frey's hall by a covered bridge.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

An apple orchard and a cornfield are near the eastern castle,(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rasos50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) and hills are found to the west.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-9)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

After [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) seizes [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) at the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn),(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) a contingent of [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) report back to Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey).(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

[Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), the [Lord Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident), is ill at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) and does not attend [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Walder_Frey_and_Joyeuse_Erenford) of Walder and his eighth wife, Lady [Joyeuse Erenford](wiki:Joyeuse_Erenford), at the Twins.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-14)

After Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) is arrested in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) and the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) are attacked by [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister), his son [Robb](wiki:Robb_Stark) leads a [northern](wiki:North) army south. Lord Frey gathers nearly four thousand soldiers and fortifies the Twins, his intentions unknown. To prevent Walder from potentially informing Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) of Robb's movements, Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully)'s archers shoot down any [ravens](wiki:Raven) sent from the Twins.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-15)

Catelyn enters the Twins as Robb's envoy and meets with Lord Frey in his hall. They negotiate an alliance between Houses [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) and Frey, which includes Robb promising to marry one of Walder's daughters and [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) to marry [Elmar Frey](wiki:Elmar_Frey).(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-16)

The Freys contribute one thousand mounted knights and near three thousand foot to Robb's cause.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) The army splits at the Twins, with Robb crossing to the west bank of the [Green Fork](wiki:Green_Fork) to take the horse to Riverrun and Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) keeping the foot to distract Tywin on the east bank. Ser [Helman Tallhart](wiki:Helman_Tallhart) remains at the Twins with four hundred archers and swordsmen to bolster and ensure the loyalty of Walder's garrison of the same size.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-17)

After being defeated by Tywin in the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork), Roose reforms the survivors at the mouth of the [causeway](wiki:Causeway).(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Having gathered his host to the Twins,(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Roose Bolton marries(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) [Walda Frey](wiki:Walda_Frey_(daughter_of_Merrett)),(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) one of Lord Walder's granddaughters, in [a ceremony at the Twins](wiki:Wedding_of_Roose_Bolton_and_Walda_Frey).(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1)(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

Roose marches south and takes the [ruby ford](wiki:Ruby_ford) and the [crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads).(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-2) When Tywin leads his host west to face Robb, Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) orders Helman Tallhart to leave the Twins and assist Roose with [capturing Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal) from its Lannister garrison.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-3)

### A Storm of Swords

Lords [Tytos Brax](wiki:Tytos_Brax) and [Regenard Estren](wiki:Regenard_Estren) are held captive at the Twins after the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

After the [storming of the Crag](wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag), Robb, now [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) and [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident), marries [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling) instead of one of Walder's daughters. In response Ser [Ryman Frey](wiki:Ryman_Frey) leads the angry Frey soldiers with Robb's army in the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) back to the Twins.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) The Freys are Riverrun also angrily depart for their home after learning of the marriage.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) In order to regain the Freys for Robb's cause, Edmure Tully, now [Lord of Riverrun](wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun) and Walder's liege, agrees to marry [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey).(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

After Robb arrives at the Twins from Riverrun amid heavy rainfall, Lord Walder has the young king apologize to the Frey women gathered in the hall. Catelyn asks for food to secure [guest right](wiki:Guest_right). Edmure, Robb, and Catelyn are given guest chambers in the Water Tower and three great feast tents are set up on the eastern bank for Robb's men. Roose arrives from [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) with the remainder of the northern foot, with Ser [Aenys Frey](wiki:Aenys_Frey) having already returned with his Frey contingent.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)

After Edmure and Roslin are wed, the highborn nobles feast in one of the Twins, while knights, lesser lords, and bastards eat in the opposing castle. Common soldiers celebrate in the tents outside. After bride and groom depart Walder's hall, however, the Freys and [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton) betray the Starks and Tullys. Robb and Catelyn are killed in the massacre known as the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rasos52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

As a reward from the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) for the Red Wedding, the Freys are to receive Riverrun, which is still held in Robb's name by Brynden the Blackfish.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) Ryman leads two thousand spears from the Twins to support the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun).(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Captives held at the Twins include the [Greatjon](wiki:Jon_Umber) and Ser [Marq Piper](wiki:Marq_Piper).(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) The Freys take Edmure to Riverrun as a hostage.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

Ser [Arwood Frey](wiki:Arwood_Frey) and the brothers [Harys](wiki:Harys_Haigh) and [Donnel Haigh](wiki:Donnel_Haigh) lead fifty men from the Twins to investigate the [raid on Saltpans](wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans).(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0) Lord Walder sends men to hunt outlaws like the [Hound](wiki:Hound).(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

Ryman's tent outside Riverrun is decorated to resemble the Twins.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0) While riding back to the Twins from Riverrun, Ryman and his party of three knights and twelve men-at-arms are ambushed by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners).(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)

After achieving Riverrun's surrender, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) tells [Edwyn Frey](wiki:Edwyn_Frey) and Ser [Walder Rivers](wiki:Walder_Rivers) that all captives from the Red Wedding held at the Twins are to be sent to King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Roose Bolton marches from the Twins to [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin) via the causeway. His vanguard consists of four hundred mounted Freys led by Ser [Aenys Frey](wiki:Aenys_Frey) and Ser [Hosteen Frey](wiki:Hosteen_Frey), while the rearguard consists of at least another thousand Frey troops.(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)

The pregnant Roslin Frey is to remain at the Twins until she gives birth, after which she and the newborn may join the captive Edmure at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Twins.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)
