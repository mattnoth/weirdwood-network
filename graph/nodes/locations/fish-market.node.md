## Appearances & Description

The fishmarket is located outside of the [River Gate](wiki:River_Gate),(wiki:Fish_market.cite_ref-Rawoiaffishmarket.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) with [Fishmonger's Square](wiki:Fishmonger's_Square) found within the gate.(wiki:Fish_market.cite_ref-Rtloiafking.27s_landing.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Origins

As [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) developed around the court of King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), a fish market sprang up along the riverside of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush).(wiki:Fish_market.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Following the [arrest of Eddard Stark](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark), his daughter [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark) sees a pair of [gold cloaks](wiki:Gold_cloaks) walking side by side through the fish market. Half the stalls are empty.(wiki:Fish_market.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

After [Shagga](wiki:Shagga) takes a ferry south across the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush), [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sees fishwives selling seafood. Tyrion, the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), orders [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) to have everything between the city walls and the water's edge burned in preparation for the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Fish_market.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
During the battle, Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) sees that the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) burned the riverfront and retreated to the [Mud Gate](wiki:River_Gate).(wiki:Fish_market.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Tyrion sees the desolation of the fishmarket and [the wharves](wiki:Harbor_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:Fish_market.cite_ref-Racok59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After the battle, Tyrion reports that the entire fish market is gone and that the River Gate should be replaced.(wiki:Fish_market.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) sometimes visits the fish market by the Mud Gate to see the day's catch.(wiki:Fish_market.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
