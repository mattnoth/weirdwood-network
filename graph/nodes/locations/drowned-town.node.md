## Appearances & Description

The Drowned Town is an area mostly submerged in water, although some people still live in the high towers and upper floors of buildings there.(wiki:Drowned_Town.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_braavos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Only the tops of half-sunken towers and domes are visible as the rest is submerged in the lagoon.(wiki:Drowned_Town.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Below the Drowned Town are a series of wharves. Residents of the Drowned Town may use the [Purple Harbor](wiki:Purple_Harbor), as locals of Braavos.(wiki:Drowned_Town.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
