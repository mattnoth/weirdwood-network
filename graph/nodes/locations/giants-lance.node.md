## Appearances & Description

The Giant's Lance is located northeast of the [Bloody Gate](wiki:Bloody_Gate), north of the [Vale proper](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn_(valley)).(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Rtloiafwesteros.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [Alyssa's Tears](wiki:Alyssa's_Tears) flows over the mountain's massive western shoulder;(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) according to legend, none of the falling water has reached the valley below.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [Falcons](wiki:Falcon) soar about the Giant's Lance.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
The [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) is a stout castle located at the foot of the mountain.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) The lower slopes of the Giant's Lance are covered in pines and [sentinel](wiki:Sentinel) trees.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Stone](wiki:Stone), [Snow](wiki:Snow_(waycastle)), and [Sky](wiki:Sky) are waycastles which guard the path up the Giant's Lance. Mules carry travelers up winding steps carved deep into the rock, with fresh mules available in stables at each waycastle.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) The trail becomes steeper and trees become sparser after Stone.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) Having endured centuries of freezing and thawing, the steps after Snow are cracked and broken.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) The path narrows as it ascends the Giant's Lance, forcing travelers to ride single file.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) At one point the path turns into a high stone saddle twenty foot long and near three feet wide, with howling winds distracting the traveler.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)
The [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), located six hundred feet above Sky on a shoulder of the Giant's Lance,(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8) is accessible through baskets drawn by great chain winches turned by oxen.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4) Instead of the baskets, travelers can also traverse a natural stone chimney which ascends to the seat of [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) like a ladder.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-9)(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5) The [Moon Door](wiki:Moon_Door) in the Eyrie's High Hall opens onto emptiness,(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and prisoners in the Eyrie's [sky cells](wiki:Sky_cells) can be tempted to leap from the mountain to their deaths.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) The [castle's garden](wiki:Godswood_of_the_Eyrie) serves as a [godswood](wiki:Godswood),(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-6) but the Arryns were unable to get a [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) to grow in the soil.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
Descending the mountain can be even more intimidating than the ascent, since the traveler looks down during the journey.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-7) The Arryn court descends to the Gates of the Moon to avoid becoming snowbound at the Eyrie during winter.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-8)

## Origins

According to legend, the [Winged Knight](wiki:Winged_Knight) flew on a huge [falcon](wiki:Falcon) to the peak of the Giant's Lance, where he then slew the [Griffin King](wiki:Griffin_King).(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-9)
During the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), stars and axes were carved by [the newcomers](wiki:Andals) throughout the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn), including at the base of the Giant's Lance.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_norvos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
The [Battle of the Seven Stars](wiki:Battle_of_the_Seven_Stars) was fought at the foot of the Giant's Lance,(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) and [Artys I Arryn](wiki:Artys_I_Arryn), the first [King of Mountain and Vale](wiki:King_of_Mountain_and_Vale), constructed the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) at the site where he had encamped before the battle.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale:_the_eyrie.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Construction of the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) and the switchback road up the mountain began during the reign of King [Roland I Arryn](wiki:Roland_I_Arryn) and continued for decades, with workers and mules dying during the project. [Marble](wiki:Marble) was imported from [Tarth](wiki:Tarth) for the new castle.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale:_the_eyrie.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) flew her [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar), to the Eyrie to deal with the Arryns during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest). In return for the submission of House Arryn, Visenya flew young [Ronnel Arryn](wiki:Ronnel_Arryn_(king)), [King of Mountain and Vale](wiki:King_of_Mountain_and_Vale), three times around the summit of the Giant's Lance.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) During the reign of King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen), Lord Ronnel was thrown to his death through the [Moon Door](wiki:Moon_Door) by his brother, [Jonos Arryn](wiki:Jonos_Arryn). Prince [Maegor Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) retaliated by flying [Balerion](wiki:Balerion) to the Eyrie and executing Jonos by defenestration through the Moon Door.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) flew [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor) up the Giant's Lance to visit the Eyrie during his royal progress in [52 AC](wiki:52_AC).(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Thirty years later, Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), flew on [Silverwing](wiki:Silverwing) to the Gates of the Moon and then the Eyrie to help her daughter, [Daella Targaryen](wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)), frightened because of her pregnancy.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Prince [Jacaerys Velaryon](wiki:Jacaerys_Velaryon) visited the Eyrie on [Vermax](wiki:Vermax) to meet with Lady [Jeyne Arryn](wiki:Jeyne_Arryn) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) brings [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) in the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn_(valley)). [Mya Stone](wiki:Mya_Stone) helps Catelyn ascend on mules to [Sky](wiki:Sky) during the night, and Lady Stark continues on to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) in a basket.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-10)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) threatens [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) shoves Lysa through the [Moon Door](wiki:Moon_Door) to fall to her death.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Marillion](wiki:Marillion), blamed by Petyr for Lysa's death, is confined to the [sky cells](wiki:Sky_cells). Sansa can hear his singing echo off the Giant's Lance.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
The [Lords Declarant](wiki:Lords_Declarant) assemble their hosts around the walls of the Gates of the Moon at the foot of the mountain. The rebel lords ascend to the Eyrie to confront Petyr in a parley, and Lord Baelish convinces them to grant him a year to set the Vale to rights.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
Snowfall buries the peak of the Giant's Lance, so the Arryn court descends from the icy Eyrie to the Gates of the Moon below. [Mord](wiki:Mord) butchers the oxen in the winch room and leaves their bodies for [falcons](wiki:Falcon). When crossing the narrow stone bridge, Sansa inspires the fearful Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn) by comparing him to the [Winged Knight](wiki:Winged_Knight).(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-10)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

The cooks at the Gates of the Moon prepare a [lemon cake](wiki:Lemon_cake) shaped like the Giant's Lance, twelve feet tall and adorned with an Eyrie made of sugar.(wiki:Giant's_Lance.cite_ref-18)
