## Origins

In [83 AC](wiki:83_AC), in preparation for his invasion of the [Stormlands](wiki:Stormlands), Prince [Morion Martell](wiki:Morion_Martell) hired fleets from the pirates of the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones), the sellsails of [Myr](wiki:Myr), and the corsairs of the Pepper Coast to invade the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands). However, it took the better part of a year to gather all his strength, and [the Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen) had known about [the upcoming attack](wiki:Fourth_Dornish_War) for half a year in advance, in part due to the indiscretion of the pirates, sellsails, and corsairs.
