## Origins

The [War on Dagger Lake](wiki:War_on_Dagger_Lake) was one of the [Rhoynish Wars](wiki:Rhoynish_Wars) between the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar) and the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold).(wiki:Dagger_Lake.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
During the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood), [Qohor](wiki:Qohor) and [Norvos](wiki:Norvos) broke [Volantene](wiki:Volantis) power on the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne) in a battle involving fire galleys on Dagger Lake.(wiki:Dagger_Lake.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
