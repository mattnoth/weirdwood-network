## Origins

After Daemon Blackfyre's defeat of Ser [Gwayne Corbray](wiki:Gwayne_Corbray) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), he stayed to defend him while the battle swept around them. While he was defending the fallen knight, [Bloodraven](wiki:Brynden_Rivers) and his [Raven's Teeth](wiki:Raven's_Teeth) had reached the top of Weeping Ridge and began targeting Daemon's standard.
