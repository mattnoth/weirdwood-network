## Origins

King [Tristifer IV Mudd](wiki:Tristifer_IV_Mudd), the Hammer of Justice, ruled from a castle, now called [Oldstones](wiki:Oldstones), which overlooked the Blue Fork.(wiki:Blue_Fork.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
According to a semi-canon source, the blue in the sigil of [House Strong](wiki:House_Strong) referred to the Blue Fork.(wiki:Blue_Fork.cite_ref-4)
[Harwyn Hoare](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), began his conquest of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) by landing one hundred longships south of [Seagard](wiki:Seagard) and marching them overland to the Blue Fork. His [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) then used rivers to conquer the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) from the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King), [Arrec Durrandon](wiki:Arrec_Durrandon), with their greatest victory being the [battle at Fairmarket](wiki:Battle_at_Fairmarket).(wiki:Blue_Fork.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
The wooden bridge across the Blue Fork at [Fairmarket](wiki:Fairmarket) is newer and larger than a bridge near Oldstones.(wiki:Blue_Fork.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
Ser [Pate of the Blue Fork](wiki:Pate_of_the_Blue_Fork) takes his title from the river.(wiki:Blue_Fork.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

After departing the [Twins](wiki:Twins), the army of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) sweeps around the headwaters of the Blue Fork and travels to the [Whispering Wood](wiki:Whispering_Wood). Robb's host is joined by small [lords](wiki:Lord), [hedge knights](wiki:Hedge_knight), and masterless [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms).(wiki:Blue_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Robb's army travels north from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) toward the [Twins](wiki:Twins) for the wedding of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) to [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey). The Blue Fork is flooding its banks, however, with rising waters washing out the bridges at [Fairmarket](wiki:Fairmarket) and [Oldstones](wiki:Oldstones). When [Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover) and two others try to cross at [Ramsford](wiki:Ramsford), two horses and one man are drowned and Galbart clings to a rock until being rescued. Robb's host thus rides around the Blue Fork, passing the ruins of Oldstones, rills and brooks near [Sevenstreams](wiki:Sevenstreams), and the bogs of [Hag's Mire](wiki:Hag's_Mire).(wiki:Blue_Fork.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
Following the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey) rides to Oldstones to ransom his kinsman [Petyr](wiki:Petyr_Frey), but both Freys are killed by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners).(wiki:Blue_Fork.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Lucias Vypren](wiki:Lucias_Vypren) and [Black Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Ryman)) hunt the outlaws of the brotherhood along the Blue Fork.(wiki:Blue_Fork.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
