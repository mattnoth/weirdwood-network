## Origins

Scholars believe the ancestors of the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) came from the lands beyond the [Bone Mountains](wiki:Bone_Mountains) in the [Further East](wiki:Further_East) of [Essos](wiki:Essos),(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) and the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) themselves believe that their race was born in Vaes Dothrak.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

The disparate buildings of Vaes Dothrak were built by [slaves](wiki:Slave) in the fashion of their original lands.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8) The monuments of the [godsway](wiki:Godsway) were brought to Vaes Dothrak after being plundered from distant lands.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)

The exiled Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) has traveled as far east as Vaes Dothrak.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Category:Images_of_Vaes_Dothrak)*

The heart of the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea) and [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) culture, Vaes Dothrak is one of the largest cities of the [known world](wiki:Known_world), due to its expansive layout, and also one of the smallest, due to its low population. The city is large enough to hold every *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)*, were they all to return at once.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Vaes Dothrak has broad, windswept streets paved in grass and mud. There are carved stone pavilions, manses of woven grass, wooden towers, stepped [pyramids](wiki:Pyramid) faced with [marble](wiki:Marble), and roofless log halls. Some of these palaces are protected by horny hedges instead of walls. All of the buildings were built by [slaves](wiki:Slaves) brought from the lands the Dothraki have plundered.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

The [godsway](wiki:Godsway) runs from the [Horse Gate of Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Horse_Gate_of_Vaes_Dothrak) to the [Mother of Mountains](wiki:Mother_of_Mountains). It is lined with monuments and holy symbols from a hundred different [religions](wiki:Religion) on both sides, showing the vast range and power of Dothraki conquests.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Along the way it passes through the Western and Eastern Markets, the lake named the [Womb of the World](wiki:Womb_of_the_World), and a pit where important ceremonies are done in Vaes Dothrak.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### Hall

Located near the Eastern Market, the cavernous wooden feasting hall has walls rising forty feet high. It has a [silk](wiki:Silk) roof which can be raised or lowered.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Inside the hall are three firepits. Those high in honor sit at a high bench, and those low in honor must sit at the back of the long hall.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) Surrounding the palace are horse yards fenced with high hedges, firepits, and hundreds of grass-covered round earthen houses.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

### Markets

The Western Market is a grand bazaar used by traders from the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities).(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) It is a great square of beaten earth surrounded by mud-baked brick, [animal](wiki:Animal) pens and whitewashed drinking halls. Hummocks rise from the earth and beneath the square lie large storerooms. The interior of the square is made up of stalls and crookback aisles shaded by woven grass.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Products offered in the Western Market include [Lysene](wiki:Lys) perfumes, [Myrish](wiki:Myr) lace, fine wools, [Pentoshi](wiki:Pentos) dyes, [goldwork](wiki:Gold) from [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport), ornately-patterned breastplates, and helms in the shapes of fanciful beasts.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Food and ingredients in the market includes garlic, hot pepper, and sausages.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

In the Eastern Market, traders from [Yi Ti](wiki:Yi_Ti), [Asshai](wiki:Asshai), and the [Shadow Lands](wiki:Shadow_Lands) come to trade.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) Animals in the market include [manticores](wiki:Manticore) in [silver](wiki:Silver) cages, grey [elephants](wiki:Elephants), and striped white and black horses ([zorses](wiki:Zorse)) of the [Jogos Nhai](wiki:Jogos_Nhai).(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) Food sold in the Eastern Market includes tree eggs, locust pie, and green noodles.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

Traders are free to cross the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea) unmolested to Vaes Dothrak as long as they keep the peace, do not profane either the [Mother of Mountains](wiki:Mother_of_Mountains) or the [Womb of the World](wiki:Womb_of_the_World), and give the traditional gifts of salt, silver, and seed to the *dosh khaleen*.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5) The [Steel Road](wiki:Steel_Road) and the [Stone Road](wiki:Stone_Road) originate in Vaes Dothrak and head east through the [Bones](wiki:Bones).(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Other routes head west to the ruins of [Vaes Leqse](wiki:Vaes_Leqse) and [Yalli Qamayi](wiki:Yalli_Qamayi).(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Rtloiafthe_known_world.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Magister [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) of [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) is said to have friends in Vaes Dothrak.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

After wedding Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) outside Pentos,(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) travels with his *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* to Vaes Dothrak,(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10) where she is presented to the *[dosh khaleen](wiki:Dosh_khaleen)*. Daenerys ceremonially eats a horse heart and washes in the [Womb of the World](wiki:Womb_of_the_World).(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) When a drunken [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) threatens his sister with an unsheathed sword in the long hall and demands the crown he was promised by Drogo, the *[khal](wiki:Khal)* has him killed by having molten [gold](wiki:Gold) poured on his head.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

Daenerys often spends her mornings in the Eastern Market, sampling its foods and observing [spellsingers](wiki:Spellsinger) and [Shadow Men](wiki:Shadow_Men) and exotic travelers from [Asshai](wiki:Asshai), [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), [Yi Ti](wiki:Yi_Ti), [Bayasabhad](wiki:Bayasabhad), [Shamyriana](wiki:Shamyriana), and [Kayakayanaya](wiki:Kayakayanaya).(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)

[Byan Votyris](wiki:Byan_Votyris) leads a caravan of four hundred horses to Vaes Dothrak from Pentos by way of [Norvos](wiki:Norvos) and [Qohor](wiki:Qohor), so Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) suggests that Daenerys see the new wares in the Western Market. Daenerys exchanges one of her silver medallions for a feathered cloak from the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles). She also acquires a dozen flasks of scented oils, and she buys a fertility charm for [Doreah](wiki:Doreah). A birdseller teaches a [parrot](wiki:Parrot) to say Daenerys's name, but she refuses to take the bird.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7) A [wineseller](wiki:Wineseller) attempts to [poison](wiki:Poison) the pregnant Daenerys, but he is stopped by her *[khas](wiki:Khas)* and Jorah. The an assassination attempt leads Drogo to announce he will lead his *khalasar* against the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-8)

Drogo's *khalasar* departs Vaes Dothrak for [Lhazar](wiki:Lhazar).(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Daenerys loses her child [Rhaego](wiki:Rhaego), and Drogo's *khalasar* disbands after the *khal* dies.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Daenerys burns Drogo's body on a pyre and hatches [dragon eggs](wiki:Dragon_egg) in the flames.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

During his coma, [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) has a vision of Vaes Dothrak beneath the [Mother of Mountains](wiki:Mother_of_Mountains).(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Ragot17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Daenerys thinks that other *khals* would attempt to slay her meager *khalasar* and drag her back to Vaes Dothrak to join the *dosh khaleen*. Her *khalasar* travels through the [red waste](wiki:Red_waste)(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) toward [Qarth](wiki:Qarth).(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Following the [siege of Meereen](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen), the envoy [Ghael](wiki:Ghael) informs Daenerys that [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) has dispatched riders to Vaes Dothrak to bring a *khalasar* against her.(wiki:Vaes_Dothrak.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
