## Appearances & Description

The Great Hall contains the high seat of [House Ashford](wiki:House_Ashford). Ashford Castle and its Great Hall are rather small. The hall has a side door and a dais. Wool [tapestries](wiki:Tapestries) of [fruits](wiki:Food) and [flowers](wiki:Plants) hung on its walls.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Ashford.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
According to Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall), the Great Hall is dim.(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Ashford.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Origins

Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) was redirected to the Great Hall by an Ashford guard when he was looking for [Plummer](wiki:Plummer). Ducan was later brought to the Great Hall when Prince [Aerion Targaryen](wiki:Aerion_Targaryen) demanded a [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven).(wiki:Great_Hall_of_Ashford.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
