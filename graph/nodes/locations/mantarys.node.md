## Origins

Mantarys was founded by the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold). Although rich and glorious, it did not have self-rule and instead men and women sent from [Valyria](wiki:Valyria) ruled it in the name of the Freehold - thus it is not counted among the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities).(wiki:Mantarys.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Mantarys remained inhabited after the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria), but its people are said to be twisted and monstrous.(wiki:Mantarys.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_the_doom_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), Queen of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), sends a mission to Mantarys hoping to find new friends to the west to balance the enmity of [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) to the south.(wiki:Mantarys.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Mantarys's answer comes by way of caravan in a cedar chest - inside she finds the pickled heads of three of her envoys. Instead, the city agrees to an alliance with Yunkai.(wiki:Mantarys.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sees a [two-headed](wiki:Two-headed_girl) [slave](wiki:Slave) girl of Mantarys in [Yezzan zo Qaggaz](wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz)'s [grotesquerie](wiki:Grotesquerie).(wiki:Mantarys.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
