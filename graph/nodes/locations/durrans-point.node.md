## Origins

Amid [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), the smuggler [Davos](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) navigated the tunnel below Durran's Point and delivered onions and salt fish to the starving garrison during the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End).(wiki:Durran's_Point.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
