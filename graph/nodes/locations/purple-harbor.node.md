## Appearances & Description

The Purple Harbor serves only Braavosi ships, such as those of local merchants and the [Iron Bank](wiki:Iron_Bank_of_Braavos).(wiki:Purple_Harbor.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The Purple Harbor is richer, nicer, and cleaner than the [Ragman's Harbor](wiki:Ragman's_Harbor) in the west of the city,(wiki:Purple_Harbor.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) which is where ships from other [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) and the rest of the [known world](wiki:Known_world) must berth.(wiki:Purple_Harbor.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
The best alehouses, inns, and brothels lie near the Purple Harbor or the [Moon Pool](wiki:Moon_Pool) near the Sealord's Palace.(wiki:Purple_Harbor.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) The streets that front on the Purple Harbor are cobbled.(wiki:Purple_Harbor.cite_ref-Radwd64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The [Blue Lantern](wiki:Blue_Lantern) and the [Dome](wiki:Dome) are located in fashionable districts near the waterfront.(wiki:Purple_Harbor.cite_ref-Rtloiaf_braavos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Purple_Harbor.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Bmercy_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Cmercy.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Every morning [Brea](wiki:Brea) wheels her wheelbarrow to the Purple Harbor to sell to Braavosi sailors whose ships anchor there.(wiki:Purple_Harbor.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) One time, when Brea takes to her bed with her moon blood, [Cat of the Canals](wiki:Arya_Stark) pushes her barrow to the Purple Harbor to sell crabs and prawns to oarsmen of Sealord [Ferrego Antaryon](wiki:Ferrego_Antaryon)'s pleasure barge, which is covered stern to stern in laughing faces.(wiki:Purple_Harbor.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) gets her first look at the [old man](wiki:Old_man) she has to kill as she wheels her barrow through the cobbled streets that front on the Purple Harbor.(wiki:Purple_Harbor.cite_ref-Radwd64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) He spends his days sitting in a soup shop near the Purple Harbor. After she assassinates the man, the [city watch](wiki:City_watch_(Braavos)) looks for a certain ugly girl known to frequent the Purple Harbor.(wiki:Purple_Harbor.cite_ref-Radwd64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

[Mercy](wiki:Arya_Stark) recalls that on a clear day from the top of the span the [Bridge of Eyes](wiki:Bridge_of_Eyes) she can look through the arches and see all the city, including the masts rising like a forest from the Purple Harbor.(wiki:Purple_Harbor.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Bmercy_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Cmercy.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
