## Origins

The [Great Barrow](wiki:Great_Barrow) of Barrowton is said to contain the grave of the [First King](wiki:First_King) of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) or a [King of the Giants](wiki:King_of_the_Giants).(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) The [Dustins](wiki:House_Dustin) of Barrowton have been bannermen of [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) since the downfall of the [Barrow Kings](wiki:Barrow_Kings) of old.(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) twice held court at Barrowton during his royal progresses.(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

[Denys Snow](wiki:Denys_Snow), the Bastard of Barrowton, was killed fighting for Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) in the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye).(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Lord Dustin held a [tourney](wiki:Tourney) for King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) and showed them the First King's barrow when they visited Barrowton in [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). While staying in the town, Alysanne heard from women speaking about the [first night](wiki:First_night).(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Near the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Prince [Jacaerys Velaryon](wiki:Jacaerys_Velaryon) gained the [north](wiki:North) for the cause of his mother, Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen). After gathering men to Barrowton,(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Lord [Roderick Dustin](wiki:Roderick_Dustin) led his [Winter Wolves](wiki:Winter_Wolves) south to join Rhaenyra's [blacks](wiki:Blacks). Although Roddy the Ruin was killed in the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton),(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) reinforcements gathered at Barrowton and joined the host of Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark).(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

[Winter Fever](wiki:Winter_Fever) struck Barrowton in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC).(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) During that year's winter, Lord Dustin closed his gates to hundreds of starving villagers outside the town's walls.(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

According to a semi-canon source, there may be an offshoot branch of the Starks in the town.(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-16) [Brandon Stark](wiki:Brandon_Stark) was fostered in Barrowton as a youth.(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

Against the wishes of his wife, Lady [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin), Lord [Willam Dustin](wiki:Willam_Dustin) personally led the Barrowton levies during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). After Willam was slain in [combat at the tower of joy](wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy), Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) returned Willam's stallion, but not his bones, to Barrowton.(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1)

## Appearances & Description

Barrowton is centered around the large hill [Great Barrow](wiki:Great_Barrow), on which was built [House Dustin](wiki:House_Dustin)'s castle, [Barrow Hall](wiki:Barrow_Hall).(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Rawoiafbarrowton.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The wooden town has wooden walls and broad straight streets, including one lined with elms. One of its inns has a wheat sheaf painted on its sign.(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Barrowtown has a [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree).(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

[House Stout](wiki:House_Stout) of [Goldgrass](wiki:Goldgrass)(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-6) has a keep near the eastern gate of Barrowton (less than one mile).(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Outside of the town are rolling plains which the wind howls over.(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin), the [Lady of Barrowton](wiki:Lady_of_Barrowton), sends a minimal number of troops from Barrowton when [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) marches south to rescue his father, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark).(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-2)

### A Clash of Kings

After hearing of the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) threat to [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Fight_at_Torrhen's_Square), Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin) sends [ravens](wiki:Raven) to [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor), the [barrowlands](wiki:Barrowlands), and the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) with instructions to summon their levies.(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Barrowton men in service to House Dustin are slain during the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sends a raven to Barrowton in a plea for help to defend [Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) from [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings).(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### A Dance With Dragons

A host from Barrowton joins the [Ryswells](wiki:House_Ryswell) in destroying [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) ships on the [Fever River](wiki:Fever_River)(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) and then participates in [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow)'s force sent to recover [Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin).(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

[Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) tells [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) she had a vision of a town with wooden walls and streets. Its banners depict a [moose](wiki:House_Hornwood), a [battle-axe](wiki:House_Cerwyn), [pine trees](wiki:House_Tallhart), [longaxes](wiki:House_Dustin), and a [horse's head](wiki:House_Ryswell).(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

Following the surrender of Moat Cailin, the [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton) call upon the northern bannermen to assemble in Barrowton to pledge loyalty to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) and attend [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark) of Ramsay to "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)", actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole),(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) before the town's [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree).(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) Houses [Stout](wiki:House_Stout) and [Dustin](wiki:House_Dustin) host the Boltons while they wait for the bannerman to arrive and while Ramsay searches the barrowlands for missing [Frey envoys](wiki:House_Frey). Lady Barbrey forbids Ramsay from [Barrow Hall](wiki:Barrow_Hall), and he is instead reluctantly hosted by [Harwood Stout](wiki:Harwood_Stout).(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) An angry Ramsay threatens to burn the wooden town.(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

After hearing that [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) has [taken Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte), Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) decides to move the wedding to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) to strengthen Ramsay's claim and to goad Stannis into fighting. While riding from the [Stouts' keep](wiki:Goldgrass) to Barrow Hall, Roose tells [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) about his sons Ramsay and [Domeric Bolton](wiki:Domeric_Bolton).(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)

A [smith](wiki:Smith) from Barrowton hammers a crude iron [kraken](wiki:Kraken) brooch for Theon to wear for the wedding. Jeyne is protected by Lady Dustin during the journey from Barrowton to Winterfell. Barbrey brings furnishings from the town for Jeyne's bedchamber in Winterfell,(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) as well as food and fodder for the levies.(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-3)

Within the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell), Barbrey confides to Theon that if the bones of [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) are found, they will not pass Barrowton.(wiki:Barrowton.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-4)
