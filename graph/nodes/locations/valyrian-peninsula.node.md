## Identity

The Valyrian peninsula is a geographic region of Essos that served as the heartland of the Valyrian Freehold. Ancient Valyria itself was sheltered amid the Fourteen Flames, a ring of volcanoes situated on the Valyrian peninsula, and the peninsula contained Valyrian colonial cities including Oros and Tyria. The Valyrians expanded westward from the peninsula, crossing the Rhoyne to make war on the Andals of Andalos.

The Valyrian peninsula was destroyed in the Doom of Valyria, an apocalyptic event that shattered the Freehold. The destruction of the peninsula created the Smoking Sea in its place. After the Doom, several powers — including Volantis, which sent a fleet, and Qohor, whose dragonlord Aurion marched thirty thousand men into the ruins — attempted to claim what remained of the peninsula, but all such expeditions vanished. The city of Elyria, located near the peninsula, survived the Doom despite its proximity. Astapor, Yunkai, and Meereen, formerly lowly colonies along Slaver's Bay to the south, rose to prominence after the peninsula's destruction.
