## Origins

The Gates of the Moon was the first seat of [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) in the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn). After the [Battle of the Seven Stars](wiki:Battle_of_the_Seven_Stars), [Artys I Arryn](wiki:Artys_I_Arryn), the first [King of Mountain and Vale](wiki:King_of_Mountain_and_Vale), began construction of the large castle where his host had encamped below the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance) the night before his victory. The two eldest sons of Artys who followed him on the throne expanded the castle.(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale:_the_eyrie.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

While formidable, the Gates of the Moon was considered by some to not be suitable for kings, and the fourth Arryn monarch, [Roland I](wiki:Roland_I_Arryn), found it lacking in comparison to grand [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) and the [Hightower](wiki:Hightower). Roland considered dismantling his seat and starting anew, but he instead decided to build the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) high on the Giant's Lance after [wild clansmen](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) descended from the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon).(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale:_the_eyrie.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

The Gates of the Moon has since been used by House Arryn as their seat during winter, but when summer comes they return to the Eyrie.(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The [Keepers of the Gates of the Moon](wiki:Keepers_of_the_Gates_of_the_Moon) have since held the castle for the Arryn kings and, after [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), for the Arryn [Wardens of the East](wiki:Wardens_of_the_East).

King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) visited the Gates of the Moon during a [royal progress](wiki:Royal_progress) in [52 AC](wiki:52_AC). Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) held a [women's court](wiki:Women's_court) at the castle.(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) After hearing that Princess [Daella Targaryen](wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)) was pregnant, Alysanne flew to the Gates of the Moon and then to the Eyrie in [82 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lady [Jeyne Arryn](wiki:Jeyne_Arryn) gathered soldiers to the Gates of the Moon on behalf of the [blacks](wiki:Blacks).(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Jeyne, the Maiden of the Vale, resided at the Gates of the Moon during winter.(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) After twice failing to depose Jeyne, Ser [Arnold Arryn](wiki:Arnold_Arryn) was imprisoned for years in the [sky cells](wiki:Sky_cells) of the Eyrie and the dungeons of the Gates of the Moon.(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Appearances & Description

Larger than the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie),(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) the Gates of the Moon is a stout castle with a moat, a gatehouse, a yard,(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) and a well.(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-TWOW_4-0)

The castle has square towers,(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) include the [East Tower](wiki:East_Tower) and the [Falcon Tower](wiki:Falcon_Tower).(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-TWOW_4-1) When viewed from the Eyrie above, the towers and keeps of the Gates of the Moon appear the size of children's toys.(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The back of the castle's [Great Hall](wiki:Great_Hall_(Gates_of_the_Moon)) has a pillared gallery which leads to tower stairs and to the inner ward.(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-TWOW_4-2) The castle's vaults contain granaries and dungeons.(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-TWOW_4-3) Prisoners can also be kept in tower cells.(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) [Guardsmen](wiki:House_Arryn_guards) at the Gates of the Moon wear sky-blue cloaks.(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

At night torches flicker at the castle's ramparts and the moon is reflected in its moat. Beyond the upper bailey's postern gate is a dense forest of pine and spruce, as well as the steep, carved steps along the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance).(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) Mules are kept at the Gates of the Moon to help transport travelers up the mountain to [Stone](wiki:Stone), [Snow](wiki:Snow_(waycastle)), and [Sky](wiki:Sky).(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

En route to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) and her companions visit the Gates of the Moon and are welcomed by Lord [Nestor Royce](wiki:Nestor_Royce), [Keeper of the Gates of the Moon](wiki:Keeper_of_the_Gates_of_the_Moon). [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn), [Lady of the Eyrie](wiki:Lady_of_the_Eyrie), has sent word for Catelyn to continue on to the Eyrie in a night ascent, but the rest of the travelers remain behind at the Gates of the Moon until daybreak. Nestor has [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) taken to a tower cell. [Mya Stone](wiki:Mya_Stone) then helps Catelyn during her dangerous journey up the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance).(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)

### A Storm of Swords

Lysa rarely allows visitors to ascend past the Gates of the Moon. The castle is maintained by [Myranda Royce](wiki:Myranda_Royce), the daughter of Nestor.(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Feast for Crows

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) recalls that the wedding party of Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) and Lysa stopped at the Gates of the Moon before ascending to the Eyrie.(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Nestor ascends to the Eyrie to investigate the death of Lysa, whose murder has been blamed by Petyr on the singer [Marillion](wiki:Marillion). Claiming it was Lysa's desire before she passed, Petyr grants the castle and the title of Keeper of the Gates of the Moon to Nestor in perpetuity, thus forming [House Royce of the Gates of the Moon](wiki:House_Royce_of_the_Gates_of_the_Moon).(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

The [Lords Declarant](wiki:Lords_Declarant) gather six thousand men about the Gates of the Moon, while Lord Nestor has a garrison of three hundred to oppose them. Petyr is able able to negotiate a truce with the Lords Declarant during a parley at the Eyrie, however.(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

With the onset of winter, Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn)'s court descends from the Eyrie to the Gates of the Moon. Within, Petyr introduces Sansa, who is disguised as "Alayne Stone", to three [hedge knights](wiki:Hedge_knight) he has taken into his service, Ser [Byron the Beautiful](wiki:Byron_the_Beautiful), Ser [Morgarth the Merry](wiki:Morgarth_the_Merry), and Ser [Shadrich of the Shady Glen](wiki:Shadrich_of_the_Shady_Glen). After the knights leave, Petyr tells Sansa of his plan to marry her to Ser [Harrold Hardyng](wiki:Harrold_Hardyng), Robert's heir.(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Nobles from throughout the Vale, including Harry the Heir, travel to the Gates of the Moon to attend the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) for the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights). Lord Nestor shows Lady [Waxley](wiki:House_Waxley) his prize [tapestries](wiki:Tapestries) which had belonged to King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). Nestor holds a feast for the competitors, and "Alayne" dances with Harrold.(wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-TWOW_4-4)
