## Origins

[Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) and her dragon, [Dreamfyre](wiki:Dreamfyre), briefly stayed at Ashemark with the [Marbrands](wiki:House_Marbrand) in [51 AC](wiki:51_AC).(wiki:Ashemark.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) visited the castle in [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Ashemark.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Willem Frey](wiki:Willem_Frey) serves as a page at Ashemark.(wiki:Ashemark.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

During the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), the [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), defeats Ser [Stafford Lannister](wiki:Stafford_Lannister) at the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross). Robb then [takes Ashemark](wiki:Taking_of_Ashemark), along with other castles of the westermen.(wiki:Ashemark.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Robb leaves Ashemark to march on [the Crag](wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag). After learning that [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)) has yielded to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) has Maester [Vyman](wiki:Vyman) send a [raven](wiki:Raven) from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) to Ashemark, hoping that a rider can then be sent to inform Robb.(wiki:Ashemark.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
