## Appearances & Description

The sweetwater river enters [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) on the south side of the city, crossing the [Long Canal](wiki:Long_Canal) and [Green Canal](wiki:Green_Canal),(wiki:Sweetwater_river.cite_ref-Rtloiafbraavos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) before ending at the [Moon Pool](wiki:Moon_Pool) in the north of the city.(wiki:Sweetwater_river.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Sweetwater_river.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_braavos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Made of grey stone(wiki:Sweetwater_river.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and brick,(wiki:Sweetwater_river.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) the aqueduct looks like a roadway supported by three tiers of arches traveling from the south. Looming above the canals and houses, it brings fresh water from the mainland of [Essos](wiki:Essos) across the mudflats and the briny shallows.(wiki:Sweetwater_river.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Rich men pipe it directly into their homes, while commoners fill buckets at public fountains.(wiki:Sweetwater_river.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Origins

Because the water from the lagoon in which [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) is located is brackish and briny,(wiki:Sweetwater_river.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) the canal water is not drinkable without first boiling it.(wiki:Sweetwater_river.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Thus, the Braavosi built the sweetwater river to supply themselves with clean water.(wiki:Sweetwater_river.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
