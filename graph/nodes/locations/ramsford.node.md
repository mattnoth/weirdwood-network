### A Storm of Swords[ ]

During the march to [the Twins](wiki:The_Twins) for [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully)'s marriage the river is exceedingly high. Due to heavy rains many crossings had been washed away. [Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover) and two men attempt to go across the Blue Fork at Ramsford. One man drowns, two of the horses are pulled under and Glover has to be rescued from the water.(wiki:Ramsford.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
