## Origins

The Perfumed Garden was one of the many holdings owned by [House Rogare](wiki:House_Rogare) under [Lysandro "the Magnificent"](wiki:Lysandro_Rogare). In [135 AC](wiki:135_AC), Lysandro drowned when his opulent barge sank whilst carrying him from the Perfumed Garden back to his palace.
A younger son of Lysandro, [Fredo](wiki:Fredo_Rogare), inherited the Perfumed Garden. When the [Rogare Bank](wiki:Rogare_Bank) ran dry, Fredo was put on trial, and the Rogare Bank was seized, along with the bed-slaves there. When the Rogare's assets proved insufficient to cover their debts, the Rogares were also sold into slavery, with the daughters of [Lysaro](wiki:Lysaro_Rogare) and Fredo Rogare becoming bed-slaves in the Perfumed Garden.(wiki:Perfumed_Garden.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
