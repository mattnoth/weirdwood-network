## Appearances & Description

The Great Keep contains the bedchambers for members of [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark).(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Ragot14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The building is heated by water from hot springs below the castle, and Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark)'s bedchambers are the hottest rooms in the Great Keep.(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The towers of the Great Keep have winding stairs.(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The bulding's cellar has narrow windows.(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s solar contains an oak chair for the [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell) and a long plank-and-trestle table.(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
There is a view of the yard from the covered bridge which connects the Great Keep and the armory.(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Ragot7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) A dimly-lit gallery leads from the [Great Hall](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell) to the Great Keep.(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
The gatehouse of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) is as large as Winterfell's Great Keep.(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Origins

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) once earned himself a thrashing by accidentally knocking [Old Nan](wiki:Old_Nan) to the ground while carelessly leaping down the Great Keep's spiral stairs.(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Theon and [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) have also often sparred on the stairs with wooden swords.(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

After visiting the comatose [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) in the castle's [sickroom](wiki:Sickroom), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) speaks with [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) in the yard and then goes to the Keep to say goodbye to [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark).(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Ragot10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) protects Bran when he is attacked in his sickbed by [an assassin](wiki:Catspaw), and the attacker is killed by the boy's unnamed [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf). The hysterical Catelyn is then brought to the Great Keep to be treated in her own chambers.(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Ragot14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
[Hodor](wiki:Hodor) transports the crippled Bran to and from his chambers after he wakes from his coma.(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Mikken](wiki:Mikken) hammers a row of iron bars into the wall of Bran's chambers so the boy can pull himself around.(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
[Northmen](wiki:North) come to Winterfell to celebrate the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast). Bran, Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin), and Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) meet with [bannermen](wiki:Bannermen) in the solar of Bran's late father, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark).(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
[Werlag](wiki:Werlag) breaks down the door to Bran's chamber during the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) by [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), and [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) takes the boy into custody.(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Calling himself [Prince of Winterfell](wiki:Prince_of_Winterfell), Theon claims Eddard's bedchamber.(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
After the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell), Bran sees the glow of a fire in the cellar beneath the Great Keep.(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) holds [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark) of his son [Ramsay](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) and "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)" (actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)) in the [godswood of Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell). After the guests feast in the [Great Hall](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell), Roose meets with bannermen in the Great Keep's solar.(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Ramsay keeps Jeyne confined to his bedchamber,(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) a room lightly damaged by the sack and adorned with new furnishings from [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton).(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) Men from the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort) guard the main doors of the Great Keep, and the [Bastard's Boys](wiki:Bastard's_Boys) guard Ramsay's chamber.(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2) "Arya's" serving girls include [Maisie](wiki:Maisie) and [Jez](wiki:Jez_(serving_girl)).(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-3)
Because several men have been found dead in Winterfell, the captive Theon is interrogated in the solar by Roose, Lady [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin), [Roger Ryswell](wiki:Roger_Ryswell), and Ser [Aenys Frey](wiki:Aenys_Frey).(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
The [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) and the [Manderlys](wiki:House_Manderly) fight after [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)) is found murdered, so Roose orders the two factions to advance on [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood). Theon and [Abel](wiki:Abel)'s washerwomen use this as an opportunity to sneak "Arya" from Ramsay's chambers in the Great Keep.(wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell).cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-4)
