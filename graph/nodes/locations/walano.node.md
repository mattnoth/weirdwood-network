## Origins

Walano was the site of the first recorded contact between the Summer Isles and the wider world. A merchant ship of the [Ghiscari Empire](wiki:Ghiscari_Empire) was blown of course by a storm and made landfall on Walano. The ship fled when its crew reacted with terror at the first sight of the local inhabitants, whom the Ghiscari mistook for [demons](wiki:Demons) burned black by the fires of hell.
