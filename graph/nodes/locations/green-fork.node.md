## Origins

According to a semi-canon source, the green in the sigil of [House Strong](wiki:House_Strong) referred to the Green Fork.(wiki:Green_Fork.cite_ref-8)
About six hundred years ago, the [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) began construction of [a bridge](wiki:Twins) spanning the northern Green Fork, where it runs high and fast.(wiki:Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
The [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) under the [Kings of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:Kings_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers) used the Trident and its tributaries to quickly traverse the riverlands.(wiki:Green_Fork.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf73.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Lady [Sharra Arryn](wiki:Sharra_Arryn) offered [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) an alliance in return for the lands between the Green Fork and the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon), but she was declined.(wiki:Green_Fork.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf31.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) ordered construction of the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad) along the Green Fork in [62 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Green_Fork.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
[Sweetwillow](wiki:Sweetwillow) on the Green Fork was attacked by Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) and his dragon [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Green_Fork.cite_ref-Rfab16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Ser [Franklyn Frey](wiki:Franklyn_Frey) was well regarded along the Green Fork.(wiki:Green_Fork.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) marches the [northern](wiki:North) army to the [Twins](wiki:Twins), where an alliance between Houses [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) and [Frey](wiki:House_Frey) is concluded.(wiki:Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Robb crosses the Green Fork with the northern horse and heads toward [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), while Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) marches south with the northern foot along the eastern bank of the river.(wiki:Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) notices blackened fields and burned holdfasts near the Green Fork.(wiki:Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) He meets the camp follower [Shae](wiki:Shae) amongst the host of his father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister). Tywin defeats Roose in the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:Green_Fork.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) reports that fighting has spread across the [Trident](wiki:Trident) almost to the Twins.(wiki:Green_Fork.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Meera Reed](wiki:Meera_Reed) tells [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) of the [Knight of the Laughing Tree](wiki:Knight_of_the_Laughing_Tree) and of a [crannogman](wiki:Crannogman) who paddled a little skin boat down the Green Fork.(wiki:Green_Fork.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
The river is swollen when Robb, now [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident), and his army return to the Twins for the wedding of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) to [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey).(wiki:Green_Fork.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Robb and his forces are then betrayed in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding); the wounded Ser [Raynald Westerling](wiki:Raynald_Westerling) is last seen throwing himself into the river.(wiki:Green_Fork.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Afterward, [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Stark)'s naked corpse is dumped into the river by the Freys in mockery of [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully)'s funeral custom.(wiki:Green_Fork.cite_ref-Rasos58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
[Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf)) eventually recovers Catelyn's body from the river.(wiki:Green_Fork.cite_ref-Rasos65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)(wiki:Green_Fork.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Lem](wiki:Lem) hunts the [Mad Dog](wiki:Hound) of [Saltpans](wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans), who had been spotted making his way north along the Green Fork, but [Rorge](wiki:Rorge)'s former [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) instead double back for the [crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads).(wiki:Green_Fork.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1)
