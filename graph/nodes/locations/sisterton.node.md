## Origins

Prior to becoming a smuggler's den, Sisterton was a [pirate](wiki:Pirate)'s nest.(wiki:Sisterton.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) flew [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) to the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters) during the [Sistermen's Rebellion](wiki:Sistermen's_Rebellion), and the sight of the [dragon](wiki:Dragon) above Sisterton led the [Sistermen](wiki:Sistermen) to quickly surrender.(wiki:Sisterton.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragons_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) spent a fortnight in the town in [19 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Sisterton.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), [Jacaerys Velaryon](wiki:Jacaerys_Velaryon), [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone), gained Houses [Borrell](wiki:House_Borrell) and [Sunderland](wiki:House_Sunderland) for the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) during his visit to Sisterton,(wiki:Sisterton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) and men began gathering in the town.(wiki:Sisterton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Half of Sisterton's population died from [Winter Fever](wiki:Winter_Fever) in [132 AC](wiki:132_AC), and the [Sistermen](wiki:Sistermen) killed [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibbenese) sailors who received the blame.(wiki:Sisterton.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) A [Pentoshi](wiki:Pentos) captain found guilty of bringing the disease from Sisterton to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) was executed by Ser [Victor Risley](wiki:Victor_Risley).(wiki:Sisterton.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) was taken to the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters) by [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) near the start of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). Against the advice of [Breakwater](wiki:Breakwater)'s [maester](wiki:Maester), Lord Borrell allowed Ned Stark to leave Sisterton and continue on to the [north](wiki:North).(wiki:Sisterton.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

While serving as the [master of ships](wiki:Master_of_ships) for King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), Lord [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) once sailed to Sisterton with a fleet and made Lord [Godric Borrell](wiki:Godric_Borrell) hang twelve of his friends, probably for smuggling. Stannis threatened to do the same to Godric if any ships were to crash if the [Night Lamp](wiki:Night_Lamp) ever went dark.(wiki:Sisterton.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

## Appearances & Description

Sisterton is a small, mean town, rank with the odors of pig waste and rotting fish. Its streets are made of mud and planks, while its are houses daub-and-wattle hovels roofed with straw. By the [Gallows Gate](wiki:Gallows_Gate) there are always hanged men with their entrails dangling out.(wiki:Sisterton.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

The [Night Lamp](wiki:Night_Lamp), a lighthouse tower,(wiki:Sisterton.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) and [Breakwater](wiki:Breakwater),(wiki:Sisterton.cite_ref-Rawoiafsisterton.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) the seat of [House Borrell](wiki:House_Borrell), are located at Sisterton, as is the [Belly of the Whale](wiki:Belly_of_the_Whale).(wiki:Sisterton.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
