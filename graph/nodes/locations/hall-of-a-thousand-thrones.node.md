## Appearances & Description

The [Pureborn](wiki:Pureborn) are enthroned on great wooden chairs of their ancestors, which rise in curved tiers from a [marble](wiki:Marble) floor to a high domed ceiling painted with scenes of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth)'s vanquished glory. The unique chairs are immense, fantastically carved, bright with gold work, and studded with [amber](wiki:Amber), [onyx](wiki:Onyx), [lapis](wiki:Lapis), and [jade](wiki:Jade). Each Pureborn wants his chair to be the most fabulous.(wiki:Hall_of_a_Thousand_Thrones.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

While residing in [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos)'s palace in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) seeks an audience with the [Pureborn](wiki:Pureborn) to plead with them for usage of their galleys and soldiers. To open the doors of the Pureborn, she tenders bribes in gold to [Mathos Mallarawan](wiki:Mathos_Mallarawan), [Wendello Qar Deeth](wiki:Wendello_Qar_Deeth), and [Egon Emeros the Exquisite](wiki:Egon_Emeros). Daenerys makes the traditional sacrifice in the [Temple of Memory](wiki:Temple_of_Memory), offers the traditional bribe to the [Keeper of the Long List](wiki:Keeper_of_the_Long_List), sends the traditional persimmon to the [Opener of the Door](wiki:Opener_of_the_Door), and finally receives the traditional blue silk slippers summoning her to the Hall of a Thousand Thrones.(wiki:Hall_of_a_Thousand_Thrones.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Daenerys garbs herself in Qartheen fashion, as Xaro warns her that the Enthroned will never listen to a [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki). She wears flowing green samite with one breast bared, a belt of black-and-white pearls about her waist, and silvered sandals on her feet. The crowned Daenerys also wears a tight silver collar set with an enchanted [amethyst](wiki:Amethyst). She takes [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal) on her shoulder. However, the men who sit in the ornate wooden seats seem so listless and world-weary that she thinks they might be asleep. Despite her attire, pleas, and flattery, the Pureborn courteously refuse Daenerys. Xaro disagrees with her refusal to weep.(wiki:Hall_of_a_Thousand_Thrones.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
