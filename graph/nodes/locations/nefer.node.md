## Appearances & Description

### Location[ ]

Nefer is a port city located in the [Further East](wiki:Further_East) of [Essos](wiki:Essos), at the mouth of a river which ends into the [Shivering Sea](wiki:Shivering_Sea). North of Nefer are the [Thousand Islands](wiki:Thousand_Islands), while south of the city lie the [Plains of the Jogos Nhai](wiki:Plains_of_the_Jogos_Nhai), the [Bleeding Sea](wiki:Bleeding_Sea), and the [Cannibal Sands](wiki:Cannibal_Sands). To the east stretch the remnants of the kingdom of [N'ghai](wiki:N'ghai) and the forests of [Mossovy](wiki:Mossovy).(wiki:Nefer.cite_ref-Rtloiaf_the_east.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Nefer is the only northern port east of the [Bone Mountains](wiki:Bone_Mountains) known to [maesters](wiki:Maesters).(wiki:Nefer.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_east_of_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### Layout[ ]

Shrouded by fog, Nefer is surrounded by tall chalk cliffs. When seen from the harbor, it appears to be no more than a small town. Nine-tenths of Nefer is said to be beneath underground, however, giving the city its nickname of the Secret City.(wiki:Nefer.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_east_of_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Origins

Wars with the [Jogos Nhai](wiki:Jogos_Nhai) strongly reduced [N'ghai](wiki:N'ghai) to Nefer and its hinterlands.(wiki:Nefer.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_bones_and_beyond:_the_plains_of_the_jogos_nhai.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
