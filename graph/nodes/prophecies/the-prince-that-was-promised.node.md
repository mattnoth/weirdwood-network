## Origins

Main article: the prince that was promised/Theories

It is not known from where the prophecy originates, as information on the prince that was promised is scant. According to [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre), a [red priestess](wiki:Red_priest) of [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor), the prince was prophesied five thousand years ago.(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) She has mentioned the prince that was promised(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and [Azor Ahai](wiki:Azor_Ahai) interchangeably but tends to use the name Azor Ahai far more often. Melisandre states that "When the red star bleeds and the darkness gathers, Azor Ahai shall be born again amidst smoke and salt."(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) mentions a bleeding star, along with smoke and salt, as being part of the heralding of the prince that was promised coming,(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) but he does not mention Azor Ahai. Archmaester [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn) recalls that according to the prophecy, the prince would be "Born amidst salt and smoke, beneath a bleeding star."(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) When he was younger Aemon thought that Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) was the prince who was promised, since the smoke would be from the fire at [Summerhall](wiki:Tragedy_at_Summerhall) and salt from the tears of those who cried there.(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Young Rhaegar agreed with Aemon that he might be the prince that was promised. According to [Arstan Whitebeard](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), one day Rhaegar found something in his scrolls that changed him and he decided to become a [knight](wiki:Knight), telling Ser [Willem Darry](wiki:Willem_Darry), "I will require a sword and armor. It seems I must be a warrior."(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Rhaegar later believed that his infant son, [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)), was the promised prince, since a comet was seen above [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) when the boy was born.(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Rhaegar was killed by Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) in the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident),(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) however, and Aegon was killed by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) in the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

When he was a boy, the future King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) recalled that King [Aerys I](wiki:Aerys_I_Targaryen) once read about the return of [dragons](wiki:Dragon) in a prophecy.(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Maester Aemon believes that "the dragon must have three heads,"(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) although it is unknown if that is connected with the prince that was promised.

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s eldest brother, the late Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), appears in one of her visions in the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying) in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth). She sees him talking to his wife, [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell), discussing what he shall name his son ([Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar))) and his destiny. He states, "He is the prince that was promised, and his is [the song of ice and fire](wiki:The_song_of_ice_and_fire)."(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

When at the [port of Qarth](wiki:Port_of_Qarth) with Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont), Daenerys mentions her brother said that the babe was the prince that was promised. Jorah observes that if Aegon was this prince that was promised, the promise was broken along with his skull when the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) dashed his head against a wall during the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing). Daenerys seems to accept this notion and asks about the song of ice and fire, but Jorah has never heard it.(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Storm of Swords

On [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)) [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) explains that [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) is the prince that was promised,(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) and that the [red comet](wiki:Red_comet) is his herald.(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

At the [Wall](wiki:Wall) Melisandre commands the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) to light nightfires to protect against the dark and she speaks of the [war for the dawn](wiki:War_for_the_dawn). When Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) asks about the prince that was promised, Melisandre states that Stannis is [Azor Ahai](wiki:Azor_Ahai).(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Feast for Crows

In [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) tells Aemon of the rumors about Daenerys and her dragons. The maester becomes convinced that Daenerys is the prophesied leader. Remembering Septon [Barth](wiki:Barth)'s conclusion that dragons are of no mixed gender, Aemon determines there was an error in the prophecy's translation. He tells Sam that the hero is not a prince but a princess, Daenerys.(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Aemon intends to find Daenerys, but he dies en route to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), however.(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) Archmaester [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn) announces his intention to travel to her at [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay) instead.(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

In [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) talks to Daenerys about her mother, [Rhaella](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen), and father, King [Aerys II](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen). He mentions that her grandsire, King [Jaehaerys II](wiki:Jaehaerys_II_Targaryen), commanded that they be wed after a [woods witch](wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart) had foretold that the prince who was promised would be born of their line. When Daenerys asks about the woods witch, Barristan mentions [Summerhall](wiki:Tragedy_at_Summerhall) and is then asked to leave by his weary queen.(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

On the Wall a doubting [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) reminds Melisandre that she has spoken of "a promised prince, born of smoke and salt", and that it seems to him that she makes nothing but mistakes.(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

When [Benerro](wiki:Benerro) preaches in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) that the [dark eye](wiki:Dark_eye) has fallen upon Daenerys, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) recalls an ancient prophecy of a hero who will save the world from darkness.(wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
