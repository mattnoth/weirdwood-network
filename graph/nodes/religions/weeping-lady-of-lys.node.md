## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

When first entering the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) notes the statue of a [marble](wiki:Marble) woman, 12 feet tall (3,66 meters). Real tears are trickling from the eyes, filling the bowl the woman cradled in her arms. The statue of a man with a lion's head stands nearby. At this point Arya cannot name the figures.(wiki:Weeping_Lady_of_Lys.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Early in her training, Arya is able to identify both the Weeping Woman and the [Lion of Night](wiki:Lion_of_Night).(wiki:Weeping_Lady_of_Lys.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
