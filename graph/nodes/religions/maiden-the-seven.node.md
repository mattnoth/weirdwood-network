## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) prays in a nameless village's sept. The sept is modest and has no statues of the Seven, only rough charcoal drawings to represent them. She goes to the Maid and beseeches her to lend her courage to [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark) and [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark), to guard them in their innocence.(wiki:Maiden_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

The [dwarf brother](wiki:Dwarf_brother) makes [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth)'s acquaintance at the [Seven Swords](wiki:Seven_Swords) inn. After chatting with her he notices Brienne looks sad and asks her if she is thinking of her [missing sister](wiki:Sansa_Stark). He pats her hand and tries to reassure her, telling her:-
The [Crone](wiki:Crone_(the_Seven)) will light your way to her, never fear. The Maiden will keep her safe.(wiki:Maiden_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
