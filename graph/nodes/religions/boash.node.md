## Origins

1322 years before the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria), a sect of religious dissidents left the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) to establish a temple on the [Lorathi](wiki:Lorath) main isle,(wiki:Boash.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf821_1-4) thereby founding Lorath as a Valyrian colony.(wiki:Boash.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf822_2-0) The ancient mazes of the [mazemakers](wiki:Mazemakers) became the towns, temples, and tombs of the Boash'i. For three-quarters of a century, they dominated the islands of Lorath. Later, men from other lands and faiths, including escaped [slaves](wiki:Slave), settled on Lorath. They were at first subservient to the followers of Boash, and so the priests continued to rule; however, over time the newcomers outnumbered the faithful. As the worship of Boash dwindled, the priests grew worldly and corrupt, forsaking their hair shirts, hoods, and piety, and growing fat and rich off the taxes they extracted from those they ruled. Finally, the lower classes rose in rebellion. The acolytes of the Blind God were slaughtered, save for a few that fled to the great temple maze on [Lorassyon](wiki:Lorassyon). There they remained for the best part of a century, before they died out.(wiki:Boash.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf821_1-5)
