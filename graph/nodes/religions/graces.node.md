### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Graces of various robe colors are present with [Galazza Galare](wiki:Galazza_Galare) at the [Temple of the Graces](wiki:Temple_of_the_Graces) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) for the [wedding of Hizdahr zo Loraq and Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen).(wiki:Graces.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)
When Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) refers to King [Hizdahr](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) as "His Grace" in the [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) fashion, [Reznak mo Reznak](wiki:Reznak_mo_Reznak) corrects him by referring to Hizdahr as "His Magnificence", "His Radiance", and "His Worship".(wiki:Graces.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
