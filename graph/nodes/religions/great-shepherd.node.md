## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Mirri Maz Duur](wiki:Mirri_Maz_Duur) is a [godswife](wiki:Godswife) of the Great Shepherd, [enslaved](wiki:Slave) by the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) of [Khal](wiki:Khal) [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), who encounters [Khaleesi](wiki:Khaleesi) [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen).(wiki:Great_Shepherd.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

In [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), the [kindly man](wiki:Kindly_man) tells [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) that [all men must die](wiki:Valar_morghulis) no matter who they worship, including the Great Shepherd.(wiki:Great_Shepherd.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

The [Red Lamb](wiki:Red_Lamb) is a former [slave](wiki:Slave) freed by [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), who became a [squire](wiki:Squire) of Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), and is now a newly made [knight](wiki:Knight). In [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), Barristan sets out to rally the troops during the [siege](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen). His knights are to accompany him, and when Barristan tells them there is no shame in fear, the Red Lamb replies that he is not afraid of death. He wishes to face the Great Shepherd, and ask him why he allows his people to be lambs in a world of [wolves](wiki:Wolves).(wiki:Great_Shepherd.cite_ref-twow_5-0)
