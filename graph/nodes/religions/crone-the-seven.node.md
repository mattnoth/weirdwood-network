### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) prays in a nameless village’s sept. The sept is modest and has no statues of the Seven, only rough charcoal drawings to represent them. She turns to the Crone and prays.(wiki:Crone_(the_Seven).cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

The [dwarf brother](wiki:Dwarf_brother) makes [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth)’s acquaintance at the [Seven Swords](wiki:Seven_Swords) inn common room. After chatting with her he notices Brienne looks sad and asks her if she is thinking of her [missing sister](wiki:Sansa_Stark), he pats her hand and tries to reassure her, telling Brienne “The Crone with light your way for her, never fear.
