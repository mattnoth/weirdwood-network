## Quotes

He has faces beyond count, little one, as many faces as there are stars in the sky. In [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), men worship as they will... but at the end of every road stands Him of Many Faces, waiting. He will be there for you one day, do not fear. You need not rush to his embrace.(wiki:Many-Faced_God.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Death is not the worst thing. It is His gift to us, an end to want and pain. On the day that we are born the Many-Faced God sends each of us a dark angel to walk through life beside us. When our sins and our sufferings grow too great to be borne, the angel takes us by the hand to lead us to the nightlands, where the stars burn ever bright. Those who come to drink from the black cup are looking for their angels.(wiki:Many-Faced_God.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)
Kindly man: Our forebears came from half a hundred lands to [this place of refuge](wiki:Braavos), to escape the [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlord) who had [enslaved](wiki:Enslaved) them. Half a hundred [gods](wiki:God) came with them, but there is one god all of them shared in common.
Arya: Him of Many Faces. Kindly man: And many names. In [Qohor](wiki:Qohor) he is the [Black Goat](wiki:Black_Goat), in [Yi Ti](wiki:Yi_Ti) the [Lion of Night](wiki:Lion_of_Night), in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) the [Stranger](wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven)). All men must bow to him in the end, no matter if they worship [the Seven](wiki:The_Seven) or the [Lord of Light](wiki:Lord_of_Light), the [Moon Mother](wiki:Moon_Mother) or the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God) or the [Great Shepherd](wiki:Great_Shepherd). All mankind belongs to him... else somewhere in the world would be a folk who lived forever. Do you know of any folk who live forever?
