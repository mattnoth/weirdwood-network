## Origins

[Larra Rogare](wiki:Larra_Rogare) worshipped the manifold gods of Lys, including Yndros of the Twilight. Dark rumors arose that she oft used the powers of an acolyte of Yndros at twilight orgies, so she might visit the brothels of the [Street of Silk](wiki:Street_of_Silk) as a man.(wiki:Yndros_of_the_Twilight.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
After the [Rogare Bank](wiki:Rogare_Bank) ran dry, [Marra Rogare](wiki:Marra_Rogare) fled to the temple of Yndros in man's garb and there claimed sanctuary.(wiki:Yndros_of_the_Twilight.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
