## Culture

The [Faceless Men](wiki:Faceless_Men) consider the Black Goat one of the faces of the [Many-Faced God](wiki:Many-Faced_God),(wiki:Black_Goat.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) and the [bearded priests](wiki:Bearded_priests) of [Norvos](wiki:Norvos) consider the Black Goat to be a [demon](wiki:Demon).(wiki:Black_Goat.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_qohor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
A black [goat](wiki:Goat) is represented in the banner of the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions),(wiki:Black_Goat.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) a sellsword company led by the Qohorik [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat).(wiki:Black_Goat.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Origins

[Qohor](wiki:Qohor) was founded by worshippers of the Black Goat who abandoned [Valyria](wiki:Valyria) in rejection of the [Freehold's](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) tenet of religious tolerance.(wiki:Black_Goat.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_qohor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Black_Goat.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_norvos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After arriving at the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) sees the statue of a shaggy black [goat](wiki:Goat) the size of an [aurochs](wiki:Aurochs).(wiki:Black_Goat.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
[Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy), a [priest of the Drowned God](wiki:Priest_of_the_Drowned_God), considers the Black Goat to be a goat-headed abomination and a false god.(wiki:Black_Goat.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
It is reported that followers of [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor) have tried to burn the Black Goat in Qohor.(wiki:Black_Goat.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
