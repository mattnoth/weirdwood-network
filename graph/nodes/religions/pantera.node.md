## Origins

When [Larra Rogare](wiki:Larra_Rogare) worshiped the various gods of Lys while living in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), she was joined by her ladies, servants, and guards. Because [cats](wiki:Cat) were often seen coming and going from her chambers, it was rumored that they were her spies. Some stories claimed Larra could even transform into a cat so she could prowl the gutters and rooftops of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Pantera.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
[Matteno Orthys](wiki:Matteno_Orthys), a magister of Lys, was a fervent worshiper of Pantera. His prized [shadowcat](wiki:Shadowcat) mauled and partly devoured him when its cage was left open one night.(wiki:Pantera.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
