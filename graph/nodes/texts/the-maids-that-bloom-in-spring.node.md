## Identity

"The Maids That Bloom in Spring" is a song performed in Westeros. Tom of Sevenstreams performs it at the Peach in Stoney Sept alongside "Six Maids in a Pool" and "Two Hearts That Beat as One." Mance Rayder, performing as the bard Abel at the wedding feast of Ramsay Bolton in Winterfell, also includes it in his repertoire during the feasts that follow the wedding.
