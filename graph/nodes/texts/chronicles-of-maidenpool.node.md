## Origins

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Maester [Norren](wiki:Norren_(House_Mooton)) was the keeper of the text. He recorded the time Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) and his alleged [paramour](wiki:Paramour), the [dragonseed](wiki:Dragonseed) [Nettles](wiki:Nettles), spent at Maidenpool as guests of Lord [Manfryd Mooton](wiki:Manfryd_Mooton).(wiki:Chronicles_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Rfab16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
