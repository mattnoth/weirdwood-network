## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

The war band of [Tormund Giantsbane](wiki:Tormund_Giantsbane) sings "The Last of the Giants" to [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), who is unfamiliar with the song.(wiki:The_Last_of_the_Giants.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Jon thinks of the song after discovering that [Mag Mar Tun Doh Weg](wiki:Mag_Mar_Tun_Doh_Weg) and [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye) killed each other during the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall).(wiki:The_Last_of_the_Giants.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Jon again thinks of the song while speaking with Ser [Godry Farring](wiki:Godry_Farring), who is now called Godry the Giantslayer after killing a small [giant](wiki:Giant).(wiki:The_Last_of_the_Giants.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
