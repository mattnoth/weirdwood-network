## Origins

The [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field) was a battle fought during the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion) in [196 AC](wiki:196_AC). Prince [Baelor Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)) (the hammer) led [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) [spearmen](wiki:Spear) against the rear of [Daemon Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre)'s rebel army and crushed it against the shield wall of Prince [Maekar Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen) (the anvil). However, Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey) felt the singers left out much from what actually happened, including Daemon's actions in the battle and how [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers) and his [Raven's Teeth](wiki:Raven's_Teeth) [archers](wiki:Archer) slew Daemon and his sons.(wiki:The_hammer_and_the_anvil.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) and the troops of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) attack the armies of [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) in the [Battle of Fire](wiki:Battle_of_Fire). Near the end of the battle, Barristan learns that [ships](wiki:Ship) of [House Greyjoy](wiki:House_Greyjoy) have arrived in [the bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay), and the [ironmen](wiki:Ironmen) are coming ashore to fight the Yunkish. Barristan gleefully exclaims, “It’s like Baelor Breakspear and Prince Maekar, the hammer and the anvil. We have them! We have them!”(wiki:The_hammer_and_the_anvil.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Bbarristan_ii_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Cbarristan_ii.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
