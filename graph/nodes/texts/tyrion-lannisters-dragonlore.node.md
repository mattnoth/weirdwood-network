### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

During [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s journey down the river [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne) on the poleboat [Shy Maid](wiki:Shy_Maid), [Griff](wiki:Jon_Connington) commands him to set down all that he knows of [dragonlore](wiki:Dragonlore).(wiki:Tyrion_Lannister's_dragonlore.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Tyrion describes the task as formidable, but he labors at it every day, scratching away as best he can as he sits cross-legged on the boat's cabin roof.
Ten years ago Tyrion managed to get his hands on a fragment of [Unnatural History](wiki:Dragons,_Wyrms,_and_Wyverns:_Their_Unnatural_History) by Septon [Barth](wiki:Barth), but he doubts any of Barth's work found its way across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea). Tyrion thinks there is little chance of him finding [Blood and Fire](wiki:Blood_and_Fire)—sometimes called The Death of Dragons—since the only surviving copy is in the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel). What he really wants is the complete text of [The Fires of the Freehold](wiki:The_Fires_of_the_Freehold), [Galendro](wiki:Galendro)'s history of [Valyria](wiki:Valyria). Tyrion considers visiting a library within the [Black Walls](wiki:Black_Walls) of [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) to seek copies of the books he has in mind.(wiki:Tyrion_Lannister's_dragonlore.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Tyrion chooses not to rely on what he considers to be idle tales—which according to him make up the greater part of accounts on dragons—so a reader of his dragonlore will not have the added task of sifting through blatant untruths.(wiki:Tyrion_Lannister's_dragonlore.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Tyrion thinks that Barth, [Munkun](wiki:Munkun), and [Thomax](wiki:Thomax) hold markedly divergent views concerning the mating habits of dragons.(wiki:Tyrion_Lannister's_dragonlore.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
Exactly how long Tyrion writes for and how much dragonlore is put to paper is unknown, as he is kidnapped by Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) in [Selhorys](wiki:Selhorys)(wiki:Tyrion_Lannister's_dragonlore.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and taken to Volantis separately from Griff.(wiki:Tyrion_Lannister's_dragonlore.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
