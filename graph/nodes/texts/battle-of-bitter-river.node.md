## Narrative Arc

Houses [Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood) and [Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken), ancient rivals, allied together to oppose the invading [Andals](wiki:Andals). According to song, the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) were shattered by 777 charging Andal [knights](wiki:Knight), who were accompanied by seven [septons](wiki:Septon), as seven is a holy number in the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:Battle_of_Bitter_River.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) The Brackens converted to the Faith while the Blackwoods remained loyal to the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods).(wiki:Battle_of_Bitter_River.cite_ref-2)
