## Identity

A devotional hymn to the Mother, one of the seven faces of the Faith of the Seven, praying for mercy and the sparing of sons from war. Sansa Stark sings it in Maegor's Holdfast during the Battle of the Blackwater, with Sandor Clegane's dagger at her throat demanding a song. The moment of singing under mortal threat becomes one of the defining images of Sansa's arc and Sandor's ambiguous chivalry.
