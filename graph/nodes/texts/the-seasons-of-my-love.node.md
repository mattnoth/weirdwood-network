## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

In the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon), [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) whistles the tune, and mentions to [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) that [Tysha](wiki:Tysha), the first girl he ever [bedded](wiki:Bedding), used to sing the song. Bronn notes that he has heard the song in inns and [whorehouses](wiki:Whore).(wiki:The_Seasons_of_My_Love.cite_ref-Ragot42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister) sings the song to Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) within the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep),(wiki:The_Seasons_of_My_Love.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) while [Rymund the Rhymer](wiki:Rymund_the_Rhymer) sings it at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:The_Seasons_of_My_Love.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Marillion](wiki:Marillion) sings "Seasons of My Love" when [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) weds [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) at the [Fingers](wiki:Drearfort).(wiki:The_Seasons_of_My_Love.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
