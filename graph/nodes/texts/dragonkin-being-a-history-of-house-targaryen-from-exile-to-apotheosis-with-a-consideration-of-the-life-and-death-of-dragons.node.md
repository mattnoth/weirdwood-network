### A Feast For Crows[ ]

Dragonkin is one of the stack of books that [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) brings up from the library beneath [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), along with the [Jade Compendium](wiki:Jade_Compendium) which Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) had commanded him to find.(wiki:Dragonkin,_Being_a_History_of_House_Targaryen_from_Exile_to_Apotheosis,_with_a_Consideration_of_the_Life_and_Death_of_Dragons.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Making his way toward the armory Sam drops both books as he goes to catch [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) by the arm. The Jade Compendium is undamaged but Dragonkin had come open as it fell, and a few pages had gotten muddy, including one with a rather nice picture of [Balerion](wiki:Balerion). Sam curses himself for a clumsy oaf as he smooths the pages down and brushes them off.
