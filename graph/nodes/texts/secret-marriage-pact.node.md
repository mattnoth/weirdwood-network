## Origins

The pact was made in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) when [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) was a little girl. Ser [Willem Darry](wiki:Willem_Darry) signed on behalf of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) and Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) signed for [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). The [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos) was a witness.(wiki:Secret_marriage_pact.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The pact was written on parchment, in the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue). It has seals, with gold and ribbons and signatures.

The pact verbatim has not been revealed. [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) was still an immature child when the pact was signed, and since he was not yet ready for the information, Willem did not inform him about it.(wiki:Secret_marriage_pact.cite_ref-Rssm6539.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) When Viserys was around thirteen or fourteen,(wiki:Secret_marriage_pact.cite_ref-3) Ser Willem took ill and died(wiki:Secret_marriage_pact.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) without ever informing him of the marriage pact.(wiki:Secret_marriage_pact.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Neither [Varys](wiki:Varys) nor [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) are aware of the secret pact.(wiki:Secret_marriage_pact.cite_ref-Rssm6539.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

According to Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), the secret marriage pact contains "not a word" about [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen),(wiki:Secret_marriage_pact.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) although Daenerys states after reading the pact that Ser Willem Darry signed "for us".(wiki:Secret_marriage_pact.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) dies at [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak) unaware of the secret pact.(wiki:Secret_marriage_pact.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Secret_marriage_pact.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

### A Feast For Crows

[Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), reveals to his daughter, Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell), that she had secretly been promised to the now-dead Viserys as part of his planned vengeance for his sister, [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell), who was killed in the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing). He informs her that the man she had been promised to is dead, and because of this, her brother [Quentyn](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) has gone across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) to meet with Viserys's sister, [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), and wed her.(wiki:Secret_marriage_pact.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Quentyn journeys to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) with the pact parchment hidden in a flap in his boot. Once presented to Daenerys, Quentyn and his two companions, [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater) and [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood), reveal themselves to the queen as Westerosi [knights](wiki:Knight). When Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) reads the parchment, yellowed with age, he thinks that King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) would have retaliated against [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) if he had known of its contents. Daenerys believes that Viserys would have crossed to Westeros if he had been aware of the pact.

Daenerys surmises that with Viserys dead, Quentyn means to marry her to secure the alliance. Quentyn tries to convince Daenerys to marry him, telling her that it will mean fifty thousand Dornish swords pledged to her service. Daenerys tells him of her upcoming marriage to [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq), however, and remarks that it would have been different if he had come earlier. After Daenerys dismisses her audience, she realizes the pact was executed in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), while they were living in the [house with the red door](wiki:House_with_the_red_door). When Barristan tells her that the sigil of [House Martell](wiki:House_Martell) is a sun transfixed by a spear, Daenerys shivers after remembering [Quaithe](wiki:Quaithe)'s warning to not trust the "sun's son".(wiki:Secret_marriage_pact.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

After the wedding feast, Barristan tells his queen that ancient House Martell has been a leal friend to House Targaryen for more than a century. Daenerys replies that she needs to protect her people, but the Martell spears are far away in Westeros. Daenerys introduces Quentyn to her imprisoned [dragons](wiki:Dragon) and tells him that ["the dragon has three heads"](wiki:Polygamy), suggesting that her marriage to Hizdahr need not be the end of his hopes. However, after Quentyn reacts in fear to the dragons, Daenerys councils him to leave, but he refuses.(wiki:Secret_marriage_pact.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Daenerys flies away on [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) after the dragon causes chaos during the re-opening ceremony of [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit).(wiki:Secret_marriage_pact.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) After Hizdahr assumes control of Meereen in Daenerys's absence, Barristan realises that Quentyn's continued presence is a provocation for the king.(wiki:Secret_marriage_pact.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) In the [audience chamber](wiki:Audience_chamber_(Great_Pyramid)) of the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid), Barristan warns Quentyn to leave Meereen. The unenforceable pact contains nothing about Daenerys or Quentyn, but instead promises Arianne's hand to the late Viserys. He adds that while Daenerys might have considered the marriage alliance while in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), Quentyn is now too late.(wiki:Secret_marriage_pact.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Quentyn decides to tame one of the dragons to prove his worth,(wiki:Secret_marriage_pact.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) but fails and is burned horribly.(wiki:Secret_marriage_pact.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Three days later, he dies in Daenerys's bed.(wiki:Secret_marriage_pact.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### The Winds of Winter

On her way to [Griffin's Roost](wiki:Griffin's_Roost), Arianne Martell wonders about Quentyn's whereabouts if he does not turn out to be with [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) and the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company). She wonders if he has wed Daenerys Targaryen, and thinks that "King Quentyn" sounds silly.(wiki:Secret_marriage_pact.cite_ref-Ar1_13-0) Near Griffin's Roost, Arianne and her party encounter the [Lyseni](wiki:Lyseni) [Lysono Maar](wiki:Lysono_Maar) with a [column](wiki:Column) of Golden Company [sellswords](wiki:Sellsword). His [Valyrian](wiki:Valyrians) appearance reminds her of House Targaryen. While Lysono thinks that is a compliment, Arianne is unnerved, and wonders if that was what Viserys looked like, perhaps it is a good thing he is dead.(wiki:Secret_marriage_pact.cite_ref-Ar2_14-0)
