## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), has his secret [mistress](wiki:Mistress), the [prostitute](wiki:Prostitute) [Shae](wiki:Shae), kept in a [manse near the Iron Gate](wiki:Manse_near_the_Iron_Gate) of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:For_hands_of_gold_are_always_cold,_but_a_woman's_hands_are_warm.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Shae has [Symon Silver Tongue](wiki:Symon_Silver_Tongue) sing to her on nights when Tyrion does not visit. When Symon recognizes Tyrion during one visit, Tyrion swears the musician to secrecy.(wiki:For_hands_of_gold_are_always_cold,_but_a_woman's_hands_are_warm.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Tyrion advises Symon to leave for the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities). Symon responds by playing his new song—which is about Tyrion and Shae—and threatening to sing it for Tyrion's elder sister, Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), or his father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), the new Hand of the King. Symon tries to blackmail Tyrion: if he will not arrange his invitation to the to the tournament of singers that will be held at the wedding feast of King [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), Symon will sing his new song in the winesinks of the city. Tyrion cannot let this information reach Tywin, who has promised to hang any whores that he finds in Tyrion's bed. Tyrion has his [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword), [Bronn](wiki:Bronn), murder Symon. Bronn says he will dispose of Symon's body in the city's [pot-shops](wiki:Bowl_of_brown).(wiki:For_hands_of_gold_are_always_cold,_but_a_woman's_hands_are_warm.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
When Tyrion is imprisoned for [Joffrey's murder](wiki:Purple_Wedding), after Shae gives evidence against him, he drunkenly sings Symon's song. He wishes he had learned all the words before having Symon killed, and considers writing more verses himself.(wiki:For_hands_of_gold_are_always_cold,_but_a_woman's_hands_are_warm.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Although condemned to death, Tyrion escapes the [black cells](wiki:Black_cells) with the aid of Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys). After finding Shae in Tywin's bed within the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand), Tyrion quotes the song's refrain while he strangles her with the Hand's chain of golden hands that she was wearing.(wiki:For_hands_of_gold_are_always_cold,_but_a_woman's_hands_are_warm.cite_ref-Rasos77.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance With Dragons[ ]

In [Essos](wiki:Essos), while traveling with [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis), Tyrion becomes inebriated and sings Symon's song, and he remembers how he had murdered Shae.(wiki:For_hands_of_gold_are_always_cold,_but_a_woman's_hands_are_warm.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
While traveling on the [Selaesori Qhoran](wiki:Selaesori_Qhoran), Tyrion tells [Penny](wiki:Penny) that he had a singer cooked in a stew for writing a song about him, and recalls the lyrics.(wiki:For_hands_of_gold_are_always_cold,_but_a_woman's_hands_are_warm.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Later, Tyrion remembers how he had killed Shae, and recalls the words of the song again.(wiki:For_hands_of_gold_are_always_cold,_but_a_woman's_hands_are_warm.cite_ref-Radwd40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
