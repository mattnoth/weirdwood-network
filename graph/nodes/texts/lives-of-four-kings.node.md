## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) gives a copy of Kaeth's work to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) as a [wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) gift in [300 AC](wiki:300_AC). The book, written by Kaeth himself, is huge, bound in leather, and gorgeously illuminated. Shortly after receiving the gift, Joffrey carves the book up with another gift, his [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) [sword](wiki:Sword) [Widow's Wail](wiki:Widow's_Wail), which leaves only three copies remaining written by Kaeth himself. Several of the guests are shocked by the wasteful and callous destruction of the book, and Tyrion is infuriated.(wiki:Lives_of_Four_Kings.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
