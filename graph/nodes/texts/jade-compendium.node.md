## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

The Jade Compendium is one of the stack of books that [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) brings up from the library beneath [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), along with [Dragonkin](wiki:Dragonkin,_Being_a_History_of_House_Targaryen_from_Exile_to_Apotheosis,_with_a_Consideration_of_the_Life_and_Death_of_Dragons), which Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) had commanded him to find. Making his way toward the armory Sam drops both books as he goes to catch [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) by the arm. The Jade Compendium is undamaged but a few pages of Dragonkin are muddied.(wiki:Jade_Compendium.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Before departing the [Wall](wiki:Wall) Samwell overhears Aemon tell [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) that he left a book for him in his chambers, and that there is a passage he might find interesting.(wiki:Jade_Compendium.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) tells [Clydas](wiki:Clydas) that he looked at the book Maester Aemon left him. When Clydas expresses amazement at [Azor Ahai](wiki:Azor_Ahai)'s legendary heat-producing sword, [Lightbringer](wiki:Lightbringer), Jon states that [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s sword is cold to the touch.(wiki:Jade_Compendium.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
