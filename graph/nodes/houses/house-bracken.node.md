## Origins

### Age of Heroes[ ]

The Brackens have the blood of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) in their veins and were kings of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) during the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Petty [river kings](wiki:River_kings) from House Bracken claimed dominion over the mouth of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) in the [Age of the Hundred Kingdoms](wiki:Age_of_the_Hundred_Kingdoms).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
The Brackens have an old and bitter feud against their neighbors, the [Blackwoods](wiki:House_Blackwood) of [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall). According to the Brackens, they were kings who ruled the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) but were usurped by their vassals, the Blackwoods.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) According to the Blackwoods, however, the Brackens were petty [lords](wiki:Lord) and [horse](wiki:Horse) breeders who hired [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords) to usurp the Blackwood kings.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### Andal Riverlands[ ]

The Brackens and Blackwoods allied together against the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals), but the First Men families were defeated in the [Battle of the Bitter River](wiki:Battle_of_the_Bitter_River).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) The feud has continued throughout the years, aided by House Bracken's conversion to the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) after the Andal victory.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-10) The Blackwoods believe the Brackens poisoned the [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) in the [godswood of Raventree Hall](wiki:Godswood_of_Raventree_Hall).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) [Battle Valley](wiki:Battle_Valley) and the village of [Blackbuckle](wiki:Blackbuckle) are part of the disputed land. There have been numerous peaces between the Brackens and Blackwoods, many sealed by marriage, but the feud always restarts.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) Benedict Rivers was a bastard born from both houses who eventually became King [Benedict I Justman](wiki:Benedict_I_Justman).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)
A Lord Bracken died in the fighting between the [Teagues](wiki:House_Teague) and the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Arlan III Durrandon](wiki:Arlan_III_Durrandon).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3)
When Lady [Agnes Blackwood](wiki:Agnes_Blackwood) led the resistance of the river lords to [Harwyn Hoare](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), Lord [Lothar Bracken](wiki:Lothar_Bracken) attacked Agnes's army from behind. It is possible Lothar hoped to become [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident) after Harwyn defeated the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Arrec Durrandon](wiki:Arrec_Durrandon), but [House Hoare](wiki:House_Hoare) instead took the riverlands for themselves. Harwyn starved Lothar to death when he later rose in rebellion.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-4)

### Targaryen Era[ ]

House Bracken aided [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) during the [Wars of Conquest](wiki:Wars_of_Conquest) and rebelled against Harwyn's grandson, King [Harren the Black](wiki:Harren_Hoare) of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) The Brackens and the Blackwoods had been weakened by a private war a decade before [the Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), however, and [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) granted the new title of [Lord Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident) to Lord [Edmyn Tully](wiki:Edmyn_Tully) of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) negotiated a double wedding between the Brackens and the Blackwoods.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Ser [Lyle Bracken](wiki:Lyle_Bracken) was one of the seven [knights](wiki:Knight) of the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons) who were slain during King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen)'s [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven) in [42 AC](wiki:42_AC).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Ser [Olyver Bracken](wiki:Olyver_Bracken) and Ser [Raymund Mallery](wiki:Raymund_Mallery), knights in Maegor's [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), defected to the cause of Prince [Jaehaerys Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) in [48 AC](wiki:48_AC). Since they had broken their vows to protect Maegor, King Jaehaerys I allowed them to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) after he gained the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Olyver and Raymund led a rebellion of former [Faith Militant](wiki:Faith_Militant) among the Watch in [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), with Lord [Walton Stark](wiki:Walton_Stark_(son_of_Brandon)) beheading Olyver with [Ice](wiki:Ice) at [Rimegate](wiki:Rimegate).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### The Dance of the Dragons[ ]

[Amos Bracken](wiki:Amos_Bracken) fought a duel with [Samwell Blackwood](wiki:Samwell_Blackwood) over [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) in [112 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest),(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) but the princess eventually wed Ser [Laenor Velaryon](wiki:Laenor_Velaryon).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the Brackens declared for [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) and the [greens](wiki:Greens) against Rhaenyra's [blacks](wiki:Blacks). Ser Amos killed Samwell during the [Battle of the Burning Mill](wiki:Battle_of_the_Burning_Mill), only to then be slain by an arrow allegedly fired by [Alysanne Blackwood](wiki:Alysanne_Blackwood). Ser [Raylon Rivers](wiki:Raylon_Rivers) led the Bracken survivors back to [Stone Hedge](wiki:Stone_Hedge), but Lord [Humfrey Bracken](wiki:Humfrey_Bracken) had already [been captured](wiki:Taking_of_Stone_Hedge) in the meantime by Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Since Aegon II began punishing lords who had supported Rhaenyra after her death, Humfrey joined the blacks in support of Rhaenyra's son, the future [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), in [131 AC](wiki:131_AC).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### Bittersteel and Bloodraven[ ]

[Lord Bracken](wiki:Lord_Bracken_(Hand_of_the_King)) served as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to King [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen). His elder daughter [Barba](wiki:Barba_Bracken) was the fifth mistress of Aegon IV and the mother of [Aegor Rivers](wiki:Aegor_Rivers), the [Great Bastard](wiki:Great_Bastard) known as Bittersteel. However, House Bracken fell from favor after Lord Bracken began talking openly of marrying Barba to Aegon IV after Queen [Naerys](wiki:Naerys) nearly died in childbirth. Lord Bracken's second daughter, [Bethany](wiki:Bethany_Bracken), was deliberately groomed by her father and elder sister to win back the king's favor by displacing [Melissa Blackwood](wiki:Melissa_Blackwood), with whom Aegon IV had replaced Barba as mistress.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
Bethany succeeded in displacing Melissa, but she found the king's embraces distressing and so sought comfort in Ser [Terrence Toyne](wiki:Terrence_Toyne) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1) After the affair was discovered, both Bethany and her father were executed.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-2)
According to Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey), [Lord Bracken](wiki:Lord_Bracken_(father_of_Otho)) was to sail with [Myrish](wiki:Myr) crossbowmen to aid [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) and Bittersteel during the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), but was delayed by storms.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
Ser [Otho Bracken](wiki:Otho_Bracken) slew Lord [Quentyn Blackwood](wiki:Quentyn_Blackwood) at a [tourney](wiki:Tourney) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in [206 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), reinvigorating the feud. Three years later Otho and some Blackwoods participated in the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) Ser [Steffon Fossoway](wiki:Steffon_Fossoway) considered convincing Otho or the Blackwoods to fight for Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) in his [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven), but decided it would be impossible to make them both fight on the same side.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-1) When Lord Bracken lay dying, Septon [Sefton](wiki:Sefton_Staunton) explained that Otho would inherit and the Blackwoods would not suffer him, meaning war. Lord Bracken's eldest son and heir had died in the [Great Spring Sickness](wiki:Great_Spring_Sickness). Lord [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) known as Bloodraven, was the son of Melissa Blackwood and thus was considered unlikely to stop it.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1)

### Recent History[ ]

Lord [Bracken](wiki:Lord_Bracken_(Aerys_II)) and Lord [Blackwood](wiki:Lord_Blackwood_(Aerys_II)) came to Riverrun when [Lysa Tully](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) was young to put their feud before the judgment of [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), [Lord Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident), with Lord Bracken's singer performing during the visit.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) Hoster attempted to marry Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) to Lord Bracken's daughter.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
The daughter of [Elys Waynwood](wiki:Elys_Waynwood) was on her way to marry a Bracken when she was kidnapped by [Burned Men](wiki:Burned_Men) and not heard from again.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Three Bracken men-at-arms, [Kurleket](wiki:Kurleket), [Lharys](wiki:Lharys), and [Mohor](wiki:Mohor), assist Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), the daughter of Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), in arresting [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) at the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) The three die during the trek to the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn), slain by [mountain clansmen](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)
After the death of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) burns out the Brackens, laying waste to their fields, slaughtering their [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) and leaving [Stone Hedge](wiki:Burning_of_Stone_Hedge) a smoking ruin. Lord [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken) is listed among the nobles commanded by King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) to do him fealty or be declared traitors.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)
Jonos arrives at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) after the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps). At the war council in the [Great Hall of Riverrun](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Riverrun), Jonos suggests to pledge fealty to King [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), perhaps only to contradict Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood)'s previous statement of marching against [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal). After [Greatjon Umber](wiki:Greatjon_Umber) speaks, Jonos joins the other river lords in declaring [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) to be [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord Jonos [recovers Stone Hedge](wiki:Liberation_of_Stone_Hedge) from the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister), but he is wounded and his nephew [Hendry](wiki:Hendry_Bracken) is killed in the fighting.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord [Quenten Banefort](wiki:Quenten_Banefort) is held captive by House Bracken.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0) Among those claimed to have been murdered by the Lannisters by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) is [Harry Rivers](wiki:Harry_Rivers), the Bastard of Bracken.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)
While traveling by a burned inn in Bracken lands, [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth), Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey), and Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) find the bodies of tavern wenches hanged for associating with Lannister troops; a sign says, They Lay With Lions.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
Lord Jonos is among the seven nobles who launch Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully)'s funeral boat at Riverrun.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)
Following the death of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) and [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident), in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Jonos surrenders to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) and receives a royal pardon.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0) The Brackens then fight [House Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood), who remain loyal to [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark), on behalf of [House Baratheon of King's Landing](wiki:House_Baratheon_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Dead soldiers bearing the colors of House Bracken have washed up on the [Quiet Isle](wiki:Quiet_Isle). According to the [Elder Brother](wiki:Elder_Brother_(Quiet_Isle)), they are buried next to their Blackwood enemies.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0) The Brackens besiege the Blackwoods at [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood) refuses to surrender to Jonos Bracken, leading Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) to attempt to resolve the [siege of Raventree](wiki:Siege_of_Raventree). Lord Jonos desires to gain the east bank of the [Widow's Wash](wiki:Widow's_Wash), from [Crossbow Ridge](wiki:Crossbow_Ridge) to [Rutting Meadow](wiki:Rutting_Meadow), [Grindcorn Mill](wiki:Grindcorn_Mill), [Lord's Mill](wiki:Lord's_Mill), [Muddy Hall](wiki:Muddy_Hall), the [Ravishment](wiki:Ravishment), [Battle Valley](wiki:Battle_Valley), [Oldforge](wiki:Oldforge), [Buckle](wiki:Buckle), [Blackbuckle](wiki:Blackbuckle), [Cairns](wiki:Cairns), [Claypool](wiki:Claypool), [Mudgrave](wiki:Mudgrave), [Waspwood](wiki:Waspwood), [Lorgen's Wood](wiki:Lorgen's_Wood), [Greenhill](wiki:Greenhill), [Barba's Teats](wiki:Teats), and [Honeytree](wiki:Honeytree). However, Tytos only agrees to transfer [Woodhedge](wiki:Woodhedge), Crossbow Ridge, Buckle, Lord's Mill, and Honeytree. Jaime orders Jonos to send one of his daughters to serve Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)
It is revealed a dozen men related by blood to Jonos were killed in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

We've had a hundred peaces with the Brackens, many sealed with marriages. There's [Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood) blood in every Bracken, and Bracken blood in every Blackwood. [The Old King's](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) Peace lasted half a century. But then [some fresh](wiki:Quentyn_Blackwood) [quarrel broke out](wiki:Otho_Bracken), and the old wounds opened and began to bleed again. That's how it always happens, [my father](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood) says. So long as men remember the wrongs done to their forebears, no peace will ever last. So we go on century after century, with us hating the Brackens and them hating us. My father says there will never be an end to it.(wiki:House_Bracken.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-11)
