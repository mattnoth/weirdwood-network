## Origins

During the [Faith Militant](wiki:Faith_Militant) [uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising), Ser Joffrey Doggett was chosen as the new [Grand Captain](wiki:Grand_Captain) of the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons), becoming a prominent rebel commander in the campaign against King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen). As a result, Maegor the Cruel later used [Balerion](wiki:Balerion) to set [aflame](wiki:Dragonflame) the seat of the Doggetts and reducing it to ash, burning alive the father, mother, and young sister of Joffrey, alongside their [sworn swords](wiki:Sworn_sword), serving men, and chattel.(wiki:House_Doggett.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Joffrey Doggett survived Maegor's reign and made peace with his nephew, King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen). With the Faith Militant abolished, Jaehaerys granted him a place in his [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:House_Doggett.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:House_Doggett.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
