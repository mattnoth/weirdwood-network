## Identity

House Bar Emmon of Sharp Point is a noble house from the crownlands, sworn to Dragonstone. Their blazon is a leaping blue swordfish on fretty silver on white (wiki:House_Bar_Emmon).

## Origins

Founded by Togarion Bar Emmon, an Andal warlord, during the Coming of the Andals. Togarion established a small kingdom north of the Blackwater Rush but was driven to Massey's Hook by the Darklyn kings of Duskendale. He married the daughter of King Josua Massey of Stonedance and built Sharp Point as the new seat for House Bar Emmon (wiki:House_Bar_Emmon).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Baratheon of Dragonstone (track_b: Overlords). Historically sworn to House Durrandon, then House Targaryen after Aegon's Conquest. Supported Aegon the Conqueror over Storm King Argilac the Arrogant. Supported Princess Rhaenys at the Great Council of 101 AC and the blacks during the Dance of the Dragons (wiki:House_Bar_Emmon).

## Appearances & Description

Sigil: a leaping blue swordfish on fretty silver on white (wiki:Rasos75). Their seat is Sharp Point in the crownlands (track_b: Seat).

## Narrative Arc

During ACOK, Lord Duram Bar Emmon supports Stannis Baratheon's claim to the Iron Throne as a vassal of Dragonstone. Stannis considers the lords sworn to him, including Bar Emmon, a poor crop. Duram turns a splotchy grey during the burning of sept statues at Dragonstone. The house contributes the ship Swordfish to Stannis's fleet, which is destroyed by wildfire at the Battle of the Blackwater (wiki:House_Bar_Emmon). After the Blackwater, House Bar Emmon is among the few houses that remain loyal to Stannis (wiki:House_Bar_Emmon).
