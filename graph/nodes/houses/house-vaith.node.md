## Identity

House Vaith is a lordly house from Dorne, seated at Vaith (wiki:House_Vaith).

## Origins

The Vaiths were adventuring Andals who settled in the hills east of the deep dunes and sands of Dorne. The nearby river soon took their name (wiki:House_Vaith).
