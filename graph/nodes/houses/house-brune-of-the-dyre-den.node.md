## Origins

The [Brothers Brune](wiki:Brothers_Brune) were legendary champions who brought peace to [Crackclaw Point](wiki:Crackclaw_Point).(wiki:House_Brune_of_the_Dyre_Den.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

[Bernarr Brune](wiki:Bernarr_Brune) was squire to Lord [Alyn Stokeworth](wiki:Alyn_Stokeworth), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to King [Aegon I](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) and [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen). When Lord Alyn was slain by the rebel [Harren the Red](wiki:Harren_the_Red), Bernarr cut down the [outlaw](wiki:Outlaw) and was rewarded with [knighthood](wiki:Knight). Bernarr was later one of the champions in [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen)'s [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven).(wiki:House_Brune_of_the_Dyre_Den.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the Brunes declared for [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen). Brune forces joined Lord [Walys Mooton](wiki:Walys_Mooton) to liberate [Rook's Rest](wiki:Rook's_Rest) from the control of [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen)'s [greens](wiki:Greens).(wiki:House_Brune_of_the_Dyre_Den.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Lord Brune marched from [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with Lord [Manfryd Mooton](wiki:Manfryd_Mooton), Lord [Leowyn Corbray](wiki:Leowyn_Corbray), and Ser [Rennifer Crabb](wiki:Rennifer_Crabb) after Aegon's death.(wiki:House_Brune_of_the_Dyre_Den.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Brunes were with [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone), at the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:House_Brune_of_the_Dyre_Den.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
