## Origins

### Dance of the Dragons[ ]

Lord Hornwood declared for the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) in the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). In late [130 AC](wiki:130_AC) he left the north and marched towards [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) as part of the army led by his liege lord, Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark). Once in the capital, his younger son disappeared in [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom) during the [Hour of the Wolf](wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf).(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
[Hallis Hornwood](wiki:Hallis_Hornwood) and [Timotty Snow](wiki:Timotty_Snow) founded the [Wolf Pack](wiki:Wolf_Pack) after the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### Recent History[ ]

A Lord Hornwood lost ownership of an [holdfast](wiki:Holdfast).(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
Females Hornwood have married into [House Karstark](wiki:House_Karstark), [House Flint](wiki:House_Flint), and [House Tallhart](wiki:House_Tallhart).(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
Members of House Hornwood were present at the [Tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) of [281 AC](wiki:281_AC).(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Citadel_12-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) sees the Hornwoods at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) after [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls the northern banners. Lord [Halys Hornwood](wiki:Halys_Hornwood) requests the return of a [holdfast](wiki:Holdfast) taken from his grandfather, hunting rights near a ridge, and permission to dam the [White Knife](wiki:White_Knife).(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
When the northern army splits at the [Twins](wiki:Twins), Lord Halys goes with Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton)'s army and his heir, [Daryn](wiki:Daryn_Hornwood), accompanies Robb to free [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun). The banners of House Hornwood are spotted by [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) at the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork), at which Halys is killed.(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Daryn is one of [Robb's personal guard](wiki:Robb's_personal_guard) at the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood), where he is killed by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Halys's widow, [Donella](wiki:Donella_Hornwood), is now [Lady of the Hornwood](wiki:Lady_of_the_Hornwood), but a matter of succession arises. House Hornwood is connected by the female line with [House Karstark](wiki:House_Karstark), [House Flint](wiki:House_Flint), and [House Tallhart](wiki:House_Tallhart), while Halys's [bastard](wiki:Bastard), [Larence Snow](wiki:Larence_Snow), is fostered at [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) by [House Glover](wiki:House_Glover). Donella arrives at Winterfell for the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast) and meets with Bran and Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel). She warns them that [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), Lord Bolton's bastard, is gathering soldiers at the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort). Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin) advises her to increase her harvest storage from a fifth to a quarter. [Mors Umber](wiki:Mors_Umber) and Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) advance themselves as possible suitors to Lady Donella. It is suggested that [Beren Tallhart](wiki:Beren_Tallhart), a son of Halys's sister [Berena](wiki:Berena_Tallhart), could take the Hornwood lands and name to prevent the House from dying out.(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)
Lady Hornwood is captured by and forced to marry Ramsay, the Bastard of Bolton. Her cousin, Wyman Manderly, sends [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) men to take the [Hornwood](wiki:Hornwood) castle, and there is [fighting in the Hornwood](wiki:Fighting_in_the_Hornwood) between Houses [Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) and [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton).(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Racok28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Rodrik tries to rescue Donella, but discovers that Ramsay locked her in a tower and allowed her to starve to death. Rodrik tells Luwin that Ramsay and Donella's wedding appears to have been lawful and that she designated Ramsay as her heir before her death.(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1)
When Rodrik aims to retake [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) from its [ironborn captors](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell), [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) spies some Hornwood banners among his host. Ramsay kills Rodrik in the [battle at Winterfell](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell), abducts Theon, and [sacks Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell).(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After the [fighting at the fords of the Trident](wiki:Fighting_at_the_fords_of_the_Trident), Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) leaves, among others, a hundred Hornwood bowmen to guard the [Trident](wiki:Trident).(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Larence Snow is held as a captive by [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) at [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth) spots the badge of House Hornwood among those collected from the dead after the [battle at Duskendale](wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale).(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) requires that Lord Wyman Manderly abandon his claim to Hornwood lands as a condition for peace with the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)
[Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) learns that [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) from along the [White Knife](wiki:White_Knife) and [Hornwood](wiki:Hornwood) have been seeking protection at [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) with [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly). Some Hornwood men are also reported to have joined [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow)'s force marching on [Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin).(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) sees the moose of the Hornwoods among the banners flying above [Barrow Hall](wiki:Barrow_Hall).(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
[Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) writes to [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) that his growing army contains some Hornwood men who survived [Ramsay's betrayal](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell) of Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) at Winterfell.(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
During the wedding of "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)", actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole), to Ramsay in [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), Ramsay is introduced as [Lord of the Hornwood](wiki:Lord_of_the_Hornwood).(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) As the Manderlys are ostensibly supportive of [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton), Lord Manderly has presumably relinquished Hornwood to Ramsay. Lady [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin) tells Theon that the Hornwood men who are at Winterfell for Ramsay's wedding and its [upcoming siege](wiki:Siege_of_Winterfell) have not forgotten how Ramsay treated Lady Donella.(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)
Larence Snow, the late Lord Halys Hornwood's [bastard](wiki:Bastard), is presumably freed from captivity when Stannis [recovers Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte) from Asha Greyjoy.(wiki:House_Hornwood.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
