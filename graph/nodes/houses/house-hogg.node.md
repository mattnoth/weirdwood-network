## Identity

House Hogg of Sow's Horn is a house of landed knights sworn to House Hayford. Sow's Horn is located in the crownlands near the border with the riverlands (wiki:House_Hogg.cite_ref-Raffc27).

## Allegiances

House Hogg is `SWORN_TO` House Hayford as their overlord (track_b: Overlord). They are located in the `REGION_OF` Crownlands (track_b: Region). Their seat is Sow's Horn (track_b: Seat). Head of house is Ser Roger Hogg, the Knight of Sow's Horn (track_b: Head).

## Narrative Arc

Historically, Jon Hogg ("Big Jon Hogg") fought for King Maegor I at the Battle at the Great Fork and was later killed by Warrior's Sons. Ser Harys Hogg was a smallfolk favourite at tourneys under Jaehaerys I. Priscella Hogg was accused of a poisoning plot and condemned to lose her nose unless she joined the Faith (wiki:House_Hogg).

During the War of the Five Kings, Sow's Horn was attacked by Karstark men and Lannister forces under Ser Amory Lorch, but the stout stone of Sow's Horn resisted the attempt to burn it down. Ser Jaime Lannister met with Ser Roger Hogg, who recounted the attacks (wiki:House_Hogg.cite_ref-Raffc27).
