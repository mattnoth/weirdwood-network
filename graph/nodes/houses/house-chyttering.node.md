## Identity

House Chyttering is a noble house of the crownlands sworn to House Baratheon of King's Landing. Their arms are three golden bendlets enhanced on white (wiki:House_Chyttering).

## Allegiances

`OVERLORD_OF` House Baratheon of King's Landing → House Chyttering (track_b: Overlord). `REGION_OF` Crownlands (track_b: Region). `RULES` Lucos Chyttering (track_b: Head).

## Narrative Arc

In ACOK, Lord Chyttering dies at the Battle of the Blackwater while fighting for Stannis Baratheon. In ASOS, young Lucos Chyttering, called "Little Lucos," is regarded by Stannis as one of the few good men remaining to him after the Blackwater defeat (wiki:Rasos10).
