## Identity

House Wylde is a noble house from Rain House in the Stormlands. They blazon their arms with a blue-green rain on a field of white, with a maelstrom in the chief. (wiki:House_Wylde)

## Allegiances

## Narrative Arc

Casper Wylde `RULES` House Wylde. (track_b: Head) During the War of the Five Kings, Ser Gladden Wylde was among those killed in the Battle of the Blackwater fighting for Stannis. (wiki:House_Wylde.cite_ref-Racok33)

In ADWD, the Golden Company under Jon Connington seizes Rain House. Casper Wylde's young bride, a Fell, betrays the castle to the invaders, and Connington takes Casper's former seat as a base of operations. Rain House is one of several Stormlands castles taken by the Golden Company during their invasion. (wiki:House_Wylde.cite_ref-Radwd61)

Historically, Jasper Wylde served as Hand of the King during the regency of Aegon III, earning the nickname "Ironrod" for his stern nature. Raynard Wylde served on the Kingsguard of King Maekar I Targaryen. (wiki:House_Wylde)
