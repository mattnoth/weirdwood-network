## Narrative Arc

The Umbers are one of the most prominent and loyal northern houses. The Greatjon was among the first lords to rally to Robb Stark, famously losing two fingers to Grey Wind before becoming one of Robb's most vocal champions and the one who proclaimed him King in the North. At the Red Wedding, the Greatjon was captured rather than killed. In ADWD, the house splits: Mors "Crowfood" Umber joins Stannis while Hother "Whoresbane" Umber treats with the Boltons, reflecting the house's divided loyalties during the post-Red Wedding northern upheaval. The Umbers are cited across all five books (wiki:House_Umber.cite_refs).
