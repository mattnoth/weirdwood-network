## Identity

House Reyne was a noble house from the westerlands, formerly seated at Castamere. They held the title Lord of Castamere. The house is now extinct, destroyed by Tywin Lannister during the Reyne-Tarbeck rebellion. (wiki:House_Reyne)

## Heraldry & Sigil

## Narrative Arc

House Reyne was the most powerful vassal of House Lannister, rivaling the main branch in wealth and power. When Lord Tytos Lannister proved a weak overlord, the Reynes and Tarbecks grew bold in their defiance. Tywin Lannister, then a young man, crushed both houses utterly in the Reyne-Tarbeck rebellion, flooding the mines of Castamere and extinguishing the line. The destruction of the Reynes inspired the song "The Rains of Castamere," which became a feared symbol of Lannister ruthlessness. (wiki:House_Reyne)
