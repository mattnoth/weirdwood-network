## Origins

House Celtigar is an ancient and proud house, with the blood of old [Valyria](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) in its veins.(wiki:House_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) [Crispian Celtigar](wiki:Crispian_Celtigar) served as [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin) to King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen).(wiki:House_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

The Celtigars claim dominion over [Crackclaw Point](wiki:Crackclaw_Point), but the inhabitants do not acknowledge their claim. When they surrendered peacefully to Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) during the [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), the people of Crackclaw were freed from pledging fealty to anyone but the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). The Celtigars occasionally send men there to collect taxes. If Lord Celtigar sends enough collectors, a few come back to him.(wiki:House_Celtigar.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord [Bartimos Celtigar](wiki:Bartimos_Celtigar) sat on the [black council](wiki:Black_council) as [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin) to Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen).(wiki:House_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

The Celtigars' castle is rumored to hold fabulous treasures that bear witness to their wealth. Among Lord [Ardrian Celtigar](wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar)'s property there are [Myrish](wiki:Myr) carpets, [Volantene](wiki:Volantis) glass, [gold](wiki:Gold) and [silver](wiki:Silver) plate, [jeweled](wiki:Jewelry) cups, magnificent [hawks](wiki:Hawk), a trained [sea eagle](wiki:Eagle), an [axe](wiki:Armament) of [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel), chests of [rubies](wiki:Rubies), [a horn](wiki:House_Celtigar_horn) said to summon [krakens](wiki:Kraken) from under the sea, and many fine [wines](wiki:Wine).(wiki:House_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:House_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Lord [Ardrian Celtigar](wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar) answers [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s summons when he crowns himself king and calls his banners. Stannis comments to Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) his disgust about having to sup with his [lords](wiki:Lord), among them Ardrian, who keeps asking which [storm lords](wiki:Storm_lords) will join him. Lord Celtigar's contribution to Stannis's fleet is the ornate ship *[Red Claw](wiki:Red_Claw)*. Ardrian is made prisoner at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), and he bends the knee to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_I_Baratheon).(wiki:House_Celtigar.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Lord Celtigar remains at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) after the Blackwater.(wiki:House_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Ser [Axell Florent](wiki:Axell_Florent) suggests that Stannis sack [Claw Isle](wiki:Claw_Isle) as a reprisal for Ardrian's surrender. Davos convinces Stannis otherwise, and he is named [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) by Stannis for his honest counsel.(wiki:House_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Lord Celtigar declares at [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s trial for the [death of King Joffrey](wiki:Purple_Wedding) that he once heard Tyrion threatening the king.(wiki:House_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
