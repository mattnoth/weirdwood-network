## Identity

House Egen is a noble house from the Vale, one of the principal families sworn to House Arryn (wiki:House_Egen).

## Appearances & Description

Their sigil is a yellow sun, white crescent moon, and silver star on blue chief above white, according to semi-canon sources. Their motto is "By Day or Night" (wiki:House_Egen).

## Narrative Arc

Ser Vardis Egen served as captain of the guards at the Eyrie and fought as Lysa Arryn's champion in Tyrion Lannister's trial by combat, where he was killed by Bronn (wiki:House_Egen).
