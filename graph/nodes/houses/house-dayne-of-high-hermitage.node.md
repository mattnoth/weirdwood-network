## Narrative Arc

Ser Gerold Dayne, known as "Darkstar," is the most prominent member of this house. He resents that he is not regarded as highly as Ser Arthur Dayne, the legendary Sword of the Morning from the main branch. Princess Arianne Martell enlists Gerold for her plot to crown Myrcella Baratheon. When the plan collapses at the Greenblood, Gerold wounds Myrcella and flees (wiki:House_Dayne). Prince Doran Martell tasks Obara Sand with leading Ser Balon Swann of the Kingsguard to hunt Darkstar at High Hermitage (wiki:House_Dayne).
