## Identity

House Martell, formally House Nymeros Martell, is the ruling Great House of Dorne, one of the constituent regions of the Seven Kingdoms. They rule from the Old Palace within the shadow city of Sunspear (wiki:House_Martell). House Martell is ancient and noble, allied with House Targaryen for over a century (wiki:House_Martell).

## Origins

House Martell was founded by Morgan Martell, an Andal adventurer who settled between the Greenblood and the Broken Arm, defeating local First Men including Houses Wade and Shell (wiki:House_Martell). The Martells were initially lesser lords, vassals of Houses Jordayne, Allyrion, and Yronwood (wiki:House_Martell). Around 700 BC, Mors Martell married Nymeria of the Rhoynar; combining their strength, they united all of Dorne in Nymeria's War. The union saw the Martells adopt Rhoynish customs: the title "Prince" instead of King, and inheritance by the eldest child regardless of sex (wiki:House_Martell).

## Narrative Arc

Dorne remained neutral at the outset of the War of the Five Kings, though expected to support Renly Baratheon (wiki:House_Martell). Tyrion Lannister secured Dornish loyalty by offering a betrothal between Princess Myrcella Baratheon and Prince Trystane Martell, justice for Princess Elia's murder, and a small council seat (wiki:House_Martell). Prince Oberyn Martell traveled to King's Landing seeking justice for Elia, served as Tyrion's champion in his trial by combat, and was killed by Ser Gregor Clegane while demanding Gregor confess to Elia's murder (wiki:House_Martell). Prince Doran arrested the Sand Snakes to prevent war. His daughter Arianne attempted to crown Myrcella as queen based on Dornish inheritance law; the plot failed and Myrcella was disfigured by Darkstar (wiki:House_Martell). Doran revealed to Arianne that he had been secretly working to restore House Targaryen since Elia's death, sending Prince Quentyn to court Daenerys (wiki:House_Martell). Quentyn reached Meereen but was refused and died attempting to tame a dragon (wiki:House_Martell). Doran dispatched his Sand Snake nieces to King's Landing to infiltrate the court and the Faith (wiki:House_Martell).
