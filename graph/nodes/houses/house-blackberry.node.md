## Identity

House Blackberry is a noble house of the Seven Kingdoms. Two members serve House Baratheon of Dragonstone, so the house is probably from the crownlands or the stormlands (wiki:House_Blackberry).

## Allegiances

`OVERLORD_OF` House Baratheon of Dragonstone → House Blackberry (track_b: Overlord). `REGION_OF` Crownlands (track_b: Region), though the wiki also suggests possibly stormlands (wiki:House_Blackberry).

## Narrative Arc

In ASOS, when Davos Seaworth arrives at the gate of Dragonstone after the Battle of the Blackwater, he asks about Jate Blackberry, captain of the gate, but the new guardsmen do not know the name and believe him dead. Omer Blackberry is one of Edric Storm's protectors when the youth flees Dragonstone (wiki:Rasos10, wiki:Rasos63).
