## Narrative Arc

Lord Cressey was part of Lord Manfryd Mooton's diplomatic mission to Braavos in 133 AC during the regency of King Aegon III Targaryen. While Manfryd treated with the Sealord, Lords Cressey and Follard spread bribes among the Braavosi keyholders, magisters, and other notables (wiki:Fire_and_Blood). No members appear in the main series.
