## Origins

House Fossoway is an old noble house of [First Men](wiki:First_Men) origin from the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes). The Fossoways claim descent from [Foss the Archer](wiki:Foss_the_Archer), a son of [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand) renowned for shooting [apples](wiki:Apple) off the head of any maid that took his fancy.(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)
During the early reign of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), [Martyn Tyrell](wiki:Martyn_Tyrell) was married to Lady [Florence Fossoway](wiki:Florence_Fossoway), who increased [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell)'s incomes by a third. When Martyn served as [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin), it was his clever wife, Lady Florence, who performed most of his duties. When Lord [Oakheart](wiki:House_Oakheart) held a [tourney](wiki:Tourney) to celebrate the birth of a son, Prince [Baelon Targaryen](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) unhorsed both [Fossoway twins](wiki:Fossoway_twins).(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_.E2.80.93_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), House Fossoway supported King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) and the [greens](wiki:Greens). [Owen Fossoway](wiki:Owen_Fossoway), Lord of [Cider Hall](wiki:Cider_Hall), was a member of the [Caltrops](wiki:Caltrops), a group of conspirators that wanted the [dragonseeds](wiki:Dragonseeds) [Ulf the White](wiki:Ulf_the_White) and [Hard Hugh Hammer](wiki:Hugh_Hammer) dead. Owen was killed at the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton).(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_.E2.80.93_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) In an unknown time between [130 AC](wiki:130_AC) and [209 AC](wiki:209_AC), the house lost the lordship.(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall.cite_ref-Rawoiafcider_hall.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall.cite_ref-knightly_4-2)
The red apple of the Fossoways was spotted by Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) among the heraldry on display at [Ashford](wiki:Ashford) in 209 AC. Ser [Steffon Fossoway](wiki:Steffon_Fossoway) and his cousin [Raymun](wiki:Raymun_Fossoway) both attended the [tourney](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) at [Ashford Meadow](wiki:Ashford_Meadow), with Steffon intending to enter the lists. Raymun served as his squire until the [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven). After promising Duncan to take his side, Steffon revealed his intention to join Prince [Maekar Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_Targaryen) and the accusers. Steffon justified himself to his cousin Raymun by alleging he would be "Lord Fossoway" at the end of the day.(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall.cite_ref-14) Raymun instead supported Dunk and to mark himself on the field, Ser Raymun, newly [knighted](wiki:Knight) by Ser [Lyonel Baratheon](wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon), repainted the apple on his shield green, founding [House Fossoway of New Barrel](wiki:House_Fossoway_of_New_Barrel).(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)
Ser [Derrick Fossoway](wiki:Derrick_Fossoway) was an exiled knight of dark repute who was a member of the [Band of Nine](wiki:Band_of_Nine), later causing the [War of the Ninepenny Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Ninepenny_Kings).(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The banners of the red-apple Fossoways are spotted beneath the walls of [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge). During the feast with [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)'s army which [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Tully) attends at Bitterbridge, Ser [Tanton Fossoway](wiki:Tanton_Fossoway) climbs on the table and swears to slay [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) in single combat.(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) During a parley, Renly boasts to his brother [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) about having the support of the Fossoways.(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
Both branches of House Fossoway turn to Stannis after Renly's death. Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose) acknowledges Ser [Jon](wiki:Jon_Fossoway) of the green-apple Fossoways and Ser [Bryan](wiki:Bryan_Fossoway) of the red during his parley with Stannis. During Stannis's [siege](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)) of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), Cortnay's lieutenant is Lord [Elwood Meadows](wiki:Elwood_Meadows), a cousin to the Fossoways, who immediately surrenders the castle after Cortnay's death.(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)
The red apple of the Fossoways of [Cider Hall](wiki:Cider_Hall) is spotted amongst the banners in the vanguard of Stannis's army, led by Ser [Guyard Morrigen](wiki:Guyard_Morrigen), as it approaches [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall.cite_ref-Racok52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) During the ensuring [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), [Lothor Brune](wiki:Lothor_Brune) cuts his way through half a hundred Fossoway men-at-arms to capture Ser Jon from the green apple branch and slay Ser Bryan and Ser [Edwyd](wiki:Edwyd_Fossoway) of the red apple branch, earning the name Lothor Apple-Eater. After the battle, both branches of House Fossoway bend the knee to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lady [Leonette Fossoway](wiki:Leonette_Fossoway) accompanies her husband, Ser [Garlan Tyrell](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell), to court in King's Landing.(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) For his valor during the Battle of the Blackwater, Ser Garlan is granted the lands, titles, and incomes of [Brightwater Keep](wiki:Brightwater_Keep), making Leonette the Lady of Brightwater Keep.(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) She often accompanies Lady [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) as part of her retinue.(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1)
At the [royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King Joffrey and Lady Margaery, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) observes a Fossoway and his pregnant wife.(wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
