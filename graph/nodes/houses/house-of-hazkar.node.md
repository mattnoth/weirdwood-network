## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When several Meereenese ships join the blockade of Meereen, [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) presents [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) a list with the names of the Meereenese captains and which families they belong too. The House of Hazkar was among the names.(wiki:House_of_Hazkar.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
The Pyramid of Hazkar was attacked by [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal) and [Viserion](wiki:Viserion) after their escape and has become a smoking ruin that eventually collapsed in on itself, with many of the ancient line of Hazkar lying dead, buried under its now blackened stones. Rhaegal attempted to make his lair atop the [pyramid of Hazkar](wiki:Pyramid_of_Hazkar), he killed the slavers who were foolish enough to object.(wiki:House_of_Hazkar.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
