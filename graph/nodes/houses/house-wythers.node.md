## Identity

House Wythers is a noble house from the Reach (wiki:House_Wythers). Their sigil is a grey squirrel on white with a red border (wiki:House_Wythers).

## Allegiances

House Wythers is `SWORN_TO` House Tyrell as overlord (track_b: Overlord).

## Narrative Arc

In ACOK, Ser Ottyn Wythers, a Night's Watch commander, is part of the great ranging beyond the Wall (wiki:House_Wythers). In ASOS, Ottyn is killed when a horse kicks him in the face during the fight at the Fist of the First Men (wiki:House_Wythers). In AFFC, Ser Willam Wythers serves as captain of the guard for Queen Margaery Tyrell in King's Landing (wiki:House_Wythers).
