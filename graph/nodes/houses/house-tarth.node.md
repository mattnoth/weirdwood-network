## Identity

House Tarth of Evenfall Hall is a noble house from the Stormlands. They rule Tarth, a beautiful island northeast of Shipbreaker Bay that controls the Straits of Tarth between the island and the mainland. They are one of the main houses sworn to House Baratheon of Storm's End. Their blazon is quartered with yellow suns on rose and white crescent moons on azure. The Lords of Tarth call themselves the Evenstar. (wiki:House_Tarth)

## Heraldry & Sigil

## Origins

Ancestors of House Tarth were once kings, and the title Evenstar dates back to the dawn of days. Tarth came under the Storm Kings when Durran the Fair married the daughter of Edwyn Evenstar, King of Tarth. Tarth was the first region of the Stormlands conquered during the coming of the Andals, but peace was eventually achieved through intermarriage. The Tarths have Andal ancestry and ties with Houses Durrandon, Baratheon, and Targaryen. (wiki:House_Tarth.cite_ref-15, wiki:House_Tarth.cite_ref-16)

## Narrative Arc

Selwyn Tarth, the Evenstar, `RULES` House Tarth. (track_b: Head) His daughter and heir is Brienne of Tarth, `HEIR_TO` the house. (track_b: Heir) Selwyn's wife died not long after Brienne's birth, and his other children — son Galladon and daughters Arianne and Alysanne — all died young. (wiki:House_Tarth)

During the War of the Five Kings, the Tarths initially declared for Renly Baratheon. Brienne won the melee at Bitterbridge and was granted a place in Renly's Rainbow Guard. After Renly's death, Brienne entered Catelyn Stark's service and was later entrusted by Jaime Lannister to search for Sansa Stark, carrying the Valyrian steel sword Oathkeeper. In AFFC, Brienne was captured by the brotherhood without banners and sentenced by Lady Stoneheart. In ADWD, she recruits Jaime at Pennytree. (wiki:House_Tarth)

Ser Endrew Tarth served at the Shadow Tower in the Night's Watch and was recalled to Castle Black as master-at-arms. He was killed fighting the Weeper's wildlings at the Bridge of Skulls. (wiki:House_Tarth.cite_ref-Rasos-28)
