## Origins

Ser [Desmond Grell](wiki:Desmond_Grell) was among those who attended [the wedding](wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully) of [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) and [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Stark). He has been the master-at-arms at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) since at least [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion),(wiki:House_Grell.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) spending forty years living at Riverrun.(wiki:House_Grell.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser [Desmond Grell](wiki:Desmond_Grell) is in charge of training new bowmen. When Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) challenges the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) force at the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords), he leaves Desmond in charge of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:House_Grell.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Lothar Frey](wiki:Lothar_Frey) replaces Desmond as one of the nobles who launch the funeral boat of Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully).(wiki:House_Grell.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser Desmond joins Ser [Robin Ryger](wiki:Robin_Ryger) in setting off to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) after the [surrender of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun).(wiki:House_Grell.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
