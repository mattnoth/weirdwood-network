## Origins

According to legend, House Bulwer descends from [Bors the Breaker](wiki:Bors_the_Breaker), a son of [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand).(wiki:House_Bulwer.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
According to semi-canon source, Ser [Humphrey Bulwer](wiki:Humphrey_Bulwer) attended the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC).(wiki:House_Bulwer.cite_ref-Rthk.5B.5Bthe_hedge_knight_.28comics.29.7Cgraphic_novel.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Ser [Buford Bulwer](wiki:Buford_Bulwer) fought at the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field) in [196 AC](wiki:196_AC), where he slew forty men, or so he claimed. In [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), he participated in the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls) where he was defeated by Ser [Uthor Underleaf](wiki:Uthor_Underleaf) in five tilts.(wiki:House_Bulwer.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
In more recent years, Lord [Jon Bulwer](wiki:Jon_Bulwer), the father of the current [lady](wiki:Alysanne_Bulwer) of the [house](wiki:List_of_Houses), died of a summer fever.(wiki:House_Bulwer.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:House_Bulwer.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
