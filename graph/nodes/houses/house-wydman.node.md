## Identity

House Wydman is a noble house from the Vale of Arryn (wiki:House_Wydman).

## Origins

Founded by a tourney knight (track_b: Founder). Their motto is "Right Conquers Might" (wiki:House_Wydman).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Arryn (track_b: Overlord). `REGION_OF` Vale of Arryn (track_b: Region).

## Appearances & Description

Their sigil is five splintered lances (3-2), striped blue and white with blue pennons, on yellow, beneath a white chief bearing a red castle, a green viper, a black broken wheel, a purple unicorn, and a golden lion (wiki:House_Wydman). The complex heraldry reflects the tourney origins of the house — the chief displays the arms of houses defeated by the founder.
