## Origins

[Torgold "the Grim" Tollett](wiki:Torgold_Tollett) was one of the casualties of the [Battle of the Seven Stars](wiki:Battle_of_the_Seven_Stars) on the [Andal](wiki:Andal) side.(wiki:House_Tollett.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

When a [war of succession](wiki:Arryn_Succession_Conflict_(134_AC)) ensued in the Vale upon the death of Lady [Jeyne Arryn](wiki:Jeyne_Arryn), the Tolletts supported Ser [Arnold Arryn](wiki:Arnold_Arryn) as heir to [the Eyrie](wiki:The_Eyrie).(wiki:House_Tollett.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Eddison Tollett](wiki:Eddison_Tollett), known as *Dolorous Edd*, is paired with [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) during [the great ranging](wiki:The_great_ranging) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:House_Tollett.cite_ref-Racok13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Edd survives the [battle of the Fist of the First Men](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fist_of_the_First_Men) and the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep), and he returns safely to the [Wall](wiki:Wall). He receives two votes in one of the final castings of votes for the [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch).(wiki:House_Tollett.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Feast for Crows

The Tolletts are among the noble houses who join Lord [Yohn Royce](wiki:Yohn_Royce) in trying to depose Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish).(wiki:House_Tollett.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
