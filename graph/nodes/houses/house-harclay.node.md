## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Ronnel Harclay](wiki:Ronnel_Harclay) is a ranger of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) and is part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) that sets out to fight [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) and look for [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark). After the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist), Ronnel is among the remaining man who retreat to [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep). He commands the rangers after the death of [Thoren Smallwood](wiki:Thoren_Smallwood).(wiki:House_Harclay.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sends a raven to House Harclay in a plea for help to defend [Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) from [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings).(wiki:House_Harclay.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
