## Identity

House Terrick is a noble house from the riverlands (wiki:House_Terrick). Their arms are per saltire purple and gold, four hawks' heads countercharged (wiki:House_Terrick).

## Heraldry & Sigil

Per saltire purple and gold, four hawks' heads countercharged (Per saltire purpure and or, four hawks erased counterchanged) (wiki:House_Terrick)

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Tully as overlord during AGOT (track_b: Overlords). `SWORN_TO` House Baelish by ADWD (track_b: Overlords). `REGION_OF` Riverlands (track_b: Region).

## Narrative Arc

Ser Alyn Terrick participated in the battle at the Great Fork during the Faith Militant uprising. Visenya Targaryen used Vhagar to burn the Terrick seat along with those of Houses Blanetree, Deddings, Lychester, and Wayn (wiki:House_Terrick). No members appear in the main series.
