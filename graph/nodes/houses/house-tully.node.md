## Identity

House Tully of Riverrun is one of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms, serving as Lords Paramount of the Trident and ruling the riverlands from their seat at Riverrun (wiki:House_Tully). Their sigil is a silver trout leaping on a striped field of blue and mud red, and their words are "Family, Duty, Honor" (wiki:House_Tully).

## Origins

House Tully is an old noble house of First Men origin (wiki:House_Tully). Edmure Tully and his sons were allies of Tristifer IV Mudd, King of the Rivers and the Hills, during the coming of the Andals, but knelt to the Andal conqueror Armistead Vance after Tristifer's death. Ser Edmure's son, Axel Tully, received land at the confluence of the Red Fork and the Tumblestone, where he constructed Riverrun (wiki:House_Tully). Unlike many Great Houses, the Tullys never ruled as kings but held Riverrun for at least a thousand years as powerful vassals (wiki:House_Tully).

## Allegiances

House Tully `SWORN_TO` House Baratheon of King's Landing as overlords during AGOT (track_b: Overlords). During ACOK-ASOS, `SWORN_TO` House Stark as part of the Kingdom of the North and the Trident (track_b: Overlords). By ADWD, nominally under `OVERLORD_OF` House Baelish, who was granted the title Lord Paramount of the Trident (track_b: Overlords). The Tullys follow the Faith of the Seven and worship in a sept inside Riverrun (wiki:House_Tully).

## Appearances & Description

Sigil: A leaping trout, silver, on a field of blue and mud red (wiki:House_Tully). Words: "Family, Duty, Honor" (wiki:House_Tully). Members tend to have fair skin, blue eyes, and auburn hair (wiki:House_Tully). Tully funeral customs involve placing the deceased on a small boat with driftwood and kindling, pushed by seven men into the Red Fork, then set afire with a flaming arrow (wiki:House_Tully).

## Narrative Arc

During Robert's Rebellion, Lord Hoster Tully allied with the rebels, binding his house to Houses Stark and Arryn through the marriages of his daughters Catelyn and Lysa. In AGOT, the Tullys are drawn into the War of the Five Kings when Lannister forces raid the riverlands. Edmure Tully commands initial river lord defenses while Hoster is bedridden. Robb Stark relieves Riverrun after the Battle of the Whispering Wood and is crowned King in the North with Tully support (wiki:House_Tully). In ACOK, Edmure defends the Fords of the Trident but inadvertently disrupts Robb's strategy. In ASOS, Hoster dies and Edmure weds Roslin Frey at the Twins, where the Red Wedding devastates the Stark-Tully alliance. Edmure is taken captive. The Blackfish holds Riverrun until AFFC, when Jaime Lannister negotiates its surrender. By ADWD, Edmure remains a captive and Riverrun has been granted to Emmon Frey (wiki:House_Tully).
