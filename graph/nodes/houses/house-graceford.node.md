## Identity

House Graceford of Holyhall is a noble house from the Reach. Their arms display the Mother's face upon white pale wavy dividing a brown field. Their words are "Work Her Will". (wiki:House_Graceford)

## Heraldry & Sigil

The Mother's face upon white pale wavy dividing a brown field (Brunâtre, on a pale wavy argent the Mother’s head caboshed proper) (wiki:House_Graceford)

Words: "Work Her Will" (wiki:House_Graceford)

## Origins

The Gracefords trace their ancestry to Andal adventurers given lands and wives by the Three Sage Kings of House Gardener during the coming of the Andals to the Reach. (wiki:House_Graceford.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf)

Lady Alyce Graceford is the current head of the house. (wiki:House_Graceford.cite_ref-Raffc7) Historical member Lord George Graceford was a Caltrop supporter of the greens during the Dance of the Dragons and became Lord Confessor during the regency of Aegon III.
