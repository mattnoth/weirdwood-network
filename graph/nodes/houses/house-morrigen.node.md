## Origins

During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), [Dickon Morrigen](wiki:Dickon_Morrigen) and the [Bastard of Blackhaven](wiki:Bastard_of_Blackhaven) commanded the vanguard of King [Argilac Durrandon](wiki:Argilac_Durrandon) during the [Last Storm](wiki:Last_Storm), though both men were killed by Queen [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) and her [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [Meraxes](wiki:Meraxes).(wiki:House_Morrigen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Ser [Damon the Devout](wiki:Damon_Morrigen), the [grand captain](wiki:Grand_Captain) of the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons), led his order from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) during the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising). Opposing the reign of King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), he challenged him to a [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven), resulting in Maegor's narrowly won victory and Damon's death at the king's hands.(wiki:House_Morrigen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:House_Morrigen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
After the death of Maegor, Ser [Gyles Morrigen](wiki:Gyles_Morrigen), a young nephew to Damon the Devout, offered himself as a champion to the new king [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), eager to prove his house's loyalty to the new monarch. After slaying Ser [Harrold Langward](wiki:Harrold_Langward) of Maegor's [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) in a [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat), Gyles was appointed [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard) by the king.(wiki:House_Morrigen.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Ser [Grance Morrigen](wiki:Grance_Morrigen) participated in the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow).(wiki:House_Morrigen.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Shortly before [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Lord Morrigen had a territorial dispute with Lord [Armond Connington](wiki:Armond_Connington), who attempted to win Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) to his side.(wiki:House_Morrigen.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The Morrigens are among the storm lords who choose to fight for [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:House_Morrigen.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Ser [Guyard Morrigen](wiki:Guyard_Morrigen), known as Guyard the Green, is a member of King Renly's [Rainbow Guard](wiki:Rainbow_Guard).(wiki:House_Morrigen.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) The Morrigens turn to King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) after Renly's death.(wiki:House_Morrigen.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
The van of Stannis's army is placed under the command of Guyard, who then leads the foot during the attack on [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:House_Morrigen.cite_ref-Racok52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) However, Guyard is slain at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater) by Ser [Garlan Tyrell](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell), who is disguised as Renly.(wiki:House_Morrigen.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord [Lester Morrigen](wiki:Lester_Morrigen) remains loyal to Stannis after the Blackwater.(wiki:House_Morrigen.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:House_Morrigen.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

According to a semi-canon source, Lester has accompanied Stannis to the [north](wiki:North).(wiki:House_Morrigen.cite_ref-Rawoiafcrow.27s_nest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
Ser [Tristan Rivers](wiki:Tristan_Rivers) leads the [taking of Crow's Nest](wiki:Taking_of_Crow's_Nest) by the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company).(wiki:House_Morrigen.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)
