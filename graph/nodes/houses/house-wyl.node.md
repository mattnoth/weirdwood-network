## Narrative Arc

The Wyls have long fought against the lords of the Dornish Marches. They have tunneled beneath their castle to link caverns for defense (wiki:House_Wyl). Wylla of Wyl was a warrior-maid who fought beside Yoren Yronwood against King Durran the Young (wiki:House_Wyl). During the First Dornish War, the Wyl of Wyl ambushed and defeated Lord Orys Baratheon's forces in the Boneway (wiki:House_Wyl). The most infamous Wyl act was at the wedding of Ser Jon Cafferen and Alys Oakheart, where Lord Wyl castrated the groom, had the bride gang-raped and sold into slavery, and killed Lord Oakheart and most guests (wiki:House_Wyl). During Daeron I's conquest of Dorne, the Wyls imprisoned Prince Aemon Targaryen after King Daeron was killed under a peace banner (wiki:House_Wyl).
