## Identity

House Volmark of Volmark is a noble house from the Iron Islands. Their seat of Volmark is on the island of Harlaw and they are sworn to the house of the same name. They have large holdings on the island and a decorated history (wiki:House_Volmark.cite_ref-Raffc11). Their sigil depicts a black leviathan on a grey sea (wiki:House_Volmark.cite_ref-Raffc11).

## Origins

After King Harren Hoare died in the burning of Harrenhal, Lord Qhorin Volmark claimed the title of King of the Iron Islands, as his grandmother was the younger sister of King Harwyn Hardhand. Qhorin was slain by King Aegon I Targaryen during the invasion of the Iron Islands in 2 AC (wiki:House_Volmark.cite_ref-History). Lord Volmark was later put to the sword by westermen during Lady Johanna Lannister's invasion in 134 AC (wiki:House_Volmark.cite_ref-History).
