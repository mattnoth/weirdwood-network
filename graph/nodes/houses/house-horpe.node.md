## Origins

Ser [Harys Horpe](wiki:Harys_Horpe) was one of the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons) who were killed in the [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven) of King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen).(wiki:House_Horpe.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Although the course of their lord is unknown, Ser [Richard Horpe](wiki:Richard_Horpe) remains in Stannis's service after the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), and is among the knights known as the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men). Ser Richard and Ser [Justin Massey](wiki:Justin_Massey) had dragged Stannis away from the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater) to save his life.(wiki:House_Horpe.cite_ref-TWOW_5-0)
During the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), the wildling [Dormund](wiki:Dormund) is killed by a [knight](wiki:Knight) with moths on his shield.(wiki:House_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser Richard becomes one of Stannis's principal sworn swords, acting as his second-in-command during his northern campaign.(wiki:House_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

Richard is still with Stannis during their march towards [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:House_Horpe.cite_ref-TWOW_5-1)
