## Origins

Unlike most great houses of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) who claim direct descent from the [Grey King](wiki:Grey_King), the Goodbrothers claim descent from the Grey King's leal eldest brother.(wiki:House_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) According to a semi-canon source, [Kayce](wiki:Kayce) was founded by [Gundor Goodbrother](wiki:Gundor_Goodbrother).(wiki:House_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Gundor_8-0)
When the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot) was used to choose the [High King of the Iron Islands](wiki:High_King_of_the_Iron_Islands), only [House Greyiron](wiki:House_Greyiron) had more kings chosen than the Goodbrothers.(wiki:House_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_iron_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:House_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_greyjoys_of_pyke.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) [Urrathon IV Goodbrother](wiki:Urrathon_IV_Goodbrother) was a high king known as "Badbrother" during the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes).(wiki:House_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:House_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
After the death of [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), at [Faircastle](wiki:Faircastle), [Gunthor Goodbrother](wiki:Gunthor_Goodbrother) slew [Alester Wynch](wiki:Alester_Wynch) in a feud over [Lysa Farman](wiki:Lysa_Farman).(wiki:House_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) During the [struggle for power in the Iron Islands](wiki:Struggle_for_power_in_the_Iron_Islands), Ser [Leo Costayne](wiki:Leo_Costayne), the [lord admiral](wiki:Lord_admiral) of the [Reach](wiki:Reach), led an invasion of the isles on behalf of Lady [Johanna Lannister](wiki:Johanna_Lannister) in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC). Leo was slain by [Arthur Goodbrother](wiki:Arthur_Goodbrother) during the fighting, and Lord Goodbrother was put to the sword by [westermen](wiki:Westermen) by war's end.(wiki:House_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), sends his daughter [Asha](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) to [Great Wyk](wiki:Great_Wyk) to meet with Lord [Gorold Goodbrother](wiki:Gorold_Goodbrother).(wiki:House_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) When Balon's son [Theon](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) returns to [Pyke](wiki:Pyke_(island)) from the [mainland](wiki:Green_lands), he sees a great number of longships, including some bearing Gorold's banded black warhorn.(wiki:House_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) The main strength of Lord Goodbrother amounts to near forty longships at [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport).(wiki:House_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) When entering the [Great Hall](wiki:Great_Keep_of_Pyke) with his father's captains, Theon sees some Goodbrothers of Great Wyk.(wiki:House_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)(wiki:House_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3)
During the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Balon declares himself first [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands)(wiki:House_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) and then [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North).(wiki:House_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Gormond Goodbrother](wiki:Gormond_Goodbrother), with [the Sparr](wiki:The_Sparr) and his son [Steffarion](wiki:Steffarion_Sparr), brings to [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) the tidings of his brother Balon's death. Aeron meets with Gormond's father, Lord Goodbrother, at [Hammerhorn](wiki:Hammerhorn).(wiki:House_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
Some Goodbrothers from Great Wyk gather at the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk) to wish [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) well. Two of Gorold's sons get into a fight at the kingsmoot.(wiki:House_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Although Lord Gorold is initially a supporter of Victarion, after [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy)'s speech he is spotted by Aeron chanting Euron's name.(wiki:House_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), Gorold is spotted by Victarion talking with [Dunstan Drumm](wiki:Dunstan_Drumm), [Lord of Old Wyk](wiki:Lord_of_Old_Wyk), and [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw), [Lord of Harlaw](wiki:Lord_of_Harlaw), in the yard of [Lord Hewett's castle](wiki:Lord_Hewett's_castle).(wiki:House_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
