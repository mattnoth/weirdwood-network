## Identity

House Grimm of Greyshield is a noble house that rules the island Greyshield, one of the Shield Islands of the Reach, from their castle Grimston. Their arms display an iron escutcheon with silver studs on a grey-green field strewn with longships proper (semi-canon heraldry). (wiki:House_Grimm)

## Allegiances

House Grimm was `SWORN_TO` House Tyrell before the ironborn invasion. After the Taking of the Shields, they fall under `OVERLORD_OF` House Greyjoy. (track_b: Overlords)

## Narrative Arc

During the Dance of the Dragons, Lord Grimm declared for the blacks but surrendered to Lord Ormund Hightower and Prince Daeron Targaryen to avoid facing the dragon Tessarion. (wiki:House_Grimm.cite_ref-Rfab) In AFFC, the Shield Islands are taken by the ironborn. Lord Guthor Grimm's septon surrendered Grimston after Ser Harras Harlaw defeated seven men in single combat. Euron Greyjoy gave Greyshield to Harras. Lord Grimm is held captive. (wiki:House_Grimm.cite_ref-Raffc29)
