## Appearances & Description

The House of Black and White sits upon a rocky knoll made of dark grey stone. It has no windows and has a black tile roof. Its wooden doors are twelve feet high and carved. The left door is [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood), the right [ebony](wiki:Ebony). In the center of the doors is a carved [moon](wiki:Moon) face ebony on weirwood, weirwood on ebony. Its steep steps of grey stone lead down to the shadowed dock.(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Besides the front door, there are also secret tunnels and hidden passages which allow entry to the temple.(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Radwd64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) A winding stair leads to the garret, and a steep wooden ladder reaches the rooftop's door and a windy perch.(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Radwd64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
The temple proper has rows of long stone benches and a rough stone floor, and hard stone beds are located in shuttered alcoves at the walls.(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) In the center is a black pool of [poisoned](wiki:Poison) water(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) ten feet across and lit by dim red candles.(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Statues of gods stand around the room.(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) There are tall statues of thirty gods in all. Among them are [Bakkalon](wiki:Bakkalon),(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) the [Black Goat of Qohor](wiki:Black_Goat_of_Qohor),(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) the [Hooded Wayfarer](wiki:Hooded_Wayfarer),(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) the [horse god](wiki:Horse_god),(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) the [Lion of Night](wiki:Lion_of_Night),(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) the [Merling King](wiki:Merling_King_(god)),(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6) the [Moon-Pale Maiden](wiki:Moon-Pale_Maiden),(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7) the [Stranger](wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven)),(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8) and the [Weeping Woman](wiki:Weeping_Woman).(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-9) There are no services or songs of worship within the temple.(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-10)
The knoll upon which the temple sits holds many passageways cut from the rock,(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-11) with a maze of vaults and tunnels located beneath the temple proper.(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The vaults and storerooms contain [armament](wiki:Armament) and clothing; the possessions of people who die in the temple are separated by the servants.(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-12)(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) The sleeping cells of priests and acolytes are located on the first level beneath the main floor. The sleeping cells for the servants are on the second.(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-13)
The holy sanctum lays on the third level, which is only available to priests.(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-14) A heavy [iron](wiki:Iron) door leads to steps which descend below the canal. At the bottom another iron door leads to a chamber filled with the collection of hanging faces used as disguises by the [Faceless Men](wiki:Faceless_Men). One side passage is walled with bones and supported by columns of skulls, and another passage leads to steps which descend even further.(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Radwd64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

In [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), [Yorko Terys](wiki:Yorko_Terys) rows [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) to the House of Black and White. Its doors are closed when she arrives, but they open after she says "[valar morghulis](wiki:Valar_morghulis)" while presenting the worn iron coin previously given to her by [Jaqen H'ghar](wiki:Jaqen_H'ghar). Arya fills a cup with water from the pool and offers it to a weeping young man, and she notices that the man and other attendees are dying or already dead.(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)
The [kindly man](wiki:Kindly_man) allows Arya to stay at the temple as a serving girl. She is required to discard her old personality, including her belongings, to begin the training as a novice. She does this, except for [Needle](wiki:Needle), which she hides under the exterior steps. Arya helps the acolytes clean dead bodies, and the kindly man eventually sends her to live at [Brusco](wiki:Brusco)'s as "Cat of the Canals".(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-23) Arya returns to the temple to serve on nights when the [moon](wiki:Moon) is black, and the kindly man has her blinded after the death of [Dareon](wiki:Dareon).(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Now going by the name Beth, Arya continues to serve at the temple, which she has difficulty navigating while blind. She learns [poisons](wiki:Poison), potions, and languages, and she practices a lying game with the waif. Arya eventually has her sight returned.(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)
After [plague face](wiki:Plague_face) tasks Arya with killing an [old man](wiki:Old_man), the kindly man leads her to the holy sanctum and gives her the face of an ugly girl. Arya receives her true face again after killing the elderly man, and she is made an acolyte.(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Radwd64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) The kindly man gives her a new face and sends her to [Izembaro](wiki:Izembaro).(wiki:House_of_Black_and_White.cite_ref-Radwd64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)
