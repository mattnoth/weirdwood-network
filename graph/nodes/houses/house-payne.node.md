## Identity

House Payne is a noble house from the westerlands, one of the principal houses sworn to House Lannister. Their arms are purple and white chequy with gold coins in the checks. There is a story behind the coins, but the details are unknown. (wiki:House_Payne.cite_ref-Ragotappendix; wiki:House_Payne.cite_ref-Rasos59)

## Narrative Arc

Ser Ilyn Payne served as captain of guards for Tywin Lannister when Tywin was Hand to Aerys II. Ilyn had his tongue torn out by the Mad King for saying Tywin truly ruled the realm (wiki:House_Payne.cite_ref-Ragot15). Robert I named Ilyn the King's Justice at his wedding to Cersei (wiki:House_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc27). In AGOT, Ilyn executes Eddard Stark with Ice on the steps of the Great Sept of Baelor (wiki:House_Payne.cite_ref-Ragot65). During the Battle of the Blackwater, Cersei stations Ilyn nearby to behead her if Stannis wins (wiki:House_Payne.cite_ref-Racok60). In AFFC, one-handed Jaime chooses Ilyn as sparring partner because the mute headsman cannot reveal his weakness (wiki:House_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc27).

Podrick Payne, from a lesser branch, squired for Ser Cedric Payne (killed in the riverlands), then Ser Lorimer (hanged for stealing a ham), then Tyrion Lannister (wiki:House_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc14). Podrick saves Tyrion from Ser Mandon Moore during the Blackwater (wiki:House_Payne.cite_ref-Racok59). He follows Brienne of Tarth searching for Sansa Stark and is captured by the Brotherhood Without Banners, sentenced to hang by Lady Stoneheart (wiki:House_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc42).
