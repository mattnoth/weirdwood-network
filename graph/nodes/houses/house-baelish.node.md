## Identity

House Baelish is a noble house from the Vale of Arryn. Their seat is an unnamed, old flint tower on the smallest of the Fingers, commanding no more than a few stony acres. Their smallfolk consists of a village of a dozen families in huts of piled stone beside a peat bog (wiki:House_Baelish). The current lord is Petyr Baelish (track_b: Head).

## Heraldry & Sigil

A field of silver mockingbirds on green (Vert, semé of mockingbirds argent) Formerly: A grey stone head with fiery eyes, on a light green field (Vert, a stone head cendrée, orbed gules) (wiki:House_Baelish)

## Origins

Founded by a hedge knight of Braavosi descent (track_b: Founder). The sigil was formerly a grey stone head with fiery eyes on a light green field; the current Lord Petyr changed it to a field of silver mockingbirds on green (wiki:House_Baelish).

## Appearances & Description

Current sigil: silver mockingbirds on green. Former sigil: a grey stone head with fiery eyes on light green (wiki:House_Baelish). Despite holding the titles Lord of Harrenhal and Lord Paramount of the Trident, the house's actual ancestral holding is among the smallest and poorest in the Vale (wiki:House_Baelish).

## Narrative Arc

House Baelish's rise is entirely due to Petyr Baelish, who parlayed his position as Master of Coin into lordship of Harrenhal and the title Lord Paramount of the Trident. Petyr was fostered at Riverrun with the Tully sisters, which gave him connections far above his birth. He serves as a key political manipulator throughout the series, orchestrating Jon Arryn's death and the Stark-Lannister conflict. He became Lord Protector of the Vale after marrying and then murdering Lysa Arryn (wiki:House_Baelish) (agot-eddard-04) (agot-eddard-05) (agot-catelyn-04).
