## Origins

The Crakehalls are a house of [First Men](wiki:First_Men) origin who claim descent from [Crake the Boarkiller](wiki:Crake_the_Boarkiller) from the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes).(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
After defeating King [Hagon Hoare](wiki:Hagon_Hoare), Ser [Aubrey Crakehall](wiki:Aubrey_Crakehall) briefly ruled as [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands) before being drowned by the [Shrike](wiki:Shrike).(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) and his sister, Princess [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)), sheltered at [Crakehall](wiki:Crakehall) when the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising) began in [41 AC](wiki:41_AC).(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Ser [Clarent Crakehall](wiki:Clarent_Crakehall) was slain in the [Battle by the Lakeshore](wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore).(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
One of the outstanding supporters of [Daemon Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) mentioned by Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey) was [Redtusk](wiki:Redtusk), who could have been a Crakehall.(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Ser [Roland Crakehall](wiki:Roland_Crakehall_(Kingsguard)) was one of the three members of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) that escorted part of the [royal family](wiki:House_Targaryen) to [Ashford](wiki:Ashford). He and his sworn brothers took part in the [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven) on the side of Prince [Aerion Targaryen](wiki:Aerion_Targaryen).(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Roland was one of the three Kingsguard knights who led the loyalist forces to thwart the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion).(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
On their journey to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) to broker the proposed marriages of [Elia](wiki:Elia_Martell) and [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) with [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), the Martell siblings and their mother stopped at Crakehall.(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Along with [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey),(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Jaime squired for Lord [Sumner Crakehall](wiki:Sumner_Crakehall) against the [Kingswood Brotherhood](wiki:Kingswood_Brotherhood).(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Ser [Elys Westerling](wiki:Elys_Westerling) and Lord [Roland Crakehall](wiki:Roland_Crakehall_(lord)) were the first to enter the throne room of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) after Jaime slew King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen).(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Among the heraldry gathered for the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) is that of a knight of Crakehall.(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) questions his paternity over the sons of his third wife, [Amarei Crakehall](wiki:Amarei_Crakehall).(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sees the brindled boar of Crakehall among the banners of his father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), at the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) The messenger that brings Tywin the news of the defeat of Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s army in the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps) wears the brindled boar of Crakehall half-obscured by dried blood.(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)) quarters his shield in one quarter the brindled boar of the Crakehalls, as his grandmother was Amarei.(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
Ser [Burton Crakehall](wiki:Burton_Crakehall) boasts that he had slain Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) until the Lightning Lord reappears, killing all the men from his [column](wiki:Column).(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)
Lord [Roland Crakehall](wiki:Roland_Crakehall_(lord)) is falsely reported slain at the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross).(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) Ser [Lyle Crakehall](wiki:Lyle_Crakehall), called Strongboar, is captured in the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords).(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)
[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) spies the brindled boar of Crakehall among the banners in Tywin's army as it leaves [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) Lord Roland participates in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lyle is held captive at [Pinkmaiden Castle](wiki:Pinkmaiden_Castle).(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Jaime includes Strongboar among the living men stronger than him.(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)
Beric shows [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) a spot above his left ear where his temple was caved in, stating that it is where Burton hit him with his mace.(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)
Ser [Merlon Crakehall](wiki:Merlon_Crakehall) is one of the nobles that accompany Tyrion to welcome Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lyle reminds Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) of her late husband, King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), and she is angry at Strongboar when he celebrates King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon)'s wedding by saying that "Westeros has two queens now", referring to Cersei and Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)
The Crakehall boar is spotted among the gathered nobility that escorts the corpse of Lord Tywin Lannister back to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)
Lyle joins Jaime's expedition to end the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun). During their trip, they cross a place named [Sow's Horn](wiki:Sow's_Horn) where Ser [Roger Hogg](wiki:Roger_Hogg) defends his towerhouse. Since Roger is a big man, Strongboar questions him about his ancestors to see if he descended from the Crakehalls.(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0) After hearing of the [sack of Saltpans](wiki:Sack_of_Saltpans) at [Darry](wiki:Darry), Strongboar insists he will slay [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane). Lyle consoles Lady [Amerei Frey](wiki:Amerei_Frey), Amarei's granddaughter, and promises he will return to deal with Beric and [his outlaws](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) after Riverrun.(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)
After Jaime peacefully ends the siege at Riverrun, Strongboar is angry because he has not had the opportunity to fight. He rides back to Darry, as promised, to fight Sandor or Beric, accompanied by Beardless [Jon Bettley](wiki:Jon_Bettley). Jaime considers the possibility of expelling Cersei and naming as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) his uncle, Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister). If Kevan refuses, Jaime thinks of Ser [Forley Prester](wiki:Forley_Prester) or Roland Crakehall as alternatives.(wiki:House_Crakehall.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0)
