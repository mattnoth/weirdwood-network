## Identity

House Blackmont is a lordly house from Dorne, seated at Blackmont. Founded before 700 BC (wiki:House_Blackmont).

## Origins

The Blackmonts are an ancient house predating the Andal invasion. King Benedict Blackmont was defeated in Nymeria's War and was one of six kings sent to the Wall by Nymeria (wiki:House_Blackmont). The house supported House Yronwood against House Nymeros Martell during the war (wiki:House_Blackmont).
