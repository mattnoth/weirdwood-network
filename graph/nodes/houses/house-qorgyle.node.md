## Identity

House Qorgyle is a lordly house from Dorne, seated at Sandstone (wiki:House_Qorgyle).

## Origins

The Qorgyles were adventuring Andals who established their domains in the unexplored deserts of Dorne, settling in the deep dunes and sands. They fortified the only well for fifty leagues around, raising Sandstone as their castle (wiki:House_Qorgyle).

## Appearances & Description

Their blazon is three black scorpions on red (wiki:House_Qorgyle).

## Narrative Arc

By the time of Nymeria's War, the Qorgyles were bannermen of House Yronwood. They supported King Yorick V Yronwood against Mors Martell and Princess Nymeria; following the war, the Qorgyles became vassals of House Martell (wiki:House_Qorgyle). During the First Dornish War, House Qorgyle never submitted to House Targaryen, and Qorgyle spearmen cut down Lord Harlan Tyrell's foragers. In 9 AC, Sandstone was burned by Balerion and Vhagar (wiki:House_Qorgyle). The Qorgyles are also known for producing Lord Commanders of the Night's Watch (wiki:House_Qorgyle).
