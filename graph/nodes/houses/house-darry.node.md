## Origins

### First Men and Andals[ ]

House Darry traces their rule to the days when the [Andals](wiki:Andals) cast down the [First Men](wiki:First_Men).(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) During the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals), three sons of Lord Darry held back the Andals of [Vorian Vypren](wiki:Vorian_Vypren) at the [Widow's Ford](wiki:Widow's_Ford) before being slain.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Lord Darry died in the fighting between the [Teagues](wiki:House_Teague) and the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Arlan III Durrandon](wiki:Arlan_III_Durrandon).(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### Targaryen Era[ ]

After King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) exterminated [House Harroway](wiki:House_Harroway) in [44 AC](wiki:44_AC), he divided up the Harroway holdings among his remaining supporters, granting [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) to Ser [Walton Towers](wiki:Walton_Towers), [Lord Harroway's Town](wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town) to Lord [Alton Butterwell](wiki:Alton_Butterwell), and the rest of the Harroway holdings to Lord [Darnold Darry](wiki:Darnold_Darry).(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Lord Darry praised King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) during the [Golden Wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding).(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
House Darry supported [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). The Darrys helped to capture [Stone Hedge](wiki:Taking_of_Stone_Hedge),(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) but Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) later defeated [Lord Darry](wiki:Lord_Darry_(Dance)), Lord [Roote](wiki:House_Roote), and Ser [Oswald Wode](wiki:Oswald_Wode) along the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye).(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) had his dragon, [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar), burn Castle Darry and the surrounding fields. Lady Darry and her younger children survived below the keep, but Lord Darry and his heir were killed on the battlements.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Lord [Derrick Darry](wiki:Derrick_Darry) participated in the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton),(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) and Lord [Roland Darry](wiki:Roland_Darry) was slain by Lord [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon) in the [Battle of the Kingsroad](wiki:Battle_of_the_Kingsroad).(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
During the [regency of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III), Ser [Damon Darry](wiki:Damon_Darry) and Ser [Regis Groves](wiki:Regis_Groves) marched to subdue [Alys Rivers](wiki:Alys_Rivers)'s rabble at Harrenhal. Damon was forced to flee back to Darry after Regis was slain.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) [Rosamund Darry](wiki:Rosamund_Darry) attended the [Maiden's Day Ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Darrys participated in Ser [Robert Rowan](wiki:Robert_Rowan)'s host which supported Ser [Joffrey Arryn](wiki:Joffrey_Arryn) in the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn).(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
The Darrys were Targaryen loyalists, supporting King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen) over [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) in the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion). Lord Darry destroyed the black dragon sign of the [Clanking Dragon](wiki:Clanking_Dragon), as he saw in it the sigil of [House Blackfyre](wiki:House_Blackfyre).(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
Ser [Desmond Darry](wiki:Desmond_Darry) participated in the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC),(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rthk_.5B.5Bthe_hedge_knight_.28comics.29.7Cgraphic_novel.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) his arms identified by Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) among the gathered attendees heraldry at the [tourney](wiki:Tourney).(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
The [Demon of Darry](wiki:Demon_of_Darry) became [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard).(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) The song of Lord [Deremond](wiki:Deremond) at the [Bloody Meadow](wiki:Bloody_Meadow) may be about a Lord of Darry.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
[Norbert Vance](wiki:Norbert_Vance) squired together with [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) for the Lord Darry of their time.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
During the reign of King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), Ser [Willem Darry](wiki:Willem_Darry) was master-at-arms of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep). When [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone), decided that he wanted to be a warrior, he asked Willem to provide him sword and armor.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Ser [Jonothor Darry](wiki:Jonothor_Darry) was a member of Aerys II's [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1)

### Robert's Rebellion[ ]

During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), the Darrys defended King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) against [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End), and their liege, [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), [Lord Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident).(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) After the [Battle of the Bells](wiki:Battle_of_the_Bells), Ser [Jonothor Darry](wiki:Jonothor_Darry) and his sworn brother of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), were sent to [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept) to rally remnants of the loyalist army of [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington), [Lord of Griffin's Roost](wiki:Lord_of_Griffin's_Roost).(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
Jonothor was one of three Kingsguard knights who accompanied Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) and the royal army to confront the rebels at the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident), where he was slain during the battle.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_fall_of_the_dragons:_robert.27s_rebellion.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) Ser [Raymun Darry](wiki:Raymun_Darry) lost his three older brothers at the [Trident](wiki:Trident) as well.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) Ser [Willem Darry](wiki:Willem_Darry) and four loyal men smuggled Aerys's surviving children, Prince [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) and Princess [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) before the [assault on Dragonstone](wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone) by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon). Willem would later die of a wasting sickness in a [house with a red door](wiki:House_with_the_red_door) in Braavos while caring for the Targaryen children.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
The defeat of the Targaryens cost the Darrys half of their lands, most of their wealth, and almost all their power.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

House Darry is among the houses that the exiled [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) believes will rise for him if he lands in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-1)
During the hunt for [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) and [Mycah](wiki:Mycah), King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) and his party stay at [Castle Darry](wiki:Darry).(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1)
Ser [Raymun Darry](wiki:Raymun_Darry) is summoned with all his strength to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) by Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully). Meanwhile, Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) and [his men](wiki:Mountain's_men) raid the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), [Lord Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident) sends Raymun with Ser [Marq Piper](wiki:Marq_Piper) and Ser [Karyl Vance](wiki:Karyl_Vance) to report the incident to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)
Raymun is part of the expedition commanded by [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion), [Lord of Blackhaven](wiki:Lord_of_Blackhaven), to kill Gregor. The party is ambushed by Gregor and [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister), however, and Raymun is killed in the [battle at the Mummer's Ford](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford).(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) According to a semi-canon source, the current [Lord Darry](wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun)), Raymun's father, dies in the battle as well.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-RPG_29-0)
After [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s victory at the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), Marq and Karyl arrive at Riverrun with Raymun's son, the young [Lyman Darry](wiki:Lyman_Darry), the new Lord Darry. Lyman attends the council at Riverrun where Robb is proclaimed [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North). Lyman assures that he will never call a Lannister his king.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)) quarters his shield to depict in one quarter the Darry plowman of his mother's house.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
When Robb, [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident), gives leave to the river lords to return to their castles, Lyman returns to Darry. Just after he arrives, Gregor returns and [kills Lyman and all his garrison](wiki:Sack_of_Darry). Lyman was only eight, and the last trueborn heir of Darry through the male line.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)
[Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) sees Darry men with the other river lords near Riverrun prior to the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords).(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)
The Lannister garrison eventually surrenders to Ser [Helman Tallhart](wiki:Helman_Tallhart) in the [siege of Darry](wiki:Siege_of_Darry). [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), [Lord of the Dreadfort](wiki:Lord_of_the_Dreadfort), sends word to Helman from [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) that Robb has ordered the surrendered garrison to be executed and for Helman to put the castle to the torch and leave.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)
After the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) awards Ser [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister) the lands, castle and rights of House Darry, creating [House Lannister of Darry](wiki:House_Lannister_of_Darry). This lordship is given in favor of a living bastard Darry cousin.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-1) The move is arranged by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) to thank his loyal brother, Ser [Kevan](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), who is Lancel's father.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Darry men are left lordless and lawless during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1)
[Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams) accuses [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) of the deaths of Raymun and Lyman Darry, although both were killed by his brother, Gregor.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)
[Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey) thinks that his marriage with [Mariya Darry](wiki:Mariya_Darry), the daughter of Lord Darry, was unfortunate, as the Darrys lost all their prominence after [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Because Castle Darry was occupied by [broken men](wiki:Broken_men) after the northmen's departure, Ser Kevan Lannister sends Ser [Harwyn Plumm](wiki:Harwyn_Plumm), called the Hardstone, to expel them.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0)
Kevan marries his son Lancel to [Amerei Frey](wiki:Amerei_Frey), the eldest daughter of the female heir to Darry, to strengthen his claim over the Darry lands.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-1) She brings to Darry a score of [Freys](wiki:House_Frey), including her Darry-born mother, Mariya, one sister, and some uncles and cousins. Probably after Kevan's recommendation, Lancel quarters his Lannister lion with the Darry plowman in the arms of House Lannister of Darry. Kevan helps Lancel to rebuild Darry, but departs soon due to disagreements with his son and his new religious zeal.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
Jaime and his company spend a night at Castle Darry on their way to the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun). Jaime recalls that as King Robert's party journeyed north to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), Jaime and his brother [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) discovered hidden tapestries of the Targaryen kings at Castle Darry, uncovering that the Darrys were still Targaryen loyalists.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) [Genna Lannister](wiki:Genna_Lannister) complains that Darry, though smaller, is a safer place than Riverrun and would have preferred it for her spouse [Emmon Frey](wiki:Emmon_Frey), since the male line of the Darrys is already extinguished.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)
While discussing the graves of dead soldiers who have washed up on the [Quiet Isle](wiki:Quiet_Isle), the [Elder Brother](wiki:Elder_Brother_(Quiet_Isle)) says that Darry and Frey men are buried side by side.(wiki:House_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0)
