## Identity

House Corbray of Heart's Home is a noble house from Heart's Home in the Vale of Arryn. It is an old but poor house (wiki:House_Corbray). The current head is Lord Lyonel Corbray, with Ser Lyn Corbray as heir (track_b: Head, Heir).

## Heraldry & Sigil

Three black ravens in flight holding three red hearts, on white (Argent, three ravens volant sable, each clutching in their claws a heart gules) (wiki:House_Corbray)

## Origins

Founded by Ser Corwyn Corbray during the Coming of the Andals (track_b: Founder) (wiki:House_Corbray). The Corbrays were historically Kings of the Fingers and Lords of the Five Fingers before bending the knee to the Arryns (track_b: Titles).

## Appearances & Description

Their sigil is three black ravens in flight holding three red hearts on white (wiki:House_Corbray). Their ancestral Valyrian steel sword is Lady Forlorn (track_b: Ancestral weapon).

## Narrative Arc

House Corbray appears among the Vale lords present during Tyrion's imprisonment and trial at the Eyrie. Ser Lyn Corbray is notable for wielding the Valyrian steel sword Lady Forlorn and for his volatile temperament. He later appears in the Vale political intrigues around Littlefinger's regency, secretly in Petyr Baelish's pay (wiki:House_Corbray) (agot-tyrion-05) (agot-catelyn-06) (agot-catelyn-07).
