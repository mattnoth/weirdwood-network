## Identity

House Nayland of Hag's Mire is a noble house from the riverlands (wiki:House_Nayland). Neither their arms nor words have appeared in the books. They may be a house of landed knights sworn to House Frey, since Hag's Mire appears to be within Frey territory (wiki:House_Nayland).

## Narrative Arc

Ser Lucas Nayland attended the Whitewalls tourney in the D&E era (wiki:House_Nayland). In AFFC, Brienne of Tarth recalls Ser Raymond Nayland as one of her suitors who participated in a bet to bed her; she defeated him in the melee at Bitterbridge as revenge (wiki:House_Nayland).
