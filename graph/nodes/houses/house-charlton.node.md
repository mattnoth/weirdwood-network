## Origins

House Charlton fought other riverlords for control of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) in the century after the fall of [House Justman](wiki:House_Justman).(wiki:House_Charlton.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Lord [Theo Charlton](wiki:Theo_Charlton) supported [Harwyn Hoare](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), against the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Arrec Durrandon](wiki:Arrec_Durrandon) in the [battle at Fairmarket](wiki:Battle_at_Fairmarket).(wiki:House_Charlton.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Lord Charlton supported Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) against King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) in the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye).(wiki:House_Charlton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord [Jon Charlton](wiki:Jon_Charlton) fought for the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) at the [Battle by the Lakeshore](wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore), where he died.(wiki:House_Charlton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Ser [Clarence Charlton](wiki:Clarence_Charlton) took part in the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls). He was soundly defeated by Ser [Tommard Heddle](wiki:Tommard_Heddle).(wiki:House_Charlton.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
At some point the Charltons became [vassals](wiki:Bannerman) of [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey),(wiki:House_Charlton.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) who began construction of the [Twins](wiki:Twins) six hundred years ago.(wiki:House_Charlton.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
