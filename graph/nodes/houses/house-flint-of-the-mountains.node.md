## Origins

The clansmen call themselves the First Flints as they say that the Flints of [Widow's Watch](wiki:House_Flint_of_Widow's_Watch) to the southeast and [Flint's Finger](wiki:House_Flint_of_Flint's_Finger) to the southwest were founded by younger sons of the family who left the [northern mountains](wiki:Northern_mountains) to find food, land, and wives.(wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) It is possible that the Flints of the mountains are the same as [House Flint of Breakstone Hill](wiki:House_Flint_of_Breakstone_Hill), who ruled as [First Men](wiki:First_Men) kings after the [Long Night](wiki:Long_Night) until being reduced to vassalage by the [Kings of Winter](wiki:Kings_of_Winter), [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_mountain_clans.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
It is suggested that the [Night's King](wiki:Night's_King) was a Flint, among the many possibilities.(wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Lord Commander [Rodrik Flint](wiki:Rodrik_Flint) is regarded as one of the worst commanders of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), for trying to make himself [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall).(wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) One of the dark legends surrounding the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort) is the rape and murder of brave young [Danny Flint](wiki:Danny_Flint).(wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
A branch of House Flint held the [Wolf's Den](wiki:Wolf's_Den) for a century after the extinction of the [Greystarks](wiki:House_Greystark).(wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
The Flints of the hills aided Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark) in hunting down the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) raiders of [Sylas the Grim](wiki:Sylas_the_Grim).(wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s maternal grandmother, [Arya Flint](wiki:Arya_Flint), was a Flint from the mountains. Old [Nan](wiki:Nan) used to say to [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) that it was her blood that made him climb so foolishly.(wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
The Flints of the mountains have been raided by [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) led by the [Weeper](wiki:Weeper).(wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The Flints are reported to have ties to [House Hornwood](wiki:House_Hornwood) through the female line, though [A Clash of Kings](wiki:A_Clash_of_Kings) does not specify which branch.(wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) recalls there are some Flints living in the [high places](wiki:Northern_mountains) during his trip to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) with [Hodor](wiki:Hodor), [Meera Reed](wiki:Meera_Reed), and [Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed).(wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The Flints join [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) in his campaign to take the north away from the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) and [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton). They hope to save "[The Ned](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s" girl, "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)", from the Boltons.(wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Prior to the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte), a Flint is part of a small group of northmen who try to infiltrate [the castle](wiki:Deepwood_Motte). They are detected by [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), however, and the Flint is killed by [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy).(wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
[Torghen Flint](wiki:Torghen_Flint) visits [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) with five fighting men, as well as a fourteen-year-old wet nurse who attends [Monster](wiki:Monster).(wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) The Flint and [the Norrey](wiki:Brandon_Norrey) are wary of Lord Commander [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow)'s plan to settle [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) in the [Gift](wiki:Gift).(wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
