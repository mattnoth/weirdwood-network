## Identity

House Wayn is a noble house from the riverlands (wiki:House_Wayn). Their arms are four wheels countercharged on quartered field blue and white (wiki:House_Wayn). It is unknown whether the Wayns are related to House Waynwood (wiki:House_Wayn).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Tully as overlord during AGOT (track_b: Overlords). `SWORN_TO` House Baelish by ADWD (track_b: Overlords). `REGION_OF` Riverlands (track_b: Region).

## Narrative Arc

Lord Tristifer Wayn participated in the battle at the Great Fork during the Faith Militant uprising. Visenya Targaryen used Vhagar to burn the Wayn seat in a single night along with those of Houses Blanetree, Deddings, Lychester, and Terrick (wiki:House_Wayn). In the main series, Utherydes Wayn serves as steward of Riverrun, of an age with Lord Hoster Tully (wiki:House_Wayn).
