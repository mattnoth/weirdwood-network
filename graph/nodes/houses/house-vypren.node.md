The Andal warlord Vorian Vypren and his knights were held at bay at the Widow's Ford for a day and night by three sons of Lord Darry before finally slaying the Darrys (wiki:House_Vypren).

In ASOS, Lord Lucias Vypren and his son Ser Damon attend the wedding of Edmure Tully and Roslin Frey at the Twins, which becomes the Red Wedding (wiki:House_Vypren).
