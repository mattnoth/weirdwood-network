## Identity

House Orkwood of Orkmont is a noble house from the Iron Islands, with their seat on the island of Orkmont. Their sigil depicts dark green pines strewn closely together on yellow (wiki:House_Orkwood.cite_ref-Raffc11).

## Heraldry & Sigil

Dark green pines strewn closely together on yellow (Or semé of pines vert) (wiki:House_Orkwood)

## Origins

Like most great houses of the Iron Islands (excepting House Goodbrother), the Orkwoods claim descent from the Grey King (wiki:House_Orkwood.cite_ref-History). They were part of the alliance of ironborn lords and Andals that brought down Rognar II Greyiron (wiki:House_Orkwood.cite_ref-History).

## Narrative Arc

The Orkwoods support Euron Greyjoy with twenty longships at the kingsmoot on Old Wyk. Lord Alyn Orkwood, called "Orkwood of Orkmont," supports Euron instead of Victarion during the kingsmoot (wiki:House_Orkwood.cite_ref-Raffc18). Some Orkwoods gather to wish Victarion well on the shores of Nagga's Cradle (wiki:House_Orkwood.cite_ref-Raffc19).
