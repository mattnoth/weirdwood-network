## Identity

House Ryger of Willow Wood is a noble house from the riverlands, one of the main houses sworn to House Tully (wiki:House_Ryger). Their arms are a weeping willow, green on white (wiki:House_Ryger).

## Narrative Arc

During Robert's Rebellion, the Rygers defied their liege Hoster Tully and stayed loyal to King Aerys II Targaryen (wiki:House_Ryger). In ACOK, Ser Robin Ryger serves as captain of the guard at Riverrun (wiki:House_Ryger). In ASOS, Tristan Ryger is a companion of Edmure Tully (wiki:House_Ryger). In AFFC, despite renewing fealty, Ryger banners are absent from the Riverrun besiegers. After the surrender, Robin Ryger joins Desmond Grell in taking the black (wiki:House_Ryger).
