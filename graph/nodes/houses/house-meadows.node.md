## Identity

House Meadows of Grassy Vale is a noble house from the Reach. Their castle is Grassfield Keep in or near the town of Grassy Vale. Their arms display a border of flowers of many colors and varieties on green (semi-canon heraldry). (wiki:House_Meadows)

## Allegiances

House Meadows was `SWORN_TO` House Tyrell. Lord Elwood Meadows subsequently declared for Stannis Baratheon, making the house sworn to `OVERLORD_OF` House Baratheon of Dragonstone by ADWD. (track_b: Overlords)

## Narrative Arc

In ACOK, the young Lord Elwood Meadows serves as Ser Cortnay Penrose's second-in-command at Storm's End. After Cortnay's death, Elwood names himself castellan and surrenders the castle to Stannis Baratheon. (wiki:House_Meadows.cite_ref-Racok42) In ASOS, Stannis tasks Ser Gilbert Farring as castellan with Lord Meadows as second. (wiki:House_Meadows.cite_ref-Rasos) By ADWD, Lord Elwood remains loyal to Stannis as seneschal of Storm's End. (wiki:House_Meadows.cite_ref-Radwd) Historically, a Lord Meadows was slain by Wat the Hewer at the Battle at Stonebridge in 42 AC while commanding forces for King Maegor I. (wiki:House_Meadows.cite_ref-Rfab)
