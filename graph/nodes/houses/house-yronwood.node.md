## Identity

House Yronwood is a lordly house from Dorne, the most powerful bannermen of House Martell, seated at Yronwood in the foothills of the Red Mountains. Founded during the Age of Heroes (wiki:House_Yronwood).

## Heraldry & Sigil

A black portcullis grill over sand (Carnation, a portcullis sable) (wiki:House_Yronwood)

Words: "We Guard the Way" (wiki:House_Yronwood)

## Origins

An ancient family of First Men origin, the Yronwoods built Yronwood and controlled the Stone Way, one of the two great passes into Dorne. Their domain eventually reached from Wyl to the headwaters of the Greenblood, and at times even the Sandship of House Martell (wiki:House_Yronwood). When the Rhoynar came with Nymeria, the Kings of Yronwood controlled half of Dorne. King Yorick V Yronwood fought Nymeria's War for nine years before being sent to the Wall (wiki:House_Yronwood).

## Narrative Arc

Lord Anders Yronwood musters and maintains an army in the Boneway during the War of the Five Kings on Doran Martell's instructions (wiki:House_Yronwood). Princess Arianne Martell fears the Yronwoods will declare for her younger brother Quentyn, not knowing Doran has different plans for him (wiki:House_Yronwood). Doran has Anders delay Ser Balon Swann at Yronwood by feasting him for a fortnight (wiki:House_Yronwood). Ser Cletus Yronwood (Anders's heir) and his cousin Ser Archibald Yronwood accompany Prince Quentyn to Essos to court Daenerys Targaryen. Cletus is killed by corsairs near the Disputed Lands. Archibald and Quentyn join the Windblown and reach Meereen, where Quentyn is mortally wounded attempting to tame a dragon (wiki:House_Yronwood).
