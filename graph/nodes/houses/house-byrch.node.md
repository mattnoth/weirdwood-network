## Identity

House Byrch is a noble house from the crownlands. Their arms are quarterly clockwise, gyronny white and black, a silver axe on green (wiki:House_Byrch).

## Heraldry & Sigil

Quarterly clockwise, gyronny white and black, a silver axe on green (Quarterly, first and third gyronny white and sable, second and fourth vert, an axe silver) (wiki:House_Byrch)

## Allegiances

## Narrative Arc

Historically, Lord Byrch came to King's Landing in 48 AC at the command of King Maegor I. Ser Balon Byrch was a captain of the City Watch of King's Landing who became its commander after Ser Luthor Largent's death (wiki:House_Byrch). In AFFC, Ser Balman Byrch is married to Falyse Stokeworth and attends Tywin Lannister's funeral. Bronn kills Balman in a duel. Brienne sees corpses with axes badges — presumably Byrch men-at-arms who became broken men during the War of the Five Kings (wiki:Raffc7, wiki:Raffc32, wiki:Raffc37).
