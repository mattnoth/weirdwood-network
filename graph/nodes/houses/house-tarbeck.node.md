## Identity

House Tarbeck was a noble house from the westerlands, formerly seated at Tarbeck Hall. They held the title Lord of Tarbeck Hall. The house is now extinct, destroyed alongside House Reyne during the Reyne-Tarbeck rebellion by Tywin Lannister. (wiki:House_Tarbeck)

## Narrative Arc

House Tarbeck, along with House Reyne, rose in defiance of Tytos Lannister's weak rule. Lady Ellyn Tarbeck (nee Reyne) was a driving force behind the rebellion. Tywin crushed both houses utterly. Ser Amory Lorch reportedly threw the last Lord Tarbeck, a three-year-old boy, down a well. The destruction of houses Reyne and Tarbeck is commemorated in "The Rains of Castamere."
