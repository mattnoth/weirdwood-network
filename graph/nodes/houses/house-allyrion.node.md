## Identity

House Allyrion of Godsgrace is a major Dornish noble house seated at Godsgrace, near the confluence of the Scourge and the Vaith into the Greenblood (wiki:House_Allyrion).

## Origins

House Allyrion was founded by Andal adventurers during the coming of the Andals to Dorne, along with Houses Jordayne, Martell, Qorgyle, Santagar, Uller, and Vaith (wiki:House_Allyrion). House Martell was once a vassal to the Allyrions of Godsgrace (wiki:House_Allyrion).

## Narrative Arc

During the First Dornish War, the Allyrions fled Godsgrace before the arrival of Queen Rhaenys Targaryen and Meraxes (wiki:House_Allyrion). Ser Ryon Allyrion and his bastard son Ser Daemon Sand accompany Prince Oberyn Martell to King's Landing in ASOS; Daemon helps Oberyn don his armor before the duel against Ser Gregor Clegane (wiki:House_Allyrion). Daemon is later arrested for demanding the release of the Sand Snakes (wiki:House_Allyrion). The Lord of Godsgrace attends the feast when Ser Balon Swann arrives in Sunspear to deliver the Mountain's skull (wiki:House_Allyrion).
