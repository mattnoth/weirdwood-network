House Slynt is a noble house from the crownlands, founded by Janos Slynt. They were briefly granted Harrenhal as their seat (track_b: Seat), though this was nominal and short-lived (wiki:House_Slynt).

## Narrative Arc

Janos Slynt rose from butcher's son to Commander of the City Watch. He was instrumental in Eddard Stark's arrest by betraying him in the throne room. As reward, Joffrey named him Lord of Harrenhal. Tyrion Lannister stripped him of Harrenhal and sent him to the Night's Watch. At the Wall, Slynt allied with Ser Alliser Thorne against Jon Snow. When Jon became Lord Commander, he ordered Janos to take command of Greyguard; Janos refused and Jon executed him by beheading (wiki:House_Slynt). After Janos's death, his son Morros became head of house. The family holds the title Lord of Harrenhal (formerly) (track_b: Title, qualifier: formerly).
