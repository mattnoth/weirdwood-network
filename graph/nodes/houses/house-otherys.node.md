## Origins

An Otherys was elected to the office of [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos). His son and a [Summer Islander](wiki:Summer_Islander) princess had a child, [Bellegere Otherys](wiki:Bellegere_Otherys),(wiki:House_Otherys.cite_ref-Mercy_2-0) who became a [pirate](wiki:Pirate) queen.(wiki:House_Otherys.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) In [161 AC](wiki:161_AC), Bellegere met Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen) when he was sent on a diplomatic mission to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) by his cousin, King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen). The two started an affair that lasted a decade, and produced three children: [Bellenora](wiki:Bellenora_Otherys), [Narha](wiki:Narha_Otherys), and [Balerion Otherys](wiki:Balerion_Otherys).(wiki:House_Otherys.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:House_Otherys.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Bellenora became a [courtesan](wiki:Courtesan), and used her mother's sobriquet of "The [Black Pearl of Braavos](wiki:Black_Pearl_of_Braavos)" for herself. Bellenora's descendants became courtesans as well, each eventually bearing the title "Black Pearl".(wiki:House_Otherys.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)(wiki:House_Otherys.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_braavos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Bellegere Otherys](wiki:Bellegere_Otherys_(courtesan)), the current Black Pearl of Braavos, purchases three cockles from [Cat of the Canals](wiki:Arya_Stark), paying ten times what the cockles are worth. She asks for hot sauce, but Cat does not have any. [Meralyn](wiki:Meralyn) tells Cat that Bellegere is "descended from [the dragons](wiki:House_Targaryen)", and some of the history of the Black Pearl courtesans from the Otherys family. When Cat returns to the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White) as a novice [Faceless Man](wiki:Faceless_Man), she relates this information to the [Kindly Man](wiki:Kindly_Man), who confirms it and provides some more details.(wiki:House_Otherys.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

### The Winds of Winter
