## Identity

House Willum is a noble house from the Reach (wiki:House_Willum). They blazon their shields with three silver longswords crossed on black beneath a dragonbone skeleton on a white chief (wiki:House_Willum).

## Allegiances

House Willum is `SWORN_TO` House Tyrell as overlord (track_b: Overlord).

## Narrative Arc

In ACOK, House Willum declares for Renly in the War of the Five Kings. Josua and Elyas Willum, sons of Lord Willum, attend the feast at Bitterbridge. After Renly's death the house joins Stannis. Lord Willum, Josua, and Elyas are captured at the Battle of the Blackwater and bend the knee to Joffrey (wiki:House_Willum). Members may have taken the black in the past, as shields displaying longswords adorned the Shieldhall walls at Castle Black (wiki:House_Willum).

## Notes

House Willum is a tribute by GRRM to Tad Williams's Memory, Sorrow, and Thorn series — the feuding brothers Josua and Elias, the magical swords, and The Dragonbone Chair (wiki:House_Willum).
