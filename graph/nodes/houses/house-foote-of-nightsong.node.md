## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The house is founded in [299 AC](wiki:299_AC) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) after Ser [Philip Foote](wiki:Philip_Foote) kills the last Lord of Nightsong, Lord [Bryce Caron](wiki:Bryce_Caron), in single combat during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). Philip is awarded the lands, rights and incomes of [House Caron](wiki:House_Caron) as a reward by the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:House_Foote_of_Nightsong.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Philip is among the gathered nobility that welcomes the [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) party headed by Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Philip is called as a witness for the prosecution in the trial of [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) for the death of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). He tells of how he saw Tyrion fill the [wedding chalice](wiki:Wedding_chalice) during the [royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding).(wiki:House_Foote_of_Nightsong.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
