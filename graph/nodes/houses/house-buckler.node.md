## Origins

During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Lords Buckler, [Errol](wiki:House_Errol), and [Fell](wiki:House_Fell) opposed [Orys Baratheon](wiki:Orys_Baratheon) during his march from the [Aegonfort](wiki:Aegonfort) toward [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), but the storm lords were forced to retreat when [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) had [Meraxes](wiki:Meraxes) set afire parts of what is now the southern [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood).(wiki:House_Buckler.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
At the beginning of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), [Lord Buckler](wiki:Lord_Buckler) was executed in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for refusing to renounce his oaths to [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen).(wiki:House_Buckler.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [Lady Buckler](wiki:Lady_Buckler) and her two daughters drowned when their ship sank in the [Blackwater](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) prior to the [Maiden's Day Ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball).(wiki:House_Buckler.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) once defeated the Bastard of Bronzegate, [Cedrik Storm](wiki:Cedrik_Storm).(wiki:House_Buckler.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

During the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Lord [Ralph Buckler](wiki:Ralph_Buckler) most likely declares for [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) before siding with his brother [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) and deserting him in turn for [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

During his journey to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) hears from Lord Buckler's steward that [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) has introduced a tax on [whoring](wiki:Prostitution).(wiki:House_Buckler.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Ralph is a guest at King Joffrey's [royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding), during which he proposes a toast.(wiki:House_Buckler.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ralph's cousin, Ser [Brus Buckler](wiki:Brus_Buckler), remains in Stannis's service and is one of the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men).(wiki:House_Buckler.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) At the wedding between [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark) and [Sigorn](wiki:Sigorn) of [Thenn](wiki:Thenn_(valley)), Brus gives Princess [Shireen Baratheon](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon) her first dance.(wiki:House_Buckler.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) arranges for Brus to wed the second daughter of the wildling raider [Gerrick Kingsblood](wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood).(wiki:House_Buckler.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
