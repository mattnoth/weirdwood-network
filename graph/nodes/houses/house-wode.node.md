## Identity

House Wode is a noble house of landed knights in the riverlands (wiki:House_Wode). Their arms are three white hedgehogs on yellow, and their motto is "Touch Me Not" (wiki:House_Wode). Their lands lie close to the border with the crownlands near Sow's Horn (wiki:House_Wode).

## Narrative Arc

In AGOT, Ser Willis Wode aids Lady Catelyn Stark at the inn at the crossroads when she captures Tyrion Lannister, then battles clansmen during the journey through the Mountains of the Moon (wiki:House_Wode). In AFFC, Jaime Lannister passes through Wode lands and finds the brothers Wode's earth and timber halls burned down. Jaime hangs outlaws sheltering in the ruins (wiki:House_Wode).
