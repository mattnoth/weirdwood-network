## Identity

House Merryweather is a noble house from the Reach, seated at Longtable. Lord Orton Merryweather is the current head, with Russell Merryweather as heir. (wiki:House_Merryweather)

## Narrative Arc

Lord Orton Merryweather becomes a close ally of Cersei Lannister during AFFC, serving on the small council. His Myrish wife Taena Merryweather becomes one of Cersei's confidantes. The Merryweathers were previously exiled under the Mad King for supporting the Blackfyre pretenders but were restored. (wiki:House_Merryweather)
