## Origins

House Brax was established during the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) through the marriage of [First Men](wiki:First_Men) and [Andal](wiki:Andal) nobles.(wiki:House_Brax.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Lord [Andros Brax](wiki:Andros_Brax) once visited [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) attempting to arrange a [marriage](wiki:Marriage) for one of his three sons to [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) or [Lysa](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) of [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully), the daughters of [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), [Lord Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident).(wiki:House_Brax.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Among the gathered [knights](wiki:Knight) at the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) is one with the arms of House Brax.(wiki:House_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
During the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), House Brax answers the call of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister). Ser [Flement Brax](wiki:Flement_Brax) commands the outriders who find [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and his [Vale mountain clans](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) descending from the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon).(wiki:House_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Flement rides in the right flank during the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:House_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Lord [Andros Brax](wiki:Andros_Brax) and his [bannermen](wiki:Bannermen) are in Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s host, and Andros commands the western camp during Jaime's siege of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:House_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Ser [Tytos Brax](wiki:Tytos_Brax) and Jaime are captured in the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:House_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
While crossing the river in an attempt to defend the other camps, Lord Andros drowns during the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps) after his raft overturns and the weight of his armor takes him under.(wiki:House_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) Tytos becomes [Lord of Hornvale](wiki:Lord_of_Hornvale) after the death of his father.(wiki:House_Brax.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser [Rupert Brax](wiki:Rupert_Brax), Andros's brother, is slain at the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross).(wiki:House_Brax.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
The Brax colors are seen by [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) within Tywin Lannister's army when it leaves [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:House_Brax.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Ser [Robert Brax](wiki:Robert_Brax) dies at the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords).(wiki:House_Brax.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) His brother, Ser Flement, also tries to cross the fords, to no avail.(wiki:House_Brax.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
In the aftermath of the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) sees the the great western lords [Lydden](wiki:Lewys_Lydden), [Crakehall](wiki:Roland_Crakehall_(lord)), and Brax in the [throne room](wiki:Throne_room).(wiki:House_Brax.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) It is unknown if this is a freed Tytos or if Sansa is confusing him with a different Brax.

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Tytos, the new [Lord of Hornvale](wiki:Lord_of_Hornvale) is listed as a captive at the [Twins](wiki:Twins).(wiki:House_Brax.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) It is unknown if this is an error or if Tytos still a prisoner after the [Whispering Wood](wiki:Whispering_Wood).
Flement, now the heir of [Hornvale](wiki:Hornvale), is one of the nobles who accompany [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to welcome Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:House_Brax.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
Flement is called as a witness for the prosecution in the trial of Tyrion for [the death](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). He tells of how he heard Tyrion threaten the king.(wiki:House_Brax.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Freed from his captivity at some point, Lord [Tytos Brax](wiki:Tytos_Brax) is among the [westermen](wiki:Westermen) who escort Tywin's corpse from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) back to the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands).(wiki:House_Brax.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
Ser [Flement Brax](wiki:Flement_Brax) accompanies Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) through the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) to resolve the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), commanding a company of two hundred heavy horse.(wiki:House_Brax.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
