## Identity

House Penrose is a noble house from Parchments in the Stormlands. Their words are "Set Down Our Deeds." They blazon their arms with white and orange crossed quills on a russet field. (wiki:House_Penrose)

## Narrative Arc

The current head of House Penrose is unknown, listed only as "Penrose." (track_b: Head) The most prominent recent member was Ser Cortnay Penrose, who served as castellan of Storm's End during the War of the Five Kings. When Renly Baratheon died, Cortnay held Storm's End against Stannis Baratheon, refusing to yield the castle or surrender Renly's young bastard nephew Edric Storm. Cortnay offered single combat to decide the issue. He was killed by a shadow assassin sent by Melisandre, and the castle fell to Stannis. (wiki:House_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok22, wiki:House_Penrose.cite_ref-Racok42)

Historically, House Penrose has intermarried with House Targaryen. Queen Elinor Penrose was the first wife of King Aegon V Targaryen, though the match was later set aside. Aelinor Penrose married King Aerys I Targaryen. (wiki:House_Penrose)
