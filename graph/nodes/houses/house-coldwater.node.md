## Origins

The Coldwaters are of [First Men](wiki:First_Men) origin. During the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn), they allied with King [Robar II Royce](wiki:Robar_II_Royce) against the invading [Andals](wiki:Andals). Robar's forces were defeated in the [Battle of the Seven Stars](wiki:Battle_of_the_Seven_Stars), however, and the Coldwaters submitted to [Artys I Arryn](wiki:Artys_I_Arryn), the new [King of Mountain and Vale](wiki:King_of_Mountain_and_Vale).(wiki:House_Coldwater.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
When a [war of succession](wiki:Arryn_Succession_Conflict_(134_AC)) ensued in the Vale upon the death of Lady [Jeyne Arryn](wiki:Jeyne_Arryn), House Coldwater supported her cousin, Ser [Arnold Arryn](wiki:Arnold_Arryn), as heir to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie).(wiki:House_Coldwater.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]
