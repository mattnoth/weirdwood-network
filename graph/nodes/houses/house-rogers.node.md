## Identity

House Rogers is a noble house from Amberly in the Stormlands. They blazon their arms with nine unicorns, yellow on black. (wiki:House_Rogers)

## Allegiances

House Rogers is `REGION_OF` Stormlands, `SEAT_OF` Amberly, and `OVERLORD_OF` (reverse) House Baratheon. (track_b: Region, Seat, Overlord)

## Narrative Arc

The current head of the house is unknown. The only known member is Ser Reynard Rogers, who was slain by Ser Gwayne Corbray at the Battle of the Redgrass Field during the First Blackfyre Rebellion. (wiki:House_Rogers)

## Notes

The name "Rogers" and the house's association with Amberly are a homage by George R.R. Martin to author Roger Zelazny, who wrote the Amber series. (wiki:House_Rogers — Behind the Scenes)
