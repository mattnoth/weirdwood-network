## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The House of Merreq is hostile to the rule of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) and its household is suspected being members of the [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy). Several members of the House, along with members of the [House of Zhak](wiki:House_of_Zhak), wish to leave the city for their estates in the hills. Daenerys says they may do so, but as a precaution in case they mean to join the [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai), states that they are forbidden to take their gold and food supplies with them.(wiki:House_of_Merreq.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
With the [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy) killings continuing, Daenerys has every noble family of Meereen who is of dubious loyalty send her a child as a hostage.(wiki:House_of_Merreq.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) One of those hostages is [Mezzara Merreq](wiki:Mezzara_Merreq), whose father rules over the [pyramid of Merreq](wiki:Pyramid_of_Merreq).(wiki:House_of_Merreq.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Daenerys hopes having noble children as hostages will halt the killings, but it does not. However, Daenerys refuses to allow any harm to come to the children. She grows fond of them all, and ignores [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq)'s counsel to kill one for every death done by the Sons of the Harpy.(wiki:House_of_Merreq.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
When several Meereenese ships join the blockade of Meereen, Skahaz presents Daenerys a list with the names of the Meereenese captains and which families they belong to, with the House of Merreq among the names.(wiki:House_of_Merreq.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
When Daenerys disappears on [Drogon](wiki:Drogon)'s back and Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) seizes control of Meereen in her name, Skahaz wants to kill the child hostages in return for the deaths of the hostages Daenerys had granted to the [besiegers of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen). Barristan refuses to allow any harm to come to the children, however.(wiki:House_of_Merreq.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
