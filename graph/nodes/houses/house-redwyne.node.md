## Identity

House Redwyne of the Arbor is one of the main noble houses sworn to Highgarden (wiki:House_Redwyne). Their seat is the Arbor, an island off the southwestern-most part of Westeros, known for making the best wines in Westeros (wiki:House_Redwyne). The Redwynes control the Redwyne fleet, the largest fleet in Westeros, containing two hundred warships and five times as many merchant vessels (wiki:House_Redwyne). Their sigil is a burgundy grape cluster on blue (wiki:House_Redwyne).

## Origins

The Redwynes claim descent from Gilbert of the Vines, a legendary son of Garth Greenhand (wiki:House_Redwyne). House Redwyne ruled as Kings of the Arbor until their last monarch was lost at sea, allowing King Meryn III Gardener to absorb the isle into the Kingdom of the Reach (wiki:House_Redwyne).

## Allegiances

House Redwyne is `SWORN_TO` House Tyrell as overlord (track_b: Overlord). Lord Paxter Redwyne is married to Mina Tyrell, sister of Lord Mace Tyrell (wiki:House_Redwyne). Paxter's elderly aunt, Lady Olenna Redwyne (the "Queen of Thorns"), is the mother of Mace and Mina (wiki:House_Redwyne). Redwynes tend to be freckled and orange of hair (wiki:House_Redwyne).

## Narrative Arc

In AGOT, House Redwyne is among those Viserys believes will rise for him (agot-daenerys-01). The Redwyne twins, Horas and Hobber, participate in the Hand's tourney (wiki:House_Redwyne). In ACOK, the twins are held hostage in King's Landing, preventing House Redwyne from declaring for Renly. Tyrion sends Hobber back as a gesture of trust while keeping Horas (wiki:House_Redwyne). The Redwynes join the Tyrell-Lannister host at the Blackwater (wiki:House_Redwyne). In ASOS, Paxter gains a small council seat and a thirty-year tax remission on Arbor wines (wiki:House_Redwyne). In AFFC, Paxter is named the new master of ships; the Redwyne fleet is dispatched to break the ironborn siege of the Shield Islands (wiki:House_Redwyne). In ADWD, the fleet passes through the Straits of the Redwyne (wiki:House_Redwyne).
