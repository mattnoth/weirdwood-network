## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

A [knight](wiki:Knight) wearing the green viper of the Lynderlys is present in the [High Hall](wiki:Eyrie) of the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) when [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) demands a [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat) to prove his innocence. He is young and is the first to offer his sword to champion Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn).(wiki:House_Lynderly.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) tells [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) that Lord [Jon Lynderly](wiki:Jon_Lynderly) is one of his friends. Littlefinger asks Lords [Grafton](wiki:Gerold_Grafton) and Lynderly to send each a son to ward, to become [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn)'s squires.(wiki:House_Lynderly.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Lord Lynderly attends [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Lyonel_Corbray_and_Lady_Corbray) of Lord [Lyonel Corbray](wiki:Lyonel_Corbray). [Gyles Grafton](wiki:Gyles_Grafton) and [Terrance Lynderly](wiki:Terrance_Lynderly) descend to the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) with Robert.(wiki:House_Lynderly.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
