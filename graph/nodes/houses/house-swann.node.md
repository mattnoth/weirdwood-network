## Identity

House Swann of Stonehelm is one of the primary noble houses of the Stormlands. They are marcher lords ruling from Stonehelm over the lands surrounding the Red Watch on Cape Wrath in the eastern Dornish Marches. They blazon their arms with argent and sable a swan counter charged. (wiki:House_Swann)

## Heraldry & Sigil

Battling swans black and white, beaks and feet golden, on per pale white and black (Per pale argent and sable, two swans combatant counterchanged, beaked and membered or) (wiki:House_Swann)

## Origins

House Swann is an old noble house, regarded as proud, powerful, and cautious. They have governed their lands for as long as any can remember. From Stonehelm they control the Slayne, a major river route inland to the Stormlands, gaining them much wealth and power. The Swanns and Carons both claim to be the oldest of the marcher lords. (wiki:House_Swann.cite_ref-6)

## Narrative Arc

Lord Gulian Swann `RULES` House Swann, with Ser Donnel Swann as `HEIR_TO`. (track_b: Head, Heir) During the War of the Five Kings, Gulian pleaded illness and took no part, but his sons represented Stonehelm on different sides. (wiki:House_Swann.cite_ref-Racok49)

Donnel, the heir, first declared for Renly Baratheon, then Stannis, and was wounded at the Battle of the Blackwater. He yielded and was ransomed, later swearing fealty to Joffrey. (wiki:House_Swann.cite_ref-Rasos67)

Ser Balon Swann, the second son, distinguished himself at the Hand's tourney, defeating Alyn of Winterfell but falling to Gregor Clegane. (wiki:House_Swann.cite_ref-Ragot29) He was later named to the Kingsguard to replace the murdered Ser Preston Greenfield. (wiki:House_Swann.cite_ref-Racok49) At Tyrion's trial, Balon stated Tyrion was brave and he could not believe the poisoning charge. (wiki:House_Swann.cite_ref-Rasos66) Cersei later sent Balon to Dorne to deliver Gregor Clegane's head; Prince Doran delayed his arrival with lavish feasts and used him to cover Arianne's failed queenmaking plot. (wiki:House_Swann.cite_ref-Radwd38)

Historically, Johanna Swann was captured by Lysene pirates and became the celebrated courtesan known as the Black Swan, eventually the ruler of Lys in all but name. Ser Barristan Selmy squired for Ser Manfred Swann and later rescued Lady Jeyne Swann from the Kingswood Brotherhood. (wiki:House_Swann)
