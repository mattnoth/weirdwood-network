## Identity

House Piper of Pinkmaiden is one of the chief noble houses from the riverlands (wiki:House_Piper). Their blazon is a pink dancing maiden in a swirl of white silk over blue, and their semi-canon motto is "Brave and Beautiful" (wiki:House_Piper).

## Heraldry & Sigil

A dancing maiden pink in a swirl of white silk, on blue (Azure, a maiden dancing carnation enwrapped with silk argent) (wiki:House_Piper)

Words: "Brave and Beautiful" (wiki:House_Piper)

## Origins

During Aegon's Conquest, the Pipers joined Aegon the Conqueror against Harren the Black (wiki:House_Piper). The house has deep historical roots in the riverlands.

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Tully as overlord during AGOT (track_b: Overlords). `SWORN_TO` House Baelish by ADWD (track_b: Overlords). Pinkmaiden Castle is located on the Red Fork close to the border with the westerlands (wiki:House_Piper).

## Appearances & Description

Sigil: A dancing maiden pink in a swirl of white silk, on blue (wiki:House_Piper). Pipers tend to have red hair and are short and runty in height (wiki:House_Piper).

## Narrative Arc

In AGOT, Ser Edmure Tully commands Lord Vance and Lord Clement Piper to defend the riverlands against Lannister incursions. Young Marq Piper fights alongside the river lords and supports Robb Stark's crowning (wiki:House_Piper). In ASOS, Marq Piper continues as one of the loyal Tully bannermen. Lord Clement Piper eventually bends the knee to the Iron Throne. In AFFC, the Piper banner is seen among those besieging Riverrun under Lannister command, though Marq Piper remains unaccounted for (wiki:House_Piper).
