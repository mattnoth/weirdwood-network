## Origins

The Lockes ruled as [First Men](wiki:First_Men) kings after the [Long Night](wiki:Long_Night), but they were eventually reduced to vassals by the [Kings of Winter](wiki:Kings_of_Winter) from [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark).(wiki:House_Locke.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
After the extinction of [House Greystark](wiki:House_Greystark), the [Flints](wiki:House_Flint) held the [Wolf's Den](wiki:Wolf's_Den) for a century and the Lockes for almost two centuries.(wiki:House_Locke.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:House_Locke.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Ser [Donnel Locke](wiki:Donnel_Locke) is captured at some point by the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister),(wiki:House_Locke.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) presumably at the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork).

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser [Mallador Locke](wiki:Mallador_Locke) joins the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) commanded by [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). He is in charge of the baggage train and the packhorses.(wiki:House_Locke.cite_ref-Racok13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Lord [Ondrew Locke](wiki:Ondrew_Locke) excuses himself from attending the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) because he is too old to dare the journey.(wiki:House_Locke.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
The acting Hand of the King, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), plans to exchange Donnel and Lord [Medger Cerwyn](wiki:Medger_Cerwyn) for [Tion Frey](wiki:Tion_Frey), but the negotiations fail.(wiki:House_Locke.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Held at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Donnel is freed by [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton).(wiki:House_Locke.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

When the Watch receives news of the approach of the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) to the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men), Mallador supports retreating back to the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:House_Locke.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) However, he is not among the survivors of the expedition who return to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) after the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist) and the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep).(wiki:House_Locke.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
While going to the [Twins](wiki:Twins) to attend Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully)'s wedding, Lockes are caught on the wrong side of the [Trident](wiki:Trident). They suffer heavy losses when attacked by [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) in the [fighting at the fords of the Trident](wiki:Fighting_at_the_fords_of_the_Trident).(wiki:House_Locke.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) During the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) sees Donnel killed by crossbow bolts.(wiki:House_Locke.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sends a raven to [Oldcastle](wiki:Oldcastle) in a plea for help to [defend Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) from [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings).(wiki:House_Locke.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Sybelle Locke](wiki:Sybelle_Glover) and her children are held captive at [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) by [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy).(wiki:House_Locke.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) is first presented to Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) at the [Merman's Court](wiki:Merman's_Court), an unknown Locke(wiki:House_Locke.cite_ref-16) speaks briefly to the detriment of [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow).(wiki:House_Locke.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
When Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) takes [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) to [Barrow Hall](wiki:Barrow_Hall), amongst the banners they see are Ondrew's crossed keys.(wiki:House_Locke.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Lord Locke follows Roose to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) for the wedding of Ramsay to "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)".(wiki:House_Locke.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) However, Wyman believes the Lockes of Oldcastle will take their lead not from [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton), but from [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly).(wiki:House_Locke.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
