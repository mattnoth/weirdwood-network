## Origins

Lord Cerwyn was believed to have been the closest friend of [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark), [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell) and [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North). Lord Cerwyn supported the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) and was at court in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) during the [Hour of the Wolf](wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf).(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Lord [Robard Cerwyn](wiki:Robard_Cerwyn) was wed to [Argelle Stark](wiki:Argelle_Stark), the daughter of [Edric Stark](wiki:Edric_Stark) and granddaughter of Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark).(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_stark_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Title_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

The Cerwyn blazon is seen by [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) when [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls his banners. While encamped there, a [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) man stabs a Cerwyn man at the [Smoking Log](wiki:Smoking_Log) in the [winter town](wiki:Winter_town) outside the castle. [Medger Cerwyn](wiki:Medger_Cerwyn), [Lord of Cerwyn](wiki:Lord_of_Cerwyn), brings his daughter [Jonelle](wiki:Jonelle_Cerwyn) with him to Winterfell, to cook for him and sit at his left hand on the march to [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin). However, [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) suspects that Medger intends to put the girl in Robb's bed. Lord [Jon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber) threatens to return home with his levies if he is placed behind the [Hornwoods](wiki:House_Hornwood) or the Cerwyns in the order of march.(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
When Robb's army split at the [Twins](wiki:Twins), Medger marches with Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton)'s eastern army. [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sees the Cerwyn battleaxe among the northern forces at the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork), at which Lord Cerwyn is wounded and taken prisoner.(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Cley Cerwyn](wiki:Cley_Cerwyn) comes to Winterfell with two dozen lances for the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast), where he dances with [Beth Cassel](wiki:Beth_Cassel). A Cerwyn [knight](wiki:Knight) ridicules King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) as Joffrey the Illborn.(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
While [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Tyrion offers to exchange Lord Medger and Ser [Donnel Locke](wiki:Donnel_Locke) for his cousin [Tion Frey](wiki:Tion_Frey), assuring that a [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) is worth two northmen.(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), disguised as a [peasant](wiki:Peasant) at Harrenhal, learns that the captive Lord Cerwyn is being treated in a tower cell. Arya intends to sneak past the guards and reveal her identity to him, but Medger dies from his wound before she can do so, however. Arya sees three [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters) taking away a body covered by a cloak of silk decorated with his battleaxe sigil.(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Arya later sees Cerwyn men amid the [fall of Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal).(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
Now [lord](wiki:Lord) of his House, Cley brings three hundred men to join Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) on his march to clear the [ironmen](wiki:Ironmen) from the [Stony Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore)(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) and [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Fight_at_Torrhen's_Square).(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
Rodrik stops at [Castle Cerwyn](wiki:Castle_Cerwyn) before launching the final attack to take back Winterfell from [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy)'s ironmen.(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Cley comes to the Winterfell walls with a peace banner, announcing to Theon that Rodrik wishes to parley with him. After [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow)'s treachery, Cley is slain by [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) men at the [battle at the winter town](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell) in front of the castle, and his corpse is presented to Theon with an arrow in his eye.(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
[Osha](wiki:Osha) considers bringing [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) to the Cerwyns after the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell), but the dying Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin) explains that Cley is dead.(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After crossing the [ruby ford](wiki:Ruby_ford), Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) leaves six hundred men to guard the crossing. Many of them are Cerwyn men under the command of Ser [Kyle Condon](wiki:Kyle_Condon), who was Lord Medger's right hand. It is unknown what happened to these men after the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sends a raven to Castle Cerwyn in a plea for help to defend [Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) from [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings).(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) spots the badge of House Cerwyn among those that have been collected from the dead after the [battle at Duskendale](wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale).(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
Later Brienne and her companions see the corpses of the perpetrators of the [raid on Saltpans](wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans) hanged from trees. Among the dead are men with axes badges sewn on their breasts, presumably [Byrch](wiki:House_Byrch) or Cerwyn men-at-arms who became [broken men](wiki:Broken_men) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lady [Jonelle Cerwyn](wiki:Jonelle_Cerwyn) swears fealty to the new [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), and the Cerwyns contribute troops to [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow)'s [siege of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin).(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) The banner of House Cerwyn is seen when Roose gathers the northern forces to [Barrow Hall](wiki:Barrow_Hall) in [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton),(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) and Cerwyn cousins attend the wedding of Ramsay to "Arya" (actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) However, Roose is skeptical of the Cerwyns' loyalty.(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)
Cerwyn survivors from the earlier [battle at Winterfell](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell) rally to the side of King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:House_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)
