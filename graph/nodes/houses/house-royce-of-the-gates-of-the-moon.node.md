## Identity

House Royce of the Gates of the Moon is a noble house from the Gates of the Moon in the Vale of Arryn. They began as a junior branch of House Royce of Runestone (wiki:House_Royce_of_the_Gates_of_the_Moon). The current head is Lord Nestor Royce (track_b: Head), with Ser Albar Royce as heir (track_b: Heir).

## Heraldry & Sigil

## Narrative Arc

Lord Nestor Royce serves as Keeper of the Gates of the Moon, the fortress at the base of the Giant's Lance below the Eyrie. Petyr Baelish secures Nestor's loyalty by granting him the Gates of the Moon as a hereditary seat rather than a caretaker position, binding Nestor's interests to Littlefinger's regime (wiki:House_Royce_of_the_Gates_of_the_Moon).
