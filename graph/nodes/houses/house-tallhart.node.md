## Narrative Arc

House Tallhart is a masterly house — one of two houses (with House Glover) holding the rank of "Master" rather than "Lord" in the North. They are prominently involved in Robb Stark's campaign: their forces appear in the host gathering at Winterfell and march south. Torrhen's Square is attacked by the ironborn during the War of the Five Kings. The house suffers significant losses across ACOK and ASOS, with Ser Helman Tallhart killed at Duskendale (wiki:House_Tallhart.cite_refs-acok, wiki:House_Tallhart.cite_refs-asos). By ADWD, Eddara Tallhart leads the house, with Brandon Tallhart as heir.
