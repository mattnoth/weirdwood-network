## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Grazdan led the [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) envoys to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) when she came to conquer their city. On behalf of the city, he attempted to bribe her with gold and try to convince her to pass over Yunkai, but was rebuffed and in turn given an ultimatum by Daenerys to let the slaves go by the third day.
After the [crushing defeat](wiki:Battle_of_Yunkai) of the Yunkai forces outside of their walls, Grazdan is sent to deliver Daenerys's ultimatum.

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Grazdan mo Eraz travels to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) to persuade the [Triarchs](wiki:Triarch_of_Volantis) to send their fleet against [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen). His bribes and feasts are said to be lavish, he brought slave girls and boys trained in Yunkai in the way of the seven sighs to pleasure the ruling class of Volantis.
While in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) he spreads many false stories about Daenerys, that she is blood thirsty, feeds her [dragons](wiki:Dragon) on the flesh of newborn babies, impales anyone who defies her on spikes to die, takes a different lover every night and bathes in blood of maidens to retain her youth. (wiki:House_of_Eraz.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
Meanwhile, Gorzhak zo Eraz joins several other of the [Wise Masters](wiki:Wise_Masters) on the march against [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). The [Windblown](wiki:Windblown), a sellsword company hired by Yunkai, mock Gorzhak as "Pudding Face".(wiki:House_of_Eraz.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Since the death of [Yurkhaz zo Yunzak](wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak) in the [fighting pits](wiki:Fighting_pits) of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) the Yunkai'i keep a daily rotation for the duty of supreme commander. Gorzhak serves as supreme commander one day, [Faezhar zo Faez](wiki:Faezhar_zo_Faez) or [Ghazdor zo Ahlaq](wiki:Ghazdor_zo_Ahlaq) the next.
Gorzhak, along with his other two supreme commanders and [Bloodbeard](wiki:Bloodbeard), present the head of captain [Groleo](wiki:Groleo) to [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) and demand the deaths of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s [dragons](wiki:Dragon) in return for the release of the other hostages [Hero](wiki:Hero), [Daario Naharis](wiki:Daario_Naharis) and [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo).

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

On the eve of battle during the [Second Siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_Siege_of_Meereen), Gorzhak zo Eraz is slain by the [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince), who turns the Windblown's cloak to join Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen).(wiki:House_of_Eraz.cite_ref-3)
