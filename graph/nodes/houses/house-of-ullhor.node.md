## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Grazdan mo Ullhor](wiki:Grazdan_mo_Ullhor), one of the [Good Masters](wiki:Good_Masters) of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor) who deals in [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied), attends a meeting with [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen). He is also present the next day, when Daenerys arrives to take control over the Unsullied of the city. When she orders her [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) to [attack](wiki:Fall_of_Astapor) after taking possession of the Unsullied, Grazdan shouts for the Unsullied to defend the Good Masters. He is killed by Daenerys's [bloodrider](wiki:Bloodrider) [Rakharo](wiki:Rakharo), who puts an arrow through his mouth.(wiki:House_of_Ullhor.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
One of Grazdan's former slaves, [Cleon](wiki:Cleon), later overthrows the [ruling council of Astapor](wiki:Ruling_council_of_Astapor) installed by Daenerys, and installs himself as King of Astapor.(wiki:House_of_Ullhor.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Following their failed attack against [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) during the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor), the survivors of the battle hold a great feast in the [pyramid of Ullhor](wiki:Pyramid_of_Ullhor), ending the night by drinking [poisoned](wiki:Poison) [wine](wiki:Wine).(wiki:House_of_Ullhor.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
