## Origins

House Kenning of Kayce was founded by [Herrock Kenning](wiki:Herrock_Kenning), an [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) warrior from [House Kenning of Harlaw](wiki:House_Kenning_of_Harlaw).(wiki:House_Kenning_of_Kayce.cite_ref-Rawoiafkayce.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) During the decline of the [driftwood kings](wiki:Driftwood_king) of the ironborn, Herrock the Whoreson used his horn as a signal for the whores of [Kayce](wiki:Kayce) to open a postern gate, allowing his men to take the town for [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister), the [Kings of the Rock](wiki:Kings_of_the_Rock).(wiki:House_Kenning_of_Kayce.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Herrock's warhorn, the [Horn of Herrock](wiki:Horn_of_Herrock), is passed down amongst the family as an heirloom.(wiki:House_Kenning_of_Kayce.cite_ref-Rawoiafkennos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
The widow of a [Knight of Kayce](wiki:Knight_of_Kayce) was captured by ironborn raiders in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC) and became one of the twenty-two [salt wives](wiki:Salt_wife) of [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:House_Kenning_of_Kayce.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
