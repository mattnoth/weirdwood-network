## Origins

During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Lords Fell, [Errol](wiki:House_Errol), and [Buckler](wiki:House_Buckler) [ambushed](wiki:Ambush_on_the_Wendwater) [Orys Baratheon](wiki:Orys_Baratheon)'s host as he crossed the [Wendwater](wiki:Wendwater). The lords later hid in the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood), but [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) set the forests ablaze with [Meraxes](wiki:Meraxes).(wiki:House_Fell.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Lord Fell was smothered in a [brothel](wiki:Brothel) by [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) assassins during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War).(wiki:House_Fell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_reign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
At the beginning of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), [Lady Fell](wiki:Lady_Fell) was executed for refusing to renounce her oaths to [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen).(wiki:House_Fell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Ser [Willis Fell](wiki:Willis_Fell), who had been a member of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) of King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen), sided with King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), and he successfully brought Princess [Jaehaera](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen) to the safety of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) after the [fall of King's Landing](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing) to Rhaenyra.(wiki:House_Fell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) After the end of the war, Willis became [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard) for [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen). Willis died of [Winter Fever](wiki:Winter_Fever), however.(wiki:House_Fell.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
In [209 AC](wiki:209_AC), Ser [Thurgood Fell](wiki:Thurgood_Fell) rode in the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow).(wiki:House_Fell.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
At the beginning of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), [Lord Fell](wiki:Lord_Fell) remained loyal to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) and together with Lords [Grandison](wiki:Lord_Grandison) and [Cafferen](wiki:Lord_Cafferen) planned to join their forces to bring Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s head to King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen). Robert defeated them in three [battles at Summerhall](wiki:Battles_at_Summerhall), slaying Lord Fell in single combat and capturing his son [Silveraxe](wiki:Silveraxe_Fell), who turned to Robert's side afterwards.(wiki:House_Fell.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord [Harwood Fell](wiki:Harwood_Fell) campaigns in the [north](wiki:North) with [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), but he dies from a chill after falling into an icy river during the [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell).(wiki:House_Fell.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Four men of [House Peasebury](wiki:House_Peasebury) are burned by the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) after they are caught cannibalizing the corpse a Fell man-at-arms.(wiki:House_Fell.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
