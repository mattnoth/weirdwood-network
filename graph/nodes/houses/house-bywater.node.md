## Identity

House Bywater is a noble house from the crownlands. Their arms are fretty blue on white, three silver fish on a blue chief (wiki:House_Bywater).

## Allegiances

`OVERLORD_OF` House Targaryen → House Bywater (?–283 AC), then House Baratheon of King's Landing (283 AC–present) (track_b: Overlords). `REGION_OF` Crownlands (track_b: Region).

## Narrative Arc

Historically, Lord Bywater was one of King Maegor I's last supporters; when Maegor died, he fled to his seat (wiki:Fire_and_Blood). Jacelyn Bywater lost a hand at the siege of Pyke during Greyjoy's Rebellion and was knighted by King Robert, wearing an iron hand in place of the lost one. In ACOK, Ser Jacelyn is named Commander of the City Watch when Janos Slynt is exiled. On Tyrion's orders, Jacelyn seizes Prince Tommen and arrests Ser Boros Blount at Rosby, earning a lordship. He assigns runners to aid Tyrion during the Blackwater. In ASOS, Bronn reports Jacelyn was killed during the Blackwater by his own fleeing men (wiki:House_Bywater).
