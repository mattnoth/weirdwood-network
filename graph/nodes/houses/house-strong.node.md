## Identity

House Strong of Harrenhal was a noble house in the riverlands, now extinct (wiki:House_Strong). Their arms are a tripartite pale blue, red, and green on white, representing the three forks of the Trident (wiki:House_Strong).

## Heraldry & Sigil

A tripartite pale, blue, red, and green on white (Argent, a pale tierced per pale azure, gules, and vert) (wiki:House_Strong)

## Origins

House Strong boasted descent from the First Men (wiki:House_Strong). During Aegon's Conquest, they followed House Tully in rebelling against Harren Hoare (wiki:House_Strong). King Jaehaerys I granted them Harrenhal after House Towers went extinct in 73 AC (wiki:House_Strong).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Tully as overlord (track_b: Overlord). Held Harrenhal as their seat (track_b: Seat). Like all houses who held Harrenhal, they met a tragic end (wiki:House_Strong).

## Appearances & Description

Sigil: A tripartite pale, blue, red, and green on white (wiki:House_Strong). Ser Harwin Strong was known as "Breakbones" for his great physical strength (wiki:House_Strong).

## Narrative Arc

Lord Lyonel Strong served Viserys I as master of laws and later Hand of the King (wiki:House_Strong). His son Ser Harwin "Breakbones" Strong was widely rumored to have fathered Rhaenyra Targaryen's three eldest sons. Both Lyonel and Harwin died in a fire at Harrenhal — foul play was suspected, with Larys Strong, Lyonel's younger son, believed responsible (wiki:House_Strong). Larys "Clubfoot" Strong served as master of whisperers during the Dance of the Dragons, playing both sides before being executed (wiki:House_Strong). The house went extinct with Larys's death.
