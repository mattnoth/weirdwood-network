## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Left-Hand Lucas](wiki:Lucas_Codd) attaches himself to [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) when Euron declares himself [king](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North).(wiki:House_Codd.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The Codds attend the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk); [Eldred Codd](wiki:Eldred_Codd) plays the [finger dance](wiki:Finger_dance) with [Qarl the Maid](wiki:Qarl_the_Maid) and loses during the festivities.(wiki:House_Codd.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Lucas supports Euron during the choosing, and he takes the [dragon horn](wiki:Dragonbinder) from [Cragorn](wiki:Cragorn) after it is blown.(wiki:House_Codd.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Lucas is among those feasting in the hall of [Lord Hewett's castle](wiki:Lord_Hewett's_castle) after the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields). He wears a tapestry he tore from the wall. After the feast he rapes one of Lord [Humfrey Hewett](wiki:Humfrey_Hewett)'s daughters.(wiki:House_Codd.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Dagon Codd](wiki:Dagon_Codd) is one of the many members of House Codd left to defend [Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin) when Victarion departs for the kingsmoot. Dagon is killed by [Adrack Humble](wiki:Adrack_Humble) for refusing to accept the terms of surrender offered by [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) on behalf of [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow).(wiki:House_Codd.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
[Bloodless Tom](wiki:Tom_Codd) sails with the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay).(wiki:House_Codd.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
