## Identity

House Mooton of Maidenpool is a noble house from the riverlands, historically far wealthier than their liege lords, House Tully of Riverrun (wiki:House_Mooton). Their blazon is a red salmon on white with a golden tressure, and their semi-canon motto is "Wisdom and Strength" (wiki:House_Mooton).

## Origins

The Mootons were petty kings in the days of the First Men. The Storm King Monfryd the Mighty defeated a Mooton king and conquered Maidenpool prior to the coming of the Andals (wiki:House_Mooton). King Florian the Brave was killed by Andals in the Fall of Maidenpool (wiki:House_Mooton). Maidenpool is associated with the legend of Florian the Fool seeing Jonquil bathing (wiki:House_Mooton).

## Narrative Arc

During Aegon's Conquest, Lord Mooton joined Lord Darklyn in opposing Aegon but was slain; his brother Jon Mooton then yielded and supported the Targaryens (wiki:House_Mooton). In the main series, Lord William Mooton bends the knee to the Iron Throne. In AFFC, Maidenpool features prominently in Brienne's quest — the town has been badly damaged by war, and Randyll Tarly holds it temporarily while dispensing justice (wiki:House_Mooton).
