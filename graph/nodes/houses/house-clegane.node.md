## Origins

The [first Clegane](wiki:Clegane_(founder)) of House Clegane was [kennelmaster](wiki:Kennelmaster) at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock). During one [autumn](wiki:Autumn) year he saved Lord [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister) from a [lioness](wiki:Lion), although he lost a leg and three dogs while doing so. Lord Tytos rewarded him with lands and a towerhouse. Ser Clegane's son was placed in Tytos's service as his squire. For his sigil Ser Clegane used the three dogs he lost saving Tytos, on the yellow of autumn grass.(wiki:House_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:House_Clegane.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
The kennelmaster's son, the [father](wiki:Clegane_(father_of_Gregor_and_Sandor)) of [Gregor](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) and [Sandor](wiki:Sandor_Clegane), died in a hunting accident. Their sister died young under queer circumstances.(wiki:House_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Gregor is known for his unspeakable cruelty; this is mirrored in the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men), who casually commit atrocities during their campaigns. Sandor was driven away by this behavior, taking service with [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) and refusing to take a knight's vows to avoid association with his elder.
Gregor was knighted by Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), the heir of King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen). During the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing), Gregor murdered Rhaegar's son, the infant Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)), before raping and murdering his mother and Rhaegar's bride, Princess [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell).

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane), known as the [Hound](wiki:Hound), accompanies the royal party to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) when King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) offers Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) the position of [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King). On the return journey, Sandor kills [Mycah](wiki:Mycah), the butcher's boy whom Prince [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) claims struck him.
During the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney), Sandor defends Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) against his brother, Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane), when the Mountain tries to kill Loras for riding a mare in heat. In gratitude, Loras cedes the final round and the tourney purse to Sandor.
Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) sends Gregor to raid the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), slaughtering the smallfolk of [Sherrer](wiki:Sherrer), [Wendish Town](wiki:Wendish_Town), and the [Mummer's Ford](wiki:Mummer's_Ford). In retribution, Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) is sent to kill Gregor, who ambushes Beric's party in a [battle at the Mummer's Ford](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford). Gregor also attacks [Pinkmaiden](wiki:Burning_of_Pinkmaiden_Castle) and [Stone Hedge](wiki:Burning_of_Stone_Hedge).(wiki:House_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Gregor commands the left and the vanguard of the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) army in the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:House_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
After the dismissal of Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), Sandor is made a brother of Joffrey's [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), though he refuses to take a knight's vows.

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Gregor and his troops perpetrate the [sack of Darry](wiki:Sack_of_Darry). The [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) capture [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) and her companions, although they are not aware of her identity. Gregor skirmishes continuously with Beric Dondarrion's [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners).
Joffrey's sending the Hound against the crowd sparks the [riot of King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing). Sandor is at first thought dead along with [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), but he returns with the girl in tow.
Gregor makes progress during the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords), but he is thrown back by Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) at the [Stone Mill](wiki:Stone_Mill).
During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Sandor flees the [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire)-covered battlefield. He offers to take Sansa from the Red Keep, but she refuses to leave.

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

When [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) repulses the [northmen](wiki:Northmen) during the [battle at Duskendale](wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale), their retreat is cut off by Gregor's forces. When Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) leaves [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) to march north to the [Twins](wiki:Twins) for the wedding of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully), Gregor attacks Roose's rearguard in the [fighting at the fords of the Trident](wiki:Fighting_at_the_fords_of_the_Trident).(wiki:House_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) He eventually seizes the [ruby ford](wiki:Ruby_ford) and leads the [capture of Harrenhal](wiki:Capture_of_Harrenhal).(wiki:House_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Sandor is caught by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) and brought to the [hollow hill](wiki:Hollow_hill). He defeats [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) in a [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat) and is allowed to leave. Sandor later captures [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) and intends to ransom her to her brother, King [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), at the Twins, but his efforts are ruined by the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding). He then tries to ransom Arya to her aunt, Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn), at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), but they are unable to pass the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon). Injuries that Sandor receives in fighting [Polliver](wiki:Polliver) and the [Tickler](wiki:Tickler) at the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads) later become infected; Arya refuses him the [gift of mercy](wiki:Gift_of_mercy) and leaves him to die near [Saltpans](wiki:Saltpans).
In [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s trial by combat for [the murder](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), Gregor is named the royal champion. Against him stands Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell), brother of the princess he slew during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell). Gregor barely manages to defeat Oberyn.

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Gregor suffers a tortuous death from the poison the Red Viper had used during their duel.
The [Elder Brother](wiki:Elder_Brother_(Quiet_Isle)) of the [Quiet Isle](wiki:Quiet_Isle) tells [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) that the [Hound](wiki:Hound) is dead and that he buried his helm, although Brienne sees a large [gravedigger](wiki:Gravedigger) on the isle.(wiki:House_Clegane.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Sandor is charged with the murder of an old septon and a young girl pledged to the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) during the [raid on Saltpans](wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans). However, it was actually [Rorge](wiki:Rorge) wearing Sandor's hound helm. After Rorge and [Biter](wiki:Biter) are killed at the crossroads, [Lem](wiki:Lem) takes the helm.(wiki:House_Clegane.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
