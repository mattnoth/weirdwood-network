## Identity

House Holt was an extinct noble house from Dorne, distinct from any other House Holt (wiki:House_Holt_(Dorne)).

## Origins

A First Men house of the Greenblood, the Holts were among a dozen houses who chose a High King of Dorne from amongst their number (wiki:House_Holt_(Dorne)). Three of the houses were destroyed after a generation of warfare following a disputed election; the remaining houses were conquered during the Andal invasion (wiki:House_Holt_(Dorne)).
