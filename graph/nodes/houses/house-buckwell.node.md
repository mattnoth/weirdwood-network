## Identity

House Buckwell of the Antlers is a noble house from Antlers in the crownlands. They are sworn to the Iron Throne. Their arms are a rack of golden antlers on vair. Their words are "Pride and Purpose" (wiki:House_Buckwell).

## Allegiances

`OVERLORD_OF` House Targaryen → House Buckwell (?–283 AC), then House Baratheon of King's Landing (283 AC–present) (track_b: Overlords). `REGION_OF` Crownlands (track_b: Region).

## Appearances & Description

Sigil: a rack of golden antlers on vair. Words: "Pride and Purpose" (wiki:House_Buckwell).

## Narrative Arc

Historically, Lord Buckwell was among King Maegor I's last supporters; when Maegor died, Buckwell and Rollingford sailed for Pentos (wiki:Fire_and_Blood). In ACOK, Ser Jarman Buckwell commands the scouts during the great ranging of the Night's Watch from Castle Black. In ASOS, Jarman survives the fight at the Fist and returns to Castle Black. In ADWD, Jon Snow remembers Jarman as one of the good men lost by the Watch (wiki:House_Buckwell).
