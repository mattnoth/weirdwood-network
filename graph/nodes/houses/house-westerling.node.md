## Identity

House Westerling is an ancient noble house from the westerlands, seated at the Crag. They hold the title Lord of the Crag. Though once a proud and wealthy house, they have fallen on hard times, their mines long since played out and their best lands sold off. (wiki:House_Westerling)

## Origins

House Westerling is one of the oldest houses in the westerlands, claiming descent from the First Men. They were once kings of the Crag before bending the knee to Casterly Rock. (wiki:House_Westerling)

## Narrative Arc

House Westerling's fortunes changed dramatically when Jeyne Westerling married King Robb Stark after he captured the Crag. This marriage broke Robb's betrothal to the Freys and contributed to the Red Wedding. Jeyne's mother Sybell Spicer secretly conspired with Tywin Lannister, giving Jeyne moon tea to prevent her conceiving Robb's heir. After the Red Wedding, Gawen was taken prisoner. In AFFC, Jaime Lannister negotiates the resolution of House Westerling's status, with their heir Rollam to serve as a page. (wiki:House_Westerling; agot-catelyn-10)
