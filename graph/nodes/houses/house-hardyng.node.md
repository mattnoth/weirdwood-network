## Identity

House Hardyng is a house of landed knights in the Vale of Arryn, sworn to House Waynwood (wiki:House_Hardyng). The current head is Ser Harrold Hardyng (track_b: Head).

## Narrative Arc

Ser Humfrey Hardyng participated in the tourney at Ashford Meadow in 209 AC, beginning as one of the champions. He took Ser Duncan the Tall's side in the trial of seven. In the current era, Ser Harrold Hardyng ("Harry the Heir") is the acknowledged heir to the Vale of Arryn through his mother's Arryn blood, making him a figure of major political importance. Petyr Baelish plans to reveal Sansa Stark's identity and betroth her to Harrold (wiki:House_Hardyng).
