## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Reznak mo Reznak](wiki:Reznak_mo_Reznak) is one of the current members of the House of Reznak. The House is supposedly less hostile to the rule of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) and Reznak serves as seneschal to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)(wiki:House_of_Reznak.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) and later continues that position to King [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq).(wiki:House_of_Reznak.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
When several Meereenese ships join the blockade of Meereen, [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) presents [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) a list with the names of the Meereenese captains and which families they belong too. The House of Reznak was among the names.(wiki:House_of_Reznak.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
