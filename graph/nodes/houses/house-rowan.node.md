## Identity

House Rowan of Goldengrove is one of the most prominent and old families from the Reach, its dominions extending along the northern borders (wiki:House_Rowan). Their blazon is a golden tree on silver (wiki:House_Rowan). Like several other major Reach houses, the Rowans trace descent from Garth Greenhand through his daughter Rowan Gold-Tree (wiki:House_Rowan).

## Heraldry & Sigil

A golden tree on silver (Argent, a tree or) (wiki:House_Rowan)

## Origins

Founded in the Age of Heroes by a daughter of Rowan Gold-Tree, a daughter of Garth Greenhand (wiki:House_Rowan). The Rowans may have been made Marshall of the Northmarch to replace the declining House Osgrey, now their bannermen (wiki:House_Rowan).

## Allegiances

House Rowan is `SWORN_TO` House Tyrell as overlord (track_b: Overlord). Their seat is Goldengrove (track_b: Seat). Vassals include House Osgrey and House Webber (wiki:House_Rowan). Lord Rowan was distant kin to Lady Rohanne Webber, since his sister married a cousin of her father (wiki:House_Rowan).

## Narrative Arc

In AGOT, Dareon claims Mathis Rowan's daughter sent him to the Wall after a seduction accusation (agot-jon-04). In ACOK, Lord Mathis rides with Renly, then joins the Tyrell-Lannister host at the Blackwater (wiki:House_Rowan). He is granted a small council seat by Lord Tywin (wiki:House_Rowan). In ASOS, Lord Rowan dances with Sansa at Tyrion's wedding feast and gives Joffrey a silk pavilion (wiki:House_Rowan). In AFFC, Cersei revokes Mathis's council seat (wiki:House_Rowan). In ADWD, Mathis co-commands the siege of Storm's End with Mace Tyrell (wiki:House_Rowan).
