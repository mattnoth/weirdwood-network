## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) remembers that [Jeyne Farman](wiki:Jeyne_Farman) married [Gareth Clifton](wiki:Gareth_Clifton), one of her brother's [Sebaston](wiki:Sebaston_Farman)'s [bannermen](wiki:Bannermen), and had a dozen children.(wiki:House_Clifton.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
[Hugh Clifton](wiki:Hugh_Clifton) is a member of Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell)'s guard in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:House_Clifton.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He is named by the [Blue Bard](wiki:Blue_Bard) as often being in Margaery's presence.(wiki:House_Clifton.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Cersei has an arrest warrant signed for Hugh as part of her plan to get rid of Margaery. He is sent to the dungeons after his arrest.(wiki:House_Clifton.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser [Humfrey Clifton](wiki:Humfrey_Clifton) accompanies [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) in his [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell) in the [north](wiki:North).(wiki:House_Clifton.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Hugh is held with the rest of Margaery's accused lovers in a dungeon under the charge of [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn).(wiki:House_Clifton.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
