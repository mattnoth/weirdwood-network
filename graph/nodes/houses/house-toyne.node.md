## Identity

House Toyne is an extinct noble house from the Stormlands. Their words were "Fly High, Fly Far." They blazoned their arms with a winged heart, black on gold. (wiki:House_Toyne)

## Narrative Arc

House Toyne is extinct — the head is listed as "Extinct." (track_b: Head) The house's downfall was tied to Ser Terrence Toyne of the Kingsguard, who was found abed with one of King Aegon IV Targaryen's mistresses. Terrence was tortured to death, and his brothers attempted to avenge him by trying to kill the king, resulting in the death of Prince Aemon the Dragonknight, who gave his life to save Aegon. House Toyne was destroyed in the aftermath. (wiki:House_Toyne.cite_ref-Raffc13)

The most notable later member was Myles Toyne, called "Blackheart," who served as captain-general of the Golden Company. He secretly made a pact with Varys and Illyrio Mopatis to support the Targaryen restoration. Myles died of a burst bladder, and the command passed to Harry Strickland. (wiki:House_Toyne.cite_ref-Radwd24)

Simon Toyne was an outlaw who led the Kingswood Brotherhood before being slain by Ser Barristan Selmy. (wiki:House_Toyne.cite_ref-Rasos67)
