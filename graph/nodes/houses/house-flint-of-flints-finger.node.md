## Origins

It is suggested that the [Night's King](wiki:Night's_King) was a Flint, among the many possibilities. Lord Commander [Rodrik Flint](wiki:Rodrik_Flint) is regarded as one of the worst commanders of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), for trying to make himself [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall). One of the dark legends surrounding the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort) is the rape and murder of brave young [Danny Flint](wiki:Danny_Flint).(wiki:House_Flint_of_Flint's_Finger.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:House_Flint_of_Flint's_Finger.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
A branch of House Flint held the [Wolf's Den](wiki:Wolf's_Den) for a century after the extinction of the [Greystarks](wiki:House_Greystark).(wiki:House_Flint_of_Flint's_Finger.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[Timotty Snow](wiki:Timotty_Snow), the Bastard of Flint's Finger, founded the [Wolf Pack](wiki:Wolf_Pack) with [Hallis Hornwood](wiki:Hallis_Hornwood) after the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:House_Flint_of_Flint's_Finger.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
When he was only ten years old, [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) scaled the [Flint Cliffs](wiki:Flint_Cliffs) to the [Blind Lord](wiki:Blind_Lord)'s haunted tower.(wiki:House_Flint_of_Flint's_Finger.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

After [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls his banners, Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin) explains that while some houses (such as the [Karstarks](wiki:House_Karstark), [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton), [Hornwoods](wiki:House_Hornwood) and [Umbers](wiki:House_Umber)) gather at the [winter town](wiki:Winter_town), others such as the Lords [Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) and Flint are waiting to join him along the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad).(wiki:House_Flint_of_Flint's_Finger.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Since House Flint of Widow's Watch is ruled by a lady, the passage probably refers to the Lord of Flint's Finger.

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The Flints are reported to have ties to House Hornwood through the female line, though [A Clash of Kings](wiki:A_Clash_of_Kings) does not specify which branch.(wiki:House_Flint_of_Flint's_Finger.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
When [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) studies the banners from the northern lords preparing to [attack Winterfell](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell) through Maester Luwin's [Myrish lens tube](wiki:Far-eye), he sees some of House Flint, though not as many as from other houses.(wiki:House_Flint_of_Flint's_Finger.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The Flints of Flint's Finger swear allegiance to Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), the new [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North). Their stone hand sigil is seen at [Barrow Hall](wiki:Barrow_Hall) when Roose gathers his bannermen(wiki:House_Flint_of_Flint's_Finger.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) and at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) for the wedding of [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) to "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)".(wiki:House_Flint_of_Flint's_Finger.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) A Flint crossbowman is found with a broken skull in Winterfell's stable.(wiki:House_Flint_of_Flint's_Finger.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
