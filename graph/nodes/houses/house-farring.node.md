## Identity

House Farring is a noble house of the crownlands. Their arms are per pale purple and white, two knights on foot combatant with swords, counter-charged (wiki:House_Farring).

## Narrative Arc

Lady Annara Farring is the seventh wife of Lord Walder Frey; rumor holds she may have slept with Black Walder Frey, one of Walder's great-grandsons (wiki:House_Farring). In ACOK, Bryen Farring, a squire to Stannis, attends the king at the burning of the sept statues at Dragonstone. In ASOS, Ser Gilbert Farring commands Stannis's garrison at Storm's End. In AFFC, Lord Mace Tyrell begins the siege of Storm's End, still held by Gilbert. In ADWD, Ser Godry Farring, nicknamed "Giantslayer" for killing a giant during the battle beneath the Wall, is among the queen's men at Castle Black. He heckles Jon Snow, trying to provoke a duel. Godry leads Stannis's vanguard during the march on Winterfell; Bryen dies during the march (wiki:House_Farring).
