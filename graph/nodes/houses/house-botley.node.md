## Origins

Amid a [struggle for power](wiki:Struggle_for_power_in_the_Iron_Islands) after the death of Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), Lord Botley was put to the sword by [westermen](wiki:Westerlands) during Lady [Johanna Lannister](wiki:Johanna_Lannister)'s invasion of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC).(wiki:House_Botley.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
The stronghold of the Botleys had been a great structure of timber and wattle, but when King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) put down [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion), he razed the structure to the ground during a [battle at Lordsport](wiki:Battle_at_Lordsport). A small square keep of stone was built afterwards.(wiki:House_Botley.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley) became friends with [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) while fostering at [Pyke](wiki:Pyke) after the rebellion, but he was sent away to [Blacktyde](wiki:Blacktyde) after he and Asha were found touching each other by Maester [Qalen](wiki:Qalen).(wiki:House_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) arrives at [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport) from [Seagard](wiki:Seagard) as an envoy of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North). He sees the new keep of the Botleys, full of pale green flags emblazoned with a shoal of silvery fish, and remembers that the last time he saw Lordsport it was a smoking wasteland.(wiki:House_Botley.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) In exchange for a horse, Theon takes [Wex Pyke](wiki:Wex_Pyke), the bastard of [Sargon Botley](wiki:Sargon_Botley), as his squire. When entering the [Great Hall](wiki:Great_Keep_of_Pyke) of [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), Theon sees some Botleys among his father [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy)'s captains.(wiki:House_Botley.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
[Maron Botley](wiki:Maron_Botley) and his sons are part of Theon's crew on the [Sea Bitch](wiki:Sea_Bitch). They take part in the [harrying of the Stony Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore) and quarrel afterwards with [Todric](wiki:Todric) over plunder.(wiki:House_Botley.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Theon later [captures Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell).(wiki:House_Botley.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Wex chooses to stay and fight alongside Theon when Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel)'s army of northmen approaches Winterfell. Two Botleys join Wex in staying by Theon; they in all probability are slain by [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) during the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell).(wiki:House_Botley.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord [Sawane Botley](wiki:Sawane_Botley) stands against [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) and claims that the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair) belongs to Theon after King [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy)'s death. Euron Crow's Eye has Sawane drowned for that.(wiki:House_Botley.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Germund Botley](wiki:Germund_Botley) pledges fealty to Euron, inheriting his Sawane's brother's lordship instead of his nephews, and retaining the part of the Botley lands that are not awarded to Lord [Waldon Wynch](wiki:Waldon_Wynch) for his support.(wiki:House_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
Sawane's son [Harren](wiki:Harren_Botley), part of [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy)'s force holding [Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin), dies from a poisoned arrow from the [crannogmen](wiki:Crannogmen).(wiki:House_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)
[Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) spots the silverfish of Botley among the banners of those gathered to support her on [Harlaw](wiki:Harlaw). She is approached by her childhood crush [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley), lawful [Lord of Lordsport](wiki:Lord_of_Lordsport) after his father Sawane's death, who says that his uncle Germund has been named lord by Euron Crow's Eye in return for his support. Half of the Botley lands, however, have been given to [House Wynch](wiki:House_Wynch) since they were the first to declare for Euron. Tristifer proposes to Asha but is refused.(wiki:House_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)
Victarion sees [Silverfin](wiki:Silverfin) on [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk) before the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), and [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) spots the banners of House Botley among those gathered for the selection. During the kingsmoot, Germund wears a gilded breastplate he had taken off a [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) captain during [Balon's first rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion). At the kingsmoot, Germund shouts for Euron and Tristifer shouts for Asha.(wiki:House_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Germund participates in the ironborn attack on the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands). After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), he is spotted by Victarion in the hall of [Lord Hewett's castle](wiki:Lord_Hewett's_castle) with a rope of pearls around his neck.(wiki:House_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

It is revealed that Wex survived the treachery of the [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) men by climbing the [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree) in the [godswood of Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell) and hiding in its branches. He eventually reached Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly), who learned from Wex the truth of the sack of Winterfell as well as the whereabouts of [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark).
