## Identity

House Arryn of the Eyrie is one of the Great Houses of Westeros and the principal noble house in the Vale of Arryn. Their words are "As High as Honor" (wiki:House_Arryn). The current head is Lord Robert Arryn, with Ser Harrold Hardyng as heir (track_b: Head, Heir).

## Origins

Founded by Ser Artys Arryn during the Coming of the Andals (wiki:House_Arryn). The Arryns claim descent from the kings of the Andals and ruled as Kings of Mountain and Vale before Aegon's Conquest (wiki:House_Arryn).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (1–282 AC), then `SWORN_TO` House Baratheon of King's Landing (284 AC–present) (track_b: Overlords). `REGION_OF` Vale of Arryn (track_b: Region). `HOLDS_TITLE` King of Mountain and Vale (pre-Conquest), Lord of the Eyrie, Defender of the Vale, Warden of the East (track_b: Titles). `CADET_BRANCH_OF` House Arryn of Gulltown (track_b: Cadet branches). The Arryns have distant relatives throughout the Vale, described by Petyr Baelish as "numerous but poor" (wiki:House_Arryn).

## Appearances & Description

Their sigil is a sky-blue falcon soaring upwards against a white moon on a sky-blue field (wiki:House_Arryn). Their main seat is the Eyrie (summer), considered impregnable, with a winter castle at the Gates of the Moon, granted to the Royces (track_b: Seats). Both fortifications sit astride the Giant's Lance, the tallest mountain in the Vale (wiki:House_Arryn).

## Narrative Arc

House Arryn is central to the political intrigue of the series. The death of Lord Jon Arryn, Hand of the King, is the inciting event that draws Eddard Stark to King's Landing. Lysa Arryn's secret letter to Catelyn Stark falsely accuses the Lannisters of Jon's murder, setting the Stark-Lannister conflict in motion. Lysa retreats to the Eyrie with young Robert Arryn, keeping the Vale neutral during the War of the Five Kings. Petyr Baelish marries Lysa and then kills her, becoming Lord Protector and regent for Robert. The Vale's forces remain uncommitted through most of the war, making them a powerful faction in reserve. Harrold Hardyng is named heir presumptive to the sickly Robert Arryn (wiki:House_Arryn) (agot-eddard-01) (agot-catelyn-01) (agot-catelyn-02).
