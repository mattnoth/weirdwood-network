## Identity

House Towers of Harrenhal was a noble house from the riverlands who ruled Harrenhal for three generations before dying out, now extinct (wiki:House_Towers_of_Harrenhal). Their arms are five black towers on white, a double tressure red and black (wiki:House_Towers_of_Harrenhal).

## Heraldry & Sigil

Five black towers on white, a double tressure red and black (Argent, five towers sable within a double-tressure, the outer gules and the inner of the second) (wiki:House_Towers_of_Harrenhal)

## Origins

After King Maegor I wiped out House Harroway in 44 AC, he decreed the strongest of his knights would have Harrenhal. Twenty-three knights fought in Lord Harroway's Town; Ser Walton Towers won but died within a fortnight from his wounds (wiki:House_Towers_of_Harrenhal). `FOUNDED` by Walton Towers (track_b: Founder).

## Narrative Arc

Walton's son Lord Jordan Towers supported King Maegor, and was among the last to see Maegor alive before his mysterious death on the Iron Throne (wiki:House_Towers_of_Harrenhal). The sickly nine-year-old Lord Maegor Towers was the last of the line, living at Harrenhal with the widowed Princess Rhaena Targaryen until his death in 61 AC. Jaehaerys I then granted Harrenhal to House Strong (wiki:House_Towers_of_Harrenhal).
