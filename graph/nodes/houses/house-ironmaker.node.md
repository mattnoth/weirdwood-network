## Identity

House Ironmaker is a noble house from the Iron Islands. Neither its arms nor its words are known (wiki:House_Ironmaker.intro).

## Allegiances

House Ironmaker is `SWORN_TO` House Greyjoy as their `OVERLORD_OF` overlord (track_b: Overlord). They are based in the `REGION_OF` Iron Islands (track_b: Region). The house is `RULES` headed by Erik Ironmaker (track_b: Head).

## Narrative Arc

Erik Ironmaker, the elderly head of the house, claims to have sailed with Lord Dagon Greyjoy (wiki:House_Ironmaker.cite_ref-Radwd26). Erik puts himself forth at the kingsmoot on Old Wyk, with his grandsons as champions and gifts of silver, bronze, and steel. When Asha Greyjoy demands he stand, he cannot, and his support crumbles. He later shouts support for Euron Greyjoy (wiki:House_Ironmaker.cite_ref-Raffc19). Euron arranges a marriage between Asha and Erik; since Asha fled Old Wyk, a seal stands in as proxy for the ceremony (wiki:House_Ironmaker.cite_ref-Radwd26). Erik serves as Lord Steward of the Iron Islands and castellan of Pyke while Euron wages war (wiki:House_Ironmaker.cite_ref-Radwd-appendix).
