## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Lord [Guncer Sunglass](wiki:Guncer_Sunglass) answers the summons of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) when he calls his banners against King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). Guncer is seen wearing moonstones at throat and wrist and finger by Maester [Cressen](wiki:Cressen) at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:House_Sunglass.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

After [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre)'s profaning of the sept of Dragonstone and the deaths and imprisonment of its defenders from house [Rambton](wiki:House_Rambton), pious Lord Sunglass declares to Stannis that he no longer supports his claim, which leads to his imprisonment.(wiki:House_Sunglass.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Three galleys belonging to House Sunglass—*[Piety](wiki:Piety)*, *[Prayer](wiki:Prayer)*, and *[Devotion](wiki:Devotion)*—are seized by Stannis for his [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet). They take part in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:House_Sunglass.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords

During the Battle of the Blackwater, Melisandre and Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) burn the prisoners in Dragonstone's dungeons, including Lord Guncer, as a sacrifice to [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor).(wiki:House_Sunglass.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) After Guncer is burned, [his brother](wiki:Lord_Sunglass), the new Lord of Sweetport Sound, flees to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis).(wiki:House_Sunglass.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

The gaoler of Dragonstone, [Lamprey](wiki:Lamprey), wears a soiled white doublet with the seven golden stars of House Sunglass embroidered on it.(wiki:House_Sunglass.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) It is unknown whether he took it from Lord Guncer or was a former servant of the house. One of the conspirators who helps Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) smuggle away [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm) is Ser [Triston of Tally Hill](wiki:Triston_of_Tally_Hill), who had been in service to Lord Guncer.(wiki:House_Sunglass.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
