## Origins

Ser [Owen Bush](wiki:Owen_Bush) was a [knight](wiki:Knight) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) in the reign of [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen). Ser Owen was found dead outside a brothel in [48 AC](wiki:48_AC), with his member cut off and stuffed in his mouth.
