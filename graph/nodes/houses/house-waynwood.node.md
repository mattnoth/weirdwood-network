## Identity

House Waynwood of Ironoaks is a noble house from Ironoaks in the Vale of Arryn. They are one of the great noble families sworn to House Arryn (wiki:House_Waynwood). The current head is Lady Anya Waynwood, with Ser Morton Waynwood as heir (track_b: Head, Heir).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Arryn (track_b: Overlord). `REGION_OF` Vale of Arryn (track_b: Region). `SEAT_OF` Ironoaks (track_b: Seat). `HOLDS_TITLE` Lord of Ironoaks Castle (track_b: Title). House Hardyng is a vassal of House Waynwood (wiki:House_Waynwood).

## Appearances & Description

Their sigil is a black broken wheel on green (wiki:House_Waynwood). Their words have not appeared in the books (wiki:House_Waynwood).

## Narrative Arc

Lady Anya Waynwood is one of the Lords Declarant who oppose Petyr Baelish's regency. She is the guardian and overlord of Harrold Hardyng, the heir to the Vale. House Waynwood is among the Vale houses present during Tyrion's imprisonment at the Eyrie and Catelyn's journey through the Vale (wiki:House_Waynwood) (agot-tyrion-05) (agot-catelyn-06) (agot-catelyn-07).
