## Identity

House Poole is a noble house from the North, sworn to House Stark (wiki:House_Poole). They blazon their arms as a blue plate on white, with a grey tressure (wiki:House_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd37).

## Narrative Arc

Vayon Poole served as steward of Winterfell. His daughter Jeyne was Sansa Stark's best friend (wiki:House_Poole.cite_ref-Ragot7). Both traveled south to King's Landing with Lord Eddard Stark (wiki:House_Poole.cite_ref-Ragot16). Vayon was killed during the fighting following Ned's arrest; Jeyne was given into Petyr Baelish's care (wiki:House_Poole.cite_ref-Ragot51). Trained by Baelish, Jeyne was given to the Boltons to impersonate Arya Stark as Ramsay Bolton's bride (wiki:House_Poole.cite_ref-Raffc17, wiki:House_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd37). Her presence at Winterfell rallied northern lords and mountain clans to Stannis Baratheon's cause (wiki:House_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd62). Theon Greyjoy ultimately freed Jeyne from Winterfell (wiki:House_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd51).
