## Origins

[Orbert Caswell](wiki:Orbert_Caswell) served as [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch).
During the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye) in [43 AC](wiki:43_AC), Lord Caswell joined Lords [Merryweather](wiki:House_Merryweather) and [Peake](wiki:House_Peake) in marching from the [Reach](wiki:Reach) to add their levies to King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen)'s host against Prince [Aegon the Uncrowned](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)). While Lords Merryweather and Caswell advanced against Aegon's host from the south, Maegor quickly killed his young nephew and the battle turned into a rout.(wiki:House_Caswell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
At the beginning of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), [Lord Caswell](wiki:Lord_Caswell_(Dance)) was among the nobles at court loyal to Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and imprisoned for his loyalties. Lord Caswell was brought before the [King's Justice](wiki:King's_Justice) and beheaded for refusing to bend the knee to King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen).(wiki:House_Caswell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) [Tom Flowers](wiki:Tom_Flowers), the [Bastard](wiki:Bastard) of [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge), was one of the commanders of the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) during the [Battle on the Honeywine](wiki:Battle_on_the_Honeywine). He was burned alive by the dragon [Tessarion](wiki:Tessarion) when Prince [Daeron the Daring](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)) joined the battle.(wiki:House_Caswell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Daeron began the [sack of Bitterbridge](wiki:Sack_of_Bitterbridge) after the death of Prince [Maelor Targaryen](wiki:Maelor_Targaryen). [Lady Caswell](wiki:Lady_Caswell), Lord Caswell's widow, hanged herself from her castle's gatehouse.(wiki:House_Caswell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
House Caswell fought for the [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen) during the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion). Ser [Kyle](wiki:Kyle) claimed to have saved the life of Lord [Armond Caswell](wiki:Armond_Caswell), Lord of Bitterbridge, during the rebellion. Armond's son, Lord [Joffrey Caswell](wiki:Joffrey_Caswell), faced Kyle in the [Whitewalls tourney](wiki:Whitewalls_tourney), unhorsing the knight in the first tilt.(wiki:House_Caswell.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
Ser [Cleyton Caswell](wiki:Cleyton_Caswell) was one of the suitors for Lady [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber)'s hand.(wiki:House_Caswell.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Old [Lord Caswell](wiki:Lord_Caswell)'s son, Ser [Lorent](wiki:Lorent_Caswell), took a gift longsword made by Bitterbridge's [armorer](wiki:Armorer) for his own son, [Rolly](wiki:Rolly_Duckfield). Lorent was wounded by Rolly in anger, after which Rolly fled in exile for the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company).(wiki:House_Caswell.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord [Lorent Caswell](wiki:Lorent_Caswell) is a wispy, young man. He follows his liege, Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), in declaring for King [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) on the outset of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). Renly's host encamps beneath the walls of [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge), which is where Lady [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Tully) meets him, finding his army engaging in tourney and feast. After Renly's death at [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)) and the fall of the [castle](wiki:Storm's_End) to [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), Lord Caswell shuts himself up within Bitterbridge.(wiki:House_Caswell.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

While searching for [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) encounters two guards at the gates of [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) wearing the livery of House Caswell. This indicates House Caswell followed their liegelords of [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) in declaring for King [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey).(wiki:House_Caswell.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
