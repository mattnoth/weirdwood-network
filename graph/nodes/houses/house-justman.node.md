## Origins

House Justman was founded after the [Andal invasion](wiki:Andal_invasion) by Benedict Rivers, a bastard from Houses [Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood) and [Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken). Over a period of thirty years Benedict managed to defeat the petty kings of the [Trident](wiki:Trident), after which he crowned himself. As he was a wise king who came to be known as Benedict the Just, his house became known as House Justman. King [Benedict I Justman](wiki:Benedict_I_Justman) extended his domains to [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) and [the Neck](wiki:The_Neck), while his son [Benedict II](wiki:Benedict_II_Justman) acquired [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale), [Rosby](wiki:Rosby), and the mouth of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush).(wiki:House_Justman.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
The Justmans ruled for almost three hundred years, but King [Bernarr II](wiki:Bernarr_II_Justman) was the last of the line. [Qhored Hoare](wiki:Qhored_Hoare), the [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), murdered Bernarr's sons in a keep of [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), known afterward as the [Bloody Keep](wiki:Bloody_Keep).(wiki:House_Justman.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Bernarr's campaign against the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) was hopeless, and the riverlands fell into anarchy for a century after the fall of the Justmans.(wiki:House_Justman.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
After the Storm King [Arlan III Durrandon](wiki:Arlan_III_Durrandon) conquered the riverlands, various pretenders unsuccessfully claimed to be river kings. One such was [Lucifer Justman](wiki:Lucifer_Justman), although his lineage with the Justmans is unconfirmed.(wiki:House_Justman.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
