## Identity

House Risley of Risley Glade is a noble house from the Reach, sworn to House Tyrell (wiki:House_Risley). Their seat is Risley Glade (track_b: Seat). They blazon their arms with a black knight on a rearing horse on white, bearing a golden lance and a white shield with the same in miniature (wiki:House_Risley).

## Allegiances

House Risley is `SWORN_TO` House Tyrell as overlord (track_b: Overlord). Located in the Reach (track_b: Region).

## Narrative Arc

Ser Victor Risley sided with the greens during the Dance of the Dragons and was a member of the Caltrops who conspired to assassinate the Two Betrayers. After the war, he served as King's Justice for Aegon III, raised by Lord Unwin Peake. Victor was found conspiring in an assassination attempt on Queen Daenaera Velaryon and was slain by Ser Gareth Long in trial by combat (wiki:House_Risley). Lord Risley attended the Whitewalls tourney in 212 AC; Ser Eden Risley had his leg crushed by his horse in the tourney (wiki:House_Risley).
