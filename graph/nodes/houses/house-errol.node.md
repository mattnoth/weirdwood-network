## Origins

During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Lords Errol, [Fell](wiki:House_Fell), and [Buckler](wiki:House_Buckler) [ambushed](wiki:Ambush_on_the_Wendwater) [Orys Baratheon](wiki:Orys_Baratheon)'s host as he crossed the [Wendwater](wiki:Wendwater). The lords later hid in the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood), but [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) set the forests ablaze with [Meraxes](wiki:Meraxes). Lord Errol was killed prior to the [Last Storm](wiki:Last_Storm).(wiki:House_Errol.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

House Errol is one of the houses from the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) that supports [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) when the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) starts, but they turn to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) after [Renly's death](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)). Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose) acknowledges the presence of the great lords [Estermont](wiki:House_Estermont), Errol and [Varner](wiki:House_Varner) during his parley with Stannis. It is unknown if this is Lady [Shyra Errol](wiki:Shyra_Errol) or her successor, Lord [Sebastion Errol](wiki:Sebastion_Errol).(wiki:House_Errol.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]
