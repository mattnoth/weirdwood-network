## Origins

### Origin

The Mormonts are an old house with a proud and honorable reputation.(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) According to [northern](wiki:North) histories, [Rodrik Stark](wiki:Rodrik_Stark), a [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), gave [Bear Island](wiki:Bear_Island) to the Mormonts after winning the isle in a wrestling match from a [driftwood king](wiki:Driftwood_king) of the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Skeptical scholars think it more likely the rival kings wrestled "with words".(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_north:_the_mountain_clans.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

The Mormonts' lands are cold and poor.(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Like the other women of Bear Island, the women of House Mormont learned how to defend themselves from ironmen and [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

House Mormont has possessed [Longclaw](wiki:Longclaw), a [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) sword, for five centuries.(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### House Targaryen

Lord [Torrhen Stark](wiki:Torrhen_Stark) sent a few longships from [Bear Island](wiki:Bear_Island) to aid King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) in his campaign against the Iron Islands.(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragons_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Lord [Alaric Stark](wiki:Alaric_Stark) was wed to a Mormont. According to Alaric, his [wife](wiki:Lady_Mormont_(wife_of_Alaric_Stark)) made a cloak from the skins of two wolves she killed when she was twelve years old.(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

Members of House Mormont were present at the [Tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) of [281 AC](wiki:281_AC).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Citadel_17-0)

### Jeor and Jorah

Lord [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) arranged a marriage for his son, [Jorah](wiki:Jorah_Mormont), to a girl from [House Glover](wiki:House_Glover) when Jorah was still relatively young. While the marriage lasted ten years, the couple had no children. Jorah's wife eventually died after not being able to recover from her third childbirth. By the time she died, Lord Jeor had joined the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch),(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) where he rose quickly through the ranks(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_jeor_mormont.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) and was elected [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) in [288 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_jeor_mormont.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1) It is likely that Jeor had joined the Night's Watch prior to [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) ([282 AC](wiki:282_AC)-[283 AC](wiki:283_AC)).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-nightswatch_19-0) When Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) called his banners, Jorah joined the rebels and fought in the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident) in [283 AC](wiki:283_AC).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

Jorah also fought in [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion) in [289 AC](wiki:289_AC), earning fame in the [siege of Pyke](wiki:Siege_of_Pyke) and [knighthood](wiki:Knight) from King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). He met and fell in love with [Lynesse Hightower](wiki:Lynesse_Hightower) at the [tourney at Lannisport](wiki:Tourney_at_Lannisport), held to celebrate Robert's victory over the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands). After winning the jousting, Jorah crowned Lynesse his [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty), and asked her father, Lord [Leyton Hightower](wiki:Leyton_Hightower), for her hand in marriage, which was granted. However, life on [Bear Island](wiki:Bear_Island) differed significantly from life at [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), and Jorah spend all of his money to keep Lynesse happy. Finally, in [293 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), after trying to sell poachers to a [Tyroshi](wiki:Tyrosh) [slaver](wiki:Slavery) to increase his income,(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) Jorah fled from the [north](wiki:North) to the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) with his wife upon learning that Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) was coming to [Bear Island](wiki:Bear_Island) to pass on the king's judgement.(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3) Jorah and Lynesse lived in exile in [Lys](wiki:Lys), with Jorah becoming a [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) after they ran out of money in half a year. However, while he was away fighting on the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne), Lynesse became the concubine of a merchant prince, [Tregar Ormollen](wiki:Tregar_Ormollen). Jorah was exiled from Lys when he returned to the Free City, continuing on to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-4)(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

[Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont), Jorah's aunt and Jeor's sister, inherited Bear Island after Jorah fled the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms). Her heir is her eldest daughter, [Dacey](wiki:Dacey_Mormont).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) Longclaw, left behind by Jorah, was returned to Jeor by Lady Maege.(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lady [Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont) answers [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s call to arms and she, her heir [Dacey](wiki:Dacey_Mormont), and their bannermen follow him south against the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Maege acts as part of Robb's war councils and Dacey rides with his [battle companions](wiki:Robb's_personal_guard) and guards, being the only woman of the group.(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1) Both Mormont women are present when Robb is named [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

[Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), meets [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) when they arrive at the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) Later he chooses Jon as his steward and acts as a mentor to the youth.(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) Jeor gives [Longclaw](wiki:Longclaw) to Jon in gratitude for the youth having saved the Lord Commander's life from [wights](wiki:Wights).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Racok60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

The exiled Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) attends Magister [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis)'s party in [Pentos](wiki:Pentos)(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) and enters the service of the also exiled [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) The knight gives Viserys's sister, [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), histories and songbooks from [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) as gifts for her wedding to Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-1) However, Jorah spies on the Targaryens for Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) and King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) After the deaths of Viserys and Robert, Jorah saves Daenerys from a wineseller's [poison](wiki:Poison) in [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) When [Drogo](wiki:Drogo)'s *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* disbands after the death of the *[khal](wiki:Khal)*, Jorah remains by Daenerys's side and is named as the first of her [Queensguard](wiki:Queensguard). Daenerys survives Drogo's funeral pyre and hatches three [dragons](wiki:Dragons) from the flame.(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Lady Maege and Dacey accompany Robb into the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands). After the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross), Maege captures thousands of livestock and drives them back to the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

Jeor leads the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) in the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Racok13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)

Jorah advises Daenerys to go to [Asshai](wiki:Asshai), but the girl instead brings her small *khalasar* to [Qarth](wiki:Qarth).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-5)(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)

### A Storm of Swords

In preparation for King Robb's plan to recover [Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin) from the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), Maege is sent by Robb on a [Mallister](wiki:House_Mallister) longship to the [Neck](wiki:Neck) to contact the [crannogmen](wiki:Crannogmen).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) Dacey and Robb are killed during the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)

After having survived the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist), Jeor is slain by [Ollo Lophand](wiki:Ollo_Lophand) during the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0) Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sends a raven to Bear Island in a plea for help to defend [Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) from [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0) Jon Snow, Jeor's one-time steward, is chosen as the new Lord Commander after the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)

Jorah is exiled from [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) by Queen Daenerys when she discovers from Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) that he had been spying on her for Varys.(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Alysane is the new heir of House Mormont after the death of her elder sister, [Dacey](wiki:Dacey_Mormont), at the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

When [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) demands the fealty of the northern lords, young [Lyanna Mormont](wiki:Lyanna_Mormont) sends Bear Island's denial.(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0) [Lyra](wiki:Lyra_Mormont) and [Jorelle Mormont](wiki:Jorelle_Mormont) are with their mother, Lady Maege,(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) whose location is unknown.

[Alysane Mormont](wiki:Alysane_Mormont) and men-at-arms of House Mormont launch an attack on the ironmen longships off the strand of [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte), managing to take them unawares by hiding fighters inside a gaggle of fishing sloops. She burns and captures all ships, securing several ironborn captains, knights, warriors, and other nobility for ransom.(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0)

After Stannis [retakes Deepwood](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte) and restores it to [House Glover](wiki:House_Glover), Alysane joins his army.(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2) She becomes one of the guards of [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) during Stannis's [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell), which is held by [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton_of_Winterfell).(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0)

Jorah takes Tyrion captive in [Selhorys](wiki:Selhorys)(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0) and travels with him to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay), where he hopes to present Tyrion to Daenerys in Meereen.(wiki:House_Mormont.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0)
