## Identity

House Osgrey of Standfast is a knightly house from the Reach. Their current seat is Standfast; they formerly held Coldmoat and the title Lord of Coldmoat. Before the Conquest, they were Marshalls of the Northmarch. The house has diminished greatly from its former glory. (wiki:House_Osgrey)

## Heraldry & Sigil

A lion, checkered green and gold, on white (Argent, a lion chequy vert and or) (wiki:House_Osgrey)

## Allegiances

House Osgrey is `SWORN_TO` House Rowan as their overlord, not directly to House Tyrell. (track_b: Overlord)

## Narrative Arc

House Osgrey features prominently in the Dunk and Egg novella "The Sworn Sword." Ser Eustace Osgrey, the aged knight of Standfast, is embroiled in a dispute with Lady Rohanne Webber of Coldmoat over the Chequy Water. The house once commanded vast lands and the title Marshall of the Northmarch but lost most of their holdings over generations of misfortune, failed rebellions (supporting the Blackfyre cause), and declining fortunes. (wiki:House_Osgrey)
