## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The Bettley colors are spotted by [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) among [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s army as it leaves [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:House_Bettley.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

One of the guardsmen at the gates of [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) wears the blue beetle of House Bettley when [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) arrives at the town.(wiki:House_Bettley.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
The banner of House Bettley is seen among those that escort the corpse of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) back to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock). Afterward, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) spots Ser [Jon Bettley](wiki:Jon_Bettley) riding at a [quintain](wiki:Quintain) in the yard of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:House_Bettley.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Beardless Jon is part of the force that marches north from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to end the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun). After the fall of Riverrun, Jon sets off with Ser [Lyle Crakehall](wiki:Lyle_Crakehall) to hunt outlaws near [Darry](wiki:Darry).(wiki:House_Bettley.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) and her companions see the corpses of the perpetrators of the [raid on Saltpans](wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans) hanged from trees. Among the dead is a man with a bettles badge sewn on his breast, presumably a Bettley man-at-arms who became a [broken man](wiki:Broken_men) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:House_Bettley.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
