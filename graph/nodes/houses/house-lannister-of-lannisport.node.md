## Origins

Lannisters from [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) developed [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) from a village into a town and then into a city. The Lannisters of Lannisport prospered off of trade, building walls to defend the city, while the Lannisters of Casterly Rock became the [Kings of the Rock](wiki:Kings_of_the_Rock).(wiki:House_Lannister_of_Lannisport.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Ser [Emory Hill](wiki:Emory_Hill), the Bastard of Lannisport, joined Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister)'s host during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), declaring for King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen). Ser Emory was among the notable casualties during the [Battle by the Lakeshore](wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore).(wiki:House_Lannister_of_Lannisport.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) During the absence of much of the westerland's strength, Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy) sacked Lannisport and took a Lannister of Lannisport as a [salt wife](wiki:Salt_wife).(wiki:House_Lannister_of_Lannisport.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
During the reign of King [Jaehaerys II Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_II_Targaryen), two Lannisters of Lannisport were captured by Lady [Ellyn Tarbeck](wiki:Ellyn_Tarbeck)(wiki:House_Lannister_of_Lannisport.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)—alongside their cousin [Stafford Lannister](wiki:Stafford_Lannister) and two sons of Lord [Prester](wiki:House_Prester)(wiki:House_Lannister_of_Lannisport.cite_ref-sample_2-1)—after Ser [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) arrested Lord [Walderan Tarbeck](wiki:Walderan_Tarbeck).(wiki:House_Lannister_of_Lannisport.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)(wiki:House_Lannister_of_Lannisport.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) The young nobles were said to have gathered near the woods of [Feastfires](wiki:Feastfires) with half a dozen common girls for the purpose of dalliances.(wiki:House_Lannister_of_Lannisport.cite_ref-sample_2-2) The hostages were later exchanged at [Castamere](wiki:Castamere) in an effort to deescalate conflict in the westerlands.(wiki:House_Lannister_of_Lannisport.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)(wiki:House_Lannister_of_Lannisport.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Smallfolk from Lannisport take part in the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:House_Lannister_of_Lannisport.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Rosamund Lannister](wiki:Rosamund_Lannister) was chosen by [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to accompany [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) due to their close resemblance. When Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) brings Princess Myrcella to [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell), he disguises Rosamund as Myrcella and tells everyone she has [redspots](wiki:Redspots).(wiki:House_Lannister_of_Lannisport.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
