## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The House of Zhak is hostile to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s rule over [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) after she [takes control](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen) of the city. Its household is suspected of being members of the [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy).(wiki:House_of_Zhak.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:House_of_Zhak.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) reports to Daenerys the Zhaks and [Merreqs](wiki:House_of_Merreq) wish to leave the city. Skahaz suggests they might go to their estates in the hills, but Daenerys is not convinced and thinks they will join the [Wise Masters](wiki:Wise_Masters) of [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) against her. She permits them to leave the city but their [gold](wiki:Gold) and [food](wiki:Food) supplies must remain in Meereen.(wiki:House_of_Zhak.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Before Meereen fell, a Zhak took the [maidenhead](wiki:Maiden) of his [slave](wiki:Slavery) bedwarmer and got her pregnant. The woman eventually marries a [freedmen](wiki:Freedmen) who pleads his case in front of Daenerys. He asks for the Zhak to be [gelded](wiki:Eunuch) for rape and to be given a purse of gold to raise the nobleman's [bastard](wiki:Bastard) as his own. As the woman was Zhak's property at the time, Daenerys judges against the gelding but grants the freedmen the gold.(wiki:House_of_Zhak.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Skahaz informs Daenerys that [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) was seen visiting the pyramid of Zhak.(wiki:House_of_Zhak.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) When several Meereenese ships join the blockade of Meereen, Skahaz presents Daenerys a list with the names of the Meereenese captains and which families they belong too, with the House of Zhak among the names.(wiki:House_of_Zhak.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
One man of Zhak told Daenerys that [palanquins](wiki:Palanquin) are popular because horses befoul the streets, but slaves do not.(wiki:House_of_Zhak.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
To ensure peace in Meereen, Daenerys has every noble family of Meereen who is of dubious loyalty send her a child as a hostage. These children are made [her cupbearers](wiki:Cupbearers_of_Daenerys_Targaryen).(wiki:House_of_Zhak.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) A Zhak child is among them.(wiki:House_of_Zhak.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
