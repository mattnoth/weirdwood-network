## Identity

House Tyrell of Highgarden is one of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms, Lords Paramount of the Mander and liege lords of the Reach (wiki:House_Tyrell). Their sigil is a golden rose on a green field, and their words are "Growing Strong" (wiki:House_Tyrell). Members tend to have curly brown hair and brown eyes (wiki:House_Tyrell). A large, wealthy house, its wealth is surpassed only by House Lannister, and the Tyrells can field the greatest armies in Westeros (wiki:House_Tyrell).

## Origins

Unlike most Great Houses, the Tyrells never ruled as kings (wiki:House_Tyrell). They trace descent through the female line to Garth the Gardener, mythical first King of the Reach, son of Garth Greenhand (wiki:House_Tyrell). The founder, Ser Alester Tyrell, was an Andal knight named champion and sworn shield of King Gwayne V Gardener (wiki:House_Tyrell). The Tyrells served as hereditary High Stewards to House Gardener (wiki:House_Tyrell). After King Mern IX Gardener died at the Field of Fire, Harlan Tyrell surrendered Highgarden to Aegon the Conqueror and was granted dominion of the Reach (wiki:House_Tyrell).

## Narrative Arc

During Robert's Rebellion, House Tyrell stayed loyal to Aerys II. Lord Mace Tyrell claimed credit for the Battle of Ashford, though Lord Randyll Tarly commanded the van (wiki:House_Tyrell). Mace besieged Storm's End for a year before bending the knee to Eddard Stark (wiki:House_Tyrell).

In AGOT, Loras Tyrell participates in the Hand's tourney. After Robert's death, Renly claims the throne and marries Margaery at Highgarden (wiki:House_Tyrell). In ACOK, Loras commands Renly's Rainbow Guard and Mace serves as Renly's Hand. After Renly's assassination, Petyr Baelish brokers an alliance with the Lannisters. The Tyrells join the Battle of the Blackwater, with Garlan wearing Renly's armor to terrify Stannis's forces (wiki:House_Tyrell). Mace gains a seat on the small council and Margaery is betrothed to Joffrey (wiki:House_Tyrell).

In ASOS, Margaery marries Joffrey, who dies at the wedding feast. Tyrion is accused of the murder (wiki:House_Tyrell). In AFFC, Cersei's growing paranoia leads her to antagonize the Tyrells. Margaery is arrested by the Faith (wiki:House_Tyrell). In ADWD, Lord Randyll Tarly receives custody of Margaery and is named justiciar (wiki:House_Tyrell).
