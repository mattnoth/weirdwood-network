## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The House is supposedly less hostile to the rule of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen). [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq), known as the Shavepate, serves as one of her Meereenese advisors. (wiki:House_of_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) The House before Daenerys came was considered a lesser House and its influence has grown due to their service to Daenerys. Many of the House of Kandaq are now [Shavepates](wiki:Shavepates) and serve in the [Brazen Beasts](wiki:Brazen_Beasts) alongside the [Freedmen](wiki:Freedmen). The house are considered traitors by the [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy).
