## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), allows [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) to pass through the [Wall](wiki:Wall) if they swear to help the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) defend against the [Others](wiki:Others). One of the free folk leaders who agrees is [Gerrick Kingsblood](wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood), a raider who boasts descent from [Raymun Redbeard](wiki:Raymun_Redbeard), a [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall) who led an invasion of the North in the time of Jon's grandfather's grandfather.(wiki:House_Redbeard.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) However, [Tormund](wiki:Tormund) claims Gerrick actually descends from Raymun's younger brother, known among the free folk as the [Red Raven](wiki:Red_Raven_(free_folk)), for having fled the [Battle of Long Lake](wiki:Battle_of_Long_Lake) in which Raymun and all his sons were killed.(wiki:House_Redbeard.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Ser [Axell Florent](wiki:Axell_Florent), the [Hand](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) of Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent), introduces Gerrick as "Gerrick of House Redbeard, King of the Wildlings". Selyse arranges the betrothals of Gerrick's three daughters to Axell and her knights Ser [Brus Buckler](wiki:Brus_Buckler) and Ser [Malegorn of Redpool](wiki:Malegorn_of_Redpool), with the marriages to be in the name of the [Lord of Light](wiki:R'hllor).(wiki:House_Redbeard.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Gerrick's son joins the youths yielded to the Night's Watch as hostages.(wiki:House_Redbeard.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
