## Origins

Lord [Lorent Grandison](wiki:Lorent_Grandison) served in the [regency of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III); [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom) observed that he loved to sleep.(wiki:House_Grandison.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Ser [Harlan Grandison](wiki:Harlan_Grandison) was a [knight](wiki:Knight) in the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) of [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen). After dying in his sleep, Harlan was replaced in the White Swords by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:House_Grandison.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Along with [Lord Cafferen](wiki:Lord_Cafferen), [Lord Grandison](wiki:Lord_Grandison) was captured by Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) at the [battles at Summerhall](wiki:Battles_at_Summerhall) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). Robert then managed to turn the two [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) loyalists into allies. Lord Grandison was wounded at the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident) fighting for Robert, and died of his wounds a year later.(wiki:House_Grandison.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
The elderly Lord [Hugh Grandison](wiki:Hugh_Grandison) was among the possible matches offered to Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell). When he traveled to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), however, he fell asleep between courses at the welcoming feast.(wiki:House_Grandison.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
