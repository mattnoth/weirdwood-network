## Identity

House Longwaters is a noble house from the crownlands. They claim descent from a bastard of House Velaryon who had Targaryen blood — the famed "drop of dragon" lineage (wiki:House_Longwaters).

## Narrative Arc

Rennifer Longwaters serves as chief undergaoler of the Red Keep's dungeons. He claims descent from a Princess Elaena Targaryen through her illegitimate son by Alyn Velaryon — giving the family their famous "drop of dragon" blood. Rennifer is proud of this lineage and mentions it to Jaime Lannister (wiki:House_Longwaters.cite_ref-Raffc8, wiki:House_Longwaters.cite_ref-Raffc27).
