## Identity

House Qoherys of Harrenhal is an extinct noble house from the riverlands, of Valyrian descent (wiki:House_Qoherys). Their arms are a flaming saltire, red and yellow, between four white skulls, on black (wiki:House_Qoherys).

## Heraldry & Sigil

A flaming saltire, red and yellow, between 4 white skulls, on black (Sable, a saltire rayonné gules and or, between four skulls argent) (wiki:House_Qoherys)

## Origins

Ser Quenton Qoherys, the master-at-arms of Dragonstone, was granted Harrenhal by King Aegon I Targaryen after House Hoare was extinguished in the burning of Harrenhal during Aegon's Conquest (wiki:House_Qoherys). `FOUNDED` by Quenton Qoherys (track_b: Founder).

## Narrative Arc

Quenton was succeeded by his grandson, Lord Gargon "the Guest," who was murdered in the rebellion of Harren the Red during King Aenys I's reign. The house went extinct and Harrenhal passed to House Harroway (wiki:House_Qoherys). Quenton's second wife was a daughter of Lord Edmyn Tully, connecting the two houses (wiki:House_Qoherys).
