## Narrative Arc

The house's only notable historical member is Jack Musgood, known as "Jolly Jack" or "Sleepy Jack," who served as Lord Commander of the Night's Watch. He was considered one of the worst Lord Commanders in the Watch's history, as he was fat, slow, and lazy. During his command, wildlings raided south of the Wall frequently and even sacked Bear Island. After a wildling raid killed the Lord of Last Hearth's sons, the Starks intervened and forced Jack to resign. (wiki:House_Musgood)
