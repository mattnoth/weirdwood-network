## Narrative Arc

Lord Lothar Mallery accompanied Beric Dondarrion on the mission to bring Gregor Clegane to justice. Lothar drowned at the Mummer's Ford when Clegane ambushed the party (wiki:House_Mallery.cite_ref-Ragot43). Historically, Ser Raymund Mallery served in King Maegor I Targaryen's Kingsguard; after Maegor's death he refused the black and demanded trial by combat (wiki:House_Mallery).
