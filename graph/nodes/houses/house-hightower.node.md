## Identity

House Hightower is one of the most powerful and ancient noble houses of the Reach, seated at the Hightower in Oldtown. Lord Leyton Hightower is the current head, with Baelor Hightower as heir. The house holds numerous titles including Lord of the Hightower, Lord of the Port, Lord of Oldtown, Voice of Oldtown, Defender of Oldtown, Defender of the Citadel, and Beacon of the South. Historically they held the title King of the High Tower. (wiki:House_Hightower)

## Heraldry & Sigil

Words: "We Light the Way" (wiki:House_Hightower)

## Origins

House Hightower was founded by Uthor of the High Tower. (track_b: Founder) The house is among the oldest in the Reach with deep ties to Oldtown and the Citadel.

## Allegiances

House Hightower is `SWORN_TO` House Tyrell as their overlord. (track_b: Overlord) The house has historically wielded immense influence due to its wealth and control of Oldtown, seat of the Citadel and the Faith.

## Appearances & Description

The ancestral Valyrian steel sword Vigilance belongs to House Hightower. (track_b: Ancestral weapon) The Hightower itself is one of the tallest structures in Westeros, a massive lighthouse overlooking the harbor of Oldtown.

## Narrative Arc

House Hightower appears across the series. In AGOT, the house is referenced through historical Kingsguard member Ser Gerold Hightower, the White Bull. (agot-bran-02) The Hightowers are mentioned among the great houses in multiple AGOT chapters. (agot-eddard-10, agot-sansa-05) Across ACOK and ASOS, the house features through its various members and marriage alliances. In AFFC, Oldtown and the Hightower become increasingly prominent with Sam's arrival at the Citadel. Lord Leyton has not descended from the Hightower in over a decade, consulting with his daughter the Mad Maid. (wiki:House_Hightower)
