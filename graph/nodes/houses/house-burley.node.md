## Origins

[Lothor Burley](wiki:Lothor_Burley) was [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) when Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) visited the [north](wiki:North) and the [Wall](wiki:Wall) in [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:House_Burley.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
[Billy Burley](wiki:Billy_Burley) was the best bowman of the [river lord](wiki:Riverlands) [Benjicot Blackwood](wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons);(wiki:House_Burley.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) it is unknown if he was connected with House Burley.

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

While going to the [Twins](wiki:Twins) to attend Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully)'s [wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), the Burleys are caught on the wrong side of the [Trident](wiki:Trident). They suffer heavy losses when attacked by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) in the [fighting at the fords of the Trident](wiki:Fighting_at_the_fords_of_the_Trident).(wiki:House_Burley.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
