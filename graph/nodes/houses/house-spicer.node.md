## Identity

House Spicer is a noble house from the westerlands, recently granted the seat of Castamere (formerly of the extinct House Reyne). They are sworn to House Lannister. (wiki:House_Spicer)

## Origins

House Spicer was founded by Rolph Spicer. The family rose from humble origins through the spice trade and later through marriage alliances. Sybell Spicer (nee Westerling's grandmother was Maggy the Frog, a fortune-teller from Lannisport. (wiki:House_Spicer)

## Narrative Arc

The Spicers are entwined with the Westerling marriage to Robb Stark. Sybell Spicer (married into House Westerling) conspired with Tywin Lannister regarding her daughter Jeyne's marriage to Robb. After the Red Wedding and the fall of House Stark's cause, Rolph Spicer was rewarded with Castamere for the family's role. The house appears extensively in ASOS and AFFC court intrigues.
