## Identity

House Paege is a noble house of landed knights sworn to Riverrun in the riverlands (wiki:House_Paege). They blazon their shields with twining red-and-white snakes on black (wiki:House_Paege).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Tully as overlord during AGOT (track_b: Overlords). `SWORN_TO` House Baelish by ADWD (track_b: Overlords). `REGION_OF` Riverlands (track_b: Region).

## Narrative Arc

In ACOK, Big Walder Frey quarters the Paege arms from his mother Sallei Paege. Hoster Frey serves as squire to Ser Damon Paege (wiki:House_Paege). Paege banners are spotted among the river lords' camps. In ASOS, Ser Robert Paege is a companion of Edmure Tully (wiki:House_Paege). In AFFC, Garrett Paege serves as squire to Jaime Lannister and accompanies him to the siege of Riverrun. Despite renewing fealty to the Iron Throne, the Paege banner is absent from the besieging force (wiki:House_Paege).
