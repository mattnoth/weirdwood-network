## Identity

House Dayne of Starfall is one of the most ancient houses in the Seven Kingdoms, dating back ten thousand years to the dawn of days. They are seated at Starfall, on an island at the mouth of the Torrentine in western Dorne (wiki:House_Dayne). They have a cadet branch, House Dayne of High Hermitage (track_b: relationship not in track_b but confirmed by wiki).

## Origins

The first Dayne is said to have raised Starfall after tracking a falling star and finding a stone of magical powers. His descendants became Kings of the Torrentine and Lords of Starfall (wiki:House_Dayne). The Daynes are of supposed First Men ancestry, and along with the Fowlers and Yronwoods were among the most powerful houses of ancient Dorne (wiki:House_Dayne). The Daynes are famed for Dawn, their ancestral sword made from the fallen star, and the men who wield it, the Swords of the Morning (wiki:House_Dayne).

## Appearances & Description

Their blazon is a white sword and falling star crossed on lilac (wiki:House_Dayne). The Daynes are "stony" Dornishmen from the western Red Mountains. Their hair ranges from pale blond (Edric) to dark (Ashara) to silver with a streak of black (Gerold). Daynes have been observed with dark blue (Edric) or purple eyes (Ashara and Gerold); their purple eyes do not necessarily indicate Valyrian descent (wiki:House_Dayne).

## Narrative Arc

The last Dayne King, Vorian "the Sword of the Evening," was defeated in Nymeria's War and sent to the Wall; the Daynes then supported Nymeria, and her third husband was Ser Davos Dayne, a Sword of the Morning (wiki:House_Dayne). King Samwell Dayne, "the Starfire," once burned Oldtown (wiki:House_Dayne). Ser Arthur Dayne, a legendary Kingsguard knight and Sword of the Morning, was killed at the Tower of Joy by Lord Eddard Stark. His sister Lady Ashara Dayne reportedly threw herself from the Palestone Sword tower at Starfall (agot-eddard-10, agot-catelyn-02). Lord Edric Dayne accompanied Beric Dondarrion in the brotherhood without banners; after Lady Stoneheart took command, Edric and others departed (wiki:House_Dayne). Ser Gerold Dayne "Darkstar" of the cadet branch wounded Princess Myrcella during Arianne Martell's failed plot and is now hunted (wiki:House_Dayne).
