## Origins

During the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising), [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) used [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) to set [aflame](wiki:Dragonflame) in a single night the seats of Houses Deddings, [Blanetree](wiki:House_Blanetree), [Lychester](wiki:Castle_Lychester), [Terrick](wiki:House_Terrick), and [Wayn](wiki:House_Wayn).(wiki:House_Deddings.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), [Davos Deddings](wiki:Davos_Deddings) was stabbed in the eye while trying to protect his sister, Lady [Darla](wiki:Darla_Deddings), from rapers in the [riots of King's landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)).(wiki:House_Deddings.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Lords [Stanton Piper](wiki:Stanton_Piper) and [Lyonel Deddings](wiki:Lyonel_Deddings) were burned by [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor) during the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton).(wiki:House_Deddings.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
