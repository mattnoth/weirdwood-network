## Identity

House Lorch is a house of landed knights from the westerlands. They blazon their shields with a black manticore on white, beneath a crimson chief with three gold coins. Their motto and seat have not been revealed. (wiki:House_Lorch.cite_ref-Racok14; wiki:House_Lorch.cite_ref-Raffcappendix)

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Lannister (track_b: Overlord). Located in the Westerlands (track_b: Region). Current head is Ser Lorent Lorch (track_b: Head).

## Narrative Arc

During the Sack of King's Landing, Ser Amory Lorch stabbed Princess Rhaenys Targaryen to death (wiki:House_Lorch.cite_ref-Ragot12). In ACOK, Amory attacked the holdfast where Yoren sheltered Night's Watch recruits (wiki:House_Lorch.cite_ref-Racok14). Left as castellan of Harrenhal by Tywin, Amory was thrown into the bear pit after the Brave Companions betrayed the garrison (wiki:House_Lorch.cite_ref-Racok47; wiki:House_Lorch.cite_ref-Rasos53). During the Faith Militant uprising, Ser Lyonel Lorch fought at the Battle at the Great Fork against Maegor I Targaryen (wiki:House_Lorch.cite_ref-Rfab). After Amory's death, Ser Lorent Lorch became head of the house (wiki:House_Lorch.cite_ref-Raffcappendix).
