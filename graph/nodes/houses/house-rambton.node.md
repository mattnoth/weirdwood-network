## Identity

House Rambton is a noble house from the crownlands. Their arms are a white ram's head with golden horns on red (*Gules, a ram's head cabossed argent attired or*) (wiki:House_Rambton).

## Appearances & Description

Sigil: A white ram's head with golden horns on red (wiki:House_Rambton.cite_ref-Ragot30).

## Narrative Arc

The arms of House Rambton are spotted by Lord Eddard Stark during the Hand's tourney (wiki:House_Rambton.cite_ref-Ragot30). Ser Hubard Rambton led his three sons to defend the sept at Dragonstone when the queen's men came to burn it for R'hllor. Hubard and one son were slain in the brawl; his two surviving sons were later burned alive as sacrifices to R'hllor by Queen Selyse Florent (wiki:House_Rambton.cite_ref-Racok10, wiki:House_Rambton.cite_ref-Rasos25).
