## Origins

### Kingdom of the Reach[ ]

According to legend, Houses Florent, [Ball](wiki:House_Ball), and [Peake](wiki:House_Peake) trace their descent from the sons of [Florys the Fox](wiki:Florys_the_Fox)'s three husbands, each kept ignorant of the existence of the others. Florys was a daughter of [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand) from the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes), the cleverest of his children.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
As vassals of [House Gardener](wiki:House_Gardener), the Florents married into the royal family numerous times.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) Through the centuries of the [Kingdom of the Reach](wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Reach), the Peakes and [Osgreys](wiki:House_Osgrey) boasted about marrying their daughters to Florents.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_.E2.80.93_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### Targaryen dynasty[ ]

During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), House Florent followed their liege, King [Mern IX Gardener](wiki:Mern_IX_Gardener), in taking up arms against [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) during the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire). After the battle, when King Mern and his sons burned and the Gardeners' line ended, Aegon named the [Tyrells](wiki:House_Tyrell) as [Lord Paramount of the Reach](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Reach), despite the Florents possessing a superior claim by their blood ties to the [Kings of the Reach](wiki:Kings_of_the_Reach).(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2)(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_house_tyrell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) House Florent was among the great houses of the [Reach](wiki:Reach) who bitterly complained about being made vassals of the Tyrells and insisted that their blood was nobler than that of the "upjumped stewards". Their protests were denied by King Aegon I, perhaps because the Florents had fought [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) when the Tyrells did not.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_house_tyrell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1)
When Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake) sought to make his daughter [Myrielle](wiki:Myrielle_Peake) the queen to King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) after the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lady [Samantha Hightower](wiki:Samantha_Hightower) nominated nubile maidens from House Florent, among other nobles houses, as potential queen consorts for the king.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_.E2.80.93_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)
In [206 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), during the reign of [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen), Ser [Arlan](wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree) and [Dunk](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) were in service to [Lord Florent](wiki:Lord_Florent_(Daeron_II)), who was blind. The Florent arms were spotted by Ser Duncan in the assembled heraldry at the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) three years later.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Ser [Aladore Florent](wiki:Aladore_Florent), heir to Brightwater Keep, and Ser [Jon Florent](wiki:Jon_Florent) were listed on the rolls.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rthkgraphic_novel.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### Baratheon dynasty[ ]

[Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) wed [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), [Lord of Dragonstone](wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone) and the brother of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). Her cousin, [Delena Florent](wiki:Delena_Florent), was despoiled by the king during the wedding feast.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) offered his [dwarf](wiki:Dwarf) son, [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), for marriage to Delena, but her father, Ser [Colin Florent](wiki:Colin_Florent), instead married her to one of his knights, [Hosman Norcross](wiki:Hosman_Norcross).(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) Delena gave birth to [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm).(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) Ser [Axell Florent](wiki:Axell_Florent), the brother of Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent) of [Brightwater Keep](wiki:Brightwater_Keep), serves Stannis as [castellan](wiki:Castellan) of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre), a [red priestess](wiki:Red_priest), has joined the court of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), [Lord of Dragonstone](wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone), and his wife, Lady [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent), fervently takes up the worship of [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor), the Lord of Light.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) Selyse becomes Stannis's queen after he announces his claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-3)
As the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) breaks out, House Florent follows [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) in supporting the late King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s youngest brother, [Renly](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End), instead of Stannis. The Florents field roughly two thousand men.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Racok0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
Upon Renly's death at [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), the Florents are the first and the most powerful of the [Reach](wiki:Reach) houses who defect to Stannis.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) Selyse's uncle, Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent), begins following R'hllor.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Alester's nephew, Ser [Erren](wiki:Erren_Florent), is one of the knights Stannis sends to recruit Renly's infantry at [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge), but they do not return.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) Alester's son-in-law, Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly), puts many Florent men-at-arms to death when he [seizes Renly's stores](wiki:Fighting_at_Bitterbridge) to ensure they do not go over to Stannis's side.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
Lord Alester is with Stannis during the parley with Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose), who refuses to yield Storm's End or his ward, [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm), the bastard son of [Delena Florent](wiki:Delena_Florent) and King Robert.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3) Edric and Melisandre are sent to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) after Cortnay's death.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)
Selyse's brother, Ser [Imry Florent](wiki:Imry_Florent), is given command of Stannis's [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet) in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). He relies on the size of his fleet and does not send scouts ahead. Almost his entire force is trapped in the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) by [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s defensive chain and destroyed by [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire); Imry dies on [Fury](wiki:Fury) along with most of his men.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-1) Despite the spirited defense of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Stannis's army seems within sight of victory. However, the Florents' rivals, the [Tyrells](wiki:House_Tyrell), arrive with Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) from south of the Blackwater and rout Stannis's men.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) A Florent [bastard](wiki:Bastard) who refuses to submit to Joffrey is sent off to be executed by Ser [Ilyn Payne](wiki:Ilyn_Payne).(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent) loses his possessions when the Lannisters overrun his camp at the Blackwater, escaping only with the mail on his back and the rings on his fingers.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) Most of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s men rescued from the shoreline by the [Lysene](wiki:Lys) fleet of [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan) wear the badge of [Brightwater Keep](wiki:Brightwater_Keep).(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-4) More than half of Stannis's men on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)) are Florents,(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) and Alester becomes his [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-5) Alester's brother, Ser [Axell Florent](wiki:Axell_Florent), arrests Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) when the Onion Knight intends to murder [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre).(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)
The Florents still dispute [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell)'s ownership of [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden).(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0) Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), Hand of the King to [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), dispossesses the Florents of their holdings and awards them to Ser [Garlan Tyrell](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell), the second son of [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), [Lord Paramount of the Reach](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Reach), as a reward for loyal service, forming [House Tyrell of Brightwater Keep](wiki:House_Tyrell_of_Brightwater_Keep).(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1) Ser [Erren Florent](wiki:Erren_Florent) is held captive at Highgarden.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-3)
When Alester plots to wed Stannis's daughter, Princess [Shireen Baratheon](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon), to Joffrey's brother, Prince [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), in an effort to make peace without Stannis's knowledge, he is thrown in the dungeons with Davos.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-1) Axell counsels Stannis to put [Claw Isle](wiki:Claw_Isle) to the torch in retaliation for Lord [Ardrian Celtigar](wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar) bending the knee, but Stannis dismisses this idea, agreeing with Davos that it is an evil notion, and Davos becomes Alester's successor as King's Hand.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2) Without Stannis's permission, Davos arranges for [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm) to be sent to [Lys](wiki:Lys) to avoid being burned alive by Melisandre.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)
During the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) spots a ring of flowers amongst the banners when Stannis's host attacks the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) camp,(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0) presumably the arms of House Florent or of [House Meadows](wiki:House_Meadows). The banners of the Florents are later spotted flying at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) by [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly).(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)

The new head of the house, Alester's son [Alekyne](wiki:Alekyne_Florent), has taken refuge in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), where his sister [Rhea](wiki:Rhea_Florent) is the wife of [Leyton Hightower](wiki:Leyton_Hightower), [Lord of the Hightower](wiki:Lord_of_the_Hightower).(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) recalls that when [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) sailed for the [Wall](wiki:Wall), [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) burned Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent) alive to obtain favorable winds for the voyage.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0) Once Stannis arrived in the [north](wiki:North), he left Ser [Axell Florent](wiki:Axell_Florent) with Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) and some fellow [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) at [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea).(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0) Axell begins calling himself the Queen's Hand.(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0)
Selyse and Axell stop at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) on their way to the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort). Axell hopes to meet the beautiful [Val](wiki:Val).(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) The queen arranges marriages between her men and the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk), betrothing Axell to the [eldest daughter](wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood's_eldest_daughter) of [Gerrick Kingsblood](wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood).(wiki:House_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0)
