## Origins

During his reign, King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) visited the Blackbars at Bandallon in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC),(wiki:House_Blackbar.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and again in [89 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). During his second visit at Bandallon, the king's [estrangement](wiki:First_Quarrel) with Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) had been noticed. Lord Blackbar's youngest daughter boldy tried to seduce the king by seating herself in his lap and attempting he feed him a grape, but Jaehaerys rebuffed her.(wiki:House_Blackbar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Ser [Elwood Blackbar](wiki:Elwood_Blackbar) was among the [knights](wiki:Knight) that participated at the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC).(wiki:House_Blackbar.cite_ref-Rthk_.5B.5Bthe_hedge_knight_.28comics.29.7Cgraphic_novel.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
