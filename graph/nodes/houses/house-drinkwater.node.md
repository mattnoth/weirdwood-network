## Narrative Arc

Ser Gerris Drinkwater accompanies Prince Quentyn Martell on his mission to Meereen to court Daenerys Targaryen. Together with Ser Archibald Yronwood, they join the Windblown sellsword company and participate in the siege of Astapor before reaching Meereen. After Quentyn's death attempting to tame a dragon, Gerris and Archibald are imprisoned and later sent as envoys to the Tattered Prince by Ser Barristan Selmy (wiki:House_Yronwood).
