## Narrative Arc

Lady Tanda Stokeworth and her daughters — the sharp Falyse and the simple Lollys — are frequent presences at King's Landing court. Lollys was assaulted and impregnated during the Riot of King's Landing. She later married Bronn, who effectively took control of Castle Stokeworth. After Lady Tanda's death, Lollys became the head of house, with Bronn acting as her protector and the de facto lord. Falyse attempted to have Bronn killed but failed and was sent to Qyburn by Cersei. Bronn named Lollys's bastard son "Tyrion" as a deliberate provocation (wiki:House_Stokeworth). Referenced extensively across all five books (cite_refs in 15 chapters across AGOT-ADWD).
