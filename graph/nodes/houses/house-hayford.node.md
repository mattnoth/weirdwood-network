## Identity

House Hayford of Hayford is a noble house from Hayford in the crownlands, less than a day's ride from King's Landing. It is sworn directly to the Iron Throne (wiki:House_Hayford). Their blazon is green fretty over gold, a green pale wavy (wiki:House_Hayford.cite_ref-Rtss).

## Appearances & Description

Sigil: Green fretty over gold, a green pale wavy (*Or fretty vert, a pale wavy of the second*) (wiki:House_Hayford). Title: Lord of Hayford (track_b: Title).

## Allegiances

House Hayford is `SWORN_TO` House Baratheon of King's Landing as their overlord (track_b: Overlord). They are located in the `REGION_OF` Crownlands (track_b: Region). Their seat is Hayford (track_b: Seat). Head of house is Lady Ermesande Hayford (track_b: Head). House Hogg of Sow's Horn is their vassal (wiki:House_Hayford.cite_ref-Raffc27).

## Narrative Arc

House Hayford has a long history of loyalty to the Targaryens. A Lord Hayford was beheaded by Maegor the Cruel after urging abdication. Another Lord Hayford was executed during the Dance of the Dragons for refusing to renounce Rhaenyra Targaryen. A Lord Hayford served as Hand of the King to Daeron II and was killed by Lord Gormon Peake at the Battle of the Redgrass Field (wiki:House_Hayford.cite_ref-Rtss).

Lady Ermesande Hayford, a baby and the last of her line, was married to Tyrek Lannister so the Lannisters could claim her lands (wiki:House_Hayford.cite_ref-Racok25). Tyrek disappeared in the Riot of King's Landing (wiki:House_Hayford.cite_ref-Racok41). Ser Jaime Lannister later camped at Hayford and found the War of the Five Kings had largely spared the Hayford lands (wiki:House_Hayford.cite_ref-Raffc27).
