## Origins

House Tuttle owned a pig farm. As the eldest son, Duncan Tuttle was expected to inherit the farm, but while visiting [Ironrath](wiki:Ironrath) one day, he struck an unexpected friendship with [Gregor Forrester](wiki:Gregor_Forrester), heir to [House Forrester](wiki:House_Forrester_(Telltale)). The two became close friends, and when Gregor inherited his family's seat, Duncan was named [castellan](wiki:Castellan).
