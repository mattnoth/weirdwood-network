## Origins

[Martyn Cassel](wiki:Martyn_Cassel) was [killed](wiki:Raid_on_the_Tower_of_Joy) at the [Tower of Joy](wiki:Tower_of_Joy) in the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) during a battle with three members of [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen)'s [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) buried him there in a cairn.(wiki:House_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Martyn fathered four sons, yet only [Jory](wiki:Jory_Cassel) lived to be a man. Jory was among those who attended the wedding of Eddard and [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Stark). In his attempt to remove the dress from Catelyn, he tore it.(wiki:House_Cassel.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Martyn's brother, Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel), was married three times and had only daughters. His only surviving child is [Beth](wiki:Beth_Cassel).(wiki:House_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
