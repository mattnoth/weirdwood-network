## Narrative Arc

Ser Alliser Thorne is the most prominent member, serving as master-at-arms at Castle Black. He fought for the Targaryens during Robert's Rebellion and was sent to the Wall after the Sack of King's Landing (wiki:House_Thorne.cite_ref-Ragot21). He is Jon Snow's antagonist at the Wall — cruel to recruits, later a rival in the Lord Commander election, and eventually sent ranging beyond the Wall by Jon. Before departing, he warns Jon that if the Others raise him as a wight, he'll come back for Jon (wiki:House_Thorne.cite_ref-Radwd28).

Historically, Ser Rickard Thorne served in the Kingsguard during the Dance of the Dragons and was slain by a mob at Bitterbridge while protecting Prince Maelor (wiki:House_Thorne.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons). Ser Adrian Thorne commanded the City Watch during the regency of Aegon III (wiki:House_Thorne.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring).
