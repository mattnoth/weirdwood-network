## Identity

House Lantell is a minor noble house from the westerlands, said to be distant kin to House Lannister of Casterly Rock. Like other Lannister kin such as houses Lanny and Lannett, they are settled at Lannisport. (wiki:House_Lantell)

## Origins

During the Age of Heroes, House Lannister grew so numerous that Casterly Rock could not contain all of them. Members of lesser branches left to settle in a village nearby, which grew into Lannisport. House Lantell descends from these lesser branches. (wiki:House_Lantell.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands)
