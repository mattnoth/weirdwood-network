## Identity

House Rhysling is a noble house from the Reach (wiki:House_Rhysling). They blazon their arms with an oaken door banded with iron, in a grey stone doorway, on black masonry (wiki:House_Rhysling).

## Heraldry & Sigil

An oaken door banded with iron, in a grey stone doorway, on black masonry (Sable masoned proper, an door brunâtre banded cendrée within a stone doorway of the last) (wiki:House_Rhysling)

## Allegiances

House Rhysling is `SWORN_TO` House Tyrell as overlord (track_b: Overlord). Located in the Reach (track_b: Region).

## Narrative Arc

Ser Robyn Rhysling, a one-eyed knight, participated in the tourney at Ashford Meadow in 209 AC. He was defeated by Lord Leo Tyrell after four lances. During the trial of seven, Robyn fought for Ser Duncan the Tall, unhorsing Prince Daeron Targaryen (wiki:House_Rhysling).
