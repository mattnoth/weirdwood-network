## Identity

House Webber of Coldmoat is a noble house from Coldmoat in the Reach, sworn to House Rowan (wiki:House_Webber). Their blazon is a white, spotted-red spider on a silver web over black (wiki:House_Webber). Their castle Coldmoat can support twenty times as many smallfolk as Standfast (wiki:House_Webber). Webber lands include the Horseshoe Hills, Leafy Lake, and the Chequy Water, with fields of barley, wheat, and corn, six orchards, and horse breeding (wiki:House_Webber).

## Heraldry & Sigil

A white and spotted-red spider upon a silver web over black (Sable, a cobweb silver thereon a spider argent spotted gules) (wiki:House_Webber)

## Origins

Coldmoat was once the greatest of the four castles of House Osgrey, taken from them during Maegor I's reign because Lord Ormond Osgrey opposed the king's laws (wiki:House_Webber). House Webber became Lords of Coldmoat sometime thereafter (wiki:House_Webber).

## Allegiances

House Webber is `SWORN_TO` House Rowan as overlord (track_b: Overlord). Their seat is Coldmoat (track_b: Seat). Their lands border those of House Osgrey and House Stackhouse (wiki:House_Webber).

## Narrative Arc

The house features prominently in the Dunk and Egg novella The Sworn Sword. Lord Wyman Webber's daughter Rohanne became sole heir after his sons died in infancy. She was married five times; her fourth husband was Ser Rolland Uffering, and she eventually wed Ser Eustace Osgrey's rival neighbor (wiki:House_Webber). The animosity between House Webber and House Osgrey stemmed from Wyman's rejection of a marriage between Rohanne and Eustace's son Addam, and from the houses supporting opposite sides in the First Blackfyre Rebellion (wiki:House_Webber). In the main series, House Webber has no named members appearing.
