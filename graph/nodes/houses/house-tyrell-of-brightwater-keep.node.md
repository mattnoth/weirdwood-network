## Identity

House Tyrell of Brightwater Keep is a new noble house in the Reach and a cadet branch of House Tyrell of Highgarden, created in 299 AC (wiki:House_Tyrell_of_Brightwater_Keep). Their sigil is two golden roses on green (wiki:House_Tyrell_of_Brightwater_Keep).

## Heraldry & Sigil

Two golden roses on green (Vert, two roses or) (wiki:House_Tyrell_of_Brightwater_Keep)

## Origins

Created after House Florent was stripped of lands and titles for rebelling against the Iron Throne. Brightwater Keep was bestowed on Ser Garlan Tyrell for his participation in the Battle of the Blackwater (wiki:House_Tyrell_of_Brightwater_Keep).

## Allegiances

House Tyrell of Brightwater Keep is `SWORN_TO` House Tyrell of Highgarden as overlord (track_b: Overlord). Their seat is Brightwater Keep, disputed by House Florent (track_b: Seat). This is a `CADET_BRANCH_OF` House Tyrell (wiki:House_Tyrell_of_Brightwater_Keep).

## Narrative Arc

In ASOS, Garlan is raised to lordship after the Blackwater; Brightwater remains held by a Florent garrison (wiki:House_Tyrell_of_Brightwater_Keep). In AFFC, Garlan brings Tyrell forces to the Reach to press his claim on Brightwater, still held by Ser Colin Florent. Before he can lay siege, news of Euron Greyjoy's conquest of the Shield Islands redirects him to gather men to retake the islands (wiki:House_Tyrell_of_Brightwater_Keep).
