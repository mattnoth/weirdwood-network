## Narrative Arc

House Marbrand is a prominent Lannister vassal. Addam Marbrand serves as a commander in the Lannister host during the War of the Five Kings, fighting at the Battle on the Green Fork and other engagements. He later becomes Commander of the City Watch of King's Landing. The house appears extensively through AGOT, ACOK, ASOS, and AFFC in battle musters and court scenes.
