## Identity

House Jordayne is a lordly house from Dorne, seated at the Tor (wiki:House_Jordayne).

## Origins

House Jordayne was founded during the coming of the Andals to Dorne, along with Houses Allyrion, Martell, Qorgyle, Santagar, Uller, and Vaith (wiki:House_Jordayne). House Martell once bent the knee to the Jordayne kings of the Tor (wiki:House_Jordayne).

## Appearances & Description

Their blazon is a golden quill on checkered dark and light green (wiki:House_Jordayne).

## Narrative Arc

By the time of Nymeria's War, House Jordayne was among the bannermen of King Yorick Yronwood, and they fought against Mors Martell and his Rhoynar allies (wiki:House_Jordayne). During the First Dornish War, the Tor's steward surrendered the castle peacefully after the death of Lord Jordayne, though the Dornishmen quickly recaptured it. Queen Visenya Targaryen later burned the Tor from dragonback in 8 AC (wiki:House_Jordayne).
