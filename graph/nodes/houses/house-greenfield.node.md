## Origins

The Greenfields are an ancient line, dating back to the days of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men). During this era, they built a vast timber castle, made entirely of [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood), called the [Bower](wiki:Bower), now known as [Greenfield](wiki:Greenfield).(wiki:House_Greenfield.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
In [209 AC](wiki:209_AC), Ser [Myles Greenfield](wiki:Myles_Greenfield) participated in the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow).(wiki:House_Greenfield.cite_ref-Rthk.5B.5Bthe_hedge_knight_.28comics.29.7Cgraphic_novel.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Ser [Garth Greenfield](wiki:Garth_Greenfield) is taken captive in the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood), probably by a [Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood), since he spends the rest of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) as a prisoner at [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall).(wiki:House_Greenfield.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) dislikes Ser [Preston Greenfield](wiki:Preston_Greenfield) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) because he treats her like a [lackwit](wiki:Lackwit) child, in addition to beating her on the orders of King [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:House_Greenfield.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Preston is stabbed and hacked brutally during the [riot of King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing). He is last seen trying to protect the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Fat_One)) after his litter had been overturned. The discovery of his body takes some time, due to the fact that his formerly white armor had been covered entirely with his blood.(wiki:House_Greenfield.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

When pondering on the celibacy vows of the Kingsguard, Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) remembers how Ser Preston used to visit a draper's house when the draper was away.(wiki:House_Greenfield.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
