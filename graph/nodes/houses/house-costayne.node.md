## Origins

Lady [Elinor Costayne](wiki:Elinor_Costayne), the wife of Ser [Theo Bolling](wiki:Theo_Bolling), was forced to marry King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) seven days after Theo's execution in [47 AC](wiki:47_AC).(wiki:House_Costayne.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Lord [Owen Costayne](wiki:Owen_Costayne) of [Three Towers](wiki:Three_Towers) declared for Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), going against his [Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) overlords in [129 AC](wiki:129_AC). He was mortally wounded by Ser [Jon Roxton](wiki:Jon_Roxton)'s Valyrian steel black blade, [Orphan-Maker](wiki:Orphan-Maker), during the [Battle of the Honeywine](wiki:Battle_of_the_Honeywine) in [130 AC](wiki:130_AC).(wiki:House_Costayne.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Along with Lords [Beesbury](wiki:House_Beesbury) and [Redwyne](wiki:House_Redwyne), Lord Costayne accompanied the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Regency_of_Aegon_III)) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for the coronation of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) and his [wedding](wiki:Wedding) to Queen [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen) in [131 AC](wiki:131_AC).(wiki:House_Costayne.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) After the death of Queen Jaehaera in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), a Costayne girl was among the names suggested by Lady [Samantha Tarly](wiki:Samantha_Tarly) as possible brides for the king.(wiki:House_Costayne.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Ser [Leo Costayne](wiki:Leo_Costayne) invaded the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) after the death of Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), but was slain at [Great Wyk](wiki:Great_Wyk) in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC).(wiki:House_Costayne.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
[Lord Costayne](wiki:Lord_Costayne_(Daeron_II)) fought for the [Blackfyres](wiki:House_Blackfyre) during the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion) in [196 AC](wiki:196_AC). The Costayne force that participated in the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field) fought on the left of [Daemon Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre)'s host.(wiki:House_Costayne.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Ser [Tommen Costayne](wiki:Tommen_Costayne) participated in the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC).(wiki:House_Costayne.cite_ref-Rthk.5B.5Bthe_hedge_knight_.28comics.29.7Cgraphic_novel.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) The son of the Blackfyre-supporting Lord Costayne participated in Lord [Ambrose Butterwell](wiki:Ambrose_Butterwell)'s wedding festivities. This [Lord Costayne](wiki:Lord_Costayne_(Aerys_I)) was married to the daughter of Lord Ambrose by his first wife, and he considered himself better born than [Lord Shawney](wiki:Lord_Shawney). Lord Costayne was defeated in the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls) by Ser [Glendon Flowers](wiki:Glendon_Flowers). After the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion) was put down in [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), a man in service to Lord [Vyrwel](wiki:House_Vyrwel) claimed to have been one of Lord [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers)'s spies. He was promptly killed by a man in service to Lord Costayne.(wiki:House_Costayne.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
Long [Tom Costayne](wiki:Tom_Costayne) served in the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) for sixty years.(wiki:House_Costayne.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
