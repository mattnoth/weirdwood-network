## Identity

House Rykker of Duskendale is a lordly house from the crownlands. Their sigil depicts two black warhammers crossed on a white saltire on blue (*Azure, on a saltire argent two warhammers saltirewise sable*) (wiki:House_Rykker). Their seat is the Dun Fort in Duskendale (wiki:House_Rykker.cite_ref-Raffcappendix).

## Narrative Arc

House Rykker received Duskendale after the Defiance of Duskendale and the fall of House Darklyn in 277 AC (wiki:House_Rykker.cite_ref-Raffc9). During a feast when Cersei first came to court, Lord Rykker joked about Lord Tywin Lannister shitting gold; Tywin stared him down until he left (wiki:House_Rykker.cite_ref-Raffc7).

Ser Jaremy Rykker fought for Aerys II during Robert's Rebellion and chose the Night's Watch over execution. At Castle Black he commanded the rangers in Benjen Stark's absence but was killed by a wight (wiki:House_Rykker.cite_ref-Ragot48, wiki:House_Rykker.cite_ref-Ragot60).

In ASOS, the Battle at Duskendale saw Tallhart and Glover march on Duskendale and get defeated by Randyll Tarly (wiki:House_Rykker.cite_ref-Rasos19). In AFFC, Lord Renfred is with Tarly at Maidenpool, leaving Ser Rufus Leek as castellan of the Dun Fort (wiki:House_Rykker.cite_ref-Raffc9).
