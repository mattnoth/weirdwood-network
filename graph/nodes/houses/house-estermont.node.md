## Origins

It is as yet unconfirmed if the Estermonts are of [First Men](wiki:First_Men) or [Andal](wiki:Andal) ancestry. During the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals), the [Storm Kings](wiki:Storm_King) from [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon) and many of their bannermen intermarried with Andals.(wiki:House_Estermont.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_andals_in_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
The daughter of [Larissa Velaryon](wiki:Larissa_Velaryon) married the elderly Lord Estermont in [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:House_Estermont.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) spotted the Estermont turtle among the heraldry present at the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC). Ser [Gunthor Estermont](wiki:Gunthor_Estermont) rode in the lists.(wiki:House_Estermont.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Lady [Cassana Estermont](wiki:Cassana_Estermont) wed Lord [Steffon Baratheon](wiki:Steffon_Baratheon) of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) and had three sons by him; [Robert](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), and [Renly](wiki:Renly_Baratheon). Both Cassana and Steffon drowned on the [Windproud](wiki:Windproud) when caught in a severe storm in [Shipbreaker Bay](wiki:Shipbreaker_Bay), after an unsuccessful quest to the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) to find a bride for King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen)'s son, Prince [Rhaegar](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen).(wiki:House_Estermont.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Two of King Robert I Baratheon's Estermont uncles remained at court in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for half a year following [his wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister) to Lady [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister). Robert insisted on repaying the visit, with Queen Cersei and members of his court accompanying him for a two-week stay at [Greenstone](wiki:Greenstone). While there, Cersei suspected that Robert was [bedding](wiki:Bedding) a [widowed cousin](wiki:Estermont_(cousin_of_Robert)) he used to play with as a child, and had her twin brother Ser [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) follow Robert to confirm her suspicions. Cersei and Jaime slept together at Greenstone, and Cersei likes to believe that was the night their eldest child Prince [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) was conceived.(wiki:House_Estermont.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Old Lord Estermont was one of the insulting matches [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), the [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), proposed to his daughter Princess [Arianne](wiki:Arianne_Martell).(wiki:House_Estermont.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

On the outset of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), [Lord Estermont](wiki:Lord_Estermont) declares for his grandson and liege lord, King [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon).(wiki:House_Estermont.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) The Estermonts' sea turtle is seen by Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) in Renly's camp. When he is preparing for battle against his brother [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), Renly decides Lord Estermont would command the reserve of his army.(wiki:House_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) The Estermonts turn to King Stannis after [Renly's death](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), however, along with most other storm lords.
Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose) acknowledges the presence of the great lords Estermont, [Errol](wiki:House_Errol) and [Varner](wiki:House_Varner) during his parley with Stannis. Lord Estermont recommends against storming the walls of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), believing it would cost too many lives. He suggests a duel against Cortnay, or a long siege to starve them. He refuses to turn to the religion of the [Lord of Light](wiki:R'hllor), and asks to fight under the [crowned stag banner](wiki:House_Baratheon) instead of the [flaming heart](wiki:House_Baratheon_of_Dragonstone).(wiki:House_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
The banner of House Estermont is spotted in the van of Stannis's army as it approaches [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:House_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Lord Estermont and his men fight in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). Together with Ser [Eldon](wiki:Eldon_Estermont), Ser [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Estermont) and Ser [Alyn](wiki:Alyn_Estermont), he bends the knee to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) after Stannis's defeat.(wiki:House_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)(wiki:House_Estermont.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Ser [Lomas](wiki:Lomas_Estermont) and Ser [Andrew Estermont](wiki:Andrew_Estermont) escape the battle, and lead part of the [Greenstone](wiki:Greenstone) men in remaining loyal to Stannis after the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush).(wiki:House_Estermont.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Estermont is a guest at King Joffrey's [royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) to Lady [Margaery](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) of [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell). He declares at [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s trial that he saw Tyrion pick up the [wedding chalice](wiki:Wedding_chalice) as Joffrey was dying and poured the remaining wine onto the floor.(wiki:House_Estermont.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
Ser [Andrew Estermont](wiki:Andrew_Estermont) helps Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) smuggle [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm) out of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). He sails away with the boy, commanding his escort.(wiki:House_Estermont.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
The Estermont banner is seen at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) after Stannis's arrival.(wiki:House_Estermont.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn) reports to Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) that [Eldon Estermont](wiki:Eldon_Estermont), the new [Lord of Greenstone](wiki:Lord_of_Greenstone),(wiki:House_Estermont.cite_ref-Lord_20-0) has married [Sylva Santagar](wiki:Sylva_Santagar).(wiki:House_Estermont.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) Unknown to those in King's Landing, this was punishment for Sylva's part in Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell)'s attempted coup to place Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) on the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:House_Estermont.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
Ser [Lomas Estermont](wiki:Lomas_Estermont) remains in Storm's End, loyal to Stannis.(wiki:House_Estermont.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Storm's End is [besieged](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(300)) by a Tyrell army.(wiki:House_Estermont.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
