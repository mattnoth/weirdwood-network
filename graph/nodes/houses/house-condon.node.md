## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser [Kyle Condon](wiki:Kyle_Condon) is considered the right hand man of Lord [Medger Cerwyn](wiki:Medger_Cerwyn). Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) leaves Kyle and [Ronnel Stout](wiki:Ronnel_Stout) in charge of a strong force with orders to defend the [Trident](wiki:Trident) against a [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) incursion.(wiki:House_Condon.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) and her companions see the corpses of the perpetrators of the [raid on Saltpans](wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans) hanged from trees. Among the dead are men with tridents badges sewn on their breasts, presumably Condon or [Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) men-at-arms who became [broken men](wiki:Broken_men) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:House_Condon.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
