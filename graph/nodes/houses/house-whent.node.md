## Identity

House Whent of Harrenhal is a noble house from the riverlands, the seventh house to rule the cursed castle of Harrenhal (wiki:House_Whent). Their arms depict nine black bats displayed over a yellow field (wiki:House_Whent).

## Origins

The Whents were originally knights in the service of the Lothstons. When Lady Danelle Lothston went mad during the reign of King Maekar I Targaryen, the Whents received Harrenhal for their role in defeating the Lothstons (wiki:House_Whent). They are a fairly young house (wiki:House_Whent).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Tully as overlord during AGOT (track_b: Overlords). Previously under `OVERLORD_OF` House Lothston before that house's extinction (track_b: Overlords). By ADWD, `SWORN_TO` House Baelish (track_b: Overlords). The Whents are connected to House Tully through marriage: Hoster Tully married Lady Minisa Whent, mother of Catelyn, Lysa, and Edmure (wiki:House_Whent).

## Appearances & Description

Sigil: Nine black bats, 4-3-2, on yellow (wiki:House_Whent). The Whents may have high cheekbones — Catelyn Stark resembles her mother Minisa Whent in cheekbones and jaw (wiki:House_Whent).

## Narrative Arc

Lord Walter Whent hosted the famous tourney at Harrenhal in 281 AC, where Lyanna Stark was crowned queen of love and beauty by Prince Rhaegar Targaryen (wiki:House_Whent). Ser Oswell Whent served in Aerys II's Kingsguard and was slain during Robert's Rebellion (wiki:House_Whent). By AGOT, Lady Shella Whent is the last of her line, dwelling among ghosts in Harrenhal's cavernous vaults (wiki:House_Whent). Harrenhal is stripped from the Whents during the War of the Five Kings and granted successively to Janos Slynt, then Petyr Baelish (wiki:House_Whent).
