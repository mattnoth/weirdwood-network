## Narrative Arc

Lord Qarlton Chelsted was the penultimate Hand of the King to Aerys II Targaryen, remembered by Jaime Lannister as the "mace-and-dagger hand." Qarlton attempted to convince Aerys not to burn King's Landing with wildfire but failed. When Aerys refused, Qarlton resigned. Aerys had him dipped in wildfire and burned alive, replacing him with the pyromancer Rossart as Hand (wiki:Rasos11).
