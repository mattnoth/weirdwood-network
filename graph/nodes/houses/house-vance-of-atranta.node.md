## Identity

House Vance of Atranta is one of two great branches of House Vance in the riverlands, the other being House Vance of Wayfarer's Rest (wiki:House_Vance_of_Atranta). Their arms are quartered: a green dragon on white and a white tower on black (wiki:House_Vance_of_Atranta).

## Origins

`FOUNDED` by Armistead Vance (track_b: Founder), the mightiest of the Andal conquerors who defeated Tristifer IV Mudd during the coming of the Andals (wiki:House_Vance_of_Atranta). It is unclear whether Atranta or Wayfarer's Rest is the younger branch (wiki:House_Vance_of_Atranta).

## Allegiances

`SEAT_OF` Atranta (track_b: Seat). `SWORN_TO` House Tully as overlord during AGOT (track_b: Overlords). `SWORN_TO` House Baelish by ADWD (track_b: Overlords). `REGION_OF` Riverlands (track_b: Region). House Vance rules over wider domains and can field a larger army than House Tully (wiki:House_Vance_of_Atranta). House Smallwood is sworn to one of the Vance families (wiki:House_Vance_of_Atranta).

## Appearances & Description

Sigil: Quartered — a green dragon on white, a white tower on black (wiki:House_Vance_of_Atranta). They supported the greens during the Dance of the Dragons (wiki:House_Vance_of_Atranta).

## Narrative Arc

In ASOS, Lord Norbert Vance fights for the Stark-Tully cause. After the Red Wedding and the collapse of the Northern cause, Lord Norbert eventually bends the knee. In AFFC, the Vance of Atranta banner appears among those at the siege of Riverrun (wiki:House_Vance_of_Atranta). Lord Norbert is blind but remains head of the house, with his heir Ronald "the Bad" Vance and several other sons (wiki:House_Vance_of_Atranta).
