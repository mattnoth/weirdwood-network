## Identity

House Roote of Lord Harroway's Town is a noble house from the riverlands (wiki:House_Roote). Their arms are a two-headed horse, brown, on a field of wavy green and green — possibly taken from Old King Andahar, as the ferry at Harroway is called the king's two-headed water horse (wiki:House_Roote).

## Narrative Arc

Ser Richard Roote was one of the first Kingsguard when the order was founded in 10 AC (wiki:House_Roote). In ASOS, Lucas Roote is squire to Ser Andrey Charlton and both are killed by Lannister forces. Sandor Clegane and Arya visit Lord Harroway's Town and find it washed out by the overflowing Trident (wiki:House_Roote). In AFFC, the Roote banner is among those aiding the Lannisters at the siege of Riverrun (wiki:House_Roote).
