## Origins

During [the Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest) of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) by [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters) rebelled against [Ronnel Arryn](wiki:Ronnel_Arryn_(king)), [King of Mountain and Vale](wiki:King_of_Mountain_and_Vale), and named as their queen [Marla Sunderland](wiki:Marla_Sunderland).(wiki:House_Sunderland.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) flew on [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) to end the [Sistermen's Rebellion](wiki:Sistermen's_Rebellion), however. The Sistermen deposed Marla, and her brother, Lord [Steffon Sunderland](wiki:Steffon_Sunderland), submitted to King Aegon I.(wiki:House_Sunderland.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:House_Sunderland.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Prince [Jacaerys Velaryon](wiki:Jacaerys_Velaryon) flew on [Vermax](wiki:Vermax) to Sisterton, where he received obeisance from Lords Sunderland and [Borrell](wiki:House_Borrell). Jacaerys gained the Three Sisters for the [blacks](wiki:Blacks).(wiki:House_Sunderland.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

[Lord Sunderland](wiki:Lord_Sunderland) led the Sistermen fighting for [House Blackfyre](wiki:House_Blackfyre) during the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion) in [196 AC](wiki:196_AC). He attended the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls) in [212 AC](wiki:212_AC) that was to launch the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion).(wiki:House_Sunderland.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Lord [Triston Sunderland](wiki:Triston_Sunderland) is recalled by Lord [Godric Borrell](wiki:Godric_Borrell) as having bemoaned the high cost of purchasing destriers for his seven sons, all of whom are determined to become [knights](wiki:Knight).(wiki:House_Sunderland.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### The Winds of Winter

Three of Triston's sons travel to the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) to participate in the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) for the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights).(wiki:House_Sunderland.cite_ref-11)
