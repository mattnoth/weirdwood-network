## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) is the [Lord Steward](wiki:Lord_Steward) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:House_Marsh.cite_ref-Ragot21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), names Bowen as castellan of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) when he leads the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) north of the [Wall](wiki:Wall) in search of [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark) and [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder).(wiki:House_Marsh.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Bowen names Ser [Wynton Stout](wiki:Wynton_Stout) as castellan when he leaves Castle Black to investigate [wildling](wiki:Wildling) incursions.(wiki:House_Marsh.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Bowen wins the [fight at the Bridge of Skulls](wiki:Fight_at_the_Bridge_of_Skulls) at heavy cost of life to the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), and he is severely injured in the battle.(wiki:House_Marsh.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
With Jeor having been killed in the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep), Bowen puts his name forth during the [choosing](wiki:Choosing) of the new Lord Commander at Castle Black.(wiki:House_Marsh.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) He withdraws his name after receiving few votes and throws his support behind Lord [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt). [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) is ultimately chosen instead.(wiki:House_Marsh.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
