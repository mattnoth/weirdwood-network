## Origins

The Carons date back to the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes), and they are a family known for creating warriors and singers. They claim their [nightingale](wiki:Nightingale) sigil has been in a thousand battles.(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_stormlands:_the_men_of_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel) states [Nightsong](wiki:Nightsong) has been sworn to [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) since time immemorial, but also that the [Kingdom of the Reach](wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Reach) once included [western marches](wiki:Western_marches) from [Horn Hill](wiki:Horn_Hill) to Nightsong.(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) The Carons and [Swanns](wiki:House_Swann) both claim to be the oldest of the [marcher lords](wiki:Marcher_lords).(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_stormlands:_the_men_of_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

During the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), Lord [Fowler](wiki:House_Fowler) burned Nightsong and took Lord Caron and his family captive.(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

The [first Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Aenys_I)) besieged Nightsong with a host of [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen), but was unable to capture the castle. The rebel was eventually defeated in the [Vulture Hunt](wiki:Vulture_Hunt) by Lady [Ellyn Caron](wiki:Ellyn_Caron), Lord [Harmon Dondarrion](wiki:Harmon_Dondarrion), and Lord [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly_(lord)).(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Lord [Morton Caron](wiki:Morton_Caron) attended the [Golden Wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC) and considered King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) to be cautious and shrewed.(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

After the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord [Royce Caron](wiki:Royce_Caron) was chosen as one of [the regents](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III) of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), but he resigned a year later.(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Ser [Arlan](wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree) and [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) served Lord [Pearse Caron](wiki:Pearse_Caron) and Lord [Dondarrion](wiki:House_Dondarrion) when they fought another [Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Daeron_II)) in the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains).(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Rawoiafpearse_caron.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Lord Pearse was one of the outstanding participants in the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow). He was a harper, singer and knight of renown, and had his personal arms emblazoned with a silver harp. Pearse chose Lord [Leo Tyrell](wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(Longthorn)) as his opponent at Ashford. The match was even, and Pearse managed to shatter Leo's shield, but in the end he yielded. Lord Caron ignored Duncan when he was asked for help in the [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven).(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)

In [287 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) was promised to the younger son of Lord [Bryen Caron](wiki:Bryen_Caron), a shy boy with a mole above his lip, but two years later a chill caused his death, also taking the lives of Lord Caron, his wife and their daughters.(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) This left young [Bryce Caron](wiki:Bryce_Caron) as [Lord of the Marches](wiki:Lord_of_the_Marches).

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord [Bryce Caron](wiki:Bryce_Caron)'s shield with its field of [nightingales](wiki:Nightingale) is seen displayed outside his tent during the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney). He is one of the outstanding knights at the tourney, but he is defeated by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

After King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) assumes the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), he orders that Bryce swear fealty.(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Along with most other storm lords, Lord Bryce declares for [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) at the outset of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), and he is named to the [Rainbow Guard](wiki:Rainbow_Guard), being known as Bryce the Orange afterwards. Lord Caron's nightingales are seen by [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) in Renly's camp at [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge). During the feast he goads Ser [Robar the Red](wiki:Robar_Royce) into juggling daggers.(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

After the failed parley between Renly and his brother [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), Bryce is given command of the left in the [coming battle](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)).(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Bryce turns to Stannis after Renly's death, however. He accompanies Stannis to the parley with Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose), where he offers to fight Cortnay in single combat but is rebuffed.(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Bryce advises Stannis against having [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) present for his upcoming attack on [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Bryce is slain in single combat by a one-eyed knight, Ser [Philip Foote](wiki:Philip_Foote). Joffrey grants the Carons' lands, rights, and incomes to Philip, forming [House Foote of Nightsong](wiki:House_Foote_of_Nightsong).(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Bryce's half-brother [Rolland Storm](wiki:Rolland_Storm), the Bastard of [Nightsong](wiki:Nightsong), is regarded by Stannis as one of the few good men that remain to him.(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) Rolland had commanded the rearguard that allows Stannis to reach the galleys and flee from the Blackwater.(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) Rolland helps Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) smuggle [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm) out of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). He is trusted by Davos because of his fierce faith in [the Warrior](wiki:Warrior_(the_Seven)) instead of [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor).(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1) Rolland is named [castellan](wiki:Castellan) of Dragonstone by Stannis before going the king sails north to the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

The arms of House Caron are spotted by [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) during the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall) between Stannis's knights and the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) of [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder).(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) After the battle, Stannis sends word for Rolland to have [obsidian](wiki:Obsidian) mined at Dragonstone.(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-1)

[Mylenda Caron](wiki:Mylenda_Caron) becomes a widow when [Lady Stoneheart](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) and the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) hang [Petyr Frey](wiki:Petyr_Frey).(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Rolland continues to serve Stannis's cause by holding [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). His fate after the [fall of Dragonstone](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone) is unknown.(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Rolland is the pretender Lord of Nightsong.(wiki:House_Caron.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
