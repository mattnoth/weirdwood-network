## Identity

House Baratheon of Dragonstone is a cadet branch of House Baratheon of Storm's End. Their seat is Dragonstone, the island castle northeast of King's Landing, and the house commands the allegiance of the lords of the narrow sea (wiki:House_Baratheon_of_Dragonstone).

## Heraldry & Sigil

A black stag enclosed within a fiery heart on yellow (Or, within a heart enflamed proper a stag sable) Formerly: A crowned black stag on a golden field (Or, a stag crowned sable) (wiki:House_Baratheon_of_Dragonstone)

## Origins

Founded in 284 AC after Robert's Rebellion when King Robert I Baratheon named his younger brother Stannis Lord of Dragonstone. Stannis took the title as a slight, preferring the richer lands of Storm's End, but Robert wanted a strong leader in the old seat of House Targaryen. Robert instead awarded Storm's End to the youngest brother, Renly (wiki:House_Baratheon_of_Dragonstone).

## Allegiances

`OVERLORD_OF` House Baratheon of King's Landing → House Baratheon of Dragonstone (284–299 AC) (track_b: Overlord). The house claims sovereignty over the Seven Kingdoms and disputes the authority of House Baratheon of King's Landing. Internal tensions exist between the "queen's men" (R'hllor converts loyal to Selyse/Melisandre) and the "king's men" (those keeping the Faith of the Seven) (wiki:House_Baratheon_of_Dragonstone).

## Appearances & Description

Sigil: originally the crowned black stag of House Baratheon. During the War of the Five Kings, Stannis adopts a black stag enclosed within a fiery red heart surrounded by orange fire on sun yellow. Later merges this with the traditional banner (wiki:House_Baratheon_of_Dragonstone).

## Narrative Arc

In AGOT, Stannis suspects Cersei's children are not Robert's and investigates with Jon Arryn. After Arryn's death, Stannis retreats to Dragonstone. In ACOK, Stannis proclaims himself rightful king and is advised by Melisandre, whose magic enables Renly's assassination. The house's assault on King's Landing fails at the Battle of the Blackwater. In ASOS, reduced to ~1,300–1,500 men on Dragonstone and 300 at Storm's End, Stannis burns traitors as sacrifices to R'hllor, then sails north to win the battle beneath the Wall. In AFFC, Dragonstone is besieged by the Redwyne fleet. In ADWD, Stannis marches south from the Wall toward Winterfell, gathering northern lords to his cause, while his garrison at Dragonstone falls (wiki:House_Baratheon_of_Dragonstone).
