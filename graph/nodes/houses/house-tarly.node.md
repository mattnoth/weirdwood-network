## Identity

House Tarly of Horn Hill is a noble house of the Reach, one of the strongest sworn to House Tyrell (wiki:House_Tarly). Their sigil is a striding red huntsman on green, and their semi-canon motto is "First in Battle" (wiki:House_Tarly). Marcher lords of the Dornish Marches, the Tarlys are a family old in honor with rich lands and a strong keep (wiki:House_Tarly). They are keepers of the Valyrian greatsword Heartsbane (wiki:House_Tarly).

## Origins

House Tarly is of First Men origin from the Age of Heroes (wiki:House_Tarly). They claim descent from twin sons of Garth Greenhand, Herndon of the Horn and Harlon the Hunter, who built their castle atop Horn Hill (wiki:House_Tarly).

## Allegiances

House Tarly is `SWORN_TO` House Tyrell as overlord (track_b: Overlord). Their seat is Horn Hill in the foothills of the Red Mountains (wiki:House_Tarly). House Hunt is a vassal (wiki:House_Tarly).

## Appearances & Description

Sigil: a striding red huntsman on green (wiki:House_Tarly). The Tarly shield is wood-and-leather, blazoned with the striding huntsman (agot-jon-04). Heartsbane is their Valyrian steel greatsword, passed from father to son for approximately 500 years (agot-jon-04).

## Narrative Arc

During Robert's Rebellion, Lord Randyll commanded the vanguard at the Battle of Ashford, killing Lord Cafferen and forcing Robert to retreat; Lord Mace Tyrell took credit (wiki:House_Tarly). In AGOT, Samwell Tarly joins the Night's Watch after Randyll removes him from the line of succession in favor of Dickon (agot-jon-04). In ACOK, Randyll joins Renly's army, later captures Renly's stores at Bitterbridge, and commands the center at the Battle of the Blackwater (wiki:House_Tarly). In ASOS, Sam slays an Other beyond the Wall and escapes the mutiny at Craster's Keep (wiki:House_Tarly). In AFFC, Jon sends Sam to the Citadel; Randyll defeats a northern army at Duskendale and captures Maidenpool; Dickon is betrothed to Eleanor Mooton (wiki:House_Tarly). In ADWD, Randyll returns to King's Landing and is named justiciar on the small council (wiki:House_Tarly).
