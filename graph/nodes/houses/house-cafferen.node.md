## Origins

[Lord Cafferen](wiki:Lord_Cafferen) was originally a [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) loyalist during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). He was captured at the [battles at Summerhall](wiki:Battles_at_Summerhall) where Houses [Fell](wiki:House_Fell) and [Grandison](wiki:House_Grandison) had intended to join their forces to his, but Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) beat them one by one. Lord Cafferen eventually came over to Robert's side, but was killed fighting for him at the [battle of Ashford](wiki:Battle_of_Ashford).
