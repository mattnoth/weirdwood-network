## Identity

House Manwoody is a lordly house from Dorne, seated at Kingsgrave (wiki:House_Manwoody).

## Origins

The arms of House Manwoody and the name of their castle, Kingsgrave, derive from the founder of the family having slain a king (Tier 2 — wiki hedges with "semi-canon source") (wiki:House_Manwoody). King Albin Manwoody claimed dominion over the Red Mountains when the Rhoynar arrived in Dorne; he was defeated in Nymeria's War and sent to the Wall in golden fetters (wiki:House_Manwoody).
