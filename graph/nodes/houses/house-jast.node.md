## Origins

House Jast was formed by the union of [First Men](wiki:First_Men) and [Andal](wiki:Andals) nobles during the [Andal invasion](wiki:Andal_invasion).(wiki:House_Jast.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
During the misrule of Lord [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister), Lords Jast and [Falwell](wiki:House_Falwell) became embroiled in a private quarrel. Rather than seek a ruling from [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), the lords decided to settle the matter with a melee. Nine men were killed, twenty-seven maimed and wounded, and still the quarrel raged on.(wiki:House_Jast.cite_ref-sample_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord [Antario Jast](wiki:Antario_Jast) joins the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) army mustered by Ser [Stafford Lannister](wiki:Stafford_Lannister). After the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross), he is reported as having died to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). His sons are also captured by the [northern](wiki:North) army.(wiki:House_Jast.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Jast was not killed at [Oxcross](wiki:Oxcross), but made prisoner instead. He spends the rest of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) prisoner at [Pinkmaiden Castle](wiki:Pinkmaiden_Castle).(wiki:House_Jast.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Antario is freed at the end of the war. The wounds received and the time he had spent prisoner affect him greatly, leaving him a shadow of the man he had been. Lord Jast joins the group which escorts Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s body from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) back to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock). Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) sees him armored in black steel, with three gold lion's heads on his breastplate.(wiki:House_Jast.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
