## Identity

House Harte is a noble house from the crownlands, sworn directly to House Baratheon of King's Landing. Their arms are three red hart's heads caboshed on a white bend on vair (wiki:House_Harte).

## Appearances & Description

Sigil: Three red hart's heads caboshed on a white bend on vair (*Vair, on a bend argent three harts' heads caboshed gules*) (wiki:House_Harte).

## Allegiances

House Harte is `SWORN_TO` House Baratheon of King's Landing as their overlord (track_b: Overlord). They are located in the `REGION_OF` Crownlands (track_b: Region). They are classed as a Lordly House in the Crownlands navbox (wiki:House_Harte).

## Narrative Arc

Historically, a Lord Harte supported King Maegor I Targaryen, then another Lord Harte was executed during the Dance of the Dragons for refusing to bend the knee to Aegon II (wiki:House_Harte.cite_ref-Rfab). Ser Denys Harte allegedly engaged the Faceless Men while visiting Braavos. Lady Hazel Harte married Daeron Velaryon and was mother of Queen Daenaera Velaryon; she died of the Winter Fever (wiki:House_Harte.cite_ref-Rfab).

In the main series, Ser Elwood Harte fought for King Joffrey at the Battle of the Blackwater, where Ser Donnel Swann surrendered to him (wiki:House_Harte.cite_ref-Rasos67). Tom of Sevenstreams sings "Lord Harte Rode Out on a Rainy Day" (wiki:House_Harte.cite_ref-Rasos39).
