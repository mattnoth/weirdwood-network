## Origins

### Origins[ ]

The Dustins claim descent from the [First King](wiki:First_King) of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) and the [Barrow Kings](wiki:Barrow_Kings) of the [barrowlands](wiki:Barrowlands) who ruled after him.(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) When the last Barrow King submitted to [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) at the end of the [Thousand Years War](wiki:Thousand_Years_War), the [King of Winter](wiki:King_of_Winter) took his daughter for wife.(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) House Dustin's claims of shared blood and descent from the Barrow Kings is regarded as truth.(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) has prospered under the Dustins, who have loyally ruled the barrowlands for the Starks.(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

### Targaryen dynasty[ ]

During his progresses across the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) held court at Barrowton twice during his reign.(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) [Denys Snow](wiki:Denys_Snow), the Bastard of Barrowton, joined Prince [Aegon the Uncrowned](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) against his uncle, King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), though Denys and the prince perished in the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye).(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Rtsotd.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
When King [Jaehaerys I](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) visited Barrowton on their [royal progress](wiki:Royal_progress) in the [north](wiki:North) in [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Lord Dustin showed them the [Great Barrow](wiki:Great_Barrow) and honored them with a small [tourney](wiki:Tourney). Alysanne held a [women's court](wiki:Women's_court) in the town.(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
House Dustin supported Princess [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aemon)) in the [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council) of [101 AC](wiki:101_AC) at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) When the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) broke out, while Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark) gathered the north's bannermen, Lord [Roderick Dustin](wiki:Roderick_Dustin) led two thousand northern soldiers, known as the [Winter Wolves](wiki:Winter_Wolves), to fight for Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and her [blacks](wiki:Blacks).(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragonsrhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Roderick led his northmen in several victories against King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen)'s [greens](wiki:Greens), most notably against the [westermen](wiki:Westerlands) in the [Battle by the Lakeshore](wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore) and in the [Butcher's Ball](wiki:Butcher's_Ball), where Lord Dustin led the charge in the battle that claimed the life of Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole).(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2) In the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton), Roderick led the remnants of his Winter Wolves against the [Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) host. In the ensuring clash, Dustin was mortally wounded by Ser [Bryndon Hightower](wiki:Bryndon_Hightower), but the Lord of Barrowton succeeded in slaying Bryndon and Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower) before succumbing to his wounds.(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-3)(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragonsrhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)
In [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), the [Winter Fever](wiki:Winter_Fever) descended on Barrowton.(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) The following year, as winter proved cruel, Lord Dustin closed his gates to hundreds of starving [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) outside the town's walls.(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### Recent History[ ]

House Dustin fought for the [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) during the [War of the Ninepenny Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Ninepenny_Kings). [Brandon Stark](wiki:Brandon_Stark), heir to Lord [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark), was fostered at Barrowton by old Lord Dustin, though he spent most of his time in the [Rills](wiki:Rills). Though Lord [Rodrik Ryswell](wiki:Rodrik_Ryswell) sought to wed his daughter [Barbrey](wiki:Barbrey_Ryswell) to Brandon, she would end up marrying Lord [Willam Dustin](wiki:Willam_Dustin), the son of the old Lord of Barrowton.(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
Members of House Dustin were present at the [Tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) of [281 AC](wiki:281_AC).(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Citadel_16-0)
Six months after their [wedding](wiki:Wedding), Lord Willam followed his liege Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) in [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) against King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen).(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Lord Dustin was one of the six companions that [fought](wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy) alongside Lord Eddard at the [tower of joy](wiki:Tower_of_joy) against the remaining members of the Mad King's [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), where he was slain.(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1) His bones were buried beneath the [red mountains](wiki:Red_mountains) of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). While Lord Eddard returned Willam's stead, he did not return his bones. Taking it as a slight, Lady Dustin held a grudge on the Starks and grew bitter with her overlords.(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

House Dustin and [Ryswell](wiki:House_Ryswell) are among the first houses in the north to recognize [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) as their new [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), due to Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton)'s marriage to [Bethany Ryswell](wiki:Bethany_Ryswell).(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) The Ryswells and Dustins surprise the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) on the [Fever River](wiki:Fever_River), putting their longships to torch.(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) and [Hother Umber](wiki:Hother_Umber) later ride south to join the Dustin and Ryswell forces, preparing an attack on [Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin), held by the ironborn.(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1) After a brief siege, the fort is taken, allowing Lord Bolton and his host to return north.(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
Lady Barbrey Dustin hosts the Boltons at Barrowton, but forbids [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Bolton) from [Barrow Hall](wiki:Barrow_Hall) as she blames him for the death of her nephew, [Domeric Bolton](wiki:Domeric_Bolton). Instead, Ramsay stays at [Goldgrass](wiki:Goldgrass), the keep of Lady Dustin's vassal [Harwood Stout](wiki:Harwood_Stout).(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) After arriving at Winterfell, during the inquiries into the murder of [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Little_Walder_Frey), Lady Barbrey reminds Ser [Aenys Frey](wiki:Aenys_Frey) that House Dustin lost men during the Red Wedding and that "the North remembers" the crimes committed by House Frey against them.(wiki:House_Dustin.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2)
