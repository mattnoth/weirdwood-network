## Identity

House Vance of Wayfarer's Rest is one of two great branches of House Vance in the riverlands, the other being House Vance of Atranta (wiki:House_Vance_of_Wayfarer's_Rest). Their arms are quartered: a black dragon on white, two golden eyes in a golden ring on black (wiki:House_Vance_of_Wayfarer's_Rest).

## Origins

`FOUNDED` by Armistead Vance (track_b: Founder), shared founder with the Atranta branch. It is unclear which branch is younger, or if both descend from an older line that died out (wiki:House_Vance_of_Wayfarer's_Rest).

## Allegiances

`SEAT_OF` Wayfarer's Rest (track_b: Seat). `SWORN_TO` House Tully as overlord during AGOT (track_b: Overlords). `SWORN_TO` House Baelish by ADWD (track_b: Overlords). `REGION_OF` Riverlands (track_b: Region). Like the Atranta branch, rules wider domains and can field a larger army than House Tully (wiki:House_Vance_of_Wayfarer's_Rest). Supported the blacks during the Dance of the Dragons (wiki:House_Vance_of_Wayfarer's_Rest).

## Narrative Arc

In AGOT, Lord Karyl Vance reports Lannister raiding in the riverlands and is sent with Beric Dondarrion to bring Gregor Clegane to justice (wiki:House_Vance_of_Wayfarer's_Rest). The Vances fight throughout the War of the Five Kings for the Stark-Tully cause. In AFFC, the Vance banner appears among those at the siege of Riverrun (wiki:House_Vance_of_Wayfarer's_Rest).
