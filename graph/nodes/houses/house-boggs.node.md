## Identity

House Boggs is a noble house from the crownlands. Their seat is on Crackclaw Point. Neither their arms nor words appear in the books (wiki:House_Boggs).

## Allegiances

`OVERLORD_OF` House Targaryen → House Boggs (?–283 AC), then House Baratheon of King's Landing (283 AC–present) (track_b: Overlords). `REGION_OF` Crownlands (track_b: Region).

## Narrative Arc

Ser Mortimer Boggs attended the wedding tourney at Whitewalls in 212 AC (wiki:The_Mystery_Knight). In 283 AC, members of House Boggs were with Prince Rhaegar Targaryen at the Battle of the Trident during Robert's Rebellion (wiki:Raffc20). No members appear in the main series narrative.
