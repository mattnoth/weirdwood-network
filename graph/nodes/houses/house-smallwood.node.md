## Identity

House Smallwood of Acorn Hall is a noble house from the riverlands (wiki:House_Smallwood). Their sigil is six brown acorns on yellow, and their semi-canon motto is "From These Beginnings" (wiki:House_Smallwood).

## Origins

The Smallwoods have a long history in the riverlands. Lord Myles Smallwood was appointed Hand of the King by Jaehaerys I in 54 AC, though he proved ill-suited to the office and was dismissed in 57 AC (wiki:House_Smallwood).
