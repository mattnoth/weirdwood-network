## Identity

House Crabb is a noble house from the crownlands, sworn to King's Landing. Their lands are on Crackclaw Point. A former seat is the Whispers, now in ruins. Neither arms nor words have appeared in the text (wiki:House_Crabb).

## Narrative Arc

The legendary Ser Clarence Crabb was a hero of Crackclaw Point whose ruined seat, the Whispers, is named for the severed heads he collected — supposedly raised from the dead by his wife to serve as whispering counselors. Multiple Crabbs served in the Kingsguard, including Ser Clement Crabb, Ser Rupert, and Ser Clarence the Short. During the Dance of the Dragons, House Crabb declared for Rhaenyra Targaryen (wiki:House_Crabb). In AFFC, Dick Crabb guides Brienne of Tarth to the Whispers while searching for Sansa Stark; Dick is killed by Shagwell the fool at the ruins. In ADWD, Ser Rennifer Longwaters, chief undergaoler of the Red Keep, claims descent from the Crabbs through a series of female lines (wiki:House_Crabb).
