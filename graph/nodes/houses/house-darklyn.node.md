## Identity

House Darklyn of Duskendale is an extinct noble house which ruled the town of Duskendale and the surrounding lands in the crownlands for centuries. Their keep was the Dun Fort atop a hill dominating the town. The house went extinct as a result of the Defiance of Duskendale in 277 AC. Their arms are fusily black and gold, seven white shields on a red tierce — the seven shields for the seven Darklyns who served in the Kingsguard (wiki:House_Darklyn).

## Origins

The Darklyns were petty kings of the First Men during the Age of Heroes. Their closest allies were House Hollard, who served them loyally and provided brides three times. The Darklyns claimed the mouth of the Blackwater Rush during the Hundred Kingdoms (wiki:House_Darklyn).

## Narrative Arc

The Darklyns were once powerful petty kings who controlled Duskendale and the Blackwater mouth. They forced Togarion Bar Emmon to flee during the Andal invasions and even after their kingdom was absorbed by larger realms, remained influential lords. Seven Darklyns served in the Kingsguard over the centuries. The house's end came with the Defiance of Duskendale — Lord Denys Darklyn's rebellion against King Aerys II Targaryen. After the Defiance was ended, House Darklyn was extinguished and House Hollard was destroyed with them, save for young Dontos Hollard whom Barristan Selmy begged to spare (wiki:House_Darklyn). In AFFC, Brienne visits Duskendale and learns that the related houses Darke, Darkwood, and Dargood still inhabit the town (wiki:Raffc9).
