## Identity

House Peake is a noble house from the Reach, seated at Starpike. Lord Titus Peake is the current head. The house formerly also held the titles Lord of Dunstonbury and Lord of Whitegrove, which they lost. Founded by Florys the Fox. (wiki:House_Peake)

## Origins

Founded by Florys the Fox. (track_b: Founder) Florys the Fox is a legendary figure from the Age of Heroes.

## Allegiances

House Peake is `SWORN_TO` House Tyrell as their overlord. (track_b: Overlord) The Peakes are a Marcher house and have a long history of ambition and rebellion, including involvement in multiple Blackfyre Rebellions. Lord Unwin Peake served as Hand of the King during the regency of Aegon III. (wiki:House_Peake)

## Narrative Arc

House Peake features prominently in the Dunk and Egg stories and Fire & Blood. In "The Mystery Knight," Lord Gormon Peake is involved in the Second Blackfyre Rebellion at the Whitewalls tourney. Historically, Unwin Peake served as Hand and regent for Aegon III, wielding enormous influence. In the main series, House Peake appears through ADWD in connection with the Golden Company's invasion plans — Laswell Peake is among the exiled lords supporting Aegon VI. (wiki:House_Peake)
