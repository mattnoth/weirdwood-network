## Identity

House Elesham of the Paps is a noble house from the Paps in the Vale (wiki:House_Elesham).

## Origins

House Elesham were once independent rulers of the Paps, until they bent the knee to Hugo the Hopeful, a King of Mountain and Vale of House Arryn (wiki:House_Elesham).
