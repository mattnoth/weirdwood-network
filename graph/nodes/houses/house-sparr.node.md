## Identity

House Sparr of Great Wyk is an ironborn noble house on the island of Great Wyk in the Iron Islands. Their lands are about six leagues from Hammerhorn. The lord is known as "the Sparr." Their sigil depicts an oak saltire on blue (wiki:House_Sparr.cite_ref-semi-canon).

## Narrative Arc

The Sparrs are among the bannermen Theon Greyjoy sees in the halls of Pyke preparing for war (wiki:House_Sparr.cite_ref-Racok24). The Sparr and his son Steffarion accompany Gormond Goodbrother to bring news of King Balon's death to Aeron Greyjoy (wiki:House_Sparr.cite_ref-Raffc1). Two members of House Sparr meet Victarion at the kingsmoot on Old Wyk (wiki:House_Sparr.cite_ref-Raffc18). Rodrik Sparr presents the wizard Moqorro to Victarion near the Isle of Cedars during the Iron Fleet's journey to Meereen (wiki:House_Sparr.cite_ref-Radwd56).
