## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser [Harys Haigh](wiki:Harys_Haigh) is with Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton)'s army at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:House_Haigh.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Meera Reed](wiki:Meera_Reed) tells [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) that during the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal), a squire serving a member of House Haigh accosted a crannogman. The knight of House Haigh was later defeated in the tourney by the [Knight of the Laughing Tree](wiki:Knight_of_the_Laughing_Tree) and told to teach his squire honor.(wiki:House_Haigh.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
While staying the night at the [Twins](wiki:Twins) before her brother [Edmure](wiki:Edmure_Tully)'s wedding, [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) encounters [Leslyn](wiki:Leslyn_Haigh), Harys, and [Donnel Haigh](wiki:Donnel_Haigh) drinking in the great hall along with [Lame Lothar Frey](wiki:Lothar_Frey), Ser [Raymund Frey](wiki:Raymund_Frey), Lord [Lucias Vypren](wiki:Lucias_Vypren), Ser [Damon Vypren](wiki:Damon_Vypren), and Ser [Hosteen Frey](wiki:Hosteen_Frey).(wiki:House_Haigh.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) and [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) encounter Donnel when they are trying to sneak into the Twins; Sandor mentions he has beaten Donnel in many melees, once almost killing him.(wiki:House_Haigh.cite_ref-Rasos50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
During the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Leslyn loses half an ear while trying to capture [Greatjon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber_(Greatjon)). Harys fights a [Vance](wiki:House_Vance) with the aid of [Black Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Ryman)).(wiki:House_Haigh.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser Harys and Ser Donnel accompany their cousin Ser [Arwood Frey](wiki:Arwood_Frey) to respond to the [raid on Saltpans](wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans).(wiki:House_Haigh.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) The banners of House Haigh are spotted by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) when he crosses to the [Frey](wiki:House_Frey) camp during the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun).(wiki:House_Haigh.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
