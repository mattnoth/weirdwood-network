## Origins

Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) spotted a pavilion striped in white and purple, possibly a use of the colors and sigil of House Hasty, while attending the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls).(wiki:House_Hasty.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Ser [Bonifer Hasty](wiki:Bonifer_Hasty) was a promising [tourney](wiki:Tourney) knight in his youth,(wiki:House_Hasty.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) and he shared a mutual infatuation with Princess [Rhaella Targaryen](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen).(wiki:House_Hasty.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) When the princess wed her brother, Prince [Aerys Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), Bonifer the Good dedicated himself to the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:House_Hasty.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser [Bonifer Hasty](wiki:Bonifer_Hasty) is captured at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater) after fighting for [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon). He submits to the rule of King [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:House_Hasty.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Bonifer and the [Holy Hundred](wiki:Holy_Hundred) are part of Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s force sent from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to resolve the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun). The force stops at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), where Jaime names Bonifer as [castellan](wiki:Castellan) of the castle during the absence of Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish).(wiki:House_Hasty.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
