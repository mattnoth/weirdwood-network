## Identity

House Lychester is a noble house in the riverlands, owning a small square keep (wiki:House_Lychester). Their arms are orange and white gyronny, a black talon (wiki:House_Lychester).

## Heraldry & Sigil

Orange and white gyronny, a black talon (Gyronny tenné and argent, an eagle's leg erased sable) (wiki:House_Lychester)

## Origins

In 42 AC, Lord Jon Lychester fought King Maegor I at the battle at the Great Fork of the Blackwater during the Faith Militant uprising. The following year, Visenya Targaryen used Vhagar to burn the Lychester seat (wiki:House_Lychester).

## Narrative Arc

Lord Lymond Lychester took six wounds defending a bridge from Ser Maynard. All of Lymond's sons died during Robert's Rebellion, some fighting for the Targaryens and some for Robert (wiki:House_Lychester). In ASOS, the brotherhood without banners visit Lord Lychester's keep, but the old knight has wandering wits and only repeats his bridge story (wiki:House_Lychester). In AFFC, Lychester banners are seen among those aiding the Lannisters at the siege of Riverrun (wiki:House_Lychester).
