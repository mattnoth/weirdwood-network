## Origins

Jalabhar Xho is the exiled Prince of the [Red Flower Vale](wiki:Red_Flower_Vale),(wiki:House_Xho.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) likely exiled for losing a highly ritualized war as per the tradition of the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles).(wiki:House_Xho.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_summer_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Following his exile, Jalabhar came to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) to request gold and swords from King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) to help him regain the Red Flower Vale. Robert always entertained the notion of conquering the Summer Isles, but continuously told Jalabhar and put it off until the next year. Although Robert always put off Jalabhar's request for help, he never firmly declined him, telling him "Next year" instead of "No".(wiki:House_Xho.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

While Jalabhar continues to occasionally petition the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) to win back his seat,(wiki:House_Xho.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) he has become a noted courtier at King's Landing. Having no other place to seek refuge,(wiki:House_Xho.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) the exiled Prince of the Red Flower Vale is one of the most colorful figures at court alongside [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr).(wiki:House_Xho.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
