## Origins

The Baneforts were petty [First Men](wiki:First_Men) kings of the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes), claiming descent from the [Hooded Man](wiki:Hooded_Man). The last [Hooded King](wiki:Hooded_King) was [Morgon Banefort](wiki:Morgon_Banefort), whose [thralls](wiki:Thrall) were defeated after twenty years of war by [Loreon I Lannister](wiki:Loreon_I_Lannister), [King of the Rock](wiki:King_of_the_Rock).(wiki:House_Banefort.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
According to a semi-canon source,(wiki:House_Banefort.cite_ref-7) the Baneforts supported [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) when he suppressed the [Reyne-Tarbeck rebellion](wiki:Reyne-Tarbeck_rebellion).

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lord [Quenten Banefort](wiki:Quenten_Banefort) is captured at the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:House_Banefort.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The colors of House Banefort are spotted by [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) leaving [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) with Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s army.(wiki:House_Banefort.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Banefort spends the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) as a prisoner of Lord [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken),(wiki:House_Banefort.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) and he is probably freed when the [Brackens](wiki:House_Bracken) submit to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) after the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:House_Banefort.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord Banefort is one of the lords who escort the corpse of Tywin from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:House_Banefort.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
