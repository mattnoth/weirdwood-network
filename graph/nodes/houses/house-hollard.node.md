## Identity

House Hollard is an extinct noble house from the crownlands. Their seat was Hollard castle, and they served as vassals to House Darklyn of Duskendale (track_b: Overlord).

## Narrative Arc

House Hollard is most notable for its role in the Defiance of Duskendale. Ser Symon Hollard murdered Ser Gwayne Gaunt of the Kingsguard during the crisis. After the Defiance was resolved by Ser Barristan Selmy's rescue of King Aerys II, House Hollard was destroyed — all members executed. Only young Dontos Hollard was spared, at Barristan's plea. Dontos later became a fool at court and played a role in Sansa Stark's escape from King's Landing before being killed on Littlefinger's orders (wiki:House_Hollard).
