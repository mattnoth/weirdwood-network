## Origins

House Cole were vassals of [House Dondarrion](wiki:House_Dondarrion), who rule from [Blackhaven](wiki:Blackhaven) in the [Dornish Marches](wiki:Dornish_Marches) of the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands).(wiki:House_Cole.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
The greatest member of the house was Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole), the son of the steward at Blackhaven;(wiki:House_Cole.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) according to a semi-canon source, Criston was common-born.(wiki:House_Cole.cite_ref-5) He served as the [sworn shield](wiki:Sworn_shield) of Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), the eldest child of King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen), and he became the [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) after the death of Ser [Harrold Westerling](wiki:Harrold_Westerling). Criston and Rhaenyra had a falling out,(wiki:House_Cole.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) and after the king's death Criston placed Viserys's son on the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) as [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) instead of Rhaenyra, Viserys's chosen successor.(wiki:House_Cole.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) This began the civil war known as the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), and Criston became known as the Kingmaker.(wiki:House_Cole.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) When Aegon grew tired of the more diplomatic approach of Ser [Otto Hightower](wiki:Otto_Hightower), his grandfather and [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Aegon appointed Criston in Otto's place to take the fight to the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) instead.(wiki:House_Cole.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Criston was killed in the [Butcher's Ball](wiki:Butcher's_Ball) while leading a host of [greens](wiki:Greens) through the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:House_Cole.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
According to semi-canon sources, the house is currently extinct.(wiki:House_Cole.cite_ref-Citadel_2-1)
