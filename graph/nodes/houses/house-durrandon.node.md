## Origins

### Ancient History[ ]

The line of the [Storm Kings](wiki:Storm_King) dates back to the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes) when their kingdom was founded by King [Durran Godsgrief](wiki:Durran_Godsgrief), a legendary hero.(wiki:House_Durrandon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_durrandon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) The [First Men](wiki:First_Men) warred with [Andals](wiki:Andals) when the newcomers [invaded the stormlands](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals), but the two peoples eventually made peace and intermarried.(wiki:House_Durrandon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_andals_in_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
The Storm Kings at times(wiki:House_Durrandon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) ruled much of the eastern half of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), an area reaching from [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath) to the [Bay of Crabs](wiki:Bay_of_Crabs).(wiki:House_Durrandon.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Their dominion was protected from [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) and the [Kingdom of the Reach](wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Reach) by the [Dornish Marches](wiki:Dornish_Marches),(wiki:House_Durrandon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) and they conquered the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) from [House Teague](wiki:House_Teague).(wiki:House_Durrandon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) The [riverman](wiki:Riverman) resented Durrandon rule, however, and some joined [Harwyn Hoare](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), when he took the [Trident](wiki:Trident) from [Arrec Durrandon](wiki:Arrec_Durrandon).(wiki:House_Durrandon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### The Conquest[ ]

The Storm Kings' power dwindled in the centuries before [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest). The [Kings of the Reach](wiki:Kings_of_the_Reach) from [House Gardener](wiki:House_Gardener) nibbled at their domains in the west, [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) were threats to the south, and [Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare), [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers), took lands north of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush). King [Argilac Durrandon](wiki:Argilac_Durrandon) managed to stop this decline for a while, but by the time of the Conquest Argilac had grown old and his prowess at arms had faded.(wiki:House_Durrandon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
During the Conquest, [Orys Baratheon](wiki:Orys_Baratheon), one of [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen)'s fiercest generals and his rumored [bastard](wiki:Bastard) brother, slew Argilac the Arrogant, last of the Storm Kings, in the [Last Storm](wiki:Last_Storm).(wiki:House_Durrandon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Orys married Argilac's daughter [Argella Durrandon](wiki:Argella_Durrandon) and adopted the sigil and words of his wife's ancestral line, creating [House Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon) of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), with his descendants having the blood of the Durrandons flowing in their veins.(wiki:House_Durrandon.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
