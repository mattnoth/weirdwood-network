## Origins

The Beesburys descend from the [First Men](wiki:First_Men). They claim to have been founded by [Ellyn Ever Sweet](wiki:Ellyn_Ever_Sweet), a daughter of the legendary [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand). According to the legend, Ellyn loved [honey](wiki:Honey) so much that she sought out the [King of the Bees](wiki:King_of_the_Bees) in his vast mountain hive and made a pact with him, to care for his children and his children's children for all time, becoming the first beekeeper.(wiki:House_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
In [42 AC](wiki:42_AC), Ser [Dickon Flowers](wiki:Dickon_Flowers), the Bastard of Beesbury, was one of the seven [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons) to face King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) and his six champions in a [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven).(wiki:House_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Ser [Braxton Beesbury](wiki:Braxton_Beesbury) was a favorite of Princess [Saera Targaryen](wiki:Saera_Targaryen). After he was embroiled in a scandal with the princess in [84 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) sentenced Braxton to mutilation. However, as a [knight](wiki:Knight), Braxton was allowed to choose a [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat). Though he expected to face a member of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), Braxton was slain in a duel with Jaehaerys.(wiki:House_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Lord [Lyman Beesbury](wiki:Lyman_Beesbury) was the [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin) and a member of the [small council](wiki:Small_council) during the reigns of Jaehaerys and King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen). In [129 AC](wiki:129_AC), he was slain at the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) by the [greens](wiki:Greens).(wiki:House_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) His grandson, Ser [Alan Beesbury](wiki:Alan_Beesbury), thus supported Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and the [blacks](wiki:Blacks), but he was captured in the [Battle on the Honeywine](wiki:Battle_on_the_Honeywine).(wiki:House_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Lords Beesbury, [Costayne](wiki:House_Costayne), and [Redwyne](wiki:Lord_Redwyne) escorted the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for the wedding of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) and Queen [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen) in [131 AC](wiki:131_AC).(wiki:House_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) After the queen's death in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Lady [Samantha Tarly](wiki:Samantha_Tarly) included a Beesbury among the [maidens](wiki:Maiden) of the [Reach](wiki:Reach) whom Aegon could marry.(wiki:House_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
During the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC), Ser [Humfrey Beesbury](wiki:Humfrey_Beesbury) splintered twelve lances against Ser [Humfrey Hardyng](wiki:Humfrey_Hardyng), his brother-in-law, in a match the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) called "the Battle of the Humfreys". Humfrey Beesbury took part in the [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven) on behalf of Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall), and was slain in the first charge by Ser [Donnel of Duskendale](wiki:Donnel_of_Duskendale) from the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:House_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
Blind [Ben Beesbury](wiki:Ben_Beesbury) was one of the candidates offered by [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), to his daughter [Arianne](wiki:Arianne_Martell) for marriage.(wiki:House_Beesbury.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) names the Beesburys as one of the houses supporting him during the parley with his brother, [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), at [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)).(wiki:House_Beesbury.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser [Bertram Beesbury](wiki:Bertram_Beesbury) meets Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) at [Brindlewood](wiki:Brindlewood) on their way to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) and gives them the news of the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:House_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Brienne recalls that Ser [Hugh Beesbury](wiki:Hugh_Beesbury) took part in the wager on her maidenhood, offering her as a present a pot of [honey](wiki:Honey) "as sweet as the maids of [Tarth](wiki:Tarth)".(wiki:House_Beesbury.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
