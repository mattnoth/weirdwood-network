## Identity

House Uller is a lordly house from Dorne, seated at the Hellholt (wiki:House_Uller).

## Heraldry & Sigil

Rayonne yellow over crimson (Per fess rayonny or and gules) (wiki:House_Uller)

## Origins

The Ullers were Andal adventurers who settled along the Brimstone (wiki:House_Uller). The Hellholt is named after an event in which rivals were invited to the castle, locked within, and burned to death — inspiring the Uller arms (wiki:House_Uller).

## Narrative Arc

During the First Dornish War, the Ullers fled the Hellholt rather than give battle. Lord Harlan Tyrell and his garrison disappeared in the sands after leaving the Hellholt. Queen Rhaenys Targaryen and Meraxes both died at the Hellholt in 10 AC; the queen's body was never recovered, and some said she survived her fall and was tortured by the Ullers (wiki:House_Uller). During the Dragon's Wroth, four consecutive Lords of the Hellholt were murdered for the Iron Throne's ransom (wiki:House_Uller). Ellaria Sand, paramour of Prince Oberyn Martell and mother of several of his Sand Snake daughters, is the bastard daughter of Lord Harmen Uller.
