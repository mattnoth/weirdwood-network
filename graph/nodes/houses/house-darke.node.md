## Identity

House Darke is a house from the crownlands. They are said to be distant kin to the extinct Darklyns of Duskendale (wiki:House_Darke).

## Origins

Jonquil Darke, known as the Scarlet Shadow, was the first known Darke — the bastard daughter of Lord Darklyn. She may have been the house's founder, though the house may have been founded by a sibling (wiki:House_Darke).

## Narrative Arc

Jonquil Darke became a sworn shield of Queen Alysanne Targaryen and dueled a wildling woman at the court of Lord Theomore Manderly. Ser Harrold Darke was killed at Dragonstone in defense of Queen Rhaenyra Targaryen during the Dance of the Dragons (wiki:House_Darke). In AFFC, a servant at the Seven Swords inn in Duskendale claims to be a Darke by birth (wiki:Raffc9).
