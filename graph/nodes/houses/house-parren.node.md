## Identity

House Parren was a noble house from the westerlands, now extinct. They blazoned their arms with per saltire: burgundy and white stripes, a black lion's head on gold. (wiki:House_Parren)

## Origins

House Parren was formed by the union of First Men and Andal nobles during the Coming of the Andals to the westerlands. (wiki:House_Parren.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands)

## Narrative Arc

In 43 AC, Lord Parren joined Prince Aegon the Uncrowned against King Maegor I Targaryen and fought in the Battle Beneath the Gods Eye (wiki:House_Parren.cite_ref-Rfab). A member of the house participated in the tourney at Ashford Meadow in 209 AC, where their arms were identified by Ser Duncan the Tall (wiki:House_Parren.cite_ref-Rthk). The house died out between 209 and 298 AC. (wiki:House_Parren)
