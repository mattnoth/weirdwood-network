## Origins

House Harroway was the third noble house to rule [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), with Lord [Lucas Harroway](wiki:Lucas_Harroway) receiving the title and lands after the extinction of [House Qoherys](wiki:House_Qoherys) in [37 AC](wiki:37_AC).(wiki:House_Harroway.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Two years later, Prince [Maegor Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) took Lord Lucas's daughter [Alys](wiki:Alys_Harroway) as his [second wife](wiki:Polygamy) in a [Valyrian](wiki:Valyria) [wedding ceremony](wiki:Marriage) on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:House_Harroway.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aenys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:House_Harroway.cite_ref-Rtsotd.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The anger that resulted from the marriage led Maegor to chose five years of exile over setting Alys aside. Alys left [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) in [40 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), accompanying her husband to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) in exile.(wiki:House_Harroway.cite_ref-Rtsotd.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
Alys became a queen upon her return to Westeros in [42 AC](wiki:42_AC), after Maegor had claimed the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). In [43 AC](wiki:43_AC), Maegor named Lord Lucas as his new [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King). Later that year, Lucas fought on Maegor's side in the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye) in [43 AC](wiki:43_AC).(wiki:House_Harroway.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) It fell to Lord Lucas to govern the realm while Maegor focused on the completion of the construction of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:House_Harroway.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
In [44 AC](wiki:44_AC) House Harroway was wiped out by Maegor after Alys had given birth to a monstrosity. Maegor's third wife, Queen [Tyanna](wiki:Tyanna_of_the_Tower), had convinced him Alys had been having affairs, and provided the names of the men she had supposedly been sleeping with. This resulted in the execution of every Harroway Maegor could find, in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Harrenhal, and [Lord Harroway's Town](wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town).(wiki:House_Harroway.cite_ref-Rtsotd.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)(wiki:House_Harroway.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
Harrenhal passed to [House Towers](wiki:House_Towers_of_Harrenhal) after the destruction of the Harroways,(wiki:House_Harroway.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) while Lord Harroway's Town passed to Lord [Alton Butterwell](wiki:Alton_Butterwell), and the remainder of the Harroway holdings to Lord [Darnold Darry](wiki:Darnold_Darry).(wiki:House_Harroway.cite_ref-Rtsotd.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)
In [48 AC](wiki:48_AC), Queen Tyanna confessed to having [poisoned](wiki:Poison) Alys's child in the womb.(wiki:House_Harroway.cite_ref-Rtsotd.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)
