## Origins

House Footly sided with [the blacks](wiki:The_blacks) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). Their town was burnt by [dragonfire](wiki:Dragonfire) in the [Treasons of Tumbleton](wiki:Treasons_of_Tumbleton), however. [Lord Footly](wiki:Lord_Footly)'s garrison yielded to [the greens](wiki:The_greens), but were bound and beheaded by the victors.(wiki:House_Footly.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
