## Identity

House Mudd of Oldstones was a noble house from the riverlands, now extinct (wiki:House_Mudd). Their arms are a golden crown studded with emeralds on a red-brown field (wiki:House_Mudd).

## Origins

The Mudds once ruled as Kings of the Rivers and the Hills from a castle along the Blue Fork whose ruins are now called Oldstones, reigning for "a thousand years" as the last First Men dynasty to rule the Trident (wiki:House_Mudd). They were the most powerful and expansive of the First Men river king dynasties, claiming land as far south as the mouth of the Blackwater Rush (wiki:House_Mudd).

## Narrative Arc

Tristifer IV Mudd was a great king who won ninety-nine of his hundred battles. His allies included Edmure Tully and his sons. When seven Andal kings banded against him, he lost his final battle and died (wiki:House_Mudd). Tristifer V could not hold the kingdom and it was destroyed during the Andal invasion (wiki:House_Mudd). Marq Mudd, the "Mad Bard," later unsuccessfully rebelled against the Durrandons (wiki:House_Mudd). In ADWD, Young John Mudd and Lorimas Mudd serve in the Golden Company, though their descent from the ancient house is unconfirmed (wiki:House_Mudd).
