## Identity

House Hewett of Oakenshield is a noble house that rules over Oakenshield, one of the Shield Islands of the Reach. Lord Hewett's castle sits above the harbor of Lord Hewett's Town, a relatively large port. Their arms display an oaken escutcheon studded with iron on a field bendy of undulating blue and white. (wiki:House_Hewett.cite_ref-Raffc29)

## Allegiances

House Hewett was `SWORN_TO` House Tyrell before the ironborn invasion. After the Taking of the Shields, they fall under `OVERLORD_OF` House Greyjoy. (track_b: Overlords)

## Narrative Arc

In ASOS, Ser Glendon Hewett goes to Castle Black with Janos Slynt and Alliser Thorne and drags Jon Snow from his bed to accuse him of betrayal. (wiki:House_Hewett.cite_ref-Rasos69) In AFFC, Euron Greyjoy and his captains celebrate the Taking of the Shields in Lord Hewett's castle. Lord Humfrey Hewett is muzzled and tied to his chair during the feast, his wife and daughters forced to serve. His bastard daughter Falia Flowers becomes Euron's lover. Oakenshield is given to Nute the Barber. Lord Hewett is reported dead. (wiki:House_Hewett.cite_ref-Raffc29)
