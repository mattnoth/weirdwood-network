## Identity

House Humble is a lesser house from the Iron Islands, descended from thralls and salt wives (wiki:House_Humble.cite_ref-Raffc18). They may come from Great Wyk, as Adrack Humble has a rock wife and three salt wives on that isle (wiki:House_Humble.cite_ref-Radwd20). According to Adrack, Humbles often have twins (wiki:House_Humble.cite_ref-Radwd20).

## Narrative Arc

Members of House Humble attend the kingsmoot on Old Wyk. Will Humble supports Victarion Greyjoy and loses a bet, having to eat his boot. Quellon Humble allies with Euron Greyjoy and informs Victarion about three warlocks on Euron's ship Silence (wiki:House_Humble.cite_ref-Raffc18). Quellon is later seen at the feast in Lord Hewett's castle after the taking of the Shields (wiki:House_Humble.cite_ref-Raffc29). Adrack Humble, a one-armed raider, is part of the garrison at Moat Cailin; he kills Dagon Codd when Codd refuses Theon Greyjoy's offer of safe passage, but after surrendering, Adrack and his companions are flayed alive under Ramsay Bolton's orders (wiki:House_Humble.cite_ref-Radwd20). Burton Humble serves on Iron Victory when Victarion sails for Slaver's Bay (wiki:House_Humble.cite_ref-Radwd56).
