## Origins

In [237 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Rhaelle's four elder siblings had been betrothed: Prince [Duncan](wiki:Duncan_Targaryen) to the daughter of Lord [Lyonel Baratheon](wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon), Prince [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_II_Targaryen) to [Celia Tully](wiki:Celia_Tully), Princess [Shaera](wiki:Shaera_Targaryen) to [Luthor Tyrell](wiki:Luthor_Tyrell), and Prince [Daeron](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_V)) to [Olenna Redwyne](wiki:Olenna_Redwyne). It was Rhaelle's eldest brother, Duncan, who broke his betrothal first, by marrying [Jenny of Oldstones](wiki:Jenny_of_Oldstones) in [239 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). This greatly angered Lord Lyonel, who rebelled against his king. A trial by combat, and the word of King [Aegon V](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) ended Lord Lyonel's rebellion, with an end result a betrothal, of Rhaelle to Lord Lyonel's son and heir, [Ormund Baratheon](wiki:Ormund_Baratheon).(wiki:Rhaelle_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

To seal the bargain, Rhaelle was sent to [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), where she would serve as a cupbearer, and be a companion to Lord Lyonel's wife.(wiki:Rhaelle_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) In [245 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), the promise of King Aegon V was fulfilled, and Rhaelle and Ormund were wed. Their son, [Steffon](wiki:Steffon_Baratheon), was born the next year.(wiki:Rhaelle_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Rhaelle's marriage to Ormund would eventually be part of the end of the rule of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen). Rhaelle's grandchildren through Steffon would be [Robert](wiki:Robert_Baratheon), [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) and [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon).(wiki:Rhaelle_Targaryen.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) It would be young Lord Robert, who would eventually lead the [rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) against Rhaelle's nephew, King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen).
