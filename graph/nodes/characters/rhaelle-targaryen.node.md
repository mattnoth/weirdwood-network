## Identity

Princess Rhaelle Targaryen was the youngest child of King Aegon V Targaryen and Queen Betha Blackwood. She married Lord Ormund Baratheon and was the grandmother of Robert, Stannis, and Renly Baratheon (wiki:Rhaelle_Targaryen).

## Narrative Arc

When her eldest brother Duncan broke his betrothal to Lord Lyonel Baratheon's daughter by marrying Jenny of Oldstones in 239 AC, Lyonel rebelled. The rebellion ended with a trial by combat and a new pact: Rhaelle was betrothed to Ormund Baratheon. She was sent to Storm's End as a cupbearer and companion to Lord Lyonel's wife. In 245 AC they wed, and their son Steffon was born the following year (wiki:Rhaelle_Targaryen.cite_ref-twoiaf).

Through Rhaelle, the Baratheons descended from Aegon V and had a blood claim to the Iron Throne. The maesters used this to justify Robert's Rebellion, though Robert preferred to cite his warhammer. Maester Aemon remembered Rhaelle calling him "Uncle Maester" as a child, and briefly hoped Stannis might be the prince that was promised because of Rhaelle's Targaryen blood before concluding he had deceived himself (wiki:Rhaelle_Targaryen.cite_ref-Raffc35).
