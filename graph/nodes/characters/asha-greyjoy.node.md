## Origins

Asha was fairly unattractive in her youth. Her uncle, [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy), remembers Asha as a little girl swimming naked in the sea and playing with her doll.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-8) Asha's eldest siblings, [Rodrik](wiki:Rodrik_Greyjoy) and [Maron](wiki:Maron_Greyjoy), died during [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion), and her younger brother [Theon](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) departed for [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) to be [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s hostage and ward. Asha thus became Lord [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy)'s only remaining child in [Pyke](wiki:Pyke).(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-7) Asha had her first [flowering](wiki:Flowering) during the war.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-8)

Asha's mother, Lady [Alannys Harlaw](wiki:Alannys_Harlaw), fostered five boys at Pyke after Theon's departure. Asha experimented with [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley), the boy closest to Asha in age, during puberty, although his feeling were stronger than hers.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-9) Tris was sent away to [Blacktyde](wiki:Blacktyde) after Maester [Qalen](wiki:Qalen) caught the youths together.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-10)

Asha grew into an attractive and bold young woman,(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10) and Balon effectively raised his wild and headstrong daughter as his heir.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) However, Asha preferred [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers), the seat of her uncle [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw), to Pyke. She lost her virtue at age 16 to a sailor from [Lys](wiki:Lys).(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-11) In [293 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) or [294 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) Asha sailed *[Black Wind](wiki:Black_Wind)* on a trading voyage which included stops at [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle), [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport), and the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor), where she introduced peaches to one of her lovers, [Qarl the Maid](wiki:Qarl_the_Maid).(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-10) Asha was away raiding Lysene [pirates](wiki:Pirate) at the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) when her father exiled her uncle, [Euron Crow's Eye](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), from the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) in [297 AC](wiki:297_AC) for having impregnated Victarion's [third wife](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy's_third_wife).(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-9) Asha has visited [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) in her travels.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

## Appearances & Description

As a child, Asha was a skinny girl with knob knees and a face full of pimples.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Now in her mid-twenties, Asha is lean and long legged, with dark eyes and black hair cut short. Her face is thin, with a big, sharp nose, and wind-chafed skin. On her neck is a faded pink scar.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Asha's brother, [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy), believes her [hawk](wiki:Hawk)-like nose(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) is too big and sharp for her small face, but he feels that her wicked smile makes up for it.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Asha is a wild and headstrong young woman,(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) raised by her mother to be bold.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) There is a boldness to the way she walks; part saunter, part sway.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) She possesses a sharp wit,(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) and has no problem with holding her own against men, both in a verbal battle(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) and in a physical fight.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Asha enjoys [ale](wiki:Ale)(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) and [wine](wiki:Wine).(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Defying the classical [gender roles](wiki:Gender_and_sexuality) of [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) culture, Asha was appalled early on by the idea of becoming a [lord](wiki:Lord)'s wife.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) After her older two brothers died and her younger brother was taken hostage, Asha's father [Balon](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) began to see her as his heir, treating her like he would a son.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Asha often thinks of herself as "the [kraken](wiki:Kraken)'s daughter".(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Asha's attire includes a brown quilted tunic,(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3) breeches of black(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) or green wool,(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-4) a studded belt,(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) and salt-stained high leather boots.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-5) She sometimes wears a sleeveless leather jerkin half-unlaced,(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-6) and she also owns a green leather jerkin covered with overlapping plates of steel.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6) Asha prefers throwing axes,(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7) which she has been practicing with since she was just a little girl,(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-7) but she also keeps a dirk with her.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

Asha personally leads her men into battle.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8) She captains her own ship, *[Black Wind](wiki:Black_Wind)*,(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) and has the devotion of her crew,(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4) who take a perverse pride in the deeds of their female captain. According to Asha, half of them love her like a daughter, while the other half want to sleep with her.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5) Regardless, Asha loves her men,(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-9) and is willing to die for them, as they are for her.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-6) Asha is also well liked by the servants of [Pyke](wiki:Pyke) and people of [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport).(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9) Asha is not devoted to the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God).(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

While her counterpart in the television series *[Game of Thrones](wiki:Game_of_Thrones)*, Yara Greyjoy, is bisexual, [George R. R. Martin](wiki:George_R._R._Martin) has confirmed that Asha is not.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-13)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Upon [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy)'s return to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands), Asha is reported to be on [Great Wyk](wiki:Great_Wyk) taking messages for her father [Balon](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2) After sailing back to [Pyke](wiki:Pyke) with Lord [Gorold Goodbrother](wiki:Gorold_Goodbrother), Asha passes herself off as Esgred, a shipwright's wife in [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport), and flirts with Theon to learn his nature. Theon's naive arrogance amuses her, but Theon grows angry at Asha's deception and her position. At a feast that night at Pyke, Asha takes the place of honor beside their father, making Theon feel cheated.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-11)

During the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Asha commands thirty longships for Balon's invasion of the [north](wiki:North).(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-12) After stopping at [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers) to visit her mother, [Alannys Harlaw](wiki:Alannys_Harlaw),(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-12) Asha leads the [taking of Deepwood Motte](wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-13) After the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell), Theon sends [Stygg](wiki:Stygg) to request reinforcements from his sister.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Asha travels through the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) to visit Theon at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), but she refuses to stay and help him defend the castle since it is so far inland.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) The ten men left by Asha eventually abandon Theon prior to the [battle at Winterfell](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell).(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Balon, [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North), is reported to have fallen to his death at Pyke.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Asha's uncle, [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy), is outraged at the prospect of a woman ruling the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) or of his brother, [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), claiming the throne, so the priest calls for a [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3)

Asha returns to the isles after her father's death to claim the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair), bringing with her the captive Lady [Sybelle Glover](wiki:Sybelle_Glover) and her children, [Gawen](wiki:Gawen_Glover) and infant [Erena Glover](wiki:Erena_Glover). At [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers), Asha informs her uncle, Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw), that she searched for Theon at Winterfell after it was [sacked](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell) but was unable to identify him amongst the bodies.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-13)(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-10) Most of her support comes from [House Harlaw](wiki:House_Harlaw), the house of her mother, Lady [Alannys Harlaw](wiki:Alannys_Harlaw), but Asha is also supported by [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley) (whose marriage proposal she refuses), Lord [Baelor Blacktyde](wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde), Lord [Meldred Merlyn](wiki:Meldred_Merlyn), and [Harmund Sharp](wiki:Harmund_Sharp).(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Rodrik offers to name Asha his heir to Ten Towers, though not to become head of his house. She refuses, desiring instead the throne of her father.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-14)

On Old Wyk, Asha realizes that she does not have as much support as she had hoped. She instead offers to share rule and back her uncle [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy), the [Lord Captain of the Iron Fleet](wiki:Lord_Captain_of_the_Iron_Fleet), if he will make her his [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King). Victarion refuses, as no King of the Isles had ever had one, much less a woman, and he disagrees with her plans to end the war.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-11)

At the kingsmoot, Asha challenges the ancient and obese [Erik Ironmaker](wiki:Erik_Ironmaker) to rise from his chair, his support crumbling when he cannot. When she makes her own claim, her champions are Tris Botley, Ser [Harras Harlaw](wiki:Harras_Harlaw), and [Qarl the Maid](wiki:Qarl_the_Maid). Unlike the others, who offer valuables, her gifts are cobblestones, pinecones and turnips. Asha tells the ironborn these gifts demonstrate this is all they've won so far by fighting the [North](wiki:North), losing fathers and sons for these "treasures". She offers them a chance for land, peace and victory. With the proposal to make peace and an alliance with the north in exchange for [Sea Dragon Point](wiki:Sea_Dragon_Point) and the [Stony Shore](wiki:Stony_Shore), Asha achieves a surprising amount of support. The crowd, however, ultimately favors her uncle, Euron Crow's Eye,(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) and Asha flees with her crew.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

While at [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers) following the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), Asha learns her uncle, King [Euron](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), married her to [Erik Ironmaker](wiki:Erik_Ironmaker) after she fled [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk), using a seal to represent her at the wedding. Asha curses this but admires Euron's cunning: this move prevents Asha from offering a marriage to another lord and winning his support. She returns to [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) with four longships, less than two hundred men, and Lady Glover, whose children remain at Ten Towers.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-11) After the [fall of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin), [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) sends a strip of [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy)'s flayed skin from [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) to Deepwood, warning Asha to leave the [north](wiki:North) or share her brother's fate.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-12)

Asha sleeps with [Qarl the Maid](wiki:Qarl_the_Maid) and refuses [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley)'s recommendation to flee and become a merchant, although Tris does remind her of the story of [Torgon the Latecomer](wiki:Torgon_Greyiron). When Deepwood comes under attack by King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s army, Asha kills a [Flint](wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains) scout and then flees with her ironmen toward their ships on the [Bay of Ice](wiki:Bay_of_Ice).(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-13) They are overwhelmed in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood), however, and defeated in the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-14) [Alysane Mormont](wiki:Alysane_Mormont) burns or captures Asha's longships.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

Asha is taken captive and continues with Stannis's host during their [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell). Alysane guards the king's prize, who is fettered in a covered [wayn](wiki:Wayn) and thinks her ankle is broken.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2) Ser [Justin Massey](wiki:Justin_Massey) courts Asha, who thinks the [knight](wiki:Knight) wants to wed her to claim the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands), and [Morgan Liddle](wiki:Morgan_Liddle) apologizes to Asha for having insulted her during the battle.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-3)

A snow storm sets in during their march, however, and the army slows to a halt at a [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village) approximately three days ride from Winterfell, with few provisions left.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-4) [Queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) such as Ser [Clayton Suggs](wiki:Clayton_Suggs) call for Asha to be sacrificed to [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor) in an attempt to thwart the snows, and she witnesses the burning of [Peasebury](wiki:House_Peasebury) men accused of cannibalism.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2) Asha and Clayton are met by [Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris) of the [Iron Bank of Braavos](wiki:Iron_Bank_of_Braavos), who has arrived from Deepwood with an escort made of Asha's surviving crew, including Qarl and Tris.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-3) Also with them are "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)" and Theon, who have escaped from Ramsay at Winterfell,(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) but Asha barely recognizes her tortured brother.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-4)

### The Winds of Winter

Asha is distressed when she sees what [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) did to her brother. Brought before Stannis and a chained Theon, she offers a ransom for her brother. Stannis refuses, as his northern allies would then abandon him. While the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) want Stannis to burn Theon for what he allegedly did to [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) after the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell), Asha urges the king to behead Theon personally, before a [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree), as Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) would have done.(wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-21)
