## Identity

Asha Greyjoy is the daughter of Lord Balon Greyjoy and Alannys Harlaw, and the only surviving daughter of House Greyjoy. She is a POV character in AFFC and ADWD, captain of the longship Black Wind, and a claimant to the Seastone Chair (wiki:Asha_Greyjoy).

## Origins

Born 275–276 AC at Pyke (track_b: Born). Daughter of Balon Greyjoy (track_b: Father) and Alannys Harlaw (track_b: Mother).

## Appearances & Description

Asha is lean, long-legged, and dark-haired, with a sharp tongue and an ironborn warrior's confidence. She dresses practically and fights with a dirk and throwing axes.

## Narrative Arc

When Theon returns to the Iron Islands in ACOK, Asha disguises herself as "Esgred" to test her brother, who does not recognize her (wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok24). She commands the capture of Deepwood Motte while Theon takes Winterfell. After Balon's death, Asha puts herself forward at the kingsmoot on Old Wyk but loses to Euron and his dragon horn (wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc19). She retreats to Deepwood Motte, where Stannis Baratheon's forces attack and capture her (wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd26). She is held prisoner during Stannis's march on Winterfell. During captivity, Euron has her forcibly married to old Erik Ironmaker to neutralize her claim (wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd42). Tycho Nestoris of the Iron Bank ransoms her and other ironborn to serve as escorts (wiki:Asha_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd62).
