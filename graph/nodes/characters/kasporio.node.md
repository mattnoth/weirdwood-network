## Appearances & Description

Kasporio is slim and elegant, with a pointed beard. He wields a slender [bravo](wiki:Bravo)'s blade with a jeweled hilt, and wears a slashed pink doublet.(wiki:Kasporio.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Kasporio, along with the rest of the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons), goes over to the [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai)'i when [Brown Ben Plumm](wiki:Ben_Plumm) thinks [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s cause is lost.(wiki:Kasporio.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Kasporio.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

When [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) writes out hundreds of documents promising large sums of [gold](wiki:Gold) to several high ranking members in the company, Kasporio is promised 10,000 [golden dragons](wiki:Currency).(wiki:Kasporio.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) The bravo reports that three [slave](wiki:Slave)-catchers are searching the camps for the missing Tyrion and [Penny](wiki:Penny).(wiki:Kasporio.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

### The Winds of Winter

As the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen) resumes, Kasporio goes with [Brown Ben Plumm](wiki:Brown_Ben_Plumm) to visit [Malazza](wiki:Malazza), the Girl General. After they return he attends the meeting Ben has called. During the meeting he remarks that Malazza and [Gorzhak zo Eraz](wiki:Gorzhak_zo_Eraz), whom he calls "Pudding Face", are no better than one another.(wiki:Kasporio.cite_ref-7)
