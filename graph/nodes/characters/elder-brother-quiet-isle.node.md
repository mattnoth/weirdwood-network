## Identity

The Elder Brother is the leader of the community on the Quiet Isle. He was formerly a knight who served House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiances, qualifier: formerly). He holds the titles of Ser and Elder Brother (track_b: Titles).

## Origins

Born circa 255–256 AC (track_b: Born, qualifier: age 44). Of Rivermen culture (track_b: Culture).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiances; formerly). `SWORN_TO` Faith of the Seven (track_b: Allegiances). `SWORN_TO` Quiet Isle (track_b: Allegiances).

## Narrative Arc

Once a knight who fought in battle, the Elder Brother experienced a spiritual transformation and joined the community on the Quiet Isle. He speaks to Brienne of Tarth about the Hound and confirms that Sandor Clegane is "at rest" (wiki:Elder_Brother_(Quiet_Isle).cite_ref-affc-31). He leads the religious community with wisdom and compassion, offering shelter and counsel to travelers.
