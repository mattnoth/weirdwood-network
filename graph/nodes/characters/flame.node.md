## Appearances & Description

Flame was a blood bay with a bright eye and a long fiery mane. She was a big, swift courser, and had some [Dornish sand steed](wiki:Dornish_sand_steed) blood in her for endurance. Flame was a magnificent horse, with long, clean limbs. She had been bred for beauty and speed.(wiki:Flame.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Origins

Lady [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber) attempted to give Flame to Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) as a gift for his service and as an apology. The [hedge knight](wiki:Hedge_knight) refused, however, saying Flame was too good for him.(wiki:Flame.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
