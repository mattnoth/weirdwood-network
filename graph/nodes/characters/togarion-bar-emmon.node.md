## Origins

During the [Andal invasion](wiki:Andal_invasion), Togarion the Terrible established a small kingdom of his own north of the [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay). However, he soon found himself hard-pressed by the [Darklyn](wiki:House_Darklyn) king of [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale).(wiki:Togarion_Bar_Emmon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_andals_in_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Across the bay, [Qarlton III Durrandon](wiki:Qarlton_III_Durrandon), the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King), reconquered [Massey's Hook](wiki:Massey's_Hook) following four generations of war, having slain the Hook's last king [Josua Massey](wiki:Josua_Massey). Sensing weakness to the south, Togarion wed Josua Softspear's daughter and crossed Blackwater Bay with all his power to establish a new kingdom on the Hook. He built his own castle of [Sharp Point](wiki:Sharp_Point) at the Hook's end, whilst driving the stormlanders from [Stonedance](wiki:Stonedance) and installing his wife's brother as a puppet [lord](wiki:Lord).(wiki:Togarion_Bar_Emmon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_andals_in_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

While Torgarion the Terrible was the first Andal warlord to invade the [Kingdom of the Storm](wiki:Stormlands), others soon followed that brought graver concerns to Qarlton III than the reloss of Massey's Hook.(wiki:Togarion_Bar_Emmon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_andals_in_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) In time, the Storm Kings would reconquer Togarion's small kingdom, and Sharp Point and Stonedance became vassals of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End).(wiki:Togarion_Bar_Emmon.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
