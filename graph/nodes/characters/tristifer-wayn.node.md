## Origins

During the Faith Militant uprising, Lord Tristifer took up arms on behalf of the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aenys_I)) and the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) against the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).
