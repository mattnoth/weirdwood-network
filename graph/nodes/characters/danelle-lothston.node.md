## Origins

When Lord [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to [Aerys I Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_I_Targaryen), marched on [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) to quash the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion), Lady Danelle was among the [riverlords](wiki:Riverlords) who came in support, marching in great strength. She led her men herself, wearing black armor.(wiki:Danelle_Lothston.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Lady Danelle turned to the [black arts](wiki:Magic) during the reign of [Maekar I Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen), causing madness and chaos and the downfall of the Lothston line.(wiki:Danelle_Lothston.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_the_lords_of_harrenhal.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

## Appearances & Description

Danelle had long red hair. She wore tight-fitting black armor.(wiki:Danelle_Lothston.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Allegedly, she was a [magic](wiki:Magic) practitioner.(wiki:Danelle_Lothston.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_the_lords_of_harrenhal.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) After her time, misbehaving children were told a story that on [moonless](wiki:Moon) nights, giant [bats](wiki:Bat) would fly from Harrenhal and take the bad children back to Mad Danelle for her cookpots.(wiki:Danelle_Lothston.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Other stories were told about mad Lady Lothston bathing in blood, and presiding over feasts of human flesh.(wiki:Danelle_Lothston.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

According to [Gendry](wiki:Gendry), the old blacksmith [Ben Blackthumb](wiki:Ben_Blackthumb) served the [Whents](wiki:House_Whent), as well as the last Lord Lothston before them.(wiki:Danelle_Lothston.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Gendry probably errs, as the last Lothston to hold Harrenhal was Lady Danelle.

### A Feast for Crows

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) carries a Lothston shield taken from [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), and is told by Ser [Illifer the Penniless](wiki:Illifer_the_Penniless) that his great-great grandfather took part in the destruction of Lady Danelle and the last of the Lothston line.(wiki:Danelle_Lothston.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) To have the shield repainted, Brienne gives the shield to [the sister of the captain of the guards](wiki:Captain's_sister) at [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale), who tells Brienne her mother would frighten her with tales of Mad Danelle.(wiki:Danelle_Lothston.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Upon returning to Harrenhal, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) recalls hearing stories about mad Lady Lothston bathing in blood and presiding over feasts of human flesh.(wiki:Danelle_Lothston.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
