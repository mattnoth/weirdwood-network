## Origins

[Named after](wiki:Named_after) Lord [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark).(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) Rickard Karstark fought for his son, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), when the [north](wiki:North) rebelled against the Mad King, [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). He participated in the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

Rickard visited [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) with his daughter, [Alys](wiki:Alys_Karstark), when she was six. He expected her to charm Eddard's son [Robb](wiki:Robb_Stark), despite Alys's age, in hopes of arranging a [betrothal](wiki:Betrothal).(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Rickard Karstark](wiki:Category:Images_of_Rickard_Karstark)*

Rickard is gaunt and tall, with a thick grey(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) beard and hair loose past his shoulders.(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He has a narrow face.(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Lord Karstark is a stern and vengeful man.(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

A fierce warrior,(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Rawoiafrickard_karstark.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Rickard wears black mail(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) and wields a lance on horseback.(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) He possesses a black wool surcoat emblazoned with the [Karstark](wiki:House_Karstark) sunburst.(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord Rickard brings his forces to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) when [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls his banners. He is accompanied by his three sons, [Harrion](wiki:Harrion_Karstark), [Eddard](wiki:Eddard_Karstark), and [Torrhen](wiki:Torrhen_Karstark).(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) At [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin) he occupies the [Drunkard's Tower](wiki:Drunkard's_Tower).(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) When the northern host splits at the [Twins](wiki:Twins), Rickard stays with Robb. He takes part in the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood), but Rickard's younger sons, Torrhen and Eddard, are killed by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) during the fighting.(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Following the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), Rickard prays to the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods) in the [godswood](wiki:Godswood) of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun). During Robb's war council, Rickard objects to ransoming the captive Jaime back to his father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister). Rickard swears fealty to Robb when Lord [Greatjon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber_(Greatjon)) declares him [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Clash of Kings

Rickard storms out of the Great Hall of Riverrun when King Robb explains his terms for peace with the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Rickard accompanies Robb when the king campaigns in the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands). During the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross), the Lord of [Karhold](wiki:Karhold) rides down Ser [Stafford Lannister](wiki:Stafford_Lannister), the commander of a [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) host, and kills him with his lance.(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) Rickard and [Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover) then lead the [raiding along the westerlands coast](wiki:Raiding_along_the_westerlands_coast).(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Storm of Swords

After learning of the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell), [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) releases Jaime Lannister from captivity at Riverrun.(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) When Robb's host returns from the westerlands, Lord Karstark considers the actions of Robb's mother to be treason.(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Lord Rickard, overcome with grief and anger, sends the Karstark horse from Riverrun, promising the hand of his daughter, [Alys](wiki:Alys_Karstark), to anyone who recovers Jaime for him.(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4)(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Afterward, Rickard leads a small group of Karstark men to murder [Willem Lannister](wiki:Willem_Lannister) and [Tion Frey](wiki:Tion_Frey), two of Robb's captives from the Whispering Wood, in their cells at Riverrun. Two guards, [Delp](wiki:Delp) and [Elwood](wiki:Elwood), are slain by Karstarks.(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5)

Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) advises Robb to keep Rickard as a [hostage](wiki:Hostage), but the king declares Lord Karstark a traitor and sentences him to die. Following the teachings of his father, the late Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), Robb carries out the sentence himself, beheading Rickard before the [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree) in the [godswood of Riverrun](wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun). With his last breath Rickard curses Robb, denouncing him as his king and calling him a [kinslayer](wiki:Kinslaying), citing the blood ties between [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) and [Karstark](wiki:House_Karstark) in hopes of saving his own life.(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Kinslaying_16-0) Rickard is succeeded by his sole surviving son, Harrion.(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-6) [Lothar Frey](wiki:Lothar_Frey) refers to Rickard's fate as "swift sure justice".(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

The [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) capture Jaime near [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool). Unaware of Rickard's death, [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat) plans to return Jaime to the Karstarks and thereby marry Alys and claim Karhold.(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) instead leaves [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) to Vargo when he departs.(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) When Roose arrives at the Twins, most of the remaining northmen in his command are [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton) and Karstarks.(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Roose then betrays Robb in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

### A Feast for Crows

The Iron Throne holds Rickard's heir, Harrion, captive at Maidenpool.(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister) had vowed not to cut his hair until he had avenged the death of his father, Stafford, at Rickard's hands. Daven decides to keep his hair long even after Rickard's execution.(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

With Harrion in captivity, Rickard's uncle [Arnolf](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark), castellan of Karhold, attempts to claim by the castle by marrying his son [Cregan](wiki:Cregan_Karstark) to Rickard's daughter, Alys. The maiden flees to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) first, however.(wiki:Rickard_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
