## Identity

Prince Oberyn Nymeros Martell, known as the Red Viper, is a member of House Martell and the younger brother of Doran Martell, Prince of Dorne. He has eight bastard daughters called the Sand Snakes, the four youngest by his paramour Ellaria Sand (wiki:Oberyn_Martell).

## Origins

Born 257–258 AC in Sunspear. Son of the Princess of Dorne (track_b: Born, Mother).

## Appearances & Description

Tall, slender, graceful, and fit with a lined, saturnine face. Thin eyebrows, black "viper" eyes, sharp nose. Lustrous black hair with a few silver streaks, receding from his brow in a widow's peak. Quick as a water snake and very athletic. Fierce, fearsome reputation. Doran describes him as "deadly, dangerous, unpredictable" — "no man dared tread on him." Tywin calls him half-mad. Witty and sharp of tongue (wiki:Oberyn_Martell).

## Narrative Arc

In his youth, Oberyn studied at the Citadel, forging several links before growing bored. He traveled with sellswords, fighting for the Second Sons in the Disputed Lands. He was caught in bed with Lord Edgar Yronwood's paramour and fought a duel, wounding Edgar with a cut that festered — earning his "Red Viper" nickname for suspected poisoned blade. Oberyn's mother toured potential matches for him and his sister Elia, but Tywin Lannister rebuffed their proposals. In ASOS, Oberyn arrives at King's Landing seeking justice for Elia's murder. He champions Tyrion Lannister in trial by combat against Gregor Clegane. Oberyn wounds Clegane repeatedly with a poisoned spear but is killed when Clegane crushes his skull after Oberyn demands a confession. His death galvanizes the Sand Snakes and plunges Dorne into political crisis (wiki:Oberyn_Martell).
