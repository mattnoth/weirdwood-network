## Origins

### Early life

As a child, Oberyn Martell played the [shoulder-riding game](wiki:Shoulder-riding_game) in the pools of the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens). His older brother [Doran](wiki:Doran_Martell) had long left the pools by the time Oberyn was old enough to play, but would watch him while visiting [their mother](wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran)). Oberyn fought fiercely and quickly, often toppling boys much bigger than himself.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-3)

In his youth, Oberyn was fostered at [Sandstone](wiki:Sandstone).(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) When in his teens, Oberyn traveled with [his mother](wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran)), the ruling [Princess of Dorne](wiki:Princess_of_Dorne), and his sister, Princess [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell), and met multiple potential [marriage](wiki:Marriage) candidates for his sister, visiting castles at [Starfall](wiki:Starfall), the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor), [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands), and [Crakehall](wiki:Crakehall). Oberyn spent his time mocking Elia's suitors. The last castle they visited was [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), where they arrived shortly after the death of Lady [Joanna Lannister](wiki:Joanna_Lannister) in childbirth. According to Oberyn, his mother and Joanna had planned to wed either Oberyn to [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), or Elia to [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), or both. Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), however, was unreceptive of the suggestion, stating that his daughter would marry Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen). He offered the newborn [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) as a husband for Elia instead, which was seen as an insult.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-3)

At the age of sixteen, Oberyn was found in bed with the [paramour](wiki:Paramour) of Lord [Edgar Yronwood](wiki:Edgar_Yronwood), so the lord challenged him to a duel. The duel was to first blood, given the prince's youth and high birth, and both took cuts. Edgar's wounds festered and killed him. Oberyn has been known as the "Red Viper" ever since by friends and foes alike, due to rumors that he fought the duel with a [poisoned](wiki:Poison) blade.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-13) Afterward, Oberyn was sent to Oldtown and then to [Lys](wiki:Lys) in temporary exile (though none called it such) in order for [House Martell](wiki:House_Martell) to make peace with [House Yronwood](wiki:House_Yronwood), and Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) was sent as a ward to [Yronwood](wiki:Yronwood).(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-winds_16-1)

Oberyn traveled extensively in the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities), where he learned much about poisons and perhaps even darker arts.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-14) He had studied at the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel), going so far as to forge six links of a [maester](wiki:Maester)'s chain before he grew bored.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-15) He soldiered in the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands) across the narrow sea, riding with the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons) for a time before forming his own company. It is said that he beds both men and women, and has begotten eight bastard daughters, called the [Sand Snakes](wiki:Sand_Snakes).(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-16) Oberyn, however, does not let his children grow up wanting: he takes responsibility for them, and actively seeks to help raise all his children (something few lords do when it comes to their [bastard](wiki:Bastard) offspring). He teaches them how to defend themselves and allows them great independence in growing up.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-2)

Prince Oberyn was unhorsed by Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) in [Lord Steffon's tourney](wiki:Lord_Steffon's_tourney) at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End).(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-24) He was also present during the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) in [281 AC](wiki:281_AC), where he danced with Lady [Ashara Dayne](wiki:Ashara_Dayne).(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### Baratheon Era

Oberyn had a close relationship with his sister [Elia](wiki:Elia_Martell). It is currently unknown where Oberyn was during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-25)

During the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing), Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) raped and murdered Elia and also killed her son, Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)). Another [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) knight, Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch) killed her daughter, Princess [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Rhaegar)). After hearing of these deaths, Oberyn attempted to raise Dorne for the exiled Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen). The new [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), traveled to Sunspear in the year following the war, and was able to keep the peace, but Oberyn has desired revenge ever since.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-17) After the war, Oberyn seldom left Dorne.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Oberyn is primarily responsible for giving modern revitalization to the ongoing feud between his House and the [Tyrells](wiki:House_Tyrell), because he accidentally crippled [Willas Tyrell](wiki:Willas_Tyrell) in a [tourney](wiki:Tourney). Oberyn struck Willas's breastplate clean, but the boy's foot caught in a stirrup as he fell and his horse fell on top of him. Oberyn sent his own maester to help treat Willas, who holds no grudge against Oberyn. The two correspond via [raven](wiki:Raven) message as both share a passion for [horses](wiki:Horse).(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-18)

Oberyn and his brother [Doran](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), worked in secret for years planning on bringing an end to the reign of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) and destroying [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-3) Oberyn traveled to Braavos, where Viserys and [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) were living with Ser [Willem Darry](wiki:Willem_Darry). Oberyn and Willem signed a [secret marriage pact](wiki:Secret_marriage_pact), with the [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos) as witness, promising Viserys the hand of Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) in marriage, in return for Dorne's help in reclaiming the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) from [House Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

Oberyn eventually granted [knighthood](wiki:Knight) on his squire, Ser [Daemon Sand](wiki:Daemon_Sand).(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-winds_16-2) Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) choose to be knighted by Lord [Anders Yronwood](wiki:Anders_Yronwood) instead of Oberyn.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) When Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell) moved to the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens) for health reasons, he trusted Oberyn to look after Sunspear for him. Oberyn reported to his brother twice a fortnight.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Appearances & Description

Oberyn has the features of a salty [Dornishman](wiki:Dornishman). He is a tall, slender, graceful, and fit man,(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) and has a lined and saturnine face with thin eyebrows, black "viper" eyes and a sharp nose.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) His hair is lustrous and black, with only a few silver streaks, and recedes from his brow in a widow's peak.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2) Oberyn is a renowned warrior,(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-3) quick as a water [snake](wiki:Snake)(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) and very athletic.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Oberyn has always been fierce.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) He has a fearsome and notorious reputation.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-4)(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) According to Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), Oberyn has always been half-mad.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Oberyn's brother [Doran](wiki:Doran_Martell) has described him as "deadly, dangerous, [and] unpredictable", stating that "no man dared tread on him".(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) However, Doran also claims Oberyn is rash.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2) A witty man who is sharp of tongue,(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-5)(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2) Oberyn has claimed to have been "a monstrous young fellow" in his youth.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) Oberyn is a bloodthirsty man, who for years has sought revenge for his sister [Elia](wiki:Elia_Martell), with whom he had been inseparable as children.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-6) He despises Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), calling him the "Fat Flower of [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden)".(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-7)(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-3)

Rumor has it that Oberyn is [bisexual](wiki:Gender_and_sexuality).(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-8) His one-time [squire](wiki:Squire),(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Ser [Daemon Sand](wiki:Daemon_Sand), is rumored to have been one of his lovers.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-winds_16-0) Additionally, it is said Oberyn has "begotten [bastard](wiki:Bastard) girls all over Dorne", though in reality only four of his eight daughters are Dornish, born to his long-time [paramour](wiki:Paramour), [Ellaria Sand](wiki:Ellaria_Sand).(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) He fearlessly claimed and raised them,(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_oberyn_martell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) arming them so they would be able to defend themselves(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) and allowing them the freedom to choose between [wedding](wiki:Wedding), or taking pleasure wherever it can be found.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1)

Oberyn has an interest in fine [horseflesh](wiki:Horse).(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-9) His [sand steed](wiki:Sand_steed) is a stallion "black as sin", with a mane and tail the color of fire.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-10) Oberyn has been known to wear a cloak of pale red [silk](wiki:Silk), an armored shirt of overlapping rows of glittering [copper](wiki:Copper) disks, and a high [gilded](wiki:Gold) helm with a copper sun on its brow.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-11) His round [steel](wiki:Steel) shield is brightly polished, and displays the Martell sun-and-spear in red gold, yellow gold, white gold, and copper.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-12)(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2)

At his trial-by-combat duel with Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane), Oberyn fights lightly armored — greaves, vambraces, gorget, spaulder, and a steel codpiece over supple leather and flowing silks, with his gleaming copper scales worn over a byrnie. His visor is removed, leaving his helm effectively a half-helm lacking even a nasal — a deliberate trade of protection for speed against the Mountain's heavy plate. (Tyrion X, ASOS — `sources/chapters/asos/asos-tyrion-10.md:173`)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Prince Oberyn comes to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to claim his brother [Doran](wiki:Doran_Martell)'s [small council](wiki:Small_council) seat, and to finally receive justice for the death of his sister [Elia](wiki:Elia_Martell),(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-19) as had been promised to the Martells by acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) The day Oberyn leaves Sunspear, he reminds Doran of how he used to topple boys much bigger than himself while playing the [shoulder-riding game](wiki:Shoulder-riding_game) in the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens), and promises Doran that he will do it again.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-4) In King's Landing, whenever Oberyn happens to see Tyrion, he asks when the "justice would be served".(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) However, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) plans to lie to Oberyn and claim that the now-deceased Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch) was responsible for the deaths of Elia, her daughter [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Rhaegar)), and her son [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)), although Amory had only killed Rhaenys during the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

Oberyn is among those Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) considers as a possible candidate in marriage for his daughter, the widowed Queen [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister).(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) Oberyn gives King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) a [red gold](wiki:Red_gold) [scorpion](wiki:Scorpion) brooch for the [royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding).(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

After Tyrion is accused of Joffrey's murder, Oberyn is one of his judges. The day before a judgment is to be pronounced, Oberyn offers to be Tyrion's champion in a [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat) against Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane), if Tyrion tells who is guilty of the murder of Elia. Tyrion denies any involvement of his father, Tywin, but tells Oberyn that Amory killed Rhaenys and Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) killed Elia and Aegon.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-4) When Tyrion realizes the witness testimonies at the trial are sure to lead to a guilty verdict, he demands a trial by battle. To the consternation of the court, Oberyn announces that he will be Tyrion's champion. Tywin is angered, but grants Tyrion's request.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-4)

The next morning, Tyrion is given leave to visit his champion. Oberyn plans to fight with a spear and use only light armor. He specifically warns Tyrion not to touch the weapon. He is confident, but Tyrion is not sure of his choice of weapon. Oberyn invites Tyrion to go to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) after the trial and talks about crowning [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon). He then changes the subject to the time he visited [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) with his [mother](wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran)) and sister Elia. His mother and Tyrion's mother [Joanna Lannister](wiki:Joanna_Lannister) were friends, and were planning on marrying Jaime to Elia and/or Cersei to Oberyn. However, when they arrived at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), Joanna had died, and Tywin refused both suggestions and said that Cersei was intended for Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen). Because Elia later married Rhaegar, Oberyn thinks that this is why Tywin had Elia killed. Oberyn proclaims that today he will have justice for Elia and her children.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-5)

In the trial by battle, Oberyn wounds Gregor several times by finding chinks in his armor, and severs Gregor's hamstring after the latter tires from chasing him. He breaks his spear in Gregor's belly, but rather than killing him, Oberyn insists that Gregor confess to Elia's murder. Changing the tide of the battle, Gregor trips Oberyn and smashes his armored fist into Oberyn's unprotected face, destroying his teeth and knocking out his eyes, then smashing in the prince's skull. Before killing Oberyn, however, Gregor announces his guilt in the death of Elia and Aegon for all to hear.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-6)

### A Feast for Crows

Oberyn achieves posthumous vengeance, as Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) dies a slow and agonizing death from [poison](wiki:Poison).(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-5)(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

Oberyn's daughters, the [Sand Snakes](wiki:Sand_Snakes), seek vengeance for his death; Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell) orders [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah) to place them under arrest to keep the peace.(wiki:Oberyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-6)

### A Dance with Dragons
