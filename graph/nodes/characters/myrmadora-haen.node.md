## Origins

Myrmadora was the daughter of [Torreo Haen](wiki:Torreo_Haen), a [Lyseni](wiki:Lys) [magister](wiki:Magister). She attended the [Maiden's Day Ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball) wearing a gown of translucent blue-green silk that matched her eyes, with only a jeweled girdle underneath. The dress shocked others, but the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) barred her from King [Aegon III](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen)'s throne room until she changed clothes.(wiki:Myrmadora_Haen.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

In [135 AC](wiki:135_AC), Myrmadora was [poisoned](wiki:Poison) along with her mother, sister(s), father, her father's siblings, and her father's supporters at the feast he held to celebrate his becoming [First Magister](wiki:First_Magister) of Lys.(wiki:Myrmadora_Haen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
