## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), where Ser Philip fights for the combined [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister)-[Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) army, he kills Lord [Bryce Caron](wiki:Bryce_Caron), the Lord of [Nightsong](wiki:Nightsong), in single combat. After the battle the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) names Philip the new Lord of Nightsong and gives him all the lands, rights and incomes of [House Caron](wiki:House_Caron), forming [House Foote of Nightsong](wiki:House_Foote_of_Nightsong).(wiki:Philip_Foote.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Storm of Swords

Philip, the new [Lord of the Marches](wiki:Lord_of_the_Marches),(wiki:Philip_Foote.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) attends [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark) of [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Philip_Foote.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He is among the gathered [nobility](wiki:Nobility) who welcome the [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) party headed by Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Philip_Foote.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Philip attends the [royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) and King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). He is called as a witness for the prosecution in the trial of Tyrion for the death of Joffrey. He tells of how he saw Tyrion fill the [wedding chalice](wiki:Wedding_chalice).(wiki:Philip_Foote.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Philip's right to Nightsong is challenged by Ser [Rolland Storm](wiki:Rolland_Storm), the [bastard](wiki:Bastard) half-brother of the late Lord Bryce Caron.(wiki:Philip_Foote.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
