## Identity

Rakharo is a Dothraki warrior who serves as one of Daenerys Targaryen's khas and later becomes one of her kos and bloodriders (wiki:Rakharo).

## Narrative Arc

Rakharo serves in Daenerys's khas throughout AGOT, accompanying her across the Dothraki Sea. He is present at the assassination attempt on Dany at the Western Market in Vaes Dothrak, eats sausages while the wineseller darts between him and Aggo (agot-daenerys-06). During the sacking of the Lhazarene town, he aids in stopping rapes (agot-daenerys-07). When Drogo is dying, Rakharo lowers him into the bath, fights and kills Haggo with an arakh through the top of his head while Jhogo assists with his whip (agot-daenerys-08). After the khalasar splinters, Rakharo reports the theft of herds and slaves but remains loyal to Daenerys (agot-daenerys-09). At Drogo's funeral pyre, Dany names Rakharo ko and gives him the gold-chased arakh. He initially offers only conditional loyalty — escort to Vaes Dothrak — but ultimately shouts "Blood of my blood" with the others (agot-daenerys-10). He continues serving Dany through ACOK, ASOS, and ADWD as ko and bloodrider.
