## Appearances & Description

Rakharo is a young [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki)(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) with drooping black mustachios.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) His eyes are dark and almond-shaped.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Rakharo is a brave warrior and a skilled scout.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The horseman wields an *[arakh](wiki:Arakh)*,(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) a bow,(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) and a whip.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) He wears a medallion belt.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Rakharo is one of four [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) warriors assigned to the *[khas](wiki:Khas)* of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) within the *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* of Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo). He accompanies Daenerys during her visit to the Western Market in [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak), and is challenged by [Quaro](wiki:Quaro) to a friendly sausage-eating contest. He helps stop an assassination attempt on Daenerys's life by a [wineseller](wiki:Wineseller).(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

When Daenerys attempts to heal Drogo with [Mirri Maz Duur](wiki:Mirri_Maz_Duur)'s aid, the [bloodriders](wiki:Bloodrider) of the *[khal](wiki:Khal)* try to stop her. [Haggo](wiki:Haggo) is killed by Rakharo's *[arakh](wiki:Arakh)* in his attempt to stop Daenerys.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

Rakharo choose the stallion around whose body Drogo's funeral pyre is built. When Daenerys names Rakharo *[ko](wiki:Ko)* and asks him to become one of her bloodriders alongside [Aggo](wiki:Aggo) and [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo), she offers him the great curving(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) *arakh* she received as a present during [her wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen). Rakharo is at first reluctant, but he accepts after the [dragons](wiki:Dragon) are born.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Clash of Kings

Rakharo serves in the [Queensguard](wiki:Queensguard) of Daenerys.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) During their journey across the [red waste](wiki:Red_waste), Rakharo warns Daenerys against travelling downriver to the three ports of [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay), as Khal [Pono](wiki:Pono) and his *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* had ridden that way to sell thousands of captives at the slave markets.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2)

While her *khalasar* rests in [Vaes Tolorro](wiki:Vaes_Tolorro), Daenerys orders her three bloodriders to scout out the country, each one in a different direction. Rakharo leaves to the south, where he finds mostly nothing but barren land and [the sea](wiki:Summer_Sea). He claims he discovered the bones of a [dragon](wiki:Dragon) so immense that he had ridden his horse through its great black jaws. Rakharo is the first bloodrider to return, and Daenerys puts him in charge of pulling up the [plaza](wiki:Plaza) in an effort to turn it into a garden.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-3)

When the *khalasar* arrives in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), Rakharo is ordered to explore the city beyond what its leaders would have Daenerys and her entourage see.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Rakharo protects the rear of the procession during Daenerys's visit to the [Hall of a Thousand Thrones](wiki:Hall_of_a_Thousand_Thrones) to petition the [Pureborn](wiki:Pureborn), and he shows contempt for [Quaithe](wiki:Quaithe)'s warnings to Daenerys to leave the city.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Rakharo accompanies Daenerys to the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying). He helps subdue [Pyat Pree](wiki:Pyat_Pree), who tries to murder Daenerys.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

Rakharo is assigned by Daenerys to protect her three dragons ([Drogon](wiki:Drogon), [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal), and [Viserion](wiki:Viserion)) and her people while [Aggo](wiki:Aggo) and [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) go with her to the [port of Qarth](wiki:Port_of_Qarth).(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Rakharo guards the ships while Daenerys discusses the purchase of [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) slaves. As one of Daenery's three bloodriders, Rakharo is often asked to keep her company as to prevent Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) from approaching her when alone.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

The night before the [fall of Astapor](wiki:Fall_of_Astapor), Daenerys sees him sharpen his arakh in preparation for the bloodshed. Rakharo protects the rear of the litter alongside Jhogo, and cuts down several [Good Masters](wiki:Good_Masters) and Astapori slavers with his *arakh* on the day of the battle. He personally kills [Grazdan mo Ullhor](wiki:Grazdan_mo_Ullhor) with an arrow through the mouth.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

On the march to [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai), Rakharo and his fellow bloodriders are also made *kos* by Daenerys and are put in charge of commanding the 30-odd mounted *Khalasar* remaining to her, leaving their guarding duties to [Arstan Whitebeard](wiki:Arstan_Whitebeard) and [Strong Belwas](wiki:Strong_Belwas). Rakharo later participates in the [battle near Yunkai](wiki:Battle_near_Yunkai), successfully leading the Dothraki mounted warriors in wedge formation against the enemy center.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

During the [siege of Meereen](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen), Rakharo almost comes to blows with [Aggo](wiki:Aggo) and [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) over who would fight [Oznak zo Pahl](wiki:Oznak_zo_Pahl), the hero of the city; Daenerys ultimately stops her bloodriders from fighting, deeming them brave but too young and valuable to lose. He later expresses annoyance in Daenerys's decision to send [Strong Belwas](wiki:Strong_Belwas).(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) After the conquest of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), Rakharo styles his hair in an oiled braid with [silver](wiki:Silver) bells and wears the [gold](wiki:Gold) and jewels of dead men.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Rakharo, [Aggo](wiki:Aggo), and [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) are commanded by Daenerys to use her *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* to subdue the hinterlands of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) and end the slavery there.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) reports the bloodriders successfully harried the slavers from the hills and struck the shackles off the slaves, and are planting and bringing crops to Meereen to market.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

Daenerys recalls Rakharo and her other bloodriders back to Meereen. Upon their return, Daenerys notes Rakharo has grown more tall and muscular, towering over both Aggo and Jhogo, and has four new bells added to his braid. Both [Irri](wiki:Irri) and [Jhiqui](wiki:Jhiqui) now desire him and argue over whom he would prefer, but Daenerys orders them to stop fighting over the blood of her blood.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

Rakharo expresses disgust at the [cannibalism](wiki:Cannibalism) of the diseased refugee survivors of the [fall of Astapor](wiki:Fall_of_Astapor). He and Jhogo are shamed by Daenerys into aiding the sufferers of the [bloody flux](wiki:Bloody_flux) by helping them bathe in the nearby ocean.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2)

Rakharo leads a scouting party beyond the [Skahazadhan](wiki:Skahazadhan), informing Daenerys of [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) scouts from an impending *khalasar* with slaves to be sold at the [Yunkish](wiki:Yunkish) slave markets outside of Meereen.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) He later accompanies Daenerys to [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit) as part of the royal procession's guard.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

Rakharo and Aggo depart Meereen and lead search parties across the Skahazadhan to locate Daenerys when she disappears from Daznak's Pit on [Drogon](wiki:Drogon)'s back.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) Both lead ten picked riders and every man has three horses each to travel far and fast to find her.(wiki:Rakharo.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
