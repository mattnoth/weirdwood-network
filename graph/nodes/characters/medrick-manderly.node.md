## Origins

Prince [Jacaerys Velaryon](wiki:Jacaerys_Velaryon) visited [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) and [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) and enlisted the [north](wiki:North) for the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). Lord [Desmond Manderly](wiki:Desmond_Manderly) sent his sons, Ser Medrick and Ser [Torrhen Manderly](wiki:Torrhen_Manderly), to aid [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). After Rhaenyra lost two [dragons](wiki:Dragon) to the [Two Betrayers](wiki:Two_Betrayers), the Manderly brothers argued that [Addam Velaryon](wiki:Addam_Velaryon) and [Nettles](wiki:Nettles) were also untrustworthy.(wiki:Medrick_Manderly.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

During the [riots at King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(the_Dance_of_the_Dragons)), Medrick led a hundred White Harbor men and restored order in the area between [Aegon's High Hill](wiki:Aegon's_High_Hill) and the [Iron Gate](wiki:Iron_Gate). Medrick was one of [The Seven Who Rode](wiki:Seven_Who_Rode) in an unsuccessful effort to rescue Prince [Joffrey Velaryon](wiki:Joffrey_Velaryon).(wiki:Medrick_Manderly.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

The Manderly brothers accompanied Rhaenyra when she fled the capital after the [Storming of the Dragonpit](wiki:Storming_of_the_Dragonpit). Medrick asked Rhaenyra to travel to White Harbor, but she insisted on sailing instead from [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) on the *[Violande](wiki:Violande)* without the Manderlys.(wiki:Medrick_Manderly.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

During the [Hour of the Wolf](wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf) after the war, Medrick agreed to transport those men [taking the black](wiki:Taking_the_black) from King's Landing to White Harbor on his galley, *[North Star](wiki:North_Star)*. [Orwyle](wiki:Orwyle) had escaped imprisonment, however, so Medrick sentenced a guard who had aided the vanished [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) in his place.(wiki:Medrick_Manderly.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Lord Desmond and the childless Medrick died of [Winter Fever](wiki:Winter_Fever) four days apart in [132 AC](wiki:132_AC), causing his brother Torrhen to resign from the [regency of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III) and return to White Harbor.(wiki:Medrick_Manderly.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
