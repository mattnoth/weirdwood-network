## Origins

Dywen paid an [Andal](wiki:Andal) warlord to cross the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) in order to use his swords against [Jon Brightstone](wiki:Jon_Brightstone), who also claimed to be King of the [Fingers](wiki:Fingers). Eventually the warlord Shell had hired turned against him and he was roasted alive inside his wooden longhall. An Andal knight named [Corwyn Corbray](wiki:Corwyn_Corbray_(lord)) took his wife for a bedwarmer.(wiki:Dywen_Shell.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf74.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
