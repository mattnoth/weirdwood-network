## Origins

Tya was married to [Gowen Baratheon](wiki:Gowen_Baratheon), the third son of a reigning [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End). Their wedding, circa [208 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), was the last between houses [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) and [Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon) until the marriage of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) and Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)(wiki:Tya_Lannister.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) some seventy-six years later.(wiki:Tya_Lannister.cite_ref-2) Tya and Gowen had one son, a "large and lusty lad" born with a full head of black hair, who died in his infancy.(wiki:Tya_Lannister.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
