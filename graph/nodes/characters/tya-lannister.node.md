## Narrative Arc

Tya married Gowen Baratheon, the third son of a Lord of Storm's End, around 208 AC. Their wedding was the last between Houses Lannister and Baratheon until Robert I married Cersei some seventy-six years later. They had one son, a "large and lusty lad" born with a full head of black hair, who died in infancy. Eddard Stark reads about this marriage while investigating the Baratheon lineage (wiki:Tya_Lannister, agot-eddard-12).
