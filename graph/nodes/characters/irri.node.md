## Identity

Irri is a Dothraki handmaid to Daenerys Targaryen (track_b: Allegiances, Culture).

## Origins

Born circa 283–284 AC in the Dothraki Sea (track_b: Born). Of Dothraki culture (track_b: Culture). She and Jhiqui were taken as slaves when Khal Drogo destroyed their father's khalasar (agot-daenerys-03).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` Khal Drogo's khalasar (track_b: Allegiances). `SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiances).

## Appearances & Description

Black-haired with almond-shaped eyes and copper skin (agot-daenerys-02).

## Narrative Arc

Irri is given to Daenerys as a wedding gift alongside Jhiqui and Doreah; she is tasked with teaching Daenerys to ride in the Dothraki manner (agot-daenerys-02). She serves Daenerys faithfully through the khalasar's journey, Drogo's death, and the birth of the dragons. She accompanies Daenerys through Qarth and into Slaver's Bay. In Meereen, she becomes Daenerys's lover (track_b: Lover). She echoes Dothraki customs with the phrase "it is known" (agot-daenerys-07).
