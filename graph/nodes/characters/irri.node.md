## Origins

Irri was born to a *[khal](wiki:Khal)* of a *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)*. It was destroyed by Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), and she was enslaved.(wiki:Irri.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Appearances & Description

Irri has skin the color of [copper](wiki:Copper), with black hair and "almond-shaped eyes".(wiki:Irri.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) She and [Jhiqui](wiki:Jhiqui) are of an age with [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen).(wiki:Irri.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) She is slimmer than Jhiqui.(wiki:Irri.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Irri.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Irri, [Doreah](wiki:Doreah), and [Jhiqui](wiki:Jhiqui) are given to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) by [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) as wedding gifts to serve as her handmaids. She is given to Daenerys to teach her how to ride horses in the Dothraki style.(wiki:Irri.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) She stays with Daenerys, even after the death of Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) and the disintegration of his *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)*.(wiki:Irri.cite_ref-Ragot68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Clash of Kings

After struggling through the [red waste](wiki:Red_waste), Irri and Jhiqui attempt to convince Daenerys not to stay in [Vaes Tolorro](wiki:Vaes_Tolorro), believing it haunted by ghosts.(wiki:Irri.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Irri and Jhiqui are [seasick](wiki:Greensick) during the voyage to [Astapor](wiki:Astapor).(wiki:Irri.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Irri becomes a [paramour](wiki:Paramour) to the lonely Daenerys. Daenerys feels guilt for this.(wiki:Irri.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Irri is present when Daenerys purchases the [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied). Irri and Jhiqui free [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal) and [Viserion](wiki:Viserion) to attack the [Astapori](wiki:Astapor) slavers when Dany has the Unsullied attack their former masters.(wiki:Irri.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Irri and Jhiqui both develop a crush on [Rakharo](wiki:Rakharo) who has grown more tall and muscular. They argue over which of them he would prefer.(wiki:Irri.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

Irri accompanies Daenerys to [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit) as part of the royal procession.(wiki:Irri.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) She and Jhiqui both depart [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) as part of the search parties dispatched across the [Skahazadhan](wiki:Skahazadhan) to locate Daenerys after she disappears on [Drogon](wiki:Drogon)'s back.(wiki:Irri.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
