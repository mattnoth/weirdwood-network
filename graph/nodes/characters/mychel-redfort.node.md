## Appearances & Description

The gallant Mychel is considered one of the best young swordsmen in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn).(wiki:Mychel_Redfort.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Mychel has a relationship with [Mya Stone](wiki:Mya_Stone), a guide on the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance). He has said that her father must have been a goat, and that she has the eyes of an [owl](wiki:Owl).(wiki:Mychel_Redfort.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Mya Stone](wiki:Mya_Stone) tells Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) that she will wed Mychel, the squire of Ser [Lyn Corbray](wiki:Lyn_Corbray), when he is given his [knighthood](wiki:Knight). However, Catelyn doubts a member of [House Redfort](wiki:House_Redfort) would ever marry a [bastard](wiki:Bastard).(wiki:Mychel_Redfort.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Mychel cares for Mya until his [father](wiki:Horton_Redfort) gives him no choice and marries the newly [anointed](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) [knight](wiki:Knight) to one of [Bronze Yohn Royce's](wiki:Yohn_Royce) daughters, [Ysilla Royce](wiki:Ysilla_Royce).(wiki:Mychel_Redfort.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Mychel_Redfort.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

### The Winds of Winter

Mychel arrives at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) to compete in Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn)'s [tourney](wiki:Tourney) for a place in the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights). He is one of the three competitors who are wed. According to [Alayne Stone](wiki:Sansa_Stark), he is one of the favorites to win. Mychel's presence in the castle causes sorrow to his former lover, [Mya Stone](wiki:Mya_Stone).(wiki:Mychel_Redfort.cite_ref-alayneI_6-0)
