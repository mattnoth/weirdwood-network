## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Harrag Sheepstealer participates in the [harrying of the Stony Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore) and the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell). He chooses to remain with [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) when [northmen](wiki:Northmen) led by Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) come to retake the castle.(wiki:Harrag_Sharp.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

After the [battle outside the gates of Winterfell](wiki:Battle_outside_the_gates_of_Winterfell), Theon orders Harrag to find [Palla](wiki:Palla) and bring the girl to "Red Helm". The slayer of Rodrik reveals himself to be [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), however, and instead demands Theon's lover, [Kyra](wiki:Kyra). Harrag is killed by [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) men during the ensuing [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Harrag_Sharp.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Feast for Crows

During the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) mentions Harrag's death to his father, [Harmund Sharp](wiki:Harmund_Sharp).(wiki:Harrag_Sharp.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
