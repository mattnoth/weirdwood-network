## Origins

Sefton served many years at the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), attending the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon). He was in King's Landing throughout the [Great Spring Sickness](wiki:Great_Spring_Sickness) and witnessed much of the city's misery.(wiki:Sefton_Staunton.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

He came to serve at [Coldmoat](wiki:Coldmoat) when his brother, Ser [Simon Staunton](wiki:Simon_Staunton), [married](wiki:Married) Lady [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber).(wiki:Sefton_Staunton.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

### The Sworn Sword

Septon Sefton was present in the yard when Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Dunk) arrived to discuss terms with Lady [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber). He took part in the discussions of the terms and dispensed gossip, informing Duncan of who the courtiers were. After Duncan's dismissal, he accompanied Rohanne to the [Chequy Water](wiki:Chequy_Water) to try and calm everyone down, to no avail.(wiki:Sefton_Staunton.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

## Appearances & Description

Sefton was a very hefty man, fat with a moon face. He was prone to drink and gossip, but was very well informed about current events. He claimed to suffer from frequent flatulence.(wiki:Sefton_Staunton.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
