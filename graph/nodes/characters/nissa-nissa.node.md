## Origins

The legend of [Azor Ahai](wiki:Azor_Ahai) states that Azor Ahai, during his third try to forge a [sword](wiki:Lightbringer) to battle the darkness with, called Nissa Nissa to him after working on his sword for a hundred days and nights. To finish his sword, he drove it through Nissa Nissa's breast. Her blood, soul, strength, and courage went into the [steel](wiki:Steel) of the sword, creating [Lightbringer](wiki:Lightbringer).(wiki:Nissa_Nissa.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Following this sacrifice, Lightbringer was as warm as Nissa Nissa had been in life.(wiki:Nissa_Nissa.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Nissa Nissa's cry of anguish and ecstasy left a crack across the face of the [moon](wiki:Moon).(wiki:Nissa_Nissa.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
