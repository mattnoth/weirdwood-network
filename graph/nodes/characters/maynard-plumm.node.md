## Origins

Ser Maynard was among the [hedge knights](wiki:Hedge_knight) encamped by the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye) near [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls). He defended the actions of Lord [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) known as Bloodraven, to Ser [Kyle the Cat](wiki:Kyle_the_Cat) and Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall).(wiki:Maynard_Plumm.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Maynard attended the [wedding](wiki:Wedding) of Lord [Ambrose Butterwell](wiki:Ambrose_Butterwell) and his [new wife](wiki:Lady_Frey_(wife_of_Ambrose_Butterwell)), but he did not take part in the [tourney](wiki:Whitewalls_tourney). After Duncan the Tall lost to Ser [Uthor Underleaf](wiki:Uthor_Underleaf) in the lists, Dunk refused Maynard's advice to leave. When Dunk was cornered by Lord [Alyn Cockshaw](wiki:Alyn_Cockshaw), Maynard watched until Alyn had fallen into the well, then proceeded to help the injured Duncan and inform him his [missing squire](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) was safe in the castle's [sept](wiki:Sept). Maynard claimed that Bloodraven knew of the conspiracy to crown [Daemon II Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre), and the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion) was indeed squashed by Lord Brynden's army the next morning. Duncan, Kyle, and Ser [Glendon Ball](wiki:Glendon_Ball) looked for Maynard, but he had disappeared during the night. Duncan praised Maynard's assistance when he spoke with Bloodraven.(wiki:Maynard_Plumm.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

## Appearances & Description

Maynard was a tall, thin man with stooped shoulders. His blond hair was long and straight. Maynard wore dun-colored roughspun and stained brown leather, and a frayed cloak which was poorly dyed purple. The cloak was fastened with a large [moonstone](wiki:Moonstone) brooch which resembled a pale white eye in certain lighting.(wiki:Maynard_Plumm.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Maynard was knowledgeable about current events in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), and he knew how to treat wounds.(wiki:Maynard_Plumm.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
