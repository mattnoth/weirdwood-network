## Origins

Tobho learned to smith in [Qohor](wiki:Qohor).(wiki:Tobho_Mott.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) and Lord [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) are among the high lords who have purchased armor from Tobho.(wiki:Tobho_Mott.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

To intimidate his opponents and their [horses](wiki:Horse) in [tourneys](wiki:Tourney), [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr) dips his sword of cheap [steel](wiki:Steel) in wildfire and sets it ablaze. Since the wildfire ruins the blade, Thoros buys a new one from Tobho after each competition, with the two of them always arguing about the price.(wiki:Tobho_Mott.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Tobho told [Gendry](wiki:Gendry), one of his apprentices, that Thoros could outdrink King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), and the armorer taught Gendry to be polite toward highborn girls.(wiki:Tobho_Mott.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Appearances & Description

He owns a black velvet coat which has silver hammers embroidered on the sleeves. A large [sapphire](wiki:Sapphire) hangs on a heavy [silver](wiki:Silver) chain about his neck.(wiki:Tobho_Mott.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Tobho is stubborn and loyal to his staff. He is one of the few armorers who can work [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel),(wiki:Tobho_Mott.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and he claims to be the only armorer in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) who can add tint to the metals in a suit of armor without using paint.(wiki:Tobho_Mott.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), investigates the death of his predecessor, Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn). [Jory Cassel](wiki:Jory_Cassel), Eddard's captain, learns from a potboy that Jon had been considering commissioning plate wrought in pale [silver](wiki:Silver) with a blue [jasper](wiki:Jasper) [falcon](wiki:Falcon) and a [mother-of-pearl](wiki:Mother-of-pearl) [moon](wiki:Moon) on the breast.

Visiting [Tobho's shop](wiki:Shop_of_Tobho_Mott), Eddard asks the master if he made a falcon helm for Lord Arryn. Tobho explains that Jon and Lord [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) did not patronize his work, but instead were interested in one of his blacksmiths. Tobho introduces Ned to [Gendry](wiki:Gendry), one of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s unacknowledged [bastards](wiki:Bastards) who has been working for him as an apprentice. While protective of Gendry's identity, Tobho reveals that the boy's apprentice fee was paid by a stout, brown-bearded man who wore a hooded velvet cloak.(wiki:Tobho_Mott.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

### A Clash of Kings

Gendry reveals to [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) that he was ordered to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) by Master Mott.(wiki:Tobho_Mott.cite_ref-Racok9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) According to a semi-canon source, Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) had Tobho send Gendry to [Yoren](wiki:Yoren) to protect the boy after Ned's [downfall](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark).(wiki:Tobho_Mott.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_varys.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Tobho_Mott.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_shop_of_tobho_mott.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Gendry tells Arya that Tobho had given him good steel so the youth could forge his first longsword, but Gendry was taken away by Yoren before he could do so.(wiki:Tobho_Mott.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

At Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s request, Tobho melts the late Lord Stark's [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) sword, [Ice](wiki:Ice), to forge two new swords. Showing his work to Tywin in the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand), the armorer admits that he tried to color the swords the crimson of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister). The steel darkened to grey and red patterns despite his [spells](wiki:Spells), however.(wiki:Tobho_Mott.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) One sword is given to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), who names it [Widow's Wail](wiki:Widow's_Wail).(wiki:Tobho_Mott.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) The other is given to Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), who names it [Oathkeeper](wiki:Oathkeeper) when he gives it in turn to [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth).(wiki:Tobho_Mott.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
