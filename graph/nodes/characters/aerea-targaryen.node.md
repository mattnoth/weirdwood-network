## Identity

Princess Aerea Targaryen was the eldest daughter of Prince Aegon and Princess Rhaena Targaryen. She had a younger twin sister, Rhaella (wiki:Aerea_Targaryen).

## Origins

Born in 42 AC at Casterly Rock (track_b: Born). Daughter of Aegon Targaryen (son of Aenys I) and Rhaena Targaryen (track_b: Father, Mother). Died in 56 AC in King's Landing (track_b: Died).

## Appearances & Description

Aerea was the mirror image of her twin sister Rhaella. She had silver-gold hair and purple eyes. As a young child, she was shy and timid, much given to tears and fears (wiki:Aerea_Targaryen).

## Narrative Arc

During the reign of Maegor the Cruel, the twins were used as political pawns. Aerea was named heir to the Iron Throne. She later disappeared after mounting the dragon Balerion and vanishing for over a year. When she returned to King's Landing in 56 AC, she was gravely ill with a mysterious and horrifying ailment, dying shortly after under the care of Septon Barth (wiki:Aerea_Targaryen).
