## Origins

According to legend, Corlos was a [huntsman](wiki:Huntsman) who lived in a village near to where [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) would eventually stand. When a [lion](wiki:Lion) began preying on the village's sheep, Corlos tracked it back to its den, a cave in the base of [the Rock](wiki:The_Rock). Armed with only a [spear](wiki:Spear), he slew the lion and his mate, but spared the newborn cubs. This act of mercy so pleased the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods) that they sent a sudden ray of sunlight deep into the cave, revealing the gleam of yellow [gold](wiki:Gold) in a vein as thick as a man's waist.(wiki:Corlos.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf76.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel) believes Corlos soon moved inside the cave and fortified its entrance and as centuries passed, his descendants delved deeper and deeper into the earth to mine the gold but also carving halls, galleries and tunnels, transforming the gigantic Rock into a castle that dwarfed every other seat in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Corlos.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf76.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
