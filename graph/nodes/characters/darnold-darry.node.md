## Origins

After King Maegor I exterminated [House Harroway](wiki:House_Harroway) in [44 AC](wiki:44_AC), he divided up the Harroway holdings among his remaining supporters. [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) was granted to Lord [Walton Towers](wiki:Walton_Towers), [Lord Harroway's Town](wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town) was given to Lord [Alton Butterwell](wiki:Alton_Butterwell), and the rest of the Harroway holdings were given to Lord Darnold.(wiki:Darnold_Darry.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Lord Darry, possibly Darnold, attended the [Golden Wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC) and approved of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Darnold_Darry.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
