## Origins

Rodrik's elder daughter, [Bethany](wiki:Bethany_Bolton), was the second wife of [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), [Lord of the Dreadfort](wiki:Lord_of_the_Dreadfort).(wiki:Rodrik_Ryswell.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

[Brandon Stark](wiki:Brandon_Stark), eldest son of [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark), [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell) and [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), was fostered at [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) with [House Dustin](wiki:House_Dustin). Brandon spent much of his time riding the [Rills](wiki:Rills), and Lord Ryswell enjoyed hosting the heir to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) during his visits. The ambitious Rodrik wanted to wed his younger daughter, [Barbrey](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin), to Brandon, and Barbrey eventually gave her [maidenhead](wiki:Maiden) to Brandon. Lord Stark, ambitious as well, did not want Brandon to marry one of his [bannermen](wiki:Bannermen), however, and Rickard instead betrothed Brandon to [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), eldest daughter of [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), [Lord of Riverrun](wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun) and [Lord Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident). Lord Rodrik next unsuccessfully hoped to betroth Barbrey to Rickard's middle son, [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), and she instead wed [Willam Dustin](wiki:Willam_Dustin), [Lord of Barrowton](wiki:Lord_of_Barrowton).(wiki:Rodrik_Ryswell.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

[Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) began after the deaths of Rickard and Brandon in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Barbrey gave the pride of Rodrik's herds, a [red stallion](wiki:Horse), to her husband when he marched south with Lord Eddard.(wiki:Rodrik_Ryswell.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Willam ended up dying in [combat at the tower of joy](wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy).(wiki:Rodrik_Ryswell.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Barbrey resents that Eddard returned the stallion to the [north](wiki:North), but not Lord Dustin's bones.(wiki:Rodrik_Ryswell.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[House Ryswell](wiki:House_Ryswell) is one of the first houses to swear allegiance to [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), [Lord of the Dreadfort](wiki:Lord_of_the_Dreadfort), after he is named the new [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North) by the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Rodrik_Ryswell.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) notices Lord Rodrik's golden horse head banner above [Barrow Hall](wiki:Barrow_Hall), the seat of his daughter [Barbrey](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin), [Lady of Barrowton](wiki:Lady_of_Barrowton).(wiki:Rodrik_Ryswell.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

Lord Ryswell, along with his sons and surviving daughter Barbrey, attends [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark) of [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) to "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)" (actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)) in [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). Theon sees Rodrik's gold horse head hanging on the walls of the [Great Hall](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell) during the wedding feast.(wiki:Rodrik_Ryswell.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
