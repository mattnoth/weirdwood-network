## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Garrett, a child hostage serving at the court in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) after House Paege submitted to the crown following the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding),(wiki:Garrett_Paege.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) is named a squire to Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister). He accompanies Ser Jaime on the ride north to end the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun).(wiki:Garrett_Paege.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

When Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister) and Jaime discuss the lynching of [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) foragers, Daven suggests that some riverlords might be responsible as they still support the northern cause, even though they have formally submitted to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). Jaime thinks to himself that he would hate to execute Garrett, as he has grown fond of the boy. Ser Jaime then suggests that Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) is a more likely suspect.(wiki:Garrett_Paege.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
