## Identity

Gormon, born Gormon Tyrell, is a maester serving at the Citadel. He is an uncle of Mace Tyrell, Lord of Highgarden (wiki:Gormon_Tyrell.cite_ref-1).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Tyrell (track_b: Allegiances). `SWORN_TO` The Citadel — forswore his family name upon taking maester's vows (track_b: Allegiances).

## Narrative Arc

After passing over Maesters Erreck and Turquin, the Conclave chose Maester Gormon as Grand Maester Pycelle's replacement after Tyrion Lannister stripped Pycelle of his office. Upon hearing whom the Citadel intended to send, Lord Tywin Lannister restored Pycelle instead (wiki:Gormon_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos12). Later, Gormon takes Archmaester Walgrave's place beneath the black iron mask to judge those attempting for the ravencraft link. He once accused Pate of theft (wiki:Gormon_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc0).
