## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

After passing over Maesters [Erreck](wiki:Erreck) and [Turquin](wiki:Turquin), the [Conclave](wiki:Conclave) chooses Maester Gormon as Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle)'s replacement after [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) strips Pycelle of his office. Upon hearing whom the Citadel intends to send as a replacement, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) restores Pycelle to his office.(wiki:Gormon_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Gormon takes Archmaester [Walgrave](wiki:Walgrave)'s place beneath the black iron mask to judge those attempting for the [ravencraft](wiki:Raven) link. He once accused [Pate](wiki:Pate_(novice)) of theft.(wiki:Gormon_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
