## Appearances & Description

Rommo is wizened, bowlegged and squint-eyed.(wiki:Rommo.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

When [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s bloodriders, [Aggo](wiki:Aggo), [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo), and [Rakharo](wiki:Rakharo), are unavailable, Rommo represents her [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) at the council in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Rommo.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

At the council called by Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), Rommo agrees that the hostage [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) must be freed.(wiki:Rommo.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Rommo gathers twenty [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) riders and is their captain in Barristan's host.(wiki:Rommo.cite_ref-4)
