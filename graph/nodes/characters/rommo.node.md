## Identity

Rommo is a jaqqa rhan (mercy-killer of the wounded) of the Dothraki, loyal to Daenerys Targaryen's khalasar (wiki:Rommo.cite_ref-Radwd16).

## Origins

Born in the Dothraki Sea (track_b: Born).

## Appearances & Description

Wizened, bowlegged, and squint-eyed (wiki:Rommo.cite_ref-Radwd16).

## Narrative Arc

When Daenerys's bloodriders Aggo, Jhogo, and Rakharo are unavailable, Rommo represents the Dothraki at the council in Meereen. He dismisses ships as "wooden horses," saying the Dothraki will ride. At a later council called by Ser Barristan Selmy, Rommo insists that the hostage Jhogo must be freed — "Blood of her blood. He must be freed. The honor of the khalasar demands it." In the Winds of Winter, Rommo gathers twenty Dothraki riders to serve as their captain in Barristan's host (wiki:Rommo.cite_ref-Radwd16, wiki:Rommo.cite_ref-Radwd70).
