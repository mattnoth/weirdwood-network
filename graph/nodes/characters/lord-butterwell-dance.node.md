## Origins

At the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord Butterwell was one of the nobles arrested and put in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep)'s dungeons by [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen)'s supporters for supporting his half-sister, Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen). When given the choice between swearing his allegiance to Aegon II or being killed by the [King's Justice](wiki:King's_Justice), Lord Butterwell, along with [Lord Rosby](wiki:Lord_Rosby) and [Lord Stokeworth](wiki:Lord_Stokeworth), pledged his support to Aegon II.(wiki:Lord_Butterwell_(Dance).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

While Rosby and Stokeworth were executed by Rhaenyra after the [fall](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing) of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing),(wiki:Lord_Butterwell_(Dance).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Lord Butterwell is not mentioned again. It is unknown if he survived the war.
