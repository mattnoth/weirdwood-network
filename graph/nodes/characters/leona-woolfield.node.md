## Appearances & Description

Leona is a plump pink woman with yellow hair, and her face is round, pink and fleshy.(wiki:Leona_Woolfield.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) She follows the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:Leona_Woolfield.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Lady Leona is present along with her daughters, [Wynafryd](wiki:Wynafryd_Manderly) and [Wylla](wiki:Wylla_Manderly), when Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) attempts to persuade Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) to support King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon). She insults Davos out of fear for her husband's safety—Ser [Wylis](wiki:Wylis_Manderly) is a hostage of the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne)—fearing that if Wyman declares his support for Stannis her beloved husband will suffer for it. When Wylla refuses to stop demanding [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly)'s loyalty to [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) and that her grandfather Wyman should support Stannis, Leona drags her from the [Merman's Court](wiki:Merman's_Court).(wiki:Leona_Woolfield.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
