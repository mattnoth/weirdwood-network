### A Feast for Crows

Dermot is spotted by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) when he is riding at a [quintain](wiki:Quintain) in the yard of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Dermot_of_the_Rainwood.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) When he is practicing with other knights, King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) asks to take a turn.(wiki:Dermot_of_the_Rainwood.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Dermot rides with Jaime to [Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun) and is assigned to carry the banner of Tommen.(wiki:Dermot_of_the_Rainwood.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) While dining at [Darry](wiki:Darry), Dermot tells stories about being a hedge knight in the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands).(wiki:Dermot_of_the_Rainwood.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Dermot commands the search north of the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) after Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) escapes from Riverrun, but returns without Brynden after being attacked by wolves, losing two sentries to them.(wiki:Dermot_of_the_Rainwood.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
