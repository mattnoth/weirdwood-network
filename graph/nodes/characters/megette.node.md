## Origins

While riding near [Fairmarket](wiki:Fairmarket) in [155 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) Prince [Aegon's](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen) horse threw a shoe, and when he sought out the local smith, he came to notice the man's young wife. He went on to buy her for seven [gold dragons](wiki:Currency) along with the threat of Ser [Joffrey Staunton](wiki:Joffrey_Staunton) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). Megette was installed in a house in [King’s Landing](wiki:King’s_Landing); she and Aegon were even "[wed](wiki:Marriage)" in a secret ceremony conducted by a [mummer](wiki:Mummer) playing a [septon](wiki:Septon). Prince [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen) put an end to it, returning Megette to her husband and placing their daughters with the [Faith](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) to be trained as septas.
