## Origins

When Dorna was younger, her father borrowed large sums of money from Lord [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister). In [260 AC](wiki:260_AC), after the [War of the Ninepenny Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Ninepenny_Kings), Ser [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), the young heir of [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), demanded that all those who took loans from [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) had to repay them immediately. Those unable to do so instead had to surrender a family member as a [hostage](wiki:Hostage) until their debt was settled. When debt collectors arrived at [Cornfield](wiki:Cornfield), Ser Harys was unable to pay, and was forced to hand Dorna over to the custody of Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister).(wiki:Dorna_Swyft.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

By [266 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Dorna and Kevan had wed.(wiki:Dorna_Swyft.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Their eldest son, Lancel, was born in [282 AC](wiki:282_AC).(wiki:Dorna_Swyft.cite_ref-7)

## Appearances & Description

According to [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), Dorna is chinless, chicken-legged, and flat-chested.(wiki:Dorna_Swyft.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) similarly believes Dorna to be chinless like her father, Ser [Harys Swyft](wiki:Harys_Swyft), the [Knight of Cornfield](wiki:Knight_of_Cornfield).(wiki:Dorna_Swyft.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) considers his wife a gentle soul, never comfortable except at home with friends and kin around her. According to Kevan, Dorna has done well by her children, and also dreams of having grandchildren. She is a devout follower of the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) and prays seven times a day. She also loves needlework and flowers. She dislikes traveling, and prefers to stay at her residence in [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport).(wiki:Dorna_Swyft.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) believes that Ser [Harys Swyft](wiki:Harys_Swyft)'s greatest accomplishment is wedding his daughter to Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), therefore attaching himself to [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Dorna_Swyft.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Because [Queen Regent](wiki:Queen_Regent) [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) thinks she has made an enemy of her uncle, Kevan Lannister, she appoints Ser Harys Swyft as the new [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), to use him as her [hostage](wiki:Hostage) against Kevan. She knows Kevan loves Dorna, and believes that as long as she holds Dorna's father, Kevan will reconsider opposing her.(wiki:Dorna_Swyft.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Cersei asks Kevan, now [Lord Regent](wiki:Lord_Regent), if he plans to bring Dorna to court. Kevan knows she would be deeply unhappy in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), and replies that she will be staying in Lannisport. Cersei appears pleased by that answer, which discomfits Kevan.(wiki:Dorna_Swyft.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

After being mortally wounded by [Varys](wiki:Varys), Ser Kevan thinks of Dorna and their children before his death.(wiki:Dorna_Swyft.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
