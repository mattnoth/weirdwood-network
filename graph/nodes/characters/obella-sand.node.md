## Identity

Obella Sand is the second bastard daughter of Prince Oberyn Martell and his paramour Ellaria Sand. She is the sixth of the Sand Snakes (wiki:Obella_Sand).

## Origins

Born 287–288 AC. Daughter of Oberyn Martell and Ellaria Sand (track_b: Born, Father, Mother).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Martell (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Dornishmen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

After Oberyn's death, Obella, her mother, and sisters are taken into custody and sent to the Water Gardens, where she and her sister Elia become terrors of the pools. In TWOW, Obella is sent to Sunspear to serve as cupbearer to the wife of Manfrey Martell, castellan of Sunspear (wiki:Obella_Sand).
