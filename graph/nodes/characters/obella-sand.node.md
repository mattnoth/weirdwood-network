## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

After the death of Prince Oberyn, Obella, her mother and her sisters are all taken into custody. Obella and her family are sent to the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens), where she - along with her elder sister [Elia](wiki:Elia_Sand) - becomes a terror of the pools.(wiki:Obella_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Obella is sent to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) to serve as [cupbearer](wiki:Cupbearer) to the wife of [Manfrey Martell](wiki:Manfrey_Martell), [castellan](wiki:Castellan) of Sunspear.(wiki:Obella_Sand.cite_ref-4)
