## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Hali travels south of the [Wall](wiki:Wall) with [Osha](wiki:Osha), two unnamed [wildling](wiki:Wildling) men, and [Stiv](wiki:Stiv) and [Wallen](wiki:Wallen), two deserters of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). When they encounter [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood), Hali insists Bran give them his horse. When Bran threatens them, Hali suggests killing the boy, which is protested by Osha. Hali then mocks [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) when he arrives on the scene. During the fighting that ensues, [Summer](wiki:Summer) bites Hali's calf. She manages to free herself from the [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) for a second, but when she stumbles and falls, Summer slams her backwards and tears at her belly, killing her.(wiki:Hali.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
