## Identity

Ser Luthor Tyrell is a knight of House Tyrell, a first cousin of Lord Mace Tyrell and the first son of Ser Moryn Tyrell, commander of Oldtown's City Watch. He is deceased, having died in 299 AC (wiki:Luthor_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn)).
