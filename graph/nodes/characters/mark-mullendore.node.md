## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser Mark is amongst the nobles who join [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) beneath the walls of [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge). He feeds his [monkey](wiki:Monkey) morsels at the feast.(wiki:Mark_Mullendore.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) later recalls that Mark was a part of the wager over who would claim her [maidenhead](wiki:Maidenhead). He used his monkey to make her laugh in his attempt to woo her, but Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) found out about the wager and put an end to it.(wiki:Mark_Mullendore.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) After the bet was discovered and ended, Brienne sought out and defeated Mark during the [melee at Bitterbridge](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge).(wiki:Mark_Mullendore.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Mark is among those who go over to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) after the death of Renly at [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)). Mark fights at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), losing part of his left arm and his monkey before getting captured by forces loyal to King [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). He bends the knee when presented to [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Mark_Mullendore.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Mark is a [sworn sword](wiki:Sworn_sword) of Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell).(wiki:Mark_Mullendore.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Mark is rumored to be [Megga Tyrell](wiki:Megga_Tyrell)'s choice for a husband by [Taena of Myr](wiki:Taena_of_Myr), as Megga has asked her cousin [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) for help in finding Mark a new monkey.(wiki:Mark_Mullendore.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) has an arrest warrant signed for Mark as part of her plans to remove Margaery. He is sent to the dungeons of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) after his arrest.(wiki:Mark_Mullendore.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Mark is held with the rest of Margaery's falsely-accused lovers in a dungeon under the charge of [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn).(wiki:Mark_Mullendore.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
