She is a bent and shriveled stick of a woman with a single back eye. She is the oldest of the crones and has a thin wavery voice.(wiki:One-eyed_crone.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

At [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak), right after [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) consumes the [stallion heart](wiki:Stallion_heart), she and her husband [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) turn to the *[dosh khaleen](wiki:Dosh_khaleen)* to hear their son’s destiny foretold. The one-eyed crone closes her single eye so that she can peer more easily into the future. When she finally opens her eye she lifts her arms and proclaims the prophecy. Afterwards she peers at Dany and asks her the name of the [stallion who mounts the world](wiki:Stallion_who_mounts_the_world). Dany replies that he shall be called [Rhaego](wiki:Rhaego).(wiki:One-eyed_crone.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
