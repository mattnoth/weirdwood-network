## Identity

Tomard, better known as Fat Tom, is a guard sworn to House Stark who served as captain of the guards in King's Landing (track_b: Title). Born between 249 and 253 AC, he died in 298 AC in King's Landing (track_b: Born, Died). He has a son, TomToo (track_b: Issue).

## Appearances & Description

Tomard is fat with ginger-colored whiskers, near fifty years old (wiki:Tomard.cite_ref-Ragot45). He is the slowest guard of Lord Eddard Stark (wiki:Tomard.cite_ref-Rasos7). Affable, loyal, tireless, and capable in a limited way (wiki:Tomard.cite_ref-Ragot45). Ned's children call him "Fat Tom" (agot-eddard-12). He was always easy to fool, according to Arya (wiki:Tomard.cite_ref-Ragot22).

## Narrative Arc

Tomard accompanied Eddard's retinue to the execution of the Night's Watch deserter Gared, helping drag the man to the block (wiki:Tomard.cite_ref-Ragot1). He went south with the fifty Stark household guards when Eddard became Hand (wiki:Tomard.cite_ref-Ragot22). He took positions along Eddard's route to watch for followers when Ned investigated Jon Arryn's death (wiki:Tomard.cite_ref-Ragot27). He gave Arya the nickname "Arya Underfoot" (wiki:Tomard.cite_ref-Ragot22). After Jory Cassel's death and Alyn's departure with Beric Dondarrion, Tomard was promoted to captain of the guards (wiki:Tomard.cite_ref-Ragot45). Ned trusted him with delivering a letter to Stannis at Dragonstone and commanding the escort for his daughters aboard the Wind Witch (agot-eddard-13). Before the ship could sail, Tomard was killed during Eddard's arrest in the throne room when a gold cloak drove a spear through his back, its wet red point bursting through his ribs (agot-eddard-14).
