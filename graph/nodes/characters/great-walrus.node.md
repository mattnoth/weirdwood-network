## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

The Great Walrus is among the wildling leaders who accompany [Tormund](wiki:Tormund) to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) in declaring a truce with the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). One or more of his sons become hostages of the Night's Watch.(wiki:Great_Walrus.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

The Great Walrus is present in the [Shieldhall](wiki:Shieldhall) at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) when [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), announces [the letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) purportedly from [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) entitled 'Bastard'. The Great Walrus supports Jon when he announces his intention to ride south and face Ramsay, but the Lord Commander is then attacked in the [mutiny at Castle Black](wiki:Mutiny_at_Castle_Black).(wiki:Great_Walrus.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
