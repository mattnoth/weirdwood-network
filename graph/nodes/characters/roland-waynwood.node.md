## Appearances & Description

Long-faceaced and lantern-jawed, Roland has stringy brown hair and a pinched nose. [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) considers him to be [horse](wiki:Horse)-faced and homely. Roland is taller and more muscular than his uncle, [Wallace Waynwood](wiki:Wallace_Waynwood).(wiki:Roland_Waynwood.cite_ref-twow_1-1)

The charming Roland is proud of his [knighthood](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Roland_Waynwood.cite_ref-twow_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter

Ser Roland accompanies his grandmother Lady [Anya Waynwood](wiki:Anya_Waynwood), his younger uncle, Ser [Wallace Waynwood](wiki:Wallace_Waynwood) and his cousin, Ser [Harrold Hardyng](wiki:Harrold_Hardyng) to the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) to participate in the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) to be held there to choose the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights). Roland flirts with [Myranda Royce](wiki:Myranda_Royce) and [Alayne Stone](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and jests with Wallace.(wiki:Roland_Waynwood.cite_ref-twow_1-3)

Alayne dances with Roland at the feast that evening. Roland makes the girl laugh by making mocking comments about other knights in attendance.(wiki:Roland_Waynwood.cite_ref-twow_1-4)
