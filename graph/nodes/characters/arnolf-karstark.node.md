## Appearances & Description

Arnolf is gaunt, crooked, and bent from age, and he walks with a blackthorn cane.(wiki:Arnolf_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) His left shoulder is half a foot higher then his right. Arnolf has a scrawny neck, squinty grey eyes, and is a few white hairs away from complete baldness. His teeth are yellow and his ragged, forked beard is grey and white.(wiki:Arnolf_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Arnolf_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) considers Arnolf, whose grey woolen cloak is bordered in [sable](wiki:Sable), to be "a poor excuse for a man".(wiki:Arnolf_Karstark.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btheon_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctheon_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Arnolf became castellan of [Karhold](wiki:Karhold) when Lord [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark) and his sons marched south with [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Arnolf_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Rickard was eventually executed at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) for treason by Robb,(wiki:Arnolf_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) and his last surviving son, [Harrion](wiki:Harrion_Karstark), is a captive of the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister)(wiki:Arnolf_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) at [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool).(wiki:Arnolf_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) After [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) sends a declaration of his arrival at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) and a demand for fealty, Arnolf is the only [northerner](wiki:North) to respond favorably to him, pledging Stannis his support.(wiki:Arnolf_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) Since [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), has rejected Stannis's offer of becoming Lord of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), Stannis leans toward offering the castle to Arnolf.(wiki:Arnolf_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)(wiki:Arnolf_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Although [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) does not recognize them, Arnolf and [Hother Umber](wiki:Hother_Umber) are seated next to [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) during the feast held at the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort) before Ramsay marches on [Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin).(wiki:Arnolf_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)(wiki:Arnolf_Karstark.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btheon_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctheon_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) Arnolf writes to Stannis that the [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton) left the strong Dreadfort with only fifty men. However, Jon convinces Stannis to recruit the [northern mountain clans](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans) and liberate [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte) from the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn).(wiki:Arnolf_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

At [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton), Theon learns from Ramsay and his father, Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) that Arnolf is conspiring against Stannis. Roose intends for Arnolf to betray Stannis when he marches on Winterfell.(wiki:Arnolf_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

[Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark), the daughter of the late Lord Rickard, flees from Karhold for the protection of Jon Snow at the [Wall](wiki:Wall). She reveals that Arnolf responded favorably to Stannis in hope that when the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) heard of the pledge they would execute her captive brother, Lord Harrion. Harry's death would make Alys the heir and Arnolf would then force her to marry his son, [Cregan](wiki:Cregan_Karstark), to claim Karhold. Roose has promised Arnolf a full pardon for this service to the Lannisters.(wiki:Arnolf_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) [Clydas](wiki:Clydas) sends a [raven](wiki:Raven) to Deepwood on Jon's behalf in hopes of informing Stannis, and the Lord Commander has Cregan imprisoned when he tries to retrieve Alys from Castle Black.(wiki:Arnolf_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

Arnolf has his army meet up with Stannis at a [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village) during the king's [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell), bringing four hundred spears, two score archers, a dozen mounted lances, and Maester [Tybald](wiki:Tybald)(wiki:Arnolf_Karstark.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btheon_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctheon_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) with a cage of [ravens](wiki:Raven). Ser [Justin Massey](wiki:Justin_Massey) is skeptical of the claims of Arnolf and his kin that Winterfell can be taken from the Boltons.(wiki:Arnolf_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

### The Winds of Winter

[Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris) brings Jon Snow's message from Deepwood to Stannis in time, and Tybald admits that he sent a raven with Stannis's location to Roose Bolton at Winterfell. Stannis has Arnolf, his son [Arthor](wiki:Arthor_Karstark), and three grandsons brought before him on the pretext of breaking their fast. Upon entering the village's watchtower, Arnolf notices the chained Theon Greyjoy in the corner and realizes that Stannis is aware of his planned treachery.(wiki:Arnolf_Karstark.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btheon_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctheon_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3)

Stannis's knights surround the five Karstarks; two of Arnolf's grandsons attempt to resist and are badly injured, but Arnolf does not put up a fight. Stannis vows that all five will die, but whether quickly or by fire will depend on their cooperation.(wiki:Arnolf_Karstark.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btheon_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctheon_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-4)
