## Identity

Missandei is a Naathi girl who serves as interpreter, scribe, and confidante to Daenerys Targaryen. She speaks many languages and is remarkably intelligent for her age.

## Origins

Born in 288-289 AC on Naath (track_b: Born). Of Naathi culture (track_b: Culture). She was taken from Naath by slavers and trained as a scribe in Astapor.

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Naathi (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Missandei is acquired by Daenerys in Astapor in ASOS when Dany purchases the Unsullied. She serves as interpreter during the transaction and remains as Dany's closest attendant thereafter. She is about ten or eleven years old and speaks the Common Tongue, High Valyrian, and bastard Valyrian of several Slaver's Bay cities. Throughout ADWD she serves as Dany's primary advisor on Meereenese affairs, attends council meetings, and provides translation. She has a brother, Marselen, who serves among the Unsullied. She appears across 18 chapters spanning ASOS and ADWD (wiki:Missandei cite_refs: asos 23,27,42,57,71; adwd 2,11,16,23,36,43,50,52,55,59,67,68,70).
