## Origins

Some time before the start of the series, Amerei was discovered in the stables of the [Twins](wiki:Twins) having sex with three grooms at the same time. Her parents were forced to marry her to Ser [Pate of the Blue Fork](wiki:Pate_of_the_Blue_Fork), a [hedge knight](wiki:Hedge_knight) of humble beginnings. Pate's cousin, [Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams), sang at the wedding.(wiki:Amerei_Frey.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Appearances & Description

According to [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), Amerei is attractive, but has a pinched, chinless face. She has long legs, full breasts, and brown eyes.(wiki:Amerei_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Amerei's husband, [Pate](wiki:Pate_of_the_Blue_Fork), is slain by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings),(wiki:Amerei_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) making her a widow,(wiki:Amerei_Frey.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) and she returns to the [Twins](wiki:Twins).(wiki:Amerei_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Amerei is among the Frey women presented to [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), when he comes to the Twins for Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully)'s [wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Amerei_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

[Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey) thinks over all the difficulties in his life on the way to [Oldstones](wiki:Oldstones) to ransom his great-nephew, [Petyr Frey](wiki:Petyr_Frey), including Amerei's promiscuous behavior and her short marriage. Merrett hopes that successfully delivering the ransom will change his fortunes in the Twins; however, both he and Petyr are hanged by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners).(wiki:Amerei_Frey.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

### A Feast for Crows

Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) has his son [Lancel](wiki:Lancel_Lannister), the new [Lord of Darry](wiki:House_Lannister_of_Darry), marry Ami to ease the change of allegiance after the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding). This allows Lancel to strengthen his claim on the Darry lands, since Ami's mother, [Mariya](wiki:Mariya_Darry), is a [Darry](wiki:House_Darry). However, Lancel, having discovered a devout piety to the [Faith](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), shows interest in neither his wife nor his lands.(wiki:Amerei_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Amerei_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

When Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s [knights](wiki:Knight) visit [Castle Darry](wiki:Darry), Amerei hosts a feast in their honor. She sobs that [outlaws](wiki:Outlaw) murdered her father, [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey). Ser [Harwyn Plumm](wiki:Harwyn_Plumm), the commander of Darry's new garrison, has promised to bring Amerei the head of the leader of the outlaws, Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion). Amerei also tries to convince Jaime to stay and protect Darry from the outlaws, complimenting him and stroking his [golden](wiki:Gold) prosthetic hand, which he cannot feel. She does charm Ser [Lyle Crakehall](wiki:Lyle_Crakehall) into agreeing to hunt the outlaws near her lands after the resolution of the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun).(wiki:Amerei_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

Jaime warns Lancel that Ami is interested in Harwyn, but Lancel does not care, and hopes they are happy together. Lancel explains he intends to renounce his [lordship](wiki:Lord) over Darry and his unconsummated marriage with Amerei, and join the re-formed [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons).(wiki:Amerei_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4) When Jaime leaves Darry, Amerei is crying over the end of her marriage while being comforted by Lyle Crakehall. Jaime finds the angry looks of Amerei's family to be concerning, as [the Freys are known to not react well](wiki:Red_Wedding) to the breaking of [marriage](wiki:Marriage) contracts.(wiki:Amerei_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

During the siege of Riverrun, Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister) shares with Jaime the reason for Amerei's nickname. He conjectures that Ami has slept with [Black Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Ryman)), who is known for bedding his female relatives. [Genna Lannister](wiki:Genna_Lannister) thinks Lancel's younger brother, [Martyn Lannister](wiki:Martyn_Lannister), could marry Ami in Lancel's place.(wiki:Amerei_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
