## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Conn is a member of the [Stone Crows](wiki:Stone_Crows) whom [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) first encounter in the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon). Tyrion offers to give his hauberk to Conn to secure passage through the mountains, but the Stone Crows insist that all he possesses is already theirs. Tyrion gains them as allies by promising them the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn).(wiki:Conn.cite_ref-Ragot42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Conn is one of the clansmen who leaves the mountains with Tyrion, and he accompanies Tyrion to the war council held by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister).(wiki:Conn.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

[Ulf](wiki:Ulf) demands blood money when three Stone Crows kill a [Moon Brother](wiki:Moon_Brother), but Conn and [Shagga](wiki:Shagga) refuse to pay. Ulf later drinks [ale](wiki:Ale) and offers to share oxmeat with Tyrion.(wiki:Conn.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

During the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork), Tyrion sees Conn's horse kick a man in the ribs. Conn is slain by a spear thrust during the battle, with Tyrion finding Shagga cradling his friend's head by a tree after the fighting.(wiki:Conn.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
