## Origins

Ser Roland, along with his two fellow brothers of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), Ser [Willem Wylde](wiki:Willem_Wylde) and Ser [Donnel of Duskendale](wiki:Donnel_of_Duskendale), escorted the royal [Targaryen](wiki:Targaryen) princes to [Ashford](wiki:Ashford) for [the tourney](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC). He helped Prince [Maekar](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen) search for his sons [Daeron](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) and [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen), who were supposed to be at the Ashford tourney. All three Kingsguard present fought on behalf of Prince [Aerion](wiki:Aerion_Targaryen) in the [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven).(wiki:Roland_Crakehall_(Kingsguard).cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
Ser Roland was among three Kingsguard who marched on [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) with Lord [Bloodraven](wiki:Brynden_Rivers) to put down the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion). He found Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) amongst the prisoners and escorted him to the [Hand's](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) pavilion to meet with him.(wiki:Roland_Crakehall_(Kingsguard).cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
