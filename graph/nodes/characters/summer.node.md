## Identity

Summer is a direwolf bonded to Bran Stark (track_b: Owner). He is the litter-mate of Grey Wind, Lady, Nymeria, Shaggydog, and Ghost. Born in 298 AC in the north (track_b: Born). Bran goes through many names before settling on "Summer" when the wolf saves his life (wiki:Summer).

## Origins

Summer was found with his litter-mates in the snow near their dead mother by Jon Snow and Robb Stark north of Winterfell (wiki:Summer).

## Allegiances

`OWNS`: Bran Stark owns Summer (track_b: Owner). Summer is Bran's constant companion and the primary vehicle for Bran's warging abilities.

## Appearances & Description

Summer is grey-furred. Jojen Reed describes him as "strong... stronger than he knows." Summer is the most prominent direwolf in the warg-dream sequences, serving as Bran's primary connection to the physical world after his fall (wiki:Summer).

## Narrative Arc

**AGOT:** After Bran's fall, Summer howls constantly beneath his window. When an assassin enters Bran's room to kill him, Summer sneaks behind the man while he grapples with Catelyn Stark and tears out his throat. Bran names the wolf "Summer" upon waking. During Bran's first ride since his fall, Summer and Grey Wind help Robb fight off wildlings — Summer kills Hali, taking a knife wound. Summer intervenes when Shaggydog attacks Maester Luwin in the crypts (wiki:Summer, agot chapters 1–66).

**ACOK:** Bran begins having regular wolf dreams through Summer. Shaggydog and Summer are confined to the godswood after Shaggydog bites Little Walder Frey. Meera Reed spars with Summer in the godswood, trapping him in her net. During the capture of Winterfell by Theon Greyjoy, Summer tries to escape the godswood. The direwolves eventually escape with their masters, evade Theon's hunting party, and witness the sack of Winterfell from outside. After the Boltons depart, Summer leads the group back through the ruins (wiki:Summer, acok chapters 4–69).

**ASOS:** Bran wargs into Summer frequently as they travel north. Summer encounters and fights a wild wolf pack for a carcass; Hodor forcibly pulls Bran out of Summer's mind on Jojen's command. At Queenscrown, Summer attacks Styr's wildlings, killing at least three and giving Jon Snow an opening to escape. Summer's back leg is wounded by an arrow. Through Summer, Bran dreams of the deaths of Robb and Grey Wind. They cross beyond the Wall through the Black Gate of the Nightfort (wiki:Summer, asos chapters 9–56).

**ADWD:** In Jon's wolf dream, Ghost can no longer sense Summer beyond the Wall. Summer accompanies Bran to the cave of the three-eyed crow beneath a weirwood hill. Summer fights the wights outside the cave and kills several. Inside, Summer lies beside Bran in the darkness as Bran trains with Bloodraven. Summer hunts in the frozen lands beyond the cave, eating frozen prey (wiki:Summer, adwd chapters 3–34).
