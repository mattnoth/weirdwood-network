## Identity

Princess Daenora Targaryen was the younger daughter of Prince Rhaegel Targaryen and Lady Alys Arryn (wiki:Daenora_Targaryen). She had an older brother and sister, the twins Aelor and Aelora.

## Origins

Born between 196 and 216 AC (track_b: Born). Daughter of Rhaegel Targaryen (track_b: Father) and Alys Arryn (track_b: Mother).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiance).

## Narrative Arc

Daenora married her cousin Prince Aerion Targaryen. Together they had one son, Prince Maegor, born in 232 AC, whose claim to the throne was passed over by the Great Council of 233 AC. Daenora died in or after 232 AC (track_b: Died) (wiki:Daenora_Targaryen).
