## Origins

Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake)'s second son served Lord Redwyne as a [page](wiki:Page) and [squire](wiki:Squire) until his death in a sailing accident.(wiki:Lord_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
When the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) began, Lord Redwyne declared for King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) It is unknown to what extent the Redwynes were involved in the war. By the end of the war, Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower)'s second son, [Martyn](wiki:Martyn_Hightower_(son_of_Ormund)), was sent to be a squire to Lord Redwyne on the Arbor.(wiki:Lord_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
In [131 AC](wiki:131_AC), Lord Redwyne along with Lords [Beesbury](wiki:House_Beesbury) and [Costayne](wiki:House_Costayne) escorted the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for the wedding of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) and Queen [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen). On the trip back to the [Reach](wiki:Reach), they were joined by Lords [Rowan](wiki:House_Rowan) and [Tarly](wiki:House_Tarly).(wiki:Lord_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
In [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Lord [Lyonel Hightower](wiki:Lyonel_Hightower) promised Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) that the Hightowers would supply twenty warships and that Lord Redwyne would supply thirty. Redwyne's ships were slow to show, however, and the impatient Lord Alyn departed Oldtown without them.(wiki:Lord_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
After the death of Queen Jaehaera, some of Lord Redwyne's relatives were proposed by Lady [Samantha Tarly](wiki:Samantha_Tarly) as a potential new bride for King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
