## Origins

When [Harwyn Hoare](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), invaded the [Riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), Ser Samwell assembled a small host and faced King Harwyn on the [Tumblestone](wiki:Tumblestone). Samwell's lines shattered when the Hardhand charged. Hundreds drowned attempting to flee, while Rivers was hacked in two, so that half of his body might be delivered to each of his parents.(wiki:Samwell_Rivers.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
