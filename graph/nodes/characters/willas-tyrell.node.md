## Origins

Willas is the eldest son of [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), [Lord of Highgarden](wiki:Lord_of_Highgarden), and Lady [Alerie Hightower](wiki:Alerie_Hightower). During his first [tourney](wiki:Tourney), which Willas entered as a [squire](wiki:Squire)(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rawoiafwillas_tyrell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) of a young age due to his father's wishes, he competed against Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), who knocked Willas from his horse. Unfortunately, Willas's foot caught in his stirrup as he fell, and he pulled his horse on top of him, crushing his leg and leaving him a cripple.(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Though the incident fanned the flames of the considerable and historical enmity between Houses [Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) and [Martell](wiki:House_Martell), Willas bore Oberyn no personal ill will, and the two long remained in correspondence, sharing a passion for horseflesh.(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) Ever since his accident, Willas has occupied himself with scholarly studies, as he cannot be a [knight](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-10) Willas's grandmother, [Olenna Tyrell](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell), despite having a low opinion of her son Mace, has a high opinion of her grandson.(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

His younger brother, [Garlan](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell), was plump as a boy. To protect him from a potential denigrating nickname, such as given to his uncle [Garth "the Gross"](wiki:Garth_Tyrell), Willas was the first to call him "Garlan the Gallant".(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) When Willas's sister [Margaery](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) was a girl, he would often read to her and draw her pictures of the stars.(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) once considered marrying Willas, but her father Prince [Doran](wiki:Doran_Martell) refused to send her to [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) to meet him. Nevertheless, she tried to travel there on her own, with the help of her cousin [Tyene Sand](wiki:Tyene_Sand), but her uncle Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) caught them at [Vaith](wiki:Vaith) and brought them back to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear).(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Willas Tyrell](wiki:Category:Images_of_Willas_Tyrell)*

Willas is crippled, with a bad leg. He is described as intelligent,(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) studious, educated, and kind, and is renowned for breeding the finest [hawks](wiki:Hawk), hounds, and [horses](wiki:Horse) in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) He sometimes hawks with an [eagle](wiki:Eagle).(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Willas is also described as mild and courtly, fond of reading books and looking at the stars.(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) considers Willas to be gentle, pious, and good-hearted, but also boring.(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

In [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Willas Tyrell is ordered to swear fealty to the new king [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), along with his father, brothers, and uncles.(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Storm of Swords

In King's Landing, Lady [Olenna Tyrell](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell) plots to have [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) married to her grandson Willas.(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) Sansa readily accepts the offer of [betrothal](wiki:Betrothal), but the plot is foiled after she reveals the information to [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard) in the [godswood](wiki:Godswood) of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) learns of the plot from Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), and hastily has [Sansa married](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark) to his son [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

Tywin further offers his daughter, Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), to be married to Willas, in order to quiet the rumors of her [incest](wiki:Incest) with her brother, Ser [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) Though [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) appears to be interested, his mother Olenna convinces him otherwise, and the Tyrells refuse the offer.(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Feast for Crows

In King's Landing, Lord Mace Tyrell dismisses Queen Regent Cersei Lannister's suggestion that he is needed in the [Reach](wiki:Reach), saying his son Willas is able and intelligent.(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields) by the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), Willas reportedly sends messengers to Lord [Leyton Hightower](wiki:Leyton_Hightower), ordering him to strengthen his defenses in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). Willas also guesses correctly that the ironborn did not follow the coast when attacking the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands) in order to take the lords of the Shields off guard. However, Queen Cersei dismisses his reports, preferring to believe he was incompetent and making excuses. Cersei also refuses to send the [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet) to transport the army Willas is raising, until the [siege of Dragonstone](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone) is won.(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

In Sunspear, Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) recalls her many failed betrothals, including the time she tried to travel to Highgarden to meet with Willas.(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

In King's Landing, at a meeting of King [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon)'s [small council](wiki:Small_council), Mace Tyrell is confident that once [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne)'s fleet sweeps the ironmen from the seas, his sons Willas and Garlan will easily retake the Shield Islands. When Mace says a better marriage match should be made for Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) than [Trystane Martell](wiki:Trystane_Martell), Tommen's regent [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) thinks that Mace wants to marry Willas to her.(wiki:Willas_Tyrell.cite_ref-Radwd72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
