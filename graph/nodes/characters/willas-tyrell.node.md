## Identity

Willas Tyrell is the eldest son and heir of Lord Mace Tyrell and Lady Alerie Hightower. He is heir to Highgarden (wiki:Willas_Tyrell).

## Origins

Born between 273–276 AC at Highgarden (track_b: Born). Son of Mace Tyrell and Alerie Hightower (track_b: Father, Mother).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Tyrell (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` the Reach (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Willas is crippled, with a bad leg from a jousting accident. He is described as intelligent, studious, educated, and kind. He breeds the finest hawks, hounds, and horses in the Seven Kingdoms and sometimes hawks with an eagle. He is mild, courtly, fond of reading books and stargazing (wiki:Willas_Tyrell). Petyr Baelish considers him gentle, pious, good-hearted, but boring (wiki:Willas_Tyrell).

## Narrative Arc

During his first tourney, entered at too young an age due to his father's ambition, Willas jousted against Prince Oberyn Martell. His foot caught in his stirrup as he fell, pulling his horse on top of him and crushing his leg. Despite this, Willas bore Oberyn no ill will and the two maintained correspondence, sharing a passion for horseflesh (wiki:Willas_Tyrell). In ASOS, Olenna plots to marry Sansa Stark to Willas, but the plan is foiled when Sansa tells Dontos, and Tywin hastily marries her to Tyrion instead. Tywin then offers Cersei to Willas, but Olenna convinces Mace to refuse (wiki:Willas_Tyrell). In AFFC, with Mace away at King's Landing, Willas manages affairs in the Reach. After the ironborn take the Shield Islands, Willas sends messengers to Lord Leyton Hightower and correctly deduces the ironborn's attack route. Cersei dismisses his reports (wiki:Willas_Tyrell). In ADWD, Mace is confident Willas and Garlan will retake the Shields once Redwyne's fleet clears the ironborn (wiki:Willas_Tyrell).
