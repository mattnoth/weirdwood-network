## Origins

According to a semi-canon source, Rast comes from the [Fingers](wiki:Fingers).(wiki:Rast.cite_ref-Rawoiafrast.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Rast was sentenced to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) for being a rapist.(wiki:Rast.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

## Appearances & Description

Rast is two years older than [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) and forty pounds heavier.(wiki:Rast.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) He is cruel(wiki:Rast.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) and grudgeful.(wiki:Rast.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Rast is in the [same group of recruits](wiki:Jon_Snow's_Night's_Watch_recruitment_group) as [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) when he comes to the [Wall](wiki:Wall). Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne), the [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms) at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), orders Rast, [Albett](wiki:Albett), and [Halder](wiki:Halder) to fight Jon when he takes the side of [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) during training. Jon defeats them with the help of [Pyp](wiki:Pypar) and [Grenn](wiki:Grenn).(wiki:Rast.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) When Jon asks his fellow recruits to refrain from fighting Sam, all agree except Rast. The next night the boys go to Rast's cell and Jon's [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), [Ghost](wiki:Ghost), nips at Rast's throat with his fangs. The next day Rast claims he cut himself shaving, but he refuses to harm Sam.(wiki:Rast.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

### A Clash of Kings

When Samwell spends the night in the library of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) and misses breakfast, Rast suggests that Sam had deserted.(wiki:Rast.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords

In the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest), Sam thinks of Rast and other tormenters before attacking an [Other](wiki:Other) with a [dragonglass](wiki:Dragonglass) dagger.(wiki:Rast.cite_ref-Rasos18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Still in training,(wiki:Rast.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Rast is among the men whom [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) leaves behind to defend [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) when the castellan departs to deal with [wildling](wiki:Wildling) attacks along the [Wall](wiki:Wall). Rast dies during the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) when a [Thenn](wiki:Thenns) shoves a spear into his belly and lifts the boy into the air.(wiki:Rast.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
