## Identity

Jacks is a member of the household guard of Lord Eddard Stark at Winterfell (wiki:Jacks).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Jacks accompanies Lord Eddard Stark to King's Landing when Eddard is named Hand (wiki:Jacks.cite_ref-Ragot22). He escorts Eddard when he visits the armorer Tobho Mott and discovers Gendry, along with Varly (wiki:Jacks.cite_ref-Ragot27). After Eddard's arrest, Jacks is presumably imprisoned, then sent north with Ser Cleos Frey when Eddard's bones are returned. He travels under the command of Hallis Mollen escorting the silent sisters bearing Eddard's bones toward Winterfell (wiki:Jacks.cite_ref-Racok39). As of ADWD, the bone escort has not yet completed the journey (wiki:Jacks.cite_ref-Radwd41).
