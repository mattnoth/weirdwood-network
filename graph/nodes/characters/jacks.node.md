## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Jacks accompanies Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) when Eddard is named [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:Jacks.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

[Varly](wiki:Varly) and Jacks make up the escort for Eddard when he visits the [armorer](wiki:Armorer) [Tobho Mott](wiki:Tobho_Mott) and discovers [Gendry](wiki:Gendry), one of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s [bastard](wiki:Bastard) children.(wiki:Jacks.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Jacks, having presumably been imprisoned during the [arrest of Eddard Stark](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark), is presumably sent with Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) when the envoy returns to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) with [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s terms and Eddard's bones.(wiki:Jacks.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Jacks is under the command of [Hallis Mollen](wiki:Hallis_Mollen).(wiki:Jacks.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) When [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark)'s party returns to Riverrun from [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), she orders Hallis and a small honor guard to escort the [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters) who are to bring the bones of Lord Eddard back to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Jacks.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Unbeknownst to Catelyn, however, [ironmen](wiki:Ironmen) led by [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) have already moved to [take Moat Cailin](wiki:Fall_of_Moat_Cailin), and [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) is on his way to [take Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) as well.(wiki:Jacks.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Jacks.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Hal continues north toward Winterfell with Jacks, [Quent](wiki:Quent), and [Shadd](wiki:Shadd).(wiki:Jacks.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Hallis and his party continue their journey.(wiki:Jacks.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

At Winterfell, [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin), [Lady of Barrowton](wiki:Lady_of_Barrowton), tells [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) that should Ned Stark's bones emerge from the [Neck](wiki:Neck), the escorts will be prevented from going north of [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton).(wiki:Jacks.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
