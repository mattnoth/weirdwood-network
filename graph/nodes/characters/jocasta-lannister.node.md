## Origins

Jocasta's husband, Lord [Lyman Lannister](wiki:Lyman_Lannister), protected Prince [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) and Princess [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) during the early years of the reign of King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), extending them [guest right](wiki:Guest_right). During their stay at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), Jocasta was the first to recognize that Rhaena was pregnant.(wiki:Jocasta_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

In early [49 AC](wiki:49_AC), Jocasta and her husband attended the small [wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Rhaena_Targaryen_and_Androw_Farman) of Rhaena to [Androw Farman](wiki:Androw_Farman).(wiki:Jocasta_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) After Rhaena was exiled from [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle) in early [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), Jocasta and Lyman welcomed them to Casterly Rock, staging a lavish feast in their honor. During the time Rhaena and her court remained at Casterly Rock, Jocasta suggested to Rhaena that one of Rhaena's [dragon eggs](wiki:Dragon_eggs) would make a fine gift to [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister), to thank the Lannisters for providing them with a place to stay. Rhaena was not responsive to the suggestion,(wiki:Jocasta_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) and departed from the Rock shortly after.(wiki:Jocasta_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
