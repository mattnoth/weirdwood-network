## Appearances & Description

Ondrew is an old man, with vulturous features(wiki:Ondrew_Locke.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and no teeth left in his mouth.(wiki:Ondrew_Locke.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Despite his age, he is outspoken and bold.(wiki:Ondrew_Locke.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ondrew is invited to the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) but considers himself too old to make the trip.(wiki:Ondrew_Locke.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ondrew, called "old Lord Locke", attends [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark) of [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Bolton) and "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)" (actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)) at Winterfell. [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) notes that the face of Lord Locke seems bestial in the shifting light, reminding Theon of a vulture.(wiki:Ondrew_Locke.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Lord Locke is concerned about the endless snowfall affecting Winterfell. He is overheard saying that [the gods](wiki:Old_gods) have turned against them all, cursing them with "a wind as cold as hell itself and snows that never end."(wiki:Ondrew_Locke.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Lord Locke is last seen having breakfast, sitting beside Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly). While Wyman eats sausages and boiled eggs, the "toothless" Locke contents himself with gruel. When Ser [Hosteen Frey](wiki:Hosteen_Frey) attacks Wyman with a [longsword](wiki:Longsword), Lord Locke starts shouting for a [maester](wiki:Maester) to tend to the wounded man.(wiki:Ondrew_Locke.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
