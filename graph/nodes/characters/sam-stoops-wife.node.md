## Origins

Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) observed the woman cleaning the hearth at [Standfast](wiki:Standfast) and washing clothes in the undercellar. She provided bowls of stew for all, thick with carrots, onions, and barley for Duncan's recruits. The servant also sewed the clothes of the late [Addam Osgrey](wiki:Addam_Osgrey) so they would be wearable for [Egg](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen). While Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey) was away, Ser [Bennis of the Brown Shield](wiki:Bennis_of_the_Brown_Shield) tied up Sam and his wife, and stole the remaining valuables from Standfast.(wiki:Sam_Stoops'_wife.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Appearances & Description

The old woman was so hunched that her head was lower than her shoulders. She was superstitious and believed that [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber), [Lady of Coldmoat](wiki:Lady_of_Coldmoat), sold her unborn children to the [Lord of the Seven Hells](wiki:Lord_of_the_Seven_Hells) and had them collected by [demons](wiki:Demons).(wiki:Sam_Stoops'_wife.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
