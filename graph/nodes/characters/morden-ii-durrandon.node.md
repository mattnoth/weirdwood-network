## Origins

During his reign the decline of [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon) turned back, thanks in part to Morden's [baseborn](wiki:Bastardy) half brother [Ronard Storm](wiki:Ronard_Storm), whom he named as his [castellan](wiki:Castellan). A fearsome warrior, Ronard became ruler of the [Stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) in all but name and took Morden's sister to wife. Within five years, he had usurped [kingship](wiki:Kingship) as well. Morden's own queen placed the crown on Ronard's head and, if songs are true, she shared his bed as well. In the meantime, Morden, deemed harmless, was confined to a cell in the tower.(wiki:Morden_II_Durrandon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_durrandon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
