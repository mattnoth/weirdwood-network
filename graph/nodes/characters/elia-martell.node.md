## Identity

Princess Elia Nymeros Martell, also known as Elia of Dorne, was a Dornish princess from House Martell. She was married to Crown Prince Rhaegar Targaryen and bore him two children: Rhaenys and Aegon (wiki:Elia_Martell). She `HOLDS_TITLE` Princess (track_b: Title).

## Appearances & Description

Said to be beautiful, slender, with black eyes and a flat chest (wiki:Elia_Martell). Always had delicate health. According to Barristan Selmy, she was a gentle, good, and gracious lady, kind and clever with a sweet wit, but frail due to her health (wiki:Elia_Martell).

## Narrative Arc

Elia married Rhaegar Targaryen and bore him two children. At the tourney at Harrenhal, Rhaegar crowned Lyanna Stark as queen of beauty over Elia, an act that scandalized the realm (agot-eddard-15). During Robert's Rebellion, Elia and her children were kept in King's Landing as hostages by the Mad King. During the Sack of King's Landing in 283 AC, Elia was raped and murdered by Ser Gregor Clegane, who killed her son Aegon before her eyes (agot-eddard-07). Her death and the murder of her children became a driving force behind Dornish politics for decades, motivating both Oberyn's quest for justice and Doran's long revenge (wiki:Elia_Martell).
