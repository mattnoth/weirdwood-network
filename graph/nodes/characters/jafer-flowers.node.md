## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Jafer is a member of the ranging party led by [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark) to discover what happened to Ser [Waymar Royce](wiki:Waymar_Royce).(wiki:Jafer_Flowers.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

When [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) says his vows in the [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) grove in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest), [Ghost](wiki:Ghost) brings to him a severed hand.(wiki:Jafer_Flowers.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The hand is then stored in a jar of vinegar in Maester [Aemon's](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) chambers.(wiki:Jafer_Flowers.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

[Bass](wiki:Bass)'s dogs refuse to follow the scent of the hand, so the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) use Ghost as a tracker instead. The party finds the bodies of Jafer and [Othor](wiki:Othor) in the haunted forest; Ghost had earlier torn off the right hand from Jafer's corpse.(wiki:Jafer_Flowers.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Upon inspection, his death had been caused by an axe blow to the neck; [Dywen](wiki:Dywen) thinks the killing blow was caused by Othor's axe. Both corpses have white flesh and black hands.(wiki:Jafer_Flowers.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

The bodies of the two men are brought back to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), but they rise as [wights](wiki:Wights) in the night and attack the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). Jafer's wight kills Ser [Jaremy Rykker](wiki:Jaremy_Rykker) and four other men. Even after Jaremy hacks off Jafer's head, the wight draws the knight's own dagger and stabs him to death. Jafer's body is eventually hacked nearly into pieces.(wiki:Jafer_Flowers.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

[Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), sends Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to show Jafer's hand to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Jafer_Flowers.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Clash of Kings

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), keeps Alliser waiting when the envoy arrives at the capital.(wiki:Jafer_Flowers.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) By the time the skeptical [small council](wiki:Small_council) agrees to see Alliser, Jafer's hand has rotted away.(wiki:Jafer_Flowers.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
