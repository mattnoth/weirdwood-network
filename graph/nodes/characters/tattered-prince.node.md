## Origins

In [262 AC](wiki:262_AC), when the Tattered Prince was twenty-three, the [magisters](wiki:Magister) of [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) chose him to be their new [Prince](wiki:Prince_of_Pentos), mere hours after they'd beheaded the previous one. Instead of accepting their offer and be their disposable puppet, he fled to the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands) and never returned to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos). Taking up the life of a [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword), over the years he rode with the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons), the [Iron Shields](wiki:Iron_Shields), and the [Maiden's Men](wiki:Maiden's_Men). Circa [270 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), along with five brothers-in-arms, he established the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown). Out of the six founders of the company, by [300 AC](wiki:300_AC) he is the only one alive.(wiki:Tattered_Prince.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)(wiki:Tattered_Prince.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_pentos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

There is enmity between the Windblown and the [Company of the Cat](wiki:Company_of_the_Cat). In [299 AC](wiki:299_AC), the two sellsword companies had been on opposite sides of the battle lines in the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands), and bad blood continues to linger. [Bloodbeard](wiki:Bloodbeard), the leader of the Company of the Cat, has a strong dislike for the Tattered Prince, and makes no secret of this, referring to his rival as *old grey-beards in rags*.(wiki:Tattered_Prince.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of the Tattered Prince](wiki:Category:Images_of_the_Tattered_Prince)*

The Tattered Prince is sad-eyed and soft-spoken. He speaks classic [High Valyrian](wiki:High_Valyrian).(wiki:Tattered_Prince.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) His hair and mail are silver-grey. His ragged cloak is made of different colored twists of cloths, blue and grey and purple, red and gold and green, magenta and vermilion and cerulean, all faded by the sun.(wiki:Tattered_Prince.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) The strips are torn and bloodstained,(wiki:Tattered_Prince.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) torn from the surcoats of men he has slain.(wiki:Tattered_Prince.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) He has stated that his appearance is so ordinary that all he has to do is remove his "tatters" and become inconspicuous.(wiki:Tattered_Prince.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) When he travels incognito, he wears a brown wool traveler's cloak.(wiki:Tattered_Prince.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

The Tattered Prince rides a huge grey [warhorse](wiki:Horse). The stallion's spotted hindquarters are covered with the same multi-colored torn rags as his master's cloak. The Tattered Prince sits straight and tall in the high saddle, and his voice is strong enough to carry to every corner of a battlefield.(wiki:Tattered_Prince.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5) [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) thinks him elegant.(wiki:Tattered_Prince.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

After [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) procures their services, the Tattered Prince leads the Windblown toward [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) in the slavers' struggle against [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen). On their way to Mereen he commands a group of Westerosi-born from his company to pretend that they have betrayed him and to join Daenerys's forces. With this plan, he hopes that even if the Yunkai lose the war, he will still be on the winning side. Among the Westerosi he sends to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) are Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) and his companions, although the Tattered Prince is not aware of their identities or goals.(wiki:Tattered_Prince.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-8)

However, when the Prince of Dorne decides to take Dany's dragons, he turns again toward the [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) Windblown. The mercenaries take his gold and promise to help him, but not before reporting his plan to the Tattered Prince. The Prince meets with Quentyn in the cellar of the [Purple Lotus](wiki:Purple_Lotus) and agrees to join forces with him if, as a reward, he is given [Pentos](wiki:Pentos).(wiki:Tattered_Prince.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) He tells Quentyn that he saw [Yurkhaz zo Yunzak](wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak) die, indicating that he was present in [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit) when [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) appeared.(wiki:Tattered_Prince.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) The plan fails since Quentyn is killed by the dragons and Meereen is fully besieged by Yunkish forces.(wiki:Tattered_Prince.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)(wiki:Tattered_Prince.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Later, Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) sends the two remaining [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) on a mission to negotiate with the Tattered Prince. Ser Barristan wants the mercenary captain to help him release the Meereenese hostages held by the [Wise Masters](wiki:Wise_Masters); as a reward, he will be given Pentos.(wiki:Tattered_Prince.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
