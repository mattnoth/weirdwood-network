## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Cedra is among the servants who are allowed access to Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) during her imprisonment at [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear). She has a crush on [Garin](wiki:Garin_(orphan)), which Arianne uses to get Cedra to smuggle a letter to Lord [Franklyn Fowler](wiki:Franklyn_Fowler) out of the castle. She is caught by the spies of [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), and is sent to work at the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens) instead.(wiki:Cedra.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
