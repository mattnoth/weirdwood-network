## Appearances & Description

Short and chinless,(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Ragot41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Clydas has a few white hairs still clinging to his scalp.(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The steward has dim pink eyes(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Ragot41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) and is hunched(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) with rounded shoulders.(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Because Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) is blind, the half-blind(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Clydas reads messages to him.(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Clydas aids Aemon when the maester treats the wounds of [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow).(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Aemon, Clydas, and most of the women from [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town) take the winch cage to the top of the Wall for safety during the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) by [Thenns](wiki:Thenns). A midwife from Moles Town aids Aemon and Clydas as they deal with those wounded in the fighting.(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) [Owen the Oaf](wiki:Owen_the_Oaf) and Clydas distribute cups of onion broth to archers during the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall).(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Clydas guides Jon with a lantern when they investigate the tunnel through the Wall, and they find the bodies of [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye) and [Mag the Mighty](wiki:Mag_the_Mighty).(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

Clydas gives flagons of goats' milk and [dreamwine](wiki:Dreamwine) to the wounded after the battle, including to black brothers, [king's men](wiki:King's_men), and [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men).(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) He helps count during the [choosing](wiki:Choosing) of a new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch).(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

### A Feast for Crows

When Aemon is sent away by Jon, the new Lord Commander, Clydas takes over the duties of tending to the ravens and the wounded. Before leaving, Aemon has Clydas mark passages in the *[Jade Compendium](wiki:Jade_Compendium)* for Jon.(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Clydas mulls wine and offers a cup to Jon, who tells the steward of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s sword, [Lightbringer](wiki:Lightbringer).(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) Clydas informs Jon of a letter sent by [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) after the [siege of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin),(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) and he also informs Jon of a letter from Stannis after the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

Clydas treats [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark) when she flees to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) After Alys tells her tale, Clydas sends a message to Deepwood hoping to warn Stannis of [Arnolf Karstark](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark)'s treachery.(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Clydas brings Jon letters from [Cotter Pyke](wiki:Cotter_Pyke).(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Jon tasks Clydas with tending to any of [Tormund](wiki:Tormund)'s people who are sick.(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

Clydas is frightened by a [letter to Jon](wiki:Bastard_Letter) entitled "Bastard" and sealed with pink [wax](wiki:Wax). He brings it to Jon unopened.(wiki:Clydas.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
