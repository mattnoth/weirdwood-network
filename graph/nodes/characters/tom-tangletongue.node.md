## Origins

Tom learned about the death of the [Grey Ghost](wiki:Grey_Ghost) from [Volantenes](wiki:Volantis). He and his father aided Ser [Alfred Broome](wiki:Alfred_Broome) during the [fall of Dragonstone](wiki:Fall_of_Dragonstone). After Ser [Marston Waters](wiki:Marston_Waters) saved Lady [Baela Targaryen](wiki:Baela_Targaryen) from Alfred, Tom took her to Dragonstone's maester.(wiki:Tom_Tangletongue.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Tom was killed during the purge of [Aegon II](wiki:Aegon_II)'s loyalists following the green defeat at the [Battle of the Kingsroad](wiki:Battle_of_the_Kingsroad). He was surprised in the castle yards as he left the stables and promptly beheaded.(wiki:Tom_Tangletongue.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
