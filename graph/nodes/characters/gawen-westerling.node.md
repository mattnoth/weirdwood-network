## Origins

As the [Lord of the Crag](wiki:Lord_of_the_Crag), Gawen headed an old and proud, though impoverished, family.(wiki:Gawen_Westerling.cite_ref-Rawoiafgawen_westerling.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Gawen wed [Sybell](wiki:Sybell_Spicer) of [House Spicer](wiki:House_Spicer), a wealthy House of [lowborn](wiki:Smallfolk) origins.(wiki:Gawen_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) This [marriage](wiki:Marriage) is said to have somewhat sordid origins,(wiki:Gawen_Westerling.cite_ref-Rawoiafgawen_westerling.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) and Gawen is rumored to have been entrapped into the marriage.(wiki:Gawen_Westerling.cite_ref-Rawoiafcrag.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Gawen once offered his elder daughter, [Jeyne](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling), as a match for either [Martyn](wiki:Martyn_Lannister) or [Willem Lannister](wiki:Willem_Lannister), but was rejected because of Jeyne's mother.(wiki:Gawen_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

## Appearances & Description

Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) describes Gawen as a good man,(wiki:Gawen_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) though believes he has more honor than sense.(wiki:Gawen_Westerling.cite_ref-Rawoiafgawen_westerling.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Gawen_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

At the outbreak of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Lord Gawen rides in Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s host besieging [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun). After Jaime's host is drawn away and ambushed by Lord [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), Gawen is captured by Lord [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister) in the ensuring [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Gawen_Westerling.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Gawen_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Clash of Kings

After being captured, Gawen is held at [Seagard](wiki:Seagard) by the [Mallisters](wiki:House_Mallister) for ransom.(wiki:Gawen_Westerling.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)(wiki:Gawen_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

During his imprisonment, the Stark army begins attacking the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) and soon focuses on capturing the [Crag](wiki:Crag) with its lord absent.(wiki:Gawen_Westerling.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Gawen's seat is soon captured by the Starks.(wiki:Gawen_Westerling.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Robb conquers Gawen's castle with the [storming of the Crag](wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag). While still imprisoned at Seagard, Gawen's wife, Lady [Sybell Spicer](wiki:Sybell_Spicer), and [castellan](wiki:Castellan), Ser [Rolph Spicer](wiki:Rolph_Spicer), conspire with Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) to help bring about Robb Stark's downfall.(wiki:Gawen_Westerling.cite_ref-Rawoiafrolph_spicer.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Gawen is freed from Seagard after his daughter, [Jeyne](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling), marries [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), although they wed without his consent.(wiki:Gawen_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

Gawen is pardoned by the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) after the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Gawen_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Gawen is present at the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun). He is with the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister)-[Frey](wiki:House_Frey) army urging the commanders to settle the siege peacefully, as he is worried about possible retaliations against his family who are within the castle.(wiki:Gawen_Westerling.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) After Jaime negotiates Riverrun's surrender, Gawen travels with his family back to the [Crag](wiki:Crag).(wiki:Gawen_Westerling.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
