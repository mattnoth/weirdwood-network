## Origins

Ser Humfrey was [tourney](wiki:Tourney) [knight](wiki:Knight) of renown. He won a [great melee](wiki:Tourney_at_Maidenpool) at [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) in [208 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), and overthrew Ser [Donnel of Duskendale](wiki:Donnel_of_Duskendale) and Lords [Arryn](wiki:Donnel_Arryn) and [Royce](wiki:House_Royce) in the lists.(wiki:Humfrey_Hardyng.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
In [209 AC](wiki:209_AC), Ser Humfrey participated in the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow), beginning the tourney as one of the five champions of the fair maid. In the first tilt, he was challenged by Lord [Medgar Tully](wiki:Medgar_Tully), who Humphrey knocked from the saddle on the second lance, finally defeating him with sword afoot. Next, he was challenged by Ser [Joseth Mallister](wiki:Joseth_Mallister), who had to be carried from the field unconscious. He also faced his brother by marriage, Ser [Humfrey Beesbury](wiki:Humfrey_Beesbury), in the lists on the first day. They broke a dozen lances apiece in an epic struggle the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) soon began calling "the Battle of Humfrey." He won against his brother by marriage as well, and in total defeated fourteen formidable knights on the first day.(wiki:Humfrey_Hardyng.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
In the final tilt of the first day, he was challenged by Prince [Aerion Targaryen](wiki:Aerion_Targaryen). During the first course, Prince Aerion's [lance](wiki:Lance) impaled his [horse](wiki:Horse) just above the [armor](wiki:Armor) that protected his breastbone, and exploded out of the back of his neck in a gout of bright blood. Ser Humfrey tried to leap free, but a foot caught in a stirrup and they heard his shriek as his leg was crushed between the splintered fence and falling horse. Lord Ashford declared Ser Humfrey the victor and awarded him Prince Aerion’s courser. Nevertheless, Humfrey was not able to continue as champion, as his leg was broken in two places. Prince [Baelor Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)) sent his own [maester](wiki:Maester) to tend him.(wiki:Humfrey_Hardyng.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
Ser Humfrey joined the side of Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) in the [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven), wanting to get his revenge on Aerion, one of Duncan's accusers. He was seriously wounded in the trial and died of his wounds later.(wiki:Humfrey_Hardyng.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)
