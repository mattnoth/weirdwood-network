## Identity

Dagmer, known as Cleftjaw, is a veteran ironborn raider and captain of the longship Foamdrinker. He served as master-at-arms at Pyke (wiki:Dagmer).

## Origins

Born in the Iron Islands (track_b: Born).

## Allegiances

## Appearances & Description

Dagmer has a fearsome cleft in his jaw from an axe wound that split his face nearly in two. He wears a forked beard and is missing teeth. Despite his appearance, he is one of Theon's few genuine friends among the ironborn (wiki:Dagmer.cite_ref-Racok11).

## Narrative Arc

Dagmer served as master-at-arms at Pyke and helped raise Theon Greyjoy before Theon was taken as a ward by Eddard Stark. When Theon returns to the Iron Islands, Dagmer is one of the few who welcomes him warmly (wiki:Dagmer.cite_ref-Racok24). Theon persuades Dagmer to lead an attack on Torrhen's Square as a feint to draw Winterfell's garrison away, enabling Theon's capture of Winterfell (wiki:Dagmer.cite_ref-Racok46). After the fall of Winterfell, Dagmer holds Torrhen's Square. By ADWD, he is besieged there and eventually surrenders to northern forces (wiki:Dagmer.cite_ref-Radwd26).
