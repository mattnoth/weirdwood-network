## Cross-identity: Theon as "Reek (III)"

In *A Dance with Dragons*, [Ramsay Bolton](ramsay-snow) breaks Theon by torture and renames him
**"Reek"** (the alias `Reek (III)` on this node) — see [the breaking of Theon Greyjoy](breaking-of-theon-greyjoy).
The name "Reek" has **three bearers** across the books and must not be collapsed into one identity:
**Reek (I)** is the original Bolton servant (the [reek](reek) node); **Ramsay-as-Reek** is the ACOK
disguise (modeled `ramsay-snow IMPERSONATES reek`); **Reek (III)** is Theon. A query for "Reek" in an
ADWD context resolves here.

## Origins

Theon refers to [Theon Stark](wiki:Theon_Stark), a [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), as his [namesake](wiki:Namesake).(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1) Theon received beatings at [Pyke](wiki:Pyke) from his older brothers, [Rodrik](wiki:Rodrik_Greyjoy) and [Maron](wiki:Maron_Greyjoy).(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) He received his first sword from [Dagmer Cleftjaw](wiki:Dagmer_Cleftjaw).(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

Theon was a boy of ten when [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion) was crushed and his brothers were killed, making him the heir to [House Greyjoy](wiki:House_Greyjoy) and the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands). Theon was taken from his father, Lord [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), as a [hostage](wiki:Hostage) and [ward](wiki:Ward) by Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) and has been raised at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) for nine years since.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

Considered to be in [fosterage](wiki:Fosterage),(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) Theon was raised with the [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) children and tutored with them in all disciplines, including instructions from [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Luwin](wiki:Luwin)(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-2) and training at arms from Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel).(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) His relationship with the Starks is respectful, and he has come to consider [Robb](wiki:Robb_Stark) a close friend and younger brother.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-6)(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) Although they trained together,(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-1) Theon ignored the sullen [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), Lord Eddard's [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son and another outsider in the family.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-7) However, though he claims to [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) that Lord Eddard was like a second father to him,(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) and although Eddard indeed attempted to be like a father to him from time to time, Theon still thought Eddard to be cold.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-8) As a child, Theon used to think that Eddard might marry him to his eldest daughter [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and claim him for a son.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Theon and Robb trained with wooden swords on the steps of Winterfell,(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3) and together with Jon Snow were trained at arms by Ser Rodrik Cassel.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-2) He played in the [castle's godswood](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell),(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-3) and participated in melees during [tourneys](wiki:Tourney).(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-9) In [296 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) Theon traveled with Lord Eddard to [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Torrhen's_Square), where he spent a fortnight with [Benfred Tallhart](wiki:Benfred_Tallhart), whom Theon teased for his large neck.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-1)

Theon has slept with numerous women,(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-10) including [Kyra](wiki:Kyra) and [Bessa](wiki:Bessa_(Winterfell)) of the [winter town](wiki:Winter_town)(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-4) and a [miller's wife](wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water) along the [Acorn Water](wiki:Acorn_Water).(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) He lost his [virginity](wiki:Virgin) to the [brewer's](wiki:Barth_(brewer)) wife in Winterfell's [godswood](wiki:Godswood).(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2)(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-4)

## Appearances & Description

In a nightmare before Winterfell's fall, Theon dreamed of feasting with the dead: King Robert with his guts spilling out, Lord Eddard headless, Robb walking in from the night with Grey Wind, "man and wolf alike bled from half a hundred savage wounds." A girl crowned in pale blue roses with a white gown spattered in gore stood among them — only Lyanna. The dream is an early ACOK foreshadowing of the Red Wedding (Bran Stark POV, acok-theon-05 — `sources/chapters/acok/acok-theon-05.md:140`)

Theon is a lean, dark, handsome youth,(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) with black hair, and a lean, dark face.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) He seems to find everything amusing.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) He is known for his confidence(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) and his cocky smile.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1) He is promiscuous and vain,(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-2)(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) but behind his arrogant exterior hides insecurities relating to his uncertain familial and social identity. Theon seeks approval from the Starks, but usually feels unsatisfied.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1)(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) The youth is not particularly religious.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-2)

Theon wears a fur-lined cloak, black silk gloves, black leather boots, silvery-grey lambswool breeches, a white leather belt, and a black velvet doublet embroidered with the [kraken](wiki:Kraken) of [House Greyjoy](wiki:House_Greyjoy). He has purchased a [golden](wiki:Gold) chain by paying the "[gold price](wiki:Currency)".(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-3) Another outfit includes a striped black and gold satin tunic, and a leather jerkin with [silver](wiki:Silver) studs.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) For combat situations Theon wears a black kraken-emblazoned surcoat over a shirt of fine mail.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

An experienced hunter(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) and [horse](wiki:Horse) rider,(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) Theon is accomplished with the longbow(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-3) and he also wields a dirk.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-4) He carries a [longsword](wiki:Longsword) in a scabbard striped with the Greyjoy colors of black and gold.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-5) Theon thinks of himself as a graceful dancer.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When the [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) boys witness the execution of [Gared](wiki:Gared), Theon ceremonially presents Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) with the [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) sword [Ice](wiki:Ice). Theon kicks away Gared's severed head afterwards.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-2) When King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) rides north to ask Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) to assume the position of [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Theon joins the king's hunt.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) Theon remains in [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) after Ned Stark leaves with Robert for [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-1)

[Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) travels to the capital to inform Lord Stark of the [catspaw](wiki:Catspaw)'s [Valyrian steel dagger](wiki:Valyrian_steel_dagger), and Ned advises his wife to watch Theon in case they need the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) of Theon's father, Lord [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy).(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

Theon and [Hallis Mollen](wiki:Hallis_Mollen) advise [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), the acting [lord](wiki:Lord) in Winterfell,(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0) and Theon thinks Robb should call the banners when Eddard is wounded in King's Landing.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-5) When a group of [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) and [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) deserters attack [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood), Theon kills [Stiv](wiki:Stiv) with an arrow. Theon feels hurt when Robb criticizes him for risking Bran's life with the shot.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-6)

At the outbreak of [war](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Theon becomes Robb's trusted advisor.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-7) He accompanies Robb and his army south,(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0) and becomes one of Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully)'s scouts.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-2) Riding a [courser](wiki:Courser)(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1) as one of [Robb's personal guard](wiki:Robb_Stark's_personal_guard),(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0) Theon takes pride in almost fighting Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) in the [Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-3)(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0) He advocates assaulting the [Twins](wiki:Twins)(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-1) and executing the captive Jaime.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-1) After the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), Theon accompanies Robb to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), where his friend is proclaimed [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)

### A Clash of Kings

[Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), now [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) and [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident), sends Theon from Riverrun to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) to forge an alliance with his father, Lord [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), asking him to attack the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) with his [longships](wiki:Longship). Robb's mother, [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), unsuccessfully advises Robb against the decision.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0)

Theon travels with Lord [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister) and [Patrek Mallister](wiki:Patrek_Mallister) to [Seagard](wiki:Seagard), where he boards the *[Myraham](wiki:Myraham)*. During his journey home, Theon sleeps with the [captain's daughter](wiki:Captain's_daughter), who wants to be his [salt wife](wiki:Salt_wife). He considers the [red comet](wiki:Red_comet) to be a good omen of his mission.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-11) Theon is met at [Pyke](wiki:Pyke)'s port by his uncle, [Aeron Damphair](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy), a [priest](wiki:Priest_of_the_Drowned_God) of the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God), who pours salt water over him as a blessing from their god. At the castle of Pyke, Theon is given chambers in the [Bloody Keep](wiki:Bloody_Keep).(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-12)

Theon brings Robb's offer of alliance to his father, hoping to lead the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) against [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport)(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) and claim [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) from [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) as his own seat.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-13) Balon—offended by Theon's northern ways and attire, as well as his failure to pay the [iron price](wiki:Iron_price)—rejects Robb's offer to ally and grant a crown.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-14)

At [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport), Theon buys a [horse](wiki:Horse), [Smiler](wiki:Smiler), from Lord [Sawane Botley](wiki:Sawane_Botley). He also acquires a [squire](wiki:Squire), the mute boy [Wex Pyke](wiki:Wex_Pyke), Sawane's [bastard](wiki:Bastard) nephew. Theon meets his sister, [Asha](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), who flirts with him under the assumed identity "[Esgred](wiki:Esgred)". On the journey back to Pyke, "Esgred" tries to convince Theon to go see his mother, [Alannys Harlaw](wiki:Alannys_Harlaw), but he says he is too busy. At a feast that night at Pyke, Asha reveals herself and takes the place of honor beside their father, humilitating Theon. Theon learns of Balon's plans to conquer the north, since most of its fighting men are south of the [Neck](wiki:Neck) with Robb. Theon receives a position of minor authority.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Theon is sent by his father to [harry the Stone Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore) under the supervision of Aeron and [Dagmer Cleftjaw](wiki:Dagmer). Theon commands eight longships, and captains the *[Sea Bitch](wiki:Sea_Bitch)* himself. He raids several villages but although he holds the command, his men fear his uncle Aeron or consider Theon to be a soft boy from the green lands. [Benfred Tallhart](wiki:Benfred_Tallhart) and his [Wild Hares](wiki:Wild_Hares) are sent to go deal with Theon and his [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) by [Leobald Tallhart](wiki:Leobald_Tallhart),(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0) but when the Wild Hares do not send scouts ahead, Theon and his men easily surprise and defeat them. Theon orders their leader to be spared for questioning, not expecting to be faced with a familiar face. Although Theon hoped to question Benfred, the latter refuses to answer any questions and instead calls Theon a [traitor](wiki:Traitor), spitting on him. For this insult, Aeron insists that Benfred be drowned.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-4) Theon decides not to witness Benfred's death, and instead goes to his looting men. Watching them squabble over plunder, Theon wishes break it up by shooting [Todric](wiki:Todric)'s drinking horn from his hand. However, Todric suddenly moves and is fatally hit by the arrow in the belly, so Theon claims he meant to do that.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-5)

Theon is dissatisfied with his duties, and feels the insignificant raids he has been ordered to do are beneath him, so he devises a new plan. He gives Aeron six longships to continue the raiding, and gives Dagmer most of the remaining men to besiege [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Torrhen's_Square). Theon knows that this attack will draw the levies from [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), so that he himself can capture the defenseless castle with only a handful of men.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-6)

### A Dance with Dragons

Theon has been [tortured](wiki:Torture) repeatedly by [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), who has since been legitimized by King Tommen as Ramsay Bolton. Ramsay and the [Bastard's Boys](wiki:Bastard's_Boys) [remove the skin](wiki:Flaying) on several of Theon's toes and fingers, leaving him in agony for days before removing the joints but only after Theon begs him to remove them. He breaks and removes several of Theon's teeth because Ramsay hates his smiles.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) It is also hinted that Ramsay may have emasculated Theon.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-2)(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-5)(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-48) Theon is forced to take on the guise of **Reek**, the name of Ramsay's [late serving man](wiki:Reek). He is forbidden to bathe and is caked from head to toe in filth and excrement.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

Due to the torture endured at Ramsay's hands, Theon looks like he has aged forty years. Besides missing teeth, many more are loose, so he cannot eat without suffering agony. He has lost three stone in weight, his skin has turned pasty, and his hair is white and brittle. He can no longer use a bow due to the loss of three fingers,(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-4) has difficulty riding,(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-5) and he hobbles like an old man due to the loss of several toes.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

Before he endured the worst of Ramsay's tortures, Theon was allowed to escape with the help of [Kyra](wiki:Kyra), his former bedwarmer at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). His captor let them get a day's head start before chasing them with his [hunting hounds](wiki:Bastard's_girls). Theon was taken back to the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort)'s dungeon, while Kyra was killed by Ramsay and his hounds. Theon is mentally and physically broken by Ramsay and lives in great fear of him.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5) He disgusts the Frey cousins, as well as [Arnolf Karstark](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark) and [Hother Umber](wiki:Hother_Umber) during a feast at the Dreadfort.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)

With Ramsay's father, Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), returning to the [north](wiki:North), Ramsay has Theon washed and properly clothed. Pretending to still be a prince, Theon is sent to deliver peace terms to the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) occupying [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin). Theon gives the [gift of mercy](wiki:Gift_of_mercy) to the dying [Ralf Kenning](wiki:Ralf_Kenning), and relays Ramsay's promise of food and safe passage to the survivors. When they do surrender, Ramsay has them all flayed alive and displays their skinless bodies along the [causeway](wiki:Causeway) to the ruined castle. As a reward, a rag-clad Theon is allowed to sleep among Ramsay's [dogs](wiki:Dog). He is present when Ramsay is introduced to his bride, "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)", whom he instantly recognizes as [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole).(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-6) With the [siege of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin) resolved, Ramsay sends a letter and a scrap of Theon's skin to his sister [Asha](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) at [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte), and to [Dagmer](wiki:Dagmer) at [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Torrhen's_Square), warning them to abandon the north.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_49-0)

Roose angers Ramsay by taking Theon from him in [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) and introducing him to Lady [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin) at [Barrow Hall](wiki:Barrow_Hall).(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Theon insists he is actually Reek, believing that "Theon" was some other man.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-7)(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) Roose's host and other northern bannermen gather at the partially-rebuilt [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-7)

Because Theon was a ward of Winterfell for ten years and is the closest thing to kin "Arya" has, he gives the bride away at [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark) in the [godswood of Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell). Jeyne pleads with Theon several times before the wedding to help rescue her, but Theon refuses. During the wedding banquet held in the Great Hall, Theon sits with Barbrey and is reviled by those in attendance.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-8) Fearing that Roose will return him to Ramsay, Theon hopes that [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) will arrive to kill them all, putting him out of his misery.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-9) During the [bedding](wiki:Bedding), Ramsay has Theon strip his bride for him and makes Theon watch as he humiliates and degrades Jeyne, then forces Theon to prepare Jeyne sexually before he rapes her.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-10)

### The Winds of Winter

Theon is held prisoner by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), who plans to execute Theon to placate [northern mountain clansmen](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans) who want vengeance for the supposed murders of [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark). Stannis intends to keep Theon alive for the time being, as he might have useful information about [Roose](wiki:Roose_Bolton) and [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow). Theon, hanging in chains from a wall, is present during the agreement between Stannis and [Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris) of the [Iron Bank of Braavos](wiki:Iron_Bank_of_Braavos), as well as the exposure of [Arnolf Karstark](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark)'s planned treachery and the subsequent arrest of Arnolf's family and men.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-twow_51-1)

Theon recalls how he confessed to [Asha](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) the truth about [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) and the [miller's sons](wiki:Miller's_sons), and about the washerwomen who were really spearwives, and how he told Jeyne that she must keep pretending to be Arya Stark. After Asha's attempts to ransom Theon fail, and Stannis says his men want to burn him alive as a sacrifice to [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor), Asha urges Stannis to execute Theon as [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) would have - by beheading him in front of a [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree). After she says it, the [maester](wiki:Maester)'s [ravens](wiki:Raven) in the room excitedly squawk the words, "the tree", and "Theon". Theon is pleased that the ravens know his name.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-twow_51-2)

When [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) and the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) arrive in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) wonders if it has something to do with Theon.(wiki:Theon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-54)
