## Origins

A wealthy vassal of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen), Daemon was among the closest friends of [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), [Lord of Dragonstone](wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone), and his sisters, [Visenya](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) and [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen). After deciding to invade and conquer [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), Aegon summoned his bannermen and friends to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) for counsel, Daemon among them. After planning his conquest with Daemon and his allies, the Lord of Dragonstone announced his intentions to the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), beginning [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Daemon_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)

Daemon's ships carried Aegon's soldiers across [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay),(wiki:Daemon_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) landing his army at the mouth of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush). After Aegon was crowned as [King of All Westeros](wiki:King_of_All_Westeros) shortly after [landing](wiki:Aegon's_Landing) on the mainland, Daemon was counted as one of the oldest supporters of House Targaryen. Daemon was therefore given the honor of becoming Aegon's [master of ships](wiki:Master_of_ships) and given control of the new [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet),(wiki:Daemon_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-11) the bulk of which was formed from the warships of the [Velaryon fleet](wiki:Velaryon_fleet).(wiki:Daemon_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

After [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) refused to bend the knee to House Targaryen, the king sent his admiral to take the city of [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown) on the coast of the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn). A hastily assembled Arryn fleet, augmented by a dozen [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) warships, managed to repel the royal fleet, and Daemon perished during the [battle in the waters off Gulltown](wiki:Battle_in_the_waters_off_Gulltown). While the Valemen sank a third of the royal fleet, Visenya avenged Daemon by burning the Arryn fleet with her [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar).(wiki:Daemon_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-12)
