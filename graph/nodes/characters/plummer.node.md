## Origins

Plummer was the [master of the games](wiki:Master_of_the_games) for the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow). He initially rejected Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall)'s entry into the tourney on the grounds that no one knew who he was, until Prince [Baelor Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)) vouched for him.(wiki:Plummer.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
