## Appearances & Description

Justin is a large,(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) fleshy man with loose limbs.(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He has pink cheeks,(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) blue eyes,(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) and a tangled mop of thick straight hair, white blond and pale as flax.(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) The fair knight has a neatly-trimmed blond beard.(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btheon_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctheon_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Justin wears a tunic displaying the triple spiral of [House Massey](wiki:House_Massey)(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) and a cloak.(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) He wields sword(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btheon_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctheon_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) and dagger(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) and rides a big destrier.(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser Justin fights alongside [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). He and Ser [Richard Horpe](wiki:Richard_Horpe) advise the retreat to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)) when the battle appears lost.(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btheon_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctheon_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)

### A Storm of Swords

Stannis sails for the [north](wiki:North) and is victorious over the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) army of [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) in the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall).(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Justin is part of Stannis's court at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Still among Stannis's knights and [sworn swords](wiki:Sworn_sword),(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Justin sits high in his councils.(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Justin and Ser [Richard Horpe](wiki:Richard_Horpe) are sent south from Castle Black, their departure noted by [Dywen](wiki:Dywen). [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), deduces the so-called wrong-way rangers are acting as envoys for Stannis.(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

Upon his return to Castle Black, Justin argues with Jon about the likely length of [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton)'s [siege of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin), believing the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) will hold out for a longer period. Justin counsels Stannis to strike against the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort). and he taunts Ser [Godry Farring](wiki:Godry_Farring) about the basis for his nickname, Giantslayer. Stannis believes that Justin and Richard both aspire to be named [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell) for their service, but the king plans to give the title to [Arnolf Karstark](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark). Stannis also believes that Justin hopes to marry [Val](wiki:Val).(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7)

Stannis captures [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) and defeats her [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) in the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Justin reluctantly follows Stannis in his [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell), fearing the long journey and the prospect of a siege in the snow. Justin commands the baggage train and is concerned about the scarcity of provisions. He believes that [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) brings good fortune to Stannis and wishes that she was with the host instead of at the [Wall](wiki:Wall), but he is less devout than some [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men).(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8)

Justin is courteous to Asha, bringing her food, [wine](wiki:Wine), and company. Asha thinks he wishes to marry her, claim her lands, seat her on the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair) at [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), and rule through her as her lord and master since he has lost [his own lands](wiki:Stonedance) through supporting Stannis. Asha believes her defeat at the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk) by her uncle, [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), and her defeat at Deepwood by Stannis has lessened her in the eyes of the ironborn, however. She does not deflate Justin's hopes but does not see him as a strong prospect.(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-9)

Justin warns Asha when the others begin to talk of [sacrificing an unbeliever](wiki:Blood_sacrifice) to [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor) to end the snowstorms that plague the march on Winterfell. Justin's baggage train suffers heavy losses with horses foundering and exhausted men sitting to die in the snow. He removes Asha's fetters on the twentieth day of the march, as the horse drawing her wayn has died.(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-10)

When Melisandre prophesies a girl coming to the Wall, Jon Snow guesses she is [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) and fears that Stannis would want to marry her to Justin, Richard, or Godry.(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) The girl turns out to be [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark), however, and Jon arranges [her marriage](wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark) to [Sigorn](wiki:Sigorn) of the [Thenns](wiki:Thenns).(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Justin's destrier dies while Stannis's host is snowbound at a [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village). Justin intervenes when Ser [Clayton Suggs](wiki:Clayton_Suggs) manhandles Asha and threatens to have her burned at the stake. When they eat together in the village's longhall, Justin argues they should remain camped instead of pressing on to Winterfell. Justin leaves the dinner when Richard accuses him of cowardice and questions his faith in R'hllor.(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5)

### The Winds of Winter

Stannis orders Ser Justin to accompany the banker [Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris) back to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos). Justin is to first escort "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)" safely to her brother [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) at the [Wall](wiki:Wall). He is to be accompanied by [Alysane Mormont](wiki:Alysane_Mormont) and the brothers of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) who escorted Tycho south from the Wall. Once in Braavos he will use Stannis's newly-acquired wealth from the [Iron Bank of Braavos](wiki:Iron_Bank_of_Braavos) to hire at least twenty thousand [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords), plus the ships needed to bring them back across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea).(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btheon_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctheon_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5)

Stannis tasks Justin to return with the sellswords even if he falls in battle, and to fight the war in the name of his daughter, [Shireen Baratheon](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon). Justin protests that his place is by Stannis's side if there is to be battle, but Stannis says that he is better suited as an envoy than a warrior. When Justin counsels Stannis to undertake the journey himself and raise an army like Ser [Aegor Rivers](wiki:Aegor_Rivers) did after the [Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field), the angry Stannis is reminded of Justin's counsel to flee the Blackwater. Before leaving Justin appeals to Stannis for a [lordship](wiki:Lord) to improve his standing as an envoy, but is rebuffed. He asks Stannis to send Asha with him, still pursuing marriage with her. Stannis refuses but allows that he may be rewarded with the marriage on his return.(wiki:Justin_Massey.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btheon_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctheon_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-6)
