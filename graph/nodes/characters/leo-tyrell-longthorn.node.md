## Identity

Leo Tyrell, known as Leo Longthorn, was the Lord of Highgarden and head of House Tyrell during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen (wiki:Leo_Tyrell_Longthorn.cite_ref-1).

## Appearances & Description

In 209 AC, Leo was a slender man with silver in his hair and a grey beard. He wore green and gold, and his shield displayed the golden rose of House Tyrell. He rode a white charger with green satin trappings patterned with golden roses (wiki:Leo_Tyrell_Longthorn.cite_ref-1). Leo Longthorn was well respected and liked. Nearly a hundred years later, he is regarded as one of the greatest knights ever produced from House Tyrell, and some consider him the finest jouster of his era (wiki:Leo_Tyrell_Longthorn.cite_ref-1).

## Narrative Arc

Leo participated in the First Blackfyre Rebellion and the Tourney at Ashford Meadow in 209 AC, as depicted in The Hedge Knight (wiki:Leo_Tyrell_Longthorn.cite_ref-1). In ASOS, he is mentioned as a past Lord of Highgarden (wiki:Leo_Tyrell_Longthorn.cite_ref-Rasos38).
