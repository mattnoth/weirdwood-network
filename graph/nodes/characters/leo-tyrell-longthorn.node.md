## Origins

### First Blackfyre Rebellion

Lord Leo participated in the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), fighting on behalf of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen). He won notable victories in the [Reach](wiki:Reach) against [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre)'s supporters, though his forces were unable to gather quickly enough to arrive in time for the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field) in [196 AC](wiki:196_AC).(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(Longthorn).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_house_tyrell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### Tourney at Ashford Meadow

Lord Leo participated in the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC), beginning the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) as one of the champions. In the first tilt, he was challenged by Lord [Damon Lannister](wiki:Damon_Lannister_(lord)), who yielded on the third lance after having his helm knocked off. Leo was next challenged by Lord [Pearse Caron](wiki:Pearse_Caron), who was defeated after his longaxe caught in Leo's shield after a fight afoot. One-eyed Ser [Robyn Rhysling](wiki:Robyn_Rhysling) lost his helm to Leo's lance in their first course, yet refused to yield. Three times more, they rode at each other, but Leo was too chivalrous to aim another lance at Robyn's unprotected head. Finally, the [Lord of Highgarden](wiki:Lord_of_Highgarden) struck Robyn's breastplate a solid thump right over the heart and sent him cartwheeling to the earth.(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(Longthorn).cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Prince [Baelor Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)), Prince [Maekar Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen), [Lord Ashford](wiki:Lord_Ashford), and Lord Leo judged Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) for having attacked Prince [Aerion Targaryen](wiki:Aerion_Targaryen); a [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven) followed.(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(Longthorn).cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

### Later Life

During the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls) in [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) and his squire, Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen), drank a toast to the health of Leo, as there were rumors that he was ailing.(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(Longthorn).cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Appearances & Description

In [209 AC](wiki:209_AC), Leo was a slender man with silver in his hair and a grey beard. He wore green and gold, and his shield was decorated with the golden rose of [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell).(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(Longthorn).cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) He rode a white charger with green satin trappings patterned with golden roses.(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(Longthorn).cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Leo Longthorn was well respected and liked.(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(Longthorn).cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Nearly a hundred years later, he is regarded as one of the greatest [knights](wiki:Knight) ever produced from House Tyrell, and some consider him the finest jouster ever to couch a lance.(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(Longthorn).cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(Longthorn).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_house_tyrell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

According to Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell), Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) wants one of his sons to be another Leo Longthorn. [Willas Tyrell](wiki:Willas_Tyrell) was encouraged to join the lists at too young an age, however, and was crippled during a joust with Oberyn. The [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) prince is skeptical of the claim that Willas's youngest brother, Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell), is a better jouster than Leo had been.(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(Longthorn).cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
