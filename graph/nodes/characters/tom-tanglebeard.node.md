## Origins

The Two Toms aided Ser [Alfred Broome](wiki:Alfred_Broome) during the [fall of Dragonstone](wiki:Fall_of_Dragonstone).(wiki:Tom_Tanglebeard.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

The Two Toms accompanied King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) when he sailed to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Tom Tanglebeard was later killed in the purge of the king's loyalists. Agents of the conspirators found him on a tavern of [Eel Alley](wiki:Eel_Alley) and drowned him in a cask of ale when he argued he was a simple fisherman looking for ale.(wiki:Tom_Tanglebeard.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
