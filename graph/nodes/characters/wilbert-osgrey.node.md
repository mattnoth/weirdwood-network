## Origins

According to a story told by Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey), a [King of the Rock](wiki:King_of_the_Rock), either [Lancel IV Lannister](wiki:Lancel_IV_Lannister) or [Lancel V Lannister](wiki:Lancel_V_Lannister), attempted to cut a piece out of the [Reach](wiki:Reach) while [Gyles III Gardener](wiki:Gyles_III_Gardener), [King of the Reach](wiki:King_of_the_Reach), was battling the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) in the east. It fell to Wilbert to stop Lancel as his his older brothers were off in the east with Gyles. During the battle, Wilbert came face to face with the King of the Rock. It is said the king had the [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) sword [Brightroar](wiki:Brightroar); however, it was probably a common steel sword of the same name, which has been conflated with the Valyrian blade.(wiki:Wilbert_Osgrey.cite_ref-6) Lancel fought Wilbert for half a day, breaking the knight's shield and sword and chopping the Little Lion to bits.(wiki:Wilbert_Osgrey.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) As Wilbert was dying, however, he drove his dagger into a gap in Lancel's armor and killed the king, causing the [westermen](wiki:Westermen) to retreat.(wiki:Wilbert_Osgrey.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)(wiki:Wilbert_Osgrey.cite_ref-Rawoiafwilbert_osgrey.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Wilbert_Osgrey.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

According to Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel), Lancel IV yielded the Valyrian steel sword Brightroar.(wiki:Wilbert_Osgrey.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) When the King of the Rock invaded the Reach, Gyles abandoned his siege of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) to deal with the westermen.(wiki:Wilbert_Osgrey.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Yandel writes that Lancel died in battle at [Red Lake](wiki:Red_Lake).(wiki:Wilbert_Osgrey.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

## Appearances & Description

Ser Wilbert was the youngest of five brothers and so earned the name the Little Lion in childhood. He grew to be a tall, valiant [knight](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Wilbert_Osgrey.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
