## Identity

The Kindly Man is a priest of the Faceless Men at the House of Black and White in Braavos. Arya Stark gives him this name because of his kind face; he is not named in the narrative. He appears to hold a position of authority within the guild, overseeing novices and directing their training. He is also sometimes referred to as "plague face," a separate identity/face he wears.

When Arya arrives at the House of Black and White, the Kindly Man is one of the first priests she meets — he receives her at the door and declines to acknowledge knowledge of Jaqen H'ghar. He accepts Arya as a servant of the temple and directs her training alongside the Waif. He instructs her in languages (High Valyrian, Braavosi), assigns her fieldwork under the identity "Cat of the Canals" with the fisherman Brusco, and oversees her initiation as a novice. He confirms certain truths about the Waif's history, demonstrating an authoritative role within the order.

After Arya confesses to killing Dareon (a Night's Watch deserter, an unsanctioned killing), the Kindly Man administers a punishment by having the Waif bring Arya poisoned milk that blinds her. Later, when Arya completes a sanctioned assassination using a poisoned coin, he restores her sight with another potion and elevates her to acolyte. He also uses the Faceless Men's magic to give Arya a new face for assassination missions, working alongside the Waif in the inner sanctum of the temple.
