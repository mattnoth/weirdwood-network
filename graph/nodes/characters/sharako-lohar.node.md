## Origins

During the [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) [civil war](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Admiral Lohar commanded a fleet of ninety [Myrish](wiki:Myrish), [Lysene](wiki:Lysene) and [Tyroshi](wiki:Tyroshi) warships under the banners of the [Three Daughters](wiki:Three_Daughters) who had allied themselves to King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) and his [supporters](wiki:Greens). On a mission to break the [blacks](wiki:Blacks)'s blockade of the [Gullet](wiki:Gullet), Sharako Lohar took the fleet of ninety warships up from the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones), bending their oars for battle.(wiki:Sharako_Lohar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Admiral Lohar was in command during what turned out to be one of the [bloodiest sea battles](wiki:Battle_in_the_Gullet) in all of history. The Three Daughters emerged victorious. Of the ninety original warships, twenty-eight survived to limp home. Of these, all but three were crewed by Lyseni. This fact caused some in Myr and Tyrosh to turn against Lohar, accusing him of holding back his Lysene crews while sending the other men of the Triarchy to their doom. This sparked a quarrel that would result in the [Daughters' War](wiki:Daughters'_War) two years later, shattering the Triarchy.(wiki:Sharako_Lohar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
During the battle, the fleet captured the cog [Gay Abandon](wiki:Gay_Abandon). Prince [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen) was made the captive of a Tyroshi captain, but Admiral Lohar soon took the Prince into his charge. Wanting for [gold](wiki:Gold) and allies due to his disgraceful defeat in the Gullet, he sold Viserys to [Bambarro Bazanne](wiki:Bambarro_Bazanne), in exchange for the prince's weight in gold and a promise of support.(wiki:Sharako_Lohar.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Eventually, he was murdered by one of his rivals for the favor of the [courtesan](wiki:Courtesan) known as the [Black Swan](wiki:Johanna_Swann).(wiki:Sharako_Lohar.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
