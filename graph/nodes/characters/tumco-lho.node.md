## Origins

Tumco is originally from the Basilisk Isles. He was training in the [fighting pits](wiki:Fighting_pits) of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) when [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) [took the city](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen) and freed the slaves from their chains.(wiki:Tumco_Lho.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

## Appearances & Description

Tumco is young and dark skinned.(wiki:Tumco_Lho.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) He has dark eyes and can speak the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue) as well as the tongue of his native [Basilisk Isles](wiki:Basilisk_Isles).(wiki:Tumco_Lho.cite_ref-winds_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Tumco spends most of *[A Dance with Dragons](wiki:A_Dance_with_Dragons)* in training in the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) to become a knight, as a [squire](wiki:Squire) to Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy). Strong and fast, the youth already has a good acquaintance with sword and battle-axe. Barristan thinks Tumco shows much promise as he is the best natural swordsman he has seen since [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister). Along with [Larraq](wiki:Larraq) he is Barristan's best squire.(wiki:Tumco_Lho.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

When Barristan goes down to the dungeons to tell [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater) and [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood) of [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell)'s demise, he takes with him two newly-made knights, Ser Tumco and the [Red Lamb](wiki:Red_Lamb).(wiki:Tumco_Lho.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### The Winds of Winter

On the brink of battle against the [Yunkish](wiki:Yunkish) besiegers, Tumco carries the banner of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen). Barristan realizes that Tumco is more afraid of the [Pale Mare](wiki:Pale_Mare) - spreading through the siege camps and launched into the city via [trebuchet](wiki:Trebuchet) - than he is of their foes.(wiki:Tumco_Lho.cite_ref-winds_5-1)
