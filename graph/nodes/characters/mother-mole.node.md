## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

After the defeat of [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) at the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), Mother Mole claims to have had a vision of a fleet of ships coming to carry the wildlings away to safety across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea).(wiki:Mother_Mole.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Thousands of those who fled the battle are desperate enough to believe her. She and her followers go east.(wiki:Mother_Mole.cite_ref-Radwd0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Mother Mole is heard to preach that the free folk will find salvation where once they found damnation.

Mother Mole leads thousands of [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) to [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome), to pray and await salvation from across the sea.
