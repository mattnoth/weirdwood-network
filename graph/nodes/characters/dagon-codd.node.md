## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

According to a guard at the [siege of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin), Dagon found only two [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) left alive in the [Children's Tower](wiki:Children's_Tower). Because they were eating corpses, he killed them both.(wiki:Dagon_Codd.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

When [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) sends [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) to give peace terms to the ironborn, a drunken Dagon is the only one of the garrison who does not wish to surrender. Because of Dagon's resistance, [Adrack Humble](wiki:Adrack_Humble) throws an axe into Dagon's forehead. Despite Ramsay's promise of safe passage, however, the remaining ironborn who surrender, including Adrack, are flayed after they yield [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin).(wiki:Dagon_Codd.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
