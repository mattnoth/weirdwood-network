## Identity

Harwin is a member of the household guard of Lord Eddard Stark at Winterfell, later knighted and known as Ser Harwin (track_b: Title). He is the son of Hullen, the master of horse at Winterfell (track_b: Father). A stocky man with a clean-shaven appearance, known for speaking boldly (wiki:Harwin).

## Origins

## Narrative Arc

Harwin grows up at Winterfell, often leading Arya Stark's pony around the yard, riding at quintain with Jon Snow and Robb Stark, and drinking too much on feast days (wiki:Harwin). He remembers Lyanna Stark's horse-riding skill and was privy to rumors about Eddard and Ashara Dayne (wiki:Harwin). Harwin accompanies the party that finds the direwolf pups (agot-bran-01) and goes south with Eddard to King's Landing (wiki:Harwin). At the Hand's tourney, he is unhorsed in his first tilt by Ser Meryn Trant (agot-sansa-02). Eddard sends Harwin with Lord Beric Dondarrion's party to bring Ser Gregor Clegane to justice — this saves him from the Lannister purge of Stark men in King's Landing (wiki:Harwin, agot-eddard-15). After Beric's party is ambushed, Harwin joins the Brotherhood without Banners and eventually serves Lady Stoneheart. In ASOS he encounters Arya and recognizes her, bridging her time between identities (wiki:Harwin).
