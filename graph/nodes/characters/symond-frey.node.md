## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Along with Ser [Jared Frey](wiki:Jared_Frey) and [Rhaegar Frey](wiki:Rhaegar_Frey), Symond travels on the *[Lionstar](wiki:Lionstar)* to [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) via [Sisterton](wiki:Sisterton) to return the bones of Ser [Wendel Manderly](wiki:Wendel_Manderly), who was killed in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), to his father, Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly).(wiki:Symond_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The Freys also want to ensure White Harbor bends the knee to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Symond_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) They offer a Frey bride for Lord Wyman and the [betrothals](wiki:Betrothal) of Rhaegar to his granddaughter [Wynafryd](wiki:Wynafryd_Manderly) and [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)) to Wyman's other granddaughter, [Wylla](wiki:Wylla_Manderly).(wiki:Symond_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

When Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) arrives in White Harbor to persuade [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) to support [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), the three Freys are already present at the [Merman's Court](wiki:Merman's_Court). Wyman publicly supports the Freys, as the Iron Throne holds hostage his surviving son and heir, Ser [Wylis Manderly](wiki:Wylis_Manderly), at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal). Wyman fakes Davos's death by having a criminal with similar features executed and his head dipped in tar so the Freys falsely believe him to be Davos.(wiki:Symond_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) As a reward for proving his loyalty, Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) has Wylis returned to White Harbor via [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool).(wiki:Symond_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Wyman later reveals to Davos that Symond had been bribing several of his servants and two [knights](wiki:Knight) to spy on the [Lord of White Harbor](wiki:Lord_of_White_Harbor) for the Freys. A [handmaid](wiki:Handmaid) of Symond's wife, [Betharios](wiki:Betharios_of_Braavos), found her way into the bed of Wyman's [fool](wiki:Fool).(wiki:Symond_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Wyman gives Jared, Rhaegar, and Symond palfreys as "guest gifts"(wiki:Symond_Frey.cite_ref-10) when the three Freys depart White Harbor for [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark) of "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)" to [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow).(wiki:Symond_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) They disappear while traveling to [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton), however, despite the search efforts of Ramsay.(wiki:Symond_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

At [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) after Ramsay's wedding, Wyman presents [three huge pies](wiki:Frey_Pies/Theories) at the wedding feast while requesting a song from the singer [Abel](wiki:Mance_Rayder) about the [Rat Cook](wiki:Rat_Cook).(wiki:Symond_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Ser [Aenys Frey](wiki:Aenys_Frey) and Ser [Hosteen Frey](wiki:Hosteen_Frey) suspect Wyman of foul play.(wiki:Symond_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
