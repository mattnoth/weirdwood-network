## Origins

Although he is not a young man, John is known as Young John because his father, [Old John Mudd](wiki:Old_John_Mudd), was also a member of the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company).(wiki:Young_John_Mudd.cite_ref-Ar2_2-2) Their surname is of a house that had once loomed large in the histories of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Young_John_Mudd.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [House Mudd](wiki:House_Mudd) ruled as [Kings of the Rivers and the Hills](wiki:King_of_the_Trident) thousands of years ago until their line was extinguished with the death of King [Tristifer V Mudd](wiki:Tristifer_V_Mudd) at the hands of the [Andals](wiki:Andals).(wiki:Young_John_Mudd.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He may or may not be of House Mudd, as in the free companies a man can call himself whatever he chooses, and not all such names are genuine.(wiki:Young_John_Mudd.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) He may also be related to another serjeant in the company, [Lorimas Mudd](wiki:Lorimas_Mudd).(wiki:Young_John_Mudd.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Appearances & Description

John has brown skin and wears brown clothes. He decorates his ears with golden coins. He has scars and weathered face. John is a brusque, brutal man who wields sword and dagger. He is not well spoken. He is skilled with [dice](wiki:Dice).(wiki:Young_John_Mudd.cite_ref-Ar2_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

John may have been present during [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington)'s rendezvous with the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) three miles south of [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys), as Jon notices that a Mudd is present during the meeting. Like the rest of the company, he swears his allegiance to [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)) before they [invade Westeros](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company).(wiki:Young_John_Mudd.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

### The Winds of Winter

Young John Mudd and [Chain](wiki:Chain) hold [Mistwood](wiki:Mistwood) for the Golden Company. They keep Lady [Mary Mertyns](wiki:Mary_Mertyns) under house arrest and host [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell)'s party for a night.(wiki:Young_John_Mudd.cite_ref-Ar2_2-3)
