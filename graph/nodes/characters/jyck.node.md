## Identity

Jyck is a guard sworn to House Lannister who serves as escort to Tyrion Lannister when he travels to the Wall (wiki:Jyck).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Lannister (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Westermen (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Tyrion considers Jyck a fairly good sword (wiki:Jyck.cite_ref-Ragot31). One of his regular tasks was hiring prostitutes for Tyrion; he sometimes failed to warn the women that Tyrion is a dwarf (wiki:Jyck.cite_ref-Ragot42).

## Narrative Arc

Jyck arrives with the royal party at Winterfell and, together with Morrec, escorts Tyrion to the Wall (wiki:Jyck.cite_ref-Ragot21). When Tyrion's party is taken captive by Catelyn Stark at the crossroads inn, Jyck becomes sullen and miserable. During an attack by Vale mountain clans in the Mountains of the Moon, Jyck is cut down from behind while fighting another man. Bronn takes Jyck's boots and Tyrion takes his horse. Tyrion later reflects that Jyck "was a fool to the end" (wiki:Jyck.cite_ref-Ragot31). Tyrion mentions Jyck's death to Tywin, who notes that no man sheds Lannister blood with impunity (wiki:Jyck.cite_ref-Ragot56).
