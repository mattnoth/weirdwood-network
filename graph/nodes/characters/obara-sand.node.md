## Identity

Obara Sand is the bastard daughter of Oberyn Martell and an Oldtown whore. She is the eldest of the Sand Snakes (wiki:Obara_Sand).

## Origins

Born 271–272 AC in Oldtown. When Oberyn came to claim her, her mother insisted another man was the father. Oberyn tossed his spear at Obara's feet and slapped her mother, telling the girl to choose between spear and tears. Obara chose the spear; her mother drank herself to death within a year (track_b: Born, Father, Mother, wiki:Obara_Sand).

## Appearances & Description

A big-boned woman near thirty, long-legged, with close-set eyes and rat-brown hair sometimes tied in a knot. Callused hands, mannish look. Prickly, hot-tempered. Wears mottled sandsilk cloak of dun and gold over brown riding leathers. Wields a spear, whip, and round shield of steel and copper. Prefers men's breeches and a calf-length linen tunic with a belt of copper suns. A confident rider who always rides stallions, often spurring them bloody. Fond of wine (wiki:Obara_Sand).

## Narrative Arc

Obara meets Doran at the Water Gardens demanding vengeance for Oberyn's death. She urges war — closing the passes and raiding the Reach. Doran confines her. Later, he reveals his plans and sends Obara with Ser Balon Swann to hunt Darkstar after the failed Myrcella crowning plot. In TWOW, she rides with Balon toward High Hermitage (wiki:Obara_Sand).
