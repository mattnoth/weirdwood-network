## Origins

Obara was born to a prostitute in Oldtown. When Prince Oberyn Martell came to claim her, her mother did not want to let her go and insisted that Obara's father was one of the other men she had been with. Oberyn tossed his spear at Obara's feet and slapped her mother in the face. Then he told the girl to choose, pointing to the spear and her mother's tears. Obara chose the former, and her mother drank herself to death within a year.(wiki:Obara_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

Obara spent part of her youth at the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens).(wiki:Obara_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

## Appearances & Description

Obara is a big-boned woman near to thirty, long-legged, with close-set eyes and with the same rat-brown hair of her mother(wiki:Obara_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) which she sometimes ties in a knot.(wiki:Obara_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) She strides quickly and angrily.(wiki:Obara_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Obara_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Obara has callused hands and can have a mannish look.(wiki:Obara_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

A prickly, hot-tempered woman,(wiki:Obara_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Obara is given to martial pursuits as she considers herself [a warrior](wiki:Woman_warrior). She wears a mottled sandsilk cloak of dun and gold over brown riding leathers and wields a spear, a whip, and a round shield of [steel](wiki:Steel) and [copper](wiki:Copper).(wiki:Obara_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Instead of a gown, fierce Obara prefers men's breeches, a calf-length linen tunic, and a belt of copper suns.(wiki:Obara_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

> Even without her whip and shield, she had an angry mannish look to her. In place of a gown, she wore men's breeches and a calf-length linen tunic, cinched at the waist with a belt of copper suns. Her brown hair was tied back in a knot.

— Areo Hotah's observation of Obara at Sunspear, ADWD The Watcher I (`sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-the-watcher-01.md:25`) [L858]

Obara is a confident rider who uses spurs, often to the point where the [animal](wiki:Animal) is bloody, and she always rides stallions.(wiki:Obara_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) Obara is also fond of [wine](wiki:Wine).(wiki:Obara_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Obara meets [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), at the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens), demanding vengeance for her father, [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell), who was slain by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Obara wants to slay Gregor's liege, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), and burn [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). Doran says he will think about it. After more visits by her half-sisters, Doran orders the arrest of all the [Sand Snakes](wiki:Sand_Snakes).(wiki:Obara_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6) The elder sisters are detained in the Spear Tower in [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear).(wiki:Obara_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

### A Dance with Dragons

Obara and her sisters are released from the Spear Tower and are present at the feast when Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) presents the [Mountain's skull](wiki:Mountain's_skull) to Doran. She places Gregor's skull on top of a [marble](wiki:Marble) column because Maester [Caleotte](wiki:Caleotte) cannot reach it.(wiki:Obara_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5) When a toast is raised by [Ricasso](wiki:Ricasso) to King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), Obara allows her wine cup to be filled before upending it onto the floor and leaving.(wiki:Obara_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)

When discussing Gregor's death with her sisters, [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah), [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell), [Ellaria Sand](wiki:Ellaria_Sand), and Prince Doran, Obara vouches for war against [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister), causing Ellaria alarm.(wiki:Obara_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7) Doran has Obara lead Balon to [High Hermitage](wiki:High_Hermitage) so he can hunt and kill Ser [Gerold Dayne](wiki:Gerold_Dayne) for his attack on Princess [Myrcella](wiki:Myrcella).(wiki:Obara_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-8)

### The Winds of Winter

[Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah) joins Obara during her journey.(wiki:Obara_Sand.cite_ref-6)
