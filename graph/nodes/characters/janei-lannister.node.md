## Identity

Janei Lannister is a member of House Lannister. She is the only daughter and youngest child of Ser Kevan Lannister and his wife Dorna Swyft. Her older siblings are Ser Lancel and the squires Willem and Martyn Lannister (wiki:Janei_Lannister).
