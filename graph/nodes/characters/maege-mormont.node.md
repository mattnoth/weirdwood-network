## Origins

Maege inherited the title of head of House Mormont and ruler of Bear Island after her brother's only heir, Jorah Mormont, fled to the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) to escape justice for unlawfully selling poachers into [slavery](wiki:Slavery) to pay for his debts. She worked hard to regain the family's noble name, and to help the estate recover from the state of near-poverty Jorah left behind.(wiki:Maege_Mormont.cite_ref-RpgBook_7-0)

Maege has five daughters: her eldest and heir, [Dacey](wiki:Dacey_Mormont); and [Alysane](wiki:Alysane_Mormont), [Lyra](wiki:Lyra_Mormont), [Jorelle](wiki:Jorelle_Mormont), and [Lyanna](wiki:Lyanna_Mormont). She has no sons.

No one knows who the father of Maege's children is, or even if she ever married.(wiki:Maege_Mormont.cite_ref-ssm_8-0) There are rumors that she beds with a bear;(wiki:Maege_Mormont.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Maege_Mormont.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) her daughter, Alysane, smilingly shares the same rumor, claiming that Mormont women are [skinchangers](wiki:Skinchanger) who turn into bears and find mates in the woods.(wiki:Maege_Mormont.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Maege prefers to keep her own counsel.(wiki:Maege_Mormont.cite_ref-ssm_8-1) Nonetheless, all of Maege's daughters carry the surname "Mormont", and none appear to be considered [bastards](wiki:Bastard). It is possible that Maege's husband came from a family of lower nobility than the Mormonts, or no nobility at all, in which case a woman can prefer to retain her own surname for herself and for her children.(wiki:Maege_Mormont.cite_ref-ssm_8-2)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Maege Mormont](wiki:Category:Images_of_Maege_Mormont)*

Maege is a short, stout, grey-haired woman, and a fierce warrior. She dresses in patched ringmail, and her favored weapon is a spiked mace.(wiki:Maege_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Maege_Mormont.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) She is dedicated to the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods), and loyal to [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark). According to her brother, Jeor, she is stubborn, short-tempered, and willful.(wiki:Maege_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lady Maege and her heir, [Dacey](wiki:Dacey_Mormont), lead their forces to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) when [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls his banners to ride south against the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Maege_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) When the northern host splits at [the Twins](wiki:The_Twins), Maege continues south in Robb's host. Her forces signal the springing of the trap at the [Battle of the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_of_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Maege_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) After Lords [Greatjon Umber](wiki:Greatjon_Umber) and [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark) proclaim Robb to be [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), Maege hails Robb as "King of Winter".(wiki:Maege_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Clash of Kings

After the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross), Lady Mormont captures thousands of cattle in the [Westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) and drives them towards the [Riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). It is not stated whether or not she manages to succeed in this task, as the passage back to the Riverlands is still held by the Lannisters.(wiki:Maege_Mormont.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Lady Maege is among the northern lords who accompanies King Robb Stark north from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) to attend the wedding of [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) at [the Twins](wiki:The_Twins). At [Hag's Mire](wiki:Hag's_Mire), however, she and [Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover) are ordered by Robb to travel to [Seagard](wiki:Seagard). There they are to take separate ships from Lord [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister) to sail up [the Neck](wiki:The_Neck) and to search for [Greywater Watch](wiki:Greywater_Watch) as envoys to the [crannogmen](wiki:Crannogmen), in preparation for Robb's planned attack on [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn)-occupied [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin). The letters they carry will contain false commands for northern lords, however, in case they are captured by enemies.(wiki:Maege_Mormont.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Maege's heir, Dacey, is one of those killed at the Twins during the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Maege_Mormont.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Alysane Mormont](wiki:Alysane_Mormont) mentions her mother, Maege, when [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) asks about her siblings. Maege is with two of her other daughters, [Lyra](wiki:Lyra_Mormont) and [Jory](wiki:Jorelle_Mormont), though it is not said where, while her youngest, [Lyanna](wiki:Lyanna_Mormont) is back on Bear Island.(wiki:Maege_Mormont.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)
