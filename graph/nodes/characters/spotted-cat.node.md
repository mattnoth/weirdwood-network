## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

The Spotted Cat, along with his fellow pit fighters [Barsena Blackhair](wiki:Barsena_Blackhair), [Camarron of the Count](wiki:Camarron_of_the_Count), [Goghor the Giant](wiki:Goghor_the_Giant), [Khrazz](wiki:Khrazz), [Fearless Ithoke](wiki:Fearless_Ithoke), and [Belaquo Bonebreaker](wiki:Belaquo_Bonebreaker), accompanies [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) when he petitions [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) for the seventh time for the [fighting pits of Meereen](wiki:Fighting_pits_of_Meereen) to be reopened. The Spotted Cat explains that his accommodations and meals have declined after being granted freedom from [slavery](wiki:Slavery) by Daenerys.(wiki:Spotted_Cat.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

The Spotted Cat fights a man as tall as Goghor and as broad as [Belwas](wiki:Belwas) when the fighting pits are reopened. After hamstringing his slower opponent, the Cat cuts the man's throat.(wiki:Spotted_Cat.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

After Daenerys disappears from [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit), the Spotted Cat and other pit fighters serve as Hizdahr's guards.(wiki:Spotted_Cat.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Spotted_Cat.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

The Spotted Cat is chosen to sit on the [ruling council of Meereen](wiki:Ruling_council_of_Meereen) along with Goghor, Camarron, and Belaquo by Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), despite the objections of [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq). The Cat suggests that he kill Ser [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater) and Ser [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood) in the fighting pits, as the two [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) had aided Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) in releasing [Viserion](wiki:Viserion) and [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal) on the city. The Spotted Cat also suggests that he face a [Yunkish](wiki:Yunkai) champion in single combat to resolve the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen).(wiki:Spotted_Cat.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### The Winds of Winter

Shortly before the resumption of the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen), Ithoke and the Spotted Cat stand together among the several hundred pit fighters who have gathered for the battle near the [Spire of Skulls](wiki:Spire_of_Skulls). Barristan orders Goghor, Camarron, and the Cat to lead the pit fighters in the upcoming battle.(wiki:Spotted_Cat.cite_ref-barristan1_6-0)
