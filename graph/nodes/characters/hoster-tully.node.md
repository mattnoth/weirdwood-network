## Identity

Lord Hoster Tully is the Lord Paramount of the Trident, Lord of Riverrun, and head of House Tully. Married to Lady Minisa Whent, he has three surviving children: Catelyn, Lysa, and Edmure. His younger brother is Ser Brynden Tully, the Blackfish (wiki:Hoster_Tully).

## Origins

Born 238–240 AC at Riverrun (track_b: Born). Son of Lord Tully (track_b: Father). Married Lady Minisa Whent (track_b: Spouse), who bore him four sons and two daughters, though only three children survived infancy (wiki:Hoster_Tully).

## Appearances & Description

In his youth, tall and broad with blue eyes and brown hair. Became portly with age, hair and beard streaked with grey. Stricken by illness since 296 AC, he has lost weight and his hair and beard have gone white (wiki:Hoster_Tully). His armor is silver plate-and-mail, cloak and surcoat in blue and red, with a silver-and-bronze scaled trout atop his greathelm (wiki:Hoster_Tully).

## Narrative Arc

Hoster served in the War of the Ninepenny Kings, where he befriended Lord Baelish, later agreeing to foster Petyr at Riverrun (wiki:Hoster_Tully). After inheriting Riverrun, he attempted to betroth Brynden to Bethany Redwyne, sparking a decades-long rift with his brother. He arranged the marriages of his daughters during Robert's Rebellion: Catelyn to Eddard Stark and Lysa to Jon Arryn, sealing the rebel alliance — but secretly forced Lysa to abort Petyr Baelish's child first, giving her moon tea prepared by Maester Kym (wiki:Hoster_Tully).

During the War of the Five Kings, Hoster lies dying at Riverrun while his lands burn. He mutters "Tansy" in his delirium, which Catelyn later realizes refers to the tansy in the moon tea he gave Lysa (agot-catelyn-08, wiki:Hoster_Tully). He dies in 299 AC and is buried in a boat sent down the Red Fork per Tully custom — Edmure misses the burning shot, and Brynden lights it instead (track_b: Died, track_b: Buried).
