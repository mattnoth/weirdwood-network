## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Prendahl takes a contract with [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) to defend them against the army of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen). Daenerys attempts to win them over, but Prendahl, being [Ghiscari](wiki:Ghiscari), personally refuses. However, the Stormcrows are a company of free men and he returns to the camp to give them the offer. [Daario Naharis](wiki:Daario_Naharis), a fellow captain, has already been won over, however. Daario kills Prendahl and [Sallor the Bald](wiki:Sallor_the_Bald) and delivers their heads to Daenerys in order to bring the Stormcrows over to her. Daenerys's [dragons](wiki:Dragon) eat the heads.(wiki:Prendahl_na_Ghezn.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Prendahl_na_Ghezn.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
