## Identity

Prince Doran Nymeros Martell is the head of House Martell, the Prince of Dorne, and the Lord of Sunspear. He is the elder brother of Elia and Oberyn Martell (wiki:Doran_Martell).

## Origins

Born in 247 or 248 AC at Sunspear (track_b: Born). Son of Princess Martell (track_b: Mother). Of Dornish culture (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

In his early fifties, Doran has a severe case of gout that has left him unable to walk, relying on a wheeled chair or palanquin. He appears much older than his true age. His body is soft and shapeless, with swollen and reddened joints in his knees, toes, and hands. He covers his legs with a blanket to hide the gout (wiki:Doran_Martell). Doran is cautious, pensive, and subtle — prone to think long on matters, weighing every word and action. While his bannermen consider him weak, he has been focused for years on revenge for the murder of his sister Elia (wiki:Doran_Martell).

## Narrative Arc

Doran was named in the traitor decree issued by Joffrey's court along with all his sons (agot-sansa-05). He married Lady Mellario of Norvos, having three children: Arianne, Quentyn, and Trystane. Despite marrying for love, the union was troubled by cultural differences, particularly over the fostering of children. Mellario eventually returned to Norvos. Throughout the War of the Five Kings, Doran appeared passive while secretly pursuing a long-term revenge plan for Elia's murder. He had arranged a secret marriage pact between Arianne and Viserys Targaryen through Willem Darry. After Viserys's death, he sent Quentyn to seek Daenerys Targaryen's hand. He foiled Arianne's Queenmaker plot but later revealed his true plans to her and the Sand Snakes (wiki:Doran_Martell).
