## Appearances & Description

Dirk takes his name from his favorite [weapon](wiki:Armament), which he silently sharpens every night.(wiki:Dirk.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Dirk is part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), the force that leaves [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) in search of [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings). Dirk is one of the conspirators who intend to kill Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) and others. His part in the conspiracy is to kill [Blane](wiki:Blane).(wiki:Dirk.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Dirk is among the survivors of the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist) who retreat to [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep). Dirk blames [Craster](wiki:Craster) when [Bannen](wiki:Bannen) dies. After members of the Watch insult their host to the point of his attacking them, Dirk slits Craster's throat, killing him during the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep).(wiki:Dirk.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Dirk remains at Craster's Keep after the betrayal, along with a few other former members of the Night's Watch.(wiki:Dirk.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Five traitors are killed by [Coldhands](wiki:Coldhands) and his ravens. [Summer](wiki:Summer), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark)'s [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), finds the bodies and eats parts of the meat.(wiki:Dirk.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) It is unknown if Dirk is among them.
