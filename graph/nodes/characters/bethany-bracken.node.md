## Origins

[Barba Bracken](wiki:Barba_Bracken) was King [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen)'s fifth mistress, and her father, [Lord Bracken](wiki:Lord_Bracken_(Hand_of_the_King)) of [Stone Hedge](wiki:Stone_Hedge), had been his [Hand](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), but they were sent away in [172 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) and [Melissa Blackwood](wiki:Melissa_Blackwood) had become Aegon's mistress. Barba and her father groomed her younger sister, Bethany, expressly to win the king's favor and displace Melissa Blackwood as his mistress.(wiki:Bethany_Bracken.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

In [177 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Bethany caught Aegon's eye when he visited Stone Hedge to see his and Barba's [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son, [Aegor Rivers](wiki:Aegor_Rivers). By then, the king was fat and foul-tempered, but Bethany delighted him, and he took her back with him to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). However, Bethany found Aegon's embraces distressing, and for comfort she turned to a(wiki:Bethany_Bracken.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) handsome(wiki:Bethany_Bracken.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [knight](wiki:Knight) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), Ser [Terrence Toyne](wiki:Terrence_Toyne). The pair was discovered abed by Aegon himself in [178 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Bethany_Bracken.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Even though they proclaimed their love,(wiki:Bethany_Bracken.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Bethany_Bracken.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Bethany was forced to watch as Terrence was dismembered piece by piece(wiki:Bethany_Bracken.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and died screaming.(wiki:Bethany_Bracken.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Both Bethany and her father were then executed.(wiki:Bethany_Bracken.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
