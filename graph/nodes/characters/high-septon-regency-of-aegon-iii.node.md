## Origins

In [131 AC](wiki:131_AC), the High Septon travelled to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to pronounce the marriage vows at [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Aegon_III_Targaryen_and_Jaehaera_Targaryen) of Prince [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) and Princess [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen) on the seventh day of the seventh [moon](wiki:Moon). He refused to make the voyage with [Lyonel Hightower](wiki:Lyonel_Hightower), the new [Lord of Oldtown](wiki:Lord_of_Oldtown), as he considered his relationship with Lady [Samantha Tarly](wiki:Samantha_Tarly), his [father](wiki:Ormund_Hightower)'s widow, to be a form of [incest](wiki:Incest) and forbade them to [marry](wiki:Marriage). Instead, the High Septon travelled to the capital accompagnied by Lords [Redwyne](wiki:House_Redwyne), [Costayne](wiki:House_Costayne), and [Beesbury](wiki:House_Beesbury). Lords [Rowan](wiki:Thaddeus_Rowan) and [Tarly](wiki:House_Tarly) joined them for the return journey to the [Reach](wiki:Reach).(wiki:High_Septon_(Regency_of_Aegon_III).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

In [136 AC](wiki:136_AC), the High Septon wrote a letter to Lord [Torrhen Manderly](wiki:Torrhen_Manderly) asking clemency for Septon [Bernard](wiki:Bernard) when he was put on trial for his involvement during the [Lysene Spring](wiki:Lysene_Spring).(wiki:High_Septon_(Regency_of_Aegon_III).cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

After his passing in [143 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), [his successor](wiki:High_Septon_(Aegon_III)) allowed the marriage of Lord Lyonel and Lady Samantha and their six children were [legitimized](wiki:Bastardy).(wiki:High_Septon_(Regency_of_Aegon_III).cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:High_Septon_(Regency_of_Aegon_III).cite_ref-4)
