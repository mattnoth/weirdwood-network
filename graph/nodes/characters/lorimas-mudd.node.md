## Origins

Lorimas is a [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) exile. His surname Mudd was a house that once loomed large in the histories of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms). He may or may not be of [House Mudd](wiki:House_Mudd), as in the free companies a man can call himself whatever he chooses. He may be related to another serjeant in the company, [Young John Mudd](wiki:Young_John_Mudd).

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Lorimas may have been present during [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington)'s rendezvous with the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) three miles south of [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys), as Jon notices that a Mudd is present during the meeting.

Like the rest of the company, Lorimas swears his allegiance to [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)) before they [invade Westeros](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company).(wiki:Lorimas_Mudd.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
