## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Lem](wiki:Category:Images_of_Lem)*

Big and brawny,(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Lem has bad teeth, a bushy brown beard, and a harsh, deep voice.(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) He has a fierce, soldier look to him, and his strong hands are calloused.(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Lem is blunt and gruff.(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) He snores loudly.(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Lem is known for his large, hooded yellow cloak which is patched with [deerskin](wiki:Deer).(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) He carries a [longsword](wiki:Longsword) and a dirk on his studded leather belt. Lem's shirt is protected with overlapping [steel](wiki:Steel) rings, and he wears a black [iron](wiki:Iron) conic helm.(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

According to a semi-canon source, Lem is part of Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion)'s royal party sent by Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), to kill Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane).(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Rawoiaflem.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Beric's party is ambushed by the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) in the [battle at the Mummer's Ford](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford), however.(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Lem and his companions in the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) stumble upon [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) and [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie) digging in a garden. After some archery from [Anguy](wiki:Anguy), they convince the three young travelers to accompany them to the [Inn of the Kneeling Man](wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man), where [Sharna](wiki:Sharna) and [Husband](wiki:Husband) are familiar with the brotherhood. Arya breaks Lem's nose with a tankard of [ale](wiki:Ale) before learning that [Harwin](wiki:Harwin) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) is Lem's ally.(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

Lem's nose heals crookedly as the outlaws travel through the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), visiting [Lord Lychester's keep](wiki:Lord_Lychester's_keep), the [Lady of the Leaves](wiki:Lady_of_the_Leaves), [Sallydance](wiki:Sallydance), [High Heart](wiki:High_Heart), [Acorn Hall](wiki:Acorn_Hall), and [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept).(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) They find Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) in the [hollow hill](wiki:Hollow_hill), where Beric tells [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) that all of the brotherhood have been [knighted](wiki:Knight). After Sandor strikes down Beric in their [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat), [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr) tasks Lem and [Jack-Be-Lucky](wiki:Jack-Be-Lucky) with tending to Beric's wounds.(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Lemtakes part in the [battle at the burning septry](wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry), where he rides down the [Bloody Mummer](wiki:Bloody_Mummer) who killed [Kyle](wiki:Kyle_(brotherhood)).(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Lem is skeptical of the [ghost of High Heart](wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart)(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) and he believes that Beric's survival after horrific wounds is due to the healing skill of Thoros, although it is actually [magical](wiki:Magic) in nature. Lem is a supporter of the [Lord of Light](wiki:Lord_of_Light), however. When Sandor returns to the outlaws to demand the return of his gold, Lem suggests that they should kill the [Hound](wiki:Hound) and take [his horse](wiki:Stranger_(horse)).(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) Lem argues they should ransom Arya to Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) as soon as possible, but Beric worries the castle will soon [come under attack](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun). Arya decides to escape the outlaws.(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Lem participates in the retaliatory hangings of [Petyr](wiki:Petyr_Frey) and [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey) after the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

### A Feast for Crows

Lem's nose heals poorly.(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) He continues to be a member of the brotherhood after [Lady Stoneheart](wiki:Lady_Stoneheart) takes command.(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) He is stationed near the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads), hiding in wait in case its orphans need assistance.(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Lem leaves the inn to hunt north along the [Green Fork](wiki:Green_Fork) in search of the the Mad Dog of Saltpans, as [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) is believed to have led a [savage sack of the town](wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans);(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) in actuality, the raid had been led by [Rorge](wiki:Rorge), who had claimed Sandor's [hound](wiki:Hound) helm.(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Lem realizes that the former [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) had doubled back, but he is delayed from returning to the inn by having to avoid a column of knights from [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey).(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

While Lem is away, [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) and her companions arrive at the inn and meet [Gendry](wiki:Gendry). Rorge also arrive at the inn, however, and Brienne chooses to defend the orphaned children from Rorge's outlaws. Brienne slays Rorge, but she is then attacked by [Biter](wiki:Biter). Gendry saves Brienne's life by killing Biter.(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) Lem decides to wear the Hound's helmet he took from Rorge's corpse, which Thoros opposes. Lem is present when Lady Stoneheart approves the hangings of Brienne, [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne), and Ser [Hyle Hunt](wiki:Hyle_Hunt), and he implies to Brienne that his wife and daughter are dead.(wiki:Lem.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)
