## Identity

Lord Aerys Targaryen was a member of House Targaryen during the Century of Blood, the second son of Lord Aegon and Lady Elaena Targaryen of Dragonstone. He had one older brother, Maegon, and three sons: Aelyx, Baelon, and Daemion Targaryen (wiki:Aerys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon)).
