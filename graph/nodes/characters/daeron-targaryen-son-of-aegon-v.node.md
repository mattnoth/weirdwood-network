## Identity

Daeron Targaryen (son of Aegon V) (228–251 AC) — child of Aegon V Targaryen and Betha Blackwood; sworn to House Targaryen; Prince. One of 6 Daeron Targaryens — see the Daeron Targaryen disambiguation entry.

## Origins

Prince Daeron was the youngest son of Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) and his wife, Lady [Betha Blackwood](wiki:Betha_Blackwood). He had three older siblings, [Duncan](wiki:Duncan_Targaryen), [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_II_Targaryen), and [Shaera](wiki:Shaera_Targaryen), and a younger sister, [Rhaelle](wiki:Rhaelle_Targaryen).(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_V).cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:targaryen_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Daeron's grandfather, King [Maekar I Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen), died in [233 AC](wiki:233_AC), when Daeron was five years old, and the [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council) selected his father to be the new king, Aegon V Targaryen, called "Aegon the Unlikely" as he was the fourth son of a fourth son.(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_V).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

When the [Fourth Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Fourth_Blackfyre_Rebellion) erupted in [236 AC](wiki:236_AC) as the self-styled King [Daemon III Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_III_Blackfyre) sailed across the [Narrow Sea](wiki:Narrow_Sea) with Ser [Aegor Rivers](wiki:Aegor_Rivers) and the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company), Daeron rode out to [Massey's Hook](wiki:Massey's_Hook) to meet the invaders alongside his father and both his elder brothers.(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_V).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

It had long been the custom of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) to [wed brother to sister](wiki:Incest) to keep the blood of the [dragon](wiki:Dragon) pure, but Aegon V had become convinced that such unions did more harm than good. Instead, he chose to [betroth](wiki:Betrothal) his children to some of the [Great Houses](wiki:Great_Houses) of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), in hopes of winning their support for his reign. In [237 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Daeron's mother Queen Betha arranged his betrothal to [Olenna Redwyne](wiki:Olenna_Redwyne) of the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor), when both children were nine years old. However, Daeron's three older siblings broke their betrothals; Duncan fell in love with and married [Jenny of Oldstones](wiki:Jenny_of_Oldstones) in [239 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), and Jaehaerys and Shaera married each other in [240 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). In [246 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), corrupted by the example of his brothers, the eighteen-year-old Daeron broke his betrothal to Olenna as well.(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_V).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

While his brothers had broken their betrothals for other women, this was not the case for Daeron, who remained unwed for the rest of his life. He preferred the companionship of Ser [Jeremy Norridge](wiki:Jeremy_Norridge), a dashing young [knight](wiki:Knight) who had been with Daeron since they were [squires](wiki:Squire) together at [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden).(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_V).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)

In [251 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Prince Daeron led an army against the rebels known as [the Rat, the Hawk, and the Pig](wiki:The_Rat,_the_Hawk,_and_the_Pig). Though they managed to quash the rebellion, Daeron was killed in battle, as was Jeremy, who died by his side.(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_V).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8)

## Appearances & Description

Daeron was a born warrior, who rejoiced in [tourneys](wiki:Tourney) and battle.(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_V).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

According to the illustration in *[The World of Ice & Fire](wiki:The_World_of_Ice_&_Fire)*, Daeron had dark silver hair, darker than his father's. He wore it long, and tied back in a ponytail.(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_V).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

When speaking with [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Lady [Olenna Tyrell](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell) tells a tale of how she had once been betrothed to a [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) prince. Olenna claims that it was she who had not wanted the marriage, and that it was she who had ended it.(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_V).cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) recalls how all three sons of Aegon V Targaryen had married for love, including Daeron, and how their father had let them have their way, resulting in tragedy and death.(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_V).cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
