## Identity

Prince Daeron Targaryen was the youngest son and fourthborn child of King Aegon V Targaryen and Queen Betha Blackwood (wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_V)).

## Origins

Born in 228 AC (track_b: Born). Son of Aegon V Targaryen (track_b: Father) and Betha Blackwood (track_b: Mother).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiance).

## Appearances & Description

Daeron was a born warrior who rejoiced in tourneys and battle. He had dark silver hair, darker than his father's, worn long (wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_V)).

## Narrative Arc

Daeron died in 251 AC (track_b: Died). He was the lover of Ser Jeremy Norridge (track_b: Lover) (wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_V)).
