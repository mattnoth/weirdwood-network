## Identity

Princess Gael Targaryen, called "The Winter Child," was the youngest daughter of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen and Queen Alysanne Targaryen (track_b: Father, Mother).

## Origins

Born in 80 AC (track_b: Born). Of Valyrian race and Crownlands culture (track_b: Race, Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Gael was described as sweet and simple. She bore a bastard child and subsequently drowned herself in Blackwater Bay in 99 AC (track_b: Died, Issue). Her death was a source of great grief to Queen Alysanne.
