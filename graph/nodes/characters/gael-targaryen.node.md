## Identity

Gael Targaryen — Princess Gael, "the Winter Child," was born in 80 AC when Alysanne was forty-four, a sweet but frail and simple girl.

## Origins

Gael was born in [80 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), a [winter](wiki:Winter) year, giving her the sobriquet the "Winter Child". (According to some, the name was because Queen Alysanne was "in the winter of her childbearing years", 44 years old at the time of Gael's birth.) Gael was cared for by [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Elysar](wiki:Elysar) and [Septa](wiki:Septa) [Lyra](wiki:Lyra), who nursed her through her first year.(wiki:Gael_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

After her sisters [Alyssa](wiki:Alyssa_Targaryen), [Daella](wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)), and [Viserra](wiki:Viserra_Targaryen) had died within the span of five years ([82](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)–[87 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)), Gael became a comfort for her mother Queen Alysanne, along with her older sister [Maegelle](wiki:Maegelle_Targaryen). Gael became Alysanne's constant shadow, and even slept with her in her bed.(wiki:Gael_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

In [99 AC](wiki:99_AC), Gael disappeared from court. It was announced that she had died of a [summer fever](wiki:Disease). After the deaths of King Jaehaerys and Queen Alysanne, it was revealed that Gael had been seduced and impregnated by a traveling singer. Gael had given birth to a stillborn son, and overwhelmed by her grief, she had walked into [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay) and drowned.(wiki:Gael_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)(wiki:Gael_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

According to some, Queen Alysanne never recovered from losing Gael. Following Gael's death, Alysanne could no longer stand to be in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) and at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep). Alysanne herself would die on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) less than a year after Gael, in [100 AC](wiki:100_AC).(wiki:Gael_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)(wiki:Gael_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

## Appearances & Description

As a babe, Gael was small and pale. She grew to be a shy and sweet girl, although [simple-minded](wiki:Lackwit) and frail.(wiki:Gael_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Gael_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
