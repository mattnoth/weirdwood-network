## Origins

Kem comes from [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom).(wiki:Kem.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) He once saw Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) riding up a hill in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), and the lad liked the little lions on the helms of Tywin's [red cloaks](wiki:House_Lannister_guards).(wiki:Kem.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Kem claims that he and his brother, [Kennet](wiki:Kennet_(Flea_Bottom)), fought in the army of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), during which Kennet was killed. Kem dropped his spear and tried to escape after the appearance of [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)'s ghost ([Garlan Tyrell](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell)), but he and thousands more were left behind by the fleeing ships.(wiki:Kem.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

As he feared being sent to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) by Tywin after the battle, Kem traveled across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) and joined the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons), a [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) company, as a means to survive.(wiki:Kem.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

## Appearances & Description

Kem has pale blond hair.(wiki:Kem.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btyrion_ii_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctyrion_ii.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

The youth dislikes Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) for the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing) and the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Kem.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Kem and the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons) go over to the [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai)'i after [Brown Ben Plumm](wiki:Brown_Ben_Plumm) feels that [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s cause is lost.(wiki:Kem.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

After [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) joins the company, [Snatch](wiki:Snatch) has Kem escort Tyrion and [Penny](wiki:Penny) to the wagons. Tyrion chats briefly with Kem on their way to the armory. He can tell that Kem is from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), as he has a strong [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom) accent. Kem says he misses a boy—a friend of his—and his brother [Kennet](wiki:Kennet_(Flea_Bottom)), who died in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Kem.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

Kem mentions he misses the food of the capital, and his love for the [bowl of brown](wiki:Bowl_of_brown) in a certain pot shop, claiming there is no equal elsewhere. Tyrion says he calls the food "singer's stew", claiming it makes him want to sing. Kem, unaware of Tyrion's reference to the late [Symon Silver Tongue](wiki:Symon_Silver_Tongue), likes the name and says he will ask for it when he returns to [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom).(wiki:Kem.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

### The Winds of Winter

As the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen) commences, Tyrion thinks Kem may be remembering his dead brother or his friend back in King's Landing. When a Yunkish lordling rides up to order the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons) to bring the company to the bay shore by the command of [Gorzhak zo Eraz](wiki:Gorzhak_zo_Eraz), Kem is confused, and he asks if that is the [Rabbit](wiki:Faezhar_zo_Faez); [Inkpots](wiki:Inkpots) tells him it is [Gorzhak zo Eraz](wiki:Gorzhak_zo_Eraz), also called Pudding Face. During the meeting to discuss [Malazza](wiki:Malazza)'s orders, Kem disturbs the Second Sons to tell them that another rider has come with new orders from the supreme commander.(wiki:Kem.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btyrion_ii_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctyrion_ii.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
