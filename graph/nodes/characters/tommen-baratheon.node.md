## Origins

Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) last saw Prince Tommen when the boy was still breastfeeding.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

Tommen once adopted a [fawn](wiki:Deer) as a pet, but his elder brother, Prince [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), later killed and skinned the animal.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) It is also implied Joffrey bullies and abuses Tommen physically or emotionally.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Following the [tourney on Prince Joffrey's name day](wiki:Tourney_on_Prince_Joffrey's_name_day), Tommen and his siblings traveled with [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) with their grandfather, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister).(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Category:Images_of_Tommen_Baratheon)*

Tommen is plump, with long white-blond hair.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He has golden curls and emerald green eyes.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) At the beginning of *[A Song of Ice and Fire](wiki:A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire)*, he is seven years old.

Tommen, unlike [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), is regarded as a good-hearted lad who always tries his best.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Tommen is tenderhearted(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and not as strong-willed as his brother. Many other characters (except his mother)(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) believe that he would be a much better king than Joffrey.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Tommen is fond of applecakes,(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) and enjoys reading.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) He is a clumsy but earnest dancer.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) He is afraid of [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane).(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Tommen is part of the entourage of his father, King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), which journeys to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) following the death of Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn). At Winterfell, Tommen spars with [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) using wooden swords.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

Tommen and [Myrcella](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) do not recognize a dirty [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) when she chases cats in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Arya bowls over Tommen when she escapes from [House Lannister guards](wiki:House_Lannister_guards).(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), the new [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), discovers that Tommen and his siblings are not the biological children of Robert, but actually fathered by Cersei's twin brother, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) With Robert dying from being gored by a [boar](wiki:Boar), Lord [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) suggests that Tommen and Myrcella be seized and that Arya wed Tommen. Eddard refuses, however.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

Tommen's older brother, Prince [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), becomes king after Robert dies. Tommen is present when [Eddard is arrested](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark) in the [throne room](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Tommen attends the [tourney on King Joffrey's name day](wiki:Tourney_on_King_Joffrey's_name_day). Joffrey laughs at Tommen when the youth is struck by a [quintain](wiki:Quintain).(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

Announcing his claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) declares that his nephew Joffrey and his siblings are abominations born of [incest](wiki:Incest).(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

Tommen cries when Myrcella sails from King's Landing for [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). The [riot of King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing) breaks out after Myrcella leaves the harbor.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

With rioting and the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) making King's Landing increasingly unsafe, Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) plots to sneak Tommen out of the city to [Rosby](wiki:Rosby) under the protection of Lord [Gyles Rosby](wiki:Gyles_Rosby), disguised as a page. However, Tommen's uncle, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), learns of the plot, and sends Ser [Jacelyn Bywater](wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater) to intercept the party on the [Rosby road](wiki:Rosby_road) and take Tommen to Rosby himself, thereby under Tyrion's control and not Cersei's.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2) Tommen adopts a fawn at Rosby, and Jacelyn tasks his men with evacuating the boy to an unknown location if King's Landing falls during the war.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

Tyrion threatens Cersei that any harm inflicted on [Alayaya](wiki:Alayaya), a [prostitute](wiki:Prostitute) believed to be Tyrion's lover and arrested under the queen's orders, will be visited upon Tommen as well.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) The [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) knight who allowed Jacelyn to take Tommen, Ser [Boros Blount](wiki:Boros_Blount), is stripped of his white cloak by Cersei.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1)

### A Storm of Swords

After Jacelyn Bywater is killed in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Cersei has Tommen brought back to King's Landing by the [Kettleblack](wiki:House_Kettleblack) brothers.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

Acting of his own accord while the Hand of Stannis, Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent) attempts to settle a peace agreement with Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), Tommen's grandfather, that involves a promise of marriage between Tommen and Stannis's daughter, Princess [Shireen](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon), but his plans do not come to pass.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

During the wedding feast of Tyrion and [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), Tommen, who is now taller than his uncle, dances with [Megga Tyrell](wiki:Megga_Tyrell) and Sansa.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)

Tommen participates in the wedding of Joffrey to [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), handing the bride's cloak to his brother. The boy scatters rose petals before the king and queen after the ceremony. Tommen screams and cries when Joffrey chokes to death at his [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding).(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

Tommen is crowned king in the aftermath of Joffrey's death, and plans are made for him to wed [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) in Joffrey's stead.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0) Not wanting Cersei to raise Tommen, Tywin wants Jaime to abdicate as [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard) and educate the boy as his ward and squire at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock). However, Jaime refuses.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-1) Jaime orders Boros Blount to serve as the boy king's food taster.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) reminds Tyrion, who is imprisoned for Joffrey's death, that according to [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) law Myrcella should succeed to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) rather than her younger brother, Tommen.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0) During Tyrion's trial, [Shae](wiki:Shae) alleges that Tyrion killed Joffrey and planned to eventually kill Tommen.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)

Tommen's great-uncle, Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), provides the boy king with documents to sign, including pardons, grants, and bills of attainder.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) Tyrion kills his father Tywin, Tommen's Hand, after Jaime helps him escape from imprisonment.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos77.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Tommen continues usage of the split [black stag](wiki:House_Baratheon) and [golden lion](wiki:House_Lannister) royal banner introduced by Joffrey.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), and [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) discuss a letter prepared by Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)), which asks for assistance from King Tommen. Jon recalls how Tommen had sparred with his half-brother [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), but that Bran is dead now while Tommen is king. Jon reluctantly agrees to have the message sent by [raven](wiki:Raven).(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)

Cersei is [Queen Regent](wiki:Regent) after the death of her father, Tywin. Tommen acts as king, his primary and favorite activity being to place the royal stamp on any document put in front of him.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0) Cersei frowns upon his meek and tractable nature, frequently comparing him with disfavor to his willful, headstrong brother, Joffrey. Tommen is frightened of the dying Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane)'s screams,(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) and he flees in disgust from Tywin's corpse.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) Kevan refuses to serve as Hand to Tommen and leaves for Casterly Rock since his niece will not yield Tommen's regency.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3) Ser [Harys Swyft](wiki:Harys_Swyft) instead becomes the King's Hand.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-1)

Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) insists that Tommen wed his daughter, Margaery, before agreeing to march on [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End). Cersei does not trust [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell), however. [When they wed](wiki:Wedding_of_Tommen_I_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell), Tommen covers Margaery with the black [Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon) cloak which King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) had used when he married Cersei. Tommen dances with Margaery and her cousins, [Alla](wiki:Alla_Tyrell), [Elinor](wiki:Elinor_Tyrell), and Megga, during the feast. Tommen is fascinated and frightened when Cersei burns the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand) with [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) after the wedding.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2)

In [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) plots to crown Tommen's sister, [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon), in opposition to the boy king.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) The plan is foiled by her father, Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), however.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)

Wanting Tommen to have younger advisors, Cersei appoints [Aurane Waters](wiki:Aurane_Waters) as [master of ships](wiki:Master_of_ships) on the [small council](wiki:Small_council).(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0) When the small council receives [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) Jon Snow's request for aid for the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), Cersei agrees to send reinforcements in Tommen's name, but some are to be tasked with [assassinating](wiki:Assassin) Jon.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-2)

Margaery begins to influence Tommen to resist his mother's commands. Margaery gives Tommen three black [kittens](wiki:Kitten), [Ser Pounce](wiki:Ser_Pounce), [Lady Whiskers](wiki:Lady_Whiskers), and [Boots](wiki:Boots).(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0) Tommen also takes an interest in [knighthood](wiki:Knight) and jousting, and reacts with anger when Cersei refuses to honor his command that Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell), now of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), be named as the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep)'s master-at-arms.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-1) Cersei assigns Ser [Osney Kettleblack](wiki:Osney_Kettleblack) as Tommen's [sworn shield](wiki:Sworn_shield),(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-2) but the queen regent also tasks him with seducing Margaery.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-3)

In return for Cersei allowing the [Faith Militant](wiki:Faith_Militant) to reform, the new [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon)—the [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow)—forgives the crown's debts to the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) and blesses King Tommen.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0) The king gives Loras a jeweled sword when the knight departs for the [siege of Dragonstone](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone).(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0) When Tommen stands up to his mother and shows assertiveness when Cersei threatens Margaery, Cersei's response is to have Boros force Tommen to beat his whipping boy, [Pate](wiki:Pate_(King's_Landing)), until he bleeds.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-1)

In order to claim the inheritance of [Gyles Rosby](wiki:Gyles_Rosby), Cersei claims the late lord had wanted his land and wealth to go to Tommen.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-2)

Cersei engineers Margaery's arrest by the Faith for allegedly sleeping with a number of men.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0)(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0) Following Cersei's own arrest for treason and adultery, Tommen continues to do the bidding of the small council, now controlled by Ser [Harys Swyft](wiki:Harys_Swyft) and [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle). A lonely Tommen asks for his wife and his mother, and he signs any decree placed before him.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-1)

Jaime informs the [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) at the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun) that King Tommen requires all captives from the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell)'s seneschal [Ricasso](wiki:Ricasso) toasts Tommen's name during the feast welcoming Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear), but many [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) refuse to drink in the boy king's honor. Balon brings word that Cersei would like Myrcella to briefly return to King's Landing, and that Tommen could befriend Prince [Trystane Martell](wiki:Trystane_Martell). Later, [Ellaria Sand](wiki:Ellaria_Sand) objects to the [Sand Snakes](wiki:Sand_Snakes)' desire for vengeance against Tywin Lannister's descendants, which includes a wish to murder Tommen.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0)

Since Cersei refused to pay back the crown's debts to the [Iron Bank of Braavos](wiki:Iron_Bank_of_Braavos), [Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris) agrees to support [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) against Tommen.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-0)

Tommen is kept hidden away in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) when his mother completes her [walk of atonement](wiki:Walk_of_atonement), as his great-uncle Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), now [Lord Regent](wiki:Regent) and [Protector of the Realm](wiki:Protector_of_the_Realm), feels no son should see his mother like that.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Radwd65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-0)

Tommen has dinner with the newly-released Cersei and Kevan. He entertains his great-uncle with the exploits of his kittens, and Kevan thinks Tommen a sweet lad. Cersei wants [Russell Merryweather](wiki:Russell_Merryweather) to come to the capital and befriend Tommen. Following the meal, [Varys](wiki:Varys) has Kevan and Pycelle murdered in support of [Aegon Targaryen's](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)) attempt to take the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) from Tommen.(wiki:Tommen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_48-0)
