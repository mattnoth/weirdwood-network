## Appearances & Description

Dancy has a pug nose and freckles. She has thick red hair, long enough to reach past her waist. [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) believes she would be a lively handful.(wiki:Dancy.cite_ref-Racok29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) visits [Chataya](wiki:Chataya)'s [brothel](wiki:Brothel) to cover his secret visits to his [mistress](wiki:Mistress), [Shae](wiki:Shae), going upstairs with [Alayaya](wiki:Alayaya) to a room with a secret passage. Dancy wagers her black [pearls](wiki:Pearl) to [Marei](wiki:Marei), another whore at the brothel, that she can bed Tyrion within a fortnight. Dancy attempts to convince Tyrion to take her upstairs, but he is faithful to Shae and refuses her advances.(wiki:Dancy.cite_ref-Racok29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Dancy changes the color of her hair to honey blond.(wiki:Dancy.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Dancy.cite_ref-Rssm1151.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

When Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) arrives at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), and suggests that he is looking for a blonde woman to share with his [paramour](wiki:Paramour) [Ellaria Sand](wiki:Ellaria_Sand), Tyrion recommends Dancy to him.(wiki:Dancy.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

[Anguy](wiki:Anguy) reveals he spent the small fortune he had won at the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) on girls from Chataya's brothel—[Jayde](wiki:Jayde), [Alayaya](wiki:Alayaya), and Dancy—roast [swan](wiki:Swan), and [Arbor](wiki:Arbor) [wine](wiki:Wine).(wiki:Dancy.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
