## Appearances & Description

The baby has soft brown hair.(wiki:Aemon_Steelsong.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He is younger than [Gilly](wiki:Gilly)'s [son](wiki:Monster), but is more robust. ## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Dalla](wiki:Dalla), the wife of [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder), the [King-beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-beyond-the-Wall), dies in childbirth during the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall). [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s men and the [Night's Watchmen](wiki:Night's_Watch) call the baby "[wildling](wiki:Wildling) prince" and "the little prince" on account of his parentage, or "born-in-battle" for the moment of his birth. The infant is fed goats' milk and potions from [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)), until [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) arrives at Castle Black and is given him to [nurse](wiki:Wet_nurse), alongside [her own infant son](wiki:Monster).(wiki:Aemon_Steelsong.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

By the design of Lord Commander [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), to protect the wilding prince from [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) (who might be interested in sacrificing him for his king's blood), he is secretly switched with Gilly's babe. The baby is sent with Gilly, [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), and Maester Aemon to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), while Gilly's babe remains at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black). For much of the journey, Gilly is overcome with grief at being separated from her child,(wiki:Aemon_Steelsong.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Aemon_Steelsong.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) but eventually comes to love Dalla's child as much as she loves her own.(wiki:Aemon_Steelsong.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
The wildling prince becomes the darling of all the women in the crew of the [Cinnamon Wind](wiki:Cinnamon_Wind), and Gilly trusts them to take care of him. [Kojja Mo](wiki:Kojja_Mo) loves bouncing the baby on her knee and singing to him in the [Summer Tongue](wiki:Summer_Tongue).(wiki:Aemon_Steelsong.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Aboard the Cinnamon Wind, Maester Aemon passes away. The night before his death, he asks Gilly if he can hold the baby, and though she is afraid the old man will drop the child, she complies. Maester Aemon rocks the babe and hums a song for him, and Dalla's baby touches Aemon's face and pulls on his lip, making him laugh. During Maester Aemon's funeral, Gilly suggests to Sam that when the baby turns two years old, he should be named Aemon Battleborn or Aemon Steelsong, [in honor of](wiki:Namesake) the late maester. After the funeral, Kojja takes care of the baby, to give Gilly and Sam a chance to be intimate.(wiki:Aemon_Steelsong.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
When the Cinnamon Wind arrives at Oldtown, Sam leaves Gilly and the boy aboard the ship while he goes to present the news of his arrival to the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel). Before leaving, he tells Gilly that he will arrange for horses and a [wagon](wiki:Wagon) to take her and the baby to his mother, [Melessa Florent](wiki:Melessa_Florent), at [Horn Hill](wiki:Horn_Hill). Gilly is supposed to tell the [Tarlys](wiki:House_Tarly) that the boy is her [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son with Samwell.(wiki:Aemon_Steelsong.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Jon Snow recalls how he convinced Gilly to secretly take Dalla's baby and leave her own child behind. He has a nightmare that he ripped the babies from her arms and cut their heads off, then swapped the heads and told Gilly to sew them back on.(wiki:Aemon_Steelsong.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
