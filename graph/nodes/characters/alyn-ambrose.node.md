## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

As a squire, Alyn wore [Elinor Tyrell](wiki:Elinor_Tyrell)'s favor in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). Shouting Elinor's name as his battle cry, he killed a [Myrish](wiki:Myr) archer and a [Mullendore](wiki:House_Mullendore) man-at-arms.(wiki:Alyn_Ambrose.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Elinor and [Megga Tyrell](wiki:Megga_Tyrell) tell [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) about Alyn's feats of arms at the Blackwater. Elinor and Alyn are to marry as soon as he is [knighted](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Alyn_Ambrose.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

At [the wedding feast](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark) for Sansa and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), Alyn dances with his betrothed. Sansa also dances with the squire, but after King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) has threatened her, and Sansa fears Alyn must think her a clumsy dancer.(wiki:Alyn_Ambrose.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Alyn accompanies Elinor when she goes for a ride with her cousin, Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Alyn_Ambrose.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
