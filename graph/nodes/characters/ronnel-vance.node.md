## Origins

After declaring his uncle [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) a usurper and a tyrant, Prince [Aegon the Uncrowned](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) gathered his supporters at [Pinkmaiden Castle](wiki:Pinkmaiden_Castle) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). Lord Ronnel Vance was one of the several lords from the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) to join his strength to Aegon's cause. However, Ronnel died with the rebels at the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye) in [43 AC](wiki:43_AC), slain by Maegor's loyalists alongside the prince.(wiki:Ronnel_Vance.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)(wiki:Ronnel_Vance.cite_ref-Rtsotd.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

On account of Ronnel's support to Prince Aegon, Dowager Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) later considered wedding her son King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) to a Vance, posthumously rewarding Ronnel's loyalty to her late son.(wiki:Ronnel_Vance.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
