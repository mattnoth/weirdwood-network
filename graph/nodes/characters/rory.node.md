## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

When [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) object to insufficient food given to them by the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), the new [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), has Rory quiet the tumult with his horn.(wiki:Rory.cite_ref-Radwd21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Rory is among the [rangers](wiki:Ranger) who escort Jon and the six new recruits of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) who follow the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods) ([Hareth](wiki:Hareth_(Mole's_Town)), [Arron](wiki:Arron), [Emrick](wiki:Emrick), [Satin](wiki:Satin) and the [wildlings](wiki:Free_folk) [Leathers](wiki:Leathers) and [Jax](wiki:Jax)) to the grove of [weirwoods](wiki:Weirwood) in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest) where they swear their vows. When they learn there are other wildlings in the grove, Jon has Rory and [Pate](wiki:Pate_(ranger)) stay behind with the horses while the rest investigate.(wiki:Rory.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Rory is is one of the rangers Jon has accompany him [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) to meet [Tormund](wiki:Tormund).(wiki:Rory.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

[Fulk the Flea](wiki:Fulk_the_Flea) and Mully stand guard when Jon informs the [Shieldhall](wiki:Shieldhall) about [the letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) from [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), and the two guards are replaced with [Horse](wiki:Hareth_(Mole's_Town)) and Rory at the changing of the watch. Horse and Rory hurry after Jon when they hear screaming at [Hardin's Tower](wiki:Hardin's_Tower). During the commotion, Jon is attacked in the [mutiny at Castle Black](wiki:Mutiny_at_Castle_Black).
