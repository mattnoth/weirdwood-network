## Origins

Maris taught generations of orphaned girls at a [motherhouse](wiki:Motherhouse) in [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown).(wiki:Maris_(Mother).cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) The building later came to be called the [Motherhouse of Maris](wiki:Motherhouse_of_Maris).(wiki:Maris_(Mother).cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
After wedding his sister, [Alysanne](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) sent [seven preachers](wiki:Seven_Speakers) to walk the realm and win [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) over to the marriage, not with swords, but with words. Mother Maris was among these seven. They traveled the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) alone, walking from village to village, telling smallfolk of Jaehaerys's wisdom and Alysanne's kindness. When challenged on the issue of [incest](wiki:Incest), the seven would say the [Valyrians](wiki:Valyria) mounted [dragons](wiki:Dragons) and thus were not like other men, and as such, an exception had to be made.(wiki:Maris_(Mother).cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
