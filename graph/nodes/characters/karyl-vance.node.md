## Appearances & Description

Karyl would be handsome except for his winestain birthmark which discolors one side of his face and half his neck.(wiki:Karyl_Vance.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He has sad eyes(wiki:Karyl_Vance.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and a melancholy look.(wiki:Karyl_Vance.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) The knight has a calm demeanor,(wiki:Karyl_Vance.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) but his birthmark can darken when he is angered.(wiki:Karyl_Vance.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Karyl accompanies his father, [Lord Vance](wiki:Lord_Vance), to the pass below the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth) when hostilities threaten to break out.(wiki:Karyl_Vance.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Ser [Marq Piper](wiki:Marq_Piper), Ser [Raymun Darry](wiki:Raymun_Darry), and Karyl are sent to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to receive King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s permission to hunt down the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) men who have been raiding the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), agrees and sends Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) with a party to kill Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane).(wiki:Karyl_Vance.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Karyl becomes [Lord of Wayfarer's Rest](wiki:Lord_of_Wayfarer's_Rest) after the death of his father in the [battle in the hills below the Golden Tooth](wiki:Battle_in_the_hills_below_the_Golden_Tooth). Karyl and Marq raid the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) across the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) of the [Trident](wiki:Trident).(wiki:Karyl_Vance.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

The new Lord Karyl is in the list of nobles King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) declares must do him homage or be named traitors to [the realm](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Karyl_Vance.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Karyl and Marq instead bring the young [Lyman Darry](wiki:Lyman_Darry), [Lord of Darry](wiki:Lord_of_Darry), to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), where [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) is declared [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) by [northmen](wiki:Northmen) and [rivermen](wiki:Rivermen).(wiki:Karyl_Vance.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Lord Karyl departs Riverrun to defend his lands with permission from his liege lord, Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully).(wiki:Karyl_Vance.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Karyl commands the defense of fords upstream from Riverrun in the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords).(wiki:Karyl_Vance.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Lord Karyl is among the seven nobles who launch the funeral boat of Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully) at Riverrun.(wiki:Karyl_Vance.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Lord Karyl bent the knee after the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding). He is one of the reluctant river lords who join with [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) in the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), and he participates in Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s war council.(wiki:Karyl_Vance.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) Once Riverrun surrenders, Karyl advises Jaime to resolve the [siege of Raventree](wiki:Siege_of_Raventree).(wiki:Karyl_Vance.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
