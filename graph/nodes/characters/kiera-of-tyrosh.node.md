## Origins

During her marriage to Prince [Valarr Targaryen](wiki:Valarr_Targaryen), Kiera gave birth to sons, though unfortunately they were stillborn.(wiki:Kiera_of_Tyrosh.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Prince Valarr died during the [Great Spring Sickness](wiki:Great_Spring_Sickness),(wiki:Kiera_of_Tyrosh.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) making Kiera a widow. An unknown amount of time later, Kiera was married to Valarr's cousin, Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)).(wiki:Kiera_of_Tyrosh.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_targaryen_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Together with Prince Daeron, Kiera had a daughter, Princess [Vaella](wiki:Vaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daeron)), born in [222 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). Vaella proved to be sweet, but sadly [simple-minded](wiki:Lackwit).(wiki:Kiera_of_Tyrosh.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maekar_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Kiera_of_Tyrosh.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
