## Origins

The secret tunnel from Chataya's brothel, to a stable three blocks away, was dug for an unspecified [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) whose honor would not let him openly visit her establishment. Chataya has closely guarded the knowledge of the tunnel's existence.(wiki:Chataya.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7)

Chataya's daughter, [Alayaya](wiki:Alayaya), was born in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), near the time of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Chataya.cite_ref-6)

## Appearances & Description

Chataya is a tall, elegant,(wiki:Chataya.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) dignified, and handsome(wiki:Chataya.cite_ref-Racok29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) black woman(wiki:Chataya.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) from the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles). She has [ebon](wiki:Ebony) skin, and sandalwood-colored eyes.(wiki:Chataya.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Her voice is smooth, and resonant with the accent of the Summer Isles.(wiki:Chataya.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) She has a graceful, gliding walk.(wiki:Chataya.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)(wiki:Chataya.cite_ref-Racok29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Following the beliefs of the Summer Islanders, Chataya feels that there is no shame in prostitution, and that people honor the [gods](wiki:Gods) with their bodies and their desires.(wiki:Chataya.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) She runs a choice establishment,(wiki:Chataya.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) that she has worked for a long time to make that way.(wiki:Chataya.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

Chataya owns a feathered gown,(wiki:Chataya.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) and a gown of flowing [silk](wiki:Silk).(wiki:Chataya.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

One of Chataya's [prostitutes](wiki:Prostitute), a [young red-haired girl](wiki:Barra's_mother), has given birth to a [bastard](wiki:Bastard) [daughter](wiki:Barra) of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). Chataya has promised the girl six months off from working, so that she can take care of the baby, and in hopes that Robert will be a return client.(wiki:Chataya.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

During Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s search for the king's bastards, [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) brings him to Chataya's brothel, where Eddard speaks with the girl and examines her baby. While Ned does so, Petyr chats with Chataya, and when they leave, he kisses her hand and tells her a joke that makes her laugh out loud.(wiki:Chataya.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6)

### A Clash of Kings

Obeying the orders of Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) to kill King Robert's bastards, the [gold cloak](wiki:Gold_cloak) [Allar Deem](wiki:Allar_Deem) kills Barra, as well as her mother when she tries to stop him.(wiki:Chataya.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) When the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sends Allar to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) (and has him killed on the way there),(wiki:Chataya.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Chataya is grateful to him. When Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) asks her, she arranges for Tyrion to use her [establishment](wiki:Chataya's_brothel) and her daughter [Alayaya](wiki:Alayaya) as a cover when he visits [Shae](wiki:Shae) in [her manse](wiki:Shae's_manse).(wiki:Chataya.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-8)
