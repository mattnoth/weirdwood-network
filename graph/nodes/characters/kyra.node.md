## Identity

Kyra is a tavern wench who works at the Smoking Log in the winter town outside Winterfell (wiki:Kyra).

## Origins

Born 280–281 AC in the winter town (track_b: Born).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Theon Greyjoy has intimate knowledge of Kyra before the events of the series (wiki:Kyra.cite_ref-Ragot37). When Theon captures Winterfell in ACOK, he has Kyra brought to him as his mistress. Initially eager, she is later raped by Theon after he has a prophetic nightmare (wiki:Kyra.cite_ref-Racok56). After the sack of Winterfell, Kyra is among the survivors held at the Dreadfort by House Bolton (wiki:Kyra). She escapes through a postern gate with stolen keys, freeing Theon as well, but the escape proves to be a game devised by Ramsay Bolton for hunting sport. Both are recaptured within the hour; Kyra throws a stone at Ramsay but misses. She is killed by Ramsay's hounds (wiki:Kyra.cite_ref-Radwd12).
