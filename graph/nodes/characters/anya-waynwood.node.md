## Origins

Lord [Nestor Royce](wiki:Nestor_Royce) hoped to marry his daughter, [Myranda](wiki:Myranda_Royce), to [Harrold Hardyng](wiki:Harrold_Hardyng), but Lady Waynwood refused. She does not want her ward to marry against his will. Myranda thinks that Harry might marry one of Anya's granddaughters.(wiki:Anya_Waynwood.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Appearances & Description

The slim Anya has greying brown hair, loose skin beneath her chin, and crows-feet around her eyes.(wiki:Anya_Waynwood.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Anya_Waynwood.cite_ref-twow_6-0)

Lady Waynwood is stern(wiki:Anya_Waynwood.cite_ref-twow_6-1) but courteous.(wiki:Anya_Waynwood.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Anya's mantle is deep green with the broken wheel of [House Waynwood](wiki:House_Waynwood) picked out in beads of [jet](wiki:Jet).(wiki:Anya_Waynwood.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) Her riding cloak is heavy green wool trimmed with brown fur, and her broken wheel brooch is inlaid with niello.(wiki:Anya_Waynwood.cite_ref-twow_6-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lady Anya is present in the [High Hall](wiki:High_Hall) of the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), surrounded by her sons, when [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) demands a [trial by battle](wiki:Trial_by_battle) to prove his innocence of the claims against him.(wiki:Anya_Waynwood.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Feast for Crows

Anya is one of the [Lords Declarant](wiki:Lords_Declarant) who swear to drive Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) out of the [Vale](wiki:Vale) and to protect their young lord, [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn). After signing a declaration at [Runestone](wiki:Runestone),(wiki:Anya_Waynwood.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) they gather six thousand men to the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon), demanding that Petyr give up his position of [Lord Protector of the Vale](wiki:Lord_Protector_of_the_Vale) and leave the province.(wiki:Anya_Waynwood.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

Petyr invites the Lords to the Eyrie for negotiations. Petyr has secretly bribed one of them, Ser [Lyn Corbray](wiki:Lyn_Corbray), to draw his sword, [Lady Forlorn](wiki:Lady_Forlorn), at the meeting. Anya finds Lyn's actions dishonorable, which gives Petyr an edge in the negotiations. Petyr asks for a year to set things right in the Vale, which the lords agree to.(wiki:Anya_Waynwood.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5) Afterward, Petyr confides in [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) that the elderly Lady Waynwood might be dead in a year.(wiki:Anya_Waynwood.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)

Anya and Ser [Symond Templeton](wiki:Symond_Templeton) unexpectedly attend the [the wedding feast](wiki:Wedding_of_Lyonel_Corbray_and_Lady_Corbray) of Lord [Lyonel Corbray](wiki:Lyonel_Corbray) in [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown). Petyr explains to Sansa that he has bought up the debts of the Waynwoods and persuaded Anya to give permission for Sansa to wed her ward, [Harrold Hardyng](wiki:Harrold_Hardyng), if Harry agrees.(wiki:Anya_Waynwood.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

### The Winds of Winter

Lady Anya, her son Ser [Wallace](wiki:Wallace_Waynwood) and grandson Ser [Roland](wiki:Roland_Waynwood), and her ward Ser Harrold Hardyng, attend the tourney for the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights) at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon). Sansa, who is disguised as "Alayne Stone", greets them when they arrive, and [Myranda Royce](wiki:Myranda_Royce) directs the Waynwoods to the East Tower. Anya has Harry insist upon dancing with Alayne at the feast.(wiki:Anya_Waynwood.cite_ref-twow_6-3)
