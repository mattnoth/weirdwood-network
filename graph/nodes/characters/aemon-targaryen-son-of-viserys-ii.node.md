## Identity

Prince Aemon Targaryen, known as the Dragonknight, was the second son of King Viserys II Targaryen and his wife Lady Larra Rogare (wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_II)).

## Origins

Born in 136 AC at the Red Keep in King's Landing (track_b: Born). Son of Viserys II Targaryen and Larra Rogare (track_b: Father, Mother). Died between 178 and 183 AC (track_b: Died).

## Appearances & Description

Aemon has been referred to as the noblest knight who ever lived, and his skill with a sword is legendary throughout the Seven Kingdoms. Even over a hundred years after his death, he remains a popular figure among highborn and smallfolk alike (wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_II)).

## Narrative Arc

Aemon joined the Kingsguard at seventeen during the reign of his uncle King Aegon III Targaryen, eventually rising to Lord Commander. He served five kings total. He was rumored to be the lover of his sister Queen Naerys Targaryen, and it was whispered that King Daeron II was actually Aemon's son rather than Aegon IV's — a claim that fueled the Blackfyre Rebellions. He died saving the life of his brother King Aegon IV Targaryen (wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_II)).
