## Narrative Arc

Rodrik was Balon Greyjoy's eldest son and heir. During Greyjoy's Rebellion in 289 AC, Rodrik led the ironborn assault on Seagard but was slain beneath the walls by Lord Jason Mallister. His death was a devastating blow to the ironborn cause and to Balon personally. Along with the death of his brother Maron at the Siege of Pyke, Rodrik's fall left Theon as Balon's only surviving son (wiki:Rodrik_Greyjoy).
