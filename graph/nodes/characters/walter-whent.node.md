## Origins

In [281 AC](wiki:281_AC), the year of the false spring, Lord Whent hosted the great [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) to honor his [maiden](wiki:Maiden) daughter.(wiki:Walter_Whent.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Walter_Whent.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) recalls that the tourney was announced by Walter shortly after he was visited by his brother, Ser [Oswell Whent](wiki:Oswell_Whent) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Walter_Whent.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Most people believed that Walter was attempting to outdo the [Anniversary Tourney](wiki:Anniversary_Tourney) hosted by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) in [272 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), but some believed that a "shadow host"—possibly Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen)—provided the funding for Walter's extravagant prizes.(wiki:Walter_Whent.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Lord Whent's daughter reigned as [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty) when the tourney began. Her four brothers and her uncle, Oswell, swore to defend her crown, but Prince Rhaegar won the joust and named [Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark) the new queen of love and beauty.(wiki:Walter_Whent.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Walter_Whent.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), Rhaegar's father, unexpectedly attended the tourney and was suspicious of Walter.(wiki:Walter_Whent.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) defeated a Lord Whent in the [tourney at Lannisport](wiki:Tourney_at_Lannisport) in [289 AC](wiki:289_AC); it is unknown if it was Walter or one of his descendants.(wiki:Walter_Whent.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
