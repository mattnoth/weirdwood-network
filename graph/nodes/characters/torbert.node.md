### A Feast for Crows

Septon Torbert is among the leading possibilities to become the new High Septon after [the old one](wiki:High_Septon_(Tyrion's)) died.(wiki:Torbert.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Torbert kisses Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s feet whenever they meet.
