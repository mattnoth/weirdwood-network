## Identity

Dalton Greyjoy, the Red Kraken, the daring and bloodthirsty sixteen-year-old Lord Reaper of Pyke, was offered the admiralty and a council seat by Otto.

## Origins

### Youth

The wild young son of the heir to [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), Dalton rowed at age five and reaved at ten, sailing with his uncle to plunder the pirate towns of the [Basilisk Isles](wiki:Basilisk_Isles). By fourteen, Dalton had sailed as far as [Old Ghis](wiki:Old_Ghis), fought in a dozen actions, and claimed four [salt wives](wiki:Salt_wife).(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf755_2-1)

Dalton claimed a [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) longsword, which he named [Nightfall](wiki:Nightfall), off a dead [corsair](wiki:Pirate). While fighting in the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) as a sellsail, the fifteen-year-old Dalton avenged his uncle after watching his death. Because Dalton emerged from the fight drenched in blood from a dozen wounds, men began calling him the Red Kraken. Later in the same year, Dalton returned to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) to claim the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair) after hearing of his father's death. Immediately he began to build longships, forge swords, and train fighters, citing that "the storm is coming" as the reason.(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf755_2-2)

### The Dance of the Dragons

The [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) began after the death of King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) in [129 AC](wiki:129_AC) when Dalton was sixteen. The Red Kraken is said to have laughed after hearing of the outbreak of war.(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf755_2-3)

The [greens](wiki:Greens) of [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) offered Dalton the position of [master of ships](wiki:Master_of_ships) and the admiralty to replace Ser [Tyland Lannister](wiki:Tyland_Lannister), who had been made [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin), if Dalton would bring his ships around Westeros to battle Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon), the Sea Snake. Instead of leaping to the offer, Dalton waited to see what the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) had to offer.(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf755_2-4) On the [black council](wiki:Black_council), Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) suggested appealing to Dalton's bloodlust to bring him on the side of [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen). Instead of asking him to sail to [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay), Rhaenyra only asked for Dalton to attack her enemies.(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf46.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

The Red Kraken chose black over green, deciding to attack the nearby [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands), vulnerable with Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister) campaigning in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). Jason's wife, Lady [Johanna](wiki:Johanna_Lannister), barred the gates of [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) but was unable to protect the rest of the west. Dalton burned the [fleet of House Lannister](wiki:Lannister_fleet) and sacked [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport), carrying off gold, grain, and trade goods. Hundreds of women and girls were taken as salt wives, including the favorite mistress of Jason and their natural daughters. Dalton nearly captured [Kayce](wiki:Kayce),(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-4) and after the fall of [Faircastle](wiki:Faircastle) and [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle)(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rfab16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) he claimed four of Lord [Farman](wiki:House_Farman)'s daughters as salt wives, giving the fifth, the "homely one", to his brother [Veron](wiki:Veron_Greyjoy).(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf755_2-5) Lord Jason was killed in the [Battle of the Red Fork](wiki:Battle_of_the_Red_Fork) in [130 AC](wiki:130_AC).(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf46.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### Control of Fair Isle

For the better part of two years,(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf755_2-6) the Red Kraken ruled the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea) like the ironborn kings of old,(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf751_6-0) although he did not claim the title [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rfab20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Ser [Tyland Lannister](wiki:Tyland_Lannister) and the [regency of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III) commanded Dalton to cease his raiding, but the Red Kraken ignored them.(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf755_2-7) When Lady [Johanna Lannister](wiki:Johanna_Lannister) began building a new fleet on behalf of her son, Lord [Loreon Lannister](wiki:Loreon_Lannister_(son_of_Jason)), Dalton's ironmen burned her shipyards and abducted another hundred women.(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rfab20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

Dalton never took a [rock wife](wiki:Rock_wife), although he had twenty-two [salt wives](wiki:Salt_wives)(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rfab22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) and boasted of having a hundred.(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rfab20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) When Dalton heard of the [Maiden's Day Ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball), he considered sending one of his sisters as a candidate to become King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen)'s second bride.(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rfab21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) After her westermen [defended Kayce](wiki:Battle_at_Kayce) again and slew Dalton's favorite uncle,(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rfab20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3) Johanna sent a ragtag fleet to discretely invade [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle). The ironmen [ambushed these westermen](wiki:Ambush_at_Fair_Isle), however, and Dalton sent the heads of Lord [Prester](wiki:House_Prester), Lord [Tarbeck](wiki:House_Tarbeck), and Ser [Erwin Lannister](wiki:Erwin_Lannister) to Johanna at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rfab21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

After Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) defeated the [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) fleet in the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) during the [Daughters' War](wiki:Daughters'_War), Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake) attempted to rid himself of Alyn Oakenfist by sending him to end Dalton's insurrection.(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rfab21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2) Dalton responded by gathering hundred of longships to [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle) and the coasts of the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands). The Red Kraken intended to conquer the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands) and [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark) and sack [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) and [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear).(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rfab22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

### Death

While sleeping in Lord Farman's bedchamber at [Faircastle](wiki:Faircastle) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Dalton was killed when one of his [salt wives](wiki:Salt_wives), the girl [Tess](wiki:Tess), cut his throat with his own dagger before throwing herself into the sea. As the Red Kraken had never taken a [rock wife](wiki:Rock_wife), his heirs were two young [salt sons](wiki:Salt_son) at [Pyke](wiki:Pyke_(island)), [Toron](wiki:Toron_Greyjoy) and [Rodrik](wiki:Rodrik_Greyjoy_(son_of_Dalton)). Dalton also had three sisters and several ambitious cousins.(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rfab22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) Toron was only five years old,(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rfab22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3) and within hours of the Red Kraken's death a bloody struggle for succession broke out among the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn).(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf755_2-8) Hundreds of ironmen were killed as [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle) rose in rebellion. Faircastle held out for a time, but the castle fell after [Gunthor Goodbrother](wiki:Gunthor_Goodbrother) slew [Alester Wynch](wiki:Alester_Wynch) while fighting for another of Dalton's salt wives, [Lysa Farman](wiki:Lysa_Farman). Having arrived to find Dalton already dead, Alyn Oakenfist left a third of his fleet with the westermen and returned home for the [crownlands](wiki:Crownlands).(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rfab22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-4)

A bloody [struggle for power](wiki:Struggle_for_power) broke out in the Iron Islands after Dalton's death.(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rfab23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) In [134 AC](wiki:134_AC) Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister)'s widow, [Johanna Lannister](wiki:Johanna_Lannister), avenged Dalton's raids by having her men-at-arms sail to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) with the fleet of Ser [Leo Costayne](wiki:Leo_Costayne), the lord admiral of the [Reach](wiki:Reach). Among the ironborn slain were two of Dalton's sisters and nine of his cousins. His younger son, Rodrik, was taken captive, gelded, and made into [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock)'s new [fool](wiki:Fool).(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf755_2-9)

Dalton's sword, [Nightfall](wiki:Nightfall), eventually passed to [House Harlaw](wiki:House_Harlaw).(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) The Red Kraken's life is included in Archmaester [Mancaster](wiki:Mancaster)'s *[Sea Demons: A History of the Children of the Drowned God of the Isles](wiki:Sea_Demons:_A_History_of_the_Children_of_the_Drowned_God_of_the_Isles)*.(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rfab20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-4)

## Appearances & Description

Dalton was a daring and bloodthirsty man. He was loved by his men but not by his wives, as he tired of women quickly. Despite his youth, Dalton was shrewd and wild. Dalton is regarded as a great hero of the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands).(wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf755_2-0)

## Notes

Historical figure from the Dance of the Dragons era. Died 133 AC at Faircastle, murdered by his salt wife Tess. Claimed Nightfall, a Valyrian steel sword later held by House Harlaw. Had at least twenty-four salt wives.
