## Identity

Dalton Greyjoy, known as the Red Kraken, was the Lord of the Iron Islands and Lord Reaper of Pyke during the Dance of the Dragons (wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy).

## Appearances & Description

Dalton was a daring and bloodthirsty man, shrewd and wild despite his youth. He was loved by his men but not by his wives, as he tired of women quickly. He is regarded as a great hero among the ironborn (wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy).

## Narrative Arc

Dalton rowed at age five and reaved at ten, sailing to plunder the Basilisk Isles. By fourteen he had sailed as far as Old Ghis and claimed four salt wives. He won a Valyrian steel longsword he named Nightfall off a dead corsair. Fighting in the Stepstones at fifteen, he emerged drenched in blood from a dozen wounds, earning the name Red Kraken. When the Dance of the Dragons began, both greens and blacks courted Dalton; he sided with Rhaenyra's blacks and attacked the westerlands, sacking Lannisport, burning the Lannister fleet, and taking Fair Isle. He held Fair Isle with an iron grip and took at least twenty-four salt wives. Dalton was killed at Faircastle in 133 AC when his salt wife Tess slit his throat and leapt from the castle walls (wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy).
