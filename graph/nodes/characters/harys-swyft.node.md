## Origins

When [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister) was [Lord of Casterly Rock](wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock), Ser Harys borrowed large sums of money from [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister). Upon Ser [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s return from the [War of the Ninepenny Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Ninepenny_Kings), the young heir of [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) was determined to return his House's status and began by demanding repayment of gold lent by his father, Tytos. Those who could not repay immediately had to send a hostage to the Rock until their debt was settled. Harys hastened to obey, stating, "The lion has awoken." Unable to pay immediately, Harys surrendered his daughter, [Dorna](wiki:Dorna_Swyft), into the custody of Tywin's younger brother Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) until the debt was settled. Kevan eventually married Dorna.(wiki:Harys_Swyft.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Appearances & Description

Harys has a small white beard(wiki:Harys_Swyft.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) over his small chin.(wiki:Harys_Swyft.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) He is bald, soft, and obsequious.(wiki:Harys_Swyft.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

Harys's yellow doublet is decorated with the bantam rooster of [House Swyft](wiki:House_Swyft) in [lapis](wiki:Lapis) beads.(wiki:Harys_Swyft.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) He also wears a cloak and breeches of yellow silk. His unnamed wife is extremely fat.(wiki:Harys_Swyft.cite_ref-Mercy_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Harys is part of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s force in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), and he participates in the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork). When a courier arrives at Tywin's camp bearing the news of the defeat of the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) forces in the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), lifting the siege of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), Harys questions Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s tactical ability, criticizing that Jaime split up his host into three different camps. Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) refutes his good-father's critique, considering Riverrun's strategic position. When the defeatist Harys wants to appeal for peace, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) explains that [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) will want vengeance for the execution of his father, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark). Harys suggests appealing to the Crown for new troops and trying to raise a new host at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Harys_Swyft.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

### A Clash of Kings

Harys is with Lord Tywin's army when they [take Harrenhal](wiki:Yielding_of_Harrenhal). During their time there Harys alienates the cooks, who spit in his food. His squire is killed in an argument with the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) over their inability to kill Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion). [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) watches Harys kiss [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat) under Tywin's orders to put the conflict behind them.(wiki:Harys_Swyft.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Harys is trapped beneath his horse after it falls on him. He is rescued by one of his men-at-arms, [Willit](wiki:Willit), who defends him against all attackers, taking grievous wounds in the process.(wiki:Harys_Swyft.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Harys is among the nobles that accompany [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to meet the [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) party when they arrive at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Harys_Swyft.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Harys is named [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) by Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), because he is a weak man whom Cersei could use as a hostage against her uncle, Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister).(wiki:Harys_Swyft.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3) Harys is confused during the meetings of the [small council](wiki:Small_council) of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Harys_Swyft.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-4)(wiki:Harys_Swyft.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

After the death of Lord [Gyles Rosby](wiki:Gyles_Rosby), Cersei names Harys [lord treasurer](wiki:Lord_treasurer) and replaces him as Hand with Lord [Orton Merryweather](wiki:Orton_Merryweather).(wiki:Harys_Swyft.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

When Cersei is arrested by the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), Harys and Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) take control of King Tommen and request that Kevan Lannister return to King's Landing as regent.(wiki:Harys_Swyft.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Harys continues to serve as lord treasurer,(wiki:Harys_Swyft.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) although his vaults are empty.(wiki:Harys_Swyft.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) In order to placate the [Iron Bank of Braavos](wiki:Iron_Bank_of_Braavos), he writes to the bankers of [Myr](wiki:Myr) in hopes of a loan and is advised by Kevan to also write to the magisters of [Pentos](wiki:Pentos). Kevan refuses to follow Harys's suggestion of raising taxes as the realm is too full of rebellion and half its lords cannot tell taxation from tyranny. Kevan suggests to Harys that he travel to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) and treat with the Iron Bank in person.(wiki:Harys_Swyft.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)

### The Winds of Winter

In Braavos, Harys attends a performance of *[The Bloody Hand](wiki:The_Bloody_Hand)* at [the Gate](wiki:The_Gate), accompanied by the [Black Pearl of Braavos](wiki:Bellegere_Otherys_(courtesan)) and some of the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) as guards, including [Raff the Sweetling](wiki:Rafford).(wiki:Harys_Swyft.cite_ref-Mercy_4-2)
