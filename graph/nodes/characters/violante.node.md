## Origins

After King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) wed his sister, [Alysanne](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), he sent [seven preachers](wiki:Seven_Speakers), including Septa Violante, to walk the [realm](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) with the mission of winning over the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) on the issue of their [incestious](wiki:Incest) [marriage](wiki:Marriage). The royal couple thought it would be easier to spread the values of the [Doctrine of Exceptionalism](wiki:Doctrine_of_Exceptionalism) to their people with words rather than [swords](wiki:Swords). Violante and the others traveled the realm alone, walking from village to village, praising Jaehaerys's wisdom and Alysanne's kindness.(wiki:Violante.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
