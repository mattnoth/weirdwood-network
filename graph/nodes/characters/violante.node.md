## Identity

Septa Violante was a septa of the Faith of the Seven during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen (wiki:Violante).

## Appearances & Description

Violante was small and renowned for her skills as a healer (wiki:Violante).

## Narrative Arc

After King Jaehaerys I Targaryen wed his sister Alysanne, he sent seven preachers, including Septa Violante, to walk the realm with the mission of winning over the smallfolk regarding their incestuous marriage. The royal couple believed the values of the Doctrine of Exceptionalism could be spread with words rather than swords. Violante and the others traveled alone from village to village, praising Jaehaerys's wisdom and Alysanne's kindness (wiki:Violante, cite: Fire & Blood, A Time of Testing - The Realm Remade).
