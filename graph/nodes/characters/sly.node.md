## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Sly, [Stalker](wiki:Stalker), and One Eye hunt down and kill a party of [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) for food. Varamyr considers which of his animals he will enter into when he dies, and decides not Sly, as he does not want to be a bitch in his new life. Varamyr dies, and enters One Eye.(wiki:Sly.cite_ref-Radwdprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark)'s [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) [Summer](wiki:Summer) encounters the wolf pack eating the remains of the dead [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) deserters. Summer recognizes that the head wolf, One Eye, has a [warg](wiki:Warg) within him, and they fight. After defeating One Eye and making him submit, Summer makes Sly and Stalker submit as well, and takes over leadership of the pack.(wiki:Sly.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Near the [cave of the last greenseer](wiki:Cave_of_the_last_greenseer), Summer and the pack find a wight [snow bear](wiki:Snow_bear), and tear it to pieces and devour it, even though the rotten meat still moves while they eat it.(wiki:Sly.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
