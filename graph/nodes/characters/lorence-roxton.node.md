## Origins

In [49 AC](wiki:49_AC), the young king [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) sought to fill five slots within his [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). At the [wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) of his mother Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) and Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon), Jaehaerys declared that the [knights](wiki:Knight) would fight on foot over a position in his Kingsguard. In the subsequent [War for the White Cloaks](wiki:War_for_the_White_Cloaks) in which hundreds of knights competed for the honor of serving in the Kingsguard, Ser Lorence Roxton was one of the five champions given the white cloak of the Kingsguard, being the only victor from a noble house.(wiki:Lorence_Roxton.cite_ref-4)(wiki:Lorence_Roxton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

Lorence and Ser [Joffrey Doggett](wiki:Joffrey_Doggett) protected Jaehaerys I when the king met with Rogar Baratheon, the [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End), after he had attempted to place Princess [Rhaella](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon)) on the throne.(wiki:Lorence_Roxton.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) During the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) celebrating the king's tenth anniversary of his coronation in [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Lorence was unhorsed by young Ser [Ryam Redwyne](wiki:Ryam_Redwyne).(wiki:Lorence_Roxton.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
