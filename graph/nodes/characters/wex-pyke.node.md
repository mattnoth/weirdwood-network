## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Wex Pyke](wiki:Category:Images_of_Wex_Pyke)*

Wex has a tangle of dark brown hair with an almost feral face. He has a wide mouth, a sharp nose and a pointed chin.(wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He was born mute.(wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Wex is a clever(wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) boy who learns quickly and is adept with daggers.(wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) returns to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) for the first time since being taken away as a hostage after [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion).(wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Wex becomes Theon's [squire](wiki:Squire) as part of the deal that Theon strikes to buy a horse, [Smiler](wiki:Smiler), from [Sawane Botley](wiki:Sawane_Botley), [Lord of Lordsport](wiki:Lord_of_Lordsport).(wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Theon has Wex wait at [Otter Gimpknee's inn](wiki:Otter_Gimpknee's_inn) when he goes to see his new longship, which he names *[Sea Bitch](wiki:Sea_Bitch)*. When Theon returns to the inn with a woman calling herself "Esgred", he sees that the clever Wex has won a pile of copper [coins](wiki:Currency) while [dicing](wiki:Dicing).(wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) Wex recognizes "Esgred" as actually being [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), and Theon strikes the boy for smirking when his sister embarrasses him.(wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

Wex participates in the [harrying of the Stony Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore),(wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and later in the [capture](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). He sleeps below the foot of Theon's bed in Winterfell. When [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) disappear, Wex is part of the search party and he surprises a [vixen](wiki:Fox) at a stream. He claps his hands to point out that they are now only seeing [wolf prints](wiki:Direwolf) and not [Hodor](wiki:Hodor)'s tread, and Theon thinks the search party has been tricked. Theon sends Wex back to Winterfell before he goes to the [Acorn Water](wiki:Acorn_Water) mill with [Aggar](wiki:Aggar), [Gelmarr](wiki:Gelmarr), [Gynir](wiki:Gynir), and [Reek](wiki:Ramsay_Snow).(wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Wex is frightened by Theon's nightmares.(wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) The boy is the first to stand alongside Theon when Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel)'s northmen come to [retake](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell) the castle.(wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Reek, actually [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), attacks the northmen in a [battle at Winterfell](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell) and then has the castle burned.(wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

### A Dance With Dragons

Wex survives the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell) by climbing the [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree) in the [godswood](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell), and he witnesses the departure of Bran and Rickon Stark along with their direwolves and companions. He follows Rickon, [Osha](wiki:Osha), and [Shaggydog](wiki:Shaggydog), learning they are abandoning the mainland and crossing the [Bay of Seals](wiki:Bay_of_Seals) to [Skagos](wiki:Skagos). Eventually falling into [Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) control, Wex is taken to [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor), where they realize he is not unintelligent, only mute. They begin to teach him his letters, asking only yes or no questions, and he uses chalk to draw pictures. Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) and [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover) learn the truth of the sack of Winterfell, and Wex repudiates Ramsay's claim of Theon being to blame.(wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

After [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) is captured and his execution is faked, Wex is introduced to Davos in the [New Castle](wiki:New_Castle), and Davos finds that Wex's eyes look older than the boy's actual age. Wex reveals the location of Rickon by throwing Robett's dagger at Skagos(wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_wex_pyke.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) on Wyman's map. Lord Manderly tasks Davos with retrieving Rickon.(wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
