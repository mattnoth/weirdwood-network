## Origins

When Maester [Cressen](wiki:Cressen) became ill in [298 AC](wiki:298_AC), Pylos was sent by the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). It was said that he was to be Cressen's assistant, but Cressen was aware that truly meant that Pylos was to be his replacement after he died. Pylos's rooms are only half a turn down the stairs from Cressen's, in earshot of the elderly maester.(wiki:Pylos.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

## Appearances & Description

No more than twenty-five years old, Pylos is solemn and polite.(wiki:Pylos.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) He is always correct and precise in his speech.(wiki:Pylos.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) The young maester is clever and diligent and well-meaning,(wiki:Pylos.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) as well as kind(wiki:Pylos.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) and courageous.(wiki:Pylos.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He can see a man's worth is more than his birth.(wiki:Pylos.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Pylos serves at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) as an assistant maester, helping Maester Cressen, who is too old and frail to perform all his duties. Among other duties, Pylos does all the work with the [ravens](wiki:Raven), since Cressen is unable to climb the stairs to the [rookery](wiki:Rookery). When the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) sends a [white raven](wiki:White_raven) to announce the end of summer, Pylos shows the bird to Princess [Shireen Baratheon](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon).(wiki:Pylos.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

That night, Cressen rests before dinner, and when he wakes, Pylos does not answer his summons. Cressen is distraught when he arrives late to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s feast in the [Great Hall](wiki:Great_Hall_(Dragonstone)), and discovers that Pylos is in his place at the table. On Cressen's query, Pylos blushes, and tells him Stannis had ordered him not to awaken Cressen. Stannis then dismisses Cressen, announcing that Pylos will now fulfill all of the duties of the Dragonstone maester. Cressen tries to kill [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) with the [strangler](wiki:Strangler), but the elderly maester instead dies from the [poison](wiki:Poison).(wiki:Pylos.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

Pylos helps prepare Stannis's letter to all the [lords](wiki:Lord) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) which proclaims him the true king. In the letter, Stannis asserts that Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), not King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), is the father of Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s children, and thus that [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) is a [bastard](wiki:Bastard) born of [incest](wiki:Incest) and is illegitimately sitting the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). When Pylos includes a "harmless courtesy" in the letter, calling Robert Stannis's "beloved brother", Stannis considers it a falsehood, and orders the words removed.(wiki:Pylos.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords

When Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) is imprisoned in Dragonstone's dungeons, he takes ill, and Maester Pylos treats him with hot garlic broth, [milk of the poppy](wiki:Milk_of_the_poppy), and leechings.(wiki:Pylos.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Pylos also leeches the sick [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm), [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son of the late King Robert; these blood-filled leeches are used by [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) in her ritual to curse the false kings, Joffrey Baratheon, [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), and [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark).(wiki:Pylos.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Maester Pylos educates the [noble](wiki:Nobility) children on Dragonstone, Princess Shireen, Edric, and [Devan Seaworth](wiki:Devan_Seaworth). Because Shireen's companion, the [fool](wiki:Fool) [Patchface](wiki:Patchface), does not sit still and his bells are always making noise, Pylos forbids him from attending Shireen's lessons.(wiki:Pylos.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

After the lowborn Davos Seaworth is raised to the position of Stannis's [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), he seeks out Maester Pylos for guidance. Pylos reassures Davos that he is well suited for the position, and he offers to teach the former smuggler how to read and write.(wiki:Pylos.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) During this process, Pylos shows Davos a letter from Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), begging for assistance from the [five kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) against the [wildling](wiki:Wildling) forces of [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) and the oncoming threat of the [Others](wiki:Others). Pylos tells Davos that Stannis had not yet seen the letter, as his previous Hand, Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent), had deemed it not worth Stannis's attention or resources.(wiki:Pylos.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

Pylos is made aware of Davos's plan to remove Edric from Dragonstone to keep him safe from Melisandre's scheme to sacrifice him, and chooses to cooperate with it. While Pylos is teaching sums to the boy, Davos arrives at [Sea Dragon Tower](wiki:Sea_Dragon_Tower) with several [king's men](wiki:King's_men). Pylos encourages the boy to follow Davos's orders; the Onion Lord thinks Pylos could be executed if Stannis judges the plan as [treason](wiki:Treason), and appreciates the maester's courage. Edric is taken on *[Mad Prendos](wiki:Mad_Prendos)* to the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities).(wiki:Pylos.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Though Stannis and his army have left for the [Wall](wiki:Wall), Pylos remains at Dragonstone as part of its garrison, with Ser [Rolland Storm](wiki:Rolland_Storm) serving as castellan.(wiki:Pylos.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Lord [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne) leads the [siege of Dragonstone](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone), with the [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet) ferrying [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister)'s soldiers to the island.(wiki:Pylos.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) In response to the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) is given command of the siege by [Queen Regent](wiki:Regent) Cersei. According to [Aurane Waters](wiki:Aurane_Waters), Loras successfully assaults [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), though the young [knight](wiki:Knight) is gravely wounded while taking the castle. Aurane reports that maesters refuse to leech Loras because he has lost too much blood already.(wiki:Pylos.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
