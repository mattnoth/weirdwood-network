## Origins

Lord Leyton was unhorsed at a [tourney at Storm's End](wiki:Tourney_at_Storm's_End) by Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy).(wiki:Leyton_Hightower.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) offered his [dwarf](wiki:Dwarf) son, [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), to Leyton for marriage to one of his daughters, but Leyton, feeling insulted by the suggestion, rejected Tywin's offer.(wiki:Leyton_Hightower.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Leyton was present for the [tourney at Lannisport](wiki:Tourney_at_Lannisport) held to celebrate the victory of the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) over the [Greyjoy](wiki:House_Greyjoy) rebels during [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion) in [289 AC](wiki:289_AC). There, his youngest daughter, [Lynesse](wiki:Lynesse_Hightower), was named the [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty) at the tourney's end by Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont), who had become smitten with Lynesse. Jorah asked Leyton permission to marry her that same night, and Leyton accepted the offer.(wiki:Leyton_Hightower.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

By [300 AC](wiki:300_AC), Leyton has not descended from the [Hightower](wiki:Hightower) in more than a decade.(wiki:Leyton_Hightower.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Lord Leyton supports [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) at the beginning of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Leyton_Hightower.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Following the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), the [small council](wiki:Small_council) rewards the absent Leyton with lesser tracts of land taken from the defeated.(wiki:Leyton_Hightower.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Feast for Crows

In response to the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields) by [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), [Willas Tyrell](wiki:Willas_Tyrell) sends word from [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) so Leyton can prepare [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown)'s defenses.(wiki:Leyton_Hightower.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Despite the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) threat, Lord Leyton remains atop the [Hightower](wiki:Hightower) supposedly reading [magic](wiki:Magic) texts with his eldest daughter, [Malora](wiki:Malora_Hightower), while his sons take care of [House Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower)'s duties.(wiki:Leyton_Hightower.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### The Winds of Winter

Lord Leyton sends his sons moving down [Whispering Sound](wiki:Whispering_Sound) against the ironborn invasion.(wiki:Leyton_Hightower.cite_ref-Forsaken_1-1)
