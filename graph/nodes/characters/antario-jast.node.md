## Appearances & Description

Lord Jast owns black [steel](wiki:Steel) [armor](wiki:Armor) with three [golden](wiki:Gold) [lion](wiki:Lion) heads inlaid on the breastplate.(wiki:Antario_Jast.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Antario is among Ser [Stafford Lannister](wiki:Stafford_Lannister)'s army. He is injured and captured, along with his sons, during the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross). A rumor soon spreads claiming that he died during this battle.(wiki:Antario_Jast.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Storm of Swords

Antario is a captive at [Pinkmaiden Castle](wiki:Pinkmaiden_Castle).(wiki:Antario_Jast.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Freed from his captivity, Antario participates in the funeral procession of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). According to Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), Antario's wounds and imprisonment have left him a shadow of the man he had been.(wiki:Antario_Jast.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Though Jaime thinks his situation is not as severe as for his cousin, [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister), the new [Lord of Darry](wiki:Lord_of_Darry).(wiki:Antario_Jast.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
