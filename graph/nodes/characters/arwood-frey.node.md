## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

When news of the [raid at Saltpans](wiki:Sack_of_Saltpans) reaches the [Twins](wiki:Twins), Ser Arwood rides to the town with two of his half-cousins, Ser [Harys](wiki:Harys_Haigh) and Ser [Donnel Haigh](wiki:Donnel_Haigh), and a small force to investigate. They find [Quincy Cox](wiki:Quincy_Cox), the Knight of Saltpans, sitting behind his walls and the town in ashes.(wiki:Arwood_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

After the marriage of his cousin [Amerei Frey](wiki:Amerei_Frey) to [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister), the new Lord of Darry, Arwood stays at [Darry](wiki:Darry), together with several family members.(wiki:Arwood_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
