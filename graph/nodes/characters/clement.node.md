## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Brother [Narbert](wiki:Narbert) recounts the story of Brother Clement, who had gone to [Saltpans](wiki:Saltpans) to sell some [mead](wiki:Mead) when a force [attacked Saltpans](wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans). One of the attackers [had cut Clement's tongue out](wiki:Tongue_mutilation), saying he no longer needed it as he had taken a [vow of silence](wiki:Vow_of_silence). Clement died of the wounds he took at Saltpans. [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) and her party see [another brother](wiki:Gravedigger) digging his grave.(wiki:Clement.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
