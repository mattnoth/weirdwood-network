## Origins

Nymeria's mother comes from the [noblest blood](wiki:Old_Blood) of [Volantis](wiki:Volantis),(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10) the [Free City](wiki:Free_City) where Nym was born.(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Rawoiafvolantis.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Young Nym sometimes joined in play at the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens) with her sister, [Tyene Sand](wiki:Tyene_Sand), her cousin, Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell), [Garin](wiki:Garin_(orphan)), [Andrey Dalt](wiki:Andrey_Dalt), [Sylva Santagar](wiki:Sylva_Santagar), the [Fowler twins](wiki:Fowler_twins), and the [Archon's green-haired daughter](wiki:Archon's_green-haired_daughter).(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Arianne keeps in her boot a throwing knife gifted to her by Nymeria.(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Appearances & Description

Nymeria is twenty-five years old, slim and slender as a willow, with straight black hair worn in a long braid which pulls back from a widow's peak.(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) She has dark eyes(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) which are large and lustrous.(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Her full lips are [wine](wiki:Wine) red and curve in a silken smile,(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) and she has high cheekbones.(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah) describes Nym as having pale white skin in *[A Feast for Crows](wiki:A_Feast_for_Crows)*,(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) a fact confirmed by the [semi-canon](wiki:Canon) app *[A World of Ice and Fire](wiki:A_World_of_Ice_and_Fire)*,(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Rawoiafnymeria_sand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) but mentions her olive skin in *[A Dance with Dragons](wiki:A_Dance_with_Dragons)*.(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) Nymeria has all the beauty that her elder sister [Obara Sand](wiki:Obara_Sand) lacks but is no less deadly.(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) Although elegant, Nym is vengeful.(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

Nymeria dresses gracefully in shimmering lilac robes with a [silk](wiki:Silk) cape of cream and [copper](wiki:Copper),(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) and she also owns a yellow silk gown so sheer it reveals spun gold and [jewels](wiki:Jewelry) worn underneath.(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

> She wore a gown of yellow silk so sheer and fine that the candles shone right through it to reveal the spun gold and jewels beneath.

— Areo Hotah's observation of Lady Nym at Sunspear, ADWD The Watcher I (`sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-the-watcher-01.md:33`) [L858] Nymeria rides a gold [sand steed](wiki:Sand_steed) with a mane like white silk.(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8) Nym usually conceals a dozen daggers on her person(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6) and is proficient with the hidden blades.(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)

Nymeria is good friends with [Jeyne](wiki:Jeyne_Fowler) and [Jennelyn Fowler](wiki:Jennelyn_Fowler).(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Nymeria is abed with the [Fowler](wiki:House_Fowler) twins, [Jeyne](wiki:Jeyne_Fowler) and [Jennelyn](wiki:Jennelyn_Fowler), when she is told of the death of her father, Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell), in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-11) Ser [Daemon Sand](wiki:Daemon_Sand) sends her a [raven](wiki:Raven) explaining how Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) was poisoned by Oberyn and is now dying.(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-12)

Accompanied by a dozen mounted spearmen, Nymeria meets [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), the [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne) and Oberyn's elder brother, en route from the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens) to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear). She demands vengeance for the death of her father and rejects the offer Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) made Doran for Gregor's head. Instead of open war, Nymeria wants to assassinate Tywin, his children Queen Regent [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) and Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), and his grandchild, King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon). After visits from Nym's other sisters, Doran has the [Sand Snakes](wiki:Sand_Snakes) arrested and confined in the Spear Tower of [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear).(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-13)

### A Dance with Dragons

Nymeria and her sisters are released and are present at the feast at Sunspear when Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) presents the [Mountain's skull](wiki:Mountain's_skull) to Doran. Balon seems discomforted by her silk gown. When Balon tells the manner of Gregor's death, Nymeria celebrates his suffering. When [Ricasso](wiki:Ricasso) raises a toast to King Tommen, Nym declines with a flick of her wrist.(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7)

Doran sends Nymeria to King's Landing to take the vacant [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) seat on the [small council](wiki:Small_council).(wiki:Nymeria_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-8)
