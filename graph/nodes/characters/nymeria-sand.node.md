## Identity

Nymeria Sand, nicknamed Lady Nym, is a bastard daughter of Oberyn Martell and a noblewoman from Volantis. She is the second oldest of the Sand Snakes (wiki:Nymeria_Sand).

## Origins

Born 274–275 AC in Volantis. Her mother comes from the noblest blood of Volantis (track_b: Born, Father, Mother, wiki:Nymeria_Sand).

## Appearances & Description

Twenty-five years old, slim and slender as a willow, with straight black hair in a long braid pulled back from a widow's peak. Large, lustrous dark eyes, full wine-red lips curving in a silken smile, high cheekbones. Dresses gracefully in shimmering lilac robes with silk cape of cream and copper. Rides a gold sand steed with a mane like white silk. Conceals a dozen daggers on her person and is proficient with hidden blades. No less deadly than Obara despite her elegance; vengeful (wiki:Nymeria_Sand, affc-the-captain-of-guards).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Martell (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Dornishmen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

When her father Oberyn is killed by Gregor Clegane, Nym demands vengeance from Prince Doran. She advocates war against the Lannisters and proposes putting Myrcella on the throne. Doran confines her to a tower cell at Sunspear. Later, Doran reveals his secret plans and sends Nym to King's Landing to take Oberyn's seat on the small council, where she is to befriend the Tyrells and work for Dorne's interests (wiki:Nymeria_Sand).
