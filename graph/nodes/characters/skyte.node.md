## Appearances & Description

Skyte is a stocky man whose beard has a white streak.(wiki:Skyte.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Skyte and two of his companions, [Uller](wiki:Uller) and [Qarl](wiki:Qarl_the_Maid), encounter [Theon](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) and [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) in [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport). There they recount the story of the death of Skyte's brother, [Eldiss](wiki:Eldiss). Asha believes that Theon should make the companions part of his crew.(wiki:Skyte.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
