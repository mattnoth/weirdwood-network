Lester, known as Red Lester, is a guard sworn to House Lannister (wiki:Lester).

Lester guards the apartments of Lord Tywin Lannister in the Tower of the Hand the night Tyrion sneaks in and kills Tywin (wiki:Lester.cite_ref-Rasos77). He later mutters condolences to Cersei after Tywin's death (wiki:Lester.cite_ref-Raffc3).
