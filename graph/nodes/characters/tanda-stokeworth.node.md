## Appearances & Description

Tanda is an old woman(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) with a brittle voice.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Racok60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) She is not considered a particularly clever(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) or attractive woman.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Racok61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

[Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) finds Lady Stokeworth to be tedious and hysterical,(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and the queen dislikes Tanda's tearful embraces.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Tanda has rich foods served at her meals, such as [lamprey](wiki:Lamprey) pie, roast sucking pig, fatted calf, wild [boar](wiki:Boar), and cream stews, to impress her guests.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Ragot33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) tells Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) about his frequent meals with Lady Tanda and his distaste for her daughter, [Lollys](wiki:Lollys_Stokeworth).(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Ragot33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

After King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s death, Petyr tells Eddard that there is small love lost between Lord [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) and the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister), naming Tanda in this context as a possible supporter for a coup.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Lady Tanda is one of the remaining courtiers in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) after the beginning of hostilities with the [north](wiki:North). She attends the [tourney for King Joffrey I's thirteenth name day](wiki:Tourney_on_King_Joffrey's_name_day) with her two daughters, [Falyse](wiki:Falyse_Stokeworth) and Lollys, and she cheers Prince [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) on as he rides against a child-sized leather warrior stuffed with straw.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

Tanda starts hounding [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) the minute he arrives at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), trying to coax the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) during lavish meals to marry Lollys. Tyrion repeatedly tries to dodge her invitations.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) informs Tyrion about a small supper held by Lady Stokeworth and hands him the guest list for inspection.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

As supplies grow short in King's Landing during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), the lands of Tanda and Gyles provide half of the food still arriving as they lie close to the city and are still untouched by war.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-2)

Tanda and Lollys are part of the procession to see Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) off to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). She is saved by Ser [Horas Redwyne](wiki:Horas_Redwyne) during the [riots in King's Landing](wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing), but her daughter is not as lucky and left behind. Lady Tanda is half-crazed about her fate when those who escaped reach the Red Keep, and she begs the king's men to locate her daughter. It turns out that Lollys has survived, but became a victim of a gang rape behind a tanner's shop in which fifty men participated.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

Tyrion and Varys discuss options for bringing [Shae](wiki:Shae) to the Red Keep without Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) becoming aware of it. Varys arranges for one of Lollys's maids to be dismissed for stealing jewels, opening a position for Shae.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

Tanda attends the installation of Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) and Ser [Osmund Kettleblack](wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack) as new members of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) in the [royal sept](wiki:Royal_sept).(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

When Lord Rosby tries to smuggle Prince Tommen out of King's Landing on Cersei's orders, Tyrion has Gyles intercepted. Tanda worries she would not be allowed to return to [Stokeworth](wiki:Stokeworth), so she bribes Cersei with a pig, a rare sight in the starving city. As they dine on the pig, Tyrion tells Cersei he would rather have Tanda stay at the Red Keep and suggests that, if she wants to feel safe, she should bring her own garrison to King's Landing.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Racok54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Falyse brings a small number of soldiers from Stokeworth to King's Landing the day before the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

Lady Stokeworth stays in the Red Keep with her two daughters during the battle, and they are invited by Cersei to seek refuge with her in the [Queen's Ballroom](wiki:Queen's_Ballroom). [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) observes Falyse coaxing a reluctant Lollys onto the drawbridge leading to [Maegor's Holdfast](wiki:Maegor's_Holdfast). Tanda kindly rejects Sansa's offer of assistance, excusing Lollys's behavior by pointing out that she has not been feeling well; Sansa knows of the rumors that Lollys is pregnant.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1) After a meal at the ballroom, Tanda and her daughters, with others, ask for leave to go to the sept, which is granted by Cersei.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Racok60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

During the presentation of heroes and captives in the [throne room](wiki:Great_Hall_(Red_Keep)) after the battle, Sansa notices that Tanda and her daughters look pretty in their matching gowns of turquoise [silk](wiki:Silk) and vair.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Upon [Shae](wiki:Shae)'s advice, Tanda hires [Symon Silver Tongue](wiki:Symon_Silver_Tongue) to sing to [Lollys](wiki:Lollys_Stokeworth) to calm her when the baby starts kicking. When Shae asks [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) whether she could dress in her fine gowns and attend the upcoming wedding of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and Lady [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), Tyrion objects and says that Tanda might wonder where her daughter's bedmaid found so many jewels. Shae argues she would find a covered spot and Tanda would not see her among the thousand guests.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Rasos12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

Lady Tanda offers Lollys as a wife for Tyrion to Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), but Tywin wants Tyrion to marry [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) to assure Lannister influence in the [north](wiki:North). When Tyrion objects to marrying Sansa, Tywin mentions Tanda's offer. Tyrion replies that he would rather cut his penis off than marry Lollys.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

Shae informs Tyrion that Tanda slips Lollys [dreamwine](wiki:Dreamwine). Shae again raises the idea of attending the royal wedding, but Tyrion explains that Cersei would be more suspicious of the backstory given to Tanda and Lollys about Shae.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Rasos58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

Lady Stokeworth gives Joffrey a pair of supple riding boots as a wedding gift,(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) and she leaves the throne room while Joffrey chokes to death at his [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding).(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Rasos61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) Tyrion is accused of murder by Cersei.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

Visiting Tyrion in his imprisonment, Ser [Bronn of the Blackwater](wiki:Bronn) tells Tyrion that he is expected at [Castle Stokeworth](wiki:Castle_Stokeworth) for dinner, revealing that he has been offered to marry Lollys. Tyrion, suspecting a plot by Cersei to get Bronn out of the way, points out that Lollys is pregnant from another man and that Falyse is Tanda's heir. Bronn is not bothered by the baby, however, and makes the ominous suggestion that Falyse might die before her mother.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Tanda remains at [Stokeworth](wiki:Stokeworth) with her younger daughter when [Lollys](wiki:Lollys_Stokeworth) becomes too pregnant to travel, so [Falyse](wiki:Falyse_Stokeworth) attends the wake of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) instead. Tanda would like the child to be named after Tywin, but an offended Cersei refuses.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

The Stokeworths do not attend [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Tommen_I_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) of King [Tommen I](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) and Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), for which Cersei is grateful.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

Tanda sends a [raven](wiki:Raven) to Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) informing Lollys has delivered a strong, healthy son named [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Tanner). Lady Tanda insists that the choice was not hers but rather that of Ser [Bronn](wiki:Bronn), Lollys's new husband.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2)

Tanda's saddle girth bursts while riding, and she shatters her hip after being thrown from her horse. A stable boy is chastised, as he should have seen that the strap was worn. Maester [Frenken](wiki:Frenken) does what he can, but Cersei is skeptical of Tanda recovering. The queen assures Falyse that she will pray for Tanda, however, telling her that Tanda is the sister she never had. Cersei manipulates Falyse and her husband, Ser [Balman Byrch](wiki:Balman_Byrch), to plot against Bronn.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

Balman attempts to kill Bronn at Stokeworth, but is slain in their duel. Calling himself Lord Stokeworth, Bronn expels Falyse from the castle and gives her clothes to Lollys. To keep her plotting against Bronn a secret, Cersei has Falyse given to [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn) for his experiments.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

Cersei receives word from Stokeworth has Tanda died from a chill in her chest brought on by her injury.(wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
