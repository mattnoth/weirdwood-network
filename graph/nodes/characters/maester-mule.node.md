## Origins

Duncan's old stot, [Chestnut](wiki:Chestnut), died in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), during the desert crossing between the [Prince's Pass](wiki:Prince's_Pass) and [Vaith](wiki:Vaith). As Chestnut had been Egg's steed, he and Dunk had to ride double on [Thunder](wiki:Thunder), as they had no other horses at the time. Eventually, Egg's brother, Prince [Aemon Targaryen](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)),(wiki:Maester_(mule).cite_ref-Aemon_2-1) a student at the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), gave them a mule, named Maester.(wiki:Maester_(mule).cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Egg rode Maester when they traveled, and the mule also carried their equipment and purchases, such as [wine](wiki:Wine) casks.(wiki:Maester_(mule).cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
In [211 AC](wiki:211_AC), after Ser Duncan did a service for Lady [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber), she intended to pay him back with a gift of a horse.
