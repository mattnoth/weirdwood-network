## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Lord [Anders Yronwood](wiki:Anders_Yronwood) sends Ser [Cletus Yronwood](wiki:Cletus_Yronwood) and three of his best young [knights](wiki:Knight), including Willam, to accompany Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) on a dangerous journey.(wiki:Willam_Wells.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Quentyn travels to [Essos](wiki:Essos) on a mission to marry [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), [Queen of Meereen](wiki:Queen_of_Meereen). While traveling to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) disguised as merchants, their ship, the *[Meadowlark](wiki:Meadowlark)*, is attacked by [corsairs](wiki:Corsairs) off the coast of the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands) and Willam and Cletus are among those killed.(wiki:Willam_Wells.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Having been rejected by Daenerys, Quentyn mentions Willam and Cletus while trying to convince Ser [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater) and Ser [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood) to aid him in claiming one of Daenerys's [dragons](wiki:Dragon) after the queen has disappeared from Meereen. Gerris responds that doing so will not bring their fallen friends back.(wiki:Willam_Wells.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
