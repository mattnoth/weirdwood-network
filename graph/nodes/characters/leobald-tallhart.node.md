## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Leobald comes to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) to take part in the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast). There he relates the story of his nephew, [Benfred Tallhart](wiki:Benfred_Tallhart), and his [Wild Hares](wiki:Wild_Hares) and offers his suggestions on the issue of the [Hornwood](wiki:House_Hornwood) problem.(wiki:Leobald_Tallhart.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) He sends Benfred to deal with the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) problem along the [Stony Shore](wiki:Battle_of_the_Stony_Shore).

When [Dagmer Cleftjaw](wiki:Dagmer) besieges Torrhen's Square, Leobald is within the castle. After Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) lifts the siege, Leobald adds his forces to his in preparation for the eventual re-capture of Winterfell.(wiki:Leobald_Tallhart.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He is killed by [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) men when they [surprise](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell) the northern force.(wiki:Leobald_Tallhart.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
