## Identity

Lyonce — Archmaester Lyonce, chosen after Ollidar, had a ring, rod, and mask of yellow gold.

## Origins

Lyonce was appointed [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) during the reign of King [Aegon I](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) following the death of Grand Maester [Ollidar](wiki:Ollidar), the first Grand Maester of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).
