## Origins

Aegon was the oldest of the children of [Daemon Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) and [Rohanne](wiki:Rohanne_of_Tyrosh) of [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh).(wiki:Aegon_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf412.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) He had a younger twin brother, [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Blackfyre),(wiki:Aegon_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) and five other younger brothers: [Daemon](wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre), [Haegon](wiki:Haegon_I_Blackfyre), [Aenys](wiki:Aenys_Blackfyre), and two whose names are currently unknown. He also had at least two sisters.(wiki:Aegon_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

The twins often bullied their younger brother Daemon, and Daemon's friend, [Alyn Cockshaw](wiki:Alyn_Cockshaw). Daemon once had a [prophetic dream](wiki:Dragon_dreams) about the deaths of Aegon and Aemon, but the disbelieving twins only laughed at their brother.(wiki:Aegon_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

During the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), when Aegon was twelve years old, he served as a [squire](wiki:Squire) to his father.(wiki:Aegon_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rssm1468.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Aegon was killed at the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field) by the arrows of [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers) and his [Raven's Teeth](wiki:Raven's_Teeth). Daemon remained by his fallen son's side, and was also struck down by arrows. Aegon's twin, Aemon, took up the sword [Blackfyre](wiki:Blackfyre) from his dying father, but was then slain by the archers as well.(wiki:Aegon_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)(wiki:Aegon_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf412.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
