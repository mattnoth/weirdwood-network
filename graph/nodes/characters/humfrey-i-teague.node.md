## Origins

Humfrey was a [pious](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) ruler who founded many [septs](wiki:Sept) and [motherhouses](wiki:Motherhouse) and also attempted to suppress worship of the [Old Gods](wiki:Old_Gods) with the aid of the [Faith Militant](wiki:Faith_Militant). This caused [House Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood) to rebel, supported by [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully) and [House Vance of Atranta](wiki:House_Vance_of_Atranta). Though Humfrey and the Faith were nearly victorious, House Teague was ultimately defeated when [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon) came to aid the Blackwoods, as Lord [Roderick Blackwood](wiki:Roderick_Blackwood) had marriage ties to them. King Humfrey perished in the campaign's [final battle](wiki:Battle_of_Six_Kings), in which [his](wiki:Humfrey_II_Teague) [three](wiki:Hollis_Teague) [sons](wiki:Tyler_Teague) and [brother](wiki:Damon_Teague) all perished, thus extinguishing House Teague.(wiki:Humfrey_I_Teague.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
