## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

After his father followed first [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) and later [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) in the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings),(wiki:Alekyne_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) the Florent heir Alekyne remained safe at the family's seat of [Brightwater Keep](wiki:Brightwater_Keep) in the wake of the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Alekyne_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Lord Alester Florent and the rest of [House Florent](wiki:House_Florent) are [attainted](wiki:Attainder) by the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) for pledging allegiance to Baratheon claimants in the war, and their castle, [Brightwater Keep](wiki:Brightwater_Keep), is granted to Ser [Garlan Tyrell](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell).(wiki:Alekyne_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Alekyne becomes the new Lord of Brightwater after the death of his father, Alester.(wiki:Alekyne_Florent.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Because Garlan is preparing an army to claim Brightwater,(wiki:Alekyne_Florent.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Alekyne flees to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) to seek refuge at the [Hightower](wiki:Hightower), where his sister [Rhea](wiki:Rhea_Florent) is married to Lord [Leyton Hightower](wiki:Leyton_Hightower), Garlan's maternal grandfather.(wiki:Alekyne_Florent.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
