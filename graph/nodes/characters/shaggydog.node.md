## Identity

Shaggydog is a direwolf bonded to Rickon Stark (track_b: Owner). He is the litter-mate of Grey Wind, Summer, Lady, Nymeria, and Ghost. Born in 298 AC in the north (track_b: Born). Rickon shortens his name to "Shaggy" (wiki:Shaggydog).

## Origins

Shaggydog was found in the snow with his litter-mates near their dead mother by Robb Stark and Jon Snow north of Winterfell. He was chosen by Rickon Stark (wiki:Shaggydog).

## Allegiances

`OWNS`: Rickon Stark owns Shaggydog (track_b: Owner). Shaggydog mirrors Rickon's emotional state — becoming wild and aggressive as Rickon grows fearful and angry.

## Appearances & Description

Shaggydog is black-furred with blazing green eyes. He is the most aggressive and unruly of the litter, reflecting Rickon's own wildness. Jojen Reed describes him as "full of fear and rage" (wiki:Shaggydog).

## Narrative Arc

**AGOT:** After Bran's fall, Shaggydog joins Summer and Grey Wind in howling beneath Bran's window. When Rickon learns Robb may go to war, Shaggydog grows aggressive, biting Gage and Mikken. Farlen chains him. Before news of Eddard's death arrives, Shaggydog attacks Maester Luwin in the Winterfell crypts but is stopped by Summer (wiki:Shaggydog, agot chapters 1–66).

**ACOK:** Shaggydog and Summer are confined to the godswood after Shaggydog bites Little Walder Frey. Bran tries to call Shaggydog through his warg bond but the wolf remains distant. After the sack of Winterfell, Shaggydog accompanies Osha and Rickon when they separate from Bran's group (wiki:Shaggydog, acok chapters 4–69).

**ASOS–ADWD:** Through his warg bond with Summer, Bran senses Shaggydog growing more distant every day. In Jon's wolf dream, Ghost senses his black brother fighting and devouring a one-horned goat. Wex Pyke claims Shaggydog is on Skagos with Rickon and Osha (wiki:Shaggydog, asos-9, adwd-3/29).

## Quotes
