## Origins

Lord Manfryd received a raven from Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) ordering him to execute [Nettles](wiki:Nettles). As she was his [guest](wiki:Guest_right), Lord Manfryd was dubious of what to do. He assembled his [Maester](wiki:Maester), [Norren](wiki:Norren_(House_Mooton)), his brother, his [captain of the guard](wiki:Captain_of_the_guard) and his champion, Ser Florian, and listened to their advices. Ser Florian was shocked the queen would demand him to do such unhonorable act.(wiki:Florian_Greysteel.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Florian_Greysteel.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
