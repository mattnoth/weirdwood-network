## Origins

Ursula Upcliff joined the alliance of [First Men](wiki:First_Men) houses, petty kings, and clans summoned by [Robar II Royce](wiki:Robar_II_Royce), [High King of the Vale, the Fingers, and the Mountains of the Moon](wiki:High_King_of_the_Vale,_the_Fingers,_and_the_Mountains_of_the_Moon), to fight against the [Andals](wiki:Andals). According to tradition, Robar won her alliance due to his "honeyed tongue."(wiki:Ursula_Upcliff.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Ursula rode a bloodred horse in the [Battle of the Seven Stars](wiki:Battle_of_the_Seven_Stars), during which she tried to curse [Torgold Tollett](wiki:Torgold_Tollett). However, according to singers, Torgold leapt upon Ursula's horse, grasped her face, and tore her head from her shoulders as she screamed for succor.(wiki:Ursula_Upcliff.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
