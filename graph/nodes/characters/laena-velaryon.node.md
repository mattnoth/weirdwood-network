## Appearances & Description

Laena had a great mane of silver-gold ringlets that fell down past her waist.(wiki:Laena_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
By the age of twelve, Laena had freshly [flowered](wiki:Flowered), and was considered a fiery young girl. It was said she had inherited the beauty of her mother, [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aemon)), and the bold, adventurous spirit from her father, [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon). By two-and-twenty, Laena had grown into a tall, slender, and beautiful woman. According to the [fool](wiki:Fool) [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom), she was "almost as pretty as her brother", [Laenor Velaryon](wiki:Laenor_Velaryon).(wiki:Laena_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
Laena loved to fly, and when she was young, showed "far more interest in flying than in boys", according to the [maester](wiki:Maester) of [High Tide](wiki:High_Tide).(wiki:Laena_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

## Origins

### Early life[ ]

Laena was born in late [92 AC](wiki:92_AC).(wiki:Laena_Velaryon.cite_ref-birth_2-1) She was the daughter of Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) and Princess [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aemon)), and the great-granddaughter of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Laena_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) Princess Rhaenys's father, Prince [Aemon Targaryen](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)), the eldest son and heir of King Jaehaerys I, died shortly after Rhaenys learned she was pregnant. Although Rhaenys was Aemon's heir, Jaehaerys I named his second son, Prince [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)), as his new heir, passing over Rhaenys and her potential unborn son. The unborn Laena, also referred to as "the boy in the belly", was subject of much debate on the succession until her birth.(wiki:Laena_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)
In [101 AC](wiki:101_AC), Laena's claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) was put forward once more during the [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council_of_101_AC), but she was passed over, together with her mother, on account of her sex.(wiki:Laena_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_viserys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Laena_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)
Before the age of twelve, Laena had become a [dragonrider](wiki:Dragonrider) by claiming the largest [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) [dragon](wiki:Dragon) left alive, [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar). Following the death of Queen [Aemma Arryn](wiki:Aemma_Arryn) in late [105 AC](wiki:105_AC), [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Runciter](wiki:Runciter) urged King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) to remarry, and suggested the just-turned-twelve and newly flowered Laena as a bride, so that Viserys could heal the rift between [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) and [House Velaryon](wiki:House_Velaryon). However, Viserys chose [another noblewoman](wiki:Alicent_Hightower), and though this angered Laena's father, Laena herself was untroubled. According to a letter the maester of [High Tide](wiki:High_Tide) wrote to the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel), Laena was interested more in flying than boys. When King Viserys I married [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower) in [106 AC](wiki:106_AC), [House Velaryon](wiki:House_Velaryon) was noticeably absent from the wedding.(wiki:Laena_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)

### Marriage and Death[ ]

At the age of twelve, Laena was [betrothed](wiki:Betrothal) to a son of the [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos). When the Sealord died, Laena's betrothed squandered his father's money and power and came to [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark). There, for nearly ten years, Laena's father repeatedly found ways to postpone their wedding, unwilling to let it go through, but unable to gracefully break the betrothal. When Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) arrived on Driftmark in [115 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), the singers claim he fell in love with Laena, although others believe he saw her as a means to further his ambition. He insulted Laena's betrothed until the boy challenged him to a duel, in which he was killed by Daemon with [Dark Sister](wiki:Dark_Sister). Laena wed Daemon a fortnight later.(wiki:Laena_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-11)
Aware that the king and court would not be pleased with their marriage, Laena and Daemon flew on their [dragons](wiki:Dragon) to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos), where they were hosted by the [Prince of the city](wiki:Prince_of_Pentos). From Pentos, Daemon and Laena traveled to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), up the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne) to [Qohor](wiki:Qohor) and [Norvos](wiki:Norvos). They eventually returned to Pentos, where Laena discovered she was pregnant. Laena and Daemon remained in Pentos for the remainder of her pregnancy. In [116 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), in Pentos, Laena gave birth to their twin daughters, [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daemon)) and [Baela](wiki:Baela_Targaryen). When the children were half a year old, Laena sailed with them to Driftmark, while Daemon flew their dragons home.(wiki:Laena_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-12)
Laena became good friends with her good-sister, [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen): it is said the princess was "fond and more than fond" of her uncle's wife. Laena and Daemon would often visit Rhaenyra on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), and Rhaenyra would often visit them on Driftmark. In [118 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Laena's two-year-old twins were betrothed to Rhaenyra's two eldest sons, with the blessing of King [Viserys I](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Laena_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-13)
In [119 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Laena became pregnant again. During the first few days of [120 AC](wiki:120_AC), Laena went into labor. Following a day and night of labor, she gave birth to a twisted and malformed son, who died within an hour of birth. With all her strength gone from her labor and weakened further from grief over her lost child, childbed fever set in. Neither Driftmark's maester nor Maester [Gerardys](wiki:Gerardys) from Dragonstone could heal her. After three days of sickness, Laena died. It is said that she attempted to reach Vhagar to fly one more time before she died, but collapsed on the tower steps, where she died. Daemon carried her back to bed, where he sat vigil over Laena's body together with Rhaenyra.(wiki:Laena_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-14)
