## Origins

As the second son to King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen)'s heir, Matarys was in line for the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). While his brother [Valarr](wiki:Valarr_Targaryen) was nicknamed the Young Prince, Matarys was dubbed the Even Younger Prince by Lord [Gawen Swann](wiki:Gawen_Swann)'s [fool](wiki:Fool).(wiki:Matarys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) However, after his father Baelor had died in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC) during the [Ashford Tourney](wiki:Ashford_Tourney), the [Great Spring Sickness](wiki:Great_Spring_Sickness) struck [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). Matarys died from the [illness](wiki:Illness), as did his older brother [Valarr](wiki:Valarr_Targaryen) and grandfather, King Daeron II, in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Matarys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) This ended the line of Prince Baelor, and led to the ascent of his brother as King [Aerys I Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Matarys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_targaryen_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Matarys was likely a teenager at the time of his death.(wiki:Matarys_Targaryen.cite_ref-6) It is unknown whether Matarys passed away before or after his older brother Valarr.

Matarys was mourned after his death. [Septon](wiki:Septon) [Sefton](wiki:Sefton_Staunton), who was in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) during the Great Spring Sickness, recalled Matarys's among the thousands of deaths during that time.(wiki:Matarys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) Later, a [Hunchbacked septon](wiki:Hunchbacked_septon) preaching against the new [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Lord [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers), alluded to Matarys's death. Afer alluding to Bloodraven's history of [kinslaying](wiki:Kinslayer) during the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), the septon implied that both Matarys's death and the other recent deaths his near kin were Bloodraven's work too.(wiki:Matarys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

While explaining the lineage of Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) to [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) says that Prince Baelor's two sons soon followed their father to the grave in the Great Spring Sickness.(wiki:Matarys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Discussing both their fathers' objections to them becoming [maesters](wiki:Maester), Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)),(wiki:Matarys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Matarys's cousin,(wiki:Matarys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_targaryen_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) tells [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) that his grandfather sent him to the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) because too many dragons were as dangerous as too few. He mentions that King Daeron had four sons, three of whom had sons of their own.(wiki:Matarys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) As the second son of King Daeron's eldest child, Prince Baelor, Matarys was one of the sons of the king's sons(wiki:Matarys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_targaryen_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) alluded to by Aemon.(wiki:Matarys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2)
