## Origins

Dyanna died before [209 AC](wiki:209_AC),(wiki:Dyanna_Dayne.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) well before Maekar's ascendance to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) in [221 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), thus she never became queen consort.(wiki:Dyanna_Dayne.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maekar_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

On his way to the [Ashford tourney](wiki:Ashford_tourney) in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC), Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) mistook young Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) for a stableboy. Aegon asked him to take him with [Ashford](wiki:Ashford) and, presuming the innkeep was his mother, asked him what his mother would say to the request. When Aegon stated that she wouldn't say anything because his mother was dead, Dunk assumed the boy was an orphan.(wiki:Dyanna_Dayne.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
