## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) comes to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) to offer the position of [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), a [welcoming feast](wiki:Feast_in_honor_of_King_Robert's_visit_to_Winterfell) is held in the castle's [Great Hall](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell). A singer plays the [high harp](wiki:Harp) and recites a ballad, but [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), who is seated on a bench away from the royal platform, has difficulty hearing the music because of the many guests eating and conversing.(wiki:Orland_of_Oldtown.cite_ref-Ragot5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Storm of Swords

[Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) tells Jon that Orland sang of dead kings beneath the sea during the feast at Winterfell.(wiki:Orland_of_Oldtown.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Orland is chosen to participate in the singers tourney at the [royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and Lady [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell);(wiki:Orland_of_Oldtown.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) the boy king is [poisoned](wiki:Poison) during the wedding feast.(wiki:Orland_of_Oldtown.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
