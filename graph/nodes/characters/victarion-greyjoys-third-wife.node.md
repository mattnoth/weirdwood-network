## Identity

Victarion Greyjoy's third wife is an unnamed woman who served as a salt wife to Victarion Greyjoy. Her given name has not been revealed. (wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy's_third_wife)

## Allegiances

She was `SWORN_TO` House Greyjoy through marriage. (track_b: Allegiance)

## Narrative Arc

After Victarion's first wife died in childbirth and his second wife died of pox, he took this unnamed woman as a salt wife. In 297 AC, she was seduced or raped by Victarion's elder brother Euron Greyjoy, becoming pregnant with Euron's child. Victarion beat her to death with his fists, sobbing as he struck her, then carried her body to the rocks for the crabs. (wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy's_third_wife.cite_ref-Raffc18)

Balon Greyjoy commanded his brothers never to speak of the incident and sent Euron into exile, forbidding kinslaying in his hall. Victarion has not touched a woman since. (wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy's_third_wife.cite_ref-Raffc18)

During the kingsmoot, Asha Greyjoy discusses her late aunt with Victarion. Later, when Hotho Harlaw offers his daughter, Victarion can only picture the wife he killed. Victarion secretly plans to wed Daenerys Targaryen partly out of vengeance for Euron's despoiling of his wife. (wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy's_third_wife.cite_ref-Raffc29)
