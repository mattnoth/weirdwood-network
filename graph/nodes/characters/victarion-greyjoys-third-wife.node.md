## Origins

After his first wife died in childbirth and his second wife died from the pox, [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) took an unnamed third woman as a [salt wife](wiki:Salt_wife). In [297 AC](wiki:297_AC),(wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy's_third_wife.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Victarion's new wife was seduced or raped by Victarion's elder brother, [Euron](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), becoming pregnant with his child. According to a semi-canon source, their relationship was an affair.(wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy's_third_wife.cite_ref-Rawoiafeuron_greyjoy.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Victarion beat his wife to death with his fists, sobbing himself(wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy's_third_wife.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) each time he struck the sobbing woman.(wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy's_third_wife.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Victarion then carried her body down to the rocks to give it to the [crabs](wiki:Crab).(wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy's_third_wife.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

[Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), commanded his younger brothers to never speak of the incident. Victarion would have killed Euron, but Balon would have no [kinslaying](wiki:Kinslaying) in his hall. Lord Balon sent Euron into exile, never to return while Balon lived. The day that Balon sent Euron off to sea, he reminded Victarion that a kinslayer is accused in the eyes of gods and men. Balon has refused to speak of her and Victarion has not touched a woman since.(wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy's_third_wife.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Hotho Harlaw](wiki:Hotho_Harlaw) offers [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy), a claimant to the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair), his twelve-year-old daughter for a queen, describing the girl to Victarion. When Victarion tries to picture her, however, he sees only the wife he had killed.(wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy's_third_wife.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Prior to the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) discusses her late aunt, whom she had never met, with her uncle, Victarion.(wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy's_third_wife.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), now [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North), tasks Victarion with retrieving [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) from [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay) and bringing her to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) to wed Euron. Victarion agrees to go, although he secretly plans to wed Daenerys himself out of vengeance for Euron having despoiled his third wife.(wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy's_third_wife.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
