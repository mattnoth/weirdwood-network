## Identity

Ser Garon Baratheon was a knight from House Baratheon and a younger brother of Lord Rogar Baratheon of Storm's End (wiki:Garon_Baratheon).

## Narrative Arc

According to his brother Rogar, Garon was "an able and leal man" though never a match for their brother Borys. After Rogar's plots to depose King Jaehaerys I failed, Rogar wrote a will absolving Garon and his brothers of involvement. When Rogar was summoned to King's Landing in 50 AC, Garon and Ronnal urged him to defy the summons. In 51 AC Garon accompanied his brothers to King's Landing for the second wedding of Jaehaerys and Alysanne. After Lady Alyssa died in childbirth, Garon was sent by Rogar to invite the royal couple to remain at Storm's End for a feast. In 55 AC he participated in the melee at the tourney celebrating the Dragonpit's completion. In 61 AC, Garon was made regent for Rogar's young heir Boremund. He died in 73 AC (wiki:Garon_Baratheon).
