## Origins

After both plots of Garon's eldest brother, Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon), to set aside King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and crown another in his place, Rogar wrote a will and confession absolving Garon and his brothers Ser [Borys](wiki:Borys_Baratheon) and Ser [Ronnal](wiki:Ronnal_Baratheon) of having played any role in his plots.(wiki:Garon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) When Rogar was summoned to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) by King Jaehaerys in [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), Garon and his brother Ser Ronnal urged Rogar to defy the summons.(wiki:Garon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

In [51 AC](wiki:51_AC), Garon accompanied his brothers Lord Rogar and Ser Ronnal and Rogar's wife, the Dowager Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon), to King's Landing for the second [wedding](wiki:Wedding) of King Jaehaerys to Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen).(wiki:Garon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

After Lady Alyssa died in childbirth, Garon was sent by Lord Rogar to invite King Jaehaerys and Queen Alysanne to remain at Storm's End for a feast in honor of the birth of Rogar's daughter, [Jocelyn](wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon).(wiki:Garon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

In [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Garon participated in the [melee](wiki:Melee) of the [tourney](wiki:Tourney_for_the_completion_of_the_Dragonpit) held at King's Landing to celebrate the completion of the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit).(wiki:Garon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Garon became a widower when his wife succumbed of the [Shivers](wiki:Shivers).(wiki:Garon_Baratheon.cite_ref-wife_4-1)

In [61 AC](wiki:61_AC), Garon was made [regent](wiki:Regent) for Rogar's heir, [Boremund](wiki:Boremund_Baratheon), as Rogar left to fight their brother Ser Borys and the [Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Jaehaerys_I)) in the [Third Dornish War](wiki:Third_Dornish_War). Garon called Rogar's plan "a fool's errand". Garon was present at his brother's side when Rogar died half a year after returning from battle.(wiki:Garon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

In [73 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Garon and his nephew Lord Boremund were given charge of the third wife of Ser [Lucamore Strong](wiki:Lucamore_Strong) and their children.(wiki:Garon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
