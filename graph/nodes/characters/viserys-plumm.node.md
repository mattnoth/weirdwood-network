## Origins

Viserys's father, Lord [Ossifer Plumm](wiki:Ossifer_Plumm), was a wealthy but aged man when he wed Princess [Elaena Targaryen](wiki:Elaena_Targaryen) in [176 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). He is said to have died during the [bedding](wiki:Bedding), after seeing his wife naked for the first time.(wiki:Viserys_Plumm.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i_.28the_sisters_of_baelor_i.29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) Nevertheless, Elaena did conceive, and Viserys was eventually born. However, rumors later suggested that Lord Ossifer had died before he was able to consummate the marriage, and that Viserys instead had been fathered by King [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen).(wiki:Viserys_Plumm.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i_.28the_sisters_of_baelor_i.29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)(wiki:Viserys_Plumm.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The claim that Ossifer fathered a child after his death led to the saying that he had had "a cock six feet long".(wiki:Viserys_Plumm.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Viserys_Plumm.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Ser [Maynard Plumm](wiki:Maynard_Plumm) claimed to be distantly related to Lord Viserys, though doubted the lord would admit to it.(wiki:Viserys_Plumm.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

When Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) tells Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) that there is no worry that her son King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) could consummate his marriage with [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), as Tommen is "much too young", Cersei replies that [Ossifer Plumm](wiki:Ossifer_Plumm) "was much too dead", but that didn't stop him from fathering a child. She intends to explain that any child of Margaery's could be recognized as being fathered by Tommen, even if it was physically impossible for him to impregnate her.(wiki:Viserys_Plumm.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) encounters [Brown Ben Plumm](wiki:Ben_Plumm), commander of the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons), and speculates that he is descended from a younger son of Viserys Plumm. He guesses that [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s [dragons](wiki:Dragon) were fond of Ben,(wiki:Viserys_Plumm.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) which amuses Ben as it is true,(wiki:Viserys_Plumm.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) and that he knows why. When Ben says his mother said his father had "a drop of [dragon blood](wiki:House_Targaryen)", Tyrion replies, "Two drops. That, or a cock six foot long",(wiki:Viserys_Plumm.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) suggesting that Tyrion believes the rumors about Ossifer's posthumous child being fathered by [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen).
