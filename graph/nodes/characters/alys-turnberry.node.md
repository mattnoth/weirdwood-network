## Origins

Alys was a [maiden](wiki:Maiden) of an age with Princess [Saera Targaryen](wiki:Saera_Targaryen), attending the royal court at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). In [82 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Alys became a favorite and dearest friend of the princess, along with [Perianne Moore](wiki:Perianne_Moore). Saera nicknamed Alys *"Sweetberry"*.(wiki:Alys_Turnberry.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

Saera and her five favorites — Alys, Perianne, [Jonah Mooton](wiki:Jonah_Mooton), Lord [Roy Connington](wiki:Roy_Connington), and Ser [Braxton Beesbury](wiki:Braxton_Beesbury), called Stinger — became inseparable at every feast and ball. They hunted and [hawked](wiki:Hawk) together, and once sailed across [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay) to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) together. When the young lords [rode at rings](wiki:Ride_at_rings) or crossed swords in the yards, the three girls cheered them on. King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) was pleased at his daughter's popularity and friendship, but Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) was concerned about the character of Alys, Perianne, and the three boys. Nevertheless, King Jaehaerys felt they could not get up to trouble, with all the servants attending them watching.(wiki:Alys_Turnberry.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

In [84 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), an incident occurred between Saera's male favorites and [Tom Turnip](wiki:Tom_Turnip), King Jaehaerys's [fool](wiki:Fool), at the [Blue Pearl](wiki:Blue_Pearl), a [brothel](wiki:Brothel) in King's Landing, something Jonah claimed had been the princess's idea. Alys and Perianne were woken in the middle of the night and questioned by Queen Alysanne, telling them to tell the truth or they would join their male friends in the [dungeons](wiki:Red_Keep). Before long, the girls were weeping and pleading for forgiveness, although their confessions said almost nothing about the incident they were being questioned about. Perianne explained that Saera had been teaching Alys how to kiss, and she asked to be taught to kiss too, to train for their future marriages. Alys revealed that one night the girls had started kissing each other with their clothes off, taking turns pretending they were boys. Then, Saera dared Alys to kiss a real boy, and she dared Perianne, and both dared Saera, who said she would kiss a man grown, a [knight](wiki:Knight). This began the ladies' affairs with Red Roy, Jonah, and Stinger. Perianne explained that Stinger did the "training" for all of them.(wiki:Alys_Turnberry.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

Queen Alysanne then demanded to know where their [septas](wiki:Septa) and servants had been during all of this. Perianne, confused, explained that servants do what you tell them, and they had sent them out of the room. As for the ones who knew, Stinger had threatened to [have their tongues out](wiki:Tongue_mutilation) if they talked. This caused Alys to break down, sobbing and tearing at her dressing gown. She apologized to the queen, and claimed that Saera and Stinger had pressured her into sexual behavior. She revealed she was with child, and that she did not know who the father was. Alysanne sent both girls to bed, and told them that the next morning they would make a confession to a septa and be forgiven by [the Mother](wiki:Mother_(the_Seven)). Alys knew that her own mother would not forgive her, however.(wiki:Alys_Turnberry.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)

Hasty [marriages](wiki:Marriage) were arranged for the two girls. However, Roy Connington refused to marry Alys, declaring that she was pregnant with Stinger's child, whom he would not make heir to [Griffin's Roost](wiki:Griffin's_Roost) and raise as his own. Alys was sent to the [Motherhouse of Maris](wiki:Motherhouse_of_Maris) in [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown), where she gave birth to a girl with bright red hair like Roy's. Afterwards, she was wed to Lord [Dunstan Pryor](wiki:Dunstan_Pryor) of [Pebble](wiki:Pebble).(wiki:Alys_Turnberry.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)

## Appearances & Description

Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) considered Alys to be a pretty, vapid, empty-headed little fool, from what she had seen of her. Grand Maester [Elysar](wiki:Elysar) called her a trull.(wiki:Alys_Turnberry.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
