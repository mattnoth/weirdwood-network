## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser Humfrey accompanies [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) in his [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell) in the [north](wiki:North). When they become stranded at the [crofter's village](wiki:Crofter's_village), Humfrey insists they press on, as they will die from cold and starvation if they stay. When [Ned Woods](wiki:Ned_Woods) concludes that the lake has been emptied by the fishermen, Humfrey insists that the lack of food is all the more reason to march, stating "*If death is our fate, let us die with swords in hand.*"(wiki:Humfrey_Clifton.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
