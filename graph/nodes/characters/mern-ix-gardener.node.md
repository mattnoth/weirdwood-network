## Origins

After [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) landed in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), set on [conquering](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest) the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms). King Mern IX of [the Reach](wiki:Reach) and King [Loren I Lannister](wiki:Loren_I_Lannister) of [the Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) agreed to join forces and throw the invaders back into the sea. The two armies gathered near [Goldengrove](wiki:Goldengrove) in the Reach. Since he had brought half as many more men, Mern had the honor of commanding the center of the combined army from the Reach and the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands). [Edmund](wiki:Edmund_Gardener), Mern's son and heir, was to lead the vanguard, while another son, [Gawen](wiki:Gawen_Gardener), accompanied the king with the banner.(wiki:Mern_IX_Gardener.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
Mern led the charge against Aegon's army alongside his kin. The combined might of the Reach and the Rock began to break the Targaryen host, but Aegon and his sisters, [Visenya](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) and [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen), took to the sky with their [dragons](wiki:Dragon). The beasts killed 4,000 men of the combined army at the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire), among them Mern and his sons, grandsons, brothers, uncles, cousins, and other kin.(wiki:Mern_IX_Gardener.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Mern_IX_Gardener.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_landing_and_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Another thousand men perished from sword and spears and arrows. One nephew of King Mern survived, but he died of his burns three days later, ending House Gardener.(wiki:Mern_IX_Gardener.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
