## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Lady Alyce comes to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in the entourage of [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell). She is pregnant.(wiki:Alyce_Graceford.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

At a luncheon in the [Maidenvault](wiki:Maidenvault) where [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) is invited, the [fool](wiki:Fool) [Butterbumps](wiki:Butterbumps) performs a trick in which he hatches an egg into a dozen baby chicks. One of the chicks runs through Lady Alyce's broth.(wiki:Alyce_Graceford.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

### A Feast for Crows

Lady Alyce attends the funeral for Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister). There, she asks Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) for leave to name her child Tywin if it is a boy or Lanna if it is a girl. Though Cersei is tired of people asking to name children after her father, she feigns delight and consents graciously.(wiki:Alyce_Graceford.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
