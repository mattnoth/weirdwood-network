## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

While at [Seagard](wiki:Seagard), the captain is hired by [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) to bring him back to [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands). He reluctantly agrees to take his ship close to [Pyke](wiki:Pyke)'s cliffs so that Theon can have a better look at his childhood castle.(wiki:Captain_of_the_Myraham.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Once his mission completed, the captain wants to leave the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) but is fobidden by Theon's father, Lord [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), who wants to prevent the news of the [host](wiki:Host) he is gathering to reach the mainland of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Captain_of_the_Myraham.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Storm of Swords

After Balon's death, chaos ensues in the Iron Islands which gives the captain the opportunity to sail back to Seagard. He is later brought before King [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) at [Hag's Mire](wiki:Hag's_Mire) by Lord [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister) to announce Balon's death to Robb.(wiki:Captain_of_the_Myraham.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
