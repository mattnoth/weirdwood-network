## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Cutjack is part of the band of recruits which are escorted north to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) by [Yoren](wiki:Yoren) from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). When being confronted by a group of [gold cloaks](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing) searching for [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) at an inn with a bathhouse, Cutjack is one of the recruits who willingly aids Yoren in resisting handing the boy over. He takes a hammer from his leather apron to use it as weapon. Eventually, the gold cloaks retreat without a fight.(wiki:Cutjack.cite_ref-Racok5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Now avoiding the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad), the group moves west- and northwards on side roads in the direction of the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye). At a burned-down village, Cutjack joins Yoren and [Murch](wiki:Murch) in investigating the holdfast. Cutjack and Murch bring back a woman whose arm has been cut off at the elbow, while Yoren holds a little girl in his arms. When the woman dies that night, Cutjack and Gendry dig a grave for her on a hillside beneath a weeping willow.(wiki:Cutjack.cite_ref-Racok9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

When the group decides to spend the night in the holdfast of a deserted town on the southern shore of the Gods Eye, Yoren assigns Cutjack, [Kurz](wiki:Kurz) and [Tarber](wiki:Tarber) the task of holding vigil from on high at the towerhouse of the town.(wiki:Cutjack.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The whole town is burned down that night by men under the command of Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch). Lorch and his men attack the holdfast, and kill most of the group of [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) recruits.(wiki:Cutjack.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) However, Lorch and his men cannot put the towerhouse on fire and lack the patience to starve Cutjack, Kurz, and Tarber out. The three later reunite with [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) and the few others who had escaped the holdfast via a tunnel.(wiki:Cutjack.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) After Kurz dies from an infection of the wound he received during the struggle, Cutjack and Tarber take everything of value and abandon the other survivors.(wiki:Cutjack.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
