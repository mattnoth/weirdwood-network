## Origins

Rodrik's mother died forty years ago.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) According to Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin), Rodrik has served [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) all his life.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

Rodrik trained [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark),(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow),(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) and [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy).(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

[Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell), sometimes invites Rodrik to join him for meals.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

When Ser [Waymar Royce](wiki:Waymar_Royce) joined the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), Lord [Yohn Royce](wiki:Yohn_Royce) accompanied his son to the [north](wiki:North). During a sparring session at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), Bronze Yohn defeated first Eddard and then Rodrik.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Category:Images_of_Rodrik_Cassel)*

Rodrik is a stout(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) man with soft flesh under his chin.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He has white hair(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) and large white whiskers which he impulsively tugs at.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

The loyal Rodrik is courteous and courageous.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) He does not like singers, believing that swordsmanship is more suitable than music for boys, and is not interested in [tourneys](wiki:Tourney).(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Rodrik speaks of [the gods](wiki:Religion),(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) but it is unknown if he follows the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods) or [the new](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).

The [knight](wiki:Knight) wears ringmail, dagger, and [longsword](wiki:Longsword) under a hooded dark cloak.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) He rides a [dappled gelding](wiki:Horse)(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) and owns a red dog.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Rodrik believes in the importance of training against skilled opponents in the yard before fighting on the battlefield,(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) and he has taught his pupils about the defensibility of spiral stairs.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Rodrik's dog whelps a small litter with two living puppies. [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) thinks she could nurse the [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) pups found by the [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) children, but Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) thinks they would tear her apart.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

As master-at-arms, Rodrik observes when [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) spars with Prince [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), and he refuses to allow Robb and Prince [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) to spar with steel.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) Rodrik is part of [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s party when the king hunts for [boar](wiki:Boar) near the castle.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

Upon the discovery of a [Valyrian steel dagger](wiki:Littlefinger's_blade) after an [attempted assassination](wiki:Catspaw) of Bran, Rodrik swears an oath to the boy's mother, Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark). Rodrik volunteers to accompany Catelyn to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) so she can discretely inform her husband, Lord Eddard.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) Rodrik suggests hiring a fishing sloop from the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters) after the pair ride to [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor), but Catelyn insists upon traveling on a galley. The voyage on *[Storm Dancer](wiki:Storm_Dancer)* is a difficult one for Rodrik, who becomes seasick and has to cut his whiskers because they become soiled from vomit. [Moreo Tumitis](wiki:Moreo_Tumitis)'s men have to save Rodrik from being knocked overboard during a storm near [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)).(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2)

In King's Landing, Rodrik brings the dagger to Ser [Aron Santagar](wiki:Aron_Santagar), the master-at-arms of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), in his armory in a vain hope that he might recognize the blade. Rodrik and Aron then drink in the common room of an [old inn on Eel Alley](wiki:Old_inn_on_Eel_Alley) at which Rodrik and Catelyn are staying. Meanwhile, Catelyn meets Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), and her childhood friend tells her that the dagger that was used in the attempt on Bran was owned by [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-3) Later, after Catelyn tells Eddard about the attempt on Bran, Lord Stark decides that Catelyn should return to Winterfell.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

Rodrik and Catelyn disguise themselves as father and daughter on their way back to Winterfell. At the [crossroads inn](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), however, they meet Tyrion Lannister, who recognizes Catelyn. Because of what Petyr has told her, Catelyn holds Tyrion responsible for the attack on her son, and Rodrik helps take the Imp prisoner.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

Catelyn takes Tyrion to the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) where her sister, Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) resides, in order to confuse her enemies. During the ride through the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon), they are twice set upon by the [Vale mountain clans](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans). In the first skirmish Rodrik bests the group's champion, but is seriously wounded, suffering a deep gash in his left arm and a spear graze to his neck.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) He makes it to the [Bloody Gate](wiki:Bloody_Gate) only by being tied to his saddle. There he and Ser [Willis Wode](wiki:Willis_Wode) are left to recover from their wounds with the aid of a [septon](wiki:Septon).(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) The two knights eventually continue on to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie),(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) and Rodrik regrows his side whiskers.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) joins Rodrik and Catelyn when they depart the Eyrie and return to the [north](wiki:North) via a ship from [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown).(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-1) When they arrive in White Harbor, Catelyn decides to join her eldest son, Robb, at [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin), and she has Rodrik return to Winterfell to serve as the castle's [castellan](wiki:Castellan).(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

Rodrik trains boys recruited to be new [House Stark guardsmen](wiki:House_Stark_guards).(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) Since [Osha](wiki:Osha) has been a loyal servant to the crippled Bran, Rodrik orders that her chain be removed.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-1)

### A Clash of Kings

With Robb, now the [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), and Lady Catelyn in the south, Rodrik holds the command of Winterfell together with Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin). Rodrik trains Catelyn's new wards, [Big Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Jammos)) and [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)), and has them sleep in [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow)'s old chambers. After [Shaggydog](wiki:Shaggydog) bites Little Walder, Rodrik has Shaggydog and [Summer](wiki:Summer) confined to the [godswood of Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)

Rodrik, Bran, and Luwin meet with various [northern](wiki:North) bannermen after they arrive for the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast_(299)), including Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly), Lady [Donella Hornwood](wiki:Donella_Hornwood), [Mors](wiki:Mors_Umber) and [Hother Umber](wiki:Hother_Umber), and [Leobald Tallhart](wiki:Leobald_Tallhart).(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) The recently-widowed Donella hints that she would not be averse to choosing Rodrik if Robb were to ask her to remarry. Rodrik, however, explains to Bran that he is too old to provide security for Donella.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-1) Rodrik orders the Umbers to cooperate with Wyman in building ships.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-2) Rodrik asks Donella to dance during the feast, but she respectfully declines.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

[Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), the Bastard of Bolton, seizes Lady Hornwood after she departs the harvest feast, so Rodrik heads east to resolve the situation.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0) Rodrik's men kill Ramsay and capture his servant, [Reek](wiki:Reek), but they are too late to save Donella, who has starved to death after being locked in a tower by Ramsay.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

During the [harrying of the Stony Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore), [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) plots to draw Rodrik and his garrison from Winterfell by having [Dagmer Cleftjaw](wiki:Dagmer_Cleftjaw) threaten [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Torrhen's_Square).(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0) Bran and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) are seized by Theon and his [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) during his [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0) After the Stark boys disappear, Theon and Reek kill [two miller's boys](wiki:Miller's_sons) and present their corpses as those of Bran and Rickon.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2)

After Rodrik defeats Dagmer's shield wall in the [fight at Torrhen's Square](wiki:Fight_at_Torrhen's_Square),(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0) he sends a [raven](wiki:Raven) from [Castle Cerwyn](wiki:Castle_Cerwyn) to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) to inform Catelyn of the supposed deaths of Bran and Rickon.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0) Rodrik gathers more forces to besiege Winterfell.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-1)(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-1)

Rodrik, Leobald, and Lord [Cley Cerwyn](wiki:Cley_Cerwyn) parley with Theon before the gates of Winterfell, demanding his life and the castle, but Theon threatens to hang Rodrik's daughter, [Beth Cassel](wiki:Beth_Cassel), if Rodrik attacks. Luwin advises Theon to surrender, stating that Rodrik would allow Theon to then [take the black](wiki:Take_the_black).(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-3) A force of [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) men approach the besiegers, apparently to join them in the siege, but the newcomers then attack Rodrik's host. After the [battle outside the gates of Winterfell](wiki:Battle_outside_the_gates_of_Winterfell), the Boltons' leader reveals to Theon that he is not Reek but actually the still-living Ramsay Snow. The Bastard of Bolton explains he killed Rodrik by chopping his left arm when the knight offered it in friendship. Ramsay then orders his men to kill Theon's ironmen and [burn Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-4)

### A Storm of Swords

At Riverrun, [Lothar Frey](wiki:Lothar_Frey) tells Robb and Catelyn that Rodrik was slain when [northmen](wiki:Northmen) tried to retake Winterfell from the ironmen. The Walders wrote to their grandfather, Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey), explaining that they and the women and children of Winterfell were taken by Ramsay to the safety of the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort).(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) At the [Twins](wiki:Twins), Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) says that his son Ramsay took command of the northmen after ironmen cut down Rodrik.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Rodrik's daughter Beth is held captive at the Dreadfort.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover) and Lord Manderly learn from [Wex Pyke](wiki:Wex_Pyke) that Ramsay's claim of Theon having killed Rodrik during a parley is false. Wyman tells Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) that it was instead Ramsay who slew the knight.(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0)

Theon recalls Rodrik and his teachings after returning to Winterfell for the [wedding of Ramsay Bolton and Arya Stark](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark).(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1)(wiki:Rodrik_Cassel.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)
