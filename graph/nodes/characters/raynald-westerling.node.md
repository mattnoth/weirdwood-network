## Appearances & Description

Raynald is young and lean, with a thick mop of chestnut hair and a bushy mustache.(wiki:Raynald_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) He wears a sand-colored surcoat blazoned with seashells.(wiki:Raynald_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Raynald is part of the entourage of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), when he returns to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) from the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands), having married Raynald's sister, [Jeyne](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling). The [marriage](wiki:Marriage) broke Robb's alliance with [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey), as he was to wed a daughter of Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey).(wiki:Raynald_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Robb enjoys spending time with Raynald; Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) believes Raynald fills the hole left by the absence of [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) and [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy).(wiki:Raynald_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

When Robb sets out for the [Twins](wiki:Twins) for the marriage of Lady [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey) and Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully), Raynald is the only [Westerling](wiki:House_Westerling) to [accompany](wiki:Robb's_personal_guard) him, serving as the royal banner-bearer.(wiki:Raynald_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Arriving at the Twins, [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind) menaces several Freys, and Robb orders Raynald to put the [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) in a kennel. Catelyn sees this as a deft move, as Robb has removed the Westerling from the jealous Freys' sight.(wiki:Raynald_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Robb is betrayed and killed by Houses Frey and [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Raynald_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Feast For Crows

According to his mother, Lady [Sybell Spicer](wiki:Sybell_Spicer), Raynald was unaware of the arrangement she and her brother, Ser [Rolph Spicer](wiki:Rolph_Spicer), had with Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister). Sybell informs Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) Raynald should have joy of marry a bride from [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock). Jaime understands this as Tywin intended to marry Raynald to his [bastard](wiki:Bastard) niece, [Joy Hill](wiki:Joy_Hill). The notion of her son marrying a bastard upsets Sybell.(wiki:Raynald_Westerling.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) However, according to a semi-canon source, this is a misunderstanding on Jaime's part(wiki:Raynald_Westerling.cite_ref-Rawoiafjoy_hill.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) as Tywin already arranged for Joy to marry one of Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey)'s natural sons.(wiki:Raynald_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

After the resolution of the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Ser Jaime Lannister asks about Raynald's fate. [Edwyn Frey](wiki:Edwyn_Frey) and [Walder Rivers](wiki:Walder_Rivers) reveal that Raynald surrendered his sword to Ser [Whalen Frey](wiki:Whalen_Frey), but then freed Grey Wind from his chains in the stables when several Frey men-at-arms tried to kill the direwolf. While doing so, Raynald was hit by a crossbow bolt and fell into the [Green Fork](wiki:Green_Fork). Raynald's body was not identified among the thousand corpses afterward, but his trail of blood led to the river's steps.(wiki:Raynald_Westerling.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
