## Identity

Robert Arryn, called Sweetrobin, is the only son of Lord Jon Arryn and Lady Lysa Tully. He is the Lord of the Eyrie, Defender of the Vale, and Warden of the East, named after King Robert I Baratheon (wiki:Robert_Arryn).

## Appearances & Description

Small and painfully thin for his age (six years old), pale with brown hair, big red runny eyes, spindly limbs, soft concave chest, and splotchy skin (wiki:Robert_Arryn). Spoiled, whiny, and prone to seizures. Maester Colemon treats him with leeches, dreamwine, milk of the poppy, and carefully rationed sweetsleep (wiki:Robert_Arryn). Not weaned from his mother's breast. Wears sky-blue velvet with gold and sapphire chain for formal occasions (wiki:Robert_Arryn).

## Narrative Arc

Robert is sickly from birth. After Jon Arryn's death, his mother Lysa flees King's Landing to the Eyrie. King Robert had suggested fostering the boy with Tywin Lannister; Jon Arryn had planned to send him to Stannis at Dragonstone — Lysa refused both (wiki:Robert_Arryn). At the Eyrie, Robert witnesses Tyrion Lannister's trial by combat and wants to "make the bad man fly" (wiki:Robert_Arryn). After Lysa marries Petyr Baelish in ASOS, Littlefinger becomes Lord Protector of the Vale. Robert grows attached to Sansa Stark (disguised as Alayne Stone), who cares for him during their descent from the Eyrie (wiki:Robert_Arryn). In TWOW, Littlefinger's plans for Robert's future remain uncertain, with the Lords Declarant demanding the boy be fostered away from Baelish's influence (wiki:Robert_Arryn).
