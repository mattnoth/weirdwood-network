## Origins

When Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) admitted to having kissed one of the [Drinkwater](wiki:Drinkwater) twins, Cletus did not mind and suggested that his friend could take one or both of the girls as [paramours](wiki:Paramour).(wiki:Cletus_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

Cletus once shot down a [heron](wiki:Heron) while hunting with Ser [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood) and Ser [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater).(wiki:Cletus_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Appearances & Description

Despite his lazy eye, Cletus is considered handsome, randy, and always laughing.(wiki:Cletus_Yronwood.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Cletus_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Cletus is one of Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell)'s closest friends.(wiki:Cletus_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Cletus gave the nickname *big man* to his cousin, Ser [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood).(wiki:Cletus_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Lord [Anders Yronwood](wiki:Anders_Yronwood) sends Cletus and three of his best [knights](wiki:Knight) to accompany Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) on a dangerous journey.(wiki:Cletus_Yronwood.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Quentyn travels to [Essos](wiki:Essos) on a mission to marry [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), [Queen of Meereen](wiki:Queen_of_Meereen). To hide the true purpose of the journey, Cletus presents himself as a merchant with Quentyn as his servant.(wiki:Cletus_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

On the way to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), the *[Meadowlark](wiki:Meadowlark)* is attacked by [pirates](wiki:Pirates) near the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands). Although the crew and the Dornishmen manage to win the fight, Cletus and Ser [Willam Wells](wiki:Willam_Wells) are killed in the battle. The sailors give them an honorable funeral, but when Quentyn and his companions are asked to say few words about the deeds of their friends, Ser [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater) is forced to lie in order not to reveal their identity.(wiki:Cletus_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

Having been rejected by Daenerys, Quentyn mentions Cletus and Willam while trying to convince Gerris and Ser [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood) to aid him in claiming one of Daenerys's [dragons](wiki:Dragon) after the queen has disappeared from Meereen. Gerris responds that doing so will not bring their fallen friends back.(wiki:Cletus_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
