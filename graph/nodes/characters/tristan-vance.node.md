## Origins

At the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) in [129 AC](wiki:129_AC), Tristan was among the prominent riverlords that supported Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and the [blacks](wiki:Blacks), despite initial reluctance.(wiki:Tristan_Vance.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

At the [Battle of the Red Fork](wiki:Battle_of_the_Red_Fork), Tristan and Lord [Petyr Piper](wiki:Petyr_Piper) met the host of Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister). Despite being out numbered, the rivermen pushed the westermen back thrice and mortally wounded Lord Lannister. On the fourth push, Ser [Adrian Tarbeck](wiki:Adrian_Tarbeck) and a hundred [knights](wiki:Knight) swam upstream and circled back to take Tristan's lines from the rear. The rivermen's host shattered and Lord Vance was slain by Ser Adrian.(wiki:Tristan_Vance.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Tristan was succeeded as the Lord of Wayfarer's Rest by the young [Hugo Vance](wiki:Hugo_Vance_(lord)).(wiki:Tristan_Vance.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
