## Origins

Titus was one of Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake)'s seven children, being his third son. He was the only one of Unwin's sons to grow to manhood.(wiki:Titus_Peake_(son_of_Unwin).cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

After the [Battle of the Honeywine](wiki:Battle_of_the_Honeywine), Titus was [knighted](wiki:Knight) by Ser [Jon Roxton](wiki:Jon_Roxton) for his valor, but Ser Titus died only six days later in a meaningless skirmish with a band of [broken men](wiki:Broken_men) he stumbled upon whilst scouting.
