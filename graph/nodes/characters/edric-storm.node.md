## Identity

Edric Storm is the acknowledged bastard son of King Robert I Baratheon and Delena Florent (wiki:Edric_Storm).

## Origins

Born 287 AC. His father is Robert I Baratheon and his mother is Delena Florent (track_b: Born, Father, Mother). He was conceived on the wedding night of Stannis Baratheon and Selyse Florent, in the couple's wedding bed (wiki:Edric_Storm).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Baratheon (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Stormlander (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Edric is a sturdily attractive youth with jet-black hair and deep blue eyes. He resembles his father Robert and has the characteristic Baratheon hair, eyes, jaw, and cheekbones, along with the large ears common to House Florent. He is charming and courteous but also fierce and proud — Davos Seaworth compares him to both Robert and Renly (wiki:Edric_Storm).

## Narrative Arc

Because Delena was a noblewoman, Robert acknowledged Edric. Stannis saw the conception as an insult to his honor and sent Edric to Storm's End to foster with Renly. He was raised under the guardianship of Ser Cortnay Penrose. Robert sent him a gift every name day — a pony, a sable cloak, a child-sized warhammer — though Robert would ask Varys what he had sent each year. Stannis intended to use Edric's Baratheon appearance as proof that Cersei's children were not Robert's. During the siege of Storm's End after Renly's death, Cortnay Penrose resisted surrendering Edric. After Cortnay's death, Stannis shipped Edric to Dragonstone. There, Melisandre pressured Stannis to sacrifice Edric for his royal blood to "wake stone dragons." Davos Seaworth smuggled Edric to safety aboard a Lysene galley before Stannis could act (wiki:Edric_Storm, agot-eddard-09).
