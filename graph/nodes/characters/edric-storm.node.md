## Origins

Edric was conceived by King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) and Delena on [the wedding night](wiki:Wedding_of_Stannis_Baratheon_and_Selyse_Florent) of Lord [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) to [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent), Delena's cousin, in the couple's wedding bed. Stannis saw this as an insult to his honor, so he sent Edric to [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) to foster with the boy's other uncle, [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon).(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Selyse believes that Edric's existence has cursed her to have no sons.(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

As Delena was a noblewoman, Edric was acknowledged by his father.(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) He was raised in relative comfort at Storm's End under the guardianship of Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose), Renly's castellan, who is fond of the boy.(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Edric was educated by [maesters](wiki:Maesters), [castellans](wiki:Castellan), and [masters-at-arms](wiki:Masters-at-arms).(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

Edric has been sent a gift from King Robert every year on his [name day](wiki:Name_day). These have included a pony, a [sable](wiki:Sable) cloak, and a child-sized warhammer similar to [his father's weapon](wiki:Robert's_warhammer). Edric would write to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to thank his father for the gifts, but upon receiving the letters Robert would laugh and ask [Varys](wiki:Varys) what he had sent that year.(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Appearances & Description

Edric is a sturdily attractive youth, with jet-black hair and deep blue eyes. He resembles his father, King [Robert I](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), and has the characteristic hair, eyes, jaw, and cheekbones of [House Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon).(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Edric also has the large ears common to [House Florent](wiki:House_Florent).(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Edric is charming and courteous, but also fierce and proud.(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) In this manner, [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) compares Edric to both Robert and the king's youngest brother, [Renly](wiki:Renly_Baratheon).(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

After having discovered [Gendry](wiki:Gendry), Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) thinks about all of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s bastards that he knows about, Edric included.(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Clash of Kings

[Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) intends to use Edric's appearance as proof that Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s children were fathered by her brother, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), instead of King Robert.(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

During the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)) after the death of [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), Stannis offers to let the garrison go free if Edric is surrendered. Renly's castellan, Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose), resists in fear of what Stannis would do to Edric, however. After Cortnay falls to his death,(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Stannis takes the castle and ships Edric and [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Edric spends time at Dragonstone with Stannis's daughter, Princess [Shireen Baratheon](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon), and her fool, [Patchface](wiki:Patchface).(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Maester [Pylos](wiki:Pylos) leeches Edric when the boy comes down with a chill.(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Edric remains faithful to the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) instead of converting to the worship of [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor).(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

[Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) pressures Stannis to burn Edric, as she believes that sacrificing his royal blood would wake [dragons](wiki:Dragon) from stone to help Stannis. Conversely, Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) pressures Stannis to spare him.(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) Edric is leeched and his blood is used in a ritual to curse the three rival kings, [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), and [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark).(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) Subsequently, Balon falls at [Pyke](wiki:Pyke),(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Robb is killed in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding),(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) and Joffrey is [poisoned](wiki:Poison) at [his royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding).(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Concerned that Stannis will burn Edric, Davos, with the help of Pylos and a small group of [king's men](wiki:King's_men), has Edric secretly shipped away to the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) aboard the *[Mad Prendos](wiki:Mad_Prendos_(galley))*. The boy wants to give his farewells to Shireen and Stannis, but Davos, speaking as the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), insists there is no time.(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) Davos appoints [Andrew Estermont](wiki:Andrew_Estermont), Edric's cousin, to act as his guardian. [Gerald Gower](wiki:Gerald_Gower), [Triston of Tally Hill](wiki:Triston_of_Tally_Hill), [Lewys the Fishwife](wiki:Lewys_the_Fishwife), and [Omer Blackberry](wiki:Omer_Blackberry) also accompany Edric as his guards and protectors.(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Davos defends his actions to a tired Stannis and a disappointed Melisandre,(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2) and the king sails north to aid the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Edric sails across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) past the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones)(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) to [Essos](wiki:Essos) on the *Mad Prendos* with Andrew and other protectors.(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Edric is in hiding in [Lys](wiki:Lys) with his guardian and protectors.(wiki:Edric_Storm.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)
