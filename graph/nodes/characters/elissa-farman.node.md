## Origins

The daughter of Lord [Marq Farman](wiki:Marq_Farman) of [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle), Elissa had two brothers: [Franklyn](wiki:Franklyn_Farman) and [Androw](wiki:Androw_Farman).(wiki:Elissa_Farman.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)(wiki:Elissa_Farman.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) As a child, it was said that she spent more time at sea than upon the land. She sailed her own boat around Fair Isle at the age of fourteen. By twenty, she had voyaged as far north as [Bear Island](wiki:Bear_Island) and as far south as the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor). Elissa was betrothed twice, once at twelve and once at sixteen, but she scared off both boys.(wiki:Elissa_Farman.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5) Thus, it came as little surprise that she became a close confidant to Queen [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)).(wiki:Elissa_Farman.cite_ref-Rtrotd61.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Elissa attended the [wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Rhaena_Targaryen_and_Androw_Farman) of her brother Androw to Queen Rhaena on Fair Isle in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC). Elissa quickly grew close to the queen, as well as Rhaena's old friends [Alayne Royce](wiki:Alayne_Royce) and [Samantha Stokeworth](wiki:Samantha_Stokeworth). Her brother Franklyn dubbed the four friends "the [Four-Headed Beast](wiki:Four-Headed_Beast)", and the friends often rode the dragon [Dreamfyre](wiki:Dreamfyre) together. Lord Farman's [maester](wiki:Maester), [Smike](wiki:Smike), speculated that the queen had only married Androw not because she loved him, but instead because of her love for Elissa.(wiki:Elissa_Farman.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)

When Marq died in [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), Franklyn became [lord](wiki:Lord) and ordered Rhaena, the so-called "Queen in the West", to leave Fair Isle, with the [dragonrider](wiki:Dragonrider) departing for [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock). Though Elissa attempted to leave as well, Franklyn confronted her at the dock and commanded she stay on Fair Isle to be wed. Elissa spat defiance at Lord Franklyn before he and his men were overwhelmed, with Rhaena's companions then sailing with Elissa on her ship, *[Maiden's Fancy](wiki:Maiden's_Fancy)*, for [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport).(wiki:Elissa_Farman.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7)

Realizing that most lords wanted to take advantage of her or were afraid of her, Rhaena and her friends briefly stayed at Casterly Rock, [Ashemark](wiki:Ashemark), [Castamere](wiki:Castamere), the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth), [Wayfarer's Rest](wiki:Wayfarer's_Rest), and [Pinkmaiden Castle](wiki:Pinkmaiden_Castle).(wiki:Elissa_Farman.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Elissa remained with Rhaena after she established her court on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). She quickly warmed to Rhaena's daughter, Princess [Aerea](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen), as both were unhappy with their lives at the island fortress. Elissa often spoke of returning to the western seas with the young princess. As Elissa was denied the incomes of Fair Isle by Lord Franklyn, she petitioned Rhaena for gold sufficient to build a ship at [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark) that could sail the Sunset Sea. Rhaena denied her request, and a rift thus opened in Elissa and Rhaena's relationship.(wiki:Elissa_Farman.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

In [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), Elissa petitioned Rhaena to leave once more. Rhaena permitted her request stone-faced, while Aerea wept for Elissa. Neither Targaryen princess knew of Elissa's destination, and she discreetly stole three [dragon eggs](wiki:Dragon_egg) from the Dragonstone hatcheries. She departed for Driftmark, where she took ship to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) and made her way overland to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) under the name **Alys Westhill** and secured an audience with the [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos), selling him the eggs. With the gold, Elissa financed the building of her carrack, the *[Sun Chaser](wiki:Sun_Chaser)*.(wiki:Elissa_Farman.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) She set sail with a word of caution from the Sealord, due to the scandal that the theft of the dragon eggs had caused the Targaryens.(wiki:Elissa_Farman.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Androw fell into depression at Dragonstone, as Elissa had been his closest friend.(wiki:Elissa_Farman.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Septon [Barth](wiki:Barth), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), was sent to Braavos to investigate the missing dragon eggs. The Sealord denied having them, but agreed to reduce the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne)'s debts to the [Iron Bank of Braavos](wiki:Iron_Bank_of_Braavos).(wiki:Elissa_Farman.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

In [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) in [56 AC](wiki:56_AC), Elissa searched for a crew for her ship for her voyage across the Sunset Sea, as she had believed since childhood that undiscovered lands existed west of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). Words of her efforts reached Lord [Donnel Hightower](wiki:Donnel_Hightower), and his grandsons, Ser [Eustace](wiki:Eustace_Hightower) and Ser [Norman](wiki:Norman_Hightower), pledged their ships, *[Lady Meredith](wiki:Lady_Meredith)* and *[Autumn Moon](wiki:Autumn_Moon)*, to her voyage. King Jaehaerys ordered Donnel to take Elissa into custody, but Elissa's three swift ships evaded the twelve pursuers sent by Lord Hightower.(wiki:Elissa_Farman.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

When he returned to Oldtown on *Lady Meredith* in [59 AC](wiki:59_AC), Eustace Hightower explained that they had sailed south by southwest years ago but lost Norman and his *Autumn Moon* to a storm. Elissa's crew towed *Lady Meredith* to three exotic islands, which she named [Aegon, Rhaenys, and Visenya](wiki:Aegon,_Rhaenys,_and_Visenya_(islands)). Despite the protests of Eustace, Elissa continued westward on *Sun Chaser* and was never seen again.(wiki:Elissa_Farman.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Many years later, during his second great voyage on *[Sea Snake](wiki:Sea_Snake)*, [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) believed he saw the old, weathered *Sun Chaser* in [Asshai](wiki:Asshai).(wiki:Elissa_Farman.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) It is therefore possible that Elissa reached [Essos](wiki:Essos) by sailing west from Westeros.

## Appearances & Description

Elissa had blue eyes and long flaxen hair,(wiki:Elissa_Farman.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) and she was high-spirited.(wiki:Elissa_Farman.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

[Archmaester](wiki:Archmaester) [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn) describes her as "sharp of wit and sharper of tongue." Elissa loved [horses](wiki:Horse), [dogs](wiki:Dog), and [hawks](wiki:Hawk). She was a fine singer and a skilled archer, but her great love was sailing.(wiki:Elissa_Farman.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)
