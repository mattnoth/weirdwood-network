## Origins

When she was twelve, she took an axe to a pack of [wolves](wiki:Wolves), killed two of them, and sewed a cloak from their skins. At some point, she married Alaric Stark, the second son of Lord [Brandon Stark](wiki:Brandon_Stark_(father_of_Walton)). They had three children together, two sons and a daughter, [Alarra](wiki:Alarra_Stark).(wiki:Lady_Mormont_(wife_of_Alaric_Stark).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

In [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), she became the Lady of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) after the death of her brother-in-law, Lord [Walton Stark](wiki:Walton_Stark_(son_of_Brandon)).
