## Origins

Helicent was one of the courtiers at [Coldmoat](wiki:Coldmoat) during the reign of King [Aerys I Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_I_Targaryen). She came to Coldmoat with her brother, Ser [Rolland Uffering](wiki:Rolland_Uffering), who was the fourth husband of Lady [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber). When Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) came to Coldmoat, Ser [Lucas Inchfield](wiki:Lucas_Inchfield) introduced Helicent as Rohanne in order to embarrass Duncan. Helicent did not understand Duncan's meaning when he asked her about the dam on the [Chequy Water](wiki:Chequy_Water), and was frightened when one of the other courtiers shouted that Duncan wanted to sleep with her.(wiki:Helicent_Uffering.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

## Appearances & Description

In [211 AC](wiki:211_AC), Helicent was about forty years old. She was soft and fleshy, stout and square. She had auburn hair that she wore piled high, and had a pointed head that her hair did not conceal. She had a red face, a big nose, and a small mouth.(wiki:Helicent_Uffering.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Helicent was a pious and [simple woman](wiki:Lackwit). She was fond of melons. She wore a gown of dark blue damask trimmed with [Myrish lace](wiki:Myrish_lace), which was so long that its hem trailed in the dirt. Three [septas](wiki:Septa) [attended her](wiki:Handmaid).(wiki:Helicent_Uffering.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
