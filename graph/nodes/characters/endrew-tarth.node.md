## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Endrew serves at the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) until [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), reassigns him to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) as the replacement for Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne).(wiki:Endrew_Tarth.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Clash of Kings

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) observes Endrew working with some new recruits brought to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) by [Conwy](wiki:Conwy). Among the recruits are [Satin](wiki:Satin), [Hop-Robin](wiki:Hop-Robin), and the brothers [Arron](wiki:Arron) and [Emrick](wiki:Emrick). Jon thinks Endrew's training is gentler than Alliser's, yet still bruising.(wiki:Endrew_Tarth.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Endrew is slain in the [fight at the Bridge of Skulls](wiki:Fight_at_the_Bridge_of_Skulls).(wiki:Endrew_Tarth.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Jon trains recruits in Endrew's absence.(wiki:Endrew_Tarth.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Jon, now Lord Commander, names [Leathers](wiki:Leathers) of the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) as Endrew's replacement.(wiki:Endrew_Tarth.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
