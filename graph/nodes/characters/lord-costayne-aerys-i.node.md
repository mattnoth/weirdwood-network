## Origins

During the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), Lord Costayne's [lordly father](wiki:Lord_Costayne_(Daeron_II)) fought for [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) in the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field).(wiki:Lord_Costayne_(Aerys_I).cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

In [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), Lord Costayne came with his retinue to [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) to attend the [wedding](wiki:Wedding) of his father-in-law, Lord [Ambrose Butterwell](wiki:Ambrose_Butterwell), and his second wife, [the daughter](wiki:Lady_Butterwell) of [Lord Frey](wiki:Lord_Frey). On his way, Lord Costayne had to cross the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye) via a ferry. He quarreled with [Lord Shawney](wiki:Lord_Shawney) on the matter of which lord should cross first; Shawney argued that as the older of the two he should go first, whereas Costayne believed that as [his House](wiki:House_Costayne) was nobler then [Shawney's](wiki:House_Shawney), he had the precedence.(wiki:Lord_Costayne_(Aerys_I).cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

During the wedding feast, Lord Costayne was seated on the dais. The next day, he competed in the [wedding tourney](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls), but lost to Ser [Glendon Flowers](wiki:Glendon_Flowers). He sent his [squire](wiki:Squire) to ransom the sorrel he lost but Glendon decided to keep the [horse](wiki:Horse). The angry lord told Glendon he had no right to use [Fireball](wiki:Quentyn_Ball)'s coat of arms. When Glendon was falsely accused of stealing a [dragon egg](wiki:Dragon_egg), Lord Costayne advocated for Glendon to die.(wiki:Lord_Costayne_(Aerys_I).cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

The fate of Lord Costayne following the arrival at Whitewalls of the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers), and the suppression of the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion) is unknown.
