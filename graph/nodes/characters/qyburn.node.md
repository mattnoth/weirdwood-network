## Identity

Qyburn is a former maester stripped of his chain by the Citadel for performing experiments on living subjects and studying necromancy. He subsequently joined the Brave Companions and later entered service with House Lannister (wiki:Qyburn). Also known as "the Bloody Maester" and "Lord Qyburn."

## Origins

Born in or after 217 AC in Westeros (track_b: Born). Studied at the Citadel, as skilled a healer as Archmaester Ebrose. Sought to surpass Ebrose by opening living bodies instead of dead ones to discover the secrets of death. When these experiments and his necromancy studies were discovered, he was stripped of his chain (wiki:Qyburn).

## Appearances & Description

Tall, slightly stooped, with crinkles around his blue or warm brown eyes (descriptions vary). Grey hair, lean frame, fatherly appearance. Torn, frayed robes, poorly sewn, lacking a maester's chain. Later attires himself in white robes decorated with whorls of gold and a golden sash. Wears roughspun wool and a blacksmith's leather apron when working in the dungeons (wiki:Qyburn).

## Narrative Arc

Qyburn arrives at Harrenhal with the Brave Companions in ACOK and takes over maester duties after the sellswords turn cloak (wiki:Qyburn.cite_ref-Racok). In ASOS, he treats Jaime Lannister's infected wrist after Vargo Hoat cuts off his sword hand, saving Jaime's arm. He accompanies Jaime back to King's Landing, monitoring his wounds and seeking to ingratiate himself with the Lannisters (wiki:Qyburn).

In AFFC, Qyburn joins Cersei's court. He disposes of Shae's body, investigates Tyrion's escape (finding a Gardener gold coin that implicates House Tyrell), and takes over the dying Gregor Clegane from Pycelle. Cersei appoints him master of whisperers to replace the missing Varys. He builds a spy network, tortures the Blue Bard to fabricate evidence against Margaery Tyrell, and experiments on prisoners including Senelle and Lady Falyse Stokeworth. He creates a mysterious armored "champion" — widely implied to be the reanimated Gregor Clegane, later known as Ser Robert Strong (wiki:Qyburn).

After Cersei's arrest, Qyburn is dismissed from the small council but retains access to Varys's whisperers and continues his dungeon work. He informs Cersei that his champion is ready to defend her in trial by combat once a Kingsguard vacancy opens (wiki:Qyburn).
