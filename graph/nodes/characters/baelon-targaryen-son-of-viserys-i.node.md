## Origins

Queen [Aemma](wiki:Aemma_Arryn) died giving birth to Baelon. He was [named after](wiki:Named_after) his grandfather, Prince [Baelon the Brave](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)), but the infant died one day after his birth.(wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I).cite_ref-Rtrp.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)(wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_viserys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I).cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

While the court was grieving and bereft over the deaths of Baelon and Aemma, Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) was observed in the [Street of Silk](wiki:Street_of_Silk) with his highborn cronies, making drunken japes about the "heir for a day". When word of this reached King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen), he became livid and named his daughter, [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), as his heir.(wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I).cite_ref-Rtrp.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)(wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I).cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
