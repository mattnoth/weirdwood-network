## Identity

Prince Baelon Targaryen was the secondborn son of King Viserys I Targaryen and Queen Aemma Arryn (wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)). He was mockingly called "Heir for a Day" by Prince Daemon Targaryen.

## Origins

Born in 105 AC in King's Landing (track_b: Born). Named after his grandfather, Prince Baelon the Brave (wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)). His mother Queen Aemma died giving birth to him (wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiance).

## Narrative Arc

Baelon died only one day after his birth in 105 AC (track_b: Died). While the court grieved, Prince Daemon Targaryen was observed at the Street of Silk making drunken japes about the "heir for a day," which when reported to King Viserys contributed to Daemon's falling out of favor (wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)).
