## Origins

Aemon was the secondborn of the children of [Daemon Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) and [Rohanne](wiki:Rohanne_of_Tyrosh) of [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh).(wiki:Aemon_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) He had an older twin brother, [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Blackfyre), and five younger brothers: [Daemon](wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre), [Haegon](wiki:Haegon_I_Blackfyre), [Aenys](wiki:Aenys_Blackfyre), and two whose names are currently unknown. He also had at least two sisters.(wiki:Aemon_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

The twins often bullied their younger brother Daemon, and Daemon's friend, [Alyn Cockshaw](wiki:Alyn_Cockshaw). Daemon once had a [prophetic dream](wiki:Dragon_dreams) about the deaths of Aemon and Aegon, but the disbelieving twins only laughed at their brother.(wiki:Aemon_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

During the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), when Aemon was twelve years old, he served as a [squire](wiki:Squire) to his father.(wiki:Aemon_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rssm1468.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) At the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field), Aemon's twin, Aegon, was killed by the arrows of [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers)(wiki:Aemon_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) and his [Raven's Teeth](wiki:Raven's_Teeth).(wiki:Aemon_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Unwilling to leave him, Daemon remained by his fallen son's side, and was also struck down by arrows, supposedly driven by Bloodraven's [magic](wiki:Magic). Aemon took up the sword [Blackfyre](wiki:Blackfyre) from his dying father, but was then slain by the archers as well.(wiki:Aemon_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
