## Appearances & Description

Dick is deaf, but he can read lips and has a keen nose. His speech is thick but intelligible.(wiki:Dick_Follard.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The experienced man wields a crossbow.(wiki:Dick_Follard.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Deaf Dick is among the brothers that remain at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) when [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), marches [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) in search of [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark) and [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder).(wiki:Dick_Follard.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Dick fights in the [attack on Castle Black‎‎](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) against the [Thenn](wiki:Thenns) raiders commanded by [Styr](wiki:Styr). He is stationed on the roof of the [King's Tower](wiki:King's_Tower) with [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Satin](wiki:Satin), and six scarecrows dressed as brothers. When the wildlings attack, Dick uses his crossbow while Jon uses a longbow. Deaf Dick is felled by [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte)'s arrow as he points out wildlings on top of the armory to Jon.(wiki:Dick_Follard.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

### A Dance with Dragons

When Jon suffers a nightmare, Dick is among the men cut down by the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch).(wiki:Dick_Follard.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
