## Origins

In [49 AC](wiki:49_AC) the young king [Jaehaerys I](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) still had five vacant spots in the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) to fill. At the [Golden Wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding), his mother [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) suggested the remaining five slots by a test of arms since knights from all over the realms had come to attend the wedding. Jaehaerys agreed, but with a twist - the knights must fight on foot, not the joust, for foes seldom strike a king from horseback.(wiki:Tom_the_Strummer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
The wild melees and bloody duels that followed were dubbed by [maesters](wiki:Maesters) as the [War for the White Cloaks](wiki:War_for_the_White_Cloaks) with hundreds of knights competing for the honor of serving in the Kingsguard. Although he did not win a white cloak, Tom the Strummer became a great favorite of the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk), mocking his opponents before each bout with ribald songs.(wiki:Tom_the_Strummer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
When Jaehaerys came of age, he had the [City Watch of King's Landing](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing) comb [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom) to find Tom the Strummer, so the king could make him the [court singer](wiki:Court_singer).(wiki:Tom_the_Strummer.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
