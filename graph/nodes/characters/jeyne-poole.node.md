## Appearances & Description

Jeyne is an attractive girl with brown eyes, dark hair, and pretty white teeth.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Ragot44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) She is skinny, and has a small bosom.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Jeyne as bride before Ramsay's wedding (Theon POV, ADWD The Prince of Winterfell): "They had dressed her in white lambswool trimmed with lace. Her sleeves and bodice were sewn with freshwater pearls, and on her feet were white doeskin slippers—pretty, but not warm. Her face was pale, bloodless. A face carved of ice, Theon Greyjoy thought as he draped a fur-trimmed cloak about her shoulders. A corpse buried in the snow." (`sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-the-prince-of-winterfell-01.md:13`) The "brown eyes, shining in the candlelight" detail at :15 confirms identity for any cross-identity query.

Jeyne is the best friend of [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark).(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Ragot7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) She mocks Sansa's sister, [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), as "Arya [Horseface](wiki:Horse)", smirking and neighing when the younger girl is near.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Ragot7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Jeyne is fond of Sansa's brother, [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark),(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and thinks the Stark [ward](wiki:Ward) [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) is handsome.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) She enjoys [lemon cakes](wiki:Lemon_cake).(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Ragot44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

During a needlework session at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) with Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon), Jeyne claims that [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) has caught the eye of Prince [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), which leads to a discussion that upsets [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark).(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Ragot7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

When Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) is named [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), [Vayon Poole](wiki:Vayon_Poole) comes with him to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Jeyne accompanies him, remaining as Sansa's [companion](wiki:Handmaid) in the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand).(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

During the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney), Jeyne is frightened by [Jalabhar Xho](wiki:Jalabhar_Xho) and giggles over [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr). She declares that she will marry Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) when she first sees the dashing lordling. After young Ser [Hugh of the Vale](wiki:Hugh_of_the_Vale) dies in the tourney, Jeyne cries hysterically and is escorted away by Septa [Mordane](wiki:Mordane) to regain her composure.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Sansa and Jeyne discuss Eddard's appointment of Lord Beric as the leader of an expedition to arrest Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane), who has been raiding the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). Sansa says Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) would have been better, but Jeyne believes Beric is as much of a hero. Sansa thinks Jeyne's crush on Beric is silly, as he is twice her age, and would consider a steward's daughter to be too far beneath him.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Ragot44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

Vayon Poole is killed in the [massacre of Lord Eddard's household](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark) following his arrest for [treason](wiki:Treason), but Jeyne is spared after [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) breaks down her door with a [warhammer](wiki:Warhammer). Ser [Boros Blount](wiki:Boros_Blount) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) brings her to the room in [Maegor's Holdfast](wiki:Maegor's_Holdfast) where [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) has been confined. Jeyne tells Sansa about the massacre, though she does not know what has happened to her father. The girls cradle each other in the same bed for comfort, and Jeyne cannot stop crying.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Ragot51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

When Sansa is called before Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) and the [small council](wiki:Small_council), she mentions that Jeyne has been confined with her. This annoys Cersei, who chides Ser Boros for not asking first, as the queen preferred Sansa ignorant of what had happened to Eddard's household. Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) says he will find a [place](wiki:Brothel) for Jeyne, not in King's Landing. Queen Cersei orders Ser Boros to take Jeyne to Lord Petyr's apartments, and assure her that Littlefinger will take her to her father. When Sansa is returned to her tower room, Jeyne and all of her things are gone.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Ragot51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

### A Clash of Kings

Sansa Stark, a prisoner of Queen Cersei Lannister in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), misses her vanished friend, Jeyne.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Sansa sings a prayer for her friends and family, the living and the dead, including Jeyne.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Storm of Swords

At [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) informs Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) that [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) has been found, and that he intends to return her to the [north](wiki:North).(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

In [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Jaime says farewell to [Steelshanks Walton](wiki:Steelshanks_Walton), Roose Bolton's [captain of the guards](wiki:Captain_of_the_guards), before he returns north. Accompanying Walton is a northern girl who claims to be Arya Stark, although Jaime does not recognize her. The girl is hollow-eyed and skinny with big brown eyes, and Jaime thinks she is older than Arya should be. She wears a grey dress, and a grey cloak with white satin trim, clasped with a [wolf](wiki:Wolf) brooch with [opal](wiki:Opal) eyes. She tells Jaime she is happy to be marrying Lord Roose Bolton's legitimized [bastard](wiki:Bastard), [Ramsay](wiki:Ramsay_Snow).(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Jaime tasks [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) with finding Sansa and bringing her to safety, as her late mother, Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), had made them swear to do. As Jaime and Brienne were also commanded by Catelyn to bring her Arya, Jaime tells Brienne that the girl being brought north by the Boltons is not Arya. He believes she is some northern girl found by his father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), and dressed up like a [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) in order to cement the [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) claim to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). Brienne is shocked, but Jaime explains that with all of Arya's kin believed dead, besides her missing sister Sansa and half-brother [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) on the [Wall](wiki:Wall), no one will be able to dispute the girl's identity. According to Jaime, the Boltons are aware the girl is a fraud.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Feast for Crows

Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) tells the [small council](wiki:Small_council) that Arya Stark will soon be marrying Ramsay Bolton. She thinks that as long as the girl plays her role well enough, the Boltons will not care that she is actually a "steward's whelp" dressed up by Petyr Baelish.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

On the way down from the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) to the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon), Sansa meets [Myranda Royce](wiki:Myranda_Royce). Myranda shares gossip with Sansa, reminding her of Jeyne.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

As "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)", Jeyne accompanies the host of Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) from the [Twins](wiki:Twins) to [siege of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin), where she meets her groom, [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow). [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy), a captive of Ramsay, recognizes Jeyne, as he grew up alongside her at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

The [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton) call upon the northern bannermen to assemble in [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) to pledge loyalty to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) and attend the wedding of "Arya" to Ramsay.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) After Roose hears that [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) has [taken Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte), he decides to move the wedding to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), both to strengthen Ramsay's claim and to goad Stannis into fighting.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Jeyne is protected by [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin), the Lady of Barrowton, during the journey from Barrowton to Winterfell.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

At [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) tells [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), the [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), she had a [vision](wiki:Vision) of a girl in grey atop a dying horse fleeing from a wedding, and they believe her to be Jon's half-sister, Arya,(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1) Melisandre sends a disguised [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) with six [spearwives](wiki:Spearwives) to find and protect the girl.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark) eventually arrives at the [Wall](wiki:Wall), fleeing a forced marriage to her cousin, [Cregan Karstark](wiki:Cregan_Karstark).(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

Mance infiltrates Winterfell as a singer named "Abel", with the spearwives disguised as washerwomen, the singer's relatives.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Rawoiafmance_rayder.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) Jeyne pleads with Theon that they should flee the castle together, but the [tortured](wiki:Torture) Theon insists that Jeyne continue impersonating Arya. Theon gives away "Arya" during [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark) in the [godswood of Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell). Theon considers cutting Jeyne's throat to end her misery, or killing Ramsay, but he is too fearful to do so.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6) Ramsay has Theon strip Jeyne for the [bedding](wiki:Bedding), and Theon sees numerous scars covering Jeyne's back where she had been [whipped](wiki:Whipping). Ramsay asks Jeyne if she knows how to please a man, and Jeyne replies fearfully that she has been trained, implying that she had been kept in one of Petyr Baelish's [brothels](wiki:Brothel). Ramsay then forces Theon to prepare Jeyne sexually for him before he rapes her.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7)

Jeyne spends all her time confined to Ramsay's bedchamber in the [Great Keep](wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell)), and she is deeply traumatized by the things Ramsay does to her. The maids who bathe her notice bruises on her body, and the girl's sobbing is heard throughout Winterfell. Theon sometimes brings her hot water for her baths.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) According to Lady Dustin, the crying of "Arya Stark" is more a danger to the Boltons than the army of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon). The [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) may not care, but the other northern houses who had served [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) for generations do, and morale is being damaged from hearing [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s "daughter" cry. Theon thinks that Barbrey is suspicious of "Arya's" identity.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Although Stannis's host is slowed by a blizzard during his [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell), the [northern mountain clansmen](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans) are motivated to rescue "Ned's girl".(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

Abel's washerwomen convince Theon to help them rescue "Arya",(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) but Theon keeps Jeyne's true identity a secret from Abel.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1) The tension in Winterfell turns to chaos after the death of [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)), and the spearwives take the opportunity to begin their attempt to rescue "Arya". The plan is to dress her up in [Squirrel](wiki:Squirrel)'s clothes, while the real Squirrel climbs away down the wall. Theon and the "washerwomen" approach Jeyne's room with water for a bath, but she is terrified to leave, thinking this is a trap set by Ramsay to test her love. Theon finally convinces Jeyne the escape is for real, and helps dress her, though the spearwives are horrified by what Ramsay has done to her body. They get her into Squirrel's clothes, and Theon tells Jeyne to follow [Holly](wiki:Holly) closely, and not to run. As they are escaping, [Frenya](wiki:Frenya) kills one of the [Bolton men](wiki:House_Bolton_guards) and Jeyne screams, which alerts the other guards. Jeyne and Theon reach the top of the walls, only to realize that the rope has been left with Frenya, who is fighting down below. Guards are shooting arrows at them, and Theon knows he and Jeyne will be returned to Ramsay if they are caught. Theon grabs Jeyne and jumps from the battlements.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-2)

The two survive due to landing in deep snow, although Theon lands on top of Jeyne and breaks some of her ribs.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btheon_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctheon_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) Jeyne and Theon are discovered by [Mors Umber](wiki:Mors_Umber), who has [Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris) of the [Iron Bank](wiki:Iron_Bank_of_Braavos) bring the escapees to Stannis's camp in the [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village).(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) Jeyne suffers from frostbite on her nose.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-1)

At Castle Black, Lord Commander Jon Snow receives [a letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) in which Ramsay claims that Mance has been captured and his spearwives slain. Ramsay demands that his bride be returned to him. A number of [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) volunteer to march with Jon against the Bastard of Bolton, but then he is stabbed in the [mutiny at Castle Black](wiki:Mutiny_at_Castle_Black).(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Theon, now a captive of Stannis, recalls that after he and Jeyne were rescued by [Mors Umber](wiki:Mors_Umber) outside [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), Crowfood asked Jeyne questions about the castle to confirm her identity, such as the names of servants, including [the cook](wiki:Gage) and [the smith](wiki:Mikken). Having lived at Winterfell, Jeyne was able to answer these questions to Mors's satisfaction. Theon remembers that as he carried Jeyne on their trek to Stannis's camp, he told her that she must still pretend to be Arya, otherwise she will be nothing to them but a [whore](wiki:Whore). It is feared that Jeyne will lose a bit of her nose to the frostbite, but Theon recalls assuring her that nobody will care what Arya looks like, every man will still want to marry her.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btheon_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctheon_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-1)

Stannis decides to send "Arya" to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) to reunite her with her brother, [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow). Theon thinks that Jon will see through Jeyne's disguise in a moment, but he does not say so. Stannis tasks Ser [Justin Massey](wiki:Justin_Massey) to bring Jeyne to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) with an escort of six men, along with a female companion, [Alysane Mormont](wiki:Alysane_Mormont). Justin and Tycho are then to continue on to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btheon_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctheon_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-2)

At the Gates of the Moon, Sansa remembers how she used to dash through Winterfell with Jeyne, with Arya running after them trying to keep up.(wiki:Jeyne_Poole.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Balayne_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Calayne_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)
