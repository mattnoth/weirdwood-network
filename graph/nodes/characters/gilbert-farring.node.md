## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) tells [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) that Ser Gilbert is one of the few men who remained loyal to him after [his loss](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater) at the [Blackwater](wiki:Blackwater_Rush). Gilbert still holds [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) for Stannis, commanding a garrison of two hundred men.(wiki:Gilbert_Farring.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Gilbert continues to hold Storm's End for Stannis as his [castellan](wiki:Castellan), with Lord [Elwood Meadows](wiki:Elwood_Meadows) as his second in command, and Maester [Jurne](wiki:Jurne) as his counselor and healer.(wiki:Gilbert_Farring.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

While Stannis is in the [north](wiki:North), an army from the [Reach](wiki:Reach) under Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) and Lord [Mathis Rowan](wiki:Mathis_Rowan) [besiege Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(300)).(wiki:Gilbert_Farring.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Gilbert_Farring.cite_ref-Rawoiafmathis_rowan.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) However, when Mace receives news that his daughter [Margaery](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) has been arrested by the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), he leaves the siege and marches his army back to King's Landing, leaving Lord Rowan to continue the siege with fewer men.(wiki:Gilbert_Farring.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

After [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) and [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)) take [Griffin's Roost](wiki:Griffin's_Roost) with the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company), they plan to attack Storm's End next.(wiki:Gilbert_Farring.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### The Winds of Winter

[Haldon Halfmaester](wiki:Haldon_Halfmaester) informs [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) that the Golden Company has taken [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), and that Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell)'s army is descending on the castle from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Gilbert_Farring.cite_ref-8)
