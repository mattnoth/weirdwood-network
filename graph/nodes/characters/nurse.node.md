## Appearances & Description

Nurse has a long narrow face, and a chin beard bound with gold wire. His red and black [Ghiscari](wiki:Ghiscari) hair is shaped into the form of a pair of taloned hands.(wiki:Nurse.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

When [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), [Penny](wiki:Penny), and [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) are purchased by Yezzan for his [grotesquerie](wiki:Grotesquerie), Nurse is put in charge of his master's new property, instructing them on the rules, disciplining them when necessary, and informing them they should treat Yezzan as if he were their own father.(wiki:Nurse.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Nurse eventually succumbs to the [pale mare](wiki:Pale_mare). However, Tyrion is implied to have been the actual culprit of Nurse's death by [poisoning](wiki:Poison) the overseer. When Nurse falls ill, it falls to Tyrion to act as his caretaker. Tyrion prepares a soup of dogtail "with slivers of mushroom in the broth,"(wiki:Nurse.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) - seemingly [the poisonous mushrooms](wiki:Tyrion's_mushrooms) Tyrion had taken from the manse of [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis). Nurse's last word is "*No*," while Tyrion's last words to Nurse are "*A Lannister always pays his debts*." (wiki:Nurse.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
