## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Albar is present with his father, Lord [Nestor Royce](wiki:Nestor_Royce), at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) when [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) demands [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat). Albar offers himself as a champion for Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn), but she selects Ser [Vardis Egen](wiki:Vardis_Egen) instead.(wiki:Albar_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

### A Feast for Crows

Albar and Nestor travel to the Eyrie to hear [Marillion](wiki:Marillion) confess that he killed Lysa. Albar does not like Marillion, as the singer made a song about pigs eating a [falcon](wiki:Falcon)'s droppings. As the arms of [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) displays a falcon and Albar and his father were in service to House Arryn, they consider the song insulting.(wiki:Albar_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) gains their support by granting the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) to Nestor, claiming it had been Lysa's intention to do so before her passing. After the Royces depart, Petyr informs [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) that Lysa had considered Nestor to be a rock and Albar a clod.(wiki:Albar_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6)

### The Winds of Winter

Disguised as "Alayne Stone", Sansa dances with Albar at the Gates of the Moon.(wiki:Albar_Royce.cite_ref-twow_4-1)
