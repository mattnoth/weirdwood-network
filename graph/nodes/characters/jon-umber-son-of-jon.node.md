## Appearances & Description

Despite his nickname, Smalljon Umber is nearly as tall as his father, the [Greatjon](wiki:Jon_Umber), and might become taller still. Like his father, he is strongly built.(wiki:Jon_Umber_(son_of_Jon).cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Smalljon Umber is one of the [thirty highborn warriors](wiki:Robb_Stark's_personal_guard) who form [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s personal guard. He fights with this unit during the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Jon_Umber_(son_of_Jon).cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Storm of Swords

The Smalljon accompanies Robb during his invasion of the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands). At the [storming of the Crag](wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag), he and [Black Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Ryman)) scale the walls while Robb attacks the main gate.(wiki:Jon_Umber_(son_of_Jon).cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

The Smalljon guards Robb at the [Twins](wiki:Twins) during the [wedding](wiki:Customs) of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) and Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey)'s daughter, [Roslin](wiki:Roslin_Frey). During the massacre that follows the wedding, the Smalljon throws a table over Robb, the wounded [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), to block the crossbow bolts that rain down on them. He is wounded by a crossbowman while running for his swordbelt, going to his knees. The Smalljon is killed in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) when men wearing mail and shaggy furs(wiki:Jon_Umber_(son_of_Jon).cite_ref-6) pour into the hall, and one beheads him with two swings of his axe.(wiki:Jon_Umber_(son_of_Jon).cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
