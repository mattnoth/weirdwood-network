## Origins

Lord Follard was one of the six [lords](wiki:Lord) who accompanied Lord [Manfryd Mooton](wiki:Manfryd_Mooton) to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC). As the Crown's envoy, Manfryd was tasked to make peace with the [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos) after the recent [conflict](wiki:Lord_Alyn's_attack) between the [Free City](wiki:Free_City) and Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon). While Manfryd treated with the Sealord, Lord Follard spread bribes among the Braavosi [keyholders](wiki:Keyholders), [magisters](wiki:Magisters), and other notables. Rather than return to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), he chose to remain in Braavos with a [courtesan](wiki:Courtesan) he had become enamored with.(wiki:Lord_Follard.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
