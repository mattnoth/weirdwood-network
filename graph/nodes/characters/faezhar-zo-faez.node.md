## Appearances & Description

Faezhar is a small man with a receding chin. Two of his front teeth are too big to fit in his mouth,(wiki:Faezhar_zo_Faez.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) so he is compared to a [rabbit](wiki:Rabbit).

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

The men of the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown), a [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) company hired by [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai), mock Faezhar as "the Rabbit".(wiki:Faezhar_zo_Faez.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Faezhar, along with his other two supreme commanders and [Bloodbeard](wiki:Bloodbeard), presents the head of captain [Groleo](wiki:Groleo) to [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) and demands the deaths of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s [dragons](wiki:Dragon) in return for the release of the other hostages, [Hero](wiki:Hero), [Daario Naharis](wiki:Daario_Naharis), and [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo).(wiki:Faezhar_zo_Faez.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Following the death of [Yurkhaz zo Yunzak](wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak) in the [fighting pits of Meereen](wiki:Fighting_pits_of_Meereen), the Yunkai'i started daily rotating the duty of supreme commander. Faezhar is among the men who take part in the rotation.(wiki:Faezhar_zo_Faez.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
