## Origins

Bloodbeard and the [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince), commander of the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown), despise each other, as the Windblown and the [Company of the Cat](wiki:Company_of_the_Cat) have fought on opposing sides in the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands) in the recent past.(wiki:Bloodbeard.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Bloodbeard.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Appearances & Description

Bloodbeard is a huge man with a great bush of a beard with fiery red whiskers,(wiki:Bloodbeard.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and long braids.(wiki:Bloodbeard.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He is described as a savage commander, with a ferocious appetite for slaughter, and no taste for peace. At table he is raucous, farts loudly, and enjoys both [wine](wiki:Wine) and women abundantly.(wiki:Bloodbeard.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Bloodbeard.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Bloodbeard accepts [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai)'s contract and is present at the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor) and later the [siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen).(wiki:Bloodbeard.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Bloodbeard is disappointed when peace is declared after Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) marries [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq), as he was looking forward to sacking [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). Daenerys observes Bloodbeard during the festivity in the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) hall celebrating peace with the Yunkai.(wiki:Bloodbeard.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Bloodbeard is present at the re-opening ceremonies of the [fighting pits of Meereen](wiki:Fighting_pits_of_Meereen), where Daenerys flies away on [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) after the [dragon](wiki:Dragon) lands in [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit).(wiki:Bloodbeard.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

The deaths of those in the pit who panicked when the dragon arrived, as well as Daenerys's disappearance, restarts hostilities between Meereen and the Yunkai. [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), while a [slave](wiki:Slave) of [Yezzan zo Qaggaz](wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz), overhears Bloodbeard recommend they send the [hostages](wiki:Hostage) Daenerys sent to the Yunkai back to Meereen via [trebuchet](wiki:Trebuchet), but Yezzan disagrees.(wiki:Bloodbeard.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) However, after Yezzan dies of the [pale mare](wiki:Pale_mare), Bloodbeard and the new commanders of the Yunkai army present the head of Admiral [Groleo](wiki:Groleo), one of the hostages, to Hizdahr, in retaliation for the death of the Yunkai commander [Yurkhaz zo Yunzak](wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak), who was trampled by the mob when Drogon landed in the pit. Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) thinks other kings he had served would order Bloodbeard's arrest or execution, but Hizdahr does nothing in reaction to Bloodbeard's provocations but says he will consult with his council.(wiki:Bloodbeard.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

The [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince) later tells Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) that Bloodbeard is urging the Yunkai to send Hizdahr another of the hostages' heads.(wiki:Bloodbeard.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) However, [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) tells Barristan that the hostages who were Hizdahr's [Loraq](wiki:Loraq) kin were returned unharmed, and that Groleo's execution for Yurkhaz's death was a sham, to give Hizdahr a pretext to kill Daenerys's other two dragons.(wiki:Bloodbeard.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
