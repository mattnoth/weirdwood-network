## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Pinchface Jon brings a dozen longships in support of [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) after he declares himself [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North).(wiki:Jon_Myre.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) He supports Euron at the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), where he trades insults with [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy).(wiki:Jon_Myre.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Jon feasts in the ruins of a castle near the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor) with some of Euron's men, and he takes part in mocking [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) when the latter is brought up from the hold of the *[Silence](wiki:Silence)*.(wiki:Jon_Myre.cite_ref-4)
