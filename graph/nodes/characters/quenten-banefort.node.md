## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord Quenten is a member of the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) host besieging [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun). He is taken prisoner at the [Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_of_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Quenten_Banefort.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Lord Banefort is a captive of Lord [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken) at [Stone Hedge](wiki:Stone_Hedge).(wiki:Quenten_Banefort.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Feast for Crows

At some point Lord Quenten has been released from captivity since he is among the gathered nobility at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) that escort the corpse of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) back to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock). Unlike Lord [Antario Jast](wiki:Antario_Jast), Quenten dealt with his imprisonment well and looks ready to return to battle. Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) thinks Lord Banefort could deal with the outlaws [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) and [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion), but he would not be a good [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:Quenten_Banefort.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
