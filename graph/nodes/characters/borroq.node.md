## Origins

Borroq was one of those present at a meeting of skinchangers that [Haggon](wiki:Haggon) took [Varamyr](wiki:Varamyr) to in his youth.(wiki:Borroq.cite_ref-Radwd0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Borroq](wiki:Category:Images_of_Borroq)*

Borroq is hulking, black browed, with a flat nose, heavy jowls dark with stubble, and small black close-set eyes.(wiki:Borroq.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Reflecting on an encounter with Borroq in his youth, [Varamyr](wiki:Varamyr) describes him as having looked so much like his boar, that all he lacked were tusks.(wiki:Borroq.cite_ref-Radwd0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Borroq's boar is as large as a bull, covered in coarse black hair, with tusks as long as a man's arm(wiki:Borroq.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) or a sword.(wiki:Borroq.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Borroq and his boar are amongst [Tormund](wiki:Tormund)'s group which crosses the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Borroq.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Because his boar does not get along with the [Ghost](wiki:Ghost), the [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) of [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), Borroq takes up residence in an ancient tomb near [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black)'s lichyard, where the boar roots amongst the graves.(wiki:Borroq.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Jon, the [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), intends to have Borroq eventually accompany [Soren Shieldbreaker](wiki:Soren_Shieldbreaker) to [Stonedoor](wiki:Stonedoor). Borroq attends Jon's speech in the [Shieldhall](wiki:Shieldhall).(wiki:Borroq.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
