## Origins

After the passing of the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon) known as the "[High Lickspittle](wiki:High_Lickspittle)" in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) traveled to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), hoping to influence the election of the next High Septon. It was their wish that the High Lickspittle's successor would be a supporter of the [Doctrine of Exceptionalism](wiki:Doctrine_of_Exceptionalism). To this end, Jaehaerys met with Lord [Donnel Hightower](wiki:Donnel_Hightower), whose agents were present in the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) at the election of the new High Septon. Donnel wished for either his younger brother, a nephew, or a cousin, who all were members of the [Most Devout](wiki:Most_Devout), to be elected to the office. However, as none of them had yet spoken in favor of the Doctrine, Jaehaerys and Donnel eventually reached an understanding which resulted in the election of Septon [Alfyn](wiki:Alfyn_(septon)) to the office. Jaehaerys assured Donnel that after Alfyn's passing, Donnel's kin would succeed to the crystal crown.
