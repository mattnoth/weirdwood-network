## Origins

Ser Steffon was a [knight](wiki:Knight) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) during the reign of King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Steffon_Darklyn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Ser [Harrold Darke](wiki:Harrold_Darke), a distant kinsman of the Darklyns of [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale), was once his squire.(wiki:Steffon_Darklyn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

When the king died in his sleep, Steffon was one of the five knights of the Kingsguard in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), the other two with Viserys' chosen heir and daughter Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). Upon learning of the king's death, Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) dispatched Steffon and the other Kingsguard knights to rouse the members of the [small council](wiki:Small_council), intent on crowning Princess Rhaenyra's half-brother as King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), usurping the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Steffon_Darklyn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) However, [House Darklyn](wiki:House_Darklyn) were among Rhaenyra's greatest supporters;(wiki:Steffon_Darklyn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) under the cover of darkness during the night, Steffon slipped away from the capital to attend Rhaenyra at Dragonstone with his squire, two stewards, four guardsmen, and Viserys' [crown](wiki:Crown), fleeing out a postern gate to a fisherman's skiff to cross the [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay).(wiki:Steffon_Darklyn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

The arrival of Steffon at Dragonstone was an occasion of much joy among Rhaenyra's supporters, especially when it was learned that he had brought her father's crown with him, allowing for a hasty coronation among her supporters.(wiki:Steffon_Darklyn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) He served as the first [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard) of Rhaenyra's [Queensguard](wiki:Queensguard). When Prince [Jacaerys Velaryon](wiki:Jacaerys_Velaryon) called for [dragonriders](wiki:Dragonrider), Steffon made an attempt to mount [Seasmoke](wiki:Seasmoke) during the [Sowing](wiki:Sowing), but he was burned to death by the [dragon](wiki:Dragon).(wiki:Steffon_Darklyn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) He was succeeded as Lord Commander by Ser [Lorent Marbrand](wiki:Lorent_Marbrand).(wiki:Steffon_Darklyn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
