## Appearances & Description

Gerold is in his late twenties.(wiki:Gerold_Dayne.cite_ref-Rawoiafgerold_dayne.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) A handsome man, he has a clean-shaven face with an aquiline nose, high cheekbones, dark [purple eyes](wiki:Purple_eyes), and a strong jaw. Gerold has collar-length thick silver hair, divided by a streak of midnight black.(wiki:Gerold_Dayne.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Despite his beauty and valor, Gerold is a cruel man with angry eyes. He is jealous of his famous cousin, the late Ser [Arthur Dayne](wiki:Arthur_Dayne), who was the [Sword of the Morning](wiki:Sword_of_the_Morning). Darkstar wields a [longsword](wiki:Longsword). He prefers to drink unsweetened [lemonwater](wiki:Lemon) instead of [summerwine](wiki:Wine).(wiki:Gerold_Dayne.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Gerold allies himself with Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) when she attempts to crown Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) in place of her younger brother, King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon). At [Shandystone](wiki:Shandystone), Arianne admits to her other conspirators that she only included Darkstar for his fighting prowess and his keep, [High Hermitage](wiki:High_Hermitage). Gerold is skeptical of Arianne's plan to provoke [Queen Regent](wiki:Queen_Regent) [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister).(wiki:Gerold_Dayne.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

Arianne's party is surprised at the [Greenblood](wiki:Greenblood) by [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah), who orders them to surrender. Gerold agrees since they are outnumbered, but Myrcella's bodygyard, Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), rides to his death in an attack on Areo. Amidst the fighting, Gerold attempts to kill Myrcella.(wiki:Gerold_Dayne.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) Her horse rears, however, and Darkstar succeeds only in slicing off her ear and scarring her face.(wiki:Gerold_Dayne.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4) He escapes Areo's guards by riding into the sands of Dorne.(wiki:Gerold_Dayne.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Myrcella is taken to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear), where Maester [Caleotte](wiki:Caleotte) saves her life. Arianne ponders whether her plan had been betrayed by Darkstar or by another of her conspirators.(wiki:Gerold_Dayne.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) arrives at Sunspear with the [Mountain's skull](wiki:Mountain's_skull). Under Arianne's instruction, Myrcella has blamed Darkstar, not Areo, for the death of Arys.(wiki:Gerold_Dayne.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Gerold_Dayne.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Arianne's father [Doran](wiki:Doran_Martell), the [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), sends [Obara Sand](wiki:Obara_Sand) with Balon to hunt Gerold at [High Hermitage](wiki:High_Hermitage).(wiki:Gerold_Dayne.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)(wiki:Gerold_Dayne.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Cersei thinks that Darkstar is a catspaw of her brother, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), whom she already blames for the deaths of her first son, King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), and her father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister).(wiki:Gerold_Dayne.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

### The Winds of Winter

While traveling to the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands), Arianne and Ser [Daemon Sand](wiki:Daemon_Sand) discuss Gerold. Arianne feels that Obara and Areo can kill Darkstar, although Daemon is skeptical. Arianne is regretful for ever trusting Gerold, thinking that he was so handsome she had not believed half the stories told about him.(wiki:Gerold_Dayne.cite_ref-arianne1_10-0)
