## Identity

Varys, also known as the Spider, is the master of whisperers on the small council of King Robert I Baratheon, a position he previously held for King Aerys II Targaryen. He is a eunuch originally from Lys (wiki:Varys, cite: AGOT).

## Origins

Born as a slave in Lys (wiki:Varys, cite: ASOS). Apprenticed as an orphan to a troupe of mummers who worked the Free Cities, Oldtown, and occasionally King's Landing. In Myr, a sorcerer purchased him, drugged him, and cut off his manhood, burning it in a brazier in a blood magic ritual. A voice emerged from the flames in a language Varys did not understand. Cast out to die, Varys survived through begging, prostitution, and thievery, eventually becoming the best thief in Myr (wiki:Varys, cite: ACOK, ASOS).

## Appearances & Description

Varys is a plump, bald eunuch with soft hands and round shoulders. He powders his face, uses perfumes of lavender, lilac, and rosewater, and wears rich silk, velvet, and damasks at court. His pointed velvet slippers allow him to move silently. His outward manner is effeminate, obsequious, and mysterious. As a trained mummer, he is a master of disguise who can alter his voice, gait, smell, and overall appearance. He operates a spy network of children called "little birds" in Westeros and "little mice" in Pentos (wiki:Varys, cite: AGOT, ACOK, ASOS).

## Narrative Arc

Impoverished in Pentos, Varys partnered with the bravo Illyrio Mopatis in an information trade that made them both rich. His fame reached King Aerys II, who brought him to court as spymaster. Varys fed Aerys's paranoia, alerting him to potential traitors and warning him about Prince Rhaegar's possible conspiracy at the tourney at Harrenhal. After Robert's Rebellion, Varys was pardoned and retained as master of whisperers under King Robert (wiki:Varys, cite: ASOS, AGOT).

During AGOT, Varys warned Eddard Stark that Queen Cersei was trying to kill King Robert and revealed Jon Arryn was poisoned with the tears of Lys. Arya overheard Varys meeting with Illyrio in the Red Keep's depths, discussing delaying a war between Stark and Lannister. After Eddard's arrest, Varys visited him in the black cells disguised as a gaoler. During ACOK, Varys served under Tyrion Lannister as Hand, providing intelligence and managing the secret tunnels of the Red Keep. In ASOS, Varys helped Tyrion navigate court politics and later assisted Jaime Lannister in freeing Tyrion from the black cells, after which Tyrion killed Tywin Lannister. Varys then vanished from King's Landing (wiki:Varys, cite: AGOT, ACOK, ASOS).

During AFFC, Varys's disappearance was noted and Cersei offered a lordship for his capture. In ADWD, Varys revealed himself to have been hiding in the Red Keep's secret passages. In the epilogue, he murdered Grand Maester Pycelle and Ser Kevan Lannister with a crossbow, explaining he acted to prevent Kevan from undoing the chaos Cersei had created — chaos needed for Aegon VI Targaryen's return to succeed (wiki:Varys, cite: AFFC, ADWD).
