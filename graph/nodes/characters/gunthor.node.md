## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Gunthor is one of the mountain clansmen whom [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) encounter in the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon). Gunthor is wary of the [lowlanders](wiki:Lowlander) and is ready to have his [Stone Crows](wiki:Stone_Crows) kill Bronn and enslave Tyrion, but Tyrion gains the clansmen as allies by promising them the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn).(wiki:Gunthor.cite_ref-Ragot42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

While other clansmen leave the mountains with Tyrion, Gunthor stays behind to raise the other clans.(wiki:Gunthor.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Storm of Swords

When [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) and [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) attempt to enter the Mountains of the Moon, they are warned off by the locals, who explain that some mountain clans have become bolder after returning from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Gunthor is mentioning as having led Stone Crows in raiding a village half a year ago, killing half the men, kidnapping every woman, and stealing all the grain.(wiki:Gunthor.cite_ref-Rasos65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
