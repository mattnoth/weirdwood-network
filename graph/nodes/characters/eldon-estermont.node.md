## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

At the onset of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Ser Eldon supports [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon).(wiki:Eldon_Estermont.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) After Renly's death, [Lord Estermont](wiki:Lord_Estermont) goes over to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s side(wiki:Eldon_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and the [Estermonts](wiki:House_Estermont) fight for him in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Eldon_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Following the defeat at the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush), Ser Eldon is either captured or changes sides and is among the Estermonts who swear fealty to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Eldon_Estermont.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Eldon is now [Lord of Greenstone](wiki:Lord_of_Greenstone).(wiki:Eldon_Estermont.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) As her punishment by [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), for having conspired with Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) to place Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) on the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), [Sylva Santagar](wiki:Sylva_Santagar) is sent to [Greenstone](wiki:Greenstone) and quickly wed to the much older Eldon.(wiki:Eldon_Estermont.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Arianne, unaware of Sylva's marriage while imprisoned at [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear), worries that her father Doran might force her to wed Lord Estermont.(wiki:Eldon_Estermont.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser [Marq Mandrake](wiki:Marq_Mandrake) [takes](wiki:Taking_of_Greenstone) the island of [Estermont](wiki:Estermont) during the [landing of the Golden Company](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company).(wiki:Eldon_Estermont.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Eldon_Estermont.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Eldon_Estermont.cite_ref-Rawoiafestermont.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
