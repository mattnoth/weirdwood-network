## Origins

At the [tourney at Lannisport](wiki:Tourney_at_Lannisport) celebrating the victory of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) over [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) and [his rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy_Rebellion), Ser Lyle was unhorsed by the eventual champion, Lord [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont).(wiki:Lyle_Crakehall.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Appearances & Description

Lyle is big and strong with a booming voice.(wiki:Lyle_Crakehall.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Lyle_Crakehall.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) regards him as one of the strongest living men in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros),(wiki:Lyle_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) but slower and less savage than [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane).(wiki:Lyle_Crakehall.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) considers Lyle an oaf of a knight reminiscent of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Lyle_Crakehall.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser Lyle is part of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s army in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). He is captured at the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords).(wiki:Lyle_Crakehall.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Lyle is held captive at [Pinkmaiden Castle](wiki:Pinkmaiden_Castle).(wiki:Lyle_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Ser Lyle attends the wedding of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell). When Lyle booms that [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) has two queens now and that the young one is as beautiful as the old one, Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) feels like slapping him.(wiki:Lyle_Crakehall.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Lyle remains in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) after the wedding. Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) spots him training at [quintain](wiki:Quintain) and cracking its shield in the yard of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Lyle_Crakehall.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Lyle is a part of the force led by Jaime to resolve the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun). During the party's stop at [Hayford](wiki:Hayford), Lyle is among those invited to the supper within the castle. He discusses ancestry at [Sow's Horn](wiki:Sow's_Horn) with Ser [Roger Hogg](wiki:Roger_Hogg), thinking that Roger might be related to [House Crakehall](wiki:House_Crakehall). At [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) Lyle tells [Pia](wiki:Pia) that she will now be protected, and he spars with Ser [Flement Brax](wiki:Flement_Brax).(wiki:Lyle_Crakehall.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

While supping at [Darry](wiki:Darry), Lyle offers his opinions about [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) who lie to their lords. He pledges to [Mariya Darry](wiki:Mariya_Darry) and [Amerei Frey](wiki:Amerei_Frey) he will return to Darry once the siege at Riverrun is over to kill [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane).(wiki:Lyle_Crakehall.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Strongboar is outraged when Jaime's host sees hanged foragers near [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Lyle_Crakehall.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) During the war council, Lyle offers to lead an assault on Riverrun's walls. Jaime instead negotiates with the captive Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully).(wiki:Lyle_Crakehall.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) After Riverrun falls, Lyle informs Jaime that he intends to return to Darry and search for the [Hound](wiki:Hound), whom he still believes to be Sandor. Strongboar departs with his squire, his men-at-arms, and Ser [Jon Bettley](wiki:Jon_Bettley).(wiki:Lyle_Crakehall.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
