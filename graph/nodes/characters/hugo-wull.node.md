## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), the [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), suggests to King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) that he visit Hugo and [Torghen Flint](wiki:Torghen_Flint) to win the allegiance of the [northern mountain clans](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans), instead of marching on the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort).(wiki:Hugo_Wull.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Big Bucket and other clan chiefs swear allegiance to Stannis when the king marches to the [northern mountains](wiki:Northern_mountains) from [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black). The chiefs insist that Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) cannot be suffered to hold [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) and that "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)" (actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)) needs to be rescued from Roose's [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son, [Ramsay](wiki:Ramsay_Snow).(wiki:Hugo_Wull.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Big Bucket joins Stannis's [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell) from [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Hugo_Wull.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) Many of Stannis's [southron](wiki:Southron) [knights](wiki:Knight) are fearful of winter, but the hill clansmen are better prepared for the harsh weather.(wiki:Hugo_Wull.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)(wiki:Hugo_Wull.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
