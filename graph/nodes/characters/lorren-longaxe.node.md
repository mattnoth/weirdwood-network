Lorren wields a longaxe and wears mail.(wiki:Lorren_Longaxe.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Lorren sails to [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) with [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) as she contemplates her next move after the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).

When five [northmen](wiki:Northmen) try to infiltrate Deepwood, Lorren kills one of them with his longaxe. Asha has Lorren and [Six-Toed Harl](wiki:Six-Toed_Harl) fetch Lady [Sybelle Glover](wiki:Sybelle_Glover) and [her maester](wiki:Galbart_Glover's_maester); Lorren strikes the maester with a mailed fist when he protests, breaking his nose. During the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte), Asha spots Lorren on the ground of the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) with one leg twisted beneath him.(wiki:Lorren_Longaxe.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
