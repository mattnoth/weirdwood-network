## Origins

Ser [Kyle the Cat](wiki:Kyle_the_Cat), a [hedge knight](wiki:Hedge_knight), once served Lord [Armond Caswell](wiki:Armond_Caswell) as a [sworn sword](wiki:Sworn_sword). He made the first [sword](wiki:Sword) for three-year-old Joffrey, carving a small wooden sword out of [pine](wiki:Pine) to fit his hand.(wiki:Joffrey_Caswell.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Lord Joffrey attended the [wedding tourney](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls) at [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) in [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), and he fell asleep in his [wine](wiki:Wine) at the [wedding](wiki:Wedding) feast. The morning after, he faced Ser Kyle in his first joust of the [tourney](wiki:Tourney). The hedge knight planned to throw the match in order to compliment Joffrey on his growth as a warrior and then become a Caswell man again. Though Joffrey's [horse](wiki:Horse) was slow, his lance was wobbly, and he barely struck Kyle's shoulder, the Cat fell off his horse into the dirt. Joffrey excitedly pumped his lance in the air after his defeat of Kyle.(wiki:Joffrey_Caswell.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

When Kyle came to Joffrey to pay the forfeit, however, and told him about how he had once made his first sword, Joffrey did not recognize him. Lord Caswell said there was no place at Bitterbridge for [knights](wiki:Knight) as feeble as Kyle the Cat had shown himself to be. He still took Kyle's arms, armor, and horse, since Kyle could not pay the ransom for them.(wiki:Joffrey_Caswell.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

While investigating the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion), Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) found an inebriated Joffrey among a group of men leaving the great hall of Whitewalls after a feast.(wiki:Joffrey_Caswell.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6)

## Appearances & Description

When he was twenty, Joffrey was thin, a poor rider, and even worse lance. He liked drinking [wine](wiki:Wine).(wiki:Joffrey_Caswell.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

The [centaur](wiki:Centaur) of [House Caswell](wiki:House_Caswell) was depicted on Joffrey's shield and on the white silk trappings of his horse. He also had a centaur of yellow [gold](wiki:Gold) atop his helm.(wiki:Joffrey_Caswell.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
