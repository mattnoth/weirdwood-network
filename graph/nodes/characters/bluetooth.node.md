## Appearances & Description

Bluetooth dresses in a [bearskin](wiki:Bear) vest and wears a helmet with [raven](wiki:Raven) wings.(wiki:Bluetooth.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Theon](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) and [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) meet Bluetooth in [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport) while riding towards [Pyke](wiki:Pyke). He tells them that his wife is about to give birth to twins. Theon thinks about offering Bluetooth a job on his new longship, *[Sea Bitch](wiki:Sea_Bitch)*, but he reconsiders when Asha tells him that Bluetooth has his own longship and that it would only insult him.(wiki:Bluetooth.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
