## Origins

After the death of Queen [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Moriah was presented to King Aegon III as a potential bride at the [Maiden's Day Ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball).(wiki:Moriah_Qorgyle.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

According to [Archmaester](wiki:Archmaester) [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn), she had the boldest tongue present, as she requested that the king come down from the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) and kiss her.
