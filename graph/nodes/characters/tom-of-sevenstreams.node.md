## Origins

Tom comes from [Sevenstreams](wiki:Sevenstreams) in the northern [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) He claims to have fathered at least seven sons long ago and claims they all have beautiful voices.(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) One of his sons supposedly resides at the [Peach](wiki:Peach) in [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept), though Tom does not claim him.(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

[House Tully](wiki:House_Tully) has never been good to Tom. Tom once ran afoul of [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) when he stole a girl that Edmure was supposed to bed to take his virginity. Edmure had drunk too much and was unable to perform. Tom then made a song about a floppy fish, which led to Edmure's hatred of music.(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) Edmure's sister, Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn), once sent Tom up the [high road](wiki:High_road), where the [Vale mountain clans](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) took his gold, horse, and clothes. He walked up to the [Bloody Gate](wiki:Bloody_Gate) with nothing but his harp. There the guards made him sing "[The Name Day Boy](wiki:The_Name_Day_Boy)" and "[The King Without Courage](wiki:The_King_Without_Courage)" before letting him pass.(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Tom sang at the wedding of his cousin, Ser [Pate of the Blue Fork](wiki:Pate_of_the_Blue_Fork), to [Amerei Frey](wiki:Amerei_Frey), though his cousin never paid him.(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)

Tom once seduced Lady [Ravella Smallwood](wiki:Ravella_Smallwood) with the song, "[Let Me Drink Your Beauty](wiki:Let_Me_Drink_Your_Beauty)".(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3)

## Appearances & Description

Tom looks around fifty years old. He is small and trim, with a thin, foxy appearance. He has thinning brown hair, a sharp nose, and a wide smile.(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He wears patched, faded greens(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) and wields a woodman's axe and throwing knives.(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) He sometimes wears a hat.(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Tom is a singer and plays a [woodharp](wiki:Harp), which he occasionally plucks when he talks.(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He has a dubious reputation.(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Tom often half sings his speech(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) and makes up songs.(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) Tom's voice is less harsh than [Lem](wiki:Lem)'s(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) and he claims to know more bawdy songs than [Whitesmile Wat](wiki:Whitesmile_Wat).(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Tom sings "[Off to Gulltown](wiki:Off_to_Gulltown)" as he and his companions, [Lem](wiki:Lem) and [Anguy](wiki:Anguy), stumble upon [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), [Gendry](wiki:Gendry), and [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie) digging in a garden. There, after a display of archery from Anguy, they force the three youths to accompany them. Tom sings "[The Bear and the Maiden Fair](wiki:The_Bear_and_the_Maiden_Fair)" during the journey.(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) At the [Inn of the Kneeling Man](wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man), [Husband](wiki:Husband) accuses Tom of impregnating [Fern](wiki:Fern), but the singer explains the girl was interested in Anguy.(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

At [Sallydance](wiki:Sallydance) Tom sings a silly song about [Big Belly Ben](wiki:Big_Belly_Ben) and the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon)'s goose.(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-4) Tom tells Arya about [High Heart](wiki:High_Heart), and its [dwarf woman](wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart) has him sing her [a song](wiki:Jenny's_song) about [Jenny of Oldstones](wiki:Jenny_of_Oldstones).(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-5) Arya's group meets Tom's one-time lover, Lady [Ravella Smallwood](wiki:Ravella_Smallwood), at [Acorn Hall](wiki:Acorn_Hall). She accuses him of seducing milkmaids with the song, "[Oh, Lay My Sweet Lass Down in the Grass](wiki:Oh,_Lay_My_Sweet_Lass_Down_in_the_Grass)". Tom sings [another love song](wiki:Oh,_Lay_My_Sweet_Lass_Down_in_the_Grass) after Arya and Gendry return from the castle's forge.(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-6) He later performs "[Six Maids in a Pool](wiki:Six_Maids_in_a_Pool)", "[The Maids that Bloom in Spring](wiki:The_Maids_that_Bloom_in_Spring)", and "[Two Hearts That Beat as One](wiki:Two_Hearts_That_Beat_as_One)" at the [Peach](wiki:Peach) in [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept).(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) When the [Mad Huntsman](wiki:Mad_Huntsman) returns to the town, his hounds are soothed by Tom's woodharp.(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Within the [hollow hill](wiki:Hollow_hill), Tom lists victims of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister)'s violence during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) to [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane).(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) He plays a dirge after the [battle at the burning septry](wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry) for [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) as they are hanged. At the septy's brewhouse, Tom sings "[The Mother's Tears](wiki:The_Mother's_Tears)," "[When Willum's Wife Was Wet](wiki:When_Willum's_Wife_Was_Wet)," "[Lord Harte Rode Out on a Rainy Day](wiki:Lord_Harte_Rode_Out_on_a_Rainy_Day)," and "[The Rains of Castamere](wiki:The_Rains_of_Castamere)".(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) He later plays [Jenny's song](wiki:Jenny's_song) to the dwarf woman when the outlaws return to High Heart.(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

Tom is playing Jenny's sad song when [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey) arrives at [Oldstones](wiki:Oldstones) to ransom [Petyr Frey](wiki:Petyr_Frey) but is captured by the brotherhood. Tom offers to release Merrett in return for the whereabouts of Sandor and Arya, but Merrett has no new information. Tom reveals that [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), who was slain in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), is still alive, and the outlaws hang Merrett.(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5)

### A Feast for Crows

Still a member of [Lady Stoneheart](wiki:Lady_Stoneheart)'s outlaws,(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Tom serves for a fortnight as a musician in the company of Ser [Ryman Frey](wiki:Ryman_Frey) during the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun).(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) has Tom follow him after claiming the captive Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) from the Freys. After Jaime is done threatening Edmure, he orders Tom to sing "The Rains of Castamere". Recognizing Tom, Edmure responds by saying, "No. Not him. Get him away from me."(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2) According to a semi-canon source, Tom informs the brotherhood of Jaime's dismissal of Ryman, allowing the outlaws to ambush and hang Ryman near [Fairmarket](wiki:Fairmarket).(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_tom.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

Tom jokes he will make a song called "Talking to the Fish" about Lord [Emmon Frey](wiki:Emmon_Frey)'s speech to the people of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun). When asked by Jaime why he does not depart with the Freys after Riverrun's surrender, Tom claims he hopes to replace [Whitesmile Wat](wiki:Whitesmile_Wat) at Riverrun. Jaime suggests he court the favor of his aunt, [Genna Lannister](wiki:Genna_Lannister), instead of Riverrun's new lord, Emmon.(wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-3)
