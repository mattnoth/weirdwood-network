## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

As part of the terms of [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) being pardoned and returning to the king's peace of [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), Wynafryd is betrothed to the widowed [Rhaegar Frey](wiki:Rhaegar_Frey).(wiki:Wynafryd_Manderly.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

When Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) visits the [Merman's Court](wiki:Merman's_Court), [Wylla](wiki:Wylla_Manderly) proclaims that the [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) killed Lord [Eddard](wiki:Eddard_Stark), Lady [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), and King [Robb](wiki:Robb_Stark), and that [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) should join [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) in the name of vengeance. Wynafryd refers to her sister's exclamation as a child's foolishness.(wiki:Wynafryd_Manderly.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Wynafryd pretends to be pleased with her betrothal to Rhaegar. After faking the execution of Davos and releasing him from imprisonment in the [Wolf's Den](wiki:Wolf's_Den), her scheming grandfather, Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly), informs Davos he had already revealed his plans to Wynafryd and she knows the marriage will never take place.(wiki:Wynafryd_Manderly.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Wynafryd's betrothal ends with the suspected death of Rhaegar on his way to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Wynafryd_Manderly.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
