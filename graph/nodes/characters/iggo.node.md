## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Iggo and three other [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions)—a pale [Lyseni](wiki:Lys), an [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibbenese) axeman, and a [Dornishman](wiki:Dornishmen)—search for Nan (actually [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)), [Gendry](wiki:Gendry), and [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie) after they escape from [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal). While sleeping, Arya [dreams](wiki:Skinchanger) of [a wolf](wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf)) hunting the sellswords as they pursue the youths in the rain. The three other Brave Companions die quickly when her [wolfpack](wiki:Wolf) attacks, but Iggo's horse kicks in the head of one wolf and the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) uses his *[arakh](wiki:Arakh)* to kill another. The great wolf leading them leaps onto Iggo's back to knock him from the saddle. Tearing off his arm, she shakes it and sends blood flying through the rain.(wiki:Iggo.cite_ref-Rasos3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
