## Origins

Edgar served as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) for [Jaehaerys II Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_II_Targaryen), one of the many seasoned, older men serving the king. Upon the king's death, he was retired from office by [Aerys II](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) when the latter ascended to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) and Ser [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), the heir of [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), was named in his place.(wiki:Edgar_Sloane.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
