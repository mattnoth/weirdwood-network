## Origins

After Princess [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria) and Prince [Mors Martell](wiki:Mors_Martell) declared supremacy over [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), they faced the [Kings of the Torrentine](wiki:King_of_the_Torrentine) in war.(wiki:Vorian_Dayne.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Vorian was ultimately defeated in [Nymeria's War](wiki:Nymeria's_War) and was one of the six kings sent to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) in golden fetters by Nymeria. The Daynes were subsequently reduced from Kings of the Torrentine to powerful [lord bannermen](wiki:Bannerman) to [House Martell](wiki:House_Martell), and Ser [Davos Dayne](wiki:Davos_Dayne) later wed Princess Nymeria.(wiki:Vorian_Dayne.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_the_coming_of_the_rhoynar.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
