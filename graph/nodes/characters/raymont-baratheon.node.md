## Identity

Ser Raymont Baratheon was a knight from House Baratheon and a member of King Aenys I Targaryen's Kingsguard (wiki:Raymont_Baratheon).

## Origins

Born between 1 BC and 25 AC. Son of Lord Orys Baratheon and Lady Argella Durrandon (track_b: Father, Mother).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Baratheon (track_b: Allegiances). `SWORN_TO` Kingsguard (track_b: Allegiances). `CULTURE_OF` Stormlander (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Ser Raymont served in the Kingsguard when the Faith Militant rebelled against King Aenys I in 41 AC. Three days before the royal family was to sail for Dragonstone, two Poor Fellows scaled the walls of the manse atop Visenya's Hill and broke into the king's bedchamber to assassinate him. Only Raymont's timely intervention saved the king's life. He died between 41 and 48 AC (wiki:Raymont_Baratheon).
