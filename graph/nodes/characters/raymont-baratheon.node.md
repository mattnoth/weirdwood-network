## Origins

Ser Raymont Baratheon served in the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) when the [Faith Militant](wiki:Faith_Militant) [rebelled](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising) against King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen) in [41 AC](wiki:41_AC).(wiki:Raymont_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_stormlands:_the_men_of_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) Three days before King Aenys and his family were to set sail for [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), two [Poor Fellows](wiki:Poor_Fellows) scaled the walls of the manse atop [Visenya's Hill](wiki:Visenya's_Hill) where the royal family resided during the construction of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), and broke into the king's bedchamber to assassinate the king.(wiki:Raymont_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Only the timely intervention of Ser Raymont saved the life of the king.(wiki:Raymont_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_stormlands:_the_men_of_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)
