## Identity

Dagon Greyjoy — sworn to House Greyjoy; Lord Reaper of Pyke. One of 2 characters named Dagon Greyjoy.

## Origins

By [211 AC](wiki:211_AC), Lord Dagon and his [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) were raiding along the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea) and the western coast of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). They burned [Little Dosk](wiki:Little_Dosk), carried off half the wealth of [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle) and a hundred women, and raided as far south as the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor).(wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The ironmen also also attacked the [north](wiki:North),(wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_lords_of_winterfell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) so Lord [Beron Stark](wiki:Beron_Stark) gathered swords in an attempt to drive them off.(wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Dagon and his forces retreated to the sea(wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) and [Pyke](wiki:Pyke)(wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) whenever his adversaries retaliated on land, so Lord [Tybolt Lannister](wiki:Tybolt_Lannister) began building ships for an attack on the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands).(wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

[Erik Ironmaker](wiki:Erik_Ironmaker) sailed with Dagon.(wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) The Lord of the Iron Islands frustrated the [Starks](wiki:House_Stark) and the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) for some time, but the Last Reaver was eventually defeated by [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen).(wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

*[Lord Dagon](wiki:Lord_Dagon)* and *[Dagon's Feast](wiki:Dagon's_Feast)*, longships of the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet), are [named after](wiki:Named_after) Dagon.(wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North), sends the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) to attack the [Reach](wiki:Reach) for the first time since Dagon's era.(wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy)'s cousin, [Dagon the Drunkard](wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy_(Drunkard)), was [named after](wiki:Named_after) Lord Dagon.(wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

[Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) recalls his ancestor Dagon while sailing with the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
