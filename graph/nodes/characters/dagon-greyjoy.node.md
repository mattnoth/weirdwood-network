## Identity

Dagon Greyjoy, called the Last Reaver, was Lord of the Iron Islands and Lord Reaper of Pyke during the reign of King Aerys I Targaryen (wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy).

## Narrative Arc

Lord Dagon led devastating raids along the Sunset Sea and western coast of Westeros during the reign of Aerys I. By 211 AC, his ironborn burned Little Dosk, carried off half the wealth of Fair Isle and a hundred women, and raided as far south as the Arbor. Lord Beron Stark gathered swords to drive the ironborn from the north, and Lord Tybolt Lannister began building ships to counterattack. Dagon frustrated both by retreating to sea whenever land forces retaliated. He was eventually defeated by House Targaryen. Old women on Fair Isle still frightened their grandchildren with tales of Lord Dagon and his men (wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf1).
