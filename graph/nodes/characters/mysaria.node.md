Mysaria had skin pale as milk. ## Origins

### Paramour[ ]

While a dancing girl in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Mysaria became the favored lover of Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen).(wiki:Mysaria.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Around [105 AC](wiki:105_AC), Mysaria became pregnant with Daemon's child while living at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). Daemon gave his paramour a [dragon egg](wiki:Dragon_egg) for the child, which angered King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen). The king ordered Daemon to return the egg and send Mysaria back to [Lys](wiki:Lys). During her return to the [Free City](wiki:Free_City), she lost the child during a storm on the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea).(wiki:Mysaria.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

### Dance of the Dragons[ ]

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) desired revenge for the death of his stepson, Prince [Lucerys Velaryon](wiki:Lucerys_Velaryon). With a pale stranger used as a go-between, two assassins, [Blood](wiki:Blood_(the_butcher)) and [Cheese](wiki:Cheese_(the_rat-catcher)), infiltrated the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) and killed Prince [Jaehaerys Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_II)), the eldest son of King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen). Blood was seized by the [gold cloaks](wiki:Gold_cloaks) trying to leave the city, and he admitted under [torture](wiki:Torture) that he had been hired by a [prostitute](wiki:Prostitute), a pale older woman, called Misery by the other harlots. However, the [gold cloaks](wiki:Gold_cloaks) were unable to find Mysaria or Cheese.(wiki:Mysaria.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)(wiki:Mysaria.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Mysaria became Daemon's [mistress of whisperers](wiki:Mistress_of_whisperers) at some point.(wiki:Mysaria.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_viserys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) After the [fall of King's Landing](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing) to Rhaenyra, Mysaria served as the queen's mistress of whisperers in all but name, although she did not join the [small council](wiki:Small_council). Rhaenyra apparently approved of Daemon sleeping with Mysaria each night.(wiki:Mysaria.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
When Queen [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower) referred to the deaths of Rhaenyra's sons by saying only [bastard](wiki:Bastard) blood had been spilled, Rhaenyra was enraged, and threatened to [cut out her tongue](wiki:Tongue_mutilation). According to the [Testimony](wiki:The_Testimony_of_Mushroom) of the [fool](wiki:Fool) [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom), Mysaria suggested a crueler punishment, proposing that the Dowager Queen and Queen [Helaena Targaryen](wiki:Helaena_Targaryen) be taken to a certain [brothel](wiki:Brothel_Queens) so they would be forcibly impregnated with bastard offspring. Archmaester [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn) discounts Mushroom's story, however.(wiki:Mysaria.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
Following the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton), Mysaria informed Rhaenyra that Daemon had been sleeping with [Nettles](wiki:Nettles), his fellow [dragonrider](wiki:Dragonrider), at [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool). Rhaenyra ordered the death of Nettles, but Lord [Manfryd Mooton](wiki:Manfryd_Mooton) disobeyed the command. Nettles disappeared on [Sheepstealer](wiki:Sheepstealer), while Daemon flew on [Caraxes](wiki:Caraxes) to [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) to await a [confrontation with his nephew](wiki:Battle_Above_the_Gods_Eye).(wiki:Mysaria.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)
After Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) was imprisoned for warning Ser [Addam Velaryon](wiki:Addam_Velaryon) of his impending arrest under charges of treason, two of the Sea Snake's [sworn swords](wiki:Sworn_swords), Ser [Denys Woodwright](wiki:Denys_Woodwright) and Ser [Thoron True](wiki:Thoron_True), planned to free their [lord](wiki:Lord). Mysaria learned of the plan from a [whore](wiki:Whore) Thoron had been [bedding](wiki:Bedding), however, and the two [knights](wiki:Knight) were promptly arrested and hanged. Septon [Eustace](wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)) suggests that Helaena Targaryen jumped to her death after being informed by Mysaria of how her son, Prince [Maelor Targaryen](wiki:Maelor_Targaryen), had been killed at [Bitterbridge](wiki:Sack_of_Bitterbridge).(wiki:Mysaria.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Mysaria.cite_ref-9)
During the [riot of King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)), Mysaria warned Rhaenyra against the coming dark. When Rhaenyra ultimately [fled the capital](wiki:Flight_to_Dragonstone), Mysaria remained at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) among other members of the court. Ser [Garth the Harelip](wiki:Garth_the_Harelip) surrendered the Red Keep to Ser [Perkin the Flea](wiki:Perkin_the_Flea) and his [gutter knights](wiki:Gutter_knights), and Mysaria attempted to flee, but was taken. Perkin informed Mysaria that if she could [travel naked](wiki:Walk_of_atonement) through the city to the [Gate of the Gods](wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods) while being whipped, she would be allowed to live. Mysaria only made it halfway, dying on the city's cobblestones.(wiki:Mysaria.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
