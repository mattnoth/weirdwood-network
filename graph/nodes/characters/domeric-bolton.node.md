## Origins

Domeric served four years as a [page](wiki:Page) in [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) to his aunt,(wiki:Domeric_Bolton.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_domeric_bolton.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) Lady [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin), and then [squired](wiki:Squire) for three years for Lord [Horton Redfort](wiki:Horton_Redfort) in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn). Domeric enjoyed the company of Horton's sons and began to consider them brothers. Lord Redfort thought the lad had the making of a [tourney](wiki:Tourney) champion.(wiki:Domeric_Bolton.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

After returning from the Vale to the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort), Domeric heard he had a [bastard](wiki:Bastard) half-brother, [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), and wished to seek him out, against his father's wishes, since he had always wanted a brother of his own. Soon after the visit, Domeric died in [297 AC](wiki:297_AC)(wiki:Domeric_Bolton.cite_ref-4) from a sickness of the bowels, according to [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Uthor](wiki:Uthor). However, Roose believes Ramsay [poisoned](wiki:Poison) Domeric, in order to become Roose's only son.(wiki:Domeric_Bolton.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) The heirless Roose subsequently brought Ramsay to live at the Dreadfort.(wiki:Domeric_Bolton.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
