## Origins

After [Melara Hetherspoon](wiki:Melara_Hetherspoon) drowned, [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) asked Septa Saranella the meaning of the word *[valonqar](wiki:Valonqar)*. She responded that it means "little brother" in [High Valyrian](wiki:High_Valyrian).(wiki:Saranella.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) tells [Taena Merryweather](wiki:Taena_Merryweather) about [Maggy the Frog](wiki:Maggy_the_Frog), telling her that [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) is the *valonqar* and that it was Septa Saranella who had told her the meaning of the High Valyrian word for "little brother".(wiki:Saranella.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
