## Identity

Desmond is a member of the household guard of Lord Eddard Stark at Winterfell (wiki:Desmond). Dutiful and single-minded, he carries a longsword (wiki:Desmond).

## Origins

Born in the North (track_b: Born). Died in 298 AC at the Red Keep, King's Landing (track_b: Died).

## Allegiances

Desmond is `SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance) and belongs to `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Desmond carries a longsword and is described as dutiful and single-minded (wiki:Desmond). He told Arya that "every northman is worth ten southron swords" (agot-arya-03, wiki:Desmond).

## Narrative Arc

Desmond accompanies Lord Eddard's party to the execution of a Night's Watch deserter and is present when the direwolf pups are found (agot-bran-01). He admits Maester Luwin to deliver Lysa Arryn's coded letter to Eddard (agot-catelyn-02, wiki:Desmond). Desmond goes south with Eddard to King's Landing, taking meals in the Small Hall with Jory, Hullen, Harwin, and Jacks (wiki:Desmond). He and Tomard take positions along Eddard's route to Tobho Mott's armory to watch for followers (agot-eddard-06, wiki:Desmond). Desmond escorts Arya from Eddard's solar after Yoren brings news of Catelyn seizing Tyrion, reassuring her of her father's safety (agot-arya-03). He and Cayn fetch Littlefinger to Eddard's chambers on the night Robert dies (agot-eddard-13). Desmond is killed during the Lannister purge of Stark men — Arya finds him dead in the stables and remembers his promises to protect her father (agot-arya-04).

## Quotes
