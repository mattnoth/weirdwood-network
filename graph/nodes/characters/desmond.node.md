## Identity

Desmond (d. 298 AC) — sworn to House Stark. One of 2 characters named Desmond.

## Appearances & Description

Dutiful and single-minded, Desmond carries a [longsword](wiki:Longsword).(wiki:Desmond.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Desmond accompanies Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s party to the trial and execution of [a deserter](wiki:Gared) from the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). He and [Tomard](wiki:Tomard) drag the man to an [ironwood](wiki:Ironwood) stump, where he is beheaded by Eddard with his [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) [greatsword](wiki:Greatsword), [Ice](wiki:Ice).(wiki:Desmond.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) On their way home to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), the party comes across a dead [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), and Desmond and [Jory Cassel](wiki:Jory_Cassel) are eventually told by Eddard to gather up its pups.(wiki:Desmond.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Desmond admits Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin) to see Eddard in Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark)'s chambers, as Luwin brings a coded letter from Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) informing her sister that Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) was murdered by Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister).(wiki:Desmond.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Desmond is one of the [guards](wiki:House_Stark_guards) who goes south when Eddard becomes [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). He takes his meals in the [Small Hall](wiki:Small_Hall) of the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand), where he can be seen in the company of Jory, [Hullen](wiki:Hullen), [Harwin](wiki:Harwin), and [Jacks](wiki:Jacks).(wiki:Desmond.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Desmond and Tomard take up positions along the route Eddard will take when he goes to the armory of [Tobho Mott](wiki:Tobho_Mott) in the [Street of Steel](wiki:Street_of_Steel). Their assignment is to look out for people following Eddard.(wiki:Desmond.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Desmond presents [Yoren](wiki:Yoren) before Eddard when the [wandering crow](wiki:Wandering_crow) informs him about Catelyn taking [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) captive on her way back to Winterfell. Desmond leads [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) out of the room on Eddard's command and to her chambers. Arya worries about her father's safety, but Desmond replies that every [northman](wiki:Northman) is worth ten [southron](wiki:Southron) swords.(wiki:Desmond.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

When Eddard sends most of his best swords with Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) to bring Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) to [justice](wiki:Justice), Desmond stays in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). He and [Cayn](wiki:Cayn) admit Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) to the Hand's chambers just as Eddard has finished his letter to Lord [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) asking him to return to King's Landing because of King Robert's expected death.(wiki:Desmond.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Desmond is killed in the Stark household purge following [Eddard's arrest](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark). Arya finds his body in the stable, lying next to the body of a [Lannister soldier](wiki:House_Lannister_guards). Recalling Desmond's words about northmen and southerners, Arya kicks Desmond's body in a sudden fury and calls him a liar.(wiki:Desmond.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
