## Origins

As a child, Maegelle attached herself to her elder sister, Princess [Alyssa](wiki:Alyssa_Targaryen), much as Alyssa herself had done to Prince [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) when she was younger. Alyssa did the best she could to evade Maegelle, bristling at "the baby" at her skirts. Later, Maegelle was the "guiding star" of her younger sister, [Daella](wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)).(wiki:Maegelle_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

In [73 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), at the age of ten, Maegelle was promised to the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) as a novice, which Queen Alysanne announced was in thanks for [the Mother](wiki:The_Mother) blessing her with many children. Maegelle eventually became a septa.(wiki:Maegelle_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

When Queen Alysanne was having a difficult time finding a husband for Princess Daella, Maegelle told her mother that the Faith would not want Daella because of her illiteracy, as she could not read her prayers.(wiki:Maegelle_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

After Princess [Saera](wiki:Saera_Targaryen) was involved in a scandal in [84 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), it was announced that her sister Septa Maegelle was to take charge of her instruction as a novice [silent sister](wiki:Silent_sister) in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown).(wiki:Maegelle_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

In [87 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Maegelle took leave from her [sept](wiki:Sept) to stay with her grieving mother, who had lost her daughters Daella, Alyssa, and [Viserra](wiki:Viserra_Targaryen) in the space of five years.(wiki:Maegelle_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

In [89 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Jaehaerys visited his daughter in Oldtown during the [First Quarrel](wiki:First_Quarrel). It was Maegelle who helped reconcile the king and queen, telling her father to conciliate with Alysanne in time for the wedding of her niece, [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aemon)), the following year.(wiki:Maegelle_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

Maegelle also played a role in ending the [Second Quarrel](wiki:Second_Quarrel), which had begun in [92 AC](wiki:92_AC), when her father, King Jaehaerys, chose Maegelle's brother, Prince [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)), as his heir over Maegelle's niece, Princess Rhaenys. This decision angered Queen Alysanne, as she felt there was no reason to favor a man over a woman. Maegelle brought her parents back together, solving the Quarrel in [94 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Maegelle_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Maegelle_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Maegelle nursed children afflicted with [greyscale](wiki:Greyscale), but became afflicted with greyscale herself and died in [96 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), her arms and legs turned to stone.(wiki:Maegelle_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)(wiki:Maegelle_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Appearances & Description

As a girl, Princess Maegelle was gentle, quiet, studious, and exceedingly bright. She was said to read from the *[Seven-Pointed Star](wiki:Seven-Pointed_Star)* every night before sleep, and was eager to take the vows of a [septa](wiki:Septa).(wiki:Maegelle_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) She was known for her compassion, and her gift for healing.(wiki:Maegelle_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
