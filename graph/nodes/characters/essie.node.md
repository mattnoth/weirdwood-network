## Origins

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Essie claimed her four-year old son, Gaemon, was a [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son of King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen). This led to the boy being installed as a King in the [House of Kisses](wiki:House_of_Kisses) after the [flight](wiki:Flight_to_Dragonstone) of Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), during the [Moon of the Three Kings](wiki:Moon_of_the_Three_Kings). From the brothel, the boy issued a series of edicts, while the whores offered their favors to any men who swore allegiance to Gaemon. When [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) was taken back in the name of King Aegon II, Essie was arrested, and confessed under [torture](wiki:Torture) that Gaemon was actually the son of a silver-haired oarsman from [Lys](wiki:Lys). She was hanged for [treason](wiki:Treason).(wiki:Essie.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Essie.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

According to [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom), Essie had a paramour, [Sylvenna Sand](wiki:Sylvenna_Sand), who was also a whore at the House of Kisses.(wiki:Essie.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_queer_customs_of_the_south.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) She was hanged alongside Essie.(wiki:Essie.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
