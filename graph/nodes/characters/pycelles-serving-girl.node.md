## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), visits [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle)'s chambers and makes inquiries about the death of the previous Hand, Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn). She serves them iced milk sweetened with honey.(wiki:Pycelle's_serving_girl.cite_ref-Ragot25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Clash of Kings

During breakfast in the chambers beneath Pycelle's [rookery](wiki:Rookery), [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) asks him to send his serving girl away after she has served their food. Pycelle complies, and Tyrion hands him two sealed copies of a secret letter for Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell).(wiki:Pycelle's_serving_girl.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

When Tyrion Lannister breaks into Pycelle's chambers with the help of [Shagga](wiki:Shagga) and [Timett](wiki:Timett), after discovering Pycelle betrayed Tyrion to his sister [Queen Regent](wiki:Regent) [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), he finds Pycelle in bed with his serving girl. She is frightened, and begs [the clansmen](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) not to hurt her, cringing away from Shagga, attempting to cover herself. While Shagga announces that he wants her and would give her a strong child, Tyrion has Timett march her into the hall.(wiki:Pycelle's_serving_girl.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
