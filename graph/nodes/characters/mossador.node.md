## Origins

When they were young, Mossador taught his sister [Missandei](wiki:Missandei) how to climb trees on [Naath](wiki:Naath). Missandei once found him sleeping in their garden with [butterflies](wiki:Butterflies) crawling on him.(wiki:Mossador.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Missandei and her three brothers, including [Marselen](wiki:Marselen) and Mossador, were captured by raiders from the [Basilisk Isles](wiki:Basilisk_Isles) and sold into [slavery](wiki:Slavery) in [Astapor](wiki:Astapor). Missandei was trained as a scribe, but Mossador and his two brothers were [gelded](wiki:Eunuch) and trained by the [Good Masters](wiki:Good_Masters) to be [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied).(wiki:Mossador.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Marselen and Mossador became Unsullied,(wiki:Mossador.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) and the third brother died at some point.(wiki:Mossador.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Appearances & Description

Mossador is close to his sister, [Missandei](wiki:Missandei). He knows how to catch fish with his hands.(wiki:Mossador.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) purchases [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) from the [Good Masters](wiki:Good_Masters) of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor). After freeing the soldiers, she turns them against the [slavers](wiki:Slavers) with the [fall of Astapor](wiki:Fall_of_Astapor).(wiki:Mossador.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Daenerys also takes into her service [Missandei](wiki:Missandei), Mossador's sister, as a scribe and handmaid.(wiki:Mossador.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Dance with Dragons

As Daenerys told the Unsullied to abolish the practice of choosing a new name every day,(wiki:Mossador.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Mossador chose to go back to his birth name.

[Grey Worm](wiki:Grey_Worm) informs Daenerys that Mossador, while on patrol with another Unsullied named [Duran](wiki:Duran), was ambushed and killed. Both were crushed by falling stones beneath the river wall of the [Skahazadhan](wiki:Skahazadhan), dropped on them by the [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy). When Missandei is told, she cries and grieves for her brother.(wiki:Mossador.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
