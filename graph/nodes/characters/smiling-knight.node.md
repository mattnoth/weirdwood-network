## Origins

During the time of the [Kingswood Brotherhood](wiki:Kingswood_Brotherhood), the Smiling Knight killed Ser [Victor Tyrell](wiki:Victor_Tyrell), a cousin of [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), [Lord of Highgarden](wiki:Lord_of_Highgarden).(wiki:Smiling_Knight.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) While rescuing Lady [Jeyne Swann](wiki:Jeyne_Swann) and her [septa](wiki:Septa) from the outlaws, Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) defeated the Smiling Knight and slew [Simon Toyne](wiki:Simon_Toyne), chief of the Brotherhood.(wiki:Smiling_Knight.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

[Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), then a squire of Lord [Sumner Crakehall](wiki:Sumner_Crakehall) and part of Ser [Arthur Dayne](wiki:Arthur_Dayne)'s campaign against the Brotherhood, desired to be the one to slay the Smiling Knight in combat,(wiki:Smiling_Knight.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) and the youth held his own in a clash against the outlaw.(wiki:Smiling_Knight.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6) Arthur next fought an extended exchange with the Smiling Knight until the outlaw bled from a dozen wounds. The chivalrous Arthur paused their fight and allowed the Smiling Knight to take a fresh blade to replace his notched longsword. The mad outlaw did so, but remarked that it was Arthur's famous sword, [Dawn](wiki:Dawn), that he truly desired. The Smiling Knight was then slain by Arthur, the [Sword of the Morning](wiki:Sword_of_the_Morning).(wiki:Smiling_Knight.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)

[Ulmer of the Kingswood](wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood) has often told the men at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) about his adventures with the Smiling Knight and other outlaws from the Brotherhood.(wiki:Smiling_Knight.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of the Smiling Knight](wiki:Category:Images_of_Smiling_Knight)*

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) recalls the Smiling Knight as having been fearless and chivalrous, yet also mad and cruel.(wiki:Smiling_Knight.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Jaime thinks of the Smiling Knight as his generation's [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) in terms of men's eagerness to best him in combat.(wiki:Smiling_Knight.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Half the size of Gregor,(wiki:Smiling_Knight.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) the Smiling Knight wielded a longsword.(wiki:Smiling_Knight.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
