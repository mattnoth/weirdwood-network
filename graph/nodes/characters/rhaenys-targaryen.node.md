## Identity

Queen Rhaenys Targaryen was the youngest sister and wife of King Aegon I Targaryen, the first Lord of the Seven Kingdoms. She was Aegon's favorite wife. Through her son Aenys I, fifteen of the sixteen Targaryen kings descended from her. She was a dragonrider who rode the dragon Meraxes (wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen).

## Origins

Born 25–26 BC at Dragonstone (track_b: Born). Valyrian race, Crownlands culture (track_b: Race, Culture).

## Appearances & Description

A beautiful woman with silver-gold hair worn long and loose, purple eyes, and a slender body. Kindhearted, graceful, playful, curious, impulsive, and given to flights of fancy with a mischievous aspect. She loved music, dancing, poetry, and flying — spending more time on Meraxes than her siblings spent on their dragons combined (wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen).

## Narrative Arc

Rhaenys participated in Aegon's Conquest alongside her siblings, flying Meraxes to take Rosby and other holdfasts. After Aegon's coronation she hailed him as "Aegon, First of his Name, King of All Westeros, and Shield of His People." During the First Dornish War, Rhaenys flew south to confront Dorne. In 10 AC (disputed), Rhaenys and Meraxes were brought down at or near Hellholt by a bolt through Meraxes's eye. The exact manner of Rhaenys's death remains uncertain (wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen).
