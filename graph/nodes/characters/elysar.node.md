## Origins

After the [Shivers](wiki:Shivers) ended in [60 AC](wiki:60_AC), the [conclave](wiki:Conclave) elected Elysar as [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) to succeed [Benifer](wiki:Benifer) who had passed away from the disease the previous year. Elysar was twenty years younger than Benifer had been when he first took the office. He was known to be very opinionated, with some suspecting the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) named him to the office to be rid of him from [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown).(wiki:Elysar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Elysar and [Septon](wiki:Septon) [Barth](wiki:Barth) were present when King Jaehaerys and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) confronted their daughter, Princess [Saera](wiki:Saera_Targaryen), about her indiscretions.(wiki:Elysar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Elysar passed away in [97 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) and was succeeded by [Allar](wiki:Allar).(wiki:Elysar.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
