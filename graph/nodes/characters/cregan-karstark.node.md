## Appearances & Description

Cregan is a strong man with brown hair, streaked with grey, a beard and mustache. He has broad shoulders.(wiki:Cregan_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Cregan's black wool surcoat is emblazoned with the white sunburst of [House Karstark](wiki:House_Karstark). His leather gloves are lined with fur, as is his cloak.(wiki:Cregan_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Cregan is twice a widower having outlived both of his wives.(wiki:Cregan_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark) and her brothers always refer to Cregan as their uncle even though he actually is their first cousin once removed.(wiki:Cregan_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Cregan and his father [Arnolf](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark), castellan of [Karhold](wiki:Karhold), conspire to [marry](wiki:Marriage) him to [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark), the daughter of the late [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark), the previous [Lord of Karhold](wiki:Lord_of_Karhold).(wiki:Cregan_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) According to Alys, after the wedding Cregan and Arnolf plan to have her brother, Lord [Harrion](wiki:Harrion_Karstark), Lord Rickard's heir, killed by the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister), who currently hold Harrion prisoner at [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool). That way, they wish to claim the lordship of [Karhold](wiki:Karhold) for Cregan. Alys fears that Cregan may kill her once she has given birth to his child.(wiki:Cregan_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

To avoid the marriage all together, Alys flees north to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) and requests protection from the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Cregan_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) Cregan follows her with four [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms), a huntsman, and a pack of [dogs](wiki:Dog). He arrives at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) a day after Alys does, and is subsequently arrested and thrown in the [ice cells](wiki:Ice_cell) before he can claim the [guest right](wiki:Guest_right), together with four of his men, while the fifth is slain.(wiki:Cregan_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), arranges [a wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark) between Alys and [Sigorn](wiki:Sigorn), the new [Magnar of Thenn](wiki:Magnar_of_Thenn). After the ceremony, Jon confronts Cregan, offering him the option of taking the black and telling him that Alys has agreed to allow every man from Karhold who had betrayed her to do so as well. When Cregan refuses, Jon warns that King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) might eventually put Cregan to death unless Cregan wears a cloak of the Night's Watch.(wiki:Cregan_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

Cregan remains in the ice cells, taking to howling in the night. He additionally begins to throw his frozen feces at the men who bring him his [food](wiki:Food). When snowstorms start to bury the access to the ice cells, Jon Snow orders Cregan moved to the undervault of the [Lord Commander's Tower](wiki:Lord_Commander's_Tower). Cregan attempts to attack the men of the Night's Watch when they move him, but has been weakened by the cold in the ice cells.(wiki:Cregan_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
