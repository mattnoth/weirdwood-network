## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Soren Shieldbreaker is among the wildling leaders who accompany [Tormund Giantsbane](wiki:Tormund) to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) in declaring a truce with the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). His son, a tall lad, is made a hostage of the Night's Watch to guarantee the peace between the wildlings and the Watch.(wiki:Soren_Shieldbreaker.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Soren is given the abandoned castle [Stonedoor](wiki:Stonedoor) by [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), to garrison and settle his own people.(wiki:Soren_Shieldbreaker.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He is present in the [Shieldhall](wiki:Shieldhall) when Jon announces the [letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) purportedly from [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) entitled 'Bastard', and he stands when Jon asks for volunteers.(wiki:Soren_Shieldbreaker.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
