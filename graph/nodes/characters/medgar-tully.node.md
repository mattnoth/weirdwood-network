## Origins

In [209 AC](wiki:209_AC), Lord Medgar participated in the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow). He challenged Ser [Humfrey Hardyng](wiki:Humfrey_Hardyng) in his first tilt but was knocked from the saddle on the second lance. When they continued their bout on foot with swords, Humfrey beat Medgar into submission.(wiki:Medgar_Tully.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
Medgar died within two years after the tourney, because in [211 AC](wiki:211_AC) the [unnamed Lord Tully of Riverrun](wiki:Lord_Tully_(successor_of_Medgar)) was a boy of eight.(wiki:Medgar_Tully.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
