## Origins

Minisa was born into [House Whent](wiki:House_Whent), the [Lords of Harrenhal](wiki:Lords_of_Harrenhal), and she wed their liege, [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), the Lord of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) and [Lord Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident). Minisa's first two children were sons who died in infancy,(wiki:Minisa_Tully.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) but her later children [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), [Lysa](wiki:Lysa_Arryn), and [Edmure](wiki:Edmure_Tully) grew to adulthood. Minisa died in childbirth with her fourth son, who also died shortly after.(wiki:Minisa_Tully.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

The sept of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) is located in Minisa's gardens.(wiki:Minisa_Tully.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

## Appearances & Description

The always calm Minisa had soft hands and a warm smile.(wiki:Minisa_Tully.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) states that his niece [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), believed by many to be pretty,(wiki:Minisa_Tully.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Minisa_Tully.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Minisa_Tully.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) resembles Minisa in her cheekbones and jaw.(wiki:Minisa_Tully.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Catelyn's elder daughter, [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark), has high cheekbones as well.(wiki:Minisa_Tully.cite_ref-Ragot7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
