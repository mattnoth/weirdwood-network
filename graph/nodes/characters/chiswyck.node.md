## Appearances & Description

Chiswyck is old and stoop-shouldered(wiki:Chiswyck.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) with a scraggy grey beard.(wiki:Chiswyck.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Chiswyck has a sadistic temperament that he masks with an outwardly jovial personality.(wiki:Chiswyck.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He thinks he is funny(wiki:Chiswyck.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) and often tells funny stories.(wiki:Chiswyck.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

The man-at-arms wears cold dirty chainmail.(wiki:Chiswyck.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Chiswyck is one of the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men), the soldiers of Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane)'s who capture [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) and [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). When Arya tries to bite Chiswyck, he hits her in the face.(wiki:Chiswyck.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) He sometimes aids the [Tickler](wiki:Tickler) in [torturing](wiki:Torture) the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk).(wiki:Chiswyck.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) For this, Arya adds Chiswyck to her [prayer kill list](wiki:Arya_Stark).(wiki:Chiswyck.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

At [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Chiswyck tells a story about the gang rape of an [innkeeper's daughter](wiki:Layna) that Gregor and the men had instigated, laughing as he recalls the details. Arya overhears this, and whispers his name into the ear of [Jaqen H'ghar](wiki:Jaqen_H'ghar) as the first of her three promised deaths. The obliging Jaqen pushes Chiswyck off a wall to his death three days later.(wiki:Chiswyck.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
