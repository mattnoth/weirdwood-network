## Origins

After the [Hour of the Wolf](wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf), [lords](wiki:Lord) and ladies from all around [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) came to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to assist to the coronation of Prince [Aegon the Younger](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) and his [marriage](wiki:Marriage) to Princess [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen).(wiki:Royce_Caron.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Royce's daughter, Lady [Elenda](wiki:Elenda_Baratheon), gave birth to a son seven days after the death of her husband, Lord [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon) of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End). Lord Borros had wished Elenda to [name](wiki:Named_after) their son Aegon after King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) but she named him Royce after her father instead. Weakened by the delivery, the newly widowed Lady of Storm's End could not make the travel to the capital. Instead she sent her daughters [Cassandra](wiki:Cassandra_Baratheon), [Floris](wiki:Floris_Baratheon) and [Ellyn](wiki:Ellyn_Baratheon) to represent [House Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon) with Lord Royce escorting his granddaughters to the city and acting as their guardian.(wiki:Royce_Caron.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

After the coronation of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), Royce became a member of the council of seven [regents](wiki:Regent) that ruled until the child-king came of age.(wiki:Royce_Caron.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Royce_Caron.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) Lord Royce, however, resigned his post in [132 AC](wiki:132_AC) to return to the [Dornish Marches](wiki:Dornish_Marches) where the [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen), encouraged by their new ruler Princess [Aliandra Martell](wiki:Aliandra_Martell), raided and plundered the region.(wiki:Royce_Caron.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) He was not able to return to court the following year after the death of the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Ser [Tyland Lannister](wiki:Tyland_Lannister), as he was still dealing with the Dornishmen.(wiki:Royce_Caron.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
