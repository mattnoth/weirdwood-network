## Identity

Lucan — sworn to House Whent. One of 2 characters named Lucan. Blacksmith and master of the armory at Harrenhal, beheaded by Steelshanks Walton for making weapons for the Lannisters during the fall of Harrenhal in 299 AC. (wiki:Lucan)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

After being brought to [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) serves Lucan in the armory.(wiki:Lucan.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

[Weese](wiki:Weese) sends [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) to Lucan to get a new sword for Ser [Lyonel](wiki:Lyonel_(knight)). The blacksmith gives Arya a heavy longsword but tells her to deliver his message to Weese that Lyonel does not deserve such a good sword. Arya lies by saying she will do so.(wiki:Lucan.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Arya tries to talk Gendry into running away from Harrenhal, but he is opposed to the idea, considering Harrenhal a better place than others and Lucan a fair enough master.(wiki:Lucan.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

After the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) turn cloak and aid [northmen](wiki:North) with the [fall of Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal), Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) has [Steelshanks Walton](wiki:Steelshanks_Walton) behead Lucan for having made weapons for the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister). Like other executed servants, Lucan's head is tarred and put on a spike on the ramparts of the gatehouse as feast for the crows.(wiki:Lucan.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Gendry blames Arya for Lucan's death, as she had helped during Harrenhal's capture.(wiki:Lucan.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
