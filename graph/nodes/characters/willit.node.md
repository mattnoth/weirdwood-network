## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

When [Harys Swyft](wiki:Harys_Swyft), the [Knight of Cornfield](wiki:Knight_of_Cornfield), is trapped beneath his dying horse during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Willit pulls him from under the horse and defends him against a dozen attackers. Willit's wounds are great. For his efforts he is awarded a spear with a silver-banded haft, a new suit of ringmail, and a full helm with visor. His sons are to be taken to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) to serve [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister), the eldest as a squire and the younger as a page with the opportunity to rise to [knighthood](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Willit.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
