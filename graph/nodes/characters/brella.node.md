## Origins

Brella was originally in service to [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), running his household while he stayed in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Brella.cite_ref-Rasos58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

On the suggestion of [Varys](wiki:Varys), [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) hires her as [Sansa's](wiki:Sansa_Stark) new maid together with [Shae](wiki:Shae), replacing earlier maids that spied on Sansa for his sister [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister). Tyrion considers her a good choice, as her earlier service for [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) has given her some practice at being blind, deaf and mute. However, [Shae](wiki:Shae) complains about her loud snoring, which Tyrion also notices.(wiki:Brella.cite_ref-Rasos58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Brella.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

After the death of King [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and the disappearance of [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), she is questioned by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), telling him of Sansa's constant praying, both in the sept and at night in the [godswood](wiki:Godswood).(wiki:Brella.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Feast for Crows

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) searches her as part of her investigation into the disappearance of [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), finding her in a brothel in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), where she does the washing. Brella explains that she has no knowledge of Sansa's disappearance. She complains bitterly that now that she has served two traitors to the crown, she can no longer find work with nobles and thus has to do washing for whores. She tells Brienne about Sansa's constant praying, both in the sept and at night in the [godswood](wiki:Godswood), and suggests that Sansa most likely fled north, as that is where her gods are.(wiki:Brella.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) She also informs Brienne about how Sansa begged King [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) for the life of Ser [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard), which makes Brienne believe that Ser Dontos helped Sansa flee and that finding him will lead her to Sansa. Brella also shares that there was little warmth between Sansa and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), making Brienne believe that Sansa might have fled her husband just as much as King [Joffrey's](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) murder.(wiki:Brella.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Brella later tells [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne) that Brienne is looking for Sansa, prompting his wish to find Brienne, as he thinks that through Sansa he might find Tyrion.(wiki:Brella.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
