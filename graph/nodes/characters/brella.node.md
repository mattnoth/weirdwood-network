## Identity

Brella is a servant in King's Landing, formerly in service to Renly Baratheon and later to Sansa Stark (wiki:Brella).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Baratheon, formerly (track_b: Allegiances). `SWORN_TO` House Lannister, formerly (track_b: Allegiances).

## Narrative Arc

Brella originally ran Renly Baratheon's household in King's Landing. On Varys's suggestion, Tyrion Lannister hired her as Sansa Stark's maid alongside Shae, replacing maids who had spied on Sansa for Cersei. Tyrion considered her a good choice, noting her prior service to Renly had given her practice at being "blind, deaf and mute." After the death of King Joffrey and Sansa's disappearance, she was questioned by Lord Tywin Lannister, reporting Sansa's constant praying. Later, Brienne of Tarth found her working in a King's Landing brothel doing washing, as serving two traitors to the crown had made her unemployable by nobles. She told Brienne that Sansa likely fled north, where her gods were (wiki:Brella).
