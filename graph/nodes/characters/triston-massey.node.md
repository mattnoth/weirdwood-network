## Origins

When [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) prepared to begin his [conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), he summoned his friends and [bannermen](wiki:Bannermen) to attend to him on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), Lord Massey among them. Despite being sworn to [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon) of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), Triston had closer ties to [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen).(wiki:Triston_Massey.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

After Aegon was crowned as [King of All Westeros](wiki:King_of_All_Westeros), he created the first [small council](wiki:Small_council) and Lord Triston had the honor of becoming [master of laws](wiki:Master_of_laws).(wiki:Triston_Massey.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)
