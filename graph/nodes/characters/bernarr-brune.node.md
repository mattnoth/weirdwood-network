## Origins

Bernarr was the [squire](wiki:Squire) of Lord [Alyn Stokeworth](wiki:Alyn_Stokeworth), who was the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) for the newly crowned King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen) in [37 AC](wiki:37_AC). When Lord Alyn was slain by the rebel [Harren the Red](wiki:Harren_the_Red), Bernarr avenged him by killing Harren. Bernarr was rewarded with a [knighthood](wiki:Knight) by the grateful King Aenys.(wiki:Bernarr_Brune.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

In [42 AC](wiki:42_AC), Ser Bernarr was present at [Visenya's Hill](wiki:Visenya's_Hill) when King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) was challenged by Ser [Damon Morrigen](wiki:Damon_Morrigen), [Grand Captain](wiki:Grand_Captain) of the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons), to a [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven) to settle his legitimacy to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). Bernarr kept quiet when Maegor called upon those who would stand beside him, but was shamed into action by the volunteering of [Dick Bean](wiki:Dick_Bean), a simple [man-at-arms](wiki:Man-at-arms). Ser Bernarr spoke out, offering to stand beside King Maegor, and noting that all present were no [true knights](wiki:True_knight). Bernarr's appeal succeeded, and many offered their swords.(wiki:Bernarr_Brune.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

During the fight against the Warrior's Sons, Ser Bernarr was killed.(wiki:Bernarr_Brune.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
