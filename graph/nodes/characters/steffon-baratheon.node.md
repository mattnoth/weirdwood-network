## Identity

Steffon Baratheon was the Lord of Storm's End and head of House Baratheon during the reigns of Jaehaerys II and Aerys II Targaryen. Father of Robert, Stannis, and Renly Baratheon (wiki:Steffon_Baratheon).

## Origins

Born 246 AC, son of Ormund Baratheon and Rhaelle Targaryen (track_b: Born, Father, Mother).

## Narrative Arc

In his youth, Steffon served as page and squire in King's Landing, becoming good friends with his cousin Prince Aerys Targaryen and fellow page Tywin Lannister. In 260 AC he participated in the War of the Ninepenny Kings as a squire; his father Ormund was struck down by Maelys Blackfyre and died in Steffon's arms. Steffon became Lord of Storm's End. Robert was born in 262 AC, Stannis in 264 AC, and Renly in 277 AC. Around 268 AC, Steffon first brought young Robert and Stannis to court. Years later he revealed that the impressive figure on the Iron Throne had been Tywin Lannister, not Aerys — who had cut himself on the throne. After the Defiance of Duskendale in 277 AC, the paranoid Aerys summoned his old friend Steffon and sent him to Volantis to find a bride of Valyrian blood for Prince Rhaegar. Steffon failed to find one, but he and Cassana found a talented fool — Patchface. On the return voyage in 278–279 AC, their ship the Windproud was wrecked in Shipbreaker Bay. Steffon and Cassana died; Robert and Stannis watched from Storm's End's parapets (wiki:Steffon_Baratheon).
