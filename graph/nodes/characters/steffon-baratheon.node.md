## Origins

### Early life

To make peace with Lord [Lyonel Baratheon](wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon), King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) betrothed his youngest daughter, Princess [Rhaelle Targaryen](wiki:Rhaelle_Targaryen), to Lyonel's heir [Ormund](wiki:Ormund_Baratheon), and sent her to live at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End). In [245 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Rhaelle married now-Lord Ormund, and their son Steffon was born a year later.(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

In his youth, Steffon served as a [page](wiki:Page) and [squire](wiki:Squire) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), where he became good friends with his cousin, Prince [Aerys Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), and another page, [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister).(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

In [260 AC](wiki:260_AC), Steffon participated as a [squire](wiki:Squire) in the [War of the Ninepenny Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Ninepenny_Kings) in the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones). Unfortunately, his father, Ormund Baratheon, [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) and commander of the expedition, was one of the first to perish in the war. Ormund was struck down by [Maelys I Blackfyre](wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre), commander of the [Band of Nine](wiki:Band_of_Nine), and died in Steffon's arms.(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) With the death of his father, Steffon became [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End) and [Lord Paramount of the Stormlands](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Stormlands).

### Lord of Storm's End

In [262 AC](wiki:262_AC), [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_Baratheon) was born to Steffon and his wife, Lady [Cassana Estermont](wiki:Cassana_Estermont), at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End).(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) Their son [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) followed in [264 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-7)

Around [268 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Steffon first took his son, Stannis (then four years old), to court at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) in King's Landing, along with his eldest son, Robert (by then aged five or six). The children believed they saw King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) holding court on the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), and they felt that the king had been as noble as the [dragon skulls](wiki:Dragon_skulls) on the walls were fearsome. Years later, Steffon revealed it had been the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), whom they had been impressed by, as King Aerys had cut himself on the throne earlier that day, and Tywin had taken over for him.(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

In [277 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Steffon and Cassana's son [Renly](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) was born at Storm's End.(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-9)

In the wake of the [Defiance of Duskendale](wiki:Defiance_of_Duskendale) in [277 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Steffon's old friend and cousin, King Aerys II Targaryen, summoned Steffon to court and named him to the [small council](wiki:Small_council). Aerys had been growing paranoid about his Hand, Tywin Lannister, and his son, Rhaegar Targaryen, believing they were conspiring against him, and so turned to his other childhood friend. In [278 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Aerys sent Steffon to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) to find a bride *"of noble birth from an old [Valyrian](wiki:Valyria) bloodline"* for Prince Rhaegar, and it was rumored that Aerys intended to replace Tywin with Steffon upon the latter's return.(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

Steffon, traveling with his wife Cassana, failed to find a suitable bride for Rhaegar, but he did send a message to Storm's End saying that they had found a very talented [fool](wiki:Fool). According to Steffon, Robert would be delighted by the fool, who was so funny he might teach Steffon's dour second son, Stannis, how to laugh. However, during the return journey, their ship was caught in a severe storm. Within sight of Robert and Stannis, who were standing on the parapets of Storm's End, the *[Windproud](wiki:Windproud)* was smashed on the rocks and sank in [Shipbreaker Bay](wiki:Shipbreaker_Bay). Steffon, Cassana, and hundreds of sailors drowned.(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) The only survivor was the fool, [Patchface](wiki:Patchface), who washed up ashore three days later, having lost his wits.(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

### Legacy

Steffon was succeeded as Lord of Storm's End by his eldest son, Robert.(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

According to [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle), Aerys was enraged by Steffon's death, and believed it had been arranged by Tywin for fear of being replaced as Hand.(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6)

The ship *[Lord Steffon](wiki:Lord_Steffon)* is likely [named after](wiki:Named_after) Steffon, as is [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth)'s son [Steffon](wiki:Steffon_Seaworth).

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

At [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), [Cressen](wiki:Cressen), [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s [maester](wiki:Maester), recalls the letter Lord Steffon sent from [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) about [Patchface](wiki:Patchface), and how he raised Robert, Stannis, and Renly, after their father's death.(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

At [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye), [armorer](wiki:Armorer) and [steward](wiki:Steward) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), tells [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) he was a blacksmith at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) while Lord Steffon was still alive.(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Stannis Baratheon's letter to all the [lords](wiki:Lord) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), which proclaims himself the true king and heir of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), includes the citation that he is the trueborn son of Steffon and Cassana.(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Stannis tells [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) that he stopped believing in [gods](wiki:Gods) when his mother and father were killed.(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

### A Storm of Swords

[Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) tells [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) how her brother [Rhaegar](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) once defeated Lord Steffon in a tourney.(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-14)

When considering the possibility that [dragons](wiki:Dragon) could be reborn, Stannis recalls the time that Steffon first took him to court, along with Robert, and they saw the [dragon skulls](wiki:Dragon_skulls) in the Red Keep.(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

### A Feast for Crows

[Queen Regent](wiki:Queen_Regent) [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) recalls that [the Estermonts](wiki:House_Estermont) are her in-laws, via her late husband [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_Baratheon)'s mother. Disparaging the Estermonts, Cersei feels that Steffon must have chosen Cassana "in a fit of lust or madness".(wiki:Steffon_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
