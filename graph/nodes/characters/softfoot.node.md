## Appearances & Description

Softfoot is a little, grey man. He claims to have raped a hundred women in his youth.(wiki:Softfoot.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Softfoot is part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), the force that leaves [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) to search for [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings). Softfoot is one of the conspirators who intend to murder Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont). His part in the conspiracy is to kill the lord commander, with the help of [Small Paul](wiki:Small_Paul).(wiki:Softfoot.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

At some point Softfoot dies and is turned into a [wight](wiki:Wight) who attacks [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) and [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) at an abandoned wildling village. They are only able to get away with the help of [Coldhands](wiki:Coldhands).(wiki:Softfoot.cite_ref-Rasos46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
