## Identity

Larra Rogare, youngest daughter of Lysandro, was married to Prince Viserys and became known as Larra of Lys.

## Origins

### Early life and marriage

Larra was born in [115 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), the youngest daughter of [Lysandro Rogare](wiki:Lysandro_Rogare), the head of the wealthy and influential banking family in [Lys](wiki:Lys). When Lysandro came into the possession of the captive Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen), he quickly(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-9) married him to his daughter Larra, despite her being seven years his senior.(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) Not long thereafter, Larra's uncle, [Drazenko Rogare](wiki:Drazenko_Rogare), approached Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) at [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear), informing him where Viserys could be found. Lysandro Rogare's ransom terms included that Velaryon had to swear upon his honor that Larra's marriage to Viserys would not be set aide, for whatever reason. When Velaryon agreed to Lysandro's terms, he brought Viserys back to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC). Larra accompanied him,(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) with three of her brothers, [Moredo](wiki:Moredo_Rogare), [Lotho](wiki:Lotho_Rogare), and [Roggerio](wiki:Roggerio_Rogare).(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), his court, and the city celebrated Prince Viserys's return, as he had been believed dead for five years. The king's brother's "revival", along with the fact that Larra was of childbearing age, helped resolve the question of succession to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), open since the end of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), and particularly since King Aegon III had married the young [Daenaera Velaryon](wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon). The [king's regents](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III) quickly confirmed the legitimacy of Larra's marriage to Viserys. Viserys also confided to [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Munkun](wiki:Munkun) that he had consummated his marriage.(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

### Life in King's Landing

Larra moved into the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) with her husband, but did not manage to adapt to the customs of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms). All her clothing came from Lys, even her smallclothes; Rogare ships delivered the latest Lysene fashions to her three times a year. She made no attempt to learn the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue), and instead used translators. She kept only Lysene servants, and even her protectors were Lysene, under the command of her brother [Moredo](wiki:Moredo_Rogare) and [Sandoq the Shadow](wiki:Sandoq_the_Shadow). Larra also had no interest in the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) or the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods) of the [north](wiki:North), but continued to worship Lysene [gods](wiki:Gods), to whom she did obeisances.(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

Though the people of King's Landing rejoiced when Prince Viserys announced to the royal court that Larra was pregnant, they soon came to dislike and mistrust her. [Cats](wiki:Cat) were frequently seen coming and going from Larra's chambers, and people began to whisper that they were her [spies](wiki:Spy). Some even claimed that Larra was able to transform herself into a cat, and darker rumors followed soon after. Her worship of the god [Yndros](wiki:Yndros) caused men to whisper that Larra would transform herself into a man to visit the [brothels](wiki:Brothel) on the [Street of Silk](wiki:Street_of_Silk). Because of her worship of [Saagael](wiki:Saagael), she was blamed every time a child went missing. Larra's brothers were also greatly disliked, as they were proud, grasping, and ambitious, and their foreign origin and practices led to suspicion and mistrust. By late [134 AC](wiki:134_AC), some of King's Landing began to fear that the Rogares would soon rule the Seven Kingdoms, with Larra holding the strings.(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)

Larra gave birth during the third month of [135 AC](wiki:135_AC) to a son whom Viserys named [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen), [after](wiki:Named_after) his brother, King Aegon III. Prince Aegon was anointed with the seven oils of the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), but when it became known that Larra wished to have Aegon blessed by her own gods as well, people began to whisper about obscene ceremonies and blood sacrifices.(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8)

Following the sudden deaths of Larra's father Lysandro and her uncle Drazenko, Larra's eldest brother [Lysaro](wiki:Lysaro_Rogare) drained the [gold](wiki:Currency) held by the [Rogare Bank](wiki:Rogare_Bank). When rumors began to spread that the Rogare Bank was unsound, [House Rogare](wiki:House_Rogare) fell from power in Lys. The consequences extended to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), where lords and merchants realized that the coin they had entrusted to the Rogare Bank was lost, and Lotho and Roggerio were arrested. Ser [Marston Waters](wiki:Marston_Waters) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), the new [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), also ordered Larra's arrest, but when Ser [Amaury Peake](wiki:Amaury_Peake) arrived at [Maegor's Holdfast](wiki:Maegor's_Holdfast) to arrest her, he found himself denied entrance by Larra's husband Viserys, and Viserys's brother, King Aegon III.(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-9)

During the [secret siege](wiki:Secret_siege), Larra, Viserys, Aegon III, Queen [Daenaera Velaryon](wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon), and several servants were trapped in Maegor's Holdfast for eighteen days. According to Marston Waters, Lord [Thaddeus Rowan](wiki:Thaddeus_Rowan), the previous Hand, had confessed to having plotted with Larra's three brothers to [poison](wiki:Poison) King Aegon III and Queen Daenaera in order to crown Viserys king, with Larra as his queen. When Rowan was brought before Maegor's Holdfast to confirm his confessions, Viserys was quick to reveal Rowan had been [tortured](wiki:Torture) into giving a false confession. Six days after Marston fulfilled his king's command to arrest those who had falsely implicated Lord Rowan and the Rogares, the siege came to an end. After his arrest, the conspirator [Septon](wiki:Septon) [Bernard](wiki:Bernard) confessed that he had been motivated by his religion, as he felt that Larra and her foreign gods had no place in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). He claimed that it had been intended for Larra to die, so Viserys could ascend the throne and take a "proper Westerosi queen."(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-10)(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

In [136 AC](wiki:136_AC), when trials were held for those implicated in the siege and turmoil that had gone before, Larra was pregnant with her second child. She attended the trials together with her husband Viserys, her young son Aegon, and his [wet nurse](wiki:Wet_nurse). Larra was also present when Lord [Torrhen Manderly](wiki:Torrhen_Manderly) passed his judgment on Lotho and Roggerio, and wept when the sentences were proclaimed.(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-11)

On the eve of [Smith's Day](wiki:Smith's_Day) in [136 AC](wiki:136_AC), Larra gave birth to her second son, [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_II)).(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-12) When Aegon struck the infant Aemon with the [dragon egg](wiki:Dragon_egg) that had been placed in his cradle, she rushed to separate them. Two years later, Larra gave birth to a daughter, [Naerys](wiki:Naerys_Targaryen).(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_viserys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

Larra never felt part of the court at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), and was never truly happy in Westeros.(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_viserys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4) In [139 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), a year after having given birth to Naerys, Larra returned to Lys. Viserys, an otherwise charming man, grew stern after Larra left him and their children.(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) Larra eventually died in [145 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-AMA_4-1) of unknown causes.

### Legacy

The royal lineage of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) descends from Larra Rogare.(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_targaryen_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4) In [153 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Viserys had his and Larra's children, Aegon and Naerys, wed to each other, while Aemon became a [knight](wiki:Knight) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2)(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_viserys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5) Aegon and Naerys's son [Daeron](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen) was born on the last day of that year.(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) After Viserys's nephew, King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen), died childless in [171 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), he ascended to the throne as King Viserys II. As Larra was long dead by that time, King Viserys II had no queen.(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_viserys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-6) A year later, Viserys died and was succeeded by his and Larra's eldest son, King [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen), with Naerys as his queen.(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_viserys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-7)

## Appearances & Description

Larra was considered a great beauty.(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_viserys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) She had pale skin,(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) and the silver-gold hair and [purple eyes](wiki:Purple_eyes) of [Valyria](wiki:Valyria).(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_viserys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Larra was tall and willowy.(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_viserys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) She only dressed in Lysene fashion, even during her time in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Larra was fluent in [High Valyrian](wiki:High_Valyrian) and the dialects of [Lys](wiki:Lysene_language), [Myr](wiki:Myrish_language), [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyroshi_language), and [Old Volantis](wiki:Volantene_language), but not the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue).(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) She worshiped several Lysene [deities](wiki:Gods), including [Pantera](wiki:Pantera), [Yndros of the Twilight](wiki:Yndros_of_the_Twilight), [Bakkalon](wiki:Bakkalon), and [Saagael](wiki:Saagael).(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

According to [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom), Larra liked to hear the music played by [Sandoq the Shadow](wiki:Sandoq_the_Shadow), her guardsman, on a large [stringed instrument](wiki:Sandoq_the_Shadow's_instrument), although it always made her cry. Mushroom also claimed that he could occasionally make Larra laugh, despite her not understanding the Common Tongue. He said that she preferred crying to Sandoq's music over laughing at his japes.(wiki:Larra_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
