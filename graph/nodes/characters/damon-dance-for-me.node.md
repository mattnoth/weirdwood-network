## Identity

Damon Dance-for-Me is one of the Bastard's Boys, a group of brutal men in service to Ramsay Bolton. He is present at the forced wedding of "Arya Stark" (actually Jeyne Poole) to Ramsay at Winterfell. He participates in seizing Theon Greyjoy when Ramsay summons him to escort the bride to the bedchamber. He is described whistling cheerfully while climbing the stairs to the bedchamber.
