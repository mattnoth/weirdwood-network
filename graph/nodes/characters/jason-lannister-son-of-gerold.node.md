## Identity

Ser Jason Lannister was a knight from House Lannister, the fourth and youngest son of Lord Gerold Lannister and his second wife, Lady Rohanne Webber (wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold)).

## Origins

Born 229 AC (track_b: Born). Son of Gerold Lannister and Rohanne Webber (track_b: Father, Mother). His mother disappeared within a year after his birth (wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold)).

## Appearances & Description

A fierce child, Jason grew into a prideful and quarrelsome man with a fiery temper. Though openly scornful of his own brother, he was quick to take offense when others disparaged Lord Tytos Lannister (wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold)).

## Narrative Arc

Jason grew up at Casterly Rock where he often quarreled with his weak-willed brother Lord Tytos. At fourteen, he sired a bastard daughter, Lynora Hill, by a serving girl. At fifteen, he deflowered Lord Stackspear's maiden daughter Alys, getting her with child. Lord Tytos insisted Jason marry Alys, likely at Lady Jeyne Marbrand's behest. Alys died in childbirth giving Jason a son, Damon. After a fortnight of mourning, Tytos and Jeyne compelled Jason to wed again, this time to Lord Prester's daughter Marla, who was twice his age. She gave Jason two sons and two daughters, including Joanna (who later married Tywin) and Stafford (wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold)).

Anxious to escape Casterly Rock, Maester Beldon was relieved when Jason left for Feastfires after his marriage. Jason died in 260 AC at Bloodstone during the War of the Ninepenny Kings (wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold)).
