## Identity

Jason Lannister (son of Gerold) (229–260 AC) — child of Gerold Lannister and Rohanne Webber; married to Alys Stackspear and Marla Prester; sworn to House Lannister; Ser. One of 2 characters named Jason Lannister.

## Origins

Jason was born the youngest of four children to Lord [Gerold the Golden](wiki:Gerold_Lannister) and his second wife, Lady [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber). His mother disappeared within a year after his birth.(wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Jason grew up at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) where he often quarreled with his weak-willed brother, Lord [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister). At age fourteen,(wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold).cite_ref-sample_4-1) he sired a [bastard](wiki:Bastard) daughter, [Lynora Hill](wiki:Lynora_Hill),(wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold).cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_lannister_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) by a serving girl. At fifteen, he deflowered Lord [Stackspear](wiki:Lord_Stackspear_(father_of_Alys))'s maiden daughter,(wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold).cite_ref-sample_4-2) the Lady [Alys](wiki:Alys_Stackspear),(wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold).cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_lannister_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) getting her with child as well. In a rare display of firmness, Lord Tytos insisted that his brother marry the girl, though most believe this was done at the behest of Tytos's wife, the Lady [Jeyne Marbrand](wiki:Jeyne_Marbrand). Lady Alys died in childbirth, giving Jason a son named [Damon](wiki:Damon_Lannister_(son_of_Jason)). Anxious to avoid further scandals and bastards, Tytos and Jeyne gave Jason a fortnight to mourn, and then compelled him to wed again, this time to Lord [Prester](wiki:House_Prester)'s daughter, the Lady [Marla](wiki:Marla_Prester). Though she was twice his age,(wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold).cite_ref-sample_4-3) she gave Jason three daughters and three sons.(wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold).cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_lannister_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) After the marriage, Jason departed Casterly Rock to reside with his lady wife at [Feastfires](wiki:Feastfires), to the relief of even Casterly Rock's [maester](wiki:Maester) [Beldon](wiki:Beldon).(wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold).cite_ref-sample_4-4)

When the [War of the Ninepenny Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Ninepenny_Kings) broke out in [260 AC](wiki:260_AC), as Lord Tytos was no warrior, Ser Jason was given command of the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) host—a thousand [knights](wiki:Knight) and ten thousand men-at-arms—at the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones). Jason died on [Bloodstone](wiki:Bloodstone).(wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) By some later accounts, he was slain by the [Blackfyre](wiki:House_Blackfyre) [pretender](wiki:Blackfyre_Pretenders), [Maelys the Monstrous](wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre), but contemporary accounts suggest that it was a flux of the bowels that killed Ser Jason.(wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold).cite_ref-sample_4-5) After his death, Lord [Roger Reyne](wiki:Roger_Reyne) seized command of the westermen host.(wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Appearances & Description

A fierce child, Jason Lannister grew into a prideful and quarrelsome man with a fiery temper. Though openly scornful of his own brother, Jason was quick to take offense when others disparaged Lord [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister).(wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold).cite_ref-sample_4-0)
