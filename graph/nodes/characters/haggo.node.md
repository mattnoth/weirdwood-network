## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

As a wedding gift to the newly-wed [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) and Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), Haggo gives them a large leather whip with a silver handle.(wiki:Haggo.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

When [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) bares steel during a feast in [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak) and threatens Daenerys, Drogo orders his bloodriders to seize him. Haggo shatters Viserys's wrist while the others pin and disarm him.(wiki:Haggo.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

When Daenerys intends to have [Mirri Maz Duur](wiki:Mirri_Maz_Duur) heal Drogo using magic, his bloodriders, including Haggo, intend to stop her. [Rakharo](wiki:Rakharo) and [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) both fight Haggo, killing him.(wiki:Haggo.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
