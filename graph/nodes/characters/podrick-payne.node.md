## Origins

Podrick was born a member of a lesser branch of [House Payne](wiki:House_Payne). His father was a [squire](wiki:Squire) to richer cousins and his mother was a chandler's daughter. Podrick's father died in [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion), and his mother abandoned him when he was four years old to run away with a wandering singer. Podrick was left with Ser [Cedric Payne](wiki:Cedric_Payne), one of the cousins his father had squired for. The knight took care of Podrick, although Cedric might have treated the boy more like a servant than a relative.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

Podrick had a good dog named [Hero](wiki:Hero_(dog)) when he was young.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Podrick Payne](wiki:Category:Images_of_Podrick_Payne)*

Podrick is a skinny boy with straight, thin hair(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and a thin neck.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Pod is quiet, shy, and timid,(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Racok67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and he stammers when he speaks.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) The boy is reluctant to make eye contact when conversing.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Despite his timidity, Podrick can be courageous.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Racok61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) calls his [banners](wiki:Bannerman), Ser [Cedric Payne](wiki:Cedric_Payne) brings Podrick along to tend his [horse](wiki:Horse) and clean his mail. Cedric dies in the fighting in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)

Podrick, alone in the army, attaches himself to Ser [Lorimer the Belly](wiki:Lorimer_the_Belly), a [hedge knight](wiki:Hedge_knight) who is part of Lord [Leo Lefford](wiki:Leo_Lefford)'s troops protecting the baggage train. Lorimer steals a ham from Lord Tywin's personal stores and is hanged for it, but Podrick, who had shared the ham, is spared due to his family's name. Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) takes charge of him.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5)

Podrick passes into the service of [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) as his squire after Tyrion joins the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) forces in the riverlands. He is so quiet that Tyrion on one occasion makes him stick his tongue out to confirm that he has not lost it, as his relative Ser [Ilyn Payne](wiki:Ilyn_Payne) did. Tyrion suspects that Podrick has been assigned to him as some cruel jape.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Pod helps prepare Tyrion for the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) The squire wakens Tyrion at the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn) when the Lannisters are informed of the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps).(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Upon their arrival in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Tyrion sends Podrick to Ser [Aron Santagar](wiki:Aron_Santagar), master-at-arms of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), for training. His instruction comes to an end when Aron perishes in the [bread riots](wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing).(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-6)

Armed with a sword, Podrick insists upon accompanying Tyrion in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), joining Tyrion's several mounted sorties.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Racok59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Racok61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) When Ser [Mandon Moore](wiki:Mandon_Moore) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) tries to assassinate Tyrion, Pod pushes the knight into the waters of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush), where Mandon drowns due to his heavy armor.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Racok61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2) After the battle, Tyrion tells Podrick to keep secret his involvement in Mandon's death.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Racok67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Podrick remains loyal to Tyrion while the [dwarf](wiki:Dwarf) recuperates from his injuries, and the squire searches for a walking stick for Tyrion.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

Podrick is part of Tyrion's party that goes forth to meet the delegation from [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), and the squire carries the royal standard of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). Tyrion tests his knowledge of [heraldry](wiki:Heraldry) by having him describe the banners of the Dornish nobles.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

After Tyrion marries [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), the pair move into spacious apartments above the [Kitchen Keep](wiki:Kitchen_Keep) within the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep). Podrick and Sansa's maids reside in small adjoining chambers.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Rasos58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Sansa is wary of Podrick at first, as he is a cousin to Ser [Ilyn Payne](wiki:Ilyn_Payne). However, she soon comes to realize that Podrick is as frightened of her as she is of Ilyn. Whenever she speaks to him, he turns red. The girl sees a pimple by Podrick's nose.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) The squire dresses in purple, white, and gold, the colors of [House Payne](wiki:House_Payne), for the wedding of Joffrey and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2)

When Tyrion is imprisoned in a tower cell after being arrested for the murder of King at his [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding), Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) allows Podrick to serve Tyrion. Podrick brings [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) to see the prisoner.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Tyrion is condemned to death after Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) kills Tyrion's champion, Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell), in [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat).(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and [Varys](wiki:Varys) help Tyrion escape, however, and Tyrion kills his father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), before disappearing from the Red Keep.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Rasos77.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Podrick talks with [Brella](wiki:Brella), [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark)'s former maid, who tells him that [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) has visited her and that she is searching for Sansa. This prompts Podrick's wish to find Brienne, as he hopes that her finding Sansa will lead him to the missing [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister). He starts to follow Brienne who is on her way towards [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale).(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Brienne notices a stye beneath one of his eyes.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

Brienne first notices Podrick from a distance in [Rosby](wiki:Rosby), riding on [his horse](wiki:Old_piebald_rounsey),(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) then again outside of Duskendale, later bumping into him on a street of the town, knocking him over. She remembers seeing him earlier and his behavior seems suspicious. She notices him again at the [Hollard castle](wiki:Hollard_castle) on the road to [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) and realizes that he has been stalking her at least since Rosby. She confronts him and learns his intention.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) Brienne agrees to bring Podrick with her and along the way helps him to improve his swordsmanship.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-7)

Brienne hires [Dick Crabb](wiki:Dick_Crabb) to guide her and Podrick from [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) to the [Whispers](wiki:Whispers), where Dick had sent a [fool](wiki:Fool); Brienne hopes it is Sansa's onetime companion, Ser [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard). Distrusting Dick, Brienne tasks Pod with staying on guard when she sleeps. Dick entertains the travelers by telling stories of [Crackclaw Point](wiki:Crackclaw_Point), including [squishers](wiki:Squishers). The boy is wary of Crackclaw's woods.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

When they arrives at the Whispers, Brienne has Podrick stay back to guard their horses. Instead of Dontos, the fool turns out to be the outlaw [Shagwell](wiki:Shagwell), who is with [Pyg](wiki:Pyg) and [Timeon](wiki:Timeon); the three are former members of the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions). After Shagwell kills Dick, Brienne slays Pyg and Timeon. Pod throws stones at Shagwell, who falls to the ground and yields to Brienne. Shagwell is forced to dig a grave for Dick, but the fool then tries to kill Brienne. Podrick warns his companion, however, and she kills the fool.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Joined by Ser [Hyle Hunt](wiki:Hyle_Hunt), Brienne and Pod return to Maidenpool, where Brienne buys an orange from *[Seastrider](wiki:Seastrider)* for the boy. They travel with Septon [Meribald](wiki:Meribald), who tells Podrick about the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), the [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters), and the [Quiet Isle](wiki:Quiet_Isle).(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) Podrick wants to stay with Brienne when they visit the Quiet Isle, but Brother [Narbert](wiki:Narbert) insists that the boy share a cell with Hyle.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1)

At the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads), Brienne battles other remnants of the Brave Companions led by [Rorge](wiki:Rorge), and [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) saves her from [Biter](wiki:Biter).(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) Brienne, Podrick, and Hyle are taken captive by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) after [Lem](wiki:Lem)'s party returns to the inn. Podrick tells their captors that he has fought in battles and has killed.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

When brought before [Lady Stoneheart](wiki:Lady_Stoneheart), Brienne is given the choice between killing Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), or dying herself by noose, a choice formulated as "the sword or the noose". When Brienne refuses to kill Jaime, she is sentenced to die. Podrick and Hyle are judged guilty for being allied with [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) as well, and are sentenced to die alongside Brienne. However, when the brotherhood begins to hang them, Brienne screams a word while watching Pod choke.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) According to a semi-canon source, she screams "sword" in an attempt to save Podrick from the noose.(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3)(wiki:Podrick_Payne.cite_ref-24)
