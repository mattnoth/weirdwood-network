## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Vylarr is the captain of the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) household guard in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), which now numbers one hundred men.(wiki:Vylarr.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) When [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) arrives in the capital as the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), he orders Vylarr to have the heads of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s retinue removed from spikes at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) and instead given over to the [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters).(wiki:Vylarr.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Vylarr commands the guard that accompanies Tyrion back to the [Broken Anvil](wiki:Broken_Anvil) through the streets of King's Landing.(wiki:Vylarr.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) is escorted by Vylarr and twenty red cloaks when she tours the gates of the city.(wiki:Vylarr.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Cersei later orders Vylarr and his men to imprison [begging brothers](wiki:Begging_brothers) in the dungeon.(wiki:Vylarr.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

When Tyrion informs Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) of his terms for peace, he commands Vylarr to escort Cleos back to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), taking all of the Lannister guardsmen with him and thereby depriving Cersei of her own red cloaks.(wiki:Vylarr.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) The thirty guards(wiki:Vylarr.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) sent by [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) to escort Cleos to the capital also return to Riverrun in Vylarr's company as well.(wiki:Vylarr.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

Unbeknownst to Vylarr, Tyrion has included a thief, a [poisoner](wiki:Poison), a [mummer](wiki:Mummer), and a murderer in the party in a [plot to free Jaime Lannister](wiki:Plot_to_free_Jaime_Lannister) from captivity at Riverrun.(wiki:Vylarr.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) After their arrival, the conspirators temporarily free Ser [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), but the Kingslayer is soon subdued. Some of the Lannister guardsmen rush to aid him but are also subdued. Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) hangs the four conspirators and any red cloaks who attempted to aid them, while those who did not are instead imprisoned.(wiki:Vylarr.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Vylarr is described as the captain-of-guards and a member of the household of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister).(wiki:Vylarr.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Vylarr is described as the captain-of-guards and a member of the household at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Vylarr.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) It is unknown if Vylarr was actually freed, before or after the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun).
