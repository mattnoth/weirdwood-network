## Appearances & Description

Snowylocks is white-haired.(wiki:Snowylocks.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Like the other children of the forest, she is smaller than humans, but not childlike. She has nut-brown skin, dappled like a [deer](wiki:Deer)'s with paler spots. Her hands have only three fingers and a thumb, with sharp black claws instead of nails. She has large ears that can hear things that no man can hear.(wiki:Snowylocks.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) She has large golden eyes slitted like those of a [cat](wiki:Cat),(wiki:Snowylocks.cite_ref-Radwd13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) allowing her to see in dark passages.(wiki:Snowylocks.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

She does not speak the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue).(wiki:Snowylocks.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

In the [cave of the three-eyed crow](wiki:Cave_of_the_three-eyed_crow) [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) and his companions [Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed) and [Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed) meet a remnant of the children of the forest, who serve the [last greenseer](wiki:Last_greenseer), Lord Brynden. Their names are too long for the human tongue, according to [Leaf](wiki:Leaf), so Bran and Meera give them names. Meera names Snowylocks for the color of her hair. Bran does not know what Snowylocks thinks about her new name.(wiki:Snowylocks.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

When Brynden says it is time for Bran to awaken his gifts as a [greenseer](wiki:Greenseer), Leaf beckons, and Snowylocks brings forth a [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) bowl carved with a dozen faces, like the ones the [heart trees](wiki:Heart_tree) wear. Inside the bowl is a white [weirwood seed paste](wiki:Weirwood_seed_paste), thick and heavy, with dark red veins running through it. Snowylocks hands the bowl to Leaf to give to Bran to eat.(wiki:Snowylocks.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
