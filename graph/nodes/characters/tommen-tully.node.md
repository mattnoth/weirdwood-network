## Origins

When the riverlands were invaded by [Harwyn Hoare](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), Tommen's natural son, Ser [Samwell Rivers](wiki:Samwell_Rivers), was slain at the [Tumblestone](wiki:Tumblestone). Lord Tommen abandoned Riverrun without a fight and fled with all his strength to join the host assembled at [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall) under Lady [Agnes Blackwood](wiki:Agnes_Blackwood). Resistance, however, proved futile and the riverlands fell to King Harwyn.(wiki:Tommen_Tully.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
