## Appearances & Description

Hugh was tall and huge, with hands strong enough to twist [steel](wiki:Steel) bars. He wielded a warhammer. Hugh was not able to read or write.(wiki:Hugh_Hammer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Origins

Hugh was a blacksmith's [bastard](wiki:Bastard) on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) when the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) began.(wiki:Hugh_Hammer.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) When Prince [Jacaerys Velaryon](wiki:Jacaerys_Velaryon) called for dragonriders in [129 AC](wiki:129_AC), Hugh was allowed to mount [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor), the [dragon](wiki:Dragon) of the late King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Hugh_Hammer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Hugh fought valiantly for the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) in the [Battle in the Gullet](wiki:Battle_in_the_Gullet).(wiki:Hugh_Hammer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Hugh began to call himself Hugh Hammer.(wiki:Hugh_Hammer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
After the [fall of King's Landing](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing), Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) suggested that Hugh be wed to the daughter of the late Lord [Rosby](wiki:House_Rosby), but Rhaenyra declined. The queen instead granted [knighthood](wiki:Knight) to [Ulf White](wiki:Ulf_White) and Hugh and granted them lands on [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark).(wiki:Hugh_Hammer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) According to [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom), Hugh beat to death one of Rhaenyra's knights in a dispute over a maiden in the [Street of Silk](wiki:Street_of_Silk). When Daemon suggested that Houses [Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon) and [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) should be destroyed for their support to King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), he proposed granting [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) to Hugh, but after Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) opposed the idea, Rhaenyra refused the suggestion.(wiki:Hugh_Hammer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)
In [130 AC](wiki:130_AC), Ulf and Hugh were sent to help the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) defend the town of [Tumbleton](wiki:Tumbleton) from Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower)'s approaching host. During the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton), however, the [Two Betrayers](wiki:Two_Betrayers) defected to the [greens](wiki:Greens).(wiki:Hugh_Hammer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6)
Calling himself Lord Hammer, Hugh desired to become a king. He gained support from the soldiery who believed a [prophecy](wiki:Prophecy) which spoke of a new king arising once a hammer falls on a dragon.(wiki:Hugh_Hammer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7)(wiki:Hugh_Hammer.cite_ref-7) Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)) threw wine in his face when he made his claim known, and Hugh threatened to beat the boy. The next day Hugh wore a crown of black iron, which Ser [Roger Corne](wiki:Roger_Corne) knocked off. Hugh nailed three horseshoes to Roger's skull, and the ensuing fight between Hugh's and Roger's supporters led to three dead and twelve wounded. Hugh slept with the widow of a knight whom he had killed during First Tumbleton.(wiki:Hugh_Hammer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
The [Caltrops](wiki:Caltrops) conspired to kill Hugh and Ulf, but their plans were interrupted by the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton). While Hugh was preparing for battle, Ser [Jon Roxton](wiki:Jon_Roxton) killed the dragonseed with his [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) blade, [Orphan-Maker](wiki:Orphan-Maker). Jon was killed in turn by Hugh's men, allegedly after slipping on one of Hugh's entrails.(wiki:Hugh_Hammer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
