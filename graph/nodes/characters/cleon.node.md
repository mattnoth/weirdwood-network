## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Cleon's master, [Grazdan mo Ullhor](wiki:Grazdan_mo_Ullhor), is killed during the [fall of Astapor](wiki:Fall_of_Astapor) to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen).(wiki:Cleon.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) She installs a [ruling council](wiki:Ruling_council_of_Astapor) of three men to rule after her departure: a healer, a priest and a scholar.(wiki:Cleon.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Cleon stirs up the people of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor), claiming that the council is attempting to reinstall the deposed [Good Masters](wiki:Good_Masters). With the backing of the people, Cleon executes the council members and installs himself as king. He also has the male children of the deposed Good Masters kidnapped and castrated to train them into new [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied).(wiki:Cleon.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Cleon the Great sends [Ghael](wiki:Ghael) to Daenerys in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), offering a pact between Meereen and Astapor to attack [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai). To seal it, he would put aside his wives and wed her.(wiki:Cleon.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

### A Dance with Dragons

Lord Ghael delivers a gift on behalf of Cleon to Daenerys, gilded leather slippers decorated with green freshwater [pearls](wiki:Pearl).(wiki:Cleon.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Cleon is defeated in the [battle at the Horns of Hazzat](wiki:Battle_at_the_Horns_of_Hazzat). He is forced to flee back to Astapor, with his faux [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) following him in retreat.(wiki:Cleon.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Ghael beseeches Daenerys to aid Cleon with her more experienced Unsullied, but the Queen of Meereen declines.(wiki:Cleon.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Cleon is slain by his own men when he commands them to march out and attack the [Yunkai'i](wiki:Yunkai'i). He is succeeded by a string of unsuccessful rulers, including a man calling himself [Cleon the Second](wiki:Cleon_the_Second), his concubine, called [Queen Whore](wiki:Queen_Whore), and Cleon the Second's killer, called [King Cutthroat](wiki:King_Cutthroat).(wiki:Cleon.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) When the [Yunkai'i](wiki:Yunkai'i) attack during the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor), Cleon's body is disinterred and clad in armor after the [Green Grace](wiki:Green_Grace) of Astapor sees a vision that he would deliver them from the Yunkai'i.(wiki:Cleon.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) The corpse is then strapped onto a horse to lead the remnants of his new, inferior Unsullied into battle. They are opposed by the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown), the [Company of the Cat](wiki:Company_of_the_Cat), and an [iron legion](wiki:Iron_legion) from [New Ghis](wiki:New_Ghis),(wiki:Cleon.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) however, and all of the Astapori soldiers are killed.(wiki:Cleon.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) [Caggo](wiki:Caggo) is nicknamed Corpsekiller for striking down Cleon's body.(wiki:Cleon.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
