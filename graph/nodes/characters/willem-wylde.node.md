## Origins

When a place opened in the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen) gave the position to Ser Willem. Either Daeron did not know, or willfully ignored his father, [Aegon IV's](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen), promise to Ser [Quentyn Ball](wiki:Quentyn_Ball) that he would be raised to the Kingsguard. This was one of the contributing factors to Ser Quentyn siding with [Daemon Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) during the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion).(wiki:Willem_Wylde.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### Tourney at Ashford Meadow

Ser Willem, along with his two fellow brothers of the Kingsguard, Ser [Roland Crakehall](wiki:Roland_Crakehall_(Kingsguard)) and Ser [Donnel of Duskendale](wiki:Donnel_of_Duskendale), escorted the royal [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) princes to [Ashford](wiki:Ashford) for the [tourney](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC). After Prince [Aerion Targaryen](wiki:Aerion_Targaryen) invoked the [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven) all three [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) members present were made part of his seven. Ser Willem was carried off the field unconscious at the end of the trial.(wiki:Willem_Wylde.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
