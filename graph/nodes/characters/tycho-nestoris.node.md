## Appearances & Description

Tycho is tall, thin, and gaunt.(wiki:Tycho_Nestoris.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) He has a narrow face with dark eyes, and a long, thin beard, which almost reaches his waist.(wiki:Tycho_Nestoris.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Tycho_Nestoris.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Tycho wears a brimless three-tiered hat of purple felt, robes of a sober purple trimmed with [ermine](wiki:Ermine), and a high stiff collar.(wiki:Tycho_Nestoris.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Tycho_Nestoris.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

The banker is cultured and courteous, and he speaks the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue) fluently with only a faint accent.(wiki:Tycho_Nestoris.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Tycho is sent to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) to negotiate payment of the debt of the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) with King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon). He comes to [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea) with three [ships](wiki:Ships), and then travels to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) with Queen [Selyse](wiki:Selyse_Florent). [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), negotiates with Tycho for a loan to purchase food for the winter, and the use of his ships for a voyage. The [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) provides Tycho with guides to reach Stannis, who has left Castle Black.(wiki:Tycho_Nestoris.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Tycho travels to [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte), where he pays Lady [Sybelle Glover](wiki:Sybelle_Glover) a ransom for six [ironmen](wiki:Ragged_nine): [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley), [Fingers](wiki:Fingers_(ironman)), [Grimtongue](wiki:Grimtongue), [Qarl the Maid](wiki:Qarl_the_Maid), [Rook](wiki:Rook), and [Roggon Rustbeard](wiki:Roggon_Rustbeard). The ironborn escort Tycho as he goes through the snow toward [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), looking for Stannis. The king is not there, but Tycho finds [Mors Umber](wiki:Mors_Umber) outside the gates with some green boys. He takes [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) and [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole), who were with Mors Umber after their escape from Winterfell.(wiki:Tycho_Nestoris.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

After three days they arrive at Stannis's camp in a [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village). [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) and Ser [Clayton Suggs](wiki:Clayton_Suggs) are surprised to see the riders, and Clayton demands to know who they are. Tycho smoothly dismounts his [garron](wiki:Garron), doffs his hat, bows, and identifies himself. Asha laughs to see a Braavosi banker in the fraught situation in Stannis's camp, but Tycho recognizes her and presents her brother Theon to her as a "gift".(wiki:Tycho_Nestoris.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

### The Winds of Winter

Tycho is brought before Stannis, and the two sign an agreement, with Stannis reluctantly acknowledging the Iron Throne's debt to the [Iron Bank](wiki:Iron_Bank_of_Braavos) and Tycho agreeing to loan to Stannis. During the signing, Tycho points out that Stannis's ink has frozen, so the king slashes his own thumb and signs in blood. Stannis has Ser [Justin Massey](wiki:Justin_Massey) escort Tycho and "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)" back to the Wall. Afterwards, Justin is to accompany Tycho back to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Tycho_Nestoris.cite_ref-twowtheon_4-0)
