## Identity

Maron Greyjoy was the third son of King Balon IX Greyjoy and Lady Alannys Harlaw (track_b: Father, Mother).

## Origins

Born between 268–275 AC (track_b: Born). Son of Balon IX Greyjoy and Alannys Harlaw (track_b: Father, Mother). Died in 289 AC at Pyke during Greyjoy's Rebellion (track_b: Died).

## Allegiances

He is `SWORN_TO` House Greyjoy (track_b: Allegiance). He is `CULTURE_OF` the ironborn (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Maron died during the Siege of Pyke in 289 AC when Robert Baratheon's forces stormed the castle during Greyjoy's Rebellion. He was crushed when a wall collapsed during the assault, killed alongside the defenders of Pyke. His death, along with that of his brother Rodrik, left Theon as Balon's only surviving son (wiki:Maron_Greyjoy).
