## Origins

According to [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), [Theon](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) lived in awe and fear of their elder brothers, [Rodrik](wiki:Rodrik_Greyjoy) and Maron.(wiki:Maron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

[Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), rose in rebellion against [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), thinking the new king did not have great support amongst the nobility of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) and that Balon would be allowed to go his own way. Rodrik was slain during the [storming of Seagard](wiki:Storming_of_Seagard), however. When Robert marshaled his forces and invaded the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands), Maron was along the walls where Robert's breach was made along the old south tower during the [battle at Pyke](wiki:Battle_at_Pyke). Maron was crushed when the tower collapsed during the fighting.(wiki:Maron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Balon blames [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell), for the deaths of Rodrik and Maron during [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion), though neither was actually slain by Eddard.(wiki:Maron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) [Alannys Harlaw](wiki:Alannys_Harlaw), Balon's grieving wife, eventually began to roam the halls of [Pyke](wiki:Pyke) in search of her lost sons, even walking the rope bridge to the [Sea Tower](wiki:Sea_Tower) while barefoot; a [maester](wiki:Maester) often had to draw splinters from her heels.(wiki:Maron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Appearances & Description

When thinking back on his brother, [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) only remembers Maron's cruel japes and compulsive lies.(wiki:Maron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

After the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) and the supposed deaths of [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark), two of [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s sons, [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) claims he has laid to rest the [ghosts](wiki:Ghosts) of his own brothers, [Rodrik](wiki:Rodrik_Greyjoy) and Maron.(wiki:Maron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Feast for Crows

[Alannys Harlaw](wiki:Alannys_Harlaw) continues to deeply grieve the loss of her eldest sons.(wiki:Maron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### The Winds of Winter

[Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) informs [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) that the deaths of Rodrik and Maron had shattered their mother, Alannys.(wiki:Maron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-6)
