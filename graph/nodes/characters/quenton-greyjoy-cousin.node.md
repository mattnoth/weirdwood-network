## Identity

Quenton Greyjoy (cousin) (d. 300 AC) — sworn to House Greyjoy. One of 2 characters named Quenton Greyjoy.

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Along with the rest of [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy)'s supporters, Quenton sails to [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) where Asha contemplates her next move after the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot).(wiki:Quenton_Greyjoy_(cousin).cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

Quenton accompanies Asha on her flight to the sea, and he slits his mare's throat after the horse shatters a leg in a pit. Quenton sends three men up trees as guards when Asha's men make camp. Quenton kills a spearman during the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte), but a [northern mountain clansman](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans) then kills Quenton with his axe.(wiki:Quenton_Greyjoy_(cousin).cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
