## Origins

Silveraxe participated in the [battles at Summerhall](wiki:Battles_at_Summerhall) as a [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) loyalist against Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). Robert slew Silveraxe's father, [Lord Fell](wiki:Lord_Fell), in single combat and captured Silveraxe in the battle. It is unknown if Silveraxe was the heir to [Felwood](wiki:Felwood). Following [Summerhall](wiki:Summerhall), Robert won Silveraxe to his cause.(wiki:Silveraxe_Fell.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
