## Origins

The ruling [Archon of Tyrosh](wiki:Archon_of_Tyrosh) was deposed when the [Band of Nine](wiki:Band_of_Nine) and its armies [sacked](wiki:Sack_of_Tyrosh) Tyrosh at the behest of one of its members, the merchant prince [Alequo Adarys](wiki:Alequo_Adarys), was then installed as the ruler of the city. Adarys reigned until [266 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), when he was poisoned by his queen.
