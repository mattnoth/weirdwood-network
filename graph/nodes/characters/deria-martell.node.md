## Identity

Princess Deria Martell was the Princess of Dorne and Lady of Sunspear during the reign of Aegon the Conqueror and his successors (wiki:Deria_Martell).

## Origins

Born around 35 BC (track_b: Born). Daughter of Nymor Martell (track_b: Father). Of Dornish culture (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Deria Martell succeeded her father Nymor Martell as Princess of Dorne. She is notable for delivering a letter to King Aegon I Targaryen that ended the First Dornish War — the contents of the letter remain unknown but caused Aegon to agree to peace (wiki:Deria_Martell). She died between 37 and 49 AC (track_b: Died).
