## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser Mallador is part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) that leaves [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) in search of the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings). He commands the baggage train in the [column](wiki:Column).(wiki:Mallador_Locke.cite_ref-Racok13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

When [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand) is delayed in reaching the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men), Mallador suggests that the Watch head toward the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower).(wiki:Mallador_Locke.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Mallador agrees with Ser [Ottyn Wythers](wiki:Ottyn_Wythers) and counsels a retreat to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) after the discovery of the massive wildling force descending on the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men). However, [Thoren Smallwood](wiki:Thoren_Smallwood) convinces him to change his mind and he then sides with Thoren for a swift attack.(wiki:Mallador_Locke.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

As the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist) begins, [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) sees Mallador ride past on his horse with a snow-speckled helm. Later in the battle, Sam thinks that Mallador might be the officer who gives a command to use spears.(wiki:Mallador_Locke.cite_ref-Rasos18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Mallador presumably dies at the Fist, the ensuing retreat, or the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep), since he is not mentioned among the few survivors who return to Castle Black.(wiki:Mallador_Locke.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
