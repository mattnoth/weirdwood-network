## Appearances & Description

Wallace is long-faced and lantern-jawed, and has stringy brown hair and a pinched nose. He talks with a stutter. Wallace is not as muscular or tall as his nephew, Ser [Roland Waynwood](wiki:Roland_Waynwood). (wiki:Wallace_Waynwood.cite_ref-Winds_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lady [Anya Waynwood](wiki:Anya_Waynwood) is present in the [High Hall](wiki:High_Hall) of the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), surrounded by her sons, when [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) demands a [trial by battle](wiki:Trial_by_battle) to prove his innocence of the claims against him.(wiki:Wallace_Waynwood.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Wallace is [knighted](wiki:Knight) in [300 AC](wiki:300_AC).(wiki:Wallace_Waynwood.cite_ref-Knight_5-0)

Ser Wallace arrives with his mother, Lady Anya, his nephew, Ser [Roland Waynwood](wiki:Roland_Waynwood), and Ser [Harrold Hardyng](wiki:Harrold_Hardyng), at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) for the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) held to decide on the first eight members of the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights). Upon arrival, Wallace is met by [Alayne Stone](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Myranda Royce](wiki:Myranda_Royce). Wallace and Roland joke about each other, and Alayne courteously helps Wallace when the newly-made knight stutters.(wiki:Wallace_Waynwood.cite_ref-Winds_1-3)

Alayne dances with Wallace at the feast that evening. Wallace stutters again when trying to comment on other guests, so Alayne takes over the conversation to spare him from embarrassment.(wiki:Wallace_Waynwood.cite_ref-Winds_1-4)
