## Origins

Jack was once imprisoned in [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun)'s dungeons.(wiki:Jack-Be-Lucky.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) His father was hanged by Lord [Clement Piper](wiki:Clement_Piper)'s bailiff, and his brother [Wat](wiki:Wat_(Night's_Watch)) was sent to the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Jack-Be-Lucky.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Jack was part of Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion)'s band sent by Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)(wiki:Jack-Be-Lucky.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) to apprehend Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane).(wiki:Jack-Be-Lucky.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Jack-Be-Lucky is part of the group of the [brotherhood](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) who escort [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) to Beric, now the outlaw lightning lord after the death of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Jack-Be-Lucky.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) He blows his hunting horn when they arrive at a seemingly-abandoned village friendly to the outlaws,(wiki:Jack-Be-Lucky.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) as well as at the village of the [Lady of the Leaves](wiki:Lady_of_the_Leaves).(wiki:Jack-Be-Lucky.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Jack explains that [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) have killed his brothers [Lennocks](wiki:Lennocks) and [Lister](wiki:Lister).(wiki:Jack-Be-Lucky.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)(wiki:Jack-Be-Lucky.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) At [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept) he flirts with [Bella](wiki:Bella) at the [Peach](wiki:Peach).(wiki:Jack-Be-Lucky.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

During the [battle at the burning septry](wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry), Jack finds [Utt](wiki:Utt) hiding under cellar steps, and the murderous [septon](wiki:Septon) is then hanged.(wiki:Jack-Be-Lucky.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Jack has no luck playing [dice](wiki:Dice) with [Anguy](wiki:Anguy), and he approves of Beric [knighting](wiki:Knight) [Gendry](wiki:Gendry).(wiki:Jack-Be-Lucky.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

When [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey) is taken by the brotherhood after the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), one-eyed Jack and [Lem](wiki:Lem) both claim to be Beric before [Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams) states that Beric is elsewhere. Jack helps in the hanging of Merrett at [Oldstones](wiki:Oldstones) for his involvement in the death of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark).(wiki:Jack-Be-Lucky.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Jack is part of [Lady Stoneheart's](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) band of outlaws(wiki:Jack-Be-Lucky.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) after the passing of Beric. Jack encourages the hangings of [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth), Ser [Hyle Hunt](wiki:Hyle_Hunt), and [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne) as retribution for Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly)'s hanging of a score of the outlaws.(wiki:Jack-Be-Lucky.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
