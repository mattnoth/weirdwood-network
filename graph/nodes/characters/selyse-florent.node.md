## Origins

Selyse married Stannis in either [286 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) or [287 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-12) During [the wedding feast](wiki:Wedding_of_Stannis_Baratheon_and_Selyse_Florent), Stannis's elder brother, King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), deflowered [Delena Florent](wiki:Delena_Florent), a cousin of Selyse, in the bed made for the newlyweds. Delena became pregnant and gave birth to a boy, [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm).(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Stannis is not fond of his wife.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) They sleep together only once or twice a year,(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-7) and despite their hopes they have only a daughter, [Shireen](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon);(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Selyse believes that Edric's existence has cursed them to have no sons.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) Stannis rarely sends letters from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to his wife at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-8)

Some years before the start of *[A Song of Ice and Fire](wiki:A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire)*, Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) informed Robert's [small council](wiki:Small_council) that Selyse had taken up with a [red priestess](wiki:Red_priest), [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) of [Asshai](wiki:Asshai), in [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2) Selyse has asked Stannis that they be wed again in a ceremony of the [Lord of Light](wiki:R'hllor).(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

## Appearances & Description

Selyse is as tall as her husband, [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Pinched and hard,(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) she is thin of body and face, with pale eyes, a sharp nose, and the [Florent](wiki:House_Florent) trait of too-large ears.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Selyse has thin lips,(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and hair grows on her upper lip despite her plucking it daily.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Her smile can be thin(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) or as bright and brittle as her jewelry.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) Her mouth is stern and her voice a whip.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Selyse is not considered a particularly attractive woman.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Selyse and Stannis have a loveless marriage, and the two have little patience for each other. They have one daughter, [Shireen](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon), a sad little girl bearing a disfigurement from [greyscale](wiki:Greyscale).(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5) Selyse is a haughty and cold woman, but she is protective of her daughter.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Selyse enjoys sewing.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) She wears [jewels](wiki:Gemstone)(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-6) and an ermine mantle.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

When [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) attached herself to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), Selyse became her most fervent supporter and immediately took to worshiping [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor), the Lord of Light. Selyse's uncle, Ser [Axell Florent](wiki:Axell_Florent), soon converts as well.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

Selyse becomes Stannis's queen after he claims the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). Within the ranks of those who follow Stannis, those who believe in R'hllor are named [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) after Selyse.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) Selyse's crown matches that of Stannis, red gold with points in the shape of flames.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2) Selyse tells Stannis that the [red comet](wiki:Red_comet) is the banner of R'hllor and a sign for her husband.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-9) When Stannis hosts a feast for his supporters, Selyse commands Maester [Cressen](wiki:Cressen) to wear the helm of the [fool](wiki:Fool) [Patchface](wiki:Patchface)(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-10) Selyse prays when [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) burns the statues of the [Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) taken from the [sept at Dragonstone](wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone).(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2)

In order to discredit Stannis, the [small council](wiki:Small_council) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) spreads rumors that the father of Selyse's daughter, [Shireen](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon), is not Stannis, but instead Patchface.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-3)

When Stannis sails for [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), he leaves Selyse and Axell on Dragonstone.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Selyse's influence on her husband becomes greater after [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) is [killed at Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)) and Selyse's family from [House Florent](wiki:House_Florent) go over to Stannis. Selyse's brother, Ser [Imry Florent](wiki:Imry_Florent), leads [Stannis's fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet).(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Their hopes are shattered when the fleet is destroyed during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1)(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Racok59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Storm of Swords

After the Battle of the Blackwater and the subsequent flight by Stannis's forces to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), Stannis remains enclosed in the [Stone Drum](wiki:Stone_Drum), unwilling to speak with anyone. Selyse, with the help of her uncle, Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent), now the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), holds court during Stannis's self-imposed silence.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Alester is later imprisoned for [treason](wiki:Treason), however.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

The queen prays at the [nightfiress](wiki:Nightfires) of [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre).(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Selyse tries to help Melisandre persuade Stannis to sacrifice [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s bastard, [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm). The sacrifice of Edric would supposedly wake the [stone dragon](wiki:Stone_dragon) statues on Dragonstone. [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth), Stannis's new Hand, prevents this and has the boy shipped off to the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities).(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1)

Davos advises that the [real enemy](wiki:Others) is in the [north](wiki:North), based on a letter from Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-2) Stannis agrees and travels with Selyse and most of his remaining army to the [Wall](wiki:Wall). Selyse and Shireen remain at [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea) while Stannis travels west to [save Castle Black](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall) from the invading [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings).(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Selyse remains at Eastwatch while Stannis is at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Selyse holds a feast for [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan) and his captains before they sail south from Eastwatch for [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor).(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) Selyse becomes unhappy with her accommodations at [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea) and her treatment by its commander, [Cotter Pyke](wiki:Cotter_Pyke). She demands that she be allowed to move into the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort) that was to be given to Stannis when repaired.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) Selyse's uncle, Ser [Axell Florent](wiki:Axell_Florent), begins styling himself the [Queen's Hand](wiki:Queen's_Hand).(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1)

When it is found that the Nightfort is habitable, Selyse leaves Eastwatch for Castle Black with [Shireen](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon), [Patchface](wiki:Patchface), [Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris), and serving girls and lady companions. The fifty [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) who accompany her include [knights](wiki:Knight), [sworn swords](wiki:Sworn_sword), and [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms), with [Narbert Grandison](wiki:Narbert_Grandison), [Benethon Scales](wiki:Benethon_Scales), [Brus Buckler](wiki:Brus_Buckler), [Patrek of King's Mountain](wiki:Patrek_of_King's_Mountain), [Dorden the Dour](wiki:Dorden_the_Dour), [Malegorn of Redpool](wiki:Malegorn_of_Redpool), [Lambert Whitewater](wiki:Lambert_Whitewater), and [Perkin Follard](wiki:Perkin_Follard) having places of honor by her side. Selyse is given the royal chamber in the [King's Tower](wiki:King's_Tower) of Castle Black. Selyse and her knights are disgusted by [Wun Weg Wun Dar Wun](wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun), but Shireen is fascinated by the [giant](wiki:Giant).(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-3) Selyse is also made anxious by [Ghost](wiki:Ghost), the [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) of [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch).(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

During the [wedding of Sigorn and Alys Karstark](wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark), Selyse reverently stares into Melisandre's fire and joins the [red priestess](wiki:Red_priest)'s prayers. Axell leads a toast to Stannis, Selyse, and R'hllor during the wedding feast, and in-between courses he leads his niece to the floor to begin the dancing.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) Selyse is cold toward [Val](wiki:Val) when the so-called wildling princess tells her that the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) will not kneel to Stannis or accept R'hllor. Val expresses her concern about Shireen's [greyscale](wiki:Greyscale) to Jon.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Selyse disapproves of Lord Commander Snow's plan to send a ranging to rescue wildlings at [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome).(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) [Tormund](wiki:Tormund) laughs at Selyse always surrounding herself with guards.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3)

Selyse arranges the weddings of [Gerrick Kingsblood](wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood)'s three daughters to Axell, Ser [Brus Buckler](wiki:Brus_Buckler), and Ser [Malegorn of Redpool](wiki:Malegorn_of_Redpool). She claims that Gerrick is the true [King of the Wildlings](wiki:King_of_the_Wildlings), descended in an unbroken male line from [Raymun Redbeard](wiki:Raymun_Redbeard), a great [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall). However, Tormund states Gerrick is descended from Raymun Redbeard's younger brother, the [Red Raven](wiki:Red_Raven_(free_folk)), instead of the king, and that kingship among the free folk is not hereditary. Selyse also wants Val to marry Ser Patrek, but is rebuffed.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-4)

Jon receives [a letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) from [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) which claims that Stannis has been killed in battle. Ramsay demands that Jon relinquish Selyse, Shireen, and Melisandre.(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-5) Narbert and Benethon attend Jon's speech in the [Shieldhall](wiki:Shieldhall), but Selyse herself does not. Patrek is killed by Wun Wun outside [Hardin's Tower](wiki:Hardin's_Tower) while trying to get to Val, and Jon is attacked in the [mutiny at Castle Black](wiki:Mutiny_at_Castle_Black).(wiki:Selyse_Florent.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-6)
