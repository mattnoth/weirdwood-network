## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Rolph serves as [castellan](wiki:Castellan) of the [Crag](wiki:Crag), where his sister, [Sybell Spicer](wiki:Sybell_Spicer), is lady of the castle. With Sybell's husband, Lord [Gawen Westerling](wiki:Gawen_Westerling), a captive at [Seagard](wiki:Seagard),(wiki:Rolph_Spicer.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Rolph yields the castle to [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), during the [storming of the Crag](wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag). [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling), Sybell's elder daughter, treats Robb's wounds after the battle, and the two youths sleep together after Robb learns of the alleged deaths of Robb's younger brothers, [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon](wiki:Rickon_Stark), at [Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell). Robb chooses to wed Jeyne, thereby breaking his alliance with [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey).(wiki:Rolph_Spicer.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Sybell begins providing a posset to Jeyne each morning, claiming it will make her fertile.(wiki:Rolph_Spicer.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Ser Rolph comes to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) as part of Robb's retinue when the king departs the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands). When [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), Robb's mother, learns that his [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind), does not like Rolph, she urges that Robb send the [knight](wiki:Knight) away.(wiki:Rolph_Spicer.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Rolph and his nephew, Ser [Raynald Westerling](wiki:Raynald_Westerling), are in attendance at Riverrun when Lord [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark) is brought before the king after murdering [Tion Frey](wiki:Tion_Frey) and [Willem Lannister](wiki:Willem_Lannister).(wiki:Rolph_Spicer.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Rolph offers his consolations to Robb after the death of the king's grandfather, Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully).(wiki:Rolph_Spicer.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Robb eventually sends Rolph to deliver [Martyn Lannister](wiki:Martyn_Lannister) to the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth) in exchange for the freeing of [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover).(wiki:Rolph_Spicer.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Robb sets off to the [Twins](wiki:Twins) for the wedding of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) and [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey). Robb and Catleyn are betrayed and murdered by the Freys and [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton) at the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), however.(wiki:Rolph_Spicer.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

According to a semi-canon source, Rolph and Sybell had conspired with the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) to trick Robb into marrying Jeyne.(wiki:Rolph_Spicer.cite_ref-Rawoiafsybell_spicer.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Rolph_Spicer.cite_ref-Rawoiafrolph_spicer.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) In the aftermath of the Red Wedding, Rolph is raised to the rank of [lord](wiki:Lord) by the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) and is given the lands and incomes of [Castamere](wiki:Castamere).(wiki:Rolph_Spicer.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Feast for Crows

After resolving the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) reminds Sybell that the [Westerlings](wiki:House_Westerling) had been pardoned and that Rolph had been named [Lord of Castamere](wiki:Lord_of_Castamere). Sybell reveals that Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) had additionally promised worthy marriages for her children during their discrete negotiations.(wiki:Rolph_Spicer.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
