## Origins

Denys was born at [Seagard](wiki:Seagard)(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Rawoiafdenys_mallister.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) to a [lord](wiki:Lord) of [House Mallister](wiki:House_Mallister).(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-9) He was a squire at twelve, a [knight](wiki:Knight) at eighteen, and a champion at twenty-two years old. He once unhorsed both of [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly)'s grandfathers ([Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent) and the father of [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly)) at the same [tourney](wiki:Tourney).(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-10)

When Denys joined the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), he was assigned to the order of [rangers](wiki:Rangers).(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Rawoiafdenys_mallister.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) He has been the commander of the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) since [267 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). The knight has considered becoming [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), but in the last two [elections](wiki:Elections) he yielded to first [Lord Commander Qorgyle](wiki:Lord_Commander_Qorgyle) and then [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont).(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-11)

[Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder), one of the rangers at the Shadow Tower, was once wounded by a [shadowcat](wiki:Shadowcat) during an expedition north of the [Wall](wiki:Wall). A wildling woman nursed him back to health and repaired tears in his cloak with scarlet silk. When Mance returned to the Shadow Tower, Denys ordered him to burn the cloak and wear only the black cloaks of the Night's Watch. Denys's stern order influenced Mance's decision to desert the Shadow Tower and the Night's Watch the next day, joining the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) living [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Denys Mallister](wiki:Category:Images_of_Denys_Mallister)*

Denys is an old man with blue-grey eyes and a long white beard. He has lost almost all his hair and his face is deeply lined. Denys still has his teeth and is graceful in his movement.(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

The commander clasps his black cloak with a silver [eagle](wiki:Eagle). He wears a black velvet doublet with [sable](wiki:Sable) trimming the collar and accenting the sleeves.(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Denys is courteous and well-spoken due to his highborn roots.(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He is also considered cautious and chivalrous.(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Denys is proud of his upbringing and experience,(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) and he writes in a flowing hand.(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) He enjoys sweet [wine](wiki:Wine).(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6)

The knight distrusts the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), and he considers [Cotter Pyke](wiki:Cotter_Pyke), commander of [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea), to be uncouth and uneducated.(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7) Cotter, meanwhile, thinks of Denys as a preening eagle and does not consider him to be a warrior.(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Denys writes to [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), reporting that [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) from the [Frostfangs](wiki:Frostfangs) are moving south, slipping past the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) in numbers greater than ever before.(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Ragot21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Denys sends another raven to Jeor, informing him rangers from the Shadow Tower have found whole wildling villages abandoned, and at night huge fires have been sighted, blazing in the mountains from dusk till dawn.(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) In the depths of the [Gorge](wiki:Gorge), [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand), one of Denys's senior rangers, captures a wildling who says that [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) is secretly massing wildlings.(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

### A Clash of Kings

[Craster](wiki:Craster) confirms for Lord Commander Mormont the reports from Denys and Qhorin that Mance has been gathering wildlings, and he adds that Mance has been doing so in the Frostfangs.(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Storm of Swords

When discussing the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) advises his father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), to deal with Denys, commander of the Shadow Tower, or [Cotter Pyke](wiki:Cotter_Pyke), commander of [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea), rather than [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt); Tywin dismisses Denys as a [Mallister](wiki:House_Mallister) of [Seagard](wiki:Seagard), one that would not serve his purposes.(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

During Mance's [attacks against the Wall](wiki:Battle_of_Castle_Black), Denys sends word of the [fight at the Bridge of Skulls](wiki:Fight_at_the_Bridge_of_Skulls) to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

Denys leaves the Shadow Tower to attend the [choosing](wiki:Choosing) for the next Lord Commander to replace Jeor, who was slain in the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep). Traveling with [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) and other survivors of the Bridge of Skulls, this party happens across [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) and [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) on the road, escorting them to Castle Black.(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Denys is a leading candidate to become the next Lord Commander, along with Cotter, collecting two hundred and thirteen votes by the time only seven candidates remain in the race.(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) Because the support between them is divided, Janos Slynt appears as a potential alternative. Sam Tarly meets with Cotter in the [Shieldhall](wiki:Shieldhall) and with Denys in the [Lance](wiki:Lance_(tower)). Although each commander expresses hatred for their rival, both agree that Janos would be a worse choice. Denys also objects to the candidacies of [Othell Yarwyck](wiki:Othell_Yarwyck), [First Builder](wiki:First_Builder), and [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh), [Lord Steward](wiki:Lord_Steward). Sam then lies to Cotter and Denys that King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) plans to name the other as Lord Commander, and he successfully persuades each to support [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) instead.(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-12)

### A Dance with Dragons

Denys sends regular [ravens](wiki:Raven) to Castle Black requesting additional men.(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) He writes back to express disappointment in the ten untrained wildlings sent from [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town) by Jon.(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

Denys reports sightings of fires in the night north of the Gorge,(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) and he believes wildings are amassing to force the [Bridge of Skulls](wiki:Bridge_of_Skulls) once more.(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Denys later writes of seeing great camps of wildlings in the mountains beyond the Shadow Tower.(wiki:Denys_Mallister.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
