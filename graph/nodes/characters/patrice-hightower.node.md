Patrice is considered one of the suspects in the death of the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aenys_I)) in [43 AC](wiki:43_AC),(wiki:Patrice_Hightower.cite_ref-3) as she had sought an audience with His High Holiness at the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) on the day of his death. However, he was known to be alive when she departed.
