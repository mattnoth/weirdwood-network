## Origins

Prior to the fall of [Mardosh](wiki:Mardosh) to the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki), the kings of the [Sarnori](wiki:Tall_Men) had remained divided. The destruction of the "City of Soldiers" and all its population led them to put aside their petty rivalries and ambitions, however, and unite under their [High King](wiki:High_King_of_Sarnor).(wiki:Mazor_Alexi.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Mazor Alexi assembled a great army from up and down the [Sarne](wiki:Sarne) and struck out east. In the tall grass between [Sarnath](wiki:Sarnath) and the ruins of [Kasath](wiki:Kasath), the Tall Men faced four [khalasars](wiki:Khalasar), with a combined might of eighty thousand horselords, in a battle that would be known as the [Field of Crows](wiki:Field_of_Crows). The Sarnori [chariots](wiki:Chariot) smashed through the center of the Dothraki horse, killing Khal [Haro](wiki:Haro) and causing his khalasar to retreat. Alexi and his mounted cavalry plunged in after them, followed by the Sarnori foot. This was a trap, however, as the fleeing Dothraki turned and unleashed a storm of arrows. Meanwhile, the khalasars of Khals [Qano](wiki:Qano) and [Zhako](wiki:Zhako) swept in from north and south and Khal [Loso](wiki:Loso) and his screamers struck the Sarnori rear. Completely surrounded, Alexi's host was cut to pieces. A hundred thousand men died, Mazor Alexi among them.(wiki:Mazor_Alexi.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
