## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Lyonel is a knight in the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) army encamped at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal). When [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) makes ready for his departure from Harrenhal, Lyonel chips his sword in the yard of Harrenhal. He sends [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) with the sword to [Lucan](wiki:Lucan), demanding a new one. Lucan believes Lyonel does not deserve one, but gives him one all the same.(wiki:Lyonel_(knight).cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
