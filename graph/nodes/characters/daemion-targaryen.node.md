## Identity

Lord Daemion Targaryen was a member of House Targaryen during the Century of Blood, the third son of Lord Aerys Targaryen of Dragonstone, and the father of Aerion Targaryen (wiki:Daemion_Targaryen).

## Origins

Born between 87 and 53 BC on Dragonstone (track_b: Born). Son of Aerys Targaryen (track_b: Father).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiance).

## Narrative Arc

Daemion became Lord of Dragonstone after his two brothers Aelyx and Baelon. He was succeeded by his only son, Aerion Targaryen. Lord Daemion kept a maester on Dragonstone (wiki:Daemion_Targaryen).
