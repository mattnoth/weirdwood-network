## Identity

Gerold Lannister, known as Gerold the Golden, was the Lord of Casterly Rock, Shield of Lannisport, Warden of the West, and head of House Lannister during the reigns of kings Aerys I, Maekar I, and part of Aegon V Targaryen (wiki:Gerold_Lannister).

## Origins

Born at or before 196 AC at Casterly Rock (track_b: Born). Youngest son of Lord Damon Lannister (the Grey Lion) and Lady Cerissa Brax (track_b: Father, Mother).

## Appearances & Description

Gerold was golden-haired and over six feet tall. He was known to be quick of wit, genial, exceedingly clever, and an exceptionally shrewd, able, and fair-minded lord. He enjoyed reading, especially later in life (wiki:Gerold_Lannister).

## Narrative Arc

After his older brother Ser Tybolt became Lord of Casterly Rock upon their father Damon's death in 210 AC, Gerold was a suitor to Lady Rohanne Webber of Coldmoat. When Tybolt died suddenly in 212 AC, Tybolt's infant daughter Cerelle succeeded, with Gerold serving as regent. When Cerelle herself died in 213 AC, Gerold became Lord of Casterly Rock. Both deaths were suspicious, and many whispered Gerold had murdered his brother and niece to seize power.

Gerold married twice — first to Alysanne Farman, then to Rohanne Webber. By his first wife he had twin sons, Tywald and Tion. By his second wife he had Tytos and Jason. When Tywald was killed by a Reyne lord during the Peake Uprising, Gerold betrothed Tion to Ellyn Reyne. Tion was later killed in battle, and Ellyn was eventually wed to Gerold himself, though this marriage was unhappy. Rohanne's mysterious disappearance occurred before this.

Gerold ruled until his death in 244 AC at Casterly Rock, succeeded by his son Tytos (wiki:Gerold_Lannister).
