## Origins

Franklyn claims that his mother was a washerwoman at [Cider Hall](wiki:Cider_Hall), who was raped by a son of [House Fossoway](wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall)'s head.(wiki:Franklyn_Flowers.cite_ref-3) Due to this, Franklyn loathes the Fossoways.(wiki:Franklyn_Flowers.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Franklyn knows [Griff](wiki:Jon_Connington), and remembers him from when he served with [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) twelve years ago. Franklyn was [knighted](wiki:Knight) some time after Griff left the company.(wiki:Franklyn_Flowers.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

## Appearances & Description

Franklyn is a big-bellied, shambling hulk of a man, with a seamed face crisscrossed with old scars. His right ear looks as if a [dog](wiki:Dog) had chewed on it, and his left ear is missing entirely. He wears a lord's ransom in [golden](wiki:Gold) arm rings, one for each year he has served with the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company).(wiki:Franklyn_Flowers.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser Franklyn is present during [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington)'s rendezvous with the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) three miles south of [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys). During the discussions about how to meet up with [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), Franklyn suggests the land route, but [Harry Strickland](wiki:Harry_Strickland) replies that the [demon road](wiki:Demon_road) is death. Franklyn is one of the first to swear his allegiance to Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(Young_Griff)). He remarks that he looks forward to attacking the [Reach](wiki:Reach) and killing some [Fossoways](wiki:House_Fossoway_of_Cider_Hall). Franklyn then takes Aegon to meet the men of the Golden Company. Instead of waiting for Daenerys, the [sellswords](wiki:Sellsword) agree to sail to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Franklyn_Flowers.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

During the [landing of the Golden Company](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company) in the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands), Franklyn takes part in the [taking of Griffin's Roost](wiki:Taking_of_Griffin's_Roost). Franklyn uses brush for concealment, leading his men to destroy the gate with a ram. [Ravens](wiki:Raven) are quickly dispatched by the castle, but are taken out by the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company)’s expert archers. When Jon tells Franklyn there are to be no more messages, the next thing to come flying from [Griffin's Roost](wiki:Griffin's_Roost) is the [maester](wiki:Maester).(wiki:Franklyn_Flowers.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

During the victors' feast in the great hall, Jon presides from the Griffin's Seat, while Franklyn shares the high table with [Harry Strickland](wiki:Harry_Strickland), [Black Balaq](wiki:Black_Balaq), and the three young [Conningtons](wiki:House_Connington) they had taken captive. Franklyn is present during Jon's war council in the castle.(wiki:Franklyn_Flowers.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
