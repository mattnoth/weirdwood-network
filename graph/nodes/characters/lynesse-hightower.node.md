## Origins

During the [tourney at Lannisport](wiki:Tourney_at_Lannisport), Lynesse allowed her favor to be worn by Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont), Lord of [Bear Island](wiki:Bear_Island), who was twice her age and widowed. He crowned her the [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty) after winning the tourney and asked for her hand in marriage, to which Lord [Leyton Hightower](wiki:Leyton_Hightower) consented. Lynesse and Jorah married shortly thereafter in [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport).(wiki:Lynesse_Hightower.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Although they had married for love, the marriage did not remain happy for long. Having grown up in [House Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) in prosperous [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), Lynesse was unprepared for life at poor Bear Island in the [north](wiki:North), and she quickly became miserable. Jorah attempted to reproduce the luxurious lifestyle his wife was accustomed to by lavishing her with expensive gifts and voyages to Lannisport, Oldtown, and [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), but Jorah quickly drove himself into financial ruin. Lynesse was unimpressed with Jorah's defeats in other tourneys, and she refused to sell her [jewelry](wiki:Jewelry).(wiki:Lynesse_Hightower.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) During a visit to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), a drunken Lynesse confessed her unhappiness to Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark).(wiki:Lynesse_Hightower.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

In desperation, Jorah began looking to less savory ways of paying off his debts, attempting to raise money by selling some convicted poachers to a [Tyroshi](wiki:Tyrosh) slaver.(wiki:Lynesse_Hightower.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) Learning that one of his [bannermen](wiki:Bannermen) had engaged in [the slave trade](wiki:Slavery), Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) declared Jorah's life forfeit.(wiki:Lynesse_Hightower.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) Rather than face the [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), Jorah fled with Lynesse to [Essos](wiki:Essos) in [293 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Lynesse_Hightower.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Jorah would have preferred to live in Braavos, but he instead chose the warmer [Lys](wiki:Lys) to make Lynesse happy. Jorah became a [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword), but Lynesse outspent his earnings tenfold. While he was fighting Braavosi on the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne), Lynesse moved into the [manse](wiki:Manse) of [Tregar Ormollen](wiki:Tregar_Ormollen), a Lysene merchant prince.(wiki:Lynesse_Hightower.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6) Tregar threatened to enslave Jorah if he did not give up Lynesse, so the northman abandoned Lys for [Volantis](wiki:Volantis).(wiki:Lynesse_Hightower.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Lynesse is now Tregar's chief concubine; even his wife goes in fear of her.(wiki:Lynesse_Hightower.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7)

## Appearances & Description

The beautiful Lynesse has golden hair, skin the color of cream, and soft hands.(wiki:Lynesse_Hightower.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) She looks similar to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen).(wiki:Lynesse_Hightower.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

While in [Vaes Tolorro](wiki:Vaes_Tolorro) Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) tells [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) of his second wife, Lynesse, and his ill-fated marriage to her.(wiki:Lynesse_Hightower.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8)

### A Storm of Swords

While traveling in the rain to the [Twins](wiki:Twins), Lady [Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont) and her heir, [Dacey](wiki:Dacey_Mormont), speak of Lynesse to [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark).(wiki:Lynesse_Hightower.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

### A Feast for Crows

When Ser [Humfrey Hightower](wiki:Humfrey_Hightower) sails to [Lys](wiki:Lys) to hire [sellsails](wiki:Mercenary) to help fight off the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) attack on the [Reach](wiki:Reach), it is a commonly-held hope that Lynesse will help him get the best possible fleet.(wiki:Lynesse_Hightower.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) While talking with [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), the captain of the *[Huntress](wiki:Huntress)* refers to Lynesse as Humfrey's "whore of a sister".(wiki:Lynesse_Hightower.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

While in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), Jorah tells his prisoner, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), of Lynesse abandoning him for [Tregar Ormollen](wiki:Tregar_Ormollen).(wiki:Lynesse_Hightower.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
