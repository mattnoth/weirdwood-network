## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser Harys is a member of the force under the command of Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) that took [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal). During a war council following the arrival of news of [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s victory in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), he doubts the chances of their 'boy king', [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), against Lord Tywin, who has defeated a seasoned commander like [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Harys_Haigh.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Harys was introduced to Lady [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Tully) at [the Twins](wiki:The_Twins) on the eve of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully)'s [wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) along with the other Haighs and their [Frey](wiki:House_Frey) kin.(wiki:Harys_Haigh.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) During the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), he wrestles a [Vance](wiki:House_Vance) and is aided when [Black Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Ryman)) hamstrings the man.(wiki:Harys_Haigh.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Feast for Crows

When news of the [raid at Saltpans](wiki:Sack_of_Saltpans) reached the [Twins](wiki:Twins), Ser [Arwood Frey](wiki:Arwood_Frey), Ser Harys and Ser [Donnel Haigh](wiki:Donnel_Haigh) rode to the town with a small force to investigate. There they found Ser [Quincy Cox](wiki:Quincy_Cox), the Knight of Saltpans, sitting behind his walls and the town in ashes.(wiki:Harys_Haigh.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
