## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Quaro is one of four young warriors assigned to guard [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen). When [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) attacks his sister in the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea), Quaro advises Daenerys to take an ear from Viserys to teach him respect.(wiki:Quaro.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He later accompanies Daenerys during her visit to the Western Market in [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak), challenging [Rakharo](wiki:Rakharo) to a friendly eating contest. Quaro helps stop an assassination attempt on Daenerys's life by a wineseller.(wiki:Quaro.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

During the raid on the [Lhazareen](wiki:Lhazareen) settlement, Quaro is commanded by Daenerys to stop the Dothraki riders raping the captured [Eroeh](wiki:Eroeh). Though he initially protests against stopping it, he complies with her command.(wiki:Quaro.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

When Daenerys intends to have Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) healed by [Mirri Maz Duur](wiki:Mirri_Maz_Duur) using blood magic, Drogo's [bloodriders](wiki:Bloodrider) try to stop it from happening. In the ensuing fight, Quaro is killed by [Qotho](wiki:Qotho).(wiki:Quaro.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Quaro is remembered by Daenerys when she thinks back to the followers who have died under her service.(wiki:Quaro.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
