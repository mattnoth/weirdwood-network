## Appearances & Description

Warren owns a stone towerhouse located on a road south of a village which is located on the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork); the [Inn of the Kneeling Man](wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man) is found six miles west of that village.(wiki:Warren.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

At the [Inn of the Kneeling Man](wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man), [Husband](wiki:Husband) explains that Ser Warren has died, and he does not know who is now holding his towerhouse during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth), Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), and Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) see lights in the towerhouse as they travel, so they keep their distance.(wiki:Warren.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
