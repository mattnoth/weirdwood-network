## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

During the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) by the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), Goady is among the scouts under the command of [Jarman Buckwell](wiki:Jarman_Buckwell).(wiki:Goady.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) He is then a member of [Thoren Smallwood](wiki:Thoren_Smallwood)'s scouting party that finds the wildling masses along the [Milkwater](wiki:Milkwater). He creeps up to the fire to see who leads the van and spots [Harma Dogshead](wiki:Harma).(wiki:Goady.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Goady stays loyal to the Watch during the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep) and is one of the few survivors of the ranging to make it all the way back to Castle Black.(wiki:Goady.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Goady.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
