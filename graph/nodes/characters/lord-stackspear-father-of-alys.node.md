## Origins

Lord Stackspear's daughter, [Alys Stackspear](wiki:Alys_Stackspear), was hastly married to the fifteen-year-old [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold)) after he took her maidenhood and got her pregnant. The marriage was short lived as Alys died giving birth to their son, [Damon Lannister](wiki:Damon_Lannister_(son_of_Jason)).(wiki:Lord_Stackspear_(father_of_Alys).cite_ref-sample_1-1)

During the misrule of the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) by Lord [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister), Lord Stackspear doubled the taxes on his [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk). Ignoring his liege lord's protests, he hired a company of [Volantene](wiki:Volantis) [sellswords](wiki:Sellsword) to enforce his extortion.(wiki:Lord_Stackspear_(father_of_Alys).cite_ref-sample_1-2)

Lord Stackspear and his levies were later part of the host of Lord Tytos's son, Ser [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), when he suppressed the [Reyne-Tarbeck revolt](wiki:Reyne-Tarbeck_revolt) in [261 AC](wiki:261_AC).(wiki:Lord_Stackspear_(father_of_Alys).cite_ref-sample_1-3)
