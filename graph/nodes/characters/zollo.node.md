## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

After Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) is captured near [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) by the Brave Companions, he is brought before [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat). Vargo says he will send a message to Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) and has Zollo cut off Jaime's sword hand.(wiki:Zollo.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

On the way back to [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Zollo, [Shagwell](wiki:Shagwell) and [Rorge](wiki:Rorge) attempt to rape [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth). They argue with Zollo over who would rape Brienne first, but Jaime thwarts them.(wiki:Zollo.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Zollo is one of the many Brave Companions who remain behind at Harrenhal after several failed foraging runs. He is present when Brienne is thrown into the bear pit and raises his whip when Jaime comes back to save her, but Vargo orders him to stand down.(wiki:Zollo.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Feast For Crows

While in [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Jaime thinks to himself that the [Stranger](wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven)) may have made off with Vargo Hoat before he could get around to him, but fat Zollo is still out there, with Shagwell, Rorge, Faithful [Urswyck](wiki:Urswyck), and the rest.(wiki:Zollo.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
