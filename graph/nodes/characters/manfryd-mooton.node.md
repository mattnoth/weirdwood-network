## Origins

### Dance of the Dragons[ ]

Manfryd supported the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) and Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). He became Lord of [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) when his brother, Lord [Walys Mooton](wiki:Walys_Mooton), was charred while trying to slay the injured dragon [Sunfyre](wiki:Sunfyre) after the [retaking of Rook's Rest](wiki:Retaking_of_Rook's_Rest).(wiki:Manfryd_Mooton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) After hearing that Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) was laying waste to the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) atop the dragon [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar), Lord Manfryd sent urgent messages to Rhaenyra. Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) and his alleged paramour, [Nettles](wiki:Nettles), then used Maidenpool as their base. Manfryd suggested the two dragonriders split up in their search for Aemond, but Daemon refused.(wiki:Manfryd_Mooton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Queen Rhaenyra was [betrayed](wiki:Two_Betrayers) by the baseborn dragonriders [Ulf White](wiki:Ulf_White) and [Hugh Hammer](wiki:Hugh_Hammer) and was convinced by the [black council](wiki:Black_council) to condemn the also baseborn Ser [Addam Velaryon](wiki:Addam_Velaryon) and Nettles. Jealous of Nettles's relationship with Daemon, the queen sent a letter to Maester [Norren](wiki:Norren_(House_Mooton)) commanding Lord Manfryd to kill Nettles, but to do no harm to Daemon. The disturbed Manfryd did not want to break [guest right](wiki:Guest_right), however, since Nettles and Daemon were guests under his roof. After discussing the letter in secret with his maester, his captain of the guard, a remaining brother, and his champion, Ser [Florian Greysteel](wiki:Florian_Greysteel), Manfryd decided to ignore Rhaenyra's instructions.(wiki:Manfryd_Mooton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Norren informed Daemon and Nettles of the letter, claiming he had not shown it to anyone else inside the castle, and the two [dragonriders](wiki:Dragonrider) departed the following morning. Daemon thanked Manfyrd for his hospitality and told him to pass on the message to Aemond if he arrived that Daemon would be waiting for Aemond at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal). Manfryd refused Norren's offer to resign. The night after Daemon and Nettles departed, Manfryd had Queen Rhaenyra's quartered standard taken down from the gates of Maidenpool and replaced them with the golden dragons of King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen). Rhaenyra ordered that a decree of attainder be drawn up against the Mootons, but she soon lost power after the [Storming of the Dragonpit](wiki:Storming_of_the_Dragonpit).(wiki:Manfryd_Mooton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### Regency[ ]

After the end of the war and the coronation of [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), Lord Manfryd became a member of the council of [seven regents](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III) that would rule until the child king came of age.(wiki:Manfryd_Mooton.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Manfryd became bedridden from [Winter Fever](wiki:Winter_Fever) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC). After the death of Ser [Tyland Lannister](wiki:Tyland_Lannister), Manfryd wanted the regents to convene and deliberate. Rather than wait, however, Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake) set aside Aegon's appointments and named himself [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:Manfryd_Mooton.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
After Lord [Alyn Velaryon's attack](wiki:Lord_Alyn's_attack) on the [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) at the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Manfryd was sent on a diplomatic mission to the [Free City](wiki:Free_City) to treat with the [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos) and return a captured [elephant](wiki:Elephant). Lord Mooton's retinue included six [lords](wiki:Lord), six singers, and threescore [knights](wiki:Knight), guards, servants, scribes, and [septons](wiki:Septon). Notables included [Lord Follard](wiki:Lord_Follard), [Lord Cressey](wiki:Lord_Cressey), Ser [Herman Rollingford](wiki:Herman_Rollingford), and Ser [Denys Harte](wiki:Denys_Harte). Manfryd was impressed with the [Titan of Braavos](wiki:Titan_of_Braavos) and the [Arsenal](wiki:Arsenal). While Lords Cressey and Follard gave bribes to notables like [keyholders](wiki:Keyholders) and [magisters](wiki:Magisters), Manfyrd negotiated with the Sealord. In return for a large indemnity, the Sealord agreed to forgive Alyn Oakenfist's attack on the Braavosi fleet. Braavos broke its alliance with [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) and [Racallio Ryndoon](wiki:Racallio_Ryndoon) and ceded the Stepstones to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), although Braavos did not control the archipelago at the time.(wiki:Manfryd_Mooton.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
Lord Peake outraged Manfryd and Lord [Thaddeus Rowan](wiki:Thaddeus_Rowan) by attempting to wed his daughter, [Myrielle Peake](wiki:Myrielle_Peake), to Aegon III after the death of Queen [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen).(wiki:Manfryd_Mooton.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Manfryd was with Aegon at the docks of King's Landing when Alyn Oakenfist returned to the city with the king's missing brother, Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Manfryd_Mooton.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Manfryd died of age and [illness](wiki:Illness) in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC).(wiki:Manfryd_Mooton.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
