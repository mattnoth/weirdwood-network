## Origins

In [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), the septon preached in the market square of [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept) against Lord [Bloodraven](wiki:Bloodraven), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) and half-uncle of King [Aerys I Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_I_Targaryen), and in favor of the [Blackfyre Pretenders](wiki:Blackfyre_Pretenders). For this [treason](wiki:Treason), he was executed, and his head was displayed on an [iron](wiki:Iron) spike above the town gates.(wiki:Hunchbacked_septon.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

When Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) and his [squire](wiki:Squire), [Egg](wiki:Egg), were leaving Stoney Sept, they saw the head. Duncan thought the face looked familiar, and Egg reminded him they had seen the hunchbacked septon preaching three days earlier. Dunk recalled that Lord Bloodraven was accused of having various [sorcerous](wiki:Magic) animal spies, which he doubted, but it was certain that Bloodraven had informers everywhere. After they were a good distance from the town, Dunk argued it was a bad idea to cut off the head of a holy man, as words were merely words, but Egg pointed out the septon's words were treason and lies. Dunk rebutted that it still set a bad precedent, since fools and liars were everywhere.(wiki:Hunchbacked_septon.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Later, when Dunk heard Ser [Kyle the Cat](wiki:Kyle_the_Cat) blaming Bloodraven for the troubles of the realm and saying that many would welcome the return of [Bittersteel](wiki:Bittersteel), he remembered the hunchbacked septon and warned Kyle that words like that could cost a man his head.(wiki:Hunchbacked_septon.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
