## Appearances & Description

Kojja is slender, and is taller than [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly). She has smooth skin, as black as polished [jet](wiki:Jet). She has extremely white teeth.(wiki:Kojja_Mo.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Kojja captains the *[Cinnamon Wind](wiki:Cinnamon_Wind)'*s red archers, and is an expert marksman with her double-curved [goldenheart](wiki:Goldenheart) bow. She is able to speak at least the [Summer](wiki:Summer_Tongue) and [Common Tongues](wiki:Common_Tongue).(wiki:Kojja_Mo.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Kojja serves as the translator for the crew of the *Cinnamon Wind* while [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) and [Maester Aemon](wiki:Maester_Aemon) and [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) travel aboard the ship. While Gilly is initially afraid of Kojja's appearance due to her sheltered upbringing, she soon discovers she is a good person, and trusts her with [her baby](wiki:Aemon_Steelsong). Kojja loves to bounce him upon her knee and sing to him in the Summer Tongue.(wiki:Kojja_Mo.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Kojja shoots a dozen [pirates](wiki:Pirate) who try to board the *Cinnamon Wind* near the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) on their journey to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). At Maester Aemon's funeral aboard the ship, Kojja translates Sam's eulogy for the rest of the crew.(wiki:Kojja_Mo.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

After the funeral, the Summer Islanders drink [rum](wiki:Rum) and have sex to honor the dead. Kojja would have gone with [Xhondo Dhoru](wiki:Xhondo_Dhoru), but instead she takes care of the baby, to give Gilly and Sam a chance to be intimate. The next day, Sam avoids Gilly over the shame of breaking his [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) vows, until Xhondo drags him to Kojja. She confronts Sam on the matter, and threatens to put him off the ship unless he goes to Gilly. Kojja tells Sam that he honored the dead and the [gods](wiki:Gods) with their lovemaking, and that Gilly is aware she cannot [marry](wiki:Marriage) him, but only wants his companionship for a time.(wiki:Kojja_Mo.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

As the *Cinnamon Wind* travels through the [Redwyne Straits](wiki:Redwyne_Straits), Kojja Mo has her archers shoot at an [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) [longship](wiki:Longship) that approaches the ship, and it veers away. When they approach Oldtown, the captain of the *[Huntress](wiki:Huntress)* boards the ship with six [knights](wiki:Knight) and asks to inspect the holds. Quhuru Mo confers with his daughter before allowing them to do so. When Sam leaves the ship at Oldtown, Kojja tells him that they will stay in port as long as necessary to sell their cargo and restock, but that Gilly can stay aboard as long as she likes.(wiki:Kojja_Mo.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
