## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lyman's father, Ser [Raymun Darry](wiki:Raymun_Darry), is killed in the [battle at the Mummer's Ford](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford).(wiki:Lyman_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) According to a semi-canon source, Lord Darry, Raymun's father, dies in the battle as well, making Lyman the new [Lord of Darry](wiki:Lord_of_Darry).(wiki:Lyman_Darry.cite_ref-RPG_7-1) Young Lyman is brought to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) by Lord [Karyl Vance](wiki:Karyl_Vance) and Ser [Marq Piper](wiki:Marq_Piper) after the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), and during the war council the boy voices his opposition to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), who is supported by [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister). The [northmen](wiki:Northmen) and [rivermen](wiki:Rivermen) assembled at Riverrun proclaim [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) to be [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Lyman_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Clash of Kings

[Castle Darry](wiki:Castle_Darry) is [retaken](wiki:Liberation_of_Darry) by the forces of [House Darry](wiki:House_Darry), but they hold it less than a fortnight until Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) comes and [puts the entire castle to the sword](wiki:Sack_of_Darry), including Lyman.(wiki:Lyman_Darry.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Aside from a bastard cousin, Lyman was the last Darry of the male line. Following the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), his lands and title are granted by King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) to Ser [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister).(wiki:Lyman_Darry.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### A Storm of Swords

The deaths of Lyman and his father are lamented by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners).(wiki:Lyman_Darry.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Feast for Crows

Lancel weds [Amerei Frey](wiki:Amerei_Frey), Lyman's paternal cousin by his aunt [Mariya](wiki:Mariya_Darry).(wiki:Lyman_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:Lyman_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
