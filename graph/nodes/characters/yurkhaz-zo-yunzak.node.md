## Origins

Yurkhaz is an old [Yunkish](wiki:Yunkai) hero.(wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

He owns a group of Yunkish castrati [slaves](wiki:Slave) who sing songs in the ancient tongues of the [Old Empire of Ghis](wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis).(wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Appearances & Description

Ancient and frail,(wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Yurkhaz is bent-backed, wrinkled, and toothless.(wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Yurkhaz is carried around upon a [palanquin](wiki:Palanquin) so large forty slaves are needed to carry it.(wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### Storm of Swords

After the [battle near Yunkai](wiki:Battle_near_Yunkai), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) forces the [Wise Masters](wiki:Wise_Masters) to free the [slaves](wiki:Slaves) in [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai).(wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) After Daenerys departs for Meereen, the Wise Masters hire [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords) and seek alliances against her.(wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Yurkhaz signs the contract hiring the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown).(wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) His army is successful in the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor), but they are only victorious because of the [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) legions hired by the Yunkai'i. The leadership of the Wise Masters is questioned by many of the sellswords.(wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) Yurkhaz tasks the Windblown and the [Company of the Cat](wiki:Company_of_the_Cat) with preventing [Astapori](wiki:Astapor) refugees from reaching Yunkai.(wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

When [Yezzan zo Qaggaz](wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz) hosts the Yunkish supreme commander during the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen), Yurkhaz is attended by a dozen other lords. The enslaved [Penny](wiki:Penny) and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) are forced to perform during the gathering. Entertained by their antics, Yurkhaz persuades Yezzan that the dwarfs should have the honor of jousting in the [Great Pit of Daznak](wiki:Daznak's_Pit) to celebrate the signing of peace between Yunkai and Meeren.(wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Yurkhaz is among the seven commanders who enter Meereen to sign the peace and witness the opening of the [fighting pits](wiki:Fighting_pits). During the feast held in the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) sees her new husband, [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq), laughing with Yurkhaz and the other Yunkish lords.(wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

During the opening of Daznak's Pit, [Drogon](wiki:Drogon), one of Daenerys's three [dragons](wiki:Dragon), appears and panic ensues. Drogon flies away with Daenerys on his back.(wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Yurkhaz perishes as he tries to flee the dragon, either having been crushed beneath the feet of his own fleeing slaves and companions, or because his heart burst in terror.(wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) In the [Purple Lotus](wiki:Purple_Lotus), the [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince) tells [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) he saw Yurkhaz trampled to death.(wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

A council of masters takes command of the Yunkish army,(wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) and supreme command passes amongst the Wise Masters.(wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2) Claiming that Yurkhaz's death is a breach of the peace, the Yunkai'i execute one of their hostages, Admiral [Groleo](wiki:Groleo), and demand the deaths of the other two dragons, [Viserion](wiki:Viserion) and [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal).(wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2) According to [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq), the Wise Masters are using Yurkhaz's death to encourage Hizdahr to kill the dragons.(wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
