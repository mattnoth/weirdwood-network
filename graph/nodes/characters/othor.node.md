## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Othor is a member of the ranging party commanded by [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark), and he sings bawdy songs when they depart [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Othor.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Some months later, when [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) and [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) say their vows beneath the [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) grove north of the [Wall](wiki:Wall), Jon's [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) [Ghost](wiki:Ghost) finds the corpses of Othor and [Jafer Flowers](wiki:Jafer_Flowers) in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest).(wiki:Othor.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

The bodies are covered in mortal wounds, and their flesh is white as milk, except for their hands, which have turned black. Their eyes are open, blue as [sapphires](wiki:Sapphire). Samwell notes that their blood is dry and crusty, yet the bodies aren't rotting or touched by scavengers, and the [dogs](wiki:Dog) and [horses](wiki:Horse) refuse to go near them. He also recognizes that there's no blood anywhere around the bodies, meaning they could not have been killed there. [Dywen](wiki:Dywen) notes that Othor did not have blue eyes, and Ser [Jaremy Rykker](wiki:Jaremy_Rykker) realizes that neither did Jafer. Several of the other rangers urge that the bodies should be burned, but [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) wants [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) to examine them further. The corpses are brought back to Castle Black, carried in slings.(wiki:Othor.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

That night, reanimated as a [wight](wiki:Wight), Othor seeks out and attempts to kill Lord Commander Mormont. Jon Snow, woken by Ghost, enters the Lord Commander's chamber where he finds the door open and [Mormont's raven](wiki:Mormont's_raven) squawking. Ghost attacks Othor, who attempts to strangle the direwolf, until Jon cuts off an arm with his sword. Jon strikes at Othor's face, but the severed hand claws at his leg. Ghost attacks the hand, but Othor slams into Jon, knocking him down. In the fall, Jon loses his sword. Othor lands atop Jon and attempts to choke him, but is pulled off by Ghost. As Jon regains his feet, he sees Lord Commander Mormont in his bedroom doorway holding an oil lamp, as the severed hand approaches him. Jon grabs the oil lamp from the Lord Commander and throws it on the drapes, setting them afire. Jon then grabs the burning drapes and throws them onto Othor, praying that the wight will burn.(wiki:Othor.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

The fire catches in the dead man's clothing and consumes him entirely. Othor staggers across the solar, crashing against the furniture while flailing at the flames. The fire, out of control, guts the interior of the top two floors of the [Lord Commander's Tower](wiki:Lord_Commander's_Tower), but also destroys Othor's corpse. The twisted thing found in the ashes is only cooked meat and charred bone.(wiki:Othor.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
