## Identity

Bennard Stark was a member of House Stark and the second son of Lord Benjen Stark and Lady Lysa Locke (wiki:Bennard_Stark).

## Origins

Born 105 AC. Died 126 AC. Son of Lord Benjen Stark and Lysa Locke (track_b: Father, track_b: Mother).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

When his elder brother Rickon Stark, Lord of Winterfell, died in 121 AC, Bennard ruled the north as regent during the minority of his lord nephew Cregan. When Cregan came of age in 124 AC, Bennard was slow to surrender his power. In 126 AC, Cregan rose up against his uncle and imprisoned Bennard and his sons (wiki:Bennard_Stark).
