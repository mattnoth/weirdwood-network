## Origins

Redwyn lived during the reign of [Dorren Stark](wiki:Dorren_Stark), a [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North). He wrote an account of a ranging from the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) to [Lorn Point](wiki:Lorn_Point) on the [Frozen Shore](wiki:Frozen_Shore). During that ranging they fought [giants](wiki:Giants) and traded with the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest).(wiki:Redwyn.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) sent a letter to the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel), early in the reign of King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen), reporting of Redwyn's account.(wiki:Redwyn.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_dawn_age.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
