## Origins

Bessa was once propositioned by [Chett](wiki:Chett), who had picked [wildflowers](wiki:Plants) for her. Bessa rejected and mocked him, however, on account of his ugliness. The angry Chett then stabbed the girl repeatedly with his knife and watched her die. Chett fled [Hag's Mire](wiki:Hag's_Mire), but was caught at [Sevenstreams](wiki:Sevenstreams) and forced by Ser [Walder Rivers](wiki:Walder_Rivers) to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Bessa_(Hag's_Mire).cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
