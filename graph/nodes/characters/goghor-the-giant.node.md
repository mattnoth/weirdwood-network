## Appearances & Description

Goghor is gigantic with a brutal, scarred face(wiki:Goghor_the_Giant.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and fists as big as hams.(wiki:Goghor_the_Giant.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) He looks more bull than man, even to the bronze ring in his nose.(wiki:Goghor_the_Giant.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Goghor the Giant, along with his fellow pit fighters [Barsena Blackhair](wiki:Barsena_Blackhair), [Khrazz](wiki:Khrazz), [Camarron of the Count](wiki:Camarron_of_the_Count), the [Spotted Cat](wiki:Spotted_Cat), [Fearless Ithoke](wiki:Fearless_Ithoke), and [Belaquo Bonebreaker](wiki:Belaquo_Bonebreaker), accompanies [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) when he petitions [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) for the seventh time for the [fighting pits of Meereen](wiki:Fighting_pits_of_Meereen) to be reopened.(wiki:Goghor_the_Giant.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

When the pits are reopened Goghor the Giant is meant to fight [Belaquo Bonebreaker](wiki:Belaquo_Bonebreaker) to the death in the final match of the day in [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit), but the fight never takes place due to the appearance of [Drogon](wiki:Drogon).(wiki:Goghor_the_Giant.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

After Daenerys's disappearance, Goghor and other pit fighters serve as Hizdahr's guards.(wiki:Goghor_the_Giant.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Goghor_the_Giant.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Goghor is chosen to sit on the [ruling council of Meereen](wiki:Ruling_council_of_Meereen) along with Belaquo, Camarron, and the Spotted Cat. Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) insists on their presence, over the objections of [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq).(wiki:Goghor_the_Giant.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Shortly before the resumption of the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen), Goghor the Giant is among the several hundred pit fighters who have gathered for the battle near the [Spire of Skulls](wiki:Spire_of_Skulls). Goghor, together with Camarron and the Spotted Cat, is to lead the pit fighters in the battle. The pit fighters are to follow the [Stormcrows](wiki:Stormcrows) into battle and follow the horsemen into the breach of the Yunkish lines in order to slaughter as much as they can.(wiki:Goghor_the_Giant.cite_ref-barristan1_6-0)
