## Origins

It is said that Barsena has slain every woman she has faced in the [fighting pits of Meereen](wiki:Fighting_pits_of_Meereen) in the last eight years.(wiki:Barsena_Blackhair.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Appearances & Description

Barsena Blackhair is a tall, dark woman who moves like a feral [panther](wiki:Panther). She is around thirty years old.(wiki:Barsena_Blackhair.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) "A tall, dark woman of some thirty years, she moved with the feral grace of a panther. Naked save for breechclout and sandals." (`sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-daenerys-09.md:187`)

Barsena wears only breechclout and sandals when fighting.(wiki:Barsena_Blackhair.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Along with her fellow pit fighters [Khrazz](wiki:Khrazz), [Camarron of the Count](wiki:Camarron_of_the_Count), [Goghor the Giant](wiki:Goghor_the_Giant), the [Spotted Cat](wiki:Spotted_Cat), [Fearless Ithoke](wiki:Fearless_Ithoke), and [Belaquo Bonebreaker](wiki:Belaquo_Bonebreaker), Barsena accompanies [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) when he petitions [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) for the seventh time for the [fighting pits of Meereen](wiki:Fighting_pits_of_Meereen) to be reopened.(wiki:Barsena_Blackhair.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

When the pits are to be reopened to celebrate the marriage of Hizdahr and Daenerys, the queen wants to forbid women from competing. Barsena protests, however, and Daenerys relents. Barsena faces a [boar](wiki:Boar) with a dagger instead of a spear. Despite her quickness and skill, the boar's tusk rips Barsena's left leg open. Moments after the boar disembowels the fallen pit fighter, [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) descends into [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit), kills the boar with [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame), and begins to devour both corpses.(wiki:Barsena_Blackhair.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
