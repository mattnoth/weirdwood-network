## Origins

Cerrick came from the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands). The maester designed the dam of the [Chequy Water](wiki:Chequy_Water), which caused a conflict over the river between Lady [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber) of [Coldmoat](wiki:Coldmoat) and Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey) of [Standfast](wiki:Standfast).(wiki:Cerrick.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

After Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) defeated Ser [Lucas Inchfield](wiki:Lucas_Inchfield) in a [trial by battle](wiki:Trial_by_battle), Maester Cerrick treated his wounds. Cerrick was able to resuscitate Duncan after he had drowned in the river during the battle, due to his study of ironborn [priests'](wiki:Priests_of_the_Drowned_God) [kiss of life](wiki:Kiss_of_life). Duncan's [squire](wiki:Squire), [Egg](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen), worried about [poison](wiki:Poison), forced Cerrick to first taste anything he wanted to give the knight while Duncan was still unconscious.(wiki:Cerrick.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
