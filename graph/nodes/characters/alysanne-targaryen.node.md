## Identity

Queen Alysanne Targaryen, known as Good Queen Alysanne, was the queen consort of her brother King Jaehaerys I Targaryen. She was a dragonrider whose mount was Silverwing (wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen).

## Origins

Born in 36 AC in King's Landing (track_b: Born). Daughter of King Aenys I Targaryen and Queen Alyssa Velaryon (track_b: Father, Mother). Died on the 1st day of the 7th moon of 100 AC, aged 64, at Dragonstone (track_b: Died). Buried at Dragonstone (track_b: Buried).

## Narrative Arc

Alysanne was one of the most beloved queens in Westerosi history, known for her wisdom, charity, and influence on her husband's policies. She was instrumental in the abolition of the lord's right to the first night and was a champion of women's rights in Westeros. She made a historic progress to the North, visiting the Wall, and convinced Jaehaerys to grant the Night's Watch the territory known as the New Gift. She bore thirteen children with Jaehaerys, though many died young. In later years, she and Jaehaerys quarreled over the succession and the treatment of their children. She died at Dragonstone in 100 AC, predeceasing her husband by three years (wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen).
