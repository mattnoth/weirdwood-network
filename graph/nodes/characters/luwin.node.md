## Identity

Maester Luwin is the maester serving House Stark at Winterfell (wiki:Luwin). He serves as counselor, healer, and tutor to the Stark children.

## Appearances & Description

Luwin wears the maester's chain with links of many metals. He possesses a Valyrian steel link in his chain, indicating study of the higher mysteries, though he is skeptical of magic (wiki:Luwin). He is small and grey, with grey eyes and grey robes (wiki:Luwin).

## Narrative Arc

Luwin serves as a trusted advisor to Lord Eddard and Lady Catelyn Stark. When Catelyn receives the secret letter from her sister Lysa Arryn implicating the Lannisters in Jon Arryn's death, Luwin counsels the Starks (wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot6). After Bran's fall, Luwin cares for the crippled boy and later presents Tyrion's saddle design so Bran can ride (wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot24). When Eddard goes south and Robb marches to war, Luwin becomes the primary counselor at Winterfell, advising young Bran in governance (wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Racok16). He is skeptical when the Reeds speak of green dreams, and discourages Bran from pursuing warging (wiki:Luwin). After Theon Greyjoy captures Winterfell, Luwin counsels Theon to take the black rather than face certain death (wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Racok66). During the sack of Winterfell by Ramsay Snow's forces, Luwin is mortally wounded by a spear. Bran, Rickon, and their companions find him dying in the godswood, where he gives final counsel and asks Osha for a merciful death (wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Racok69).
