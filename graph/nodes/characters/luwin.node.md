## Origins

Presumably the replacement at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) for [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Walys](wiki:Walys), Luwin delivered all(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) of the children of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) and Lady [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Stark): [Robb](wiki:Robb_Stark) (who was born at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun)),(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark), [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark), [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark), and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark). Luwin educated the Stark children, as well as Eddard's [bastard](wiki:Bastard), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), and Eddard's [ward](wiki:Ward), [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy). For instance, he taught about maesters(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) and stars(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Rasos26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) to Jon, the history of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) to Bran,(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Rasos40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) and about the historical [lords of Winterfell](wiki:Lords_of_Winterfell) and the beliefs of the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) to Theon.(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Luwin also treated Arya's cuts and scrapes.(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

Luwin once made a boy out of pottery and threw it from Winterfell's walls to show Bran what would happen if the boy were to fall while climbing, but Bran ignored the warning.(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

## Appearances & Description

A tireless maester,(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Luwin is a small grey man with grey eyes and thinning hair.(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He wears a robe of grey wool with voluminous sleeves. In the sleeves are sewn pockets, from which Luwin is always drawing things.(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) His maester's collar is a simple choker,(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and he often tugs at the chain around his neck when he is uncomfortable.(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) His turret at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) is always so cluttered that it seems to [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) a wonder that Luwin ever finds anything. He owns a bronze [far-eye](wiki:Far-eye) from [Myr](wiki:Myr).(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Luwin is one of the few maesters who have bothered to earn the [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) link, which signifies the study of the [higher mysteries](wiki:Higher_mysteries).(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Racok28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) He is skeptical of [hedge wizards](wiki:Hedge_wizard)(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and [Old Nan](wiki:Old_Nan)'s stories,(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) believing that the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) and [giants](wiki:Giants) are extinct.(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

At [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) Maester Luwin receives and brings [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) a letter from her sister, [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn), saying that Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) murdered her husband, Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King). After Catelyn reads the letter, Luwin joins Catelyn in urging Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) to accept Lord Arryn's vacant post. The Starks decide that they must continue on as if nothing happened, with Ned continuing south with the king, [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

After [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) is crippled from a fall, Luwin tends to the boy and tries to aid a shocked Catelyn in administering the castle. He also treats Cat's wounds after a [catspaw](wiki:Catspaw) attacks Bran and her with a [Valyrian steel dagger](wiki:Valyrian_steel_dagger). Luwin is part of the group Catelyn confers with about what to do next before she leaves for [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), urging caution.(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) provides the maester with plans for a saddle for the crippled Bran,(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) and Luwin creates a wicker basket which [Hodor](wiki:Hodor) uses to carry Bran.(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) Luwin again treats Bran's wounds after he is attacked by [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood).(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

When [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) departs south against Luwin's advice after Lord Eddard has been taken prisoner, he advises Bran to listen to the maester's counsel.(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3) Once Catelyn reaches [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) during her return to the [north](wiki:North), Cat sends Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) back to Winterfell to serve as castellan and help govern with Luwin.(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

Luwin accompanies Bran into the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell), where the maester is bitten by [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark)'s direwolf, [Shaggydog](wiki:Shaggydog). He then discusses [dragonglass](wiki:Dragonglass), the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest), and the [Pact](wiki:Pact) with Bran. The maester is saddened when Winterfell is informed of Eddard's execution in King's Landing.(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

### A Clash of Kings

With Robb Stark's departure, the day-to-day rule of the north falls to Rodrik and Luwin, who are acting with Bran's voice. The maester provides sleeping draughts to help Bran with his [dreams](wiki:Greensight).(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Racok28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) Luwin unsuccessfully advises Bran to befriend [Big Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Jammos)) and [Little Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)), Catelyn's new wards,(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-1) and the maester scolds the two [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) for bullying Hodor(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) and for not being sympathethic after the death of their uncle, Ser [Stevron Frey](wiki:Stevron_Frey).(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) Winterfell hosts the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast), which is attended by many of the northern bannermen. Luwin and Rodrik assist Bran when he meets Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly), Lady [Donella Hornwood](wiki:Donella_Hornwood), [Mors](wiki:Mors_Umber) and [Hother Umber](wiki:Hother_Umber), and [Leobald Tallhart](wiki:Leobald_Tallhart).(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1)(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

With Rodrik's departure to deal with [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), the fight over the [Hornwood](wiki:House_Hornwood) inheritance,(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Racok28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) and [Dagmer Cleftjaw](wiki:Dagmer) threatening [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Torrhen's_Square), governance falls completely on Luwin's shoulders. With reports of [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) attacking the north, Luwin sends [ravens](wiki:Raven) to White Harbor, the [barrowlands](wiki:Barrowlands), and the wolfswood.(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

Luwin is wounded during the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) by [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy), although he manages to send one raven to White Harbor.(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-1) The maester continues to serve in the ironborn-held castle, and Theon forces him to participate in the unsuccessful search for Bran and Rickon after they disappear.(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) Theon presents the bodies of two [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) as the Starks and refuses Luwin's request to bury "Bran" and "Rickon" in the crypts.(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

When Rodrik gathers an army to retake Winterfell, Luwin advises Theon to surrender and join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), which Theon considers. Rodrik is then betrayed by Ramsay Snow in the [battle at Winterfell](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell), however. Maester Luwin is then speared and run down during the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell) by a [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) knight.(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1) Luwin is able to crawl to the [godswood of Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell), where he is found by Bran and his companions after they leave the crypts. As he lies dying beneath the [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree), Luwin instructs [Osha](wiki:Osha) to split up Bran and Rickon. He is then given the [gift of mercy](wiki:Gift_of_mercy) by Osha after the children are led away.(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

At Winterfell for the marriage of Ramsay to "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)", Theon notices that Luwin's ravens are living in the ruins of the maester's tower as well as in the castle's godswood.(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) brings three maesters, [Henly](wiki:Henly_(maester)), [Medrick](wiki:Medrick), and [Rhodry](wiki:Rhodry), to tend to the ravens.(wiki:Luwin.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-1)
