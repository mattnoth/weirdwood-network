## Identity

Loreon Lannister was the Lord of Casterly Rock, Shield of Lannisport, Warden of the West, and head of House Lannister during the reign of King Aegon III Targaryen (wiki:Loreon_Lannister_(son_of_Jason)).

## Origins

Born 126 AC (track_b: Born). Father: Jason Lannister (track_b: Father). Mother: Johanna Westerling (track_b: Mother). He had five trueborn sisters, including Cerelle and Tyshara, as well as several bastard half-sisters (wiki:Loreon_Lannister_(son_of_Jason)).

## Narrative Arc

In 130 AC, when Loreon was four, his father Jason died at the Battle at the Red Fork. Loreon became Lord of Casterly Rock, with his mother Johanna ruling as regent during his minority. She waged war on the ironborn in his name. Rodrik Greyjoy, a salt son of Lord Dalton Greyjoy, was captured, gelded, and made Loreon's fool (wiki:Loreon_Lannister_(son_of_Jason).cite_ref-Rfab).
