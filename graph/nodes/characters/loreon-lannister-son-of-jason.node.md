## Origins

Loreon was the son of Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister) and his wife, Lady [Johanna Westerling](wiki:Johanna_Lannister).(wiki:Loreon_Lannister_(son_of_Jason).cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He had five trueborn sisters, including [Cerelle](wiki:Cerelle_Lannister_(daughter_of_Jason)) and [Tyshara](wiki:Tyshara_Lannister), as well as several [bastard](wiki:Bastard) half-sisters.(wiki:Loreon_Lannister_(son_of_Jason).cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

In [130 AC](wiki:130_AC), when Loreon was four years old, his father Jason died during the [Battle at the Red Fork](wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork). Loreon subsequently became the [Lord of Casterly Rock](wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock). His mother, the Dowager Lady Johanna, ruled as his [regent](wiki:Regent) during his [minority](wiki:Customs). During the regency, his mother waged [war](wiki:Struggle_for_power) on the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) in his name.(wiki:Loreon_Lannister_(son_of_Jason).cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [Rodrik Greyjoy](wiki:Rodrik_Greyjoy_(son_of_Dalton)), one of the [salt sons](wiki:Salt_son) of Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), was made Loreon's [fool](wiki:Fool) after being captured and [gelded](wiki:Eunuch).(wiki:Loreon_Lannister_(son_of_Jason).cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Loreon_Lannister_(son_of_Jason).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands_-_the_red_kraken.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
