## Appearances & Description

Lady has grey fur and golden(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Ragot44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) yellow eyes. She is the smallest of the litter.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Ragot16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Lady likes to have her ears scratched, and when fed under the table, takes the food delicately. She is very protective of Sansa.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lady is discovered by [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) and [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), along with the rest of her litter, after her mother was killed by a [stag](wiki:Stag).(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Lady bonds with [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), and adopts her mistress's dainty manners and trusting nature.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

At the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn) on the journey from [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Sansa is frightened when she meets Ser [Ilyn Payne](wiki:Ilyn_Payne), the [King's Justice](wiki:King's_Justice), and Lady growls and moves to protect her. Prince [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), Sansa's betrothed, courts Sansa and asks her to ride with him, and she leaves Lady behind at the camp.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) Near the [ruby ford](wiki:Ruby_ford), Joffrey and Sansa come across Sansa's sister, [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), playing at swords with her friend [Mycah](wiki:Mycah). Joffrey draws his sword, [Lion's Tooth](wiki:Lion's_Tooth), and threatens Mycah, but Arya fights with him; her direwolf [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf)) disarms and superficially wounds him.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

In a trial at [Darry](wiki:Darry), King [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_Baratheon) orders Nymeria killed, but she cannot be found, so the spiteful Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) orders Lady to be put down instead. Despite Sansa's protests, her father, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), cannot get Robert to relent. Eddard chooses to perform the execution himself, as is his custom, and to spare Lady pain she might suffer if Ilyn Payne were to do the deed. The trusting Lady does not sense Eddard's intention, and is killed with a single blow of his greatsword, [Ice](wiki:Ice). Eddard orders for four of his men to take Lady's body north to be buried at Winterfell.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Ragot16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

When Lady's bones arrive at Winterfell, her litter-mates [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind), [Shaggydog](wiki:Shaggydog), and [Summer](wiki:Summer) all sense her death and howl mournfully. Lady is buried in the [lichyard](wiki:Lichyard) at Winterfell, where the [Kings of Winter](wiki:Kings_of_Winter) had laid their faithful servants.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

In King's Landing, when Eddard hears that [Summer](wiki:Summer) had saved [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark)'s life, he wonders if the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods) had sent the direwolves, and what it might mean that he had killed Sansa's.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Sansa weeps for days, but cannot hate Joffrey for Lady, and instead places the majority of the blame on her sister Arya for Lady's death. Sansa blames Queen Cersei as well, but softens to her nevertheless.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Sansa sometimes dreams of Lady, and feels as if the direwolf were there watching her.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Ragot44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

When [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) receives the letter Queen Cersei had forced Sansa to write to Winterfell, calling Eddard Stark a [traitor](wiki:Traitor) and summoning Robb to swear fealty to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_I_Baratheon), he does not understand what could have caused Sansa to write such things. Their brother Bran believes it is due to Sansa having lost Lady.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Clash of Kings

At Winterfell, Bran Stark listens to Summer and Shaggydog howl, and wonders if they miss their brothers and sisters too, if they are calling to Grey Wind and Ghost and Nymeria and the ghost of Lady.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Sansa visits the [godswood](wiki:Godswood) in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), and thinks Lady would have liked it there. When Ser [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard) offers to help her escape, Sansa is uncertain whether to trust him, and thinks of Lady and how she could smell lies.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

King Joffrey, when threatening Sansa with a crossbow, tells her he hasn't forgotten how her direwolf assaulted him. Sansa protests that Lady never hurt him, but he killed her anyway.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Arya includes Queen Cersei and King Joffrey on her [death list](wiki:Arya_Stark) for many reasons, but also for Lady's death.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) She is glad to hear that Castle Darry will be burned, because it was where Cersei had made Eddard Stark kill Lady.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Sansa has a nightmare of the [riot of King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing), and calls for Lady to help her.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Racok52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), when Sansa fears that she will die, she wonders if she will meet Lady again when she's dead.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Storm of Swords

During a supper with Joffrey's new betrothed [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), her grandmother [Olenna](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell) questions Sansa about the king's nature. Olenna gets Sansa to speak the truth about Joffrey's cruelty, and Sansa confesses that he lied about the incident with Mycah and made her father kill Lady.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

Bran Stark [skinchanges](wiki:Skinchanger) his direwolf Summer, who remembers his litter-mates, including Lady, "the sister they had lost."(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Rasos9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

The night before [Joffrey's wedding to Margaery](wiki:Purple_wedding), Sansa dreams that she is back at home in Winterfell, running through the godswood with Lady. When she wakes, and begins to prepare for the wedding and for her planned escape, she thinks that if Lady were with her, she would not be afraid.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

At the [tower](wiki:Drearfort) of [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) in the [Fingers](wiki:Fingers), Sansa bonds with an [old blind dog](wiki:Old_blind_dog), and after waking from a dream, she tells him she wishes he was Lady.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Summer forms a new pack with [Varamyr](wiki:Varamyr)'s [wolves](wiki:Wolves), and Bran reminds him that his old one is still out there, even though Lady and Grey Wind are dead.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

Jon Snow recalls his siblings, including Sansa brushing out Lady's coat and singing to herself.(wiki:Lady.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
