## Identity

Brandon Stark, typically called Bran, is the second son of Lord Eddard Stark and Lady Catelyn Tully. A major POV character in AGOT, ACOK, ASOS, and ADWD (mentioned only in AFFC). Named after his late uncle Brandon Stark. His direwolf is Summer, with whom he shares a strong warg connection. Seven years old at the start of AGOT (wiki:Bran_Stark).

## Origins

Born 290 AC at Winterfell. Son of Eddard Stark and Catelyn Tully (track_b: Father, track_b: Mother). Has siblings Robb, Jon, Sansa, Arya, and Rickon (wiki:Bran_Stark).

## Appearances & Description

Favors his mother Catelyn in appearance: thick auburn hair and deep blue eyes of the Tullys. Sweet and thoughtful, well-loved by everyone at Winterfell. Has a fascination with climbing and exploring the castle walls and ramparts. Owns grey breeches and a white doublet trimmed with vair. His favorite clasp is a wolf's-head brooch of silver and polished jet (wiki:Bran_Stark).

## Narrative Arc

Bran witnesses his father execute a Night's Watch deserter, then finds six direwolf pups. While climbing the broken tower at Winterfell, he discovers Jaime and Cersei Lannister in an incestuous act and is pushed from the window by Jaime, falling and losing the use of his legs. An assassination attempt follows, foiled by his direwolf Summer and his mother Catelyn. After Robb marches south, Bran serves as acting lord of Winterfell. Jojen and Meera Reed arrive and help Bran understand his greensight and warging abilities. When Theon Greyjoy captures Winterfell, Bran and Rickon escape through the crypts with the Reeds, Hodor, and Osha. The group splits — Rickon goes with Osha while Bran heads north beyond the Wall with the Reeds and Hodor. They encounter Coldhands, who guides them to the cave of the three-eyed crow beneath a great weirwood. There, Bran meets the last greenseer, Brynden Rivers (Bloodraven), and begins training to use the weirwood network to see through time (wiki:Bran_Stark).
