### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After finishing with the freckled [sunset girl](wiki:Sunset_girl) in a [Selhorys](wiki:Selhorys) brothel, a drunken [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) stumbles downstairs and is recognized by Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) who has engaged the services of the silvery haired whore. Upon seeing the girl squirming in the knight's lap, Tyrion thinks to himself that he never saw her and if he had he would have taken her upstairs instead of the freckled girl.
