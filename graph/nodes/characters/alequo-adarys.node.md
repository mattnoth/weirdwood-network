## Origins

The [Band of Nine](wiki:Band_of_Nine) were a group of merchants, sellswords and pirates who joined together to carve kingdoms out for themselves. After conquering the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands), Alequo convinced the others to take [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh). After successfully [sacking](wiki:Sack_of_Tyrosh) the city and deposing the [current](wiki:Archon_of_Tyrosh_(War_of_the_Ninepenny_Kings)) [Archon](wiki:Archon_of_Tyrosh), Alequo was installed as its head of state. The Band of Nine next meant to conquer [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) for [Maelys I Blackfyre](wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre), and fought a war called the [War of the Ninepenny Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Ninepenny_Kings) on the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) against the Westerosi forces. The Band of nine was defeated there, and Maelys killed. While the remainder of the Band of Nine was defeated half a year later, Alequo, known as the Tyrant of Tyrosh, would rule the Free City for six more years, until he was poisoned by his queen. With Alequo's death, the Archon of Tyrosh was restored.(wiki:Alequo_Adarys.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
