## Origins

### Youth

When he was a boy, Euron had a dream that he could fly.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-4) He often mocked his younger brother [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) when they were children.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Another brother, [Aeron](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy), has terrible memories associated with Euron and the sound of a rusted iron hinge.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-8)

[Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) and his younger brothers, Euron and Victarion, convinced their father, Lord [Quellon Greyjoy](wiki:Quellon_Greyjoy), to join [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), and they raided the [Reach](wiki:Reach) late in the war. After Quellon was killed in a [battle at the Mander](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mander), however, the new Lord Balon returned to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) with his brothers.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### Greyjoy's Rebellion

Rejecting the authority of the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), Balon declared himself [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands) in [289 AC](wiki:289_AC). At the outset of [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion), Euron concocted a plan to sail into [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) and [burn the Lannister fleet](wiki:Burning_of_the_Lannister_fleet) at anchor. Led by Victarion, the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) followed Euron's plan and destroyed the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands)' naval force.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Balon's rebellion was eventually defeated by King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), however.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### Exile

Euron has often gone reaving.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-9) In [297 AC](wiki:297_AC), he was sent away from the Iron Islands by Lord Balon as punishment for seducing or raping [Victarion's third wife](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy's_third_wife), a [salt wife](wiki:Salt_wife), and he was warned never to return while Balon was alive. Victarion would have killed Euron if not for the taboo against [kinslaying](wiki:Kinslaying).(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) Euron laughed at Victarion when they parted.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-10)

Euron has [several sons](wiki:Euron's_mongrel_sons) born to [whores](wiki:Whores) and captured women, but does not have any regard for his [bastards](wiki:Bastard). One such boy is ten years old with woolly hair and skin the color of mud.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-5)

Since Euron's banishment, the *[Silence](wiki:Silence)* has sailed, pillaged, and raped all over the [known world](wiki:Known_world). Euron boasts to have travelled to [Asshai](wiki:Asshai) and even sailed the [Smoking Sea](wiki:Smoking_Sea) and walked the smoking ruins of [Valyria](wiki:Valyria).(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Euron also claims to have owned a [dragon egg](wiki:Dragon_egg) which a [Myrish](wiki:Myr) wizard was unable to hatch.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-6) The Crow's Eye alleges he tossed the egg into the sea during one of his foul moods.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-7)

## Appearances & Description

A handsome man, Euron is the most comely of the sons of the late Lord [Quellon Greyjoy](wiki:Quellon_Greyjoy).(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) He has pale skin, black hair, and a neat dark beard.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

Euron wears a black leather patch over his left eye,(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) and sometimes a red leather eyepatch.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) He is nicknamed Crow's Eye, possibly because the leather patch conceals a "black eye shining with malice", per his nephew, [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy).(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-9) Euron's right eye is as blue as the summer sky and is called his "smiling eye".(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4) Additionally, since Euron has begun drinking [shade of the evening](wiki:Shade_of_the_evening), his lips have turned blue.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

> His hair was still black as a midnight sea, with never a whitecap to be seen, and his face was still smooth and pale beneath his neat dark beard. A black leather patch covered Euron's left eye, but his right was blue as a summer sky.

— [Victarion Greyjoy](victarion-greyjoy)'s first sight of Euron at the kingsmoot feast, AFFC The Iron Captain I (`sources/chapters/affc/affc-the-iron-captain-01.md:137`) [L886]

> He wore the sable cloak he took from Blacktyde, his red leather eye patch, and nothing else.

— Euron at the victory feast at Lord Hewett's Town after the Taking of the Shields, AFFC The Reaver I (`sources/chapters/affc/affc-the-reaver-01.md:227`) [L896]

Euron has a black reputation.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) He is considered cunning(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) and mocking,(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5)(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) and has a glib tongue.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3) The Crow's Eye is hated by his brothers [Aeron](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy),(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) who considers him [godless](wiki:God), and [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy), as Euron impregnated [his third wife](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy's_third_wife).(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-6)

Despite his dangerous and mercurial nature, Euron has little trouble in drawing men to his service, especially freaks and fools.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) While most [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) captains keep the lion's share of plunder, Euron takes almost nothing for himself.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Forsaken_12-0) Victarion thinks of Euron's supporters as his pets.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-7)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Sailing on *[Silence](wiki:Silence)*, Euron is an [outlaw](wiki:Outlaw), [pirate](wiki:Pirate), and raider.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Clash of Kings

Having returned to [Pyke](wiki:Pyke_(island)) for the first time since [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion), [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) notes that his uncle Euron's ship, *Silence*, is not in the fleet gathered at [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport) by his father, Lord [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy).(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

### A Storm of Swords

News comes to [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), via [the captain](wiki:Captain_of_the_Myraham) of the *[Myraham](wiki:Myraham)* that Balon, self-styled [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North), fell to his death from [Pyke](wiki:Pyke). Euron returned to Lordsport and claimed the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair). When Lord [Sawane Botley](wiki:Sawane_Botley) objected to his claim, Euron had him drowned in a cask of seawater.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Euron claims to have captured a [galleas](wiki:Ship) out of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) that held four [warlocks](wiki:Warlocks),(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-8) including [Pyat Pree](wiki:Pyat_Pree),(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rawoiafeuron_greyjoy.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) When one warlock threatened him, Euron killed the man and fed him to the other three, who he now holds [hostage](wiki:Hostage).(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-9) The ship had a cask of [shade of the evening](wiki:Shade_of_the_evening), the [wine](wiki:Wine) of warlocks, which Euron took up drinking.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-10) According to a semi-canon source, he also seized the warlocks' [dragon horn](wiki:Dragonbinder).(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rawoiafqarth.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

[Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) is informed that his elder brother, Euron, returned to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) the day after Balon's death and seized the Seastone Chair. The Damphair calls for a [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot) to contest Euron.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6) The timing of Euron's return is questioned by [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) and Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw).(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) Sawane's son [Tristifer](wiki:Tristifer_Botley) reports that the Crow's Eye has been bribing supporters with treasure.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2)

Euron mocks his younger brothers, [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) and Aeron, and his niece, [Asha](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), when he meets his kin at [Nagga's Cradle](wiki:Nagga's_Cradle).(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-11) During the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), Euron has [Cragorn](wiki:Cragorn) blow the dragon horn, which Euron claims he found in the feared ruins of [Valyria](wiki:Valyria), and he brags about having sailed all over the [known world](wiki:Known_world), including [Asshai](wiki:Asshai). Euron promises to conquer all of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) for the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) using [dragons](wiki:Dragons), which he claims he can bind to his will with the horn.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1) Although Aeron considers the Crow's Eye to be a godless man, Euron is elected king, besting his Victarion, Asha, and other contenders.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-2)

Asha flees from Old Wyk, and Aeron disappears after promising to raise [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) in rebellion.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-11) Euron gives plunder from throughout the [known world](wiki:Known_world) to the ironborn captains.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-12) The Crow's Eye has Lord [Baelor Blacktyde](wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde) killed when the man refuses to accept him as his king, taking Baelor's sable cloak as a trophy.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-13) Cragorn dies with charred lungs.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-14)

Euron begins raids upon the [Reach](wiki:Reach), intending to sell captives into [slavery](wiki:Slavery) in [Essos](wiki:Essos). After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), Euron holds a victory's feast in [Lord Hewett's castle](wiki:Lord_Hewett's_castle) with the tied and gagged Lord [Humfrey Hewett](wiki:Humfrey_Hewett) seated next to him. The Crow's Eye forces Humfrey's daughters and granddaughters to serve food and drink to the victorious ironborn. Euron takes Humfrey's bastard daughter, [Falia Flowers](wiki:Falia_Flowers), as a bedmate, and under her suggestion commands the noble-born ladies to strip and serve his men naked.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-15)

Euron weakens possible rivals by giving lands and titles to key followers of theirs. However, he fails to move the ironborn beyond raiding, as they are reluctant to embark on a dangerous journey in search of dragons after Lord Harlaw points out all the hazards of such an endeavor. Euron sends Victarion and the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) to find [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), court her in his name, and bring her and her dragons to Westeros. He tells Victarion that he can succeed him on the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair) after Euron takes the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-16)

Ironborn attack the [Mander](wiki:Mander),(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) the [Arbor](wiki:Invasion_of_the_Arbor),(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1) and reach the [Whispering Sound](wiki:Battle_on_Whispering_Sound).(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) tasks Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) with resolving the [siege of Dragonstone](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone) so that the [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet) can be freed to deal with the ironborn.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-2)

### A Dance with Dragons

Euron styles himself [King of the Iron Islands and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North),(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) although he ignores Balon's conquests in the [north](wiki:North), including [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin), [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte), and [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Torrhen's_Square).(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) With [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) having fled the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) for Deepwood, Euron weds her to [Erik Ironmaker](wiki:Erik_Ironmaker) using a [seal](wiki:Seal) as her proxy.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1)

Before [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) departs with the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) for [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), Euron gives him Maester [Kerwin](wiki:Kerwin),(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) an unnamed [dusky woman](wiki:Dusky_woman),(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-2), and the [dragon horn](wiki:Dragonbinder).(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) Victarion plans to claim Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) as his own bride instead of bringing her to the Crow's Eye.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-3) During his voyage east, Victarion remembers that "Euron's gifts are poisoned" when he hears [Moqorro](wiki:Moqorro), a rescued [red priest](wiki:Red_priest), state that those who blow the horn die.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1)

### The Winds of Winter

Euron has his mutes capture Aeron Damphair after the kingsmoot, and imprisons him in the belly of the *Silence*. Euron admits to causing the deaths of [Harlon](wiki:Harlon_Greyjoy), [Robin](wiki:Robin_Greyjoy), and Balon, making him a [kinslayer](wiki:Kinslaying). He also admits he expects the followers to which he granted the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands) to fail to hold them, thus ridding himself of potential enemies. Euron no longer wears his driftwood [crown](wiki:Crown), but instead wears an [iron](wiki:Iron) crown with points made from [shark](wiki:Shark) teeth. He forces Aeron to drink [shade of the evening](wiki:Shade_of_the_evening), and the Damphair has visions of Euron on a throne of skulls and then a bloody [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), with gods impaled on its spikes. A later vision sees Euron as a [kraken](wiki:Kraken)-like figure with a tentacled face, seated on the Iron Throne, with a shadow woman by his side.

Prior to sailing against the [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet) and the ships of [House Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower), Euron wears a suit of black scale armor with various patterns, glyphs, and arcane symbols. Aeron believes it to be made of [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel).(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Forsaken_12-1)

Euron has Aeron bound to the prow of the *Silence*, where he is joined by Falia, pregnant but tongueless. Euron also orders his captains to bind the priests and [septons](wiki:Septon) and warlocks he has kidnapped to the prows of their respective ships.(wiki:Euron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Forsaken_12-2)
