## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Red Ralf urges Lord Captain [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) to make a claim on the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair),(wiki:Ralf_Stonehouse.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) and he is one of Victarion's champions at the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot).(wiki:Ralf_Stonehouse.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Victarion's brother, [Euron Crow's Eye](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), is instead chosen king.(wiki:Ralf_Stonehouse.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

At the feast celebrating the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), Ralf is among those who shout down King Euron's plans to sail to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay), preferring instead to attack the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor).(wiki:Ralf_Stonehouse.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) gives Red Ralf the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet)'s swiftest ships to sail the [corsair's road](wiki:Corsair's_road) along the northern coast of [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos), where there are provisions for men who are not afraid to pay the [iron price](wiki:Iron_price) in the mud-and-blood towns in the [Basilisk Isles](wiki:Basilisk_Isles). Victarion gave orders to reunite off the southern tip of the [Isle of Cedars](wiki:Isle_of_Cedars); only nine of the ships that had sailed with Red Ralf turn up, with Ralf himself among the missing.(wiki:Ralf_Stonehouse.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
