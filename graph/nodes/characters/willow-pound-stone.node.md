## Origins

In *[The Testimony of Mushroom](wiki:The_Testimony_of_Mushroom)*, the [dwarf](wiki:Dwarf) asserts it was Willow who killed Prince [Maelor Targaryen](wiki:Maelor_Targaryen) by clutching him so tightly that she broke his back and crushed him to death.(wiki:Willow_Pound-Stone.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Afterward, Willow was hanged from the center span of the old bridge at the command of Lady [Caswell](wiki:House_Caswell).(wiki:Willow_Pound-Stone.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

## Appearances & Description

According to [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom), Willow weighed thirty stone (420 lbs; 190,5 kg), and was [simpleminded](wiki:Simple) and half-mad. She had earned her name pounding clothes clean in the [Mander](wiki:Mander).(wiki:Willow_Pound-Stone.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
