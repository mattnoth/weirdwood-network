## Origins

One of Lord Theomore's [household knights](wiki:Household_knight) [fostered](wiki:Fosterage) a [wildling](wiki:Wildling) girl who been captured by [rangers](wiki:Rangers) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Theomore_Manderly.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)

Theomore's daughter [Mara](wiki:Mara_Manderly) served as a companion to Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) in [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). Lord Manderly lavishly entertained Alysanne when the queen visited [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) in [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) during a [royal progress](wiki:Royal_progress). Theomore served an [aurochs](wiki:Aurochs) at the welcoming feast, and his daughter, [Jessamyn](wiki:Jessamyn_Manderly), acted as the queen's [cupbearer](wiki:Cupbearer). Theomore held a small [tourney](wiki:Tourney) to honor the queen and present his [knights](wiki:Knight). Alysanne hosted a [women's court](wiki:Women's_court) in [Lord Manderly's hall](wiki:Merman's_Court), something previously unheard of in the [north](wiki:North).(wiki:Theomore_Manderly.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-11)

Theomore and Alysanne negotiated the [betrothals](wiki:Betrothal) of two of her [ladies-in-waiting](wiki:Handmaid) to Theomore's younger sons and a third to a nephew. His eldest daughter and three nieces joined the queen's own party as well, with the expectation they would be pledged to suitable [lords](wiki:Lord) and knights at the [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) court of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Theomore_Manderly.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-12)

In [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) Lord Manderly attended a tourney in the capital to celebrate the tenth anniversary of Jaehaerys's coronation.(wiki:Theomore_Manderly.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-13) One of Theomore's daughters [married](wiki:Married) Lord [Darklyn](wiki:House_Darklyn) at [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) in [72 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Theomore_Manderly.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Queen Alysanne betrothed her daughter, Princess [Viserra Targaryen](wiki:Viserra_Targaryen), to Theomore, who had been four times a widower, in [86 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). But the headstrong girl died before her departure to White Harbor, when she was thrown from her [horse](wiki:Horse)'s saddle in the streets of King's Landing, in [87 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Theomore_Manderly.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)(wiki:Theomore_Manderly.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Appearances & Description

Theomore had at least three sons and several daughters, as well as nieces and nephews. According to Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), Theomore had a famously large family.(wiki:Theomore_Manderly.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)

Theomore grew stout in his old age. He was a skilled warrior and canny [lord](wiki:Lord).(wiki:Theomore_Manderly.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
