## Origins

Roland's daughter [Johanna](wiki:Johanna_Lannister) was married to [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister), the [Lord of Casterly Rock](wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock) and [Warden of the West](wiki:Warden_of_the_West). After the death of Lord Jason during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), rule of the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) passed to Lady Johanna and Lord Roland, for Jason's son and heir [Loreon](wiki:Loreon_Lannister_(son_of_Jason)) was a boy of four.(wiki:Roland_Westerling.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Roland and Johanna later attended the coronation of the young [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in [131 AC](wiki:131_AC). A [council](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III) of [regents](wiki:Regent) was selected to rule the realm during Aegon's minority, with Roland selected as one of the seven regents. Lord Westerling's daughter Johanna was also a consideration for the regency. When the council discussed Aegon III's heir, Roland was against naming Princess [Baela](wiki:Baela_Targaryen), for [her dragon](wiki:Moondancer) had brought down the late [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen). He feared that crowning Baela would open old wounds among the [greens](wiki:Greens).(wiki:Roland_Westerling.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

When the [Winter Fever](wiki:Winter_Fever) struck the capital in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Roland grew ill and later succumbed to the disease, partly due to his age.(wiki:Roland_Westerling.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
