## Origins

In [54 AC](wiki:54_AC) when a mysterious [illness](wiki:Illness) killed Maester [Culiper](wiki:Culiper) and several of [Rhaena's](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) close friends on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), Maester Anselm sent a [raven](wiki:Raven) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with a description of the disease.(wiki:Anselm.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

When the [small council](wiki:Small_council) realized that the description matched the [poison](wiki:Poison) known as the [tears of Lys](wiki:Tears_of_Lys), it sent a warning to the castle. Anselm was found in the [rookery](wiki:Rookery) with a dagger in his back. Rhaena's alienated husband, [Androw Farman](wiki:Androw_Farman), poisoned his wife's friends in revenge against her, who he felt had belittled him.(wiki:Anselm.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
