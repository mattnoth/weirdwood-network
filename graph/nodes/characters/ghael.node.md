## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ghael is sent to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) on board the [Indigo Star](wiki:Indigo_Star) to offer terms for a pact between King Cleon and [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen). Among the terms was an offer to combine forces to attack [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai), and marriage between the two leaders.(wiki:Ghael.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Cleon sends Ghael again as an envoy to Daenerys Targaryen in Meereen, this time as a titled lord (of what, they are unsure). He gives a gift of slippers and this time only offers an alliance against Yunkai.(wiki:Ghael.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Daenerys refuses him and advises against attacking Yunkai.(wiki:Ghael.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Cleon ignores Daenerys's advice and attacks Yunkai. Ghael is later sent before Daenerys again and begs that she send [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) to protect Astapor. Daenerys refuses him yet again, prompting the envoy to spit in her face. Belwas slams Ghael into the ground, smashing his teeth, before he is dragged out of court by his feet.(wiki:Ghael.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
