## Identity

Ser Elmo Tully was the Lord of Riverrun and head of House Tully during the Dance of the Dragons, briefly succeeding his grandfather Lord Grover Tully before dying forty-nine days later (wiki:Elmo_Tully).

## Origins

Son of one of Lord Grover Tully's sons. Born approximately 100 AC (track_b: Born). Father of Kermit Tully and Oscar Tully (track_b: Issue).

## Narrative Arc

At the start of the Dance of the Dragons, Lord Grover Tully wanted to support the greens, but no word of support left Riverrun. Elmo became Lord of Riverrun upon Grover's death shortly after the Second Battle of Tumbleton, and raised a host of six thousand men for the blacks. He died forty-nine days into his lordship after drinking bad water while marching to King's Landing (wiki:Elmo_Tully). Archmaester Gyldayn described him as a reluctant and pragmatic warrior (wiki:Elmo_Tully).
