## Origins

At the [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council) of [101 AC](wiki:101_AC), Lord Ellard supported first Princess [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aemon))(wiki:Ellard_Stark.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and then her son, [Laenor Velaryon](wiki:Laenor_Velaryon).(wiki:Ellard_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) This may have been because [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) was angry with King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) for earlier transferring the [New Gift](wiki:New_Gift) from [northern](wiki:North) control to the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Ellard_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_lords_of_winterfell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen), Rhaenys's cousin, was chosen by the assembled lords as the new heir, however.(wiki:Ellard_Stark.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
