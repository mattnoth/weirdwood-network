## Identity

Ser Stafford Lannister is a knight of House Lannister. He is the good-brother to Lord Tywin Lannister, as his sister Joanna married their cousin Tywin (wiki:Stafford_Lannister).

## Origins

Born between 245–253 AC (track_b: Born). Son of Jason Lannister and Marla Prester (track_b: Father, Mother). Died in 299 AC at Oxcross (track_b: Died).

## Appearances & Description

Ser Jaime Lannister and Ser Brynden Tully are both unimpressed by Stafford's martial capacities, with Jaime considering him blundering (wiki:Stafford_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33, cite_ref-Racok39).

## Narrative Arc

As a young squire, Stafford was captured by Lady Ellyn Tarbeck in retaliation for the imprisonment of her husband by Tywin. Lord Tytos forced a hostage exchange (wiki:Stafford_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33). Stafford negotiated to betroth his son Daven to Desmera Redwyne, though no agreement was reached with Lord Paxter Redwyne (wiki:Stafford_Lannister.cite_ref-Racok32).

After Jaime's capture in the Whispering Wood, Stafford begins raising a new army near Lannisport from inexperienced men. Feeling safe in the westerlands, he does not post sentries. Robb Stark avoids the Golden Tooth via a goat track found by his direwolf and surprises Stafford's host at Oxcross, destroying his force. Stafford is killed in the battle (wiki:Stafford_Lannister.cite_ref-Racok39, cite_ref-Racok55).
