## Origins

Valerion was born in [77 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) after a troubled labor that left his mother, Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), bedridden for half a year. Valerion was small at birth, similar to his brother [Gaemon](wiki:Gaemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)). The sickly babe was attended by half a dozen nurses. However, he died the following year, a fortnight before his first [nameday](wiki:Nameday).(wiki:Valerion_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
