## Identity

Prince Valerion Targaryen was the twelfth-born child of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen and Queen Alysanne Targaryen (wiki:Valerion_Targaryen, cite: Fire & Blood).

## Origins

Born in 77 AC after a troubled labor that left his mother Queen Alysanne bedridden for half a year. Valerion was small at birth, similar to his brother Gaemon (wiki:Valerion_Targaryen).
