## Origins

Robert participated in the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC). Along with his older brother, Ser [Androw](wiki:Androw_Ashford), he was one of [his sister's](wiki:Lord_Ashford's_daughter) champions in the tourney.
