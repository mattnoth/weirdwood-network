## Origins

### Early life

Born at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell),(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rawoiaflyanna_stark.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) Lyanna was the only daughter of Lord [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark) and Lady [Lyarra Stark](wiki:Lyarra_Stark). She had two older brothers, [Brandon](wiki:Brandon_Stark) and [Eddard](wiki:Eddard_Stark), and one younger brother, [Benjen](wiki:Benjen_Stark).(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_stark_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

Eddard was [fostered](wiki:Fostered) at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) by Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) since the age of eight, where he befriended his fellow ward, [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) Eddard brought Robert's suit to Winterfell,(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rawoiaflyanna_stark.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4) and Rickard agreed to betroth his daughter to the young [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End).(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1) However, that night Lyanna confided to Eddard that she believed Robert would never be able to keep to one bed. She had heard that Robert had [an illegitimate daughter](wiki:Mya_Stone) in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn), which Eddard could not deny. He attempted to persuade her that what Robert had done before their [betrothal](wiki:Betrothal) was insignificant and that he would love her, but Lyanna informed him that love could not change a man's nature.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-2)

### Tourney at Harrenhal

Lyanna was present at the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) during the year of the false spring, [281 AC](wiki:281_AC). She happened upon a [crannogman](wiki:Crannogman), [Howland Reed](wiki:Howland_Reed), who was being bullied by three young [squires](wiki:Squire), none older than fifteen. She roared "That's [my father's](wiki:Rickard_Stark) man you're kicking", and attacked them with a tourney sword. The bullies scattered and Lyanna took Howland back to her tent, where she cleaned his wounds and bound them with linen. Then she introduced him to her brothers, [Brandon](wiki:Brandon_Stark), [Eddard](wiki:Eddard_Stark), and [Benjen](wiki:Benjen_Stark). That evening, a feast was held to celebrate the start of the tourney. Lyanna persuaded Howland to attend the feast, as he was highborn and had as much right to attend as anyone. During the feast [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone) and a noted musician, performed a sad and beautiful song that made Lyanna weep. However, when Benjen teased her for crying, she poured [wine](wiki:Wine) over her younger brother's head.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) During the feast, Lyanna recognized the three squires who had bullied Howland and pointed them out to her brothers.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

During the tourney's jousting, a short [mystery knight](wiki:Mystery_knight) known as the [Knight of the Laughing Tree](wiki:Knight_of_the_Laughing_Tree) defeated the knights the three bullies served, and demanded they be chastised as ransom payment.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) Some readers [have suggested Lyanna for the identity](wiki:Knight_of_the_Laughing_Tree/Theories) of the mystery knight. King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) demanded that the mystery knight's identity be revealed and even sent his son Rhaegar in search of him,(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) but nothing was found aside from the knight's shield in a tree.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

Prince Rhaegar won the tournament by defeating Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), but instead of crowning his own wife, Princess [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell), as the [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty), Rhaegar shocked those present by presenting the laurel of blue [winter roses](wiki:Winter_rose) to Lyanna, placing it in her lap with the tip of his lance.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1)(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-2) At that moment "all the smiles died".(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-2) Brandon believed Rhaegar's action to have been a slight upon Lyanna's honor. Some people say that [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), Lyanna's betrothed, laughed at Rhaegar's action, claiming that Rhaegar had only paid Lyanna her due; however, those closer to Robert claim that Robert brooded over the insult, and grew resentful of Rhaegar henceforth.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-3)

### Robert's Rebellion

The next year, Lyanna came face to face with [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) ten leagues from [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal),(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-4) and she was taken by him with the help of Ser [Arthur Dayne](wiki:Arthur_Dayne) and Ser [Oswell Whent](wiki:Oswell_Whent), both of his father's [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rawoiafrhaegar_targaryen.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) When her brother Brandon, on his way to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) for his [wedding](wiki:Wedding), learned about Lyanna's disappearance, he changed course towards [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), accompanied by several companions ([Ethan Glover](wiki:Ethan_Glover), [Jeffory Mallister](wiki:Jeffory_Mallister), [Kyle Royce](wiki:Kyle_Royce) and Ser [Elbert Arryn](wiki:Elbert_Arryn)). At the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), Brandon roared for Rhaegar "to come out and die". Though Rhaegar was not present, King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) had Brandon and his companions arrested and charged them with treason, accusing them of plotting the murder of the crown prince.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

Aerys summoned the captives' fathers to court, supposedly to answer the charge against their sons. Lord [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark) demanded a [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat), and though the king granted him the request, he chose "fire" as his champion. Lord Rickard was suspended from rafters of the [throne room](wiki:Great_Hall_(Red_Keep)) over a blazing fire whilst dressed in his armor, while Brandon, attached to a device which tightened a cord around his neck whenever he moved, was forced to watch as his father was slowly cooked inside his armor. Brandon strangled himself in an attempt to get to a [longsword](wiki:Longsword) placed just out of his reach, to save Rickard. When King Aerys next demanded the heads of [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) and [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) from the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) responded by raising his banners in revolt, starting [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-1)(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_robert.27s_rebellion.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)

Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark).(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) However, Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) is convinced that "Prince Rhaegar loved his Lady Lyanna".(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0) [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) believes that Rhaegar had stolen Lyanna away from her betrothed,(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0) and when she is about to marry, Daenerys thinks to herself that if [her paramour](wiki:Daario_Naharis) loved her, he would "come and carry you off at swordpoint, as Rhaegar carried off his northern girl".(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0) According to [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen), Rhaegar had fought and died for "the woman he loved",(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0) something which his sister Daenerys also believes.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0) Additionally, one of the visions Daenerys sees in the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying) depicts Rhaegar's death, in which he whispers Lyanna's name with his last also believes that Rhaegar had simply not been happy with his marriage to Elia, which had led to him taking Lyanna.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-1) [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), who was infatuated with Rhaegar, has similar beliefs, as she is convinced that Rhaegar would never have looked twice at the "wolf girl" if he had been satisfied in his wife.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Cersei's uncle, Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), believes that, if Aerys had accepted The war, led by Lyanna's fiancé Robert, her brother Eddard, and their foster father Jon, lasted "close to a year".(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0) Lyanna's exact whereabouts during this time are unknown. Rhaegar himself could not be found, either, for a large part of the war,(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rawoiafrhaegar_targaryen.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-2) though he finally returned "from the south"(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0) after King Aerys II had dispatched Ser [Gerold Hightower](wiki:Gerold_Hightower), [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard), to find him.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rawoiafrhaegar_targaryen.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-3) Robert and Eddard faced Rhaegar and the royal army in the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident), in which Robert slew Rhaegar in single combat at the [ruby ford](wiki:Ruby_ford) and the rebels defeated the royal army.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-5) A fortnight later, the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing) officially ended the war,(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_end.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0) with King Aerys slain by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) of the Kingsguard.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-1)

Lyanna still remained missing, however, and the last opposition still had to be dealt with. Robert and Eddard had had a falling out after Robert was accepting of the manner at which Rhaegar's wife, [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell), and two young children, Princes [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Rhaegar)) and Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)), had been slain by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s men during the Sack of King's Landing.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-2) From King's Landing, Eddard rode south to the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) to lift the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End). From there he rode to a place Rhaegar was said to have named the [tower of joy](wiki:Tower_of_joy), located in the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### Death

Eddard brought only six companions with him: [Martyn Cassel](wiki:Martyn_Cassel), [Theo Wull](wiki:Theo_Wull), [Ethan Glover](wiki:Ethan_Glover), Ser [Mark Ryswell](wiki:Mark_Ryswell), [Howland Reed](wiki:Howland_Reed) and Lord [Willam Dustin](wiki:Willam_Dustin). At the [tower of joy](wiki:Tower_of_joy), they found Ser [Arthur Dayne](wiki:Arthur_Dayne), Ser [Oswell Whent](wiki:Oswell_Whent), and Ser [Gerold Hightower](wiki:Gerold_Hightower), the three remaining loyalist [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) of the late [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen)'s [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). The men [met in battle](wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy), with only Eddard and Howland surviving.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Inside the tower, Eddard found Lyanna in a "bed of blood"(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rawoiaflyanna_stark.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5) within a room smelling of "blood and roses".(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-6) A fever had taken her strength, and her voice had been faint as a whisper. There was fear in Lyanna's eyes, while she called for "Ned" to promise her something. When her brother gave his word, the fear left her. She smiled, and her fingers clutched his own in a tight grip.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-7) Lyanna died shortly thereafter.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-3) Eddard was still holding her as she "gave up her hold on life" and rose petals slipped from her palm, dead and black. Later, Howland found Eddard still holding the corpse. The [crannogman](wiki:Crannogman) separated the hands of the living man and the dead girl, though Eddard had no recollection of what happened after Lyanna died.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-8)

It is unknown what promise Lyanna extracted from Eddard, and what had caused her fear. Lyanna's last words, "Promise me, Ned", would haunt Eddard, who had to pay a price in order to keep his promises to her,(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-4) for the rest of his life.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0)(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-3)

At the time of her death, Lyanna had been sixteen years old.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-9) Though Eddard buried the bodies of his deceased companions and the three slain Kingsguard [knights](wiki:Knight) in cairns he created out of the stones from the tower,(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) he transported Lyanna's bones from [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) back to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-2) According to Eddard, Lyanna had wanted to be buried beside her father and brother.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-10) Her tomb lies in the [crypts of Winterfell](wiki:Crypts_of_Winterfell), beside that of Lord [Rickard](wiki:Rickard_Stark) and [Brandon](wiki:Brandon_Stark).(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-1) While only the [kings](wiki:King_in_the_North) and [lords of Winterfell](wiki:Lords_of_Winterfell) traditionally have statues made in their likeness, not their family members, both Lyanna and Brandon were given a statue by Eddard because he wished to honor his beloved siblings.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-2)

### Legacy

Lyanna's death and the grief they shared over her passing eventually reconciled [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) and the new monarch, King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). Though Robert had no desire to marry after Lyanna's death, he was eventually convinced by Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) that [the realm](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) needed an heir. As such, Robert was wed to [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister).(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1) A drunken Robert called Cersei "Lyanna" while in bed on [their wedding night](wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister),(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0) however, which began strains in the new marriage. Robert remained in love with Lyanna,(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) and he named the beautiful war galley *[Lady Lyanna](wiki:Lady_Lyanna)* of the [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet) in her honor.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0)

At [Bear Island](wiki:Bear_Island), Lady [Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont) named her youngest daughter [Lyanna](wiki:Lyanna_Mormont), after Lyanna Stark, which according to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) was an attempt to win the favor of Lord Eddard Stark.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0)

Eddard brings flowers to Lyanna's tomb whenever possible, as she had been fond of flowers.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-11)

## Appearances & Description

Lyanna was slim of frame.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), with her brown hair, long face and grey eyes,(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) resembles Lyanna in appearance, according to [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark).(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), also brown of hair,(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) is believed by some to resemble Lyanna, though Eddard disagrees.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

Though Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel) describes Lyanna as "a wild and boyish young thing with none of the Princess [Elia's](wiki:Elia_Martell) delicate beauty",(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) recalls Lyanna having a "wild beauty" about her.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) According to her brother Eddard, Lyanna had been a child-woman of surpassing loveliness.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

According to Eddard Lyanna had a touch of wildness to her, which was described as "the [wolf blood](wiki:Wolf_blood)" by her father, Lord [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark). Eddard compares Lyanna to his tomboyish daughter Arya in personality, adding that Lyanna probably would have carried a [sword](wiki:Sword) if their father had allowed it.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) Lyanna was strong of will.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), who had been deeply infatuated with Lyanna, remains in love with her memory over fifteen years after her death to the point of causing major strain in his marriage to Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister);(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Robert believes Lyanna would never have publicly shamed him the way Cersei does.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Lyanna seems to have been less enthusiastic about their betrothal than Robert was, as she commented to Ned that Robert's love for her would not prevent him from being unfaithful after their marriage.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

Lyanna is noted to have been a skilled rider who loved to ride.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) According to [Harwin](wiki:Harwin) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), Lyanna rode "like a [northman](wiki:Northman)",(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1) while Lady [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin) describes her as a centaur(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1) and Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) states that Lyanna was "half a [horse](wiki:Horse) herself".(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) According to a semi-canon source, Lyanna also practiced at tilting at rings.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rawoiaflyanna_stark.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

Fond of flowers,(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3) Lyanna loved the scent of [winter roses](wiki:Winter_rose).(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Upon arriving at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) to ask Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) to become the new [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) after the death of Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), insists that his childhood friend bring him to the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell) so he can pay his respects to Lyanna at her tomb. The king feels the stonemason failed to capture her beauty.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-12)

Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) tells her brother, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), that Robert is still in live with Lyanna. Cersei worries that Robert will put her aside.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-2)

When Eddard's daughter [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) pleads for the life of her [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), [Lady](wiki:Lady), at [Darry](wiki:Darry), it reminds him of Lyanna's pleading.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0)

Lord [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) plots to have Robert replace Cersei with his own sister, [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), so he shows Eddard a locket with a painted miniature of Margaery. Renly hopes that Margaery resembles Lyanna, but Eddard disagrees.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2)

Under the influence of [milk of the poppy](wiki:Milk_of_the_poppy) after being wounded in the streets of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Eddard has a fever dream about the [combat at the tower of joy](wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy). While Eddard and his six companions battle three [knights](wiki:Knight) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), Lyanna is heard screaming his name inside the tower.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

Eddard has a nightmare involving Lyanna and the statue of her placed at her tomb. In the dream, Eddard walks through the crypts of Winterfell. He can hear the statue of Lyanna whispering: "Promise me, Ned". He notices the statue wearing a garland of [pale blue roses](wiki:Winter_rose), while her eyes weep blood. Eddard immediately wakes with his heart racing.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-1) As Robert dies after being mortally wounded by a [boar](wiki:Boar), he tells Ned he will give Lyanna his love.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-2) King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) later has Eddard executed for treason.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Having proclaimed himself [Prince of Winterfell](wiki:Prince_of_Winterfell) after [capturing the castle](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell), [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) has a nightmare involving a feast of the dead at Winterfell. Many of the dead men and women attending are people that Theon knew personally or were slain by his own hand or command. He also notices faces that he had never known in life. Among them is Lyanna: a slim, sad girl wearing a crown of pale blue roses and a white gown splattered with blood. Her older brother [Brandon](wiki:Brandon_Stark) stands beside her, their father [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark) just behind them.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

### A Storm of Swords

[Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed) and [Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed) tell [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) about the [tourney of Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_of_Harrenhal) in which a she-wolf defended [a crannogman](wiki:Howland_Reed).(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) and [Arstan Whitebeard](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) discuss the tourney at Harrenhal in which Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) named Lyanna the [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty).(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-2)

### A Dance with Dragons

Within the [cave of the three-eyed crow](wiki:Cave_of_the_three-eyed_crow) found [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) witnesses a vision from the past in which he sees a boy and a girl playing with wooden branches in the [godswood of Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-0) Some readers believe this to have been [Benjen](wiki:Benjen_Stark) and his older sister, Lyanna, as Bran describes the girl as being the eldest of the two, and states that she looks like his sister [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark),(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-1) who is said to look like Lyanna had.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2) Additionally, the girl refers to [Old Nan](wiki:Old_Nan),(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-1) placing the event Bran witnesses as having taken place in more recent generations.

Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) regrets having been defeated by Rhaegar at Harrenhal, thinking he would have named [Ashara Dayne](wiki:Ashara_Dayne) as queen of love and beauty instead and [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) would have been avoided.(wiki:Lyanna_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-1)
