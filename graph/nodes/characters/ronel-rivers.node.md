## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ronel is nearby when [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) informs her son, [Robb](wiki:Robb_Stark), of the alliance negotiated with Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) at the [Twins](wiki:Twins).(wiki:Ronel_Rivers.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Ronel is a captive of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), together with his half-brothers, Ser [Danwell](wiki:Danwell_Frey), Ser [Hosteen](wiki:Hosteen_Frey), and Ser [Jared Frey](wiki:Jared_Frey).(wiki:Ronel_Rivers.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Tywin holds captives from the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork) at the [Tower of Dread](wiki:Tower_of_Dread) in [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Ronel_Rivers.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) observes the four [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) exercising in the [Flowstone Yard](wiki:Flowstone_Yard) of Harrenhal and then being ransomed by their kin.(wiki:Ronel_Rivers.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Ronel is among the Freys with Ser [Aenys Frey](wiki:Aenys_Frey) under the command of Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) at Harrenhal. Ronel regrets that [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) was defeated by Tywin in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Ronel_Rivers.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Ronel_Rivers.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
