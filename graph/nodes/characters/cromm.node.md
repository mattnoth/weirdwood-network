## Identity

Cromm is an ironborn warrior and one of Asha Greyjoy's crew on the Black Wind (wiki:Cromm).

## Narrative Arc

Cromm sails with Asha to Deepwood Motte after the kingsmoot. During the fight by Deepwood Motte against Stannis Baratheon's forces, Cromm is wounded and later dies of his injuries (wiki:Cromm.cite_ref-Radwd26). He is mentioned among those lost in the battle (wiki:Cromm.cite_ref-Radwd62).
