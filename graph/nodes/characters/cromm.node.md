## Appearances & Description

Cromm loves fighting even more than women.(wiki:Cromm.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) He wields an axe and follows the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God).(wiki:Cromm.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Cromm sails to [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) with [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) as she contemplates her next move after the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk). He stands watch at the castle with [Hagen the Horn](wiki:Hagen_the_Horn).(wiki:Cromm.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Cromm flees with Asha toward the [Bay of Ice](wiki:Bay_of_Ice), but is wounded in the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte). Cromm is taken captive by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) along with several other ironborn, whom he calls the [ragged nine](wiki:Ragged_nine).(wiki:Cromm.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Cromm dies of his wounds in captivity at Deepwood.(wiki:Cromm.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
