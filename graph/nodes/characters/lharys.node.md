## Appearances & Description

Lharys is an ungainly man, with wild tufts of rust-colored hair that stick out of a conical cap.(wiki:Lharys.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Along with [Mohor](wiki:Mohor) and [Kurleket](wiki:Kurleket), Lharys is one of three Bracken men-at-arms at the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn) when Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) takes [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) as a prisoner. He is among those who escort her into the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon).(wiki:Lharys.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) He is the only Bracken man-at-arms to survive the first attack from the [Vale mountain clans](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans). Lharys claims [Jyck](wiki:Jyck)'s [horse](wiki:Horse) as his own in the aftermath. He is slain in the party's second clash with clansmen.(wiki:Lharys.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
