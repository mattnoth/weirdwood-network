## Identity

Orys Baratheon, also known as Orys One-Hand and the King's Stump, was the founder of House Baratheon and the first Lord of Storm's End after House Durrandon. He is considered the first Hand of the King, serving Aegon the Conqueror (wiki:Orys_Baratheon).

## Origins

Born around 20 BC. Rumored bastard half-brother of Aegon I Targaryen by their father Aerion, Lord of Dragonstone. According to Jaehaerys I Targaryen, it was well-known that Aerion was Orys's father (track_b: Born, Father). `PARENT_OF` (reverse): Aerion Targaryen — rumored (Tier 2 — wiki states "rumored" and "never proven") (track_b: Father).

## Appearances & Description

Orys had black hair, black eyes, and a black beard. He was a fierce warrior known for his prowess in battle and strong loyalty to Aegon I. After losing his hand in the First Dornish War, he became crabbed and bitter, obsessed with revenge on Dorne (wiki:Orys_Baratheon).

## Narrative Arc

Orys was one of Aegon's fiercest commanders and regarded as his only true friend. When King Argilac Durrandon offered Aegon his daughter Argella in marriage, Aegon instead offered Orys — which Argilac rejected, calling Orys a bastard. During Aegon's Conquest, Orys commanded ground forces in the first battle and was raised to Hand of the King after Aegon's coronation. Orys and Queen Rhaenys with Meraxes were tasked with taking Storm's End. He slew Argilac in single combat, married Argella Durrandon, and adopted the Durrandon sigil, words, and seat. During the First Dornish War, Orys was captured and lost his sword hand. He resigned as Hand and became bitter. In 37 AC during the Vulture Hunt, Orys's forces crushed the Vulture King's host at Stonehelm. He avenged the loss of his hand by cutting off the hands and feet of Lord Walter Wyl. Orys died on the march home to Storm's End from wounds taken in battle (wiki:Orys_Baratheon).
