## Appearances & Description

Around thirty years old, Rhaegar is round shouldered and kettle-bellied. He has a silky beard and a sardonic smile.(wiki:Rhaegar_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He is [named after](wiki:Named_after) [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone).(wiki:Rhaegar_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Rhaegar's soft grey lambskin doublet is trimmed with cloth-of-silver. His cloak, cloth-of-silver and lined with vair, is clasped with a brooch depicting the [Twins](wiki:Twins).(wiki:Rhaegar_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Rhaegar's wife, [Jeyne Beesbury](wiki:Jeyne_Beesbury), has died of a wasting [illness](wiki:Illness) making him a widower.(wiki:Rhaegar_Frey.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Along with Ser [Jared Frey](wiki:Jared_Frey) and [Symond Frey](wiki:Symond_Frey), Rhaegar travels on the *[Lionstar](wiki:Lionstar)* to [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) via [Sisterton](wiki:Sisterton) to return the bones of Ser [Wendel Manderly](wiki:Wendel_Manderly), who was killed in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), to his father, Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly).(wiki:Rhaegar_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) The Freys also want to ensure White Harbor bends the knee to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Rhaegar_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

The widower Rhaegar is betrothed to Lord Wyman's granddaughter, [Wynafryd](wiki:Wynafryd_Manderly). They also offer Wyman a Frey bride and the betrothal of [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)) to Wyman's other granddaughter, [Wylla Manderly](wiki:Wylla_Manderly).(wiki:Rhaegar_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

When Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) arrives in White Harbor to persuade [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) to support [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), the three Freys are already present. The Freys insult Davos and the memory of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), the [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North). Rhaegar explains that Robb abandoned the [north](wiki:North) and the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), and that all must support King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Rhaegar_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

The angered Wyman pretends to agree with Rhaegar, as the Iron Throne holds hostage his surviving son and heir, Ser [Wylis Manderly](wiki:Wylis_Manderly), at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal). Wyman fakes Davos's death by having a criminal with similar features executed and his head dipped in tar so the Freys falsely believe him to be Davos.(wiki:Rhaegar_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) As a reward for proving his loyalty, Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) has Wylis returned to White Harbor via [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool).(wiki:Rhaegar_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Wyman gives Rhaegar, Jared, and Symond palfreys as guest gifts(wiki:Rhaegar_Frey.cite_ref-10) when the three Freys depart White Harbor for [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark) of "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)" to [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow).(wiki:Rhaegar_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) They disappear while traveling to [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton), however, despite the search efforts of Ramsay.(wiki:Rhaegar_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

At [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) after Ramsay's wedding, Wyman presents [three huge pies](wiki:Frey_Pies/Theories) at the wedding feast while requesting a song from the singer [Abel](wiki:Mance_Rayder) about the [Rat Cook](wiki:Rat_Cook).(wiki:Rhaegar_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Ser [Aenys Frey](wiki:Aenys_Frey), Rhaegar's father, and Ser [Hosteen Frey](wiki:Hosteen_Frey) suspect Wyman of foul play.(wiki:Rhaegar_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
