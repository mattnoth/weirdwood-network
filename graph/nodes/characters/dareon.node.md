## Origins

While an apprentice singer in the [Reach](wiki:Reach), Dareon was caught in bed with [a daughter](wiki:Mathis_Rowan's_daughter) of Lord [Mathis Rowan](wiki:Mathis_Rowan) of [Goldengrove](wiki:Goldengrove). Though Dareon maintains that the girl was waiting for him naked and helped him into her room, under her father's eye she named it rape. Dareon was sent to the [Wall](wiki:Wall),(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) though he maintains his innocence.(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)

## Appearances & Description

A handsome(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) young man, Dareon has hazel eyes(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and fair hair(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) the color of sand and [honey](wiki:Honey).(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He has a warm smile.(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

The singer is a lothario, and [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) thinks Dareon has "a loose tongue and quick temper".(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) He is a better singer than a fighter.(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Dareon is an aspiring songwriter who plays the [fiddle](wiki:Fiddle) and [woodharp](wiki:Harp).(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) His voice is "honey poured over thunder", according to Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)).(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Dareon knows numerous songs, including "[The Day They Hanged Black Robin](wiki:The_Day_They_Hanged_Black_Robin)", "[The Mermaid's Lament](wiki:The_Mermaid's_Lament)", "[Autumn of My Day](wiki:Autumn_of_My_Day)", "[Iron Lances](wiki:Iron_Lances)", "[Seven Swords for Seven Sons](wiki:Seven_Swords_for_Seven_Sons)," "[Milady's Supper](wiki:Milady's_Supper)", "[Her Little Flower](wiki:Her_Little_Flower)", "[Meggett Was a Merry Maid, a Merry Maid Was She](wiki:Meggett_Was_a_Merry_Maid,_a_Merry_Maid_Was_She)", "[The Bear and the Maiden Fair](wiki:The_Bear_and_the_Maiden_Fair)", and "[A Thousand Eyes, and One](wiki:A_Thousand_Eyes,_and_One)".(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) He is also familiar with songs about [Skagos](wiki:Skagos).(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Dareon is in the [same group of recruits](wiki:Jon_Snow's_Night's_Watch_recruitment_group) as [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) when he comes to the [Wall](wiki:Wall),(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) and he sings sometimes at dinner.(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) According to Jon, Dareon is "quick, but afraid of being hit" during their training.(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

Dareon is raised to a man of the Watch at the same time as Jon, with both named to the [stewards](wiki:Steward). [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), sends Dareon to [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea), hoping the singer can aid [Cotter Pyke](wiki:Cotter_Pyke) when dealing with merchant ships.(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

### A Clash of Kings

Dareon serves as a steward and singer at Eastwatch.(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Jon Snow, after being chosen Lord Commander, names Dareon as a replacement for [Yoren](wiki:Yoren), a missing [wandering crow](wiki:Wandering_crow).(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7) He sends Dareon south with [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), the elderly Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)), and [Gilly](wiki:Gilly), with the idea that his singing could bolster recruitment in southern [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) for the Night's Watch.(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

While sailing from Eastwatch to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), Dareon sings numerous songs to the oarsmen of *[Blackbird](wiki:Blackbird)*, and they join him in singing "[The Bear and the Maiden Fair](wiki:The_Bear_and_the_Maiden_Fair)".(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-8) The singer dislikes the [wildling](wiki:Wildling) Gilly and [the baby with her](wiki:Aemon_Steelsong),(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-9) and he is often drunk on [firewine](wiki:Firewine).(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-10)

Aemon's condition worsens in Braavos, and they are forced to spend their coin on a healer and an inn. With their money running low, Dareon is sent to sing for coin, with which to buy food and passage. Instead, he becomes increasingly distant and desultory, and sings largely for wine and trade from the city's whores. He prefers to perform at the [Inn of the Green Eel](wiki:Inn_of_the_Green_Eel), the [Black Bargeman](wiki:Black_Bargeman), and [Moroggo's](wiki:Moroggo's), establishments along [Ragman's Harbor](wiki:Ragman's_Harbor) where the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue) is more likely to be spoken. He also sings at the [Moon Pool](wiki:Moon_Pool), where the [Moonshadow](wiki:Moonshadow) gives him a kiss on one occasion.(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

Sam finds Dareon in a whorehouse called the [Happy Port](wiki:Happy_Port); Dareon has wed a whore called the [Sailor's Wife](wiki:Sailor's_Wife), who only beds the men she weds. Sam confronts him for this and accuses him of breaking his vows, and Dareon responds by throwing his cloak in Sam's face and renouncing the Night's Watch. Sam begins pummeling Dareon,(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4) so [Meralyn](wiki:Meralyn) has Sam tossed out of the brothel into the canal.(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5) Sam, Gilly, and Aemon find passage on *[Cinnamon Wind](wiki:Cinnamon_Wind)* and leave Braavos without the singer.(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

After his desertion, Dareon is successful for a time as a singer in Braavos, visiting the [Cattery](wiki:Cattery), the Happy Port, and the [House of Seven Lamps](wiki:House_of_Seven_Lamps). The whores call him the black singer, though he begins wearing fancy clothing with only his boots remaining black.(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) He sleeps at the Happy Port, where [Lanna](wiki:Lanna) often asks him to sing love songs.(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Dareon is recognized as a deserter of the Night's Watch by [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), who, under her guise of "Cat of the Canals", sells oysters for the Braavosi fishmonger [Brusco](wiki:Brusco) in the same areas where Dareon sings. Arya accompanies Dareon when he departs the Happy Port one night, and he brags he will one day perform in the [Sealord's Palace](wiki:Sealord's_Palace). The singer also admits he remained behind when Sam left Braavos. Arya lures Dareon into an alley and slits his throat as punishment for his desertion. She dumps his body into a canal, though she keeps his boots.(wiki:Dareon.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
