Qalen died between 289–299 AC, before Theon Greyjoy's return to the Iron Islands — by the time Theon arrives at Pyke, Maester Wendamyr has replaced him (wiki:Qalen.cite_ref-Racok11, wiki:Qalen.cite_ref-Raffc11).
