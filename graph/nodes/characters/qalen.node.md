## Origins

After [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion), [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley) and four other boys were brought to [Pyke](wiki:Pyke) to be fostered by Lady [Alannys Harlaw](wiki:Alannys_Harlaw). After Maester Qalen found Tristifer inappropriately touching [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), the daughter of Lord [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), the boy was promptly shipped off to [Blacktyde](wiki:Blacktyde).(wiki:Qalen.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Qalen died before the return of [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) to Pyke, and his duties were taken by Maester [Wendamyr](wiki:Wendamyr). [Helya](wiki:Helya) tells Theon that Qalen now sleeps in [the sea](wiki:Sunset_sea).(wiki:Qalen.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
