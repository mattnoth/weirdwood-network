## Origins

Richard wanted to join the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) when he was a squire, but King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) chose another [knight](wiki:Knight) after Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) objected to him.(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

## Appearances & Description

Ser Richard is lean,(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-TWOW_2-1) dark-haired,(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-TWOW_2-2) and hard-eyed.(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-TWOW_2-3) His ravaged face(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) is marred by pockmarks and old scars;(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-TWOW_2-4) according to a semi-canon source this is because of the pox.(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Rawoiafrichard_horpe.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Richard enjoys battle and killing.(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) He is one of the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) and is high in the councils of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon),(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) who considers the knight to be ambitious.(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Richard's quilted doublet and faded surcoat display the three death's-head moths of [House Horpe](wiki:House_Horpe).(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-TWOW_2-5) He armors himself in plate and mail.(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

When the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater) turns against [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), Richard and Ser [Justin Massey](wiki:Justin_Massey) convince Stannis to retreat back to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-TWOW_2-6)

### A Storm of Swords

During the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), the wildling [Dormund](wiki:Dormund) is killed by a [knight](wiki:Knight) with moths on his shield,(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) possibly Richard.

### A Feast for Crows

One of the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men), Richard is part of Stannis's court at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Still among Stannis's knights and [sworn swords](wiki:Sworn_sword),(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Richard sits high in his councils.(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Richard and Ser [Justin Massey](wiki:Justin_Massey) are sent south from Castle Black, their departure noted by [Dywen](wiki:Dywen). [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), deduces the so-called wrong-way rangers are acting as envoys for Stannis.(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

Stannis believes that Justin and Rich both aspire to be named [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell) for their service, but the king plans to give the title to [Arnolf Karstark](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark).(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8) During Stannis's war council, Richard advocates for attacking the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort).(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9) Jon dissuades Stannis, however, and the king instead marches to court the [northern mountain clans](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans).(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Stannis is victorious in the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte),(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) and Richard serves as his second-in-command during the [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell).(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Richard incorrectly believes the snowstorm which slows them down will break soon. He later concludes that the weakest in Stannis's host are beyond saving. The army stops at a [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village) in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood), so Richard tasks his men with fishing in two nearby lakes.(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

When [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) prophesies a girl coming to the [Wall](wiki:Wall), Jon Snow guesses she is [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) and fears that Stannis would want to marry her to Richard, Justin, or Ser [Godry Farring](wiki:Godry_Farring).(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) The girl turns out to be [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark), however, and Jon arranges [her marriage](wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark) to [Sigorn](wiki:Sigorn) of the [Thenns](wiki:Thenns).(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Richard is present when four [Peasebury](wiki:House_Peasebury) men are [burned](wiki:Blood_sacrifice) at the village for [cannibalism](wiki:Cannibalism). With Stannis's men slowed by the blizzard, Richard insists they must press on to attack Winterfell. He questions Justin's courage and dedication to [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor).(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

### The Winds of Winter

Richard is present, along with Ser [Godry Farring](wiki:Godry_Farring) and [Clayton Suggs](wiki:Clayton_Suggs), when Stannis interrogates Maester [Tybald](wiki:Tybald) about his loyalties. Richard is then put in charge of disarming [Arnolf Karstark](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark)'s sleeping men, while Stannis breaks his fast with Arnolf, his son [Arthor](wiki:Arthor_Karstark), and grandsons. Richard later reports that most of the Karstarks yielded, although a few were killed after resisting.(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-TWOW_2-7)

Richard and other queen's men want Stannis to sacrifice [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) to the [Lord of Light](wiki:Lord_of_Light), although Stannis is skeptical of Richard's sincerity.(wiki:Richard_Horpe.cite_ref-TWOW_2-8)
