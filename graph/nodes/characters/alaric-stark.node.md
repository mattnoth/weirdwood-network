## Identity

Alaric Stark was the Lord of Winterfell and head of House Stark during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen (wiki:Alaric_Stark).

## Origins

Son of Lord Brandon Stark. Alaric's older brother Walton preceded him as Lord of Winterfell (track_b: Father). Alaric attended the Golden Wedding in King's Landing in 49 AC with his father and brother (wiki:Alaric_Stark).

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Alaric was notoriously prickly with a flinty reputation — hard, stern, unforgiving, tight-fisted, humorless, joyless, and cold. He was respected by the northmen but not loved, except by the garrison at Winterfell. Queen Alysanne Targaryen came to know him as having a taciturn humor with an edge to it, and as not an ill host despite being careful with coin (wiki:Alaric_Stark).

## Narrative Arc

Walton was slain beyond the Wall by giants in 50 AC while pursuing Night's Watch rebels. Alaric became Lord of Winterfell and blamed King Jaehaerys I for his brother's death, as the rebels had been spared by the king. Alaric wed a Mormont from Bear Island; she died in 55 AC. He received Queen Alysanne during the royal progress to Winterfell in 58 AC, asking her not to bring her dragon Silverwing within the castle walls. He warmed to the queen, took her hunting, and showed her a giant's bones. Alysanne charmed Alaric into granting the New Gift to the Night's Watch. Alaric died in 72 AC (wiki:Alaric_Stark).
