## Appearances & Description

Silver Denys claimed to be descended from a [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son of King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Silver_Denys.cite_ref-3)
While none denied that Silver Denys had [Valyrian](wiki:Valyrian) blood due to his silver hair and [purple eyes](wiki:Purple_eyes),(wiki:Silver_Denys.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) his claim of being a descendant of Maegor is called into doubt as the king was infamously unable to produce a living heir, despite marrying multiple fertile women.(wiki:Silver_Denys.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) It is possible that Denys's bloodline come from a different Valyrian.

## Origins

When Prince [Jacaerys Velaryon](wiki:Jacaerys_Velaryon) [called](wiki:Sowing) for dragonriders, Denys had his arm ripped off in his attempt to mount [Sheepstealer](wiki:Sheepstealer). While his sons were trying to staunch the wound, [the Cannibal](wiki:Cannibal_(dragon)) descended on them and devoured Denys along with his sons.(wiki:Silver_Denys.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
