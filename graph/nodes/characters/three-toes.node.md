## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Three Toes is one of the many Brave Companions who remain behind at Harrenhal after several failed foraging runs. He is present when [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) is thrown into the bear pit and raises his sword against [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) when he comes back to save her, but [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat) orders him to stand down.(wiki:Three_Toes.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
