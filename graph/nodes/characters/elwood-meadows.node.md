## Appearances & Description

In [299 AC](wiki:299_AC) Elwood is a green boy of twenty.(wiki:Elwood_Meadows.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Lord Elwood is Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose)'s second-in-command and his lieutenant at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) during the [castle's siege](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)) by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon). Members of [House Fossoway](wiki:House_Fossoway) under Stannis's command believe that should Cortnay die, Elwood would surrender the castle without a fight.(wiki:Elwood_Meadows.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) After Cortnay's death, Elwood promptly surrenders Storm's End and is named the new [castellan](wiki:Castellan) in return.(wiki:Elwood_Meadows.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Stannis names Ser [Gilbert Farring](wiki:Gilbert_Farring) as the new castellan of Storm's End and places Elwood as his second-in-command.(wiki:Elwood_Meadows.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Lord Meadows remains loyal to Stannis as the seneschal of Storm's End.(wiki:Elwood_Meadows.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
