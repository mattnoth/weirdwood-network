## Identity

Aegon Frey (son of Stevron) (249–299 AC) — child of Stevron Frey and Jeyne Lydden; sworn to House Frey. One of 2 Aegon Freys — see the Aegon Frey disambiguation entry.

## Origins

Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) claims to have hosted three [kings](wiki:King) at the [Twins](wiki:Twins),(wiki:Aegon_Frey_(son_of_Stevron).cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) but Jinglebell was not introduced to them.(wiki:Aegon_Frey_(son_of_Stevron).cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

## Appearances & Description

Aegon is about fifty, thin and stooped. He has long grey hair. He strongly resembles his grandfather, Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey), but has larger and friendlier eyes.(wiki:Aegon_Frey_(son_of_Stevron).cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Aegon is a simple and harmless [lackwit](wiki:Lackwit) with a vacant expression.(wiki:Aegon_Frey_(son_of_Stevron).cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Aegon wears fine blue wool and grey satin, as well as a crown and collar decorated with small [brass](wiki:Brass) bells.(wiki:Aegon_Frey_(son_of_Stevron).cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Jinglebell is not in attendance when Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) negotiates with his grandfather, Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey), at the [Twins](wiki:Twins).(wiki:Aegon_Frey_(son_of_Stevron).cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)(wiki:Aegon_Frey_(son_of_Stevron).cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)

### A Clash of Kings

Ser [Stevron Frey](wiki:Stevron_Frey), Aegon's father and the heir of Lord Walder, dies after the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross).(wiki:Aegon_Frey_(son_of_Stevron).cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Big Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Jammos)) and [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)) mention Aegon and his sons while discussing the new line of succession,(wiki:Aegon_Frey_(son_of_Stevron).cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) but Jinglebell is not known to have any offspring.(wiki:Aegon_Frey_(son_of_Stevron).cite_ref-8)

### A Storm of Swords

When [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident), comes to the Twins to apologize for breaking his [marriage](wiki:Marriage) vows, Lord Walder Frey has Jinglebell present when he receives Robb. The king's mother, Catelyn Stark, interprets this as an intentionally insulting breach of courtesy. Regardless, Robb compliments the bravery of Aegon's late father, Ser Stevron. When Walder introduces the women of the Twins to Robb, Lady [Joyeuse Erenford](wiki:Joyeuse_Erenford) restrains Aegon from rising as well.(wiki:Aegon_Frey_(son_of_Stevron).cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5)

During the wedding feast of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) and [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey), Aegon prances to "[Alysanne](wiki:Alysanne_(song))", and he covers his ears when the [Greatjon](wiki:Jon_Umber) sings "[The Bear and the Maiden Fair](wiki:The_Bear_and_the_Maiden_Fair)" while musicians play "[Flowers of Spring](wiki:Flowers_of_Spring)".(wiki:Aegon_Frey_(son_of_Stevron).cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) He sucks [wine](wiki:Wine) from his fingers, hops about during the [bedding ceremony](wiki:Bedding), and steals [food](wiki:Food) from the plates of guests.(wiki:Aegon_Frey_(son_of_Stevron).cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

After the killing begins in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Aegon hides under a table. Catelyn seizes Jinglebell, however, and threatens to kill him if Walder does not release Robb, who has been struck by arrows. Walder replies that he would readily trade a half-wit grandson for a son. After Robb is stabbed by Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), Catelyn saws through Aegon's throat with a knife, killing him.(wiki:Aegon_Frey_(son_of_Stevron).cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) Ser [Raymund Frey](wiki:Raymund_Frey) then slits Catelyn's throat.(wiki:Aegon_Frey_(son_of_Stevron).cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Before being hanged by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey) uses the murder of Aegon as a justification for the Red Wedding.(wiki:Aegon_Frey_(son_of_Stevron).cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser [Jared Frey](wiki:Jared_Frey) claims to the [Merman's Court](wiki:Merman's_Court) that Robb was a [warg](wiki:Warg) who turned into a [wolf](wiki:Wolf) and tore out Jinglebell's throat.(wiki:Aegon_Frey_(son_of_Stevron).cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
