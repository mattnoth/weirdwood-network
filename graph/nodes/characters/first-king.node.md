## Origins

It was under the First King's leadership that the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) traveled across the [Arm of Dorne](wiki:Arm_of_Dorne) from [Essos](wiki:Essos) to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:First_King.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) In contrast to the [northern](wiki:North) legend of the First King, some legends from the [Reach](wiki:Reach) claim that [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand) was the [High King of the First Men](wiki:High_King_of_the_First_Men) who led his people to Westeros.(wiki:First_King.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

According to folklore the [Great Barrow](wiki:Great_Barrow) in [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) contains the grave of the First King or a [King of the Giants](wiki:King_of_the_Giants).(wiki:First_King.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) *[Passages of the Dead](wiki:Passages_of_the_Dead)* by Maester [Kennet](wiki:Kennet_(maester)) mentions a curse placed on the Great Barrow which prevents any living man from rivaling the First King.(wiki:First_King.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The monarch was succeeded by the [Barrow Kings](wiki:Barrow_Kings).(wiki:First_King.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

[House Dustin](wiki:House_Dustin), who rule the [barrowlands](wiki:Barrowlands) from Barrowton on behalf of [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark), claim descent from the First King and the Barrow Kings of old.(wiki:First_King.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Lord [Dustin](wiki:House_Dustin) showed the barrow of the First King to King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) in [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:First_King.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
