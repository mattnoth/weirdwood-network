## Identity

Lady Lanna Lannister is a noblewoman of House Lannister, a distant cousin of the main branch — a first cousin twice removed of Tywin Lannister (wiki:Lanna_Lannister). She is the daughter of Ser Damion Lannister and Shiera Crakehall, and the sister of Ser Lucion Lannister.

## Origins

Born in or after 269 AC (wiki:Lanna_Lannister.cite_ref). Father: Damion Lannister (track_b: Father). Mother: Shiera Crakehall (track_b: Mother).

## Narrative Arc

Lanna married Lord Antario Jast, head of House Jast. It is unconfirmed whether Antario's sons are from Lanna or an earlier wife (wiki:Lanna_Lannister).
