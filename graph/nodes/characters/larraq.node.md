## Appearances & Description

Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) considers Larraq to be a fierce fighter. Larraq is skilled with a whip, which he snaps around the legs of his opponents to yank them off their feet, and trident, but Ser Barristan believes it will be years before he masters the sword and lance.(wiki:Larraq.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Larraq is being trained by Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) at the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) to become a knight. Barristan does not approve of Larraq's fighting style, but recognizes his skill and considers him one of the best two of his students.(wiki:Larraq.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### The Winds of Winter

Larraq is one of three squires who is to ride beside Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) in the [Battle of Slaver's Bay](wiki:Battle_of_Slaver's_Bay). Larraq carries the standard of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). He later alerts Selmy of the fire signal upon Meereen's [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid).(wiki:Larraq.cite_ref-3)
