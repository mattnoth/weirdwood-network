## Origins

After being trained by Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey)'s sons with other peasants, Dake went off to fight for [House Osgrey](wiki:House_Osgrey) during the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion). Eustace considered him to be an expert forager, and Dake was able enough to provide for the whole group as they marched. Dake was one of the only three surviving men that returned with Ser Eustace, along with [Pate](wiki:Pate_(Standfast)) and [Lem](wiki:Lem_(Standfast)).(wiki:Dake.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Years later, Dake was accused of stealing sheep by members of [House Webber](wiki:House_Webber), whose lands bordered House Osgrey's. Lady [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber) sent her [knights](wiki:Knight) across the [Chequy Water](wiki:Chequy_Water) to seize Dake and bring him to [Coldmoat](wiki:Coldmoat) for punishment. When Eustace Osgrey arrived at Coldmoat to demand his release, he learned that Dake had been sewn into a sack with rocks, which was sunk in the moat of Coldmoat.(wiki:Dake.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
