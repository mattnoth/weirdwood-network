## Origins

Once a serjeant in the [City Watch of King's Landing](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing), Blood lost his gold cloak for beating a [whore](wiki:Whore) to death whilst in a drunken rage. He spent time in [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom).(wiki:Blood_(butcher).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
After Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) killed Prince [Lucerys Velaryon](wiki:Lucerys_Velaryon) at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) wanted vengeance against the [greens](wiki:Greens). Blood and the ratcatcher called [Cheese](wiki:Cheese_(ratcatcher)) were employed by [Mysaria](wiki:Mysaria), Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen)'s [spymaster](wiki:Spymaster), to slay one of the sons of Aemond's older brother, King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Blood_(butcher).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
Cheese led Blood into the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) through a [forgotten passageway](wiki:Secret_passages), and creeping through walls, they entered the chambers of Aegon's mother, Queen [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower), in the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand). After Cheese bound and gagged the Dowager Queen and Blood strangled her [bedmaid](wiki:Bedmaid), the assassins waited for Aegon's wife, Queen [Helaena Targaryen](wiki:Helaena_Targaryen), and her children to arrive.(wiki:Blood_(butcher).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
Blood killed Helaena's guardsman once she arrived, and Cheese grabbed her youngest son, Prince [Maelor Targaryen](wiki:Maelor_Targaryen). Cheese asked Helaena which of her sons she wanted them to kill. Helaena offered herself but Blood refused, stating it had to be a son. When Helaena prevaricated, Cheese warned her that Blood would rape her six-year-old daughter, Princess [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen). Although Helaena eventually named her youngest son, Maelor, Blood beheaded Prince [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_II)) instead. Blood and Cheese then fled through the secret passages with the prince's head in hand.(wiki:Blood_(butcher).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)
Blood was seized two days later at the [Gate of the Gods](wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods) while attempting to bring Jaehaerys's head to Daemon at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) and collect his reward. Under [torture](wiki:Torture), he admitted to having been hired by a woman whose description matched Mysaria. Blood died on the thirteenth day of torture. Ser [Luthor Largent](wiki:Luthor_Largent) had the gold cloaks search for Mysaria and Cheese, but no trace was found of them.(wiki:Blood_(butcher).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8) Aegon II wanted to attack [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) to avenge his son's murder, but Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) instead suggested sending Ser [Arryk Cargyll](wiki:Arryk_Cargyll) to infiltrate the castle.(wiki:Blood_(butcher).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
