## Appearances & Description

Although not accounted as a beautiful woman, Ellaria is regarded as attractive and eye-catching, with an exotic, sensuous flair.(wiki:Ellaria_Sand.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) She has black hair.(wiki:Ellaria_Sand.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) She is described as a good woman, strong and brave, with a gentle heart.(wiki:Ellaria_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Ellaria is believed to worship a [Lysene](wiki:Lys) love goddess.(wiki:Ellaria_Sand.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) She is described by her paramour, [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell), as being sexually adventurous and potentially bisexual.(wiki:Ellaria_Sand.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Lady [Olenna Tyrell](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell), who is angry at Oberyn for having crippled her grandson [Willas Tyrell](wiki:Willas_Tyrell) in a [tourney](wiki:Tourney) accident, disparages Ellaria as "the [serpent](wiki:Serpent)'s [whore](wiki:Whore)".(wiki:Ellaria_Sand.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

When Oberyn Martell travels to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to take the seat on the [small council](wiki:Small_council) offered to his brother [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), Ellaria accompanies him.(wiki:Ellaria_Sand.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) Oberyn hints to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) that Ellaria wants to sleep with Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister).(wiki:Ellaria_Sand.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)(wiki:Ellaria_Sand.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

She is a guest at the [royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), seated with other [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) at a place of honor, but away from the [Tyrells](wiki:House_Tyrell).(wiki:Ellaria_Sand.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Ellaria is also present at the [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat) for Tyrion Lannister, where Oberyn serves as his champion, and she witnesses Oberyn's death at the hands of Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane).(wiki:Ellaria_Sand.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Feast for Crows

After Oberyn's death, Ellaria escorts his remains back to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). After Prince Doran arrests the eldest [Sand Snakes](wiki:Sand_Snakes), Ellaria and her daughters are secluded in the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens), to prevent them from being used in the plots of others.(wiki:Ellaria_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ellaria is present at the feast in honor of Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann)'s arrival at [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear), where he delivers the skull of Gregor Clegane to Prince Doran.(wiki:Ellaria_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Ellaria is later summoned to Doran's private chambers for a conference between him, his daughter [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell), and the eldest Sand Snakes. She becomes distraught when she hears [Obara Sand](wiki:Obara_Sand)'s statement that Gregor's death "is a start". With [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_Baratheon), [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch) and now the Mountain dead, everyone who was part of the death of [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell) and her children, Ellaria wants it to be an end. Ellaria fears for her own daughters, who admire Obara, [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria_Sand), and [Tyene Sand](wiki:Tyene_Sand), and fears where the quest for revenge will lead them. Doran comforts Ellaria, and tells her to go to her girls while promising no harm will come to them, and then continues the conference without her.(wiki:Ellaria_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

### The Winds of Winter

Doran sends Ellaria to her father [Harmen Uller](wiki:Harmen_Uller)'s seat at [Hellholt](wiki:Hellholt), along with her youngest daughter, [Loreza Sand](wiki:Loreza_Sand). Ellaria's daughter [Elia](wiki:Elia_Sand) is one of Arianne's traveling companions, [Obella](wiki:Obella_Sand) is sent to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) to serve as a [cupbearer](wiki:Cupbearer) to the wife of its [castellan](wiki:Castellan), Ser [Manfrey Martell](wiki:Manfrey_Martell), and [Dorea](wiki:Dorea_Sand) remains at the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens) with Doran.(wiki:Ellaria_Sand.cite_ref-10)
