## Identity

Lady Janna Fossoway, née Tyrell, is the wife of Ser Jon Fossoway of the green-apple Fossoways. She is the youngest daughter of Lord Luthor Tyrell and Lady Olenna Tyrell, and sister of Mace Tyrell and Mina Tyrell (wiki:Janna_Fossoway.cite_ref-Rasos6).

## Origins

Born between 247 and 282 AC (track_b: Born).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Tyrell (track_b: Allegiances). `SWORN_TO` House Fossoway of New Barrel — by marriage (track_b: Allegiances).

## Appearances & Description

Janna is buxom and enjoys gossip (wiki:Janna_Fossoway.cite_ref-Rasos6, wiki:Janna_Fossoway.cite_ref-Rasos16).

## Narrative Arc

Janna is among the retinue of her niece Margaery Tyrell when Margaery comes to King's Landing. She shares gossip with Sansa Stark (wiki:Janna_Fossoway.cite_ref-Rasos16). During Sansa's wedding to Tyrion Lannister, Ser Kevan Lannister dances with Janna (wiki:Janna_Fossoway.cite_ref-Rasos28). After Margaery's wedding to Tommen, Janna remains in King's Landing as part of the Tyrell entourage (wiki:Janna_Fossoway.cite_ref-Raffc24).
