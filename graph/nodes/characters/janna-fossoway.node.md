## Appearances & Description

Janna is buxom(wiki:Janna_Fossoway.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) and enjoys gossip.(wiki:Janna_Fossoway.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Janna Fossoway is among the retinue of her niece, [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), when Margaery comes to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to marry King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Janna_Fossoway.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Janna shares gossip with [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark).(wiki:Janna_Fossoway.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

During [Sansa's wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark) to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) dances with Janna.(wiki:Janna_Fossoway.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Feast for Crows

After [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Tommen_I_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) of Margaery to King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_I_Baratheon), and the departure of most of the lords and ladies from the [Reach](wiki:Reach), Lady Janna remains in King's Landing as one of Margaery's [ladies-in-waiting](wiki:Ladies-in-waiting). She often [hawks](wiki:Hawk) with Queen Margaery and [Meredyth Crane](wiki:Meredyth_Crane).(wiki:Janna_Fossoway.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

When [Queen Regent](wiki:Queen_Regent) [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) returns to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) from the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor), she encounters Margaery and her ladies returning from picking [autumn](wiki:Autumn) flowers in the [Kingswood](wiki:Kingswood). Janna and Meredyth wear flowers in their hair, and are escorted by Janna's nephews, Ser [Horas](wiki:Horas_Redwyne) and Ser [Hobber Redwyne](wiki:Hobber_Redwyne).(wiki:Janna_Fossoway.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
