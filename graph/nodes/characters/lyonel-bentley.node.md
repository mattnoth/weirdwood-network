## Origins

Ser Lyonel Bentley was inducted into the Queensguard after Queen Rhaenyra [seized](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing) [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) and commanded Ser [Lorent Marbrand](wiki:Lorent_Marbrand) to recruit six knights to serve as members.(wiki:Lyonel_Bentley.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Lyonel accompanied Rhaenyra as her party escaped [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) after the [storming of the Dragonpit](wiki:Storming_of_the_Dragonpit). On the road between [Stokeworth](wiki:Stokeworth) and [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale), they were attacked by [broken men](wiki:Broken_men) and Lyonel suffered a blow that cracked his helmet. He died raving the following day.(wiki:Lyonel_Bentley.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
