## Origins

Septa Maryam was one of [Rhaena's](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) newer companions at the time of her death in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC). At first, it was thought she had died of an [illness](wiki:Illness). Later, it was revealed that she had been [poisoned](wiki:Poison) with the [tears of Lys](wiki:Tears_of_Lys) by Rhaena's husband, [Androw Farman](wiki:Androw_Farman), who was jealous of Rhaena's friendships with her ladies and weary of their mockery toward him.(wiki:Maryam.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Rhaena much lamented her death, and Septa [Edyth](wiki:Edyth) was sent to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) in her place.(wiki:Maryam.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
