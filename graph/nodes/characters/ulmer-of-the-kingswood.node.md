## Origins

Ulmer was once a member of the [Kingswood Brotherhood](wiki:Kingswood_Brotherhood), [outlaws](wiki:Outlaw) active during the reign of King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen). Ulmer was trained by [Fletcher Dick](wiki:Fletcher_Dick),(wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood.cite_ref-Rawoiafulmer.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) who he considers the finest archer who ever lived.(wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) Ulmer claims he once put an arrow through the hand of Ser [Gerold Hightower](wiki:Gerold_Hightower), [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard), in order to steal a kiss from a [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) princess (presumably [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell)). He also stole her jewels and a chest of coins.(wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) Ulmer helped [Wenda the White Fawn](wiki:Wenda_the_White_Fawn) burn her mark in the buttocks of her highborn captives,(wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) such as [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey).(wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) After the destruction of the Brotherhood by Ser [Arthur Dayne](wiki:Arthur_Dayne), Ulmer was sent to the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood.cite_ref-Rawoiafulmer.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Appearances & Description

Ulmer is stooped with a grey beard. His limbs and skin are loose.(wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

The roguish Ulmer is a skilled archer and dancer.(wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He often tells stories about the Kingswood Brotherhood to the men at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)(wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Ulmer is part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), the force that leaves [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) and the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) in search of [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark) and [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder), and he survives the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist). Ulmer has an archery contest with [Donnel Hill](wiki:Donnel_Hill) at [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep).(wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), gives four [dragonglass](wiki:Dragonglass) arrows to Ulmer.(wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6) [Craster's wives](wiki:Craster's_wives) give two loaves of bread to the surviving black brothers. They refuse Ulmer's request for more bread, causing unrest among the guests.(wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7) Ulmer stays loyal to the Watch during the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep), and he is one of the few survivors of the ranging to make it back to Castle Black.(wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

After the election of [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) as the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), Ulmer of the Kingswood congratulates Jon.(wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Jon orders that the garrison of Castle Black practice the bow daily under the guidance of Ulmer. [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) sometimes misses his daily practice with Ulmer, due to losing track of time in the library while researching the [Others](wiki:Others). In a meeting with Jon Snow, Sam says that his archery skills are improving under Ulmer's tutelage, although he still considers himself a terrible archer. He tells Jon that he likes Ulmer's stories and that someone should put them in a book, and Jon commands Sam to do so at the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel).(wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

When [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) sacrifices "[Mance Rayder](wiki:Rattleshirt)" to [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor) at Castle Black, burning him alive, Jon has Ulmer, [Donnel Hill](wiki:Donnel_Hill), [Garth Greyfeather](wiki:Garth_Greyfeather), and [Bearded Ben](wiki:Bearded_Ben) shoot arrows at the burning man to end his suffering.(wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

The roguish Ulmer gracefully dances with serving girls at [the wedding feast](wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark) of [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark) and [Sigorn](wiki:Sigorn) of [Thenn](wiki:House_Thenn).(wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

After Jon treats with [Tormund Giantsbane](wiki:Tormund_Giantsbane), Ulmer acts as representative for the Watch's men who wish to know if they will have peace or war. Jon responds that there will be peace.(wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Three days later, when Tormund leads his [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) through the [Wall](wiki:Wall) to safety, Jon gives Ulmer the task of commanding the Wall. Ulmer stands atop the Wall with forty of Castle Black's best archers, ready to respond if there is any trouble.(wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
