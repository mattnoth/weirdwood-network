## Identity

Arsa Stark was a noblewoman from House Stark during the latter half of the second century after Aegon's Conquest. She was the only daughter of Lord Brandon Stark and Lady Alys Karstark (wiki:Arsa_Stark).

## Origins

Born between 173 and 213 AC. Daughter of Brandon Stark and Alys Karstark (track_b: Father, track_b: Mother). She had two full older brothers, Rodwell and Beron, and an older bastard half-brother, Lonnel Snow (wiki:Arsa_Stark).
