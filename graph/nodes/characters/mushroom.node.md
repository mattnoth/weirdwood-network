## Identity

Mushroom was a court fool (jester) who served House Targaryen during the Dance of the Dragons era. He later served House Manderly (track_b: Allegiances).

## Origins

Born in 103 AC. Died in 157 AC (track_b: Born, Died). Of Crownlands culture (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Mushroom was a dwarf jester who served at the Targaryen court. He is one of the three primary sources cited in Fire & Blood — his account, "The Testimony of Mushroom," is frequently invoked as an alternative (often salacious) version of events during the Dance of the Dragons. His testimony is treated with skepticism by Archmaester Gyldayn but occasionally contains details confirmed by other sources. After the Dance, he entered the service of House Manderly.
