## Origins

Cohollo saved Drogo from [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords) when he was still young.(wiki:Cohollo.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Appearances & Description

Cohollo is the oldest of Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo)'s bloodriders, a squat bald man with a crooked nose and a mouth full of broken teeth.(wiki:Cohollo.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

As a wedding gift to the newly-wed [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) and Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), Cohollo gives them an *[arakh](wiki:Arakh)* chased in [gold](wiki:Gold).(wiki:Cohollo.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

When [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) bares steel during a feast in [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak) and threatens Daenerys, Drogo orders his bloodriders to seize him. [Qotho](wiki:Qotho) pins Viserys while the others disarm him.(wiki:Cohollo.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Cohollo treats Daenerys kindly until she attempts to heal Drogo with the help of [Mirri Maz Duur](wiki:Mirri_Maz_Duur). Fearing the *[maegi](wiki:Maegi)*, Cohollo and the other bloodriders attempt to stop her. Cohollo nearly slashes the *[khaleesi](wiki:Khaleesi)'s* throat during the attempt, but he is killed by an arrow from [Aggo](wiki:Aggo).(wiki:Cohollo.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
