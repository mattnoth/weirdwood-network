## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Septa Unella is one of the septas assigned to get a confession out of Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell); the queen confesses that she wants to claw Unella's eyes out. After Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) is arrested, Unella becomes her confessor as well.(wiki:Unella.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Unella continues to be one of the holy septas who visit Cersei every hour of the day or night asking her to confess. During one visit Cersei relents and tells Unella she wishes to confess her sins to his High Holiness, and Unella is one of the septas who bring her before the [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow). When the High Septon allows Cersei visitors Unella summons Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) to see her. Unella is asked by the High Septon to stay and record Cersei's confession.(wiki:Unella.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Septas Unella, [Moelle](wiki:Moelle) and [Scolera](wiki:Scolera) are part of the escort for Cersei as she completes her [walk of atonement](wiki:Walk_of_atonement) through the streets of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Prior to doing so Unella has the queen shorn by [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters).(wiki:Unella.cite_ref-Radwd65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
