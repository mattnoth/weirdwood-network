## Identity

The Handsome Man is one of the unnamed Faceless Men who visit the House of Black and White in Braavos. He is a visiting priest observed by Arya Stark during her novitiate. He is always comely in appearance but wears a different beard color and a different nose on each visit, demonstrating the Faceless Men's skill at changing their faces. Arya identifies him by his consistent comeliness rather than any fixed feature.
