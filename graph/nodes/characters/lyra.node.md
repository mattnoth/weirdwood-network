## Origins

In [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), Lyra was one of three women sent to Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) by Dowager Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon), to see to Queen Alysanne's religious instruction. Her companions were Septa [Ysabel](wiki:Ysabel), the novice [Edyth](wiki:Edyth), and the famously pious Lady [Lucinda Tully](wiki:Lucinda_Tully). Queen Regent Alyssa hoped that the four women could make Alysanne, and perhaps even King [Jaehaerys I](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), understand that the [Faith](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) considered their [incestuous](wiki:Incest) [marriage](wiki:Marriage) an abomination. Alyssa hoped her children would repent their marriage as a result, before it would tear the realm apart again. However, Lyra and the other women were moved by Alysanne's devotion to her husband and her kindness, and became her loyal servants instead.(wiki:Lyra.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

By [51 AC](wiki:51_AC), Lyra had sworn her vows to become a septa. She attended Queen Alysanne at [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) along with Septa Edyth, and defended the pregnant queen when she was attacked by women while attending [Jonquil's Pool](wiki:Jonquil's_Pool). In [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), when Alysanne was pregnant again, Septa Lyra accompanied her to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), where the queen eventually gave birth to Prince [Aemon Targaryen](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)).(wiki:Lyra.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Lyra.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Lyra comforted Alysanne after she lost her three month old baby, [Gaemon](wiki:Gaemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) in [73 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), telling Alysanne that Gaemon was with [the Mother Above](wiki:Mother_(the_Seven)). Following the birth of the sickly and frail Princess [Gael Targaryen](wiki:Gael_Targaryen) in [80 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Lyra assisted Grand Maester [Elysar](wiki:Elysar) in nursing the child during the first year of her life, watching over the babe night and day.(wiki:Lyra.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
