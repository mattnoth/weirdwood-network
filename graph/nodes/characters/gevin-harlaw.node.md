## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Gevin is among the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) who participate in the [harrying of the Stony Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore). He kneels on the chest of a dead [northman](wiki:Northman) and saws a finger off to get a ring. (wiki:Gevin_Harlaw.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Four [Harlaws](wiki:House_Harlaw) remain with [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) after the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) and are slain during the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell) by [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton);(wiki:Gevin_Harlaw.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) it is unknown if [Dykk](wiki:Dykk_Harlaw) and Gevin are among them or if they are among the ironborn who abandon Theon beforehand.
