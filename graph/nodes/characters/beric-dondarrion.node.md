## Origins

In either [294 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) or [295 AC](wiki:295_AC),(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-14) Beric was betrothed to Lady [Allyria Dayne](wiki:Allyria_Dayne). Following their betrothal, Beric took Allyria's nephew [Edric Dayne](wiki:Edric_Dayne), the seven-year-old [Lord of Starfall](wiki:Lord_of_Starfall), back with him to [Blackhaven](wiki:Blackhaven) to serve as his [page](wiki:Page). He later makes Edric his squire.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

## Appearances & Description

Beric is a slight man,(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) handsome with red-gold hair.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) He is a dashing(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) but inexperienced young man.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Beric comes to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to fight in the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) celebrating Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s appointment as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2) [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole) wishes to marry him as soon as she sees him.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) During the tournament, Beric loses a horse in a tilt with a [hedge knight](wiki:Hedge_knight) and is unhorsed by [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr) shortly thereafter.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-2) After the conclusion of the tournament, Beric remains in King's Landing. This leads to Eddard appointing him leader of an expedition to bring the [king's justice](wiki:King's_justice_(concept)) to Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane), who has been raiding the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) after the capture of [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

Beric's party is ambushed in the [battle at the Mummer's Ford](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford), however, with many killed. By the time that [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) and his allies are fighting the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) armies near the [Trident](wiki:Trident), Beric's survivors continually raid the Lannisters' foraging parties.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Lord Dondarrion is named among the nobles King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) insists must do homage to him or be named traitors.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Rumors have Beric as missing or perhaps dead.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Ser [Burton Crakehall](wiki:Burton_Crakehall),(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch),(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) and Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane)(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1) all claim to have killed him. Yet, he is still raiding the Lannister foraging parties(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1) and the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) are actively seeking him,(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) as are Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s forces stationed at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

Beric's group meets [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) plagued by the ravage of Lannister forces but does not trouble them, and he attempts to pay for the food they take.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-1) Beric's group slays Burton's [column](wiki:Column) of [westermen](wiki:Westerlands) in a trap.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-2)

### A Storm of Swords

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), [Gendry](wiki:Gendry), and [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) are captured by members of the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), who plan to bring them to Beric.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) Meanwhile, Beric travels throughout the riverlands, including a visit to [Acorn Hall](wiki:Acorn_Hall).(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) Some of his men disguise themselves as the lightning lord to suggest he can be everywhere.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2)

The captives are brought to Beric within the [hollow hill](wiki:Hollow_hill), where he sits on [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) roots and is revealed as a follower of the [Lord of Light](wiki:Lord_of_Light). They watch as [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) duels Beric in a [trial by battle](wiki:Trial_by_battle). While [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr) always lit his blades with [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire), Beric uses his own blood to make his sword magically aflame. Despite Sandor's fear of fire, he is able to slay Beric, cleaving him from shoulder to breastbone. However, Beric is revived by Thoros, much to the surprise of the captives.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

It is revealed that Beric had been mortally wounded by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane)'s lance at the [Mummer's Ford](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford), but his squire [Edric Dayne](wiki:Edric_Dayne) and [Green Gergen](wiki:Green_Gergen) protected him and helped him to escape. Despite the efforts of Thoros, Beric died in an ash grove that night. However, Beric was unexpectedly resurrected when Thoros performed the [last kiss](wiki:Last_kiss) of the [Lord of Light](wiki:Lord_of_Light) at his funeral.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) He became the leader of an outlaw band protecting smallfolk in the name of the late King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). Beric was killed several more times, but was repeatedly brought back to life by Thoros.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4)

The weary Beric has become a scarecrow of a man, his battered body bearing all the wounds of his previous deaths.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6) He is missing an eye, in its place a raw red pit. His head is broken, caved in at the temple. There is a dark ring around his throat from being hanged, and there is a terrible gash in his chest from where the Hound killed him. Arya has never seen Beric eat or sleep, though he occasionally drinks wine. Beric's memory is impaired, having forgotten details about [his castle](wiki:Blackhaven) and [the woman](wiki:Allyria_Dayne) he was pledged to marry.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5) The lord still wears his ragged black cloak and dented breastplate, and a scarf around his throat to hide the marks there.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-6)

Beric is aided by Edric during the [battle at the burning septry](wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry) against the [Bloody Mummers](wiki:Bloody_Mummers), after which he [knights](wiki:Knight) Gendry.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-7) Beric later visits the [ghost of High Heart](wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart) to hear her wisdom. Because Beric wants to ransom Arya to her family, the girl runs away from the brotherhood.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

[Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey) goes to [Oldstones](wiki:Oldstones) to ransom [Petyr Frey](wiki:Petyr_Frey) from the brotherhood, but [Tom Sevenstrings](wiki:Tom_Sevenstrings) tells him that Beric is elsewhere. [A woman](wiki:Lady_Stoneheart) grants approval for the outlaws to hang Merrett.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) informs Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) that Beric has been hanging [Freys](wiki:House_Frey).(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) [Mariya Darry](wiki:Mariya_Darry), [Amerei Frey](wiki:Amerei_Frey), and Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister) believe the river lords and smallfolk are helping Beric.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2)(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) In order to turn the smallfolk against the brotherhood, Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) spreads rumors that the [Hound](wiki:Hound) joined Beric's outlaws.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) Ser [Harwyn Plumm](wiki:Harwyn_Plumm) promises Amerei that he will kill Beric.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-3) The mysterious [Stoneheart](wiki:Stoneheart) is rumored to be Beric's lover.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-1)

Thoros reveals to Brienne that Beric truly died when he gave his life to bring back Catelyn Stark, whose body had been found by the outlaws after the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding). Thoros refused to revive Catelyn, so Beric passed the flame of life from him to her. The brotherhood has fractured with Catelyn, now called Lady Stoneheart, as their leader, with some leaving the outlaw band.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) is prepared to fight Beric and his outlaws, not realizing the lightning lord has died for the final time.(wiki:Beric_Dondarrion.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)
