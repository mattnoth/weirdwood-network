## Origins

In [105 AC](wiki:105_AC), Ser Harwin was brought to court by his father, Lord [Lyonel Strong](wiki:Lyonel_Strong), alongside his younger brother [Larys](wiki:Larys_Strong) and his two sisters. Harwin was made a captain in the [gold cloaks](wiki:Gold_cloaks) of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Harwin_Strong.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
During [112 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Harwin was one of many suitors to pay court to [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), the [Princess of Dragonstone](wiki:Princess_of_Dragonstone).(wiki:Harwin_Strong.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)
According to [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom) in [113 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Harwin one night returning to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) from a night of reveling in the capital happened upon a spurned Princess Rhaenyra Targaryen and she took solace in his company, being found abed together by Mushroom the following morning.(wiki:Harwin_Strong.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)
In [114 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Princess Rhaenyra granted Harwin her favor at the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) celebrating her [wedding](wiki:Wedding) with Ser [Laenor Velaryon](wiki:Laenor_Velaryon). At that tourney, Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) broke his collarbone and shattered his elbow. Mushroom mocked him as "Brokenbones" after this event. Thereafter Harwin was a consistent presence at Rhaenyra's side and was the foremost member among the [blacks](wiki:Blacks).(wiki:Harwin_Strong.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)
In late [115 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), according to [Septon](wiki:Septon) [Eustace](wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)), during the birth of Rhaenyra's second son, [Lucerys](wiki:Lucerys_Velaryon), both Rhaenyra's husband Ser Laenor and Breakbones were in attendance at her bedside. When Rhaenyra moved to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) after the rivalry with Queen [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower) and the [greens](wiki:Greens) grew to be too much, Breakbones went with her as her personal [sworn shield](wiki:Sworn_shield).(wiki:Harwin_Strong.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_viserys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Harwin_Strong.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8)
In [120 AC](wiki:120_AC), after Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) called Rhaenyra's sons "[Strongs](wiki:House_Strong)" and a brawl broke out, Harwin was removed as Princess Rhaenyra's champion by King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) and he returned to [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), being replaced by Ser [Erryk Cargyll](wiki:Erryk_Cargyll) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). Harwin died in a fire at Harrenhal that same year, with it also killing his father, Lord Lyonel Strong. The fire was said to be an accident and blamed on the [curse](wiki:Curse_of_Harrenhal) of Harrenhal. Other non-mystical suspects included Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen), whose motive would have been eliminating a rival for Princess Rhaenyra's affections; Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon), as revenge for Harwin cuckolding his son; Harwin's brother, Larys, to claim the inheritance of Harrenhal; and King Viserys I, to eliminate the man who brought dishonor on his House.(wiki:Harwin_Strong.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-9)
