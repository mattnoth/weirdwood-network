## Origins

Ser Jon married [Alys Oakheart](wiki:Alys_Oakheart), the daughter of the Lord of [Old Oak](wiki:Old_Oak), in [12 AC](wiki:12_AC). During the [wedding](wiki:Wedding), Lord [Wyl of Wyl](wiki:Wyl_of_Wyl) turned up uninvited and was admitted through the postern gate by a treacherous servant. Lord [Oakheart](wiki:House_Oakheart) and most of the guests were slain, and Jon's wife was forced to watch him be [castrated](wiki:Eunuch). His wife and her [handmaids](wiki:Handmaid) were gang-raped by Wyl's men and then sold off to a [Myrish](wiki:Myr) slaver.
