## Origins

Lord Yorbert Royce was the [regent](wiki:Regent) and the [Lord Protector of the Vale](wiki:Lord_Protector_of_the_Vale) during the minority of Lady [Jeyne Arryn](wiki:Jeyne_Arryn),(wiki:Yorbert_Royce.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale:_house_arryn.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)(wiki:Yorbert_Royce.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) after her [lord father](wiki:Lord_Arryn_(father_of_Jeyne)) and elder brothers were slain by the [Stone Crows](wiki:Stone_Crows).(wiki:Yorbert_Royce.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) In that capacity, he attended the [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council) of [101 AC](wiki:101_AC) in her stead.(wiki:Yorbert_Royce.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale:_house_arryn.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)(wiki:Yorbert_Royce.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) The text does not state the candidate Yorbert supported in the final vote at the council, however.(wiki:Yorbert_Royce.cite_ref-5)

[Rhea Royce](wiki:Rhea_Royce), the heir to [Runestone](wiki:Runestone), was married to Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) in [97 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Yorbert_Royce.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) By the time of the accession of King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) in [103 AC](wiki:103_AC), Rhea had succeeded Yorbert as Lady of Runestone, indicating that he had passed away.(wiki:Yorbert_Royce.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)
