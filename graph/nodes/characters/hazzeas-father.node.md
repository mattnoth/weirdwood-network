## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

After Hazzea is killed by the black [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Drogon](wiki:Drogon), her father brings her burned bones to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) in the [Great Pyramid](wiki:The_Great_Pyramid) of Meereen. Daenerys is plagued by guilt, and orders her three dragons caged, but Drogon escapes and heads to the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea).(wiki:Hazzea's_father.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

[Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) suggests that the father just used Drogon and Dany as an opportunity to get rid of an unwanted child. It is also suggested the [sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy) did the deed, and made it look like Drogon did it. Dany wishes she could believe this, but finds herself unable to, since Hazzea's father had waited until the entire court had been cleared before confronting Dany with his daughter's bones.(wiki:Hazzea's_father.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Hazzea's_father.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Instead of killing Hazzea's father, as the [Shavepate](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) urges Daenerys to do, Dany decides to pay the [blood price](wiki:Blood_price) for Hazzea. On the condition that her family never speaks a word of what has happened to her, Daenerys will pay Hazzea's family money each year on Hazzea's [nameday](wiki:Nameday). Hazzea herself is put to rest in the [Temple of the Graces](wiki:Temple_of_the_Graces), with a hundred candles burning day and night in her memory.(wiki:Hazzea's_father.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
