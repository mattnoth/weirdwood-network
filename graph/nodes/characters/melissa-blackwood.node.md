## Origins

Melissa became the sixth mistress of [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen) early in the king's reign, in [172 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), replacing Lady [Barba Bracken](wiki:Barba_Bracken), who had been sent from court in disgrace. Even though she was Aegon's mistress, Melissa's kind heart and generosity led her to befriend Aegon's sister-wife Queen [Naerys](wiki:Naerys_Targaryen), her brother Prince [Aemon the Dragonknight](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_II)), and her son Prince [Daeron](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Melissa_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

While Melissa was slender of frame, Barba had been buxom. When Barba was heard to joke that Melissa was flat as a boy, King Aegon, who previously had renamed the hills known as the [Mother's Teats](wiki:Mother's_Teats) after Barba ("Barba's Teats"), renamed the hills for Melissa instead and granted them to [House Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood).(wiki:Melissa_Blackwood.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Melissa_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9) Members of [House Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken) still refer to the hills as "Barba's Teats", while members of House Blackwood continue to call them "Missy's Teats".(wiki:Melissa_Blackwood.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Melissa was King Aegon's mistress for five years, during which she gave birth to three children: two daughters, [Mya](wiki:Mya_Rivers) and [Gwenys](wiki:Gwenys_Rivers), and a son, [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers). Brynden would later be able to maintain close relations at court, due to the fact that his mother had been well loved during her time there, even after her dismissal.(wiki:Melissa_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

[Lord Bracken](wiki:Lord_Bracken), with the help of his daughter Barba, groomed his younger daughter Lady [Bethany](wiki:Bethany_Bracken) to replace Melissa. In [177 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), King Aegon visited [Stone Hedge](wiki:Stone_Hedge) to see his [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son by Barba, [Aegor Rivers](wiki:Aegor_Rivers), and Bethany caught his eye and returned with him to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Melissa_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)

A statue of Melissa can be found in the [godswood of Raventree Hall](wiki:Godswood_of_Raventree_Hall), the seat of [House Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood).(wiki:Melissa_Blackwood.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

### A Dance with Dragons

In a [cave beyond the Wall](wiki:Cave_of_the_three-eyed_crow), the [three-eyed crow](wiki:Three-eyed_crow) tells [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) that his mother gave him the name [Brynden](wiki:Brynden_Rivers).(wiki:Melissa_Blackwood.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Hoster also tells Jaime the story of why the Brackens and Blackwoods call the [Teats](wiki:Teats) different names.(wiki:Melissa_Blackwood.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)
