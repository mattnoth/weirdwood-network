## Origins

### Dance of the Dragons[ ]

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lady Sabitha greeted Lord [Roderick Dustin](wiki:Roderick_Dustin) and the [Winter Wolves](wiki:Winter_Wolves) outside of the [Twins](wiki:Twins) during their march south. Sabitha's husband, Lord [Forrest Frey](wiki:Forrest_Frey), was slain in the [Battle by the Lakeshore](wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore).(wiki:Sabitha_Frey.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Lady Sabitha seized [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) three days after it had been abandoned by Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) and Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen). When Aemond unexpectedly returned, Sabitha hid in a privy to escape [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar)'s [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame). She then returned to the Twins.(wiki:Sabitha_Frey.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
Sabitha and her kin from [House Vypren](wiki:House_Vypren) joined Ser [Addam Velaryon](wiki:Addam_Velaryon) for the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton),(wiki:Sabitha_Frey.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and she participated in the [Battle of the Kingsroad](wiki:Battle_of_the_Kingsroad).(wiki:Sabitha_Frey.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Sabitha and [Alysanne Blackwood](wiki:Alysanne_Blackwood) shared tents during the campaign.(wiki:Sabitha_Frey.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) The women joined the [Lads](wiki:Lads) in marching on [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), where they were welcomed by the populace of the capital.(wiki:Sabitha_Frey.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

### Reign of Aegon III[ ]

When a council of [regents](wiki:Regent) was selected to help rule during the [minority](wiki:Minority) of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), Sabitha was one of the notable omissions from the council, though Septon [Eustace](wiki:Eustace) writes that Sabitha was not slighted by this exclusion. Instead she returned home to the Twins, to try to reestablish some semblance of order in the northern riverlands. Afterwards she held [Widow Fairs](wiki:Widow_Fairs) at the Twins, matching landless Stark soldiers who stayed in the south with widows who lost their husbands in the war.(wiki:Sabitha_Frey.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
In [134 AC](wiki:134_AC), Lady Sabitha contributed six hundred [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) to the host of Ser [Robert Rowan](wiki:Robert_Rowan) when he marched on the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) to intervene in the [Arryn succession conflict](wiki:Arryn_Succession_Conflict_(134_AC)).(wiki:Sabitha_Frey.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
In [136 AC](wiki:136_AC), Lord [Torrhen Manderly](wiki:Torrhen_Manderly) and Aegon's regents made plan for a great [royal progress](wiki:Royal_progress) around the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) to celebrate Aegon III's coming of age. They planned for the king to stop at the Twins where Lady Sabitha would host him. Aegon cancelled the progress, however.(wiki:Sabitha_Frey.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
