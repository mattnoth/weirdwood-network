## Origins

During the [Andal Invasion](wiki:Andal_Invasion) of the [Vale](wiki:Vale), Lord Ruthermont was part of the [Andal](wiki:Andal) coalition led by Ser [Artys Arryn](wiki:Artys_I_Arryn).
