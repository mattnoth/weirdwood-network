## Origins

Harwood's [grandsire](wiki:Lord_Fell) was killed by Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) at [Summerhall](wiki:Battle_of_Summerhall) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). Harwood admires Robert for his martial prowess.(wiki:Harwood_Fell.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Appearances & Description

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

In support of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), Lord Harwood is among Stannis's lords who sail north to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) and ride for [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black). He is one of the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men), the supporters of Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) and followers of [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor).(wiki:Harwood_Fell.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Harwood counts three hundred men of fighting age among the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) who pass through the Wall.(wiki:Harwood_Fell.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

He accompanies Stannis on the [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell).(wiki:Harwood_Fell.cite_ref-Radwd21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) He tries to save three teamsters and four horses that fall through a hidden pond's ice, falling through himself. His knights save him from drowning, but he dies of a fever soon after.(wiki:Harwood_Fell.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
