## Identity

Ko Aggo is a Dothraki horserider who serves as one of Daenerys Targaryen's bloodriders (wiki:Aggo).

## Appearances & Description

Aggo is a young Dothraki with dark, almond-shaped eyes (wiki:Aggo). He has a flat, broad face (agot-daenerys-09). He is a brave warrior and skilled scout who wields a bow, knife, axe, arakh, and whip (wiki:Aggo).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` Khal Drogo (formerly), House Targaryen, Queensguard (track_b: Allegiances). `HOLDS_TITLE` Ko, Bloodrider (track_b: Titles).

## Narrative Arc

Aggo was one of four warriors assigned to Daenerys's khas within Drogo's khalasar. He accompanied Daenerys during the assassination attempt by the wineseller at Vaes Dothrak (agot-daenerys-06). When Drogo's bloodriders turned against Daenerys, Aggo killed Cohollo with an arrow through the lungs and heart (agot-daenerys-08). After Drogo's death, Daenerys named Aggo ko and gave him the dragonbone bow from her wedding gifts. He initially resisted, saying "I cannot say these words. Only a man can lead a khalasar," but knelt after the dragons hatched (agot-daenerys-10). He continues to serve as one of Daenerys's bloodriders through ADWD (wiki:Aggo).
