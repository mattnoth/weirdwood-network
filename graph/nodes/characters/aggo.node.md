## Appearances & Description

Aggo is a young(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) with dark, almond-shaped eyes.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Aggo is a brave warrior and a skilled scout.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He wields a bow,(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) a knife,(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) an axe,(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) an *[arakh](wiki:Arakh)*,(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and a whip.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Aggo is one of four [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) warriors assigned to the *[khas](wiki:Khas)* of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) within the *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* of Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo). Aggo accompanies Daenerys during her visit to the Western Market in [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak), where helps stop an assassination attempt on Daenerys's life by a [wineseller](wiki:Wineseller).(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Daenerys claims [Eroeh](wiki:Eroeh) from rapers, and Aggo kills one of them with an arrow.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

When Daenerys attempts to heal Drogo with [Mirri Maz Duur](wiki:Mirri_Maz_Duur)'s aid, his bloodriders attempt to stop her. [Cohollo](wiki:Cohollo) is prevented from slitting Daenerys's throat when he is killed by an arrow from Aggo.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Daenerys decides to execute Mirri on Drogo's funeral pyre, which is constructed around the body of a stallion which Aggo kills with his axe.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

When Daenerys names Aggo *[ko](wiki:Ko)* and asks him to become one of her [bloodriders](wiki:Bloodrider) alongside [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) and [Rakharo](wiki:Rakharo), she offers him the [dragonbone](wiki:Dragonbone) bow she received as a present during [her wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen). He is at first reluctant, but he accepts after the [dragons](wiki:Dragon) are born.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

### A Clash of Kings

Aggo serves in the [Queensguard](wiki:Queensguard) of Daenerys.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) During their journey across the [red waste](wiki:Red_waste), Aggo tells Daenerys that [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) is [Balerion the Black Dread](wiki:Balerion) reincarnated.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

While her *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* rests in [Vaes Tolorro](wiki:Vaes_Tolorro), Daenerys orders her three bloodriders to scout out the country, each one in a different direction. Aggo leaves to the south-west, where he find nothing but sterile desert and the ruins of two abandoned cities, one of which is warded by skulls and the other where he finds an iron bracelet, which he brings back to Daenerys.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) Aggo is the second bloodrider to come back to Vaes Tolorro. After his return, Daenerys sets him to repairing the gates to defend the town should they come under attack.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2)

Aggo guards Daenerys's [palanquin](wiki:Palanquin) as she travels through [Qarth](wiki:Qarth).(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) He accompanies Daenerys to the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying), where he asks her to allow him and [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) to go with her and protect her as her sworn bloodrider, though Daenerys insists there are places even a *khal* must walk alone.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

Afterwards, Aggo, along with [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo), rides with Daenerys to the [port of Qarth](wiki:Port_of_Qarth) to seek a ship, where he buys a skewer of honey-roasted [mice](wiki:Mice) to eat.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) He rushes to Daenerys's aid during her second near assassination attempt and seizes [Arstan Whitebeard's](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) staff. He draws his *arakh* on [Strong Belwas](wiki:Strong_Belwas) when the latter mocks him, but he stands down on the orders of Daenerys.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Aggo guards Daenerys when she visits the [Plaza of Pride](wiki:Plaza_of_Pride) to discuss the purchase of [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) slaves in [Astapor](wiki:Astapor).(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) As one of Daenery's three bloodriders, he often is asked to keep her company as to prevent Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) from approaching her when alone.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)

Aggo is present when Daenerys purchases and turns the Unsullied upon the slavers of Astapor. The night before the battle, Daenerys sees him fit a new string to his dragonbone bow, and recalls his opinion that [Eroeh](wiki:Eroeh)'s rape by [Mago](wiki:Mago) was the Lhazareen girl's fate.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

Aggo leads the litter during the [fall of Astapor](wiki:Fall_of_Astapor), and feathers several [Good Masters](wiki:Good_Masters) and Astapori slavers on the day of the battle with his great bow, calmly mowing down anyone with *[tokars](wiki:Tokar)* with pinpoint precision.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)

On the march to [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai), Aggo and his fellow bloodriders are also made *kos* by Daenerys and are put in charge of commanding the thirty-odd mounted *khalasar* remaining to her, leaving their guarding duties to [Arstan Whitebeard](wiki:Arstan_Whitebeard) and [Strong Belwas](wiki:Strong_Belwas). Aggo later participates in the [battle near Yunkai](wiki:Battle_near_Yunkai), successfully leading the Dothraki mounted warriors in wedge formation against the enemy center.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

During the [siege of Meereen](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen), Aggo almost comes to blows with [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) and [Rakharo](wiki:Rakharo) over who would fight [Oznak zo Pahl](wiki:Oznak_zo_Pahl), the hero of the city; Daenerys ultimately stops her bloodriders from fighting, deeming them brave but too young and valuable to lose.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) After the conquest of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), Aggo styles his hair in an oiled braid with [silver](wiki:Silver) bells and wears the gold and jewels of dead men.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Aggo, [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) and [Rakharo](wiki:Rakharo) are commanded by Daenerys to use her *khalasar* to subdue the hinterlands of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) and end the slavery there.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Ser Barristan later reports the bloodriders successfully harried the slavers from the hills and struck the shackles off the slaves, and are planting and bringing crops to Meereen to market.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

Daenerys later recalls Aggo and her other bloodriders back to Meereen. They arrive in time to assist her in feeding the thousands of survivors from the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor), where Aggo expresses disgust at the cannibalism of the diseased refugees. He later watches in disbelief as Daenerys and his comrades help the sufferers of the [bloody flux](wiki:Bloody_flux).(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

Aggo later accompanies Daenerys to [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit) as part of the royal procession's guard.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) He and Rakharo both depart Meereen and lead search parties across the Skahazadhan to locate Daenerys when she disappears on [Drogon](wiki:Drogon)'s back.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) Both lead ten picked riders and every man has three horses each to travel far and fast to find her.(wiki:Aggo.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
