## Identity

Osha is a spearwife of the free folk from north of the Wall (wiki:Osha).

## Origins

Born beyond the Wall (track_b: Born).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Free folk (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Osha is tall and lean, standing a head taller than Robb Stark. She has a hard face, shaggy brown hair, and is flat-chested. Her bony arms are wiry but strong, and her body bears many scars (wiki:Osha). She wears mail and leather with a bowl-shaped halfhelm and carries an eight-foot spear of black oak with a rusted steel tip (wiki:Osha). She worships the old gods and is knowledgeable about the lands beyond the Wall (wiki:Osha).

## Narrative Arc

Osha travels south of the Wall with a group fleeing the Others, including the Night's Watch deserters Stiv and Wallen. They encounter Bran Stark in the wolfswood and attempt to take him hostage, but Robb Stark and Theon Greyjoy rescue Bran. Osha yields and is taken as a captive to Winterfell (wiki:Osha.cite_ref-Ragot37). She is put to work in the kitchens. Osha warns the Starks about the threat beyond the Wall but is largely ignored (wiki:Osha). In ACOK, when Theon captures Winterfell, Osha seduces him and uses the opportunity to help Bran and Rickon escape. They hide in the crypts beneath Winterfell while Theon's search party hunts for them (wiki:Osha.cite_ref-Racok66). After the sack, Osha emerges from the crypts with Bran, Rickon, the Reeds, and Hodor. At Maester Luwin's dying counsel, the group splits: Osha takes Rickon and his direwolf Shaggydog in one direction while Bran heads north (wiki:Osha.cite_ref-Racok69). In ADWD, Wyman Manderly reveals that Osha and Rickon are on the island of Skagos (wiki:Osha.cite_ref-Radwd29).
