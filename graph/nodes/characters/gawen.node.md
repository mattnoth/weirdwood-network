## Origins

Gawen served as [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) for King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), succeeding Grand Maester [Lyonce](wiki:Lyonce) in [12 AC](wiki:12_AC).(wiki:Gawen.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Gawen and Ser [Osmund Strong](wiki:Osmund_Strong), Aegon's [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), were charged with raising walls around [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Gawen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Gawen continued to hold the office of Grand Maester during the reign of Aegon I's heir, King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen). When Aenys fell ill at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) in late [41 AC](wiki:41_AC), Gawen despaired of improving the king's condition.(wiki:Gawen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aenys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Following the death of Aenys I, the elderly and grey-headed Gawen protested the coronation of Aenys's half-brother, [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), by his mother, [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen).(wiki:Gawen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Gawen stated that following the [laws of inheritance](wiki:Laws_of_inheritance) the crown should pass to Aenys's oldest son, Prince [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)). Maegor beheaded Gawen for his protests with [Blackfyre](wiki:Blackfyre). After Gawen's death, there were no further protests at Dragonstone.(wiki:Gawen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Gawen was the first of three Grand Maesters who were executed during Maegor's reign,(wiki:Gawen.cite_ref-Rasos12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) the others being [Myros](wiki:Myros) and [Desmond](wiki:Desmond_(Grand_Maester)).
