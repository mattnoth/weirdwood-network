## Identity

Prince Baelor Targaryen, known as Baelor Breakspear, was a member of House Targaryen and the Prince of Dragonstone (wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)). He was the eldest son and heir of King Daeron II Targaryen and Queen Myriah Martell. He served as Hand of the King (wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)).

## Origins

Born in 170 AC (track_b: Born). Son of Daeron II Targaryen (track_b: Father) and Myriah Martell (track_b: Mother). Named after his grandfather's cousin, King Baelor I (wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)).

## Appearances & Description

Baelor had the dark hair of his Dornish mother Myriah Martell, kept cut short and peppered with grey by his late thirties. He had a strong, clean-shaven jaw and a nose broken twice. He was tall, lean, and broad-shouldered. Though he dressed simply, he evoked a sense of power and certainty (wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)). Many muttered he was more Martell than Targaryen due to his dark coloring (wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)).

## Narrative Arc

Baelor was one of the most powerful warriors in the Seven Kingdoms, rivalling the Kingsguard, famed for his prowess with sword and lance. He was as open-handed and just as his father. He served as Hand of the King after the Battle of the Redgrass Field until his death at the Tourney at Ashford Meadow in 209 AC, where he died from a blow struck by his own brother Maekar during a trial of seven championing the hedge knight Ser Duncan the Tall (wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)).
