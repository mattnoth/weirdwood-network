## Origins

Drako was the son of Lysandro Rogare. He had five trueborn brothers, [Lysaro](wiki:Lysaro_Rogare), [Moredo](wiki:Moredo_Rogare), [Lotho](wiki:Lotho_Rogare), [Fredo](wiki:Fredo_Rogare), and [Rogerrio](wiki:Roggerio_Rogare), and three trueborn sisters, [Larra](wiki:Larra_Rogare), [Lysara](wiki:Lysara_Rogare), and [Marra](wiki:Marra_Rogare) as well as sixteen bastard siblings.(wiki:Drako_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

After the death of his father, [Lysandro "the Magnificent"](wiki:Lysandro_Rogare), Drako inherited his ships. When the [Rogare Bank](wiki:Rogare_Bank) ran dry, Drako escaped to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) on one of his galleys before he could be tried like many of his siblings. However, for every ship saved, nine were lost, together with their cargos and the Rogare wharves and storehouses. When his brother [Lysaro](wiki:Lysaro_Rogare) arrived in Volantis after being captured at [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys), the [Triarchs](wiki:Triarch) offered to ransom him to Drako, but he declined, suggesting instead that the Volantenes sell him back to Lys.(wiki:Drako_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
