## Origins

Archmaester Perestan claims [the world](wiki:Known_world) is forty thousand years old, though his colleague [Mollos](wiki:Mollos) believes it is five hundred thousand years old.(wiki:Perestan.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Because the double-bladed axe was a symbol used by the [Andals](wiki:Andals) of old and the modern [Norvoshi](wiki:Norvos) see the axe as a symbol of power, Perestan proposed that Andals were the first to settle Norvos. The archmaester suggested the [bearded priests](wiki:Bearded_priests) of Norvos took the emblem from ruins they found as they established the city.(wiki:Perestan.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_norvos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) He also suggested that the name "[Erreg](wiki:Erreg_the_Kinslayer)" was a corruption of an Andal title.(wiki:Perestan.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Perestan suggested that the ancient [Valyrians](wiki:Valyria) once reached [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) but turned away after an unknown calamity.(wiki:Perestan.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) The archmaester theorized that [Lann the Clever](wiki:Lann_the_Clever) inherited [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) after marrying the daughter of the last [Casterly](wiki:House_Casterly).(wiki:Perestan.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) Regarding the legend of Ser [Artys Arryn](wiki:Winged_Knight) riding a huge falcon, Perestan suggests the storytellers were inspired by [dragonriders](wiki:Dragonriders).(wiki:Perestan.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale:_house_arryn.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Appearances & Description

Perestan's ring, rod, and mask are made of [copper](wiki:Copper).(wiki:Perestan.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He has a poor opinion of Archmaester [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn), believing him to be of unsound mind.(wiki:Perestan.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Perestan highly regards Archmaester [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn)'s uncompleted work, *[Fire & Blood, Being a History of the Targaryen Kings of Westeros](wiki:Fire_&_Blood,_Being_a_History_of_the_Targaryen_Kings_of_Westeros)*, which drew Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel)'s attention.(wiki:Perestan.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Perestan.cite_ref-GyldaynRetcon_5-0)
