## Origins

In [134 AC](wiki:134_AC), Moredo and his brothers, Roggerio and Lotho, accompanied their sister, [Larra Rogare](wiki:Larra_Rogare), when she came to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to live at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) with her husband, Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen). Moredo was the commander of his sister's guards.(wiki:Moredo_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

The [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) respected all the conditions for Prince Viserys's return, as negotiated between Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) and Lysandro, these included granting [lordships](wiki:Lord) to Moredo and his two brothers.(wiki:Moredo_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Moredo_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

A war of succession broke out in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) after the death of Lady [Jeyne Arryn](wiki:Jeyne_Arryn), and the killing of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen)'s [regent](wiki:Regent), Ser [Corwyn Corbray](wiki:Corwyn_Corbray). Lord [Thaddeus Rowan](wiki:Thaddeus_Rowan), Aegon's [Hand](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), gave Moredo the command of the army carried with the [Velaryon](wiki:House_Velaryon) fleet to the Vale. It was a very poor choice as Moredo was not fluent in the Common Tongue and had great difficulty communicating with his [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) men. He lost hundreds of men taking the harbor and city of [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown).(wiki:Moredo_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

When word of the fall of House Rogare reached Gulltown, Moredo yielded up his command to Lord Alyn and sailed for [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Moredo_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6) Once in Braavos, he treated with the [keyholders](wiki:Keyholders) of the [Iron Bank](wiki:Iron_Bank_of_Braavos) and recieved gold from them. He then used the Braavosi gold to hire ships and swords in [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh), and prepared to attack [Lys](wiki:Lys) in retaliation for what happened to his family.(wiki:Moredo_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)(wiki:Moredo_Rogare.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_quarrelsome_daughters:_myr.2C_lys.2C_and_tyrosh.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [Archmaester](wiki:Archmaester) [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn) does not go into the details of Moredo's attack in [his book](wiki:Fire_&_Blood,_Being_a_History_of_the_Targaryen_Kings_of_Westeros), however.(wiki:Moredo_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8)

In [138 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Moredo interred the bones of his dead [brother](wiki:Lysaro_Rogare) in the family crypt.(wiki:Moredo_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-9)

## Appearances & Description

Moredo was a tall and stern man with white-blond hair and blazing blue eyes. A soldier, he carried the [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) [longsword](wiki:Longsword) [Truth](wiki:Truth_(sword)). Unlike his younger brothers, [Roggerio](wiki:Roggerio_Rogare) and [Lotho](wiki:Lotho_Rogare), he was not fluent in the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue).(wiki:Moredo_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
