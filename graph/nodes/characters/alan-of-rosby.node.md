## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Alan survives the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist) during the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) and is among the survivors that make it to [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep).(wiki:Alan_of_Rosby.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) When [Craster](wiki:Craster) is hurting one of his younger daughters, Alan goes outside so he does not have to hear the girl's screams.(wiki:Alan_of_Rosby.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Alan remains at Craster's Keep after [the death](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep) of [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), along with a few other former members of the Night's Watch.(wiki:Alan_of_Rosby.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Dance With Dragons[ ]

Five betrayers wander in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest), planning to head south over the [Wall](wiki:Wall). However, the traitors are killed by [Coldhands](wiki:Coldhands) and his [ravens](wiki:Raven). [Summer](wiki:Summer), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark)'s [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), finds the bodies and eats parts of the meat.(wiki:Alan_of_Rosby.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) It is unknown if Alan is among them.
