### The Winds of Winter

Mary is held under gentle imprisonment after [Mistwood](wiki:Mistwood) falls during the [landing of the Golden Company](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company). She joins their captains, [Young John Mudd](wiki:Young_John_Mudd) and [Chain](wiki:Chain), at dinner when Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) passes through Mistwood on her way north to meet Lord [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington). Mary is annoyed at the situation but unafraid, scolding the captains at the table while wagging a leg of duck at them. She explains her sons and grandsons rode off to join [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) when the war started. A grandson was wounded at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), but he has since recovered. Mary expects her kin to return and hang the [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords). She says she knew Jon Connington's mother, and that she would be disappointed in him.(wiki:Mary_Mertyns.cite_ref-Ar2_3-2)
