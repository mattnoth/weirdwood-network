## Origins

In his youth, Forrest had once been a suitor to Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen). When Rhaenyra visited the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) in [112 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), he got dubbed "Fool Frey" for boldly asking openly for the [Princess of Dragonstone](wiki:Princess_of_Dragonstone)'s hand.(wiki:Forrest_Frey.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He later married Lady [Sabitha Vypren](wiki:Sabitha_Vypren),(wiki:Forrest_Frey.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) and became the [Lord of the Crossing](wiki:Lord_of_the_Crossing).(wiki:Forrest_Frey.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Lord Forrest was among the prominent riverlords that sided with Princess Rhaenyra and the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), despite initial reluctance among the riverlords.(wiki:Forrest_Frey.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Forrest added his strength to Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen)'s host and participated in [capturing](wiki:Taking_of_Stone_Hedge) [Stone Hedge](wiki:Stone_Hedge) from Lord [Humfrey Bracken](wiki:Humfrey_Bracken).(wiki:Forrest_Frey.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) He brought two hundred knights and six hundred infantry when he joined Lord [Roderick Dustin](wiki:Roderick_Dustin) and [Robb Rivers](wiki:Robb_Rivers) by the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye). In the ensuring battle, Forrest died fighting the remains of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister)'s army from the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) in the [Battle by the Lakeshore](wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore).(wiki:Forrest_Frey.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
His young son succeeded him as [Lord of the Crossing](wiki:Lord_of_the_Crossing) and his wife, Lady Sabitha, took command of the Frey levies.(wiki:Forrest_Frey.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
