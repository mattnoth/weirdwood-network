## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Black Lorren](wiki:Category:Images_of_Black_Lorren)*

Lorren is a squat, thick man with a long black beard.(wiki:Black_Lorren.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He wields axe and sword.(wiki:Black_Lorren.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Black Lorren and [Qarl the Maid](wiki:Qarl_the_Maid) are considered among the fiercest [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) warriors, behind only [Andrik the Unsmiling](wiki:Andrik_the_Unsmiling).(wiki:Black_Lorren.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Lorren is willing to die in battle and is contemptuous of underhanded tactics.(wiki:Black_Lorren.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Black Lorren is among the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) who participate in the [harrying of the Stony Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore), during which he and [Urzen](wiki:Urzen) silence animals too badly wounded to be saved. (wiki:Black_Lorren.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Lorren is part of [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy)'s force that [captures Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Black_Lorren.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) When Theon notices that [Summer](wiki:Summer) and [Shaggydog](wiki:Shaggydog) have gone missing, he tasks Lorren with making a round of the castle's gates. Lorren finds [Drennan](wiki:Drennan)'s body by the [Hunter's Gate](wiki:Hunter's_Gate), and Theon has him pull [Squint](wiki:Squint)'s body from the moat with a pike.(wiki:Black_Lorren.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Theon leaves Lorren as his castellan when he searches for [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood).(wiki:Black_Lorren.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

When [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) arrives from [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte), Black Lorren brings her to the [Great Hall of Winterfell](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell) for breakfast. He and Asha both believe that Theon should abandon Winterfell, but Theon refuses their advice.(wiki:Black_Lorren.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

When [northmen](wiki:Northmen) led by Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) arrive to retake Winterfell, Theon gathers the ironmen and offers them the choice of departing or staying to fight by his side. [Wex Pyke](wiki:Wex_Pyke), Theon's squire, is the first to step forward in reponse, and Black Lorren follows next, shamed and scowling. Lorren advises Theon to expel the smallfolk of Winterfell to prevent them from attacking the remaining defenders, but Theon refuses. Lorren offers to defend Winterfell's drawbridge against attacking northmen by himself, but he is again refused. Instead, Theon chooses to stay Rodrik's attack by threatening to have Lorren hang his daughter, [Beth Cassel](wiki:Beth_Cassel), during a parley; a disapproving Lorren views Theon with contempt.(wiki:Black_Lorren.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Black Lorren and Theon watch the [battle outside the gates of Winterfell](wiki:Battle_outside_the_gates_of_Winterfell) from the castle's battlements. Lorren mans the main gate when "Red Helm" has the bodies of Rodrik, [Leobald Tallhart](wiki:Leobald_Tallhart), and [Cley Cerwyn](wiki:Cley_Cerwyn) brought forward by men of the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort). Lorren, Theon, and Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin) meet the victors in the yard, where "Red Helm" reveals himself to be [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow). Lorren defends himself with his sword when four men from [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) attack him.(wiki:Black_Lorren.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

After the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell) by the Boltons, Bran Stark and his companions emerge from hiding in [the crypt](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell). They find Black Lorren's body in the yard, and [Osha](wiki:Osha) notices that he had been able to kill a few attackers before being slain.(wiki:Black_Lorren.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
