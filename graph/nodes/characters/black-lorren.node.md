## Identity

Black Lorren is a fierce ironborn warrior in the service of House Greyjoy (wiki:Black_Lorren).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Greyjoy (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` ironborn (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Black Lorren is described as a hard, lean man with a face that seldom smiles. He is considered one of the fiercest fighters among the ironborn at Winterfell (wiki:Black_Lorren.cite_ref-Racok46).

## Narrative Arc

Black Lorren accompanies Theon Greyjoy in the capture of Winterfell. Theon names him castellan of Winterfell when he rides out to deal with Rodrik Cassel's forces (wiki:Black_Lorren.cite_ref-Racok56). When Ramsay Bolton's forces sack Winterfell, Black Lorren is killed in the fighting (wiki:Black_Lorren.cite_ref-Racok69).
