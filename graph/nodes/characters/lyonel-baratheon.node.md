## Identity

Lyonel Baratheon, known as the Laughing Storm, was the Lord of Storm's End and head of House Baratheon during the reign of King Aegon V Targaryen (wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon).

## Origins

Born between 174 and 194 AC at Storm's End (track_b: Born).

## Appearances & Description

Lyonel had black hair and blue eyes, typical of House Baratheon. He was tall and powerfully built — "a swaggering giant of a man," nearly as tall as Ser Duncan the Tall (measured at 6'11"). In tourneys he wore a cloth-of-gold surcoat with the crowned stag and a helm with iron antlers. He had a habit of laughing loudly at opponents, earning his nickname and making him a great favorite of the smallfolk (wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon).

## Narrative Arc

At the tourney at Ashford Meadow in 209 AC, young Lyonel defeated Ser Robert Ashford after breaking nine lances and continuing the fight sword against mace. He became champion and fought many lesser foes, laughing as they struck his shield. When Prince Duncan Targaryen spurned Lyonel's daughter to elope with Jenny of Oldstones in 239 AC, Lyonel rebelled against the Iron Throne and briefly crowned himself Storm King. The rebellion was settled when the king's champion defeated Lyonel in single combat. King Aegon V then promised Princess Rhaelle Targaryen would marry Lyonel's heir Ormund. Lyonel died between 239 and 245 AC (wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon).
