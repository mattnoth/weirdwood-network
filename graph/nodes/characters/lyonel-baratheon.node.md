## Origins

### Ashford Tourney

According to a semi-canon source, Lyonel was heir to [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) when he participated in the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC).(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon.cite_ref-rollofarms_7-0) In the first [tilt](wiki:Joust), he challenged Ser [Robert Ashford](wiki:Robert_Ashford), one of the brothers and champions of the [fair maid](wiki:Lord_Ashford's_daughter). After breaking nine [lances](wiki:Lance), they both lost their saddles on their tenth course, only to rise together to fight on, [sword](wiki:Sword) against [mace](wiki:Mace). Finally a battered Robert admitted defeat, and Lyonel become a champion in his place.(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

Lyonel fought multiple matches against lesser foes, often breaking into booming laughter the moment they touched his shield. If his challengers wore any sort of crest on their [helm](wiki:Helm), Lyonel would strike it off and fling it into the crowd. This made him a great favorite of the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) since the crests were sometimes enameled or [gilded](wiki:Gold) or made of [silver](wiki:Silver). However, the men he defeated did not care for this behavior, and soon only crestless men were challenging him.(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

On the third day of the tourney, Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) was forced to defend himself in a [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven) against Prince [Aerion Targaryen](wiki:Aerion_Targaryen). Duncan's [squire](wiki:Squire), Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen), asked Lyonel to join on Duncan's side. Lyonel eagerly accepted the chance to participate in the first trial of seven in a hundred years. Lyonel [knighted](wiki:Knight) [Raymun Fossoway](wiki:Raymun_Fossoway) shortly before the start of the trial, stepping in when Duncan was hesitant to do so. During the trial, the Laughing Storm fought alongside Prince [Baelor Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)) against Prince [Maekar Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_Targaryen); Baelor Breakspear was mortally wounded during the fighting.(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

### Lord of Storm's End

As [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End), Lyonel was a leal supporter of King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen). In [237 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), the king was pleased to arrange a betrothal of his eldest son and heir, Prince [Duncan Targaryen](wiki:Duncan_Targaryen), to Lord Lyonel's daughter.(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

Prince Duncan later broke that betrothal, renouncing his claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), when he fell in love and married the commonborn [Jenny of Oldstones](wiki:Jenny_of_Oldstones). This enraged Lord Lyonel, who felt the honor of [House Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon) was insulted. He renounced his fealty to the throne and declared himself [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King). A [short and bloody rebellion](wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon's_rebellion) followed, which ended when Lyonel yielded to Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) during a [trial by battle](wiki:Trial_by_combat). King Aegon agreed to betroth his youngest daughter, Princess [Rhaelle Targaryen](wiki:Rhaelle_Targaryen), to Lyonel's heir, [Ormund Baratheon](wiki:Ormund_Baratheon). Aegon also sent Rhaelle to [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) to be Lyonel's [cupbearer](wiki:Cupbearer) and [companion](wiki:Handmaid) to his lady wife.(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6)(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon.cite_ref-9)

## Appearances & Description

Lyonel had black hair and blue eyes like his ancestors from [House Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon).(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rawoiaflyonel_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) He was tall and powerfully built,(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rawoiaflyonel_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) a "swaggering giant of a man".(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Lyonel is described as being a head taller than Ser [Raymun Fossoway](wiki:Raymun_Fossoway) and almost as tall as Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall),(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) who was measured at 6'11".(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

In [tourneys](wiki:Tourney), Lyonel wore a cloth-of-[gold](wiki:Gold) surcoat bearing the crowned [stag](wiki:Stag) of his house. His helm had a rack of [iron](wiki:Iron) antlers.(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Lyonel was considered one of the finest fighters of his day,(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) and was famed for his prowess in battle.(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) Lyonel had the habit of laughing loudly at his opponents, as well as in general, which earned him his nickname of "the Laughing Storm" and made him a great favorite of the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk).(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

According to Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel), Lyonel brought glory to [House Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon).(wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)
