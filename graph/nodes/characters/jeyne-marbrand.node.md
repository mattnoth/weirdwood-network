## Origins

### Early life

The daughter of [Alyn Marbrand](wiki:Alyn_Marbrand),(wiki:Jeyne_Marbrand.cite_ref-6) Lord of [Ashemark](wiki:Ashemark), Jeyne was married to [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister) in [235 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), in a double ceremony with Tytos's older brother, Ser [Tion](wiki:Tion_Lannister), who married Lady [Ellyn Reyne](wiki:Ellyn_Reyne).(wiki:Jeyne_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### Lady of Casterly Rock

Tion died the following year, however, leaving Ellyn a widow. With her influence waning at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), Ellyn tried to seduce Tytos, the new heir, to convince him to set Jeyne aside. He did not go through with the act, but went to Jeyne and confessed it all. Jeyne forgave her husband, but not Ellyn. Jeyne told Lord [Gerold Lannister](wiki:Gerold_Lannister), who married off Ellyn to Lord [Walderan Tarbeck](wiki:Walderan_Tarbeck), thereby removing her from Casterly Rock. [Lord Toad](wiki:Lord_Toad), the [fool](wiki:Fool) at the Rock, called the resulting bitter rivalry between Jeyne and Ellyn the [War of the Wombs](wiki:War_of_the_Wombs).(wiki:Jeyne_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Jeyne gave birth to five children in total. In [242 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), she birthed Tytos his heir, a boy named [Tywin](wiki:Tywin_Lannister). Two years later, she gave birth to a second son, [Kevan](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), and the year after a daughter, [Genna](wiki:Genna_Lannister). Jeyne also had another son, [Tygett](wiki:Tygett_Lannister).(wiki:Jeyne_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Tytos became Lord of Casterly Rock, and Jeyne its lady, with the death of Lord Gerold in [244 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Jeyne_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) According to semi-canon sources, most believed Jeyne was the one to suggest to her lord husband the [marriage](wiki:Marriage) of her brother-in-law, [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold)), to [Alys Stackspear](wiki:Alys_Stackspear) after he impregnated her.(wiki:Jeyne_Marbrand.cite_ref-westerlands_5-2)

Jeyne's reactions to Tytos's rule are not known, nor the reaction to her daughter's early betrothal to [Emmon Frey](wiki:Emmon_Frey) in [252 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). Giving birth in [255 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) for the fifth time, Jeyne delivered a healthy son, [Gerion](wiki:Gerion_Lannister), but she died within a month. While Tytos had always been known as "the Laughing Lion", after Jeyne's death no one could call him that anymore.(wiki:Jeyne_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

## Appearances & Description

According to semi-canon sources, Jeyne was a "solemn, shy, plain-faced child" who blossomed into a great beauty.(wiki:Jeyne_Marbrand.cite_ref-westerlands_5-0)
