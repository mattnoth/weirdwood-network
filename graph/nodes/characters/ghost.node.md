## Identity

Ghost is an albino direwolf bonded to Jon Snow (track_b: Owner). He is the litter-mate of Grey Wind, Lady, Nymeria, Summer, and Shaggydog. Born in 298 AC in the north (track_b: Born). Ghost is distinguished from his siblings by his white fur, red eyes, and complete silence — he never howls or makes a sound (wiki:Ghost).

## Origins

Ghost was discovered by the Stark children after his mother, a direwolf, was killed by a stag north of Winterfell. Ghost had wandered away from the litter unnoticed and was found separately by Jon Snow, the only human to hear him. Theon Greyjoy wanted to kill the albino pup, but Jon claimed him (wiki:Ghost).

## Allegiances

`OWNS`: Jon Snow owns Ghost (track_b: Owner). Ghost accompanies Jon to the Wall and serves alongside him in the Night's Watch throughout.

## Appearances & Description

Ghost is an albino direwolf — pure white fur with red eyes. He is notably silent, never howling or barking. He grows to be very large, described as standing on hind legs tall enough to scrabble at doors. His silence makes him an effective scout and sentinel (wiki:Ghost).

## Narrative Arc

**AGOT:** Ghost accompanies Jon to the Wall. He defends his meal from larger dogs at the Winterfell feast. At Castle Black, Jon uses Ghost to protect Sam from bullying. Beyond the Wall, Ghost tears the hand from the dead Jafer Flowers, then alerts Jon to the wight attack on Mormont's tower, saving the Lord Commander. Jon receives the Valyrian steel sword Longclaw, reforged with a white wolf pommel in Ghost's likeness. When Jon deserts, Ghost disappears near Mole's Town then betrays his hiding spot so friends can bring him back (wiki:Ghost, agot chapters 1–70).

**ACOK:** During the great ranging, Ghost hunts away from the column and discovers a cache of dragonglass weapons at the Fist of the First Men. He accompanies Qhorin Halfhand's scouting mission. Jon begins experiencing warg dreams through Ghost. During Jon's forced duel with Qhorin, Ghost bites the Halfhand's calf, enabling Jon to kill him (wiki:Ghost, acok chapters 13–68).

**ASOS:** Ghost keeps Ygritte at bay before she seduces Jon. Jon sends Ghost toward Castle Black before climbing the Wall with Styr's group. Ghost is missing for much of the book. He emerges from the haunted forest when Jon ventures north of the Wall after the battle, and their reunion helps Jon decline Stannis's offer of Winterfell. Jon is then chosen Lord Commander (wiki:Ghost, asos chapters 9–79).

**ADWD:** Ghost remains at Castle Black with Jon as Lord Commander. Jon uses Ghost to intimidate Janos Slynt before executing him. The direwolf is confined more often as Jon's duties grow. After Jon is stabbed by his own men in the mutiny, his last word is "Ghost" (wiki:Ghost, adwd chapters 3–69).
