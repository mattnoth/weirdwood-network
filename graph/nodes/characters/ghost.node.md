## Appearances & Description

Ghost is an albino,(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) with thick white fur,(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) red eyes,(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and a bushy tail.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) At the outset, Ghost is the smallest of his pack, but he eventually grows to be larger than his litter mates.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Ragot10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) He receives [his name](wiki:Ghosts) from [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) because of his fur color and the fact that he never makes a sound.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Ragot5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) The [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) does not whine or howl,(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Rasos26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) but sometimes bares his teeth in a silent snarl.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Jon has noted that Ghost's red eyes are similar to the faces on [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) [heart trees](wiki:Heart_tree) carved by the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest).(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Ghost enjoys tugging Jon's hand back and forth as a game,(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) and also enjoys rolling in fresh snow.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Along with his litter-mates, Ghost is discovered by the [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) children after his mother was killed by a [stag](wiki:Stag). Ghost was the first pup to open his eyes and had wandered off unnoticed by the time the Starks arrived. [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) convinces his father, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), to allow his children to keep the pups by noting that the five remaining pups match the five Stark children, excluding himself. Only afterwards does Jon hear Ghost nearby, the only one of the humans to do so. [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) wants to kill the albino pup, but Jon claims him.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

During [the feast](wiki:Feast_in_honor_of_King_Robert's_visit_to_Winterfell) for King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), the young Ghost defends his meal from a bitch three times his size.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Ragot5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Ghost accompanies Jon to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) when the youth joins the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). The direwolf joins Jon when he hunts for food for [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch),(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) and Ghost cheers [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) by licking away the boy's tears.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Jon threatens [Rast](wiki:Rast) with Ghost to prevent further bullying of Sam.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

At the [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) grove [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall), Ghost tears off the right hand of the dead [Jafer Flowers](wiki:Jafer_Flowers). The growing direwolf wakes Jon by scratching at his door that night, alerting Jon to the presence of [wights](wiki:Wights), and they are able to save Lord Commander Mormont from the undead [Othor](wiki:Othor). Ghost bites the wight's severed arm and tears into its belly.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) In gratitude, Jeor gifts Jon with the [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) sword [Longclaw](wiki:Longclaw), having replaced the ruined bear's head pommel with that of a white wolf's head in the likeness of Ghost, although Jon believes Ghost is the one who deserves to be honored.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

When Jon deserts the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), Ghost disappears near [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town). The direwolf later returns and betrays the hiding Jon's position so that his friends can bring him back to Castle Black.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Clash of Kings

During the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), Ghost hunts well away from the main [column](wiki:Column) of Night's Watch,(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Racok13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) often at night.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) is frightened by the direwolf, who kills two rabbits at [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep). Ghost gives one of the rabbits to Jon instead of [Lark](wiki:Lark).(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

Ghost discovers a cache of [dragonglass](wiki:Dragonglass) weapons near the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men) and leads Jon to it.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Racok34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

Ghost accompanies Jon during [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand)'s scouting mission into the [Frostfangs](wiki:Frostfangs). Sometimes during sleep, Jon inhabits Ghost's body through a [warg](wiki:Warg) bond, although he does not seem to fully understand it at first. He dreams of his lost siblings, and Jon hears Ghost howl mournfully in one dream.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Racok53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) The [eagle](wiki:Eagle) of [Orell](wiki:Orell) gashes Ghost with its talons, but is unable to break the direwolf's neck.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Racok53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1) Jon is captured by [Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt)'s [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) in the [Skirling Pass](wiki:Skirling_Pass). When Qhorin forces Jon into a duel, Ghost bites the Halfhand's calf and Jon uses the distraction to kill the ranger.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Racok68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

### A Storm of Swords

After Jon is brought to [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder)'s host, the youth uses Ghost to keep [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte) away from him at night. Ygritte eventually seduces Jon, however.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

Before ascending the [Wall](wiki:Wall) with [Styr](wiki:Styr)'s group, Jon sends Ghost away with instructions to go to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Rasos26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) He does not know if Ghost understands his instructions and does not hear of Ghost's whereabouts for a great while. By the time Jon returns to Castle Black, he seems to have attained at least a tentative acceptance of his warg powers.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) Ghost is still missing beyond the Wall, however, and Jon cannot sense him.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-interview_24-0)

In the aftermath of the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), Jon is reluctant to accept [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s offer to become [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell), as doing so would require the burning of the [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree) in the [castle's godswood](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell). Ghost emerges from the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest) when Jon ventures north of the Wall, and they are reunited to their mutual joy. Jon sees it as a sign to decline Stannis's offer, and he is then surprised by being chosen as the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch).(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2)

### A Feast for Crows

When Sam Tarly is brought before Lord Commander Snow in the armory of Castle Black, he sees Ghost gnawing on an ox bone trying to get at its marrow.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ghost sleeps on a rug in Jon's new quarters, once those of [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye) in the armory.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) When Jon has a [wolf dream](wiki:Wolf_dream), he senses that the direwolf knows that two of his litter-mates have died. Jon dreams Ghost sensing [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf)) sing to the [moon](wiki:Moon) with her pack of [wolves](wiki:Wolf) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), and [Shaggydog](wiki:Shaggydog) fighting a [unicorn](wiki:Unicorn) on [Skagos](wiki:Skagos). Even though [Summer](wiki:Summer) is alive, Ghost cannot sense him, and understands this is because Summer is [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall). When Jon wakes, he realizes that Ghost knows [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind) is dead.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-1)

Ghost seems to like [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre)(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) and [Val](wiki:Val),(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) and the former warns Jon to keep Ghost close.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-2)(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) Ghost accompanies Jon to the weirwood grove north of the Wall, where they meet [Wun Wun](wiki:Wun_Wun) and a group of [free folk](wiki:Free_folk).(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

Ghost growls at the giant [boar](wiki:Boar) later brought by the skinchanger [Borroq](wiki:Borroq) to Castle Black, so Jon keeps Ghost locked up in his quarters. Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) also wants Ghost to be penned up, claiming the direwolf scares Princess [Shireen Baratheon](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon). An angry Ghost bites at [Mully](wiki:Mully), and sniffs at [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh), but Jon attributes his agitation to Borroq's boar, and keeps Ghost within his chambers.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

While being stabbed by [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) and his cronies in the [mutiny at Castle Black](wiki:Mutiny_at_Castle_Black), Jon whispers *"Ghost"* as his last words.(wiki:Ghost.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-1)
