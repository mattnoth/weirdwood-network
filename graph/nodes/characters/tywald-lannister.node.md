## Origins

Tywald was the eldest son of [Gerold the Golden](wiki:Gerold_Lannister), Lord of [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), and Lady [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber), his second wife. In his youth, Tywald was betrothed to [Ellyn Reyne](wiki:Ellyn_Reyne) and was a squire to her father, Lord [Robert Reyne](wiki:Robert_Reyne) of [Castamere](wiki:Castamere).

During the [Peake Uprising](wiki:Peake_Uprising) in [233 AC](wiki:233_AC), Tywald fought on the side of King [Maekar I Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen), but he was mortally wounded by a spear at [Starpike](wiki:Storming_of_Starpike). Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) knighted the squire, while Tywald laid dying in his twin brother [Tion's](wiki:Tion_Lannister) arms. According to a semi-canon source, Tywald asked his brother to "take care of Lady Ellyn" with his final words. This later led Lord Gerold to agree to a betrothal between Tion and Ellyn.(wiki:Tywald_Lannister.cite_ref-4)(wiki:Tywald_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
