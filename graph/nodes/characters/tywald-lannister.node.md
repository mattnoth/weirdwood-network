## Identity

Ser Tywald Lannister was a knight from House Lannister, the eldest son and heir of Lord Gerold Lannister and Lady Rohanne Webber, and the twin of Tion (wiki:Tywald_Lannister).

## Origins

Born between 211–219 AC (track_b: Born). Son of Gerold Lannister and Rohanne Webber (track_b: Father, Mother). Died in 233 AC at Starpike (track_b: Died).

## Narrative Arc

Maester Yandel refers to Tywald and Tion as Gerold's "glorious twins" (wiki:Tywald_Lannister). In his youth, Tywald was betrothed to Ellyn Reyne and served as squire to her father Lord Robert Reyne of Castamere (wiki:Tywald_Lannister).

During the Peake Uprising in 233 AC, Tywald fought for King Maekar I Targaryen but was mortally wounded by a spear at Starpike. Prince Aegon Targaryen knighted the squire while Tywald lay dying in his twin Tion's arms. According to semi-canon sources, Tywald's last words asked his brother to "take care of Lady Ellyn," which later led Lord Gerold to agree to a betrothal between Tion and Ellyn (wiki:Tywald_Lannister.cite_ref-TWOIAF).
