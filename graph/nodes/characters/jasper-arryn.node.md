## Identity

Jasper Arryn was Lord of the Eyrie, Defender of the Vale, Warden of the East, and head of House Arryn (wiki:Jasper_Arryn).

## Narrative Arc

Jasper was the father of Jon, Alys, and Ronnel Arryn. While Jasper lived, his eldest son Jon served as Keeper of the Gates of the Moon. After Jasper's death, Jon inherited the lordship (wiki:Jasper_Arryn).
