## Appearances & Description

Salloreon is an older man. His rich attire includes a damask tunic with [silver](wiki:Silver) fastenings and a cloak lined with foxfur.(wiki:Salloreon.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Salloreon is one of the smiths approached by [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to build his monstrous [steel](wiki:Steel) chain. He complains about being given work only fit for a common smith, and offers to forge Tyrion a suit of [armor](wiki:Armor) and a [demon](wiki:Demon)'s head helm. Tyrion replies that he can either make chains or wear them.(wiki:Salloreon.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

The armorer joins the [Antler Men](wiki:Antler_Men), a group of rich merchants, tradesman, and crafters who conspire to aid [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) in taking [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) from King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). Tyrion orders his arrest.(wiki:Salloreon.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), captured Antler Men have antlers nailed to their heads and are flung over the walls of King's Landing by the three great trebuchets called the [Three Whores](wiki:Three_Whores).(wiki:Salloreon.cite_ref-Racok59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
