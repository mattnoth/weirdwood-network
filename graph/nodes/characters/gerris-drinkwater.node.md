## Origins

Gerris once led [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) and some other friends into the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) to find the lair of the [Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King).(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) Quentyn's first kiss was with one of Gerris's [twin sisters](wiki:Drinkwater_twins).(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Category:Images_of_Gerris_Drinkwater)*

Gerris is tall and lean and comely, with blue-green eyes and sandy, sun-streaked hair.(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) He has a swordsman's grace and a courtier's wit.(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) His confidence is often close to arrogance.(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) Gerris does not speak the [Volantene](wiki:Volantis) dialect of [High Valyrian](wiki:High_Valyrian) as well as [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell).(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

Gerris wears an expensive cloak of soft brown wool lined with sandsilk.(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) thinks Gerris is shallow(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) and a worse warrior than Ser [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood).(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser Gerris accompanies Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) on his mission to wed [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen). They are forced to fend off [corsairs](wiki:Corsairs) when the *[Meadowlark](wiki:Meadowlark)* is attacked near the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands).(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)

When they reach [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), Gerris pretends to be a wine merchant looking for a ship to take him and his two servants to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), where Daenerys has become queen. The only captain to accept them is the master of *[Adventure](wiki:Adventure)*, but the [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) fear he will betray them.(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7) Posing as poor exiles, they instead join the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown), who have been hired by [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) for the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen).(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Having sold their fine armor in Volantis, the Dornish receive used equipment from the Windblown, with Gerris's inferior to that of his companions, Quentyn and Ser [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood). Among the [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords) Gerris is known as Dornish Gerrold or Drink, after Archibald accidentally calls him that.(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

The Windblown sail from Volantis and fight new [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) in the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor).(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) The three [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) plan to desert after they reach Yunkai, but the [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince), the commander of the Windblown, summons them with other exiles from the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) or sellswords of [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) descent. The Tattered Prince tasks the group of twenty with turning their cloak from Yunkai and joining the service of Daenerys. With this move the Tattered Prince wants to make sure that if the siege turns unfortunate he will be still on the winning side.(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Once the Dornishmen reach Meereen(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) and present themselves to Daenerys, Gerris is the first one to say his real name. He becomes angry when [Daario Naharis](wiki:Daario_Naharis) mocks Quentyn, and the knight unsuccessfully tries to convince Daenerys not to wed [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq).(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) The Dornishmen attend Hizdahr's feast but are seated far from the king. Quentyn refuses Daenerys's suggestion that he leave Meereen.(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

After Daenerys flees from [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit) atop [Drogon](wiki:Drogon),(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) considers that his queen may not have wed Hizdahr if Gerris had been the Dornish prince instead of the less handsome Quentyn. Barristan advises the three Dornishmen to return to Westeros and forget about the [secret marriage pact](wiki:Secret_marriage_pact), but Gerris, who is unafraid of Hizdahr, turns down the advice of the old knight.(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

Quentyn hopes that since he has partial [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) ancestry, he will be able to tame at least one of Daenerys's [dragons](wiki:Dragon), two of which are imprisoned in the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid). Although Gerris tries to dissuade the prince, Quentyn is determined. In return for [Pentos](wiki:Pentos), the Tattered Prince agrees to have the Windblown aid Quentyn.(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Gerris saves Quentyn from a guard's spear when the Dornishmen and Windblown infiltrate the Great Pyramid. When Quentyn attempts to tame [Viserion](wiki:Viserion), Gerris and Archibald try to warn him of the other dragon, [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal), whose [flame](wiki:Dragonfire) burns the prince.(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) The Windblown flee, but Gerris and Archibald are arrested by [Brazen Beasts](wiki:Brazen_Beasts), with Gerris dropping his sword the moment the guards appear.(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

Quentyn succumbs to his wounds after three days.(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) Since Rhaegal and Viserion have been set loose, [Symon Stripeback](wiki:Symon_Stripeback) and [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) want Gerris and Archibald to be hanged while the [Spotted Cat](wiki:Spotted_Cat) wants to slay them in the [fighting pits of Meereen](wiki:Fighting_pits_of_Meereen).(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3) When Barristan comes to see the Dornish prisoners, Gerris blames Daenerys for what had happened with Quentyn, saying that her actions forced the prince to risk his life in order to win her. Barristan tells them that they are to be hanged but he could instead arrange a ship for them to take Quentyn's bones to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). In return, the [Hand of the Queen](wiki:Hand_of_the_Queen) wants the Dornishmen to inform the Tattered Prince that Barristan will pay his price of Pentos if the hostages in the Yunkish camp are returned to Meereen. Although Gerris is skeptical, Archibald accepts for both of them.(wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-4)
