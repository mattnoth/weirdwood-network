## Origins

Rolly was born in the [Reach](wiki:Reach). His father was a [blacksmith](wiki:Blacksmith) at [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge) in service to old [Lord Caswell](wiki:Lord_Caswell). Rolly's strength and skill with a blade earned him a place in the castle garrison, but Rolly had dreams of [knighthood](wiki:Knight). He resented seeing Lord Caswell's weakling son [Lorent](wiki:Lorent_Caswell) become a page, a squire, then a knight. Lord Caswell had only one son, so no one in the castle was allowed to say a word against Lorent. In the yard the other squires barely touched him.(wiki:Rolly_Duckfield.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

On his sixteenth name day, Rolly's father made a longsword for Rolly as a present, but Lorent Caswell saw it and claimed it for his own, saying to Rolly that he was only fit to wield a hammer, not a sword. Rolly then fetched a hammer and broke both of Lorent's arms and half his ribs. Afterwards, Rolly quickly fled across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) to escape Lord Caswell's wrath. He joined the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) as an apprentice smith for a few years, and then served Ser [Harry Strickland](wiki:Harry_Strickland) as a [squire](wiki:Squire).(wiki:Rolly_Duckfield.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

When [Griff](wiki:Jon_Connington) needed someone to train his son [Young Griff](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(Young_Griff)) in skill at arms, Harry Strickland sent him Rolly. A year later Rolly was knighted by Griff. Having no surname, Rolly chose the name Duckfield because he was in a field with some [ducks](wiki:Duck) when he was knighted, earning him the nickname "Duck".(wiki:Rolly_Duckfield.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Rolly meets [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis)'s litter and escorts [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to meet the rest of the party. They set sail on the *[Shy Maid](wiki:Shy_Maid)* to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis).(wiki:Rolly_Duckfield.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) Young Griff is revealed to be [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(Young_Griff)), and Griff to be [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington),(wiki:Rolly_Duckfield.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) and they are supported by the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company).(wiki:Rolly_Duckfield.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Duck and Jon participate in the [landing of the Golden Company](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company) in the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands). Duck is named to the new [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) by Aegon, but Jon does not approve of the choice. He thinks Duck is a solid man and true, but not worthy of the Kingsguard, and that they should be reserving positions for famous warriors and younger sons of great lords whose support they will need in the fight for the throne. However, Aegon feels that it is most important that Rolly will die for him, citing the [Kingslayer](wiki:Kingslayer), Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), as an example that matches Jon's preferences.(wiki:Rolly_Duckfield.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
