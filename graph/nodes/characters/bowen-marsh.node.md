## Appearances & Description

Bowen is round and red as a pomegranate,(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Ragot21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) with plump hands,(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) round cheeks,(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and a red face.(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The [Lord Steward](wiki:Lord_Steward) has a mind for counts and measures.(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) He wears a bearskin(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) and carries a dagger.(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

As Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) is known as the "Old Bear", [Edd Tollett](wiki:Edd_Tollett) refers to Bowen as the "Old Pomegranate".(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Although he is a [northman](wiki:North), Bowen appears to follow the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Jeor does not feel that Bowen would make a suitable replacement as Lord Commander.(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Ragot21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Bowen is one of the guests at the dinner organized by [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), on the eve of [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s departure.(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Ragot21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

The [Lord Steward](wiki:Lord_Steward) accompanies [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) and [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), who have been assigned as [stewards](wiki:Stewards), when they pronounce their vows in the [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) circle found [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest).(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

### A Storm of Swords

Bowen is assigned as [castellan](wiki:Castellan) of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) by Lord Commander Mormont while the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) takes place. He sends letters to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), requesting assistance for the Night's Watch.(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

After Jeor Mormont's [death at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep), Bowen temporarily takes command of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) until the election of a new Lord Commander. [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder), [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall), sends groups of wildlings to attack along the [Wall](wiki:Wall), feints to distract the Watch. When Bowen obliges by leading a force west to deal with the wildlings, he appoints the ancient Ser [Wynton Stout](wiki:Wynton_Stout) to command Castle Black in his absence.(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

The Lord Steward chases wildlings to the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) and into the [Gorge](wiki:Gorge). He defeats the [Weeper](wiki:Weeper) in a [fight at the Bridge of Skulls](wiki:Fight_at_the_Bridge_of_Skulls), but the Night's Watch suffers heavy losses.(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Bowen suffers a head wound(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) and is treated by Maester [Mullin](wiki:Mullin) at the Shadow Tower.(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

Bowen and the survivors from the Bridge of Skulls are brought back to Castle Black by Ser [Denys Mallister](wiki:Denys_Mallister). While riding east, they meet and join with Sam Tarly and [Gilly](wiki:Gilly).(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)

Bowen is a candidate in the [election](wiki:Election) of a new Lord Commander. The Lord Steward withdraws his name by the third day as his vote totals dwindle, however, and he instead begins supporting [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt),(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-2) who is favored by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) The castellan objects to King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s desire to take castles along the Wall and land in the [Gift](wiki:Gift) from the Watch, and he is wary of Stannis's and [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre)'s intention for nightfires to be lit at each castle.(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) Bowen attempts to sway his sworn brothers and Stannis of Janos's worth,(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2) but Jon Snow is eventually chosen instead.(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Bowen returns to his duties as Lord Steward.(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Bowen has lost weight and part of an ear after his battle at the Bridge of Skulls,(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) occasionally rubbing at his scar,(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) and he has become more cautious.(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Radwd21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Bowen is at first helpful to Jon, but he becomes increasingly angry with his decisions. These include appointing a [baseborn](wiki:Baseborn) lad, [Satin](wiki:Satin), as the Lord Commander's new steward,(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) appointing the wildling [Leathers](wiki:Leathers) as the new [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms) at Castle Black,(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) and allowing wildlings to join the Watch and diminish the food supply.(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1)(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) Bowen is shocked when Jon executes Janos for disobedience.(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

Supported by most stewards and [builders](wiki:Builders) of the Watch,(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Bowen suggests sealing the gate through the Wall at Castle Black, but his advice is rejected by Jon.(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-2) He tries to discourage the Lord Commander from leading some recruits to recite their vows in the haunted forest, suggesting Castle Black's [sept](wiki:Sept) instead.(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Bowen refuses to attend the wedding of [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark) and [Sigorn](wiki:Sigorn).(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

Jon tasks Bowen with collecting tolls from wildlings passing through the Wall and sending the riches to [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea).(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3)(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

The final straw for Bowen is when Jon reads aloud [a letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) sent by [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) and states his intention to march on [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), threatening the neutrality of the Night's Watch. A crying Bowen and fellow conspirators stab Jon several times in the [mutiny at Castle Black](wiki:Mutiny_at_Castle_Black).(wiki:Bowen_Marsh.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)
