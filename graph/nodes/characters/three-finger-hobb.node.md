## Origins

According to Hobb, he joined the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) so he could kill [wildlings](wiki:Free_folk).(wiki:Three-Finger_Hobb.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Appearances & Description

Hobb's nickname suggests one of his hands is maimed. The cook is famous for this three-meat stew, which members of the Watch say consists of mutton, mutton, and mutton.(wiki:Three-Finger_Hobb.cite_ref-Radwd21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) His manner of speaking suggests he is [lowborn](wiki:Lowborn).(wiki:Three-Finger_Hobb.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Three-Finger Hobb cooks a special meal for the eight recruits ([Todder](wiki:Todder), [Halder](wiki:Halder), [Grenn](wiki:Grenn), [Dareon](wiki:Dareon), [Albett](wiki:Albett), [Pypar](wiki:Pypar), [Matthar](wiki:Matthar) and [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow)) ready to become men of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) and swear their oaths. They enjoy "rack of lamb baked in a crust of garlic and herbs, garnished with sprigs of mint, and surrounded by mashed yellow turnips swimming in butter." Hobb also prepares "salads of spinach and chickpeas and turnip greens, and afterward bowls of iced blueberries and sweet cream."(wiki:Three-Finger_Hobb.cite_ref-Ragot41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

After [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) learns that Jon's father, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), has been [imprisoned in King's Landing](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark), Hobb gives Jon an extra helping of [venison](wiki:Deer) stew.(wiki:Three-Finger_Hobb.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

[Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), complains to Jon that the last mutton served by Hobb was grey.(wiki:Three-Finger_Hobb.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) On a later occasion, Hobb's breakfast for Jeor includes "three brown eggs boiled hard, with fried bread and ham steak and a bowl of wrinkled plums", as well as a [lemon](wiki:Lemon) for the Lord Commander's [beer](wiki:Beer).(wiki:Three-Finger_Hobb.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords

While part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) thinks fondly of Hobb's "thick cream of wheat, with a big spoon of butter melting in the middle and a dollop of honey".(wiki:Three-Finger_Hobb.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Three-Finger Hobb is among those left behind by [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) when the [First Steward](wiki:First_Steward) sets off after [wildling](wiki:Wildling) raiders.(wiki:Three-Finger_Hobb.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Women and children from [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town) help Hobb in the kitchen.(wiki:Three-Finger_Hobb.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Hobb and his helpers stay safe atop the [Wall](wiki:Wall) during the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) by [Styr](wiki:Styr),(wiki:Three-Finger_Hobb.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) and he gives black bread and onion broth to the defenders when they oppose [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder)'s wildlings.(wiki:Three-Finger_Hobb.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Following the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), Hobb is among the candidates at the [choosing](wiki:Choosing) of the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch). He promises roast haunch of [mammoth](wiki:Mammoth) in hopes of gaining votes.(wiki:Three-Finger_Hobb.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Jon Snow is eventually chosen, however.(wiki:Three-Finger_Hobb.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Hobb gives Samwell salted ham for his [name day](wiki:Name_day).(wiki:Three-Finger_Hobb.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Three-Finger Hobb gathers with two score other black brothers near the gate of Castle Black after word spreads that the heads of three [rangers](wiki:Rangers) have been found impaled on spikes on the other side of the Wall. Lord Commander Snow verifies that [Jack Bulwer](wiki:Jack_Bulwer), [Hairy Hal](wiki:Hairy_Hal), and [Garth Greyfeather](wiki:Garth_Greyfeather) have been slain by the [Weeper](wiki:Weeper). A friend of Garth's, [Alf of Runnymudd](wiki:Alf_of_Runnymudd), breaks down at the news, and Hobb is ordered by Jon to get Alf to bed and give him mulled [wine](wiki:Wine).(wiki:Three-Finger_Hobb.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

[Devan Seaworth](wiki:Devan_Seaworth) delivers fresh bread from Hobb's ovens to [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre).(wiki:Three-Finger_Hobb.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)

Three-Finger Hobb prepares [elk](wiki:Elk) and roast vegetables for [the feast](wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark) after [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark) weds [Sigorn](wiki:Sigorn); Jon is impressed with the meal, as Hobb has no experience in cooking for wildlings or preparing for wedding feasts. The cook also mulls wine with cinnamon and cloves.(wiki:Three-Finger_Hobb.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
