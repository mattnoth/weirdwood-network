## Origins

Mullin was the maester at the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) when [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) was stationed at the castle. When Mance was wounded by a [shadowcat](wiki:Shadowcat) while ranging [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall), his fellow [rangers](wiki:Rangers) took him to a nearby [wildling](wiki:Wildling) village for treatment because they feared Mance would die before they could return to the Shadow Tower for Mullin's healing.(wiki:Mullin.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
