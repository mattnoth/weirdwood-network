## Appearances & Description

Patrek enjoys drink, women, and [hawking](wiki:Hawk).(wiki:Patrek_Mallister.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He is good friends with Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully), the heir to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Patrek_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Patrek attends the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) with his father, Lord [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister). They pass [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) and Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) during their journey to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), but do not recognize the disguised travelers.(wiki:Patrek_Mallister.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Patrek is a member of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s [personal guard](wiki:Robb_Stark's_battle_guard) during the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Patrek_Mallister.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Clash of Kings

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) helps Patrek and his father bring hostage [westermen](wiki:Westerlands) to the Mallister seat of [Seagard](wiki:Seagard).(wiki:Patrek_Mallister.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Patrek gets along well with Theon during their journey, so his father, Lord Jason, reminds him that Seagard was built to keep out the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn). The following day, however, Patrek and Theon drink apple [wine](wiki:Wine) and visit the amorous young wife of a miller whom Patrek knows.(wiki:Patrek_Mallister.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Theon then sails from Seagard to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) as an envoy for Robb, now the [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Patrek_Mallister.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Following the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords), Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) spends much of his time with his friends, Ser [Marq Piper](wiki:Marq_Piper) and Patrek.(wiki:Patrek_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Edmure becomes [Lord of Riverrun](wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun) after the death of his father, Lord [Hoster](wiki:Hoster_Tully).(wiki:Patrek_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Patrek is among the nobles who accompany Edmure to the [Twins](wiki:Twins) for his wedding to [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey).(wiki:Patrek_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) He serves as one of Robb's guards for the evening, abstaining from drink during the wedding feast. When it is time for Edmure and Roslin to depart the hall for the [customary](wiki:Custom) [bedding](wiki:Bedding), Patrek proposes a toast to Edmure's one-eyed fish.(wiki:Patrek_Mallister.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Unlike most of Robb's party, Patrek survives the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) and is taken captive by [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey) at the Twins.(wiki:Patrek_Mallister.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Patrek is brought by [Black Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Ryman)) to the [siege of Seagard](wiki:Siege_of_Seagard), where Walder threatens to hang him if Lord Jason does not surrender the castle. Once Jason yields, he and his son are held captive within their castle.(wiki:Patrek_Mallister.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Patrek_Mallister.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

After Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) negotiates an end to the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), he informs [Edwyn Frey](wiki:Edwyn_Frey) that King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) requires all captives taken during the Red Wedding, which displeases the Freys.(wiki:Patrek_Mallister.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
