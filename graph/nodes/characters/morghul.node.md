## Origins

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and her [blacks](wiki:Blacks) [took](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing) [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) from [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) and his [greens](wiki:Greens). Morghul's owner, Princess [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen), was successfully spirited away from the capital, however.(wiki:Morghul.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Morgul stayed in the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit) alongside [Dreamfyre](wiki:Dreamfyre), [Tyraxes](wiki:Tyraxes), [Shrykos](wiki:Shrykos), and [Seasmoke](wiki:Seasmoke) (before he fled the city with his ridder, Ser [Addam Velaryon](wiki:Addam_Velaryon)) during Rhaenyra's half-year reign over the capital. Overtime Rhaenyra's relations with the citizens of the city deteriorated to the point of [riots](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)). In one of these riots a one-handed prophet called the [Shepherd](wiki:Shepherd_(one-handed_prophet)) began to rant against the [dragons](wiki:Dragons) and convinced the mob of Kingslanders to go to the Dragonpit and kill them all. Despite being shackled by heavy chains, Morghul slew scores of people with its [fire](wiki:Dragonflame). A huge man in heavy [armor](wiki:Armament) remember as the "[Burning Knight](wiki:Burning_Knight)" is credited for killing of Morghul. It is said he thrusted his spear through Morghul's eye as the dragon bathed him with fire. Morghul died never having been ridden.(wiki:Morghul.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
