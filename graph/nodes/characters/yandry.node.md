## Identity

Yandry is the master and captain of the poleboat Shy Maid. He is married to Ysilla (wiki:Yandry, cite: ADWD Tyrion III).

Yandry is an orphan of the Greenblood who has returned to Mother Rhoyne. He has a Rhoynish cast to his features (wiki:Yandry, cite: ADWD Tyrion III).
