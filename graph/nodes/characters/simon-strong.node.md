## Origins

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Simon [surrendered Harrenhal](wiki:Assault_on_Harrenhal) at the sight of [Caraxes](wiki:Caraxes), Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen)'s [dragon](wiki:Dragon). Simon and his grandsons were then held as [hostages](wiki:Hostage) by Daemon.(wiki:Simon_Strong.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Simon was freed from captivity when Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole), [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), [recovered](wiki:Aemond's_march_on_Harrenhal) the castle. However, Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen), angry after the [fall of King's Landing](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing), believed Simon to be a [traitor](wiki:Traitor) who had too easily yielded Harrenhal to Daemon. Aemond forced Simon into a duel, and then after he slew the aged knight, fed his remains to his dragon [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar). Aemond then ordered the executions of the remaining Strongs at Harrenhal, except for [Alys Rivers](wiki:Alys_Rivers), whom he took as his [bedmate](wiki:Paramour).(wiki:Simon_Strong.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons:_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
