## Origins

Shireen was born at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone)(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rawoiafshireen_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) in [289 AC](wiki:289_AC)(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Cal_1-1) to Lord [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) and Lady [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent). She contracted [greyscale](wiki:Greyscale) as an infant. The disease almost killed her, and left her disfigured. [Cressen](wiki:Cressen), Dragonstone's [maester](wiki:Maester), feels that her sadness is a mark of his failure.(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

As the daughter and heir of Stannis, Shireen resides in Dragonstone and is educated by Maester Cressen. She is usually seen in the company of [Patchface](wiki:Patchface), their mad [fool](wiki:Fool) that she calls "Patches", though his ravings sometimes frighten her.(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

## Appearances & Description

When introduced in *[A Song of Ice and Fire](wiki:A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire)*, Shireen is nine years old.(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Shireen is regarded as a sad, sweet, gentle child, but she is not considered pretty.(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) She has blue eyes, and inherited her father's square, jutting jaw, as well as her mother's large ears.(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) [Greyscale](wiki:Greyscale) has left half of her left cheek and most of her neck covered in cracked and flaking, grey and black stony skin. She has suffered from nightmares since infancy.(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) refers to Shireen as his brother [Stannis's](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) "ugly daughter".(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Shireen is on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)) with her father, Stannis, and mother, [Selyse](wiki:Selyse_Florent). Maester [Cressen](wiki:Cressen) pities the sad-looking child, and has his assistant, Maester [Pylos](wiki:Pylos), show her the [white raven](wiki:White_raven) that has arrived from the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) to announce the onset of [autumn](wiki:Autumn). Shireen tells Cressen about her nightmares that [dragons](wiki:Dragon) are coming to eat her, and he tries to reassure her, saying that the [stone dragons](wiki:Stone_dragon) of Dragonstone can never come to life. She is uncertain, as [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) has said the [red comet](wiki:Red_comet) is "dragonsbreath", and Cressen reassures her that it is just a comet and will soon be gone.(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

Cressen suggests to Stannis that Shireen be betrothed to Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn) and sent to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) to make an alliance with Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn), whom he thinks will want vengeance for Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn)'s death, but Stannis is unsure of the idea.(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)

Stannis writes a letter to the lords of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), informing them that [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), [Myrcella](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon), and [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) are the offspring of incest between Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) and her brother, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister). In retaliation, the [small council](wiki:Small_council) under [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) discusses schemes to spread rumors that Shireen is not Stannis's child, expecting that this would hurt Stannis's sense of honor. These schemes make use of Shireen's looks: Tyrion proposes that Shireen's great-uncle, Ser [Axell Florent](wiki:Axell_Florent), could be the father because she has big [Florent](wiki:House_Florent) ears, but Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) prevails with his suggestion that [Patchface](wiki:Patchface) being the father of Shireen would make for a more juicy tale. He refers to them looking somewhat alike with their speckled, half-frozen faces, and also that the fool is close with the girl. The rumor, strongly supported by Cersei, is meant to be spread via [prostitutes](wiki:Prostitute) in Petyr's [brothels](wiki:Brothel), and with the help of Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) through alehouses and [pot shops](wiki:Pot_shop).(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Later, Renly refers to this rumor about Shireen's parentage during his meeting with Stannis at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), causing great offense.(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords

With Stannis having declared himself [King of Westeros](wiki:King_of_Westeros), Shireen is now referred to as a princess.(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) She often plays with her [bastard](wiki:Bastard) cousin, [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm), in [Aegon's Garden](wiki:Aegon's_Garden) at Dragonstone, and grows very fond of him. [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) notes Shireen is much happier around Edric.(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Shireen, Edric, and [Devan Seaworth](wiki:Devan_Seaworth) are educated by Maester Pylos.(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

While attempting to bring peace between Stannis and King Joffrey I, Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent) suggests that Shireen be betrothed to Joffrey's younger brother, Prince Tommen. Alester is imprisoned for [treason](wiki:Treason), negotiating with Stannis's enemies without his approval.(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Stannis's army and retinue travels from Dragonstone to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) to respond to the [wildling](wiki:Wildling) threat. At the Wall, he leaves Shireen at [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea) with her mother, while his army [marches to Castle Black](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall).(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Shireen, Selyse, and Patchface are brought to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), where the girl is intrigued by the [giant](wiki:Giant) [Wun Wun](wiki:Wun_Wun).(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

After [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark) marries [Sigorn](wiki:Sigorn) of the [Thenns](wiki:Thenns), Shireen dances with Ser [Brus Buckler](wiki:Brus_Buckler) during [the wedding feast](wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark).(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

[Val](wiki:Val) of the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) is frightened of Shireen due to her [greyscale](wiki:Greyscale) scars, and tells [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) that the child is "not clean" and should be killed or she will put others in danger. Jon is horrified by Val's statement, and is grateful that Selyse and her [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) did not hear it. Val refuses to let [Gilly's baby](wiki:Monster) and his [wet nurses](wiki:Wet_nurse) stay in the same tower as "the dead girl".(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Stannis orders Ser [Justin Massey](wiki:Justin_Massey) to travel from the [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village) to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) to recruit [sellswords](wiki:Sellsword). Stannis instructs Justin to support Shireen's claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) if the king were to die in battle.(wiki:Shireen_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btheon_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctheon_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
