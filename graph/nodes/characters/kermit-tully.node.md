## Identity

Kermit Tully was the Lord of Riverrun, Lord Paramount of the Trident, and head of House Tully at the end of the Dance of the Dragons and during the reign of Aegon III Targaryen. He was the most prominent of Tully lords, bringing the house to the height of their power (wiki:Kermit_Tully).

## Origins

Born approximately 112 AC (track_b: Born). Son of Lord Elmo Tully (track_b: Father). Brother of Ser Oscar Tully (wiki:Kermit_Tully).

## Appearances & Description

Kermit had the red hair of House Tully and wielded a morningstar in battle. He was wild and headstrong in youth, vital and bold as a leader (wiki:Kermit_Tully).

## Narrative Arc

Kermit became Lord of Riverrun at nineteen after his father Elmo died forty-nine days into his lordship. He led the Tully host at the Battle of the Kingsroad, the final battle of the Dance, where he slew Lord Borros Baratheon with his morningstar when Borros refused to surrender (wiki:Kermit_Tully). With his brother Oscar and Lord Benjicot Blackwood, Kermit proved himself as an undoubted leader. After the war, he was among those who welcomed Prince Aegon as king. Kermit later clashed with Lord Cregan Stark during the Hour of the Wolf, objecting to Cregan's mass arrests (wiki:Kermit_Tully). He died in 136 AC (track_b: Died).
