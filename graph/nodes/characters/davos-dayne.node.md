## Origins

During [Nymeria's War](wiki:Nymeria's_War), [Vorian Dayne](wiki:Vorian_Dayne), the last [King of the Torrentine](wiki:King_of_the_Torrentine), was defeated by Princess [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria) and Lord [Mors Martell](wiki:Mors_Martell).(wiki:Davos_Dayne.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_the_coming_of_the_rhoynar.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Afterwards, the Daynes became vassals of [House Martell](wiki:House_Martell), and supported Nymeria and her husband against King [Yorick V Yronwood](wiki:Yorick_V_Yronwood).(wiki:Davos_Dayne.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

After the death of her second husband, the aged [Lord Uller](wiki:Lord_Uller_(husband_of_Nymeria)), Ser Davos married Princess Nymeria and became the [Prince Consort of Dorne](wiki:Prince_Consort_of_Dorne), and the couple had one son together. Nymeria remained the unquestioned ruler of Dorne, with Davos serving only as a counselor and consort. After Nymeria's death, she was succeeded by her eldest daughter by Lord Martell rather than by her son with Ser Davos, for the [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) had come to adopt the [Rhoynish](wiki:Rhoynar) custom of equal primogeniture.(wiki:Davos_Dayne.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

## Appearances & Description

Ser Davos is described as "dashing" by Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel). As the [Sword of the Morning](wiki:Sword_of_the_Morning), Ser Davos was deemed worthy of wielding [House Dayne](wiki:House_Dayne)'s ancestral greatsword [Dawn](wiki:Dawn).(wiki:Davos_Dayne.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
