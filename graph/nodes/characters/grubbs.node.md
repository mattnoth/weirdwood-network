## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Grubbs is part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) which leaves [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) in search of the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings). Grubbs draws the watch the night the conspirators intend to kill Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont).(wiki:Grubbs.cite_ref-Rasos0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Grubbs remains at [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep) after [a mutiny](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep), along with a few other former members of the Night's Watch.(wiki:Grubbs.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Dance With Dragons

Five former members of the Night's Watch wander in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest), planning to head south over the Wall. [Coldhands](wiki:Coldhands) kills them and buries their bodies, which are found by [Summer](wiki:Summer), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark)'s [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf).(wiki:Grubbs.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) It is unknown if Grubbs is among them.
