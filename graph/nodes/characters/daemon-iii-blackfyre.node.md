## Origins

### Youth

Born in exile,(wiki:Daemon_III_Blackfyre.cite_ref-3) Daemon was the eldest son of [Haegon](wiki:Haegon_I_Blackfyre),(wiki:Daemon_III_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) who was the fourth son of [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre).

### Fourth Blackfyre Rebellion

Following the death of his father Haegon and the escape of [House Blackfyre](wiki:House_Blackfyre)'s greatest supporter, [Aegor Rivers](wiki:Aegor_Rivers), Daemon was crowned king by Aegor, who was called Bittersteel.(wiki:Daemon_III_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) He led the [Fourth Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Fourth_Blackfyre_Rebellion) and together with Bittersteel and the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) he landed on [Massey's Hook](wiki:Massey's_Hook). Few rallied to Daemon's banners, however. King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) rode out to meet him, with his three sons by his side. Eventually, in the [Battle of Wendwater Bridge](wiki:Battle_of_Wendwater_Bridge), Daemon was slain by Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) and the Blackfyres suffered a shattering defeat.(wiki:Daemon_III_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
