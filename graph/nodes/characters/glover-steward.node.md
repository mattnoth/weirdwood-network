## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

When [Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover), the [Master of Deepwood Motte](wiki:Master_of_Deepwood_Motte), and his younger brother, [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover) went south with [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), they left Robett's wife, Lady [Sybelle Glover](wiki:Sybelle_Glover), in charge of Deepwood Motte. However, Lady Sybelle is only in charge nominally as it is the steward who makes decisions at Deepwood.(wiki:Glover_steward.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Not wanting to seperate from her young children, [Gawen](wiki:Gawen_Glover) and [Erena](wiki:Erena_Glover), Lady Sybelle sends her steward in her place at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) for the [Harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast) celebrations. Believing a [hedge wizard](wiki:Hedge_wizard)'s prediction of a long summer, the steward reports to the Starks he has only set a tenth of the Glover harvest aside for winter. Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel), Winterfell's [castellan](wiki:Castellan), commands him to double that. The steward is later questioned on [Larence Snow](wiki:Larence_Snow), the [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son of the late Lord [Halys Hornwood](wiki:Halys_Hornwood) [fostered](wiki:Fosterage) at Deepwood Motte. The steward praised the boy's wits and courage.(wiki:Glover_steward.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

The steward returns to Deepwood Motte and fights when [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) [takes](wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte) the castle. He loses one leg in the fighting and is made prisoner alongside Lady Glover and her people.(wiki:Glover_steward.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) marches on Deepwood Motte to liberate it from the [Ironborn](wiki:Ironborn). He sends a few men to climb Deepwood's eastern wall in an attempt to open the gate, but they are slain by Asha's guards. Asha sees the steward being carried up from the cellar when she gathers the inhabitants of the castle. In the end, Stannis succeeds in [liberating](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte) the castle and its people.(wiki:Glover_steward.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
