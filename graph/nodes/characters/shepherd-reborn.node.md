Despite claiming to be the reincarnation of the Shepherd, he did not ressemble him and still had his two arms unlike the late one-armed [prophet](wiki:Prophet).(wiki:Shepherd_reborn.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Origins

After the arrest and execution of the prophet known as the [Shepherd](wiki:Shepherd) on the last day of [130 AC](wiki:130_AC),(wiki:Shepherd_reborn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) several individuals in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) claimed to be the reincarnation of the late prophet. One such preached against the [northmen](wiki:Northmen) in Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark)'s army, as they believed in the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods) rather than following the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven). Hundreds flocked to hear him but the gold cloaks of the [City Watch](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing) captured him and removed his tongue.(wiki:Shepherd_reborn.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
