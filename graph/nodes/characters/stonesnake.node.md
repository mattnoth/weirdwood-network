## Appearances & Description

Stonesnake is a short and wiry man, near fifty with a grey beard.(wiki:Stonesnake.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He is stronger than he looks, and has the best night-eyes of anyone Jon has ever known. Stonesnake is regarded as one of the best climbers of the Night's Watch by [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand).(wiki:Stonesnake.cite_ref-Racok53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Stonesnake is part of the one-hundred men reinforcements from the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) to arrive at the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men).(wiki:Stonesnake.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) He is chosen by [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand) alongside [Ebben](wiki:Ebben), [Squire Dalbridge](wiki:Dalbridge) and [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) to scout the [Skirling Pass](wiki:Skirling_Pass) to search for [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings).(wiki:Stonesnake.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

Stonesnake leads the mission to scale the Skirling Pass at night to ambush a trio of wildling watchers, where along with Jon, he kills two of the sentries.(wiki:Stonesnake.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) He expresses distrust in [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte) and later informs Qhorin of what she told Jon.(wiki:Stonesnake.cite_ref-Racok53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

After the death of Dalbridge, Stonesnake's horse panicks at the sight of a [shadowcat](wiki:Shadowcat) and breaks its leg. Stonesnake is then ordered by Qhorin to make best possible speed afoot to the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men).(wiki:Stonesnake.cite_ref-Racok68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Stonesnake is lost in the Skirling Pass.(wiki:Stonesnake.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Stonesnake.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Stonesnake.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) When [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) asks [Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt) for his scouting report, the latter confirms that three of the four other rangers in Jon's squad are dead while the fourth climbed up a mountain, out of reach and in terrain too rough for them to follow him.(wiki:Stonesnake.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Stonesnake is remembered by Jon when he watches [Jarl](wiki:Jarl) and his raiders climb the [Wall](wiki:Wall), whose appearances remind him of the sinewy ranger. Jon recalls Stonesnake is still afoot and unaccounted for.(wiki:Stonesnake.cite_ref-Rasos30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
