## Origins

The Red Princes were the successors of Prince [Mors II Martell](wiki:Mors_II_Martell), the grandson of Princess [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria) and Prince [Mors Martell](wiki:Mors_Martell).(wiki:Red_Princes.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_queer_customs_of_the_south.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Their reigns were marked by wars within and around [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). The Red Princes attempted to outlaw the use of the [Rhoynar language](wiki:Rhoynish_language) among the [orphans](wiki:Orphans_of_the_Greenblood) of the [Greenblood](wiki:Greenblood). The Red Princes established the [Planky Town](wiki:Planky_Town) as a place of gathering, lashing together poleboats and ferries. The town grew under their successors, who established a citadel to guard it.(wiki:Red_Princes.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_queer_customs_of_the_south.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
