## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Dacey Mormont](wiki:Category:Images_of_Dacey_Mormont)*

Dacey is six feet tall, a lanky woman who finds herself at ease in a lady's garb or in leather armor.(wiki:Dacey_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Despite her long features, Dacey remains elegant in dress and dance.(wiki:Dacey_Mormont.cite_ref-Rasos52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Dacey rides south with her mother, Lady [Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont), against the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) with [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), with Dacey being numbered among [Robb's battle companions](wiki:Robb's_personal_guard) and personal guards during the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Dacey_Mormont.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

### A Storm of Swords

Dacey, one of Robb's [sworn swords](wiki:Sworn_sword),(wiki:Dacey_Mormont.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) is among the northern nobles who march to the [Twins](wiki:Twins) to attend the [wedding](wiki:Wedding) between Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) and [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey), and then to recover [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin) from the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn). En route she tells [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) about the [Mormont keep](wiki:Mormont_Keep) and [Lynesse Hightower](wiki:Lynesse_Hightower).(wiki:Dacey_Mormont.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Dacey is one of the Robb's guards during the wedding feast at the Twins, and she dances with the king during the festivities. [Edwyn Frey](wiki:Edwyn_Frey)'s refusal to dance with Dacey is one of the last signs of tension before the [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) turn on their guests in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding). Dacey manages to fend off Ser [Benfrey Frey](wiki:Benfrey_Frey), smashing a flagon of [wine](wiki:Wine) in his face, but she is killed when Ser [Ryman Frey](wiki:Ryman_Frey) drives his longaxe into her belly.(wiki:Dacey_Mormont.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
