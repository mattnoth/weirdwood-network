## Origins

In [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), Ser Rymond and his brother, Lord [Darnold](wiki:Darnold_Arryn), were killed at the hands of [mountain clan](wiki:Mountain_clans_of_the_Vale) raiders they had unwisely pursued into the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon).
