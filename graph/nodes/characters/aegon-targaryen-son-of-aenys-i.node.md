## Identity

Prince Aegon Targaryen, known as Aegon the Uncrowned, was the second child and eldest son of King Aenys I Targaryen and Queen Alyssa Velaryon (wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)). He was a dragonrider who rode Quicksilver, his late father's dragon (wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)).

## Origins

Born in 26 AC (track_b: Born). Son of Aenys I Targaryen and Alyssa Velaryon (track_b: Father, Mother). Died in 43 AC at the Gods Eye (track_b: Died).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiance). `HOLDS_TITLE` Prince of Dragonstone (track_b: Titles). Claimed the title King of the Andals, the Rhoynar, and the First Men after his uncle Maegor usurped the throne (track_b: Titles).

## Appearances & Description

Aegon at fifteen was lean and handsome (wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)).

## Narrative Arc

After his father Aenys I died, Aegon's uncle Maegor the Cruel seized the Iron Throne. Aegon challenged Maegor for the crown but was defeated and killed at the Battle Beneath the Gods Eye in 43 AC, earning the posthumous epithet "the Uncrowned" (wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)).
