## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

After the death of [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) beneath [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Ser Imry joins [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) alongside all members of [House Florent](wiki:House_Florent).(wiki:Imry_Florent.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Aboard *[Fury](wiki:Fury)*, Imry is made admiral of Stannis's [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet) in anticipation of the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), with the title of [Lord High Captain](wiki:Lord_High_Captain). His ships capture six fishing skiffs near [Merling Rock](wiki:Merling_Rock).(wiki:Imry_Florent.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) Imry warns that Joffrey's defenders may use [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) during the upcoming battle, although the [Alchemists' Guild](wiki:Alchemists'_Guild) is not as strong as they once were.(wiki:Imry_Florent.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) Unbeknownst to Imry, however, Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) has tasked the alchemists of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with making more wildfire. Wisdom [Hallyne](wiki:Hallyne) attributes the unexpected success of their [magical](wiki:Magic) spells to the possible reappearance of [dragons](wiki:Dragon).(wiki:Imry_Florent.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Because Stannis's fleet is four times larger than that of [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), Imry does not bother to send scouts ahead as Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) advises.(wiki:Imry_Florent.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5) Distrusting [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan), Imry tasks the sellsail with the rearguard in [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay).(wiki:Imry_Florent.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)

Imry leads the main force up the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush). To avoid scorpions and spitfires defending the walls of King's Landing, Imry's ships lower their sails and only use oars. The Lord High Captain disregards two new towers at the mouth of the river. After wildfire is utilized against Imry's fleet, a chain is raised between the towers and his ships become trapped in the river. While *Fury* battles *[Godsgrace](wiki:Godsgrace_(ship))*, both ships are engulfed by green flame.(wiki:Imry_Florent.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7)

### A Storm of Swords

The imprisoned Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent) tells Davos that his nephew Imry died at the Blackwater.(wiki:Imry_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Stannis recalls Imry as a fool for not avoiding [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s trap.(wiki:Imry_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
