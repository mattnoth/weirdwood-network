## Origins

Urrigon's brother [Peremore](wiki:Peremore_Hightower) had an insatiable thirst for knowledge and therefore surrounded himself with scholars, healers, priests and sorcerers. After Peremore died, King Urrigon granted a tract of land beside the [Honeywine](wiki:Honeywine) to "Peremore's pets" so they might establish and continue their quest for knowledge.
