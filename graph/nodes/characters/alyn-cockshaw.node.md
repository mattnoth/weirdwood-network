## Origins

Alyn was a childhood friend of [Daemon II Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre). He and Daemon were bullied by Daemon's older twin brothers, [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Blackfyre) and [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Blackfyre). He wept when Daemon went into exile after the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion).(wiki:Alyn_Cockshaw.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

Alyn was ecstatic at Daemon's return to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). He was part of the conspiracy to start the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion) at the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls) in [212 AC](wiki:212_AC). Alyn hoped to become the [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard) of Daemon's [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Alyn_Cockshaw.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

On the way to [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls), while part of Lord [Gormon Peake](wiki:Gormon_Peake)'s entourage accompanying Ser John the Fiddler (Daemon in disguise), Alyn first encountered Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall). He did not care for Dunk and was jealous every time "John" approached him. During the tourney, Alyn was unhorsed by Ser [Glendon Flowers](wiki:Glendon_Flowers), and his mood towards [hedge knights](wiki:Hedge_knight) took an even darker turn.(wiki:Alyn_Cockshaw.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)

Alyn deeply resented Duncan for the loss of Daemon's attention and became his secret enemy. He arranged for Ser [Uthor Underleaf](wiki:Uthor_Underleaf) to kill Duncan "accidentally" in the tourney joust. However, Duncan survived the blow to his head, much to Alyn's dismay.(wiki:Alyn_Cockshaw.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)

After [Egg](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) went missing at the tourney, Alyn convinced Dunk to follow him if he wanted to find his squire. Alyn lured Duncan to a well, where he intended to kill him. Duncan foiled his plan, and instead it was Alyn who fell into the well, though not before badly injuring Dunk's arm with his dagger. Alyn was left to drown in the well by Duncan and Ser [Maynard Plumm](wiki:Maynard_Plumm), who had seen the entire episode.(wiki:Alyn_Cockshaw.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-11)

## Appearances & Description

Alyn had a pudgy face with curly blond hair the color of dark honey.(wiki:Alyn_Cockshaw.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Alyn had a close relationship with [Daemon II Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre),(wiki:Alyn_Cockshaw.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) and he was jealous when Daemon paid attention to other men. He was prone to forgetting his courtesies, instead acting churlish. According to Daemon, Alyn "grows tiresome when he drinks".(wiki:Alyn_Cockshaw.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
