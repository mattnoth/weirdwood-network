## Identity

Grazdan mo Ullhor was a slaver in Astapor who owned Cleon, a butcher slave, before the city's fall. When Daenerys Targaryen's Unsullied sacked Astapor under Cleon's leadership, Cleon killed his former master and seized power, styling himself "Cleon the Great" and later "Cleon the Butcher King."
