## Appearances & Description

Mebble is called Pinkeye because of his runny eyes. He is an alcoholic who drinks [ale](wiki:Ale) at breakfast and [wine](wiki:Wine) in the evening. The bellicose servant threatens to hit everyone, but he never follows through on his threats.(wiki:Mebble.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) The older man dislikes steps.(wiki:Mebble.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

After [Weese](wiki:Weese)'s death at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Pinkeye is put in charge of the servants, one of whom is [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) under the name "Nan". When captured [northmen](wiki:Northmen) and [rivermen](wiki:Rivermen) are brought to Harrenhal by [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat), Pinkeye emerges from a stairwell to observe the newcomers. Arya evades Pinkeye before the [fall of Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal). The next morning he reports to his servants under the [Kingspyre](wiki:Kingspyre) that Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch)'s garrison was killed by the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions).(wiki:Mebble.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

On his way to lifting the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) returns to Harrenhal and puts the castle back to order. [Ben Blackthumb](wiki:Ben_Blackthumb), [Pia](wiki:Pia), and a cook are the only servants from Lady [Shella Whent](wiki:Shella_Whent)'s time who are still around.(wiki:Mebble.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Pinkeye's fate is unknown.
