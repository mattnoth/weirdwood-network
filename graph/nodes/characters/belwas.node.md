## Identity

Belwas, commonly known as Strong Belwas, is a eunuch former pit fighter and slave from Meereen (wiki:Belwas). He allows each opponent to cut him once before killing them, so his scars can be counted to show how many he has slain (wiki:Belwas).

## Origins

Of Meereenese culture (track_b: Culture). Formerly a slave and pit fighter in the fighting pits of Meereen (wiki:Belwas).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiances). `SWORN_TO` Queensguard (track_b: Allegiances). `SWORN_TO` Ruling council of Meereen (track_b: Allegiances).

## Appearances & Description

Belwas is a huge man with nut-brown skin, a gap in his teeth, a gleaming bald head, smooth eunuch cheeks, a huge chest, a massive belly, and arms compared to tree-trunks. He is three times the size of Daenerys Targaryen, estimated at fifteen to twenty stone. His torso is covered in scars, one for each opponent he has killed (wiki:Belwas).

## Narrative Arc

Belwas is sent with Arstan Whitebeard by Illyrio Mopatis to find Daenerys Targaryen in Qarth. He serves as her protector during the campaign in Slaver's Bay. Before the walls of Meereen, Belwas champions Daenerys in single combat against Oznak zo Pahl, the city's champion, defeating him easily. In Meereen he is later poisoned by locusts at Daznak's Pit but survives (wiki:Belwas).
