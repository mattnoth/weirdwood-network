## Origins

King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) and his wife, Queen [Betha Blackwood](wiki:Betha_Blackwood), made several [betrothals](wiki:Betrothal) for their children in [237 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), amongst them was Celia and Prince [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_II_Targaryen). However, the betrothal was broken by Jaehaerys three years later, and he married his sister, Princess [Shaera](wiki:Shaera_Targaryen), for love instead.(wiki:Celia_Tully.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
