## Identity

Celia Tully was a noblewoman from House Tully during the reign of King Aegon V Targaryen, and a daughter of the ruling Lord of Riverrun (wiki:Celia_Tully).

## Origins

Daughter of the Lord of Riverrun (track_b: Father). Her father was either the successor of Medgar Tully or Hoster Tully's father (wiki:Celia_Tully).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Tully (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Rivermen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

In 237 AC, King Aegon V and Queen Betha Blackwood betrothed Celia to Prince Jaehaerys Targaryen. However, the betrothal was broken three years later when Jaehaerys married his sister Princess Shaera for love instead (wiki:Celia_Tully).
