## Origins

He was defeated by Ser [Arlan of Pennytree](wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree) in the [melee](wiki:Martial_games) at a [tourney](wiki:Tourney) at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in [193 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Bastard_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

In [209 AC](wiki:209_AC), Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) was trying to prove his [knightly](wiki:Knight) status in order to participate in the [tourney](wiki:Ashford_tourney) at [Ashford](wiki:Ashford), claiming that he was knighted by his late master, Ser Arlan of Pennytree. [Lord Ashford](wiki:Lord_Ashford) said they had no proof of Arlan's existence or, if he did exist, that Duncan ever [squired](wiki:Squire) for him. However, [Baelor "Breakspear" Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)), [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone), stated that he recalled Ser Arlan, citing the tourney at King's Landing where he defeated the Bastard of Harrenhal, as well as from other tourneys. Prince Baelor asked Duncan further questions to prove his story, which he answered correctly.(wiki:Bastard_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
