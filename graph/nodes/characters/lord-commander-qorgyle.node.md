## Origins

Lord Commander Qorgyle entered the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) some time before Ser [Denys Mallister](wiki:Denys_Mallister).(wiki:Lord_Commander_Qorgyle.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) During his term, [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) sent patrols east towards [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea) every third day and west towards the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) every second day.(wiki:Lord_Commander_Qorgyle.cite_ref-Rasos26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

The Lord Commander once visited [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) with an escort of a dozen men, including [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder), to meet with Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark). During the visit, Eddard's sons [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) and [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) piled up snow above a gate to dump on a unsuspecting passerby. Mance caught them but did not reveal their prank, and the boys eventually dropped the snow onto [Tomard](wiki:Tomard).(wiki:Lord_Commander_Qorgyle.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

After Qorgyle's death, [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) was chosen as the new Lord Commander in [288 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Lord_Commander_Qorgyle.cite_ref-Rawoiafjeor_mormont.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
