## Identity

Donnel Arryn was Lord of the Eyrie and head of House Arryn during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen (wiki:Donnel_Arryn).

## Allegiances

## Narrative Arc

During the First Blackfyre Rebellion in 196 AC, Donnel called his banners in support of King Daeron II and led the vanguard at the Redgrass Field. His forces were nearly overwhelmed until Ser Gwayne Corbray of the Kingsguard arrived with reinforcements (wiki:Donnel_Arryn). In 208 AC, Donnel participated in a tourney at Maidenpool, where he was unhorsed by Ser Humfrey Hardyng (wiki:Donnel_Arryn). During the Great Spring Sickness, Donnel sealed off the Vale, shutting down the high road and sea traffic, preventing the plague from spreading into the region (wiki:Donnel_Arryn).
