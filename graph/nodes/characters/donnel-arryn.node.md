## Origins

When [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) declared himself the true king of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) and sought to overthrow his half-brother King Daeron II in [196 AC](wiki:196_AC), Lord Donnel called his banners in support of King Daeron and marched his army to the [Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field) in support. Donnel boldly lead the vanguard, which was on the verge of being overwhelmed by the Blackfyre forces until the arrival of Ser [Gwayne Corbray](wiki:Gwayne_Corbray) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) with reinforcements.(wiki:Donnel_Arryn.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)(wiki:Donnel_Arryn.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

In [208 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Donnel participated in a [tourney at Maidenpool](wiki:Tourney_at_Maidenpool), where he was overthrown in the lists by Ser [Humfrey Hardyng](wiki:Humfrey_Hardyng), alongside Lord [Royce](wiki:House_Royce) and Ser [Donnel of Duskendale](wiki:Donnel_of_Duskendale).(wiki:Donnel_Arryn.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) During the [Great Spring Sickness](wiki:Great_Spring_Sickness), Donnel ordered the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) sealed off, shutting down traffic from the [high road](wiki:High_road) and by sea, preventing the [illness](wiki:Illness) from spreading into the region.(wiki:Donnel_Arryn.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)(wiki:Donnel_Arryn.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
