## Origins

In the dispute over succession after Lady [Jeyne Arryn](wiki:Jeyne_Arryn)'s death, Lord Quenton's uncle, Ser [Corwyn Corbray](wiki:Corwyn_Corbray), was killed at [Runestone](wiki:Runestone) by a crossbowman whilst trying to confront a claimant to [the Eyrie](wiki:The_Eyrie), Ser [Arnold Arryn](wiki:Arnold_Arryn). As Ser Corwyn was one of the king's [regents](wiki:Regent), killing him was an act of treason, and [House Corbray](wiki:House_Corbray) rallied to Lady Jeyne's chosen heir, Ser [Joffrey Arryn](wiki:Joffrey_Arryn).(wiki:Quenton_Corbray.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

In [136 AC](wiki:136_AC), Lord Quenton made his way to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with Lord Arryn, Lord [Gunthor Royce](wiki:Gunthor_Royce), and [Isembard Arryn](wiki:Isembard_Arryn) when the new regents were to be chosen by lot.(wiki:Quenton_Corbray.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
