## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Frenya accompanies [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) along with five other [spearwives](wiki:Spearwife) to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) on a mission to free "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)" (the girl is actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)), who is to wed [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow). Mance disguises himself as "Abel", a singer, and the six spearwives pretend to be his mother, wife, two daughters, and two sisters, who accompany him on instruments. Abel and the spearwives play at [the wedding feast](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark).(wiki:Frenya.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) spots [Rickard Ryswell](wiki:Rickard_Ryswell) (or maybe [Roger](wiki:Roger_Ryswell), since he tends to confuse the brothers who look alike)(wiki:Frenya.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) nuzzling at the neck of one of the spearwives, whose description corresponds to Frenya, near the [Burned Tower](wiki:Broken_tower) of Winterfell.(wiki:Frenya.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

After several [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms) and [squires](wiki:Squire) are found dead at Winterfell, Theon Greyjoy disbelieves the spearwives' claims of innocence.(wiki:Frenya.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Frenya.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) They later enlist the help of a reluctant and fearful Theon in freeing Jeyne.(wiki:Frenya.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)(wiki:Frenya.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) The six spearwives, disguised as maids and accompanied by Theon, walk into Jeyne's room under the pretext of bringing hot water so Jeyne can have a bath. During the rescue, Frenya wears a rope as a girdle. As they fight their way out of the castle, the spearwives are taken down one by one by the [Bolton men](wiki:House_Bolton). Frenya fights six guardsmen by the drawbridge, leaving Theon and Jeyne without a rope to escape down the outer wall.(wiki:Frenya.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

According to [a letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) Ramsay sends to [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) on the [Wall](wiki:Wall), Mance was captured and now wears the skins of the six women who accompanied him to Winterfell.(wiki:Frenya.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
