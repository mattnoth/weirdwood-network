## Origins

Lady Jeyne was married to Lord [Donald Tarly](wiki:Donald_Tarly). They had at least two daughters, [Samantha](wiki:Samantha_Tarly)(wiki:Jeyne_Rowan.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) and [Sansara](wiki:Sansara_Tarly).(wiki:Jeyne_Rowan.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

As Samantha and Sansara lived during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) and shortly after, Lady Jeyne may have been the mother of [Alan Tarly](wiki:Alan_Tarly), who was head of House Tarly during the Dance.(wiki:Jeyne_Rowan.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) It is unknown if she was still alive during the Dance.
