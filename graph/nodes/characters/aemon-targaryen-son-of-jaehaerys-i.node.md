## Identity

Aemon Targaryen (son of Jaehaerys I) (55–92 AC) — child of Jaehaerys I Targaryen and Alysanne Targaryen; married to Jocelyn Baratheon; sworn to House Targaryen; Prince. One of 3 Aemon Targaryens — see the Aemon Targaryen disambiguation entry.

## Origins

### Early life

Aemon was born in [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) to King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen). The night following his birth, at the suggestion of Queen Alysanne, King Jaehaerys I placed a [dragon's egg](wiki:Dragon's_egg) in Aemon's cradle,(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) a practise that was started by Princess [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) who put a dragon egg in Jaehaerys and Alysanne's own cradles.(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

From the moment of his birth, King Jaehaerys I openly called Aemon his heir. Queen Alysanne, on the other hand, would reply that Princess [Daenerys](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)) was older than Aemon, and should therefore be queen, to which Jaehaerys would respond that, once Aemon and Daenerys married, they would rule together as king and queen. The answer never entirely pleased Queen Alysanne.(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

The [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) lined the streets outside the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) when Jaehaerys and Alysanne returned to the city a month after Aemon's birth, hoping to catch a glimpse of the baby. Jaehaerys eventually climbed the ramparts of the main gates of the castle, raising Aemon over his head so all could see him. It was said that in response, a roar went up that was so loud that it could be heard across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea).(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

Aemon's brother Prince [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) was born in [57 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). The two children became as close as could be. As soon as Baelon could walk, he began to follow Aemon around everywhere he went.(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

In [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Aemon attended a [tourney at King's Landing on the anniversary of Jaehaerys's coronation](wiki:Tourney_at_King's_Landing_on_the_anniversary_of_the_king's_coronation). There, he shared the royal box with his parents, his sister Daenerys, and his brother Baelon.(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

### Prince of Dragonstone

In [62 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), King Jaehaerys formally granted the seven-year-old Aemon the title of [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone) and heir to the throne.(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6)(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) At the feast that followed Aemon's appointment, Queen Alysanne sat Aemon beside Lady [Jocelyn Baratheon](wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon). The two children spent the entire evening talking and laughing together.(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7) King Jaehaerys had originally wished for Aemon to marry his older sister, Daenerys.(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9) After Daenerys's death in [60 AC](wiki:60_AC), Jaehaerys came to consider his second daughter, [Alyssa](wiki:Alyssa_Targaryen), but that idea had been discouraged by Queen Alysanne, who had noted Alyssa's interest in Baelon. As such, in [68 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Aemon's betrothal to Lady Jocelyn was announced. They were married two years later, in a ceremony as grand as the [Golden Wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding).(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8)

In [72 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Aemon was the victor in the squires' melee during the [tourney at Duskendale in honor of Lord Darklyn's wedding](wiki:Tourney_at_Duskendale_in_honor_of_Lord_Darklyn's_wedding). He later distinguished himself in the lists, and was subsequently [knighted](wiki:Knight). Not long after his return to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Aemon claimed the [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [Caraxes](wiki:Caraxes), the fiercest of the young dragons present in the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit).(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-9)

In [74 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Aemon became a father when Jocelyn gave birth to their daughter, Princess [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aemon)), on the seventh day of the seventh month of the year. Seven years later, upon reaching his twenty-sixth nameday, Aemon was appointed to the office of [lord justiciar](wiki:Justiciar) and [master of laws](wiki:Master_of_laws).(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-10)

In [83 AC](wiki:83_AC) Aemon, along with his brother [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) and their father Jaehaerys, ended the attempted [invasion](wiki:Fourth_Dornish_War) of the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) by Prince [Morion Martell](wiki:Morion_Martell) of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). Mounted atop their [dragons](wiki:Dragon), they destroyed the [Dornish](wiki:Dornish) fleet before it could even make landfall. The war was fought and won in a day, and upon their return to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Aemon, Jaehaerys, and Baelon received a riotous welcome, as they had won a war without losing a single man, something not even [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) had been able to boast.(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-11)

In [88 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Aemon joined King Jaehaerys at a number of the castles he visited in the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) during one of his [royal progresses](wiki:Royal_progress), but he failed to persuade Jaehaerys to return to the Red Keep.(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-12)

In [92 AC](wiki:92_AC), Aemon led the assault on the [Myrish](wiki:Myr) [pirates](wiki:Pirates) who had conquered the eastern half of [Tarth](wiki:Tarth) during the struggle known as the [Myrish Bloodbath](wiki:Myrish_Bloodbath). Aemon left for Tarth on the ninth day of the third month that year, shortly after learning that his daughter Rhaenys was pregnant with her first child. Aemon arrived before the fleet of his son-by-marriage, Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon). On Tarth, Aemon met with Lord [Cameron Tarth](wiki:Cameron_Tarth) in the mountains located at the center of the island, where the Lord of Tarth had made camp. A pair of Myrish scouts eventually discovered the camp, and recognized Lord Cameron. One of them took a shot at Lord Cameron with his crossbow. Although he missed Lord Tarth, Aemon, who had been standing by Lord Cameron's side, was struck in his throat, and drowned in his own blood.(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-13)

Aemon's death caused an issue with the succession, as Aemon's only child was a daughter, [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aemon)). King Jaehaerys chose Aemon's younger brother, Prince [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)), as his new heir, passing over Rhaenys, and, as she was pregnant, her unborn child.(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) For this, and the [Great Council of 101 AC](wiki:Great_Council_of_101_AC), the dwarf [court fool](wiki:Fool) [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom) would later name Rhaenys "The Queen Who Never Was".(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

## Appearances & Description

Aemon had eyes "as [pale as lilac](wiki:Purple_eyes)". His hair was a rare color, rare even in [Old Valyria](wiki:Old_Valyria): it was pale, and shone like white gold.(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Aemon was tall and handsome, even as a boy of seven. He was taller than his younger brother [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)), and stronger. By the time Aemon was fifteen years old, he was six feet and two inches tall.(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

As a young child, Aemon was very serious, cautious, careful, and obedient. Before he was able to read, he loved being read to. His mother Alysanne would often say, while laughing, that Aemon's first word had been, "Why?".(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Aemon was a modest child, and neither as quick nor as fierce as his brother Baelon was.(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Aemon was fast friends with his brother Baelon, and would train with him in the yard. They were evenly matched against each other, and their contests were very spirited, always drawing crowds of onlookers.(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

At first, Aemon was shy around [dragons](wiki:Dragon),(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) although he would eventually claim [Caraxes](wiki:Caraxes) as his own mount.(wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)
