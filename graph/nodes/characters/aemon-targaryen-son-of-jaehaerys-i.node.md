## Identity

Prince Aemon Targaryen was the third born child of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen and Queen Alysanne Targaryen. He was a dragonrider whose mount was Caraxes (wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)).

## Origins

Born in 55 AC at Dragonstone (track_b: Born). Son of Jaehaerys I Targaryen and Alysanne Targaryen (track_b: Father, Mother). Died in 92 AC at Tarth, aged 37 (track_b: Died).

## Appearances & Description

Aemon had eyes "as pale as lilac" and hair that was a rare color even in Old Valyria: pale, shining like white gold. He was tall and handsome; by fifteen he was six feet two inches tall. As a child, he was very serious, cautious, careful, and obedient (wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)).

## Narrative Arc

Aemon served as Prince of Dragonstone and held various offices under his father Jaehaerys I. He married Lady Jocelyn Baratheon, and they had a daughter, Princess Rhaenys. Aemon was taller and stronger than his younger brother Baelon. He died in 92 AC at Tarth at age 37, creating a succession crisis since his daughter Rhaenys was passed over in favor of his brother Baelon (wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)).
