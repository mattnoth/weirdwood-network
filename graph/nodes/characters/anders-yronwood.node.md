## Origins

Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) was [fostered](wiki:Fosterage) with Lord Yronwood and served as first his page and then his squire. Quentyn chose to be [knighted](wiki:Knight) by Anders instead of by his uncle, Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell).(wiki:Anders_Yronwood.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

## Appearances & Description

Lord [Franklyn Fowler](wiki:Franklyn_Fowler) has never gotten along with Anders.(wiki:Anders_Yronwood.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

[Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) considers Anders to be a second father.(wiki:Anders_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), Quentyn's father, believes that Anders has been more a father to Quentyn than Doran has been.(wiki:Anders_Yronwood.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) According to [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell), Quentyn's elder sister, Anders does not believe that women should rule over men.(wiki:Anders_Yronwood.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Trying to gain Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) for her plot to crown [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon), Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) tells Arys that Lord Yronwood is himself plotting to have her brother, Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell), succeed their father, [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), as [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne).(wiki:Anders_Yronwood.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Imprisoned at [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear), Arianne rules out contacting Lord Anders, considering him to be loyal to Doran after taking Quentyn as his [ward](wiki:Fosterage).(wiki:Anders_Yronwood.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

Lord Yronwood hosts Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) of the Kingsguard for a fortnight during his journey from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) with the [Mountain's skull](wiki:Mountain's_skull).(wiki:Anders_Yronwood.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5) Anders sends a host to the [Boneway](wiki:Boneway).(wiki:Anders_Yronwood.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)

Anders sends his own son and heir, Ser [Cletus Yronwood](wiki:Cletus_Yronwood), and three of his best knights to accompany Quentyn on a dangerous mission.(wiki:Anders_Yronwood.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7)

### A Dance with Dragons

Cletus is killed by [pirates](wiki:Pirate) during Quentyn's journey to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), [Queen of Meereen](wiki:Queen_of_Meereen).(wiki:Anders_Yronwood.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Lord Anders commands his host in the Boneway, which includes men from [House Toland](wiki:House_Toland).(wiki:Anders_Yronwood.cite_ref-7)
