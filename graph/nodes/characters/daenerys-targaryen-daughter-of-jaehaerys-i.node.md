## Identity

Princess Daenerys Targaryen was the eldest daughter and secondborn child of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen and Queen Alysanne Targaryen (wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)).

## Origins

Born in the seventh moon of 53 AC in King's Landing (track_b: Born). Daughter of Jaehaerys I Targaryen (track_b: Father) and Alysanne Targaryen (track_b: Mother).

## Appearances & Description

As a babe, Daenerys was strong and healthy. She had the silver-gold hair and purple eyes of House Targaryen and was considered more beautiful than her younger sister Alyssa. She was a happy child (wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiance).

## Narrative Arc

Daenerys died in early 60 AC at the age of six in King's Landing and was buried on Dragonstone (track_b: Died, Buried).
