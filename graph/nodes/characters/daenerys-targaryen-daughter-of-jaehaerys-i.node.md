## Identity

Daenerys Targaryen (daughter of Jaehaerys I) (53–60 AC) — child of Jaehaerys I Targaryen and Alysanne Targaryen; sworn to House Targaryen; Princess. One of 3 characters named Daenerys Targaryen.

## Origins

Born during the seventh moon of [53 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) in King's Landing, Daenerys was the eldest child of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), her older brother [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) having died some years before. All of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) were delighted by her birth, save for Princess [Aerea Targaryen](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen), the eldest daughter of Jaehaerys's older sister [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen), as Aerea had been the king's heir until then.(wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Daenerys began to talk well before her first nameday. By the time she was one and a half years old, she could already walk and run.(wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

In [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Daenerys was crowned the [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty) by Ser [Simon Dondarrion](wiki:Simon_Dondarrion) of [Blackhaven](wiki:Blackhaven). That same year, when Queen Alysanne became pregnant, Daenerys was firm in telling her mother that she wanted a sister. When Alysanne gave birth, the child turned out to be a boy, [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)). Daenerys eventually warmed to Aemon. By the time she was four, her favorite toy had become a broomstick [dragon](wiki:Dragon), on which she "flew" everywhere. Daenerys delighted in telling her younger brothers Aemon and [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) what to do.(wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Queen Alysanne believed that Daenerys, as the eldest of their three living children, should be the king's heir and first in line to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). King Jaehaerys, however, regarded Prince Aemon as his heir since the day he had been born. Though Alysanne would voice her displeasure about this to the king, reminding him that Daenerys was older and should therefore be queen, Jaehaerys would reply telling her that Daenerys would become queen after she married Aemon, and rule the kingdoms by his side. According to [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Benifer](wiki:Benifer), the king's response did not entirely please Alysanne.(wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Princess Daenerys became one of the many victims of the [Shivers](wiki:Shivers) in [60 AC](wiki:60_AC). The first night of the new year, she woke her mother at the [hour of the owl](wiki:Hour_of_the_owl), complaining of feeling cold. All that could be done was done for her. She was given poultices, hot soups and scalding baths, blankets, furs, hot stones, nettle tea, mother's milk. [Septons](wiki:Septon) and [septas](wiki:Septa) prayed, and [maesters](wiki:Maester) came and went, but her shivering grew worse. Near dawn, King Jaehaerys suddenly insisted that his daughter needed a [dragon](wiki:Dragon) and had [ravens](wiki:Raven) sent to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), instructing the [Dragonkeepers](wiki:Dragonkeepers) to immediately bring a hatchling to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep). It was too late. Daenerys died a day and a half after she had first complained of feeling cold.(wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

After her daughter's death, Alysanne collapsed in Jaehaerys's arms, shaking so hard that some worried that she had the Shivers too. Jaehaerys had her put to bed with [milk of the poppy](wiki:Milk_of_the_poppy), then flew to Dragonstone on [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor) to inform the Dragonkeepers that there was no need for the hatchling. On his return to the Red Keep, Jaehaerys asked Septon [Barth](wiki:Barth) why the [gods](wiki:Gods) had taken his daughter, but the wise Barth had no answer. According to Archmaester [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn), though many other children died of the Shivers that winter, the death of Daenerys must have seemed more cruel to Jaehaerys and Alysanne, as it struck at the very heart of the [Doctrine of Exceptionalism](wiki:Doctrine_of_Exceptionalism) that Jaehaerys had created to validate his marriage to his sister. The doctrine stated that Targaryens never became ill with common [diseases](wiki:Disease), and indeed it was unknown in history for any to succumb to such.(wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

### Legacy

Daenerys was cremated, and her ashes interred on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). Afterwards, Queen Alysanne stopped attending meetings of the [small council](wiki:Small_council). But within two months of Daenerys's death, the queen discovered that she was pregnant again. Late in [60 AC](wiki:60_AC), Alysanne gave birth to Princess [Alyssa](wiki:Alyssa_Targaryen), who was so like Daenerys as a baby that Alysanne often wept to behold her, remembering the deceased Daenerys. The likeness faded as Alyssa aged, however, and Alysanne also realized that Alyssa took after Baelon in personality rather than Daenerys. It was briefly thought, after Daenerys's death, that Aemon be betrothed to Alyssa instead, but Aemon eventually married [Jocelyn Baratheon](wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon) in [70 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), and Alyssa married Baelon in [75 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). In [82 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), when Princess [Daella](wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)) died, her ashes were interred beside her sister Daenerys.(wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)
