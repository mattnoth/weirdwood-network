## Appearances & Description

By the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Roderick was an old and hoary warrior known as "Roddy the Ruin".(wiki:Roderick_Dustin.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) Despite his advanced age, he was still considered a savage combatant on the battlefield.(wiki:Roderick_Dustin.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
Lord Dustin wore a shield, a helm, and [warhorn](wiki:Horn) in battle.(wiki:Roderick_Dustin.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

## Origins

The [north](wiki:North) supported the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). Roderick led two thousand northern soldiers, known as the [Winter Wolves](wiki:Winter_Wolves), south of the [Neck](wiki:Neck) to support the claim of [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen). They marched first to the [Twins](wiki:Twins), where they met Lady [Sabitha Frey](wiki:Sabitha_Frey), the wife of Lord [Forrest Frey](wiki:Forrest_Frey).(wiki:Roderick_Dustin.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)
In the [Battle by the Lakeshore](wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore) along the western [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye), Lord Dustin's Winter Wolves led the attack and charged at [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) spearmen five times, with more than two-thirds of the northern force killed or wounded in their victory. Lord Frey was also slain in the battle. Roderick was scarred in the fighting against the Lannisters.(wiki:Roderick_Dustin.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)
After the Kingmaker, Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole), was slain by archers, Roddy the Ruin sounded the charge which began the [Butcher's Ball](wiki:Butcher's_Ball).(wiki:Roderick_Dustin.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)
Roderick died during the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton) while leading his remaining Winter Wolves against Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower). Although Ser [Bryndon Hightower](wiki:Bryndon_Hightower) chopped off Roderick's shield arm, the frenzied Lord Dustin was able to slay both [Hightowers](wiki:House_Hightower) before dying himself.(wiki:Roderick_Dustin.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-11)
