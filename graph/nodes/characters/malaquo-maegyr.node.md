## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Malaquo unsuccessfully attempts to hire the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) to silence the High Red Priest of [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor), [Benerro](wiki:Benerro), and his followers, as they preach for [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) to support [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen). Malaquo dares not use the city guard, the [tiger cloaks](wiki:Tiger_cloaks), as most are followers of R'hllor.(wiki:Malaquo_Maegyr.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

The Golden Company ends up three miles south of [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys) due to Malaquo coming north with five thousand foot and one thousand horse to cut them off from the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne) delta road.(wiki:Malaquo_Maegyr.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

It is said Malaquo plans on supporting [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) and the fleet of Volantis will sail soon to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) to wage war and re-enslave all of the former [slaves](wiki:Slavery) freed by Daenerys. He is up again for election as triarch and many believe he will be re-elected. In western Volantis [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sees a large elephant lumber past; on its back are a dozen half-naked slave girls campaigning for Malaquo.(wiki:Malaquo_Maegyr.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
