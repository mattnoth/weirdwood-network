## Origins

Ebrose has studied all known accounts of the "[butterfly fever](wiki:Butterfly_fever)" of [Naath](wiki:Naath) and concluded that it is spread by a large black-and-white variety of butterflies with wings as big as a man's hand.(wiki:Ebrose.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_naath.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Ebrose has also examined the accounts of dangerous diseases of [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos).(wiki:Ebrose.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_sothoryos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The archmaester praises the healthful qualities of mother's milk.(wiki:Ebrose.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Ebrose informed Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel) that Archmaester [Edgerran](wiki:Edgerran) allowed Yandel to be raised at the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) so that Edgerran could test theories involving infants.(wiki:Ebrose.cite_ref-Rtwoiafpreface.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

[Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn) desired to surpass Ebrose as a healer, so he began opening the bodies of the living. Qyburn was thus expelled from the [maesters](wiki:Maesters) for disobeying their rules.(wiki:Ebrose.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Appearances & Description

The elderly Ebrose is kind and soft spoken. He awards links of [silver](wiki:Silver) to those who pass his exam.(wiki:Ebrose.cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) His ring, mask, and rod are made out of silver.(wiki:Ebrose.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
