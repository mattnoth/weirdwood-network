## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Big Boil is one of the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) under [Styr](wiki:Styr)'s command who passes the [Wall](wiki:Wall) to attack [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) from behind. During [Summer](wiki:Summer_(direwolf))'s attack at [Queenscrown](wiki:Queenscrown), Big Boil stumbles backward and knocks down three men.(wiki:Big_Boil.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
During the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black), Big Boil tries to break down the door of [Hardin's Tower](wiki:Hardin's_Tower) with his axe. [Mully](wiki:Mully) puts an arrow through his leg from atop the [Flint Barracks](wiki:Flint_Barracks), however, and Big Boil crawls away in retreat. The weakened wildling is taken into custody by the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) after the battle.(wiki:Big_Boil.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
