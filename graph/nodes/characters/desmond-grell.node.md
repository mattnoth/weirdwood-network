## Origins

Desmond was born of [House Grell](wiki:House_Grell), one of the minor houses of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). He entered the service of Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully) at a young age. He was a squire when Hoster's daughter [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) was born, a [knight](wiki:Knight) by the time she learned to ride, and the [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) before she was wed to Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark).(wiki:Desmond_Grell.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) During Catelyn's [bedding](wiki:Bedding) on [her wedding night](wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully), Desmond drunkenly apologized for each bawdy joke he made.(wiki:Desmond_Grell.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Desmond trains new bowmen after the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps) and the proclamation of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) as [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North). Instead of accompanying [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) to see their father, Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) goes to speak with the bowmen.(wiki:Desmond_Grell.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

During [the attempt](wiki:Plot_to_free_Jaime_Lannister) to free Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) from his cell at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), Desmond's squire [Myles](wiki:Myles_(squire)) is slain by Jaime.(wiki:Desmond_Grell.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) Desmond is introduced to [Brienne Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth) when she and Catelyn return from [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)).(wiki:Desmond_Grell.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

When Edmure rides out to meet the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) forces at the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords), he leaves Desmond as commander of Riverrun's meager garrison in his absence. Desmond provides updates about the fighting to Catelyn.(wiki:Desmond_Grell.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) Desmond drunkenly sings along when [Rymund the Rhymer](wiki:Rymund_the_Rhymer) performs "[Wolf in the Night](wiki:Wolf_in_the_Night)" in the [Great Hall of Riverrun](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Riverrun).(wiki:Desmond_Grell.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) The [knight](wiki:Knight) is elated when Edmure sends word of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s retreat from the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork), and he has some casks opened to celebrate Edmure's victory at [Stone Mill](wiki:Stone_Mill).(wiki:Desmond_Grell.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5) Desmond later has twenty casks of nut-brown [ale](wiki:Ale) brought up from the cellar so that the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) within Riverrun can celebrate Edmure's imminent return and the [storming of the Crag](wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag) by King Robb, Catelyn's son.(wiki:Desmond_Grell.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Desmond was named [castellan](wiki:Castellan) of Riverrun when Edmure departed to defend the fords of the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork). After Catelyn releases Jaime Lannister from the dungeon without permission, [Utherydes Wayn](wiki:Utherydes_Wayn) and a red-faced Desmond reluctantly address her treason. Desmond agrees with Catelyn's suggestion to be confined in her father Hoster's chambers while awaiting the return of her brother Edmure and son Robb.(wiki:Desmond_Grell.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Desmond sends Ser [Robin Ryger](wiki:Robin_Ryger) in pursuit of Jaime, Brienne, and Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey),(wiki:Desmond_Grell.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) but Robin and his men are forced to retreat after their river galley is destroyed.(wiki:Desmond_Grell.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Desmond escorts Catelyn to the Great Hall when Robb returns from the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands).(wiki:Desmond_Grell.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Desmond is to be one of the nobles who launch Lord Hoster's funeral boat after his passing. When [Lothar Frey](wiki:Lothar_Frey), the envoy from the [Twins](wiki:Twins), arrives with Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey)'s response to Robb's overtures, however, Robb quietly asks Desmond to step aside so that Lothar can participate in the ceremony.(wiki:Desmond_Grell.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Feast for Crows

After Jaime achieves the [surrender of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Desmond and Robin ask to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). Both are to be escorted to [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) by a dozen of the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) under the command of [Raff the Sweetling](wiki:Rafford).(wiki:Desmond_Grell.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
