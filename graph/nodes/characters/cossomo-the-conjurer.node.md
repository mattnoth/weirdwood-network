## Appearances & Description

Cossomo is a conjurer who performs sleight of hand tricks and calls it [magic](wiki:Magic).(wiki:Cossomo_the_Conjurer.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Cossomo teaches sleight of hand tricks to [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), who is disguised as "Cat of the Canals", pretending to swallow mice and then pulling them from her ears.(wiki:Cossomo_the_Conjurer.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### The Winds of Winter

Prior to the [Gate](wiki:Gate_(playhouse))'s performance of *[The Bloody Hand](wiki:The_Bloody_Hand)*, [Mercy](wiki:Arya_Stark) takes a quick peek out into the house and sees Cossomo the Conjurer with [Yna](wiki:Yna) on his arm in the pit.(wiki:Cossomo_the_Conjurer.cite_ref-2)
