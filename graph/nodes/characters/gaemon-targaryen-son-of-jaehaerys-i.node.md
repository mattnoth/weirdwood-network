## Identity

Gaemon Targaryen (son of Jaehaerys I) (73–73 AC) — child of Jaehaerys I Targaryen and Alysanne Targaryen; sworn to House Targaryen; Prince. One of 2 Gaemon Targaryens — see the Gaemon Targaryen disambiguation entry.

## Origins

Gaemon was born to King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) in [73 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Gaemon was born early, following a long and difficult labor. Though his mother eventually recovered despite the [maesters](wiki:Maester)' fears for her life, Gaemon did not and died short of the new year, less than three months old. As with the deaths of his elder siblings, Alysanne took the loss of Gaemon hard and questioned whether his passing was the result of her own failing. [Septa](wiki:Septa) [Lyra](wiki:Lyra), the queen's close confidant and companion, comforted her and assured her he was in the care of the [Mother](wiki:Mother_(the_Seven)) now.(wiki:Gaemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Years later in [83 AC](wiki:83_AC) when Jaehaerys and Alysanne's final child [Gael Targaryen](wiki:Gael_Targaryen) was born small and frail, [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Elysar](wiki:Elysar) was so determined that she not suffer the fate of Gaemon or their brother [Valerion](wiki:Valerion_Targaryen) that he personally nursed her through her first year of life.(wiki:Gaemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

## Appearances & Description

When he was born, Gaemon was a scrawny thing, almost half the size his brother [Vaegon](wiki:Vaegon_Targaryen) had been at birth. He was [named after](wiki:Named_after) [Gaemon the Glorious](wiki:Gaemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenar)), the greatest of the [Lords of Dragonstone](wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone) before the [Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Gaemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
