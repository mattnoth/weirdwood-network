## Origins

Florian was a great [fool](wiki:Fool) and a great [knight](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He is described in tales of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men)(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) as a knight, although true knighthood only came to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) with the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals).(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Scholars doubt the existence of Florian and his peers.(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

According to legend, Florian was homely,(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) wore “a suit of iron motley,”(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Radwd40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and was not of noble birth.(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) He bore a famous sword.(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Florian first spied Jonquil and her sisters bathing in [a pool](wiki:Jonquil's_Pool) that is located in the town of [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool).(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

There are songs about Florian and Jonquil; "[Six Maids in a Pool](wiki:Six_Maids_in_a_Pool)" may be one of them.(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Singers compared the sudden marriage of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) to the great romance of Florian and [Jonquil](wiki:Jonquil).(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

During the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow), Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) observed [Tanselle](wiki:Tanselle) performing a puppet show of Florian and Jonquil.(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

While playing as children at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) and [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) would pretend to be Prince [Aemon the Dragonknight](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_II)) and Florian the Fool.(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Songs about Florian and Jonquil are favorites of [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark).(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) learned about Florian and other heroes from her nurse.(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Imprisoned in [Maegor's Holdfast](wiki:Maegor's_Holdfast), [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) reads a favorite book of legends, including Florian and [Jonquil](wiki:Jonquil).(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Ragot51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Ser [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard) claims to be Florian in his promise to get Sansa out of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

There is a famous song about Florian and [Jonquil](wiki:Jonquil) which Sansa knows. She offers to sing it to [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) but he declines.(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), a harper sings beautiful, sad songs, including one about Florian, in the Queen's Ballroom at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Racok60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Sandor demands the unnamed song about Florian and Jonquil when he visits Sansa's chamber, but Sansa sings him the [Mother's](wiki:Mother_(the_Seven)) hymn instead.(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Storm of Swords

The half-mad [Easy](wiki:Easy) thinks himself Florian the Fool reborn.(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Imprisoned in the [sky cells](wiki:Sky_cell) of the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), [Marillion](wiki:Marillion) sings of Jonquil's fool and other sad songs.(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Early on in her quest to find Sansa, [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) remembers that when she was a child her nurse had filled her ears with tales of valor, regaling her with the noble exploits of many champions, among them Florian the Fool.(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

### A Dance with Dragons

Aboard the *[Selaesori Qhoran](wiki:Selaesori_Qhoran)*, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) mocks Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont)'s plan to get back into [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s good graces by comparing the [knight](wiki:Knight) to Florian the Fool.(wiki:Florian_the_Fool.cite_ref-Radwd40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
