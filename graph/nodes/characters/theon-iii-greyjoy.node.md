## Origins

Theon III was chosen at a [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot). His reign was among those which saw the decline of [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) power over the western coast of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), including Houses [Gardener](wiki:House_Gardener) and [Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) ceasing to pay tribute. Theon sailed against them, only to be slain by Lord [Lymond Hightower](wiki:Lymond_Hightower), who revived the practice of [thralldom](wiki:Thrall) long enough to set the ironmen captured in battle to hard labor strengthening [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown)'s walls.(wiki:Theon_III_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
