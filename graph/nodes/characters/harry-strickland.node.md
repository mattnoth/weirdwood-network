## Origins

Harry's great-grandsire lost his lands when he rose in the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion) with [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre). Harry was born in exile and raised with the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company), as was his father and his grandfather. After the death of the previous commander, Ser [Myles Toyne](wiki:Myles_Toyne), Harry was made the new captain-general, and [Gorys Edoryen](wiki:Gorys_Edoryen) replaced Harry as paymaster.(wiki:Harry_Strickland.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) Ser [Rolly Duckfield](wiki:Rolly_Duckfield) was once Harry's [squire](wiki:Squire),(wiki:Harry_Strickland.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and his current squire is [Watkyn](wiki:Watkyn).(wiki:Harry_Strickland.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

## Appearances & Description

Harry is portly, with a big round head, mild grey eyes, and thinning grey hair that he brushes sideways to cover up a bald spot. He likes [elephants](wiki:Elephant).(wiki:Harry_Strickland.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Harry likes to boast "[gold](wiki:Golden_Company) for four generations". He complains often of blisters on his feet from long marches, and is not the bravest of men.(wiki:Harry_Strickland.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Harry brings the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) to three miles south of [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys), where they are met by [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) and [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)).(wiki:Harry_Strickland.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

During the [landing of the Golden Company](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company) in the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands), Harry is weary and reluctant to fight. Jon constantly thinks of him as a coward.(wiki:Harry_Strickland.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### The Winds of Winter

[Chain](wiki:Chain) tells Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) that someone once made off with Harry's favorite [elephant](wiki:Elephant).(wiki:Harry_Strickland.cite_ref-Ar2_5-0)
