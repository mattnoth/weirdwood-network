## Appearances & Description

Forley is short and hard, with a pinched nose, a bald pate and grizzled brown beard.(wiki:Forley_Prester.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) thinks the capable Forley resembles an innkeeper.(wiki:Forley_Prester.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

The [knight](wiki:Knight)'s surcoat depicts a bull's head, and his helmet has horns.(wiki:Forley_Prester.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Forley serves in Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s host when war breaks out, taking part in the [battle in the hills below the Golden Tooth](wiki:Battle_in_the_hills_below_the_Golden_Tooth) and the [battle under the walls of Riverrun](wiki:Battle_under_the_walls_of_Riverrun). When [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) smashes Jaime's host in the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), Forley commands the southern camp. Upon seeing that the other two besieging camps are lost, Forley retreats in good order, saving two thousand spearmen and as many bowmen. The [Tyroshi](wiki:Tyrosh) sellsword leading Forley's freeriders strikes his banners and goes over to Robb, however.(wiki:Forley_Prester.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Forley is stationed at the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth).(wiki:Forley_Prester.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) According to a semi-canon source, Forley sends many of his veterans to Ser [Stafford Lannister](wiki:Stafford_Lannister) at [Oxcross](wiki:Oxcross), to augment and help train the inexperienced levies comprising Stafford's forces.(wiki:Forley_Prester.cite_ref-4) Stafford and many of his men are killed in the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross), however, by the host of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), the new [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Forley_Prester.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Once Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister), Stafford's son, reforms Oxcross survivors who have gathered in [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport), he is to link up with Forley at the Golden Tooth and prepare to march on the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Forley_Prester.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) They are tasked with attacking [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) after Robb is killed in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Forley_Prester.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Feast for Crows

During the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Forley and [Emmon Frey](wiki:Emmon_Frey) are encamped south of the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) with remnants of Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s former command and river lords who have bent the knee to King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Forley_Prester.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) When Jaime arrives to resolve the siege, Forley is made part of his war council. Forley is skeptical of Ser [Lyle Crakehall](wiki:Lyle_Crakehall)'s suggestion to end the siege by dueling Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully), who holds Riverrun.(wiki:Forley_Prester.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) has Riverrun surrender to the royal forces, but Brynden the Blackfish escapes in the night. Jaime gives Forley command of the escort taking Edmure to captivity in [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock). Jaime initially gives Forley a hundred men, including twenty [knights](wiki:Knight), but then doubles the force in case they come under attack by the Blackfish. The [Westerlings](wiki:House_Westerling) and [Whitesmile Wat](wiki:Whitesmile_Wat) also join Forley's [column](wiki:Column) returning to the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands). Jaime advises Forley to keep archers near [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling), since she is the widowed queen of Robb Stark. Forley is told to kill Edmure and Jeyne if they try to escape.(wiki:Forley_Prester.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

After Forley departs, Jaime thinks the able knight would make a good choice as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) for Tommen.(wiki:Forley_Prester.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
