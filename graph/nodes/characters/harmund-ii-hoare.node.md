## Origins

Harmund spent his youth as a ward of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) of [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock). A great traveler, Harmund II is said to have been the first iron king to visit mainland [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) in peace. Once king, he married Lady [Lelia Lannister](wiki:Lelia_Lannister), the daughter of the [King of the Rock](wiki:King_of_the_Rock). He also visited [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) and [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) to discuss trade.(wiki:Harmund_II_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Harmund followed both the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God) and the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven). He spoke of the "Eight Gods" and believed that the Drowned God was an aspect of the [Stranger](wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven)), which angered many of the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn). His children were raised in the Faith.(wiki:Harmund_II_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
