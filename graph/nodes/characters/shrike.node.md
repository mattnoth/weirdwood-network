## Origins

Going against tradition, King [Harmund III Hoare](wiki:Harmund_III_Hoare) announced that [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) reavers would be hanged as [pirates](wiki:Pirates). The king outlawed the taking of [salt wives](wiki:Salt_wife), declaring the children of such unions no more than [bastards](wiki:Bastard) with no right to inheritance. Harmund also considered ending the practice of [thralldom](wiki:Thrall).(wiki:Shrike.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The Shrike began to preach against Harmund, and the lords of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) listened when other [priests of the Drowned God](wiki:Priests_of_the_Drowned_God) took up the cry. [Septons](wiki:Septon) and their followers stood by King Harmund, but the Shrike managed to overthrow Harmund bloodlessly. Afterward, the Shrike [took the deposed king's tongue](wiki:Tongueless) so he may never again speak "lies and blasphemies". Harmund was also blinded and his nose cut off so "all men might see him for the monster he is".(wiki:Shrike.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Harmund's brother, [Hagon Hoare](wiki:Hagon_Hoare), was declared king. Hagon allowed the mutilation of his own mother, Dowager Queen [Lelia Lannister](wiki:Lelia_Lannister), whom the Shrike blamed for turning her husband, [Harmund II Hoare](wiki:Harmund_II_Hoare), and their sons away from the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God) to the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:Shrike.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
Hagon the Heartless was brought down by the [westermen](wiki:Westermen) led by Ser [Aubrey Crakehall](wiki:Aubrey_Crakehall), who wanted vengeance for the mutilation of Lelia. Surprisingly, Aubrey then claimed the kingship of the Iron Isles for himself. The reign of "King Aubrey" lasted less than half a year before he was captured by the ironborn and sacrificed to the sea by the Shrike.(wiki:Shrike.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
