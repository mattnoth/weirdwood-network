## Identity

Willam Stark was a Lord of Winterfell and Warden of the North (track_b: Titles). Born between 185 and 211 AC, he died in 226 AC at Long Lake (track_b: Born, Died). He is buried in the Crypt of Winterfell (track_b: Buried).

## Narrative Arc

Willam Stark was Lord of Winterfell who died in battle at Long Lake in 226 AC (track_b: Died). He married twice: first Lyanne Glover and later Melantha Blackwood (track_b: Spouses). He fathered Brandon Stark, Edwyle Stark, and Jocelyn Stark (track_b: Issue). Through his son Edwyle, he is the grandfather of Rickard Stark and great-grandfather of Eddard Stark.
