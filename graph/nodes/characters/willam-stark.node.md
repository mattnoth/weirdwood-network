## Origins

Willam became [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell) after the death of his older brother, [Donnor Stark](wiki:Donnor_Stark).(wiki:Willam_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_stark_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Willam first married [Lyanne Glover](wiki:Lyanne_Glover) and had one child with her, [Brandon](wiki:Brandon_Stark_(son_of_Willam)),(wiki:Willam_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_stark_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Willam_Stark.cite_ref-wives_3-0) but Lyanne died while giving birth to the boy. [Nan](wiki:Old_Nan) came to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) to be Brandon's [wet nurse](wiki:Wet_nurse), but he died at age three of a summer chill.(wiki:Willam_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Willam then took [Melantha Blackwood](wiki:Melantha_Blackwood) as his second wife, and together they had two children, [Edwyle](wiki:Edwyle_Stark) and [Jocelyn Stark](wiki:Jocelyn_Stark).(wiki:Willam_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_stark_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)(wiki:Willam_Stark.cite_ref-wives_3-1)

When [Raymun Redbeard](wiki:Raymun_Redbeard), the [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall), went over the [Wall](wiki:Wall), Lord Willam called his banners and marched with Lord [Harmond Umber](wiki:Harmond_Umber), with the [northmen](wiki:Northmen) meeting the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) on the shores of [Long Lake](wiki:Long_Lake) in [226 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). Willam was killed and beheaded in the [battle at Long Lake](wiki:Battle_at_Long_Lake), but his younger brother, [Artos](wiki:Artos_Stark), avenged him by slaying Raymun.(wiki:Willam_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Willam_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_lords_of_winterfell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Willam was succeeded as Lord of Winterfell by his son, [Edwyle](wiki:Edwyle_Stark).(wiki:Willam_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_stark_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) Willam is buried in the [crypts of Winterfell](wiki:Crypts_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Willam_Stark.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
