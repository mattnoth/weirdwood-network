## Identity

Aeron Greyjoy, known as the Damphair, is a priest of the Drowned God and the youngest surviving son of Lord Quellon Greyjoy by his second wife, Lady Sunderly (track_b: Mother). He is a POV character in AFFC (wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy).

## Origins

Born between 269–273 AC on Pyke (track_b: Born). Son of Quellon Greyjoy (track_b: Father) and Lady Sunderly (track_b: Mother). Before his religious conversion, Aeron was a wild youth known as the "Golden Storm" who loved drinking, wenching, and playing the finger dance (wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc1).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Greyjoy (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` ironborn (track_b: Culture). Aeron serves as a priest of the Drowned God, performing baptisms and blessings for the ironborn (wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc1).

## Appearances & Description

Aeron is tall and thin, with fierce eyes and a long black beard that he never cuts or combs, tangled with seaweed. He wears roughspun robes and carries a waterskin of seawater for performing blessings. He goes barefoot (wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc1).

## Narrative Arc

In his youth, Aeron was a reckless reveler called the Golden Storm. He nearly drowned during Balon's first rebellion, an experience that transformed him into a devout priest of the Drowned God (wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok11). After Balon's death, Aeron calls for a kingsmoot on Old Wyk to choose a new king, opposing Euron's claim. He performs the traditional rites and champions the old ways, but Euron wins the kingsmoot with the dragon horn (wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc19). Aeron harbors a deep fear and loathing of his brother Euron, rooted in childhood trauma (wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc1).
