## Identity

Eustace (Dance of the Dragons) — sworn to Faith of the Seven; Septon. One of 2 characters named Eustace.

## Origins

### Viserys I

Eustace was born in [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) He served in the [royal sept](wiki:Royal_sept) when King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) sat the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), becoming a confidant and confessor to the king and his wives.(wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Eustace and [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom) gave conflicting accounts of events during Viserys's reign and the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), such as the relationships between Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and her uncle Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) and Rhaenyra's relationship with the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) knight, Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole).(wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

### Dance of the Dragons

Following the death of King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen), Grand Maester [Orwyle](wiki:Orwyle) told the [small council](wiki:Small_council) that Eustace should be summoned to perform the last rites and to pray for Viserys's soul. However, Ser [Otto Hightower](wiki:Otto_Hightower), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), said that they must first settle the matter of succession. According to Eustace, Viserys's eldest son, Prince [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), was initially opposed to the [greens](wiki:Greens)' attempt to crown him instead of Aegon's elder half-sister, Princess [Rhaenyra](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), and he only agreed to accept the crown after Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole), the [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard), convinced him he would be killed if Rhaenyra became queen.(wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Due to him being a confidant of the royal family, Eustace was chosen to annoint and bless King Aegon II during his coronation in the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit) in place of the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)) who was too old and frail to travel from [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

After the [fall of King's Landing](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing) to the [blacks](wiki:Blacks), Eustace wrote that the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) cut Rhaenyra when she sat upon it, although she was still clad in armor.(wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) He remained in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) during Rhaenyra's brief rule and even after she fled the capital. Unlike the [knights](wiki:Knight) loyal to Rhaenyra and the [mistress of whisperers](wiki:Mistress_of_whisperers), Lady [Mysaria](wiki:Mysaria), Eustace was spared when Ser [Perkin the Flea](wiki:Perkin_the_Flea) took the Red Keep to install his [squire](wiki:Squire), [Trystane Truefyre](wiki:Trystane_Truefyre), on the throne.(wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Following the death of King Aegon II, Eustace was arrested during the [Hour of the Wolf](wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf), after Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark) took control of the Red Keep to investigate the [poisoning](wiki:Poison) of the king. During the Judgment of the Wolf, Eustace was the first accused brought before Lord Cregan and the first released, as there was no proof he had been a conspirator.(wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

### Aegon III

Eustace remained in the court afterward. He was present at the coronation of the young [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), and his records indicated it was a cold but sunny day and that [Maris Baratheon](wiki:Maris_Baratheon) was not present, unlike her sisters, for she had joined the [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters). He also recorded that Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake) was the only lord angered at not being included in the [council of regents](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III) following Aegon's coronation. The septon, for his part, approved of the council.(wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Eustace argued to the Hand, Ser [Tyland Lannister](wiki:Tyland_Lannister), to find a husband for Lady [Baela Targaryen](wiki:Baela_Targaryen) lest she bring dishonor to [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen).(wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

After the death of Ser Tyland in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Lord Unwin became Hand in his place. He dismissed Eustace from court and replaced him with one of his relatives, Septon [Bernard](wiki:Bernard). Eustace stayed in the capital long enough to see the public executions ordered by Unwin on the [Feast Day of Our Father Above](wiki:Feast_Day_of_Our_Father_Above) which included [Orwyle](wiki:Orwyle), the former [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester). Eustace returned to [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept) the next day where he wrote his account of the war.(wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

At some point in later years, Eustace rose to the ranks of the [Most Devout](wiki:Most_Devout).(wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

## Appearances & Description

Eustace's writing is sober, ponderous, and less salacious than that of [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom),(wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) and Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel) considers Eustace to have been the more accurate of the two.(wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_viserys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Eustace did not care for Mushroom's preferred monarch, [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen),(wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) favoring instead her younger half-brother [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-7) Eustace considered most [northmen](wiki:Northmen) to be savages.(wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons).cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
