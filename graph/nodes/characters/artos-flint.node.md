## Narrative Arc

### A Dance With Dragons

Artos is among the Flints who march with [King](wiki:King) [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s army to retake [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) from [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy). After the [battle](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte), Artos sits upon Stannis' war council alongside several other Northern clansmen. He endorses the plan for Stannis' army to march on the [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton) in [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), noting that it is only a three-hundred mile march to the castle.(wiki:Artos_Flint.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
