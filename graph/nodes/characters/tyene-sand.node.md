## Identity

Tyene Sand is the bastard daughter of Prince Oberyn Martell and an unnamed septa. She is one of the Sand Snakes (wiki:Tyene_Sand).

## Origins

Born 276–277 AC in Westeros. Her mother was a septa from the Reach (track_b: Born, Father, Mother).

## Appearances & Description

Fair, with golden hair and deep blue eyes. Dimples in her cheeks, gentle sweet voice. Has a widow's peak like her sisters and the viper eyes of her father. Despite her innocent, pious persona, regarded as treacherous — her soft pale hands as deadly as Obara's callused ones. Shares her father's knowledge of poisons. Owns a clinging gown of pale blue samite with sleeves of Myrish lace and a modest cream and green gown with long lace sleeves. Envious of Myrcella's curls (wiki:Tyene_Sand).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Martell (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Dornishmen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Grew up at the Water Gardens with her cousin Arianne, learning to read, ride, and dance together — as close as sisters. When Oberyn dies, Tyene demands vengeance from Doran, proposing poison: she suggests lacing Tommen's communion wafer with the strangler. Doran confines her. Later, Doran reveals his plans and sends Tyene to King's Landing disguised as a septa, to infiltrate the court and get close to the High Sparrow and the Faith (wiki:Tyene_Sand).
