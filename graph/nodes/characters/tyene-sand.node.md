## Origins

When Tyene was in the cradle her mother, a [septa](wiki:Septa), read to her from *[The Seven-Pointed Star](wiki:The_Seven-Pointed_Star)*.(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

Tyene spent her youth at the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens) with her cousin, Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell), as well as with [Sylva Santagar](wiki:Sylva_Santagar), [Andrey Dalt](wiki:Andrey_Dalt), and [Garin](wiki:Garin_(orphan)). Tyene and Arianne grew as close as sisters, learning to read, ride, and dance together. They have shared meals, [jewelry](wiki:Jewelry), beds, and onetime a flagon of [wine](wiki:Wine) stolen by Arianne. The two would have lost their virginity together to Andrey as well, but he was premature in Tyene's hands during the encounter.(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

Arianne once crossed the [Mander](wiki:Mander) with three of the [Sand Snakes](wiki:Sand_Snakes) to visit Tyene's mother.(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Tyene and Arianne once fled [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) to try to marry Arianne to [Willas Tyrell](wiki:Willas_Tyrell) at [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden), but they were caught by Tyene's father, [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell), at [Vaith](wiki:Vaith).(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) Oberyn also once brought Tyene with Arianne and [Sarella Sand](wiki:Sarella_Sand) to [Shandystone](wiki:Shandystone), where he showed Tyene how to milk [vipers](wiki:Viper) for their venom.(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

[Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), kept a [marriage pact](wiki:Secret_marriage_pact) hidden from Arianne because he feared his daughter would not have kept it a secret from Tyene, who would then have informed her sisters, [Obara](wiki:Obara_Sand) and [Nymeria Sand](wiki:Nymeria_Sand).(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4)

## Appearances & Description

Tyene is fair, with golden hair and deep blue eyes.(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Dimples bloom in her cheeks, and she has a gentle, sweet voice.(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) According to a semi-canon source, Tyenne has a widow's peak like [her sisters](wiki:Sand_Snakes),(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Rawoiaftyene_sand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) and they all have the [viper](wiki:Viper) eyes of their father, [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell).(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) is especially close with Tyene, considering her cousin to be the sister she never had.(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Tyene is also close with her sisters [Obara](wiki:Obara_Sand) and [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria_Sand).(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

Despite her innocent and pious persona, Tyene is regarded as treacherous. Her soft pale hands are as deadly as Obara's callused ones.(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) She shares her father Oberyn's knowledge of [poisons](wiki:Poison), her chosen weapon.(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) Tyene disapproves of the cautious nature of her uncle, [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne).(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Tyene owns a clinging gown of pale blue samite with sleeves of [Myrish](wiki:Myr) lace,(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) as well as a modest cream and green gown with long lace sleeves.(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

> Her gown was cream and green, with long lace sleeves, so modest and so innocent that any man who looked at her might think her the most chaste of maids.

— Areo Hotah's observation of Tyene at Sunspear, ADWD The Watcher I (`sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-the-watcher-01.md:37`) [L858] Tyene is envious of Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon)'s curls.(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Tyene waits for [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), in the throne room of [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) to demand vengeance for her father, [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell), who was slain by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). She works on an embroidery of a smiling Oberyn armored in red atop a [sand steed](wiki:Sand_steed).(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7)

[Nymeria Sand](wiki:Nymeria_Sand) wants Tyene to aid her in assassinating Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), and King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon). Tyene instead wants to crown Tommen's older sister, Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon), as queen, offering to arrange the wedding between Mycella and Doran's youngest son, Prince [Trystane Martell](wiki:Trystane_Martell). If the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) and [Tyrells](wiki:House_Tyrell) invade [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) in response to Myrcella's crowning, they would then be slain in the Dornish mountains and sands. After Doran states that he will think about her proposal, Tyene asks for his blessing. Since Doran laid his hand on Tyene's head before she departed, Maester [Caleotte](wiki:Caleotte) searches for scratches and puncture marks, demonstrating a fear of her knowledge of [poisons](wiki:Poison).(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8)

Doran has his captain, [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah), detain Tyene and [her sisters](wiki:Sand_Snakes) in the [Spear Tower](wiki:Spear_Tower) as a gesture of good will towards Tommen's court, with Areo tasked with securing Tyene, [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria_Sand) and [Obara](wiki:Obara_Sand) before their younger siblings.(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-9)

Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) is also retained the Spear Tower after her own plot to crown Myrcella is foiled by Areo.(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5)

### A Dance with Dragons

Doran has Arianne, Obara, Nymeria, and Tyene released from their cells in the Spear Tower. All are present when Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) presents the [Mountain's skull](wiki:Mountain's_skull) to the Prince of Dorne. Tyene is pleased to learn that Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) had died screaming for days from [poison](wiki:Poison). When [Ricasso](wiki:Ricasso) raises a toast to King Tommen during the feast, Tyene declines with a murmur.(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

After the feast, Arianne and her elder cousins meet with Doran in his solar. [Ellaria Sand](wiki:Ellaria_Sand), Oberyn's paramour and the mother of his youngest daughters, disapproves of the vengeful plans of Obara, Nym, and Tyene. After Ellaria departs, Doran has the sisters swear their allegiance so he can confide in them his plans. Doran tasks Nymeria with returning Myrcella to King's Landing and joining the [small council](wiki:Small_council). Tyene is to accompany her sister to the capital and, disguised as a [septa](wiki:Septa), gain the confidence of the [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow) at the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor).(wiki:Tyene_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)
