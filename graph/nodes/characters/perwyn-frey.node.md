## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Perwyn attends the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with five other members of [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey).(wiki:Perwyn_Frey.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

During [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark)'s parley with Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) within the [Twins](wiki:Twins), Perwyn is left outside in the company of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) to ensure his mother's safety.(wiki:Perwyn_Frey.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Five Freys serve in [Robb's personal guard](wiki:Robb's_personal_guard) during the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Perwyn_Frey.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Perwyn is a member of the party that escorts Catelyn as she travels to treat with [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) at [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge)(wiki:Perwyn_Frey.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) and [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End).(wiki:Perwyn_Frey.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) When they return to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) after [Renly's death](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Perwyn is informed of Robb's victories in the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) by his half brother, [Martyn Rivers](wiki:Martyn_Rivers).(wiki:Perwyn_Frey.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Perwyn jests when he sees [hanged plotters](wiki:Plot_to_free_Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Perwyn_Frey.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

### A Storm of Swords

While under house arrest for freeing Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), Catelyn watches Perwyn and Martyn, who fought in the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords),(wiki:Perwyn_Frey.cite_ref-Rawoiafperwyn_frey.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) unexpectedly leave Riverrun.(wiki:Perwyn_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Catelyn later learns the Freys left because of Robb's marriage to [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling) instead of one of Walder's daughters.(wiki:Perwyn_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

Catelyn is surprised that Perwyn is not present at the Twins for the wedding of his sister, [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey), to Catelyn's brother, Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully). [Lothar Frey](wiki:Lothar_Frey) explains that Perwyn is away and delayed by the flooding of the [Trident](wiki:Trident).(wiki:Perwyn_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) As the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) begins, Catelyn thinks of the absences of Perwyn, [Olyvar](wiki:Olyvar_Frey), and [Alesander Frey](wiki:Alesander_Frey), as well as Roslin's crying, as warnings of House Frey's betrayal.(wiki:Perwyn_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Perwyn is present at the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), where he tells Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister) that his pregnant sister Roslin is in love with Edmure and is praying for a girl. Unlike most of the Freys at the siege, Daven approves of Perwyn.(wiki:Perwyn_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
