## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Lord Duram supports [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s claim to the throne by pledging himself to be Stannis's vassal. He attends the feast in which Maester [Cressen](wiki:Cressen) dies from [poison](wiki:Poison).(wiki:Duram_Bar_Emmon.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Duram's face turns a splotchy grey when [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) burns statues of [the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) from the [sept at Dragonstone](wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone).(wiki:Duram_Bar_Emmon.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords

After Stannis's defeat in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Duram remains loyal to him. However, his support is disparaged by both Stannis and Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent), for the [Lord of Sharp Point](wiki:Lord_of_Sharp_Point) is a feeble, fat boy of fifteen.(wiki:Duram_Bar_Emmon.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Duram_Bar_Emmon.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

The Bar Emmon banners are seen in [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) after Stannis's arrival,(wiki:Duram_Bar_Emmon.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) as Lord Duram sent men with Stannis on his journey to the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Duram_Bar_Emmon.cite_ref-Rawoiafsharp_point.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
