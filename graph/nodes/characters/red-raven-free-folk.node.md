## Identity

The Red Raven is a Free Folk man, the younger brother of Raymun Redbeard. He is known as a craven — Tormund Giantsbane tells Jon Snow the story of the Red Raven fleeing from the battle at Long Lake, and notes the Free Folk sang a mocking song about him that rhymed his name with "craven." His cowardice was apparently memorable enough that Free Folk storytellers made it a subject of song.
