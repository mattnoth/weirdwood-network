## Appearances & Description

Kraznys has an oiled red and black beard, and is so fat that [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) thinks that he has bigger breasts than her.(wiki:Kraznys_mo_Nakloz.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Kraznys shows [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) the [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) when she comes to [Astapor](wiki:Astapor). Daenerys feigns ignorance and pretends she does not speak [High Valyrian](wiki:High_Valyrian) so Kraznys speaks to her via a slave girl named [Missandei](wiki:Missandei). He insults Daenerys several times during the buying unaware she understands every word he says.(wiki:Kraznys_mo_Nakloz.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

When she wants to buy them, Kraznys is the one who barters the deal that she be sold every [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) in Astapor in exchange for a [dragon](wiki:Dragon). The exchange is done on the [Plaza of Punishment](wiki:Plaza_of_Punishment), but [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) (the dragon chosen by the slavers) refuses to obey him. Daenerys answers that a dragon is no slave, and orders [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) to unleash a jet of [flame](wiki:Dragonflame) into Kraznys' face. The Unsullied, who now belong to her, do not lift a finger to help their former masters and are quickly ordered to [eliminate](wiki:Sack_of_Astapor) all slavers.(wiki:Kraznys_mo_Nakloz.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Survivors of the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor) mention that the pyramid of Kraznys mo Nakloz is set aflame by the population of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor) when the [Yunkai'i](wiki:Yunkai'i) besiege the city, because they blame the late Kraznys for their woes.(wiki:Kraznys_mo_Nakloz.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
