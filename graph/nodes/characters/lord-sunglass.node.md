## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

While King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) is fighting the [battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), his wife Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) has the [red priestess](wiki:Red_priestess) [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) burn alive Lord [Guncer Sunglass](wiki:Guncer_Sunglass) and the sons of [Hubard Rambton](wiki:Hubard_Rambton), as a sacrifice to [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor).(wiki:Lord_Sunglass.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Guncer's brother becomes the new Lord of [Sweetport Sound](wiki:Sweetport_Sound). He abandons [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s cause, and sails to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) for fear of being sacrificed like his brother.(wiki:Lord_Sunglass.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
