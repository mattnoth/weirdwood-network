## Origins

King Theon defended the [north](wiki:North) during the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals). Aided by [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton), Theon defeated the [Andal](wiki:Andals) warlord [Argos Sevenstar](wiki:Argos_Sevenstar) in the [Battle of the Weeping Water](wiki:Battle_of_the_Weeping_Water). The King of Winter then sailed across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) to the coast of [Andalos](wiki:Andalos) with Argos's body displayed on the prow of his ship like a figurehead. Theon burned a score of Andal villages, killing hundreds and capturing three tower houses and a fortified [sept](wiki:Sept). The king displayed the spiked heads of his victims along his coastline to deter future invaders.(wiki:Theon_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Theon later conquered the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters) in the [Bite](wiki:Bite) and landed an army on the [Fingers](wiki:Fingers), possibly beginning the [War Across the Water](wiki:War_Across_the_Water). He also defeated rebels from the [Rills](wiki:Rills) and aided the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) in imposing a generational defeat on [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:Theon_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

[Harrag Hoare](wiki:Harrag_Hoare), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), led a fleet of [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) longships against the western coast of the north, conquering the [Stony Shore](wiki:Stony_Shore) and burning the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood). Harrag's son, [Ravos the Raper](wiki:Ravos_Hoare), used [Bear Island](wiki:Bear_Island) as a base. Theon eventually slew Ravos, however, and expelled the ironmen from his shores, including Bear Island and [Cape Kraken](wiki:Cape_Kraken).(wiki:Theon_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

## Appearances & Description

The [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell) contains a stone-crowned statue of Theon which depicts him as thin, with long hair and a skinny beard. He earned his nickname, the Hungry Wolf, from the constant state of war the [north](wiki:North) was in during his reign and his own gaunt appearance.(wiki:Theon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Theon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
