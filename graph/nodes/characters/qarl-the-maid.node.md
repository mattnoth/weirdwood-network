## Origins

When [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) first met Qarl in [293 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) or [294 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), she called his attempt at growing a beard "peach fuzz". Since he had never seen a peach, she encouraged him to join her crew. They traded at [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle), [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport), and the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor),(wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7) and raided [Lyseni](wiki:Lys) pirates in the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones).(wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Qarl and Asha began sleeping together after sampling peaches at the Arbor.(wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8)

## Appearances & Description

Qarl the Maid is beardless and pink-cheeked.(wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) His straight sandy hair brushes against his shoulders.(wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) He has a lean swimmer's physique,(wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) a shy smile,(wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) and is sarcastic.(wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

Qarl is renowned as one of the fiercest warriors of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands).(wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He wields a longsword and dagger, and he is not an experienced [horse](wiki:Horse) rider.(wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

Qarl is [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy)'s favorite lover, which upsets [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley).(wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Qarl and two companions, [Skyte](wiki:Skyte) and [Uller](wiki:Uller), encounter [Theon](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) and [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) in [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport). There they recount the story of the death of Skyte's brother, [Eldiss](wiki:Eldiss). Asha tells Theon that he should make them part of his crew.(wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Qarl serves aboard Asha's ship, the *[Black Wind](wiki:Black_Wind)*. He counts the longships gathered in support of her at [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers) and finds the number lacking.(wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

During the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), Qarl wins the [finger dance](wiki:Finger_dance) against [Eldred Codd](wiki:Eldred_Codd), with one of Eldred's fingers landing in the [wine](wiki:Wine) cup of [Ralf the Limper](wiki:Ralf_the_Limper).(wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Qarl is one of the champions for Asha at the kingsmoot, but [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) is instead chosen [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North).(wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

### A Dance with Dragons

Qarl sails to [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) with Asha as she contemplates her next move after the kingsmoot. Qarl sleeps with Asha in [Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover)'s bedchamber.(wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-9)

Qarl wields his longsword in the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte), and he kills a [northman](wiki:Northman) who grabs at Asha's hair. At one point he fights back to back with his lover. Qarl is captured along with Asha, [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley), and several [other ironborn](wiki:Ragged_nine) by the army of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Qarl is later freed and ransomed along with the other ironborn by [Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris), as the [Braavosi banker](wiki:Iron_Bank_of_Braavos) needs an escort to get him to Stannis whose army is [marching on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell).(wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Qarl and Tristifer accompany Asha to her meeting with Stannis, when she asks about the fate of Theon, her captive brother. Theon considers Qarl and Tris to be Asha's pets.(wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-11)
