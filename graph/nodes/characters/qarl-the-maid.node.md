## Identity

Qarl, known as "the Maid" for his beardless face, is an ironborn warrior and the lover of Asha Greyjoy (wiki:Qarl_the_Maid).

## Origins

Born in the Iron Islands (track_b: Born). He is the grandson of a thrall, making him lowborn by ironborn standards (wiki:Qarl_the_Maid).

## Allegiances

He is `SWORN_TO` House Greyjoy (track_b: Allegiance). He is `CULTURE_OF` the ironborn (track_b: Culture). He is `LOVER_OF` Asha Greyjoy (track_b: Lover).

## Appearances & Description

Qarl is beardless with pink cheeks and sandy hair, giving him the youthful appearance that earned his mocking nickname. He is lean and a strong swimmer, yet a fierce and dangerous warrior despite his boyish looks (wiki:Qarl_the_Maid).

## Narrative Arc

Qarl serves aboard the *Black Wind* as one of Asha Greyjoy's most trusted fighters. He becomes her lover, a relationship Asha values for both his companionship and skill at arms. He fights at the taking of Deepwood Motte and later during Stannis Baratheon's surprise attack on the ironborn camp, where he acquits himself bravely. He is captured alongside Asha when the ironborn are overwhelmed by Stannis's forces (wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Raffc11, wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Radwd26, wiki:Qarl_the_Maid.cite_ref-Radwd42).
