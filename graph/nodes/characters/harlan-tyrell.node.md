## Identity

Lord Harlan Tyrell was the head of House Tyrell and High Steward of the Reach. He was the first Tyrell to rule the Reach as Lord of Highgarden after Aegon's Conquest (wiki:Harlan_Tyrell.cite_ref-1).

## Narrative Arc

At the time of Aegon's Conquest in 2 BC, Harlan served as High Steward to Mern IX Gardener, King of the Reach. After Mern died at the Field of Fire, Aegon I Targaryen marched on Highgarden, where Harlan yielded the castle without a fight and pledged his support. For this, Aegon named him Lord of Highgarden and gave him dominion over the Reach (wiki:Harlan_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf3). Harlan died in 5 AC in the Dornish desert east of the Hellholt during the First Dornish War (wiki:Harlan_Tyrell.cite_ref-1).
