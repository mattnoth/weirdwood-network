## Origins

At the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) in [129 AC](wiki:129_AC), Lord Mallister was among the prominent riverlords that supported Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and the [blacks](wiki:Blacks), despite initial reluctance.(wiki:Jorah_Mallister.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Lord Jorah joined the host of Lord [Elmo Tully](wiki:Elmo_Tully) as they marched to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) against the army of Lord [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon), resulting in the [Battle of the Kingsroad](wiki:Battle_of_the_Kingsroad). During the battle, Jorah and Lord [Roland Darry](wiki:Roland_Darry) were slain by the unhorsed Lord Baratheon.(wiki:Jorah_Mallister.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Jorah_Mallister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
