## Identity

Sara Snow — A "wolf girl" of Mushroom's account, said to be a bastard daughter of the late Lord Rickon Stark. According to Mushroom, Jacaerys lay with her and wed her in Winterfell's godswood.

## Origins

By [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom)'s account, [Jacaerys Velaryon](wiki:Jacaerys_Velaryon), the [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone), fell in love with Sara when he arrived at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) in [129 AC](wiki:129_AC) to secure the support of Sara's half-brother, Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark), for Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). According to Mushroom, Sara and Jacaerys slept together, and Cregan grew angry when he learned about this. However, he calmed when Sara claimed that she and Jacaerys had secretly wed, having spoken their vows in the [godswood of Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell) before she and Jacaerys had sex amidst the snows.(wiki:Sara_Snow.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Archmaester [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn) questions whether Sara ever existed and dismisses the tale of [marriage](wiki:Marriage) as Mushroom's "fevered imaginings", stating that what is known about Jacaerys's character makes it unlikely he would break his betrothal of eleven years to Lady [Baela Targaryen](wiki:Baela_Targaryen) to protect the virtue of the [northern](wiki:North) [bastard](wiki:Bastard) girl. Although Gyldayn concedes that it is possible Jacaerys had bedded Sara, if she indeed existed, he considers the talk of [marriage](wiki:Marriage) to be "preposterous".(wiki:Sara_Snow.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
