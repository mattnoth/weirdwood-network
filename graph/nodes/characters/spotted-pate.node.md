## Origins

Pate is a pig boy, a good-hearted, empty-headed lunkhead, who always manages to succeed, often when his stupidity turns out to be a clumsy sort of cunning. He usually bests fat [lords](wiki:Lord), haughty [knights](wiki:Knight), and pompous [septons](wiki:Septon). The stories all end well for Spotted Pate, including sitting on a high lord's seat or bedding a knight's daughter.(wiki:Spotted_Pate.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

Because of his legendary status, "Pate" is a popular name among the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Pate](wiki:Pate_(novice)), a [novice](wiki:Novice) at the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel), hates when people compare him to Pate the pig boy. When [Leo Tyrell](wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn)) mocks Pate by calling him "our spotted pig boy", he considers hitting Leo in the mouth with his tankard and knocking out his teeth.(wiki:Spotted_Pate.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Months later, [a man who has killed Pate and replaced him](wiki:Alchemist) introduces himself to [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) as "I'm Pate, like the pig boy."(wiki:Spotted_Pate.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
