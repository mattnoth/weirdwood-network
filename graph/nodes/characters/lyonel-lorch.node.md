## Origins

During the Faith Militant uprising, Ser Lyonel took up arms on behalf of the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aenys_I)) and the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) against the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). He fought in the [battle at the Great Fork](wiki:Battle_at_the_Great_Fork) alongside Lord [Rupert Falwell](wiki:Rupert_Falwell) and other knights from the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) and [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), as well as hundreds of [Poor Fellows](wiki:Poor_Fellows), against the royal army led by King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) himself.
