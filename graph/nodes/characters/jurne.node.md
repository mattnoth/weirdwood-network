## Identity

Maester Jurne is the maester in service to Lord Renly Baratheon at Storm's End. He is sometimes spelled "Jurene" in the appendix of A Feast for Crows (wiki:Jurne).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` The Citadel (track_b: Allegiances). `SWORN_TO` House Baratheon (track_b: Allegiances). `SWORN_TO` House Baratheon of Dragonstone (track_b: Allegiances).

## Narrative Arc

Jurne served as maester, counselor, healer, and tutor at Storm's End under Renly. After Renly's death, he continued serving under Ser Gilbert Farring, the castellan appointed by Stannis Baratheon (wiki:Jurne).
