## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) and her companions join Septon [Meribald](wiki:Meribald)'s circuit through the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), and they meet Dog. Sometimes Dog strays or bounds ahead and leads the way; he seems to know the road as well as Meribald.(wiki:Dog_(Meribald).cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) Dog barks repeatedly while Meribald waits at the gate of the castle at [Saltpans](wiki:Saltpans).(wiki:Dog_(Meribald).cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
On the mudflats on the way to the [Quiet Isle](wiki:Quiet_Isle), Dog stays near to Meribald's heels, sniffing at every rock, shell, and clump of seaweed, as Meribald probes the way ahead with his quarterstaff. The [brothers](wiki:Brown_brothers) on the isle always have a bone to spare for Dog. The [gravedigger](wiki:Gravedigger) at the septry drops his spade and scratches Dog behind his ear when he goes to sniff him.(wiki:Dog_(Meribald).cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
At the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads), Meribald feeds two morsels of food to Dog for every one he eats himself. Brienne hears Dog barking during her fight with [Biter](wiki:Biter) at the inn.(wiki:Dog_(Meribald).cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) allow Meribald and Dog to safely depart after the fight.(wiki:Dog_(Meribald).cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Dog_(Meribald).cite_ref-Rawoiafmeribald.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
