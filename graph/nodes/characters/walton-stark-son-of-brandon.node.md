## Identity

Walton Stark (son of Brandon) (d. 50 AC) — child of Brandon Stark (father of Walton); sworn to House Stark; Lord of Winterfell. One of 2 characters named Walton Stark.

## Origins

Walton attended the [Golden Wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with his father, Lord [Brandon Stark](wiki:Brandon_Stark_(father_of_Walton)), and younger brother, [Alaric](wiki:Alaric_Stark), in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC).(wiki:Walton_Stark_(son_of_Brandon).cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Brandon died soon after returning to the [north](wiki:North) and was succeeded by Walton.(wiki:Walton_Stark_(son_of_Brandon).cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

When the disgraced former [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) knights, Ser [Olyver Bracken](wiki:Olyver_Bracken) and Ser [Raymund Mallery](wiki:Raymund_Mallery), launched a mutiny of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) in [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), Lord Walton rode north to aid loyal black brothers in [putting down the rebellion](wiki:Night's_Watch_rebellion_of_50_AC). After Walton beheaded Olyver with [Ice](wiki:Ice) at [Rimegate](wiki:Rimegate), other mutineers fled [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) from [Sable Hall](wiki:Sable_Hall). Walton pursued them into the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest), but his party came under attack by [giants](wiki:Giants). It is claimed that Lord Stark slew two giants before he was torn apart, with the surviving northmen bringing his body parts back to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Walton_Stark_(son_of_Brandon).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Alaric, who succeeded Walton as Lord of Winterfell, blamed King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) for his brother's death, as the mutineers' lives had been spared by the young king after the wars of [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen). Alaric showed Walton's tomb in the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell) to Jaehaerys when the king visited Winterfell in [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Walton_Stark_(son_of_Brandon).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
