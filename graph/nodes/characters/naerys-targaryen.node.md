## Identity

Princess Naerys Targaryen was a princess and queen consort of House Targaryen, wife of King Aegon IV Targaryen. She held the title of Princess (track_b: Titles).

## Origins

Born in 138 AC in King's Landing (track_b: Born). Daughter of King Viserys II Targaryen and Larra Rogare (track_b: Father, Mother). Of Valyrian race and Crownlands culture (track_b: Race, Culture).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiance).

## Narrative Arc

Naerys was married to her brother Aegon IV Targaryen. She was devout, gentle, and frail, preferring a life of religious devotion. She was rumored to have been the lover of her other brother, Prince Aemon the Dragonknight (track_b: Lover — qualifier: rumored). Naerys bore Aegon a son, Daeron (later Daeron II), and a daughter, Daenerys. Aegon IV's legitimization of his bastards on his deathbed (the Blackfyre crisis) was partly motivated by his claim that Daeron was not his son but Aemon's — a rumor Naerys denied. She died between 179-184 AC in King's Landing (track_b: Died).
