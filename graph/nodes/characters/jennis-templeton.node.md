## Origins

Jennis Templeton was a [companion](wiki:Handmaid) to Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) from [51 AC](wiki:51_AC).(wiki:Jennis_Templeton.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

During the second wedding of King Jaehaerys and Queen Alysanne, Jennis helped to disrobe the king as part of the [bedding](wiki:Bedding) ceremony.(wiki:Jennis_Templeton.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

During Alysanne's pregnancy with Princess [Daenerys](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)), she sent Jennis to hold [women's courts](wiki:Women's_court) in her place.(wiki:Jennis_Templeton.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

In [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Jennis married Lord [Mullendore](wiki:House_Mullendore) of [Uplands](wiki:Uplands) as arranged by Alysanne. It was considered an exceptional match for her, and a triumph for the queen.(wiki:Jennis_Templeton.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
