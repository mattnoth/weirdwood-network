## Origins

Nevio was chosen as Prince during the latest war between [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) and [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) over [slavery](wiki:Slavery), which ended in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC). He was the fifth prince chosen in the span of that single year, for the war had been a disaster for Pentos, where [Magisters](wiki:Magisters) sacrifice the Prince should a crop be lost or a war be lost. Nevio convinced the magisters to sue for peace after a rare victory - one that is rumored to have been purchased by Nevio himself through bribery.
