## Origins

Xanda Qo, herself being a slave for a time, ended slavery on the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles) and united all the islands under her rule. She invented the famous [goldenheart](wiki:Goldenheart) bows, from a wood found only on [Jhala](wiki:Jhala) and [Omboru](wiki:Omboru).
