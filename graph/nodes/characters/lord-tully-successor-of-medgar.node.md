## Identity

Lord Tully was the Lord of Riverrun and head of House Tully during the reign of King Aerys I Targaryen, succeeding Lord Medgar Tully as a boy (wiki:Lord_Tully_(successor_of_Medgar)).

## Origins

Born approximately 202–203 AC (track_b: Born). Father of Celia Tully (track_b: Issue), or possibly the predecessor of Celia's father (wiki:Lord_Tully_(successor_of_Medgar)).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Tully (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Rivermen (track_b: Culture). `HOLDS_TITLE` Lord of Riverrun, Lord Paramount of the Trident (track_b: Titles).

## Narrative Arc

Became head of House Tully after Lord Medgar's death while still a boy. At the start of his rule, the young lord was surrounded by women. His rule coincided with the renewal of the Blackwood-Bracken feud, as Lord Bracken was dying and his belligerent second son Ser Otho "the Brute of Bracken" was set to inherit. During the Second Blackfyre Rebellion, the young lord hosted the wedding tourney at Whitewalls where Lord Ambrose Butterwell married a Frey (wiki:Lord_Tully_(successor_of_Medgar)).
