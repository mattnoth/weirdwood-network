## Origins

Lord Tully became the head of his house after the death of Lord [Medgar Tully](wiki:Medgar_Tully) when he was still a boy. At the start of his rule, the boy lord was surrounded by women. This period saw the renewal of the ancestral feud between the [Blackwoods](wiki:House_Blackwood) of [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall) and the [Brackens](wiki:House_Bracken) of [Stone Hedge](wiki:Stone_Hedge). Since [Lord Bracken](wiki:Lord_Bracken_(father_of_Otho)) was dying and his belligerent second son, Ser [Otho Bracken](wiki:Otho_Bracken) was set to inherit, tensions grew. The fact that Otho had killed Lord [Quentyn Blackwood](wiki:Quentyn_Blackwood) in a [tourney](wiki:Tourney) a few years back and earned the nickname of the "Brute of Bracken" only worsen the situation.(wiki:Lord_Tully_(successor_of_Medgar).cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Lord_Tully_(successor_of_Medgar).cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Septon [Sefton](wiki:Sefton_Staunton) speculated their young liege lord would do little to inforce a peace between the bitter neighbors.(wiki:Lord_Tully_(successor_of_Medgar).cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

He was invited to the [wedding](wiki:Wedding) of his [bannerman](wiki:Bannerman), Lord [Ambrose Butterwell](wiki:Ambrose_Butterwell) of [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls), to a [daughter](wiki:Lady_Frey_(wife_of_Ambrose_Butterwell)) of [Lord Frey](wiki:Lord_Frey) of the [Twins](wiki:Twins), but did not attend it. The guests toasted him at the wedding feast.(wiki:Lord_Tully_(successor_of_Medgar).cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

He was eventually succeeded as [Lord of Riverrun](wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun) and [Lord Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident) by the [father of Hoster Tully](wiki:Lord_Tully_(father_of_Hoster)).(wiki:Lord_Tully_(successor_of_Medgar).cite_ref-Succession_6-0)
