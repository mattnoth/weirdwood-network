## Identity

Tom Turnip, also called Turnip, was a royal jester and fool at court in the Red Keep during the late reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen (wiki:Tom_Turnip, cite: Fire & Blood, The Long Reign - Jaehaerys and Alysanne).

## Appearances & Description

Tom was described as a simpleton. He was clumsy and prone to tremors (wiki:Tom_Turnip).

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Crownlands (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Princess Saera Targaryen often played cruel pranks on Tom, including telling him it would amuse the court if he performed naked at a feast (which was poorly received) and telling him that climbing the Iron Throne would make him king, causing him to slice his arms and legs. In 84 AC, Ser Braxton Beesbury, Jonah Mooton, and Lord Roy Connington were found drunk at the Blue Pearl with Tom surrounded by six whores — an exploit that led to the uncovering of Saera's scandalous activities (wiki:Tom_Turnip, cite: Fire & Blood, The Long Reign - Jaehaerys and Alysanne).
