## Identity

Prince Quentyn Nymeros Martell is a knight from House Martell. He is the second child and eldest son of Doran Martell, Prince of Dorne, and Lady Mellario of Norvos (wiki:Quentyn_Martell).

## Origins

Born 281 AC in Sunspear, Dorne. Fostered at Yronwood, where he came to see Cletus Yronwood as a brother and Lord Anders Yronwood as a second father (track_b: Born, Father, Mother, wiki:Quentyn_Martell).

## Appearances & Description

Short-legged and stocky, thickly built, with a plain square face, brown hair, brown eyes, stubble. High forehead, broad nose, square jaw. Looks like his father Doran. Does not smile easily. Earnest, well-behaved, sensible, dutiful, but solemn and not handsome. Cautious by nature, filled with doubts, fears, and insecurities. Speaks Volantene and Ghiscari; reads and writes High Valyrian but has little practice speaking it (wiki:Quentyn_Martell).

## Narrative Arc

Doran secretly betrothed Quentyn to Daenerys Targaryen as part of a pact signed by Ser Willem Darry and Oberyn. Quentyn traveled to Meereen with companions Cletus Yronwood and Maester Kedry, joined the Windblown as sellswords to reach Slaver's Bay. Cletus and Kedry were killed in fighting. In Meereen, Quentyn presented himself to Daenerys as "Frog," but she rejected the match, having already married Hizdahr zo Loraq. After Daenerys disappeared on Drogon, Quentyn attempted to tame her remaining dragons Viserion and Rhaegal. He was engulfed in dragonflame and died of his burns in 300 AC, aged eighteen. He is called "The Prince Who Came Too Late" (wiki:Quentyn_Martell).
