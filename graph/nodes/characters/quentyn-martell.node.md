## Origins

Quentyn was born the second child of [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), and his wife, Lady [Mellario](wiki:Mellario) of [Norvos](wiki:Norvos).(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) At a young age, Quentyn was sent to be fostered by [Anders Yronwood](wiki:Anders_Yronwood), [Lord of Yronwood](wiki:Lord_of_Yronwood), whom he served as a page and later squire.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Quentyn was fostered at [Yronwood](wiki:Yronwood) to repair the relationship between Houses [Martell](wiki:House_Martell) and [Yronwood](wiki:House_Yronwood), which had been damaged by a duel between Quentyn’s uncle, Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell), and Lord [Edgar Yronwood](wiki:Edgar_Yronwood), more than a decade before. Edgar and Oberyn had both taken cuts during this duel, but while Oberyn recovered, Edgar's wounds festered and he eventually died. This led to whispers that Oberyn had fought with a [poisoned](wiki:Poison) blade,(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) so sending Quentyn to Yronwood was to settle this blood debt.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Mellario felt Quentyn was too young to be sent away, however, and the boy's fosterage was one of the strains on his parents' marriage.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)

When Quentyn first arrived at Yronwood, he was smitten with Lord Anders's eldest daughter, [Ynys](wiki:Ynys_Yronwood). His crush lasted until the day she wed [Ryon Allyrion](wiki:Ryon_Allyrion) and left Yronwood. Quentyn received his first kiss from one of the [Drinkwater twins](wiki:Drinkwater_twins), though he never knew which one.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-11)

About [290 AC](wiki:290_AC), Prince Doran wrote a letter in which he instructed his son to do all required of him by the [maester](wiki:Maester) and [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms) at Yronwood, as Quentyn would one day rule [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), and he therefore needed to be strong of both mind and body.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) Quentyn's elder sister, Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell), then fourteen years old, secretly discovered the letter while it was still being written, however. Arianne is Doran's heir by Dornish custom, and she has since resented Quentyn under the belief that Doran intends to be succeeded by her younger brother.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-2) Arianne is unaware, however, that the Prince of Dorne had negotiated a [secret marriage pact](wiki:Secret_marriage_pact) by which Arianne is to wed the exiled [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) and Quentyn would instead become the successor to Dorne.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

At Yronwood, Quentyn befriended Anders's eldest son, [Cletus](wiki:Cletus_Yronwood), Cletus's cousin [Archibald](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood), [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater), and [Willam Wells](wiki:Willam_Wells).(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-12)

Quentyn received his [knighthood](wiki:Knight) at the age of eighteen,(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rawoiafquentyn_martell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) from Lord Anders Yronwood, after turning down the same offer made by his uncle, Prince Oberyn.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-3)

Quentyn has been riding [horses](wiki:Horse) since he was six,(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-3) and has been trained with spear and sword and shield since he was old enough to walk.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Amongst the people in Dorne, Quentyn is little known.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-2)

## Appearances & Description

Quentyn looks like his father, [Doran](wiki:Doran_Martell).(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-twow_9-0) Quentyn is short-legged and stocky, thickly built, with a plain square face,(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) brown hair,(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) brown eyes, and stubble on his cheeks.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) He has a high forehead, broad nose, and square jaw.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) He does not smile easily.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Quentyn is earnest, well-behaved,(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-twow_9-1) sensible, and dutiful,(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) but solemn and not handsome.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2)(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) He is cautious by nature(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) and filled with doubts, fears, and insecurities.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Quentyn does not want to be remembered as a failure, and cannot bear the thought of his father's disappointment,(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) nor does he want friends to die for nothing.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

[Fostered](wiki:Fosterage) at [Yronwood](wiki:Yronwood), Quentyn came to see [Cletus Yronwood](wiki:Cletus_Yronwood), his dearest friend, as a brother in all but blood,(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) and Cletus's father, Lord [Anders](wiki:Anders_Yronwood), as a second father.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) Quentyn's elder sister, Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell), has resented him for years under the belief that Doran intends to name Quentyn as heir to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-twow_9-2)

Quentyn speaks the [Volantene](wiki:Volantis)(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8) and [Ghiscari](wiki:Ghis) tongues.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) While he can read and write [High Valyrian](wiki:High_Valyrian), he has had little practice in speaking it.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9) A [virgin](wiki:Virgin),(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2) Quentyn is uncomfortable around girls.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

After [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) has been crowned king, [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), and his sons are among those commanded to present themselves at court in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to swear fealty to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Quentyn is among those Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), Joffrey's [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), considers as a possible candidate in [marriage](wiki:Marriage) for his daughter, the widowed Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister).(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Quentyn is believed to be in the [Boneway](wiki:Boneway) with the host of [Anders Yronwood](wiki:Anders_Yronwood), [Lord of Yronwood](wiki:Lord_of_Yronwood). However, in truth, Quentyn has crossed the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) in secret.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-4) His sister, Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell), erroneously believes Quentyn is recruiting the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) to displace her as heir of Dorne, with the approval of their father, Doran.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-5)(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-3)

After Arianne's coup to crown Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) fails,(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-4) she is arrested and confined at [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) for several weeks.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1) When she confronts her father, Arianne reveals that she is aware that Quentyn traveled across the narrow sea, and that Prince Doran had considered Quentyn his heir. Doran then tells Arianne the truth about a [secret marriage pact](wiki:Secret_marriage_pact), and reveals what Quentyn has been sent east for.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-2)

### A Dance with Dragons

Quentyn has been sent by his father [Doran](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), to bring [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), [Queen of Meereen](wiki:Queen_of_Meereen), to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). Quentyn's party includes Ser [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater), Ser [Willam Wells](wiki:Willam_Wells), the cousins Ser [Cletus](wiki:Cletus_Yronwood) and Ser [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood), and Maester [Kedry](wiki:Kedry). They sail from the [Planky Town](wiki:Planky_Town) across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) to [Lys](wiki:Lys), where Quentyn reluctantly plays the role of a merchant.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-13) They change ships in Lys, boarding the *[Meadowlark](wiki:Meadowlark)*. When the *Meadowlark* is boarded by corsairs near the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands), however, Willam, Cletus, and Kedry are slain.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-14)

Quentyn and his remaining companions, Gerris and Archibald, spend more time in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) than they had expected, due to the [Targaryen campaign in Slaver's Bay](wiki:Targaryen_campaign_in_Slaver's_Bay), attempting to find a ship willing to bring them to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). Gerris pretends to be a wine merchant looking for a ship to take him and his two "servants" to Meereen, but every captain they try to convince to take them on this journey refuses, and in the end they are forced to consider seeking passage on the smuggler's ship *[Adventure](wiki:Adventure)*.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-15) While the war between [Astapor](wiki:Astapor) and [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) has not yet reached Meereen, no trade ships are leaving for the city as the [fighting pits](wiki:Fighting_pits) are closed and the [slave trade](wiki:Slavery) has been banned.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-16)

In Dorne, Prince Doran tells his daughter, Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell), that he has heard how a great fleet, mostly Volantene ships, has set sail from Lys, and that there had been talk about [elephants](wiki:Elephant). Believing that it might be Daenerys, Doran states that, if Quentyn is with her, he will bring her up to the [Greenblood](wiki:Greenblood) if he can.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) In truth, however, the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) has set sail from Volantis for their [landing in the stormlands](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company).(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

Eventually, Quentyn and his two companions join the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown), a mercenary company hired by Yunkai, as a way to reach Daenerys from Volantis.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-17)(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) Quentyn is given the nickname "Frog" by the other sellswords, and he acts as the squire to Archibald, who is called "Greenguts".(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1)

The three disguised Dornishmen fight alongside the Windblown during the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor). Quentyn, Gerris and Archibald plan to desert once they are north of Yunkai, but the [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince) orders them and other [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) from the company to defect to Daenerys's side. The Tattered Prince wants to make sure that, should the tide turn, he will be still on the winning side.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) The feigned Windblown turncloaks are eventually found by the [Stormcrows](wiki:Stormcrows), and [Daario Naharis](wiki:Daario_Naharis) brings them to Meereen,(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) where Quentyn dresses plainly.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

Daario brings the feigned turncloaks to Queen Daenerys in her [audience chamber](wiki:Audience_chamber_(Great_Pyramid)) and presents them as recruits of his Stormcrows. Quentyn and his friends reveal that they are [knights](wiki:Knight), but ask for privacy in order to reveal their names. After the court is cleared, Quentyn hands over his "gift", a parchment containing [a secret pact](wiki:Secret_marriage_pact), signed by Ser [Willem Darry](wiki:Willem_Darry) and Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell), with the [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos) witnessing the signatures. The pact was for [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) to wed Quentyn's sister, Arianne, in return for Dorne's support in helping the exile regain the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). With Viserys having died at [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak), however, Quentyn now offers himself as a husband for Daenerys in return for the support of Dorne. Daario mocks Quentyn, angering his companions. Daenerys orders Quentyn and his friends to be treated courteously, and reveals her upcoming marriage to [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq). She gives the Dornishmen a room in the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid).(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-3) While Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), [Lord Commander of the Queensguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Queensguard), believes Quentyn's arrival changes everything, Daenerys disagrees, and she wonders about [Quaithe](wiki:Quaithe)'s prophecy and the "sun's son".(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-4)

Quentyn approaches Daenerys the next day, the day of her wedding, wondering if he has displeased her. Daenerys tells Quentyn that one day, she will come to Westeros and look to Dorne for help.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-5) Quentyn reveals to Daenerys and her court that the other Windblown who had joined the Stormcrows had done so under false pretenses, and the feigned turncloaks are arrested.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

Quentyn and his companions are present at the feast held to celebrate the signing of the peace between Meereen and Yunkai. Queen Daenerys invites him to meet [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal) and [Viserion](wiki:Viserion). The [dragons](wiki:Dragon) frighten Quentyn, but nonetheless he tells Daenerys about his own [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) blood. Daenerys tells him that he does not belong in Meereen, and urges him to return home, as her court is not a safe place for him. Quentyn refuses, and tells Daenerys about [her namesake](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon_IV)) during their journey back up the pyramid.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6)

Quentyn tries to speak with Daenerys on the day [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit) is opened, but is denied an audience. He is in attendance when Daenerys disappears from Daznak's Pit atop [Drogon](wiki:Drogon),(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) and is later present when King Hizdahr is presented with Captain [Groleo](wiki:Groleo)'s head. Barristan questions Quentyn about his possible involvement in the food which poisoned [Belwas](wiki:Belwas), but soon concludes that Quentyn is innocent. With Daenerys gone, Barristan advises the three Dornishmen to return home and forget about the pact, but Quentyn declines, stating that he cannot return to Dorne empty handed.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5)

Quentyn's companions are keen to return to Dorne, but the prince plans to tame at least one of Daenerys's dragons due to his Targaryen ancestry. He arranges a meeting with the Tattered Prince, hiring the Windblown's help in seizing Rhaegal and Viserion. The Tattered Prince agrees, asking for [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) in return. Quentyn signs an agreement.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

Dressed as [Brazen Beasts](wiki:Brazen_Beasts), Quentyn and his companions go to the [pit](wiki:Dragon_pit_(Great_Pyramid)) where the dragons are kept. The Tattered Prince had given them the code word of "dog" to be used by the Brazen Beasts that day, but with Barristan's coup occurring at the same time, the Brazen Beasts guarding the door are not aware of the code word. Gaining access to the dragons, Quentyn eventually attempts to tame Viserion, but Rhaegal bathes Quentyn from behind with [dragonfire](wiki:Dragonfire). The Windblown mercenaries flee, and both dragons escape.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-4)

[Missandei](wiki:Missandei) takes care of Quentyn, but he dies in Daenerys's bed three days later, covered in burns. Upon seeing the extent of the prince's injuries, Barristan, [Hand of the Queen](wiki:Hand_of_the_Queen) in Daenerys's absence, thinks it would have been better if Rhaegal had just eaten him alive, as his death would haven been quicker and less painful in such a case.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) The imprisoned Archibald and Gerris claim Quentyn's actions were done for love of Daenerys, but Barristan insists they were done out of duty for Dorne. The surviving Dornish knights agree to act as Barristan's envoys to the Tattered Prince.(wiki:Quentyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)
