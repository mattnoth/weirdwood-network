## Identity

Alyn is a member of the household guard of Lord Eddard Stark at Winterfell, also known as Alyn of Winterfell (wiki:Alyn).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Sansa Stark considers Alyn more handsome than Jory Cassel. The skilled guard dreams of becoming a knight (wiki:Alyn).

## Narrative Arc

Alyn accompanied Lord Eddard south to King's Landing when Ned became Hand of the King (wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Ragot12). He admitted Petyr Baelish to Eddard's chambers and led Eddard to the armory of Tobho Mott. Alyn took part in the Hand's tourney, where he was defeated by Ser Balon Swann (wiki:Alyn). After Jory Cassel was killed in Jaime Lannister's ambush, Alyn was promoted to commander of the Stark household guard (wiki:Alyn). He sent the bodies of the dead Stark guards to the silent sisters and kept Eddard informed of events. During the Lannister purge, Alyn was likely killed defending the Stark household — his fate is uncertain but he is listed as dead in 298-299 AC (wiki:Alyn).
