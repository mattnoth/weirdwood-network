## Appearances & Description

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) considers Alyn more handsome than [Jory Cassel](wiki:Jory_Cassel).(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Ragot44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The skilled(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) guard dreams about becoming a [knight](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Alyn is among the guardsmen who accompany Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) when he travels south to assume the office of [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) On the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad), it is Alyn who wakes Eddard on the morning that Robert takes his Hand on a ride through the [barrowlands](wiki:Barrowlands) to discuss matters of state.(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

Like the rest of the [Stark household](wiki:House_Stark), Alyn does nothing to prevent [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) from killing the butcher son's, [Mycah](wiki:Mycah). [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), remembering how Alyn is going to be a [knight](wiki:Knight) one day, resents him over his inaction.(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

In [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Alyn admits Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) to Eddard's chambers, when Littlefinger comes to offer help in relation to the death of Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn). Later, Alyn leads Eddard to the armory of [Tobho Mott](wiki:Tobho_Mott) in the [Street of Steel](wiki:Street_of_Steel).(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Ragot25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

When [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt), commander of the [City Watch of King's Landing](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing), asks for reinforcements to deal with increased disorder caused by the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney), Eddard gives him twenty men from his household guard under the command of Alyn.(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Alyn himself takes part in the tourney where he is defeated by Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann). Eddard sends Alyn to recruit [Anguy](wiki:Anguy), the tournament's archery champion, but Anguy refuses the offer. Later that day, Alyn is guarding the stairs in the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand) when Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) appears to talk about Jon Arryn's death. Eddard is surprised that Varys got past Alyn and his other guards, but is told that there are ways in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) known only to ghosts and spiders.(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

After Eddard is attacked in the street by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), Alyn sends a [raven](wiki:Raven) to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) with the news of Eddard's wounding and the deaths of [Jory Cassel](wiki:Jory_Cassel), [Wyl](wiki:Wyl_(guard)), and [Heward](wiki:Heward).(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) With Jory dead, Alyn is promoted to commander of the Stark household guard.(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

When Lord Eddard awakes, Alyn informs him that Jaime has fled the city, probably to join with his father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), and that the news of Lady [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) taking [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) captive has become common knowledge. He tells Eddard that [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) is praying quietly while Arya is full of anger over what has happened. Eddard tells him to keep his daughters safe under any circumstance. Alyn also informs Eddard that he has given the bodies of the dead Stark guards over to the [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters) so they can prepare them to be sent back for burial at Winterfell. Eddard applauds Alyn's actions.(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

Alyn is present in the throne room when Lord Eddard commissions Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) to bring Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) to [justice](wiki:Justice).(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) As with the City Watch, Lord Eddard sends twenty of his own guardsmen under the command of Alyn along with Beric. As commander of the Stark contingent, Alyn carries the [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) standard of House Stark. When the force is leaving the Red Keep and Alyn rides alongside Lord Beric, Sansa notices that he is handsomer than Jory Cassel, and she believes that he is going to be a knight.(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Ragot44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

After Alyn's departure, command of the Stark household guard passes to [Tomard](wiki:Tomard), which disquiets Eddard, as "Fat Tom" is past fifty and has never been energetic.(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) Lying in his dark cell after his arrest, Eddard tries to build up hope by thinking of the return of Alyn and the other Stark men after they have dealt with Gregor.(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Alyn participates in the [Battle at the Mummer's Ford](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford), where the men led by Lord Beric Dondarrion are caught in a trap set by [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) forces under the command of Ser Gregor Clegane. At the end of the engagement, when their situation is seemingly hopeless, Alyn manages to restore order to the ranks, and a third of the force manages to break free, led by [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr).(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Clash of Kings

During the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast_(299)) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) muses the number of new faces at the feast this year, filling the positions of those of the Stark household who went south with Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) and died eventually. Bran counts Alyn among the dead. However, this is likely just an assumption, as Bran has little knowledge of the [Mummer's Ford](wiki:Mummer's_Ford) and also thinks of [Harwin](wiki:Harwin), who is alive.(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Storm of Swords

[Harwin](wiki:Harwin) tells [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) about the [battle at the Mummer's Ford](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford) and how Alyn's actions contributed to the escape of a third of the force led by Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion), including Harwin himself, Lord Beric and [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr).(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

When [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) is put on trial by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), [Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams) lists Alyn among the deaths for which Sandor supposedly shares responsibility. It is not clear whether Alyn died at the Mummer's Ford or in a later conflict with Lannister men.(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

During [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark) of [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) and "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)" at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) thinks of former members of the Stark household who have died since the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) began, including Alyn "with his dreams of [knighthood](wiki:Knight)".(wiki:Alyn.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
