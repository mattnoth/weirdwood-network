### A Dance of Dragons

Justifying his demand for territory from [House Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood), Lord [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken) tells Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) that his natural son was killed by Lannisters.
