## Origins

At the age of fourteen, Cassella became a companion to Princess [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC). She died at the end of the year. At first, it was thought she had died of an [illness](wiki:Illness). Later, it was revealed that she had been [poisoned](wiki:Poison) by [Androw Farman](wiki:Androw_Farman) using the [tears of Lys](wiki:Tears_of_Lys), who had grown jealous of his wife Rhaena's friendships with her ladies and weary of their mockery toward him.(wiki:Cassella_Staunton.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
