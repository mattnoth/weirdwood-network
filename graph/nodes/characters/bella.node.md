## Appearances & Description

Bella has curly hair black as coal, like [Robert](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s.(wiki:Bella.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) According to a semi-canon source she has blue eyes,(wiki:Bella.cite_ref-Rawoiafbella.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) also like the king's.(wiki:Bella.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Origins

According to [Leslyn](wiki:Leslyn), Bella's mother was the favorite prostitute of Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) at the [Peach](wiki:Peach) when he hid in [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). Bella is [named after](wiki:Named_after) the [Battle of the Bells](wiki:Battle_of_the_Bells),(wiki:Bella.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) which occurred when Lord [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) attacked the town in search of the future king.(wiki:Bella.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) and [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) meet Bella when they pass through [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept) with the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) and stay at the [Peach](wiki:Peach). Ignorant that Gendry is also a bastard of the late King [Robert](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), Bella flirts with him, but he declines her advances. She then flirts instead with [Jack-Be-Lucky](wiki:Jack-Be-Lucky).(wiki:Bella.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
