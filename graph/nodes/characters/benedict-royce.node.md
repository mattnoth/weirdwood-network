## Origins

Benedict was a younger son of Lord [Raymar Royce](wiki:Raymar_Royce). He was married to [Jocelyn Stark](wiki:Jocelyn_Stark) and had three daughters. According to [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Tully), one married a [Waynwood](wiki:House_Waynwood), another a [Corbray](wiki:House_Corbray), and the third might have married a [Templeton](wiki:House_Templeton).(wiki:Benedict_Royce.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Benedict_Royce.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_stark_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
