## Appearances & Description

Loreza idolizes her older sisters, [Elia Sand](wiki:Elia_Sand) and [Obella Sand](wiki:Obella_Sand).(wiki:Loreza_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

After the death of her father, Prince Oberyn Martell, Loreza's uncle [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell) places her and all her elder sisters into custody out of concern for their reactions to their father's death at the hands of [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane), despite Loreza being only six years old at the time.(wiki:Loreza_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Loreza has just turned seven and is accompanying her mother to [Hellholt](wiki:Hellholt), seat of Loreza's grandfather, Lord [Harmen Uller](wiki:Harmen_Uller).(wiki:Loreza_Sand.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Barianne_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Carianne_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
