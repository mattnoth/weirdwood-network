## Origins

Lord [Torghen Flint](wiki:Torghen_Flint) hates the Weeper for blinding girls and for raping, stealing, or slaying three of [his kin](wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains).(wiki:Weeper.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

[Jarl](wiki:Jarl) has gone over the [Wall](wiki:Wall) with raiders like [Alfyn Crowkiller](wiki:Alfyn_Crowkiller) and the Weeper.(wiki:Weeper.cite_ref-Rasos26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of the Weeper](wiki:Category:Images_of_the_Weeper)*

The Weeper is a thick, blond-haired man, nicknamed for his red rheumy eyes.(wiki:Weeper.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

The Weeper carries a large curved scythe of [steel](wiki:Steel).(wiki:Weeper.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Known for killing [rangers](wiki:Rangers) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) and raiding the [northern mountain clans](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans), he often blinds his victims.(wiki:Weeper.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

[Thistle](wiki:Thistle) believes the Weeper to be a brave raider.(wiki:Weeper.cite_ref-Radwdprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) compares the Weeper to a [demon](wiki:Demon),(wiki:Weeper.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) and [Othell Yarwyck](wiki:Othell_Yarwyck) believes him to be an untrustworthy raper and murderer.(wiki:Weeper.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand) brings news to the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) encampment on the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men) that the Weeping Man has allied himself with [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder), [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall).(wiki:Weeper.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Leading a group of Mance's outriders, the Weeper encounters [Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt) and the captive [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow). The Weeper instructs the Lord of Bones to have Jon brought before Mance.(wiki:Weeper.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) The Weeper leads a band of wildlings which helps keep Mance's vast host moving.(wiki:Weeper.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

The Weeper is spotted near [Icemark](wiki:Icemark) during the wildling assault on the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Weeper.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Along with about three hundred other wildlings, he is defeated by Night's Watch forces led by [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) in a [fight at the Bridge of Skulls](wiki:Fight_at_the_Bridge_of_Skulls). However, the Weeper survives the battle.(wiki:Weeper.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

After Mance's army is defeated in the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), the Weeper gathers warriors at the [Milkwater](wiki:Milkwater) and plans to cross the [Bridge of Skulls](wiki:Bridge_of_Skulls) to take the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower).(wiki:Weeper.cite_ref-Radwdprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Weeper.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) While Jon Snow, now [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), thinks that the remaining free folk [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) will rally behind [Tormund](wiki:Tormund), the captured Mance worries that they will instead support the more belligerent Weeper.(wiki:Weeper.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

The Weeper and his men capture three [rangers](wiki:Rangers) of the Watch, [Garth Greyfeather](wiki:Garth_Greyfeather), [Black Jack Bulwer](wiki:Black_Jack_Bulwer), and [Hairy Hal](wiki:Hairy_Hal). He has them killed, their heads removed, and their eyes plucked out, then leaves their heads on spears near the Wall for the men of the Night's Watch to find.(wiki:Weeper.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

Jon is willing to offer clemency to the Weeper and passage through the Wall if the raider joins the Night's Watch. [Othell Yarwyck](wiki:Othell_Yarwyck) discounts the suggestion, however, and [Torghen Flint](wiki:Torghen_Flint) wants the Weeper imprisoned for his crimes.(wiki:Weeper.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)
