## Origins

Lotho was the son of Lysandro Rogare. He had five trueborn brothers: [Lysaro](wiki:Lysaro_Rogare), [Moredo](wiki:Moredo_Rogare), [Drako](wiki:Drako_Rogare), [Fredo](wiki:Fredo_Rogare), and [Roggerio](wiki:Roggerio_Rogare); and three trueborn sisters: [Larra](wiki:Larra_Rogare), [Lysara](wiki:Lysara_Rogare), and [Marra](wiki:Marra_Rogare), as well as sixteen [bastard](wiki:Bastard) siblings.(wiki:Lotho_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

When his sister Larra came to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) as the wife of Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen), Lotho, Moredo, and Roggerio came with her. Lotho and Rogerrio were fluent in the [Common tongue](wiki:Common_tongue), although Moredo was not. Lotho set about establishing a branch of the [Rogare Bank of Lys](wiki:Rogare_Bank) atop [Visenya's Hill](wiki:Visenya's_Hill). With the Rogares growing in power late in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC), it was feared by some that they would soon rule [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) as well, though [Archmaester](wiki:Archmaester) [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn) notes that suspicion was largely caused from them being foreign. However, it is known that Lotho used [gold](wiki:Gold) to buy friends at court.(wiki:Lotho_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

After Lotho's father [Lysandro "the Magnificent"](wiki:Lysandro_Rogare) drowned, his brother [Lysaro](wiki:Lysaro_Rogare) inherited the Rogare Bank, quickly running it dry with debt and embezzlement. Lotho attempted to depart King's Landing before he could be punished, but was arrested by the [Commander of the City Watch](wiki:Commander_of_the_City_Watch), Ser [Lucas Leygood](wiki:Lucas_Leygood). Because of Lotho's relationship to Prince Viserys's wife, he was imprisoned in the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand) rather than the [black cells](wiki:Black_cells).(wiki:Lotho_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

After the events of the [secret siege](wiki:Secret_siege), Lotho was tried, accused of fraud and theft, and found guilty. Lord [Torrhen Manderly](wiki:Torrhen_Manderly) gave him the chance of joining the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) or losing his right hand [like a common thief](wiki:Laws_and_justice_of_the_Seven_Kingdoms); as Lotho was left-handed, he chose the latter. He was then taken up by Lady [Samantha Tarly](wiki:Samantha_Tarly), [paramour](wiki:Paramour) of Lord [Lyonel Hightower](wiki:Lyonel_Hightower), who had him establish the [Bank of Oldtown](wiki:Bank_of_Oldtown).(wiki:Lotho_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)
