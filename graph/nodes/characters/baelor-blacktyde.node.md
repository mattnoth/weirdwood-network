## Origins

Baelor's father, [Lord Blacktyde](wiki:Lord_Blacktyde), died during the [rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion) launched by [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands). After the war, Baelor spent eight years as a hostage in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). By the time he came back to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands), he had converted to the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven). To this day, Baelor still resents Balon for his father's death, calling him "Balon the Widowmaker".(wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Balon sent [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley) to [ward](wiki:Fosterage) with Baelor after Tris was caught sexually experimenting with Balon's daughter, [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy).(wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Appearances & Description

Baelor is smooth-faced and handsome.(wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) He wears a lambswool tunic of black-and-green vairy, and he pins his [sable](wiki:Sable) cloak with a [silver](wiki:Silver) [seven-pointed star](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Lord Baelor stops at [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers) to consult with Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw), before making his way to [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk) for the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) At the kingsmoot, he asks [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) if he would end the war. When Victarion rejects the idea, Baelor moves on.(wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) He is one of the first to shout [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy)'s name at the kingsmoot.(wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

After [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) is raised as [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands) at the kingsmoot, Baelor refuses to acknowledge Euron and attempts to return to [Blacktyde](wiki:Blacktyde). However, Victarion closes [Nagga's Cradle](wiki:Nagga's_Cradle) with the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) and captures *[Nightflyer](wiki:Nightflyer)*, and Baelor is taken and delivered to King Euron in chains. As punishment for his refusal to acknowledge his new liege, Baelor is cut by Euron's crew into seven pieces, representing the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Euron claims Baelor's [sable](wiki:Sable) cloak for himself.(wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
