## Identity

Grey Worm is an Unsullied warrior and commander sworn to Daenerys Targaryen (track_b: Title, Allegiances).

## Origins

Born in Essos (track_b: Born). His birth name is unknown; "Grey Worm" is the name he chose to keep after Daenerys freed the Unsullied, as it was the name he bore on the day she liberated them.

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` Unsullied (track_b: Allegiances). `SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiances).

## Narrative Arc

Grey Worm serves as a leader among the Unsullied after Daenerys purchases and frees them in Astapor. When told to choose new names, he keeps "Grey Worm" as his lucky name. He serves Daenerys loyally through her conquest of Slaver's Bay and the governance of Meereen, commanding the Unsullied in her name.
