## Origins

During the rite of manhood for his clan, Timett burned one of his eyes out of its socket with a hot knife. Because the typical sacrifice for the rite is a nipple or finger, the impressed clan elders immediately named him a [red hand](wiki:Red_hand) of the tribe. Timett was young for this position, but he inspired fear even among his fellow clansmen.(wiki:Timett.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Timett](wiki:Category:Images_of_Timett)*

Timett is a tall, fierce young man with a burned out left eye, which has been left an empty pit.(wiki:Timett.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He is not yet twenty years of age.(wiki:Timett.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Timett enjoys games like [dice](wiki:Dice)(wiki:Timett.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) and [tiles](wiki:Tiles).(wiki:Timett.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Timett joins the group of [Vale mountain clansmen](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) who choose to follow [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and join the army of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister). He serves as the leader of the [Burned Men](wiki:Burned_Men) in the group.(wiki:Timett.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) Timett fights in the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork), vaulting from his horse when it dies during the charge. After the battle, he loots the bodies of the slain with his Burned Men.(wiki:Timett.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Timett follows Tyrion to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). While gambling on the Street of Silver, Timett catches a wineseller's son cheating at tiles. Timett pins the man's wrist to the table with a dagger and then tears out his throat barehanded.(wiki:Timett.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

Timett becomes one of Tyrion's chief enforcers, along with [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) and [Shagga](wiki:Shagga), during Tyrion's tenure as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King). He participates in Tyrion's intimidation of Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle).(wiki:Timett.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

Before the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Tyrion sends Timett with the rest of the remaining clansmen to the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood), where he raids enemy forces.(wiki:Timett.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Timett attempts to return to King's Landing to serve Tyrion, but is driven off because of Tyrion's injury. He and his men instead return to the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon) with their plunder from [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s camp at the Blackwater.(wiki:Timett.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

When [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) attempts to enter the mountains to travel to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), he is warned off by locals, one of whom states how ferocious the mountain clans have become after Timett One-Eye's return.(wiki:Timett.cite_ref-Rasos65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

During the trial of Tyrion for [the death](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), Tyrion's lover [Shae](wiki:Shae) is the last witness for the Crown. She claims that Tyrion had ordered Timett and [Shagga](wiki:Shagga) to forcefully bring her to Tyrion after the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:Timett.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) In actuality, she was a willing camp follower found by Bronn.(wiki:Timett.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
