## Appearances & Description

Bobono is a dwarf. He has a big voice for such a little man, when on stage he can make it ring off the highest rafters. Bobono fancies [Mercy](wiki:Arya_Stark).(wiki:Bobono.cite_ref-mercy_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) gets a report claiming that her brother [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) is hiding with a group of [mummers](wiki:Mummer) in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Bobono.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### The Winds of Winter

As the mummers prepare to perform *[The Bloody Hand](wiki:The_Bloody_Hand)* Mercy helps them with their wardrobe, Bobono included.(wiki:Bobono.cite_ref-mercy_1-1)
