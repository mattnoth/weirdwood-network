## Identity

Butterbumps is the fool and jester of House Tyrell (wiki:Butterbumps.cite_ref-Rasos6).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Tyrell (track_b: Allegiance). Serves as the household jester.

## Appearances & Description

Butterbumps is immensely fat (wiki:Butterbumps.cite_ref-Racokappendix). He wears a jester's suit of green and yellow feathers with a floppy coxcomb. Despite his size, he is capable of tumbling, juggling, singing, and sleight of hand. He can also fart at will (wiki:Butterbumps.cite_ref-Rasos6).

## Narrative Arc

Butterbumps travels to King's Landing with the Tyrell retinue. When Olenna Redwyne and Margaery Tyrell invite Sansa Stark to lunch to question her about Joffrey, they have Butterbumps sing "The Bear and the Maiden Fair" at exceptional volume to mask their conversation (wiki:Butterbumps.cite_ref-Rasos6). He performs at the Purple Wedding alongside Moon Boy (wiki:Butterbumps.cite_ref-Rasos60) and later at the wedding of Margaery and Tommen (wiki:Butterbumps.cite_ref-Raffc12).
