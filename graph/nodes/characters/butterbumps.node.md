## Appearances & Description

Butterbumps is immensely fat.(wiki:Butterbumps.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He owns a jester's suit of green and yellow feathers with a floppy coxcomb.(wiki:Butterbumps.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Despite his size, he is capable of tumbling, juggling, singing, and sleight of hand.(wiki:Butterbumps.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Butterbumps.cite_ref-Rawoiafbutterbumps.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He can also fart at will.(wiki:Butterbumps.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Butterbumps travels to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with the retinue of [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell). When [Olenna Redwyne](wiki:Olenna_Redwyne) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) invite [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) to lunch to question the girl about the character of Margaery's betrothed, King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), they mask their conversation from unwanted ears by having Butterbumps sing *[The Bear and the Maiden Fair](wiki:The_Bear_and_the_Maiden_Fair)* at exceptional volume.(wiki:Butterbumps.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Butterbumps is present at [the wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of Margaery and Joffrey, where he performs together with [Moon Boy](wiki:Moon_Boy).(wiki:Butterbumps.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Butterbumps entertains the guests at [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Tommen_I_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) of [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) and King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) together with [Moon Boy](wiki:Moon_Boy) between dishes.(wiki:Butterbumps.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
