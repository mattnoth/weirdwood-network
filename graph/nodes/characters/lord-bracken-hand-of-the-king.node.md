## Origins

When the Princesses [Daena](wiki:Daena_Targaryen), [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon_III)), and [Elaena Targaryen](wiki:Elaena_Targaryen) were confined to the [Maidenvault](wiki:Maidenvault) in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), they were accompanied only by [maidens](wiki:Maiden) who had been sent to court by lords and knights who wanted to gain favor with King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord_Bracken_(Hand_of_the_King).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Near the end of Baelor's reign, one of these maidens was Lord Bracken's daughter [Barba](wiki:Barba_Bracken). When King Baelor I died in [171 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), King [Viserys II Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen) released Daena, Rhaena, and Elaena from their confinement, and Barba met Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen), who became entranced with her. On King Aegon IV Targaryen's ascent to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) in [172 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), he named Lord Bracken as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) and openly took Barba as his fifth mistress.(wiki:Lord_Bracken_(Hand_of_the_King).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Later that year, when Queen [Naerys Targaryen](wiki:Naerys_Targaryen) was lingering near death from a difficult childbirth, Lord Bracken talked openly of wedding Barba to the king. However, these talks became Lord Bracken's undoing, as Naerys's son, Prince [Daeron](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen), and her brother, Prince [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_II)), forced Aegon to send Barba and the king's newborn [bastard](wiki:Bastard) [son](wiki:Aegor_Rivers) away from court.(wiki:Lord_Bracken_(Hand_of_the_King).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

After Lord Bracken's daughter was replaced as the king's mistress by [Melissa Blackwood](wiki:Melissa_Blackwood), Lord Bracken and Barba started grooming his younger daughter, [Bethany](wiki:Bethany_Bracken), to win the king's favor. In [177 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Bethany caught the king's eye, and he took her as his seventh mistress. However, in [178 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Bethany was caught in bed with Ser [Terrence Toyne](wiki:Terrence_Toyne), a [knight](wiki:Knight) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). Both Bethany and Lord Bracken were executed.(wiki:Lord_Bracken_(Hand_of_the_King).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
