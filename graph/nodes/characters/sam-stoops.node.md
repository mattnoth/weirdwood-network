## Origins

When Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) trained [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk), Sam stuffed some old sacks with soiled straw for them to practice on. he also provided straw pallets in the undercroft of [Standfast](wiki:Standfast) for the recruits to rest upon. He began praying when he saw [Wat's Wood](wiki:Wat's_Wood) afire. While Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey) was away, Ser [Bennis of the Brown Shield](wiki:Bennis_of_the_Brown_Shield) tied up Sam and [his wife](wiki:Sam_Stoops'_wife), and stole the remaining valuables from Standfast.(wiki:Sam_Stoops.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
