## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Meredyth is one of [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell)'s [lady attendants](wiki:Handmaid) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Meredyth_Crane.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Feast for Crows

Meredyth is part of Margaery's retinue. She often goes [hawking](wiki:Hawk) with [Janna Fossoway](wiki:Janna_Fossoway) and Queen Margaery.(wiki:Meredyth_Crane.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Merry and Janna are escorted by the twins Ser [Horas](wiki:Horas_Redwyne) and Ser [Hobber Redwyne](wiki:Hobber_Redwyne) during one of Margaery's rides, returning with [flowers](wiki:Flower) in their hair.(wiki:Meredyth_Crane.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
