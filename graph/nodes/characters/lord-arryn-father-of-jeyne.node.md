## Origins

Lord Arryn and his sons were killed by [Stone Crows](wiki:Stone_Crows) in [97 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). He was succeeded as ruler of the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) by his daughter, [Jeyne](wiki:Jeyne_Arryn).(wiki:Lord_Arryn_(father_of_Jeyne).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) As Jeyne was three years old at the time of her father's death, Lord [Yorbert Royce](wiki:Yorbert_Royce) ruled in her stead as [regent](wiki:Regent) and [Lord Protector of the Vale](wiki:Lord_Protector_of_the_Vale) during her [minority](wiki:Minority).(wiki:Lord_Arryn_(father_of_Jeyne).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale:_house_arryn.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Lord_Arryn_(father_of_Jeyne).cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
