## Identity

Lord Arryn was the Lord of the Eyrie and head of House Arryn during the late reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen. His personal name is unknown (wiki:Lord_Arryn_(father_of_Jeyne)).

## Origins

## Narrative Arc

Lord Arryn and his sons were killed by Stone Crows in 97 AC. He was succeeded by his three-year-old daughter Jeyne, with Lord Yorbert Royce serving as regent and Lord Protector of the Vale during her minority (wiki:Lord_Arryn_(father_of_Jeyne)).
