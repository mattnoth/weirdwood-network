## Identity

Sandoq the Shadow — A towering mute from the fighting pits of Meereen, given to Larra by her father Lysandro.

## Origins

Sandoq's history before his service to [House Rogare](wiki:House_Rogare) is uncertain, but it is said that he had been a veteran [pit fighter](wiki:Pit_fighter) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), with a hundred wins to his name.(wiki:Sandoq_the_Shadow.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

In [135 AC](wiki:135_AC), during the plot against the Rogare family amidst the [Lysene Spring](wiki:Lysene_Spring), Sandoq the Shadow protected his lady's husband, Prince [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen), and his brother, King [Aegon III](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), from Ser [Amaury Peake](wiki:Amaury_Peake) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) and a dozen guardsmen, slaying them all on the drawbridge of [Maegor's Holdfast](wiki:Maegor's_Holdfast). Thus began an [eighteen-day standoff](wiki:Secret_siege) before the plot unraveled.(wiki:Sandoq_the_Shadow.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

Sandoq, alongside several of the white cloaks, accompanied King Aegon upon his sixteenth [nameday](wiki:Nameday) when he entered the [small council](wiki:Small_council) chambers. Sandoq's presence unnerved the [Hand of the King](wiki:Torrhen_Manderly) and the [regents](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III) as the king dismissed them from their service.(wiki:Sandoq_the_Shadow.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

## Appearances & Description

Sandoq stood nearly seven feet tall, with black skin and hair. His face was horribly disfigured; it bore numerous thin white scars, and his lips and tongue had been removed. He typically hid his disfigurement under a black silk veil. Sandoq the Shadow was also illiterate, which combined with his disfigurement meant it was impossible for him to communicate complex messages.(wiki:Sandoq_the_Shadow.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

While at first impression the mute giant could seem a menacing brute, Sandoq was actually a skilled musician: he delicately played sweet sad songs on a [stringed instrument](wiki:Sandoq_the_Shadow's_instrument) of [goldenheart](wiki:Goldenheart) and [ebony](wiki:Ebony).(wiki:Sandoq_the_Shadow.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Sandoq wielded a great curved sword of [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) with a [dragonbone](wiki:Dragonbone) hilt, as well as a tall black shield of [nightwood](wiki:Nightwood), boiled hide, and [iron](wiki:Iron).(wiki:Sandoq_the_Shadow.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
