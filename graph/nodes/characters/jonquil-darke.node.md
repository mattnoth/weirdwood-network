## Origins

At the [Golden Wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC), a test of arms that became known as the [War for the White Cloaks](wiki:War_for_the_White_Cloaks) was held, with hundreds of [knights](wiki:Knight) competing for the honor of serving in the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) of [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen). Jonquil took part as a slender [mystery knight](wiki:Mystery_knight) known as the *[Serpent](wiki:Serpent) in Scarlet*. She was eventually defeated and unmasked, and became a great favorite with the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk).(wiki:Jonquil_Darke.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

In [51 AC](wiki:51_AC), after an attempted assassination of Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) at the sacred waters known as [Jonquil's Pool](wiki:Jonquil's_Pool) in [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool), it was decided that the queen needed a protector who could accompany her where men couldn't go. Jaehaerys sent a raven to [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale), commanding the new Lord [Darklyn](wiki:House_Darklyn) to send his bastard half-sister to court. Jonquil arrived in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) a few days later, and gladly accepted the position of Queen Alysanne's [sworn shield](wiki:Sworn_shield) and protector. In time she became known as the *Scarlet Shadow*, so closely did she guard her lady.(wiki:Jonquil_Darke.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

When the [High Septon](wiki:High_Lickspittle) passed away in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), Jaehaerys and Alysanne traveled to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) upon their [dragons](wiki:Dragon) to pay their respects, and to influence the choice of the next [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon). They took two protectors with them. To balance the weight for the dragons, Jonquil rode with King Jaehaerys on [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor), while Ser [Joffrey Doggett](wiki:Joffrey_Doggett) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) rode with Alysanne on [Silverwing](wiki:Silverwing).(wiki:Jonquil_Darke.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Jonquil traveled with Queen Alysanne when she went on a [progress](wiki:Progress) of the [North](wiki:North) in [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). A small [tourney](wiki:Tourney) was staged in [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) by [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) in the queen's honor. One of the competitors was a [wildling](wiki:Wildling) woman, captured by [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) [rangers](wiki:Rangers) north of the [Wall](wiki:Wall), and given to one of Lord [Theomore Manderly](wiki:Theomore_Manderly)'s knights to [foster](wiki:Foster). Impressed with the woman's daring, Alysanne had Jonquil spar with her. The two women dueled with sword and spear, while the northmen roared with approval. (wiki:Jonquil_Darke.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

When the scandals committed by Princess [Saera Targaryen](wiki:Saera_Targaryen) came to light in [84 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), and the young princess was confined to her room only to later attempt to escape to the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit), the king confined Saera to a tower cell, with Jonquil guarding her all day and night, even in the privy. When King Jaehaerys faced one of Saera's lovers, Ser [Braxton Beesbury](wiki:Braxton_Beesbury), in a [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat), Jonquil made sure Saera watched from the window of her cell as her father slew him with [Blackfyre](wiki:Blackfyre).(wiki:Jonquil_Darke.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
