## Identity

Arrana Stark was a noblewoman of House Stark during the latter half of the second century after Aegon's Conquest. She was the older daughter of Edric Stark and Serena Stark (wiki:Arrana_Stark).

## Origins

Born between 154 and 209 AC. Died 165 AC. Daughter of Edric Stark and Serena Stark (track_b: Father, track_b: Mother).
