## Origins

Gerardys served Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). He also flew with Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) to help his wife Lady [Laena Velaryon](wiki:Laena_Velaryon) after a difficult childbirth, but they arrived too late. When King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) fell and sliced his hand open on the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), Rhaenyra flew Gerardys, accounted a more gifted healer, to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) after [Grand Maester Mellos](wiki:Mellos) was unsuccessful in treating the laceration. Gerardys amputated two of the king's fingers, saving his life.(wiki:Gerardys.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

After [Mellos](wiki:Mellos) passed away, Rhaenyra recommended that Gerardys take his place as Grand Maester. [Queen Alicent](wiki:Alicent_Hightower), on the other hand, wanted Maester [Alfador](wiki:Alfador), who served [House Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower), named to the office. Beset from both sides, Viserys chose neither, reminding both his wife and daughter that the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) would decide who filled the office. Archmaester [Orwyle](wiki:Orwyle) was in the end selected by the [conclave](wiki:Conclave) as the new Grand Maester.(wiki:Gerardys.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Gerardys.cite_ref-3)
