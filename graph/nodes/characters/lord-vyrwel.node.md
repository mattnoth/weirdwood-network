## Origins

In [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), Lord Vyrwel came with [his lady wife](wiki:Lady_Vyrwel) to [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) to attend the [wedding](wiki:Wedding) of [the daughter](wiki:Lady_Butterwell) of [Lord Frey](wiki:Lord_Frey) to Lord [Ambrose Butterwell](wiki:Ambrose_Butterwell). During the wedding feast, Lord Vyrwel was seated at the top of the hall in a place of high honor.(wiki:Lord_Vyrwel.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Ser [Glendon Flowers](wiki:Glendon_Flowers) was imprisoned after being accused of stealing the prize of the [wedding's tourney](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls), Ambrose's treasured [dragon egg](wiki:Dragon_egg). When Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) publicly declared that Glendon had been falsely accused, [Daemon II Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre) ordered Lords Vyrwel, [Sunderland](wiki:Lord_Sunderland), and [Smallwood](wiki:Lord_Smallwood) to fetch Glendon from the dungeons.(wiki:Lord_Vyrwel.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

After the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion) was put down, a man in service to Lord Vyrwel claimed to have been one of the spies of Lord [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers). He was promptly killed by a [knight](wiki:Knight) in service to [Lord Costayne](wiki:Lord_Costayne_(Aerys_I)).(wiki:Lord_Vyrwel.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) It is unknown if Lord Vyrwel suffered any punishment for the part he played in the rebellion.
