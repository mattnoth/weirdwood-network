## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Garth is among the members of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) who accompany [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), on the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging). He survives the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist) and is among the survivors who make it to [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep). Garth is skeptical about [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) having slain an [Other](wiki:Other). Garth becomes concerned when [Craster](wiki:Craster) beats one of his [younger wives](wiki:Craster's_wives), and he thinks that Craster is hoarding apples. The blade-wielding Garth and [Ollo Lophand](wiki:Ollo_Lophand) prevent Jeor from interceding during the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep), and Ollo mortally stabs the Lord Commander. During the turmoil, Garth of Greenaway kills [Garth of Oldtown](wiki:Garth_of_Oldtown).(wiki:Garth_of_Greenaway.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Garth_of_Greenaway.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Garth remains at Craster's Keep after the betrayal, along with a few other former members of the Night's Watch.(wiki:Garth_of_Greenaway.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance With Dragons[ ]

Five betrayers wander in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest), planning to head south over the [Wall](wiki:Wall). However, the traitors are killed by [Coldhands](wiki:Coldhands) and his [ravens](wiki:Raven). [Summer](wiki:Summer), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark)'s [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), finds the bodies and eats parts of the meat.(wiki:Garth_of_Greenaway.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) It is unknown if Garth is among them.
According to a semi-canon source, Garth is killed by [Others](wiki:Others) and turned into a [wight](wiki:Wight).(wiki:Garth_of_Greenaway.cite_ref-Rawoiafgarth_of_greenaway.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
