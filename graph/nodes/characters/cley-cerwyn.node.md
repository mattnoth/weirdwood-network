## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

While his father Lord [Medger Cerwyn](wiki:Medger_Cerwyn) is a captive at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Cley attends the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) in his place, bringing two dozen lances with him. He brings the news to Winterfell of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) being a king and of his letter concerning the illegitimacy of Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s children.(wiki:Cley_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) During the harvest feast Cley dances with [Beth Cassel](wiki:Beth_Cassel).(wiki:Cley_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He becomes Lord of Cerwyn(wiki:Cley_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) when his father dies at Harrenhal.(wiki:Cley_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

When the [ironmen](wiki:Ironmen) attack [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Torrhen's_Square), Lord Cley brings three hundred men as part of the relief force.(wiki:Cley_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Cley joins Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) and marches to the relief of Winterfell. [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) leads a [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) force in [betraying Rodrik's forces](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell); Cley is killed from an arrow through the eye.(wiki:Cley_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Cley is succeeded by his older sister, Lady [Jonelle Cerwyn](wiki:Jonelle_Cerwyn).(wiki:Cley_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
