## Origins

Khal Loso joined forces with Khals [Haro](wiki:Haro), [Qano](wiki:Qano), and [Zhako](wiki:Zhako) to fight against High King [Mazor Alexi](wiki:Mazor_Alexi), who had united all of the [Sarnori](wiki:Tall_Men) lesser kings to defend their homeland against the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) hordes. The battle took place halfway between [Sarnath](wiki:Vaes_Khewo) and the ruins of [Kasath](wiki:Vojjor_Samvi). The Sarnori [chariots](wiki:Chariot) smashed the center of the Dothraki horde. Haro was trampled and died, while his riders fled, chased by Alexi and his cavalry. However, the escape was feigned and Haro's riders turned back, raining arrows on the Sarnori, while the other *khals* surrounded the Tall Men on all sides, with Loso's *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* attacking the rear.(wiki:Loso.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Less than a fortnight later, Loso sacked Sarnath and put the city to the torch. Not even the [Palace With a Thousand Rooms](wiki:Palace_With_a_Thousand_Rooms) was spared.(wiki:Loso.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
