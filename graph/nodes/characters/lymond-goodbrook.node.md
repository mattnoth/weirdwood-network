## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Lord Lymond is among the nobles from the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) who accompany Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) to his wedding at [the Twins](wiki:The_Twins).(wiki:Lymond_Goodbrook.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

After the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Lymond is among the riverlords pardoned by King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) and is subsequently welcomed back into the king's peace.(wiki:Lymond_Goodbrook.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Lord Lymond is presumably one of the riverlords present at the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun) since Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) sees the Goodbrook banner when he arrives at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Lymond_Goodbrook.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
