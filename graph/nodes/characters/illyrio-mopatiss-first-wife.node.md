## Origins

With the aid of [Varys](wiki:Varys), Illyrio Mopatis reached a position where the daughter of a Magister was willing to marry him.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis's_first_wife.cite_ref-1)

Illyrio upset the cousin of his first wife, the [Prince of Pentos](wiki:Prince_of_Pentos), after he married [Serra](wiki:Serra), a former bedslave he had bought and come to love.
