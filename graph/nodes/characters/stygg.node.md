## Appearances & Description

Stygg is bald.(wiki:Stygg.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He wields an axe(wiki:Stygg.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and a spear three feet taller than he is.(wiki:Stygg.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) does not think Stygg is a particularly skilled headsman(wiki:Stygg.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) or rider.(wiki:Stygg.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Stygg participates in the [harrying of the Stony Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore) under [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy)'s command. Stygg and [Werlag](wiki:Werlag) drown the captured [Benfred Tallhart](wiki:Benfred_Tallhart) upon the recommendation of Theon's uncle, [Aeron Damphair](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy).(wiki:Stygg.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Stygg is also part of Theon's force in the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell). When Theon speaks with the castle's inhabitants demanding fealty, [Mikken](wiki:Mikken) shouts insults and is beaten by Stygg to silence him. When the [smith](wiki:Smith) persists, Stygg kills him, stabbing him in the throat with his spear. The captive [Osha](wiki:Osha) says she will serve in return for being armed again, to which Stygg harasses her. In response, Osha knees Stygg in the crotch and knocks him to the ground with the butt of his own spear while the other [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) laugh.(wiki:Stygg.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Theon sends Stygg to request aid from his sister, [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), at [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Stygg.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Asha eventually arrives at Winterfell with a small group of men.(wiki:Stygg.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

When [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel)'s northmen arrive to retake Winterfell, Stygg is among those ironborn who abandon Theon to join Asha, though he is the only one who looks ashamed about it.(wiki:Stygg.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
