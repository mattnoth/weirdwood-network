## Origins

[House Humble](wiki:House_Humble) descends from salt wives and thralls.(wiki:Salt_wife.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
The [Wulls](wiki:House_Wull) who live along the [Bay of Ice](wiki:Bay_of_Ice) hate the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) for taking their wives and daughters as [thralls](wiki:Thrall) and salt wives.(wiki:Salt_wife.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_mountain_clans.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Harwyn Hoare](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), killed Lady [Agnes Blackwood](wiki:Agnes_Blackwood) during his conquest of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) when she refused to become his salt wife.(wiki:Salt_wife.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Queen [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) urged King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) to forbid the stealing of women in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), so salt marriages have declined since [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Salt_wife.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Ironborn still raid the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) and the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea), and some wish the return of the [Old Way](wiki:Old_Way).(wiki:Salt_wife.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy) claimed four of Lord [Farman's](wiki:House_Farman) daughters as salt wives.(wiki:Salt_wife.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) After the civil war, the Red Kraken refused the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne)'s order to restore women carried away by Dalton's ironmen.(wiki:Salt_wife.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Dalton was eventually killed by [Tess](wiki:Tess), one of his twenty-two salt wives. Dalton did not have a [rock wife](wiki:Rock_wife), so succession passed to [Toron Greyjoy](wiki:Toron_Greyjoy), the eldest of his [salt sons](wiki:Salt_son). Toron's claim was contested, however, and a [struggle for power in the Iron Islands](wiki:Struggle_for_power_in_the_Iron_Islands) broke out.(wiki:Salt_wife.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_revents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
When he was fifteen, [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) reaved in the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) with [Dagmer Cleftjaw](wiki:Dagmer_Cleftjaw) and took his first two salt wives.(wiki:Salt_wife.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
[Victarion Greyjoy's third wife](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy's_third_wife) was a salt wife.(wiki:Salt_wife.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[The daughter](wiki:Daughter_of_the_captain_of_the_Myraham) of [the captain](wiki:Captain_of_the_Myraham) of the [Myraham](wiki:Myraham) sleeps with [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) and offers to become his salt wife, but he refuses.(wiki:Salt_wife.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
During the [harrying of the Stony Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore), wives and daughters from a fishing village are claimed as salt wives.(wiki:Salt_wife.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Salt wives and [thralls](wiki:Thrall) serve the ironborn who attend the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Salt_wife.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
Instead of having them brought back to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) as salt wives or [thralls](wiki:Thrall), [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) decides to have captives from the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields) sold as [slaves](wiki:Slaves), which upsets his more traditional brother [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy).(wiki:Salt_wife.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The [Willing Maiden](wiki:Willing_Maiden) is captured while the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) sails toward [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), and Victarion announces that enslaved girls on the ship can be honored by becoming salt wives.(wiki:Salt_wife.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
