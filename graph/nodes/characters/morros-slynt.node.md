### A Clash of Kings

Morros, a new-made squire, wears a checkered black-and-gold cloak over black armor inlaid with [golden](wiki:Gold) scrollwork. He rides in the [tourney on King Joffrey's name day](wiki:Tourney_on_King_Joffrey's_name_day), but he is unhorsed by Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) in the second tilt of the day.(wiki:Morros_Slynt.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Upon Janos's banishment to the [Wall](wiki:Wall), Morros has become a lord.(wiki:Morros_Slynt.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) spies Morros getting beaten in the yard by [Osfryd Kettleblack](wiki:Osfryd_Kettleblack).(wiki:Morros_Slynt.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) warns [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) that Morros and his brothers would gladly inform on Tyrion to avenge their father.(wiki:Morros_Slynt.cite_ref-Rasos12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Morros testifies at the trial of Tyrion for the death of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) that he had seen Tyrion gather the [poisoned](wiki:Poison) [chalice](wiki:Wedding_chalice) off the floor and poured the remaining contents on the floor.(wiki:Morros_Slynt.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
