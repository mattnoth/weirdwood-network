## Origins

At the start of [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), the lords of the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters) took advantage of the chaos in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) and rebelled against young King [Ronnel Arryn](wiki:Ronnel_Arryn), installing Lady Marla as their queen and launching the [Sistermen's Rebellion](wiki:Sistermen's_Rebellion).(wiki:Marla_Sunderland.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons_-_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) As their fleet had largely been [destroyed](wiki:Battle_in_the_waters_off_Gulltown) during the Conquest, the [Arryns](wiki:House_Arryn) had little means of quelling the rebellion themselves.(wiki:Marla_Sunderland.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

With the Conquest over and the Vale now part of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) tasked Lord [Torrhen Stark](wiki:Torrhen_Stark), newly named by the Conqueror as [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), with suppressing the Sistermen's Rebellion. Led by Ser [Warrick Manderly](wiki:Warrick_Manderly), the [northern](wiki:North) host sailed to the Three Sisters aboard galleys hired from [Braavos](wiki:Braavos). The sight of the sails, and the sudden appearance of Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) mounted on her [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar), took the heart out of the Sistermen. Queen Marla was deposed by her own people with her younger brother [Steffon](wiki:Steffon_Sunderland) bending the knee and renewing fealty to House Arryn.(wiki:Marla_Sunderland.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) The deposed Marla was exiled and imprisoned. After five years, in [6 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest),(wiki:Marla_Sunderland.cite_ref-Rtrotd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) she had her tongue removed and ended her days with the [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters), tending to the noble dead.(wiki:Marla_Sunderland.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)(wiki:Marla_Sunderland.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
