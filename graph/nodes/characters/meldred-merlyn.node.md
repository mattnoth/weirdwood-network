## Appearances & Description

Meldred is a round and fleshy man, bald and bearded. He has a fat white face(wiki:Meldred_Merlyn.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) which turns red when he shouts.(wiki:Meldred_Merlyn.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He dresses in furs and velvets and has a fox-fur mantle.(wiki:Meldred_Merlyn.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Meldred uses the title "[lord](wiki:Lord)" in the manner of the [green lands](wiki:Green_lands), but [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) thinks of him as "the Merlyn".(wiki:Meldred_Merlyn.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

At [Pebbleton](wiki:Pebbleton), Lord Meldred is blessed with seawater by [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy), and he is among the first to hear the Damphair call for a [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot).(wiki:Meldred_Merlyn.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Lord Merlyn attends the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), where he is among the supporters of [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy).(wiki:Meldred_Merlyn.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
