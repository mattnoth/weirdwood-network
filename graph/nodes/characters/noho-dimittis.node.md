## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Noho travels to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to convince the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) to resume paying its debts to the [Iron Bank of Braavos](wiki:Iron_Bank_of_Braavos). During the first fortnight of his stay at King's Landing, Noho is forestalled by Lord [Gyles Rosby](wiki:Gyles_Rosby), the lord treasurer. Noho meets with Lord Gyles six times, but the latter cannot placate him.(wiki:Noho_Dimittis.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

When he is finally allowed to meet with Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), Noho is not amused to be told by Cersei to speak with Gyles a seventh time, as the number seven is [sacred to the gods](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). When Noho scowls at her, Cersei brings the audience to an abrupt end, informing him that the Iron Bank will receive its money after the rebellions in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) are dealt with.(wiki:Noho_Dimittis.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
