## Origins

Jory is the son of [Martyn Cassel](wiki:Martyn_Cassel) and the nephew of Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel), master-at-arms of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Jory attended the [weddings of Catelyn and Lysa Tully](wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun). During the [bedding](wiki:Bedding) custom, he ripped [Catelyn's](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) gown in haste.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) At the end of the war, Jory's father [died fighting](wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy) alongside Eddard in the [combat at the tower of joy](wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy).(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

Jory has served as Lord Eddard's captain of the [guard](wiki:House_Stark_guards) for more than eight years.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) Jory once brought [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark), and [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) into the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) to fish for trout in a rushing stream near Winterfell.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3) Robb and Jon have often hunted in the forest with their father and Jory.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) The captain has also often sparred in Winterfell's yard with Robb, Jon, and [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy).(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

A laughing Jory once pulled apart [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) when the sisters were having a snowball fight after a summer's snow.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Jory has chased Bran across the roofs of Winterfell on multiple occasions.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Jory accompanies Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) to witness the execution of [Gared](wiki:Gared), a deserter from the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). When the party finds [direwolves](wiki:Direwolf) in the snow while returning home, he claims it is an omen that the direwolf had an antler stuck in its throat and that the pups had been born after their mother had died.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

As part of the plans for [the feast](wiki:Feast_in_honor_of_King_Robert's_visit_to_Winterfell) in King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s honor, Eddard plans to send Jory south to meet Robert on the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad) with an honor guard.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Jory joins Robert's hunt for [boar](wiki:Boar) before the king departs [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) for the south.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

Jory accompanies Lord Stark south to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) as his captain of the guard. After the incident at the [Trident](wiki:Trident) where [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)'s direwolf assaults Prince [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), Jory is the first to find Arya, who had fled.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) He helps Arya chase away [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf)) by throwing stones at her, reassuring Arya that the woods have plenty of game for the direwolf to hunt.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

At [Darry](wiki:Darry), Jory tells Robert that no trace of Arya's direwolf was found. When Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) insists that [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark)'s direwolf, [Lady](wiki:Lady), be killed instead, Ned has Jory bring him [Ice](wiki:Ice). After Ned kills his elder daughter's pet, Jory sends four men to escort the bones of Lady back to Winterfell.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1)

As a guard for the new [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Jory receives a new cloak, heavy grey wool with a white satin border.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1) Lord Stark has Jory provide twenty of his fifty guardsmen to aid the [City Watch of King's Landing](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing) in keeping peace during the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney).(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2)

Jory aids Eddard into his investigation of the death of his predecessor, Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), speaking with former members of the late Hand's household. The newly-knighted Ser [Hugh](wiki:Hugh), once Jon's squire, refuses to speak with Jory, but the captain is able to speak with a stableboy-turned-watchman, a serving girl, and a potboy-turned-cordwainer.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-3) Since the watchman revealed that Jon and Lord [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) had visited a [brothel](wiki:Brothel), Ned has Jory investigate various whorehouses in the city.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-4)(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)

Jory takes part in the tourney, unhorsing Ser [Horas Redwyne](wiki:Horas_Redwyne) and one of the [Freys](wiki:House_Frey). He loses to [Lothor Brune](wiki:Lothor_Brune) in the tourney only after a judgement by King Robert.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-3) Ser Hugh of the Vale is killed by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) in the lists.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-4) Jory wakes Eddard in the night to inform him that Robert intends to fight in the melee, and Ned and Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) convince the king not to do so.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-2)

Jory accompanies Lord Eddard to [Chataya's brothel](wiki:Chataya's_brothel) to find one of Robert's [bastards](wiki:Bastard), [Barra](wiki:Barra). On the return trip to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), however, Jory, [Heward](wiki:Heward), and [Wyl](wiki:Wyl_(guard)) are slain in the streets of King's Landing by [House Lannister guards](wiki:House_Lannister_guards). Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) intends their deaths to be a lesson to Eddard, as Eddard's wife, [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), had abducted Jaime's brother, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister). After the Lannisters depart, an injured Ned cradles Jory's body in the street.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

Lord Stark appoints [Alyn](wiki:Alyn) as Jory's replacement. Ned sends Jory's body to the [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters), so they can prepare it for burial at Winterfell besides the grave of his grandfather.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Following his [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell), [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) sees Jory while having a nightmare of a feast.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

Jory is among those for whom [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) sings during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

After returning to Winterfell as a prisoner of [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton), Theon recalls Jory and other people of the castle who have died.(wiki:Jory_Cassel.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)
