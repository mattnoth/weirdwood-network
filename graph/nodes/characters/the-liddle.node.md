## Origins

The man learned the phrase "[dark wings](wiki:Raven), dark words" from his mother, who died nine years ago.(wiki:The_Liddle.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Appearances & Description

The Liddle is generous with food and shelter. His [squirrelskin](wiki:Squirrel) cloak is clasped with a [pinecone](wiki:Pine) made of [gold](wiki:Gold) and [bronze](wiki:Bronze), and he carries a knife and [green and white](wiki:House_Liddle) cloth.(wiki:The_Liddle.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

One day when [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark), [Hodor](wiki:Hodor), and [Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed) and [Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed) are traveling through the [northern mountains](wiki:Northern_mountains) toward the [Wall](wiki:Wall), a burst of freezing rain makes them look for shelter. Bran's [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) [Summer](wiki:Summer) finds a shallow cave behind a [sentinel](wiki:Sentinel) tree, but when they enter, they find it already occupied. However, the traveler invites them to share the shelter of the cave and the warmth of his fire. The man does not ask their names, nor gives his. Bran assumes he is a [Liddle](wiki:House_Liddle) because of the man's [pinecone](wiki:Pine)-shaped cloak clasp, however, and the man is implied to recognize Bran and his direwolf as [Starks](wiki:House_Stark). The Liddle offers oatcakes, blood sausage, and [ale](wiki:Ale) to Bran's party. He informs them that [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) are in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood), and he looks at Bran and Summer after warning that the [Bastard](wiki:Ramsay_Snow)'s [boys](wiki:Bastard's_boys) are hunting along the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad). He also mentions the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) into the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest) by the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:The_Liddle.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

The Liddle departs before Bran and his companions wake in the morning, but he leaves them a sausage and twelve oatcakes containing pinenuts or blackberries, wrapped in green and white cloth. Bran has one of each oatcake, and greatly enjoys the food. He thinks that when [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) returns to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), he will pay back House Liddle a hundredfold.(wiki:The_Liddle.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
