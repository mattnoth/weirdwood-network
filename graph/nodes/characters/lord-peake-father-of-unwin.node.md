## Origins

After the death of Prince [Baelon Targaryen](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)), [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone) and [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) summoned the nobility of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) to form a [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council) and choose the new heir to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). Lord Peake came and voted for Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) instead of Princess [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aemon)) or her son [Laenor Velaryon](wiki:Laenor_Velaryon), as he did not believe woman should rule over men.(wiki:Lord_Peake_(father_of_Unwin).cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
