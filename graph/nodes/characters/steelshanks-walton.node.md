## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Walton](wiki:Category:Images_of_Walton)*

Walton is a tall and dour man(wiki:Steelshanks_Walton.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Steelshanks_Walton.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) known as Steelshanks for the steel greaves he wears over his long legs.(wiki:Steelshanks_Walton.cite_ref-Rasos3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Walton also armors himself in a mail shirt(wiki:Steelshanks_Walton.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) and wields a spear.(wiki:Steelshanks_Walton.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Steelshanks is loyal and brutal, but not cruel.(wiki:Steelshanks_Walton.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Walton is a part of Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton)'s force that [captures Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal). He assists with the executions of servants who aided [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Steelshanks_Walton.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

### A Storm of Swords

After [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) escapes from Harrenhal, the girl thinks Roose will send Walton or [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) to pursue her.(wiki:Steelshanks_Walton.cite_ref-Rasos3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Walton commands Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s escort of two hundred men from Harrenhal to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Steelshanks_Walton.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Jaime persuades him with the promise of gold to turn back to Harrenhal so Jaime can save [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) from [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat). After Jaime jumps into the castle's bear pit to save Brienne from a bear, Steelshanks' crossbowmen slay the bear to protect Jaime's life.(wiki:Steelshanks_Walton.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

Upon their arrival at King's Landing, Walton and his men are rewarded the gold they were promised. Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) finds quarters for the Bolton men.(wiki:Steelshanks_Walton.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

After Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) slays Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) in [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat), Walton takes possession of a girl claimed to be Arya. Walton's party departs the capital to bring the girl to Lord Roose, as the girl is to marry [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) and cement [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton)'s claim to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Steelshanks_Walton.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Walton commands Roose's guards while the new [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North) stays in [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton).(wiki:Steelshanks_Walton.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Walton travels with Lord Bolton to Winterfell for the wedding of Ramsay to "Arya" (actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)).(wiki:Steelshanks_Walton.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_walton.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) After several men-at-arms have been found dead, Walton escorts [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) to a meeting with Roose, Lady [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin), [Roger Ryswell](wiki:Roger_Ryswell), and Ser [Aenys Frey](wiki:Aenys_Frey).(wiki:Steelshanks_Walton.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Steelshanks and his men break up the fight between [Manderlys](wiki:House_Manderly) and [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) when Ser [Hosteen Frey](wiki:Hosteen_Frey) attacks Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly).(wiki:Steelshanks_Walton.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
