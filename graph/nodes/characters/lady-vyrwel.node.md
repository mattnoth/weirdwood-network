## Origins

In [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), [Lord Vyrwel](wiki:Lord_Vyrwel) came with his lady wife to [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) to attend [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls) of [the daughter](wiki:Lady_Butterwell) of [Lord Frey](wiki:Lord_Frey) to Lord [Ambrose Butterwell](wiki:Ambrose_Butterwell). During the wedding feast, the Vyrwels were seated at the top of the hall in a place of high honor. Lady Vyrwel began weeping for an unknown reason during the celebration, though she recovered later and helped pull Ambrose from his chair when it came time for the [bedding](wiki:Bedding) custom.(wiki:Lady_Vyrwel.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
