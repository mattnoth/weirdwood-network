## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Shadd is one of the twenty guardsmen who accompany Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) south to meet with [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) and [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon). He complains about stiffness in his back, stirs oats into a kettle, and brews tea after finding nettles.(wiki:Shadd.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

Catelyn's party sets up camp outside [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), and Shadd slices carrots into a kettle.(wiki:Shadd.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) During the journey back to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) helps Shadd cook and clean game.(wiki:Shadd.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Catelyn orders [Hallis Mollen](wiki:Hallis_Mollen) and a small honor guard to escort the [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters) who are to bring the bones of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) back to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Shadd.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Unbeknownst to Catelyn, however, [ironmen](wiki:Ironmen) led by [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) have already moved to [take Moat Cailin](wiki:Fall_of_Moat_Cailin), and [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) is on his way to [take Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) as well.(wiki:Shadd.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Shadd.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
