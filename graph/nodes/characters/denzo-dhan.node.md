## Origins

Denzo is a seasoned warrior who does not come from [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). He is a veteran of hundred battles.(wiki:Denzo_D'han.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) He is a poet.(wiki:Denzo_D'han.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Denzo is part of the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor), and he is present when the [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince) commands several [Windblown](wiki:Windblown) who are originally from [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) to go over to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen).(wiki:Denzo_D'han.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

After Ser [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater) informs the Windblown that Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) wants to meet with the Tattered Prince, Denzo relays the message that the sellsword captain will meet them near the spice market of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Denzo_D'han.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Denzo is present alongside [Caggo](wiki:Caggo) and [Meris](wiki:Meris) when the Tattered Prince meets Quentyn, Gerris, and Ser [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood) at the [Purple Lotus](wiki:Purple_Lotus) to hear Quentyn's proposal. Denzo whistles when Quentyn asks for aid in stealing one of Daenerys's [dragons](wiki:Dragon).(wiki:Denzo_D'han.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
