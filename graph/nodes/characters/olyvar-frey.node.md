## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) takes Olyvar as a squire as part of the pact sealed between [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) and Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) at the [Twins](wiki:Twins) to join the [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) to the [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) cause in the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Olyvar_Frey.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Two years older than Robb, Olyvar is considered an excellent squire who proves his devotion to Robb and the Starks. He is part of Robb's [battle guard](wiki:Robb_Stark's_battle_guard) in the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Olyvar_Frey.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Clash of Kings

Olyvar is beside Robb when he presents his terms concerning peace with the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) to Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey).(wiki:Olyvar_Frey.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords

When the Freys abandon Robb's cause after his marriage to [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling), Olyvar wishes to stay with him at the [Crag](wiki:Crag). The other members of his family force him to return to the Twins, however.(wiki:Olyvar_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) [Rollam Westerling](wiki:Rollam_Westerling) becomes Robb's new squire.(wiki:Olyvar_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Catelyn expects Olyvar to attend his sister [Roslin](wiki:Roslin_Frey)'s marriage at the Twins to Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully).(wiki:Olyvar_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) Ser [Ryman Frey](wiki:Ryman_Frey) claims that Olyvar cannot attend because of duty, however, and Olyvar is not present for the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding). Catelyn remembers Olyvar as being "devoted to her son",(wiki:Olyvar_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) and it is possible that the other Freys kept him away due to his friendship with Robb.
