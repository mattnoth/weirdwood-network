## Appearances & Description

Dunstan is an old man, but not as old as [Erik Ironmaker](wiki:Erik_Ironmaker). Lord Drumm carries the [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) sword [Red Rain](wiki:Red_Rain),(wiki:Dunstan_Drumm.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and he is served by [Andrik the Unsmiling](wiki:Andrik_the_Unsmiling), who is possibly the fiercest warrior of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands).(wiki:Dunstan_Drumm.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), sends [Dagmer Cleftjaw](wiki:Dagmer) to [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk) to rouse [House Drumm](wiki:House_Drumm), as Balon plans to join the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Dunstan_Drumm.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Feast for Crows

After Balon falls to his death at [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), Lord Dunstan puts forth his name for consideration at the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot) at Old Wyk.(wiki:Dunstan_Drumm.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) His champions are his two sons, [Denys](wiki:Denys_Drumm) and [Donnel](wiki:Donnel_Drumm), as well as [Andrik the Unsmiling](wiki:Andrik_the_Unsmiling). Dunstan's speech about the history of House Drumm, from [Dale the Dread](wiki:Dale_Drumm) to [Gormond the Oldfather](wiki:Gormond_Drumm) bores the attendees, however. When his chests of gifts are opened, the ironborn sneer at the [bronze](wiki:Bronze) treasures. [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) is instead chosen [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North).(wiki:Dunstan_Drumm.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), Lord Captain [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) and [Nute the Barber](wiki:Nute_the_Barber) spot Dunstan talking with Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw) and Lord [Gorold Goodbrother](wiki:Gorold_Goodbrother) in the castle yard at [Lord Hewett's Town](wiki:Lord_Hewett's_Town). They discuss their fears that the gains from the battle are little compared to the enmity of the [Reach](wiki:Reach) they have earned.(wiki:Dunstan_Drumm.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Euron deprives Lord Drumm of the service of Andrik by granting [Southshield](wiki:Southshield) to the warrior.(wiki:Dunstan_Drumm.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
