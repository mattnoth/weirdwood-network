## Identity

Princess Nymeria of Ny Sar was a princess of the Rhoynar who led her people to Dorne after the Rhoyne was conquered by the Valyrian Freehold. She took Lord Mors Martell as her husband and established House Nymeros Martell's supremacy over Dorne, ruling for well over two decades as the Princess of Dorne (wiki:Nymeria).

## Origins

Ruler of Ny Sar, where she had a palace, at the time of the Rhoynish Wars (wiki:Nymeria).

## Appearances & Description

Said to be beautiful. Described by Maester Yandel as strong-willed and indomitable, with cunning, skill, and wisdom. Remembered as a "warrior queen" in songs, but she did not bear arms in battle — she commanded her armies from the battlefield (wiki:Nymeria).

## Narrative Arc

After the Rhoynish Wars, Nymeria led the famous Ten Thousand Ships carrying Rhoynar refugees across the narrow sea. After failed settlements at several locations, she brought her people to Dorne and married Lord Mors Martell of the Sandship. Together they conquered the rest of Dorne in Nymeria's War, defeating House Yronwood and five other Dornish kings. Mors was slain by Yorick V Yronwood in the Third Battle of the Boneway, but Nymeria forced Yorick to submit. She later took Lord Uller of Hellholt and then Ser Davos Dayne as husbands. She burned the Rhoynar ships to prevent her people from leaving. Through her rule, the Dornish adopted Rhoynish customs and laws, including equal primogeniture. She ruled for over twenty-seven years (wiki:Nymeria).
