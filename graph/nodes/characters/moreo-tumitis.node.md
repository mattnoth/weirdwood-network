## Origins

Moreo has sailed the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) for roughly thirty years, first as oarman, then as quartermaster, and now as captain of trading galleys. *[Storm Dancer](wiki:Storm_Dancer)* is his fourth ship and the fastest of them.(wiki:Moreo_Tumitis.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Moreo is at [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) with his ship when [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) and Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) arrive to take passage south to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). They book passage on *[Storm Dancer](wiki:Storm_Dancer)*, which is the swiftest available in the harbor.(wiki:Moreo_Tumitis.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

When the galley arrives at the capital, Moreo suggests the travelers stay at an [old inn on Eel Alley](wiki:Old_inn_on_Eel_Alley). Catelyn rewards each of his oarmen with a [silver stag](wiki:Currency). Moreo offers to hold onto his crew's silver until they return to [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh), but Catelyn insists on personally paying each oarman.(wiki:Moreo_Tumitis.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

The [City Watch of King's Landing](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing) arrive at the old inn with orders to escort Catelyn to Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep); Catelyn assumes that Moreo sold her whereabouts for a price. When Catelyn asks Petyr how he knew she was in the city, her childhood friend replies that Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) knows all.(wiki:Moreo_Tumitis.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

### A Dance with Dragons

*Storm Dancer* is in White Harbor when Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) arrives on *[Merry Midwife](wiki:Merry_Midwife)*.(wiki:Moreo_Tumitis.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
