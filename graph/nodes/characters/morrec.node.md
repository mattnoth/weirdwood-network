## Appearances & Description

Morrec is a better archer than a swordsman, but he is still not a soldier.(wiki:Morrec.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Morrec accompanies [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) north to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) and then to the [Wall](wiki:Wall), and he prepares a stew during the journey.(wiki:Morrec.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) At [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) he prepares the fire in Tyrion's chamber.(wiki:Morrec.cite_ref-Ragot21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) On the return journey south, Morrec and [Jyck](wiki:Jyck) are taken captive with Tyrion at the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn) and forced to ride into the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon), with [Mohor](wiki:Mohor) watching over them.(wiki:Morrec.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) After a second attack by the [Vale mountain clans](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans), Morrec dies from a fever caused by a festering wound.(wiki:Morrec.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
