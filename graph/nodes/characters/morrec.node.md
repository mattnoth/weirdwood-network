## Identity

Morrec is a servant of Tyrion Lannister who accompanies him on his travels (wiki:Morrec).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Lannister (track_b: Allegiance).

## Appearances & Description

Morrec is a better archer than a swordsman, but not a soldier — part groom, part cook, part body servant (wiki:Morrec.cite_ref-Ragot31).

## Narrative Arc

Morrec accompanies Tyrion north to Winterfell and then to the Wall, preparing stew during the journey and tending the fire in Tyrion's chamber at Castle Black (wiki:Morrec.cite_ref-Ragot13, wiki:Morrec.cite_ref-Ragot21). On the return south, Morrec and Jyck are taken captive with Tyrion at the crossroads inn and forced to ride into the Mountains of the Moon (wiki:Morrec.cite_ref-Ragot31). After a second attack by the Vale mountain clans, Morrec dies from a fever caused by a festering wound (wiki:Morrec.cite_ref-Ragot34).
