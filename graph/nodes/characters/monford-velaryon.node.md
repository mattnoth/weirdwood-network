## Appearances & Description

Monford is handsome with long, fair hair. He sometimes wears sea-green [silk](wiki:Silk) with a white [gold](wiki:Gold) seahorse brooch.(wiki:Monford_Velaryon.cite_ref-Racok0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Monford favors aggressive maneuvers in warfare.(wiki:Monford_Velaryon.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Monford_Velaryon.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The [lowborn](wiki:Lowborn) Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) believes that the [highborn](wiki:Highborn) Lord Velaryon would never confide in him.(wiki:Monford_Velaryon.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

As a vassal of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), Monford supports [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). However, he threatens to take his forces home if they do not strike soon.(wiki:Monford_Velaryon.cite_ref-Racok0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Lord Monford is present when [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) burns statues of [the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) taken from the [sept at Dragonstone](wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone); Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) notices that Monford watches Stannis rather than the conflagration.(wiki:Monford_Velaryon.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

Monford wants Stannis to sail from Dragonstone to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to challenge King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), but Stannis instead sails to [Shipbreaker Bay](wiki:Shipbreaker_Bay) against his brother, [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon). During the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Stannis thinks that Lord Velaryon wants to assault the castle instead of starving it.(wiki:Monford_Velaryon.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Lord Velaryon's *[Pride of Driftmark](wiki:Pride_of_Driftmark)* crashes between two Lannister river runners, but is later set ablaze with [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire).(wiki:Monford_Velaryon.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Aurane Waters](wiki:Aurane_Waters), Monford's half brother, kneels to Joffrey after the battle.(wiki:Monford_Velaryon.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords

It is reported by Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent) that Lord Monford burned with *Pride of Driftmark* on the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush).(wiki:Monford_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) He is succeeded by his young son, [Monterys Velaryon](wiki:Monterys_Velaryon).(wiki:Monford_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
