## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser Marwyn becomes the new [captain of the guards](wiki:House_Arryn_guards) at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) after the death of Ser [Vardis Egen](wiki:Vardis_Egen) in a [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat).(wiki:Marwyn_Belmore.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Feast for Crows

[Marillion](wiki:Marillion), a favorite singer of Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn), mocks Marwyn as "Ser Ding-Dong" during his tenure at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie). For that impertinence, Marwyn vows to have the singer's [tongue removed](wiki:Tongue_mutilation) but Lysa forbids it.(wiki:Marwyn_Belmore.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Upon the arrival of Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), Marwyn is removed from his position and he is replaced by one of Baelish's men, Ser [Lothor Brune](wiki:Lothor_Brune). Marwyn later returns to the Eyrie to hear Marillion confess to the murder of Lady Lysa.(wiki:Marwyn_Belmore.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Marwyn now serves as the captain of the guards at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) as part of Lord [Nestor Royce](wiki:Nestor_Royce)'s household.(wiki:Marwyn_Belmore.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
