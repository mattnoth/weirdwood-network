## Identity

Hagen's daughter is an unnamed ironborn woman who serves in the crew of Asha Greyjoy's longship *Black Wind* (wiki:Hagen's_daughter). She is the daughter of Hagen, one of Asha's supporters.

## Origins

Born between 282–283 AC (track_b: Born). Her father is Hagen, an ironborn warrior loyal to Asha Greyjoy.

## Allegiances

She is `SWORN_TO` House Greyjoy (track_b: Allegiance). She is `CULTURE_OF` the ironborn (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Hagen's daughter is described as beautiful, with long legs and a mane of thick red-brown hair. She fights bare-breasted, distracting opponents during the battle at Deepwood Motte (wiki:Hagen's_daughter).

## Narrative Arc

She serves aboard the *Black Wind* as one of four women in Asha's crew. During the taking of Deepwood Motte, she fights naked from the waist up to distract the northmen. When Stannis Baratheon's forces attack the ironborn camp at Deepwood Motte, she is captured along with Asha (wiki:Hagen's_daughter).
