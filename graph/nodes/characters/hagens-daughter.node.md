## Appearances & Description

She is a beautiful woman with long red hair. She can steer a ship as well as any man.(wiki:Hagen's_daughter.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Hagen's daughter, along with the rest of [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy)'s supporters, sails to [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) with Asha as she contemplates her next move. When talking with [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley), Asha tells Tris that Hagen's daughter looks at him, but he tells Asha that he does not want Hagen's daughter.(wiki:Hagen's_daughter.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Fleeing from Deepwood back to the ships in the [Bay of Ice](wiki:Bay_of_Ice), Asha and her men rest in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) and wait for sunrise. During the respite Hagen's daughter seizes Tristifer by the hand and attempts to draw him off into the trees for an amorous encounter. When Tris refuses her, she goes off with [Six-Toed Harl](wiki:Six-Toed_Harl) instead.(wiki:Hagen's_daughter.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

The [northmen](wiki:Northmen) soon catch up to the ironmen, however, and a bloody, confusing battle ensues. In the thick of the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte), Asha sees Hagen's daughter burst naked from beneath the trees with two northmen at her heels. Asha takes one of the men down with her axe, while the daughter slays the second man with the sword of the first.(wiki:Hagen's_daughter.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) Her fate after the battle is unknown. She was likely slain as [few ironborn](wiki:Ragged_nine) survived the battle.(wiki:Hagen's_daughter.cite_ref-3)
