## Origins

Casper was once a [knight](wiki:Knight) who served as a captain in the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons). After their abolition in the aftermath of the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising), Straw served as steward to the [High Septon](wiki:High_Lickspittle).(wiki:Casper_Straw.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

In [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), Ser [Orryn Baratheon](wiki:Orryn_Baratheon) and a dozen [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms) came to the [motherhouse](wiki:Motherhouse) attached to the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). On orders from his brother Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon), Orryn demanded that Princess [Rhaella Targaryen](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon)), a novice of the [Faith](wiki:Faith), be given into his custody. The suspicious [Septa](wiki:Septa) [Karolyn](wiki:Karolyn), under the guise of summoning the girl, instead sent for the High Septon. The [stormlanders](wiki:Stormlands) were soon opposed by the alert and wary Casper and thirty armed [septons](wiki:Septon), who falsely claimed that forty [Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) knights were on their way, upon which Orryn and his men surrender and were taken into custody.(wiki:Casper_Straw.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
