## Origins

During the reign of King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen), the [old High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Baelor_I)) died, and King Baelor informed the [Most Devout](wiki:Most_Devout) that [the Seven](wiki:The_Seven) had revealed the new High Septon's identity to him.
