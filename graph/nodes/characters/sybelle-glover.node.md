## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

When [Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover), [Master of Deepwood Motte](wiki:Master_of_Deepwood_Motte), and his brother [Robett](wiki:Robett_Glover) travel south with [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), they leave [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) in the hands of Robett's wife, Sybelle. However, it is [her steward](wiki:Glover_steward) who truly rules the castle. He goes in her place with some Glover men to the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast_(299)) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), as Sybelle does not want to subject her small children to the long journey nor leave them behind.(wiki:Sybelle_Glover.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Sybelle and her children are captured when [Deepwood is taken](wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte) by [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) led by [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy).(wiki:Sybelle_Glover.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Sybelle and her children, [Gawen](wiki:Gawen_Glover) and the infant [Erena](wiki:Erena_Glover), are kept captive at Deepwood Motte.(wiki:Sybelle_Glover.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Sybelle_Glover.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Asha brings Sybelle and her children to [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers) on [Harlaw](wiki:Harlaw). Along the way, Sybelle's milk dries up, and they stop at the [Stony Shore](wiki:Stony_Shore) to search for a [wet nurse](wiki:Wet_nurse) for Erena; instead they find a goat. Asha has Sybelle and her children quartered in one of the castle's towers instead of a dungeon.(wiki:Sybelle_Glover.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Asha returns to Deepwood with Sybelle after the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), but the children remain as [hostages](wiki:Hostage) at Ten Towers with the [Harlaws](wiki:House_Harlaw). Sybelle spends her time in Deepwood's [godswood](wiki:Godswood), praying for the safe return of her children and her husband, as Robett is still making his way north after his own captivity. Asha allows [Galbart Glover's maester](wiki:Galbart_Glover's_maester) to inform Sybelle about a letter sent from [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) by [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow).(wiki:Sybelle_Glover.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

When [mountain clansmen](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans) try to infiltrate Deepwood in advance of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s attack on the castle, Asha has [Lorren Longaxe](wiki:Lorren_Longaxe) and [Six-Toed Harl](wiki:Six-Toed_Harl) bring Lady Glover and the [maester](wiki:Maester) before her. Once Sybelle arrives from the godswood on the arm of her [bedmaid](wiki:Bedmaid), she tells Asha that she had previously warned that [northmen](wiki:Northmen) would try to recover Deepwood. Lady Glover urges Asha to surrender to Stannis and be exchanged for Sybelle's children, but Asha decides instead to make for her [longships](wiki:Longship) on the [Bay of Ice](wiki:Bay_of_Ice). Asha is subsequently captured in the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Sybelle_Glover.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

King Stannis, courteous with Sybelle but uncomfortable in her presence,(wiki:Sybelle_Glover.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) restores Deepwood to the Glovers.(wiki:Sybelle_Glover.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Sybelle sends men sworn to Deepwood with Stannis for his [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell), including guides, trackers, and hunters.(wiki:Sybelle_Glover.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

Sybelle ransoms six [ironborn prisoners](wiki:Ragged_nine) to [Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris), who has them escort him through the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) to find Stannis.(wiki:Sybelle_Glover.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
