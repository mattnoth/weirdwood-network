## Appearances & Description

Megga is round and loud(wiki:Megga_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) with a boisterous laugh.(wiki:Megga_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) She is a [maiden](wiki:Maiden) close in age to [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), and has not [flowered](wiki:Flowering) yet.(wiki:Megga_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Megga and [Alla Tyrell](wiki:Alla_Tyrell) follow their cousin [Elinor](wiki:Elinor_Tyrell), who has already flowered.(wiki:Megga_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Megga does not have a beautiful singing voice. Dreaming of being kissed by a man, she sometimes plays a kissing game with Alla.(wiki:Megga_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Megga is one of Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell)'s [ladies-in-waiting](wiki:Handmaid). She is a constant companion of Margaery. She meets and befriends [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) when she is invited to dinner with the Tyrells.(wiki:Megga_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Megga attends Sansa's [marriage](wiki:Marriage) to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister). During [the wedding feast](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark), Megga and her cousins ignore the bride to show their dissatisfaction with the match, as the Tyrells had hoped for Sansa to marry [Willas Tyrell](wiki:Willas_Tyrell) instead. Megga dances with Prince [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Megga_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Megga attends [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Tommen_I_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) of Margaery and Tommen, where she dances with the new king and Ser [Tallad the Tall](wiki:Tallad_the_Tall). [Taena of Myr](wiki:Taena_of_Myr) tells Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) that her maid, [Senelle](wiki:Senelle), brings messages to Megga and her cousins.(wiki:Megga_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Margaery's cousins tease Ser [Osney Kettleblack](wiki:Osney_Kettleblack) about his nose.(wiki:Megga_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Ser [Osmund Kettleblack](wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack) tells Cersei that Margaery often sews with Megga.(wiki:Megga_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Taena reports to Cersei that Megga has a new suitor every fortnight, and that she kissed a potboy in the kitchen. There is talk of Megga marrying [Lady Bulwer's brother](wiki:Lady_Bulwer's_brother), though she would prefer Ser [Mark Mullendore](wiki:Mark_Mullendore).(wiki:Megga_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Mark accompanies Megga during one of Margaery's rides.(wiki:Megga_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

Megga sobs when Cersei tells Margaery that Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) has been wounded in the [siege of Dragonstone](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone).(wiki:Megga_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Due to Queen Cersei's plotting, Megga is arrested by the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) for trumped-up charges of fornication, along with Elinor and Margaery, and held captive in the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor). While being taken into custody, Megga sends a [septa](wiki:Septa) crashing into the candles and sets her robe afire. When examined by a septa, Megga's [maidenhead](wiki:Maiden) is found to be broken.(wiki:Megga_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Almost all of the men named as Margaery's lovers have denied the accusation or recanted, so she and her cousins, including Megga, are handed over by the [High Septon](wiki:High_Sparrow) to the custody of Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly). Randyll swears a holy oath to return them for their trial.(wiki:Megga_Tyrell.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
