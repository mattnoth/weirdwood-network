## Identity

Megga Tyrell is a member of a junior branch of House Tyrell, the granddaughter of Ser Quentin Tyrell, a cousin of Lord Mace Tyrell. She is the only daughter of Ser Olymer Tyrell and Lysa Meadows, with two older brothers, Raymund and Rickard (wiki:Megga_Tyrell).

## Appearances & Description

Megga is round and loud with a boisterous laugh. She is a maiden close in age to Sansa Stark and has not yet flowered. She does not have a beautiful singing voice and sometimes plays a kissing game with her cousin Alla (wiki:Megga_Tyrell).

## Narrative Arc

Megga is one of Queen Margaery's ladies-in-waiting, accompanying her to King's Landing. She befriends Sansa Stark at the Tyrells' dinner. At Sansa's wedding to Tyrion, Megga and her cousins ignore the bride to show displeasure with the match (wiki:Megga_Tyrell). In AFFC, she attends Margaery and Tommen's wedding. There is talk of Megga marrying Lady Bulwer's brother, though she prefers Ser Mark Mullendore. When the Faith arrests Margaery, Megga and her cousins are also confined (wiki:Megga_Tyrell). In ADWD, Megga is released to Lord Randyll Tarly's custody pending trial (wiki:Megga_Tyrell).
