## Origins

When King Humfrey attempted to suppress the worship of the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods), Lord Elston rose in rebellion along with Lord [Roderick Blackwood](wiki:Roderick_Blackwood). He did not survive to see the end of the rebellion and the extinction of [House Teague](wiki:House_Teague).(wiki:Elston_Tully.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
