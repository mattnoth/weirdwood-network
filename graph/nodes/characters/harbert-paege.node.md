## Origins

In [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), Harbert was present at the wedding of Lord [Ambrose Butterwell](wiki:Ambrose_Butterwell) to [the daughter](wiki:Lady_Butterwell) of [Lord Frey](wiki:Lord_Frey) at [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls). During the [the tourney](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls) held to celebrate the marriage, Harbert was defeated by Ser [Franklyn Frey](wiki:Franklyn_Frey) after five tilts.(wiki:Harbert_Paege.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
