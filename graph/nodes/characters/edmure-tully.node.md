## Identity

Ser Edmure Tully is a knight and the heir of House Tully of Riverrun, the only living son of Lord Hoster Tully and Lady Minisa Whent. He is the younger brother of Catelyn Stark and Lysa Arryn (wiki:Edmure_Tully). Edmure coined the nickname "Littlefinger" for Petyr Baelish because of House Baelish's holdings on the Fingers and Petyr's short stature (wiki:Edmure_Tully).

## Origins

Born 270–274 AC at Riverrun (track_b: Born). Son of Hoster Tully and Minisa Whent (track_b: Father, Mother). Third child and only surviving son; his mother died in childbirth with his younger brother, who also died (wiki:Edmure_Tully).

## Appearances & Description

Auburn hair and deep blue eyes, typical Tully coloring, with a fiery red beard and stocky build. A head taller than Catelyn but not as tall as Lord Tytos Blackwood (wiki:Edmure_Tully). Fond of smiling and pretty girls, soft-hearted and protective of his smallfolk, but sulky when angered (wiki:Edmure_Tully). Wears bright mail for battle with a silver trout cresting his greathelm (wiki:Edmure_Tully).

## Narrative Arc

When war breaks out, Edmure commands the defense of Riverrun, sending Lords Vance and Piper to hold the pass below the Golden Tooth (agot-catelyn-08). After Robb Stark lifts the siege, Edmure is named acting Lord of Riverrun. He fights the Battle of the Fords against Tywin Lannister's forces, turning them back — but this unwittingly prevents Robb's plan to trap Tywin in the west, allowing Tywin to march to King's Landing and win the Battle of the Blackwater (wiki:Edmure_Tully).

As penance for his mistake, Edmure agrees to marry Roslin Frey at the Twins (track_b: Spouse). The wedding is a trap — the Red Wedding — where Robb, Catelyn, and much of the northern army are murdered. Edmure is spared as a hostage and taken prisoner (wiki:Edmure_Tully). He is forced to command the garrison of Riverrun to yield to Jaime Lannister, ending the siege. He is then sent to Casterly Rock as a prisoner, with Roslin carrying his unborn child (track_b: Issue, wiki:Edmure_Tully).
