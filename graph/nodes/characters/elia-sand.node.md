## Origins

Elia was born in [285 AC](wiki:285_AC) or [286 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)(wiki:Elia_Sand.cite_ref-birth_3-1) the fifthborn daughter of Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell), and the first by his longtime [paramour](wiki:Paramour) [Ellaria Sand](wiki:Ellaria_Sand). Elia was most likely [named after](wiki:Named_after) her late aunt, [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell), whom Oberyn was close to.(wiki:Elia_Sand.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Elia and [Obella](wiki:Obella_Sand) worship their eldest sisters—[Obara](wiki:Obara_Sand), [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria_Sand), and [Tyene](wiki:Tyene_Sand)—and in turn are worshipped by their younger sisters, [Dorea](wiki:Dorea_Sand) and [Loreza](wiki:Loreza_Sand).(wiki:Elia_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Appearances & Description

Elia wears her hair in a black braid,(wiki:Elia_Sand.cite_ref-twowarianne1_2-2) and has her [father's](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) eyes.(wiki:Elia_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Elia has a wild and haughty personality. She is fond of [horses](wiki:Horse) and [jousting](wiki:Joust), and her weapon of choice is the [lance](wiki:Armament). Her cousin, Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell), thinks Elia's sobriquet of *Lady Lance* was given to her by her father. Elia rides a black filly.(wiki:Elia_Sand.cite_ref-twowarianne1_2-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

After the death in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) of Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell), Elia, her mother [Ellaria Sand](wiki:Ellaria_Sand), and six of her seven sisters ([Sarella Sand](wiki:Sarella_Sand) is outside of Dorne) are taken into custody by [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah). While [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), does not think Elia and her younger sisters will be a danger to his rule, he fears they might be used by others in plots.(wiki:Elia_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) mentions to Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) that she is not permitted to see her cousins by her father, Doran.(wiki:Elia_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Elia is sent with her mother and three younger sisters to the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens), where she and [Obella](wiki:Obella_Sand) become the terror of the pools.(wiki:Elia_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

When the [Mountain's skull](wiki:Mountain's_skull) is delivered to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear), [Obara](wiki:Obara_Sand), [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria_Sand), and [Tyene Sand](wiki:Tyene_Sand) continue to demand more vengeance against the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister). Ellaria worries how the cycle of violence would affect her daughters.(wiki:Elia_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

### The Winds of Winter

Now fourteen years old,(wiki:Elia_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Elia joins Arianne on her journey to meet with [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington). Lady Lance outraces Arianne and [Valena Toland](wiki:Valena_Toland) on horses to [Ghost Hill](wiki:Ghost_Hill), covering herself in chalk and dust. While traveling on *[Peregrine](wiki:Peregrine)*, Elia flirts with and challenges Ser [Joss Hood](wiki:Joss_Hood) and Ser [Garibald Shells](wiki:Garibald_Shells), so Arianne sends her below deck to end the conversation.(wiki:Elia_Sand.cite_ref-twowarianne1_2-4)
