## Identity

Princess Jaehaera Targaryen was the daughter of King Aegon II Targaryen and Queen Helaena Targaryen (track_b: Father, Mother). She briefly served as queen consort to Aegon III Targaryen (track_b: Spouse).

## Origins

Born in 123 AC (track_b: Born). Of Valyrian race and Crownlands culture (track_b: Race, Culture).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiances). `SWORN_TO` Greens (track_b: Allegiances).

## Narrative Arc

Jaehaera was a twin, born alongside her brother Jaehaerys. During the Dance of the Dragons, the Greens used the children as bargaining pieces. After the war's end, Jaehaera was married to Aegon III as part of the peace settlement between the Greens and Blacks. She died in 133 AC in King's Landing under mysterious circumstances — she fell from a window of the Red Keep, possibly thrown (track_b: Died).
