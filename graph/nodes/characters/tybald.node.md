## Appearances & Description

Tybald is a red-haired and round-shouldered man with close-set eyes. He wears a heavy [bearskin](wiki:Bear) pelt over his grey robes.(wiki:Tybald.cite_ref-TWOW_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter

[Arnolf Karstark](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark) brings Tybald with him when he joins [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s host, to tend to the ravens and the wounded. Upon learning of [House Karstark](wiki:House_Karstark)'s planned treachery, Stannis has Tybald brought before him in the [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village), knowing that Tybald is in fact the maester of the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort), not [Karhold](wiki:Karhold). Aware that Stannis knows of his allegiance to [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton), Tybald is so fearful he evacuates his bladder in Stannis's presence and confesses that Arnolf had him send a map of the current location of Stannis's army to Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). Stannis orders Tybald imprisoned until he decides what to do with him.(wiki:Tybald.cite_ref-TWOW_1-2)
