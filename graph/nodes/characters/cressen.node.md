## Identity

Maester Cressen was a maester of the Citadel in service to Stannis Baratheon at Dragonstone. He is the POV character in the prologue of A Clash of Kings (wiki:Cressen).

## Origins

## Appearances & Description

Cressen is nearly eighty years old. He is frail with a bad hip, causing trouble walking and climbing stairs. He has wrinkly, thin skin and trembling hands (wiki:Cressen).

## Narrative Arc

Cressen served as maester of Storm's End. After the wreck of the Windproud in 278 AC that killed Lord Steffon and Lady Cassana Baratheon, Cressen helped identify the deceased and raised the Baratheon children. His favorite was Stannis, whom he regarded as the son he never had, because Stannis lacked the charm of his brothers. During Robert's Rebellion, Cressen was at Storm's End during the siege; he convinced Stannis to imprison rather than catapult would-be deserters, reasoning they might serve as "good meat" if starvation worsened. When Stannis was named Lord of Dragonstone, Cressen followed him. He saved Shireen Baratheon from greyscale around 289–290 AC, though the disease left her disfigured. In 297 AC Cressen broke his hip and could no longer climb to the rookery. The Citadel sent Maester Pylos as his replacement. Cressen died in 299 AC at Dragonstone, aged 79 (wiki:Cressen).
