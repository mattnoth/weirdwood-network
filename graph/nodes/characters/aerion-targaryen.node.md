## Identity

Prince Aerion Targaryen, known as Aerion the Monstrous or Aerion Brightflame, was the second son of King Maekar I Targaryen and Dyanna Dayne (wiki:Aerion_Targaryen).

## Origins

## Narrative Arc

Aerion was a cruel and vicious prince. At the Ashford Tourney, he attacked a puppet show and brutalized a puppeteer, leading to a Trial of Seven in which Dunk (later Ser Duncan the Tall) championed the puppeteer's cause. After the trial, Aerion was exiled to Lys and later served with the Second Sons mercenary company in Essos. He married Princess Daenora Targaryen and had a son, Maegor. Aerion died in 232 AC after drinking wildfire, believing it would transform him into a dragon (wiki:Aerion_Targaryen).
