## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

The [wandering crow](wiki:Wandering_crow) [Conwy](wiki:Conwy) arrives at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) with new recruits for the Night's Watch, including "a raggy man with a clubfoot".(wiki:Hop-Robin.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) This recruit may be Hop-Robin.

### A Storm of Swords

Hop-Robin is trained by [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) due to the lack of a capable replacement for Ser [Endrew Tarth](wiki:Endrew_Tarth) as [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms) at Castle Black.(wiki:Hop-Robin.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Dance with Dragons
