## Origins

Jaremy remained loyal to [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), and fought on the battlements during the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing). Afterwards, he was given the choice by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) between the [Wall](wiki:Wall) or execution. Both Jaremy and Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne) joined the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Jaremy_Rykker.cite_ref-Ragot21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Appearances & Description

According to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), Jaremy has a noble face.(wiki:Jaremy_Rykker.cite_ref-Ragot21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) He is not known for his patience,(wiki:Jaremy_Rykker.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and has a sardonic smile.(wiki:Jaremy_Rykker.cite_ref-Ragot21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Jaremy wears gleaming black mail and and an embossed breastplate. His heavy cloak, richly trimmed with [sable](wiki:Sable), is clasped in [silver](wiki:Silver) with the crossed hammers of [House Rykker](wiki:House_Rykker).(wiki:Jaremy_Rykker.cite_ref-Racok13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Jaremy is present when [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) has dinner at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) with [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), the [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), and other senior officers.(wiki:Jaremy_Rykker.cite_ref-Ragot21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

After [First Ranger](wiki:First_Ranger) [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark) goes missing [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall), Jaremy leads two sweeps in search of him, while [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand) leads another search party going north from the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower). Nothing is found except a few blazes in trees that Benjen used to mark his way. The marks stop in the stony highlands to the northwest, and all trace of Benjen vanishes.(wiki:Jaremy_Rykker.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Jaremy serves as First Ranger in the absence of Benjen. He is present during the swearing-in ceremony for the new members of the Night's Watch at the [sept](wiki:Sept) of Castle Black, and again when [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), and others are sworn in at the [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) grove beyond the Wall.(wiki:Jaremy_Rykker.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

After the latter ceremony, the bodies of two rangers are found by [Ghost](wiki:Ghost). Jaremy identifies them as [Othor](wiki:Othor) and [Jafer Flowers](wiki:Jafer_Flowers), who both belonged to Benjen's party. Lord Commander Mormont is displeased that Benjen has not been found and rangers have died close to the Wall, but Jaremy explains he lacks rangers and that the Lord Commander had ordered closer patrols. When they return to Castle Black, Jeor conveys an order to Jaremy, through Jon Snow, to put the dead men into a storeroom until Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) is ready to examine them.(wiki:Jaremy_Rykker.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

At Castle Back, Jafer Flowers rises as a [wight](wiki:Wight) and kills five people, including Jaremy. The knight manages to hack the wight's already loose head off with his sword, but is killed nonetheless when the headless wight pulls his dagger from its sheath and thrusts it into Jaremy's bowels.(wiki:Jaremy_Rykker.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Clash of Kings

[Thoren Smallwood](wiki:Thoren_Smallwood) states he is First Ranger with Benjen lost and Jaremy killed, a claim rejected by Lord Commander Mormont.(wiki:Jaremy_Rykker.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Thoren receives Jaremy's clothing and armor, right down to the [House Rykker](wiki:House_Rykker) emblem used as a clasp on Jaremy's cloak, as the Night's Watch does not waste things.(wiki:Jaremy_Rykker.cite_ref-Racok13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

The death of Jaremy by the hand of a [wight](wiki:Wight) is brought up by Lord Commander Mormont in his conversation with [Craster](wiki:Craster).(wiki:Jaremy_Rykker.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne) mentions in front of the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) that Jaremy has been killed by a wight, when he appeals to the Crown to provide more men for the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), causing people to snigger and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to mock Alliser.(wiki:Jaremy_Rykker.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

### A Storm of Swords

When [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) questions [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) at the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men), he asks him who had been in command there, suggesting, among others, that it might have been Jaremy. After some hesitation, Jon admits that the ranging was led by Lord Commander Mormont himself.(wiki:Jaremy_Rykker.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

As the [wildlings](wiki:Free_folk) are about to pass through the Wall, Lord Commander [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) has last doubts about his decision, thinking that a more experienced man should have dealt with [Tormund Giantsbane](wiki:Tormund_Giantsbane). In this context, he thinks of Jaremy, among others.(wiki:Jaremy_Rykker.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
