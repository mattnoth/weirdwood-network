## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Chezdhar zo Rhaezn, along with his brothers [Maezon zo Rhaezn](wiki:Maezon_zo_Rhaezn) and [Grazdhan zo Rhaezn](wiki:Grazdhan_zo_Rhaezn), are mocked by the sellsword companies as the **[Clanker Lords](wiki:Clanker_Lords)**, since they came up with the idea of chaining all the slave soldiers together so they cannot flee when facing the [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) (as their previous soldiers did). The soldiers march at a snail's pace, due to the weight of the chains.(wiki:Chezdhar_zo_Rhaezn.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
