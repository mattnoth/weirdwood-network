## Appearances & Description

Tytos is a thin and tall man, though not as tall as his sixteen-year-old son, [Hoster](wiki:Hoster_Blackwood), who is almost seven feet tall.(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He has a close-cropped salt-and-pepper beard, a hook nose,(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and long hair.(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister) considers Tytos to be an old man.(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Lord Blackwood appreciates honor and chivalry.(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Tytos has a feud with Lord [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken).(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Tytos owns bright yellow [armor](wiki:Armor) inlaid with [jet](wiki:Jet) in elaborate vine-and-leaf patterns.(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Another armor set he possesses is burnished scarlet, featuring in [silver](wiki:Silver) inlay the dead [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) tree of [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall) surrounded by [onyx](wiki:Onyx) [ravens](wiki:Raven) taking flight.(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) Tytos often wears a "magnificent" raven-feather cloak.(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord Tytos participates in the [battle under the walls of Riverrun](wiki:Battle_under_the_walls_of_Riverrun), and he leads the remnants of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) force back within the walls of [the castle](wiki:Riverrun) after Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) is defeated by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

With Lord Blackwood away, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) and his brother, Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), lead the capture of Tytos's castle, [Raventree Hall](wiki:Fall_of_Raventree_Hall).(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

During the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), Tytos leads a sortie from Riverrun that takes the middle [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) camp in the rear and rescues Edmure.(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

After the victory, Lord Blackwood, being a follower of the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods), joins [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) in praying in the [godswood of Riverrun](wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun). During Robb's war council in the [Great Hall of Riverrun](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Riverrun), Tytos is seated as far as possible from his rival, [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken), [Lord of Stone Hedge](wiki:Lord_of_Stone_Hedge). When Tytos suggests pressing against the Lannisters by joining with Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton)'s host and marching against [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Jonos argues they should instead support [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon). Tytos joins the other [river lords](wiki:River_lords) in declaring Robb to be [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

### A Clash of Kings

[Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) suggests that Robb send Tytos, Lord [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister), or Ser [Stevron Frey](wiki:Stevron_Frey) as an envoy to [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), but Robb insists on sending Balon's son, [Theon](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy).(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Given leave by Robb, Tytos departs Riverrun and leads the [liberation of Raventree Hall](wiki:Liberation_of_Raventree_Hall). The Blackwood lands have been left a desert, however.(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

When Tywin marches west from Harrenhal, Edmure gathers [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully)'s banners back to Riverrun. After returning from [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Catelyn spots the scarlet banner of House Blackwood above Tytos's tent. [Lucas Blackwood](wiki:Lucas_Blackwood), one of Catelyn's escorts, meets with his father in the camps. Both Tytos and Jonos Bracken support Edmure's plan to oppose Tywin at the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) with the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords).(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Tytos is present when Robb Stark, [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident), returns to Riverrun from his raiding in the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands).(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Lord Blackwood is among the seven nobles who launch the funeral boat of Edmure's father, [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), [Lord of Riverrun](wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun), into the [Tumblestone](wiki:Tumblestone).(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

Tytos's son Lucas is cut down by Ser [Hosteen Frey](wiki:Hosteen_Frey) in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), and King Robb is killed by Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton).(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Lord Bracken accepts a pardon from the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) after the massacre, but Lord Blackwood fights on for honor's sake.(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Feast for Crows

The [Brackens](wiki:House_Bracken) attack Blackwood lands in open war,(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) and Tytos becomes penned up within Raventree Hall.(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

After the [fall of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Raventree becomes the last Stark loyalist stronghold in the south. With Tytos refusing to surrender to Jonos, Lord [Karyl Vance](wiki:Karyl_Vance) implores Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), the [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard), to go to Raventree to end the siege, believing that Tytos would be willing to instead yield to Jaime.(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Robert Blackwood](wiki:Robert_Blackwood), Tytos's youngest son, dies of [illness](wiki:Illness) during the [siege of Raventree](wiki:Siege_of_Raventree).(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

Jaime arrives at [Blackwood Vale](wiki:Blackwood_Vale) to resolve the siege. Despite running low on supplies, Tytos refuses to surrender to his rival, Jonos Bracken, saying he will yield only to King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon). Jaime meets with Jonos, who hands Jaime a map with the lands he wants for subduing the Blackwoods, and recommends that Jaime take Tytos's only daughter, [Bethany](wiki:Bethany_Blackwood), as a [hostage](wiki:Hostage).(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6)

At Raventree, Jaime does not make Tytos kneel to him publicly in the yard, but tells him he can do so in his [solar](wiki:Solar) after they discuss terms. In Raventree's solar, Tytos surrenders to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), but denies knowing the whereabouts of Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully). Tytos agrees to give up some land to House Bracken, although less than Jonos desired, and to send some [gold](wiki:Gold) and [silver](wiki:Silver) to the Iron Throne. Tytos requests that Lucas's bones be returned so he can see him decently buried beneath Raventree's [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood), and Jaime agrees.(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7)

When Jaime states that he requires one of Tytos's children as a hostage, Lord Blackwood convinces him to take one of his sons, [Hoster](wiki:Hoster_Blackwood), rather than Bethany. Tytos recommends that Jaime get a hostage from Jonos as well, and alleges that Jonos's [natural](wiki:Bastard) son, [Harry Rivers](wiki:Harry_Rivers), was not actually fathered by Lord Bracken. Tytos politely asks Jaime to stay for dinner, but Jaime refuses, knowing Raventree is starving. Before Jaime leaves with Hoster, he reminds Tytos that if he learns the Blackwoods are protecting or aiding any [outlaws](wiki:Outlaw) or rebels against the Iron Throne, he will not hesitate to execute Tytos's son.(wiki:Tytos_Blackwood.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8)
