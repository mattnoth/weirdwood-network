## Appearances & Description

Malazza is short, with a thick waist and heavy bosom. She wears a sculpted black steel breastplate inlaid with gold, showing a harpy rising with chains dangling from her claws.(wiki:Malazza.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

While part of the [Yunkish](wiki:Yunkish) army, Malazza rides a white [horse](wiki:Horse) with a red mane, and commands a hundred young, strapping [slave](wiki:Slave) soldiers, bred and trained by herself. All of them are lean, young, rippling with muscle, and naked but for breechclouts, yellow cloaks, and long [bronze](wiki:Bronze) shields with erotic inlays.(wiki:Malazza.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

The [Windblown](wiki:Windblown) [sellswords](wiki:Sellsword) think that the Girl General fancies herself Yunkai's own [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen). They mock her when drunk.(wiki:Malazza.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Malazza is present at a [slave](wiki:Slave) auction held outside [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) spots her in the crowd; a pair of slave soldiers have lifted her to shoulder height on a shield. She bids twenty-five hundred on Tyrion and [Penny](wiki:Penny), but gives up on the purchase when [Brown Ben Plumm](wiki:Brown_Ben_Plumm) bids three thousand on them.(wiki:Malazza.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### The Winds of Winter

Malazza sends for [Brown Ben Plumm](wiki:Brown_Ben_Plumm) just as the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen) is commencing. She commands the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons) to defend the [Wicked Sister](wiki:Wicked_Sister), as she figures that Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) means to bring down all the trebuchets. However, Malazza's turn to be the [Yunkish](wiki:Yunkish) supreme commander ended at dawn. During Ben's absence, a Yunkish officer gives [Inkpots](wiki:Inkpots) the order from [Gorzhak zo Eraz](wiki:Gorzhak_zo_Eraz) to bring the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons) to the shore of the bay. Inkpots refuses the order as Brown Ben is absent.(wiki:Malazza.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btyrion_ii_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctyrion_ii.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

When Ben returns, he and his captains immediately have a meeting. Ben gives them the Girl General's order to defend the Wicked Sister, which does not go down well. Inkpots is baffled as to why the Girl General is still giving orders, as dawn has come and gone and she is behaving as if she were still the supreme commander. Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) reasons that the Girl General is still giving commands because she knows [Pudding Face](wiki:Pudding_Face) is about to assume command. Before the sellswords can decide what to do about Malazza's order, another Yunkish rider arrives with new orders from yet another supreme commander, [Morghar zo Zherzyn](wiki:Morghar_zo_Zherzyn).(wiki:Malazza.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btyrion_ii_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctyrion_ii.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
