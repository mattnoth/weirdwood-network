## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne) tells [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) that he once served his cousin, Ser [Cedric Payne](wiki:Cedric_Payne). When Cedric died during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Podrick attached himself to Lorimer, who was under the command of Lord [Leo Lefford](wiki:Leo_Lefford) and tasked to protect the baggage train. Lorimer was found to have stolen a ham and was hanged as a thief. Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) then sent Podrick, whose family name saved him from sharing Lorimer's fate, to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to be his squire.(wiki:Lorimer_the_Belly.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
