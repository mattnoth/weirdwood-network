## Origins

### Early life

Prince Viserys was born in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in [276 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) to King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) and his sister-wife Queen [Rhaella](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen), their first child to survive in the seventeen years since their son [Rhaegar](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen)'s birth, after multiple stillbirths, miscarriages, and infant deaths. The celebrated Viserys was small but robust and beautiful.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

Aerys's paranoia made him fear for the infant's life beyond reason, however. The [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) watched him day and night, and Rhaella was forbidden to be alone with her son. Aerys insisted that his food taster drink from the breasts of the prince's [wet nurse](wiki:Wet_nurse), to make sure she had not smeared [poison](wiki:Poison) on her nipples. The king burned gifts sent by Westerosi [lords](wiki:Lord), thinking they might be [cursed](wiki:Magic) or. Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) hosted a [tournament in honor of Viserys's birth](wiki:Tournament_in_honor_of_Viserys's_birth) in [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport), but only Aerys and Rhaegar attended from the royal family. When Rhaegar married [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell) in the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) in [280 AC](wiki:280_AC), Aerys refused to allow Viserys to attend. In the years following the wedding, rumors spread that Aerys intended to disinherit his estranged heir in favor of Viserys, even by the king's own men.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3)

### Robert's Rebellion

Viserys was only a young boy at the time of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), and Queen [Rhaella](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen) sheltered him from King [Aerys's](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) madness as much as she could.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) When his brother [Rhaegar](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) was killed at the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident), Viserys was named his father's heir, passing over Rhaegar's infant son [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)). Viserys was sent with the pregnant Rhaella to the fortified island of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)).(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_end.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) After news of Aerys's death and the deaths of Rhaegar's children in the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing) reached Dragonstone, Viserys was declared king.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rawoiafrhaella_targaryen.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

In [284 AC](wiki:284_AC), nine months after they fled King's Landing, Rhaella died while giving birth to Viserys's younger sister, [Daenerys](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), during a storm which destroyed most of the [Targaryen fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet), which had been anchored at Dragonstone.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-11) [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) was about to set sail with his new fleet for the [assault on Dragonstone](wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone), and the garrison of Dragonstone was prepared to turn Viserys and his sister over to him, but Ser [Willem Darry](wiki:Willem_Darry) and four loyal men smuggled the children to the [Braavosian coast](wiki:Braavosian_coast).(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-12)(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Racok0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

### Exile

Viserys and [Daenerys](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) lived under Ser [Willem Darry](wiki:Willem_Darry)'s protection in a [house with a red door](wiki:House_with_a_red_door) in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-13) While they lived in Braavos, Willem signed a [secret marriage pact](wiki:Secret_marriage_pact) with Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), with the [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos) as a witness, agreeing that Viserys would take Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell), daughter to [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), the [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), as his bride in exchange for Dorne supporting his claim for the throne. However, Viserys was never made aware of this pact,(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1) as he was an immature child when it was decided, and he was not ready for the information.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-31) Willem fell ill five years after fleeing from Dragonstone, however, and eventually died.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-14)(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rawoiafdaenerys_targaryen.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)

After Willem's death, the servants stole what little money the children had, leaving Viserys and his sister impoverished. They were put out of the house in Braavos soon after.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-15) Viserys and Daenerys began to wander the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) as guests of powerful citizens. From Braavos, they went to [Myr](wiki:Myr), next to [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh), on to [Qohor](wiki:Qohor), [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), and [Lys](wiki:Lys), never staying long at one location. Viserys was convinced they were being followed by the hired knives of the [Usurper](wiki:Usurper), King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). While originally they were welcomed into the homes of the powerful citizens of the Free Cities, their welcome eventually faded, and they were forced to sell off their last possessions to survive.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-16) Selling Rhaella's crown took the last joy had from Viserys, leaving only rage.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1) Over time, men started calling him the Beggar King.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-17)(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) At some point Viserys feasted the captains of the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company), in hopes they might take up his cause and restore him to the throne of Westeros. They ate his food and heard his pleas, but only laughed at him.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)

While his relationship with his sister was initially warm, Viserys grew to resent Daenerys for the death of their mother during her birth, and began treating her abusively, both with cruel words and with physical assaults. He would frequently warn her not to "wake the [dragon](wiki:Dragon)" and incite his anger.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-18) Viserys taught Daenerys all he knew of Westeros history and the history of House Targaryen, teaching her to take pride in her family heritage,(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-19)(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-5) though sometimes abusively.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-3) Daenerys always expected she would marry Viserys when she came of age, as Viserys had often told her the line must be kept pure and that Targaryens did not mingle their blood with that of lesser men.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-20)

Viserys and Daenerys have been guests for half a year at [the manse](wiki:Illyrio's_manse) of [Magister](wiki:Magister) [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) in [Pentos](wiki:Pentos).(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-21) One servant, a [young girl](wiki:Girl_bought_to_please_the_king) trained in [Lys](wiki:Lys), is purchased by Illyrio to please the would-be king and keep him from claiming Daenerys' [maidenhead](wiki:Maidenhead).(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)

## Appearances & Description

Viserys possesses the classical Valyrian features: he has pale skin, silver-blond hair, and pale [lilac eyes](wiki:Purple_eyes).(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) He had been a small, robust, and healthy child,(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) but in his early twenties, after more than a decade of living in exile, Viserys has become a gaunt young man with hard lines in his face and a feverish look in his eyes.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) He prefers to dress in silks and wool,(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1)(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) keeps a sachet in the sleeve of his tunic to sniff,(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) and wears a borrowed sword to look more "kingly".(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3) He was not as tall as his older brother [Rhaegar](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) had been.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

In [Pentos](wiki:Pentos), Magister [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) takes an interest in returning Viserys to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) in return for rewards upon his ascension. Illyrio and Viserys arrange a marriage between [Daenerys](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) and Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), a powerful [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) horselord who promises Viserys an army which he can use to conquer the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms). Viserys believes he could conquer Westeros with only ten thousand Dothraki screamers, and that among the houses that would rise for him upon his landing would be the [Tyrells](wiki:House_Tyrell), [Redwynes](wiki:House_Redwyne), [Darrys](wiki:House_Darry), [Greyjoys](wiki:House_Greyjoy), and [Martells](wiki:House_Martell), and that the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) cry out for the return of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen). Viserys says when he takes Westeros, he will kill the [Usurper](wiki:Usurper), King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), and also the [Kingslayer](wiki:Kingslayer), Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-22)

Viserys, while willing to wed Daenerys to Drogo, is resentful of having to give her up, and the night before the wedding tries to enter her room and claim her [maidenhead](wiki:Maidenhead). Illyrio, however, has the foresight to post guards on her room and ensure the impetuous Viserys does not undo "years of planning".(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-2) At the wedding feast, Viserys meets the exile knight Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont),(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-23) who swears himself to his service. Viserys gifts his sister with three handmaids, [Irri](wiki:Irri), [Jhiqui](wiki:Jhiqui), and [Doreah](wiki:Doreah). Viserys is angered at having to sit in a place below Daenerys and at being offered the food that only the *khal* and his bride have already refused. Before Daenerys and Drogo leave to consummate the marriage, Viserys warns his sister that she must please Drogo, or else she "will see the dragon wake as it has never woken before."(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)

After the wedding, Khal Drogo's *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* leaves for [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak) to present the new *[khaleesi](wiki:Khaleesi)* Daenerys to the *[dosh khaleen](wiki:Dosh_khaleen)*. Viserys insists on traveling along to ensure the *khal* honors the agreement of support. Viserys believes that Drogo is delaying fulfillment of his promise, and grows increasingly insistent on making a start to the invasion. He believes he sold Daenerys to Drogo, and that the *khal* has not paid him for her, although Illyrio and Jorah try to explain that the Dothraki do not buy or sell, but rather receive gifts and give gifts back eventually in return.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-2)

Viserys tries to browbeat Daenerys into persuading Drogo, but with her new position of authority as *khaleesi* she begins standing up for herself. When Daenerys wishes to experience the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea) in solitude, she requests that Jorah tell Viserys and the rest of her *[khas](wiki:Khas)* to stay behind. Viserys becomes infuriated at Daenerys giving him a command, and verbally and physically assaults her. She defends herself, and one of the young warriors of her *[khas](wiki:Khas)*, [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) defends his *khaleesi*, snapping a whip around Viserys's neck. Daenerys commands that he not be harmed, only that his horse be taken away, forcing Viserys to walk. Viserys demands that Jorah hurt Daenerys and kill the Dothraki for this insult, but the knight ignores Viserys and enforces Daenerys's orders. Jorah regrets swearing himself to Viserys's service, and intimates to Daenerys that Viserys would be a terrible king. Daenerys realizes that Viserys will never win the Iron Throne and take her back to Westeros, as he is not respected and could not lead an army even if Khal Drogo gives him one.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-3)

When Viserys walks back to the *khalasar*, he is greatly shamed in the eyes of the Dothraki, as they feel a man who does not ride is no man at all, and this earns him the mocking nickname *Khal Rhae Mhar*, the Sorefoot King. Khal Drogo later offers him a place in a [cart](wiki:Cart) and Viserys accepts, in his ignorance believing it the *khal's* way of apologizing to him for the wrong Daenerys did to him. Unknown to Viserys, this is a greater insult than walking on foot — as carts are for cripples, [eunuchs](wiki:Eunuch), the very old or very young — which earns Viserys the new nickname *Khal Rhaggat*, the Cart King.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-6)

At Vaes Dothrak, Daenerys wishes to reconcile with Viserys, and prepares gifts for him, including new Dothraki-style clothing "fit for a *khal*" to replace his filthy garments. However, when Daenerys sends Doreah to request that Viserys join her at dinner, he takes offense that she would send a whore to give him commands and strikes the woman, before angrily approaching Daenerys. He mocks the clothes and the idea that he should dress like a "savage", and begins to assault Daenerys until she defends herself, hitting him with the belt of bronze medallions she had hoped to give him. Viserys storms off angrily, saying she will rue this day when he regains his kingdom.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-7)

The day of the ritual ceremony for Daenerys's unborn child, [Rhaego](wiki:Rhaego), Jorah stops Viserys from stealing Daenerys's [dragon eggs](wiki:Dragon_egg), intending to sell to the traders of Vaes Dothrak for gold to buy himself a [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) army. The feasting Dothraki are then greatly offended by a drunken Viserys wielding a sword in Drogo's hall, as blades are banned in the sacred city of Vaes Dothrak and it is forbidden to shed a free man's blood there. Khal Drogo does not let Viserys sit at the high bench but rather tells him to sit with the cripples and boys, and all the Dothraki laugh and mock Viserys. Viserys draws his sword, angering the Dothraki, and threatens Daenerys's unborn child while again insisting that Drogo give him his crown. In response, Drogo has Viserys seized by his [bloodriders](wiki:Bloodrider), melts down his belt of golden medallions, and upends the pot over Viserys's head, fatally but bloodlessly "crowning" him with molten gold.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-6)

After Daenerys is nearly assassinated in the Vaes Dothrak marketplace, she learns that King Robert has offered lands and [lordships](wiki:Lord) for her death or Viserys's;(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0) she half-hysterically sobs and tells Jorah that the Usurper owes Drogo a lordship.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Word of Viserys's death reaches Westeros not long after King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon)'s execution of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark).(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

In the [red waste](wiki:Red_waste), Daenerys remembers that Viserys told her when they were children that only [dragons](wiki:Dragon) and men eat cooked meat, which enables her to finally find a way to feed her newborn [dragons](wiki:Dragon). Daenerys names her white dragon [Viserion](wiki:Viserion) in his memory.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1)

While wondering how she can succeed in taking back her father's throne, Daenerys recalls that Viserys had believed that the realm would rise for its rightful king. Although she considers the notion to have been a foolish one,(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1) there appears to have been some truth to his beliefs.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-39)

In [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), after being refused help by the [Pureborn](wiki:Pureborn), Daenerys remembers how Viserys was forced to survive in order to take care of them, and the negative effect it had on him, and that she has now found herself in the same position.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-2)

### A Storm of Swords

While traveling to [Astapor](wiki:Astapor) by ship, Daenerys recalls a young Viserys telling her tales of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) and of how much better their lives would be once he claimed his crown.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-4)

In [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) tells Daenerys that Viserys was [his father](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen)'s son, in ways [Rhaegar](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) never was.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-1)

### A Feast for Crows

At [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear), [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) learns from her father, [Doran](wiki:Doran_Martell), of a [secret marriage pact](wiki:Secret_marriage_pact) between Houses Targaryen and [Martell](wiki:House_Martell). While her younger brother [Quentyn](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) was to rule [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), Arianne was intended to marry the exiled Viserys. Doran, the [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), had suggested older consorts for her to marry, knowing she would reject them. Doran planned to send Arianne to [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) to serve as the [Archon](wiki:Archon)'s cupbearer and meet Viserys in secret, but Doran's wife, [Mellario](wiki:Mellario), had been so upset by Quentyn's fostering at [Yronwood](wiki:Yronwood) that Doran could not bear to take another of her children from her, and Arianne was never told of her betrothal to Viserys. With Viserys now dead, Doran intends for Arianne to rule Dorne and sends Quentyn to court Daenerys in Meereen.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

In Meereen, Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) tells Daenerys that after he was dismissed from the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) by [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), he realised that he had to redeem himself by finding the true king, meaning Viserys, and serve him loyally with all the strength that still remained to him.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-1) Daenerys remembers that Viserys had told her stories of the [tourneys](wiki:Tourney) he had witnessed in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0)

Ser [Tristan Rivers](wiki:Tristan_Rivers) reveals that one of [Magister](wiki:Magister) [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis)'s plans was for Viserys to join the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) with fifty thousand [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) screamers at his back, but then the Beggar King died, putting an end to that plan.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Upon [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell)'s arrival at Daenerys's court in Meereen, he informs her of the [secret marriage pact](wiki:Secret_marriage_pact) concluded by Ser [Willem Darry](wiki:Willem_Darry) and [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) with the [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos) as witness, while Viserys and Daenerys were living in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos). It promised that if Viserys wed [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) he would receive [Dorne](wiki:Dorne)'s aid in placing him upon the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). Daenerys realizes that if her brother had known that he had a Dornish princess waiting for him, he would have crossed to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) as soon as he was old enough to wed. Quentyn agrees that this would have brought [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s wrath down on Dorne and that Doran was content to wait until Viserys found an army. Quentyn had been sent to Meereen by his father to fulfill the pact; instead of Viserys marrying Arianne, Quentyn would marry Daenerys and give her the support of Dorne.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-3) Barristan tells Quentyn that the pact, written by two dead men and referring to a dead man, cannot be enforced for him and Daenerys.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0)

Near "[Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(hill))" in the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea) after fleeing [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit) atop [Drogon](wiki:Drogon),(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0) Daenerys dreams of Viserys. Her burnt and gilded brother accuses her of not having mourned him. The siblings argue about Daenerys marriage to [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), the delay in Viserys receiving a Dothraki *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)*, and Daenerys receiving [dragon eggs](wiki:Dragon_egg) from Illyrio. Viserys's jaw falls away as he laughs in the dream.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0)

### The Winds of Winter

[Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) tells Ser [Justin Massey](wiki:Justin_Massey) that he will not leave [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) to raise an army, as exiles rarely return, using the beggar king Viserys as an example.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btheon_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctheon_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

While sailing the [Sea of Dorne](wiki:Sea_of_Dorne) to [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath), Arianne discusses Viserys and his death with Ser [Daemon Sand](wiki:Daemon_Sand).(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Barianne_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Carianne_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0) Near [Griffin's Roost](wiki:Griffin's_Roost), Arianne encounters [Lysono Maar](wiki:Lysono_Maar) of the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company). She says the [Lyseni](wiki:Lys)'s appearance (long silver-gold hair and pale [lilac eyes](wiki:Purple_eyes)) reminds her of a Targaryen, which he finds flattering. She is somewhat unnerved by Lysono, however, and thinks it is perhaps a good thing that Viserys is dead if that is what her betrothed looked like.(wiki:Viserys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Barianne_ii_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Carianne_ii.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0)
