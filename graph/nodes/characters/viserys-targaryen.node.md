## Identity

Prince Viserys Targaryen is the heir of his father King Aerys II Targaryen following the death of his older brother Prince Rhaegar during Robert's Rebellion. He styles himself King Viserys III. In the Free Cities he was mockingly dubbed the Beggar King (wiki:Viserys_Targaryen, cite: AGOT).

## Origins

Born in 276 AC in the Red Keep, King's Landing, to King Aerys II Targaryen and Queen Rhaella Targaryen — the first child to survive in seventeen years since Rhaegar's birth, after multiple stillbirths and miscarriages. Aerys's paranoia made him fear for the infant's life beyond reason (wiki:Viserys_Targaryen, cite: TWOIAF).

## Appearances & Description

Viserys possesses classical Valyrian features: pale skin, silver-blond hair, and pale lilac eyes. As a child he was small but robust and beautiful. After a decade of exile he became a gaunt young man with hard lines in his face and a feverish look in his eyes. He was cruel, weak, frightened, impatient, vain, greedy, and violent — abusive to his sister Daenerys, whom he blamed for their mother's death. According to Barristan Selmy, Viserys already showed signs of madness as a young child (wiki:Viserys_Targaryen, cite: AGOT, ADWD).

## Narrative Arc

When Rhaegar was killed at the Trident, Viserys was named his father's heir and sent with the pregnant Rhaella to Dragonstone. After Aerys's death, Viserys was declared king. In 284 AC Rhaella died giving birth to Daenerys during a storm that destroyed the Targaryen fleet. Ser Willem Darry smuggled the children to Braavos, where they lived in a house with a red door. A secret marriage pact was signed between Willem Darry and Prince Oberyn Martell, promising Viserys would wed Princess Arianne Martell in exchange for Dornish support — though Viserys was never told (wiki:Viserys_Targaryen, cite: AGOT, AFFC).

After Darry's death, the servants stole their money and the children were put out. They wandered the Free Cities, selling off possessions to survive. Selling Rhaella's crown took the last joy from Viserys, leaving only rage. At some point he feasted the captains of the Golden Company, who laughed at his pleas. Viserys grew to resent Daenerys and treated her abusively (wiki:Viserys_Targaryen, cite: AGOT).

In Pentos, Magister Illyrio Mopatis arranged Daenerys's marriage to Khal Drogo in exchange for a Dothraki army. Viserys traveled with the khalasar but grew increasingly frustrated and erratic. He struck Daenerys, drew a sword in Vaes Dothrak (a sacred prohibition), and demanded his crown from Drogo. Khal Drogo killed Viserys by pouring molten gold over his head — the "golden crown" Viserys had demanded. Daenerys watched and noted that a true dragon would not have been killed by fire (wiki:Viserys_Targaryen, cite: AGOT).
