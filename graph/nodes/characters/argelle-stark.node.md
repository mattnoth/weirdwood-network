## Identity

Argelle Stark was a noblewoman of House Stark during the latter half of the second century after Aegon's Conquest. She was the younger daughter of Edric Stark and Serena Stark (wiki:Argelle_Stark).

## Origins

Born between 155 and 210 AC. Died 166 AC. Daughter of Edric Stark and Serena Stark (track_b: Father, track_b: Mother).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiances). `SWORN_TO` House Cerwyn (track_b: Allegiances). `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Argelle married Lord Robard Cerwyn, the Lord of Cerwyn, and they had children together (wiki:Argelle_Stark).

## Notes

The character is named "Argelle Stark" in George R. R. Martin's notes, while she is misspelled as "Aregelle" in the family tree in The World of Ice & Fire (wiki:Argelle_Stark).
