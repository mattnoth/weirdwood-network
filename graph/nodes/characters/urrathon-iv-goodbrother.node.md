## Origins

When King [Urragon III Greyiron](wiki:Urragon_III_Greyiron) died, a [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot) was called by his family while one of the king's sons, [Torgon Greyiron](wiki:Torgon_Greyiron), was raiding the [Mander](wiki:Mander). The king's younger sons were hoping that one of them would be elected king, but the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) chose Urrathon Goodbrother of [Great Wyk](wiki:Great_Wyk) instead. Urrathon IV's first act was to have all of the late king's sons at the kingsmoot executed. Thus Urrathon became known as "Badbrother".(wiki:Urrathon_IV_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Urrathon ruled for two years and made many enemies due to his cruelty. Torgon, the only surviving son of the previous king, returned to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) and declared the kingsmoot decision unlawful as he had not been present to put forth his claim as was his right. The priests, [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk), and lords rallied to Torgon's banner and Urrathon was cast down, hacked to pieces by his own captains. Torgon the Latecomer succeeded Badbrother as king.(wiki:Urrathon_IV_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Urrathon_IV_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
