## Origins

### Dance of the Dragons

After the death of King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) in [129 AC](wiki:129_AC), Willis choose the side of King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) and the [greens](wiki:Greens) in the ensuing civil war known as the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Willis_Fell.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) In preparation for Aegon's coronation, Queen [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower) dispatched Ser Willis to inform her son Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) of the king's death and to bring him before the [small council](wiki:Small_council).(wiki:Willis_Fell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

When [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) [fell](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing) to the [blacks](wiki:Blacks), Willis and the royal family were noticeably absent from the city.(wiki:Willis_Fell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Lord [Larys Strong](wiki:Larys_Strong), the [master of whisperers](wiki:Master_of_whisperers), had smuggled King Aegon II, Princess [Jaehaera](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen), and Prince [Maelor Targaryen](wiki:Maelor_Targaryen) to safety through a passage within [Maegor's Holdfast](wiki:Maegor's_Holdfast) known only to himself. The party was accompanied by Ser Willis and Ser [Rickard Thorne](wiki:Rickard_Thorne) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) offered rewards for information about the vanished "false knights", and King Aegon's rival also sent [knights inquisitor](wiki:Knights_inquisitor) in search of Willis and Ser Rickard.(wiki:Willis_Fell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Willis_Fell.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Willis was commanded to see Princess Jaehaera safely to Lord [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon) at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), while Ser Rickard was sent to bring Prince Maelor to Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower). Neither man was informed of the other's destination, so neither could betray the other if captured.(wiki:Willis_Fell.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)(wiki:Willis_Fell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Willis succeeded in his mission and safely delivered the princess to Storm's End.(wiki:Willis_Fell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### Lord Commander

After the death of Aegon II, Lady [Elenda Baratheon](wiki:Elenda_Caron) charged Willis with escorting Princess Jaehaera, three of her daughters, and her father Lord [Royce Caron](wiki:Royce_Caron) to King's Landing for the coronation of [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen).(wiki:Willis_Fell.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Shortly after the king's wedding to Jaehaera in [131 AC](wiki:131_AC), Aegon III rose Willis to [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard), as he was the sole surviving knight of Viserys' Kingsguard. Ser [Marston Waters](wiki:Marston_Waters) was named Willis' second and, as Willis and Marston were former greens, the rest of the Kingsguard was filled with former blacks.(wiki:Willis_Fell.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

As the Lord Commander, Willis served on the small council during the king's [regency](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III). When deciding on the king's heir, Willis was in favor of naming Princess [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daemon)) for she had a [dragon](wiki:Dragon), while her sister [Baela](wiki:Baela_Targaryen) did not. Willis reaffirmed his opinion after Baela wed her cousin Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) without the council's opinion, disapproving of Alyn's illegitimate birth. The regents concurred and Rhaena was named Aegon's heir.(wiki:Willis_Fell.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

When the [Winter Fever](wiki:Winter_Fever) struck the capital in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Willis succumbed to the disease.(wiki:Willis_Fell.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) The king named Ser [Robin Massey](wiki:Robin_Massey) as his successor.(wiki:Willis_Fell.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
