## Origins

Maelys was a descendant of the male line of [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre).(wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) According to a semi-canon source, he supposedly devoured his twin in the womb (making him a [kinslayer](wiki:Kinslayer), albeit not by choice), which resulted in his grotesquely large upper body and vestigial second head.(wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rrpgguardiansorder.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Maelys grew up in exile in [Essos](wiki:Essos), and eventually became a kinslayer deliberately, when he fought his own cousin, [Daemon](wiki:Daemon_Blackfyre_(cousin_of_Maelys_I)), for command of the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company). Maelys killed Daemon's destrier with a single punch, and then twisted Daemon's head until he tore it from his shoulders. Following Daemon's death, Maelys assumed command of the Golden Company.(wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

In [258 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Maelys gathered with eight other [outlaws](wiki:Outlaw), exiles and [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) captains beneath the [Tree of Crowns](wiki:Tree_of_Crowns) in the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands) in [Essos](wiki:Essos). There, they formed an alliance called the [Band of Nine](wiki:Band_of_Nine), promising to aid each other in carving out a kingdom for each member. The Band of Nine pledged to help Maelys in conquering the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) While it was generally believed that the Band of Nine would founder in Essos,(wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)(wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) they managed to sack and take [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh), and seize the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones), which made them a real threat to Westeros. Maelys declared himself to be King Maelys I Blackfyre.(wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

In [260 AC](wiki:260_AC), King [Jaehaerys II Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_II_Targaryen) sent an army to the Stepstones to meet the Band of Nine in battle. The war, dubbed the [War of the Ninepenny Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Ninepenny_Kings), was fought entirely on the Stepstones. Maelys personally killed Lord [Ormund Baratheon](wiki:Ormund_Baratheon), Jaehaerys's [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), early in the war. Towards the war's end, Maelys himself was killed by Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), a young [knight](wiki:Knight) from the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands), who thereby ended the Blackfyre threat to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)(wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)(wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Maelys's death decided the war, as the other members of the Band of Nine were barely interested in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), and fell back to their own domains. Their union would not last much longer.(wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

## Appearances & Description

Maelys had a grotesquely huge torso and arms.(wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) His skull was huge and malformed, and he had a second head sprouting from his neck, about the size of a child's fist.(wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He was inhumanly strong, able to kill a [destrier](wiki:Horse) with a single punch, and tear a man's head clean off his shoulders. He was named "Maelys the Monstrous" for his grotesque body, fearsome strength, and savage nature.(wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

[Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) reads [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy)'s page in the [White Book](wiki:White_Book) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), where it is written that he slew Maelys the Monstrous, last of the Blackfyre Pretenders, in single combat during the War of the Ninepenny Kings.(wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Feast for Crows

Jaime recalls visiting [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) as a boy, ignoring [Lysa Tully](wiki:Lysa_Tully) while begging her uncle [Brynden](wiki:Brynden_Tully) for tales of Maelys the Monstrous and the [Ebon Prince](wiki:Ebon_Prince).(wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

In [Essos](wiki:Essos), [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) tells [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) that when Maelys died, the male line of [House Blackfyre](wiki:House_Blackfyre) came to an end. That night, Tyrion has a nightmare where he has two heads, and fights in battle alongside Barristan Selmy and [Bittersteel](wiki:Bittersteel), slaying [his father](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) and [brother](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) while his second head weeps.(wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

At the camp of the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company), [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) sees that [the captain-general](wiki:Harry_Strickland)'s tent is surrounded by a ring of pikes topped with the [gilded skulls](wiki:Golden_skulls) of previous captain-generals. Maelys's skull is larger than the rest, grotesquely malformed, and below it is a second skull, no larger than a child's fist, that of Maelys's nameless twin brother.(wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Barristan Selmy recalls that King [Jaehaerys II Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_II_Targaryen) had granted him the white cloak of the Kingsguard after he slew Maelys Blackfyre during the War of the Ninepenny Kings.(wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
