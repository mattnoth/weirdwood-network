## Origins

Ben's mother is a Dothraki, and his grandmother was half-[Ibbenese](wiki:Ibbenese) and half-[Qohorik](wiki:Qohor). His grandfather was killed by a Dothraki prior to Ben's birth.(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Ben says he is part [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos), [Summer Islander](wiki:Summer_Islands), [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibben), [Qohorik](wiki:Qohor), [Dornish](wiki:Dorne), [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki), and [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros). He claims to have a drop of [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) blood, which must be why [dragons](wiki:Dragon), particularly [Viserion](wiki:Viserion), have an affinity for him. He states an [old member](wiki:Ossifer_Plumm) of [House Plumm](wiki:House_Plumm) married a "[dragon princess](wiki:Elaena_Targaryen)". He vaguely says that the wedding took place in the reign of [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen). [Daenerys](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) points out that there were five Aegons who reigned in Westeros and asks which one, but Ben fails to remember the regnal number requested.(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) suspects him to be a distant descendant of a younger son of [Viserys Plumm](wiki:Viserys_Plumm) and a possible distant relation to the famous [Ossifer Plumm](wiki:Ossifer_Plumm), and that Ben thus has "two drops" of dragon blood, not just one,(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) as Viserys Plumm was rumored to have been fathered by King [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen) on Princess [Elaena Targaryen](wiki:Elaena_Targaryen), Ossifer's wife.(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Ben once served as a bodyguard to the uncle of [Oznak zo Pahl](wiki:Oznak_zo_Pahl) in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), before joining the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons). When someone Ben knew was killed by Oznak for looking at a woman the wrong way, Ben fled, escaping the city through its sewer system, an experience that haunts him.(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

While plundering after his first battle with the Second Sons, Plumm took some leather armor from a dead sellsword, and discovered a fortune of coins the man had sewn into the lining of the armor. In his naivety, Ben told a fellow sellsword whom he believed a friend. The fellow sellsword told their sergeant, and his colleagues relieved Ben of his fortune. Ben loves [gold](wiki:Gold), but this incident made him realize that it is useless if one is not alive to enjoy it.(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Ben claims to have fought in a hundred battles, but he does not claim to have fought bravely in all of them.(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

## Appearances & Description

Ben is an aging man, but still fit.(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He has a broad, weathered face, brown skin, a broken nose, and grey-white hair.(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He has a salt and pepper beard.(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He has large, dark, almond-shaped [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) eyes that he inherited from his mother.(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) There are wrinkles at the corners of his eyes.(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Ben is elected as the new commander of the Second Sons after [Mero](wiki:Mero) disappears during the fight at [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai). Ben sits on [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s war council. It is his idea to use the sewer system of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) as a route to besiege the city.(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

### A Dance with Dragons

Daenerys sends Brown Ben and the Second Sons to guard the south against any incursions by Yunkai.(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) When Plumm returns to the city and discovers Daenerys could no longer control her dragons, he decides her cause is lost and goes over to the Yunkai'i.(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

During the slave market held outside [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), Plumm sees [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and [Penny](wiki:Penny) being sold, and attempts to buy them,(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) with a mind to delivering Tyrion's head to Daenerys as a wedding gift,(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2), or to [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) to claim the reward of a lordship.(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) However, he is outbid by [Yezzan zo Qaggaz](wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz).

Ben is present when [Yezzan zo Qaggaz](wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz) feasts the Yunkish commanders. He plays [cyvasse](wiki:Cyvasse) with Tyrion, losing the games to Tyrion, and a small fortune to Yezzan.(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Ben, along with the other three sellsword commanders, is allowed to enter Meereen when peace is declared. He explains to Dany the reasons he turned his cloak.(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3) After Tyrion and [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) escape the dying Yezzan, Plumm allows them to join the Second Sons. Tyrion writes sixty documents promising large sums of gold to several high ranking members in the company. To Plumm, he promises the largest reward, "One hundred thousand gold dragons, fifty hides of fertile land, a castle, and a lordship". Tyrion promises to honor the documents when he becomes [Lord of Casterly Rock](wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Ben_Plumm.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
