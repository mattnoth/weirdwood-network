## Origins

When [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) was sent to the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor) with intent to become Paxter's [page](wiki:Page) and [cupbearer](wiki:Cupbearer), Hobber and [Horas](wiki:Horas_Redwyne) despised him on sight. They found a new method to humiliate him in the practice yard every day. One day, Hobber dressed a kitchen girl in his [armor](wiki:Armor) and allowed her to beat Sam with a wooden [sword](wiki:Sword) until he cried. When she revealed herself, all the [squires](wiki:Squire) and pages and stableboys laughed.(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

## Appearances & Description

Hobber is homely, with orange hair and a square, freckled face.(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) (agot-arya-05:107: "homely youths with orange hair and square, freckled faces"; mocking nicknames "Ser Horror and Ser Slobber" coined by Sansa Stark and Jeyne Poole)

Hobber despises [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), the son of Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly).(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) "Ser Slobber", a mocking nickname given to Hobber by [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole), becomes his address by the entire court.(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) is unable to tell the the twins apart(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) and does not consider them to be particularly intelligent.(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) The twins often argue over which one will be the next [Lord of the Arbor](wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor).(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

Hobber has a chestnut mare(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) and a black stallion caparisoned in burgundy and blue.(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Hobber and his brother, Ser [Horas](wiki:Horas_Redwyne), take part in the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole) mock Hobber and his brother, Ser [Horas](wiki:Horas_Redwyne), as Ser Slobber and Ser Horror, respectively, and giggle whenever they see them.(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

The twins remain in the capital after the death of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), and ignore Sansa Stark when she attends King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon)'s first court session in the [throne room](wiki:Throne_room).(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), hiding in [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom), is nearly run down by the twins and their guardsmen as they race to the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor). The twins are present for the execution of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) at the Great Sept.(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)

### A Clash of Kings

The [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) keeps the Redwyne twins as [hostages](wiki:Hostage) for the good behavior of their father, Lord [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne), during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Unlike other lords from the [Reach](wiki:Reach), Paxter refrains from joining his forces to that of [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), the uncle and rival of King Joffrey.(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

During the [tourney on King Joffrey's name day](wiki:Tourney_on_King_Joffrey's_name_day), Hobber faces Ser [Meryn Trant](wiki:Meryn_Trant) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), and they both break their first lances but remain seated. However, on the second round, Ser Meryn's lance strikes Hobber in the chest and unhorses him. Joffrey disapproves of Hobber's performance, while Horas rushes out to help his dazed brother from the field.(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

Hobber and Horas attempt to sneak out of King's Landing by bribing a guard and sailing away on *[Moonrunner](wiki:Moonrunner)*, but the plot is discovered by Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys). [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), has the guard seized and sent to the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), and the twins are placed under closer supervision.(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

The twins are in attendance when Tyrion holds court in the throne room,(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) and they ignore [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) after the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross).(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

After Renly's death, Hobber is allowed to accompany Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) on his mission to recruit [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) at [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge) while Horas remains in captivity at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) This show of good faith allows Paxter to ride with the [Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) host to defend the capital during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Paxter and his sons are among those honored in the throne room after the battle.(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Sansa notices one of the Redwyne twins sparring with Ser [Tallad the Tall](wiki:Tallad_the_Tall) in an inner yard of the Red Keep.(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), now Joffrey's Hand, considers having his daughter, the widowed Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), wed one of the Redwyne twins.(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

Ser Hobber attends the wedding of [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) and King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) at the [Great Sept](wiki:Great_Sept), but the boy king is [poisoned](wiki:Poison) during the [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding).(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) Hobber is called as a witness for the prosecution in the Tyrion's trial for Joffrey's death, and relates that he saw Tyrion fill the [wedding chalice](wiki:Wedding_chalice).(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Hobber, Horas, and their father Paxter attend Tywin's funeral at the Great Sept.(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

According to [Taena Merryweather](wiki:Taena_Merryweather), the twins are both in love with Queen Margaery, now the wife of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), forgetting their arguments over who will be the next [Lord of the Arbor](wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor) and instead wanting to join the Kingsguard just to be near her.(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3) Hobber and Horas visit with Margaery often, with Hobber bringing fruit and flowers and Horas taking up the [lute](wiki:Lute).(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) During one of Margaery's riding expeditions, the brothers escort [Meredyth Crane](wiki:Meredyth_Crane) and [Janna Fossoway](wiki:Janna_Fossoway).(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) On another occasion, Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) sees Ser [Osmund Kettleblack](wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack) sparring in the yard with one of the brothers.(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

As part of her plan to remove Margaery, Cersei has the [Blue Bard](wiki:Blue_Bard) include the Redwyne twins among those alleged to have slept with the younger queen. So as not to lose the support of [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet),(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) Cersei intends for the brothers to be falsely accused. Arrest warrants are signed for Hobber and Horas, and they are given comfortable rooms in a tower.(wiki:Hobber_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)
