## Identity

Edmyn Tully was the Lord of Riverrun and head of House Tully during Aegon's Conquest (wiki:Edmyn_Tully).

## Narrative Arc

Edmyn was the first of the riverlords to join Aegon the Conqueror against King Harren Hoare. When Harren and his line perished, Aegon rewarded Edmyn by raising him to Lord Paramount of the Trident (wiki:Edmyn_Tully). Edmyn helped repair damage done to the riverlands by Harren the Black and served as Hand of the King to Aegon I Targaryen. He had three daughters, one of whom married Quenton Qoherys, Lord of Harrenhal (wiki:Edmyn_Tully).
