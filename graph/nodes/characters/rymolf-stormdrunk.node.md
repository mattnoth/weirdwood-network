## Identity

Rymolf Stormdrunk is an ironborn steersman in the crew of Victarion Greyjoy (wiki:Rymolf_Stormdrunk.cite_ref-Racok24).

## Allegiances

He is `SWORN_TO` House Greyjoy (track_b: Allegiance). He is `CULTURE_OF` the ironborn (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Victarion Greyjoy loans Rymolf to Theon Greyjoy when Theon is putting together a new crew for the *Sea Bitch*. Asha Greyjoy, disguised as "Esgred," remarks that Rymolf is "a good man, so long as he's sober" (wiki:Rymolf_Stormdrunk.cite_ref-Racok24).
