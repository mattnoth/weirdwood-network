## Origins

Roro had sailed the *Cobblecat* past [the Wall](wiki:The_Wall), into a hundred little coves that had never seen a trading ship before, and traded arms to the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) in exchange for furs and other precious goods. On his return south of the Wall, the *Cobblecat* was intercepted by a [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) fleet and escorted to [Eastwatch-by-the-sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-sea).(wiki:Roro_Uhoris.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) There, Roro was executed on the deck of his ship for trading [arms](wiki:Armament) to the wildlings, and all the goods that he had received were confiscated.(wiki:Roro_Uhoris.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Appearances & Description

Roro claimed that he could tell one port from another just by the way they smelled. Cities were like women, he insisted; each one had its own unique scent. [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) was as flowery as a perfumed dowager. [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) was a milkmaid, fresh and earthy, with woodsmoke in her hair. [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) reeked like some unwashed whore. [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) was sharp, salty, and a little fishy like a mermaid should be.(wiki:Roro_Uhoris.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He said the more seals on [Seal Rock](wiki:Seal_Rock), the luckier the voyage would be.(wiki:Roro_Uhoris.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
