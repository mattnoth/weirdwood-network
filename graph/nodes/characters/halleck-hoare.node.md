## Appearances & Description

According to Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel), Halleck had closer ties to the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) than the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) despite ostensibly being a follower of the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God).(wiki:Halleck_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Like his father [Harwyn](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), Halleck was warlike and ruled with a heavy hand.(wiki:Halleck_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Origins

Halleck succeeded his father, King [Harwyn Hoare](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), who had conquered the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) from [Arrec Durrandon](wiki:Arrec_Durrandon), one of the [Storm Kings](wiki:Storm_King) from [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End). As [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers), Halleck visited the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) only three times for a total of two years.(wiki:Halleck_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
The remainder of King Halleck's time was spent on mainland [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) warring against the neighbors of the riverlands. Halleck was not as successful as his father, Harwyn Hardhand, as he was defeated in wars against the [Kingdom of the Rock](wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Rock) and the [Kingdom of the Storm](wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Storm). Halleck also failed three times in attacks on the [Bloody Gate](wiki:Bloody_Gate), the bulwark of the [Kings of Mountain and Vale](wiki:Kings_of_Mountain_and_Vale).(wiki:Halleck_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Halleck did succeed in extending control east to [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) and [Rosby](wiki:Rosby), however.(wiki:Halleck_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
When not at war Halleck ruled from a modest tower house in [Fairmarket](wiki:Fairmarket).(wiki:Halleck_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) His son and successor, King [Harren the Black](wiki:Harren_Hoare), built [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) as a grander seat on the shores of the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye) in the riverlands.(wiki:Halleck_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) Another son of Halleck was [Lord Commander Hoare](wiki:Lord_Commander_Hoare) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Halleck_Hoare.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Quotes

Though he called himself [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), sacrificed to the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God), and always kept three [priests](wiki:Priests_of_the_Drowned_God) at his side, there was more of the [Trident](wiki:Trident) than of the [salt sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea) in Halleck Hoare, and he seemed to look upon [the islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) only as a source of [arms](wiki:Armament), [ships](wiki:Ship), and [men](wiki:Army).(wiki:Halleck_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)
Like [his sire](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), King Halleck spent a great deal of his reign in camp tents, on campaign. When not at war, he ruled his broad domains from a modest tower house at [Fairmarket](wiki:Fairmarket) in the heart of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), near the site of his father's [greatest victory](wiki:Battle_at_Fairmarket).(wiki:Halleck_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)
