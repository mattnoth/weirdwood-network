## Origins

When [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) went forth to seize the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) and [conquer King's Landing](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing), she named Ser Robert as castellan of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), passing Ser [Alfred Broome](wiki:Alfred_Broome) over despite the fact that he was the most senior of the knights on the island and expected to be named castellan. Rhaenyra chose Robert because of his more affable character.(wiki:Robert_Quince.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

When Robert heard the news that the [Grey Ghost](wiki:Grey_Ghost) had been killed by another dragon he was quick to name the [Cannibal](wiki:Cannibal_(dragon)) as the killer. The fisherfolk urged Robert to dispatch knights to the dragon's lair but he refused, reasoning that if they did not trouble the Cannibal he would not trouble them. He then forbade fishing in the waters beneath the [Dragonmont](wiki:Dragonmont)'s eastern face where the Grey Ghost's body lay rotting.(wiki:Robert_Quince.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Although Robert was loyal to the queen and her cause some of the men under him were less than leal and harbored certain resentments and grudges for old wrongs whether real or imagined. Prominent amongst them was Alfred. Prior to, and during, the [fall of Dragonstone](wiki:Fall_of_Dragonstone) to King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), Alfred proved more than willing to betray his queen. He led the men who burst into Robert's chambers and slew him, driving a spear into his huge pale belly. The thrust was delivered with such force that the spear went out Robert's back, through the feather-bed and straw mattress, and into the floor beneath.(wiki:Robert_Quince.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Rhaenyra eventually [fled back to Dragonstone](wiki:Flight_to_Dragonstone). When she came ashore Rhaenyra was met by Ser Alfred Broome. She asked why Robert had not come himself to meet her, to which Alfred replied that the queen would be seeing "our fat friend" at the castle.(wiki:Robert_Quince.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Rhaenyra and her party were taken by escort to Robert's charred corpse, which was burned beyond all recognition, and hanging from the battlements of the gatehouse beside Dragonstone's steward, master-at-arms, captain of guards and the upper torso of Grand Maester [Gerardys](wiki:Gerardys). Only by the enormous size of the corpse did they recognize that it was Robert's.(wiki:Robert_Quince.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
