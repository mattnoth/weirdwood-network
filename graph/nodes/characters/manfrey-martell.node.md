## Origins

When Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell) withdrew to the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens) in [298 AC](wiki:298_AC), he left Manfrey as castellan of [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear). Although Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) had command in Doran's absence, Manfrey helped to govern with [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Myles](wiki:Myles), [Ricasso](wiki:Ricasso) the seneschal, and the treasurer [Alyse Ladybright](wiki:Alyse_Ladybright).(wiki:Manfrey_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Appearances & Description

According to Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart), Manfrey is ancient.(wiki:Manfrey_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He is married.(wiki:Manfrey_Martell.cite_ref-twow_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Manfrey tells Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) that Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) is with Lord [Anders Yronwood](wiki:Anders_Yronwood)'s host in the [Boneway](wiki:Boneway).(wiki:Manfrey_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

After her coup is prevented, Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) is taken into the custody of Ser Manfrey at Sunspear. He informs her that her companions will be taken to [Ghaston Grey](wiki:Ghaston_Grey) and then walks Arianne to her pleasantly-appointed cell in the Spear Tower.(wiki:Manfrey_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
