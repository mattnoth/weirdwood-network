## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

During her quest to find [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) notices a skinny boy on a piebald rounsey at [Rosby](wiki:Rosby). Later, the hedge knight Ser [Creighton Longbough](wiki:Creighton_Longbough) mentions seeing some farm boy on a piebald horse go by while he was on watch.(wiki:Old_piebald_rounsey.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Going about her business, Brienne keeps catching glimpses of the skinny boy on the piebald rounsey, including at [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale).(wiki:Old_piebald_rounsey.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Near the [Hollard castle](wiki:Hollard_castle), she confronts him by sneaking up and giving the old horse a whack across the rump with the flat of her longsword, causing the horse to rear and the boy to fall off. The boy turns out to be [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s former squire, [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne).(wiki:Old_piebald_rounsey.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
Pod joins Brienne on her quest to find Sansa and rides the old piebald rounsey during their travels. Ser [Hyle Hunt](wiki:Hyle_Hunt) is unimpressed with the quality of Pod's horseflesh and says so at [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool).(wiki:Old_piebald_rounsey.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
When the travelers reach the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads), [Willow Heddle](wiki:Willow_Heddle) has [Wat](wiki:Wat_(orphan)) help them with their horses.(wiki:Old_piebald_rounsey.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
