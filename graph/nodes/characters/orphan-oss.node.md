## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Orphan Oss is a member of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) that leaves [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) and the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) in search of [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark) and [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder). He survives the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist) and is among those who make it back to [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep). There, he breaks [guest right](wiki:Guest_right) and takes part in the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep) against the [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont).(wiki:Orphan_Oss.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Orphan Oss remains at Craster's Keep after the betrayal, along with a few other former members of the Night's Watch.(wiki:Orphan_Oss.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance With Dragon

Five mutineers wanders in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest), planning to head south over the [Wall](wiki:Wall). However, they encounter [Coldhands](wiki:Coldhands), who kills and buries their bodies, which are found by [Summer](wiki:Summer), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark)'s [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf).(wiki:Orphan_Oss.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) It is unknown if Orphan Oss is among them.
