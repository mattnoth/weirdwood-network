## Origins

Rickard was born at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell)(wiki:Rickard_Stark.cite_ref-Rawoiafrickard_stark.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) the only child of Lord [Edwyle Stark](wiki:Edwyle_Stark) and Lady [Marna Locke](wiki:Marna_Locke).(wiki:Rickard_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_stark_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Rickard married his cousin once removed, [Lyarra Stark](wiki:Lyarra_Stark), and had four children: [Brandon](wiki:Brandon_Stark) (b. [262 AC](wiki:262_AC)), [Eddard](wiki:Eddard_Stark) (b. [263 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)), [Lyanna](wiki:Lyanna_Stark) (b. [266 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) or [267 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)), and [Benjen](wiki:Benjen_Stark). Lord Rickard visited [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in [264 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), leading King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) to consider building another [Wall](wiki:Wall) and claiming more land in the [far north](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:Rickard_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Lady [Olenna Redwyne](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell) was loosely acquainted with Rickard.(wiki:Rickard_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

After killing a miller and raping [his wife](wiki:Miller's_wife), Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) had the tongue cut out from the miller's brother to prevent Lord Rickard from learning what happened.(wiki:Rickard_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Rickard negotiated the betrothal of his heir Brandon to [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Rickard_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) According to a semi-canon source, Rickard accepted a betrothal between his daughter Lyanna and Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) when his younger son Eddard carried Robert's proposal to Winterfell.(wiki:Rickard_Stark.cite_ref-Rawoiafeddard_stark.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) According to [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin), both proposed marriages were the idea of Rickard's [maester](wiki:Maester) at the time, [Walys](wiki:Walys_Flowers).(wiki:Rickard_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

In [282 AC](wiki:282_AC), Brandon was traveling to meet Rickard's party coming down from the north in preparation of Brandon's wedding to Catelyn, when news of Lyanna's abduction at the hand of Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) reached him.(wiki:Rickard_Stark.cite_ref-Rawoiafcatelyn_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Brandon went to King's Landing with a small company of young nobles to challenge Rhaegar, but was imprisoned with his companions by King Aerys II Targaryen on the charge of conspiring to kill the crown prince in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep). Aerys next summoned Rickard and the fathers of Brandon's companions to come and answer for the crimes of their sons.(wiki:Rickard_Stark.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

Rickard demanded a [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat), which was granted by the king. According to Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), Rickard expected to fight one of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). Dressed in his steel armor, he was brought to the [throne room](wiki:Great_Hall_(Red_Keep)), where King Aerys II declared that fire was the champion of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen). Rickard was suspended from the rafters, and a fire was lit beneath him by the [pyromancers](wiki:Pyromancers). Rickard was slowly cooked to death in his armor, while his son Brandon, with a [Tyroshi](wiki:Tyrosh) noose around his neck and a sword just out of his reach, was made to watch his father roast. Trying to reach the sword to save his dying father, Brandon strangled himself.(wiki:Rickard_Stark.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) Ser [Gerold Hightower](wiki:Gerold_Hightower) and Ser Jaime Lannister of the Kingsguard were among those present for Rickard's and Brandon's execution. The other nobles and their sons were also executed by Aerys, with the exception of [Ethan Glover](wiki:Ethan_Glover).(wiki:Rickard_Stark.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3)

Rickard was succeeded by his second son, Eddard. Together with Lords [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), and Robert Baratheon, Eddard was a leader of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), the revolt against the Targaryens that overthrew their dynasty. Some have blamed Rickard's alliances for the near fall of [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark).(wiki:Rickard_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_lords_of_winterfell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Lord Eddard did not reveal the true manner of Rickard's death to his family; both his wife Catelyn and his son [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) believed Rickard was beheaded.(wiki:Rickard_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)(wiki:Rickard_Stark.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-4)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Rickard Stark](wiki:Category:Images_of_Rickard_Stark)*

In the [crypts of Winterfell](wiki:Crypts_of_Winterfell), there is a statue of Rickard at his tomb, which is flanked by those of his eldest son, [Brandon](wiki:Brandon_Stark), and his daughter, [Lyanna](wiki:Lyanna_Stark). Rickard's engraving is said to match his appearance in life with a long, stern face and quiet dignity,(wiki:Rickard_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) thinks the statue's eyes look sad.(wiki:Rickard_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) There is a stone [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) at the statue's feet.(wiki:Rickard_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Lord Stark owned [steel](wiki:Steel) [armor](wiki:Armor) with [golden](wiki:Gold) spurs.(wiki:Rickard_Stark.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

According to [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin), Rickard was ambitious.(wiki:Rickard_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Clash of Kings

Rickard appears in [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy)'s nightmare of King Robert's feast of the dead at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Rickard_Stark.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Dance with Dragons
