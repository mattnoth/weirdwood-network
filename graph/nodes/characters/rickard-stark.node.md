## Identity

Rickard Stark was the Lord of Winterfell and Warden of the North, father of Brandon, Eddard, Lyanna, and Benjen Stark (track_b: Issue). Born between 230 and 250 AC at Winterfell, he was killed in 282 AC in the Great Hall of King's Landing by order of Aerys II Targaryen (track_b: Born, Died). He is buried in the Crypt of Winterfell (track_b: Buried).

## Origins

Son of Edwyle Stark and Marna Locke (track_b: Father, Mother). Born at Winterfell in the North (track_b: Born).

## Appearances & Description

His statue in the Winterfell crypts has a long, stern face with a stone sword across his lap (agot-eddard-01). Rickard Stark's father was said to have coined the term "the wolf blood" to describe a certain wildness in the Stark temperament (agot-arya-02).

## Narrative Arc

Rickard Stark served as Lord of Winterfell before Robert's Rebellion. He promised his daughter Lyanna's hand to Robert Baratheon (agot-eddard-09). When his eldest son Brandon rode to King's Landing to confront Prince Rhaegar over Lyanna's disappearance, Aerys II Targaryen summoned Rickard to answer for his son's actions. Rickard was burned alive in his armor while Brandon strangled himself trying to reach him (agot-eddard-01). Their deaths were among the sparks that ignited Robert's Rebellion. He went south once, to answer the summons of a king, and never came home (agot-catelyn-02). His statue stands in the Winterfell crypts between those of Brandon and Lyanna (agot-eddard-01).

## Quotes
