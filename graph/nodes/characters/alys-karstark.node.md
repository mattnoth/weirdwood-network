## Origins

When she was six, Alys and her father, Lord [Rickard](wiki:Rickard_Karstark), visited [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). Despite Alys's young age, Rickard wanted her to charm [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) in hopes of arranging a [betrothal](wiki:Betrothal). She danced with Robb, whom she considered courteous, and with [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), who was sullen.(wiki:Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Alys is now betrothed to [Daryn Hornwood](wiki:Daryn_Hornwood), heir to the [Hornwood](wiki:Hornwood); they are waiting for her to [flower](wiki:Flowering) before they marry.(wiki:Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Alys Karstark](wiki:Category:Images_of_Alys_Karstark)*

Alys is a tall, skinny, coltish girl. She weaves her brown hair into a braid and has a small bosom. The pale girl has a long face, a pointy chin, blue-grey eyes, and small ears.(wiki:Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Alys is almost as old as [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow).(wiki:Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Alys' betrothed, [Daryn Hornwood](wiki:Daryn_Hornwood), as well two of her brothers, [Eddard](wiki:Eddard_Karstark) and [Torrhen](wiki:Torrhen_Karstark), are killed at the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood) by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords

After the escape of Jaime from captivity at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), Lord [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark) promises Alys's hand to whoever brings him the Kingslayer.(wiki:Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat) maims and captures Jaime with the hopes of gaining Alys in marriage, which would give him the chance to become Lord of [Karhold](wiki:Karhold).(wiki:Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), executes Alys's father Rickard for treason, for murdering the captive [Willem Lannister](wiki:Willem_Lannister) and [Tion Frey](wiki:Tion_Frey).(wiki:Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) prophesies to [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), of a grey girl on a dying horse riding to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) to escape an arranged marriage. Melisandre believes it is Jon's half-sister, [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), who is supposed to marry [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow).(wiki:Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Jon sends [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) with six [spearwives](wiki:Spearwives) south to aid the girl.(wiki:Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Alys Karstark has now flowered at Karhold. [Arnolf Karstark](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark), the castellan of Karhold and the uncle of Alys's late father, hopes to wed the girl to his son, [Cregan Karstark](wiki:Cregan_Karstark). Arnolf also declares for [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) to goad the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) into executing the rightful heir of Karhold, Alys's brother [Harrion](wiki:Harrion_Karstark). To avoid marrying Cregan, Alys flees her cousin and seeks aid from Jon at the Wall.(wiki:Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

[Ty](wiki:Ty) and [Dannel](wiki:Dannel) find a weakened Alys on a dying horse near [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town) and bring her to the chambers of [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black). Alys reveals to Jon that Arnolf plans to betray Stannis on behalf of Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), the new [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North).(wiki:Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) When Cregan arrives at the Wall to claim Alys, he is thrown into an [ice cell](wiki:Ice_cell) by Jon.(wiki:Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Ser [Patrek of King's Mountain](wiki:Patrek_of_King's_Mountain) hopes to wed Alys, but Jon instead arranges for Alys to marry [Sigorn](wiki:Sigorn), [Magnar](wiki:Magnar) of [Thenn](wiki:Thenn_(valley)), thus creating a [new noble house](wiki:House_Thenn) that would claim Karhold should Harrion perish. Two of Cregan's men join Alys, who believes that Karhold will open its gates to her and Sigorn's two hundred [Thenns](wiki:Thenns).

During [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark), which is performed at Castle Black by Melisandre, Alys wears lace in her hair and leaps across a flaming ditch with her new husband.(wiki:Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) Because of the arrival of winter, Jon advises Alys during the wedding feast to send Karhold's old men and spare boys to help at the Wall. Sigorn is reluctant to dance during the feast, but he enjoys dancing with Alys once he is inebriated.(wiki:Alys_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2)
