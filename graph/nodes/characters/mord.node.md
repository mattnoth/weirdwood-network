## Identity

Mord is the gaoler of House Arryn, in charge of the sky cells of the Eyrie (wiki:Mord).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Arryn (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Valemen (track_b: Culture).

## Origins

Born in the Vale of Arryn (track_b: Born).

## Appearances & Description

A large man weighing twenty stone, with rotting brown teeth and small dark eyes. The left side of his face is scarred and lopsided from an axe blow that took his ear and part of his cheek. Has a heavy white belly and thick fingers. Brutish, fond of tormenting prisoners. Wears ill-fitting rank-smelling clothes, steel-toed boots, and carries a leather strap and lash (wiki:Mord).

## Narrative Arc

In AGOT, Mord is Tyrion Lannister's jailer in the sky cells. He claims Tyrion's shadowskin cloak, beats and taunts him, but Tyrion bribes Mord with promises of gold to carry his message to Lysa. After Tyrion wins his trial by combat, he pays Mord a purse of gold (wiki:Mord). In AFFC, Mord has capped his teeth with gold. Under Petyr Baelish's orders, he tortures Marillion in the sky cells, removing fingers from the singer's hands. Marillion eventually confesses to murdering Lysa (wiki:Mord).
