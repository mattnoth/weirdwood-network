## Identity

Ser Ronnal Baratheon was a knight from House Baratheon and a younger brother of Lord Rogar Baratheon of Storm's End (wiki:Ronnal_Baratheon).

## Origins

## Narrative Arc

When Lord Rogar was dismissed as Hand, he sent Ronnal to retrieve Princess Aerea Targaryen from her quarters, but the girl had been spirited away on orders of Alyssa Velaryon. Rogar and his brothers returned to Storm's End. When Rogar was summoned to King's Landing in 50 AC, Ronnal and Garon urged him to defy the summons. The Shivers struck Westeros during the winter of 59–60 AC, and Ronnal, his wife, and his sons all perished of the disease at Storm's End. In the aftermath, Rogar brought Ronnal's two surviving daughters and his own daughter Jocelyn to court, asking Jaehaerys I and Alysanne to foster the girls (wiki:Ronnal_Baratheon).
