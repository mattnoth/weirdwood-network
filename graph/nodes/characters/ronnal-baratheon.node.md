## Origins

When Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) was dismissed as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), he sent his brother Ronnal to retrieve Princess [Aerea Targaryen](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen) from her quarters, but the girl had been spirited away on the orders of [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon), the [Queen Regent](wiki:Regent). Thereafter Lord Rogar and his brothers returned to [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End).(wiki:Ronnal_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

When Rogar was summoned to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) by King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) in [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), Ronnal and his brother Ser [Garon](wiki:Garon_Baratheon) urged Rogar to defy the summons.(wiki:Ronnal_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

The [Shivers](wiki:Shivers) hit Westeros during the winter of [59](wiki:59_AC)–[60 AC](wiki:60_AC), Ronnal, his wife, his sons, as well as Garon's wife all perished of the disease at Storm's End.(wiki:Ronnal_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) In the aftermath of the winter, Lord Rogar came to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with Ronnal's two surviving daughters and his own daughter, [Jocelyn](wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon), to ask King Jaehaerys I and Queen [Alysanne](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) to [foster](wiki:Foster) the three Baratheon girls at court. The royal couple accepted.(wiki:Ronnal_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)
