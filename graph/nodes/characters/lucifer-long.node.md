The lanky Lucifer bears a grudge against [Caggo](wiki:Caggo), who took a [slave](wiki:Slave) girl from him and instead of returning her after he was done, Caggo killed her.(wiki:Lucifer_Long.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
