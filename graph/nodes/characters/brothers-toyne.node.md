## Origins

After Ser Terrence Toyne was found abed with [Bethany Bracken](wiki:Bethany_Bracken), Aegon IV's mistress, by the king himself, Ser Terrence was sentenced to death by dismemberment. Terrence's two brothers sought vengeance against the King, but were themselves slain by Prince [Aemon the Dragonknight](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_II)), though they managed to kill the prince. These series of actions led to the downfall of House Toyne.(wiki:Brothers_Toyne.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### Legacy

The brothers Toyne went down in history as infamous traitors, along the likes of [Daemon Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_Blackfyre), [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Hareth](wiki:Hareth_(Grand_Maester)) and Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen).(wiki:Brothers_Toyne.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
