## Origins

At the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Tyler Norcross declared for King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) and the [greens](wiki:Greens). Like the rest of the supporters of the greens from the [Reach](wiki:Reach), he joined the host of Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower) on his march to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Tyler_Norcross.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

After the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton) and the death of Lord Ormund, the army was paralyzed in the [city](wiki:Tumbleton) without a clear commander and without knowing with certainty the intentions of the [Two Betrayers](wiki:Two_Betrayers), [Hugh Hammer](wiki:Hugh_Hammer) and [Ulf White](wiki:Ulf_White). One night, Tyler and twelve other [lords](wiki:Lord) and [landed knights](wiki:Landed_knight) of the Reach assembled at an inn, the [Bloody Caltrops](wiki:Bloody_Caltrops), to conspire against Hugh and Ulf. During the discussions, Tyler expressed he wanted to use Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I))'s [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Tessarion](wiki:Tessarion), to retake the capital. The [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton) broke out before the [Caltrops](wiki:Caltrops) could carry out their plans, however.(wiki:Tyler_Norcross.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Tyler and most of his companions died during the battle.(wiki:Tyler_Norcross.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Tyler_Norcross.cite_ref-Deaths_1-1)
