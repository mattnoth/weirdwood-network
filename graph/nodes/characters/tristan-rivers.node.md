## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Tristan Rivers is present during [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington)’s rendezvous with the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) three miles south of [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys), and voices a strong opinion on [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis)'s plans:

> Which plan? The fat man’s plan? The one that changes every time the [moon](wiki:Moon) turns? First [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) was to join us with fifty thousand [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) screamers at his back. Then the Beggar King was dead, and it was to be [the sister](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), a pliable young child queen who was on her way to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) with three new-hatched [dragons](wiki:Dragon). Instead the girl turns up on [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay) and leaves a string of burning cities in her wake, and the fat man decides we should meet her by [Volantis](wiki:Volantis). Now that plan is in ruins as well. I have had enough of Illyrio’s plans. [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) won the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) without the benefit of dragons. We can do the same.(wiki:Tristan_Rivers.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Tristan, like the rest of the company, swears his allegiance to [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)) before they [invade Westeros](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company).

While a quarter of the company’s available strength [takes Griffin's Roost](wiki:Taking_of_Griffin's_Roost), Tristan sets off simultaneously for the seat of [House Morrigen](wiki:House_Morrigen) at [Crow's Nest](wiki:Crow's_Nest), and [Laswell Peake](wiki:Laswell_Peake) for [Rain House](wiki:Rain_House), the stronghold of [House Wylde](wiki:House_Wylde), each with a formidable force of comparable size. If Rivers and Peake are successful, [Harry Strickland](wiki:Harry_Strickland) argues that the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) will control a better part of [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath).(wiki:Tristan_Rivers.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Tristan successfully [takes](wiki:Taking_of_Crow's_Nest) Crow's Nest.(wiki:Tristan_Rivers.cite_ref-Rawoiafcrow.27s_nest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
