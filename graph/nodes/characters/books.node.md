## Appearances & Description

Books is a notorious reader; he always seems to have his nose poked in some crumbly scroll. He considers [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) to be both murderous and [mad](wiki:Targaryen_madness).(wiki:Books.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
