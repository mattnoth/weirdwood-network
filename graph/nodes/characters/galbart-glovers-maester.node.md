## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

While [Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover) is fighting in southern [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), his castle of [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) is [captured](wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte) by [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) as part of the invasion of the [north](wiki:North) by [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn).(wiki:Galbart_Glover's_maester.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Asha returns to Deepwood after [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) is selected in the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk). Galbart's maester, always anxious around Asha, brings her a letter sent from [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) in which [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) demands the ironborn vacate the north. Asha does not have a reply sent, but allows the maester to inform Lady [Sybelle Glover](wiki:Sybelle_Glover), Galbart's sister-in-law, of its contents.(wiki:Galbart_Glover's_maester.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
When [mountain clans](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans) try to infiltrate Deepwood in preparation of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s attack on the castle, Asha has the people of Deepwood gathered in the bailey. [Lorren Longaxe](wiki:Lorren_Longaxe) strikes the maester with a mailed fist when he protests, breaking his nose. The maester urges Asha to surrender to Stannis, but she decides instead to make for her longships on the [Bay of Ice](wiki:Bay_of_Ice). Asha is subsequently captured in the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Galbart_Glover's_maester.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), has [Clydas](wiki:Clydas) send a [raven](wiki:Raven) to Deepwood in an attempt to warn Stannis of [Arnolf Karstark](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark)'s planned treachery.(wiki:Galbart_Glover's_maester.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

[Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris) brings Jon's message from Deepwood to Stannis at a [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village) in time to warn the king about Arnolf.(wiki:Galbart_Glover's_maester.cite_ref-4)
