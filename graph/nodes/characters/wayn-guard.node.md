## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Wayn is one of the guards, [Quent](wiki:Quent) being the other, who accompany [Robb](wiki:Robb_Stark) and [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) to the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) for Bran's first ride since his fall. He and Quent fall behind and then follow [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) after he spies a turkey, failing to guard Bran, who is seized by [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings). After the skirmish, Wayn binds [Osha](wiki:Osha) for the return to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Wayn_(guard).cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
