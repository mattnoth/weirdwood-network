## Identity

Maester Kyrie was a maester of the Citadel who served House Baratheon at Storm's End during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen (wiki:Kyrie).

## Narrative Arc

In 54 AC, Lord Rogar Baratheon's pregnant wife Alyssa Velaryon fell ill and went into premature labor. Maester Kyrie attended with a midwife and three bedmaids but could only relieve Alyssa's pain. When King Jaehaerys I and Queen Alysanne arrived at Storm's End, Kyrie told them he could not save Alyssa, but might save her child by cutting her open. Lord Rogar told him to save the child, and Kyrie delivered Jocelyn Baratheon (wiki:Kyrie).
