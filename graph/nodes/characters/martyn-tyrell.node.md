## Origins

After the murder of Lord [Rego Draz](wiki:Rego_Draz), a new [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin) was needed. Septon [Barth](wiki:Barth) put forward Martyn who he knew from his time serving at [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden);(wiki:Martyn_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) while not praising his wits, he mentioned that Martyn's wife, the Lady [Florence Fossoway](wiki:Florence_Fossoway), had been handling the [Reach](wiki:Reach)'s accounts for years and had increased the incomes of Highgarden by a third. King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) agreed with the septon and summoned Martyn and his wife to court, believing that Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) would be fond of the intelligent Lady Florence.(wiki:Martyn_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Martyn—aided and abetted by his clever wife—proved almost as able as Rego, the so-called Lord of Air, had been as master of coin. This made the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) prosperous and allowed King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) to expand his project of roads, creating the [roseroad](wiki:Roseroad), the [ocean road](wiki:Ocean_road), the [river road](wiki:River_road), and the [goldroad](wiki:Goldroad) once the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad) was completed.(wiki:Martyn_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Appearances & Description

Martyn was a young man when his lord father passed away in [59 AC](wiki:59_AC). Lord [Manfryd Redwyne](wiki:Manfryd_Redwyne) believed Martyn to be a dolt, though Septon [Barth](wiki:Barth) favored him as young and eager, but could not vouch for his wits.(wiki:Martyn_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
