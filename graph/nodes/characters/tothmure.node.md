## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) is aided by three maesters during his occupation of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Tothmure.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) It is unclear if Tothmure accompanied Tywin's army from the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands), or if he was already the castle's maester under [Shella Whent](wiki:Shella_Whent), the former Lady of Harrenhal. Tothmure receives [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s letter concerning the illegitimacy of Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s children with King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). Tywin orders the maester to burn the letter and never speak of the subject again. [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) overhears Tothmure's serving girl tell her brother about the letter.(wiki:Tothmure.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Because Tothmure sends ravens to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) and [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) during the [fall of Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal) to [northmen](wiki:Northmen), the castle's new occupant, Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), has the maester executed and his head put on a spike.(wiki:Tothmure.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
