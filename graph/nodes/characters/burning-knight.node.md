## Origins

During the [Storming of the Dragonpit](wiki:Storming_of_the_Dragonpit), the Burning Knight slew the [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [Morghul](wiki:Morghul). Encased in heavy [armor](wiki:Armor), he rushed headlong into the [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame) with spear in hand, thrusting the point into the dragon's eye repeatedly, even as the flames melted the [steel](wiki:Steel) and burning his flesh, thus giving him his nickname.(wiki:Burning_Knight.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
