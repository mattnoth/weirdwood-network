## Identity

Princess Saera Targaryen was the ninthborn child and fifthborn daughter of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen and Queen Alysanne Targaryen (wiki:Saera_Targaryen).

## Origins

Born 67 AC (track_b: Born). Valyrian race, Crownlands culture (track_b: Race, Culture).

## Appearances & Description

A pretty girl, taller than her sister Daella. Courageous and clever, as clever as her brother Vaegon, as strong and spirited as her sister Alyssa. Tempestuous, demanding, and disobedient — her first word was "no." Difficult to resist when she wanted to be charming (wiki:Saera_Targaryen).

## Narrative Arc

Saera was fierce and stubborn, thriving on attention. Her eldest brothers Aemon and Baelon were amused by her mischiefs but never knew the worst of them. According to Septon Barth, all her sisters disliked her. At age twelve, she desired to be the firstborn or even the king's only child.

Saera's scandal involved a sexual affair with multiple men — Ser Braxton Beesbury, Lord Roy Connington, and Lord Jonah Mooton. When discovered, Jaehaerys sentenced her to the Faith as a novice at the motherhouse in Oldtown. She escaped across the narrow sea, eventually becoming a wealthy woman in Volantis where she kept a pleasure house and took a Triarch of Volantis as a lover, bearing at least three bastard sons.

When the Great Council of 101 AC was called to settle the succession, Saera put forward one of her bastard sons as a claim, which was treated with derision. She died in or after 101 AC (wiki:Saera_Targaryen).
