## Origins

Old Crackbones is remembered for bringing peace to [Crackclaw Point](wiki:Crackclaw_Point) before [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), though the peace did not last long beyond his death.(wiki:Crackbones.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Crackbones allegedly tied the neck of a dragon into a knot.(wiki:Crackbones.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
