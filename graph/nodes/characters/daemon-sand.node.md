## Origins

Daemon is not much older than Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell).(wiki:Daemon_Sand.cite_ref-twow-arianne_3-3) He took her [maidenhead](wiki:Maidenhead) when they were both fourteen years old,(wiki:Daemon_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and their couplings had been both clumsy and ardent. Arianne recalls that it had been sweet.(wiki:Daemon_Sand.cite_ref-twow-arianne_3-4) Although her father [Doran](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), eventually learned of this, he did not react to it.(wiki:Daemon_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Daemon asked Doran for Arianne's hand, but was refused,(wiki:Daemon_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and relations between Daemon and Arianne were not the same after.(wiki:Daemon_Sand.cite_ref-twow-arianne_3-5)

Daemon [squired](wiki:Squire) for Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell),(wiki:Daemon_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and received his [knighthood](wiki:Knight) from the Red Viper himself.(wiki:Daemon_Sand.cite_ref-twow-arianne_3-6) There are rumors he also became Oberyn's lover, though this is seldom said to his face.(wiki:Daemon_Sand.cite_ref-twow-arianne_3-7)

## Appearances & Description

Daemon is handsome, with a strong jaw, sky blue eyes, and light sandy brown hair. He has a close-cropped beard, and dimples appear when he smiles.(wiki:Daemon_Sand.cite_ref-twow-arianne_3-1)

Daemon is tall and gallant on horseback.(wiki:Daemon_Sand.cite_ref-twow-arianne_3-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Daemon is part of the nobles and knights that accompany Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to take his place on the [small council](wiki:Small_council), as had been negotiated by Oberyn's brother, [Doran](wiki:Doran_Martell).(wiki:Daemon_Sand.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) When Oberyn champions [Tyrion's](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) cause in a [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat), Daemon and Lord [Dagos Manwoody](wiki:Dagos_Manwoody) attend the Red Viper.(wiki:Daemon_Sand.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Feast for Crows

After Oberyn's death at the hands of Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane), Daemon sends a [raven](wiki:Raven) to [Nymeria Sand](wiki:Nymeria_Sand), telling of the fates of her father and the Mountain.(wiki:Daemon_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) When Daemon returns to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), he is swiftly imprisoned by Doran for demanding that the already-imprisoned [Sand Snakes](wiki:Sand_Snakes) be set free.(wiki:Daemon_Sand.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Doran frees Daemon, [Obara](wiki:Obara_Sand), [Tyene](wiki:Tyene_Sand), and Nymeria Sand. The knight is present at the feast when Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) arrives in [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) to deliver the [Mountain's skull](wiki:Mountain's_skull). When [Ricasso](wiki:Ricasso) raises a toast to King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), Daemon declines the toast.(wiki:Daemon_Sand.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Daemon becomes the [sworn shield](wiki:Sworn_shield) to Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell), traveling with her and her companions to the [Stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) to meet with Lord [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) and the alleged Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(Young_Griff)). On the way to [Ghost Hill](wiki:Ghost_Hill), Daemon informs Arianne what he knows of Jon Connington. At Ghost Hill, Arianne attempts to seduce Daemon, but he rejects her, as Doran had considered his bastard status unworthy of her hand. Instead, they talk of whether Aegon could be who he claims to be, an idea that Daemon rejects. From Ghost Hill, Arianne and her companions sail across the [Sea of Dorne](wiki:Sea_of_Dorne) to [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath). Aboard the *[Peregrine](wiki:Peregrine)*, Daemon defeats Arianne in a game of *[cyvasse](wiki:Cyvasse)*, and mocks her strategy. Arianne is frustrated by the fact that Prince Doran assigned Daemon as her shield, despite knowing of their history together.(wiki:Daemon_Sand.cite_ref-twow-arianne_3-8)

At [Weeping Town](wiki:Weeping_Town), Daemon gathers rumors for Arianne. From there, the company sets out for [Mistwood](wiki:Mistwood), traveling by horse through the [rainwood](wiki:Rainwood). They take shelter from the rain in a cave, and Arianne loses another game of cyvasse to Daemon. They are about to sleep when Arianne realizes that her cousin, [Elia Sand](wiki:Elia_Sand), has not returned from exploring the cave. Daemon accompanies Arianne while the company searches for Elia, and they discover carvings of the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) on the cave walls. The others find Elia, and after Arianne berates her, she asks Daemon why Doran inflicted the wild girl on her. Daemon jokingly suggests that perhaps it was revenge. From Mistwood, they are escorted by members of the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) to [Griffin's Roost](wiki:Griffin's_Roost), and Arianne overhears that the [sellswords](wiki:Sellsword) plan to take [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End). Both she and Daemon are perplexed why they would do so, but soon realize taking a great stronghold would make the realm consider Connington and his pretender more seriously. At Griffin's Roost, [Haldon Halfmaester](wiki:Haldon_Halfmaester) informs Arianne that they have taken Storm's End, and they will bring her there by ship the next morning. Daemon advises Arianne to refuse them, and send him to Storm's End in her stead, so that if Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) recaptures the castle, he will be considered just some knight, and there will be no proof that Dorne conspired with the pretender. However, Arianne reminds him that Doran entrusted her with the task of discovering the truth about Aegon, and she will go.(wiki:Daemon_Sand.cite_ref-twow-arianne2_11-0)
