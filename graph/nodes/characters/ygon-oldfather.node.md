## Appearances & Description

Ygon commands a following composed mostly of his own sons and grandsons. He has eighteen wives, half of whom were stolen on raids.(wiki:Ygon_Oldfather.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Ygon Oldfather is among the wildling leaders who accompany [Tormund Giantsbane](wiki:Tormund) to [the Wall](wiki:The_Wall) in declaring a truce with the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). He is present in the [Shieldhall](wiki:Shieldhall) when [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) announces the [letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) purportedly from [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Bolton) entitled 'Bastard'.(wiki:Ygon_Oldfather.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
