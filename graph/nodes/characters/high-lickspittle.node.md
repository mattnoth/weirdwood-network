## Origins

He replaced the elderly and amiable [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Pater)) after his death in [44 AC](wiki:44_AC). The Faith Militant dubbed him "the High Lickspittle," because he never spoke against King Maegor while hundreds of [Poor Fellows](wiki:Poor_Fellows) were hunted and murdered for the bounty Maegor put on their scalps. In defiance, a band of Poor Fellows crowned one of their leaders, [Septon](wiki:Septon) [Moon](wiki:Moon_(septon)), as their own High Septon.(wiki:High_Lickspittle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

In [47 AC](wiki:47_AC), the High Septon sailed from [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), to officiate at the [wedding](wiki:Wedding) between King Maegor and the three women known as the [Black Brides](wiki:Black_Brides).(wiki:High_Lickspittle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

After Maegor's death, the High Septon anointed King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) when he visited [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown).(wiki:High_Lickspittle.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

In [49 AC](wiki:49_AC), he personally performed the marriage rites between Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) and the [Queen Regent](wiki:Regent), [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) during the [Golden Wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding). A tale was spread that he wetted himself when the [dragons](wiki:Dragons) [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor) and [Silverwing](wiki:Silverwing) ascended during the marriage.(wiki:High_Lickspittle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

The High Septon died in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC) while climbing steps to his bedchamber. [Alfyn](wiki:Alfyn) was eventually chosen as his successor.(wiki:High_Lickspittle.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
