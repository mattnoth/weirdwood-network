## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser Guyard is named to the [Rainbow Guard](wiki:Rainbow_Guard), [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)'s equivalent of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). During the feast at [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge), Guyard the Green sings and plays the harp.(wiki:Guyard_Morrigen.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

After the parley between the brothers Renly and [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) during the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Guyard asks for command of the vanguard in the imminent battle between the two forces, but he is passed over in favor of Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell), the [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander) of the Rainbow Guard.(wiki:Guyard_Morrigen.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) When Renly is murdered within his tent before the battle, Guyard is not present.(wiki:Guyard_Morrigen.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

After Renly's death, Guyard goes over to Stannis. He is present beneath the walls of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) for the parley with Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose). The castellan offers to duel Guyard or Lord [Bryce Caron](wiki:Bryce_Caron) to determine the siege's fate, but Stannis refuses.(wiki:Guyard_Morrigen.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Guyard opposes [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) being Stannis's standard-bearer.(wiki:Guyard_Morrigen.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

When Stannis's army approaches [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Guyard is given command of the vanguard.(wiki:Guyard_Morrigen.cite_ref-Racok52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), he is slain by "Renly's ghost" in single combat.(wiki:Guyard_Morrigen.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Loras reveals that "Renly's ghost" was actually his brother, Ser [Garlan Tyrell](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell), in Renly's armor.(wiki:Guyard_Morrigen.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
