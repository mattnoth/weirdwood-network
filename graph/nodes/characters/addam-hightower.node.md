## Origins

In [10 AC](wiki:10_AC), during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), Lord [Fowler](wiki:House_Fowler) led an army into the [Reach](wiki:Reach). By the time the [marcher lords](wiki:Marcher_lords) realized the threat, a dozen villages had already been burned and [Nightsong](wiki:Nightsong) had been captured. When word reached [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), Addam was sent by his father with a strong force to retake the castle. When Addam arrived at Nightsong, Lord Fowler had already put the castle to the torch and the garrison to the sword, taking Lord [Caron](wiki:House_Caron), his wife, and their children back to Dorne as captives. Worse, while Addam had marched from Oldtown to Nightsong, a second Dornish force had come down to attack the city, burning fields, farms and villages nearby, killing Addam's younger brother [Garmon](wiki:Garmon_Hightower) in battle. Instead of pursuing the Dornish host that had taken the Carons, Addam returned to Oldtown to relieve the city, only to find the Dornish host gone upon their arrival.(wiki:Addam_Hightower.cite_ref-Rfab2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Soon after Lord [Manfred Hightower](wiki:Manfred_Hightower_(Aegon's_Conquest)) died, and Addam succeeded his father as the Lord of the High Tower.(wiki:Addam_Hightower.cite_ref-Rfab2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Addam was eventually succeeded by his son,(wiki:Addam_Hightower.cite_ref-son_3-1) [Manfred](wiki:Manfred_Hightower_(son_of_Addam)).
