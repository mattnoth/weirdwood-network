## Appearances & Description

Stranger is a heavy [courser](wiki:Horse), almost as large as a destrier but much faster.(wiki:Stranger_(horse).cite_ref-Rasos47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He is a handsome black stallion with a mean temper. He is gentle with his master, [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane), but aggressive toward others.(wiki:Stranger_(horse).cite_ref-Rasos47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) The horse was blasphemously [named after](wiki:Named_after) [the Stranger](wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven)), the death aspect of the [Seven-Faced God](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:Stranger_(horse).cite_ref-Rasos47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Stranger_(horse).cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Dismounting just before the [riot of King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing), [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) is left afoot when Stranger gallops away with the royal party. When [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sends Sandor and [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) to contain the fire in [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom), Sandor only goes so he can find his horse.(wiki:Stranger_(horse).cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Sandor rides Stranger up the plank and on to the deck of [Prayer](wiki:Prayer), hacking down anyone who blunders within reach.(wiki:Stranger_(horse).cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After deserting the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater) because of the [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire), Sandor escapes [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) on Stranger. After he is captured by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), Sandor wins his [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat) in the [hollow hill](wiki:Hollow_hill) and is freed.(wiki:Stranger_(horse).cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) orders [Anguy](wiki:Anguy) to kill Sandor's horse if he spots Sandor following them, but the archer and [Lem](wiki:Lem) would prefer to save the horse for their own use.(wiki:Stranger_(horse).cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
After capturing [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), Sandor rides with her on Stranger through the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). Arya rides double with Sandor, though he threatens that if she doesn't behave he'll truss her up and throw her over the back of the horse. Shortly after being kidnapped, thinking she can ride off before the Hound can catch her, Arya tries to steal Stranger when Sandor is relieving himself against a tree. Instead Stranger almost bites her face off.(wiki:Stranger_(horse).cite_ref-Rasos47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
Sandor attempts to ransom Arya to Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) by bringing her to the [Twins](wiki:Twins), where Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) is to marry [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey). They approach the Twins disguised as peasants delivering salt pork for the wedding feast, with Stranger tethered to the [wagon](wiki:Wagon). When Ser [Donnel Haigh](wiki:Donnel_Haigh) and two [squires](wiki:Squire) accost them to check their business at the Twins, Haigh inquires about Stranger, who is too fine a horse for [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk). Sandor claims that the horse is a wedding gift from Lady [Shella Whent](wiki:Shella_Whent) to Lord Tully, an excuse Haigh accepts. One of the squires almost winds up in the mud when Stranger bites at the squire's own mount.(wiki:Stranger_(horse).cite_ref-Rasos50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
When the battle at [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) begins, some [Frey](wiki:House_Frey) knights ride at the wagon. Clegane cuts Stranger loose with a single slash of his sword, and leaps onto his back to fight. As Arya runs towards the Twins' open gates, the Hound rides her down, catching her with the flat of an axe.(wiki:Stranger_(horse).cite_ref-Rasos52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
After they escape the Twins, Arya acquires a horse to ride, which she calls [Craven](wiki:Craven). Although the mare is a good enough horse, Arya resents her for running away from the fighting at the Twins, and thinks that Stranger would have remained to fight.(wiki:Stranger_(horse).cite_ref-Rasos65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) When Arya abandons Sandor as he lays dying by the banks of the [Trident](wiki:Trident), she saddles up Craven and rides away, but leaves Stranger with his master.(wiki:Stranger_(horse).cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

As [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) and her companions arrive on the [Quiet Isle](wiki:Quiet_Isle), they make their way to a whitewashed stable. At the far end of the stable, away from other animals, a huge black stallion trumpets at the sound of their voices and kicks at the door of his stall. Brother [Narbert](wiki:Narbert) reveals that the horse, now called Driftwood to avoid blasphemy, had broken Brother [Rawney](wiki:Rawney)'s shinbone, and bitten off Brother [Gillam](wiki:Gillam)'s ear when he tried to geld him. The [Elder Brother](wiki:Elder_Brother_(Quiet_Isle)) explains the horse was once Sandor Clegane's steed, Stranger.(wiki:Stranger_(horse).cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
