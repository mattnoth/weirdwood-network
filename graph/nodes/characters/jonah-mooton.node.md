## Origins

Jonah Mooton, [Red Roy Connington](wiki:Roy_Connington), and [Braxton Beesbury](wiki:Braxton_Beesbury) became the three male favorites of Princess [Saera Targaryen](wiki:Saera_Targaryen) in [82 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). Jonah, Roy, Braxton, Saera, [Alys Turnberry](wiki:Alys_Turnberry), and [Perianne Moore](wiki:Perianne_Moore) became inseparable at every feast and ball, hunted and [hawked](wiki:Hawk) together, and once sailed across [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay) to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Jonah_Mooton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

In [84 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Jonah, Roy, and Braxton were found drunk at the [Blue Pearl](wiki:Blue_Pearl), a [brothel](wiki:Brothel) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), laughing as the king's [fool](wiki:Fool), [Tom Turnip](wiki:Tom_Turnip), was surrounded by [whores](wiki:Prostitution). When asked by men of the [City Watch](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing), Roy admitted they thought it would be funny to see the fool do the deed, and Jonah laughed, saying it had been Saera's notion, praising her cleverness.(wiki:Jonah_Mooton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

When Perianne and Alys were questioned about the incident, it was revealed that Alys was pregnant outside of wedlock, and that Saera had coerced them into sexual acts with her male favorites. Jonah, Roy, and Braxton were then imprisoned in the [dungeons](wiki:Black_cells) for one night.(wiki:Jonah_Mooton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

When questioned by King [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), Saera defended herself by saying Jonah Mooton said he loved her. She confessed that she had [bedded](wiki:Bedded) all three of her male favorites, with each of them thinking he was the first to take her [maidenhead](wiki:Maidenhead). She proposed a solution of redemption by [marrying all three](wiki:Polygamy) of her favorites, like King [Maegor](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) and his six wives, at which the king grew furious.(wiki:Jonah_Mooton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

A hasty [marriage](wiki:Marriage) was arranged for Perianne to Jonah, with the king telling him he could play a part in her redemption as he had played a part in her ruin. The marriage was a success, and they later ruled together as Lord and Lady of Maidenpool. King Jaehaerys later asked Lord Jonah and Lady Perianne to keep watch for Saera after she fled from her punishment in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown).(wiki:Jonah_Mooton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
