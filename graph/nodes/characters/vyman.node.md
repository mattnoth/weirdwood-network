## Identity

Maester Vyman is the maester at Riverrun, serving House Tully and later House Frey of Riverrun (wiki:Vyman).

## Appearances & Description

Vyman is old, with lined and wrinkled hands (wiki:Vyman).

## Narrative Arc

Vyman prepares dreamwine and milk of the poppy for the ailing Lord Hoster Tully (wiki:Vyman, cite: AGOT Catelyn XI). He tends the wounds of Delp after Lannister agents try to free Jaime, and prepares the map of the riverlands for Robb Stark's kingdom (wiki:Vyman, cite: ACOK Catelyn V). He brings Catelyn the news that her father has died and later informs her that Edmure has been ordered to yield Riverrun (wiki:Vyman, cite: ASOS). When the Freys take control of Riverrun, Vyman remains as castle maester and serves under the new regime (wiki:Vyman, cite: AFFC).
