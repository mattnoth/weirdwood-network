## Appearances & Description

Vyman is old, with lined and wrinkled hands.(wiki:Vyman.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Maester Vyman prepares [dreamwine](wiki:Dreamwine) and [milk of the poppy](wiki:Milk_of_the_poppy) for the ailing [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), Lord of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Vyman.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Clash of Kings

Vyman prepares the map depicting the borders of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) claimed by [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) and [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Vyman.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He treats the wounds of [Delp](wiki:Delp) after [Lannister agents](wiki:House_Lannister_guards) try to free Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Vyman.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Hoster's daughter, Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), has Vyman send a [raven](wiki:Raven) to [Ashemark](wiki:Taking_of_Ashemark) after Riverrun learns of the [fall of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)) to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Vyman.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

When Vyman receives word by letter that [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) are dead at [Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell), he brings the letter to their mother, Catelyn.(wiki:Vyman.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Vyman informs Catelyn, who has been detained for having released Jaime, that Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) left the riverlands after the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords), and that her son Robb was wounded during the [storming of the Crag](wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag).(wiki:Vyman.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Despite Vyman's best efforts,(wiki:Vyman.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)(wiki:Vyman.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Lord Hoster passes away.(wiki:Vyman.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Jaime resolves the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun). After the castle passes from [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully) to [House Frey of Riverrun](wiki:House_Frey_of_Riverrun), Vyman remains in service to its new lord, [Emmon Frey](wiki:Emmon_Frey). When a message comes to Riverrun from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Vyman accidentally reads its contents, which happened to be Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) declaring her love for her brother, Jaime. He brings the message to Jaime and tells him that the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) has declared that winter has come.(wiki:Vyman.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
