## Origins

Harmund III was raised in the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) by his parents, King [Harmund the Haggler](wiki:Harmund_II_Hoare) and Queen [Lelia Lannister](wiki:Lelia_Lannister). He outraged the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) and the [priests of the Drowned God](wiki:Priests_of_the_Drowned_God) by outlawing reaving and the taking of [salt wives](wiki:Salt_wives). He was also opposed to the practice of [thralldom](wiki:Thrall). A priest known as the [Shrike](wiki:Shrike) led a rapid rebellion, and Harmund was cast down and mutilated, losing his eyes, [tongue](wiki:Tongueless), and nose.(wiki:Harmund_III_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Harmund's brother, [Hagon Hoare](wiki:Hagon_Hoare), took the throne and restored traditional ironborn practices. Harmund was confined to the dungeons of [Hoare Castle](wiki:Hoare_Castle) and their mother, Lelia, was mutilated and returned to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), which led to war with [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister). When the [westermen](wiki:Westermen) conquered [Great Wyk](wiki:Great_Wyk) in the seventh year of fighting, Ser [Aubrey Crakehall](wiki:Aubrey_Crakehall) considered restoring Harmund to the throne but instead decided to give the broken man the [gift of mercy](wiki:Gift_of_mercy).(wiki:Harmund_III_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
