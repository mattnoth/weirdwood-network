## Identity

Davos Baratheon was a son of Lord Orys Baratheon and Lady Argella Durrandon, and possibly the second Lord of Storm's End of House Baratheon (wiki:Davos_Baratheon).

## Origins

Son of Orys Baratheon, founder of House Baratheon, and Argella Durrandon, last of the Durrandon line (track_b: Father, Mother).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Baratheon (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Stormlands (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Davos fought beside his father Orys during the Vulture Hunt in 37 AC. At the battle of Stonehelm, Orys's forces crushed part of the Vulture King's host. Davos was present when Orys died on the march home to Storm's End from wounds taken in battle. According to Davos's own accounts, his father died content, having avenged the loss of his hand by cutting off the hands and feet of Lord Walter Wyl. A grateful King Aenys I rewarded Davos with gold, offices, and honors (wiki:Davos_Baratheon).
