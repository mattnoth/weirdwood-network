## Identity

Septa Rhaena, born Rhaena Targaryen, was the second daughter of King Aegon III Targaryen and his second wife Queen Daenaera Velaryon (wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon_III)).

## Origins

Born 147 AC (track_b: Born). Valyrian race, Crownlands culture (track_b: Race, Culture).

## Appearances & Description

At fourteen, just as lovely as her sister Daena but with a softer, sweeter, more feminine beauty. Her hair was more gold than silver, carefully coiffed. Soft, kind eyes and a shy, sweet smile. She loved to dress in white and gold, fond of lace trim. She loved to sew and embroider religious scenes on her clothing (wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon_III)).

## Narrative Arc

Rhaena had two older brothers, Daeron and Baelor, and two sisters, the older Daena and younger Elaena. During the reign of their brother Baelor the Blessed, Rhaena and her sisters were confined in the Maidenvault within the Red Keep — ostensibly to preserve their innocence, though many suspected Baelor feared being tempted by their beauty. Unlike her sisters, Rhaena never chafed at her confinement. She was almost as pious as Baelor and eventually became a septa. After Baelor's death, the claims of Rhaena and her sisters were set aside in favor of their uncle Viserys II (wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon_III)).

Died in or after 171 AC (track_b: Died).
