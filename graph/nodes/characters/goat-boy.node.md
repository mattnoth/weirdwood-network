## Edges

## Appearances & Description

The goat boy has twisted (deformed) hairy "[goat](wiki:Goat)-legs." He is young and speaks the guttural sailor's pidgin called the [trade talk](wiki:Trade_talk). Where Yezzan acquired him is not known.(wiki:Goat_boy.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Along with the rest of Yezzan's special treasures, the goat boy accompanies his master to the [Yunkish](wiki:Yunkish) encampment outside of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Goat_boy.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

He performs during Yezzan's banquet for [Yurkhaz zo Yunzak](wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak). He comes out and dances a grotesque jig, whilst one of Yurkhaz's [slaves](wiki:Slave) plays on a [bone flute](wiki:Bone_flute).(wiki:Goat_boy.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

When Yezzan contracts the [pale mare](wiki:Pale_mare), it falls to Yezzan's special treasures to attend to him as he is dying. The goat-legged boy hopes the [silver queen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) will save them. [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and [Penny](wiki:Penny) escape before they can learn of the fate of Yezzan's grotesquerie. The fate of the goat boy is unknown.(wiki:Goat_boy.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
