## Origins

When [Lord Corbray](wiki:Lord_Corbray_(father_of_Lyonel)) was wounded during the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident), Lyonel carried his father back to the [maesters](wiki:Maesters) in the rear, while his younger brother, Ser [Lyn Corbray](wiki:Lyn_Corbray), grabbed their father's sword, [Lady Forlorn](wiki:Lady_Forlorn), and led a charge against the [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen). When Lord Corbray died, Lyonel inherited the titles and lands while Lyn received Lady Forlorn. Lyonel felt he was cheated of his birthright.(wiki:Lyonel_Corbray.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

Lyonel's wife bore a sickly child who died in infancy, so Lyonel's heir is his brother, Lyn.(wiki:Lyonel_Corbray.cite_ref-TWOW_4-2)

## Appearances & Description

Lyonel is over forty years old.(wiki:Lyonel_Corbray.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Ser [Lyn Corbray](wiki:Lyn_Corbray) dislikes his elder brother.(wiki:Lyonel_Corbray.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Having [married Lysa Arryn](wiki:Wedding_of_Petyr_Baelish_and_Lysa_Arryn) and been named [Lord Protector of the Vale](wiki:Lord_Protector_of_the_Vale), [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) travels to meet with the [Corbrays](wiki:House_Corbray).(wiki:Lyonel_Corbray.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Lord Lyonel does not join with the [Lords Declarant](wiki:Lords_Declarant) who try to depose Petyr as Lord Protector.(wiki:Lyonel_Corbray.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Lyonel marries the sixteen-year-old [daughter](wiki:Lady_Corbray) of a rich [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown) merchant, a marriage brokered by Petyr after the death of Lyonel's first wife.(wiki:Lyonel_Corbray.cite_ref-TWOW_4-3) Lyonel receives a significant [dowry](wiki:Dowry) for marrying a [commoner](wiki:Smallfolk). It is [a well-attended wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Lyonel_Corbray_and_Lady_Corbray), with Lord [Benedar Belmore](wiki:Benedar_Belmore), Lord [Gerold Grafton](wiki:Gerold_Grafton), Lord [Jon Lynderly](wiki:Jon_Lynderly), Ser [Symond Templeton](wiki:Symond_Templeton), Lord [Waxley](wiki:House_Waxley), and Lady [Anya Waynwood](wiki:Anya_Waynwood) present.(wiki:Lyonel_Corbray.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Petyr sends word that Lyonel is pleased with his new wife and her dowry.(wiki:Lyonel_Corbray.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

### The Winds of Winter

Lord Lyonel does not attend the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) for the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights) at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon), remaining at [Heart's Home](wiki:Heart's_Home) with his new wife, who is now pregnant with his long-desired heir.(wiki:Lyonel_Corbray.cite_ref-TWOW_4-4)
