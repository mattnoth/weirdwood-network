## Origins

Willam participated in the [War for the White Cloaks](wiki:War_for_the_White_Cloaks) in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC) during the [wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) of [Queen Regent](wiki:Regent) [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) and Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon), hoping to win one of the vacant places in the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). Willam quickly became a favorite of the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk), who started calling him "the Keg o' Ale" and would sing the *"Hail, Hail Keg o' Ale"* whenever he took the field. In the end did not win a white cloak.(wiki:Willam_Stafford.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

In [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Willam participated in the [tourney](wiki:Tourney_for_the_completion_of_the_Dragonpit) held to celebrate the completion of the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit), to the joy of the smallfolk. When he fell, he was replaced as a favorite of the commoners by Ser [Harys Hogg](wiki:Harys_Hogg).(wiki:Willam_Stafford.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Appearances & Description

Willam Stafford was a short and stout man, with a large belly and a fondness for [drinking](wiki:Alcoholic_beverages). He always appeared intoxicated, to the point where it was considered a wonder that he was able to stand and fight.(wiki:Willam_Stafford.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

By [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Willam was considered old.(wiki:Willam_Stafford.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
