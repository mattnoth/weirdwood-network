## Origins

The grandson of King [Artys I](wiki:Artys_I_Arryn), Roland fostered with an [Andal](wiki:Andal) king in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) as a boy. He traveled far and wide after becoming a [knight](wiki:Knight), including visits to the [Hightower](wiki:Hightower), [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), and the castles of the [Trident](wiki:Trident).(wiki:Roland_I_Arryn.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale:_the_eyrie.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Returning to the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) to don the Falcon Crown, Roland felt that the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon), the seat of his house, was ugly compared to the grander castles of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men). He initially wanted to tear down the Gates of the Moon and rebuild it, but was reminded of the site's vulnerability after thousands of [wildlings](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) descended from the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon) in search of food during winter.(wiki:Roland_I_Arryn.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale:_the_eyrie.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Legends say Roland's future wife, [Teora Hunter](wiki:Teora_Hunter), reminded him how his grandsire Artys had defeated [Robar II Royce](wiki:Robar_II_Royce), by attacking from high ground during the [Battle of Seven Stars](wiki:Battle_of_Seven_Stars). Roland began the slow development of the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) and its approaches atop the highest ground possible, the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance). The king preferred [marble](wiki:Marble) from [Tarth](wiki:Tarth) instead of the Vale.(wiki:Roland_I_Arryn.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale:_the_eyrie.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

After a twenty-six year reign, Roland was killed in a surprise attack by [Painted Dogs](wiki:Painted_Dogs), who pulled him from his horse and crushed his head with a stone maul before he could draw his longsword. Roland I had lived long enough to see the first stones laid for the castle he envisioned, and he was succeeded by his son.(wiki:Roland_I_Arryn.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale:_the_eyrie.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
