## Origins

Fighting on the side of the [blacks](wiki:Blacks), Robb brought three hundred archers to the [Battle by the Lakeshore](wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). Caught by armies from the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) and the [north](wiki:North), Lord [Humfrey Lefford](wiki:Humfrey_Lefford) sent [ravens](wiki:Raven) to Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) for assistance. However, Robb used his archery to prevent any of the ravens from reaching Aemond.(wiki:Robb_Rivers.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Robb and two of his archers later slew Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) during the [Butcher's Ball](wiki:Butcher's_Ball).(wiki:Robb_Rivers.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Robb's bowmen covered Ser [Garibald Grey](wiki:Garibald_Grey)'s men as they retreated during the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton).(wiki:Robb_Rivers.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) It is probable Robb did not survive the battle, which was a victory for the [greens](wiki:Greens).(wiki:Robb_Rivers.cite_ref-3)
