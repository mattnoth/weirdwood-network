## Origins

After twenty years of warfare against petty kings of [Andalos](wiki:Andalos), Qarlon's domain extended along the [Shivering Sea](wiki:Shivering_Sea) from the future site of the [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) lagoon in the west and the [Axe](wiki:Axe) in the east. To the south Qarlon controlled the headwaters of the [Upper Rhoyne](wiki:Upper_Rhoyne) and the [Noyne](wiki:Noyne), but this led to conflict with other Andal kings and the [Free City](wiki:Free_Cities) of [Norvos](wiki:Norvos). When the Norvoshi closed the Noyne, Qarlon defeated them twice in the [Hills of Norvos](wiki:Hills_of_Norvos). Unwisely, however, he then marched against the city of Norvos.(wiki:Qarlon_the_Great.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_lorath.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

The Norvoshi sent to distant [Valyria](wiki:Valyria) for help, and the [Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) rose in defense of its colony. According to *[The Fires of the Freehold](wiki:The_Fires_of_the_Freehold)*, a hundred [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlord) burned Qarlon the Great and his Andal host outside Norvos. The dragonlords then attacked the Lorathi isles, burning Qarlon's keep. It is said nobody survived the [Scouring of Lorath](wiki:Scouring_of_Lorath).(wiki:Qarlon_the_Great.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_lorath.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
