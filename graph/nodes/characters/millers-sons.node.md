## Appearances & Description

The two boys are of an age with [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark), and are reportedly similar in size and coloring,(wiki:Miller's_sons.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) which may mean they have auburn hair.

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

After Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) captures "[Reek](wiki:Ramsay_Snow)",(wiki:Miller's_sons.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) his party stops at the mill for a while before continuing to Winterfell. The [miller's wife](wiki:Miller's_wife) sells them hay for their [horses](wiki:Horse), and Ser Rodrik speaks with the miller's sons.(wiki:Miller's_sons.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
[Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed) has a [green dream](wiki:Green_dream) of Reek skinning off the faces of [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark), who laid dead at his feet. After Reek is brought to Winterfell and imprisoned in the dungeons, Jojen tells Bran about that green dream and others.(wiki:Miller's_sons.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
When [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) leads [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) raiders to [capture Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell), Bran and Rickon become his prisoners and [hostages](wiki:Hostage). '"Reek", freed from Winterfell's dungeons, swears himself to Theon.(wiki:Miller's_sons.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Bran and Rickon apparently escape Winterfell with the help of [Osha](wiki:Osha), [Hodor](wiki:Hodor), and [Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed) and Jojen Reed. Theon gathers a search party, including Reek, Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin), and the [kennelmaster](wiki:Kennelmaster) [Farlen](wiki:Farlen) and his [dogs](wiki:Dog), but fails to track them down in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood). However, Reek tells Theon he has a feeling that the Stark boys are hiding at the mill, and secretly presents Theon with the boys' fine clothing he had brought along in a bag. Theon sends the rest of the search party back to Winterfell, except for Reek and three ironborn, [Aggar](wiki:Aggar), [Gelmarr](wiki:Gelmarr), and [Gynir](wiki:Gynir).(wiki:Miller's_sons.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
At the mill, Reek kills the miller's two sons, and when their [mother](wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water) begs Theon for mercy, Gelmarr cuts her down with one blow of his axe. The dead children are decapitated and their heads [skinned](wiki:Flaying) and dipped in tar by Reek, and Theon dresses their bodies in the Stark children's finery. Theon returns to Winterfell and presents the heads and headless bodies as Bran and Rickon. The people of Winterfell are shocked and horrified to see this, and Farlen attempts to attack Theon until he is beaten senseless. Maester Luwin begs to sew their heads back on and bury the bodies in the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell), but Theon forbids it. Instead, he mounts their heads on the castle walls, and burns the bodies and clothing to bones and ashes.(wiki:Miller's_sons.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
At [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) receives word that Theon Greyjoy captured and killed her sons at the mill on the Acorn Water, and mounted their heads on the walls of Winterfell.(wiki:Miller's_sons.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
When the three ironborn who had gone to the mill with Theon and Reek die mysteriously, Theon blames Farlen and executes him. However, it was actually "Reek" who, with Theon's approval, killed Aggar, Gelmarr, and Gynir, to secure the secret of the "Stark boys'" deaths. Afterwards, Theon has a dream of dressing the dead bodies at the mill. Theon also has a nightmare that he is being chased by [giant wolves](wiki:Direwolf) with the heads of small children, while he pleads to himself that they are dead. Theon's sister [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) visits Winterfell and tells him he was a fool for killing Bran and Rickon and mounting their heads on [the gate](wiki:Main_gates_of_Winterfell), as he has a made an enemy of every man for a thousand leagues, and only gives him ten men as reinforcements. "Reek" promises Theon he can get him two hundred men from the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort), and Theon allows him to leave. The next morning, Theon walks along the outer walls of Winterfell and gazes at the miller's sons' heads, thinking of how easily people were fooled.(wiki:Miller's_sons.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Ser Rodrik Cassel and his army return and confront Theon outside Winterfell. Theon uses Rodrik's daughter [Beth Cassel](wiki:Beth_Cassel) as a [hostage](wiki:Hostage), displaying her to her father standing atop the walls of the castle, between the heads of the two boys, with a noose around her neck. Rodrik is anguished, but still determined to attack. However, Reek returns with [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton)'s [garrison](wiki:House_Bolton_guards), and [betrays and destroys the army attempting to retake Winterfell](wiki:Battle_outside_the_gates_of_Winterfell), then reveals himself to Theon as actually [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son of Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton). Ramsay then betrays Theon as well, and begins the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Miller's_sons.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Afterwards, Bran Stark and his companions emerge from the crypts where they had been hiding, to find Winterfell in ruins and filled with dead residents and dead ironmen. They find a mortally-wounded Maester Luwin, who tells them that he knew the bodies could not have been theirs, as the older boy's leg muscles were not withered like Bran's paralyzed ones.(wiki:Miller's_sons.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Theon, who has been [tortured](wiki:Torture) at the Dreadfort by Ramsay for months, finds it difficult to recall the truth of what happened to Bran and Rickon, remembering that they dipped their heads in tar.(wiki:Miller's_sons.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Having returned to Winterfell for [Ramsay Bolton's wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark), a regretful Theon feels that the castle is full of [ghosts](wiki:Ghosts) for him.(wiki:Miller's_sons.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Miller's_sons.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Miller's_sons.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) He thinks of the old ghosts from the [crypts](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell) and the younger ones that he had made himself, including the miller's wife from Acorn Water and her two young sons.(wiki:Miller's_sons.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)
In the [godswood of Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell), Theon sees Bran's face in the [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) and momentarily thinks he is being haunted by his ghost, before recalling that they had not actually killed Bran and Rickon, but instead only miller's sons. He speaks aloud that he had to have two heads or he would have been mocked, and is overheard by [Holly](wiki:Holly) and [Rowan](wiki:Rowan).(wiki:Miller's_sons.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2) Rowan accuses Theon of being a [kinslayer](wiki:Kinslayer), and Theon denies any relationship to Bran and Rickon while thinking to himself that the two children he killed were just some miller's sons.(wiki:Miller's_sons.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
