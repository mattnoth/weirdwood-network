## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord Halys goes to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) when [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls the banners. He gives gifts to Robb in hopes that he will give him leave to dam the [White Knife](wiki:White_Knife), hunting privileges north of a ridge, and a holdfast taken from his grandfather.(wiki:Halys_Hornwood.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Lord Hornwood is sent with Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) when the northern army splits at the [Twins](wiki:Twins), and he is killed during the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:Halys_Hornwood.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Halys's son and heir, [Daryn](wiki:Daryn_Hornwood), is slain at the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood) around the same time as Halys.(wiki:Halys_Hornwood.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Lady [Donella Hornwood](wiki:Donella_Hornwood) rules [House Hornwood](wiki:House_Hornwood) after the deaths of her husband and son.(wiki:Halys_Hornwood.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
