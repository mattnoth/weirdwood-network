## Origins

While the [Reach](wiki:Reach) enjoyed an unprecedented prosperity, called the [Golden Reign](wiki:Golden_Reign), under King [Garth VII Gardener](wiki:Garth_VII_Gardener),(wiki:Gyles_III_Gardener.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_the_gardener_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) it was during the reign of Gyles III that [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) reached the apex of its power.(wiki:Gyles_III_Gardener.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Leading a glittering host of armored [knights](wiki:Knight), Gyles went to war against the aged [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) in the east, defeated the armies of the [stormlanders](wiki:Stormlanders), and conquered all the lands north of the [rainwood](wiki:Rainwood) except for [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), which he besieged for two years.(wiki:Gyles_III_Gardener.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) While the King of the Reach was away with his army in the east, the Reach was attacked by the [King of the Rock](wiki:King_of_the_Rock), [Lancel IV Lannister](wiki:Lancel_IV_Lannister), forcing Gyles to lift his siege of Storm's End and hurry home to deal with the [westermen](wiki:Westermen).(wiki:Gyles_III_Gardener.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
[House Osgrey](wiki:House_Osgrey) were the [Marshalls of the Northmarch](wiki:Marshalls_of_the_Northmarch) at the time. Since his elder brothers were all with Gyles, Ser [Wilbert Osgrey](wiki:Wilbert_Osgrey) called up the reserve levies and marched to stop King Lancel. During the battle between the two forces, Wilbert met the King of the Rock and slew him at the cost of his own life, stopping the attack in its tracks. The westermen retreated and the Reach was saved.(wiki:Gyles_III_Gardener.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel) writes that had Lancel not invaded the Reach, Gyles might have been able to complete his conquest in the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands).(wiki:Gyles_III_Gardener.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
A broader war followed involving three [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) kings and two from the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). The fighting eventually ended with Gyles dead of a [bloody flux](wiki:Bloody_flux) and the borders between the realms restored to more or less what they had been before hostilities began.(wiki:Gyles_III_Gardener.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
