## Identity

Alfyn was a septon of the Faith of the Seven who became High Septon during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen (wiki:Alfyn).

## Appearances & Description

Alfyn was a fierce man and by far the oldest of the Seven Speakers. He was legless and travelled in a litter (wiki:Alfyn).
