## Origins

After King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) wed his sister, [Alysanne](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), Jaehaerys sent [seven preachers](wiki:Seven_Speakers) to walk the [realm](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) and win the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) over to the [marriage](wiki:Marriage), not with swords, but with words. Septon Alfyn was among these seven. They traveled the realm alone, walking from village to village, telling the smallfolk of Jaehaerys's wisdom and Alysanne's kindness. When challenged on the issue of [incest](wiki:Incest), the seven would say the [Valyrians](wiki:Valyria) mounted [dragons](wiki:Dragons) and thus were not like other men, and as such, an exception had to be made.(wiki:Alfyn.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

When the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(High_Lickspittle)) died in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), the king and queen hoped to prevent the election of a High Septon that would denounce their marriage as incestuous and begin an uprising. Septon [Mattheus](wiki:Mattheus) of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) was a likely candidate, and fiercely opposed to the [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) practice of incest. King Jaehaerys and Queen Alysanne flew to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), and according to Septon [Barth](wiki:Barth)'s account there struck a deal with Lord [Donnel Hightower](wiki:Donnel_Hightower) to support Alfyn, a fierce champion of the [Doctrine of Exceptionalism](wiki:Doctrine_of_Exceptionalism), as the new High Septon. King Jaehaerys promised Lord Donnel that, as Alfyn's old age would likely mean he would soon die, Alfyn's successor would be a [Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) on the condition that the Hightowers would support the king's doctrine.(wiki:Alfyn.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

At [Ashford](wiki:Ashford), Alfyn was astonished to learn he had been elected High Septon. It took him a fortnight to travel to Oldtown and take up the crystal crown. Alfyn and [his successor](wiki:High_Septon_(brother_of_Donnel_Hightower)), a brother of Lord Donnel Hightower, made the Doctrine of Exceptionalism an official tenet of the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:Alfyn.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

In [59 AC](wiki:59_AC), the ruling [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon) died of the [Shivers](wiki:Shivers). It is currently unknown if this High Septon is Alfyn or one of his successors.(wiki:Alfyn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Appearances & Description

Alfyn was a fierce man and by far the oldest of the [Seven Speakers](wiki:Seven_Speakers). He was legless and travelled in a [litter](wiki:Litter).(wiki:Alfyn.cite_ref-Rtrotd62.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
