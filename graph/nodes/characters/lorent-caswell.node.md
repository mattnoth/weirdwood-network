## Origins

Lorent became a page, a squire, and finally a [knight](wiki:Knight) in service to his father, [Lord Caswell](wiki:Lord_Caswell_(father_of_Lorent)). Lorent had four sisters, but no brothers, so the other squires of Bitterbridge would barely lay a finger on him in the yard. One day, Lorent claimed a longsword that was a gift for the castle [armorer](wiki:Armorer)'s son, [Rolly](wiki:Rolly_Duckfield), who was a member of the Bitterbridge garrison, and told Rolly he was fit to hold a hammer, not a sword. Rolly then beat Lorent with a hammer until both arms and half his ribs were broken.(wiki:Lorent_Caswell.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Appearances & Description

Lorent is a wispy, young man who has two daughters.(wiki:Lorent_Caswell.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) [Rolly](wiki:Rolly_Duckfield) considers Lorent to be a weakling and a weedy, pinch-faced sneak.(wiki:Lorent_Caswell.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Lord Caswell supports King [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), and welcomes his army at [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge), where [a great melee](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge) is held. This is where Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) meets with Renly, speaking to him during a feast held by Lorent. News arrives of the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)) by King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Lorent_Caswell.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

After Renly's [assassination at Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) goes to Bitterbridge to take back the supplies that had been stored there. Lorent locks himself inside his castle, refusing to implicate himself any more.(wiki:Lorent_Caswell.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Feast for Crows

While searching for [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) encounters two guards at the gates of [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) wearing the livery of House Caswell. This indicates Lorent followed his liegelords of [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) in declaring for King [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey).(wiki:Lorent_Caswell.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
