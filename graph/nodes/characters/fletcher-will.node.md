## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Fletcher Will is slain by the forces of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). When [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) was captured by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) and put on trial for crimes committed by his fellow [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) forces, Fletcher Will was among the long list of Lannister victims named by [Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams) whose murders Clegane was to stand trial for.(wiki:Fletcher_Will.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
