## Origins

### Maegor the Cruel

Lord Prentys became one of the first great [lords](wiki:Lord) to abandon King Maegor I in [48 AC](wiki:48_AC), rallying to the banners of the young Prince [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Prentys_Tully.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) As Maegor's support began evaporating, word soon arrived at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) that Prentys had opened the gates of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) to Ser [Joffrey Doggett](wiki:Joffrey_Doggett), the fugitive [Grand Captain](wiki:Grand_Captain) of the outlawed [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons).(wiki:Prentys_Tully.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) With the support of Lord Tully's wife, Lady [Lucinda](wiki:Lucinda_Broome), Ser Joffrey moved freely between the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) and [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands).(wiki:Prentys_Tully.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### Master of laws

After the death of Maegor the Cruel, Lord Prentys and Lady Lucinda attended the coronation of King Jaehaerys I in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), accompanied by Ser Joffrey.(wiki:Prentys_Tully.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Prentys was summoned to court to serve as [lord justiciar](wiki:Lord_justiciar) and [master of laws](wiki:Master_of_laws) during the minority of the young king.(wiki:Prentys_Tully.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Prentys was undistinguished in this office.(wiki:Prentys_Tully.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Hoping to please the [Faith](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), Prentys suggested that the king wed the pious [Ella Broome](wiki:Ella_Broome), the younger sister of his wife, but Jaehaerys instead married his own sister, Princess [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), in secret at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Prentys_Tully.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Outraged by the marriage, Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) sought to confront the king at Dragonstone, though Lord Prentys warned him that the king may simply close the gates of the citadel against them. Instead, Queen Dowager [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) dispatched Prentys's wife to Dragonstone to take charge of Alysanne's household. When Lord Baratheon spoke of putting the king aside in his absence, Prentys accused him of claiming the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) for himself, which Rogar denied vehemently for he desired to place the tractable and young Princess [Aerea](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen) on the throne. However, the [small council](wiki:Small_council) refused to support him and Queen Alyssa demanded his resignation as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:Prentys_Tully.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) King Jaehaerys I returned to King's Landing shortly after, much to Lord Tully's relief.(wiki:Prentys_Tully.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

When the king reached his majority and began to rule in his own right in [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), Jaehaerys thanked Prentys for his service and replaced him with Lord [Albin Massey](wiki:Albin_Massey) of [Stonedance](wiki:Stonedance).(wiki:Prentys_Tully.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

### Later life

On account of his undistinguished term sitting on the small council, Prentys was passed over during the consideration of a new Hand of the King, after the resignation of Lord [Daemon Velaryon](wiki:Daemon_Velaryon_(son_of_Aethan)) in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC). However, Lord Tully had a favorable opinion of his bannerman, Lord [Myles Smallwood](wiki:Myles_Smallwood) of [Acorn Hall](wiki:Acorn_Hall), as the new Hand of the King.(wiki:Prentys_Tully.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

King Jaehaerys visited Lord Tully at Riverrun during a [royal progress](wiki:Royal_progress) across the riverlands.(wiki:Prentys_Tully.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) In late [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Prentys attended the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) at King's Landing commemorating the tenth anniversary of the king's coronation.(wiki:Prentys_Tully.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Prentys and Lucinda died at Riverrun from the [Shivers](wiki:Shivers) a day apart from each other in the winter of [59 AC](wiki:59_AC).(wiki:Prentys_Tully.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
