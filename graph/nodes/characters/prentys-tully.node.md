## Identity

Prentys Tully was the Lord of Riverrun and head of House Tully during the later reign of King Maegor I Targaryen and the early reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen. He was married to the pious Lady Lucinda of House Broome (wiki:Prentys_Tully).

## Narrative Arc

Lord Prentys was one of the first great lords to abandon King Maegor I in 48 AC, rallying to the banners of Prince Jaehaerys. He opened the gates of Riverrun to Ser Joffrey Doggett, the fugitive Grand Captain of the Warrior's Sons (wiki:Prentys_Tully). Prentys served on the small council as master of laws, though Archmaester Gyldayn described his term as undistinguished. He died at Riverrun in 59 AC (track_b: Died).
