## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Drennan is one of the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) raiders who accompany [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) to raid along the [Stony Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore). During the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell), he and another ironman rape [Palla](wiki:Palla). Theon has Drennan whipped as punishment.(wiki:Drennan.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

While on guard duty with [Squint](wiki:Squint) at the [Hunter's Gate](wiki:Hunter's_Gate), Drennan is seduced by [Osha](wiki:Osha), who cuts his throat from ear to ear in the gatehouse. She then escapes [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) with [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark), [Jojen](wiki:Jojen_Reed) and [Meera Reed](wiki:Meera_Reed), and [Hodor](wiki:Hodor). Afterwards, Theon finds Drennan's body in the gatehouse. Since Drennan is half-naked with his breeches around his feet and there is cheese and [wine](wiki:Wine) on the table with two cups, Theon deduces what happened.(wiki:Drennan.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
