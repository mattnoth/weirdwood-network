## Identity

Myles (squire) — sworn to House Tully. One of 2 characters named Myles. Squire to Ser Desmond Grell, the master-at-arms of Riverrun, he was slain by Jaime Lannister during a rescue attempt in 299 AC. (wiki:Myles_(squire))

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Four men sent as part of Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey)'s honor guard of [red cloaks](wiki:Red_cloaks) from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) attempt to [free](wiki:Plot_to_free_Jaime_Lannister) Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) from his cell at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun). They are partially successful, managing to get him out of his cell. Once out, Jaime slays [Poul Pemford](wiki:Poul_Pemford) and Myles before being subdued.(wiki:Myles_(squire).cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
