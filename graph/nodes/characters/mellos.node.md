## Appearances & Description

Mellos was called a moderating voice while on the [small council](wiki:Small_council), always urging calm and compromise whenever issues arose between the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) and the [greens](wiki:Greens). King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) regarded Mellos as a trusted friend.(wiki:Mellos.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Origins

Maester Mellos replaced [Runciter](wiki:Runciter) as [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) in [112 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). He encouraged the match between [Laenor Velaryon](wiki:Laenor_Velaryon) and Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), despite the fact that Laenor was known not to be interested in women. He also counselled forgiveness for Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen), the controversial brother of King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Mellos.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
In [120 AC](wiki:120_AC), Mellos in his writings is the one that suggested that the fire at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) that killed Lord [Lyonel Strong](wiki:Lyonel_Strong) and his heir, Ser [Harwin Strong](wiki:Harwin_Strong), was ordered by Viserys. Mellos implies that the king had come to accept the rumors that his grandchildren by his daughter, Rhaenyra, were really bastards sired by Harwin, thus he desired to keep the truth concealed and kill the man who had dishonored his daughter.(wiki:Mellos.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
When King Viserys fell and sliced his hand open on the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), Mellos was unsuccessful in treating the laceration. Princess Rhaenyra flew her own maester, [Gerardys](wiki:Gerardys), from [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) to treat her father. Gerardys swiftly amputated two of the king's fingers, saving his life.(wiki:Mellos.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
Mellos passed away one night in [127 AC](wiki:127_AC), collapsing while climbing the serpentine steps. Rhaenyra recommended that Gerardys take his place as Grand Maester. Queen [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower), on the other hand, wanted Maester [Alfador](wiki:Alfador), who served [House Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower), named to the office. Beset from both sides, Viserys chose neither, reminding both his wife and daughter that the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) would decide who filled the office. Archmaester [Orwyle](wiki:Orwyle), in the end, was selected by the [Conclave](wiki:Conclave) as the new Grand Maester.(wiki:Mellos.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
Mellos's written accounts were used frequently by Archmaester [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn) in his own writings. Gyldayn considered Mellos's frequent leechings to be less effective than Maester Gerardys's potions and tinctures.(wiki:Mellos.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
