## Identity

Henly is a young maester present at Winterfell during the Bolton occupation. He enters with Maesters Medrick and Rhodry during the forced wedding feast of "Arya Stark" to Ramsay Bolton. Theon Greyjoy notes that Henly had previously served Lord Slate before the war. He is described as young.
