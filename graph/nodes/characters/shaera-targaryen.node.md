## Identity

Queen Shaera Targaryen was the eldest daughter and thirdborn child of King Aegon V Targaryen and Queen Betha Blackwood. She became queen consort to her brother King Jaehaerys II Targaryen (wiki:Shaera_Targaryen).

## Narrative Arc

Aegon V tried to end the Targaryen tradition of incestuous marriages and betrothed Shaera to Luthor Tyrell and Jaehaerys to Celia Tully in 237 AC. However, Jaehaerys and Shaera were in love. After their eldest brother Duncan broke his betrothal by marrying Jenny of Oldstones in 239 AC, Jaehaerys noted the court's eventual acceptance. In 240 AC, when Shaera was fourteen and Jaehaerys fifteen, they eluded their guards and secretly married before a heart tree. The marriage was consummated, and the betrothals to Tyrell and Tully were broken — causing lasting political damage. Their children were Aerys II and Rhaella Targaryen (wiki:Shaera_Targaryen).

Died in or after 259 AC (track_b: Died).
