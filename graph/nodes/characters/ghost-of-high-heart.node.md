## Appearances & Description

The ghost of High Heart is no more than three feet tall.(wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) She has white hair, so long that it almost touches the ground. Her flesh is very pale, and she has red eyes.(wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) She walks with a gnarled black cane.(wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Origins

When [Jenny of Oldstones](wiki:Jenny_of_Oldstones) came to court after marrying Prince [Duncan Targaryen](wiki:Duncan_Targaryen), she was accompanied by her friend, a [dwarf](wiki:Dwarf) albino woman who was reputed to be a [woods witch](wiki:Woods_witch) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). Lady Jenny claimed that she was one of the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest), although this was not true. After hearing the woods witch's [prophecy](wiki:Prophecy) that the [prince that was promised](wiki:Prince_that_was_promised) would come from the line of [Aerys](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) and [Rhaella Targaryen](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen), their father Prince [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_II_Targaryen) insisted that they marry.(wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The woods witch was present at the [tragic destruction](wiki:Tragedy_of_Summerhall) of [Summerhall](wiki:Summerhall) in [259 AC](wiki:259_AC), and was believed to have died there.(wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) However, she somehow survived and returned to the riverlands,(wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) where she mourns for her friend Jenny.(wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) and the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) camp overnight at [High Heart](wiki:High_Heart), to meet with the ghost of High Heart to hear her tell the future, and to learn the whereabouts of Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion).(wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) She relates her prophetic [dreams](wiki:Dreams) about the deaths of Lord [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)(wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and King [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy),(wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) in wording that suggests that Balon is killed by a [Faceless Man](wiki:Faceless_Men) contracted by someone among the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), likely [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy). She also talks of the [death](wiki:Red_Wedding)(wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart.cite_ref-Rasos65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) and resurrection(wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) of [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark).(wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)
Later, the [outlaws](wiki:Outlaw) return to High Heart, and the dwarf woman tells them of new dreams she has had. She relates that Balon Greyjoy has died per her prophecy, and that [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat) waits in [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) where [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) will soon arrive to deal with him. She also speaks of the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) and [King Joffrey's wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding), and prophecies related to [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark). When she notices Arya, she becomes distressed, insinuating the girl is associated with death, and saying she has enough grief from [Summerhall](wiki:Summerhall).(wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
The old woman requests that [Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams) play [Jenny's song](wiki:Jenny's_song) for her as payment for her prophecies. As she listens to the music, she murmurs the words and cries.(wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) receives a report that "[Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) had become a hermit in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), living on some [haunted hill](wiki:High_Heart)."(wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) reveals to [Daenerys](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) that her grandsire, King [Jaehaerys II Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_II_Targaryen), arranged the marriage between her parents, Prince [Aerys II](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) and Princess [Rhaella](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen). Jaehaerys did this after hearing a prophecy from a woods witch that [the prince that was promised](wiki:The_prince_that_was_promised) would be born from their line. While most people believed the woods witch to be a dwarf, her friend, [Jenny of Oldstones](wiki:Jenny_of_Oldstones), claimed she was one of the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest). While Barristan believes the woods witch died at the [Tragedy of Summerhall](wiki:Tragedy_of_Summerhall), the description fits the ghost of High Heart.(wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)
While aboard the [Selaesori Qhoran](wiki:Selaesori_Qhoran), [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) recalls what he considers to be a less reliable tale that had reached his ears, that of a dwarf witch who haunts a hill in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Ghost_of_High_Heart.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
