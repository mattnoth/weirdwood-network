## Appearances & Description

Rickard's look resembles that of his brother [Roger](wiki:Roger_Ryswell), to the point [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) struggles to tell them apart.(wiki:Rickard_Ryswell.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Rickard and his brothers Roger and [Roose](wiki:Roose_Ryswell) are said to be quarrelsome. They each take a horsehead of a different color for their sigil, the jape being that the [Ryswells](wiki:House_Ryswell) cannot even agree upon the color of their arms.(wiki:Rickard_Ryswell.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) sees a Ryswell lead a [column](wiki:Column) of riders to the camp of [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) at the [siege of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin), but is unable to tell the difference between Rickard and his brother [Roger](wiki:Roger_Ryswell).(wiki:Rickard_Ryswell.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Along with the rest of [House Ryswell](wiki:House_Ryswell), Rickard attends [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark) of Ramsay to "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)"—actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)—in [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). Theon spots Rickard nuzzling at the neck of one of [Abel](wiki:Abel)'s washerwomen—supposedly [Frenya](wiki:Frenya)—near the [Burned Tower](wiki:Burned_Tower) of Winterfell (however, as mentioned before, it is possible Theon mistakes Roger for Rickard).(wiki:Rickard_Ryswell.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) After a foolish [freerider](wiki:Freerider) is thrown from the [Battlements Gate](wiki:Battlements_Gate), Rickard laughs at his fate.(wiki:Rickard_Ryswell.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
