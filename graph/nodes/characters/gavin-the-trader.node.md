### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Gavin the Trader is among the [free folkleaders](wiki:Free_folk) who accompany [Tormund Giantsbane](wiki:Tormund) to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) in declaring a truce with the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Gavin_the_Trader.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) When [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), the new [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), meets with [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) and [Othell Yarwyck](wiki:Othell_Yarwyck), he mentions Gavin among the possible suggestions of wildling chiefs who could hold [Hoarfrost Hill](wiki:Hoarfrost_Hill) and [Rimegate](wiki:Rimegate).(wiki:Gavin_the_Trader.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Gavin is in the [Shieldhall](wiki:Shieldhall), whispering with [Harle the Handsome](wiki:Harle_the_Handsome), when Jon announces [the letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) purportedly from [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) entitled 'Bastard'.(wiki:Gavin_the_Trader.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
