## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Alebelly stays at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) after both Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) and then his son [Robb](wiki:Robb_Stark) leave. He is still a member of the guards when [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon](wiki:Rickon_Stark) are the only representatives of [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) within the castle.(wiki:Alebelly.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

[Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed) announces to Bran that he has seen the deaths of several persons, including [Mikken](wiki:Mikken), Septon [Chayle](wiki:Chayle) and Alebelly, in a [green dream](wiki:Greenseer).(wiki:Alebelly.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Alebelly is killed during the ironborn's [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell). He is on the gate that is assaulted and is the first casualty suffered that night.(wiki:Alebelly.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
