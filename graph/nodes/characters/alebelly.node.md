## Identity

Alebelly is a fat guard of Winterfell (wiki:Alebelly).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Alebelly stayed at Winterfell after both Lord Eddard Stark and then his son Robb departed. Jojen Reed announced to Bran that he had seen Alebelly's death in a green dream (wiki:Alebelly.cite_ref-Racok21). Alebelly was killed during the ironborn capture of Winterfell — he was on the gate that was assaulted and was the first casualty suffered that night (wiki:Alebelly.cite_ref-Racok46).
