## Origins

Lord Eon attended the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) and was talking with Lords [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) and [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) when Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) gave [Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark) a [crown of flowers](wiki:Blue_winter_rose) as the [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty).(wiki:Eon_Hunter.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Appearances & Description

Eon is older than [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), and half-crippled by gout. His hands are covered in liver spots(wiki:Eon_Hunter.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) and his legs are gouty.(wiki:Eon_Hunter.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Eon is one of [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn)'s suitors after the death of Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn).(wiki:Eon_Hunter.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) As such, he sends [wine](wiki:Wine) from his own cellars as a gift to Lysa.(wiki:Eon_Hunter.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) Lord Hunter is in attendance in the [High Hall](wiki:High_Hall) when [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) demands [trial by battle](wiki:Trial_by_battle) to prove his innocence, and he is the second to offer to champion Lysa in the trial by combat.(wiki:Eon_Hunter.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

### A Storm of Swords

Maester [Willamen](wiki:Willamen_Frey) serves Lord Hunter.(wiki:Eon_Hunter.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

After ruling for nearly sixty years,(wiki:Eon_Hunter.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Eon dies suddenly, leading to calls by his younger sons, [Eustace](wiki:Eustace_Hunter) and [Harlan](wiki:Harlan_Hunter), that he had been murdered by his eldest, [Gilwood](wiki:Gilwood_Hunter).(wiki:Eon_Hunter.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) reveals to [Alayne Stone](wiki:Sansa_Stark) that it was Eon's youngest son, Harlan, who arranged his father's death.(wiki:Eon_Hunter.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)
