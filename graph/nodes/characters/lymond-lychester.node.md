## Origins

Lord Lymond once held Ser [Maynard](wiki:Maynard) at the bridge, taking six wounds before killing the other knight. There is no further information as to who Maynard was or where the bridge is located, though [Lem](wiki:Lem) and [Anguy](wiki:Anguy) believe it to be the small bridge near [Lychester's keep](wiki:Lord_Lychester's_keep).(wiki:Lymond_Lychester.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

All of Lord Lychester's sons died during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), fighting for one side or the other. The old knight has since become senile with age, and is now cared for by young Maester [Roone](wiki:Roone_(maester)).(wiki:Lymond_Lychester.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)(wiki:Lymond_Lychester.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Appearances & Description

Lymond is an old done [knight](wiki:Knight). He is a large man whose hair has turned grey with age.(wiki:Lymond_Lychester.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

With the death of his sons in [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), the old knight has become senile and inattentive. When spoken to, Lymond only smiles and mutters the story of his battle with Ser [Maynard](wiki:Maynard) at the bridge.(wiki:Lymond_Lychester.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) Lymond's mind has become prone to wandering.(wiki:Lymond_Lychester.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Lymond_Lychester.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

The [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) visits with Lord Lychester, but the old knight only repeats the story of his battle against Ser [Maynard](wiki:Maynard). Maester [Roone](wiki:Roone_(maester)) gives them information on Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion)'s supposed whereabouts, and directs them to find the [Lady of the Leaves](wiki:Lady_of_the_Leaves).(wiki:Lymond_Lychester.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)

### A Feast for Crows

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) spots the banner of House Lychester among those of the riverlords who have bent the knee and now aid [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) in the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun).(wiki:Lymond_Lychester.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
