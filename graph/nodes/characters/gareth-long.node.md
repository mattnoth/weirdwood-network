## Origins

Ser Gareth served Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake) as the [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms) at [Starpike](wiki:Starpike) in the [Reach](wiki:Reach). When Unwin became [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), he made Gareth the new master-at-arms of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep). Gareth trained King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), a sullen student who often ignored instructions or refused to cooperate. Since Gareth was not allowed to harm the king, he advised Lord Peake to make Aegon's friend, [Gaemon Palehair](wiki:Gaemon_Palehair), the king's [whipping boy](wiki:Whipping_boy). Gareth quickly became despised in the Red Keep, especially by the king.(wiki:Gareth_Long.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Gareth's regard of Aegon improved slightly after the king wed his second wife, Queen [Daenaera Velaryon](wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon).(wiki:Gareth_Long.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Gareth remained in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) after Lord Peake resigned as Hand in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC).(wiki:Gareth_Long.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) During the [secret siege](wiki:Secret_siege) in [135 AC](wiki:135_AC), Gareth's entreaty for Aegon to leave [Maegor's Holdfast](wiki:Maegor's_Holdfast) only angered the king. Lord [George Graceford](wiki:George_Graceford) named Gareth as an accomplice to a plot against [House Rogare](wiki:House_Rogare) in the capital. The master-at-arms never denied the charges, claiming Aegon was a weakling unfit to sit the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). He also accused Ser [Victor Risley](wiki:Victor_Risley), the [King's Justice](wiki:King's_Justice), of being a conspirator.(wiki:Gareth_Long.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Gareth and George were condemned to death, but both were allowed to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). Victor instead demanded a [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat), so his accuser, Gareth, was chosen to face him before leaving for the [Wall](wiki:Wall). Gareth promptly killed Victor in the throne room. Ser [Lucas Lothston](wiki:Lucas_Lothston_(husband_of_Falena)) replaced Gareth as master-at-arms at the Red Keep.(wiki:Gareth_Long.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
