## Origins

Lord [Gawen Westerling](wiki:Gawen_Westerling) once suggested that Willem or his twin [Martyn](wiki:Martyn_Lannister) marry Gawen's daughter, [Jeyne](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling). Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) refused this offer because he considered Jeyne of dubious blood due to the lineage of her mother, [Sybell Spicer](wiki:Sybell_Spicer).(wiki:Willem_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Appearances & Description

Willem's hair is either long and blond or brown.(wiki:Willem_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Willem is part of the army of his cousin, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), that invades the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). Willem and another cousin, [Tion Frey](wiki:Tion_Frey), are captured during the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood) by the [northern](wiki:North) army of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark).(wiki:Willem_Lannister.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Robb, now [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), attempt to negotiate Willem's release. Robb offers Willem and Tion in exchange for [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark) and [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark). Tyrion declines that offer, but offers Lord [Medger Cerwyn](wiki:Medger_Cerwyn) and Ser [Donnel Locke](wiki:Donnel_Locke) for Tion and [Harrion Karstark](wiki:Harrion_Karstark) and Ser [Wylis Manderly](wiki:Wylis_Manderly) for Willem. No agreement is reached.(wiki:Willem_Lannister.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) ponders having one of his twins, Willem and [Martyn](wiki:Martyn_Lannister), or their cousin, Tion, marry Sansa, but all three boys are held captive at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Willem_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

[Torrhen](wiki:Torrhen_Karstark) and [Eddard Karstark](wiki:Eddard_Karstark), the younger sons of Lord [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark), were both killed by Jaime Lannister in the Whispering Wood.(wiki:Willem_Lannister.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Rickard wants to kill Jaime in retaliation,(wiki:Willem_Lannister.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) but Jaime is released from captivity at Riverrun by [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) without Robb's knowledge.(wiki:Willem_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

When Robb's host returns to Riverrun,(wiki:Willem_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Lord Karstark feels robbed of his revenge against the missing Jaime and chooses Willem and Tion instead. With some of his men, Rickard kills the boys and two of their [House Tully guards](wiki:House_Tully_guards). Catelyn observes that one of the boys was blond (presumably Willem as a [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister)), wore his hair long, and was trying to grow a beard. He was probably killed in his sleep. His cousin was brown-haired (presumably Tion as a [Frey](wiki:House_Frey)) and had fought for his life. Both were pale from their imprisonment.(wiki:Willem_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Furious about the killing of his captives, Robb executes Rickard with his accomplices at Riverrun.(wiki:Willem_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
