## Identity

Willem Lannister is a squire from House Lannister, the second son of Ser Kevan Lannister and Lady Dorna Swyft, and twin to Martyn (wiki:Willem_Lannister).

## Origins

Born between 285–287 AC in the Westerlands (track_b: Born). Son of Kevan Lannister and Dorna Swyft (track_b: Father, Mother). Died in 299 AC at Riverrun (track_b: Died).

## Allegiances

Willem is `SWORN_TO` House Lannister (track_b: Allegiance). His culture is Westermen (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Willem's hair is described as either long and blond or brown (wiki:Willem_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos).

## Narrative Arc

Lord Gawen Westerling once suggested Willem or his twin Martyn marry his daughter Jeyne, but Kevan refused due to the lineage of Jeyne's mother Sybell Spicer (wiki:Willem_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos19).

Willem is part of Jaime Lannister's army invading the riverlands. He and his cousin Tion Frey are captured in the Whispering Wood (wiki:Willem_Lannister.cite_ref-Ragot63). Robb Stark and Tyrion Lannister attempt to negotiate their release but reach no agreement (wiki:Willem_Lannister.cite_ref-Racok25).

Torrhen and Eddard Karstark murder Willem and Tion Frey in their cells at Riverrun in vengeance for their father Lord Rickard Karstark's slain sons. Robb Stark executes Lord Rickard for the murders (wiki:Willem_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos14, cite_ref-Rasos20). Willem's death is one of several events that erode Robb's alliance.
