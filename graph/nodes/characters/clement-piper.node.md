## Appearances & Description

Clement is short, fat, bowlegged and has red hair that is bushy and wild. His younger son, [Lewys](wiki:Lewys_Piper), resembles him.(wiki:Clement_Piper.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Lord Piper believes himself to be an honest and honorable man, and Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) considers him to be bold and not easily cowed.(wiki:Clement_Piper.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) commands Lord Clement Piper and [Lord Vance](wiki:Lord_Vance) to guard the pass below the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth) when the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) start to mass troops there.(wiki:Clement_Piper.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) While Lord Piper and Lord Vance are guarding the Golden Tooth, their lands are raided by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane). The Tullys have their sons, Ser [Marq Piper](wiki:Marq_Piper) and Ser [Karyl Vance](wiki:Karyl_Vance), take a small force to gather the survivors of the attack and report the crime to King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Clement_Piper.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) descends on the two lords and [smashes](wiki:Battle_in_the_hills_below_the_Golden_Tooth) their army, with Lord Vance killed. Lord Clement is put to flight and retreats to the amassed Tully host at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), with Ser Jaime trailing closely behind.(wiki:Clement_Piper.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The riverlords are defeated [beneath the walls](wiki:Battle_under_the_walls_of_Riverrun) of Riverrun by the Lannisters, and the Pipers are [burnt out](wiki:Burning_of_Pinkmaiden_Castle) of [Pinkmaiden Castle](wiki:Pinkmaiden_Castle) by Ser Gregor.(wiki:Clement_Piper.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords

[Jack-Be-Lucky](wiki:Jack-Be-Lucky), of the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), laments that his father was hanged by Lord Piper's bailiff.(wiki:Clement_Piper.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

After his son and heir, Ser Marq, is captured at the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Lord Clement sends men under a peace banner to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to bend the knee to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) in his stead.(wiki:Clement_Piper.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Lord Clement's younger son, [Lewys](wiki:Lewys_Piper), serves Ser Jaime as a [squire](wiki:Squire) and hostage for his father's good behavior.(wiki:Clement_Piper.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) As Jaime starts campaigning in the riverlands, he grows fond of the boy and hopes that his father stays loyal for his son's sake.(wiki:Clement_Piper.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Lord Clement is among the reluctant riverlords who take part in the [Siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun) and, along with both Lord Vances, represents the riverlords at war councils. During the first war council with Ser Jaime present, Lord Clement dismisses Lord [Norbert Vance](wiki:Norbert_Vance)'s idea of peaceful resolution, believing that Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) has too much sense to yield Riverrun to [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey). Clement insults [Edwyn Frey](wiki:Edwyn_Frey) and demands the return of his son, while also questioning the fate of twenty [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms) and five [knights](wiki:Knight), Lord Piper's nephews and cousins, that had accompanied Marq to the wedding. When Edwyn gives a dismissive answer and calls them traitors, Clement challenges Edwyn who pales before him, until the more martial Ser [Walder Rivers](wiki:Walder_Rivers) steps in. Jaime demands both men to sit, but Piper storms out of the council with Jaime reminding Edwyn that the Freys are twice as treacherous as Clement.(wiki:Clement_Piper.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

After Riverrun surrenders, Jaime grants Clement leave to return to his own lands and promises that his kin will be ransomed back.(wiki:Clement_Piper.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
