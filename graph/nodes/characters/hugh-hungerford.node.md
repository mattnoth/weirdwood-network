## Origins

Hugh was the Windblown's [paymaster](wiki:Paymaster) until he was caught stealing from the war chest. The [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince) had three of his fingers removed for theft.(wiki:Hugh_Hungerford.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Appearances & Description

Hugh is slim and saturnine, long-faced, long-legged, and clothed in faded finery.(wiki:Hugh_Hungerford.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He is sour.(wiki:Hugh_Hungerford.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Dance with Dragons
