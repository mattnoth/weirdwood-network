## Appearances & Description

Black Balaq is a white-haired [Summer Islander](wiki:Summer_Isles) with skin as dark as soot. He wears his worldly wealth upon his person, including a feathered cloak of green and orange which is magnificent to behold. He wears a lord's ransom in golden arm rings.(wiki:Black_Balaq.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Black Balaq is present during [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington)'s rendezvous with the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) three miles south of [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys). Like the rest of the company, Black Balaq swears his allegiance to [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)).(wiki:Black_Balaq.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
For the long voyage to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) Jon insists that [Harry Strickland](wiki:Harry_Strickland) break Black Balaq's command into ten companies of a hundred men and place each company upon a different ship. Six of the ships stay together well enough for the [landing of the Golden Company](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company) on [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath), which leaves the company with six hundred bows until the lagging ships turn up. For the [taking of Griffin's Roost](wiki:Taking_of_Griffin's_Roost) two hundred bows are sufficient.(wiki:Black_Balaq.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
During the victors' feast in the castle's great hall, Jon presides from the Griffin's Seat while Black Balaq shares the high table with Homeless Harry, [Franklyn Flowers](wiki:Franklyn_Flowers), and the three young [Conningtons](wiki:House_Connington) they had taken captive. Black Balaq is one of nine men present during the company's war council.(wiki:Black_Balaq.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
