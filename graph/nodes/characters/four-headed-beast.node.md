## Origins

In [49 AC](wiki:49_AC), when Queen [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) [married](wiki:Wedding_of_Rhaena_Targaryen_and_Androw_Farman) [Androw Farman](wiki:Androw_Farman), the second son of Lord [Marq Farman](wiki:Marq_Farman) of [Faircastle](wiki:Faircastle), [Alayne Royce](wiki:Alayne_Royce) and [Samantha Stokeworth](wiki:Samantha_Stokeworth) rushed to stand by her at the wedding, along with the groom's sister, [Elissa Farman](wiki:Elissa_Farman).(wiki:Four-Headed_Beast.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) They remained on [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle) after the wedding. Rhaena, her new sister-in-law, and Alayne and Samantha, two of Rhaena's oldest friends, formed an inseparable group that Ser [Franklyn Farman](wiki:Franklyn_Farman), Elissa and Androw's elder brother, called "the Four-Headed Beast". Rhaena frequently took her friends flying on her [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Dreamfyre](wiki:Dreamfyre). Androw was occasionally admitted to the circle, but never often enough to be called a fifth head, and he never went flying with Rhaena.(wiki:Four-Headed_Beast.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Ser Franklyn did not care for the way that the Four-Headed Beast had become the center of court life at Faircastle, and the way Rhaena seemed to encourage Elissa's "wilful ways". Lord Marq Farman died a fortnight after Rhaena and Androw's first anniversary, and the day after the funeral, the new Lord Franklyn expelled Rhaena and her friends from Faircastle, but commanded Elissa to stay and be wed to a man of his choice. Rhaena set flight on Dreamfyre to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), and Androw and the others (including Elissa, defying her brother) followed in the [Maiden's Fancy](wiki:Maiden's_Fancy).(wiki:Four-Headed_Beast.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
At Casterly Rock, Lord [Lyman Lannister](wiki:Lyman_Lannister) and his wife [Jocasta Tarbeck](wiki:Jocasta_Tarbeck) welcomed Rhaena, her dragon, her husband, and her friends. The Four-Headed Beast and Androw were assigned a regally appointed suite of apartments within the Rock. They remained at Casterly Rock for more than a month, until Rhaena became wary of Lyman's ulterior motives.(wiki:Four-Headed_Beast.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Rhaena and her court took their leave of Casterly Rock and traveled the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) and the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), visiting [Ashemark](wiki:Ashemark), [Castamere](wiki:Castamere), the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth), [Wayfarer's Rest](wiki:Wayfarer's_Rest), and [Pinkmaiden Castle](wiki:Pinkmaiden_Castle). Eventually, in [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), Rhaena was granted [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) by her brother, King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), and settled there with her friends, her husband, and her daughter [Aerea](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen).(wiki:Four-Headed_Beast.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Rhaena held court at Dragonstone with her companions for several years, until [54 AC](wiki:54_AC) when her jealous husband Androw poisoned Alayne and Samantha, then killed himself by leaping out a castle window. Soon after a rift continued to widen between Rhaena and the surviving Elissa Farman, who wished to continue her explorations at sea. Elissa left Dragonstone later that same year, along with a trio of dragon eggs she stole to fund her expedition - on which she was ultimately lost at sea and never seen again. Near the end of that same disastrous year, Rhaena's daughter Aerea claimed [Balerion](wiki:Balerion) and disappeared, then suddenly died upon her return to King's Landing in [56 AC](wiki:56_AC). Afterwards Rhaena, devastated and alone, removed to live out the rest of her life in isolation at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Four-Headed_Beast.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
