## Origins

Sylva is a childhood friend of Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell).(wiki:Sylva_Santagar.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) They were children at the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens) together, usually a group of five, along with [Tyene Sand](wiki:Tyene_Sand), [Garin](wiki:Garin), and [Andrey Dalt](wiki:Andrey_Dalt).(wiki:Sylva_Santagar.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Because of her freckles, Sylva received the nickname "Spotted Sylva". Her friends like to pretend the nickname comes from the fact that she is the heir to [Spottswood](wiki:Spottswood), as the Santagars' sigil is a spotted [leopard](wiki:Leopard).(wiki:Sylva_Santagar.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) invites Sylva to be one her co-conspirators to make Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) the [Queen of the Andals, the Rhoynar, and the First Men](wiki:Queen_of_the_Andals,_the_Rhoynar,_and_the_First_Men). During their travels, they are intercepted by [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah) on the [Greenblood](wiki:Greenblood). Sylva is among those captured.(wiki:Sylva_Santagar.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Though [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), the [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), deigns not to punish Sylva for her role in the conspiracy, she is sent home to her father, Ser [Symon Santagar](wiki:Symon_Santagar). Symon quickly [betroths](wiki:Betrothal) Sylva to the aged [Eldon Estermont](wiki:Eldon_Estermont), [Lord of Greenstone](wiki:Lord_of_Greenstone). Sylva is sent to [Greenstone](wiki:Greenstone) and married soon after.(wiki:Sylva_Santagar.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Sylva_Santagar.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
