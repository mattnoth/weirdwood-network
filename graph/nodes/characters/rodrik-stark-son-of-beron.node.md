## Identity

Rodrik Stark, known as the Wandering Wolf, is a historical member of House Stark (track_b: aliases). Born between 190 and 227 AC, he died in 214 AC (track_b: Born, Died). He is the son of Beron Stark and Lorra Royce (track_b: Father, Mother).

## Origins

## Narrative Arc

Rodrik earned the nickname "the Wandering Wolf" for his travels in Essos, where he served with the Second Sons sellsword company (track_b: Allegiances). He married Arya Flint and fathered two daughters: Branda Stark and Lyarra Stark (track_b: Spouse, Issue). Lyarra Stark later married her cousin Rickard Stark, making Rodrik the maternal grandfather of Eddard, Brandon, Lyanna, and Benjen Stark. He appears only in a mentioned capacity in ADWD (wiki:Rodrik_Stark_(son_of_Beron).cite_ref-adwd66).
