## Origins

Lord Sunderland fought for [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) during the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion).(wiki:Lord_Sunderland.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

In [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), Lord Sunderland came to [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) to attend the [wedding](wiki:Wedding) of [the daughter](wiki:Lady_Butterwell) of [Lord Frey](wiki:Lord_Frey) to Lord [Ambrose Butterwell](wiki:Ambrose_Butterwell). Ser [Glendon Flowers](wiki:Glendon_Flowers) was imprisoned after being accused of stealing the prize of the [wedding's tourney](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls), Ambrose's treasured [dragon egg](wiki:Dragon_egg). When Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) publicly declared that Glendon had been falsely accused, [Daemon II Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre) ordered Lords Sunderland, [Vyrwel](wiki:Lord_Vyrwel), and [Smallwood](wiki:Lord_Smallwood) to fetch Glendon from the dungeons.(wiki:Lord_Sunderland.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) It is unknown if Lord Sunderland suffered any punishment for the part he played in the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion), which was suppressed by Lord [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers).(wiki:Lord_Sunderland.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
