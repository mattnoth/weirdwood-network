## Origins

A [dragonseed](wiki:Dragonseed) of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), Ulf the White was a [man-at-arms](wiki:Man-at-arms) when the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) began. When Prince [Jacaerys Velaryon](wiki:Jacaerys_Velaryon) called for [dragonriders](wiki:Dragonrider) in [129 AC](wiki:129_AC), Ulf was able to mount [Silverwing](wiki:Silverwing). Ulf and his fellow dragonseeds—[Hugh Hammer](wiki:Hugh_Hammer), [Nettles](wiki:Nettles), and [Addam Velaryon](wiki:Addam_Velaryon)—fought valiantly besides Jacaerys in the [Battle of the Gullet](wiki:Battle_of_the_Gullet), but Jace fell during the battle.(wiki:Ulf_White.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) The dragonrider began to call himself "Ulf White".(wiki:Ulf_White.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
After the [fall of King's Landing](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing), Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) suggested that Ulf be wed to the daughter of the late Lord [Stokeworth](wiki:House_Stokeworth), but Rhaenyra declined. The queen instead granted [knighthood](wiki:Knight) to Ulf and Hugh and granted them lands on [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark). According to [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom), a drunken Ulf rode through [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom) wearing only golden spurs.(wiki:Ulf_White.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) When Daemon suggested that Houses [Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon) and [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) should be destroyed for their support of King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), he proposed granting [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) to Ulf, but after Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) opposed the idea, Rhaenyra refused the suggestion.(wiki:Ulf_White.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)
In [130 AC](wiki:130_AC), Ulf White and Hugh Hammer were sent to help the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) defend the town of [Tumbleton](wiki:Tumbleton) from Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower)'s approaching [host](wiki:Host). During the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton), however, the [Two Betrayers](wiki:Two_Betrayers) defected to the [greens](wiki:Greens). Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)) named Ulf as Lord of [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge), but Ulf desired to receive [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) as his seat, not considering Bitterbridge to be sufficient. According to Mushroom, Ulf raped three [maidens](wiki:Maiden) each night and fed those who displeased him to his [dragon](wiki:Dragon), Silverwing.(wiki:Ulf_White.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) Ulf took the [Bawdy Badger](wiki:Bawdy_Badger) as his favored inn.(wiki:Ulf_White.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
The [Caltrops](wiki:Caltrops) conspired to kill both Ulf and Hugh Hammer. Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake) had warrants for their executions drawn up, which were signed by Prince Daeron. The day that the Caltrops planned to strike, however, the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton) took place. Ulf survived the battle, drunkenly sleeping through every attempt to wake him. When he learned that Hugh had died during the battle, Ulf informed Lord Peake that he was going to claim the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Ulf_White.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
The next day, Ser [Hobert Hightower](wiki:Hobert_Hightower) brought Ulf two casks of [wine](wiki:Wine), one Dornish red and one [Arbor gold](wiki:Arbor_gold). As Ulf preferred the sweeter vintage, the Arbor gold had been [poisoned](wiki:Poison). Suspicious of Hobert's behavior, Ulf commanded the Dornish red to be set aside, and only dared drink from the poisoned Arbor gold after he had seen Hobert drink from it himself. After Hobert asked for a second cup, Ulf drank three cups himself, and soon fell asleep before dying peacefully due to the poison.(wiki:Ulf_White.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
