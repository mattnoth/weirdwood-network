## Origins

He ruled [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) when the Young Dragon, King [Daeron I Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen), started his [conquest](wiki:Conquest_of_Dorne) of Dorne in [157 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). The following year, he and forty of the most powerful Dornish [lords](wiki:Lord) bent their knees in the [Submission of Sunspear](wiki:Submission_of_Sunspear).(wiki:Prince_of_Dorne_(father_of_Maron).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

After several long years of war, Daeron's brother and successor, King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen), walked barefoot from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) in [161 AC](wiki:161_AC) to forge a peace between the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) and Dorne. The Prince accepted Baelor's proposal of peace, and [betrothed](wiki:Betrothal) his daughter and heir, [Myriah](wiki:Myriah_Martell), to Baelor's cousin, Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Prince_of_Dorne_(father_of_Maron).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

After the terms were arranged, he offered Baelor a [galley](wiki:Ship) to take him back to King's Landing, but Baelor insisted to walk. The Prince made arrangements to insure Baelor's safely on his way back and commanded [Lord Wyl](wiki:Lord_Wyl_(conquest_of_Dorne)) to released Baelor's cousin, Prince [Aemon Targaryen](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_II)).(wiki:Prince_of_Dorne_(father_of_Maron).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

By [174 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), his son, [Maron](wiki:Maron_Martell), had succeeded him as [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne) and [Lord of Sunspear](wiki:Lord_of_Sunspear).(wiki:Prince_of_Dorne_(father_of_Maron).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
