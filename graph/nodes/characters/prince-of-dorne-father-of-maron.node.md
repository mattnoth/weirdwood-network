## Identity

Prince Nymeros Martell was the ruling Prince of Dorne and head of House Martell during the reigns of kings Daeron I and Baelor I Targaryen. His personal name is not recorded in canon (wiki:Prince_of_Dorne_(father_of_Maron)).

## Origins

Born on or before 146 AC. Died 161–174 AC (track_b: Born, Died).

## Narrative Arc

Ruled Dorne when Daeron I Targaryen launched his conquest in 157 AC. He and forty powerful Dornish lords knelt in the Submission of Sunspear. After years of war, Baelor I walked barefoot from King's Landing to Sunspear in 161 AC. The Prince accepted peace and betrothed his daughter Myriah to Baelor's cousin Daeron. He offered Baelor a galley to return but Baelor insisted on walking. The Prince ensured Baelor's safety and commanded Lord Wyl to release Prince Aemon Targaryen. By 174 AC, his son Maron had succeeded him (wiki:Prince_of_Dorne_(father_of_Maron)).
