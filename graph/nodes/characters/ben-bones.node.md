## Appearances & Description

Ben is an old man, tasked with keeping the [bastard's girls](wiki:Bastard's_girls), [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow)'s pack of female hunting hounds.(wiki:Ben_Bones.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He likes the dogs better than their master.(wiki:Ben_Bones.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Ben claims he was instructed by Ramsay to train the dogs to kill [wolves](wiki:Wolves).(wiki:Ben_Bones.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Ben is present in the great hall of [the Dreadfort](wiki:The_Dreadfort) when [Little Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)) and [Big Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Jammos)) bring "[Reek](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy)" before Ramsay, who is joined by [Arnolf Karstark](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark) and [Hother Umber](wiki:Hother_Umber).(wiki:Ben_Bones.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) At Ramsay's command he makes a collar for Reek, and lets him eat and sleep with [the hounds](wiki:Bastard's_girls).(wiki:Ben_Bones.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
