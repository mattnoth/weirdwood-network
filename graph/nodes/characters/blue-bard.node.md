## Origins

Wat was the son of a cooper and was raised in the trade, but he found he had more talent for making [lutes](wiki:Lute) than barrels. When he was twelve years old, he ran away to join a musician troupe. He wandered half the [Reach](wiki:Reach) before coming to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in search of patronage.(wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

## Appearances & Description

Wat is a handsome young man(wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) with healthy teeth.(wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) He has blue eyes and [dyes](wiki:Hair_dye) his curly, shoulder-length brown hair blue in the [Tyroshi](wiki:Tyrosh) fashion.(wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

The Blue Bard dresses all in blue,(wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) including a blue silk tunic slashed with blue satin, blue wool breeches, and blue calfskin boots.(wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) He plays a lute.(wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

As part of the [Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) entourage, the Blue Bard sings at [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Tommen_I_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) [Taena of Myr](wiki:Taena_of_Myr) informs Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) that the Blue Bird has become Margaery's favorite entertainer.(wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) The Blue Bird plays for Margaery and her entourage when they go to pick flowers in the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood).(wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Taena learns that the Blue Bird performs at an inn when he is not attending to Margaery.(wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Cersei contrives to use the Blue Bard as a pawn in her efforts to remove Margaery from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), and she has the musician perform while Cersei has dinner with Taena and Lord [Orton Merryweather](wiki:Orton_Merryweather). During the meal, the queen has the Blue Bard arrested and sent to the dungeons for seducing Margaery, though he claims never to have done so. In front of Cersei, the Blue Bird is stripped and mutilated by [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn), who removes one each of his eyes and nipples, and is told to tell the story as Cersei wants him to. Under torture he names Ser [Osney Kettleblack](wiki:Osney_Kettleblack), Ser [Tallad the Tall](wiki:Tallad_the_Tall), Ser [Lambert Turnberry](wiki:Lambert_Turnberry), [Jalabhar Xho](wiki:Jalabhar_Xho), [Hugh Clifton](wiki:Hugh_Clifton), Ser [Mark Mullendore](wiki:Mark_Mullendore), Ser [Bayard Norcross](wiki:Bayard_Norcross), and [Hamish the Harper](wiki:Hamish_the_Harper) as Margaery's lovers.(wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) ' The Blue Bard also names Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) and the Redwyne twins, Ser [Horas](wiki:Horas_Redwyne) and Ser [Hobber](wiki:Hobber_Redwyne), but Cersei has him recant the three; Loras because the accusation is not credible and the twins because she needs the support of [House Redwyne](wiki:House_Redwyne) and [their ships](wiki:Redwyne_fleet) to deal with the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn).(wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

After Margaery is successfully framed, Cersei has the Blue Bard arrested again. He is tortured once again by the [Faith](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) but continues to tell the story Cersei wants him to.(wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

The Blue Bard is still held by the Faith. According to Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), the singer appears to have gone mad.(wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
