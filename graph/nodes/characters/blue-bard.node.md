## Identity

The Blue Bard is a young singer in service with House Tyrell. His real name is Wat (wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Raffc39). He was the son of a cooper and raised in the trade, but found more talent for making lutes than barrels (wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Raffc39).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Tyrell — serves as a musician in their retinue (track_b: Allegiance).

## Appearances & Description

Wat is a handsome young man with blue eyes and curly, shoulder-length brown hair dyed blue in the Tyroshi fashion (wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Raffc39). He has healthy, straight white teeth. He dresses entirely in blue: blue silk tunic slashed with blue satin, blue wool breeches, and blue calfskin boots. He plays a lute (wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Raffc39).

## Narrative Arc

The Blue Bard sings at the wedding of Tommen and Margaery Tyrell, becoming Margaery's favorite entertainer (wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Raffc12, wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Raffc28). Cersei contrives to use him as a pawn in her plot against Margaery, having him arrested and sent to Qyburn, who mutilates him — removing an eye and a nipple — and forces him to name several men as Margaery's lovers. Under torture he names Osney Kettleblack, Tallad the Tall, Lambert Turnberry, Jalabhar Xho, Hugh Clifton, Mark Mullendore, Bayard Norcross, and Hamish the Harper (wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Raffc39). After Margaery is framed, the Faith holds and further tortures the Blue Bard. By ADWD, Kevan Lannister reports the singer appears to have gone mad (wiki:Blue_Bard.cite_ref-Radwd54).
