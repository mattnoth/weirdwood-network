## Identity

Serena Stark is a historical member of House Stark, born between 141 and 162 AC, who died in 154 AC (track_b: Born, Died). She is the daughter of Rickon Stark and Jeyne Manderly (track_b: Father, Mother). She held title at Last Hearth through marriage to Jon Umber (track_b: Title).

## Origins

Daughter of Rickon Stark, son of Cregan, and Jeyne Manderly (track_b: Father, Mother).

## Narrative Arc

Serena Stark married twice: first to Jon Umber, and later to Edric Stark (track_b: Spouses). She bore children including Torrhen Stark, Cregard Stark, Arrana Stark, and Argelle Stark (track_b: Issue).
