## Origins

After the death of her [father](wiki:Rickon_Stark_(son_of_Cregan)) in the [Conquest of Dorne](wiki:Conquest_of_Dorne), Serena's sister Sansa was set to inherit Winterfell after her grandfather, Lord Cregan. However, the [lordship of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell) instead passed to Cregan's second son, [Jonnel Stark](wiki:Jonnel_Stark), who also married Sansa.(wiki:Serena_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_stark_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7) The north lamented the death of Rickon, for the reigns of Jonnel and his brothers after him were troublesome.(wiki:Serena_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_lords_of_winterfell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Serena married twice in her life. She first married Lord [Jon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber_(husband_of_Serena)) with whom she did not have issue, and then she married her father's half-brother, [Edric Stark](wiki:Edric_Stark_(son_of_Cregan)), with whom she had four children: first the twin sons [Torrhen](wiki:Torrhen_Stark_(son_of_Edric)) and [Cregard](wiki:Cregard_Stark), then daughters [Arrana](wiki:Arrana_Stark) and [Argelle Stark](wiki:Argelle_Stark).(wiki:Serena_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_stark_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8)(wiki:Serena_Stark.cite_ref-wives_4-2)
