## Origins

Gerold Lannister was courting [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber) in [211 AC](wiki:211_AC),(wiki:Alysanne_Farman.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) but it is unknown whether he was a widower at the time, or whether after Rohanne married [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey), Gerold married Alysanne. Gerold became [Lord of Casterly Rock](wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock) in [213 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), and if he were married to Alysanne at the time, she would have become Lady of Casterly Rock. However, this is unknown. Nevertheless, at some point Alysanne did die, and at some point after her death, Gerold married the widowed Rohanne.(wiki:Alysanne_Farman.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf761_4-0)
