## Appearances & Description

Sweets has a willowy body, and has [dyed](wiki:Hair_dye) purple hair and [violet eyes](wiki:Violet_eyes). Sweets dresses in [moonstones](wiki:Moonstone) and [Myrish](wiki:Myr) lace. Sweets is fluent in four tongues, one of them [High Valyrian](wiki:High_Valyrian).(wiki:Sweets.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Sweets is rather droll and astute - she titters a ghastly titter at [Penny](wiki:Penny)'s naïveté as to their fate if Yezzan dies. Sweets is fond of her master, Yezzan. Because Sweets is Yezzan's "special pet", indulged and favored, she is hated by Yezzan's other slaves.(wiki:Sweets.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Sweets is referred to alternately by the pronouns she/her by [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister),(wiki:Sweets.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)(wiki:Sweets.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) but occasionally as he/him, by Tyrion and by Yezzan's serjeant [Scar](wiki:Scar).(wiki:Sweets.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) When first meeting Tyrion and Penny, Sweets guesses that they are trying to decide whether she is a man or a woman and tells them she is both, revealing what is under her skirts.(wiki:Sweets.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Along with the rest of [Yezzan zo Qaggaz](wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz)'s "special treasures", Sweets accompanies her master to the [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) encampment outside of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). During the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown)'s march to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), [Dick Straw](wiki:Dick_Straw) tells [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) some of the gossip about Yezzan and his passion for grotesques, including word that among his slaves is a hermaphrodite(wiki:Sweets.cite_ref-hermaphrodite_5-1) who warms his bed at night.(wiki:Sweets.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Yezzan purchases [Penny](wiki:Penny) and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) for his [grotesquerie](wiki:Grotesquerie). His overseer, [Nurse](wiki:Nurse), introduces them to Sweets, who reveals what is under her skirts. Sweets is loathed by some slaves because she is the master's favorite, but she is friendly and candid with Tyrion and Penny, warning them to be careful around Nurse.(wiki:Sweets.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4) Sweets identifies [Ben Plumm](wiki:Ben_Plumm) to Tyrion, before he and Penny are summoned to perform during Yezzan's banquet held in [Yurkhaz zo Yunzak](wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak)'s honor.(wiki:Sweets.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5)

When Yezzan contracts the [pale mare](wiki:Pale_mare), it falls to Sweets, Penny, Tyrion, and his other treasures to attend to him. Tyrion thinks to himself that Sweets was right in that Yezzan was not so bad as masters went. When Tyrion tells Penny sardonically that they are all going to die, Sweets gives them a desperate look, and replies that Yezzan "must not die". Unlike Penny, Sweets is under no illusions as to their fate if Yezzan dies. She remarks to Penny that favorites sometimes accompany their masters to the grave and serve him in the afterlife. Tyrion thinks that Sweets should know, as his will be the first throat slit.(wiki:Sweets.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3)

When the [goat boy](wiki:Goat_boy) hopefully mentions the slave-freeing Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), Sweets replies that she is dead, drowned in the [Dothraki Sea](wiki:Dothraki_Sea), either not knowing that the Dothraki Sea is grassland and not water, or making an ironic pun. When Tyrion informs Sweets that they will see to fetching fresh water for the master, Sweets sounds numb when she replies that this is good of Tyrion, causing Tyrion to realize that Sweets's numbness is caused by more than just the fear of being killed - Sweets had truly been fond of Yezzan, the only one of Yezzan's treasures to feel that way.(wiki:Sweets.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-4)

Tyrion uses the task of fetching water to escape with Penny, as well as his knowledge of how Yezzan's other slaves feel about Sweets. Tyrion commands Yezzan's brutal and stupid serjeant, [Scar](wiki:Scar), to take pails and fetch water, and when Scar is insulted at being ordered around by a slave and strikes him, Tyrion claims that Sweets said Yezzan must have water. Scar insults Sweets, saying he doesn't take orders from him either. Scar orders Tyrion and Penny to fetch the water, and Tyrion manipulates him into letting them go with [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) as well. As they are escaping to the camp of the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons), Penny becomes distressed at leaving her animals, [Pretty Pig](wiki:Pretty_Pig) and the [dog](wiki:Dog) [Crunch](wiki:Crunch), and Tyrion lies to her and says Sweets will make sure they're taken care of.(wiki:Sweets.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-5)

Later in the Second Sons' camp, Penny says they had been better off with Yezzan, saying they were his favorites, like Sweets. To shock Penny out of her listlessness, Tyrion lies to her and tells her that Yezzan's special slaves are all dead from the pale mare, and that Sweets was the first to go.(wiki:Sweets.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) The fate of Sweets is unknown, although according to a semi-canon source, she did die of the pale mare.(wiki:Sweets.cite_ref-Rawoiafsweets.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
