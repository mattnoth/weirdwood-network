## Identity

Jhogo is a Dothraki warrior who serves as one of Daenerys Targaryen's khas, later named ko and bloodrider. He holds the titles of Ko and Bloodrider (track_b: Titles).

## Origins

Born between 281-283 AC in the Dothraki sea (track_b: Born). Of Dothraki culture (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Whip-thin with a faint shadow of first mustache on his upper lip, about sixteen years old during AGOT (agot-daenerys-08). Wields an arakh and a whip (agot-daenerys-08).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` Khal Drogo's khalasar, then `SWORN_TO` House Targaryen and `SWORN_TO` Queensguard (track_b: Allegiances). `CULTURE_OF` Dothraki (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Jhogo serves in Daenerys's khas from AGOT onward. He cracks his whip around Viserys's throat when Viserys assaults Dany (agot-daenerys-03). He captures the fleeing wine merchant who attempts to poison Dany, using his whip to trip the man (agot-daenerys-06). He assists Rakharo in killing Haggo when the bloodriders turn against Dany (agot-daenerys-08). He reports Eroeh's fate at the hands of Mago and Khal Jhaqo (agot-daenerys-09). Dany names him ko and gives him a silver-handled whip; he initially hesitates, saying it would shame him to be bloodrider to a woman, but kneels after the dragons hatch, declaring "Blood of my blood" (agot-daenerys-10). He continues to serve Daenerys through her campaigns in Slaver's Bay across ACOK, ASOS, and ADWD.
