## Origins

Husband and his wife Sharna were displaced during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). They had two sons, one of whom was killed by [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) men, whilst the other died of the flux, and they later adopted an orphaned [boy](wiki:Boy) whose mother had been killed by the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions). They came upon the Inn of the Kneeling Man and found the previous owners dead, so buried them and took over running the establishment.(wiki:Husband.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Husband meets [Brienne](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth), [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), and [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) when they come to the Inn of the Kneeling Man. After feeding them, he sells them the horses he has in his barn for three gold dragons and their skiff.(wiki:Husband.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

He is present when [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), who had been captured by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), reveals her true identity to [Harwin](wiki:Harwin).(wiki:Husband.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
