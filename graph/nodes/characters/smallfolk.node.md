## Origins

During the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood), many smallfolk in [Essos](wiki:Essos) fled before the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki).(wiki:Smallfolk.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_96-0)
[Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare) beggared lords and smallfolk during the construction of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Smallfolk.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_87-1)
Queen [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) was concerned for smallfolk and aided her brother, King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), in gaining their allegiance.(wiki:Smallfolk.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_70-1) Smallfolk turned against their son, [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen), after he wed his children, Princess [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) and Prince [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) to each other.(wiki:Smallfolk.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aenys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_88-1)
Tens of thousands of smallfolk died during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Smallfolk.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_red_kraken.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_97-0) King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) tried to feed the hungry and entertain the smallfolk.(wiki:Smallfolk.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_98-0)
Smallfolk loved King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen), although his generosity alarmed the [nobility](wiki:Nobility).(wiki:Smallfolk.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_82-1)
King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) was also beloved by the commoners, although most of his efforts to aid them with laws and reform were eventually reversed by the nobles.(wiki:Smallfolk.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_83-1)
