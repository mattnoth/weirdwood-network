## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Eladon Goldenhair was on patrol with another Unsullied, [Loyal Spear](wiki:Loyal_Spear), when the pair were [poisoned](wiki:Poison) at a [wineshop](wiki:Wine) that they usually visited each night on their rounds. The wineshop owner and his daughters were arrested — although they pleaded ignorance and begged for mercy, Daenerys ordered [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) to question them sharply.(wiki:Eladon_Goldenhair.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
