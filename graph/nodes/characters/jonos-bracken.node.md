## Origins

Jonos has five daughters from his three [marriages](wiki:Marriage), and a [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son, [Harry Rivers](wiki:Harry_Rivers). However, his rival, Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood), does not believe that Harry was fathered by Jonos.(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

## Appearances & Description

Jonos is heavier than Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), but not as tall. He has brown eyes, coarse brown hair (including on his back), and thick shoulders and arms.(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) He often glowers and blusters.(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

Lord Bracken wears a brown woolen tunic embroidered with the red [stallion](wiki:Stallion) of [House Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken). When ready for combat, he wears plate and mail and a grey [steel](wiki:Steel) [greathelm](wiki:Greathelm) with a [horsehair](wiki:Horse) crest.(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Before arresting [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) at the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn), Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) has [Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken) [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms) affirm that Jonos is loyal to her father, [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), the [Lord Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

After the death of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) decrees that Jonos and other [river lords](wiki:River_lords) swear fealty to the new king, his successor, [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) sends Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) to attack Jonos's castle, and the Mountain rapes one of Lord Bracken's daughters(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7) during the [burning of Stone Hedge](wiki:Burning_of_Stone_Hedge).(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

After the siege of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) is broken in the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), Jonos returns from ruined [Stone Hedge](wiki:Stone_Hedge). At [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s war council in the [Great Hall of Riverrun](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Riverrun), he sits as far away as possible from his rival, [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood), Lord of [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall). Jonos suggests allying with King [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) against King Joffrey and the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister), and surprisingly agrees with Tytos in denying Catelyn's suggestion of peace. Jonos joins the other river lords in declaring Robb to be [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North)(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) and [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Clash of Kings

During the [liberation of Stone Hedge](wiki:Liberation_of_Stone_Hedge), Lord Jonos is wounded and his nephew, [Hendry](wiki:Hendry_Bracken), is slain.(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Jonos agrees with his rival, Lord Blackwood, that Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) has devised a good plan to trap Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Jonos's [natural son](wiki:Bastardy), [Harry Rivers](wiki:Harry_Rivers), is revealed to have been killed by the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Jonos is cold toward Catelyn because she released Robb's captive, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Lord Bracken takes part in the launching of Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully)'s funeral boat with six other nobles at Riverrun.(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Tywin expects that Jonos will yield after the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) A dozen of Jonos's own kin are killed at the event.(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8) After Joffrey's death, the new king, [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), pardons Jonos for his part in the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Lord Jonos besieges [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall) on behalf of the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). After Jaime resolves the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Lord [Karyl Vance](wiki:Karyl_Vance) implores him to intervene at Raventree since Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood) will never surrender to Jonos.(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) arrives to end the [siege of Raventree](wiki:Siege_of_Raventree), finding Jonos [abed](wiki:Bedding) in his tent with a [common](wiki:Smallfolk) woman named [Hildy](wiki:Hildy). When Jaime inquires about Lady Bracken, Jones responds that she spends all her time praying after [Stone Hedge was burned](wiki:Burning_of_Stone_Hedge).(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-9)

Jonos hands Jaime a map with the lands he wants for subduing Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood), along with compensation for the damage Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) and the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) did to his land and family. Jaime points out Jonos has not subdued Tytos and that letting him keep his head after initially declaring for [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) seems recompense enough for the damage caused by Gregor, who is already dead. Jonos insists that he will keep faith with the crown as he did with the Starks.(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-10)

During his surrender to Jaime, Tytos agrees to surrender a smaller portion of Blackwood land than requested by Lord Bracken. Jonos is also unhappy that Jaime is taking one of Tytos's sons, [Hoster](wiki:Hoster_Blackwood), as a [hostage](wiki:Hostage) instead of his only daughter, [Bethany](wiki:Bethany_Blackwood). Jaime upsets Jonos by ordering him to send one of his own daughters to attend Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) at court in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Jonos_Bracken.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-11)
