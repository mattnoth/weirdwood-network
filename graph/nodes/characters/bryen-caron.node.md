## Origins

Lord Bryen was the father of [Bryce Caron](wiki:Bryce_Caron), a second, younger legitimate son, as well as a bastard son, [Rolland Storm](wiki:Rolland_Storm). He also had several daughters. He, along with his wife, second son, and all his daughters died of a chill in [289 AC](wiki:289_AC).(wiki:Bryen_Caron.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) Bryen's second son was twelve and had been engaged to [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) for two years at his time of death.(wiki:Bryen_Caron.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)
