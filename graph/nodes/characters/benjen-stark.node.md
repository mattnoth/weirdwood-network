## Identity

Benjen Stark — child of Rickard Stark and Lyarra Stark; sworn to House Stark; First Ranger. One of 6 characters named Benjen Stark.

## Origins

### Youth

Benjen was the youngest child of Lord [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark) and Lady [Lyarra Stark](wiki:Lyarra_Stark).(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_stark_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Benjen attended the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) with his siblings, [Brandon](wiki:Brandon_Stark), [Eddard](wiki:Eddard_Stark), and [Lyanna](wiki:Lyanna_Stark), in [281 AC](wiki:281_AC). At the tournament Lyanna rescued their father's vassal, [Howland Reed](wiki:Howland_Reed), from an attack by three [squires](wiki:Squire). Benjen offered Howland a [horse](wiki:Horse) and some [armor](wiki:Armor) so he could challenge his attackers. During the feast held to celebrate the start of the tournament, Benjen teased Lyanna when she cried for a sad and beautiful song performed by Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), a noted musician. Lyanna poured wine over Benjen's head for teasing her.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) When a black brother from the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) spoke up during the feast, attempting to convince the gathered people to join the black brothers,(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Benjen took the plea to heart.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Rawoiafbenjen_stark.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

The first time Benjen got well and truly drunk, he was younger than fourteen.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

### Night's Watch

Benjen was the Stark in [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). Although House Stark had been reduced to Benjen, Lord Eddard, Eddard's infant son [Robb](wiki:Robb_Stark), and Eddard's infant [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) due to the events of the war, Benjen joined the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) within a few months of Eddard's return north. His reasons for doing so are unknown.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-15) Benjen took his vows in front of a [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree),(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) presumably at the [weirwood grove](wiki:Weirwood_grove) located in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest) half a league from [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).

Benjen's skills served him well as a [ranger](wiki:Ranger),(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-17) and he quickly rose to the position of [First Ranger](wiki:First_Ranger).(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Rawoiafbenjen_stark.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) He has heard [direwolves](wiki:Direwolves) during rangings [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) The seasoned Benjen is familiar with the haunted forest(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) and considers [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome) to be cursed.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Like other rangers, Benjen is aware that [Craster](wiki:Craster) sacrifices [his sons](wiki:Craster's_sons).(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

Benjen visited Winterfell on several occasions over the years.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) During one visit, [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) asked his uncle about the mysterious [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort), but Benjen refused to confirm or deny its stories.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1) Benjen and Eddard had discussed repopulating the [Gift](wiki:Gift) by raising new lords, so they could function as a shield against the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings). Benjen was convinced that [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), could be convinced to part with the land, on the condition that taxes would be paid to Castle Black instead of Winterfell.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos76.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

Benjen has written his brother Eddard about the [military strength](wiki:Military_strength) of the Night's Watch.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) Since the manpower of the black brothers has declined over time, Benjen originated the tactic of sending larger forces to one of the abandoned castles along the [Wall](wiki:Wall) for a fortnight or a [moon](wiki:Moon)'s turn to make wildlings unsure of the Watch's strength.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) According to [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand), Benjen and Lord Commander Mormont have grown concerned about threats beyond the Wall and the return of winter.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Racok53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Benjen Stark](wiki:Category:Images_of_Benjen_Stark)*

The gaunt Benjen has sharp features and blue-grey eyes, in which a hint of laughter is often present. The thin [ranger](wiki:Ranger) has long legs.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Benjen dresses in black, as befits a man from the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). On formal occasions he wears rich velvet, high leather boots, a [silver](wiki:Silver) buckle on his wide belt, and a heavy silver chain.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Benjen is observant(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) and genial,(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) although he shares his brother [Eddard's](wiki:Eddard_Stark) dislike for [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) He enjoys [summerwine](wiki:Wine).(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) The [First Ranger](wiki:First_Ranger) is a likely successor to [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch).(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Rawoiafbenjen_stark.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

Ser [Denys Mallister](wiki:Denys_Mallister), Commander of the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower), thinks of Benjen as a great man,(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) and [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) considers his uncle to be a man of honor.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) refers to Benjen, who does not know of the [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall) by sight, as the bane of the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk).(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) rides for the [north](wiki:North), Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) sends word to Benjen on the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-1) During the [feast in honor of King Robert's visit to Winterfell](wiki:Feast_in_honor_of_King_Robert's_visit_to_Winterfell), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) impresses his uncle by reciting the names of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) castles along the Wall.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1) Also during the feast, Benjen speaks with Eddard's [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son, [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow). The youth rashly asks to be allowed to join the Watch, which results in an argument after Benjen states Jon is rather young. Jon angrily refuses Benjen's suggestion that he father some bastard sons of himself before committing to a life at the Wall.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) Nonetheless, Benjen approaches Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin) about Jon, informing the maester of Jon's question. This leads to Luwin informing Eddard, and eventually Eddard approves of Jon joining the Watch.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) Benjen is part of Robert's party when the king hunts for [boar](wiki:Boar) near [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) decides to join Benjen and Jon for the journey north so that he can see the Wall. Tyrion is accompanied by [Jyck](wiki:Jyck) and [Morrec](wiki:Morrec), and Jon brings [Ghost](wiki:Ghost), his [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf). They are joined in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) by [Yoren](wiki:Yoren), who has two recruits for the Watch. Benjen's party has twenty [horses](wiki:Horse) and a cage of [ravens](wiki:Raven) given to him by Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin).(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

Once back at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), Benjen is busy with his duties as [First Ranger](wiki:First_Ranger) and does not have much time for Jon. He reminds his nephew that the men of the Night's Watch leave their families and loyalties behind when they join the brotherhood.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

[Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), sends Benjen with six [rangers](wiki:Rangers)(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) to find Ser [Waymar Royce](wiki:Waymar_Royce), who has not returned from a ranging [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1) Benjen thinks they might have to search as far as the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower). Jon asks to accompany them, but Benjen refuses because of the youth's inexperience.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

When there is no word from Benjen, Ser [Jaremy Rykker](wiki:Jaremy_Rykker) leads two sweeps in search of him, while [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand) leads another search party going north from the Shadow Tower. Nothing is found except a few blazes in trees that Benjen used to mark his way. The marks stop in the stony highlands to the northwest and all trace of Benjen vanishes.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0) Jaremy takes over command of the rangers in Benjen's absence.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1)

The bodies of the rangers [Othor](wiki:Othor) and [Jafer Flowers](wiki:Jafer_Flowers), who had accompanied Benjen on his ranging, are later found in a [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) grove near the Wall. There is no trace of Benjen or any of the others, however.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) That night, the two corpses rise as [wights](wiki:Wights) at Castle Black and attempt to kill Lord Commander Mormont.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

[Dywen](wiki:Dywen) and [Hake](wiki:Hake) unsuccessfully search for Ben.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0) Jeor plans to find Benjen, either dead or alive, and makes it one of the goals of his [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging).(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0) When Jeor tells Jon about mysteries beyond the Wall, [Mormont's raven](wiki:Mormont's_raven) repeats the name "Ben Jen".(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-1)

### A Clash of Kings

Jeor rejects [Thoren Smallwood](wiki:Thoren_Smallwood)'s insistence that he is now [First Ranger](wiki:First_Ranger) with Benjen missing.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0) The great ranging finds wildling villages to be abandoned, and Jon and Lord Commander Mormont wonder if Benjen's disappearance is connected.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Racok13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)

When arriving at [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep), the Lord Commander asks [Craster](wiki:Craster) whether he had seen Benjen recently, as Benjen was supposed to have passed the keep during his ranging the previous year. Craster claims not to have seen Benjen in three years.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-2)

Jeor believes that Benjen cannot fail to notice the trail two hundred men from the Night's Watch and their horses leave behind during their great ranging, and is convinced that, if Benjen is still alive, he will be able to find his brothers at the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men).(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Racok34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0) When Jon finds an [old warhorn](wiki:Old_warhorn) and a cache of [dragonglass](wiki:Dragonglass) wrapped in an old cloak of the Night's Watch,(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Racok34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-1) he wonders if it had been hidden by his uncle.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)

Jon asks the captured [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte) if she knows anything about Benjen, but the wildling does not answer.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0)

### A Storm of Swords

[Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder), the [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall), tells Jon that one of his reasons for infiltrating King Robert's feast at Winterfell was to evaluate Benjen, the First Ranger.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne) accuses Jon of being a turncloak and ponders if the missing Benjen is conspiring with Mance.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

During the [choosing](wiki:Choosing) of a new Lord Commander after Jeor Mormont's death, [Othell Yarwyck](wiki:Othell_Yarwyck) mentions Jon's relation to Ben Stark among the reasons to support the youth. Jon is ultimately chosen by the black brothers.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

Benjen is presumed dead.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Black Jack Bulwer](wiki:Jack_Bulwer) has replaced Benjen as First Ranger.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0) Jon, now Lord Commander, includes his uncle among losses of the Night's Watch.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0) He still hopes that rangers will find some sign of Benjen, however.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0) After Jon arrange the [wedding of Sigorn and Alys Karstark](wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark), he wonders how his father and uncle would have handled the situation.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0)

[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) witnesses a vision from the past in which he sees a boy and a girl playing with wooden branches in the [godswood of Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0) Some readers believe this to have been Benjen and his older sister, [Lyanna](wiki:Lyanna_Stark), as Bran describes the girl as being the eldest of the two, and states that she looks like his sister [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark),(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0) who is said to look like Lyanna had.(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0) Additionally, the girl refers to [Old Nan](wiki:Old_Nan),(wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-1) placing the event Bran witnesses as having taken place in more recent generations.
