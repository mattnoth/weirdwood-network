## Identity

Benjen Stark, also known as Ben Stark, is the First Ranger of the Night's Watch and the younger brother of Lord Eddard Stark of Winterfell (wiki:Benjen_Stark).

## Origins

Born 267 AC at Winterfell. Youngest child of Lord Rickard Stark and Lady Lyarra Stark (track_b: Father, track_b: Mother). Siblings: Brandon, Eddard, and Lyanna Stark (wiki:Benjen_Stark).

## Appearances & Description

Gaunt with sharp features and blue-grey eyes, with a hint of laughter often present. Thin with long legs. Dresses in black. On formal occasions wears rich velvet, high leather boots, a silver buckle on his wide belt, and a heavy silver chain. Observant and genial, shares Eddard's dislike for House Lannister. Enjoys summerwine (wiki:Benjen_Stark).

## Narrative Arc

Benjen attended the tourney at Harrenhal with his siblings in 281 AC, where Lyanna rescued Howland Reed from squires and Benjen offered Howland a horse and armor (wiki:Benjen_Stark). He joined the Night's Watch at some point after Robert's Rebellion and rose to First Ranger. At the feast welcoming King Robert to Winterfell, Benjen sat with Jon Snow, and Jon expressed his desire to join the Watch (wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot6). Benjen departed on a ranging beyond the Wall shortly after arriving at Castle Black with Jon, and never returned (wiki:Benjen_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot19). His disappearance beyond the Wall is a recurring mystery; search parties were sent but found nothing. In ASOS, Coldhands appears north of the Wall, and some speculate a connection to Benjen, though this is unconfirmed in the books (Tier 2 — community speculation, not stated in text).
