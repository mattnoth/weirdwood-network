## Appearances & Description

He is lean and hard, with a white smile. He wears his hair drawn up in the shape of a [unicorn](wiki:Unicorn)'s horn on his brow. His *[tokar](wiki:Tokar)* is fringed with [golden](wiki:Gold) [Myrish](wiki:Myr) lace.(wiki:Grazdan_mo_Eraz.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Grazdan leads the [Yunkish](wiki:Yunkish) envoys to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) when she comes to conquer their city. On behalf of the city, he attempts to bribe her with gold and tries to convince her to pass over Yunkai, but is rebuffed and in turn given an ultimatum by Daenerys to let the slaves go by the third day.

After the crushing defeat of the Yunkish forces outside of their walls, Grazdan is sent to deliver Daenerys's ultimatum.

### A Dance with Dragons

Grazdan mo Eraz travels to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) to persuade the [Triarchs](wiki:Triarch_of_Volantis) to send their fleet against [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen). His bribes and feasts are said to be lavish, he brought slave girls and boys trained in Yunkai in the [way of the seven sighs](wiki:Way_of_the_seven_sighs) to pleasure the ruling class of Volantis.

While in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) he spreads many false stories about Daenerys, that she is blood thirsty, feeds her [dragons](wiki:Dragon) on the flesh of newborn babies, impales anyone who defies her on spikes to die, takes a different lover every night and bathes in blood of maidens to retain her youth.(wiki:Grazdan_mo_Eraz.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
