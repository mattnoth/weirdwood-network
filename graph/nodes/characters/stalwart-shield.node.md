## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Stalwart Shield would occasionally go to a brothel in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) where he would pay women just to lie with him and hold him. On one such visit, he was attacked by the [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy), who killed him. He is the first of Daenerys' fighting men to be murdered by the Sons. His corpse is shown to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), who begins an investigation and orders that none of her men are to walk alone at night.(wiki:Stalwart_Shield.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

A company of freedmen named the [Stalwart Shields](wiki:Stalwart_Shields) is formed.(wiki:Stalwart_Shield.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
