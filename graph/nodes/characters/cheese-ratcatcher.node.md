## Origins

Cheese was a ratcatcher in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), and he also spent time in [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom).(wiki:Cheese_(ratcatcher).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
After Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) killed Prince [Lucerys Velaryon](wiki:Lucerys_Velaryon) in a [fight](wiki:Fight_above_Shipbreaker_Bay) above [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) wanted vengeance against the [greens](wiki:Greens). Cheese and the swordsman called [Blood](wiki:Blood_(butcher)) were employed by [Mysaria](wiki:Mysaria), Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen)'s [spymaster](wiki:Spymaster), to slay one of the sons of Aemond's older brother, King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Cheese_(ratcatcher).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
Cheese led Blood into the Red Keep through a forgotten passageway, and creeping through walls, they entered the chambers of Aegon's mother, Queen [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower), in the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand). After Cheese bound and gagged the Dowager Queen and Blood strangled her bedmaid, the assassins waited for Aegon's wife, Queen [Helaena Targaryen](wiki:Helaena_Targaryen), and her children to arrive.(wiki:Cheese_(ratcatcher).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
Blood killed Helaena's guardsman once she arrived, and Cheese grabbed her youngest son, Prince [Maelor Targaryen](wiki:Maelor_Targaryen). Cheese asked Helaena which of her sons she wanted them to kill. Helaena offered herself but Blood refused, stating it had to be a son. Although Helaena eventually named her youngest son, Maelor, Blood beheaded Prince [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_II)) instead. Blood and Cheese then fled through the secret passages with the prince's head in hand.(wiki:Cheese_(ratcatcher).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
While Blood was captured at the [Gate of the Gods](wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods) and tortured to death, no trace of Cheese was found by the [gold cloaks](wiki:Gold_cloaks) of Ser [Luthor Largent](wiki:Luthor_Largent). In his fury, Aegon II ordered all of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing)'s ratcatchers to be hanged. Afterward, Ser [Otto Hightower](wiki:Otto_Hightower) brought one hundred [cats](wiki:Cat) into the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) to replace them.(wiki:Cheese_(ratcatcher).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) Aegon II wanted to attack [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) to avenge his son's murder, but Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) instead suggested sending Ser [Arryk Cargyll](wiki:Arryk_Cargyll) to infiltrate the castle.(wiki:Cheese_(ratcatcher).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
