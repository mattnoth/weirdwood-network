## Identity

Dorcas is a servant of House Lannister in King's Landing (wiki:Dorcas).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Lannister (track_b: Allegiance).

## Appearances & Description

Dorcas is a big woman. Cersei Lannister thinks she is stronger than Senelle, though also clumsier (wiki:Dorcas, cite_ref-Raffc17).

## Narrative Arc

After Cersei gives Senelle to Qyburn for his experiments, Dorcas becomes Cersei's chief handmaid. She is present when three lowborn men present the head of a dwarf to Cersei, intending to pass it as Tyrion's head. Dorcas blushes when Cersei orders her to fetch Ser Osney Kettleblack — Lady Taena Merryweather notes Dorcas fancies Osney and likes his scars (wiki:Dorcas, cite_ref-Raffc17). Dorcas also makes a toy mouse from scraps of fur tied to a fishing pole to amuse King Tommen's kittens (wiki:Dorcas, cite_ref-Raffc43).
