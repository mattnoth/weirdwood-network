## Appearances & Description

Dorcas is a big woman. [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) thinks she is stronger than [Senelle](wiki:Senelle), though also clumsier than her.(wiki:Dorcas.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

After Cersei gives Senelle to [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn) to be a subject in his experiments, Dorcas becomes Cersei's chief [handmaid](wiki:Handmaid). She is present when three lowborn men present to the Cersei the head of a [dwarf](wiki:Dwarf), intending to pass it as the head of [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister). After seeing the rotting head crawling with maggots, Dorcas gasps.(wiki:Dorcas.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Dorcas blushes when Cersei orders her to fetch Ser [Osney Kettleblack](wiki:Osney_Kettleblack). Lady [Taena Merryweather](wiki:Taena_Merryweather) asks Cersei why Dorcas reacted that way, and Cersei replies that Dorcas fancies Osney, and that she believes Dorcas likes his scars. Taena agrees that scars make a man look dangerous, and that danger excites women.(wiki:Dorcas.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

To amuse young King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_I_Baratheon)'s [kittens](wiki:Kitten), Dorcas makes a toy [mouse](wiki:Mouse) from scraps of fur, tied to a fishing pole.(wiki:Dorcas.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
