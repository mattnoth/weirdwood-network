## Origins

Born in [44 AC](wiki:44_AC) to Lord [Jordan Towers](wiki:Jordan_Towers), Maegor was [named after](wiki:Named_after) King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen),(wiki:Maegor_Towers.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) who had granted his grandsire, Ser [Walton Towers](wiki:Walton_Towers), the lordship of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) that same year. Lord Jordan was a staunch supporter of the king and one of the last [lords](wiki:Lord) of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) who remained loyal to Maegor when the great lords of the realm flocked to Prince [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Maegor_Towers.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Lord Jordan was one of the few lords that remained at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) to turn it over to Jaehaerys,(wiki:Maegor_Towers.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) and he was pardoned in exchange for certain holdings and for sending the young Maegor to court to serve as a [hostage](wiki:Hostage).(wiki:Maegor_Towers.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

As his elder brothers died in King Maegor's wars, the young Maegor became [Lord of Harrenhal](wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal) after his father passed away from a chest congestion.(wiki:Maegor_Towers.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) When King Jaehaerys began a [royal progress](wiki:Royal_progress) in [53 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) with his [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor) to celebrate the new year, his first stop was to see the new Lord of Harrenhal, then only nine years of age.(wiki:Maegor_Towers.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

In a castle built to house thousands, Maegor was an impoverished lord who resided in the [Tower of Dread](wiki:Tower_of_Dread) with only a cook and three elderly [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms). Since the rest of Harrenhal was empty, King Jaehaerys settled his widowed sister, Princess [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)), in the [Widow's Tower](wiki:Widow's_Tower) in [56 AC](wiki:56_AC). Maegor and Rhaena eventually became friends, and she cared for his servants after Maegor passed away in [61 AC](wiki:61_AC).(wiki:Maegor_Towers.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) As Maegor was last of his line with no heirs,(wiki:Maegor_Towers.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Harrenhal was granted to [House Strong](wiki:House_Strong) after Rhaena passed away in [73 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Maegor_Towers.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)
