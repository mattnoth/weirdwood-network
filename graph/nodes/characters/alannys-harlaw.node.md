## Identity

Alannys Harlaw is the wife of Lord Balon Greyjoy and mother of Rodrik, Maron, Asha, and Theon Greyjoy. She is of House Harlaw (wiki:Alannys_Harlaw).

## Origins

Born on Harlaw (track_b: Born). Daughter of a Lord Harlaw (track_b: Father).

## Narrative Arc

Alannys suffered greatly from the deaths of her eldest sons Rodrik and Maron during Balon's first rebellion and the loss of Theon as a hostage to Eddard Stark. By the time of ACOK, she has retreated to her family's seat at Ten Towers on Harlaw, her mind weakened by grief. She reportedly wanders the halls asking for her sons (wiki:Alannys_Harlaw.cite_ref-Racok11). Asha visits her mother and finds her diminished and confused (wiki:Alannys_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11).
