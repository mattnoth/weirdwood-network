## Identity

Duncan Targaryen, known as "Duncan the Small" or the "Prince of Dragonflies," was a prince of House Targaryen who renounced his claim to the Iron Throne to marry Jenny of Oldstones (track_b: Title, qualifier: renounced).

## Origins

Born between 220 and 224 AC (track_b: Born). Son of King Aegon V Targaryen and Betha Blackwood (track_b: Father, Mother). Of Valyrian race and Crownlands culture (track_b: Race, Culture).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiance). `SPOUSE_OF` Jenny of Oldstones (track_b: Spouse).

## Narrative Arc

Duncan was the eldest son of Aegon V and heir to the Iron Throne as Prince of Dragonstone. He renounced his title to marry Jenny of Oldstones, a woman of common birth. He died in 259 AC at Summerhall (track_b: Died), during the tragedy that also claimed his father's life.
