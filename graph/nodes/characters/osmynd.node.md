## Origins

Septon Osmynd instructed Catelyn Tully in the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) when she was a girl. He taught her that the seven-faced-god was a single deity with seven aspects, just as a [sept](wiki:Sept) was a single building with seven walls. He told Catelyn that, though seven different aspects of the Seven existed, each embodied all of the Seven.(wiki:Osmynd.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

By [299 AC](wiki:299_AC), Septon Osmynd was long-dead.(wiki:Osmynd.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Appearances & Description

When Septon Osmynd sang, he had a thin, quavering voice. Catelyn believed he would have listened patiently to her troubles about the murder of [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) and perhaps even known both what she'd witnessed meant and what to do about the [shadow](wiki:Shadow_child) that now haunted her [dreams](wiki:Dreams_and_prophecies). To the young Catelyn, Osmynd always seemed to know everything.(wiki:Osmynd.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Catelyn recalls her lessons with Septon Osmynd while praying at sept in a village near [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End).(wiki:Osmynd.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Osmynd's former post in the sept at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) is now filled by an earnest young man close in age to [Ser](wiki:Knight) [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) who is unfamiliar to Catelyn. Though he has a rich and pleasant singing voice, Catelyn finds herself yearning for the thin, quavering voice of Osmynd. She imagines she could confide in him her troubles about the murder of Renly Baratheon, and Osmynd would patiently listen.(wiki:Osmynd.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
