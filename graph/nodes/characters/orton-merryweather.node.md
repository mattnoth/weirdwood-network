## Origins

Orton is the grandson of Lord [Owen Merryweather](wiki:Owen_Merryweather),(wiki:Orton_Merryweather.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) a [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) who was exiled from [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) for failing to contain [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Orton_Merryweather.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Orton went into exile with his grandfather where he found a [Myrish](wiki:Myr) bride, [Taena](wiki:Taena_of_Myr).(wiki:Orton_Merryweather.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Orton_Merryweather.cite_ref-Rawoiaforton_merryweather.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

During his reign,(wiki:Orton_Merryweather.cite_ref-7) King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) was persuaded by Orton to restore the lands, castle and titles stripped by Aerys to the Merryweathers. Robert did not grant them all of their lands back, however, and also emptied the family treasury, leaving Orton a less wealthy and powerful lord than his grandfather had been.(wiki:Orton_Merryweather.cite_ref-Rawoiaflongtable.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Orton_Merryweather.cite_ref-Rawoiaforton_merryweather.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Orton_Merryweather.cite_ref-9)(wiki:Orton_Merryweather.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Appearances & Description

Orton has a large, lumpy and bulbous nose with messy orange-red hair. He is always courteous(wiki:Orton_Merryweather.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and can be sycophantic.(wiki:Orton_Merryweather.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Orton_Merryweather.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Orton is among the lords who travel to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) when [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) is promised to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). He brings his wife, Lady [Taena of Myr](wiki:Taena_of_Myr).(wiki:Orton_Merryweather.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Lord Merryweather dances with [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) at [her wedding feast](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark).(wiki:Orton_Merryweather.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Joffrey chokes to death during his [royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding).(wiki:Orton_Merryweather.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Taena falsely testifies that she saw [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) drop something into the [wedding chalice](wiki:Wedding_chalice) during the feast.(wiki:Orton_Merryweather.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Feast for Crows

[Queen Regent](wiki:Regent) [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) names Lord Orton to the [small council](wiki:Small_council) of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) as [justiciar](wiki:Justiciar). In their first session, he counsels making cause with the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) to make up for the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne)'s lack of sea power after the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), and granting them part of the [north](wiki:North) despite [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton)'s dominion there. Orton further suggests turning on the ironmen if the Boltons defeat [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), though ultimately Cersei elects to build [her own fleet](wiki:Cersei's_dromonds).(wiki:Orton_Merryweather.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) thinks that Orton convinced Cersei to name Ser [Bonifer Hasty](wiki:Bonifer_Hasty) as castellan of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Orton_Merryweather.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

Orton's wife Taena becomes Cersei's confidant and often sleeps in the queen's bed.(wiki:Orton_Merryweather.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Taena conspires with Cersei against Queen Margaery, the new bride of the regent's son, King Tommen. When Cersei decides to have the Merryweathers sup with her in the middle of the night, a pale-faced Orton drinks heavily and steals glances at the singer performing for them, the [Blue Bard](wiki:Blue_Bard), whom Cersei is planning to frame. Cersei raises Orton to be the new [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) after she demotes Ser [Harys Swyft](wiki:Harys_Swyft) to [lord treasurer](wiki:Master_of_coin). Cersei falsely accuses the Blue Bard of having slept with Margaery, and she orders Orton to have the singer taken to the dungeons.(wiki:Orton_Merryweather.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Lord Merryweather worries what the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) will think after Margaery is imprisoned by the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven). After Cersei is herself imprisoned by the Faith, Lord Orton resigns his position and flees back to [Longtable](wiki:Longtable) with Taena.(wiki:Orton_Merryweather.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
