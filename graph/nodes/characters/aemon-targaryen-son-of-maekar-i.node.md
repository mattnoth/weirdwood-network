## Identity

Maester Aemon, born Aemon Targaryen, was the third son of King Maekar I Targaryen and his wife Dyanna Dayne. He served as maester to the Night's Watch at Castle Black for so long that the rest of the Seven Kingdoms forgot about his Targaryen blood (wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)).

## Origins

Born in 198 AC in King's Landing (track_b: Born). Son of Maekar I Targaryen and Dyanna Dayne (track_b: Father, Mother). Died in 300 AC aged 102 aboard the Cinnamon Wind, off the coast of Dorne in the Summer Sea (track_b: Died).

## Appearances & Description

By the time of the main series, Aemon is over 100 years old, blind, frail, and bald. Despite his advanced age, he remains sharp-witted and perceptive (wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)).

## Narrative Arc

Aemon forged his maester's chain at the Citadel and served at Dragonstone before the death of his brother King Aegon V. He was offered the Iron Throne but refused, ceding it to his younger brother Aegon. He took the black to remove himself from political machinations. At Castle Black, he served as maester for decades and became a mentor to Jon Snow, revealing his Targaryen identity. In AFFC, Samwell Tarly accompanies the dying Aemon by ship toward Oldtown. Aemon dies at sea, still mourning the knowledge that Daenerys Targaryen has hatched dragons — calling her "the prince that was promised" (wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)).
