## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

In order to gain information about [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) and the [Triarchs](wiki:Triarch) intentions towards [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), [Haldon](wiki:Haldon) has [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) play [cyvasse](wiki:Cyvasse) against Qavo.(wiki:Qavo_Nogarys.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

While playing Qavo reveals much of the Triarchs' intentions and the mood of the Volantene citizenry. He even repeats some of the (false) rumours the Yunkai envoy [Grazdan mo Eraz](wiki:Grazdan_mo_Eraz) has been spreading about Queen Daenerys Targaryen.(wiki:Qavo_Nogarys.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
