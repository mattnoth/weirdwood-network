## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Harmen and his brother [Ulwyck](wiki:Ulwyck_Uller) are among nobles who accompany Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for the wedding of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) to Lady [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Harmen_Uller.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) intends to crown Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) as the Queen of the Seven Kingdoms at Harmen's castle, [Hellholt](wiki:Hellholt), but her plan fails when her group is intercepted by [her father's](wiki:Doran_Martell) guards.(wiki:Harmen_Uller.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) While she is a captive, Arianne considers contacting Lord Harmen for aid, considering him strong enough to challenge her father. However, she is worried that doing so might cause even more bloodshed, something which she wants to avoid and thus eventually decides against involving Harmen further.(wiki:Harmen_Uller.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Harmen, his brother Ulwyck, and his [bastard](wiki:Bastard) daughter [Ellaria Sand](wiki:Ellaria_Sand) are present at the feast held at [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) for the arrival of Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) with the skull of Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane). Both Harmen and his brother refuse to drink when [Ricasso](wiki:Ricasso) raises a toast to King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Harmen_Uller.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
