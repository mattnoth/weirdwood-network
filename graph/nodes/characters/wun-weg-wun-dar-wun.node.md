## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Wun Weg Wun Dar Wun](wiki:Category:Images_of_Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun)*

Wun Wun is at least fourteen feet tall, even larger than [Mag the Mighty](wiki:Mag_Mar_Tun_Doh_Weg), and he possesses the strength of twelve men.(wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) He has hands as large as hams(wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) and big square teeth.(wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Wun Wun is a vegetarian who enjoys roots, onions, turnips, and raw neeps,(wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) and he also likes roast vegetables.(wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) A speaker of the [Old Tongue](wiki:Old_Tongue),(wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) the giant's voice sounds like a tumbling boulder and his laughter is a roar.(wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Whilst typically passive, Wun Wun will lash out if provoked.(wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)(wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) His oaken maul is six feet long with a stone head the size of a loaf of bread.(wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Along with some [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings), Wun Weg Wun Dar Wun is discovered when [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), leads new recruits to the [weirwood grove](wiki:Weirwood_grove) in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest). With the help of some diplomacy by the new wildling recruit [Leathers](wiki:Leathers), they avoid a fight and manage to convince the wildlings to return with them to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) Leathers calls the giant Wun Wun.(wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

Jon speaks with Wun Wun as often as he can, with the help of Leathers, learning more about giants and their stories.(wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

Wun Wun had never tasted [wine](wiki:Wine) before he came to Castle Black, but he develops a liking for it.(wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) The giant sleeps in the entry hall of [Hardin's Tower](wiki:Hardin's_Tower), and he guards [Val](wiki:Val) when she moves into the tower.(wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

When Queen [Selyse](wiki:Selyse_Florent) arrives at Castle Black, Wun Wun kneels in front of her and Princess [Shireen](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon), who is fascinated by the giant. Wun Wun is distracted by [Patchface](wiki:Patchface), who jumps away and falls over into a snowdrift. Wun Wun laughs loudly, which causes Ser [Patrek of King's Mountain](wiki:Patrek_of_King's_Mountain) to draw his sword.(wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) Selyse and the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) are displeased by the giant's presence.(wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

After Jon receives [a letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) from [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), he hears Wun Wun roar and a man scream. When he arrives at Hardin's Tower, Jon finds a bleeding Wun Wun smashing a dead Patrek against a tower wall. While he is distracted by Wun Wun's rage, the men of the Watch turn on Jon and stab him repeatedly, chanting "For the Watch" as they attempt to assassinate him.(wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
