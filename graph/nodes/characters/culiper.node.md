## Identity

Culiper — Aged Maester of Dragonstone, eighty and serving since Visenya's day.

## Origins

Culiper served at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) since Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen)'s day. By [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), he was being assisted by two younger maesters.(wiki:Culiper.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Culiper.cite_ref-4)

Culiper wrote about King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), during the king's time on Dragonstone during his minority.(wiki:Culiper.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) He later wrote to the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) about [Androw Farman](wiki:Androw_Farman), the husband of Queen [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)).(wiki:Culiper.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

In [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), when a sickness struck Dragonstone, Culiper ruled out the sweating sickness, the shaking sickness, and [greyscale](wiki:Greyscale), and informed Rhaena that a number of diseases were suspect. Culiper was the first to die of the [illness](wiki:Illness), two days after he first felt ill. After Culiper's death, all the female close friends of Queen Rhaena died of the same illness. Once the symptoms of the illness were described to the [small council](wiki:Small_council) of King Jaehaerys by Maester [Anselm](wiki:Anselm), Culiper's successor, Lord [Rego Draz](wiki:Rego_Draz) recognized it as the symptoms of the [tears of Lys](wiki:Tears_of_Lys), a strong [poison](wiki:Poison). Draz hypothesized that Culiper had been poisoned first because he would have recognized the signs of the poison. Queen Rhaena's husband, Androw Farman, later admits that he had poisoned Culiper and the others, as revenge on Rhaena and her friends, who had belittled him.(wiki:Culiper.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
