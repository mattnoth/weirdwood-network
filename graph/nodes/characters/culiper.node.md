## Identity

Culiper was a maester of the Citadel who served House Targaryen at Dragonstone during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen (wiki:Culiper).

## Origins

Born between 36 and 31 BC (track_b: Born).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` Citadel (track_b: Allegiances). `SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiances).

## Narrative Arc

Culiper served at Dragonstone since Queen Visenya Targaryen's day. By 50 AC he was being assisted by two younger maesters. He wrote about King Jaehaerys I during the king's minority on Dragonstone, and later wrote to the Citadel about Androw Farman. In 54 AC, when sickness struck Dragonstone, Culiper ruled out the sweating sickness, the shaking sickness, and greyscale. He was the first to die of the illness, two days after he first felt ill (wiki:Culiper).
