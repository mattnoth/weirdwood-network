## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Squint is among [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy)'s force that participates in the [harrying of the Stony Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore) and the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell). While he and [Drennan](wiki:Drennan) are on guard duty at the [Hunter's Gate](wiki:Hunter's_Gate), Squint is killed on the wallwalk by the [direwolves](wiki:Direwolves) [Summer](wiki:Summer) and [Shaggydog](wiki:Shaggydog). Since Squint's body is found in the moat, Theon conjectures that [Osha](wiki:Osha) pushed his remains into the water.(wiki:Squint.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
