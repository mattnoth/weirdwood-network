## Origins

Rolley comes from [Sisterton](wiki:Sisterton) on [Sweetsister](wiki:Sweetsister), the main port of the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters).

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Rolley is part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), the Night's Watch force that leaves [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) and the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) in search of [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark) and [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder).

[Lark](wiki:Lark) and his cousins, such as Rolley,(wiki:Rolley_of_Sisterton.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) conspire with [Chett](wiki:Chett) against Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), but the conspiracy is halted when [wights](wiki:Wights) attack the Night's Watch.(wiki:Rolley_of_Sisterton.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Rolley is among the survivors of the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist) who make it back to [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep). He is among the men who [mutiny at Craster's](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep), but breaks his neck when he falls out of the loft while trying to get at [Craster](wiki:Craster)'s wives.(wiki:Rolley_of_Sisterton.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
