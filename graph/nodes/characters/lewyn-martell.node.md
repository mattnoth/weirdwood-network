## Identity

Prince Lewyn Nymeros Martell, also known as Lewyn of Dorne, was a knight from House Martell who served as a member of Aerys II Targaryen's Kingsguard. He was the uncle of the current Prince of Dorne, Doran Martell — the younger brother of the former ruling Princess of Dorne (wiki:Lewyn_Martell).

## Narrative Arc

Prince Lewyn accompanied his niece Elia Martell to court at King's Landing when she married Prince Rhaegar Targaryen. He became a confidante of Rhaegar's. During Robert's Rebellion, Aerys used Elia as a hostage against Lewyn and sent him to command ten thousand Dornishmen joining Rhaegar's forces. Lewyn died at the Battle of the Trident in 283 AC at the hands of Ser Lyn Corbray, though he was already mortally wounded before their final duel (wiki:Lewyn_Martell). His death, along with Elia's murder, incensed Dorne and drove Prince Oberyn to attempt raising the kingdom for Viserys Targaryen.
