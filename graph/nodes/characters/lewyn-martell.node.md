## Origins

Prince Lewyn was the younger brother of the former ruling [Princess of Dorne](wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran)). Lewyn was among the [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) who came to court at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with his niece, Princess [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell),(wiki:Lewyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf51.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) who was betrothed in early [279 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) and [married](wiki:Married) a year later in [280 AC](wiki:280_AC), to Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen).(wiki:Lewyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf417.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He became a confidante of Rhaegar's.(wiki:Lewyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf51.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Lewyn was appointed a member of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) in the service of King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) at an unknown time.

Lewyn was present at the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) in [281 AC](wiki:281_AC).(wiki:Lewyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Aerys used Lewyn's niece, Elia, as a hostage against him, and sent him to command the ten thousand Dornishmen that were to join Rhaegar's force that was assembling to take on Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Lewyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Lewyn died during the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident) in [283 AC](wiki:283_AC) at the hands of Ser [Lyn Corbray](wiki:Lyn_Corbray), although he was mortally wounded before his final duel with Lyn.(wiki:Lewyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

[Dorne](wiki:Dorne) was incensed by the deaths of Lewyn and Elia during the war, and Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) tried to raise the kingdom to support [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen), the surviving son of the late Aerys. In response, Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) travelled to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) the year after Robert took the throne ([284 AC](wiki:284_AC)) to return the bones of Lewyn and was able to broker a peace with [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), the ruling Prince of Dorne.(wiki:Lewyn_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

[Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) recalls her great-uncle fondly, remembering when she was a little girl how her great-uncle Lewyn would tower over her and tickle her till she could barely breathe from laughter.(wiki:Lewyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

It was a closely held secret that Prince Lewyn kept a [paramour](wiki:Paramour) despite the Kingsguard oaths of chastity.(wiki:Lewyn_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) and his other sworn brothers were aware of this but kept their sworn brother's secret.(wiki:Lewyn_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
