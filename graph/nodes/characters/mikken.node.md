## Identity

Mikken is the blacksmith at Winterfell, serving House Stark (wiki:Mikken).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Mikken crafts the sword Needle for Jon Snow, who gifts it to Arya Stark (wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Ragot10). Eddard later recognizes Mikken's maker's mark on Needle (wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Ragot53). In ACOK, Mikken installs metal bars along the walls so crippled Bran can pull himself around his room (wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Racok4). Jojen Reed foresees Mikken's death in a green dream — drowning when the sea flows over Winterfell's walls — but Mikken laughs it off (wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Racok35). When Theon Greyjoy captures Winterfell, Mikken angrily refuses to bend the knee and is killed by Stygg, who drives a spear through his neck (wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Racok46). In ADWD, Theon wanders through the ruins of Mikken's forge (wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Radwd51).
