## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Mikken crafts for [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) the sword [Needle](wiki:Needle), which Jon gifts to his half-sister [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark).(wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Ragot10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

While Arya is in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), her father, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), discovers Needle and recognizes Mikken's maker's mark.(wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

[Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) is upset, and his [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) [Shaggydog](wiki:Shaggydog) bites Mikken, tearing a chunk of flesh from his thigh.(wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Clash of Kings

In order for [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) to get around his room, Mikken installs metal bars along the walls so Bran can pull himself around.(wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

[Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed) foresees the death of Mikken in his [green dreams](wiki:Green_dream), seeing him drowned when the sea flows over Winterfell's walls, but Mikken laughs it off when Bran tells him.(wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

When [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) [captures Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell), Mikken angrily refuses to [bend the knee](wiki:Kneeler), and is beaten by [Stygg](wiki:Stygg), an [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) raider in Theon's service. After Mikken insults Theon and the ironborn, Stygg drives his spear through Mikken's neck.(wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Theon has a nightmare of eating with the dead at Winterfell, including Mikken.(wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

When Bran and his companions leave Winterfell after [its sack](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell), [Osha](wiki:Osha) carries the [sword](wiki:Sword) from Eddard's [tomb](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell), one of the last swords to bear Mikken's mark.(wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Theon, who has been captured and [tortured](wiki:Torture) by [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), wanders through the destroyed structures of Winterfell, including the ruins of Mikken's forge.(wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Theon Greyjoy and [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole) escape Winterfell, and are discovered by [Mors Umber](wiki:Mors_Umber). Crowfood asks Jeyne questions about the castle to confirm her assumed identity of "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)", such as the names of servants, including [the cook](wiki:Gage) and the smith. Jeyne has a little trouble recalling Mikken's name, frustrating Theon, but is soon able to answer these questions to Mors's satisfaction.(wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-TWOW_11-0)
