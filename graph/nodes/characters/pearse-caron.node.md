## Origins

In [206 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Lord Pearse and Lord [Dondarrion](wiki:Lord_Dondarrion_(father_of_Manfred)) raised a host and defeated the [Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Daeron_II)), burning him out of the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains).(wiki:Pearse_Caron.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)(wiki:Pearse_Caron.cite_ref-Rawoiafpearse_caron.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Lord Pearse participated in the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC), challenging Lord [Leo Tyrell](wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(Longthorn)) in the second tilt. They fought afoot with blunted longaxes, but Pearse was defeated after his axehead caught in Leo's shield. After Ser [Humfrey Hardyng](wiki:Humfrey_Hardyng)'s injury, [Lord Ashford](wiki:Lord_Ashford) considered naming Pearse as a replacement champion, though Prince [Baelor Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)) did not find it seemly to remove Hardyng as a champion. When Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) searched for a seventh knight for his [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven), Pearse ignored his call for help, whispering a comment to Lord [Gawen Swann](wiki:Gawen_Swann).(wiki:Pearse_Caron.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

## Appearances & Description

Pearse was a renowned singer and [harper](wiki:Harp), and a skilled [tourney](wiki:Tourney) [knight](wiki:Knight). He was as skilled with the high harp as he was with a lance. He blazoned his shield with a silver harp on yellow, though his surcoat was patterned with the [nightingales](wiki:Nightingale) of [House Caron](wiki:House_Caron).(wiki:Pearse_Caron.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
