## Identity

Princess Daella Targaryen was the eighth-born child of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen and Queen Alysanne Targaryen (wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)).

## Origins

Born in 64 AC (track_b: Born). Daughter of Jaehaerys I Targaryen (track_b: Father) and Alysanne Targaryen (track_b: Mother).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiances). `SWORN_TO` House Arryn (track_b: Allegiances).

## Appearances & Description

Daella was delicate and small of stature (wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)).

## Narrative Arc

Daella married Lord Rodrik Arryn and had one child, Aemma Arryn, who would later become Queen to Viserys I Targaryen. Daella died in 82 AC aged 18 at the Eyrie and was buried on Dragonstone (track_b: Died, Buried).
