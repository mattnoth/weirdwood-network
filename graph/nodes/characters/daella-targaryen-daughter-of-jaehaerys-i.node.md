## Identity

Daella Targaryen (daughter of Jaehaerys I) (64–82 AC) — child of Jaehaerys I Targaryen and Alysanne Targaryen; married to Rodrik Arryn; sworn to House Targaryen; Princess. One of 2 Daella Targaryens — see the Daella Targaryen disambiguation entry.

## Origins

### Early life

Daella was born in [64 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) She was a shy and frightened girl, who considered her sister [Maegelle](wiki:Maegelle_Targaryen) as her "guiding star". When she was young, Daella had a kitten whom she loved, but after it scratched her she did not dare to go near cats anymore.(wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

Daella was the victim of some of the pranks of her younger sister, Princess [Saera](wiki:Saera_Targaryen). Saera always sneaked cats into Daella's bedchamber, knowing that Daella was afraid of them, and she once filled Daella's chamberpot with bees.(wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)

King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) assumed that Daella would one day marry her brother, Prince [Vaegon](wiki:Vaegon_Targaryen), who was only one year older than she was. However, as time passed it became clear that the two children did not like one another. Vaegon tolerated Daella's presence, but no more, and considered her to be stupid. Daella, on the other hand, was frightened of Vaegon, and considered him to be mean. Although Jaehaerys believed that the two children would eventually warm to one another, in truth their dislike only increased. In [73 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), one of Alysanne's [lady companions](wiki:Lady_companion) asked Daella and Vaegon when they would be wed. Vaegon's public reaction was a grave insult to Daella, insisting that she needed to find a husband who wanted stupid children, as those were the only kind she would be able to birth. After Daella fled the hall in tears, followed by her mother, their sister Alyssa emptied a flagon of [wine](wiki:Wine) over Vaegon's head. Following this incident, Jaehaerys and Alysanne agreed that they would need to find other spouses for their children.(wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)

Finding a husband for Daella proved a difficult task for Queen Alysanne. At the age of thirteen, Daella traveled to [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark) to meet [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon). However, Daella became seasick when she crossed [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay), and after her return home, complained that Corlys had seemed to like his boats better than he liked her. At the age of fourteen, Daella kept company with several promising [squires](wiki:Squire) of a similar age: [Denys Swann](wiki:Denys_Swann), [Simon Staunton](wiki:Simon_Staunton_(squire)), [Gerold Templeton](wiki:Gerold_Templeton), and [Ellard Crane](wiki:Ellard_Crane). By the end of the year, after Simon attempted to get her to drink [wine](wiki:Wine) and Crane had kissed her on the lips, Daella hated them all.(wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-11)

At the age of fifteen, Daella traveled by [wheelhouse](wiki:Wheelhouse) with Queen Alysanne to the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) where [Royce Blackwood](wiki:Royce_Blackwood), the son of Lord Blackwood, paid court to her. Daella was smitten with the boy, who would sing ballads he had composed for her, and Alysanne and Lord Blackwood began to discuss plans for the wedding. However, when Daella learned that [House Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood) did not follow the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) but instead worshiped the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods), and that she would be expected to wed in front of a [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood), she was horrified. The betrothal subsequently did not happen.(wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-12)

On the first day of [80 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), King Jaehaerys told Queen Alysanne that he wanted Daella wed before the year was over, declaring he no longer cared to whom she was wed. Alysanne, worried that Daella would not accept a husband, inquired what would happen to her if she failed the deadline, as joining the Faith was not an option, since they did not accept a girl that could not read the prayers. In response, Jaehaerys proposed the [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters), imploring Alysanne not to let it come to that.(wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-13)

Alysanne explained to Daella what Jaehaerys had commanded, and allowed her to choose between three men: [Boremund Baratheon](wiki:Boremund_Baratheon), the [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End), Ser [Tymond Lannister](wiki:Tymond_Lannister), the heir to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), and [Rodrik Arryn](wiki:Rodrik_Arryn), the [Lord of the Eyrie](wiki:Lord_of_the_Eyrie). Daella eventually chose Lord Rodrik, declaring to Alysanne that he seemed “good and wise, like Father". Concerning Rodrik's four children from an earlier marriage, Daella declared "I'm to be their new mother!" According to Archmaester [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn), Queen Alysanne's response to this was not recorded, the account written by Grand Maester [Elysar](wiki:Elysar) about that day only said "Gods be good".(wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-14)

### Marriage

Daella and Lord [Rodrik Arryn](wiki:Rodrik_Arryn) were married before the year turned in a small ceremony in the [sept](wiki:Sept) on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) in front of a small crowd. As Daella had declared that she could not stand the shame of a [bedding](wiki:Bedding) ceremony, there was no bedding following the wedding. Afterwards, Daella accompanied Rodrik to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), where she met his children. Although she immediately clashed with [Elys Arryn](wiki:Elys_Arryn), the eldest and three years older than her new stepmother, she became fond of the younger three, and they of her. Meanwhile, as he had promised, Rodrik was kind and caring to Daella, and pampered and protected her, calling her "my precious princess." From time to time, Daella sent letters to Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), written by Lord Rodrik's younger daughter, [Amanda](wiki:Amanda_Arryn), in which she spoke of how happy she was.(wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-15)

In [82 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), after a year and a half of marriage, Daella wrote to her mother in her own hand to inform her of her pregnancy, telling her mother that she was frightened and asking her to come. Alysanne flew to her daughter on [Silverwing](wiki:Silverwing), arriving in the Eyrie three months before Daella was due. Alysanne, deeply concerned for her daughter, remained with Daella until she gave birth. Daella went into labor a fortnight too early, and had a long and troubled labor. Although her daughter, [Aemma Arryn](wiki:Aemma_Arryn), was healthy, [childbed fever](wiki:Disease) began soon after the birth, and Daella could not nurse her child as she wished to. Her fever only grew worse, and by morning Daella died, at the age of eighteen.(wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-16)

Rodrik begged Alysanne's permission to bury his late wife in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn), but was refused. Alysanne declared that Daella would be cremated, and that her ashes would be interred on Dragonstone beside those of her sister, Princess [Daenerys](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)). According to Archmaester [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn), Daella’s death was the first hint of [the rift](wiki:First_Quarrel) that was to open between King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and his queen, as Alysanne believed her husband had pushed Daella into marriage at too early an age.(wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-17)

## Appearances & Description

Daella was delicate and small of stature: on her toes she stood five feet and two inches. Everyone who met her judged her younger than she was in truth, as there was a childish aspect to her. Her mother, Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), described Daella as "my little flower". As she grew to [maidenhood](wiki:Maiden) she was described as pretty, enough to attract the attention of young lords, but wasn't singled out as exceptionally beautiful.(wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Daella was considered sweet, kind, and gentle, with a tender heart. However, she was also a shy, tongue-tied girl, who was easily frightened and quick to cry. She liked flowers but was afraid of gardens, [bees](wiki:Bee), [cats](wiki:Cat), and [horses](wiki:Horse). She was also terrified of [dragons](wiki:Dragon) and of [Summer Islanders](wiki:Summer_Islanders).(wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

The princess was not clever. Although she could read, Daella could only do so haltingly, and without full comprehension. She did not speak until she was almost two years of age. She was unable to memorize even the simplest prayers. Daella had a sweet voice, but was afraid to sing, being unable to recall the correct words. The mildest scolding would reduce Daella to tears.(wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Daella worshiped her mother, Queen Alysanne, and liked her sister, Princess [Maegelle](wiki:Maegelle_Targaryen). She was terrified of her other sister, Princess [Alyssa](wiki:Alyssa_Targaryen), and frightened of her brother, Prince [Vaegon](wiki:Vaegon_Targaryen).(wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
