## Appearances & Description

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Plague face comes to the meeting of the eleven priests of the [Many-Faced God](wiki:Many-Faced_God) at the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White) by secret ways, through tunnels and secret passages. He interrogates [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), who is not afraid of his appearance. He tasks her with assassinating an [old man](wiki:Old_man).(wiki:Plague_face.cite_ref-Radwd64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
