## Origins

During the early reign of King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), the wife of the [Evenstar](wiki:Evenstar) of [Tarth](wiki:Tarth) gave birth to triplets daughters. Aegon's sister-wife, Queen [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen), arranged [betrothals](wiki:Betrothal) for the girls with members of Houses [Corbray](wiki:House_Corbray), [Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower), and [Harlaw](wiki:House_Harlaw).(wiki:Tarth_triplets.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
