## Appearances & Description

Coldhands is dressed in the mottled blacks and greys(wiki:Coldhands.cite_ref-Rasos46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) with a black wool scarf concealing his pale face.(wiki:Coldhands.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He is armored in boiled leather and ringmail,(wiki:Coldhands.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) and he wields a sword.(wiki:Coldhands.cite_ref-Radwd13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) His ungloved hands are black and cold as ice, and he rides a [great elk](wiki:Great_elk).(wiki:Coldhands.cite_ref-Rasos46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) A flock of [ravens](wiki:Raven) flies under his command(wiki:Coldhands.cite_ref-Rasos46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) and brings him information.(wiki:Coldhands.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) Thin and gaunt, he speaks with a rattling voice and does not breathe.(wiki:Coldhands.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Coldhands saves [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), [Gilly](wiki:Gilly), and [her son](wiki:Monster) when they are beset by [wights](wiki:Wights) at [Whitetree](wiki:Whitetree), lost in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest) after fleeing [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep), addressing Sam as "Brother!"(wiki:Coldhands.cite_ref-Rasos46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) He takes the two under his protection and brings them to the Black Gate of the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort) located underneath the [Wall](wiki:Wall). Coldhands cannot pass through himself, due to the Wall's [magic](wiki:Magic), but he charges Sam and Gilly with bringing [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) and his companions to the Black Gate.(wiki:Coldhands.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Sam continues on to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), entrusted to secrecy by Coldhands.(wiki:Coldhands.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Coldhands guides Bran, [Hodor](wiki:Hodor), and [Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed) and [Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed) to the last [greenseer](wiki:Greenseer) north of the Wall. Along the way he departs to deal with "foes" behind them, sending his charges on to a lakeside village. Bran, having slipped into his [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), [Summer](wiki:Summer), as a [warg](wiki:Warg), discovers Coldhands and his ravens had killed five men of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Coldhands.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7) Unknown to Bran is that they were the surviving traitors responsible for the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep), such as [Ollo Lophand](wiki:Ollo_Lophand).(wiki:Coldhands.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8) When Bran wakes, Meera tells him that Coldhands has arrived with a sow, which the girl is roasting. Bran and his friends discover that Coldhands is indeed dead, but he continues to refuse to show his face.(wiki:Coldhands.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-9)

When his great elk collapses along their journey, Coldhands whispers a blessing in an [unknown language](wiki:True_Tongue) and slits the [animal](wiki:Animal)'s throat; he and Meera butcher the carcass for food.(wiki:Coldhands.cite_ref-Radwd13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Coldhands leads Bran and his companions to the [cave of the three-eyed crow](wiki:Cave_of_the_three-eyed_crow), his flock of ravens dwindling during the journey.(wiki:Coldhands.cite_ref-Radwd13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) Coldhands cannot enter the cave, but he fights off wights as Bran and his friends climb to the cave entrance.(wiki:Coldhands.cite_ref-Radwd13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
