## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Preston is one of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) who accompanies King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) on his hunt in the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood), during which Robert is gored by a [boar](wiki:Boar).(wiki:Preston_Greenfield.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Preston is present in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep)'s [throne room](wiki:Great_Hall_(Red_Keep)) when Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) is dismissed from the Kingsguard, and he laughs at his former sworn brother's shame.(wiki:Preston_Greenfield.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Ser Preston beats [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) when ordered to by King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_I_Baratheon). Sansa feels that Preston treats her like a [lackwit](wiki:Lackwit) child.(wiki:Preston_Greenfield.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

When Joffrey practices shooting at hares in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), one of his bolts almost hits Preston in the groin.(wiki:Preston_Greenfield.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Ser Preston guards the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) when [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sends Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) back to [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), the [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), with his terms for peace. When Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne), angry about how he has been treated by him, tries to grab Tyrion's sleeve after the reception, he is rebuffed by Preston: *"No closer, ser"*. Alliser relents, knowing that it is unwise to challenge a knight of the Kingsguard.(wiki:Preston_Greenfield.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Ser Preston is part of the procession to see Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) off to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). When the [mob riots](wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing) in the streets of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Preston tries to cut his way back to aid the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Fat_One)) against the mob, and is killed by the maddened people. It takes the [gold cloaks](wiki:Gold_cloaks) a few days to find his body, as they search for a knight in white [armor](wiki:Armor). However, the rioters have so severely stabbed and hacked at Preston's corpse that he is "red-brown from head to heel" and barely identifiable.(wiki:Preston_Greenfield.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Preston is replaced in the Kingsguard by Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann).(wiki:Preston_Greenfield.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Tyrion Lannister receives some hostile reactions among the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) of King's Landing when he is inspecting the devastation left beyond the [King's Gate](wiki:King's_Gate) after the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). He assumes that, if he was not protected by Ser [Bronn of the Blackwater](wiki:Bronn), the people would pull him off his horse and smash his face in with a cobblestone, as they did for Preston.(wiki:Preston_Greenfield.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

When Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) assumes his duties as [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard), he notices that in his absence the [White Book](wiki:White_Book) has not been updated, as that is the Lord Commander's duty, and that the death of Preston has yet to be recorded.(wiki:Preston_Greenfield.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) tells Sansa Stark that when he was arranging the contract for a [marriage](wiki:Marriage) between King Joffrey and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) in [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden), he let his men spread information about the gruesome details of Preston's death and the rape of Lady [Lollys Stokeworth](wiki:Lollys_Stokeworth) during the riot. Littlefinger suggests that by doing so, he successfully planted the idea in Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell)'s mind that his son Ser [Loras](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) should join the Kingsguard in order to be able to protect his sister.(wiki:Preston_Greenfield.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) asks the guilt-ridden Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) during one of their encounters whether he believes that he is the only member of the Kingsguard who ever loved a woman. Arys shares, among other things, that he knew Preston used to visit a draper's wife when her husband was away.(wiki:Preston_Greenfield.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
