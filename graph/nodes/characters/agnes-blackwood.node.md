## Origins

Agnes led the Blackwoods when King [Harwyn Hoare](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare) of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) invaded the northern [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). After [Samwell Rivers](wiki:Samwell_Rivers)' small force was destroyed by Harwyn on the [Tumblestone](wiki:Tumblestone), Lord [Tommen Tully](wiki:Tommen_Tully) joined his strength to that of Agnes at [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall). When she marched on Harwyn's [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), however, Agnes was attacked from behind by her neighboring rival, Lord [Lothar Bracken](wiki:Lothar_Bracken). Lothar delivered Agnes and two of her sons to Harwyn, who strangled the boys himself. Harwyn offered to make Agnes a [salt wife](wiki:Salt_wife), as he was impressed with her demeanor, but she said she would rather die; he stabbed her after her rejection. The defeat of the Blackwoods ended the rivermen's resistance to Harwyn, but the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Arrec Durrandon](wiki:Arrec_Durrandon) then marched on the ironborn.(wiki:Agnes_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
