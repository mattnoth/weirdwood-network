### A Storm of Swords

Boy is present when [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth), Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), and Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) arrive at the [Inn of the Kneeling Man](wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man).
