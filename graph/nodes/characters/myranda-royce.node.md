## Origins

Myranda was once married to an elderly [lord](wiki:Lord).(wiki:Myranda_Royce.cite_ref-Rawoiafmyranda_royce.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Her husband died while having sex with her.(wiki:Myranda_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4) She maintains the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) for her father, Lord [Nestor Royce](wiki:Nestor_Royce).(wiki:Myranda_Royce.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Appearances & Description

According to [Alayne Stone](wiki:Alayne_Stone), Myranda is short and fleshy and buxom.(wiki:Myranda_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) She is taller than Ser [Shadrich](wiki:Shadrich),(wiki:Myranda_Royce.cite_ref-twow_10-0) who is five feet tall.(wiki:Myranda_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Myranda has a small mouth, brown eyes, and brown curly hair.(wiki:Myranda_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Myranda is clever,(wiki:Myranda_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) playful,(wiki:Myranda_Royce.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) and given to gossip.(wiki:Myranda_Royce.cite_ref-Rawoiafmyranda_royce.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) She is good friends with [Mya Stone](wiki:Mya_Stone), one of her father's retainers.(wiki:Myranda_Royce.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) thinks her aunt [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) may banish her to the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon), and welcomes the idea, having heard that the merry Myranda has a lively household at the castle.(wiki:Myranda_Royce.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

### A Feast for Crows

Myranda ascends to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) with Mya Stone, to help Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn) descend to the Gates of the Moon. She enters a lively discussion with [Alayne Stone](wiki:Alayne_Stone), attempting to get information about her and her father [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), and saying that she and Alayne will be friends. She tells Alayne about the death of her first husband, and that her father tried to marry her to [Harrold Hardyng](wiki:Harrold_Hardyng), but was rebuffed by Harry's guardian Lady [Anya Waynwood](wiki:Anya_Waynwood).(wiki:Myranda_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5)

### The Winds of Winter

Myranda helps her father to host the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) that will determine the first eight [knights](wiki:Knight) to join the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights), an order newly founded by [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn). Along with [Alayne Stone](wiki:Alayne_Stone), Myranda welcomes the [Waynwoods](wiki:House_Waynwood) and Ser Harrold Hardyng when they arrive at the Gates for the tourney.(wiki:Myranda_Royce.cite_ref-twow_10-1)
