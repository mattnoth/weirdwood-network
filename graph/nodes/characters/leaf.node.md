## Edges

## Origins

Leaf was born in the time of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen)'s dynasty in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), and walked the world of men for two hundred years to watch, listen, and learn. She says she might be walking still, but her legs were sore and her heart was weary, so she turned her feet for home.(wiki:Leaf.cite_ref-Radwd13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

## Appearances & Description

Like the other children of the forest, Leaf is smaller than humans, but not childlike. She has nut-brown skin, dappled like a [deer](wiki:Deer)'s with paler spots. Her hands have only three fingers and a thumb, with sharp black claws instead of nails. She has large ears that can hear things that no man can hear.(wiki:Leaf.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) She has large gold and green eyes slitted like those of a [cat](wiki:Cat),(wiki:Leaf.cite_ref-Radwd13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) allowing her to see in dark passages.(wiki:Leaf.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Leaf's hair is a tangle of brown, red and gold, autumn colors, with vines and twigs and withered flowers woven through it. She wears a cloak of leaves.(wiki:Leaf.cite_ref-Radwd13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Bran's first sight of her: "her skin dappled like a doe's beneath a cloak of leaves. Her eyes were queer—large and liquid, gold and green, slitted like a cat's eyes ... Her hair was a tangle of brown and red and gold, autumn colors, with vines and twigs and withered flowers woven through it." Bran POV, ADWD Bran II, `sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-bran-02.md:135`

[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) thinks that from far away, Leaf looks no older than himself or his sisters, [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) or [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark), but close at hand, Leaf seems far older.(wiki:Leaf.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) Her voice is high and sweet, but also sad.(wiki:Leaf.cite_ref-Radwd13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) She is the only one of the children in the [cave of the three-eyed crow](wiki:Cave_of_the_three-eyed_crow) who speaks the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Leaf.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Outside the [cave of the three-eyed crow](wiki:Cave_of_the_three-eyed_crow) [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) and his companions — [Hodor](wiki:Hodor), [Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed), and [Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed) — are attacked by [wights](wiki:Wight). A cloud of [ravens](wiki:Raven) pours from the cave, and Bran sees what appears to be a girl, almost like his sister [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark), chasing off the wights with the fire of a torch. Bran then realizes she is not a girl, but a child of the forest, who leads them into the cave of the [last greenseer](wiki:Last_greenseer), Lord Brynden.(wiki:Leaf.cite_ref-Radwd13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

The little woman explains about the children of the forest to Bran and his companions, calling them "those who sing the song of earth". She explains that the children in the cave have names too long for the human tongue, so Bran and Meera give them names. Meera calls the little woman Leaf. Leaf warns the company not to wander too far in the cave because of its dangers, saying that not even her people have explored all its passages despite living there for a thousand thousand years.(wiki:Leaf.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

Leaf helps Lord Brynden train Bran as a [greenseer](wiki:Greenseer), giving him [weirwood paste](wiki:Weirwood_paste). When Bran sees a [vision](wiki:Vision) of his late father, [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), Leaf warns Bran not to try to call him back from death.(wiki:Leaf.cite_ref-Radwd34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)
