## Origins

Vogarro was an [elephant](wiki:Elephants_(Volantis)), the party of merchants, and was elected [triarch](wiki:Triarch) seven times. Vogarro owned piers and storehouses, brokered cargoes, changed money and insured shipowners against the hazards of the sea. He also dealt in [slaves](wiki:Slavery). He fell in love with a pleasure slave who was taught the [way of the seven sighs](wiki:Way_of_the_seven_sighs) in [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai). He freed and married her, causing a scandal. When he died he left his businesses to his wife, the former slave. She began to manage them and became known as the [widow of the waterfront](wiki:Widow_of_the_waterfront).
