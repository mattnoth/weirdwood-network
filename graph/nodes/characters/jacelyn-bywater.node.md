## Origins

Jacelyn received a [knighthood](wiki:Knight) from King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) for his valor at the [siege of Pyke](wiki:Siege_of_Pyke) during [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion). Jacelyn lost his right hand during the fighting.(wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Jacelyn Bywater](wiki:Category:Images_of_Jacelyn_Bywater)*

Jacelyn is tall, lantern-jawed, with deep-set eyes, a prominent brow, and salt-and-pepper hair. He has an iron hand strapped to his right wrist where he lost his hand, hence his nickname.(wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Ironhand is considered brave and honorable by [Varys](wiki:Varys), but is seen as rigid by the corrupt [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt).(wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser Jacelyn has been captain of the [Mud Gate](wiki:River_Gate) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for three years when [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) arrives to take up the position of [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) in [299 AC](wiki:299_AC). He is named [Commander of the City Watch](wiki:Commander_of_the_City_Watch) after Tyrion dismisses [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt). Tyrion instructs Jacelyn to make sure that [Allar Deem](wiki:Allar_Deem) is thrown overboard while *[Summer's Dream](wiki:Summer's_Dream)* sails to [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea).(wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Jacelyn receives Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) when the latter arrives to give terms from [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) After [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) is imprisoned by Tyrion, Jacelyn defies Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s attempt to free the [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester).(wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater.cite_ref-Racok29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Jacelyn leads a wedge of mounted lancers during the [riot of King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing) after Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon)'s departure to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Jacelyn tells Tyrion about the spread of [bloody flux](wiki:Bloody_flux) at Pisswater Bend, about the growing revolt among the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk), and how Tyrion is personally blamed for the hard times. Tyrion orders Jacelyn to find the missing [Tyrek Lannister](wiki:Tyrek_Lannister).(wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Jacelyn obeys Tyrion's orders to seize Prince [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) on the [Rosby road](wiki:Rosby_road), imprisoning Lord [Gyles Rosby](wiki:Gyles_Rosby) and Ser [Boros Blount](wiki:Boros_Blount) in the process, and keeping Tommen safe and out of reach of Cersei. For this Ironhand earns the queen's enmity, as well as a lordship from Tyrion.(wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater.cite_ref-7)

Jacelyn providers runners to Tyrion during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater.cite_ref-Racok59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords

[Bronn](wiki:Bronn) informs Tyrion that when King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) retreated to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) during the Battle of the Blackwater, half of the gold cloaks tried to retreat as well. Jacelyn tried to rally them, but was then struck in the throat by an arrow. The gold cloaks dragged Ironhand from his horse and killed him.(wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) During a meeting of the [small council](wiki:Small_council), Tyrion suggests that Jacelyn's killers should have their knees broken.(wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) With Jacelyn dead, Cersei sends the [Kettleblacks](wiki:House_Kettleblack) to free Tommen from Rosby.(wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

Having lost his sword hand, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) considers wearing a golden hand similar to the iron hand once worn by Jacelyn.(wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
