## Origins

Simon was a member of [House Toyne](wiki:House_Toyne), a family fallen into disgrace after [altercations](wiki:Terrence_Toyne) with [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen). Under Simon's leadership, the [Kingswood Brotherhood](wiki:Kingswood_Brotherhood) achieved no small measure of infamy as they kidnapped various nobles and evaded capture. According to Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), Simon entered a [tourney at Storm's End](wiki:Lord_Steffon's_tourney) as a [mystery knight](wiki:Mystery_knight), where he was unhorsed by Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen); a semi-canon source, however, states that Barristan may have misremembered the tourney.(wiki:Simon_Toyne.cite_ref-3)

The forest folk of the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood) had looked to Simon and his outlaws for protection. When Ser [Arthur Dayne](wiki:Arthur_Dayne) led a campaign into the forest, however, the [Sword of the Morning](wiki:Sword_of_the_Morning) gained the support of these [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) by paying them for supplies, bringing their grievances to King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), and allowing them to gain some additional rights.(wiki:Simon_Toyne.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) When Barristan rescued Lady [Jeyne Swann](wiki:Jeyne_Swann) and her [septa](wiki:Septa) from the outlaws, he defeated the [Smiling Knight](wiki:Smiling_Knight) and slew Simon.(wiki:Simon_Toyne.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

[Ulmer of the Kingswood](wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood) has often told the men at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) about his adventures with Simon and other outlaws from the Brotherhood.(wiki:Simon_Toyne.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
