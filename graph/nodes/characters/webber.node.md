## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Webber fights in the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor) as a member of the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown). Webber and the other [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) members of the company are then told by their leader, the [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince), to go over to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), the [Queen of Meereen](wiki:Queen_of_Meereen). The Tattered Prince tells Webber that when he meets Daenerys, the excuse he should give for deserting is that he has some claims to lost lands in Westeros.(wiki:Webber.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
