## Appearances & Description

Arlan was a small, slim man. [Dunk](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) thought the [hedge knight](wiki:Hedge_knight) was closer to sixty than fifty when he died.(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
Arlan was a kindly man,(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) generous with giving praise. He enjoyed watching sunsets.(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) The [knight](wiki:Knight) spoke highly of [Baelor Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)), [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone), and [Leo Tyrell](wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(Longthorn)), [Lord of Highgarden](wiki:Lord_of_Highgarden). Arlan affectionately claimed that [Dunk](wiki:Dunk) was "thick as a castle wall and slow as an [aurochs](wiki:Aurochs)."(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Arlan's weaponry included a [longsword](wiki:Longsword), an eight-foot ash [war lance](wiki:War_lance), and a [dagger](wiki:Knife). His armor included a chain-mail hauberk, a dented [iron](wiki:Iron) [halfhelm](wiki:Halfhelm), and greaves and gorget. His battered [oak](wiki:Oak) shield depicted his winged chalice sigil.(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

## Origins

Arlan was born in the village of [Pennytree](wiki:Pennytree) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). When Arlan was a little boy, his grandfather took him to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), where he saw the [last dragon](wiki:Last_dragon). The dragon died a year later.(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)
In [193 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Ser Arlan unhorsed Lord [Stokeworth](wiki:House_Stokeworth) and the [Bastard of Harrenhal](wiki:Bastard_of_Harrenhal) in a [melee](wiki:Melee) at King's Landing. Years prior to that, he had unhorsed Ser [Damon Lannister](wiki:Damon_Lannister_(lord)) in a [tourney](wiki:Tourney) at [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport).(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8) In [200 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Arlan was defeated by Prince [Baelor Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)), [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone), in a tourney at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End). Arlan later said he broke seven lances against Baelor, while Baelor remembered four lances. Arlan chose never to joust again after that prestigious showing.(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-9)
Arlan's nephew, [Roger of Pennytree](wiki:Roger_of_Pennytree), was his [squire](wiki:Squire). During the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), Arlan and Roger fought beneath [Lord Hayford](wiki:Lord_Hayford)'s banners, taking part in the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field), during which Roger was killed.(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Arlan was next to Lord Hayford when the lord was slain by Lord [Gormon Peake](wiki:Gormon_Peake).(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
At some later point Arlan met [Dunk](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) in the slums of [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom) in King's Landing, and took him for his squire.(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) The two of them remained together for years. Arlan and Ser [Bennis of the Brown Shield](wiki:Bennis_of_the_Brown_Shield) were among knights hired by a [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) merchant to escort him from [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) to the [Prince's Pass](wiki:Prince's_Pass).(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) Arlan and Dunk also served at [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) for a time,(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-10) and they also marched with a few peasant levies.(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) Arlan once helped hang an [outlaw](wiki:Outlaw) who had been stealing rings.(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6) Arlan also served a minor lord in the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) in a regional dispute.(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7)
Three years before the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow), Arlan and Dunk served Lord [Dondarrion](wiki:House_Dondarrion) in the battle with the [Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Daeron_II)). Also three years before the Ashford tourney, Arlan spent a half year in service to [Lord Florent](wiki:Lord_Florent). Once, a drunken Arlan promised to take Dunk to a [brothel](wiki:Brothel), but the knight did not remember the promise once sober.(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-11)
Arlan died of a chill on the road leading to [Ashford Meadow](wiki:Ashford_Meadow) in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC). Dunk buried Arlan's body on the western slope of a hill, facing the sunset. Dunk continued on to the tourney. At [Ashford](wiki:Ashford) he claimed to [Plummer](wiki:Plummer) that Arlan had knighted him as Ser Duncan the Tall before his passing, although there were no witnesses to the ceremony.(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-12) Two years later, Duncan also claimed to Lady [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber) that he had been knighted by Arlan.(wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8)
