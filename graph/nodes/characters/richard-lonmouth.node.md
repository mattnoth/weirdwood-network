## Origins

Richard followed [Myles Mooton](wiki:Myles_Mooton) in [squiring](wiki:Squire) for [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone), and each man received his knighthood from Rhaegar when they were ready.(wiki:Richard_Lonmouth.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Richard and Ser Myles were close friends of Rhaegar and strong supporters of the prince at court, as were Lord [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington), Prince [Lewyn Martell](wiki:Lewyn_Martell), and Ser [Arthur Dayne](wiki:Arthur_Dayne).(wiki:Richard_Lonmouth.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Richard was present at the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal), where he played a drinking game with [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End).(wiki:Richard_Lonmouth.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Halfway through the tourney, he and Robert each promised Rhaegar's father, King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), they would find and discover the true identity of the [Knight of the Laughing Tree](wiki:Knight_of_the_Laughing_Tree).(wiki:Richard_Lonmouth.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

It is unknown if Richard chose to follow his liege lords, [House Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon), during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), or if he stayed loyal to Prince Rhaegar and [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen).
