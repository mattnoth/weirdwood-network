## Appearances & Description

Bartimus is a descendant of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) who lived at the mouth of the [White Knife](wiki:White_Knife) before the coming of the [Manderlys](wiki:House_Manderly). He is often drunk. He has one leg and one eye. Though he is a knight he worships the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods).(wiki:Bartimus.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
