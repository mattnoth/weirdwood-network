## Identity

Prince Aegon Targaryen was the thirdborn son of King Aerys II Targaryen and Queen Rhaella Targaryen (wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aerys_II)).

## Origins

Born in 272 AC, two months premature (track_b: Born). Son of Aerys II Targaryen and Rhaella Targaryen (track_b: Father, Mother). Died in 273 AC as an infant (track_b: Died). Like his brother Daeron before him, Aegon did not live long (wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aerys_II)).
