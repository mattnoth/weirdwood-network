## Origins

Lord Armond often liked to take his son [Jon](wiki:Jon_Connington) up to the tallest tower of [Griffin's Roost](wiki:Griffin's_Roost) to show him the view of their ancestral lands. When Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) was still a boy, he visited Griffin's Roost after a trip to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), Armond attempted to win the young prince to his side in an argument he had with Lord [Morrigen](wiki:House_Morrigen).(wiki:Armond_Connington.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Armond died a few years before [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) and is buried beneath Griffin's Roost's [sept](wiki:Sept).(wiki:Armond_Connington.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Armond_Connington.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
