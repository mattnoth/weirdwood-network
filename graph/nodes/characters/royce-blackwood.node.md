## Origins

Royce's father, Lord Blackwood, attempted to betroth his son to Princess [Daella Targaryen](wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)) when the girl was brought to [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall) by Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen). Although Daella was charmed by Royce's singing, the princess lost interest when she learned that [House Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood) followed the old gods instead of the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:Royce_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
