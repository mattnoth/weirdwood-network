## Appearances & Description

Kella is a fat middle-aged woman. She has many [children](wiki:Bastardy) by different fathers. She tends House Baelish's herds of [sheep](wiki:Bestiary).(wiki:Kella.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Kella is among Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish)'s attendants who meet him when he comes home to [his tower](wiki:Drearfort). She brings her daughter, a girl of two or three with a sty on her eye, whom Lord Petyr has not met before. The father of the child is unknown to Kella.(wiki:Kella.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
