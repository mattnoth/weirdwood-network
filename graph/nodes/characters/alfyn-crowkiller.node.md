## Appearances & Description

Alfyn gets his name from his history of killing members of the Night's Watch.(wiki:Alfyn_Crowkiller.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) He is one of the bloodiest of the wildling raiders.(wiki:Alfyn_Crowkiller.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Alfyn has at least four sons, a scrawny dark-haired boy of twelve(wiki:Alfyn_Crowkiller.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) and three boys fathered on three different mothers, hostages of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Alfyn_Crowkiller.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) sends Alfyn as a scout toward the [Wall](wiki:Wall). As he is returning from his mission, Alfyn encounters [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand) and his [rangers](wiki:Ranger) as they are on their way to the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men) to join [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) and his expedition. Alfyn is killed during the skirmish by Qhorin.(wiki:Alfyn_Crowkiller.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)(wiki:Alfyn_Crowkiller.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Alfyn_Crowkiller.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
