## Origins

Lord Costayne supported [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) during his [rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion) in [196 AC](wiki:196_AC). He fought on the left side of the Blackfyre host at the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field). As a young boy, Prince [Aegon "Egg" Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) would re-enact the battle with his brother [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) using small painted soldiers and banners. From these childhood games, he recognized the previous Lord Costayne and his lineage as Blackfyre rebels and knew where their forces fought in the battle.(wiki:Lord_Costayne_(Daeron_II).cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

By [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), Lord Costayne had been succeeded as head of the house by his [son](wiki:Lord_Costayne_(Aerys_I)).(wiki:Lord_Costayne_(Daeron_II).cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
