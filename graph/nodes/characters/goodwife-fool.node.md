## Origins

The Goodwife was among the many artists brought to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) by Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) after her husband, King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), began to rule in his own right. When Alysanne relocated to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) following the death of Prince [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)), the queen was accompanied by her lady attendants and the entertaining Goodwife.(wiki:Goodwife_(fool).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Appearances & Description

The Goodwife was a fat man who dressed as a woman and said ribald, shocking things with his wooden "children", a pair of carved puppets.(wiki:Goodwife_(fool).cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
