## Appearances & Description

The Widower is a scarred and sour axeman.(wiki:Widower.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) He owns a gaunt grey [horse](wiki:Horse). In battle, he wears an iron halfhelm sprouted by a fan of black featers from one temple, a shield and ringmail under a faded cloak. He wields a spiked [battle-axe](wiki:Armament).(wiki:Widower.cite_ref-winds_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

When Daario is given over as a hostage to the [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai), the Widower and [Jokin](wiki:Jokin) become the joint commanders of the Stormcrows. They are present at the war council called by [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy).(wiki:Widower.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### The Winds of Winter

The Widower and other commanders of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s armies meet with Ser Barristan before the start of the [battle](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen) where he reminds them of the battle plans. The Stormcrows are to ride towards Yunkai's [trebuchets](wiki:Six_sisters) and destroy them. Should Barristan fall, the Widower has the command after him.(wiki:Widower.cite_ref-winds_2-1)
