## Identity

Lady Baela Targaryen, also known as Baela Velaryon, was a daughter of Prince Daemon Targaryen and Lady Laena Velaryon (wiki:Baela_Targaryen). She had a younger twin sister, Rhaena Targaryen, and two younger half-brothers, Aegon and Viserys. She was a dragonrider who rode Moondancer (wiki:Baela_Targaryen).

## Origins

Born in 116 AC in Pentos (track_b: Born). Named after her paternal grandfather, Prince Baelon Targaryen. She and her twin were born small and sickly (wiki:Baela_Targaryen).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiances). `SWORN_TO` House Velaryon (track_b: Allegiances). `SWORN_TO` Blacks during the Dance of the Dragons (track_b: Allegiances).

## Appearances & Description

Baela and her twin had fine features, silver-white hair, and purple eyes (wiki:Baela_Targaryen). At fourteen she was slim and short; by sixteen she was tall, lean, and quick. She cropped her hair short so it would not whip about her face when riding. Like her father Daemon, she was wild, willing, and fearless (wiki:Baela_Targaryen). She had a pet monkey (wiki:Baela_Targaryen).

## Narrative Arc

Baela grew up on Dragonstone and bonded with the young dragon Moondancer. She was betrothed to Prince Jacaerys Velaryon in 118 AC. During the Dance of the Dragons, she supported the Blacks. She married Lord Alyn Velaryon and became Lady of Driftmark, having a daughter named Laena Velaryon (wiki:Baela_Targaryen).
