## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

The brothers of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) who defend the [Wall](wiki:Wall) during [the attack by the wildlings](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall) name one of the straw dummies after Watt.(wiki:Watt_of_Long_Lake.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

Watt is killed by an axe through his skull during the [fight at the Bridge of Skulls](wiki:Fight_at_the_Bridge_of_Skulls). His corpse is knocked off the [Bridge of Skulls](wiki:Bridge_of_Skulls) by the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings), landing in a deep pool of water.(wiki:Watt_of_Long_Lake.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
