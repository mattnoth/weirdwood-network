## Origins

Amanda was the daughter of Lord [Rodrik Arryn](wiki:Rodrik_Arryn) and his first wife. She had three siblings, including an elder sister, [Elys](wiki:Elys_Arryn). After her mother died, her father married again to Princess [Daella Targaryen](wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)). Amanda largely wrote letters on behalf of her step-mother to Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen).(wiki:Amanda_Arryn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Daella died in childbirth, giving Amanda a younger half-sister, [Aemma Arryn](wiki:Aemma_Arryn).(wiki:Amanda_Arryn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
