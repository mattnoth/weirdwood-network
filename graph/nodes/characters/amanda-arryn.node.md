## Identity

Amanda Arryn was a noblewoman from House Arryn during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen (wiki:Amanda_Arryn).

## Origins

Born approximately 62–74 AC (track_b: Born). Daughter of Rodrik Arryn (track_b: Father).

## Narrative Arc

Amanda was the daughter of Lord Rodrik Arryn and his first wife. She had an elder sister, Elys, and two brothers. After her mother's death, her father married Princess Daella Targaryen. Amanda largely wrote letters on behalf of her step-mother to Queen Alysanne Targaryen. Daella died in childbirth, giving Amanda a younger half-sister, Aemma Arryn (wiki:Amanda_Arryn).
