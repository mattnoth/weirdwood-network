## Origins

During the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), after capturing the major strongholds of the [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) nobility, King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) marched upon [House Martell](wiki:House_Martell)'s seat of [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear). Upon finding it half-deserted and the Martells absent, Aegon declared victory and informed the remaining Dornish dignitaries that they were now part of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms). Lord Jon Rosby was named [Castellan](wiki:Castellan) of Sunspear and [Warden of the Sands](wiki:Warden_of_the_Sands), commanded to rule Dorne in the king's name, while stewards and [castellans](wiki:Castellan) were named for other castles and lands. Soon after, Aegon and his host departed Dorne.(wiki:Jon_Rosby.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) While ruling from Sunspear, Rosby was to be supported by Lord [Harlan Tyrell](wiki:Harlan_Tyrell)'s garrison at the [Hellholt](wiki:Hellholt).(wiki:Jon_Rosby.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

When the [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) soon returned from the [shadow city](wiki:Shadow_city), Rosby was quickly bound and captured. While Aegon's other castellans and stewards were tortured, Rosby had a kinder end than most in the resulting [Defenestration of Sunspear](wiki:Defenestration_of_Sunspear), with the aged [Princess](wiki:Princess_of_Dorne) [Meria Martell](wiki:Meria_Martell) throwing him from the top of the Spear Tower. Lord Tyrell set out from Hellholt with his host to avenge Rosby, but disappeared soon after in [5 AC](wiki:5_AC).(wiki:Jon_Rosby.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)(wiki:Jon_Rosby.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
