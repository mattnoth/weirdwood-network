## Identity

Jocelyn Stark was the only daughter of Lord Willam Stark and Lady Melantha Blackwood (wiki:Jocelyn_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos45).

Born between 198–227 AC (track_b: Born). Died 212 AC (track_b: Died). Father: Willam Stark (track_b: Father). Mother: Melantha Blackwood (track_b: Mother).

## Narrative Arc

Jocelyn married Benedict Royce and had three daughters. According to Catelyn Stark, one daughter married a Waynwood, another a Corbray, and the third might have married a Templeton (wiki:Jocelyn_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos45).
