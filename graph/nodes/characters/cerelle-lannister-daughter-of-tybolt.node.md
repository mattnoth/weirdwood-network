## Identity

Lady Cerelle Lannister was the Lady of Casterly Rock and the head of House Lannister during the beginning of King Aerys I Targaryen's reign (wiki:Cerelle_Lannister_(daughter_of_Tybolt)).

## Origins

## Narrative Arc

When her father Tybolt died in 212 AC, Cerelle succeeded him as the Lady of Casterly Rock at the age of three. Her uncle Gerold Lannister served as her regent. Cerelle's rule lasted less than a year — she died in 213 AC. After her death, Gerold succeeded her as Lord of Casterly Rock. Since both Cerelle and her father died suddenly and under suspicious circumstances, it was whispered that their deaths had been caused by Gerold so that he would inherit the westerlands. The most common tale was that Gerold smothered her with a pillow as she slept (wiki:Cerelle_Lannister_(daughter_of_Tybolt)).
