## Origins

Rohanne's betrothal to [Daemon Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) was arranged by King [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen), Daemon's father, and the [Archon](wiki:Archon) of [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh). Following the death of King Aegon IV in [184 AC](wiki:184_AC), his successor and Daemon's half-brother, King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen), paid the [dowry](wiki:Dowry) the late King Aegon IV had promised the Archon, and saw his half-brother wed. Rohanne was married to the fourteen-year old Daemon in [184 AC](wiki:184_AC).(wiki:Rohanne_of_Tyrosh.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Their first children, the twins [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Blackfyre) and [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Blackfyre), were born that same year.(wiki:Rohanne_of_Tyrosh.cite_ref-3) In total, Rohanne would birth seven sons for Daemon, and at least two daughters.(wiki:Rohanne_of_Tyrosh.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

After the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion) ended with the deaths of Daemon and his eldest two sons, Rohanne and her remaining children fled to [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) along with another one of Daemon's half-brothers, [Aegor Rivers](wiki:Aegor_Rivers). They remained in Tyrosh for years after.(wiki:Rohanne_of_Tyrosh.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

In [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), when [Daemon II Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre) was captured at [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) by the crown after the abortive [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion), [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers) noted if he executed him, his mother would mourn.(wiki:Rohanne_of_Tyrosh.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
