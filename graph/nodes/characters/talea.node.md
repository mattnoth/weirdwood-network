## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

She and her older sister, [Brea](wiki:Brea), share their bed with [Cat of the Canals](wiki:Arya_Stark) when Cat is staying at Brusco's home. When her father's boat leaves the dock she catches the rope that Cat throws to her. She confirms Brea's statement that Cat growls in her sleep. She sells shellfish from a wheelbarrow in the allies around the [Moon Pool](wiki:Moon_Pool) or on the [Isle of the Gods](wiki:Isle_of_the_Gods) by the temples.(wiki:Talea.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
