## Appearances & Description

Overweight and with heavy legs, Marghaz tries to hide his physique behind a muscled breastplate. He also wears a [lion](wiki:Lion)'s mask as commander of the [Brazen Beasts](wiki:Brazen_Beasts).(wiki:Marghaz_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Soon after [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) marries [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), [Queen of Meereen](wiki:Queen_of_Meereen), he replaces [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) as commander of the [Brazen Beasts](wiki:Brazen_Beasts) with his own cousin, Marghaz.(wiki:Marghaz_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

When Skahaz and Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) plot against Hizdahr, Skahaz reassures the knight that Marghaz will not be be a threat to their plans.(wiki:Marghaz_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
