## Identity

Princess Daenerys Targaryen was the daughter of Aegon IV Targaryen and Naerys Targaryen, and the younger sister of King Daeron II Targaryen (wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon_IV)). Daenerys Targaryen, daughter of Aerys II, was named after her (wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon_IV)).

## Narrative Arc

Daenerys was married to Prince Maron Martell as part of the diplomatic arrangement that peacefully brought Dorne into the Seven Kingdoms under Daeron II. She became Princess Consort of Dorne and had children, establishing the Martell-Targaryen tie. Daemon Blackfyre's desire for her was one of the sparks that ignited the First Blackfyre Rebellion (wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon_IV)).
