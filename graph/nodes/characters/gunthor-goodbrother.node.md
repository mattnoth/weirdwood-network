## Origins

After Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy) was killed by his own [salt wife](wiki:Salt_wife) [Tess](wiki:Tess) at [Faircastle](wiki:Faircastle) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) and remaining [knights](wiki:Knight) of [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle) rose up in rebellion against the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn). Alongside the sly [Alester Wynch](wiki:Alester_Wynch), Gunthor was one of the commanders of the [Greyjoy](wiki:House_Greyjoy) garrison at Faircastle, composed of the Red Kraken's close companions and brothers-in-battle. While the ironborn fled the island or were otherwise murdered by its people, the garrison at Faircastle held out stubbornly. In a dispute over another of the Red Kraken's salt widows, [Lysa Farman](wiki:Lysa_Farman), Gunthor slew Alester Wynch. The longships thus fled and Fair Isle was free.(wiki:Gunthor_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
