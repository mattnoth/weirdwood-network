## Identity

Garth Tyrell, known as Garth the Gross, is a member of House Tyrell and an uncle of Lord Mace Tyrell of Highgarden. He serves his nephew as the Lord Seneschal of Highgarden (wiki:Garth_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc7). He has two bastard sons, Garse and Garrett Flowers (wiki:Garth_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc7).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Tyrell (track_b: Allegiance). Serves as Lord Seneschal of Highgarden.

## Appearances & Description

Garth is known to be flatulent, earning his nickname "Garth the Gross" (wiki:Garth_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc7).

## Narrative Arc

Garth served Lord Luthor Tyrell as Lord Seneschal of Highgarden. While dancing with Sansa Stark at her wedding to Tyrion Lannister, Ser Garlan Tyrell mentions his uncle Garth in a jape that makes Sansa laugh (wiki:Garth_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos28). Lord Tywin Lannister had invited Garth to become the new master of coin after Tyrion's arrest, with his two bastard sons to join the gold cloaks. However, Cersei blocks this by naming Lord Gyles Rosby instead (wiki:Garth_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc7). Later, when Mace Tyrell becomes Hand of the King, he again pushes to replace Ser Harys Swyft as master of coin with his uncle Garth (wiki:Garth_Tyrell.cite_ref-Radwd72).
