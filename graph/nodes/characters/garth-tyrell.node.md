## Origins

Garth served Lord [Luthor Tyrell](wiki:Luthor_Tyrell) as [Lord Seneschal of Highgarden](wiki:Lord_Seneschal_of_Highgarden).(wiki:Garth_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Appearances & Description

Garth is known to be flatulent.(wiki:Garth_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

While dancing with [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) at her wedding to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), Ser [Garlan Tyrell](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell) mentions his uncle Garth in a jape that makes the depressed Sansa laugh.(wiki:Garth_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Feast for Crows

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) had invited Garth to become the new [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin) after [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s arrest. His two sons were also supposed to join the [gold cloaks](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing). However, once Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) finds out about this, she names Lord [Gyles Rosby](wiki:Gyles_Rosby) to the position of master of coin on the [small council](wiki:Small_council).(wiki:Garth_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

### A Dance with Dragons

Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), now the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) following the elevation of Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) to the position of Lord [Regent](wiki:Regent) and [Protector of the Realm](wiki:Protector_of_the_Realm), wants to replace Ser [Harys Swyft](wiki:Harys_Swyft) as master of coin with his uncle Garth.(wiki:Garth_Tyrell.cite_ref-Radwd72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
