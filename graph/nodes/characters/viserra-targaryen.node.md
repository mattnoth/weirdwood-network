## Identity

Viserra Targaryen — Viserra, the most beautiful of the queen's daughters, was betrothed at fifteen in 86 AC to old Lord Theomore Manderly.

## Origins

Viserra was born to King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) in [71 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). She was their tenth child and sixth daughter.(wiki:Viserra_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

In [73 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), after Viserra's brother [Vaegon](wiki:Vaegon_Targaryen) had refused their sister [Daella](wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)) as a bride, Viserra and her older sister [Saera](wiki:Saera_Targaryen) were briefly considered as a potential spouse for Vaegon. However, Saera was only six years old and Viserra two, and Vaegon had never looked at either of them. As such, the notion was rejected.(wiki:Viserra_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)

According to Alysanne, Viserra aimed to become a queen and had her eye set on her fourteen-year older brother, [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)), who had been a widower since the death of his sister-wife [Alyssa](wiki:Alyssa_Targaryen) two years before. Viserra saw no reason as to why she could not be wed to an older brother. Queen Alysanne, however, was determined to prevent such a [marriage](wiki:Marriage), and [betrothed](wiki:Betrothal) the fifteen-year old Viserra in [86 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) to [Theomore Manderly](wiki:Theomore_Manderly), [Lord of White Harbor](wiki:Lord_of_White_Harbor).(wiki:Viserra_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10) The betrothal was possibly also a reaction to Viserra's elder sister Saera's fall from grace.(wiki:Viserra_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtrotd64.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Viserra was not excited about the marriage, as Theomore had grown very stout during his life and had been widowed four times already. Viserra first turned to her father for help, but Jaehaerys agreed with the match and refused to interfere. Viserra then turned to Baelon, hoping for him to rescue her according to court gossip. One night, she slipped past Baelon's guards and climbed naked into his bed, but Baelon sent the drunk girl away when he arrived.(wiki:Viserra_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-11)

In [87 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), not long after the incident with Baelon, as arrangements were being made for Viserra's departure to [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor), Viserra traded clothes with one of her maids and escaped her guards. She slipped from the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) for "one last night of laughter" with her companions, two minor lordlings and four young knights. Near midnight the princess and her remaining companions raced back to the Red Keep. At the foot of [Aegon's High Hill](wiki:Aegon's_High_Hill), Viserra's [palfrey](wiki:Horse) collided with the mare of one of her companions. Viserra was thrown from her saddle into a wall, and she died of a broken neck at the age of fifteen.(wiki:Viserra_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-12)

## Appearances & Description

Viserra was the most beautiful of Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen)'s daughters. She had deep [purple eyes](wiki:Purple_eyes) and silver-gold hair, flawless white skin, and fine features.(wiki:Viserra_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Viserra was a vain girl. Once, when a young [squire](wiki:Squire) called her a goddess, she simply agreed with him. The attention of great [lords](wiki:Lord), famous [knights](wiki:Knight), and callow boys fed her vanity until it "became a raging fire".(wiki:Viserra_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Though disinterested in exploiting men's bodies, Viserra delighted in toying with their emotions,(wiki:Viserra_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtrotd64.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) playing one boy off against another and setting them on foolish quests or having them perform contests.(wiki:Viserra_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Viserra was a wild, high-spirited girl.(wiki:Viserra_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) She was described as sly, and had a grace that was uncanny and unsettling in someone as young as she was.(wiki:Viserra_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

According to Alysanne, Viserra desired to become a queen, and therefore aimed to marry her brother [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)), not for love but for ambition.(wiki:Viserra_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)
