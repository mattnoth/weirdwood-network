## Identity

Princess Viserra Targaryen was the tenth-born child and sixth daughter of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen and Queen Alysanne Targaryen (wiki:Viserra_Targaryen, cite: Fire & Blood).

## Origins

Born in 71 AC to King Jaehaerys I Targaryen and Queen Alysanne Targaryen (wiki:Viserra_Targaryen).

## Appearances & Description

Viserra was the most beautiful of Queen Alysanne's daughters, with deep purple eyes, silver-gold hair, flawless white skin, and fine features. She was vain, wild, high-spirited, sly, and had an uncanny grace. She delighted in toying with men's emotions, playing one off against another (wiki:Viserra_Targaryen, cite: Fire & Blood).

## Narrative Arc

According to Alysanne, Viserra aimed to become queen and desired to marry her brother Baelon, not for love but for ambition. Alysanne was determined to prevent such a match and betrothed the fifteen-year-old Viserra in 86 AC to Theomore Manderly, Lord of White Harbor, who had grown stout and been widowed four times. Viserra was not pleased; she petitioned her father, who refused to interfere, then attempted to seduce Baelon by slipping naked into his bed while drunk, but Baelon sent her away (wiki:Viserra_Targaryen, cite: Fire & Blood).

In 87 AC, as arrangements were being made for her departure to White Harbor, Viserra disguised herself, slipped from the Red Keep for one last night of revelry. Racing back near midnight, her palfrey collided with another rider's mare at the foot of Aegon's High Hill. Viserra was thrown into a wall and died of a broken neck at age fifteen (wiki:Viserra_Targaryen, cite: Fire & Blood).
