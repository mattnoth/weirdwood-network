## Origins

Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) managed to arrange a marriage between his ninth son, [Merrett](wiki:Merrett_Frey), and one of the daughters of the [Lord of Darry](wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun)), Mariya. The marriage was an excellent match for Merrett, for the Darrys were a wealthy and prominent house that stood high in the favor of King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen). However, almost immediately after the marriage, [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) broke out in [282 AC](wiki:282_AC), leading to the fall of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen); the Darrys suffered grievously for their staunch support of the Mad King, costing them half their lands and most of their wealth.(wiki:Mariya_Darry.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Since the start of their marriage, Mariya quickly found Merrett to be a great disappointment and, to Merrett's displeasure, the marriage initially only produced daughters, as well as a stillbirth and a girl that died in infancy, before finally producing a son, called ["Little" Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)).(wiki:Mariya_Darry.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

## Appearances & Description

Mariya is a handsome woman with grey streaks in her hair.(wiki:Mariya_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Her husband, Merrett Frey, considers her to be a shrew.(wiki:Mariya_Darry.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Mariya Darry is widowed after her husband, [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey), is hanged by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) for his involvement in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Mariya_Darry.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

### A Feast for Crows

After the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Ser [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister) is award the [lordship](wiki:Lord_of_Darry) of [Darry](wiki:Darry), for the Darrys are extinct in the legitimate male-line.(wiki:Mariya_Darry.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) arranges for his son Lancel to wed Mariya's daughter, [Amerei Frey](wiki:Amerei_Frey), to strengthen his claim on the Darry lands through Amerei's half-Darry blood. After the marriage, Mariya resides at [Castle Darry](wiki:Darry) with her daughter. She is present when Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) visits the castle, providing Jaime with information on the death of her husband and the activities of the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) and other outlaws roaming the riverlands. Mariya does not believe the outlaws can be rid of until the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) accept Lancel as lord, as they loved her father and grandfather.(wiki:Mariya_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Mariya's younger sister, [Jeyne](wiki:Jeyne_Darry), is furious that her sons with Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) were not granted the Darry lordship.(wiki:Mariya_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
