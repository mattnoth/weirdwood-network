## Origins

Prince Qyle was a son of Prince [Qoren Martell](wiki:Qoren_Martell). His elder sister, [Aliandra](wiki:Aliandra_Martell) became [Princess of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne) after their father's death.(wiki:Qyle_Martell.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) The [small council](wiki:Small_council) of the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) suggested Qyle as a possible husband for Lady [Baela Targaryen](wiki:Baela_Targaryen) and father of a potential male heir to the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), but he was soon dismissed. Baela was instead arranged to marry Lord [Thaddeus Rowan](wiki:Thaddeus_Rowan), though she broke the betrothal by marrying Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) without the consent of the council.(wiki:Qyle_Martell.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Qyle and his sister [Coryanne](wiki:Coryanne_Martell) were later angered by the attentions the flirtatious Aliandra lavished upon Lord Alyn when he came to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear).(wiki:Qyle_Martell.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
