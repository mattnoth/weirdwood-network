## Identity

Qyle Nymeros Martell was a Dornish prince and younger brother to Princess Aliandra Martell (wiki:Qyle_Martell).

## Origins

Born 116–132 AC. Son of Prince Qoren Martell (track_b: Born, Father).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Martell (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Dornishmen (track_b: Culture). `HOLDS_TITLE` Prince (track_b: Title).

## Narrative Arc

The small council of the Iron Throne suggested Qyle as a possible husband for Lady Baela Targaryen and father of a potential male heir, but he was dismissed. Baela instead married Lord Alyn Velaryon. Qyle and his sister Coryanne were angered by the attentions the flirtatious Aliandra lavished upon Lord Alyn when he came to Sunspear (wiki:Qyle_Martell).
