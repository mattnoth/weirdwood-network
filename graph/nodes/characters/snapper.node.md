## Appearances & Description

The Snapper is the wardrobe mistress, she makes all the costumes for the mummers. [Izembaro](wiki:Izembaro) might be the King of the Mummers, but the Snapper is the one that they all fear.(wiki:Snapper.cite_ref-Mercy_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter

As the mummers prepare to perform *[The Bloody Hand](wiki:The_Bloody_Hand)* the Snapper assists them with their wardrobe.(wiki:Snapper.cite_ref-Mercy_1-2)
