## Origins

The woman's origins and name are unknown. She may come from [Naath](wiki:Naath), as the Naathi people are described as having "dusky skin" and are widely sought after by [slavers](wiki:Slavery) who consider them to make the best slaves.(wiki:Dusky_woman.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_naath.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Dusky_woman.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

[Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) takes the woman from a slaver bound for [Lys](wiki:Lys), and he has her tongue sliced(wiki:Dusky_woman.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) and torn out.(wiki:Dusky_woman.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Appearances & Description

The dusky woman is beautiful, with skin described as brown as oiled teak(wiki:Dusky_woman.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and as charcoal and earth.(wiki:Dusky_woman.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) She is missing her tongue.(wiki:Dusky_woman.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

The woman is quiet, biddable and compliant.(wiki:Dusky_woman.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast For Crows

After [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) turns Lord [Baelor Blacktyde](wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde) over to [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), the new [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North), Euron gives the dusky woman as a bedwarmer to his brother. Victarion is reluctant to accept anything from the hated Euron, but he relents when Euron threatens to have the woman killed if he refuses.(wiki:Dusky_woman.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Victarion has sex with the dusky woman in the cabin of *[Iron Victory](wiki:Iron_Victory)* after his victory at the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), and she treats his wounded hand with boiled vinegar. Victarion later confides his doubts regarding Euron to her.(wiki:Dusky_woman.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) Euron tasks Victarion, the [Lord Captain of the Iron Fleet](wiki:Lord_Captain_of_the_Iron_Fleet), with sailing to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay) and bringing back [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) to be Euron's bride, though Victarion secretly plans to claim Daenerys for himself.(wiki:Dusky_woman.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

### A Dance with Dragons

Victarion assumes that Euron bedded the dusky woman before he gave her away.(wiki:Dusky_woman.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

The dusky woman accompanies Victarion aboard *Iron Victory* when the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) departs for [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). He keeps her in his cabin as his bedwarmer and maid.(wiki:Dusky_woman.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) Victarion often confides in the woman, who never tries to talk back, and she continues to help treat his wounded hand.(wiki:Dusky_woman.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

The woman hisses when she sees [Moqorro](wiki:Moqorro), so Victarion knocks her down to the deck. The [red priest](wiki:Red_priest) apparently uses [magic](wiki:Magic) to heal Victarion's hand.(wiki:Dusky_woman.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)

The dusky woman and Victarion grow closer during their journey, and they continue to sleep together.(wiki:Dusky_woman.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Aboard *[Iron Victory](wiki:Iron_Victory)* in Victarion's cabin, when [Moqorro](wiki:Moqorro) brings [Dragonbinder](wiki:Dragonbinder) forth, the dusky woman lifts up a lantern to give everyone present a look at the horn. After everyone leaves Moqorro asks Victarion if he wishes him to bleed him. Victarion seizes the dusky woman by the wrist and pulls her to him and tells Moqorro that she will do it.(wiki:Dusky_woman.cite_ref-6)
