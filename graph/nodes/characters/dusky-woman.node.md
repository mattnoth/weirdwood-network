## Identity

The dusky woman is a comely mute given by Euron Greyjoy as a bedwarmer to his brother Victarion Greyjoy (wiki:Dusky_woman.cite_ref-Raffc29).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Greyjoy (track_b: Allegiance, qualifier: forcibly as a thrall).

## Appearances & Description

The dusky woman is beautiful, with skin described as brown as oiled teak and as charcoal and earth. She is missing her tongue, which was torn out on Euron's orders. She is quiet, biddable, and compliant (wiki:Dusky_woman.cite_ref-Raffc29).

## Narrative Arc

The dusky woman's origins are unknown; she may come from Naath. Euron Greyjoy took her from a slaver bound for Lys and had her tongue removed. After the kingsmoot, Euron gives her to Victarion as a bedwarmer, threatening to kill her if Victarion refuses. Victarion is reluctant but accepts (wiki:Dusky_woman.cite_ref-Raffc29). She accompanies Victarion aboard Iron Victory on the voyage to Meereen, tending his wounded hand and serving as his confidante — though she cannot speak. She hisses at the red priest Moqorro when he first appears. Despite Victarion's initial intention to sacrifice her to the Drowned God, he never follows through, and they grow closer during the voyage (wiki:Dusky_woman.cite_ref-Radwd56).
