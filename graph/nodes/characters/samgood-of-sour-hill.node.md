## Identity

Samgood of Sour Hill, called Sour Sam, was a scarred one-eyed hedge knight of sixty-three, the eldest champion, who fought with the quickness of a younger man.

## Origins

By [49 AC](wiki:49_AC) the young king [Jaehaerys I](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) still had five vacant spots in the Kingsguard to fill. At the [wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) of his mother [Queen Regent](wiki:Regent) [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) and Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon), it was decided to fill the remaining five slots by a test of arms since [knights](wiki:Knight) from all over the [realm](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) had come to attend the wedding. Jaehaerys agreed, but with a twist - the knights must fight on foot, not the joust, for foes seldom strike a king from [horseback](wiki:Horse).(wiki:Samgood_of_Sour_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

The wild [melees](wiki:Melee) and bloody duels that followed were dubbed by [maesters](wiki:Maesters) as the [War for the White Cloaks](wiki:War_for_the_White_Cloaks) with hundreds of knights competing for the honor of serving in the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). While Ser Samgood of Sour Hill was the eldest of the five champions to win and be given a white cloak, his skills had been honed from a lifetime of battles and he was quicker than men half his age.(wiki:Samgood_of_Sour_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

During Jaehaerys's [minority](wiki:Minority) on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), Samgood and his [sworn brothers](wiki:Sworn_Brother) sparred with their young king, who spent every morning until midday in the castle yard training with sword, shield and any other [weapon](wiki:Armament).(wiki:Samgood_of_Sour_Hill.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Samgood accompanied young Prince [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) when the lad visited the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit) and stated that the prince was either brave or [mad](wiki:Targaryen_madness) after he saw the child smack [Balerion the Black Dread](wiki:Balerion) on the snout. Prince Baelon was known as *Baelon the Brave* after this.(wiki:Samgood_of_Sour_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

In [59 AC](wiki:59_AC) when the winter [disease](wiki:Disease) known as the [Shivers](wiki:Shivers) came to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Samgood and his sworn brother Ser [Victor the Valiant](wiki:Victor_the_Valiant) were among those carried off by the disease. Samgood was seventy-three years old.(wiki:Samgood_of_Sour_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Appearances & Description

When he became a Kingsguard in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC), Samgood was a grizzled man of sixty-three. He was one-eyed, bald and nearly toothless with a gaunt frame.(wiki:Samgood_of_Sour_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
