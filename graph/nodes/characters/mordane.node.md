## Origins

Mordane has tutored [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) in the gentle arts and turning them into noblewomen.(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) When Arya wanted to go [boar](wiki:Boar) hunting in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood), Mordane explained that boar hunting was not for ladies.(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Arya has often daydreamed when Mordane tries to teach the girls about noble [houses](wiki:House).(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Mordane once went to the market square of the [winter town](wiki:Winter_town) with Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark), and [Hodor](wiki:Hodor). The simple Hodor wandered off, and when they eventually found the stableboy he was being teased with sticks by youths.(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Mordane](wiki:Category:Images_of_Mordane)*

Mordane has a bony face and sharp eyes. The [septa](wiki:Septa) has a thin, lipless mouth. She starches her skirts.(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

While strict, Mordane is a good woman who cares about both Sansa and Arya.(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) She finds Sansa to be a delightful student, but is often exasperated with Arya's antics and rebellious nature.(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Mordane presides over the needlework with Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon), [Beth Cassel](wiki:Beth_Cassel), [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole), [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), and [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark). When Mordane admonishes Arya for her poor needlework, Arya becomes angry and embarrasses herself in front of the princess.(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

When Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) is appointed [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Mordane goes south to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in his entourage, to watch over his daughters.(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) The septa criticizes how Arya messily packs for the journey.(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Arya screams at Mordane and refuses to unlock her door while examining [Needle](wiki:Needle), so Lord Stark has his daughter apologize to the septa.(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Mordane accompanies Sansa and Jeyne to the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney), where she approves of Sansa's composure during the tilts. Mordane escorts Jeyne away after she becomes hysterical watching Ser [Hugh](wiki:Hugh) die in a tilt. After the septa returns, she introduces [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) to Sansa.(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Later, at the feast, Mordane and Sansa have seats of high honor by Prince [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). Mordane spills [wine](wiki:Wine) on herself while laughing at [Moon Boy](wiki:Moon_Boy)'s antics. The septa drinks too much and falls asleep at the table, leaving Sansa to be escorted back to the castle by [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane).(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) Mordane is ill the final day of the lists.(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

When Arya goes missing in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), Mordane prays in the [royal sept](wiki:Royal_sept) for the girl's return.(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) The septa is in attendance with Sansa when Eddard holds court as Hand,(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) and she complains of sore feet from standing in the gallery of the [throne room](wiki:Throne_room).(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Lord Stark intends to send Mordane back to Winterfell with his daughters.(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) Mordane is killed, however, along with most of the other members of Eddard's household, after [he is arrested](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark) for treason. Her head is mounted on a spike atop the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Sansa misses Mordane while a captive of the Lannisters.(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) The late septa is among those for whom Sansa sings during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Storm of Swords

After Sansa is [forced to wed](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark) the disfigured [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), she recalls Mordane's teachings that all men are beautiful and thus tries to find the beauty in her new husband.(wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
