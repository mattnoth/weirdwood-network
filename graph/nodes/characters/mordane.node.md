## Identity

Septa Mordane is the septa at Winterfell, serving as tutor to the Stark girls, Sansa and Arya (wiki:Mordane).

## Appearances & Description

Mordane has a bony face, sharp eyes, and a thin lipless mouth. She starches her skirts (wiki:Mordane). While strict, she is a good woman who cares about both Sansa and Arya, though she finds Sansa a delightful student and is often exasperated with Arya's rebellious nature (wiki:Mordane).

## Narrative Arc

Mordane tutors the Stark girls in needlework and the gentle arts. She presides over needlework with Princess Myrcella, Beth Cassel, Jeyne Poole, and the Stark girls; when she admonishes Arya for poor needlework, Arya embarrasses herself before the princess (wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot7). Mordane travels south to King's Landing with Eddard's household (wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot15). During the Tourney of the Hand, Mordane gets drunk on wine and falls asleep, leaving Sansa unattended (wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot29). When Eddard is arrested, Mordane bravely tells Sansa to lock herself in her room, then stands to face the Lannister guards. Her head is later mounted on the walls of the Red Keep (wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot67).
