## Origins

Pate the [hedge knight](wiki:Hedge_knight) married [Amerei Frey](wiki:Amerei_Frey), the eldest daughter of [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey), after she was caught in the stables of the [Twins](wiki:Twins) having sex with three grooms at the same time.(wiki:Pate_of_the_Blue_Fork.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) The bard [Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams), Pate's cousin, sang at his wedding to Amerei, but afterwards Pate did not want to pay for his singing.(wiki:Pate_of_the_Blue_Fork.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Amerei Frey](wiki:Amerei_Frey) is widowed because of Pate's death.(wiki:Pate_of_the_Blue_Fork.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) tells [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) he had married off his granddaughter Amerei to Pate, but Pate was then killed by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Pate_of_the_Blue_Fork.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

### A Feast for Crows

When [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) visits [Castle Darry](wiki:Darry), Lady Amerei tells how Pate had sworn to kill Gregor Clegane when [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey) was still supporting Robb Stark.(wiki:Pate_of_the_Blue_Fork.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
