## Origins

In his youth, Jeremy was a [squire](wiki:Squire) at [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden), where he befriended Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_V)), third son of King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen). The two became constant companions, to the point that Daeron broke his betrothal to [Olenna Redwyne](wiki:Olenna_Redwyne) in [246 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Jeremy_Norridge.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

In [251 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Prince Daeron led an army against the rebels known as [the Rat, the Hawk, and the Pig](wiki:The_Rat,_the_Hawk,_and_the_Pig). Though they managed to quash the rebellion, Daeron was killed in battle, as was Jeremy, who died by his side.(wiki:Jeremy_Norridge.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
