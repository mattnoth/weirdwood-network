### A Clash of Kings

Poxy Tym is hired as a guard at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) after both Lord [Eddard](wiki:Eddard_Stark) and [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) go south.(wiki:Poxy_Tym.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Poxy_Tym.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Tym weeps after the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) by [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn).(wiki:Poxy_Tym.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) orders him to accompany him on his search for [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark).(wiki:Poxy_Tym.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Following the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell), Bran finds that Tym was killed by an axe to his face.(wiki:Poxy_Tym.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
