Kedry has spent half his life studying the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities).(wiki:Kedry.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Appearances & Description

Kedry is fluent in all [the tongues](wiki:High_Valyrian) of the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities), as well as [mongrel Ghiscari](wiki:Old_Ghiscari).(wiki:Kedry.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), tells his daughter, Princess [Arianne](wiki:Arianne_Martell), that Maester Kedry is one of Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell)'s travel companions during his travel to Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), who resides at [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) in [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay).(wiki:Kedry.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

In [Lys](wiki:Lys), Maester Kedry and his five companions take rooms in an inn so Kedry can help Ser [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood) regain his strength, as travelling at [sea](wiki:Summer_Sea) does not agree with the [knight](wiki:Knight). At some point, he gives Ser [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater) a book on [Volantis](wiki:Volantis). They next board the *[Meadowlark](wiki:Meadowlark)* to travel to Volantis.(wiki:Kedry.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

*Meadowlark* is attacked by [corsairs](wiki:Corsairs) while anchored off the coast of the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands), and Maester Kedry is among those slain. Together with Ser [Cletus Yronwood](wiki:Cletus_Yronwood) and Ser [Willam Wells](wiki:Willam_Wells), two other Dornish companions, Kedry is burried at sea.(wiki:Kedry.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
