## Appearances & Description

Anguy is skinny with freckles and red hair. He is fairly young.(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Anguy carries a longbow(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) and uses arrows tipped with bodkins as well as broadheads.(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) He is highly valued due to his exceptional skill with a bow.(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) The only songs he knows are [marcher ballads](wiki:Marcher_ballads).(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

[Fern](wiki:Fern), a girl at [Lambswold](wiki:Lambswold), fancies Anguy.(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

During the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in celebration of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s ascension as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Anguy wins the archery competition, outshooting Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) and [Jalabhar Xho](wiki:Jalabhar_Xho) at a hundred paces and earning himself a sum of ten thousand [golden dragons](wiki:Currency). He refuses an offer from Eddard to join the [Hand's guard](wiki:House_Stark_guards).(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Having spent and lost his fortune, Anguy joins Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) when Eddard dispatches him to bring Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) to [justice](wiki:Laws_and_justice_of_the_Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Rawoiafanguy.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Anguy is with the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) party that captures [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), [Gendry](wiki:Gendry), and [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie). He shoots a duck which the outlaws give to [Sharna](wiki:Sharna),(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) and then a [goose](wiki:Goose) as they continue their travels.(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Arya, who is unable to draw Anguy's longbow, is told tales of the [Dornish Marches](wiki:Dornish_Marches) by the archer.(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) He offers to make a light bow for her once they get to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) Anguy uses his arrows to put [northmen](wiki:Northmen) starving in [crow cages](wiki:Crow_cage) out of their misery at [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept), and he flirts with a freckle-faced wench at the [Peach](wiki:Peach).(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

The outlaws bring the three captured youths to Beric in the [hollow hill](wiki:Hollow_hill).(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Like most members of the brotherhood, Anguy is now a follower of [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor).(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Anguy takes part in the [battle at the burning septry](wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry) with the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions), shooting several of the sellswords.(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) He reveals he squandered his fortune at [Chataya's brothel](wiki:Chataya's_brothel), enjoying lavish excesses with [Jayde](wiki:Jayde), [Alayaya](wiki:Alayaya), and [Dancy](wiki:Dancy).(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) Beric orders Anguy to aid in keeping watch for [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane).(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3) Arya helps Anguy dry horses at the ruins of a [Goodbrook](wiki:House_Goodbrook) village.(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Feast for Crows

After Beric's final death,(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) a part of the brotherhood, including Anguy, go their separate way from those who chose to follow the resurrected, vengeful [Lady Stoneheart](wiki:Lady_Stoneheart).(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)(wiki:Anguy.cite_ref-Rawoiafanguy.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
