## Origins

Ser Gwayne fought during the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion) in [196 AC](wiki:196_AC), at the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field) in Lord [Donnel Arryn](wiki:Donnel_Arryn)'s vanguard. Lord Arryn's lines were shattered and nearly overrun by [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre), until Gwayne appeared with reinforcements.(wiki:Gwayne_Corbray.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale:_house_arryn.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Gwayne came across Daemon and the two dueled ahorsed for nearly an hour. The duel was considered a sight to behold, partly because of the clashing of two [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) swords against each other, with Gwayne wielding [Lady Forlorn](wiki:Lady_Forlorn) and Daemon with [Blackfyre](wiki:Blackfyre). Ultimately, Daemon cut through Gwayne's helm and left him blind and bleeding. Daemon dismounted to ensure the wounded knight was not trampled and ordered [Redtusk](wiki:Redtusk) to bring Gwayne to the [maesters](wiki:Maester) in the rear to see to his wounds.(wiki:Gwayne_Corbray.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Daemon's duel and subsequent compassion to Gwayne was a fatal error, as it allowed for Lord [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers) to position his [Raven's Teeth](wiki:Raven's_Teeth) atop a [ridge](wiki:Weeping_Ridge) and rain arrows upon Daemon and his twin sons, [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Blackfyre) and [Aemon Blackfyre](wiki:Aemon_Blackfyre).(wiki:Gwayne_Corbray.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
