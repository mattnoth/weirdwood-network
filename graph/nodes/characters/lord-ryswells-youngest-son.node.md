## Origins

Lord Ryswell's son was one of the legendary [seventy-nine sentinels](wiki:Seventy-nine_sentinels) who deserted their posts at the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort) and went south of the [Wall](wiki:Wall). He led them to the lands of [House Ryswell](wiki:House_Ryswell), hoping his father would aid them, but instead they were captured and brought back to the Wall. The son and his fellow deserters were then sealed within the Wall as punishment.(wiki:Lord_Ryswell's_youngest_son.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
