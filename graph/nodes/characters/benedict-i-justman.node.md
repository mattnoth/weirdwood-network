## Origins

A [bastard](wiki:Bastard) from Houses [Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood) and [Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken), Benedict Rivers was despised as a boy. He became a great [knight](wiki:Knight), however, and his prowess earned him the support of his parents' families and then other riverlords. Benedict was able to defeat all petty kings in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) over more than thirty years, after which he allowed himself to be crowned.(wiki:Benedict_I_Justman.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
As [king](wiki:Kingship), Benedict the Bold became known as Benedict the Just, which led to him naming his family [House Justman](wiki:House_Justman). During his twenty-three-year reign, Benedict I expanded east to [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) and north to the [Neck](wiki:Neck). He was succeeded by his son, King [Benedict II Justman](wiki:Benedict_II_Justman).(wiki:Benedict_I_Justman.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
