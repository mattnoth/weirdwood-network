## Origins

Torghen hates the [Weeper](wiki:Weeper) for blinding girls, and raping, stealing, or slaying three of [his clan](wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains).(wiki:Torghen_Flint.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

## Appearances & Description

Torghen is a stout man with gnarled, red-knuckled hands as big as hams. He is half a head shorter than [Brandon Norrey](wiki:Brandon_Norrey), but is twice as heavy.(wiki:Torghen_Flint.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

A gruff man,(wiki:Torghen_Flint.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Torghen believes in the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods)(wiki:Torghen_Flint.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and distrusts [free folk](wiki:Free_folk).(wiki:Torghen_Flint.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Old Flint uses a blackthorn cane and wears fur and [iron](wiki:Iron).(wiki:Torghen_Flint.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Old Flint gives aid to King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) when the king marches to the [northern mountains](wiki:Northern_mountains) and then defeats [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) in the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Torghen_Flint.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) His sons [Donnel](wiki:Donnel_Flint) and [Artos Flint](wiki:Artos_Flint) join Stannis's [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell), but Torghen is too old to go himself.(wiki:Torghen_Flint.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Torghen later journeys to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) to attend [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark) of [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark) to [Sigorn](wiki:Sigorn), the new [Magnar of Thenn](wiki:Magnar_of_Thenn). He enjoys the southern [wine](wiki:Wine) at the feast.(wiki:Torghen_Flint.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Old Flint brings five fighting men and a [wet nurse](wiki:Wet_nurse).(wiki:Torghen_Flint.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) Torghen and [Brandon Norrey](wiki:Brandon_Norrey) accompany [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) to the top of the [Wall](wiki:Wall); Torghen is wary of Jon's plan to settle [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) on the [Gift](wiki:Gift).(wiki:Torghen_Flint.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)
