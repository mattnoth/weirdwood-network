## Identity

Grey Wind is a direwolf bonded to Robb Stark (track_b: Owner). He is the litter-mate of Lady, Nymeria, Summer, Shaggydog, and Ghost. Born in 298 AC in the north (track_b: Born). Died in 299 AC at the Twins (track_b: Died). Named for his speed when running (wiki:Grey_Wind).

## Origins

Grey Wind was discovered with his litter-mates in the snow near their dead mother by Robb Stark and Jon Snow north of Winterfell (wiki:Grey_Wind).

## Allegiances

`OWNS`: Robb Stark owns Grey Wind (track_b: Owner). Grey Wind follows Robb through the War of the Five Kings, fighting in multiple battles.

## Appearances & Description

Grey Wind grows large and fierce, adding significantly to Robb's fearsome battlefield reputation. He is bold and faithful, mirroring Robb's own temperament (wiki:Grey_Wind).

## Narrative Arc

**AGOT:** Grey Wind forms a bond with Robb. He kills the wildling Wallen during an ambush on Bran. When the Greatjon menaces Robb during an audience, Grey Wind bites two of his fingers off, helping Robb earn the respect of his bannermen. Grey Wind follows Robb into the War of the Five Kings, killing several men at the Battle of the Whispering Wood (wiki:Grey_Wind, agot chapters 1–69).

**ACOK:** At Riverrun, Grey Wind intimidates Ser Cleos Frey. Robb uses Grey Wind to discover a secret goat track past the Golden Tooth into the westerlands. At the Battle of Oxcross, Grey Wind stampedes the Lannister horses after Brynden Tully's scouts cut the lines, causing a rout of Stafford Lannister's host. Rumors spread that Robb fed Stafford's heart to the direwolf (wiki:Grey_Wind, acok chapters 7–39).

**ASOS:** Grey Wind kills men at Ashemark and the Crag. He distrusts the smell of Ser Rolph and Lady Sybell Spicer, but Robb — having lost faith in the direwolves as guardians after Bran and Rickon's reported deaths — sends Grey Wind away from him more often. At the Twins, Grey Wind growls at the Freys and balks at the gatehouse. Catelyn urges Robb to trust the direwolf's instincts, but Robb has him tethered in the stables during the Red Wedding. Grey Wind is killed during the massacre; the Freys sew his head onto Robb's corpse and parade it as a mockery (wiki:Grey_Wind, asos chapters 14–64).

**Post-death:** Grey Wind's killing is recalled in AFFC and ADWD. Robb's body with Grey Wind's head is paraded by the Freys. Lady Stoneheart's Brotherhood later retaliates against the Freys (wiki:Grey_Wind, affc-44, adwd-3/4).
