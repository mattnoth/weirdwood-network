## Origins

Bethany was the daughter of Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower) and his first wife, who died in childbed at some point. She had three brothers, [Lyonel](wiki:Lyonel_Hightower), [Martyn](wiki:Martyn_Hightower_(son_of_Ormund)), and [Garmund](wiki:Garmund_Hightower). During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), her father married again to [Samantha Tarly](wiki:Samantha_Tarly), but Ormund died in the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton). Years later, Bethany's step-mother would become her good-sister when Sam married again to Ormund's heir, Lyonel.(wiki:Bethany_Hightower.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

After the death of Queen [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC) and the subsequent planned betrothal between King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) and [Myrielle Peake](wiki:Myrielle_Peake), Lady Samantha wrote a bold letter proposing that Aegon marry both Bethany and Sam's sister, [Sansara Tarly](wiki:Sansara_Tarly).(wiki:Bethany_Hightower.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Appearances & Description

According to [Samantha Tarly](wiki:Samantha_Tarly), Bethany was a beautiful girl with smooth soft skin, lustrous hair, and the sweetest manner.
