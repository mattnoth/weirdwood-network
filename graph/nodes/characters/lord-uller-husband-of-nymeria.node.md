## Origins

During [Nymeria's War](wiki:Nymeria's_War), Lord Uller supported Princess [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria) and her husband Lord [Mors Martell](wiki:Mors_Martell) against King [Yorick V Yronwood](wiki:Yorick_V_Yronwood) and his [bannermen](wiki:Bannermen).(wiki:Lord_Uller_(husband_of_Nymeria).cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

At some point after the death of Lord Martell at the [Third Battle of the Boneway](wiki:Third_Battle_of_the_Boneway), Lord Uller married Nymeria and served her as a counselor and [consort](wiki:Prince_Consort_of_Dorne) since Nymeria remained the unquestioned [ruler of Dorne](wiki:Princess_of_Dorne). Their marriage remained childless and Nymeria married Ser [Davos Dayne](wiki:Davos_Dayne), the [Sword of the Morning](wiki:Sword_of_the_Morning), after Uller's death.(wiki:Lord_Uller_(husband_of_Nymeria).cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
