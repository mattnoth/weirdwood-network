## Identity

Whitesmile Wat is a singer with a high, sweet voice, in service to House Lannister (wiki:Whitesmile_Wat).

## Allegiances

Wat is `SWORN_TO` House Lannister (track_b: Allegiance).

## Narrative Arc

During his stay at Harrenhal as part of Lord Tywin Lannister's army, Wat possibly impregnates the jailer's wife (wiki:Whitesmile_Wat.cite_ref-Racok30). Genna Lannister later brings Wat from Lannisport to Riverrun for the siege. He returns to the west after the fall of Riverrun, joining Ser Forley Prester's force (wiki:Whitesmile_Wat.cite_ref-Raffc44).
