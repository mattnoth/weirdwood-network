## Origins

Young Lord Willam had been married to [Barbrey Ryswell](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin) for less than half a year before the outbreak of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). Barbrey urged him to send one of his kin in his place, but Willam insisted on leading the levies from [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) himself. Barbrey gave her husband a fine [red stallion](wiki:Horse), the pride of the herds of her father, Lord [Rodrik Ryswell](wiki:Rodrik_Ryswell). Willam vowed he would return to her mounted on the steed.(wiki:Willam_Dustin.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Willam was present at [the wedding](wiki:Weddings_of_Catelyn_and_Lysa_Tully) of his liege, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), to Lady [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun). During the [bedding](wiki:Bedding) custom, Willam praised Catelyn's breasts to Eddard.(wiki:Willam_Dustin.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Willam was one of six companions who accompanied Eddard to the [tower of joy](wiki:Tower_of_joy) in the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), where Lord Stark sought his sister, [Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark). They were opposed by three [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), however, and only Eddard and Lord [Howland Reed](wiki:Howland_Reed) survived the [ensuing battle](wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy). Eddard buried the fallen in cairns raised from the tower's stones.(wiki:Willam_Dustin.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) Lord Stark returned the horse to Willam's widow, Barbrey, when he returned to the [north](wiki:North), and told her that Willam had died an honorable death.(wiki:Willam_Dustin.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

While under the influence of the [milk of the poppy](wiki:Milk_of_the_poppy), taken because of his broken leg,(wiki:Willam_Dustin.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) has a fever dream of Willam and his companions at the [combat at the tower of joy](wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy).(wiki:Willam_Dustin.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

### A Dance with Dragons

In the [crypts of Winterfell](wiki:Crypts_of_Winterfell), [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin), the widow of Willam and the [Lady of Barrowton](wiki:Lady_of_Barrowton), reveals to [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) that she still resents that Eddard brought Willam's red stallion back from [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) but not his bones. She mentions that Eddard did bring home his sister [Lyanna](wiki:Lyanna_Stark)'s bones, but Barbrey promises that Eddard's own bones (being transported north from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun)) will never be laid to rest in the crypts beside hers, as she intends to feed them to her [dogs](wiki:Dog).(wiki:Willam_Dustin.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)
