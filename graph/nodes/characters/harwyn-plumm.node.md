## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Harwyn and his brothers, Ser [Dennis](wiki:Dennis_Plumm) and Ser [Peter](wiki:Peter_Plumm), are among the men sent by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) to the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) in search of his son, after Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) has been released from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) by Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark).(wiki:Harwyn_Plumm.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Ser Harwyn clears [broken men](wiki:Broken_men) from [Darry](wiki:Darry), allowing Lord [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister) to take his seat there.(wiki:Harwyn_Plumm.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Subsequently, Hardstone serves as commander of the garrison at the castle.(wiki:Harwyn_Plumm.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Harwyn considers the tales that Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) cannot be killed to be lies. He promises Lancel's wife, Lady [Amerei](wiki:Amerei_Frey), whose father [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey) was hanged by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), that he is going to bring her Beric's head. He believes the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) in the riverlands secretly hide and support the brotherhood, lying about this to their lords.(wiki:Harwyn_Plumm.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Upon hearing reports that there are [outlaws](wiki:Outlaws) on the other side of the [Trident](wiki:Trident), Harwyn takes five [knights](wiki:Knight) and twenty archers and goes searching for them, making him miss Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s visit to Darry when Jaime is on his way to ending the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun). Jaime suspects that "Gatehouse Ami" has an amorous interest in Harwyn. When Jaime warns Lancel, his cousin expresses his indifference, announcing that he is going to renounce lordship and marriage in favor of joining the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons) and that Harwyn can have both, if he wants to.(wiki:Harwyn_Plumm.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)
