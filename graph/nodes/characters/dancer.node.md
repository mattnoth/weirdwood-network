## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Dancer is trained by [Joseth](wiki:Joseth), Winterfell's [master of horse](wiki:Master_of_horse), especially for [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) after he becomes crippled. She is taught to respond to touch, sound and the reins due to Bran's inability to use his legs.(wiki:Dancer.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Bran is riding Dancer in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) when he is threatened by [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) and deserters from the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Dancer.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Bran later rides Dancer when [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) departs [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) with the [northern](wiki:North) host.(wiki:Dancer.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Bran rides Dancer when [Cley Cerwyn](wiki:Cley_Cerwyn) arrives at Winterfell for the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast).(wiki:Dancer.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The horse is draped in bardings of snowy white wool emblazoned with the grey [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) of [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) when Bran rides into the Great Hall for the feast.(wiki:Dancer.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

After the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) by [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy), Bran eventually disappears from the castle. According to [Aggar](wiki:Aggar), all of the horses remain in the stable.(wiki:Dancer.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

When Bran emerges from the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell) after the [sacking of the castle](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell), Bran sees dead horses in the burned stables and assumes Dancer is among them.(wiki:Dancer.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
