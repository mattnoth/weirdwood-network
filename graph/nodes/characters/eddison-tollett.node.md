## Origins

Dolorous Edd appears to have been born to a lesser branch of [House Tollett](wiki:House_Tollett), as he speaks of being raised in a crude house such as the poorer of the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) might have. Per [George R. R. Martin](wiki:George_R._R._Martin), he was recruited to the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) by [Yoren](wiki:Yoren), who told him women could not resist a man in uniform, leaving out the Night's Watch vow of celibacy.(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Rssm1186.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Edd once lost track of his white [horse](wiki:Horse) while it was snowing.(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Rasos0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Appearances & Description

Eddison is a thin man, dour and grey-haired.(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Racok13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Rasos0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He has a long face, like a mule's.(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He has a dry and pessimistic sense of humor.(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Racok13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He is well-liked by his brothers in the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Edd is part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), trusts his horse to Edd, and he also has Edd retrieve his axe to give as a gift to [Craster](wiki:Craster).(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) When [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) joins [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand)'s mission into the [Frostfangs](wiki:Frostfangs), Qhorin reminds Jeor that Edd can serve the Lord Commander as his steward.(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Edd survives when [wights](wiki:Wights) attack the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men), helping [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) retreat.(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Rasos18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) They are among the survivors to make it back to [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep).(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Once at Craster's Keep, Edd is one of the few men that takes Sam seriously when he tells the men he slew an [Other](wiki:Others) with his [dragonglass](wiki:Dragonglass) dagger.(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Rasos18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) Edd, along with [Dywen](wiki:Dywen), makes Sam and [Grenn](wiki:Grenn) tell Lord Commander Mormont the tale.(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) After the death of the Lord Commander during the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep), Edd flees with Grenn, [Giant](wiki:Bedwyck) and other loyalists back to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) for fear of being killed by the mutineers.(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

When the [Weeper](wiki:Weeper) starts his assault near the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower), [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) assembles the garrison of Castle Black, along with Edd, to aid Ser [Denys Mallister](wiki:Denys_Mallister).(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) During the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), the defenders name a straw sentinel after Edd.(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

Against his wishes, Edd receives a few votes during the election of the new Lord Commander.(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) Edd later puts forth [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow)'s name instead, and Jon is chosen by the black brothers.(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Feast for Crows

When Samwell Tarly leaves Castle Black, Edd says farewell to him.(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Edd carries on his duties as Lord Commander Snow's steward. He is one of the men brought along as muscle to assist Jon in escorting [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) out of Castle Black to carry out his duties as garrison commander of [Greyguard](wiki:Greyguard). When Janos refuses to obey the order, Edd and [Iron Emmett](wiki:Emmett) escort Janos outside for his execution, and Edd fetches Jon a block per his request.(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

While riding to [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town), Jon jokes that the solemn face carved by wildlings on an ash [heart tree](wiki:Heart_tree) resembles Edd.(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Radwd21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

Eventually, Lord Commander Snow assigns Emmett to [Long Barrow](wiki:Long_Barrow), where Jon sent [wildling](wiki:Wildling) [spearwives](wiki:Spearwife), with Edd as his steward and second in command.(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) [Satin](wiki:Satin) replaces Edd as the Lord Commander's steward.(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

Edd briefly returns to Castle Black with six wagons so that more spearwives can be transported to Long Barrow.(wiki:Eddison_Tollett.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
