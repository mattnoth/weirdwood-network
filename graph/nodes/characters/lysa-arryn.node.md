## Identity

Lysa Arryn, née Tully, is a noblewoman of House Tully and the youngest daughter of Lord Hoster Tully and Lady Minisa Whent. She is the wife of Jon Arryn and after his death, Lady Regent of the Vale of Arryn (wiki:Lysa_Arryn).

## Origins

Born 266–268 AC at Riverrun (track_b: Born). Daughter of Hoster Tully and Minisa Whent (track_b: Father, Mother). Two years younger than Catelyn (wiki:Lysa_Arryn).

## Appearances & Description

Was a pretty, slender, high-breasted girl in her youth, dimpled and delicate. Has grown thick of body by her thirties from many pregnancies, miscarriages, and stillbirths. Despite being younger than Catelyn, she looks ten years older; her face has become pale and puffy. Has pale watery Tully blue eyes, a small petulant mouth, and beautiful auburn hair falling to her waist (wiki:Lysa_Arryn). By turns proud, fearful, cruel, dreamy, reckless, impulsive, timid, stubborn, vain, and inconstant. Fiercely protective of her son Robert (wiki:Lysa_Arryn).

## Narrative Arc

As a girl at Riverrun, Lysa fell in love with her father's ward Petyr Baelish. She became pregnant by Petyr, but Hoster forced her to drink moon tea to abort the child (wiki:Lysa_Arryn). During Robert's Rebellion, Hoster married Lysa to Jon Arryn — twice his age — to secure the rebel alliance. The marriage lacked warmth; Lysa suffered numerous miscarriages and stillbirths, producing only the sickly Robert Arryn (track_b: Issue, wiki:Lysa_Arryn).

At Petyr Baelish's urging, Lysa poisoned Jon Arryn with tears of Lys and sent a letter to Catelyn falsely blaming the Lannisters — the act that sets in motion the entire plot of the series (wiki:Lysa_Arryn). She flees to the Eyrie with Robert and refuses to join any faction in the War of the Five Kings (wiki:Lysa_Arryn).

When Catelyn brings Tyrion Lannister to the Eyrie for trial, Lysa threatens him with the Moon Door. Tyrion demands trial by combat and escapes (wiki:Lysa_Arryn). Later, Petyr Baelish arrives at the Eyrie with Sansa Stark disguised as his bastard daughter Alayne. Lysa marries Petyr (track_b: Spouses), but in a jealous rage after seeing him kiss Sansa, Lysa tries to throw Sansa through the Moon Door. Petyr intervenes, declaring "Only Cat," and pushes Lysa to her death through the Moon Door (wiki:Lysa_Arryn). Died 300 AC at the Eyrie (track_b: Died).
