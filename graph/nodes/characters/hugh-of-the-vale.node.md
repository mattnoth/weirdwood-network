## Identity

Ser Hugh of the Vale was a young knight, formerly squire to Lord Jon Arryn for four years. Knighted by King Robert I Baratheon in Jon's memory upon his lord's death (wiki:Hugh_of_the_Vale).

## Allegiances

## Appearances & Description

Young with rough-hewn features, not handsome. Owns shiny new armor and wears a blue cloak with crescent moon trim (wiki:Hugh_of_the_Vale).

## Narrative Arc

Hugh stayed in King's Landing after Lysa Arryn's departure. When Eddard Stark investigated Jon Arryn's death, Hugh was brusque and refused to speak with anyone but the Hand himself. Before Eddard could question him directly, Hugh entered the Hand's tourney and was killed by Ser Gregor Clegane when Gregor's lance drove through his throat (wiki:Hugh_of_the_Vale). Ser Barristan Selmy stood vigil for Hugh. Varys later suggested Jon may have been poisoned by his squire, though this remains unconfirmed (wiki:Hugh_of_the_Vale). Eddard sent Hugh's armor and remains to his mother in the Vale (wiki:Hugh_of_the_Vale).
