## Origins

Rodrik unsuccessfully advised [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), not to rebel against King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). Rodrik had two tall sons who died in the [sea battle off Fair Isle](wiki:Sea_battle_off_Fair_Isle) during [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion).(wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

When the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) was still tolerated in the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands), Rodrik kept [septons](wiki:Septon) at [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers) to help care for his books, which he reads in the [Book Tower](wiki:Book_Tower). Rodrik's sister, [Gwynesse](wiki:Gwynesse_Harlaw), insists that she should rule Ten Towers, as she is seven years his elder.(wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6)

Rodrik is a widower,(wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw.cite_ref-Rawoiafrodrik_harlaw.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) who does not expect to wed again, and his heir is his cousin [Harras](wiki:Harras_Harlaw), the Knight of [Grey Garden](wiki:Grey_Garden).(wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7)

## Appearances & Description

An average-looking man, Rodrik has brown hair and eyes, and a short, neat beard that is grey. He has requested a [Myrish lens](wiki:Myrish_lens) to assist with his vision.(wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

> Lord Rodrik Harlaw was neither fat nor slim; neither tall nor short; neither ugly nor handsome. His hair was brown, as were his eyes, though the short, neat beard he favored had gone grey.

— Asha Greyjoy's first sight of her uncle at Ten Towers, AFFC The Kraken's Daughter I (`sources/chapters/affc/affc-the-krakens-daughter-01.md:55`) [L897]

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

After the death of [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North), Rodrik supports his niece, Balon's daughter [Asha](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), and calls her supporters to [Harlaw](wiki:Harlaw).(wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Rodrik is visited by Lords [Baelor Blacktyde](wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde) and [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley) prior to the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), which has been called by [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy).(wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8)

Rodrik assembles those faithful to Princess Asha at the [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers). [Hotho Harlaw](wiki:Hotho_Harlaw), who wants Rodrik to marry his daughter, brings his lord a copy of Archmaester [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn)'s *[Book of Lost Books](wiki:Book_of_Lost_Books)*. In the [Book Tower](wiki:Book_Tower), Rodrik tries to convince Asha not to attend the kingsmoot, fearing a bad outcome. He offers to name Asha as the heir to Ten Towers, with the [island of Harlaw](wiki:Harlaw) passing to Ser [Harras Harlaw](wiki:Harras_Harlaw), but she refuses.(wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-9)

At the kingsmoot Rodrik is one of the first to call Asha's name. However, her uncle, [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), is chosen king instead.(wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), Rodrik is spotted by Lord Captain [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) and [Nute the Barber](wiki:Nute_the_Barber) talking with Lords [Gorold Goodbrother](wiki:Gorold_Goodbrother) and [Dunstan Drumm](wiki:Dunstan_Drumm) in the yard of [Lord Hewett's castle](wiki:Lord_Hewett's_castle) on [Oakenshield](wiki:Oakenshield). They discuss their fears that the gains from the battle are little compared to the enmity of the [Reach](wiki:Reach) they have earned. During the feast in [House Hewett](wiki:House_Hewett)'s hall, King Euron tells the ironborn they will conquer [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). Rodrik is skeptical of Euron's planning as well as his claims of having sailed the [Smoking Sea](wiki:Smoking_Sea) and seen [Valyria](wiki:Valyria). With Euron giving [Greyshield](wiki:Greyshield) to Harras, Hotho feels that he is now Rodrik's heir.(wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

At [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte), Asha recalls how after Euron was named the new king at the kingsmoot, Rodrik told her to flee while her uncle was busy. He also recommended she read Archmaester [Haereg](wiki:Haereg)'s *[History of the Ironborn](wiki:History_of_the_Ironborn)*, which recounts the only time a kingsmoot decision was overturned.(wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
