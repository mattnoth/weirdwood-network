## Origins

[Missandei](wiki:Missandei) and her three brothers, including Marselen and [Mossador](wiki:Mossador), were captured on their native island of [Naath](wiki:Naath) by raiders from the [Basilisk Isles](wiki:Basilisk_Isles), and sold into [slavery](wiki:Slavery) in [Astapor](wiki:Astapor). Missandei was trained as a scribe, but Marselen and his two brothers were [gelded](wiki:Eunuch) and trained by the [Good Masters](wiki:Good_Masters) to be [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied).(wiki:Marselen.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Marselen and Mossador became Unsullied,(wiki:Marselen.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) and the third brother died at some point.(wiki:Marselen.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) purchases [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) from the [Good Masters](wiki:Good_Masters) of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor). After freeing the soldiers, she turns them against the [slavers](wiki:Slavers) with the [fall of Astapor](wiki:Fall_of_Astapor).(wiki:Marselen.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Daenerys also takes into her service [Missandei](wiki:Missandei), Marselen's sister, as a scribe and handmaid.(wiki:Marselen.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Dance with Dragons

As Daenerys told the Unsullied to abolish the practice of choosing a new name every day,(wiki:Marselen.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Marselen chose to go back to his birth name

[Mossador](wiki:Mossador), a brother of Marselen and Missandei, is killed in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) by the [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy).(wiki:Marselen.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Marselen remains Missandei's only living brother.(wiki:Marselen.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

When three free companies are formed of [freedmen](wiki:Freedmen), Marselen helps train the [Mother's Men](wiki:Mother's_Men) and is named their captain.(wiki:Marselen.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) He represents the Mother's Men when Daenerys hold a council.(wiki:Marselen.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

Marselen accompanies Daenerys when she meets Astapori refugees outside of Meereen.(wiki:Marselen.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

After Daenerys disappears from [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit), Marselen shares Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy)'s caution about warring against [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai).(wiki:Marselen.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) He attends Barristan's council.(wiki:Marselen.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
