## Appearances & Description

Walder is tall and stout, with a red face and big round belly. He is known as "Little Walder" because he is fifty-two days younger than his cousin, ["Big Walder" Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Jammos)), although Little Walder has since grown half a foot taller.(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Little Walder's lambswool doublet is silver-grey with dark blue trim.(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) His armor is fine silver plate with enameled blue chasings. His crest has streamers of blue and grey silk and he rides a [dappled grey courser](wiki:Horse).(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) The boy carries a dagger on his hip.(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

Walder is boastful and mean.(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) The cruel boy enjoys games.(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3)(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Little Walder is present at the [Twins](wiki:Twins) when [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) comes to treat with Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey). As part of the terms allowing [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) to cross at the Twins, Little Walder is sent to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) to be fostered.(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Little Walder and his cousin, [Big Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Jammos)), are sent to Winterfell to become wards of [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark). They are tutored by Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin).(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Racok28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Little Walder quickly makes friends with [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark), but is not friendly with [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark).(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) The children play [monsters and maidens](wiki:Monsters_and_maidens), [rats and cats](wiki:Rats_and_cats), [come-into-my-castle](wiki:Come-into-my-castle), and [lord of the crossing](wiki:Lord_of_the_crossing), which Little Walder wins more often than not.(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2) Rickon angers Bran by showing the Freys the [crypts of Winterfell](wiki:Crypts_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-3)

During the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast_(299)), Little Walder trains with knights from [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor); Bran thinks Little Walder strikes the [quintain](wiki:Quintain) harder than Big Walder. Little Walder taunts the simple-minded [Hodor](wiki:Hodor), and he boasts of being able to slay Bran's [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), [Summer](wiki:Summer).(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-4) Bran sends boiled beets to Little Walder during the celebratory meal. Little Walder dislikes the [Reed](wiki:House_Reed) siblings, [Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed) and [Jojen](wiki:Jojen_Reed), considering the [crannogmen](wiki:Crannogmen) to be frogeaters and mudmen.(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

When word of Ser [Stevron Frey](wiki:Stevron_Frey)'s death at [Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross) arrives at Winterfell, Little Walder and Big Walder argue over who is next in line to inherit the Twins, showing no sadness at their relative's passing.(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

The Frey cousins are taken captive by [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) when he leads his [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) in [capturing Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) One of the cousins volunteers for Theon's party that searches the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) for Bran and Rickon when they disappear from the castle, claiming he has hunting experience. Although Theon does not identify which one, the boy states his desire for a wolfskin cloak and denigrates crannogmen. The Frey flushes three rabbits from the wood and is able to shoot one with an arrow. Theon eventually sends the boy back to Winterfell with most of the hunting party before continuing on to the [Acorn Water](wiki:Acorn_Water).(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

The Freys are spared by [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) during the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Storm of Swords

When Meera tells Bran about the [Knight of the Laughing Tree](wiki:Knight_of_the_Laughing_Tree), the cruel behavior of three squires toward [the crannogman](wiki:Howland_Reed) in the story reminds Bran of Little Walder.(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

A letter written by Big Walder and signed by both Frey cousins is sent to the [Twins](wiki:Twins) claiming [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) burned Winterfell. At [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), [Lame Lothar Frey](wiki:Lothar_Frey) conveys this false report to [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), now [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Little Walder has become a [squire](wiki:Squire) to Ramsay.(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Little Walder is offered as a husband to [Wylla Manderly](wiki:Wylla_Manderly) by [Rhaegar Frey](wiki:Rhaegar_Frey) when Frey envoys go to [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) to return the bones of Ser [Wendel Manderly](wiki:Wendel_Manderly) to his father, Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly).(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

Little Walder and [Big Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Jammos)) bring [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy), who has been mutilated by [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), from the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort)'s dungeon to Ramsay's feast with [Arnolf Karstark](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark) and [Hother Umber](wiki:Hother_Umber).(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2)

The Frey cousins are with Ramsay at the [siege of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin).(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

Lady [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin) gives grey colts to Walder and his cousin when they join Ramsay in searching for [missing Freys](wiki:Frey_Pies/Theories) near [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton).(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) When Ramsay has Lord [Harwood Stout](wiki:Harwood_Stout) host a feast, Little Walder is tasked with keeping Ramsay's cup full.(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2) Theon notes Little Walder is becoming more and more like Ramsay, which disturbs Theon and Big Walder.(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-3)

Little Walder attends [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark) of Ramsay and "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)" in the [godswood of Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell), and the mists make the boy resemble a red bull to Theon.(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) With a snowstorm striking Winterfell, Little Walder joins(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) other squires in building snowmen along the battlements and in the yard.(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) One of [Abel](wiki:Abel)'s girls teaches Little Walder how to dance.(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-1)

Later, Little Walder's frozen, bloody body is discovered in a snowbank below the ruined [First Keep](wiki:First_Keep) of Winterfell. A blood-spattered Big Walder accompanies Little Walder's uncle, Ser [Hosteen Frey](wiki:Hosteen_Frey), and his soldiers when they enter the [Great Hall of Winterfell](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell). According to Big Walder, Little Walder was looking for a [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) knight who owed him [silver](wiki:Silver) from a game of [dice](wiki:Dice). Wyman denies Hosteen's accusation of having ordered Walder's death, and an enraged Hosteen attacks Lord Manderly after he insults House Frey. The confrontation leaves six White Harbor men and two Freys dead. It takes the intervention of Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton)'s men to prevent a battle within Winterfell.(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Theon suspects Abel and his girls of Little Walder's murder, but [Rowan](wiki:Rowan) claims it is no work of theirs.(wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
