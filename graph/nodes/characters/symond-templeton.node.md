## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Ser Symond joins with Lord [Yohn Royce](wiki:Yohn_Royce) in becoming one of the [Lords Declarant](wiki:Lords_Declarant).(wiki:Symond_Templeton.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He attends the parley between the Lords Declarant and Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), arriving with Bronze Yohn. Petyr intends to befriend Symond after the meeting.(wiki:Symond_Templeton.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

To everyone's surprise, Symond attends [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Lyonel_Corbray_and_Lady_Corbray) of Lord [Lyonel Corbray](wiki:Lyonel_Corbray) in [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown), an event masterminded by Petyr.(wiki:Symond_Templeton.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
