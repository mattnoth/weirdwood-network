## Identity

Joy Hill is the bastard daughter of Gerion Lannister and Briony, a common woman (wiki:Joy_Hill). She is approximately ten years old during the main events.

## Origins

Born 288 AC (track_b: Born). Father: Gerion Lannister (track_b: Father). Mother: Briony (track_b: Mother). Gerion was lost at sea nearly a decade ago, when Joy was two or three (wiki:Joy_Hill).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Lannister (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Westermen (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

According to her cousin Jaime, Joy is a sweet child, but a lonely one (wiki:Joy_Hill.cite_ref-Raffc33).

## Narrative Arc

As part of the pact between Tywin Lannister and Walder Frey for the Red Wedding, Joy is to wed a natural son of Walder once she is older (wiki:Joy_Hill.cite_ref-Rasos51). After the siege of Riverrun, Lady Sybell Spicer mentions to Jaime that Tywin had promised a bride from Casterly Rock for her son Raynald Westerling. Because Sybell uses the word "joy," Jaime believes Tywin intended Joy Hill to marry Raynald. Sybell is angered at the idea of her son marrying a bastard. According to a semi-canon source, this is a misunderstanding on Jaime's part (wiki:Joy_Hill.cite_ref-semi_canon).
