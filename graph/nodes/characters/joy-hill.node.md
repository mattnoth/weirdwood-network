## Origins

[Gerion Lannister](wiki:Gerion_Lannister) fathered Joy on [Briony](wiki:Briony), a [common woman](wiki:Smallfolk).(wiki:Joy_Hill.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_lannister_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Gerion was lost at sea nearly a decade ago,(wiki:Joy_Hill.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) when Joy was two or three years old.

## Appearances & Description

According to her cousin [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), Joy is a sweet child, but a lonely one.(wiki:Joy_Hill.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

As part of the pact between Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) and Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) concerning the betrayal of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Joy is to wed a [natural son](wiki:Bastard) of Walder from [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey) once she is older.(wiki:Joy_Hill.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows

After the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Lady [Sybell Spicer](wiki:Sybell_Spicer) mentions to Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) that his late father, Tywin, had promised a bride from [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) for her eldest son, Ser [Raynald Westerling](wiki:Raynald_Westerling). Because Sybell mentions "joy", Jaime thinks Tywin intended for Joy Hill to marry Raynald. Sybell is angered at the idea of her son marrying a bastard.(wiki:Joy_Hill.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) According to a semi-canon source, this is a misunderstanding on Jaime's part, as he did not know of Tywin's earlier plans with Joy and House Frey.(wiki:Joy_Hill.cite_ref-Rawoiafjoy_hill.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
