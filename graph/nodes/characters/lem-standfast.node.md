## Origins

After being trained by Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey)'s sons with other peasants from his lands, Lem went off to fight for [House Osgrey](wiki:House_Osgrey) during the [Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Blackfyre_Rebellion). He was one of the only three surviving men that returned with Ser Eustace, along with [Dake](wiki:Dake) and [Pate](wiki:Pate_(Standfast)). Lem brought back a knife and a fine pair of boots from the war.
