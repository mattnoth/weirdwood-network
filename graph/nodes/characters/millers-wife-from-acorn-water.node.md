## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

After Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) captures "[Reek](wiki:Ramsay_Snow)", his party stops at the mill for a while before continuing to Winterfell. The miller's wife sells them hay for their [horses](wiki:Horse), and Ser Rodrik speaks with [her sons](wiki:Miller's_sons).(wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

When [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) leads [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) raiders to [capture Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell), [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) become his prisoners and [hostages](wiki:Hostage). '"Reek", freed from Winterfell's dungeons, swears himself to Theon.(wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Bran and Rickon apparently escape Winterfell. Theon gathers a search party, including Reek, Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin), and the kennelmaster [Farlen](wiki:Farlen) and his [dogs](wiki:Dog), but fails to track them down in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood). However, Reek tells Theon he has a feeling that the Stark boys are hiding at the mill, and secretly presents Theon with the boys' fine clothing he had brought along in a bag. Theon sends the rest of the search party back to Winterfell, except for Reek and three ironborn, [Aggar](wiki:Aggar), [Gelmarr](wiki:Gelmarr), and [Gynir](wiki:Gynir).(wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

At the mill, Reek kills the miller's two sons, and when their mother begs Theon for mercy, Gelmarr cuts her down with one blow of his axe. The dead children are decapitated and their heads [skinned](wiki:Flaying) and dipped in tar by Reek, and Theon dresses their bodies in the Stark children's finery. Theon returns to Winterfell and presents the heads and headless bodies as Bran and Rickon. Theon mounts their heads on the castle walls, and burns the bodies and clothing to bones and ashes.(wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Later, Theon has a nightmare that he is having sex with the miller's wife again, but she has teeth below, and she both tears out his throat and bites off his manhood. Theon also dreams of the feast for King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s arrival at Winterfell, but soon realizes he is dining with the dead, including the miller's wife.(wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

### A Dance with Dragons

Having returned to Winterfell for [Ramsay Bolton's wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark), a regretful Theon feels that the castle is full of [ghosts](wiki:Ghosts) for him.(wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) He thinks of the old ghosts from the [crypts](wiki:Crypts_of_Winterfell) and the younger ones that he had made himself, including the miller's wife from Acorn Water and her two young sons.(wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

In the [godswood of Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell), Theon sees Bran's face in the [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) and momentarily thinks he is being haunted by his ghost, before recalling that they had not actually killed Bran and Rickon, but instead only miller's sons. He speaks aloud that he had to have two heads or he would have been mocked, and is overheard by [Holly](wiki:Holly) and [Rowan](wiki:Rowan).(wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Rowan accuses Theon of being a [kinslayer](wiki:Kinslayer), and Theon denies any relationship to Bran and Rickon while thinking to himself that the two children he killed were just some miller's sons. He thinks that he had known the miller's wife for years, and remembers that she had big heavy breasts, a sweet mouth, and a merry laugh, pleasures that he can never again experience.(wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
