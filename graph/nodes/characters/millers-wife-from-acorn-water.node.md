## Identity

The miller's wife from Acorn Water is the unnamed wife of the miller at Acorn Water, a settlement near Winterfell (wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

She has big soft breasts, stretch marks on her belly, a sweet mouth, and a merry laugh. Theon Greyjoy bedded her several times over the years but cannot recall her name, only her features (wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water).

## Narrative Arc

When Bran and Rickon Stark escape Winterfell in ACOK, Theon's search party fails to track them. Ramsay Snow (disguised as "Reek") suggests the boys are hiding at the mill. At the mill, Ramsay kills the miller's two young sons; when their mother begs Theon for mercy, Gelmarr cuts her down with his axe. The dead children's heads are skinned and tarred, then presented as the Stark boys to maintain the deception (wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water.cite_ref-Racok56). In ADWD, Theon recalls the truth: the dead boys were the miller's sons, not Bran and Rickon. He rationalizes that Ramsay killed the woman and children, not him (wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water.cite_ref-Radwd37).
