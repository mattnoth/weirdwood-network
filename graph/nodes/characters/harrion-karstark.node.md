## Appearances & Description

Harrion is a fierce young man with a beard.(wiki:Harrion_Karstark.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He wears a black cloak patterned with white suns.(wiki:Harrion_Karstark.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Harrion accompanies his father, Lord [Rickard](wiki:Rickard_Karstark), and his two brothers, [Eddard](wiki:Eddard_Karstark) and [Torrhen](wiki:Torrhen_Karstark), to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) when [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls the banners in response to the [arrest of Eddard Stark](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Harrion bows respectfully when welcomed by [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark).(wiki:Harrion_Karstark.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

When the [northern](wiki:North) host reaches the [Twins](wiki:Twins) and splits, Harrion is placed under the command of Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) and marches down the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad). He is captured during the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork)(wiki:Harrion_Karstark.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) by a [hedge knight](wiki:Hedge_knight).(wiki:Harrion_Karstark.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

### A Clash of Kings

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), offers to trade Harrion and Ser [Wylis Manderly](wiki:Wylis_Manderly) for [Willem Lannister](wiki:Willem_Lannister) in an exchange of prisoners.(wiki:Harrion_Karstark.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) holds captives from the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork) at the [Tower of Dread](wiki:Tower_of_Dread) in [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Harrion_Karstark.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) observes Harrion walking the battlements alone.(wiki:Harrion_Karstark.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

After Tywin departs Harrenhal, the castle [is taken](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal) by Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), a bannermen of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Harrion_Karstark.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Roose sends Harrion, [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover), Ser [Helman Tallhart](wiki:Helman_Tallhart), and other northerners to take [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale).(wiki:Harrion_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) They are defeated by Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) in the [battle at Duskendale](wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale), however, and the survivors are attacked by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) while retreating to the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad).(wiki:Harrion_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

King Robb executes Lord Rickard Karstark for treason after he murders the captive [Willem Lannister](wiki:Willem_Lannister) and [Tion Frey](wiki:Tion_Frey) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun). Robb fears this will make him an enemy of Harrion, Rickard's heir.(wiki:Harrion_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Robb is betrayed by the [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) and [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton) in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Harrion_Karstark.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Harrion is held captive at [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool).(wiki:Harrion_Karstark.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Harrion remains a captive of the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) at Maidenpool.(wiki:Harrion_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Harrion_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

[Arnolf Karstark](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark) declares for [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), hoping that when the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) learns of this they will execute Harrion, thus making Harrion's sister [Alys](wiki:Alys_Karstark) the heir to [Karhold](wiki:Karhold).(wiki:Harrion_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Arnolf's plan is to wed his son [Cregan](wiki:Cregan_Karstark) to Alys and thereby claim Karhold.(wiki:Harrion_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Arnolf's declaration for Stannis is also a plot orchestrated alongside Roose Bolton; the plan is to turn against Stannis once his forces [reach Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell).(wiki:Harrion_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

Arnolf's plot to claim Karhold is ruined when [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), arranges [a marriage](wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark) between Alys and [Sigorn](wiki:Sigorn), the new [Magnar of Thenn](wiki:Magnar_of_Thenn). Jon has Cregan imprisoned at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) and has [Clydas](wiki:Clydas) send a [raven](wiki:Raven) to [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) in an attempt to warn Stannis of Arnolf's treachery. Cregan, who believes Harrion to be dead, is outraged at Alys marrying a [wildling](wiki:Wildling).(wiki:Harrion_Karstark.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
