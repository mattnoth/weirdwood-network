## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Glendon is among the men allied with Lord [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) when they arrive at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) from [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea).(wiki:Glendon_Hewett.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) Glendon and his men drag [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) with [Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt) before Janos and Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne);(wiki:Glendon_Hewett.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) Glendon kicks Jon in the ribs.(wiki:Glendon_Hewett.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Glendon also kicks Rattleshirt into silence.(wiki:Glendon_Hewett.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

### A Feast for Crows

Glendon serves as master-at-arms of Eastwatch.(wiki:Glendon_Hewett.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

In the absence of [Cotter Pyke](wiki:Cotter_Pyke), Glendon is named temporary commander of Eastwatch. Jon, now [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), is informed of Glendon's appointment in a message from Cotter and Maester [Harmune](wiki:Harmune). Jon is troubled by the news, as Glendon, while capable, was friendly with Alliser and the late Janos.(wiki:Glendon_Hewett.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
