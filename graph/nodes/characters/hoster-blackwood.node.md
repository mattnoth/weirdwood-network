## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

After Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) negotiates the end of the [siege of Raventree](wiki:Siege_of_Raventree) with Lord [Tytos](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood), Hoster is sent with Jaime as a hostage to ensure his father's good behavior and continued allegiance to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). Hoster tells Jaime about [Blackwood Vale](wiki:Blackwood_Vale) and the feud between the Blackwoods and the [Brackens](wiki:House_Bracken).(wiki:Hoster_Blackwood.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
