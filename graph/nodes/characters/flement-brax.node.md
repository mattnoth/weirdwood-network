## Appearances & Description

Flement wears [silver](wiki:Silver) armor inlaid with [amethysts](wiki:Amethyst) and a striped purple-and-silver cloak. His [horsehead](wiki:Horse) helmet has a spiral horn two feet long, and his shield displays the [unicorn](wiki:Unicorn) of [House Brax](wiki:House_Brax).(wiki:Flement_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Flement is part of the force commanded by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Flement_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Flement leads a party of mounted horsemen to challenge [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and the [clansmen](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) accompanying him when they approach the camp of Tywin's army. He tells Tyrion that Lord Tywin has taken the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads) for his quarters, leading Tyrion there.(wiki:Flement_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Flement rides under the command of Ser [Addam Marbrand](wiki:Addam_Marbrand) in the right flank during the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:Flement_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

News arrives at Lord Tywin's camp of the defeat of the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) forces in the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps). The messenger was part of Lord [Andros Brax](wiki:Andros_Brax)'s camp on the west side and tells of Andros's fateful attempt to cross the [Tumblestone](wiki:Tumblestone) on rafts to come to help to the north camp. They came under attack by a force led by Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully), however, and an incredulous Flement is told Andros drowned under the weight of his armor.(wiki:Flement_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Flement's older brother, [Tytos Brax](wiki:Tytos_Brax), becomes the new [Lord of Hornvale](wiki:Lord_of_Hornvale).(wiki:Flement_Brax.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

When Lord Tywin orders Tyrion to act as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) in his place in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Tyrion asks why Tywin is not choosing someone else, mentioning Flement in this context, among others. Tyrion receives the answer that Tywin has chosen him, because he is his son.(wiki:Flement_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Clash of Kings

Ser Flement is among Lord [Tywin's](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) army as it attempts to cross the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) of the [Trident](wiki:Trident), leading to the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords). Early in the engagement, Tywin commands him to force a crossing six miles south of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), but his attempt is repulsed when [Mallister](wiki:House_Mallister) bowmen reign high arcing shots over the shields of the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) soldiers and [scorpions](wiki:Scorpion_(weapon)) send heavy stones on them to break up the formation.(wiki:Flement_Brax.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Flement is among the nobles sent with [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to greet the [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) party, headed by Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell), when they arrive at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Tyrion presents him as the heir to [Hornvale](wiki:Hornvale).(wiki:Flement_Brax.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Flement is called as a witness for the prosecution in the trial of Tyrion for [the death](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). He tells of how he heard Tyrion threaten the king.(wiki:Flement_Brax.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Ser Flement leads two hundred heavy horse that are part of Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s force to [take Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun). He joins Jaime and others for supper at [Hayford](wiki:Hayford) on the first night of their ride. During the meal, Flement annoys Jaime with admiration for his golden hand, especially after Jaime accidentally knocks over a goblet of [wine](wiki:Wine).(wiki:Flement_Brax.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

While at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Flement engages in a training fight with Ser [Lyle Crakehall](wiki:Lyle_Crakehall) in the yard, cheered on by soldiers. Jaime thinks the Strongboar will have the upper hand in the fight.(wiki:Flement_Brax.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
