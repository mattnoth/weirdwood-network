## Identity

Boremund Baratheon (b. 52 AC) — child of Alyssa Velaryon and Rogar Baratheon; sworn to House Baratheon; Lord of Storm's End.

## Appearances & Description

At birth, Boremund was a robust and healthy baby, red-faced with a fuzz of jet-black hair.(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) At eight-and-twenty, he was the image of his father, brawny and powerful, with a booming laugh, a great black beard, and a mane of thick black hair.(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
According to [Septon](wiki:Septon) [Eustace](wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)), Boremund was like stone, "hard and strong and unmoving".(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) He was a belligerent and formidable man,(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) though not as belligerent as his son [Borros](wiki:Borros_Baratheon).(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Boremund was an outspoken and staunch supporter of the claims of his niece, [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aemon)), and a steadfast friend to her.(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

## Origins

### Youth[ ]

Boremund was the eldest son of Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) and Dowager Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon).(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_targaryen_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Alyssa announced her pregnancy with Boremund in [51 AC](wiki:51_AC). As she was forty-four years old at the time and thought to be well beyond her child-bearing years, the pregnancy was received as a miracle, with the [High Septon](wiki:High_Lickspittle) himself proclaiming it a blessing from the [gods](wiki:The_Seven). Boremund was born in early [52 AC](wiki:52_AC). Boremund was named by his father. His mother never completely recovered from his birth, and died two years later, giving birth to Boremund's sister [Jocelyn](wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon).(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
In late [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Boremund attended the [tourney](wiki:Tourney_at_King's_Landing_on_the_anniversary_of_the_king's_coronation) at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) commemorating the tenth anniversary of the king's coronation, alongside his father and sister.(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) When the [Shivers](wiki:Shivers) struck the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) in [59 AC](wiki:59_AC), both Boremund and Jocelyn were stricken, but recovered.(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
The relationship between Boremund's father Rogar and uncle, Ser [Borys](wiki:Borys_Baratheon), Rogar's former heir, deteriorated after Boremund's birth. While Rogar's health declined, Borys left the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) and by [61 AC](wiki:61_AC) had made common cause with the [Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Jaehaerys_I)) in the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains). Fearing for Boremund's safety after his death, Rogar requested King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) to watch over Boremund, while sending Boremund's sister and cousins to King's Landing to serve as [wards](wiki:Ward) of the king. Half a year after Rogar and King Jaehaerys fought against the Vulture Kind and Borys in the [Third Dornish War](wiki:Third_Dornish_War), defeating their enemies, Rogar died at Storm's End with Boremund by his side.(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

### Lord of Storm's End[ ]

Boremund inherited the titles of [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End) and [Lord Paramount of the Stormlands](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Stormlands) from his father. Due to his young age, his uncle Ser [Garon](wiki:Garon_Baratheon) served as his [regent](wiki:Regent) and guardian. In [73 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Ser [Lucamore Strong](wiki:Lucamore_Strong) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) was disgraced for having broken his vows by fathering children and having families. The knight's third wife and her young children were sent to Storm's End to be fostered with Boremund and Ser Garon.(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
In [80 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Lord Boremund was raised as a potential suitor for Princess [Daella Targaryen](wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)) by Queen Alysanne, selected for his wealth and high birth. Daella's good relationship with Boremund's sister, Jocelyn, was believed to be much in his favor. However, Daella eventually chose Lord [Rodrik Arryn](wiki:Rodrik_Arryn) for her husband.(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)
King Jaehaerys and Boremund learned that Prince [Morion Martell](wiki:Morion_Martell) of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) sought to land at [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath) and invade the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) half a year before the Dornish prince attempted his invasion. When Morion finally sailed for the stormlands in [83 AC](wiki:83_AC), Boremund awaited him on Cape Wrath with his army. However, the Dornish fleet was burned by Jaehaerys and his sons with their [dragons](wiki:Dragon) before they ever came ashore. In [92 AC](wiki:92_AC), Boremund crossed to [Tarth](wiki:Tarth) to join forces with Lord [Cameron Tarth](wiki:Cameron_Tarth) against the invading [Myrish](wiki:Myr) pirates. After the death of Prince [Aemon Targaryen](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)), Boremund and Lord Cameron Tarth repelled the Myrmen from the island together with Aemon's brother, Prince [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)), and his dragon [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar).(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)
With Prince Aemon dead, Jaehaerys announced that his new heir was his second son Prince [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)), rather than Aemon's daughter and Boremund's niece Princess [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aemon)). Both Boremund and his sister Jocelyn were angered by the king's decision. When Baelon died in [101 AC](wiki:101_AC), Jaehaerys convened a [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council) to decide the matter of succession.(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) Boremund gave his support to Princess Rhaenys and her young son, [Laenor Velaryon](wiki:Laenor_Velaryon), but eventually Baelon's son [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) was named heir.(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### Legacy[ ]

Boremund had at least one son, [Borros](wiki:Borros_Baratheon), who succeeded him as Lord of Storm's End, and gave him four granddaughters and one grandson.(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) When the succession crisis after Viserys's death escalated into the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), it was believed that Borros would support the side of Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), as Boremund had been a staunch supporter of Princess Rhaenys and her son, Rhaenyra's late husband Ser Laenor Velaryon.(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) However, Lord Borros was infuriated by Rhaenyra's assumption of House Baratheon's support, and sided with the claim of King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3)(wiki:Boremund_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2)
