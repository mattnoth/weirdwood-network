## Origins

[Dacks](wiki:Dacks) was a squire for a Redfort, presumably Lord Horton, when [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) and [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) were wards of Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie).(wiki:Horton_Redfort.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

[Domeric Bolton](wiki:Domeric_Bolton) squired for three years for Horton(wiki:Horton_Redfort.cite_ref-Rawoiafdomeric_bolton.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) before returning to the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort). Lord Redfort apparently had a high opinion of Domeric, thinking the lad had the making of a [tourney](wiki:Tourney) champion. Domeric had enjoyed the company of Horton's sons and came to regard them with brotherly affection.(wiki:Horton_Redfort.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Appearances & Description

Horton is a short, old man with a well-kept beard, grey hair, and mild eyes.(wiki:Horton_Redfort.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He is polite.(wiki:Horton_Redfort.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) believes that Lord Horton, a possible suitor for the widowed Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn), is a dangerous and proud man in his own way.(wiki:Horton_Redfort.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Horton is one of the lords who joins with Lord [Yohn Royce](wiki:Yohn_Royce) at [Runestone](wiki:Runestone)(wiki:Horton_Redfort.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) to try and depose [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), [Lord Protector of the Vale](wiki:Lord_Protector_of_the_Vale).(wiki:Horton_Redfort.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

When the [Lords Declarant](wiki:Lords_Declarant) come to parley with Petyr, Horton ascends to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) in the basket because he is too old to make the climb. Horton suggests that Petyr departs for the war-torn [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), as Petyr is also the new [Lord Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident), but Littlefinger declines. Horton gently cautions Ser [Lyn Corbray](wiki:Lyn_Corbray) when the knight threatens Ser [Lothor Brune](wiki:Lothor_Brune) with [Lady Forlorn](wiki:Lady_Forlorn).(wiki:Horton_Redfort.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Petyr convinces the Lords Declarant to give him a year to settle the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn), but he privately tells [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) that the elderly Horton and Lady [Anya Waynwood](wiki:Anya_Waynwood) may be dead in a year.(wiki:Horton_Redfort.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Horton forces his youngest son, the newly-knighted Ser [Mychel](wiki:Mychel_Redfort), to marry [Ysilla Royce](wiki:Ysilla_Royce), the daughter of Bronze Yohn.(wiki:Horton_Redfort.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
