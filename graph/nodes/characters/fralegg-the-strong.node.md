### A Feast for Crows

During the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot), Fralegg offers [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) his support.(wiki:Fralegg_the_Strong.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) He is listed as one of Victarion's supporters at the kingsmoot.(wiki:Fralegg_the_Strong.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
