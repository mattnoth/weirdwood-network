## Identity

Heward is a guard sworn to House Stark who accompanies Lord Eddard Stark to King's Landing (track_b: Allegiance).

## Allegiances

Heward is `SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance) and belongs to `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Heward accompanies Eddard Stark to King's Landing. He is present at Chataya's brothel, playing forfeits with a wench while Eddard meets with Littlefinger and visits one of Robert's bastards (agot-eddard-09). When Jaime Lannister ambushes Eddard's party in the streets, Heward is killed — struck by a spear in the belly (agot-eddard-09). Arya later recalls Heward among the Stark men killed by the Lannisters (agot-arya-04).
