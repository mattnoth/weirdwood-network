## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Heward accompanies Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) south to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) when Eddard is made the new [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). [Jory Cassel](wiki:Jory_Cassel), [Wyl](wiki:Wyl_(guard)) and Heward escort Eddard to the [brothel](wiki:Brothel) that one of King Robert's [bastards](wiki:Barra) resides in and plays forfeits with a brothel wench. The three guards are slain by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s men in the streets of the capital as a lesson to Eddard.(wiki:Heward.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
