## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Ronnel is among the rangers in the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), the force that leaves [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) and the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) in search of [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark) and [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder). He survives the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist) and become the acting [First Ranger](wiki:First_Ranger), commanding the rangers after the death of [Thoren Smallwood](wiki:Thoren_Smallwood).(wiki:Ronnel_Harclay.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Ronnel's fate during the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep) is unknown, although he is not specified by [Grenn](wiki:Grenn) as having returned to Castle Black,(wiki:Ronnel_Harclay.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and [Black Jack Bulwer](wiki:Jack_Bulwer) later replaces him as First Ranger,(wiki:Ronnel_Harclay.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) making it likely he was slain during the mutiny.
