## Origins

Although [Rosbys](wiki:House_Rosby) are not known for their robustness, Bethany was healthy enough to have many children, one each year she was married to Walder. Of these, five survived to adulthood: Ser [Perwyn](wiki:Perwyn_Frey), Ser [Benfrey](wiki:Benfrey_Frey), Maester [Willamen](wiki:Willamen_Frey), [Olyvar](wiki:Olyvar_Frey) and [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey).(wiki:Bethany_Rosby.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Appearances & Description
