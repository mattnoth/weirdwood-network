## Origins

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Adrian took command of the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) host after Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister) fell in the [Battle at the Red Fork](wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork). Thrice repelled by the rivermen, Adrian and a hundred handpicked [knights](wiki:Knight) stripped off their heavy armor and swam across the river, upstream of the battle. Adrian then led the knights in circling around to take Lord [Tristan Vance](wiki:Tristan_Vance)'s lines in the rear, personally slaying the riverlord. The ranks of the rivermen shattered, allowing the [westermen](wiki:Westermen) to cross into the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Adrian_Tarbeck.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

The Lannister host continued to march east towards [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal),(wiki:Adrian_Tarbeck.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) winning victories under Adrian's command.(wiki:Adrian_Tarbeck.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The westermen were briefly checked at [Acorn Hall](wiki:Acorn_Hall) by Lord [Joseth Smallwood](wiki:Joseth_Smallwood) and Lord [Petyr Piper](wiki:Petyr_Piper), but Lord Piper died [in battle](wiki:Battle_at_Acorn_Hall) and Lord Smallwood retreated back to his castle. Three days later, the rivermen regrouped under a [hedge knight](wiki:Hedge_knight), Ser [Harry Penny](wiki:Harry_Penny), and gave battle to the westermen again. While the Lannister host once again prevailed, Ser Adrian and Ser Harry killed each other in the fighting.(wiki:Adrian_Tarbeck.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

With Adrian's death, command of the westermen passed to the aged and wounded Lord [Humfrey Lefford](wiki:Humfrey_Lefford).(wiki:Adrian_Tarbeck.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Adrian_Tarbeck.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
