## Origins

After King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) was crowned and flew on [Balerion](wiki:Balerion) to [Visenya's Hill](wiki:Visenya's_Hill) to raise his standard, his mother Dowager Queen [Visenya](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) challenged those who questioned her son's right to rule to prove themselves, a challenge that the Warrior's Sons accepted. Ser Damon proposed a [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven), as had been done in [Andalos](wiki:Andalos). A trial of seven took place with Damon and six of his Warrior's Sons against King Maegor and his six champions.(wiki:Damon_Morrigen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Damon_Morrigen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Damon and his comrade Ser [Willam the Wanderer](wiki:Willam_the_Wanderer) were the last two of the Warrior's Sons standing against Maegor, whose champions had all been killed. Badly wounded, Damon fell to the king and [Blackfyre](wiki:Blackfyre).(wiki:Damon_Morrigen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
