## Origins

In [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) sent three women to see to Queen [Alysanne](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen)'s religious instruction: Septa [Ysabel](wiki:Ysabel), and the novices [Lyra](wiki:Lyra) and Edyth, alongside the famously pious Lady [Lucinda Tully](wiki:Lucinda_Broome). It was the hope of the Queen Regent that the fiercely righteous women would convince Alysanne and perhaps even King [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) that brother lying with sister was an abomination in the eyes of the [Faith](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) before their incestuous union could tear the realm apart again. Instead, they were moved by the queen's devotion to her husband.(wiki:Edyth.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

By [51 AC](wiki:51_AC), she had sworn her vows to become a septa. She attended Queen Alysanne at [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) where she was slashed across the face defending the queen when she was attacked by women attending [Jonquil's Pool](wiki:Jonquil's_Pool) who wished to kill the "[abomination](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I))" Alysanne was carrying.(wiki:Edyth.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

After Queen [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I))'s beloved Septa [Maryam](wiki:Maryam) was poisoned by [Androw Farman](wiki:Androw_Farman) in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), Edyth arrived on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) to assume her place.(wiki:Edyth.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

In [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), she accompanied Queen Alysanne during her pregnancy with Prince [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)).(wiki:Edyth.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Edyth died of the [Shivers](wiki:Shivers) of [59 AC](wiki:59_AC) and [60 AC](wiki:60_AC).(wiki:Edyth.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
