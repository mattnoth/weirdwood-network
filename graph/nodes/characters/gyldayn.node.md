## Origins

Gyldayn began writing *[Fire & Blood, Being a History of the Targaryen Kings of Westeros](wiki:Fire_&_Blood,_Being_a_History_of_the_Targaryen_Kings_of_Westeros)*, which includes *[The Sons of the Dragon](wiki:The_Sons_of_the_Dragon)*, about Kings [Aenys I](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen) and [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen);(wiki:Gyldayn.cite_ref-1) *[The Rogue Prince](wiki:The_Rogue_Prince,_or,_A_King's_Brother)*, about Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen);(wiki:Gyldayn.cite_ref-2)(wiki:Gyldayn.cite_ref-Rtrp.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) *[The Princess and the Queen](wiki:The_Princess_and_the_Queen,_or,_the_Blacks_and_the_Greens)*, a history of the civil war known as the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons); and other sections.

Currently only the first volume of Gyldayn's *[Fire & Blood, Being a History of the Targaryen Kings of Westeros](wiki:Fire_&_Blood,_Being_a_History_of_the_Targaryen_Kings_of_Westeros)* is known to have been completed.(wiki:Gyldayn.cite_ref-Rfablineages_and_family_tree.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Gyldayn shared his uncompleted work with Archmaester [Perestan](wiki:Perestan), however, and he permitted Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel) to use fragments of his work in *[The World of Ice and Fire](wiki:The_World_of_Ice_and_Fire_(Yandel))*.(wiki:Gyldayn.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Gyldayn.cite_ref-GyldaynRetcon_6-0)

According to a semi-canon source, Gyldayn lived into the reign of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Gyldayn.cite_ref-Gyldayn_7-0)
