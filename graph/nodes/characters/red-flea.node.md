## Identity

Red Flea is one of the daily-assigned names of an Unsullied purchased by Daenerys Targaryen. He has previously been known as Black Rat, Brown Flea, and possibly Blue Toad or Blue Worm (wiki:Red_Flea.cite_ref-Rasos23).

## Appearances & Description

A Ghiscari native. Daenerys thinks he looks like he might be Kraznys mo Nakloz's taller, fitter brother (wiki:Red_Flea.cite_ref-Rasos23).

## Narrative Arc

While Daenerys inspects available Unsullied for sale in Astapor, Kraznys mo Nakloz demonstrates that Unsullied are not permitted permanent names by questioning this soldier. Red Flea recites his current name, yesterday's (Black Rat), the day before's (Brown Flea), and is uncertain about three days ago — possibly Blue Toad or Blue Worm (wiki:Red_Flea.cite_ref-Rasos23).
