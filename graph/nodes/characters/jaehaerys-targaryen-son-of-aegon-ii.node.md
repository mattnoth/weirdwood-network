## Identity

Prince Jaehaerys Targaryen was a prince of House Targaryen, son of King Aegon II Targaryen and Queen Helaena Targaryen (track_b: Father, Mother). He held the title of Prince (track_b: Title). He was aligned with the Greens during the Dance of the Dragons (track_b: Allegiances).

## Origins

Born in 123 AC (track_b: Born). His father was Aegon II Targaryen and his mother was Helaena Targaryen (track_b: Father, Mother). He was of Valyrian race and Crownlands culture (track_b: Race, Culture).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiances). `SWORN_TO` Greens (track_b: Allegiances).

## Narrative Arc

Jaehaerys died in 129 AC at the Tower of the Hand in King's Landing (track_b: Died). He was one of the victims of the Blood and Cheese incident during the Dance of the Dragons, murdered as part of a scheme to exact revenge on the Greens.
