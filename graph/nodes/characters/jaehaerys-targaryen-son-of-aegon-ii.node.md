## Identity

Jaehaerys Targaryen (son of Aegon II) (123–129 AC) — child of Aegon II Targaryen and Helaena Targaryen; sworn to House Targaryen; Prince. One of 4 Jaehaerys Targaryens — see the Jaehaerys Targaryen disambiguation entry.

## Origins

### Early life

Prince Jaehaerys was born in the year [123 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), barely a year after his parents had wed. Jaehaerys was named by his mother, Helaena. The [greens](wiki:Greens) at the court of King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) were happy to proclaim that Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) now had heirs of his own. A [dragon egg](wiki:Dragon_egg) was placed in Jaehaerys's cradle, and as well one in the cradle of his twin sister, Princess [Jaehaera](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen). Both eggs soon hatched, and for Jaehaerys, the dragon [Shrykos](wiki:Shrykos) was born.(wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_II).cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

That same year, two of Jaehaerys's half-siblings were born: a boy, whose mother was a [whore](wiki:Whore) in the [Street of Silk](wiki:Street_of_Silk), and a girl, whose mother was a maidservant of Jaehaerys's grandmother, Queen [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower). In [127 AC](wiki:127_AC), Jaehaerys's trueborn brother Prince [Maelor](wiki:Maelor_Targaryen) was born.(wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_II).cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_II).cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

In [129 AC](wiki:129_AC), on the third day of the third moon, Jaehaerys, his sister Jaehaera, his brother Maelor, and their mother, Princess [Helaena Targaryen](wiki:Helaena_Targaryen), went to visit their grandfather, King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen). There, Jaehaerys listened to a fictional tale(wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_II).cite_ref-4) Viserys told, about their great-great-grandsire, King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), Jaehaerys's namesake, and how he had flown north of the [Wall](wiki:Wall) on his [dragon](wiki:Dragon) to fight against [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings), [giants](wiki:Giants), and [wargs](wiki:Warg). That night, King Viserys died in his sleep.(wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_II).cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

### The Dance of the Dragons

At the time of the death of King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen), Jaehaerys and his twin were six years old. His father, [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), was crowned king by the [greens](wiki:Greens) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), but Aegon's older half-sister, [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), who had been Viserys's proclaimed heir, was crowned queen by the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). This provoked a civil war that became known as the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). With his father now king, Jaehaerys became the heir to the throne.(wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_II).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

At the start of the war, Jaehaerys's uncle, Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen), killed Prince [Lucerys Velaryon](wiki:Lucerys_Velaryon), the second son of Queen Rhaenyra. Her husband Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) declared vengeance, writing that there would be "An eye for an eye, a son for a son".(wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_II).cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Sometime thereafter, two men, remembered as [Blood](wiki:Blood_(the_butcher)) and [Cheese](wiki:Cheese_(the_rat-catcher)), snuck into the chambers of Dowager Queen [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower) in the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand). As was her custom, Queen [Helaena Targaryen](wiki:Helaena_Targaryen) took her three children to visit their grandmother at dusk, before they went to bed. Once Helaena entered the chambers with Jaehaerys, Jaehaera, and Maelor, Blood barred the door and slew Helaena's guardsman, while Cheese snatched Maelor. Cheese told Helaena that they were two "debt collectors", and asked which one of her sons she wanted to lose. The queen offered herself but the two killers refused, telling her that it had to be a son, a boy. As the queen agonized over the decision, Cheese warned her to make a choice soon, before Blood grew bored and raped Jaehaera. The weeping Helaena eventually named her youngest child, two-year-old Maelor. Cheese told Maelor his mother wanted him dead, then grinned at Blood, who slew Prince Jaehaerys instead, striking off the boy's head with a single blow from his sword. The two men did no further harm to Helaena, Jaehaera, or Maelor, fleeing with Jaehaerys's head in hand while Helaena screamed.(wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_II).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Appearances & Description

Jaehaerys was larger and healthier than his twin, but he was also born with six fingers on his left hand, and six toes on each foot.(wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_II).cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
