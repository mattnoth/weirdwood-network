## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of the Tickler](wiki:Category:Images_of_the_Tickler)*

The Tickler is of medium height and build, with a face so ordinary it is difficult to establish his age.(wiki:Tickler.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) The Tickler casually inflicts pain.(wiki:Tickler.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

The Tickler wears plain garb like that of the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk).(wiki:Tickler.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) An agile man, he uses a shortsword, dagger, and throwing knives.(wiki:Tickler.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

The Tickler is one of the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men), the group that captures [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), [Gendry](wiki:Gendry), and [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie) near the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). On the way to [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), the Tickler tortures smallfolk on the orders of Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane), trying to gain information on the whereabouts of Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) and any food and valuables they might know the locations of.(wiki:Tickler.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) The Tickler is sometimes aided by other men-at-arms, such as [Chiswyck](wiki:Chiswyck). For this, Arya adds their names to [her list](wiki:Arya_Stark).(wiki:Tickler.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

The Mountain takes [Rafford](wiki:Rafford), Chiswyck, and the Tickler on a foraging mission from Harrenhal.(wiki:Tickler.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The Tickler eventually departs Harrenhal alongside Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) as part of Gregor's vanguard.(wiki:Tickler.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Tywin's host is repelled at the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) in the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords).(wiki:Tickler.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Arya informs [Harwin](wiki:Harwin) and the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) of the Tickler's atrocities.(wiki:Tickler.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) The Tickler continues to be included in Arya's list, though as time goes on she struggles to remember his face as well as the other marked members of Gregor's men-at-arms. His face in particular proves elusive, due to his ordinary looks.(wiki:Tickler.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

After Gregor's [capture of Harrenhal](wiki:Capture_of_Harrenhal) from [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat), the Tickler and [Polliver](wiki:Polliver) take a [pimply squire](wiki:Pimply_squire) to the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads) so the youth can lose his [virginity](wiki:Virgin) to one of the [whores](wiki:Whores) there. They encounter Arya and Gregor's brother, [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane), who alleges that the men-at-arms plan to torture the innkeeper to find his gold. When a fight ensues, the Tickler and Polliver begin to overwhelm Sandor. Arya stabs the unarmored Tickler in the back, and when he falls to the ground she stabs him repeatedly while screaming the questions he had asked smallfolk while interrogating them. Arya and Sandor claim a few [pennies](wiki:Currency) from the Tickler's purse.(wiki:Tickler.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

### A Feast for Crows

Raff tells Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) that the Tickler, Polliver, and the Sarsfield squire were hacked to death by Sandor and a [peasant](wiki:Peasant) boy.(wiki:Tickler.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

While training at the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White), Arya sees the Tickler's face hanging on a wall during a dream.(wiki:Tickler.cite_ref-Radwd64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
