## Identity

Sansa Stark is the eldest daughter of Lord Eddard Stark and Catelyn Stark, born in 286 AC at Winterfell (track_b: Born, Father, Mother). She holds the titles Princess and Lady of Winterfell (track_b: Titles). Known by several aliases including "Little bird" (Sandor Clegane's name for her), "Alayne Stone" (her disguise identity in the Vale), and "Lady Lannister" (through marriage to Tyrion) (track_b: aliases).

## Appearances & Description

Sansa has long, thick auburn hair (agot-sansa-01, agot-sansa-02) and Tully coloring like her brothers Robb, Bran, and Rickon (agot-catelyn-03). She is described as beautiful — Littlefinger says she is "the very image of Cat at the same age" (agot-sansa-04). She typically wears fine clothing: a blue gown that brings out the auburn of her hair (agot-sansa-02), or mourning attire of ivory silk dyed black (agot-sansa-05). Her direwolf is Lady, who is killed early in AGOT (agot-bran-02).

## Narrative Arc

Sansa begins AGOT as a romantic idealist, charmed by the prospect of marrying Prince Joffrey and becoming queen. She names her direwolf Lady and is paired with Joffrey in the royal procession, looking radiant (agot-jon-01). During the incident at the Trident, Sansa claims she "cannot remember" what happened, leading to Arya's fury and Lady's execution in Nymeria's place (agot-eddard-03). In King's Landing, Sansa is dazzled by the tourney and Ser Loras Tyrell (agot-sansa-02). She attends court, gossips with Jeyne Poole, and idealizes Joffrey despite growing evidence of his cruelty (agot-sansa-03). When Eddard discovers the truth about Cersei's children and plans to flee King's Landing, Sansa — unknowingly — goes to Cersei and reveals her father's plans (agot-sansa-04). After Eddard's arrest, Sansa is confined as a hostage in Maegor's Holdfast. Cersei manipulates her into writing letters summoning Robb to swear fealty (agot-bran-06, agot-sansa-04). She pleads for her father's life before the court, kneeling before Joffrey (agot-sansa-05). After Eddard's execution, Joffrey forces her to view the severed heads on the battlements, including her father's and Septa Mordane's (agot-sansa-06). She is married to Tyrion Lannister against her will and later flees King's Landing under Littlefinger's protection, disguised as his bastard daughter Alayne Stone.
