## Origins

Instead of fighting off the [Andals](wiki:Andals), Garth IX brought a [septon](wiki:Septon) to his court and made him part of his councils. The first [sept](wiki:Sept) at [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) was built during Garth's reign, although he continued to worship the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods) in the [godswood of Highgarden](wiki:Godswood_of_Highgarden).(wiki:Garth_IX_Gardener.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf773_1-2)
Garth took Andals into his service as household [knights](wiki:Knights) and retainers, and he bequeathed lands and wives and lordships for the more powerful of the Andal kings and other Andal adventurers descending on the [Reach](wiki:Reach) in exchange for pledges of fealty.(wiki:Garth_IX_Gardener.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf773_1-3)
Garth also sought after Andal craftsmen, especially blacksmiths and stonemasons, to settle throughout the Reach, supplying him and his bannermen with [iron](wiki:Iron) instead of [bronze](wiki:Bronze) and strengthening their castles and holdfasts with Andal masonry.(wiki:Garth_IX_Gardener.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf773_1-4)
