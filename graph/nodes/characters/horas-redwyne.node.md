## Origins

When [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) visited the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor), Horas and [Hobber](wiki:Hobber_Redwyne) despised him on sight. They found a new method to humiliate him in the practice yard every day. One day, Horas made Sam squeal like a pig while he begged for quarter. After Sam returned to [Horn Hill](wiki:Horn_Hill), he learned that he was supposed to have become Paxter's [page](wiki:Page) and [cupbearer](wiki:Cupbearer) while Horas became Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly)'s [ward](wiki:Ward), but the plans fell through because of Sam's unsuitability at the Arbor.(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

## Appearances & Description

Horas is homely, with orange hair and a square, freckled face.(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) (agot-arya-05:107: "homely youths with orange hair and square, freckled faces"; mocking nicknames "Ser Horror and Ser Slobber" coined by Sansa Stark and Jeyne Poole)

Horas despises [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), the son of Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly).(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) "Ser Horror", a mocking nickname given to Horas by [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole), becomes his address by the entire court.(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) is unable to tell the the twins apart(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) and does not consider them to be particularly intelligent.(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) The twins often argue over which one will be the next [Lord of the Arbor](wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor).(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

Horas has a chestnut mare.(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Horas and his brother, Ser [Hobber](wiki:Hobber_Redwyne), take part in the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Horas is unhorsed by [Jory Cassel](wiki:Jory_Cassel) in his first joust.(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole) mock Horas and his brother, Ser [Hobber](wiki:Hobber_Redwyne), as Ser Horror and Ser Slobber, respectively, and giggle whenever they see them.(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

The twins remain in the capital after the death of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), and ignore Sansa Stark when she attends King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon)'s first court session in the [throne room](wiki:Throne_room).(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), hiding in [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom), is nearly run down by the twins and their guardsmen as they race to the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor). The twins are present for the execution of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) at the Great Sept.(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)

### A Clash of Kings

The [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) keeps the Redwyne twins as [hostages](wiki:Hostage) for the good behavior of their father, Lord [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne), during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Unlike other lords from the [Reach](wiki:Reach), Paxter refrains from joining his forces to that of [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), the uncle and rival of King Joffrey.(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

During the [tourney on King Joffrey's name day](wiki:Tourney_on_King_Joffrey's_name_day), Horas encourages Hobber in the lists, then rushes to his brother's aid after he is unhorsed by Ser [Meryn Trant](wiki:Meryn_Trant). Joffrey is not impressed when Horas unhorses an elderly knight bedecked with silver [griffins](wiki:Griffin) on a striped blue and white field.(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Horas and Hobber attempt to sneak out of King's Landing by bribing a guard and sailing away on *[Moonrunner](wiki:Moonrunner)*, but the plot is discovered by Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys). [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), has the guard seized and sent to the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), and the twins are placed under closer supervision.(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

The twins are in attendance when Tyrion holds court in the throne room,(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) and they ignore [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) after the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross).(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

After Renly's death, Hobber is allowed to accompany Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) on his mission to recruit [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) at [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge) while Horas remains in captivity at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) This show of good faith allows Paxter to ride with the [Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) host to defend the capital during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

Ser Horas is among the procession to see Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) off to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). He protects Lady [Tanda Stokeworth](wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth) during the [riots in King's Landing](wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing).(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

Horas is injured in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1) Paxter, Hobber, and a limping Horas are among those honored in the throne room after the battle.(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-2)

### A Storm of Swords

Sansa notices one of the Redwyne twins sparring with Ser [Tallad the Tall](wiki:Tallad_the_Tall) in an inner yard of the Red Keep.(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), now Joffrey's Hand, considers having his daughter, the widowed Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), wed one of the Redwyne twins.(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Horas, Hobber, and their father Paxter attend Tywin's funeral at the Great Sept.(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

According to [Taena Merryweather](wiki:Taena_Merryweather), the twins are both in love with Queen Margaery, now the wife of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), forgetting their arguments over who will be the next [Lord of the Arbor](wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor) and instead wanting to join the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) just to be near her.(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3) Horas and Hobber visit with Margaery often, with Horas taking up the [lute](wiki:Lute) and Hobber bringing fruit and flowers.(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) During one of Margaery's riding expeditions, the brothers escort [Meredyth Crane](wiki:Meredyth_Crane) and [Janna Fossoway](wiki:Janna_Fossoway).(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) On another occasion, Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) sees Ser [Osmund Kettleblack](wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack) sparring in the yard with one of the brothers.(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

As part of her plan to remove Margaery, Cersei has the [Blue Bard](wiki:Blue_Bard) include the Redwyne twins among those alleged to have slept with the younger queen. So as not not to lose the support of [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet),(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) Cersei intends for the brothers to be falsely accused. Arrest warrants are signed for Horas and Hobber, and they are given comfortable rooms in a tower.(wiki:Horas_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
