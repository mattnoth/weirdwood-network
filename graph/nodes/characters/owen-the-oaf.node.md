## Origins

Owen's mother dropped him on his head as a baby and left him [dimwitted](wiki:Lackwit).(wiki:Owen_the_Oaf.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Appearances & Description

Owen is tall and blond, friendly, diligent, and a surprisingly good carpenter.(wiki:Owen_the_Oaf.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) He sometimes has a sausage stuffed into his scabbard instead of a sword.(wiki:Owen_the_Oaf.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Owen plays the [fiddle](wiki:Fiddle).(wiki:Owen_the_Oaf.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Owen is among the men that [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) leaves behind to defend [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Owen_the_Oaf.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) He is kissed by [Zei](wiki:Zei) when wildlings are repulsed during the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall).(wiki:Owen_the_Oaf.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Owen often forgets that King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) has died,(wiki:Owen_the_Oaf.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) and he dreams that the king will ride to the rescue of the black brothers defending the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Owen_the_Oaf.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) The late king's brother, [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), does arrive to defeat the wildlings in the battle beneath the Wall, however.(wiki:Owen_the_Oaf.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Owen is stationed at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Owen_the_Oaf.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Dance With Dragons

Owen is one of the men brought along to assist Lord Commander [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) in escorting [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) out of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) to carry out his duties as garrison commander of [Greyguard](wiki:Greyguard). After Janos's execution for disobedience, Owen asks if he can have his boots.(wiki:Owen_the_Oaf.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Alongside two score other black brothers, Owen gathers at the gate through the Wall after rumors spread that the heads of three rangers were found impaled on spikes north of the Wall. When Jon and his party return from inspecting the heads, Owen asks if [Dywen](wiki:Dywen) is among the dead, but Jon informs the gathered men that the heads belong to [Jack Bulwer](wiki:Jack_Bulwer), [Hairy Hal](wiki:Hairy_Hal), and [Garth Greyfeather](wiki:Garth_Greyfeather), who had all been slain by the [Weeper](wiki:Weeper).(wiki:Owen_the_Oaf.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Owen attends the [wedding of Sigorn and Alys Karstark](wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark), and he plays his fiddle at the wedding feast. Owen also dances with [Patchface](wiki:Patchface), which causes the other guests to laugh. He listens nervously with the rest of the attendees after two horn blasts are heard coming from the watchers on the Wall, and titters with relief after no third blast is heard.(wiki:Owen_the_Oaf.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Owen stands sentry at the top of the Wall alongside [Ty](wiki:Ty) when Jon arrives upon the elevator with [Leathers](wiki:Leathers) for a meeting with other officers of the Night's Watch.(wiki:Owen_the_Oaf.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
