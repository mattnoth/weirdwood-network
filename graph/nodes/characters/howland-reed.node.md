## Origins

### Youth

According to a tale told by his daughter [Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed), Howland grew up hunting, fishing, climbing trees, and learning the [magic](wiki:Magic) of the [crannogmen](wiki:Crannogmen).(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

### Isle of Faces and Harrenhal

After he reached the [age of majority](wiki:Age_of_majority), Howland sought additional knowledge. He boldly paddled a small skin boat down the [Green Fork](wiki:Green_Fork), evaded the [Twins](wiki:Twins) at night, and visited the [Isle of Faces](wiki:Isle_of_Faces) in search of the [green men](wiki:Green_men). He stayed at the island all winter.(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

Howland left the Isle of Faces in [281 AC](wiki:281_AC), the [year of the false spring](wiki:Year_of_the_false_spring), to observe the great [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) held by Lord [Walter Whent](wiki:Walter_Whent). As Howland walked across the tourney field, he was attacked by three [squires](wiki:Squire), younger but taller. [Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark) came to his rescue, however, as the crannogman was a vassal of her father, Lord [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), and the girl chased the three squires away with a tourney sword. Afterwards, she brought him to her tent, where she bound up his wounds and he was introduced to her three brothers, [Brandon](wiki:Brandon_Stark), [Eddard](wiki:Eddard_Stark), and [Benjen](wiki:Benjen_Stark). Lyanna insisted he attend the feast held that evening at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) to mark the start of the tourney, and so Howland accompanied the four [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) siblings, dressed in clothing Benjen had found to suit his [high birth](wiki:Highborn). When they recognized the three squires who had attacked him earlier, Benjen offered to find Howland a [horse](wiki:Horse) and [armor](wiki:Armor) to he could avenge himself, but Howland gave no answer. Although he wanted to take revenge, he feared making a fool of himself as he was no [knight](wiki:Knight). Howland prayed at the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye) to the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods), and was given a place in Eddard's tent for the night.(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

On the second day of jousting, a short [mystery knight](wiki:Mystery_knight), dubbed the [Knight of the Laughing Tree](wiki:Knight_of_the_Laughing_Tree), entered the [joust](wiki:Joust) and challenged the three knights whose squires who had attacked the crannogman. The three knights were defeated, but for a ransom, the mystery knight only demanded that the knights teach their squires honor.(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6) King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) was convinced the mystery knight was Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), newly of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) and other nobles went in search of the Knight of the Laughing Tree, but the mystery knight's identity was never revealed.(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)

### Robert's Rebellion

The abduction of [Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark) by Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) led to the imprisonment and executions of Lord [Rickard](wiki:Rickard_Stark) and [Brandon Stark](wiki:Brandon_Stark) by King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen). Howland joined the [northern](wiki:North) host during the ensuing war, [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion),(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Rssm1221.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) and was one of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s staunch companions.(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

After the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing) and the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End), Howland and five other northmen accompanied Ned Stark south to the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) in search of Lyanna.(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2) Howland and Eddard were the only survivors of the [combat at the tower of joy](wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy) between the seven northmen and three knights of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-3) Ned later told his second son, [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark), that Howland had saved his life from Ser [Arthur Dayne](wiki:Arthur_Dayne).(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3) Eddard found Lyanna and was with his sister when she died. Howland was among those who found Ned, still holding Lyanna, silent with grief, and he was the person who took Lyanna's hand from Eddard's.(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### Baratheon Era

Howland returned to the [north](wiki:North) and has not departed the swamps of the [Neck](wiki:Neck) since.(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Rawoiafhowland_reed.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) has periodically sent letters to his friend, but Bran cannot recall [crannogmen](wiki:Crannogmen) having visited [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-4) Howland taught net-fighting to his daughter, [Meera Reed](wiki:Meera_Reed).(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Racok28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) It is unknown when Howland became [Lord of Greywater Watch](wiki:Lord_of_Greywater_Watch), whether before or after [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).

## Appearances & Description

Howland is a small man,(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) like all [crannogmen](wiki:Crannogmen). [Meera Reed](wiki:Meera_Reed) describes her father as having been brave, strong, and smart in his youth.(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He is a great friend of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark),(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) who considered Howland a worthy man.(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Young Howland wore a shirt of [bronze](wiki:Bronze) scales and wielded a three-pronged [spear](wiki:Spear) and a leather shield.(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) recalls his sister [Lyanna](wiki:Lyanna_Stark)'s death, and how Howland had found him still holding her body and taken her hand from his.(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

During a fever dream, Eddard recalls Howland and his other former companions at the tower of joy.(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-4)

[Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls the [northern](wiki:North) banners to rescue his father, Eddard, from imprisonment in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), and sends word to Howland to have the [crannogmen](wiki:Crannogmen) bleed the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) if they march up the [causeway](wiki:Causeway) through the [Neck](wiki:Neck).(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

### A Clash of Kings

Howland learns that his son [Jojen](wiki:Jojen_Reed) has had a [green dream](wiki:Greensight) of a winged [wolf](wiki:Wolf) bound in chains, and sends his children from [Greywater Watch](wiki:Greywater_Watch) to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Racok28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) Jojen and his elder sister, [Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed), recite an oath of fealty to King Robb's younger brother, Prince [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark), during the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast).(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-5)

After [Winterfell is captured](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) by [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) and his [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn). The Reed siblings escape with Bran and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark), and Theon hunts them in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood). [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Luwin](wiki:Luwin) convinces Theon not to harm Meera and Jojen, but to hold them as [hostages](wiki:Hostage) to prevent Howland from attacking the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) of Lord Captain [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) at [Moat Cailin](wiki:Fall_of_Moat_Cailin).(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) However, the Reeds and their companions escape from Theon, and hide in the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Storm of Swords

While traveling in the [northern mountains](wiki:Northern_mountains), Meera tells Bran the story of her father's journey to the [Isle of Faces](wiki:Isle_of_Faces) and the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal), and of the [knight of the Laughing Tree](wiki:Knight_of_the_Laughing_Tree). Though Jojen thinks Bran must have heard the story a hundred times from Eddard, Bran cannot recall ever hearing it before.(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8)

At [Hag's Mire](wiki:Hag's_Mire), King Robb tasks [Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover) and [Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont) with sailing into the Neck to inform Howland of his plan to recover [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin) from Victarion's ironborn.(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) However, Robb is killed in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) before the plan can be put into action.(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Attacks by crannogmen weaken the ironborn garrison at Moat Cailin.(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

[Outlaws](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) led by [Lady Stoneheart](wiki:Lady_Stoneheart) are said to have vanished into the Neck, with Ser [Danwell Frey](wiki:Danwell_Frey) thinking they are being sheltered by crannogmen.(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Theon resolves the [siege of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin), with the meager ironborn garrison surrendering to [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow).(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) utilizes a decoy in case of an assassination attempt by crannogmen.(wiki:Howland_Reed.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1)
