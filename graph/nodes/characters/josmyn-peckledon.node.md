## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), young Josmyn fights on behalf of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). He kills two [knights](wiki:Knight), wounds a third, and captures two more. For his efforts, the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) awards him a sword, a suit of armor, a choice of any [warhorse](wiki:Horse) in the royal stables, and knighthood when he comes of age.(wiki:Josmyn_Peckledon.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

### A Storm of Swords

Josmyn is among those that testify against [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) in his trial for the death of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), witnessing that Tyrion had picked the [wedding chalice](wiki:Wedding_chalice) up and poured the last of its contents onto the floor during the [royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding).(wiki:Josmyn_Peckledon.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) takes [Garrett Paege](wiki:Garrett_Paege), [Lewys Piper](wiki:Lewys_Piper), and Josmyn as squires when he rides for [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Josmyn_Peckledon.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Jaime has Peck retrieve his golden hand when the [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard) dines with the castellan of Lady [Ermesande Hayford](wiki:Ermesande_Hayford).(wiki:Josmyn_Peckledon.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Jaime has Josmyn throw the head of [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat) into the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye),(wiki:Josmyn_Peckledon.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) and the squire finds a gelding for [Pia](wiki:Pia) to ride.(wiki:Josmyn_Peckledon.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

While at [Darry](wiki:Darry), Jaime urges a bashful Peck to take [Pia](wiki:Pia) as a lover, if she will have him, though he commands him to be kind to her. Jaime suggests they sleep in the unused bed of [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister), [Lord of Darry](wiki:Lord_of_Darry).(wiki:Josmyn_Peckledon.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) As the host travels west, Peck and Pia regularly have sex in the corner of Jaime's tent.(wiki:Josmyn_Peckledon.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

After arriving at the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Jaime has Josmyn set up their tent in the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) camp between the [Tumblestone](wiki:Tumblestone) and the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork).(wiki:Josmyn_Peckledon.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Jaime has Peck pour [wine](wiki:Wine) for [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully), Pia clean the captive lord's clothes, and Lewys prepare bathwater.(wiki:Josmyn_Peckledon.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Jaime achieve the surrender of Riverrun, although Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) escapes the besieging army. Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) sends a letter to Riverrun begging Jaime to aid her against the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), but Jaime has Peck burn his twin's letter.(wiki:Josmyn_Peckledon.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Peck carries the peace banner when Jaime's band arrives at [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall). Once the [siege of Raventree](wiki:Siege_of_Raventree) has been resolved, Peck rides with Jaime and [Hoster Blackwood](wiki:Hoster_Blackwood) to [Pennytree](wiki:Pennytree), where they share wine.(wiki:Josmyn_Peckledon.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
