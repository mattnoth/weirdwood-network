## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Moon Boy](wiki:Category:Images_of_Moon_Boy)*

Moon Boy is pie-faced, and he wears the motley clothing common to [fools](wiki:Fool).(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He has big, round eyes and carries a rattle.(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

The acrobatic man is able to cartwheel,(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) walk on stilts,(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Racok29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Racok60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) and juggle.(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

Moon Boy is considered a [simpleton](wiki:Lackwit),(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) though [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard) suspect him of not being as simple as he seems.(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Racok52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Moon Boy performs during the royal feast during the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney), making witticisms about the various nobles in attendance and walking on stilts. His song about the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(fat_one)) makes Septa [Mordane](wiki:Mordane) laugh.(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

### A Clash of Kings

King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) strips [knighthood](wiki:Knight) from [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard)(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) and names him a [fool](wiki:Fool) lower than Moon Boy.(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

Moon Boy jokes that the High Septon is so pious he has a meal for each of [the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) whenever he eats.(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Dontos tells [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) he thinks that Moon Boy has been in the employment of [Varys](wiki:Varys) for years. Dontos claims to hear all sorts of things as a fool he never heard when he was a knight.(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Racok52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

### A Storm of Swords

After Sansa weds [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), Moon Boy and Dontos dance during the feast.(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

At the breakfast before Joffrey's wedding to [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), Moon Boy makes farting sounds with his cheeks and sings rude songs about the guests.(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) During the feast following the wedding, Moon Boy chases [Butterbumps](wiki:Butterbumps) on stilts and pelts Dontos with dates.

After Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) confesses to Tyrion that [Tysha](wiki:Tysha) was never in fact a [whore](wiki:Whore), Tyrion becomes furious and tells Jaime that their sister [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), Jaime's longtime lover, has been having sex with their cousin, Ser [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister), and Ser [Osmund Kettleblack](wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack). Tyrion sarcastically adds, "and probably Moon Boy for all I know."(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Rasos77.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Moon Boy and Butterbumps entertain during [the wedding feast](wiki:Wedding_of_Tommen_I_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) and Margaery.(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) The fool capers when Cersei, the Queen Regent, meets with members of the merchants guilds.(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) When Tommen is permitted to name the last five of [Cersei's dromonds](wiki:Cersei's_dromonds), he chooses *Moon Boy* for one, but is dissuaded by the [master of ships](wiki:Master_of_ships), [Aurane Waters](wiki:Aurane_Waters).(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

At [Darry](wiki:Darry), Jaime confronts his cousin Lancel about Cersei. Lancel admits that he had sex with her, and Jaime wonders how many more men his sister has slept with. He then wonders if Tyrion's comment about Moon Boy was just a gibe.(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Jaime later has a dream where he finds his sister having sex with Moon Boy, after which he kills the fool and smashes Cersei's teeth to splinters.(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

Moon Boy dubs the new [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon) as the [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow).(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

Moon Boy is present when [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) hears that the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) has arrested Margaery Tyrell. He appears dumbstruck.(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

### A Dance with Dragons

Jaime continues to be bothered by Tyrion's remark about Cersei and Moon Boy.(wiki:Moon_Boy.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
