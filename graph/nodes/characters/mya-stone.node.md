## Origins

Young [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End), fathered Mya, his first child, on [a commoner](wiki:Mya_Stone's_mother)(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Rawoiafmya_stone.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) while he and [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) were being fostered in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) at the court of [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), [Lord of the Eyrie](wiki:Lord_of_the_Eyrie).(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) When [Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark), Ned's sister, heard of Mya's birth and parentage, she became less enthusiastic about her betrothal to Robert.(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Robert often brought Ned along during his daily visits to see Mya, even after he lost interest in her mother.(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) Mya remained in the Vale at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon)(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Rawoiafmya_stone.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) when Robert became king after [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) and took up residence in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

At some point after the birth of Prince [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), Robert expressed interest in taking one of his baseborn daughters (apparently Mya) to court in King's Landing. Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) did not protest, but spitefully stated that "the city is not a healthy place for a growing girl", inferring that Cersei would harm her. Robert grew enraged enough to strike his wife, but did not press the point.(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

Mya is somewhat openly known to be the [bastard](wiki:Bastardy) daughter of the king, although she was not officially acknowledged by her father. She has vague memories of him, as a big strong man tossing her in the air and catching her.(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-6) While [Maddy](wiki:Maddy) knows the truth, it is unclear if Mya is aware that the king is her father.(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-7)

Lord [Nestor Royce](wiki:Nestor_Royce) has tried to arrange matches for Mya, but she has declined them all.(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-8)

## Appearances & Description

Mya is a wiry girl,(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) slim and sinewy.(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) She has black hair which she has cropped short and round on one occasion,(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) and later cut short and shaggy.(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Mya's eyes are big and blue.(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) She is about seventeen or eighteen by [298 AC](wiki:298_AC).(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

Mya wears riding leathers and a light shirt of silvered ringmail.(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

Sansa's first good look at Mya preparing to descend the Giant's Lance: "Slim and sinewy, Mya looked as tough as the old riding leathers she wore beneath her silvery ringmail shirt. Her hair was black as a raven's wing, so short and shaggy that Alayne suspected that she cut it with a dagger. Mya's eyes were her best feature, big and blue. She could be pretty, if she would dress up like a girl." — also: "those are his eyes, and she has his hair too, the thick black hair he shared with Renly" (Robert's physical inheritance confirmed), AFFC Alayne II (`sources/chapters/affc/affc-alayne-02.md:181`) [q672]

Mya has a relationship with the squire [Mychel Redfort](wiki:Mychel_Redfort).(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

Mya is a calm and capable guide on the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance).(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4) Mychel has remarked that her father must have been a [goat](wiki:Goat) and that she has the eyes of an [owl](wiki:Owl),(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7) with the girl repeating the claims to others.(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Having learned that [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) is one of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s [bastards](wiki:Bastards), Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) recalls Mya from when he was fostered with Robert at the court of Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn).(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3)

After [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark)'s arrival at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon), she is ordered to ascend to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) by Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn). Mya assists Catelyn up the mountain past [Stone](wiki:Stone) and [Snow](wiki:Snow_(waycastle)) to [Sky](wiki:Sky) using mules, after which Lady Stark ascends to the Eyrie in a basket. Catelyn is initially wary of Mya, as the girl's [bastard](wiki:Bastard) status reminds her of [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow). During the journey, Mya tells Cat that she is in love with a squire, [Mychel Redfort](wiki:Mychel_Redfort), and hopes to marry him when he is made a [knight](wiki:Knight). Cat believes this unlikely to happen, as Mya is baseborn and [House Redfort](wiki:House_Redfort) descends from [First Men](wiki:First_Men) nobility.(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-8)

### A Storm of Swords

Mya is now nineteen years old, King Robert's eldest child.(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Feast for Crows

[Myranda Royce](wiki:Myranda_Royce) reveals to "[Alayne Stone](wiki:Sansa_Stark)" that Mya lost her [virginity](wiki:Maiden) to Mychel. Mya still hoped for [marriage](wiki:Marriage), till the newly-knighted Mychel was ordered by his father, Lord [Horton Redfort](wiki:Horton_Redfort), to marry [Ysilla Royce](wiki:Ysilla_Royce), daughter of Lord [Yohn Royce](wiki:Yohn_Royce) of [Runestone](wiki:Runestone).(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-9)

Mya catches the eye of the older Ser [Lothor Brune](wiki:Lothor_Brune).(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-10) Mya assists Alayne and Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn) when they descend from the Eyrie to the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon). She offers to ride with Robert, but the boy lord insists upon riding with Alayne. Mya commands other mule handlers, including [Carrot](wiki:Carrot) and [Ossy](wiki:Ossy).(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-11)

### The Winds of Winter

Mya informs young Robert that Lady [Anya Waynwood](wiki:Anya_Waynwood)'s party has arrived at the Gates of the Moon for the tourney for the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights). Alayne notices that Mya, who has straw in her hair, is unhappy with Mychel's presence at the castle, and also that the baseborn girl seems nervous around nobility.(wiki:Mya_Stone.cite_ref-twow_12-0)
