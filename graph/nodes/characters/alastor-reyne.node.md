## Origins

During the early reign of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), Ser Alastor was the champion of the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) and the [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms) at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), in the service of Lord [Lyman Lannister](wiki:Lyman_Lannister).(wiki:Alastor_Reyne.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Ser Alastor was one of the notables that participated in the grand melee in the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit) in the [tourney for its completion](wiki:Tourney_for_the_completion_of_the_Dragonpit) in [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Alastor_Reyne.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
