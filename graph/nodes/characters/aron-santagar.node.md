## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) and Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) go to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in search of the owner of the [Valyrian steel dagger](wiki:Littlefinger's_blade) used by the [catspaw](wiki:Catspaw) who attempted to kill [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark), Rodrik visits Aron, who as [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms) might recognize it, in the armory. The two knights then drink in the common room of the [old inn on Eel Alley](wiki:Old_inn_on_Eel_Alley) where Catelyn and Rodrik are staying.(wiki:Aron_Santagar.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Aron participates in the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney). His joust against [Lothor Brune](wiki:Lothor_Brune) ends in a draw after three tilts, and Aron is then defeated by Lord [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister).(wiki:Aron_Santagar.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

During the first court session of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) after the death of his father, King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) thinks that Aron is gloomy.(wiki:Aron_Santagar.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Aron gives a blunted silver longsword with a leaf-shaped blade to Prince [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) when the boy practices against a quintain.(wiki:Aron_Santagar.cite_ref-Racok1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Aron is part of the retinue which attends Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon)'s departure for [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), and he and Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) bear King Joffrey's banners. When the [riots in King's Landing](wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing) break out, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sees Aron pulled from his saddle by [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk), who tear away the [Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon) banner. Four men use a cobblestone to crush Aron's head within his helmet. [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) guts one of Aron's attackers, but not in time to save the master-at-arms.(wiki:Aron_Santagar.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) learns that [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne) had been training with Aron until the master-at-arms was killed in the bread riots.(wiki:Aron_Santagar.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Since Aron's position has not yet been replaced, Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) considers naming a [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) the new master-at-arms to ease tension with [House Martell](wiki:House_Martell).(wiki:Aron_Santagar.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
