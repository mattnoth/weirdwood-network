## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Six-Toed Harl, along with the rest of [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy)'s, supporters sails to [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) with Asha as she contemplates her next move after the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Six-Toed_Harl.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

When five [northmen](wiki:Northmen) try to infiltrate Deepwood, Harl kills one of them on the walls. Asha has Harl and [Lorren Longaxe](wiki:Lorren_Longaxe) fetch Lady [Sybelle Glover](wiki:Sybelle_Glover) and her [maester](wiki:Maester).(wiki:Six-Toed_Harl.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) While fleeing from Deepwood back to the [Bay of Ice](wiki:Bay_of_Ice), Asha and her men rest in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) and wait for sunrise. During the respite [Hagen's daughter](wiki:Hagen's_daughter) goes off with Harl for an amorous encounter after having first been refused by [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley). Sometime thereafter Harl and Hagen's daughter are interrupted by the [northmen](wiki:Northmen) who have caught up to the [ironmen](wiki:Ironmen).(wiki:Six-Toed_Harl.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Harl's fate after the [liberation](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte) of the castle is unknown.(wiki:Six-Toed_Harl.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Six-Toed_Harl.cite_ref-2)
