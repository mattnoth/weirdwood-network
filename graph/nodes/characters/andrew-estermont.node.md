## Origins

The only son of Ser [Lomas Estermont](wiki:Lomas_Estermont), Andrew is a former [squire](wiki:Squire) of Lord [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Andrew_Estermont.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Appearances & Description

Andrew has a long pointy beard, and bushy brown eyebrows. He is very tall, standing a head above [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth).(wiki:Andrew_Estermont.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser Andrew is listed as supporting King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) at the beginning of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Andrew_Estermont.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords

One of the [king's men](wiki:King's_men), Andrew is considered one of the few men Stannis can still rely on after the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Andrew_Estermont.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Andrew supports Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth), and escorts [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm) when Davos arranges for the boy to leave [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) on the *[Mad Prendos](wiki:Mad_Prendos_(galley))*, in order to protect him from [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre)'s fires.(wiki:Andrew_Estermont.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Andrew sails across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) to [Essos](wiki:Essos), on the *[Mad Prendos](wiki:Mad_Prendos_(galley))* commanding Edric's escort.(wiki:Andrew_Estermont.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Andrew hides in [Lys](wiki:Lys) with Edric and his protectors.(wiki:Andrew_Estermont.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
