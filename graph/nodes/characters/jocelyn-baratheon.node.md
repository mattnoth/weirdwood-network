## Identity

Lady Jocelyn Baratheon was the daughter of Lord Rogar Baratheon and Dowager Queen Alyssa Velaryon. She had an older brother, Boremund, and six elder half-siblings from Alyssa's earlier marriage to King Aenys I Targaryen (wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon).

## Origins

Born one month premature in 54 AC at Storm's End. Her mother Alyssa Velaryon fell severely ill and died from the birth — Maester Kyrie could only save Jocelyn by cutting Alyssa open at Lord Rogar's command (track_b: Born, wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon).

## Appearances & Description

Jocelyn had large dark eyes and black hair — thick and straight, falling to her waist by sixteen. A solemn girl, she grew to be one of the greatest beauties of the realm. Frail as a babe, she became a tall young girl by six, and nearly six feet tall at sixteen (wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon).

## Narrative Arc

Born under tragic circumstances, Jocelyn was raised mostly by the wives of her father's uncles. In 58 AC she came to King's Landing with Rogar and Boremund for the tourney celebrating King Jaehaerys I's tenth anniversary on the throne. She married Prince Aemon Targaryen and bore him a daughter, Rhaenys Targaryen. Jocelyn died between 92 and 129 AC (wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon).
