## Identity

Jocelyn Baratheon (b. 54 AC) — child of Alyssa Velaryon and Rogar Baratheon; married to Aemon Targaryen (son of Jaehaerys I); sworn to House Baratheon.

## Origins

### Early life

Jocelyn was born one month premature in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC). Her mother, [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon), fell severely ill due to the pregnancy, and was unable to give birth by herself. According to Maester [Kyrie](wiki:Kyrie), he could not save Alyssa, but by cutting her open he might be able to save Jocelyn. Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) then told the maester to save his child. Some days after her birth, Rogar named his daughter.(wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

In [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Jocelyn came to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with her father Rogar and her older brother [Boremund](wiki:Boremund_Baratheon) for the [tourney held on the anniversary of King Jaehaerys I's tenth anniversary on the throne](wiki:Tourney_at_King's_Landing_on_the_anniversary_of_the_king's_coronation).(wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Jocelyn was raised mostly by the wives of her father's brothers, but they both favored their own children, and died eventually of [Winter Fever](wiki:Winter_Fever). In [61 AC](wiki:61_AC), at the age of six, Jocelyn was once again brought to court by her father, together with two daughters of her father's brother [Ronnal](wiki:Ronnal_Baratheon). Lord Rogar requested to King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) that Jocelyn be raised at court as their [ward](wiki:Fosterage). The king and queen gladly accepted.(wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

In [62 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), during the feast held to celebrate the investiture of Prince [Aemon Targaryen](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) as [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone), Jocelyn was seated beside Prince Aemon by Queen Alysanne. The two young people ignored all those around them all evening.(wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

### Marriage

Jocelyn was betrothed to her nephew Prince [Aemon Targaryen](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) in [68 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). They were married two years later, in a ceremony that was said to rival the [Golden Wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) of her parents.(wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) Jocelyn gave birth to their first and only child, Princess [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aemon)), in [74 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) She became a widow in [92 AC](wiki:92_AC), when Prince Aemon was killed on [Tarth](wiki:Tarth) while attempting to put a halt to the invasion of the island by [Myrish](wiki:Myr) pirates. Jocelyn wept bitter tears upon hearing of her husband's death. When King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) passed over Jocelyn's daughter Rhaenys in favor of granting the title [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone) to his next oldest son, Prince [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)), Jocelyn was angered. Jocelyn became a grandmother at the end of [92 AC](wiki:92_AC), when her daughter gave birth to [Laena Velaryon](wiki:Laena_Velaryon).(wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)(wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Jocelyn had died by the time of the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). Her nephew, Lord [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon), claimed he never knew his aunt.(wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Appearances & Description

Jocelyn had large dark eyes and black hair. By the time she was sixteen, her thick straight hair fell to her waist. A solemn girl, she grew to become one of the greatest beauties of the realm.(wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

She had been frail as a babe, but by the age of six had become a tall young girl. At the age of sixteen, Jocelyn was an inch short of six feet tall.(wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
