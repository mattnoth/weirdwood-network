## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Maester Frenken is one of the first to receive the letters from [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) declaring [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s children with [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) illegitimate. He sends his copy of the letter from [Stokeworth](wiki:Stokeworth) on to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Frenken.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Later at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), Frenken treats [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark)'s wounds after she is beaten by Ser [Boros Blount](wiki:Boros_Blount)(wiki:Frenken.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and when she is injured during the [riot of King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:Frenken.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He also brings messages to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:Frenken.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Frenken treats a scared lady(wiki:Frenken.cite_ref-Racok60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and the wounded Ser [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister).(wiki:Frenken.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) After the battle, when the wounded Tyrion dismisses Maester [Ballabar](wiki:Ballabar) on suspicions of trying to kill him, Frenken takes up the task of seeing to Tyrion's recovery.(wiki:Frenken.cite_ref-Racok67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords

After the death of the King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) at his [royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding), Frenken and Ballabar perform the autopsy that refutes the theory of a suffocation with the pigeon pie. During the trial of Tyrion for the death of the king, Frenken is called as a witness for the prosecution, and he agrees that poison was to blame.(wiki:Frenken.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Maester Frenken remains busy at Stokeworth caring for [Lollys](wiki:Lollys_Stokeworth) and her mother, Lady [Tanda Stokeworth](wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth), who fell and broke her hip.(wiki:Frenken.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) During a duel, Ser [Bronn of the Blackwater](wiki:Bronn) kills Ser [Balman Byrch](wiki:Balman_Byrch) before the wounded Balman can be treated by Frenken.(wiki:Frenken.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
