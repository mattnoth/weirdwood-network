## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) gives her young husband King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) three black kittens,(wiki:Ser_Pounce.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) whom he names [Boots](wiki:Boots), [Lady Whiskers](wiki:Lady_Whiskers), and Ser Pounce. [Dorcas](wiki:Dorcas) makes Tommen a [mouse](wiki:Mouse) from scraps of fur and ties it on a fishing pole. The kittens love to chase it, and Tommen likes nothing better than jerking it across the floor as the cats pounce on it.(wiki:Ser_Pounce.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Ser Pounce catches a mouse but Lady Whiskers steals it from him.(wiki:Ser_Pounce.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When King Tommen dines with his mother and his great-uncle, Lord Regent [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), the boy brings his kittens. At dinner, he claims Ser Pounce hissed at the [bad cat](wiki:Balerion_(cat)) that was at his window, and scared him away. Tommen serves morsels of [pike](wiki:Pike) to his pets. After dinner, the king and his kittens are escorted to the royal bedchamber by Ser [Boros Blount](wiki:Boros_Blount).(wiki:Ser_Pounce.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
