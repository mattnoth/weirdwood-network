## Identity

Ser Brynden Rivers, called Lord Bloodraven, was the bastard son of King Aegon IV Targaryen by his sixth mistress, Lady Melissa Blackwood (wiki:Brynden_Rivers). He had two older sisters, Mya and Gwenys, and numerous half-siblings. As his mother was highborn, he was counted among the Great Bastards (wiki:Brynden_Rivers).

## Origins

Born in 175 AC in King's Landing (track_b: Born). Son of Aegon IV Targaryen (track_b: Father) and Melissa Blackwood (track_b: Mother). Legitimized by King Aegon IV in 184 AC along with his bastard siblings (wiki:Brynden_Rivers).

## Appearances & Description

Brynden was an albino with milk-white skin, long white hair, and red eyes. On the right side of his face he had a red winestain birthmark extending from his throat up to his right cheek — the reason he was called "Bloodraven" (wiki:Brynden_Rivers). His personal arms were a white dragon on black, breathing red fire (wiki:Brynden_Rivers). He wielded a weirwood longbow (wiki:Brynden_Rivers).

## Narrative Arc

Brynden was a Targaryen loyalist who played a decisive role at the Battle of the Redgrass Field during the First Blackfyre Rebellion, where his archers — the Raven's Teeth — rained arrows upon the rebel forces. He served as Master of Whisperers, then Hand of the King to both Aerys I and Maekar I. Known for his network of spies and rumored sorcery, he became feared and reviled. He was eventually sent to the Night's Watch by King Aegon V, where he rose to Lord Commander. He disappeared beyond the Wall and was later encountered by Bran Stark as an ancient figure merged with a weirwood, known as the three-eyed crow and the last greenseer (wiki:Brynden_Rivers).
