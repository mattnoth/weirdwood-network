## Identity

Margaery Tyrell is the only daughter of Lord Mace Tyrell and Lady Alerie Hightower. She is a member of House Tyrell and has been queen consort to three kings: Renly Baratheon, Joffrey Baratheon, and Tommen Baratheon (wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).

## Origins

Born circa 283 AC at Highgarden (track_b: Born). Daughter of Mace Tyrell and Alerie Hightower (track_b: Father, Mother).

## Appearances & Description

Margaery has thick, softly curling brown hair and large brown eyes, with a slender but womanly figure and smooth, unblemished pale skin. She has a shy and sweet smile and is regarded as beautiful by most (wiki:Margaery_Tyrell). Both Sansa and Cersei note she resembles her brother Loras; according to Cersei, the siblings look more alike than Cersei and Jaime (wiki:Margaery_Tyrell). Renly claims people say she resembles Lyanna Stark (wiki:Margaery_Tyrell). She is intelligent and shrewd, taking after her grandmother (wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).

## Narrative Arc

In AGOT, Renly shows Eddard a locket with Margaery's portrait, hoping she'd remind him of Lyanna. Renly and Loras scheme to make her Robert's queen, but Robert dies first. Renly flees to Highgarden, marries Margaery, and claims the crown (agot-eddard-06, agot-tyrion-09). After Renly's assassination, the Tyrells ally with the Lannisters. Margaery travels to King's Landing betrothed to Joffrey, winning the smallfolk's love by distributing food. She and Olenna privately learn of Joffrey's cruelty from Sansa (wiki:Margaery_Tyrell). The Tyrells plot to marry Sansa to Willas, but the plan is foiled (wiki:Margaery_Tyrell). After Joffrey's death at his wedding feast, Margaery marries the boy king Tommen (wiki:Margaery_Tyrell). In AFFC, Cersei plots against Margaery, engineering accusations of infidelity. The Faith arrests Margaery and her cousins (wiki:Margaery_Tyrell). In ADWD, the evidence against Margaery proves weak; she and her cousins are released to Randyll Tarly's custody pending trial. Margaery plans to let the Faith judge her (wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).
