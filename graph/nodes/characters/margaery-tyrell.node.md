## Origins

Margaery was born at [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden)(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rawoiafmargaery_tyrell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1) in [283 AC](wiki:283_AC)(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Cal_4-1) to Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) and Lady [Alerie Hightower](wiki:Alerie_Hightower). She has three older brothers, [Willas](wiki:Willas_Tyrell), Ser [Garlan](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell), and Ser [Loras](wiki:Loras_Tyrell). When she was a little girl, Willas used to read to her, and draw her pictures of the [stars](wiki:Astronomy).(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-4) Margaery has been riding horses since she was old enough to walk.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-3)

## Appearances & Description

Margaery has thick, softly curling brown hair and large brown eyes.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) She has a slender(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) but womanly figure(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) with smooth and unblemished(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) pale skin(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) and small breasts.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Margaery is fair and lively,(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) with a shy and sweet smile.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) She is regarded as beautiful(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) or pretty(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6) by most. Margaery's attire includes a sheer gown of [ivory](wiki:Ivory) silk, [Myrish](wiki:Myr) lace, and seed [pearls](wiki:Pearl).(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8) She also wears green with a cloak of [autumn](wiki:Autumn) flowers,(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-3) and a pale green samite gown with a tight-laced bodice that bares her shoulders and the top of her bosom.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) shows Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) a rose gold locket with a picture of Margaery inside, painted in the [Myrish](wiki:Myr) style. Renly hopes Ned would agree she looks like [Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark), Ned's late sister, but the [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell) disagrees.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2) Unbeknownst to Eddard, Renly and Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) hope to rid themselves of Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) by getting King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), who is still in love with Lyanna's memory after fourteen years, to become infatuated with Margaery, as Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) informs [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis). According to Varys, Loras writes his father, Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), urging him to send Margaery to court.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2) During the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney), Robert mentions to Eddard that Renly has been telling him about Loras's sister, a young girl as lovely as a dawn.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

Following Robert's death, Renly and Loras flee [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden), where Renly [marries](wiki:Wedding_of_Renly_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) Margaery and is crowned king in opposition to Robert's heir, [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Margaery accompanies Renly and his army on their slow march to King's Landing. At the [melee at Bitterbridge](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge), Margaery courteously gives her condolences to [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) for the death of her husband, Eddard. During the feast, Catelyn observes Renly giving more of his attention to Loras than his new bride.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) While they are at [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge), word arrives that [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) has besieged [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), Renly's seat. Renly departs with his cavalry, leaving Margaery at Bitterbridge with his infantry.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

After hearing Renly has been killed during the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), the [small council](wiki:Small_council) sends Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) as an envoy to Bitterbridge. The Tyrells ally with the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) by planning to wed Margaery to King Joffrey.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) Margaery, who has returned to Highgarden, is sent for by her family.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Now betrothed to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), Margaery travels to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). She is accompanied by a train of ladies, most of whom are either relatives of her or the daughters of her father's bannermen, while others are to be her ladies-in-waiting. Margaery's cousins [Alla](wiki:Alla_Tyrell), [Elinor](wiki:Elinor_Tyrell), and [Megga Tyrell](wiki:Megga_Tyrell) are among the retinue, as are Lady [Alysanne Bulwer](wiki:Alysanne_Bulwer), Lady [Alyce Graceford](wiki:Alyce_Graceford), Lady [Taena Merryweather](wiki:Taena_Merryweather), [Meredyth Crane](wiki:Meredyth_Crane), and Septa [Nysterica](wiki:Nysterica).(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

Prior to Margaery's arrival, wagons of food have been sent to King's Landing for the starving population. As this is done in Margaery's name, she is greeted in the capital by cheering crowds.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-30) Margaery invites [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) to supper. There, she and her grandmother, [Olenna Tyrell](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell), make inquiries about Joffrey's nature, until Sansa reveals how Joffrey and Cersei have been treating her. Although Sansa fears the revelation will cause Margaery to refuse the marriage, Margaery and Olenna confide in Sansa that Mace is determined to see Margaery become a queen, while simply calling Joffrey's nature "a pity".(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-5) Margaery and Olenna suggest to Sansa that she travel to [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) after Margaery's wedding, secretly plotting to marry Sansa to Margaery's eldest brother, [Willas](wiki:Willas_Tyrell).(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-6) Margaery and her ladies take Sansa in, taking her hawking, doing needlework with her, and other such pastimes.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-3) While [hawking](wiki:Hawk), Margaery's [peregrine](wiki:Falcon) takes a heron in full flight.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-4)

When [the Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) discover the Tyrell plot to marry Sansa to Willas, Sansa is quickly married off to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister). Although Margaery and the Tyrells are not present for the wedding ceremony, they are present at the feast, where Margaery gives Sansa a sad look. When Tyrion refuses to participate in the first dance with Sansa, Margaery and Joffrey lead in their stead.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

Margaery marries Joffrey on the first day of [300 AC](wiki:300_AC) at the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor), with the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Tyrion's)) officiating.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2) Following the ceremony, [a great feast](wiki:Purple_wedding) is held in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep). [Hamish the Harper](wiki:Hamish_the_Harper) brings tears to Margaery's eyes during his performance of "[Lord Renly's Ride](wiki:Lord_Renly's_Ride)". Later in the feast, Joffrey dies after drinking [poisoned wine](wiki:Strangler).(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-3)

Margaery attends the trial of Tyrion, who is charged with the murder, dressed in mourning clothes.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2) Lord Mace wants Tyrion to die, oft repeating that his daughter had also drank from the [wedding chalice](wiki:Wedding_chalice).(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-3)

Sansa is smuggled out of the city by [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard), who brings her to Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) in [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay). Littlefinger reveals that Olenna had poisoned Joffrey to prevent Joffrey from hurting Margaery and Loras from becoming a kingslayer in order to protect his sister. He reasons that, although Mace is insistent on making Margaery queen, Olenna realized that Joffrey was not needed for this. He predicts that Margaery will soon marry Joffrey's younger brother, [Tommen](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)

Littlefinger's prediction proves to be correct. The Tyrells soon begin insisting that Tommen should be married to Margaery.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0) Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) suggests to his son [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) that he, as heir to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), could leave the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) and marry Margaery, but Jaime is opposed to the idea.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-1) Jaime sees no harm in the match between Margaery and Tommen, as Tommen, who has been lonely ever since his sister, [Myrcella](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) left for [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), likes having Margaery and her ladies around. Cersei, Tommen's mother, is opposed to it, however.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Margaery attends several of the services held in the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) for the wake of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), placing a bouquet of golden roses at his funeral bier.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1)(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-2)

Wearing the same gown as when she wed Joffrey, Margaery marries King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) in a small ceremony in the [royal sept](wiki:Royal_sept). During [the small feast held afterward](wiki:Wedding_of_Tommen_I_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell), Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) is approached by [Taena of Myr](wiki:Taena_of_Myr), who claims that Margaery is paying Cersei's maid [Senelle](wiki:Senelle) to spy on Cersei for her.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-9) Following the feast, Margaery accompanies the guests outside to watch the [pyromancers](wiki:Pyromancers) burn down the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand) with [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire), per Cersei's instructions.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-10) As the Tyrells have insisted that Margaery and Tommen share a bed during their wedding night, despite the fact that Tommen is too young for consummation, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) escorts them to Tommen's room and stays there all night on the orders of Cersei, to prevent the boy from being left alone with his new wife.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-11)

When Cersei asks Margaery's leave to take Taena on as her own companion, Margaery reacts thrilled. Both Cersei and Jaime believe Taena to be a spy informing Margaery of Cersei's doings. Cersei gives Taena only the information she wants Margaery to know, with most of it being untrue. Additionally, Taena informs Cersei of everything Margaery does.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0) Cersei's hatred for Margaery and her family has been building for years, and after Taena informs Cersei that Ser [Horas](wiki:Horas_Redwyne) and Ser [Hobber Redwyne](wiki:Hobber_Redwyne) are in love with Margaery, Cersei begins to plot Margaery's downfall.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Cersei does not believe that Margaery is still a maiden, as she claims to be.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0)(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-12) She convinces Ser [Osney Kettleblack](wiki:Osney_Kettleblack) to seduce Margaery, and plans to have Osney's brother [Osmund](wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack) catch them abed. She tells Taena to inform Margaery of a secret admirer.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Margaery is quick to understand that it would come down to either Ser [Meryn Trant](wiki:Meryn_Trant) or Ser [Boros Blount](wiki:Boros_Blount),(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-40) neither of whom would probably fare well against Osney Kettleblack. However, he refuses to allow Cersei to take custody of Ser Osney, and reveals that, under further questioning and torture, Osney admitted to having slept with Cersei, not Margaery. Cersei is subsequently arrested as well. The mob remains in front of the Great Sept, calling out Margaery's name.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-5)

When news of his daughter's arrest reaches him, Lord Mace abandons his siege of Storm's End to march most of his army back to King's Landing. His bannerman, Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly), leaves [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) with his own army to give Margaery his aid.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-6)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) is the first to reach [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). As all the men who stand accused—with the exception of the [Blue Bard](wiki:Blue_Bard), who appears to be half-mad—have denied the charges or recanted, the evidence against Margaery and her cousins is weak. As such, the [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow) agrees to hand them over to Lord Tarly's custody, who swears that he will return them for the trial.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0)

During her [walk of atonement](wiki:Walk_of_atonement), [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) hears some of the angry [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) shout out the names of Margaery and [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Radwd65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0)

Although the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) are [under attack by the Golden Company](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company), Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) refuses to leave King's Landing with his army until Margaery's trial is over and she is declared innocent.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-1) Margaery plans to let the Faith sit in judgement of her.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Radwd65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-1) Mace wishes for King Tommen to declare Margaery innocent, but Lord Regent [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) argues against the idea, knowing it would mean that the whispers of accusation would follow Margaery forever. Kevan quietly wonders why Mace insists on having his army present when Margaery faces her accusers, if she is as innocent as Mace would have them all believe.(wiki:Margaery_Tyrell.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0)
