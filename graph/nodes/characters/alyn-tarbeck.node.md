## Origins

After declaring his uncle [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) a usurper and a tyrant, Prince [Aegon the Uncrowned](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) gathered his supporters at [Pinkmaiden Castle](wiki:Pinkmaiden_Castle) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). Lord Alyn Tarbeck was one of the several lords from the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) to join Aegon's cause. However, Lord Alyn died with the rebels at the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye) in [43 AC](wiki:43_AC), slain by Maegor's loyalists alongside the prince.(wiki:Alyn_Tarbeck.cite_ref-Rtsotd.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Alyn_Tarbeck.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

His wife, Lady [Jeyne](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling_(wife_of_Maegor_I)) of [House Westerling](wiki:House_Westerling), gave him a posthumous [son](wiki:Lord_Tarbeck_(son_of_Alyn)) a few months later who succeeded him as [Lord of Tarbeck Hall](wiki:Lord_of_Tarbeck_Hall).(wiki:Alyn_Tarbeck.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)(wiki:Alyn_Tarbeck.cite_ref-Rtsotd.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Alyn_Tarbeck.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
