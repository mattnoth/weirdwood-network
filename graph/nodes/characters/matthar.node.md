## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Matthar is in the [same class of recruits](wiki:Jon_Snow's_Night's_Watch_recruitment_group) as [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow). When he learns he is to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), he celebrates with Jon and the others, drinking [wine](wiki:Wine) and throwing snowballs with [Jeren](wiki:Jeren).(wiki:Matthar.cite_ref-Ragot41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Matt is named to the [rangers](wiki:Rangers) when he is considered ready to man the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Matthar.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
After [the arrest](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark) of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Matthar asks Septon [Cellador](wiki:Cellador) to light a candle for Jon's father.(wiki:Matthar.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Matthar is present when [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) reveals his new sword [Longclaw](wiki:Longclaw) to his friends.(wiki:Matthar.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Matt is among the group of friends who stop Jon from abandoning the Night's Watch and forswearing himself.(wiki:Matthar.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
