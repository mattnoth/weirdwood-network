## Origins

[Archmaester](wiki:Archmaester) [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn) records that Lord Monkeyface's antics greatly amused Queen Visenya. After he choked to death on a peach pit, Visenya replaced him with an [ape](wiki:Ape), which she had dressed in the late Lord Monkeyface's clothing.(wiki:Lord_Monkeyface.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
