## Origins

King Tommen II sailed with his great fleet to [Valyria](wiki:Valyria) after the Doom with the intention of plundering the wealth and sorcery he was sure still remained. It was a quest from which he never returned. The [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) greatsword [Brightroar](wiki:Brightroar) was lost with him.(wiki:Tommen_II_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

The last report of him is a [Volantene](wiki:Volantis) chronicle titled *[The Glory of Volantis](wiki:The_Glory_of_Volantis)* which mentions a "golden fleet" bearing the "Lion King" stayed at Volantis for supplies, and the that [triarchs](wiki:Triarch_of_Volantis) showered him with gifts. According to the chronicle, Tommen II swore that half of all he would find would be given to the triarchs in return for their generosity and the promise to send their fleet to his aid when he requested it. Afterward he sailed away. The next year, the chronicle states that Triarch [Marqelo Tagaros](wiki:Marqelo_Tagaros) dispatched a squadron toward Valyria to find any sign of Tommen's golden fleet but returned empty handed.(wiki:Tommen_II_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
