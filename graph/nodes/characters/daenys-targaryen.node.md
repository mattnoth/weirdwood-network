## Identity

Daenys Targaryen, called Daenys the Dreamer, was a noblewoman of House Targaryen and the daughter of Aenar Targaryen, a nobleman from the Valyrian Freehold and the Lord of Dragonstone (wiki:Daenys_Targaryen).

## Origins

Born on or before 126 BC in Valyria (track_b: Born). Daughter of Aenar Targaryen (track_b: Father).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiance).

## Narrative Arc

When Daenys was still a maiden, she had a powerful prophetic dream foretelling the Doom of Valyria. Her father Aenar heeded her warning and moved the entire family from Valyria to Dragonstone, twelve years before the Doom destroyed the Freehold. She married her brother Gaemon Targaryen and they had children including Aegon and Elaena. She died on or after 101 BC on Dragonstone (track_b: Died) (wiki:Daenys_Targaryen).
