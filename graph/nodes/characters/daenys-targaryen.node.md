## Origins

When Daenys was still a [maiden](wiki:Maiden),(wiki:Daenys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Daenys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf29.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) she had a powerful prophetic [dream](wiki:Dragon_dreams), showing the destruction of [Valyria](wiki:Valyria) by fire. In [114 BC](wiki:Years_before_Aegon's_Conquest), her father, Lord [Aenar Targaryen](wiki:Aenar_Targaryen), heeded her dream and sold his holdings in the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) and moved his family and all of their belongings to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)), an island in the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) off the eastern coast of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) and the westernmost outpost of the Freehold. With them, they took five [dragons](wiki:Dragon), including [Balerion](wiki:Balerion). Though the Targaryens' retreat to faraway Dragonstone was perceived as an act of cowardice and surrender by rival [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlord), many of whom were more powerful than they at the time, when the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria) came twelve years later, Daenys' prophecy proved true, and [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) was the only family of [dragonriders](wiki:Dragonriders) which survived.(wiki:Daenys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfab1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)(wiki:Daenys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf31.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7)

Daenys was married to her brother [Gaemon](wiki:Gaemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenar)), who followed their father as [Lord of Dragonstone](wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone). Their children were [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Gaemon)) and [Elaena Targaryen](wiki:Elaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Gaemon)).(wiki:Daenys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfab1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)(wiki:Daenys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf31.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8) Daenys and Gaemon also had at least one other younger daughter, who would marry a petty [lord](wiki:Lord).(wiki:Daenys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfab12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

By the time of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), it is believed that [visions](wiki:Vision) written down by Daenys before the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria) were in a book called *[Signs and Portents](wiki:Signs_and_Portents)*, that is now lost. [Archmaester](wiki:Maester) [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn) claims he found three pages from it in his *[Book of Lost Books](wiki:Book_of_Lost_Books)*. After his kinsman [Hotho Harlaw](wiki:Hotho_Harlaw) brought him a copy of the *Book of Lost Books* from Oldtown, Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw) recounted Marwyn's claims about Daenys' alleged prophetic writings to his niece, [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy).(wiki:Daenys_Targaryen.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
