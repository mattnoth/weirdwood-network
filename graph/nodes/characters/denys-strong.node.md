## Origins

Denys is a [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) exile. His surname Strong is of a house that had once loomed large in the histories of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Denys_Strong.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) [House Strong](wiki:House_Strong) once held the infamous castle of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) until they were extinguished during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Denys_Strong.cite_ref-Rfab16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Denys_Strong.cite_ref-Rfab19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He may or may not be of House Strong, as in the free companies a man can call himself whatever he chooses, though not all are genuine.(wiki:Denys_Strong.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) He may be related to another serjeant in the company, [Duncan Strong](wiki:Duncan_Strong).

## Appearances & Description

Denys wears his worldly wealth upon his person and a lord's ransom in golden arm rings. Each ring signifies one year's service with the Golden Company.(wiki:Denys_Strong.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
