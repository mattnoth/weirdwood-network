## Appearances & Description

Denyo is twelve years old, plump and cheerful. He keeps his father's cabin neat. Denyo helps his brother [Yorko Terys](wiki:Yorko_Terys) do his sums. He is more friendly than Yorko.(wiki:Denyo_Terys.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Denyo speaks the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue) well. He loves to talk about the *Titan's Daughter*, and loves to talk about [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), of which he is very proud.(wiki:Denyo_Terys.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Denyo befriends [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) on the journey of the *Titan's Daughter* from [Saltpans](wiki:Saltpans) to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos). At some point, he takes her up in the ship's crow's nest.(wiki:Denyo_Terys.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

As they approach the city, Denyo points out the distant stars that are the fiery eyes of the [Titan of Braavos](wiki:Titan_of_Braavos). He tells Arya about the [gods](wiki:Gods) of Braavos, though he seems unnerved by the [Many-Faced God](wiki:Many-Faced_God). When the Titan roars, he laughs, and tells Arya not to be afraid. As they pass the [Arsenal](wiki:Arsenal), Denyo proudly explains that it can build a [war galley](wiki:Ship) in a day.(wiki:Denyo_Terys.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

As Arya leaves the ship aboard one of its boats, rowed by Yorko, she realizes she never said farewell to Denyo, and wonders if she will ever see him again.(wiki:Denyo_Terys.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)
