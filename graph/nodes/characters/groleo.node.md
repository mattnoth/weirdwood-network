## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Groleo](wiki:Category:Images_of_Groleo)*

Groleo is an aging [Pentoshi](wiki:Pentos) man.(wiki:Groleo.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Captain Groleo is sent by [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) with three ships, including the great cog *[Saduleon](wiki:Saduleon)*, to bring [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) back to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos). Daenerys instead renames the ships after [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) [dragons](wiki:Dragon)(wiki:Groleo.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and commands Groleo to head for [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay).(wiki:Groleo.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

During the [siege of Meereen](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen) Daenerys realizes she cannot take the city without siege engines, but there is no available wood because the Meereenese have burned all nearby trees. Daenerys commands Groleo to drive his ships ashore, and her [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) and sellswords use the wood of the ships to create siege engines, turtles and battering rams to take the city.(wiki:Groleo.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Groleo is made an admiral by [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), but without a fleet it is an empty title. He inspects the thirteen ships brought by [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos) and pronounces them seaworthy.(wiki:Groleo.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Groleo, [Daario Naharis](wiki:Daario_Naharis), [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo), [Hero](wiki:Hero) and three of the kin of [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) are given as hostages as a guarantee for the safe conduct of the Yunkish commanders who visit [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) when a peace treaty is made between [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) and [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai). [Bloodbeard](wiki:Bloodbeard) subsequently kills Groleo and presents the head of the captain to Hizdahr in retaliation for the death of the Yunkish commander, [Yurkhaz zo Yunzak](wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak), who was killed when [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) appeared in the fighting pits.[*[citation needed](wiki:Help:Manual_of_Style)*]

Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) is enraged and wishes to kill Bloodbeard. The [Brazen Beasts](wiki:Brazen_Beasts) who are in the room when the head is presented are eager to cut down the Yunkish nobles in retaliation, but are puzzled when the order is not given by Hizdahr. Barristan has Hizdahr request Groleo's body be returned so he can be decently buried. Barristan remembers Groleo had a wife, children and grandchildren and all the man wanted to do was return home to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos).[*[citation needed](wiki:Help:Manual_of_Style)*]

When Barristan, [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq), the [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) and the Brazen Beasts seize control of Meereen they use the password *Groleo* in honor of the admiral.(wiki:Groleo.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
