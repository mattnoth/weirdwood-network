## Origins

Urron was the [salt king](wiki:Salt_king) of [Orkmont](wiki:Orkmont) during the rule of his great-uncle, [Urragon IV Greyiron](wiki:Urragon_IV_Greyiron), who had assumed the title [High King of the Iron Islands](wiki:High_King_of_the_Iron_Islands) without being chosen in a [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot). It was the dying Urragon's wish for Urron to succeed him, but the priests of the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God) were determined not to lose the power of kingmaking for a third time after Urragon's assumption of the throne and, before that, [Torgon Greyiron](wiki:Torgon_Greyiron)'s usurpation from [Urrathon IV Goodbrother](wiki:Urrathon_IV_Goodbrother).

Urron put an end to the institution of kingsmoot when he descended with his axemen on [Nagga's Hill](wiki:Nagga's_Hill) on [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk), slaying all captains, the thirteen [salt](wiki:Salt_king) and [rock kings](wiki:Rock_king), and half a hundred priests and prophets assembled for the [choosing](wiki:Choosing). Urron made the high kingship hereditary.(wiki:Urron_Greyiron.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Calling himself simply King of the Iron Islands, Urron had his crown be made of black iron instead of the traditional driftwood.(wiki:Urron_Greyiron.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_iron_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

During Urron's reign of twenty-two years,(wiki:Urron_Greyiron.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) the rulers of the various Iron Islands were reduced to [lords](wiki:Lord), and several ancient lines that refused to bend the knee were extinguished.(wiki:Urron_Greyiron.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_iron_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Along with the kingsmoot, [Galon Whitestaff](wiki:Galon)'s decree against ironborn making war upon each other ended. Urron Redhand and his descendants had to deal with half a dozen major rebellions and at least two major [thrall](wiki:Thrall) uprisings.(wiki:Urron_Greyiron.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_iron_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) [House Greyiron](wiki:House_Greyiron) is said to have ruled the islands for a thousand years, until the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals).(wiki:Urron_Greyiron.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Lord [Baelor Blacktyde](wiki:Baelor_Blacktyde) believes that [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) will descend on all those gathered for the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot) and kill them all, as Urron Greyiron had once done.(wiki:Urron_Greyiron.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw) similarly believes that Euron sounds too much like Urron, and that history is bound to repeat itself.(wiki:Urron_Greyiron.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
