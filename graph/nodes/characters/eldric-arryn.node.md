## Identity

Eldric Arryn was a knight of House Arryn during the regency of King Aegon III Targaryen. Described as shrewd and ambitious (wiki:Eldric_Arryn).

## Origins

Born 119 AC (track_b: Born). Died 134 AC (track_b: Died). Son of Arnold Arryn (track_b: Father).

## Narrative Arc

After Lady Jeyne Arryn died in 134 AC naming Ser Joffrey Arryn as heir, Eldric pressed his mad father Arnold's closer claim to the Eyrie. Many lords supported him, insisting inheritance law could not be set aside by "the whim of a dying woman." Ser Corwyn Corbray, a regent of Aegon III, upheld Jeyne's will and had Eldric executed (wiki:Eldric_Arryn).
