## Identity

Princess Myriah Nymeros Martell was a Dornish princess, wife and queen consort of King Daeron II Targaryen (wiki:Myriah_Martell).

## Origins

Eldest daughter of the Prince of Dorne during the reign of Daeron I Targaryen. Had a younger brother, Prince Maron Martell. Born 146–154 AC in Dorne (track_b: Born, wiki:Myriah_Martell).

## Appearances & Description

Myriah had the typical dark hair and eyes of House Martell. Those traits were passed on to her eldest son, Prince Baelor Targaryen (wiki:Myriah_Martell).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Martell and House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiances). `CULTURE_OF` Dornishmen (track_b: Culture). `HOLDS_TITLE` Princess, Queen (track_b: Titles).

## Narrative Arc

After Daeron I was killed during his attempted conquest of Dorne, King Baelor I Targaryen walked barefoot to Sunspear and forged a peace, which included the betrothal of Myriah to Baelor's cousin Prince Daeron. Their marriage was happy and fruitful, producing four sons: Baelor (170 AC), Aerys, Rhaegel, and Maekar. Myriah's marriage paved the way for Dorne to enter the realm. When Aegon IV died in 184 AC, Daeron became King Daeron II and Myriah became queen. Many Dornishmen flocked to court, alienating other nobles (wiki:Myriah_Martell).
