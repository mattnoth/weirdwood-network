According to her father, Alarra was "as sweet to look upon as any southron lady" (wiki:Alarra_Stark).

## Narrative Arc

When Queen Alysanne Targaryen visited Winterfell in 58 AC, Alarra and Alysanne became particularly close. Near the end of 58 AC, Alarra traveled to King's Landing with her two brothers to attend the tourney celebrating the tenth anniversary of King Jaehaerys I's coronation, and became a lady-in-waiting to the Queen (wiki:Alarra_Stark).
