## Origins

When Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) visited [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) in [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Alarra and Alysanne became particularly close.(wiki:Alarra_Stark.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Near the end of [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Alarra came to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with her two brothers to attend the [tourney celebrating the tenth anniversary](wiki:Tourney_at_King's_Landing_on_the_anniversary_of_the_king's_coronation) of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen)'s coronation. At this time, Alarra also became a [lady-in-waiting](wiki:Lady-in-waiting) to the Queen.(wiki:Alarra_Stark.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

## Appearances & Description

According to her father, Lord [Alaric Stark](wiki:Alaric_Stark), Alarra was "as sweet to look upon as any [southron lady](wiki:Southron)."(wiki:Alarra_Stark.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
