## Origins

Trianna was returned four times to the office of [triarch](wiki:Triarch_of_Volantis), but no woman has been elected since. She was one of the members of the [elephant party](wiki:Elephants_(Volantis)) that brought down [tiger](wiki:Tigers_(Volantis)) rule ending the old wars of the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood).(wiki:Trianna.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
