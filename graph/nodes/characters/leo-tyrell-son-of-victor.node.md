## Identity

Ser Leo Tyrell is a knight of House Tyrell, the son of Ser Victor Tyrell. He is married to Alys Beesbury, with whom he has five children: Alla, Leona, Lyonel, Lucas, and Lorent Tyrell (wiki:Leo_Tyrell_son_of_Victor.cite_ref-1).
