## Origins

Some time between [217 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) and [221 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)(wiki:The_Rat,_the_Hawk,_and_the_Pig.cite_ref-4), these three men attacked [Aelora Targaryen](wiki:Aelora_Targaryen), [Princess of Dragonstone](wiki:Princess_of_Dragonstone), at a masked ball. She committed suicide soon afterward.(wiki:The_Rat,_the_Hawk,_and_the_Pig.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
In [251 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), the trio led a rebellion that was crushed by Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_V)). Daeron perished in the final battle, alongside his lover, Ser [Jeremy Norridge](wiki:Jeremy_Norridge), but the rebels were slain or hanged.(wiki:The_Rat,_the_Hawk,_and_the_Pig.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
