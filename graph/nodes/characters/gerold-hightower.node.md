## Origins

### In the  Kingsguard

It is unknown when Gerold joined the order of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). The young Gerold(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-9) became [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard) around [260 AC](wiki:260_AC), following the presumed death of his predecessor, Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall).(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Rawoiafduncan.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) During the [War of the Ninepenny Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Ninepenny_Kings), Gerold took command of the royal forces after the death of the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Lord [Ormund Baratheon](wiki:Ormund_Baratheon).(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) After the war, he accepted Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) into the order.(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Around [280 AC](wiki:280_AC)/[281 AC](wiki:281_AC), Gerold was shot through the hand by [Ulmer](wiki:Ulmer), an [outlaw](wiki:Outlaw) of the [Kingswood Brotherhood](wiki:Kingswood_Brotherhood), who went on to steal a kiss and some jewels from Princess [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell) of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) In [281 AC](wiki:281_AC), at the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal), Gerold fastened the white cloak of the Kingsguard about the shoulders of the newest member, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) That evening, when King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) ordered Jaime to return to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to guard Queen [Rhaella](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen) and Prince [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen), Gerold offered to go in Jaime's stead, so Jaime could remain at Harrenhal and take part in the tourney, but Aerys refused.(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### Robert's Rebellion

Gerold was present when King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) burned Lord [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark) alive in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep)'s throne room, and while [Brandon Stark](wiki:Brandon_Stark) strangled himself to death. After the two Starks died, Gerold took Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) aside and reminded him they were sworn to defend the king, not to judge him.(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

During the [ensuing rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), after the [Battle of the Bells](wiki:Battle_of_the_Bells), Gerold was sent to find Prince [Rhaegar](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen).(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Rawoiafrhaegar_targaryen.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) While Rhaegar eventually returned to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Gerold did not. He was next seen at the [tower of joy](wiki:Tower_of_joy) in the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), with two of his sworn brothers, Ser [Arthur Dayne](wiki:Arthur_Dayne) and Ser [Oswell Whent](wiki:Oswell_Whent),(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) both of whom had been with Rhaegar when he disappeared originally.(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Rawoiafrhaegar_targaryen.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1) Here, they were guarding [Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark).(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Rawoiafrhaegar_targaryen.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-2)(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

The three Kingsguard were eventually found at the tower by Lyanna's brother, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), and six of Eddard's companions. After a short conversation with Eddard, during which the three knights refused several opportunities to leave unharmed, [combat ensued](wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy) in which Gerold died.(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) Stones from the tower were used to build his cairn upon a nearby ridge.(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Gerold Hightower](wiki:Category:Images_of_Gerold_Hightower)*

Gerold was considered to be fierce, despite his old age,(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) and in his heyday, he would have been stronger than [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) at his prime.(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Gerold's writing in the [White Book](wiki:White_Book) is big and forceful.(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark), who wishes to become a knight of the Kingsguard someday, lists the members of the order he knows from songs and [Old Nan](wiki:Old_Nan)'s stories, among them is Ser Gerold.(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) has a fever dream of Gerold, Ser [Arthur Dayne](wiki:Arthur_Dayne), and Ser [Oswell Whent](wiki:Oswell_Whent) at the [tower of joy](wiki:Tower_of_joy).(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6)

### A Storm of Swords

Resting on a [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) stump, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) dreams of he and [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) being visited by the shades of Gerold, Oswell, Arthur, Ser [Jon Darry](wiki:Jonothor_Darry), Prince [Lewyn Martell](wiki:Lewyn_Martell), and Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) in the depths of [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Gerold_Hightower.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)
