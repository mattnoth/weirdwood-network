## Identity

Creylen is a maester of the Citadel assigned to Casterly Rock (wiki:Creylen).

## Narrative Arc

Creylen serves as maester at Casterly Rock, succeeding the previous maester Beldon. He serves under Lord Tywin Lannister and later Cersei Lannister as Lady of Casterly Rock (wiki:Creylen).
