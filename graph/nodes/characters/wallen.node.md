## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Wallen and [Stiv](wiki:Stiv) travel south of the [Wall](wiki:Wall) with [Osha](wiki:Osha), [Hali](wiki:Hali), and two unnamed [wildling](wiki:Wildling) men. The group attempts to rob [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) when the boy is left unattended during a hunt in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood). However, [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) arrives with [Summer](wiki:Summer) and [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind). When Wallen grabs the reins of Bran's [horse](wiki:Horse), [Dancer](wiki:Dancer), he is knocked into a stream by Grey Wind and killed by the [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf). After Bran has been saved, Robb orders his guards to hack off the heads of the fallen so they can be sent to the Wall, with their bodies left behind for carrion [crows](wiki:Crow).(wiki:Wallen.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
