## Origins

During the [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council) of [136 AC](wiki:136_AC), Lord Marq was among three [regents](wiki:Regent) selected by lot to serve in [the regency](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III) for King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen).(wiki:Marq_Merryweather.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Marq was considered an inoffensive, though undistinguished, choice.(wiki:Marq_Merryweather.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

While the regents and Lord [Torrhen Manderly](wiki:Torrhen_Manderly), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), were discussing Aegon's first [royal progress](wiki:Royal_progress), the young king entered the room and dismissed them all, for he had [come of age](wiki:Age_of_majority) and no longer wanted their oversight.(wiki:Marq_Merryweather.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
