## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Weese](wiki:Weese) has his dog tear a chunk from the leg of one of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal)'s latrine boys because the youth annoys him.(wiki:Spotted_dog.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Because of Weese's abusive nature, [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) gives his name to [Jaqen H'ghar](wiki:Jaqen_H'ghar). Weese is later found dead, his dog having torn out his throat. While the dog eats parts of his face and ears, someone kills her with a crossbow bolt.(wiki:Spotted_dog.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

While at the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White), Arya learns of a paste mixed with [basilisk](wiki:Basilisk) blood which can cause madness in man or beast.(wiki:Spotted_dog.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Jaqen H'ghar may have fed the paste to the spotted dog before she attacked Weese.
