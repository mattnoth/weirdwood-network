## Appearances & Description

The sardonic Grimtongue wields a spear.(wiki:Grimtongue.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Along with the rest of [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy)'s supporters, Grimtongue sails to [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) as Asha contemplates her next move after the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Grimtongue.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Five [northmen](wiki:Northmen) are caught trying to infiltrate Deepwood, with one, a [Flint](wiki:House_Flint_of_the_mountains), pinned to the ground by Grimtongue's spear. Asha's ironborn flee toward the [Bay of Ice](wiki:Bay_of_Ice) when Deepwood comes under attack. When [Rolfe the Dwarf](wiki:Rolfe_the_Dwarf) is killed by northmen during the night, Grimtongue remarks about his beheading. Grimtongue counts each northmen he slays during the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte), reaching at least nine.(wiki:Grimtongue.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Grimtongue is captured along with Asha, [Tris Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley), and several [other ironborn](wiki:Ragged_nine) by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Grimtongue.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Grimtongue is later freed and ransomed along with the other ironborn by [Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris), who needs an escort to get him to Stannis during the king's [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell).(wiki:Grimtongue.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
