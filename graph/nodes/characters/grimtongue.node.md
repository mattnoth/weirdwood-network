## Identity

Grimtongue is a sardonic ironborn warrior and one of Asha Greyjoy's crew on the Black Wind (wiki:Grimtongue).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Greyjoy (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` ironborn (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Grimtongue wields a spear and has a sardonic wit (wiki:Grimtongue.cite_ref-Radwd26).

## Narrative Arc

Grimtongue sails with Asha to Deepwood Motte. When northmen try to infiltrate the fortress, Grimtongue pins a Flint man to the ground with his spear. During the fight by Deepwood Motte, he counts each northman he kills, reaching at least nine. He remarks about Rolfe the Dwarf's beheading: "Rolfe the Dwarf's not so tall as he once was." Grimtongue is captured along with Asha by Stannis Baratheon (wiki:Grimtongue.cite_ref-Radwd26). He is later freed and ransomed by Tycho Nestoris of the Iron Bank, who needs an ironborn escort to reach Stannis during the march on Winterfell (wiki:Grimtongue.cite_ref-Radwd62).
