## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Planning for the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s admiral, [Lord High Captain](wiki:Lord_High_Captain) Ser [Imry Florent](wiki:Imry_Florent), assigns Davos Seaworth and his sons' ships a place in the second line of battle, on the dangerous starboard wing. While [Allard Seaworth](wiki:Allard_Seaworth) voices his belief that it is a place of honor, Davos states it is a place of peril. Maric and his other sons look pityingly at him for his nervousness.(wiki:Maric_Seaworth.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Maric's ship, the *Fury*, is destroyed during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater) by an explosion of [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire).(wiki:Maric_Seaworth.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

### A Storm of Swords

Davos, a survivor of the Battle of the Blackwater, remembers his four sons who died during the battle.(wiki:Maric_Seaworth.cite_ref-Rasos5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Imprisoned beneath [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) along with Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent), Davos asks him if there had been any word of survivors from Alester's nephew Imry's ship, as his son Maric was the oarmaster. Alester tells Davos that the *Fury* burned and sank with all hands.(wiki:Maric_Seaworth.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Davos tells Stannis he planned to murder [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) because he believed she sent his sons to the flames, but Stannis tells him the fires were not her work.(wiki:Maric_Seaworth.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
