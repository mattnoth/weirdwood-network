## Origins

Amos defeated [Samwell Blackwood](wiki:Samwell_Blackwood) in a duel when Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) visited the [Trident](wiki:Trident) in [112 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), as the rivals unsuccessfully fought for her hand.(wiki:Amos_Bracken.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
[House Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken) declared for the [greens](wiki:Greens) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), while [House Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood) supported the [blacks](wiki:Blacks). Amos cut down Lord Samwell during the [Battle of the Burning Mill](wiki:Battle_of_the_Burning_Mill), only to then be slain by a [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) arrow allegedly fired by Samwell's sister, [Black Aly](wiki:Alysanne_Blackwood). Ser [Raylon Rivers](wiki:Raylon_Rivers) led the Bracken survivors back to the [Stone Hedge](wiki:Stone_Hedge), which had been [stormed](wiki:Storming_of_Stone_Hedge) in the meantime by Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen).(wiki:Amos_Bracken.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
