## Appearances & Description

Ser Marq is short and "runty", according to Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister).(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) A swaggering and overly confident young [knight](wiki:Knight), Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) and Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) consider Marq to be hot-headed and brash.(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Marq is good friends with Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully).(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) commands Lord [Clement Piper](wiki:Clement_Piper) and [Lord Vance](wiki:Lord_Vance) to guard the pass below the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth) when the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) start to mass troops there;(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) the lords are attended by their eldest sons and heirs, Ser Marq and Ser [Karyl Vance](wiki:Karyl_Vance). While away from their own domains, their lands are raided by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane).(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

In response, [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), [Lord Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident), sends Ser Marq, Ser Karyl, and Ser [Raymun Darry](wiki:Raymun_Darry) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with the survivors of the raided village [Sherrer](wiki:Sherrer) to ask the permission of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) to destroy the [westermen](wiki:Westermen) who are raiding the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). Marq swears that the leader of the raiders is Ser Gregor himself, violating the [king's peace](wiki:King's_peace) and requesting the crown's permission to exact revenge against Clegane. Desiring justice over vengeance, [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), sends Raymun with [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion), [Lord of Blackhaven](wiki:Lord_of_Blackhaven), to execute Clegane.(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

After their fathers are defeated by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) in the [battle in the hills below the Golden Tooth](wiki:Battle_in_the_hills_below_the_Golden_Tooth), Marq and Ser [Karyl Vance](wiki:Karyl_Vance) begin launching raids across the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) of the [Trident](wiki:Trident) into the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands).(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Their approximately fifty men attack [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) supply lines, hampering Jaime's siege of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Marq and Karyl continue raiding until [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) arrives and breaks the siege with the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood) and the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps). The loss of the Lannister outriders was thought to be due to Marq, and so it was not realized that the host lead by Robb was approaching Riverrun.(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Afterward, Marq sits at Robb's war council at Riverrun, where he calls for a march against [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) and also for allying with [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon). Robb is instead proclaimed [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) by the [northmen](wiki:Northmen) and [rivermen](wiki:Rivermen) assembled at Riverrun.(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Clash of Kings

At the insistence of Edmure, Robb gives the river lords leave to return to their lands to defend their domains or restore their damaged seats. Marq is among the first to leave Riverrun with his men to defend his [burned lands](wiki:Burning_of_Pinkmaiden_Castle), where he wins a few small victories against the Lannisters.(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Marq leads [House Piper](wiki:House_Piper) during the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords).(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Storm of Swords

With his father Hoster dying and his sister [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) under house arrest, Edmure spends much of his time with his friends, Marq and [Patrek Mallister](wiki:Patrek_Mallister).(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Marq is one of the seven nobles who launch Hoster's [funeral boat](wiki:Customs) at Riverrun.(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

During Edmure's journey to the [Twins](wiki:Twins) for his wedding to [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey), he rides with Marq, Patrek, [Lymond Goodbrook](wiki:Lymond_Goodbrook), and young [Vances](wiki:House_Vance).(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Marq's retinue from House Piper includes at least five kinsmen who are [knights](wiki:Knight), and twenty [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms).(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Marq is among Robb's nobles who eat the food and drink of [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey), [Lord of the Crossing](wiki:Lord_of_the_Crossing), upon arrival at the Twins, granting them [guest right](wiki:Guest_right).(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) During the wedding feast, Marq plays a drinking game with Ser [Danwell Frey](wiki:Danwell_Frey) and jests with [Alyx Frey](wiki:Alyx_Frey). He joins the group which takes Roslin from the hall, and [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) observes him removing the shoe of Edmure's bride as part of the [bedding custom](wiki:Bedding).(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) The [Starks](wiki:House_Stark) and [Tullys](wiki:House_Tully) are betrayed by Houses [Frey](wiki:House_Frey) and [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton), however, and Robb is killed by [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), [Lord of the Dreadfort](wiki:Lord_of_the_Dreadfort).(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1)

Traveling through the riverlands, [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) and [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) encounter a Piper bowman sworn to Marq. The bowman was deathly wounded by a Bolton during the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), and is given the [gift of mercy](wiki:Gift_of_mercy) by the [Hound](wiki:Hound).(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Rasos65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Marq is held by the Freys as a [hostage](wiki:Hostage) at the Twins after the Red Wedding, to the consternation of his father, Lord [Clement Piper](wiki:Clement_Piper), who believes that his bending his knee to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) will lead to his son being returned. That does not happen, which enrages Clement, leading him to threaten [Edwyn Frey](wiki:Edwyn_Frey) at the war council during the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun).(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)

After Riverrun surrenders, Jaime Lannister informs Edwyn that King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) requires all captives taken during the Red Wedding, which displeases the Freys.(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Marq remains a captive.(wiki:Marq_Piper.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
