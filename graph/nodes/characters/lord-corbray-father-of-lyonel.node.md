## Origins

Lord Corbray fought for Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_Baratheon) during the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident). He was wounded during the battle and his eldest son and heir, [Lyonel](wiki:Lyonel_Corbray), carried him back to the [maesters](wiki:Maesters) in the rear, while his second son, Ser [Lyn](wiki:Lyn_Corbray), grabbed his sword, [Lady Forlorn](wiki:Lady_Forlorn), and led a charge against the [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) led by Prince [Lewyn Martell](wiki:Lewyn_Martell).(wiki:Lord_Corbray_(father_of_Lyonel).cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Lord Corbray was eventually succeeded by Lyonel, but gave Lady Forlorn to Lyn.(wiki:Lord_Corbray_(father_of_Lyonel).cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
