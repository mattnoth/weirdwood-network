## Appearances & Description

Galyeon is a big barrel-chested man. He is bald and has a black beard.(wiki:Galyeon_of_Cuy.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) He is renowned for the length of his songs.(wiki:Galyeon_of_Cuy.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Galyeon is one of the seven singers chosen to compete at the [wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and Lady [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Galyeon_of_Cuy.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) At the wedding, he is the third singer to perform. Accompanied by six musicians, he sings only one song: a [seventy-seven verses long song](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater_(song)) about the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Galyeon_of_Cuy.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Galyeon testifies during [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s trial that he saw how Tyrion picked up Joffrey's [chalice](wiki:Wedding_chalice) and pour the last of the [poisoned](wiki:Poison) [wine](wiki:Wine) on the floor.(wiki:Galyeon_of_Cuy.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
