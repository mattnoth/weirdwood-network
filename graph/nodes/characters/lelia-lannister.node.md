## Identity

Queen Lelia Lannister was a noblewoman from House Lannister who became Queen of the Iron Islands through her marriage to King Harmund II Hoare (wiki:Lelia_Lannister). Known as "the fairest flower of the west."

## Origins

Daughter of the King of the Rock (track_b: Father). Titles included Princess, Queen of the Iron Islands, Lady of Hoare Castle, and Dowager Queen (track_b: Titles).

## Narrative Arc

Harmund II Hoare, known as Harmund the Haggler, spent his youth as a ward of the Lannisters at Casterly Rock. Once king, he married Lady Lelia. Their children were raised in the Faith of the Seven (wiki:Lelia_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf). When their eldest son Harmund III became king, he was opposed by ironborn priests for his opposition to reaving. The Shrike, a priest, led a rebellion and crowned the younger brother Hagon Hoare. Both Harmund III and the Dowager Queen Lelia were mutilated — Lelia's lips, ears, eyelids, and tongue were removed, and she was sent back to the Rock by longship. Her outraged nephew, the King of the Rock, went to war against the ironborn. Hagon was defeated and suffered the same mutilations before being hanged (wiki:Lelia_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf).
