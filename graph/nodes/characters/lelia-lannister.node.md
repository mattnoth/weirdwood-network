## Origins

[Harmund II Hoare](wiki:Harmund_II_Hoare), known as Harmund the Haggler, spent his youth as a ward of the Lannisters at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock). Once [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), Harmund married Lady Lelia, the daughter of the [King of the Rock](wiki:King_of_the_Rock) and the "fairest flower of the west".(wiki:Lelia_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Their children were raised in the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:Lelia_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

When Lelia's eldest son became king as [Harmund III Hoare](wiki:Harmund_III_Hoare), he was opposed by many [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) and the priests of the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God) for his opposition to reaving, [salt wives](wiki:Salt_wives), and [thralls](wiki:Thrall). A priest known as the [Shrike](wiki:Shrike_(priest)) led a quick and successful rebellion which crowned Harmund's younger brother, [Hagon Hoare](wiki:Hagon_Hoare), who restored ironborn traditions. Harmund III and the Dowager Queen Lelia were both mutilated for having turned away from the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God). Lelia's lips, ears, eyelids, and tongue were removed, after which she was sent by longship back to the Rock.(wiki:Lelia_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

The then-King of the Rock, Lelia's outraged nephew, went to war against the ironborn. Hagon the Heartless was defeated by Ser [Aubrey Crakehall](wiki:Aubrey_Crakehall) in the seventh year of fighting. Before being hanged, he suffered the same mutilations which had been done to Lelia.(wiki:Lelia_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
