## Origins

Olyvar died at the side of his king, [Daeron I Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen), as depicted in a [tapestry](wiki:Tapestry) at [Old Oak](wiki:Old_Oak).(wiki:Olyvar_Oakheart.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) King Daeron was slain under a banner of peace when the [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) betrayed and attacked him during the [Conquest of Dorne](wiki:Conquest_of_Dorne) in [161 AC](wiki:161_AC). Three knights of his Kingsguard died in his defense,(wiki:Olyvar_Oakheart.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_daeron_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and Olyvar Oakheart may have been of these three men.
