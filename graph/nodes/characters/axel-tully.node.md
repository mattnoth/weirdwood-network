## Identity

Axel Tully was the first Lord of Riverrun and a head of House Tully (wiki:Axel_Tully). His father was Ser Edmure Tully, a turncoat who supported the Andal warlord Armistead Vance (track_b: Father).

## Origins

Son of Ser Edmure Tully, who fought under Tristifer IV Mudd before switching allegiance to Armistead Vance after the king's death (wiki:Axel_Tully).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Tully (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Rivermen (track_b: Culture). `HOLDS_TITLE` Lord of Riverrun (track_b: Title).

## Narrative Arc

Axel received a grant of lands at the juncture of the Red Fork and the Tumblestone from Armistead Vance, where he raised a red castle he named Riverrun (wiki:Axel_Tully).
