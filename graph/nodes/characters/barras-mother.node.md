## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Thanks to Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) finds [Barra](wiki:Barra) and her mother in his search for the [bastards](wiki:Bastard) of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). The mother asks to see Robert again, just to show him how beautiful his daughter is. Eddard promises to talk about it with the king.(wiki:Barra's_mother.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Clash of Kings

Barra and her mother are killed by [Allar Deem](wiki:Allar_Deem), an officer of the [City Watch](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing), on orders from Commander [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt), who is carrying out a purge of King Robert's bastards at the command of Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister).(wiki:Barra's_mother.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) later orders Allar to be tossed overboard from *[Summer's Dream](wiki:Summer's_Dream)*.(wiki:Barra's_mother.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
