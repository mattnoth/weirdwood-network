## Identity

Alla Tyrell is the eldest daughter of Ser Leo Tyrell and Lady Alys Beesbury, a granddaughter of Ser Victor Tyrell and distant cousin of Lord Mace Tyrell of Highgarden (wiki:Alla_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6).

## Origins

Born roughly 286 AC in the Reach (track_b: Born). Daughter of Leo Tyrell and Alys Beesbury (track_b: Father, Mother).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Tyrell (track_b: Allegiance). One of Margaery Tyrell's ladies-in-waiting and constant companions (wiki:Alla_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6).

## Appearances & Description

Alla is shy and pretty, close in age to Sansa Stark, and has not yet flowered. She has a beautiful singing voice and can play the woodharp. She sometimes plays a kissing game with Megga Tyrell (wiki:Alla_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos16).

## Narrative Arc

Alla accompanies Margaery Tyrell to King's Landing and befriends Sansa Stark. She attends Sansa's wedding to Tyrion Lannister, where she and her cousins ignore the bride to protest the match, as the Tyrells had hoped Sansa would marry Willas Tyrell. She remains in King's Landing as part of Margaery's entourage through Margaery's marriages to Joffrey and Tommen (wiki:Alla_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6, wiki:Alla_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12).
