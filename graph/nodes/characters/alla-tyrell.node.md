## Appearances & Description

Alla is shy and pretty. She is a [maiden](wiki:Maiden) close in age to [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), and has not [flowered](wiki:Flowering) yet.(wiki:Alla_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Alla and [Megga Tyrell](wiki:Megga_Tyrell) follow their cousin [Elinor](wiki:Elinor_Tyrell), who has already flowered.(wiki:Alla_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Alla has a beautiful singing voice and can play the [woodharp](wiki:Harp).(wiki:Alla_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) She sometimes plays a kissing game with Megga.(wiki:Alla_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Alla is one of Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell)'s [ladies-in-waiting](wiki:Handmaid). She is a constant companion of Margaery. She meets and befriends [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) when she is invited to dinner with the Tyrells.(wiki:Alla_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Alla attends Sansa's [marriage](wiki:Marriage) to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister). During [the wedding feast](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark), Alla and her cousins ignore the bride to show their dissatisfaction with the match, as the Tyrells had hoped for Sansa to marry [Willas Tyrell](wiki:Willas_Tyrell) instead.(wiki:Alla_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Alla attends [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Tommen_I_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) of Margaery and King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), during which she dances with Tommen and Margaery.(wiki:Alla_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Taena of Myr](wiki:Taena_of_Myr) tells Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) that her maid, [Senelle](wiki:Senelle), brings messages to Alla and her cousins.(wiki:Alla_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

Margaery's cousins tease Ser [Osney Kettleblack](wiki:Osney_Kettleblack) about his nose.(wiki:Alla_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Ser [Osmund Kettleblack](wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack) tells Cersei that Margaery often sings with Alla, and that Ser [Tallad the Tall](wiki:Tallad_the_Tall) desires Alla and her cousin [Elinor](wiki:Elinor_Tyrell).(wiki:Alla_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Tallad accompanies Alla during one of Margaery's rides.(wiki:Alla_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Alla runs from the room when Cersei tells Margaery that Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) has been wounded in the [siege of Dragonstone](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone).(wiki:Alla_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Cersei conspires to have Queen Margaery implicated in extramarital relations, so she has the [Blue Bard](wiki:Blue_Bard) confess that Margaery and her cousins had sex with many men. Alla allegedly only watched, however, and tried to persuade Margaery to stop. Cersei thinks this would make the confession more believable.(wiki:Alla_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Taena tries to persuade Alla to confess and bear witness against Margaery and their cousins. Cersei then has Margaery and her cousins, including Alla, imprisoned by the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:Alla_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Almost all of the men named as Margaery's lovers have denied the accusation or recanted, so she and her cousins, including Alla, are handed over by the [High Septon](wiki:High_Sparrow) to the custody of Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly). Randyll swears a holy oath to return them for their trial.(wiki:Alla_Tyrell.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
