## Origins

Allar was appointed [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) in [97 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) during the late reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen, after the death of Grand Maester [Elysar](wiki:Elysar). He served for four years until he died of a stroke in [101 AC](wiki:101_AC). His successor as Grand Maester was [Runciter](wiki:Runciter).(wiki:Allar.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
