## Identity

Allar was a maester of the Citadel who served as Grand Maester during the late reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen (wiki:Allar).

## Origins

Died in 101 AC in King's Landing (track_b: Died). Appointed Grand Maester in 97 AC after the death of Grand Maester Elysar. Served for four years until he died of a stroke. His successor was Grand Maester Runciter (wiki:Allar).
