## Origins

When the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) broke out in [129 AC](wiki:129_AC), Ser [Otto Hightower](wiki:Otto_Hightower) suspected that the Evenstar of Tarth may side with Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and the [blacks](wiki:Blacks).(wiki:Bryndemere_Tarth.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) It is unknown if he did or if he followed his liege lord, [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon), and supported the [greens](wiki:Greens).

In [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Ser [Gedmund Peake](wiki:Gedmund_Peake) and [Ned Bean](wiki:Ned_Bean) were sent against [Racallio Ryndoon](wiki:Racallio_Ryndoon), the self-proclaimed [King of the Narrow Sea](wiki:King_of_the_Narrow_Sea). On their way to the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones), they were joined first by the Velaryon fleet commanded by Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) and then by Lord Bryndemere who brought a dozen longships to further swell their ranks. At [Tarth](wiki:Tarth), Lord Bryndemere told them Racallio had allied himself with the [Archon](wiki:Archon) of [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) and the [Sealord](wiki:Sealord) of [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Bryndemere_Tarth.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
