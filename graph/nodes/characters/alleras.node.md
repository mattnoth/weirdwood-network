## Origins

Alleras has a [Dornishman](wiki:Dornishmen) for a father and a [Summer Islander](wiki:Summer_Isles) for a mother.(wiki:Alleras.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9) He has been at the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) for a year, and has forged three links in his [maester](wiki:Maester)'s chain, the most recent of which is [copper](wiki:Copper).(wiki:Alleras.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10) At some point in their time at the Citadel, Leo Tyrell gave Alleras the nickname "Sphinx", as he was "a bit of this, a bit of that", like a [sphinx](wiki:Sphinx) is made of multiple creatures.(wiki:Alleras.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-11)

## Appearances & Description

Alleras is slender, comely, and has a soft smile.(wiki:Alleras.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) He has curly black hair, a widow's peak, and big black eyes like [onyx](wiki:Onyx).(wiki:Alleras.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Alleras has skin the color of teak(wiki:Alleras.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) or a light brown [ale](wiki:Ale).(wiki:Alleras.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The youth usually wears doeskin breeches, and a snug green brigandine with [iron](wiki:Iron) studs.(wiki:Alleras.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) He speaks with a soft [Dornish](wiki:Dornish) drawl.(wiki:Alleras.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Alleras prefers the sweet [wines](wiki:Wine) of the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles) rather than of those of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Alleras.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) He displays remarkable skill with his [goldenheart](wiki:Goldenheart) longbow, also from the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles).(wiki:Alleras.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

Alleras is in a circle of friends that includes [Armen](wiki:Armen), [Pate](wiki:Pate_(novice)), [Mollander](wiki:Mollander), and [Roone](wiki:Roone). He does not consider their fellow novice [Leo Tyrell](wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn)) to be a friend.(wiki:Alleras.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) Alleras is doted on by the serving wenches of the [Quill and Tankard](wiki:Quill_and_Tankard), including [Rosey](wiki:Rosey), which makes Pate jealous.(wiki:Alleras.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Having forged his copper link, Alleras celebrates at the [Quill and Tankard](wiki:Quill_and_Tankard) by purchasing drinks for his friends. He practices his archery skills by shooting at apples thrown above the [Honeywine](wiki:Honeywine) by [Mollander](wiki:Mollander). Alleras strikes them all, except for the last apple which he allows to fall into the river untouched, as he believes the day you make them all, you stop improving. He and his friends speak of rumors of [dragons](wiki:Dragon) in [Essos](wiki:Essos), and Alleras tells them that [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) is still alive, and that "the dragon has three heads". [Leo Tyrell](wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn)) arrives at the inn, and insults the companions while confirming Alleras's story, and informing them that there is a [glass candle](wiki:Glass_candle) burning in [Archmaester](wiki:Archmaester) [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn)'s chambers. The friends decide to head back to the Citadel to retire for the evening, except for [Pate](wiki:Pate_(novice)) who is left with Leo. Before he goes, Alleras reassures Pate that if Leo does not have the [coin](wiki:Coin) to buy wine, he cannot afford [Rosey](wiki:Rosey) either.(wiki:Alleras.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-12)

Via a glass candle, Archmaester Marwyn [foresees](wiki:Prophecy) the arrival in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) of [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), and sends Alleras to the [Seneschal's Court](wiki:Seneschal's_Court) to meet him. After Sam tells his story to Alleras — including the fact that his traveling companion, [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)), mysteriously spoke that "the [sphinx](wiki:Sphinx) is the riddle, not the riddler", before his death — the Sphinx brings him to Marwyn at the [Ravenry](wiki:Ravenry). Maester Aemon believed that [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) was the [prince that was promised](wiki:Prince_that_was_promised), and must be taught and protected by a maester; but since Aemon died on the way to Oldtown, Marwyn decides to sail to find her in [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay). The archmaester tasks Alleras with looking after Sam.(wiki:Alleras.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)
