## Identity

Sly (stableboy). One of 2 characters named Sly. Sly was a stableboy at Hogs Head, a disreputable inn in Bitterbridge, during the Dance of the Dragons. (wiki:Sly_(stableboy))

## Origins

The disguised Ser [Rickard Thorne](wiki:Rickard_Thorne) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) and his charge, Prince [Maelor Targaryen](wiki:Maelor_Targaryen), tried to stay overnight in the [Hogs Head](wiki:Hogs_Head) while journeying to the safety of Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower)'s host at the nearby [siege of Longtable](wiki:Siege_of_Longtable). Sly was instructed by the owner of the inn, [Ben Buttercakes](wiki:Ben_Buttercakes), to search for [coins](wiki:Currency) in Rickard's belongings, but he found Maelor's [dragon egg](wiki:Dragon_egg) wrapped in Rickard's white cloak instead. When he informed the inn's common room of his discovery, Rickard fled with Maelor, slaying Ben on his way out.
