## Identity

Mace Tyrell is the Lord of Highgarden, Lord Paramount of the Mander, Defender of the Marches, High Marshal of the Reach, and Warden of the South. He is the head of House Tyrell (wiki:Mace_Tyrell).

## Origins

Son of Lord Luthor Tyrell and Lady Olenna Redwyne. Mace became Lord of Highgarden after his father rode off a cliff while hawking (track_b: Father, wiki:Mace_Tyrell). Born between 256–263 AC at Highgarden (track_b: Born).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Tyrell (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` the Reach (track_b: Culture). Mace's closest friend is his brother-in-law Lord Paxter Redwyne (wiki:Mace_Tyrell). He has a great dislike for Dornishmen, especially Prince Oberyn Martell, who accidentally crippled his heir Willas (wiki:Mace_Tyrell).

## Appearances & Description

Mace is a handsome, once-powerful-looking man who has turned fat, with curly brown hair and a triangular beard speckled with white and grey. He is often red-faced and dresses in green velvet trimmed with sable, with a gold mantle over green (wiki:Mace_Tyrell). He has a generally jovial personality but is considered an oaf by his mother Olenna and a fool by Oberyn Martell and Cersei Lannister (wiki:Mace_Tyrell).

## Narrative Arc

During Robert's Rebellion, Mace is credited with defeating Robert at the Battle of Ashford, though the van under Lord Randyll Tarly won most of the victory. He then besieged Storm's End until surrendering to Eddard Stark (wiki:Mace_Tyrell). After Robert I's death, Mace crowns Renly Baratheon and weds his daughter Margaery to him. Following Renly's death, Littlefinger negotiates an alliance bringing the Tyrells to the Lannister cause. Mace and his sons arrive for the Battle of the Blackwater (agot-tyrion-09, wiki:Mace_Tyrell). Named master of ships, Mace is housed in the Maidenvault. He insists Loras join the Kingsguard to protect Margaery (wiki:Mace_Tyrell). In AFFC, Cersei sends Mace to besiege Storm's End to remove him from court. When Margaery is arrested by the Faith, Mace marches his army back to King's Landing (wiki:Mace_Tyrell). In ADWD, Ser Kevan Lannister names Mace Hand of the King to placate House Tyrell. Mace refuses to march his army from the city until Margaery's trial concludes (wiki:Mace_Tyrell).
