## Origins

Mace became [Lord of Highgarden](wiki:Lord_of_Highgarden) once his father, Lord [Luthor Tyrell](wiki:Luthor_Tyrell), rode off a cliff while hawking.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Mace was one of the attendees at the [tourney](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) in [281 AC](wiki:281_AC).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Mace was in command of troops from the [Reach](wiki:Reach) who marched against Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End). Mace is credited with defeating Robert in the [Battle of Ashford](wiki:Battle_of_Ashford), although the van under Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) achieved most of the victory before Mace's arrival at [Ashford](wiki:Ashford).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-5) He then led the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End), which was successfully held by Robert's brother, [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Mace and his closest bannerman, Lord [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne), surrendered to Robert's ally, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), after the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

According to Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell), Mace encouraged his heir, [Willas Tyrell](wiki:Willas_Tyrell), to participate in [tourneys](wiki:Tourney) before he was ready, leading to the accidental crippling of Willas by Oberyn.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Some of the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne)'s debts under the new king, Robert I Baratheon, are owed to Lord Mace.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Mace Tyrell](wiki:Category:Images_of_Mace_Tyrell)*

Mace is a handsome and once-powerful-looking man who has since turned fat.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) He has curly brown hair and cuts his beard, which has white and grey specks in it, into a triangular shape.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) He is often red-faced.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) thinks of Mace as the same age as her father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), although Mace is closer in age to her than to Tywin.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-2)

Mace has a generally jovial personality.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) He thinks himself a great warrior,(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) although his reputation rests solely on the victory over [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) in the [Battle of Ashford](wiki:Battle_of_Ashford) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), which had largely been won by Mace's bannerman, Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly), before Mace arrived at the battlefield,(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2)(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) and the command Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-3) Mace is ambitious, wishing to see a grandchild of his on the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Despite that, his mother, Lady [Olenna Tyrell](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell), can easily hector him, and so influence his decisions.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Mace is considered an oaf by his mother(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) and by Cersei Lannister,(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) and a fool by ## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

After the death of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), Mace and his brothers,(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-23) uncles, and sons are ordered to come to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to swear fealty to the new king, [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) Instead, Mace and his sons crown Robert's youngest brother, [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), at [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden), where Renly also [weds](wiki:Wedding_of_Renly_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) Mace's daughter, [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Mace serves Renly as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) While Renly slowly marches toward King's Landing with a huge host, Mace remains at Highgarden with ten thousand men.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) Mace closes the [roseroad](wiki:Roseroad),(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) leading to famine and [rioting](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing) in the capital.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

Renly is [slain at Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)) by a [shadow assassin](wiki:Shadow_assassin) sent by [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre);(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0) Margaery claims the marriage was unconsummated.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0) Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) rides to [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge) to meet Mace's angry son, Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell),(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-2) and then to Highgarden to meet with Mace and his mother, Lady [Olenna Tyrell](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0) Littlefinger negotiates an alliance between Houses [Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon_of_King's_Landing), [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister), and [Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell), including a marriage between King Joffrey and Margaery. Mace and his two younger sons, Ser [Garlan](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell) and Ser Loras, wait with a huge host at [Tumbler's Falls](wiki:Tumbler's_Falls), where they are joined by Lords [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), [Mathis Rowan](wiki:Mathis_Rowan), and [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly). The combined army floats on Mace's fleet of barges along the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) toward the capital. They disembark half a day from King's landing, marching on to defeat [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0) Mace commands the host's left wing during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)

### A Storm of Swords

After the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), the victorious great [lords](wiki:Lord) meet to divide the spoils, of which Mace is granted the lands of [House Florent](wiki:House_Florent) for his second son, [Garlan Tyrell](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Mace, who is named [master of ships](wiki:Master_of_ships),(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) and his entourage are housed in the [Maidenvault](wiki:Maidenvault).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) As part of the alliance, Mace insists that his youngest son, Loras, join the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) to protect his sister, Margaery.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-1)

During [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark) of [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), Mace dances sedately with his wife, [Alerie](wiki:Alerie_Hightower). Red-faced and sweaty, he also dances with Sansa.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) Mace refuses Tywin's offer to wed [Willas Tyrell](wiki:Willas_Tyrell) to the widowed Queen Dowager [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1)

As a wedding gift, Mace gives King Joffrey a seven-sided [wedding chalice](wiki:Wedding_chalice) decorated with great houses: [ruby lion](wiki:House_Lannister), [emerald rose](wiki:House_Tyrell), [onyx stag](wiki:House_Baratheon), [silver trout](wiki:House_Tully), [blue jade falcon](wiki:House_Arryn), [opal sun](wiki:House_Martell), and [pearl direwolf](wiki:House_Stark).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0) Mace removes Margaery's [maiden](wiki:Maiden) cloak during her wedding to Joffrey. With the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) seemingly winding down, Mace complains there are no victories left for him.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-1) King Joffrey chokes to death during the [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-2)

Mace is meeting with Tywin and Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) when Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) returns to the capital.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0) Mace, Tywin, and Oberyn serve as judges at the trial of [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) for the murder of Joffrey.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0) Mace wants Tyrion dead because his daughter was drinking from the chalice that contained [the poison](wiki:Strangler) that killed Joffrey.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) However, Littlefinger reveals to [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) that Mace's mother, Lady [Olenna Tyrell](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell), [poisoned](wiki:Poison) Joffrey.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-2)

### A Feast for Crows

Cersei, the [Queen Regent](wiki:Regent) for her second son, the boy king [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_I_Baratheon), does not trust Mace and his friends from the [Reach](wiki:Reach).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0) Tywin Lannister had wanted Mace's uncle, [Garth Tyrell](wiki:Garth_Tyrell), to become [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin), but Cersei countermands her late father's decision and instead names Lord [Gyles Rosby](wiki:Gyles_Rosby).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-1) Cersei refuses to name Mace as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), fearing he conspired with Tyrion to murder Tywin.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-4) Cersei names [Aurane Waters](wiki:Aurane_Waters) as [lord admiral](wiki:Lord_admiral).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0)

Cersei follows Jaime's suggestion(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) to task Mace with capturing [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), which is held by Ser [Gilbert Farring](wiki:Gilbert_Farring) for Stannis.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0) After [Margaery weds King Tommen](wiki:Wedding_of_Tommen_I_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) in the royal sept of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), Mace sets out for Storm's End with Lord [Mathis Rowan](wiki:Mathis_Rowan) and half the Tyrell strength.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0)(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0)

With Lord Tyrell gone from the capital, Cersei plots against his daughter, Queen Margaery.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-1) Accusations of immorality lead to the imprisonment of Margaery by the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven). When Mace receives news of this he leaves the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(300)) and marches his army back to King's Landing, leaving Lord Rowan to continue the siege with fewer men. Cersei allows Aurane to launch their [new dromonds](wiki:Cersei's_dromonds) on the Blackwater to deter Mace, but Aurane absconds for open waters with the ships.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0) The [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow) has Cersei arrested within the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

At [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys), Ser [Laswell Peake](wiki:Laswell_Peake) hints to [Harry Strickland](wiki:Harry_Strickland) that the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) still has friends in the [Reach](wiki:Reach), and that Mace overestimates his strength.(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0)

Mace arrives at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to find that his daughter [Margaery](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) and her cousins have already been released by the High Sparrow, given the weak evidence against them. They are turned over to the custody of Mace's bannerman, Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly), whose army reached the city first from [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool). The girls' release is conditional on Randyll's holy oath to return them for trial. Cersei is stripped of the regency. Her uncle, Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), arrives in the city and is made [regent](wiki:Regent) to King Tommen. To placate House Tyrell following Cersei's accusations, Kevan names Mace as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Lord Tarly as [justiciar](wiki:Justiciar), and Lord [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne) as [lord admiral](wiki:Lord_admiral).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-0)

Despite numerous threats facing the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), including [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)) and the Golden Company, Mace refuses to march his army from the city before Margaery's trial. Kevan grows increasingly resentful of Mace's apparent insatiability. Mace adds a hundred of [his own men](wiki:House_Tyrell_guards) to the [City Watch of King's Landing](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing) and plans a new [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand) double the size of its predecessor,(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-0) which Cersei had burned with [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-1) Mace seats himself on an oaken throne carved in the shape of a hand, and he intends to name his uncle Garth as [lord treasurer](wiki:Lord_treasurer).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-1) Mace is unaware that [Nymeria Sand](wiki:Nymeria_Sand) is traveling from [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) to the capital to join the [small council](wiki:Small_council).(wiki:Mace_Tyrell.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-2)
