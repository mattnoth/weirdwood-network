## Identity

Prince Rhaegel Targaryen was the third son of King Daeron II Targaryen and Queen Myriah Martell. He was touched by madness — sweet and gentle but meek, sickly, and feeble-minded (wiki:Rhaegel_Targaryen).

## Origins

Born 173–178 AC in King's Landing (track_b: Born). Valyrian race, Crownlands culture (track_b: Race, Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Considered sweet and gentle but touched by madness. Meek, sickly, and feeble-minded to the point that some believed him as good as dead. Rumored to have danced naked through the halls of the Red Keep. Lacked martial prowess (wiki:Rhaegel_Targaryen).

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiance).

## Narrative Arc

Rhaegel learned to fight from Ser Quentyn Ball, the master-at-arms of the Red Keep, but lacked martial prowess. He married Lady Alys Arryn and had three children: twins Aelor and Aelora and daughter Daenora. He and his brother Aerys seldom left court, leading their father Daeron II to pass Summerhall to youngest brother Maekar.

When Aerys ascended the Iron Throne in 209 AC, Rhaegel became Prince of Dragonstone as heir. Despite being heir, he was not considered for the Handship — Maekar quarreled bitterly, expecting it himself. Some mistook Maekar as the true heir, forgetting Rhaegel and his children. Those who mistrusted Bloodraven as Hand considered Rhaegel impotent to oppose him. Rhaegel died in 215 AC (wiki:Rhaegel_Targaryen.cite_ref-mystery_knight).
