## Identity

Ser Tion Lannister was a knight of House Lannister, the second son of Lord Gerold Lannister and Lady Rohanne Webber, and the twin brother of Tywald (wiki:Tion_Lannister).

## Narrative Arc

Maester Yandel refers to Tion and Tywald as Gerold's "glorious twins" (wiki:Tion_Lannister). Tion became heir to Casterly Rock after his twin Tywald died in the Peake Uprising in 233 AC. According to semi-canon sources, Tion was squire to Prince Aegon Targaryen during the conflict (wiki:Tion_Lannister).

Tion had been betrothed to a daughter of Lord Rowan, but after Tywald's death, Tywald's betrothed Ellyn Reyne convinced Tion to espouse her instead. Lord Gerold opposed the match but eventually gave way. Tion and Ellyn married at Casterly Rock in 235 AC, in the same ceremony where his brother Tytos married Jeyne Marbrand (wiki:Tion_Lannister).

In 236 AC, during the Fourth Blackfyre Rebellion, Tion was killed at the Battle of Wendwater Bridge. His widow Ellyn became influential as the future Lady of Casterly Rock (wiki:Tion_Lannister).
