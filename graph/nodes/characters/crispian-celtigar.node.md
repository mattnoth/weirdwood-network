## Origins

The Celtigars were closely allied with [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) and [House Velaryon](wiki:House_Velaryon) of [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark), families of [Valyrian](wiki:Valyrian) descent. Crispian was a vassal of the Targaryens and joined his strength to Lord [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) when he sought to [conquer](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest) the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Crispian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

After Aegon was crowned as [King of All Westeros](wiki:King_of_All_Westeros), he established the first [small council](wiki:Small_council) and Crispian was named the first [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin).(wiki:Crispian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Crispian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) In that office, he advised Aegon on matters relating to taxation, debts, and incomes.(wiki:Crispian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
