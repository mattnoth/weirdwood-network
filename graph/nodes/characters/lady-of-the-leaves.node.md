## Appearances & Description

The Lady of the Leaves is an old, thin white-haired woman. She wears roughspun.(wiki:Lady_of_the_Leaves.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

The Lady of the Leaves is known to the [maester](wiki:Maester) of [Lymond Lychester](wiki:Lymond_Lychester), who directs the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) to meet with her in order to get information on the whereabouts of Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion).(wiki:Lady_of_the_Leaves.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

When the brotherhood finds the Lady of the Leaves, she tells them she heard that Beric was killed by [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane). She rejoices when they tell her that Beric is still alive.(wiki:Lady_of_the_Leaves.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) The woman tells the outlaws that once winter comes, her people will have to abandon their hidden village as they will become otherwise too exposed. [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) thinks the lady sounds sick.(wiki:Lady_of_the_Leaves.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)
