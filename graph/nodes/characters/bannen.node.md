## Appearances & Description

Bannen is originally from the lands of [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) near [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor).(wiki:Bannen.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) He is a good tracker.(wiki:Bannen.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Bannen is part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) lead by Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont). He is a scout under [Jarman Buckwell](wiki:Jarman_Buckwell)'s command. He participates in the scouting mission around the [Milkwater](wiki:Milkwater) led by [Thoren Smallwood](wiki:Thoren_Smallwood). Bannen and [Kedge Whiteye](wiki:Kedge_Whiteye) observe [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder)'s host.(wiki:Bannen.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Due to his great tracking skills, Bannen is one of those chosen to die by the conspirators who intend to kill Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont).(wiki:Bannen.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) [Chett](wiki:Chett) plans to have [Lark](wiki:Lark) and his cousins murder Bannen. However, their plans fail when [wights](wiki:Wights) attack the Night's Watch, and Bannen's foot is wounded during the ensuing [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist). Kedge Whiteye later tries to save Bannen by chopping off his mangled foot, but the scout dies from the wound at [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep). His body is burned at sunset.(wiki:Bannen.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
