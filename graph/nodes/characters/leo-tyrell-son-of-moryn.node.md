## Identity

Leo Tyrell, known as Lazy Leo, is a member of House Tyrell and a first cousin of Mace Tyrell, Lord of Highgarden. He is the second son of Ser Moryn Tyrell, the commander of Oldtown's City Watch. Leo is a novice at the Citadel (wiki:Leo_Tyrell_son_of_Moryn.cite_ref-Raffc0).

## Appearances & Description

Leo has sly hazel eyes and ash-blond hair that falls over one eye — coloring that stands out from the traditional brown eyes and brown hair of the Tyrells. He is handsome, with pale skin and a soft, sly voice. He dresses in satin striped in the Tyrell colors of green and gold, with a black silk half cape pinned by a jade rose. The arrogant and malicious Leo often gives mocking nicknames to acquaintances and speaks unpleasantly of Dornishmen and Summer Islanders. He is disliked by his fellow students (wiki:Leo_Tyrell_son_of_Moryn.cite_ref-Raffc0).
