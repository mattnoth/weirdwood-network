## Origins

Leo was acquainted with [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) during their childhood. Sam does not have fond memories of their interactions.(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn).cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

At some point in their time at the Citadel, Leo nicknamed [Alleras](wiki:Alleras) "the Sphinx", as he was "a bit of this, a bit of that", like a [sphinx](wiki:Sphinx) is made of multiple creatures. Their fellow students picked up on the nickname,(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn).cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) and Alleras himself even uses it.(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn).cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4) However, Leo's nickname of "Hopfrog" for [Mollander](wiki:Mollander) (for his clubfoot) is detested.(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn).cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

## Appearances & Description

Leo has sly hazel eyes and ash-blond hair that falls over one eye,(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn).cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn).cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) coloring that stands out from the traditional brown eyes and brown hair of the [Tyrells](wiki:House_Tyrell). He is handsome, with pale skin.(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn).cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Leo has a soft, sly voice.(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn).cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Leo dresses in satin striped in the Tyrell colors of green and [gold](wiki:Gold). He has worn a black [silk](wiki:Silk) half cape, pinned to his shoulder by a [jade](wiki:Jade) rose,(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn).cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) and a half cape striped in green and gold.(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn).cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

The arrogant and malicious Leo often gives mocking nicknames to his acquaintances, and speaks unpleasantly of [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) and [Summer Islanders](wiki:Summer_Isles). He is disliked by his fellow students. Leo sometimes gambles at the [Checkered Hazard](wiki:Checkered_Hazard), to the point of losing all his money and having to beg for [drinks](wiki:Alcoholic_beverages) from acquaintances. He has been trained to arms, and is known to be deadly with a [bravo](wiki:Bravo)'s blade and dagger.(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn).cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Leo had been confined to the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) for some unspecified offense, but leaves three days early to gamble and drink in the inns of [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). He arrives at the [Quill and Tankard](wiki:Quill_and_Tankard), where [Alleras](wiki:Alleras), [Roone](wiki:Roone), [Armen](wiki:Armen), [Mollander](wiki:Mollander), and [Pate](wiki:Pate_(novice)) have been celebrating Alleras's new [link](wiki:Maesters) and talking about [dragons](wiki:Dragon). Leo confirms that the rumors of the sailors are true, that [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) is alive and has hatched three living [dragons](wiki:Dragon). He also reveals that there is a [glass candle](wiki:Glass_candle) burning in the chambers of [Archmaester](wiki:Archmaester) [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn). As the others leave the inn, he threatens to take the [virginity](wiki:Virgin) of [Rosey](wiki:Rosey), the girl that Pate has fallen for, but Alleras reassures Pate that if Leo cannot afford to buy [wine](wiki:Wine), he cannot afford a [gold](wiki:Gold) [dragon](wiki:Currency) to pay Rosey's [mother](wiki:Emma).(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn).cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

[Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), newly arrived at the Citadel, recognizes Leo outside the chambers of Marwyn at the [Ravenry](wiki:Ravenry). Leo is staring intently into a candle flame, and when Alleras asks him if he is searching for his destiny or his death, Leo blinks and replies, "Naked women." Sam is intimidated by Leo, remembering him from their youth, but nevertheless tells him of his deeds in the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) and that he is now called "[Sam the Slayer](wiki:A_Storm_of_Swords-Chapter_18)". Leo laughs, but has no chance to reply as Sam enters Marwyn's chambers.(wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(son_of_Moryn).cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5)
