## Origins

Ashara was a [lady-in-waiting](wiki:Lady-in-waiting) to Princess [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell), the wife of Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen). She had not been long at court by the false spring of [281 AC](wiki:281_AC).(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) She was present at the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) that year, where she danced with a knight of the Kingsguard, Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell), and Lord [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington). After [Brandon Stark](wiki:Brandon_Stark) approached her on behalf of his younger brother [Eddard](wiki:Eddard_Stark), Ashara danced with Eddard as well.(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) According to [Allyria Dayne](wiki:Allyria_Dayne), Ashara's younger sister, Ashara and Eddard Stark fell in love at Harrenhal.(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) of the Kingsguard was also in love with Ashara, although he never told her so. Barristan recalls that, had he won the tourney, he would have named Ashara his [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty).(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4) According to Barristan, Ashara was "dishonored" by a man at Harrenhal.(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5) In addition, Barristan states that Ashara "looked" at "Stark" following the final tilts at the tourney, although he does not identify the Stark in question.(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-6)

Ashara's brother [Arthur](wiki:Arthur_Dayne) died [the war](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), fighting during the [combat at the tower of joy](wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy). Eddard traveled to [Starfall](wiki:Starfall) to return Arthur's greatsword [Dawn](wiki:Dawn) to [House Dayne](wiki:House_Dayne).(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Some time after his visit, Ashara jumped from the top of the Palestone Sword, one of the towers of Starfall, into the sea.(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Several characters have cited possible reasons for Ashara's decision to commit suicide: a broken heart,(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) because of her stillborn daughter,(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-7) because of a stolen child,(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) for the "man who had dishonored her at Harrenhal",(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-8) or due to grief over Arthur's death.(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3) Her body was never found.(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-9)

At [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), Eddard Stark's soldiers spread the tale that Ashara was the mother of Eddard's [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow). When Eddard's wife, [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), heard these stories from her maids, she confronted Eddard, who refused to confirm or deny anything regarding Jon's mother, and subsequently silenced all talk of Ashara at Winterfell. Her name was never mentioned at the castle again.(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Ashara Dayne](wiki:Category:Images_of_Ashara_Dayne)*

Ashara was a young, beautiful, tall and fair [maiden](wiki:Maiden), with haunting [violet eyes](wiki:Violet_eyes).(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Her long dark hair tumbled around her shoulders.(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) According to Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s eyes resemble Ashara's eyes.(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) recalls confronting her husband [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) years before about the whispers she had heard about Ashara being [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow)'s mother.(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

While the Stark confronts Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) about the real parentage of King [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_Baratheon)'s three legitimate children, Cersei is offended by Eddard’s honorable persona and accuses the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) of many possible debaucheries, such as pushing Lady Ashara to suicide, by stealing Jon Snow from her or killing her brother [Arthur](wiki:Arthur_Dayne).(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

### A Storm of Swords

When [Meera Reed](wiki:Meera_Reed) tells [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) the story of the [Knight of the Laughing Tree](wiki:Knight_of_the_Laughing_Tree) and the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) in [281 AC](wiki:281_AC), and refers to Ashara as "a maid with laughing [purple eyes](wiki:Purple_eyes)".(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

[Edric Dayne](wiki:Edric_Dayne) tells [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) about Ashara and Eddard.(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-4)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) recalls the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal), and regrets losing the final tilt to Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), as this prevented him from crowning Ashara as the [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty).(wiki:Ashara_Dayne.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-9)
