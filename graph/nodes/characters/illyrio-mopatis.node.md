## Identity

Illyrio Mopatis is a Magister of the Free City of Pentos, a wealthy and powerful merchant who conspires to restore House Targaryen to the Iron Throne (track_b: Title, Allegiance).

## Origins

Born in Pentos (track_b: Born). Of Pentoshi culture (track_b: Culture).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiance).

## Appearances & Description

An enormously fat man with a forked yellow beard, fingers dripping with rings (agot-daenerys-01, agot-daenerys-02). Known derisively as "the cheesemonger" and "the fat man" by those aware of his schemes (track_b: aliases).

## Narrative Arc

Illyrio hosts Viserys and Daenerys Targaryen in his manse in Pentos for nearly half a year, arranging the marriage of Daenerys to Khal Drogo in exchange for a Dothraki army (agot-daenerys-01). He provides the dragon eggs as a wedding gift (agot-daenerys-02). He conspires with Varys in the dungeons of the Red Keep to advance their plans, overheard by Arya Stark (agot-arya-04). He sends a warning letter about Robert's assassination bounty on Daenerys via Ser Jorah (agot-daenerys-06). Later, he hosts Tyrion Lannister and sends him east toward Daenerys, while also supporting Young Griff's claim. He provides ships, supplies, and the Golden Company's contract for the Targaryen restoration. He was married twice: first to a cousin of the Prince of Pentos, and second to Serra, whom he loved deeply (track_b: Spouses).
