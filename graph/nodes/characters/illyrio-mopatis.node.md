## Origins

As a young man, Illyrio was a poor [bravo](wiki:Bravos), living by his blade. After the young [Varys](wiki:Varys) arrived in [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) they formed an unlikely friendship and worked together and became rich. Illyrio grew respectable and married the [maiden daughter](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis's_first_wife) of a cousin of the [Prince of Pentos](wiki:Prince_of_Pentos);(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4) only with the help of Varys did he reach a position where the daughter of a magister was willing to marry him.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-9)

After his first wife died, Illyrio remarried at least once, wedding [Serra](wiki:Serra), a [Lysene](wiki:Lys) [pillow house](wiki:Pillow_house) worker. This infuriated the Prince of Pentos, who barred the palace to Illyrio forever after. Illyrio was happy with Serra until the *[Treasure](wiki:Treasure)*, a [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) trading galley carrying the [grey death](wiki:Grey_death), anchored in Pentos. Through the rats the plague left the ship and killed two thousand in the city, among them Serra. Illyrio keeps a locket with Serra's picture and her hands in his bedchamber as a reminder of his lost love.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5)

Illyrio has kept in close contact with Varys in the decades since he left to serve in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-4)(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-12)(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-6) Illyrio was also acquainted with [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington), [Lord of Griffin's Roost](wiki:Lord_of_Griffin's_Roost), who was exiled by King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) and reportedly drank himself to death(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) in [Lys](wiki:Lys).(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Illyrio has kept an eye on the remaining [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen), [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) and [Daenerys](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), for years whilst they lived in exile in the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities).(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Rawoiafillyrio_mopatis.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) Eventually, he took them into [his manse](wiki:Illyrio's_manse), keeping them under his protection.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-16) He was smitten at first by the beauty of Daenerys and thought of eliminating Viserys and claiming the girl as a wife, but he dismissed this idea soon as he believed Daenerys too timid and frightened and that coupling with her would give him no pleasure. Illyrio claims he protected Viserys and Daenerys on the grounds that Viserys promised to make him the [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin) upon his ascension to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-7) Illyrio found [Doreah](wiki:Doreah) in a [pleasure house](wiki:Pleasure_house) in Lys,(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) and he has allowed Viserys to bed her.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Category:Images_of_Illyrio_Mopatis)*

Illyrio was once a muscular and handsome [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword), slender and tall.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Rawoiafillyrio_mopatis.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) A statue of his youthful self carved by [Pytho Malanon](wiki:Pytho_Malanon) graces the court in [his manse](wiki:Illyrio's_manse) in [Pentos](wiki:Pentos). It depicts a naked boy, no older than sixteen, standing in the water of a [marble](wiki:Marble) pool, poised to duel with a bravo's blade shimmering like [steel](wiki:Steel). The statue is lithe and handsome with straight blond hair brushing its shoulders.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

After having gained significant wealth and power, however, Illyrio has grown morbidly obese.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) He has pig's eyes and fat cheeks. He has a huge white belly and a pair of heavy breasts that sag like sacks of suet covered with coarse yellow hair. When he laughs his flesh bounces vigorously.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Illyrio's forked yellow beard,(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) which he tends to stroke,(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) is oiled so it gleams like [gold](wiki:Gold).(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6)(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) Although he uses heavy perfumes, Illyrio's flesh can still be smelled by bystanders.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7) He has crooked yellow teeth.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8) Despite his bulk, the magister can walk lightly,(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) a remnant of having been a [bravo](wiki:Bravo) as a youth. Illyrio often eats and drinks,(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) and he takes frequent breaks to empty his bladder.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

An amiable man who speaks with a honeyed voice,(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-9) Illyrio has friends throughout the [known world](wiki:Known_world), including the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities), [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak), and the [Jade Sea](wiki:Jade_Sea).(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-10) He is close to Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys),(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8) the [master of whisperers](wiki:Master_of_whisperers) to King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon),(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9) and is fond of [Young Griff](wiki:Young_Griff).(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), however, distrusts the magister.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-11) Illyrio speaks the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue) with an accent of the Free Cities.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) He can speak the [Dothraki language](wiki:Dothraki_language) as well,(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-12) and is familiar with [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) customs.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

Although [slavery](wiki:Slavery) is outlawed in Pentos according to its treaty with [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), Illyrio's servants are slaves in all but name. [His favorite](wiki:Girl_bought_to_please_the_king) is a fair-haired, blue-eyed sixteen-year-old woman.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-13)

Illyrio owns some of the orchards, farms, and mines in the [Flatlands](wiki:Flatlands), although he rarely visits them.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) Gemstone rings owned by Illyrio include [amethyst](wiki:Amethyst), black [diamond](wiki:Diamond), [emerald](wiki:Emerald), green [pearl](wiki:Pearl), [jade](wiki:Jade), [jet](wiki:Jet), [onyx](wiki:Onyx), [opal](wiki:Opal), [ruby](wiki:Ruby), [sapphire](wiki:Sapphire), slitted yellow tiger's eye, and [tourmaline](wiki:Tourmaline).(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3)(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10) His bedrobe is large enough to serve as a tourney pavilion.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-11) Illyrio's [palanquin](wiki:Palanquin) is elaborately carved and is carried by a dozen strong men.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-14) The magister keeps a [panther](wiki:Panther) in his menagerie.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-15)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Illyrio has been hosting the exiles [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) and [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) at [his manse](wiki:Illyrio's_manse) in [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) for almost half a year. He hopes to be rewarded if Viserys is able to reclaim the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) for [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen), and he encourages Viserys's ambitions by telling him of [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) people awaiting the return of their rightful king.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-17)

Illyrio brokers an arranged marriage between Daenerys and Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), so that Viserys can use Drogo's powerful *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* as an invasion force. Before introducing the girl to the *[khal](wiki:Khal)*, Illyrio attires Daenerys with a plum silken gown, a tiara, gilded sandals, [golden](wiki:Gold) bracelets encrust with [amethysts](wiki:Amethyst), and a golden collar decorated with [Valyrian](wiki:Valyria) glyphs.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-18)

The magister provides fine [wines](wiki:Wine) for [the wedding feast](wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen), where he gives Daenerys three petrified [dragon eggs](wiki:Dragon_egg) as wedding gifts.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) Viserys gives his sister three servants—[Doreah](wiki:Doreah), [Irri](wiki:Irri), and [Jhiqui](wiki:Jhiqui)—although Daenerys realizes the women were actually provided by Illyrio.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

While exploring the lower levels of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) overhears Illyrio and Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) scheming on behalf of Daenerys against King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), although she does not recognize them. Arya tells her father, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), but the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) thinks his daughter simply overheard a pair of [mummers](wiki:Mummers) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney).(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-5)

Magister Illyrio encourages Viserys to remain with him in Pentos, but the would-be king insists on accompanying Drogo and Daenerys when they depart the coastal city.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) Viserys ends up killed in [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak), the holy city of the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki).(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

Illyrio sends a letter warning that King Robert is offering rewards for the deaths of the remaining Targaryens, as Daenerys is pregnant with Drogo's child.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Daenerys hatches the eggs after the deaths of Drogo and [Rhaego](wiki:Rhaego), with three [dragons](wiki:Dragon) emerging from Drogo's funeral pyre.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont), Daenerys's [Queensguard](wiki:Queensguard),(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) distrusts Illyrio.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

Illyrio continues to look after Daenerys from afar, sending her [Strong Belwas](wiki:Belwas) and his squire, [Arstan Whitebeard](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), along with three ships commanded by [Groleo](wiki:Groleo)(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) to take her back to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos), along with her young dragons, [Drogon](wiki:Drogon), [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal), and [Viserion](wiki:Viserion).(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Aboard the *[Balerion](wiki:Balerion_(ship))*, Jorah and Daenerys discuss Illyrio. The knight counsels that Daenerys return to Pentos and Illyrio in her own time, and not alone. He advises her to use the valuable cargo of the *Balerion*, *[Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar_(ship))* and *[Meraxes](wiki:Meraxes_(ship))* to help purchase slaves, but Daenerys tells him that the cargo belongs to Illyrio. Jorah argues that if Illyrio would deny her then he is only [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos) with four chins. Daenerys heeds his counsel and sails to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay) where she can buy [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied).(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1) Arstan advises Daenerys to trust Illyrio.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

One of Illyrio's ships, the *[Bountiful Harvest](wiki:Bountiful_Harvest)*, is seized by [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan), who has been named [Lord of Blackwater Bay](wiki:Lord_of_Blackwater_Bay).(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

After [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) is convicted of treason in [the poisoning](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), Varys arranges to smuggle him out of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) and across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) into Illyrio's care in Pentos. The cheesemonger takes Tyrion into his manse, although the exile is wary of possibly being [poisoned](wiki:Poison) by [mushrooms](wiki:Mushroom_(fungi)).(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-13) Illyrio offers Tyrion a way to become [Lord of Casterly Rock](wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock). He reveals the truth about Daenerys to Tyrion, who reluctantly agrees to travel to her. Illyrio also reveals that his interest in supporting the Targaryens is to become [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin) for the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-14)(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-8)

Tyrion and the magister travel in a litter and eventually meet with some of Illyrio's other cohorts.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-9) Illyrio greets [Haldon](wiki:Haldon) and [Rolly Duckfield](wiki:Rolly_Duckfield) and has Tyrion join them to continue the voyage to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis). Illyrio bids them a pleasant journey and returns to Pentos and his manse.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Haldon and Rolly bring Tyrion and six chests from Illyrio to [Ghoyan Drohe](wiki:Ghoyan_Drohe), where they meet "Griff" and the crew of the *[Shy Maid](wiki:Shy_Maid)*.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) Griff, actually the exiled [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington), says that Illyrio has paid [Nyessos Vhassar](wiki:Nyessos_Vhassar), a [triarch of Volantis](wiki:Triarch_of_Volantis) from the [elephants](wiki:Elephants_(Volantis)), enough to own him eight times over.(wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis.cite_ref-Radwd18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
