## Appearances & Description

A fat mummer, Izembaro only wants to portray kings in his plays lately. Izembaro gives his troupe advice as to how to please the Gate's crowd during their performances. These “wisdoms” include "You have to please the pit" and "Always give them something they haven't seen before".(wiki:Izembaro.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Bmercy_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Cmercy.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

The [kindly man](wiki:Kindly_man), one of the [Faceless Men](wiki:Faceless_Men), announces to [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) she is to be sent for her first apprenticeship to Izembaro to continue her training.(wiki:Izembaro.cite_ref-Radwd64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Izembaro and his group of mummers portray a new play written by [Phario Forel](wiki:Phario_Forel), named *[The Bloody Hand](wiki:The_Bloody_Hand)*. Izembaro will play the role of King [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_Baratheon) in this play. He loses his crown an hour before the show starts, and after Arya has retrieved it for him from the privy where he had left it, he loses his boar spear.(wiki:Izembaro.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Bmercy_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Cmercy.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
