## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser Patrek loses [his lands](wiki:King's_Mountain) in southern [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) when King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) sails for the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Patrek_of_King's_Mountain.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) When Stannis marches for the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), Ser Patrek remains at [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea) with Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent). Patrek accompanies her to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), is prepared to fight [Wun Weg Wun Dar Wun](wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun), and escorts Selyse to the [King's Tower](wiki:King's_Tower).(wiki:Patrek_of_King's_Mountain.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) He commands the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) protecting Selyse at the castle.(wiki:Patrek_of_King's_Mountain.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Thinking he would have made a better husband for [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark) than [Sigorn](wiki:Sigorn), Patrek is angry during [their wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark). He is hostile toward [Satin](wiki:Satin), and has become smitten with [Val](wiki:Val).(wiki:Patrek_of_King's_Mountain.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)(wiki:Patrek_of_King's_Mountain.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

When Selyse decides that Patrek should marry Val, [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), explains that [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) must be stolen, showing the man's strength.(wiki:Patrek_of_King's_Mountain.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Patrek is later slain at [Hardin's Tower](wiki:Hardin's_Tower), where Val is staying, killed by Wun Wun. After tearing off the knight's sword arm, the [giant](wiki:Giants) smashes Patrek's body against the tower, ruining the knight's head. Jon attempts to calm Wun Wun, but the Lord Commander is then stabbed in the [mutiny at Castle Black](wiki:Mutiny_at_Castle_Black).(wiki:Patrek_of_King's_Mountain.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
