## Origins

Merion was made [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) at the age of sixty-six and sent to serve the court of King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to succeed Grand Maester [Ellendor](wiki:Ellendor). He died on his way to King's Landing and was succeeded by Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle).(wiki:Merion.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Merion's work [The Red Dragon and the Black](wiki:The_Red_Dragon_and_the_Black) was left unfinished.(wiki:Merion.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Merion once referred to the custom of [Lys](wiki:Lys), [Myr](wiki:Myr), and [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) to grant licenses on ships to prey upon those of their foes as "[piracy](wiki:Piracy) with a [wax](wiki:Wax) seal".(wiki:Merion.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_quarrelsome_daughters:_myr.2C_lys.2C_and_tyrosh.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
