## Identity

Sansa Stark is a historical member of House Stark, born between 140 and 161 AC, who died in 209 AC (track_b: Born, Died). She is the daughter of Rickon Stark and Jeyne Manderly (track_b: Father, Mother). She possibly held title at Winterfell (track_b: Title, qualifier: possibly).
