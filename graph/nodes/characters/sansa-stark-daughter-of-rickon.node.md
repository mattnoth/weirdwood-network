## Identity

Sansa Stark (daughter of Rickon) — child of Rickon Stark (son of Cregan) and Jeyne Manderly; married to Jonnel Stark; sworn to House Stark. One of 2 characters named Sansa Stark.

## Origins

After the death of her father [Rickon](wiki:Rickon_Stark_(son_of_Cregan)) in the [Conquest of Dorne](wiki:Conquest_of_Dorne), Sansa was set to inherit Winterfell after her grandfather, Lord Cregan. However, the [lordship of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell) instead passed to Cregan's second son, [Jonnel Stark](wiki:Jonnel_Stark), Sansa's father's half-brother. Jonnel also married Sansa.(wiki:Sansa_Stark_(daughter_of_Rickon).cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_stark_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) The north lamented the death of Rickon, for the reigns of Jonnel and his brothers after him were troublesome.(wiki:Sansa_Stark_(daughter_of_Rickon).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_lords_of_winterfell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Sansa had no issue with Jonnel. After her death, Lord Jonnel married again to [Robyn Ryswell](wiki:Robyn_Ryswell).(wiki:Sansa_Stark_(daughter_of_Rickon).cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_stark_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)(wiki:Sansa_Stark_(daughter_of_Rickon).cite_ref-wives_7-0) It is unknown if Sansa was still alive when her husband succeeded Lord Cregan.
