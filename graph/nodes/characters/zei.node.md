## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Zei flees [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town) for [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) at the [Wall](wiki:Wall) when the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) [attack the castle](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) from the south. She helps defend the castle, proving to be efficient with a crossbow.(wiki:Zei.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) When the attack from the south is over, she stays at Castle Black to help defend the Wall from the wildling army [besieging the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall) in an attempt to break through the gates.(wiki:Zei.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) sends Zei to Mole's Town to request help from the villagers who still remain. When she does not return, Jon sends [Mully](wiki:Mully) after her. Mully reports that he found the town deserted, and that Zei was gone as well.(wiki:Zei.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
