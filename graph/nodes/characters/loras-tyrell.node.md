## Identity

Ser Loras Tyrell, also known as the Knight of Flowers, is a knight from House Tyrell and the third son of Lord Mace Tyrell of Highgarden and Lady Alerie Hightower. He is a highly skilled knight and jouster whose tournament successes, dazzling good looks, and ostentatious showmanship have made him a celebrated figure in the Seven Kingdoms. His personal arms are three golden roses on green, indicating his position as a third son (wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot18).

## Origins

Born 282 AC at Highgarden (track_b: Born). Son of Mace Tyrell and Alerie Hightower (track_b: Father, Mother). He has two older brothers, Willas and Ser Garlan, and one younger sister, Margaery. Loras squired for Lord Renly Baratheon at Storm's End and grew close to him (wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot18).

## Appearances & Description

Loras is exceptionally handsome with a mass of lazy brown curls tumbling over his eyes, which are described variously as brown, liquid gold, big, intelligent, and lively. Tyrion thinks him beautiful and already a legend — half the girls in the Seven Kingdoms want to bed him and all the boys want to be him. Sansa considers him lithe and graceful. Despite his youth and slender frame, Loras is a capable warrior using axes, lances, morningstars, and swords. His silver armor, decorated with sapphires and twining black vines, was made by Tobho Mott. He rides a speedy grey mare and a tall white stallion (wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot18, wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot29).

## Narrative Arc

In AGOT, Loras participates in the Hand's tourney, giving a red rose to Sansa Stark. He rides a mare in heat against Gregor Clegane's stallion, causing the Mountain to be unhorsed and attack him — Sandor Clegane intervenes. Loras forfeits the final to the Hound. He secretly plots with Renly to have Robert set aside Cersei for Margaery. After Robert's death, Loras flees King's Landing with Renly (agot-eddard-07, agot-eddard-14).

In ACOK, Loras serves as Lord Commander of Renly's Rainbow Guard. After Renly's assassination by a shadow, a grief-maddened Loras kills three of Renly's guards including Emmon Cuy and Robar Royce. He later joins the Lannister-Tyrell alliance and fights at the Battle of the Blackwater (wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok22).

In ASOS, Loras is appointed to Joffrey's Kingsguard. He has a confrontation with Jaime Lannister about their respective reputations. He attends the Purple Wedding (wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6).

In AFFC, Loras volunteers to lead the assault on Dragonstone to free the Redwyne fleet. He reportedly takes the castle but is said to be grievously wounded — drenched in boiling oil with a mace wound to his ribs and a manticore helm through his shoulder (wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc32).

In ADWD, reports of Loras's condition conflict — whether he truly lies dying at Dragonstone remains unclear (wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Radwd54).
