## Origins

Loras was born at [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden)(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_loras_tyrell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) in [282 AC](wiki:282_AC).(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-cal_6-1) He has two older brothers, [Willas](wiki:Willas_Tyrell) and Ser [Garlan](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell), and one younger sister, [Margaery](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).

Loras squired for Lord [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End),(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) and grew close to him. According to a semi-canon source, he soon became Renly's lover.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_loras_tyrell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) At the young age of fifteen, Loras received his [knighthood](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rawoiafloras_tyrell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

Loras purchased his armor from [Tobho Mott](wiki:Tobho_Mott)'s shop in the [Street of Steel](wiki:Street_of_Steel) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-1) During the [tourney on Prince Joffrey's name day](wiki:Tourney_on_Prince_Joffrey's_name_day) in [298 AC](wiki:298_AC), at the age of fifteen, Loras defeated Ser [Hosteen Frey](wiki:Hosteen_Frey)(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) and Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) in the joust.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) Renly's brother, King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), won a [Valyrian steel dagger](wiki:Valyrian_steel_dagger) from Loras's victory.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Loras Tyrell](wiki:Category:Images_of_Loras_Tyrell)*

Loras is exceptionally handsome.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) His hair is a mass of lazy brown curls(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) and ringlets(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) which tumble over his eyes,(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) which have been described as brown,(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) liquid gold,(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) big,(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) and intelligent and lively.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2) [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) thinks Loras is beautiful and already a legend, and that half the girls in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) want to bed him and all the boys want to be him.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) also thinks Loras is beautiful, and that he is lithe and graceful with wonderful eyes.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

Loras is beloved by the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk), especially women. While courteous he is still thirsty for glory and can be short-tempered and impetuous. Tyrion finds him a prickly lad.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) According to a semi-canon source, Loras is homosexual,(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-16) and his father, [Mace](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), and grandmother, [Olenna](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell), are aware of his sexuality.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-17) Loras is Mace's favorite son.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

Despite his youth and slender frame, Loras is a capable warrior, using axes, lances, morningstars, and swords to deadly effect.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-3)(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) According to [George R. R. Martin](wiki:George_R._R._Martin), a case can be made that Loras is the greatest active swordsman in Westeros.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-20) When asked about the strongest fighters, he again mentions Loras.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-21) Loras's silver armor, which is decorated with [sapphires](wiki:Sapphire) and twining black vines,(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) was made by [Tobho Mott](wiki:Tobho_Mott).(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) He also uses silver mail and a green shield.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-4) Golden [roses](wiki:Rose) decorate the crest of his helm.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-5) Loras wears a cloak of forget-me-nots sewn to a heavy woolen cape.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1) His horses include a speedy grey mare(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-2) and a tall white stallion.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-6)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Loras participates in the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney), wearing armor wrought with jeweled flowers and a cape of woven [roses](wiki:Rose). Amongst his victories are Ser [Robar Royce](wiki:Robar_Royce) and three members of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). With each victory, he hands a white rose to a pretty girl in the crowd. He then hands a red rose to [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), who is overwhelmed by his gallantry and is thereafter infatuated with him.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) Although reputed to be gallant, Loras rides a mare in heat against Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane), noted as an underhanded tactic by some. This inflames the huge knight's stallion, which bucks him, prompting Gregor to attack Loras. After [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) comes to his rescue, Loras forfeits the final round to the [Hound](wiki:Hound).(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

Lord [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) and Loras secretly plot to have Renly's eldest brother, King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), spurn Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) in favor of Loras's sister, [Margaery](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) Renly approaches Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) with a painting of the girl in hopes that she resembles Eddard's late sister, [Lyanna](wiki:Lyanna_Stark), but this is not the case.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-2)

When Eddard, sitting the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) in Robert's stead as the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), sends a party led by Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) to capture or kill Gregor Clegane, Loras is disappointed at not having been chosen instead of Beric.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

Loras is mentioned by Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) as a possible ally for Lord Stark against Cersei in the succession crisis after Robert's death, but Ned decides it is the [City Watch](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing) he must have. When Renly flees [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) after Lord Stark spurns his request for aid in seizing power, Loras rides south out of the city with him, along with fifty retainers. Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) suspects they ride for either [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) or [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden).(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0) After [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) is crowned in the power struggle they fled from, both Loras and Renly are named among the nobles he demands must swear fealty to him or stand accused of treason.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

Renly [weds](wiki:Wedding_of_Renly_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) Margaery at Highgarden and claims the throne with the support of Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) and his sons.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Ser Loras's father, Lord Mace Tyrell, serves as Hand to Renly Baratheon.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Their army gathers at Highgarden, and Renly makes Loras the head of his [Rainbow Guard](wiki:Rainbow_Guard), an ostentatious version of the traditional Kingsguard. The two remain inseparable even after Renly's [wedding](wiki:Wedding). During his march, Renly holds a great [melee at Bitterbridge](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge) of one hundred sixteen knights. Wielding a longaxe, Loras is one of the final two combatants and Renly cheers for him, but he loses to [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth), who is given the last place in his Rainbow Guard.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-7)

After his failed parley with his elder brother, [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), at [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Renly appoints Loras to lead the vanguard in the coming battle, choosing him over Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly), Ser [Guyard Morrigen](wiki:Guyard_Morrigen), and many other candidates.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0) When the bannermen leave Renly's pavilion, the king asks Loras to stay and help him pray.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0) Later, after Renly's death at the hands of a [shadow assassin](wiki:Shadow_assassin),(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-1) Loras goes berserk, killing Ser [Emmon Cuy](wiki:Emmon_Cuy) and Ser [Robar Royce](wiki:Robar_Royce), the two Rainbow Guards who were outside the king's tent when he was slain, as well as another guard.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0) Loras later admits to [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) that he concluded that since they had done nothing to stop Renly's death, and let the culprit escape, they must have aided the murder willingly.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-2)

With Renly dead, King Joffrey's [small council](wiki:Small_council) expects Loras to return to Margaery at [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge). Lord Petyr Baelish is dispatched to Bitterbridge to seek an alliance.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-1) Stannis sends Ser [Parmen Crane](wiki:Parmen_Crane) and Ser [Erren Florent](wiki:Erren_Florent) to recruit Renly's infantry at Bitterbridge,(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0) but they are [captured](wiki:Fighting_at_Bitterbridge) by the [Tyrells](wiki:House_Tyrell).(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)

Petyr travels to Bitterbridge and then Highgarden, where he negotiates an alliance between the Tyrells and [Baratheons](wiki:House_Baratheon_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0) Meanwhile, riders from Bitterbridge inform Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), who rendezvouses with Mace's army.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0) Loras participates in the defeat of Stannis by the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) and Tyrells in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0) Afterward, Joffrey agrees to wed Renly's widow, Margaery, and Loras is granted a place in the Kingsguard.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Loras joins the Kingsguard at the behest of his father, Lord Mace, to protect his sister, Margaery, when she becomes queen.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0) The appointment also allows Loras to avoid marriage, a prospect complicated by his ambiguous sexuality, and his continued mourning for Renly. Loras quickly becomes popular with the citizens of King's Landing. When Tyrion asks him why he chose to join the Kingsguard at only seventeen, Loras explains that he is a third son and that "When the [sun has set](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), no candle can replace it".(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2)

Sansa Stark thinks Loras looks even finer in the white of the Kingsguard than in the greens and golds of House Tyrell. The only spot of color on him now is a brooch that clasps his cloak, the rose of Highgarden wrought in soft yellow gold, nestled in a bed of delicate green jade leaves. The grip of his sword is white leather and its pommel is a rose in alabaster.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) During her wedding celebrations with [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), Sansa is asked to dance by Loras's brother, Ser [Garlan](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell). Although Sansa is unhappy to marry the Imp, Garlan tells her Tyrion would be a better husband than Loras.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0)

Loras attends the wedding of King Joffrey and his sister, Margaery Tyrell. After Joffrey dies from [poison](wiki:Poison) during his [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding), Queen Cersei Lannister orders the Kingsguard to arrest Tyrion.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0)

When Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), absentee [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard), returns to the King's Landing, Loras recognizes Brienne of Tarth among his companions and confronts her immediately about Renly's mysterious death. At his request, Jaime has Brienne arrested.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0) During the Kingsguard meeting in the [White Sword Tower](wiki:White_Sword_Tower), Loras blames Sansa, who has disappeared from the capital, for Joffrey's poisoning.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-3) Loras reveals to Jaime that his brother, Garlan the Gallant, wore Renly's famous armor and impersonated the fallen king at the Battle of the Blackwater, since Renly's armor was too large to fit Loras.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-4) Loras also reveals he buried Renly in a place they knew from when he was a [squire](wiki:Squire) at Storm's End. Loras agrees to question and judge Brienne fairly.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-5) Loras confronts Brienne and becomes more accepting of her innocence in the death of Renly.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0) Loras laughs during the trial of Tyrion.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-0)

At the [Fingers](wiki:Drearfort), Petyr Baelish tells Sansa that during his negotiations with the Tyrells at Highgarden, he planted the idea of Loras joining the Kingsguard.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Ser Loras stands vigil with Ser Jaime over the corpse of Lord Tywin Lannister.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-0) Jaime later notices Loras skillfully training and tries to educate the youth about historical members of the Kingsguard.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-1) Cersei objects to Loras's offer to train young King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_I_Baratheon) in the ways of an armed knight.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1) When Jaime departs to bring order to the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), he wants Loras to command in his stead in King's Landing, which upsets Cersei.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_48-0) King Tommen is impressed with Loras's actions and demeanor, and the knight spends much of his time with his sister, Queen Margaery.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_49-0)

After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields) by the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), Cersei refuses to allow the [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet) to depart the [siege of Dragonstone](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone) and deal with [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North). Loras volunteers to take command and hasten the siege.(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_50-0) Three thousand [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk), mostly women, see Loras off when he sails to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)) aboard the [new dromonds](wiki:Cersei's_dromonds) of the [grand admiral](wiki:Grand_admiral), Lord [Aurane Waters](wiki:Aurane_Waters).(wiki:Loras_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_51-0)

Aurane tells Cersei that Loras has taken Dragonstone.
