## Identity

Areo Hotah is the captain of the household guard at Sunspear for Prince Doran Martell. He is originally from the Free City of Norvos, where he was trained by the bearded priests (wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc2). He is a POV character in AFFC and ADWD.

## Origins

Born in or before 257 AC in Norvos (track_b: Born). He was the youngest of six children, with two brothers and three sisters. Sold to the bearded priests at a young age, he completed his training at sixteen and was wedded to his longaxe, branded on his chest (wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc2).

## Appearances & Description

Broad-shouldered with white hair that was once dark, and a seamed and scarred face (wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc2, cite_ref-Raffc40). Speaks the Common Tongue with a gruff, deep voice and thick Norvoshi accent. Wears a shirt of copper scales with a billowing cloak of dun-and-yellow sandsilk and an iron halfhelm wrapped in orange silk. His longaxe has a shaft of mountain ash six feet long — he considers it his "ash-and-iron wife" (wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Radwd38).

## Narrative Arc

Areo came to Dorne as guardsman to Lady Mellario's family and was present when she first met Doran Martell. He entered House Martell's service and watched all of Doran and Mellario's children grow up, developing a fatherly affection for Princess Arianne (wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc2). After Oberyn Martell's death, Areo arrested the Sand Snakes on Doran's orders. He was sent to end Arianne's Queenmaker plot at the Greenblood, where he killed Ser Arys Oakheart with his longaxe (wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc21). In ADWD, he attended the feast where Ser Balon Swann presented Gregor Clegane's skull. He was present when Doran revealed his plans to Arianne and the Sand Snakes (wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Radwd38).
