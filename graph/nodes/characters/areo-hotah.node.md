## Origins

Areo was born in [Norvos](wiki:Norvos)(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Rawoiafareo_hotah.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) as the youngest of six children, with two brothers and three sisters, and he was sold to the [bearded priests](wiki:Bearded_priests) at a young age. When he was sixteen years old, Areo completed his [training](wiki:Holy_guard). He was wedded to his axe and branded on his chest to signify the event.(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)

Areo became a guardsman to the family of Lady [Mellario](wiki:Mellario), and was present the day she first met her future husband, [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne).(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Rawoiafareo_hotah.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) He escorted Mellario when she traveled with Doran to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), having learned [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) speech. Upon arrival in Dorne, Areo found it a strange place; the Dornish women were lewd, [Dornish wine](wiki:Dornish_red) was sour, and Dornish food was full of queer hot [spices](wiki:Spices). He also thought the Dornish [sun](wiki:Sun) was hotter than the pale, wan sun of Norvos.(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Areo entered the service of [House Martell](wiki:House_Martell), and Mellario married Doran.(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8)

Areo watched all of Doran and Mellario's children grow up, and developed a father-like love for Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell), whom he refers to as "little princess."(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-9)(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) Despite this affection, however, he is utterly loyal to Prince Doran.(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-10)(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Areo eventually became the captain of Prince Doran's guard, and remained in Dorne to serve the Martell's even after Mellario returned to Norvos. Even after all his years in Dorne, Areo still reminds himself of the vows he took when he was sixteen, *"Serve. Obey. Protect."*(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-11)

When Doran Martell moved from [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) to the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens) due to his deteriorating health, Areo accompanied him. On occasion, Areo is sent to Sunspear by Doran.(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-12)

## Appearances & Description

Areo is broad-shouldered with white hair which was once dark,(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) and he has a seamed and scarred face.(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Areo speaks the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue) with a gruff, deep voice and thick [Norvoshi](wiki:Norvos) accent.(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) The captain is loyal, obedient, and confident in his martial abilities,(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) and he has a bellowing voice when commanding [his guardsmen](wiki:House_Martell_guards).(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Areo usually stations himself to the left of [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), and he carries the prince gently when needed.(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

In [Norvos](wiki:Norvos), Areo wore a heavy [horsehair](wiki:Horse) cape, a studded leather tunic, and an [iron](wiki:Iron) halfhelm with a crest of sharpened spikes. When he arrived in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), he quickly realized he had to change his clothing to acclimatize to the Dornish [sun](wiki:Sun). Nowadays, he wears a shirt of [copper](wiki:Copper) scales with a billowing cloak of dun-and-yellow sandsilk to keep the sun off the copper. The sun beating down on the metal of his Norvoshi halfhelm caused him headaches, so he wrapped it in orange silk.(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) Areo's [longaxe](wiki:Armament) has a shaft of mountain ash six feet long and is kept in a sling across his back,(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6) and he considers it his ash-and-[iron](wiki:Iron) wife.(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Areo is an old friend and protector of Doran's daughter and heir, Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell).(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Areo is a [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) serving as [captain of guards](wiki:House_Martell_guards) for [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne).(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Areo is at the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens) with Doran. Following the death of Doran's younger brother, Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell), Areo accompanies Doran when the latter returns to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear). He is ordered by Doran to arrest Oberyn's [bastard](wiki:Bastard) daughters, the [Sand Snakes](wiki:Sand_Snakes).(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-13)

Areo is sent by Doran to put an end to [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell)'s plot to steal Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) away with the intention of crowning her [Queen of the Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Queen_of_the_Seven_Kingdoms). During the confrontation at the [Greenblood](wiki:Greenblood), Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), Myrcella's [sworn shield](wiki:Sworn_shield), charges Areo and his guards. After Arys's horse is brought down by crossbowmen, Areo chops off the knight's right arm at the shoulder and beheads him with his longaxe.(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Areo orders his men to pursue Ser [Gerold Dayne](wiki:Gerold_Dayne), who has wounded Myrcella. Darkstar escapes, however, which angers Areo.(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

Areo escorts Arianne back to Sunspear to await an audience with Doran.(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6) After she has spent several weeks in confinement in [Spear Tower](wiki:Spear_Tower), it is Areo who retrieves Arianne and brings her to her father.(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7)

### A Dance with Dragons

Areo is present at the feast at Sunspear in which Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) presents Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell) with [the skull](wiki:Mountain's_skull) of Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane). Areo notes that Balon looks to be a more formidable warrior than Ser [Arys](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) had been, and wonders if it will come to a fight between them as well.(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

Areo is present when Prince Doran discusses his plans in a private [solar](wiki:Solar) with [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) and [Obara](wiki:Obara_Sand), [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria_Sand) and [Tyene Sand](wiki:Tyene_Sand). They decide to tell Balon that Arys was killed by Darkstar.(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4)

### The Winds of Winter

Areo joins Obara and Balon on their mission to hunt and kill the rogue knight Darkstar.(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5)(wiki:Areo_Hotah.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Barianne_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Carianne_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
