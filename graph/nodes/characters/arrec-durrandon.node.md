## Origins

Arrec was the last Storm King to rule the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), an area conquered by his ancestor [Arlan III Durrandon](wiki:Arlan_III_Durrandon) some three hundred years earlier. [Harwyn Hardhand](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), used the mobility of [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) longships to defeat the stormlanders in the riverlands. Arrec's greatest defeat was in the [Battle at Fairmarket](wiki:Battle_at_Fairmarket). Later in life, Arrec tried twice to cross the [Blackwater](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) and take back the lost lands, but failed.(wiki:Arrec_Durrandon.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Arrec_Durrandon.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Arrec was succeeded by his eldest son, [Arlan V](wiki:Arlan_V_Durrandon).(wiki:Arrec_Durrandon.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
