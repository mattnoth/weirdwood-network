## Origins

In [23 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) proposed to betrothed her son, Prince [Maegor](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), to her new born great niece, Princess [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)). The High Septon strongly objected the idea, however. Instead, he suggested his own niece, Lady [Ceryse Hightower](wiki:Ceryse_Hightower), the maiden daughter of Lord [Manfred Hightower](wiki:Manfred_Hightower_(son_of_Addam)) of [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown).
