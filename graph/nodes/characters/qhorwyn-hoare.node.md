## Origins

Qhorwyn was a shrewd [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands) who avoided war and instead gathered wealth during his reign. He tripled the size of [House Hoare](wiki:House_Hoare)'s fleets and ordered the creation of additional [armament](wiki:Armament) to dissuade enemies from attacking.(wiki:Qhorwyn_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Qhorwyn's eldest son died of [greyscale](wiki:Greyscale). When his third son, [Harwyn](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), returned to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) from adventuring two years later, Qhorwyn was dying. The king's middle son, Prince [Harlan Hoare](wiki:Harlan_Hoare), died after falling from a horse while Qhorwyn was lying ill. Though it was said to be an accident, Harlan's death was considered suspicious by some, especially when King Qhorwyn passed six days later.(wiki:Qhorwyn_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Harwyn succeeded Qhorwyn as king, and the Hardhand soon used his father's strengthened military to conquer the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) from [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon) of the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands)(wiki:Qhorwyn_Hoare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) and become the first [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers).(wiki:Qhorwyn_Hoare.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
