## Identity

Victarion Greyjoy is a member of House Greyjoy, younger brother of Balon Greyjoy, Lord of the Iron Islands. He is the Lord Captain of the Iron Fleet, and his flagship is the Iron Victory. He is a POV character in A Feast for Crows and A Dance with Dragons. (wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy)

## Origins

## Appearances & Description

Victarion is a large and powerful man with a bull's broad chest, a boy's flat stomach, and huge hands. His hair is flecked with grey. He is less handsome than his brother Euron. (wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18) He is a devoted follower of the Drowned God. He wears heavy grey chain mail over boiled black leather, and in combat adds heavy plate with iron gauntlets of lobstered steel. His tall black warhelm is decorated with an iron kraken whose tentacles coil below his jaw. He prefers his fearsome steel axe with a shield. His cloak is nine layers of gold cloth sewn like the kraken of his House. (wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy)

## Narrative Arc

Victarion, Balon, and Euron convinced their father Quellon to join Robert's Rebellion; Quellon was killed at the Mander. Victarion's first wife died in childbirth and his second of pox. He commanded the Iron Fleet during Greyjoy's Rebellion, burning the Lannister fleet at Lannisport, but was later smashed by Stannis Baratheon at Fair Isle. He took a third wife (a salt wife) who was seduced or raped by Euron; Victarion beat her to death and Balon exiled Euron. (wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc18)

During the War of the Five Kings, Balon sent Victarion with the Iron Fleet up the Saltspear and Fever River; Moat Cailin fell to his surprise assault. (wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Racok49) After Balon's death, Victarion returned to contest the kingsmoot on Old Wyk but lost to Euron, who promised dragons. (wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc19)

Victarion led the Taking of the Shields, defeating Ser Talbert Serry in single combat but wounding his left hand. Euron then tasked Victarion with sailing to Slaver's Bay to retrieve Daenerys Targaryen as a bride for Euron. Victarion agreed but secretly planned to court Daenerys himself. (wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc29)

Sailing east, Victarion's fleet captured prizes and split into three squadrons. He rescued the red priest Moqorro from the sea; Moqorro healed Victarion's infected hand with fire magic, leaving it charred but functional. Victarion converted to worship both the Drowned God and R'hllor. As his fleet approached Meereen, he prepared for battle with the slavers besieging the city. (wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Radwd56, cite_ref-Radwd63)
