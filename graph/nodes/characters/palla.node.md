## Identity

Palla is a kennel girl at Winterfell, the daughter of Farlen the kennelmaster (wiki:Palla).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Palla plays lord of the crossing with the Frey wards and other castle children (wiki:Palla.cite_ref-Racok4). When Theon Greyjoy captures Winterfell, Palla is raped by Drennan and another ironman. Bran sees her enter the Great Hall in pain, her dress ripped, supported by her limping father Farlen. Theon has the two rapists whipped (wiki:Palla.cite_ref-Racok46). After Bran and Rickon escape, Theon forces Farlen and his dogs to join the search by threatening Palla's safety. Theon later uses Farlen as a scapegoat and executes him (wiki:Palla.cite_ref-Racok50). Ramsay Snow, disguised as "Reek," demands Palla as his reward for returning with Bolton reinforcements. Theon refuses, but Ramsay takes Kyra instead (wiki:Palla.cite_ref-Racok66). After the sack, Palla is among the survivors taken to the Dreadfort (wiki:Palla).
