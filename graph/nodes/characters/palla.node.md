## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Palla plays [lord of the crossing](wiki:Lord_of_the_crossing) with [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark)'s [wards](wiki:Ward), [Big Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Jammos)) and [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)), along with [Turnip](wiki:Turnip), [Bandy](wiki:Bandy), [Shyra](wiki:Shyra), [Calon](wiki:Calon), and [TomToo](wiki:TomToo). During the game, they all get soaked and muddy; Palla gets mud all over herself, and moss in her hair, but laughs so hard she loses her breath.(wiki:Palla.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

When Winterfell is [captured](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) by [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) raiders led by [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy), Palla is raped by [Drennan](wiki:Drennan) and another ironman. Theon has the people of Winterfell brought to the [Great Hall](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell) so they can hear [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) yield the castle. Bran sees Palla enter the room, walking in pain with a ripped dress she struggles to hold together, supported by her limping father, [Farlen](wiki:Farlen).(wiki:Palla.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Theon has the two rapists whipped for raping Palla.(wiki:Palla.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

After Bran and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) escape from the castle, Theon forces Farlen and his [dogs](wiki:Dog) to accompany his search party by threatening Palla's safety.(wiki:Palla.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) After three of Theon's men who had been part of the search party die mysteriously, Theon uses Farlen as a scapegoat and executes him.(wiki:Palla.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

"[Reek](wiki:Ramsay_Bolton)" offers to help Theon by taking a large sum of money to the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort) and returning with much-needed reinforcement, to which Theon reluctantly agrees. When Theon asks Reek what he wants as his reward, Reek names Palla. Thinking that he cannot back out of the deal now, Theon says if Reek returns with two hundred men as promised, then Palla is his.(wiki:Palla.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Reek returns with [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton)'s [garrison](wiki:House_Bolton_guards) and [destroys the army attempting to retake Winterfell](wiki:Battle_outside_the_gates_of_Winterfell), then reveals himself to Theon as actually [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son of Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton). Theon tells [Harrag](wiki:Harrag) to bring Palla, but Ramsay replies that he would rather have Theon's bedwarmer [Kyra](wiki:Kyra). When Theon protests, Ramsay strikes him with his armored fist, shattering Theon's cheekbone and knocking him to the ground, and begins the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Palla.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Storm of Swords

The sack of Winterfell is blamed on Theon's ironborn, who allegedly killed many of Winterfell's people. Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) is heartsick at the thought of the deaths of so many, including Palla, but she is told that Ramsay Snow took the women and children to the safety of the Dreadfort.(wiki:Palla.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Palla remains among the captives held at the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort).(wiki:Palla.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
