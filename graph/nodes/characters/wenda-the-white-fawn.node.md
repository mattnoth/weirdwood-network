## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

While riding with [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) wonders if she should go back to [Sharna](wiki:Sharna)'s inn, where she could stay with [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie) or rejoin the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners). She briefly thinks of becoming an outlaw, like Wenda the White Fawn in the songs, but then thinks the idea is stupid.(wiki:Wenda_the_White_Fawn.cite_ref-Rasos65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

On his way to pay the ransom for [Petyr Frey](wiki:Petyr_Frey), [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey) recalls that Wenda burned a fawn into the cheek of his arse while he was her captive among the [Kingswood Brotherhood](wiki:Kingswood_Brotherhood).(wiki:Wenda_the_White_Fawn.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

During [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark)'s wedding celebrations, [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) thinks that [Ulmer of the Kingswood](wiki:Ulmer_of_the_Kingswood), an adept dancer, is regaling his partners with his tales of the Kingswood Brotherhood, when he rode with [Simon Toyne](wiki:Simon_Toyne) and [Big Belly Ben](wiki:Big_Belly_Ben) and helped Wenda burn her mark in the buttocks of her highborn captives.(wiki:Wenda_the_White_Fawn.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
