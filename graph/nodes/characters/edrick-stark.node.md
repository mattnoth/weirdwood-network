## Origins

Edrick ruled the north for almost a hundred years.(wiki:Edrick_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) His reign was marked by quarrels between his descendants, as well as external threats from the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), [wildlings](wiki:Free_folk), and the Starks' ancient rivals, [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton). The outer walls of Winterfell were thus raised during Edrick's time.(wiki:Edrick_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_winterfell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Slavers from the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) captured the [Wolf's Den](wiki:Wolf's_Den) once Edrick grew feeble.(wiki:Edrick_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He is buried in the [crypts beneath Winterfell](wiki:Crypts_beneath_Winterfell).(wiki:Edrick_Stark.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
