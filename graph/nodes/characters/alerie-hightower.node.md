## Appearances & Description

Alerie is tall and handsome, with long silvery hair which she braids with jeweled rings.(wiki:Alerie_Hightower.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Alerie_Hightower.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) She is younger than her husband, Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell).(wiki:Alerie_Hightower.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) thinks she always has a pinched expression.(wiki:Alerie_Hightower.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) finds Alerie to be dignified,(wiki:Alerie_Hightower.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) but [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) thinks her vapid.(wiki:Alerie_Hightower.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Lady Alerie is part of the retinue of her daughter, [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), when they travel to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Alerie_Hightower.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Lady Tyrell sedately dances with her husband, Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) during [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark) of [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark).(wiki:Alerie_Hightower.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Alerie proudly attends [the wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_I_Baratheon) and Margaery. When Joffrey chokes to death during the feast, Alerie comforts her daughter by explaining that the king choked on pie. Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), however, insists that her son was [poisoned](wiki:Poison) and accuses her brother, Tyrion.(wiki:Alerie_Hightower.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Lady Alerie attends the funeral of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) with her family.(wiki:Alerie_Hightower.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

Alerie echoes her good-mother, Lady [Olenna Tyrell](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell), that King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_I_Baratheon) should share a bed with Margaery after they are wed; Cersei acquiesces to the couple spending only their wedding night together, however.(wiki:Alerie_Hightower.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Alerie makes the arrangements for [the modest wedding feast](wiki:Wedding_of_Tommen_I_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) held in the [Small Hall](wiki:Small_Hall).(wiki:Alerie_Hightower.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

After the wedding, Alerie leaves King's Landing for the Reach with Olenna and her second son, Ser [Garlan](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell).(wiki:Alerie_Hightower.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
