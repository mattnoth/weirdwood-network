## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

At the start of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Lord Willum declares for [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon). He and his two sons are part of Renly's army camped beneath the walls of [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge).(wiki:Lord_Willum.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

After the death of Renly, Lord Willum goes over to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon). He and his sons fight for Stannis at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), but are captured, and later bend the knee to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord_Willum.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
