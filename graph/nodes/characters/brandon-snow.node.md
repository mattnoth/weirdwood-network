## Identity

Brandon Snow was the bastard half-brother of Torrhen Stark, King in the North (wiki:Brandon_Snow).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

During Aegon I Targaryen's conquest, when Torrhen Stark marched south with thirty thousand northmen to meet Aegon at the Trident, Brandon offered to sneak into the Targaryen camp alone at night to assassinate the dragons while they slept. Torrhen instead sent Brandon and three maesters to negotiate, and the next day knelt and swore fealty (wiki:Brandon_Snow).

In ADWD, Bran Stark has a vision through Winterfell's heart tree of a pale, fierce, dark-eyed young man who slices three branches off the weirwood and shapes them into arrows. It has been theorized this may be Brandon Snow preparing arrows to kill Aegon's three dragons (Tier 3 — community theory, not confirmed in text) (wiki:Brandon_Snow.cite_ref-Radwd34).
