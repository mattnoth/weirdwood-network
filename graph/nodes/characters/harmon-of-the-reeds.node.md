## Origins

During the [Storming of the Dragonpit](wiki:Storming_of_the_Dragonpit), Ser Harmon was one of the [Seven Who Rode](wiki:Seven_Who_Rode), men sent by Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) to recover her son, Prince [Joffrey Velaryon](wiki:Joffrey_Velaryon), as he tried to save the [dragons](wiki:Dragon). Joffrey was killed after falling off [Syrax](wiki:Syrax), however, and a mob tore apart his body. Iron-Banger survived the battle with [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) over the prince's remains.(wiki:Harmon_of_the_Reeds.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
Harmon and twenty other knights loyal to Rhaenyra were executed by Ser [Perkin the Flea](wiki:Perkin_the_Flea) after the city gates were opened by Ser [Garth the Harelip](wiki:Garth_the_Harelip).(wiki:Harmon_of_the_Reeds.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
