## Origins

Following the [Faith Militant](wiki:Faith_Militant)'s defeats in [42 AC](wiki:42_AC) in the battles at [Stonebridge](wiki:Battle_of_Stonebridge) and the [Great Fork of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_at_the_Great_Fork), new leaders arose across the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) in [43 AC](wiki:43_AC). While the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons) were all [knights](wiki:Knight), the lower order of [Poor Fellows](wiki:Poor_Fellows) contained male and female members. Poxy Jeyne led the Poor Fellows in the forested lands between [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) and [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), making travel impassable. The forested terrain made Jeyne's cell more difficult to fight than others, and the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood) was in close proximity to King's Landing.(wiki:Jeyne_Poore.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

When King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) launched a new military campaign against the Faith Militant late in the year [45 AC](wiki:45_AC), he targeted Jeyne's Poor Fellows. Scores of them were hunted down, and any taken alive were hanged if they refused to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Jeyne_Poore.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

Although Jeyne eluded Maegor's forces for some time, she was eventually betrayed by three followers who received pardons and knighthoods for turning her over to the king. Three [septons](wiki:Septon) with Maegor declared Jeyne to be a [witch](wiki:Witch), and the king ordered her to be burned in a field by the [Wendwater](wiki:Wendwater). On the day of [Jeyne's execution](wiki:Burning_of_Jeyne_Poore), three hundred Poor Fellows and peasants under the leadership of Ser [Horys Hill](wiki:Horys_Hill) attempted to rescue her. Jeyne's public execution was a trap to lure out her remaining followers, however, with Maegor's forces waiting and ready for them in an ambush. The rescuers were surrounded and slaughtered.(wiki:Jeyne_Poore.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

After a year of campaigning, Maegor and his army returned to King's Landing in [46 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) in triumph, along with two thousand skulls which he questionably claimed to all have been Faith Militant. Other leaders of the Faith Militant had meanwhile regrown their strength, however. Septon [Moon](wiki:Moon_(septon)) openly preached against Maegor the Cruel to huge crowds in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) and the [Reach](wiki:Reach), and Ser [Joffrey Doggett](wiki:Joffrey_Doggett) had become the ruler in all but name of the hill country north of the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth) in the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands). [Dennis the Lame](wiki:Dennis_the_Lame) and [Ragged Silas](wiki:Ragged_Silas) continued their efforts as well.(wiki:Jeyne_Poore.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)
