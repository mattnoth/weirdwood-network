## Origins

According to legend, he was born of the last of the [Fisher Queens](wiki:Fisher_Queens) and took to wife the daughters of the greatest lords and kings of the [Gipps](wiki:Gipps), the [Cymmeri](wiki:Cymmeri), and the [Zoqora](wiki:Zoqora). His Zoqora wife drove his [chariot](wiki:Chariot), and his Cymmeri wife made his armor.
