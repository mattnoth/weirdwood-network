## Origins

Elinda Massey was among the four [ladies-in-waiting](wiki:Lady-in-waiting) who accompanied Rhaenyra during her [flight](wiki:Flight_to_Dragonstone) from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). Supposedly she gouged out her own eyes at the sight of her queen being devoured by the [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [Sunfyre](wiki:Sunfyre).(wiki:Elinda_Massey.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
After Rhaenyra's death, Elinda and the three other ladies-in-waiting were put in cells in [Sea Dragon Tower](wiki:Sea_Dragon_Tower) to await ransom.(wiki:Elinda_Massey.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
