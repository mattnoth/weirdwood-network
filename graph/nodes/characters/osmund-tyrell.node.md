## Origins

Lords [Peake](wiki:House_Peake) and [Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) instigated a [civil war](wiki:Anarchy_in_the_Reach) to see their respective wives, both daughters to the senile old King [Garth X Gardener](wiki:Garth_X_Gardener), succeed as Queen of the Reach. Ser Osmund led an alliance of twoscore houses to put down the rebels and reclaim the ruins of [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden), as a [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) king and his raiders had sacked the seat of [House Gardener](wiki:House_Gardener) and killed Garth X.

Ser Osmund placed a second cousin of the late Garth X upon the throne as King [Mern VI Gardener](wiki:Mern_VI_Gardener). With the counsel of Osmund, Mern VI began the reconstruction of the Reach and Highgarden and the power of House Gardener. Osmund was succeeded by his son, Ser [Robert Tyrell](wiki:Robert_Tyrell).(wiki:Osmund_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
