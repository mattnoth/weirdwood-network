## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Jate serves with the force gathered to besiege [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun). After Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) escapes Riverrun, Jate is sent north under the command of Ser [Dermot of the Rainwood](wiki:Dermot) to search for him. While Jate is on picket duty, the camp is attacked by [wolves](wiki:Wolves).
