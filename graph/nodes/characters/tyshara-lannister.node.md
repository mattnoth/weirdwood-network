## Origins

Tyshara was a daughter of Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister) and his wife, Lady [Johanna Westerling](wiki:Johanna_Lannister).(wiki:Tyshara_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) She had four sisters, including [Cerelle](wiki:Cerelle_Lannister_(daughter_of_Jason)),(wiki:Tyshara_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) and one brother, Lord [Loreon](wiki:Loreon_Lannister_(son_of_Jason)).(wiki:Tyshara_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

After the death of Queen [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Lady Johanna set aside her war with the [ironmen](wiki:Ironmen) long enough to write to Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), that her daughters Tyshara and Cerelle were [maidens](wiki:Maiden) of noble birth and marriageable age.(wiki:Tyshara_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Not long after the [Maiden's Day Ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball) was announced, Tyshara was found abed with a groom. Tyshara claimed that the groom had climbed through her window, but [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Munkun](wiki:Munkun)'s examination found her maidenhead to be broken. However, it is still likely that the groom was planted, as it was heavily rumored that the King's Hand set out to ruin various maidens to promote his [own daughter](wiki:Myrielle_Peake) as Aegon III's second queen.(wiki:Tyshara_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) Moreover, it is quite common for highborn ladies to lose their maidenheads while [horseback](wiki:Horse) riding.(wiki:Tyshara_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
