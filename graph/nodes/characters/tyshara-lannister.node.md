## Identity

Tyshara Lannister was a member of House Lannister during the reign of King Aegon III Targaryen (wiki:Tyshara_Lannister).

## Origins

Born between 103–121 AC (track_b: Born). Daughter of Jason Lannister and Johanna Westerling (track_b: Father, Mother). She had four sisters including Cerelle and one brother, Lord Loreon (wiki:Tyshara_Lannister).

## Allegiances

Tyshara is `SWORN_TO` House Lannister (track_b: Allegiance). Her culture is Westermen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

After the death of Queen Jaehaera Targaryen in 133 AC, Lady Johanna wrote to the Hand Lord Unwin Peake that her daughters Tyshara and Cerelle were maidens of noble birth and marriageable age (wiki:Tyshara_Lannister.cite_ref-FB). Shortly after the Maiden's Day Ball was announced, Tyshara was found abed with a groom. She claimed the groom had climbed through her window, but Grand Maester Munkun's examination found her maidenhead broken. It is likely the groom was planted, as Lord Unwin Peake was rumored to have set out to ruin various maidens to promote his own daughter as Aegon III's second queen (wiki:Tyshara_Lannister.cite_ref-FB).
