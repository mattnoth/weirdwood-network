### A Dance with Dragons

Arron and his brother Emrick swear their vows with four other recruits ([Horse](wiki:Hareth_(Mole's_Town)), [Satin](wiki:Satin), [Leathers](wiki:Leathers), and [Jax](wiki:Jax)) at the grove of [weirwoods](wiki:Weirwood) north of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), inside the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest). They are escorted by Jon, now [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), along with ten rangers. Jon, [Iron Emmett](wiki:Emmett), and [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) weigh each recruit and assign them to an order. Arron is assigned to the [stewards](wiki:Stewards).(wiki:Arron.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
