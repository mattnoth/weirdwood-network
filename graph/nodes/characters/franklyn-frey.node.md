## Origins

According to a a semi-canon source, Ser Franklyn participated in the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC).(wiki:Franklyn_Frey.cite_ref-3)

As part of the retinue of his brother, [Lord Frey](wiki:Lord_Frey), Franklyn attended the marriage of [his niece](wiki:Lady_Butterwell) to Lord [Ambrose Butterwell](wiki:Ambrose_Butterwell) at [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) in [212 AC](wiki:212_AC). A drunken Franklyn nearly dropped the bride during the [bedding](wiki:Bedding) custom.(wiki:Franklyn_Frey.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

He took part in the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls). On the back of his sorrel, Franklyn defeated Ser [Harbert Paege](wiki:Harbert_Paege) after five [tilts](wiki:Jousting). While riding his dapple grey, however, Franklyn was defeated by Ser [John the Fiddler](wiki:John_the_Fiddler) in his next joust, and the victorious [hedge knight](wiki:Hedge_knight) helped Franklyn to his feet.(wiki:Franklyn_Frey.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) According to a semi-canon source, Franklyn's defeat may have been suspect because of Lord Frey's involvement in the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion),(wiki:Franklyn_Frey.cite_ref-Rawoiaffranklyn_frey.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) which intended for John, actually [Daemon II Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre), to win the competition and claim a [dragon egg](wiki:Dragon_egg).(wiki:Franklyn_Frey.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

When Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) revealed his identity to Lord Butterwell, Lord Frey departed Whitewalls with his retinue to avoid participating in the faltering rebellion.(wiki:Franklyn_Frey.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

## Appearances & Description

Franklyn was considered a good [tourney](wiki:Tourney) fighter.(wiki:Franklyn_Frey.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The knight wore grey silks and silver armor, and has two horses: a sorrel and a dapple grey with a flowing silver mane.(wiki:Franklyn_Frey.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) On shield and surcoat and grey silk horse trappers, he bore the twin towers of [Frey](wiki:House_Frey).(wiki:Franklyn_Frey.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
