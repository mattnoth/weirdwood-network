## Origins

While his father had done much to restore the [Reach](wiki:Reach) and [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) after the [civil war](wiki:Anarchy_in_the_Reach) instigated by [House Peake](wiki:House_Peake) and [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly), and the [Dornish](wiki:Dornishmen) raiding that sacked Highgarden, Garth XI did the rest, taking such a terrible vengeance upon the Dornish that [Lord Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) said the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) had been green until Garth painted them with Dornish blood.(wiki:Garth_XI_Gardener.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf773_1-1)
For the remainder of his long reign, the king was known as Garth the Painter.(wiki:Garth_XI_Gardener.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf773_1-2)
