## Origins

In [42 AC](wiki:42_AC), Ser Willam was stationed at the [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) chapter of the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons), based in the [Sept of Remembrance](wiki:Sept_of_Remembrance). He fought in the [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven) in which seven of the Warrior's Sons faced King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) and his six champions.(wiki:Willam_the_Wanderer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Willam and Ser [Damon Morrigen](wiki:Damon_Morrigen) were the last Warrior's Sons standing against Maegor, whose six champions had all been killed. Badly wounded, the two were killed by Maegor with [Blackfyre](wiki:Blackfyre), the king's [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) sword. As he fell, however, Ser Willam dealt the king a terrible blow to the head, cracking his helm and leaving him insensate. However, this was not enough to kill the king and Maegor was declared the winner.(wiki:Willam_the_Wanderer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
