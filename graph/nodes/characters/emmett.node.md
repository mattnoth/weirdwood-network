## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Iron Emmett](wiki:Category:Images_of_Iron_Emmett)*

Emmett is tall and lanky. He has great endurance and strength and is a terrific swordsman; he is considered the pride of [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea).(wiki:Emmett.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Iron Emmett, the [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms) at [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea),(wiki:Emmett.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) comes to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) and takes part in the election of the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch). While at Castle Black, he trains with [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) and is able to best his future Lord Commander in several training sessions. During one sparring, [Halder](wiki:Halder) and [Horse](wiki:Hareth_(Mole's_Town)) come to Emmett's aid when a distracted Jon does not notice that Emmett has yielded.(wiki:Emmett.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

### A Feast for Crows

Emmett is the new master-at-arms at Castle Black.(wiki:Emmett.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Jon oversees Iron Emmett's training sessions with the new recruits Horse and [Hop-Robin](wiki:Hop-Robin), noting that although [Cotter Pyke](wiki:Cotter_Pyke) was upset at losing Eastwatch's best swordsman, Emmett is a natural teacher who knows how to train men how to fight and loves doing it.(wiki:Emmett.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Emmett is one of the men brought by Jon to escort [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) out of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) to carry out his duties as garrison commander of [Greyguard](wiki:Greyguard). Jon orders Janos to be hanged when he refuses to obey, however, Emmett chokes Janos into submission when he attempts to escape. Emmett kicks Janos to his knees and places his head on the chopping block when Jon orders Janos be beheaded instead.(wiki:Emmett.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Emmett is named master-at-arms at Castle Black in replacement of the late Ser [Endrew Tarth](wiki:Endrew_Tarth).(wiki:Emmett.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) His best trainees are [Arron](wiki:Arron), [Emrick](wiki:Emrick), and [Jace](wiki:Jace_(Night's_Watch)).(wiki:Emmett.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) The three, along with [Jax](wiki:Jax), [Leathers](wiki:Leathers), and [Satin](wiki:Satin), are later admitted to the Watch.(wiki:Emmett.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Jon eventually names Emmett commander of [Long Barrow](wiki:Long_Barrow), where Jon sends the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) [spearwives](wiki:Spearwife).(wiki:Emmett.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) and Leathers is chosen to replace Emmett as master-at-arms.(wiki:Emmett.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Dolorous Edd](wiki:Dolorous_Edd) mentions to Jon that Emmett has begun bedding [Black Maris](wiki:Black_Maris), one of the spearwives.(wiki:Emmett.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
