## Origins

Ser Danwell rode in the [tourney on Prince Joffrey's name day](wiki:Tourney_on_Prince_Joffrey's_name_day) held in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in [298 AC](wiki:298_AC). He was unhorsed by a [hedge knight](wiki:Hedge_knight), which embarrassed his father, Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey).(wiki:Danwell_Frey.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Danwell attends the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with six of his kin—Ser [Hosteen](wiki:Hosteen_Frey), Ser [Jared](wiki:Jared_Frey), Ser [Emmon](wiki:Emmon_Frey), Ser [Theo](wiki:Theo_Frey), Ser [Perwyn](wiki:Perwyn_Frey), and [Martyn Rivers](wiki:Martyn_Rivers).(wiki:Danwell_Frey.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

During the parley with Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) at the [Twins](wiki:Twins), Danwell and Ser [Whalen Frey](wiki:Whalen_Frey) help their father, Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey), to his chair.(wiki:Danwell_Frey.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Clash of Kings

Danwell is a captive of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), together with his brothers Ser [Jared](wiki:Jared_Frey) and Ser [Hosteen](wiki:Hosteen_Frey) and his half-brother [Ronel Rivers](wiki:Ronel_Rivers).(wiki:Danwell_Frey.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Tywin holds captives from the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork) at the [Tower of Dread](wiki:Tower_of_Dread) in [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Danwell_Frey.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) observes the four [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) exercising in the [Flowstone Yard](wiki:Flowstone_Yard) of Harrenhal and then being ransomed by their kin.(wiki:Danwell_Frey.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Danwell is among the Freys who meet Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) when the maimed Kingslayer arrives at Harrenhal with the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions). He urges Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) to ransom Jaime back to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Danwell_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Danwell attends the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) at the Twins. He plays a drinking game with [Marq Piper](wiki:Marq_Piper),(wiki:Danwell_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) who is later held captive by the Freys.(wiki:Danwell_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Danwell travels to [Darry](wiki:Darry), where his niece [Amerei](wiki:Amerei_Frey) lives with her new husband, Lord [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister). Danwell informs Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) that packs of [wolves](wiki:Wolf) attacked his baggage train as they traveled south from the Twins. Danwell thinks it possible that the [Reeds](wiki:House_Reed) gave shelter to Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) and [his outlaws](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners).(wiki:Danwell_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
