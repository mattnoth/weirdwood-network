## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Lord Gulian agrees to meet Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) but refuses to join the cause of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Gulian_Swann.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) During the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Lord Gulian remains in his castle pleading [illness](wiki:Illness).(wiki:Gulian_Swann.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Gulian's eldest son, Ser [Donnel](wiki:Donnel_Swann), supports first [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)(wiki:Gulian_Swann.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and then Stannis.(wiki:Gulian_Swann.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) He is captured during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater) and kneels to [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Gulian_Swann.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Gulian's second son, Ser [Balon](wiki:Balon_Swann), joins Joffrey's [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) after the death of Ser [Preston Greenfield](wiki:Preston_Greenfield).(wiki:Gulian_Swann.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
