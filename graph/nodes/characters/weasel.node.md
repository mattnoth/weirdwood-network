## Appearances & Description

No older than two,(wiki:Weasel.cite_ref-Racok9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Weasel is a young child with matted hair.(wiki:Weasel.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [Lommy Greenhands](wiki:Lommy_Greenhands) gives her the name because he thinks she looks like [the animal](wiki:Weasel_(mammal)).(wiki:Weasel.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Weasel often cries, and she never speaks a word after she is rescued. She is stubborn and resilient, but has a bad habit of eating mud.(wiki:Weasel.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

While [Yoren](wiki:Yoren) and his band of [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) recruits travel north from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), they come across a village and holdfast which have been raided during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). Yoren, [Murch](wiki:Murch), and [Cutjack](wiki:Cutjack) investigate the ruins and emerge with a little girl and a one-armed woman who dies soon after.(wiki:Weasel.cite_ref-Racok9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) hopes that [Shella Whent](wiki:Shella_Whent), [Lady of Harrenhal](wiki:Lady_of_Harrenhal), might be able to care for the child.(wiki:Weasel.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

At an unnamed [Gods Eye town](wiki:Gods_Eye_town), the girl eagerly eats [goose](wiki:Goose) given to her by [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie).(wiki:Weasel.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) The settlement comes under attack by Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch) and his band of [westermen](wiki:Westermen), and Arya and [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) help the traumatized girl through a secret tunnel to escape the [fight at the holdfast](wiki:Fight_at_the_holdfast).(wiki:Weasel.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

The crying girl stops whimpering, so [Lommy Greenhands](wiki:Lommy_Greenhands) starts calling her Weasel because of her appearance.(wiki:Weasel.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) The girl is unafraid of eating bugs, and she kicks Hot Pie.(wiki:Weasel.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) When the youths are discovered by the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men), Weasel hears the soldiers coming and runs off into the woods. She is not found again.(wiki:Weasel.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

While imprisoned and forced to serve at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) by [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister), Arya takes the name "Weasel" for her own.(wiki:Weasel.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Feast for Crows

At the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White) in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), the big eyes and hollow face of the [waif](wiki:Waif) remind Arya of Weasel.(wiki:Weasel.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
