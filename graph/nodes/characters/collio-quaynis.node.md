## Appearances & Description

Collio's [Tyroshi accent](wiki:Tyroshi_language) is thick, making it sometimes hard to understand him when he sings in the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue).(wiki:Collio_Quaynis.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Collio is one of the seven singers chosen to compete in the singers tourney at [the wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and Lady [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Collio_Quaynis.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) He sings "[The Dance of the Dragons](wiki:The_Dance_of_the_Dragons_(song))", "[Bessa the Barmaid](wiki:Bessa_the_Barmaid)", "[The Rains of Castamere](wiki:The_Rains_of_Castamere)", and an unnamed ballad in [High Valyrian](wiki:High_Valyrian) about two lovers dying in the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria).(wiki:Collio_Quaynis.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
