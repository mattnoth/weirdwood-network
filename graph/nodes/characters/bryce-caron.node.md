## Origins

Bryce became the head of [House Caron](wiki:House_Caron) after [his father](wiki:Bryen_Caron), mother, brother, and all his sisters succumbed to a terrible chill in [289 AC](wiki:289_AC). Bryce's only known living relative is his [bastard](wiki:Bastard) half-brother, Ser [Rolland Storm](wiki:Rolland_Storm).(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Appearances & Description

Lord Bryce is a skilled warrior,(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) though he is not as competent ahorse as Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Bryce is a much better swordsman than Ser [Jon Fossoway](wiki:Jon_Fossoway), according to Jon himself.(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

The shield of the [Lord of Nightsong](wiki:Lord_of_Nightsong) depicts a field of [nightingales](wiki:Nightingale).(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose) considers the fame-seeking Bryce to be a pup.(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord Bryce participates in the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) given in the honor of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), but he is easily defeated by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) Eddard sees Bryce's field of [nightingales](wiki:Nightingale) on a shield outside of his tent.(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

Bryce's name is read aloud during King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon)'s first court session, placing him in a list of nobles who are instructed to come to King's Landing to swear fealty to Joffrey or else be named traitors to [the realm](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Clash of Kings

After [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End), declares himself king in opposition to Joffrey, Bryce chooses to support him and is named to Renly's new [Rainbow Guard](wiki:Rainbow_Guard). Lord Caron wears the color orange, which gives him the nickname "Bryce the Orange".(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) He wears a long rainbow-striped cloak.(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3)

Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) recognizes Bryce's standard when she arrives at the [melee at Bitterbridge](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge) as an envoy for her son, [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark). During the feast held at [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge) by Lord [Lorent Caswell](wiki:Lorent_Caswell), Bryce goads his fellow knight of the Rainbow Guard, Ser [Robar Royce](wiki:Robar_Royce), into juggling daggers.(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

When Renly learns that [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) has laid [siege to Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Bryce travels with him to [Shipbreaker Bay](wiki:Shipbreaker_Bay). He is present at the failed parley beneath the walls of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) between Renly and Stannis. Renly gives him command of the left flank in the coming battle between the brothers.(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) However, before the battle can take place, Renly is assassinated by a [shadow](wiki:Shadow_child)(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) sent by [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre).(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-4) Bryce believes Ser [Emmon Cuy](wiki:Emmon_Cuy)'s claim that [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth) murdered Renly.(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-5)

Bryce rallies to Stannis after Renly's death. He offers to fight Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose), castellan of Storm's End, in single combat to win the castle for Stannis, but Stannis refuses the suggestion.(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-6) Melisandre instead sends a shadow to kill Cortnay,(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-7) and Lord [Elwood Meadows](wiki:Elwood_Meadows) quickly surrenders Storm's End to Stannis.(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

Lord Caron tells Stannis that Melisandre should be sent back to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) before the assault on [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), lest it be thought that Stannis owed the crown to her spells.(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Bryce the Orange is killed in single combat by Ser [Philip Foote](wiki:Philip_Foote) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). His lands, titles and incomes are subsequently granted to Philip by the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Bryce's [bastard](wiki:Bastard) half-brother, Ser [Rolland Storm](wiki:Rolland_Storm), is a pretender [Lord of Nightsong](wiki:Lord_of_Nightsong), laying claim to [Nightsong](wiki:Nightsong) under Stannis's banner.(wiki:Bryce_Caron.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
