## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Ormond is slain by forces of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). When [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) is captured by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) and put on trial for crimes committed by his fellow [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) forces, Ormond is among the long list of [rivermen](wiki:Rivermen) victims named by [Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams) whose murders Sandor is to stand trial for.(wiki:Ormond_(knight).cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

When Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) passes through [Pennytree](wiki:Pennytree) on his way through the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) and introduces his party as king's men, the villagers cite Ser Ormond's death at the hands of king's men as one of the reason they are distrustful of him.(wiki:Ormond_(knight).cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
