## Origins

Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) made Nestor Royce [Keeper of the Gates of the Moon](wiki:Keeper_of_the_Gates_of_the_Moon) and [High Steward of the Vale](wiki:High_Steward_of_the_Vale), to rule the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) while Jon served as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7) Nestor served in that position for fourteen years.(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7) Nestor hoped to marry his daughter, [Myranda Royce](wiki:Myranda_Royce), to [Harrold Hardyng](wiki:Harrold_Hardyng). However, Harry's guardian, Lady [Anya Waynwood](wiki:Anya_Waynwood), rejected the offer, though it is uncertain whether she was displeased with Myranda's character or her dowry.(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Nestor was somewhat annoyed that his daughter was widowed so quickly after her marriage.(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Winds_7-0)

## Appearances & Description

Nestor is a massive, barrel-chested man,(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) with a thick neck(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and a bluff face.(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He is balding, and has a greying beard.(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) According to Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn), Nestor has a mole.(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Nestor is a gruff, stern, and suspicious man, proud and prickly.(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) called him a rock, but not as a compliment.(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) Though Nestor is from a lesser branch of House Royce, he is still a formidable lord in his own right.(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) Because he virtually ruled the Vale for so long while [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) was serving as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Nestor feels he is owed for his long years of service.(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5) Nestor is somewhat envious of his senior relatives from the main branch of House Royce, and wants more for his son.(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord Nestor hopes to marry the widowed Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) to become [regent](wiki:Regent) for young Lord [Robert](wiki:Robert_Arryn), but she rejects him. Nestor greets Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) and her companions when they visit the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) en route to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie).(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8) He is present in the Eyrie for [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat).(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Storm of Swords

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), in disguise as Alayne Stone, [bastard](wiki:Bastard) daughter of [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), briefly meets Nestor Royce when Lysa and Petyr's wedding party stays overnight at the Gates of the Moon before ascending to the Eyrie, but he barely notices her.(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-8) Sansa hears that Nestor's frolicsome daughter, [Myranda Royce](wiki:Myranda_Royce), keeps the Gates of the Moon for her father.(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Nestor comes the Eyrie to hear Lord Protector Petyr Baelish and Alayne Stone give witness about the events of Lysa Arryn's murder, and to hear the singer [Marillion](wiki:Marillion)'s confession to the crime.(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-9) In order to secure Nestor's loyalty, Petyr grants him hereditary lordship over the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon), forming [House Royce of the Gates of the Moon](wiki:House_Royce_of_the_Gates_of_the_Moon). The parchment contains the Arryn seal, but was signed by Petyr as Lord Protector since Lysa was murdered before she could sign it, making Nestor's family's advancement dependent on Petyr's status.(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-10)

Lord Nestor closes the Gates of the Moon against the [Lords Declarant](wiki:Lords_Declarant) when they besiege the Eyrie. He later escorts the Lords Declarant up to the Eyrie to meet with Petyr and Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn), and seats himself next to Petyr during the meeting.(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

With the onset of winter, Lord Robert Arryn's court descends from the Eyrie to the Gates of the Moon. Lord Nestor prepares a feast for their arrival, and his daughter Myranda arrives at [Sky](wiki:Sky) to escort Robert and his party to the Gates.(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### The Winds of Winter

Lord Nestor hosts a [tourney](wiki:Tourney) at the Gates of the Moon. The winners of the tourney will become the first members of a new knightly order, the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights). Nestor is happy to show his guests the hunting [tapestries](wiki:Tapestry) that once belonged to King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), gifted to him by Petyr Baelish.(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Winds_7-1)(wiki:Nestor_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
