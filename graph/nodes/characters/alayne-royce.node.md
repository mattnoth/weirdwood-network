## Origins

Alayne was the daughter of Lord [Allard Royce](wiki:Allard_Royce) of [Runestone](wiki:Runestone). She became the favorite of Princess [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)), who cherished her so much that she sometimes took Alayne flying on the back of her [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Dreamfyre](wiki:Dreamfyre).(wiki:Alayne_Royce.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

During Prince [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) and Princess Rhaena's [royal progress](wiki:Royal_progress), Alayne and [Melony Piper](wiki:Melony_Piper) were the only lady companions of the princess who stayed loyal to her after King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) usurped the inheritance of Prince Aegon.(wiki:Alayne_Royce.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Alayne arrived on [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle) at the beginning of [49 AC](wiki:49_AC) for Rhaena's [wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Rhaena_Targaryen_and_Androw_Farman) to [Androw Farman](wiki:Androw_Farman).(wiki:Alayne_Royce.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) She stayed with the princess at [Faircastle](wiki:Faircastle), where she, Rhaena, [Samantha Stokeworth](wiki:Samantha_Stokeworth), and [Elissa Farman](wiki:Elissa_Farman) became nigh inseparable and often rode Dreamfyre together. Ser [Franklyn Farman](wiki:Franklyn_Farman) dubbed the four friends, "the [Four-Headed Beast](wiki:Four-Headed_Beast)".(wiki:Alayne_Royce.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

When Rhaena left Fair Isle upon the ascension of Lord Franklyn, Alayne came with her to her new court upon [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). After Elissa betrayed the princess with her theft of Dreamfyre's [dragon eggs](wiki:Dragon_egg), Alayne observed that the marriage between Rhaena and Androw had become loveless.(wiki:Alayne_Royce.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Alayne died at the end of [54 AC](wiki:54_AC). At first, it was thought she had died of an [illness](wiki:Illness). Later, it was revealed that she had been [poisoned](wiki:Poison) by Androw Farman, who was jealous of Rhaena's friendships with her ladies and weary of their mockery toward him.(wiki:Alayne_Royce.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
