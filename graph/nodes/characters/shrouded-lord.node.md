## Origins

The Shrouded Lord is said to rule the mists around the [Sorrows](wiki:Sorrows) since the time of [Garin](wiki:Garin_(Prince)) and to spread [greyscale](wiki:Greyscale) through the grey kiss, although he does not bestow his kiss lightly according to [Haldon](wiki:Haldon).(wiki:Shrouded_Lord.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
According to some rumors, he is thought to be Prince Garin himself, risen from his watery grave. Others believe that there have been numerous Shrouded Lords, and when one dies another one takes his place; the one currently holding the title is a [corsair](wiki:Corsair) from the [Basilisk Isles](wiki:Basilisk_Isles). [Rolly Duckfield](wiki:Rolly_Duckfield) likes yet another version of the tale, in which the Shrouded Lord was a statue at first, and a grey woman from the fog kissed life to it with lips as cold as ice.(wiki:Shrouded_Lord.cite_ref-Radwd18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) They say the Shrouded Lord will grant a boon to any man who can make him laugh.(wiki:Shrouded_Lord.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) According to Septa [Lemore](wiki:Lemore) the stone men were proud, and the Shrouded Lord was the proudest of all.

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

As the [Shy Maid](wiki:Shy_Maid) and her crew travel on the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne) discussion turns to the Shrouded Lord and the various tales about him. [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) comes to associate the Shrouded Lord with [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), the father he slew, and has bad dreams of both.(wiki:Shrouded_Lord.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
