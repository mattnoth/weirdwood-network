## Origins

Pia was born at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) She was a young girl during the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) arranged by Lord [Walter Whent](wiki:Walter_Whent) in [281 AC](wiki:281_AC).(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Appearances & Description

A pretty, giggling girl, Pia is sweet and simple.(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

The [yielding of Harrenhal](wiki:Yielding_of_Harrenhal) by Lady [Shella Whent](wiki:Shella_Whent) to Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) occurs during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). Pia works at the castle's buttery(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) and sleeps with many of Tywin's [knights](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) When Pia claims to have seen something in the buttery one night, [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie) assumes it is a ghost.(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

On the night of the [fall of Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) notices Pia abed with one of the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) in the buttery's loft.(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) When Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) arrives after the castle's fall, he sets up stocks near the bear pit for women who slept with [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) men-at-arms, including Pia. Stripped and shaved, the women are locked up for any man to use. One morning Arya Stark observes the women being used by three men-at-arms from [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey).(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Pia is sent by [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn) to Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s bedchamber as Jaime recovers in Harrenhal after losing his sword hand. She mentions that as a little girl she witnessed him being raised to the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) during the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal), and that she has long fantasized about him. Nonetheless, Jaime sends her away. Qyburn tells Jaime that the girl is healthy.(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) leads the [capture of Harrenhal](wiki:Capture_of_Harrenhal).(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Feast for Crows

For speaking when he wants silence, Gregor smashes Pia's face with a mailed fist, breaking her nose and knocking out half of her teeth.(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) Pia develops a habit of covering her mouth when she speaks, to hide her broken teeth.(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

When Jaime returns to Harrenhal, the only servants from Lady Whent's time who still remain in the castle are Pia, [Ben Blackthumb](wiki:Ben_Blackthumb), and a cook. The sobbing girl clings to Jaime's leg until Ser [Lyle Crakehall](wiki:Lyle_Crakehall) pulls her off. Jaime tells her that no one will hurt her now, but that only makes her sob louder.(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) He thinks she is still pretty enough as long as she keeps her mouth closed.(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) When one of the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) tries to rape Pia, Jaime commands Ser [Ilyn Payne](wiki:Ilyn_Payne) to take his head off. As they force him to his knees the perplexed man says they had her hundreds of times. When Ilyn presents Pia with his head she smiles through her ruined teeth.(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2)

When the godly Ser [Bonifer Hasty](wiki:Bonifer_Hasty) of the [Holy Hundred](wiki:Holy_Hundred) is named by Jaime as temporary [castellan](wiki:Castellan) of Harrenhal, he refers to Pia as a [whore](wiki:Whore) and wants her gone from the castle. Jaime agrees to take her into his entourage as a washerwoman.(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) Jaime believes she is likely barren, as she has not become pregnant after being with numerous soldiers.(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-3)

Pia rides with Jaime's [squires](wiki:Squire), on a [gelding](wiki:Horse) found for her by [Josmyn Peckledon](wiki:Josmyn_Peckledon); Jaime notices that Peck is interested in her. Upon arriving at [Darry](wiki:Darry), Jaime bathes in a tub, and is uncomfortably aware of Pia. After dismissing her Jaime asks Peck if he wants her and advises the youth to be kind if he [beds](wiki:Bedding) her. Jaime suggests they use the empty bed of [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister), [Lord of Darry](wiki:Lord_of_Darry).(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-4)

By the time they reach the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Peck and Pia are regularly having sex. Jaime often goes to sleep to the sound of them in a corner of the tent.(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) When Jaime meets with his cousin, Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister), Pia mulls [wine](wiki:Wine) in a kettle.(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) Pia is present in the tent when Jaime delivers his terms to Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully), and Jaime has her find clean clothing for the captive.(wiki:Pia.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
