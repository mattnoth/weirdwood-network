### A Feast for Crows

Masha's nephew, the father of Jeyne and [Willow](wiki:Willow_Heddle) per a semi-canon source,(wiki:Jeyne_Heddle.cite_ref-Rawoiafjeyne_heddle.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) re-opens the inn, bringing in [whores](wiki:Whore) to attract customers.(wiki:Jeyne_Heddle.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Jeyne_Heddle.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) He is eventually slain, by [Rorge](wiki:Rorge)(wiki:Jeyne_Heddle.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) or by an unknown lord.(wiki:Jeyne_Heddle.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Jeyne and Willow reopen the inn for business, and as a refuge for orphan children. The sisters ally with the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), and [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) comes to work for the inn as a [smith](wiki:Smith).(wiki:Jeyne_Heddle.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

The [wench](wiki:Wench)(wiki:Jeyne_Heddle.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) is away with the brotherhood when [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth)'s party arrives at the crossroads inn. The remnants of the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) threaten Willow and the inn's children, and Brienne fights them off,(wiki:Jeyne_Heddle.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) but is severely injured. After the fight is over, Jeyne arrives and takes care of Brienne, dressing her wounds and splinting her broken arm. Jeyne and Gendry bring Brienne and her companions, [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne) and Ser [Hyle Hunt](wiki:Hyle_Hunt), to [a cave](wiki:Hollow_hill) to meet with [Lady Stoneheart](wiki:Lady_Stoneheart).(wiki:Jeyne_Heddle.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
