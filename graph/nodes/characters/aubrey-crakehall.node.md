## Origins

The [Shrike](wiki:Shrike_(priest)), a priest of the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God), led a revolt against King [Harmund III Hoare](wiki:Harmund_III_Hoare) which replaced him with his younger brother, [Hagon Hoare](wiki:Hagon_Hoare). Their mother, the Dowager Queen [Lelia Lannister](wiki:Lelia_Lannister), was mutilated and sent back to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock). Her nephew, the [King of the Rock](wiki:King_of_the_Rock), raised his banners in vengeance and Ser Aubrey commanded the [westermen](wiki:Westermen) in the war.(wiki:Aubrey_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
In the seventh year of fighting, Aubrey defeated the army of Hagon the Heartless on [Great Wyk](wiki:Great_Wyk). He considered restoring the throne to Harmund, who had been found in the dungeon of [Hoare Castle](wiki:Hoare_Castle), but instead gave the broken man the [gift of mercy](wiki:Gift_of_mercy). Aubrey had Hagon mutilated the same way that Lelia had been, and then had him hanged. Aubrey also ordered that Hoare Castle be razed.(wiki:Aubrey_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Instead of returning to the westerlands, however, surprisingly Aubrey declared himself [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands). The Lannisters did not support this decision, and King Aubrey was overthrown by the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) after less than six months. The [Shrike](wiki:Shrike) drowned him as a sacrifice to the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God).(wiki:Aubrey_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
