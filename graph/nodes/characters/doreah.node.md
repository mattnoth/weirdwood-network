## Identity

Doreah is a Lysene handmaid to Daenerys Targaryen (wiki:Doreah). She has blue eyes, soft white skin, and fair golden hair the color of honey (wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot11-3, wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot23-6). At the start of the series she is almost twenty (wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot23-6).

## Origins

Born in 278 or 279 AC in Lys (track_b: Born). Magister Illyrio Mopatis found Doreah in a pleasure house in Lys. Viserys Targaryen bedded her while a guest at Illyrio's manse in Pentos (wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot36-4).

## Allegiances

## Appearances & Description

Fair-haired with blue eyes; described as having "hair the color of honey, and eyes like the summer sky" (wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot23-6). Experienced in pillow tricks and the arts of love (wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot6-8, wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot11-3). An attentive handmaid (wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot54-7).

## Narrative Arc

Doreah, Irri, and Jhiqui are given to Daenerys as wedding gifts to serve as handmaids; Doreah is to instruct the young bride in the arts of love (wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot11-3). Daenerys remembers Doreah's lessons when she sleeps with Drogo (wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot23-6). At Vaes Dothrak, when Viserys strikes Doreah, Daenerys later offers him a cloak the Lyseni girl had sewn (wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot36-4). At the Western Market, Daenerys buys Doreah a fertility charm and Doreah later prevents the pregnant Daenerys from falling on her belly during the wineseller's attack (wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot54-7). She helps treat the dying Drogo and later prepares Daenerys for the funeral pyre (wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot64-11, wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot72-12). After Drogo's death, Doreah dies of a wasting disease during the march through the red waste (wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Racok12-5). Daenerys later uses Doreah's lessons with Daario Naharis and thinks of her among women she has lost (wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Radwd43-13, wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Radwd50-14).
