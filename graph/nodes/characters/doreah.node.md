## Origins

Magister [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) found Doreah in a [pleasure house](wiki:Pleasure_house) in [Lys](wiki:Lys). [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) bedded her while he was a guest at [Illyrio's manse](wiki:Illyrio's_manse) in [Pentos](wiki:Pentos).(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Doreah](wiki:Category:Images_of_Doreah)*

Doreah has blue eyes(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) and soft white skin.(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Her soft(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) blond hair(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) is fair(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) and golden.(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) At the start of the series, she is almost twenty.(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

Doreah is an attentive(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) handmaid experienced in [pillow tricks](wiki:Pillow_house)(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) and the arts of love.(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Doreah, [Irri](wiki:Irri), and [Jhiqui](wiki:Jhiqui), are given to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) as [wedding gifts](wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen) to serve as her handmaidens; Doreah is to instruct the young bride in the "womanly" arts of love.(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) Daenerys remembers Doreah's lessons when she sleeps with Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo).(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

[Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) is usually kinder to Doreah than to Daenerys's [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) handmaids, and Daenerys has the Lyseni sew a fine sandsilk cloak for her brother. When Daenerys sends Doreah to bring Viserys to dinner, however, he strikes the handmaid. Daenerys then offers the cloak to her brother, but Viserys refuses to wear the Dothraki garb.(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

Doreah drapes Daenerys in a scented sandsilk robe after she has sex with Drogo by the [Womb of the World](wiki:Womb_of_the_World).(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

The handmaid accompanies Daenerys when she visits the Western Market of [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak). At a [magician](wiki:Magic)'s booth, Daenerys purchases a fertility charm for Doreah. When a wineseller attacks Daenerys, Doreah prevents the pregnant girl from falling on her belly.(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

Daenerys claims [Eroeh](wiki:Eroeh) and tasks Doreah with tending to her wounds.(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Daenaerys's handmaids help treat Drogo as the *khal* is dying.(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Doreah washes and brushes Daenerys's hair in preparation for Drogo's funeral.(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Clash of Kings

After [Drogo](wiki:Drogo)'s death, Daenerys takes what remains of her *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* and marches out into the [red waste](wiki:Red_waste) in search of a safe place. On the march, Doreah dies of a wasting disease,.(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

### A Dance with Dragons

Daenerys, now [Queen of Meereen](wiki:Queen_of_Meereen), uses Doreah's lessons when she sleeps with [Daario Naharis](wiki:Daario_Naharis).(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Having reluctantly reopened the [fighting pits of Meereen](wiki:Fighting_pits_of_Meereen), Daenerys thinks of Doreah and other women she has lost.(wiki:Doreah.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
