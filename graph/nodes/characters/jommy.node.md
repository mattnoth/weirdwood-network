## Identity

Jommy was a man in service to House Baratheon at Storm's End during the reign of King Aerys II Targaryen (wiki:Jommy).

## Narrative Arc

When Lord Steffon Baratheon's ship the Windproud sank in Shipbreaker Bay, Jommy was among the men sent to reclaim corpses. He found Patchface and to his dying day swore the fool was dead when found — but when Jommy grabbed his ankles, Patchface spat up water and sat up (wiki:Jommy).
