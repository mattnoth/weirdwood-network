## Origins

Ser Victor was one of the [Caltrops](wiki:Caltrops) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Victor_Risley.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

When Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake) became [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) during [the regency](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III) of [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), he had Victor named the [King's Justice](wiki:King's_Justice). During public executions, Victor used his headsman's axe to behead the [false Shepherd](wiki:False_Shepherd) and a [Pentoshi](wiki:Pentos) captain accused of bringing the [Winter Fever](wiki:Winter_Fever) from [Sisterton](wiki:Sisterton) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Victor killed [Orwyle](wiki:Orwyle), a former Grand Maester, with a sword.(wiki:Victor_Risley.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He retained his office after Lord Peake resigned as Hand.(wiki:Victor_Risley.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

During a plot against [House Rogare](wiki:House_Rogare) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in [135 AC](wiki:135_AC), Victor was named by Ser [Gareth Long](wiki:Gareth_Long) as being an accomplice and he was arrested. The trial of the people involved in the plot took place the [following year](wiki:136_AC) after a [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council) named a new Hand of the King and regents for Aegon III. During the trial, rather than join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), Victor demanded a [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat) to prove his innocence. Victor was quickly slain in the duel by his accuser, Gareth, who had agreed to take the black.(wiki:Victor_Risley.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
