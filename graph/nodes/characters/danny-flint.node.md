## Origins

Danny was raped and murdered at the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort). She is remembered for being brave and young.(wiki:Danny_Flint.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) "[Brave Danny Flint](wiki:Brave_Danny_Flint)" is a sad and pretty song written about her. In some versions of the song her ghost still walks the Nightfort.(wiki:Danny_Flint.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Upon arriving at the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort) with his companions, [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) recalls the tale of what happened to Danny Flint.(wiki:Danny_Flint.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

In [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), during the wedding banquet held in honour of [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) to "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)", Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) requests [a song](wiki:Brave_Danny_Flint) about brave young Danny Flint to make everyone weep.(wiki:Danny_Flint.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

As three thousand [wildlings](wiki:Free_folk) pass the [Wall](wiki:Wall) at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) notices that two of the boy hostages are girls in disguise. Jon asks [Tormund Giantsbane](wiki:Tormund_Giantsbane) if [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) ever sang a song about Danny Flint. Tormund says he does not think so and asks who he was. Jon tells him about Danny, saying that her song is sad and pretty but what happened to her was not.(wiki:Danny_Flint.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
