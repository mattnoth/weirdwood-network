## Identity

Borys Baratheon — Eldest of Lord Rogar's four brothers, named in some versions of the book as the man at the inn and later blamed by Archmaester Crey for the calumny.

## Origins

### Early reign of Jaehaerys I

In the older, shorter versions of the book *[A Caution for Young Girls](wiki:A_Caution_for_Young_Girls)*, entitled *Sins of the Flesh* and *The High and the Low*, in [50 AC](wiki:50_AC) Borys met Lady [Coryanne Wylde](wiki:Coryanne_Wylde) in the inn on the south bank of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush), beside the ferry landing, as she was traveling to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to begin serving Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Borys_Baratheon.cite_ref-5) There, he supposedly gave her, on behalf of his older brother, Lord [Rogar](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon), the order to seduce King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and so ruin his [marriage](wiki:Marriage) to Queen Alysanne.(wiki:Borys_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) According to [Archmaester](wiki:Archmaester) [Crey](wiki:Crey), the meeting at the inn never happened, and the story had been an attempt to blacken Lord Rogar's name, attributing the origins of the lie to Borys. And according to Maester [Ryben](wiki:Ryben), the story was nothing more than a bawdy tale amongst the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk).(wiki:Borys_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

After Rogar had attempted and failed twice to set King Jaehaerys aside and crown another in his place, Borys was named as heir to [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) by Rogar, who had no children himself.(wiki:Borys_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) After Jaehaerys had reached the [age of majority](wiki:Age_of_majority) and taken the rule into his own hands, he summoned Rogar to King's Landing. Borys counseled his brother to go to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), as he was convinced that Jaehaerys would have Rogar beheaded otherwise.(wiki:Borys_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

In [55 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), during the [tourney](wiki:Tourney_for_the_completion_of_the_Dragonpit) held at King's Landing to celebrate the completion of the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit), Borys participated in the melee.(wiki:Borys_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### Exile and Third Dornish War

In [52 AC](wiki:52_AC), Borys lost his status as heir to Storm's End when his older brother, Lord Rogar finally became a father following the birth of his son, [Boremund](wiki:Boremund_Baratheon). Borys became sour and angry,(wiki:Borys_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) quarreling bitterly with Rogar.(wiki:Borys_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) Borys eventually left the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) and went to [Myr](wiki:Myr) and next to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis). He eventually returned to the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), where he joined the [Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Jaehaerys_I)) in the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). There, Borys assisted the rebel in the raiding and pillaging of his former homeland.(wiki:Borys_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

In [61 AC](wiki:61_AC), during the [Third Dornish War](wiki:Third_Dornish_War), Lord Rogar and King Jaehaerys I Targaryen went to war against Borys and the Vulture King. Borys was the first to be cornered in the Red Mountains after his men had been defeated by the men of his older brother. Although Rogar had intended to face Borys himself, Jaehaerys did not want to let Rogar be labeled a [kinslayer](wiki:Kinslayer), and personally challenged Borys to single combat. Borys accepted and charged at Jaehaerys while shouting a taunt. He was cut down by King Jaehaerys with the [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) [sword](wiki:Armament) [Blackfyre](wiki:Blackfyre).(wiki:Borys_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

## Appearances & Description

Borys was often considered to be the most volatile and belligerent of the five Baratheon brothers.(wiki:Borys_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
