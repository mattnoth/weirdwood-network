## Identity

Ser Borys Baratheon was a knight from House Baratheon during the reigns of Aegon I, Aenys I, Maegor I, and Jaehaerys I Targaryen. He was the eldest of Lord Rogar Baratheon's four younger brothers (wiki:Borys_Baratheon).

## Origins

Born between 18 and 23 AC (track_b: Born). He was often considered the most volatile and belligerent of the five Baratheon brothers (wiki:Borys_Baratheon).

## Narrative Arc

In 50 AC, Borys was named heir to Storm's End by his brother Rogar, who had no children at the time, after Rogar's plots to depose King Jaehaerys I failed. Borys was allegedly involved in a scheme involving Lady Coryanne Wylde, ordered by Rogar to seduce King Jaehaerys, though Archmaester Crey claims the meeting never happened. After Rogar left to fight in the Third Dornish War, Borys accompanied him and died in battle in the Red Mountains of Dorne in 61 AC (wiki:Borys_Baratheon).
