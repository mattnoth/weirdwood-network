## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Robar Royce](wiki:Category:Images_of_Robar_Royce)*

Ser Robar is "comely in a rough-hewn way".(wiki:Robar_Royce.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He has pale eyes.(wiki:Robar_Royce.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Robar is a good [tourney](wiki:Tourney) warrior and a skilled swordsman.(wiki:Robar_Royce.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)(wiki:Robar_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) thinks that Robar and the company he keeps are young and foolish because they see war as a game.(wiki:Robar_Royce.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Robar's [silvered](wiki:Silver) steel plate is filigreed in [bronze](wiki:Bronze) with inscribed [runes](wiki:Runes). Like those worn by his kin, the ancient runes are said to protect [House Royce](wiki:House_Royce) from harm.(wiki:Robar_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Robar attends the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with his father, Lord [Yohn Royce](wiki:Yohn_Royce), and his elder brother, Ser [Andar](wiki:Andar_Royce). Robar defeats [Lothor Brune](wiki:Lothor_Brune) and is defeated in turn by Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell).(wiki:Robar_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

After Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) decides to send a party to bring Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) to justice for raiding the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), he sends Ser Robar to the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood) to inform King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) and Yohn.(wiki:Robar_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) After Robert dies from being gored by a [boar](wiki:Boar), the Royces are among the nobles who King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) insists must do homage to him or be named traitors.(wiki:Robar_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Clash of Kings

As a second son Robar has to make his own name, so he decides to support [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)'s claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) as the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) begins. Renly appoints him to his [Rainbow Guard](wiki:Rainbow_Guard) as Robar the Red and gives him a suit of blood red armor and a rainbow cloak.(wiki:Robar_Royce.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

When [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) attends the feast near [Bitterbridge](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge), Lord [Bryce Caron](wiki:Bryce_Caron), a fellow member of the Rainbow Guard as Bryce the Orange, goads Robar into juggling daggers.(wiki:Robar_Royce.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

On the eve of the battle at [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)) between the brothers Renly and [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), Robar escorts Catelyn from Renly's camp to a nearby [sept](wiki:Sept) and back.(wiki:Robar_Royce.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) The quiet Robar discusses the upcoming battle with the loud Ser [Wendel Manderly](wiki:Wendel_Manderly).(wiki:Robar_Royce.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

Seconds after the [murder of Renly](wiki:Shadow_assassin), Robar and Ser [Emmon Cuy](wiki:Emmon_Cuy) burst into the tent with two [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms) to find Renly's dead body in the arms of their fellow Rainbow Guard, [Brienne Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth). They both think that Brienne the Blue has killed Renly, and Emmon the Yellow and others attack her. Catelyn, who has seen the murder, is able to convince a frightened Robar that Brienne is innocent. The young knight tells Catelyn to take Brienne away, and he holds the attackers at bay as Catelyn and Brienne depart.(wiki:Robar_Royce.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

When he finds Renly dead, Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell), the [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard) of the Rainbow Guard, kills Robar and Emmon in a red rage for their "failure" to protect the king.(wiki:Robar_Royce.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Now a member of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), Loras sadly recalls that he killed Robar in his rage.(wiki:Robar_Royce.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) He regrets his actions after Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) tells of Brienne's innocence.(wiki:Robar_Royce.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
