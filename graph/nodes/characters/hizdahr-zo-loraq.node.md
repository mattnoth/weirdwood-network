## Origins

Hizdahr is a Meereenese merchant who has become famously and fabulously rich.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-9) He has visited [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), [Lys](wiki:Lys), and [Qarth](wiki:Qarth).(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-10) He has many friends(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) and wields significant influence, both within Meereen and outside,(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) stretching as far as [New Ghis](wiki:New_Ghis).(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-11)

Hizdahr has fathered two [bastard](wiki:Bastard) children, a boy and a girl.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

## Appearances & Description

Hizdahr is a tall and very slender man,(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) with long legs.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) He has gentle hands,(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) placid eyes,(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and a long face.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) His amber skin, typical of Ghiscari, is flawless,(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) and is sometimes described as pale.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Hizdahr has the Ghiscari wiry red-black hair, which he originally twisted into the shape of wings sprouting from his temples, making it appear as if his head was about to take flight.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) He eventually shaves off the wings.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Hizdahr also initially has a beard, which makes his long face appear even longer, with golden rings bound into it.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4) When he cuts his hair, he shaves his beard off as well.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) considers Hizdahr to be a handsome man.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5) He is also very limber.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-6)

Hizdahr is a cunning(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2) and persuasive man, with many friends.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) believes Hizdahr does not seem to be of a jealous nature.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Overall, Hizdahr is well respected within [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Hizdahr's favorite meal is [dog](wiki:Dog) in honey, stuffed with prunes and peppers.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

As befits his status of birth, Hizdahr often dresses in a [tokar](wiki:Tokar).(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-7)(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) He has several tokars, including a purple tokar fringed with [amethysts](wiki:Amethyst) and [pearls](wiki:Pearl),(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-8) and a burgundy tokar with a [golden](wiki:Gold) stripe and a fringe of golden beads.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3) On some occasions, Hizdahr dresses more plainly, in a robe,(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-3)(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) sometimes with a quilted vest.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

After [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) [takes Meereen and frees its slaves](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen), she has the city's [fighting pits](wiki:Fighting_pits) closed. Hizdahr buys the pits at a reduced price, and makes multiple petitions to Daenerys to reopen them, but each time she refuses him.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-12) For his seventh petition, Hizdahr arrives with cut hair and shaved beard, in a simple robe rather than a tokar. He brings the former [pit fighters](wiki:Pit_fighters) [Khrazz](wiki:Khrazz), [Barsena Blackhair](wiki:Barsena_Blackhair), [Camarron of the Count](wiki:Camarron_of_the_Count), [Goghor the Giant](wiki:Goghor_the_Giant), [Spotted Cat](wiki:Spotted_Cat), [Fearless Ithoke](wiki:Fearless_Ithoke), and [Belaquo Bonebreaker](wiki:Belaquo_Bonebreaker), who also beg Daenerys to reopen the pits. Faced with her people's wishes, Daenerys decides to think over Hizdahr's petition.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-4)

With the [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy) continuing to cause trouble in the streets, the [Green Grace](wiki:Green_Grace) [Galazza Galare](wiki:Galazza_Galare), High Priestess of Meereen, advises Daenerys to take a husband of Ghiscari blood to placate the Meereenese, and recommends Hizdahr.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6) Though Daenerys is reluctant, she asks Hizdahr to bring her ninety days of peace with no more Sons of the Harpy killings before she will consider marrying him, and he agrees.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7)

Hizdahr lives up to his end of the bargain, and brings Daenerys the peace she desires. However, [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) advises Daenerys that Hizdahr must be the [Harpy](wiki:Harpy), the one who had given the Sons their orders in the first place, which would explain why the killings stopped.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2) Daenerys dismisses this, however, and [marries Hizdahr](wiki:Wedding_of_Hizdahr_zo_Loraq_and_Daenerys_Targaryen). Although Daenerys has no desire for him, she is desperate for peace in Meereen.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-3)(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) As soon as Hizdahr is crowned, he removes Skahaz from command of the [Brazen Beasts](wiki:Brazen_Beasts), and appoints his cousin [Marghaz zo Loraq](wiki:Marghaz_zo_Loraq) over them.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

The pits are reopened for a series of bloodsports held in [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit) to celebrate the wedding. Daenerys does not like this, but Hizdahr insists the fighters volunteered for the arena in a chance to win glory. At the reopening event, Hizdahr offers Daenerys [spiced locusts](wiki:Poisoned_locusts), but she refuses them, and [Strong Belwas](wiki:Belwas) eagerly eats them instead. During [Barsena Blackhair](wiki:Barsena_Blackhair)'s fight with a [boar](wiki:Boar), Daenerys's fugitive [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) appears over the arena, drawn by the stench of blood and the noise of the crowd. Once provoked, Drogon sets much of the arena ablaze, and causes chaos and carnage as the citizens flee and trample one another. Hizdahr shouts for his men to kill Drogon, and Daenerys leaps into the pit, running to her "child". When Daenerys flees Daznak's Pit [on Drogon's back](wiki:Dragonrider), Hizdahr becomes a king without his queen.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2)

Belwas becomes very ill, as the spiced locusts were laced with [poison](wiki:Poison), but survives due to his strength and bulk.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Hizdahr takes up residence in [the heart](wiki:Heart_(Great_Pyramid)) of the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) during Daenerys's absence. He tries to gain control of Meereen after the disappearance of Daenerys, but [Grey Worm](wiki:Grey_Worm) and his [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) refuse to obey him, and Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) considers Hizdahr vain and weak.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) When the [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) lords, along with [Bloodbeard](wiki:Bloodbeard), present Hizdahr with the head of [Groleo](wiki:Groleo) and demand the deaths of the [dragons](wiki:Dragons) in return for the Meereen [hostages](wiki:Hostage), Hizdahr states he must ponder their request. Ser Barristan, who is present at this parley and had served three kings ([Jaehaerys II Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_II_Targaryen), [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), and [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_Baratheon)), thinks that none of them would have acted as meekly as Hizdahr when presented with such a threat.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

Several of Daenerys's supporters, such as Ser Barristan and Skahaz mo Kandaq, suspect Hizdahr of poisoning the locusts in hopes of getting rid of Daenerys, since he had offered her the delicacy but did not touch it himself.(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-2) The delicate political situation leads Ser Barristan to talk with Skahaz(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) and later with [Grey Worm](wiki:Grey_Worm).(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) They capture Hizdahr, but Barristan refuses to kill him until he is sure Hizdahr is guilty. After Hizdahr's capture, the [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy) resume their shadow war, and [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) continues their [siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen).(wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2)
