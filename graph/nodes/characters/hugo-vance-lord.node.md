## Origins

Hugo became Lord of Wayfarer's Rest after the death of Lord [Tristan Vance](wiki:Tristan_Vance) at the hands of Ser [Adrian Tarbeck](wiki:Adrian_Tarbeck) during the [Battle of the Red Fork](wiki:Battle_of_the_Red_Fork) in [130 AC](wiki:130_AC).(wiki:Hugo_Vance_(lord).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

In the prelude to the [Butcher's Ball](wiki:Butcher's_Ball), the [Myrish](wiki:Myr) sellswords of [Black Trombo](wiki:Black_Trombo) in the employ of Lord Vance harassed and ambushed the host of Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole).(wiki:Hugo_Vance_(lord).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Hugo provided three hundred Vance men, as well as Black Trombo's sellswords, to the host of Ser [Addam Velaryon](wiki:Addam_Velaryon) before the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton), following his liege Lord [Elmo Tully](wiki:Elmo_Tully) into battle.(wiki:Hugo_Vance_(lord).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Hugo later joined the host of Lord Tully as they marched to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) against the army of Lord [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon), resulting in the [Battle of the Kingsroad](wiki:Battle_of_the_Kingsroad).(wiki:Hugo_Vance_(lord).cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) His fate is unknown.
