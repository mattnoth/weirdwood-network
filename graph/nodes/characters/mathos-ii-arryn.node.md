## Origins

During the [Rape of the Three Sisters](wiki:Rape_of_the_Three_Sisters), the name by which the [Northern](wiki:North) conquest of the isles is best known, the atrocities done by the [Kings of Winter](wiki:Kings_of_Winter) were horrible enough, that the remaining lords of the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters) asked King Mathos II for his help, help he gladly gave. The help came, provided upon the condition that the Three Sisters agreed to do fealty to him and House Arryn and acknowledge the right of the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) to rule them.(wiki:Mathos_II_Arryn.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
His wife questioned the wisdom of fighting this [War Across the Water](wiki:War_Across_the_Water), but he replied: "that he would sooner have a [pirate](wiki:Pirate) than a [wolf](wiki:House_Stark) for his neighbour".(wiki:Mathos_II_Arryn.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
He set sail for [Sisterton](wiki:Sisterton) with a hundred warships but he never returned. His sons carried on the war after him.(wiki:Mathos_II_Arryn.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
