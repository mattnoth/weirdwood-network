## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Wyl is one of the guards that go south with Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) when he is named [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King). [Jory Cassel](wiki:Jory_Cassel), [Heward](wiki:Heward) and Wyl escort Eddard to a [brothel](wiki:Brothel) to find one of [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s [bastards](wiki:Bastards), [Barra](wiki:Barra). On the return trip to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), the three guards are slain by [House Lannister guards](wiki:House_Lannister_guards) under orders from Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) as a lesson to Eddard.(wiki:Wyl_(guard).cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
