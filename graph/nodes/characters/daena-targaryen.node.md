## Identity

Princess Daena Targaryen, also known as Daena the Defiant, was the eldest daughter of King Aegon III Targaryen and Queen Daenaera Velaryon (wiki:Daena_Targaryen). She was the sister-wife of King Baelor I Targaryen and the mother of Daemon Blackfyre (wiki:Daena_Targaryen).

## Appearances & Description

Daena was wild almost from birth, strong, beautiful, and willful with Valyrian looks. Her long silver-gold hair was thick and curly, framing a heart-shaped face with sparkling purple eyes and a fearless smile. She was lithe and athletic — an expert horsewoman, hunter, and fine archer who used a Dornish short recurved bow. She always wore a golden three-headed dragon pendant inherited from her father. As a child she wore all black in emulation of her father; after Baelor refused to consummate their marriage, she changed to all white (wiki:Daena_Targaryen).

## Narrative Arc

Born the third child of Aegon III, Daena married her brother Baelor in 160 AC. The marriage was unhappy — her pious brother was obsessed by the Faith and refused consummation, eventually dissolving the marriage. Baelor imprisoned Daena and her sisters in the Maidenvault to remove temptation. After Baelor's death, Daena had an affair with the future Aegon IV Targaryen and bore his bastard son Daemon, who would become Daemon I Blackfyre. She died in or after 171 AC (track_b: Died) (wiki:Daena_Targaryen).
