## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Praed is the first to notice a row of freshly dug graves along the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad) when [Yoren](wiki:Yoren)'s band makes its way north not far from [King’s Landing](wiki:King’s_Landing).(wiki:Praed.cite_ref-Racok5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

He is constantly coughing. When [Arry](wiki:Arya_Stark) wakes up one night, she thinks it is oddly calm, but she doesn't realize what she is missing is Praed's cough. They find the sellsword dead in the morning. He is the first of the group to die and only the fourth recruit Yoren has lost in thirty years as a wandering crow. Yoren strips the dead of his gear, distributing it among the group, handing Praed's longsword to [Gendry](wiki:Gendry). They bury Praed where he slept, with [Tarber](wiki:Tarber) dropping a handful of acorns on his body first, so that an [oak](wiki:Oak) might grow to mark the place of the grave.(wiki:Praed.cite_ref-Racok5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Praed.cite_ref-Racok9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
