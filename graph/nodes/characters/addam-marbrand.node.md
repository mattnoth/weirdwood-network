## Origins

During his youth, Addam was a [page](wiki:Page) at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), and a childhood friend of Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

## Appearances & Description

Addam is rangy, with shoulder-length dark [copper](wiki:Copper) hair.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Bold,(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rawoiafaddam_marbrand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) gallant(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) and charming,(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Addam is an excellent horseman and swordfighter.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) He is described as a daring commander(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) whom others would willingly follow into battle.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) He is both a skilled swordsman and horseman.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rawoiafaddam_marbrand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Addam is a trusted friend of Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Addam wears burnished [bronzed](wiki:Bronze) armor, and the burning tree of the [Marbrands](wiki:House_Marbrand) is etched upon his breastplate.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Addam rides a spirited red courser, barded with bronze-colored trappings emblazoned with the burning tree.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3) He owns cloaks of bronze(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-4) and smoke grey.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) is taken captive by [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), his father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), calls his banners and raids the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). Commanding Tywin's outriders,(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Addam reports that Catelyn's son, [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), is marching a [northern](wiki:North) host down the [causeway](wiki:Causeway). Tywin orders Addam to withdraw and harass the opposing force's flanks, while he marches the Lannister host north to engage them.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) In the opening weeks of the war, Addam's scouts raid the area around the [Twins](wiki:Twins), and skirmish against the soldiers of [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey). Catelyn tasks her uncle, Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully), with preventing Addam's men from learning that Robb is splitting his forces at the Twins.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)

When Addam commands Tywin's right wing at the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork), his force numbers four thousand mounted men and three quarters of Tywin's knights, amongst them Ser [Flement Brax](wiki:Flement_Brax) and men from [Crakehall](wiki:Crakehall) and [Cornfield](wiki:Cornfield).(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) Following their victory, Addam reports to Tywin the capture of Lord [Medger Cerwyn](wiki:Medger_Cerwyn), Ser [Wylis Manderly](wiki:Wylis_Manderly), [Harrion Karstark](wiki:Harrion_Karstark), and four [Freys](wiki:House_Frey), and the death of Lord [Halys Hornwood](wiki:Halys_Hornwood). The captives admit Robb crossed at the Twins with the majority of his cavalry to retake [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

Tywin holds a council following the defeats of the [Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood) and the [Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps). Addam counsels against seeking a truce, and advises marching on the Stark host. Tywin orders his host to [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), dispatching Addam's outriders to screen their movements. When Tywin makes Tyrion the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Addam is among the alternatives that Tyrion suggests.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Well-liked by the women of Harrenhal, Addam is also considered by [Weese](wiki:Weese) to be Tywin's most daring commander. When Tywin's forces leave Harrenhal during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Addam is the first to depart, a day ahead of his liege lord.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-5) He rides west, joining Tywin in an attempt to cross the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork), but is thrown back three times at the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords).(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) His crossing prevented, Tywin then receives word of an imminent attack on [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), and turns his forces south to the city. Addam distinguishes himself defending King's Landing at the ensuing [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), and is recognized by King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Storm of Swords

After the Blackwater, Addam is made [Commander](wiki:Commander_of_the_City_Watch) of the [City Watch of King's Landing](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing) by Tywin. Tyrion considers him a shrewd choice, despite the fact that Addam is an outsider, because he is a natural leader. Addam receives an ornate black breastplate and a cloth-of-[gold](wiki:Gold) cloak.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

Addam is concerned about paying the forty-four hundred gold cloaks, but he is not allowed to dismiss any, by order of Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister). Tywin is also angry that Addam has not been able to find the missing squire [Tyrek Lannister](wiki:Tyrek_Lannister), lost in the [riot of King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2) Addam later discovers crystals from the crown of the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(fat_one)), who was killed during the riots. When deserters from the Blackwater return to the barracks, Tywin orders Addam to break their knees to prevent them running again, and leave them as beggars as a reminder to others.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

Addam attends [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark) of [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) He is part of the group that accompanies Tyrion to meet the delegation from [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), to add more political weight to the Lannister party.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Addam gives Joffrey a pair of [silver](wiki:Silver) spurs before his royal wedding to [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), and he cautions the king to be careful with his new [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) blade, [Widow's Wail](wiki:Widow's_Wail).(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Addam provides a guard for the couple after they are wed at the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor). The knight plans to deliver a toast during their wedding feast in the [throne room](wiki:Great_Hall_(Red_Keep)), but Joffrey [dies choking](wiki:Purple_Wedding) before he can do so.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

Addam's men take Tyrion, his squire [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne), and Sansa's maids, into custody on suspicion of [poisoning](wiki:Poison) Joffrey. He is assigned to search for the missing Sansa, and is responsible for guarding Tyrion while he is brought to trial. Tywin does not send the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) to guard Tyrion, because the majority of them will testify against Tyrion, and he does not think it proper.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) Addam escorts Tyrion again, on the final day of his trial, when Tyrion demands [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat).(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

When Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) returns to King's Landing, he asks Addam to help him practice fighting with his left hand, as [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat) had [Zollo](wiki:Zollo) cut off Jaime's sword hand.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) He selects Addam because of their history, and hopes that he can trust him not to reveal his weakness with a sword. Addam bests Jaime easily, disarming him three times, and leaving him battered and bruised.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Ser Addam and the City Watch stand guard during the funeral of [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), after his death at the hands of his son [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister). Trying to discover how Tyrion escaped, Addam and Jaime link him to [Rugen](wiki:Rugen), a missing gaoler,(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) but Addam cannot find him in the [brothels](wiki:Brothel) along the [Street of Silk](wiki:Street_of_Silk).(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) Jaime tasks Addam with organizing the guarding and [tasting](wiki:Food_taster) of King [Tommen I Baratheon's](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) food.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) When Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) plans to leave King's Landing to hunt outlaws in the riverlands, Jaime unsuccessfully suggests that Kevan should send Addam in his place and remain as the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) When Jaime is ordered by the Queen Regent, his sister Cersei, to retrieve prisoners at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) and resolve the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), he insists he be accompanied by Addam and Ser [Ilyn Payne](wiki:Ilyn_Payne). Addam is happy to leave the gold cloaks and again command the outriders.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

While dining at [Hayford](wiki:Hayford), Addam reports his inability to find [Tyrek Lannister](wiki:Tyrek_Lannister), the Lord of [Hayford](wiki:Hayford) by marriage to the infant Lady [Ermesande Hayford](wiki:Ermesande_Hayford). Jaime replaces Addam with Ilyn as a sparring partner, preferring the tongueless [King's Justice](wiki:King's_Justice), but Addam does not reveal his friend's secret to others. The outriders are troubled by [wolves](wiki:Wolves), and one of Addam's men has his [horse](wiki:Horse) run off and killed by a pack.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2) At Darry, Ser [Danwell Frey](wiki:Danwell_Frey) reports that wolves attacked his baggage train on his way south from the Twins, and Addam commiserates. Addam charms the woman seated next to him during the feast there. When talk turns to the hanging of [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey) at the hands of outlaws, Addam repeats the rumor that Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) heads a [group of outlaws](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) and cannot be killed.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

When Jaime's group arrives at Riverrun, Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister) reports several foraging parties have gone missing. Jaime tasks Addam with inspecting the perimeter of their camp.(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) During Jaime's unsuccessful parley with Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully), Brynden the Blackfish insults Jaime as a cripple, and suggests that he name Addam as a champion in his place. Participating in Jaime's war council, Addam offers to lead a night raid. Jaime instead negotiates with the captive Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully).(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

After the Blackfish's escape from Riverrun, Addam commands the search to the south of the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) of the [Trident](wiki:Trident).(wiki:Addam_Marbrand.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)
