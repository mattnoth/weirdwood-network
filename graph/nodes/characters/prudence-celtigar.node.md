## Origins

Prudence was the daughter of Lord [Edwell Celtigar](wiki:Edwell_Celtigar) of [Claw Isle](wiki:Claw_Isle).(wiki:Prudence_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) She had at least one sister, [Prunella](wiki:Prunella_Celtigar),(wiki:Prudence_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) and a brother.(wiki:Prudence_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

In [47 AC](wiki:47_AC), her father urged King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) to marry either Prudence or Prunella, or both if he so desired. The king did not choose Prudence or her sister, instead marrying the [Black Brides](wiki:Black_Brides), [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)), [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling_(wife_of_Maegor_I)) and [Elinor Costayne](wiki:Elinor_Costayne).(wiki:Prudence_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

In [49 AC](wiki:49_AC), her father made the same offer to Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) and Queen Regent [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon), that King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) marry either Prudence or Prunella; Lord Baratheon rebuffed this offer.(wiki:Prudence_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Prudence became a lady companion to Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) during her time on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Prudence_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) During King Jaehaerys and Queen Alysanne's second [wedding](wiki:Wedding), she disrobed the king during the [bedding](wiki:Bedding) ceremony.(wiki:Prudence_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

At [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool), Prudence was stabbed through the shoulder defending the queen when she was attacked by septas attending [Jonquil's Pool](wiki:Jonquil's_Pool) who wished to kill the "[abomination](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I))" Alysanne was carrying.(wiki:Prudence_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

In [52 AC](wiki:52_AC), the betrothal of Prudence to Lord [Grafton](wiki:House_Grafton) was arranged.(wiki:Prudence_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

## Appearances & Description
