## Origins

### Early life

Born in [276 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest),(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Calculation_1-1) Arianne is the eldest child of her father, [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), and his wife, Lady [Mellario](wiki:Mellario) of [Norvos](wiki:Norvos). As her father's firstborn, she is Doran's heiress, according to [Rhoynish](wiki:Rhoynish) customs and Dornish laws.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-7)

Arianne spent several years of her early life at the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens). She has two younger brothers, but since [Quentyn](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) was sent away as a page to [Yronwood](wiki:Yronwood) at an early age and [Trystane](wiki:Trystane_Martell) is eleven years younger than herself, Arianne has never been close to them. Instead, her best companions during childhood were [Andrey Dalt](wiki:Andrey_Dalt), [Garin](wiki:Garin_(orphan)) (whose mother had been her [wet nurse](wiki:Wet_nurse)),(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5) [Sylva Santagar](wiki:Sylva_Santagar), and her cousin [Tyene Sand](wiki:Tyene_Sand).(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3) While playing the [shoulder-riding game](wiki:Shoulder-riding_game) in the Water Gardens, Arianne sometimes rode the shoulders of [Jeyne](wiki:Jeyne_Fowler) or [Jennelyn Fowler](wiki:Jennelyn_Fowler), or her friend [Frynne](wiki:Frynne). Her favorite steed was Garin, since if she rode him, nobody could defeat them, not even [Nymeria Sand](wiki:Nymeria_Sand) and the [Archon's green-haired daughter](wiki:Archon's_green-haired_daughter).(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-4) After breaking one of Garin's teeth, Arianne bought him a golden replacement.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-6)

Tyene in particular was Arianne's best friend, since they were the same age, and they became close as sisters.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-8) Arianne and Tyene shared everything, including learning how to read, ride, and dance; they even shared a flagon of [wine](wiki:Wine) they had stolen at the age of ten, and an early attempted sexual experience with Andrey.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-5) Arianne's older cousin Nymeria joined Arianne and her four companions on occasion, and sometimes her cousin [Sarella Sand](wiki:Sarella_Sand) attempted to join them as well.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-6) Arianne crossed the [Mander](wiki:Mander) in the [Reach](wiki:Reach) when she and three of [her cousins](wiki:Sand_Snakes) went to visit Tyene's mother.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-7)

Arianne once held her young cousin, Princess [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Rhaegar)), but she was so young at the time that she no longer remembers.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-twow_3-3) Arianne does remember her great-uncle, Prince [Lewyn Martell](wiki:Lewyn_Martell) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), of whom she has cherished memories, and who used to tickle her until she could not stop laughing.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-9) When [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) ended, Arianne was seven years old.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-twow_3-4) At the age of eight, Arianne became afflicted with [redspots](wiki:Redspots), and she learned that the disease, while harmless for children, could be lethal for adults.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-8)

From a young age, Arianne was half in love with her uncle, Prince [Oberyn](wiki:Oberyn_Martell),(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Rawoiafarianne_martell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) the father of her cousins. At some point, Oberyn brought Arianne, Garin, and her cousins Tyene and [Sarella Sand](wiki:Sarella_Sand) to the abandoned holdfast of [Shandystone](wiki:Shandystone). While Oberyn showed Tyene the best way to milk a [snake](wiki:Snake)'s venom and Sarella went exploring, Arianne sat on a rock, dreaming that a tall, hard robber knight, with black eyes and a widow's peak, had taken her there to have his way with her.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-9)

### Secret Betrothal

Arianne had a close relationship with her father [Doran](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), when she was a girl. She would run to him when she had hurt herself,(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-7) and she would give him a good night kiss.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-10)

When Arianne was fourteen years old, she stumbled across a half-written letter from Doran to her younger brother, [Quentyn](wiki:Quentyn_Martell), who was fostering at [Yronwood](wiki:Yronwood). Doran stated that Quentyn would one day sit in his place and rule [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), suggesting to Arianne that Doran intended for her younger brother to succeed him, passing over Arianne entirely. Arianne cried about her discovery for many days.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-11) Arianne longed to be a dutiful daughter, but she wanted her father to show that he valued her(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Rawoiafarianne_martell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) and she began to rebel against Doran in small ways.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Rawoiafarianne_martell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) When she was still fourteen, Arianne lost her virginity to [Daemon Sand](wiki:Daemon_Sand), the Bastard of [Godsgrace](wiki:Godsgrace),(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-12) an encounter she remembers as clumsy but sweet.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-twow_3-5) Arianne told Doran about the encounter immediately afterwards.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Rawoiafarianne_martell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) Doran did nothing about it, adding to Arianne's feelings that he was "very good at doing nothing".(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-13)

In truth, Doran was planning for Quentyn to inherit the rule of Dorne because he intended for Arianne to become Queen of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms). Unbeknownst to Arianne, her uncle, Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell), had traveled to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) years before and signed a [secret marriage pact](wiki:Secret_marriage_pact) in which Arianne was betrothed to the exiled [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen).(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-8)(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) In return for Arianne marrying Viserys, Doran would support Viserys's claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) Doran once planned to reveal the truth about the betrothal to Arianne, intending to send her to [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) to serve as a [cupbearer](wiki:Cupbearer) to [the Archon](wiki:Archon_of_Tyrosh) and secretly meet with Viserys. However, Doran abandoned that plan due to suicidal threats from Arianne's mother [Mellario](wiki:Mellario), who was already deeply upset that Quentyn had been sent away to be fostered.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-9) Doran then planned to tell Arianne about her betrothal and his plans to help restore the [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen) to the Iron Throne when Arianne turned sixteen. However, he changed his mind over time, and kept the betrothal a secret from Arianne because he felt that she was too willful, and feared that if he told her, she would tell her cousins (particularly [Tyene](wiki:Tyene_Sand)), and there would be no way to ensure the secret would remain a secret.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-10)

When Arianne reached the age of marriage, Doran presented her with several suitors, all of whom were elderly lords (including [Ben Beesbury](wiki:Ben_Beesbury), [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey), [Gyles Rosby](wiki:Gyles_Rosby), [Eldon Estermont](wiki:Eldon_Estermont), and [Hugh Grandison](wiki:Hugh_Grandison)), while rejecting an offer from Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), who had invited Arianne to travel to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) and meet his heir [Edmure](wiki:Edmure_Tully). Arianne refused all of the elderly suitors, and asked to travel to [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) to meet [Willas Tyrell](wiki:Willas_Tyrell). When Doran refused, Arianne and Tyene tried to go on their own, but were intercepted by Prince Oberyn at [Vaith](wiki:Vaith). Arianne attempted to seduce Lord [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) when he visited Dorne, but he seemed confused by her attempts and she was not successful.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-11) Because of having read the letter to Quentyn, the insulting marriage candidates Doran presented her with, and Doran's apparent coolness towards her, Arianne became convinced her father hated her.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Rawoiafarianne_martell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) In truth, Doran presented her with the elderly lords as suitors because he feared that it would be suspicious if he did not attempt to arrange a marriage for her, and because he knew that Arianne would refuse them (which she forcefully did),(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-10) thus keeping her free to marry Viserys.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-12)

During those years, Arianne also had suitors from Dorne. Her friend [Andrey Dalt](wiki:Andrey_Dalt) and his brother [Deziel](wiki:Deziel_Dalt) had both wanted to marry her, and her first lover, Daemon Sand, had even gone to Prince Doran to ask for her hand, though he was rejected. Arianne believed that her father did not wish for her to marry a Dornishman, and also realized that a [bastard](wiki:Bastard) was not a worthy consort for a princess. While her friendship with Daemon was never the same after her father rejected his offer, Arianne took on multiple lovers over the years, including at least one of her childhood friends, as well as Ser [Gerold Dayne](wiki:Gerold_Dayne) of [High Hermitage](wiki:High_Hermitage).(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Rawoiafarianne_martell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-twow_3-6)(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-11)(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-13)

Arianne had long been dissatisfied with her father and his style of rule, thinking Doran weak and vacillating.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-14) When her father moved to the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens) in [298 AC](wiki:298_AC), Arianne remained in [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear). However, instead of being made the castellan, or given a position of power, she was put in charge of "feasts and frolics", while Prince Oberyn was given the highest place of power at Sunspear's court. While her uncle would visit Doran at the Water Gardens twice every fortnight, Arianne was summoned by her father only twice a year, which all further enforced her belief that her father did not want her as his heir.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-14)

## Appearances & Description

As a young girl, Arianne was pudgy and flat-chested, and she would pray to [the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) nightly, hoping that she would be given beauty when she was older.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-twow_3-0) Now a woman in her early twenties, Arianne is buxom and beautiful, with olive skin, large dark eyes and long, thick black hair that falls in ringlets to the middle of her back. She has full lips, a husky voice, and round ripe breasts with huge dark nipples. Favoring her mother [Mellario](wiki:Mellario), Arianne is short, standing at five foot two.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) She perfumes her hair.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Arianne is calculating, adventurous, and fierce-tempered. When she sees something she desires, she strives to obtain it at all cost. She does not shy away from using her looks to get what she wants, nor from seducing men to get them to do her bidding, occasionally granting sexual favors in this context.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-twow_3-1) Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) thinks that Arianne's nature compares more to her uncle [Oberyn's](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) than to her father [Doran's](wiki:Doran_Martell).(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Arianne considers handsome men her one great weakness, in particular when they have an air of adventure and the forbidden.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-twow_3-2)(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Arianne has a vivid sexual imagination(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) and has had sexual experiences from an early age.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

The death of [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) on the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea), commanded by his sister [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s husband, Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo),(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) puts an end [to the marriage plans](wiki:Secret_marriage_pact) Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell) had for Viserys and Arianne, and to his hope that his daughter would one day become Queen of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-15)

### A Clash of Kings

As part of an alliance [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) has struck between the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) and [House Martell](wiki:House_Martell), Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) is sent to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) as a ward, and she is to marry Arianne's youngest brother, Prince [Trystane Martell](wiki:Trystane_Martell), when she turns fourteen.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Myrcella is accompanied by Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) as her [sworn shield](wiki:Sworn_shield).(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) informs his son Tyrion that Arys has written from Dorne. The knight writes that Myrcella and Trystane get on well, and that Myrcella has taken a great liking to Princess Arianne.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Feast for Crows

With [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), away at the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens), it falls on Arianne to greet Princess [Myrcella](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) and Ser [Arys](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) upon their arrival at [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear).(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-15) Arianne tries to seduce the knight, but it takes half a year before she is successful.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-16) Arianne drinks [moon tea](wiki:Moon_tea) to avoid pregnancy.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-16)

After the death of Prince [Oberyn](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) is in uproar, with Oberyn's elder three bastard daughters, [Obara](wiki:Obara_Sand), [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria_Sand) and [Tyene](wiki:Tyene_Sand), in particular arguing to avenge the Red Viper. Doran, who seemingly pursues a conciliatory approach, returns to Sunspear after having spent two years at the Water Gardens. Upon arrival in the city, he is greeted by Arianne, who has ordered a feast prepared in his honor.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

Unbeknownst to Doran, Arianne has learned that her brother, Prince [Quentyn](wiki:Quentyn_Martell), has secretly traveled across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea). Arianne fears that Lord [Anders Yronwood](wiki:Anders_Yronwood), Quentyn's current foster-father, is part of the plan to make Quentyn Doran's heir, displacing Arianne. When the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) breaks its contract with [Myr](wiki:Myr), she fears that Quentyn might have hired them to rob her of her birthright, and that he has Doran's support.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-17)(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-12)(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-17) In addition, Doran has arrested the [Sand Snakes](wiki:Sand_Snakes) (including the younger ones, but excluding [Sarella](wiki:Sarella_Sand), who is absent from Dorne) in an apparent attempt to placate the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-18)

To fight the supposed challenge to her own birthright, to avenge Oberyn and [Elia](wiki:Elia_Martell), and to free her cousins,(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-13)(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-18) Arianne puts into motion her scheme: she wants to crown Myrcella as [Queen of the Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Queen_of_the_Seven_Kingdoms), based on Dornish law—in which an older sister would inherit before a younger brother—thereby precipitating a war with the Iron Throne and Myrcella's younger brother, King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon). Arianne also hopes that the [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) will rally to this cause with the support of the released Sand Snakes. Arianne assumes this would make her the inevitable choice to replace her father, whom she wants to live out his days as captive at the Water Gardens together with her brother Quentyn.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-19)(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-14)

During a tryst, Arianne manipulates Arys into aiding and abetting her plan, promising that Queen Myrcella would permit them to marry despite his [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) vows. Arianne convinces Arys that they need to act before her father brings Myrcella from Sunspear to the Water Gardens.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-20) In truth, Arianne has no intention of marrying Arys, whom she likes but does not love.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-twow_3-7)(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-15)

Arianne assembles her closest friends as co-conspirators to help her implement her scheme: [Andrey Dalt](wiki:Andrey_Dalt), heir to his brother [Deziel](wiki:Deziel_Dalt), the Knight of [Lemonwood](wiki:Lemonwood); [Garin](wiki:Garin_(orphan)), an [orphan of the Greenblood](wiki:Orphan_of_the_Greenblood); and [Sylva Santagar](wiki:Sylva_Santagar), the heir of [House Santagar](wiki:House_Santagar). She also recruits the fierce Ser [Gerold Dayne](wiki:Gerold_Dayne), known as Darkstar, although she does not trust him completely. Meanwhile, Arys has the task of smuggling Myrcella out of Sunspear, using a feigned [illness](wiki:Illness) of the princess and her lookalike bedmaid [Rosamund Lannister](wiki:Rosamund_Lannister), as well as a stand-in for himself. Arianne and the others wait for Arys and Myrcella at the abandoned holdfast of [Shandystone](wiki:Shandystone). From there, Arianne plans to head out to the [Greenblood](wiki:Greenblood) and travel with an orphan poleboat westward on that river and [the Vaith](wiki:Vaith_(river)), then continue by land. The final destination is to be [Hellholt](wiki:Hellholt), the seat of [Ellaria Sand](wiki:Ellaria_Sand)'s father [Harmen Uller](wiki:Harmen_Uller), where Arianne intends to crown Myrcella as queen and to raise her own banners. While waiting at Shandystone, Arianne grows increasingly wary of Darkstar, who dismisses her plan as unrealistic and alludes to killing Myrcella.

When Arianne's band arrives at the Greenblood, Prince Doran's captain of guards, [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah), and [a dozen guardsmen](wiki:House_Martell_guards) emerge from the poleboat. Arianne and Darkstar wish to yield, but Arys charges the guards and is killed by Areo's longaxe. While Arianne falls in desperation and nausea, Gerold Dayne seriously injures Myrcella (who loses an ear) then manages to escape Areo's men. While she is being arrested, Areo tells Arianne that someone betrayed her plans.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-16)(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-19)

Prince Doran keeps his daughter isolated within the [Spear Tower](wiki:Spear_Tower) in the custody of [Ricasso](wiki:Ricasso) and Ser [Manfrey Martell](wiki:Manfrey_Martell). The pleasantly-appointed cell has history books, maps, and a *[cyvasse](wiki:Cyvasse)* table, which Arianne does not utilize. She realizes that either Drey, Garin, or Spotted Sylva must have betrayed her, and that Arys died in a suicide charge, which he undertook to wipe out his shame about his relationship with her. Arianne is forbidden contact with the outside world and attended by servants who will not speak to her. Her attempt to send a message to Lord [Franklyn Fowler](wiki:Franklyn_Fowler) through [Cedra](wiki:Cedra) is intercepted by Doran, after which Arianne begins a hunger strike.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-20) Her cell is fed well despite the confinement — a midday meal of "kid roasted with lemon and honey" and "grape leaves stuffed with a mélange of raisins, onions, mushrooms, and fiery dragon peppers" (affc-the-princess-in-the-tower-01:47), the comfort of her captivity contrasted with her co-conspirators' ship's-biscuit-and-salt-beef passage to Ghaston Grey.

After several weeks, Arianne is brought before her father. Doran informs her that Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) is traveling towards Sunspear, and Arianne realizes that Doran needs her help to convince Myrcella to lie about what has transpired. When an angry Arianne reveals she had read the letter meant for Quentyn, Doran explains that the letter and his apparent inaction were part of a larger plan to avenge the deaths of Elia and her children at the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing). He reveals he had only offered Arianne elderly, unacceptable suitors because he had [secretly promised](wiki:Secret_marriage_pact) her to another. [This man has died](wiki:A_Game_of_Thrones-Chapter_46), however, and Quentyn is on a mission for vengeance and justice for House Martell.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-21)

### A Dance with Dragons

Arianne is now aware of her father's plans.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) Doran had first intended to make her the queen of [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen), with Quentyn succeeding Doran as [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne).(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-22) With Viserys having died at [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak), however, Doran now intends for Arianne to become [Princess of Dorne](wiki:Princess_of_Dorne), with Quentyn marrying Viserys's sister, [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), now the [Queen of Meereen](wiki:Queen_of_Meereen).(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

At the feast welcoming Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) and his companions to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear), Arianne sits on the right side of her father, a place of high honor. Together with [Obara](wiki:Obara_Sand), [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria_Sand), [Tyene](wiki:Tyene_Sand), and [Ellaria Sand](wiki:Ellaria_Sand), as well as [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah) and half the nobility of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), Arianne and Doran witness the delivery of the [Mountain's skull](wiki:Mountain's_skull).(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

Arianne flirts with Balon, but he is not as easily distracted or seduced from his duty as his sworn brother [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) was. Arianne has convinced Myrcella to lie to Balon and claim that Arys was slain by Ser [Gerold Dayne](wiki:Gerold_Dayne). Doran has planned for them all to travel to the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens), where Myrcella is staying, and where Balon can learn from Myrcella what Gerold has done. According to Doran's plan, Myrcella is to plead with Balon to bring Gerold to justice. After Doran reveals to the eldest three Sand Snakes what their respective roles in his plans will be, Arianne wishes them well, and remains with her father after they leave. Doran tells Arianne a fleet has taken to water from [Lys](wiki:Lys).(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3)

Unbeknownst to the Martells, the fleet from Lys carries not Quentyn and Daenerys, but [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(Young_Griff)) and the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company), who [invade the stormlands](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company).(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Daenerys refuses to marry Quentyn when he arrives in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), and she weds [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) instead.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2) Quentyn thinks that Arianne would be scornful if he were to return to Dorne without completing his mission.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) After Daenerys disappears from [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit),(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Quentyn attempts to claim one of her [dragons](wiki:Dragon) from within the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid)(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) but dies from [dragonfire](wiki:Dragonfire).(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Prince Doran receives a letter from Lord [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington), revealing the survival of Elia's son, [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(Young_Griff)). Doran sends Arianne to discover the truth about Aegon and Jon. She travels with several companions, including her new sworn shield Ser [Daemon Sand](wiki:Daemon_Sand), [Joss Hood](wiki:Joss_Hood), [Garibald Shells](wiki:Garibald_Shells), [Nate](wiki:Nate), [Jayne Ladybright](wiki:Jayne_Ladybright), and her cousin [Elia Sand](wiki:Elia_Sand). They visit [Ghost Hill](wiki:Ghost_Hill) and take ship to the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands).(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-twow_3-8)

Arianne and company travel through the [rainwood](wiki:Rainwood), and they explore a cave once inhabited by the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest). At [Mistwood](wiki:Mistwood), they encounter members of the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company), who have taken the castle, and the sellswords escort the Dornishmen to [Griffin's Roost](wiki:Griffin's_Roost). At that castle, Arianne learns that the Golden Company, Jon, and Aegon have taken [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), and she decides to sail to meet them there.(wiki:Arianne_Martell.cite_ref-twow2_20-0)
