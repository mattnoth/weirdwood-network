## Identity

Princess Arianne Nymeros Martell is a member of House Martell and the eldest daughter and heir of Prince Doran Martell. She is a POV character in AFFC and TWOW (wiki:Arianne_Martell).

## Origins

Born in 276 AC at Sunspear (track_b: Born). Daughter of Doran Martell (track_b: Father) and Mellario of Norvos (track_b: Mother). Of Dornish culture (track_b: Culture).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Martell (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Dornishmen (track_b: Culture). `HOLDS_TITLE` Princess (track_b: Title). Under Dornish custom she is heiress to Sunspear and the future ruling Princess of Dorne.

## Appearances & Description

Buxom and beautiful, with olive skin, large dark eyes, and long thick black hair falling in ringlets to the middle of her back. Full lips, a husky voice, and round ripe breasts. Favoring her mother Mellario, Arianne is short at five foot two (wiki:Arianne_Martell). She perfumes her hair. Calculating, adventurous, and fierce-tempered — Ser Arys Oakheart compares her nature to her uncle Oberyn's rather than her father Doran's (wiki:Arianne_Martell).

## Narrative Arc

Arianne grew up believing her father meant to disinherit her in favor of her brother Quentyn, based on a letter she found. This drove a wedge between father and daughter. She devised the Queenmaker plot to crown Myrcella Baratheon as queen under Dornish succession law, recruiting Ser Arys Oakheart, Darkstar, and others. The plot was foiled by Areo Hotah on Doran's orders; Arys Oakheart was killed and Myrcella was wounded by Darkstar. After weeks imprisoned in the Spear Tower, Arianne was brought before Doran, who revealed his true plans — a secret marriage pact with House Targaryen and his long game of revenge for Elia's murder (wiki:Arianne_Martell). In ADWD, Arianne is involved in Doran's revised plans after the failure of Quentyn's mission to Daenerys.
