Cellador has a strong tendency to [alcoholism](wiki:Alcohol).(wiki:Cellador.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) He wears a seven-sided crystal around his neck.(wiki:Cellador.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Cellador attends the recruits of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) who believe in the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) when they say their vows.(wiki:Cellador.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

After [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) learns of the [arrest of Eddard Stark](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow)'s friends ask Cellador to light a candle for Jon's father.(wiki:Cellador.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords

With his drunken prayers, Cellador takes part in the defense of the [Wall](wiki:Wall) against the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) of [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder).(wiki:Cellador.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Cellador.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) After the arrest of Jon by [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt)'s men, Cellador supports the accusations against Jon, who had taken his oath in front of a [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood). Cellador doubts Jon's sincerity because he believes in the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods) also worshipped by the wildlings, but the septon's protests are overridden by Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)).(wiki:Cellador.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Like [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh), Septon Cellador is opposed to many of the decisions Jon Snow makes as the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), such as making [Satin](wiki:Satin) his squire, learning about [wights](wiki:Wights), and bringing the wildlings of [Tormund](wiki:Tormund) south of the [Wall](wiki:Wall). Cellador is still often in his cups.(wiki:Cellador.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Cellador.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
