## Appearances & Description

Archibald is six-and-a-half-feet tall, broad of shoulder and huge of belly. He has legs like tree trunks, hands the size of hams, and no neck. His arms are thick enough to rival even [Strong Belwas](wiki:Strong_Belwas).(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Arch's bald head reminds others of a stone.(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) He is older than [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell).(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

Arch wields a spiked [warhammer](wiki:Warhammer)(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and a dagger,(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) and has fine [armor](wiki:Armor).(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He is a better fighter than Quentyn Martell or [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater).(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) He is aggressive at dicing(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) and easily becomes [seasick](wiki:Greensick).(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) Arch does not remember [Ghiscari](wiki:Ghiscari) names well.(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), tells his daughter, Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell), that her younger brother, Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell), has been sent with a small party on a dangerous voyage.(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Quentyn has been sent to wed [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), now the Queen of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). The prince has been joined by Ser Archibald, his cousin Ser [Cletus Yronwood](wiki:Cletus_Yronwood), [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Kedry](wiki:Kedry), Ser [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater), and Ser [Willam Wells](wiki:Willam_Wells).(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8) Arch becomes seasick during the voyage, with Kedry treating him for four days in [Lys](wiki:Lys).(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9) The party's [ship](wiki:Ship), *[Meadowlark](wiki:Meadowlark)*, is ambushed by [corsairs](wiki:Corsair) off the coast of the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands), and Cletus, Kedry, and Willam are slain.(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)

In [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), the survivors are unsuccessful in hiring a ship that will transport them to Meereen. Archibald suggests taking the [demon road](wiki:Demon_road), but his idea is dismissed in favor of Gerris's plan to join the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown), a [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) company on its way to the city.(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-11) To hide his identity, Archibald goes by the name "Greenguts", while Quentyn pretends to be his [squire](wiki:Squire), "Frog". The three companions sell their fine armor in Volantis and acquire battered armor from the Windblown.(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) When Quentyn, Gerris, and Arch discuss deserting the Windblown after the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor), they are summoned by the [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince). The captain orders them to join a party of [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) mercenaries who are to pretend that they betrayed the company and want to join Daenerys.(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

The Dornishmen present themselves to Daenerys in Meereen and the prince reveals their reason to be there, but Daenerys declines Quentyn's offer for marriage since she is about to wed [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq). The queen advises the [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) to return to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), but they are determined to stay.(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Archibald and Gerris reluctantly help Quentyn in his scheme to take Daenerys's [dragons](wiki:Dragon) after she vanishes from Meereen.(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) Arch wears a bull's mask while disguised as one of the [Brazen Beasts](wiki:Brazen_Beasts), and he uses his warhammer to break a lock deep within the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid).(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) When [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal) mortally burns Quentyn with [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame), Archibald tries to beat out the flames with his bare hands, to little success. When the Brazen Beasts find them, Archibald is cradling Quentyn's burned body. Both Archibald and Gerris are arrested, but neither gives resistance in their grief.(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), the [Hand of the Queen](wiki:Hand_of_the_Queen), visits the Dornishmen in their prison cells, and tells them that they are to be hanged for [treason](wiki:Treason). Gerris blames everything that has happened on Daenerys, but Arch manages to calm him down. The big man tells Barristan what happened when they tried to capture the dragons, and also that Quentyn promised [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) to the Tattered Prince for the Windblown's help in the plan. The old knight sees opportunity, and offers the prisoners a ship to Dorne and Quentyn's bones if they deliver a message to the Windblown's leader. Barristan wants the Dornishmen to tell the Tattered Prince that he will be given Pentos if he helps in freeing the Meereenese [hostages](wiki:Hostage) held by the [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai)'i. Gerris is reluctant, citing the fact that Archibald cannot even hold a sword with his burned hands. However, Archibald accepts, and convinces his younger companion to go along with the plan.(wiki:Archibald_Yronwood.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)
