## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

TomToo's father, [Tomard](wiki:Tomard), is killed by a [gold cloak](wiki:Gold_cloak) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:TomToo.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Clash of Kings

TomToo plays [Lord of the crossing](wiki:Lord_of_the_crossing) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) with the [Frey](wiki:House_Frey) wards, [Big Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Jammos)) and [Little Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)).(wiki:TomToo.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Storm of Swords

The women and children of Winterfell are said to have been taken by [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) to the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort).(wiki:TomToo.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) It is unknown if TomToo is with them or if he died in the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell).
