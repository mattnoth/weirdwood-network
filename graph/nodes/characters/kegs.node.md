## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Kegs is one of the men left behind by [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) to garrison [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black). During the [attack on Castle Black](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black), Kegs is part of the defense of the tunnel under the [Wall](wiki:Wall). While retreating with his sworn brothers to the stair, Kegs is caught by the cloak and nearly killed by a [Thenn](wiki:Thenns) with an axe, but he is saved by a crossbow shot from [Satin](wiki:Satin).(wiki:Kegs.cite_ref-Rasos55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Kegs blows a warhorn when [giants](wiki:Giants) approach the Wall with a ram during the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall). He operates catapults(wiki:Kegs.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and scorpions(wiki:Kegs.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) during the battle.
