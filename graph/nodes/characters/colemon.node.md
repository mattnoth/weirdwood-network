## Appearances & Description

Thin with little hair and a long, skinny neck with a visible Adam's apple (wiki:Colemon). Much taller than Petyr Baelish. Wears a threadbare grey cloak lined with squirrel fur. Often nervous, especially in violent situations. Grand Maester Pycelle considers him young (wiki:Colemon).

## Narrative Arc

Colemon served as Jon Arryn's maester in King's Landing. When Jon fell ill, Colemon treated him with purging and pepper juice, but Pycelle sent Colemon away — Pycelle later admitted he ensured Jon's death by removing Colemon's care (wiki:Colemon). After Jon's death, Colemon returned to the Eyrie with Lysa's household. He tends to the sickly Robert Arryn, carefully managing sweetsleep dosages due to its cumulative toxicity (wiki:Colemon). He is nervous about Littlefinger's influence and Marillion's torture but remains dutiful. In TWOW, he continues treating Robert during the descent to the Gates of the Moon (wiki:Colemon).
