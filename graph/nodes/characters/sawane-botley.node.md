## Origins

During [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion), the timber-and-wattle castle of [House Botley](wiki:House_Botley) was burned to the ground by King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) during a [battle at Lordsport](wiki:Battle_at_Lordsport). Lord Sawane rebuilt in stone after the war.(wiki:Sawane_Botley.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Sawane bought [a difficult horse](wiki:Smiler) at [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) in [298 AC](wiki:298_AC).(wiki:Sawane_Botley.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Lord Sawane sells the horse to [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy), who gives it the name [Smiler](wiki:Smiler). As part of the transaction, Theon takes on [Wex Pyke](wiki:Wex_Pyke), the bastard son of Sawane's half brother [Sargon](wiki:Sargon_Botley), as his squire.(wiki:Sawane_Botley.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Feast for Crows

After King [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy)'s death, his brother, [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), returns to proclaim himself [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands). Sawane refuses to do him fealty, stating that the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair) belongs to [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) by rights. Euron has Lord Sawane drowned.(wiki:Sawane_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Sawane_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Sawane_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Sawane's sons are dispossessed of the lordship. Half of the lands are given to [House Wynch](wiki:House_Wynch), while the remnants are left to Sawane's brother, [Germund Botley](wiki:Germund_Botley), the new [Lord of Lordsport](wiki:Lord_of_Lordsport).(wiki:Sawane_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) intends to restore Sawane's lands to his eldest son and heir, [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley).(wiki:Sawane_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
