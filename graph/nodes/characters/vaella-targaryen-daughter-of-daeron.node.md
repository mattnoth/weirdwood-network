## Identity

Princess Vaella Targaryen, called Vaella the Simple, was the daughter of Prince Daeron Targaryen (the Drunken) and Kiera of Tyrosh (wiki:Vaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daeron), cite: TWOIAF).

## Origins

Born in 222 AC to Prince Daeron the Drunken, the eldest son of King Maekar I Targaryen, and Kiera of Tyrosh, the widow of Prince Valarr Targaryen (wiki:Vaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daeron)).

## Appearances & Description

Vaella was sweet but simple-minded (wiki:Vaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daeron), cite: TWOIAF).

## Narrative Arc

Her father died before his own father King Maekar, leaving Vaella as his only heir. When Maekar died in 233 AC in battle, a Great Council was called. As the only child of Maekar's eldest son, Vaella's claim to the Iron Throne was considered but dismissed immediately by the lords — on account of her mental capacity and gender. The Great Council ultimately chose her uncle Aegon V (wiki:Vaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daeron), cite: ACOK, TWOIAF).
