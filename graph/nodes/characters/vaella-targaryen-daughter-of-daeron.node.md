## Origins

Vaella was born in [222 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) to Prince [Daeron the Drunken](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)), the eldest son of King [Maekar I Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen),(wiki:Vaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daeron).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf414.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) and [Kiera of Tyrosh](wiki:Kiera_of_Tyrosh), the widow of Prince [Valarr Targaryen](wiki:Valarr_Targaryen), Daeron's cousin.(wiki:Vaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daeron).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf101.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Her father, then [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone) and Maekar's heir, died before his own father when Vaella was still young, leaving her as his only heir.(wiki:Vaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daeron).cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

When King Maekar died in [233 AC](wiki:233_AC) in battle against [a rebel lord](wiki:Peake_Uprising), a [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council) was called to settle the murky question of his successor. As the only child of Maekar's eldest son, Vaella's claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) was considered, but dismissed immediately by the lords before any of the other candidates,(wiki:Vaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daeron).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf414.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) both on account of her mental capacity and gender. Ultimately, the Great Council instead chose Vaella's uncle, Maekar's youngest son [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen), and declared him Aegon the Fifth of His Name.(wiki:Vaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daeron).cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

When explaining the family history of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch)'s [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)), an uncle of Vaella's, to his personal steward [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) told Snow of Vaella's father's death, her mental faculties, and how she was quickly passed over at the Great Council of [233 AC](wiki:233_AC) in favor of her other uncle, Aegon.(wiki:Vaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daeron).cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
