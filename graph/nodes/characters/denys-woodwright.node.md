## Origins

After Lord Corlys was imprisoned under charges of treason for warning Ser [Addam Velaryon](wiki:Addam_Velaryon) of his impending arrest, Ser Denys and Ser [Thoron True](wiki:Thoron_True), another sworn sword to the Sea Snake, cut their way into the dungeons of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) to liberate their lord. However, their plans had been betrayed to [Lady Misery](wiki:Mysaria) by a [whore](wiki:Whore) Ser Thoron had been bedding.
