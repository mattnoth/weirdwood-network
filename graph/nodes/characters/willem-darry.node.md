## Origins

When a new master-at-arms of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) needed appointing, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), wished to name his brother Ser [Tygett](wiki:Tygett_Lannister) to the post, but King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) chose Willem instead.(wiki:Willem_Darry.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) When young Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) decided that he must be a knight, he went to Willem to be trained.(wiki:Willem_Darry.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

After King Aerys II learned of the death of Prince Rhaegar at the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident), the king sent Queen [Rhaella Targaryen](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen) and Prince [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone),(wiki:Willem_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)(wiki:Willem_Darry.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) along with Willem.(wiki:Willem_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) After Aerys's death in the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing) and Rhaella's death in childbirth, the garrison of Dragonstone considered selling the two Targaryen children, Viserys and the infant Princess [Daenerys](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), to the new king, [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). However, before the garrison could come to a decision and before [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) arrived for his [assault on Dragonstone](wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone), Willem and four loyal men broke into the nursery at Dragonstone, and escaped with Viserys and Daenerys under cover of darkness, sailing for the safety of the [Braavosian coast](wiki:Braavosian_coast).(wiki:Willem_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)(wiki:Willem_Darry.cite_ref-Racok0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Willem and the Targaryen children then settled in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), living in a [house with a red door](wiki:House_with_the_red_door).(wiki:Willem_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) While in Braavos, Willem was visited by Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell), and together, with the [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos) as witness, they signed a [pact](wiki:Secret_marriage_pact) promising that Viserys Targaryen would one day wed Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) in return for the support of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) in winning Viserys the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Willem_Darry.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) However, Willem became ill from a wasting sickness when Daenerys was five years old (either in [289 AC](wiki:289_AC) or [290 AC](wiki:290_AC)).(wiki:Willem_Darry.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_daenerys_targaryen.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Willem_Darry.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) He slowly wasted away, dying an unknown amount of time later, after which the servants of the house stole their money, and Viserys and Daenerys were thrown into the streets to fend for themselves.(wiki:Willem_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

## Appearances & Description

Willem was staunchly loyal to [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen). By the end of his life, he was a grey bear of a man who was half-blind(wiki:Willem_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) and walked with the aid of a stick.(wiki:Willem_Darry.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He had a gruff, kind voice, and big wrinkled hands, soft as old leather.(wiki:Willem_Darry.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) remembers him as a gentle,(wiki:Willem_Darry.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) "sweet old bear,"(wiki:Willem_Darry.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) who roared orders at the servants from his sickbed, but was always kind to her.(wiki:Willem_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

In a [vision](wiki:Vision) at the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying) in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) sees a room within the [house with the red door](wiki:House_with_the_red_door), where Willem Darry beckons to her, but she remembers that he is dead and rejects it as false.(wiki:Willem_Darry.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

### A Dance With Dragons

In [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), Daenerys Targaryen is visited by Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell), who presents her with a parchment. The parchment details the [secret pact](wiki:Secret_marriage_pact) contracting a marriage between [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) and [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell), and is signed by Willem Darry for the Targaryens and [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) for [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Willem_Darry.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)
