## Identity

Prince Baelon Targaryen, known as Baelon the Brave and the Spring Prince, was the fourth-born child of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen and Queen Alysanne Targaryen (wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)). He served as Prince of Dragonstone and Hand of the King.

## Origins

Born in 57 AC (track_b: Born). Son of Jaehaerys I Targaryen (track_b: Father) and Alysanne Targaryen (track_b: Mother). Named "the Spring Prince" for the season of his birth (wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiance).

## Narrative Arc

Baelon was a notable knight and prince who served as Hand of the King to his father Jaehaerys I. He married his sister Princess Alyssa Targaryen and fathered three sons: Viserys, Daemon, and Aegon. He died in 101 AC aged 44 at the Tower of the Hand in King's Landing (track_b: Died). His death precipitated the Great Council of 101 AC to determine the succession (wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)).
