## Origins

### Early life

Baelon was born in [57 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), two days after the start of spring had been announced by the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel). As such, he was dubbed the *Spring Prince*. Baelon was [named after](wiki:Named_after) [Baelon Targaryen](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aerys)), who had ruled as [Lord of Dragonstone](wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone) before [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest). The year after his birth, Baelon was present at the [tourney at King's Landing on the anniversary of Jaehaerys's coronation](wiki:Tourney_at_King's_Landing_on_the_anniversary_of_the_king's_coronation).(wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Baelon admired his older brother [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) growing up. As soon as Baelon could walk, he began following Aemon around everywhere he went. When Aemon began training at arms with a wooden sword, Baelon was deemed to be too young to join. Regardless, Baelon brought a stick to the yard, and began whacking at Aemon. Baelon and Aemon would become very close, training together at arms every day in spirited contests which drew large crowds of spectators.(wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

The first time Baelon visited the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit), he hit [Balerion](wiki:Balerion) on the snout, causing Ser [Samgood](wiki:Samgood_of_Sour_Hill) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) to remark that he was either brave or [mad](wiki:Targaryen_madness). Ever since, Baelon was also known as *Baelon the Brave*.(wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

In [72 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) Baelon took part in a squires melee during a [tourney held to celebrate Lord Darklyn's wedding](wiki:Tourney_at_Duskendale_in_honor_of_Lord_Darklyn's_wedding), in which he was defeated by Aemon.(wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) Baelon won his spurs the following year, at the age of sixteen, taking part in a [tourney at Old Oak](wiki:Tourney_at_Old_Oak), entering the lists as a [mystery knight](wiki:Mystery_knight) called the *Silver Fool*. Baelon unhorsed Lord [Rowan](wiki:House_Rowan), Ser [Alyn Ashford](wiki:Alyn_Ashford), both [Fossoway twins](wiki:Fossoway_twins), and Lord Oakheart's heir, Ser [Denys](wiki:Denys_Oakheart), before falling to Ser [Rickard Redwyne](wiki:Rickard_Redwyne). Ser Rickard helped him to his feet, unmasked him, and knighted him on the spot. Prince Baelon remained at Old Oak only for the feast. Afterwards, he returned to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), where he became a [dragonrider](wiki:Dragonrider) by claiming the [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar).(wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

That same year, at the request of his father, Baelon began training his younger brother [Vaegon](wiki:Vaegon_Targaryen). They continued to train for a year, but Vaegon had no skill and no interest in his training. After Baelon made Vaegon fight against their sister [Alyssa](wiki:Alyssa_Targaryen), resulting in Vaegon leaving the yard humiliated, their trainings were at an end.

### Marriage

Baelon had always been close to his sister Alyssa, and married her in [75 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). The sounds that came from their bedchamber during the bedding were a great amusement to the wedding guests. Their first child, a son who they named [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) (possibly in honor of [their late uncle](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I))), was born in [77 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). Their second son, [Daemon](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen), was born in [81 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

In [83 AC](wiki:83_AC) Baelon, together with his brother Aemon and father, King Jaehaerys I, ended the attempted [invasion](wiki:Fourth_Dornish_War) of the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) by Prince [Morion Martell](wiki:Morion_Martell) of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). Mounted atop their [dragons](wiki:Dragon), they destroyed the Dornish fleet before it could even make landfall. Upon their return to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Jaehaerys, Aemon, and Baelon received a riotous welcome as they had won a war without losing a single man, something not even [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) had been able to boast.(wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

When Alyssa became pregnant for a third time, Baelon told his brother Aemon he was praying for a daughter. In [84 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Alyssa gave birth to a son, [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Baelon)), after a difficult labor. Alyssa did not recover from Aegon's birth, however, and died within six months. Aegon died half a year later, just shy of his first [nameday](wiki:Nameday). Her death devastated Baelon, who always continued to honor her memory.

A few years after Alyssa's death, Baelon's young, ambitious sister [Viserra](wiki:Viserra_Targaryen) tried to seduce him, hoping to end her betrothal to Lord [Theomore Manderly](wiki:Theomore_Manderly). She sneaked into his bedchamber, and waited naked in his bed for him to arrive. When Baelon found her, he was unresponsive to her advances, and instead sent her away.(wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

### Prince of Dragonstone

In [92 AC](wiki:92_AC), Prince Aemon was slain on [Tarth](wiki:Tarth) by one of the [Myrish](wiki:Myr) exiles who occupied the eastern part of the isle. Baelon flew on [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) to Tarth to avenge his brother, and fought against the exiles with [Dark Sister](wiki:Dark_Sister). He was cheered by crowds when he returned to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

Baelon was named [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone) and heir to the throne by King Jaehaerys after Aemon's death, ahead of Aemon's daughter and heir, Princess [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aemon)).(wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) In [100 AC](wiki:100_AC), Baelon was named [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), replacing Ser [Ryam Redwyne](wiki:Ryam_Redwyne) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

In [101 AC](wiki:101_AC), Baelon complained of a stitch in his side during a hunting trip. The pain worsened after he returned to King's Landing, and he eventually became bedridden in the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand). After five days of [illness](wiki:Illness), Baelon died of a burst belly.(wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-5) His funeral pyre was personally lit by his father, King Jaehaerys.(wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)(wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

## Appearances & Description

At birth, Baelon was smaller than his older brother, [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)). He was louder and lustier, however.(wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
