## Appearances & Description

Ser Jared is a tall, stooped man of fifty years.(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He is balding and pockmarked.(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The arrogant Jared(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) is lean of limb and clean-shaved, but for a thin grey mustache like a [Myrish](wiki:Myr) stiletto.(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Jared attends the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with six of his kin—Ser [Hosteen](wiki:Hosteen_Frey), Ser [Danwell](wiki:Danwell_Frey), Ser [Emmon](wiki:Emmon_Frey), Ser [Theo](wiki:Theo_Frey), Ser [Perwyn](wiki:Perwyn_Frey), and [Martyn Rivers](wiki:Martyn_Rivers).(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Jared attends Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) at the parley with Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) at the [Twins](wiki:Twins). He is nearby when Catelyn informs her son, [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), of the alliance negotiated with Walder.(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

### A Clash of Kings

Jared is a captive of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), together with his brothers Ser [Hosteen](wiki:Hosteen_Frey) and Ser [Danwell Frey](wiki:Danwell_Frey) and his half-brother [Ronel Rivers](wiki:Ronel_Rivers).(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Tywin holds captives from the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork) at the [Tower of Dread](wiki:Tower_of_Dread) in [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) observes the four [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) exercising in the [Flowstone Yard](wiki:Flowstone_Yard) of Harrenhal and then being ransomed by their kin.(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

Jared is a member of Ser [Aenys Frey](wiki:Aenys_Frey)'s contingent after the [fall of Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal), which is now controlled by Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton).(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Ser [Tytos Frey](wiki:Tytos_Frey), Jared's son, and Ser [Garse Goodbrook](wiki:Garse_Goodbrook), the husband of Jared's daughter [Kyra](wiki:Kyra_Frey), are killed during the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Along with [Rhaegar Frey](wiki:Rhaegar_Frey) and [Symond Frey](wiki:Symond_Frey), Jared travels on the *[Lionstar](wiki:Lionstar)* to [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) via [Sisterton](wiki:Sisterton) to return the bones of Ser [Wendel Manderly](wiki:Wendel_Manderly), who was killed in the Red Wedding, to his father, Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly).(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) The Freys also want to ensure White Harbor bends the knee to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) They offer Wyman a Frey bride and betrothals of Rhaegar to his granddaughter [Wynafryd](wiki:Wynafryd_Manderly) and [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)) to Wyman's other granddaughter, [Wylla](wiki:Wylla_Manderly).(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

When Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) arrives in White Harbor persuade [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) to support [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), the three Freys are already present. While Wendel was actually murdered by the Freys, Jared claims the Red Wedding was the doing of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), the [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), and that Wendel died defending Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) from Robb, who had [changed into a rabid wolf](wiki:Skinchanger). A shocked Davos calls Jared a liar. Jared draws his sword and is close to attacking Davos, but Wyman says he will have no bloodshed in the [Merman's Court](wiki:Merman's_Court).(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3)

The angered Wyman pretends to agree with the Freys, as the Iron Throne holds hostage his surviving son and heir, Ser [Wylis Manderly](wiki:Wylis_Manderly), at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal). Wyman fakes Davos's death by having a criminal with similar features executed and his head dipped in tar so the Freys falsely believe him to be Davos.(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) As a reward for proving his loyalty, Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) has Wylis returned to White Harbor via [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool).(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

Wyman gives Jared, Rhaegar, and Symond palfreys as "[guest gifts](wiki:Guest_right)" when the three Freys depart White Harbor for [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark) of "[Arya Stark](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)" to [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow).(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) They disappear while traveling to [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton), however, despite the search efforts of Ramsay.(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

At [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) after Ramsay's wedding, Wyman presents [three huge pies](wiki:Frey_Pies/Theories) at the wedding feast while requesting a song from the singer [Abel](wiki:Mance_Rayder) about the [Rat Cook](wiki:Rat_Cook).(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Aenys and Ser [Hosteen Frey](wiki:Hosteen_Frey) suspect Wyman of foul play.(wiki:Jared_Frey.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
