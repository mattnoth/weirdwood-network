## Identity

Elinor Tyrell is a member of a junior branch of House Tyrell and a lady-in-waiting of Margaery Tyrell. She is betrothed to Alyn Ambrose, whom she is to marry once he becomes a knight (wiki:Elinor_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6).

## Origins

Born roughly 286 AC in the Reach (track_b: Born). Daughter of Theodore Tyrell and Lia Serry (track_b: Father, Mother).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Tyrell (track_b: Allegiance). Serves as one of Margaery's closest companions and bedmaid.

## Appearances & Description

Elinor is willowy and witty. She is a maiden flowered and rules Margaery's younger cousins Megga and Alla by right of womanhood. She loves to flirt (wiki:Elinor_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos16).

## Narrative Arc

Elinor accompanies Margaery to King's Landing. Her betrothed Alyn Ambrose wore her favor during the Battle of the Blackwater (wiki:Elinor_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6). She befriends Sansa Stark when Margaery brings Sansa into their company. At Sansa's wedding to Tyrion, Elinor and her cousins ignore the bride to protest the match. She remains in King's Landing through Margaery's marriages to Joffrey and Tommen (wiki:Elinor_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6, wiki:Elinor_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12).
