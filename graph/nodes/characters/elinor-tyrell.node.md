## Appearances & Description

Elinor is willowy and witty. She is a maiden [flowered](wiki:Flowered), and rules Margaery's younger cousins, [Megga](wiki:Megga_Tyrell) and [Alla](wiki:Alla_Tyrell), by right of womanhood. Elinor loves to flirt.(wiki:Elinor_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Elinor's betrothed, [Alyn Ambrose](wiki:Alyn_Ambrose), wore her favor during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Elinor_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Elinor is one of the ladies attending Lady [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Elinor_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) She is a [bedmaid](wiki:Bedmaid), friend, and confidante of Margaery. Elinor becomes one of [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark)'s friends when Margaery brings her into their company.(wiki:Elinor_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

At [the wedding feast](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark) for Sansa and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), though the Tyrells attend, Elinor and her cousins ignore the bride to show their dissatisfaction with the match, as the Tyrells had hoped for Sansa to marry [Willas Tyrell](wiki:Willas_Tyrell) instead. Elinor dances with her betrothed, [Alyn Ambrose](wiki:Alyn_Ambrose).(wiki:Elinor_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

At [the wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of Margaery and King [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), Sansa compliments Elinor on her gown.(wiki:Elinor_Tyrell.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Elinor attends [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Tommen_I_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) of Margaery and King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), during which she dances with Tommen and shares a cup of [wine](wiki:Wine) with [Aurane Waters](wiki:Aurane_Waters).(wiki:Elinor_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Taena of Myr](wiki:Taena_of_Myr) tells Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) that her maid, [Senelle](wiki:Senelle), brings messages to Elinor and her cousins.(wiki:Elinor_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Margaery's cousins tease Ser [Osney Kettleblack](wiki:Osney_Kettleblack) about his nose.(wiki:Elinor_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Ser [Osmund Kettleblack](wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack) tells Cersei that Margaery often reads with Elinor, and that Ser [Tallad the Tall](wiki:Tallad_the_Tall) desires Elinor and her cousin [Alla](wiki:Alla_Tyrell).(wiki:Elinor_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Alyn accompanies Elinor during one of Margaery's rides.(wiki:Elinor_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Due to Cersei's plotting,(wiki:Elinor_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Elinor is arrested by the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) for trumped-up crimes of fornication, along with [Megga Tyrell](wiki:Megga_Tyrell) and Margaery, and held captive in the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor). When examined by a [septa](wiki:Septa), Elinor's [maidenhead](wiki:Maiden) is found to be broken.(wiki:Elinor_Tyrell.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Almost all of the men named as Margaery's lovers have denied the accusation or recanted, so she and her cousins, including Elinor, are handed over by the [High Septon](wiki:High_Sparrow) to the custody of Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly). Randyll swears a holy oath to return them for their trial.(wiki:Elinor_Tyrell.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
