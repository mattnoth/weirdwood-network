## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Clubfoot Karl is a member of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), the force that leaves [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) in search of the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings). One of the conspirators who intend to kill Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) near the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men), Karl is to cut the tethers among the horse lines.(wiki:Clubfoot_Karl.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Karl survives the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist). After complaining about food provided by [Craster](wiki:Craster) and his wives, Karl supports [Dirk](wiki:Dirk) during the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep).(wiki:Clubfoot_Karl.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

### A Feast for Crows

Karl remains at [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep) after the betrayal, along with a few other former members of the Night's Watch.(wiki:Clubfoot_Karl.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance With Dragons

Five surviving betrayers wander in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest), planning to head south over the [Wall](wiki:Wall). However, the traitors are killed by [Coldhands](wiki:Coldhands) and his ravens. [Summer](wiki:Summer), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark)'s [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), finds the bodies and eats parts of the meat.(wiki:Clubfoot_Karl.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) It is unknown if Karl is among them.
