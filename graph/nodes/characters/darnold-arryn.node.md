## Origins

Lord Arryn was present at the [wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) of Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) and Dowager Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC), but it is unknown if this was Darnold.(wiki:Darnold_Arryn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

In [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), Lord Darnold and his brother, Ser [Rymond](wiki:Rymond_Arryn), were killed at the hands of [mountain clan](wiki:Mountain_clans_of_the_Vale) raiders they had unwisely pursued into the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon). He was succeeded as Lord of the Eyrie by his nephew, the ten-year-old [Rodrik](wiki:Rodrik_Arryn).(wiki:Darnold_Arryn.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
