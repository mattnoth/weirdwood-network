## Identity

Darnold Arryn was Lord of the Eyrie, Defender of the Vale, Warden of the East, and head of House Arryn at the beginning of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen's reign (wiki:Darnold_Arryn).

## Origins

Born 31 AC (track_b: Born). Died 54 AC in the Mountains of the Moon (track_b: Died).

## Allegiances

## Narrative Arc

In 54 AC, Darnold and his brother Ser Rymond were killed by mountain clan raiders they had unwisely pursued into the Mountains of the Moon. He was succeeded by his ten-year-old nephew Rodrik (wiki:Darnold_Arryn).
