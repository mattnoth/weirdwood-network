## Identity

Nymor Martell was the ruling Prince of Dorne and head of House Martell during the reign of King Aegon I Targaryen (wiki:Nymor_Martell).

## Origins

Born ~47 BC. Son of Princess Meria Martell, the Yellow Toad of Dorne. Died 13–23 AC (track_b: Born, Died, Mother).

## Narrative Arc

During the War of Conquest, Dorne defied the Targaryens. In 4 AC, Aegon I began the First Dornish War, lasting until 13 AC. When Nymor's mother Meria died in 13 AC, he inherited rule of Dorne. Already old and failing, Nymor wanted peace. He sent his daughter Deria to King's Landing to negotiate — a peace between equals, not vassal and overlord. Aegon was about to refuse when Deria handed him Nymor's letter. Aegon clenched the Iron Throne so hard his hand bled, burned the letter, flew to Dragonstone on Balerion, and returned the next morning to sign the treaty. The letter's contents remain one of the great mysteries — theories include a simple plea, a list of dead lords, a promise to end captive Rhaenys's torment, or a threat to hire Faceless Men against Prince Aenys (wiki:Nymor_Martell).
