## Origins

During the civil war known as the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord Joseth Smallwood sided with the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) and [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen).(wiki:Joseth_Smallwood.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

In [130 AC](wiki:130_AC), the advance of the [westermen](wiki:Westerlands) through the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) was briefly halted at [Acorn Hall](wiki:Acorn_Hall) when Lord Joseth sallied forth to join Lord [Petyr Piper](wiki:Petyr_Piper) and the remains of his defeated host, but Petyr died in the [ensuring battle](wiki:Battle_at_Acorn_Hall) and Joseth fell back inside his castle.(wiki:Joseth_Smallwood.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

At the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton), Lord Smallwood joined the host led by Ser [Addam Velaryon](wiki:Addam_Velaryon) and scraped together fresh levies of greybeards and green boys, despite the grievous losses he had suffered in the [autumn](wiki:Autumn) battles.(wiki:Joseth_Smallwood.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
