## Origins

When Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake) became [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), he made his uncle Ser [Gedmund Peake](wiki:Gedmund_Peake) commander of the [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet) with the more experienced Ned Bean as his second-in-command. Ser Gedmund and Ned, with reinforcement from the [Velaryon](wiki:House_Velaryon) fleet commanded by Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon), were sent against [Racallio Ryndoon](wiki:Racallio_Ryndoon), the self-proclaimed [King of the Narrow Sea](wiki:King_of_the_Narrow_Sea). When Racallio allied with [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh), Ser Gedmund and Ned remained at [Tarth](wiki:Tarth) to await further instructions from Lord Unwin. Lord Alyn protested they would lose time, however, and left them behind. [Lord Alyn's attack](wiki:Lord_Alyn's_attack) was a great victory against the [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Ned_Bean.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
