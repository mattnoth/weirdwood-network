## Origins

In the great [tourney](wiki:Tourney) of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in [98 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), to celebrate the fiftieth year of King [Jaehaerys I](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen)'s reign, Ser Clement and Ser [Ryam Redwyne](wiki:Ryam_Redwyne) broke thirty lances against each other, before Jaehaerys proclaimed them co-champions. Their competition is often called the finest display of [jousting](wiki:Joust) in the history of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Clement_Crabb.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Clement_Crabb.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
