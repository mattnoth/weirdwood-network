## Origins

When King Jaehaerys was on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) during his [regency](wiki:Regency), he spent every morning until midday in the castle yard training with sword, shield, and any other [armament](wiki:Armament). Ser Merrell, along with his two sons, Ser [Elyas Scales](wiki:Elyas_Scales) the [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms), and [knights](wiki:Knight) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) sparred with the king.(wiki:Merrell_Bullock.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

When [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) was given [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) as her seat, Ser Merrell continued in his duties as the commander of the castle's garrison. In [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), after the theft of three [dragon eggs](wiki:Dragon_egg) from [Dreamfyre](wiki:Dreamfyre)'s clutch by [Elissa Farman](wiki:Elissa_Farman), Ser Merrell was suddenly relieved of his position, as Rhaena, furious by the betrayal by her former friend, became suspicious of everyone.(wiki:Merrell_Bullock.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
