## Origins

Ser Raymun's three older brothers all died fighting for the [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen) in the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Raymun_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Under Raymun, [House Darry](wiki:House_Darry) has secretly kept its loyalty to the Targaryens after [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Raymun_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Appearances & Description

Raymun and [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) have a wary relationship, since [House Darry](wiki:House_Darry) supported [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Raymun_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Raymun dislikes [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Raymun_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) goes missing after her [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf), [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf)), attacks Prince [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) along the [Trident](wiki:Trident). King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s royal party stays at [Darry](wiki:Darry) uninvited during the search for the girl. The king appropriates Raymun's audience chamber, sitting in his high seat,(wiki:Raymun_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) as well as his bedchamber.(wiki:Raymun_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Raymun keeps his emotions guarded when Arya is eventually brought before Robert.(wiki:Raymun_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) Raymun is not formally mentioned as "Lord of Darry", but Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) thinks of him as the castle's lord.(wiki:Raymun_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) It is possible that [Raymun's father](wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun)) was still alive, having retired from active rule in the years after the rebellion, with Raymun being the *de facto* lord.(wiki:Raymun_Darry.cite_ref-retirement_6-1)

In response to the raising of a [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) host at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock),(wiki:Raymun_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Raymun rides to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) with all his strength as Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) prepares the defenses of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Raymun_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) leads raids across the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) of the Trident.(wiki:Raymun_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), [Lord Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident), sends Raymun, Ser [Marq Piper](wiki:Marq_Piper), and Ser [Karyl Vance](wiki:Karyl_Vance) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with the survivors of [Sherrer](wiki:Sherrer) to ask the permission of King Robert to retaliate against the raiders. [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), sends Raymun with [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion), [Lord of Blackhaven](wiki:Lord_of_Blackhaven), to execute Gregor.(wiki:Raymun_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3)

Beric's party is ambushed by the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men), and Raymun is killed in the [battle at the Mummer's Ford](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford).(wiki:Raymun_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) According to a semi-canon source, his father, the current [Lord of Darry](wiki:Lord_of_Darry), dies in the battle as well.(wiki:Raymun_Darry.cite_ref-RPG_5-1) His son, [Lyman Darry](wiki:Lyman_Darry), becomes the new lord and is among the nobles assembled at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Raymun_Darry.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Lyman is killed by Gregor in the [sack of Darry](wiki:Sack_of_Darry).(wiki:Raymun_Darry.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Storm of Swords

[Harwin](wiki:Harwin) tells Arya that Gregor killed Raymun with one blow, cutting off his arm at the elbow and killing his horse.(wiki:Raymun_Darry.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) When the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) list the victims of House Lannister to [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane), [Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams) mentions "Ser Raymun Darry, Lord Darry, young Lord Darry"; it is possible that "Lord Darry" and "young Lord Darry" refer respectively to Raymun's father and son.(wiki:Raymun_Darry.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) recalls that his brother [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) had found portraits of all of the kings from [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) in [Darry](wiki:Darry)'s cellar; Raymun had removed the tapestries for King Robert's visit to the castle.(wiki:Raymun_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) While practicing in the castle's [godswood](wiki:Godswood), Jaime recounts to Ser [Ilyn Payne](wiki:Ilyn_Payne) that he had slept with his twin, Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), in Raymun's bed after a drunken Robert had passed out on the floor.(wiki:Raymun_Darry.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)
