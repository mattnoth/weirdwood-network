## Origins

In [10 AC](wiki:10_AC) during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), Lord [Manfred Hightower](wiki:Manfred_Hightower_(Aegon's_Conquest)) dispatched his eldest son, Ser [Addam](wiki:Addam_Hightower), to lead a host against Lord [Fowler](wiki:House_Fowler) at [Nightsong](wiki:Nightsong). With most of the Hightower strength away, Ser [Joffrey Dayne](wiki:Joffrey_Dayne) led a second army against [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). The young Garmon led a sortie against the [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen), but he was slain in battle by Ser Joffrey.(wiki:Garmon_Hightower.cite_ref-Rfab2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
