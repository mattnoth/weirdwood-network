## Origins

In [244 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), the young [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister_(son_of_Gerold)) [deflowered](wiki:Flowering) and impregnated [Alys Stackspear](wiki:Alys_Stackspear), the maiden daughter of [Lord Stackspear](wiki:Lord_Stackspear_(father_of_Alys)). Jason was compelled by his brother Lord [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister) to wed Alys, likely at the behest of Tytos' wife, Lady [Jeyne](wiki:Jeyne_Marbrand). However, Alys died giving birth to Damon.(wiki:Damon_Lannister_(son_of_Jason).cite_ref-3)

It is currently unknown whether Damon is still alive in *[A Song of Ice and Fire](wiki:A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire)*. The fact that [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) appointed his son Damion as castellan instead of him might indicate Damon has passed but it is not a definitive argument.
