## Identity

Damon Lannister is a nobleman of House Lannister and the only son of Ser Jason Lannister by his first wife, Alys Stackspear. He is a cousin and half-brother-in-law to Lord Tywin Lannister of Casterly Rock (wiki:Damon_Lannister_(son_of_Jason)).

## Origins

Born 244 or 245 AC (track_b: Born). His mother Alys Stackspear died giving birth to him (wiki:Damon_Lannister_(son_of_Jason)).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Lannister (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Westermen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

In 244 AC, the young Jason Lannister deflowered and impregnated Alys Stackspear, the maiden daughter of Lord Stackspear. Jason was compelled by his brother Lord Tytos Lannister to wed Alys, likely at the behest of Tytos's wife, Lady Jeyne. Alys died in childbirth giving Damon life. Damon later wed Ella Lannister, a distant cousin from House Lannister of Lannisport. He has one son, Ser Damion (wiki:Damon_Lannister_(son_of_Jason)).
