## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser Jon is among the nobles who declare for [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) and is present beneath the walls of [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge) when Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) arrives. At the feast that evening, Jon and Catelyn are seated beside each other, and he makes jests with her.(wiki:Jon_Fossoway.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) When the parley at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) between the brothers Renly and [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) breaks down and battle seems imminent, Jon offers to lead the van into battle.(wiki:Jon_Fossoway.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Jon goes over to Stannis's forces after Renly's death during the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)). The castle's castellan, Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose), proposes to settle the siege with single combat. Jon advises Stannis to take him up on the offer. Jon is prepared to take the challenge himself, although he admits that he is not as good as Lord [Bryce Caron](wiki:Bryce_Caron) or Ser [Guyard Morrigen](wiki:Guyard_Morrigen).(wiki:Jon_Fossoway.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Jon fights for Stannis at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater) but is captured by [Lothor Brune](wiki:Lothor_Brune). He bends the knee to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Jon_Fossoway.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Jon is a [sworn sword](wiki:Sworn_sword) of Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell).(wiki:Jon_Fossoway.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Jon's wife, [Janna Fossoway](wiki:Janna_Fossoway), [hawks](wiki:Hawk) with [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Jon_Fossoway.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
