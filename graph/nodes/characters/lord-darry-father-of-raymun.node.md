## Origins

In Lord Darry's days, [House Darry](wiki:House_Darry) stood high in King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen)'s favor.(wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun).cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) At the start of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), the Darrys sided with [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) instead of their liege lord, [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully).(wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun).cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Three of Lord Darry's sons died in the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun).cite_ref-Ragot16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) With the fall of the Targaryens, the Darrys lost half their lands, most of their wealth, and almost all their power.(wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun).cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

In [298 AC](wiki:298_AC), [Castle Darry](wiki:Darry) is run by Ser [Raymun Darry](wiki:Raymun_Darry).(wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun).cite_ref-Ragot16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) It is possible that Lord Darry was still alive, having retired from active rule in the years after the rebellion, with Raymun being the *de facto* lord.(wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun).cite_ref-retirement_5-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser [Raymun Darry](wiki:Raymun_Darry) dies in the [battle at the Mummer's Ford](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford).(wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun).cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) According to a semi-canon source, Lord Darry dies in the battle as well.(wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun).cite_ref-RPG_4-1) Raymun's son, [Lyman Darry](wiki:Lyman_Darry), is in attendance when [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) is proclaimed [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun).cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Clash of Kings

After the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), Robb allows the riverlords to return to their lands. Lyman [recaptures Castle Darry](wiki:Recapture_of_Darry), but he is killed less than a fortnight later in the [sack of Darry](wiki:Sack_of_Darry) by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane). Lyman is not formally called "Lord of Darry", but Ser [Brynden Blackfish](wiki:Brynden_Blackfish), [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and a semi-canon source refer to him as Darry's lord.(wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun).cite_ref-RPG_4-2)(wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun).cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun).cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

With Lyman's death, the male line of House Darry is extinguished.(wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun).cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) The female line still lives through her aunts [Mariya](wiki:Mariya_Darry) and [Jeyne Darry](wiki:Jeyne_Darry),(wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun).cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) both married to members of [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey).(wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun).cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) A [bastard](wiki:Bastard) cousin remains as well.(wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun).cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)

### A Storm of Swords

When the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) lists victims of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) to the captured [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane), [Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams) includes "Ser Raymun, Lord Darry, young Lord Darry".(wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun).cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) It is possible that "Lord Darry" and "young Lord Darry" refer respectively to Raymun's father and son.(wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun).cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

### A Feast for Crows

Mariya's daughter, [Amerei Frey](wiki:Amerei_Frey), becomes Lady of Darry through her marriage to Darry's new lord, [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister). Lancel does not show any interest in his lordship or wife, though. Mariya agrees with Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) that the smallfolk will not abandon the brotherhood until they come to love Lancel as much as they once loved her father.(wiki:Lord_Darry_(father_of_Raymun).cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
