## Appearances & Description

Squat and round, Harma Dogshead is a huge woman with white cheeks.(wiki:Harma_Dogshead.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Harma hates [dogs](wiki:Dog), and she kills one every fortnight to create a new totem for her banner.(wiki:Harma_Dogshead.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) She owns pigs.(wiki:Harma_Dogshead.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Origins

Before [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) made peace between them, Harma once warred with [Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt).(wiki:Harma_Dogshead.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
Harma has become a well known raider among the brothers of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Harma_Dogshead.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Harma the Dogshead is among the leaders of [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder)'s army that the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) are wary of.(wiki:Harma_Dogshead.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Harma leads the van of the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) army that marches south down the [Milkwater](wiki:Milkwater). [Goady](wiki:Goady) creeps up on her camp and spots her with five hundred horsemen. [Thoren Smallwood](wiki:Thoren_Smallwood) convinces [Tumberjon](wiki:Tumberjon) not to shoot Harma with an arrow.(wiki:Harma_Dogshead.cite_ref-Rasos0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Harma is present when the free folk discover the remains of the camp of the Night's Watch following the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist). She is skeptical when [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) reveals that the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) is led by [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), and she smirks when [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte) says she has been sleeping with Jon.(wiki:Harma_Dogshead.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
During the wildling assault on the [Wall](wiki:Wall), Harma is spotted near [Woodswatch-by-the-Pool](wiki:Woodswatch-by-the-Pool).(wiki:Harma_Dogshead.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Harma returns from her raids and feints to the main encampment of Mance's army by [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Harma_Dogshead.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) When Jon arrives at Mance's tent, she urges Mance to kill him.(wiki:Harma_Dogshead.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) When [rangers](wiki:Rangers) from [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea) appear, Mance has Harma ride against them with thirty raiders. The rangers are followed by the [southron](wiki:Southron) host of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), however, and one column of armored [knights](wiki:Knight) smashes Harma. Later in the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), Jon sees someone waving Harma's head on a pole.(wiki:Harma_Dogshead.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Harma's brother [Halleck](wiki:Halleck) kneels before Stannis and yields her pigs.(wiki:Harma_Dogshead.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
