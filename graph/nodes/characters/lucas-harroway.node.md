## Origins

During the reign of King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen), Lucas was named the [Lord of Harrenhal](wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal) after the violent death of Lord [Gargon Qoherys](wiki:Gargon_Qoherys) in [37 AC](wiki:37_AC).(wiki:Lucas_Harroway.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) In [39 AC](wiki:39_AC), he saw his daughter Lady [Alys](wiki:Alys_Harroway) wed to Prince [Maegor Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) in a [Valyrian](wiki:Valyria) [wedding ceremony](wiki:Marriage) on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). As Maegor was already married to Lady [Ceryse Hightower](wiki:Ceryse_Hightower), the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aenys_I)) denounced the marriage as "sin and fornication" and condemning Alys as the "this whore of Harroway". Pious lords across the realm began to turn against the crown and in a fruitless effort to pacify the Faith, Aenys exiled Maegor and Alys to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos).(wiki:Lucas_Harroway.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Aenys further exacerbated the situation in [41 AC](wiki:41_AC) by [incestuously](wiki:Incest) wedding his daughter Princess [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)), to his son and heir, Prince [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)). The young couple embarked on a [royal progress](wiki:Royal_progress) across the realm and upon reaching Harrenhal, Lord Lucas refused to admit them to his castle unless they agreed to acknowledge his daughter as Maegor's true and lawful wife. Aegon and Rhaena refused and were forced to spend a cold night in tents beneath Harrenhal. Aenys died the following year and Maegor claimed the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), making Alys queen.(wiki:Lucas_Harroway.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

In [43 AC](wiki:43_AC), Lucas was named [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) by Maegor, but it was said that Lucas did not have the ear of the king, who was instead ruled by Alys and his third wife, Queen [Tyanna of the Tower](wiki:Tyanna_of_the_Tower), and his mother, Queen Dowager [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen).(wiki:Lucas_Harroway.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) When Prince Aegon attempted to claim the crown from his uncle, Lucas raised his levies from Harrenhal and joined Lord [Tully](wiki:House_Tully) in support of Maegor, advancing on Prince Aegon's host from the north. In the resulting [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye), Aegon was slain by Maegor, securing the king's place on the Iron Throne.(wiki:Lucas_Harroway.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Lucas_Harroway.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) While Maegor focused on the completion of the construction of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), Lucas was left to govern the realm for a while.(wiki:Lucas_Harroway.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

In [44 AC](wiki:44_AC), after Alys had a stillborn, monstrously deformed baby, Queen Tyanna convinced Maegor that the boy had not been of his seed, but the result of one of Alys's many affairs, and that Lord Lucas himself had helped his daughter conceive a son for the king by sending men of proven fertility to her chambers. Tyanna provided the names of the men she had supposedly been sleeping with, with one man found with gold paid to him by Lord Harroway. The questioning and torture was done in secret and swiftly so the Harroways had no inkling of the king's suspicions until the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) was dispatched to seize the Harroways. Alys was taken and tortured to death, while Lord Harroway's children, brothers, and nephews at King's Landing were executed. Lucas himself was thrown from the roof of the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand) by [knights](wiki:Knight) of the Kingsguard, while he was inspecting the tower's construction.(wiki:Lucas_Harroway.cite_ref-5) Maegor then marched upon [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), held by his unsuspecting nephew, and [Lord Harroway's Town](wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town), and executed every man, woman, and child with a drop of Harroway blood, thus completing the destruction of [House Harroway](wiki:House_Harroway).(wiki:Lucas_Harroway.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Lucas_Harroway.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)
