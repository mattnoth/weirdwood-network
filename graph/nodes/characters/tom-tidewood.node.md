## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Tom accompanies [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) when the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) sails to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay). Victarion has Tom sew new robes in the colors of [House Greyjoy](wiki:House_Greyjoy) for [Moqorro](wiki:Moqorro). After Victarion kills the master of a fishing ketch, Tom and [Longwater Pyke](wiki:Longwater_Pyke) toss the body into the sea.(wiki:Tom_Tidewood.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
