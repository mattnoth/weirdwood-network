## Origins

Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) and Princess [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell) visited [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) on their way to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) where their [mother](wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran)) was to broker their [marriage](wiki:Marriage) to the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) twins, [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) and [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister). While there they met Baelor, whom Oberyn considered Elia's best suitor. Even though he liked Baelor more than the others, he called him 'Baelor Breakwind' after he farted in their presence and Elia could no longer look at him without laughing.(wiki:Baelor_Hightower.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
