## Identity

Lady Mellario, also known as Mellario of Norvos, is a noblewoman of the Free City of Norvos and the estranged wife of Prince Doran Martell of Dorne (wiki:Mellario).

## Origins

Born between 248 and 264 AC in Norvos (track_b: Born). Of Norvoshi culture (track_b: Culture).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Martell (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Norvoshi (track_b: Culture). Formerly `HOLDS_TITLE` Princess Consort of Dorne (track_b: Titles, qualifier: formerly).

## Narrative Arc

Mellario met Doran Martell during his visit to Norvos, catching his eye during a festival when the three bells were ringing and bears danced. She traveled to Dorne with Doran, escorted by her guardsman Areo Hotah. Despite marrying for love, the union was troubled by cultural differences. When Doran sent their eldest son Quentyn to be fostered by Lord Anders Yronwood, Mellario protested bitterly — Norvoshi do not foster out their children. When Doran planned to send Arianne to Tyrosh, Mellario threatened to harm herself. Doran relented on Arianne but Mellario never forgave him over Quentyn. She eventually returned to Norvos, leaving Doran and her children behind (wiki:Mellario).
