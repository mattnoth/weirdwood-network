## Origins

Mellario was born in [Norvos](wiki:Norvos).(wiki:Mellario.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Accompanied by her guardsman, [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah), she met Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell) when he was visiting the city of her birth. Doran caught her eye during a festival, where the [three bells of Norvos](wiki:Norvos) were ringing and the bears were dancing.(wiki:Mellario.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) When Doran returned to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), Mellario went with him, escorted by Areo Hotah.(wiki:Mellario.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) By [273 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Doran and Mellario were betrothed.(wiki:Mellario.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) They would marry not long after, as their first child, Princess [Arianne](wiki:Arianne_Martell) was born in [276 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Mellario.cite_ref-8) Two more children would follow: Prince [Quentyn](wiki:Quentyn_Martell), born in [281 AC](wiki:281_AC),(wiki:Mellario.cite_ref-9) and Prince [Trystane](wiki:Trystane_Martell), born in [287 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Mellario.cite_ref-10)

Despite the fact that Mellario and Doran had married for love, it was not a good [marriage](wiki:Marriage).(wiki:Mellario.cite_ref-SSM_11-0) Half of their marriage, Mellario, and Doran spent arguing with each other.(wiki:Mellario.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) The customs in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) were much different from those in [Norvos](wiki:Norvos), including the [fostering](wiki:Fosterage) away of children.(wiki:Mellario.cite_ref-SSM_11-1) When Doran sent their eldest son, Quentyn, to be fostered by Lord [Anders Yronwood](wiki:Anders_Yronwood), Mellario heavily protested. Norvoshi did not foster out their children, and Mellario believed her son to be much too young. Doran would send Quentyn away regardless, and Mellario never forgave him.(wiki:Mellario.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) When Doran planned to send their daughter, Arianne, to [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh), to serve as the [Archon](wiki:Archon)'s cupbearer, Mellario threatened to harm herself, should Doran steal another one of her children. This time, Doran relented, and Arianne remained in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Mellario.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

Despite this, however, the issues in their marriage were still unsolved. Since divorce in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) is uncommon,(wiki:Mellario.cite_ref-SSM_11-2) Mellario eventually returned to [Norvos](wiki:Norvos).(wiki:Mellario.cite_ref-14)(wiki:Mellario.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) There's some bitterness about this from Mellario, as Doran was the [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), which enabled his children to stay with him, while she had to leave them behind.(wiki:Mellario.cite_ref-SSM_11-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

When Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) discusses potential marriage candidates for his daughter, [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), he considers Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell). However, as Doran is still married to Mellario, and as Mellario is younger than her husband, and likely to outlive him, Tywin decides against that option.(wiki:Mellario.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Feast for Crows

During her imprisonment, Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) considers appealing to her mother Mellario for help. However, since Prince Doran has not listened to his wife in many years, and because Mellario is so far away, she is against trying to contact her.(wiki:Mellario.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) As punishment for his part in Arianne's conspiracy to crown Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon), Ser [Andrey Dalt](wiki:Andrey_Dalt) is sent to serve Lady Mellario for three years in [Norvos](wiki:Norvos).(wiki:Mellario.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

### A Dance with Dragons

When Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) is in [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), about to attempt stealing a [dragon](wiki:Dragon), he realises he wants to visit [Norvos](wiki:Norvos), to see where his mother came from, and to assure her that he has not forgotten her.(wiki:Mellario.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)
