## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) mocks some of the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions), including Pyg, when he leaves [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) after being released by Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton).(wiki:Pyg.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Feast for Crows

The Brave Companions scatter with the [capture of Harrenhal](wiki:Capture_of_Harrenhal) by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane).(wiki:Pyg.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Jaime hopes to find Pyg and [Shagwell](wiki:Shagwell) in Harrenhal's dungeon when he returns to the castle, but they have already departed.(wiki:Pyg.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Pyg, Shagwell, and [Timeon](wiki:Timeon) torture an outlaw from the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) and learn that [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) was with [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), and they later hear a similar story from the innkeep at the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn).(wiki:Pyg.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

In [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool), the sellswords purchase a map from [Dick Crabb](wiki:Dick_Crabb), who claims they can escape [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) from a smuggler's cove at [Crackclaw Point](wiki:Crackclaw_Point). With Nimble Dick's help, [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) finds them abandoned at the [Whispers](wiki:Whispers) during her search for [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark). Pyg is caked with dirt and wields a broken sword. During the ensuing battle, Brienne cuts Pyg's thigh with [Oathkeeper](wiki:Oathkeeper) and then stabs him in the throat.(wiki:Pyg.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) While Dick is buried in a grave, Brienne brings the heads of the three sellswords to Maidenpool and leaves their bodies for [crows](wiki:Crow).(wiki:Pyg.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)(wiki:Pyg.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Brienne later has nightmares involving Pyg, Shagwell, and Timeon.(wiki:Pyg.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)(wiki:Pyg.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
