## Identity

Vickon Greyjoy, Lord Reaper of Pyke, was chosen by the ironborn as their own lord paramount.

## Origins

After the demise of [House Hoare](wiki:House_Hoare) in the [burning of Harrenhal](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal), the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) became engulfed in chaos as claimants battled over the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair). In [2 AC](wiki:2_AC), [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) descended on the islands and put an end to the fighting.(wiki:Vickon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Afterward, the issue arose as to who should rule the Iron Islands and Aegon allowed the ironborn to choose their own lord paramount. They elected Vickon, the [Lord Reaper of Pyke](wiki:Lord_Reaper_of_Pyke), a famous captain who boasted a distinguished lineage and descent from the [Grey King](wiki:Grey_King).(wiki:Vickon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_greyjoys_of_pyke.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) Vickon did homage to Aegon and became the first [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Vickon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Ruling from [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), Vickon's writ only extended to the Iron Islands; the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) which had also been part of the kingdom of House Hoare had already been given to [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully) of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Vickon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Vickon commanded that the practice of reaving be confined to distant waters, forbidding his men to reave the shores of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms). He also angered the [priests of the Drowned God](wiki:Priests_of_the_Drowned_God) and many pious lords by allowing the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) to return to the Iron Islands. Vickon paid them no heed, maintaining his loyalty to Aegon the Conqueror. Vickon counselled his son, [Goren](wiki:Goren_Greyjoy), that only a fool would rise against Aegon and his [dragons](wiki:Dragon).(wiki:Vickon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_greyjoys_of_pyke.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

Vickon died in [33 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) and was succeeded by Goren.(wiki:Vickon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_greyjoys_of_pyke.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)

## Appearances & Description

A famous captain, Vickon was a stern, but cautious, ruler. He was loyal to [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) and considered himself [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen)'s man.(wiki:Vickon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_greyjoys_of_pyke.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
