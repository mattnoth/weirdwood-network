## Origins

In [155 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Ser Joffrey was riding with Prince [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen) near [Fairmarket](wiki:Fairmarket), when the prince's [horse](wiki:Horse) threw a shoe. After they found the local [smith](wiki:Smith), the prince liked his buxom young wife, [Megette](wiki:Megette). The prince absconded with the blacksmith's wife, buying her for seven [gold dragons](wiki:Currency), along with the threat of Ser Joffrey.(wiki:Joffrey_Staunton.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf411.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
