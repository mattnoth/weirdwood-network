## Identity

Balerion (cat). One of 2 characters named Balerion. An old black tomcat with a torn ear, believed to be the kitten once owned by Princess Rhaenys Targaryen. (wiki:Balerion_(cat))

## Edges

## Appearances & Description

The cat believed to be Balerion is an old black male [cat](wiki:Cat), with one chewed(wiki:Balerion_(cat).cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and torn ear(wiki:Balerion_(cat).cite_ref-Radwd72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) that leads him to appear one-eared.(wiki:Balerion_(cat).cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He is filthy and foul-tempered, and has been known to attack [ravens](wiki:Raven) in the [rookery](wiki:Rookery) of the Red Keep.(wiki:Balerion_(cat).cite_ref-Radwd72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Origins

The black tomcat in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) once stole a roast [quail](wiki:Quail) served to Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) at a dinner with King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Balerion_(cat).cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) He also once clawed Prince [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon)'s hand.(wiki:Balerion_(cat).cite_ref-Radwd72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

‎[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) is assigned by [Syrio Forel](wiki:Syrio_Forel) to catch every cat in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), and she succeeds with all, except for one mean one-eared black tomcat. She chases him halfway across the castle before she finally catches him, and kisses his forehead while he tries to claw her. Prince [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), his sister Princess [Myrcella](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon), and their [septa](wiki:Septa) observe this, and believe that Arya is a strange [peasant](wiki:Peasant) boy who does not belong in that part of the castle. The septa commands [red cloaks](wiki:Red_cloaks) to grab Arya, but she escapes.(wiki:Balerion_(cat).cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) speaks to Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) when he is imprisoned in the [black cells](wiki:Black_cells) of the Red Keep, and tells him about Princess [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Rhaegar)) and her kitten.(wiki:Balerion_(cat).cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), visiting the [godswood of the Red Keep](wiki:Godswood_of_the_Red_Keep) late at night, encounters the one-eared black tom. He brushes against her leg, then spits at her and runs away.(wiki:Balerion_(cat).cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Tommen, now king, is gifted three black kittens by his wife, Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell). The boy king's mother, Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), wishes they were not black, as she feels Princess Rhaenys's black kitten brought her bad luck.(wiki:Balerion_(cat).cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Arya Stark, as the [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) street urchin and fishmonger Cat of the Canals, encounters many cats, one of which reminds her of the one-eared cat she had chased in the Red Keep.(wiki:Balerion_(cat).cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

King Tommen Baratheon tells his uncle, Lord Regent [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), that the "bad cat" had been outside his window the previous night, but his kitten [Ser Pounce](wiki:Ser_Pounce) had hissed at him and he ran off. Cersei explains about the old black tom, who once attacked her son [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Balerion_(cat).cite_ref-Radwd72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)
