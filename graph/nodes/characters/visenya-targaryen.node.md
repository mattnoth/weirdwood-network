## Identity

Queen Visenya Targaryen was the older sister and wife of King Aegon I Targaryen, the first Lord of the Seven Kingdoms. Her younger sister Rhaenys was also married to Aegon. Visenya was a dragonrider who rode the dragon Vhagar (wiki:Visenya_Targaryen, cite: Fire & Blood, TWOIAF).

## Origins

Born in 29 BC on Dragonstone, the eldest child of Lord Aerion Targaryen and Lady Valaena Velaryon (wiki:Visenya_Targaryen, cite: Fire & Blood).

## Appearances & Description

Visenya possessed classical Valyrian features: long silver-gold hair (often braided or bound in rings) and purple eyes. A voluptuous and sensual woman, hers was a harsher, more austere beauty than her sister Rhaenys. Even those who loved her best considered her stern, serious, and unforgiving. Some claimed she dabbled in dark sorceries and played with poisons. She was both a dragonrider and a warrior, comfortable in silk and ringmail, and always wielded the Valyrian steel longsword Dark Sister when garbed for war (wiki:Visenya_Targaryen, cite: Fire & Blood, TWOIAF).

## Narrative Arc

Visenya trained at arms with Aegon from a young age and was wed to him following Valyrian custom — though it was said Aegon wed Visenya out of duty and Rhaenys out of desire. During Aegon's Conquest, Visenya subdued House Stokeworth, claimed the riches of Duskendale, and placed the Valyrian steel circlet on Aegon's head at his coronation. She accompanied the Targaryen fleet against Gulltown and the Vale, where she accepted the submission of the Eyrie after the Queen Regent flew Vhagar into the castle's inner courtyard. During the Field of Fire she joined Aegon and Rhaenys, all three dragons burning the Gardener-Lannister host (wiki:Visenya_Targaryen, cite: Fire & Blood, TWOIAF).

During the First Dornish War, Visenya was ambushed in the Boneway and took an arrow to the shoulder. After Rhaenys was killed in Dorne, Visenya urged Aegon to utterly destroy the Dornish, but Aegon refused. She proposed the Kingsguard after an assassination attempt on Aegon, modeling it on the Night's Watch and the Faith Militant. Visenya bore Aegon a son, Maegor, in 12 AC. After Aegon's death she served as regent and protector of her son. She died in 44 AC at Dragonstone (wiki:Visenya_Targaryen, cite: Fire & Blood, TWOIAF).
