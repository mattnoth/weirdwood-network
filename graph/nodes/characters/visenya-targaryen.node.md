## Identity

Visenya Targaryen (29 BC–44 AC) — child of Aerion Targaryen (son of Daemion) and Valaena Velaryon; married to Aegon I Targaryen; sworn to House Targaryen. One of 2 characters named Visenya Targaryen.

## Origins

### Early life

Born in [29 BC](wiki:Years_before_Aegon's_Conquest)(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Calculation_1-1) on the island [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)),(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rawoiafvisenya_targaryen.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Visenya was the eldest child of Lord [Aerion Targaryen](wiki:Aerion_Targaryen_(son_of_Daemion)) and Lady [Valaena Velaryon](wiki:Valaena_Velaryon). Her two younger siblings, [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) and [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen), were born within a few years following her birth.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-ssm_4-4) Since young age, Visenya trained together with Aegon at arms, and she became a skilled warrior.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)

As expected, Visenya was wed to her brother Aegon following Valyrian custom. However, Aegon took their younger sister as a wife as well. People claimed that he had wed Visenya out of duty, and Rhaenys out of desire. Prior to her wedding, Visenya became a dragonrider, having bonded with the dragon [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar).(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5)

In her youth Visenya was reported to have visited the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) of [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) and Lord [Redwyne](wiki:House_Redwyne) of the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor) together with her brother Aegon.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-6)

### The Conquest

When [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest) of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) began, Visenya sailed with her siblings from Dragonstone. While [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) began construction of the [Aegonfort](wiki:Aegonfort), Visenya subdued [House Stokeworth](wiki:House_Stokeworth) by setting ablaze the roofs of [Stokeworth](wiki:Stokeworth). After the defeat of [House Darklyn](wiki:House_Darklyn) in [Aegon's first test](wiki:Aegon's_first_test), Visenya claimed the riches of [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale), swelling the coffers of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen). Having conquered a dozen houses and secured the mouth of the [Blackwater](wiki:Blackwater_Rush), Aegon was crowned king at the Aegonfort. Visenya, now queen, placed a [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) circlet on Aegon's head while their sister [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) hailed him as the new [King of All Westeros](wiki:King_of_All_Westeros). Following the coronation, Visenya accompanied the Targaryen fleet to [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown) and the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn). When the Arryn fleet sunk a third of the Targaryen ships and captured many more in a [battle in the waters off Gulltown](wiki:Battle_in_the_waters_off_Gulltown), Visenya descended from the sky upon [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar), burning their ships.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-7)

Next, Visenya was sent to [Crackclaw Point](wiki:Crackclaw_Point) by Aegon. The local lords had learned of the death of [Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare), [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers), at the [burning of Harrenhal](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal) by Aegon, so the Crackclaws submitted quickly to Visenya, swearing oaths of fealty. Visenya took them as her own men, and from that day forth the lords owed their loyalty directly to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-8)(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Visenya met Rhaenys, Aegon, and the Targaryen host at [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept), from where they raced south. There, they met the combined hosts of the [Reach](wiki:Reach) and the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) in battle. Visenya and her siblings fought from dragonback, setting the dry grasses and stands of wheat on the battlefield afire. With more than four thousand men dead from the fire, and tens of thousands wounded by the flames, the battle became known as the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire). [Mern IX Gardener](wiki:Mern_IX_Gardener), [King of the Reach](wiki:King_of_the_Reach), died in battle while [Loren I Lannister](wiki:Loren_I_Lannister), [King of the Rock](wiki:King_of_the_Rock) fled, giving the Targaryens the victory.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-9) According to legend, Visenya thanked the gods that Loren had ridden forth to face the Targaryens when she saw [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), as she believed that [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame) would not have been able to dishearten the Lannister king if he had remained inside the Rock.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_casterly_rock.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Visenya had taken an arrow in the shoulder during the Field of Fire, but soon recovered. Following the surrender of King Loren, the three Targaryen siblings parted ways once more, but met soon again at the banks of the [Trident](wiki:Trident), where they awaited the host of [Torrhen Stark](wiki:Torrhen_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North). After Torrhen [bent the knee](wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man), Visenya parted from her siblings and returned to the Vale, where [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) had strengthened their defenses. A strong host had been positioned at the [Bloody Gate](wiki:Bloody_Gate), and the garrisons of [Stone](wiki:Stone_(waycastle)), [Snow](wiki:Snow_(waycastle)), and [Sky](wiki:Sky) had been tripled. However, Visenya bypassed these hosts by flying up to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) upon Vhagar's back. She landed in the courtyard, where she was met by [Ronnel Arryn](wiki:Ronnel_Arryn_(king)), the young [King of Mountain and Vale](wiki:King_of_Mountain_and_Vale). Ronnel's regent and mother, Lady [Sharra Arryn](wiki:Sharra_Arryn), surrendered the Eyrie to Visenya.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-10)

### First Dornish War

Visenya fought alongside her siblings in the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), which lasted from [4 AC](wiki:4_AC) to [13 AC](wiki:13_AC). After Rhaenys died in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) in [10 AC](wiki:10_AC), Visenya and Aegon, grief-stricken at her death, set every castle, keep, and holdfast in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) ablaze at least once, with the exception of [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) and its [shadow city](wiki:Shadow_city).

They also placed bounties on the heads of the Dornish lords. Although half a dozen and more of those lords were killed by assassins, only two of the killers lived to collect their reward. In retaliation, the Dornish lords hired catspaws themselves.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Visenya and her escort were once attacked in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), and she cut down the last would-be assassin down herself with [Dark Sister](wiki:Dark_Sister).(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Visenya also twice wielded her longsword to defend her brother,(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) with one occasion occurring in [10 AC](wiki:10_AC).(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) When Aegon argued that his guards were still sufficient for his protection, Visenya drew Dark Sister. Before [his guards](wiki:House_Targaryen_guards) could react, she had cut Aegon's cheek, pointing out to him that his guards were slow and lazy.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) This event led to the birth of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). Visenya modeled the vows of the Kingsguard upon the vows of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), and she selected the first seven members herself.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2)

In [13 AC](wiki:13_AC), [Deria Martell](wiki:Deria_Martell), [Princess of Dorne](wiki:Princess_of_Dorne), arrived in King's Landing in [13 AC](wiki:13_AC) with a delegation to discuss peace. With her, she brought the skull of Rhaenys's dragon, [Meraxes](wiki:Meraxes), which was ill-received by Visenya. Deria and Aegon eventually reached an agreement, striking a peace which ended the war.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

### Motherhood

The day-to-day governance of the realm was often left in the charge of Visenya and Rhaenys, and Aegon's councilors.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-3)

Rhaenys's death in Dorne in [10 AC](wiki:10_AC) shattered the health of her son [Aenys](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen), and people began to doubt as to whether he would live. As Visenya had not yet gotten pregnant by Aegon despite more than a decade of marriage, some had come to believe that she was barren, and even went as far as to suggest to Aegon that he should take a new wife. Although many young women were presented to Aegon, he refused to voice his opinion on the matter. In [11 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Visenya announced she was pregnant with a son, and the next year, she gave birth to Aegon's second son, [Maegor](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen)(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rawoiafmaegor_i_targaryen.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Visenya raised Maegor on Dragonstone, and gave him her [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) sword, [Dark Sister](wiki:Dark_Sister), as a [nameday](wiki:Nameday) present when the boy turned thirteen.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

In [23 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Aenys's first child, Princess [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)), was born. Shortly after Maegor turned twelve, Visenya proposed a betrothal between her son Maegor and Rhaena, to settle the issue of the royal succession, as Rhaena's birth had sparked the discussion about who was next in line behind Aenys: his own daughter, or his brother.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6) However, the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aegon_I)) protested, and suggested his own niece, Lady [Ceryse Hightower](wiki:Ceryse_Hightower), as a bride for the young prince instead.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aenys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Visenya's relationship with Aegon, which had never been a warm one, grew more distant. When Aegon in [35 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) had the [Aegonfort](wiki:Aegonfort) torn down so they could start building the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), Visenya was left in charge of the construction. In his histories, [Archmaester](wiki:Archmaester) [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn) states that it was suggested at court that Aegon had left Visenya at King's Landing so that he would not have to endure her presence on Dragonstone.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-4)

### Reign of Aenys

When Aegon died, both Visenya and Maegor were present as his body was cremated and [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen) was crowned. Aenys gifted Maegor with [Blackfyre](wiki:Blackfyre) saying he was more fit to wield it. Visenya Targaryen voiced the opinion that Aenys was unfit to rule, claiming that, by giving [Blackfyre](wiki:Blackfyre) to [Maegor](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), Aenys had admitted that he lacked the strength to rule.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7)

In [39 AC](wiki:39_AC), Visenya's son Maegor shocked the realm by taking a second wife and married [Alys Harroway](wiki:Alys_Harroway). For lack of a septon, Visenya officiated the [Valyrian](wiki:Valyria) [wedding ceremony](wiki:Marriage). The outrage that followed resulted in Maegor being sent into exile.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aenys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) Visenya was present the next year at the wedding of Princess [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) to Prince [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)). At the feast that followed the ceremony, King Aenys named Aegon [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone), a title which had previously belonged to Maegor. Visenya left the feast in protest and returned to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) upon [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar). Singers claim that the moon turned red as they passed it.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8)

When King Aenys abandoned King's Landing at the start of the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising) and fled to Dragonstone, Visenya counseled him to burn down the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) and the [Sept of Remembrance](wiki:Sept_of_Remembrance) in King's Landing. Aenys, incapable of making a firm decision, instead fell ill and chose not to heed her counsel. Visenya took over his care and for a time his health improved. Aenys suffered a collapse when he learned his son [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) and daughter [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) were besieged in [Crakehall](wiki:Crakehall), and he died three days later.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aenys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-2) As soon as Aenys was buried, Visenya then mounted [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) and flew to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) to retrieve her son Maegor from exile.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)

### Reign of Maegor

With Visenya's aid, Maegor claimed the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). Visenya accompanied him to King's Landing, where she challenged anyone who denied Maegor's right to rule, which resulted in a [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven) between Maegor and the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons). Though severely injured, Maegor was the only survivor.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2)

In [42 AC](wiki:42_AC), Visenya flew to [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark) upon Vhagar to convince Aenys's widow, Dowager Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon), to leave her father's castle to where she had fled after her husband's death. At the royal court, it was said that Maegor's [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) and council members enacted his will but did not have his ear - but instead, he was ruled by three queens: his mother Visenya, his paramour and second wife [Alys Harroway](wiki:Alys_Harroway), and his new third bride, the Pentoshi [spymaster](wiki:Spymaster) [Tyanna of the Tower](wiki:Tyanna_of_the_Tower), a rumored sorceress. Visenya came to distrust Tyanna and a rivalry developed between them.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-9)

In [43 AC](wiki:43_AC) Visenya aided her son against those who refused his royal summons. Mounted upon her dragon [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar), she burned the seats of several houses in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), as once she had burned castles in Dorne: [House Blanetree](wiki:House_Blanetree), [Terrick](wiki:House_Terrick), [Deddings](wiki:House_Deddings), [Lychester](wiki:House_Lychester), and [Wayn](wiki:House_Wayn).(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-15) Next Visenya and Maegor turned towards [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), threatening to incinerate the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) in response to the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aenys_I))'s condemnation of her sons [polygamous marriages](wiki:Polygamy). When the High Septon suddenly died, Lord [Martyn Hightower](wiki:Martyn_Hightower) opened the city's gates, saving Oldtown from the fire. Visenya watched as the [new High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Pater)) anointed her son as king.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-10)

After Maegor's victory in the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye), Visenya persuaded her son to spare the lives of several highborn rebels. After Prince Aegon's death, Visenya held Alyssa and her two youngest children, [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and [Alysanne](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), as her wards on Dragonstone.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-11)

By [44 AC](wiki:44_AC) - despite being healthy enough to ride her dragon to battle the year before - Visenya had grown thin and haggard, as the flesh had "melted" from her bones.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-12) Visenya died that same year while Maegor was on campaign. Upon her death, Alyssa and her two children slipped away from the castle in the confusion, taking Visenya's [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) sword [Dark Sister](wiki:Dark_Sister) with them.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-13)(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-3)

Visenya was cremated as per Targaryen [custom](wiki:Custom). Her bones and ashes were interred beside those of her brother, King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-14)(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-17)

## Appearances & Description

Like her siblings, Visenya possessed the classical [Valyrian](wiki:Valyria) features: long, silver-gold hair, which she often braided or bound up in rings, and [purple eyes](wiki:Purple_eyes).(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-ssm_4-0)(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) A voluptuous and sensual woman,(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-ssm_4-1) hers was a harsher, more austere beauty than her sister, [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen).(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Their personalities were also much dissimilar, for Visenya was passionate and dark to Rhaenys' playful and mischevious.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-ssm_4-2) Even those who loved her best considered Visenya stern, serious, and unforgiving. Some claimed that Visenya dabbled in [dark sorceries](wiki:Magic) and played with [poisons](wiki:Poison).(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

Visenya was both a [dragonrider](wiki:Dragonrider) and a warrior, comfortable in both silk and in ringmail.(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) When garbed as a warrior, she always wielded the [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) [longsword](wiki:Longsword) [Dark Sister](wiki:Dark_Sister).(wiki:Visenya_Targaryen.cite_ref-ssm_4-3)
