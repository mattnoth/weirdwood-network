## Origins

Oscar was [knighted](wiki:Knight) on the battlefield after slaying three men in the [Battle of the Kingsroad](wiki:Battle_of_the_Kingsroad). He was one of the [Lads](wiki:Lads) who marched to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) after the battle, but they later deferred to the formidable Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark).(wiki:Oscar_Tully.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
After the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) ended, Oscar created the [Stormbreakers](wiki:Stormbreakers), a [free company](wiki:Free_company) which left [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) for the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities).(wiki:Oscar_Tully.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
