## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Septon Barre protests when men sworn to Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) sack [his castle sept](wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone), and he is thrown into [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone)'s jails. Also imprisoned are the two surviving sons of Ser [Hubard Rambton](wiki:Hubard_Rambton), who defended the sept from the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) by force, and Lord [Guncer Sunglass](wiki:Guncer_Sunglass), who renounces his support for [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Barre.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Guncer and the Rambtons are burned for treason by [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) and Selyse.(wiki:Barre.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Barre's fate is not mentioned.
