## Origins

Lark comes from the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters), a group of islands ruled by the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn). His cousin is [Rolley](wiki:Rolley_of_Sisterton) of [Sisterton](wiki:Sisterton).(wiki:Lark.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Appearances & Description

Lark is a thin man, with sharp features and nervous eyes.(wiki:Lark.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He is considered mean.(wiki:Lark.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Lark and [Chett](wiki:Chett) come upon [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) as he is talking to [Gilly](wiki:Gilly), one of [Craster](wiki:Craster)'s wives. Lark attempts to snatch a rabbit from [Ghost](wiki:Ghost), but falls on his back instead.(wiki:Lark.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Jon later overhears him talking to Chett about Chett's unwillingness to go into the [Frostfangs](wiki:Frostfangs).(wiki:Lark.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Lark becomes one of Chett's conspirators in the mutiny plot while they reside. Lark is supposed to kill [Dywen](wiki:Dywen) and [Bannen](wiki:Bannen) with the help of his cousins.(wiki:Lark.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Although he wishes to have a double amount of co-conspirators, Chett refuses the suggestion. Lark and his cousins plan to make for the coast after escaping the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), hoping to sail back to the Sisters. The night the mutiny is supposed to take place, Lark accompanies Chett and [Small Paul](wiki:Small_Paul) on a hunt at the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men). They return empty-handed to the camp. The mutiny plot fails that night when the brothers of the Night's Watch are attacked by [wights](wiki:Wights).(wiki:Lark.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Lark is turned into a wight himself, at some point, for he is one of the wights that come after [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) and [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) in the unnamed wildling village after they have escaped [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep) following the death of [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont). The undead Lark is attacked by [ravens](wiki:Raven), allowing Sam and Gilly to be rescued by [Coldhands](wiki:Coldhands).(wiki:Lark.cite_ref-Rasos46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
