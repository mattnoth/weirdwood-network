## Origins

At one point, Hugh was proposed as a potential [betrothal](wiki:Betrothal) to Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) by her father, [Doran](wiki:Doran_Martell), the [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne). In order to facilitate the proposal, the Greybeard visited [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), but he fell asleep during courses at the welcoming feast. While Hugh slept, [Garin](wiki:Garin_(orphan)) challenged Arianne to tie his beard into a knot, but she refused. Though Arianne found him less unappealing than the other old suitors her father proposed, she turned down Hugh like all the others.(wiki:Hugh_Grandison.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

## Appearances & Description

Hugh is an older man whose once-grey beard has become white as snow.(wiki:Hugh_Grandison.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

[Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) regards the Greybeard to be pleasant and more robust than others his age, but still undesirable.(wiki:Hugh_Grandison.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
