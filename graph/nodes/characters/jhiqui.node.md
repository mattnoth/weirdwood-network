## Origins

Jhiqui was born to a *[khal](wiki:Khal)* of a *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)*. It was destroyed by Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), and she was [enslaved](wiki:Slavery).(wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

## Appearances & Description

Jhiqui has skin the color of [copper](wiki:Copper), with black hair and almond-shaped eyes.(wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) She has wide hips and heavy breasts, and is bigger boned than [Irri](wiki:Irri).(wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

She is of an age with [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) and [Irri](wiki:Irri).(wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Jhiqui, [Doreah](wiki:Doreah), and [Irri](wiki:Irri) are given to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) as wedding gifts to serve as her handmaids.(wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) She is given to Daenerys to teach her the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) [tongue](wiki:Dothraki_language).(wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Jhiqui remains with Daenerys after the death of Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo).(wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Ragot68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Clash of Kings

After struggling through the [red waste](wiki:Red_waste), [Irri](wiki:Irri) and Jhiqui attempt to convince Daenerys not to stay in [Vaes Tolorro](wiki:Vaes_Tolorro), believing it haunted by ghosts.(wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

After Daenerys destroys the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying), Jhiqui fastens a [silver](wiki:Silver) [bell](wiki:Bells_(Dothraki)) in her braid to mark the victory.(wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Jhiqui and Irri are [seasick](wiki:Greensick) during the voyage to [Astapor](wiki:Astapor).(wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Jhiqui is present when Daenerys purchases the [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied). Jhiqui and Irri free [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal) and [Viserion](wiki:Viserion) to attack the [Astapori](wiki:Astapor) slavers when Daenerys has the Unsullied attack their former masters.(wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) She later praises [Strong Belwas](wiki:Strong_Belwas) in defeating [Oznak zo Pahl](wiki:Oznak_zo_Pahl) and remarks she would braid his hair the Dothraki way if he had any.(wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Jhiqui and [Irri](wiki:Irri) both develop a crush on [Rakharo](wiki:Rakharo), who has grown more tall and muscular. They argue over which of them he would prefer.(wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

Jhiqui later accompanies Daenerys to [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit) as part of the royal procession.(wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) She and Irri both depart [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) as part of the search parties dispatched across the [Skahazadhan](wiki:Skahazadhan) to locate Daenerys after she disappears on [Drogon](wiki:Drogon)'s back.(wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
