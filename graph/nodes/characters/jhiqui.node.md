## Identity

Jhiqui is a Dothraki handmaid of Daenerys Targaryen (wiki:Jhiqui). She is of an age with Daenerys and Irri (wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Ragot23).

## Origins

Born in 284 AC in the Dothraki Sea (track_b: Born). She was born to a khal whose khalasar was destroyed by Khal Drogo, after which she was enslaved (wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Ragot23).

## Appearances & Description

Jhiqui has copper-colored skin, black hair, and almond-shaped eyes (wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Ragot11). She has wide hips, heavy breasts, and is bigger boned than Irri (wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Radwd2). Confirmed by Pass 1: copper-skinned, black hair, almond-shaped eyes (agot-daenerys-02).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` Khal Drogo's khalasar, then `SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiances). `CULTURE_OF` Dothraki (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Given to Daenerys as a wedding gift along with Irri and Doreah, tasked with teaching her the Dothraki tongue (wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Ragot11). She remains with Daenerys after Drogo's death (wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Ragot68). In ACOK, she and Irri warn Dany about ghosts in Vaes Tolorro; after the destruction of the House of the Undying, Jhiqui fastens a silver bell in Dany's braid to mark the victory (wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Racok63). In ASOS, she is seasick on the voyage to Astapor; she and Irri free Rhaegal and Viserion during the sack (wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Rasos27). In ADWD, she and Irri argue over Rakharo; she accompanies Dany to Daznak's Pit and joins the search after Dany disappears on Drogon (wiki:Jhiqui.cite_ref-Radwd52, cite_ref-Radwd55).
