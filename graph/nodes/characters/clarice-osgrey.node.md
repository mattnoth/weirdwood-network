## Origins

Clarice was the widowed aunt of [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake), [Lord of Starpike](wiki:Lord_of_Starpike). It is unknown if she was a maternal or paternal aunt, and whether she was an [Osgrey](wiki:House_Osgrey) by birth or by marriage. When her nephew became [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), he brought his own trusted kin to court, and Unwin put Clarice in charge of Queen [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen)'s household, supervising her maids, servants, and [lady companions](wiki:Lady_companion).(wiki:Clarice_Osgrey.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) After Jaehaera's death in the 9th moon of [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Clarice managed the household of the new queen, [Daenaera Velaryon](wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon), and stayed in her position after her nephew resigned as Hand in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC).(wiki:Clarice_Osgrey.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

In [135 AC](wiki:135_AC), the king and queen suffered an attempted [poisoning](wiki:Poison), and a [conspiracy was made to frame](wiki:Secret_siege) Lady [Larra Rogare](wiki:Larra_Rogare), the wife of Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen). When the conspirators were exposed, three of the queen's ladies were accused of being among them. Clarice was also suspected, as she supervised the ladies in Daenaera's household, and was well-acquainted with all the confessed conspirators, including George Graceford. If Clarice had been involved, it was likely Unwin Peake had been in the conspiracy as well. However, the three accused ladies, [Cassandra Baratheon](wiki:Cassandra_Baratheon), [Lucinda Penrose](wiki:Lucinda_Penrose), and [Priscella Hogg](wiki:Priscella_Hogg), all denied Clarice's involvement, only shaking their heads when asked directly.(wiki:Clarice_Osgrey.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
