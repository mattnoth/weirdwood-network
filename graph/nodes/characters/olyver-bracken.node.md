## Origins

Ser Olyver and his sworn brother of the Kingsguard, Ser [Raymund Mallery](wiki:Raymund_Mallery), abandoned King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) in [48 AC](wiki:48_AC) when Prince [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) made his claim for the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). Since both men had broken their [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) oaths by not protecting Maegor, and Jaehaerys did not wish for oathbreakers to serve him, after taking the throne he offered them the choice of execution or taking the black. They both chose to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Olyver_Bracken.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

The [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) gave Ser Olyver command of [Rimegate](wiki:Rimegate) and Ser Raymund command of [Sable Hall](wiki:Sable_Hall), with orders to restore the crumbling forts of the [Wall](wiki:Wall). However, in [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), Olyver and Raymund [led a rebellion](wiki:Night's_Watch_rebellion_of_50_AC) of former [Faith Militant](wiki:Faith_Militant) in the Watch, trying to make themselves [lords](wiki:Lord) of their castles. Lord [Walton Stark](wiki:Walton_Stark_(son_of_Brandon)) rode north to aid the loyal brothers with ending the mutiny. The defeated rebels at [Rimegate](wiki:Rimegate) were hanged, with the exception of Olyver, who was beheaded by Lord Stark with the [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) [greatsword](wiki:Greatsword) [Ice](wiki:Ice).(wiki:Olyver_Bracken.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
