## Origins

Temmo came into conflict with Khal [Dhako](wiki:Dhako), the Dragon of the North, who was old by then. Temmo's *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* broke Dhako's and Temmo took the elder khal captive, and fed Dhako to the flames, cutting off his hands, feet, and genitals, and roasting them before his eyes, after first burning his wives and sons in the same manner.(wiki:Temmo.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Khal Temmo led his *khalasar* to [Qohor](wiki:Qohor) to sack the city. He destroyed the city's defenders on the first day and retired to his camps for the night, planning to sack the city the following day. The next morning he awoke to find three thousand [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) barring the way. Eighteen times the *khalasar* attacked the Unsullied, to no avail. Khal Temmo was slain in the battle, along with all his sons, and [bloodriders](wiki:Bloodrider). His *khalasar* suffered grievous losses and eventually chose to retreat.(wiki:Temmo.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
