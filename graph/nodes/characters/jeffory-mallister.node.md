## Origins

When news of [Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark)'s kidnapping by Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) reached her brother, [Brandon Stark](wiki:Brandon_Stark), he raced to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) accompanied by his party, which included Jeffory. Brandon and his party rode into the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) demanding Rhaegar's head. Rhaegar's father, King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), had them arrested and demanded that their fathers come to King's Landing to answer for their sons' crimes. Aerys had all of them killed save for [Ethan Glover](wiki:Ethan_Glover).(wiki:Jeffory_Mallister.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
