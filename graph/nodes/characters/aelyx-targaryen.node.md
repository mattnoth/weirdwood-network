## Identity

Lord Aelyx Targaryen was a member of House Targaryen during the Century of Blood, the first son of Lord Aerys Targaryen of Dragonstone. He had two younger brothers, Baelon and Daemion Targaryen (wiki:Aelyx_Targaryen).

## Origins
