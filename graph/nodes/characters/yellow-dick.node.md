## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Yellow Dick is present in the great hall of the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort) when [Big Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Jammos)) and [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)) bring [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) before [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), [Arnolf Karstark](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark), and [Hother Umber](wiki:Hother_Umber).(wiki:Yellow_Dick.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The [Bastard's Boys](wiki:Bastard's_Boys) accompany Ramsay and Theon to the [siege of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin).(wiki:Yellow_Dick.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Yellow Dick is present at the wedding of Ramsay and "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)" (actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). After the feast, Theon is brought to Ramsay's chamber by Yellow Dick and five other Bastard's Boys.(wiki:Yellow_Dick.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) During a breakfast, [wine](wiki:Wine) snorts out Yellow Dick's nose when he hears [Abel](wiki:Abel) change the lyrics of "[The Dornishman's Wife](wiki:The_Dornishman's_Wife)".(wiki:Yellow_Dick.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

While the army of Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) awaits the arrival of the army of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), several [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms) and [squires](wiki:Squire) are found dead. Their deaths are thought to be accidents, until Yellow Dick is found dead in the snow with his [genitals cut off](wiki:Eunuch) and shoved into his mouth, with three broken teeth. Roose orders Yellow Dick's body to be discreetely burned, and Ramsay offers a bounty of a [golden dragon](wiki:Currency) for his killer.(wiki:Yellow_Dick.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Suspicion is cast on Theon at first,(wiki:Yellow_Dick.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) then on [knights](wiki:Knight) from [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) after [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Little_Walder_Frey) is found murdered. [Holly](wiki:Holly) and [Rowan](wiki:Rowan), two of the disguised [spearwives](wiki:Spearwife) who came to Winterfell with Abel to rescue Arya, imply responsibility for some of the murders, including Yellow Dick's. However, they deny that the spearwives killed Little Walder, but Theon does not believe them.(wiki:Yellow_Dick.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
