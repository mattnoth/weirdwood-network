## Appearances & Description

Haldon is clean-shaven, with a lined, ascetic face. He wears his hair pulled back, and tied in a knot behind his head. He has cool grey eyes. He studied at the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) but did not earn enough links to become a [maester](wiki:Maester).(wiki:Haldon.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Haldon is a patient teacher.(wiki:Haldon.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Haldon has the largest cabin on the *[Shy Maid](wiki:Shy_Maid)*. Its walls are lined with bookshelves, there are bins for old parchments and scrolls, and there are racks of ointments, herbs, and potions. The cabin is furnished with a bunk, writing desk, chair, stool, and *[cyvasse](wiki:Cyvasse)* table.(wiki:Haldon.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Haldon meets [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis)'s litter, and escorts [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to meet up with the rest of the party as they set sail to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis). Soon after meeting Haldon, Tyrion realizes that he would do well to be careful around him.(wiki:Haldon.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

During the journey on the river [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne), Haldon plays *[cyvasse](wiki:Cyvasse)* with Tyrion, who suggests that they play for secrets; Haldon is confident he will win. They play for three hours(wiki:Haldon.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) and it is implied that Haldon loses.(wiki:Haldon.cite_ref-4)

After an attack by the [stone men](wiki:Stone_men), Haldon checks Tyrion for [greyscale](wiki:Greyscale) by pricking Tyrion's toes and fingers with a knife. [Griff](wiki:Jon_Connington) sends Haldon to [Selhorys](wiki:Selhorys) to investigate rumors that [Yandry](wiki:Yandry) and [Ysilla](wiki:Ysilla) heard there, and Haldon takes Tyrion with him. He lets Tyrion go to a [whorehouse](wiki:Brothel), where Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) surprises Tyrion and takes him prisoner.(wiki:Haldon.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Haldon goes to [Griffin's Roost](wiki:Griffin's_Roost) with Griff, who is actually [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington), and assumes the duties of the castle [maester](wiki:Maester), as the previous maester was killed in the [taking of the castle](wiki:Taking_of_Griffin's_Roost). He relays what he learns of the castle's letters to Jon.(wiki:Haldon.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
