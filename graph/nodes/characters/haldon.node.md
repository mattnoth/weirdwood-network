## Identity

Haldon, known as "Haldon Halfmaester" or "The Halfmaester," is an acolyte who serves as tutor and advisor to Young Griff (Aegon Targaryen) (track_b: Title, aliases).

## Origins

Born in Westeros (track_b: Born).

## Narrative Arc

Haldon serves as tutor to Young Griff aboard the Shy Maid, teaching him history, languages, and cyvasse. He accompanies the party down the Rhoyne and through the journey toward Westeros. He earned the nickname "Halfmaester" for having studied at the Citadel without completing his chain. He continues to serve the Targaryen cause as the group invades the Stormlands.
