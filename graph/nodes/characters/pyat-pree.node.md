## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Pyat, [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos), and [Quaithe](wiki:Quaithe) are representatives of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) who accompany [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) back to [Vaes Tolorro](wiki:Vaes_Tolorro) after he was sent by [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) to discover what lies near the abandoned city.(wiki:Pyat_Pree.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He escorts Daenerys to Qarth where he tries to convince her to come with him and visit the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying).(wiki:Pyat_Pree.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

When Dany arrives at the House of the Undying, accompanied by Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont), her [bloodriders](wiki:Bloodrider), and [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos), Pyat Pree counsels her on the rules of entering the palace of the warlocks. Upon exiting the House of the Undying after [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) burns the [Undying Ones](wiki:Undying_Ones), Dany finds Pyat gibbering in some unknown tongue and hopping from one foot to the other. Pyat draws a knife and advances on Dany, at which point Jhogo and [Rakharo](wiki:Rakharo) intercept and disarm him.(wiki:Pyat_Pree.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Xaro informs Daenerys that Pyat is gathering the remaining warlocks against her.(wiki:Pyat_Pree.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Aboard the *[Balerion](wiki:Balerion_(ship))* Daenerys remembers that Pyat had sent a [Sorrowful Man](wiki:Sorrowful_Man) after her to avenge the Undying she had burned in their House of Dust.(wiki:Pyat_Pree.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows

[Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North), tells his brother [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) he had captured four [warlocks](wiki:Warlocks) on a galleas from Qarth. Euron killed one for threatening him and then fed him to the remaining warlocks. The surviving warlocks are reportedly coaching Euron in [black magic](wiki:Magic).(wiki:Pyat_Pree.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

According to a semi-canon source, Pree is among the hostage warlocks,(wiki:Pyat_Pree.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_euron_greyjoy.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) and their [dragon horn](wiki:Dragonbinder) was also taken from them by Euron.(wiki:Pyat_Pree.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_qarth.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Xaro informs Daenerys, now [Queen of Meereen](wiki:King_of_Meereen), that she had not been gone a fortnight when Pyat set out from Qarth with three of his fellow warlocks to seek her in [Pentos](wiki:Pentos), in order to seek revenge on her for the burning of the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying).(wiki:Pyat_Pree.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### The Winds of Winter

[Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy), held captive by his brother Euron in the hold of the *[Silence](wiki:Silence)*, is joined by mutilated warlocks. One of the captives repeats the word, "Pree".(wiki:Pyat_Pree.cite_ref-11)
