## Origins

### Dance of the Dragons

When he was eleven years old, Benjicot became the head of [his House](wiki:House_Blackwood) after the death of his father, Lord [Samwell Blackwood](wiki:Samwell_Blackwood), in the [Battle of the Burning Mill](wiki:Battle_of_the_Burning_Mill) in [129 AC](wiki:129_AC) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) A member of the [blacks](wiki:Blacks), Benjicot participated in the [Battle by the Lakeshore](wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore) the following year. Although the soldiers from the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) and the [north](wiki:North) defeated the [westermen](wiki:Westermen) army in the battle, Benjicot wept when he saw all of the dead.(wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

When Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) raided the riverlands atop [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar), Benjicot asked Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) to protect the riverlords with her [dragons](wiki:Dragon), but she remained in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Benjicot's men hunted the foragers of Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) after the Kingmaker left [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) Benjicot commanded the center during the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton).(wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) Following the battle, he had one of his longbowmen, [Billy Burley](wiki:Billy_Burley), put three arrows through the eye of the weakened dragon [Tessarion](wiki:Tessarion).(wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

During the [Battle of the Kingsroad](wiki:Battle_of_the_Kingsroad) in [131 AC](wiki:131_AC), Benjicot led the army with his liege, [Kermit Tully](wiki:Kermit_Tully), [Lord of Riverrun](wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun), and Kermit's brother, Ser [Oscar Tully](wiki:Oscar_Tully).(wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) Benjicot's rivermen broke the flank of Lord [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon)'s [greens](wiki:Greens) in the battle, allowing the archers of his aunt, [Alysanne](wiki:Alysanne_Blackwood), to bring down the green [knights](wiki:Knight). Benjicot was remembered after the battle as Bloody Ben.(wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Benjicot, Kermit, and Oscar became known as "the [Lads](wiki:Lads)", a name bestowed upon them by their men on account of their youth. Together with Black Aly and Lady [Sabitha Frey](wiki:Sabitha_Frey), they were well received when they arrived at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). The youths deferred to the more formidable [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark), [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell), after the [northern](wiki:North) army appeared at the capital, however.(wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

### Regency of King Aegon III

Lord Blackwood returned to [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall) after the coronation of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) in [131 AC](wiki:131_AC). With peace having been achieved, many northmen agreed to settle in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) instead of returning to the [north](wiki:North), and [Widow Fairs](wiki:Widow_Fairs) were held at Raventree and other river castles.(wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) The following year, Ben took the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad) to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) to attend the winter wedding of his aunt, [Alysanne Blackwood](wiki:Alysanne_Blackwood), to Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark). Ben's retinue was attacked three times en route by outlaws, however, and his wedding gifts were stolen.(wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

When Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake) planned for Aegon to wed his daughter, [Myrielle Peake](wiki:Myrielle_Peake), after the death of Queen [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen), Lord Blackwood and other lords questioned Unwin's hastiness. Instead, the [Maiden's Day Ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball) was held in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), and Aegon wed [Daenaera Velaryon](wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon).(wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

In [135 AC](wiki:135_AC), Benjicot and a thousand Blackwood men supported Ser [Robert Rowan](wiki:Robert_Rowan)'s campaign to end the [succession conflict](wiki:Arryn_succession_conflict_(134_AC)) in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn). Robert's host of nine thousand was slowed by snow on the [high road](wiki:High_road), however, and repeatedly attacked by desperate [Vale mountain clansmen](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans). After Robert was crushed by a boulder, the young but experienced Benjicot took command. The royal host had lost a third of their men in reaching the [Bloody Gate](wiki:Bloody_Gate), and were therefore less useful for the cause of Ser [Joffrey Arryn](wiki:Joffrey_Arryn).(wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) Benjicot and Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) eventually forced the other claimants to accept Joffrey, and Benjicot returned to Raventree.(wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### Later life

Benjicot had a long life.(wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) In [138 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), he allowed Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) to bring the bones of his brother [Addam](wiki:Addam_Velaryon), which had been taken to [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall) after [Second Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton) in [130 AC](wiki:130_AC), to [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark) so they could be entombed in [Hull](wiki:Hull).(wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Appearances & Description

Benjicot was tall and slim, with a shy and sensitive manner. When armored for battle, however, he was quite fierce.(wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
