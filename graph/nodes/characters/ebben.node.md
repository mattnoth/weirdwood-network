## Appearances & Description

Ebben is a squat, heavily-muscled, bald man.(wiki:Ebben.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) He has served the Night's Watch since boyhood.(wiki:Ebben.cite_ref-Racok53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) He is regarded by [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand) as the ranger to go to when he needs a wildling interrogated or killed.(wiki:Ebben.cite_ref-Racok53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ebben is part of the one-hundred men reinforcements from the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower) to arrive at the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men).(wiki:Ebben.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Before reaching the Fist, the Shadow Tower group is attacked by [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings). Ebben questions the survivor and learns that wildlings have gathered in search of a great power in the [Frostfangs](wiki:Frostfangs), though his interrogation is too harsh and the prisoner dies.(wiki:Ebben.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Ebben is later chosen by [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand) alongside [Stonesnake](wiki:Stonesnake), [Squire Dalbridge](wiki:Dalbridge), and [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) to scout the [Skirling Pass](wiki:Skirling_Pass) to search for wildlings.(wiki:Ebben.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

After the death of Dalbridge, Ebben is sent on with the remaining horses to ride as fast as he can to Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont). He is killed by a wildling scouting party commanded by [Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt), his head kept as a prize and shown to Jon later. One of the spearwives notes that he died bravely.(wiki:Ebben.cite_ref-Racok68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
