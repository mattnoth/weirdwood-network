## Origins

Early in the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Meredyth became Lady of [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) after Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) [captured](wiki:Sack_of_Duskendale) the town and had Lord Darklyn beheaded. Alarmed by reports that Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) was burning the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) atop [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar), Lady Meredyth sent requests for help to Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen).(wiki:Meredyth_Darklyn.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Meredyth_Darklyn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Rhaenyra fled [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) after the [Storming of the Dragonpit](wiki:Storming_of_the_Dragonpit) and traveled toward Duskendale. Ser [Harrold Darke](wiki:Harrold_Darke), once the squire of Ser [Steffon Darklyn](wiki:Steffon_Darklyn), convinced Meredyth to allow the queen's party within the town walls, but only for a short time. Rhaenyra continued on to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) after leaving Duskendale.(wiki:Meredyth_Darklyn.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Meredyth_Darklyn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
