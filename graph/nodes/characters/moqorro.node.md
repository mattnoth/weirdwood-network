## Appearances & Description

Moqorro's skin is black as pitch,(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) darker than coal or [jet](wiki:Jet).(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He is a monster of a man, huge, over six feet tall and as wide as two men, with a belly like a boulder.(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) A tangle of pure white hair grows from his face like a mane of a [lion](wiki:Lion).(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) He has a deep booming voice like a bass [drum](wiki:Drum).(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) Moqorro has flame [tattoos](wiki:Tattoo) inked across his cheeks and forehead, which are yellow-and-orange colored, according to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) According to [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy), Moqorro's flame tattoos are red-and-orange.(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Moqorro wears scarlet robes with sleeves, collar, and hems embroidered with orange satin flames. He carries an [iron](wiki:Iron) staff as tall as he is, with a [dragon](wiki:Dragon)'s head that spits crackling green flame when brought down.(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6)(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) He speaks the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue) well, with only a hint of accent.(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7)

Like [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre)(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and [Benerro](wiki:Benerro),(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Moqorro claims to [see the future](wiki:Dreams_and_prophecies) in the flames of [nightfires](wiki:Nightfire).(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

Clad in the new black-and-gold Greyjoy robes Victarion has sewn for him, Moqorro is shunned by the ironborn crew: "Clad in black from head to heel, with a mask of red-and-orange flames tattooed across his face, the priest appeared more sinister than ever." (adwd-victarion-01.md:25) [Greyjoy-black robes / flame-mask tattoo; crew shuns him]

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

In [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), [Quaithe](wiki:Quaithe) warns Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) that the [kraken](wiki:Kraken) and dark flame are coming to her, among others, and that she should trust none of them.(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Moqorro is sent by [Benerro](wiki:Benerro), the [High Priest](wiki:High_Priest_(R'hllor)) of the [Temple of the Lord of Light](wiki:Temple_of_the_Lord_of_Light) in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), to offer [guidance and help](wiki:The_way) to Daenerys Targaryen.(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8) He and his five soldiers of the [Fiery Hand](wiki:Fiery_Hand) join the crew of the [ship](wiki:Ship) *[Selaesori Qhoran](wiki:Selaesori_Qhoran)* heading to [Qarth](wiki:Qarth),(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-9) although Benerro has seen in his flames that the ship will not reach Qarth.(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) The [widow of the waterfront](wiki:Widow_of_the_waterfront) tells Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) about the ship, and he books passage in order to bring his prisoner, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), to Daenerys. On the ship, Moqorro and Tyrion converse, and Moqorro tells Tyrion he has seen [dragons](wiki:Dragon) in the flames, with Tyrion snarling in the midst of them all.(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-10)

The ship is hit by a great storm and later captured by [slavers](wiki:Slaver), which Moqorro had foreseen as the reason the ship would never reach Qarth. Moqorro is lost overboard during the storm.(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Ten days after the storm, *[Grief](wiki:Grief)*, the ship of [Rodrik Sparr](wiki:Rodrik_Sparr), discovers Moqorro clinging to wreckage in the sea. Rodrik brings Moqorro to [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) and the rest of the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) near the [Isle of Cedars](wiki:Isle_of_Cedars). The [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) want him put to death, but Moqorro is unafraid and calm, which further unnerves the crew. Moqorro tells to Victarion he knows of his quest to seek Daenerys Targaryen, and that Victarion will die from his wounded hand without Moqorro's help. At Victarion's cabin, his bedmate, the [dusky woman](wiki:Dusky_woman), hisses when she sees Moqorro, so Victarion knocks her down to the deck. Using [magic](wiki:Magic), Moqorro manages to cure Victarion's wound and to aid Victarion in his quest. He has Victarion sacrifice the ship's [maester](wiki:Maester), [Kerwin](wiki:Kerwin), to bring good winds.(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)

Since Moqorro's red garb was ruined in the sea, Victarion has [Tom Tidewood](wiki:Tom_Tidewood) make Moqorro new robes from the black and gold of the [Greyjoy](wiki:House_Greyjoy) sails. Victarion's crew live in great fear of Moqorro, and among them he is known as the "Black Flame". This name is given to him by [Steffar Stammerer](wiki:Steffar_Stammerer), who cannot pronounce Moqorro's name properly.(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Moqorro studies the [Valyrian glyphs](wiki:High_Valyrian) on the [dragon horn](wiki:Dragonbinder), and tells Victarion he must claim it with blood if he wishes to bind dragons to his will.(wiki:Moqorro.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
