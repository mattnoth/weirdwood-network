## Origins

Following the [tourney on Prince Joffrey's name day](wiki:Tourney_on_Prince_Joffrey's_name_day), Myrcella and her siblings traveled with [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) with their grandfather, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister).(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Myrcella has a garden at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Princess Myrcella accompanies her father, King [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_Baratheon), north to visit [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) and the [Starks](wiki:House_Stark). She is smitten with [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) during the feast.(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) She rides in the royal [wheelhouse](wiki:Wheelhouse) with her mother, Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister).(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

Myrcella and Prince [Tommen](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) do not recognize a dirty [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) when she chases cats in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), the new [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), discovers that Myrcella and her siblings are not the biological children of Robert, but actually of Cersei's twin brother, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) With Robert dying from being gored by a [boar](wiki:Boar), Lord [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) suggests that Tommen and Myrcella be seized and that Robb wed Myrcella. Eddard refuses, however.(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

After Robert's death, Myrcella is in the [throne room](wiki:Great_Hall_(Red_Keep)) when Eddard confronts Cersei and is betrayed by Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), then [arrested](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark).(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Myrcella attends the [tourney on King Joffrey's name day](wiki:Tourney_on_King_Joffrey's_name_day).(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Announcing his claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) declares that his niece and her siblings are abominations born of incest.(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

In an effort to win [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) to the side of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) in the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Myrcella's uncle, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), offers to marry her to [Trystane Martell](wiki:Trystane_Martell), the youngest son of [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne).(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) The offer is accepted and Princess Myrcella, accompanied by her [sworn shield](wiki:Sworn_shield) Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), sets sail on the *[Seaswift](wiki:Seaswift)* for [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), where they will be met by an escort from Dorne. The [riot of King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing) breaks out after Myrcella leaves the harbor.(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Myrcella arrives at [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) and befriends Princess [Arianne](wiki:Arianne_Martell) and Prince Trystane, the children of Prince Doran.(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Her chambers are in the [Old Palace](wiki:Old_Palace)'s Tower of the Sun.(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

After the death of King Joffrey at [his wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding), Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) reminds Tyrion that according to [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) law, Myrcella should succeed to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) rather than her younger brother, King [Tommen I](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Myrcella adapts quickly to spicy Dornish cuisine, unlike her bodyguard, Ser Arys. She also enjoys playing *[cyvasse](wiki:Cyvasse)* with Prince Trystane. Doran intends to bring the princess to the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens) when he departs Sunspear.(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

Following the death of her father, Oberyn, [Tyene Sand](wiki:Tyene_Sand) suggests to her uncle, Prince Doran, that Myrcella be crowned [queen](wiki:Queen_of_the_Andals,_the_Rhoynar,_and_the_First_Men) in accordance with Dornish law.(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

To get revenge against the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) for the deaths of Oberyn and [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell), Arianne schemes to crown Myrcella queen after Joffrey's death, since Myrcella is older than Tommen. Arianne hopes that the [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) will rise to defend her claim for a chance to war against the Lannisters, who would be expected to support Tommen.(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)

One of the [new dromonds](wiki:Cersei's_dromonds) for the [royal fleet](wiki:Royal_fleet) is named *[Princess Myrcella](wiki:Princess_Myrcella)* by Tommen in honor of his sister.(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

Arianne and a small group of friends travel with Myrcella through the desert, leaving the princess's cousin and [handmaid](wiki:Handmaid), [Rosamund Lannister](wiki:Rosamund_Lannister), as a decoy in [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear). Myrcella is claimed to be suffering from [redspots](wiki:Redspots) to prevent Trystane from interfering. When they come to their destination on the [Greenblood](wiki:Greenblood), Doran's guardsmen are waiting for them, for their conspiracy has been betrayed. Myrcella's bodyguard, Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart), attacks the guardsmen, but is killed by their captain, [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah). During the chaos, Ser [Gerold Dayne](wiki:Gerold_Dayne), called Darkstar, strikes at Myrcella to force Doran into a war against the Lannisters.(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) He fails to deal a killing blow, as Myrcella's horse veers away, but the princess is still wounded. Maester [Caleotte](wiki:Caleotte) saves her life, but Myrcella loses an ear and has a hideous facial scar.(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Tyrion, fled in exile to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos), considers crowning Myrcella, but is convinced by [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) to support [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) instead.(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) arrives at Sunspear, where he tells Prince Doran that King Tommen wishes his sister to return to King's Landing for a visit, inviting along Myrcella's betrothed, Prince Trystane. Doran assures the knight they will see Myrcella at the Water Gardens.(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

During a private conversation with Doran and the [Sand Snakes](wiki:Sand_Snakes), Arianne tells [Obara](wiki:Obara_Sand), [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria_Sand) and Tyene Sand that Myrcella will tell Balon that her Kingsguard, Arys, had been slain by Gerold Dayne, and that Gerold had tried to kill Myrcella as well. Prince Doran is convinced Myrcella will ask Balon to hunt down Gerold, and that Balon will not be able to refuse. Meanwhile, he decides that Myrcella will, indeed, return to King's Landing, in the company of Nym.(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-1)

Reflecting in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), Jaime Lannister thinks he will need to tell Myrcella the truth about her paternity, though the Dornishmen will not like it.(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

During her imprisonment by the [Faith](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), Cersei Lannister learns of Gerold's attack on her daughter from her uncle, Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister). Kevan tells her that Myrcella blames Darkstar for both the attack on her, as well as the death of Arys.(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) During a meeting of the [small council](wiki:Small_council), Kevan discusses the preparations to be made for Myrcella's return. The princess has begun her journey to King's Landing with Nymeria.(wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)
