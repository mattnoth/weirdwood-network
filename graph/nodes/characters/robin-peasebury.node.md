## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Lord Robin is among those who march with [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) on [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) and then [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). Four of his men eat the body of one of late Lord [Harwood Fell](wiki:Harwood_Fell)'s men and are burned for [cannibalism](wiki:Cannibalism) at a [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village). It is rumored that Robin may have eaten with them.(wiki:Robin_Peasebury.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
