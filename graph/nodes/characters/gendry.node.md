## Origins

Gendry was born in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), but he does not know that his father is King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) [His mother](wiki:Gendry's_mother), a worker at an [alehouse](wiki:Ale), died when he was still a young boy. All he remembers of her was that she had blond hair and would sometimes sing to him.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)

Gendry saw King Robert from afar at [tourneys](wiki:Tourney) and once at the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor). A drunken Robert also once nearly rode down Gendry when the boy was playing near the [Mud Gate](wiki:Mud_Gate).(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

[Tobho Mott](wiki:Tobho_Mott), a master smith, took Gendry on as an apprentice after being paid double the customary fee by a [stout brown-bearded man with a hooded face](wiki:Varys).(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5) Lords [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) and [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) called upon Tobho and Gendry during their investigation into Robert's children.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-6)

## Appearances & Description

Gendry is of a similar age to [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark). Tall and muscled, he has blue eyes and thick, black hair.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) When he grows a beard, it is also thick and black.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Gendry looks like a young [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), albeit with a squarer jaw, bushier brows, and tangled hair.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) For the Robert resemblance: AFFC Brienne VII (`sources/chapters/affc/affc-brienne-07.md:119`) — Brienne's POV: "He had the heavy shoulders and muscular right arm so often seen on smiths . . . his eyes had been that same deep blue, Lord Renly's eyes had always been warm and welcoming . . . whereas this boy's eyes brimmed with anger and suspicion." The comparison freezes Brienne because Renly died in her arms; Gendry's Robert build (not Renly's, she corrects herself) underscores the bastard's true lineage.

Gendry is a smart boy, but stubborn, sullen, and suspicious.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Rasos3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) thinks Gendry looks pained when he tries to think.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) The smith can be shy around women.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

The apprentice is skilled at smithing but is not a good horseman.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Rasos3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) Gendry made a rounded and curved steel helm shaped like a bull's head, with a slit visor and two great curving metal horns.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Racok9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) While Gendry often polishes the helm, he is reluctant to wear it.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Racok9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) investigates the death of Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), his predecessor as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Eddard meets several people who had met with Jon shortly before his death, including Master [Tobho Mott](wiki:Tobho_Mott) and his apprentice, Gendry. At once Lord Stark sees the similarity with King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) and recognizes that Gendry is his [bastard](wiki:Bastard). The promising Gendry has made a helmet in the shape of a bull. Eddard compliments the helmet, offering to purchase it, but Gendry refuses, to the shame of Tobho. Eddard tells the master smith that if Gendry ever shows interest in wielding a sword, he should send him to Eddard.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-7)

### A Clash of Kings

Tobho gives Gendry a piece of metal so he can make his first sword.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Before the youth can do so, however, arrangements are made by Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) for [Yoren](wiki:Yoren) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) to take Gendry to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) with him, to protect him from Cersei.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Gendry travels north with Yoren and thirty other recruits, including a disguised [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), [Lommy Greenhands](wiki:Lommy_Greenhands), [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie), [Jaqen H'ghar](wiki:Jaqen_H'ghar), [Rorge](wiki:Rorge), [Biter](wiki:Biter), and others.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Racok1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) After [Praed](wiki:Praed) passes away, his longsword of cheap steel is given to Gendry by Yoren.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Racok5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

In the aftermath of Lord Stark's arrest and execution, Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) orders the [City Watch of King's Landing](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing) to hunt down the late King Robert's bastards.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) Not far from King's Landing, Yoren's party is stopped by several gold cloaks, who demand that Yoren give up Gendry. Yoren refuses, however, and with his recruits chases off the gold cloaks.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Racok5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) Lommy assumes that Gendry is Lord Stark's bastard.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Racok9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2)

Later, at an [abandoned town](wiki:Gods_Eye_town) by the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye), the recruits are [attacked](wiki:Storming_of_the_Gods_Eye_town_holdfast) by soldiers led by Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch). Gendry wears his bull helm during the fighting. Praed's cheap sword breaks on a helm, so Gendry takes a new sword after he, Arya, and Hot Pie kill a soldier. Yoren is slain, however, and Gendry is one of the few to escape.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

Gendry advises the survivors to avoid roads. Whilst on reconnaissance with Arya, Gendry confronts her about being a girl. He eventually has the truth out of her, that she is Lord Stark's daughter, and he is subsequently embarrassed by how crassly he had spoken to her when she was in fact highborn.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3) The youths are captured in a village by the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men), soldiers led by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane), and [Raff the Sweetling](wiki:Rafford) kills Lommy.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Racok19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-4)

The soldiers spare Gendry's life, since a smith is useful, although his horned helm is worn first by [Dunsen](wiki:Dunsen)(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) and later [Polliver](wiki:Polliver).(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) The captives are taken to [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), where Gendry works in the forge(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) as a smith for [Lucan](wiki:Lucan).(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) He does not take part in the [fall of Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal) to [northmen](wiki:North).(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

Gendry continues to smith at Harrenhal after the castle is occupied by Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton). Arya persuades Gendry and Hot Pie to escape the castle with her, since Roose intends to grant Harrenhal to [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat) and the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions). Gendry wears oiled chainmail under a cloak and brings stolen swords and his blacksmith's hammer with him.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Arya, Hot Pie, and Gendry travel through hills, woods, and fields in the direction of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Rasos3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2) They are apprehended by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) at the [Inn of the Kneeling Man](wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man), where Hot Pie decides to remain as a baker.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) [Greenbeard](wiki:Greenbeard) leads the group to the [Lady of the Leaves](wiki:Lady_of_the_Leaves), [High Heart](wiki:High_Heart), and [Acorn Hall](wiki:Acorn_Hall). Gendry and Arya tussle in the forge of Acorn Hall.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) Gendry is embarrassed at the [Peach](wiki:Peach) in [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept), and he declines to sleep with [Bella](wiki:Bella). When an old man propositions Arya at the brothel, Gendry defends her and claims she is his sister.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

The brotherhood brings Gendry and Arya to the [hollow hill](wiki:Hollow_hill), where they witness Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion)'s duel with [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane).(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) [Beardless Dick](wiki:Beardless_Dick) and [Mudge](wiki:Mudge) guard the youths during the [battle at the burning septry](wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry). Impressed with the ideals of the brotherhood, Gendry offers to serve them as a blacksmith. He is [knighted](wiki:Knight) by Beric as Ser Gendry, knight of the hollow hill.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) Gendry is distrustful of Beric's squire, Lord [Edric Dayne](wiki:Edric_Dayne).(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

Arya, feeling abandoned by Gendry and Hot Pie and angry at the brotherhood's slowness in taking her to Riverrun, escapes from the outlaws.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1) [Lady Stoneheart](wiki:Lady_Stoneheart) replaces Beric as the leader of the brotherhood.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

### A Feast for Crows

While questing to find [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) encounters Gendry working as a smith at the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads), and she is shocked by his striking resemblance to [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon). Gendry wants Brienne's party to leave, but [Willow Heddle](wiki:Willow_Heddle) allows them to stay at the inn. Gendry has begun following the [Lord of Light](wiki:Lord_of_Light) instead of the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

While Gendry is making a sword at the inn's forge, Brienne questions the boy about his parentage. Their conversation is interrupted by the arrival of former Brave Companions, however. Brienne duels with [Rorge](wiki:Rorge) and slays him, only to be savaged by [Biter](wiki:Biter). Gendry saves Brienne's life by driving a spear through Biter's neck.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) Brienne is taken captive by the brotherhood. Gendry accompanies them as far as a river, then returns to the inn to protect its orphaned children.(wiki:Gendry.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1)
