## Identity

Prince Aemond Targaryen, known as Aemond One-Eye and later Aemond the Kinslayer, was a member of House Targaryen. He was the second son of King Viserys I Targaryen by his second wife Queen Alicent Hightower, and the younger brother of King Aegon II Targaryen. He was a dragonrider who rode Vhagar (wiki:Aemond_Targaryen).

## Origins

Born in 110 AC (track_b: Born). Son of Viserys I Targaryen and Alicent Hightower (track_b: Father, Mother). Died on the 22nd day of the 5th moon of 130 AC, aged 20, at the Gods Eye (track_b: Died).

## Narrative Arc

Aemond lost an eye as a child when he claimed the dragon Vhagar, leading to a fight with his nephews. During the Dance of the Dragons, he was a key military leader for the Greens, serving as Prince Regent and Protector of the Realm. He earned the epithet "the Kinslayer" for killing his nephew Lucerys Velaryon and his dragon Arrax above Shipbreaker Bay. He died at the Battle Above the Gods Eye in single combat against his uncle Prince Daemon Targaryen, both dragonriders falling into the lake (wiki:Aemond_Targaryen).
