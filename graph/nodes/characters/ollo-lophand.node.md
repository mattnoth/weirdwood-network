## Origins

Ollo is from [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh). He lost a hand after being caught for thievery and was sent to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) after being found abed with a [knight](wiki:Knight)'s wife.(wiki:Ollo_Lophand.cite_ref-Rasos0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Ollo Lophand is part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), the force that leaves [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) in search of the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings). He is one of the conspirators who intend to kill [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), at the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men). If the conspiracy is successful, he plans to return to [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh). Their scheme is interrupted when [wights](wiki:Wights) attack the Fist, however.(wiki:Ollo_Lophand.cite_ref-Rasos0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

Ollo survives the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist) and is among the men who make it back to [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep). He and [Tim Stone](wiki:Tim_Stone) feed and water the surviving horses, and he thinks that [Craster](wiki:Craster) is hoarding food. [Garth of Greenaway](wiki:Garth_of_Greenaway) and Ollo wield blades and prevent Jeor from interceding during the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep). When Jeor reaches for his own dagger, Ollo stabs Jeor, mortally wounding the Lord Commander. Ollo then rapes one of [Craster's wives](wiki:Craster's_wives)(wiki:Ollo_Lophand.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and searches the walls for food.(wiki:Ollo_Lophand.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Ollo remains at Craster's Keep after the betrayal, along with a few other former members of the Night's Watch.(wiki:Ollo_Lophand.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Whilst escorting [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall), [Coldhands](wiki:Coldhands) tracks the Night's Watch traitors and kills them in the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest). Ollo Lophand's one-handed corpse, the largest of them, is found with its throat slit in the snow by [Summer](wiki:Summer), who eats part of the meat.(wiki:Ollo_Lophand.cite_ref-Radwd4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
