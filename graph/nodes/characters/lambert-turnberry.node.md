## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Lambert approaches Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) at Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s funeral and promises her the head of her brother, [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister). He wears a patch over his good right eye and swears he will not remove it till Tyrion is dead.(wiki:Lambert_Turnberry.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) spots Lambert riding at a [quintain](wiki:Quintain) in the yard of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Lambert_Turnberry.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Lambert is often seen in the court of Queen [Margaery](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) according to [Taena of Myr](wiki:Taena_of_Myr).(wiki:Lambert_Turnberry.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

During his torture, the [Blue Bard](wiki:Blue_Bard) is forced to list those he claims to have been Queen Margaery Tyrell's secret lovers, including Lambert's name on the list.(wiki:Lambert_Turnberry.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Cersei has an arrest warrant signed for Lambert as part of her plans to remove Margaery from power. Lambert is sent to the dungeons after his arrest.(wiki:Lambert_Turnberry.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Lambert is held with the rest of Margaery's falsely accused lovers in a dungeon under the charge of [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn).(wiki:Lambert_Turnberry.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
