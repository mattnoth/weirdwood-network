## Appearances & Description

Del is a horse-faced youth about [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow)'s age.(wiki:Del.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Del is part of [Jarl](wiki:Jarl)'s group of raiders who climb the [Wall](wiki:Wall) to attack [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) from behind. After Jarl's death, Del follows [Styr](wiki:Styr)'s commands. The youth tells [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) that he intends to 'steal' a wildling girl with [red hair](wiki:Kissed_by_fire).(wiki:Del.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Together with [Errok](wiki:Errok), Del goes ahead to scout the ruins of [Queenscrown](wiki:Queenscrown). He returns almost at once to inform the raiders that there is someone in the town.(wiki:Del.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) He is killed by [Summer](wiki:Summer_(direwolf)), the [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) of [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark), at Queenscrown.(wiki:Del.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
