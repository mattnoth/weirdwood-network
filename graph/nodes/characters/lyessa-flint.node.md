### A Dance with Dragons

Lady Lyessa survived the sickness at Widow's Watch and she is still the head of the house.(wiki:Lyessa_Flint.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) However, it is unknown if her baby survived.
