## Origins

Garigus, together with Wisdoms [Rossart](wiki:Rossart) and [Belis](wiki:Belis), took part in King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen)'s [wildfire plot](wiki:Wildfire_plot), the plan to destroy the capital, during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). According to Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), Garigus was as enthusiastic about the plan as the other two pyromancers. The three secretly deployed wildfire caches across the whole city, to be ignited at the command of King Aerys. Their constant consultations with the king, both day and night, made the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Lord [Qarlton Chelsted](wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted), suspicious, leading to him confronting the king.(wiki:Garigus.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

A couple of days after he had killed Rossart and Aerys at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), Jaime Lannister hunted down Garigus and Belis and slew them as well. Garigus wept for mercy, and was giving a [quick death](wiki:Gift_of_mercy).(wiki:Garigus.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
