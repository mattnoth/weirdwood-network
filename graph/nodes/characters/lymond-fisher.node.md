## Origins

Lymond claimed the title of [river king](wiki:River_king) or [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident) in the generations after the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) were conquered by the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Arlan III Durrandon](wiki:Arlan_III_Durrandon). Lymond had a brief, inglorious reign, presumably ruling from [Oldstones](wiki:Oldstones), before being put down by the might of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End). His connection with the extinct [Fisher](wiki:House_Fisher) dynasty is unconfirmed.(wiki:Lymond_Fisher.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
