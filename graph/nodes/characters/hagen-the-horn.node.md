## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Hagen, along with the rest of Asha's supporters, sails to [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) with Asha as she contemplates her next move after the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Hagen_the_Horn.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

While standing watch over the castle with [Cromm](wiki:Cromm), Hagen blows his horn when they spot two [northmen](wiki:Northmen) infiltrating Deepwood. He sounds his horn when Asha rallies her men, and he blows three short blasts to inform the ironborn that they are to retreat tow their ships in the [Bay of Ice](wiki:Bay_of_Ice). Hagen is struck in his belly by an arrow while descending the watchtower, however, and falls headfirst. [Rolfe the Dwarf](wiki:Rolfe_the_Dwarf) pulls [Hagen's daughter](wiki:Hagen's_daughter) away as the ironborn flee before the army of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Hagen_the_Horn.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
