## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Kenned is among the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) who [raid](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore) the [Stony Shore](wiki:Stony_Shore). He is part of [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy)'s force that captures [Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell). When the [northmen](wiki:Northmen) led by Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) come to [liberate](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell) the castle, he remains with Theon. Afterward, he is killed by [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) men during the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell) per [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow)'s orders.(wiki:Kenned_the_Whale.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
