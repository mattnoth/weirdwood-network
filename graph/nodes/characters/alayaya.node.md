## Appearances & Description

Alayaya is a beautiful young [Summer Islander](wiki:Summer_Isles), with skin as smooth and black as polished jet, and wide dark eyes. She is taller than [Shae](wiki:Shae), but not as tall as her own mother. She dresses in flowing silks, cinched at the waist with a beaded belt.(wiki:Alayaya.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
Alayaya speaks the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue) with only a light accent.(wiki:Alayaya.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) She is learning to read.(wiki:Alayaya.cite_ref-Racok29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Of her, Tyrion feels that "a sweeter, braver, more innocent girl he had seldom met".(wiki:Alayaya.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Anguy](wiki:Anguy) spends the small fortune he had won at the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) on [Jayde](wiki:Jayde), Alayaya and [Dancy](wiki:Dancy), girls from [Chataya](wiki:Chataya)'s brothel, and eating and drinking roast swan and Arbor wine.(wiki:Alayaya.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Thanks to [Varys](wiki:Varys), who shows him a secret passage between a small stable and [Chataya](wiki:Chataya)'s establishment, [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) uses Alayaya as a cover for his visits to [Shae](wiki:Shae).(wiki:Alayaya.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) Those in the brothel think Tyrion is using her services, but Alayaya actually spends this free time sleeping.(wiki:Alayaya.cite_ref-Racok29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Just before the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Alayaya is abducted by the [Kettleblack brothers](wiki:House_Kettleblack) at Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s demand in order to pressure Tyrion. Tyrion promises that whatever the Kettleblacks do to her, he will have done to Prince [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Alayaya.cite_ref-Racok54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After the battle, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) has Alayaya tied to a post and whipped, then thrown out of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) naked and injured.(wiki:Alayaya.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) In an effort to apologize for her sacrifice and pain, Tyrion sends her a necklace of silver and jade and a pair of matching bracelets.(wiki:Alayaya.cite_ref-Rasos12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
After receiving his new knighthood, [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) visits [Chataya's brothel](wiki:Chataya's_brothel) and sleeps with Alayaya and [Marei](wiki:Marei) simultaneously.(wiki:Alayaya.cite_ref-Rasos12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) beds Alayaya during his visit to King's Landing.(wiki:Alayaya.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
