## Origins

Dick served [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) since he was a boy.(wiki:Dick_Bean.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

When King [Maegor](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) was challenged to a [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven) by Ser [Damon Morrigen](wiki:Damon_Morrigen), [Grand Captain](wiki:Grand_Captain) of the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons), Dick was the first to answer Maegor's call to those who would stand beside him, shaming many [knights](wiki:Knight) and noble lords into answering Maegor's summons. One account claimed that Dick was the first to die during the trial, cut down by Ser [Lyle Bracken](wiki:Lyle_Bracken).(wiki:Dick_Bean.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
