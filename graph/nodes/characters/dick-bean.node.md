## Identity

Dick Bean was a man-at-arms in service of House Targaryen who served since he was a boy (wiki:Dick_Bean.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon-1).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Crownlands (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

When King Maegor I was challenged to a trial of seven by Ser Damon Morrigen, Grand Captain of the Warrior's Sons, Dick was the first to answer Maegor's call to stand beside him, shaming many knights and lords into answering. One account claims Dick was the first to die during the trial, cut down by Ser Lyle Bracken (wiki:Dick_Bean.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon-1). He died in 42 AC in King's Landing (track_b: Died).
