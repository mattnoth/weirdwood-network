## Appearances & Description

Skinner is skilled at [flaying](wiki:Flaying),(wiki:Skinner.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and he enjoys playing [dice](wiki:Dice).(wiki:Skinner.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He believes that [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) murdered [Domeric Bolton](wiki:Domeric_Bolton).(wiki:Skinner.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Skinner flays [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy)'s fingers for [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow). He is present in the great hall of the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort) when [Big Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Jammos)) and [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)) bring Theon before Ramsay, who is joined by [Arnolf Karstark](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark) and [Hother Whoresbane](wiki:Hother_Umber).(wiki:Skinner.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

When Ramsay marries "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)" (actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), Skinner boasts that he will be given a piece of the sheets from the couple's bedchamber.(wiki:Skinner.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Skinner and [Sour Alyn](wiki:Sour_Alyn) toss a freerider from the Battlements Gate after the man angers Ramsay.(wiki:Skinner.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
