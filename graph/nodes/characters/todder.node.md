## Origins

Todder learned bawdy drinking songs in the [winesink](wiki:Winesink) owned by his father.(wiki:Todder.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Appearances & Description

Toad is a short, ugly boy with an unpleasant voice(wiki:Todder.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and a shrill laugh.(wiki:Todder.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He has pig eyes, small and shiny.(wiki:Todder.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Todder follows the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:Todder.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Toad is in the [same class of recruits](wiki:Jon_Snow's_Night's_Watch_recruitment_group) as [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow). Fed up with being beaten daily during sword practice, he is among the recruits who confront Jon after practice. The fight is put to an end by [Donal Noye](wiki:Donal_Noye).(wiki:Todder.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Jon tells Todder and other recruits to stop bullying [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly).(wiki:Todder.cite_ref-Rasos26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

When [Gueren](wiki:Gueren) is reported to be coming up the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad) with new recruits, Toad is among those chosen to become black brothers,(wiki:Todder.cite_ref-Ragot41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and he is named to the [rangers](wiki:Rangers).(wiki:Todder.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

When Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne) calls Jon a traitor's bastard after [the arrest](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark) of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), Jon's father, Toad helps stop Jon from attacking the master-at-arms.(wiki:Todder.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Todder is eager to see [Longclaw](wiki:Longclaw) when Jon receives the [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) sword from [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch).(wiki:Todder.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Toad is among the group of friends who stop Jon from abandoning the Night's Watch and forswearing himself.(wiki:Todder.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Clash of Kings

Although Todder and [Pypar](wiki:Pypar) want to participate in the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) venturing [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall), they remain at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Todder.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) To celebrate [Grenn](wiki:Grenn)'s first ranging, he is purchased a [prostitute](wiki:Prostitute) at the [Mole's Town brothel](wiki:Mole's_Town_brothel) by Pyp, [Halder](wiki:Halder), and Toad.(wiki:Todder.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

When Jon kills [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand) in the [Skirling Pass](wiki:Skirling_Pass) on his command to prove that he has forsaken the Night's Watch so that he can infiltrate the camp of [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder), Jon wonders whether he has lost his friends.(wiki:Todder.cite_ref-Racok68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Samwell mentions Toad to [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) and tells her that he looks like a toad and has an awful voice.(wiki:Todder.cite_ref-Rasos46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Toad celebrates [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow)'s election as the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch).(wiki:Todder.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Toad is one of the friends present in the new common hall who try to invite Lord Commander Snow to sit with them, to no avail. He complains about [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre)'s preaching about [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor). Jon decides to separate his friends and transfers Toad and [Halder](wiki:Halder) west to the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower).(wiki:Todder.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
