## Identity

Daella Targaryen (daughter of Maekar I) (b. 199 AC) — child of Maekar I Targaryen and Dyanna Dayne; sworn to House Targaryen; Princess. One of 2 Daella Targaryens — see the Daella Targaryen disambiguation entry.

## Origins

Born in [199 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest),(wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Cal_1-1) Daella was the eldest daughter of Prince [Maekar Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen) and his wife, Lady [Dyanna Dayne](wiki:Dyanna_Dayne). She had three older brothers, [Daeron](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)), [Aerion](wiki:Aerion_Targaryen), and [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)), one younger brother, [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen), and one younger sister, [Rhae](wiki:Rhae_Targaryen).(wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf101.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Once, in their youth, Daella's sister [Rhae](wiki:Rhae_Targaryen) gave a [love potion](wiki:Love_potion) to their youngest brother, [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen), so he would marry Rhae, instead of Daella.(wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Daella may have been betrothed to her youngest brother, Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen).(wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-4) Like most of their siblings, Daella called Aegon "Egg," the nickname coined by Aemon.(wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Later in life, Daella was married, and had children.(wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
