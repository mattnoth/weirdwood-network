## Appearances & Description

Allard is the rashest of Davos Seaworth's sons. Davos feels if he had remained a smuggler, Allard might have ended up sentenced to the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Allard_Seaworth.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Allard is pleased with a chance to prove his valor, even if dangerous.(wiki:Allard_Seaworth.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Allard has girlfriends in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), and [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Allard_Seaworth.cite_ref-Rasos5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

During [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre)'s burning of the [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) [sept](wiki:Sept)'s statues of the [Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), Allard expresses his disapproval, but only in a low voice so not to be overheard by any believers in [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor). King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) orders Allard to travel to Braavos and the other [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) to deliver a letter to their rulers. The letter accuses Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) of an incestuous relationship with her brother, Ser [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), denying the legitimacy of her children and asserting Stannis's claim to the throne of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Allard_Seaworth.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Planning for the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s admiral, [Lord High Captain](wiki:Lord_High_Captain) Ser [Imry Florent](wiki:Imry_Florent), assigns Davos Seaworth and his sons' ships a place in the second line of battle, on the dangerous starboard wing. Allard voices his belief that it is a place of honor, but Davos states it is a place of peril. Allard and his other sons look pityingly at him for his nervousness.(wiki:Allard_Seaworth.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Allard battles on *Lady Marya* during the Battle of the Blackwater, fighting in the naval combat on the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush). During the fight, the *Lady Marya* is caught in an explosion of [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire), destroying it and killing Allard.(wiki:Allard_Seaworth.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)(wiki:Allard_Seaworth.cite_ref-Rasos5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Davos, a survivor of the Battle of the Blackwater, remembers his four sons who died during the battle. He thinks that Allard's girlfriends will weep when they hear about his death.(wiki:Allard_Seaworth.cite_ref-Rasos5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

Davos asks [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan) if *Lady Marya* had managed to escape the destruction of the battle. Salladhor tells him no, and comforts him, saying Allard was a good man.(wiki:Allard_Seaworth.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Davos tells Stannis he planned to murder Melisandre because he believed she sent his sons to the flames, but Stannis tells him the fires were not her work.(wiki:Allard_Seaworth.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
