## Origins

Ser Harrold served in the Kingsguard during Maegor's reign. After King Maegor's mysterious death upon the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), the new young king [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) offered Maegor's surviving Kingsguard a choice between execution or [taking the black](wiki:Night's_Watch). Harrold was the only one of the surviving white cloaks who did not take the black, but instead asked for [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat). He fought and was subsequently slain by Ser [Gyles Morrigen](wiki:Gyles_Morrigen), a young knight from the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands).(wiki:Harrold_Langward.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
