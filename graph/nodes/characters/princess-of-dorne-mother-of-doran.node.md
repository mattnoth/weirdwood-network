## Identity

The Princess of Dorne was the unnamed ruler of Dorne during the reign of King Aerys II Targaryen. She was the mother of Doran, Elia, and Oberyn Martell. Her brother was Prince Lewyn Martell of the Kingsguard (wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran)).

## Narrative Arc

In 247–248 AC, she gave birth to her firstborn Doran. She bore two more sons, Mors and Olyvar, who died in infancy, and miscarried several times. Nine years after Doran, she bore Elia (one month premature but survived), and a year later Oberyn. She was a lady-in-waiting to Princess Rhaella Targaryen and became friends with Joanna Lannister. In 270 AC, King Aerys II visited Sunspear and told her of plans to dig a canal beneath the Red Mountains. She later toured potential marriage matches for Elia and Oberyn, visiting Starfall, the Arbor, Oldtown, the Shield Islands, Crakehall, and Casterly Rock. She had planned with Joanna to match their children but arrived at Casterly Rock to find Joanna dead in childbirth. Tywin Lannister offered only Tyrion for Elia, which she took as an insult (wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran)).
