## Origins

In [247 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) or [248 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), the Princess gave birth to her firstborn child, [Doran](wiki:Doran_Martell). In the years that followed, she gave birth to two more sons, [Mors](wiki:Mors_Martell) and [Olyvar](wiki:Olyvar_Martell), who died during infancy, and she miscarried several times.(wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran).cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran).cite_ref-Rawoiafdoran_martell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Nine years after the birth of Doran, the Princess gave birth to a daughter, [Elia](wiki:Elia_Martell). Although born one month premature, the girl survived. A year later, the Princess gave birth to another son, [Oberyn](wiki:Oberyn_Martell).(wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran).cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

The Princess was one of the [ladies-in-waiting](wiki:Handmaid) of Princess [Rhaella Targaryen](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen). She became friends with [Joanna Lannister](wiki:Joanna_Lannister), another of Rhaella's ladies.(wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran).cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran).cite_ref-6)

In [270 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), during a visit to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear), King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) told the Princess that he would "make the [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) deserts bloom" by digging a great underground canal beneath the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) to bring water down from the [rainwood](wiki:Rainwood). But like most of his grandiose plans, it was quickly forgotten.(wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Several years later, the Princess was accompanied on a journey by her daughter Elia, her younger son Oberyn, and her consort. From Dorne, they travelled to several potential suitors for both Elia and Oberyn. Amongst the places they visited were [Starfall](wiki:Starfall), the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor), [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands), [Crakehall](wiki:Crakehall), and finally [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran).cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) Although she had learned of Joanna Lannister's [death in childbirth](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) while at Oldtown in [273 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), the Princess decided to travel onwards to Casterly Rock. Oberyn believes that his mother had planned with Joanna to marry either Elia or Oberyn to one of Joanna's twins, [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), since there had been an empty cabin on their ship, meant for someone of high birth. Waiting until it was politely decent, the Princess eventually broached the subject of marriage to Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), Joanna's husband. She first suggested a match between Cersei and Oberyn, next a match between Elia and Jaime. Tywin refused both matches brusquely, informing the Princess how Cersei was meant to marry the crown prince, [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen). When Tywin suggested his newborn [dwarf](wiki:Dwarf) son [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to be betrothed to Elia instead, the Princess took it as an insult.(wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran).cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)(wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran).cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran).cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

In [279 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), the Princess succeeded in betrothing Elia to Rhaegar.(wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran).cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)(wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) By the time Elia married the prince in early [280 AC](wiki:280_AC),(wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) the Princess had already died.(wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran).cite_ref-10) On her deathbed, she revealed to her son Oberyn how Tywin Lannister had brusquely refused her suggestions for a betrothal, and that she felt she had defeated him with Elia's betrothal to Rhaegar.(wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran).cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)
