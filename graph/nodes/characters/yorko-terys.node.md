## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

After the *[Titan's Daughter](wiki:Titan's_Daughter)* arrives in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), Captain [Ternesio Terys](wiki:Ternesio_Terys) has his son Yorko row [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) ashore in one of the ship's boats. Yorko rows through the canals, describing some of the notable sights of the city to Arya. When the boat arrives at the dock for the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White), Yorko confirms that Arya knows his name, and replies *"[Valar dohaeris](wiki:Valar_dohaeris)"*, before leaving her there.(wiki:Yorko_Terys.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
