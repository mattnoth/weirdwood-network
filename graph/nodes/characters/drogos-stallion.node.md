## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) rides his stallion to greet [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) outside [Pentos](wiki:Pentos).(wiki:Drogo's_stallion.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

After Drogo becomes ill, he falls off his great red stallion.(wiki:Drogo's_stallion.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [Mirri Maz Duur](wiki:Mirri_Maz_Duur) puts Drogo into a bath, and has his stallion brought into the tent so she can use its blood. [Rakharo](wiki:Rakharo), [Quaro](wiki:Quaro), and [Aggo](wiki:Aggo) force the screaming stallion to the tub, despite the horse being frightened by the stench of death. Mirri Maz Duur chants, then uses a red [bronze](wiki:Bronze) knife covered in glyphs to cut the horse's throat so the blood fills the tub.(wiki:Drogo's_stallion.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Later, as Daenerys walks into Drogo's funeral pyre, she glimpses Drogo on his smoky stallion for just a moment.(wiki:Drogo's_stallion.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
