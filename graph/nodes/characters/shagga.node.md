## Appearances & Description

Shagga is a massive, hairy man dressed in skins.(wiki:Shagga.cite_ref-Ragot42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Shagga.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He has a deep voice and a loud laugh,(wiki:Shagga.cite_ref-Ragot42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) as well as a fierce frown.(wiki:Shagga.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The unwashed Shagga has an overpowering stench.(wiki:Shagga.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Shagga fights with a weapon in each hand.(wiki:Shagga.cite_ref-Ragot42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) He enjoys strong [alcohol](wiki:Alcohol).(wiki:Shagga.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

After [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) wins his [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat) in the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), Shagga is in [Gunthor](wiki:Gunthor)'s band of [Stone Crows](wiki:Stone_Crows) whom Tyrion encounters in the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon). He wields a club in his right hand and an wood-axe in his left. Shagga and other [Vale mountain clansmen](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) agree to serve Tyrion for pay and equipment.(wiki:Shagga.cite_ref-Ragot42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)(wiki:Shagga.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
When they arrive at the camp of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), Shagga is protective of his mare. When the [captain of the guard](wiki:House_Lannister_guards) attempts to block the clansmen from entering the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn), Shagga breaks the captain's sword over his knee and tosses the man against the hearth.(wiki:Shagga.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) After Stone Crows kill one of the [Moon Brothers](wiki:Moon_Brothers), Shagga and [Conn](wiki:Conn) refuse to pay blood money demanded by [Ulf](wiki:Ulf) son of [Umar](wiki:Umar).(wiki:Shagga.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
Now wielding two [steel](wiki:Steel) battle-axes and wearing layers of mail and leather, Shagga fights in the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork) between the [westermen](wiki:Westermen) under Lord Tywin and the [northmen](wiki:Northmen) under Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton). Shagga survives several arrows, but is saddened by the death of his friend, Conn.(wiki:Shagga.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Shagga accompanies Tyrion to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) as a guard for the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King). When Tyrion goes to interrogate [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle), Shagga hacks down the [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester)'s door with three axe swings. Shagga is interested in the serving girl who had been with Pycelle, but the young girl is scared by the clansman. Tyrion has Shagga cut off the Grand Maester's beard to scare him.(wiki:Shagga.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
During the [riots in King's Landing](wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing), Tyrion sends Shagga to protect [Shae](wiki:Shae) in her [manse near the Iron Gate](wiki:Manse_near_the_Iron_Gate).(wiki:Shagga.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
Before the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Shagga and the rest of the mountain clansmen who accompanied Tyrion to King's Landing are sent to the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood) in order to raid and delay [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s forces.(wiki:Shagga.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After the Battle of the Blackwater, Shagga and his clansmen remain in the kingswood, preferring to stay in the forest instead of returning home to the Mountains of the Moon.(wiki:Shagga.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Tyrion is accused of murdering [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) after the king dies at [his wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding). During the trial, Shae claims that Tyrion had threatened to give her to Shagga and [Timett](wiki:Timett) before the battle on the Green Fork.(wiki:Shagga.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Shagga is listed as a clan chief of the Stone Crows, leading a band in the kingswood.(wiki:Shagga.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

While attempting to join the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons), Tyrion mentions Shagga and Timett as examples of men who have benefited from his generosity.(wiki:Shagga.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
