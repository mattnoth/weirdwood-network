## Origins

### Early life

Daemon was born in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros),(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rawoiafdaemon_ii_blackfyre.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) the third son of [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) and [Rohanne of Tyrosh](wiki:Rohanne_of_Tyrosh). He was [named after](wiki:Named_after) his father.(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-11) As a child, he became close friends with [Alyn Cockshaw](wiki:Alyn_Cockshaw), and they were bullied by Daemon's older brothers, [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Blackfyre) and [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Blackfyre). In [196 AC](wiki:196_AC), when he was seven years old, his father began the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion). Daemon dreamed that Aegon and Aemon would die, and told them this, but they only laughed at him. After the death of Daemon's father and his older brothers at the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field), Daemon went into exile in [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) with his surviving family and with his uncle [Aegor "Bittersteel" Rivers](wiki:Aegor_Rivers).(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-12)(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### Second Blackfyre Rebellion

In Tyrosh, Daemon dreamed that a [dragon](wiki:Dragon) would be born at [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls). Lord [Gormon Peake](wiki:Gormon_Peake), a Blackfyre supporter, somehow heard of this, and convinced Daemon to cross the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) and make a play for the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). The pretext for gathering other [lords](wiki:Lord) to support Daemon was a [wedding tourney](wiki:Whitewalls_tourney) that Lord [Ambrose Butterwell](wiki:Ambrose_Butterwell) would hold in [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), at which the grand prize would be Butterwell's [dragon egg](wiki:Dragon_egg). However, Bittersteel did not think much of Peake's plan, and mistrusted Daemon's dreams (or perhaps was troubled by his homosexuality), and so did not give him his support nor the sword [Blackfyre](wiki:Blackfyre).(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-13)(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf413.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Under the guise of the [hedge knight](wiki:Hedge_knight) Ser John the Fiddler, Daemon happened across Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) and [Egg](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) while heading to [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) for the tourney. Daemon was very friendly to Dunk, and suggested he come to the tourney. During the wedding feast, he was given a place of honor on the dais, uncommon for someone who was supposed to be a hedge knight. During the [bedding](wiki:Bedding), John had Dunk carry the new [Lady Butterwell](wiki:Lady_Butterwell) upstairs to the bedroom. Afterwards, Daemon, still under the guise of John, approached Duncan and told him about the [dreams](wiki:Dreams) he was having, about Duncan wearing a [white cloak](wiki:Kingsguard) in the future. He tried to win Duncan over to his side, but Duncan remained unsure, and then they were interrupted by Lord [Gormon Peake](wiki:Gormon_Peake). Alyn Cockshaw, who had been ecstatic at Daemon's return to Westeros, deeply resented Dunk for the loss of Daemon's attentions, and became his secret enemy.(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-14)

During the tourney, Daemon unseated Ser [Franklyn Frey](wiki:Franklyn_Frey) and Ser [Galtry the Green](wiki:Galtry_the_Green), but Dunk discovered that Peake had been bribing Daemon's opponents so that he would win the tourney. The dragon egg went missing, and Ser [Glendon Flowers](wiki:Glendon_Flowers), who had turned down Peake's bribe, was charged with its theft and taken away to be [tortured](wiki:Torture). Duncan learned of Daemon's true identity via Alyn Cockshaw and [Maynard Plumm](wiki:Maynard_Plumm), and that evening approached him at the feast, publicly addressing him as "Daemon" and "Your Grace", to tell him that Glendon had been framed. Daemon decided to prove his tourney skills, and also give Glendon the power to defend himself against the charges, through a [trial by battle](wiki:Trial_by_battle) with Daemon himself. While Daemon chose a war lance, Duncan suggested to Glendon that he use a tourney lance instead because of its longer length (as he had learned from Prince [Baelor Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)) in his [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven) at the [Ashford Tourney](wiki:Ashford_Tourney)). Glendon, though still suffering from the effects of the torture, unhorsed Daemon in the first tilt of their joust. Daemon landed in a mud puddle, gaining the epithet "the Brown Dragon".(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-15)

News quickly spread that Lord [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers), Bloodraven, was marching on Whitewalls to end what had become the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion). Daemon tried to inspire the armsmen who were within the castle, but they had no intention of fighting for such a weak warrior who did not even have Blackfyre. Daemon met the oncoming army alone and challenged Bloodraven to single combat, but was instead arrested by Lord Rivers.(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-16)

### Later life

Bloodraven kept Daemon alive as a [hostage](wiki:Hostage) to prevent [Aegor Rivers](wiki:Aegor_Rivers) from crowning Daemon's younger brother, [Haegon](wiki:Haegon_I_Blackfyre).(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-17) However, Daemon the Younger died a few years later while a hostage in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), and Haegon and Bittersteel launched the [Third Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Third_Blackfyre_Rebellion).(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf413.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

## Appearances & Description

When meeting Daemon under his alias of "Ser John the Fiddler", Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) thought of him as a young man, lean and lithe, with a handsome clean-shaven face and fine features. He had collar-length black hair, and sparkling eyes that caught the deep blue of his doublet. Dunk later recognized that he had "[Egg](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen)'s eyes", that is, [dark purple](wiki:Purple_eyes). After revealing himself as the son of [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre), Daemon the Younger washed the black [dye](wiki:Hair_dye) from his hair to reveal its true silver-gold color.(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

As John the Fiddler, Daemon wore a doublet made of dark blue [silk](wiki:Silk) edged in [gold](wiki:Gold) satin. The doublet's chest was embroidered with gold thread to depict his [sigil](wiki:Sigil), an engrailed cross with a golden [fiddle](wiki:Fiddle) and golden [sword](wiki:Sword) in the opposite quarters. He also wore a white silk doublet had with dagged sleeves lined with red satin, so long their points drooped down past his knees, and a heavy [silver](wiki:Silver) chain studded with large dark [amethysts](wiki:Amethyst) whose color matched his eyes. He also owned a purple tunic that brought out the color of his eyes. John wore enameled blue armor with gilded ringmail,(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6) and per a semi-canon source, his helmet was also gilded.(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-tmkcomic_5-1) His [horse](wiki:Horse)'s trappings were blue silk with golden fiddles and golden swords. After Daemon revealed himself, the trappings of his warhorse displayed the three-headed [dragon](wiki:Dragon) of [House Blackfyre](wiki:House_Blackfyre), black on a field of red.(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7) According to a semi-canon source, Daemon's armor was enameled red, with a red helm crested with a black dragon.(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-tmkcomic_5-2)

According to Duncan, Daemon smelled of oranges and [limes](wiki:Lemon), with a hint of some strange eastern [spice](wiki:Spice) beneath.(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8)

Daemon had a love of music and fine things. He had a close relationship with Lord [Alyn Cockshaw](wiki:Alyn_Cockshaw) and appeared to be attracted to Dunk.(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-9)(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf413.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) [George R. R. Martin](wiki:George_R._R._Martin) has confirmed that Daemon was gay.(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-7)

Mercurial in temperament,(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rawoiafdaemon_ii_blackfyre.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Daemon had [prophetic dreams](wiki:Dragon_dreams). He correctly dreamed that his brothers [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Blackfyre) and [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Blackfyre) would die, and that Dunk would become a [knight](wiki:Knight) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). He also dreamed that a [dragon](wiki:Dragon) would hatch from an [egg](wiki:Dragon_egg) at [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls), but this turned out to be a metaphor for [Aegon "Egg" Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) revealing himself as a [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) prince.(wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-10)
