## Identity

Ser Jaime Lannister, known as the Kingslayer, is a knight from House Lannister. He is the second child and first-born son of Lord Tywin Lannister and Lady Joanna Lannister, and the twin brother of Queen Cersei Lannister. Raised at fifteen to the Kingsguard of King Aerys II Targaryen, he was the youngest member in the history of the order. He earned the name "Kingslayer" when he slew Aerys near the end of Robert's Rebellion. He becomes a POV character in A Storm of Swords (wiki:Jaime_Lannister).

## Appearances & Description

Jaime is tall and handsome, with curled hair the color of beaten gold and flashing cat-green eyes. Jon Snow thinks "this is what a king should look like" and Bran feels Jaime looks like the knights in the stories (wiki:Jaime_Lannister, agot-jon-01, agot-bran-02). He is extremely strong and muscular. As a Kingsguard knight he wears white armor and a white cloak; he also wears golden armor with the Lannister lion and a golden lance (wiki:Jaime_Lannister). He is a born warrior with little interest in politics. By his own admission, he only feels truly alive when fighting or making love. He comes across as arrogant, amoral, and dishonorable — though his reputation is worse than the reality. He is rash, headstrong, and angers quickly (wiki:Jaime_Lannister). Pass 1 confirms: tangled golden hair, very strong, deep gouges in forearm from Bran's fingers (agot-bran-02); glittering golden armor, ornate lion helmet (agot-eddard-07, agot-sansa-02).

## Narrative Arc

In AGOT, Jaime pushes Bran Stark from a tower window after the boy discovers his incestuous relationship with Cersei (agot-bran-02). He ambushes Ned Stark's party in King's Landing after Catelyn arrests Tyrion, killing Ned's men Jory, Heward, and Wyl (agot-eddard-09). Jaime then commands the Lannister force besieging Riverrun but is captured by Robb Stark in the Whispering Wood, killing Torrhen and Eddard Karstark and Daryn Hornwood before being taken (agot-catelyn-10).

In ACOK, Jaime remains imprisoned at Riverrun. In ASOS, Catelyn releases him with Brienne of Tarth as escort to exchange for Sansa and Arya. During the journey, Jaime loses his sword hand when the Brave Companions' Vargo Hoat orders it cut off. This trauma catalyzes his transformation — he reveals to Brienne the true story of why he killed Aerys, who planned to burn King's Landing with wildfire. He rescues Brienne from a bear pit at Harrenhal.

In AFFC, Jaime refuses Cersei's offer to be Hand of the King and is named Lord Commander of the Kingsguard. He leads the campaign to end the siege of Riverrun and bring the riverlands to heel, developing into a more diplomatic and thoughtful commander. He burns Cersei's letter pleading for his help when she is arrested by the Faith. In ADWD, Jaime disappears from his camp after Brienne finds him, claiming the Hound has Sansa Stark (wiki:Jaime_Lannister).
