## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Andrik](wiki:Category:Images_of_Andrik)*

Andrik is a huge man with thick arms.(wiki:Andrik_the_Unsmiling.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) According to singers, he is the fiercest living [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) warrior.(wiki:Andrik_the_Unsmiling.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

The taciturn Andrik has a reputation for not smiling.(wiki:Andrik_the_Unsmiling.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Andrik accompanies Lord [Dunstan Drumm](wiki:Dunstan_Drumm) to the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk) and is, along with [Denys](wiki:Denys_Drumm) and [Donnel Drumm](wiki:Donnel_Drumm), one of Lord Drumm's champions during the [choosing](wiki:Choosing). [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) is chosen [king](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North), however.(wiki:Andrik_the_Unsmiling.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Wearing rings on each finger and with a woman under each arm, Andrik celebrates with the other ironborn in [Lord Hewett's castle](wiki:Lord_Hewett's_castle) after the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields). Andrik, Dunstan's strong right arm, is made Lord of [Southshield](wiki:Southshield) by King Euron during the feast; [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) sees this as part of Euron's plan to buy or remove the supporters of his rivals.(wiki:Andrik_the_Unsmiling.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
