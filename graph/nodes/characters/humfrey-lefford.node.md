## Origins

When the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) broke out in [129 AC](wiki:129_AC), Lord Humfrey followed his liege Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister) in siding with King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) and the [greens](wiki:Greens).(wiki:Humfrey_Lefford.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) The formidable [westermen](wiki:Westermen) host moved east to join Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Humfrey_Lefford.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
By [130 AC](wiki:130_AC), Humfrey assumed command of the westermen host after the deaths of Lord Lannister at the [Red Fork](wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork) and Ser [Adrian Tarbeck](wiki:Adrian_Tarbeck) near [Acorn Hall](wiki:Acorn_Hall). Lord Lefford had suffered numerous wounds in battle, and the aged lord had to be transported on a litter while the westermen slowly marched east towards Harrenhal, unaware that fresh levies from the [north](wiki:North) were descending upon them.(wiki:Humfrey_Lefford.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
Upon reaching the western shores of the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye), the westermen encountered a large army athwart their path. When Lord Lefford halted to confront the foes before him, [black](wiki:Blacks) levies descended upon him from the south. Hesitant to move against either army, Lord Lefford had the westermen put their back towards the Gods Eye, dig in, and send [ravens](wiki:Raven) to Harrenhal asking Prince Aemond for aid, but the birds were shot down by [Robb Rivers](wiki:Robb_Rivers) and his archers. When more rivermen turned up the next day, Humfrey was slain in the ensuing [Battle by the Lakeshore](wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore), the bloodiest battle of the war.(wiki:Humfrey_Lefford.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)(wiki:Humfrey_Lefford.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Humfrey_Lefford.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
