## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Tarle the Thrice-Drowned](wiki:Tarle_the_Thrice-Drowned) claims that Maron, as heir to the "black line" of [House Hoare](wiki:House_Hoare), has the best claim to the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair) after the passing of King [Balon IX Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy).(wiki:Maron_Volmark.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Lord Volmark, however, is one of [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy)'s supporters at the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Maron_Volmark.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

After [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) is chosen king, he commands the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) to attack the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands). During the feast celebrating the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), a hesitant Maron is named the new Lord of [Greenshield](wiki:Greenshield) by King Euron.(wiki:Maron_Volmark.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
