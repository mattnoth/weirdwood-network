## Origins

Elaena married her brother, [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Gaemon)), and together they had two sons: [Maegon](wiki:Maegon_Targaryen) and [Aerys Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon)).(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Gaemon).cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Elaena and Aegon also had a younger sister who married a petty [lord](wiki:Lord).(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Gaemon).cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Elaena and Aegon inherited the lordship of Dragonstone from their father and mother, they jointly ruled Dragonstone and were succeeded by Maegon.(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Gaemon).cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
