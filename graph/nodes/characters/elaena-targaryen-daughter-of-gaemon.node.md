## Identity

Elaena Targaryen was an early Targaryen noblewoman, Lady of Dragonstone, daughter of Gaemon Targaryen and Daenys Targaryen (track_b: Father, Mother).

## Origins

Born circa 114–80 BC on Dragonstone (track_b: Born). Of Valyrian race (track_b: Race).

## Narrative Arc

Elaena held the title Lady of Dragonstone (track_b: Title). She married Aegon Targaryen (track_b: Spouse) and bore two children: Maegon Targaryen and Aerys Targaryen (track_b: Issue).
