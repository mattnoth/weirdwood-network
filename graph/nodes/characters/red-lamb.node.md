## Identity

The Red Lamb is a former slave and Lhazarene freedman in Meereen, training to become a knight under Ser Barristan Selmy. His real name is not known (wiki:Red_Lamb.cite_ref-Radwd67).

## Origins

Born 279–285 AC in Lhazar (track_b: Born). The Dothraki mock Lhazarene as "Lamb Men" (wiki:Red_Lamb.cite_ref-Radwd2).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Lhazarene (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Freed when Daenerys conquered Meereen, the Red Lamb was taken as a squire by Ser Barristan Selmy and trained in the Great Pyramid to fight with lance and longsword in Westerosi fashion. Barristan considers him one of his best squires after Larraq and Tumco Lho — all ferocity with no technique, though improvable with training. Barristan later knighted him, and the Red Lamb accompanied Ser Tumco when Barristan visited the dungeons to inform Gerris Drinkwater and Archibald Yronwood of Quentyn Martell's death. On the eve of the second siege of Meereen, Barristan gave him a great silver-banded warhorn to sound commands. During the battle, the Red Lamb caught the fleeing Paezhar zo Myraq (the Little Pigeon) and killed him with a mace blow to the head (wiki:Red_Lamb.cite_ref-Radwd67, wiki:Red_Lamb.cite_ref-Radwd70).
