## Appearances & Description

Ternesio keeps his grey hair(wiki:Ternesio_Terys.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) cut short and neat. He has a square, windburnt face, that he keeps shaven.(wiki:Ternesio_Terys.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Ternesio is stout, and wears a long purple wool captain's coat.(wiki:Ternesio_Terys.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Ternesio_Terys.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Ternesio speaks the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue) well,(wiki:Ternesio_Terys.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)(wiki:Ternesio_Terys.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) in addition to the Braavosi tongue. He has a deep voice. Ternesio often jokes with his crew, but when he frowns, they run.(wiki:Ternesio_Terys.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Ternesio Terys meets [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) at [Saltpans](wiki:Saltpans), where the *[Titan's Daughter](wiki:Titan's_Daughter)* is docked. Arya asks for passage to the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) castle [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea), but Ternesio refuses her, as she has little [money](wiki:Money), and the [north](wiki:North) is too dangerous. However, when Arya shows him the [iron coin](wiki:Faceless_Men) that was given to her by [Jaqen H'ghar](wiki:Jaqen_H'ghar), and says the phrase *"[Valar morghulis](wiki:Valar_morghulis)"* as Jaqen had taught her, Ternesio replies, *"[Valar dohaeris](wiki:Valar_dohaeris)"*, and offers her a cabin.(wiki:Ternesio_Terys.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

### A Feast for Crows

Ternesio captains the *Titan's Daughter* from [Saltpans](wiki:Saltpans) to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos). When the ship arrives at Braavos, he tells Arya that she has no need to wait for the customs check, and that he will lower a boat for his son [Yorko](wiki:Yorko_Terys) to row her ashore. Arya wishes she could stay aboard the ship, but she can tell by the captain's anxiousness that he wants to be rid of her. Before Arya leaves, Ternesio asks her to remember his name, and the service he did for her.(wiki:Ternesio_Terys.cite_ref-Raffc6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
