## Appearances & Description

Othell is a big man with a long lantern jaw.(wiki:Othell_Yarwyck.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

According to [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), Othell is stolid and taciturn, but also unimaginative.(wiki:Othell_Yarwyck.cite_ref-Radwd21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [Cotter Pyke](wiki:Cotter_Pyke) considers the [First Builder](wiki:First_Builder) to be a follower.(wiki:Othell_Yarwyck.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Othell considers [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome) an unholy place.(wiki:Othell_Yarwyck.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He dislikes the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) because of raiders like the [Weeper](wiki:Weeper),(wiki:Othell_Yarwyck.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and he considers the wildlings to be undisciplined.(wiki:Othell_Yarwyck.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Othell is present as [First Builder](wiki:First_Builder) when the new members of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) swear their vows.(wiki:Othell_Yarwyck.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Othell believes the [Lance](wiki:Lance_(tower)), the tallest tower of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), is unstable and risk to topple soon.(wiki:Othell_Yarwyck.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Othell survives the [fight at the Bridge of Skulls](wiki:Fight_at_the_Bridge_of_Skulls).(wiki:Othell_Yarwyck.cite_ref-Rawoiafothell_yarwyck.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

The brothers of the Night's Watch left to defend the [Wall](wiki:Wall) from the [assault by the wildlings](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall) name one of the straw dummies after Othell.(wiki:Othell_Yarwyck.cite_ref-Rasos69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

During the [choosing](wiki:Choosing) of the new [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), Othell puts his name forth.(wiki:Othell_Yarwyck.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) After withdrawing his name from the election, he eventually changes his support from [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) to [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow).(wiki:Othell_Yarwyck.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Othell and [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) want to seal the gates of the [Wall](wiki:Wall) to prevent [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) coming south.(wiki:Othell_Yarwyck.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Othell and his builders are sent by Lord Commander [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) to restore the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort).(wiki:Othell_Yarwyck.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Othell later reports back on the advancement of their work. Jon offers him [Wun Weg Wun Dar Wun](wiki:Wun_Weg_Wun_Dar_Wun), but Othell declines the aid of the [giant](wiki:Giant).(wiki:Othell_Yarwyck.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Jon notes Othell's absence, among others, at the [wedding](wiki:Wedding) of [Alys Karstark](wiki:Alys_Karstark) and [Sigorn](wiki:Sigorn), the [Magnar of Thenn](wiki:Magnar_of_Thenn).(wiki:Othell_Yarwyck.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Jon orders a reluctant Othell to provide carts and wagons for [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) who are coming through the Wall.(wiki:Othell_Yarwyck.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

When Jon advises Othell to have wildlings help his [builders](wiki:Builders), Othell explains that most wildlings are poor workers.(wiki:Othell_Yarwyck.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Othell disapproves of Jon's decision to send [Borroq](wiki:Borroq), a wildling [skinchanger](wiki:Skinchanger) capable of entering the mind of a huge [boar](wiki:Boar), to [Stonedoor](wiki:Stonedoor) as the nearby woods are full of wild boars. Othell departs the [Shieldhall](wiki:Shieldhall) when Jon announces his intent on marching south to confront [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow).(wiki:Othell_Yarwyck.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)
