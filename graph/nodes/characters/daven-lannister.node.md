## Origins

Ser [Stafford Lannister](wiki:Stafford_Lannister) had been negotiating to betroth his son Daven to [Desmera Redwyne](wiki:Desmera_Redwyne), although an agreement had not been reached with Lord [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne).(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

## Appearances & Description

Daven has lively hazel eyes, a pug nose, long thick and tangled yellow hair, and a big chin covered by a huge mane of a beard.(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He is blunt and outspoken, but also jovial.(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) do not think much of the capabilities of Ser [Stafford Lannister](wiki:Stafford_Lannister), but they hold his son Daven in higher esteem.(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Daven wears [gilded](wiki:Gold) ringmail, a helm, and a fox-fur cloak.(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When fighting breaks out in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), Ser Daven offers Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) to lead the foraging for the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) host, but he is refused.(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) Tywin instead sends out Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane), Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch), and [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat).(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Daven's father, Ser [Stafford Lannister](wiki:Stafford_Lannister), is killed in the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross) by Lord [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark).(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Daven vows to let his hair grow until he has avenged his father.(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

### A Storm of Swords

After the [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) army returns to the riverlands, Daven gathers the remaining soldiers of his father's army at [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport).(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

[Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), the [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), executes Lord Karstark for [treason](wiki:Treason) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), as Rickard had killed [Willem Lannister](wiki:Willem_Lannister) and [Tion Frey](wiki:Tion_Frey) to avenge his own sons' deaths at the hands of Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) in the [Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Daven is thus robbed of his chance to take revenge on Rickard, the man who killed his father. Daven keeps letting his hair grow, although his vow has become obsolete.(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)

As part of the pact between Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) and Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) concerning the betrayal of Robb Stark, Daven is to wed a [Frey](wiki:House_Frey) to consolidate the alliance between the two houses.(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

After the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Daven and Ser [Forley Prester](wiki:Forley_Prester) join forces at the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth) and meet up with Ser [Ryman Frey](wiki:Ryman_Frey) to besiege Riverrun.(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Daven now has long shaggy hair, a bushy mustache, sidewhiskers as thick as a hedgerow, and a bristling beard.(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7)(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) After Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister)'s refusal to serve as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) names Daven [Warden of the West](wiki:Warden_of_the_West) and commands him to lay siege to Riverrun, where Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) still holds a sizable garrison.(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

The [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun) does not go easy. Brynden has taken all the food he could find to Riverrun, so the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) army cannot live off the land. Furthermore, there are quarrels among the besiegers. The [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) do not help their allies and only gather food for themselves. Daven does not like the Freys, but he intends to wed one, as he knows what happened to Robb Stark in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-8) In addition, a part of the forces consists of riverlords who have surrendered. Although taken back in the [king's peace](wiki:King's_peace), they are still less than enthusiastic about fighting their former allies. Daven wants to storm the castle, but is not able to persuade his commanders, especially Ser [Emmon Frey](wiki:Emmon_Frey).(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-9)

When Jaime, the [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard), arrives to take command of the siege, Daven is pleased to be relieved of this task. He jokes about Jaime's missing hand and happily informs Jaime of the siege's troubles.(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-10) Daven talks with Jaime about the true loyalty of the rivermen in front of some of their pages, [Lewys Piper](wiki:Lewys_Piper) and [Garrett Paege](wiki:Garrett_Paege). During the war council, he openly says that Ser [Ryman Frey](wiki:Ryman_Frey) is drunk instead of sick.(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) Jaime successfully negotiates the surrender of Riverrun, although Brynden the Blackfish is able to escape.(wiki:Daven_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
