## Identity

Ser Daven Lannister is a knight from House Lannister, son of Ser Stafford Lannister, a cousin and brother-in-law of Lord Tywin Lannister of Casterly Rock (wiki:Daven_Lannister).

## Origins

Born circa 272 AC in the Westerlands (track_b: Born). Son of Stafford Lannister and Myranda Lefford (track_b: Father, Mother).

## Appearances & Description

Daven has lively hazel eyes, a pug nose, long thick and tangled yellow hair, and a big chin covered by a huge mane of a beard. He is blunt and outspoken, but also jovial. Both Jaime Lannister and Ser Brynden Tully hold Daven in higher esteem than they do his father Stafford. Daven wears gilded ringmail, a helm, and a fox-fur cloak (wiki:Daven_Lannister).

## Narrative Arc

When fighting breaks out in the riverlands, Daven offers Lord Tywin to lead the foraging for the Lannister host but is refused. After his father Stafford is killed at the Battle of Oxcross by Lord Rickard Karstark, Daven vows to grow a beard and not to cut it until he has avenged his father (wiki:Daven_Lannister).

Daven wins the Battle of the Red Fork, where he defeats Marq Piper and Clement Piper, and then joins Lord Tywin's host at Harrenhal. During ASOS, Daven commands the force that pursues Ser Brynden Tully after the Red Wedding. In AFFC, Cersei names Daven the new Warden of the West — a role previously held by Tywin. Daven leads the siege of Riverrun alongside Jaime, where he proves a capable commander. He is betrothed to a Frey girl as part of the Lannister-Frey alliance, though he makes jests about marrying a weasel (wiki:Daven_Lannister).
