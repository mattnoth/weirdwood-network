## Identity

Arya Stark is the third child and second daughter of Lord Eddard Stark and Lady Catelyn Tully. A major POV character across all five books. She has five siblings: Robb, Sansa, Bran, Rickon, and half-brother Jon Snow. Her direwolf is Nymeria, named for the Rhoynar warrior-queen (wiki:Arya_Stark).

## Origins

Born 289 AC at Winterfell. Daughter of Eddard Stark and Catelyn Tully (track_b: Father, track_b: Mother).

## Appearances & Description

Nine years old at the start of AGOT. More Stark than Tully in appearance: long face, grey eyes, brown hair. Skinny and athletic. Often mistaken for a boy. Bullied as "Arya Horseface" by her sister and other girls at Winterfell. Others describe her as pretty. Eddard says she looks like her late aunt Lyanna Stark. Left-handed, quick, and dexterous. Excels at mathematics, household management, and horseback riding. Knows some High Valyrian (wiki:Arya_Stark).

## Narrative Arc

Arya receives the sword Needle from Jon Snow before traveling south to King's Landing with her father. On the kingsroad, an incident with Prince Joffrey leads to her direwolf Nymeria being driven away and Sansa's Lady being killed. In King's Landing, Eddard hires Syrio Forel to teach Arya water dancing. When the Lannisters purge the Stark household, Syrio is killed defending Arya, and she escapes. Yoren of the Night's Watch takes her north disguised as "Arry," but the group is attacked and Arya ends up a captive at Harrenhal under Lannister occupation, where she serves as cupbearer to Roose Bolton and uses Jaqen H'ghar's three kills. After escaping Harrenhal, she is captured by the Brotherhood Without Banners and then by Sandor Clegane. She arrives at the Twins during the Red Wedding, witnessing the aftermath. The Hound abandons her wounded, and she takes a ship to Braavos using the iron coin from Jaqen. In Braavos, Arya enters training at the House of Black and White under the Faceless Men, enduring blindness and identity dissolution while maintaining a secret connection to Nymeria through wolf dreams (wiki:Arya_Stark).
