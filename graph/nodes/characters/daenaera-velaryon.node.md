## Origins

Daenaera's mother, [Hazel Harte](wiki:Hazel_Harte), was one of the casualties of the [Winter Fever](wiki:Winter_Fever). Her father, [Daeron Velaryon](wiki:Daeron_Velaryon), drowned in the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) during [Lord Alyn's attack](wiki:Lord_Alyn's_attack) in the [Daughters' War](wiki:Daughters'_War) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC). Daenaera was left an orphan at the age of six. Lady [Baela Targaryen](wiki:Baela_Targaryen) and her husband, Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon), took her as their [ward](wiki:Ward) afterward.(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

### The Maiden's Day Ball

Young King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen)'s first marriage to Princess [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen) had ended tragically in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), with Jaehaera's apparent suicide. The [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake), attempted to betroth his own daughter to the thirteen-year-old king, but was prevented in this by [Aegon's regents](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III). However, Lord Peake did not give up his attempts, and declared [a ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball) to be held in the throne room of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) on [Maiden's Day](wiki:Maiden's_Day) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC) to find a new wife for the king, with his daughter as one of the nearly thousand maidens presented to Aegon.(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

However, at almost the very end of the ball, Aegon's half-sisters [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daemon)) and [Baela Targaryen](wiki:Baela_Targaryen) brought forth Daenaera, telling Aegon that they had found his new queen.(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) Even though she was only six years old, Daenaera was seen as breathtakingly beautiful, and people believed the happy little girl would be a perfect antidote for the young king's gloom. When Aegon returned her smile and told her she looked very pretty, all knew who his selection would be. Daenaera wed King Aegon on the last day of the year [133 AC](wiki:133_AC).(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)

### The poisoned tarts and the secret siege

Lord Peake was greatly frustrated by Aegon's choice of Daenaera, as her young age prevented his plans for Aegon to produce a heir of his own.(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8) He also took his daughter's rejection in favor of Daenaera as a personal affront.(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) His efforts to have the marriage put aside were opposed by both Aegon and the other regents, and was one of the factors that eventually resulted in his resignation as Hand in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC).(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)

Daenaera's happy nature did briefly improve Aegon's gloomy temperament, and on three occasions he took his new queen out of the Red Keep to show her the sights of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). But in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC), when Aegon's brother Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen) was recovered from [Lys](wiki:Lys) by Alyn Velaryon, he became the king's constant companion, and Daenaera was neglected.(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) However, in [135 AC](wiki:135_AC), when Viserys and Aegon quarreled over the king forcing his brother to send his treasured [dragon egg](wiki:Dragon_egg) away to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), Aegon began to spend more time with Daenaera again. Unfortunately, it was during a quiet dinner that Aegon shared with his queen, as well as his friend and [food taster](wiki:Food_taster) [Gaemon Palehair](wiki:Gaemon_Palehair), that Gaemon collapsed from belly cramps and Daenaera also suffered from stomach pains.(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5)(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Munkun](wiki:Munkun) gave Daenaera a powerful purgative, which probably saved her life, but Gaemon died within the hour. Viserys mended his rift with his mourning brother as they sat by Daenaera's bedside.(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

It was determined that the [poison](wiki:Poison) was the [tears of Lys](wiki:Tears_of_Lys), baked in the apple tarts. As Aegon usually did not eat sweets, he was not harmed. However, after the downfall of [House Rogare](wiki:House_Rogare), the Hand [Thaddeus Rowan](wiki:Thaddeus_Rowan) was [tortured](wiki:Torture) until he falsely confessed to plotting with the Rogares to poison both King Aegon and Queen Daenaera, to replace them with Prince Viserys and his wife [Larra Rogare](wiki:Larra_Rogare). During the [secret siege](wiki:Secret_siege) of [Maegor's Holdfast](wiki:Maegor's_Holdfast), Aegon and Viserys refused entry for eighteen days to those demanding the arrest of Lady Larra. Though the truth of the plot was revealed and the real perpetrators arrested (except for the [secret mastermind](wiki:Unwin_Peake) behind it all), Aegon and Viserys continued to be mistrustful and did not open the gates of Maegor's Holdfast until they saw Munkun send [ravens](wiki:Raven) to the king's leal [lords](wiki:Lord). By that point, they had run so short of food within the holdfast that Daenaera cried herself to sleep at night, and two of her [lady companions](wiki:Lady_companion) were so weak from hunger that they had to be helped across the moat.(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Queen Daenaera did not attend the [treason](wiki:Treason) trials in [136 AC](wiki:136_AC). Three of those put on trial were [highborn](wiki:Highborn) ladies, her attendants: [Lucinda Penrose](wiki:Lucinda_Penrose), [Cassandra Baratheon](wiki:Cassandra_Baratheon), and [Priscella Hogg](wiki:Priscella_Hogg). Lucinda admitted to wanting Daenaera dead, saying that if she had not been [attacked and disfigured](wiki:Maiden's_Day_curse) before the Maiden's Day Ball, Lucinda would be queen and Daenaera would be serving her. It was strongly suspected that Lady [Clarice Osgrey](wiki:Clarice_Osgrey), aunt of Unwin Peake and supervisor of all Queen Daenaera's maids, companions, and attendants, was also involved with the conspiracy, but no-one would confess to her complicity.(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

### The grand progress that never was

The new Hand of the King, Lord [Torrhen Manderly](wiki:Torrhen_Manderly), did not care for the "sullen" King Aegon, but he doted upon Queen Daenaera. As the king's sixteenth [name day](wiki:Name_day) was approaching in [136 AC](wiki:136_AC), the Hand decided that Aegon and Daenaera should make a royal progress for his [coming of age](wiki:Coming_of_age). Torrhen felt that the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) would love the sweet young queen, and she would make up for any charm that Aegon might lack. The regents agreed, and the spring progress, planned to take a full year across all the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), would be a true beginning for Aegon's reign, and show [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) healing from the scars of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). Detailed plans were made for the progress, down to the clothing the royals would wear: on days when Daenaera would wear [green](wiki:Greens), Aegon would wear [black](wiki:Blacks), and on days when the queen would wear Targaryen red-and-black, the king would wear a green cloak. However, on Aegon's name day, he coldly dismissed his regents and Hand, and canceled the progress.(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

### Later life

While Aegon and Daenaera were married when he was thirteen and she only six years old, the marriage was not consummated until long after Daenaera had [flowered](wiki:Flowered). Aegon was very melancholy, and had come to dislike being touched, even by the hand of his beautiful queen.(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-6) But eventually Aegon called Daenaera to his bed, and they had five children in their marriage: [Daeron](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen) (born [143 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)), [Baelor](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen) (born [144 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)), [Daena](wiki:Daena_Targaryen) (born [145 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)), [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon_III)) (born [147 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)), and [Elaena](wiki:Elaena_Targaryen) (born [150 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)). King Aegon III died in [157 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) It is currently unknown if Daenaera outlived her husband.

## Appearances & Description

Daenaera was a "surpassingly beautiful" child, and was also called beautiful when she came to adulthood.(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) She had long silver hair laced with gold. Her eyes were "blue as a summer sea" and her skin was "smooth and pale as winter snow". At age six, Daenaera had a sweet and bold and mischievous smile, that made people think of her as a bright, sweet, and happy little girl.(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) At her presentation at the [Maiden's Day Ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball), Daenaera wore a gown of pale white [silk](wiki:Silk), [Myrish](wiki:Myr) lace, and [pearls](wiki:Pearl).(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

She had a sunny nature,(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) and charmed the people with her happy waves, flushed cheeks, and shy, sweet smiles.(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) When [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daemon)) first took flight on her [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [Morning](wiki:Morning), the sight so pleased Daenaera she was heard to say that she wanted a dragon of her own.(wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
