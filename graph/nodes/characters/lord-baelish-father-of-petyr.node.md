## Origins

His [grandfather](wiki:Baelish_(sellsword)) was a [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) [sellsword](wiki:Baelish_(sellsword)) in the service of Lord [Corbray](wiki:House_Corbray), and his [father](wiki:Baelish_(hedge_knight)) was a [hedge knight](wiki:Hedge_knight) who took the head of the [Titan of Braavos](wiki:Titan_of_Braavos) as his sigil when he was [knighted](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Lord_Baelish_(father_of_Petyr).cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

During the [War of the Ninepenny Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Ninepenny_Kings), Lord Baelish became friends with Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully). He later "cashed in" on the friendship to have his son [Petyr](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) [fostered](wiki:Fostered) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Lord_Baelish_(father_of_Petyr).cite_ref-4)

Lord Baelish died several years before the start of the series and was succeeded by his son, Petyr.(wiki:Lord_Baelish_(father_of_Petyr).cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
