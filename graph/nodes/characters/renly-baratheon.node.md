## Origins

Renly was born in [277 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Cal_1-1) at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End),(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rawoiafrenly_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) the youngest of the three sons of Lord [Steffon Baratheon](wiki:Steffon_Baratheon) and Lady [Cassana Estermont](wiki:Cassana_Estermont).(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_targaryen_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) Less than a year later, King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) sent his cousin Steffon to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) to find a bride of [Valyrian](wiki:Valyria) blood for Prince [Rhaegar](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen).(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) Steffon was unable to do so, although he and Cassana did send a message to Storm's End saying that they had found a very talented [fool](wiki:Fool). On their return journey from the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) in [278 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Steffon and Cassana's ship, the *[Windproud](wiki:Windproud)*, was caught in a severe storm and sank in [Shipbreaker Bay](wiki:Shipbreaker_Bay), with no survivors except the fool, [Patchface](wiki:Patchface).(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)

Renly grew up at Storm's End. Young Renly loved bright colors and fabrics. He loved games as well, and would run laughing through the halls of Storm's End, pretending to be a [dragon](wiki:Dragon) or a [wizard](wiki:Wizard) or the [rain god](wiki:Rain_god).(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2) He was still a young boy at the time of his eldest brother [Robert](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s [rebellion against King Aerys](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) in [282](wiki:282_AC)–[283 AC](wiki:283_AC). Renly and his older brother [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) spent the war [under siege in Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End). Renly was witness to the desperation of the starving garrison, while Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) and his [bannermen](wiki:Bannermen) feasted within sight of the walls.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-3)(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1)(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rawoiafrenly_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

In [285 AC](wiki:285_AC) or [286 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) granted his brother Stannis the lordship of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), traditionally the holding of the king's heir,(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rssm1046.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) and granted his youngest brother Renly the seat of Storm's End and named him [Lord Paramount of the Stormlands](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Stormlands). This upset Stannis, who felt that since he was the elder of Robert's younger brothers, Storm's End was his by right.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-4)

When Renly turned sixteen in [293 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), he made a [lord's progress](wiki:Lord's_progress) across the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) to mark his [coming of age](wiki:Coming_of_age). During this progress, he visited the isle of [Tarth](wiki:Tarth), where Renly courteously danced with [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth), and she fell in love with him.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) Renly also visited [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) once; Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) recalls that she had tried her best to seduce him, but remembers that Renly had seemed more bemused than inflamed by her attempts.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

During his time as Lord of Storm's End, Renly took [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) as his [squire](wiki:Squire).(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) Eventually, Renly and Loras became lovers. Although it is not widely known, various others at court are aware of their forbidden relationship.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rssm1316.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1)(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rawoiafrenly_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

Renly was eventually named to the [small council](wiki:Small_council), where he occupies the position of [master of laws](wiki:Master_of_laws).(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) While he is at court, his [castellan](wiki:Castellan) at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) is Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose), who also has been given the charge of raising King Robert's [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son, [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm).(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) [Brella](wiki:Brella) runs Renly's household in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for him.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) Renly has thirty men in his personal guard at the capital.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

## Appearances & Description

Renly is described as a powerful man(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) who is lean and lithe.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) He has a handsome, clean-shaven face.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Renly greatly resembles a young King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon),(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) being near as tall as his eldest brother had been.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Like Robert, Renly has an easy smile.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) He is taller and broader in the chest than Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell).(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Renly's thick(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) hair is described as black as [jet](wiki:Jet)(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) and coal.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) His fine and straight(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3) hair currently falls to his shoulders,(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) although Renly has sometimes kept it short or tied it behind his head with a golden ribbon.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) He always keeps his hair clean and combed.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

Renly has been described as having laughing green eyes that match his armor,(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4) as well as deep blue eyes(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4) that resemble Robert's eyes.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-4)(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) According to a semi-canon source, [George R. R. Martin](wiki:George_R._R._Martin) reconciled the difference by suggesting Renly has blue-green eyes that change color depending on what he wears.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rssm1395.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Renly is a charismatic man, winning friends easily, but he also strikes some people as frivolous.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-5) Renly enjoys [tourneys](wiki:Tourney) and hunting, but is not driven by the passion for food, drink, or wenching as King Robert came to be. Renly is quite popular with [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) due to his good looks, friendly ways, and charisma.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) According to Maester [Cressen](wiki:Cressen), Renly is a bold man who acts from impulse.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) dislikes her brother-in-law because of his mockery.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Martin has said that Renly is "a carefree and careless soul".(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rssm1067.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

Renly wears expensive attire, usually combining the Baratheon [sigil](wiki:Sigil) with the colors of [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell),(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-6) such as a dark green velvet doublet embroidered with [golden](wiki:Gold) [stags](wiki:Stag). He drapes a [cloth-of-gold](wiki:Cloth-of-gold) half cape over the doublet with an [emerald](wiki:Emerald) brooch.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Renly has a suit of enameled green armor with a helm adorned with a pair of golden antlers,(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-7) which was made by [Tobho Mott](wiki:Tobho_Mott).(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) He wields [longsword](wiki:Longsword) and dagger.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

Unbeknownst to the realm at large, and despite the many women who are attracted to him, Renly is homosexual.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rawoiafrenly_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rssm1316.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rssm5457.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-unionsquare_21-0) According to his lover, Loras, Renly disdains book learning, believing that books should only be read by [maesters](wiki:Maesters). Despite this, he owns books with "drawings that would turn a [septon](wiki:Septon) blind", implying some kind of pornography.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

When [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) is brought back to the Red Keep dying of his boar wound, Renly comes straight from the hunt still in his hunting greens, his cloak spattered with blood. (Eddard XIII, AGOT — `sources/chapters/agot/agot-eddard-13.md:35`)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

While King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s procession is returning from [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), Renly meets up with them, accompanied by Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard), and the [King's Justice](wiki:King's_Justice), Ser [Ilyn Payne](wiki:Ilyn_Payne). He does not identify himself to [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), but she recognizes him by his antlered helmet and his youth.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5) Renly is deeply amused by Prince [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon)'s accusations against [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)'s direwolf [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf)) at [Darry](wiki:Darry), and when he laughs at the thought of Joffrey being disarmed and beaten by a girl smaller and younger than him, Robert orders him to leave the room.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

Renly performs well in the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney), but loses to the [Hound](wiki:Hound), [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane). When Renly is unhorsed, one of the golden tines of his antlered helm breaks off, and he graciously offers it to the victor. However, Sandor tosses the bit of gold into the crowd, who begin to fight for it, until Renly walks out among them to restore the peace.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)

Renly shows Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) a painted portrait of [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), and asks if she looks like [Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark), King Robert's late betrothed. Eddard is puzzled by this, stating the girl looks nothing like his sister Lyanna. He wonders if Renly is imitating Robert's behavior in falling in love with a girl he believes looks like Lyanna.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3) Robert even tells Eddard that Renly has told him about Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell)'s lovely sister.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-1) In actuality, Renly and Loras want Robert to [put aside](wiki:Put_aside) Cersei and take Margaery as his new queen.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)

When the [small council](wiki:Small_council) learns that [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) is pregnant, Renly advises Robert to have the exiled girl assassinated.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0) Renly joins Robert when the king goes hunting in the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood) for a [white hart](wiki:White_hart).(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0) While Robert lies dying after being gored by a [boar](wiki:Boar), Renly approaches Eddard and suggests they seize control of the royal children before Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) can strike.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-1) Eddard refuses the offer, however, and Renly flees [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) along with Loras.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)

After Robert's death, Renly is among the nobles ordered to swear fealty to King Joffrey I, Renly's nephew.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0) Rumors spread that Eddard had murdered Robert and that Renly then avenged his brother, or that Renly had killed Robert in a drunken quarrel.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)

Renly manages to win the support of powerful [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) and many of their bannermen through [marriage](wiki:Wedding_of_Renly_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) to Loras's sister Margaery. Renly is crowned [Lord of the Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Lord_of_the_Seven_Kingdoms) at [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden).(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0)(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Renly's [crown](wiki:Crown) of soft gold depicts a ring of [roses](wiki:Rose). On its front is a stag's head made of dark green [jade](wiki:Jade), with golden eyes and antlers.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-8)

Through his charisma with and ties to [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) and familiarity with his [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) bannermen, Renly's claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) is backed by the largest army in the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), despite his elder brother [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) having the better claim. Renly takes to wearing the stag of Baratheon but in the colors of House Tyrell.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-9) Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), the father of [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), serves as Renly's [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0) Because the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) is already serving Joffrey, Renly creates his own Kingsguard, naming them the [Rainbow Guard](wiki:Rainbow_Guard) in honor of the [Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rssm1316.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-2) Renly appoints his lover, Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell), as their [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard).(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-5)

[Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), the young [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), sends his mother [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) to [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge) to negotiate an alliance with Renly against Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), who is fortified at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0) Renly has been spending a great deal of time moving through the [Reach](wiki:Reach), gaining support and men at castles such as [Horn Hill](wiki:Horn_Hill), while waiting for the right moment to strike.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0) He blocks the supply of food from the [roseroad](wiki:Roseroad) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), causing the capital to suffer starvation.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-10)(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0) He hosts [tourneys](wiki:Tourney) for his men, including a great [melee at Bitterbridge](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge), which Catelyn arrives in time to see. [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth), who is infatuated with Renly, wins the melee, and asks to join the Rainbow Guard as her prize, to be close to him.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-11)

In Catelyn's meeting with Renly, he states that if Robb does him homage, he might allow Robb to keep his title, but he refuses to allow the [north](wiki:North) to secede from the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms). Renly shows Catelyn his great army, which he feels justifies his claim to the throne, and tells her that once Robb allies with him against the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister), the war is good as done. Renly's plan goes awry, however, when Stannis unexpectedly [besieges Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), forcing Renly to respond.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-12)

Catelyn convinces the brothers to parley outside [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), but Renly makes light of the situation, refusing to back down from his claim and advising his older brother to give up and eat [a peach](wiki:Renly's_peach). Renly also denies Catelyn leave to return to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), so she may witness the fate of "rebels".(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-1) However, while preparing for battle with Stannis's forces the next dawn, Renly is slain by an [animated shadow](wiki:Shadow_assassin) conjured by his brother's [sorceress](wiki:Sorceress) adviser, [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre). Catelyn and Brienne, who were alone with Renly during the assassination, flee back to Riverrun.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-2)

After learning of Renly's death, an enraged Loras slays three of the king's guards, including Ser [Emmon Cuy](wiki:Emmon_Cuy) and Ser [Robar Royce](wiki:Robar_Royce) of the Rainbow Guard.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0) Stannis is troubled by terrible nightmares.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0) Rumors spread that Renly was killed by a knight of his Rainbow Guard, Stannis, Brienne, Catelyn, or a spurned lover.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-1) Most of Renly's lords join Stannis's host,(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-2) although prominent nobles like Loras, [Arwyn Oakheart](wiki:Arwyn_Oakheart), [Mathis Rowan](wiki:Mathis_Rowan), and [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) refuse and instead return to Bitterbridge.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-0) Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose), the castellan of Storm's End, disbelieves that Renly has died, but his body goes missing before it can be presented as proof.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-3) Cortnay refuses to surrender Storm's End and his charge, [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm), but the castellan is also assassinated by a shadow.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-1)(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-2)

### A Storm of Swords

Tyrion asks [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) about the rumors of Renly's ghost. Bronn says that he saw nothing but the battle being routed, but there are hundreds of men in King's Landing who will claim they saw Renly's ghost killing one man or another.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_50-0) Captain [Khorane Sathmantes](wiki:Khorane_Sathmantes) tells [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) of the end of the battle, saying that King Renly's shade was reportedly seen slaying men while leading the van. Allegedly his armor had a ghostly glow from [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire), and his antlered helm was covered in golden flames.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_51-0)

Ser Loras Tyrell, now a member of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon)'s [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), escorts [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) to a supper with Renly's widow, Margaery, who has been [betrothed](wiki:Betrothed) to Joffrey. Sansa unknowningly offends Loras when she tells him it must have been terrible for Margaery when Renly was killed. Sansa expresses her condolences to Margaery directly, but Margaery's grandmother, Lady [Olenna Tyrell](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell), is dismissive of Renly's campaign, and also dismissive of her son Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) for allying with him.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_50-1)

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) asks Ser Loras why he joined the Kingsguard at such a young age, giving up hope of a family and children and love. Loras replies *"When the sun has set, no candle can replace it,"'* implying that his great love has ended and he has no wish for another lesser one.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_52-0)

Following King Joffrey's wedding to Margaery, [Hamish the Harper](wiki:Hamish_the_Harper) performs "[Lord Renly's Ride](wiki:Lord_Renly's_Ride)" during the [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding). Hamish sings of Renly's ghost repenting from his attempt to usurp his nephew, returning to this world to defend the realm from Stannis and then returning to Highgarden to see his true love's face one last time, which brings tears to Margaery's eyes.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_53-0)

When Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and Brienne of Tarth arrive in King's Landing, they are confronted by Ser Loras Tyrell, who still believes Brienne guilty of Renly's regicide. Jaime defends Brienne from the accusation, in his position the [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard) ordering Loras to sheathe his sword. However, he has Brienne put in custody for her own protection.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_54-0) Jaime later meets with Loras, and asks about "Renly's ghost", wanting to know who actually wore Renly's armor at the Blackwater, and Loras reluctantly reveals that it was his brother, Ser [Garlan Tyrell](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell), who fit the armor better than Loras did. He tells Jaime that the masquerade was done per the suggestion of [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), who said the appearance of Renly's ghost, returned for vengeance, would frighten Stannis's men. Loras then tells Jaime that he buried Renly in a place they knew from when he was a squire at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), a place that no one else could ever find and disturb his remains. Jaime repeats his defense of Brienne, and advises Loras to speak with her and hear her account of the assassination, to be assured that Brienne had not done it and that it was indeed a shadow that killed Renly. Loras recalls that Renly had believed Brienne to be ridiculous but utterly loyal and willing to die for him; he realizes that if she is innocent, then he had killed Robar Royce and Emmon Cuy for nothing.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Brienne continues to face accusations that she killed Renly, from the [hedge knights](wiki:Hedge_knight) Ser [Illifer](wiki:Illifer) and Ser [Creighton Longbough](wiki:Creighton_Longbough), and swears by [the Seven](wiki:The_Seven) that she did not harm him.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-1) Later, while bathing, Brienne remembers how Jaime Lannister had looked while joining her in the baths at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), and tries to recall Renly's face instead.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_55-0) She has another nightmare of Renly's death, but after the shadow cuts him, she sees that the man in the armor is actually Jaime.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_55-1)

At [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool), Brienne encounters Ser [Hyle Hunt](wiki:Hyle_Hunt) and Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly), and tells them both that she did not slay Renly. Ser Hyle assures her that since the [Reach](wiki:Reach) lords are allied with the Lannisters now, no one cares. Lord Randyll believes Brienne's defense, but judges her for failing Renly and for playing at knighthood.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_56-0)

[Taena of Myr](wiki:Taena_of_Myr) tells Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) that she was in attendance at [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) for Renly's wedding to [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) and the [bedding](wiki:Bedding) afterwards, and she questions Margaery's claim that the marriage was never consummated.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_57-0)

King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) names *[Lord Renly](wiki:Lord_Renly)*, one of [Cersei's dromonds](wiki:Cersei's_dromonds), [after](wiki:Namesake) his late uncle.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_57-1)

When Brienne encounters [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) at the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads), she initially mistakes him for Renly. She soon realizes that while Gendry has Renly's coloring, his build is more like Robert Baratheon, and suspects that he is one of Robert's bastards.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5) In the [Vale](wiki:Vale), [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) also recognizes that Robert's bastard [Mya Stone](wiki:Mya_Stone) has the same thick black hair as Robert and Renly.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

After being attacked by [Biter](wiki:Biter) and falling into a fever, Brienne has nightmares of being surrounded by dead men, including Renly, blood streaming from his throat.(wiki:Renly_Baratheon.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_58-0)
