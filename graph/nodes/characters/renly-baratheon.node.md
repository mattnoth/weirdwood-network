## Identity

Renly Baratheon is the Lord of Storm's End and Lord Paramount of the Stormlands, the youngest brother of King Robert I and Lord Stannis Baratheon. He serves as master of laws on Robert's small council (wiki:Renly_Baratheon).

## Origins

Born 277 AC at Storm's End, the youngest son of Lord Steffon Baratheon and Lady Cassana Estermont (track_b: Born, Father, Mother).

## Appearances & Description

Renly is powerful, lean, and lithe, with a handsome clean-shaven face. He greatly resembles a young Robert, being near as tall as his eldest brother had been, with an easy smile. His thick hair is black as jet and coal, falling to his shoulders. He has blue-green eyes that change color depending on what he wears (GRRM clarification reconciling descriptions of green and blue eyes). He is taller and broader in the chest than Ser Loras Tyrell. Renly wears expensive attire combining the Baratheon sigil with Tyrell colors — a dark green velvet doublet with golden stags, a cloth-of-gold half cape with an emerald brooch. He has a suit of enameled green armor with a helm adorned with golden antlers, made by Tobho Mott (wiki:Renly_Baratheon).

## Narrative Arc

Renly grew up at Storm's End, loving bright colors, games, and laughter — running through the halls pretending to be a dragon or wizard. During Robert's Rebellion he and Stannis endured the siege of Storm's End, witnessing the garrison's starvation. After the war, Robert gave Renly Storm's End (a slight Stannis never forgave, as the elder brother received only Dragonstone). Renly became a charismatic presence at court as master of laws, winning friends easily though some found him frivolous. When Robert died, Renly fled King's Landing rather than support Stannis or Ned Stark's claim for the regency. He crowned himself king, winning the Reach and Stormlands through his marriage to Margaery Tyrell and his relationship with Loras Tyrell. His massive host of 100,000 marched slowly toward King's Landing. At Storm's End he was killed by a shadow assassin sent by Melisandre, dying in Brienne of Tarth's arms at age 21 in 299 AC (wiki:Renly_Baratheon, agot-eddard-03).
