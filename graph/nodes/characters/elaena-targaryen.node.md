## Identity

Elaena Targaryen (b. 150 AC) — child of Aegon III Targaryen and Daenaera Velaryon; married to Ossifer Plumm and Ronnel Penrose and Michael Manwoody; sworn to House Targaryen; Princess. One of 2 characters named Elaena Targaryen.

## Origins

Born in [150 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Elaena was the third daughter and youngest child of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) and Queen [Daenaera](wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon) of [House Velaryon](wiki:House_Velaryon). She had two older brothers, [Daeron](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen) and [Baelor](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen), and two older sisters, [Daena](wiki:Daena_Targaryen) and [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon_III)).(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_targaryen_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Elaena's most cherished possession was a [dragon's egg](wiki:Dragon's_egg) whose stony shell showed the same colors as her hair.(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen.cite_ref-ssm_4-1)

### The Maidenvault

Elaena's brother, [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen), came to the throne in [161 AC](wiki:161_AC), when she was eleven. Shortly thereafter Baelor the Blessed imprisoned Elaena and her sisters in their own "Court of Beauty", in the part of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) that would become known as the [Maidenvault](wiki:Maidenvault), to preserve their innocence and so the sight of them would not tempt him or others in his court to mortal lusts. The three sisters were known afterwards as the "Three Maids in the Tower". Elaena hated her imprisonment, and had always been told that her hair was her "crowning glory"; so, she cut off her braid and sent it to Baelor, hoping that if she rid herself of her beauty, he would allow her out of the Maidenvault. Her pleas fell on deaf ears, and she remained in the Maidenvault until Baelor's death in [171 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen.cite_ref-ssm_4-2)(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

After Baelor died, there were some amongst the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) and the [lords](wiki:Lord) who felt that Elaena's eldest sister [Daena](wiki:Daena_Targaryen) should inherit the throne, as she was Aegon III's eldest surviving child. However, Daena had been isolated for a decade in the Maidenvault, which had left her and her sisters without powerful allies. The memories of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) and of [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), the last woman to sit the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), left many wary of the idea of a ruling queen. The precedents of the [Great Council of 101 AC](wiki:Great_Council_of_101_AC) and the Dance were cited, the claims of Daena and her sisters were set aside, and Elaena's uncle was crowned King [Viserys II Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_viserys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### Later life

After being freed from the Maidenvault, Elaena outlived her siblings and led a tumultuous life. Although Elaena had three husbands during her lifetime, the great love of her life was her cousin, Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon). She gave birth to two [bastard](wiki:Bastard) children by him, the twins [Jon](wiki:Jon_Waters) and [Jeyne Waters](wiki:Jeyne_Waters).(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen.cite_ref-ssm_4-3) Elaena had hoped to marry Alyn, but he was lost at sea; a year after his disappearance, she gave up hope and agreed to marry another.(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

In [176 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), during the reign of King [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen), Elaena married her first husband, the wealthy but elderly Lord [Ossifer Plumm](wiki:Ossifer_Plumm). He died on their wedding night (supposedly after seeing her naked), but Elaena became pregnant and gave birth to a son, called [Viserys Plumm](wiki:Viserys_Plumm). There were those who were dubious of the boy's parentage,(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and had suspicions on who might actually be the boy's [father](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen).(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) None dared speak their suspicions aloud, however.(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

Elaena married her second husband, Lord [Ronnel Penrose](wiki:Ronnel_Penrose), in the reign of King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen). He sat on the king's [small council](wiki:Small_council) as [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin); but it was widely known that Elaena was the one performing the duties, as she was shrewd and intelligent with money, while Ronnel did not have a great facility for numbers. King Daeron trusted her with many important matters of state, and she grew influential.(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen.cite_ref-ssm_4-4)(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6) Elaena birthed Ronnel four children: [Robin](wiki:Robin_Penrose), [Laena](wiki:Laena_Penrose), [Jocelyn](wiki:Jocelyn_Penrose), and [Joy Penrose](wiki:Joy_Penrose).(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_targaryen_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) After the birth of her seventh child, Elaena declared that if seven was sufficient for [the gods](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), it would do for her as well.(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen.cite_ref-ssm_4-5)

Not long after Ronnel's death, Elaena married once more, for love and by her own choosing. With King Daeron II's blessing, Elaena was married to Ser [Michael Manwoody](wiki:Michael_Manwoody), a [Dornishman](wiki:Dornishman) who had attended the court of Queen [Myriah Martell](wiki:Myriah_Martell). Michael was sent as an envoy to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) on several occasions, and there is record of correspondence between him and the [keyholders](wiki:Keyholders) of the [Iron Bank](wiki:Iron_Bank_of_Braavos). These letters, while signed with Michael's name and fixed with his seal, appear to have been in the handwriting of Elaena. While her husband was a cultured man of great wit and learning, Elaena said it was not those traits that had caused her to fall in love with him, but his love of music, and he was known to play the [harp](wiki:Harp) for her. When Michael died, Elaena commanded his effigy to be carved with him holding a harp, instead of the sword and spurs of [knighthood](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7)

### Legacy

A descendant of Elaena's son [Viserys Plumm](wiki:Viserys_Plumm) crossed the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea), and became the ancestor of [Brown Ben Plumm](wiki:Brown_Ben_Plumm).(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Elaena's bastard son [Jon Waters](wiki:Jon_Waters) had a son who changed his last name to [Longwaters](wiki:Longwaters), to show that he was trueborn himself, and [his descendants](wiki:House_Longwaters) still live in the [crownlands](wiki:Crownlands) today. One, [Rennifer Longwaters](wiki:Rennifer_Longwaters), is the chief undergaoler of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), and is fond of explaining his descent from a Targaryen princess, though he is not often believed.(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Appearances & Description

According to semi-canon sources, Elaena was not a great beauty in her youth, unlike like her sisters. At age eleven, she was described as a "skinny little thing", awkward and angular, shy and charming by turns. However, her beauty matured as she grew older, and its was said that she was more beautiful at age seventy than at age seventeen. Her eyes were a soft [lilac](wiki:Purple_eyes), her mouth thin-lipped and often angry. Her hair was a [platinum](wiki:Platinum) white with a bright golden streak down the middle, an unusual color even for the [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen). She wore it long, pulled back, and braided, and was always being told it was her crowning beauty. However, she cut off her braid to defy her brother [Baelor](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen) for imprisoning her in the [Maidenvault](wiki:Maidenvault), and afterwards wore her hair short for years.

As a child, Elaena liked to dress in black, simply because her older sister [Daena](wiki:Daena_Targaryen) also dressed in black. In character, she had more than a little of Daena's willfulness, as she would prove when she grew older. Elaena was also shrewd and intelligent, especially with money.(wiki:Elaena_Targaryen.cite_ref-ssm_4-0)
