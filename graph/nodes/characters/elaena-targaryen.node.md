## Identity

Elaena Targaryen was a princess of House Targaryen, daughter of King Aegon III and Daenaera Velaryon (track_b: Father, Mother). Known as the "Dragon princess" (track_b: aliases).

## Origins

Born in 150 AC (track_b: Born). Died in 220 AC (track_b: Died). Of Valyrian race and Crownlands culture (track_b: Race, Culture).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiances). Also associated with House Plumm, House Penrose, and House Manwoody through her marriages (track_b: Allegiances).

## Narrative Arc

Elaena was one of the most politically active Targaryen princesses. She held the title of Princess and unofficially served as Master of coin, handling royal finances through her management of "Parchments" (track_b: Titles). She married three times: Ossifer Plumm, Ronnel Penrose, and Michael Manwoody (track_b: Spouses). She was lover to Alyn Velaryon and rumored lover of Aegon IV Targaryen (track_b: Lovers). She bore seven children: bastards Jon Waters and Jeyne Waters, Viserys Plumm, and Robin, Laena, Jocelyn, and Joy Penrose (track_b: Issue).
