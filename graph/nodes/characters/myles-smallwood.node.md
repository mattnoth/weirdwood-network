## Origins

In [37 AC](wiki:37_AC), Lord Myles rode beside Lord [Alyn Stokeworth](wiki:Alyn_Stokeworth), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) for [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen), when Alyn was hunting for the [outlaw](wiki:Outlaw), [Harren the Red](wiki:Harren_the_Red). In [42 AC](wiki:42_AC), he faced [Wat the Hewer](wiki:Wat_the_Hewer) in single combat in the [battle at Stonebridge](wiki:Battle_at_Stonebridge) during the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising). Myles fought on the side of Prince [Aegon the Uncrowned](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) in the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye) against King [Maegor the Cruel](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Myles_Smallwood.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death_and_betrayal_under_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

One of Myles's sworn swords, Ser [Willam the Wasp](wiki:Willam_the_Wasp), was a victor in the [War for the White Cloaks](wiki:War_for_the_White_Cloaks). Willam joined the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) of [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Myles_Smallwood.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_tree_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

When Lord [Daemon Velaryon](wiki:Daemon_Velaryon_(son_of_Aethan)) resigned in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), Myles was chosen as the new Hand. Since many of Jaehaerys's advisors were bookish, the [small council](wiki:Small_council) desired that the king also be advised by a martial man such as Lord Smallwood. Jaehaerys was convinced by the recommendations of Ser Willam the Wasp and his liege lords, Lord [Prentys](wiki:Prentys_Tully) and Lady [Lucinda Tully](wiki:Lucinda_Broome).(wiki:Myles_Smallwood.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death_and_betrayal_under_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)

Myles disapproved of the conduct of the king's sister, [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)), toward her host at [Faircastle](wiki:Faircastle), Lord [Franklyn Farman](wiki:Franklyn_Farman). Jaehaerys did not take issue with Smallwood's point, only his words. After Willam was slain by [outlaws](wiki:Outlaws) in the [Velvet Hills](wiki:Velvet_Hills), Jaehaerys declined Myles's advice to punish [Pentos](wiki:Pentos). Although undoubtedly well-intentioned and a leal man, Myles was dismissed as Hand of the King in [57 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), as the Lord of Acorn Hall proved unsuited for the office, a fact that even Myles had acknowledged. Lord Smallwood was succeeded in the office by [Septon](wiki:Septon) [Barth](wiki:Barth).(wiki:Myles_Smallwood.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
