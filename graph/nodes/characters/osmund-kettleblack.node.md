## Origins

Osmund's father, [Oswell Kettleblack](wiki:Oswell_Kettleblack), has been in the service of Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) for a long time.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Osmund and his brothers have known Petyr since they were children.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rawoiafosmund_kettleblack.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Ser Osmund served in the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) and the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands) with the [Gallant Men](wiki:Gallant_Men) fighting for [Lys](wiki:Lys) and then [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh). He claims to have been [knighted](wiki:Knight) on a battlefield by Ser [Robert Stone](wiki:Robert_Stone), who is now dead.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

## Appearances & Description

Osmund stands six feet and six inches, most of it muscle. He has a hook nose, bushy eyebrows, and a spade-shaped beard(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) described as brown(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) or black.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Osmund has broad shoulders(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) but narrow buttocks.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) His chest is covered with black hair.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Osmund thinks that he and [Osfryd](wiki:Osfryd_Kettleblack) are stronger than their younger brother [Osney](wiki:Osney_Kettleblack).(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Like his two brothers, Osmund is illiterate.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Osmund and his brothers are amiable, being popular with both [knights](wiki:Knight) and servants, and amorous with serving women.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) considers all three brothers to be deceitful and unsavory,(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) but none as dangerous as [Bronn](wiki:Bronn).(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) also considers the brothers to be treacherous.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) thinks of Osmund as a dolt(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) and less skilled than Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell).(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

In order to match her brother [Tyrion's](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) personal power base of [sellswords](wiki:Sellsword), Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) calls on the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Fat_One)) at the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) as cover for secret meetings with the [Kettleblack](wiki:House_Kettleblack) brothers, who are supposed to hire other sellswords for her. Unbeknownst to Cersei, however, the Kettlebacks are already being paid by [Bronn](wiki:Bronn), Tyrion's captain.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Ser Osmund sells Cersei's secrets and provides Bronn with information about Cersei's moves.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

After Ser [Boros Blount](wiki:Boros_Blount) is stripped of his white cloak for cowardice on the [Rosby road](wiki:Rosby_road), Cersei names Osmund to the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). Tyrion thinks the appointment gives him another agent close to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Osmund and his brothers become great favorites in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), getting along with both servants and [highborns](wiki:Highborn) with their easygoing manner and their humor. It is rumored that they get best along with the serving wenches, although Osmund's position in the Kingsguard implies celibacy. Osmund eventually takes the position next to Joffrey that used to be reserved for [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane), and [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) hears servants saying Osmund is as strong as the Hound, but younger and faster. However, Sansa doubts this, as she has never heard of the Kettleblacks before Osmund was named to the Kingsguard.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Osmund and Ser [Meryn Trant](wiki:Meryn_Trant) guard King Joffrey during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) When Joffrey goes off to catapult the [Antler Men](wiki:Antler_Men) into the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush), Tyrion warns Osmund to keep the king safe and in the city.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) When Osney tells Cersei that the [Mud Gate](wiki:River_Gate) has come under attack by a ram, however, she issues an order for Osmund to bring the king from the city back to the Red Keep. When Osney points out Tyrion's diametrically opposite orders, Cersei threatens that she will send the Kettleblacks into the middle of the battle if they do not do as told.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) The flight of the king from the battlefield provokes some members of the [City Watch](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing) to mutiny.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Osmund later conveys the news to Osney that the defenders have been saved by the combined forces of the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) and the [Tyrells](wiki:House_Tyrell).(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1)

After the battle, Osmund and the other remaining members of the Kingsguard spend hours knighting more than six hundred men.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) While later standing guard, he leers at [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) when she visits the Red Keep's [godswood](wiki:Godswood).(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1) While Tyrion is recuperating from wounds suffered at the Blackwater, he regrets having trusted Osmund.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Racok67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Osmund's brothers, Osney and Osfryd, receive their [knighthoods](wiki:Knight) after the Battle of the Blackwater.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Cersei has the Kettleblacks retrieve Prince [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) from [Rosby](wiki:Rosby).(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1) [Varys](wiki:Varys) informs Tyrion that Cersei has been flirtatious toward Osmund.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

Ser Osmund is one of the Kingsguard assigned to bring Sansa to the [royal sept](wiki:Royal_sept) for her marriage to Tyrion Lannister. While Meryn's mere look promises violence, Osmund speaks to Sansa kindly and convinces her to come along quietly. During the feast, Osmund laughs when Tyrion mocks King Joffrey.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

When Joffrey chokes at [his own wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding), Osmund rips open the king's collar in an attempt to save the boy king.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

Osmund stands guard outside the royal sept while Cersei is inside with Joffrey's corpse. He does not recognize Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) when Cersei's disheveled brother arrives, and Osmund is forced to apologize to the [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard) after disrespecting him.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

Osmund is called as a witness for the prosecution during the trial of Tyrion for [the death of King Joffrey](wiki:Purple_Wedding), but his testimony is made up, infuriating Tyrion.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

Osmund is amiable towards Jaime when the Kingsguard meet in [White Sword Tower](wiki:White_Sword_Tower). Osmund jokingly accuses the new [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Tyrion's)) of Joffrey's murder, and he laughs when Boros is appointed the [food taster](wiki:Food_taster) for King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon). Jaime is skeptical of Osmund's claimed history and his knighthood.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) Osmund brings Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) his shield when the Mountain duels Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) in Tyrion's [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat).(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) reveals to Sansa that he had Oswell send his sons to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) when Bronn was hiring sellswords for Tyrion, and thus the Kettleblack brothers have been secretly in his employ all along, unbeknownst to Cersei and Tyrion. However, Petyr believes the brothers are too treacherous to have been trusted with his scheme to kill Joffrey, and that Osmund has become especially unreliable since he was named to the Kingsguard.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

When Jaime helps Tyrion escape from the [black cells](wiki:Black_cells), Tyrion learns from Jaime that his first wife, [Tysha](wiki:Tysha), had not been a [whore](wiki:Whore) as claimed by their father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), but was as she had said: an innocent, orphaned crofter's daughter. Furious, Tyrion tells Jaime that Cersei has been having sex with Osmund, Ser [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister), and probably [Moon Boy](wiki:Moon_Boy). Tyrion murders Tywin, the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), before disappearing from the Red Keep.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Rasos77.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Ser Osmund and Ser [Boros Blount](wiki:Boros_Blount) bring the news to Cersei that Tywin is dead. Cersei has Osmund escort her to the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand), where she finds that Tyrion has also killed [Shae](wiki:Shae) in Tywin's bed. Queen Cersei orders Ser Osmund and his brothers to secretly dispose of Shae's corpse. Cersei tells them that what they do with the corpse is of no concern, as long as nobody knows she was ever there.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) Cersei also orders Ser Boros Blount and the Kettleblacks to kill two sleeping gaolers, which upsets Jaime.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

Osmund guards one of the doors of the [Small Hall](wiki:Small_Hall) for the feast after King Tommen weds [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell). Afterward, Cersei has Osmund guard her while she has the Tower of the Hand destroyed with [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire).(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

In order to ruin Margaery, Cersei plots for the young queen to be found in bed with Osney by Osmund.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) Osney is unable to seduce Margaery, however.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)

Tyrion's claim that Cersei has been sleeping with Osmund bothers Jaime.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0) Cersei wants Osmund to command the Kingsguard when Jaime departs for the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), but the Lord Commander insists that Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) have command instead.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-1) Jaime considers [gelding](wiki:Eunuch) Osmund and sending him to the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)

Osmund is among Cersei's escort when she visits the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) to visit the [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow).(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)

Osmund supports Cersei when she interrogates [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle),(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0) and the Queen Regent has Osmund stay out of sight for when his brother Osney's accusation of adultery against Margaery is made public.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) However, Cersei and Osney both end up imprisoned by the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) as well.(wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)
