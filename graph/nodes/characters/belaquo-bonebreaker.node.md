## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Belaquo, along with his fellow pit fighters [Barsena Blackhair](wiki:Barsena_Blackhair), [Khrazz](wiki:Khrazz), [Camarron of the Count](wiki:Camarron_of_the_Count), the [Spotted Cat](wiki:Spotted_Cat), [Fearless Ithoke](wiki:Fearless_Ithoke), and [Goghor the Giant](wiki:Goghor_the_Giant), accompanies [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) when the latter petitions Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) for the seventh time to have the [fighting pits of Meereen](wiki:Fighting_pits_of_Meereen) reopened.(wiki:Belaquo_Bonebreaker.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

When the fighting pits are reopened, Belaquo is to fight Goghor to the death in the final match of the day. The fight does not take place, however, due to [Drogon](wiki:Drogon)'s sudden appearance.(wiki:Belaquo_Bonebreaker.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

After Daenerys disappears from [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit), Belaquo and other pit fighters serve as Hizdahr's guards.(wiki:Belaquo_Bonebreaker.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Belaquo_Bonebreaker.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Belaquo is chosen to sit on the [ruling council of Meereen](wiki:Ruling_council_of_Meereen) along with Goghor, Camarron, and the Spotted Cat by Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), despite the objections of [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq).(wiki:Belaquo_Bonebreaker.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
