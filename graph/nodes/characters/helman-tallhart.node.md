## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Before [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) leaves [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), her husband, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), orders her to send word to Helman and [Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover) once she comes back to the [north](wiki:North). Lord Stark commands them to raise a hundred bowmen each and fortify [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin).(wiki:Helman_Tallhart.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

When [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls the northern [banners](wiki:Bannerman) in response to Eddard's arrest, Tallhart men march to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell)(wiki:Helman_Tallhart.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) while Helman's brother [Leobald](wiki:Leobald_Tallhart) remains at [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Torrhen's_Square) as castellan.(wiki:Helman_Tallhart.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Helman is the first of the northern bannermen to greet Catelyn when she joins Robb's war council at Moat Cailin.(wiki:Helman_Tallhart.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

When the northmen arrive at the [Twins](wiki:Twins), Helman is skeptical of their chances at taking the castles by force.(wiki:Helman_Tallhart.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) After an alliance is formed between Houses [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) and [Frey](wiki:House_Frey), Robb names Helman as the commander of the garrison of four hundred archers and swordsmen left at the Twins to ensure the loyalty of Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey).(wiki:Helman_Tallhart.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

### A Clash of Kings

Helman's son, [Benfred Tallhart](wiki:Benfred_Tallhart), is killed by [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) during the [harrying of the Stony Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore).(wiki:Helman_Tallhart.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) orders Helman to leave the Twins and join up with Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) to [seize Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal).(wiki:Helman_Tallhart.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Helman retakes [Castle Darry](wiki:Darry) after [a small siege](wiki:Siege_of_Darry) with the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) garrison surrendering after a short period. At [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Roose sends word for Helman to torch Darry and kill the captives in Robb's name, and then join forces with [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover) on a march to [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale).(wiki:Helman_Tallhart.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Helman's brother Leobald is killed by [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton) during the [battle at Winterfell](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell).(wiki:Helman_Tallhart.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Helman and much of the northern infantry are killed in the [battle at Duskendale](wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale), as Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) had been anticipating them.(wiki:Helman_Tallhart.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Helman is succeeded by his daughter [Eddara](wiki:Eddara_Tallhart) in rule of Torrhen's Square.(wiki:Helman_Tallhart.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
