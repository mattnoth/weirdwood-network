## Identity

Queen Cersei Lannister is the only daughter and eldest child of Lord Tywin Lannister of Casterly Rock and Lady Joanna Lannister. She is the twin of Ser Jaime Lannister. After Robert's Rebellion, she married King Robert I Baratheon and became Queen of the Seven Kingdoms. She becomes a POV character in A Feast for Crows (wiki:Cersei_Lannister).

## Origins

Born 266 AC at Casterly Rock in the westerlands (track_b: Born). Daughter of Tywin Lannister and Joanna Lannister (track_b: Father, Mother). As children, Cersei and Jaime looked so alike not even their father could tell them apart (wiki:Cersei_Lannister).

## Appearances & Description

Cersei is strikingly beautiful with curly golden hair, emerald green eyes, fair skin, perfect teeth, and a slender graceful figure (wiki:Cersei_Lannister). She is willful, ambitious, and has "a certain low cunning" per Tyrion. She is hungry for power, resents being excluded from it on account of her gender, and thinks of herself as a female version of Tywin. She is impatient, never forgets a slight, and frequently makes rash decisions without considering consequences (wiki:Cersei_Lannister). Pass 1 confirms: green eyes shining with triumph (agot-eddard-03); tousled golden hair, bruise on jaw (agot-eddard-13); gown of sea-green silk with Myrish lace (agot-eddard-14).

## Narrative Arc

In AGOT, Cersei and Jaime's incestuous relationship is discovered by Bran Stark, leading Jaime to push the boy from a window (agot-bran-02). Cersei manipulates Robert throughout his reign and orchestrates his death by having Lancel provide strong wine during the hunt (agot-eddard-15). After Robert's death, she tears up his letter naming Ned as Protector and seizes power through Joffrey (agot-eddard-14). Cersei confesses her incest to Ned in the godswood, telling him "when you play the game of thrones, you win or you die" (agot-eddard-12).

In ACOK, she rules through Joffrey but loses control of governance to Tyrion, who serves as Hand. In ASOS, Tywin takes direct control, marginalizing Cersei. She arranges Tyrion's marriage to Sansa and later Bronn's marriage to Lollys Stokeworth to deprive Tyrion of allies. After Joffrey's death at his wedding feast, Cersei has Tyrion arrested for the murder.

In AFFC, now Queen Regent for Tommen, Cersei governs with increasing paranoia and incompetence. She fills offices with sycophants, arms the Faith Militant, and takes multiple lovers including Lancel, the Kettleblacks, and Taena Merryweather. She is arrested by the Faith for her crimes and forced to perform a walk of atonement through King's Landing. In ADWD, she awaits trial (wiki:Cersei_Lannister).
