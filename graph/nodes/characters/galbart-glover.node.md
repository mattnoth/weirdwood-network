## Origins

The unwed(wiki:Galbart_Glover.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Galbart may be a widower.(wiki:Galbart_Glover.cite_ref-6) The appendices of *[A Clash of Kings](wiki:A_Clash_of_Kings-Appendix)* and *[A Storm of Swords](wiki:A_Storm_of_Swords-Appendix)* state that Galbart's nephew [Gawen](wiki:Gawen_Glover) is the heir to [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte), while the appendices of *[A Feast for Crows](wiki:A_Feast_for_Crows-Appendix)* and *[A Dance with Dragons](wiki:A_Dance_with_Dragons-Appendix)* state that Galbart's brother [Robett](wiki:Robett_Glover) is the heir.

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

After Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) is shown the [Valyrian steel dagger](wiki:Valyrian_steel_dagger) by his wife, [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), Eddard tasks her with having Galbart and Ser [Helman Tallhart](wiki:Helman_Tallhart) fortify [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin) with a hundred bowmen each.(wiki:Galbart_Glover.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

When [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls the banners to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), Galbart's brother, [Robett](wiki:Robett_Glover), unsuccessfully asks for command.(wiki:Galbart_Glover.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Galbart is part of Robb's war council at Moat Cailin,(wiki:Galbart_Glover.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and he warns Robb not to trust Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey).(wiki:Galbart_Glover.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

When the northern host splits at the [Twins](wiki:Twins), Galbart stays with Robb, participating in the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood)(wiki:Galbart_Glover.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) and the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps). Following the battles, Galbart prays in the [godswood of Riverrun](wiki:Godswood_of_Riverrun). He objects to supporting King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and is in attendance when Robb is proclaimed [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) within the [Great Hall of Riverrun](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Riverrun).(wiki:Galbart_Glover.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Clash of Kings

With Galbart and Robett campaigning in the south, Galbart's steward administers [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) in their absence.(wiki:Galbart_Glover.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Lord [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) sends [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) to [capture the lightly-held Deepwood](wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Galbart_Glover.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

After the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross), Galbart raids along the coast of the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands).(wiki:Galbart_Glover.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Galbart returns to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) with Robb. His demeanor is cool toward Catelyn, as she had freed Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) from captivity in the castle.(wiki:Galbart_Glover.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

When news reaches Riverrun of Robett's capture at [Duskendale](wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale), Galbart fears for his brother's life.(wiki:Galbart_Glover.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) It is partly that fear that causes King Robb Stark to trade [Martyn Lannister](wiki:Martyn_Lannister) for Robett.(wiki:Galbart_Glover.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Galbart is among the [northern](wiki:North) lords who march with Robb to the Twins to attend the wedding of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully). With Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) remaining at Riverrun as [Warden of the Southern Marches](wiki:Warden_of_the_Southern_Marches), Galbart is named commander of Robb's scouts and outriders. Galbart and two men try to cross the flooding [Blue Fork](wiki:Blue_Fork) at [Ramsford](wiki:Ramsford), but two horses and one man are drowned and Galbart has to be rescued while clinging to a rock.(wiki:Galbart_Glover.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

At [Hag's Mire](wiki:Hag's_Mire), after receiving news of the death of King Balon Greyjoy, Robb advances his plan to [retake Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin) while Lord Captain [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) is away. Galbart is ordered by Robb to go with Lord [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister) and Lady [Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont) to [Seagard](wiki:Seagard) and sail up the [Neck](wiki:Neck) to find [Greywater Watch](wiki:Greywater_Watch) so that the [crannogmen](wiki:Crannogmen) can direct Robb's army around the rear of the ancient fortress.(wiki:Galbart_Glover.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

Robb is killed in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Galbart_Glover.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Though Jason later remains at Seagard, Galbart's and Maege's whereabouts are currently unknown.

### A Dance with Dragons

Returning to Deepwood after the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), Asha feels ill at ease in Galbart's home.(wiki:Galbart_Glover.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) defeats Asha in the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte),(wiki:Galbart_Glover.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1) and he offers to restore the castle to [House Glover](wiki:House_Glover).(wiki:Galbart_Glover.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
