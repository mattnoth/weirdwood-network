## Appearances & Description

According to [Septon](wiki:Septon) [Eustace](wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)), Lord Jasper's sobriquet Ironrod derived from his unbending attitudes to matters of [law](wiki:Law_and_justice). The [ribald account](wiki:The_Testimony_of_Mushroom) of [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom), however, claims the name came from the stiffness of Jasper's member, as he sired twenty-nine children on four wives before the last died of exhaustion.(wiki:Jasper_Wylde.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

## Origins

When King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) died in his sleep in [129 AC](wiki:129_AC), Lord Jasper was called to the [small council](wiki:Small_council) convened in the chambers of Queen [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower). Plans were made to crown Alicent's son as [Aegon II](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), instead of Princess [Rhaenyra](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), who had been named Viserys's heir. With Lord [Lyman Beesbury](wiki:Lyman_Beesbury) the only member of the council in support of Rhaenyra, Jasper reminded him of the [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council) of [101 AC](wiki:101_AC), and the Old King [Jaehaerys I](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) in [choosing](wiki:Second_Quarrel) Prince [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) as his heir over Princess [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aemon)) in [92 AC](wiki:92_AC). Jasper also discussed [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) and his sister-wives [Visenya](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) and [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen), and the [Andal](wiki:Andal) tradition wherein the rights of a trueborn son come before a daughter. When Grand Maester [Orwyle](wiki:Orwyle) asked about those not willing to bend the knee to Aegon II, Ironrod responded they would die as [traitors](wiki:Traitor).(wiki:Jasper_Wylde.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
As the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) progressed, Jasper advised [Prince Regent](wiki:Regent) [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) to summon Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower) and Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)) from the [Reach](wiki:Reach). Aemond, however, disregarded Jasper's caution, and instead headed for [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Jasper_Wylde.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Jasper was taken captive by Rhaenyra during the [fall of King's Landing](wiki:Fall_of_King's_Landing) in [130 AC](wiki:130_AC).(wiki:Jasper_Wylde.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Rhaenyra had several nobles executed for [treason](wiki:Treason); Jasper was the second one beheaded, after Ser [Otto Hightower](wiki:Otto_Hightower). To his death, Ironrod insisted that the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) goes to a king's son before a daughter.(wiki:Jasper_Wylde.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
