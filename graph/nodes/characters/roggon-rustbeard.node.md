## Identity

Roggon Rustbeard is an ironborn warrior serving in the crew of Asha Greyjoy aboard the *Black Wind* (wiki:Roggon_Rustbeard).

## Allegiances

He is `SWORN_TO` House Greyjoy (track_b: Allegiance). He is `CULTURE_OF` the ironborn (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Roggon has a huge red beard that gives him his nickname. He is noted as the best horse rider among the ironborn — an unusual distinction for a seafaring people (wiki:Roggon_Rustbeard).

## Narrative Arc

Roggon serves aboard the *Black Wind* as one of Asha Greyjoy's crew. He fights at Deepwood Motte and during Stannis Baratheon's surprise attack on the ironborn camp. He is captured by Stannis's forces and later ransomed back to the ironborn (wiki:Roggon_Rustbeard.cite_ref-Radwd26, wiki:Roggon_Rustbeard.cite_ref-Radwd42, wiki:Roggon_Rustbeard.cite_ref-Radwd62).
