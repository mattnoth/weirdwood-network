## Appearances & Description

Roggon has a huge red beard.(wiki:Roggon_Rustbeard.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) He and [Rolfe the Dwarf](wiki:Rolfe_the_Dwarf) are [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy)'s best [horse](wiki:Horse) riders.(wiki:Roggon_Rustbeard.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Roggon sails to [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) with [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), along with the rest of her supporters, as she contemplates her next move after the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk) chose [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy).(wiki:Roggon_Rustbeard.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Asha sends Rogon and [Rolfe the Dwarf](wiki:Rolfe_the_Dwarf) ahead to scout for [northmen](wiki:Northmen) as her crew flees Deepwood for the [Bay of Ice](wiki:Bay_of_Ice).(wiki:Roggon_Rustbeard.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Roggon is captured along with Asha, [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley), and several [other ironborn](wiki:Ragged_nine) by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) during the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Roggon_Rustbeard.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Roggon is later freed and ransomed, along with the other ironborn, by [Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris), who needs an escort to get to Stannis during his [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell).(wiki:Roggon_Rustbeard.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
