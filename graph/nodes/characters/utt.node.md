## Appearances & Description

Utt is a balding, slender, round-shouldered man who wears black chainmail over grey septon's robes.(wiki:Utt.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Utt.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He is an acknowledged pedophile known for his preference of little boys.(wiki:Utt.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

According to [Shagwell](wiki:Shagwell), Utt kills his child victims once he is done with them, although he weeps and prays for forgiveness and often asks other members of [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) to whip and scourge him, which they often do as they find it amusing.(wiki:Utt.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Utt is one of the many members of the Brave Companions mercenary company that arrives at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) under the employ of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister).(wiki:Utt.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) With the Brave Companions [he changes sides](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal) and serves under Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) in Harrenhal.(wiki:Utt.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) As lieutenant, he leads one of [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat)'s four foraging parties to root out [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) forces, where his camp is attacked by a pack of [wolves](wiki:Wolves).(wiki:Utt.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

### A Storm of Swords

Utt leads another foraging party of around thirty-odd men out into the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), where his men rape and reave the local populace and murder several septons of a remote village septry. His group is eventually defeated by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) and he is captured, having been found hiding in the cellar of the [burning septry](wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry). Utt is given a swift trial, in which he confesses his crimes before he is hanged from an [elm](wiki:Elm).(wiki:Utt.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
