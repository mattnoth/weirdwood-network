## Origins

### Aegon's Conquest

Prior to [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Loren oversaw a brief period of peace with the Kingdom of the [Reach](wiki:Reach).(wiki:Loren_I_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtrotd214_3-0) After [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) landed on [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), and set about conquering the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), Loren used the peace between their kingdoms to make an alliance with [Mern IX Gardener](wiki:Mern_IX_Gardener), the [King of the Reach](wiki:King_of_the_Reach). The [Two Kings](wiki:Two_Kings) joined their hosts at [Goldengrove](wiki:Goldengrove) in the northern part of the [Reach](wiki:Reach).(wiki:Loren_I_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfab1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Commanding the mightiest host in the history of the Seven Kingdoms, the armies of the [Two Kings](wiki:Two_Kings) met Aegon's smaller host in the plains south of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush). As Mern IX brought more men than Loren I, he commanded the center while Loren and his knights formed the right. The combined might of the Reach and [the west](wiki:Westerlands) broke Aegon's formation of spearmen. However, Aegon and his sisters, [Visenya](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) and [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen), took to the sky with their [dragons](wiki:Dragon). Near four thousand men died that day on the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire), including Mern. Loren rode through fire and smoke once he realized the battle was lost.(wiki:Loren_I_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfab1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Loren was captured the following day. After he submitted to the Targaryens, Aegon the Conqueror named Loren as [Lord of Casterly Rock](wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock) and [Warden of the West](wiki:Warden_of_the_West).(wiki:Loren_I_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf31.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Loren_I_Lannister.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Loren's actions led his bannermen to yield to [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen).(wiki:Loren_I_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfab1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) According to legend, Visenya was grateful that Loren had ridden to war instead of remaining within Casterly Rock, as she felt the seat of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) would have been able to withstand even [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame).(wiki:Loren_I_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf762_6-0)

Aegon called upon his new followers to aid him against the [northern](wiki:North) host of [Torrhen Stark](wiki:Torrhen_Stark). Once at the [Trident](wiki:Trident), however, the [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) submitted as well instead of opposing Aegon by force.(wiki:Loren_I_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfab1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) After the submission of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands), some suggested for the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) to be made vassals of the Lannisters.(wiki:Loren_I_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfab2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### Lord of Casterly Rock

The Queens Visenya and Rhaenys arranged marriages between noble houses from all over the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), hoping to unite the realm. One of these marriages was the union between Loren's eldest son and heir with a noblewoman from [House Redwyne](wiki:House_Redwyne) of the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor).(wiki:Loren_I_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfab3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Loren_I_Lannister.cite_ref-9)

Throughout the reign of Aegon the Conqueror, Casterly Rock had the honor of hosting the king numerous times during his [royal progresses](wiki:Royal_progress).(wiki:Loren_I_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfab3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) While the vast wealth of House Lannister remained untouched, Loren did not have close relations to [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) and was too proud to scrabble for a place of prominence under the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). As such, the Lannisters did not have a significant impact on the realm during the course of Loren's lifetime.(wiki:Loren_I_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf761_10-0)
