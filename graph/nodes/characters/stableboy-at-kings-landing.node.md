## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

During the [arrest of Eddard Stark](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark) at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), [Syrio Forel](wiki:Syrio_Forel) defends [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) from Ser [Meryn Trant](wiki:Meryn_Trant) and allows the girl to escape the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand). The stableboy encounters Arya when she is in the stables. She asks for his help saddling a [horse](wiki:Horse), but the stableboy instead tries to apprehend her in hopes of reward from Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister). When he grabs her arm, Arya stabs the stableboy with her sword, [Needle](wiki:Needle), killing the youth.(wiki:Stableboy_at_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Clash of Kings

Arya worries what [Yoren](wiki:Yoren) would think if he knew that she had killed the stableboy.(wiki:Stableboy_at_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Racok5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords

When Arya tells [Harwin](wiki:Harwin) about what has happened to her, she omits the death of the stableboy.(wiki:Stableboy_at_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Arya worries about her brother [Robb](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), finding out about the stableboy.(wiki:Stableboy_at_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Arya thinks of the stableboy while speaking with Lord [Edric Dayne](wiki:Edric_Dayne).(wiki:Stableboy_at_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

While at the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White) in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), Arya has a dream in which she sees the hanging faces of those she has slain, including the stableboy's.(wiki:Stableboy_at_King's_Landing.cite_ref-Radwd64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
