## Origins

The [Saan family](wiki:Saan_family) have a long and distinguished history as pirate-lords stretching back at least three centuries to the reign of [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) One of his relatives, [Samarro Saan](wiki:Samarro_Saan), was one of the infamous [Band of Nine](wiki:Band_of_Nine).(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Rawoiafsalladhor_saan.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Before becoming a [knight](wiki:Knight) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), the one-time smuggler Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) often bought cargo from Salladhor.(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Salladhor Saan](wiki:Category:Images_of_Salladhor_Saan)*

Salladhor is slim and friendly,(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) with a streak of black humor like that of [Mad Prendos](wiki:Mad_Prendos).(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The spry old man(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Rawoiafsalladhor_saan.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) has wispy white curls(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) which, according to a semi-canon source, were once fair.(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Rawoiafsalladhor_saan.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

The old pirate refers to having both wives and concubines.(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) According to his friend Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth), however, Salladhor only has concubines.(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

One outfit includes flashing cloth-of-silver, with dagged sleeves pooling on the floor. His buttons are monkeys of carved [jade](wiki:Jade) and his jaunty green cap is decorated with a fan of [peacock](wiki:Peacock) feathers.(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) Another outfit includes a wine-colored tunic and high boots of bleached white leather inlaid with [silver](wiki:Silver) scrollwork.(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Besides his galleas *[Valyrian](wiki:Valyrian_(ship))*, Salladhor commands a fleet of two dozen striped galleys.(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) journeyed to [Lys](wiki:Lys) to recruit Salladhor for [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6) Salla is one of the mercenaries pledged to Stannis while he plans to take the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) His galleas, the *[Valyrian](wiki:Valyrian_(ship))*, is spotted offshore of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)). Another of his ships, *[Bird of Thousand Colors](wiki:Bird_of_Thousand_Colors)*, brings news from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Salladhor tells Davos of the legend of [Lightbringer](wiki:Lightbringer).(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7)

During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Salladhor and his fleet stay in [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay) outside of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush) to keep watch for any hidden fleets that may sneak up from behind. Davos thinks this a waste of Salla's resources.(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) After Stannis's army is smashed by the combined [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) and [Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) force, Salladhor orders his ships to pick up the remnants along the shore, including Stannis(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) and [Devan Seaworth](wiki:Devan_Seaworth).(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

### A Storm of Swords

Salladhor is made [Lord of Blackwater Bay](wiki:Lord_of_Blackwater_Bay) by Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent), who as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) has control of Stannis's king's seal after the Blackwater.(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) He sets his fleet to intercepting any trade ships crossing [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay) and taxing them on their goods.(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Salla's galley *[Shayala's Dance](wiki:Shayala's_Dance)* rescues Davos from Blackwater Bay. Salladhor counsels Davos upon his return to Dragonstone not to seek out [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) but is rebuffed.(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

Alester attempts to have one of Salladhor's men transport a letter with terms for peace to Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), but Alester is imprisoned when his plot is discovered.(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Salladhor and Ser [Axell Florent](wiki:Axell_Florent) plot an attack on [Claw Isle](wiki:Claw_Isle) because Lord [Ardrian Celtigar](wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar) knelt to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), but Davos dissuades Stannis from the plan.(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Salladhor informs Dragonstone of the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) One of Salla's ships, *[Mad Prendos](wiki:Mad_Prendos_(galley))*, transports [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm) to [Lys](wiki:Lys) without the king's knowledge.(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Rasos63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

Salladhor's fleet helps to transport Stannis's army north to the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Feast for Crows

With Stannis at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), Salladhor is left at [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea).(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) feasts Salladhor at Eastwatch.(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) His fleet of twenty-nine ships sets sail from Eastwatch for [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) to take Davos as an envoy from Stannis to Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly). However, when most of his fleet is destroyed by storms, Salladhor loses heart off the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters) and decides to abandon Stannis, sick of waiting for payment from the king. He refuses to take Davos to White Harbor but does not throw him overboard as he threatened. Davos is set ashore on [Sweetsister](wiki:Sweetsister) by rowboat.(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) Sallador then goes on to the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones) to engage in piracy.(wiki:Salladhor_Saan.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2)
