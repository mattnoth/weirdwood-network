## Origins

Urrigon and his younger brother [Aeron](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) were great friends during their childhood.(wiki:Urrigon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) They were both sexually abused by their brother [Euron](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) as children.(wiki:Urrigon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-balticon_5-1) Urrigon and Aeron often played at the [finger dance](wiki:Finger_dance) together. Once, Aeron threw an axe that shaved off part of Urrigon's hand. The [maester](wiki:Maester) of [their father's](wiki:Quellon_Greyjoy) new wife, [Lady Piper](wiki:Lady_Piper_(wife_of_Quellon_Greyjoy)), insisted on sewing Urrigon's fingers back on instead of treating the amputation. Urrigon's hand became infected. They eventually amputated his arm, but it was already too late, and Urrigon died at the age of fourteen as a result of the infection.(wiki:Urrigon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Urrigon's father, Lord [Quellon Greyjoy](wiki:Quellon_Greyjoy), and older brothers, [Balon](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), Euron, and [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy), had been away from [Pyke](wiki:Pyke) at the time, fighting the final battles of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). Lord Quellon died, and Urrigon's brother Balon returned home as the new [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands). To avenge his brother's death, he took off the maester's fingers and had Lady Piper sew them back on. The maester died raving.(wiki:Urrigon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter

When [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) is captive and chained in the hold of the *[Silence](wiki:Silence)*, his brother [Euron](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) forces him to drink [shade-of-the-evening](wiki:Shade-of-the-evening), which causes him to have numerous nightmarish visions. One of them features Urrathon with his arm infested with maggots.(wiki:Urrigon_Greyjoy.cite_ref-balticon_5-2)
