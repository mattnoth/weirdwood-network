## Identity

Urrigon Greyjoy, affectionately called Urri by his brother Aeron, was the fourth son of Lord Quellon Greyjoy and Lady Sunderly. In a sample chapter for The Winds of Winter, he is called Urrathon Greyjoy. (wiki:Urrigon_Greyjoy)

## Narrative Arc

Urrigon and his younger brother Aeron were close friends during childhood. Both were sexually abused by their elder brother Euron. (wiki:Urrigon_Greyjoy) Urrigon and Aeron often played the finger dance together. During one game, Aeron threw an axe that shaved off part of Urrigon's hand. The maester of their father's new wife, Lady Piper, insisted on sewing the fingers back on rather than treating the amputation. Urrigon's hand became infected; they eventually amputated his arm, but it was too late. He died at age fourteen, around 283–284 AC. (wiki:Urrigon_Greyjoy)

His father Quellon and older brothers Balon, Euron, and Victarion had been away fighting in Robert's Rebellion. When Balon returned as the new Lord of the Iron Islands, he avenged Urrigon's death by having the maester's fingers cut off and sewn back on. The maester died raving. (wiki:Urrigon_Greyjoy)
