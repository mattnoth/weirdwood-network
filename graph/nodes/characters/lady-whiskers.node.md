## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) gives her young husband King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) three black kittens,(wiki:Lady_Whiskers.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) whom he names [Boots](wiki:Boots), [Ser Pounce](wiki:Ser_Pounce), and Lady Whiskers. [Dorcas](wiki:Dorcas) makes Tommen a [mouse](wiki:Mouse) from scraps of fur and ties it on a fishing pole. The kittens love to chase it, and Tommen likes nothing better than jerking it across the floor as the cats pounce on it.(wiki:Lady_Whiskers.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Ser Pounce catches a mouse but Lady Whiskers steals it from him.(wiki:Lady_Whiskers.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

When King Tommen dines with his mother and Lord Regent [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), he brings his kittens. Tommen serves morsels of [pike](wiki:Pike) to his pets. After dinner, the king and his kittens are escorted to the royal bedchamber by Ser [Boros Blount](wiki:Boros_Blount).(wiki:Lady_Whiskers.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
