## Identity

Syrio Forel is a bravo from Braavos, a master fencer and former First Sword to the Sealord of Braavos (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot22, wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot50). He holds the titles First Sword and Water Dancer (track_b: Titles).

## Origins

Born in Braavos (track_b: Born). Died in 298 AC in King's Landing (track_b: Died).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Braavosi (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Syrio is a slight, bald man with a beak of a nose (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot22). He wears a leather vest and teaches with slender wooden swords with lead cores (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot50). He is demanding and creative in his teachings but protective of his pupils (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot22). He often says "Just so" (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Raffc22).

## Narrative Arc

Syrio was chosen as First Sword to the Sealord of Braavos because he saw the truth of a common tomcat when others pretended it was exotic — the Sealord valued his perception over flattery (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot50). Lord Eddard Stark hired Syrio to teach Arya to use her sword Needle in the Braavosi water dance style (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot22). He trained her in the Small Hall of the Tower of the Hand, teaching her not just swordsmanship but perception and fearlessness (agot-arya-02). He set her to catching cats in the Red Keep to improve her speed (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot32). When Cersei sent Ser Meryn Trant to seize Arya during the arrest of Eddard Stark, Syrio ordered Arya to flee while he held off five Lannister guards with only a wooden sword. He killed or disabled all five, then faced Meryn in full plate armor. His last known words were "The first sword of Braavos does not run" (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot50). According to a semi-canon source, Syrio was killed in King's Landing (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Rawoiafsyrio_forel). Arya continued to invoke his teachings — "Fear cuts deeper than swords" — throughout her subsequent journey (agot-arya-04, agot-arya-05).
