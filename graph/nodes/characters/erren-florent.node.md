## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

After the death of [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) beneath [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Ser Erren and Ser [Parmen Crane](wiki:Parmen_Crane) were sent by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) to [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge) to retrieve the better part of Renly's army, made up of foot soldiers.(wiki:Erren_Florent.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Ser Erren and Ser Parmen were [captured](wiki:Fighting_at_Bitterbridge) by the [Tyrells](wiki:House_Tyrell) at Bitterbridge.
