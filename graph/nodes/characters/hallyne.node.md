## Appearances & Description

Hallyne is a pallid man with soft damp hands.(wiki:Hallyne.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He dresses in striped black-and-scarlet robes trimmed with patchy and moth-eaten [sable](wiki:Sable).(wiki:Hallyne.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Origins

Hallyne's family has been involved in the [Alchemists' Guild](wiki:Alchemists'_Guild) for three generations. His father was [wisdom](wiki:Wisdom) in the days of King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) and his grandfather in those of [Jaehaerys II Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Hallyne.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
An acolyte during the reign of Aerys II,(wiki:Hallyne.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Hallyne was trained by Wisdom [Pollitor](wiki:Pollitor).(wiki:Hallyne.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) He was likely unaware of the [wildfire plot](wiki:Wildfire_plot) being planned by [Rossart](wiki:Rossart) and the other Wisdoms, as according to Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) the alchemists did all the work in secret and did not trust their own apprentices with the knowledge.(wiki:Hallyne.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Hallyne is part of Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s retinue when the queen does a circuit of the walls of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Hallyne.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Hallyne guides [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) through the [Guildhall of the Alchemists](wiki:Guildhall_of_the_Alchemists) explaining [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) and the role of the Guild to him.(wiki:Hallyne.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
During the preparations for the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Hallyne reports to Tyrion that the [pyromancers](wiki:Pyromancers) have several thousands of jars of wildfire more prepared than expected, claiming that it partially is because of the discovery of a lost cache of Lord [Rossart](wiki:Rossart) underneath the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit), and partially because the secret spells used to make the wildfire are working better than they had been doing before. He wonders aloud to Tyrion whether there are any [dragons](wiki:Dragon) again, to explain for the improved efficiency of the spells.(wiki:Hallyne.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
For services rendered during the Battle of the Blackwater, Hallyne is raised to a [lordship](wiki:Lord), although no lands or incomes are given along with the title.(wiki:Hallyne.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After the funeral for Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), Lord Hallyne promises Cersei to put a flaming hand in the sky above King's Landing.(wiki:Hallyne.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) He and the other alchemists burn down the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand) with [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) under orders from Cersei after [the wedding celebration](wiki:Wedding_of_Tommen_I_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Hallyne.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Cersei considers Hallyne as a candidate for the office of [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King). [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) tells her that Hallyne would be a terrible choice.(wiki:Hallyne.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
After [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) has been captured by the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), Hallyne asks Cersei if the pyromancers might be allowed to hatch any [dragon's eggs](wiki:Dragon's_egg) that might be found upon Dragonstone.(wiki:Hallyne.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
