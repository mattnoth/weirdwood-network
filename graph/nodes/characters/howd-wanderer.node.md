## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Howd Wanderer is among the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) leaders who accompany [Tormund Giantsbane](wiki:Tormund) to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) in declaring a truce with the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). He swears his oath to the Watch upon his sword. One or more of his sons is made a hostage of the Night's Watch.(wiki:Howd_Wanderer.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) He is present in the [Shieldhall](wiki:Shieldhall) when [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) announces [the letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) purportedly from [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) entitled 'Bastard'.(wiki:Howd_Wanderer.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
