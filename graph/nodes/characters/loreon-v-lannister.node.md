## Origins

Loreon V Lannister was a grandson of King [Loreon IV Lannister](wiki:Loreon_IV_Lannister), known as "the Lackwit". Loreon V was fond of dressing in his wife's clothing and wandering the docks of [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) in the guise of a common [whore](wiki:Whore), for which he was dubbed "Queen Lorea".(wiki:Loreon_V_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

After the reigns of Loreon V and his grandsire, the name Loreon became noticeably less common among Lannister princes.(wiki:Loreon_V_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)
