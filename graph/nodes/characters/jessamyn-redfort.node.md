## Origins

Jessamyn was a "dear companion" of [Jeyne Arryn](wiki:Jeyne_Arryn), the [Lady of the Eyrie](wiki:Lord_of_the_Eyrie). Jeyne, who never married, died in Jessamyn's arms at the [Motherhouse of Maris](wiki:Motherhouse_of_Maris) in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC).(wiki:Jessamyn_Redfort.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
