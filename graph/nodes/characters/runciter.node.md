## Origins

Runciter was appointed in [101 AC](wiki:101_AC) after the death of Grand Maester [Allar](wiki:Allar). He had only been in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) a short time before Prince [Baelon Targaryen](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) fell ill, complaining of severe pain in his stomach. Runciter treated Baelon and was able to bring his fever down and eased his pain with [milk of the poppy](wiki:Milk_of_the_poppy), but the prince's condition continued to worsen until he died on the fifth day of his [illness](wiki:Illness). Runciter opened the corpse and attributed the prince's death to a burst belly.(wiki:Runciter.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Runciter.cite_ref-Rtrp.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Runciter.cite_ref-3)

Runciter later served King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) after Jaehaerys passed away in [103 AC](wiki:103_AC).(wiki:Runciter.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) In his records, he only cites a quarrel with King Viserys as the reason for Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen)'s second exile.(wiki:Runciter.cite_ref-Rtrp.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) He died in [112 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) and was replaced by Grand Maester [Mellos](wiki:Mellos).(wiki:Runciter.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Runciter.cite_ref-Rtrp.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
