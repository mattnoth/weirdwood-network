## Origins

[Barra's mother](wiki:Barra's_mother) was a young [virgin](wiki:Maiden) [prostitute](wiki:Prostitute), who became pregnant when King [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_Baratheon) visited [Chataya's brothel](wiki:Chataya's_brothel). She named her daughter "Barra" after him.(wiki:Barra.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Appearances & Description

An infant, Barra has fine, dark hair.(wiki:Barra.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) She resembles her half-sister [Mya Stone](wiki:Mya_Stone) as a child.(wiki:Barra.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

With the help of [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) finds Barra and her mother in his search for King Robert's bastards. The mother asks to see Robert again, just to show him how beautiful his daughter is. Eddard promises to talk about it with the king.(wiki:Barra.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) However, when he tells Robert about Barra, the king is displeased, saying he thought the prostitute had more sense.(wiki:Barra.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

When Robert is dying, he tells Eddard to take care of his children. Eddard thinks of Barra and Robert's other bastards, and promises to protect them.(wiki:Barra.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Barra and her mother are killed by [Allar Deem](wiki:Allar_Deem), an officer of the [City Watch of King's Landing](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing), on orders of [Commander](wiki:Commander_of_the_City_Watch) [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt), as Queen [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) wanted Robert's bastards dead.(wiki:Barra.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) After [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) learns about the murder, he arranges for Janos Slynt, Allar Deem, and several other watchmen to be sent to the [Wall](wiki:Wall). Tyrion orders for Allar to be "washed overboard" during the journey of *[Summer's Dream](wiki:Summer's_Dream)* to [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea).(wiki:Barra.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
