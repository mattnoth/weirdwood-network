## Identity

Princess Aelora Targaryen was the daughter of Prince Rhaegel Targaryen and Lady Alys Arryn. She had a twin brother, Aelor, to whom she was married, and a younger sister, Daenora (wiki:Aelora_Targaryen).

## Narrative Arc

Aelora was married to her twin brother Prince Aelor Targaryen. When Aelor died in 217 AC, Aelora became Princess of Dragonstone and heir to King Aerys I Targaryen. She is said to have taken her own life, though the circumstances are unclear (wiki:Aelora_Targaryen).
