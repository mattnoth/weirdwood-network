## Identity

Daario Naharis is a Tyroshi captain of the sellsword company the Stormcrows (wiki:Daario_Naharis).

## Appearances & Description

Daario is lithe and smooth-skinned with bright, deep blue eyes that can appear almost purple. His curly hair reaches his collar and he keeps his beard cut in three prongs. He dyes his hair and trident beard blue or deep purple, and his fingernails are enameled blue. He has a large curving nose and a golden tooth. He wears loud colors and finery. He carries a Dothraki arakh on his left hip and a Myrish stiletto on his right, with golden hilts shaped as naked women (wiki:Daario_Naharis).

## Narrative Arc

Daario is one of the captains of the Stormcrows when they contract to defend Yunkai against Daenerys Targaryen. He kills the other two captains, Prendahl na Ghezn and Sallor, and brings their heads to Daenerys, bringing the Stormcrows to her side. He becomes her lover and a key military commander during the campaign in Slaver's Bay. In Meereen, he serves on her ruling council but their relationship grows complicated as she agrees to marry Hizdahr zo Loraq for peace. Daario is given as a hostage to the Yunkai'i as a condition of peace (wiki:Daario_Naharis).
