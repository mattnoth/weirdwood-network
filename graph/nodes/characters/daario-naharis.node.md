## Appearances & Description

Daario is lithe and smooth skinned(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) with bright, deep blue eyes which can appear almost [purple](wiki:Purple_eyes).(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) His curly hair reaches his collar, and he keeps his beard cut in three prongs.(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Daario [dyes](wiki:Hair_dye) his hair and trident beard, sometimes in blue(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) and other times in deep purple.(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) His fingernails are also enameled blue. Daario's mustachios are painted [gold](wiki:Gold), and he has a large, curving nose.(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) A golden tooth gleams in his mouth when he smiles.(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)

Daario is dangerous(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-10) and like most sellswords is fickle, faithless, and brutal.(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) He is quick to anger and is reckless.(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) The sellsword is mocking,(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) glib,(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) and greedy.(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) He is unable to read the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

The sellsword dresses in loud colors and finery. One yellow outfit, for instance, includes a foam of butter-colored [Myrish lace](wiki:Myrish_lace), a doublet with [brass](wiki:Brass) medallions in the shape of dandelions, and suede gloves. His high leather boots have ornamental goldwork, and his belt has gilded rings.(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5) Another outfit consists of striped pantaloons, high boots of purple leather, a white [silk](wiki:Silk) shirt, and a vest of golden rings.(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) Daario also possesses a heavy black cloak.(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-6)

Daario wears a [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) *[arakh](wiki:Arakh)* on his left hip and a [Myrish](wiki:Myr) stiletto on his right hip. Their golden hilts are a matched pair of naked women who appear wanton, which he sometimes caresses obscenely.(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-7)(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Daario is part of the [Stormcrows](wiki:Stormcrows) when they take on a contract to defend [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) from the forces of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) during the [Targaryen campaign in Slaver's Bay](wiki:Targaryen_campaign_in_Slaver's_Bay). Daario is one of the captains of the Stormcrows who attend the parley with Daenerys. She offers the captains gold and plunder if the Stormcrows come over to her side, but is rebuffed by [Prendahl na Ghezn](wiki:Prendahl_na_Ghezn), one of the other captains.(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-8)

The Stormcrows are a company of freedmen, and therefore the question of breaking the contract with Yunkai has to be put to each man. While that debate is going on in the camp, Daario, won over by Daenerys, kills Prendahl and his fellow captain, [Sallor the Bald](wiki:Sallor_the_Bald), and brings their heads to Daenerys as proof that the Stormcrows are joining her. Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) does not trust Daario, but the Stormcrows contribute to Daenerys's victory in the [battle near Yunkai](wiki:Battle_near_Yunkai).(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-9)

Daario leads the Targaryen van during the march on [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). Each day he gives Daenerys flowers and plants, and he tries to spare her from seeing the bodies of slave children killed by the [Great Masters](wiki:Great_Masters).(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3) After the [siege of Meereen](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen), Daario leads the Stormcrows in collecting a tenth whenever someone sells themselves into slavery.(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

### A Dance with Dragons

Daenerys sends Daario as an ambassador to the [Lhazareen](wiki:Lhazareen) with the mission of reopening the land trade routes between [Lhazar](wiki:Lhazar) and [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) He leads the [Stormcrows](wiki:Stormcrows) in [fighting on the Khyzai Pass](wiki:Fighting_on_the_Khyzai_Pass) against the [Long Lances](wiki:Long_Lances).(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-3)

After the [Green Grace](wiki:Green_Grace) suggests that Daenerys wed [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) to bring peace, Daario angers the queen by suggesting she slaughter the [Great Masters](wiki:Great_Masters) who would assemble at the wedding feast. Daenerys orders the Stormcrows to patrol the Yunkish hinterlands and protect the [Khyzai Pass](wiki:Khyzai_Pass), with Daario reporting to Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), [Lord Commander of the Queensguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Queensguard).(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-4)

While evading the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen), Daario is wounded by a crossbow which cuts his temple. The captain claims to have killed one of his serjeants who wanted to defect to the Yunkai'i.(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Infatuated with the sellsword,(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-5)(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Daenerys begins sleeping with Daario after his return to Meereen,(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) and the sellsword angers Barristan by kissing the queen in public.(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4) Daario refers to the aged knight as "Ser Grandfather".(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell), Ser [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood), and Ser [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater), who have disguised themselves as sellswords of the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown), defect to Daario's Stormcrows. After the [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) knights reveal their true identities to Daenerys, an angry Daario distrusts them.(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5)(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

Daario becomes bad tempered when Dany agrees to weds Hizdahr. Although he does not attend the wedding at the [Temple of the Graces](wiki:Temple_of_the_Graces), Daenerys secretly hopes that her sellsword lover will intervene and abscond with her.(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-6) The sellsword becomes more wild after the wedding. Daario, [Groleo](wiki:Groleo), [Hero](wiki:Hero), [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo), and Hizdahr's sister and two cousins are given as [hostages](wiki:Hostage) to the Yunkai'i to vouchsafe for Daenerys's good intentions for peace.(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2) Daario leaves his blades in Daenerys's care, and she hangs them on the wall beside her bed.(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

After Daenerys disappears from [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit) on the back of [Drogon](wiki:Drogon),(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) the Yunkai'i refuse to release the hostages until her other two [dragons](wiki:Dragon), [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal) and [Viserion](wiki:Viserion), are executed.(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) When Barristan and [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) plot against King Hizdahr, the knight wants Daario to be rescued from the Yunkai'i, while the Shavepate suggests that Daario be allowed to die so that the Stormcrows can choose a new leader.(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1) As Daario remains a prisoner of the Yunkai'i, command of the Stormcrows passes to the [Widower](wiki:Widower) and [Jokin](wiki:Jokin).(wiki:Daario_Naharis.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
