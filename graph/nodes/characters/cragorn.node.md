## Identity

Cragorn is an ironborn man in the service of House Greyjoy who blew the dragon horn Dragonbinder at the kingsmoot on Old Wyk (wiki:Cragorn).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Greyjoy (track_b: Allegiance).

## Narrative Arc

At the kingsmoot on Old Wyk, Euron Greyjoy produces Dragonbinder, a great horn banded with red gold and Valyrian steel. Cragorn volunteers to blow the horn. He sounds it three times, producing a terrible sound that silences the crowd and wins the kingsmoot for Euron. Cragorn collapses afterward and is found dead with charred lungs, burned from the inside out by the horn's magic (wiki:Cragorn.cite_ref-Raffc19). Victarion later recalls Cragorn's death when considering who will blow the horn for him (wiki:Cragorn.cite_ref-Radwd63).
