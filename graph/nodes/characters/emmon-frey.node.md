## Origins

Emmon Frey is the second son of [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey), [Lord of the Crossing](wiki:Lord_of_the_Crossing), and his first wife, Lady [Perra Royce](wiki:Perra_Royce).(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Sensing a weakness and a desire for approval in Lord [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister), Lord Walder traveled to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) and convinced him to [betroth](wiki:Marriage) his only daughter, Lady [Genna Lannister](wiki:Genna_Lannister), to his second son, Emmon. A third son himself, Lord Tytos agreed and announced the betrothal at a feast, scandalizing the west and outraging Lord [Roger Reyne](wiki:Roger_Reyne) and Genna's brother, the ten-year-old [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister). While Lord Reyne stormed out from the feast and Lady [Ellyn Tarbeck](wiki:Ellyn_Tarbeck) laughed, Tywin denounced the marriage in the strongest terms in front of half the nobility of the west, but still the betrothal remained. Emmon has since been fearful of Tywin, now the [Lord of Casterly Rock](wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock) and [Warden of the West](wiki:Warden_of_the_West).(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Following their [marriage](wiki:Marriage), Emmon remained at Casterly Rock with his wife and children. At many a feast, Emmon would nervously poke at his food while his wife jested with whatever [household knight](wiki:Household_knight) happened to be seated to her left. The marriage resulted in four sons, [Cleos](wiki:Cleos_Frey), [Lyonel](wiki:Lyonel_Frey), [Tion](wiki:Tion_Frey), and [Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Emmon)). Genna claims that all four boys are Emmon's and none dare to question her, least of all Emmon.(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Emmon Frey](wiki:Category:Images_of_Emmon_Frey)*

Emmon is a small man, very thin with a pointed head. Half of his hair was gone before he was thirty and, by sixty, he only had a few remaining white wisps.(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) believes Emmon's son Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) inherited his father's stringy brown hair, weak chin, and thin face.(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Emmon has a prominent apple in his throat that unflatteringly accentuates his weak chin.(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

A sullen and cowardly man,(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) believes Emmon to be a dullard(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and a fretful man with nervous hands. He is fearful of his wife, Lady [Genna Lannister](wiki:Genna_Lannister), and his brother-in-law, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister).(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) Emmon has an anxious deposition.(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Emmon enjoys chewing on [sourleaf](wiki:Sourleaf), though it leaves a pinkish froth on his lips.(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Emmon attends the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with six of his kin—Ser [Hosteen](wiki:Hosteen_Frey), Ser [Danwell](wiki:Danwell_Frey), Ser [Jared](wiki:Jared_Frey), Ser [Theo](wiki:Theo_Frey), Ser [Perwyn](wiki:Perwyn_Frey), and [Martyn Rivers](wiki:Martyn_Rivers).(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Although [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey) allied with [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully) and [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) at the onset of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Emmon took the side of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) and his elder sons fight under their cousin, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Over the course of the war, Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) is killed by [outlaws](wiki:Outlaw) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands),(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) and his third son, [Tion Frey](wiki:Tion_Frey), is murdered alongside his cousin [Willem Lannister](wiki:Willem_Lannister) by Lord [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark).(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

In the aftermath of the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), as part of the deal between Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) and Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey), Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) is attainted by the [crown](wiki:Iron_Throne) and stripped of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun). The lands, incomes, and castle are granted to Emmon and his wife, Lady Genna Lannister.(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Emmon is to receive Riverrun once Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) yields the castle.(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Upon learning that he had been made a lord, Emmon and his wife immediately depart for Riverrun. Emmon is among the Freys at the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), encamping south of the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork), and tries to direct Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister) on how to conduct the siege, hoping to avoid damage to his new seat. He regularly beseeches Ser Daven to insist that Ser [Ryman Frey](wiki:Ryman_Frey) hang the captive Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully), to no avail.(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8)

With Ser Brynden Tully refusing to surrender Riverrun, Ser Jaime Lannister is sent from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with reinforcements to help resolve the siege. Upon conferring with Emmon and his aunt Genna, Jaime lies to them about the manner of Cleos' death, claiming Cleos had bravely scattered the outlaws that killed him; this pleases Emmon and Genna. As the new Lord of Riverrun, Emmon mistakenly believes he is the new overlord of the riverlands until Jaime clarifies that honor is held by [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), the new [Lord Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident), much to Emmon's displeasure. Believing Emmon to be a poor lord, Genna is displeased with Riverrun as the Tullys still flourish in the male line, preferring [Darry](wiki:Darry) as [House Darry](wiki:House_Darry) is extinct in the legitimate male line and Cleos was married to Lady [Jeyne Darry](wiki:Jeyne_Darry).(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-9)

Emmon continues to demand the death of Edmure and to avoid any damage to Riverrun, frequently pulling out his parchment from the crown, much to the annoyance of Jaime, the riverlords, and even the other Freys. Eventually, Jaime is able to bloodlessly resolve the siege by having Edmure speak with his uncle, Ser Brynden.(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) However, Emmon is furious when Brynden escapes with assistance from Edmure. Emmon demands the execution of Edmure, but Jaime intends to send the attainted Tully to Casterly Rock as a hostage. Emmon holds Riverrun with a garrison of two hundred men, but fears that Brynden will return to retake the castle. Now in control of Riverrun, he pontificates to the castle's servants for nearly three hours, and the singer [Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams) decides to enter Emmon's service.(wiki:Emmon_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
