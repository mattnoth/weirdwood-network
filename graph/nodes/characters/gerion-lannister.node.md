## Origins

### Targaryen Era

Gerion was born at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock)(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Rawoiafgerion_lannister.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) in [255 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), the fifth child and fourth son of [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister), Lord of [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), and his wife, Lady [Jeyne Marbrand](wiki:Jeyne_Marbrand). He was healthy, but his mother died within a month of his birth.(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Though Tytos was greatly saddened by the death of his wife, he soon became enamored of Gerion's [wet nurse](wiki:Wet_nurse) and made her [his first mistress](wiki:Tytos_Lannister's_first_mistress).(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Like his brothers, [Kevan](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) and [Tygett](wiki:Tygett_Lannister), Gerion was overshadowed by their older brother, Tywin. While Kevan built up a life at Tywin's side, and Tygett tried to be independent, Gerion mocked this "game" and made jokes.(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Gerion's relationship with his eldest brother was notoriously stormy.(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

At sixteen, in [271 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Gerion visited the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) on his [coming of age](wiki:Customs) tour.(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) In [276 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Gerion participated in the [tournament in honor of Viserys's birth](wiki:Tournament_in_honor_of_Viserys's_birth) at [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport), but he was unhorsed by Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen).(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

Once when Tyrion was young, he begged his uncle for a [dragon](wiki:Dragon) for his [name day](wiki:Name_day) present, which Gerion found hilarious.(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Gerion once gifted Tyrion on his name day with the books *[Wonders](wiki:Wonders)* and *[Wonders Made by Man](wiki:Wonders_Made_by_Man)* by [Lomas Longstrider](wiki:Lomas_Longstrider), and often had Tyrion recite the sixteen wonders of the world.(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) Gerion also taught Tyrion some tumbling tricks when he was six or seven,(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) and would praise his skills.(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Radwd40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### Baratheon Era

[Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) ([282](wiki:282_AC)–[283 AC](wiki:283_AC)) put an end to the rule of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) over [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) when [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), the young [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End), took the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). In order to solidify his rule, Robert married Gerion's niece, [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister). Gerion offered a [gilded](wiki:Gold) dagger with an [ivory](wiki:Ivory) grip and a [sapphire](wiki:Sapphire) pommel as [a wedding gift](wiki:Wedding_of_Robert_I_Baratheon_and_Cersei_Lannister) to the new king.(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

In [288 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Gerion had a [bastard](wiki:Bastard) daughter named [Joy Hill](wiki:Joy_Hill),(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-16) whose mother was a common woman named [Briony](wiki:Briony).(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_lannister_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4)

Circa [291 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest),(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-valyria_5-1) Gerion went on a quest to find [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister)'s ancestral [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) [greatsword](wiki:Greatsword), [Brightroar](wiki:Brightroar), and any other treasures that might have survived the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria).(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Gerion has never returned, not for nearly a decade since his ship, the *[Laughing Lion](wiki:Laughing_Lion)*, left Lannisport. Lord Tywin sent men to look for his lost brother, who traced him as far as [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), where half of Gerion's crew had deserted him because of his intent to sail into the [Smoking Sea](wiki:Smoking_Sea). He had been forced to buy [slaves](wiki:Slaves) to replace them.(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1)

## Appearances & Description

Gerion was the youngest and most reckless brother of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister). He was said to be a man quick to laugh, and had a gift for making others laugh.(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) According to Gerion's sister [Genna](wiki:Genna_Lannister), his nephew [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) smiles like Gerion did.(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

[Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and Jaime often refer to Gerion as their favorite uncle.(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Gerion_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
