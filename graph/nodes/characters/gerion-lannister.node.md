## Identity

Gerion Lannister was the youngest brother of Tywin Lannister, Lord of Casterly Rock. He was known as Uncle Gery by his nephew Tyrion (wiki:Gerion_Lannister).

## Origins

Born 255 AC at Casterly Rock (track_b: Born). Fifth child and fourth son of Tytos Lannister and Lady Jeyne Marbrand (track_b: Father, Mother). His mother died within a month of his birth (wiki:Gerion_Lannister).

## Appearances & Description

Gerion was the youngest and most reckless of Lord Tywin's brothers, a man quick to laugh with a gift for making others laugh. According to his sister Genna, Jaime smiles like Gerion did. Tyrion and Jaime often refer to Gerion as their favorite uncle (wiki:Gerion_Lannister).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Lannister (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Westermen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

While his brothers Kevan and Tygett dealt with Tywin's dominance in different ways, Gerion mocked the "game" and made jokes. His relationship with Tywin was notoriously stormy. At sixteen, Gerion visited the Free Cities, beginning his reputation as an adventurer (wiki:Gerion_Lannister).

Gerion fathered a bastard daughter, Joy Hill, with a woman named Briony. In 291 AC, Gerion sailed to Valyria in search of the lost Lannister ancestral Valyrian steel sword, Brightroar. He was never heard from again. His ship, the Laughing Lion, was found abandoned in the harbor of Volantis, but Gerion had hired a second ship to continue the voyage and was never seen again (wiki:Gerion_Lannister).
