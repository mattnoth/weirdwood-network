## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

After [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) is acclaimed [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) and [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident), Lucas is one of his [sworn swords](wiki:Sworn_sword) and [battle companions](wiki:Robb_Stark's_battle_guard).(wiki:Lucas_Blackwood.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Lucas is a member of the party that escorts Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) south to meet with [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon). At [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge), Renly finds room for Lucas and Lady Stark's other highborn companions among his own lords and [knights](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Lucas_Blackwood.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Near [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), Lucas sharpens his dagger and tells Catelyn that the armies of Renly and [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) are expected to fight at dawn.(wiki:Lucas_Blackwood.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

After [Renly's death](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Lucas helps protect Catelyn as she returns to the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). Once they reach the camps of the river lords outside [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), Lucas departs Catelyn to find his father, Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood).(wiki:Lucas_Blackwood.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords

A friend and companion of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully),(wiki:Lucas_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Lucas is among the nobles who attend Edmure's wedding to Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey)'s daughter, [Roslin](wiki:Roslin_Frey), at the [Twins](wiki:Twins). However, the Freys betray the Starks and Tullys, and Lucas is cut down and slain during the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) by Ser [Hosteen Frey](wiki:Hosteen_Frey).(wiki:Lucas_Blackwood.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

While negotiating and end to the [siege of Raventree](wiki:Siege_of_Raventree), Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) promises Lord Blackwood he will have his son's body returned to him so he can be decently buried. Tytos intends to have Lucas buried beneath the great [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) in the [godswood of Raventree Hall](wiki:Godswood_of_Raventree_Hall), and considers his murder to be [kinslaying](wiki:Kinslaying) as Lord Walder's fourth wife was [Alyssa Blackwood](wiki:Alyssa_Blackwood).(wiki:Lucas_Blackwood.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
