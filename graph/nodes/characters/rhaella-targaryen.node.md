## Identity

Queen Rhaella Targaryen was the sister, wife, and queen of King Aerys II Targaryen, and the only daughter of King Jaehaerys II Targaryen and his sister-wife Queen Shaera Targaryen. She was the mother of Rhaegar, Viserys, and Daenerys Targaryen (wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen).

## Narrative Arc

Rhaella was betrothed and married to her brother Aerys at a young age on the command of their father Jaehaerys, who was influenced by a woods witch brought to court by Jenny of Oldstones. The witch prophesied that the prince that was promised would be born from Aerys and Rhaella's line. According to Barristan Selmy, Rhaella had been in love with Ser Bonifer Hasty in her youth, and there was no fondness between Aerys and Rhaella on their wedding day (wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen.cite_ref-Radwd67).

In 259 AC, Rhaella gave birth to Prince Rhaegar at Summerhall while the tragedy raged. After Rhaegar's birth, Rhaella suffered seventeen years of miscarriages, stillbirths, and infant deaths: Princess Shaena (stillborn, 267 AC), Prince Daeron (died at half a year, 269 AC), Prince Aegon (premature, died 273 AC), and Prince Jaehaerys (died in infancy, 274 AC), among others. Aerys became suspicious and accused her of infidelity, confining her to Maegor's Holdfast with two septas in her bed. Yet it was Aerys who was unfaithful — Rhaella once dismissed Joanna Lannister from her service for this reason (wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf).

In 276 AC, Rhaella gave birth to Prince Viserys. During Robert's Rebellion, the pregnant queen was sent to Dragonstone with Viserys. She died in 284 AC giving birth to Daenerys during a great storm that destroyed the Targaryen fleet (wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen.cite_ref-Ragot3).
