## Origins

Lord [Walter Whent](wiki:Walter_Whent) of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Oswell's older brother, announced the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) shortly after a visit from Oswell. It was proposed by [Varys](wiki:Varys) and believed by some that Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) was secretly sponsoring the tourney in order to gather lords together to deal with the matter of his mad father, King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), and used Oswell as a go-between to Lord Whent.(wiki:Oswell_Whent.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Oswell_Whent.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Oswell was present at the tourney when Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) became a member of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Oswell_Whent.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

According to a semi-canon source,(wiki:Oswell_Whent.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_rhaegar_targaryen.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Oswell, along with his other sworn brother Ser [Arthur Dayne](wiki:Arthur_Dayne), aided Rhaegar in the kidnapping of [Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark) near Harrenhal as the [year of the false spring](wiki:Year_of_the_false_spring) was ending.(wiki:Oswell_Whent.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) began shortly after.

At the end of the rebellion, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) encountered Ser Oswell and two other knights of the Kingsguard, Arthur and Ser [Gerold Hightower](wiki:Gerold_Hightower), at the [tower of joy](wiki:Tower_of_joy) in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). Eddard told them that [King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing) had already fallen and that King Aerys II Targaryen was dead. In the [ensuing conflict](wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy), all three Kingsguard were killed. Eddard had been with six [northern](wiki:North) companions, but he and [Howland Reed](wiki:Howland_Reed) were the only survivors.(wiki:Oswell_Whent.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Stones from the tower were used to build Oswell's cairn upon a nearby ridge.(wiki:Oswell_Whent.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Oswell Whent](wiki:Category:Images_of_Oswell_Whent)*

Oswell wore a distinctive helmet emblazoned with a black [bat](wiki:Bat) with its wings spread, symbolizing House Whent.(wiki:Oswell_Whent.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He was known and remembered for his dark humor.(wiki:Oswell_Whent.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), fevered in the [black cells](wiki:Black_cells) of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), dreams of the [combat at the tower of joy](wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy), where he and his companions encountered and battled Oswell and two other knights of the Kingsguard, Ser [Gerold Hightower](wiki:Gerold_Hightower) and Ser [Arthur Dayne](wiki:Arthur_Dayne).(wiki:Oswell_Whent.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

### A Storm of Swords

With his head on a [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) stump, [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) [dreams](wiki:Dreams) he is below [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), and is confronted with the shades of Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), and his fellow Kingsguard, Oswell, Arthur, Gerold, [Jon Darry](wiki:Jonothor_Darry), and [Lewyn Martell](wiki:Lewyn_Martell). They admonish Jaime for his murder of King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), and for his failure to protect Rhaegar's wife [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell), and children [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Rhaegar)) and [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)).(wiki:Oswell_Whent.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) recalls that Lord [Walter Whent](wiki:Walter_Whent) announced the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) shortly after a visit from his brother, Oswell.(wiki:Oswell_Whent.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
