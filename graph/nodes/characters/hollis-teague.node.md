## Origins

Hollis was the second son of King [Humfrey I Teague](wiki:Humfrey_I_Teague), who caused a massive uprising of riverlords by attempting to suppress worship of the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods). In the campaign's [final battle](wiki:Battle_of_Six_Kings), Humfrey I perished. The crown passed on to Hollis' elder brother, [Humfrey](wiki:Humfrey_II_Teague), who was killed. Hollis picked up the crown and died in the battle, passing the crown to his younger brother, [Tyler](wiki:Tyler_Teague), became king, only to be killed in turn. After Tyler's death, the princes' uncle, [Damon](wiki:Damon_Teague) also picked up the crown but was killed. Thus House Teague became extinct.(wiki:Hollis_Teague.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
