## Origins

Ser Goodwin fought in the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones). He had a friend during the [War of the Ninepenny Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Ninepenny_Kings), a fierce warrior in the yard who died young when he hesitated in killing a man.(wiki:Goodwin.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Goodwin taught [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) how to fight.(wiki:Goodwin.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) He has been dead for a long time.(wiki:Goodwin.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
