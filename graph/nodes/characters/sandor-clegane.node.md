## Origins

When Sandor was seven years old, a woodcarver in the village by the [family keep](wiki:Clegane's_Keep) gifted toys to the Cleganes to buy favor. Sandor desired his brother [Gregor's](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) gift, a jointed wooden [knight](wiki:Knight), and took it to play with, though he was afraid of what the brutal Gregor might do. When Gregor found him, he shoved the side of Sandor's face into the burning coals of a brazier and held him there while he screamed, until three grown men were able to drag him off. The Cleganes' [maester](wiki:Maester) attempted to heal the burns with ointments.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-15)

Sandor's [father](wiki:Clegane_(father_of_Gregor_and_Sandor)) explained the burns by claiming that his bedding caught fire,(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-16) though there are still rumors that Gregor was involved.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-1) There are also rumors of a sister who died young in mysterious circumstances, and their father was killed in a hunting accident, which is also implied to have been suspicious, leaving the family estates to Gregor.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-2) According to a semi-canon source, Sandor admired knights when he was a child, but became greatly disillusioned when Gregor was knighted by Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen).(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-29)

Sandor left to join the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) household as a [sworn sword](wiki:Sworn_sword) on the day Gregor came into his inheritance, and he has never returned to his family home.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-3) He claims to have killed a man when he was twelve years old,(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) possibly during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), as he was part of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s host during the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-30) Sandor has never taken a knight's vows.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-4)

Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) assigned Sandor to protect her eldest son, Prince [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon),(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) and the [sworn shield](wiki:Sworn_shield) has protected the boy for many years.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0) Joffrey is very fond(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0) of Sandor and often calls him "dog",(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) but his younger brother, Prince [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), is frightened of Sandor's face and voice.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)

Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) unhorsed Sandor during a [tourney](wiki:Tourney) in [297 AC](wiki:297_AC).(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Sandor guards Prince [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) when the retinue of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) travels to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). When [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) is comatose after a fall, Sandor offers to silence the [boy's direwolf](wiki:Summer) when Joffrey complains of its howling.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

While Robert's retinue is returning to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Joffrey dismisses his bodyguard so the boy can explore the [Trident](wiki:Trident) with his betrothed, [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark). The prince is wounded by [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf)), the direwolf of Sansa's sister [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark), however.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) When Arya goes missing, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and Sandor lead searches for her north of the river; the girl is eventually found by [Jory Cassel](wiki:Jory_Cassel). Sandor earns the enmity of Arya by acting on [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) orders to kill her friend [Mycah](wiki:Mycah), the son of a butcher, who is believed to have harmed Joffrey.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0)

Sandor rides well during the first day of the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney), unhorsing Lord [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon).(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-17) After that night's feast, Joffrey has Sandor escort [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) back to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep). Drunk, despite the final jousts the next day, Sandor becomes enamored with Sansa. Angry that she cannot look him in the eye due to his disfigurement, he mocks her for her polished manners, calling her a "little bird" who chirps on command. Sandor reveals to Sansa how was face was burned in childhood, then threatens to kill the girl if she tells his story to anyone else.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-18)

Renly wins a hundred [golden dragons](wiki:Currency) from Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) by betting on Sandor against Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), and the Hound advance to the finals. Sandor saves Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) from the wrath of his brother, Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane), during their joust. He fights heatedly with his brother, but kneels when King Robert commands them to stop. Loras allows Sandor to be the champion without a final joust, and the Hound is cheered by Sansa and the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk).(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-4)

Joffrey and Sandor are among Robert's retinue when the king goes hunting in the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood),(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0) but the prince and his bodyguard return to the capital after Robert decides to search for a monstrous boar.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-1) The king is eventually mortally wounded by the boar.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0)

When Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), attempts to take Cersei and her illegitimate children into custody with the help of the [gold cloaks](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing)—unaware that Commander [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) is already under the employ of the treacherous Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish)—Sandor participates in the widespread slaughter of Eddard's remaining [household guard](wiki:House_Stark_guards) alongside his erstwhile allies, personally killing [Cayn](wiki:Cayn).(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0) The Hound also takes [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole) into custody, placing her in Sansa's room, after breaking down her door with a warhammer.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-1)

Sandor laughs when Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) is dismissed from the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), and the Hound is named as his replacement despite refusing to become a [knight](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0) Sandor is often assigned to watch over Sansa, and despite his gruff manner, he treats her with kindness and tries to spare her from Joffrey's sadism. When Joffrey orders Ser [Meryn Trant](wiki:Meryn_Trant) to beat her, Sandor delicately dabs Sansa's bloody lip.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0) Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) disapproves of Sandor's appointment to the Kingsguard upon learning of it.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0)

### A Clash of Kings

According to Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), it was King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon)'s decision to name Sandor to the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-1) Sandor strategically defends [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) from Joffrey's wrath many times and is the only [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) who is never commanded to beat her.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-5) Sandor is present for the [tourney on King Joffrey's name day](wiki:Tourney_on_King_Joffrey's_name_day) as Joffrey's bodyguard. He is dismissive of the competitors, calling them gnats, that it would be a waste of effort to compete himself, and that even Joffrey could beat them.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-6)

When returning from the Red Keep's [godswood](wiki:Godswood) after meeting for the first time with Ser [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard), Joffrey's new [fool](wiki:Fool), Sansa runs into a drunk Sandor. He admires her blooming womanhood, but sees her as still a "stupid little bird […] singing all the songs they taught you". He asks her to sing "some song about knights and fair maids", and mocks Sansa's admiration of [true knights](wiki:True_knight), but she says she will sing for him gladly. Sansa is frightened by him during this encounter, but though he hurts her by grabbing her wrist to stop her from falling, he is also gentle when he protects her from Ser [Boros Blount](wiki:Boros_Blount), and she asks Sandor why he does not let anyone call him a knight. When he takes her back unharmed to the Red Keep, he warns her she is surrounded by liars.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1)

### A Storm of Swords

With Ser [Mandon Moore](wiki:Mandon_Moore) drowning at the Blackwater and Sandor fleeing the battle, Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) joins the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) and Ser [Boros Blount](wiki:Boros_Blount) is restored to the white cloaks.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-0)(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_48-0)

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) considers [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) to be "the Hound with teats".(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_49-0) [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) includes Sandor in her list of those she wants to die.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_50-0) [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) wishes Sandor was still there and keeps his bloodied Kingsguard cloak hidden in a chest,(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-1) and she [believes that he kissed her](wiki:UnKiss) the night of the Blackwater.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_51-0) When Sansa is forced to marry [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), she considers the Imp to be uglier than the Hound.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_52-0)

The [Mad Huntsman](wiki:Mad_Huntsman) captures the vagabond Sandor and brings him to [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). The Hound is taken into custody of the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), who also have Sansa's younger sister, Arya.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_53-0) In the [hollow hill](wiki:Hollow_hill), the brotherhood put Sandor on trial for various atrocities committed by Lannister soldiers, but no crime can be attributed to him personally until Arya testifies to his killing of [Mycah](wiki:Mycah), the butcher's boy. Since there is not enough evidence for a court trial, the brotherhood's leader, Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion), decrees that Sandor will face him in a [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat). A follower of [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor), Beric fights with a magic flaming blade, and Sandor's left arm is burned when his shield catches fire. Despite his fear of fire, Sandor manages to [defeat Beric](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion).(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1)

The brotherhood strip Sandor of most of his possessions, including the remains of his winnings from the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney), and set him free, though he is allowed to keep his arms, armor, and warhorse, [Stranger](wiki:Stranger_(horse)). When several of the brotherhood state that Sandor should be killed, Beric says R'hllor must have a purpose for the Hound.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) Sandor soon returns to try to reclaim his gold by force, upon which he stumbles across Arya as she tries to escape. He kidnaps her instead(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_54-0) and intends to ransom her back to her brother, [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), who is going to attend the wedding of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) and [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey) at the [Twins](wiki:Twins).(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-4)(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_55-0)

Sandor and Arya cross the [Trident](wiki:Trident) at flooded [Harroway](wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town), where the Hound pays the outraged [ferrymen](wiki:Bent-backed_man) with a promissory note from Beric instead of gold.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-5) They arrive at the Twins(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_55-1) shortly before the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) massacre takes place, however.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_56-0) Amidst the fighting, Sandor kills several men—possibly including [Tytos Frey](wiki:Tytos_Frey) and [Garse Goodbrook](wiki:Garse_Goodbrook)—and incapacitates Arya to prevent her from running into the massacre.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-1)(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_57-0)

Sandor hopes to find another relative to whom he can ransom Arya, possibly her aunt, [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn), in the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn). While traveling away from the Twins, Sandor gives the [gift of mercy](wiki:Gift_of_mercy) to a [Piper](wiki:House_Piper) archer who was wounded by [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton) during the Red Wedding.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) Sandor and Arya stay at a village in the foothills of the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon) for a few weeks, but the village elder turns them away after work is finished on a palisade. With the [high road](wiki:High_road) to the Eyrie closed, Sandor decides to bring Arya to Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully), her great-uncle at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2)

Seeking shelter at the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads), Sandor and Arya encounter two of [Gregor's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) and their [pimply squire](wiki:Pimply_squire), who inform them of the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun) by [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey). When the two groups come to blows, Sandor kills [Polliver](wiki:Polliver) and holds off the [Tickler](wiki:Tickler) until the torturer is killed by Arya, who also kills the squire. The hungry and inebriated Sandor is seriously injured, receiving wounds on his thigh, ribs, neck, and face, and the stump of his burned ear is sliced off. Though Arya dresses his wounds, they quickly became infected during their journey to [Saltpans](wiki:Saltpans). Conflicted about her feelings after she leaves him off her list, Arya refuses to grant Sandor the gift of mercy despite his pleas. She rides off, leaving him under a tree by the Trident.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_58-0) The brotherhood without banners search for Sandor.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_57-1)

### A Feast For Crows

Reports spread of the [Hound](wiki:Hound) leading a pack of outlaws in a brutal spree of banditry near the Trident,(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_59-0) and there is confusion over whether he has joined [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion)'s [brotherhood](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners).(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_60-0) Witnesses state a roaring brute in a hound's head helm led the vicious [raid on Saltpans](wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans). Ser [Bonifer Hasty](wiki:Bonifer_Hasty), Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), and Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) intend to bring outlaws like Sandor to justice near [Darry](wiki:Darry), [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), and [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool).(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-1)(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_61-0)

During dinner at Darry, Ser [Lyle Crakehall](wiki:Lyle_Crakehall) claims to have been moved by the distress of [Mariya Darry](wiki:Mariya_Darry) and [Amerei Frey](wiki:Amerei_Frey), and gives them his word that once [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) has fallen he shall return to hunt down the Hound and kill him for them, adding that dogs do not frighten him.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Seeking [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) acts on information that she received from [Timeon](wiki:Timeon) of the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) that Sansa had been carried away by the Hound. Brienne's travels with Septon [Meribald](wiki:Meribald) take her to a [septry](wiki:Septry) on the [Quiet Isle](wiki:Quiet_Isle). The [Elder Brother](wiki:Elder_Brother_(Quiet_Isle)) of the monastery informs her that it was the younger sister that Sandor made off with, [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark). The Elder Brother knows they were headed for [Saltpans](wiki:Saltpans), but he states the Hound is dead, "by the sword, as he had lived" and he buried him personally and left his hound helm as a marker. Someone else found the helm and went on, with others, to rape and kill at Saltpans. The Elder Brother tells Brienne she would have pitied Sandor if she had seen him at the end, crying in pain and begging for the [gift of mercy](wiki:Gift_of_mercy). He tells her the Hound died in his arms, and Sandor Clegane is at rest. He also tells her Sandor's big black stallion, [Stranger](wiki:Stranger_(horse)) is in their stables.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-1)

While at the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn), Brienne encounters a group of [broken men](wiki:Broken_men) led by [Rorge](wiki:Rorge), who wears Sandor's hound helm.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_62-0) Prior to the [capture of Harrenhal](wiki:Capture_of_Harrenhal), Rorge, [Biter](wiki:Biter), and other former Brave Companions had traveled east to seek ship at Saltpans. Rorge had taken Sandor's snarling dog helm from his grave site, and Rorge was responsible for Sandor's alleged atrocities, such as the raid on Saltpans. Rorge is slain in a duel with Brienne.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_62-1)

When the brotherhood returns to the inn, [Lem Lemoncloak](wiki:Lem) takes Sandor's helm from Rorge's corpse. [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr) advises Lem to abandon the helm, seeing it as a symbol of Sandor's rage, but the bitter Lem refuses. This new Hound serves [Lady Stoneheart](wiki:Lady_Stoneheart).(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_63-0)

With the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun) ended, Lyle Crakehall tells Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) he is going to hunt down Beric and the Hound.(wiki:Sandor_Clegane.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_64-0)
