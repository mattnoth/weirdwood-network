## Origins

According to song, King Erreg attacked [High Heart](wiki:High_Heart), a place sacred to the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) of the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) and protected by [First Men](wiki:First_Men) river kings. Erreg's men killed the children and First Men, and then cut down the hill's [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood) grove.(wiki:Erreg_the_Kinslayer.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_arrival_of_the_andals.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) It is believed by [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) that High Heart is still haunted by the dead children.(wiki:Erreg_the_Kinslayer.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

In contrast to the legend, *[True History](wiki:True_History)* insists that the children of the forest had already abandoned the riverlands before the [Andal invasion](wiki:Andal_invasion).(wiki:Erreg_the_Kinslayer.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Archmaester [Perestan](wiki:Perestan) suggests in *[A Consideration of History](wiki:A_Consideration_of_History)* that "Erreg" was not a given name but corruption of an Andal title. Perestan suggests this unnamed chieftain who attacked High Heart did so on behalf of a [river king](wiki:River_king)'s rival.(wiki:Erreg_the_Kinslayer.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
