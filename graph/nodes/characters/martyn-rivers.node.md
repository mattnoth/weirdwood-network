The [baseborn](wiki:Baseborn) Martyn is ruddy, unlike his relatives from [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey).(wiki:Martyn_Rivers.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Martyn attends the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with six of his kin—Ser [Jared](wiki:Jared_Frey), Ser [Hosteen](wiki:Hosteen_Frey), Ser [Danwell](wiki:Danwell_Frey), Ser [Emmon](wiki:Emmon_Frey), Ser [Theo](wiki:Theo_Frey), and Ser [Perwyn Frey](wiki:Perwyn_Frey).(wiki:Martyn_Rivers.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Martyn is at the [Twins](wiki:Twins) when [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s host arrives at the seat of the Freys.(wiki:Martyn_Rivers.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Clash of Kings

While Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) is raiding in the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) with Robb, the new [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), Martyn commands the outriders in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). Martyn escorts [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) when her party nears [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), telling her of Robb's victory in the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross).(wiki:Martyn_Rivers.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Storm of Swords

Catelyn spots Martyn among the Freys who angrily leave [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) after news reaches the castle of Robb's marriage with [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling) instead of a Frey.(wiki:Martyn_Rivers.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
