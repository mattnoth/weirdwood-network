## Identity

Prince Daeron Targaryen, sometimes referred to as Daeron the Drunken, was the eldest son of King Maekar I Targaryen and his wife Dyanna Dayne (wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)).

## Origins

Born in 190 or 191 AC (track_b: Born). Son of Maekar I Targaryen (track_b: Father) and Dyanna Dayne (track_b: Mother).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiance).

## Narrative Arc

Daeron held the titles of Prince of Dragonstone and later Prince of Summerhall. He was known as a drunkard and was featured prominently in The Hedge Knight, where the young Ser Duncan the Tall encountered him. He married Kiera of Tyrosh and had one daughter, Vaella Targaryen. He died between 221 and 233 AC (track_b: Died) (wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)).
