## Identity

Daeron Targaryen (son of Maekar I) — child of Maekar I Targaryen and Dyanna Dayne; married to Kiera of Tyrosh; sworn to House Targaryen; Ser. One of 6 Daeron Targaryens — see the Daeron Targaryen disambiguation entry.

## Origins

### Youth

Daeron was born the eldest child of Prince [Maekar Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen) and his wife, Lady [Dyanna Dayne](wiki:Dyanna_Dayne). He was [named after](wiki:Named_after) his grandfather, King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) He had three younger brothers, [Aerion](wiki:Aerion_Targaryen), [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)), and [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen), and two younger sisters, [Daella](wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Maekar_I)) and [Rhae.](wiki:Rhae_Targaryen)(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_targaryen_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Daeron and his three younger brothers were all given [dragon eggs](wiki:Dragon_egg) when they were young.(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### Tourney at Ashford Meadow

Prince [Maekar Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen) sent his sons, Daeron and [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen), from [Summerhall](wiki:Summerhall) to the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow), hoping they would outshine the sons of his brother, Prince [Baelor Breakspear](wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)). As he was uninterested in participating in the tourney, however, Daeron stole away from his escort, taking Aegon with him. Daeron shaved the head of Aegon, his [squire](wiki:Squire), to hide his Targaryen features. Drinking at an [inn near Ashford](wiki:Inn_near_Ashford), incognito, Daeron encountered [Dunk](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall), and told him he had [dreamed](wiki:Dragon_dreams) of him, ordering Dunk to stay away from him. Aegon, who wanted to participate in the tourney, stayed incognito as "Egg", and asked Dunk if he could be his squire. Though Dunk denied him then, Aegon left the inn and followed him to [Ashford](wiki:Ashford).(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

Maekar sent Ser [Roland Crakehall](wiki:Roland_Crakehall_(Kingsguard)) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) in search of Daeron and Aegon, then left Ashford to search for them himself. When Daeron was found drunk at the inn, he told his father that a huge robber knight made off with Aegon on the road and that he spent the whole time pursuing them. This lie added evidence against Dunk after he was arrested for assaulting [Aerion Targaryen](wiki:Aerion_Targaryen).(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

Daeron apologized to Dunk for lying, saying he had not noticed Aegon was gone. He also said he had dreamed a great [dragon](wiki:Dragon) would fall on Dunk, dead, but the knight would walk off alive. Daeron did not know what it meant, but that his dreams came true and that he was frightened of Dunk. Daeron also told Dunk that he would not fight, and after the first charge in the [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven), he would stay on the ground and would also withdraw his accusations.(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)

In the trial, Daeron was unhorsed by Ser [Robyn Rhysling](wiki:Robyn_Rhysling), and did as he had promised, staying on the ground. His own horse stepped on him, possibly breaking his foot, but otherwise he was uninjured. Nevertheless, it turned out that Daeron's dream was a true [prophecy](wiki:Prophecy), as Prince Baelor died from his injuries in Dunk's arms.(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7)

### Later life

Daeron's father ascended to the throne as [Maekar I Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen) in [221 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) after the death of King [Aerys I Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_I_Targaryen), who had no other living heirs. This made Daeron the [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone) and heir to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), though he preferred to be called [Prince of Summerhall](wiki:Prince_of_Summerhall) as he considered [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) "such a gloomy abode".(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maekar_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Daeron's brother [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) served as his [maester](wiki:Maester).(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Daeron married [Kiera of Tyrosh](wiki:Kiera_of_Tyrosh), the widow of his cousin, Prince [Valarr Targaryen](wiki:Valarr_Targaryen).(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_targaryen_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Their daughter, Princess [Vaella Targaryen](wiki:Vaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daeron)) was born in [222 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), and proved to be sweet but [simple-minded](wiki:Lackwit).(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maekar_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Daeron died some time before [233 AC](wiki:233_AC), reputedly from a [pox](wiki:Pox) he caught from a [whore](wiki:Whore).(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) His brother Aemon claimed the dreams of dragons that plagued Daeron killed him, as they did their brothers [Aerion](wiki:Aerion_Targaryen) and [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen).(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Daeron predeceasing his father was part of what led to the succession crisis after Maekar's death, which was resolved in the [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council) of [233 AC](wiki:233_AC).(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maekar_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

## Appearances & Description

Daeron was a thin man with sallow skin, sandy brown hair, and a blond beard. He had a fine damask mantle and a red-and-black doublet. When in his cups, his clothes could be stained with wine.(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Oft drunk, Daeron was not much of a [knight](wiki:Knight), disliking [horses](wiki:Horse) and swords. In combat, he wore an overlapping black and [gold](wiki:Gold) scaled [armor](wiki:Armor) with a green [silk](wiki:Silk) plume attached to his helm, and rode a bay destrier.(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), tells his steward, [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), that Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) and his oldest brother Daeron, together with their other brothers, had been summoned to court when their father had been crowned King [Maekar I Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen). Daeron, as the [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone), was joined by his second brother Aemon, who had served as Daeron's [maester](wiki:Maester). Jeor tells Jon that Daeron died of the [pox](wiki:Pox), leaving only a [feeble-witted daughter](wiki:Vaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daeron)) behind as his heir.(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3)

### A Feast for Crows

In the twilight of his life, Maester Aemon recalls how all three of his brothers [dreamed of dragons](wiki:Dragon_dreams) and how the dreams killed each of them.(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) During one of his last good days, Aemon wonders if he will be reunited with his siblings, Daeron amongst them, when he dies. Aemon also mentions his brother's dream and a prophecy before telling Sam that [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen),(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) who birthed the first dragons in over a century,(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) is their hope. Although Aemon does not specify which brother or what the dream was about,(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) Daeron did dream that the dragons would return.(wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I).cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
