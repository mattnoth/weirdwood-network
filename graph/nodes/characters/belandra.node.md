## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Belandra is among the servants who are allowed access to Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) during her imprisonment in the [Spear Tower](wiki:Spear_Tower) of [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear). Arianne seeks her aid, but her requests are ignored by Belandra.(wiki:Belandra.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
