## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord Vance of Wayfarer's Rest and Lord [Clement Piper](wiki:Clement_Piper) of [Pinkmaiden Castle](wiki:Pinkmaiden_Castle) are tasked by Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) to guard the pass below the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth) and prevent the [westermen](wiki:Westermen) to enter in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Lord_Vance.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) leads an attack against the outnumbered riverlords and defeats them. Lord Vance is slain in the [battle](wiki:Battle_in_the_hills_below_the_Golden_Tooth) while Lord Clement retreats back to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) with the remnants of his forces.(wiki:Lord_Vance.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Lord Vance is succeeded by his son, Ser [Karyl Vance](wiki:Karyl_Vance).(wiki:Lord_Vance.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
