## Origins

With Wisdoms [Rossart](wiki:Rossart) and [Garigus](wiki:Garigus), Belis took part in King Aerys's [wildfire plot](wiki:Wildfire_plot) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). According to Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), Belis was as enthusiastic about the plan as the other two pyromancers. The three secretly deployed wildfire caches across the whole city, to be ignited at the command of the king.(wiki:Belis.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
A couple of days after he had killed Rossart and Aerys at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), Jaime hunted down Belis and Garigus and slew them as well. Belis tried to bribe Jaime with gold in exchange for his life.(wiki:Belis.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
