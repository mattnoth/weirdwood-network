## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

After the war council called by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) at the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Jaime visits the captive [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) on the gallows passing Ser [Ryman Frey](wiki:Ryman_Frey)'s camp pavilion, who had sent his son [Edwyn Frey](wiki:Edwyn_Frey) to the council in his stead claiming to be indisposed, within enjoying some entertainment from the sounds of a [woodharp](wiki:Harp) and a [singer](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams) with a woman's drunken laughter. Ryman shortly arrives at the gallows with his whore, both are drunk and the woman's breasts are exposed. Ryman has crowned her with [the crown](wiki:Crown) of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), taken by [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey) after the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding). When Jaime strikes Ryman in the face with his [golden](wiki:Gold) hand he falls back into the queen of whores arms. While dismissing Ryman from the siege, Jaime tells him he may take his whore, but not her crown.(wiki:Queen_o'_whores.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Ryman is hanged by [outlaws](wiki:Outlaws) while returning to the [Twins](wiki:Twins).(wiki:Queen_o'_whores.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) After being captured by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth) sees [Lady Stoneheart](wiki:Lady_Stoneheart) holding Robb's crown.(wiki:Queen_o'_whores.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The fate of Ryman's prostitute is unknown.
