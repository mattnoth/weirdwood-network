## Origins

When Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake) became [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), he decided to replace Septon [Eustace](wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)) with a younger man, his cousin Bernard. After Unwin resigned and left the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC), Bernard remained behind.(wiki:Bernard.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) When [Larra Rogare](wiki:Larra_Rogare), the wife of Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen), gave birth to Prince [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen), Septon Bernard anointed the child with the seven sacred oils in the [royal sept](wiki:Royal_sept).(wiki:Bernard.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

During the plot against [House Rogare](wiki:House_Rogare) in [135 AC](wiki:135_AC), Septon Bernard tried to persuade King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) to leave [Maegor's Holdfast](wiki:Maegor's_Holdfast) during the [secret siege](wiki:Secret_siege). Bernard wrote down Lord [Thaddeus Rowan](wiki:Thaddeus_Rowan)'s confession as the man was being [tortured](wiki:Torture). Bernard was later named as being an accomplice to the plot, and eventually confessed to his involvement, citing his hatred of the [Lyseni](wiki:Lys) and their foreign [gods](wiki:God). He claimed that it had been intended for Larra to die, so Viserys could ascend the throne and take a "proper Westerosi queen."(wiki:Bernard.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

During the [treason](wiki:Treason) trials, the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Regency_of_Aegon_III)) wrote asking that Bernard be spared execution so that he might repent. King Aegon obliged, and Bernard was [gelded](wiki:Eunuch) and sent to walk barefoot from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) with his manhood hung around his neck. He spent the rest of his life as a scribe copying holy books at the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept), under a vow of silence.(wiki:Bernard.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
