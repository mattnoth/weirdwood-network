## Appearances & Description

Ser Andar's [silvered](wiki:Silver) steel plate is filigreed in [bronze](wiki:Bronze) with inscribed [runes](wiki:Runes). Like those worn by his kin, the ancient runes are said to protect [House Royce](wiki:House_Royce) from harm.(wiki:Andar_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Lady [Anya Waynwood](wiki:Anya_Waynwood) considers Andar and his brothers to be fine sons and well-raised.(wiki:Andar_Royce.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser Andar travels to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to participate in the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) with his father, Lord [Yohn](wiki:Yohn_Royce), and his younger brother, Ser [Robar Royce](wiki:Robar_Royce). During the jousts, Andar is easily defeated by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Andar_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

The Royces accompany King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) on his hunt into the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood).(wiki:Andar_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) After Robert dies from being gored by a [boar](wiki:Boar), the Royces are among the nobles who King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) insists must do homage to him or be named traitors.(wiki:Andar_Royce.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Clash of Kings

As the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) breaks out, Andar and his father remain in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) in service to Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn).(wiki:Andar_Royce.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
