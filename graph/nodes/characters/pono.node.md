## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When Drogo falls ill, Pono is the first to declare himself *[khal](wiki:Khal)* and takes part of Drogo's *khalasar* with him.(wiki:Pono.cite_ref-Ragot68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

### A Clash of Kings

When [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) sets out with her *khalasar*, she is advised against going downriver by [Rakharo](wiki:Rakharo). He says that is the direction that Pono went with ten thousand of Drogo's *khalasar*. Pono intends to sell thousands of captives at [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay).(wiki:Pono.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

The *khalasar* of Khal Pono is reported to be near the headwaters of the [Selhoru](wiki:Selhoru). His *khalasar* is said to be thirty thousand strong and other *khals* such as [Zekko](wiki:Zekko) and [Motho](wiki:Motho) fear Pono's *khalasar*.(wiki:Pono.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [Qavo Nogarys](wiki:Qavo_Nogarys) is not alarmed that Pono is outside [Selhorys](wiki:Selhorys), stating:

> The horselords come, we give them gifts, the horselords go.(wiki:Pono.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
