## Origins

A foundling from his birth, left in an empty stall in the [Scribe's Hearth](wiki:Scribe's_Hearth) of the Citadel, Yandel was found by an [acolyte](wiki:Maesters) who took him to the [Seneschal](wiki:Maesters) of that year, [Archmaester](wiki:Archmaester) [Edgerran](wiki:Edgerran). Edgerran announced that Yandel might prove of use, which when Yandel first heard the story, he believed to mean that Edgerran foresaw his destiny as a maester. It would be only later that Yandel would discover that Edgerran had been writing a treatise on the swaddling of infants at the time, and had wished to test certain theories.(wiki:Yandel.cite_ref-Rtwoiafpreface.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Yandel was given to the care of servants, and received the occasional attention of maesters. He was raised as a servant amongst the halls, chambers, and libraries. Archmaester [Walgrave](wiki:Walgrave) taught him how to read and write. Yandel desired nothing more than to become one of the maesters. He wanted to read about faraway places and long-dead men, to gaze at the stars and measure the passing of the seasons. At the age of thirteen, in [285 AC](wiki:285_AC), Yandel forged the first link of his chain, and other links would follow, until he completed his studies in [292 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Yandel.cite_ref-Rtwoiafpreface.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Having finished his studies, Yandel remained at the Citadel, serving the archmaesters and aiding them in all that they required. Meanwhile, he set about completing his work, *[The World of Ice & Fire](wiki:The_World_of_Ice_&_Fire_(Yandel))*.(wiki:Yandel.cite_ref-Rtwoiafpreface.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Archmaester [Perestan](wiki:Perestan) reminded Yandel that Archmaester [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn) was working on a history of the Targaryen kings, namely, *[Fire & Blood, Being a History of the Targaryen Kings of Westeros](wiki:Fire_&_Blood,_Being_a_History_of_the_Targaryen_Kings_of_Westeros)*. Yandel persuaded Gyldayn to permit him to use fragments of the uncompleted *Fire & Blood* in his *The World of Ice & Fire*.(wiki:Yandel.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Yandel.cite_ref-GyldaynRetcon_4-0)

Yandel dedicated *The World of Ice & Fire* to King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). Upon Robert's death, however, someone had his name replaced with [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon)'s and later [Tommen](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon)'s. Yandel himself, for fear of angering the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister), also cut out the vast majority of material on [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) and [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), and several others.(wiki:Yandel.cite_ref-5)
