## Identity

Quent is a member of the household guard sworn to House Stark of Winterfell (wiki:Quent).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

A younger guardsman whose beard is soft brown fuzz (wiki:Quent.cite_ref-Ragot37).

## Narrative Arc

Quent accompanies Robb and Bran Stark into the wolfswood for Bran's first ride since his fall. He and Wayn fall behind following Theon Greyjoy after he spies a turkey, failing to guard Bran, who falls into the hands of wildlings and Night's Watch deserters (wiki:Quent.cite_ref-Ragot37). Later, Quent serves under Hallis Mollen's command escorting the silent sisters carrying Lord Eddard's bones north toward Winterfell, alongside Jacks and Shadd (wiki:Quent.cite_ref-Rasos39). Lady Barbrey Dustin tells Theon that should the bones emerge from the Neck, the escorts will be prevented from going north of Barrowton (wiki:Quent.cite_ref-Radwd41).
