## Appearances & Description

Quent is a younger guardsman whose beard is soft brown fuzz.(wiki:Quent.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Quent is part of the party that accompanies [Robb](wiki:Robb_Stark) and [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) into the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) for Bran's first ride since his fall. He and [Wayn](wiki:Wayn_(guard)) fall behind and then follow [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) after he spies a turkey. They fail to guard Bran, who is left to his own devices and falls into the hands of a small party of [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) and deserters from the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Quent.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Clash of Kings

When Catelyn's party returns to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) from [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), she orders [Hallis Mollen](wiki:Hallis_Mollen) and a small honor guard to escort the [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters) who are to bring the bones of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) back to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Quent.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Unbeknownst to Catelyn, however, [ironmen](wiki:Ironmen) led by [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) have already moved to [take Moat Cailin](wiki:Fall_of_Moat_Cailin), and Theon is on his way to [take Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) as well.(wiki:Quent.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Quent.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Hal continues north toward Winterfell with Quent, [Jacks](wiki:Jacks), and [Shadd](wiki:Shadd).(wiki:Quent.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons
