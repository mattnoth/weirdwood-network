## Origins

Mern was a second cousin of the late [Garth X Gardener](wiki:Garth_X_Gardener), who had died after becoming a tool in a [war](wiki:Anarchy_in_the_Reach) between his goodsons from [House Peake](wiki:House_Peake) and [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) for supremacy. Ser [Osmund Tyrell](wiki:Osmund_Tyrell), [High Steward](wiki:High_Steward), had led an alliance of houses against the two factions, defeated them, and placed Mern on the throne as King Mern VI Gardener.(wiki:Mern_VI_Gardener.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_house_tyrell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
Although a man of modest gifts, Mern ruled well, rebuilding [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) and doing much and more to restore the power of the [Reach](wiki:Reach) and House Gardener, relying on the counsel of his stewards: Ser Osmund, and later Osmund's son Ser [Robert Tyrell](wiki:Robert_Tyrell), and finally Robert's son [Lorent Tyrell](wiki:Lorent_Tyrell_(steward)).(wiki:Mern_VI_Gardener.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) King Mern was so pleased with them that he gave Ser Robert the hand of his youngest daughter in marriage, thereby allowing the following generations of [Tyrells](wiki:House_Tyrell) to claim decent from [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand).(wiki:Mern_VI_Gardener.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_house_tyrell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Mern VI was succeeded by his son [Garth XI](wiki:Garth_XI_Gardener).(wiki:Mern_VI_Gardener.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
