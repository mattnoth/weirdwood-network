## Appearances & Description

Marlon is well over six feet tall (nearly a head taller than [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth)) and quite stout. The knight has a grey beard and slate-grey eyes.(wiki:Marlon_Manderly.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

The frank Ser Marlon can be haughty and disdainful.(wiki:Marlon_Manderly.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Marlon wears ornate [silver](wiki:Silver) armor with niello engravings meant to look like flowing seaweed. His helm is modeled after the head of the [Merling King](wiki:Merling_King_(god)), having a crown of [mother-of-pearl](wiki:Mother-of-pearl) and a beard of [jet](wiki:Jet) and [jade](wiki:Jade).(wiki:Marlon_Manderly.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Marlon escorts Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth), an envoy of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), to the [Merman's Court](wiki:Merman's_Court) in the [New Castle](wiki:New_Castle). Standing by the right hand of Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly), Marlon curtly questions Davos about Stannis's prospects. Wyman publicly orders his cousin to have Davos taken to the [Wolf's Den](wiki:Wolf's_Den) and executed.(wiki:Marlon_Manderly.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) The Manderlys secretly spare Davos, however.(wiki:Marlon_Manderly.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
