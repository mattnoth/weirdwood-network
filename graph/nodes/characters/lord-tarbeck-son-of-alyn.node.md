## Origins

Lord Tarbeck was born a few months after the death of his father, Lord [Alyn Tarbeck](wiki:Alyn_Tarbeck), who died during the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye) in [43 AC](wiki:43_AC).(wiki:Lord_Tarbeck_(son_of_Alyn).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

In [47 AC](wiki:47_AC), King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) summoned his mother, Lady [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling_(wife_of_Maegor_I)), and him to court, where he married the widow as one of his [Black Brides](wiki:Black_Brides), using the young Lord Tarbeck as a hostage to prevent any defiance. Lord Tarbeck was later confirmed as Alyn's heir and [Lord of Tarbeck Hall](wiki:Lord_of_Tarbeck_Hall). He was sent to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) as a [ward](wiki:Ward) to Lord [Lyman Lannister](wiki:Lyman_Lannister).(wiki:Lord_Tarbeck_(son_of_Alyn).cite_ref-Rtsotd.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Lord_Tarbeck_(son_of_Alyn).cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
