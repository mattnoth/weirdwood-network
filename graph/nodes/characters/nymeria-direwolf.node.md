## Appearances & Description

Nymeria has dark golden eyes(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Ragot10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and grey fur.(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

The [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) loves [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) and trusts the girl.(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Ragot7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) The direwolf is trainable and learns from Arya's instructions.(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Ragot10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Nymeria is found with her litter-mates near their dead mother in the snow by [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) and [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) as they return from [the hills](wiki:Northern_mountains) near [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) allows his children to each adopt a [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf).(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Eddard's younger daughter, [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), names her new pet after the famous [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria) of the [Rhoynar](wiki:Rhoynar).(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Ragot7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) The girl keeps Nymeria as close as possible in Winterfell, even allowing the direwolf to sleep at the foot of her bed.(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Ragot7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) Eddard accepts King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s offer to become [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), and Arya has Nymeria help her pack when she prepares to leave Winterfell for the journey south to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Ragot10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

Near the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn), Arya cleans dried mud from Nymeria and brushes her tangles by the banks of the [Trident](wiki:Trident). Arya and her new friend [Mycah](wiki:Mycah) then hunt for [rubies](wiki:Ruby) at the [ruby ford](wiki:Ruby_ford). When Prince [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and Arya's sister [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) stumble upon Arya and Mycah playing at swords, Joffrey challenges the butcher's boy to a duel and cuts him in the process. Joffrey refuses to relent, which leads Arya to hit the prince with her stick. When Joffrey attacks Arya with his sword, [Lion's Tooth](wiki:Lion's_Tooth), Nymeria lunges at Joffrey, biting his arm and knocking the prince to the ground. After letting him go, Nymeria flees with Arya.(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

[Jory Cassel](wiki:Jory_Cassel) finds Arya and Nymeria in the woods. Fearing for Nymeria's life, Arya and Jory chase her away by shouting and throwing rocks at her.(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) After bringing Arya to [Darry](wiki:Darry), Jory tells King Robert that no trace was found of her direwolf. Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), angry that her son Joffrey had been attacked, convinces Robert to order the death of Sansa's innocent direwolf, [Lady](wiki:Lady_(direwolf)).(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

In King's Landing, Eddard reveals to Arya he knew that she and Jory had lied about Nymeria running off, and Arya admits that they had to force the wolf away.(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

### A Clash of Kings

On her march north with [Yoren](wiki:Yoren) and his recruits for the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), Arya hears talk of a huge female wolf who leads a pack around the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye) that has no fear of men. Lord [William Mooton](wiki:William_Mooton) and his sons ride out from [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) to hunt her, but are forced to retreat from the wolf's lair, losing their wolfhounds in the process. Arya disputes the storyteller's claim that the wolf took a baby out a mother's arms, insisting that wolves do not eat babies.(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Racok5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

When Arya is introduced to Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) after the [fall of Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal), she identifies herself as "Nymeria".(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Arya sees a pack of wolves in the distance after escaping from [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), and the largest of them returns her call when she howls at them.(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Rasos3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Arya then has a [wolf dream](wiki:Wolf_dream) in which she is hunted by four [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) sent from Harrenhal. Nymeria kills [Iggo](wiki:Iggo), and her wolves kill the other three [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords).(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Rasos3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

After escaping the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) with [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane), Arya has nightly dreams of leading a wolfpack. In one of them she retrieves [a woman's body](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) from [a river](wiki:Green_Fork) and tries to wake it, but then flees from the body when she sees [unknown men](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) approaching.(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Rasos65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) In another dream, Arya leads her pack in running down a riderless horse.(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Arys begins serving as "no one" at the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White) in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos). When the [kindly man](wiki:Kindly_man) asks about the candles burning in the temple, Arya thinks to herself that they remind her of her wolf. Arya dreams at night of leading a great pack in a forest.(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Arya continues the dreams while acting as "Cat of the Canals", and [Brea](wiki:Brea) claims that the girl growls in her sleep.(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

Septon [Meribald](wiki:Meribald) reports that a great pack of hundreds of wolves prowls the [Trident](wiki:Trident). He has not seen them himself, but he and [Dog](wiki:Dog_(Meribald)) are scared by their howls.(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) leads a royal host from King's Landing to bring order to the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). They are forced to be wary of wolves at night, and one of Ser [Addam Marbrand](wiki:Addam_Marbrand)'s outriders has his horse killed by the predators.(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) At [Darry](wiki:Darry), Ser [Danwell Frey](wiki:Danwell_Frey) complains of an unprecedented number of wolves in the riverlands and tells Jaime that packs of wolves attacked their baggage train as they traveled from the [Twins](wiki:Twins).(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) While they spar at night, Jaime admits to Ser [Ilyn Payne](wiki:Ilyn_Payne) that he might have maimed or killed Arya for Cersei if he had found her near Darry instead of her father's men.(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Raffc30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1)

Ser [Dermot of the Rainwood](wiki:Dermot_of_the_Rainwood) scouts near [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), and he reports to Jaime of finding hundreds of fearless wolves which killed two of his sentries and attacked his horses. One of the dying men said the pack was led by "a she-wolf of monstrous size".(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

In [his own wolf dream](wiki:Ghost), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) senses Nymeria howling to the [moon](wiki:Moon), with a hundred wolves of her pack singing with her. She was in hills warmer than the area of the [Wall](wiki:Wall), and full of food. Her pack hunts sheep, cows, horses, and even men.(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Now serving the [Faceless Men](wiki:Faceless_Men) as "Blind Beth", Arya continues having wolf dreams at night, and she wants to hunt down her enemies as the night wolf. She sees snowfall in the riverlands, and understands that prey has been scarcer since the snow began. During one of these dreams, Nymeria and her pack feast on a shepherd, his dogs, and his flock.(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### The Winds of Winter

Arya, now using the identity of "Mercy", a [mummer](wiki:Mummer) at [the Gate](wiki:Gate_(playhouse)) in Braavos, wakes from a wolf dream of Nymeria running through a forest with her pack, hunting prey, and being watched by [a tree](wiki:Bran_Stark).(wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf).cite_ref-Mercy_20-0)
