## Identity

Nymeria is a direwolf bonded to Arya Stark. She is a litter-mate of Grey Wind, Lady, Summer, Shaggydog, and Ghost. Born in 298 AC in the north. Named after Princess Nymeria of the Rhoynar (wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf)).

## Origins

Nymeria was discovered with her litter-mates in the snow near their dead mother by the Stark children north of Winterfell (wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf)).

## Allegiances

`OWNS`: Arya Stark owns Nymeria (wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf) infobox: Owner).

## Appearances & Description

No distinctive physical description is given beyond her being a direwolf. She grows to enormous size in the wild and leads a massive wolfpack of hundreds of wolves in the riverlands (wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf)).

## Narrative Arc

**AGOT:** Near the crossroads inn, Joffrey attacks Arya and Mycah. Nymeria lunges at Joffrey, biting his arm and knocking him down, then flees. Arya and Jory Cassel chase Nymeria away by throwing rocks to save her from Cersei's wrath. Lady is killed in Nymeria's stead (wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf), agot-7).

**ACOK:** Reports spread of a huge female wolf leading a pack around the Gods Eye with no fear of men. Lord William Mooton rides out from Maidenpool to hunt her but is forced to retreat. When Arya is brought before Roose Bolton at Harrenhal, she gives her name as "Nymeria" (wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf)).

**ASOS:** Arya has wolf dreams of leading Nymeria's pack. In one dream, Nymeria kills Iggo and her wolves kill three other Brave Companions sent from Harrenhal. In another, she retrieves a woman's body from a river — this is Catelyn Stark's corpse, which Nymeria drags from the Green Fork before the Brotherhood Without Banners finds it (wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf), asos chapters).

**AFFC:** Arya continues having wolf dreams in Braavos. Septon Meribald reports a great pack of hundreds of wolves prowling the Trident. Jaime Lannister's royal host is harried by wolves at night. Ser Dermot reports seeing a she-wolf leading the pack, "as big as an aurochs" (wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf), affc chapters).

**ADWD:** Arya continues dreaming of Nymeria's pack from Braavos. The wolfpack remains active in the riverlands (wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf), adwd chapters).
