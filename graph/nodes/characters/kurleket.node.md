### A Game of Thrones

When called upon for loyalty to [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully) by Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), along with [Lharys](wiki:Lharys) and [Mohor](wiki:Mohor), Kurleket assists in the capture of [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) at the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn).
