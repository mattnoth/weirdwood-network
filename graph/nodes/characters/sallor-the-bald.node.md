## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Sallor is one of the senior captains of the [Stormcrows](wiki:Stormcrows) when they take a contract to defend [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) against the forces of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen). He is among those who attend the parley with Daenerys where she attempts to bribe the captains to come to her side but is rebuffed by [Prendahl na Ghezn](wiki:Prendahl_na_Ghezn). However, the Stormcrows are free men and the captains return to camp to put the question to their men. [Daario Naharis](wiki:Daario_Naharis), a fellow captain, has already been won over, however. Daario kills Sallor and Prendahl and delivers their heads to Daenerys in order to bring the Stormcrows over to her. Daenerys's [dragons](wiki:Dragon) eat the heads.(wiki:Sallor_the_Bald.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Sallor_the_Bald.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
