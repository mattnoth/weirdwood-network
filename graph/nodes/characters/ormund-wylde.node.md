## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser Ormund supports [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), and he accompanies King Stannis to the [Wall](wiki:Wall). In a betting pool organized by lower-born [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms), Ormund is the heavy favorite to die next from the cold during Stannis's [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell).(wiki:Ormund_Wylde.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

During a discussion among Stannis's lords and knights in the longhall of the [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village) the army has stopped at, Ormund suggests that they remain there until the snowy weather breaks since they have some shelter and can fish from the nearby lake.(wiki:Ormund_Wylde.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
