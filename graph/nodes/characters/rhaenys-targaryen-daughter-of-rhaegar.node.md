## Identity

Princess Rhaenys Targaryen was the daughter of Prince Rhaegar Targaryen and Princess Elia Martell. She was murdered as a toddler during the Sack of King's Landing in 283 AC (wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Rhaegar)).

## Origins

Born 280 AC at Dragonstone (track_b: Born). Valyrian race, Crownlands culture (track_b: Race, Culture).

## Appearances & Description

According to a semi-canon source, Rhaenys looked like a Martell. She had a small black kitten she named Balerion, after Balerion the Black Dread, and liked to pretend it was a real dragon (wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Rhaegar)).

## Narrative Arc

After her birth in 280 AC, Rhaenys was presented at court by Rhaegar. Queen Rhaella embraced her warmly, but King Aerys II refused to touch or hold her, saying she "smelled Dornish." Princess Arianne Martell was told she held Rhaenys once when she was too young to remember.

During Robert's Rebellion, Aerys kept Elia, Rhaenys, and infant Aegon as hostages in the Red Keep to ensure Dornish loyalty, refusing to let them flee to Dragonstone with Rhaella and Viserys. During the Sack of King's Landing, Tywin Lannister commanded the deaths of Rhaegar's children. Ser Amory Lorch dragged Rhaenys from under her father's bed, where she had been hiding with her kitten Balerion, and stabbed her to death — reportedly half a hundred times. Her mother Elia and brother Aegon were killed by Ser Gregor Clegane (wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Rhaegar)).

Varys later tells Ned Stark the story of Rhaenys's kitten Balerion, and invokes her fate as a threat to Sansa's safety (agot-eddard-15).
