## Appearances & Description

Mollono looks more like a scribe than a warrior, with a heavy paunch and inky hands. However, he is very clever.(wiki:Mollono_Yos_Dob.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Mollono Yos Dob is freed by [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) during her efforts to end slavery in [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay).

### A Dance with Dragons

Mollono is named commander of the newly formed company of [freedmen](wiki:Freedmen), [Stalwart Shields](wiki:Stalwart_Shields). He is present at several war council meetings, and gives good advice.(wiki:Mollono_Yos_Dob.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Mollono_Yos_Dob.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) However, he dies from the [pale mare](wiki:Pale_mare) infection that sweeps [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), and is replaced as commander by [Tal Toraq](wiki:Tal_Toraq).(wiki:Mollono_Yos_Dob.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
