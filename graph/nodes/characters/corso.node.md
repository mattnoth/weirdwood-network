## Identity

Corso was the maester at Summerhall by the end of King Aegon V Targaryen's reign (wiki:Corso).

## Allegiances

## Narrative Arc

Corso sent a letter describing the Tragedy at Summerhall before his death in 259 AC, but a mishap led to ink blotting out much of the information. The surviving fragments mention "the blood of the dragon," "seven eggs," pyromancers, wildfire, and flames growing out of control (wiki:Corso).

## Quotes

"...the blood of the dragon gathered in one... ...seven eggs, to honor the seven gods, though the king's own septon had warned... ...pyromancers... ...wild fire... ...flames grew out of control...towering... burned so hot that... ...died, but for the valor of the Lord Comman..." (wiki:Corso)
