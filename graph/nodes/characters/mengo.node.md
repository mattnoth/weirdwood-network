## Origins

Compelled by his mother [Doshi](wiki:Doshi), Mengo united all Dothraki tribes and [khalasars](wiki:Khalasar) under his rule, becoming the first great khal. He enslaved or killed those who refused to follow him.(wiki:Mengo.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
In his old age, he turned his attentions westward and began to move against the [Kingdom of Sarnor](wiki:Kingdom_of_Sarnor). As the [Sarnori](wiki:Tall_Men) were contemptuous of the horselords, they sought to use the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) in their own wars, offering them gold, slaves, and other gifts to fight their rivals. Khal Mengo took these gifts and then took the conquered lands as well, burning fields and towns and farms to return the [grasslands](wiki:Dothraki_sea) to their former state, as Dothraki consider it sinful to cut the flesh of the earth with plows, spades, and axes. In time he was succeeded by his son [Moro](wiki:Moro_(Century_of_Blood)).(wiki:Mengo.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
