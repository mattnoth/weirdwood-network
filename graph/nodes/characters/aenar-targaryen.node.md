## Identity

Aenar Targaryen, known as Aenar the Exile, was a nobleman and dragonlord of House Targaryen from the Valyrian Freehold. He was the father of Daenys "the Dreamer" and Gaemon "the Glorious" (wiki:Aenar_Targaryen).

## Origins

Born in or before 138 BC in Valyria (track_b: Born). Died in or after 102 BC at Dragonstone (track_b: Died). Had several wives (track_b: Spouse).

## Narrative Arc

Aenar relocated his family and dragons from Valyria to Dragonstone twelve years before the Doom of Valyria, heeding the prophetic dreams of his maiden daughter Daenys the Dreamer. This decision saved House Targaryen from destruction while every other dragonlord family perished in the Doom. He became known as "the Exile" and "the Lord who Left" (wiki:Aenar_Targaryen).
