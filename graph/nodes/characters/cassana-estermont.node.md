## Origins

Cassana was the daughter of [Lord Estermont](wiki:Lord_Estermont), [Lord of Greenstone](wiki:Lord_of_Greenstone).(wiki:Cassana_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Cassana_Estermont.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

In [262 AC](wiki:262_AC), [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) was born to Cassana and her husband, Lord [Steffon Baratheon](wiki:Steffon_Baratheon), at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End).(wiki:Cassana_Estermont.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Their son [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) followed in [264 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest),(wiki:Cassana_Estermont.cite_ref-5) and [Renly](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) in [277 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Cassana_Estermont.cite_ref-6)

In [278 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Steffon Baratheon was sent to the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) by his cousin, King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), to search for a bride for his son, Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen). Steffon and Cassana's quest was unsuccessful, but in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) they did find a remarkable [fool](wiki:Fool), called [Patchface](wiki:Patchface).(wiki:Cassana_Estermont.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Cassana_Estermont.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

During the return journey, their ship, the *[Windproud](wiki:Windproud)*, was caught in a severe storm. Within sight of Robert and Stannis, who were standing on the parapets of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), the ship was smashed on the rocks and sank in [Shipbreaker Bay](wiki:Shipbreaker_Bay). Cassana, Steffon, and hundreds of men drowned; Patchface was the only survivor.(wiki:Cassana_Estermont.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)(wiki:Cassana_Estermont.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s letter to all the [lords](wiki:Lord) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), which proclaims himself the true king and heir of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), includes the citation that he is the trueborn son of [Steffon](wiki:Steffon_Baratheon) and Cassana.(wiki:Cassana_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Stannis tells Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) that he stopped believing in gods when his mother and father were killed.(wiki:Cassana_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

At Stannis and [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)'s confrontation at [their parley at Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Stannis grants his brother a day to rethink his position, for the sake of their late mother.(wiki:Cassana_Estermont.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Stannis tells Davos that since his loss at the [battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), most of his lords have abandoned his cause and bent the knee to King [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), even including [Lord Estermont](wiki:Lord_Estermont), his mother's father.(wiki:Cassana_Estermont.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Feast for Crows

[Queen Regent](wiki:Queen_Regent) [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) recalls that [the Estermonts](wiki:House_Estermont) are her in-laws, via he mother of her late husband, King Robert. Disparaging the Estermonts, Cersei feels that Steffon must have chosen Cassana "in a fit of lust or madness".(wiki:Cassana_Estermont.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
