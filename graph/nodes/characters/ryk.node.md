## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

After [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) kills [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand) in the [Skirling Pass](wiki:Skirling_Pass),(wiki:Ryk.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) [Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt) orders Longspear Ryk and [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte) to guard Jon after he proves he is a turncloak.(wiki:Ryk.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Ryk claims Qhorin's helm, but he gives it to Ygritte because it does not fit him.(wiki:Ryk.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

After Jon is told by [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) to join [Tormund](wiki:Tormund)'s force, Ryk and Ygritte abandon Rattleshirt and join Tormund as well. Jon grows fond of Ryk as they travel.(wiki:Ryk.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Ryk.cite_ref-Rasos26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) Longspear sings "[The Last of the Giants](wiki:The_Last_of_the_Giants)" with Ygritte, Tormund, and Tormund's family.(wiki:Ryk.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

During Jon's parley amid Mance's [attack on the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), Tormund informs Jon that Ryk stole his daughter, [Munda](wiki:Munda), after fighting off his younger sons, [Dryn](wiki:Dryn) and [Dormund](wiki:Dormund).(wiki:Ryk.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Tormund informs Jon that Ryk has married Munda.(wiki:Ryk.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
