## Appearances & Description

The Nightingale is renowned for her beauty.(wiki:Nightingale_(courtesan).cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Like every Braavosi courtesan, she has her own barge and servants to pole her to trysts.(wiki:Nightingale_(courtesan).cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Men can be challenged to a duel by [bravos](wiki:Bravo) if they do not say that the Nightingale is the most beautiful woman in the world when asked.(wiki:Nightingale_(courtesan).cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

While performing at the [House of Seven Lamps](wiki:House_of_Seven_Lamps), [Dareon](wiki:Dareon), a deserter from the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), tells the Nightingale he would never be able to leave her to return to the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Nightingale_(courtesan).cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

[Cat of the Canals](wiki:Arya_Stark) advises [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) to say that the Nightingale is the most beautiful woman in the world if asked by [bravos](wiki:Bravo), claiming that her father was killed when he said her mother was more beautiful than the courtesan.(wiki:Nightingale_(courtesan).cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
