## Identity

Ronnel Arryn, the boy King of Mountain and Vale at Aegon's Conquest — son of Sharra Arryn, the queen regent who yielded the Eyrie to Visenya Targaryen. Called "the King Who Flew" after Visenya took him thrice about the summit of the Giant's Lance on Vhagar; made Lord of the Eyrie under Aegon I and wed a daughter of Torrhen Stark. Later deposed, imprisoned, and murdered by his brother Jonos during the reign of Aenys I. Minted S208 (review-bucket recovery) — the bare slug `ronnel-arryn` is a disambiguation entry, so this node carries the standard `-son-of-` qualifier.
