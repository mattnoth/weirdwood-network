## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) recalls that Big Ben courted her when [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)'s army was encamped beneath [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden).(wiki:Ben_Bushy.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He sent his squire to clean her mail and gifted her with a [silver](wiki:Silver) drinking horn. Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) later discovered that there was a wager on who could take Brienne's [maidenhead](wiki:Maiden), a wager started by Big Ben, Ser [Hyle Hunt](wiki:Hyle_Hunt) and Ser [Edmund Ambrose](wiki:Edmund_Ambrose).(wiki:Ben_Bushy.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) After the bet was discovered and ended, Brienne sought out and defeated Big Ben during the [melee at Bitterbridge](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge).(wiki:Ben_Bushy.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Hyle informs Brienne that Big Ben was killed at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). He does not mention which side Ben fought for.(wiki:Ben_Bushy.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
