## Origins

During the [storming of the Dragonpit](wiki:Storming_of_the_Dragonpit) in [130 AC](wiki:130_AC), Prince [Joffrey Velaryon](wiki:Joffrey_Velaryon) attempted to save the [dragons](wiki:Dragon) in the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit) from the crazed mob, by flying there from the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) on his mother's dragon, [Syrax](wiki:Syrax). Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) sent the [Seven Who Rode](wiki:Seven_Who_Rode), including Ser Willam, to rescue her son and heir. Willam and the other six [knights](wiki:Knight) faced many perils as they fought their way across [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), while [the city burned in riot](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)).

Unfortunately, Prince Joffrey fell from Syrax and died from the impact, and the mob began tearing apart his remains. While trying to recover Joffrey's body, three of the Seven died fighting the mob. Willam Royce was killed when a man leaped down from a rooftop to land on his back.
