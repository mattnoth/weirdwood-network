## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Walda weds [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton),(wiki:Walda_Frey_(daughter_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Lord of the Dreadfort](wiki:Lord_of_the_Dreadfort), in [a ceremony](wiki:Wedding_of_Roose_Bolton_and_Walda_Frey) at the [Twins](wiki:Twins).(wiki:Walda_Frey_(daughter_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) While Roose holds [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), Walda frequently sends letters to him which he reads and then burns without reply. Roose allows his [cupbearer](wiki:Cupbearer) "Nan", actually an incognito [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), to read him the letters.(wiki:Walda_Frey_(daughter_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Roose reveals to Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) that [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey), [Lord of the Crossing](wiki:Lord_of_the_Crossing), had offered Roose his prospective bride's weight in [silver](wiki:Currency) for her [dowry](wiki:Dowry). Lord Bolton chose the fattest available female member of [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey).(wiki:Walda_Frey_(daughter_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

During the feast at the [Twins](wiki:Twins) after the [wedding](wiki:Wedding) of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) and [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey), Walda tells Ser [Wendel Manderly](wiki:Wendel_Manderly) she is glad to have been chosen by Roose. She leaves the hall during the [bedding custom](wiki:Bedding) and is not present for the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) massacre.(wiki:Walda_Frey_(daughter_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

In the aftermath of the Red Wedding, Walda's father [Merrett](wiki:Merrett_Frey) is hanged by the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) at [Oldstones](wiki:Oldstones).(wiki:Walda_Frey_(daughter_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Walda travels north with her husband along with his army and her kin, Ser [Aenys](wiki:Aenys_Frey) and Ser [Hosteen Frey](wiki:Hosteen_Frey). She meets her stepson, [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), and [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy), after the [siege of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin) is resolved.(wiki:Walda_Frey_(daughter_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) At [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton), Roose tells Theon he expects Ramsay will kill any sons birthed by Walda.(wiki:Walda_Frey_(daughter_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Walda is present at [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Ramsay_Bolton_and_Arya_Stark) of Ramsay and "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)", actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole), in the [godswood of Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell). During the wedding feast in the [Great Hall of Winterfell](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell), she has three slices of [pork pie](wiki:Frey_Pies/Theories).(wiki:Walda_Frey_(daughter_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

As [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) becomes snowbound by a blizzard, Theon notices that Walda is pregnant. Her younger brother, [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)), is found murdered at Winterfell.(wiki:Walda_Frey_(daughter_of_Merrett).cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
