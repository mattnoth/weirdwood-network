## Origins

### During the reign of Aegon IV

Quentyn was the [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms) of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) during King [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen)'s days. In that office, he taught the sons of Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen) ([Baelor](wiki:Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)), [Aerys](wiki:Aerys_I_Targaryen), [Rhaegel](wiki:Rhaegel_Targaryen) and [Maekar](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen)) as well as the [Great Bastards](wiki:Great_Bastards) ([Daemon Waters](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre), [Aegor Rivers](wiki:Aegor_Rivers) and [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers)) how to fight.(wiki:Quentyn_Ball.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) At some point, Aegon promised Quentyn a place in the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) once an opening were to become apparent. In order to facilitate this Quentyn made his wife join the [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters). Aegon died before an opening in the Sworn Brotherhood became vacant, however.(wiki:Quentyn_Ball.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_ii_targaryen.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### First Blackfyre Rebellion

When a place in the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) became available under King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen), son and heir of Aegon IV, Quentyn reminded him of his father's promise. Fireball was refused the white cloak for reasons unknown, and it was instead given to Ser [Willem Wylde](wiki:Willem_Wylde). This betrayal caused Quentyn to choose [Daemon Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre)'s side during Daemon's rebellion. It was rumored that Fireball had as much a part as [Bittersteel](wiki:Aegor_Rivers) in convincing Daemon to claim the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Quentyn_Ball.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_ii_targaryen.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

It was Fireball who rescued Daemon when King Daeron sent the Kingsguard to arrest him. During the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), Quentyn killed Lord [Lefford](wiki:House_Lefford) at [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) and sent Lord [Damon Lannister](wiki:Damon_Lannister_(lord)) back to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) when he came out to confront him. At the crossing of the [Mander](wiki:Mander) he slew all the sons of Lady [Penrose](wiki:House_Penrose) save for the youngest, whom he spared as a kindness to her. Quentyn was slain by a common archer on the eve of the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field) when he stopped for a drink of water.(wiki:Quentyn_Ball.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Quentyn_Ball.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

A young boy named [Glendon Flowers](wiki:Glendon_Flowers) believed himself to be Quentyn's [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son.(wiki:Quentyn_Ball.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)

## Appearances & Description

Quentyn was red-haired, with a fiery temper, hence the moniker *Fireball*.(wiki:Quentyn_Ball.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) He was considered to be one of the greatest [knights](wiki:Knight) of his day.(wiki:Quentyn_Ball.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
