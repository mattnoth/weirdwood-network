## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Meralyn buys a dozen oysters from Cat ([Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)) whenever Cat passes by the [Happy Port](wiki:Happy_Port), and she shares them with her girls. Merry allows [Dareon](wiki:Dareon) to sing to her girls.(wiki:Meralyn.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
When [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) visits the Happy Port in search of Dareon, Merry is turning [tiles](wiki:Tiles) with a massive [Summer Islander](wiki:Summer_Isles), [Xhondo Dhoru](wiki:Xhondo_Dhoru). The proprietor has Sam tossed into the nearby canal after he punches the singer.(wiki:Meralyn.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Now serving the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White) as a blind girl, Arya fondly recalls Merry and the Happy Port.(wiki:Meralyn.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
