## Origins

Lord [Gawen Westerling](wiki:Gawen_Westerling) once proposed that his daughter [Jeyne](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling) wed Martyn or his brother, [Willem Lannister](wiki:Willem_Lannister), but the twins' father, Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), refused because of [House Westerling](wiki:House_Westerling)'s connection with [House Spicer](wiki:House_Spicer).(wiki:Martyn_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Martyn is a part of the army gathered by Ser [Stafford Lannister](wiki:Stafford_Lannister) at [Oxcross](wiki:Oxcross). He is among those captured when [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), and his army take the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) force by surprise in the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross).(wiki:Martyn_Lannister.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) ponders having one of his twins, [Willem](wiki:Willem_Lannister) and Martyn, or their cousin, [Tion Frey](wiki:Tion_Frey), marry [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), but all three boys are held captive at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Martyn_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

After the murders of Willem and Tion at the hands of Lord [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark),(wiki:Martyn_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Robb fears for Martyn's survival. Guilt-ridden over Rickard's actions, Robb triples the guard around Martyn and arranges for him to be exchanged for [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover).(wiki:Martyn_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

In exchange for the freeing of Robett at [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale), Robb has Ser [Rolph Spicer](wiki:Rolph_Spicer) deliver Martyn to Lannister forces at the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth).(wiki:Martyn_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Feast for Crows

While speaking about Lord [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister)'s decision to leave his wife, [Amerei Frey](wiki:Amerei_Frey), and [Castle Darry](wiki:Darry) in order to swear service as part of the newly reformed [Faith Militant](wiki:Faith_Militant), [Genna Lannister](wiki:Genna_Lannister) proposes to Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) the idea of Martyn, Lancel's younger brother, marrying Amerei to secure Darry for House Lannister.(wiki:Martyn_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
