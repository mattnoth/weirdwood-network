## Identity

Martyn Lannister is a squire from House Lannister, the third son of Ser Kevan Lannister and Lady Dorna Swyft, and twin to Willem Lannister (wiki:Martyn_Lannister).

## Origins

Born 285–287 AC in the Westerlands (track_b: Born). Father: Kevan Lannister (track_b: Father). Mother: Dorna Swyft (track_b: Mother).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Lannister (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Westermen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Lord Gawen Westerling once proposed that his daughter Jeyne wed Martyn or Willem, but Kevan refused because of House Westerling's connection with House Spicer (wiki:Martyn_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33). Martyn is captured when Robb Stark's army surprises the Lannister force at the Battle of Oxcross (wiki:Martyn_Lannister.cite_ref-Racok32). Kevan ponders having Martyn, Willem, or their cousin Tion Frey marry Sansa Stark, but all three are captive at Riverrun (wiki:Martyn_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos19). After Lord Rickard Karstark murders Willem and Tion Frey, Martyn is moved to a safer location. Robb Stark agrees to exchange Martyn for Robett Glover (wiki:Martyn_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos35). Martyn eventually returns home, and Jaime later considers using him in marriage alliances (wiki:Martyn_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33).
