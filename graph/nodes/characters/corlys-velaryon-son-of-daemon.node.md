## Appearances & Description

Like his fellow knights of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), Ser Corlys Velaryon is remembered for being quick and strong in battle, and an observant warrior. He was famed for his prowess with [sword](wiki:Sword) and shield.
