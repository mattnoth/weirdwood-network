### A Feast for Crows

Tywin is first in line to inherit [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) after it is granted to Lord Emmon, the head of the new [House Frey of Riverrun](wiki:House_Frey_of_Riverrun).(wiki:Tywin_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Tywin_Frey.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

During the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and Lady [Genna Lannister](wiki:Genna_Lannister) discuss her family's new fragile status as [Lord of Riverrun](wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun). Genna tells him her husband will make a wretched lord but she is hopeful Tywin will make a good lord someday as long as he listens to her advice. To appease his aunt's mind, Jaime proposes that if [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) and [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey)'s unborn child turns out to be a girl, they should marry her to Tywin to strengthen their hold on Riverrun.(wiki:Tywin_Frey.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
