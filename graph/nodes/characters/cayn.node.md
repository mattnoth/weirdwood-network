## Identity

Cayn is a guard sworn to House Stark who accompanied Lord Eddard Stark to King's Landing (wiki:Cayn). He has a son, Calon (track_b: Issue).

## Origins

A Northman in service to House Stark (track_b: Allegiance, Culture). Father of Calon (track_b: Issue).

## Allegiances

Cayn is `SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance) and belongs to `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Cayn accompanies Lord Eddard Stark to King's Landing as part of his household guard. He is one of the few northern guardsmen who remain with Ned after the death of Jory Cassel and the departure of Alyn (wiki:Cayn). Cayn helps Ned dress and carries a taper when summoned to Robert's deathbed; he and Desmond are sent to fetch Littlefinger (agot-eddard-13). After the death of King Robert I Baratheon, Cayn is slain by Sandor Clegane during the arrest of Eddard in the throne room — Clegane cuts off his sword hand, then opens his throat (wiki:Cayn, agot-eddard-14).
