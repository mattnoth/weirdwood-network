## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

As a [guard of House Stark](wiki:House_Stark_guards), Cayn accompanies Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). He is one of the few northern guardsmen who remain with Ned after the death of [Jory Cassel](wiki:Jory_Cassel) and the departure of [Alyn](wiki:Alyn).(wiki:Cayn.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

After the death of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), Cayn is slain by [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) during the [arrest of Eddard](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark).(wiki:Cayn.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Theon has a nightmare of eating with the dead at Winterfell, including Cayn.(wiki:Cayn.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Theon recalls Cayn among the dead of Winterfell.(wiki:Cayn.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
