## Origins

Documents of the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort) indicate that the Warg King ruled [Sea Dragon Point](wiki:Sea_Dragon_Point). He was allied with the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest), but they were defeated by the [Kings of Winter](wiki:Kings_of_Winter) of [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark).
