## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Germund's brother [Sawane](wiki:Sawane_Botley), Lord of [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport), is drowned for defying [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy). Instead of Sawane's heir, [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley), Germund is awarded half of [House Botley](wiki:House_Botley)'s lands in return for swearing allegiance to Euron,(wiki:Germund_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) with the other half granted to [House Wynch](wiki:House_Wynch). Lord Germund is listed as one of Euron's champions at the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot).(wiki:Germund_Botley.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
