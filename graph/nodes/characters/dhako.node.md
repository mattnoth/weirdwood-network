## Origins

When Dhako approached the city of [Ibbish](wiki:Vaes_Aresak) to sack it, its inhabitants took to their ships and fled to [Ib](wiki:Ib). Dhako put the city to the torch and named it Vaes Aresak, or City of Cowards. In his wroth, Dhako burned not only the abandoned city, but also much of the surrounding countryside. Thereafter he was known as *the Dragon of the North*.(wiki:Dhako.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Dhako.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

The [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) adventurer [Terrio Erastes](wiki:Terrio_Erastes) was a guest of Khal Dhako when Ibbish was destroyed. He wrote in his chronicle *[Fire Upon the Grass](wiki:Fire_Upon_the_Grass)* that Dhako took great pride in being known as the Dragon of the North, but came to rue it when his *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* was defeated in battle by that of Khal [Temmo](wiki:Temmo), who took the elder Dhako captive and fed him to the flames after cutting off his hands, feet, and [genitals](wiki:Eunuch), and roasting them before his eyes, after first burning his wives and sons in the same manner.(wiki:Dhako.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
