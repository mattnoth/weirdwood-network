## Origins

Delena was a [bedmaid](wiki:Bedmaid) for her cousin, [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent), and attended [her wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Stannis_Baratheon_and_Selyse_Florent) to Lord [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon). During the wedding feast, while the bride and groom were still dancing, Stannis's brother, King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), carried Delena upstairs to the bedroom of the newlyweds and broke in the wedding bed.(wiki:Delena_Florent.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Robert later told Stannis he had not recognized the bedchamber because he was drunk, and had not meant to cause shame.(wiki:Delena_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) However, Stannis felt his brother's actions were an insult to [House Florent](wiki:House_Florent).(wiki:Delena_Florent.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

The coupling eventually led to the birth of [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm). Because Delena had been a [maiden](wiki:Maiden) of noble birth when Robert slept with her, he had to acknowledge the child as his [bastard](wiki:Bastard).(wiki:Delena_Florent.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)(wiki:Delena_Florent.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Stannis had the infant sent to [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) to be [fostered](wiki:Fostered) by his younger brother, Lord [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon).(wiki:Delena_Florent.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) once offered to betroth his son [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to Delena, but her father, Ser [Colin Florent](wiki:Colin_Florent), refused, and instead married her to one of his [household knights](wiki:Household_knight), Ser [Hosman Norcross](wiki:Hosman_Norcross).(wiki:Delena_Florent.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Appearances & Description

Since her bastard son, [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm), has the large ears common to the [Florents](wiki:House_Florent),(wiki:Delena_Florent.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Delena presumably shares this physical characteristic.
