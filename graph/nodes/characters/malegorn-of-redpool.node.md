## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Malegorn is one of the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) in the service of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Malegorn_of_Redpool.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) When Stannis rides to [Castle Black](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall) and then [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Retaking_of_Deepwood_Motte), Malegorn stays with Queen [Selyse](wiki:Selyse_Florent) at [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea) as one of her protectors. When Selyse leaves Eastwatch to go to the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort), he accompanies her to Castle Black. Malegorn and Ser [Brus Buckler](wiki:Brus_Buckler) tell the brothers of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) about events at Eastwatch.(wiki:Malegorn_of_Redpool.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

During the [wedding of Sigorn and Alys Karstark](wiki:Wedding_of_Sigorn_and_Alys_Karstark), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) sees Malegorn cupping the arse of the woman next to him.(wiki:Malegorn_of_Redpool.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Afterward, Jon, the [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), orders his steward [Satin](wiki:Satin) to escort Selyse to the feast. Malegorn interrupts and escorts the queen instead, as he dislikes Satin, a former [prostitute](wiki:Prostitute).(wiki:Malegorn_of_Redpool.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Malegorn thinks that Jon's plan for a ranging to [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome) is foolish. Selyse arranges for Malegorn to wed the [youngest daughter](wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood's_youngest_daughter) of [Gerrick Kingsblood](wiki:Gerrick_Kingsblood).(wiki:Malegorn_of_Redpool.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
