## Origins

Chett was born around [Hag's Mire](wiki:Hag's_Mire). His father collected [leeches](wiki:Leech) and sold them to [maesters](wiki:Maester), and Chett learned the trade from his father. He was an ugly boy and so girls did not take interest in him. Chett picked flowers for a girl named [Bessa](wiki:Bessa_(Hag's_Mire)) who had slept with every boy in their village, but she spurned and insulted him. Chett became so angry that he killed her with his knife. After being caught near [Sevenstreams](wiki:Sevenstreams), Chett was judged by Ser [Walder Rivers](wiki:Walder_Rivers), one of Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey)'s [bastards](wiki:Bastardy), and sent to the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Chett.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Chett has been a [steward](wiki:Steward) assisting Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) for four years.(wiki:Chett.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) The only women he has slept with have been [prostitutes](wiki:Prostitutes) from [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town_brothel).(wiki:Chett.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

At the [Wall](wiki:Wall), Chett is a [steward](wiki:Steward) whose job is to care for the ravens and old Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)), with [Clydas](wiki:Clydas). This was a rather comfortable position, but it is taken away from him by [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), who advises Aemon that the job is better suited for the literate [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly).(wiki:Chett.cite_ref-Ragot41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) After that, Chett is assigned to the kennels(wiki:Chett.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and aids the kennelmaster, [Bass](wiki:Bass).(wiki:Chett.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Chett takes care of the dogs on the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) of the Watch [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall). He and [Lark the Sisterman](wiki:Lark) come upon Jon when he is talking to [Gilly](wiki:Gilly), one of [Craster](wiki:Craster)'s wives.(wiki:Chett.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Jon later overhears Chett and Lark talking about mutiny.(wiki:Chett.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Morale is low during the great ranging. Fourteen(wiki:Chett.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) brothers conspire to desert, including the ringleader Chett, [Dirk](wiki:Dirk), [Softfoot](wiki:Softfoot), [Ollo Lophand](wiki:Ollo_Lophand), [Sweet Donnel Hill](wiki:Donnel_Hill), Lark the Sisterman, [Rolley of Sisterton](wiki:Rolley_of_Sisterton), [Clubfoot Karl](wiki:Clubfoot_Karl), [Maslyn](wiki:Maslyn), [Small Paul](wiki:Small_Paul), and [Sawwood](wiki:Sawwood). To make sure they will not be caught, they plan to kill some of their Night's Watch brothers:

- Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), to allow command to go to Ser [Ottyn Wythers](wiki:Ottyn_Wythers), whom they expect to withdraw to the Wall instead of going after the deserters.
- [Blane](wiki:Blane), a stern officer of the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower).
- [Grubbs](wiki:Grubbs) and [Aethan](wiki:Aethan), the men who have drawn watch.
- [Dywen](wiki:Dywen) and [Bannen](wiki:Bannen), to prevent the deserters from being tracked.
- [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), to prevent a raven with the message of what they had done from reaching the Wall.

Chett intends to murder Samwell himself. After the killings he plans to set free his dogs, which would frighten the horses, running them off. Chett has not fed the dogs for a few days so they will be extra fierce. In the confusion that would follow, the deserters could flee with the remaining horses and the food they had hoarded.(wiki:Chett.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

Unfortunately for the conspirators, it begins to snow a few hours before they intend to set their plan into motion. Chett realizes that in fresh snow they would be easily followed and would not make good time, so he cancels the plan. Regardless, he decides to kill Samwell out of spite. However, at the moment Chett finds Sam they hear three horn blows, signalling that [Others](wiki:Others) have been sighted.(wiki:Chett.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

When [wights](wiki:Wights) attack the Night's Watch, beginning the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist), a trusting Samwell asks Chett for help in sending off [ravens](wiki:Raven), but the kennel keeper runs away.(wiki:Chett.cite_ref-Rasos18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Chett is killed during the battle, as Sam later encounters him as a wight in a [wildling](wiki:Wildling) village, possibly [Whitetree](wiki:Whitetree). A flock of ravens attacks the undead Chett and other wights, allowing Samwell to be rescued by [Coldhands](wiki:Coldhands).(wiki:Chett.cite_ref-Rasos46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
