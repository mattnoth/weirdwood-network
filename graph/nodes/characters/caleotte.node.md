## Origins

Caleotte did not have [redspots](wiki:Redspots) when he was a child.(wiki:Caleotte.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

The maester has been serving [House Martell](wiki:House_Martell) since [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell)'s [mother](wiki:Princess_of_Dorne_(mother_of_Doran)) ruled as [Princess of Dorne](wiki:Princess_of_Dorne).(wiki:Caleotte.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

Caleotte designed a rolling chair with goose-down cushions and wheels of [ebony](wiki:Ebony) and iron for Doran.(wiki:Caleotte.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

## Appearances & Description

Maester Caleotte is five feet tall, bald, and has a smooth face. He is fat(wiki:Caleotte.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and has soft pink hands.(wiki:Caleotte.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The maester wears slippers on his feet.(wiki:Caleotte.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Caleotte is clever but meek.(wiki:Caleotte.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

After a letter arrives to announce the death of Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Maester Caleotte gives it to [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah) without breaking its seal. Areo then relays it to Oberyn's brother [Doran](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne).(wiki:Caleotte.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

Caleotte rides a donkey when Doran returns to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) from the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens). The maester looks at Doran's hands after he meets with [Tyene Sand](wiki:Tyene_Sand) to make sure she did not [poison](wiki:Poison) the prince. Doran asks Caleotte for a thimble cup of [milk of the poppy](wiki:Milk_of_the_poppy).(wiki:Caleotte.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) disguises [Rosamund Lannister](wiki:Rosamund_Lannister) as Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon) and tells Caleotte that Myrcella has come down with [redspots](wiki:Redspots). Arianne flees Sunspear with Myrcella in an attempt to crown the girl.(wiki:Caleotte.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

After Myrcella is wounded by Ser [Gerold Dayne](wiki:Gerold_Dayne) at the [Greenblood](wiki:Greenblood), Caleotte saves her life.(wiki:Caleotte.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Caleotte receives new robes with broad bands of dun and butternut and narrow stripes of red.(wiki:Caleotte.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

When Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) presents the head of Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) to Doran and his court at Sunspear, Caleotte opens the box's latch and removes the [Mountain's skull](wiki:Mountain's_skull). He afterwards escorts Doran, Arianne, and [Obara](wiki:Obara_Sand), [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria_Sand), and Tyene Sand to Doran's [solar](wiki:Solar), taking the skull with him. Caleotte leaves the solar before Doran discusses his plans with his daughter and nieces.(wiki:Caleotte.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)
