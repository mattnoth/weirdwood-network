## Identity

Alyssa Targaryen — Fifth child of Jaehaerys and Alysanne, born late 60 AC on Dragonstone and named for the queen's late mother. As a babe she resembled Daenerys but grew long-faced and skinny with mismatched eyes.

## Appearances & Description

As a baby, Alyssa resembled her deceased sister [Daenerys](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)), but this resemblance faded as Alyssa aged. She became long-faced and skinny. Alyssa did not have typical [Valyrian](wiki:Valyrians) silver-gold hair, but instead dirty blonde hair without a trace of silver. It was often tangled. She had mismatched eyes, [one violet](wiki:Purple_eyes), the other green. She had big ears and a lopsided smile. At the age of six she broke her nose, which healed crooked, but she apparently did not care.(wiki:Alyssa_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
In her character, Alyssa took after her brother [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)). She had a warrior's heart, according to [Archmaester](wiki:Archmaester) [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn). As a child, she did not act like a girl, and shunned the company of girls. She would dress in boy's clothes whenever possible, and preferred to ride, climb, and duel with wooden swords, instead of more lady-like activities like sewing and reading and singing. As a child, she refused to eat porridge.(wiki:Alyssa_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
Alyssa was strong, quick, and spirited. She was not shy, and loved to boast that she was "as bawdy a wench as any barmaid in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing)".(wiki:Alyssa_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

## Origins

### Early life[ ]

Princess Alyssa was born in late [60 AC](wiki:60_AC) to King [Jaehaerys I](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen). She was [named for](wiki:Named_after) her grandmother, [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon),(wiki:Alyssa_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7) who had died six years before.(wiki:Alyssa_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfab9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
As a young child, Alyssa followed her brother [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) everywhere since she was able to walk. Baelon, four years older, complained that she was like a puppy, and was further annoyed that she was a girl, as he preferred the company of his older brother [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)). Soon after Alyssa's sister [Maegelle](wiki:Maegelle_Targaryen)'s birth in [62 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), she began to cling to Alyssa the same way as Alyssa had to Baelon, to Alyssa's annoyance and Baelon's amusement. Alyssa avoided her younger sister as well she could. When Alyssa was six years old, she was playing in the training yard when she was accidentally hit in the face by a wooden [sword](wiki:Sword), which broke her nose. It healed crooked, but this did not seem to bother her.(wiki:Alyssa_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)
When marriage options were being considered for Jaehaerys and Alysanne's eldest son, Prince Aemon, some thought he should marry Alyssa, as she was his eldest living sister following the death of Princess [Daenerys](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)). However, Queen Alysanne Targaryen firmly refused, saying that Alyssa was meant for Baelon, and Aemon instead married [Jocelyn Baratheon](wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon) in [70 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). In [72 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Alyssa attended a [tourney at Duskendale](wiki:Tourney_at_Duskendale_in_honor_of_Lord_Darklyn's_wedding), held in honor of the wedding of Lord [Darklyn](wiki:House_Darklyn) to a daughter of Lord [Theomore Manderly](wiki:Theomore_Manderly).(wiki:Alyssa_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)
Alyssa and Baelon finally became inseparable, and by [73 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) plans for their eventual marriage were already being made. That same year, when her brother [Vaegon](wiki:Vaegon_Targaryen) severely insulted their sister [Daella](wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)), Alyssa poured a flagon of [wine](wiki:Wine) over his head. Over the following year, Baelon began training Vaegon at arms; he once brought Alyssa to the training yard, dressed in a man's mail, hoping that having to face a girl would lead to Vaegon making more of an effort. Alyssa, still remembering the incident between Vaegon and Daella, mocked and laughed at Vaegon as she danced around him, humiliating him, whilst Daella watched. Vaegon, embarrassed too much to bear, threw down his sword, left the yard, and never came back.(wiki:Alyssa_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)

### Marriage[ ]

Alyssa married Baelon in [75 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), when she was fifteen and he was eighteen. Their [bedding](wiki:Bedding) sparked much ribald humor in the days after the wedding, as it was said Alyssa's "sounds of pleasure could be heard all the way to [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale)." Alyssa was not embarrassed by the jokes, and made some of her own. That same year, Alyssa decided to claim a [dragon](wiki:Dragon), a year younger than Baelon had done so. The [Dragonkeepers](wiki:Dragonkeepers) persuaded her not to claim [Balerion](wiki:Balerion), assuring her that she would prefer a faster dragon than the old Black Dread. Eventually, Alyssa claimed [Meleys](wiki:Meleys), who had never been ridden before.(wiki:Alyssa_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-11)
When not flying on their dragons, Alyssa and Baelon spent every hour together, most often in their bedchamber. In [77 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Alyssa gave birth to her first child, a son they named [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen). Although Alyssa was advised not to do it, when Viserys was nine days old she put him in swaddling clothes and took him for a flight on Meleys. Afterwards, she claimed Viserys giggled the entire time. In [81 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Alyssa gave birth to her second son, [Daemon](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen). Within a fortnight of his birth, she took him up in the sky upon her dragon as well.(wiki:Alyssa_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-12)
In [83 AC](wiki:83_AC), Alyssa was announced to be pregnant again, and Baelon told his brother Aemon he was praying for a daughter. However, in [84 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) Alyssa gave birth to another son, [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Baelon)), after a long and difficult labor. Though Alyssa was optimistic about having more children, she never fully recovered from Aegon's birth. She died within a year, at the age of twenty-four. Aegon died half a year after his mother, not yet even a year old. Alyssa's death shattered Baelon, who never remarried and always continued to honor her memory.(wiki:Alyssa_Targaryen.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-13)
