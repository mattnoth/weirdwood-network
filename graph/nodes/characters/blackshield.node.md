## Origins

Blackshield participated as a mystery knight at a [tourney](wiki:History_of_Tourneys_in_Westeros) held at [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) sometime between [278 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) and [281 AC](wiki:281_AC). He was unhorsed by Ser [Barristan the Bold](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), who unmasked him and revealed him as the **Bastard of Uplands**.(wiki:Blackshield.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

Blackshield's given name is unknown. His surname is presumably "Flowers", the [bastard](wiki:Bastard) name of the [Reach](wiki:Reach), as that is where [House Mullendore](wiki:House_Mullendore)'s seat of [Uplands](wiki:Uplands) is located.
