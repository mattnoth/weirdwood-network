## Origins

In [209 AC](wiki:209_AC), [Lord Ashford](wiki:Lord_Ashford) hosted the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) to celebrate his daughter's thirteenth [name day](wiki:Name_day). Five champions wore a wisp of orange silk as her favor and defended her position as the reigning [Queen of Love and Beauty](wiki:Queen_of_Love_and_Beauty); these included her brothers Ser [Androw](wiki:Androw_Ashford) and Ser [Robert Ashford](wiki:Robert_Ashford), Lord [Leo Tyrell](wiki:Leo_Tyrell_(Longthorn)), Ser [Humfrey Hardyng](wiki:Humfrey_Hardyng), and Prince [Valarr Targaryen](wiki:Valarr_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord_Ashford's_daughter.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
Lord Ashford and his daughter watched the tourney from a viewing stand alongside princes of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen). During the [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven), mud landed on Lord Ashford and the fair maid when Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) shook the defeated Prince [Aerion Targaryen](wiki:Aerion_Targaryen) before the viewing stand.(wiki:Lord_Ashford's_daughter.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
