## Identity

Brandon Stark (262–282 AC) — child of Rickard Stark and Lyarra Stark; sworn to House Stark. One of 15 characters named Brandon Stark.

## Origins

### Early Life

Born in [262 AC](wiki:262_AC)(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell),(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Rawoiafbrandon_stark.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) Brandon was the eldest son and heir of Lord [Rickard](wiki:Rickard_Stark) and Lady [Lyarra Stark](wiki:Lyarra_Stark).(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_stark_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Brandon was [fostered](wiki:Fostered) at [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) with Lord [Dustin](wiki:House_Dustin), father of [Willam Dustin](wiki:Willam_Dustin), and he spent most his time riding in the [Rills](wiki:Rills).(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-4) He took the [maidenhead](wiki:Maiden) of [Barbrey Ryswell](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin), who was infatuated with him, and her father [Rodrik](wiki:Rodrik_Ryswell), [Lord of the Rills](wiki:Lord_of_the_Rills), desired to wed her to the heir of Winterfell.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-5) However, Lord Stark eventually [betrothed](wiki:Betrothed) Brandon to [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-6)(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Brandon swore to Barbrey he did not want to marry Catelyn, but Rickard's ambitions did not allow him to marry the daughter of a northern [bannerman](wiki:Bannerman).(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-7)

In [281 AC](wiki:281_AC), Brandon attended the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal), where he was introduced by his sister, [Lyanna](wiki:Lyanna_Stark), to [Howland Reed](wiki:Howland_Reed), a [crannogman](wiki:Crannogman) from the [Neck](wiki:Neck).(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Brandon laughed during the festivities.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) At the opening feast of the tourney, Brandon asked [Ashara Dayne](wiki:Ashara_Dayne) to dance with his younger brother [Eddard](wiki:Eddard_Stark), who was too shy to ask her himself.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Brandon entered the lists, but he was unhorsed by [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone).(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) After Rhaegar won the tournament, he crowned Lyanna as his [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty), passing over his own wife, Princess [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell).(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2)(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Brandon had to be restrained from confronting Rhaegar, as he took the prince's action as a slight upon Lyanna's honor, since Lyanna was already betrothed to Eddard's friend, Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)

In [282 AC](wiki:282_AC),(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-16) Brandon was present at Riverrun when the date for his wedding to Catelyn was announced.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Rawoiafpetyr_baelish.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), the [ward](wiki:Ward) of Catelyn's father, Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), challenged Brandon to a duel for her hand. Catelyn begged Brandon to spare Petyr's life, and Brandon promised her to do so.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Catelyn gave Brandon her favor to wear during the duel.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Brandon [armored](wiki:Armor) himself for battle, aided by Catelyn's young brother, [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully), who served as Brandon's [squire](wiki:Squire). When Brandon saw that the smaller Petyr wore only a helm, a breastplate, and mail, he took off most of his own armor. Brandon was more than a match for the much younger Petyr. He offered the boy several chances to yield during their duel in the castle's lower bailey, but when Petyr kept refusing, Brandon ended the duel by injuring Petyr severely.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Afterwards he departed Riverrun, promising Catelyn to return soon so they could wed.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### Death

From [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), Brandon left to join his father [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark)'s wedding party, coming down from the [north](wiki:North).(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Rawoiafcatelyn_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) They were on their way back to Riverrun when word reached Brandon of Lyanna's abduction by Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen). Brandon, along with his squire, [Ethan Glover](wiki:Ethan_Glover), and companions [Kyle Royce](wiki:Kyle_Royce), [Elbert Arryn](wiki:Elbert_Arryn), and [Jeffory Mallister](wiki:Jeffory_Mallister), rode to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) immediately.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) Upon entering the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), Brandon shouted for Rhaegar to "come out and die". Rhaegar was not present, however, and Brandon and his companions were arrested by King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) and charged with plotting Rhaegar's murder. Their fathers were called to court to answer the charges.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2)(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

Rickard demanded a [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat), which King Aerys granted, but he declared that fire was the champion of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen). Rickard was suspended from the rafters of the [throne room](wiki:Great_Hall_(Red_Keep)), above a fire lit by [pyromancers](wiki:Pyromancers). Brandon's hands were chained behind his back, and a leather cord was wrapped around his neck, attached to a [Tyroshi](wiki:Tyrosh) device that would tighten the cord as it was pulled on. However, his legs were free, and his sword was placed just beyond his reach. Brandon was forced to watch as his father was burned alive in his own armor, and he strangled himself attempting to get to the sword so he could save his father. He was twenty years old at the time.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-3) With the exception of Ethan, Brandon's companions and their fathers were also killed by the Mad King.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-4)

After executing Lord Rickard and Brandon, King Aerys sent word to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) for Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) to behead his former wards, [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) and [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon). Jon's refusal began [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) Instead of Brandon, [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) wed Eddard at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), with Lord Arryn marrying Catelyn's sister [Lysa](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) at the same time.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1) [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) was overthrown during the war, and Lyanna and Ethan died [in the south](wiki:Tower_of_joy).(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

### Legacy

Brandon was buried in the [crypts beneath Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell), beside his father.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-1) Though only the [kings](wiki:King_in_the_North) and lords of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) normally have statues in their likeness placed beside their tombs, both Brandon and his sister [Lyanna](wiki:Lyanna_Stark) were given statues by their brother, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), as he had loved them very much.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-2)(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

Eddard named his second trueborn son [Brandon](wiki:Bran_Stark) (affectionately known as "Bran") in his elder brother's memory.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)

## Appearances & Description

The grey-eyed(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Brandon was taller than his younger brother [Eddard](wiki:Eddard_Stark),(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Rawoiafbrandon_stark.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) and [Catelyn Tully](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) found Brandon more handsome than Eddard.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) He was also wilder and more mirthful than the somber Eddard.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) Brandon was rather hot-blooded,(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) a trait his father, Lord [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark), described as originating from "the [wolf blood](wiki:Wolf_blood)". Brandon shared this trait with his sister, [Lyanna](wiki:Lyanna_Stark), and Eddard believes this led them both to early deaths.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

Brandon was a better [swordsman](wiki:Sword) and [jouster](wiki:Joust) than his younger brothers, Eddard and [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark).(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Rawoiafbrandon_stark.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Rssm1142.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) He loved to hone his sword, preferring it to be as sharp as possible.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) He also loved to ride, and has been described by [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin) as a [centaur](wiki:Centaur) in that regard.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully) considers Brandon to have been a gallant fool.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Brandon was not shy about taking what he wanted, including women.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2) Lady [Barbrey Ryswell](wiki:Barbrey_Ryswell), one of Brandon's lovers, believes he enjoyed taking her [virginity](wiki:Maiden).(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-3) When asked if Brandon had any issue, [George R. R. Martin](wiki:George_R._R._Martin) responded:

> It'd be an exaggeration to say that Brandon died before he could have children. It's established in the books that he was no virgin. He could very well have left behind some little [Snows](wiki:Bastard) in the various places he visited. But what's absolutely clear is that he had no legitimate children.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Rssm6539.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) and King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) see the tombs of Brandon, Lord [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark), and [Lyanna](wiki:Lyanna_Stark) in the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-3)

### A Clash of Kings

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) has a nightmare involving a feast of the dead at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). Many of the dead men and women attending are people that Theon knew personally or were slain by his own hand and/or command. Yet he also notices faces that he had never known in life. Among them is Brandon, standing with his father and sister.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

When Bran, [Rickon](wiki:Rickon_Stark), [Osha](wiki:Osha), [Hodor](wiki:Hodor), and [Meera](wiki:Meera_Reed) and [Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed) escape the crypts after the [Sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell), Bran takes with him the sword of Brandon's statue, from the crypts. As he does so, he notes the relevance of using the [steel](wiki:Steel) of his [namesake](wiki:Namesake).(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-1)

When [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) visits Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s cell in [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), he tells her the tale of Brandon's death after he notices she does not know the details. Catelyn calls the story so hideous that it must be true.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-5)

### A Dance with Dragons

Theon and Lady [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin) discuss Brandon while in the the crypts of Winterfell. Reek notices that Brandon's sword is missing, prompting Barbrey to state that Brandon would not be happy.(wiki:Brandon_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-8)
