## Identity

Brandon Stark was the oldest son of Lord Rickard Stark and Lady Lyarra Stark and the heir to Winterfell prior to Robert's Rebellion. His younger siblings were Eddard, Lyanna, and Benjen (wiki:Brandon_Stark).

## Origins

Born 262 AC at Winterfell. Died 282 AC in the Great Hall of King's Landing. Son of Rickard Stark and Lyarra Stark (track_b: Father, track_b: Mother). Buried in the Crypt of Winterfell (track_b: Buried).

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Grey-eyed, taller than Eddard, and Catelyn Tully found him more handsome. Wilder and more mirthful than the somber Eddard. Hot-blooded — a trait Rickard described as "the wolf blood," shared with Lyanna. A better swordsman and jouster than Eddard and Benjen. Loved to ride and was described by Barbrey Dustin as a centaur. Not shy about taking what he wanted, including women (wiki:Brandon_Stark).

## Narrative Arc

Brandon was fostered at Barrowton, where he took the virginity of Barbrey Ryswell (wiki:Brandon_Stark). He was betrothed to Catelyn Tully. When his sister Lyanna was allegedly abducted by Prince Rhaegar Targaryen, Brandon rode to King's Landing demanding Rhaegar come out and die. King Aerys II had Brandon and his companions arrested, then summoned their fathers. Lord Rickard demanded trial by combat and was burned alive in his armor by wildfire while Brandon strangled himself trying to reach a sword to free his father. Their deaths were among the key events sparking Robert's Rebellion (wiki:Brandon_Stark).
