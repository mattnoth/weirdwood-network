## Origins

### Rhaena Targaryen

Dreamfyre was hatched during the reign of King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen). As a hatchling, she bonded with nine-year old Princess Rhaena Targaryen, Aegon I's first grandchild, in [32 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Three years later, she became Dreamfyre's [rider](wiki:Dragonrider).(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) In [41 AC](wiki:41_AC) Rhaena was wed to her brother, [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)), and sent on a [royal progress](wiki:Royal_progress) by their father King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen). Rhaena wished to take Dreamfyre with her, but her father forbade it, as Aegon had not yet claimed a dragon, and the king did not want his son to be seen riding a horse, while his wife rode a dragon. Dreamfyre remained in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

Rhaena's father, King Aenys, died in [42 AC](wiki:42_AC) and the crown was usurped by their uncle, [Maegor](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen). Rhaena and her husband smuggled themselves into [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in [43 AC](wiki:43_AC), while Maegor was at [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). Rhaena was reunited with Dreamfyre and Aegon finally claimed a dragon of his own, his father's dragon [Quicksilver](wiki:Quicksilver). Knowing they could not hold the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), they left King's Landing on their dragons to raise an army. When Aegon went to war against their uncle, Rhaena and Dreamfyre did not accompany them. Aegon, along with Quicksilver, was killed in the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye) by Maegor mounted on [Balerion the Black Dread](wiki:Balerion). Since Dreamfyre was younger and smaller than [Quicksilver](wiki:Quicksilver), many historians believe that Dreamfyre would not have been much of a threat to the Black Dread.(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)

Princess Rhaena sent her twin daughters [Aerea](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen) and [Rhaella](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon)) into hiding. However, she felt that she could not hide herself, as she had Dreamfyre with her, who was not easily hidden. Therefore she flew Dreamfyre to [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle) off the coast in the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea), where they stayed at [Faircastle](wiki:Faircastle). There they remained until [47 AC](wiki:47_AC), when Maegor commanded Rhaena to return to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Not wanting to bring Maegor's wrath and Balerion's fires down on Fair Isle, Rhaena flew on Dreamfyre to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), only to find out that Maegor wished to make Rhaena his bride and that his [mistress of whisperers](wiki:Master_of_Whisperers), [Tyanna of the Tower](wiki:Tyanna_of_the_Tower), had found her daughters and used them as hostages.(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6)

The next year, Rhaena fled the Red Keep upon Dreamfyre, together with her elder daughter [Aerea](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen), and Maegor's [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) sword [Blackfyre](wiki:Blackfyre), to join her only surviving brother, [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7)

By [43 AC](wiki:43_AC), Dreamfyre had already produced two clutches of [dragon eggs](wiki:Dragon_eggs).(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8) During Rhaena's second residence at [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle) in [49](wiki:49_AC)–[50 AC](wiki:50_AC), Dreamfyre laid another clutch of eggs. Dreamfyre's presence on the island made some of the residents anxious, and the eggs caused a [begging brother](wiki:Begging_brother) to start preaching that Fair Isle "would soon be overrun by dragons" who would eat both sheep and men, and called for a [dragonslayer](wiki:Dragonslayer) to kill the [dragons](wiki:Dragon) to prevent it. The man was arrested, but nonetheless his words inspired fear wherever they were spread. Rhaena chose to depart Fair Isle, taking Dreamfyre and the eggs with her. After some time at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), where Dreamfyre had been granted an entire stable, Lord [Lyman Lannister](wiki:Lyman_Lannister) expressed his interest in the three eggs and asked how they would hatch, and when that could be expected. Lady [Jocasta Lannister](wiki:Jocasta_Lannister) suggested to Rhaena that gifting the Lannisters one or more of the three eggs would be a good way to demonstrate her gratitude. After Rhaena refused, Lord Lyman offered to buy the eggs.(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Rhaena left Casterly Rock and toured the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands), but many Western lords desired a dragon of their own, and she grew uncomfortable with how they looked at Dreamfyre or her eggs. In [51 AC](wiki:51_AC) Rhaena visited King's Landing upon Dreamfyre to attend the second wedding of her siblings King [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), and explained her difficulties. Rhaena was eventually granted [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) as her own seat.(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Dreamfyre made her lair in the dragon yards at Dragonstone, and the clutch of eggs laid at Fair Isle soon hatched. Rhaena introduced her daughter, Princess [Aerea](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen), to Dreamfyre's hatchlings, and urged her to choose one and fly.(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

In [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), Rhaena discovered her former close friend and companion, [Elissa Farman](wiki:Elissa_Farman), had stolen three dragon eggs from the Dragonstone hatcheries and fled to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos), and flew Dreamfyre to King's Landing to inform her brother King Jaehaerys of the theft. Jaehaerys made plans with Rhaena to make war with their dragons [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor) and Dreamfyre should hatchling dragons appear anywhere in the world, though this turned out to be unnecessary. Later that year, Rhaena flew Dreamfyre to [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) to reconcile with her mother, Dowager Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon), but arrived too late for Alyssa's death in childbirth. Still within that year, Rhaena's female companions were all murdered by her husband, [Androw Farman](wiki:Androw_Farman), using the [tears of Lys](wiki:Tears_of_Lys). When his crime was discovered, Androw committed suicide by leaping from the window of the [Chamber of the Painted Table](wiki:Chamber_of_the_Painted_Table); Rhaena had his body hacked to pieces and fed to Dreamfyre and the other dragons of Dragonstone.(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Near the end of [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), Rhaena discovered that her daughter Aerea had mounted Balerion and fled Dragonstone. Rhaena flew Dreamfyre to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), convinced that Aerea had gone there. Rhaena remained at court while Jaehaerys sent forth [ravens](wiki:Raven) across the realm, but after seven days, she declared that she was going to look for Aerea herself and left on Dreamfyre in search of her daughter.(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) For more than a year, Rhaena flew Dreamfyre all over [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), from [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) to [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) to [Crakehall](wiki:Crakehall), [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) and [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle), to the [barrowlands](wiki:Barrowlands) and the banks of the [Fever River](wiki:Fever_River), and as far south as the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) and the canyons of the [Torrentine](wiki:Torrentine). Rhaena and Dreamfyre were seen flying over the [Fingers](wiki:Fingers) and the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon), the [rainwood](wiki:Rainwood) on [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath), the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands), and the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor); however, her search was in vain. In [56 AC](wiki:56_AC), while residing at [Greenstone](wiki:Greenstone), Rhaena received a message that Princess Aerea had returned to King's Landing on Balerion. She had been infected with an insidious disease that killed her that same day. By the time the raven carrying the news had reached Rhaena, Aerea had already been cremated. Rhaena returned to King's Landing and took possession of her daughter's ashes. Refusing Jaehaerys's offer to have Aerea's ashes interred on Dragonstone as Aerea had hated living there, Rhaena instead took the ashes into the sky upon Dreamfyre and released them into the wind, claiming that Aerea had "*wanted to fly*".(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Rhaena went into self-imposed isolation at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) after her daughter's death. Although she still frequently rode Dreamfyre, she wished to be left alone. Dreamfyre remained at Harrenhal with Rhaena until her death in [73 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), after which Dreamfyre made her lair in the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit) at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### Helaena Targaryen

During the reign of King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen), Dreamfyre bonded with Princess [Helaena Targaryen](wiki:Helaena_Targaryen). Helaena was riding Dreamfyre by age eleven,(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and the dragon was beloved to her.(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), after Helaena's [six-year old son](wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_II)) was slain during the civil war, Helaena spent her days in darkness, weeping, and was not capable of riding Dreamfyre anymore. Dreamfyre resided in the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit), and it is said that when Helaena threw herself to her death from her window in [Maegor's Holdfast](wiki:Maegor's_Holdfast), Dreamfyre rose suddenly with a roar that shook the Dragonpit and snapped two of the chains that bound her.(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Dreamfyre remained chained in the Dragonpit when the people of King's Landing [stormed the pit](wiki:Storming_of_the_Dragonpit). Out of the four dragons present, Dreamfyre was the only dragon able to break free of her chains when the mob broke in. She took wing, circled the cavernous interior of the dome and swooped down to attack the men below. She slew more men than the other three dragons combined. Archers and crossbowmen loosed arrows and quarrels at the her, and whenever she landed men swarmed her to attack, driving her back into the air. Eventually one of her eyes was nicked by a crossbow bolt. Half-blind and maddened, Dreamfyre flew into the Dragonpit's great dome above, which cracked on impact. Half of it came tumbling down, crushing her and the [dragonslayers](wiki:Dragonslayer) under tons of broken stone and rubble.(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

## Appearances & Description

Dreamfyre was a slender she-dragon. Her coloration was primarily pale blue, with silver markings.(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) She had silver crests and pale blue wings.(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) By [35 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Dreamfyre was large enough for the twelve-year-old Rhaena Targaryen to mount her.(wiki:Dreamfyre.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
