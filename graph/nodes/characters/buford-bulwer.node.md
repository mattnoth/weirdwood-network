## Origins

Buford is said to have slain forty men in the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field) in [196 AC](wiki:196_AC). However, according to Ser [Maynard Plumm](wiki:Maynard_Plumm), the number Buford had slain on the Redgrass Field grew higher every single passing year.(wiki:Buford_Bulwer.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Ser Buford attended the [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) [tourney](wiki:Tourney) in [212 AC](wiki:212_AC). He faced Ser [Uthor Underleaf](wiki:Uthor_Underleaf) in the lists, but was defeated in the fifth tilt. He was injured upon being unhorsed and taken to the [maester](wiki:Maester).(wiki:Buford_Bulwer.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

## Appearances & Description

Early in the reign of [Aerys I Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_I_Targaryen), Buford is past sixty, and has become fat and soft. He is blind in his right eye. During the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls), he wore blood red armor and a helm decorated with black bull's horns on either side. He was considered a knight of some renown.(wiki:Buford_Bulwer.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
