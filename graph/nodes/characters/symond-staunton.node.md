## Origins

When the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) was announced, based on the notion that Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) was secretly funding the tourney as a way to call a [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council) to discuss the rule of King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), Symond unsuccessfully suggested to prohibit tourneys. When Rhaegar won the final joust, Symond and Lord [Qarlton Chelsted](wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted) inflamed the king's suspicions by declaring that Rhaegar had taken part only to win the favor of the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) and remind the [lords](wiki:Lord) present that he was a true heir to King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen). Because Rhaegar crowned [Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark) as the [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty), Symond considered it an attempt to win support from [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Symond_Staunton.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Symond's fate during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) is as yet unknown.
