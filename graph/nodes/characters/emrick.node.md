### A Dance with Dragons

Emrick and his brother [Arron](wiki:Arron) swear their vows with four other recruits ([Horse](wiki:Hareth_(Mole's_Town)), [Satin](wiki:Satin), [Leathers](wiki:Leathers), and [Jax](wiki:Jax)) at the grove of [weirwoods](wiki:Weirwood) north of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), inside the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest). They are escorted by [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), along with ten [rangers](wiki:Rangers). Jon, [Iron Emmett](wiki:Iron_Emmett), and [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) weigh each recruit and assign them to an order. Emrick is assigned to the [rangers](wiki:Rangers).(wiki:Emrick.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
