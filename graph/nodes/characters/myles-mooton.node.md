## Origins

Myles was the squire of Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), and received his knighthood from Rhaegar himself.(wiki:Myles_Mooton.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) As one of the younger men at court, he remained one of Rhaegar's close companions after having been knighted.(wiki:Myles_Mooton.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Myles_Mooton.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Ser Myles fought in the [Battle of the Bells](wiki:Battle_of_the_Bells) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). He was one of the six men killed in the battle by Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) that day.(wiki:Myles_Mooton.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Myles_Mooton.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_fall_of_the_dragons:_robert.27s_rebellion.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
