## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

In [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) confronts and fights [Dareon](wiki:Dareon) in the [Happy Port](wiki:Happy_Port) [brothel](wiki:Brothel), after Dareon claims he is giving up the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) and abandoning Samwell, [Gilly](wiki:Gilly), and [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)). During the fight, Sam is thrown out into the canals, where he almost drowns. Xhondo, who had watched the fight while playing [tiles](wiki:Tiles) in the brothel, dives into the canal after him, pulls Sam from the canal, and pounds the water from his lungs. Xhondo then tells Samwell that he knows about the [dragons](wiki:Dragon), information that Sam has been looking for on behalf of Maester Aemon.(wiki:Xhondo_Dhoru.cite_ref-Raffc26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Xhondo's information about [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) and her dragons, which he had seen in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), helps revitalize Maester Aemon from his [illness](wiki:Illness). Sam arranges passage on the *Cinnamon Wind* to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) for himself, Gilly, and Maester Aemon. Xhondo translates for the captain, [Quhuru Mo](wiki:Quhuru_Mo), regarding Sam's payment for the trip, which ends up being almost all that Sam possesses, including books meant for the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel), as well as Sam working as a crewman on the ship. Sam also gives Xhondo his [sword](wiki:Sword) and scabbard in repayment for the feather cloak that been ruined when Xhondo dove into the canal to save him.(wiki:Xhondo_Dhoru.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

After Maester Aemon's funeral, Xhondo has the crew open a cask of spiced [rum](wiki:Rum) to drink his memory. Summer Islanders have sex to honor the dead, and Xhondo would have gone with [Kojja Mo](wiki:Kojja_Mo), but instead she takes care of [Gilly's baby](wiki:Aemon_Steelsong) to give her and Sam a chance to be intimate. The next day, Sam avoids Gilly out of guilt, until Xhondo drags him to Kojja, who confronts him on the matter.(wiki:Xhondo_Dhoru.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

As the *Cinnamon Wind* approaches Oldtown, Xhondo and Sam view the wreckage of a sea battle in the [Whispering Sound](wiki:Whispering_Sound). Sam wonders who would be so mad as to raid so close to Oldtown, and Xhondo points out a half-sunken [longship](wiki:Longship) with a [banner they do not recognize](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy).(wiki:Xhondo_Dhoru.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
