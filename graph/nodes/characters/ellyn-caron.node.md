## Origins

When the [Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Aenys_I)) raided the [Dornish Marches](wiki:Dornish_Marches) in the [Second Dornish War](wiki:Second_Dornish_War), he led half of his host against [Nightsong](wiki:Nightsong) and [Horn Hill](wiki:Horn_Hill). The [outlaw](wiki:Outlaw) "king" was unable to capture Nightsong and abandoned the siege, marching his army west. However, Lady Ellyn sallied forth behind him and joined her strength with a strong force led by Lord [Harmon Dondarrion](wiki:Harmon_Dondarrion). Meanwhile, Lord [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly_(lord)) joined the battle athwart the Dornish line of march. Although the Vulture King's army was twice the size of his three foes combined, his host shattered when surrounded by armored [knights](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Ellyn_Caron.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Afterward, Ellyn was one of the many nobles rewarded by a grateful King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen) with gold, offices, and honors.(wiki:Ellyn_Caron.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
