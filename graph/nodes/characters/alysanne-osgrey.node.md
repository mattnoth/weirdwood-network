## Origins

After the defeat of [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) on the [Redgrass Field](wiki:Redgrass_Field), all of his supporters who survived the [war](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion) and remained in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) were made to pay for their treasons. As Alysanne's brothers were all dead, she became a hostage at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) at the age of seven to ensure the good behavior of her rebel father, Ser Eustace Osgrey. At some point she became a [silent sister](wiki:Silent_sister) and died in the capital at the age of twenty of the [Great Spring Sickness](wiki:Great_Spring_Sickness).(wiki:Alysanne_Osgrey.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
