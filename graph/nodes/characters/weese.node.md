## Appearances & Description

Weese is a squat man with boils near one corner of his lips and a fleshy carbuncle nose.(wiki:Weese.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) He is an abusive man, resorting to violence at the slightest provocation.(wiki:Weese.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) He keeps a [spotted dog](wiki:Spotted_dog).(wiki:Weese.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), who calls herself Weasel, is assigned to Weese when she is brought to [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) by the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men).(wiki:Weese.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Weese abuses Arya hitting her for being slow and for looking at him. Arya eventually whispers his name to [Jaqen H'ghar](wiki:Jaqen_H'ghar). Weese is later found with his throat torn out and his face being eaten by his [spotted dog](wiki:Spotted_dog), who is then shot with a crossbow.(wiki:Weese.cite_ref-Racok38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Arya later asks Jaqen how he made the dog to attack her master, but he does not elaborate.(wiki:Weese.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Arya learns from the [waif](wiki:Waif) while training at the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White) in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos). [Faceless Men](wiki:Faceless_Men) have access to a paste which, when spiced with [basilisk](wiki:Basilisk) blood, makes cooked meat smell savory but induces violent madness when consumed.(wiki:Weese.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) This may have been how Jaqen manipulated the dog into killing Weese.
