## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Albett is in the [same group of recruits](wiki:Jon_Snow's_Night's_Watch_recruitment_group) as [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) when he comes to the [Wall](wiki:Wall). Albett is given the nickname "Pimple" by Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne). After Jon intervenes during a practice session between [Halder](wiki:Halder) and [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), Alliser sends Albett and [Rast](wiki:Rast) to help Halder beat Jon. However, [Pypar](wiki:Pypar) and [Grenn](wiki:Grenn) aid Jon, and after a relatively quick fight, Albett yields after watching his two comrades fall.(wiki:Albett.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
After swearing his vows, Albett is assigned to the [builders](wiki:Builders).(wiki:Albett.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]
