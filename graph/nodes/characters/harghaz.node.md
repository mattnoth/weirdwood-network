## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

In celebration of the marriage between Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) and [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq), the [fighting pits](wiki:Fighting_pits) of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) are reopened. Several fights and spectacles take place in [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit), and the noise and smell of blood attracts [Drogon](wiki:Drogon). The black [dragon](wiki:Dragon) lands in the pit after a fight between [Barsena Blackhair](wiki:Barsena_Blackhair) and a [boar](wiki:Boar), and devours both the corpses of the [pit fighter](wiki:Pit_fighter) and the boar that killed her. Most of the attendees start to run when they see the dragon, but Harghaz, one of the [spearmen](wiki:Spear) sent out to drive the boar back to his pen, decides to be a hero, and attacks Drogon. His reasons are unknown; Daenerys conjectures that Harghaz may have loved Barsena, may want vengeance for [Hazzea](wiki:Hazzea), or simply wants glory.(wiki:Harghaz.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Harghaz wounds Drogon and almost succeeds in killing him, but is set aflame by the dragon when he flies from the pit with Daenerys.(wiki:Harghaz.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Harghaz.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
To the [Yunkai'i](wiki:Yunkai'i) and [Meereenese](wiki:Meereen) who oppose the rule of Daenerys, the would-be [dragonslayer](wiki:Dragonslayer) is remembered as "Harghaz the Hero".(wiki:Harghaz.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Harghaz.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
