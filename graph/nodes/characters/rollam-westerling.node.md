## Appearances & Description

Rollam Westerling was a dutiful squire to [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), and his mother [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) believed her own son [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) would have been much the same.(wiki:Rollam_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The boy was respectful, initially kneeling before Catelyn when first introduced before realizing his family members merely bowed. He was hesitant to leave Robb's side to settle into his accommodations after the Westerlings' arrival at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun)(wiki:Rollam_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and complained bitterly when left behind for the [wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) at the [Twins](wiki:Twins).(wiki:Rollam_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Rollam replaces [Olyvar Frey](wiki:Olyvar_Frey) as the squire of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), after Robb marries [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling) and [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey) withdraws their forces from Robb's.(wiki:Rollam_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Rollam accompanies Robb to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) when Robb returns from the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands). Rollam remains at Riverrun with his sisters, [Jeyne](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling) and [Eleyna](wiki:Eleyna_Westerling), and his mother, [Sybell Spicer](wiki:Sybell_Spicer), when Robb goes to the wedding of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) at the [Twins](wiki:Twins). Robb is then slain in the massacre dubbed the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Rollam_Westerling.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Rollam's elder brother, Ser [Raynald Westerling](wiki:Raynald_Westerling), is presumed to have died in the Red Wedding. After Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) resolves the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Lord [Gawen Westerling](wiki:Gawen_Westerling) departs for the [Crag](wiki:Crag) with his surviving family.(wiki:Rollam_Westerling.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
