## Identity

Septa Rhaella, born Rhaella Targaryen, was a daughter of Prince Aegon and Princess Rhaena Targaryen. She had an older twin sister, Aerea (wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon)).

## Origins

Born 42 AC at Casterly Rock (track_b: Born). Valyrian race, Crownlands culture (track_b: Race, Culture).

## Appearances & Description

The mirror image of her twin sister Aerea. Silver hair and purple eyes. As a young child, Rhaella was bolder and more robust than Aerea — a willful child who was a terror to her septas (wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon)).

## Narrative Arc

Born at Casterly Rock where her parents were hiding from the Poor Fellows rebellion. After her father Aegon was killed fighting Maegor in 43 AC, Rhaena disguised the twins and sent them away. Tyanna of the Tower found them and used them to ensure Rhaena's cooperation. As the younger twin, Rhaella was sent to Oldtown to train as a septa, while Aerea was named Maegor's heir.

When Rhaena fled King's Landing to join Jaehaerys's cause, Maegor sent a rider to Oldtown commanding Lord Hightower to behead Rhaella in retaliation. However, Lord Hightower refused. After Maegor's fall and Jaehaerys's coronation at Oldtown, Rhaella underwent a dramatic change in character — devoted herself to prayer and study, never again requiring chastisement. This simultaneous change in both twins' behavior led to persistent rumors that Rhaella and Aerea had secretly switched identities while both were in Oldtown (wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon)).

Died in or after 73 AC (track_b: Died).
