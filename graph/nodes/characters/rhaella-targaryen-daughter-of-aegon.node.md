## Identity

Rhaella Targaryen (daughter of Aegon) — child of Aegon Targaryen (son of Aenys I) and Rhaena Targaryen (daughter of Aenys I); sworn to House Targaryen; Princess. One of 2 characters named Rhaella Targaryen.

## Origins

### Maegor the Cruel

Rhaella was born in [42 AC](wiki:42_AC) at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), where her father, Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)), and her mother, Princess [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)), were residing, hiding from the [Poor Fellows](wiki:Poor_Fellows) who had risen in [rebellion](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising) against [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen).(wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon).cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

Upon the death of Rhaella's grandfather, King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen), the crown had been claimed by her uncle [Maegor](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), who thereby usurped the claim of her father Aegon.(wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) [Aegon finally went to battle against Maegor](wiki:Aegon_the_Uncrowned's_rebellion) in [43 AC](wiki:43_AC), but was killed in [battle](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye) later that year. Rhaena disguised Rhaella and her twin sister [Aerea](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen) and sent them away with allies. Not even Rhaena herself was aware where Rhaella and Aerea went. Both girls were found by [Tyanna of the Tower](wiki:Tyanna_of_the_Tower), one of King Maegor's wives. When Rhaena was summoned to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in [47 AC](wiki:47_AC) to marry Maegor, Tyanna used the girls' presence to assure her cooperation.(wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)(wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon).cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

As she was the younger of the two girls, Rhaella was sent to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) to be trained as a septa, while her twin sister was named Maegor's heir and kept at court. When Rhaella's mother, Queen Rhaena, learned that Prince [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), the youngest of her brothers, had [announced his claim](wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen's_uprising) to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), Rhaena fled [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) on [Dreamfyre](wiki:Dreamfyre) to join Jaehaerys's cause, taking Aerea and the [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) sword [Blackfyre](wiki:Blackfyre) with her. An enraged Maegor sent a rider to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), commanding Lord [Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) to behead Rhaella as punishment for Rhaena's betrayal. However, Lord Hightower refused and instead imprisoned the messenger.(wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon).cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

### Reign of Jaehaerys I

Rhaella was present in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) at the coronation of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) in [48 AC](wiki:48_AC), together with her mother and sister. After her sister had left with King Jaehaerys back to King's Landing, a change in the behaviors of both girls was noticed, and Rhaella's behavior appeared much as that of her sister [Aerea](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen), whilst Aerea became much more like Rhaella had been. At the time, it was widely believed that someone had used the presence of both girls at Oldtown to switch them. This was never proven, however, and no one was inclined to question the potential deception, as Aerea (or now Rhaella disguised as Aerea) was the heir of King Jaehaerys I until he had children of his own.(wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon).cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

In [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), after he had failed to undo the secret marriage of King Jaehaerys I to his younger sister, [Alysanne](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) suggested setting Jaehaerys aside before he reached [manhood](wiki:Age_of_majority), and in his place crowning Princess Aerea. For this, Rogar was fired from [his office](wiki:Hand_of_the_King). He attempted to seize Aerea by force, but the [small council](wiki:Small_council) had acted faster and hidden the girl. Unwilling to give up, Lord Rogar then sent his brother [Orryn](wiki:Orryn_Baratheon) to the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) at [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) to demand that the High Septon turn over Rhaella, still a novice, so she could be taken to Storm's End. There, he planned to force her to confess that she was the actual Princess Aerea, not Rhaella. Rogar subsequently hoped to crown her queen. However, this plot failed as well.(wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon).cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

In [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), while Jaehaerys and Alysanne stayed in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) awaiting the election of the new [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon), Alysanne met Rhaella, still a novice, when she spent a day with the septas who cared for the city's sick and destitute. Alysanne pronounced her to have become "a learned and devout young woman though much given to stammers and blushes.".(wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon).cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

After her sister Aerea had disappeared on [Balerion](wiki:Balerion) later that same year, Queen Rhaena visited Oldtown, hoping that Aerea might have sought out her twin sister Rhaella. Aerea was not in Oldtown, however. Rhaena visited with Rhaella for a time, and expressed regret about not having been a better mother. In reply, Rhaella embraced her mother and told her *"I have had the best mother any child could wish for, the [Mother Above](wiki:Mother_(the_Seven)), and you are to thank for her.”*.(wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Following the death of Aerea in [56 AC](wiki:56_AC), Queen Rhaena would never again visit [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) or [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), but she did travel to Oldtown once a year to visit Rhaella at the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) until her death in [73 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

By [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Rhaella had taken her vows as a septa. Near the end of [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), the High Septon sent Rhaella to King's Landing for the tourney held to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the coronation of King Jaehaerys. It was said that Queen Alysanne wept when she saw Rhaella, as she was the very image of Aerea, only older.(wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon).cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Appearances & Description

Rhaella was the mirror image of her twin-sister, [Aerea](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen).(wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon).cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) She had silver hair and purple eyes.(wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon).cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

As a young child, Rhaella was a bolder and more robust girl than her sister Aerea. She was said to be a bold and willful child who was a terror to her septas.(wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon).cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Following the coronation of her uncle [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) at [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), however, Rhaella devoted herself to prayer and study, and never again required chastisement. This change in character, as well as a simultaneous change in the character of her twin, Aerea, led to rumors that Rhaella had switched places with her sister Aerea while both girls had been in the city.(wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon).cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
