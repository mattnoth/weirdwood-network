## Identity

Septa Lemore is a woman traveling with Young Griff's party aboard the Shy Maid on the Rhoyne, posing as a septa. Also known as Lady Lemore (track_b: aliases). She holds the title of Septa (track_b: Title).

## Origins

Born around 259 AC in Westeros (track_b: Born). Of Westerosi culture (track_b: Culture).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` Faith of the Seven (track_b: Allegiances). `SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiances).

## Narrative Arc

Lemore appears in ADWD as part of the group accompanying Young Griff (Aegon) on the Shy Maid. She teaches Young Griff and swims naked in the Rhoyne, revealing stretch marks that suggest she has borne a child. Her true identity is a subject of much speculation among readers (wiki:Lemore.cite_ref-Radwd14, cite_ref-Radwd18, cite_ref-Radwd22).
