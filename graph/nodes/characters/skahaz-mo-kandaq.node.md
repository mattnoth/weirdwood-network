## Appearances & Description

Skahaz has a a beetled brow and small eyes with heavy bags beneath them. His big nose is dark with blackheads, and his oily skin looks more yellow than the amber of most [Ghiscari](wiki:Ghiscari).(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) and Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) consider him to be ugly.(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Skahaz is a stern(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) man who often scowls.(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) He rarely smiles,(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) but when he does so his smile can look savage.(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) The ruthless nobleman(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) is an effective torturer(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) who is willing to kill hostages.(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) He has an iron grip.(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Skahaz dislikes [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq).(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) The Shavepate is hated by the [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy) for having abandoned the old ways of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

After the [fall of Meereen](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen) to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), Skahaz becomes one of her councilors. Since [Ghiscari](wiki:Ghiscari) traditionally style their hair into fanciful shapes, he shaves his hair to show he has abandoned old Meereen to serve the new ways. Several of [his kin](wiki:House_of_Kandaq) and followers do the same, becoming known as the [shavepates](wiki:Shavepates).(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9) Skahaz offers to put aside his wife to marry Daenerys, but she declines.(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)

After several of her [freedmen](wiki:Freedmen) are killed by the [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy), Daenerys allows Skahaz Shavepate to use torture to uncover the murderers.(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Under Daenerys's orders, Skahaz creates the [Brazen Beasts](wiki:Brazen_Beasts), an organization composed of shavepates and freedmen that serves as a city watch to Meereen. The guardsmen wear [brass](wiki:Brass) masks in the shapes of fanciful beasts to protect their identities from the [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy), who have promised death to the families of any man who serves the dragon queen.(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

Skahaz supports the reopening of the [fighting pits of Meereen](wiki:Fighting_pits_of_Meereen),(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) and he argues with Daenerys on several occasions over her refusal not to harm any of her [cupbearers](wiki:Cupbearers_of_Daenerys_Targaryen) who are serving as hostages.(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) Many Meereenese ships participate in [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai)'s blockade of [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay), so Skahaz provides Daenerys with a list of Meereenese participants and advises her to seize their family members. The queen refuses to do so in the interests of peace, however, and burns the list.(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) If the Yunkai'i were to conquer Meereen, Skahaz says he would put his own family to death to prevent them from being raped and enslaved.(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

Daenerys marries [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq)(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) against Skahaz's advice,(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3) and the new king replaces the Shavepate as commander of the Brazen Beasts with his own cousin, [Marghaz zo Loraq](wiki:Marghaz_zo_Loraq).(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2) Hizdahr names Skahaz as [Warden of the River](wiki:Warden_of_the_River) in charge of the [Skahazadhan](wiki:Skahazadhan), but the Shavepate declines and retires to the [pyramid of Kandaq](wiki:Pyramid_of_Kandaq).(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-4)

Daenerys reluctantly agrees to reopen the fighting pits, but she disappears on [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) from [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit).(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-3) Skahaz suspects that Daenerys's new husband tried to [poison](wiki:Poison) her at the arena.(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Skahaz takes his suspicions to Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) that [Strong Belwas](wiki:Belwas) had eaten [poisoned locusts](wiki:Poisoned_locusts) that may have been meant for Daenerys, and he accuses Hizdahr of being the [Harpy](wiki:Harpy).(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-5)

Barristan, Skahaz and [Grey Worm](wiki:Grey_Worm) seize Hizdahr and take control of Meereen.(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2) With Barristan serving as [Hand of the Queen](wiki:Hand_of_the_Queen) in Daenerys's absence, they prepare for war with Yunkai.(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-2) Skahaz opposes Barristan's decision to send [Galazza Galare](wiki:Galazza_Galare) to meet with the [Wise Masters](wiki:Wise_Masters) of Yunkai, as well as the participation of [pit fighters](wiki:Pit_fighters) in Barristan's council.(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-3) Skahaz also wants Ser [Cletus Yronwood](wiki:Cletus_Yronwood) and Ser [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater) to be hanged for their part in the freeing of [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal) and [Viserion](wiki:Viserion) from the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid).(wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-4)
