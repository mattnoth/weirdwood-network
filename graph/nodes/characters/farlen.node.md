## Narrative Arc

Farlen tends the hounds and kennels at Winterfell. He taught Bran Stark the differences between the dogs (agot-bran-05). After Robb departs for war, Farlen keeps Rickon's direwolf Shaggydog chained in the kennels to contain the wolf's growing aggression (agot-bran-06). When Theon Greyjoy captures Winterfell, Farlen's daughter Palla is raped; Farlen is later killed — falsely accused by Theon and executed — his body appearing among the dead at the sack (wiki:Farlen).
