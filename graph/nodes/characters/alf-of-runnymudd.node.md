## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) and [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) are established at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), Alf of Runnymudd converts to the faith of [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor) and is among the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men).(wiki:Alf_of_Runnymudd.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Alf gathers with other members of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) at the gate of Castle Black after word spreads that the heads of three rangers were found impaled upon spears on the other side of the [Wall](wiki:Wall). When [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) and his party return from inspecting the heads, Alf inquires if his friend [Garth Greyfeather](wiki:Garth_Greyfeather) is among them. He lets out a howl of grief upon learning that Garth is dead, and has to be escorted to a bed and given mulled wine by [Hobb](wiki:Hobb).(wiki:Alf_of_Runnymudd.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
When Jon gathers men to the [Shieldhall](wiki:Shieldhall), Alf of Runnymudd sits with [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh), [Wick Whittlestick](wiki:Wick_Whittlestick), and [Left Hand Lew](wiki:Left_Hand_Lew). He is presumably one of the four men who stabbed Jon Snow during the [mutiny at Castle Black](wiki:Mutiny_at_Castle_Black).(wiki:Alf_of_Runnymudd.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
