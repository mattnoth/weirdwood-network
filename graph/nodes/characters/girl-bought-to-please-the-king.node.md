## Identity

Girl bought to please the king is a character.human from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges

- CULTURE_OF: Lys (track_b: Culture)

## Origins

The girl was trained as a [bed slave](wiki:Pillow_house) in [Lys](wiki:Lys) and was taught the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue) at some point. She was bought to please the king, [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis)'s one-time guest [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen), in the bedchamber.(wiki:Girl_bought_to_please_the_king.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Appearances & Description

The girl is blond, blue-eyed, fair, and willowy.(wiki:Girl_bought_to_please_the_king.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) She is a talkative girl, sixteen years of age, and is the favorite of [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis).(wiki:Girl_bought_to_please_the_king.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

The girl and an older woman help bathe [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) before the princess's introduction to [Drogo](wiki:Drogo). The young servant is envious that Daenerys will marry the powerful *[khal](wiki:Khal)*.(wiki:Girl_bought_to_please_the_king.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

After drinking at [Illyrio's manse](wiki:Illyrio's_manse), [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) wakes to find the girl tending to him, having been told by Magister [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) to bathe the dwarf and warm his bed.(wiki:Girl_bought_to_please_the_king.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Tyrion initially declines the girl's offer to sleep together. After thinking of the girl's revulsion for him, however, Tyrion reconsiders and disparages the girl.(wiki:Girl_bought_to_please_the_king.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) When he joins Illyrio for supper, Tyrion tells the magister that the girl did all that was required of her.(wiki:Girl_bought_to_please_the_king.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
