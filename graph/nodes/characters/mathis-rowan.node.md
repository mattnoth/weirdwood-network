## Origins

During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Mathis stayed loyal to King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen). He assisted his liege lord, [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), during the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End), which was defended by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon). Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) lifted the siege following the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing), and Rowan bent the knee to the new king, [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Lord Mathis found [Dareon](wiki:Dareon) in bed with [his daughter](wiki:Mathis_Rowan's_daughter), who was two years older than the singer. Under the eyes of her father, she claimed rape and Dareon was sent to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch). Dareon insists the relationship was consensual.(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

## Appearances & Description

Mathis is stout, florid, and clean-shaven.(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He wears a snowy doublet with a great tree worked upon the breast in gold thread.(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

According to Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), Mathis is sensible, prudent, well liked, able, and loyal.(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) thinks Mathis is gruff, blunt, and to the point.(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

At the outset of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Mathis joins his liege lord, [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), in declaring for King [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon). After the [melee at Bitterbridge](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge) where [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) wins a spot in the [Rainbow Guard](wiki:Rainbow_Guard), Mathis and several other nobles are with Renly when he meets with [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), who has come as an envoy for her son, [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), the [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North). Mathis is dismayed to learn Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) was not executed after being captured by Robb's army in the [Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood). He later sits next to Catelyn during a feast at [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge).(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

After the parley with King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) beneath the walls of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), Mathis advises Renly to leave Stannis to besiege the formidable Storm's End and instead go on to take [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). His opinion is overruled, however, as Renly decides to deal with Stannis before the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister). Mathis is placed in command of the center in the coming battle with Stannis's forces.(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Shortly before dawn, Mathis and Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) make final battle preparations with Renly. Mathis suggests attacking at once, but Renly refuses since he had agreed with Stannis to begin the battle at dawn. After receiving their final orders from Renly, Mathis and Randyll depart to ready their soldiers. Before the battle can take place, however, [Renly is assassinated](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)).(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Mathis is among the lords who, like Lord Tyrell, do not side with Stannis.(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) He is one of the leaders of the Reach that joins Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) at the headwaters of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush), marching alongside him until [Tumbler's Falls](wiki:Tumbler's_Falls), where Mace waits with an even larger force. They embark on barges and sail to just outside [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), where they take Stannis's army in the rear during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) As a hero of the Blackwater, Lord Rowan is rewarded by King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) in the throne room.(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Mathis attends a [small council](wiki:Small_council) meeting after the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), Joffrey's mother, attempts to influence Rowan by jests and smiles. Tywin Lannister, Joffrey's grandfather and the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), grants lesser tracts of land to the Rowans. Mathis appears upset when Tywin mentions the unresolved deaths of [Elia Martell](wiki:Elia_Martell) and her children in the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

During [the wedding feast](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark) of [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), Mathis dances with Cersei and Sansa.(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

As a guest at Joffrey's [royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding), Mathis gives the king a red silk [tourney](wiki:Tourney) pavilion as a wedding gift.(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Feast for Crows

After the death of the Hand of the King, Lord Tywin, Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) mentions Lord Mathis as a possibility to fill the vacant position.(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Cersei considers Mathis to be one of Mace's cronies,(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Raffc3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) however, and she chooses the tractable Ser [Harys Swyft](wiki:Harys_Swyft) as Hand and removes Mathis from the council.

Lord Rowan attends [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Tommen_I_Baratheon_and_Margaery_Tyrell) of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_I_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

Mathis rides with Lord Tyrell to [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), still held by Stannis's man, Ser [Gilbert Farring](wiki:Gilbert_Farring).(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) When Mace hears that his daughter Margaery has been arrested by the [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow), he returns to the capital and leaves Lord Rowan in command of [the siege](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(300)). Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) thinks Mathis would make an able Hand of the King.(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

The [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) lands in the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) and quickly seizes several castles, gaining a beachhead for [Aegon VI Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)). Their next target is Storm's End,(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) where Mathis still holds the command of a token force laying siege to the castle.(wiki:Mathis_Rowan.cite_ref-Rawoiafmathis_rowan.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
