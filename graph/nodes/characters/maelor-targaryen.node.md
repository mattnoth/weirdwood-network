## Identity

Prince Maelor Targaryen was a prince of House Targaryen, son of King Aegon II Targaryen and Queen Helaena Targaryen. He held the title of Prince (track_b: Title). He was aligned with the Greens during the Dance of the Dragons (track_b: Allegiances).

## Origins

Born in 127 AC in King's Landing (track_b: Born). His father was Aegon II Targaryen and his mother was Helaena Targaryen (track_b: Father, Mother). Of Valyrian race and Crownlands culture (track_b: Race, Culture).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiances). `SWORN_TO` Greens (track_b: Allegiances).

## Narrative Arc

Maelor was the younger son of Aegon II and Helaena. During the Blood and Cheese incident, the assassins forced Helaena to choose which of her sons would die; she chose Maelor (the younger), but they killed Jaehaerys instead. Maelor was later sent away from King's Landing for safety but died in 130 AC at Bitterbridge, torn apart by a mob fighting over possession of the prince (track_b: Died).
