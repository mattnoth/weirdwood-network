## Identity

Arnold Arryn, called the Mad Heir, was a knight of House Arryn and a claimant to the Eyrie. He was a first cousin of Lady Jeyne Arryn (wiki:Arnold_Arryn).

## Narrative Arc

Arnold twice rose against his cousin Jeyne Arryn to contest her inheritance, claiming women were too soft to rule. After his second rising, he was imprisoned in the Eyrie's sky cells, where he went mad (wiki:Arnold_Arryn). When Jeyne died in 134 AC naming her fourth cousin Ser Joffrey as heir, Arnold's son Ser Eldric pressed the claim. Ser Corwyn Corbray upheld Jeyne's will; Eldric was executed. Arnold escaped to Runestone under Lord Gunthor Royce's protection. War engulfed the Vale until Alyn Velaryon and Benjicot Blackwood forced Arnold's supporters to submit to Joffrey by 136 AC (wiki:Arnold_Arryn).
