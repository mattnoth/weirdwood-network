## Origins

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord Perryn sided with the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) and [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord_Perryn.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Lord_Perryn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Lord Perryn, alongside with [Lord Bigglestone](wiki:Lord_Bigglestone) and [Lord Chambers](wiki:Lord_Chambers), joined Ser [Pate of Longleaf](wiki:Pate_of_Longleaf) and his ragged band of survivors from earlier battles and fought together in the [Battle by the Lakeshore](wiki:Battle_by_the_Lakeshore).(wiki:Lord_Perryn.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Lord_Perryn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

It is unknown if he survived the war.
