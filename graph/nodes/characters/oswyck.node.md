## Identity

Septon Oswyck was a member of the Faith of the Seven who served on Dragonstone from the reign of Aegon the Conqueror through the early reign of Jaehaerys I Targaryen (wiki:Oswyck).

## Narrative Arc

Oswyck was a novice in the court of Queen Rhaenys Targaryen during Aegon I's rule, and later became septon at Dragonstone and tutor of royal children in the Faith. When Jaehaerys I and Alysanne fled to Dragonstone in 49 AC, Oswyck personally performed their wedding ceremony. He later assisted Jaehaerys and Septon Barth in developing the Doctrine of Exceptionalism. He died of natural causes in 54 AC — the first death in what became known as the Year of the Stranger. At the king's request, his remains were interred on Dragonstone (wiki:Oswyck).
