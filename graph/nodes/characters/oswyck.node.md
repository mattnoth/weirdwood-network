## Identity

Oswyck, an old septon of Dragonstone, had known Jaehaerys and Alysanne since birth and instructed them in the Seven.

## Origins

Oswyck had been the septon at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) for some time. He was the tutor of the royal children in the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) during the reign of [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen) and was a novice in the court of Queen [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) during the rule of [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen). When [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) fled to Dragonstone in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC) and asked to be wed, he personally performed the ceremony.(wiki:Oswyck.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Later in King Jaehaerys's reign, Oswyck, along with Septon [Barth](wiki:Barth), assisted the king in the development of the [Doctrine of Exceptionalism](wiki:Doctrine_of_Exceptionalism), which would later be added to the teachings of the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:Oswyck.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Septon Oswyck passed away in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC). His was the first death that year which would become known as the *Year of the Stranger*, though his death was due to natural causes. At the king's request, his remains were interred on Dragonstone.(wiki:Oswyck.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
