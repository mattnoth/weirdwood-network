## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Garth is among the members of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) who accompany [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), on the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging). Garth survives the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist) and is among the survivors who make it to [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep). Garth is skeptical about [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) having slain an [Other](wiki:Other), and he is one of the brothers who complain loudly about [Craster](wiki:Craster) hoarding food. After [Bannen](wiki:Bannen) dies, Garth and [Tim Stone](wiki:Tim_Stone) swing his body into the funeral pyre. During the [mutiny](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep), Garth is killed by [Garth of Greenaway](wiki:Garth_of_Greenaway), though Sam does not know why.(wiki:Garth_of_Oldtown.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Later at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) is informed that Garth of Oldtown had been among the men who turned against [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch).(wiki:Garth_of_Oldtown.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
