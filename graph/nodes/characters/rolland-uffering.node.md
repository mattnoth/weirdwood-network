## Origins

Ser Rolland Uffering was the fourth husband of Lady Rohanne Webber of [Coldmoat](wiki:Coldmoat). His sister was Lady [Helicent Uffering](wiki:Helicent_Uffering). Ser Rolland and his wife had not been married long when he died during the [Great Spring Sickness](wiki:Great_Spring_Sickness). After his death, his sister remained at Coldmoat, as the kin of other deceased husbands of Lady Rohanne had done before her. It was rumored after his death that Rohanne killed him, possibly by poison, as she supposedly had multiple husbands before him.(wiki:Rolland_Uffering.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
