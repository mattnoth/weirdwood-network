## Identity

Septon Barth was a septon who served as Hand of the King for King Jaehaerys I Targaryen. He is considered by Archmaester Gyldayn to have been "the wisest man ever to serve as the Hand of the King" (wiki:Barth.cite_ref-Rfab6). He was a blacksmith's son given to the Faith of the Seven when young (wiki:Barth.cite_ref-Rfab8).

## Origins

Born in or before 37 AC (track_b: Born). A blacksmith's son of the Reach, he first served as a septon at Highgarden before being sent to King's Landing to tend the Red Keep library in 50 AC (wiki:Barth.cite_ref-Rfab9).

## Narrative Arc

Barth officiated the second marriage of Jaehaerys I and Alysanne Targaryen in 51 AC and helped write the Doctrine of Exceptionalism. He became one of Jaehaerys's most trusted advisors. In 55 AC, after Lord Daemon Velaryon resigned as Hand, Jaehaerys ultimately chose Barth for the office despite his low birth. He served as Hand for forty years until his death in 98 AC at the Red Keep. Barth was plainspoken but brilliant, though his enemies claimed he was more sorcerer than septon. His writings include works on dragons and dragonlore (wiki:Barth.cite_ref-Rfab4, wiki:Barth.cite_ref-Rfab5).
