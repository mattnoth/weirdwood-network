## Identity

Septon Chayle is the septon at Winterfell, serving both as a clergyman of the Faith of the Seven and as a member of the Stark household (track_b: Title, Allegiances). He grew up on the shores of the White Knife and is a very good swimmer (wiki:Chayle).

## Origins

Born near the White Knife (track_b: Born). Died in 299 AC at Winterfell (track_b: Died).

## Allegiances

Chayle is `SWORN_TO` the Faith of the Seven and `SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiances). He `HOLDS_TITLE` Septon (track_b: Title) and belongs to `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Chayle wears the crystal of his order on a silver chain (agot-tyrion-01). He is described as young (agot-tyrion-01).

## Narrative Arc

Chayle tends Winterfell's small sept and assists in the library. He falls asleep reading about Grand Maester Aethelmure while Tyrion explores the library (agot-tyrion-01). After Winterfell's library tower fire, Chayle helps sort through damaged scrolls and interprets the red comet as "the sword that slays the season" (wiki:Chayle). Jojen Reed foresees Chayle's death in a green dream; when Bran warns him, Chayle holds to his faith, saying the gods will take him when they see fit and he scarcely thinks it likely he'll drown (wiki:Chayle). After Theon Greyjoy captures Winterfell, Chayle is thrown into a well to honor the Drowned God — an ironic death given his swimming skill and his own dismissal of drowning as unlikely (wiki:Chayle).
