## Origins

Chayle grew up on the shores of the [White Knife](wiki:White_Knife) and is a very good swimmer.(wiki:Chayle.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Appearances & Description

Chayle is young(wiki:Chayle.cite_ref-Ragot9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) and cheerful.(wiki:Chayle.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He believes that [Hodor](wiki:Hodor) has a gentle spirit.(wiki:Chayle.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He wears the crystal of his order on a silver chain.(wiki:Chayle.cite_ref-Ragot9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Septon Chayle falls asleep reading about Grand Maester [Aethelmure](wiki:Aethelmure) while [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) explores [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell)'s library.(wiki:Chayle.cite_ref-Ragot9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) The [library tower](wiki:Library_tower) is set afire when the [catspaw](wiki:Catspaw) tries to assassinate [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark).(wiki:Chayle.cite_ref-Ragot14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Clash of Kings

While they are sorting through some scrolls from Winterfell's library fire, Chayle tells Bran that the [red comet](wiki:Red_comet) "is the sword that slays the season." Bran thinks he probably is right since a [white raven](wiki:White_raven) has arrived from [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) bringing word of [autumn](wiki:Autumn).(wiki:Chayle.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

[Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed) foresees the death of Chayle in his [green dreams](wiki:Greenseer). When Bran tells Chayle of his nearing death, Chayle holds to his faith.(wiki:Chayle.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) After [Winterfell is taken](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) by the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), Chayle is struck when he tries to assist [Palla](wiki:Palla),(wiki:Chayle.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) who has been raped.(wiki:Chayle.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) has Chayle thrown into a well to honor the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God).(wiki:Chayle.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

A dripping Chayle appears in Theon's nightmare of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s feast of the dead at Winterfell.(wiki:Chayle.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2) [Lady Catelyn's sept](wiki:Lady_Catelyn's_sept) is burned during the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Chayle.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
