## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

After the deaths of Lord [Halys Hornwood](wiki:Halys_Hornwood) and his son, [Daryn](wiki:Daryn_Hornwood), [Leobald Tallhart](wiki:Leobald_Tallhart) suggests that Lady [Donella Hornwood](wiki:Donella_Hornwood) adopt his son Beren as her heir. Because Beren's mother [Berena](wiki:Berena_Hornwood) is a Hornwood he has a claim to the lands and titles of [House Hornwood](wiki:House_Hornwood). Leobald suggests that Beren could take on the Hornwood name.(wiki:Beren_Tallhart.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Beren is held captive at [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Torrhen's_Square) along with his mother, [Berena](wiki:Berena_Hornwood), and brother, [Brandon](wiki:Brandon_Tallhart), by [Dagmer](wiki:Dagmer)'s [ironmen](wiki:Ironmen).(wiki:Beren_Tallhart.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
