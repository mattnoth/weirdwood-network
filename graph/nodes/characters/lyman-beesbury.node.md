## Origins

Lyman served as [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin) and [lord treasurer](wiki:Lord_treasurer) almost uninterrupted since the reign of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) until the end of King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen)'s reign.(wiki:Lyman_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Lyman_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Lyman was a man of eighty when King Viserys I passed away prior to the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). When the [small council](wiki:Small_council) gathered in the chambers of Queen [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower), Lyman was the only one to espouse the claim of Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), Viserys's daughter and preferred heir, over the claim of Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), Alicent's eldest son.(wiki:Lyman_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
According to [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Orwyle](wiki:Orwyle), who was present in the chamber, Ser [Otto Hightower](wiki:Otto_Hightower), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), had Lyman arrested and sent to the [black cells](wiki:Black_cells), where he eventually died of a chill. In contrast, [Septon](wiki:Septon) [Eustace](wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)) claimed that Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole), the [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard), slit Lyman's throat with his dagger. [Court fool](wiki:Court_fool) [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom) alleges that Criston instead tossed Lyman from the window onto the spikes of the [Maegor's Holdfast](wiki:Maegor's_Holdfast)'s moat. Eustace and Mushroom were not present, however. Lyman was succeeded as master of coin by Ser [Tyland Lannister](wiki:Tyland_Lannister).(wiki:Lyman_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
Lyman's death was kept secret, with most believing he was languishing in a black cell.(wiki:Lyman_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) Lyman's heir, Ser [Alan Beesbury](wiki:Alan_Beesbury), loudly demanded the release of his grandsire.(wiki:Lyman_Beesbury.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
