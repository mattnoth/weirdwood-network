## Origins

After the death of [Jeyne Arryn](wiki:Jeyne_Arryn), the [Lady of the Eyrie](wiki:Lady_of_the_Eyrie), a [succession dispute](wiki:Arryn_succession_conflict_(134_AC)) emerged between her chosen heir, Ser [Joffrey Arryn](wiki:Joffrey_Arryn), and a closer cousin, Ser [Arnold Arryn](wiki:Arnold_Arryn). Isembard emerged as a third claimant and used his wealth to bribe lesser [lords](wiki:Lord) and hire [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords) to support his claim.(wiki:Isembard_Arryn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Ser [Corwyn Corbray](wiki:Corwyn_Corbray), one of [the regents](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III) for the young king [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), ruled that Jeyne's will must prevail and declared Joffrey the rightful Lord of the Eyrie. Isembard and his sons were imprisoned, but [House Grafton](wiki:House_Grafton) still continued to support his claim. Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) eventually defeated Isembard's sellsails and captured [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown)'s harbor.(wiki:Isembard_Arryn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

By the time new regents were to be chosen by lot in [136 AC](wiki:136_AC), Alyn and Lord [Benjicot Blackwood](wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood) had at last forced the supporters of Isembard and Arnold to do homage to Lord Joffrey as their liege. Isembard made his way to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for the lottery. After Lord [Torrhen Manderly](wiki:Torrhen_Manderly) was chosen [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Alyn suggested that Isembard be named [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin). Isembard and Torrhen reformed taxes and provided some relief for those suffering from the collapse of the [Rogare Bank](wiki:Rogare_Bank).(wiki:Isembard_Arryn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
