## Identity

Nate, known as Feathers, is a serving man and maester's assistant in service of House Martell. He is known by his nickname because he has long worked with the ravens at the Water Gardens (wiki:Nate).

## Appearances & Description

A tall young lad (wiki:Nate).

## Allegiances

## Narrative Arc

Nate is one of Princess Arianne Martell's six companions on her journey to meet Jon Connington and Aegon Targaryen in the Stormlands. He tends seven ravens from the Water Gardens, which Arianne uses to send messages to Prince Doran. Elia Sand takes an interest in Feathers, kissing him at Mistwood, for which Arianne reprimands her (wiki:Nate, TWOW Arianne I-II).
