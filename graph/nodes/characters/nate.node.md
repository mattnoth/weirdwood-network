## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter

Nate is one of Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell)'s six companions on her journey to meet [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) and [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)) in the [Stormlands](wiki:Stormlands). He tends the seven [ravens](wiki:Raven) from the Water Gardens,(wiki:Nate.cite_ref-Rtwow_.5B.5Barianne_i_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Carianne_i.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) which Arianne uses to send messages back to her father Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell).(wiki:Nate.cite_ref-Rtwow_.5B.5Barianne_ii_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Carianne_ii.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) [Elia Sand](wiki:Elia_Sand) takes an interest in Feathers, kissing him at [Mistwood](wiki:Mistwood), and is reprimanded for this by Arianne.(wiki:Nate.cite_ref-Rtwow_.5B.5Barianne_ii_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Carianne_ii.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
