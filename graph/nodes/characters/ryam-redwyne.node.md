## Origins

Ryam and his older brothers, [Robert](wiki:Robert_Redwyne) and [Rickard](wiki:Rickard_Redwyne), accompanied their father, Lord [Manfryd Redwyne](wiki:Manfryd_Redwyne), to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) as squires when King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) named Manfryd as [master of ships](wiki:Master_of_ships) in [50 AC](wiki:50_AC).(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

During the [tourney](wiki:Tourney_at_King's_Landing_on_the_anniversary_of_the_king's_coronation) celebrating Jaehaerys's tenth year of coronation, young Ryam unhorsed Ser [Ronnal Baratheon](wiki:Ronnal_Baratheon), Ser [Arthor Oakheart](wiki:Arthor_Oakheart), Lord [Simon Dondarrion](wiki:Simon_Dondarrion), and Ser [Harys Hogg](wiki:Harys_Hogg), as well as Ser [Lorence Roxton](wiki:Lorence_Roxton) and Ser [Lucamore Strong](wiki:Lucamore_Strong) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). Ryam crowned Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) as the tourney's [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty).(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Ser [Gyles Morrigen](wiki:Gyles_Morrigen), [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard), recommended that Ryam and Ser [Robin Shaw](wiki:Robin_Shaw) join the white cloaks in [60 AC](wiki:60_AC) after two previous knights had perished from the [Shivers](wiki:Shivers).(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

In late [73 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) Ryam discovered that his sworn brother, Ser Lucamore Strong, had broken his Kingsguard vows of not taking a wife or fathering children. Lucamore the Lusty had fathered sixteen children among three women he had secretly wed, with each woman unaware of the others. Ryam brought the revelation to an enraged Gyles. Lucamore was [gelded](wiki:Eunuch) by his former sworn brothers of the Kingsguard, after which King Jaehaerys sent the disgraced knight to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)

Ryam was the victor of a tourney held in Jaehaerys's honor at [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) in [89 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-2) In a tourney at King's Landing to celebrate the fiftieth year of Jaehaerys's reign in [98 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Ryam and Ser [Clement Crabb](wiki:Clement_Crabb) broke thirty lances against each other before the king proclaimed them co-champions. It is often called the finest display of jousting in the history of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

In [99 AC](wiki:99_AC), Ryam, the Lord Commander, was named [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) by Jaehaerys, after the death of the previous Hand, Septon [Barth](wiki:Barth). Considered one of the worst Hands the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) has seen,(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Ryam was replaced by the king's son and heir, Prince [Baelon Targaryen](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)), less than a year after having been raised to the office.(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

Ryam served King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) after the death of Jaehaerys in [103 AC](wiki:103_AC) until he died in [105 AC](wiki:105_AC). His slot in the Kingsguard was taken by Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole).(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) once recalled that Ryam won victories against terrible foes.(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

When playing as children, [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) pretended to be King [Daeron I Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen), while [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) pretended to be Ser Ryam.(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

## Appearances & Description

Ryam was considered the greatest knight of his day,(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) accomplished at [sword](wiki:Sword) and lance.(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) There are songs about Ryam praising his chivalry.(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) dreams of becoming a [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) like Ryam.(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Clash of Kings

While having a nightmare of the [riot of King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing), [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) hopes to be saved by Ryam, [Florian the Fool](wiki:Florian_the_Fool), or Prince [Aemon the Dragonknight](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_II)), but none appear.(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Racok52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

### A Storm of Swords

The [lowborn](wiki:Lowborn) [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) is reluctant to serve as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), but Maester [Pylos](wiki:Pylos) reminds Davos that there were accomplished knights like Ryam who served poorly as Hand.(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

To help convince Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) to join the Kingsguard, Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) has bards bribed to sing of the glories of Ryam, Aemon, and [Serwyn of the Mirror Shield](wiki:Serwyn_of_the_Mirror_Shield).(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), now [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard), feels himself unworthy of a position once held by Ryam.(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Jaime advises Loras to learn about their predecessors in the Kingsguard, such as Ryam.(wiki:Ryam_Redwyne.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
