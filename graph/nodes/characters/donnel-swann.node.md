## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Donnel gives his support to [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) at the beginning of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Donnel_Swann.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) After Renly dies at [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), he goes over to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Donnel_Swann.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Donnel fights in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). He becomes wounded and yields to Ser [Elwood Harte](wiki:Elwood_Harte). He is ransomed afterwards and bends the knee to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Donnel_Swann.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords

During the first meeting of the new [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard) [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) mockingly calls Donnel *"Ser Donnel the Constant"* to his younger brother, Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann), and suggests he add a weathervane to his shield. Jaime asks Balon how the latter will react when Donnel decides to swear fealty to a fifth king, and Balon will have to chose between the king he has sworn to protect and his own blood, citing how he once faced the same decision.(wiki:Donnel_Swann.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
