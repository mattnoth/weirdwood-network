## Origins

Aelinor was born in her [family](wiki:House_Penrose)'s seat, [Parchments](wiki:Parchments).(wiki:Aelinor_Penrose.cite_ref-Rawoiafaelinor_targaryen.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Aelinor was married to her cousin, Prince [Aerys Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_I_Targaryen). He never showed any interest in getting her with child, instead shunning her bed. Rumor had it that Aerys never even consummated the marriage.(wiki:Aelinor_Penrose.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf413.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Aelinor_Penrose.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) When Aerys ascended to the throne as King Aerys I in [209 AC](wiki:209_AC), his [small council](wiki:Small_council) urged the king to [put Aelinor aside](wiki:Marriage) and marry another, hoping that Aerys's behavior was caused by dislike of her. However, King Aerys would not listen, and so Aelinor remained his wife.(wiki:Aelinor_Penrose.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf413.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

During the reign of Aerys I, it was said that Queen Aelinor would pray at the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) daily, beseeching [the Mother Above](wiki:Mother_(the_Seven)) to bless her with a child.(wiki:Aelinor_Penrose.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Aelinor and Aerys had no children.(wiki:Aelinor_Penrose.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf413.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
