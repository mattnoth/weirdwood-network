## Identity

Hobb the Hewer — A woodsman who leapt onto Shrykos's neck and drove his axe into the beast's skull, striking seven blows for the Seven.

## Edges

- CULTURE_OF: Crownlands (track_b: Culture)

## Origins

During the [Storming of the Dragonpit](wiki:Storming_of_the_Dragonpit), Hobb managed to slay [Shrykos](wiki:Shrykos) when he leapt on the [dragon](wiki:Dragon)'s neck, driving his axe into the beast's skull as Shrykos tried to shake him off. Hobb locked his legs around the dragon's neck and continued to rain blows on its skull, calling out the names of the [Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven). The seventh blow (known as [the Stranger](wiki:Stranger_(the_Seven))'s blow) ended Shrykos's life.(wiki:Hobb_the_Hewer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Grand Maester [Munkun](wiki:Munkun) wrote that Hobb slew the dragon [Syrax](wiki:Syrax) as well, but most historians concur this is highly unlikely. It is unknown if Hobb survived the collapsing of the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit).(wiki:Hobb_the_Hewer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
