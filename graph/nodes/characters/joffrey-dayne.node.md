## Origins

In [10 AC](wiki:10_AC) during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen)'s attempt to conquer [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), Lord [Fowler](wiki:House_Fowler) captured [Nightsong](wiki:Nightsong). When Ser [Addam Hightower](wiki:Addam_Hightower) led a strong host to retake the castle, Ser Joffrey marched an army from [Starfall](wiki:Starfall) to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). Unable to breach the city's walls, Joffrey had the fields, farms, and villages burned for twenty leagues around the city.(wiki:Joffrey_Dayne.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)(wiki:Joffrey_Dayne.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) When Lord [Manfred Hightower](wiki:Manfred_Hightower_(Aegon's_Conquest))'s younger son [Garmon](wiki:Garmon_Hightower) led a sortie against Dayne, Joffrey slew the boy. By the time Addam returned to Oldtown, Joffrey's army had melted back into the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains).(wiki:Joffrey_Dayne.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

In response to Ser Joffrey's attack, Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) and her [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar), burned Starfall.(wiki:Joffrey_Dayne.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)(wiki:Joffrey_Dayne.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
