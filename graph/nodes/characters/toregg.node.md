## Appearances & Description

Toregg is a foot taller than his father, [Tormund](wiki:Tormund),(wiki:Toregg.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) and he has a deep voice.(wiki:Toregg.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Toregg is one of the members of [Tormund](wiki:Tormund)'s war party that [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) is attached to. Toregg, [Dormund](wiki:Dormund), and [Munda](wiki:Munda) join their father and [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte) in singing "[The Last of the Giants](wiki:The_Last_of_the_Giants)".(wiki:Toregg.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) When [Longspear Ryk](wiki:Ryk) comes to 'steal' Toregg's sister, Munda, Toregg sleeps through the fight.(wiki:Toregg.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Toregg is with his father during a parley with Jon, [Leathers](wiki:Leathers), and [Hareth](wiki:Hareth_(Mole's_Town)), and he brings [Val](wiki:Val)'s horse to her.(wiki:Toregg.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Toregg is with his younger brother, [Dryn](wiki:Dryn), Tormund, and Jon as the wildlings pass the [Wall](wiki:Wall) at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Toregg.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Toregg stops the fighting of two wildling men in the long line, and Tormund tasks his son with hurrying their people through the Wall.(wiki:Toregg.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Toregg burns the bodies of the wildlings that died while encamped on the north side of the Wall, and he leads Tormund's rear guard of a dozen warriors.(wiki:Toregg.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

Tormund settles his people at [Oakenshield](wiki:Oakenshield), and Toregg reports to Leathers that Tormund will provide Jon with eighty fighting men. Tormund eventually brings only fifty, however.(wiki:Toregg.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Leathers thinks Toregg has taken a liking to one of [Monster](wiki:Monster)'s milkmaids, although Jon believes Toregg prefers [Val](wiki:Val).(wiki:Toregg.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Toregg is present in the [Shieldhall](wiki:Shieldhall) when Jon announces [the letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) purportedly from [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) entitled 'Bastard'.(wiki:Toregg.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
