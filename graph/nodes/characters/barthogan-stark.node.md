## Identity

Barthogan Stark, also called Barth Blacksword, was the Lord of Winterfell and head of House Stark during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen (wiki:Barthogan_Stark).

## Origins

Born between 139 and 182 AC. Died between 184 and 209 AC. Fourth son of Lord Cregan Stark, the third by his last wife Lady Lynara Stark (track_b: Father, track_b: Mother).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Barthogan became Lord of Winterfell after the death of his brother Jonnel, who had no issue. He was killed when the Skagosi rose up against Winterfell during the reign of King Daeron II. He was succeeded by his younger brother Brandon (wiki:Barthogan_Stark). Barthogan is entombed in the crypts of Winterfell (wiki:Barthogan_Stark.cite_ref-Racok69).
