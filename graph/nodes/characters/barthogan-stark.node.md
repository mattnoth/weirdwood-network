## Origins

Barthogan became [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell) after the death of his brother [Jonnel](wiki:Jonnel_Stark), who had no issue. Barthogan was killed when [Skagosi](wiki:Skagos) rose up against [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) during the reign of King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Barthogan_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_stoneborn_of_skagos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Barthogan is entombed in the [crypts of Winterfell](wiki:Crypts_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Barthogan_Stark.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) He was succeeded as Lord of Winterfell by his younger brother, [Brandon](wiki:Brandon_Stark_(son_of_Cregan)).(wiki:Barthogan_Stark.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_stark_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
