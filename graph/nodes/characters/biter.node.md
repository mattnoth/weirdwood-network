## Origins

According to a semi-canon source, Biter was found as an orphaned child by [Rorge](wiki:Rorge), who raised him ferally to fight in Rorge's [dog](wiki:Dog) and [bear](wiki:Bear) [fighting pit](wiki:Rat_pits) in [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom).(wiki:Biter.cite_ref-Rssm1400.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Biter.cite_ref-Rssm1401.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Appearances & Description

Biter is enormous and bald, with soft, doughy flesh. His tongue has been cut out and he does not speak, except to hiss. His teeth have been filed into points.(wiki:Biter.cite_ref-Racok5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) His weight is enough to make a [courser](wiki:Horse) look half-ready to collapse when riding.(wiki:Biter.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Biter is one of three known criminals imprisoned in the [black cells](wiki:Black_cells) of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Biter.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Yoren recruits him for the Night's Watch, but considers him so dangerous that he keeps him chained in a [wagon](wiki:Wagon), along with [Jaqen H'ghar](wiki:Jaqen_H'ghar) and [Rorge](wiki:Rorge), for the journey to the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Biter.cite_ref-Racok1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

When Yoren's band is [attacked](wiki:Storming_of_the_Gods_Eye_town_holdfast) at the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye_town) by raiders led by Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch), Biter and his companions find themselves trapped in the wagon in the middle of a fire, but [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), before escaping herself, throws an axe into the wagon so that they could break free and save themselves.(wiki:Biter.cite_ref-Racok14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Biter and his companions manage to escape and join Ser Amory's raiding party for a time, eventually arriving at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Biter.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

The three men remain in the garrison holding Harrenhal after [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) departs the castle. They help Arya free the captive [northmen](wiki:North) during the [fall of Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal), though Rorge and Biter do it reluctantly and only because Jaqen requested their help. With Lorch's men slaughtered, Rorge and Biter join the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions).(wiki:Biter.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Rorge tells Jaime that Biter chewed the breasts and nipples off a [septa](wiki:Septa) sometime during their foraging and pillaging as part of the Brave Companions.(wiki:Biter.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Feast for Crows

After the death of [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat), leader of the Brave Companions, Biter travels with Rorge alongside remnants of the Companions and participates in the [sacking of Saltpans](wiki:Sack_of_Saltpans).

At the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads), after Rorge duels with and is slain by [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth), Biter quickly overpowers Brienne and begins savagely biting her face. He is stopped by [Gendry](wiki:Gendry), who drives a spear through the back of his neck, killing him.(wiki:Biter.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
