## Origins

During the wars between the [driftwood kings](wiki:Driftwood_king) and the [Kings of the Rock](wiki:Kings_of_the_Rock), Herrock the Whoreson used [his horn](wiki:Horn_of_Herrock) as a signal for the whores of [Kayce](wiki:Kayce) to open a postern gate. His men then captured the town for [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) of [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).
