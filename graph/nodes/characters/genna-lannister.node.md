## Origins

### Youth

Genna was born in [245 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-calculation_1-1) to [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister), [Lord of Casterly Rock](wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock), and his wife, Lady [Jeyne Marbrand](wiki:Jeyne_Marbrand).(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_lannister_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) She had two older brothers, [Tywin](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) and [Kevan](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), and would later have two younger brothers, [Tygett](wiki:Tygett_Lannister) and [Gerion](wiki:Gerion_Lannister), as well.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_targaryen_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### Marriage

Genna's father, Lord [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister), wanted to be liked and loved.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Sensing that weakness, [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey), [Lord of the Crossing](wiki:Lord_of_the_Crossing) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), convinced Tytos to [betroth](wiki:Marriage) his only daughter to one of Walder's sons.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4) As such, in [252 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest),(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-11) at the young age of seven,(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5)(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rawoiafgenna_lannister.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Genna was betrothed to [Emmon Frey](wiki:Emmon_Frey), Walder's second son and seven years her senior. This was announced at a feast at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) with half the [nobility](wiki:Nobility) of the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) in attendance. Genna's eldest brother, [Tywin](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), spoke out against the match. While Lord Tytos went white as [mare](wiki:Horse)'s milk, the betrothal remained standing.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-6)(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2) Although Genna did not approve of all Tywin did, nor enjoyed the company of the man he later became, Genna loved her brother for having been the only person to speak out against her betrothal.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-7)

Although Genna herself had not been satisfied with her husband either, she has given Emmon four sons: [Cleos](wiki:Cleos_Frey), [Lyonel](wiki:Lyonel_Frey), [Tion](wiki:Tion_Frey), and [Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Emmon)). Genna claims that all four boys are Emmon's, and none dare to question her, least of all Emmon.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-8) Following their [marriage](wiki:Marriage), Genna and Emmon remained at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### Aunt Genna

Genna was present for the birth of [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) and [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), the twins of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) and Lady [Joanna Lannister](wiki:Joanna_Lannister).(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-9) After Joanna died giving birth to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) in [273 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest),(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-3)(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Genna took on a somewhat maternal role for the three children. She was always pinching Jaime's ear.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-10)

In [276 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), during the [tourney](wiki:Tournament_in_honor_of_Viserys's_birth) Tywin organized in the west to celebrate the birth of Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen), Genna confided in Cersei that the plan was that her betrothal to the crown prince, [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), a match Lord Tywin had desired for his daughter for years, would be announced during the final feast. When no such announcement was made and King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) had left the westerlands, Cersei ran to Genna with her questions, at which point Genna confided in her niece that Tywin's proposal had been refused by Aerys. She promised Cersei that her father would find her a better husband than Rhaegar.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Cersei eventually wed King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) after [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-4)

When Genna told Tywin that his youngest son, Tyrion, a [dwarf](wiki:Dwarf) he strongly disliked and blamed for Joanna's death, resembled him the most, Tywin grew so angry he refused to speak to Genna for half a year.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-11)

Genna had been her father's "precious little princess", and later her brother Tywin's as well. However, according to her, she fell out of Tywin's favor when she "disappointed him",(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-12) although it is not known what Genna did.

## Appearances & Description

In her youth, Genna was shapely, always threatening to overflow her bodice. However, as she aged, she became fat. She now has a square figure, and an enormous bosom, with a broad and smooth face.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Genna is intelligent and shrewd, loving and sarcastic.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) She bosses around her husband, Ser [Emmon Frey](wiki:Emmon_Frey), whom she considers inept.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) Her personality is such that she is able to claim a place in Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s war council without opposition,(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) which is uncommon for a woman. Genna is one of the few family members who recognizes [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s talent for political maneuvering.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Genna's third son, [Tion Frey](wiki:Tion_Frey), is murdered by [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark), [Lord of Karhold](wiki:Lord_of_Karhold), at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) while a captive of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Genna's eldest son, Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey), is killed near [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) by outlaws while escorting Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

[Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully), [Lord of Riverrun](wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun), is attainted by King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) after the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding). Riverrun and its lands and incomes are granted to Ser [Emmon Frey](wiki:Emmon_Frey) and his wife, Lady Genna.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Riverrun is still held by Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully), however, so the castle is placed under siege.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Rasos74.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Genna and Emmon are present for the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), which is led by Emmon's nephew, Ser [Ryman Frey](wiki:Ryman_Frey), and the newly appointed [Warden of the West](wiki:Warden_of_the_West), Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister). Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) sends her brother [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard), to resolve the siege.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) When Genna declares that Cleos's remains should be laid to rest at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), Jaime lies that he will have his cousin's bones sent home, as the remains have been lost during war.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-13) Genna tells Jaime that his younger brother [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) was most like their father Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), while Jaime himself is not, and that this is what scares her the most, following Tywin's death.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-14)

Genna advises Jaime to execute the captive Edmure, as she considers him a danger. She also sees a potential danger in Edmure's unborn child, as well as Edmure's uncle, Brynden the Blackfish, not considering Lannister hold over Riverrun to be safe as long as a male [Tully](wiki:House_Tully) heir remains alive. She believes if Edmure's unborn child is a girl, the child could marry her grandson, [Tywin Frey](wiki:Tywin_Frey), thereby uniting the [Frey](wiki:House_Frey) and Tully lines. However, she would have preferred control of [Darry](wiki:Darry) instead of Riverrun, despite it being less rich than Riverrun, as [House Darry](wiki:House_Darry) is extinct in the male line, as opposed to House Tully.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-15)

Genna is present during Jaime's war council near Riverrun. Following the departure from the tent of the other [lords](wiki:Lord), Genna asks Jaime what he means to do about the danger that Edmure himself represents, but Jaime only states that Edmure be left to him.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) Jaime wonders to himself whether Genna would agree whether he sounds like Tywin, after he threatens the life of Edmure's unborn child.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

Edmure eventually surrenders Riverrun, but he allows his uncle Brynden to escape before the Lannister soldiers enter the castle. When Emmon complains that Brynden might return to claim Riverrun, Genna informs him that he must hold the castle himself, or destroy it and return to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock). Genna suggests that [Riverrun's guards](wiki:House_Tully_guards) could be questioned about Brynden's whereabouts, but Jaime refuses, having promised Edmure that the garrison would not be harmed if the castle yields peacefully. When Jaime agrees to allow the garrison to leave, Genna is skeptical they will keep to their vows never to take up arms against [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister). Genna and Emmon hold Riverrun with a garrison of their own, consisting of two hundred men.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

[Tom of Sevenstreams](wiki:Tom_of_Sevenstreams) remains at Riverrun after the Frey and Lannister hosts depart.(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2) Unbeknownst to Genna and Emmon, however, the singer is affiliated with the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), now led by [Lady Stoneheart](wiki:Lady_Stoneheart).(wiki:Genna_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
