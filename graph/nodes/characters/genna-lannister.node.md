## Identity

Genna Lannister is a noblewoman of House Lannister, the only daughter of Lord Tytos Lannister and Lady Jeyne Marbrand, and the younger sister of Tywin Lannister. She is wed to Ser Emmon Frey (wiki:Genna_Lannister).

## Origins

Born 245 AC at Casterly Rock (track_b: Born). Daughter of Tytos Lannister and Jeyne Marbrand (track_b: Father, Mother).

## Appearances & Description

In her youth, Genna was shapely, always threatening to overflow her bodice. She has since become fat, with a square figure, enormous bosom, and a broad smooth face. She is intelligent, shrewd, loving, and sarcastic. She bosses around her husband Emmon Frey, whom she considers inept. She is able to claim a place in Jaime's war council without opposition, which is uncommon for a woman (wiki:Genna_Lannister).

## Narrative Arc

Genna was betrothed at age seven to Emmon Frey by her father Tytos, an act considered beneath House Lannister — the Freys were not then considered a prominent house. Ten-year-old Tywin reportedly said "That is not a fit match" to his father in the hearing of all, the only person to oppose the marriage. After Tywin's wife Joanna died, Genna took on a maternal role for Cersei, Jaime, and Tyrion (wiki:Genna_Lannister).

Genna is one of the few family members who recognizes Tyrion's talent for governance. She famously told Jaime that Tyrion is Tywin's true son — "Jaime, sweetling, I have known you since you were a babe at Joanna's breast. You smile like Gerion and fight like Tyg, and there's some of Kevan in you else you would not wear that cloak... but Tyrion is Tywin's son, not you" (wiki:Genna_Lannister).

During the siege of Riverrun in AFFC, Genna is present at Jaime's camp and participates in his war council. After the castle's surrender, Genna and Emmon are installed as the new Lord and Lady of Riverrun (wiki:Genna_Lannister).
