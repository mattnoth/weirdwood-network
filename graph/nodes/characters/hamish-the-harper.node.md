## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Hamish is chosen as one of the seven singers who will sing at the [wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and Lady [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), to compete for a prize of a [gilded](wiki:Gold) [lute](wiki:Lute) with [silver](wiki:Silver) strings. [Symon Silver Tongue](wiki:Symon_Silver_Tongue) tries to blackmail [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) into dismissing Hamish for himself, claiming that Hamish's age makes him often forget what he is singing. Instead, Tyrion arranges for his man [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) to tell Symon that Hamish has broken his arm and that he must be fitted for new clothes for court, to convince Symon to go with him before Bronn [makes him disappear](wiki:Bowl_of_brown).(wiki:Hamish_the_Harper.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
Hamish writes a new song for the wedding, called "[Lord Renly's Ride](wiki:Lord_Renly's_Ride)", which makes Margaery teary-eyed, and makes Tyrion think he has likely won the gilded lute. Hamish also sings "[A Rose of Gold](wiki:A_Rose_of_Gold)" to flatter the [Tyrells](wiki:House_Tyrell), "[The Rains of Castamere](wiki:The_Rains_of_Castamere)" to flatter [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), "[Maiden, Mother, and Crone](wiki:Maiden,_Mother,_and_Crone)" for the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Tyrion's)), and "[My Lady Wife](wiki:My_Lady_Wife)" for the romantics.(wiki:Hamish_the_Harper.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Hamish plays for Queen Margaery and her entourage once a fortnight.(wiki:Hamish_the_Harper.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) As part of [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s plans to remove Margaery with a false claim of adultery, she has an arrest warrant signed for Hamish. While being arrested, Hamish faints. He is consigned to the dungeons, where he continually requests a [maester](wiki:Maester), as he has trouble breathing. Cersei realizes that Hamish is too old to be credible as a lover to Margaery, and tells [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn) to inform Hamish he can have a maester if he falsely confesses to having played for Margaery while she entertained her actual lovers. Qyburn tells Cersei he will help Hamish remember the details.(wiki:Hamish_the_Harper.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) reports to Cersei that Hamish has died. It is unknown whether Hamish died from his illness or from Qyburn's [methods](wiki:Torture).(wiki:Hamish_the_Harper.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
