## Identity

Lord Toad was a fool at Casterly Rock during the time of Lord Gerold the Golden (wiki:Lord_Toad). His real name is unknown.

## Appearances & Description

Lord Toad was a hunchback, old, with a scathingly acidic wit (wiki:Lord_Toad.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf).

## Narrative Arc

When Lord Gerold Lannister retreated into grief, Lady Ellyn Reyne held sway at Casterly Rock, lavishing her kin with offices and honors. Lord Toad quipped: "Lady Ellyn must surely be a sorceress, for she has made it rain inside the Rock all year" (wiki:Lord_Toad.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf). He was whipped for the jape on Ellyn's command. After Ser Tion's death in the Fourth Blackfyre Rebellion, Lord Toad rejoiced that the "Reign of the Reynes" was over and was merciless in his mockery of Ellyn when her false pregnancy was exposed (wiki:Lord_Toad.cite_ref-semi_canon).
