## Origins

During the [Storming of the Dragonpit](wiki:Storming_of_the_Dragonpit), Ser Gyles was one of the [Seven Who Rode](wiki:Seven_Who_Rode), men sent by Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) to recover her son, Prince [Joffrey Velaryon](wiki:Joffrey_Velaryon), as he tried to save the [dragons](wiki:Dragon) from the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit). Joffrey was killed after falling off the dragon [Syrax](wiki:Syrax), however, with a mob tearing apart his body.
