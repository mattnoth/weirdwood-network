## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Willow accompanies [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) along with five other [spearwives](wiki:Spearwife) to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) on a mission to free "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)" (the girl is actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)) who is to wed [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow). Mance disguises himself as "Abel", a singer, and the six spearwives pretend to be his mother, wife, two daughters, and two sisters, who perform the instruments with him. Abel and the spearwives play at the wedding feast.(wiki:Willow_Witch-eye.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

After several men-at-arms and squires are found dead at Winterfell, [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) disbelieves the spearwives' claims of innocence.(wiki:Willow_Witch-eye.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Willow_Witch-eye.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The spearwives enlist the help of a reluctant and fearful Theon in freeing Jeyne.(wiki:Willow_Witch-eye.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Willow_Witch-eye.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) The six spearwives, disguised as maids and accompanied by Theon, walk into Jeyne's room under the pretext of bringing hot water so Jeyne can have a bath. As they fight their way out of the castle, Willow and the other spearwives are taken down one by one by the [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) men.(wiki:Willow_Witch-eye.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

According to [a letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) received by [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), Ramsay has captured Mance and has him wearing as a cloak made from the skins of the six women who accompanied him to Winterfell.(wiki:Willow_Witch-eye.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
