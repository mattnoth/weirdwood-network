## Origins

Lucas served [Wyman Webber](wiki:Wyman_Webber), [Lord of Coldmoat](wiki:Lord_of_Coldmoat). The two men laughed after Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey), Knight of [Standfast](wiki:Standfast), asked Wyman about betrothing their children, [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber) and [Addam Osgrey](wiki:Addam_Osgrey). On his deathbed, Wyman tasked Lucas with protecting Rohanne from unworthy suitors. Lucas remained as castellan of [Coldmoat](wiki:Coldmoat) after Wyman's passing. Rohanne worried that Lucas would attempt to marry her by force.(wiki:Lucas_Inchfield.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-10)

When Eustace and Lady Rohanne feuded over the [Chequy Water](wiki:Chequy_Water), Ser Lucas informed Eustace about the death of [Dake](wiki:Dake). Lucas was present in the yard at Coldmoat when Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) arrived to discuss terms with Rohanne, and the castellan took an immediate dislike to the taller [hedge knight](wiki:Hedge_knight). Lucas presented Duncan to Lady [Helicent Uffering](wiki:Helicent_Uffering), letting him believe that it was Rohanne he was talking to and making a fool out of Duncan. He was dismissed by Rohanne and told to find Maester [Cerrick](wiki:Cerrick).(wiki:Lucas_Inchfield.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-11)

Lucas commanded the force brought by Rohanne to the [Chequy Water](wiki:Chequy_Water) to take Ser [Bennis](wiki:Bennis) by force. He championed [House Webber](wiki:House_Webber) in the [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat), expecting that he would marry Rohanne after defeating Duncan. The Longinch was stabbed and drowned in the Chequy Water by the hedge knight in their duel, however.(wiki:Lucas_Inchfield.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-12)

## Appearances & Description

Lucas was between forty and fifty years old when he died. He was a big man estimated to be six feet and seven inches, sinewy rather than muscular but standing straight as a lance. Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) considered Lucas remarkably ugly, with a broad nose, thick lips, crooked yellow teeth, and protruding eyes.(wiki:Lucas_Inchfield.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)

The Longinch had a cool and proud demeanor, but he was also cruel. He looked down upon [hedge knights](wiki:Hedge_knight).(wiki:Lucas_Inchfield.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)

Lucas wore black silk and cloth-of-silver, and his armor was black enameled plate and silvery ringmail. The Longinch's helm was decorated with an [onyx](wiki:Onyx) spider to represent [House Webber](wiki:House_Webber), and his shield depicted the arms of [House Inchfield](wiki:House_Inchfield). Lucas wielded [sword](wiki:Sword) and two-handed poleaxe. His [chestnut courser](wiki:Horse) was clad in crinet, chanfron, and a coat of light chain.(wiki:Lucas_Inchfield.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-9)
