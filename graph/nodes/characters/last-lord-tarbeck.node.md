## Origins

Lord [Walderan Tarbeck](wiki:Walderan_Tarbeck) was executed during the [Reyne-Tarbeck rebellion](wiki:Reyne-Tarbeck_rebellion), and his wife, Lady [Ellyn](wiki:Ellyn_Reyne), and last surviving son, [Tion the Red](wiki:Tion_Tarbeck), died during the assault on [Tarbeck Hall](wiki:Tarbeck_Hall) by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister). Ellyn's daughters, [Rohanne](wiki:Rohanne_Tarbeck) and [Cyrelle](wiki:Cyrelle_Tarbeck), were taken captive and forced to join the [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters). Rohanne's husband had been executed alongside Walderan, and their three-year-old son disappeared during the assault. Although the boy was probably thrown down a well by Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch), one of Tywin's [knights](wiki:Knight), some stories claim the boy was smuggled to [Essos](wiki:Essos) and became a bard popular for his sad ballads.(wiki:Last_Lord_Tarbeck.cite_ref-sample_1-6)
