## Origins

Sellswords helped the [Golden Empire of Yi Ti](wiki:Golden_Empire_of_Yi_Ti) conquer [Leng](wiki:Leng) centuries ago.(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_bones_and_beyond:_leng.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
During the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), some [Andals](wiki:Andals) were hired as mercenaries by [First Men](wiki:First_Men) to fight their rivals, only to betray their patrons.(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) Andals and sellswords helped to bring down [House Greyiron](wiki:House_Greyiron) in the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_iron_islands:_the_iron_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) Lord [Torrence Teague](wiki:Torrence_Teague) used wealth raided from the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) to hire sellswords from [Essos](wiki:Essos), allowing him to eventually become [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)
Many free companies were formed during the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood), the aftermath of the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_the_doom_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1) The [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) often hired the companies for their endless wars in the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands) and the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-SSMMercenaries_1-5) Likewise, most sellsail fleets were formed during the wars of [Lys](wiki:Lys), [Myr](wiki:Myr), and [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_the_quarrelsome_daughters:_myr.2C_lys.2C_and_tyrosh.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-2)
The [Bright Banners](wiki:Bright_Banners) and the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons) fled instead of facing the [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar) of Khal [Temmo](wiki:Temmo), but the [Three Thousand of Qohor](wiki:Three_Thousand_of_Qohor) held off the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)
Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) hired sellswords for his [war for the Stepstones](wiki:War_for_the_Stepstones).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Rtrp.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0) During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Ser [Perkin the Flea](wiki:Perkin_the_Flea) granted [knighthood](wiki:Knight) to any sellswords who supported [Trystane Truefyre](wiki:Trystane_Truefyre), and mercenaries flocked to support [Hugh Hammer](wiki:Hugh_Hammer).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) The collapse of the [Triarchy](wiki:Triarchy) greatly increased demand for mercenaries in Essos, causing surplus [northmen](wiki:Northmen) after the [Hour of the Wolf](wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf) and other Westerosi to found new free companies.(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)
The [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) was founded by Ser [Aegor Rivers](wiki:Aegor_Rivers) and other [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) exiles in the aftermath of the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_daeron_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0) Ser [Lucas Inchfield](wiki:Lucas_Inchfield) considered Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) and Ser [Bennis of the Brown Shield](wiki:Bennis_of_the_Brown_Shield) to have been sellswords when they served Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey) as sworn swords.(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)
The peace between [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) and [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) prohibits the Pentoshi from hiring sellswords or free companies.(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_pentos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0) [House Baelish](wiki:House_Baelish) descends from a Braavosi sellsword.(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) ponders abdicating the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) and fighting as a sellsword.(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0) Hiring an army of sellswords is not as expensive as hiring a [Faceless Man](wiki:Faceless_Man) to kill a merchant.(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Ragot33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)
The sellswords [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) and [Chiggen](wiki:Chiggen) help [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) arrest [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) in hope of reward, and Bronn defends Tyrion in his [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat) in hope of gold from [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Ragot42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0)
Sellswords and freeriders flock to the service of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) at the Rock as tensions rise in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0) Similar men join the host of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) hires sellswords and sellsails from [Lys](wiki:Lys) and [Myr](wiki:Myr) in preparation for the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0)(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0)
Ser [Stafford Lannister](wiki:Stafford_Lannister) raises a new host from sellswords, freeriders, and boys from [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0) The host is destroyed by Robb in the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross), however.(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0)
Tywin employs the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions) as reavers and foragers in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat) betrays the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister), however, and his Bloody Mummers aid in the [fall of Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

The Mummers abandon the wounded Vargo almost to a man, allowing Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) to easily [take Harrenhal](wiki:Capture_of_Harrenhal).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-0)
Following the [fall of Astapor](wiki:Fall_of_Astapor) to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) hires the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons) and the [Stormcrows](wiki:Stormcrows).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-0) [Daario Naharis](wiki:Daario_Naharis) brings the Stormcrows to Daenerys's side, however, and they are victorious in the [battle near Yunkai](wiki:Battle_near_Yunkai).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-1) [Ben Plumm](wiki:Ben_Plumm) leads the defeated Second Sons who join Daenerys after the battle.(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_48-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

The [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company) breaks its contract with [Myr](wiki:Myr), the first time it has ever done so.(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_49-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After the [fall of Meereen](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen) to Daenerys, Yunkai hires several free companies to defeat her.(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_50-0) [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), sends his son, Prince [Quentyn](wiki:Quentyn_Martell), to [Essos](wiki:Essos) to ally with and wed Daenerys.(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_51-0) Quentyn and his companions, Ser [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater) and Ser [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood), join the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown) during their journey.(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
Ben leads the Seconds Sons from Daenerys's service over to the Yunkai'i.(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_52-0) Tyrion and Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) join the company during the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_53-0) After Daenerys disappears from Meereen, Quentyn allies with the [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince) in a failed scheme to claim [dragons](wiki:Dragon).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_54-0)(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_55-0)
Supporting [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)), [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) and [Harry Strickland](wiki:Harry_Strickland) lead the [landing of the Golden Company](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company) in the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands).(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1) There are reports of sellswords landing throughout southern Westeros.(wiki:Mercenary.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_56-0)
