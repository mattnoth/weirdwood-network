## Identity

Longwater Pyke is an ironborn bastard serving in the crew of Victarion Greyjoy aboard the *Iron Victory* (wiki:Longwater_Pyke). The surname "Pyke" marks him as a bastard born in the Iron Islands.

## Allegiances

He is `SWORN_TO` House Greyjoy (track_b: Allegiance). He is `CULTURE_OF` the ironborn (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Longwater serves aboard the *Iron Victory* during Victarion's voyage to Slaver's Bay. He is among the crew members who confront Moqorro, the red priest found floating in the sea, when the priest is brought aboard. He expresses suspicion of the foreigner (wiki:Longwater_Pyke.cite_ref-Radwd56, wiki:Longwater_Pyke.cite_ref-Radwd63).
