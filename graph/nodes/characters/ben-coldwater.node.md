### The Winds of Winter

Ben attends the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) to select the members of the newly-created [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights). During the feast at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon), after musicians begin playing for the dance, he inquires into the lack of singers and is the first of the knights to ask Alayne Stone ([Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark)) to dance with him.(wiki:Ben_Coldwater.cite_ref-Winds_1-1)
