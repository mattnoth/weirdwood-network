## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Erena is captured, along with her brother, [Gawen](wiki:Gawen_Glover), and mother, [Sybelle](wiki:Sybelle_Glover), when the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) [take Deepwood Motte](wiki:Battle_of_Deepwood_Motte).(wiki:Erena_Glover.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords

The Glovers are held captive at [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) by [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy).(wiki:Erena_Glover.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Feast for Crows

The Glovers are taken to [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers) in the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) by Asha. Along the way they stop at the [Stony Shore](wiki:Stony_Shore) to search for a [wet nurse](wiki:Wet_nurse) for Erena; instead they find a goat.(wiki:Erena_Glover.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

While Asha takes Sybelle back to Deepwood Motte, Erena and her brother are left captive at Ten Towers under the care of [Gwynesse Harlaw](wiki:Gwynesse_Harlaw). Asha fears Erena too unhealthy for a second sea voyage.(wiki:Erena_Glover.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
