## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Errok is part of [Jarl](wiki:Jarl)'s group of raiders who climbs the [Wall](wiki:Wall) to take [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) from behind. He leads a group of four raiders to the top of Wall.(wiki:Errok.cite_ref-Rasos30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) After Jarl's death, Errok follows [Styr](wiki:Styr)'s commands. Together with [Del](wiki:Del), he goes ahead to scout the ruins of [Queenscrown](wiki:Queenscrown). When he returns to the other raiders, he reports that there's an old man with a horse present in the town.(wiki:Errok.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
