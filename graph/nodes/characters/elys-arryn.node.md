## Identity

Elys Arryn — Eldest daughter of Lord Rodrik, three years older than her stepmother Daella, with whom she clashed from the first.

## Origins

Elys was the eldest child of Lord [Rodrik Arryn](wiki:Rodrik_Arryn) and his first wife. She had three siblings: a sister, [Amanda](wiki:Amanda_Arryn) and two brothers. After her mother died, her father married again to Princess [Daella Targaryen](wiki:Daella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Jaehaerys_I)), who was three years younger than her. The two of them clashed from the first.(wiki:Elys_Arryn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) visited [the Eyrie](wiki:The_Eyrie) when Princess Daella was pregnant. Elys was dismissive, telling the queen, "You would think she was the first woman ever to have a baby," for the princess burst into tears for the slightest reason, and sometimes no reason at all. Daella died in childbirth, giving Elys a younger half-sister, [Aemma Arryn](wiki:Aemma_Arryn).(wiki:Elys_Arryn.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
