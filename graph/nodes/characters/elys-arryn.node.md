## Identity

Elys Arryn was a noblewoman from House Arryn during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen (wiki:Elys_Arryn).

## Origins

Born 61 AC (track_b: Born). Eldest child of Rodrik Arryn and his first wife (track_b: Father).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Arryn (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Valemen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Elys was the eldest daughter of Lord Rodrik Arryn. She had a sister Amanda and two brothers. When her father married Princess Daella Targaryen (three years younger than Elys), the two clashed from the start. Elys was dismissive of Daella's pregnancy anxieties, telling Queen Alysanne, "You would think she was the first woman ever to have a baby." Daella died in childbirth, giving Elys a younger half-sister, Aemma Arryn (wiki:Elys_Arryn).
