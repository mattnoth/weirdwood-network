## Origins

### Lord of the Tides

After his father, Lord [Daemon](wiki:Daemon_Velaryon), died in [battle](wiki:Battle_in_the_waters_off_Gulltown) near [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown) in [2 BC](wiki:Years_before_Aegon's_Conquest),(wiki:Aethan_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf31.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Aethan succeeded him in his titles and as King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen)'s [lord admiral](wiki:Lord_admiral) and [master of ships](wiki:Master_of_ships).(wiki:Aethan_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-11) This began a tradition that essentially made those offices hereditary with [House Velaryon](wiki:House_Velaryon).(wiki:Aethan_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfab12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

In [22 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Aethan's daughter, Lady [Alyssa](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon), was married to her cousin, Prince [Aenys Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen), as the prince lacked [a sister to wed](wiki:Incest) and the Velaryons were also of [Valyrian](wiki:Valyria) stock.(wiki:Aethan_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-12) In [29 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) and [30 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Lord Aethan was aided by Prince [Maegor Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) and Ser [Osmund Strong](wiki:Osmund_Strong) during campaigns against [Sargoso Saan](wiki:Sargoso_Saan), a pirate-king of the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones).(wiki:Aethan_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-13)

Aethan's daughter became a queen when Aegon I passed away and Aenys succeeded him as [Lord of the Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Lord_of_the_Seven_Kingdoms). After the death of King Aenys, and Maegor's [usurpation](wiki:Usurper) of the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), Queen Alyssa fled to her lord father's [seat](wiki:Castle_Driftmark) on [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark), taking her children with her.(wiki:Aethan_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-14)

### Death and legacy

Aethan passed away during the reign of [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), and was succeeded as Lord of the Tides by his son [Daemon Velaryon](wiki:Daemon_Velaryon_(son_of_Aethan)), who also succeeded him as Maegor's master of ships and lord admiral.(wiki:Aethan_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfab4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-15)

Aethan was the [namesake](wiki:Namesake) for *[Lord Aethan's Glory](wiki:Lord_Aethan's_Glory)*, a notable [warship](wiki:Ship) serving the [Velaryon fleet](wiki:Velaryon_fleet) under Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon).(wiki:Aethan_Velaryon.cite_ref-Rfab18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
