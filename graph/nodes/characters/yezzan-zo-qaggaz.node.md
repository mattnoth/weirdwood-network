## Origins

Yezzan has come to own a [boy with hairy, deformed legs resembling a goat's legs](wiki:Goat_boy), a [bearded woman](wiki:Bearded_woman), a [feeble-minded](wiki:Lackwit) [two-headed girl](wiki:Two-headed_girl) from [Mantarys](wiki:Mantarys), and a hermaphrodite named [Sweets](wiki:Sweets), who also sleeps with him.(wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)(wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4) Yezzan used to own a [giant](wiki:Giant), who he would make copulate with slave women so he could watch. However, the giant died, and Yezzan would pay much [gold](wiki:Gold) to acquire a new one.(wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

According to [Sweets](wiki:Sweets), Yezzan contracted some disease ten years ago while in [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos), and has been dying ever since, "rotting from the inside out". If anyone makes him forget this even for a little while, they are well rewarded by him.(wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

## Appearances & Description

Yezzan has yellow eyes. He is so morbidly obese he can no longer stand, and is described as being large enough to make four of [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis). He is sickly, and cannot hold his water, and so always smells of urine that even perfume cannot hide. He wears yellow [silk](wiki:Silk) [tokars](wiki:Tokar) with gold fringe.(wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

While overweight, he is still shrewd and intelligent, a trait some of the other [Wise Masters](wiki:Wise_Masters) do not share.(wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Yezzan is obsessed with grotesques, and often purchases slaves with physical deformities to add to his "[collection](wiki:Grotesquerie)".(wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and several of his other slaves believe Yezzan to be *"not that bad"* compared to some of the other slavers. He treats his slaves well, as long as they obey and cause no trouble, and his slaves are usually well fed and looked after.(wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) The slaves in Yezzan's collection wear [gilded](wiki:Gold) [iron](wiki:Iron) collars, with his name incised in [Valyrian glyphs](wiki:High_Valyrian); a pair of tiny bells are affixed below the ears, so the wearer produces a tinkling sound with every step.(wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) Described in situ when collars are fitted — "The collars were made of iron, lightly gilded to make them glitter in the light. Yezzan's name was incised into the metal in Valyrian glyphs, and a pair of tiny bells were affixed below the ears" — ADWD Tyrion X, `sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-tyrion-10.md:219`

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Members of the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown), a [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) company that is hired by Yunkai, mock Yezzan as the "Yellow Whale", and gossip about his [collection of grotesques](wiki:Grotesquerie).(wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

When peace is made between [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) and [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai), a slave market is set up outside Meereen.(wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Yezzan purchases several slaves there. [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), [Penny](wiki:Penny), and [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) are also up for sale, and Tyrion forces a bidding war between Yezzan, [Ben Plumm](wiki:Ben_Plumm), the [Girl General](wiki:Girl_General), and [Zahrina](wiki:Zahrina), but Yezzan bids five thousand [silver](wiki:Silver) [pieces](wiki:Currency) for the lot and wins. He gives his new slaves into the charge of his overseer, [Nurse](wiki:Nurse), and Tyrion and Penny become part of Yezzan's grotesquerie.(wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)

Yezzan is one of the few Yunkish lords who wishes to honor the peace between [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) and [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). He dies of the [pale mare](wiki:Pale_mare) that spread throughout the Yunkish siege lines, and several of his slaves use the opportunity to escape, including Tyrion, Penny, and Jorah.(wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Book-cite of his death: "Yezzan was burning with fever, squirming fitfully in a pool of his own excrement. His shit had turned to brown slime streaked with blood" — ADWD Tyrion XI, `sources/chapters/adwd/adwd-tyrion-11.md:13`
