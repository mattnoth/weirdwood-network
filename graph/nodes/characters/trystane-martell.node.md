## Origins

Born in [287 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest),(wiki:Trystane_Martell.cite_ref-Calculation_2-1) Trystane was the youngest child of his father, Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), and mother, Lady [Mellario](wiki:Mellario) of [Norvos](wiki:Norvos).(wiki:Trystane_Martell.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) At the age of four, Trystane suffered from [redspots](wiki:Redspots).(wiki:Trystane_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) While he was still young, his mother left [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) and returned to [Norvos](wiki:Norvos), the city of her birth.(wiki:Trystane_Martell.cite_ref-6)

## Appearances & Description

Trystane has olive skin and straight black hair.(wiki:Trystane_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) is crowned, Trystane is summoned to court, along with his father and brother [Quentyn](wiki:Quentyn_Martell), to swear fealty to the new King.(wiki:Trystane_Martell.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Clash of Kings

The acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) concocts a plan to prevent the Dornish from supporting [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) enemies, by suggesting a [betrothal](wiki:Betrothal) between his niece, Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon), and Trystane. Trystane's father, Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), accepts the betrothal offer, and Myrcella departs [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) together with Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart), a member of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Trystane_Martell.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Trystane_Martell.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) informs his son Tyrion that he has received a letter from Ser Arys, who writes that Trystane is enchanted with Myrcella.(wiki:Trystane_Martell.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Trystane has becomes fond of the game [cyvasse](wiki:Cyvasse), and Myrcella has learned the rules so they can play together. Myrcella wins more often than Trystane, but as Ser Arys Oakheart notes, Trystane does not mind.(wiki:Trystane_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Trystane's older sister, [Arianne](wiki:Arianne_Martell), conspires to have Myrcella crowned as ruling [Queen of the Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Queen_of_the_Seven_Kingdoms), and spirits Myrcella out of [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) with the help of Ser Arys. Myrcella's [bedmaid](wiki:Bedmaid), [Rosamund Lannister](wiki:Rosamund_Lannister), is left behind to impersonate the princess. In order to keep anyone from discovering that Myrcella is gone, Ser Arys has told the maester that Myrcella suffers from redspots. However, this does not prevent Trystane from wanting to visit her, as he already had his own redspots years before.(wiki:Trystane_Martell.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) sends Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) to Sunspear to deliver the [skull](wiki:Mountain's_skull) of Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane). At the welcoming feast, he presents Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell) with a letter by Queen Cersei, in which she requests Myrcella to return home, for Prince Doran to travel to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to take up his seat on the [small council](wiki:Small_council), and for Trystane to come visit King's Landing. Prince Doran agrees with Ser Balon that Trystane will go to King's Landing, but later that evening, during a private meeting with his daughter [Arianne](wiki:Arianne_Martell), nieces [Obara](wiki:Obara_Sand), [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria_Sand) and [Tyene Sand](wiki:Tyene_Sand), and captain of guards [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah), Doran reveals that Queen Cersei's invitation is a ruse. Trystane is supposed to die on the way to King's Landing during an attack in the [Kingswood](wiki:Kingswood), which will be blamed on [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), and Doran is only invited so he can witness the attack personally, absolving Cersei of any blame.(wiki:Trystane_Martell.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
