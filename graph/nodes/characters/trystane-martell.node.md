## Identity

Prince Trystane Nymeros Martell is the youngest child of the ruling Prince of Dorne, Doran Martell, and his wife Lady Mellario of Norvos. He is affectionately called Trys by his older sister Arianne (wiki:Trystane_Martell).

## Origins

Born 287 AC in Sunspear. While still young, his mother left Dorne and returned to Norvos (track_b: Born, Father, Mother, wiki:Trystane_Martell).

## Appearances & Description

## Narrative Arc

When Joffrey is crowned, Trystane is summoned to court with his father and brother to swear fealty. Tyrion Lannister arranges a betrothal between Princess Myrcella Baratheon and Trystane to keep Dorne from supporting Lannister enemies. Doran accepts and Myrcella departs for Sunspear. Arys Oakheart writes that Trystane is enchanted with Myrcella. The two become fond of cyvasse together; Myrcella wins more often but Trystane does not mind. At age four, Trystane had suffered from redspots. In ADWD, Doran reveals plans for Trystane to sit on the small council (wiki:Trystane_Martell).
