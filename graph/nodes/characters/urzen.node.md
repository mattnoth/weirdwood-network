## Appearances & Description

Urzen is a sinewy man with a scrawny throat. He carries a round shield.(wiki:Urzen.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Urzen is among the ironborn who [raid](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore) the [Stony Shore](wiki:Stony_Shore). He and [Black Lorren](wiki:Black_Lorren) silence animals too badly wounded to be saved. (wiki:Urzen.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Urzen is part of [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy)'s force that [captures Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell). Theon has him investigate the [godswood of Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell) when [Summer](wiki:Summer) and [Shaggydog](wiki:Shaggydog) are no longer heard. When the disemboweled body of [Squint](wiki:Squint) is found, Theon mocks and shoves Urzen for not understanding the dangers of [direwolves](wiki:Direwolves).(wiki:Urzen.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

Urzen serves as one of Theon's guards at Winterfell.(wiki:Urzen.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

When [northmen](wiki:Northmen) led by Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) arrive to [retake Winterfell](wiki:Battle_outside_the_gates_of_Winterfell), Theon gives a speech asking ironborn to fight beside him. Urzen shuffles his feet and chooses to depart instead.(wiki:Urzen.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Urzen is a member of the crew of the *[Sea Bitch](wiki:Sea_Bitch)*.(wiki:Urzen.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
