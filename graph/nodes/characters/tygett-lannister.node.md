## Origins

Born as the fourth of Lord [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister)'s children by Lady [Jeyne Marbrand](wiki:Jeyne_Marbrand), Tygett was the third of four brothers. Tyg always felt he was living in the shadow of his eldest brother, [Tywin](wiki:Tywin_Lannister),(wiki:Tygett_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) with whom he always had a difficult relationship.(wiki:Tygett_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Tygett fought in the [War of the Ninepenny Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Ninepenny_Kings) in [260 AC](wiki:260_AC), killing a man during his first battle and three more later in the war, including a knight of the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company). Although Tywin and [Kevan](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) earned [knighthoods](wiki:Knight) from the war, ten-year-old(wiki:Tygett_Lannister.cite_ref-sample_6-0) Tygett was too young to earn his spurs. His courage and skill was remarked upon, however.(wiki:Tygett_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) According to a semi-canon source, Tygett served as a squire when Tywin suppressed the [revolt](wiki:Reyne-Tarbeck_revolt) of Houses [Tarbeck](wiki:House_Tarbeck) and [Reyne](wiki:House_Reyne) in [261 AC](wiki:261_AC).(wiki:Tygett_Lannister.cite_ref-sample_6-1) Tygett was eventually knighted.(wiki:Tygett_Lannister.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_tygett_lannister.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

At sixteen, Tygett visited the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) on his [coming of age](wiki:Coming_of_age) tour.(wiki:Tygett_Lannister.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

By [270 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) had come to disregard Tywin, his [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King). When Tywin tried to name Tygett as the [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms) at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), the king instead chose Ser [Willem Darry](wiki:Willem_Darry).(wiki:Tygett_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Tygett and his younger brother [Gerion](wiki:Gerion_Lannister) were unhorsed by Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) during the [tournament in honor of Viserys's birth](wiki:Tournament_in_honor_of_Viserys's_birth) at [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) in [276 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Tygett_Lannister.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

When young [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) asked for a [dragon](wiki:Dragon) as a present for his [nameday](wiki:Nameday), Tygett upset his nephew by explaining that dragons died out a century before.(wiki:Tygett_Lannister.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Tygett was married to [Darlessa Marbrand](wiki:Darlessa_Marbrand), with whom he had one son, [Tyrek](wiki:Tyrek_Lannister).(wiki:Tygett_Lannister.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Tygett_Lannister.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Tygett eventually died of a [pox](wiki:Pox).(wiki:Tygett_Lannister.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

## Appearances & Description

Tygett was a man of considerable prowess in battle.(wiki:Tygett_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) He had a stormy relationship with his elder brother, [Tywin](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), resenting living in Tywin's shadow.(wiki:Tygett_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Regardless, Tygett was kind to Tywin's second son, [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Tygett_Lannister.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
