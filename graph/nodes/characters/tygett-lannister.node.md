## Identity

Ser Tygett Lannister, called Tyg by his family, was a knight from House Lannister. He was the fourth child and third son of Lord Tytos Lannister and Lady Jeyne Marbrand (wiki:Tygett_Lannister).

## Origins

Born in 250 AC at Casterly Rock (track_b: Born). Son of Tytos Lannister and Jeyne Marbrand (track_b: Father, Mother). Died in or after 285 AC (track_b: Died).

## Appearances & Description

Tygett was a man of considerable prowess in battle (wiki:Tygett_Lannister). He had a stormy relationship with his elder brother Tywin, resenting living in Tywin's shadow. Regardless, Tygett was kind to Tywin's second son Tyrion (wiki:Tygett_Lannister.cite_ref-Racok41).

## Narrative Arc

Tygett fought in the War of the Ninepenny Kings in 260 AC, killing a man in his first battle and three more later including a knight of the Golden Company. Though too young at ten for knighthood, his courage was noted (wiki:Tygett_Lannister.cite_ref-TWOIAF). He served as squire during the Reyne-Tarbeck revolt. At sixteen, he visited the Free Cities on his coming-of-age tour (wiki:Tygett_Lannister).

When Tywin tried to name Tygett master-at-arms at the Red Keep, King Aerys II chose Ser Willem Darry instead (wiki:Tygett_Lannister). Tygett and his brother Gerion attended Tywin's resignation celebration. Tygett eventually died of a pox (wiki:Tygett_Lannister.cite_ref-Raffc33).
