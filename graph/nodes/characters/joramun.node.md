## Origins

According to myth, in ancient days he blew the [Horn of Winter](wiki:Horn_of_Winter) and "woke the [giants](wiki:Giants) from the earth".(wiki:Joramun.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)(wiki:Joramun.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Joramun brought down the [Night's King](wiki:Night's_King) alongside the [King of Winter](wiki:King_in_the_North), [Brandon the Breaker](wiki:Brandon_Stark_(Breaker)).(wiki:Joramun.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Joramun.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_wall_and_beyond:_the_night.E2.80.99s_watch.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

[Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand) lists Joramun among [Kings-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall) who either broke their strength on the [Wall](wiki:Wall), or had their strength broken by the power of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) on the far side.(wiki:Joramun.cite_ref-Racok23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) recalls that [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) had claimed to have found the [Horn of Joramun](wiki:Horn_of_Joramun) in Joramun's grave beneath a glacier, high up in the [Frostfangs](wiki:Frostfangs).(wiki:Joramun.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) However, according to [Tormund](wiki:Tormund), they had found the horn in the grave of a [giant](wiki:Giant).(wiki:Joramun.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
