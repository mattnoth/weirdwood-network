## Origins

Lady Sunderly was wed by a [priest](wiki:Priest_of_the_Drowned_God) of the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God) to Lord [Quellon Greyjoy](wiki:Quellon_Greyjoy), after the death of his [first rock wife](wiki:Lady_Stonetree_(wife_of_Quellon_Greyjoy)).(wiki:Lady_Sunderly_(wife_of_Quellon_Greyjoy).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

She was the mother of [Balon](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Euron](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy), [Urrigon](wiki:Urrigon_Greyjoy) and [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy). After her death, Lord Quellon took a new rock wife, [a daughter](wiki:Lady_Piper_(wife_of_Quellon_Greyjoy)) of Lord [Piper](wiki:House_Piper) of [Pinkmaiden Castle](wiki:Pinkmaiden_Castle).(wiki:Lady_Sunderly_(wife_of_Quellon_Greyjoy).cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-8)(wiki:Lady_Sunderly_(wife_of_Quellon_Greyjoy).cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
