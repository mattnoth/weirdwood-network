## Identity

Prince Morion Nymeros Martell, known as Morion the Mad, was the ruling Prince of Dorne and head of House Martell during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen (wiki:Morion_Martell).

## Origins

Son of the previous Prince of Dorne, who kept Dorne out of Lord Rogar's War in 61 AC (track_b: Father). A young man when he became Prince (wiki:Morion_Martell).

## Narrative Arc

Morion succeeded his elderly father as Prince of Dorne, angered by his father's inaction during Lord Rogar's War. Regarded as rash and foolish by Archmaester Gyldayn, he assembled armies at Ghost Hill and the Tor, loading Dornishmen onto ships of Stepstones pirates, Myrish sellsails, and Pepper Coast corsairs. He intended to invade the stormlands via Cape Wrath. The preparations took nearly a year, during which spies informed King Jaehaerys. Lord Boremund Baratheon awaited the Dornish on shore while Jaehaerys and his sons burned the fleet with dragons. No Dornishman reached shore alive. Morion died in this Fourth Dornish War in 83 AC. He was succeeded by Mara Martell (wiki:Morion_Martell).
