## Identity

Rickon Stark is the youngest child of Lord Eddard Stark and Catelyn Tully, born in 295 AC at Winterfell (track_b: Born, Father, Mother). He holds the title Prince of Winterfell (track_b: Title).

## Origins

Son of Eddard Stark and Catelyn Tully (track_b: Father, Mother). Born at Winterfell in the North (track_b: Born).

## Appearances & Description

At the start of AGOT, Rickon is only three years old. He has Tully coloring like his siblings Robb, Bran, and Sansa (agot-catelyn-03). He is described as red-eyed from crying, defiant, near four years old (agot-bran-06). Jon Snow remembers "little Rickon, bright eyes shining as he begged for a sweet" (agot-jon-03).

## Narrative Arc

Rickon is too young to fully understand the events unfolding around him. When the Stark household begins to fracture — Eddard departing south, Catelyn leaving on her own mission — Rickon becomes increasingly wild and distraught, following Robb around crying (agot-catelyn-03). He refuses to say farewell when Robb marches south (agot-bran-06). Bran later finds him hiding in the Winterfell crypts beside their father's tomb, having had the same prophetic dream as Bran about Eddard's death (agot-bran-07). Named in the decree as a traitor alongside his family (agot-sansa-05). His direwolf Shaggydog grows increasingly feral, reflecting Rickon's own wildness. He is named "the baby" by Jon's count of five trueborn children (agot-bran-01).
