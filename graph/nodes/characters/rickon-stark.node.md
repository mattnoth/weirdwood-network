## Identity

Rickon Stark (b. 295 AC) — child of Eddard Stark; sworn to House Stark; Prince. One of 3 characters named Rickon Stark.

## Origins

Born at Winterfell in [295 AC](wiki:295_AC), Rickon is the third son and youngest child of [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) and [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell). [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Luwin](wiki:Luwin) helped deliver the child.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Rickon's brothers are [Robb](wiki:Robb_Stark) and [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark), while his sisters are [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark). Rickon also has a [bastard](wiki:Bastard) half-brother, [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), from whom Rickon begs sweets.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

## Appearances & Description

Rickon favors his mother [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) in appearance, having the bright blue eyes, auburn hair, and easy smile of a [Tully](wiki:House_Tully).(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Young Rickon is playful, but temperamental and stubborn.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) He has breathless laughter(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) and fiercely tries to be like his older brothers.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Rickon's family often thinks of him as a baby.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Rickon wears an ermine mantle.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) His bedchamber is half a turn below Bran's in a tower at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) allows his children to adopt a litter of [direwolves](wiki:Direwolves) found by [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow).(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Rickon is initially afraid of his pup,(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) which he eventually names [Shaggydog](wiki:Shaggydog).(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) At the feast welcoming King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), Jon prods his half brother on when Rickon stops to visit him during the procession.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

When Eddard departs for [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to serve Robert as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), he leaves his youngest son at Winterfell with the boy's mother, [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), and eldest brother, [Robb](wiki:Robb_Stark).(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1) [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) was supposed to join his sisters, [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark), with Eddard in the capital, but Rickon's older brother becomes comatose after falling from the [First Keep](wiki:First_Keep).(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1)

A grief-stricken Catelyn lingers at Bran's bedside, so Rickon follows Robb around, crying. After Bran wakes from his coma, Catelyn departs Winterfell to inform Eddard of the [catspaw](wiki:Catspaw) assassin, leaving Robb to rule the castle.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Rickon enjoys running with Shaggydog, [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind), and [Summer](wiki:Summer)—the direwolves of Robb and Bran—in the castle's yard.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) Rickon becomes upset whenever Robb departs Winterfell to address matters in the [north](wiki:North).(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) When the direwolves threaten [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), Rickon calls Shaggy back to him.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3)

Having learned that Robb is marching south to rescue their father from imprisonment in King's Landing, an upset Rickon hides in the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell). Rickon slashes at Robb's men with a rusty sword, and Shaggydog wounds [Gage](wiki:Gage) and [Mikken](wiki:Mikken). Rickon cries even more when Shaggy is chained in [Farlen](wiki:Farlen)'s kennel, and the boy refuses to bid farewell when Robb departs Winterfell.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

Bran has a [dream](wiki:Dream) of Eddard in the crypt, so [Osha](wiki:Osha) and [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Luwin](wiki:Luwin) bring the boy to the vaults so he can see that his father's tomb is empty. Shaggydog jumps out of the shadows and wounds Luwin, however, and Summer begins fighting with Shaggy. Four-year-old Rickon, who had freed Shaggy from the kennel, explains that he had also dreamed that Eddard would be coming home. Rickon accompanies the group to Luwin's tower, and the maester shows [obsidian](wiki:Obsidian) arrowheads to the boys. Rickon cries when a [raven](wiki:Raven) then arrives at the tower bringing word that Eddard has been executed by order of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) in the capital.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

After the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), Robb is proclaimed [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) by [northmen](wiki:Northmen) and [rivermen](wiki:Rivermen),(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) and his brothers become princes.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Rickon is initially upset when [Big Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Jammos)) and [Little Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)) arrive at Winterfell as [wards](wiki:Ward), according to the alliance between Houses [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) and [Frey](wiki:House_Frey).(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) When Little Walder knocks the Rickon into a pool while playing [lord of the crossing](wiki:Lord_of_the_crossing), Shaggydog bites him. Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel), the [castellan](wiki:Castellan) of Winterfell, confines Shaggydog and Summer, both direwolves at Winterfell, to the [godswood](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-1)

Rickon then becomes friends with the Frey cousins, playing other games like [monsters and maidens](wiki:Monsters_and_maidens), [rats and cats](wiki:Rats_and_cats), and [come-into-my-castle](wiki:Come-into-my-castle). Bran is angered when his brother shows the crypt of Winterfell to their guests.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-2) Rickon sits near the Freys during the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast).(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)

With his mother Catelyn away in the south, Rickon refuses to have his hair cut, and instead bites a girl who tries to cut his long, unruly hair.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-2) Maester Luwin discounts the dreams which Bran and Rickon experienced before Winterfell was informed of Lord Eddard's death,(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) while [Jojen Reed](wiki:Jojen_Reed) explains he has had [green dreams](wiki:Green_dreams) of the Stark brothers in the crypt(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) and also of the boys being flayed by [Reek](wiki:Ramsay_Snow).(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2)

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) and his [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) raiders [capture](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) Winterfell. Rickon, Bran, and the Reed siblings become his [hostages](wiki:Hostage). [Meera Reed](wiki:Meera_Reed) protects Rickon when [Stygg](wiki:Stygg) kills [Mikken](wiki:Mikken).(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) After [Osha](wiki:Osha), [Hodor](wiki:Hodor), the direwolves, and the Reed and Stark siblings vanish from the castle, Theon and Reek hunt for them in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood) to no avail.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1) The hunters instead kill two [miller's sons](wiki:Miller's_sons) at the [Acorn Water](wiki:Acorn_Water); Reek [flays](wiki:Flay) the children's bodies, and Theon presents their tarred heads to Winterfell as those of the missing Starks.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

In [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), [Varys](wiki:Varys) receives word of Bran and Rickon's murder, and informs acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), who informs his sister, Queen [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister). Both fear that Catelyn Stark will kill Robb's hostage, their brother [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), in revenge. Cersei notes that they still hold their own hostage, [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), and Tyrion reminds her that they must take good care of her.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

At [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), Catelyn receives word of Bran and Rickon's murder, and decides to free Jaime Lannister to exchange for her daughters.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0) During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Sansa Stark sings a prayer for her siblings.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0) At [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) becomes teary-eyed after learning of her brothers' fate.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)

In actuality, Rickon and his companions have secretly hidden from Theon in the crypts, and they survive the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell) by Reek, who is actually [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow).(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The group emerges from the crypts to find Winterfell in ruins. In the godswood, they discover a mortally wounded Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin), who tells them that Bran and Rickon need to be separated and taken into hiding. Osha agrees to look after Rickon, taking the prince and Shaggydog to parts unknown.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Storm of Swords

Traveling north toward the [Wall](wiki:Wall), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) wonders if [Osha](wiki:Osha) took his brother to [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) or the [Last Hearth](wiki:Last_Hearth).(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0)

[Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), the [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), sleeps with [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling) at the [Crag](wiki:Crag) after having been informed of his brothers' supposed deaths. Robb then marries Jeyne, breaking his promise to marry a daughter of Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey).(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)

After Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully) dies at Riverrun, Catelyn thinks her father will be awaited in the afterlife by his grandsons, Bran and Rickon.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0)

When [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) returns to [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) after the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) and spying on a group of [free folk](wiki:Free_folk), Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) informs him of his brothers' murders and of the burning of Winterfell. Jon cannot quite believe Theon would do that.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0)

Sansa, expecting to marry [Willas Tyrell](wiki:Willas_Tyrell), hopes to one day name sons after Rickon, Bran, and Eddard.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0) However, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) forces her to marry [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister),(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0)(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0) so that [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) can one day claim Winterfell.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-1) To prevent that possibility, King Robb names his half brother Jon as his heir.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0)(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Rawoiafrobb_stark.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0) Robb and Catelyn are then murdered by [Freys](wiki:House_Frey) and [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton) in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Most of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) believes Rickon dead, but Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) and [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover) learn from [Wex Pyke](wiki:Wex_Pyke), Theon's onetime [squire](wiki:Squire) and a survivor of the sack of Winterfell, that the boy is alive. Wex tells them—via writing and chalk drawings since he is mute—that Ramsay killed Rodrik Cassel, not Theon, in the [battle at Winterfell](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell), and that Wex followed a boy, a woman, and a direwolf after they left the castle.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-0) Wex learned that Osha was taking Rickon to [Skagos](wiki:Skagos).(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Rawoiafwex_pyke.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-0)

When Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) attempts to convince Lord Manderly to take up [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s cause, Wyman brings Wex into their meeting in the [New Castle](wiki:New_Castle) and explains to Davos what Wex witnessed. Wyman explains that in order for [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) to accept Stannis as king, Davos must use his smuggling skills to retrieve Wyman's liege lord from Skagos—Rickon, the heir of [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) with Robb dead and Bran's fate unknown.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-1)

Ramsay, who is now [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell),(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_48-0) tells his father, [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), that he intends to kill Bran and Rickon with [his hounds](wiki:Bastard's_girls) if the Stark boys reappear.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_49-0)

Theon, who has been [tortured](wiki:Torture) by Ramsay, is hated and considered a [kinslayer](wiki:Kinslayer) for his alleged murder of Bran and Rickon.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_50-0)(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_51-0) Theon has a difficult time remembering what really happened,(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_49-1) but eventually recalls more clearly.(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_50-1)

### The Winds of Winter

[Northmen](wiki:Northmen) in Stannis's army at the [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village) want the king to execute Theon for having killed Bran and Rickon. Theon incoherently tries to explain his true actions to his sister, [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy).(wiki:Rickon_Stark.cite_ref-52)
