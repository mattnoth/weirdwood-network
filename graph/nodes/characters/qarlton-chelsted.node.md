## Origins

### Tourney at Harrenhal

Lord Qarlton was the [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin) late in the reign of King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen),(wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and he was a firm supporter of Aerys over the king's eldest son, Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen). When the [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) was announced, based on the notion that Rhaegar was secretly funding the tourney as a way to call a [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council) to discuss Aerys's rule, Qarlton unsuccessfully suggested to Aerys to forbid the tournament.(wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Qarlton and Aerys eventually attended the tourney, and when Prince Rhaegar won the final joust, Qarlton and Lord [Symond Staunton](wiki:Symond_Staunton) inflamed the king's suspicions by declaring that Rhaegar had taken part of the tourney only to win the favor of the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) and remind the [lords](wiki:Lord) present that he was a true heir to King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen). Qarlton later took Rhaegar's crowning of [Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark) as [queen of love and beauty](wiki:Queen_of_love_and_beauty) at the end of the tourney as further proof of the prince's perfidy.(wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

### Robert's Rebellion

During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Qarlton succeeded to the position of [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) after his predecessor, Lord [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington), had been sent into exile by Aerys following his defeat in the [Battle of the Bells](wiki:Battle_of_the_Bells). While Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) was busy marshaling an army, Qarlton was present during the king's systematic build up and placement of [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) throughout [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), and the suspicious Hand noticed the coming and going of [Belis](wiki:Belis), [Garigus](wiki:Garigus), and [Rossart](wiki:Rossart) of the [Alchemists' Guild](wiki:Alchemists'_Guild).(wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

After Qarlton discovered Aerys's [wildfire plot](wiki:Wildfire_plot) to burn King's Landing to ashes should the rebels try to take the city, he attempted to prevent its execution. Lord Chelsted protested, pleaded with Aerys not to do this, and eventually resigned the position of Hand when the king refused.(wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Aerys had him dipped in wildfire and burned alive.(wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)(wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) According to [a historic account](wiki:The_World_of_Ice_and_Fire_(Yandel)) written by [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Yandel](wiki:Yandel), Qarlton was "burned alive for bad counsel during the war".(wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_end.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Aerys raped his queen, [Rhaella Targaryen](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen), later that day.(wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) After the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident), Qarlton was succeeded as Hand by the [pyromancer](wiki:Pyromancer) Rossart.(wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_end.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Appearances & Description

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) considered Qarlton to be a craven, with the exception of the day he confronted King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Because of the sigil of [House Chelsted](wiki:House_Chelsted), Jaime thought of Qarlton as "the mace-and-dagger Hand".(wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

In [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal)'s bathhouse, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) tells [Brienne](wiki:Brienne_Tarth) of [Tarth](wiki:Tarth) about King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen)'s court during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), including how Qarlton had been executed after he resigned his office as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

### A Feast for Crows

Watching the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand) burning, [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) enjoys thinking of the deaths of King's Hands she has known over the years, Qarlton included.(wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Jaime remembers Queen [Rhaella Targaryen](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen)'s cries through the oaken door he was guarding as Aerys raped her, thinking Rhaella's cries were worse than Lord Chelsted's screams as he was burned alive.(wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) remembers he had expected Aerys to recall Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) after Lord [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington)'s defeat in the [Battle of the Bells](wiki:Battle_of_the_Bells), but instead the king had turned to Lords Chelsted and [Rossart](wiki:Rossart).(wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted.cite_ref-Radwd72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
