## Identity

Gaemon Palehair, mockingly called "King Cunny" or "The Cunny King," was a young boy proclaimed king during the Moon of the Three Kings in King's Landing during the Dance of the Dragons (track_b: Title, aliases).

## Origins

Born in 126 AC (track_b: Born). Son of Essie (track_b: Mother). Of Crownlands culture (track_b: Culture).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Targaryen (track_b: Allegiance).

## Narrative Arc

Gaemon was a child of four when the Moon of the Three Kings erupted in King's Landing. His mother Essie, a whore, claimed he was the bastard son of Aegon II. The mob crowned him briefly during the chaos. He died aged nine in 135 AC in the Red Keep (track_b: Died).
