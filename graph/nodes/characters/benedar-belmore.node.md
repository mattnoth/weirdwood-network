## Appearances & Description

Benedar is a fat man with a pear belly and rounded shoulders.(wiki:Benedar_Belmore.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) An unkempt reddish-grey beard grows from his multiple chins.(wiki:Benedar_Belmore.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

[Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) believes Lord Belmore to be corrupt.(wiki:Benedar_Belmore.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

The Belmores support Lord [Yohn Royce](wiki:Yohn_Royce) in pressuring Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) to enter the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Benedar_Belmore.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Lord Benedar is among the powerful [Vale](wiki:Vale) lords to sign the document of the [Lords Declarant](wiki:Lords_Declarant), which demands the handover of Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn) and the dismissal of his councilors. He is the first of the Lords Declarant to arrive at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) and begin the blockade, preventing fresh food from going up to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie).(wiki:Benedar_Belmore.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

When the Lords Declarant travel to the Eyrie, Benedar cannot climb the last stage to the castle and has to be drawn up in the basket. During the parley with Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), Benedar is distrustful of the [Lord Protector of the Vale](wiki:Lord_Protector_of_the_Vale) and wants the young Lord Robert to leave the Eyrie.(wiki:Benedar_Belmore.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

Benedar reconciles with Petyr and is a surprise guest at [the wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Lyonel_Corbray_and_Lady_Corbray) of Lord [Lyonel Corbray](wiki:Lyonel_Corbray) in [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown).(wiki:Benedar_Belmore.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Benedar attends Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn)'s tourney, competing for a spot in the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights) at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon). While there, he meets with [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) and Lord [Gerold Grafton](wiki:Gerold_Grafton) to discuss selling their reserves of food. Benedar believes the current prices are fair, but Petyr wants to wait for the demand to go higher. When [Alayne Stone](wiki:Alayne_Stone) arrives and informs them that the [Waynwoods](wiki:House_Waynwood) have arrived with Ser [Harrold Hardyng](wiki:Harrold_Hardyng), Benedar is stunned to hear that Lord [Yohn Royce](wiki:Yohn_Royce) allowed Harrold to come to the tourney.(wiki:Benedar_Belmore.cite_ref-twow_4-0)
