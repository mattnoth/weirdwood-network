## Origins

Pol Qo is the first of the [orange emperors](wiki:Orange_emperors) of the [Golden Empire of Yi Ti](wiki:Golden_Empire_of_Yi_Ti), in defiance of [Bu Gai](wiki:Bu_Gai), the seventeenth [azure emperor](wiki:Azure_emperor), enthroned in the capital city of [Yin](wiki:Yin). Qo's seat is located in the garrison city of [Trader Town](wiki:Trader_Town). Another pretender is a [sorcerer lord](wiki:Sorcerer_lord), claiming to be the sixty-ninth [yellow emperor](wiki:Yellow_emperors) despite the fall of that dynasty a thousand years ago, who is exiled to the city of [Carcosa](wiki:Carcosa), far to the east of Yi Ti.
