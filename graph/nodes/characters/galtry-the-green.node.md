## Origins

Galtry participated in the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls). He faced Ser [John the Fiddler](wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre) in his final joust, losing after ten tilts.(wiki:Galtry_the_Green.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Appearances & Description

Galtry [dyed](wiki:Hair_dye) his beard green in the fashion of the [Tyroshi](wiki:Tyrosh). He owned a cloak made of a patchwork of many squares of fabric, each a different shade of green.(wiki:Galtry_the_Green.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

In the lists, Galtry rode a white stallion with a green plume on his crinet that match the one on his helm. The greaves and gauntlets of his [armor](wiki:Armor) had [gold](wiki:Gold) inlay. His shield bore his personal arms of nine [jade](wiki:Jade) mullets upon a leek-green field.(wiki:Galtry_the_Green.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
