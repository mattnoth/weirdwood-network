## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Raymund is introduced to Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) at the [Twins](wiki:Twins) when he is sitting in the great hall, drinking with his half-brother [Lame Lothar](wiki:Lothar_Frey), his brother Ser [Hosteen](wiki:Hosteen_Frey), his goodbrother Lord [Lucias Vypren](wiki:Lucias_Vypren), nephew Ser [Damon Vypren](wiki:Damon_Vypren), and the [Haighs](wiki:House_Haigh).(wiki:Raymund_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

The next day, Raymund attends the wedding feast of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) and [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey).(wiki:Raymund_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Once the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) begins, the [Smalljon](wiki:Jon_Umber_(son_of_Jon)) defends himself by striking Raymund in the face with a leg of mutton. After Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) stabs King [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), Catelyn saws the neck of Raymund's half-nephew, [Jinglebell](wiki:Aegon_Frey_(son_of_Stevron)). When the mad Catelyn then begins clawing her own face,(wiki:Raymund_Frey.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Raymund slits her throat from ear to ear.(wiki:Raymund_Frey.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
