## Identity

Lyman Lannister was the Lord of Casterly Rock, Warden of the West, Shield of Lannisport, and head of House Lannister during the reigns of King Maegor I Targaryen and the early reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen (wiki:Lyman_Lannister).

## Origins

Born in or before 16 AC. Died 59 AC (track_b: Born, Died). Father: Loren I Lannister (presumably) (track_b: Father). Spouse: Jocasta Tarbeck (track_b: Spouse).

## Appearances & Description

A cunning and powerful lord. Lyman was ambitious, wanting to raise House Lannister's influence above the Velaryons, Baratheons, and Hightowers. Rhaena Targaryen believed he sought a dragon of his own. Alleged to be the richest lord in Westeros. Jaehaerys I distrusted him and considered him too proud (wiki:Lyman_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfab).

## Narrative Arc

Lyman served during the contentious reign of Maegor, seeking to advance his house's standing. He fathered trueborn sons with Jocasta Tarbeck and also a bastard, Tyler Hill (wiki:Lyman_Lannister.cite_ref-Rfab).
