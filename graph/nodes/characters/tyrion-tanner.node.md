## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

During the [riots in King's Landing](wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing), [Lollys Stokeworth](wiki:Lollys_Stokeworth) is knocked from her saddle as the royal party returns to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep). The [gold cloaks](wiki:Gold_cloaks) later find her wandering naked on [Sowbelly Row](wiki:Sowbelly_Row) after having been raped by "half a hundred" men behind a tanner's shop.(wiki:Tyrion_Tanner.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Lollys, traumatized by the event, becomes afraid to leave her chambers.(wiki:Tyrion_Tanner.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Lady [Tanda Stokeworth](wiki:Tanda_Stokeworth) hires [Symon Silver Tongue](wiki:Symon_Silver_Tongue) to sing for her pregnant daughter; his music calms Lollys when her baby begins to kick.(wiki:Tyrion_Tanner.cite_ref-Rasos12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) is arrested for the murder of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_I_Baratheon) at [his wedding to Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Purple_Wedding). In his tower cell, Tyrion summons his [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword), Ser [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) of the [Blackwater](wiki:Blackwater_Rush), in case he needs him as a champion in a potential [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat). Bronn comes, but after some time and only reluctantly, as he is to marry Lollys in two days. Tyrion realizes that Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) has arranged this marriage to take away his ally, and tries to argue Bronn out of the marriage. He says that Lollys is [lackwitted](wiki:Lackwit) and is pregnant with another man's [bastard](wiki:Bastard) child, and is not even the heir to [Castle Stokeworth](wiki:Castle_Stokeworth). Bronn replies he doesn't want wits, that soon after Lollys gives birth she'll be pregnant with his child, and that her sister [Falyse](wiki:Falyse_Stokeworth) has no children and might die before her mother. As Tyrion cannot match Cersei's offer of a [highborn](wiki:Highborn) wife and a castle, versus fighting Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) to the death, he says farewell to Bronn.(wiki:Tyrion_Tanner.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Feast for Crows

During the funeral of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), who was murdered by Tyrion, Lollys is in labor, so she and her mother remain at [Stokeworth](wiki:Stokeworth). Falyse tells Queen Cersei that Lady Tanda asked if the baby is a boy, if they could [name him after](wiki:Named_after) Tywin, but Cersei forbids it, indignant at the insult to her father.(wiki:Tyrion_Tanner.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

When Lollys delivers a healthy son, Bronn names him after Tyrion.(wiki:Tyrion_Tanner.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) finds this amusing, but Cersei is displeased by the sellsword's insolence.(wiki:Tyrion_Tanner.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Cersei tells Lady Falyse and her husband, Ser [Balman Byrch](wiki:Balman_Byrch), that the name of Lollys's son may mean that Bronn is hiding knowledge of Tyrion's whereabouts(wiki:Tyrion_Tanner.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) (though she later thinks that she does not truly believe this),(wiki:Tyrion_Tanner.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) and conspires with them to kill Bronn.(wiki:Tyrion_Tanner.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) However, the plan backfires, and Bronn kills Balman and exiles Falyse from Stokeworth. When Falyse flees to Cersei in terror for her life, Cersei gives her to [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn) for his experiments in the [black cells](wiki:Black_cells), to make her disappear and cover up the conspiracy.(wiki:Tyrion_Tanner.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

After the death of Lady Tanda from a [chill](wiki:Disease) brought on by a broken hip, Lollys becomes the [Lady of Stokeworth](wiki:Lady_of_Stokeworth), with her husband Bronn calling himself Lord Stokeworth.(wiki:Tyrion_Tanner.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Falyse also dies soon afterwards from Qyburn's experiments.(wiki:Tyrion_Tanner.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
