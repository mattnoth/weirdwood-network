## Appearances & Description

Talbert is a brave young knight without a beard.(wiki:Talbert_Serry.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Talbert is a good and quick fighter. In battle, he wields a castle-forged [longsword](wiki:Longsword) and shield. The white rose of [House Serry](wiki:House_Serry) is depicted on his surcoat and his shield.(wiki:Talbert_Serry.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

During the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields) by the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) fleet, Ser Talbert commands a ship in the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands) fleet that defends the mouth of the [Mander](wiki:Mander). [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy), [Lord Captain of the Iron Fleet](wiki:Lord_Captain_of_the_Iron_Fleet), rams Talbert's smaller ship with *[Iron Victory](wiki:Iron_Victory)*. Talbert ably fights Victarion, and the raider is forced to defend himself by catching the knight's longsword in his gauntlet, with the blade cutting into Victarion's hand. Victarion throws Talbert's sword into the sea, overpowers the younger man, and forces him overboard. After the battle, Victarion thinks his adversary likely drowned in his armor.(wiki:Talbert_Serry.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

According to [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), Lord [Osbert Serry](wiki:Osbert_Serry)'s son and heir was slain.(wiki:Talbert_Serry.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Victarion thinks about Talbert on his voyage to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay), as his infected left hand is not healing. Victarion wonders if Talbert's sword had been [poisoned](wiki:Poison), but he discounts the idea of a knight using poison. Victarion instead considers blaming Maester [Kerwin](wiki:Kerwin).(wiki:Talbert_Serry.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
