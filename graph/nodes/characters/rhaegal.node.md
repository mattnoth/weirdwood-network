## Appearances & Description

Rhaegal is a green and bronze [dragon](wiki:Dragon).(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) His dark scales(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and wings(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) are jade-green, and his eyes are described as bronze.(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-7) The dragon has black claws(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) and teeth like black needles.(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) His [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame) has been described as being yellow,(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) red and yellow,(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and "orange-and-yellow fire shot through with veins of green".(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

At the wedding of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) to Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) by [Pentos](wiki:Pentos), she receives multiple bride gifts, including three petrified [dragon eggs](wiki:Dragon_egg) from Magister [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis). Rhaegal's egg is a deep green, with burnished bronze flecks that comes and goes depending on how Daenerys turns it.(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

The *[maegi](wiki:Maegi)* [Mirri Maz Duur](wiki:Mirri_Maz_Duur) proclaims that in order to bring new life one has to give life. Daenerys burns Drogo's body, along with Mirri herself and the three eggs, birthing Rhaegal, along with his brothers, [Viserion](wiki:Viserion) and [Drogon](wiki:Drogon).(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Clash of Kings

In the [red waste](wiki:Red_waste), Daenerys names the green dragon Rhaegal, after her late brother [Rhaegar](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen).(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) At [Vaes Tolorro](wiki:Vaes_Tolorro), Rhaegal tries to fly in Daenerys's tent and screams after landing on her carpet.(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

When Daenerys arrives in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), Rhaegal and the other dragons are a marvel in the ancient city.(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Rhaegal is with Daenerys when she receives the news of the death of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) from [Quhuru Mo](wiki:Quhuru_Mo).(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

Daenerys takes Rhaegal on her shoulder when she petitions the [Pureborn](wiki:Pureborn) in the [Hall of a Thousand Thrones](wiki:Hall_of_a_Thousand_Thrones) and then travels with [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos) in his [palanquin](wiki:Palanquin).(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

### A Storm of Swords

Rhaegal grows to the size of a small [dog](wiki:Dog), and he flies above as Daenerys sails toward [Astapor](wiki:Astapor). When Daenerys has the [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) attack the [Good Masters](wiki:Good_Masters) in the [sack of Astapor](wiki:Sack_of_Astapor), [Jhiqui](wiki:Jhiqui) frees Rhaegal to attack the [slavers](wiki:Slavers).(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Rhaegal hisses at [Grazdan mo Eraz](wiki:Grazdan_mo_Eraz) when Daenerys meets with the envoy outside [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai).(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

The green dragon belches flame when an angry Daenerys confronts Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) and Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy).(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Radwd57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

After [conquering Meereen](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen) and claiming the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid), Daenerys finds Rhaegal sunning himself on the pyramid's terrace.(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Tales of dragons start to reach [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Rhaegal and the other dragons begin to misbehave, with Rhaegal snapping at [Irri](wiki:Irri).(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) His head grows larger than a [horse](wiki:Horse)'s.(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

When [Drogon](wiki:Drogon) allegedly kills the young girl [Hazzea](wiki:Hazzea) outside [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), Daenerys has Viserion and Rhaegal chained in a [makeshift "dragon pit"](wiki:Dragon_pit_(Great_Pyramid)) in the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid). Rhaegal fights fiercely while being removed from the terrace, and six men are burned during the three days it takes to move the green dragon.(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Rhaegar becomes more savage during his confinement.(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

Daenerys introduces Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) to Rhaegel and Viserion in the pit.(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

After Daenerys disappears on Drogon's back from [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit), Quentyn attempts to steal a dragon after enlisting the help of the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown). Quentyn attempts to dominate Viserion with a whip, but, unbeknownst to the knight, Rhaegal is behind him. The green dragon looses his [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame)(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Radwd68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6) and fatally burns Quentyn.(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

Once loosed from the pit, Rhaegal attempts to make his lair atop the [pyramid of Hazkar](wiki:Pyramid_of_Hazkar), killing many slavers there, but the building collapses into ruin.(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1) Rhaegal then makes his new lair in the [pyramid of Yherizan](wiki:Pyramid_of_Yherizan).(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-2) Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) notes that thus far, Rhaegal has shown himself to be more dangerous than his brother Viserion.(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-3)

### The Winds of Winter

As the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen) begins, Rhaegal and Viserion are both drawn to the battle. [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) sees Rhaegal circling above the bay, banking and turning as longships and galleys clash and burn below him. When [Ben Plumm](wiki:Ben_Plumm) and his companions come galloping back from the camp of [Malazza](wiki:Malazza), the Girl General, Rhaegal is still prowling, soaring in wide circles above the city and the bay on great green wings.(wiki:Rhaegal.cite_ref-Rtwow.5B.5Btyrion_ii_.28the_winds_of_winter.29.7Ctyrion.5D.5D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
