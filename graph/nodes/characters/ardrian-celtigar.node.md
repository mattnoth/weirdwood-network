## Appearances & Description

Ardrian is a sour old man(wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) with a wrinkled face.(wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Ardrian is known for his avarice and his [wealth](wiki:Currency).(wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) He looks down upon the lowborn Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth),(wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)(wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) though Lord [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) is dismissive of Ardrian's own opinions.(wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Lord Celtigar owns a mantle patterned with red [crabs](wiki:Crab) picked out in [garnets](wiki:Garnet)(wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Racok0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) and a square of linen embroidered in red crabs.(wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) His ship is the ornate *[Red Claw](wiki:Red_Claw)*.(wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3) Ardrian's treasures at [Claw Isle](wiki:Claw_Isle) are said to include a [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) axe(wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) and [a horn](wiki:House_Celtigar_horn) capable of summoning [krakens](wiki:Kraken).(wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Lord Ardrian wants to know which [storm lords](wiki:Storm_lords) will support Lord [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) when he lays claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). Ardrian attends Stannis's feast at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) He has a coughing fit when [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) burns the statues of the [Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) taken from the [sept at Dragonstone](wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone).(wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4)

Lord Celtigar participates in Stannis's [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)).(wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

The Red Crab is captured at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater)(wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) and publicly bends the knee to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

### A Storm of Swords

Ardrian stays in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) after the Blackwater, with Ser [Axell Florent](wiki:Axell_Florent) thinking that Lord Celtigar fears reprisals from Stannis. Axell suggests that Stannis sack [Claw Isle](wiki:Claw_Isle) in Ardrian's absence, but Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) objects to the plan. Stannis sides with Davos the Onion Knight, explaining that he will instead deal with Lord Celtigar personally after becoming king.(wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-3)

Ardrian is present at [the wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King Joffrey I to Lady [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), and later testifies against [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) at his trial for regicide.(wiki:Ardrian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
