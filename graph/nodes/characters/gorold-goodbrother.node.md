Lord Goodbrother appreciates [maesters](wiki:Maesters) and is protective of his [lordly](wiki:Lord) rights.(wiki:Gorold_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-7)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

When Lord [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) calls his [banners](wiki:Bannerman) to [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), Lord Gorold comes from [Great Wyk](wiki:Great_Wyk) with near forty [longships](wiki:Longship). He sails along with [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy).(wiki:Gorold_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Lord Gorold sends one of his sons, [Gormond](wiki:Gormond_Goodbrother), to the shore of Great Wyk to inform [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) of the death of his brother, [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North). Aeron travels to [Hammerhorn](wiki:Hammerhorn) to discuss the news with Gorold. The Damphair demands that [Murenmure](wiki:Murenmure) not be present for their conversation, but Lord Goodbrother insists that his [maester](wiki:Maester) stays. Murenmure informs Aeron of [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy)'s arrival at [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), and Gorold tells the [priest of the Drowned God](wiki:Priest_of_the_Drowned_God) about the death of Lord [Sawane Botley](wiki:Sawane_Botley).(wiki:Gorold_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-8)

Gorold attends the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), where he is listed as one of [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy)'s supporters.(wiki:Gorold_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) During the feast held by Victarion, two of Gorold's sons knock over a table while fighting.(wiki:Gorold_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) After Euron's speech at the kingsmoot, Aeron spies Gorold shouting his support for Euron.(wiki:Gorold_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), Gorold is spotted by Victarion and [Nute the Barber](wiki:Nute_the_Barber) talking with Lords [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw) and [Dunstan Drumm](wiki:Dunstan_Drumm) in the yard at [Lord Hewett's castle](wiki:Lord_Hewett's_castle). They discuss their fears that the gains from the battle are little compared to the enmity of the [Reach](wiki:Reach) they have earned.(wiki:Gorold_Goodbrother.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
