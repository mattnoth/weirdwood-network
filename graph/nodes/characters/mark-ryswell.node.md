## Origins

Mark was one of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s companions, fighting alongside him during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). He was slain at the [tower of joy](wiki:Tower_of_joy) in the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) [during a fight](wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy) with three of King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen)'s [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). Afterwards, Eddard had the tower pulled down, and used its stones for cairns for the fallen.(wiki:Mark_Ryswell.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
