## Origins

On the account of his prowess at arms and reputation, Ser Gawen was chosen by Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) as the first [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms) for her son, Prince [Maegor Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).
