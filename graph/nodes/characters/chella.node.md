## Appearances & Description

> *See also: [Images of Chella](wiki:Category:Images_of_Chella)*

Chella is a small woman, dark and hard.(wiki:Chella.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Chella.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) thinks she is flat like a boy.(wiki:Chella.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Chella is a serious woman(wiki:Chella.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) with a throaty chuckle.(wiki:Chella.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Chella wears a necklace of ears.(wiki:Chella.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) She has sons who have claimed fifty ears between them.(wiki:Chella.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Chella meets [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) in the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon). She scouts ahead and finds Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s encampment at the [crossroads](wiki:Crossroads), and she accompanies Tyrion to the meeting with his father. There she accepts to fight for the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Chella.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

Chella shrieks when riding during the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork), and she takes four ears during the fighting.(wiki:Chella.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Clash of Kings

The group continues with Tyrion to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Tyrion entrusts Chella with the guard of [Shae](wiki:Shae) when he leaves his mistress in an inn, the [Broken Anvil](wiki:Broken_Anvil), before going to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep). Chella's necklace consists of forty-six dried, wrinkled ears.(wiki:Chella.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Tyrion offers her to [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) as a bodyguard in order to protect her from the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), who have been beating her on the order of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Chella.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

With her clansmen, Chella is sent to the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood) to harass [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s troops while they march on [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Chella.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords

After the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Chella returns to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to be greeted by a hostile crowd which forces her and the Black Ears to return to the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon).(wiki:Chella.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
