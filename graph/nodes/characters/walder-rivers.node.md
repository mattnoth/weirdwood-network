## Origins

Walder's mother died after falling from a [horse](wiki:Horse).(wiki:Walder_Rivers.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

When [Chett](wiki:Chett) was caught for murder near [Sevenstreams](wiki:Sevenstreams), Walder was sent from the [Twins](wiki:Twins) to judge him. Chett ended up being sent to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) in the company of [Yoren](wiki:Yoren).(wiki:Walder_Rivers.cite_ref-Rasos0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Appearances & Description

Walder is gruff, dour, and has grey hair.(wiki:Walder_Rivers.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He has the same suspicious face of his father, Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey).(wiki:Walder_Rivers.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Though not a trueborn [Frey](wiki:House_Frey), Bastard Walder is often put in positions of responsibility due to his strong reputation as a warrior.(wiki:Walder_Rivers.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) The misanthropic(wiki:Walder_Rivers.cite_ref-Rawoiafwalder_rivers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) [knight](wiki:Knight) has cold courtesy(wiki:Walder_Rivers.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and is resentful of his [bastard](wiki:Bastard) status.(wiki:Walder_Rivers.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) considers him harsh and sour,(wiki:Walder_Rivers.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) and [Lothar Frey](wiki:Lothar_Frey) explains that his half-brother has a soldier's bluntness.(wiki:Walder_Rivers.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)

Walder's shield depicts the arms of House Frey with the colors reversed, and a red bend sinister across the towers.(wiki:Walder_Rivers.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) He uses a modest tent when on campaign.(wiki:Walder_Rivers.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Walder commands the forty-strong escort of [Lothar Frey](wiki:Lothar_Frey) on his trip south to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) where Lothar is to treat with [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark).(wiki:Walder_Rivers.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5) He is quiet and curt during supper with King [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully), and [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark).(wiki:Walder_Rivers.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-6)

Walder accompanies Lothar on his meeting with Robb outside the gates of the [Twins](wiki:Twins), but is much less friendly than his half-brother.(wiki:Walder_Rivers.cite_ref-Rasos49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) During the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Bastard Walder commands the attack on the soldiers' camp.(wiki:Walder_Rivers.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Walder is present at the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), where he has a modest tent. He participates in the heated stalemate that plagues the besieging army's command. Walder challenges Lord [Clement Piper](wiki:Clement_Piper) after his nephew [Edwyn](wiki:Edwyn_Frey) is insulted, but Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) prevents them from fighting.(wiki:Walder_Rivers.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

After Edmure arranges for the surrender of Riverrun, Walder informs Jaime that Edwyn's father, Ser [Ryman Frey](wiki:Ryman_Frey), was hanged by [outlaws](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) near [Fairmarket](wiki:Fairmarket). Edwyn is convinced that his own brother, [Black Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Ryman)), is responsible for the death of their father, but Bastard Walder points out he has no proof. Walder also tells Jaime that Ser [Raynald Westerling](wiki:Raynald_Westerling) is presumed dead from the Red Wedding.(wiki:Walder_Rivers.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
