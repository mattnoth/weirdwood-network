## Origins

Under Jack's watch, [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall) [Raymun Redbeard](wiki:Raymun_Redbeard) and his [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) host slipped past the [Wall](wiki:Wall) and headed south. This drew the forces of [House Umber](wiki:House_Umber) and [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) to [Long Lake](wiki:Long_Lake), where Raymun and Lord [Willam Stark](wiki:Willam_Stark) were [killed in battle](wiki:Battle_at_Long_Lake). The [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) arrived too late to participate in the battle and were ordered by [Artos Stark](wiki:Artos_Stark) to dispose of the dead.
