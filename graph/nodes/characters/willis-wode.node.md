## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

When [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) takes [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) prisoner at the [inn at the crossroads](wiki:Inn_at_the_crossroads), Ser Willis is the first of the [rivermen](wiki:Rivermen) to proclaim his loyalty to [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully) and aid Catelyn.(wiki:Willis_Wode.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)(wiki:Willis_Wode.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

Catelyn decides to bring Tyrion to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) instead of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Willis_Wode.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) When Catelyn's party is attacked by [mountain clansmen](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) on the [high road](wiki:High_road), [Morrec](wiki:Morrec) helps Willis with his armor when [Marillion](wiki:Marillion) is too frightened to assist. Willis cries out "[Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal)!" as he rides against the wildings. Catelyn wishes to bury the dead after the battle, but Willis urges that they continue their journey through the dangerous [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon) instead of delaying.(wiki:Willis_Wode.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5)

Willis is the only one of the rivermen to survive the journey to the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn_(valley)). He and Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) remain at the [Bloody Gate](wiki:Bloody_Gate) to have their wounds treated by a [septon](wiki:Septon) while Catelyn continues on with Tyrion to the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon).(wiki:Willis_Wode.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

Willis and Rodrik continue on to the Eyrie, and Willis chuckles when Tyrion publicly demands a [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat).(wiki:Willis_Wode.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) His whereabouts after Tyrion leaves the Eyrie(wiki:Willis_Wode.cite_ref-Ragot42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) are unknown.

### A Feast for Crows

On the journey to resolve the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and his host come across the lands once held by the [brothers Wode](wiki:House_Wode), two [landed knights](wiki:Landed_knight) sworn to [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal): it is possible that one of them is Ser Willis. Jaime encounters neither member of the Wode family or their [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) but finds both of their timber keeps burned down, and he hangs several [outlaws](wiki:Outlaws) taking shelter in the cellar beneath one of the ruined halls.(wiki:Willis_Wode.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
