## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

When King [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) comes with the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) after the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), Lord [Humfrey Hewett](wiki:Humfrey_Hewett) is tied to his chair and made to watch his wife and daughters serve the raiders. Falia, who becomes Euron's lover, suggests the idea that the women serve naked. Lord Humfrey's wife and daughters have their clothes removed.(wiki:Falia_Flowers.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

Euron later takes Falia upstairs to Lord Hewett's chambers. When [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) is summoned to Euron, he spots Falia sleeping naked on the bed.(wiki:Falia_Flowers.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

### The Winds of Winter

When [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) is brought to [Oakenshield](wiki:Oakenshield) he is fed by Falia. Aeron asks her to tell Victarion to rescue him, but she says he has already departed the island. She reveals that she is pregnant and claims Euron will make her his [salt wife](wiki:Salt_wife) and her sons will be before his other [bastard](wiki:Bastard) sons. Falia states that [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) will be Euron's [rock wife](wiki:Rock_wife) and that Falia will be like a sister to her. Aeron warns her to flee, saying Euron will hurt her and kill her, but Falia refuses in disbelief, stating Euron loves her and gives her silks and jewels.(wiki:Falia_Flowers.cite_ref-TWOW_2-2)

Later, when Aeron is lashed to the prow of the *[Silence](wiki:Silence)*, Falia is tied next to him. She is naked, pregnant, her [tongue has been cut out](wiki:Tongue_mutilation), and her face is red from crying.(wiki:Falia_Flowers.cite_ref-TWOW_2-3)
