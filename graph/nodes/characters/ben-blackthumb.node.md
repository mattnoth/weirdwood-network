## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

After [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) is taken by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), Ben Blackthumb enters the service of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Ben_Blackthumb.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) He keeps the swords of the armory locked up.(wiki:Ben_Blackthumb.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Ben, [Pia](wiki:Pia), and a cook are the only servants from the time of Lady [Shella Whent](wiki:Shella_Whent) who remain at Harrenhal when Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) returns on his way to lift the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun).(wiki:Ben_Blackthumb.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
