## Appearances & Description

Zahrina is a small older woman with skin white like a maggot or mare's milk, and her hair is so thin that her scalp can be seen beneath it.(wiki:Zahrina.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Zahrina.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Her wrinkled face puckers when she is displeased.(wiki:Zahrina.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

Zahrina owns a violet *[tokar](wiki:Tokar)*,(wiki:Zahrina.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) as well as one dark red and fringed with tiny golden skulls.(wiki:Zahrina.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

Zahrina is present at the [slave](wiki:Slave) market auction set up by the [Yunkai'i](wiki:Yunkai'i) at the mouth of the [Skahazadhan](wiki:Skahazadhan) outside [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). She bids by usually adding "and one" to the last bidder's price, which annoys the slave auctioneers. Zahrina dislikes [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s appearance and hisses in outrage when he makes a vulgar gesture toward her. Tyrion thinks of her as a crone and a hag. Zahrina attempts to purchase Tyrion, [Penny](wiki:Penny), [Pretty Pig](wiki:Pretty_Pig), and [Crunch](wiki:Crunch), but she gives up at three thousand. She later attempts to purchase Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont), but is outbid by [Yezzan zo Qaggaz](wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz), after Tyrion convinces Yezzan's overseer, [Nurse](wiki:Nurse), that Jorah is part of their show.(wiki:Zahrina.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

When [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell), [Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater), and [Archibald Yronwood](wiki:Archibald_Yronwood) meet with the [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince), they agree on the [Purple Lotus](wiki:Purple_Lotus) as the location for their meet-up. Inside the [winesink](wiki:Winesink), Quentyn sees naked men dueling in a [fighting pit](wiki:Fighting_pit). Zahrina directs the [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) to the cellar, where they meet with the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown). She offers patrons food, but according to the Tattered Prince the bread is stale and her stew is unspeakable.(wiki:Zahrina.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
