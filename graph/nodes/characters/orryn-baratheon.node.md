## Identity

Ser Orryn Baratheon was a knight from House Baratheon and a younger brother of Lord Rogar Baratheon of Storm's End (wiki:Orryn_Baratheon).

## Narrative Arc

When Rogar was Hand of the King, he wanted to betroth Orryn to Princess Alysanne Targaryen. In 50 AC, Orryn went to the Starry Sept in Oldtown demanding Princess Rhaella Targaryen be given into his custody as part of Rogar's plot to crown Rhaella (claiming she was Princess Aerea). The Faith refused, and Orryn was captured by thirty armed septons. He confessed and was imprisoned. King Jaehaerys I pardoned Orryn but exiled him to Essos for ten years. Orryn entered the service of the Archon of Tyrosh and within a year married the Archon's daughter. He had a daughter by her. Orryn died in 51 AC in the Disputed Lands (wiki:Orryn_Baratheon).
