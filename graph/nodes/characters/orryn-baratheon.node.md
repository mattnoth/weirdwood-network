## Identity

Orryn Baratheon, the youngest of Lord Rogar's brothers, was chosen by the council to wed Princess Alysanne.

## Origins

When Orryn's older brother, Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon), was [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), he wanted to betroth Orryn to Princess [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen).(wiki:Orryn_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

When Rogar was dismissed as Hand, Orryn returned to [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) along with his brothers, Ser [Borys](wiki:Borys_Baratheon), Ser [Garon](wiki:Garon_Baratheon), and Ser [Ronnal](wiki:Ronnal_Baratheon).(wiki:Orryn_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

In [50 AC](wiki:50_AC) Orryn arrived at the [motherhouse](wiki:Motherhouse) attached to the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) demanding Princess [Rhaella](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon)), a novice of the [Faith](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), be given into his custody. However, the Faith refused, and Orryn and his men-at-arms were instead captured by thirty armed [septons](wiki:Septons) led by the [High Septon's](wiki:High_Lickspittle) steward, [Casper Straw](wiki:Casper_Straw). Orryn was questioned and confessed to a plot: he was to bring Rhaella to Storm's End, where Rogar planned to have her confess that she was really her older twin sister, Princess [Aerea](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen), and then he meant to name her queen. For this, Lord [Donnel Hightower](wiki:Donnel_Hightower) imprisoned Orryn. When he came of age, King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) decided to be merciful and pardoned Lord Rogar for his plots. Orryn was also pardoned by the king, but was exiled to [Essos](wiki:Essos) for ten years.(wiki:Orryn_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)

Orryn and the dozen men who had accompanied him to Oldtown traveled to the [Free City](wiki:Free_City) of [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) and went into the service of its [Archon](wiki:Archon_of_Tyrosh). Within a year Orryn [married](wiki:Married) the Archon's buxom blue-green haired [maiden](wiki:Maiden) [daughter](wiki:Archon's_daughter_(Jaehaerys_I)), who his brother Rogar had once hoped to wed to King Jaehaerys. Orryn had a daughter by her, but there were doubts if he was her true father, as his wife was known to be free with her favor. When the Archon's term ended Orryn left Tyrosh for [Myr](wiki:Myr) and joined the [Maiden's Men](wiki:Maiden's_Men). He was slain soon after in the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands) in a battle against the [Men of Valor](wiki:Men_of_Valor). Nothing is known of the fate of his wife and daughter.(wiki:Orryn_Baratheon.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)
