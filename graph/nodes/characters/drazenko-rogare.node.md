## Origins

Drazenko was one of the envoys from Lys to the court of the [Princess of Dorne](wiki:Princess_of_Dorne), [Aliandra Martell](wiki:Aliandra_Martell), at [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear). Drazenko, alongside his brother [Lysandro](wiki:Lysandro_Rogare), were seen as the architects of the alliance between [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) and the [Triarchy](wiki:Triarchy).(wiki:Drazenko_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) It is therefore likely that Drazenko was already part of Sunspear's court under Prince [Qoren Martell](wiki:Qoren_Martell), Aliandra's father.

On [Maiden's Day](wiki:Maiden's_Day) of [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Drazenko approached Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) during the feast organized to celebrate [the Maiden](wiki:Maiden_(the_Seven)), and asked for a private word. He whispered in Alyn's ear, informing him that Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen), believed dead ever since the [Battle of the Gullet](wiki:Battle_of_the_Gullet), was in fact alive and with Drazenko's brother in Lys. Lord Alyn, who was returning to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) after his expedition to the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) to deal with the threat of Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy)'s [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), changed his plans and sailed towards the Free City the very next day.(wiki:Drazenko_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

At some point, Drazenko married [Aliandra Martell](wiki:Aliandra_Martell), and became the [Prince Consort of Dorne](wiki:Prince_Consort_of_Dorne). Aliandra even conferred him the title of "[Lord of the Stepstones](wiki:Lord_of_the_Stepstones)". This marriage, and the return of Prince Viserys to King's Landing, marked the apex of [House Rogare](wiki:House_Rogare)'s power in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), called the [Lysene Spring](wiki:Lysene_Spring).(wiki:Drazenko_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

In [135 AC](wiki:135_AC), he and his brother died within a day of one another, beginning the decline of the Rogare family in both Lys and the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Drazenko_Rogare.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_quarrelsome_daughters:_myr.2C_lys.2C_and_tyrosh.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Drazenko_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5) Drazenko died first, choking upon a piece of bacon, and Lysandro drowned when his barge sank. Their deaths were widely believed at the time to have been the act of hired [Faceless Men](wiki:Faceless_Men).(wiki:Drazenko_Rogare.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-6)
