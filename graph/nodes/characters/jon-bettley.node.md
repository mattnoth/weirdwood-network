## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

Beardless Jon was spotted by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) riding at a [quintain](wiki:Quintain) in the yard of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Jon_Bettley.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) He was part of the force that marched north to aid in the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun) under the command of Ser Jaime. After the fall of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), Ser Jon joined Ser [Lyle Crakehall](wiki:Lyle_Crakehall) in setting out for [Darry](wiki:Darry) to begin hunting [outlaws](wiki:Outlaws).(wiki:Jon_Bettley.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
