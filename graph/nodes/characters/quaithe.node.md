## Appearances & Description

Quaithe wears a dark red lacquer wooden mask.(wiki:Quaithe.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Quaithe.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Quaithe.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Her eyes are visible behind the mask, wet and shiny.(wiki:Quaithe.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) She wears a long hooded robe.(wiki:Quaithe.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

Quaithe is usually cryptic and quiet.(wiki:Quaithe.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Quaithe is one of the three representatives from [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) who arrive at [Vaes Tolorro](wiki:Vaes_Tolorro) with [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) in search of [dragons](wiki:Dragon), the other two being [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos) and [Pyat Pree](wiki:Pyat_Pree).(wiki:Quaithe.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Quaithe introduces herself, speaking in the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue).(wiki:Quaithe.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) The three seekers escort [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) and her *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* from the [red waste](wiki:Red_waste) to Qarth. While Xaro and Pyat offer Daenerys promises, Quaithe warns her to beware of those who would seek to control her young dragons ([Drogon](wiki:Drogon), [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal), [Viserion](wiki:Viserion)).(wiki:Quaithe.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

When Daenerys sees a [firemage](wiki:Firemage) creating and climbing a fiery ladder in the bazaar, Quaithe appears from the crowd and explains that the mage is not using trickery. Quaithe says the mage's skill has increased since Daenerys became the Mother of Dragons. When Quaithe touches Daenerys's wrist, [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo) warns her away. Quaithe urges Daenerys to leave Qarth as soon as possible, warning that if Daenerys lingers she will not be permitted to leave the city at all. Daenerys notices that her wrist tingles, an effect of Quaithe's touch. Before Quaithe disappears into the crowd, Daenerys is told she must "pass beneath the shadow" and that she will find truth in [Asshai](wiki:Asshai).(wiki:Quaithe.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### A Storm of Swords

While sailing from Qarth to [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay) aboard the ship *[Balerion](wiki:Saduleon)*, Daenerys contemplates various potential enemies and their motives. She cannot determine whether Quaithe is an enemy or a dangerous friend.(wiki:Quaithe.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

At [Astapor](wiki:Astapor), Daenerys awakens to find that a woman is in her cabin with her, although she appears only as a faint shadow. The figure repeats the warning about passing beneath the shadow, and Daenerys realizes that it is Quaithe. She jumps out of bed and opens the door, but there is no sign of Quaithe on *Balerion*, and Daenerys dismisses the appearance as a dream.(wiki:Quaithe.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

When Daenerys has another vision of Quaithe in the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), Quaithe states that neither experience was a dream. Daenerys learns that Quaithe is not physically there, however, having [come another way](wiki:Glass_candle) without the knowledge of the queen's guards. Quaithe again cryptically warns Daenerys against trusting those who would seek her confidence and her dragons.(wiki:Quaithe.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

While Daenerys is later wandering lost, starving, and alone in the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea), she slips into a restless sleep and hears Quaithe's original instructions again. She then sees Quaithe, whose mask is made of starlight, and the stars whisper in a woman's voice, "Remember who you are, Daenerys. The dragons know. Do you?" The queen is eventually found by the *khalasar* of Khal [Jhaqo](wiki:Jhaqo).(wiki:Quaithe.cite_ref-Radwd71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
