## Identity

Prince Maron Martell was the Prince of Dorne and Lord of Sunspear who brought Dorne into the Seven Kingdoms through marriage to Princess Daenerys Targaryen (wiki:Maron_Martell).

## Origins

Born between 147 and 158 AC (track_b: Born). Son of an unnamed Martell (track_b: Father — "Martell"). Of Dornish culture (track_b: Culture).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Martell (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Dornishmen (track_b: Culture). `HOLDS_TITLE` Prince of Dorne and Lord of Sunspear (track_b: Titles).

## Narrative Arc

Maron Martell negotiated the union of Dorne with the Iron Throne through his marriage to Princess Daenerys Targaryen, sister of King Daeron II. This peaceful union achieved what the Targaryens' dragons had failed to accomplish through force — the incorporation of Dorne into the Seven Kingdoms (wiki:Maron_Martell).
