## Identity

Porther is a member of the household guard of Lord Eddard Stark at Winterfell (wiki:Porther).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Porther accompanies Lord Eddard to King's Landing when Ned is named Hand. While in the capital, he is known to make the rounds of the brothels (wiki:Porther.cite_ref-Ragot22). He is killed during or after Eddard's arrest. In ACOK, Bran lists Porther among the dead, and Theon has a nightmare of eating with the dead at Winterfell, including Porther (wiki:Porther.cite_ref-Racok21).
