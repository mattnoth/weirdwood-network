## Identity

Benifer was a maester of the Citadel who served as Grand Maester during the reigns of kings Maegor I Targaryen and Jaehaerys I Targaryen (wiki:Benifer).

## Narrative Arc

Benifer became Grand Maester after the execution of Grand Maester Desmond. He was among the most diligent of Grand Maesters in keeping written records, and his letters and journals provided detailed accounts of his service. He died in 59 AC in King's Landing (track_b: Died), having witnessed notable events including the death of Aerea Targaryen (wiki:Benifer).
