## Identity

Benifer — Grand Maester, newly returned from self-imposed exile in Pentos, who praised Jaehaerys as learned and pious.

## Origins

Benifer became Grand Maester after the execution of Grand Maester [Desmond](wiki:Desmond_(Grand_Maester)).(wiki:Benifer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

When Maegor decided to take another wife, Benifer suggested a match with Lady [Clarisse Dayne](wiki:Clarisse_Dayne) of [Starfall](wiki:Starfall) in the hopes of detaching her lands and [house](wiki:House_Dayne) from [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Benifer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

In [48 AC](wiki:48_AC) when the realm had enough of Maegor's tyranny and were flocking to join their support to the claim put forth by Prince [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), Maegor ordered Grand Maester Benifer to send forth his [ravens](wiki:Ravens) summoning all Maegor's leal lords and bannermen to gather at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Benifer instead abandoned Maegor and secretly fled on a ship to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos). Benifer was the only Grand Maester to have survived Maegor's reign as Maegor had beheaded the previous three.(wiki:Benifer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Upon Maegor's death, the new young king [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) sent an invitation to Benifer asking him to return to King's Landing. Although Benifer was fearful he might be executed for treason, he returned and was reinstated to his office by Jaehaerys.(wiki:Benifer.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

When [Balerion](wiki:Balerion) returned to King's Landing in [56 AC](wiki:56_AC) with the afflicted Princess [Aerea](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen) barely clinging to him. The princess was carried to Benifer by Ser [Lucamore Strong](wiki:Lucamore_Strong) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard). [Septon](wiki:Septon) [Barth](wiki:Barth) aided Benifer in attempting to heal the girl, but their efforts failed and the princess perished, both men were traumatized by the girl's death.(wiki:Benifer.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Benifer was one of the victims of the [Shivers](wiki:Shivers) when that disease came to King's Landing in [59 AC](wiki:59_AC). He died after serving as Grand Maester for a total of fifteen years under two different kings.(wiki:Benifer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) [Elysar](wiki:Elysar), who was twenty years younger than him, succeeded him as [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester).(wiki:Benifer.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Appearances & Description

Benifer was among the most diligent of the forty odd men who served as Grand Maester in terms of keeping written records, and his letters and journals provided detailed accounts of all he saw and did in service to his kings.(wiki:Benifer.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
