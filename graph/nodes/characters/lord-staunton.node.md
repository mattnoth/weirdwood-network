## Origins

At the outbreak of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord Staunton was one of the lesser lords that sat on the [black council](wiki:Black_council) in support of Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). Lord Staunton counted himself among Rhaenyra's loyalists, well-aware that King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) would name him a traitor, having already received a summons from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) demanding an oath of loyalty from him. At the first meeting of the black council, Lord Staunton agreed with Princess [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aemon)) that Rhaenyra would need to win the war with her superior number of [dragons](wiki:Dragon).(wiki:Lord_Staunton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
After the [sack of Duskendale](wiki:Sack_of_Duskendale), Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) advanced with his host towards [Rook's Rest](wiki:Rook's_Rest).(wiki:Lord_Staunton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Having already received word of Cole's march,(wiki:Lord_Staunton.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Lord Staunton closed his gates, but could not stop the [greens](wiki:Greens) from burning his lands and attacking his [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) and herds. He sent a [raven](wiki:Raven) to Dragonstone when supplies ran low,(wiki:Lord_Staunton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) and Princess Rhaenys and her dragon [Meleys](wiki:Meleys) arrived nine days later. However, Aegon II with [Sunfyre](wiki:Sunfyre) and Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) with [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) joined the [battle](wiki:Battle_of_Rook's_Rest), killing Rhaenys and Meleys. Criston and Aemond then took Rook's Rest and put the garrison to the sword. Lord Staunton's head was taken to King's Landing and mounted above the [Old Gate](wiki:Old_Gate).(wiki:Lord_Staunton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
After his death, his [widow](wiki:Lady_Staunton) took charge of Rook's Rest and rode from [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with Ser [Corwyn Corbray](wiki:Corwyn_Corbray) after the ascension of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord_Staunton.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
