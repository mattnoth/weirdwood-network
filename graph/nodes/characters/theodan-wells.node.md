## Narrative Arc

### A Feast of Crows

When the [Faith Militant](wiki:Faith_Militant) is revived, Ser Theodan is one of the first [knights](wiki:Knight) to pledge himself to the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons), and he is named their commander by the new [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon), the [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow). Theodan escorts [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) when she visits the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) to see his High Holiness after the arrest of [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Theodan_Wells.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

### A Dance with Dragons

Theodan is charged by the High Septon to lead the escort comprised of Warrior's Sons and [Poor Fellows](wiki:Poor_Fellows) to see Cersei safely to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) as she does her [walk of atonement](wiki:Walk_of_atonement) through the streets of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). During the walk Theodan drags Cersei as he fears the crowd is becoming restless.(wiki:Theodan_Wells.cite_ref-Radwd65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
