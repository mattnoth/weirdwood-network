## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

During the [battle at the burning septry](wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry), Notch fires arrows as the [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions).(wiki:Notch.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

Notch tells [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) and [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) how [House Goodbrook](wiki:House_Goodbrook) suffered under Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully) for supporting the [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Notch.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

After the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Notch is among the outlaws who help kill [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey) at [Oldstones](wiki:Oldstones).(wiki:Notch.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Notch is listed as a member of Lady [Stoneheart](wiki:Catelyn_Stark)'s band of outlaws.(wiki:Notch.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
