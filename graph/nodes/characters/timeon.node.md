## Appearances & Description

The swarthy(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Timeon speaks the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue) with a [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) drawl.(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) considers him to be cruel,(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) a killer, a raper, and a liar.(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

The sellsword wears a chainmail byrnie, and ties a stained scarf of red silk about his [iron](wiki:Iron) halfhelm.(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Timeon wields throwing spears,(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) a sword,(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) a dagger,(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) and a whip.(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Timeon is part of the foraging company which captures Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) near [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool), and he advises [Rorge](wiki:Rorge) to rape Brienne. After the sellswords beat the pair into submission, Timeon binds them together on Brienne's horse for the ride to meet their commander, [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat). Timeon and an [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibbenese) later shove Jaime toward Vargo, and the captain has [Zollo](wiki:Zollo) chop off Jaime's sword hand.(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

Jaime grabs Timeon's sword one morning and tries to escape. [Shagwell](wiki:Shagwell) dodges all of the knight's swings, however, and Rorge kicks the sword away.(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) When Jaime laughs after hearing that [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) is their destination, Timeon slashes Jaime's face with his whip.(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

### A Feast for Crows

When [Harrenhal is captured](wiki:Capture_of_Harrenhal) by Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane), the Brave Companions scatter. They [torture](wiki:Torture) an outlaw from the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners), and learn that [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) was with [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark), and they later hear a similar story from the innkeep and [whores](wiki:Whore) at the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn).(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4) While the others head to [Saltpans](wiki:Saltpans) and [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), Timeon, [Pyg](wiki:Pyg), and Shagwell go to [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) to find passage across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) to [Essos](wiki:Essos).(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5) The docks are guarded by Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly)'s men, so the three outlaws hope to be smuggled from [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) on a [ship](wiki:Ship).(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Dick Crabb](wiki:Dick_Crabb) cons Shagwell into buying a map to the [Whispers](wiki:Whispers), which he says is a popular [smugglers](wiki:Smuggler)' cove on [Crackclaw Point](wiki:Crackclaw_Point), but Dick fails to mention that ships have not gone there for thirty years.(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) The three sellswords camp in the ruins of the Whispers.(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-6)

Brienne has been tasked by Jaime with finding [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), who has gone missing after [the death](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). Sansa is believed to be in the company of the [fool](wiki:Fool) [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard).(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Brienne learns of Dick boasting of having "fooled a fool",(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) and Dick later agrees to escort Brienne to the Whispers so she can find the fool in question.(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) When they arrive, Shagwell kills Dick for leading them astray. Timeon informs Brienne about Vargo's fate, and also tells her that the Stark girl was not with a fool, but with the Hound. Timeon tells Brienne if she yields and gives them her [gold](wiki:Currency), they will let her and her [squire](wiki:Squire) [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne) go, after the three sellswords have had sex with her. Otherwise, they will rape her and take the gold anyway.(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-7)

During the ensuing battle, Timeon cuts Brienne's cheek with a throwing spear as she finishes Pyg. When he faces Timeon, he wounds her shoulder with a second spear as she charges him, but Brienne slashes his ear and cheek with [Oathkeeper](wiki:Oathkeeper), and then stabs the Dornishman in the belly. As she pulls her blade from him, Timeon tries to get her with a dagger, so she cuts his hand off, considering it revenge for Jaime Lannister's maiming. Timeon begs for [mercy](wiki:Gift_of_mercy), so she finishes him, then moves on to Shagwell.(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-8)

After Dick is buried in a grave, Brienne leaves the bodies of the three sellswords for [crows](wiki:Crow), but Ser [Hyle Hunt](wiki:Hyle_Hunt) brings their heads to Maidenpool. The heads are rotting and swarming with flies and maggots by the time they reach Maidenpool, but Hyle refuses to bury them. Randyll Tarly is incredulous that Brienne killed the three men by herself, but Hyle insists. The heads are mounted above Maidenpool's gates.(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Brienne later has nightmares involving Pyg, Shagwell, and Timeon.(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)(wiki:Timeon.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
