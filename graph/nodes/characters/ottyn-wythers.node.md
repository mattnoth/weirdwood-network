## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ottyn is part of the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) that leaves [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) in search of the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings). He commands the rearguard in the [column](wiki:Column).(wiki:Ottyn_Wythers.cite_ref-Racok13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Ottyn is one of the commanders of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) at the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men). He urges retreat when the wildling masses are discovered. He is second in command on the ranging behind Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont).(wiki:Ottyn_Wythers.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) [Chett](wiki:Chett) plans to have Jeor assassinated, expecting that the [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) would be succeeded by an ineffective Ottyn unwilling to pursue conspirators.(wiki:Ottyn_Wythers.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

Ottyn is killed during the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist) when a riderless horse kicks his face as he stares at the chaos and death, kneeling in the snow.(wiki:Ottyn_Wythers.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)(wiki:Ottyn_Wythers.cite_ref-Rasos18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) briefly contemplates Ottyn as a possible successor to Jeor, but then remembers he is dead.(wiki:Ottyn_Wythers.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
