## Origins

During the [riots of King's landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(the_Dance_of_the_Dragons)), Ser Balon led his men against the rioters, and managed to restore some semblance of order to the streets north and east of [Rhaenys's Hill](wiki:Hill_of_Rhaenys).(wiki:Balon_Byrch.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Balon_Byrch.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

After the death of Ser [Luthor Largent](wiki:Luthor_Largent), Balon was made the new [Commander of the City Watch](wiki:Commander_of_the_City_Watch). He accompanied the guard that escorted Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to find safe haven. When the escort was attacked near [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) by [broken men](wiki:Broken_men), he was slain by an arrow.(wiki:Balon_Byrch.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:Balon_Byrch.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
