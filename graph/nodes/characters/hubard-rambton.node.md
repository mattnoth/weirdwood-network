## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

When [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) has the castle [sept at Dragonstone](wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone) sacked so that statues of [the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) can be burned, Ser Hubard leads his three sons to defend it. They slay four of the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) before being overcome. Hubard and one of his sons are killed, while the other two are imprisoned along with Septon [Barre](wiki:Barre) and Lord [Guncer Sunglass](wiki:Guncer_Sunglass).(wiki:Hubard_Rambton.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

### A Storm of Swords

While Stannis is fighting the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Hubard's remaining sons and Guncer are burned alive for treason by Queen [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) and Melisandre on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)), as sacrifices to [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor).(wiki:Hubard_Rambton.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
