Allar is feared on the streets and loyally follows the orders of [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt).(wiki:Allar_Deem.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Obeying the orders of Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), Allar kills the infant [Barra](wiki:Barra), a [bastard](wiki:Bastard) daughter of the late King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), as well as [her mother](wiki:Barra's_mother) when she tries to stop him in [Chataya's brothel](wiki:Chataya's_brothel).(wiki:Allar_Deem.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

[Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt), now [Lord of Harrenhal](wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal), places Allar first on the list of six potential successors as [Commander of the City Watch](wiki:Commander_of_the_City_Watch) that he submits to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), not knowing that Tyrion has already chosen Ser [Jacelyn Bywater](wiki:Jacelyn_Bywater). Tyrion condemns the six to be sent to the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), and he gives an order to Jacelyn for Allar to be thrown overboard from *[Summer's Dream](wiki:Summer's_Dream)* during the journey to [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea).(wiki:Allar_Deem.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
