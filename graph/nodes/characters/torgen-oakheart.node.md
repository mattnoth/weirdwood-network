## Origins

In [48 AC](wiki:48_AC), Lord Torgen and Lord [Rickard Rowan](wiki:Rickard_Rowan) marched with Septon [Moon](wiki:Moon_(septon)) against the increasingly brutal King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), defending the septon and his followers when they camped beneath the walls of [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown).(wiki:Torgen_Oakheart.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) After the death of Maegor the Cruel, Moon continued to denounce the [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen) and the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(High_Lickspittle)). Lord [Donnel Hightower](wiki:Donnel_Hightower) refused to take up arms against Moon's followers, with many claiming the real reason was not wishing to offer battle to Lords Oakheart and Rowan. However, the lords were reluctant to offer their armies to the septon when he planned to attack Oldtown itself, with Torgen decamping a day after Rickard. Moon was later assassinated with the guards, ending his uprising.(wiki:Torgen_Oakheart.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Torgen attended the [Golden Wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC), where King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) entertained lords in the intimacy of his [solar](wiki:Solar), encouraging them to speak freely and share their views on the problems of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) and how they might best be overcome. Lord Oakheart thought the king was grim.(wiki:Torgen_Oakheart.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

In [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Torgen was present in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to celebrate the tenth anniversary of Jaehaerys coming to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Torgen_Oakheart.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
