## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Ser Byam participates in the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) under the command of Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) which goes in search of [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark) and [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder).(wiki:Byam_Flint.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

The men of the Night's Watch make their stand at the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men), but they are attacked by [wights](wiki:Wights). They retreat to [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep), but many are wounded. Among them is Byam, who has a wound at the shoulder.(wiki:Byam_Flint.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

The men are hungry and accuse [Craster](wiki:Craster) of keeping food from them. Craster reaches for his dagger but is killed by [Dirk](wiki:Dirk). Dying, Craster falls on Byam. It is not known what happens to Byam during the ensuing [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep), but afterwards [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) notices that Byam does not moan anymore.(wiki:Byam_Flint.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
