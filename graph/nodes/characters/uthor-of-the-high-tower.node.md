## Origins

According to legend, Uthor married [Maris the Maid](wiki:Maris_the_Maid), a daughter of [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand), before she could be claimed by [Argoth Stone-Skin](wiki:Argoth_Stone-Skin), who had won Maris's hand in the first [tourney](wiki:Tourney) ever held in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Uthor_of_the_High_Tower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

Uthor is credited with commissioning [Bran the Builder](wiki:Bran_the_Builder) or his son [Brandon Stark](wiki:Brandon_Stark_(son_of_Bran_the_Builder)) to rebuild the [Hightower](wiki:Hightower), turning it from a tall timber tower and a beacon to a structure of stone that rose two hundred feet above the [Whispering Sound](wiki:Whispering_Sound).(wiki:Uthor_of_the_High_Tower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
