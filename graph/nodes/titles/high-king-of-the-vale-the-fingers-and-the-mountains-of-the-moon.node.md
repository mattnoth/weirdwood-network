## Origins

[Robar II Royce](wiki:Robar_II_Royce) unified the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) in the [Vale proper](wiki:Vale_proper), the [Fingers](wiki:Fingers), and the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon) under his rule with the goal of fighting against the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals), who had already carved out kingdoms for themselves by taking advantage of the rivalries between the First Men. Robar gained the allegiance of numerous houses and clans, including [Belmores](wiki:House_Belmore), [Coldwaters](wiki:House_Coldwater), [Hunters](wiki:House_Hunter), [Redforts](wiki:House_Redfort), and [Upcliffs](wiki:House_Upcliff).(wiki:High_King_of_the_Vale,_the_Fingers,_and_the_Mountains_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

King Robar was the only one to bear the title, however, as he was slain in the [Battle of the Seven Stars](wiki:Battle_of_the_Seven_Stars), after which the entirety of the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) came under the rule of [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) as [Kings of Mountain and Vale](wiki:King_of_Mountain_and_Vale).(wiki:High_King_of_the_Vale,_the_Fingers,_and_the_Mountains_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
