## Origins

At the end of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord Cerwyn was part of the northern host marching on [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) led by Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark). He was Lord Stark's closest friend.(wiki:Lord_of_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Lord [Robard Cerwyn](wiki:Robard_Cerwyn) was wed to [Argelle Stark](wiki:Argelle_Stark), the granddaughter of Lord Cregan.(wiki:Lord_of_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_stark_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Title_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord [Medger Cerwyn](wiki:Medger_Cerwyn) comes to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) when [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls his banners.(wiki:Lord_of_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) The seriously wounded Medger(wiki:Lord_of_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) is captured in the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:Lord_of_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Lord Medger dies of his wounds at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Lord_of_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) His son, [Cley Cerwyn](wiki:Cley_Cerwyn), succeeded him as Lord of Cerwyn.(wiki:Lord_of_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

When the [ironmen](wiki:Ironmen) attack [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Torrhen's_Square), Lord Cley brings three hundred men as part of the relief force.(wiki:Lord_of_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Afterward Cley joins Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) and marches to the relief of Winterfell. [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) leads a [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) force in [betraying Rodrik's forces](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell); Cley is killed from an arrow through the eye.(wiki:Lord_of_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Cley is succeeded by his older sister, [Jonelle Cerwyn](wiki:Jonelle_Cerwyn).(wiki:Lord_of_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
