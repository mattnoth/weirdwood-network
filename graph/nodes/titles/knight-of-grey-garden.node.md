## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser [Harras Harlaw](wiki:Harras_Harlaw) is one of [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy)'s champions at the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Knight_of_Grey_Garden.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
During the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), Harras plants his standard beneath [Grimston](wiki:Grimston), the castle of [House Grimm](wiki:House_Grimm) on [Greyshield](wiki:Greyshield), and defeats seven men, slaying five in the process. The [septon](wiki:Septon) of the castle believes the [gods](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) have spoken and surrenders Grimston to the knight.(wiki:Knight_of_Grey_Garden.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Harras is named the new [Lord of Greyshield](wiki:Lord_of_Greyshield) by [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North).(wiki:Knight_of_Grey_Garden.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
