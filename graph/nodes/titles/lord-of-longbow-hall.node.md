## Origins

During the [Andal invasion](wiki:Andal_invasion), King [Robar II Royce](wiki:Robar_II_Royce) formed an alliance of [First Men](wiki:First_Men) Houses from the Vale to repel the Andals. He gained the Hunters as allies after he bested Lord Hunter at an archery contest. Robar's forces were defeated in the [Battle of the Seven Stars](wiki:Battle_of_the_Seven_Stars), however, and the Hunters submitted to [Artys I Arryn](wiki:Artys_I_Arryn), the new [King of Mountain and Vale](wiki:King_of_Mountain_and_Vale).(wiki:Lord_of_Longbow_Hall.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Lord [Alec Hunter](wiki:Alec_Hunter) travelled to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC) to assist to the [wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) of Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) and Lady [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon).(wiki:Lord_of_Longbow_Hall.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Lord [Eon Hunter](wiki:Eon_Hunter) was present at the [Tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) in [281 AC](wiki:281_AC).(wiki:Lord_of_Longbow_Hall.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Despite his old age, Lord Eon offers himself as [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn)'s champion in [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat).(wiki:Lord_of_Longbow_Hall.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Eon dies suddenly, and his two younger sons, [Eustace](wiki:Eustace_Hunter) and [Harlan](wiki:Harlan_Hunter), accuse the elder brother and new Lord Hunter, [Gilwood](wiki:Gilwood_Hunter), of having murdered him.(wiki:Lord_of_Longbow_Hall.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord Gilwood is one of the six [Lords Declarant](wiki:Lords_Declarant)(wiki:Lord_of_Longbow_Hall.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) who opposes [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish)'s rule over the Vale as [Lord Protector of the Vale](wiki:Lord_Protector_of_the_Vale) during Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn)'s [minority](wiki:Customs).(wiki:Lord_of_Longbow_Hall.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
