## Identity

Knights Inquisitor were agents dispatched by Queen Rhaenyra Targaryen during the Dance of the Dragons to hunt down enemies and fugitives after she took King's Landing in 130 AC. Rhaenyra sent knights inquisitor to pursue those who had helped Aegon II Targaryen escape the capital when it fell to the blacks — specifically Ser Willis Fell and Ser Rickard Thorne of the Kingsguard, who had accompanied the smuggled king to safety through a hidden passage in Maegor's Holdfast. The knights inquisitor were part of Rhaenyra's broader campaign of retribution upon seizing power, which included filling the city gates' spikes with the heads of perceived enemies.
