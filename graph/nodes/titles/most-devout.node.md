## Origins

Before the arrival of the [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen), the seat of the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) was the ornate [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown).(wiki:Most_Devout.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Many of the Most Devout were displeased when the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aegon's_Conquest)) advised Lord [Manfred Hightower](wiki:Manfred_Hightower_(Aegon's_Conquest)) not to oppose King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), as the Faith disapproved of the [incest](wiki:Incest) and [polygamy](wiki:Polygamy) practiced by House Targaryen.(wiki:Most_Devout.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
When King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) was approaching Oldtown during the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising), Lord [Martyn Hightower](wiki:Martyn_Hightower) had his men force the Most Devout from their beds to quickly choose a new leader of the Faith after the sudden death of the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aenys_I)). [Pater](wiki:Pater) was chosen as the new High Septon by the assembled wise men and women.(wiki:Most_Devout.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
Seventy-seven of the Most Devout escorted the [High Septon](wiki:High_Lickspittle) to King's Landing in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC).(wiki:Most_Devout.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) The High Septon and the Most Devout were concerned about the orthodoxy of Septon [Barth](wiki:Barth) when he was appointed [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) by [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Most_Devout.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Barth regretted that corrupt Most Devout were easily manipulated by kings and [lords](wiki:Lord).(wiki:Most_Devout.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_tesitng_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Twoscore of the Most Devout died of the [Shivers](wiki:Shivers).(wiki:Most_Devout.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Disregarding the Most Devout, [a simple stonemason](wiki:High_Septon_(stonemason)) and [a young boy](wiki:High_Septon_(child)) were High Septons during the reign of King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Most_Devout.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) The High Septon and the Most Devout now convene in the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Most_Devout.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
A third of the Most Devout died during the [Great Spring Sickness](wiki:Great_Spring_Sickness).(wiki:Most_Devout.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Seven of the Most Devout attend the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Tyrion's)) before the funeral for Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister).(wiki:Most_Devout.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Most Devout are among the hundreds of [septons](wiki:Septons) who come to mourn the late [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), with one of them growing faint from the body's appearance and smell.(wiki:Most_Devout.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
After the High Septon's death, the [small council](wiki:Small_council) hears that Septons [Torbert](wiki:Torbert), [Raynard](wiki:Raynard), [Ollidor](wiki:Ollidor), and [Luceon](wiki:Luceon_Frey) are likely contenders to succeed him, with [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn) reporting that Luceon hosted a dinner for thirty of the Most Devout.(wiki:Most_Devout.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
The Most Devout meet in the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) to choose the new High Septon. When Septon Luceon is nine votes away from being selected, [sparrows](wiki:Sparrows) armed with axes enter the Great Sept and force the Most Devout to choose the [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow) as the new High Septon.(wiki:Most_Devout.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
After being imprisoned by the Faith, Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) is asked to confess her sins by [Moelle](wiki:Moelle) and [Unella](wiki:Unella),(wiki:Most_Devout.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) septas of the Most Devout.(wiki:Most_Devout.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
