## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

In reaction to the [fall of Astapor](wiki:Fall_of_Astapor) to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), the Wise Masters hire the [Stormcrows](wiki:Stormcrows) and the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons) to defend [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai).(wiki:Wise_Masters.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) One of the slavers, [Grazdan mo Eraz](wiki:Grazdan_mo_Eraz), is sent to offer Daenerys fifty thousand [golden marks](wiki:Currency) to leave [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay) and head to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), but Daenerys refuses.(wiki:Wise_Masters.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Victorious in the ensuing [battle near Yunkai](wiki:Battle_near_Yunkai), Daenerys forces the Yunkai'i free their slaves(wiki:Wise_Masters.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) but does not conquer the city.(wiki:Wise_Masters.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Daenerys is successful in her [siege of Meereen](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen). [Cleon the Great](wiki:Cleon), who has taken control of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor), sends [Ghael](wiki:Ghael) as an envoy to Daenaerys. Ghael informs her that Yunkai is raising new soldiers, hiring more [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords), and sending envoys to other slave cities. The envoy offers a pact between [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) and Astapor to defeat the Wise Masters.(wiki:Wise_Masters.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The Wise Masters returned to slaving after Daenerys departed for Meereen.(wiki:Wise_Masters.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
The Wise Masters and their sellsword companies are successful in their [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor).(wiki:Wise_Masters.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The [Windblown](wiki:Windblown), under contract from Yunkai, give mocking nicknames to each of the topmost Wise Masters in order to identify them.(wiki:Wise_Masters.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
The Yunkai'i march north for the [second siege of Meereen](wiki:Second_siege_of_Meereen) and establish their slave pens and auction block just south of the [Skahazadhan](wiki:Skahazadhan).(wiki:Wise_Masters.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Yurkhaz zo Yunzak](wiki:Yurkhaz_zo_Yunzak) leads the Yunkish army,(wiki:Wise_Masters.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) but after his death the Wise Masters rotate supreme command among themselves.(wiki:Wise_Masters.cite_ref-Radwd60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

By the time hostilities resume between Meereen and Yunkai, the mocking nicknames given by the Windblown to the Wise Masters have spread to the Second Sons.(wiki:Wise_Masters.cite_ref-9) Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) sees two trebuchets defend by two dozen Wise Masters, each with their own detachment of slave soldiers.(wiki:Wise_Masters.cite_ref-10)
