## Origins

Lord Vance was slain in the war between the [Teagues](wiki:House_Teague) and the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Arlan III Durrandon](wiki:Arlan_III_Durrandon).(wiki:Lord_of_Wayfarer's_Rest.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Lord [Robert Vance](wiki:Robert_Vance) was a would-be [river king](wiki:River_king) during the rule of the Storm Kings;(wiki:Lord_of_Wayfarer's_Rest.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) it is unknown which branch he was from.
Lord [Ronnel Vance](wiki:Ronnel_Vance) supported Prince [Aegon the Uncrowned](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) against King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), but he was slain in the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye);(wiki:Lord_of_Wayfarer's_Rest.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) it is unknown which branch he was from.
In [49 AC](wiki:49_AC), Lord Vance attended the [Golden Wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) and observed King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord_of_Wayfarer's_Rest.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
At the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord [Tristan Vance](wiki:Tristan_Vance) declared for the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) and Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord_of_Wayfarer's_Rest.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Lord Tristan was slain by Ser [Adrian Tarbeck](wiki:Adrian_Tarbeck) during the [Battle of the Red Fork](wiki:Battle_of_the_Red_Fork).(wiki:Lord_of_Wayfarer's_Rest.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Tristan was succedded by the young [Hugo Vance](wiki:Hugo_Vance_(lord)).(wiki:Lord_of_Wayfarer's_Rest.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Lord Hugo supported Ser [Addam Velaryon](wiki:Addam_Velaryon) in the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton),(wiki:Lord_of_Wayfarer's_Rest.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) and he then joined the host of Lord [Elmo Tully](wiki:Elmo_Tully).(wiki:Lord_of_Wayfarer's_Rest.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

When the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) start massing men at the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth), Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) commands Lord [Clement Piper](wiki:Clement_Piper) and [Lord Vance](wiki:Lord_Vance) to guard the pass.(wiki:Lord_of_Wayfarer's_Rest.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
Lord Vance is slain when the Lannisters [attack from the Golden Tooth](wiki:Battle_in_the_hills_below_the_Golden_Tooth). His son, Ser [Karyl Vance](wiki:Karyl_Vance), becomes the new Lord of Wayfarer's Rest.(wiki:Lord_of_Wayfarer's_Rest.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Karyl is among the river lords at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) when [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) is crowned [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Lord_of_Wayfarer's_Rest.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord Karyl leaves Riverrun to defend Wayfarer's Rest.(wiki:Lord_of_Wayfarer's_Rest.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) He later command the defense of the fords north of Riverrun in the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords).(wiki:Lord_of_Wayfarer's_Rest.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Karyl is among the seven nobles who launch the funeral boat of Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully).(wiki:Lord_of_Wayfarer's_Rest.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) signs the pardon of Lord Vance in the aftermath of the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Lord_of_Wayfarer's_Rest.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) It is unknown if it is for Karyl or Lord [Norbert](wiki:Norbert_Vance) of [Atranta](wiki:Atranta), but afterwards both have been welcomed to the [King's Peace](wiki:King's_Peace).(wiki:Lord_of_Wayfarer's_Rest.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Both branches of House Vance are present at the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun),(wiki:Lord_of_Wayfarer's_Rest.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1) and their lords take part in the war councils.(wiki:Lord_of_Wayfarer's_Rest.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) After finishing the siege, Lord Karyl recommends to Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) he go to the [siege of Raventree](wiki:Siege_of_Raventree), since Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood) will never surrender to Lord [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken).(wiki:Lord_of_Wayfarer's_Rest.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
