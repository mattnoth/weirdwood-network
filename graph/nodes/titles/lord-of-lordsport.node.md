## Origins

Lord Botley was killed by [westermen](wiki:Westerlands) during Lady [Johanna Lannister](wiki:Johanna_Lannister)'s invasion of the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC).(wiki:Lord_of_Lordsport.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Lord [Sawane Botley](wiki:Sawane_Botley) bought a difficult horse at [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) in [298 AC](wiki:298_AC).(wiki:Lord_of_Lordsport.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord Sawane sells the horse to [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy), who gives it the name [Smiler](wiki:Smiler).(wiki:Lord_of_Lordsport.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Sawane stands against [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) and claims that the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair) belongs to Theon after King [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy)'s death. Euron Crow's Eye has Sawane drowned for that.(wiki:Lord_of_Lordsport.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) [Germund Botley](wiki:Germund_Botley) pledges fealty to Euron, inheriting his brother's lordship instead of his nephews, and retaining the part of the Botley lands that are not awarded to Lord [Waldon Wynch](wiki:Waldon_Wynch) for his support.(wiki:Lord_of_Lordsport.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

During the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), [Tristifer Botley](wiki:Tristifer_Botley), Sawane's second son and the lawful Lord of Lordsport, supports Balon's daughter, [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), while Germund is one of Euron's champions.(wiki:Lord_of_Lordsport.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Lordsport.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Lord Germund participates in the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields). He is spotted by [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) in the hall of [Lord Hewett's castle](wiki:Lord_Hewett's_castle) with a rope of pearls around his neck.(wiki:Lord_of_Lordsport.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After Euron is proclaimed [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North) at the kingsmoot, Tristifer sails on his longship to [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte) with three others of Asha's as she contemplates her next move.(wiki:Lord_of_Lordsport.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Tristifer proposes that the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) under Asha abandon [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) and try to make their fortune as traders. When [northern mountain clansmen](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans) approach Deepwood, Asha abandons the castle and leads her ironborn toward the [Bay of Ice](wiki:Bay_of_Ice). Traveling in the [wolfswood](wiki:Wolfswood), Tristifer refuses to sleep with [Hagen's daughter](wiki:Hagen's_daughter). They are defeated in the [fight by Deepwood Motte](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte) by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s men, however.(wiki:Lord_of_Lordsport.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) While Asha is taken by Stannis for his [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell), Tris and the other ironborn survivors are imprisoned at Deepwood.(wiki:Lord_of_Lordsport.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
The ironborn at Deepwood are eventually ransomed from Lady [Sybelle Glover](wiki:Sybelle_Glover) by [Tycho Nestoris](wiki:Tycho_Nestoris), an envoy of the [Iron Bank of Braavos](wiki:Iron_Bank_of_Braavos). They escort him to Stannis, whose army is encamped at a [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village).(wiki:Lord_of_Lordsport.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
