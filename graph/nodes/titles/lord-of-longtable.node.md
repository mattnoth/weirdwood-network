## Origins

During the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye) in [43 AC](wiki:43_AC), old Lord Merryweather fought for King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) against Prince [Aegon the Uncrowned](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)).(wiki:Lord_of_Longtable.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Old Lord Merryweather was killed the following year by the outlawed [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons) who remained loyal to Ser [Joffrey Doggett](wiki:Joffrey_Doggett).(wiki:Lord_of_Longtable.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
At the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord Merryweather was at court in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). He was imprisoned in the dungeons under the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) along with other lords suspected to support Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) over her half-brother, King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen). When brought in front of the King, Lord Merryweather refused to support Aegon and was beheaded by the [King's Justice](wiki:King's_Justice).(wiki:Lord_of_Longtable.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Lady Merryweather, his widow, [yielded Longtable](wiki:Siege_of_Longtable) to Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower).(wiki:Lord_of_Longtable.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Later during the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton), levies from Longtable joined the [blacks](wiki:Blacks)'s host to fortify [Tumbleton](wiki:Tumbleton) against the Hightowers.(wiki:Lord_of_Longtable.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
In [136 AC](wiki:136_AC), Lord [Marq Merryweather](wiki:Marq_Merryweather) was chosen by lot in a [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council) to become a [regent](wiki:Regent) for King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord_of_Longtable.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) When Aegon [came of age](wiki:Customs) later that year, he dismissed Marq from his office.(wiki:Lord_of_Longtable.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Lord [Owen Merryweather](wiki:Owen_Merryweather) replaced Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) for King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) after the former resigned.(wiki:Lord_of_Longtable.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) When tensions grew between Aerys and his heir, Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), it fell to Lord Owen and Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) to keep the peace between their supporters.(wiki:Lord_of_Longtable.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Aerys II later stripped the Merryweathers of their lands and exiled Lord Owen in [282 AC](wiki:282_AC) for his failures as Hand to prevent [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Lord_of_Longtable.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Longtable.cite_ref-12)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Orton is present at the wedding of [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark).(wiki:Lord_of_Longtable.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) names Lord Orton to her [small council](wiki:Small_council) as [justiciar](wiki:Justiciar) and [master of laws](wiki:Master_of_laws).(wiki:Lord_of_Longtable.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Cersei later raises him to be the new [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) after she demotes Ser [Harys Swyft](wiki:Harys_Swyft) to [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin) and [lord treasurer](wiki:Lord_treasurer).(wiki:Lord_of_Longtable.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) After Cersei's imprisonment by the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), Orton and his wife, Lady [Taena of Myr](wiki:Taena_of_Myr), flee King's Landing and return to [Longtable](wiki:Longtable).(wiki:Lord_of_Longtable.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
