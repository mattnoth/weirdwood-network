## Origins

[Lysandro Rogare](wiki:Lysandro_Rogare), the head of the [Rogare family](wiki:House_Rogare) and the [Rogare Bank](wiki:Rogare_Bank), was a powerful banker and the most influential man in [Lys](wiki:Lys). He was elected First Magister by the people of Lys, who called him *Lysandro the Magnificent*. Lysandro ruled the city as a tyrant and later styled himself [First Magister for Life](wiki:First_Magister_for_Life). During his time as First Magister, Lysandro declared a day of feasting in honor of the birth of his grandson, Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen).(wiki:First_Magister.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

In [135 AC](wiki:135_AC), Lysandro drowned under suspicious circumstances when his opulent barge sank whilst carrying him from his [Perfumed Garden](wiki:Perfumed_Garden) back to his palace. His death plunged Lys into [chaos](wiki:Lysene_Spring) and led to the downfall of the Rogare family. Lysandro's eldest son, [Lysaro Rogare](wiki:Lysaro_Rogare), aspired to succeed him but failed. [Torreo Haen](wiki:Torreo_Haen) was elected First Magister, but he was [poisoned](wiki:Poison) along [his family](wiki:House_Haen) and supporters shortly after his election.(wiki:First_Magister.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
