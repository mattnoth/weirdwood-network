## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Acting as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) has Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) thrown into the dungeons of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) as punishment for betraying his confidential information to Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister).(wiki:Grand_Maester.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Accepting Pycelle's dismissal, the Conclave gathers to discuss who his successor should be. After pretending to consider two lowborn maesters, the Conclave comes close to electing Maester [Gormon](wiki:Gormon_Tyrell), who had been born a [Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) of [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden). Thanks to the presence of his [little birds](wiki:Little_birds) at the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel), Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys) learns of this, and warns Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), the Hand of the King. To prevent the Tyrells from gaining more influence at court, Tywin immediately restores Pycelle to his office.(wiki:Grand_Maester.cite_ref-Rasos12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

### A Dance with Dragons

Following the imprisonment of Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) by the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), Grand Maester Pycelle successfully seizes control of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) and the [small council](wiki:Small_council). He offers Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) the [regency](wiki:Regency). Together, they attempt to restore the damage done by Cersei and to mend the Lannister-Tyrell alliance that supports Tommen's hold on the Iron Throne. When he sees they are going to succeed, Varys, the former [master of whisperers](wiki:Master_of_whisperers), assassinates both Pycelle and Kevan.(wiki:Grand_Maester.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
