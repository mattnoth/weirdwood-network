## Origins

[House Dayne](wiki:House_Dayne) is an ancient family that claims origin to the [Dawn of Days](wiki:Dawn_Age). At the mouth of the [Torrentine](wiki:Torrentine), the Daynes raised their seat of [Starfall](wiki:Starfall). They subsequently ruled over the Torrentine and western [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) for centuries as Kings of the Torrentine and [Lords of Starfall](wiki:Lord_of_Starfall). Alongside the [Fowlers](wiki:House_Fowler) of [Skyreach](wiki:Skyreach) and [Yronwoods](wiki:House_Yronwood) of [Yronwood](wiki:Yronwood), the Daynes were the most prestigious and powerful kings.(wiki:King_of_the_Torrentine.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_kingdoms_of_the_first_men.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
King [Samwell Dayne](wiki:Samwell_Dayne) once sacked [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), the seat of the [Kings of the High Tower](wiki:King_of_the_High_Tower).(wiki:King_of_the_Torrentine.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
During [Nymeria's War](wiki:Nymeria's_War), Princess [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria) and Prince [Mors Martell](wiki:Mors_Martell) declared their supremacy over [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:King_of_the_Torrentine.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) The Martells soon met the Kings of the Torrentine in war, facing against King [Vorian Dayne](wiki:Vorian_Dayne), considered to be the greatest [knight](wiki:Knight) in Dorne. However, Vorian was defeated and forced to bend the knee. Vorian was subsequently sent to the [Wall](wiki:Wall) to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), alongside the other conquered kings.(wiki:King_of_the_Torrentine.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_the_coming_of_the_rhoynar.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The Daynes were reduced from petty kings to powerful lord bannermen of House Martell, and later aided the Martells in subduing King [Yorick V Yronwood](wiki:Yorick_V_Yronwood).(wiki:King_of_the_Torrentine.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
