## Identity

Warden of the North is a military-administrative title within the Seven Kingdoms, designating the supreme commander responsible for the defense of the North in the event of foreign invasion. During peacetime, the office is purely honorary. The title is traditionally held hereditarily by the lord of a great house — in this case, the Lords of Winterfell of House Stark, who were formerly Kings in the North before Aegon's Conquest. Following Torrhen Stark's submission to Aegon I Targaryen, he was named Lord of Winterfell and Warden of the North in recognition of his fealty; the title passed through successive Stark lords thereafter.

The title is granted by the Iron Throne, and while tradition favors the lord of the customary house, the king retains the right to award it to another. At the opening of the narrative, Warden of the North is held by Lord Eddard Stark of Winterfell, who accepts the post of Hand of the King but remains Warden until his execution on the orders of King Joffrey Baratheon. Following the Red Wedding, in which Lord Roose Bolton personally slew Robb Stark and played a central role in the massacre of northern and riverland forces, the Iron Throne rewarded Bolton by naming him the new Warden of the North, simultaneously legitimizing his bastard son Ramsay Snow as Ramsay Bolton. House Bolton thus displaced House Stark as the paramount power in the North.

Roose Bolton's hold on the wardenship was contested from the outset. Some northern lords — among them Wyman Manderly and Robett Glover — were openly distrustful of the Boltons, while houses such as Dustin and Ryswell, connected to Roose through his second wife Bethany, were among the first to declare for him. The Iron Throne pressed this advantage by conditioning the return of northern prisoners (including Wylis Manderly) on local submission to the new Warden. As Warden of the North, Roose Bolton coordinated the Bolton and Frey response to Stannis Baratheon's northern campaign, returning to the North after his son ended the siege of Moat Cailin.
