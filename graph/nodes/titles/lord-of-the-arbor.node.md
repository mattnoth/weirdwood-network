## Edges

## Origins

During the reign of King [Mern III Gardener](wiki:Mern_III_Gardener), Lord Redwyne built ships to strengthen the Reach's defenses against the [incoming Andals](wiki:Andal_Invasion).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Prior to his [conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Lord [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) was hosted at the Arbor by Lord Redwyne.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
In [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), Lord [Manfryd Redwyne](wiki:Manfryd_Redwyne) was the first non-[Velaryon](wiki:House_Velaryon) to be named [Master of ships](wiki:Master_of_ships) by the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). He served until his own death in [89 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) His eldest son, Ser [Robert Redwyne](wiki:Robert_Redwyne), succeed him but died soon after.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), [Lord Redwyne](wiki:Lord_Redwyne) declared for the [greens](wiki:Greens).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) At the end of the war, Lord Redwyne along with Lords [Beesbury](wiki:House_Beesbury) and [Costayne](wiki:House_Costayne) escorted the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for the wedding of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) and Queen [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen). On the trip back to the [Reach](wiki:Reach), they were joined by Lords [Rowan](wiki:House_Rowan) and [Tarly](wiki:House_Tarly).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
During the [War of the Usurper](wiki:War_of_the_Usurper), Lord [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne) joined his cousin-goodbrother, [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), in staying loyal to King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen). His main contribution to the war was the naval blockade of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) during Mace's [siege](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End), ferrying men across [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay) and blockading [Shipbreaker Bay](wiki:Shipbreaker_Bay).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne)'s two sons, Ser [Hobber](wiki:Hobber_Redwyne) and Ser [Horas](wiki:Horas_Redwyne), are kept captive at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in the early stage of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), preventing Lord Paxter to join his men against [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Once they are sent back to their father and an alliance between the Lannisters and [Tyrells](wiki:House_Tyrell) is established,(wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Lord Paxter follows his liege lord and cousin, Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), and fight against [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) in the [battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) grants Paxter an advisory seat on the [small council](wiki:Small_council), along with a remission of taxes on certain vintages from the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor) for thirty years.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
Lord Paxter and his sons become fixtures of the court. As a wedding present, Paxter gives King [Joffrey I](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) a scale replica of [King Joffrey's Valor](wiki:King_Joffrey's_Valor), which is being built in the Arbor.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) They attend the wedding of Joffrey and witness [his murder](wiki:Purple_Wedding). Lord Paxter and Hobber are called as witnesses for the prosecution in the trial of Tyrion for the death of Joffrey.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord Paxter attends the funeral of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), and the wedding of King [Tommen I](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) and Queen [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
Lord Paxter's council seat is revoked by Queen Regent [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) after Tywin's death. The [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet) led by Lord Paxter, is sent to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) by Cersei to remove him from the capital and eliminate the nearest military threat.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) After the [siege](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone), Lord Paxter sails to defend the Reach against King [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy)'s [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) raiders.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Paxter and his Redwyne fleet are last reported as passing [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) during their voyage back to the Arbor.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Arbor.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)
