## Origins

Lord Hayford urged King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) to abdicate and was beheaded for it.(wiki:Lord_of_Hayford.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
[Lord Hayford](wiki:Lord_Hayford_(Dance)) supported [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) in the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). He was arrested by her half-brother's supporters and beheaded when he refused to switch allegiance to [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord_of_Hayford.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
[Lord Hayford](wiki:Lord_Hayford), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) of King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen), was killed during the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field).(wiki:Lord_of_Hayford.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
