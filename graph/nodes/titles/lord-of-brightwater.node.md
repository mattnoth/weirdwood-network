## Origins

[Lord Florent](wiki:Lord_Florent) hired Ser [Arlan of Pennytree](wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree) and his squire, [Duncan](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall), in his service for half of the year [206 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Lord_of_Brightwater.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent) was born at Brightwater Keep.(wiki:Lord_of_Brightwater.cite_ref-Rawoiafalester_florent.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) He was once unhorsed in a [tourney](wiki:Tourney) by Ser [Denys Mallister](wiki:Denys_Mallister).(wiki:Lord_of_Brightwater.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord Alester follows [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) in supporting Lord [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) as king at the start of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Lord_of_Brightwater.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) After Renly's death, Alester rallies his banners and is the first of those who swore to Renly to go over to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s cause.(wiki:Lord_of_Brightwater.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Lord Alester quickly converts to the worship of [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor), just like his niece and Stannis's wife, [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent), did before him.(wiki:Lord_of_Brightwater.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) He is named Stannis's [Hand](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) mostly due to his connection through his niece.(wiki:Lord_of_Brightwater.cite_ref-Rawoiafalester_florent.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

For their support of Stannis, the Florents are stripped of [Brightwater Keep](wiki:Brightwater_Keep) by the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Lord_of_Brightwater.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Lord Alester tries to make peace with [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) by having Stannis give up his claim to the Iron Throne in exchange for him being named [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End) and [Dragonstone](wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone), with Florent vowing to do the same in exchange for Brightwater Keep and all his lands. For this treason, he is imprisoned in [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone)'s dungeons, placed in the same cell as Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth).(wiki:Lord_of_Brightwater.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) When Ser Davos is retrieved from the dungeons, Alester's brother, Ser [Axell Florent](wiki:Axell_Florent), denounces him as a traitor and takes the torch from his cell.(wiki:Lord_of_Brightwater.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
The Iron Throne raises Ser [Garlan Tyrell](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell), the second son of Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), to Lord of Brightwater Keep as a reward for the Battle of Blackwater.(wiki:Lord_of_Brightwater.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord Alester is reported to have been sacrificed by [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) to [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor) as a traitor. His son [Alekyne](wiki:Alekyne_Florent) succeeds him as the attained Lord of Brightwater Keep, though he has fled to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown).(wiki:Lord_of_Brightwater.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
Garlan is preparing men to press his claim on Brightwater Keep, which is held by Ser [Colin Florent](wiki:Colin_Florent),(wiki:Lord_of_Brightwater.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) when news of the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn)'s [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields) reaches him. He plans on gathering men to retake the islands instead of marching on Brightwater Keep.(wiki:Lord_of_Brightwater.cite_ref-Raffc32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
