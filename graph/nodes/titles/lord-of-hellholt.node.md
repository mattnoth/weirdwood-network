## Origins

After the death of [Mors Martell](wiki:Mors_Martell), Princess [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria) took the aged [Lord Uller](wiki:Lord_Uller_(husband_of_Nymeria)) as her second husband.(wiki:Lord_of_Hellholt.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
During the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), Lord [Harlan Tyrell](wiki:Harlan_Tyrell) was sent by King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) to the Hellholt, seat of Lord [Uthor Uller](wiki:Uthor_Uller). However, by the time Harlan's host arrived, the castle was abandoned. During the [Dragon's Wroth](wiki:Dragon's_Wroth), four consecutive Lords Uller (Uthor was possibly amongst them) were murdered for the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne)'s ransom on their head.(wiki:Lord_of_Hellholt.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
