## Origins

When the *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* of Khal [Temmo](wiki:Temmo) threatened [Qohor](wiki:Qohor) four centuries ago, the [Free City](wiki:Free_Cities) doubled their guard and hired two companies of [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords), the [Bright Banners](wiki:Bright_Banners) and the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons). Temmo's [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) defeated the Qohorik heavy horse, and the mercenary companies fled the battlefield. A Qohorik was able to hire three thousand [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied), however, and the Dothraki were unable to defeat the [eunuch](wiki:Eunuch) soldiers after they arrived. According to Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont), the city's guard has consisted exclusively of Unsullied since the famed [Three Thousand of Qohor](wiki:Three_Thousand_of_Qohor).
