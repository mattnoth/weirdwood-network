## Origins

In [Braavos](wiki:Braavos), justiciars are civil servants. Like [keyholders](wiki:Keyholders), they dress in drab coats of brown and grey.(wiki:Justiciar.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
In [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell) employs justiciars to sit in judgment in [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) while remaining at the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens).(wiki:Justiciar.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After the death of [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), Queen [Regent](wiki:Regent) [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) replaces the office of [master of laws](wiki:Master_of_laws) with the office of "justiciar", in the style of the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities), and grants the post to [Orton Merryweather](wiki:Orton_Merryweather).(wiki:Justiciar.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
During her time as "Cat of the Canals", [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) sells clams and cockles to keyholders and justiciars.(wiki:Justiciar.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After assuming the regency, Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) restores the office of master of laws but also maintains that of justiciar and names Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) to the offices.(wiki:Justiciar.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Justiciar.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
