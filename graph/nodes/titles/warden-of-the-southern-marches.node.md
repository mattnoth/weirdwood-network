## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

During the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), the young [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) and [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident), marches north from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) to attend the wedding of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) at the [Twins](wiki:Twins) and to [retake Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin).(wiki:Warden_of_the_Southern_Marches.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Robb creates the title Warden of the Southern Marches for Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully), who remains behind at Riverrun, and the king tasks his great uncle with holding the [Trident](wiki:Trident).(wiki:Warden_of_the_Southern_Marches.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Brynden continues to hold Riverrun for [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) after Robb's death in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Warden_of_the_Southern_Marches.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) resolves the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), but Brynden is able to escape before the castle falls to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Warden_of_the_Southern_Marches.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
