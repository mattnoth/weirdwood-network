## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The Green Grace of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), [Galazza Galare](wiki:Galazza_Galare), acts as an advisor to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), [Queen of Meereen](wiki:Queen_of_Meereen).(wiki:Green_Grace.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Two of Daenerys's favorite [cupbearers and hostages](wiki:Cupbearers_of_Daenerys_Targaryen) are [Qezza](wiki:Qezza) and [Grazhar](wiki:Grazhar), cousins to the Green Grace.(wiki:Green_Grace.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
The [Green Grace of Astapor](wiki:Green_Grace_(Astapor)) remains unnamed. During the [siege of Astapor](wiki:Siege_of_Astapor), their Green Grace has a vision that [Cleon](wiki:Cleon), the Butcher King, can save the city from the besieging forces of [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai), despite the fact that the man was already dead and buried. The corpse of Cleon is fitted back into his [armor](wiki:Armor) and strapped onto the back of a starving [horse](wiki:Horse), which leads the remnants of his new [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) on a failed sortie. They encounter a legion from [New Ghis](wiki:New_Ghis), however, and are all killed. For her failed prophecy, the Green Grace of Astapor is impaled upon a stake in the [Plaza of Punishment](wiki:Plaza_of_Punishment) and left there until she dies.(wiki:Green_Grace.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell), one of the besiegers, recalls the priestess in her torn robes, impaled upon a stake and attended by a cloud of glistening green flies.(wiki:Green_Grace.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
