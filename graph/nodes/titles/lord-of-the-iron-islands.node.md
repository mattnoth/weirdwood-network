## Origins

[House Hoare](wiki:House_Hoare), which ruled as [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers), was destroyed during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest). After the death of King [Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare) in the [burning of Harrenhal](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal), King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) granted rule over the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) to Lord [Edmyn Tully](wiki:Edmyn_Tully), naming him [Lord Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

The [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) fell into civil war as a result of Harren the Black's death, and in [2 AC](wiki:2_AC) Aegon led an [invasion of the Iron Islands](wiki:Invasion_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands.cite_ref-Rfabthe_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) After defeating claimants who refused to kneel, Aegon the Conqueror allowed the surviving ironborn to choose who would rule the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) in his name.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_greyjoys_of_pyke.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Lord [Vickon](wiki:Vickon_Greyjoy) of [House Greyjoy](wiki:House_Greyjoy) was chosen.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Lord [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) declared himself [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands) in [289 AC](wiki:289_AC), but [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion) was defeated by King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
