## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

On the outset of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), [Lord Estermont](wiki:Lord_Estermont), an ancient man,(wiki:Lord_of_Greenstone.cite_ref-Lord_4-1) declares for King [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord_of_Greenstone.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) When he is preparing for battle against his brother [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), Renly decides Lord Estermont will command the reserve of his army.(wiki:Lord_of_Greenstone.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) After [Renly's death](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Lord Estermont turns his support to Stannis.(wiki:Lord_of_Greenstone.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Lord Estermont(wiki:Lord_of_Greenstone.cite_ref-Lord_4-2) and his men fight in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). Lord Estermont and two of his relatives, Ser [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Estermont) and Ser [Alyn Estermont](wiki:Alyn_Estermont), are made prisoners and bend the knee to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) after Stannis's defeat.(wiki:Lord_of_Greenstone.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Lord Estermont(wiki:Lord_of_Greenstone.cite_ref-Lord_4-3) is a guest at King Joffrey's [royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) to [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell). He declares at [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s trial that he saw Tyrion pick up the [wedding chalice](wiki:Wedding_chalice) as Joffrey was dying and poured the remaining [wine](wiki:Wine) onto the floor.(wiki:Lord_of_Greenstone.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Feast for Crows

[Eldon Estermont](wiki:Eldon_Estermont), the new Lord of Greenstone,(wiki:Lord_of_Greenstone.cite_ref-Lord_4-4) marries [Sylva Santagar](wiki:Sylva_Santagar).(wiki:Lord_of_Greenstone.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
