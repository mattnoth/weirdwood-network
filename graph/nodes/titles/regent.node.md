## Origins

Septon [Robeson](wiki:Robeson) acted as regent for the [newborn son](wiki:Triston_Hightower) of the late Lord [Damon Hightower](wiki:Damon_Hightower).(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) A member of [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) served as regent for the young King [Garland VI Gardener](wiki:Garland_VI_Gardener).(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_house_tyrell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Queen [Sharra Arryn](wiki:Sharra_Arryn) was the regent of the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) for her young son [Ronnel Arryn](wiki:Ronnel_Arryn_(king)), [King of Mountain and Vale](wiki:King_of_Mountain_and_Vale), during the [War of Conquest](wiki:War_of_Conquest).(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) served as Queen Regent during the first two years of the reign of her son, King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), who was fourteen when he came to the throne in [48 AC](wiki:48_AC).(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) While Jaehaerys was still unmarried with no heirs but his young nieces, Queen Alyssa and her second husband [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), worried that should anything happen to Jaehaerys, his sister Queen [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) would be an unsuitable regent for her daughter, [Aerea](wiki:Aerea_Targaryen).(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) After Jaehaerys eloped with his sister [Alysanne](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC), there was a bitter conflict between the Queen Regent and the Hand over what to do about it, with Rogar suggesting putting Jaehaerys aside and naming Aerea the new queen; however, the [small council](wiki:Small_council) supported the Queen Regent.(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Jaehaerys chose not to announce his marriage to Alysanne until after he reached his majority in [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), so that he would not appear to be a boy who had wed in defiance of his regent's wishes.(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

While Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) was absent from [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) to fight the [Third Dornish War](wiki:Third_Dornish_War) in [61 AC](wiki:61_AC), his brother [Garon](wiki:Garon_Baratheon) served as regent for Rogar's young son [Boremund](wiki:Boremund_Baratheon). Garon continued to serve as regent after Rogar's death in [62 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

During the minority of Lady [Jeyne Arryn](wiki:Jeyne_Arryn), Lord [Yorbert Royce](wiki:Yorbert_Royce) served as her regent and [Lord Protector of the Eyrie and the Vale of Arryn](wiki:Lord_Protector_of_the_Eyrie_and_the_Vale_of_Arryn). In that capacity, he attended the [Great Council of 101 AC](wiki:Great_Council_of_101_AC) in her stead.(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_.E2.80.93_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

After Lord [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark_(son_of_Benjen)) died in [121 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), his brother [Bennard](wiki:Bennard_Stark) served as regent for Rickon's son Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark), until his coming of age in [124 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). Bennard was slow to surrender his power to his nephew, and Cregan chafed at the limits imposed on him. In [126 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Lord Cregan rose up against his uncle, imprisoned Bennard and his sons, and took the rule of the [north](wiki:North) into his own hands.(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

In [129 AC](wiki:129_AC), at the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the mother of the infant Lord [Lyonel Tyrell](wiki:Lyonel_Tyrell_(lord)) was regent for the [Reach](wiki:Reach).(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) During Lyonel's minority, his mother, [castellan](wiki:Castellan), and [steward](wiki:Steward), acting as his regents, thought better of their support for King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) and decided that [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) would remain neutral for the remainder of the war.(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

During the Dance of the Dragons, when King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) was severely wounded during the [battle at Rook's Rest](wiki:Battle_at_Rook's_Rest) and was incapacitated for a year, his brother Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) served as Prince Regent.(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)

After the Dance of the Dragons, Lady [Johanna Westerling](wiki:Johanna_Westerling) and her father, Lord [Roland Westerling](wiki:Roland_Westerling), served as regents for her young son [Loreon Lannister](wiki:Loreon_Lannister), [Lord of Casterly Rock](wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock). Lady [Elenda Caron](wiki:Elenda_Caron) was regent for her infant son, [Royce Baratheon](wiki:Royce_Baratheon), [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End).(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

In [131 AC](wiki:131_AC), [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) became king at the age of ten. The [regency of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III) featured a council of seven regents.(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1) Regents were replaced as they resigned or died, the last three being chosen by lot at the [Great Council of 136 AC](wiki:Great_Council_of_136_AC).(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Aegon greatly chafed under the control of his regents, his royal decisions and appointments set aside or ignored by them.(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) In late [136 AC](wiki:136_AC), the regents and the Hand of the King, Lord [Torrhen Manderly](wiki:Torrhen_Manderly), planned a royal progress to celebrate the king's coming of age, but Aegon coldly dismissed his regents and Hand on his [name day](wiki:Name_day), and canceled the progress.(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

When Aegon III died in [157 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), his eldest son, [Daeron I Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen), was only fourteen years old when he took the throne. His uncle and Hand, Prince [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen), chose not to insist on a regency while the king was in his minority, perhaps because of Daeron's charm and genius, or perhaps because of his memory of what had transpired during the regency of Daeron's father.(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

In [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), [Gerold Lannister](wiki:Gerold_Lannister) served as regent for his young niece, Lady [Cerelle Lannister](wiki:Cerelle_Lannister_(daughter_of_Tybolt)), but she died less than a year later.(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

At the [Great Council of 233 AC](wiki:Great_Council_of_233_AC) to determine the successor of King [Maekar I Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen), only a few lords spoke up for Prince [Maegor](wiki:Maegor_Targaryen_(son_of_Aerion)), son of Maekar's second son [Aerion](wiki:Aerion_Targaryen), as an infant king would have meant a long, contentious regency.(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maekar_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

In [281 AC](wiki:281_AC), the great [Tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) was announced. Some believed that Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) was the secret sponsor of the tourney, with the intent to gather an informal [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council) to discuss how to deal with the madness of his father, King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), possibly by means of a regency or a forced abdication. Some of Aerys's advisors suggested that he should disinherit his "disloyal" son, and name his younger brother, Prince [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen), as heir to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) instead. As Prince Viserys was only seven years old, his eventual ascension would mean a regency, where these advisors would rule as regents.(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) However, [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) put an end to these schemes.

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) rules the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) as regent for her young son, Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn).(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

When Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) leaves [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) for [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), his wife Lady [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) is left as regent for her son [Robb](wiki:Robb_Stark).(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Ragot6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

In the will of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), Lord Eddard Stark is named Lord Regent to [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon),(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Ragot47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) but his regency never transpires. Robert's widow, [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), declares herself Queen Regent to Joffrey after Eddard is arrested.(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Cersei's control over Joffrey is tempered by her father [Tywin](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), Joffrey's [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), and her brother [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), Tywin's representative in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Cersei continues serving as Queen Regent for the new king, [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), after the deaths of Joffrey and Tywin, and the flight from the capital by Tyrion. Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) rejects the office of [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) when Cersei refuses to make him Regent.(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Raffc3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

The death of Lysa Arryn leads to her second husband, Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), consolidating his role as regent for his step-son Robert Arryn, in his role as [Lord Protector of the Eyrie and the Vale of Arryn](wiki:Lord_Protector_of_the_Eyrie_and_the_Vale_of_Arryn).(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) is made Lord Regent to Tommen by Ser [Harys Swyft](wiki:Harys_Swyft) and [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle), after Cersei's imprisonment by the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven).(wiki:Regent.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)
