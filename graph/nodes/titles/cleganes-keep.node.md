## Appearances & Description

The towerhouse(wiki:Clegane's_Keep.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) has a fearsome reputation, one that lives up to that of its [landed knight](wiki:Landed_knight), Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane). It is said that servants disappear unaccountably, and even the dogs are afraid to enter the hall.(wiki:Clegane's_Keep.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) There is a village under the keep.(wiki:Clegane's_Keep.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Origins

The keep was given to the [first Clegane knight](wiki:Clegane_(founder)), the [kennelmaster](wiki:Kennelmaster) at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), who saved Lord [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister) from a lion and lost a leg in the effort.(wiki:Clegane's_Keep.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) A woodcarver set up shop in the village and made toys for the young [Gregor](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) and [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane); a dispute over one of the toys led to Gregor burning the face of his younger brother.(wiki:Clegane's_Keep.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) After a hunting accident killed the [boys' father](wiki:Clegane_(father_of_Gregor_and_Sandor)), Gregor inherited the keep and estates and Sandor left to serve [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Clegane's_Keep.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) raids [Sherrer](wiki:Sherrer), [Wendish Town](wiki:Wendish_Town), and the [Mummer's Ford](wiki:Mummer's_Ford) in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), then retreats across the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) into the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands). Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), charges Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) with riding to Gregor's keep and bringing the king's justice to the knight.(wiki:Clegane's_Keep.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Beric's party is ambushed by Gregor in a [battle at the Mummer's Ford](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford), however.(wiki:Clegane's_Keep.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
