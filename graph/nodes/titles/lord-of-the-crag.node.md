## Origins

Lord Westerling supported Prince [Aegon the Uncrowned](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I))'s claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) and fought for him the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye) in [43 AC](wiki:43_AC).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) His fate is unknown.

Lord Westerling died from the [Shivers](wiki:Shivers) in [59 AC](wiki:59_AC).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

After the death of Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister) in the [Battle at the Red Fork](wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork), Lord [Roland Westerling](wiki:Roland_Westerling) and his daughter, Jason's widow [Johanna](wiki:Johanna_Lannister), ruled over the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands) in the name of young Lord [Loreon Lannister](wiki:Loreon_Lannister).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Roland was part of the [regency of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III),(wiki:Lord_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) but he died of [Winter Fever](wiki:Winter_Fever).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)

Lord [Gawen Westerling](wiki:Gawen_Westerling) offered his daughter [Jeyne](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling) as a possible match for [Willem](wiki:Willem_Lannister) or [Martyn Lannister](wiki:Martyn_Lannister), but he was turned down by their father, Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord [Gawen Westerling](wiki:Gawen_Westerling) is captured by Lord [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister) in the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:Lord_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Gawen is held at [Seagard](wiki:Seagard) during his imprisonment.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Gawen is freed from Seagard after his daughter, [Jeyne Westerling](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling), marries [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), after the [storming of the Crag](wiki:Storming_of_the_Crag).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

Lord Westerling is pardoned by the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) after the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Feast for Crows

During the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Gawen expresses to Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister) his concern for his family within the castle.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) When the siege ends, Gawen is reunited with his family. They are part of the westermen who march back home.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Crag.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
