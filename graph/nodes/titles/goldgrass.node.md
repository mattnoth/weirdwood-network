## Appearances & Description

[House Stout](wiki:House_Stout)'s modest keep is near the eastern gate of [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) in the [barrowlands](wiki:Barrowlands). It has a hall, stables, and gates.(wiki:Goldgrass.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Lord [Harwood Stout](wiki:Harwood_Stout)'s young and pasty-faced guards wear leather brigandines and cloaks of gold and russet.(wiki:Goldgrass.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) returns to [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton) after unsuccessfully searching for missing [Frey envoys](wiki:House_Frey), he insists that the petty lord [Harwood Stout](wiki:Harwood_Stout) hold a feast in his hall, as [Barbrey Dustin](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin), [Lady of Barrowton](wiki:Lady_of_Barrowton), has barred Ramsay from nearby [Barrow Hall](wiki:Barrow_Hall). [Maude](wiki:Maude) and [Grey Jeyne](wiki:Grey_Jeyne), two of the [Bastard's girls](wiki:Bastard's_girls), kill one of Lord Stout's old hounds. After [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), [Lord of the Dreadfort](wiki:Lord_of_the_Dreadfort), arrives, he speaks privately with his son in the hall, with [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) the only other attendee. Roose then brings Theon with him to speak with Barbrey at Barrow Hall.(wiki:Goldgrass.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
