## Origins

The Lords of [Stone Hedge](wiki:Stone_Hedge) from [House Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken) were [river kings](wiki:King_of_the_Trident) during the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes) and became bitter rivals with their neighbors, the [Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood) kings from [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Lord Bracken died in the fighting between the [Teagues](wiki:House_Teague) and the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Arlan III Durrandon](wiki:Arlan_III_Durrandon), and the region fell under the control of [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

[Harwyn Hoare](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), invaded the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) during the reign of King [Arrec Durrandon](wiki:Arrec_Durrandon). Lady [Agnes Blackwood](wiki:Agnes_Blackwood) and her sons marched against Harwyn's [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), but they were attacked from behind by Lord [Lothar Bracken](wiki:Lothar_Bracken), who had made an alliance with the ironborn believing Harwyn would make him [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident). Lord Lothar helped Harwyn defeat Arrec's host at the [Battle at Fairmarket](wiki:Battle_at_Fairmarket), but the ironborn claimed the riverlands for themselves. Half a year later, Lothar led a rebellion against Harwyn, but Harwyn crushed the rebellion, sacked Stone Hedge, and starved Lothar in a [crow cage](wiki:Crow_cage).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

### Targaryen Era

During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Lord Bracken joined [Edmyn Tully](wiki:Edmyn_Tully), [Lord of Riverrun](wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun), and other riverlords who rebelled against [Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare), [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Lord [Humfrey Bracken](wiki:Humfrey_Bracken) declared for King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) at the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). Together with his third wife, [baseborn paramour](wiki:Bastard), and younger children, he was taken captive by Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen), when [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen)'s forces [took Stone Hedge](wiki:Taking_of_Stone_Hedge). After the [Battle of the Burning Mill](wiki:Battle_of_the_Burning_Mill), Humfrey's bastard, Ser [Raylon Rivers](wiki:Raylon_Rivers), yielded to prevent his father's family from coming to harm.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Near the end of the war, Lord Humfrey joined the [black](wiki:Blacks) host led by his liege, Lord [Elmo Tully](wiki:Elmo_Tully).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

[Lord Bracken](wiki:Lord_Bracken) was named [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) for King [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen) in [172 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). His daughter [Barba](wiki:Barba_Bracken) became Aegon's mistress, and later that year gave birth to Aegon's bastard son, [Aegor Rivers](wiki:Aegor_Rivers). When Queen [Naerys Targaryen](wiki:Naerys_Targaryen) lingered near death following her own childbirth a fortnight later, Barba's father spoke openly of marrying his daughter to Aegon IV, until she was sent from court following the queen's recovery. Lord Bracken later groomed his younger daughter [Bethany](wiki:Bethany_Bracken) with Barba's help to displace [Melissa Blackwood](wiki:Melissa_Blackwood) as Aegon's mistress, in which he succeeded in [177 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). Lord Bracken and Bethany were executed in [178 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), after Bethany was caught by Aegon in bed with Ser [Terrence Toyne](wiki:Terrence_Toyne), a [knight](wiki:Knight) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

During the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), [Lord Bracken](wiki:Lord_Bracken_(father_of_Otho)) sailed across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea) to hire [Myrish](wiki:Myr) crossbowmen for [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre). However, he was delayed by storms and did not return in time for the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) At the start of the reign of King [Aerys I Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_I_Targaryen), Lord Bracken was dying. Since his eldest son had died during the [Great Spring Sickness](wiki:Great_Spring_Sickness), his second son, Ser [Otho Bracken](wiki:Otho_Bracken), was set to inherit Stone Hedge.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

### Recent History

Lords [Bracken](wiki:Lord_Bracken_(Aerys_II)) and [Blackwood](wiki:Lord_Blackwood_(Aerys_II)) came to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) when [Lysa Tully](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) was young to put their feud before the judgment of [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), [Lord Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Hoster attempted to marry Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully) to Lord Bracken's daughter.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) arrives to end the [siege of Raventree](wiki:Siege_of_Raventree), finding Jonos abed with a [common](wiki:Smallfolk) woman named [Hildy](wiki:Hildy). Jonos hands Jaime a map with the lands he wants for subduing Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood), along with compensation for the damage Gregor and the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) did to his land and family. During his surrender to Jaime, Tytos agrees to surrender a smaller portion of Blackwood land than requested by Lord Bracken. Jaime upsets Jonos by ordering him to send one of his own daughters to attend Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) at court in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Stone_Hedge.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)
