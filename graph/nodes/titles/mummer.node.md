## Origins

Ser [Humfrey the Mummer](wiki:Humfrey_the_Mummer), a [hedge knight](wiki:Hedge_knight), was among the first [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) of [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) Queen [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) and her son, King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen), sponsored many mummers.(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aenys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
Mummers and singers heralded the birth of King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen)'s children.(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Rtrp.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), some mummers offered to attempt to tame [dragons](wiki:Dragon) on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)).(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)
Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen) "wed" [Megette](wiki:Megette) with the aid of a mummer who portrayed a septon.(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) He also fathered [bastards](wiki:Bastard) on mummer's maidens.(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)
[Varys](wiki:Varys) claims to have been born a slave in [Lys](wiki:Lys) that was sold and apprenticed to a traveling folly, a troupe of mummers who worked the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities), [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), and occasionally [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), by means of a fat little cog owned by the troupe's master.(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)
The [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) included a mummer show.(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)
Lady [Ellyn Reyne](wiki:Ellyn_Reyne) sponsored mummers when she held court at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) Mummers that featured [dwarfs](wiki:Dwarf) in their troupes learned to stay away from [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) and the Rock, because Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) dislikes his dwarf son, [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)
Before coming to the [Wall](wiki:Wall), [Pypar](wiki:Pypar) traveled the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) with a mummers' troupe. He can imitate a hundred different voices and loves to act.(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) The [Brave Companions](wiki:Brave_Companions), a sellsword company, are pejoratively called the Bloody Mummers.(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0) Archmaester [Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn) is said to consort with mummers and others with questionable reputations.(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)
[Hop-Bean](wiki:Hop-Bean) was a famous dwarf mummer who bought his freedom.(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-1) His children, [Oppo](wiki:Oppo) and [Penny](wiki:Penny), became accomplished at jousting while on animals.(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Radwd40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0) [Izembaro](wiki:Izembaro) calls himself the King of the Mummers.(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Mercy_8-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) sees a mummer on stilts along the [Street of Steel](wiki:Street_of_Steel) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0) A dozen troupes come to the capital for the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney).(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) listens to two unidentified men—actually [Varys](wiki:Varys) and [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis)—conspiring in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), but Eddard thinks they are mummers.(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

A mummer is among four agents, disguised as [House Lannister guards](wiki:House_Lannister_guards), sent by [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to free Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0) The mummer impersonates Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully)'s voice, but they are eventually caught and hanged by [House Tully guards](wiki:House_Tully_guards).(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0)
Within the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) has a vision of a mummer's dragon on poles among a cheering crowd.(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Mummers are hired to perform at the [royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell).(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Mummers perform at Margaery's court.(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0)
Arya, calling herself Cat of the Canals, befriends mummers in [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Mummers are sometimes used to officiate "weddings" involving the [Sailor's Wife](wiki:Sailor's_Wife).(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Raffc34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Quaithe](wiki:Quaithe) warns Daenerys against a mummer's dragon.(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0)
While at [Volon Therys](wiki:Volon_Therys), [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) notices a gaily painted mummers' barge next to the [Shy Maid](wiki:Shy_Maid).(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0)
When [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)'s eyes are taken at the [House of Black and White](wiki:House_of_Black_and_White), the [waif](wiki:Waif) also shaves her head in a mummer's cut.(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Radwd45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)
Tyrion, [Penny](wiki:Penny), and Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) are forced to become mummers when [enslaved](wiki:Slavery) and added to [Yezzan zo Qaggaz](wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz)'s [[grotesquerie].(wiki:Mummer.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0)
