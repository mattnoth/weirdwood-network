## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord [Guncer Sunglass](wiki:Guncer_Sunglass) supports [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) as his [bannerman](wiki:Bannerman).(wiki:Lord_of_Sweetport_Sound.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The pious Guncer is thrown in the cells beneath [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) after he tells Stannis he can not support him if he supports the destruction the castle's [sept](wiki:Sept) by the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men).(wiki:Lord_of_Sweetport_Sound.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Guncer is burned alive on the orders of Queen [Selyse](wiki:Selyse_Florent) while Stannis is fighting the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Lord_of_Sweetport_Sound.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) [His brother](wiki:Lord_Sunglass) succeeds him as head of the house and sails to [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) afraid [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) would want to burn him too.(wiki:Lord_of_Sweetport_Sound.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
