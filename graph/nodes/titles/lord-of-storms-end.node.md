## Origins

After the death of King [Argilac Durrandon](wiki:Argilac_Durrandon), the last [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King), his only child, [Argella](wiki:Argella_Durrandon), barred her gates. The garrison of Storm's End, however, preferred to surrender and chained and gagged Argella, delivering her naked to [Orys Baratheon](wiki:Orys_Baratheon), who was in command of the [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) army. Orys took Argella to wife, the [arms](wiki:Heraldry) and words of House Durrandon for his own, and was granted [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End) as his own seat.(wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

Lord Orys remained loyal to House Targaryen, taking part in the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), in which he was captured, and maimed by the [Wyl of Wyl](wiki:Wyl_of_Wyl), also known as the Widow lover.(wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Orys got his revenge during the [Vulture Hunt](wiki:Vulture_Hunt) early in the reign of [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen), when the Widow lover's son was captured.(wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) was the first Lord to declare for Prince [Jaehaerys Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) His daughter, [Jocelyn](wiki:Jocelyn_Baratheon), married Jaehaerys's eldest son, Prince [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)).(wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_house_targaryen_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Rogar's son, Lord [Boremund](wiki:Boremund_Baratheon), was an outspoken and staunch supporter of the claims of his niece, Princess [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aemon)), and a steadfast friend to her.(wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)(wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Boremund's son and Rogar's grandson, Lord [Borros](wiki:Borros_Baratheon), declared for [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), and captured [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for Aegon II during the [Moon of the Three Kings](wiki:Moon_of_the_Three_Kings).(wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)

Lord [Lyonel Baratheon](wiki:Lyonel_Baratheon) betrothed his daughter to Prince [Duncan Targaryen](wiki:Duncan_Targaryen), the heir to King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen). In [239 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Duncan broke his betrothal by marrying [Jenny of Oldstones](wiki:Jenny_of_Oldstones), after which Lyonel declared the independence of the [Stormlands](wiki:Stormlands). A [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat) against a knight from the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) and the promise of the hand of Princess [Rhaelle Targaryen](wiki:Rhaelle_Targaryen) in marriage to Lyonel's heir, [Ormund](wiki:Ormund_Baratheon), resulted in an end to the defiance.(wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)

Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) was betrothed to [Lyanna Stark](wiki:Lyanna_Stark) when she was kidnapped by Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen), Robert's cousin.(wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) When King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) demanded both Robert's head, and the head of [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), Robert's foster father, Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) of the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), rose in rebellion. Robert fought during [this rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) as one of its leaders, and was crowned King of the Seven Kingdoms at its end, following the death of Aerys II.(wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_glorious_reign.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Robert granted Storm's End to his youngest brother, [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Following the death of his oldest brother, King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), Lord Renly Baratheon declares himself king.(wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Following Renly's death, his brother [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) lays claim on Storm's End.(wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Following the loss at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Stannis's [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent), attempts to negotiate with the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), suggesting that Stannis relinquish his royal claim and remain both Lord of Dragonstone and Lord of Storm's End. Stannis has Alester imprisoned for treason.(wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Feast for Crows

In her ploy to gain control over her own [birthright](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) argues to Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) that Storm's End belongs to [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) by rights, following Rhoynish inheritance [customs](wiki:Customs).(wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
