## Identity

Master of Deepwood Motte is a title from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges

- REGION_OF: North (track_b: Region)
- HEIR_TO: Robett Glover (track_b: Heir)

## Origins

The [Glovers](wiki:House_Glover) ruled as [First Men](wiki:First_Men) kings after the [Long Night](wiki:Long_Night), but they were eventually reduced to vassals by the [Kings of Winter](wiki:Kings_of_Winter) from [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark).(wiki:Master_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover) supports [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), participating in the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood)(wiki:Master_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps). He objects to supporting King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and is in attendance when Robb is proclaimed [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Master_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Clash of Kings

With Galbart fighting in the south, [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) leads the [taking of Deepwood Motte](wiki:Taking_of_Deepwood_Motte),(wiki:Master_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and is known as its conqueror.(wiki:Master_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

After the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross), Galbart raids along the coast of the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands).(wiki:Master_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Storm of Swords

When news reaches Riverrun of [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover)'s capture at [Duskendale](wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale), Galbart fears for his brother's life.(wiki:Master_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

After receiving news of the death of [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North), Robb advances his plan to [retake Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin) while Lord Captain [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) is away. Galbart is ordered by Robb to go with Lord [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister) and Lady [Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont) to [Seagard](wiki:Seagard) and sail up the [Neck](wiki:Neck) to find [Greywater Watch](wiki:Greywater_Watch) so that the [crannogmen](wiki:Crannogmen) can direct Robb's army around the rear of the ancient fortress.(wiki:Master_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Robb is killed in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), however.(wiki:Master_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Though Jason later remains at Seagard,(wiki:Master_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Galbart's and Maege's whereabouts are currently unknown.

### A Dance with Dragons

Asha returns to Deepwood Motte after she fails to be elected queen at the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot), though she and her followers abandon the motte-and-bailey when [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) and the [northern mountain clans](wiki:Northern_mountain_clans) appear to retake the seat.(wiki:Master_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Galbart is restored as Master, though his whereabouts are still unknown. With her husband a prisoner in [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) and her children at [Ten Towers](wiki:Ten_Towers), [Sybelle Glover](wiki:Sybelle_Glover) takes charge of Deepwood Motte and sends trackers and hunters to guide Stannis's army.(wiki:Master_of_Deepwood_Motte.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
