## Origins

Lord [Lorent Grandison](wiki:Lorent_Grandison) was one of the three lords chosen by lot to become King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen)'s [regents](wiki:Regent) in [136 AC](wiki:136_AC).(wiki:Lord_of_Grandview.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

At the beginning of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), [Lord Grandison](wiki:Lord_Grandison) remained loyal to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) and together with Lords [Fell](wiki:Lord_Fell) and [Cafferen](wiki:Lord_Cafferen) planned to join their forces to bring Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s head to King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen). Robert defeated them in three [battles at Summerhall](wiki:Battles_at_Summerhall), however. Lord Grandison became Robert's ally thereafter and later died of wounds received at the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Lord_of_Grandview.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Lord [Hugh Grandison](wiki:Hugh_Grandison) was among the possible matches offered to Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell).(wiki:Lord_of_Grandview.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
