## Origins

The [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) fleet at [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea) arrests and executes smugglers who sell [weaponry](wiki:Armament) to the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk).(wiki:Smuggler.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)(wiki:Smuggler.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
When King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) sat the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), smugglers would be killed if caught by Ser [Tristimun](wiki:Tristimun) and the [sea watch](wiki:Sea_watch).(wiki:Smuggler.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), almost a year into the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End), the notorious smuggler [Davos](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) evaded the [Redwyne fleet](wiki:Redwyne_fleet)'s blockade of [Shipbreaker Bay](wiki:Shipbreaker_Bay) to smuggle onions and salted fish into [the castle](wiki:Storm's_End),(wiki:Smuggler.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) knowing the starving garrison would pay him the best price.(wiki:Smuggler.cite_ref-11) The food allowed [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s men to hold on until Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) arrived to break the siege.(wiki:Smuggler.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) As a reward for his service, Stannis [knighted](wiki:Knight) Davos, giving him choice lands on [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath) and allowing him to choose Seaworth as the name of [his new house](wiki:House_Seaworth). However, as payment for his past crimes, Davos lost the first joint from each finger of his left hand. Davos found the ruling just, and agreed to the terms if Stannis would swing the blade himself.(wiki:Smuggler.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

While incarcerated in the [Wolf's Den](wiki:Wolf's_Den), Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) speaks about his past life as a smuggler with [Therry](wiki:Therry). Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) enlists Davos to find [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) because of his smuggling skills.(wiki:Smuggler.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
