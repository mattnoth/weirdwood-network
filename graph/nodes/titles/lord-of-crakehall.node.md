## Origins

Lord [Sumner Crakehall](wiki:Sumner_Crakehall) and his squires, [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)(wiki:Lord_of_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and [Merrett Frey](wiki:Merrett_Frey),(wiki:Lord_of_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) participated in the campaign against the outlaws of the [Kingswood Brotherhood](wiki:Kingswood_Brotherhood). Sumner ransomed Merrett back after he was captured by [Wenda the White Fawn](wiki:Wenda_the_White_Fawn) and later kindly sent him back to the [Twins](wiki:Twins).(wiki:Lord_of_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

Lord [Roland Crakehall](wiki:Roland_Crakehall_(lord)) led the Crakehall men during the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing) at the end of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:Lord_of_Crakehall.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
