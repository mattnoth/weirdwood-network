## Origins

### Kingdom of the Reach[ ]

House Peake boast of several legendaries Lords of Starpike, such as: [Meryn the Scribe](wiki:Meryn_Peake), [Yrma of the Golden Bowl](wiki:Yrma_Peake), [Eddison the Elder](wiki:Eddison_Peake_(the_Elder)), [Eddison the Younger](wiki:Eddison_Peake_(the_Younger)), and [Emerick the Avenger](wiki:Emerick_Peake).(wiki:Lord_of_Starpike.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
A Lord Peake accepted the judgment of King [Gwayne III Gardener](wiki:Gwayne_III_Gardener) over a quarrel he had with Lord [Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) of [Dunstonbury](wiki:Dunstonbury).(wiki:Lord_of_Starpike.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_reach:_the_gardener_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Another Lord Peake married one daughter of King [Garth X Gardener](wiki:Garth_X_Gardener) while Lord Manderly married the other one. Both lords hoped to see their wives succeed their father as he did not father any sons. After a decade of war, a distant cousin of the late Garth ascended the throne as King [Mern VI Gardener](wiki:Mern_VI_Gardener) thanks to Ser [Osmund Tyrell](wiki:Osmund_Tyrell), the [High Steward](wiki:High_Steward).(wiki:Lord_of_Starpike.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Lord [Lorimar Peake](wiki:Lorimar_Peake) drove the Manderlys into exile from the Reach upon the behest of King [Perceon III Gardener](wiki:Perceon_III_Gardener). Lorimar was rewarded the Manderly castle, Dunstonbury,(wiki:Lord_of_Starpike.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) as well as a marriage between his daughter with Perceon's son, Prince [Gwayne](wiki:Gwayne_Gardener_(son_of_Perceon_III)).(wiki:Lord_of_Starpike.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

### Targaryen Era[ ]

Lord [Armen Peake](wiki:Armen_Peake) and his sons perished upon the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire).(wiki:Lord_of_Starpike.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
During the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising), Lord Peake remained loyal to King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen). His son and heir was killed by [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons).(wiki:Lord_of_Starpike.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Lord [Uther Peake](wiki:Uther_Peake) ruled during the reign of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and married one of Queen [Alysanne](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen)'s companions, Lady [Prunella Celtigar](wiki:Prunella_Celtigar).(wiki:Lord_of_Starpike.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
[Lord Peake](wiki:Lord_Peake_(father_of_Unwin)) supported Prince [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen)'s claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) at the [Great Council](wiki:Great_Council) of [101 AC](wiki:101_AC). Three decades later, his son, Lord [Unwin](wiki:Unwin_Peake), supported Viserys's son, King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) in the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). Unwin took command of the [green](wiki:Greens) army from the Reach after the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton) and led its retreat.(wiki:Lord_of_Starpike.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
After the death of Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon), Lord Unwin was given a position as one of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen)'s [regents](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III). He eventually became [Protector of the Realm](wiki:Protector_of_the_Realm) and [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), which he later resigned when the king would not marry his daughter, [Myrielle Peake](wiki:Myrielle_Peake).(wiki:Lord_of_Starpike.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Starpike.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)
Lord [Gormon Peake](wiki:Gormon_Peake) was a fierce supporter of [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) and was considered one of the finest [knights](wiki:Knight) during the reign of King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen). After the Peakes chose the side of [House Blackfyre](wiki:House_Blackfyre) in the failed [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), they had two of their castles stripped from them, leaving them only Starpike.(wiki:Lord_of_Starpike.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Gormon was a part of [Daemon II Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre)'s entourage at the [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) [wedding tourney](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls) in [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), and was subsequently executed for treason.(wiki:Lord_of_Starpike.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Starpike.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
In [233 AC](wiki:233_AC), Lord Peake rose against the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) launching the [Peake Uprising](wiki:Peake_Uprising).(wiki:Lord_of_Starpike.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
