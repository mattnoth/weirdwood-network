## Origins

### Pirate Kings[ ]

Prior to the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) and the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), the [Sistermen](wiki:Sistermen) were an independent people with their own religion, worshipping deities known as the [Lady of the Waves](wiki:Lady_of_the_Waves) and the [Lord of the Skies](wiki:Lord_of_the_Skies). Their monarchs acted as [pirate](wiki:Pirate) kings(wiki:Kings_on_the_Three_Sisters.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) whose longships raided the [Bite](wiki:Bite), the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea), and the [Shivering Sea](wiki:Shivering_Sea).(wiki:Kings_on_the_Three_Sisters.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) The [Borrells](wiki:House_Borrell) of [Sisterton](wiki:Sisterton) on [Sweetsister](wiki:Sweetsister) were among the petty kings of the Sistermen.(wiki:Kings_on_the_Three_Sisters.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### War Across the Water[ ]

Desiring to control the Bite, the [Kings of Winter](wiki:Kings_of_Winter) from [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) eventually retaliated against the pirate kings of the Three Sisters.(wiki:Kings_on_the_Three_Sisters.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) According to [maesters](wiki:Maesters), two thousand years ago the islands were conquered by the [north](wiki:North) in an invasion known as the [Rape of the Three Sisters](wiki:Rape_of_the_Three_Sisters). In response, the Sistermen bent the knee to [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) of the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) to expel the [northmen](wiki:Northmen), ending the era of kings on the Sisters and putting the archipelago under the rule of the [Kings of Mountain and Vale](wiki:Kings_of_Mountain_and_Vale). Conflict over the isles between northmen and [Valemen](wiki:Valemen) would continue for a thousand years in the [War Across the Water](wiki:War_Across_the_Water), with the Arryns eventually achieving nominal control over the Sisters.(wiki:Kings_on_the_Three_Sisters.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Kings_on_the_Three_Sisters.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### Sistermen's Rebellion[ ]

During the [Wars of Conquest](wiki:Wars_of_Conquest) of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen), the Sistermen rebelled against House Arryn, declaring [Marla Sunderland](wiki:Marla_Sunderland) as their queen instead of submitting to [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen). Marla was deposed, however, when a [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) fleet hired by Lord [Torrhen Stark](wiki:Torrhen_Stark), newly-named [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), approached the islands. Marla's brother bent the knee, ending the [Sistermen's Rebellion](wiki:Sistermen's_Rebellion), and Marla ended her days as a [silent sister](wiki:Silent_sister).(wiki:Kings_on_the_Three_Sisters.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
