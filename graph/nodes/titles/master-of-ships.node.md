## Identity

Master of Ships is a formal title designating the member of the Small Council responsible for overseeing the royal fleet and naval affairs of the Seven Kingdoms. The position is among the core Small Council offices alongside the Hand of the King, Master of Coin, Master of Laws, and Master of Whisperers. The holder of the office administers the royal fleet, advises the king on naval strategy, and typically commands the fleet in wartime.

The title was so closely associated with House Velaryon for the first century after Aegon's Conquest that it was considered almost hereditary within that family, as the Velaryon fleet formed the bulk of the royal fleet. Known holders include Daemon Velaryon (the first, appointed by Aegon I after Aegon's Landing; died at the battle in the waters off Gulltown), Aethan Velaryon, Daemon Velaryon (son of Aethan), Corlys Velaryon (the Sea Snake, later Lord of Driftmark), Alyn Velaryon (Alyn Oakenfist, who held the position under Daeron I and participated in the conquest of Dorne), and Lucerys Velaryon (who served Aerys II and supported the king against Rhaegar). In the present narrative, Stannis Baratheon held the position under Robert I. After the War of the Five Kings Cersei Lannister appoints Aurane Waters (a Velaryon bastard), who uses his position to commission new dromonds before absconding with the ships after Cersei's arrest. Mace Tyrell, Paxter Redwyne, Gedmund Peake, and Manfryd Redwyne also held or were associated with the title.
