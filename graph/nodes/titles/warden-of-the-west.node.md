## Identity

Warden of the West is a title from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges

- REGION_OF: Seven Kingdoms (track_b: Region)

## Origins

[Loren I Lannister](wiki:Loren_I_Lannister), [King of the Rock](wiki:King_of_the_Rock), was captured the day after the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest). Loren the Last submitted to King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen), and was named the first Warden of the West.(wiki:Warden_of_the_West.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), Warden of the West, and Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), supported King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) during [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion).(wiki:Warden_of_the_West.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), Warden of the West and [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), is murdered by his son [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) in the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand).(wiki:Warden_of_the_West.cite_ref-Rasos77.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Feast for Crows

[Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), the [Queen Regent](wiki:Regent), wants her uncle, Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), to succeed her father as Hand, but Kevan insists he will only do so if he is named regent in her place and she removes herself to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Warden_of_the_West.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Cersei instead names Ser [Harys Swyft](wiki:Harys_Swyft) as Hand, Ser [Damion Lannister](wiki:Damion_Lannister) as [castellan](wiki:Castellan) of the Rock, and Ser [Daven Lannister](wiki:Daven_Lannister) as Warden of the West.(wiki:Warden_of_the_West.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

Daven participates in the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun).(wiki:Warden_of_the_West.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
