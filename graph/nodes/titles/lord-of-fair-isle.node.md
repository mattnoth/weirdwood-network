## Origins

The [Farmans](wiki:House_Farman) ruled over [Fair Isle](wiki:Fair_Isle) as kings until the [King of the Rock](wiki:King_of_the_Rock), [Tommen I Lannister](wiki:Tommen_I_Lannister), brought the island into his kingdom by taking the daughter of the last Farman king to wife.(wiki:Lord_of_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

The old Lord of Fair Isle during the reign of King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) died along his first son at the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye) in [43 AC](wiki:43_AC) fighting for Prince [Aegon the Uncrowned](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)). The wounded Ser [Franklyn Farman](wiki:Franklyn_Farman) brought the corpses of his grandfather and uncle home. The lordship of Fair Isle passed to [Marq Farman](wiki:Marq_Farman), Franklyn's father.(wiki:Lord_of_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Lord Marq hosted Princess [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) at [Faircastle](wiki:Faircastle) for a few years, but Franklyn forced her to leave after his father's death.(wiki:Lord_of_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) of [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), invaded Fair Isle. [Lord Farman](wiki:Lord_Farman_(father_of_Lysa)) sheltered behind his walls sending out pleas for help, but no help ever came. The ironborn eventually captured the castle. While Lord Farman and his sons were ransomed back to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) for their weights in silver, his five daughters became [salt wives](wiki:Salt_wives) to Dalton and his brother, [Veron Greyjoy](wiki:Veron_Greyjoy).(wiki:Lord_of_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

During the reign of King [Aerys I Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_I_Targaryen), Lord Farman repaired his defenses after the attacks of Lord [Dagon Greyjoy](wiki:Dagon_Greyjoy).(wiki:Lord_of_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

According to semi-canon sources, Lord Farman built a fleet of [warships](wiki:Ships) to defend his coasts against the ironborn, in defiance of Lord [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister), who did not wish to give offense to the [Greyjoys](wiki:House_Greyjoy) of [Pyke](wiki:Pyke).(wiki:Lord_of_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-westerlands_7-0)

Some years after the [Reyne-Tarbeck revolt](wiki:Reyne-Tarbeck_revolt), Lord Farman grew truculent. Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) sent an envoy to Faircastle to sing "[The Rains of Castamere](wiki:The_Rains_of_Castamere)". Understanding the meaning of the message, Lord Farman never troubled Tywin again.(wiki:Lord_of_Fair_Isle.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
