## Edges

## Origins

The [First Men](wiki:First_Men) of antiquity only wrote with runes on rocks, so stories about the [Dawn Age](wiki:Dawn_Age), [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes), and and the [Long Night](wiki:Long_Night) were written down thousands of years later by septons(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) and [maesters](wiki:Maesters),(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_age_of_heroes.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) with septons choosing to include or alter details as they saw fit.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_age_of_heroes.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-1)

Seven septons are said to have been with the victorious [Andals](wiki:Andals) in the [Battle of Bitter River](wiki:Battle_of_Bitter_River).(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) During the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to the [Reach](wiki:Reach), King [Garth IX Gardener](wiki:Garth_IX_Gardener) made a septon part of his council and built the first [sept](wiki:Sept) at [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden).(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) Septon [Robeson](wiki:Robeson) served as regent for Lord [Triston Hightower](wiki:Triston_Hightower) and became the first [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon), and Triston built the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) in his honor.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) Septons stopped the [Sistermen](wiki:Sistermen) from casting [dwarfs](wiki:Dwarfs) into the [Bite](wiki:Bite) as offerings to their gods of old.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

Septons and [septas](wiki:Septa) were expelled from the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) by King [Hagon Hoare](wiki:Hagon_Hoare),(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0) and again by Lord [Goren Greyjoy](wiki:Goren_Greyjoy).(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_greyjoys_of_pyke.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)

During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aegon's_Conquest)) advised against opposing [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) after praying in the Starry Sept for seven days and seven nights.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Septons accompanied King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) during his [royal progresses](wiki:Royal_progress) throughout the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0) Aegon affirmed that accused septons could only be tried in the courts run by the Faith.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)

The [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aenys_I)) during the reign of [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen) opposed the incestual marriage of the king's children, Prince [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) and Princess [Rhaena](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)), having his objections spread throughout the realm by ten thousand septons.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-1) Septon [Murmison](wiki:Murmison), Aenys's [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), was expelled from the Faith for performing the marriage, and he was soon assassinated by [Poor Fellows](wiki:Poor_Fellows).(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-2) King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) allegedly killed a dozen septons before finding one willing to officiate his marriage with [Tyanna of the Tower](wiki:Tyanna_of_the_Tower).(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-3) Septon [Moon](wiki:Moon_(septon)) was one of the leaders of the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising)..(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-4)

Septons [Barth](wiki:Barth) and [Oswyck](wiki:Oswyck) helped King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) develop the [Doctrine of Exceptionalism](wiki:Doctrine_of_Exceptionalism),(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0) and the brilliant Barth later became King's Hand.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0)  [Casper Straw](wiki:Casper_Straw) led thirty armed septons in protecting [Rhaella Targaryen](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon)) from Ser [Orryn Baratheon](wiki:Orryn_Baratheon).(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1)

The writings of Septon [Eustace](wiki:Eustace_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)) are one of the main sources for scholars writing about the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_viserys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)

When Prince [Aemon the Dragonknight](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_II)) was carrying the snake-bitten King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen) back from [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), a village septon in the [Red Mountains](wiki:Red_Mountains) gave Aemon clothing and a donkey.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0) Baelor the Blessed convinced the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Baelor_I)) to dissolve his marriage with [Daena Targaryen](wiki:Daena_Targaryen).(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-1) Baelor eventually took the vows of a septon himself,(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-2) and he is thus remembered as the septon king.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen) filled his court with septons, [maesters](wiki:Maesters), singers, and [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen), which upset more militant members of society.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1) During the reign of King [Aerys I Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_I_Targaryen), a [hunchbacked septon](wiki:Hunchbacked_septon) was executed at [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept) for preaching against Lord [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0)

During the [Battle of the Bells](wiki:Battle_of_the_Bells), septons rang all of [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept)'s bells to alert [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) of danger.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0)

## Culture

Septons can study at great centers of religious training, such as the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor), but not all local septons are well educated.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Various_2-0) Upon taking his vows, a septon sets aside his last name, even if he come from a noble family.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Although septons take a vow of chastity,(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) some of the priests have fathered children.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Septons hang a crystal about their necks on a thong,(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Ragot9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and well-to-do septons wear white robes and woven seven-color belts.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Members of the [Most Devout](wiki:Most_Devout), the most esteemed septons who choose new [High Septons](wiki:High_Septon),(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) wear cloth-of-silver vestments and crystal coronals.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

Septons use a censer and incense during service.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) To reflect light and depict the rainbow, they wield a seven-sided crystal(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) or a faceted crystal sphere.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) The High Septon wears a crystal crown.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Septons can perform marriages,(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Ragot42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) provide prayers of passage when someone is dying,(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) and copy letters.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Septas who copy and illuminate texts for the Faith are known to alter or remove texts they deem unacceptable.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Followers of the Faith can seek spiritual guidance(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Ragot70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) and be forgiven for their sins by septons.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Some septons tend to the wounded, but the healing skills of [maesters](wiki:Maesters) are considered superior.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) When a man becomes a [knight](wiki:Knight), it is customary to stand a vigil and be anointed by a septon before reciting the knightly vows.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) Three times a year, the Great Sept has septons provide heraldic drawings and illuminations for the [White Book](wiki:White_Book) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

While many septons serve at a single sept in villages, towns, cities, or castles, there are hundreds of traveling septons who provide services for poor villages.(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)(wiki:Septon.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
