## Origins

According to legend, [Owen Oakenshield](wiki:Owen_Oakenshield) drove [selkies](wiki:Selkies) and [merlings](wiki:Merlings) from the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands) into the sea.(wiki:Greenshield.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
[Garth VII Gardener](wiki:Garth_VII_Gardener), [King of the Reach](wiki:King_of_the_Reach), expelled the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) from the islands and resettled them with warriors to help defend the [Mander](wiki:Mander).(wiki:Greenshield.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_the_gardener_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
In [89 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Princess [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aemon)) informed her grandfather, King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), of her intention to marry Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) while the two were in Lord [Chester's](wiki:House_Chester) hall on Greenshield.(wiki:Greenshield.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)) forced the submission of the Lords of the Shield Islands to the [greens](wiki:Greens).(wiki:Greenshield.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Four galleys from Greenshield joined the fleet of [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon), [Lord of the Tides](wiki:Lord_of_the_Tides), when he sailed to face [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Greenshield.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Greenshield is conquered by the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) during the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields). After the battle, [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North), raises [Maron Volmark](wiki:Maron_Volmark), already [Lord of Volmark](wiki:Lord_of_Volmark), to [Lord of Greenshield](wiki:Lord_of_Greenshield).(wiki:Greenshield.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
