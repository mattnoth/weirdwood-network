## Origins

During the reign of King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen), Ser [Michael Manwoody](wiki:Michael_Manwoody) was sent by the king on several missions to the keyholders of the Iron Bank. There is record of their correspondence between them regarding these negotiations.(wiki:Keyholders.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
