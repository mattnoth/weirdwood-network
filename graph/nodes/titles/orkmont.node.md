## Origins

[Urras Greyiron](wiki:Urras_Greyiron), the [salt king](wiki:Salt_king) of Orkmont, was crowned the first [High King of the Iron Islands](wiki:High_King_of_the_Iron_Islands) in a [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot) on [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk).(wiki:Orkmont.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Longships from Orkmont attacked the western shores of the [north](wiki:North) during the reign of King [Harrag Hoare](wiki:Harrag_Hoare).(wiki:Orkmont.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
[Urron Greyiron](wiki:Urron_Greyiron), a salt king from Orkmont, slaughtered his rivals at a kingsmoot on Old Wyk, becoming the first hereditary [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Orkmont.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Orkmont.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) [House Orkwood](wiki:House_Orkwood) of Orkmont helped to overthrow [House Greyiron](wiki:House_Greyiron) during the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands).(wiki:Orkmont.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_iron_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) During the rule of the iron kings of [House Hoare](wiki:House_Hoare), merchants and traders from Orkmont were known to call at [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport), [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), and the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities).(wiki:Orkmont.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Following the death of [Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare), [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers), in the [burning of Harrenhal](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal), an ironman of Orkmont declared himself king(wiki:Orkmont.cite_ref-Rfabreignof_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) but was killed in the [succession squabble](wiki:Invasion_of_the_Iron_Islands).(wiki:Orkmont.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_greyjoys_of_pyke.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
During [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion), the forces of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) conquered Orkmont.(wiki:Orkmont.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord [Alyn Orkwood](wiki:Alyn_Orkwood) of Orkmont supports [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) with twenty longships.(wiki:Orkmont.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) Besides the [Orkwoods](wiki:House_Orkwood), [Goodbrothers](wiki:House_Goodbrother_of_Orkmont) and [Tawneys](wiki:House_Tawney) also travel from Orkmont to the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Orkmont.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
