## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

With [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), leading two hundred brothers north of the [Wall](wiki:Wall) during the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), most of the four hundred men who remain at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) are [builders](wiki:Builders) and stewards.(wiki:Stewards.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Several members of the Night's Watch, including multiple stewards, plot to kill the lord commander at the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men), but their plans are interrupted by the [fight at the Fist](wiki:Fight_at_the_Fist).(wiki:Stewards.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Later, several of the stewards participate in the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep).(wiki:Stewards.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Feast for Crows

[Clubfoot Karl](wiki:Clubfoot_Karl), [Orphan Oss](wiki:Orphan_Oss) and [Muttering Bill](wiki:Muttering_Bill), former stewards since their desertion, remain at Craster's Keep following the mutiny, along with several other of their brothers.(wiki:Stewards.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

[Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh), the [Lord Steward](wiki:Lord_Steward), betrays Lord Commander [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) in the [mutiny at Castle Black](wiki:Mutiny_at_Castle_Black).(wiki:Stewards.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
