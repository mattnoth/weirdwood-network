## Appearances & Description

Silence has a single mast,(wiki:Silence.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) black sails, and dark red hull.(wiki:Silence.cite_ref-Racok24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Its decks are painted red to better hide the blood that soaks them. The ship's figurehead is a mouthless maiden with one arm outstretched. The figure is made of black [iron](wiki:Iron), with long shapely legs, a slender waist, and high breasts, and has streaming black iron hair and [mother-of-pearl](wiki:Mother-of-pearl) eyes.(wiki:Silence.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
The Silence is crewed by mutes(wiki:Silence.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) and mongrels from throughout the [known world](wiki:Known_world).(wiki:Silence.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) thinks of his uncle's ship as being lean and terrible,(wiki:Silence.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) considers Silence to be cruel and fast.(wiki:Silence.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)

## Origins

[Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) was exiled from the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) by his older brother [Balon](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), after impregnating [Victarion Greyjoy's third wife](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy's_third_wife). Euron sailed east on a lengthy voyage,(wiki:Silence.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) and he claims to have sailed the [Smoking Sea](wiki:Smoking_Sea) and seen [Valyria](wiki:Valyria).(wiki:Silence.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Returning to [Pyke](wiki:Pyke_(island)) aboard [Myraham](wiki:Myraham), [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) does not see Silence in port, so his uncle [Euron](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) must not be there to greet him.(wiki:Silence.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

The captain of the Myraham reports that Euron sailed the Silence into [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport) soon after the death of [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North).(wiki:Silence.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) According to a semi-canon source, the Silence had earlier intercepted a galleas containing four [warlocks](wiki:Warlocks) of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) who were seeking [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen),(wiki:Silence.cite_ref-Rawoiafeuron_greyjoy.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and Euron claimed their [dragon horn](wiki:Dragon_horn).(wiki:Silence.cite_ref-Rawoiafqarth.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw) tells Euron's niece, [Asha](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), he heard that Silence returned the day after Balon's death. Euron uses treasures from its hold to gain [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) support for the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk).(wiki:Silence.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
[Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) spots the Silence at anchor in [Nagga's Cradle](wiki:Nagga's_Cradle).(wiki:Silence.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6) Euron claims to have sailed the [Smoking Sea](wiki:Smoking_Sea).(wiki:Silence.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) [Cragorn](wiki:Cragorn), one of the ship's crew, dies after blowing the dragon horn.(wiki:Silence.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

[Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) was captured after the kingsmoot by Euron's mutes and chained up in the hold of the Silence. Several weeks later, Euron has Aeron and [Falia Flowers](wiki:Falia_Flowers) bound to the prow of the Silence before the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) engage the ships of [House Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) and [House Redwyne](wiki:House_Redwyne) in battle.(wiki:Silence.cite_ref-9)
