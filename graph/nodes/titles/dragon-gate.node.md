## Origins

In [19 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) decreed that new city walls with seven gates, each with a massive gatehouse and defensive towers, be built to protect [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). Grand Maester [Gawen](wiki:Gawen) and Ser [Osmund Strong](wiki:Osmund_Strong), [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), headed the construction, which took place between [20](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest)–[26 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Dragon_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
During his reign, King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) had his own wife and queen, [Alys Harroway](wiki:Alys_Harroway), [tortured](wiki:Torture) to death and executed. Following her death, Alys's body was cut into seven parts and mounted on spikes above each of the seven gates of the city, one of which was above the Dragon Gate.(wiki:Dragon_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Dragon_Gate.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), a [massive riot](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)) broke out in the city. The captains of the [Old Gate](wiki:Old_Gate) and the Dragon Gate, Ser [Balon Byrch](wiki:Balon_Byrch) and Ser [Garth the Harelip](wiki:Garth_the_Harelip), managed to sally out and restore some semblance of order to the streets north and east of [Rhaenys's Hill](wiki:Rhaenys's_Hill). Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and her escort [fled](wiki:Flight_to_Dragonstone) King's Landing through the Dragon Gate.(wiki:Dragon_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
During the [regency of Aegon III](wiki:Regency_of_Aegon_III), the King's Hand, [Tyland Lannister](wiki:Tyland_Lannister) ordered the city gates to be strengthened so they could better resist attacks from within the city walls.(wiki:Dragon_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

After the [arrest of Eddard Stark](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark), his daughter [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark) finds that the Dragon Gate, the [Lion Gate](wiki:Lion_Gate), and the [Old Gate](wiki:Old_Gate) are closed and barred.(wiki:Dragon_Gate.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) is sent by his sister, Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), to inspect each of the city gates.(wiki:Dragon_Gate.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
The captain of the Dragon Gate, [Humfrey Waters](wiki:Humfrey_Waters), is named the new commander of the [City Watch of King's Landing](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing) after the arrest of Ser [Osfryd Kettleblack](wiki:Osfryd_Kettleblack).(wiki:Dragon_Gate.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
