## Origins

King [Artys I Arryn](wiki:Artys_I_Arryn) raised the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon) at the foot of the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance) after the [Battle of the Seven Stars](wiki:Battle_of_the_Seven_Stars). King [Roland I Arryn](wiki:Roland_I_Arryn), fearing the castle was vulnerable to [mountain clans](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) and desiring a grander seat, began construction of the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) atop the mountain.(wiki:Keeper_of_the_Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_vale:_the_eyrie.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) The [Kings of Mountain and Vale](wiki:Kings_of_Mountain_and_Vale) resided at the Eyrie in summer and descended to the Gates in winter.(wiki:Keeper_of_the_Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
The [Lords of the Eyrie](wiki:Lords_of_the_Eyrie) have usually named kinsmen from [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) as Keepers of the Gates of the Moon, and the Gates are considered an Arryn castle.(wiki:Keeper_of_the_Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Like the [Knight of the Gate](wiki:Knight_of_the_Gate), the Keeper is not a hereditary position,(wiki:Keeper_of_the_Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) and the current Keeper is the [High Steward of the Vale](wiki:High_Steward_of_the_Vale), Lord [Nestor](wiki:Nestor_Royce) from the [junior branch](wiki:House_Royce_of_the_Gates_of_the_Moon) of [House Royce](wiki:House_Royce).(wiki:Keeper_of_the_Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) meets the Keeper, Lord [Nestor Royce](wiki:Nestor_Royce), when she brings the captive [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn).(wiki:Keeper_of_the_Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), the [Lord Protector of the Eyrie and the Vale of Arryn](wiki:Lord_Protector_of_the_Eyrie_and_the_Vale_of_Arryn), grants the title and the castle in perpetuity to Nestor and his descendants—in order to secure Nestor's support against the [Lords Declarant](wiki:Lords_Declarant)—thus forming [House Royce of the Gates of the Moon](wiki:House_Royce_of_the_Gates_of_the_Moon).(wiki:Keeper_of_the_Gates_of_the_Moon.cite_ref-Raffc10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
