## Origins

During the reign of [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen), [Savage Sam Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly_(lord)) was among the [marcher lords](wiki:Marcher_lords) who participated in the [Vulture Hunt](wiki:Vulture_Hunt).(wiki:Lord_of_Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryens_kings:_aenys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
In [49 AC](wiki:49_AC), Lord Tarly was present at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for the [wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) of [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) and [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord_of_Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Lord [Alan Tarly](wiki:Alan_Tarly) declared for Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) and the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), fighting and being taken captive in the [battle on the Honeywine](wiki:Battle_on_the_Honeywine).(wiki:Lord_of_Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) commanded the vanguard of the [Reach](wiki:Reach) at the [Battle of Ashford](wiki:Battle_of_Ashford), where he killed [Lord Cafferen](wiki:Lord_Cafferen) and forced Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) to retreat. Randyll's liege, Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), took credit for the victory, however.(wiki:Lord_of_Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord Randyll is among the lords that join their forces to [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)'s beneath [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge).(wiki:Lord_of_Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) After [Renly's death at Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Lord Randyll rides back to Bitterbridge where he captures Renly's stores and puts many men to death, mainly [Florent](wiki:House_Florent) men-at-arms, lest they go over to Stannis along with their lords.(wiki:Lord_of_Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Randyll joins his liege lord, Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), and the [Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell)-[Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) alliance at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater),(wiki:Lord_of_Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) where he commands the center of the host.(wiki:Lord_of_Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Randyll leaves the capital and march north towards [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) where he defeats the [northern](wiki:North) army led by Ser [Helman Tallhart](wiki:Helman_Tallhart) and [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover).(wiki:Lord_of_Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) He then captures [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool).(wiki:Lord_of_Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

The timid and cowardly [Lord of Maidenpool](wiki:Lord_of_Maidenpool), [William Mooton](wiki:William_Mooton),(wiki:Lord_of_Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) does not dare confront Lord Randyll, who rules the town in all but name.(wiki:Lord_of_Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1)(wiki:Lord_of_Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Raffc25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Lord Randyll arranges a [betrothal](wiki:Betrothal) for his son and heir, [Dickon Tarly](wiki:Dickon_Tarly), to William's eldest daughter and heir, [Eleanor Mooton](wiki:Eleanor_Mooton).(wiki:Lord_of_Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-2)(wiki:Lord_of_Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Randyll returns to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) upon receiving news of the arrest of [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell). He receives custody of her and her cousins after swearing a holy oath to return them for trial. As part of Lord Regent [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister)'s efforts to assuage the men of the [Reach](wiki:Reach), Lord Tarly is named [justiciar](wiki:Justiciar) on the [small council](wiki:Small_council) of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord_of_Horn_Hill.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
