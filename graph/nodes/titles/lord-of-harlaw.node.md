## Origins

Lord Harlaw was put to the sword by [westermen](wiki:Westerlands) during Lady [Johanna Lannister](wiki:Johanna_Lannister)'s invasion of the Iron Islands in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC).(wiki:Lord_of_Harlaw.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
After losing three sons in the cradle, Lord Theomore decided that the dampness and constant flooding of Harlaw Hall was to blame. He ordered the construction of the Ten Towers, a better sited castle as his seat.(wiki:Lord_of_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
Lord [Rodrik Harlaw](wiki:Rodrik_Harlaw) advised [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [Lord of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands), not to rebel against King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord_of_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After the death of Balon Greyjoy, [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North), Rodrik supports his niece, Balon's daughter [Asha](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy), and calls her supporters to [Harlaw](wiki:Harlaw).(wiki:Lord_of_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) He offers to name Asha as the heir to Ten Towers, with the [island of Harlaw](wiki:Harlaw) passing to Rodrik's cousin and current heir, Ser [Harras Harlaw](wiki:Harras_Harlaw), but she refuses.(wiki:Lord_of_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
At the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot) Rodrik is one of the first to call Asha's name. However, her uncle, [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), is chosen king instead.(wiki:Lord_of_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), Lord Rodrik along side with Lord [Dunstan Drumm](wiki:Dunstan_Drumm) and Lord [Gorold Goodbrother](wiki:Gorold_Goodbrother) discuss their fears that the gains from the battle are little compared to the enmity of the [Reach](wiki:Reach) they have earned.(wiki:Lord_of_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
During the feast in [House Hewett's hall](wiki:Oakenshield_Castle), King Euron tells the ironborn they will conquer [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). Rodrik is skeptical of Euron's planning as well as his claims of having sailed the [Smoking Sea](wiki:Smoking_Sea) and seen [Valyria](wiki:Valyria). With Euron giving [Greyshield](wiki:Greyshield) to Harras, an other of Rodrik's cousins, [Hotho Humpback](wiki:Hotho_Harlaw), feels that he is now his heir.(wiki:Lord_of_Harlaw.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)
