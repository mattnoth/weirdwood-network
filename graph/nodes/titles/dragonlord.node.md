## Origins

While [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) is believed to have been the westernmost outpost of Valyria,(wiki:Dragonlord.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_doom_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) some [maesters](wiki:Maesters) believe that the base of the [Hightower](wiki:Hightower) was created by dragonlords thousands of years ago.(wiki:Dragonlord.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) The dragonlords are not believed to have traveled as far east as the [Five Forts](wiki:Five_Forts), though at least one dragonlord was Empress of [Yi Ti](wiki:Yi_Ti) through her marriage to [God-Emperor](wiki:God-Emperor) [Chai Duq](wiki:Chai_Duq), who kept her dragon at his court.(wiki:Dragonlord.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
Almost all dragonlords died during the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria). Some in Lys and [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) survived the catastrophe but were killed in its aftermath. The dragonlord [Aurion](wiki:Aurion) led an army from [Qohor](wiki:Qohor) to the ruins of Valyria, but he was never seen again.(wiki:Dragonlord.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_doom_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Although not [dragonriders](wiki:Dragonrider), many [Volantenes](wiki:Volantis) consider themselves the successors of the dragonlords.(wiki:Dragonlord.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_volantis.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Volantis waged war against rival [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) during the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood).(wiki:Dragonlord.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_doom_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
The [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen) were one of the great dragonlord families, but far from the most powerful.(wiki:Dragonlord.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) They were the last dragonlord house to survive the Doom, as [Aenar the Exile](wiki:Aenar_Targaryen) had led his family to Dragonstone twelve years prior.(wiki:Dragonlord.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_doom_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) Riding three dragons, the Targaryens eventually conquered most of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Dragonlord.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4)
[Galendro](wiki:Galendro)'s [The Fires of the Freehold](wiki:The_Fires_of_the_Freehold) describes the feuds of the dragonlords,(wiki:Dragonlord.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_valyria.27s_children.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) and [Wroth of the Dragonlords](wiki:Wroth_of_the_Dragonlords) is the first play by [Phario Forel](wiki:Phario_Forel).(wiki:Dragonlord.cite_ref-18)
