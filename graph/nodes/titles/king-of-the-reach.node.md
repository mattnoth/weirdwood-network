## Origins

The mythical [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand) is said to have been the High King who lead the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) across the [Arm of Dorne](wiki:Arm_of_Dorne). His firstborn son was [Garth the Gardener](wiki:Garth_the_Gardener), the legendary first King of the Reach.(wiki:King_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) The kings from [House Gardener](wiki:House_Gardener) initially ruled the [Reach proper](wiki:Reach_proper), focused on [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) and the [Mander](wiki:Mander),(wiki:King_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) but over time they used their claim of descending from Garth the Gardener to claim supremacy over neighboring kings and [lords](wiki:Lord).(wiki:King_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_the_gardener_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
King [Garth the Great](wiki:Garth_III_Gardener) allied with [Old Oak](wiki:Old_Oak), [Red Lake](wiki:Red_Lake), and [Goldengrove](wiki:Goldengrove), expanding the kingdom's borders north. The kingdom of the Reach expanded southward with the addition of the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor) during the reign of King [Meryn III](wiki:Meryn_III_Gardener), and soon after [Garland the Bridegroom](wiki:Garland_II_Gardener) arranged marriages with [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) to join their realms. King [Garth the Goldenhand](wiki:Garth_VII_Gardener) conquered the Misty Islands from the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), fortifying them as the [Shield Islands](wiki:Shield_Islands).(wiki:King_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_the_gardener_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) The Kings of the Reach also acquired the [western marches](wiki:Western_marches) near [Horn Hill](wiki:Horn_Hill).(wiki:King_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
The kings and at least one queen ruled from the [Oakenseat](wiki:Oakenseat) in Highgarden until the throne's destruction during the reign of [Garth X](wiki:Garth_X_Gardener).(wiki:King_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The Gardeners often warred with the [Kings of the Rock](wiki:Kings_of_the_Rock) from [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister), the [Storm Kings](wiki:Storm_King) from [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon), [river kings](wiki:River_kings) from the [Trident](wiki:Trident), [ironborn kings](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands) from the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands), and [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen).
The Gardeners initially fortified their kingdom during the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), but they eventually welcomed the newcomers to their realm. [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell), a house of [Andal](wiki:Andal) origin, became the stewards of the Kings of the Reach.(wiki:King_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
The Gardeners ruled as kings until [Mern IX](wiki:Mern_IX_Gardener) rode out to meet [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) and his sister, [Visenya](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) and [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen), during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), and the Gardeners met their destruction upon the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire).(wiki:King_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) House Tyrell has since ruled from Highgarden as [Lords Paramount of the Reach](wiki:Lords_Paramount_of_the_Reach) for the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:King_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_house_tyrell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
The Kingdom of the Reach had its own local [coinage](wiki:Currency) before the Conquest, with gold coins that featured the hand-shaped sigil of House Gardener on one side and the face of a king on the other - for this they were called "hands". Gold hands were smaller and thinner than gold dragons, roughly half their weight and value.(wiki:King_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:King_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
