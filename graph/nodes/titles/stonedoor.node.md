## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), plans to re-garrison [Greyguard](wiki:Greyguard), but [Qhorin Halfhand](wiki:Qhorin_Halfhand) advises Stonedoor instead, due to Greyguard's poor condition.(wiki:Stonedoor.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) is with a group of wildlings who attempt to climb the Wall between Stonedoor and Greyguard.(wiki:Stonedoor.cite_ref-Rasos26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Stonedoor.cite_ref-Rasos30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

Now Lord Commander, Jon gives Stonedoor to [Soren Shieldbreaker](wiki:Soren_Shieldbreaker) to garrison and settle his own people. [Borroq](wiki:Borroq) is scheduled to join them.(wiki:Stonedoor.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
