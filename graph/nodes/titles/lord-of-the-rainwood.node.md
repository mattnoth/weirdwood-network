## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

When [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) names Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) as his new [Hand](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), the king also grants the titles Lord of the Rainwood and [Admiral of the Narrow Sea](wiki:Admiral_of_the_Narrow_Sea) to the former smuggler. This promotes Davos from a mere [landed knight](wiki:Landed_knight) to a high [lord](wiki:Lord) on par with the rest of the [stormlords](wiki:Stormlords).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Rainwood.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Lord_of_the_Rainwood.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Davos is proud his son [Devan](wiki:Devan_Seaworth) will one day be a lord.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Rainwood.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
