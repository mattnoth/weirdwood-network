## Origins

[Karlon Stark](wiki:Karlon_Stark), a younger son of a [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), was the first Lord of Karhold. He built the castle on the lands he received from his father. Originally called Karl's Hold, the name was later changed to [Karhold](wiki:Karhold) and Karlon's descendants became the [Karstarks](wiki:House_Karstark).(wiki:Lord_of_Karhold.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

Lord [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark) participated in [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) and fought for the rebels at the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Lord_of_Karhold.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) He once came to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) hoping to betroth his daughter, [Alys](wiki:Alys_Karstark), to the heir of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), but to no avail.(wiki:Lord_of_Karhold.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord [Rickard Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark) brings his forces to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) when [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls his banners. He is accompanied by his three sons, [Harrion](wiki:Harrion_Karstark), [Eddard](wiki:Eddard_Karstark), and [Torrhen](wiki:Torrhen_Karstark).(wiki:Lord_of_Karhold.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Rickard takes part in the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood), but Rickard's younger sons, Torrhen and Eddard, are killed by Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) during the fighting.(wiki:Lord_of_Karhold.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Rickard swears fealty to Robb when Lord [Greatjon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber) declares him [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Lord_of_Karhold.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Rickard accompanies Robb when the king campaigns in the [westerlands](wiki:Westerlands). During the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross), he rides down Ser [Stafford Lannister](wiki:Stafford_Lannister), the commander of a [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) host, and kills him with his lance.(wiki:Lord_of_Karhold.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Rickard and [Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover) then lead the [raiding along the westerlands coast](wiki:Raiding_along_the_westerlands_coast).(wiki:Lord_of_Karhold.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

Harrion is held captive at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) after the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:Lord_of_Karhold.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### A Storm of Swords

After learning of the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell), [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) releases Jaime Lannister from captivity at Riverrun.(wiki:Lord_of_Karhold.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Karhold.cite_ref-Rasos2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) When Robb's host returns from the westerlands, Lord Karstark is furious and considers the actions of Robb's mother to be treason.(wiki:Lord_of_Karhold.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Rickard, overcome with grief and anger, sends the Karstark horse from Riverrun, promising the hand of Alys to anyone who recovers Jaime for him.(wiki:Lord_of_Karhold.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)(wiki:Lord_of_Karhold.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat) hopes to wed Alys and claim [Karhold](wiki:Karhold).(wiki:Lord_of_Karhold.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1) After Rickard leads a small group of Karstark men to murder [Willem Lannister](wiki:Willem_Lannister) and [Tion Frey](wiki:Tion_Frey), two of Robb's captives, in their cells at Riverrun, Robb beheads Rickard for treason.(wiki:Lord_of_Karhold.cite_ref-Rasos20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4)

### A Feast for Crows

Rickard's eldest son, Lord Harrion, is a captive at [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool).(wiki:Lord_of_Karhold.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Rickard's uncle [Arnolf](wiki:Arnolf_Karstark), the castellan of Karhold, declares his support for [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), secretly hoping his action will lead to the execution of the captive Harrion by the Lannisters. Arnolf would then force Alys to wed his son, [Cregan Karstark](wiki:Cregan_Karstark), so that Karhold could be claimed.(wiki:Lord_of_Karhold.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) Alys flees to the safety of the [Wall](wiki:Wall),(wiki:Lord_of_Karhold.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) and she weds [Sigorn](wiki:Sigorn) of the [Thenns](wiki:Thenns) at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Lord_of_Karhold.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
