## Origins

The [Ryders](wiki:House_Ryder) were [First Men](wiki:First_Men) kings after the [Long Night](wiki:Long_Night). They were eventually defeated and reduced to vassals by the [Kings of Winter](wiki:Kings_of_Winter), [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Rills.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [House Ryswell](wiki:House_Ryswell) ruled the Rills after them.

When the [youngest son](wiki:Lord_Ryswell's_youngest_son) of a [Lord Ryswell](wiki:Lord_Ryswell_(Night's_Watch)) deserted the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), he rode to the [barrowlands](wiki:Barrowlands) with his fellow deserters, hoping his father would help them. Instead, Lord Ryswell sent them back to the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort) where the [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) had the [seventy-nine deserters](wiki:Seventy-nine_sentinels) sealed up alive in the ice. When he was old and dying, Lord Ryswell volunteered for the Night's Watch so he could rejoin his son.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Rills.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows

[House Ryswell](wiki:House_Ryswell) is one of the first houses to swear allegiance to [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), [Lord of the Dreadfort](wiki:Lord_of_the_Dreadfort), after he is named the new [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North) by the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). The Ryswells join his son, [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), for the [siege of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Rills.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Lord [Rodrik Ryswell](wiki:Rodrik_Ryswell), along with his sons and surviving daughter [Barbrey](wiki:Barbrey_Dustin), attends the [wedding](wiki:Wedding) of Ramsay to "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)" (actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)) in [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Rills.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
