## Origins

The [first Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Aenys_I)) was a [Dornishman](wiki:Dornishman) who rose against [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen). He was defeated through the [Vulture Hunt](wiki:Vulture_Hunt) at the end of the [Second Dornish War](wiki:Second_Dornish_War).(wiki:Vulture_King.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aenys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
A [second Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Jaehaerys_I)) troubled the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) during the reign of [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen). He was slain in the [Third Dornish War](wiki:Third_Dornish_War) by Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) of [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End).(wiki:Vulture_King.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
A [third Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Aegon_II)) was defeated by Lord [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon) during the reign of [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Vulture_King.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
A [later Vulture King](wiki:Vulture_King_(Daeron_II)) rose against [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen), but was eventually defeated by Lords [Caron](wiki:Pearse_Caron) and [Dondarrion](wiki:Lord_Dondarrion_(father_of_Manfred)).(wiki:Vulture_King.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[Gerris Drinkwater](wiki:Gerris_Drinkwater) once led six Dornishmen, including Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell), to find the old lair of a Vulture King.(wiki:Vulture_King.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
