## Origins

Originally a [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) ship, the galley was captured during [Lord Alyn's attack](wiki:Lord_Alyn's_attack) during the [Daughters' War](wiki:Daughters'_War). [Alyn Oakenfist](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) [renamed](wiki:Named_after) the ship Lady Baela to honor his wife, Lady [Baela Targaryen](wiki:Baela_Targaryen).(wiki:Lady_Baela.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Lady Baela was Alyn's flagship when he sailed from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea) to confront Lord [Dalton Greyjoy](wiki:Dalton_Greyjoy) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), the first of his six [Great Voyages](wiki:Great_Voyages). When he approached the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones), Alyn left his other ships behind and sailed Lady Baela into [Bloodstone](wiki:Bloodstone) to treat with [Racallio Ryndoon](wiki:Racallio_Ryndoon), the [King of the Narrow Sea](wiki:King_of_the_Narrow_Sea). After spending more than a fortnight in Racallio's company, the Oakenfist was allowed to sail his fleet south to the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea).(wiki:Lady_Baela.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Alyn visited [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) and [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) before arriving at [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport). During his return voyage, Alyn visited [Lys](wiki:Lys) and rescued Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Lady_Baela.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
