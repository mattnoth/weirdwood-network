## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Bryce Caron](wiki:Bryce_Caron), Lord of the Marches, is slain by Ser [Philip Foote](wiki:Philip_Foote) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater). Following the battle, the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) grants the lands and titles of the Carons to Philip, creating [House Foote of Nightsong](wiki:House_Foote_of_Nightsong).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Marches.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
