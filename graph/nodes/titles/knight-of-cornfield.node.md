## Origins

Like many others, Ser [Harys Swyft](wiki:Harys_Swyft) borrowed large sums of money from [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) during the rule of [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister), [Lord of Casterly Rock](wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock). When Tytos's heir, Ser [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), demanded repayment of the gold, Ser Harys, unable to repay it, surrendered his daughter, [Dorna](wiki:Dorna_Swyft), as a hostage into the custody of Tywin's younger brother, Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), until the debt was settled.(wiki:Knight_of_Cornfield.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser [Harys Swyft](wiki:Harys_Swyft) is part of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s force in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands), and he participates in the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:Knight_of_Cornfield.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Harys alienates the cooks of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), who spit in his food.(wiki:Knight_of_Cornfield.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Harys is trapped beneath his horse after it falls on him. He is protected by one of his men-at-arms, [Willit](wiki:Willit).(wiki:Knight_of_Cornfield.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Harys is among the nobles who accompany [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) to meet the [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) party when they arrive at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Knight_of_Cornfield.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Harys is named [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) by Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister),(wiki:Knight_of_Cornfield.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) but later demoted to [lord treasurer](wiki:Lord_treasurer).(wiki:Knight_of_Cornfield.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

When Cersei is arrested by the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), Harys and Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) take control of King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) and request that Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) return to King's Landing as regent.(wiki:Knight_of_Cornfield.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Harys continues to serve as lord treasurer,(wiki:Knight_of_Cornfield.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) although his vaults are empty.(wiki:Knight_of_Cornfield.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) Kevan suggests to Harys that he travel to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) to treat with the [Iron Bank](wiki:Iron_Bank_of_Braavos).(wiki:Knight_of_Cornfield.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)
