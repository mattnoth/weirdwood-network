## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord [Gorold Goodbrother](wiki:Gorold_Goodbrother) sends his son, [Gormond](wiki:Gormond_Goodbrother), to inform [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) of the death of his brother, King [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy). Aeron travels to Hammerhorn and discusses the news with Gorold and his maester. Gorold informs Aeron of [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy)'s arrival and the death of Lord [Sawane Botley](wiki:Sawane_Botley).(wiki:Lord_of_Hammerhorn.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Lord Gorold attends the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot), where he is listed as one of [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy)'s supporters.(wiki:Lord_of_Hammerhorn.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) After Euron's speech, Gorold shouts his support for Euron.(wiki:Lord_of_Hammerhorn.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) After the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), he becomes afraid of the [Reach](wiki:Reach) enmity the [Ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) have earned.(wiki:Lord_of_Hammerhorn.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
