## Origins

Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) and Ser [Bennis of the Brown Shield](wiki:Bennis_of_the_Brown_Shield) were [hedge knights](wiki:Hedge_knight) who swore their service to Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey) during the [landed knight](wiki:Landed_knight)'s quarrel with Lady [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber).(wiki:Sworn_shield.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
