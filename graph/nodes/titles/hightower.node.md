## Appearances & Description

According to a semi-canon source, the Hightower is a massive stepped tower with a beacon on top to guide ships into port.(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-SSM_3-0) The color of the beacon's fire is changeable, as green flame can be used to call [House Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower)'s banners.(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) The labyrinthine square fortress of unadorned [black stone](wiki:Black_stone) at the castle's foundation contains gloomy halls, vaults, and chambers.(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The Hightower contains a high hall.(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The top of the tower contains the apartments of the [Lord of the Hightower](wiki:Lord_of_the_Hightower).(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Deep vaults are located underground.(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
The Hightower is the tallest structure in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms),(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-SSM_3-1) even higher than the seven-hundred-foot [Wall](wiki:Wall),(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rawoiafhightower.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and it is the tallest tower in the [known world](wiki:Known_world).(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) The people of Oldtown can tell the time of day by the tower's shadow, and some claim that the Wall can be seen from its top.(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock), however, is three times taller than the Hightower or the Wall.(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands:_casterly_rock.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
It is speculated by fans that the Hightower is one of the nine [Wonders Made by Man](wiki:Wonders_Made_by_Man) written about by [Lomas Longstrider](wiki:Lomas_Longstrider).(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-12)

## Origins

### Black Stone Fortress[ ]

The foundation of the Hightower is a fortress of [black stone](wiki:Black_stone) of uncertain origin on [Battle Isle](wiki:Battle_Isle). The stone is reminiscent of [Valyrian roads](wiki:Valyrian_roads) and the [Black Walls](wiki:Black_Walls) of [Volantis](wiki:Volantis).(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) A possible [Valyrian](wiki:Valyria) origin is supported by the claim of Maester [Jellicoe](wiki:Jellicoe) that Oldtown began as a trading post for ships of Valyria, [Old Ghis](wiki:Old_Ghis), and the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles), predating the arrival of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). Septon [Barth](wiki:Barth) claimed Valyrians came to Westeros because their priests prophesied that the Doom of Man would come out of the land beyond the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea).(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
In contrast to the Valyrian theory, Archmaester [Quillion](wiki:Quillion) suggests the fortress was made by the [mazemakers](wiki:Mazemakers). Maester [Theron](wiki:Theron_Pyke) suggests it was created by [Deep Ones](wiki:Deep_Ones), citing its similarities to the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair). Some [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) believe the Hightower simply appeared.(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3)

### House Hightower[ ]

[House Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower) of the Hightower is an ancient and noble family of impeccable lineage,(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) as they were [First Men](wiki:First_Men) kings during the [Dawn of Days](wiki:Dawn_of_Days).(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Dwelling in the chambers of the fortress, the family built a wooden beacon tower rising some fifty feet above to light the way for trading vessels in foggy [Whispering Sound](wiki:Whispering_Sound). This first "high tower" was followed by taller timber towers.(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-4)
King [Uthor of the High Tower](wiki:Uthor_of_the_High_Tower) paid for the construction of a stone tower. Some say this fifth tower, which rose two hundred feet above the harbor and made the castle a seat worthy of a [great house](wiki:Great_house), was designed by [Bran the Builder](wiki:Bran_the_Builder), while others say it was by his son, [another Brandon](wiki:Brandon_Stark_(son_of_Bran_the_Builder)).(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-5)
[Gyles I Gardener](wiki:Gyles_I_Gardener), [King of the Reach](wiki:King_of_the_Reach), sacked [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) but was unable to capture the Hightower.(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-6) The Hightower and Oldtown were incorporated into the Kingdom of the Reach during the rule of the Sea Lion, [Lymond Hightower](wiki:Lymond_Hightower).(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_the_gardener_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Lord [Jason Hightower](wiki:Jason_Hightower) rebuilt the tower a hundred feet higher.(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-7)
[Roland I Arryn](wiki:Roland_I_Arryn), [King of Mountain and Vale](wiki:King_of_Mountain_and_Vale), was inspired to build the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) after visiting the Hightower and [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rfabthe_vale:_the_eyrie.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### Modern Era[ ]

During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Lord [Manfred Hightower](wiki:Manfred_Hightower_(Aegon's_Conquest)) heeded the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Aegon's_Conquest)), who prophesied that the Hightower would burn with the rest of [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) if the city resisted the [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen). When [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) arrived at Oldtown on [dragonback](wiki:Dragon), Lord Hightower opened his gates. Aegon spared the city, and the High Septon anointed him and acknowledged Aegon's right to rule the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
[Ceryse Hightower](wiki:Ceryse_Hightower) returned to the Hightower in [39 AC](wiki:39_AC) when her husband, Prince [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), fled to [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) in exile with his second wife, [Alys Harroway](wiki:Alys_Harroway). After becoming king, Maegor, reconciled with Ceryse in [43 AC](wiki:43_AC) and a great feast was held at the Hightower.(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
When King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) visited Oldtown in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), the queen landed [Silverwing](wiki:Silverwing) atop the Hightower. The royal couple resided in the apartments of Lord [Donnel Hightower](wiki:Donnel_Hightower) at the top of the tower during their stay.(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
Maester [Alfador](wiki:Alfador) served at the Hightower during the reign of King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower)'s young sons remained at the Hightower while Ormund led an army for the [greens](wiki:Greens).(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Ser [Tyland Lannister](wiki:Tyland_Lannister), the [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin) for King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), shipped a quarter of the Crown's treasury to safekeeping in the Hightower.(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1) After the war, [Samantha Tarly](wiki:Samantha_Tarly) became the lover of Lord [Lyonel Hightower](wiki:Lyonel_Hightower) and the mistress of the Hightower.(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) The fleet of Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) used the Hightower's beacon to guide them during their journey toward the [Sunset Sea](wiki:Sunset_Sea). Alyn Oakenfist stayed at the castle with Lyonel and Sam during his visit to Oldtown.(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
Lord [Leyton Hightower](wiki:Leyton_Hightower), the Old Man of Oldtown, has not descended from the Hightower in more than a decade.(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Alekyne Florent](wiki:Alekyne_Florent), the attainted Lord of [Brightwater](wiki:Brightwater_Keep), flees to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) to seek refuge at the Hightower.(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
While sailing on the [Cinnamon Wind](wiki:Cinnamon_Wind) to Oldtown, [Gilly](wiki:Gilly) is impressed with the size of [Three Towers](wiki:Three_Towers), but [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) tells her to wait until she sees the Hightower. When they reach Oldtown, the morning fog is so thick that the Hightower's beacon is the only part of the city visible.(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
Lord [Leyton Hightower](wiki:Leyton_Hightower) and his daughter, the Mad Maid [Malora](wiki:Malora_Hightower), are said to have locked themselves atop the Hightower, consulting books of spells in response to the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) attacks on the [Reach](wiki:Reach).(wiki:Hightower.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-1)
