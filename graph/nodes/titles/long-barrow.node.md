## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) climbs the [Wall](wiki:Wall) near Long Barrow when travels incognito to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) for the visit of King [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_Baratheon).(wiki:Long_Barrow.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) plans to re-garrison the castle using men from [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea).(wiki:Long_Barrow.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Prior to the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), [Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt) is seen near Long Barrow.(wiki:Long_Barrow.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [Cotter Pyke](wiki:Cotter_Pyke) later captures [Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt) at the abandoned castle.(wiki:Long_Barrow.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Commander [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) re-garrisons Long Barrow with [spearwives](wiki:Spearwife) under the command of [Iron Emmett](wiki:Emmett) with [Dolorous Edd](wiki:Eddison_Tollett) as his chief steward.(wiki:Long_Barrow.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The men of the Night's Watch now call it Whore's Barrow.(wiki:Long_Barrow.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
