## Origins

During [the Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest) of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) by [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen), King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) proclaimed his half-brother, [Orys Baratheon](wiki:Orys_Baratheon), to be "my shield, my stalwart, my strong right hand."(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Because of Aegon's description, Orys is regarded to have been the first Hand of the King.(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) Orys resigned after losing a hand in the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War).(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

Lord [Osmund Strong](wiki:Osmund_Strong) oversaw construction of the walls of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing),(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1) and Lord [Alyn Stokeworth](wiki:Alyn_Stokeworth) and Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) oversaw construction of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) executed Lord [Lucas Harroway](wiki:Lucas_Harroway) and his kin after his daughter, Queen [Alys Harroway](wiki:Alys_Harroway), was accused of infidelity.(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1)

## Culture

Duties of the Hand include commanding the king's armies,(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) drafting laws,(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) dispensing justice,(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) and generally managing the day-to-day running of the kingdom. The Hand may act on the king's behalf when he is indisposed, and sit the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) while doing so. The Hand also sits on the [small council](wiki:Small_council) and will run the council if the king is not present.(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

The office typically makes the Hand the second-most powerful man in the kingdom next to the king,(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) though some Hands have had the reputation of ruling their kings.(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

It is considered by some to be a difficult and unglamorous job of great responsibility. A popular saying goes, "What the king dreams, the Hand builds," but the lowborn say, "The king eats, and the Hand takes the shit."(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) Many able men have failed in the office throughout history.(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

The Hand resides in the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand) within the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) His badge of office always includes a hand, though each Hand can design his own. [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) and [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers) used brooches of [gold](wiki:Gold)(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) and [iron](wiki:Iron),(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) respectively, and a [silver](wiki:Silver) clasp has also been used.(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Ragot33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Several Hands have used chains,(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_go9ld.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) such as a necklace of linked golden hands.(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Servants of the Hand might wear the emblem of a hand.(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Upon the unexpected death of his Hand, Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) asks his childhood friend, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), to serve as Jon's replacement.(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)

After Robert dies, Eddard is imprisoned for treason against his son, King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Ragot52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0) Joffrey's grandfather, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), is named as the new Hand,(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0) and Joffrey eventually orders Eddard's execution.(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0) Because Tywin is campaigning in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) with the outbreak of war in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), he sends his younger son, [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), to serve as acting Hand in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0)

### A Clash of King

Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) serves as Hand to [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon),(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0) a rival claimant to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), but Renly is killed at [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)).(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Racok33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0)

Tyrion's presence at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) upsets his sister, [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), who serves as [Queen Regent](wiki:Queen_Regent) for her son, King Joffrey.(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0)(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0) Many Kingslanders hate Tyrion, considering the [dwarf](wiki:Dwarf) to be an evil councillor.(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0)(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0)

Lord Tywin assumes his position as King's Hand for Joffrey after defeating [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Tywin serves as Hand to Joffrey and, after [Joffrey's death](wiki:Purple_Wedding), to King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon),(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-0) until he is murdered in the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand) by Tyrion. Tyrion also murders his former [mistress](wiki:Mistress), [Shae](wiki:Shae), strangling her with the golden chain of linked hands that she was wearing after sleeping with Tywin.(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Rasos77.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-0)

Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent) serves as the Hand to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), a rival claimant to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_48-0) After Alester has been arrested for [treason](wiki:Treason), [Axell Florent](wiki:Axell_Florent) tries to make Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) advise Stannis to name himself as Hand of the King. Instead of Axell, Stannis chooses Davos to be his new Hand.(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_49-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Cersei, Queen Regent for her son Tommen, orders the burning of the Tower of the Hand by [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire).(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_50-0) She fails to convince her brother, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), to become Hand,(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Raffc3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_51-0) and she refuses to meet the terms her uncle, Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), has for the office.(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_52-0) Cersei instead appoints first Ser [Harys Swyft](wiki:Harys_Swyft)(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_53-0) and later Lord [Orton Merryweather](wiki:Orton_Merryweather)(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Raffc39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_54-0) as Tommen's Hand. After Cersei is arrested by the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), Orton resigns from the [small council](wiki:Small_council) and flees King's Landing.(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_55-0)

Prior to the [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk), [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) offers to support her uncle [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) as [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North) if he names her his Hand. Victarion rejects her offer due to the absence of the office in [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) culture, Asha's gender, and their contrasting views on the ironborn war for the [north](wiki:North).(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_56-0) Asha and Victarion each stand in the kingsmoot, both losing to [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy).(wiki:Hand_of_the_King.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_57-0)
