## Origins

At the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), the [Two Kings](wiki:Two_Kings) led five thousand [knights](wiki:Knight) and ten times as many [freeriders](wiki:Freerider) and men-at-arms.(wiki:Man-at-arms.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
Prince [Maegor Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) was able to defeat seasoned men-at-arms in the yard when he was only twelve.(wiki:Man-at-arms.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) When King Maegor the Cruel asked for champions to defend him in a [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven), the simple man-at-arms [Dick Bean](wiki:Dick_Bean) was the first to volunteer.(wiki:Man-at-arms.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2) During the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising), knights and men-at-arms disappeared in the countryside after being sent to apprehend [Dennis the Lame](wiki:Dennis_the_Lame) and [Ragged Silas](wiki:Ragged_Silas).(wiki:Man-at-arms.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-3)
After the ascension of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), Queen [Elinor Costayne](wiki:Elinor_Costayne) departed [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) with only a handmaid and a loyal man-at-arms as her companions.(wiki:Man-at-arms.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) A dozen knights and forty men-at-arms protected Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) and Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) when they sailed to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)) to confront Jaehaerys after he wed his sister, [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen).(wiki:Man-at-arms.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) When Rogar and Alyssa returned to [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), they were escorted through the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood) by a hundred men-at-arms.(wiki:Man-at-arms.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1) Lord [Maegor Towers](wiki:Maegor_Towers) resided at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) with only a cook and three older men-at-arms.(wiki:Man-at-arms.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) Because men-at-arms often guarded river fords and charged travelers for the right to cross, King Jaehaerys desired to build more durable roads throughout the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Man-at-arms.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
Thousands of knights, men-at-arms and peasants from the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) rose in support of [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Man-at-arms.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) marched against [Rosby](wiki:Rosby) and [Stokeworth](wiki:Stokeworth) with a hundred knights and five hundred men-at-arms from the household of [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen), and three thousand men-at-arms were in the army of [greens](wiki:Greens) led by [Ormund Hightower](wiki:Ormund_Hightower), [Lord of the Hightower](wiki:Lord_of_the_Hightower).(wiki:Man-at-arms.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) [Ulf the White](wiki:Ulf_the_White) was a man-at-arms who mounted [Silverwing](wiki:Silverwing) during the [Sowing of the Seeds](wiki:Sowing_of_the_Seeds).(wiki:Man-at-arms.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-1) [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) killed six knights and twoscore men-at-arms in the service of Lady [Sabitha Frey](wiki:Sabitha_Frey) when Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) attacked Harrenhal.(wiki:Man-at-arms.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) During the [riots of King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing_(Dance_of_the_Dragons)), only sixteen of Ser [Lorent Marbrand](wiki:Lorent_Marbrand)'s hundred knights and men-at-arms survived an expedition into [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom). Twenty men-at-arms were with the [Seven Who Rode](wiki:Seven_Who_Rode) to rescue Prince [Joffrey Velaryon](wiki:Joffrey_Velaryon) from the mob.(wiki:Man-at-arms.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) An unknown man-at-arms is remember as one of the possible killers of Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)) during [Second Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Tumbleton).(wiki:Man-at-arms.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-1) Lady [Jeyne Arryn](wiki:Jeyne_Arryn) assembled fifteen hundred knights and eight thousand men-at-arms in support of the [blacks](wiki:Blacks).(wiki:Man-at-arms.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)
