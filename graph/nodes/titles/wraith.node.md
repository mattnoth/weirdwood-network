### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Dale sails the Wraith beyond [Cape Wrath](wiki:Cape_Wrath) and the [Broken Arm](wiki:Broken_Arm), along the coast of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) to the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor) to distribute [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s claim against the legitimacy of [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)'s children with King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Wraith.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Wraith is among Stannis's fleet that sails into [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay) during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Wraith.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) The ship does not survive the battle.(wiki:Wraith.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
