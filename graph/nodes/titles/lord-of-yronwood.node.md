## Origins

King [Olyvar Yronwood](wiki:Olyvar_Yronwood) repeatedly led incursions into the [Stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) during the reign of the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Erich VII Durrandon](wiki:Erich_VII_Durrandon).(wiki:Lord_of_Yronwood.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_andals_in_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
King [Yorick V Yronwood](wiki:Yorick_V_Yronwood) was the richest and most powerful dornish king when Princess [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria) of [Ny Sar](wiki:Ny_Sar) and [her people](wiki:Rhoynar) arrived in Dorne, and subsequently allied with Lord [Mors Martell](wiki:Mors_Martell) of the [Sandship](wiki:Sandship) and started [conquering Dorne](wiki:Nymeria's_War). In the ninth year of the war, Yorick personally killed Mors in the [Third Battle of the Boneway](wiki:Third_Battle_of_the_Boneway). However, he was forced to submit to Nymeria two years later, and he was sent to the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Lord_of_Yronwood.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Despite not being a royal house since Yorick submitted to the [Martells](wiki:House_Martell) a millennia ago, the Lords of Yronwood still use the title of the [Bloodroyal](wiki:Bloodroyal).(wiki:Lord_of_Yronwood.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Lord [Edgar Yronwood](wiki:Edgar_Yronwood) fought a duel to first blood against Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell), after he was found [abed](wiki:Bedding) with Edgar's [paramour](wiki:Paramour). Both men took cuts. However, while Oberyn soon recovered, Lord Edgar's wounds festered and he died. Afterward it was rumored that Oberyn had [poisoned](wiki:Poison) his sword, gaining his nickname of "the Red Viper".(wiki:Lord_of_Yronwood.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Yronwood.cite_ref-Winds_10-0)
[Ormond Yronwood](wiki:Ormond_Yronwood), Edgar's son, succeeded him after his death. Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), Oberyn's brother, send his son, Prince [Trystane Martell](wiki:Trystane_Martell), as a [ward](wiki:Ward) to Yronwood as he thought that was the only solution Lord Ormond would accept to settle the blood debt between the two houses.(wiki:Lord_of_Yronwood.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Eventually, Ormond died and was succeeded by his son, [Anders Yronwood](wiki:Anders_Yronwood). Trystane remained a ward under Lord Anders and served him first as a [page](wiki:Page) then as a [squire](wiki:Squire).(wiki:Lord_of_Yronwood.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Lord Anders [knighted](wiki:Knight) Trystane when he was eighteen years old.(wiki:Lord_of_Yronwood.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)(wiki:Lord_of_Yronwood.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_quentyn_martell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
