## Origins

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), [Lord Footly](wiki:Lord_Footly) declared for the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) and hosted them in Tumbleton until the town was betrayed in the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton).(wiki:Lord_of_Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Ser [Jon Roxton](wiki:Jon_Roxton) claimed Lord Footly's wife, Lady [Sharis](wiki:Sharis_Footly), as a "prize of war." When her lord husband protested, Bold Jon cut him nigh in two with [Orphan-Maker](wiki:Orphan-Maker).(wiki:Lord_of_Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Lord Footly was succeeded by his posthumous son, born six months after the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton). Though claimed by Lady Sharis as her late lord husband's trueborn heir, it was far more likely that he had been fathered by Bold Jon.(wiki:Lord_of_Tumbleton.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
