## Origins

Ser [Quentyn Ball](wiki:Quentyn_Ball), a master-at-arms at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), supported [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) during the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion).(wiki:Master-at-arms.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
Ser [Willem Darry](wiki:Willem_Darry) helped train Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen),(wiki:Master-at-arms.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) and he later helped Prince [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen) and Princess [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) escape for [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) before the [assault on Dragonstone](wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone).(wiki:Master-at-arms.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) has a contentious relationship with the master-at-arms of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), Ser [Alliser Thorne](wiki:Alliser_Thorne).(wiki:Master-at-arms.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)(wiki:Master-at-arms.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Dagmer Cleftjaw](wiki:Dagmer), the master-at-arms of [Pyke](wiki:Pyke),(wiki:Master-at-arms.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) joins [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) in the [harrying of the Stony Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore).(wiki:Master-at-arms.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

With Ser [Aron Santagar](wiki:Aron_Santagar) dead, Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) refuses to name Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) as the new master-at-arms of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Master-at-arms.cite_ref-Raffc24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Jon, now [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch), names the wildling [Leathers](wiki:Leathers) as the new master-at-arms for Castle Black.(wiki:Master-at-arms.cite_ref-Radwd58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
