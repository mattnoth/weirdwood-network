## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

The current Seneschal is Archmaester [Theobald](wiki:Theobald), chosen after the senile Archmaester [Walgrave](wiki:Walgrave) drew the first lot. His predecessor was Archmaester [Norren](wiki:Norren).(wiki:Seneschal_of_the_Citadel.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
[Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) travels to the [Seneschal's Court](wiki:Seneschal's_Court) to meet the Seneschal, but he is forced to wait by [Lorcas](wiki:Lorcas). Alleras arrives and brings Sam instead to the [Ravenry](wiki:Ravenry).(wiki:Seneschal_of_the_Citadel.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
