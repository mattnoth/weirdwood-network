## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

The comatose [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) has a [dream](wiki:Dream) of his mother, [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), and a seasick Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) sailing through the [Bite](wiki:Bite).(wiki:Storm_Dancer.cite_ref-Ragot17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Catelyn and Rodrik take the Storm Dancer from [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), but Rodrik becomes seasick during the journey. During a storm near [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), the knight clings to a rope when nearly swept overboard and has to be rescued by three crewmembers.(wiki:Storm_Dancer.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
When the galley arrives in the capital, Catelyn rewards each of the oarmen with a [silver stag](wiki:Currency). [Moreo Tumitis](wiki:Moreo_Tumitis) offers to hold onto his crew's silver until they return to [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh), but Catelyn insists on personally paying each oarman. She also gives a copper to two crewmembers who bring the travelers' chests to an [old inn on Eel Alley](wiki:Old_inn_on_Eel_Alley).(wiki:Storm_Dancer.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Storm Dancer is in White Harbor when Lord [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) arrives on [Merry Midwife](wiki:Merry_Midwife). Davos sees one of the ship's oarsmen drinking in the [Lazy Eel](wiki:Lazy_Eel).(wiki:Storm_Dancer.cite_ref-Radwd15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
