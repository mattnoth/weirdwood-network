## Origins

When [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) was driven from the [Reach](wiki:Reach) by the [Gardener Kings](wiki:House_Gardener),(wiki:Lord_of_White_Harbor.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Lord_of_White_Harbor.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Lord_of_White_Harbor.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_white_harbor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) they fled north. The [Starks](wiki:House_Stark) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) welcomed them as their own bannermen and awarded them the [Wolf's Den](wiki:Wolf's_Den).(wiki:Lord_of_White_Harbor.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) With their southron wealth, the House Manderly constructed the city of [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) and further developed it over the centuries that followed.(wiki:Lord_of_White_Harbor.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) sends his sons [Wylis](wiki:Wylis_Manderly) and [Wendel](wiki:Wendel_Manderly) to fight in the army of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) at the outbreak of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Lord_of_White_Harbor.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord Wyman Manderly travels to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) where he proposes to mint new [coinage](wiki:Currency) for Robb Stark, the new [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), and a new war fleet for the [north](wiki:North).(wiki:Lord_of_White_Harbor.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Wyman Manderly orders [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) to be imprisoned after arriving at White Harbor to treat in favor of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), a claimant to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Lord_of_White_Harbor.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Wyman later reveals his intentions to betray [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) and [king upon the Iron Throne](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) to Davos.(wiki:Lord_of_White_Harbor.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) When Wyman travels from White Harbor to [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton), his three guests from [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey) disappear while en route, after they had decided to ride ahead.(wiki:Lord_of_White_Harbor.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Although Wyman acts distraught, some suspect him of foul play.(wiki:Lord_of_White_Harbor.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
Wyman accompanies the [Boltons](wiki:House_Bolton) from Barrowton to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). Tension runs high within the castle walls following numerous murders. Wyman is attacked by [Hosteen Frey](wiki:Hosteen_Frey) after Hosteen's young nephew [Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)) is found dead.(wiki:Lord_of_White_Harbor.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
