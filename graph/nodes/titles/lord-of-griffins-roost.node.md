## Edges

## Origins

During the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), the Lord of Griffin's Roost was murdered by [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) assassins while hunting.(wiki:Lord_of_Griffin's_Roost.cite_ref-Rfabthe_reign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
When [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) usurped the throne from his nephew Prince [Aegon the Uncrowned](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)), the fourth son of the Lord of Griffin's Roost rallied to the prince's banners at the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye) in [43 AC](wiki:43_AC).(wiki:Lord_of_Griffin's_Roost.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Lord [Kyle Connington](wiki:Kyle_Connington) later attended the [wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) of Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) and Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC), where he met with King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord_of_Griffin's_Roost.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Lord [Roy Connington](wiki:Roy_Connington) was a favorite of Princess [Saera Targaryen](wiki:Saera_Targaryen). After a scandal, Connington was commanded to wed the pregnant [Alys Turnberry](wiki:Alys_Turnberry). When Connington refused, Jaehaerys had the young lord exiled for ten years, though the Lord of Griffin's Roost died in exile shortly before he was to return to the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Lord_of_Griffin's_Roost.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Lord [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) served King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King). However, the Mad King stripped Lord Connington of his titles and lands after the [Battle of the Bells](wiki:Battle_of_the_Bells). After Aerys's death during the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing), the new king, [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), allowed Jon's cousin, Ser [Ronald Connington](wiki:Ronald_Connington), to retain Griffin's Roost. However, Ronald was stripped of his title and nine-tenths of his land, and the Conningtons were reduced to [landed knights](wiki:Landed_knight).(wiki:Lord_of_Griffin's_Roost.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Griffin's_Roost.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Ser [Ronnet Connington](wiki:Ronnet_Connington), Ronald's son, is the current [Knight of Griffin's Roost](wiki:Knight_of_Griffin's_Roost).(wiki:Lord_of_Griffin's_Roost.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

During the [landing of the Golden Company](wiki:Landing_of_the_Golden_Company) in the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands), the exiled [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington) leads the [taking of Griffin's Roost](wiki:Taking_of_Griffin's_Roost). Ser [Ronnet Connington](wiki:Ronnet_Connington), the [Knight of Griffin's Roost](wiki:Knight_of_Griffin's_Roost), is away in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) and his castle quickly falls to the invading sellswords. After capturing his castle, Jon begins to style himself as the Lord of Griffin's Roost again.(wiki:Lord_of_Griffin's_Roost.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)
