## Origins

Following the death of King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), Prince [Jaehaerys Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) ordered the arrests of the Lord Confessor, the [King's Justice](wiki:King's_Justice), the [Chief Gaoler](wiki:Chief_Gaoler), the [Commander of the City Watch](wiki:Commander_of_the_City_Watch), and the four remaining [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) who had remained loyal to the late king.(wiki:Lord_Confessor.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0).
[Larys Strong](wiki:Larys_Strong), [Lord of Harrenhal](wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal), served as Lord Confessor as well as [master of whisperers](wiki:Master_of_whisperers) to Kings [Viserys I](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) and [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) after serving as a [confessor](wiki:Confessor).(wiki:Lord_Confessor.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
After becoming [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to young [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake), [Lord of Starpike](wiki:Lord_of_Starpike), named his supporter, [George Graceford](wiki:George_Graceford), [Lord of Holyhall](wiki:Lord_of_Holyhall), as Lord Confessor.(wiki:Lord_Confessor.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) When [Torrhen Manderly](wiki:Torrhen_Manderly), [Lord of White Harbor](wiki:Lord_of_White_Harbor), became Hand to Aegon III, he named the young Maester [Rowley](wiki:Rowley) as Lord Confessor at Grand Maester [Munkun](wiki:Munkun)'s behest.(wiki:Lord_Confessor.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
There has not been a Lord Confessor since the reign of King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord_Confessor.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
