## Origins

In the century following the fall of [House Justman](wiki:House_Justman), the [Mallisters](wiki:House_Mallister) were one of the many river lords who were petty [river kings](wiki:River_king), with [House Teague](wiki:House_Teague) eventually controlling the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands).(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Lord [Petyr Mallister](wiki:Petyr_Mallister) rose against the [Storm Kings](wiki:Storm_King) of [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon), having a brief and inglorious reign as [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident) before his defeat.(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Lord Mallister joined [Edmyn Tully](wiki:Edmyn_Tully), [Lord of Riverrun](wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun), and other riverlords who rebelled against [Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare), [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers).(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

The Lord Mallister ruling in [49 AC](wiki:49_AC) was present at the [Golden Wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding).(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Rfabthe_years_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Lord [Lymond Mallister](wiki:Lymond_Mallister) was unhorsed by Ser [Criston Cole](wiki:Criston_Cole) at [the tourney](wiki:Tourney_for_King_Viserys_I's_accession) celebrating King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen)'s accession to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) in [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool).(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Rtrp.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the Mallisters declared for the [blacks](wiki:Blacks). Lord [Jorah Mallister](wiki:Jorah_Mallister) was killed by Lord [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon) in the [Battle of the Kingsroad](wiki:Battle_of_the_Kingsroad).(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_short.2C_sad_reign_of_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_baratheon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

### Recent History

Lord [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister) fought for the rebels in [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) and killed three of Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen)'s [bannermen](wiki:Bannermen) at the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) During [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion) Lord Jason slew the heir of [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), [Rodrik Greyjoy](wiki:Rodrik_Greyjoy), beneath the walls of [Seagard](wiki:Storming_of_Seagard).(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister) participates in the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney) at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-1) Jason joins [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s host when the [northmen](wiki:North) descend from the [Twins](wiki:Twins) to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun),(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) and he captures Lord [Gawen Westerling](wiki:Gawen_Westerling)(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) in the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1) Jason joins the other river lords in declaring Robb to be [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Jason is one of Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully)'s commanders at the [Battle of the Fords](wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords), during which he prevent [westermen](wiki:Westermen) from crossing the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork).(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### A Storm of Swords

After the death of [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North), Robb commands Jason to send two Mallister longships into the [Neck](wiki:Neck), one carrying [Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover) and the other [Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont), to find [Greywater Watch](wiki:Greywater_Watch) and inform Lord [Howland Reed](wiki:Howland_Reed) of Robb's plan to retake [Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin).(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Robb intends for his mother, [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), to spend the rest of the war in safety with Lord Mallister at Seagard after Lord Edmure weds [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey) at the Twins.(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) However, Robb and Catelyn are betrayed by Houses [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) and [Frey](wiki:House_Frey) and murdered during the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Lord Jason [surrenders his castle](wiki:Siege_of_Seagard) to [Black Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Ryman)) after he threatens to hang Jason's son, [Patrek](wiki:Patrek_Mallister), who had been taken captive at the Red Wedding.(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) Black Walder keeps Jason as a prisoner inside his own castle, alongside his son.(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Jason and Patrek remain imprisoned at Seagard.(wiki:Lord_of_Seagard.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
