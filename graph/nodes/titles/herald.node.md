## Identity

Herald is a title from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges


## Culture

The [Prince of Pentos](wiki:Prince_of_Pentos) travels around the city in an [ivory](wiki:Ivory) and [gold](wiki:Gold) [palanquin](wiki:Palanquin), with three heralds going before him, carrying the golden scales of trade, the [iron](wiki:Iron) [sword](wiki:Sword) of war, and the [silver](wiki:Silver) scourge of [justice](wiki:Justice).(wiki:Herald.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Origins

In [50 AC](wiki:50_AC), when King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) reached the [age of majority](wiki:Age_of_majority) and claimed his rule for himself, he replaced the royal herald, among other lesser offices.(wiki:Herald.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
In [131 AC](wiki:131_AC), the royal herald of [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) was among those arrested by Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark) for the king's murder. He chose to join the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) to avoid execution.(wiki:Herald.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
In [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), the [Maiden's Day Ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball) was held to find a new queen consort for King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen). More than one thousand [maidens](wiki:Maiden) from throughout [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), and even [Essos](wiki:Essos), crowded into the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) on the day of the ball. The royal herald announced the name and lineage of each [noblewoman](wiki:Nobility) who presented themselves to the young king, but lost his voice in the process and had to be replaced. Aegon chose Lady [Daenaera Velaryon](wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon), who was announced by the second herald, who also was somewhat hoarse by that point.(wiki:Herald.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
In [209 AC](wiki:209_AC), at the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow), heralds boomed out the name of each [knight](wiki:Knight) that challenged the champions of the fair maid.(wiki:Herald.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
In [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), during the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls), the [Butterwell](wiki:House_Butterwell) herald was a portly man. He openly mocked Ser [Glendon Flowers](wiki:Glendon_Flowers) as the "Knight of the [Pussywillows](wiki:Pussywillows)" and "Ser Glendon the [Bastard](wiki:Bastard)", to the amusement of the crowd.(wiki:Herald.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
In [246 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) or [247 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), at the [tourney](wiki:List_of_tourneys_in_Westeros) at [Blackhaven](wiki:Blackhaven), the ten-year-old [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) entered the lists as a [mystery knight](wiki:Mystery_knight). The herald laughed when he saw young Barristan in his ill-fitting [armor](wiki:Armor).(wiki:Herald.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

A herald announces the arrival of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and his mother, Dowager Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), in Joffrey's first court session as king. The herald announces [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) before she approaches the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) to ask for mercy for her [father](wiki:Eddard_Stark).(wiki:Herald.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

A herald presents the participants of the [tourney on King Joffrey's name day](wiki:Tourney_on_King_Joffrey's_name_day) before they joust.(wiki:Herald.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) brings a long tail of retainers with him to the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast) of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), including heralds.(wiki:Herald.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) gives a signal to the herald to end the court session.(wiki:Herald.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) During the [bread riots](wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Tyrion commands three [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) to escort heralds who will command the people to return to their homes.(wiki:Herald.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
During the award ceremony after the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), heralds cry the name and deeds of the heroes of the battle as each one enters the [throne room](wiki:Throne_room).(wiki:Herald.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Heralds call out the names and titles of the lords and ladies making their entrance to the throne room during the [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_wedding) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell). Heralds later summon the singers to entertain the guests.(wiki:Herald.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
A herald announces the witnesses heard at Tyrion's trial.(wiki:Herald.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After Cersei announces to the court that Queen Margaery is no [maiden](wiki:Maiden), the royal herald tries to quell the noise by beating on the floor with his staff.(wiki:Herald.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The [Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) herald slams his staff on the plank floor before announcing Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) to the [Merman's Court](wiki:Merman's_Court).(wiki:Herald.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
A [shavepate](wiki:Shavepate) herald, wearing a shirt of polished [copper](wiki:Copper) disks and carrying a [drum](wiki:Drum), cries to let the royal procession make its way to [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit).(wiki:Herald.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
[Missandei](wiki:Missandei) takes on the role of herald for Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen),(wiki:Herald.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) but King [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) later replaces her.(wiki:Herald.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
