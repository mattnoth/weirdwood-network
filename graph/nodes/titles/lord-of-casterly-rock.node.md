## Origins

The [Casterlys](wiki:House_Casterly) were the first Lords of Casterly Rock in the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes) until [Lann the Clever](wiki:Lann_the_Clever) winkled the Casterlys out of the Rock. [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) thus ruled not as Lords of Casterly Rock, but [Kings of the Rock](wiki:King_of_the_Rock) until [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

Following the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire),(wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) King [Loren I Lannister](wiki:Loren_I_Lannister) bent the knee to King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen). For this, he was named Lord of Casterly Rock and [Warden of the West](wiki:Warden_of_the_West).(wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Lord [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister) supported King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), but was killed in the [Battle at the Red Fork](wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork).(wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_iron_islands:_the_red_kraken.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) His four-year old son [Loreon](wiki:Loreon_Lannister_(son_of_Jason)) became Lord of Casterly Rock, with Lord Jason's widow, Lady [Johanna](wiki:Johanna_Lannister) ruling in Lord Loreon's minority.(wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Several decades later, during the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), Lord [Damon Lannister](wiki:Damon_Lannister_(lord)) was defeated by Ser [Quentyn Ball](wiki:Quentyn_Ball) outside the gates of [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport). Tybolt inherited the lordship from Damon when the latter died of [the Great Spring Sickness](wiki:The_Great_Spring_Sickness) in [210 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). His own death, two years later, occurred under suspicious circumstances.(wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Tybolt's only heir was his three-year old daughter, [Cerelle](wiki:Cerelle_Lannister_(daughter_of_Tybolt)), who ruled Casterly Rock with her uncle [Gerold](wiki:Gerold_Lannister) as her regent, for less than a year. The suddenness of her death at such a young age caused rumors that Gerold, who inherited Casterly Rock after Cerelle, had played a role in the deaths of both Tybolt and Cerelle.(wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) Gerold was struck by [illness](wiki:Illness) and grief following the death of his heir, [Tywald](wiki:Tywald_Lannister), in [233 AC](wiki:233_AC) during the [Peake Uprising](wiki:Peake_Uprising), but roused himself after his second son, [Tion](wiki:Tion_Lannister), died during the [Third Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Third_Blackfyre_Rebellion) in [236 AC](wiki:236_AC), and began preparing his third son, [Tytos](wiki:Tytos_Lannister), for the rule of the Westerlands.(wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

Tytos inherited Casterly Rock in [244 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). During his reign, the power of House Lannister waned, as Tytos was weak-willed and indecisive, and was willing to forgive most debts and insults. His eldest son, [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), took matters into his own hands when he arrested Lord [Walderan Tarbeck](wiki:Walderan_Tarbeck). When Tarbeck's wife, Lady [Ellyn Reyne](wiki:Ellyn_Reyne), took three Lannisters captive in response, Tytos ordered everyone released. Nigh on a year later, both [House Tarbeck](wiki:House_Tarbeck) and [House Reyne](wiki:House_Reyne) were destroyed by Tywin during the [Reyne-Tarbeck revolt](wiki:Reyne-Tarbeck_revolt).(wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-4)

Tywin inherited Casterly Rock in [267 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest). He served as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) for [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) for twenty-one years.(wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands:_house_lannister_under_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-5)(wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Though he remained neutral throughout most of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), he marched his army to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) near the end of the war, where he declared loyalty to Aerys and asked to be allowed inside the city.(wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Once the gates opened, Tywin's men [sacked King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords

Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) is killed by his own son, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock.cite_ref-Rasos77.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Feast for Crows

With Tyrion attainted, and [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) in the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard), Dowager Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) becomes the Lady of Casterly Rock.(wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

After being bought as a slave by [Yezzan zo Qaggaz](wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz), Tyrion introduces himself as "Yollo" to Yezzan's slave overseer [Nurse](wiki:Nurse), though he had felt tempted to introduce himself as the rightful Lord of Casterly Rock.(wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

Later on, Tyrion claims the title of Lord of Casterly Rock and signs his contract with the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons) as such.(wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock.cite_ref-Radwd66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
