## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

During [the campaign](wiki:Targaryen_campaign_in_Slaver's_Bay) of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) in [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay), the Great Maesters of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) harvest crops and burn fields as Daenerys's host travels north from [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai). To intimidate her, they nail a [slave](wiki:Slave) child up on every milepost along the coast road, with an arm pointing in the direction of Meereen; Daenerys counts 163 children along the way.(wiki:Great_Masters.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
[Oznak zo Pahl](wiki:Oznak_zo_Pahl) is sent out to challenge Daenerys's champion when her host arrives before Meereen. [Strong Belwas](wiki:Strong_Belwas) slays Oznak in single combat, shaming the Great Masters.(wiki:Great_Masters.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) After [conquering Meeren](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen), Daenerys has the Great Masters and their women gathered in the plaza before the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid). Daenerys has 163 of their leaders nailed to wooden posts, each with an arm pointed to the next.(wiki:Great_Masters.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Daenerys rules as [Queen of Meereen](wiki:Queen_of_Meereen). From their pyramids, the Great Masters complain that the dragon queen has brought beggars, thieves, and whores to their city.(wiki:Great_Masters.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Daenerys refuses to compensate the Great Masters after freeing their slaves,(wiki:Great_Masters.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) and she orders that each pyramid send her a boy and a girl to serve as [her pages and cupbearers](wiki:Cupbearers_of_Daenerys_Targaryen).(wiki:Great_Masters.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
When Daenerys sends an envoy to the [Tolosi](wiki:Tolos), they demand that she restore the Great Masters to power.(wiki:Great_Masters.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) informs the queen that all of the Meereenese ships participating in a blockade of the city are captained by Great Masters.(wiki:Great_Masters.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The Great Masters occupy the red and orange benches when Daenerys reopens [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit).(wiki:Great_Masters.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
