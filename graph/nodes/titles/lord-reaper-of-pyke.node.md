## Origins

During the age of the [driftwood kings](wiki:Driftwood_kings), the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) were infamous for raiding the [green lands](wiki:Green_lands) of the [kingdoms](wiki:Hundred_Kingdoms) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), taking food back to the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) as autumn waned and winter approached. Even in the worst of winters, the ironborn ate off the food that was planted, sowed, and harvested by the men of the green lands, and so "We do not sow" became the boast and [words](wiki:House_words) of [House Greyjoy](wiki:House_Greyjoy), who began styling themselves as Lords Reaper of Pyke.(wiki:Lord_Reaper_of_Pyke.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_iron_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
The Lords Reaper have sat the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair) as the [Lords of the Iron Islands](wiki:Lord_of_the_Iron_Islands) since the [War of Conquest](wiki:War_of_Conquest), when the lords of the Iron Islands selected [Vickon Greyjoy](wiki:Vickon_Greyjoy), Lord Reaper of Pyke, as their [lord paramount](wiki:Lord_paramount).(wiki:Lord_Reaper_of_Pyke.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Lord_Reaper_of_Pyke.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_greyjoys_of_pyke.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
