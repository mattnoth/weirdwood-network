## Origins

During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Lord Piper summoned his levies and joined King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) against [Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare), [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers).(wiki:Lord_of_Pinkmaiden_Castle.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Lord [Jon Piper](wiki:Jon_Piper) fought for Prince [Aegon the Uncrowned](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) against King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord_of_Pinkmaiden_Castle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Lord [Petyr Piper](wiki:Petyr_Piper) declared for the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) and [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Lord_of_Pinkmaiden_Castle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) After the [Battle at the Red Fork](wiki:Battle_at_the_Red_Fork), he knighted [Pate of Longleaf](wiki:Pate_of_Longleaf) for mortally wounding [Jason Lannister](wiki:Jason_Lannister), [Lord of Casterly Rock](wiki:Lord_of_Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Lord_of_Pinkmaiden_Castle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Petyr died soon after in a [battle at Acorn Hall](wiki:Battle_at_Acorn_Hall) and was succeeded by Lord [Stanton Piper](wiki:Stanton_Piper).(wiki:Lord_of_Pinkmaiden_Castle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Stanton and Lord [Lyonel Deddings](wiki:Lyonel_Deddings) were burned by [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor) during the [Second Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:Second_Battle_of_Tumbleton).(wiki:Lord_of_Pinkmaiden_Castle.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
