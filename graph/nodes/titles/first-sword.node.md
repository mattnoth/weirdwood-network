## Culture

The First Sword commands the [Sealord's](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos) guardsmen and protects him in public. The First Sword is often the deadliest of the [bravos](wiki:Bravo) and [water dancers](wiki:Water_dance) of [Braavos](wiki:Braavos).(wiki:First_Sword.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_braavos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Origins

First Swords have participated in a dozen wars and protected numerous [Sealords](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos). Some of the most famous duels have involved First Swords.(wiki:First_Sword.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_braavos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Syrio Forel](wiki:Syrio_Forel), a former first sword, tells [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) that he had been chosen by the [Sealord of Braavos](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos) because he was the most observant of the [bravos](wiki:Bravos), and most able to protect the Sealord from threats seen and unseen.(wiki:First_Sword.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
