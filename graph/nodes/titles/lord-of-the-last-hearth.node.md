## Origins

House Umber once ruled as [First Men](wiki:First_Men) kings but they were reduced to vassals of [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) by the [Kings of Winter](wiki:Kings_of_Winter).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
The Lord Umber ruling in [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) hosted Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) when she visited the Last Hearth on her way from [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) to the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Lord [Jon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber_(husband_of_Serena)) was wed to [Serena Stark](wiki:Serena_Stark), the granddaughter of Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_stark_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Title_6-0)
Lord [Osric Umber](wiki:Osric_Umber) was wed to [Arrana Stark](wiki:Arrana_Stark), the daughter of Lady Serena Stark.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Rtwoiafappendix:_stark_lineage.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Title_6-1)
Lord [Harmond Umber](wiki:Harmond_Umber) helped Lord [Willam Stark](wiki:Willam_Stark) and his brother [Artos Stark](wiki:Artos_Stark) defeat King [Raymun Redbeard](wiki:Raymun_Redbeard) in a [battle at Long Lake](wiki:Battle_at_Long_Lake).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Lord [Hoarfrost Umber](wiki:Hoarfrost_Umber) sent his youngest son, [Hother](wiki:Hother_Umber), to the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) to become a [maester](wiki:Maester).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lord [Jon Umber](wiki:Jon_Umber) leads his men when [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls the bannermen of [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). At first he challenges the young man's authority, threatening to take his forces home if he is placed behind Lords [Halys Hornwood](wiki:Halys_Hornwood) or [Medger Cerwyn](wiki:Medger_Cerwyn) in the order of march. After being called out by Robb, the Greatjon draws his sword, only to have the [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) [Grey Wind](wiki:Grey_Wind) knock the sword from his hands and bite off two fingers. Impressed with Robb's courage, the Greatjon makes a joke of it instead of becoming angry and becomes Robb's greatest champion.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Lord Jon participates in the battles of [the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood) and [the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), where he sets fire to the Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s siege towers.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) After [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) is relieved and news of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s death arrives, the Greatjon is the first to declare Robb the [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

After participating in the [Battle of Oxcross](wiki:Battle_of_Oxcross), the Greatjon goes on to capture the gold mines at [Castamere](wiki:Castamere), [Nunn's Deep](wiki:Nunn's_Deep), and the [Pendric Hills](wiki:Pendric_Hills).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After Robb breaks his marriage contract with the [Freys](wiki:House_Frey), Greatjon Umber offers to make marriage contracts for his uncles, [Mors](wiki:Mors_Umber) and [Hother](wiki:Hother_Umber), in Robb's place.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) In Robb's plan to retake [Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin) from the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), the Greatjon is given command of the van and told to make his diversionary attack convincing, as Robb's own force is to circle behind Moat Cailin with the guidance of the [crannogmen](wiki:Crannogmen).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
During the [wedding](wiki:Wedding) feast at the [Twins](wiki:Twins), the Greatjon drunkenly sings "[The Bear and the Maiden Fair](wiki:The_Bear_and_the_Maiden_Fair)" while musicians perform "[Flowers of Spring](wiki:Flowers_of_Spring)". He carries [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully)'s bride, [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey), over his shoulder and takes her from the hall as part of the [bedding](wiki:Bedding) [customs](wiki:Customs).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
In preparation for the Freys' betrayal of House Stark, [Petyr](wiki:Petyr_Frey), [Merrett](wiki:Merrett_Frey), and Ser [Whalen Frey](wiki:Whalen_Frey) were tasked with getting the Greatjon too drunk to fight. Despite consuming enough alcohol to kill three normal men, when the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) begins eight men are needed to subdue the Greatjon in chains. He takes the sword of the first man to attack him, kills one, wounds two others, and bites off half the ear of Ser [Leslyn Haigh](wiki:Leslyn_Haigh).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Rasosepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) His son and heir, [the Smalljon](wiki:Jon_Umber_(son_of_Jon)), is slain during the massacre.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

The Greatjon is held captive at the Twins.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Following the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), Jaime informs Ser [Edwyn Frey](wiki:Edwyn_Frey) and [Walder Rivers](wiki:Walder_Rivers) that the King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) requires the transfer of all captives taken by House Frey in the Red Wedding.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

With the Greatjon still a captive of the Freys, a portion of [House Umber](wiki:House_Umber), led by his uncle Hother Whoresbane, grudgingly swears fealty to [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton), though according to Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) their loyalty is highly dubious.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) The other half of the Umbers, under [Mors Crowfood](wiki:Mors_Umber), take up the cause of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord_of_the_Last_Hearth.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
