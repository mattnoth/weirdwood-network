## Origins

During summertime, historical Lords Commander had [builders](wiki:Builders) raise the [Wall](wiki:Wall) higher.(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch.cite_ref-Ragot21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)

The [Night's King](wiki:Night's_King), the legendary thirteenth leader of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), had his name stricken from history.(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), [Lord Commander Hoare](wiki:Lord_Commander_Hoare) did not leave the Wall to oppose [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen), even though his brother, King [Harren the Black](wiki:Harren_Hoare), was burned alongside his kin by [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

[Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers) was elected as Lord Commander in [239 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) but he disappeared while ranging [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall) in [252 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

An election for a new Lord Commander occurred circa [269 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest),(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch.cite_ref-19) where Ser [Wynton Stout](wiki:Wynton_Stout) was a dozen votes short of being elected Lord Commander.(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

In [288 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), following the death of [Lord Commander Qorgyle](wiki:Lord_Commander_Qorgyle), [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) was elected Lord Commander.(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch.cite_ref-Rawoiafjeor_mormont.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

According to [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) has known a dozen lords commander during his years of service at the Wall.(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch.cite_ref-22)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) becomes the steward of Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont).(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) After the [Lord Commander's Tower](wiki:Lord_Commander's_Tower) burns, Jeor moves his chambers to the [King's Tower](wiki:King's_Tower).(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-1)

### A Clash of Kings

Jeor leads the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall).(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch.cite_ref-Racok13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

When Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin) advises [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) to surrender [Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) and take the black, Theon imagines himself rising high among the Night's Watch and possibly becoming Lord Commander. Theon ends up defeated by [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), however, with the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Jeor is slain during the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep).(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

Through the efforts of [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly), Jon is elected as Jeor's successor as Lord Commander.(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch.cite_ref-Rasos78.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Traditionally, there have been 998 Lords Commander of the Night's Watch, as of Lord Commander Snow. However, the exact number of the Lords Commander is uncertain: the oldest list in [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black)'s vaults, as found by Samwell, mentions 674 Lords Commander.(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Jon beheads [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) for insubordination.(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch.cite_ref-Radwd7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) After receiving [a letter](wiki:Bastard_Letter) from Ramsay and announcing his intention to march on Winterfell, the Lord Commander is attacked during the [mutiny at Castle Black](wiki:Mutiny_at_Castle_Black).(wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)
