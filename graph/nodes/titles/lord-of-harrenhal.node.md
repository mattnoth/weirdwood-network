## Origins

### House Hoare[ ]

The castle of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) was constructed on the orders of [Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare), the last [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers), who ruled over the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) and the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). It took almost forty years to build the castle. On the day of its completion, [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) landed on the shores of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), beginning [his Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Aegon eventually arrived at Harrenhal, where he offered Harren the Black a chance to surrender. The king refused, so at evenfall, Aegon and his [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Balerion](wiki:Balerion), descended on the castle, burning everything that was flammable with [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonflame), and even melting the stone with which the castle had been built with.(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) Harren and his sons died in the [burning of Harrenhal](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal) at the tower which afterwards became known as [Kingspyre Tower](wiki:Kingspyre_Tower).(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) It is said by some that the ghosts of Harren and his sons still haunt the castle.(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

### House Qoherys[ ]

Ser [Quenton Qoherys](wiki:Quenton_Qoherys), the [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms) at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), was made Lord of Harrenhal by King Aegon I following the death of Harren.(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) In [9 AC](wiki:9_AC), Quentyn died from a fall from his horse and was succeeded by his grandson, [Gargon](wiki:Gargon_Qoherys), who quickly gained a bad reputation for taking advantage of the lord's right of the [first night](wiki:First_night). In [37 AC](wiki:37_AC), Gargon was killed by the rebel [Harren the Red](wiki:Harren_the_Red), who claimed to be a grandson of Harren the Black. After killing Gargon, Harren declared himself to be the Lord of Harrenhal. Harren was slain in battle later that year.(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### House Harroway[ ]

Harrenhal was then granted to [House Harroway](wiki:House_Harroway).(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) In [44 AC](wiki:44_AC), King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) had Lord [Lucas Harroway](wiki:Lucas_Harroway), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), and his entire family killed, including Queen [Alys](wiki:Alys_Harroway), Maegor's own wife. At both Harrenhal and [Lord Harroway's Town](wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town), Maegor subsequently killed every man, woman, and child with a drop of Harroway blood.(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-2)

### House Towers[ ]

After the extinction of the Harroways, Maegor allowed twenty-three [knights](wiki:Knight) of his household to fight for the rights to the castle. The victor was Ser [Walton Towers](wiki:Walton_Towers), who was named Lord of Harrenhal but died of his wounds from the battle within a fortnight, after which his eldest son, [Jordan](wiki:Jordan_Towers), inherited the seat.(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-3)
Lord Jordan remained a loyal [bannerman](wiki:Bannerman) to Maegor throughout the king's reign, and was one of the last people to see Maegor alive before his mysterious death upon the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) in [48 AC](wiki:48_AC).(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-4) Jordan was one of the few lords loyal to Maegor who were brave enough to stay, and he yielded the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) to Prince [Jaehaerys Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) when he arrived to claim the Iron Throne. Jordan was sent to the [black cells](wiki:Black_cells), but was eventually pardoned by King Jaehaerys after surrendering some of his lands.(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_-_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Jordan died of a chest congestion in [53 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), after which Harrenhal passed to his last surviving son, [Maegor](wiki:Maegor_Towers).(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
Lord Maegor occupied only the [Tower of Dread](wiki:Tower_of_Dread), living in his castle with only a cook and three men-at-arms. He later accepted the presence and friendship of Queen [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)) in [56 AC](wiki:56_AC). After Maegor died in [61 AC](wiki:61_AC), Rhaena took in Maegor's household servants and continued to live in the [Widow's Tower](wiki:Widow's_Tower) until her own death in [73 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).

### House Strong[ ]

After Rhaena's death, the lordship was granted to Ser [Bywin Strong](wiki:Bywin_Strong), the brother of Ser [Lucamore Strong](wiki:Lucamore_Strong) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard).(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)
[House Strong](wiki:House_Strong) held Harrenhal throughout the remainder of the reign of King Jaehaerys, and throughout the reigns of kings [Viserys I](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) and [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen). Following the death of Lord [Lyonel Strong](wiki:Lyonel_Strong), along with his eldest son and heir, Ser [Harwin](wiki:Harwin_Strong), in a fire at Harrenhal in [120 AC](wiki:120_AC), the lordship passed to his younger son Lord [Larys Strong](wiki:Larys_Strong),(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) who continued to hold the title despite losing the castle temporarily to Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) Larys, the [Lord Confessor](wiki:Lord_Confessor) and [master of whisperers](wiki:Master_of_whisperers), was executed by the King's Hand Lord [Cregan Stark](wiki:Cregan_Stark) during the [Hour of the Wolf](wiki:Hour_of_the_Wolf) in [131 AC](wiki:131_AC).(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### House Lothston[ ]

In [151 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) convinced his brother, King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), to bestow Harrenhal upon Ser [Lucas Lothston](wiki:Lucas_Lothston_(husband_of_Falena)), the master-at-arms at the Red Keep.(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-2) Lord [Lucas "the Pander" Lothston](wiki:Lucas_Lothston_(father_of_Manfryd)) and his son [Manfryd](wiki:Manfryd_Lothston) were renowned for their black deeds.(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Manfryd is said to have betrayed [Daemon Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre) in the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion).(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
In [212 AC](wiki:212_AC) Lady [Danelle Lothston](wiki:Danelle_Lothston) led [Lothston](wiki:House_Lothston) men-at-arms when Lord [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers), the [King's Hand](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to [Aerys I Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_I_Targaryen), marched in great strength on the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls) to quash the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion).(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) During the reign of King [Maekar I Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen), Lady Danelle turned to the black arts, which ended the line of the Lothstons in madness and chaos.(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-3)

### House Whent[ ]

Harrenhal was granted to [House Whent](wiki:House_Whent) for their role in defeating the Lothstons.(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-4) Lord [Walter Whent](wiki:Walter_Whent) hosted the great [tourney at Harrenhal](wiki:Tourney_at_Harrenhal) in [281 AC](wiki:281_AC).(wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
