## Origins

When King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) arrived at Ghost Hill in the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), Lord Toland sent his champion to face him. After killing the man, Aegon learned he was no champion at all but simply Lord Toland's mad fool sent to give time for the Tolands to escape the castle.(wiki:Lord_of_Ghost_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
During the [Dragon's Wroth](wiki:Dragon's_Wroth), Lady Toland was murdered for the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne)'s ransom on her head.(wiki:Lord_of_Ghost_Hill.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lady [Nymella Toland](wiki:Nymella_Toland) is ordered by Prince [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell) to delay Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) at [Ghost Hill](wiki:Ghost_Hill) when he arrives. She plans to outdo Lady [Jordayne](wiki:House_Jordayne), who had arranged games in his honor.(wiki:Lord_of_Ghost_Hill.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lady Nymella is present at the feast when [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) arrives in [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) to deliver the head of Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane). She drinks when [Ricasso](wiki:Ricasso) raises a toast to King [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord_of_Ghost_Hill.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

Along with her daughters, Nymella hosts [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) and her companions when they arrive at [Ghost Hill](wiki:Ghost_Hill).(wiki:Lord_of_Ghost_Hill.cite_ref-6)
