## Origins

### Essos[ ]

The ancient title of [Warden of the River](wiki:Warden_of_the_River) is used by the [Meereenese](wiki:Meereen) official in charge of ferries, dredges, and irrigation ditches along the [Skahazadhan](wiki:Skahazadhan).(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

### Westeros[ ]

Wardens have historically been used through [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) south of the [Wall](wiki:Wall), including by petty kings of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) prior to [Nymeria's War](wiki:Nymeria's_War).(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_ten_thousand_ships.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest) and the unification of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) granted new warden titles to monarchs who submitted to [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen), including [Loren Lannister](wiki:Loren_I_Lannister), [Ronnel Arryn](wiki:Ronnel_Arryn_(king)), and [Torrhen Stark](wiki:Torrhen_Stark). Aegon also named [Harlan Tyrell](wiki:Harlan_Tyrell), a former steward, as warden.(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Wardens for the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) act as supreme military leaders responsible for the defense of their region in the event of foreign invasion.(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-SSM_4-0) In times of peace, this prestigious title is purely honorary. Tradition holds that each title is hereditarily given to the lord of a certain family, but the king retains the right to award it to another of his choosing. This typically only happens if the traditional holder dies and his heir is not yet of age, although in times of turbulence the office may be offered to those holding a monarch's favor.(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
The four major directional wardens as per the start of the books:
[Warden of the East](wiki:Warden_of_the_East), held by [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), Lord of the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie). [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) were once [Kings of Mountain and Vale](wiki:Kings_of_Mountain_and_Vale).(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_vale:_house_arryn.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), held by [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), Lord of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) were once [Kings in the North](wiki:Kings_in_the_North).(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_north:_the_kings_of_winter.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
[Warden of the South](wiki:Warden_of_the_South), held by [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), Lord of [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden). [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) were once stewards to [House Gardener](wiki:House_Gardener), the [Kings of the Reach](wiki:Kings_of_the_Reach).(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_reach:_house_tyrell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
[Warden of the West](wiki:Warden_of_the_West), held by [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), Lord of [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock). [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) were once the [Kings of the Rock](wiki:Kings_of_the_Rock).(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_westerlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
There are also smaller-scale wardens:
[Warden of the Prince's Pass](wiki:Warden_of_the_Prince's_Pass), guardian of the [Prince's Pass](wiki:Prince's_Pass) in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne). Held by Lord [Franklyn Fowler](wiki:Franklyn_Fowler) as per the tradition of [House Fowler](wiki:House_Fowler).
[Warden of the Sands](wiki:Warden_of_the_Sands), guardian of the deserts of Dorne. Only granted to Lord [Jon Rosby](wiki:Jon_Rosby) during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War).
[Warden of Stone Way](wiki:Warden_of_Stone_Way), guardian of the [Boneway](wiki:Boneway) in Dorne. Held by Lord [Anders Yronwood](wiki:Anders_Yronwood) as per the tradition of [House Yronwood](wiki:House_Yronwood).
[Warden of the White Knife](wiki:Warden_of_the_White_Knife), guardian of the [White Knife](wiki:White_Knife) in the [north](wiki:North). Held by Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) of [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) as per the tradition of [House Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly).
After reaching [majority](wiki:Customs) and beginning to rule in his own right, King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) replaced the [Warden of the King's Mint](wiki:Warden_of_the_King's_Mint), among other lesser offices.(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) called upon Tywin, his Warden of the West, and Eddard, his Warden of the North, to help suppress [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion).(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_iron_islands:_the_old_way_and_the_new.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), the [Warden of the East](wiki:Warden_of_the_East), dies unexpectedly.(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) bestows the title on Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister),(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) which upsets the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn). Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) refers to her son, Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn), as the True Warden of the East.(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), the [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North), is executed upon the order of King [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) His son, [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), is declared [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) by his bannermen in rebellion to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) and [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident), creates the new title of [Warden of the Southern Marches](wiki:Warden_of_the_Southern_Marches) for his uncle, Ser [Brynden "Blackfish" Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully), in recognition of his prominent military role in the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Robb is murdered at the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding) by Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton).(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) For his service to the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister), the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) grants Roose the title of [Warden of the North](wiki:Warden_of_the_North).(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) restore the title of [Warden of the East](wiki:Warden_of_the_East) to Robert Arryn once Lysa weds Petyr Baelish, thereby restoring the allegiance of House Arryn to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Tywin, the [Warden of the West](wiki:Warden_of_the_West), is slain by his son and lawful heir, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Warden.cite_ref-Rasos77.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
