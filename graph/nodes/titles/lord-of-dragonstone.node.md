## Edges

## Origins

When his daughter, [Daenys Targaryen](wiki:Daenys_Targaryen), foresaw the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria), Lord [Aenar Targaryen](wiki:Aenar_Targaryen) moved his wives, children, sibling, other kin, wealth and slaves to [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), a castle located on an [island with the same name](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)) in [114 BC](wiki:Years_before_Aegon's_Conquest). Dragonstone had been the westernmost outpost of the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) for two centuries by then. By moving his family to the outpost, Aenar became the first known Lord of Dragonstone. Aenar's descendants continued to rule Dragonstone after his death.(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
Through [the Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Lord [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) conquered the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) and ruled from the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) [Maegor Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), the younger son of King Aegon I, was raised on Dragonstone by his mother, Queen [Visenya](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen), and came to be the [Prince of Dragonstone](wiki:Prince_of_Dragonstone).(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) After Maegor left the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) in exile, King [Aenys I](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen) granted the title to his eldest son, Prince [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)), in [41 AC](wiki:41_AC).(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) "Prince of Dragonstone" thus became established as the title of the presumptive heir to the Iron Throne.(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
This custom continued until [283 AC](wiki:283_AC), when the Targaryen dynasty was ended at the conclusion of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion). King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) and his eldest son, Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen),(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) were slain, as were Rhaegar's children, [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Rhaegar)) and [Aegon](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)).(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Aerys's sister-wife, Queen [Rhaella Targaryen](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen), had fled to Dragonstone with her younger son, [Viserys](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen), shortly before the end of the war, where she spent nine months until her own death in childbirth during a fierce storm.(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) During this time, Rhaella crowned her son Viserys as king,(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Rawoiafrhaella_targaryen.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) and following the birth of his sister, [Daenerys](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), Viserys named her Princess of Dragonstone.(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
The Targaryen children were forced to flee before the [assault on Dragonstone](wiki:Assault_on_Dragonstone), however, and the castle was captured by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), the younger brother of the new king, [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Robert gave Stannis Dragonstone as his seat, making Stannis the first Lord of Dragonstone in nearly three hundred years. Stannis wanted to be named [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End), but Robert granted that title instead to the youngest of the brothers, [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), Lord of Dragonstone, is a claimant to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Following Stannis's defeat in the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), his [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent), attempts to negotiate with Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), the Hand for King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). Without Stannis's knowledge, Alester suggests that Stannis relinquish his royal claim while remaining both Lord of Dragonstone and [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End). Stannis has Alester imprisoned for treason.(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Rasos25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Stannis sails from Dragonstone to [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea),(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) and his army arrives in time to defeat the [wildling](wiki:Free_folk) host gathered by [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder), [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall), during the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall).(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Stannis, who has been attainted,(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) remains at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) leads the [capture of Dragonstone](wiki:Siege_of_Dragonstone).(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Stannis, considered a rebel Lord of Dragonstone and pretender to the Iron Throne,(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) marches against [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton).(wiki:Lord_of_Dragonstone.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
