## Origins

During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Lords Fell, [Buckler](wiki:House_Buckler), and [Errol](wiki:House_Errol) opposed [Orys Baratheon](wiki:Orys_Baratheon) during his march from the [Aegonfort](wiki:Aegonfort) toward [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), but the storm lords were forced to retreat when [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) had [Meraxes](wiki:Meraxes) set [afire](wiki:Dragonfire) parts of what is now the southern [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood).(wiki:Lord_of_Felwood.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Lord Fell was smothered in a [brothel](wiki:Brothel) by [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) assassins during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War).(wiki:Lord_of_Felwood.cite_ref-Rfabthe_reign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

At the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) had Lady Fell executed because she supported Aegon's rival, [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord_of_Felwood.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

At the beginning of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), [Lord Fell](wiki:Lord_Fell) remained loyal to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) and together with Lords [Grandison](wiki:Lord_Grandison) and [Cafferen](wiki:Lord_Cafferen) planned to join their forces to bring Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s head to King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen). Robert defeated them in three [battles at Summerhall](wiki:Battles_at_Summerhall), slaying Lord Fell in single combat and capturing his son [Silveraxe](wiki:Silveraxe_Fell), who turned to Robert's side afterwards.(wiki:Lord_of_Felwood.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
