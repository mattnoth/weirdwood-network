## Origins

Lord [Edgerran Oakheart](wiki:Edgerran_Oakheart) is known for fighting the [Dornish](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Lord_of_Old_Oak.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Lord Oakheart led King [Mern IX Gardener](wiki:Mern_IX_Gardener)'s left at the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire).(wiki:Lord_of_Old_Oak.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
In [12 AC](wiki:12_AC), during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), Lord Oakheart's daughter, [Alys Oakheart](wiki:Alys_Oakheart), married Ser [Jon Cafferen](wiki:Jon_Cafferen). At the wedding, the notorious [Wyl of Wyl](wiki:Wyl_of_Wyl) led attackers that slew Lord Oakheart and most of the guests.(wiki:Lord_of_Old_Oak.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_.E2.80.93_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) His successor proposed to King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) to send Princess [Deria Martell](wiki:Deria_Martell) to the meanest brothels of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), King Aegon rejected Lord Oakheart's proposition.(wiki:Lord_of_Old_Oak.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_.E2.80.93_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
In [48 AC](wiki:48_AC), Lords [Torgen Oakheart](wiki:Torgen_Oakheart) and [Rickard Rowan](wiki:Rickard_Rowan) supported [Septon](wiki:Septon) [Moon](wiki:Moon_(septon)) and marched with him towards [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). When Moon was assassinated, Lord Torgen decamped the next day.(wiki:Lord_of_Old_Oak.cite_ref-Rfabprince_into_king_.E2.80.93_the_ascension_of_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) The following year, Lord Torgen later attended [the wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding) of [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) and [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon) at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), where he met King [Jaehaerys I](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), pronouncing him grim.(wiki:Lord_of_Old_Oak.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_.E2.80.93_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
In [73 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Lord Oakheart held a tourney to celebrate the birth of a son, where Prince [Baelon](wiki:Baelon_Targaryen_(son_of_Jaehaerys_I)) unhorsed Ser [Denys](wiki:Denys_Oakheart), heir to Old Oak.(wiki:Lord_of_Old_Oak.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_.E2.80.93_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
In [112 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Lord Oakheart hosted Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) during her [royal progress](wiki:Royal_progress) in the south.(wiki:Lord_of_Old_Oak.cite_ref-Rtrp.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Old_Oak.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_.E2.80.93_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
During the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), Lord Oakheart tried to keep one foot in each camp.(wiki:Lord_of_Old_Oak.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
