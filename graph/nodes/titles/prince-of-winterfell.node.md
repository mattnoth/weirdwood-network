## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Prince [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark), the heir of his elder brother [Robb](wiki:Robb_Stark), is the [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) in Winterfell while Robb, the [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), campaigns in the south amid the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Prince_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Prince_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) takes Bran and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) prisoner with the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell) and sits the throne within the castle's Great Hall.(wiki:Prince_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) His sister [Asha](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) later mocks him for his inability to properly hold Winterfell, calling him Prince of Fools.(wiki:Prince_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Prior to the [battle at Winterfell](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell), the outnumbered Theon tells Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel)'s army of [northmen](wiki:Northmen) that all who swear fealty to his father, [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), as their king and to Theon as Prince of Winterfell will be confirmed in their rights. However, Theon is captured by [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) during the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Prince_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After noticing [other people](wiki:Free_folk) at [Queenscrown](wiki:Queenscrown), Bran tells himself not to be afraid, as he was the Prince of Winterfell.(wiki:Prince_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Rasos40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Having been tortured by [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) and the now-legitimized Ramsay, Theon emerges from the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort) as "Reek". While Theon had been arrogant and cocksure during his brief time as Prince of Winterfell, "Reek" is traumatized and ruined.(wiki:Prince_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
Theon travels under a peace banner to present Ramsay Bolton's terms of rendition during the [siege of Moat Cailin](wiki:Siege_of_Moat_Cailin). Since Theon is the son of the late Balon Greyjoy, [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers), Ramsay instructs Theon to act as a prince when speaking with the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) garrison guarding the three towers. However, Ramsay reminds Theon that he is still truly "Reek" at heart.(wiki:Prince_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Later in the [godswood of Winterfell](wiki:Godswood_of_Winterfell), Theon is chosen to ceremoniously present "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)" (actually [Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)) in marriage to Ramsay, since Theon was the longtime ward of [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), [Lord of Winterfell](wiki:Lord_of_Winterfell), and it is believed that Arya's trueborn brothers are dead. Ramsay mocks Theon as Prince of Winterfell before his bedding with Jeyne,(wiki:Prince_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and a guard later mocks Theon as the Prince of Stink.(wiki:Prince_of_Winterfell.cite_ref-Radwd51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
