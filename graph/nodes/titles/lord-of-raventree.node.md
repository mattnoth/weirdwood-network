## Origins

The Lords of [Raventree Hall](wiki:Raventree_Hall) from [House Blackwood](wiki:House_Blackwood) were [river kings](wiki:King_of_the_Trident) during the [Age of Heroes](wiki:Age_of_Heroes) and became bitter rivals with their neighbors, the [Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken) kings from [Stone Hedge](wiki:Stone_Hedge).(wiki:Lord_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Lord [Roderick Blackwood](wiki:Roderick_Blackwood) led a rebellion against [Humfrey I Teague](wiki:Humfrey_I_Teague), King of the Rivers and the Hills, when Humfrey attempted to suppress worship of the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods). Roderick's son-in-law, King [Arlan III Durrandon](wiki:Arlan_III_Durrandon), led a [stormlander](wiki:Stormlander) host to help Lord Roderick. Roderick died during the campaign and [House Teague](wiki:House_Teague) was extinguished in the [Battle of Six Kings](wiki:Battle_of_Six_Kings), allowing the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) to add the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) to the domain of [House Durrandon](wiki:House_Durrandon).(wiki:Lord_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

Three centuries later, control of the riverlands passed to [Harwyn Hoare](wiki:Harwyn_Hoare), [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands), after he defeated Arlan's descendant, King [Arrec Durrandon](wiki:Arrec_Durrandon). Lady [Agnes Blackwood](wiki:Agnes_Blackwood) and her sons marched against King Harwyn's [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), but they were attacked from behind by her rival, Lord [Lothar Bracken](wiki:Lothar_Bracken), and killed by Harwyn after the battle.(wiki:Lord_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)

### Targaryen Era

During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Lord Blackwood joined [Edmyn Tully](wiki:Edmyn_Tully), [Lord of Riverrun](wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun), and other riverlords in rebelling against [Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare), [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers).(wiki:Lord_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

In [79 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) made preparations for the [betrothal](wiki:Betrothal) of her daughter, Princess [Daella Targaryen](wiki:Daella_Targaryen) to Lord Blackwood's son, [Royce Blackwood](wiki:Royce_Blackwood). Daella became afraid when she learned the Blackwoods worshiped the old gods, however, and the betrothal was called off.(wiki:Lord_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

Lord Blackwood supported [Laenor Velaryon](wiki:Laenor_Velaryon)'s claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) at the [Great Council of 101 AC](wiki:Great_Council).(wiki:Lord_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

Lord [Samwell Blackwood](wiki:Samwell_Blackwood) and his son [Benjicot](wiki:Benjicot_Blackwood) supported the [blacks](wiki:Blacks) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Lord_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Young Bloody Ben Blackwood, Lord [Elmo Tully](wiki:Elmo_Tully), and Ser [Oscar Tully](wiki:Oscar_Tully) were called the [Lads](wiki:Lads) by their men.(wiki:Lord_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_house_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) After the war Lord Benjicot organized, with the help of his aunt [Alysanne](wiki:Alysanne_Blackwood), a [Widow Fair](wiki:Widow_Fairs) at Raventree.(wiki:Lord_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) During the reign of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen), Benjicot and a thousand Blackwood men supported Ser [Robert Rowan](wiki:Robert_Rowan)'s campaign to end the civil war in the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn);(wiki:Lord_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) Benjicot and Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) eventually forced the other claimants to accept [Joffrey Arryn](wiki:Joffrey_Arryn) as [Lord of the Eyrie](wiki:Lord_of_the_Eyrie).(wiki:Lord_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

In [206 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Lord [Quentyn Blackwood](wiki:Quentyn_Blackwood) was killed in a [tourney](wiki:Tourney) by Ser [Otho Bracken](wiki:Otho_Bracken), reigniting the feud between the Blackwoods and Brackens.(wiki:Lord_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

In [212 AC](wiki:212_AC), Lord Blackwood was part of the army taken to [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) by Lord [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers) to end the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion).(wiki:Lord_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood) fights in the [battle under the walls of Riverrun](wiki:Battle_under_the_walls_of_Riverrun), leading the remnants of the riverlands force back within the walls of [the castle](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Lord_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) During the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), he leads a sortie which takes the middle [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) camp in the rear and rescues Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully).(wiki:Lord_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) He joins the other river lords in declaring [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) to be [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Lord_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Lord Tytos [retakes his lands](wiki:Liberation_of_Raventree_Hall) from the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Lord_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Lord Tytos is present at the funeral of his liege [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), [Lord Paramount of the Trident](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Lord_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) His son [Lucas](wiki:Lucas_Blackwood) is killed in the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Lord_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Lord Tytos stays loyal to the [Stark](wiki:House_Stark) cause, despite Robb's death in the Red Wedding. He is besieged in his own castle by his rival, Lord [Jonos Bracken](wiki:Jonos_Bracken), who has bent the knee to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Raffc44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) arrives at [Blackwood Vale](wiki:Blackwood_Vale) to resolve the [siege of Raventree](wiki:Siege_of_Raventree), as Tytos refuses to surrender to Jonos despite running low on supplies. Tytos agrees to give up some land to [House Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken), although less than Jonos desired, and to send some gold and silver to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). Tytos convinces Jaime to take one of his sons, [Hoster](wiki:Hoster_Blackwood), as a hostage, rather than his only daughter, [Bethany](wiki:Bethany_Blackwood).(wiki:Lord_of_Raventree.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
