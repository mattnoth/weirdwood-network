## Edges

## Origins

After Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) conquered all but two of [the islands](wiki:Stepstones) in [109 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) during the [War for the Stepstones](wiki:War_for_the_Stepstones), he declared himself King of the Stepstones and the Narrow Sea, and Lord [Corlys Velaryon](wiki:Corlys_Velaryon) placed a crown on his ally's head. The [Kingdom of the Three Daughters](wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Three_Daughters) responded the following year by sending a force led by [Racallio Ryndoon](wiki:Racallio_Ryndoon).(wiki:King_of_the_Stepstones_and_the_Narrow_Sea.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Another of Daemon's opponents was [Qoren Martell](wiki:Qoren_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne).(wiki:King_of_the_Stepstones_and_the_Narrow_Sea.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Daemon returned to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in [111 AC](wiki:111_AC) to attend a tourney celebrating the fifth anniversary of the marriage between his brother, King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen), and Queen [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower). Daemon offered his crown to Viserys in homage and reconciliation. Daemon returned to the Stepstones after half a year, however, after again quarreling with his royal brother.(wiki:King_of_the_Stepstones_and_the_Narrow_Sea.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Daemon abandoned the Stepstones after the death of his estranged wife, Lady [Rhea Royce](wiki:Rhea_Royce), in [115 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), but he failed to claim Rhea's lands at [Runestone](wiki:Runestone). Tired of the Stepstones, Daemon instead wed Corlys's daughter, [Laena Velaryon](wiki:Laena_Velaryon), at [Driftmark](wiki:Driftmark).(wiki:King_of_the_Stepstones_and_the_Narrow_Sea.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
Five men would follow Daemon as Kings of the Narrow Sea before the short-lived kingdom collapsed.(wiki:King_of_the_Stepstones_and_the_Narrow_Sea.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Ser [Otto Hightower](wiki:Otto_Hightower) offered to cede the Stepstones to the Triarchy during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), although the islands had never been claimed by the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne).(wiki:King_of_the_Stepstones_and_the_Narrow_Sea.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
When the [Daughters' War](wiki:Daughters'_War) broke out among the Triarchy after the death of [Sharako Lohar](wiki:Sharako_Lohar) in [131 AC](wiki:131_AC), [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) sent [Racallio Ryndoon](wiki:Racallio_Ryndoon) to conquer the Stepstones. The captain-general slew the reigning King of the Narrow Sea, only to then claim the title himself.(wiki:King_of_the_Stepstones_and_the_Narrow_Sea.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) After Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) defeated the [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) fleet at the Stepstones in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Lord [Manfryd Mooton](wiki:Manfryd_Mooton) was sent to [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) to negotiate with the [Sealord](wiki:Sealord_of_Braavos). Braavos agreed to end its support of Racallio and to cede the Stepstones to the Iron Throne, although the islands were still occupied by Racallio and [Pentos](wiki:Pentos).(wiki:King_of_the_Stepstones_and_the_Narrow_Sea.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) "Queen" Racallio was at his greatest strength in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC),(wiki:King_of_the_Stepstones_and_the_Narrow_Sea.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) but he fled for the [Basilisk Isles](wiki:Basilisk_Isles) the following year.(wiki:King_of_the_Stepstones_and_the_Narrow_Sea.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) At that time [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) occupied most of the Stepstones for Princess [Aliandra Martell](wiki:Aliandra_Martell).(wiki:King_of_the_Stepstones_and_the_Narrow_Sea.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
