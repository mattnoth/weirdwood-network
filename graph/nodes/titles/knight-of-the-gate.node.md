## Origins

Ser [Joffrey Arryn](wiki:Joffrey_Arryn) was appointed as Knight of the Gate in [124 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) by his fourth cousin [Jeyne Arryn](wiki:Jeyne_Arryn), the [Lady of the Eyrie](wiki:Lady_of_the_Eyrie). He served for ten years until her death in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC).(wiki:Knight_of_the_Gate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
After [Lysa Tully](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) wed [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), her uncle, Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully), accompanied her to the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) and was named Knight of the Gate by Jon, the [Lord of the Eyrie](wiki:Lord_of_the_Eyrie).(wiki:Knight_of_the_Gate.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

With [war](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings) threatening the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), Ser Brynden resigns as Knight of the Gate to instead aid [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully) of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Knight_of_the_Gate.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
