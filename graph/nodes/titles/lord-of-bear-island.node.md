## Origins

Presumably prior to [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion),(wiki:Lord_of_Bear_Island.cite_ref-2) Jeor abdicated his seat and joined the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), leaving the lordship of [Bear Island](wiki:Bear_Island) to his son, [Jorah](wiki:Jorah_Mormont).(wiki:Lord_of_Bear_Island.cite_ref-Rawoiafjeor_mormont.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

In [289 AC](wiki:289_AC) Lord Jorah followed his liege lord, Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark), and fought in [Greyjoy's Rebellion](wiki:Greyjoy's_Rebellion), during which he earned [knighthood](wiki:Knight) from King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord_of_Bear_Island.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Some years later, however, Jorah exiled himself in order to avoid execution by Eddard for having sold poachers into [slavery](wiki:Slavery).(wiki:Lord_of_Bear_Island.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Jorah's aunt, [Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont), became the new Lady of Bear Island.(wiki:Lord_of_Bear_Island.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lady Maege and her eldest daughter and heir, [Dacey](wiki:Dacey_Mormont), are among the [northern](wiki:North) host brought south by [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark).(wiki:Lord_of_Bear_Island.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Maege and Dacey take part in the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Lord_of_Bear_Island.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Robb is proclaimed [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:Lord_of_Bear_Island.cite_ref-Ragot71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Lady Maege is among the northern lords who accompanies King Robb north from Riverrun to attend the wedding of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) at the [Twins](wiki:Twins). At [Hag's Mire](wiki:Hag's_Mire), she and [Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover) are ordered by Robb to travel to [Seagard](wiki:Seagard). There they are to take separate ships from Lord [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister) to sail up the [Neck](wiki:Neck) and to search for [Greywater Watch](wiki:Greywater_Watch) as envoys to the [crannogmen](wiki:Crannogmen), in preparation for Robb's planned attack on [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn)-occupied [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin). The letters they carry will contain false commands for northern lords, however, in case they are captured by enemies.(wiki:Lord_of_Bear_Island.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

The heir of Bear Island, Dacey, is one of those killed at the Twins during the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Lord_of_Bear_Island.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

The former Lord of Bear Island, [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont), is slain by [Ollo Lophand](wiki:Ollo_Lophand) during the [mutiny at Craster's Keep](wiki:Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep).(wiki:Lord_of_Bear_Island.cite_ref-Rasos33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Feast for Crows

[Alysane Mormont](wiki:Alysane_Mormont) becomes the new heir of Bear Island.(wiki:Lord_of_Bear_Island.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Lady Maege is in an unknown location with two of her daughters ([Lyra](wiki:Lyra_Mormont) and [Jorelle](wiki:Jorelle_Mormont)).(wiki:Lord_of_Bear_Island.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

Alysane and men-at-arms of [House Mormont](wiki:House_Mormont) launch an attack on the ironborn longships off the strand of [Deepwood Motte](wiki:Deepwood_Motte), managing to take them unawares by hiding fighters inside a gaggle of fishing sloops. She burns and captures all ships, securing several ironborn captains, knights, warriors, and other nobility for ransom.(wiki:Lord_of_Bear_Island.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) After [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) [retakes Deepwood](wiki:Fight_by_Deepwood_Motte) and restores it to [House Glover](wiki:House_Glover), Alysane joins his army.(wiki:Lord_of_Bear_Island.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)
