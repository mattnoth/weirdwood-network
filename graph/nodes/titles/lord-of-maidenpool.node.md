## Origins

At the start of the [Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Lord Mooton allied himself with Lord [Darklyn](wiki:House_Darklyn) to fight [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen). Both Lords died in the following [battle](wiki:Aegon's_first_test), Lord Mooton's brother, [Jon Mooton](wiki:Jon_Mooton), yielded to Aegon and became the new Lord of Maidenpool. Lord Jon later fought for the [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen) in the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire), slaying men who tried to escape the [dragons](wiki:Dragon).(wiki:Lord_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)(wiki:Lord_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
During the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising), Lord Mooton slew Lord [Rupert Falwell](wiki:Rupert_Falwell) in the [battle at the Great Fork](wiki:Battle_at_the_Great_Fork).(wiki:Lord_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Rtsotd.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)(wiki:Lord_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
In his youth, Lord [Jonah Mooton](wiki:Jonah_Mooton) was a friend of Princess [Saera Targaryen](wiki:Saera_Targaryen). When she disappeared in [85 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) her father, King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), asked Jonah to watch for her in Maidenpool.(wiki:Lord_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
At the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord [Walys Mooton](wiki:Walys_Mooton) supported Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen)'s claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). In that effect, Lord Walys [retook](wiki:Retaking_of_Rook's_Rest) [Rook's Rest](wiki:Rook's_Rest) for the [blacks](wiki:Blacks). He was later slain in a failed attempt to kill King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen)'s dragon, [Sunfyre](wiki:Sunfyre). The older of his two brothers, [Manfryd](wiki:Manfryd_Mooton), succeeded him as Lord of Maidenpool and continued to support the blacks.(wiki:Lord_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)(wiki:Lord_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)
Lord Manfryd hosted the [dragonriders](wiki:Dragonrider) Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) and [Nettles](wiki:Nettles) at Maidenpool for a time while the two were searching for Prince [Aemond One-Eye](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) and his dragon, [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar). Lord Manfryd changed side and aligned his house with King Aegon and the [greens](wiki:Greens) after refusing to kill Nettles at Rhaenyra's behest.(wiki:Lord_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)(wiki:Lord_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
After the war, Lord Manfryd served as one of the seven initial council of [regents](wiki:Regent) for the King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen). Lord Mandfryd served until his own death due to [illness](wiki:Illness) in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC).(wiki:Lord_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)
When Lord [Bloodraven](wiki:Brynden_Rivers) marched on [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) to quash the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion), among the river lords who came in support was Lord Mooton.(wiki:Lord_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord [William Mooton](wiki:William_Mooton) supports King [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Lord_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
According to a nameless traveller, Lord [William Mooton](wiki:William_Mooton) and his sons try to hunt [a huge she-wolf](wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf)) that had attacked some [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk).(wiki:Lord_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Racok5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Despite Maidenpool having been sacked three times during the war, Lord William still holds the town.(wiki:Lord_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) arrives at Maidenpool and sets out at taking control of the town and fighting outlaws in the region while Lord William remains safely behind his walls.(wiki:Lord_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1) Lord William agrees to betroth his eldest daughter and heir, [Eleanor Mooton](wiki:Eleanor_Mooton), to Randyll's son and heir, [Dickon Tarly](wiki:Dickon_Tarly).(wiki:Lord_of_Maidenpool.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-2)
