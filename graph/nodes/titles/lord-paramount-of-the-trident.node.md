## Edges


## Origins

During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully) of [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) were the first of the [rivermen](wiki:Rivermen) support [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) against [Harren Hoare](wiki:Harren_Hoare), [King of the Isles and the Rivers](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_Rivers). After Harren's death in the [burning of Harrenhal](wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal), Aegon the Conqueror rewarded Lord [Edmyn Tully](wiki:Edmyn_Tully) by granting him the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) and naming him Lord Paramount of the Trident.(wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Although the Tullys have long been powerful river lords,(wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_house_tully.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) the domains of Riverrun yield smaller armies than those of the [Blackwoods](wiki:House_Blackwood), [Brackens](wiki:House_Bracken), [Freys](wiki:House_Frey), and [Vances](wiki:House_Vance). The Tully lineage was less proud than that of the [Mallisters](wiki:House_Mallister), and they were not as wealthy as as the [Mootons](wiki:House_Mooton).(wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Riverrun is a strong castle,(wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands:_riverrun.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) but it is significantly smaller than ruined [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) The Tullys have had difficulty at times in managing the quarrelsome river lords.(wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

With [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully), Lord Paramount of the Trident, ailing at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), his heir, Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully), supports [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), the newly-crowned [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North)(wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and [King of the Trident](wiki:King_of_the_Trident).(wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rasos14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
After the assassination of King [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) at [Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), plot to form an alliance with [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) to win over any lords from the [Reach](wiki:Reach) or the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) who did not immediately declare for King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) after Renly's death. Petyr volunteers to travel to [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge) to broker the deal,(wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Racok36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and then in [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) he is successful in forging a [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister)-Tyrell alliance through the engagement of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell),(wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Racok54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) the daughter of [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell), [Lord Paramount of the Mander](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Mander).
Petyr returns to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with Tyrell forces alongside those of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister). The allied armies are able to take Stannis's forces in the rear in the last hours of the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), with Petyr providing the idea to dress Ser [Garlan Tyrell](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell) in the late King Renly's armor during the battle so as to frighten Stannis's [men-at-arms](wiki:Men-at-arms).(wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
After the battle, Petyr is rewarded with the title Lord Paramount of the Trident and is made the new liege lord over the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) according to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne). Petyr is also made [Lord of Harrenhal](wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal), although the castle is still held by Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) in Robb's name. Though Petyr has not set foot in [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), the title gives him nominal control over the war-torn riverlands and elevates his rank considerably.(wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Hoster Tully passes away at Riverrun(wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rasos35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) and his successor, Lord Edmure Tully, is attainted by the Iron Throne(wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) after being captured at the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
In addition to becoming Lord Paramount of the Trident, Petyr Baelish becomes [Lord Protector of the Vale](wiki:Lord_Protector_of_the_Vale) after wedding Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn).(wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)(wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Having been named [Lord of Riverrun](wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun) by the Iron Throne, Ser [Emmon Frey](wiki:Emmon_Frey) assumes that he is now the liege of the [rivermen](wiki:Rivermen), including even his own father, Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey). At the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun), however, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) explains to Emmon that Lord Petyr Baelish of Harrenhal is Lord Paramount.(wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Trident.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
