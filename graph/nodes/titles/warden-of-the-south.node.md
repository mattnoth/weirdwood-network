## Origins

[Mern IX Gardener](wiki:Mern_IX_Gardener), [King of the Reach](wiki:King_of_the_Reach), and his kin from [House Gardener](wiki:House_Gardener) were killed at the [Field of Fire](wiki:Field_of_Fire) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest). When King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) arrived at [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden), the castle was surrendered to him by its steward, [Harlan Tyrell](wiki:Harlan_Tyrell). Aegon the Conqueror rewarded Harlan by naming him Warden of the South and [Lord Paramount of the Mander](wiki:Lord_Paramount_of_the_Mander).(wiki:Warden_of_the_South.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

During the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), Aegon sent Harlan to march against [Uthor Uller](wiki:Uthor_Uller), [Lord of the Hellholt](wiki:Lord_of_the_Hellholt). Harlan's host was greatly weakened in the Dornish deserts, however, and they found the [Hellholt](wiki:Hellholt) abandoned by the [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen). Harlan marched east toward [Vaith](wiki:Vaith) after the [Defenestration of Sunspear](wiki:Defenestration_of_Sunspear), but his host disappeared in the red sands of central Dorne in [5 AC](wiki:5_AC).(wiki:Warden_of_the_South.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) When Aegon wanted to respond to Dornish attacks on [Nightsong](wiki:Nightsong) and [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) in [10 AC](wiki:10_AC), young Lord [Theo Tyrell](wiki:Theo_Tyrell) was reluctant to support another invasion of Dorne.(wiki:Warden_of_the_South.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Lord [Lyonel Tyrell](wiki:Lyonel_Tyrell_(lord)) participated in the [conquest of Dorne](wiki:Conquest_of_Dorne) by King [Daeron I Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_I_Targaryen), but he was assassinated at [Sandstone](wiki:Sandstone).(wiki:Warden_of_the_South.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
