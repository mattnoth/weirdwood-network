## Origins

Lord [Lyman Beesbury](wiki:Lyman_Beesbury) served as [lord treasurer](wiki:Lord_treasurer)(wiki:Lord_of_Honeyholt.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin)(wiki:Lord_of_Honeyholt.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon.E2.80.94a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) for kings [Jaehaerys I](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord_of_Honeyholt.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
After Viserys's death, Lord Lyman pushed for his daughter and designated heir, Princess [Rhaenyra](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), to be crowned as the new queen. The [small council](wiki:Small_council) led by Queen [Alicent Hightower](wiki:Alicent_Hightower) and her [party](wiki:Greens) prefered Prince [Aegon the Elder](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) instead. Chroniclers disagree of the manner of Lyman's death, but all conquer that he was the first casualties of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Lord_of_Honeyholt.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Lord_of_Honeyholt.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
Lyman's death was kept secret, with most believing he was languishing in a black cell.(wiki:Lord_of_Honeyholt.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_blacks_and_the_greens.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) His grandson and heir, Ser [Alan Beesbury](wiki:Alan_Beesbury), succeeded him as Lord of Honeyholt.(wiki:Lord_of_Honeyholt.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Lord Alan fought for Rhaenyra and her [partyduring](wiki:Blacks) the war. He was captured during the [Battle of the Honeywine](wiki:Battle_of_the_Honeywine).(wiki:Lord_of_Honeyholt.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
A Lord Beesbury (possibly Alan) along with Lord [Costayne](wiki:House_Costayne) and [Lord Redwyne](wiki:Lord_Redwyne) escorted the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) for the wedding of King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) and Queen [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen).(wiki:Lord_of_Honeyholt.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[Ben Beesbury](wiki:Ben_Beesbury), an old and blind Lord of Honeyholt, was once proposed as a match for Prince [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell).(wiki:Lord_of_Honeyholt.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
