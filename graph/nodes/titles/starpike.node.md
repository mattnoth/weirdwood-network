## Origins

The [Peakes](wiki:House_Peake) of Starpike have an older lineage than that of [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell),(wiki:Starpike.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_reach:_house_tyrell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) claiming descent from [Florys the Fox](wiki:Florys_the_Fox).(wiki:Starpike.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Starpike is the last castle that remains of the three once held by House Peake,(wiki:Starpike.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) as [Dunstonbury](wiki:Dunstonbury) and [Whitegrove](wiki:Whitegrove)(wiki:Starpike.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) were stripped after the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion) because of Lord [Gormon Peake](wiki:Gormon_Peake)'s support for [Daemon I Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre).(wiki:Starpike.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
According to a semi-canon source, King [Maekar I Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen)'s helm was crushed by a rock from the battlements of Starpike during the [Peake Uprising](wiki:Peake_Uprising). Lord [Robert Reyne](wiki:Robert_Reyne) and Ser [Tywald Lannister](wiki:Tywald_Lannister) were also killed in the [Storming of Starpike](wiki:Storming_of_Starpike).(wiki:Starpike.cite_ref-sample_6-0)
