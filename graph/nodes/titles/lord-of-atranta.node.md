## Origins

Lord Vance was slain in the war between the [Teagues](wiki:House_Teague) and the [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Arlan III Durrandon](wiki:Arlan_III_Durrandon).(wiki:Lord_of_Atranta.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Lord [Robert Vance](wiki:Robert_Vance) was a would-be [river king](wiki:River_king) during the rule of the Storm Kings;(wiki:Lord_of_Atranta.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) it is unknown which branch he was from.
Lord [Ronnel Vance](wiki:Ronnel_Vance) supported Prince [Aegon the Uncrowned](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)) against King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), but he was slain in the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye);(wiki:Lord_of_Atranta.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) it is unknown which branch he was from.
Lord [Norbert Vance](wiki:Norbert_Vance) squired for Lord [Darry](wiki:House_Darry) with [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully).(wiki:Lord_of_Atranta.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

Lord Norbert is present at the [siege of Riverrun](wiki:Siege_of_Riverrun),(wiki:Lord_of_Atranta.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) and takes part in the war councils. Norbert offers to parley with the besieged Ser [Brynden Tully](wiki:Brynden_Tully), whom he squired with, but his offer is turned down.(wiki:Lord_of_Atranta.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
