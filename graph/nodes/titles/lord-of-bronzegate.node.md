## Origins

During [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest), Lords Buckler, [Errol](wiki:House_Errol), and [Fell](wiki:House_Fell) opposed [Orys Baratheon](wiki:Orys_Baratheon) during his march from the [Aegonfort](wiki:Aegonfort) toward [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), but the storm lords were forced to retreat when [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen) had [Meraxes](wiki:Meraxes) set afire with [dragonflame](wiki:Dragonfire) parts of what is now the southern [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood).(wiki:Lord_of_Bronzegate.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

At the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), [Lord Buckler](wiki:Lord_Buckler) was at court in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). He was imprisoned in the dungeons under the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) along with other lords suspected to support Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) over her half-brother, King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen). When brought in front of the king, Lord Buckler refused to support Aegon and was thus beheaded by the [King's Justice](wiki:King's_Justice).(wiki:Lord_of_Bronzegate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
