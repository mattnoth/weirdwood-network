## Origins

During the [Battle Beneath the Gods Eye](wiki:Battle_Beneath_the_Gods_Eye) in [43 AC](wiki:43_AC), Lord Caswell fought for King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen) against Prince [Aegon the Uncrowned](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Aenys_I)).(wiki:Lord_of_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) He may have been the first Caswell to held the title of "Lord of Bitterbridge", as the town was, up until the previous year, known as "Stonebridge".(wiki:Lord_of_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
At the start of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lord Caswell was at court in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). He was imprisoned in the dungeons under the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) along with other lords suspected to support Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) over her half-brother, King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen). When brought in front of the King, Lord Caswell refused to support Aegon and was beheaded by the [King's Justice](wiki:King's_Justice).(wiki:Lord_of_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
As Lord Caswell's son and successor was still a young child,(wiki:Lord_of_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) the Bastard of Bitterbridge, [Tom Flowers](wiki:Tom_Flowers), took command of the Caswell men in the field,(wiki:Lord_of_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) while Lord Caswell's widow, Lady Caswell, ruled Bitterbridge in the name of her young son.(wiki:Lord_of_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)
After learning the news of his [nephew](wiki:Maelor_Targaryen)'s death at Bitterbridge, the furious Prince [Daeron Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_Targaryen_(son_of_Viserys_I)) [sacked](wiki:Sack_of_Bitterbridge) and burned the town. In the aftermath of the sack, Prince Daeron stripped the young Lord Caswell of his lordship and sent him and his siblings to [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) as hostages of [House Hightower](wiki:House_Hightower). Daeron named the [dragonseed](wiki:Dragonseed), Ser [Ulf White](wiki:Ulf_White), as the new Lord of Bitterbridge for his service in the [First Battle of Tumbleton](wiki:First_Battle_of_Tumbleton).(wiki:Lord_of_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Lord Ulf died soon after, [poisoned](wiki:Poison) by Ser [Hobert Hightower](wiki:Hobert_Hightower).(wiki:Lord_of_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
In [196 AC](wiki:196_AC), Lord [Armond Caswell](wiki:Armond_Caswell) fought for [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) during the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion).(wiki:Lord_of_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Armond's son, Lord [Joffrey Caswell](wiki:Joffrey_Caswell), was present at the [Wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls) in [212 AC](wiki:212_AC).(wiki:Lord_of_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
Old [Lord Caswell](wiki:Lord_Caswell) was the Lord of Bitterbridge during [Rolly Duckfield](wiki:Rolly_Duckfield)'s childhood. He was succeeded by his son, [Lorent Caswell](wiki:Lorent_Caswell), who was wounded by Rolly before he fled in exile in [Essos](wiki:Essos).(wiki:Lord_of_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord Lorent Caswell supports King [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), and welcomes his army at Bitterbridge, where [a great melee](wiki:Melee_at_Bitterbridge) is held.(wiki:Lord_of_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
After Renly's [assassination at Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), Lorent locks himself inside his castle, refusing to implicate himself any more.(wiki:Lord_of_Bitterbridge.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
