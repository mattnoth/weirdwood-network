## Origins

Lord Meadows had the command of King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen)'s army at the start of the [Faith Militant uprising](wiki:Faith_Militant_uprising). He was killed in [battle](wiki:Battle_at_Stonebridge) by [Wat the Hewer](wiki:Wat_the_Hewer) in [42 AC](wiki:42_AC).(wiki:Lord_of_Grassfield_Keep.cite_ref-Rtsotd.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Grassfield_Keep.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord [Elwood Meadows](wiki:Elwood_Meadows) is Ser [Cortnay Penrose](wiki:Cortnay_Penrose)'s second-in-command at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End).(wiki:Lord_of_Grassfield_Keep.cite_ref-Racok42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) After the death of Ser Cortnay, Elwood names himself as the new [castellan](wiki:Castellan) and surrenders the castle to King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon).(wiki:Lord_of_Grassfield_Keep.cite_ref-Racok45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Elwood is confirmed as second-in-command by Stannis but the position of castellan of Storm's End is given to Ser [Gilbert Farring](wiki:Gilbert_Farring).(wiki:Lord_of_Grassfield_Keep.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Elwood remains loyal to Stannis as the seneschal of Storm's End.(wiki:Lord_of_Grassfield_Keep.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
