## Origins

Goodwives, fishermen, and fieldhands cheered loudly when King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) was crowned at the [Aegonfort](wiki:Aegonfort) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Goodwife.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
The first [fool](wiki:Fool) to serve at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) was the [Goodwife](wiki:Goodwife_(fool)), a fat man who dressed as a woman.(wiki:Goodwife.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Amabel](wiki:Amabel) and [Harra](wiki:Harra) are fierce old goodwives who supervise servants at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Goodwife.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) After the [fall of Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal), Goodwife Harra is executed by [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) for having told Lady [Shella Whent](wiki:Shella_Whent)'s household to serve [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Goodwife.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Maerie](wiki:Maerie_(goodwife)) and [Nolla](wiki:Nolla) are goodwives in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) who are killed by Lannisters during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Goodwife.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

During her search for [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark), [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) speaks with a goodwife at [Rosby](wiki:Rosby).(wiki:Goodwife.cite_ref-Raffc4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Outside of [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale), Brienne addresses a woman on a turnip cart as goodwife.(wiki:Goodwife.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
