## Origins

[House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) ruled as [Kings of Mountain and Vale](wiki:Kings_of_Mountain_and_Vale) after the [Battle of the Seven Stars](wiki:Battle_of_the_Seven_Stars), which was part of the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros).(wiki:Defender_of_the_Vale.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) The Arryns submitted to [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) during [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest),(wiki:Defender_of_the_Vale.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) however, and have since served as Defenders of the Vale and [Wardens of the East](wiki:Wardens_of_the_East).(wiki:Defender_of_the_Vale.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
