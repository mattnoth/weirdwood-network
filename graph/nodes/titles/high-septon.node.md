## Identity

High Septon is a title from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges

- REGION_OF: Seven Kingdoms (track_b: Region)

## Origins

The first High Septon was Septon [Robeson](wiki:Robeson), who had served as the [regent](wiki:Regent) for and helped raise Lord [Triston Hightower](wiki:Triston_Hightower). After Robeson's death, Triston raised the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) in his honor.(wiki:High_Septon.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_oldtown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

The [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(fat_one)), known as the fat one, is a grossly fat man who has been High Septon for many years. He is regarded by many lords and smallfolk as corrupt.(wiki:High_Septon.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Clash of Kings

The fat High Septon is killed in the [riot of King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:High_Septon.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) His crystal crown is destroyed by thieves.

### A Storm of Swords

The [new High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Tyrion's)), who was selected by [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister),(wiki:High_Septon.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) weds King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) to [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) and wears a grander crystal crown gifted by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister).(wiki:High_Septon.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Tyrion's High Septon is regarded as a weak willed but good man. He prayed with Ser [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister) when he was injured and near death. Lancel confesses his sins to him, such as bedding Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) and giving King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) a more potent wine than he was used to which resulted in his death. The High Septon said he must forgive her though. Cersei, paranoid about what Lancel may have told him, orders the High Septon's death. [Osney Kettleblack](wiki:Osney_Kettleblack) sneaks into the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) and suffocates the man with a pillow while he sleeps.(wiki:High_Septon.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

The next High Septon is imposed on the Most Devout by the "[sparrows](wiki:Sparrows)" who turn up as refugees from the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). He's dubbed the [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow)(wiki:High_Septon.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1)(wiki:High_Septon.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) The [Faith Militant](wiki:Faith_Militant) is reinstated during his term when Queen Cersei wants the crown's debt to the Faith settled.

A devout and holy man with an iron will, the High Sparrow has much of the Faith's riches, such as crowns and rings, sold to raise funds for the poor.(wiki:High_Septon.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) He has Cersei and Margaery arrested due to accusations of infidelity and adultery.(wiki:High_Septon.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:High_Septon.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
