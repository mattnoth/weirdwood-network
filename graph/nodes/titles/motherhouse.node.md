## Origins

[Humfrey I Teague](wiki:Humfrey_I_Teague), [King of the Rivers and the Hills](wiki:King_of_the_Rivers_and_the_Hills), founded [septs](wiki:Sept) and motherhouses in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) in his efforts to stamp out worship of the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods).(wiki:Motherhouse.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_riverlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) [Merle I Gardener](wiki:Merle_I_Gardener), [King of the Reach](wiki:King_of_the_Reach), encouraged construction of [septries](wiki:Septry), septs and motherhouses in the [Reach](wiki:Reach).(wiki:Motherhouse.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Princess [Rhaella Targaryen](wiki:Rhaella_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aegon)) was a novice at the motherhouse attached the [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). Septa [Karolyn](wiki:Karolyn) prevented Ser [Orryn Baratheon](wiki:Orryn_Baratheon) from absconding with the girl.(wiki:Motherhouse.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
Queen [Elinor Costayne](wiki:Elinor_Costayne) gave up all claims to nobility later in life, becoming Mother Elinor at the great motherhouse in [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport).(wiki:Motherhouse.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
In [54 AC](wiki:54_AC) Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) was hosted by [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters) at their motherhouse in Oldtown for a day of prayer and contemplation.(wiki:Motherhouse.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
[Maegelle Targaryen](wiki:Maegelle_Targaryen) was a pious princess who became a [septa](wiki:Septa). Her sister [Saera](wiki:Saera_Targaryen), however, ran away from the Oldtown motherhouse in [85 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest) while still a novice.(wiki:Motherhouse.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) used [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar) to destroy the motherhouse at [Bechester](wiki:Bechester).(wiki:Motherhouse.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Lady [Jeyne Arryn](wiki:Jeyne_Arryn) died at the [Motherhouse of Maris](wiki:Motherhouse_of_Maris) in [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown) in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC).(wiki:Motherhouse.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
The journey of King [Baelor I Targaryen](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen) to [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) is remembered in songs popularized by septries and motherhouses.(wiki:Motherhouse.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_baelor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

The great-aunt of Lady [Ravella Smallwood](wiki:Ravella_Swann) is a septa at a motherhouse in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown). Ravella sends her daughter, [Carellen Smallwood](wiki:Carellen_Smallwood), to the city for safety during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Motherhouse.cite_ref-Rasos22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
