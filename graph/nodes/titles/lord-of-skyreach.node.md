## Origins

The Lords of Skyreach have long fought against the [marcher lords](wiki:Marcher_lords) of the [Dornish Marches](wiki:Dornish_Marches) to the north.(wiki:Lord_of_Skyreach.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_blackmont.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Lord_of_Skyreach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_queer_customs_of_the_south.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) This includes King [Ferris Fowler](wiki:Ferris_Fowler) who led ten thousand [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) through the Wide Way to the [Kingdom of the Reach](wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Reach). However, King [Garth VII Gardener](wiki:Garth_VII_Gardener) turned them back.(wiki:Lord_of_Skyreach.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_reach:_the_gardener_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
When House Fowler bent the knee to [House Martell](wiki:House_Martell) during [Nymeria's War](wiki:Nymeria's_War), they gave up their royal title and their lordship over the [Wide Way](wiki:Wide_Way), but remained Lords of Skyreach and were granted the title of [Warden of the Prince's Pass](wiki:Warden_of_the_Prince's_Pass). The last Fowler King, [Garrison Fowler](wiki:Garrison_Fowler), was exiled to the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) and the other members of House Fowler sided with the Martells against the [Yronwoods](wiki:House_Yronwood) for the rest of the war.(wiki:Lord_of_Skyreach.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)(wiki:Lord_of_Skyreach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_the_coming_of_the_rhoynar.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
In [10 AC](wiki:10_AC), during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), Lord Fowler, leading a Dornish host, burned [Nightsong](wiki:Nightsong) and took hostages from the [marcher](wiki:Marcher_lords) castle. The [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen) retaliated by unleashing their [dragons](wiki:Dragon) on Skyreach and other Dornish castles during the [Dragon's Wroth](wiki:Dragon's_Wroth).(wiki:Lord_of_Skyreach.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_dorne:_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Lord Fowler was murdered for the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne)'s ransom on his head.(wiki:Lord_of_Skyreach.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
