## Origins

In the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) the [priests](wiki:Priest_of_the_Drowned_God) of the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God) act as prophets. According to Maester [Yandel](wiki:Yandel), the greatest of these was [Galon Whitestaff](wiki:Galon_Whitestaff).(wiki:Prophet.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
In the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), during the [Moon of the Three Kings](wiki:Moon_of_the_Three_Kings), mad prophets ruled the streets of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) along with [mummer](wiki:Mummer) kings and [gutter knights](wiki:Gutter_knights).(wiki:Prophet.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) One of these was known only as the [Shepherd](wiki:Shepherd).(wiki:Prophet.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
After nearly drowning in a storm and washing up ashore, safely and unharmed, Aeron Greyjoy underwent a spiritual reawakening, and dedicated himself as a priest of the Drowned God and prophet.(wiki:Prophet.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The appearance of the [red comet](wiki:Red_comet) leads to the rise of a number of priests, preachers and prophets foretelling doom and destruction in the winesinks and pot-shops of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Prophet.cite_ref-Racok8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
A [begging brother](wiki:Begging_brothers) of the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) and prophet indirectly denounces the [incest](wiki:Incest) between [Cersei](wiki:Cersei) and [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and accuses King [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey) of being a puppet of [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), whom the preacher regards as a twisted monkey [demon](wiki:Demon). He also spreads the rumor of [Shireen Baratheon](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon) being a monster product of [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) coupling with the fool [Patchface](wiki:Patchface).(wiki:Prophet.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Acting under orders of the Queen Regent, [Vylarr](wiki:Vylarr) leads the [red cloaks](wiki:Red_cloaks) in the arrest of half a dozen of prophets, whom are dragged to the dungeons of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Prophet.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
