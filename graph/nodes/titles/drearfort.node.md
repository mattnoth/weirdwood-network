## Appearances & Description

The old flint tower is located on the smallest of the [Fingers](wiki:Fingers).(wiki:Drearfort.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) It has arrowslits for defense but no windows.(wiki:Drearfort.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
An open stone stair winds around the inside wall from the undercroft to the roof, and each of its floors has only one room. The undercroft is likely used for storage. The ground level houses servants—including [Umfred](wiki:Umfred), [Grisel](wiki:Grisel), [Bryen](wiki:Bryen), and [Kella](wiki:Kella)—and dogs in the kitchen. The middle floor is a modest hall, and a broken longsword and an oaken shield depicting the [titan's head](wiki:Titan_of_Braavos) of [House Baelish](wiki:House_Baelish) hang above its hearth. There is a little alcove in the hall beneath the stairs. The third floor contains the lord's bedchamber.(wiki:Drearfort.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
The vicinity includes a flock of [sheep](wiki:Sheep), a village of a dozen families near a peat bog, and a hermit's cave. There is also one place where the tide comes jetting up out of a blowhole to shoot thirty feet into the air, and another where someone had chiseled the seven-pointed star of the [new gods](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) upon a boulder. According to [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), the star marks a landing place during the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals) to the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn). It takes only half a day to traverse the territory, and there is no safe anchorage nearby for large ships.(wiki:Drearfort.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) brings [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) to the [Fingers](wiki:Fingers) in the aftermath of King [Joffrey I Baratheon's](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) death at [his royal wedding](wiki:Purple_Wedding). Sansa befriends [Bryen](wiki:Bryen)'s [old blind dog](wiki:Old_blind_dog).(wiki:Drearfort.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)
Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) marries Petyr shortly after arriving at the tower, and [they feast outside](wiki:Wedding_of_Petyr_Baelish_and_Lysa_Arryn). When [Marillion](wiki:Marillion) tries to seduce Sansa within the hall at night, she is protected by Ser [Lothor Brune](wiki:Lothor_Brune).(wiki:Drearfort.cite_ref-Rasos68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-5)
