## Origins

[Ronnel Arryn](wiki:Ronnel_Arryn_(king)) was the last [King of Mountain and Vale](wiki:King_of_Mountain_and_Vale), reigning until [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest). Since King Ronnel was only a boy, rule of the [Vale of Arryn](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn) fell to his mother, [Sharra Arryn](wiki:Sharra_Arryn). As other regions of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) fell to Aegon, the [Queen Regent](wiki:Regent) amassed the Vale's army at the [Bloody Gate](wiki:Bloody_Gate). However, King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) sent his sister-wife, Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen), to the Eyrie directly, riding her [dragon](wiki:Dragon), [Vhagar](wiki:Vhagar). When Sharra returned to the Eyrie, she found her son sitting on Visenya's lap, asking if he could ride the dragon with Visenya. Sharra quickly bent the knee, submitting to Targaryen rule. The Arryns were declared Wardens of the East and Defenders of the Vale, and Lord Ronnel received his ride on Vhagar.(wiki:Warden_of_the_East.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Warden_of_the_East.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Warden_of_the_East.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

After the death of Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) names Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) to the office of Warden of the East. Overtly this is because Jon's son, Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn), is still a child, and Robert is concerned about the threat of [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_Targaryen)'s alliance with [Drogo](wiki:Drogo), a *[khal](wiki:Khal)* of the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki), but it is also a sign of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister)'s influence on the king.(wiki:Warden_of_the_East.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

The appointment of Jaime angers the widowed Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) and the lords of the [Vale](wiki:Vale_of_Arryn), who begin calling Robert Arryn the "True Warden of the East".(wiki:Warden_of_the_East.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Clash of Kings

Lady Lysa and the Vale have remained neutral in the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) for [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_I_Baratheon), considers restoring the title of Warden of the East to Robert Arryn to make peace with Lysa, and to obtain the allegiance of the Vale lords.(wiki:Warden_of_the_East.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords

[Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish)'s new status as the [Lord of Harrenhal](wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal) gives him the necessary standing to be considered by Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), Hand of the King, for a marriage to Lysa Arryn, with the intent of delivering the Vale of Arryn to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) without violence. Petyr is sent to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) to woo Lysa, with the promise that if she takes him as a husband and returns to the king's peace, Tywin will restore the title of Warden of the East to Robert Arryn.(wiki:Warden_of_the_East.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

The title is restored to Robert Arryn.(wiki:Warden_of_the_East.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
