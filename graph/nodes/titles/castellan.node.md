## Origins

Some of the [Tyrells](wiki:House_Tyrell) who served [House Gardener](wiki:House_Gardener) as [High Steward](wiki:High_Steward) were appointed castellan in times of war.(wiki:Castellan.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_house_tyrell.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
When King [Morden II Durrandon](wiki:Morden_II_Durrandon) named his [baseborn](wiki:Bastard) half brother [Ronard Storm](wiki:Ronard_Storm) as his castellan, Ronard came to rule the [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands) in all but name. He wed Morden's sister, and claimed the kingship within five years of his appointment. His rule lasted nigh unto thirty years.(wiki:Castellan.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_durrandon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
Following their initial conquest during the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), the [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen) left Lord [Jon Rosby](wiki:Jon_Rosby) as the castellan of [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear). He was killed by [Meria Martell](wiki:Meria_Martell), the ancient [Princess of Dorne](wiki:Princess_of_Dorne), who threw him out of a window atop the [Spear Tower](wiki:Spear_Tower).(wiki:Castellan.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_dorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
When Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) left [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) for [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Ser [Alfred Broome](wiki:Alfred_Broome), the most senior of Rhaenyra's knights at Dragonstone, expected to be named as castellan. After Rhaenyra passed him over in favor of Ser [Robert Quince](wiki:Robert_Quince), Ser Alfred participated in the coup against Robert when King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen)'s supporters sought support on [the island](wiki:Dragonstone_(island)).(wiki:Castellan.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s [bastard](wiki:Bastard) son [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm) is raised at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End), the seat of Robert's younger brother [Renly](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), who subsequently has the boy fostered by his castellans.(wiki:Castellan.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)(wiki:Castellan.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)(wiki:Castellan.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) is appointed castellan of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) by Lady [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) after she decides to join her son, [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), at [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin).(wiki:Castellan.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

When Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) suggests spreading the rumor that Lady [Selyse Florent](wiki:Selyse_Florent) had an affair from which the daughter of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), [Shireen](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon), [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) offers Dragonstone's castellan, Ser [Axell Florent](wiki:Axell_Florent), as the rumored father.(wiki:Castellan.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)
When [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) attempts to smuggle her son [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) from [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to keep him from harm during the upcoming [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), she sends him disguised to [Rosby](wiki:Rosby). [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) orders his men to intercept Tommen's escort and take the boy, continuing as planned to [Rosby](wiki:Rosby), where he judges Lord [Gyles Rosby](wiki:Gyles_Rosby) will order his castellan to open the gates.(wiki:Castellan.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

When [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh), castellan of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black),(wiki:Castellan.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) leaves to battle the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk), he leaves the old Ser [Wynton Stout](wiki:Wynton_Stout) as Castle Black's new castellan.(wiki:Castellan.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

After [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) refuses to name her uncle, Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), as King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon)'s [regent](wiki:Regent) and [Hand](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), he requests to be named castellan of [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Castellan.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) In her anger, Cersei names her cousin, [Damion Lannister](wiki:Damion_Lannister), as her castellan for Casterly Rock to spite him.(wiki:Castellan.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) is surprised that the letter of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) to [Bear Island](wiki:Bear_Island) is answered by the young [Lyanna Mormont](wiki:Lyanna_Mormont), as he believes Lady [Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont) would have left at least one of her older daughters behind as castellan when she left home to answer [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s call to arms.(wiki:Castellan.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
