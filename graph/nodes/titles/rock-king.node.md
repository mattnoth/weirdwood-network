## Identity

The Rock King was one of two traditional petty-kingship titles of the Iron Islands, paired with the Salt King. According to the oldest surviving records at the Citadel of Oldtown, each of the Iron Islands was ruled by two kings: a rock king who governed the island on land, and a salt king who commanded at sea. Rock kings and salt kings alike were chosen through kingsmoot.

The rock kingship was held by notable figures in Iron Islands history. Regnar Drumm, rock king of Old Wyk, was chosen as the second High King of the Iron Islands after the prophet Galon Whitestaff compelled the ironborn to depose Erich I Greyiron, who had seized the crown of his father without a kingsmoot. House Drumm historically held the rock kingship of Old Wyk. The entire institution of rock and salt kings was abolished when Urron Greyiron slaughtered the assembled thirteen salt and rock kings at Nagga's Hill and established hereditary rule over the Iron Islands.
