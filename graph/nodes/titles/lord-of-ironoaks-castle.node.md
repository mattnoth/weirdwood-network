## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Lady [Anya Waynwood](wiki:Anya_Waynwood) is present in the [High Hall](wiki:Eyrie) of the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), surrounded by her sons, when [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) demands a [trial by battle](wiki:Trial_by_battle) to prove his innocence of the claims against him.(wiki:Lord_of_Ironoaks_Castle.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Anya is one of the [Lords Declarant](wiki:Lords_Declarant) who swear to drive Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) out of the [Vale](wiki:Vale) and to protect their young lord, [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn). They gather six thousand men to the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon), demanding that Petyr give up his position of [Lord Protector](wiki:Lord_Protector_of_the_Vale) and leave the Vale.(wiki:Lord_of_Ironoaks_Castle.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

Anya is present at the [wedding](wiki:Marriage) of Lord [Lyonel Corbray](wiki:Lyonel_Corbray) in [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown). After buying out her debts, Anya is persuaded by Lord Baelish to give permission for Alayne Stone (in reality [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark)) to wed her [ward](wiki:Ward), [Harrold Hardyng](wiki:Harrold_Hardyng), if Harry agrees.(wiki:Lord_of_Ironoaks_Castle.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### The Winds of Winter

Lady Anya attends the [tourney](wiki:Tourney) for the [Brotherhood of Winged Knights](wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights) at the [Gates of the Moon](wiki:Gates_of_the_Moon).(wiki:Lord_of_Ironoaks_Castle.cite_ref-Winds_4-1)
