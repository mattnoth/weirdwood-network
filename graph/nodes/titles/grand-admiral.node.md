## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Queen [Regent](wiki:Regent) [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) renames the office of [master of ships](wiki:Master_of_ships) as "grand admiral". She removes Lord [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne) from the council and names [Aurane Waters](wiki:Aurane_Waters) to the seat.(wiki:Grand_admiral.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) After Cersei's arrest by the [Faith](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), Aurane flees the capital with the [new dromonds](wiki:Cersei's_dromonds) she had commissioned.(wiki:Grand_admiral.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Paxter Redwyne has been restored to the post of master of ships by Ser [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), the [Lord Regent](wiki:Lord_Regent), and also granted the titles of both [lord admiral](wiki:Lord_admiral) and grand admiral.(wiki:Grand_admiral.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Grand_admiral.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
