## Narrative Arc

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

[Valena Toland](wiki:Valena_Toland) tells [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) that the Lord of the Waters has installed himself on [Torturer's Deep](wiki:Torturer's_Deep). He has "monstrous large" three-decker warships.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Waters.cite_ref-1)
Presumably he is [Aurane Waters](wiki:Aurane_Waters), former [grand admiral](wiki:Grand_admiral) to Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), who ordered the construction of [a fleet of dromonds](wiki:Cersei's_dromonds) and crewed them with criminals,(wiki:Lord_of_the_Waters.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) then fled [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with the warships after Cersei's arrest.(wiki:Lord_of_the_Waters.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The title may be a play on Aurane's [bastard](wiki:Bastard) surname of Waters and one of the titles of his father's house, [House Velaryon](wiki:House_Velaryon), [Lord of the Tides](wiki:Lord_of_the_Tides).
