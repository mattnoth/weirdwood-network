## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Ser [Helman Tallhart](wiki:Helman_Tallhart), the Master of [Torrhen's Square](wiki:Torrhen's_Square), goes with his men to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) when [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) calls the [northern](wiki:North) [banners](wiki:Bannerman) in response to Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s arrest.(wiki:Master_of_Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Helman leaves his brother [Leobald](wiki:Leobald_Tallhart) as [castellan](wiki:Castellan).(wiki:Master_of_Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Robb names Helman as the commander of the garrison of four hundred archers and swordsmen left at the [Twins](wiki:Twins) to ensure the loyalty of [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey).(wiki:Master_of_Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Ragot59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Clash of Kings

Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) orders Helman to leave the Twins and join with Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) to aid in the [fall of Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal).(wiki:Master_of_Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) Helman retakes [Castle Darry](wiki:Darry) after a [small siege](wiki:Siege_of_Darry) with the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) garrison surrendering after a short period. Roose orders him to torch the castle, kill the captives, and join forces with [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover) to attack [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale).(wiki:Master_of_Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) aids Leobald in fending off the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) of [Dagmer Cleftjaw](wiki:Dagmer_Cleftjaw) in at [fight at Torrhen's Square](wiki:Fight_at_Torrhen's_Square),(wiki:Master_of_Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) but Rodrik and Leobald are later killed in the [battle at Winterfell](wiki:Battle_at_Winterfell).(wiki:Master_of_Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Racok66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Helman is killed during the [battle at Duskendale](wiki:Battle_at_Duskendale).(wiki:Master_of_Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Having been predeceased by his son, [Benfred](wiki:Benfred_Tallhart), Helman's heir is his daughter, [Eddara](wiki:Eddara_Tallhart).(wiki:Master_of_Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

### A Feast for Crows

Eddara is held captive by the [ironmen](wiki:Ironmen) at Torrhen's Square, following its [capture](wiki:Sack_of_Torrhen’s_Square) by Dagmer.(wiki:Master_of_Torrhen's_Square.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
