## Origins

After the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria), during the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood), the Volantenes considered themselves the heirs to the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) and rightful rulers of the world.(wiki:Elephants_(Volantis).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_the_doom_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Thus the militaristic tigers, supported by the [Old Blood](wiki:Old_Blood), held sway for almost a century, and led Volantis into conflict with the other Free Cities. This conflict would eventually involve all of the Free Cities except for (as far as is known) [Lorath](wiki:Lorath). At first, Volantis succeeded in their conquest. But an alliance of [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) and [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) resisted Volantis, and [Myr](wiki:Myr) and [Lys](wiki:Lys) rebelled. Volantis was further defeated by an alliance of [Qohor](wiki:Qohor) and [Norvos](wiki:Norvos), by [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki), by [Storm King](wiki:Storm_King) [Argilac Durrandon](wiki:Argilac_Durrandon), and by Lord [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone). In the end, the elephants, the faction favoring peace took power from the tigers, and the fighting was at an end.(wiki:Elephants_(Volantis).cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_the_doom_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)(wiki:Elephants_(Volantis).cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Their followers went on a rampage, knocking the heads from the statues of those they blamed for all the wars and deaths.(wiki:Elephants_(Volantis).cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
For the last three centuries, since taking power from the tigers, at least two of the three triarchs in power have been elephants.(wiki:Elephants_(Volantis).cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) no more than one tiger has been named triarch at a time during this period.(wiki:Elephants_(Volantis).cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Some of the first elephants were women, one of whom, [Trianna](wiki:Trianna), was returned four times. Volantis hasn't had a female triarch for three hundred years.(wiki:Elephants_(Volantis).cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance of Dragons[ ]

[Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) of [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) has paid Triarch [Nyessos Vhassar](wiki:Nyessos_Vhassar), of the elephants, enough to own him eight times over.(wiki:Elephants_(Volantis).cite_ref-Radwd18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
The [Kingfisher](wiki:Kingfisher) informs the crew of the [Shy Maid](wiki:Shy_Maid) that [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) will go to war for "Nyessos and [Malaquo](wiki:Malaquo_Maegyr) go hand in hand, and the elephants show stripes".(wiki:Elephants_(Volantis).cite_ref-Radwd18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
[Doniphos Paenymion](wiki:Doniphos_Paenymion), like the other two triarchs, is up for re-election and many believe he is unlikely to be re-elected, for he is one of the few who does not wish war between [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) and [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), Queen of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). According to [Qavo Nogarys](wiki:Qavo_Nogarys), Doniphos will not be returned as triarch as the city of Volantis thirsts for war. Nogarys also reports that Nyessos has also been bought by the [Wise Masters](wiki:Wise_Masters).(wiki:Elephants_(Volantis).cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
The [widow of the waterfront](wiki:Widow_of_the_waterfront) tells [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) that even some elephants support going to war against [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). For example, Nyessos Vhassar owes much of his wealth to the slave trade.(wiki:Elephants_(Volantis).cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
