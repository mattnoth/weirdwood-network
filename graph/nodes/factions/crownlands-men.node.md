## Culture

[King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), the bustling capital of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), is located in the [crownlands](wiki:Crownlands), and the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) is found in the [throne room](wiki:Great_Hall_(Red_Keep)) of the city's [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep).(wiki:Crownlands_men.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) is another prominent town on the shores of [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay).(wiki:Crownlands_men.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) "Waters" is a common [bastard](wiki:Bastard) name around the bay.(wiki:Crownlands_men.cite_ref-Raffc8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
The [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) is the predominant religion of the province, with the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon) seated at the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) in King's Landing.(wiki:Crownlands_men.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
[Crackclaw Point](wiki:Crackclaw_Point) is a rugged peninsula in the northern crownlands. Descending from the [First Men](wiki:First_Men),(wiki:Crownlands_men.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) the [Crackclaws](wiki:Crackclaws) are considered half wild by some.(wiki:Crownlands_men.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
The [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood) is divided between the southern crownlands and the northern [stormlands](wiki:Stormlands). [Outlaws](wiki:Outlaw) have been known to hide in the forested land.(wiki:Crownlands_men.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
