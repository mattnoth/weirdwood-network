## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Benfred Tallhart](wiki:Benfred_Tallhart) gathers companions after he hears of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark)'s successes in the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings). They consider themselves young [wolves](wiki:House_Stark), as the oldest is not nineteen. After [Leobald Tallhart](wiki:Leobald_Tallhart) mocks them by calling them young [rabbits](wiki:Rabbit), they take the name of Wild Hares.(wiki:Wild_Hares.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
The Wild Hares scour the country, rabbit skins attached to their spears, singing chivalric songs, until Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel) orders Leobald to sort them out.(wiki:Wild_Hares.cite_ref-Racok16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Benfred and his company ride out to the [Stony Shore](wiki:Stony_Shore) to see if the rumors of [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) raiding are true. They do not expect that the enemy will have many men and did not send scouts ahead. [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) and his ironmen easily [surprise and defeat](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore) the Wild Hares.(wiki:Wild_Hares.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
