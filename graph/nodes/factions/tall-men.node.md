## Origins

The Tall Men traced their descent to [Huzhor Amai](wiki:Huzhor_Amai), the mythic king descended from the last of the equally legendary [Fisher Queens](wiki:Fisher_Queens) and who took as wives the daughters of the greatest lords and kings of the [Cymmeri](wiki:Cymmeri), [Zoqora](wiki:Zoqora), and [Gipps](wiki:Gipps). In fact, it was the Tall Men who conquered these three races and eventually assimilated them.(wiki:Tall_Men.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
Proud and quarrelsome, the Tall Men seldom unified under a single ruler but nevertheless established [a civilization](wiki:Kingdom_of_Sarnor) that was one of the greatest in the [known world](wiki:Known_world) for more than two thousand years. They warred against the [Qaathi](wiki:Qaathi) and allied with the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) during the [Second](wiki:Second_Ghiscari_War) and [Third Ghiscari Wars](wiki:Third_Ghiscari_War). During the [Fourth Ghiscari War](wiki:Fourth_Ghiscari_War), rival Sarnori kings supported either the Freehold or the [Old Empire of Ghis](wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis).(wiki:Tall_Men.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) [Lomas Longstrider](wiki:Lomas_Longstrider) observed a fallen obelisk upon which were carved depictions of the [Ghiscari](wiki:Ghiscari)'s allies, with the tallest of them being helmeted Tall Men.(wiki:Tall_Men.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
The Sarnori saw the twilight of their power during the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood). Ignoring the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki), they fell prey to their depredations. When the Tall Men finally unified it was too late, as the Dothraki [khalasars](wiki:Khalasar) burned their cities to the ground. Today only in [Saath](wiki:Saath), the sole Sarnori city that is not a ruin, do men still call themselves Tagaez Fen and worship their gods. There are less than twenty thousand of them, when once they numbered in the millions.(wiki:Tall_Men.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
