## Identity

The Caltrops were a conspiracy of thirteen nobles during the Dance of the Dragons (130 AC) who plotted to eliminate the Two Betrayers — Hugh Hammer and Ulf White — dragonriders who had switched sides from the blacks to the greens at the First Battle of Tumbleton. (wiki:Caltrops)

## Narrative Arc

After the First Battle of Tumbleton, Hugh Hammer and Ulf White (called the Two Betrayers) became unruly and ambitious. Hugh sought to claim the throne himself. Thirteen green nobles formed a secret conspiracy called the Caltrops to eliminate the threat posed by the dragonriders. The conspirators succeeded in their aims: Hugh was killed by Ser Jon Roxton (using the Valyrian steel sword Orphan-Maker), and Ulf was poisoned by Ser Hobert Hightower. However, the resulting chaos during the Second Battle of Tumbleton proved devastating for both sides. (wiki:Caltrops)

## Allegiances

The Caltrops were aligned with the greens during the Dance of the Dragons, though their conspiracy was an internal faction action rather than a sanctioned military operation. (wiki:Caltrops)
