## Identity

The Two Betrayers were Hugh Hammer (rider of Vermithor) and Ulf the White, also called Ulf son of Umar (rider of Silverwing). They were dragonseeds — common-born bastards of Targaryen descent — given dragons to ride during the Dance of the Dragons to bolster Rhaenyra Targaryen's forces. Both turned their cloaks and defected to the Greens (Aegon II's faction) during the Battle of the Gullet's aftermath, betraying the Blacks. Hugh Hammer declared himself king. Both were eventually killed: Ulf was poisoned at the Honeywine (the Second Tumbleton), and Hugh was slain by Ser Tylly Turnberry.
