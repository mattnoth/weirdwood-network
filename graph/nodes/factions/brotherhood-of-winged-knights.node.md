## Identity

The Brotherhood of Winged Knights is a knightly order founded by House Arryn in 300 AC to serve as a personal guard for the Lord of the Eyrie (wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights).

## Origins

The idea was conceived by Alayne Stone (Sansa Stark), who noticed that young Lord Robert Arryn was comforted by tales of his ancestor Ser Artys Arryn, the Winged Knight. She suggested surrounding him with his own bodyguard of Winged Knights. Lord Petyr Baelish, regent for Robert, approved and set plans in motion (wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights.cite_ref-Winds).

## Appearances & Description

The Brotherhood contains eight members, increased from Alayne's original suggestion of seven so Robert would have more guards than King Tommen. Members serve for three years. Each competitor in the founding tourney received a cloak of cloth-of-silver and a lapis brooch shaped as falcon's wings; the eight victors receive warhelms decorated with falcon's wings (wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights.cite_ref-Winds).

## Narrative Arc

Founded at a tourney at the Gates of the Moon where sixty-four knights from across the Vale competed for the eight positions. Because Lady Lysa Arryn had kept the Vale out of the War of the Five Kings, many young unblooded knights were eager to prove themselves, drawing many participants (wiki:Brotherhood_of_Winged_Knights.cite_ref-Winds).
