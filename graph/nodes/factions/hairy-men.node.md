## Origins

There are tales of Hairy Men, a race of savage and shaggy warriors who rode to battle on [unicorns](wiki:Unicorns), established on the central grasslands of [Essos](wiki:Essos) stretching between the [Forest of Qohor](wiki:Forest_of_Qohor) to the [Bone Mountains](wiki:Bone_Mountains). These people were larger in size than the [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibbenese), who may descend from them. [Huzhor Amai](wiki:Huzhor_Amai), the mythic hero king and ancestor of the [Sarnori](wiki:Sarnori), wore about his shoulders a great cloak made from the pelt of a king of the Hairy Men.(wiki:Hairy_Men.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_beyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
According to legend, the original inhabitants of what would become [Norvos](wiki:Norvos) were driven away by an onslaught of hairy men from the east.(wiki:Hairy_Men.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_norvos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Hairy men are remembered in some [Pentoshi](wiki:Pentos) histories as having been settled in western [Essos](wiki:Essos), in what would become [Andalos](wiki:Andalos), until they were driven out by the [Andals](wiki:Andals).(wiki:Hairy_Men.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_the_arrival_of_the_andals.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) The Pentoshi and most [maesters](wiki:Maesters) of the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) believe the Ibbenese are akin to the hairy men, though it is unknown if the hairy men settled [Ib](wiki:Ib) from Essos or if the hairy men originated on the island and then spread to the mainland.(wiki:Hairy_Men.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_the_arrival_of_the_andals.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3)
Hairy men akin to the Ibbenese, small and dark, also settled on [Lorath](wiki:Lorath) but shunned the mazes of the [mazemakers](wiki:Mazemakers). They, in turn, were displaced by Andals, who invaded the Lorathi islands and slaughtered them in the name of [the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), taking their women and children as slaves.(wiki:Hairy_Men.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_free_cities:_lorath.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
