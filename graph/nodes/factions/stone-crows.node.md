## Origins

In [97 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Stone Crows killed [the father](wiki:Lord_Arryn_(father_of_Jeyne)) and brothers of [Jeyne Arryn](wiki:Jeyne_Arryn), leaving her to become [Lady of the Eyrie](wiki:Lord_of_the_Eyrie).(wiki:Stone_Crows.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

A group of Stone Crows led by [Gunthor son of Gurn](wiki:Gunthor) encounter [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) in the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon) after they leave the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie). They threaten to kill Tyrion and Bronn, but Tyrion manages to win them over by promising them weapons, armor, and glory, in return for their help.(wiki:Stone_Crows.cite_ref-Ragot42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Stone Crows led by [Conn](wiki:Conn) and [Shagga](wiki:Shagga) are among the near three hundred clansmen who accompany Tyrion as he leaves the mountains, while Gunthor stays behind to raise the other clans.(wiki:Stone_Crows.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) A [Moon Brother](wiki:Moon_Brother) stabs a Stone Crow over a sausage, so three Stone Crows slit the Moon Brother's throat in the night. [Ulf](wiki:Ulf) demands blood money in return, but Conn and Shagga refuse to pay.(wiki:Stone_Crows.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Conn invites Tyrion to eat oxmeat with the Stone Crows by their cookfire.(wiki:Stone_Crows.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
Stone Crows fight in the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork) under Tyrion, with Conn being slain in the fighting.(wiki:Stone_Crows.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The group continues with Tyrion to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), and Tyrion instructs that they be garrisoned separately from the Moon Brothers in the barracks beneath the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand).(wiki:Stone_Crows.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) They guard the tower(wiki:Stone_Crows.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and eat in the [Small Hall](wiki:Small_Hall).(wiki:Stone_Crows.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Shagga aids Tyrion in intimidating Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle).(wiki:Stone_Crows.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)
Before the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Tyrion send Stone Crows into the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood) to harass and harry [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s army as it approaches the city.(wiki:Stone_Crows.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) They are so effective at killing Stannis's scouts and outriders(wiki:Stone_Crows.cite_ref-Racok52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) that he remains blind to the [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) and [Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) force that comes upon him from the kingswood.(wiki:Stone_Crows.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After the Blackwater, Shagga and his clansmen remain in the kingswood.(wiki:Stone_Crows.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
In the foothills of the Vale's mountains, a village elder tells [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) that Gunthor led Stone Crows in raiding a village half a year ago, killing half the men, kidnapping every woman, and stealing all the grain.(wiki:Stone_Crows.cite_ref-Rasos65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
