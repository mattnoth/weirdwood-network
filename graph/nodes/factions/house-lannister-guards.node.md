## Appearances & Description

Along with their red cloaks, some Lannister guards wear mail shirts over boiled leather and steel caps with [lion](wiki:Lion) crests.(wiki:House_Lannister_guards.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:House_Lannister_guards.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

## Origins

Ser [Ilyn Payne](wiki:Ilyn_Payne) was the captain of the guard for Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) when he served as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen).(wiki:House_Lannister_guards.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
In the aftermath of the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing), the bodies of [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Rhaegar)) and [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)) were wrapped in the crimson cloaks of Lannister guards, with the red helping to mask their blood.(wiki:House_Lannister_guards.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
After having discovered the relationship between [Tysha](wiki:Tysha) and his son [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), Tywin gave Tysha to a barracks of Lannister guards to be gang raped.(wiki:House_Lannister_guards.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

While chasing a [black cat](wiki:Balerion_(cat)) in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) is met by a [septa](wiki:Septa). She sends [Godwyn](wiki:Godwyn) and another red cloak to catch Arya, but the girl escapes into the cellars of the castle.(wiki:House_Lannister_guards.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)
A squad of Lannister guards led by [Tregar](wiki:Tregar) accompany Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) when he confronts Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) in the streets of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:House_Lannister_guards.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Tregar and four other red cloaks die from the ensuing battle, while three [House Stark guards](wiki:House_Stark_guards) are killed and Ned is wounded.(wiki:House_Lannister_guards.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) is guarded by twenty Lannister guards when Eddard presents the late King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s will, but Ned expects the support of the [City Watch of King's Landing](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing). [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) has a hundred gold cloaks support Cersei instead of Ned, however, and the remaining Stark guards are killed during [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish)'s coup.(wiki:House_Lannister_guards.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Ser [Meryn Trant](wiki:Meryn_Trant) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) brings five red cloaks when he tries to arrest Arya. [Syrio Forel](wiki:Syrio_Forel) defeats the five and allows Arya to escape from Meryn.(wiki:House_Lannister_guards.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Captain [Vylarr](wiki:Vylarr) commands the hundred-strong household guard of [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) in the capital.(wiki:House_Lannister_guards.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Following the queen's orders, Vylarr's men take begging brothers to the dungeons.(wiki:House_Lannister_guards.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) becomes the captain of the personal guards of [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), and is given a crimson cloak. The former sellsword is also named an officer of the gold cloaks.(wiki:House_Lannister_guards.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
Depriving his sister of her guards, Tyrion sends Vylarr with the hundred as Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey)'s escort when he returns to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun).(wiki:House_Lannister_guards.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Four of the guards are false envoys sent by Tyrion to free Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) from Riverrun's dungeon, however. The four are hanged after their failed attempt, along with some red cloaks who came to their aid against [Tully guards](wiki:House_Tully_guards). The rest of these red cloaks are imprisoned in Riverrun.(wiki:House_Lannister_guards.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
[Osfryd Kettleblack](wiki:Osfryd_Kettleblack) commands Cersei's replacement household guard in King's Landing. These new red cloaks are little more than undiscipined [sellswords](wiki:Sellswords), however.(wiki:House_Lannister_guards.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Following the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), red cloaks of Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) chase [Chella](wiki:Chella)'s [Black Ears](wiki:Black_Ears) from King's Landing.(wiki:House_Lannister_guards.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Osfryd leaves the red cloaks and succeeds Ser [Addam Marbrand](wiki:Addam_Marbrand) as the Commander of the [City Watch of King's Landing](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:House_Lannister_guards.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Vylarr serves as the captain of guards at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:House_Lannister_guards.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) There are hundreds of Lannister guardsmen in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) in the aftermath of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:House_Lannister_guards.cite_ref-Radwdappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
