## Identity

The Silent Sisters are a female religious order within the Faith of the Seven, tasked with preparing the deceased for burial. They dress in grey robes with their faces hooded and heavily shawled so that only their eyes remain visible. Their silence is a devotional practice and a mark of their order; they are sworn to speak no words in the presence of the dead.

Their duties are extensive: they remove the bowels and organs of the deceased, drain the blood, and may stuff the body with fragrant herbs and salts to preserve it and hide the smell of decomposition. When a noble corpse is transported back to its family, one or more silent sisters travel as escort. They prepared the body of Eddard Stark after his execution, and his bones were escorted north by Hallis Mollen under their care. Similarly, Jory Cassel's body was sent to the silent sisters by Eddard so they could prepare it for burial at Winterfell.

The order suffered grievously during the Great Spring Sickness, when the High Septon, a third of the Most Devout, and nearly all of the silent sisters in King's Landing died from the plague. The order also records individual members who have been sworn to it, including Alysanne Osgrey, Cyrelle Tarbeck, Rohanne Tarbeck, and Marla Sunderland — women of noble families who took vows with the sisters.
