## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thones[ ]

A Lhazarene town is encountered in the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea) by both Khal [Ogo](wiki:Ogo) and Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo). Their town is destroyed by Drogo, and its people are taken as slaves.(wiki:Lhazareen.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Under Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) makes an alliance with Lhazar.(wiki:Lhazareen.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Fighters from [Lhazar](wiki:Lhazar) are among those who come to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) to battle in [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit).(wiki:Lhazareen.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
A former Lhazarene slave, [the Red Lamb](wiki:The_Red_Lamb), goes into training in the [Great Pyramid](wiki:Great_Pyramid) to become a [knight](wiki:Knight), and is a squire to Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy).(wiki:Lhazareen.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) By the end of the novel he is a newly-made knight.(wiki:Lhazareen.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
