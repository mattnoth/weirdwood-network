## Identity

The Order of the Green Hand was a knightly order found in the Kingdom of the Reach (wiki:Order_of_the_Green_Hand). Their symbol was a green hand, the sigil of House Gardener (wiki:Order_of_the_Green_Hand). Only the most virtuous and skilled in arms were offered membership after proving themselves in tourneys of love and beauty held at Highgarden (wiki:Order_of_the_Green_Hand).

## Origins

The order was founded by House Gardener, the Kings of the Reach (wiki:Order_of_the_Green_Hand). The last members died with King Mern IX Gardener at the Field of Fire during Aegon's Conquest (wiki:Order_of_the_Green_Hand).

## Allegiances

The Order was `SWORN_TO` the Kings of the Reach (track_b: Allegiance). `FOUNDED` by House Gardener (track_b: Founder). Its traditions are still recalled in the Reach, and knights from House Manderly in the north still claim membership, as the Manderlys were exiled from the Reach centuries before the Conquest (wiki:Order_of_the_Green_Hand).

## Narrative Arc

In ADWD, Lord Wyman Manderly is introduced at the Merman's Court in White Harbor with "Knight of the Order of the Green Hand" among his many titles (wiki:Order_of_the_Green_Hand).
