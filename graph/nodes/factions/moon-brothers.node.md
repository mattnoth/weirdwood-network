## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Lharys](wiki:Lharys) claims it was either members of the [Milk Snakes](wiki:Milk_Snakes) or the Moon Brothers who were the first to descend upon [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark)'s party in the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon).(wiki:Moon_Brothers.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
The Moon Brothers are among the clans who join [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) on the promise of weapons and armor. The party with Tyrion is led by [Ulf](wiki:Ulf), the son of [Umar](wiki:Umar).(wiki:Moon_Brothers.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Ulf demands blood money when three [Stone Crows](wiki:Stone_Crows) kill a Moon Brother, but [Conn](wiki:Conn) and [Shagga](wiki:Shagga) refuse to pay.(wiki:Moon_Brothers.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Moon Brothers participate in the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork). After the fighting ends, Tyrion finds the corpses of Ulf and a dozen other Moon Brothers.(wiki:Moon_Brothers.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Moon Brothers led by [Crawn](wiki:Crawn)(wiki:Moon_Brothers.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) continue with Tyrion to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), and Tyrion instructs that they be garrisoned separately from the Stone Crows in the barracks beneath the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand).(wiki:Moon_Brothers.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) They guard the tower(wiki:Moon_Brothers.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and eat in the [Small Hall](wiki:Small_Hall).(wiki:Moon_Brothers.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
In preparation for [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s arrival before the gates of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Tyrion sends the [Black Ears](wiki:Black_Ears) and the Moon Brothers into the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood) to harass and harry Stannis's army on its march to King's Landing before the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Moon_Brothers.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
