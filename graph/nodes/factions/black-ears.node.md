## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

The Black Ears are among the [Vale mountain clans](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans) who follow [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) out of the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon) and accept the offer to fight for [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Black_Ears.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) They fight in the western vanguard during the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork).(wiki:Black_Ears.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The Black Ears accompany Tyrion to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), where he is to serve as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King). He puts them in the [Broken Anvil](wiki:Broken_Anvil) to guard [Shae](wiki:Shae). Three of them drink wine at the inn's common room with Chella, Shae, and [Varys](wiki:Varys). Tyrion also has [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) secure them a place in the barracks beneath the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand) with the rest of the clans.(wiki:Black_Ears.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
Tyrion is escorted by mounted Black Ears during his first visit to [Chataya](wiki:Chataya)'s [brothel](wiki:Chataya's_brothel), and he gives permission for them to patronize the businesses on the [Street of Silk](wiki:Street_of_Silk).(wiki:Black_Ears.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
After Tyrion stops King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) from abusing and humiliating [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) in front of the court, he offers her the Black Ears as guards but is refused.(wiki:Black_Ears.cite_ref-Racok32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
In preparation for [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s arrival to the gates of King's Landing, Tyrion sends the Black Ears and the Moon Brothers into the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood) to harass and harry Stannis's army on its march to the capital.(wiki:Black_Ears.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Chella and a dozen Black Ears attempt to return to King's Landing, but they are chased off and mocked by the Kingslanders. Tyrion considers the city folk to be ingrates.(wiki:Black_Ears.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
