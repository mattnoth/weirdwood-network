## Origins

Lord [Unwin Peake](wiki:Unwin_Peake) was suspicious and untrusting. When he became [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) to [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), Unwin hired [mercenaries](wiki:Mercenaries) with no competing loyalties and paid them lavishly in [gold](wiki:Currency). Unwin's personal guard became known as his "Fingers" since there were ten of them and he was the King's Hand. Their captain was [Tessario the Tiger](wiki:Tessario_the_Tiger), an adventurer from [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), although [Mushroom](wiki:Mushroom) mocked him as Tessario the Thumb.(wiki:Fingers_(guards).cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Aegon disliked the sellswords.(wiki:Fingers_(guards).cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
On the feast day of [the Father](wiki:Father_(the_Seven)) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), the Fingers aided the [King's Justice](wiki:King's_Justice), Ser [Victor Risley](wiki:Victor_Risley), in beheading prisoners at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep)'s gates.(wiki:Fingers_(guards).cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Mushroom suggested that Ser [Mervyn Flowers](wiki:Mervyn_Flowers) might have allowed one of the Fingers to enter the bedchamber of Queen [Jaehaera Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen) and thrown the girl to her death.(wiki:Fingers_(guards).cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) When Lord Peake resigned as the Hand in [134 AC](wiki:134_AC), he bequeathed his guard to his successor, Lord [Thaddeus Rowan](wiki:Thaddeus_Rowan).(wiki:Fingers_(guards).cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
