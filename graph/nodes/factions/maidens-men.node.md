## Origins

During his exile from [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), Ser [Orryn Baratheon](wiki:Orryn_Baratheon) traveled from [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) to [Myr](wiki:Myr), where he signed on with the Maiden's Men. He was killed soon afterwards in the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands) during a battle with the [Men of Valor](wiki:Men_of_Valor).(wiki:Maiden's_Men.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
After the defeat at the [Battle of the Redgrass Field](wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field) in the [First Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:First_Blackfyre_Rebellion), those lords and knights who went into exile with Ser [Aegor Rivers](wiki:Aegor_Rivers) began to sign on to sellsword companies, such as the Maiden's Men. This would cause Aegor to make his own company, the [Golden Company](wiki:Golden_Company).(wiki:Maiden's_Men.cite_ref-Radwd5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince) was once a member of the company prior to founding the [Windblown](wiki:Windblown).(wiki:Maiden's_Men.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
