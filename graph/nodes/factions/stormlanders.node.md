## Culture

### Religion[ ]

The [First Men](wiki:First_Men) of antiquity followed the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods), but the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) gained ascendancy during the reign of King [Ormund III Durrandon](wiki:Ormund_III_Durrandon).(wiki:Stormlanders.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_andals_in_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)

### Customs[ ]

The stormlanders have a strong martial tradition, with the [marcher lords](wiki:Marcher_lords) being especially known for their ferocity.(wiki:Stormlanders.cite_ref-4)(wiki:Stormlanders.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_the_men_of_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The [Dornish Marches](wiki:Dornish_Marches) are well known for their famous bowmen and ballads, and the stormlands are also praised for their seamen and sailors.(wiki:Stormlanders.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_the_men_of_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
Many stormlanders live in moss-covered cottages among the region's rainy woods and green hills.(wiki:Stormlanders.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The men of the [rainwood](wiki:Rainwood) have castles which appear to have been grown from the earth.(wiki:Stormlanders.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_the_men_of_the_stormlands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)
Stormlanders, especially the marchers,(wiki:Stormlanders.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_queer_customs_of_the_south.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Stormlanders.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) can have fierce rivalries with [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) to their south.(wiki:Stormlanders.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Stormlanders.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Noble [bastards](wiki:Bastardy) raised in the stormlands can be given the surname Storm.(wiki:Stormlanders.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
