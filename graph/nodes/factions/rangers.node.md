## Origins

[Redwyn](wiki:Redwyn) wrote about his rangings during the reign of [Dorren Stark](wiki:Dorren_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North).(wiki:Rangers.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

When King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and his sister-wife, Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen), visited the [north](wiki:North) in [58 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), Lord Commander [Lothor Burley](wiki:Lothor_Burley) and a hundred rangers escorted Alysanne during a visit to the [haunted forest](wiki:Haunted_forest).(wiki:Rangers.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

A hundred rangers helped hunt down the raiders of [Sylas the Grim](wiki:Sylas_the_Grim) in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC).(wiki:Rangers.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

[Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) was attacked by a [shadowcat](wiki:Shadowcat) while ranging north of the [Shadow Tower](wiki:Shadow_Tower).(wiki:Rangers.cite_ref-Rasos7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

During a ranging [beyond the Wall](wiki:Beyond_the_Wall), rangers [Gared](wiki:Gared), [Will](wiki:Will), and Ser [Waymar Royce](wiki:Waymar_Royce) encounter [Others](wiki:Others). Will and Waymar Royce are slain,(wiki:Rangers.cite_ref-Ragotprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) while Gared manages to escape south of the [Wall](wiki:Wall), where he is eventually beheaded as punishment for desertion.(wiki:Rangers.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)(wiki:Rangers.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

A great number of rangers have gone missing.(wiki:Rangers.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2)(wiki:Rangers.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1) [Benjen Stark](wiki:Benjen_Stark), the [First Ranger](wiki:First_Ranger), goes out to search for Waymar with six rangers,(wiki:Rangers.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-3) but does not return. Other rangers are sent to find Benjen, but they return empty-handed.(wiki:Rangers.cite_ref-Ragot26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) Upon the absence of Benjen, Ser [Jaremy Rykker](wiki:Jaremy_Rykker) takes over command of the rangers.(wiki:Rangers.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Clash of Kings

Out of the two hundred brothers Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) leads from [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) for the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), some hundred and fifty are rangers.(wiki:Rangers.cite_ref-Racok6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2)

### A Dance with Dragons

After the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), the new Lord Commander, sends three parties led by [Dywen](wiki:Dywen), [Black Jack Bulwer](wiki:Jack_Bulwer), and [Kedge Whiteye](wiki:Kedge_Whiteye) to range beyond the Wall.(wiki:Rangers.cite_ref-Radwd28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Black Jack's party is eventually slain by the [Weeper](wiki:Weeper).(wiki:Rangers.cite_ref-Radwd53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
