## Culture

The Skagosi call themselves the stoneborn, as skagos means "stone" in the [Old Tongue](wiki:Old_Tongue).(wiki:Skagosi.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) They are an isolated people descended from the [First Men](wiki:First_Men). Because of their huge size and foul smell, some [maesters](wiki:Maester) believe the hairy Skagosi have a strong admixture of [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibbenese) blood, whereas others suggest they may be partially descended from [giants](wiki:Giants).(wiki:Skagosi.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_stoneborn_of_skagos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
The Skagosi are said to live in caves or grim mountain fastnesses.(wiki:Skagosi.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Traders claim the [lords](wiki:Lord) of the stoneborn clans ride shaggy [unicorns](wiki:Unicorn).(wiki:Skagosi.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_stoneborn_of_skagos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
The people of Skagos are little liked by other [northmen](wiki:Northmen), who derisively call them "Skaggs".(wiki:Skagosi.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) They regard Skagosi as little more than tribes of raiders and savages, not dissimilar to [wildlings](wiki:Free_folk). Skagosi are considered a backward folk, rumored to still perform human sacrifices to [weirwoods](wiki:Weirwood) and lure passing ships to destruction with false lights. The Skagosi supposedly engage in [cannibalism](wiki:Cannibalism) during winter,(wiki:Skagosi.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) with their warriors eating the hearts and livers of the men they slay.(wiki:Skagosi.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-3) According to Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), the inhabitants of Skagos still continue the lord's right of the [first night](wiki:First_night),(wiki:Skagosi.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) which has been forbidden in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) since the reign of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Skagosi.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
When they trade with outsiders, Skagosi offer pelts, [obsidian](wiki:Obsidian) blades and arrowheads, and unicorn horns.(wiki:Skagosi.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_stoneborn_of_skagos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Origins

According to [The Edge of the World](wiki:The_Edge_of_the_World) by Maester [Balder](wiki:Balder), a war fleet from [Skagos](wiki:Skagos) sailed to the nearby island [Skane](wiki:Skane), seizing all the women, killing all the men, and [feasting on their flesh](wiki:Cannibalism) for a fortnight. The smaller isle has reportedly been uninhabited since the [Feast of Skane](wiki:Feast_of_Skane).(wiki:Skagosi.cite_ref-Raffc15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-4)(wiki:Skagosi.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Some attribute the destruction of [Hardhome](wiki:Hardhome) to cannibals from Skagos.(wiki:Skagosi.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_wall_and_beyond:_the_wildlings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
In response to Skagosi raids on the mainland, [Brandon IX Stark](wiki:Brandon_IX_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North), broke their power, destroying the Skagosi ships and forbidding them the sea.(wiki:Skagosi.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_stoneborn_of_skagos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
According to legend, [Bael the Bard](wiki:Bael_the_Bard) entered [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) under the guise of a singer named Sygerrik of Skagos; sygerrik means "deceiver" in the [Old Tongue](wiki:Old_Tongue).(wiki:Skagosi.cite_ref-Racok51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Some Skagosi have served in the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) over the past millennia. [A Crowl](wiki:Lord_Commander_Crowl) was [Lord Commander](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch) over a thousand years ago, and, according to the [Annals of the Black Centaur](wiki:Annals_of_the_Black_Centaur), [a Stane](wiki:First_Ranger_Stane) served as [First Ranger](wiki:First_Ranger) for a brief time.(wiki:Skagosi.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_stoneborn_of_skagos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)
The Skagosi rose in rebellion against [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) during the rule of King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen). The rebellion lasted for years and claimed the lives of thousands, among them Lord [Barthogan Stark](wiki:Barthogan_Stark), before being put down.(wiki:Skagosi.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north:_the_stoneborn_of_skagos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-5)
