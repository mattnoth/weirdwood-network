## Origins

The Band of Nine was born in [258 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest), when nine outlaws, exiles, pirates, and sellsword captains met beneath the [Tree of Crowns](wiki:Tree_of_Crowns) as to form an alliance, swearing to aid each other in conquering kingdoms for each of the nine members.(wiki:Band_of_Nine.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) They began their conquests by overrunning the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands) and [sacking](wiki:Sack_of_Tyrosh) [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh), where they set up [Alequo Adarys](wiki:Alequo_Adarys) as a tyrant. They then marched on the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones), conquering them with ease.(wiki:Band_of_Nine.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

King [Jaehaerys II Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_II_Targaryen) had known that the Band of Nine meant to win the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) for [Maelys the Monstrous](wiki:Maelys_I_Blackfyre), who styled himself "King Maelys I Blackfyre". But he hoped that the alliance of rogues would founder in Essos, or fall at the hands of another alliance in the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities). When that did not happen, the thin and scrawny king, who did not lack for courage or intelligence, resolved to meet the Ninepenny Kings upon the Stepstones, choosing to take the war to them. The actions that followed are collectively known as the [War of the Ninepenny Kings, or the Fifth Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:War_of_the_Ninepenny_Kings).(wiki:Band_of_Nine.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

The [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen) responded quickly, sending their own force to the Stepstones. [Maester](wiki:Maester) [Eon](wiki:Eon)'s *[Account of the War of the Ninepenny Kings](wiki:Account_of_the_War_of_the_Ninepenny_Kings)*, is a splendid source for the details of fighting, with its many battles on land and sea. It showed that the war ended when Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) killed the pretender Maelys I Blackfyre. The remainder of the Ninepenny Kings had no interest in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) and fell back to their own domains. Half a year of hard fighting remained before the Band of Nine lost the Stepstones and the Disputed Lands, but Alequo, the Tyrant of Tyrosh, managed to remain in power for another six years, before the [Archon](wiki:Archon) of Tyrosh was restored.(wiki:Band_of_Nine.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_jaehaerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
