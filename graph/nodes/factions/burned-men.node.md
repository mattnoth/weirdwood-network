## Origins

The Burned Men are seemingly an offshoot of the [Painted Dogs](wiki:Painted_Dogs) who branched off in the years after the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons). They were said to have worshiped a fire-[witch](wiki:Witch), sending their boys to bring her gifts and risk the flames of her [dragon](wiki:Dragon) to prove their manhood.(wiki:Burned_Men.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn) suggests this was [Nettles](wiki:Nettles) and [Sheepstealer](wiki:Sheepstealer).(wiki:Burned_Men.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
The Burned Men carried off the last daughter of [Alys Arryn](wiki:Alys_Arryn) and Ser [Elys Waynwood](wiki:Elys_Waynwood) on her way to marry a [Bracken](wiki:House_Bracken).(wiki:Burned_Men.cite_ref-Raffc41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Burned Men are among the near three hundred clansmen who join [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) on the promise of weapons and armor. The Burned Men are led by [Timett son of Timett](wiki:Timett), and their cookfire is separate from those of other clans.(wiki:Burned_Men.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
While the other clans shout in support of Tyrion when he rallies them before the [battle on the Green Fork](wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork), the Burned Men instead rattle their swords and spears. Timett and his men loot the slain after the battle.(wiki:Burned_Men.cite_ref-Ragot62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The group continues with Tyrion to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), and Tyrion instructs that the Burned Men be given a hall to themselves.(wiki:Burned_Men.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Timett leads an escort of Burned Men when Tyrion visits the [Guildhall of the Alchemists](wiki:Guildhall_of_the_Alchemists), as the clansmen intimidate the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) of the capital.(wiki:Burned_Men.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
In preparation for [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon)'s arrival at [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), Tyrion sends the Burned Men into the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood) to harass and harry Stannis's march.(wiki:Burned_Men.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Timett and his clansmen take their plunder and return to the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon).(wiki:Burned_Men.cite_ref-Rasos4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) A village elder in the foothills of the mountains tells [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) that the Burned Men under Timett have become fearless.(wiki:Burned_Men.cite_ref-Rasos65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
