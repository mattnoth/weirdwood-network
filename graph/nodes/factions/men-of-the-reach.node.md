## Culture

### Religion[ ]

The [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) is the predominant religion in the [Reach](wiki:Reach), with the new gods having been welcomed by the [Three Sage Kings](wiki:Three_Sage_Kings).(wiki:Men_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_andals_in_the_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) The [Starry Sept](wiki:Starry_Sept) in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) was the seat of the [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon) for "a thousand years prior" to the [Wars of Conquest](wiki:Wars_of_Conquest).(wiki:Men_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Men_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Pate_7-0)

### Customs[ ]

The traditions of chivalry and singers are said to have begun in the [Reach](wiki:Reach),(wiki:Men_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) where legends claim chivalry was brought by the First Knight, [John the Oak](wiki:John_the_Oak).(wiki:Men_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) [Knighthood](wiki:Knighthood) is most esteemed in the Reach compared to the rest of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms),(wiki:Men_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-8) and many [tourneys](wiki:Tourney) are held in the region.(wiki:Men_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_highgarden.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
The [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) of the educated [maesters](wiki:Maesters) is found in the ancient city of [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown).(wiki:Men_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Some say the [Arbor](wiki:Arbor) makes the best [wine](wiki:Wine) in the [known world](wiki:Known_world).(wiki:Men_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Considered the breadbasket of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), the Reach has a rich farming culture.(wiki:Men_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)
Many men of the Reach consider [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) to their south to be their ancient enemies.(wiki:Men_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Men_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_queer_customs_of_the_south.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Men_of_the_Reach.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
Noble [bastards](wiki:Bastardy) born in the Reach can be given the surname Flowers.
