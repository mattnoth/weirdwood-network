## Culture

Some Sistermen, such as the [Borrells](wiki:House_Borrell), have webbing between their hands and feet, which they call the mark.(wiki:Sistermen.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Some [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) pejoratively consider Sistermen to have fishbreath.(wiki:Sistermen.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
The Sistermen originally worshiped the [Lady of the Waves](wiki:Lady_of_the_Waves) and the [Lord of the Skies](wiki:Lord_of_the_Skies) instead of the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods) adopted by the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) of the mainland. They would also cast [dwarfs](wiki:Dwarf) into the sea as an offering to the gods, but [septons](wiki:Septon) stopped the practice after the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals).(wiki:Sistermen.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
[Sister's stew](wiki:Sister's_stew) is a popular dish in the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters).(wiki:Sistermen.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Some Sistermen use false lights to cause unsuspecting ships to wreck.(wiki:Sistermen.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
According to a semi-canon source, Sistermen can wear [sealskin](wiki:Seal) cloaks.(wiki:Sistermen.cite_ref-AlayneI_3-0)

## Origins

The Sistermen were once a free people [whose rulers](wiki:King_of_the_Three_Sisters) acted as [pirate](wiki:Pirate) kings.(wiki:Sistermen.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) In response to the [Rape of the Three Sisters](wiki:Rape_of_the_Three_Sisters), the Sistermen bent the knee to the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie) to expel the invading [northmen](wiki:North), ending the era of native kings. The [Kings of Mountain and Vale](wiki:Kings_of_Mountain_and_Vale) from [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) and [Kings in the North](wiki:Kings_in_the_North) from [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark) fought over the [Three Sisters](wiki:Three_Sisters) for a thousand years in the [War Across the Water](wiki:War_Across_the_Water),(wiki:Sistermen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) depleting much of the islands' resources.(wiki:Sistermen.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5) Some Sistermen still resent northmen.(wiki:Sistermen.cite_ref-Radwd9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
The islanders briefly rebelled against the Arryns in the [Sistermen's Rebellion](wiki:Sistermen's_Rebellion) amid [Aegon's Conquest](wiki:Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Sistermen.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
