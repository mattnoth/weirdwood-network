## Origins

Centuries of study convinced the Conclave that the [world](wiki:Known_world) is a sphere.(wiki:Conclave.cite_ref-Rfab10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
In [5 AC](wiki:5_AC), King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) asked the Conclave to send an archmaester from [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to serve as a royal advisor, and [Ollidar](wiki:Ollidar) thus became the first [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester).(wiki:Conclave.cite_ref-Rfab3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
After the [High Septon](wiki:High_Lickspittle) died in [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) flew on their [dragons](wiki:Dragon) to Oldtown. Alysanne remained in the city when Jaehaerys visited nearby castles. While feasting in the dining hall of the archmaesters, the queen unsuccessfully advised the Conclave to accept highborn girls into the Citadel.(wiki:Conclave.cite_ref-Rfab9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) In [60 AC](wiki:60_AC), some believed that [Elysar](wiki:Elysar) was named Grand Maester to rid the Conclave of his presence.(wiki:Conclave.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Jaehaerys was feasted by the Conclave in [89 AC](wiki:Years_after_Aegon's_Conquest).(wiki:Conclave.cite_ref-Rfab11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), removes [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) as [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester). Some [archmaesters](wiki:Archmaesters) at the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel) object, however, and insist that only the Conclave may appoint or remove a Grand Maester. After agreeing with Pycelle's dismissal, the Conclave meets to choose his successor. Although the lowborn [Turquin](wiki:Turquin) and [Erreck](wiki:Erreck) are considered, the highborn [Gormon](wiki:Gormon_Tyrell), who was born into [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell), is eventually chosen to be next Grand Maester. Lord [Varys](wiki:Varys), the [master of whisperers](wiki:Master_of_whisperers), has spies in [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), however, and he informs Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), the new Hand, of the Conclave's decision. Tywin has Pycelle restored to his positions at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), in order to keep the Tyrells from gaining another seat in the small council.(wiki:Conclave.cite_ref-Rasos12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
