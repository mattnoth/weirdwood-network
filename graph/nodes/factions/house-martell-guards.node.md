## Appearances & Description

The Martell guards wield spears and round shields,(wiki:House_Martell_guards.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) sometimes using throwing spears and crossbows.(wiki:House_Martell_guards.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Their captain, [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah), carries a longaxe.(wiki:House_Martell_guards.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) The guardsmen wear the colors of the [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne).(wiki:House_Martell_guards.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne), is guarded at the [Water Gardens](wiki:Water_Gardens) by thirty spears commanded by [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah). When Doran returns to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) after the death of his brother [Oberyn](wiki:Oberyn_Martell), Areo leaves ten men to watch the children remaining at the Water Gardens.(wiki:House_Martell_guards.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Angry [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) pelt Doran's escort with fruit when they reach the gates of Sunspear.(wiki:House_Martell_guards.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4) Doran has his captain discretely confine Oberyn's daughters, the [Sand Snakes](wiki:Sand_Snakes), in the Spear Tower.(wiki:House_Martell_guards.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) plots to crown Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon), but her plan is foiled at the [Greenblood](wiki:Greenblood). Areo appears from a poleboat with more than a dozen spearmen and crossbowmen. Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) slashes two spearmen and his horse strikes a crossbowmen, but the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) is beheaded by Areo.(wiki:House_Martell_guards.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2) Ser [Gerold Dayne](wiki:Gerold_Dayne) wounds Myrcella and then flees from Areo's men into the sands of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:House_Martell_guards.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Doran has his daughter confined and placed under guard in the Spear Tower.(wiki:House_Martell_guards.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
