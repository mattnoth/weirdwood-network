## Origins

During the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War), guardsmen twice saved King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) from [Dornish](wiki:Dornishmen) assassins.(wiki:House_Targaryen_guards.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) was also attacked in the streets of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) one night, and she slew the last assassin with [Dark Sister](wiki:Dark_Sister) after two of her escorts were killed.(wiki:House_Targaryen_guards.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Visenya advised Aegon to wear mail at all times for his own protection. After the king refused, Visenya quickly drew Dark Sister and cut her brother's cheek before his guards could react. Recognizing his vulnerability, Aegon decided to have the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) formed for better protection.(wiki:House_Targaryen_guards.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

Although only a mere man-at-arms, [Dick Bean](wiki:Dick_Bean) fought as one of King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen)'s champions in a [trial of seven](wiki:Trial_of_seven) against members of the [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons).(wiki:House_Targaryen_guards.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

During his [minority](wiki:Customs) on [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) trained at arms every morning. The young king sparred with his [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) and Ser [Elyas Scales](wiki:Elyas_Scales), the [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms). Jaehaerys also practiced with Ser [Merrell Bullock](wiki:Merrell_Bullock), the commander of the garrison, and Merrell's sons, [Alyn](wiki:Alyn_Bullock) and [Howard](wiki:Howard_Bullock).(wiki:House_Targaryen_guards.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) In [56 AC](wiki:56_AC) Jaehaerys founded the [Dragonkeepers](wiki:Dragonkeepers), a unit of guards created to guard the Targaryen [dragons](wiki:Dragon).(wiki:House_Targaryen_guards.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) named Ser [Robert Quince](wiki:Robert_Quince) as her [castellan](wiki:Castellan) on Dragonstone instead of Ser [Alfred Broome](wiki:Alfred_Broome).(wiki:House_Targaryen_guards.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Alfred later betrayed Rhaenyra with the [fall of Dragonstone](wiki:Fall_of_Dragonstone).(wiki:House_Targaryen_guards.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

Among the men-at-arms who escorted the Targaryen princes to the [tourney at Ashford Meadow](wiki:Tourney_at_Ashford_Meadow) were [Wate](wiki:Wate) and [Yorkel](wiki:Yorkel).(wiki:House_Targaryen_guards.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Ser [Tristimun](wiki:Tristimun) commanded the [sea watch](wiki:Sea_watch) on Dragonstone during the reign of King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen).(wiki:House_Targaryen_guards.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) The Targaryen dynasty fell from power and lost the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne) during [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion).(wiki:House_Targaryen_guards.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Walking through a corridor of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) sees empty suits of black steel armor with dragon scales on the crests of their helms,(wiki:House_Targaryen_guards.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) possibly the armor of Targaryen guards or the [Dragonkeepers](wiki:Dragonkeepers).

### A Clash of Kings
