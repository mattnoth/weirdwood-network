## Identity

The Blacks were the faction supporting Queen Rhaenyra Targaryen's claim to the Iron Throne during the Dance of the Dragons (129–131 AC). Their sigil was a black dragon on red. Named in contrast to the rival faction, the Greens (supporters of King Aegon II Targaryen). Composed primarily of lords and houses sworn to Rhaenyra, including House Velaryon, House Stark, and others.
