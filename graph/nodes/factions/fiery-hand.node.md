### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

While watching the [red priest](wiki:Red_priest) [Benerro](wiki:Benerro) preach at the [Temple of the Lord of Light](wiki:Temple_of_the_Lord_of_Light_(Volantis)) in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) see soldiers of the Fiery Hand guarding the temple's doors.(wiki:Fiery_Hand.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Soldiers of the Fiery Hand escort the priest [Moqorro](wiki:Moqorro) aboard the [Selaesori Qhoran](wiki:Selaesori_Qhoran). Tyrion nicknames them "fiery fingers", as there are five of them. When Moqorro recites the nightly prayers to R'hllor before the [nightfire](wiki:Nightfire), the guards lead the responses.(wiki:Fiery_Hand.cite_ref-Radwd33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Only three of the fingers survive the storm that wrecks the ship.(wiki:Fiery_Hand.cite_ref-Radwd40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) After the ship is captured by slavers, they are brought to the slave auction held outside of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Fiery_Hand.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) Tyrion believes that the flames tattooed upon their faces will ensure some [red temple](wiki:Red_temple) will purchase them from the slavers.(wiki:Fiery_Hand.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)
