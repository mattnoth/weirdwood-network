## Identity

The Winter Wolves were a force of northern veterans who marched south with Lord Cregan Stark during the Dance of the Dragons. Approximately 2,000 strong, they were old men who had survived many winters and chose to die in battle in the south rather than face another winter. They fought for the Blacks (Rhaenyra's faction). Under the command of Lord Roderick Dustin (who died in the Battle of the Burning Mill), they played a significant role in the northern campaigns of the Dance. Their march culminated in Cregan Stark's arrival at King's Landing after Rhaenyra's death — the Hour of the Wolf.
