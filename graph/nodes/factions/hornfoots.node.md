## Culture

Hornfoots wear nothing on their feet, which turns the soles of their feet hard and black.(wiki:Hornfoots.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Hornfoots.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Hornfoot men wear fur(wiki:Hornfoots.cite_ref-Radwd21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) and wield long spears.(wiki:Hornfoots.cite_ref-Rasos73.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Origins

The Hornfoot folk warred with the [Nightrunners](wiki:Nightrunners) before [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder), [King-Beyond-the-Wall](wiki:King-Beyond-the-Wall), negotiated a peace between them.(wiki:Hornfoots.cite_ref-Rasos15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
