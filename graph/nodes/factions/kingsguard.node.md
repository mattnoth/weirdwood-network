## Origins

The Kingsguard was founded during the reign of the first [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) king on the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), [Aegon the Conqueror](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen). The first Kingsguard was created at the suggestion of Aegon's sister-wife, Queen [Visenya Targaryen](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen), after a [Dornish](wiki:Dorne) assassination attempt on their lives in the streets of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) in [10 AC](wiki:10_AC). She deliberately modeled the Kingsguard vows of holding no lands or title on the ancient vows of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch), and seven [knights](wiki:Knight) were chosen because the king ruled the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

When Prince [Jaehaerys Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) put forth his claim to the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne), two Kingsguards of the reigning King [Maegor I Targaryen](wiki:Maegor_I_Targaryen), [Olyver Bracken](wiki:Olyver_Bracken) and [Raymund Mallery](wiki:Raymund_Mallery), abandoned him.(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0) According to some, Maegor was killed by a member of the Kingsguard who could no longer abide his tyranny.(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0) As king, Jaehaerys selected five members of the Kingsguard in the [War for the White Cloaks](wiki:War_for_the_White_Cloaks).(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0)

During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), the Kingsguard split between knights serving King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) and [Queensguard](wiki:Queensguard) serving Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen).(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rtpatq.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

A [hedge knight](wiki:Hedge_knight), Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall),(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1) rose to become Lord Commander for King [Aegon V Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen).(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rasos67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-2)

During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Ser [Jonothor Darry](wiki:Jonothor_Darry) and Prince [Lewyn Martell](wiki:Lewyn_Martell) were killed during the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident), while Ser [Arthur Dayne](wiki:Arthur_Dayne), Ser [Oswell Whent](wiki:Oswell_Whent), and Lord Commander [Gerold Hightower](wiki:Gerold_Hightower) died in [combat at the tower of joy](wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy). Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) slew his charge, King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), during the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing), earning himself the "Kingslayer" sobriquet. The new king, [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), allowed Jaime to remain in the Kingsguard and elevated the other surviving member of the Mad King's white cloaks, Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy), to the position of Lord Commander.(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Ragot33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

After the death of Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) names Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) the new [Warden of the East](wiki:Warden_of_the_East), even though Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) speaks of Jaime also being in line to inherit the title of [Warden of the West](wiki:Warden_of_the_West).(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) Robert later threatens to name Jaime his [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Ragot39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-1)

Robert dies after being gored by a [boar](wiki:Boar), and his successor, King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon), dismisses the aged [Lord Commander of the Kingsguard](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Kingsguard), Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy). This breaks tradition, as all previous Kingsguard served for life. Joffrey elevates his uncle, Jaime, to Lord Commander and adds to the Kingsguard [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane), who is not a [knight](wiki:Knight).(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-3) Jaime is captured by [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) during the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood).(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0)

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) names Ser [Jorah Mormont](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) as the first member of her [Queensguard](wiki:Queensguard).(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0)

### A Clash of Kings

During the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), King [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) is protected by his own Kingsguard, which he names the [Rainbow Guard](wiki:Rainbow_Guard).(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0)

Joffrey has his Kingsguard beat his betrothed, [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark). Ser [Preston Greenfield](wiki:Preston_Greenfield) is killed during the [riot of King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing)(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0) and replaced by Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann).(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-2) Ser [Boros Blount](wiki:Boros_Blount), who is removed from the white cloaks for not protecting Prince [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon), is replaced with Ser [Osmund Kettleblack](wiki:Osmund_Kettleblack).(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-3)

Ser [Mandon Moore](wiki:Mandon_Moore) drowns during the [battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater),(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Racok61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0) while Sandor Clegane flees the battlefield.(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell) joins the Kingsguard after the battle.(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-0)

[Aggo](wiki:Aggo), [Jhogo](wiki:Jhogo), and [Rakharo](wiki:Rakharo) are members of Daenerys's Queensguard.(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Boros is restored to the Kingsguard.(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rasos12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) In return for Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn) returning to the [king's peace](wiki:King's_peace), Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), Joffrey's Hand, names Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn) the Warden of the East instead of Jaime.(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_48-0)

Jaime, released from imprisonment at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) by [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark),(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rasos1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_49-0) returns to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-2)

[Strong Belwas](wiki:Belwas) is a member of Daenerys's Queensguard.(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Rasosappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_50-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Jaime, standing vigil for his father, Lord Tywin, wears a long hauberk, its scales [mother-of-pearl](wiki:Mother-of-pearl) chased with gold.(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_51-0)

During the failed plot to crown Princess [Myrcella Baratheon](wiki:Myrcella_Baratheon), Ser [Arys Oakheart](wiki:Arys_Oakheart) is killed by [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah) at the [Greenblood](wiki:Greenblood) in [Dorne](wiki:Dorne).(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

In [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), Barristan becomes the Lord Commander of Daenerys's Queensguard.(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_52-0)

Against the wishes of Lord [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington), Ser [Rolly Duckfield](wiki:Rolly_Duckfield) is named the first of the Kingsguard of [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)).(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_53-0)

Ser [Robert Strong](wiki:Robert_Strong) is named to the Kingsguard of King Tommen I Baratheon.(wiki:Kingsguard.cite_ref-Radwd65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_54-0)
