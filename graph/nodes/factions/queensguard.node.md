## Origins

Although Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) appointed [Jonquil Darke](wiki:Jonquil_Darke) as her [sworn shield](wiki:Sworn_shield) after an assassination attempt,(wiki:Queensguard.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) the first known implementation of the Queensguard was founded by Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen), as an elite bodyguard for her and her family during the [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) civil war known as the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons).(wiki:Queensguard.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Organization

Historically composed of seven knights sworn to a lifetime of service, members are to serve unto death despite age or any physical or mental ailments, with an invalid member's duties being taken up by his sworn brothers. During his time as a member of the Queensguard, a member is not allowed to hold lands, sire children, marry, or have any worldly allegiance except to his monarch. All members of the Queensguard are traditionally sworn knights.(wiki:Queensguard.cite_ref-Ragot8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Queensguard.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)(wiki:Queensguard.cite_ref-4)
