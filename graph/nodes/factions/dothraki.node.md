## Origins

### Origins

Scholars believe the ancestors of the Dothraki came from the lands beyond the [Bone Mountains](wiki:Bone_Mountains) in the [Further East](wiki:Further_East) of [Essos](wiki:Essos), leaving behind the bones that give the mountains their name.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_38-0) The Dothraki themselves, however, believe their people came from [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak) by the [Mother of Mountains](wiki:Mother_of_Mountains),(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-5) with the first man emerging from the [Womb of the World](wiki:Womb_of_the_World) on the back of the first [horse](wiki:Horse) a thousand thousand years ago.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-12)

The nomadic Dothraki do not have a tradition of settlements. To make a house a thousand years ago, they dug a hole in the ground and covered it with a woven grass roof.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-19)

For centuries the [Tall Men](wiki:Tall_Men) considered the Dothraki to be no more than nuisances.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-6)

### Conquests

Four hundred years ago,(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-7)(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-1) the Dothraki rode toward the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) out of the east, sacking and burning every town and city in their path, including the [Kingdom of Sarnor](wiki:Kingdom_of_Sarnor), the [Qaathi](wiki:Qaathi) cities in the [red waste](wiki:Red_waste),(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-8) and the [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibben) settlements in the [Kingdom of the Ifequevron](wiki:Kingdom_of_the_Ifequevron).(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-1) Khal [Temmo](wiki:Temmo) and his *khalasar* of at least fifty-thousand were stopped by the [Three Thousand of Qohor](wiki:Three_Thousand_of_Qohor).(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Rasos8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-2) During the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood) after the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria), the Dothraki drove [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) from their hovels and nobles from their estates, until only grass and ruins remained from the [Forest of Qohor](wiki:Forest_of_Qohor) to the headwaters of the [Selhoru](wiki:Selhoru).(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_39-0)

Ruined cities and regions ring the vast plains of the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea), including the Kingdom of Sarnor, the Kingdom of the Ifequevron, the upper [Skahazadhan](wiki:Skahazadhan), and the red waste.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Rtloiaf.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_40-0) The [godsway](wiki:Godsway) in Vaes Dothrak contains monuments the Dothraki have taken from conquered people.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-20)

[Terrio Erastes](wiki:Terrio_Erastes) wrote *[Fire Upon the Grass](wiki:Fire_Upon_the_Grass)* about his time as a guest of Khal [Dhako](wiki:Dhako).(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-2)

## Appearances & Description

Dothraki typically have [copper](wiki:Copper)-toned skin, black hair,(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) and black,(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) almond-shaped eyes.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Rasos23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Dothraki men wear their hair in long braids, oiled(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) or greased with fat from the rendering pits.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) Dothraki warriors carry small [bells](wiki:Bells_(Dothraki)) in their braids, which ring softly when they move.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) They are only allowed to braid their hair after they have won a victory.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) A bell is added to their braid after each victory;(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) The braid is only cut when a Dothraki warrior is defeated in battle,(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) an act through which he lets the world know his shame.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1) Only few men die with their braids never having been cut off.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) Men further might wear metal rings bound in their mustachios.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2)

Both men and women might wear painted leather vests over bare chests and horsehair leggings cinched by [bronze](wiki:Bronze) medallion belts,(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) although wealthier Dothraki might also have [silver](wiki:Silver) and [golden](wiki:Gold) medallions on their belts.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Others might wear sandsilk trousers.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) For footwear Dothraki wear leather boots,(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) leather sandals which lace up to the knee,(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) or sandals of woven grass.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-1) However, when visiting the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities), the Dothraki wear rich fabrics and sweet perfumes.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)

Being accustomed to riding [horses](wiki:Horse) every entire day, Dothraki warriors walk with a bowlegged swagger on the ground.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)

The Dothraki scrub themselves clean with sand.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) weds Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) in a [a ceremony](wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen) outside of [Pentos](wiki:Pentos).(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-18) She joins Drogo's *[khalasar](wiki:Khalasar)* and rides to [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak).(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-21) Daenerys becomes pregnant with [Rhaego](wiki:Rhaego), whom the Dothraki believe to be the [stallion who mounts the world](wiki:Stallion_who_mounts_the_world).(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-13) After a [wineseller](wiki:Wineseller) attempts to assassinate the *[khaleesi](wiki:Khaleesi)* in the Western Market, Drogo announces his intention to conquer the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-7)

Drogo's *khalasar* sacks a town of the [Lhazareen](wiki:Lhazareen) and defeats the rival *khalasar* of Khal [Ogo](wiki:Ogo), although Drogo is wounded in the fighting.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-7) Daenerys asks [Mirri Maz Duur](wiki:Mirri_Maz_Duur) to save the *khal's* life,(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-8) but Rhaego dies in the ceremony and Daenerys ends up smothering the comatose Drogo.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-2) The *khalasar* splinters into a dozen new groups, with the most prominent led by [Jhaqo](wiki:Jhaqo) and [Pono](wiki:Pono).(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-3) Taking command of a small group of Dothraki and freed slaves, Daenerys walks into Drogo's funeral pyre and miraculously hatches three [dragons](wiki:Dragon) from petrified [dragon eggs](wiki:Dragon_eggs).(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-5)

### A Clash of Kings

Avoiding other *khalasars* in the [Dothraki sea](wiki:Dothraki_sea), Daenerys's small *khalasar* struggles south through the [red waste](wiki:Red_waste)(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-10) but is able to reach the safety of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth).(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-1)

### A Storm of Swords

Daenerys's tiny *khalasar* sails for [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay) and participates in the defeats of [Astapor](wiki:Fall_of_Astapor),(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2) [Yunkai](wiki:Battle_near_Yunkai),(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1) and [Meereen](wiki:Siege_of_Meereen).(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0) The Yunkai'i send riders to Vaes Dothrak to seek aid against Daenerys.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-1)

### A Dance with Dragons

Outriders from [Motho](wiki:Motho)'s *khalasar* are seen north of [Dagger Lake](wiki:Dagger_Lake), and [Zekko](wiki:Zekko)'s horde moves through the [Forest of Qohor](wiki:Forest_of_Qohor). [Haldon Halfmaester](wiki:Haldon_Halfmaester) believes they are fleeing the thirty thousand men of Khal Pono, whose *khalasar* has been spotted near the headwaters of the [Selhoru](wiki:Selhoru).(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Radwd8.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0)

After Daenerys disappears from [Daznak's Pit](wiki:Daznak's_Pit) on [Drogon](wiki:Drogon), [Aggo](wiki:Aggo) and [Rakharo](wiki:Rakharo) lead most of her *khalasar* from Meereen across the [Skahazadhan](wiki:Skahazadhan) in search of the queen.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0)(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0)

Jhaqo's*khalasar* discovers Daenerys and Drogon in the Dothraki sea.(wiki:Dothraki.cite_ref-Radwd71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-2)
