## Origins

The bearded priests were originally a sect that abandoned [Valyria](wiki:Valyria), finding the religious tolerance of the [Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) intolerable.(wiki:Bearded_priests.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_norvos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

[Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah) was trained by bearded priests and guarded [Mellario](wiki:Mellario).(wiki:Bearded_priests.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) He accompanied Mellario from [Norvos](wiki:Norvos) to [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear) when she wed [Doran Martell](wiki:Doran_Martell), and Areo now serves as [captain of the guards](wiki:Captain_of_the_guards) to the [Prince of Dorne](wiki:Prince_of_Dorne).(wiki:Bearded_priests.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3)
