## Identity

The Bastard's Boys were the personal retinue of men serving Ramsay Snow (later legitimized as Ramsay Bolton), the bastard son of Lord Roose Bolton of the Dreadfort. They assisted Ramsay in his hunts — including hunting women for sport. Members included Damon Dance-for-Me, Luton, Heke, Grunt, Yellow Dick, and others. They participated in Ramsay's capture and torture of Theon Greyjoy (renamed "Reek") and various atrocities in the North.
