## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

After [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) enters [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos) tells [Pyat Pree](wiki:Pyat_Pree) that the Thirteen shall set a crown of black jade and fire opals upon her head.(wiki:Thirteen.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) When the [Tourmaline Brotherhood](wiki:Tourmaline_Brotherhood) gives Daenerys a crown, Daenerys rejects Xaro's suggestion to sell that crown in expectation of a grander one from the Thirteen.(wiki:Thirteen.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Daenerys asks for ten ships from each of the Thirteen, but she is refused.(wiki:Thirteen.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
Xaro's influence prevents the Thirteen from adding their voices to that of the other trade guilds demanding Daenerys's expulsion from Qarth and possible assassination after the destruction of the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying).(wiki:Thirteen.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Xaro sails to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) with a fleet of thirteen galleys, and men to pull the oars, to gift to Daenerys so that she can sail home to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). The queen believes Xaro convinced each of his fellow members of the Thirteen to give up one ship. When Daenerys refuses Xaro's advice to depart Mereen, the merchant leaves behind a bloodstained glove symbolizing war.(wiki:Thirteen.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
