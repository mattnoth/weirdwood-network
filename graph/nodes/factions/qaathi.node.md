## Origins

Little is known of the Qaathi, aside from them arising from towns in the grassslands of central [Essos](wiki:Essos). They sometimes came into conflict with the [Sarnori](wiki:Tall_Men) to their north. The Qaathi often lost these wars, so they began founding new city-states farther south, among them Qarth on the coast of the [Summer Sea](wiki:Summer_Sea). However, these lands began to turn into desert, the [red waste](wiki:Red_waste), just as the Qaathi established their foothold there.(wiki:Qaathi.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The Qaathi were further hampered by the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria), as the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) began venturing into the desert during the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood). The horselords destroyed all remaining Qaathi cities save for Qarth, which is protected by its [triple walls](wiki:Triple_walls_of_Qarth).(wiki:Qaathi.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_grasslands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2) Several ruined Qartheen settlements have now been given Dothraki names, including [Vaes Orvik](wiki:Vaes_Orvik), [Vaes Qosar](wiki:Vaes_Qosar), [Vaes Shirosi](wiki:Vaes_Shirosi), and possibly [Vaes Tolorro](wiki:Vaes_Tolorro).
