## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

The Sons of the Harpy have begun a [shadow war](wiki:Shadow_war) against Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), following her occupation of the city of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). They slay [freedmen](wiki:Freedmen), [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) and [shavepates](wiki:Shavepates). They kill during the night, and leave a mark over the body of their victim, a harpy, drawn in blood.(wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Amongst those slain is [Stalwart Shield](wiki:Stalwart_Shield).(wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) Following his death, Queen Daenerys raises the reward concerning the Sons of the Harpy from one hundred honors to one thousand.(wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-2) Not long after, the Sons murder six of Daenerys's Unsullied, including [Mossador](wiki:Mossador), and three [freedmen](wiki:Freedmen) in one night. Following these murders, a new guard is set over Meereen, made up from the Meereenese themselves:(wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) the [Brazen Beasts](wiki:Brazen_Beasts).(wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

The Sons of the Harpy offer a reward: they promise wealth and glory to the man who slays Queen Daenerys, as well as a hundred virgin slave girls.(wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) As the Sons also promise death to anyone who serves Daenerys as well as their families, the members of her new city watch, the [Brazen Beasts](wiki:Brazen_Beasts), hide their true identities by wearing masks.(wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2)

Daenerys demands hostages from the noble families of Meereen.(wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) However, when the time comes, Daenerys refuses to have her hostages executed, having grown fond of them.(wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Following the advice of [Galazza Galare](wiki:Galazza_Galare), Daenerys agreed to a pact with [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq): if he can make the Sons of the Harpy halt their killing for ninty days, they shall marry.(wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2) After twenty-six days without a murder, [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) discusses with Daenerys how Hizdahr has been able to stop the killing, wondering if he himself is "[the Harpy](wiki:The_Harpy)", who leads the Sons. Daenerys, however, wonders whether there even is a Harpy, as questioning the captive Sons did not yield any results.(wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1)

Following ninety days of peace, Daenerys and Hizdahr are wed.(wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy.cite_ref-Radwd43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) However, following a failed poisoning attempt, during which only [Belwas](wiki:Belwas) was injured, Daenerys departs Meereen on the back of her dragon [Drogon](wiki:Drogon).(wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy.cite_ref-Radwd52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) [Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) arrests Hizdahr's confectioner, who admits to having poisoned [the locusts](wiki:Poisoned_locusts), claiming that the Sons of the Harpy had taken his daughter. As Daenerys survived, the Sons killed the daughter, and returned her to her father in nine pieces.(wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Skahaz is still convinced that Hizdahr is the Harpy, but he and [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) agree to take Hizdahr into custody, and not to harm him until he is proven guilty of playing a role in the poisoning attempt.(wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)

Following Hizdahr's arrest,(wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy.cite_ref-Radwd67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) the Sons of the Harpy resume their shadow war, killing three people the first night, nine the second night, and twenty-nine the third night. One more is killed during the day. According to [Galazza Galare](wiki:Galazza_Galare), the killings will stop when Hizdahr is freed.(wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
