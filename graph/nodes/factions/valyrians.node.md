## Appearances & Description

Valyrians are famous for having [silver-gold](wiki:Category:Characters_with_silver_hair) hair and [violet-purple eyes](wiki:Purple_eyes), characteristics not found amongst any other peoples of the world. This can vary from white to silver-gold to blond hair, and from lilac, to deep purple, and pale blue eyes. An aquiline nose is also common among Valyrians.(wiki:Valyrians.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) The most noble of Valyrians were considered strikingly, some say inhumanly,(wiki:Valyrians.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) beautiful.(wiki:Valyrians.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The species of lemur living in the [Forest of Qohor](wiki:Forest_of_Qohor) sharing similar physical features is called [Little Valyrian](wiki:Little_Valyrian).(wiki:Valyrians.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_qohor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Origins

Valyrians were originally a civilization sheltered in the [Fourteen Flames](wiki:Fourteen_Flames), a ring of volcanoes on the [Valyrian peninsula](wiki:Valyrian_peninsula).(wiki:Valyrians.cite_ref-6)(wiki:Valyrians.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_ancient_history:_the_rise_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Valyrian stories claim that [dragons](wiki:Dragon) originated in their lands, although tales from [Asshai](wiki:Asshai) claim the beasts were brought to Valyria from the [Shadow Lands](wiki:Shadow_Lands).(wiki:Valyrians.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_rise_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) The Valyrians tamed the dragons with [magic](wiki:Magic), taught by a vanished people according to the Asshai'i,(wiki:Valyrians.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_rise_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) and made the beasts into devastating weapons of war.(wiki:Valyrians.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) With the aid of dragons, the Valyrians established the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold).(wiki:Valyrians.cite_ref-Ragot34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Valyrians survive in [Lys](wiki:Lys), where even the [smallfolk](wiki:Smallfolk) have the pale skin, silver-gold hair, and [purple](wiki:Purple_eyes), lilac, and pale blue eyes of the [dragonlords](wiki:Dragonlords) of old,(wiki:Valyrians.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_the_quarrelsome_daughters:_myr.2C_lys.2C_and_tyrosh.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) as well as in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis), among the [Old Blood](wiki:Old_Blood), which traces its descent to the [Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) itself.(wiki:Valyrians.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_volantis.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Valyrians.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Other survivors are the members of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen), the sole surviving family of dragonlords, as well as those of [Velaryon](wiki:House_Velaryon) and [Celtigar](wiki:House_Celtigar).
Due to the practice of the [first night](wiki:First_night), Valyrian features were also passed unto the so-called [dragonseeds](wiki:Dragonseeds).
Though unrelated to the Valyrians, the members of [House Dayne](wiki:House_Dayne), an ancient Westerosi noble house, also possess violet eyes.
