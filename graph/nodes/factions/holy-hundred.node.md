## Organization

The Holy Hundred are led by Ser [Bonifer Hasty](wiki:Bonifer_Hasty), are well-disciplined, and ride tall grey [geldings](wiki:Horse).(wiki:Holy_Hundred.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Because of their spotless reputation, Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) once joked that Bonifer had [gelded](wiki:Eunuch) the riders as well.(wiki:Holy_Hundred.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) recognizes them as being as well-disciplined as any soldiers in the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Holy_Hundred.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

Ser [Bonifer Hasty](wiki:Bonifer_Hasty) and the Holy Hundred join King [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) at the beginning of the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Holy_Hundred.cite_ref-Rawoiafbonifer_hasty.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) Upon Renly's [death at Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)), they switch loyalties to King [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon). The Holy Hundred lose fourteen men fighting on behalf of Stannis at the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), bringing their number down to eighty-six.(wiki:Holy_Hundred.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) After the battle, Bonifer and his soldiers submit to King [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Holy_Hundred.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Feast for Crows

The Holy Hundred join Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s force which is sent to bring an end to fighting in the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands). They are assigned to garrison [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) when Bonifer the Good is named castellan in Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish)'s absence. Jaime thinks they make a lovely sight as they wheel and prance their horses.(wiki:Holy_Hundred.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)
