## Origins

In [56 AC](wiki:56_AC), the Adventurers were caught between a great fire that swept across the [Disputed Lands](wiki:Disputed_Lands), fanned by strong winds and fed by dry grasses on one side, and a [Tyroshi](wiki:Tyrosh) host under the command of the [Archon of Tyrosh](wiki:Archon_of_Tyrosh), whom they were fighting, on the other side. Most of the Adventurers ultimately chose to die fighting the Tyroshi rather than be burned alive. Not a single man of the Adventurers survived.(wiki:Adventurers.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
