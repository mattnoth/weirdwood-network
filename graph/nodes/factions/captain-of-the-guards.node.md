## Edges


## Origins

Ser [Merrell Bullock](wiki:Merrell_Bullock) was the commander of the castle garrison at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) during the reign of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1) In [54 AC](wiki:54_AC), after [Elissa Farman](wiki:Elissa_Farman) stole three [dragon eggs](wiki:Dragon_egg) from the Dragonstone hatcheries, he was relieved of his position by Princess [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aenys_I)). When [Androw Farman](wiki:Androw_Farman) asked if he could be made commander of the garrison in Ser Merrell's place, Rhaena and her [ladies](wiki:Lady-in-waiting) burst out laughing.(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), King [Aegon II Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_II_Targaryen) [retook Dragonstone](wiki:Fall_of_Dragonstone) from his half-sister, [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen). His men hanged the captain of the guard from the battlements, alongside Ser [Robert Quince](wiki:Robert_Quince), [Grand Maester](wiki:Grand_Maester) [Gerardys](wiki:Gerardys), the [master-at-arms](wiki:Master-at-arms), and the [steward](wiki:Steward) of the castle.(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Ser [Tommard Heddle](wiki:Tommard_Heddle) commanded the garrison at [Whitewalls](wiki:Whitewalls) during the [Second Blackfyre Rebellion](wiki:Second_Blackfyre_Rebellion).(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
Ser [Ilyn Payne](wiki:Ilyn_Payne), Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s captain of the guards during his time as [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) at King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen)'s court, had his [tongue torn out with hot pincers](wiki:Tongue_mutilation) for saying that it was Tywin who truly ruled the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf_the_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Ilyn later became [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s [King's Justice](wiki:King's_Justice).(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

At the start of the series, [Jory Cassel](wiki:Jory_Cassel) has been serving as [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell)'s captain of the guard for more than eight years.(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Ragot37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Jory goes south with Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) when he is appointed Robert's Hand, and [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) selects [Hallis Mollen](wiki:Hallis_Mollen) as his replacement.(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Ragot14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3) Jory is eventually slain in the streets of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) by [House Lannister guards](wiki:House_Lannister_guards) under orders from Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Jory is replaced as Ned's captain of the guards by [Alyn](wiki:Alyn).(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) However, Alyn leaves the capital with the force led by Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) to bring Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) to [justice](wiki:Laws_and_justice_of_the_Seven_Kingdoms), and is replaced by [Tomard](wiki:Tomard).(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) When Eddard is [arrested](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark) in the throne room of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), Tomard is slain by [gold cloaks](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing).(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
Ser [Vardis Egen](wiki:Vardis_Egen), the captain of the guards at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), is killed by [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) in [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister)'s [trial by combat](wiki:Trial_by_combat).(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Ragot40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
Hallis Mollen goes south with Robb Stark when he leaves Winterfell to fight the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), carrying the banner of [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark).(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) During the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood), Hallis personally commands the guards protecting [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark), after asking for the honor.(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
[Igon Vyrwel](wiki:Igon_Vyrwel) serves as the captain of the guards for [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) at [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden), Ser [Robin Ryger](wiki:Robin_Ryger) serves [House Tully](wiki:House_Tully) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), and [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah) serves [House Martell](wiki:House_Martell) at [Sunspear](wiki:Sunspear).(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Ragotappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

When Tyrion Lannister is appointed acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), he names Bronn as the captain of his personal guards.(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Racok17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
Hallis Mollen guards Catelyn when she goes south to meet [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) and negotiate a possible alliance.(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
[Vylarr](wiki:Vylarr), the captain of the [red cloaks](wiki:House_Lannister_guards) in King's Landing, is sent to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), leading an escort of one hundred men for the envoy, Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey). Vylarr is unaware that as part of Tyrion's [plan to free his brother](wiki:Plot_to_free_Jaime_Lannister), Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), the escort also includes a thief, a [poisoner](wiki:Poison), a [mummer](wiki:Mummer), and a murderer.(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) After their arrival, the conspirators temporarily free Jaime, but the Kingslayer and the guards aiding him are soon subdued. Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) hangs the four conspirators and any red cloaks who attempted to aid them, but those who did not (including Vylarr) are imprisoned.(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)
After Catelyn and her escorts return to Riverrun, she orders Hallis to lead the escort transporting the remains of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) to Winterfell, and to bury them in the [crypt of Winterfell](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1)
[Steelshanks Walton](wiki:Steelshanks_Walton) is the captain of the guards for [House Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton). He is among [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton)'s army who [captures](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal) [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal).(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
Ser [Marwyn Belmore](wiki:Marwyn_Belmore) is appointed the new captain of the guards at the Eyrie.(wiki:Captain_of_the_guards.cite_ref-Racokappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)
