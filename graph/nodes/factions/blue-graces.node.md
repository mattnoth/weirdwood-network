## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons

After the murder of [Stalwart Shield](wiki:Stalwart_Shield), Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) gives an order to send men to the [Temple of the Graces](wiki:Temple_of_the_Graces) to ask if any man has come to the Blue Graces with a sword wound.(wiki:Blue_Graces.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

When [Galazza Galare](wiki:Galazza_Galare), the [Green Grace](wiki:Green_Grace) of [Meereen](wiki:Meereen), returns to Daenerys, she is accompanied by three Blue Graces from the temple. They bring news of a sick and wounded rider who arrived on a pale horse. [Grey Worm](wiki:Grey_Worm) adds that he gave orders for the rider to be brought to the Blue Graces. One of the Blue Graces takes up the tale and tells Daenerys that the [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) brought the man to the temple, where they attended to him, though he died within the hour. A Blue Grace named [Ezzara](wiki:Ezzara) informs Daenerys that the man's fever was not brought on by the arrow in his thigh and that it may be that [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) has more to fear than the arrows of the [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai)'i.(wiki:Blue_Graces.cite_ref-Radwd30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

As the [bloody flux](wiki:Bloody_flux) known as the "pale mare" spreads, Daenerys sends healers, including Blue Graces, outside the walls of Meereen to aid the sick, but some of the healers sicken as well.(wiki:Blue_Graces.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

After [Strong Belwas](wiki:Belwas) is poisoned by eating [honeyed locusts](wiki:Poisoned_locusts), he is treated at the temple by Blue Graces.(wiki:Blue_Graces.cite_ref-Radwd55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Blue_Graces.cite_ref-Radwd59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

After Prince [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) is fatally wounded by [Rhaegal](wiki:Rhaegal)'s dragonflame, Barristan summons the Blue Graces four times to attend to Quentyn, but they never come. He wonders if the last of them have been carried off by the pale mare by now.(wiki:Blue_Graces.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
