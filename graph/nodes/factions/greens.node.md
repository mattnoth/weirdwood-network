## Identity

The Greens were the faction supporting King Aegon II Targaryen's claim to the Iron Throne during the Dance of the Dragons (129–131 AC). Named for the green that Queen Alicent Hightower wore at the Great Council of 101 AC, signaling her opposition to Rhaenyra. Their stronghold was King's Landing. Led by House Hightower and supported by lords loyal to Aegon II. Named in contrast to the rival faction, the Blacks (supporters of Rhaenyra Targaryen).
