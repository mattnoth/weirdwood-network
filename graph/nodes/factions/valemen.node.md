### Religion[ ]

The [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), brought by the [Andals](wiki:Andals), is the predominant religion among [lowland](wiki:Lowland) Valemen. Carvings of seven-pointed stars and double-bladed axes, left by the Andal conquerors, can be found in the [Fingers](wiki:Fingers), the [Mountains of the Moon](wiki:Mountains_of_the_Moon), and at the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance).(wiki:Valemen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_norvos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) fostered learning in [septries](wiki:Septry) and [septs](wiki:Sept).(wiki:Valemen.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale:_house_arryn.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) The [mountain clansmen](wiki:Vale_mountain_clans), however, do not follow the Seven.(wiki:Valemen.cite_ref-Ragot42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
