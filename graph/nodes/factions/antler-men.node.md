## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

According to [Varys](wiki:Varys), the Antler Men plot betrayal against King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and intend to abet the fall of the city to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon). They arm several hundred men and plan, once fighting between Joffrey's loyalists and Stannis's forces has begun, to seize the [Old Gate](wiki:Old_Gate) and let the enemy into the city. Master [armorer](wiki:Armorer) [Salloreon](wiki:Salloreon) is a member of the group. [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) signs an order for their arrest.(wiki:Antler_Men.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), captured Antler Men have antlers nailed to their heads and are flung over the walls of King's Landing by the three great trebuchets called the [Three Whores](wiki:Three_Whores).(wiki:Antler_Men.cite_ref-Racok59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Storm of Swords

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) finds out that some of the Antler Men owed considerable sums of money to the crown.(wiki:Antler_Men.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Feast for Crows

[Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) discovers that another of the Antler Men was a cloth merchant who had purchased the position of [Chief Gaoler](wiki:Chief_Gaoler) of the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep)'s dungeons from [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish).(wiki:Antler_Men.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
