## Origins

The Stormbreakers were founded by Ser [Oscar Tully](wiki:Oscar_Tully) in the aftermath of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) and the collapse of the [Triarchy](wiki:Triarchy). At the time, the free company consisted of men from throughout the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Stormbreakers.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_quarrelsome_daughters:_myr.2C_lys.2C_and_tyrosh.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)(wiki:Stormbreakers.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
The company may have been [named after](wiki:Named_after) the victory of the [rivermen](wiki:Rivermen) against the [stormlanders](wiki:Stormlanders) in the [battle of the Kingsroad](wiki:Battle_of_the_Kingsroad), during which [Kermit Tully](wiki:Kermit_Tully), [Lord of Riverrun](wiki:Lord_of_Riverrun) and Oscar's brother, killed [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon), [Lord of Storm's End](wiki:Lord_of_Storm's_End).(wiki:Stormbreakers.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) The victory was all the more important for Oscar as he was [knighted](wiki:Knight) on the battlefield.(wiki:Stormbreakers.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
