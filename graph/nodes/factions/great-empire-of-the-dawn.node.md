## Origins

### God-on-Earth[ ]

The Golden Empire's first ruler was the [God-on-Earth](wiki:God-on-Earth), the only son of the [Lion of Night](wiki:Lion_of_Night) and the [Maiden-Made-of-Light](wiki:Maiden-Made-of-Light). The God-on-Earth is said to have traveled in a [palanquin](wiki:Palanquin) carved from a single [pearl](wiki:Pearl) and carried by a hundred queens, his wives. The God-on-Earth allegedly ruled for ten thousand years until he ascended to the stars to join his forebears.(wiki:Great_Empire_of_the_Dawn.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)

### Successors[ ]

Dominion passed to the God-on-Earth's eldest son, the [Pearl Emperor](wiki:Pearl_Emperor), who ruled for a thousand years. Some believe the [Five Forts](wiki:Five_Forts) were raised by the Pearl Emperor to protect against the Lion of Night and his [demons](wiki:Demon).(wiki:Great_Empire_of_the_Dawn.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
Power then passed to the [Jade Emperor](wiki:Jade_Emperor), the [Tourmaline Emperor](wiki:Tourmaline_Emperor), the [Onyx Emperor](wiki:Onyx_Emperor), the [Topaz Emperor](wiki:Topaz_Emperor), and the [Opal Emperor](wiki:Opal_Emperor). Each reigned for a shorter and more troubled time than the previous emperor, for wild men and beasts pressed the borders of the Empire, lesser kings grew proud and rebellious, and the common people gave themselves to sin.(wiki:Great_Empire_of_the_Dawn.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)

### Downfall[ ]

When the daughter of the Opal Emperor ascended to power as the [Amethyst Empress](wiki:Amethyst_Empress), her envious brother cast her down and proclaimed himself the [Bloodstone Emperor](wiki:Bloodstone_Emperor). The new ruler began a reign of terror and [slavery](wiki:Slavery), in which he practiced dark arts and [necromancy](wiki:Necromancy), took a [tiger-woman](wiki:Tiger-woman) for his bride, [feasted on human flesh](wiki:Cannibalism) and cast down the gods of [Yi Ti](wiki:Yi_Ti) to worship a black stone fallen from the sky. This [Blood Betrayal](wiki:Blood_Betrayal), as it is known in the annals of the [Further East](wiki:Further_East), ushered in the [Long Night](wiki:Long_Night), with the Maiden-Made-of-Light turning her back on the world, while the Lion of Night came forth to punish the wickedness of man.(wiki:Great_Empire_of_the_Dawn.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
The darkness ended when a [great warrior](wiki:Azor_Ahai) rose to lead the virtuous into battle with the sword [Lightbringer](wiki:Lightbringer) in his hand. Light was restored, but the Great Empire was not reborn; the restored world was a broken place where every tribe of men went its own way, fearful of all the others, and war, lust, and murder had endured.(wiki:Great_Empire_of_the_Dawn.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-7)
