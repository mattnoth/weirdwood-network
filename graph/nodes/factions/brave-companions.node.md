## Identity

Brave Companions is a organization.faction from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges

## Origins

According to semi-canon sources, the Brave Companions are not as old as other mercenary companies and were established before [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat) became their leader. Their numbers consist of the scum of the west, and a few from the east and the south. Their previous leader was likely not a [Qohorik](wiki:Qohor), and their next might possibly be a [Lyseni](wiki:Lys), a [Dornishman](wiki:Dornishmen) or even an [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibben).(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-7)

Their diverse ranks are said to include [copper men](wiki:Dothraki) with bells in their braids;(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) lancers astride [black-and-white horses](wiki:Zorse);(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3) bowmen with powdered cheeks;(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) [squat hairy men](wiki:Ibben) with shaggy shields;(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5) [brown-skinned men](wiki:Summer_Isles) in feathered cloaks;(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6) swordsmen with [fantastic forked beards](wiki:Tyroshi) dyed green and purple and silver;(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7) spearmen with [colored scars](wiki:Volantis) that [cover their cheeks](wiki:Slavery);(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-8)(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Myrish](wiki:Myr) crossbowmen,(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1) pale [Lyseni](wiki:Lyseni);(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Rasos3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and dark [Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen).(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Rasos3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Following the [Battle of the Camps](wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps), Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) sends [Vargo Hoat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat), Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane), and Ser [Amory Lorch](wiki:Amory_Lorch) to pillage the [riverlands](wiki:Riverlands) near the [Gods Eye](wiki:Gods_Eye) and the [Red Fork](wiki:Red_Fork) with three hundred horse each.(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Ragot69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Clash of Kings

The Brave Companions are reported to have slain Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-9)

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) observes the sellswords' arrival at [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), where they are housed in the [Widow's Tower](wiki:Widow's_Tower).(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-10) Vargo's men quarrel with Amory's garrison, causing both to be reprimanded by Tywin. On the night they arrive, a fight breaks out between the company and [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) men, resulting in the deaths of Ser [Harys Swyft](wiki:Harys_Swyft)'s squire, two wounded Brave Companions who are later hanged, and one of Lord [Lewys Lydden](wiki:Lewys_Lydden)'s archers who started the fight.(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Racok30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-11)

After Tywin leaves Harrenhal, Vargo secretly aligns with Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) in support of [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), [King in the North](wiki:King_in_the_North). The Brave Companions bring [northmen](wiki:North) led by [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover) and Ser [Aenys Frey](wiki:Aenys_Frey) into Harrenhal as prisoners with the intention of freeing them to attack Amory's garrison from the inside. Separately, Jaqen, Rorge, and Biter help [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) free the northerner prisoners, and Rorge tells Robett that the three men have joined the Brave Companions. With the northmen freed early, the Companions help slaughter the Lannister garrison and open the castle's gates to Roose after the [fall of Harrenhal](wiki:Fall_of_Harrenhal).(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Racok47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Lord Bolton tasks the Brave Companions with rooting out Lannister forces in the surrounding countryside.(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) Vargo, who is to be granted the castle as [Lord of Harrenhal](wiki:Lord_of_Harrenhal) after Roose's eventual departure, divides his men into four bands, giving smaller ones to his trusted captains while he leads the largest one himself.(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) He uses his past affiliation with Lord Tywin to fool Lannister garrisons that are unaware of their betrayal, before seizing them and returning with bags of coin as well as baskets of heads.(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-2) Septon [Utt](wiki:Utt)'s camp is attacked by a pack of wild [wolves](wiki:Wolf) which kills two of their horses.(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Racok64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-3)

### A Storm of Swords

[Iggo](wiki:Iggo), an [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibben) axeman, a [Dornish](wiki:Dornishmen) archer, and a [Lyseni](wiki:Lys) hunt Arya, [Gendry](wiki:Gendry), and [Hot Pie](wiki:Hot_Pie) after they flee Harrenhal, but the four Companions fall prey to a wolf pack led by Arya's direwolf, [Nymeria](wiki:Nymeria_(direwolf)).(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Rasos3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-2) Mummers forage as far south as [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept).(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

Within the [hollow hill](wiki:Hollow_hill), [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) offends the [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) by calling them "brave companions".(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Rasos34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Beric and [Thoros of Myr](wiki:Thoros_of_Myr) lead the brotherhood in attacking a [sept](wiki:Sept) held by a detachment of the Brave Companions which includes, among others, a [Myrman](wiki:Myr), two [Ibbenese](wiki:Ibben), three [Volantenes](wiki:Volantis), and several [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki). Captured Companions, including Utt, are hanged by the brotherhood after this [battle at the burning septry](wiki:Battle_at_the_burning_septry).(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Rasos39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)

Following the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), Vargo grows to suspect that his new [Bolton](wiki:House_Bolton) allies will join the Lannisters. After [Urswyck](wiki:Urswyck)'s group captures Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth), Vargo has [Zollo](wiki:Zollo) chop off Jaime's sword hand to drive a wedge between Tywin and Roose.(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Rasos21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn), a former maester, treats Jaime's stump.(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Rasos31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) The ploy to drive a wedge between Tywin and Roose does not work, however, and Roose soon departs for the [Twins](wiki:Twins) and leaves Harrenhal with Vargo. While attempting to rape the imprisoned Brienne in Harrenhal, Vargo's ear is bitten off and the wound becomes infected.(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

In the aftermath of the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding), Lord Tywin offers to spare any castle which yields, with the exception of Harrenhal; a vengeful Tywin instead orders Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) to put the Brave Companions to the sword.(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Rasos53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) With Lannister reprisal looming and Vargo slowly maddening from a fever, most of the Companions desert the group. By the time Gregor [retakes Harrenhal](wiki:Capture_of_Harrenhal)(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) and kills Vargo, the band has dissolved into smaller groups.(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Rasos62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Feast for Crows

In the aftermath of the war, many Brave Companions become bandits and vagabonds. One particular group led by Rorge burns and slaughters during the [raid on Saltpans](wiki:Raid_on_Saltpans). Because Rorge wears the infamous helm of [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane), these deeds are attributed to the [Hound](wiki:Hound).(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

When Brienne comes across [Timeon](wiki:Timeon), Shagwell, and [Pyg](wiki:Pyg) at the [Whispers](wiki:Whispers), Timeon tells her that Urswyck was heading towards [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown) with a small band. The three Companions are then killed by Brienne.(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) Rorge and Biter are later killed in a confrontation with Brienne at the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn).(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Raffc37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) [Zollo](wiki:Zollo), [Togg Joth](wiki:Togg_Joth), and [Three Toes](wiki:Three_Toes) remain on the run throughout Westeros.(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

Qyburn rises to the position of [master of whisperers](wiki:Master_of_whisperers) for Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) and is allowed to continue his experiments with people Cersei wants rid of.(wiki:Brave_Companions.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
