## Culture

Dosh khaleen are all former [khaleesis](wiki:Khaleesi), the wives of [khals](wiki:Khal) who have died. When a khal passes away, his khaleesi is expected to return to [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak) and join the other widows.(wiki:Dosh_khaleen.cite_ref-Radwd71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
The dosh khaleen are the only permanent residents of Vaes Dothrak.(wiki:Dosh_khaleen.cite_ref-Ragot36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) They enjoy great honor and status amongst the [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) and serve as seers foretelling the future and interpreting omens for their people. The crones are served by [eunuchs](wiki:Eunuch) and [slaves](wiki:Slaves).(wiki:Dosh_khaleen.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
The prospective wives of khals must be presented to the dosh khaleen for acceptance.(wiki:Dosh_khaleen.cite_ref-Ragot11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Merchants give traditional gifts of salt, [silver](wiki:Silver), and seed to the dosh khaleen when they come to Vaes Dothrak to trade.(wiki:Dosh_khaleen.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

After [her wedding](wiki:Wedding_of_Drogo_and_Daenerys_Targaryen) outside [Pentos](wiki:Pentos), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) is taken to [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak) to be introduced to the dosh khaleen. She eats a [stallion heart](wiki:Stallion_heart) before the crones, and they declare that [the son](wiki:Rhaego) of Daenerys and Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo) will be the [stallion who mounts the world](wiki:Stallion_who_mounts_the_world), a great conqueror whose coming is long foretold in [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) prophecies.(wiki:Dosh_khaleen.cite_ref-Ragot46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
After Drogo dies, Daenerys refuses to join the dosh khaleen.(wiki:Dosh_khaleen.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Venturing south into the [red waste](wiki:Red_waste) with her small [khalasar](wiki:Khalasar), Daenerys thinks that other Dothraki hosts would force her to join the dosh khaleen if she were to be captured by them.(wiki:Dosh_khaleen.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
While in the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying) in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth), Daenerys has a vision of a line of naked crones creeping from [a great lake](wiki:Womb_of_the_World) and bowing before her.(wiki:Dosh_khaleen.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

According to [Ghael](wiki:Ghael) of [Astapor](wiki:Astapor), the [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai)'i have sent riders to Vaes Dothrak to bring a khalasar upon Daenerys at [Meereen](wiki:Meereen).(wiki:Dosh_khaleen.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
