## Origins

The Wolf Pack was founded by [Hallis Hornwood](wiki:Hallis_Hornwood) and [Timotty Snow](wiki:Timotty_Snow) in the aftermath of the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons) and the collapse of the [Triarchy](wiki:Triarchy), when the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) of [Lys](wiki:Lys), [Myr](wiki:Myr), and [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) were hiring sellswords in anticipation of the [Daughters' War](wiki:Daughters'_War). At the time, the new free company consisted only of [northmen](wiki:Northmen),(wiki:Wolf_Pack.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) who are often dubbed "[wolves](wiki:Wolf)" on account of the [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) sigil of the rulers of the [north](wiki:North), [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark).
