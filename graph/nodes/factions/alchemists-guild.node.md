## Origins

The ancient(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Alchemists' Guild was once powerful, but they have been supplanted by [maesters](wiki:Maesters) almost everywhere in recent centuries.(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-7)

### Aegon IV Targaryen

King [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen), who dreamed of conquering [Dorne](wiki:Dorne), commanded the Guild of Alchemists to build him "[dragons](wiki:Dragon)". Thus, the dubious alchemists constructed [massive devices](wiki:Dragons_(mechanical)) fitted with pumps that shot jets of [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire). One of the engines went up in flames in the [kingswood](wiki:Kingswood) and soon all seven burned, taking a quarter of the forest with them and forcing Aegon to abandon his scheme.(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_iv.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### Daeron II Targaryen

Because of the [Great Spring Sickness](wiki:Great_Spring_Sickness), Lord [Brynden Rivers](wiki:Brynden_Rivers), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), had [pyromancers](wiki:Pyromancer) burn the corpses that had been piled in the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit). A quarter of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) burned in the aftermath.(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### Aegon V Targaryen

Pyromancers are mentioned in the fragments of [Gyldayn](wiki:Gyldayn)'s writings which survived the [tragedy at Summerhall](wiki:Tragedy_at_Summerhall).(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aegon_v.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### Aerys II Targaryen

The alchemists enjoyed a period of increased influence during the reign of King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen), visiting his court more frequently as the king's fascination with [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) increased.(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) When Aerys tasked them with driving off winter at the start of [282 AC](wiki:282_AC), the wisdoms burned green fires along the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep)'s walls for a moon's turn.(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_fall_of_the_dragons:_the_year_of_the_false_spring.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

A few years before his reign ended, the Mad King also began using fire to burn traitors, murderers, and plotters;(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2) one such victim was Lord [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark).(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) [Rossart](wiki:Rossart), the grand master of the guild, supervised the executions, and the king rewarded him with the title of [Lord](wiki:Lord) and added him to the [small council](wiki:Small_council).(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-3)

During [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), King Aerys tasked Lord Rossart and two other wisdoms, [Garigus](wiki:Garigus) and [Belis](wiki:Belis), with creating a vast quantity of wildfire for his plan to destroy King's Landing, should the war be lost. When Lord [Qarlton Chelsted](wiki:Qarlton_Chelsted), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), opposed Aerys's [wildfire plot](wiki:Wildfire_plot) and renounced his duties in disgust, the Mad King had him burned to death with wildfire and made Rossart as his next and final Hand.(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) This was one of the reasons why Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) slew both Lord Rossart and King Aerys during the [Sack of King's Landing](wiki:Sack_of_King's_Landing). Days after the sack, Jaime hunted down Garigus and Belis and killed them as well.(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)

The acolytes who remained at the Guild were inexperienced in destroying the wildfire. Some jars remained in the vaults of the Guild, while others were lost and forgotten.(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-8)

When Wisdom [Hallyne](wiki:Hallyne) asked why their spells seemed to not be as effective as their scrolls promised, Wisdom [Pollitor](wiki:Pollitor) explained that [magic](wiki:Magic) began to leave the [known world](wiki:Known_world) when the [last dragon](wiki:Last_dragon) died.(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)

## Organization

A full member of the Alchemists' Guild is called a *[wisdom](wiki:Wisdom)*. There are also apprentices and [acolytes](wiki:Acolyte_(Alchemists'_Guild)) to assist them.(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) The order is led by a wisdom known as the [grand master](wiki:Grand_master).(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

The guild was powerful in its earlier years, and they once claimed to be able to transmute metals.(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2) By the reign of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), however, their abilities and influence have been greatly diminished. Now only a few of the old order remain.(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

Although they still hint at possessing vast secret stores of knowledge, the [pyromancers](wiki:Pyromancer) of the guild only demonstrate the skill to create [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire), which they call *the substance*. The wildfire is created in the [Guildhall of the Alchemists](wiki:Guildhall_of_the_Alchemists), located on the [Street of the Sisters](wiki:Street_of_the_Sisters) close to [Visenya's Hill](wiki:Visenya's_Hill) in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-4) The hall is an imposing labyrinth of cold, black stone.(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Rawoiafguild_of_alchemists.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-5) Great care is taken when producing wildfire. Trained acolytes prepare the product in bare stone cells, and once a jar is ready, it is immediately removed by an apprentice and brought to the vaults.(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-6)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

The alchemists find a cache of two hundred jars of [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) under the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) in [298 AC](wiki:298_AC)(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-9) and then three hundred jars under the [Dragonpit](wiki:Dragonpit) in [299 AC](wiki:299_AC).(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)

During the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings), Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) commissions ten thousand jars of wildfire for the defenses of [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Racok3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), meets with Wisdom [Hallyne](wiki:Hallyne) in the [Guildhall of the Alchemists](wiki:Guildhall_of_the_Alchemists).(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-10) Hallyne later reports an unexplained increase in the effectiveness of their spells, noting it has not been as easy since the time of the [dragons](wiki:Dragons);(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-2) unbeknownst to them, [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) has three living dragons in [Qarth](wiki:Qarth).(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

Tyrion utilizes the alchemists' wildfire during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater).(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Racok59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Storm of Swords

During the [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding) of King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) and [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell), four [pyromancers](wiki:Pyromancer) entertain by conjuring beasts of living flame which tear at each other with fiery claws.(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

### A Feast for Crows

Lord [Hallyne](wiki:Hallyne) and his Guild of Alchemists are employed by Cersei to burn down the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand).(wiki:Alchemists'_Guild.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
