## Identity

House Stark guards is a organization.faction from the AWOIAF wiki.

## Edges


## Origins

Before the outbreak of [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion), Lord [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark) went south to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) with two hundred of his best men(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) since King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) had imprisoned his heir, [Brandon](wiki:Brandon_Stark). Father and son were executed by the Mad King,(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Racok55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) and none of their men returned to the [north](wiki:North).(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

## Appearances & Description

Guardsmen at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) wear mailed shirts and coifs and wield [steel](wiki:Steel).(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s guards in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) wear [silver](wiki:Silver) mail,(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) steel caps,(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and long cloaks of heavy grey wool decorated with white satin borders.(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) Their cloaks are pinned with hands of beaten silver, as Ned is the new [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

[Jory Cassel](wiki:Jory_Cassel) serves as the captain of Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark)'s household guard.(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) With the retinue of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) approaching, Ned sends Jory with an honor guard to meet them on the [kingsroad](wiki:Kingsroad) and escort them to [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell).(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Ned brings the best(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) fifty of his men, a quarter of Winterfell's normal guard,(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) under the command of Jory.(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-2) [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark) names [Hallis Mollen](wiki:Hallis_Mollen) the new captain of the guards at Winterfell,(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) and Robb often drills the remaining guards.(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)

The bones of [Lady](wiki:Lady_(direwolf)) are brought from [Darry](wiki:Darry) to Winterfell by four guards.(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

Ned has twenty Stark guards under the command of [Alyn](wiki:Alyn) assist the [gold cloaks](wiki:Gold_cloaks) in keeping order in King's Landing.(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) Jory, [Alyn](wiki:Alyn), and [Harwin](wiki:Harwin) participate in the [Hand's tourney](wiki:Hand's_tourney).(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) Ned invites [Anguy](wiki:Anguy) to join his guards, but the archer refuses.(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot30.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

After Jory is killed while fighting [red cloaks](wiki:Red_cloaks) in the streets of King's Landing,(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) Ned names Alyn his new captain.(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0) Twenty guards led by Alyn join Lord [Beric Dondarrion](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion)'s force to bring Ser [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) to justice,(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) and Alyn carries the Stark banner when they depart the capital.(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) The injured Ned remains in King's Landing and names [Tomard](wiki:Tomard) his new captain.(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-1)

In the aftermath of King Robert's death, Fat Tom and the other Stark guards in King's Landing [are killed](wiki:Arrest_of_Eddard_Stark) when [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt)'s gold cloaks and Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) ally with [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) instead of Ned.(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Three men wearing the grey cloaks of House Stark guard the *[Wind Witch](wiki:Wind_Witch)*, the ship which Ned had hoped would take his daughters [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) and [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark) from King's Landing. The incognito Arya does not recognize the three men, however, and leaves before her identity can be discovered.(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)

When Robb leads the [northern](wiki:North) host south from Winterfell in response to Ned's imprisonment, Hal Mollen wields the Stark banner on a standard of grey ash.(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) Most of Winterfell's guards are part of the host.(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1) Hal protects [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) during the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood), while Robb has a [personal guard](wiki:Robb_Stark's_personal_guard) of thirty nobles.(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Ragot63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

### A Clash of Kings

When Robb sends Ser [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey) to King's Landing with his peace offer, among his demands are the return of the remains of Ned and his father's slain guards. Cleos travels under a peace banner with thirty of Robb's men.(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) They are attacked twice by [broken men](wiki:Broken_men) during the journey, with three guards killed and six wounded.(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) Once they arrive at King's landing, [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) forbids the northmen from entering the city to prevent Robb from learning about conditions in the capital.(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Racok20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-1) When Cleos and Robb's men return to Riverrun with the [Iron Throne](wiki:Iron_Throne)'s own terms, Tyrion has them joined by one hundred [House Lannister guards](wiki:House_Lannister_guards) led by [Vylarr](wiki:Vylarr).(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Racok25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

Meanwhile, Hallis commands the twenty Winterfell men, including [Shadd](wiki:Shadd), who are part of Catelyn's escort when she travels to [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)'s camp at [Bitterbridge](wiki:Bitterbridge).(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) Cat later tasks with Hal with a small honor guard to protect the [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters) bringing Ned's bones from [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) back to Winterfell.(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Racok39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

[Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark), the Stark at Winterfell while Robb is away, notices a number of new men at Winterfell during the [harvest feast](wiki:Harvest_feast).(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Racok21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) According to [Meera Reed](wiki:Meera_Reed), the guardsmen talk to each other in [their hall](wiki:Guards_Hall) about Bran's shouting in the night.(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Racok28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) and a small group of [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) kill or capture Bran's inexperienced guards in the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell).(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Racok46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) After the [sack of Winterfell](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell) by [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow)'s men from the [Dreadfort](wiki:Dreadfort), Bran emerges from the [crypt](wiki:Crypt_of_Winterfell) and finds the body of [Poxy Tym](wiki:Poxy_Tym) among the slain.(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Racok69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

### A Storm of Swords

Harwin recounts to Arya how Alyn and many others from Beric's party were killed by the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) during the [battle at the Mummer's Ford](wiki:Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford).(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Rasos17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)

Robb and most of his host are killed during the [Red Wedding](wiki:Red_Wedding).(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)(wiki:House_Stark_guards.cite_ref-Rasos52.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)
