## Culture

Crackclaws are regarded as half-wild by the rest of the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms). They are distrustful of outsiders and their blood feuds run deep and dark.(wiki:Crackclaws.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
When fighting, Crackclaws employ their knowledge of the bogs and forests and retreat into caverns when necessary.(wiki:Crackclaws.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)

## Origins

Crackclaws are descendants of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men), ruled by lords established on each valley of Crackclaw Point. They resisted attempts by the petty kings of [House Darklyn](wiki:House_Darklyn) and [House Mooton](wiki:House_Mooton) to annex the peninsula into their domains. During their [migration](wiki:Arrival_of_the_Andals) to [Westeros](wiki:Westeros), the [Andals](wiki:Andals) attempted to conquer the Point but failed due to the resistance of the locals and the hostile terrain. Thus the Andals opted to intermarry their daughters to the local lords.(wiki:Crackclaws.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
Champions such as Lord [Lucifer Hardy](wiki:Lucifer_Hardy), the [Brothers Brune](wiki:Brothers_Brune), [Old Crackbones](wiki:Old_Crackbones) and Ser [Clarence Crabb](wiki:Clarence_Crabb) would bring peace among the Crackclaws, but these wouldn't last past their lifetimes.(wiki:Crackclaws.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)
[House Celtigar](wiki:House_Celtigar) of [Claw Isle](wiki:Claw_Isle) have also tried to conquer the region and failed.(wiki:Crackclaws.cite_ref-Raffc20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
During the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), half-wild Crackclaws of [House Crabb](wiki:House_Crabb) and [House Brune](wiki:House_Brune) joined with [House Mooton](wiki:House_Mooton) and [House Celtigar](wiki:House_Celtigar) to [retake](wiki:Retaking_of_Rook's_Rest) [Rook's Rest](wiki:Rook's_Rest) for the blacks.(wiki:Crackclaws.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
