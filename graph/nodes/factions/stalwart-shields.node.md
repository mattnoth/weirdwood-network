## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The company's members are all former slaves, known as [freedmen](wiki:Freedmen), who are loyal to [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen). Their first commander is [Mollono Yos Dob](wiki:Mollono_Yos_Dob), but he eventually dies from the [pale mare](wiki:Pale_mare), and [Tal Toraq](wiki:Tal_Toraq) is now the new commander. The company appears to have been [named after](wiki:Named_after) [Stalwart Shield](wiki:Stalwart_Shield), an [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) soldier who was the first of Daenerys's fighting men to be murdered by the [Sons of the Harpy](wiki:Sons_of_the_Harpy).(wiki:Stalwart_Shields.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)(wiki:Stalwart_Shields.cite_ref-Radwd70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
