## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

During the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging), several builders accompany the rangers led by Lord Commander [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont). A score of builders is set to clearing brush, digging latrines, and untying the bundles of fire-hardened stakes upon the arrival at the [Fist of the First Men](wiki:Fist_of_the_First_Men).(wiki:Builders.cite_ref-Racok34.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Storm of Swords

With Lord Commander Mormont leading two hundred brothers north of the [Wall](wiki:Wall), most of the four hundred men who remain at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) are builders and [stewards](wiki:Stewards).(wiki:Builders.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Most of the four hundred men who remained at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) when Lord Commander Jeor Mormont led two hundred brothers out for the [great ranging](wiki:Great_ranging) are builders and [stewards](wiki:Stewards).(wiki:Builders.cite_ref-Rasos41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)

When the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk) army of [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) [attack the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), several of the builders argue that the ice and broken beams, the result of [the attack](wiki:Attack_on_Castle_Black) carried out by a hundred [Thenns](wiki:Thenns) and some raiders, should be left to block the inner gate, as they believe it can serve as an obstacle for Mance.(wiki:Builders.cite_ref-Rasos64.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Feast for Crows

The new lord commander, [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), has the builders working hard to repair the staircase leading up to the top of the Wall.(wiki:Builders.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

Most of the builders at [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) agree with [Bowen Marsh](wiki:Bowen_Marsh) to close the gates and seal them up with stone and ice.(wiki:Builders.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

Lord Commander Snow offers to lend builders to [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) to restore the [Nightfort](wiki:Nightfort), once the stair to the top of the Wall is done.(wiki:Builders.cite_ref-Radwd3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) The [First Builder](wiki:First_Builder), [Othell Yarwyck](wiki:Othell_Yarwyck), eventually leads builders off to the Nightfort, and attempts to restore it.(wiki:Builders.cite_ref-Radwd35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) While they are able to restore most of the keep and place a new roof over the kitchens,(wiki:Builders.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) most of it still remains in ruins.(wiki:Builders.cite_ref-Radwd44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) According to Othell, restorations could be done much faster if there were more builders available.(wiki:Builders.cite_ref-Radwd39.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)
