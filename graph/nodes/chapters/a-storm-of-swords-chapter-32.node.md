## Narrative Arc

[Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) visits [Symon Silvertongue](wiki:Symon_Silvertongue), and thinks to pay him to leave [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing). But the singer wishes to join the tournament of singers that will take place during [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon)’s wedding. He blackmails Tyrion by indicating that he will tell about his relation with [Shae](wiki:Shae). Tyrion promises him that one of the other singers will be removed, and [Bronn](wiki:Bronn) will visit him in three days' time. Outside, Tyrion orders Bronn to kill Symon.

Later, [Tyrion's father](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) shows him the two new [Valyrian](wiki:Valyrian_steel) swords he had made for Joffrey and [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister). Master [Tobho Mott](wiki:Tobho_Mott) reworked the metal, and the color of the blade on each sword is black and red. [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) has not had a Valyrian sword in hundreds of years, and try as he might, Tywin could never get any of the lesser houses to sell theirs. Where the steel for these new swords came from is not revealed. Tywin tells him that he must consummate his marriage to [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark), and that [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) rejected the marriage proposal between [Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) and [Willas](wiki:Willas_Tyrell), probably because the [Queen of Thorns](wiki:Olenna_Redwyne) told him to.

[Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) brings dire tidings from [the Wall](wiki:The_Wall), and that Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) fears Lord [Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) is dead. Tywin tells Pycelle to send a veiled message that no men shall be sent to the Wall unless [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt) is considered for the post of [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch). Tyrion thinks to himself that he should have had Slynt and Pycelle put to death when he had the chance.
