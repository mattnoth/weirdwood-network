## Narrative Arc

As their ship *[Storm Dancer](wiki:Storm_Dancer)* prepares to dock in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) is joined on deck by Ser [Rodrik Cassel](wiki:Rodrik_Cassel). Catelyn notes that Rodrik looks much better than he has for days. Rodrik has been chronically seasick throughout the voyage, forcing him to shave his befouled cheek whiskers.

Catelyn personally pays each oarmen a [silver stag](wiki:Currency). After Captain [Moreo Tumitis](wiki:Moreo_Tumitis) leaves them, Catelyn and Rodrik discuss how to go about their investigation of the [Valyrian steel dagger](wiki:Littlefinger's_blade). Catelyn suggests speaking to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep)'s master-at-arms, Ser [Aron Santagar](wiki:Aron_Santagar), who may recognize the weapon. Rodrik reminds Catelyn that they must be careful she is not recognized. This brings Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish), the king's [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin), to Catelyn's mind. Petyr—still known by the nickname "Littlefinger" given to him by Catelyn's brother [Edmure](wiki:Edmure_Tully)—was raised with her at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) as her father [Hoster's](wiki:Hoster_Tully) ward. They had been close until Petyr had challenged Catelyn's betrothed, [Brandon Stark](wiki:Brandon_Stark), for her hand. Brandon had only spared Petyr because Catelyn had pleaded for his life. After his recovery, Petyr had been sent away and Catelyn has not seen him since. Rodrik proposes that he go to the Red Keep alone because, without his whiskers, he is unrecognizable even to the few who might know him.

After they disembark, Catelyn and Rodrik move into a modest [old inn on Eel Alley](wiki:Old_inn_on_Eel_Alley) suggested by Moreo. After Rodrik leaves, Catelyn tries to get some sleep but is woken by the [City Watch of King's Landing](wiki:City_Watch_of_King's_Landing). They show her a seal depicting a [mockingbird](wiki:Mockingbird) in grey [wax](wiki:Wax), the sigil of Petyr Baelish. As she prepares to accompany the soldiers (who do not recognize her), Catelyn wonders how Petyr knew she was in King’s Landing and settles on *Storm Dancer's* captain as the culprit.

Catelyn is escorted to a tower room occupied by Petyr. When Catelyn asks how he knew she was in the city, Petyr replies that [Varys](wiki:Varys) knows everything that happens in the city. He does admit, however, that Varys does not know the reason for Catelyn's visit. Catelyn lies that she merely yearned to see her husband and daughters. Petyr does not believe this for a moment and asks Catelyn to let him help.

Before Catelyn can answer they are joined by Varys. After profuse courtesies, Varys asks to see the dagger. Catelyn is astounded by Varys's knowledge of things no one could possibly know and produces the dagger for inspection. Upon examining the weapon, Petyr reveals that it once belonged to him until he lost it betting on Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) in the [tourney on Prince Joffrey's name day](wiki:Tourney_on_Prince_Joffrey's_name_day). Petyr explains that when Jaime was unhorsed by Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell), Petyr's dagger was won by [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).
