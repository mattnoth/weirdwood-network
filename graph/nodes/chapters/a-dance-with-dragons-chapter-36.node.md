## Narrative Arc

[Daenerys](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) visits the thousands of [Astapori](wiki:Astapor) camped outside [Meereen](wiki:Meereen). She finds them sick and dying of the [bloody flux](wiki:Bloody_flux), and walks among them with her [Bloodriders](wiki:Bloodrider). She does not fear catching the bloody flux because she thinks that the blood of the dragon does not get sick. She sends [Aggo](wiki:Aggo) back to Meereen to fetch [Grey Worm](wiki:Grey_Worm) and 50 [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied) to help wash and feed the living and burn the dead. Grey Worm asks for permission to allow some Unsullied to bathe in the sea to be purified by their goddess.

Daenerys returns to her pyramid in Meereen, where [Irri](wiki:Irri) and [Jhiqui](wiki:Jhiqui) are arguing about [Rakharo](wiki:Rakharo). She takes a much needed bath and dons a [tokar](wiki:Tokar) to dine with [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq). [Reznak mo Reznak](wiki:Reznak_mo_Reznak) and [Galazza Galare](wiki:Galazza_Galare) inform her that her body must be inspected by the man's house before the wedding. Daenerys first refuses to follow this tradition but is then convinced to do so. She is asked to open the fighting pits as a wedding gift but refuses to do that.

While waiting for dinner, Hizdahr informs her that [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) will make peace for a fee and the freedom to resume slaving, but then [Ser Barristan](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) notifies her that the Yunkai'i are on the march and [Daario Naharis](wiki:Daario_Naharis) has returned. Daenerys excuses herself from dinner and meets with Daario.

Daario informs her that several of Yunkai's sellswords defected to him,(wiki:A_Dance_with_Dragons-Chapter_36.cite_ref-1) but [Brown Ben Plumm](wiki:Brown_Ben_Plumm) and the [Second Sons](wiki:Second_Sons) in turn went over to Yunkai. Daenerys commands the gates closed (with the Astapori outside), and gives in to her desires and beds Daario.
