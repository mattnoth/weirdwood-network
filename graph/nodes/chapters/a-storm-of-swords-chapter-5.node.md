## Narrative Arc

[Davos](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) has washed up on the shore of a small island among the [spears of the Merling King](wiki:Spears_of_the_Merling_King) in [Blackwater Bay](wiki:Blackwater_Bay). He remembers swimming beneath the chain boom during the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), and then getting caught by the current and gulping seawater. But, somehow, he is alive, and believes [the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) still have some use for him. Despairing over the loss of four of his sons in the sea battle, he contemplates dying on the island, but decides instead to live. A [Lysene](wiki:Lys) ship rescues him, one of [Salladhor Saan](wiki:Salladhor_Saan)'s fleet.
