## Narrative Arc

[Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Tully) reflects on how she has always done her duty, remembering that she never comforted [Littlefinger](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) after [Brandon](wiki:Brandon_Stark) had injured him, nor did she bid him farewell when [her father](wiki:Hoster_Tully) sent him off. Later, Maester [Vyman](wiki:Vyman) shows her a letter from Lord [Elwood Meadows](wiki:Elwood_Meadows), the new castellan at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End). The man does not make note of [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm), and she wonders again why [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) is so interested in the boy, thinking he might mean to use the boy’s appearance as proof of [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon)’s ill heritage. Catelyn is considering the nature of bastards and their fathers, and recalls [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton)’s recent missive where he calls his bastard, [Ramsay Snow](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), a boy of tainted blood and that he counts himself "well rid of him". Bolton states that he hopes [Robb](wiki:Robb_Stark) will weigh his capture of [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal) against the crimes of his bastard son.

Catelyn is drawn away as fighting begins across the [Red Fork](wiki:Trident), yet each time Lord [Tywin](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)’s troops attempt to cross, [Edmure](wiki:Edmure_Tully)’s men repel them, raining arrows down upon them as they attempt to ford the river. Catelyn is unimpressed despite the victory her brother has won against one of the most famous battlefield commanders alive. She sends wine to [Cleos Frey](wiki:Cleos_Frey), and then attempts to gain information from him, but the man knows little of use. All she learns is that Ser Cleos saw only [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) at court, and she looked drawn; Catelyn muses that either Cersei is keeping [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark) hidden for fear of what she might say or do, or her younger daughter is dead. Catelyn does consider [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) a man who might be trusted at his word, despite what has transpired between the two of them. She then learns the results of the great victory, that [Leo Lefford](wiki:Leo_Lefford) has drowned, [Gregor Clegane](wiki:Gregor_Clegane) was badly wounded, and the great knight [Strongboar](wiki:Lyle_Crakehall) taken captive. Yet despite the fact that they were winning, she is still afraid.
