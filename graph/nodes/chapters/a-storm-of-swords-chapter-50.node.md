## Narrative Arc

[Sandor](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) and [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark) arrive at [the Twins](wiki:The_Twins) where Sandor hopes to ransom Arya to [her family](wiki:House_Stark). To get there The Hound had robbed a farmer of his [wagon](wiki:Wagon) and casks to disguise themselves as simple farmers who have come to bring pork to the feast. The castle is closed so they are sent to the pavilions instead where the common soldiers are. Arya recognizes a few coats of arms but there is nobody she knows. The soldiers drink to the health of Queen [Jeyne](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling) whose name is unfamiliar to Arya. As Sandor wants to ransom Arya they head to the castle.
