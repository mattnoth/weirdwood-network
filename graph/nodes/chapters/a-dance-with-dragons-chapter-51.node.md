## Narrative Arc

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) is with some of the [spearwives](wiki:Spearwife), [Rowan](wiki:Rowan) as well as [Abel](wiki:Mance_Rayder). He tells them that it is madness to try to escape, as [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Bolton) will chase them and hunt them down with his [dogs](wiki:Dog). Abel replies that [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) must be close by the sounds of the horns, and it is unlikely Ramsay would ride out into that. Theon also begs that he not be allowed to fall back into Ramsay's hands alive, to which [Squirrel](wiki:Squirrel) replies "Abel's word".

[Hosteen Frey](wiki:Hosteen_Frey) barges into the [Great Hall](wiki:Great_Hall_of_Winterfell), announcing that [Little Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Merrett)), son of his brother [Merrett](wiki:Merrett_Frey), has been killed. Theon looks at the spearwives, but Rowan replies that this is no work of theirs. [Big Walder](wiki:Walder_Frey_(son_of_Jammos)) implicates the [Manderly](wiki:House_Manderly) men, saying that the boy went out to get some [silver](wiki:Silver) owed to him. The dispute turns into a spar of words, and when Lord [Wyman Manderly](wiki:Wyman_Manderly) insults both the dead boy and [House Frey](wiki:House_Frey) simultaneously, Hosteen draws his sword in a blind fury and attacks Wyman, who is sliced under his chins and collapses; his [knights](wiki:Knight) leap to defend him, and a vicious and bloody fight breaks out. In the end, six [White Harbor](wiki:White_Harbor) men lie dead, as do two [Frey](wiki:House_Frey) men, and one of Ramsay's [Bastard Boys](wiki:Bastard_Boys), [Luton](wiki:Luton). Roose and Ramsay quell the tension by telling the men present if they want blood, they should turn their anger on Stannis. Hosteen replies that once they bring Stannis's head to Roose, they will come for Wyman's.

Roose asks Abel to play them a song, and the Freys leave. It is then that Rowan grasps Theon's arm, telling him that the time must be now, and that Abel can fend for himself. The plan is to dress ["Arya"](wiki:Jeyne_Poole) up as [Squirrel](wiki:Squirrel), while the real Squirrel climbs away down the wall. They find the girl in her room in the [Great Keep](wiki:Great_Keep_(Winterfell)), where she is terrified to leave, thinking this is a trap set up by Ramsay. The girl tells the washerwomen that she has been trained, she will not escape, Ramsay does not need to chop off her feet. They finally get her into Squirrel's clothes, and Theon tells her to follow [Holly](wiki:Holly) closely, and not to run.

As they get to the gate they see some guards. Holly seduces one and kills him in the process, while [Frenya](wiki:Frenya) struggles and kills the other with her spear. Jeyne screams, revealing them to the other guards. Frenya waits at the bottom and fights the rest of the "kneelers" that come to catch them, while Holly and the others get to the top of the wall. Holly then realizes that Frenya has the rope, just before being killed by arrows.

Theon thinks that if they are caught now they will be brought to Ramsay and [tortured](wiki:Torture). He then grabs Jeyne, and jumps from the battlements.
