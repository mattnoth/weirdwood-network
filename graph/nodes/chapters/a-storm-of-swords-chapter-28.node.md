## Narrative Arc

[Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) is dressing in the gown [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) had made for her, thinking to herself what it would be like to marry [Willas Tyrell](wiki:Willas_Tyrell). However, her daydreams are shattered when the queen reveals that she is to marry [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) that very afternoon. Sansa does her duty, realizing there is no way out. Tyrion apologizes to her, saying that it was his father [Tywin](wiki:Tywin_Lannister)'s wish to see them wed. [During the ceremony](wiki:Wedding_of_Tyrion_Lannister_and_Sansa_Stark) in the [royal sept](wiki:Royal_sept), Sansa does not bend to allow Tyrion to place his cloak over her shoulders, causing embarrassment for the dwarf.

At the feast in the [Small Hall](wiki:Small_Hall), Ser [Garlan Tyrell](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell) comforts Sansa, saying that Tyrion will make a good husband, "He is a bigger man than he seems." When the time comes to have them bedded, Tyrion demands that there will be no bedding. He has to threaten King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) to keep him from making the tense moment worse. In bed that night, Tyrion realizes that Sansa is petrified at the thought of losing her [maidenhood](wiki:Maid) to him, and he tells her that he will not touch her until she wants him to.
