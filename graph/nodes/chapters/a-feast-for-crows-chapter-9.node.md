## Narrative Arc

When [Brienne](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) reaches [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale), she finds the gates barred for the night. The area surrounding it is littered with corpses of both [northmen](wiki:North) and men from the [Reach](wiki:Reach).(wiki:A_Feast_for_Crows-Chapter_9.cite_ref-1)

The gates open at morning, and the captain tells Brienne that his sister can paint over the black [bat](wiki:Bat) of [Lothston](wiki:House_Lothston) on her shield. After finding and telling the sister what sigil she wants, Brienne heads to the [Dun Fort](wiki:Dun_Fort) to speak to the lord. Since Lord [Rykker](wiki:Renfred_Rykker) is in the field, she meets with the castellan, Ser [Rufus Leek](wiki:Rufus_Leek), and a maester, who tells her that many came before her asking if [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard) and [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) had come to Duskendale. The Dun Fort's maester tells the story of the [Defiance of Duskendale](wiki:Defiance_of_Duskendale), which seems to have been the incident that finally sent King [Aerys II](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) over the edge.

Either of his own initiative, or from the urgings of his wife [Serala](wiki:Serala), [Denys Darklyn](wiki:Denys_Darklyn) took Aerys hostage. When [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) (who was [Hand](wiki:Hand_of_the_King) at the time) surrounded the Dun Fort, Lord Darklyn threatened to kill Aerys. When Aerys was captured, [Symon Hollard](wiki:Symon_Hollard) killed one of his Kingsguard, Ser [Gwayne Gaunt](wiki:Gwayne_Gaunt).

After [Barristan the Bold](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) slipped into the Dun Fort and rescued the king, Aerys had nearly every member of the [Darklyn](wiki:House_Darklyn) and [Hollard](wiki:House_Hollard) families killed or attainted. [Dontos](wiki:Dontos_Hollard), who was young at the time, survived because Barristan asked Aerys to stay his hand.

The castellan tells Brienne that Duskendale would have been the last place Dontos would have fled to. Despairing that she will never find Sansa, Brienne bumps into a skinny boy whom she also saw back at [Rosby](wiki:Rosby) on a [piebald rounsey](wiki:Old_piebald_rounsey), but he runs away.

Visiting the [Seven Swords](wiki:Seven_Swords) inn for dinner, Brienne meets a [dwarf brother](wiki:Dwarf_brother), who tells her that he overheard a man called [Nimble Dick](wiki:Dick_Crabb) in [Maidenpool](wiki:Maidenpool) bragging that he had *"fooled a [fool](wiki:Shagwell)"* seeking passage for three across the [narrow sea](wiki:Narrow_sea). That night, Brienne dreams of [Renly](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)'s death, but when he topples after the shadow killed him, the body is that of [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister).

The next day, Brienne picks up her shield, painted with the sigil that [Tanselle](wiki:Tanselle) had painted for Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall). Proceeding past fishing villages on the way to Maidenpool, Brienne camps by the ruins of the [Hollard castle](wiki:Hollard_castle), and hears a rider. Fearing it may be Ser [Shadrich](wiki:Shadrich) and that a battle might ensue, she discovers the boy who seems to be stalking her. It turns out to be [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne), who asks to stay with her, hoping that if she finds Sansa, it may lead the boy back to [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).
