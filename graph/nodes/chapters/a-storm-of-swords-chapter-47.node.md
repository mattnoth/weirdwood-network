## Narrative Arc

[Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark) attempts to kill [the Hound](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) at night while the man is asleep, but he awakens and takes the rock from her hands. They reach [Lord Harroway's Town](wiki:Lord_Harroway's_Town), but find it flooded. Sandor promises to pay some ferrymen to take them across the [Trident](wiki:Trident), but gives them only the parchment [Lord Beric](wiki:Beric_Dondarrion) had given him promising reimbursement after the war. Arya tells Sandor that she had once been captured by [his brother](wiki:Gregor_Clegane), and the Hound finds it amusing that Gregor never knew who he had caught. Clegane says, "I’ll be sure and tell him that, before I cut his heart out." She asks why he would kill his brother and the Hound asks didn't she ever want to kill a brother or sister? He realizes from Arya’s expression that the "wolf bitch" hates the "pretty bird"--Arya and her sister don't get along. Sandor admits that maybe he is a monster for killing so many but also tells her that he saved [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark)’s life when the mob tried to kill her. The Hound informs her that he is taking her to [the Twins](wiki:The_Twins), and will ransom her to [Robb](wiki:Robb_Stark) and perhaps join the Young Wolf’s service, because "even a dog gets tired of being kicked."
