## Narrative Arc

[Davos](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) and a few hand-picked men he knows to be loyal only to [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) begin their mission in the dark of night. They find [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm) in [Pylos](wiki:Pylos)' solar, and the maester tells the boy to listen to Davos, as he is [the King's Hand](wiki:Hand_of_the_King). Davos and his men bring Edric in secrecy to a ship of [Salladhor](wiki:Salladhor_Saan)'s, and Davos tells the boy that he must go with Ser [Andrew Estermont](wiki:Andrew_Estermont) on an adventure. With the boy safely away from [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone), Davos goes to face Stannis. He tells the King that [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) is dead, and [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) states that [Tommen](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) will be named king. When she asks Stannis for the boy, the King warns her that she will pay with her life if she is wrong. Melisandre is sure that Stannis is the [one prophesied 5,000 years ago](wiki:Azor_Ahai), that the comet was his herald. But Davos reveals that Edric Storm is gone, and judges based on her reaction that Melisandre has not foreseen this in her flames. Davos tells Stannis that a king must protect his people or he is no king at all. When Stannis draws [Lightbringer](wiki:Lightbringer) to behead Davos, the Onion Knight begins to read out the letter that Maester Pylos received from the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch).
