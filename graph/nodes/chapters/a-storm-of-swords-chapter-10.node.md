## Narrative Arc

[Davos](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) learns that his son [Devan](wiki:Devan_Seaworth) is alive, and that mainly only the [Florents](wiki:House_Florent) remain to King [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon). Many of his other bannermen who were captured during [the Battle](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater) bent the knee to King [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon). He also learns that Lord [Guncer Sunglass](wiki:Guncer_Sunglass) was sacrificed to the fire by [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) as an offering to [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor). [Salladhor](wiki:Salladhor_Saan) tells Davos that Lord [Alester](wiki:Alester_Florent) is now [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), but Stannis remains in the [Stone Drum](wiki:Dragonstone) and sees no one but Melisandre.

Davos plans to kill Melisandre because he blames her for the defeat and the death of his sons. He reveals as much to Salladhor who tries to talk him out of it. When put ashore, he meets [Shireen](wiki:Shireen_Baratheon) and [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm). The boy greatly resembles [Robert](wiki:Robert_Baratheon) and [Renly](wiki:Renly_Baratheon), and he reveres his father. Edric says that his uncle Stannis should never have chopped off Davos’s fingers, but the Onion Knight knows that Robert was a different man than Stannis. Before Edric can talk further, Ser [Axell Florent](wiki:Axell_Florent) arrives and takes Davos into custody for plotting to kill Melisandre.
