## Narrative Arc

While hiding in the [Tumbledown Tower](wiki:Tumbledown_Tower), [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) gains the ability to open his third eye at will, and become [Summer](wiki:Summer_(direwolf)). [Jojen](wiki:Jojen_Reed) again warns him not to spend too much time as Summer, neglecting his body and forgetting what he should do when in contact with his [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf). They have been traveling north; staying away from civilization for fear that Bran might be seen, especially by the [ironmen](wiki:Ironmen) or [Bolton men](wiki:House_Bolton). Jojen insists they make for the [Wall](wiki:Wall) so that Bran can find the [three-eyed crow](wiki:Three-eyed_crow), because Jojen can teach him no more.
