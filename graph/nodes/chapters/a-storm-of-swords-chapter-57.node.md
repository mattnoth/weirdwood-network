## Narrative Arc

[Meereen](wiki:Meereen) is enclosed by a great wall, with hundreds of defenders to guard it. [Daenerys](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen)'s huge army consists mostly of [freed slaves](wiki:Freedmen). They are eating up the surrounding lands, and Daenerys realizes that she must claim the city before the army starves. Aware that storming the walls will cost her a great deal, Daenerys sends [Strong Belwas](wiki:Belwas) to dispatch the Hero of Meereen. Belwas kills [Oznak zo Pahl](wiki:Oznak_zo_Pahl), but the city still stands strong. [Brown Ben Plumm](wiki:Ben_Plumm) reveals a way into the city through the sewers. Dany's [dragons](wiki:Dragon) seem to like Brown Ben, and they perch on his shoulder when he visits.

Daenerys finds herself drawn to [Daario Naharis](wiki:Daario_Naharis), and she keeps thinking of his blue eyes. Later, she goes off with [Arstan](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) and [Missandei](wiki:Missandei) to visit the camps, and there she is attacked by [Mero](wiki:Mero) of [Braavos](wiki:Braavos). Arstan kills the Titan's Bastard with his staff alone, and when Dany reaches her pavilion, [Jorah](wiki:Jorah_Mormont) is shocked to learn that a squire with a staff could defeat Mero with such ease. Arstan reveals that he is actually a [knight](wiki:Knight), and Jorah finally recognizes him as Barristan the Bold. Barristan apologizes for deceiving her, and swears his sword in her service. Jorah warns her not to accept the man, for he went over to [Robert](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) after [the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident). Barristan reveals that Jorah has spied on her on behalf of [Varys](wiki:Varys) and the [small council](wiki:Small_council) in hopes of gaining a royal pardon. Jorah pleads that he did so only at first, but he has been loyal to her since the birth of the dragons. Daenerys, disgusted, dismisses them both. She does not want them in her service any more. When she wonders where to send them, she suddenly has an idea.
