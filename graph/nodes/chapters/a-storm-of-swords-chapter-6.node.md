## Narrative Arc

[Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) is invited to supper by [Margaery](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) and her grandmother, the Lady [Olenna](wiki:Olenna_Tyrell). She is escorted to the party by [Loras](wiki:Loras_Tyrell), and unknowingly upsets him by talking of [Renly](wiki:Renly_Baratheon)'s death. The party is held in the [Maidenvault](wiki:Maidenvault), where King [Baelor the Blessed](wiki:Baelor_I_Targaryen) confined his sisters so they would not tempt him, as he had sworn a vow of celibacy. She is introduced to all of Margaery's cousins and companions, but it is the Queen of Thorns who takes the most interest in Sansa.

Lady Olenna reveals that the [Baratheons](wiki:House_Baratheon) have [Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) blood, and that the [Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell) claim to [Highgarden](wiki:Highgarden) has always been shaky since several other houses in the [Reach](wiki:Reach) can claim descent from [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand) as well. The old woman makes it known that [her son](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) is an oaf for making this alliance with the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister), and she wishes to learn from Sansa the truth of what [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) is. Sansa fears that [Varys](wiki:Varys) is listening, but Lady Olenna commands [Butterbumps](wiki:Butterbumps) to sing at the top of his voice. Sansa reveals the truth about Joffrey to the Queen of Thorns and Margaery, telling them that the King is a monster and that she fears for Margaery. Lady Olenna assures Sansa that the wedding will still go on, as Mace is determined that his daughter wed the King. The Queen of Thorns offers Sansa a proposal to visit Highgarden after the wedding, and wishes Sansa to marry her grandson [Willas](wiki:Willas_Tyrell).
