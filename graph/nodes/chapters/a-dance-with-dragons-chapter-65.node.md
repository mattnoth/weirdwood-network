## Narrative Arc

[Cersei](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) is forced on a [walk of atonement](wiki:Walk_of_atonement) from the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), as penance for her confessed sins. It is the only way to escape the sept, as her uncle [Kevan](wiki:Kevan_Lannister) will not oppose the [Faith](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) and Cersei does not have enough friends in the sept to risk a trial like [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell); her only hope is a [trial by battle](wiki:Trial_by_battle). Cersei feels fear at the thought of being paraded through the streets naked, remembering the violence the smallfolk caused during the [riots in King's Landing](wiki:Riots_in_King's_Landing), but reassures herself that no harm will come to her.

When morning arrives, her goalers arrive with a group of [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters), who strip Cersei naked and shave her of every hair on her body. Cersei is then led by an escort of [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons) (among them, her cousin [Lancel](wiki:Lancel_Lannister), much to Cersei's disgust) to the courtyard, where Cersei finds herself standing close to the spot where [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) was executed. As the septas accompanying her announce her sins to the watching crowd, Cersei muses on how [Joffrey](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) undid all her plans and the work of [Varys](wiki:Varys) and [Littlefinger](wiki:Littlefinger). Had Joffrey done as he was told and sent Stark to the [Wall](wiki:Wall), Ned and his knowledge of Cersei's incest would have been removed and the Lannisters would have had peace with [Robb Stark](wiki:Robb_Stark), a hostage in the form of [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark), and the freedom to deal with [Stannis](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) and [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon). Joffrey instead demanded Stark's head, and after that everything fell apart. Cersei's thoughts then turn to memories of how her father [Tywin](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) did away with [the mistress](wiki:Tytos_Lannister's_second_mistress) of her grandfather [Tytos](wiki:Tytos_Lannister) mistress after his death, having her stripped naked and marched through the streets of [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport), but Cersei insists to herself she will not be broken and humiliated so easily.

Cersei is then forced to strip before the crowd and walk to the Red Keep. At first, Cersei walks with dignity, ignoring the insults and worse (such as rotten vegetables and a cat carcass) the crowd throw at her. As the walk continues and the insults grow worse, mocking her faded beauty and the signs of age on her body, however, Cersei struggles to maintain her composure. As she walks, Cersei begins to hallucinate, seeing faces of others in the crowd: her father frowning; [Melara Hetherspoon](wiki:Melara_Hetherspoon); Ned and Sansa Stark, along with [Sansa's wolf](wiki:Lady_(direwolf)) staring accusingly at her; [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) laughing at her; and finally, the face of [Maggy the Frog](wiki:Maggy_the_Frog) intoning her prophecy, "Queen you shall be, until there comes another, younger and more beautiful, to cast you down and take all you hold most dear."

At this, Cersei breaks down in tears and runs for the Red Keep, collapsing as soon as she staggers into the courtyard. Kevan Lannister orders his niece covered up and removed, at which point Cersei is picked up by an eight-foot-tall giant of a man, clad in the armor and white cloak of a [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) knight. [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn) appears and introduces Cersei to this champion, Ser [Robert Strong](wiki:Robert_Strong), and she is most pleased.
