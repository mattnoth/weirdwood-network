## Narrative Arc

[Jon](wiki:Jon_Snow) is watching as [Jarl](wiki:Jarl)’s men are competing with [the Magnar](wiki:Styr)’s to see who will reach the top of the [Wall](wiki:Wall) first. Many of the climbers fall to their death, including Jarl, but several eventually reach the top. As long rope ladders are dropped down to the base to allow the others to climb, Jon notices [Ygritte](wiki:Ygritte) with tears in her eyes. She tells him that they never found the [Horn of Joramun](wiki:Horn_of_Joramun), which would have helped them bring the Wall down without a fight.
