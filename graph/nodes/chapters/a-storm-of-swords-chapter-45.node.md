## Narrative Arc

Traveling in the rain to the [Twins](wiki:Twins) for the wedding of Lord [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully) and [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey), [Catelyn](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) recalls [Robb](wiki:Robb_Stark) sending Ser [Rolph Spicer](wiki:Rolph_Spicer) to deliver [Martyn Lannister](wiki:Martyn_Lannister) to the [Golden Tooth](wiki:Golden_Tooth) in an exchange of captives, with [Robett Glover](wiki:Robett_Glover) put on a ship at [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) as part of the agreement. Robb left his wife [Jeyne](wiki:Jeyne_Westerling) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun), and only Ser [Raynald Westerling](wiki:Raynald_Westerling) travels with them. Robb has brought 3,500 men with him, those who have been with him since the [battle in the Whispering Wood](wiki:Battle_in_the_Whispering_Wood), and who will follow him to [Moat Cailin](wiki:Moat_Cailin) after the wedding. While passing through [the site of the battle](wiki:Whispering_Wood), Catelyn thinks of [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) boasting he had almost crossed swords with Ser [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) and how much ill would have been undone if Theon had died in place of [Lord Karstark](wiki:Rickard_Karstark)'s sons.

When they reach [Oldstones](wiki:Oldstones), Cat recalls that the ruins were once a great castle of the [Kings of the Rivers and the Hills](wiki:Kings_of_the_Rivers_and_the_Hills) during the [Mudd dynasty](wiki:House_Mudd). The Mudds were [First Men](wiki:First_Men), but during the time of King [Tristifer IV](wiki:Tristifer_IV_Mudd), the kingdoms of the First Men were falling during the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals). Tristifer IV died when seven Andal kings joined forces against him.

Robb tells his mother that he plans to legitimize his brother [Jon](wiki:Jon_Snow) and name him heir should he and Jeyne not have a child. Catelyn is stunned by this proclamation, and pleads with him not to make the mistake the [Targaryens](wiki:House_Targaryen) made when King [Aegon IV Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen) legitimized all his bastards on his deathbed. The [Blackfyre Pretenders](wiki:House_Blackfyre) plagued the Targaryens for five generations until Ser [Barristan Selmy](wiki:Barristan_Selmy) slew [Maelys Blackfyre](wiki:Maelys_Blackfyre) on the [Stepstones](wiki:Stepstones). They both fear that [Sansa](wiki:Sansa_Stark) will give [the Imp](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) a son and thus control of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) and agree that must be prevented. She pleads that Robb consider naming his sister [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark) as heir, but Robb insists that no one has seen Arya since his father [Eddard's](wiki:Eddard_Stark) death and that his sister is likely also dead. Catelyn tells Robb that she cannot support his choice of Jon but Robb reminds her that he does not have to ask for her support, stating his reason as, "I'm the king."

Lord [Jason Mallister](wiki:Jason_Mallister) soon joins Robb's force at [Hag's Mire](wiki:Hag's_Mire), bringing with him the captain of the *[Myraham](wiki:Myraham)*. The captain tells them that King [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy) fell to his death when a wind blew him off one of the bridges between the towers of [Pyke](wiki:Pyke), and no sooner was he dead than [Euron Crow's Eye](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) returned to claim the [Seastone Chair](wiki:Seastone_Chair). [Lord Botley](wiki:Sawane_Botley) was drowned when he objected to Euron.

Knowing [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) and [Asha](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy) will not stand for Euron's actions, Robb commands [Maege Mormont](wiki:Maege_Mormont) and [Galbart Glover](wiki:Galbart_Glover) to sail through the marshes of the [Neck](wiki:Neck) on Mallister longships to seek out [Howland Reed](wiki:Howland_Reed) at [Greywater Watch](wiki:Greywater_Watch). After he is joined by [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) at the Twins and Edmure's wedding is done, Robb plans to march on Moat Cailin with 12,000 men. Howland's [crannogmen](wiki:Crannogmen) will guide a small force under Robb's command across the swamps in order to reach Moat Cailin from the north. When Galbart warns him what should happen if the crannogmen were to fail him, Robb replies, "They will not fail. My father knew the worth of Howland Reed." Robb then announces that Catelyn will be taken to [Seagard](wiki:Seagard) under the protection of Jason after the wedding. King Robb then calls his bannermen to fix their seals as witnesses to the decree naming his heir.
