## Narrative Arc

[Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) and his companions aboard the *[Shy Maid](wiki:Shy_Maid)* continue their way down the [Rhoyne](wiki:Rhoyne). As they encounter heavy amounts of fog in the [Sorrows](wiki:Sorrows), severely hindering their visibility, [Ysilla](wiki:Ysilla) insists that it is [not a common fog](wiki:Garin's_Curse), claiming that restless spirits surround them.

Tyrion is initially unconcerned and begins to make japes, but he quickly begins to re-evaluate the situation as the surroundings grow more and more eerie. The *Shy Maid* floats past the ruins of a palace in the ruins of [Chroyane](wiki:Chroyane), and Tyrion recalls to himself the brief joy he once had with [Tysha](wiki:Tysha).

Fearing an attack from the fog, Griff orders Young Griff to accompany Septa [Lemore](wiki:Lemore) to safety below deck. Tyrion reveals his knowledge regarding the boy, and confirms bits of information he apparently learned from his previous bet with [Haldon](wiki:Haldon). Young Griff is in fact young [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)), although Tyrion is uncertain how he survived the events following the [Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident). He also comes to the conclusion that Griff is the late [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen)'s friend [Jon Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington), the former [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King).

Tyrion also reveals his [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) roots to the party, but there is no time to discuss loyalties or intentions. The *Shy Maid* is attacked by [stone men](wiki:Stone_men). He helps to make efforts to ensure the safety of the now-revealed young prince on board, but the dwarf is knocked into the water, dragged down by one of the stone men.
