## Narrative Arc

[Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) is anchored near the [Isle of Cedars](wiki:Isle_of_Cedars), right outside [Slaver's Bay](wiki:Slaver's_Bay), to find [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen). He was sent by his brother, King [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy), to command the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) and bring Dany to him, but Victarion means to take her to wife himself.

Victarion's shield hand was hurt fighting [Talbert Serry](wiki:Talbert_Serry) in the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), and the wound has festered. Maester [Kerwin](wiki:Kerwin) has had little success treating the wound. Victarion learned that the strong [Volantene](wiki:Volantis) fleet is on their way to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) as well, while his own fleet was scattered on their way and only half have come back together.

The 54th ship to arrive, *[Grief](wiki:Grief)*, has happened upon [Moqorro](wiki:Moqorro), who was swept overboard *[Selaesori Qhoran](wiki:Selaesori_Qhoran)* when [Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) traveled her to Meereen. The [red priest](wiki:Red_priest) claims to have seen in his flames that Victarion will die unless the priest helps him. Moqorro succeeds in healing him, and Victarion has the maester killed. Victarion makes for Meereen.
