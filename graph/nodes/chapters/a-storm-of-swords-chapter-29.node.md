## Narrative Arc

[Harwin](wiki:Harwin) tells [Arya](wiki:Arya_Stark) about the [Battle of the Bells](wiki:Battle_of_the_Bells), in which [her father](wiki:Eddard_Stark) defeated [Lord Connington](wiki:Jon_Connington), [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen)'s [Hand](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), in a fierce battle fought in the streets of [Stoney Sept](wiki:Stoney_Sept). [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) was injured and hiding in the town when the Hand's army invaded the town. Many credit Robert with the victory, but the king always said that Eddard had won that battle.

Arya is disturbed to find [northmen](wiki:Northmen) being starved in cages, and gives one of them water. Possibly [Karstarks](wiki:House_Karstark) as they were searching for Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), they were captured by the [Mad Huntsman](wiki:Mad_Huntsman). The northmen are guilty of rape and pillaging, and [Anguy](wiki:Anguy) puts the survivors out of their misery.

The [brotherhood without banners](wiki:Brotherhood_without_banners) spend the night at the [Peach](wiki:Peach), a brothel. [Gendry](wiki:Gendry) is propositioned by [Bella](wiki:Bella), who claims to be a daughter of the late King Robert, but Gendry refuses. When an old man makes an advance toward Arya, Gendry claims to be her sister and warns the man away. During the night, they hear a commotion, and watch as the [Mad Huntsman](wiki:Mad_Huntsman) brings someone as a prisoner into the town in chains. Arya recognizes the captive.
