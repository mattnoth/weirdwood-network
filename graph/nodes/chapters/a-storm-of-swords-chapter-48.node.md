## Narrative Arc

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) reaches [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town) and warns the populace to evacuate. At [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black), Jon learns that [Jarman Buckwell](wiki:Jarman_Buckwell) and his men have returned with tidings of [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder)’s army. He discovers that the [wildlings](wiki:Wildlings) have been feinting all along [the Wall](wiki:The_Wall), and groups of black brothers have been dispersed to counter them. Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) also tells him that [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) was betrayed and killed, and that only twelve men made it back from [Craster's Keep](wiki:Craster's_Keep), including [Giant](wiki:Bedwyck), [Grenn](wiki:Grenn), [Dywen](wiki:Dywen), Sweet [Donnel Hill](wiki:Donnel_Hill) and [Dolorous Edd](wiki:Edd_Tollett). Aemon cleans and wraps Jon’s wound, and Grenn tells him that [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) has been burned to the ground and that [Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and [Rickon Stark](wiki:Rickon_Stark) are dead at the hands of [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy). Jon tells them that there is some kind of mistake, for he saw a grey direwolf at [Queenscrown](wiki:Queenscrown). Jon wonders if Bran lives on through [his direwolf](wiki:Summer_(direwolf)).
