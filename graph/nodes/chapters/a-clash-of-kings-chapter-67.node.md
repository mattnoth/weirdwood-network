## Narrative Arc

[Tyrion](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) lies abed dreaming of the men who died fighting alongside him in [the battle](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), as well as those he killed. He dreams of [Tysha](wiki:Tysha) as well, and when he awakens, realizes that he is not at the [Tower of the Hand](wiki:Tower_of_the_Hand), and the maester hovering over him is one he does not recognize. Unable to speak, the maester gives him [milk of the poppy](wiki:Milk_of_the_poppy). Later, when the maester returns, Tyrion knocks the poppy from his hands and demands the cast be removed from his face. Maester [Ballabar](wiki:Ballabar) keeps mumbling about [the Queen](wiki:Cersei_Lannister)’s orders, but Tyrion will not listen. He demands wine not poppy, and looks upon his destroyed face in a mirror. Three-quarters of his nose is gone along with part of his lip, and the stitching job was sloppy. Tyrion sends the maester away and asks for [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne). The boy arrives, and Tyrion swears him to silence about what occurred at the docks with Ser [Mandon Moore](wiki:Mandon_Moore). He tells Pod to bring Maester [Frenken](wiki:Frenken), for he does not trust this new maester.
