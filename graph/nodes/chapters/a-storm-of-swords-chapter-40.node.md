## Narrative Arc

[Bran](wiki:Bran_Stark) and the others reach a small, deserted village on a lake. There is a [holdfast tower](wiki:Queenscrown) on an island in the middle of the lake, and Bran tells them that Good Queen [Alysanne](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) once stayed there. [Brandon's Gift](wiki:Brandon's_Gift) is a strip of land south of the Wall to a distance of 25 leagues which was once given to the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) to support them. The [New Gift](wiki:New_Gift), given to the Watch by Queen Alysanne and her husband King [Jaehaerys I](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), doubled that distance. Bran tells them there is a secret walkway just beneath the lake’s surface that will allow them to reach the tower.

They stay in the tower that night during a terrible thunderstorm, and Bran tells them that all the gates through the Wall were sealed when each of the castles had to be abandoned, leaving only three gates open at each of the manned castles. When [Hodor](wiki:Hodor) starts to scream in fear of the thunder, Bran reaches out to him the way he does to [Summer](wiki:Summer_(direwolf)), and calms the big man. [Jojen](wiki:Jojen_Reed) spots men on the shore in the village, and Bran enters Summer to investigate.
