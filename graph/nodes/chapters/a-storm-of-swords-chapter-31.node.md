## Narrative Arc

On the way to [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister)'s wound is infected, and he is feverish from the loss of his hand (which has been cut off by [Zollo](wiki:Zollo)). At the camp some of the [Bloody Mummers](wiki:Brave_Companions) come to rape [Brienne](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth). Jaime knows that if she resists they will probably kill her so he advises her to take her mind far away. He recalls that when [Aerys](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) cooked Lord [Rickard Stark](wiki:Rickard_Stark) alive while [Brandon](wiki:Brandon_Stark) died trying to rescue him, Jaime did the same. Brienne is stubborn and wants to resist. Jaime saves her again by yelling “sapphires” loud enough for the [Goat](wiki:Vargo_Hoat) to hear. Brienne thanks him as Jaime considers the debt (revenge) that he owes to Vargo Hoat and his Bloody Mummers: a [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) always pays his debts.

After reaching Harrenhal, Hoat presents the Kingslayer to [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton), who is disgusted that the Mummers hacked off his hand. He has [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn) cleanse the stump and bandage it. Jaime asks Qyburn to see to Brienne's wounds as well.
