## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings

When Daenerys Targaryen first enters the city of Qarth she notices that the Qartheen women are wearing gowns that leave one breast bare.(wiki:Qartheen_gown.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)

Later for the audience with the [Pureborn](wiki:Pureborn) in the [Hall of a Thousand Thrones](wiki:Hall_of_a_Thousand_Thrones) Daenerys has to garb herself in [Qartheen](wiki:Qarth) fashion, as [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos) warns her that the Enthroned will never listen to a [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki), so she takes care to go before them in flowing green samite [Qartheen](wiki:Qarth) gown.(wiki:Qartheen_gown.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

While in the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying) Daenerys sees a false vision of the [Undying Ones](wiki:Undying_Ones) - a great hall and a splendor of [wizards](wiki:Wizard). When a kingly man in rich robes tells Dany (falsely) that they are the Undying of Qarth there is a woman beside him who then tells Dany that they have long awaited her. Dany notices that the woman clad in rose and [silver](wiki:Silver) and that the breast she has left bare in the Qartheen fashion is as perfect as a breast could be. Shortly thereafter, after discovering the real [Undying Ones](wiki:Undying_Ones), Dany sees that one of them, an ancient woman seated on the opposite side of the table, is wearing a gown of pale [silk](wiki:Silk) that as rotted on her body, one withered breast is left bare in the Qartheen manner.(wiki:Qartheen_gown.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

When Dany decides that she and her people must flee Qarth she goes down to the [docks](wiki:Port_of_Qarth) to seek ships passage dressed in [Dothraki](wiki:Dothraki) fashion. That morning [Irri](wiki:Irri) had brought her a Qartheen gown to wear, an airy confection of ivory samite patterned with seed pearls, but Dany refused it, telling Irri that the docks are no place for a lady's finery.(wiki:Qartheen_gown.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords

In [Astapor](wiki:Astapor), so as not to seem a beggar, Daenerys wears a Qartheen gown and her [crown](wiki:Crown) when she goes to make an offer to the [Good Masters](wiki:Good_Masters) of Astapor for all 8,600 [Unsullied](wiki:Unsullied), plus all the [slave](wiki:Slave) boys in training. The gown is a deep violet silk, which brings out the [purple of her eyes](wiki:Purple_eyes).(wiki:Qartheen_gown.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

During [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos)'s visit to [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) Dany dons a [Qartheen](wiki:Qarth) gown in his honour.(wiki:Qartheen_gown.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
