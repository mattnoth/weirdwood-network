## Appearances & Description

Dragonbone is black due to its high [iron](wiki:Iron) content. It is used to make many items of value because of its rarity and its special properties. It is as strong as other metals, such as [steel](wiki:Steel), yet lighter and more flexible.(wiki:Dragonbone.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Rare dragonbone bows are highly sought after,(wiki:Dragonbone.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_summer_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) as they can outrange even the [goldenheart](wiki:Goldenheart) bows of the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles).(wiki:Dragonbone.cite_ref-Radwd61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

## Culture

The [heraldry](wiki:Heraldry) of [House Willum](wiki:House_Willum) depicts a dragonbone skeleton.(wiki:Dragonbone.cite_ref-5)
It is disputed if the [Grey King's Hall](wiki:Grey_King's_Hall) on [Nagga's Hill](wiki:Nagga's_Hill) on [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk) is made from the petrified bones of the legendary sea dragon [Nagga](wiki:Nagga) or not.(wiki:Dragonbone.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Origins

Dragonbones have been found as far north as the island of [Ib](wiki:Ib), and as far south as in the jungles of [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos).(wiki:Dragonbone.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_rise_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
Upon ascending the rule of [Dorne](wiki:Dorne) after the death of [his mother](wiki:Meria_Martell), Prince [Nymor Martell](wiki:Nymor_Martell) send a peace delegation to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing), which included the skull of [Meraxes](wiki:Meraxes), to stop the [First Dornish War](wiki:First_Dornish_War).(wiki:Dragonbone.cite_ref-Rfabreign_of_the_dragon_-_the_wars_of_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Dragonbone.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne_against_the_dragons.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
Lord [Rogar Baratheon](wiki:Rogar_Baratheon) often boasted that his axe was "large and heavy enough to cleave through a dragon's skull.(wiki:Dragonbone.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Among her efforts to rebuilt [Tumbleton](wiki:Tumbleton) after the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Lady [Sharis Footly](wiki:Sharis_Footly) had the heads of the dragons [Seasmoke](wiki:Seasmoke) and [Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor) cleaned and displayed in the town square, where travelers paid a [penny](wiki:Currency) to look at them, and a star to touch them.(wiki:Dragonbone.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_hooded_hand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
[Sandoq the Shadow](wiki:Sandoq_the_Shadow) wielded a great curved sword of [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) with a dragonbone hilt.(wiki:Dragonbone.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
During the rule of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) over the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms), the [skulls](wiki:Skulls_of_the_Targaryen_dragons) of some of their [dragons](wiki:Dragon) decorated the walls of the [throne room](wiki:Iron_Throne) in the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep). The skulls were stored in a dank cellar after [Robert's Rebellion](wiki:Robert's_Rebellion) and replaced with banners and tapestries.(wiki:Dragonbone.cite_ref-Ragot13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)(wiki:Dragonbone.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Dragonbone.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
