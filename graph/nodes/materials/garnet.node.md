## Culture

## Origins

[Dunk](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) found a chipped garnet in the possessions of Ser [Arlan of Pennytree](wiki:Arlan_of_Pennytree) after the [hedge knight](wiki:Hedge_knight) passed away.(wiki:Garnet.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) Ser [Steffon Fossoway](wiki:Steffon_Fossoway) clasped his cloak with an apple made of [gold](wiki:Gold) and garnets.(wiki:Garnet.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1) After Ser [Eustace Osgrey](wiki:Eustace_Osgrey) wed [Rohanne Webber](wiki:Rohanne_Webber), [Lady of Coldmoat](wiki:Lady_of_Coldmoat), Eustace gave Duncan the Tall an [ivory](wiki:Ivory) spider brooch with spotted clusters of crushed garnets on its back.(wiki:Garnet.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
[Brienne Tarth](wiki:Brienne_Tarth) wore a bodice decorated with garnets when she was first introduced to Ser [Ronnet Connington](wiki:Ronnet_Connington).(wiki:Garnet.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

When [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) has [Longclaw](wiki:Longclaw) reworked for [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow), [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) purchases garnets in [Mole's Town](wiki:Mole's_Town) so the gemstones can depict red eyes on the sword's new wolf's pommel.(wiki:Garnet.cite_ref-Ragot60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Two of the [Mountain's men](wiki:Mountain's_men) squabble over a dented goblet set with garnets taken from a village.(wiki:Garnet.cite_ref-Racok26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

While celebrating the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), [Germund Botley](wiki:Germund_Botley) wears a rope of [pearls](wiki:Pearl) and garnets over his gilded breastplate.(wiki:Garnet.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow) wears a garnet cut in the shape of a drop of blood in his ear.(wiki:Garnet.cite_ref-Radwd12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) When he weds "[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark)" ([Jeyne Poole](wiki:Jeyne_Poole)) at [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell), Ramsay wears a black velvet doublet slashed with pink silk and glittering with garnet teardrops. The pink bride's cloak is also spattered with red garnets.(wiki:Garnet.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
