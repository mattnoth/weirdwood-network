## Culture

Northwestern [Essos](wiki:Essos) and the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) are rich in lead ores.(wiki:Lead.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)(wiki:Lead.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Lead.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_braavos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Lead is one of the metals used by [maesters](wiki:Maesters) for their chains; the subject it represents is unknown. The chains of [Armen the Acolyte](wiki:Armen), Maester [Aemon](wiki:Aemon_Targaryen_(son_of_Maekar_I)) and Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) have links of lead.(wiki:Lead.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Lead.cite_ref-Rasos48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Lead.cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The ring, mask, and rod of Archmaester [Theobald](wiki:Theobald) are also made of lead.(wiki:Lead.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
