## Culture

Amethysts are among the gems on the [maester](wiki:Maester)'s chain of Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle).(wiki:Amethyst.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
Ser [Flement Brax](wiki:Flement_Brax)'s [silver](wiki:Silver) armor is inlaid with amethysts,(wiki:Amethyst.cite_ref-Ragot56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and Ser [Tytos Brax](wiki:Tytos_Brax) wears an amethyst [unicorn](wiki:Unicorn) pin.(wiki:Amethyst.cite_ref-Raffc16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

The purple [tokar](wiki:Tokar) of [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) is fringed with amethysts and [pearls](wiki:Pearl).(wiki:Amethyst.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) [Lysono Maar](wiki:Lysono_Maar) also decorates his ears with amethysts and pearls.(wiki:Amethyst.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Origins

The [Amethyst Empress](wiki:Amethyst_Empress) was a ruler of the [Great Empire of the Dawn](wiki:Great_Empire_of_the_Dawn).(wiki:Amethyst.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
During the [wedding tourney at Whitewalls](wiki:Wedding_tourney_at_Whitewalls), [Daemon II Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_II_Blackfyre), disguised as Ser John the Fiddler, wore a heavy silver chain studded with huge dark amethysts which matched his eyes.(wiki:Amethyst.cite_ref-Rtmk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

Servants give [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) [golden](wiki:Gold) bracelets encrusted with amethysts before the girl meets Khal [Drogo](wiki:Drogo).(wiki:Amethyst.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) She later has a [vision](wiki:Vision) of ghosts with eyes of amethyst, [jade](wiki:Jade), [opal](wiki:Opal), and [tourmaline](wiki:Tourmaline).(wiki:Amethyst.cite_ref-Ragot68.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos) gives Daenerys a [silver](wiki:Silver) collar with an enchanted amethyst, which he claims will protect the girl from [poison](wiki:Poison).(wiki:Amethyst.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) He also gives her a thousand miniature [knights](wiki:Knight) carved of [amber](wiki:Amber), amethyst, [beryl](wiki:Beryl), jade, [onyx](wiki:Onyx), opal, and tourmaline, with armor of silver and gold.(wiki:Amethyst.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)
The [fool](wiki:Fool) Ser [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard) provides [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) with a silver hair net, decorated with what he claims to be black amethysts from [Asshai](wiki:Asshai). He claims it is [magic](wiki:Magic) that will give her justice, vengeance, and take her home.(wiki:Amethyst.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

After King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) chokes to death at his [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding), Sansa realizes that one of the amethysts from her hair net is missing.(wiki:Amethyst.cite_ref-Rasos61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) During the trial of [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) for Joffrey's death, Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle) states that the king was poisoned with the [strangler](wiki:Strangler).(wiki:Amethyst.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) tells his brother [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) that Daenerys has [amethyst eyes](wiki:Purple_eyes).(wiki:Amethyst.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
