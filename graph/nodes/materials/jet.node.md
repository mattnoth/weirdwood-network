## Culture

[Oznak zo Pahl](wiki:Oznak_zo_Pahl) wears armor with [copper](wiki:Copper) and jet scales.(wiki:Jet.cite_ref-Rasos57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
The dark hair of characters like [Boremund Baratheon](wiki:Boremund_Baratheon),(wiki:Jet.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon),(wiki:Jet.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0) [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm),(wiki:Jet.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) [Alleras](wiki:Alleras),(wiki:Jet.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) and [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell)(wiki:Jet.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) can be called jet black.
Some [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) compare the dark skin of [Summer Islanders](wiki:Summer_Isles) like [Alayaya](wiki:Alayaya),(wiki:Jet.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) [Quhuru Mo](wiki:Quhuru_Mo),(wiki:Jet.cite_ref-Racok27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) and [Kojja Mo](wiki:Kojja_Mo)(wiki:Jet.cite_ref-Raffc35.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0) to polished jet.
