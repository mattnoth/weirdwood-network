## Culture

Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) often wears emeralds which match her green eyes.(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Ragot5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) She owns a tiara jeweled with emeralds(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Ragot5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and a woven belt studded with emeralds.(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Racok54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Cersei has necklaces of emeralds(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Racok41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) and [diamonds](wiki:Diamond) with emeralds.(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) One of the queen's white gowns has swirls of tiny emeralds on its sleeves, and she also owns a [golden](wiki:Gold) hair net with larger emeralds.(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Rasos72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Cersei has emeralds the size of pigeon eggs on a golden ring(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and a necklace.(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
The arms of [House Mudd](wiki:House_Mudd) depict a golden crown studded with emeralds.(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-8)
Lord [Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) clasps his half cape with an emerald brooch.(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
Emeralds are among the decorations on the [maester](wiki:Maester)'s chain of Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle).(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
[Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) has emeralds among his various rings.(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
Emeralds are among the gems adorning the nose of [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos).(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Radwd16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

## Origins

Emeralds have been found in the [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles)(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_summer_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) and [Sothoryos](wiki:Sothoryos).(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
[Racallio Ryndoon](wiki:Racallio_Ryndoon) was known to freely give his possessions, such as emerald rings, to those who admired them.(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
At the [tourney on Prince Joffrey's name day](wiki:Tourney_on_Prince_Joffrey's_name_day), Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) lost an emerald pendant by betting on her brother, Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), who was unhorsed by Ser [Loras Tyrell](wiki:Loras_Tyrell). The pendant was returned to the queen, however.(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) wears a chain of [gold](wiki:Gold) and emeralds at the [siege of Storm's End](wiki:Siege_of_Storm's_End_(299)).(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
While in the [House of the Undying](wiki:House_of_the_Undying), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) has a vision of a warrior in shining emerald armor.(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Racok48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
During the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), [wildfire](wiki:Wildfire) is reminiscent of [jade](wiki:Jade) and emerald.(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Racok58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Racok62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

The seven-sided golden [chalice](wiki:Wedding_chalice) given by Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) has an emerald rose to represent [House Tyrell](wiki:House_Tyrell).(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Queen Regent [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) wears a new crown of pale spun gold set with emeralds.(wiki:Emerald.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
