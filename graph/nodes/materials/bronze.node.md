## Origins

The [obsidian](wiki:Obsidian), wood, and stone weapons of the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) were outmatched by the bronze weapons and scales of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) during [their war](wiki:War_of_the_First_Men_and_the_children_of_the_forest).(wiki:Bronze.cite_ref-Ragot66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_41-0) The bronze of the First Men was eventually surpassed by [iron](wiki:Iron) and [steel](wiki:Steel), however, with the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals).(wiki:Bronze.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_arrival_of_the_andals.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_42-0)(wiki:Bronze.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-0)
Some [Kings of the Reach](wiki:Kings_of_the_Reach) from [House Gardener](wiki:House_Gardener) wore thorned crowns of first bronze and later iron when they went to war.(wiki:Bronze.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_the_gardener_kings.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_44-0) One of the [Storm Kings](wiki:Storm_Kings) was known as [Durran Bronze-Axe](wiki:Durran_Durrandon_(Bronze-Axe)).(wiki:Bronze.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_stormlands:_house_durrandon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_45-0)
The [Valyrians](wiki:Valyria) of antiquity used [slaves](wiki:Slaves) to unearth [copper](wiki:Copper) and [tin](wiki:Tin) from the [Fourteen Flames](wiki:Fourteen_Flames).(wiki:Bronze.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_valyria.27s_children.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_46-0)
The ancient monarchs from [House Royce](wiki:House_Royce) of [Runestone](wiki:Runestone) were known as [Bronze Kings](wiki:Bronze_Kings).(wiki:Bronze.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_43-1) Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) referred to his first wife, Lady [Rhea Royce](wiki:Rhea_Royce), as "the bronze bitch".(wiki:Bronze.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_47-0) Lord [Gunthor Royce](wiki:Gunthor_Royce) was known as the Bronze Giant.(wiki:Bronze.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_48-0)
[Ollidar](wiki:Ollidar) had ring, rod, and mask of bronze when he served as an archmaester.(wiki:Bronze.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_49-0)
[Vermithor](wiki:Vermithor), one of the [dragons](wiki:Dragon) of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen), was called the Bronze Fury(wiki:Bronze.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_50-0) for his scales colored bronze and tan.(wiki:Bronze.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_51-0)
