## Culture

[Lead](wiki:Lead), [iron](wiki:Iron), and tin are abundant on [Great Wyk](wiki:Great_Wyk), [Harlaw](wiki:Harlaw), and [Orkmont](wiki:Orkmont) in the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands).(wiki:Tin.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) The [Forest of Qohor](wiki:Forest_of_Qohor) has [silver](wiki:Silver), tin, and [amber](wiki:Amber),(wiki:Tin.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_qohor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) and the mainland near [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) has iron, tin, lead, and [slate](wiki:Slate).(wiki:Tin.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_braavos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) [Gold](wiki:Gold), iron, and tin are abundant on mountain [Ib](wiki:Ib).(wiki:Tin.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_ib.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Tin is one of the metals used by [maesters](wiki:Maesters) for their chain links.(wiki:Tin.cite_ref-Ragot41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Thenns](wiki:Thenns) mine tin and [copper](wiki:Copper) to make [bronze](wiki:Bronze).(wiki:Tin.cite_ref-Radwd49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

## Origins

The [Valyrians](wiki:Valyrians) of antiquity used [slaves](wiki:Slaves) to unearth [copper](wiki:Copper) and tin from the [Fourteen Flames](wiki:Fourteen_Flames).(wiki:Tin.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_valyria.27s_children.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
[House Yronwood](wiki:House_Yronwood) grew rich from mining [iron](wiki:Iron), [silver](wiki:Silver), and tin from their lands.(wiki:Tin.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_kingdoms_of_the_first_men.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
