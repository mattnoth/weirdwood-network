## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

A former potboy tells [Jory Cassel](wiki:Jory_Cassel) that Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), the late [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), had visited a master [armorer](wiki:Armorer) and commissioned plate armor wrought in pale [silver](wiki:Silver) with a blue jasper falcon and a [mother-of-pearl](wiki:Mother-of-pearl) [moon](wiki:Moon) on the breast.(wiki:Jasper.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) When Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) asks [Tobho Mott](wiki:Tobho_Mott) if the armorer had made a falcon helm for Jon, Tobho says he did not receive Lord Arryn's patronage when the Hand visited.(wiki:Jasper.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

While at the [port of Qarth](wiki:Port_of_Qarth), [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) is given a wooden box with a [mother-of-pearl](wiki:Mother-of-pearl) lid inlaid with jasper and [chalcedony](wiki:Chalcedony) by a [Sorrowful Man](wiki:Sorrowful_Man). A [manticore](wiki:Manticore) attacks the girl when she opens the box.(wiki:Jasper.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
