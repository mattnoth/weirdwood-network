## Culture

Altars in [septs](wiki:Sept) can be inlaid with onyx, [mother-of-pearl](wiki:Pearl), and [lapis lazuli](wiki:Lapis_lazuli).(wiki:Onyx.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) Within the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor), a chamber has carvings of [the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) with eyes of onyx, [jade](wiki:Jade), [malachite](wiki:Malachite), and yellow [moonstone](wiki:Moonstone).(wiki:Onyx.cite_ref-Raffc43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
The massive chairs of the [Pureborn](wiki:Pureborn) of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth) are decorated with [goldwork](wiki:Gold) and studded with [amber](wiki:Amber), onyx, lapis, and jade.(wiki:Onyx.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Carved onyx is available at the [port of Qarth](wiki:Port_of_Qarth).(wiki:Onyx.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
[Cyvasse](wiki:Cyvasse) pieces can be carved of [ivory](wiki:Ivory) and onyx.(wiki:Onyx.cite_ref-Raffc40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) has onyx among his various rings.(wiki:Onyx.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
The scarlet armor of Lord [Tytos Blackwood](wiki:Tytos_Blackwood) depicts a flock of [ravens](wiki:Raven) with onyx.(wiki:Onyx.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
People with dark eyes can often be considered as having onyx eyes.(wiki:Onyx.cite_ref-Ragot3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Onyx.cite_ref-Ragot19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Onyx.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

## Origins

The [Onyx Emperor](wiki:Onyx_Emperor) was a ruler of the [Great Empire of the Dawn](wiki:Great_Empire_of_the_Dawn).(wiki:Onyx.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
The helm of Ser [Lucas Inchfield](wiki:Lucas_Inchfield) was decorated with an onyx spider to represent [House Webber](wiki:House_Webber).(wiki:Onyx.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) While disguised as Egg, Prince [Aegon Targarye](wiki:Aegon_V_Targaryen) kept hidden a [gold](wiki:Gold) and onyx signet depicting the arms of his father, Prince [Maekar Targaryen](wiki:Maekar_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Onyx.cite_ref-Rtss.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)
When King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) wed [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), the cloak draped upon Cersei's back was decorated with the [Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon) stag in beads of onyx.(wiki:Onyx.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The [Tourmaline Brotherhood](wiki:Tourmaline_Brotherhood) gives [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) a dragon crown with three heads carved from [ivory](wiki:Ivory), [jade](wiki:Jade), and onyx.(wiki:Onyx.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) [Xaro Xhoan Daxos](wiki:Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos) gives her a thousand miniature [knights](wiki:Knight) carved of [amber](wiki:Amber), [amethyst](wiki:Amethyst), [beryl](wiki:Beryl), jade, onyx, [opal](wiki:Opal), and [tourmaline](wiki:Tourmaline), with armor of [silver](wiki:Silver) and [gold](wiki:Gold).(wiki:Onyx.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

The seven-sided golden [chalice](wiki:Wedding_chalice) given by Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) has an onyx stag to represent [House Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon).(wiki:Onyx.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) During his [wedding feast](wiki:Purple_Wedding), Joffrey wears a doublet with onyx studs.(wiki:Onyx.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser [Balon Swann](wiki:Balon_Swann) clasps his [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) cloak with a silver brooch with battling ivory and onyx swans.(wiki:Onyx.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
