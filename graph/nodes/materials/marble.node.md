## Origins

King [Roland I Arryn](wiki:Roland_I_Arryn) ordered the construction of the Eyrie to be the new main seat of [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn), but since he did not like the look of the marble from the Vale of Arryn, he imported it from Tarth. This forced the construction to be painfully slow, intermittent over generations, as the cost was prohibitive as well as requiring much time and effort to carry the marble up the [Giant's Lance](wiki:Giant's_Lance).(wiki:Marble.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_vale:_the_eyrie.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-3)
At the end of the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood), the [Elephants](wiki:Elephants_(Volantis)) rose to power in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis). Their supporters knocked the heads off the statues of [Tigers](wiki:Tigers_(Volantis)) they blamed for the military defeats, including Triarch [Horonno](wiki:Horonno)'s in [Selhorys](wiki:Selhorys).(wiki:Marble.cite_ref-Radwd22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)
King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) once proposed to build a new "white city", made entirely of marble, on the south bank of the [Blackwater Rush](wiki:Blackwater_Rush).(wiki:Marble.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)
