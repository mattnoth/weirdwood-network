## Culture

### Westeros[ ]

Copper is one of the metals used by [maesters](wiki:Maesters) for their chains; the subject it represents is uncertain, but may be history. The ring, mask, and rod of Archmaester [Perestan](wiki:Perestan), the historian, are made of copper.(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle), Maester [Luwin](wiki:Luwin), and the acolytes [Armen](wiki:Armen) and [Alleras](wiki:Alleras) have links of copper.(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Ragot41.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Raffc0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
According to semi-canon sources, the coat of arms of Houses [Shermer](wiki:House_Shermer) and [Foote](wiki:House_Foote) both feature objects made of copper.(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-24)(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-25)
When [riding](wiki:Dragonrider) the [dragon](wiki:Dragon) [Meleys](wiki:Meleys), Princess [Rhaenys Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Aemon)) donned [steel](wiki:Steel) and copper armor that flashed in the [sun](wiki:Sun).(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_the_red_dragon_and_the_gold.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)
[Dornishmen](wiki:Dornishmen) use a lot of copper in their armor, but mainly for ornamental purposes, as it shines in the sun beautifully, but is less expensive than gold.(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-26) Prince [Oberyn Martell](wiki:Oberyn_Martell) owns a [gilded](wiki:Gold) helm displaying a copper sun on its brow,(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Rasos38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-2)(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) and a round steel shield with the sun-and-spear sigil of [House Martell](wiki:House_Martell) in red [gold](wiki:Gold), yellow gold, white gold, and copper.(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Rasos70.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-1) Like her father, [Obara Sand](wiki:Obara_Sand) owns a round steel and copper shield.(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2) She also wears a belt of copper suns.(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Radwd38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0) [Nymeria Sand](wiki:Nymeria_Sand) owns a great cream and copper-colored silk cape.(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-3) [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) owns a band with copper suns,(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-4) an ornate [snake](wiki:Snake) bracelet with copper and gold scales,(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Raffc13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) as well as a copper helm.(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0) [Quentyn Martell](wiki:Quentyn_Martell) owns a belt ornamented by a line of copper suns.(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Radwd50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1) [Areo Hotah](wiki:Areo_Hotah) wears a shirt of copper scales, with a billowing cloak of dun-and-yellow sandsilk to keep the sun off the copper.(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Raffc2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-5)
[Renly Baratheon](wiki:Renly_Baratheon) owns a wood-and-copper tub large enough for two.(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Racok22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1)
[Marwyn](wiki:Marwyn) has a copper kettle in his chambers at the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel).(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)
[Coppersmith's Wynd](wiki:Coppersmith's_Wynd) is a street in King's Landing.(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Racok44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)
"Counting coppers" is a pejorative used by King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) to describe the management of the treasury of the [realm](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms) and the position of [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin).(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1)(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Rasos19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0) [Randyll Tarly](wiki:Randyll_Tarly) also uses it when he describes [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) to [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth).(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Raffc14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_36-0)
[Pennytree](wiki:Pennytree) is [named for](wiki:Named_after) a huge old [oak](wiki:Oak) tree that grows by the duck pond, to which hundreds of copper pennies have been nailed.(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Radwd48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_37-0)

## Origins

The [Valyrians](wiki:Valyria) of [Old Valyria](wiki:Old_Valyria) used slaves to mine the ores beneath the [Fourteen Flames](wiki:Fourteen_Flames), which included copper.(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_valyria.E2.80.99s_children.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
[Steely Pate](wiki:Steely_Pate) only asks Ser [Duncan the Tall](wiki:Duncan_the_Tall) for a copper for the repair of his shield, in gratitude for Dunk's rescue of [Tanselle](wiki:Tanselle).(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_53-0)
[Haggon](wiki:Haggon) once traded with the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) men of [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea); a dozen strings of [amber](wiki:Amber) and a [sled](wiki:Sled) piled high with pelts, for six skins of [wine](wiki:Wine), a block of salt, and a copper kettle.(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Radwd0.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1)
In his childhood, Ser [Davos Seaworth](wiki:Davos_Seaworth) begged for coppers in [Flea Bottom](wiki:Flea_Bottom).(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_54-0)
The stableboy who took care of Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn)'s [horse](wiki:Horse) always received a copper coin from him on his [name day](wiki:Name_day).(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_55-0)
According to [Varys](wiki:Varys), the mother of one of King Robert's [bastards](wiki:Bastard) had copper hair.(wiki:Copper.cite_ref-Racok15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_56-0)
