## Culture

The [Summer Isles](wiki:Summer_Isles) are rich in pearls of many sorts. The [Pearl Palace](wiki:Pearl_Palace) is the seat of the Prince of [Koj](wiki:Koj).(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Rtwoiafbeyond_the_free_cities:_the_summer_isles.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)
Only the [god-emperor](wiki:God-emperor) is allowed to wear the traditional gowns of [cloth-of-gold](wiki:Gold), green pearls, and [jade](wiki:Jade) in [Yi Ti](wiki:Yi_Ti).(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
Altars in [septs](wiki:Sept) can be inlaid with [onyx](wiki:Onyx), mother-of-pearl, and [lapis lazuli](wiki:Lapis_lazuli).(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Racok18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
Prince [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) has a doublet with the prancing stag of [House Baratheon](wiki:House_Baratheon) sewn in pearls.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Black pearls are among the decorations on the [maester](wiki:Maester)'s chain of Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle).(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
The helm of Ser [Marlon Manderly](wiki:Marlon_Manderly) depicts the [merling king](wiki:Merling_King_(god)) with a crown of mother-of-pearl.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Princess [Arianne Martell](wiki:Arianne_Martell) has a veil of green and yellow silk decorated with small green pearls.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Raffc21.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
The figurehead on the prow of [Silence](wiki:Silence) depicts a mouth-less maiden with eyes of mother-of-pearl and hair of black [iron](wiki:Iron).(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
The most storied and infamous courtesans of [Braavos](wiki:Braavos) are known as the [Black Pearls](wiki:Black_Pearl).(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_braavos.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
[Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) has a green pearl among his various rings.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) [Lysono Maar](wiki:Lysono_Maar) decorates his ears with [amethysts](wiki:Amethyst) and pearls.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
According to the [Ghiscari](wiki:Ghiscari), pearls signify fertility.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0) The [tokar](wiki:Tokar) of [Grazdan mo Ullhor](wiki:Grazdan_mo_Ullhor) is fringed with fat white pearls which clank,(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Rasos27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) while the purple tokar of [Hizdahr zo Loraq](wiki:Hizdahr_zo_Loraq) is fringed with amethysts and pearls.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Radwd2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) [Ghael](wiki:Ghael) wears ropes of pearls and spun gold on his neck.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Rasos71.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

## Origins

The legendary [God-on-Earth](wiki:God-on-Earth) is said to have traveled in a palanquin carved from a single pearl and carried by his hundred queens. His eldest son succeeded him in ruling the [Great Empire of the Dawn](wiki:Great_Empire_of_the_Dawn) as the [Pearl Emperor](wiki:Pearl_Emperor).(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_yi_ti.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) The [pearl-white emperors](wiki:Pearl-white_emperors) were a dynasty who ruled the [Golden Empire of Yi Ti](wiki:Golden_Empire_of_Yi_Ti). [Lo Tho](wiki:Lo_Tho) allegedly ate the living brains of his enemies with a pearl-handled spoon.
According to [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) legend, warriors were seated upon thrones carved from mother-of-pearl when they feasted at the [Grey King's Hall](wiki:Grey_King's_Hall).(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
The [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon) presented King [Aenys I Targaryen](wiki:Aenys_I_Targaryen) with a golden crown decorated with the faces of [the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) in [jade](wiki:Jade) and pearl.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0) Many women at court dazzled in pearls and [diamonds](wiki:Diamond) during the era of Aenys and his wife, Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon).(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
The [Blue Pearl](wiki:Blue_Pearl) was a brothel in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) during the reign of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen).(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Rfabthe_long_reign_-_jaehaerys_and_alysanne:_policy.2C_progeny.2C_and_pain.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
Prince [Daemon Targaryen](wiki:Daemon_Targaryen) gave pearls to Princess [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen).(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) Shortly before dying in [129 AC](wiki:129_AC), King [Viserys II Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen) gave a pearl ring to his grandchildren, [Jaehaerys](wiki:Jaehaerys_Targaryen_(son_of_Aegon_II)) and [Jaehaera](wiki:Jaehaera_Targaryen), for them to play with as he told them stories.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Rfabheirs_of_the_dragon_-_a_question_of_succession.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-1) After Queen Jaehaera died in [133 AC](wiki:133_AC), one of her bedmaids lost a hand for having stolen two of her dolls and a pearl necklace.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0) [Daenaera Velaryon](wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon) wore pearls when she was presented before King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) at the [Maiden's Day Ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball) the following year.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

A former potboy tells [Jory Cassel](wiki:Jory_Cassel) that Lord [Jon Arryn](wiki:Jon_Arryn), the late [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), had visited a master [armorer](wiki:Armorer) and commissioned plate armor wrought in pale [silver](wiki:Silver) with a blue [jasper](wiki:Jasper) falcon and a mother-of-pearl [moon](wiki:Moon) on the breast.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) When Lord [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) asks [Tobho Mott](wiki:Tobho_Mott) if the armorer had made a falcon helm for Jon, Tobho says he did not receive Lord Arryn's patronage when the Hand visited.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Ragot27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1)
Jon's widow, Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn), wears black silk mourning clothes with the moon-and-falcon of [House Arryn](wiki:House_Arryn) sewn on the breast in pearls.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Ragot38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

At [Chataya's brothel](wiki:Chataya's_brothel), [Dancy](wiki:Dancy) wages her black pearls with [Marei](wiki:Marei) that she can sleep with [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) within a fortnight.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Racok29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)
[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) wears a belt of black-and-white pearls when she meets with the [Pureborn](wiki:Pureborn) of [Qarth](wiki:Qarth).(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) Daenerys also has a Qartheen gown of [ivory](wiki:Ivory) samite patterned with seed pearls.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) While at the [port of Qarth](wiki:Port_of_Qarth), Daenerys is given a wooden box with a mother-of-pearl lid inlaid with [jasper](wiki:Jasper) and [chalcedony](wiki:Chalcedony) by a [Sorrowful Man](wiki:Sorrowful_Man). A [manticore](wiki:Manticore) attacks the girl when she opens the box.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Racok63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-1)

When [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) weds [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) is forced to wed [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister), her maiden's cloak is a long cloak of white velvet decorated with pearls and a [direwolf](wiki:Direwolf) in [silver](wiki:Silver).(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)
The seven-sided golden [chalice](wiki:Wedding_chalice) given by Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) to King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) has a pearl direwolf to represent [House Stark](wiki:House_Stark).(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) When Sansa flees [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) after [Joffrey's death](wiki:Purple_Wedding), she wears a brown woolen dress decorated with freshwater pearls.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Rasos61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

When Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) of the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) stands vigil for his father, Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), the knight's hauberks has scales of mother-of-pearl chased with gold.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0) To replace his missing sword hand, Jaime has an armorer craft him a hand of [gold](wiki:Gold) with inlaid nails of mother-of-pearl.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)
[Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) wears an ivory silk gown with [Myrish lace](wiki:Myrish_lace) and seed pearls when she marries King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon); it is the same attire she wore when she wed Joffrey.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Raffc12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)
While celebrating the [taking of the Shields](wiki:Taking_of_the_Shields), [Germund Botley](wiki:Germund_Botley) wears a rope of pearls and [garnets](wiki:Garnet) over his gilded breastplate.(wiki:Pearl.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)
