## Culture

[Lady Forlorn](wiki:Lady_Forlorn), the [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) blade of Ser [Lyn Corbray](wiki:Lyn_Corbray), has a heart-shaped ruby in its pommel.(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[House Celtigar](wiki:House_Celtigar) is said to have chests of rubies at [Claw Isle](wiki:Claw_Isle).(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
Rubies are among the decorations on the [maester](wiki:Maester)'s chain of Grand Maester [Pycelle](wiki:Pycelle).(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Ragot20.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
[Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis) has rubies among his various rings.(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Origins

The crown of King [Aegon I Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) was a [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) circlet studded with rubies.(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
[Rego Draz](wiki:Rego_Draz), a native of [Pentos](wiki:Pentos), was resented for flaunting his wealth by wearing ruby rings in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing).(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
The sisters [Baela Velaryon](wiki:Baela_Targaryen) and [Rhaena Targaryen](wiki:Rhaena_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Daemon)) wore rubies at their throats when they presented [Daenaera Velaryon](wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon) to King [Aegon III Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_III_Targaryen) at the [Maiden's Day Ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball).(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
The black breastplate of Prince [Rhaegar Targaryen](wiki:Rhaegar_Targaryen) was decorated with the red three-headed [dragon](wiki:Dragon) of [House Targaryen](wiki:House_Targaryen) in rubies.(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Ragot58.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) When Lord [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon) killed Rhaegar with his warhammer at the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident), the rubies were scattered into the river.(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Ragot4.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) That crossing of the [Trident](wiki:Trident) subsequently became known as the [ruby ford](wiki:Ruby_ford).(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Ragot55.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) and [Mycah](wiki:Mycah) depart the [crossroads inn](wiki:Crossroads_inn) to search for rubies at the [ruby ford](wiki:Ruby_ford).(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
After the death of King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) wears a black mourning gown. Sewn into the bodice are a hundred dark red rubies in the shape of teardrops.(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Ragot51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) King [Joffrey I Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) wears a [golden](wiki:Gold) crown crusted with rubies and black [diamonds](wiki:Diamond).(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Ragot57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Melisandre's ruby](wiki:Melisandre's_ruby) glows when Maester [Cressen](wiki:Cressen) fails in his attempt to poison [the red priestess](wiki:Melisandre).(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Racokprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) [Lightbringer](wiki:Lightbringer), the sword of [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon), has a great square-cut ruby set in its hilt.(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Racok31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
The pommel of [Hearteater](wiki:Hearteater), the new blade of King Joffrey, depicts a heart-shaped ruby in the jaws of a [lion](wiki:Lion).(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Racok57.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0) Following the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), the young king wears a mantle studded with rubies.(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0) Joffrey rewards Lord [Mace Tyrell](wiki:Mace_Tyrell) and his sons, Ser [Garlan](wiki:Garlan_Tyrell) and Ser [Loras](wiki:Loras_Tyrell), with chains of roses wrought in soft yellow gold. Each necklace has a golden disc with the lion of [Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister) picked out in rubies.(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Racok65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) wears a chain of rubies and lions' heads when he weds [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark).(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)
Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) has [Tobho Mott](wiki:Tobho_Mott) melt down [Ice](wiki:Ice) to create two new [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel) swords. The blades' scabbards are ornamented with a row of studded golden lion's heads with ruby eyes.(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0) The new blade for Joffrey, which he names [Widow's Wail](wiki:Widow's_Wail),(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0) has lions' paws with unsheathed ruby claws on its crossguard.(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-1)
The seven-sided golden [chalice](wiki:Wedding_chalice) given by Mace to Joffrey has a ruby lion sun to represent [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Rasos59.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-1) The pommel of Ser [Ilyn Payne](wiki:Ilyn_Payne)'s new greatsword is skull-shaped [dragonglass](wiki:Dragonglass) with ruby eyes.(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Oathkeeper](wiki:Oathkeeper), the second blade made from Ice, has a golden lion's head with ruby eyes on its pommel.(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)
When Tywin's body lies in state, a longsword in a gilded scabbard studded with rubies is placed on his chest.(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Raffc7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0)
The [Elder Brother](wiki:Elder_Brother_(Quiet_Isle)) of the [Quiet Isle](wiki:Quiet_Isle) informs [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) six rubies from the [Battle of the Trident](wiki:Battle_of_the_Trident) have washed up nearby.(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Rattleshirt](wiki:Rattleshirt) tells [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) that the big square-cut gem which adorns the captive [wildling](wiki:Wildling)'s iron cuff was a gift from Melisandre.(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Radwd17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0) In actuality, Melisandre has used a [glamor](wiki:Glamor) to disguise [Mance Rayder](wiki:Mance_Rayder) as Rattleshirt, so that the real Rattleshirt can be publicly burned to death while Mance secretly survives.(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Radwd10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_32-0)(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Radwd31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_33-0)
Prince [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(son_of_Rhaegar)) wears three huge square-cut rubies on a chain of black iron; the youth had received them as a gift from Magister [Illyrio Mopatis](wiki:Illyrio_Mopatis).(wiki:Ruby.cite_ref-Radwd24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0)
