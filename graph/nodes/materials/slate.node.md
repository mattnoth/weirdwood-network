## Culture

The roofs of the [Maidenvault](wiki:Maidenvault)(wiki:Slate.cite_ref-Rasos6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) and the [Inn of the Kneeling Man](wiki:Inn_of_the_Kneeling_Man)(wiki:Slate.cite_ref-Rasos13.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) are made of slate. ## Origins

The kitchen of the [Aegonfort](wiki:Aegonfort) had a slate roof in case of fire.(wiki:Slate.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon_-_governance_under_king_aegon_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
Prince [Joffrey Velaryon](wiki:Joffrey_Velaryon) is said to have fallen from [Syrax](wiki:Syrax) onto a steep roof during the [Storming of the Dragonpit](wiki:Storming_of_the_Dragonpit), with shards of slate then raining down upon the boy.(wiki:Slate.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
