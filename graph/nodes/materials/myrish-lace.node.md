## Origins

King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) and his [master of coin](wiki:Master_of_coin), [Rego Draz](wiki:Rego_Draz), instituted a series of luxury taxes on expensive foreign imports, such as Myrish lace.(wiki:Myrish_lace.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing_-_the_realm_remade.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

[Daenaera Velaryon](wiki:Daenaera_Velaryon) wore a pale white silk, Myrish lace, and [pearls](wiki:Pearl) at the [Maiden's Day Ball](wiki:Maiden's_Day_Ball).(wiki:Myrish_lace.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_war_and_peace_and_cattle_shows.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

Trade between Westerosi ports and the Free Cities boomed during the [Lysene Spring](wiki:Lysene_Spring), including the trade of Myrish lace.(wiki:Myrish_lace.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

The inhabitants of [Duskendale](wiki:Duskendale) considered [Serala of Myr](wiki:Serala_of_Myr) to be responsable for the [Defiance of Duskendale](wiki:Defiance_of_Duskendale) and the downfall of [House Darklyn](wiki:House_Darklyn). They gave her the nickname of the "Lace Serpent" in reference to her city of origin.(wiki:Myrish_lace.cite_ref-Raffc9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones

Meeting [Eddard Stark](wiki:Eddard_Stark) in the [throne room](wiki:Throne_room) after King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon)'s death, [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister) wears a gown of sea-green [silk](wiki:Silk), trimmed with Myrish lace as pale as foam.(wiki:Myrish_lace.cite_ref-Ragot49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)

[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) sees [slaves](wiki:Slavery) carrying bolts of intricate Myrish lace in the Western Market of [Vaes Dothrak](wiki:Vaes_Dothrak).(wiki:Myrish_lace.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### A Clash of Kings

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) hears the [captain](wiki:Captain_of_the_Myraham) of the *[Myraham](wiki:Myraham)* say they carry bolts of Myrish lace when docking on the [Lordsport](wiki:Lordsport).(wiki:Myrish_lace.cite_ref-Racok11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

Trader captains bring [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) bolts of lace from Myr as tokens.(wiki:Myrish_lace.cite_ref-Racok40.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

### A Storm of Swords

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) is informed by a seamstress her new gown will include Myrish lace.(wiki:Myrish_lace.cite_ref-Rasos16.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) When the gown is done, the lace is dove-gray, and it is worn in her wedding to [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister).(wiki:Myrish_lace.cite_ref-Rasos28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

In [Harrenhal](wiki:Harrenhal), [Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) is given a pink gown sewn with pink Myrish lace and satin, as those were the only women's garments found for her.(wiki:Myrish_lace.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Myrish_lace.cite_ref-Rasos44.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

[Daario Naharis](wiki:Daario_Naharis) wears clothes with Myrish lace the color of butter on his collar and cuff when first meeting [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen).(wiki:Myrish_lace.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) [Grazdan mo Eraz](wiki:Grazdan_mo_Eraz) wears a [tokar](wiki:Tokar) fringed with golden Myrish lace when meeting with [Daenerys](wiki:Daenerys) as an envoy from [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai).(wiki:Myrish_lace.cite_ref-Rasos42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)

[Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell) wears a wedding gown of [ivory](wiki:Ivory) silk and Myrish lace when marrying [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon).(wiki:Myrish_lace.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)

### A Dance with Dragons

When discussing wedding preparations, [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) asks to be given a green tokar to wear, fringed with Myrish lace.(wiki:Myrish_lace.cite_ref-Radwd36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)

[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) takes notice that [Sweets](wiki:Sweets) is dressed in [moonstones](wiki:Moonstone) and Myrish lace when meeting [Yezzan zo Qaggaz](wiki:Yezzan_zo_Qaggaz)'s "collection" of slaves.(wiki:Myrish_lace.cite_ref-Radwd47.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)

A Myrish cog named *[Dove](wiki:Dove)*, on her way to [Yunkai](wiki:Yunkai) by way of New Ghis, carries a cargo of carpets, sweet green wines, and Myrish lace.(wiki:Myrish_lace.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0)
