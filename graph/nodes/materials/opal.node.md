## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

In a ruined city within the [red waste](wiki:Red_waste), [Aggo](wiki:Aggo) finds an [iron](wiki:Iron) bracelet set with a large uncut fire opal.(wiki:Opal.cite_ref-Racok12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
