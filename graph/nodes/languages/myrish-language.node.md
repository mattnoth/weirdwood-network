## Origins

[Larra Rogare](wiki:Larra_Rogare), the wife of [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen), was fluent in [High Valyrian](wiki:High_Valyrian) and the tongues of Myr, [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyroshi_language), [Volantis](wiki:Volantene_language), and her native [Lys](wiki:Lysene_language).(wiki:Myrish_language.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
"[The Seasons of My Love](wiki:The_Seasons_of_My_Love)" is a Myrish love song popular in [Westeros](wiki:Westeros). It is considered sweet and sad if you can understand its words.(wiki:Myrish_language.cite_ref-Ragot42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
