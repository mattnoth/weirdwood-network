## Origins

Some of the oldest remaining ancient texts were written by [Andals](wiki:Andals), [Valyrians](wiki:Valyria), [Ghiscari](wiki:Ghiscari), and [Asshai'i](wiki:Asshai).(wiki:High_Valyrian.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_dawn_age.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) After the [Old Empire of Ghis](wiki:Old_Empire_of_Ghis) was conquered by the [Valyrian Freehold](wiki:Valyrian_Freehold) in the [Ghiscari wars](wiki:Ghiscari_wars), the Ghiscari began speaking the High Valyrian of their conquerors.(wiki:High_Valyrian.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_rise_of_valyria.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
High Valyrian is no longer widely spoken due to the [Doom of Valyria](wiki:Doom_of_Valyria),(wiki:High_Valyrian.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) and most Valyrian records were destroyed in the catastrophe.(wiki:High_Valyrian.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_valyria.27s_children.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) The tongues of the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities) have continued to evolve from the original High Valyrian.(wiki:High_Valyrian.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
Queen [Alysanne Targaryen](wiki:Alysanne_Targaryen) is said to have begun learning how to read from Valyrian scrolls while still at the breast of her mother, Queen [Alyssa Velaryon](wiki:Alyssa_Velaryon).(wiki:High_Valyrian.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Alysanne's husband, King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen), was fascinated with the Old Valyrian scrolls in the library of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:High_Valyrian.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
[Racallio Ryndoon](wiki:Racallio_Ryndoon) is said to have spoken a dozen dialects of Valyrian.(wiki:High_Valyrian.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) Lord [Alyn Velaryon](wiki:Alyn_Velaryon) studied Valyrian treaties about warship design and sea tactics when he visited the [Citadel](wiki:Citadel).(wiki:High_Valyrian.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-1) [Larra Rogare](wiki:Larra_Rogare), the wife of Prince [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen), was fluent in High Valyrian and the dialects of [Lys](wiki:Lys), [Myr](wiki:Myr), [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh), and [Volantis](wiki:Volantis).(wiki:High_Valyrian.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Some highborn children of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) are still taught Valyrian as a sign of their noble education.(wiki:High_Valyrian.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:High_Valyrian.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0) Songs(wiki:High_Valyrian.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) and scrolls(wiki:High_Valyrian.cite_ref-Ragot9.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)(wiki:High_Valyrian.cite_ref-Raffcprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) are still sung and read in High Valyrian, although by [300 AC](wiki:300_AC) most Westerosi nobles cannot understand the language.(wiki:High_Valyrian.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-1)
