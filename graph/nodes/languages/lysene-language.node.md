## Origins

[Larra Rogare](wiki:Larra_Rogare), the [Lysene](wiki:Lys) wife of [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen), was fluent in [High Valyrian](wiki:High_Valyrian) and the tongues of [Myr](wiki:Myrish_language), [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyroshi_language), [Volantis](wiki:Volantene_language), and her native Lys. She made no effort to learn the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue) of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) while residing at the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep), however.(wiki:Lysene_language.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
