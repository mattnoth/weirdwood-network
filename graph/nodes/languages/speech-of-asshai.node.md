## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

[Mirri Maz Duur](wiki:Mirri_Maz_Duur), who studied in [Asshai](wiki:Asshai),(wiki:Speech_of_Asshai.cite_ref-Ragot61.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) sings in a foreign tongue while being burned by [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen), her voice described as "shrill" and "ululating".(wiki:Speech_of_Asshai.cite_ref-Ragot72.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

Before burning statues of the [Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven) taken from the [sept at Dragonstone](wiki:Sept_at_Dragonstone), [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) prays to [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor) "once in the speech of Asshai, once in [High Valyrian](wiki:High_Valyrian), and once in the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue)." The [red priestess](wiki:Red_priest) then sings in the tongue of Asshai while the statues burn.(wiki:Speech_of_Asshai.cite_ref-Racok10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-2)
