## Origins

[The daughter](wiki:Archon's_daughter_(Jaehaerys_I)) of the [Archon of Tyrosh](wiki:Archon_of_Tyrosh) during the early reign of King [Jaehaerys I Targaryen](wiki:Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen) was considered to have a delightful accent when she attended the [Golden Wedding](wiki:Golden_Wedding).(wiki:Tyroshi_language.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides_-_49_ac.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Larra Rogare](wiki:Larra_Rogare), the wife of [Viserys Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_II_Targaryen), was fluent in [High Valyrian](wiki:High_Valyrian) and the tongues of Tyrosh, [Myr](wiki:Myrish_language), [Volantis](wiki:Volantene_language), and her native [Lys](wiki:Lysene_language).(wiki:Tyroshi_language.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
[Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) learned to speak Free Cities Valyrian with a Tyroshi accent from her time living in [Tyrosh](wiki:Tyrosh) as a child.(wiki:Tyroshi_language.cite_ref-Ragot54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)
[Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) learned a few Tyroshi phrases from a [sellsword](wiki:Sellsword) he knew at [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock).(wiki:Tyroshi_language.cite_ref-Radwd1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

[Chett](wiki:Chett) considers Tyroshi to be a wet girly tongue.(wiki:Tyroshi_language.cite_ref-Rasosprologue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
[Symon Silver Tongue](wiki:Symon_Silver_Tongue) believes that [Collio Quaynis](wiki:Collio_Quaynis)'s Tyroshi accent is absurd in the [Common Tongue](wiki:Common_Tongue),(wiki:Tyroshi_language.cite_ref-Rasos32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0) and [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) finds Collio's singing to be ludicrous.(wiki:Tyroshi_language.cite_ref-Rasos60.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Near [Oldtown](wiki:Oldtown), the captain of the [Huntress](wiki:Huntress) tells [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) that [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn) captured a Tyroshi merchantman in the [Redwyne Straits](wiki:Redwyne_Straits). They killed the ship's crew and disguised themselves as Tyroshi sailors intending to sneak into Oldtown. Their plot was foiled when the oarsmaster of [Lady of the Tower](wiki:Lady_of_the_Tower) hailed the ironmen in the Tyroshi language and they failed to reply to his greeting.(wiki:Tyroshi_language.cite_ref-Raffc45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Maester [Kedry](wiki:Kedry) is fluent in the tongues of all of the [Free Cities](wiki:Free_Cities).(wiki:Tyroshi_language.cite_ref-Radwd6.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)
[Young Griff](wiki:Aegon_Targaryen_(Young_Griff)) is fluent in [High Valyrian](wiki:High_Valyrian), the dialects of Tyrosh, [Pentos](wiki:Pentoshi_language), [Myr](wiki:Myrish_language), and [Lys](wiki:Lysene_language), as well as the [trade talk](wiki:Trade_talk) of sailors.(wiki:Tyroshi_language.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)
