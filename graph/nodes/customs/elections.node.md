## Origins

Before the [coming of the Andals](wiki:Coming_of_the_Andals), the [High King of Dorne](wiki:High_King_of_Dorne) was a monarch of the [First Men](wiki:First_Men) who ruled near the [Greenblood](wiki:Greenblood). The High King was chosen from among a dozen local families, but the tradition fell apart after a contested result.(wiki:Elections.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf792_33-0)
[Urron Greyiron](wiki:Urron_Greyiron) ended the tradition of the [kingsmoot](wiki:Kingsmoot) in the [Iron Islands](wiki:Iron_Islands) by massacring rivals at [Old Wyk](wiki:Old_Wyk) and establishing the [King of the Iron Islands](wiki:King_of_the_Iron_Islands) as a hereditary position.(wiki:Elections.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf751_20-5)
During the [Century of Blood](wiki:Century_of_Blood), the beloved military commander [Horonno](wiki:Horonno) was re-elected forty years in a row as a [triarch of Volantis](wiki:Triarch_of_Volantis). When Horonno tried to declare himself triarch for life, however, he was caught by rioters and executed by being tied to two [elephants](wiki:Elephant) and ripped in half.(wiki:Elections.cite_ref-Rtwoiaf826_3-1)(wiki:Elections.cite_ref-Radwd27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-3) The [tigers](wiki:Tigers_(Volantis)) were in power for much of the Century of Blood, but after suffering numerous defeats they were finally voted out for the [elephants](wiki:Elephants_(Volantis)), who have elected at least two out of three triarchs in every annual election in [Volantis](wiki:Volantis) for the past three hundred years.(wiki:Elections.cite_ref-Radwd14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

After the death of the [previous High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Fat_One)) during the [riot of King's Landing](wiki:Riot_of_King's_Landing), [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) selects his replacement by influencing the Most Devout. Tyrion, the acting [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), believes the new High Septon will be loyal to him.(wiki:Elections.cite_ref-Racok49.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Following the death of [Jeor Mormont](wiki:Jeor_Mormont) and the [battle beneath the Wall](wiki:Battle_beneath_the_Wall), the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) holds an election to choose the next [Lord Commander of the Night's Watch](wiki:Lord_Commander_of_the_Night's_Watch). [Janos Slynt](wiki:Janos_Slynt), [Cotter Pyke](wiki:Cotter_Pyke), and Ser [Denys Mallister](wiki:Denys_Mallister) receive a number of votes, but [Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) is eventually chosen by the brothers gathered in the cellar below the [armory](wiki:Armory_(Castle_Black)) of [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black).(wiki:Elections.cite_ref-Rasos79.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-1)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) calls for a [kingsmoot on Old Wyk](wiki:Kingsmoot_on_Old_Wyk) after the death of his oldest brother, [Balon Greyjoy](wiki:Balon_Greyjoy), [King of the Isles and the North](wiki:King_of_the_Isles_and_the_North), during the [War of the Five Kings](wiki:War_of_the_Five_Kings).(wiki:Elections.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_34-0) Half a dozen major candidates put their names forward, most prominently Aeron's older brothers, [Euron](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) and [Victarion](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy), as well as Balon's daughter, [Asha Greyjoy](wiki:Asha_Greyjoy).(wiki:Elections.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-1) Each candidate in the kingsmoot is supported by three champions.(wiki:Elections.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-2) Heavily divided between Victarion's insistence on continued raiding and Asha's plea to stop before the mainland inevitably counter-attacks, the kingsmoot is eventually swayed by Euron's audacious plan to conquer all of [Westeros](wiki:Westeros) using the [dragons](wiki:Dragon) of [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen).(wiki:Elections.cite_ref-Raffc19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-3)
[Tyrion's High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Tyrion's)) dies in his sleep, secretly smothered with a pillow at the order of [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), and the [Most Devout](wiki:Most_Devout) begin the selection process to see who of them will become his successor.(wiki:Elections.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-0) Cersei expects the new High Septon will be [Torbert](wiki:Torbert) or [Raynard](wiki:Raynard).(wiki:Elections.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_35-1) According to [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn), the [sparrows](wiki:Sparrows) forcibly take over the selection process. They burst into the chambers with axes in hand, and with their leader, a small septon, on their shoulders. The Most Devout relent in fear, and name him the new High Septon. Afterwards, he is nicknamed the [High Sparrow](wiki:High_Sparrow).(wiki:Elections.cite_ref-Raffc28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
