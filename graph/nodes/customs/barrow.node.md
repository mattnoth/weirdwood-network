## Culture

The [barrowlands](wiki:Barrowlands) in the southern regions of the [north](wiki:North) have many long, low hummocks.(wiki:Barrow.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) [Barrow Hall](wiki:Barrow_Hall) by the [Great Barrow](wiki:Great_Barrow) is the seat of [House Dustin](wiki:House_Dustin) in [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton), a town within the barrowlands.(wiki:Barrow.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)
[Long Barrow](wiki:Long_Barrow) is one of the castles of the [Night's Watch](wiki:Night's_Watch) along the [Wall](wiki:Wall).(wiki:Barrow.cite_ref-Racok43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)

## Origins

The [First Men](wiki:First_Men) of antiquity buried their dead in barrows(wiki:Barrow.cite_ref-Ragot12.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) and left runes on stone monuments in barrow fields.(wiki:Barrow.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_age_of_heroes.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
Maester [Kennet](wiki:Kennet_(maester)) studied the barrow fields, graves, and tombs in the [north](wiki:North), including a barrow by [Long Lake](wiki:Long_Lake). In [Passages of the Dead](wiki:Passages_of_the_Dead), Kennet suggested that [giants](wiki:Giants) could have also buried their dead.(wiki:Barrow.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_dawn_age.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) The remains of the [First King](wiki:First_King) or a [King of the Giants](wiki:King_of_the_Giants) are said to rest in the [Great Barrow](wiki:Great_Barrow) at [Barrowton](wiki:Barrowton),(wiki:Barrow.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) and the [Barrow Kings](wiki:Barrow_Kings) were rulers in the [barrowlands](wiki:Barrowlands).(wiki:Barrow.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_north.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
