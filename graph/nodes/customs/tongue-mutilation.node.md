## Culture

A tongueless person is disadvantaged by being rendered mute, and can therefore tell no tales.(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0)(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0) Removal of the tongue can be done out of cruelty, punishment, [torture](wiki:Torture), or vengeance. The mere threat to rip out tongues can be effective.(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) Tongues can be torn out with hot pincers(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Raffc17.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Radwd19.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) or cut out with a blade.(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Radwd32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
[Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) removes the tongues of many who serve him.(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Rasos45.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1) The [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters) are falsely rumored to have had their tongues removed,(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Rasos65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) but in actuality they have taken a vow of silence.(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)

## Origins

King [Viserys I Targaryen](wiki:Viserys_I_Targaryen) decreed that any person who questioned the paternity of his daughter [Rhaenyra's](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) children with Ser [Laenor Velaryon](wiki:Laenor_Velaryon) would have their tongues ripped out.(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_viserys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)
King [Aerys II Targaryen](wiki:Aerys_II_Targaryen) had Ser [Ilyn Payne](wiki:Ilyn_Payne)'s tongue ripped out with pincers(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Ragot15.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0) as punishment for claiming that Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister), the [Hand of the King](wiki:Hand_of_the_King), was the one who truly ruled the [Seven Kingdoms](wiki:Seven_Kingdoms).(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)
[Varys](wiki:Varys) employs children with their tongues removed as his [little birds](wiki:Little_birds).(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Ragot32.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) espies the [Silence](wiki:Silence) and is disturbed to note that her motley crew of mutes and mongrels speak no word as the [Iron Victory](wiki:Iron_Victory) sails near. (wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Raffc18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0) Victarion later confides his doubts regarding [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) to the tongueless [dusky woman](wiki:Dusky_woman).(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Raffc29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)
Lord [Robert Arryn](wiki:Robert_Arryn) complains about [Marillion](wiki:Marillion)'s singing and says to [Alayne Stone](wiki:Alayne_Stone) that her supposed father, Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) should have cut out the imprisoned singer's tongue. Alayne thinks to herself that Marillion needed his tongue so that he could confess to the murder of Lady [Lysa Arryn](wiki:Lysa_Arryn).(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Raffc23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) reveals many of his darker secrets to [Ilyn Payne](wiki:Ilyn_Payne), knowing the mute and illiterate knight can never reveal them to anyone.(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Raffc27.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)
The normally placid King [Tommen I Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon) becomes furious when his mother says she would gladly have [Margaery Tyrell](wiki:Margaery_Tyrell)'s tongue torn out.(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0) To punish him she orders that Tommen is to whip [Pate](wiki:Pate_(whipping_boy)) and if His Grace refuses or protests, [Qyburn](wiki:Qyburn) is to be summoned to remove Pate's tongue. (wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Raffc36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Skahaz mo Kandaq](wiki:Skahaz_mo_Kandaq) advises Queen [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) to at least rip out [Hazzea](wiki:Hazzea)'s father's tongue to silence him about the girl's death.(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Radwd11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
[Daario Naharis](wiki:Daario_Naharis) cuts out the tongue of one of the [Long Lances](wiki:Long_Lances) for saying [Meereen](wiki:Meereen) would soon be under siege.(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Radwd23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
When [Ralf the Limper](wiki:Ralf_the_Limper) suggests that the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) is on a cursed voyage, Victarion slaps him twice and threatens to nail his tongue to the mast.(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-Radwd56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-2)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

[Falia Flowers](wiki:Falia_Flowers) is lashed next to [Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) onto the prow of the [Silence](wiki:Silence). He sees that she is naked, pregnant and her tongue has been cut out.(wiki:Tongue_mutilation.cite_ref-TWOW_19-0)
