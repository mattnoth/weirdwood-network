## Culture

In Westeros, children are named shortly after their birth.(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne_-_their_triumphs_and_tragedies.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Rfabbirth.2C_death.2C_and_betrayal_under_king_jaehaerys_i.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0) A person's age is counted in years since their name day.(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0) Among the [free folk](wiki:Free_folk), children aren't named until their second or third year – it's considered bad luck to a name a child sooner, because so many infants die young.(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Rasos46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Rasos75.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0)
In the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), newborns are named in a [sept](wiki:Sept) and anointed with the seven oils.(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Ragot2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Ragot48.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0) Among the [ironborn](wiki:Ironborn), newborns may be [ritually drowned](wiki:Drowned_God) on their name day.(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)
A person celebrating their name day may receive gifts from friends and family, and a feast may be held for them.(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Ragot31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Ragot67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-1) A [tourney](wiki:Tourney) may be held to celebrate a prince or king or noble's name day.(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Ragot18.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-0)(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Ragot25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-0)(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-2)(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Rthk.7B.7B.7B2.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_14-0)(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_aerys_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_15-0)
In Westeros, a person reaches their [age of majority](wiki:Age_of_majority) on their sixteenth name day, after which they are considered "a man grown" or "a woman grown".(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-ageofsexualrelations_16-0)(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-17)
There are expressions such as "naked as his/her name day"(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Rasos26.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) and "wearing their name day suits"(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1) or "in her name day gown".(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Rasos24.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0) The latter two phrases are the equivalent of the real-world expression "birthday suit", meaning wearing no clothes at all.(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-21)
There is a song called "[The Name Day Boy](wiki:The_Name_Day_Boy)".(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Rasos43.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)

## Origins

[Steffarion Sparr](wiki:Steffarion_Sparr) and [Gormond Goodbrother](wiki:Gormond_Goodbrother) were ritually drowned in service to the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God) on their name day.(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Raffc1.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-1)
[Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm) received gifts from his father, King [Robert I Baratheon](wiki:Robert_I_Baratheon), on his name days.(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Rasos36.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

On her fourteenth name day, [Daenerys Targaryen](wiki:Daenerys_Targaryen) realizes she is pregnant by her husband, [Khal](wiki:Khal) [Drogo](wiki:Drogo).(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Ragot23.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)
Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey) marries his eighth wife, [Joyeuse Erenford](wiki:Joyeuse_Erenford), on his ninetieth name day.(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Ragot28.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark) claims that it is bad luck to kill a man on your name day, to prevent King [Joffrey Baratheon](wiki:Joffrey_Baratheon) from executing Ser [Dontos Hollard](wiki:Dontos_Hollard) at the [tourney for his thirteenth name day](wiki:Tourney_on_King_Joffrey's_name_day). She believes she made up the custom, and is surprised to hear the [Kingsguard](wiki:Kingsguard) [Sandor Clegane](wiki:Sandor_Clegane) support the claim.(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Racok2.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-3)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Three-Finger Hobb](wiki:Three-Finger_Hobb) gives a salted ham to [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) for his name day.(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0) As the [Braavosi](wiki:Braavos) and [Westerosi](wiki:Westeros) [measure](wiki:Measurement) time differently, [Arya Stark](wiki:Arya_Stark) does not remember when her name day is.(wiki:Name_day.cite_ref-Raffc22.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)
