## Origins

Some of the oldest tales of [Garth Greenhand](wiki:Garth_Greenhand) claim he demanded blood sacrifice from his worshippers to ensure a bountiful harvest.(wiki:Blood_sacrifice.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reach:_garth_greenhand.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_16-0)
In legends about the [hammer of the waters](wiki:Hammer_of_the_waters), hundreds of [greenseers](wiki:Greenseers) from the [children of the forest](wiki:Children_of_the_forest) are said to have fed a thousand captive men to the [weirwood](wiki:Weirwood). Alternatively, the greenseers are said to have used the blood of their own young.(wiki:Blood_sacrifice.cite_ref-Rtwoiafdorne:_the_breaking.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_17-0)
Because the [Night's King](wiki:Night's_King) sacrificed to the [Others](wiki:Others), all records of him were destroyed after his fall.(wiki:Blood_sacrifice.cite_ref-Rasos56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_18-0)
King [Qhored I Hoare](wiki:Qhored_I_Hoare) had King [Bernarr II Justman](wiki:Bernarr_II_Justman) drowned as a sacrifice to the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God).(wiki:Blood_sacrifice.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_driftwood_crowns.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_19-0) [Aubrey Crakehall](wiki:Aubrey_Crakehall) was sacrificed to the sea by the [Shrike](wiki:Shrike).(wiki:Blood_sacrifice.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_iron_islands:_the_black_blood.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_20-0)
When [Ice Eyes](wiki:Brandon_Stark_(Ice_Eyes)) recovered the [Wolf's Den](wiki:Wolf's_Den) from [slavers](wiki:Slavers), slaves freed from the dungeon are said to have hung the slavers' entrails in the limbs of [a weirwood](wiki:Godswood_of_White_Harbor) as an offering to the [old gods](wiki:Old_gods).(wiki:Blood_sacrifice.cite_ref-Radwd29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)
The [Patrimony of Hyrkoon](wiki:Patrimony_of_Hyrkoon) sacrificed tens of thousands of [Jogos Nhai](wiki:Jogos_Nhai) to their dark and hungry gods.(wiki:Blood_sacrifice.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_bones_and_beyond:_the_plains_of_the_jogos_nhai.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_21-0)
Maester [Pol](wiki:Pol) wrote that he was exiled from [Qohor](wiki:Qohor) after discovering that blood sacrifice was used by the city's smiths to work [Valyrian steel](wiki:Valyrian_steel), with slaves even young as infants being slain.(wiki:Blood_sacrifice.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_free_cities:_qohor.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_12-1)
Before battle, [Racallio Ryndoon](wiki:Racallio_Ryndoon) would throw the bones to determine which god he should honor with a sacrifice.(wiki:Blood_sacrifice.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents_-_the_voyage_of_alyn_oakenfist.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_22-0)
During the [Lysene Spring](wiki:Lysene_Spring), there were rumors in [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) that blood sacrifices were being made in [Maegor's Holdfast](wiki:Maegor's_Holdfast).(wiki:Blood_sacrifice.cite_ref-Rfabthe_lysene_spring_and_the_end_of_regency.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_23-0)
Legends from [Pentos](wiki:Pentos) claim that [Hukko](wiki:Hukko) slew seven [swan maidens](wiki:Swan_maidens) as sacrifices to his gods.(wiki:Blood_sacrifice.cite_ref-Rtwoiafancient_history:_the_arrival_of_the_andals.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_24-0) After the execution of the [Prince of Pentos](wiki:Prince_of_Pentos), the [Tattered Prince](wiki:Tattered_Prince) fled after being chosen as the new prince by [magisters](wiki:Magisters) of the city.(wiki:Blood_sacrifice.cite_ref-Radwd25.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_25-0)

## Narrative Arc

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Aeron Greyjoy](wiki:Aeron_Greyjoy) drowns [Benfred Tallhart](wiki:Benfred_Tallhart) in seawater during the [harrying of the Stony Shore](wiki:Harrying_of_the_Stony_Shore),(wiki:Blood_sacrifice.cite_ref-Racok37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1) and [Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) has Septon [Chayle](wiki:Chayle) thrown into a well to drown after the [capture of Winterfell](wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell).(wiki:Blood_sacrifice.cite_ref-Racok50.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord [Guncer Sunglass](wiki:Guncer_Sunglass) and the surviving sons of Ser [Hubard Rambton](wiki:Hubard_Rambton), all imprisoned by [Stannis Baratheon](wiki:Stannis_Baratheon) for treason, are burned by [Melisandre](wiki:Melisandre) at [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone).(wiki:Blood_sacrifice.cite_ref-Rasos10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_13-1) The [red priestess](wiki:Red_priest) desires to sacrifice [Edric Storm](wiki:Edric_Storm), believing that doing so will awaken a [stone dragon](wiki:Stone_dragon).(wiki:Blood_sacrifice.cite_ref-Rasos54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_26-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord [Alester Florent](wiki:Alester_Florent) has been burned as a sacrifice to [R'hllor](wiki:R'hllor) as a traitor by Melisandre.(wiki:Blood_sacrifice.cite_ref-Raffcappendix.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_27-0)
It is rumored that [Euron Greyjoy](wiki:Euron_Greyjoy) appeases the [Storm God](wiki:Storm_God) with blood sacrifice.(wiki:Blood_sacrifice.cite_ref-Rasos29.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_28-0)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Suffering from a blizzard during Stannis's [march on Winterfell](wiki:March_on_Winterfell), some of the [queen's men](wiki:Queen's_men) desire to make a sacrifice to R'hllor to end the storm.(wiki:Blood_sacrifice.cite_ref-Radwd42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_29-0) Four [Peasebury](wiki:House_Peasebury) soldiers are burned by queen's men at a [crofters' village](wiki:Crofters'_village) after being accused of [cannibalism](wiki:Cannibalism).(wiki:Blood_sacrifice.cite_ref-Radwd62.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_30-0)
After the [Iron Fleet](wiki:Iron_Fleet) captures [Ghiscari Dawn](wiki:Ghiscari_Dawn), [Victarion Greyjoy](wiki:Victarion_Greyjoy) has its captain bound and thrown overboard as a sacrifice to the [Drowned God](wiki:Drowned_God).(wiki:Blood_sacrifice.cite_ref-Radwd63.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_31-0)
