## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

After he falls from the [First Keep](wiki:First_Keep), [Bran Stark](wiki:Bran_Stark) is brought to the sickroom of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell). [Catelyn Stark](wiki:Catelyn_Stark) stays by her son's side as Bran lies comatose.(wiki:Sickroom.cite_ref-Ragot10.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-0) A [catspaw](wiki:Catspaw) attempts to assassinate Bran, but the boy is saved by [his direwolf](wiki:Summer).(wiki:Sickroom.cite_ref-Ragot14.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

The solar of [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully) at [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) is used as his sickroom as his condition worsens.(wiki:Sickroom.cite_ref-Racok7.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
After the [Battle of the Blackwater](wiki:Battle_of_the_Blackwater), the wounded [Tyrion Lannister](wiki:Tyrion_Lannister) is treated by Maester [Ballabar](wiki:Ballabar) in a chamber over the [Queen's Ballroom](wiki:Queen's_Ballroom). [Podrick Payne](wiki:Podrick_Payne), Tyrion's squire, is reluctant to visit the sickroom.(wiki:Sickroom.cite_ref-Racok67.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
