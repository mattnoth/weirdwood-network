## Origins

After Queen [Rhaenyra Targaryen](wiki:Rhaenyra_Targaryen) fled [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) during the [Dance of the Dragons](wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons), Ser [Garth the Harelip](wiki:Garth_the_Harelip) surrendered the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep) to Ser [Perkin the Flea](wiki:Perkin_the_Flea) and his [gutter knights](wiki:Gutter_knights). [Mysaria](wiki:Mysaria), Rhaenyra's hated [mistress of whisperers](wiki:Mistress_of_whisperers), was taken while attempting to flee the castle. Perkin informed Mysaria that if she could travel naked through the city to the [Gate of the Gods](wiki:Gate_of_the_Gods) while being whipped, she would be allowed to live. Mysaria only made it halfway before dying on the city's cobblestones.(wiki:Walk_of_atonement.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)
Lord [Tytos Lannister](wiki:Tytos_Lannister)'s [mistress](wiki:Tytos_Lannister's_second_mistress) was a [common-born](wiki:Smallfolk) woman and the daughter of a chandler. She had dominated Tytos utterly, ordered the household [knights](wiki:Knights) about, dismissed servants, and sat in attendance at court when Tytos was absent.(wiki:Walk_of_atonement.cite_ref-Rasos66.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)(wiki:Walk_of_atonement.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1) When Tytos died, his son [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) returned to [Casterly Rock](wiki:Casterly_Rock) to find the woman wearing one of the gowns of his late mother, [Jeyne Marbrand](wiki:Jeyne_Marbrand), as well as her [jewelry](wiki:Jewelry).(wiki:Walk_of_atonement.cite_ref-Raffc3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0) Tywin had her stripped naked and forced the sobbing woman to walk through the streets of [Lannisport](wiki:Lannisport) into exile at the docks, confessing to every man she met that she was a thief and a [harlot](wiki:Whore).(wiki:Walk_of_atonement.cite_ref-Raffc3.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-1)(wiki:Walk_of_atonement.cite_ref-Radwd65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-4)(wiki:Walk_of_atonement.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-2)

## Narrative Arc

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

In King's Landing, Dowager Queen [Cersei Lannister](wiki:Cersei_Lannister), imprisoned by the [Faith of the Seven](wiki:Faith_of_the_Seven), confesses to fornication (sleeping with [Lancel Lannister](wiki:Lancel_Lannister) and the [Kettleblack](wiki:House_Kettleblack) brothers after the death of her husband, King [Robert Baratheon](wiki:Robert_Baratheon)).(wiki:Walk_of_atonement.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) While she still must stand trial for the crimes she denies ([incest](wiki:Incest), adultery, Robert's murder, and the murder of the previous [High Septon](wiki:High_Septon_(Tyrion's))), Cersei's uncle, [Lord Regent](wiki:Lord_Regent) [Kevan Lannister](wiki:Kevan_Lannister), arranges with the [High Septon](wiki:High_Sparrow) for Cersei to perform a walk of atonement for her admitted sins.(wiki:Walk_of_atonement.cite_ref-Radwd54.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)(wiki:Walk_of_atonement.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-3) Cersei does not want to agree, but eventually accedes, as it is the only way to leave her imprisonment and return to her son, King [Tommen Baratheon](wiki:Tommen_Baratheon).(wiki:Walk_of_atonement.cite_ref-Radwd65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-5)
Cersei is shaved of hair from her head and her entire body by [silent sisters](wiki:Silent_sisters), then stripped naked, before beginning her barefoot walk from the [Great Sept of Baelor](wiki:Great_Sept_of_Baelor) to the [Red Keep](wiki:Red_Keep). An escort of [Warrior's Sons](wiki:Warrior's_Sons), [Poor Fellows](wiki:Poor_Fellows), and several [septas](wiki:Septa) protect her from the leering and jeering crowds that have flocked to see her shame. Septa [Scolera](wiki:Scolera) follows behind Cersei, ringing a bell and singing, "Shame, shame, shame upon the sinner, shame, shame." Cersei tries to hang on to her pride during the humiliating walk, in spite of the people throwing filth at her and yelling bawdy insults. But she eventually begins to hallucinate, then breaks down in tears and runs while trying to cover herself, stumbling and crawling up [the hill](wiki:Aegon's_Hill) to the Red Keep. Once Cersei enters the gates, her [handmaid](wiki:Handmaid) [Jocelyn Swyft](wiki:Jocelyn_Swyft) covers her with a blanket, and Ser [Robert Strong](wiki:Robert_Strong) carries her into the castle.(wiki:Walk_of_atonement.cite_ref-Radwd65.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-6)
Afterwards, Ser Kevan feels his niece has lost her power and that he has "pulled her claws". Kevan tells himself he has no reason to feel guilty, as he acted for the good of [House Lannister](wiki:House_Lannister).(wiki:Walk_of_atonement.cite_ref-Radwdepilogue.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-4)
