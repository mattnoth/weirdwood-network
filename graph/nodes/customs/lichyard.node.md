## Narrative Arc

### A Game of Thrones[ ]

The [bones](wiki:Bone) of [Lady](wiki:Lady) are buried in the lichyard of [Winterfell](wiki:Winterfell) after her remains are returned from [Darry](wiki:Darry).(wiki:Lichyard.cite_ref-Ragot53.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-1)

### A Clash of Kings[ ]

[Mikken](wiki:Mikken) is buried at Winterfell after his execution.(wiki:Lichyard.cite_ref-Racok56.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

When [Sansa Starks](wiki:Sansa_Stark) builds a snow castle of Winterfell while at the [Eyrie](wiki:Eyrie), she uses bark to represent its lichyard.(wiki:Lichyard.cite_ref-Rasos80.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

[Jon Snow](wiki:Jon_Snow) has [Samwell Tarly](wiki:Samwell_Tarly) depart [Castle Black](wiki:Castle_Black) for [Eastwatch-by-the-Sea](wiki:Eastwatch-by-the-Sea) at first light from Castle Black's lichyard.(wiki:Lichyard.cite_ref-Raffc5.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-1)
[Brienne of Tarth](wiki:Brienne_of_Tarth) spots a lame [gravedigger](wiki:Gravedigger) in the lichyard of the [Quiet Isle](wiki:Quiet_Isle).(wiki:Lichyard.cite_ref-Raffc31.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_1-1)

### A Dance with Dragons[ ]

[Theon Greyjoy](wiki:Theon_Greyjoy) thinks of Winterfell, damaged from [its sack](wiki:Sack_of_Winterfell) by [Ramsay Bolton](wiki:Ramsay_Snow), as a "half-frozen lichyard of a castle".(wiki:Lichyard.cite_ref-Radwd37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) The body of Ser [Aenys Frey](wiki:Aenys_Frey)'s squire is found naked in the castle's lichyard.(wiki:Lichyard.cite_ref-Radwd46.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0) [Borroq](wiki:Borroq) settles with his [boar](wiki:Boar) in the Castle Black's lichyard.(wiki:Lichyard.cite_ref-Radwd69.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)

### The Winds of Winter[ ]

The play [The Bloody Hand](wiki:The_Bloody_Hand) starts in a lichyard.(wiki:Lichyard.cite_ref-9)
