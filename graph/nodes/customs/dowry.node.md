## Origins

### Targaryen Era[ ]

King [Argilac Durrandon](wiki:Argilac_Durrandon) wished to create an alliance with Lord [Aegon Targaryen](wiki:Aegon_I_Targaryen) of [Dragonstone](wiki:Dragonstone) in order to thwart [Harren the Black](wiki:Harren_Hoare) to conquer more lands. Argilac offered his [maiden](wiki:Maiden) daughter, [Argella](wiki:Argella_Durrandon), in marriage to Aegon with all the lands east of the [God's Eye](wiki:God's_Eye) as her dowry. However, Aegon refused the proposal as he was already married to his two sisters, [Visenya](wiki:Visenya_Targaryen) and [Rhaenys](wiki:Rhaenys_Targaryen).(wiki:Dowry.cite_ref-Rfabaegon.27s_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_2-0)(wiki:Dowry.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons:_the_conquest.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_3-0)
According to Grand Maester [Munkun](wiki:Munkun), Lord [Borros Baratheon](wiki:Borros_Baratheon) and Prince [Aemond Targaryen](wiki:Aemond_Targaryen) were negotiating dowries for the upcoming marriage of Aemond to one of Borros' [daughters](wiki:Four_Storms) when Prince [Lucerys Velaryon](wiki:Lucerys_Velaryon) arrived at [Storm's End](wiki:Storm's_End).(wiki:Dowry.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_a_son_for_a_son.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_4-0)
King [Daeron II Targaryen](wiki:Daeron_II_Targaryen) paid the dowry his late father, King [Aegon IV](wiki:Aegon_IV_Targaryen), had promised to the [Archon of Tyrosh](wiki:Archon_of_Tyrosh) so that his half-brother, Ser [Daemon Blackfyre](wiki:Daemon_I_Blackfyre), could marry [Rohanne of Tyrosh](wiki:Rohanne_of_Tyrosh).(wiki:Dowry.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_daeron_ii.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_5-0)
Lord [Tywin Lannister](wiki:Tywin_Lannister) intended to [betroth](wiki:Betrothal) his son and heir, [Jaime](wiki:Jaime_Lannister), to Lord [Hoster Tully](wiki:Hoster_Tully)'s second daughter, [Lysa](wiki:Lysa_Arryn). Jaime, who was [fostered](wiki:Fosterage) at [Crakehall](wiki:Crakehall) at the time, was even sent to [Riverrun](wiki:Riverrun) once so that Hoster could see him in person.(wiki:Dowry.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-0) It seems Hoster was happy with the match as Tywin later invited him to [King's Landing](wiki:King's_Landing) to discuss the question of Lysa's dowery.(wiki:Dowry.cite_ref-Rasos11.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_7-0)

### Baratheon Era[ ]

Lord [Nestor Royce](wiki:Nestor_Royce) once hoped to marry his daughter, [Myranda](wiki:Myranda_Royce), to Lady [Anya Waynwood](wiki:Anya_Waynwood)'s ward, [Harrold Hardyng](wiki:Harrold_Hardyng), but Anya refused. Myranda does not know if it is her or her dowry that Lady Anya found unsuitable.(wiki:Dowry.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-0)
The only daughter of Lord [Paxter Redwyne](wiki:Paxter_Redwyne), [Desmera](wiki:Desmera_Redwyne), is known to be nicely dowered.(wiki:Dowry.cite_ref-Raffc33.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_6-1)

## Narrative Arc

### A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord [Roose Bolton](wiki:Roose_Bolton) agrees to take a [Frey](wiki:House_Frey) for his new wife. He chooses the fattest Frey maiden, [Walda](wiki:Walda_Frey_(daughter_of_Merrett)), because the patriarch of the family, Lord [Walder Frey](wiki:Walder_Frey), has offered him the bride's weight in [silver](wiki:Silver) as a dowry.(wiki:Dowry.cite_ref-Rasos37.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_9-0)(wiki:Dowry.cite_ref-Rasos51.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_10-0)

### A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser [Jaime Lannister](wiki:Jaime_Lannister) promises his captive, Ser [Edmure Tully](wiki:Edmure_Tully), that if his pregnant wife, [Roslin Frey](wiki:Roslin_Frey), gives him a daughter, then the [Lannisters](wiki:House_Lannister) will give her a good dowry when she is old enough to marry.(wiki:Dowry.cite_ref-Raffc38.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_11-0)
Lord [Petyr Baelish](wiki:Petyr_Baelish) arranges the marriage of Lord [Lyonel Corbray](wiki:Lyonel_Corbray) of [Heart's Home](wiki:Heart's_Home) to the sixteen-year-old daughter of a rich [Gulltown](wiki:Gulltown) merchant. Lyonel is pleased with the match but even more so with her immense dowry.(wiki:Dowry.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-1)(wiki:Dowry.cite_ref-TWOW_12-0)
Petyr later offers Lady Anya Waynwood an even bigger dowery than the one Lyonel received to marry his daughter Alayne Stone (in reality [Sansa Stark](wiki:Sansa_Stark)) to Harrold Hardyng. Anya accepts his offer.(wiki:Dowry.cite_ref-Raffc42.7B.7B.7B3.7D.7D.7D.7B.7B.7B4.7D.7D.7D_8-2)(wiki:Dowry.cite_ref-TWOW_12-1)
