## Identity

Ser Morgarth the Merry is a hedge knight who enters the service of Lord Petyr Baelish (wiki:Morgarth_the_Merry.cite_ref-Raffc41).

## Appearances & Description

Morgarth is burly with a thick salt-and-pepper beard. He has a red, bulbous nose with broken veins and large, gnarled hands (wiki:Morgarth_the_Merry.cite_ref-Raffc41).

## Narrative Arc

Along with Ser Shadrich and Ser Byron the Beautiful, Ser Morgarth enters the service of Lord Petyr Baelish at the Gates of the Moon. He remarks that Petyr did not mention that his daughter Alayne Stone is beautiful (wiki:Morgarth_the_Merry.cite_ref-Raffc41).
