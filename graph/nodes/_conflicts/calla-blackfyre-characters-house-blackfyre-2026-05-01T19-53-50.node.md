## Identity

Calla Blackfyre was the eldest daughter of Daemon I Blackfyre and Rohanne of Tyrosh (track_b: Father, Mother). She was of Valyrian race and Crownlands culture (track_b: Race, Culture).

## Origins

Her father was Daemon I Blackfyre and her mother was Rohanne of Tyrosh (track_b: Father, Mother).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Blackfyre (track_b: Allegiance). She was promised in marriage to her half-uncle Aegor Rivers, known as Bittersteel (track_b: Spouse, qualifier: promised).

## Narrative Arc

Calla was betrothed to Aegor Rivers (Bittersteel), her father's half-brother. This betrothal was part of a political arrangement: after Daemon agreed to the match between Calla and Aegor, Bittersteel increased his efforts to press Daemon's claim to the Iron Throne (wiki:Calla_Blackfyre). It is unknown whether the marriage actually took place (wiki:Calla_Blackfyre).
