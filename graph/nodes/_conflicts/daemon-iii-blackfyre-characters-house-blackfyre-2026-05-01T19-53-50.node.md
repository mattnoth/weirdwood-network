## Identity

Daemon III Blackfyre was the eldest son of Haegon I Blackfyre and a grandson of Daemon I Blackfyre (track_b: Father). He was of Valyrian race and Free Cities culture, having been born and raised in exile (track_b: Race, Culture).

## Origins

Born in or after 202 AC in exile. His father was Haegon I Blackfyre (track_b: Father).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Blackfyre (track_b: Allegiance). He claimed the title `HOLDS_TITLE` King of the Andals, the Rhoynar, and the First Men (track_b: Title, qualifier: claimant).

## Narrative Arc

Born in exile, Daemon was the eldest son of Haegon, the fourth son of Daemon I Blackfyre. Following the death of his father Haegon and the escape of Aegor Rivers (Bittersteel) from captivity, Daemon was crowned king by Bittersteel (wiki:Daemon_III_Blackfyre). He led the Fourth Blackfyre Rebellion, landing on Massey's Hook with Bittersteel and the Golden Company. Few rallied to his banners. King Aegon V Targaryen rode out to meet him with his three sons. In the Battle of Wendwater Bridge in 236 AC, Daemon was slain by Ser Duncan the Tall of the Kingsguard, and the Blackfyres suffered a shattering defeat (wiki:Daemon_III_Blackfyre).
