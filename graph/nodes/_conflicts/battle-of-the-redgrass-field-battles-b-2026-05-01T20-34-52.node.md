## Identity

The Battle of the Redgrass Field was the largest battle of the First Blackfyre Rebellion, fought in late 196 AC. It effectively ended the rebellion and confirmed King Daeron II Targaryen's rule of the Seven Kingdoms (wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field).

## Allegiances

Part of `FIGHTS_IN` First Blackfyre Rebellion. Loyalists (Princes Baelor and Maekar Targaryen, Brynden Rivers and the Raven's Teeth, Houses Arryn, Hayford, Templeton, Waynwood, Webber) fought against rebels (Daemon I Blackfyre, Aegor Rivers/Bittersteel, Houses Blackfyre, Costayne, Peake, Shawney, Osgrey) (track_b: Conflict).

## Narrative Arc

Daemon Blackfyre, legitimized bastard of Aegon IV who had received the Valyrian steel sword Blackfyre, claimed the Iron Throne in 196 AC. On the eve of battle, Ser Quentyn Ball was slain by a common archer. Daemon proved unstoppable in battle, cutting through Lord Donnel Arryn's van and dueling Ser Gwayne Corbray of the Kingsguard (Blackfyre vs. Lady Forlorn). Daemon paused to show mercy to the wounded Gwayne. During this lull, Brynden Rivers (Bloodraven) and the Raven's Teeth crested the Weeping Ridge and rained arrows on Daemon's position, killing Daemon and both his elder twin sons Aegon and Aemon. Bittersteel rallied the rebels and fought Bloodraven, taking his eye, but Prince Baelor Breakspear struck the rebel rear with stormlords and Dornishmen, shattering their lines. Ten thousand men died. Bittersteel fled to the Free Cities with Daemon's surviving children (wiki:Battle_of_the_Redgrass_Field).
