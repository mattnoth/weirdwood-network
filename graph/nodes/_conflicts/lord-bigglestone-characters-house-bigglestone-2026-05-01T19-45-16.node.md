## Allegiances

Lord Bigglestone was `SWORN_TO` House Bigglestone and supported the `SWORN_TO` Blacks faction during the Dance of the Dragons, siding with Rhaenyra Targaryen. (track_b: Allegiances) (wiki:Lord_Bigglestone.cite_ref-Rtpatq-1)

## Narrative Arc

During the Dance of the Dragons, Lord Bigglestone sided with the blacks and Rhaenyra Targaryen. He joined Lord Chambers, Lord Perryn, and Ser Pate of Longleaf and his ragged band of survivors from earlier battles. Together they fought in the Battle by the Lakeshore on the western shore of the Gods Eye. Lord Bigglestone did not survive the battle, dying in 130 AC. (wiki:Lord_Bigglestone.cite_ref-Rtpatq-1) (wiki:Lord_Bigglestone.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons-2)
