Sour Alyn is a man-at-arms sworn to House Bolton and one of the Bastard's Boys. (wiki:Sour_Alyn.cite_ref-Radwd12)

## Appearances & Description

Has rotten teeth and foul breath. (wiki:Sour_Alyn.cite_ref-Radwd32) Described as mean and witless. Wields a sword. (wiki:Sour_Alyn.cite_ref-Radwd51; wiki:Sour_Alyn.cite_ref-Radwd41)

## Narrative Arc

Sour Alyn is present at the Dreadfort when Theon is brought before Ramsay. (wiki:Sour_Alyn.cite_ref-Radwd12) At Winterfell, he and Skinner toss a freerider from the Battlements Gate after the man angers Ramsay. (wiki:Sour_Alyn.cite_ref-Radwd46) Sour Alyn and Grunt are on guard at Ramsay's bedchamber when the spearwives go with Theon to rescue "Arya Stark" (Jeyne Poole). (wiki:Sour_Alyn.cite_ref-Radwd51)
