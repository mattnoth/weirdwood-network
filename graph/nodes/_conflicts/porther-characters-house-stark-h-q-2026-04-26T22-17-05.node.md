## Identity

Porther is a member of the household guard of Lord Eddard Stark at Winterfell (wiki:Porther).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Eddard Stark considers Porther brave but headstrong (wiki:Porther.cite_ref-Ragot47).

## Narrative Arc

Porther accompanies Lord Eddard to King's Landing when Ned is named Hand of the King (wiki:Porther.cite_ref-Ragot22). While in the capital, Porther is known to frequent brothels (wiki:Porther.cite_ref-Ragot27). He is killed during the purge of Stark men. Bran Stark lists Porther as dead (wiki:Porther.cite_ref-Racok21). Theon has a nightmare of eating with the dead at Winterfell, including Porther (wiki:Porther.cite_ref-Racok56).

## Quotes
