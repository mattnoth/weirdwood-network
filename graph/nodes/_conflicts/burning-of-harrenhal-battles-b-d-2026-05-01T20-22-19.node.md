## Identity

The Burning of Harrenhal was the destruction of Harrenhal by dragonfire during Aegon's Conquest, circa 2 BC. King Aegon I Targaryen rode Balerion the Black Dread over the castle, melting its towers and killing King Harren the Black and his sons within. (wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal)

## Narrative Arc

King Harren Hoare had just completed Harrenhal, the largest castle in Westeros, when Aegon began his conquest. Harren refused to yield, trusting in his massive walls. Aegon flew Balerion over the walls and bathed the castle in dragonfire. The intense heat melted and twisted the stone towers. Harren and all his sons burned within the castle, extinguishing House Hoare. The riverlords who had been Hoare subjects — led by Edmyn Tully — rose against the ironborn, and Aegon granted the riverlands to House Tully. Harrenhal was subsequently awarded to Quenton Qoherys, Aegon's master-at-arms. (wiki:Burning_of_Harrenhal, acok-30)
