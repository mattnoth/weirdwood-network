## Identity

The Butcher's Ball was a battle during the Dance of the Dragons in 130 AC. It resulted in the destruction of the green army led by Ser Criston Cole, the Kingmaker. (wiki:Butcher's_Ball)

## Narrative Arc

Ser Criston Cole marched his green host through the riverlands but was intercepted by riverlord forces loyal to the blacks. Cole's army was ambushed and routed. Criston Cole himself was killed by arrows — accounts differ on whether he died fighting or knelt to accept death. His army was slaughtered, and the battle earned its name from the one-sided nature of the carnage. The defeat was a major blow to the green cause. (wiki:Butcher's_Ball)
