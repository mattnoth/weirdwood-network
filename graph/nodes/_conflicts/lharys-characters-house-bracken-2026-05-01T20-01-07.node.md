## Identity

Lharys is a man-at-arms in service to House Bracken. (wiki:Lharys)

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Bracken (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Rivermen (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

An ungainly man with wild tufts of rust-colored hair sticking out of a conical cap. (wiki:Lharys.cite_ref-1) Pass 1 notes he serves as lookout and scout. (agot-tyrion-04)

## Narrative Arc

Along with Mohor and Kurleket, Lharys is one of three Bracken men-at-arms at the crossroads inn who assist Lady Catelyn Stark in capturing Tyrion Lannister. He escorts the party into the Mountains of the Moon. (wiki:Lharys.cite_ref-1) Lharys is the only Bracken man-at-arms to survive the first attack from Vale mountain clans. He claims Jyck's horse in the aftermath. He is slain in the party's second clash with clansmen. (wiki:Lharys.cite_ref-2)
