## Identity

Petyr Baelish, commonly known as Littlefinger, is the Lord and head of House Baelish (wiki:Petyr_Baelish.cite_ref-Ragot18). He serves as master of coin on the small council of King Robert I Baratheon (wiki:Petyr_Baelish.cite_ref-Ragot20). Petyr wears a mockingbird as his personal sigil and has replaced the traditional Baelish arms (depicting the Titan's head) with a field of silver mockingbirds on green (wiki:Petyr_Baelish.cite_ref-Ragot18, wiki:Petyr_Baelish.cite_ref-Ragot25). The nickname "Littlefinger" was given to him in youth by Edmure Tully on account of his short size and slender build and the smallness of his family's holdings on the Fingers (wiki:Petyr_Baelish.cite_ref-Ragot18).

## Origins

Born in 268 AC on the Fingers in the Vale of Arryn (track_b: Born). His father was "the smallest of small lords," his grandfather a hedge knight, and his great-grandfather a Braavosi sellsword in the service of Lord Corbray (wiki:Petyr_Baelish.cite_ref-Racok17). His mother was named Alayne (wiki:Petyr_Baelish.cite_ref-Rasos68). His father befriended Hoster Tully during the War of the Ninepenny Kings, and this friendship led to Petyr being fostered as a ward at Riverrun (wiki:Petyr_Baelish.cite_ref-Racok17).

## Appearances & Description

Short man of slender build with sharp features and a small pointed beard. Dark hair with threads of silver (also described as a silver streak). Laughing gray-green eyes, though Sansa notes that when he laughs his eyes do not. Breath always fresh, smelling like mint, which he enjoyed chewing as a boy (wiki:Petyr_Baelish.cite_ref-Ragot18, wiki:Petyr_Baelish.cite_ref-Raffc10). Typically wears fine clothing — slashed velvet doublets, plum-colored doublets with mockingbird embroidery, blue velvets with a silver mockingbird cape clasp (agot-eddard-04, agot-eddard-12, agot-eddard-13).

## Narrative Arc

Raised at Riverrun alongside Catelyn, Lysa, and Edmure Tully. Fell in love with Catelyn and challenged her betrothed, Brandon Stark, to a duel — nearly dying before Catelyn asked Brandon to spare him. Lysa nursed him back to health (agot-catelyn-07). Lysa convinced Jon Arryn to give Petyr the customs at Gulltown, where he increased incomes tenfold, then brought him to court in King's Landing. Within three years he became master of coin (wiki:Petyr_Baelish.cite_ref-Racok17).

In AGOT, Petyr intercepts Catelyn in King's Landing and falsely identifies the Valyrian steel dagger used in Bran's assassination attempt as having been lost to Tyrion Lannister (wiki:Petyr_Baelish.cite_ref-Ragot18). He promises to help Eddard Stark investigate Jon Arryn's death, guides Ned to key witnesses, but ultimately betrays him in the throne room, holding a dagger to Ned's throat (agot-eddard-14). He brokers the alliance between House Lannister and House Tyrell after the Battle of the Blackwater and is rewarded with the lordship of Harrenhal and the title Lord Paramount of the Trident (wiki:Petyr_Baelish.cite_ref-Racok55).

In ASOS, Petyr informs Tywin Lannister of the Tyrell plot to marry Sansa to Willas, enabling the Lannisters to wed Sansa to Tyrion instead. He orchestrates the poisoning of Joffrey at the Purple Wedding through Dontos Hollard and the poisoned hairnet, then spirits Sansa away from King's Landing. He marries Lysa Arryn and murders her by pushing her through the Moon Door after she threatens Sansa (wiki:Petyr_Baelish.cite_ref-Rasos68).

In AFFC, as Lord Protector of the Vale, Petyr manipulates the Lords Declarant through bribery, blackmail, and the staged disruption of Lyn Corbray (a secret agent in his pay). He conceals Sansa as his bastard daughter "Alayne Stone" and arranges her betrothal to Harrold Hardyng, heir to the Vale (affc-alayne-01, affc-alayne-02).
