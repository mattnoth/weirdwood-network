## Narrative Arc

In 212 AC, Ser Clarence participated in the wedding tourney at Whitewalls held by Lord Ambrose Butterwell to celebrate his second marriage to the daughter of Lord Frey. Clarence was soundly defeated in the lists by Butterwell's commander and son-in-law, Ser Tommard Heddle. (wiki:Clarence_Charlton.cite_ref-Rtmk)
