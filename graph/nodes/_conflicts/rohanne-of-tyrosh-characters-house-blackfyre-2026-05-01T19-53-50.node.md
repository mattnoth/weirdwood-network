## Identity

Rohanne of Tyrosh was a noblewoman from Tyrosh and the wife of Daemon I Blackfyre (wiki:Rohanne_of_Tyrosh). She was the mother of at least seven sons and two daughters (wiki:Rohanne_of_Tyrosh).

## Origins

Born approximately 141–172 AC (track_b: Born). Of Tyroshi culture (track_b: Culture). Her betrothal to Daemon was arranged by King Aegon IV Targaryen and the Archon of Tyrosh (wiki:Rohanne_of_Tyrosh).

`SWORN_TO` House Blackfyre (track_b: Allegiance). `SPOUSE_OF` Daemon I Blackfyre (track_b: Spouse), married in 184 AC (wiki:Rohanne_of_Tyrosh).

## Narrative Arc

Rohanne was a Tyroshi noblewoman whose marriage to Daemon was arranged by King Aegon IV and the Archon of Tyrosh to secure a marriage alliance. She married Daemon in 184 AC and bore him seven sons — including twins Aegon and Aemon (born 184 AC), Daemon II, Haegon, and Aenys — and at least two daughters, including Calla (wiki:Rohanne_of_Tyrosh). After Daemon's defeat and death at the Battle of the Redgrass Field in 196 AC, Rohanne fled to Tyrosh with Daemon's surviving children and his half-brother Aegor Rivers (Bittersteel) (wiki:Rohanne_of_Tyrosh).
