## Appearances & Description

Tall and lean, standing a head taller than Robb Stark (wiki:Osha). She has a hard face, shaggy brown hair, and is flat-chested (wiki:Osha). Her bony arms are wiry but strong, and her body bears many scars (wiki:Osha). She is stronger than Alebelly (wiki:Osha). She wears mail and leather and a bowl-shaped halfhelm; her spear is eight feet of black oak with a tip of rusted steel (wiki:Osha). Osha worships the old gods and is knowledgeable about the lands beyond the Wall (wiki:Osha).

## Narrative Arc

Osha travels south of the Wall with three wildlings and two Night's Watch deserters, Stiv and Wallen, fleeing the Others (wiki:Osha). In the wolfswood near Winterfell they come upon crippled Bran Stark. Robb Stark, Theon Greyjoy, Grey Wind, and Summer arrive to rescue Bran; Robb rides Osha down and she calls for mercy after Stiv is killed by Theon (wiki:Osha). Osha is taken to Winterfell and eventually given limited freedom as a kitchen servant, with iron mankles on her ankles. She takes the cook Gage as a lover, finding him gentler than Stiv (wiki:Osha). Osha warns Maester Luwin about the dangers beyond the Wall and voices concern about the comet, believing it heralds dragons (wiki:Osha). When Theon captures Winterfell, Osha feigns submission and offers Theon her service, which he accepts reluctantly (wiki:Osha). She then helps Bran, Rickon, Hodor, and the Reeds escape through the crypts of Winterfell (wiki:Osha). After the sack, Osha departs with Rickon and Shaggydog for parts unknown while the others head north (wiki:Osha). Wyman Manderly later sends Davos Seaworth to retrieve Rickon from Skagos, where Osha has reportedly taken him (wiki:Osha).
