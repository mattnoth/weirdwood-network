## Identity

The Battle of Three Armies was a battle between House Gardener of the Reach and an alliance of House Durrandon of the Stormlands and House Lannister of the Rock (wiki:Battle_of_Three_Armies).

## Narrative Arc

In his last and greatest war, King Garth VII Gardener faced an alliance between the Storm King and the King of the Rock, who intended to divide the Reach between them. Garth defeated them both, then with cunning words sowed discord between the two kings so they turned on each other. In the aftermath, Garth married his daughters to their heirs and signed pacts fixing the borders between the three kingdoms (wiki:Battle_of_Three_Armies).
