## Identity

The Battle of the Seven Stars was a massive battle fought at the foot of the Giant's Lance during the Andal invasion of the Vale. It was the conclusive battle between the First Men and the Andals in the Vale (wiki:Battle_of_the_Seven_Stars). Also known as "Battle of Seven Stars" (wiki:Battle_of_the_Seven_Stars, cite: *The Rise of the Dragon*).

## Allegiances

Part of the `FIGHTS_IN` Andal invasion. The First Men, led by King Robar II Royce (Houses Royce, Redfort, Upcliff, Hunter, Belmore, Coldwater, Shett), fought against the Andals led by Ser Artys Arryn (Houses Arryn, Corbray, Grafton, Ruthermont, Templeton, Tollett) (track_b: Conflict).

## Narrative Arc

The remaining First Men houses unified behind Robar II Royce, crowning him High King of the Vale, the Fingers, and the Mountains of the Moon. The Andals united behind Ser Artys Arryn. The First Men fortified their position with the Giant's Lance at their back. The First Men resisted six Andal attacks, but the seventh, led by Torgold Tollett, broke through. King Robar killed Torgold, then dueled and slew what he believed was Artys Arryn — actually a decoy. Five hundred Andal knights then attacked from the rear via a goat track, led by the real Artys. The First Men were routed, and all thirty of Robar's lords were killed. Fourteen First Men houses went extinct. The surviving houses submitted. Artys was crowned King of Mountain and Vale. Those who did not submit fled into the Mountains of the Moon, becoming the mountain clans (wiki:Battle_of_the_Seven_Stars).
