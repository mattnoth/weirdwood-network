## Identity

Kyra is a tavern wench who works at the Smoking Log in the winter town outside of Winterfell (wiki:Kyra).

## Origins

Born 280–281 AC in the winter town (track_b: Born). Died 300 AC in forests near the Dreadfort (track_b: Died).

## Allegiances

## Narrative Arc

While riding through the winter town, Theon Greyjoy calls out to Kyra, making her blush; he claims intimate knowledge of her (wiki:Kyra.cite_ref-Ragot37). When Theon captures Winterfell, he has Kyra brought to him as his mistress (wiki:Kyra.cite_ref-Racok50). After Theon has a prophetic nightmare, he rapes her, leaving her bruised and sobbing (wiki:Kyra.cite_ref-Racok56). When Ramsay Snow demands Kyra as payment for his service, Theon refuses, and Ramsay strikes him, shattering his cheekbone, before proceeding to sack Winterfell (wiki:Kyra.cite_ref-Racok66). After the fall, Kyra is held at the Dreadfort by House Bolton. She escapes through a postern gate with stolen keys, freeing Theon as well, but the escape proves to be a hunt devised by Ramsay Bolton. Both are caught within the hour; Kyra throws a stone at Ramsay but misses. She suffers a painful death (wiki:Kyra.cite_ref-Radwd37). Ramsay later names a hunting hound after her (wiki:Kyra.cite_ref-Radwd41).
