## Identity

Lord Alton Butterwell was the Lord of Lord Harroway's Town and head of House Butterwell during the reign of King Maegor I Targaryen. He served as Maegor's master of coin. (wiki:Alton_Butterwell)

## Origins

## Narrative Arc

After King Maegor exterminated House Harroway in 44 AC, he divided their holdings among supporters. Lord Harroway's Town was granted to Lord Alton, while Harrenhal went to House Towers. (wiki:Alton_Butterwell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon-1)

As master of coin, Alton continuously raised taxes and imposed new ones to fund Maegor's ruinously expensive wars, but these measures failed to bring in sufficient revenue. Maegor then demanded tribute of gold from the high lords, making him even more loathed. (wiki:Alton_Butterwell.cite_ref-Rfaba_surfeit_of_rulers-2)

When Maegor needed new brides after executing Queens Alys Harroway and Tyanna of the Tower, Lord Alton suggested his stout, widowed sister as a match, citing her fertility in bearing seven children. Though Maegor scorned the offer, Alton's remark on fertility influenced the king's choice of the three Black Brides in 47 AC. (wiki:Alton_Butterwell.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon-1)
