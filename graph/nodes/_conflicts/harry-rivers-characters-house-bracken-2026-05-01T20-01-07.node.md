## Identity

Harry Rivers, known as the Bastard of Bracken, is the bastard son of Lord Jonos Bracken of Stone Hedge. (wiki:Harry_Rivers.cite_ref-1) His paternity is disputed by Lord Tytos Blackwood.

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Bracken (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Rivermen (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Harry is fair-haired and comely. (wiki:Harry_Rivers.cite_ref-2) Tytos Blackwood notes there is little physical resemblance between Harry and Jonos, implying Jonos may not be his true father.

## Narrative Arc

Harry is killed by Lannisters in the riverlands during the War of the Five Kings, presumably during the burning of Stone Hedge or its liberation. (wiki:Harry_Rivers.cite_ref-1) During Jaime's visit to end the siege of Raventree, Jonos cites Harry's death as justification for territorial demands. Tytos Blackwood alleges Jonos was not Harry's father, citing the lack of physical resemblance. (wiki:Harry_Rivers.cite_ref-2)
