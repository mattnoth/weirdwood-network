## Identity

The battle under the walls of Riverrun was the second major battle of the War of the Five Kings, fought in 298 AC. Ser Jaime Lannister defeated the massed Tully forces outside the walls of Riverrun. (wiki:Battle_under_the_walls_of_Riverrun, agot-56)

## Narrative Arc

After defeating the Pipers and Vances at the Golden Tooth, Jaime Lannister marched on Riverrun where Ser Edmure Tully had assembled his father Hoster Tully's bannermen. Edmure's force was diminished — men had been sent to guard the borders and the Golden Tooth, and the Frey forces were absent. Jaime met the Tullys under the walls of Riverrun and routed them. Edmure and many knights were captured. Lord Tytos Blackwood led survivors back within Riverrun, where Jaime placed them under siege with approximately 12,000 foot and 2,000–3,000 horse. (wiki:Battle_under_the_walls_of_Riverrun, agot-56, agot-59, agot-63)

## Quotes
