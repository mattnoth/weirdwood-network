## Identity

Medger Cerwyn is the Lord of Cerwyn and head of House Cerwyn. He has a daughter, Jonelle, and a son, Cley. (wiki:Medger_Cerwyn)

## Origins

Born in or before 255 AC at Castle Cerwyn. (wiki:Medger_Cerwyn.cite_ref-2, wiki:Medger_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_medger_cerwyn)

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Cerwyn. (track_b: Allegiance) `CULTURE_OF` Northmen. (track_b: Culture) Supporter of Robb Stark in the War of the Five Kings. (wiki:Medger_Cerwyn)

## Appearances & Description

Medger is soft-spoken. (wiki:Medger_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Ragot53) His body was draped in a cloak of silk decorated with the battle-axe of House Cerwyn when the silent sisters bore his remains from Harrenhal. (wiki:Medger_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok30)

## Narrative Arc

Medger and his son Cley often visited Winterfell. (wiki:Medger_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok30) When Robb Stark called his banners, Lord Cerwyn came to Winterfell accompanied by his daughter Jonelle. (wiki:Medger_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Ragot53) One of Lord Roose Bolton's men knifed one of Medger's at the Smoking Log, and Lord Jon Umber objected to being placed behind the Cerwyns in the order of march. (wiki:Medger_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Ragot53)

Medger was placed under Lord Bolton's command when the northern army split at the Twins and was captured at the Battle on the Green Fork. (wiki:Medger_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Ragot62) Tyrion Lannister unsuccessfully offered to trade Lord Medger and Ser Donnel Locke for Tion Frey in a prisoner exchange. (wiki:Medger_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok25) Lord Tywin Lannister held Medger at the Tower of Dread in Harrenhal. Arya Stark, disguised as a servant, hoped to reach him for help but Medger never left his cell. He died of his battle wounds; Arya saw silent sisters take his body from Harrenhal. (wiki:Medger_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Racok30)
