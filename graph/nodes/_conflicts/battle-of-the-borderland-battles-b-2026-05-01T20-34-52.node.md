## Identity

The Battle of the Borderland was a major battle involving four of the nine Free Cities of Essos, fought in 96 AC (wiki:Battle_of_the_Borderland).

## Narrative Arc

Lys, Myr, and Tyrosh put their differences aside and formed an alliance to drive Volantis from the Disputed Lands. The alliance defeated the Volantenes in the Battle of the Borderland. After the battle, the three victorious cities forged an "eternal alliance" and formed the Triarchy, a powerful new political entity in Essos (wiki:Battle_of_the_Borderland).
