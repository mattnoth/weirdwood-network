## Identity

Clement Celtigar was the Lord of Claw Isle and head of House Celtigar during the late part of the Dance of the Dragons. He was the son and heir of Lord Bartimos Celtigar. (wiki:Clement_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf)

## Origins

Of Valyrian descent, from the Crownlands. Son of Bartimos Celtigar. (track_b: Father, Race, Culture)

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Celtigar and the Blacks faction. (track_b: Allegiances)

## Narrative Arc

Clement became Lord of Claw Isle after the death of his father during the riot in King's Landing in 130 AC. (wiki:Clement_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown) After the ascension of King Aegon III Targaryen, Lord Clement rode to King's Landing from Duskendale with Ser Corwyn Corbray and Lady Staunton. (wiki:Clement_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabaftermath_-_the_hour_of_the_wolf)
