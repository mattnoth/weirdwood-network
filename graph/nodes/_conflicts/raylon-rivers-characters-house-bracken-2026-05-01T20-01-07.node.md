## Identity

Ser Raylon Rivers was a knight from House Bracken during the Dance of the Dragons. He was a bastard, the half-brother of Ser Amos Bracken and the son of Lord Humfrey Bracken of Stone Hedge. (wiki:Raylon_Rivers.cite_ref-1)

## Narrative Arc

House Bracken declared for the greens during the Dance of the Dragons. Ser Amos Bracken was slain during the Battle of the Burning Mill, after which Raylon led the survivors back toward Stone Hedge. However, Prince Daemon Targaryen had already taken Stone Hedge and Lord Humfrey Bracken, so Raylon surrendered to spare his kin from harm. (wiki:Raylon_Rivers.cite_ref-1)
