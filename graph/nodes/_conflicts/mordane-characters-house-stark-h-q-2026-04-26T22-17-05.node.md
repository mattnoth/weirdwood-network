## Identity

Mordane is the septa at Winterfell, serving as tutor to the Stark girls, Sansa and Arya (wiki:Mordane).

## Origins

Born in Westeros (track_b: Born). Died 298 AC at the Red Keep, King's Landing (track_b: Died).

## Appearances & Description

Mordane has a bony face and sharp eyes with a thin, lipless mouth. She starches her skirts (wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot7). While strict, she is a good woman who cares about both Sansa and Arya (wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot15). She finds Sansa a delightful student but is often exasperated with Arya's rebellious nature (wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot7).

## Narrative Arc

Mordane presides over needlework with Princess Myrcella, Beth Cassel, Jeyne Poole, Sansa, and Arya; when she admonishes Arya for poor needlework, Arya embarrasses herself before the princess (wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot7). Mordane accompanies Lord Eddard south to King's Landing to watch over his daughters (wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot10). She accompanies Sansa to the Hand's tourney and is present when Sansa visits the court (wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot29). When Arya is playing at swordplay with Syrio Forel, Mordane and Sansa disapprove (wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot32). During the purge of Stark men, Mordane tells Sansa to lock herself in her room, then goes to confront the Lannister guards. She is killed and her head placed on a spike on the castle walls alongside other Stark household members; Sansa is forced to look upon it by Joffrey (wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Ragot67). Arya later recalls Mordane among those she failed to save (wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Racok2). Sansa remembers how Mordane had always said a lady's armor was her courtesy (wiki:Mordane.cite_ref-Racok18).
