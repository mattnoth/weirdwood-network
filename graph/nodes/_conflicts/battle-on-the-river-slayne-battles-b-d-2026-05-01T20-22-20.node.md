## Identity

The battle on the river Slayne was an engagement during the Coming of the Andals, fought on the banks of the river Slayne in the stormlands. (wiki:Battle_on_the_river_Slayne)

## Narrative Arc

A combined host of Durrandon storm kings and Dornish kings fought against the Andal warlord Drox the Corpse-Maker. The First Men coalition achieved a victory, halting this particular Andal advance. The battle is one of several engagements during the prolonged Andal migration into Westeros. (wiki:Battle_on_the_river_Slayne)
