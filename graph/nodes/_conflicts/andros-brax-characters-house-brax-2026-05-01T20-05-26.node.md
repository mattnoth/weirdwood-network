## Identity

Lord Andros Brax is the Lord of Hornvale and the head of House Brax. He has three knighted sons: Tytos, Robert, and Flement. (wiki:Andros_Brax.cite_ref-Rasosappendix-1)

## Origins

Born in or before 264 AC. A Westermen nobleman of House Brax. (wiki:Andros_Brax)

## Narrative Arc

Lord Andros once visited Riverrun hoping to arrange a marriage between one of his sons and Catelyn or Lysa Tully. (wiki:Andros_Brax.cite_ref-Racok45-3)

During Ser Jaime Lannister's siege of Riverrun, Andros commanded the western camp between the Tumblestone and the Red Fork. When Ser Brynden Tully launched a surprise attack in the Battle of the Camps, Lord Brax attempted to cross the Tumblestone on rafts to reinforce the northern camp. Catapults atop Riverrun overturned his raft, and Andros drowned under the weight of his plate armor. (wiki:Andros_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot69-4)

He was succeeded as Lord of Hornvale by his eldest son, Tytos Brax. (wiki:Andros_Brax.cite_ref-Rasosappendix-1)
