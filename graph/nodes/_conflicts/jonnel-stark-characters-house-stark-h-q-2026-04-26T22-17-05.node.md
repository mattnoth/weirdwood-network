## Identity

Jonnel Stark, known as One-Eye, was a Lord of Winterfell and head of House Stark in the latter half of the second century after Aegon's Conquest (wiki:Jonnel_Stark). He was the second son of Lord Cregan Stark and the first by Cregan's third wife, Lynara Stark (wiki:Jonnel_Stark).

## Narrative Arc

Jonnel became heir to Winterfell after the death of his older half-brother Rickon in the conquest of Dorne, passing over Rickon's daughters Sansa and Serena Stark. He married his half-niece Sansa. Jonnel became Lord of Winterfell after the death of his father Cregan (wiki:Jonnel_Stark). The North lamented Rickon's death, as the reigns of Jonnel and his brothers were troublesome (wiki:Jonnel_Stark). His marriage with Sansa was childless; after her death he married Robyn Ryswell, but also had no issue. He was succeeded by his brother Barthogan Stark (wiki:Jonnel_Stark).
