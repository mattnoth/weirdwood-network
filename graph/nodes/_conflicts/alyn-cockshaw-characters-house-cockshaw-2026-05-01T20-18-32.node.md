## Identity

Lord Alyn Cockshaw was a knight and lord of House Cockshaw during the reign of King Aerys I Targaryen. (wiki:Alyn_Cockshaw.cite_ref-Rtmk-1)

## Origins

Born around 189 AC in Westeros. (track_b: Born) He was a childhood friend of Daemon II Blackfyre; the two were bullied by Daemon's older twin brothers, Aegon and Aemon Blackfyre. (wiki:Alyn_Cockshaw.cite_ref-Rtmk-1)

## Appearances & Description

Alyn had a pudgy face with curly blond hair the color of dark honey. (wiki:Alyn_Cockshaw.cite_ref-Rtmk-1) He was prone to forgetting his courtesies and acting churlish, particularly when drinking. (wiki:Alyn_Cockshaw.cite_ref-Rtmk-1)

## Narrative Arc

Alyn wept when Bittersteel carried Daemon into exile after the First Blackfyre Rebellion. He was ecstatic at Daemon's return to Westeros and joined the conspiracy to start the Second Blackfyre Rebellion at the wedding tourney at Whitewalls in 212 AC, hoping to become Lord Commander of Daemon's Kingsguard. (wiki:Alyn_Cockshaw.cite_ref-Rtmk-1)

Traveling with Lord Gormon Peake's entourage accompanying Ser John the Fiddler (Daemon in disguise), Alyn encountered Ser Duncan the Tall and grew jealous of Daemon's attention toward him. During the tourney, Alyn was unhorsed by Ser Glendon Flowers. He arranged for Ser Uthor Underleaf to kill Duncan in the joust, but Duncan survived. (wiki:Alyn_Cockshaw.cite_ref-Rtmk-1)

Alyn lured Duncan to a well intending to kill him, but Duncan foiled the plan and Alyn fell in instead, badly injuring Duncan's arm with his dagger before falling. He was left to drown by Duncan and Ser Maynard Plumm. (wiki:Alyn_Cockshaw.cite_ref-Rtmk-1)
