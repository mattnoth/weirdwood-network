## Identity

Ser Robert Ashford was a knight from House Ashford, the younger son of Lord Ashford during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen. (wiki:Robert_Ashford.cite_ref-Rthk-5) (wiki:Robert_Ashford.cite_ref-Rawoiaf-1)

## Origins

Born at Ashford. (wiki:Robert_Ashford.cite_ref-Rawoiaf-1) His father was Lord Ashford. (track_b: Father)

## Allegiances

Robert held the title of Ser and was `SWORN_TO` House Ashford. He was of the Reach in culture. (track_b: Allegiance)

## Appearances & Description

Robert bore personal arms: a white sun-and-chevron on orange within a white border (tenné, beneath a chevron inverted a sun-in-splendour argent, all within a bordure of the last). (wiki:Robert_Ashford.cite_ref-Rthk-graphic-novel-4)

## Narrative Arc

Robert participated in the tourney at Ashford Meadow in 209 AC. Along with his older brother Ser Androw, he was one of his sister's champions. In the first tilt he was challenged by Ser Lyonel Baratheon, who beat him after ten lances and a fight afoot. (wiki:Robert_Ashford.cite_ref-Rthk-5)
