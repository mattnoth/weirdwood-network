## Identity

Lucas Codd, known as Left-Hand Lucas Codd, is an ironborn raider, captain, and member of House Codd. He was born in the Iron Islands. (wiki:Lucas_Codd, wiki:Lucas_Codd.cite_ref-Rawoiaflucas_codd)

## Origins

Born in the Iron Islands. (track_b: Born)

## Allegiances

Lucas is `SWORN_TO` House Codd. (track_b: Allegiance)

He is a follower and supporter of Euron Greyjoy. (wiki:Lucas_Codd.cite_ref-Raffc11)

## Narrative Arc

Lucas joined Euron Greyjoy when Euron declared himself King of the Isles and the North. (wiki:Lucas_Codd.cite_ref-Raffc11) At the kingsmoot on Old Wyk, he traded insults with Asha Greyjoy, mocking her gender by asking what she would give them — "knitting?" (wiki:Lucas_Codd.cite_ref-Raffc18) After the dragon horn was blown, Lucas took it from Cragorn. (wiki:Lucas_Codd.cite_ref-Raffc19) He was the first to shout "EURON!" after the horn sounded. (affc-the-drowned-man-01)

While feasting at Lord Hewett's castle, Lucas tore tapestries from the walls and wore one as a cloak. After the feast, he raped one of Lord Humfrey Hewett's daughters. (wiki:Lucas_Codd.cite_ref-Raffc29)

In ADWD, Asha reflects that Erik Ironmaker, her unwanted proxy husband, is at least better than Lucas or the Red Oarsman. (wiki:Lucas_Codd.cite_ref-Radwd26)
