## Identity

Aegon Blackfyre was the eldest son and firstborn child of Daemon I Blackfyre and Rohanne of Tyrosh (track_b: Father, Mother). He was a twin to his brother Aemon Blackfyre (wiki:Aegon_Blackfyre). He was a squire at age twelve (wiki:Aegon_Blackfyre).

## Origins

Born in 184 AC in Westeros (track_b: Born). His father was Daemon I Blackfyre and his mother was Rohanne of Tyrosh (track_b: Father, Mother). He was of Crownlands culture (track_b: Culture).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Blackfyre (track_b: Allegiance).

## Appearances & Description

Aegon was described as a "witless bully" by Alyn Cockshaw, along with his twin Aemon, for their treatment of their younger brother Daemon II (wiki:Aegon_Blackfyre).

## Narrative Arc

Aegon grew up as the eldest son of the Blackfyre pretender. He and his twin Aemon bullied their younger brother Daemon. During the First Blackfyre Rebellion in 196 AC, Aegon fought for his father's cause at the Battle of the Redgrass Field. After his father Daemon I was slain by arrows from Brynden Rivers's Raven's Teeth, Aegon continued fighting but was himself killed by Brynden Rivers's archers at the Redgrass Field (wiki:Aegon_Blackfyre). Their younger brother Daemon II had prophetic dreams foretelling that Aegon and Aemon would die (wiki:Aegon_Blackfyre).
