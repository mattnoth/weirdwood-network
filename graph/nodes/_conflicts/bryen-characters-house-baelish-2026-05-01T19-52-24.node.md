## Identity

Bryen is the captain of the guards for House Baelish at their tower in the Fingers (wiki:Bryen.cite_ref-Rasos68).

## Appearances & Description

Bryen is past eighty years old and white-haired (wiki:Bryen.cite_ref-Rasos68). He has multiple dogs, including an old blind dog (wiki:Bryen.cite_ref-Rasos68). Born no later than 220 AC per wiki calculation.

## Narrative Arc

Bryen was named captain of the guards the last time Lord Petyr Baelish visited his home on the Fingers. He meets Petyr when he comes ashore with Sansa Stark. He and Umfred lift Sansa from the boat so she does not get her skirts wet (wiki:Bryen.cite_ref-Rasos68).
