## Identity

Lord Chambers was the lord and head of House Chambers during the Dance of the Dragons. His personal name is not recorded. (wiki:Lord_Chambers.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant-1)

## Allegiances

Lord Chambers was `SWORN_TO` House Chambers and supported the `SWORN_TO` Blacks faction during the Dance of the Dragons, siding with Rhaenyra Targaryen. (wiki:Lord_Chambers.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant-1) His culture was that of the `CULTURE_OF` Rivermen. (track_b: Culture)

## Narrative Arc

During the Dance of the Dragons, Lord Chambers sided with the blacks and Rhaenyra Targaryen. He joined Lord Bigglestone, Lord Perryn, and Ser Pate of Longleaf's ragged band of survivors from earlier battles and fought together in the Battle by the Lakeshore. (wiki:Lord_Chambers.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant-1)
