## Identity

Ser Mortimer Boggs was a knight from House Boggs of Crackclaw Point, active during the reign of King Aerys I Targaryen. (wiki:Mortimer_Boggs.cite_ref-Rtmk-2)

## Origins

## Narrative Arc

Ser Mortimer was a knight of some repute as a jouster. (wiki:Mortimer_Boggs.cite_ref-Rtmk-2) He attended the wedding tourney at Whitewalls in 212 AC, where he was defeated in the lists by Ser Glendon Flowers. (wiki:Mortimer_Boggs.cite_ref-Rtmk-2) Per the customs of the tourney, he owed Glendon his horse and armor, but because Glendon was a bastard, Ser Mortimer refused to send the horse — saying he would rather eat it — and hammered holes through his armor before sending it over. (wiki:Mortimer_Boggs.cite_ref-Rtmk-2)
