## Identity

Lord Borrell was the Lord of Sweetsister, Shield of Sisterton, Master of Breakwater Castle, and Keeper of the Night Lamp. He was the head of House Borrell during Robert's Rebellion. (wiki:Lord_Borrell_(father_of_Godric).cite_ref-Radwd9-1) His personal name is not recorded; he is identified only as Godric Borrell's father.

## Origins

He died between 282 and 298 AC. (wiki:Lord_Borrell_(father_of_Godric).infobox.Died)

## Narrative Arc

During Robert's Rebellion, Lord Eddard Stark washed up on Sweetsister while trying to sail north to raise his banners. Lord Borrell could easily have turned Eddard over to King Aerys II Targaryen for a reward, but he let him go. His parting words to Ned were: "If you lose, you were never here." (wiki:Lord_Borrell_(father_of_Godric).cite_ref-Radwd9-1)

He was succeeded as Lord of Sweetsister by his son Godric Borrell. (wiki:Lord_Borrell_(father_of_Godric).succession_box)
