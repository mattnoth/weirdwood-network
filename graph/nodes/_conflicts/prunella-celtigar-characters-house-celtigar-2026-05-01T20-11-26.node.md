## Identity

Prunella Celtigar was a noblewoman from House Celtigar during the reigns of kings Maegor I and Jaehaerys I Targaryen. She later became the Lady of Starpike, Dunstonbury, and Whitegrove by marriage to Lord Uther Peake. (wiki:Prunella_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon; wiki:Prunella_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne)

## Origins

Daughter of Lord Edwell Celtigar of Claw Isle. Of Valyrian descent, from the Crownlands. Born 34 or 35 AC. (wiki:Prunella_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon; track_b: Father, Race, Culture)

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Celtigar (by birth) and House Peake (by marriage). (track_b: Allegiances)

## Appearances & Description

Lord Rogar Baratheon scornfully claimed that Prunella and her sister Prudence had "no chins, no teats, and no sense." (wiki:Prunella_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides)

## Narrative Arc

In 47 AC, Prunella's father urged King Maegor I to marry either Prunella or her sister Prudence; the king instead married the Black Brides. (wiki:Prunella_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon) In 49 AC, her father made the same offer to Lord Rogar Baratheon for King Jaehaerys I; Baratheon rebuffed it. (wiki:Prunella_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides)

Prunella became a lady companion to Queen Alysanne Targaryen during her time on Dragonstone. (wiki:Prunella_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_year_of_the_three_brides) She participated in the bedding ceremony at Jaehaerys and Alysanne's second wedding. (wiki:Prunella_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfaba_time_of_testing)

In 55 AC, Queen Alysanne arranged a marriage between Prunella and Lord Uther Peake. It was considered an exceptional match and a triumph for the queen. (wiki:Prunella_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabjaehaerys_and_alysanne)
