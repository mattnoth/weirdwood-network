## Identity

Varly is a household guard in service to Lord Eddard Stark and House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). He is of Northern culture (track_b: Culture). He died in 298 AC in King's Landing (track_b: Died).

## Narrative Arc

Varly served as one of Eddard Stark's household guards in King's Landing. He rode with Ned through the city streets (agot-eddard-06) and helped support the injured Ned to the godswood after the street fight with Jaime Lannister, Tomard holding one arm and Varly the other (agot-eddard-12). During the betrayal in the throne room when the gold cloaks turned on the Stark men, Janos Slynt slashed Varly's throat open (agot-eddard-14).
