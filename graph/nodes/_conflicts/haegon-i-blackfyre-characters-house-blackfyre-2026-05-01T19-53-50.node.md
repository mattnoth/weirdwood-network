## Identity

Haegon I Blackfyre was the fourth son of Daemon I Blackfyre and Rohanne of Tyrosh (track_b: Father, Mother). He was the Blackfyre Pretender who succeeded his brother Daemon II and was succeeded by his son Daemon III (wiki:Haegon_I_Blackfyre).

## Origins

Born in the 190s AC, of Crownlands culture (track_b: Culture). His father was Daemon I Blackfyre and his mother was Rohanne of Tyrosh (track_b: Father, Mother).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Blackfyre (track_b: Allegiance).

## Narrative Arc

After the death and capture of his older brother Daemon II Blackfyre during the failed Second Blackfyre Rebellion, Haegon became the Blackfyre claimant. He was the heir to Daemon II's claim (wiki:Haegon_I_Blackfyre). In 219 AC, Haegon and Aegor Rivers (Bittersteel) launched the Third Blackfyre Rebellion. Haegon was slain treacherously after surrendering — killed despite having yielded (wiki:Haegon_I_Blackfyre). He left behind a son, Daemon III Blackfyre, who would continue the Blackfyre cause (track_b: Issue).
