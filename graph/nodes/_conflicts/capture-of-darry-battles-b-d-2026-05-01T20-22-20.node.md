## Identity

The capture of Darry was the Lannister seizure of Castle Darry during the War of the Five Kings, part of the initial Lannister invasion of the riverlands. (wiki:Capture_of_Darry)

## Narrative Arc

Lannister forces captured Castle Darry, the seat of House Darry, as part of the coordinated campaign that also saw the burning of Stone Hedge, Pinkmaiden Castle, and other riverlords' holdings. The castle changed hands multiple times during the war — it was later recaptured, sacked, and besieged in subsequent engagements. (wiki:Capture_of_Darry, agot-56, acok-7)
