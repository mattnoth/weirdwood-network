## Identity

The Conquest of Dorne was the military campaign by King Daeron I Targaryen (the Young Dragon) to bring Dorne into the Seven Kingdoms, beginning in 157 AC. It resulted in a short-lasting occupation followed by a Dornish rebellion that killed Daeron and tens of thousands of his men. (wiki:Conquest_of_Dorne)

## Narrative Arc

King Daeron I launched an invasion of Dorne with a massive host, achieving initial military success including the Submission of Sunspear. The Young Dragon wrote a book also called "Conquest of Dorne" boasting of his victory. However, the Dornish refused to submit and launched a guerrilla rebellion. Lord Tyrell, left as governor, was assassinated — killed by a hundred scorpions according to one account. The occupation collapsed, and Daeron himself was killed during the subsequent fighting, reportedly at only eighteen years of age. The campaign is regarded as a cautionary tale about the difficulty of holding Dorne by force — a lesson that informed later Targaryen policy of bringing Dorne into the realm through marriage rather than conquest under Daeron II. (wiki:Conquest_of_Dorne, agot-5, asos-54, asos-59, asos-66, affc-13, affc-21, affc-40, adwd-17)
