## Identity

Grunt is a man-at-arms sworn to House Bolton and one of the Bastard's Boys. (wiki:Grunt.cite_ref-Radwd12)

## Appearances & Description

Grunt lost his tongue for speaking carelessly within earshot of Roose Bolton. (wiki:Grunt.cite_ref-Radwd12) He is described as brutal and has spent most of his life in service at the Dreadfort. (wiki:Grunt.cite_ref-Radwd51)

## Narrative Arc

Grunt is present in the great hall of the Dreadfort when Big Walder and Little Walder Frey bring Theon Greyjoy before Ramsay Bolton. (wiki:Grunt.cite_ref-Radwd12) Later at Winterfell, Sour Alyn and Grunt are on guard at the door of Ramsay's bedchamber when the spearwives go with Theon to rescue "Arya Stark" (Jeyne Poole). (wiki:Grunt.cite_ref-Radwd51)
