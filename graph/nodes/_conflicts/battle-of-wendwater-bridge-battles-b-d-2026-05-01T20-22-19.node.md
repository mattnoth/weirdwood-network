## Identity

The Battle of Wendwater Bridge was a battle during the Fourth Blackfyre Rebellion in 236 AC. (wiki:Battle_of_Wendwater_Bridge)

## Narrative Arc

The Golden Company, led by pretender Daemon III Blackfyre, invaded the crownlands. They were met at the Wendwater by a Targaryen force. The battle resulted in a decisive Targaryen victory. Daemon III Blackfyre was slain, reportedly by Ser Duncan the Tall of the Kingsguard. The Fourth Blackfyre Rebellion collapsed after this engagement. (wiki:Battle_of_Wendwater_Bridge)
