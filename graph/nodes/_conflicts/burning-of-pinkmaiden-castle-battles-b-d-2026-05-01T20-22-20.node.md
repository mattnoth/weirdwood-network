## Identity

The burning of Pinkmaiden Castle was the destruction of the seat of House Piper during the War of the Five Kings, carried out by Lannister forces under Ser Gregor Clegane. (wiki:Burning_of_Pinkmaiden_Castle)

## Narrative Arc

After the Lannister invasion of the riverlands, Ser Gregor Clegane's raiding parties burned Pinkmaiden Castle, the seat of House Piper. Lord Clement Piper's lands were devastated as part of the broader Lannister campaign to subdue the Tully bannermen. The burning was reported alongside similar attacks on Stone Hedge, Darry, and other riverlords' seats. (wiki:Burning_of_Pinkmaiden_Castle, agot-56, acok-7)
