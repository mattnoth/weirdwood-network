## Identity

Crispian Celtigar was a member of House Celtigar during the reign of King Aegon I Targaryen. He served as the first master of coin on Aegon's small council. (wiki:Crispian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons_the_conquest)

## Origins

Of Valyrian descent, from the Crownlands. The Celtigars were closely allied with House Targaryen of Dragonstone and House Velaryon of Driftmark, families of Valyrian descent. (wiki:Crispian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabaegons_conquest; track_b: Race, Culture)

## Narrative Arc

Crispian was a vassal of the Targaryens and joined his strength to Lord Aegon Targaryen when he sought to conquer the Seven Kingdoms of Westeros. (wiki:Crispian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabaegons_conquest) After Aegon was crowned King of All Westeros, he established the first small council and Crispian was named the first master of coin. (wiki:Crispian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_reign_of_the_dragons_the_conquest; wiki:Crispian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabaegons_conquest) In that office, he advised Aegon on matters relating to taxation, debts, and incomes. (wiki:Crispian_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon)
