## Identity

Mikken is the blacksmith at Winterfell (wiki:Mikken).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Mikken crafts for Jon Snow the sword Needle, which Jon gifts to Arya Stark (wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Ragot10). Lord Eddard Stark later discovers Needle in King's Landing and recognizes Mikken's maker's mark (wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Ragot22). Rickon Stark's direwolf Shaggydog bites Mikken, tearing a chunk from his thigh (wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Ragot53). In Winterfell, Mikken installs metal bars along Bran's walls so the crippled boy can pull himself around his room (wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Racok4). Jojen Reed foresees Mikken's death in green dreams — the sea flowing over Winterfell's walls — but Mikken laughs it off (wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Racok35). When Theon Greyjoy captures Winterfell, Mikken angrily refuses to bend the knee and is beaten by Stygg; after insulting the ironborn, Stygg drives a spear through Mikken's neck (wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Racok46). Osha carries a sword from Eddard's tomb after the sack, one of the last to bear Mikken's mark (wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Racok69). Theon later wanders through the ruins of Mikken's forge at the destroyed Winterfell (wiki:Mikken.cite_ref-Radwd41).
