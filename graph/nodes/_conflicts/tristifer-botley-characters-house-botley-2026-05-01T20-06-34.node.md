## Identity

Tristifer Botley, known as Tris, is the second son of Lord Sawane Botley of Lordsport. He is the titular Lord of Lordsport, though his uncle Germund disputes the title. (wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc11, wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Raffcappendix)

## Origins

Born 274–277 AC at Lordsport. (wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Rawoiaftristifer_botley) Tristifer was fostered at Pyke as one of five boys brought to Lady Alannys Harlaw after Greyjoy's Rebellion. He became friends with Asha Greyjoy, but was sent away to Blacktyde after he and Asha were found touching by Maester Qalen. He wrote letters to Asha from Blacktyde, but Maester Joseran refused to send them. (wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc11)

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Botley (track_b: Allegiance). Tristifer is a devoted supporter of Asha Greyjoy, serving as one of her champions at the kingsmoot and following her to Deepwood Motte. (wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc19)

## Appearances & Description

Tris has messy brown hair and large eyes, described as "trusting as a seal's." He was pimpled as a boy but has since filled out. He wears a sealskin cloak. Asha considers him "too sweet for the Iron Islands." (wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc11)

## Narrative Arc

After his father Sawane is drowned by Euron Greyjoy and his elder brother Harren dies at Moat Cailin, Tris becomes the titular Lord of Lordsport. However, Euron gives half the Botley lands to House Wynch and installs Tris's uncle Germund as lord. (wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc11)

Tris meets Asha at the Ten Towers and declares his love, but she spurns him. He serves as one of her champions at the kingsmoot on Old Wyk. (wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc19) After Euron wins, Tris sails with Asha to Deepwood Motte, growing a thick brown beard and wearing furs. He tells Asha the story of Torgon the Latecomer, earning a kiss. He proposes the ironborn abandon Westeros and become traders. (wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Radwd26)

Despite not being a warrior, Tris fights bravely in the fight by Deepwood Motte against Stannis Baratheon's men. He is captured and imprisoned at Deepwood while Asha is taken on Stannis's march to Winterfell. (wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Radwd26, wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Radwd42) Eventually Tris and the other ironborn survivors are ransomed by Tycho Nestoris of the Iron Bank of Braavos and escort the envoy to Stannis's camp at a crofters' village. (wiki:Tristifer_Botley.cite_ref-Radwd62)
