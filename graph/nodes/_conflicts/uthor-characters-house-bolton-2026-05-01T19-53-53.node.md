## Identity

Uthor was a maester of the Citadel who served Lord Roose Bolton at the Dreadfort. There are other characters named Uthor; this is the maester. (wiki:Uthor.cite_ref-Radwd32)

## Narrative Arc

The old maester insisted that Reek's appalling stench was a sign of sickness. (wiki:Uthor.cite_ref-Radwd32) When Domeric Bolton died, Uthor attributed the death to a sickness of the bowels. Lord Roose, however, suspected his son had been poisoned by Ramsay Snow. (wiki:Uthor.cite_ref-Radwd32) Uthor's fate is unknown; Maester Tybald now serves as the Dreadfort's maester. He presumably died between 297 and 300 AC. (wiki:Uthor.cite_ref-Rtwow)
