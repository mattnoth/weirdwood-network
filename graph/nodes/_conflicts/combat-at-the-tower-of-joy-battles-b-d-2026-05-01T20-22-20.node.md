## Identity

The combat at the tower of joy was a skirmish at the end of Robert's Rebellion in 283 AC, fought at the tower of joy in the Red Mountains of Dorne between Lord Eddard Stark and six companions against three Kingsguard knights guarding Lyanna Stark. (wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy, agot-39)

## Narrative Arc

After the Sack of King's Landing and lifting the siege of Storm's End, Eddard Stark rode south to the Red Mountains of Dorne seeking his sister Lyanna. Prince Rhaegar Targaryen had kept Lyanna at the tower of joy, guarded by Lord Commander Gerold Hightower, Ser Arthur Dayne, and Ser Oswell Whent of the Kingsguard. Eddard arrived with six companions: Howland Reed, Lord Willam Dustin, Ethan Glover, Martyn Cassel, Theo Wull, and Ser Mark Ryswell. They were seven against three. In the ensuing combat, only Eddard and Howland survived. All three Kingsguard were killed. Eddard found Lyanna dying in a room smelling of blood and roses; she claimed a promise from him before she died. Ned returned Arthur Dayne's sword Dawn to Starfall and pulled stones from the tower to build eight cairns. (wiki:Combat_at_the_tower_of_joy, agot-4, agot-6, agot-12, agot-35, agot-39, agot-45, agot-66, acok-21, adwd-41)
