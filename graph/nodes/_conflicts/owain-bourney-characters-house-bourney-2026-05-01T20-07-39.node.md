## Identity

Lord Owain Bourney, known as Lord Owain the Betrayer, was the head of House Bourney during the Dance of the Dragons. (wiki:Owain_Bourney.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant-1) He was described as a "Blackwater man," placing his lands along the Blackwater Rush. (wiki:Owain_Bourney.cite_ref-2)

## Origins

Owain hailed from the Blackwater Rush region. The Blackwater Rush has headwaters in the Westerlands and Riverlands and runs through the Crownlands and the northern Reach, so his precise region of origin is uncertain. (Tier 2 — wiki notes ambiguity across four possible regions) (wiki:Owain_Bourney.cite_ref-2)

## Allegiances

Owain was `SWORN_TO` House Bourney as its lord and head. (track_b: Allegiances) He initially marched with the `SWORN_TO` Blacks, joining the host led by Ser Garibald Grey and Lord Roderick Dustin. (wiki:Owain_Bourney.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant-1) However, he was secretly a supporter of King Aegon II Targaryen and `SWORN_TO` the Greens, betraying the blacks at the First Battle of Tumbleton. (wiki:Owain_Bourney.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant-1)

## Narrative Arc

When the blacks' host marched south from the riverlands to Tumbleton, Owain and Ser Roger Corne joined them as men from the Blackwater Rush. Owain was secretly a green supporter. During the First Battle of Tumbleton, when the Two Betrayers began attacking the blacks, Owain opened the town castle's gates to the greens while Roger Corne opened Tumbleton's town gates. Owain killed Ser Merrell the Bold with his spear and later claimed responsibility for the greens' victory. (wiki:Owain_Bourney.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant-1)

After the battle, the greens' commanders regrouped at Tumbleton. Owain desired to take command of the host but was opposed by Ser Hobert Hightower, Lord Unwin Peake, and Ser Jon Roxton. Unwin Peake stabbed Owain in the eye during a war council, killing him. (wiki:Owain_Bourney.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant-1)
