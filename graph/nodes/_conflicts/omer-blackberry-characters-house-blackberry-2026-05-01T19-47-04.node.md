## Identity

Omer Blackberry is a sworn sword from House Blackberry in service at Dragonstone. (wiki:Omer_Blackberry.cite_ref-Rasosappendix)

## Allegiances

Omer is `SWORN_TO` House Blackberry and `SWORN_TO` House Baratheon of Dragonstone. He is a member of the king's men loyal to Stannis Baratheon. (track_b: Allegiances)

## Narrative Arc

Omer is among the small group of knights loyal to Stannis Baratheon who escort Edric Storm onto the galley *Mad Prendos*, smuggling him away from Dragonstone. (wiki:Omer_Blackberry.cite_ref-Rasos63)

He remains a guard and protector of Edric in the Free Cities. (wiki:Omer_Blackberry.cite_ref-Radwdappendix)
