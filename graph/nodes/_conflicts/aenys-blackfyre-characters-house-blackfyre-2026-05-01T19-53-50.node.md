## Identity

Aenys Blackfyre was the fifth son of Daemon I Blackfyre and Rohanne of Tyrosh (track_b: Father, Mother). He was of Valyrian race (track_b: Race).

## Origins

Born in the 190s AC, of Crownlands culture (track_b: Culture). His father was Daemon I Blackfyre and his mother was Rohanne of Tyrosh (track_b: Father, Mother).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Blackfyre (track_b: Allegiance).

## Narrative Arc

After the deaths of his father and older brothers, Aenys lived in exile. When a Great Council was called in 233 AC to determine the succession, Aenys put forward his claim to the Iron Throne. Brynden Rivers, the Hand of the King, offered him safe conduct to come to King's Landing and present his claim. Aenys accepted and traveled to the capital, but upon arrival he was seized and executed on Bloodraven's orders (wiki:Aenys_Blackfyre). His execution under a guarantee of safe conduct was considered treacherous; it led to Brynden Rivers's own arrest and exile to the Wall (wiki:Aenys_Blackfyre).
