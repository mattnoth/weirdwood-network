## Identity

The Battle of the Camps was an early battle in the War of the Five Kings, fought in 299 AC at Riverrun. It was a great victory for House Stark and House Tully, who smashed the Lannister force besieging Riverrun (wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps).

## Allegiances

Part of `FIGHTS_IN` War of the Five Kings. Robb Stark's host (northmen under Houses Stark, Glover, Karstark, Mormont, Umber; rivermen under Houses Tully, Blackwood, Mallister) fought against the Lannister siege force (~12,500 men in three camps under Houses Brax, Crakehall, Prester) (track_b: Conflict).

## Narrative Arc

After Jaime Lannister was captured in the Whispering Wood, his leaderless siege force remained divided in three camps around Riverrun, unaware of his defeat. Ser Brynden Tully led Robb's van in a night attack on the camp north of the Tumblestone, overrunning the sleeping, leaderless men. Lord Andros Brax led his men from the middle camp onto rafts to cross the river but Riverrun pelted them with rocks; the rafts sank and Brax drowned. Robb led armored horse from the west to overrun the camp between the rivers. Grey Wind killed four men and a dozen horses. The Lannisters formed a shieldwall but were taken in the rear by a sortie from Riverrun led by Tytos Blackwood, who released prisoners including Edmure Tully. Jon Umber burned the siege towers. In the southern camp, the Tyroshi sellsword leading Forley Prester's freeriders switched sides; Prester retreated with four thousand men. Robb was afterward proclaimed King in the North at Riverrun (wiki:Battle_of_the_Camps, cite: AGOT Ch. 63, 69, 71).
