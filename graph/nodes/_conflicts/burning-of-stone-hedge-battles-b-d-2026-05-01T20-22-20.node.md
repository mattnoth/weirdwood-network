## Identity

The burning of Stone Hedge was the destruction of the seat of House Bracken during the War of the Five Kings, carried out by Lannister forces under Ser Gregor Clegane. (wiki:Burning_of_Stone_Hedge)

## Narrative Arc

As part of the Lannister invasion of the riverlands, Gregor Clegane's forces burned Stone Hedge, the ancestral seat of House Bracken. Harry Rivers, a Bracken bastard, was killed during the attack. The burning was part of the broader campaign alongside the destruction of Pinkmaiden Castle, Darry, and other riverlords' holdings. Stone Hedge later changed hands multiple times during the war. (wiki:Burning_of_Stone_Hedge, agot-56, agot-71, asos-1, asos-34, adwd-48)
