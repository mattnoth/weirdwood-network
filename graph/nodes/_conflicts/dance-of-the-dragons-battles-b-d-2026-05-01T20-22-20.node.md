## Identity

The Dance of the Dragons was a devastating Targaryen civil war fought from 129 to 131 AC between the faction of King Aegon II Targaryen (the greens) and the faction of Queen Rhaenyra Targaryen (the blacks), both claiming the Iron Throne after the death of King Viserys I Targaryen. (wiki:Dance_of_the_Dragons)

## Narrative Arc

Upon the death of Viserys I, the greens — led by the Hightower faction at court — crowned Aegon II despite Viserys having named his daughter Rhaenyra as heir. Rhaenyra was crowned on Dragonstone, and war erupted. The conflict saw the greatest concentration of dragon combat in Westerosi history: dragon fought dragon at Rook's Rest, the Gods Eye (where Daemon Targaryen and Aemond Targaryen killed each other), Tumbleton, and elsewhere. The war devastated the realm: most living dragons were killed, and the Targaryen dynasty's power was permanently diminished. Rhaenyra briefly held King's Landing before being overthrown and fed to Aegon II's dragon Sunfyre. Aegon II himself was later poisoned by his own councillors. The war ended with the ascension of Rhaenyra's son as Aegon III Targaryen, married to Aegon II's daughter Jaehaera — a peace settlement that effectively made both sides' claims moot.
