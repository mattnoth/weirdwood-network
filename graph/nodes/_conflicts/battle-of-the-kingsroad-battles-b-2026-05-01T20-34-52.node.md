## Identity

The Battle of the Kingsroad, called the Muddy Mess by those who fought in it, was the last battle of the Dance of the Dragons, fought in 131 AC on the kingsroad north of King's Landing between the greens and the blacks (wiki:Battle_of_the_Kingsroad).

## Allegiances

Part of `FIGHTS_IN` Dance of the Dragons. Greens (Borros Baratheon with stormlanders and crownlands levies) against blacks (rivermen under Kermit Tully and Benjicot Blackwood) (track_b: Conflict).

## Narrative Arc

Lord Borros Baratheon had seized King's Landing during the Moon of the Three Kings and gathered near six thousand men. Lord Elmo Tully's riverman army of similar size marched south; command passed to his young son Kermit after Elmo died during the march. Borros's army was larger and unbloodied but inexperienced; the rivermen were battle-hardened veterans. The forces met where the kingsroad passed between a forest and a hill on muddy ground. Alysanne Blackwood's longbowmen broke Borros's cavalry charge from the hill. Benjicot Blackwood flanked from the forest. The crownlands levies from Hayford, Rosby, and Stokeworth refused to fight; Flea Bottom rabble fled; Duskendale knights switched sides. Borros killed Lords Roland Darry and Jorah Mallister but was slain by Kermit Tully's morningstar after refusing to surrender (wiki:Battle_of_the_Kingsroad).

The victory led to the poisoning of Aegon II and the Hour of the Wolf (wiki:Battle_of_the_Kingsroad).
