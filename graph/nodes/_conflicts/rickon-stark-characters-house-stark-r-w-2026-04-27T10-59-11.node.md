## Identity

Rickon Stark is the youngest child of Lord Eddard Stark and Lady Catelyn Tully (track_b: Father, Mother). He holds the title of Prince (track_b: Title). Born in 295 AC at Winterfell (track_b: Born), he is approximately three to four years old during AGOT.

## Origins

Son of Eddard Stark and Catelyn Tully (track_b: Father, Mother). Born at Winterfell in the North (track_b: Born). Of Northern culture (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Red-eyed from crying, defiant, nearly four years old (agot-bran-06). Has Tully coloring like Robb, Bran, and Sansa (agot-catelyn-03). Described as wild and fierce — "half the age of Robert Arryn and five times as fierce" (agot-catelyn-06). Named his direwolf Shaggydog (agot-bran-02).

## Narrative Arc

Rickon is the baby of the Stark family, too young to understand the events around him. When the royal party arrives at Winterfell, he is included in the formal procession (agot-jon-01). After Ned departs for King's Landing, Rickon becomes increasingly wild and distraught, refusing to say farewell to Robb when Robb rides south (agot-bran-06). He follows Robb around crying, not understanding why everyone is leaving (agot-catelyn-03). Jon Snow remembers "little Rickon, bright eyes shining as he begged for a sweet" (agot-jon-03). When Bran and Rickon both dream of their father in the crypts, Rickon is found hiding there with Shaggydog, tears on his face (agot-bran-07). He is named in the traitor decree along with his siblings (agot-sansa-05). By ADWD, Rickon is believed to be on the island of Skagos with Osha and Shaggydog.
