## Identity

The Battle of the Fords was a series of engagements fought between the forces of House Tully and House Lannister during the War of the Five Kings in 299 AC, on the fords of the Red Fork of the Trident (wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords).

## Allegiances

Part of `FIGHTS_IN` War of the Five Kings. Rivermen (Ser Edmure Tully with 11,000 — Houses Tully, Blackwood, Bracken, Darry, Frey, Mallister, Paege, Piper, Vance) defended the fords against westermen (Lord Tywin Lannister with ~20,000 — Houses Lannister, Brax, Clegane, Crakehall, Lefford, Marbrand) (track_b: Conflict).

## Narrative Arc

After Robb Stark marched west from Riverrun, he left Ser Edmure Tully to hold Riverrun and guard his rear. Tywin Lannister left Harrenhal to stop Robb's raiding of the westerlands and attempted to cross the Red Fork. Edmure organized every ford north and south of Riverrun to be defended — Lord Jason Mallister commanded the closest fords while Lord Karyl Vance held upstream. Archers and scorpions were placed in the wooded west bank; caltrops and iron spikes spread in the river. Tywin probed for unguarded crossings. Ser Flement Brax was defeated by Mallister archers and scorpions. Three days later, Tywin attempted a dozen crossings simultaneously — all were repelled. Lord Leo Lefford drowned, Ser Robert Brax was slain, Ser Lyle Crakehall was captured, and Ser Addam Marbrand was forced to retreat three times. The fiercest fighting was at Stone Mill where Edmure faced Ser Gregor Clegane; though the Mountain's men gained the west bank, Edmure turned them back with his reserve. Tywin retreated southeast, eventually linking with the Tyrells to win the Battle of the Blackwater (wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords, cite: ACOK Ch. 45, ASOS Ch. 14).

The aftermath proved the battle a pyrrhic victory: Robb and Brynden Tully criticized Edmure for engaging Tywin rather than letting him pass into the westerlands where Robb had planned to trap him. By blocking Tywin's crossing, Edmure inadvertently allowed him to turn east and save King's Landing (wiki:Battle_of_the_Fords, cite: ASOS Ch. 14).
