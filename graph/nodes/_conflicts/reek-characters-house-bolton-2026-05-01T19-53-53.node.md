## Identity

Reek is a man-at-arms in service to House Bolton, personal servant of Ramsay Snow. His real name may be Heke. (wiki:Reek.cite_ref-Racok46; wiki:Reek.cite_ref-Raffcappendix) Not to be confused with Ramsay Snow or Theon Greyjoy, who both later go by the name "Reek."

## Origins

Born in the North. Born with a terrible smell that never went away — a condition the smallfolk called a curse from the gods. (track_b: Born; wiki:Reek.cite_ref-Radwd32)

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Bolton. (track_b: Allegiance)

## Appearances & Description

Reek's defining characteristic is his appalling stench. Despite bathing three times daily, wearing flowers in his hair, and even drinking perfume, nothing masked the smell. Maester Uthor diagnosed the stench as sickness, but Reek was otherwise healthy and strong. He slept in a pig sty because no one at the Dreadfort could bear to be near him. (wiki:Reek.cite_ref-Radwd32)

## Narrative Arc

Roose Bolton gave Reek to Ramsay's mother as a servant — intended as a joke — but Ramsay and Reek became inseparable. Reek served as Ramsay's informal tutor in arms, which explains Ramsay's vicious fighting style. (wiki:Reek.cite_ref-Radwd32)

Together, Ramsay and Reek became infamous for their "hunts" of peasant girls. Ramsay would rape and kill the women; Reek reportedly had his way with the bodies while still warm. (wiki:Reek.cite_ref-Racok16; wiki:Reek.cite_ref-Racok66)

When Ser Rodrik Cassel's men caught them during such a hunt, Ramsay gave Reek his own clothes and identity markers, then sent him to ride for help. Rodrik's men killed Reek, believing him to be Ramsay. Ramsay took on Reek's identity and was taken prisoner to Winterfell. (wiki:Reek.cite_ref-Racok35; wiki:Reek.cite_ref-Racok66) Reek died in 299 AC. (track_b: Died)
