## Identity

Joseth is a stableman at Winterfell who later becomes master of horse (wiki:Joseth).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture). `HOLDS_TITLE` master of horse (track_b: Title).

## Appearances & Description

A stick-thin man (wiki:Joseth.cite_ref-Ragot37). He has twin daughters, Bandy and Shyra (wiki:Joseth.cite_ref-Racok4).

## Narrative Arc

Robb Stark names Joseth as master of horse at Winterfell while Hullen is away with Lord Eddard in King's Landing (wiki:Joseth.cite_ref-Ragot37). After Tyrion Lannister's visit, Joseth trains Dancer to specification so the crippled Bran Stark can ride (wiki:Joseth.cite_ref-Ragot37). During the harvest feast, Bran sends Joseth a huge lobster for his work with Dancer, and later sees Joseth having sex with an unknown woman in the gallery outside the Great Hall (wiki:Joseth.cite_ref-Racok21). When Bran and Rickon Stark disappear after Winterfell's capture, Joseth is forced to join Theon Greyjoy's search party (wiki:Joseth.cite_ref-Racok50). His fate following the sack of Winterfell is unknown, but it is likely he died during the sack (wiki:Joseth.cite_ref-Rasos35). (Tier 2 — wiki infers likely death but does not confirm)
