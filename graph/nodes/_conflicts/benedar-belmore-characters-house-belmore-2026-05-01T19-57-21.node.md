## Identity

Benedar Belmore is the Lord of Strongsong and head of House Belmore, a noble house of the Vale. (wiki:Benedar_Belmore.cite_ref-Raffc23-1)

## Allegiances

Lord Benedar is `SWORN_TO` House Belmore. (track_b: Allegiances) He was formerly `SWORN_TO` the Lords Declarant, a coalition of Vale lords who opposed Petyr Baelish's rule as Lord Protector. (track_b: Allegiances) His culture is that of the Valemen. (track_b: Culture)

## Appearances & Description

Benedar is a fat man with a pear belly and rounded shoulders. An unkempt reddish-grey beard grows from his multiple chins. (wiki:Benedar_Belmore.cite_ref-Raffc23-1) Petyr Baelish believes Lord Belmore to be corrupt. (wiki:Benedar_Belmore.cite_ref-Raffc23-1) The Belmore sigil is six silver bells on purple. (affc-alayne-01)

## Narrative Arc

During the War of the Five Kings, the Belmores support Lord Yohn Royce in pressuring Lady Lysa Arryn to bring the Vale into the conflict. (wiki:Benedar_Belmore.cite_ref-Rasos80-2) Lord Benedar signs the document of the Lords Declarant demanding the handover of Lord Robert Arryn and the dismissal of his councilors. He is the first of the Lords Declarant to arrive at the Gates of the Moon and begins the blockade, preventing fresh food from reaching the Eyrie. (wiki:Benedar_Belmore.cite_ref-Raffc23-1) When the Lords Declarant ascend to the Eyrie, Benedar cannot manage the final climb and must be drawn up in a basket. During the parley with Petyr Baelish, he is distrustful and wants young Lord Robert removed from the Eyrie. (wiki:Benedar_Belmore.cite_ref-Raffc23-1) He later reconciles with Petyr and attends the wedding of Lord Lyonel Corbray in Gulltown. (wiki:Benedar_Belmore.cite_ref-Raffc41-3)
