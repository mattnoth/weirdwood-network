## Identity

The burning of the Sept of Remembrance was the destruction of the Sept of Remembrance in King's Landing by dragonfire during the Faith Militant uprising under King Maegor I Targaryen. (wiki:Burning_of_the_Sept_of_Remembrance)

## Narrative Arc

King Maegor I Targaryen rode Balerion to destroy the Sept of Remembrance, which had become a stronghold of the Warrior's Sons. Approximately 700 members of the Warrior's Sons were killed in the conflagration. The destruction was part of Maegor's brutal campaign to crush the Faith Militant after they rose against Targaryen rule. (wiki:Burning_of_the_Sept_of_Remembrance)
