## Identity

Ser Tytos Brax is a knight of House Brax and the eldest son of Lord Andros Brax. He becomes Lord of Hornvale after his father's death. (wiki:Tytos_Brax.cite_ref-Rasosappendix-1)

## Origins

Born in or before 276 AC. A Westermen noble of House Brax. (wiki:Tytos_Brax)

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Brax. `HOLDS_TITLE` Ser, Lord of Hornvale. `CULTURE_OF` Westermen. (track_b: Allegiance, Titles, Culture)

## Appearances & Description

Tytos wears a pale grey doublet slashed with cloth-of-silver, with the amethyst unicorn of House Brax pinned above his heart. (wiki:Tytos_Brax.cite_ref-Raffc16-3)

## Narrative Arc

Tytos is part of the Lannister host besieging Riverrun. He is captured at the Battle in the Whispering Wood. (wiki:Tytos_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot63-4) His father Lord Andros dies during the Battle of the Camps. (wiki:Tytos_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot69-5)

A Brax is among western lords at King Joffrey's first audience after the Battle of the Blackwater; it is unknown whether this is Tytos, who may have been ransomed or escaped captivity. (wiki:Tytos_Brax.cite_ref-Racok65-6) (Tier 2 -- wiki hedges with "It is unknown")

Previously a captive at the Twins, Tytos becomes Lord of Hornvale. His younger brother Ser Flement is his heir. (wiki:Tytos_Brax.cite_ref-Rasosappendix-1) (wiki:Tytos_Brax.cite_ref-Rasos38-7)

Tytos is among the nobility escorting Lord Tywin Lannister's corpse to Casterly Rock. Ser Jaime Lannister considers that Lord Brax could deal with the outlaws Sandor Clegane and Beric Dondarrion, but would not make a good Hand of the King. (wiki:Tytos_Brax.cite_ref-Raffc16-3)
