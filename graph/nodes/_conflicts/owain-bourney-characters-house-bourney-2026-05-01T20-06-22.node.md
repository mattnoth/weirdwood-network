## Identity

Lord Owain Bourney, known as Lord Owain the Betrayer, was the head of House Bourney during the Dance of the Dragons. He was from the Blackwater Rush region. (wiki:Owain_Bourney.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant-1)

## Origins

Owain is described as a "Blackwater man," placing his origin along the Blackwater Rush, which runs through the Westerlands, Riverlands, Crownlands, and the northern Reach. His precise region is uncertain. (Tier 2 -- wiki note states the Blackwater Rush crosses four regions; exact origin unspecified)

## Allegiances

Owain was nominally `SWORN_TO` the Blacks, marching south with their host. He was secretly a supporter of King Aegon II Targaryen and `BETRAYS` the Blacks during the First Battle of Tumbleton, switching allegiance to the `SWORN_TO` Greens. He was head of `SWORN_TO` House Bourney. (wiki:Owain_Bourney.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant-1)

## Narrative Arc

Owain joined the host of blacks led by Ser Garibald Grey and Lord Roderick Dustin as they marched south from the riverlands to Tumbleton, accompanied by Ser Roger Corne. He was secretly loyal to King Aegon II. When the Two Betrayers began attacking the blacks during the First Battle of Tumbleton, Roger Corne opened Tumbleton's gates to the greens while Owain opened the castle gates. Owain killed Ser Merrell the Bold with his spear and later claimed responsibility for the greens' victory. (wiki:Owain_Bourney.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant-1)

After the battle, the greens' commanders regrouped at Tumbleton. Owain desired to take command of the host but was opposed by Ser Hobert Hightower, Lord Unwin Peake, and Ser Jon Roxton. Unwin Peake stabbed Owain in the eye during a war council, killing him in 130 AC. (wiki:Owain_Bourney.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant-1)
