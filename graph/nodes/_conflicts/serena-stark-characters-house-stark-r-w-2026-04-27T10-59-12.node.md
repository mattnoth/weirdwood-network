## Identity

Serena Stark was a member of House Stark, daughter of Rickon Stark and Jeyne Manderly (track_b: Father, Mother). She was born between 141 and 162 AC and died in 154 AC (track_b: Born, Died).

## Origins

Daughter of Rickon Stark (son of Cregan) and Jeyne Manderly (track_b: Father, Mother). Of Northern culture (track_b: Culture).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiances). `SWORN_TO` House Umber (track_b: Allegiances) — through her first marriage. `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture). She held authority at Last Hearth through her Umber marriage (track_b: Title).

## Narrative Arc

Serena married twice: first Jon Umber, then Edric Stark (track_b: Spouses). Through her second marriage to Edric Stark, she had four children: Torrhen Stark, Cregard Stark, Arrana Stark, and Argelle Stark (track_b: Issue). Her marriages helped consolidate Stark power through alliances with both House Umber and within the Stark family itself.
