## Identity

Lord Bracken was the Lord of the Stone Hedge and head of House Bracken during the reigns of kings Daeron II and Aerys I Targaryen. His given name is unknown. (wiki:Lord_Bracken_(father_of_Otho))

## Narrative Arc

During the First Blackfyre Rebellion, Lord Bracken declared for House Blackfyre. He sailed to Myr to hire crossbowmen for Daemon I Blackfyre and Bittersteel but was delayed by storms. (wiki:Lord_Bracken_(father_of_Otho).cite_ref-2)

By 211 AC, Lord Bracken was dying. His eldest son had died during the Great Spring Sickness, leaving his belligerent second son Ser Otho as heir. It is unclear if he died before or during 212 AC; the conspirators of the Second Blackfyre Rebellion hoped Otho would add Bracken forces to their cause, suggesting Otho may have already succeeded his father. (wiki:Lord_Bracken_(father_of_Otho).cite_ref-1)
