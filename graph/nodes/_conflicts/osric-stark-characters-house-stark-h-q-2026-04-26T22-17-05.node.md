## Identity

Osric Stark was a member of House Stark who became Lord Commander of the Night's Watch around 400 BC (wiki:Osric_Stark).

## Origins

He was a son or brother of a Stark King in the North (wiki:Osric_Stark.cite_ref-Raffc5). Died at age 70 (track_b: Died).

## Narrative Arc

Osric was only ten years old when elected Lord Commander of the Night's Watch, making him the youngest ever chosen for that position (wiki:Osric_Stark.cite_ref-Raffc5). Maester Balder wrote *The Edge of the World* during Osric's rule of sixty years (wiki:Osric_Stark).
