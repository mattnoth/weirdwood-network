## Identity

Germund Botley is a member of House Botley and the younger brother of Lord Sawane Botley. He is the current Lord of Lordsport, having been granted the title and half of House Botley's lands by Euron Greyjoy after Sawane's death. (wiki:Germund_Botley)

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Botley (track_b: Allegiance). Germund is a supporter of Euron Greyjoy, serving as one of his champions at the kingsmoot. (wiki:Germund_Botley.cite_ref-Raffcappendix)

## Appearances & Description

Germund wears a gilded breastplate that he took off a Lannister captain during Greyjoy's Rebellion. (wiki:Germund_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc18)

## Narrative Arc

After Germund's brother Sawane is drowned for defying Euron Greyjoy, Germund is awarded half of House Botley's lands and the lordship of Lordsport in return for swearing allegiance to Euron. The other half of the lands are granted to House Wynch. (wiki:Germund_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc11) Germund is listed as one of Euron's champions at the kingsmoot. (wiki:Germund_Botley.cite_ref-Raffcappendix)
