## Identity

Wex Pyke is the bastard son of Sargon Botley of Lordsport. He was born mute. (wiki:Wex_Pyke, wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok24)

## Origins

Born 286 AC in the Iron Islands. (wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Rawoiafwex_pyke)

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Botley and House Greyjoy. (track_b: Allegiances) Wex serves as squire to Theon Greyjoy during the War of the Five Kings. (wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok24)

## Appearances & Description

Wex has a tangle of dark brown hair with an almost feral face, a wide mouth, a sharp nose, and a pointed chin. (wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Radwd29) He is clever and a quick learner, adept with daggers. (wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok24, wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Radwd29)

## Narrative Arc

Wex becomes Theon Greyjoy's squire as part of the deal when Theon buys the horse Smiler from Lord Sawane Botley. (wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok24) He participates in the harrying of the Stony Shore and the capture of Winterfell. (wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok37) During the search for the escaped Bran and Rickon Stark, Wex notices they are seeing only wolf prints and not Hodor's tread, alerting Theon that they have been tricked. (wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok50)

When Ser Rodrik Cassel's northmen come to retake Winterfell, Wex is the first to stand alongside Theon. (wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Racok66) He survives the sack of Winterfell by climbing the heart tree in the godswood. From there, he witnesses the departure of Bran and Rickon with their direwolves and companions. He follows Rickon, Osha, and Shaggydog, learning they cross the Bay of Seals to Skagos. (wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Radwd29)

Eventually falling into Manderly control, Wex is taken to White Harbor, where they realize he is not unintelligent, only mute. They teach him letters using yes/no questions and chalk drawings. Lord Wyman Manderly and Robett Glover learn the truth of the sack, and Wex repudiates Ramsay's claim that Theon was to blame. Wex reveals Rickon's location by throwing a dagger at Skagos on Wyman's map, and Manderly tasks Davos Seaworth with retrieving Rickon. (wiki:Wex_Pyke.cite_ref-Radwd29)
