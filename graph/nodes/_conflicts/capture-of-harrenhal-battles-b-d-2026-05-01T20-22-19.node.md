## Identity

The capture of Harrenhal was Ser Gregor Clegane's retaking of Harrenhal from the Brave Companions during the War of the Five Kings, in 299 AC. (wiki:Capture_of_Harrenhal, asos-60)

## Narrative Arc

After Lord Roose Bolton left Harrenhal to its new lord Vargo Hoat of the Brave Companions, Bolton marched north to the Twins for the Red Wedding, deliberately allowing Gregor Clegane to overwhelm his rearguard at the ruby ford. Vargo suffered from infection after Brienne of Tarth bit off his ear. Under orders from Lord Tywin Lannister, Gregor and the Mountain's men retook Harrenhal easily — most Brave Companions had fled. A cook, angered by Vargo cutting off his foot, opened a postern gate. Gregor's men butchered most inhabitants, though the cook, Ben Blackthumb, and Pia survived. Gregor dismembered Vargo alive, feeding him his own flesh as "roast goat," and forced northern captives including Ser Wylis Manderly to eat as well. (wiki:Capture_of_Harrenhal, asos-44, asos-49, asos-53, asos-60, asos-62, asos-74, affc-20, affc-27)
