## Identity

The burning of Sow's Horn was an engagement during the War of the Five Kings in 299 AC, in which Ser Amory Lorch attempted to burn Sow's Horn, the seat of House Hogg, but was repelled. (wiki:Burning_of_Sow's_Horn)

## Narrative Arc

Ser Amory Lorch and his men attacked Sow's Horn as part of the Lannister raiding campaign in the riverlands. However, the Hoggs defended their stronghold successfully, and Lorch failed to take or destroy the castle. The engagement is notable as a rare case of a minor house successfully resisting the Lannister campaign. (wiki:Burning_of_Sow's_Horn, affc-27)
