## Identity

Lyarra Stark was a noblewoman from House Stark, Lady of Winterfell (wiki:Lyarra_Stark). She was a daughter of Rodrik Stark and Arya Flint, and the younger sister of Branda Stark (wiki:Lyarra_Stark).
