## Identity

Maester Kennet was a maester who served Lord Cregan Stark at Winterfell (wiki:Kennet_(maester)). He authored *Passages of the Dead*, a study of northern burial methods (wiki:Kennet_(maester)).

## Narrative Arc

Kennet investigated barrow fields, graves, and tombs across the North (wiki:Kennet_(maester).cite_ref-twoiaf_dawn_age). He studied a giant's barrow near Long Lake containing obsidian arrowheads possibly used by the children of the forest (wiki:Kennet_(maester).cite_ref-twoiaf_dawn_age). In *Passages of the Dead* he recorded the legend of a curse on the Great Barrow to prevent any man from rivaling the First King (wiki:Kennet_(maester).cite_ref-twoiaf_the_north). Kennet also determined that the First Keep of Winterfell was raised after the coming of the Andals (wiki:Kennet_(maester).cite_ref-twoiaf_winterfell).
