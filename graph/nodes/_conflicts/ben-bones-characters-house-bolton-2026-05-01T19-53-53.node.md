## Identity

Ben Bones is a kennelmaster at the Dreadfort sworn to House Bolton. He is one of the Bastard's Boys, the men loyal to Ramsay Bolton. (wiki:Ben_Bones.cite_ref-Radwd12)

## Origins

Born in the North. (track_b: Born)

## Allegiances

`CULTURE_OF` Northmen. (track_b: Culture)

## Appearances & Description

An old man, tasked with keeping the bastard's girls — Ramsay Bolton's pack of female hunting hounds. He likes the dogs better than their master. (wiki:Ben_Bones.cite_ref-Radwd32)

## Narrative Arc

Ben is present in the great hall of the Dreadfort when Little Walder and Big Walder Frey bring "Reek" (Theon Greyjoy) before Ramsay. (wiki:Ben_Bones.cite_ref-Radwd12) At Ramsay's command, Ben makes a collar for Reek and lets him eat and sleep with the hounds. (wiki:Ben_Bones.cite_ref-Radwd32) Ben claims he was instructed by Ramsay to train the dogs to kill wolves. (wiki:Ben_Bones.cite_ref-Radwd32) Theon fears that if he escapes, Ramsay will hunt him down with Ben Bones and the girls. (wiki:Ben_Bones.cite_ref-Radwd51)

## Quotes

> Even if we do escape, Lord Ramsay will hunt us down, him and Ben Bones and the girls. — "Reek" to "Abel" (wiki:Ben_Bones.cite_ref-Radwd51)
