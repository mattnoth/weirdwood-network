## Identity

Maron Botley, also known as Old Botley or Fishwhiskers, is a member of House Botley. He has three sons who accompany him on raids. (wiki:Maron_Botley.cite_ref-Racok37)

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Botley (track_b: Allegiance). Maron serves as part of the crew of the *Sea Bitch* under Theon Greyjoy. (wiki:Maron_Botley.cite_ref-Rasosappendix)

## Narrative Arc

Maron takes part in the harrying of the Stony Shore with his sons. One of his sons gets into an argument with Todric over plunder and loses, leading Theon Greyjoy to put an arrow in Todric's belly. Theon orders Maron and his sons to finish Todric; they slash his throat and loot his corpse. (wiki:Maron_Botley.cite_ref-Racok37)

Two Botleys remain with Theon after the capture of Winterfell and are slain during the sack of Winterfell by House Bolton. It is unknown if Maron or his sons are among them or if they abandoned Theon beforehand. (wiki:Maron_Botley.cite_ref-Racok66)
