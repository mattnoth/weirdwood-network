## Identity

Ser Balon Byrch was a knight of House Byrch who served as a captain and later Commander of the City Watch of King's Landing during the Dance of the Dragons. (wiki:Balon_Byrch.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons) (wiki:Balon_Byrch.cite_ref-Rtpatq)

## Narrative Arc

Balon served as captain of the Old Gate in King's Landing. During the riots of King's Landing in the Dance of the Dragons, he led his men against the rioters and restored order to the streets north and east of Rhaenys's Hill. (wiki:Balon_Byrch.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons) (wiki:Balon_Byrch.cite_ref-Rtpatq)

After the death of Ser Luthor Largent, Balon was made Commander of the City Watch. He accompanied the guard escorting Queen Rhaenyra Targaryen from King's Landing to find safe haven. When the escort was attacked near Duskendale by broken men, Balon was slain by an arrow in 130 AC. (wiki:Balon_Byrch.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons) (wiki:Balon_Byrch.cite_ref-Rtpatq)
