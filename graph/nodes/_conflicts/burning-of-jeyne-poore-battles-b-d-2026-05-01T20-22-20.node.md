## Identity

The Burning of Jeyne Poore was an engagement during the Faith Militant uprising, circa 45–46 AC. King Maegor I Targaryen destroyed a community called Poxy Jeyne (or Jeyne Poore), which had sheltered members of the Faith Militant. (wiki:Burning_of_Jeyne_Poore)

## Narrative Arc

During Maegor's campaign against the Faith Militant, the town of Poxy Jeyne harbored Poor Fellows and other militant faithful. Maegor's forces attacked and burned the settlement. Horys Hill, a notable Poor Fellows leader, was killed in the engagement. The destruction was part of Maegor's broader campaign of terror against the Faith Militant resistance. (wiki:Burning_of_Jeyne_Poore)
