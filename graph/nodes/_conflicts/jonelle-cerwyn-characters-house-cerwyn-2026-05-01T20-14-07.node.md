## Identity

Jonelle Cerwyn is a member of House Cerwyn and the daughter of Lord Medger Cerwyn. She became Lady of Cerwyn in 299 AC after the deaths of her father and younger brother Cley. (wiki:Jonelle_Cerwyn)

## Origins

Born in 267 AC at Castle Cerwyn. (wiki:Jonelle_Cerwyn.cite_ref-1) Daughter of Lord Medger Cerwyn. (track_b: Father)

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Cerwyn. (track_b: Allegiance) `CULTURE_OF` Northmen. (track_b: Culture) Initially a supporter of Robb Stark, she later swore fealty to the Iron Throne. (wiki:Jonelle_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_jonelle_cerwyn)

## Appearances & Description

Jonelle is plump and homely. (wiki:Jonelle_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Ragot53)

## Narrative Arc

Jonelle accompanied her father to Winterfell when Robb Stark called his banners. Lord Medger claimed she was coming to cook for him, though Theon Greyjoy believed Medger intended for Robb to find her in his bed one night. (wiki:Jonelle_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Ragot53)

After the deaths of her father Medger at Harrenhal and her brother Cley at the Battle at Winterfell, Jonelle became Lady of Cerwyn. (wiki:Jonelle_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Rasosappendix) She swore fealty to the Iron Throne and was present at Barrowton after Moat Cailin was recovered from the ironborn. (wiki:Jonelle_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Rawoiaf_jonelle_cerwyn) She put her signature on letters from Ramsay Bolton to Asha Greyjoy at Deepwood Motte and to Jon Snow at Castle Black. (wiki:Jonelle_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Radwd26, wiki:Jonelle_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Radwd28) Cerwyn men were later present at Winterfell for Ramsay Bolton's wedding to "Arya Stark," though it is unknown if Jonelle accompanied them. (wiki:Jonelle_Cerwyn.cite_ref-Radwd41)

## Notes

Jonelle is approximately 32 years old during the events of AGOT. She is roughly 17 years older than her brother Cley. Her swearing fealty to the Iron Throne during ADWD marks a shift from the Cerwyns' earlier support of Robb Stark — likely compelled by the Bolton takeover of the North rather than a willing change of allegiance.
