## Identity

Beatrice Butterwell was a noblewoman from House Butterwell during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen. (wiki:Beatrice_Butterwell)

## Allegiances

## Appearances & Description

According to Archmaester Gyldayn, Beatrice was empty-headed. (wiki:Beatrice_Butterwell.cite_ref-Rfab-1)

## Narrative Arc

Beatrice was the closest friend of Princess Viserra Targaryen. In 87 AC, the princess complained to Beatrice that her brother Prince Baelon showed no desire in marrying her after the death of his wife, their sister Princess Alyssa, and told Beatrice how she believed herself to be far more beautiful than Alyssa. (wiki:Beatrice_Butterwell.cite_ref-Rfab-1)
