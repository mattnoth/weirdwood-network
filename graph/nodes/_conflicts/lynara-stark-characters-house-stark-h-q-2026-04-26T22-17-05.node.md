## Identity

Lynara Stark was a noblewoman of House Stark, Lady of Winterfell, and the third wife of Lord Cregan Stark (wiki:Lynara_Stark).

## Origins

Born in or before 167 AC (track_b: Born). Died in or after 140 AC (track_b: Died). A descendant of a younger son of Lord Brandon "the Boisterous" Stark (wiki:Lynara_Stark).

## Narrative Arc

Lynara married her distant cousin, Cregan Stark, Lord of Winterfell and Warden of the North, becoming his third wife. They had five children: Jonnel, Edric, Lyanna, Barthogan, and Brandon Stark. The main line of House Stark's lineage descends from her (wiki:Lynara_Stark).
