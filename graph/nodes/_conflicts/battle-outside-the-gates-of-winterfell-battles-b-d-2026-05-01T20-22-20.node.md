## Identity

The battle outside the gates of Winterfell was an engagement during the War of the Five Kings in 299 AC. Ramsay Snow betrayed the northern forces gathered to retake Winterfell from Theon Greyjoy's ironborn, leading to the sack of Winterfell. (wiki:Battle_outside_the_gates_of_Winterfell)

## Narrative Arc

Ser Rodrik Cassel gathered a host including men from House Cerwyn, Manderly, Tallhart, and other northern houses to retake Winterfell from Theon Greyjoy. Ramsay Snow arrived with Bolton forces, ostensibly to assist. However, Ramsay betrayed the northern host — killing Ser Rodrik Cassel and Lord Cley Cerwyn, and scattering the gathered forces. Leobald Tallhart was also killed. Ramsay's men then entered Winterfell, sacked and burned the castle. Theon was taken captive by the Boltons. The destruction of Winterfell and the betrayal of the northern forces served Roose Bolton's strategy to supplant the Starks as rulers of the North. (wiki:Battle_outside_the_gates_of_Winterfell, acok-66, adwd-29, adwd-35)
