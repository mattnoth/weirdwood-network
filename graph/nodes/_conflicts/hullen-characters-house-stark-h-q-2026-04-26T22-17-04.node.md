## Identity

Hullen is a member of the Stark household, serving as master of horse at Winterfell (wiki:Hullen). He is the father of Harwin (track_b: Issue).

## Origins

Born in the North (track_b: Born). Died in 298 AC at the Red Keep, King's Landing (track_b: Died).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture). `HOLDS_TITLE` Master of horse at Winterfell (track_b: Title).

## Appearances & Description

Arya Stark considers him to be an old man (wiki:Hullen.cite_ref-Ragot50).

## Narrative Arc

Hullen is among the party that discovers the direwolf pups in the snow south of the Wall; he is uneasy about direwolves being found so far south (agot-bran-01). He accompanies Lord Eddard Stark south when Eddard is named Hand of the King, helping Sansa mount her horse before departure and giving up his position as master of horse (wiki:Hullen.cite_ref-Ragot14). He is killed when Eddard is arrested; Arya finds him dying in the Red Keep's stables (wiki:Hullen.cite_ref-Ragot50). His death is later reported at Winterfell (wiki:Hullen.cite_ref-Racok21). Arya tells his son Harwin about the manner of his death (wiki:Hullen.cite_ref-Rasos17). Theon Greyjoy recalls Hullen among the dead of Winterfell (wiki:Hullen.cite_ref-Radwd51).

## Quotes

> "He always said he'd die in a stable, but we all thought some bad-tempered stallion would be his death, not a pack of lions." — Harwin, after learning of Hullen's death (wiki:Hullen.cite_ref-Rasos17)
