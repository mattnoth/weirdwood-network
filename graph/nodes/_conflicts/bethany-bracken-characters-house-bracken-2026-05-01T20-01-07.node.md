## Identity

Bethany Bracken was a member of House Bracken who became Aegon the Unworthy's seventh mistress. (wiki:Bethany_Bracken)

## Origins

Born 156 AC or later. (track_b: Born) Daughter of Lord Bracken of Stone Hedge, the Hand of the King. (track_b: Father) Younger sister of Barba Bracken.

## Narrative Arc

After Barba Bracken was displaced as Aegon IV's mistress by Melissa Blackwood, Barba and her father groomed Bethany to win back the king's favor. In 177 AC, Bethany caught Aegon's eye when he visited Stone Hedge to see his and Barba's bastard son Aegor Rivers. By then the king was fat and foul-tempered, but Bethany delighted him, and he took her to King's Landing. (wiki:Bethany_Bracken.cite_ref-2)

However, Bethany found Aegon's embraces distressing and turned to a handsome knight of the Kingsguard, Ser Terrence Toyne, for comfort. The pair was discovered abed by Aegon himself in 178 AC. Even though they proclaimed their love, Bethany was forced to watch as Terrence was dismembered and died screaming. Both Bethany and her father were then executed. (wiki:Bethany_Bracken.cite_ref-2)

## Notes

Bethany's execution alongside her father and Ser Terrence Toyne triggered lasting consequences, including the Toyne conspiracy against the Kingsguard and House Toyne's downfall. Her story is referenced in AFFC and ASOS by Arys Oakheart, Jaime Lannister, and Loras Tyrell as a cautionary tale for Kingsguard members.
