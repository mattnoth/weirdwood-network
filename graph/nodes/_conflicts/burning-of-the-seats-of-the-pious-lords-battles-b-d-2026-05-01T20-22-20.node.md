## Identity

The burning of the seats of the pious lords was a series of dragonfire attacks in 43 AC during the Faith Militant uprising, in which Queen Visenya Targaryen on Vhagar and King Maegor I Targaryen on Balerion destroyed the castles of lords who had supported the Faith Militant. (wiki:Burning_of_the_seats_of_the_pious_lords)

## Narrative Arc

In 43 AC, Visenya Targaryen riding Vhagar burned five seats, while Maegor riding Balerion burned four more. The targeted lords had supported the Faith Militant resistance against Targaryen rule. The houses whose seats were destroyed included members of the riverlands and crownlands nobility. The campaign of destruction was intended to terrorize the remaining lords into abandoning the Faith Militant cause. (wiki:Burning_of_the_seats_of_the_pious_lords)
