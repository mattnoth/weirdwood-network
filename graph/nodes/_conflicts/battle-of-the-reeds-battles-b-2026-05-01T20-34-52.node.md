## Identity

The Battle of the Reeds was fought on the southern shore of the Gods Eye during Aegon's Conquest, circa 2 BC (wiki:Battle_of_the_Reeds).

## Narrative Arc

Aegon the Conqueror defeated an attack by men of Harren Hoare at the Reeds, but the Targaryens then suffered heavy losses at the Wailing Willows (wiki:Battle_of_the_Reeds).
