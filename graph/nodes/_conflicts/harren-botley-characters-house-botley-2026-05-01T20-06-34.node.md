## Identity

Harren Botley is the eldest son of Lord Sawane Botley and the heir of House Botley. He has a younger brother, Tristifer. (wiki:Harren_Botley.cite_ref-Raffcappendix)

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Botley (track_b: Allegiance)

## Narrative Arc

Harren is slain during the siege of Moat Cailin after being hit by one of the crannogmen's poisoned arrows. His brother Tristifer informs Asha Greyjoy of his death. (wiki:Harren_Botley.cite_ref-Raffc11)
