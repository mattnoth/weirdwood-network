## Identity

Ser Theo Bolling was a landed knight of House Bolling during the reign of King Maegor I Targaryen. (wiki:Theo_Bolling.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i)

## Origins

Theo was a Stormlander of House Bolling. He married Lady Elinor Costayne, with whom he had three sons. (wiki:Theo_Bolling.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon)

## Narrative Arc

Ser Theo was a landed knight who participated in one of King Maegor I Targaryen's campaigns against the Poor Fellows during the Faith Militant uprising. In 47 AC, Theo was arrested by knights of the Kingsguard and accused of conspiring with Dowager Queen Alyssa Velaryon to place Prince Jaehaerys Targaryen on the Iron Throne. Despite declaring his innocence, he was found guilty and beheaded that same day. (wiki:Theo_Bolling.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon) (wiki:Theo_Bolling.cite_ref-Rtwoiafthe_targaryen_kings:_maegor_i)

Seven days after Theo's execution, King Maegor announced he would wed Theo's widow, Lady Elinor. Maegor then dispersed Theo's three sons: one was fostered at the Eyrie by House Arryn, another at Highgarden by Lord Tyrell, and the youngest was given to a wetnurse. (wiki:Theo_Bolling.cite_ref-Rfabthe_sons_of_the_dragon)
