## Identity

Rosamund Ball was a noblewoman of House Ball during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen. (wiki:Rosamund_Ball.cite_ref-Rfab-1)

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Ball. (track_b: Allegiance)

## Narrative Arc

Rosamund served as a lady and companion to Queen Alysanne Targaryen. When the queen was attacked by an order of holy sisters while attempting to bathe in the sacred waters of Jonquil's Pool at Maidenpool, Rosamund put herself between the queen and the assassins. She was stabbed in the stomach during the attack. The shouts alerted the Kingsguard guarding the bathhouse entrance, who intervened and ended the plot. Rosamund died a few days later from the wound, in 51 AC. (wiki:Rosamund_Ball.cite_ref-Rfab-1)
