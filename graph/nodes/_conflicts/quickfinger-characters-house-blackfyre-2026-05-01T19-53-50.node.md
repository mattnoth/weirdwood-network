## Identity

Quickfinger was the nickname of a thief who lived during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen (wiki:Quickfinger).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Blackfyre during the First Blackfyre Rebellion (track_b: Allegiance).

## Narrative Arc

During the First Blackfyre Rebellion, Quickfinger sided with the rebels and supported Daemon Blackfyre. He was caught with stolen dragon eggs. Eustace Osgrey, a former rebel, later lamented that if Quickfinger had not been caught, the stolen dragon eggs might have changed the tide of the war (wiki:Quickfinger.cite_ref-Rtss-1).
