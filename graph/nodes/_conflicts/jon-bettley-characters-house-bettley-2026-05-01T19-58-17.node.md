## Identity

Ser Jon Bettley, better known as Beardless Jon, is a knight of House Bettley from the Westerlands. (wiki:Jon_Bettley.cite_ref-Rawoiafjon_bettley-1)

## Origins

Born in the Westerlands. (wiki:Jon_Bettley.cite_ref-Rawoiafjon_bettley-1)

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Bettley. (track_b: Allegiance)

Supporter of Joffrey Baratheon and later Tommen Baratheon during the War of the Five Kings. He serves in Jaime Lannister's host during the riverlands campaign. (affc-jaime-03)

## Appearances & Description

Jon is beardless. According to Jaime Lannister, Jon's homely wife is famous for having the beard he lacks. (wiki:Jon_Bettley.cite_ref-Raffc44-2)

## Narrative Arc

During the events of A Feast for Crows, Beardless Jon is spotted by Jaime Lannister riding at a quintain in the yard of the Red Keep, where he scores a solid hit. (wiki:Jon_Bettley.cite_ref-Raffc16-3) (affc-jaime-02)

He marches north as part of Jaime's force to break the siege of Riverrun. During the march, he is among Jaime's knights and is sent to bear the peace banner and a message to the besieged garrison. (affc-jaime-05)

After the fall of Riverrun, Ser Jon joins Ser Lyle Crakehall (Strongboar) in setting out for Darry to hunt outlaws, preferring that duty to returning home. (wiki:Jon_Bettley.cite_ref-Raffc44-2) (affc-jaime-07)
