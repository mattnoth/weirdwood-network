## Identity

Lady Alayne Baelish was the mother of Petyr Baelish (wiki:Alayne_Baelish.cite_ref-Rasos68). Her first name was Alayne; her maiden name and origins are unrecorded (track_b: Spouse).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Baelish (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Westeros (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Alayne is known only through a single mention. When Petyr Baelish disguises Sansa Stark as his bastard daughter, he names her "Alayne" after his mother and gives her the surname "Stone" (wiki:Alayne_Baelish.cite_ref-Rasos68).

## Notes

Alayne is mentioned only in ASOS. Her title "Lady" comes from the infobox (track_b: Title). The wiki page distinguishes her from Alayne Royce (daughter of Lord Allard Royce) and from the alias "Alayne Stone" used by Sansa Stark.
