## Identity

Aemon Blackfyre was the second son of Daemon I Blackfyre and Rohanne of Tyrosh, and the younger twin of Aegon Blackfyre (wiki:Aemon_Blackfyre). He was of Valyrian race (track_b: Race).

## Origins

Born in 184 AC in Westeros (track_b: Born). His father was Daemon I Blackfyre and his mother was Rohanne of Tyrosh (track_b: Father, Mother). He was of Crownlands culture (track_b: Culture).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Blackfyre (track_b: Allegiance).

## Appearances & Description

Like his twin Aegon, Aemon was characterized as a "witless bully" by Alyn Cockshaw for their treatment of their younger brother Daemon II Blackfyre (wiki:Aemon_Blackfyre).

## Narrative Arc

Aemon fought alongside his father and twin brother during the First Blackfyre Rebellion. At the Battle of the Redgrass Field in 196 AC, after Daemon I was slain, Aemon took up the Valyrian steel sword Blackfyre from his dying father's hand and continued the fight. He too was killed by arrows from Brynden Rivers and the Raven's Teeth atop the Weeping Ridge (wiki:Aemon_Blackfyre). He was one of the wielders of the sword Blackfyre, however briefly.
