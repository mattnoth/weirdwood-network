## Identity

Ser Lyle Bracken was a knight of House Bracken and a member of the Warrior's Sons at the start of the reign of King Maegor I Targaryen. (wiki:Lyle_Bracken.cite_ref-1)

In 42 AC, Ser Lyle was stationed at the King's Landing chapter of the Warrior's Sons based in the Sept of Remembrance. He fought in the trial of seven in which seven Warrior's Sons faced King Maegor I and his six champions. Ser Lyle died during the fight. (wiki:Lyle_Bracken.cite_ref-1)
