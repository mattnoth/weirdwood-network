## Identity

Jynessa Blackmont is a noblewoman of House Blackmont. She is the eldest child and heir of Larra Blackmont, the Lady of Blackmont. (wiki:Jynessa_Blackmont.cite_ref-Rasosappendix)

## Origins

Jynessa is the daughter of Larra Blackmont, Lady of Blackmont. Her father is unknown. She has a younger brother, Perros Blackmont, and additional younger siblings. (wiki:Jynessa_Blackmont.cite_ref-Rasosappendix)

## Narrative Arc

Jynessa accompanies her mother Larra and brother Perros as part of the escort that brings Prince Oberyn Martell to King's Landing when he comes to claim his seat on the small council. (wiki:Jynessa_Blackmont.cite_ref-Rasos38)
