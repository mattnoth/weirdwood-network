## Identity

Tomard, commonly known as Fat Tom, was a household guard and captain of the guard for Lord Eddard Stark (track_b: Title, Aliases). He was of Northern culture and served House Stark (track_b: Allegiance, Culture). Born between 249 and 253 AC, he died in 298 AC in King's Landing (track_b: Born, Died).

## Origins

Of Northern culture (track_b: Culture). Father of TomToo (track_b: Issue). Ned's children gave him the nickname "Fat Tom" (agot-eddard-12).

## Narrative Arc

Tomard was a loyal and dependable member of Eddard Stark's household. He served as a guard and doorkeeper, announcing visitors to Ned's solar (agot-eddard-08). After Jory Cassel's death in the street fight with Jaime Lannister, Fat Tom took over command of the household guard (agot-eddard-12). He helped the injured Ned walk to the godswood and carried sealed messages on his behalf (agot-eddard-12). Ned reassigned the escort of his daughters from Porther to Tomard, increasing the guard from ten to twenty men, and entrusted him with delivering a letter to Stannis at Dragonstone (agot-eddard-13). During the betrayal in the throne room, a gold cloak drove a spear through Tomard's back, the wet red point bursting out through his ribs (agot-eddard-14). He missed his wife and was apprehensive about the danger Ned faced (agot-eddard-13).
