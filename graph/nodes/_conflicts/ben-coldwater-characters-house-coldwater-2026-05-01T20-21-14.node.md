Ser Ben Coldwater is a knight from House Coldwater in the Vale. (wiki:Ben_Coldwater.cite_ref-Winds_1-0)

## Narrative Arc

Ben attends the tourney held to select members of the newly-created Brotherhood of Winged Knights. During the feast at the Gates of the Moon, after musicians begin playing for the dance, he inquires about the lack of singers and is the first knight to ask Alayne Stone (Sansa Stark) to dance.
