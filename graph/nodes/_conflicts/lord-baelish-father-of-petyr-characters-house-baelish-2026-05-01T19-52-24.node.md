## Identity

The father of Petyr Baelish was "the smallest of small lords" in the Vale of Arryn (wiki:Lord_Baelish_(father_of_Petyr).cite_ref-Racok17). His first name is unknown. He held the title Lord and was head of House Baelish (track_b: Title).

## Origins

His grandfather was a Braavosi sellsword in the service of Lord Corbray, and his father was a hedge knight who took the head of the Titan of Braavos as his sigil when knighted (wiki:Lord_Baelish_(father_of_Petyr).cite_ref-Rasos68). He was born at least several years before 256 AC. He married Lady Alayne, and they had one son, Petyr (track_b: Spouse, Issue).

## Narrative Arc

During the War of the Ninepenny Kings, Lord Baelish became friends with Lord Hoster Tully. He later leveraged that friendship to have his son Petyr fostered at Riverrun (wiki:Lord_Baelish_(father_of_Petyr).cite_ref-4). Lord Baelish died several years before the start of the series and was succeeded by his son Petyr (wiki:Lord_Baelish_(father_of_Petyr).cite_ref-Ragot18). He died between 282 and 296 AC per wiki calculation.
