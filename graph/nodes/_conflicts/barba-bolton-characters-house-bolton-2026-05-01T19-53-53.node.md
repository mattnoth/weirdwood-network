## Identity

Barba Bolton was a noblewoman of House Bolton during the reign of King Aegon III Targaryen. (wiki:Barba_Bolton.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents)

## Origins

## Narrative Arc

In 133 AC, the Maiden's Day Ball was held at the Red Keep to find a second wife for King Aegon III. Barba attended the procession of maidens but rather than simply curtsying, she asked the king that if he did not choose her, to at least send food to the north, as the region was suffering greatly from winter. (wiki:Barba_Bolton.cite_ref-Rfabunder_the_regents)
