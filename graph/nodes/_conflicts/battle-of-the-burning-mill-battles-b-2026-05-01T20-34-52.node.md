## Identity

The Battle of the Burning Mill took place in 129 AC near the start of the Dance of the Dragons. It was the first true battle in the civil war between opposing armies in the field, as the preceding fall of Harrenhal had been bloodless (wiki:Battle_of_the_Burning_Mill).

## Allegiances

Part of the `FIGHTS_IN` Dance of the Dragons. House Blackwood declared for Queen Rhaenyra Targaryen (blacks), while House Bracken supported King Aegon II Targaryen (greens) (track_b: Conflict).

## Narrative Arc

Lord Samwell Blackwood sent raiders into Bracken lands, prompting Ser Amos Bracken to march on Blackwood territory. The Blackwoods surprised the Brackens while camped by a mill near a river, possibly the Red Fork. Amos slew Lord Blackwood in single combat, but was himself killed by a weirwood arrow said to have been fired by Samwell's sister, Alysanne Blackwood. The mill was put to the torch during the fighting. Ser Raylon Rivers led the Bracken survivors in retreat to Stone Hedge, but the castle had been taken in the meantime by Prince Daemon Targaryen (wiki:Battle_of_the_Burning_Mill).
