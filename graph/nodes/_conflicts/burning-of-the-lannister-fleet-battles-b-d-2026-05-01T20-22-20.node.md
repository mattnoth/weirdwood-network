## Identity

The burning of the Lannister fleet was a naval attack during Greyjoy's Rebellion in 289 AC, in which the ironborn destroyed the Lannister fleet at anchor at Lannisport. (wiki:Burning_of_the_Lannister_fleet)

## Narrative Arc

The attack was devised by Euron Greyjoy, who conceived the plan to strike the Lannister fleet before it could be mobilized. Victarion Greyjoy led the Iron Fleet into Lannisport and threw the first torch. The ironborn burned the Lannister fleet at anchor, dealing a devastating blow to the westerlands' naval power. The surprise attack was a Greyjoy victory that opened the rebellion, though the ironborn ultimately lost the war. (wiki:Burning_of_the_Lannister_fleet, affc-18, adwd-56, adwd-63)
