## Identity

Ser Flement Brax is a knight from House Brax and the third son of Lord Andros Brax of Hornvale. He is married to Morya Frey and they have three sons: Robert, Walder, and Jon. (wiki:Flement_Brax.cite_ref-Rasosappendix-1)

## Origins

Born in or before 278 AC. Son of Lord Andros Brax. (wiki:Flement_Brax)

## Appearances & Description

Flement wears silver armor inlaid with amethysts and a striped purple-and-silver cloak. His horsehead helmet has a spiral horn two feet long, and his shield displays the unicorn of House Brax. (wiki:Flement_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot56-3)

## Narrative Arc

Flement serves in Lord Tywin Lannister's host in the riverlands, where he leads a mounted party that challenges Tyrion Lannister and the Vale mountain clansmen on approach to camp. (wiki:Flement_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot56-3) He rides under Ser Addam Marbrand's command in the right flank during the Battle on the Green Fork. (wiki:Flement_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot62-4) He learns of his father Andros's drowning at the Battle of the Camps. (wiki:Flement_Brax.cite_ref-Ragot69-5)

Tywin commands Flement to force a crossing six miles south of Riverrun during the Battle of the Fords, but Mallister bowmen and scorpions repulse his attempt. (wiki:Flement_Brax.cite_ref-Racok45-6)

In King's Landing, Tyrion presents Flement as the heir to Hornvale when greeting Prince Oberyn Martell's Dornish party. (wiki:Flement_Brax.cite_ref-Rasos38-7) Flement testifies as a prosecution witness at Tyrion's trial for the death of King Joffrey, telling how he heard Tyrion threaten the king. (wiki:Flement_Brax.cite_ref-Rasos66-8)

Flement leads two hundred heavy horse in Ser Jaime Lannister's force to take Riverrun. He joins Jaime for supper at Hayford, where he annoys Jaime with admiration for his golden hand. At Harrenhal, Flement spars with Ser Lyle Crakehall in the yard. (wiki:Flement_Brax.cite_ref-Raffc27-9)
