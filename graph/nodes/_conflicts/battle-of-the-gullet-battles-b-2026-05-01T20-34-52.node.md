## Identity

The Battle of the Gullet was a massive naval battle fought in the Gullet on the 5th day of the 1st moon of 130 AC during the Dance of the Dragons. It remains among the bloodiest sea battles in all of history (wiki:Battle_of_the_Gullet).

## Allegiances

Part of `FIGHTS_IN` Dance of the Dragons. Blacks (House Velaryon fleet, dragonriders Jacaerys Velaryon on Vermax, Addam Velaryon on Seasmoke, Hugh Hammer on Vermithor, Ulf White on Silverwing, Nettles on Sheepstealer, Aegon III on Stormcloud) fought against the Triarchy fleet (90 warships under Admiral Sharako Lohar of Lys, allied with the Greens) (track_b: Conflict).

## Narrative Arc

Otto Hightower, Hand of the King to Aegon II, arranged an alliance with the Triarchy to break the Velaryon blockade of Blackwater Bay. Meanwhile, Prince Jacaerys Velaryon decided to send his half-brothers Aegon and Viserys to Pentos for safety on the cog Gay Abandon. The Triarchy fleet intercepted the Gay Abandon; Viserys was captured by a Tyroshi captain, while young Aegon escaped on his dragon Stormcloud, who was mortally wounded by arrows and scorpion bolts and died after reaching Dragonstone. Sharako Lohar divided his fleet into two squadrons entering the Gullet north and south of Dragonstone, attacking at sunrise. Jacaerys on Vermax swept down on a line of Lysene galleys; when joined by four other dragonriders the enemy line shattered. But Vermax flew too low and crashed into the sea, possibly struck by a crossbow bolt. Jacaerys leapt free but was killed by Myrish crossbowmen. The southern squadron bypassed Dragonstone and sacked Driftmark, burning Spicetown and High Tide. Only 28 of 90 Triarchy ships returned home. The Velaryon fleet lost a third of its strength. The battle was a tactical green victory but a strategic black win — the Triarchy took such heavy losses they played no further part in the war. The Myrish and Tyroshi blamed Sharako for holding back his Lysene ships, sparking the quarrel that led to the Daughters' War. Sharako sold the captive Viserys to a Lysene magister (wiki:Battle_of_the_Gullet).
