## Identity

Palla is a kennel girl at Winterfell, daughter of Farlen the kennelmaster (wiki:Palla).

## Origins

Father: Farlen (track_b: Father).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Palla plays lord of the crossing with the Stark wards Big Walder and Little Walder Frey, along with other children, getting muddy and laughing so hard she loses her breath (wiki:Palla.cite_ref-Racok4). When Winterfell is captured by Theon Greyjoy's ironborn, Palla is raped by Drennan and another ironman. Bran sees her enter the Great Hall walking in pain with a ripped dress, supported by her limping father (wiki:Palla.cite_ref-Racok46). Theon has the two rapists whipped (wiki:Palla.cite_ref-Racok50). After Bran and Rickon escape, Theon forces Farlen and his dogs to join the search by threatening Palla's safety (wiki:Palla.cite_ref-Racok50). Theon later uses Farlen as a scapegoat and executes him (wiki:Palla.cite_ref-Racok56). When "Reek" (Ramsay Snow) asks for Palla as his reward for bringing reinforcements, Theon reluctantly agrees; but Ramsay instead demands Theon's bedwarmer Kyra (wiki:Palla.cite_ref-Racok56). The sack of Winterfell is blamed on Ramsay's men and the ironborn (wiki:Palla.cite_ref-Rasos35). Palla's fate after the sack is unknown.
