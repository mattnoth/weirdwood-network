## Identity

Ser Alyn Ashford was a knight of House Ashford during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen. (wiki:Alyn_Ashford.cite_ref-Rfab-1)

## Allegiances

Alyn held the title of Ser and was `SWORN_TO` House Ashford. He was of the Reach in culture. (track_b: Allegiance)

## Narrative Arc

In 73 AC, Alyn participated in a tourney at Old Oak held by Lord Oakheart. He was defeated by a mystery knight known as the Silver Fool, who was later revealed to be Prince Baelon Targaryen. (wiki:Alyn_Ashford.cite_ref-Rfab-1)
