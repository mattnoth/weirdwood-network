## Identity

Ser Edmund Breakstone is a knight of House Breakstone, a noble house of the Vale. (wiki:Edmund_Breakstone.cite_ref-Winds_1-0)

## Allegiances

Ser Edmund is `SWORN_TO` House Breakstone. (track_b: Allegiance) He is of the Valemen culture. (track_b: Culture)

## Narrative Arc

Ser Edmund travels to the Gates of the Moon to participate in the tourney held to determine the membership of the Brotherhood of Winged Knights. During the feast, noting the splendid gifts provided for all competitors and their attending relatives and friends, Ser Edmund comments that their host, Lord Nestor Royce, has an open hand. (wiki:Edmund_Breakstone.cite_ref-Winds_1-1)
