## Narrative Arc

Jacks accompanies Lord Eddard Stark to King's Landing when Eddard is named Hand of the King (wiki:Jacks.cite_ref-Ragot22). He and Varly escort Eddard when he visits the armorer Tobho Mott and discovers Gendry (wiki:Jacks.cite_ref-Ragot27). After Eddard's arrest, Jacks is presumably imprisoned and later sent with Ser Cleos Frey's party returning to Riverrun with Eddard's bones (wiki:Jacks.cite_ref-Racok24). He serves under Hallis Mollen's command (wiki:Jacks.cite_ref-Racok25). Hal continues north toward Winterfell with Jacks, Quent, and Shadd, escorting the silent sisters carrying Lord Eddard's bones (wiki:Jacks.cite_ref-Rasos39). Lady Barbrey Dustin tells Theon that should the bones emerge from the Neck, the escorts will be prevented from going north of Barrowton (wiki:Jacks.cite_ref-Radwd41).
