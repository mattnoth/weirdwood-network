## Identity

Syrio Forel is a bravo from Braavos, a master fencer and former First Sword to the Sealord of Braavos (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot22, wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot50). He served for nine years as First Sword (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot22). He also holds the title of water dancer (track_b: Titles).

## Origins

Born in Braavos (track_b: Born). Of Braavosi culture (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Syrio is a slight, bald man with a beak of a nose (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot22). He wears a leather vest (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot50) and teaches with slender wooden swords with lead cores (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot22, wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot50). He is demanding and creative in his teachings but protective of his pupils (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot50). He often says "Just so" (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Raffc22).

## Narrative Arc

Syrio became First Sword after correctly identifying a rare cat on the Sealord's lap as an ordinary tomcat — because he saw the truth rather than what others wanted him to see (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot50). Lord Eddard Stark hired Syrio to teach his daughter Arya the water dance style of swordsmanship, believing the slender Needle would suit the Braavosi style (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot22). Syrio trained Arya in the Small Hall of the Tower of the Hand, teaching her not just sword technique but perception and fearlessness (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot22, wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot30). He had her catch cats to improve her speed (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot32). When Cersei sent Ser Meryn Trant with Lannister guards to seize Arya during the arrest of Eddard Stark, Syrio ordered Arya to flee and held off the attackers with only his wooden practice sword. He killed or disabled five guards, then faced Meryn in full plate armor with only a broken wooden sword, refusing to run: "The first sword of Braavos does not run" (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Ragot50). According to a semi-canon source, Syrio was killed in King's Landing (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Rawoiafsyrio_forel). Arya continues to invoke his teachings throughout ACOK, ASOS, and AFFC as she escapes, survives, and trains at the House of Black and White (wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Racok1, wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Rasos3, wiki:Syrio_Forel.cite_ref-Raffc6).
