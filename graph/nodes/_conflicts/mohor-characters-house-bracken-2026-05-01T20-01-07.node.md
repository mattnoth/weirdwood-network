## Identity

Mohor is a man-at-arms sworn to House Bracken. (wiki:Mohor.cite_ref-1)

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Bracken (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Westeros (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

A spearman who wears a rounded iron cap that makes him look like he has a bowl on his head. (wiki:Mohor.cite_ref-1) He carries an iron-tipped spear and a dagger. (agot-tyrion-04)

## Narrative Arc

Along with Lharys and Kurleket, Mohor is one of three Bracken men-at-arms at the inn at the crossroads who assist Lady Catelyn Stark in capturing Tyrion Lannister and bringing him toward the Vale of Arryn. He is slain during their first fight with Vale mountain clans. (wiki:Mohor.cite_ref-1) (agot-tyrion-04)
