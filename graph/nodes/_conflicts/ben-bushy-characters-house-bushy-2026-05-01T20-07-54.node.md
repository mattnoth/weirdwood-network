## Identity

Ser Ben Bushy, known as Big Ben Bushy, is a knight of House Bushy from the Reach. (wiki:Ben_Bushy.cite_ref-Raffc14)

## Appearances & Description

Ben Bushy was one of the few men in Renly Baratheon's service whose height exceeded Brienne of Tarth's. (wiki:Ben_Bushy.cite_ref-Raffc14)

## Narrative Arc

While Renly Baratheon's army was encamped beneath Highgarden, Big Ben courted Brienne of Tarth, sending his squire to clean her mail and gifting her a silver drinking horn. (wiki:Ben_Bushy.cite_ref-Raffcappendix) Together with Ser Hyle Hunt and Ser Edmund Ambrose, he started a wager on who could take Brienne's maidenhead; the bet was eventually discovered by Lord Randyll Tarly. (wiki:Ben_Bushy.cite_ref-Raffc14) After the wager was ended, Brienne sought out and defeated Big Ben during the melee at Bitterbridge. (wiki:Ben_Bushy.cite_ref-Raffc20) He was subsequently killed at the Battle of the Blackwater in 299 AC; which side he fought for is not stated. (wiki:Ben_Bushy.cite_ref-Raffc14)
