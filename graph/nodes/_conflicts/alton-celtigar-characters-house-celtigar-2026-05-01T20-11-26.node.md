## Identity

Alton Celtigar was the Lord of Claw Isle and head of House Celtigar during the reign of King Aegon I Targaryen. He served as the third Hand of the King. (wiki:Alton_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon)

## Origins

Of Valyrian descent, from the Crownlands. (track_b: Race, Culture)

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Celtigar. (track_b: Allegiance)

## Narrative Arc

Alton was named Hand of the King by Aegon I following the resignation of Lord Edmyn Tully in 9 AC. He served ably as Hand until he died from natural causes in 17 AC. He was succeeded as Hand by Ser Osmund Strong. (wiki:Alton_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon)

His relations to Crispian Celtigar, master of coin during Aegon's Conquest, and to Edwell Celtigar, a later Lord of Claw Isle and Hand of the King, are unknown. (wiki:Alton_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthree_heads_had_the_dragon)
