## Identity

Lord Bracken was the Lord of the Stone Hedge and head of House Bracken during the reigns of kings Baelor I, Viserys II, and Aegon IV Targaryen. His given name is unknown. He had two daughters, Barba and Bethany. (wiki:Lord_Bracken_(Hand_of_the_King).cite_ref-1)

## Narrative Arc

Lord Bracken sent his daughter Barba to the Maidenvault as a maiden companion to the imprisoned Targaryen princesses during Baelor I's reign. When Aegon IV took the throne in 172 AC, he named Lord Bracken as Hand of the King and took Barba as his mistress. (wiki:Lord_Bracken_(Hand_of_the_King).cite_ref-1)

When Queen Naerys nearly died in childbirth, Lord Bracken spoke openly of wedding Barba to the king, but when Naerys recovered, Prince Daeron and Prince Aemon forced Aegon to send Barba away. After Melissa Blackwood replaced Barba, Lord Bracken and Barba groomed his younger daughter Bethany to win back the king's favor. In 177 AC Bethany became Aegon's mistress, but in 178 AC she was caught abed with Ser Terrence Toyne. Both Bethany and Lord Bracken were executed. (wiki:Lord_Bracken_(Hand_of_the_King).cite_ref-1)
