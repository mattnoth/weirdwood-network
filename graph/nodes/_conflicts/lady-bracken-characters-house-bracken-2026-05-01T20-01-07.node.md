## Identity

Lady Bracken is the third and current wife of Jonos Bracken, Lord of the Stone Hedge and head of House Bracken. Her given name and birth house are unknown. (wiki:Lady_Bracken.cite_ref-2)

## Narrative Arc

Lady Bracken has three daughters with Jonos: Catelyn, Bess, and Alysanne. (wiki:Lady_Bracken.cite_ref-1) Since the burning of Stone Hedge, she spends all her time praying with her septon. (wiki:Lady_Bracken.cite_ref-2)
