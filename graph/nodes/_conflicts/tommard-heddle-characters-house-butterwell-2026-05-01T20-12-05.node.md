## Identity

Ser Tommard Heddle, known as Black Tom, was a knight from the Heddle family. He was the son-in-law of Lord Ambrose Butterwell of Whitewalls and commander of the garrison there. (wiki:Tommard_Heddle.cite_ref-Rtmk-1)

## Appearances & Description

Tommard was heavyset, broad, and barrel chested. His bald head was also broad, and he had a coal-black square beard. Tom had red boils on his cheek and neck. His voice was a deep bass, angry and rumbling. (wiki:Tommard_Heddle.cite_ref-Rtmk-1)

Tom wore black plate over boiled leather, and his helm resembled a scaled and slavering demon. He carried a longsword. His personal arms displayed a green winged demon. (wiki:Tommard_Heddle.cite_ref-Rtmk-1)

## Narrative Arc

According to Uthor Underleaf, Tommard won the hand of Lord Ambrose Butterwell's eldest daughter by killing three of her other suitors, and he once unhorsed Lord Damon Lannister in a tourney. Tommard commanded the garrison at Whitewalls. (wiki:Tommard_Heddle.cite_ref-Rtmk-1)

At the wedding tourney at Whitewalls, Black Tom conspired with Lord Gormon Peake to unveil "Ser John the Fiddler" as Daemon II Blackfyre, attempting to foment the Second Blackfyre Rebellion. Tom defeated all comers in the tourney. After the dragon egg was reported missing, Black Tom struck Ser Glendon Ball with his mailed fist while arresting him for the crime. (wiki:Tommard_Heddle.cite_ref-Rtmk-1)

When word reached Tommard that Prince Aegon Targaryen was within the castle, he came to the sept to take the prince hostage against Lord Ambrose's wishes. Ser Duncan the Tall fought Tom to protect Aegon, and despite being wounded early in the fight, Duncan smashed Tom with his shield, cut his thigh, chopped off his forearm, and stabbed him in the throat, killing him. After Lord Brynden Rivers subdued the rebellion, the severed heads of Gormon and Tom were impaled on spears before the Hand's pavilion. (wiki:Tommard_Heddle.cite_ref-Rtmk-1)
