## Identity

Perros Blackmont is a member of House Blackmont and the son of Larra Blackmont, the Lady of Blackmont. He serves as a squire. (wiki:Perros_Blackmont.cite_ref-Rasosappendix)

## Origins

Perros is the son of Larra Blackmont. His father is unknown. He has an elder sister, Jynessa Blackmont, and additional younger siblings. (wiki:Perros_Blackmont.cite_ref-Rasosappendix)
