## Identity

Luton is a man-at-arms sworn to House Bolton. He is one of the Bastard's Boys. (wiki:Luton.cite_ref-Radwd37)

## Origins

Born in the North. (track_b: Born)

## Narrative Arc

Luton is present at the wedding of Ramsay Bolton to "Arya Stark" (Jeyne Poole). (wiki:Luton.cite_ref-Radwd37) When several men-at-arms in Winterfell are murdered, suspicion falls on White Harbor knights, leading to a skirmish between Freys and Manderlys. (wiki:Luton.cite_ref-Radwd46) During this confrontation, Luton is severely wounded. As he tries to push his entrails back into his wound, crying for his mother, Ramsay loses patience and drives a spear through Luton's chest, killing him. (wiki:Luton.cite_ref-Radwd51)
