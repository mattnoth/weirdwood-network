## Identity

Cosgrove was the steward of House Butterwell and master of the games at Whitewalls during the reign of King Aerys I Targaryen. (wiki:Cosgrove)

## Narrative Arc

During the wedding tourney at Whitewalls, Cosgrove acted as the master of the games. Ser Duncan the Tall sent his squire Egg to meet with Cosgrove and enter the hedge knight in the lists as a mystery knight called the Gallows Knight. Ser Uthor Underleaf bribed Cosgrove for favorable matches. (wiki:Cosgrove.cite_ref-Rtmk-1)

Cosgrove was reluctant to admit Ser Glendon Flowers to Lord Ambrose Butterwell's feast within Whitewalls, insisting that Ser Quentyn Ball did not have a son and disregarding Glendon's claim of having been knighted by Ser Morgan Dunstable. Ser Kirby Pimm spoke with Cosgrove, and Glendon was eventually allowed to attend the feast. (wiki:Cosgrove.cite_ref-Rtmk-1)
