## Identity

Domeric Bolton was the trueborn son and heir of Lord Roose Bolton and Lady Bethany Ryswell. He was a page, squire, and skilled horseman who died under suspicious circumstances in 297 AC. (wiki:Domeric_Bolton.cite_ref-Radwd32)

## Appearances & Description

Domeric was quiet but accomplished — a skilled rider who won a horse race at a tourney, and a player of the high harp. He served as a page to Lady Barbrey Dustin in Barrowton and as a squire to Lord Horton Redfort in the Vale. (wiki:Domeric_Bolton.cite_ref-Radwd32)

## Narrative Arc

Domeric was the only trueborn son of Roose Bolton to survive the cradle. After serving as a page and squire, he sought out his illegitimate half-brother Ramsay Snow, wanting a brother of his own since his trueborn brothers had all died in infancy. Shortly after making contact with Ramsay, Domeric died of what Maester Uthor called a sickness of the bowels. Lord Roose, however, suspected Ramsay had poisoned Domeric. (wiki:Domeric_Bolton.cite_ref-Radwd32)
