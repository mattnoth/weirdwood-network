## Identity

Arthor Celtigar was a member of House Celtigar during the Dance of the Dragons. Born 115 AC, died 130 AC in King's Landing. (wiki:Arthor_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown)

## Narrative Arc

When riots broke out in King's Landing in 130 AC, Arthor was in the manse of his kinsman Lord Bartimos Celtigar, who served as master of coin to Queen Rhaenyra Targaryen. The building was overrun by a mob, and Arthor made a brave stand in a doorway, holding the mob at bay with a sword, until a treacherous serving girl let rioters in from a back way. Arthor was slain when he was stabbed in the back with a spear. (wiki:Arthor_Celtigar.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_overthrown)
