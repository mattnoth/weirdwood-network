## Identity

The grandfather of Petyr Baelish was a hedge knight of the Vale of Arryn and the founder of House Baelish (wiki:Baelish_(hedge_knight).cite_ref-Racok17, wiki:Baelish_(hedge_knight).cite_ref-Rasos68). His given name is unknown.

## Origins

His father was a Braavosi sellsword in the service of Lord Corbray. When the hedge knight was knighted, he took the head of the Titan of Braavos as his sigil (wiki:Baelish_(hedge_knight).cite_ref-Rasos68). His personal arms were a grey stone head with fiery eyes on a light green field (track_b: Personal arms).

## Narrative Arc

The hedge knight is an ancestral figure mentioned only in retrospect. He rose from lowborn origins — his father a Braavosi sellsword — to earn a knighthood and found House Baelish, establishing the minor noble house that would eventually produce Petyr Baelish. He was the first head of the house and was succeeded by his son, Lord Baelish (wiki:Baelish_(hedge_knight).cite_ref-Racok17).
