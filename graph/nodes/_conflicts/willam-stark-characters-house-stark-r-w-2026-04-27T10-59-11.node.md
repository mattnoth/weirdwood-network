Willam Stark was a Lord of Winterfell and Warden of the North (track_b: Titles). He was the son of Beron Stark and Lorra Royce (track_b: Father, Mother). Born between 185 and 211 AC, he died in 226 AC at Long Lake (track_b: Born, Died).

## Narrative Arc

Willam Stark served as Lord of Winterfell. He married twice: first Lyanne Glover, then Melantha Blackwood (track_b: Spouses). He fathered Brandon Stark, Edwyle Stark, and Jocelyn Stark (track_b: Issue). Edwyle Stark would succeed him as Lord of Winterfell and father Rickard Stark. Willam died in 226 AC at Long Lake (track_b: Died), likely in battle. He was buried in the Crypt of Winterfell (track_b: Buried).
