## Identity

Ser Olyver Bracken was a knight from House Bracken and a member of the Kingsguard during the reign of King Maegor I Targaryen. (wiki:Olyver_Bracken.cite_ref-1)

## Narrative Arc

Ser Olyver and his sworn brother Ser Raymund Mallery abandoned King Maegor I in 48 AC when Prince Jaehaerys made his claim for the Iron Throne. Since both had broken their Kingsguard oaths, Jaehaerys offered them execution or taking the black. Both chose the Night's Watch. (wiki:Olyver_Bracken.cite_ref-1)

The Lord Commander gave Olyver command of Rimegate and Raymund command of Sable Hall, with orders to restore the crumbling forts. However, in 50 AC, Olyver and Raymund led a rebellion of former Faith Militant in the Watch, trying to make themselves lords of their castles. Lord Walton Stark rode north to end the mutiny. The defeated rebels at Rimegate were hanged, except Olyver, who was beheaded by Lord Stark with the Valyrian steel greatsword Ice. (wiki:Olyver_Bracken.cite_ref-2)
