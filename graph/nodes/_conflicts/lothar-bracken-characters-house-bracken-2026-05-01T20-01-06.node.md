Lothar Bracken was a Lord of the Stone Hedge and head of House Bracken. (wiki:Lothar_Bracken.cite_ref-1)

## Narrative Arc

When Harwyn Hoare, King of the Iron Islands, invaded the northern riverlands during Storm King Arrec Durrandon's rule, Lady Agnes Blackwood led the rivermen in resistance. Lord Lothar attacked her army from behind, possibly hoping to become King of the Trident himself. However, Harwyn claimed the riverlands for House Hoare. Six months later, Lothar rebelled against the new King of the Isles and the Rivers, but Harwyn crushed the rebellion, sacking and destroying Stone Hedge, and imprisoning Lothar in a crow cage. Lothar died of starvation most of a year later. (wiki:Lothar_Bracken.cite_ref-1)
