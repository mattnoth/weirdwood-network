## Identity

Lord Bracken was the Lord of the Stone Hedge and head of House Bracken during the reign of King Aerys II Targaryen. His given name is unknown. (wiki:Lord_Bracken_(Aerys_II))

## Narrative Arc

In 276 or 277 AC, Lord Bracken and Lord Blackwood travelled to Riverrun to lay their feud before Hoster Tully. During their stay, Hoster announced the betrothal of his daughter Catelyn Tully to Brandon Stark. (wiki:Lord_Bracken_(Aerys_II).cite_ref-3) When a border dispute over a mill arose, the two lords went to King's Landing where Hand of the King Tywin Lannister arbitrated in favor of Lord Blackwood, but was overruled by King Aerys II who gave the disputed mill to Lord Bracken. (wiki:Lord_Bracken_(Aerys_II).cite_ref-2)

Lord Bracken offered the hand of his daughter to Hoster Tully's brother, Ser Brynden Tully, who refused the match. (wiki:Lord_Bracken_(Aerys_II).cite_ref-1)
