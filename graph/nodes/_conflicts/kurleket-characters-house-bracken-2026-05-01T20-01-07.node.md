## Identity

Kurleket is a man-at-arms sworn to House Bracken. (wiki:Kurleket.cite_ref-1)

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Bracken (track_b: Allegiance).

## Appearances & Description

A great fat oaf with short-cropped hair and a pig's face. (wiki:Kurleket.cite_ref-1) Pass 1 notes he threatens Tyrion and grabs him by the hair. (agot-tyrion-04)

## Narrative Arc

When Catelyn Stark calls upon Bracken loyalty to House Tully at the crossroads inn, Kurleket along with Lharys and Mohor assists in capturing Tyrion Lannister. He escorts the party into the Mountains of the Moon and is slain during their first battle with Vale mountain clans, his face smashed by a mace. Tyrion takes his dirk. (wiki:Kurleket.cite_ref-1) (agot-tyrion-04)

## Notes

Kurleket is a reference to Curly of the Three Stooges (SSM). Along with Lharys (Larry) and Mohor (Moe), the three Bracken men-at-arms are named after the Three Stooges.
