Vickon Botley is the fifth son of Lord Sawane Botley. He is a noble of House Botley from the Iron Islands. After Euron Greyjoy seized the Seastone Chair, Vickon and his brothers were dispossessed of their lands and titles. (wiki:Vickon_Botley)
