## Identity

Ser Byron the Beautiful is a hedge knight who enters the service of Lord Petyr Baelish (wiki:Byron_the_Beautiful.cite_ref-Raffc41).

## Appearances & Description

Byron is young and elegant, with thick blond hair (wiki:Byron_the_Beautiful.cite_ref-Raffc41).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Baelish (track_b: Allegiance). `HOLDS_TITLE` Ser (track_b: Title).

## Narrative Arc

Along with Ser Shadrich and Ser Morgarth the Merry, Ser Byron enters the service of Lord Petyr Baelish at the Gates of the Moon. He compliments Alayne Stone on her beauty when introduced and kisses her hand when he leaves the room (wiki:Byron_the_Beautiful.cite_ref-Raffc41).
