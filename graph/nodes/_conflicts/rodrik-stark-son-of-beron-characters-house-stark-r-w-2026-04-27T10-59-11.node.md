## Identity

Rodrik Stark, known as "the Wandering Wolf," was a son of Lord Beron Stark and Lorra Royce (track_b: Father, Mother, Aliases). He was born between 190 and 227 AC and died in 214 AC (track_b: Born, Died).

## Origins

## Narrative Arc

Rodrik earned the nickname "the Wandering Wolf" and served with the Second Sons, a sellsword company in Essos (track_b: Allegiances). He married Arya Flint and fathered Branda Stark and Lyarra Stark (track_b: Spouse, Issue). Lyarra Stark would later marry Rickard Stark, making Rodrik the maternal grandfather of Eddard, Brandon, Lyanna, and Benjen Stark.
