## Identity

Ser Androw Ashford was a knight from House Ashford, the eldest son and heir of Lord Ashford during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen. (wiki:Androw_Ashford.cite_ref-Rthk-2)

## Origins

Born at Ashford. (wiki:Androw_Ashford.cite_ref-Rawoiaf-1) His father was Lord Ashford. (track_b: Father)

## Allegiances

Androw held the title of Ser and was `SWORN_TO` House Ashford. He was of the Reach in culture. (track_b: Allegiance)

## Narrative Arc

Androw participated in the tourney at Ashford Meadow in 209 AC. Along with his younger brother Ser Robert, he was one of his sister's champions in the tourney. He was defeated in the first tilt by Ser Tybolt Lannister after six broken lances. (wiki:Androw_Ashford.cite_ref-Rthk-2)
