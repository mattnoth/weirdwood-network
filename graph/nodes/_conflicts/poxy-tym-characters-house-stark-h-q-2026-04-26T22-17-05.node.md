## Identity

Poxy Tym is a member of the household guard sworn to House Stark of Winterfell (wiki:Poxy_Tym).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Stark (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Northmen (track_b: Culture).

## Appearances & Description

Considered a fine bowman (wiki:Poxy_Tym.cite_ref-Racok50).

## Narrative Arc

Poxy Tym is hired as a guard at Winterfell after both Lord Eddard and Robb Stark go south (wiki:Poxy_Tym.cite_ref-Racok16). He weeps after the capture of Winterfell by ironborn (wiki:Poxy_Tym.cite_ref-Racok46). Theon Greyjoy orders him to accompany the search for Bran and Rickon Stark (wiki:Poxy_Tym.cite_ref-Racok50). Following the sack of Winterfell, Bran finds that Tym was killed by an axe to his face (wiki:Poxy_Tym.cite_ref-Racok69).
