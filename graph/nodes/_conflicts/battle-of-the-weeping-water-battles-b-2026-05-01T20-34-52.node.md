## Identity

The Battle of the Weeping Water was a battle fought near the Weeping Water in the North during the Andal invasion of Westeros (wiki:Battle_of_the_Weeping_Water).

## Allegiances

Part of the `FIGHTS_IN` Andal invasion. The First Men of the North, led by `SWORN_TO` House Stark, fought alongside `ALLIES_WITH` House Bolton against the Andals (track_b: Conflict).

## Narrative Arc

King Theon Stark allied with the Boltons to crush Argos Sevenstar and his Andals at the Weeping Water. Argos Sevenstar was killed in the battle, resulting in a Northern victory (wiki:Battle_of_the_Weeping_Water).
