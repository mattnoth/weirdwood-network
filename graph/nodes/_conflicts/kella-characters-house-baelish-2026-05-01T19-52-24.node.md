## Identity

Kella is a servant sworn to House Baelish at their tower on the Fingers (wiki:Kella.cite_ref-Rasos68).

## Appearances & Description

Kella is a fat middle-aged woman. She tends House Baelish's herds of sheep. She has many bastard children by different fathers (wiki:Kella.cite_ref-Rasos68).

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Baelish (track_b: Allegiance). `CULTURE_OF` Valemen (track_b: Culture).

## Narrative Arc

Kella is among Lord Petyr Baelish's attendants who meet him when he comes home to his tower. She brings her daughter, a girl of two or three with a sty on her eye, whom Lord Petyr has not met before. The father of the child is unknown to Kella (wiki:Kella.cite_ref-Rasos68).
