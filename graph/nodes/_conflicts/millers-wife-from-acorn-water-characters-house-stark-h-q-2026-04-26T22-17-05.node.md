## Identity

The miller's wife from Acorn Water is the wife of the miller of Acorn Water near Winterfell. She has two young sons (wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water).

## Origins

Born in the North (track_b: Born). Died 299 AC at Acorn Water (track_b: Died).

## Allegiances

## Appearances & Description

She has big soft pillowy breasts with wide dark nipples, stretch marks on her belly, a sweet mouth, and a merry laugh (wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water). Theon has known her for years and bedded her several times; he thinks there was nothing special about her but later recalls her features and that she would claw at his back, though he cannot recall her name (wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water).

## Narrative Arc

When Ser Rodrik Cassel captures "Reek," his party stops at the mill; the miller's wife sells them hay and Rodrik speaks with her sons (wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water.cite_ref-Racok50). After Bran and Rickon escape Winterfell, "Reek" suggests they are hiding at the mill. At the mill, Reek kills the two boys. When the mother begs Theon for mercy, Gelmarr cuts her down with one axe blow. The dead children are decapitated and their heads skinned and dipped in tar, then presented as Bran and Rickon (wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water.cite_ref-Racok56). Theon later has a nightmare of having sex with her again, seeing her among the dead at a feast (wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water.cite_ref-Racok56). At Winterfell for Ramsay Bolton's wedding, a regretful Theon recalls her and her murdered sons (wiki:Miller's_wife_from_Acorn_Water.cite_ref-Radwd37).
