## Identity

Ser Marwyn Belmore is a knight from House Belmore. He formerly served as captain of the guards at the Eyrie and now holds the same position at the Gates of the Moon. He is mockingly called "Ser Ding-Dong" by the singer Marillion. (wiki:Marwyn_Belmore.cite_ref-Raffc10-1)

Marwyn is lanky and has red hair. (wiki:Marwyn_Belmore.cite_ref-Raffc10-1)

## Narrative Arc

Ser Marwyn becomes captain of the guards at the Eyrie after the death of Ser Vardis Egen in a trial by combat. (wiki:Marwyn_Belmore.cite_ref-Racokappendix-2) During his tenure, the singer Marillion mocks him as "Ser Ding-Dong." Marwyn vows to have the singer's tongue removed, but Lady Lysa Arryn forbids it. (wiki:Marwyn_Belmore.cite_ref-Raffc10-1) Upon Lord Petyr Baelish's arrival at the Eyrie, Marwyn is removed from his position and replaced by Ser Lothor Brune. (wiki:Marwyn_Belmore.cite_ref-Raffc10-1) He later returns to the Eyrie to hear Marillion confess to the murder of Lady Lysa. (wiki:Marwyn_Belmore.cite_ref-Raffc10-1) Marwyn subsequently serves as captain of the guards at the Gates of the Moon as part of Lord Nestor Royce's household. (wiki:Marwyn_Belmore.cite_ref-Raffcappendix-3)
