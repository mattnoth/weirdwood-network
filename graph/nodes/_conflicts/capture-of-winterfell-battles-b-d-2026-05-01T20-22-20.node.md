## Identity

The capture of Winterfell, also called the fall or taking of Winterfell, was a daring raid by Theon Greyjoy that resulted in the ironborn seizure of Winterfell, the ancestral seat of House Stark, during the War of the Five Kings in 299 AC. (wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell, acok-46, acok-50, acok-56)

## Narrative Arc

After Dagmer Cleftjaw besieged Torrhen's Square as a diversion, Ser Rodrik Cassel marched Winterfell's garrison to relieve it, leaving the castle minimally defended. Theon traveled inland with approximately thirty ironborn and took Winterfell by surprise in a night assault. Four ironborn scaled the double walls, killed the guard Alebelly, and opened a postern gate. Theon declared himself Prince of Winterfell and took Bran and Rickon Stark as hostages. When the Stark boys escaped with the help of Osha and Hodor, Theon had Ramsay Snow (disguised as "Reek") kill two miller's boys as substitutes, claiming Bran and Rickon were dead. The alleged deaths had far-reaching consequences — Robb Stark's grief led him to marry Jeyne Westerling, breaking his Frey alliance, and Catelyn freed Jaime Lannister in hopes of exchanging him for her daughters. (wiki:Capture_of_Winterfell, acok-35, acok-37, acok-46, acok-50, acok-55, acok-56, acok-66)
