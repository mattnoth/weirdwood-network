Lord Jon Charlton was the head of House Charlton during the reign of King Aegon II Targaryen. (wiki:Jon_Charlton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant)

Jon was slain in the Battle by the Lakeshore at the Gods Eye in 130 AC, fighting for the blacks during the Dance of the Dragons. (wiki:Jon_Charlton.cite_ref-Rfabthe_dying_of_the_dragons_-_rhaenyra_triumphant) The succession box on the wiki indicates his predecessor as Lord Charlton was Theo Charlton, though the intervening holders are unknown.
