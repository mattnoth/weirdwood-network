## Identity

Barba Bracken was a noblewoman of House Bracken during the reigns of kings Baelor I, Viserys II, and Aegon IV Targaryen. She was the fifth of Aegon the Unworthy's mistresses. (wiki:Barba_Bracken)

## Appearances & Description

Barba was buxom with dark hair. She was vivacious and not very modest. The Blackwoods claim she was not as beautiful as Melissa Blackwood, and Maester Yandel also describes Melissa as prettier. (wiki:Barba_Bracken.cite_ref-2) (wiki:Barba_Bracken.cite_ref-3)

## Narrative Arc

Barba was sent to the Maidenvault in the Red Keep as a maiden companion to the imprisoned Targaryen princesses during Baelor I's reign. When Viserys II released them in 171 AC, sixteen-year-old Barba met Prince Aegon, who became entranced with her. (wiki:Barba_Bracken.cite_ref-3) When Aegon became king in 172 AC, he named Barba's father Hand and openly took Barba as his mistress. She bore him a bastard son, Aegor Rivers (Bittersteel). When Queen Naerys nearly died in childbirth, Barba's father spoke openly of wedding Barba to the king, but when Naerys recovered, Prince Daeron and Prince Aemon the Dragonknight forced Aegon to send Barba and her son away from court. (wiki:Barba_Bracken.cite_ref-1)

After her displacement by Melissa Blackwood, Barba and her father groomed her younger sister Bethany to win back the king's favor. (wiki:Barba_Bracken.cite_ref-2)

## Notes

Barba's notoriety stems from the "Barba's Teats" toponym — two hills near King's Landing named after her by Aegon IV. The name endured long after her fall from favor.
