## Identity

The battle on the Green Fork was a major engagement of the War of the Five Kings, fought on the Green Fork of the Trident in 298 AC between Lannister forces under Lord Tywin Lannister and a northern host under Lord Roose Bolton. (wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork)

## Narrative Arc

The battle was part of Robb Stark's strategy to relieve the siege of Riverrun. Robb split his forces at the Twins: he sent Roose Bolton with the northern foot south along the kingsroad to engage Tywin's host on the Green Fork, while Robb himself led the horse west to strike Jaime's besieging force at Riverrun. Bolton's force served as a feint — engaging Tywin to prevent him from reinforcing Jaime. The Lannisters won the tactical engagement on the Green Fork, but the strategic victory belonged to Robb, whose cavalry won the Battle in the Whispering Wood and the Battle of the Camps while Tywin was occupied. Tyrion Lannister fought in the battle's vanguard and was knocked unconscious early in the fighting. (wiki:Battle_on_the_Green_Fork, agot-62, agot-63, agot-69)
