## Appearances & Description

A big man, pop-eyed and wide of mouth, with dead white flesh. He looked as if his father had sired him on a fish, but he still wore a longsword. (wiki:Dagon_Codd.cite_ref-Radwd20)

## Narrative Arc

During the siege of Moat Cailin, Dagon found only two ironborn left alive in the Children's Tower. Because they were eating corpses, he killed them both. (wiki:Dagon_Codd.cite_ref-Radwd20)

When Ramsay Bolton sent Theon Greyjoy to offer peace terms to the ironborn garrison, a drunken Dagon was the only one who refused to surrender. Adrack Humble threw an axe into Dagon's forehead to end his resistance. Despite Ramsay's promise of safe passage, the remaining ironborn who surrendered, including Adrack, were flayed after yielding Moat Cailin. (wiki:Dagon_Codd.cite_ref-Radwd20)
