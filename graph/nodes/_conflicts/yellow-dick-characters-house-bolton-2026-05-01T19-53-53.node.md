## Identity

Yellow Dick is a man-at-arms sworn to House Bolton. He is one of the Bastard's Boys. (wiki:Yellow_Dick.cite_ref-Radwd12)

## Origins

Born in the North. (track_b: Born)

## Allegiances

`SWORN_TO` House Bolton. (track_b: Allegiance)
`CULTURE_OF` Northmen. (track_b: Culture)

## Appearances & Description

Yellow Dick is a eunuch, having lost his manhood in unclear circumstances. Despite this, he is known for boasting of his prowess with women. (wiki:Yellow_Dick.cite_ref-Radwd20; wiki:Yellow_Dick.cite_ref-Radwd37)

## Narrative Arc

Yellow Dick is present at the Dreadfort with the other Bastard's Boys. (wiki:Yellow_Dick.cite_ref-Radwd12) He accompanies the Bolton host to Winterfell for Ramsay's wedding. (wiki:Yellow_Dick.cite_ref-Radwd37) During the series of mysterious murders at Winterfell, Yellow Dick is found dead in the snow with his genitals mutilated — his member shoved into his mouth. (wiki:Yellow_Dick.cite_ref-Radwd46) He is among the first casualties of the killings at Winterfell, dying in 300 AC. (track_b: Died)
