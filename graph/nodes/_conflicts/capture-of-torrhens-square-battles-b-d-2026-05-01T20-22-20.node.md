## Identity

The capture of Torrhen's Square was the ironborn seizure of Torrhen's Square, seat of House Tallhart, during the War of the Five Kings in 299 AC. (wiki:Capture_of_Torrhen's_Square)

## Narrative Arc

After the initial Fight at Torrhen's Square — a feint to draw Winterfell's garrison away — Dagmer Cleftjaw retreated to the Stony Shore. Ser Rodrik Cassel, Lord Cley Cerwyn, and Leobald Tallhart then departed to retake Winterfell from Theon Greyjoy, but were betrayed and killed by Ramsay Snow at the battle outside the gates of Winterfell. Dagmer returned to find Torrhen's Square sparsely defended and captured the keep. He took Berena Hornwood and her children Brandon and Beren Tallhart, as well as Eddara Tallhart, the heir of Torrhen's Square, as captives. (wiki:Capture_of_Torrhen's_Square, acok-46, acok-56, acok-66, asos-76)
