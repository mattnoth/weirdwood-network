## Identity

Ser Herbert Bolling is a knight of House Bolling from the Stormlands. (wiki:Herbert_Bolling.cite_ref-Raffc4)

## Narrative Arc

Ser Herbert fought on the side of Stannis Baratheon during the War of the Five Kings. He was killed at the mouth of the Blackwater Rush during the Battle of the Blackwater in 299 AC. Ser Creighton Longbough claims to have slain Ser Herbert during the battle. (wiki:Herbert_Bolling.cite_ref-Raffc4)
