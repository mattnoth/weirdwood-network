## Identity

The battle on Whispering Sound was a naval engagement during the War of the Five Kings in 300 AC, fought between ironborn raiders and Reach forces near the Whispering Sound. (wiki:Battle_on_Whispering_Sound)

## Narrative Arc

Ironborn forces raided along the coast of the Reach, burning villages and fields along Whispering Sound. The Hightowers and their bannermen attempted to repel the ironborn raiders. Lord Baelor Hightower led the Reach fleet, while the ironborn were part of the broader Greyjoy campaign in the south. The outcome is uncertain — villages and fields were burnt, but the full result is not clearly established. (wiki:Battle_on_Whispering_Sound, affc-32, affc-36, affc-45)
