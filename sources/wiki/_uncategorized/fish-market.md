Layout[ ]

The fishmarket is located outside of the River Gate, with Fishmonger's Square found within the gate.

History[ ]

As King's Landing developed around the court of King Aegon I Targaryen, a fish market sprang up along the riverside of the Blackwater Rush.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Following the arrest of Eddard Stark, his daughter Arya sees a pair of gold cloaks walking side by side through the fish market. Half the stalls are empty.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

After Shagga takes a ferry south across the Blackwater Rush, Tyrion Lannister sees fishwives selling seafood. Tyrion, the acting Hand of the King, orders Bronn to have everything between the city walls and the water's edge burned in preparation for the Battle of the Blackwater.

During the battle, Ser Davos Seaworth sees that the Lannisters burned the riverfront and retreated to the Mud Gate. Tyrion sees the desolation of the fishmarket and the wharves.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

After the battle, Tyrion reports that the entire fish market is gone and that the River Gate should be replaced.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Queen Margaery Tyrell sometimes visits the fish market by the Mud Gate to see the day's catch.
