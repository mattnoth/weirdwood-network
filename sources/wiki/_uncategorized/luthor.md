## Full Text

Luthor is a Westerosi given name. It can refer to:

Luthor Largent, a knight and commander of the gold cloaks during the Dance of the Dragons

Luthor Tyrell, former Lord of Highgarden and father of Mace Tyrell

Luthor Tyrell, a knight and son of Moryn Tyrell

Luthor Tyrell, a squire and only son of Theodore Tyrell and Lia Serry
