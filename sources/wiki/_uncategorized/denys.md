## Full Text

Denys is a common name in Westeros. It can refer to:

Denys Arryn, known as the Darling of the Vale

Denys Darklyn, the last Darklyn lord of Duskendale

Denys Drumm, the son of Lord Dunstan Drumm

Denys Harte, a knight during the reign of King Aegon III

Denys Mallister, Commander of the Shadow Tower

Denys Marbrand, a knight of House Marbrand during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen

Denys Oakheart, the son and heir of Lord Oakheart during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen

Denys Redwyne, a squire of House Redwyne

Denys Snow, known as the Bastard of Barrowton

Denys Strong, a Serjeant for the Golden Company

Denys Swann, a member of House Swann during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen

Denys Woodwright, a knight in service of Lord Corlys Velaryon

Silver Denys, a man living on Dragonstone during the Dance of the Dragons
