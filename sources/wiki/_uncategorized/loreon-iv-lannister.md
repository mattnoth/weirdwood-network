## Full Text

Loreon IV Lannister, known as Loreon the Lackwit, was a King of the Rock of House Lannister of Casterly Rock.

Character[ ]

Maester Yandel counted Loreon IV amount the weak, cruel, and feeble Kings of the Rock. As he was known as "Loreon the Lackwit", it is possible that Loreon was a simpleton.

History[ ]

He was the grandfather of Loreon V Lannister.
