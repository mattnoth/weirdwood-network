Appearance[ ]

Leona is a plump pink woman with yellow hair, and her face is round, pink and fleshy. She follows the Faith of the Seven.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lady Leona is present along with her daughters, Wynafryd and Wylla, when Lord Davos Seaworth attempts to persuade Lord Wyman Manderly to support King Stannis Baratheon. She insults Davos out of fear for her husband's safety—Ser Wylis is a hostage of the Iron Throne—fearing that if Wyman declares his support for Stannis her beloved husband will suffer for it. When Wylla refuses to stop demanding House Manderly's loyalty to House Stark and that her grandfather Wyman should support Stannis, Leona drags her from the Merman's Court.
