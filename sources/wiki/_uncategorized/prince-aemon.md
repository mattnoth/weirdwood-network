## Summary

Prince Aemon is a war galley of the royal fleet in service at King's Landing. It is a slow ship and long in service. It is possibly named after a Prince Aemon of House Targaryen of which there were three: Aemon Targaryen, the son of Jaehaerys I Targaryen and a long-time Prince of Dragonstone; Aemon Targaryen, the son of King Viserys II Targaryen and the famed "Dragonknight" of Kingsguard; and Aemon Targaryen, the third son of King Maekar I Targaryen who served as a maester of the Citadel.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Prince Aemon is a part of King Joffrey I Baratheon's fleet at the Battle of the Blackwater. The ship and its captain survive the battle, and the captain is honored by the Iron Throne.
