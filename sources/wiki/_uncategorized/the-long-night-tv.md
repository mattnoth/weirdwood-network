## Summary

"The Long Night" is the third episode of the eighth season of HBO's fantasy television series Game of Thrones, and the 70th overall. The episode was written by David Benioff & D.B. Weiss and directed by Miguel Sapochnik. It first aired on April 28, 2019, and had 12.02 million viewers.
