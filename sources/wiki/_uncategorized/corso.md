## Full Text

Corso was the maester at Summerhall by the end of King Aegon V Targaryen's reign.

History[ ]

Corso sent a letter describing the Tragedy at Summerhall before his death, but a mishap led to ink blotting out much of the information:

the blood of the dragon gathered in one …

… seven eggs, to honor the seven gods, though the king's own septon had warned …

… pyromancers …

… wild fire …

… flames grew out of control … towering … burned so hot that …

… died, but for the valor of the Lord Comman …

—Corso's letter

Behind the Scenes[ ]

Corso only appears in latest prints of The World of Ice & Fire. In earlier prints, his role was played by Archmaester Gyldayn.
