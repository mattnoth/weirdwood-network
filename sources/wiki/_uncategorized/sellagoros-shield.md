## Full Text

The Titan of Braavos, as depicted by Paolo Puggioni in The World of Ice & Fire

Sellagoro's Shield is one of the outlying islands which protect the lagoon of Braavos. The island is located west of the Drowned Town and the Ragman's Harbor, and the northeastern tip of Sellagoro's Shield forms the left leg of the Titan of Braavos.

The stony ridges and steep slopes of Sellagoro's Shield are covered with soldier pines and black spruce. The Braavosi do not cut down the trees, instead using them as windbreaks.
