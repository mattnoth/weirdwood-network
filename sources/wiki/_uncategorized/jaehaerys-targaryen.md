## Full Text

Jaehaerys Targaryen is the name of several members of House Targaryen. These include:

King Jaehaerys I Targaryen, fourth Targaryen King on Iron Throne, third son of King Aenys Targaryen and Queen Alyssa Velaryon.

Prince Jaehaerys Targaryen, eldest son of King Aegon II Targaryen and Queen Helaena Targaryen, who died during the Dance of the Dragons.

King Jaehaerys II Targaryen, sixteenth Targaryen King on Iron Throne, second son of King Aegon V Targaryen and Queen Betha Blackwood.

Prince Jaehaerys Targaryen, fourth son of King Aerys II Targaryen and Queen Rhaella Targaryen, who died young.

Feminine equivalent[ ]

Jaehaera Targaryen, only daughter of King Aegon II Targaryen and Queen Helaena Targaryen, first queen of King Aegon III.
