The stormlands and the location of Evenfall Hall

Evenfall Hall, or simply Evenfall, is the seat of House Tarth in the stormlands. It is located on the western coast of the island of Tarth along Shipbreaker Bay.

History[ ]

King Aegon I Targaryen visited Evenfall Hall during his royal progresses.

Brienne of Tarth was trained by Ser Goodwin, master-at-arms of the castle.
