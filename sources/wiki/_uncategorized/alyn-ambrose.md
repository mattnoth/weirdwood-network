Appearance and Character[ ]

Alyn is gangly. He believes Elinor Tyrell's favor makes him fearless in battle.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

As a squire, Alyn wore Elinor Tyrell's favor in the Battle of the Blackwater. Shouting Elinor's name as his battle cry, he killed a Myrish archer and a Mullendore man-at-arms.

Elinor and Megga Tyrell tell Sansa Stark about Alyn's feats of arms at the Blackwater. Elinor and Alyn are to marry as soon as he is knighted.

At the wedding feast for Sansa and Tyrion Lannister, Alyn dances with his betrothed. Sansa also dances with the squire, but after King Joffrey I Baratheon has threatened her, and Sansa fears Alyn must think her a clumsy dancer.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Alyn accompanies Elinor when she goes for a ride with her cousin, Queen Margaery Tyrell.
