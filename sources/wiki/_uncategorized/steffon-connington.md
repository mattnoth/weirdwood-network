## Full Text

Steffon Connington was a knight from House Connington and the second husband of the Dowager Lady Elenda Baratheon of Storm's End.

Appearance and Character[ ]

Steffon was handsome and a fierce warrior.

History[ ]

Ser Steffon was the second son of Lord Connington of Griffin's Roost.

With Lord Borros Baratheon dead after the Dance of the Dragons and his son, Lord Royce Baratheon a mere infant, attacks from Dornish raiders in the Marches became increasingly common. Lady Elenda, regent of Storm's End, felt she needed a strong man's hand to keep the peace, and thus married Steffon, who was twenty years her junior.

In 133 AC he was slain by Wyland Wyl in the Dornish Marches while pursuing raiders across the marches, and Elenda was widowed once more.
