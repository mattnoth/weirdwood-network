A Storm of Swords[ ]

At some point, Donnel is released from captivity. One of Robb Stark's sworn swords, Donnel attends the wedding of Lord Edmure Tully to Lady Roslin Frey at the Twins. He is among those nobles killed during the Red Wedding, getting hit with crossbow bolts.
