## Summary

Khiara the Great was a God-Empress of Leng and the founder of the current dynasty that rules the island. She was of pure Lengii descent, but to please her subjects she took two husbands, one Lengii and one YiTish. This custom was followed by her daughters and their daughters in turn. By tradition the Lengii consort commands the imperial armies whereas the YiTish one commands the fleets.
