Jorgen Velaryon was one of the children of Lord Daemon Velaryon, the Lord of the Tides and the head of House Velaryon during the reign of King Maegor I Targaryen and King Jaehaerys.

History[ ]

When King Jaehaerys I Targaryen and Queen Alysanne Targaryen flew their dragons to Driftmark and toured the shipyards, they were escorted by Jorgen and his brothers Corwyn and Victor.

Family[ ]

The second son of Lord Daemon Velaryon died of the Shivers in 59 AC. This son could be Jorgen.
