## Full Text

Theo is a male given name in Westeros.

Characters named Theo include:

Theo Bolling, a landed knight during the reign of King Maegor I Targaryen.

Theo Charlton, an ally riverlord of King Harwyn Hoare.

Theo Frey, a knight from House Frey.

Theo Teague, a King of the Trident.

Theo Tyrell, a Lord of Highgarden.

Theo Wull, a companion of Eddard Stark at the Tower of Joy.

Characters named a variant of Theo include:

Theobald, an archmaester of the Citadel.

Theodan Wells, the Commander of the Warrior's Sons.

Theodore Tyrell, a knight of a junior branch of House Tyrell.

Theomar Smallwood, the Lord of Acorn Hall.

Theomore Harlaw, a Lord of Harlaw.

Theomore Lannister, the maester at New Castle.

Theomore Manderly, a Lord of White Harbor.

Theon Greyjoy, the heir of Pyke.

Theon III Greyjoy, a High King of the Iron Islands.

Theon Stark, a King in the North.
