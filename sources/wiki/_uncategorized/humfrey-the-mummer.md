## Full Text

Humfrey the Mummer was a hedge knight during the reign of King Aegon I Targaryen. 

Character[ ]

Humfrey is remembered for being quick, strong, observant, and famed for his prowess with sword and shield. He was devoted to his king.

History[ ]

Humfrey was chosen by Queen Visenya Targaryen to be one of the first sworn brothers of the Kingsguard when the order was founded in 10 AC by King Aegon I Targaryen.
