Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Arya Stark boards the Titan's Daughter in Saltpans intending to go to Braavos.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

The Titan's Daughter brings Arya to Braavos. Many of the crew, including Captain Ternesio Terys and his son Yorko, ask her the favor of remembering their names. The Titan's Daughter is spotted casting off at Maidenpool by Brienne of Tarth.
