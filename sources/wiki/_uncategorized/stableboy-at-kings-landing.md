Appearance[ ]

The stableboy wears a filthy white undertunic beneath a soiled jerkin.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

During the arrest of Eddard Stark at the Red Keep, Syrio Forel defends Arya Stark from Ser Meryn Trant and allows the girl to escape the Tower of the Hand. The stableboy encounters Arya when she is in the stables. She asks for his help saddling a horse, but the stableboy instead tries to apprehend her in hopes of reward from Queen Cersei Lannister. When he grabs her arm, Arya stabs the stableboy with her sword, Needle, killing the youth.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Arya worries what Yoren would think if he knew that she had killed the stableboy.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

When Arya tells Harwin about what has happened to her, she omits the death of the stableboy. Arya worries about her brother Robb, King in the North, finding out about the stableboy. Arya thinks of the stableboy while speaking with Lord Edric Dayne.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

While at the House of Black and White in Braavos, Arya has a dream in which she sees the hanging faces of those she has slain, including the stableboy's.
