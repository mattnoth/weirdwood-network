Description[ ]

Sandstone may come in red or yellow.

Culture[ ]

The seat of House Qorgyle in Dorne is called Sandstone.

The outer walls, the inner keep and the sept of Riverrun are built with red sandstone.

The merlons of the seat of House Hollard were made of yellow sandstone.

The outer wall of the triple walls of Qarth is made of red sandstone and richly decorated with animals.

The walls of Selhorys are made of sandstone.

Meereen and Lhazar are separated by a range of rounded sandstone mountains that can be crossed via the Khyzai Pass.

There is a slanting sandstone ridge three leagues south of Yunkai.

Sunspear is made of sandstone.

Great sandstone cliffs can be found in the Red Mountains separating the stormlands and Dorne.
