## Summary

Yunkish is a form of bastard Valyrian spoken in the city of Yunkai. Like the mongrel Ghiscari of the other Slaver Cities, the tongue of Yunkai is a form of High Valyrian influenced by Old Ghiscari. The dialect of Yunkai is close enough to that of Astapor to be mutually intelligible.
