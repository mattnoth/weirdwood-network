Background[ ]

The younger sister of Harwyn Hoare, King of the Isles and the Rivers, had married into House Volmark. After the death of Harwyn's grandson, King Harren Hoare, in the burning of Harrenhal during Aegon's Conquest, Qhorin Volmark claimed to be the heir of the black line of House Hoare. He was the first of many rival claimants to the title King of the Iron Islands. However, he was defeated during the invasion of the Iron Islands and beheaded by Aegon I Targaryen instead. The ironborn then elected Vickon Greyjoy, the Lord Reaper of Pyke, as the Lord of the Iron Islands

The Conspiracy[ ]

Following the death of Vickon Greyjoy in 33 AC, a conspiracy sought to restore the "black line" of House Hoare by crowning the son of the late Qhorin Volmark and overthrow House Greyjoy. However, Vickon's successor, Goren Greyjoy, quickly put down the conspiracy.

Aftermath[ ]

Factions seeking independence from the Iron Throne remained in the Iron Islands, four years later, a man claiming to be the priestking Lodos returned from the sea led a revolt against the rule of House Targaryen.
