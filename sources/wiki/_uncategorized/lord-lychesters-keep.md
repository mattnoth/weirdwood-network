Layout[ ]

The keep is small and square, but has fallen into disrepair. There is a little stone bridge behind the keep. 

History[ ]

During the Faith Militant uprising, Visenya Targaryen used Vhagar to set aflame in a single night the seats of Houses Lychester, Blanetree, Deddings, Terrick, and Wayn.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

The brotherhood without banners stop at the keep. The outlaws speak with Maester Roone, as the aged Lord Lymond Lychester's wits have wandered.
