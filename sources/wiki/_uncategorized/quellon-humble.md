Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Quellon allies himself with Euron Greyjoy at the kingsmoot. He tells Victarion Greyjoy about the warlocks and priests that Euron keeps on his ship as slaves.

Quellon is present at the feast held by Euron and his men in Lord Humfrey Hewett's castle after the taking of the Shields. At the feast, he wears one of Lord Hewett's tapestries, which he had torn down, while eating. Quellon is one of the men who shout down King Euron's plans to sail to Slaver's Bay.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Quellon is listed among the crew of the Iron Victory, the ship of Victarion Greyjoy.
