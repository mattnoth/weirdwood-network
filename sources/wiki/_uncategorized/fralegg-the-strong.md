Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

During the kingsmoot, Fralegg offers Victarion Greyjoy his support. He is listed as one of Victarion's supporters at the kingsmoot.

At the feast celebrating the taking of the Shields, Fralegg is one of those who shout down King Euron Greyjoy's plans to sail to Slaver's Bay, proposing to raid Highgarden instead.
