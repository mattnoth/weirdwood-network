Appearance[ ]

Nurse has a long narrow face, and a chin beard bound with gold wire. His red and black Ghiscari hair is shaped into the form of a pair of taloned hands.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When Tyrion Lannister, Penny, and Jorah Mormont are purchased by Yezzan for his grotesquerie, Nurse is put in charge of his master's new property, instructing them on the rules, disciplining them when necessary, and informing them they should treat Yezzan as if he were their own father.

Nurse eventually succumbs to the pale mare. However, Tyrion is implied to have been the actual culprit of Nurse's death by poisoning the overseer. When Nurse falls ill, it falls to Tyrion to act as his caretaker. Tyrion prepares a soup of dogtail "with slivers of mushroom in the broth," - seemingly the poisonous mushrooms Tyrion had taken from the manse of Illyrio Mopatis. Nurse's last word is "No," while Tyrion's last words to Nurse are "A Lannister always pays his debts."
