A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Malegorn is one of the queen's men in the service of Stannis Baratheon. When Stannis rides to Castle Black and then Deepwood Motte, Malegorn stays with Queen Selyse at Eastwatch-by-the-Sea as one of her protectors. When Selyse leaves Eastwatch to go to the Nightfort, he accompanies her to Castle Black. Malegorn and Ser Brus Buckler tell the brothers of the Night's Watch about events at Eastwatch.

During the wedding of Sigorn and Alys Karstark, Jon Snow sees Malegorn cupping the arse of the woman next to him. Afterward, Jon, the Lord Commander of the Night's Watch, orders his steward Satin to escort Selyse to the feast. Malegorn interrupts and escorts the queen instead, as he dislikes Satin, a former prostitute.

Malegorn thinks that Jon's plan for a ranging to Hardhome is foolish. Selyse arranges for Malegorn to wed the youngest daughter of Gerrick Kingsblood.
