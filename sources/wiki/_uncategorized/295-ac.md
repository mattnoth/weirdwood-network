Year 295 After the Conquest (295 AC) is a year in the Westerosi calendar, the 295th after Aegon's crowning in Oldtown that maesters and educated people use as a reference to date the years.

Events[ ]

The thirteen-year-old Pate started his training at the Citadel.

Lord Tywin Lannister organized a tourney at Casterly Rock.

King Robert I Baratheon impregnated a servant of Casterly Rock with twins.

Births[ ]
