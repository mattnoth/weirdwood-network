Daemon Blackfyre was a member of House Blackfyre. He was a cousin of Maelys I Blackfyre and a captain-general of the Golden Company.

History[ ]

Daemon fought with his cousin, Maelys, over the command of the Golden Company. Maelys killed Daemon's destrier with a single punch, and then twisted Daemon's head until he tore it from his shoulders.

Family[ ]

Daemon's descent from his namesake, the first Daemon Blackfyre, is currently unknown.
