## Full Text

Marahai

Location of Marahai in the known world

Marahai is a crescent-shaped island in the central Jade Sea, off the southern coast of Essos. West of Marahai are Great Moraq, the Isle of Whips, and the Isle of Elephants. Additionally, Leng and the Manticore Isles are east of Marahai. The city of Yin in Yi Ti is to the north across the Jade Sea.

Marahai is called a paradise isle. Within its curvature are two small volcanic isles which often emit smoke and flame.
