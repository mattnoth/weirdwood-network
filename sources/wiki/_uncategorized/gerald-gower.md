Appearance[ ]

Gerald is broad and bluff with blond hair.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

After the Battle of the Blackwater, where Ser Gerald fought well, Gerald is among those who question the new faith that Stannis Baratheon has joined. Davos Seaworth seeks Gerald out and befriends him. Gerald is among Lord Davos's men when he ships Edric Storm out of Dragonstone.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Gerald remains a guard and protector of Edric.
