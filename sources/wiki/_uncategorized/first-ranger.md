History[ ]

A Stane of Skagos once briefly served as First Ranger.

Benton Glover was First Ranger when Queen Alysanne Targaryen visited the Wall in 58 AC.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

With First Ranger Benjen Stark missing during a ranging beyond the Wall, Ser Jaremy Rykker commands the rangers in Benjen's absence. However, Jaremy is slain by a wight.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Thoren Smallwood declares he is First Ranger, but Lord Commander Jeor Mormont insists that Benjen will remain First Ranger until his fate is confirmed.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Thoren is killed during the fight at the Fist. Ronnel Harclay commands the rangers after the death of Thoren's death.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Black Jack Bulwer is officially appointed as First Ranger, but he is slain by the Weeper while ranging north of the Wall.
