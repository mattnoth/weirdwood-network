A Feast for Crows[ ]

Iron Wind is among the ships that make the voyage to the kingsmoot on Old Wyk. After the taking of the Shields, Victarion Greyjoy keeps Iron Wind, Lord Dagon, Grief, and Maiden's Bane as part of his rearguard, commanding from his own ship, Iron Victory.
