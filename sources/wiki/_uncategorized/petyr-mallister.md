Petyr Mallister was a Lord of Seagard and a head of House Mallister.

History[ ]

Petyr claimed the title of river king or King of the Trident in the generations after the riverlands were conquered by the Storm King Arlan III Durrandon. Lord Petyr had a brief, inglorious reign before being put down.
