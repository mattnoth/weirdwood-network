Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Nyessos, like the other two triarchs, is up for reelection. Griff says that Illyrio Mopatis has paid Nyessos enough to own him eight times over. He owes much of his wealth to the slave trade. It is said in Volantis that Nyessos has been bought by Yunkai and plans to have the Volantene fleet sail against Daenerys Targaryen of Meereen upon reelection.
