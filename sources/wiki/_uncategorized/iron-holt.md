Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

After Euron Greyjoy drowns Lord Sawane Botley, Lord Waldon Wynch is the first man to recognize Euron as King of the Isles and the North. Euron rewards House Wynch by giving half of House Botley's lands to Iron Holt.
