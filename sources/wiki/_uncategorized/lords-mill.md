History[ ]

Lord's Mill was burned by the dragon Vhagar during the Dance of the Dragons.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Jonos Bracken wants Lord's Mill for subduing Lord Tytos Blackwood. Tytos surrenders Lord's Mill as part of the price to submit to the Iron Throne.
