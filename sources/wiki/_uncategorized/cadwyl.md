Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Cadwyl participates in the harrying of the Stony Shore led by Theon Greyjoy. He is later one of the warriors chosen by Theon to capture Winterfell. After Theon displayed the heads of the boys above the gate and Farlen attacks him, Cadwyl and Urzen beat the kennelmaster senseless with the butts of their spears.

When northmen led by Ser Rodrik Cassel come to retake the castle, Cadwyl decides against remaining with Theon.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Cadwyl is a crewmember of Sea Bitch.
