## Full Text

Lord Tarbeck was the Lord of Tarbeck Hall and the head of House Tarbeck during the reign of King Maegor I Targaryen.

History[ ]

Lord Tarbeck was born a few months after the death of his father, Lord Alyn Tarbeck, who died during the Battle Beneath the Gods Eye in 43 AC.

In 47 AC, King Maegor I Targaryen summoned his mother, Lady Jeyne Westerling, and him to court, where he married the widow as one of his Black Brides, using the young Lord Tarbeck as a hostage to prevent any defiance. Lord Tarbeck was later confirmed as Alyn's heir and Lord of Tarbeck Hall. He was sent to Casterly Rock as a ward to Lord Lyman Lannister.

A Lord Tarbeck died of the Shivers in 59 AC, it is unknown if it was him or another lord.
