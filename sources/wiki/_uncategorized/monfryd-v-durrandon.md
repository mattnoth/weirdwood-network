## Full Text

Monfryd V Durrandon was a Storm King of House Durrandon during the Andal invasion and the heir and successor of King Qarlton III Durrandon.

History[ ]

Like his father and grandfather, Monfryd V reigned in times of war against the Andals. He defeated the Holy Brotherhood of the Andals, an alliance of seven petty kings and warlords, at the Battle of Bronzegate, though he lost his life in the process.
