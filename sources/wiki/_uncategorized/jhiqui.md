Jhiqui is a Dothraki handmaid of Daenerys Targaryen.

In the television adaptation Game of Thrones, Jhiqui is portrayed by Sarita Piotrowski.

Appearance[ ]

See also: Images of Jhiqui

Jhiqui has skin the color of copper, with black hair and almond-shaped eyes. She has wide hips and heavy breasts, and is bigger boned than Irri.

She is of an age with Daenerys Targaryen and Irri.

History[ ]

Jhiqui was born to a khal of a khalasar. It was destroyed by Khal Drogo, and she was enslaved.

Recent Events[ ]

  

Irri and Jhiqui, by Rae Lavergne ©

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Jhiqui, Doreah, and Irri are given to Daenerys Targaryen as wedding gifts to serve as her handmaids. She is given to Daenerys to teach her the Dothraki tongue. 

Jhiqui remains with Daenerys after the death of Khal Drogo.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

After struggling through the red waste, Irri and Jhiqui attempt to convince Daenerys not to stay in Vaes Tolorro, believing it haunted by ghosts.

After Daenerys destroys the House of the Undying, Jhiqui fastens a silver bell in her braid to mark the victory.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Jhiqui and Irri are seasick during the voyage to Astapor. Jhiqui is present when Daenerys purchases the Unsullied. Jhiqui and Irri free Rhaegal and Viserion to attack the Astapori slavers when Daenerys has the Unsullied attack their former masters. She later praises Strong Belwas in defeating Oznak zo Pahl and remarks she would braid his hair the Dothraki way if he had any.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Jhiqui and Irri both develop a crush on Rakharo, who has grown more tall and muscular. They argue over which of them he would prefer.

Jhiqui later accompanies Daenerys to Daznak's Pit as part of the royal procession. She and Irri both depart Meereen as part of the search parties dispatched across the Skahazadhan to locate Daenerys after she disappears on Drogon's back.
