Culture[ ]

  

Door of the House of Black and White, by Donato Giancola ©

The Mountains of the Moon are a range of mountains which surround the Vale of Arryn, and the sigil of House Arryn is the moon-and-falcon. The Moon Brothers are one of the Vale mountain clans. The Gates of the Moon is a castle at the foot of the Giant's Lance. The Moon Door at the Eyrie is decorated with a crescent moon. Lord Robert Arryn's silver brooch is a crescent moon. The cloak of Ser Hugh of the Vale is trimmed with crescent moons.

The sigil of House Wynch depicts a blood moon, while House Egen and House Fell use a crescent moon. The arms of House Pryor include a black moon, and the Harclays' sigil depicts three phases of the moon. House Tarth uses quartered sun-and-moon heraldry.

Windows on the dome of the Temple of the Moonsingers in Braavos depict the phases of the moon. The Moon Pool is a pool near the Sealord's Palace in the city. The doors of the House of Black and White depict the face of the moon in ebony and weirwood.

Moonstone is a gemstone named after the moon. The Moonmaid is a constellation. The Moon-Pale Maiden and Moon Mother are deities worshipped in Essos.

Moon Boy is the fool of King Robert I Baratheon. The Moonshadow is a courtesan in Braavos who wears only white and silver.

Menstruation can be called moon blood or moon's blood. Moon tea can be used to prevent pregnancies. A saying in the Seven Kingdoms is to "never drink with Dornishmen when the moon is full." In the westerlands, a black new moon is known as a traitor's moon. Wolves often howl at the moon.

History[ ]

  

House Yronwood soldiers make their way during a full moon, by Tomasz Jedruszek © Fantasy Flight Games

Qartheen believe that dragons were hatched from a second moon that came too close to the sun and cracked, while Dothraki believe that the moon is a goddess, wife to the sun.

According to the myth of Azor Ahai, the face of the moon cracked due to Nissa Nissa's cry of anguish and ecstasy when Azor Ahai drove Lightbringer into her chest to temper the blade.

Red priests believe R'hllor made the sun, moon, and stars to light the way.

Walton Stark was known as the Moon King.

Stories say that when Queen Visenya Targaryen, upset that the title Prince of Dragonstone had been removed from her son Maegor, flew away from King's Landing on Vhagar, the moon turned red as blood when her dragon passed before it.

Autumn Moon was a ship captained by Ser Norman Hightower.

Moondancer was a dragon ridden by Lady Baela Targaryen.

A story told to misbehaving children said that on moonless nights bats would fly from Harrenhal and take the bad children to Danelle Lothston.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

  

Twilight Market, by German Nobile © Fantasy Flight Games

When Ser Waymar Royce faces one of the Others, his adversary's blade of translucent crystal appears alive with moonlight.

Khal Drogo refers to his new bride, Daenerys Targaryen, as the moon of his life.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

According to a semi-canon source, House Royce of the Gates of the Moon has a white crescent moon in their sigil.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser Tristan Rivers thinks that Illyrio Mopatis's plan changes every time the moon turns.
