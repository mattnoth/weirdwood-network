Erich Durrandon, also known as Erich the Sailmaker, was a Storm King of House Durrandon.

History[ ]

Erich was the first to claim Estermont and the lesser isles farther south for the Kingdom of the Storm. Scholars speculate he was most likely Erich III. He was the grandson of King Durran the Fair.
