Appearance and Character[ ]

See also: Images of Murenmure

Murenmure is a slim man who wears his maester's chain about his neck and has fine grey robes.

Murenmure is knowledgeable about the succession laws of Westeros.

Aeron Greyjoy, a priest of the Drowned God, dislikes maesters and thinks of Murenmure as a thrall.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Aeron Greyjoy travels to Hammerhorn to speak about the death of his brother Balon, King of the Isles and the North, with Gorold Goodbrother, Lord of Hammerhorn. The Damphair demands that Murenmure not be present for their conversation, but Gorold insists that his maester remain. When Aeron decides to leave instead, Murenmure informs him that Euron Greyjoy has returned to the Iron Islands and claimed the Seastone Chair. Murenmure debates the rules of succession with Aeron, with the maester stating that Theon and Asha Greyjoy would be Balon's heirs as his children and Aeron considering Balon's other brothers Euron and Victarion instead.
