The Winds of Winter[ ]

Nate is one of Princess Arianne Martell's six companions on her journey to meet Jon Connington and Aegon Targaryen in the Stormlands. He tends the seven ravens from the Water Gardens, which Arianne uses to send messages back to her father Prince Doran Martell. Elia Sand takes an interest in Feathers, kissing him at Mistwood, and is reprimanded for this by Arianne.
