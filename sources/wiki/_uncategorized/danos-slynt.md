Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

When Janos Slynt is named Lord of Harrenhal, two of his three sons accompany him before the Iron Throne.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

When Tyrion Lannister, acting Hand of the King, sends Janos to the Wall, he promises that Danos and his elder brother Jothos will be fostered out as squires.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Danos is now a page. Lord Varys warns Tyrion that Danos and his brothers would gladly inform on Tyrion to avenge their father.
