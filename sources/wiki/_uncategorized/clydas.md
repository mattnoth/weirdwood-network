Appearance[ ]

Short and chinless, Clydas has a few white hairs still clinging to his scalp. The steward has dim pink eyes and is hunched with rounded shoulders.

Jon considers Clydas to be ugly and estimates him to be about sixty years old. 

History[ ]

For years Clydas and Chett have helped tend to Maester Aemon's needs and helped with the ravens in the rookery at Aemon's tower at Castle Black.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Because Maester Aemon is blind, the half-blind Clydas reads messages to him.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Clydas aids Aemon when the maester treats the wounds of Jon Snow.

Aemon, Clydas, and most of the women from Mole's Town take the winch cage to the top of the Wall for safety during the attack on Castle Black by Thenns. A midwife from Moles Town aids Aemon and Clydas as they deal with those wounded in the fighting. Owen the Oaf and Clydas distribute cups of onion broth to archers during the battle beneath the Wall. Clydas guides Jon with a lantern when they investigate the tunnel through the Wall, and they find the bodies of Donal Noye and Mag the Mighty.

Clydas gives flagons of goats' milk and dreamwine to the wounded after the battle, including to black brothers, king's men, and queen's men. He helps count during the choosing of a new Lord Commander of the Night's Watch.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

When Aemon is sent away by Jon, the new Lord Commander, Clydas takes over the duties of tending to the ravens and the wounded. Before leaving, Aemon has Clydas mark passages in the Jade Compendium for Jon.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Clydas mulls wine and offers a cup to Jon, who tells the steward of Stannis Baratheon's sword, Lightbringer. Clydas informs Jon of a letter sent by Ramsay Bolton after the siege of Moat Cailin, and he also informs Jon of a letter from Stannis after the fight by Deepwood Motte.

Clydas treats Alys Karstark when she flees to Castle Black. After Alys tells her tale, Clydas sends a message to Deepwood hoping to warn Stannis of Arnolf Karstark's treachery. Clydas brings Jon letters from Cotter Pyke. Jon tasks Clydas with tending to any of Tormund's people who are sick.

Clydas is frightened by a letter to Jon entitled "Bastard" and sealed with pink wax. He brings it to Jon unopened.
