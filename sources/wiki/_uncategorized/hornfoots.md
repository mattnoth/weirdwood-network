Culture[ ]

Hornfoots wear nothing on their feet, which turns the soles of their feet hard and black. Hornfoot men wear fur and wield long spears.

History[ ]

The Hornfoot folk warred with the Nightrunners before Mance Rayder, King-Beyond-the-Wall, negotiated a peace between them.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

The Hornfoot men join with Mance Rayder for his attack on the Wall.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Two clan chiefs of the Hornfoots kneel to Stannis Baratheon at Castle Black. Jon Snow, Lord Commander of the Night's Watch, meets a Hornfoot with maimed feet.
