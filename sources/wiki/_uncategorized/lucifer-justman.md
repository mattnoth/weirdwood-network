## Full Text

Lucifer Justman, known as Lucifer the Liar, was a presumed member of House Justman living four centuries prior to Aegon's Conquest.

History[ ]

Lucifer claimed the title of river king or King of the Trident in the generations after the riverlands were conquered by the Storm King Arlan III Durrandon. Lucifer had a brief, inglorious reign before being put down by the power of Storm's End. His connection with the extinct Justman dynasty is unconfirmed.
