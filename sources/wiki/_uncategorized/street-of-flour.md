Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Arya Stark catches a pigeon on the Street of Flour and remarks the sweet perfume coming from the bakeries.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

When Tyrion Lannister arrives in King's Landing, he sees guards posted at every shop door on the Street of Flour. Tyrion reflects on the food shortage in the capital, due to House Tyrell cutting off the commerce coming from the Roseroad, and realizes it must be grave if the bakers found sellswords cheaper than bread.
