## Summary

Breakstone Hill is the seat of House Flint of Breakstone Hill, who ruled as First Men kings until being defeated by the Kings of Winter, House Stark of Winterfell. If the Breakstone Flints are the same as House Flint of the mountains, Breakstone is probably located in the northern mountains.
