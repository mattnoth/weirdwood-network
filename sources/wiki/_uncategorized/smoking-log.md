Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

When Bran Stark, Robb Stark, and Theon Greyjoy pass through the winter town on Bran's first ride since his fall, they pass by the Smoking Log. They encounter Kyra and another girl standing by the sign outide the inn.

A Bolton man stabs a Cerwyn man in the Smoking Log after Robb Stark calls his banners.
