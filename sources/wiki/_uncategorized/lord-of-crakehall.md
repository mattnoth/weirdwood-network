History[ ]

Lord Sumner Crakehall and his squires, Jaime Lannister and Merrett Frey, participated in the campaign against the outlaws of the Kingswood Brotherhood. Sumner ransomed Merrett back after he was captured by Wenda the White Fawn and later kindly sent him back to the Twins.

Lord Roland Crakehall led the Crakehall men during the Sack of King's Landing at the end of Robert's Rebellion.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

When war breaks out, Lord Roland Crakehall supports King Joffrey I Baratheon.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord Roland is rumored to have died at the Battle of Oxcross, but he is present at Joffrey's first audience after the Battle of the Blackwater.
