Geography[ ]

  

Daenerys Targaryen and Drogon at Dragonstone, by Wyatt Abernathy ©

The hill appears as a stony island within the green grassland of the Dothraki plains. Scrub grass and thorny bushes cover its lower slopes, while a jagged tangle of bare rock thrusts into the sky. There is a shallow cave amidst the broken boulders, razor-sharp ridges, and needle spires. There are fish in a nearby spring-fed pool.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After having fled Meereen atop the dragon Drogon, Daenerys Targaryen ends up in the Dothraki sea. She stays in the dragon's lair, a cave in a hill that she names Dragonstone. She lives on local edible vegetation, and on meat from kills that Drogon brings back, and catches one fish from the pool.

Daenerys eventually decides to return to Meereen, but is unable to get Drogon to follow her commands, leading to her traveling on foot. She follows a stream that she thinks will flow into the Skahazadhan.
