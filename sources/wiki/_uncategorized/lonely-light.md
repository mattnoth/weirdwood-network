Geography[ ]

  

The Lonely Light, by Martina Pilcerova © Fantasy Flight Games

While most of the Iron Islands lie in Ironman's Bay, there is a smaller group of thirteen islands clustered around the Lonely Light lying eight days sail northwest of Great Wyk. It lies well into the Sunset Sea and is probably the most westerly location in the known world aside from the isles Aegon, Rhaenys, and Visenya.

The Lonely Light is surrounded by rookeries of seals and sea lions and ocean. Some say that House Farwynd of the Lonely Light and their smallfolk sleep with seals to birth half-human children, and others claim they are skinchangers who take the forms of sea lions, walruses, and spotted whales.

History[ ]

A thousand years after the time of King Brandon the Burner, ironborn sailing out of Great Wyk were blown off course to a cluster of islands eight days sail from the closest shore. Their captain built a tower and a beacon there, took the name "Farwynd", and declared the Lonely Light his seat.
