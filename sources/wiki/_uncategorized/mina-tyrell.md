## Summary

Mina Tyrell is a noblewoman of House Tyrell, the eldest daughter of Lord Luthor Tyrell and Lady Olenna Redwyne. She has two siblings: Mace Tyrell, the current Lord of Highgarden, and a younger sister, Janna Tyrell.
