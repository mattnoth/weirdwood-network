## Summary

Princess Rhaenys is a war galley of the royal fleet in service to Stannis Baratheon. It has one hundred oars. It is named after either Princess Rhaenys Targaryen, the daughter of Prince Rhaegar Targaryen, or Princess Rhaenys Targaryen, the daughter of Prince Aemon Targaryen.
