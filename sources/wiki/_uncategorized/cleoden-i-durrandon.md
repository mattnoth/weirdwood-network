## Full Text

Cleoden I Durrandon was a Storm King of the stormlands from House Durrandon. He ruled a generation after Durran XXI Durrandon.

History[ ]

During the coming of the Andals to the stormlands, he made an unlikely alliance with three Dornish kings and won a major victory over Drox the Corpse-Maker on the river Slayne near Stonehelm.
