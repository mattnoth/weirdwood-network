## Full Text

Viserys is a Valyrian given name.

It is borne by:

Viserys Plumm, a lord of House Plumm

Viserys I Targaryen, fifth king of the Seven Kingdoms

Viserys II Targaryen, tenth king of the Seven Kingdoms

Viserys Targaryen, second son of King Aenys I Targaryen

Viserys Targaryen, the exiled second son of King Aerys II Targaryen, styling himself as Viserys, the third of his name, called the Beggar King
