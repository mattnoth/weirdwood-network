History[ ]

According to legend, a land bridge called the Arm of Dorne once connected Westeros and Essos. The Broken Arm and the Stepstones were created when the Arm of Dorne was shattered by the Breaking, the usage of the hammer of the waters by the children of the forest.

During the coming of the Andals, Morgan Martell established House Martell between the Broken Arm and the Greenblood.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Stannis Baratheon sends Dale Seaworth past the Broken Arm to spread word of his claim on the Iron Throne to the Dornishmen.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Obara Sand claims the people of the Broken Arm are outraged at the death of her father, Prince Oberyn Martell.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Paxter Redwyne leads the Redwyne fleet past the Broken Arm toward the Reach.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Krakens have been sighted attacking crippled galleys off the Broken Arm.
