Character and Appearance[ ]

Loyal to the Night's Watch, Mallador has a longsword and helm and rides a horse.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser Mallador is part of the great ranging that leaves Castle Black in search of the wildlings. He commands the baggage train in the column.

When Qhorin Halfhand is delayed in reaching the Fist of the First Men, Mallador suggests that the Watch head toward the Shadow Tower.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Mallador agrees with Ser Ottyn Wythers and counsels a retreat to the Wall after the discovery of the massive wildling force descending on the Fist of the First Men. However, Thoren Smallwood convinces him to change his mind and he then sides with Thoren for a swift attack.

As the fight at the Fist begins, Samwell Tarly sees Mallador ride past on his horse with a snow-speckled helm. Later in the battle, Sam thinks that Mallador might be the officer who gives a command to use spears. Mallador presumably dies at the Fist, the ensuing retreat, or the mutiny at Craster's Keep, since he is not mentioned among the few survivors who return to Castle Black.
