## Full Text

Pennyroyal is a flowering herb which is used for its medical properties. It is used as an abortifacient.

History[ ]

Grand Maester Orwyle, disguised as "Old Wyl", mixed potions of tansy and pennyroyal for the prostitutes from Mother's, a brothel in King's Landing, to abort unwanted children.

When he discovered his youngest daughter Lysa was pregnant, Lord Hoster Tully gave her moon tea, with tansy, mint, wormwood, a spoon of honey and a drop of pennyroyal, to abort her child.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

Pennyroyal is likely a reference to Mentha pulegium, commonly called (European) pennyroyal, which is used as an insecticide, pest repellent, an emmenagogue, and an abortifacient. Historically, real-life European pennyroyal has also been used as a mint flavoring in herbal teas and foods. Tea made from pennyroyal were used for cold relief, fevers, coughs, indigestion, liver problems, kidney problems, and headaches.
