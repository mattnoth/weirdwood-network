Culture[ ]

Elms can be found in the godswoods of the Red Keep, Riverrun, White Harbor, and Winterfell. Elms line a street in Barrowton which leads to Barrow Hall.

Ser Duncan the Tall included an elm and a shooting star in his sigil.

Crossed Elms is a village near the Gods Eye in the riverlands.

History[ ]

  

Egg, Dunk, and Sweetfoot, by Ted Nasmith ©

Ser Duncan the Tall set up his camp by a tall, leafy elm during the tourney at Ashford Meadow.

Lord Brynden Rivers's pavilion was raised by a spreading elm tree near Whitewalls during his suppression of the Second Blackfyre Rebellion. 

When he was a child, Edmure Tully broke his arm in by falling from an elm in the godswood of Riverrun.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lord Tywin Lannister takes his evening meal at a trestle table set up underneath an elm near the kingsroad in the northern riverlands.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Arya Stark sleeps in the branches of an elm while traveling near the Gods Eye.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Arya sees mossy elms in the riverlands.

The brotherhood without banners hang Septon Utt beneath a tall elm following the battle at the burning septry.

Merrett Frey passes a skeletal elm near Oldstones.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Brienne Tarth finds wild elms growing amidst the ruins of Hollard castle.

The brotherhood without banners hang outlaws from elms and other trees, and they attempt to hang Ser Hyle Hunt and Podrick Payne from elms.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Tyrion Lannister sees bare brown elms along the Valyrian road between Pentos and Ghoyan Drohe.
