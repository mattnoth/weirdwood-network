Appearance and Character[ ]

Grunt lost his tongue for speaking carelessly within earshot of Roose Bolton. He is described as brutal. Grunt enjoys touching women's breasts, although not always with permission.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Grunt is present in the great hall of the Dreadfort when Big Walder and Little Walder Frey bring Theon Greyjoy before Ramsay Bolton, who is joined by Arnolf Karstark and Hother Umber.

Sour Alyn and Grunt are on guard at the door of Ramsay's bedchamber when the spearwives go with Theon to rescue "Arya Stark" (actually Jeyne Poole).
