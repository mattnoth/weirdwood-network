Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Captain Qos and the Wind Witch are hired by Lord Eddard Stark to take his daughters, Sansa and Arya, home to Winterfell. Before Qos can sail, however, Lord Eddard is seized during Lord Petyr Baelish's coup. Arya later sees the Wind Witch still at harbor in King's Landing.
