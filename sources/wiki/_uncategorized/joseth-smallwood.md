## Full Text

Joseth Smallwood was the Lord of Acorn Hall and the head of House Smallwood during the Dance of the Dragons.

History[ ]

During the civil war known as the Dance of the Dragons, Lord Joseth Smallwood sided with the blacks and Rhaenyra Targaryen.

In 130 AC, the advance of the westermen through the riverlands was briefly halted at Acorn Hall when Lord Joseth sallied forth to join Lord Petyr Piper and the remains of his defeated host, but Petyr died in the ensuring battle and Joseth fell back inside his castle.

At the Second Battle of Tumbleton, Lord Smallwood joined the host led by Ser Addam Velaryon and scraped together fresh levies of greybeards and green boys, despite the grievous losses he had suffered in the autumn battles.
