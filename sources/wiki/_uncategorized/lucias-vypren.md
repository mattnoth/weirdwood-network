Lucias Vypren is the Lord and the head of House Vypren. He is married to Lythene Frey, the second daughter of Lord Walder Frey. They have two children together: Elyana and Damon.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Lucias is present at the Twins for the wedding of Lord Edmure Tully and Lady Roslin Frey. At the Twins, he is introduced to Lady Catelyn Stark while he was sitting with his brothers-in-law the evening before the wedding.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

After Merrett Frey has been killed by the Brotherhood without Banners, Lucias tracks some of the brotherhood's members to Fairmarket, where he loses them.
