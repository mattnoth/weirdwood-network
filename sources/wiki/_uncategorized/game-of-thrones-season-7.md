## Full Text

Season 7 of Game of Thrones is the seventh season of the series. The seventh season was officially announced in April, 2016.

Unlike previous seasons, Season 7 contains only seven episodes, as was officially announced by HBO in July, 2016. In addition, filming will start in September, 2016 and continue until February, 2017, with the summer of 2017 as officially confirmed air-date. The weather has been cited as the reason that the seventh season is both filmed later and will be aired later, as grim, gray weather is needed due to the arrival of winter in the sixth season.
