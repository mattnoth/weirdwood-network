Character[ ]

Umma likes to talk as she works.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

During her training at the House of Black and White, Arya Stark is sent to the kitchens to help prepare food, helping Umma. Although the cook talks to Arya, the Westerosi is unable to understand her. Umma is the only person in the House of Black and White who gives Arya her name. Umma sometimes smacks Arya with her spoon when the girl gets in her way.

While serving as Cat of the Canals, Arya still cooks with Umma sometimes. One one occasion the cook saves a piece of nice fried cod and some mashed yellow turnips for the girl.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Umma begins calling Arya "Stick" after the girl is blinded. Umma bakes bread in the morning and serves salt-crusted crabs for supper one evening.
