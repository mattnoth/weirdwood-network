## Full Text

Lhazar

Essos west of the Bone Mountains and the location of Lhazar

Lhazar is an area of the semi-arid lands south of the Dothraki sea. A region of pastures and hills, it is inhabited by the Lhazareen, a people of sheep herders, and in the southern parts by Ghiscari. There are three cities in Lhazar, Hesh, Lhazosh, and Kosrak. The Skahazadhan passes through northern Lhazar.

Lhazar borders the Dothraki sea to the north and Slaver's Bay to the south. It is connected to Meereen via the Khyzai Pass in the sandstone mountains.
