## Full Text

Robert Redwyne was a knight from House Redwyne during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

Robert was the eldest son of Manfryd Redwyne, the Lord of the Arbor and master of ships to King Jaehaerys I Targaryen. While still a squire, he came to King's Landing with his father and brothers, Rickard and Ryam, when Lord Manfryd was appointed to the small council in 50 AC.

After the death of Ser Qarl Corbray, who succumbed to the Shivers in 59 AC, Robert, by now a knight, was appointed to command the City Watch of King's Landing.

Robert's father, Lord Manfryd Redwyne, passed away in 89 AC, making Robert the new Lord of the Arbor. Robert followed his father into the grave soon thereafter, however.
