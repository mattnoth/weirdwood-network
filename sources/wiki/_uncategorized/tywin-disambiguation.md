Tywin is a given name in Westeros. Characters named Tywin include:

Tywin Frey, eldest son of Ser Cleo Frey and Jeyne Darry

Tywin Frey, fourth son and seventh child of Ser Raymund Frey and Beony Beesbury

Tywin Lannister, Lord of Casterly Rock and Warden of the West

Tywin Graceford (possibly), the unborn child of Lady Alyce Graceford, should it be a boy

Additionally, Tywin may refer to:

Lord Tywin, one of Cersei's dromonds
