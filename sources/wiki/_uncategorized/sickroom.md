Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

  

Summer rescues Catelyn and Bran from the catspaw, by Chris Dien © Fantasy Flight Games

After he falls from the First Keep, Bran Stark is brought to the sickroom of Winterfell. Catelyn Stark stays by her son's side as Bran lies comatose. A catspaw attempts to assassinate Bran, but the boy is saved by his direwolf.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

The solar of Hoster Tully at Riverrun is used as his sickroom as his condition worsens.

After the Battle of the Blackwater, the wounded Tyrion Lannister is treated by Maester Ballabar in a chamber over the Queen's Ballroom. Podrick Payne, Tyrion's squire, is reluctant to visit the sickroom.
