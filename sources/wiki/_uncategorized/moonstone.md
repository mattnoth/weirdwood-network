Culture[ ]

Lady Lysa Arryn has a necklace of sapphires and moonstones.

Lord Guncer Sunglass wears moonstones on his neck, wrists, and fingers.

Nightfall, the Valyrian steel sword of Ser Harras Harlaw, has a moonstone pommel.

Within the Great Sept of Baelor, a chamber has carvings of the Seven with eyes of onyx, jade, malachite, and yellow moonstone.

History[ ]

Ser Maynard Plumm clasped his cloak with a moonstone brooch as large as a hen's egg.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

At the tourney for King Joffrey I Baratheon's name day, Sansa Stark wears a moonstone hair net that had been a gift from him. Sansa also wears the moonstone hair net when the court rides to the harbor to see Princess Myrcella Baratheon off to Dorne.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

When Sansa is forced to wed Tyrion Lannister, Queen Cersei Lannister has her wear the moonstone necklace and earrings that Joffrey had given her.

Lady Lysa Arryn gives Marillion a belt studded with moonstones.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Tyrion Lannister meets Sweets, the beloved slave of Yezzan zo Qaggaz, who dresses in moonstones and Myrish lace.
