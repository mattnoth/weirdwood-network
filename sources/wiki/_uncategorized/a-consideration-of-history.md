## Full Text

A Consideration of History is a history written by Archmaester Perestan. It includes the War Across the Water, in which the Vale and the North fought over the Three Sisters. The text also suggests "Erreg" was the corrupted title of an Andal chieftain who cut down weirwoods on behalf of a river king's rival.
