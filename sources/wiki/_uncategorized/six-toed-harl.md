Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Six-Toed Harl, along with the rest of Asha Greyjoy's, supporters sails to Deepwood Motte with Asha as she contemplates her next move after the kingsmoot on Old Wyk.

When five northmen try to infiltrate Deepwood, Harl kills one of them on the walls. Asha has Harl and Lorren Longaxe fetch Lady Sybelle Glover and her maester. While fleeing from Deepwood back to the Bay of Ice, Asha and her men rest in the wolfswood and wait for sunrise. During the respite Hagen's daughter goes off with Harl for an amorous encounter after having first been refused by Tristifer Botley. Sometime thereafter Harl and Hagen's daughter are interrupted by the northmen who have caught up to the ironmen. Harl's fate after the liberation of the castle is unknown.
