## Full Text

Melaquin was a maester in service at Summerhall during the reigns of Daeron II and Aerys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

Melaquin was a tutor to the sons of Prince Maekar. While a child, the future Aegon V Targaryen would re-enact the Battle of the Redgrass Field on Melaquin's green table with painted soldiers.
