Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

The brotherhood engages the Brave Companions in the battle at the burning septry. Watty the Miller, positioned as a bowman, is injured by an opposing archer. When they leave the septry, he serves as a scout for the brotherhood.

Watty catches a cold from heavy rainfall.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

After Beric's final death, a part of the brotherhood, including Watty, apparently go their separate way from those who chose to follow the resurrected, vengeful Lady Stoneheart.
