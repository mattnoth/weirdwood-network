Layout[ ]

Scribe's Hearth is found just past the gates of the Citadel. Acolytes there can be hired by the people of Oldtown to write wills and read letters. Books and maps are sold at other stalls.

History[ ]

Maester Yandel was a foundling in an empty stall of the Scribe's Hearth in 272 AC, where he was found by an acolyte.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Samwell Tarly stops at Scribe's Hearth after arriving at the Citadel and looks at a map for the fastest way to the Seneschal's Court.
