History[ ]

According to Roose, Bethany never made a sound in bed. Bethany had one son with Roose, Domeric. During his youth, Domeric served as a page at Barrowton with Bethany's sister, Lady Barbrey Dustin. Reek, a servant at the Dreadfort, was once caught stealing perfume from Bethany's bedchamber in order to his mask his unpleasant scent; the following year Reek was caught drinking the perfume.

Bethany died of a fever. Domeric died in 297 AC, allegedly poisoned by her husband's bastard, Ramsay Snow.
