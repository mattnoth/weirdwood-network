## Summary

Brienne of Tarth recalls that Harry participated in a bet beneath the walls of Highgarden to see who would be the first to claim her maidenhead. After the bet was discovered and ended, Brienne sought out and defeated Harry during the melee at Bitterbridge by riding over him. He survived the Battle of the Blackwater, though it is unknown in which side he fought.
