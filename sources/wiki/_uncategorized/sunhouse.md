## Summary

Sunhouse, also called Sunflower Hall is the seat of House Cuy in the Reach. The castle is located on the southern coast of the Reach, east of the Redwyne Straits and southeast of Oldtown. The town of Cuy is located outside of Sunhouse's walls.
