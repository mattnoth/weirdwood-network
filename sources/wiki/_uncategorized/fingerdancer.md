A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The Fingerdancer is part of the Iron Fleet contingent dispatched to Slaver's Bay. En route to Meereen, near Ghozai, she participes along with Iron Victory, Grief, and Sparrowhawk in the capture of the merchant cog named Ghiscari Dawn.
