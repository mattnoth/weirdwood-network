Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Septon Torbert is among the leading possibilities to become the new High Septon after the old one died. Torbert kisses Queen Cersei Lannister's feet whenever they meet. Cersei looks for him and Septon Raynard when she visits the Great Sept of Baelor, but Torbert cannot be located. According to the High Sparrow, Torbert is confined to a penance cell on nothing but bread and water as the High Sparrow believes it is sinful for any man to be as plump as Torbert with half the realm starving.
