## Full Text

Season 4 of Game of Thrones is the fourth season of the series. The series consists of ten episodes. 

Season 4 is closely based on the second half of A Storm of Swords, the third  novel in the A Song of Ice and Fire novel series by George R. R. Martin.

The trailer for season 4 Game of Thrones which was published on January 12th 2014 received 10 million views in two days when placed on YouTube. The season premiered on April 6th 2014.
