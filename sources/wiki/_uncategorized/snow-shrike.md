A Game of Thrones[ ]

While waiting in a forest between the Blue Fork and the Tumblestone within the riverlands, Catelyn Stark hears the high, sharp trilling of snow shrikes. It is, however, unknown if they are actually bird calls or instead signaling by northmen to begin the battle in the Whispering Wood.
