## Summary

Pigrun Alley is an alley in King's Landing. It is enclosed by tall timber-and-stone buildings whose upper stories lean out so far over the streets that they nearly touch those of the buildings across from them.

Pigrun Alley is an alley in King's Landing. It is enclosed by tall timber-and-stone buildings whose upper stories lean out so far over the streets that they nearly touch those of the buildings across from them.
