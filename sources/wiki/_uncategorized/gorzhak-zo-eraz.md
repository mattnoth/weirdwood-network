Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The Windblown, a sellsword company hired by Yunkai, mock Gorzhak as "Pudding Face". Since the death of Yurkhaz zo Yunzak in the fighting pits of Meereen, the Yunkish leaders, including Gorzhak, keep a daily rotation for the duty of supreme commander.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

On the eve of battle during the Second Siege of Meereen, Gorzhak is supposed to become the Supreme Commander according to the rotation chart but is slain by the Tattered Prince, who turns the Windblown's cloak to join Queen Daenerys Targaryen.
