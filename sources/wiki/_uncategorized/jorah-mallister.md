## Full Text

Jorah Mallister was the Lord of Seagard and the head of House Mallister during the Dance of the Dragons.

In the television adaptation House of the Dragon, Jorah Mallister is not present. He has been replaced by Lady Mallister, who is portrayed by Anna Francolini.

History[ ]

At the start of the Dance of the Dragons in 129 AC, Lord Mallister was among the prominent riverlords that supported Queen Rhaenyra Targaryen and the blacks, despite initial reluctance.

Lord Jorah joined the host of Lord Elmo Tully as they marched to King's Landing against the army of Lord Borros Baratheon, resulting in the Battle of the Kingsroad. During the battle, Jorah and Lord Roland Darry were slain by the unhorsed Lord Baratheon.
