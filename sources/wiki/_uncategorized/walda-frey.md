## Full Text

Walda Frey is the name of several members of House Frey of the Crossing.
As with the name Walder, the name may be an attempt of the parents to please Lord Walder Frey.

Walda Frey, also known as Fair Walda, the daughter of Walton Frey and Deana Hardyng.

Walda Frey, also known as Fat Walda, second daughter of Merrett Frey and Mariya Darry, third wife to Roose Bolton.

Walda Frey, also known as White Walda, the daughter of Rhaegar Frey and Jeyne Beesbury.

Walda Frey, the daughter of Edwyn Frey and Janyce Hunter.

Walda Frey, second daughter of Lothar Frey and Leonella Lefford.
