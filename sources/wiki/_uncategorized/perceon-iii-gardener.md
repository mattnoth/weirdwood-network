Perceon III Gardener was a King of the Reach and a head of House Gardener.

History[ ]

Fearing House Manderly's swelling power in the Reach, Perceon had Lord Lorimar Peake drive the Manderlys into exile. Perceon III's son Gwayne married Lord Lorimar's daughter and in time succeeded Perceon.
