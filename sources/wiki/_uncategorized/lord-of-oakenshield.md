Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

The ironborn led by King Euron Greyjoy capture Oakenshield during the taking of the Shields. Lord Humfrey Hewett is killed after the fighting. Euron names Nute the Barber as the new Lord of Oakenshield.
