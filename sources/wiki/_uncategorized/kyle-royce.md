## Full Text

Kyle Royce was a member of House Royce during the reign of King Aerys II Targaryen. His relation to Yohn Royce, the Lord of Runestone, is unknown.

History[ ]

When news of Lyanna Stark's kidnapping by Prince Rhaegar Targaryen reached her brother, Brandon Stark, he raced off to King's Landing accompanied by a party of nobles, which included Kyle. Brandon and his party rode into the Red Keep demanding Rhaegar's head. King Aerys II Targaryen had them all arrested and demanded that their fathers come to King's Landing to answer for their sons' crimes. Aerys had all of them killed save for Ethan Glover.
