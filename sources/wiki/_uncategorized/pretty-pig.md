## Summary

Pretty Pig, or Pretty for short, is a trained pig that is owned by the dwarf girl Penny. She was ridden by Penny's brother Oppo in their dwarf jousting act. After Tyrion Lannister and Penny were sold into slavery she was ridden by Tyrion.
