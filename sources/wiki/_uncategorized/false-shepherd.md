The false Shepherd was one of the men claiming to be the reincarnation of the Shepherd after his death.

History[ ]

After the arrest and execution of the prophet known as the Shepherd on the last day of 130 AC, several individuals in King's Landing claimed to be the reincarnation of the late prophet. One such was executed by Ser Victor Risley, the King's Justice, by order of Lord Unwin Peake in 133 AC.
