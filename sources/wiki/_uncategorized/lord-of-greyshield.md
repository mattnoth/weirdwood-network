History[ ]

Lord Grimm supported the blacks during the Dance of the Dragons.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

The ironborn led by King Euron Greyjoy capture Greyshield during the taking of the Shields. Lord Guthor Grimm is taken captive by Ser Harras Harlaw. Euron names Ser Harras as the new Lord of Greyshield.
