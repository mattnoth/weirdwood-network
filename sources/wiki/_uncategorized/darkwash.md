## Summary

The Darkwash or Darkwater is a river in Essos whose headwaters lie in the Hills of Norvos south of the Axe. It flows into the Qhoyne near the ruins of Ar Noy, and the rivers north of Ar Noy are ruled by the Free City of Qohor. East of the Darkwash is the Forest of Qohor.

Darkwash

Western Essos and the location of the Darkwash

The Darkwash or Darkwater is a river in Essos whose headwaters lie in the Hills of Norvos south of the Axe. It flows into the Qhoyne near the ruins of Ar Noy, and the rivers north of Ar Noy are ruled by the Free City of Qohor. East of the Darkwash is the Forest of Qohor.

Gold and amber from the Axe flow into Dagger Lake by way of the Qhoyne and its tributary, the Darkwash.
