Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

When Melisandre has the castle sept at Dragonstone sacked so that statues of the Seven can be burned, Ser Hubard leads his three sons to defend it. They slay four of the queen's men before being overcome. Hubard and one of his sons are killed, while the other two are imprisoned along with Septon Barre and Lord Guncer Sunglass.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

While Stannis is fighting the Battle of the Blackwater, Hubard's remaining sons and Guncer are burned alive for treason by Queen Selyse Florent and Melisandre on Dragonstone, as sacrifices to R'hllor.
