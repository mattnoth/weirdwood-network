## Summary

The Peregrine takes Princess Arianne Martell and her party from Ghost Hill across the Sea of Dorne to meet Jon Connington in the stormlands. The crossing takes a day and a night, and Jayne Ladybright becomes seasick. Arianne loses to Daemon Sand and Garibald Shells in games of cyvasse. The party disembarks at the Weeping Town.
