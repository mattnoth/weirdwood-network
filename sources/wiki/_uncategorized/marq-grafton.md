## Full Text

Marq Grafton was a member of House Grafton during the reign of King Aerys II Targaryen.

History[ ]

At the start of Robert's Rebellion, Lord Grafton stayed loyal to King Aerys II Targaryen instead of following his liege, Lord Jon Arryn of the Eyrie, into rebellion. Marq was slain by Lord Robert Baratheon at the Gulltown walls when the rebels took the city.
