## Full Text

Masha Heddle is the innkeeper of the crossroads inn in the riverlands.

In the television adaptation Game of Thrones, Masha is portrayed by Susie Kelly.

Appearance and Character[ ]

Masha is a fat and kind middle-aged woman. Her teeth have turned blood-red due to her fondness of chewing sourleaf. She also enjoys honey cakes.

History[ ]

Masha let Septon Meribald sleep beside the hearth when she did not have room for him, and gave him food when he left the inn. At some point, Masha told him the history of the inn.

When Catelyn Tully was a child, Masha was kind to her, and gave her and other children sweet cakes.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Upon her arrival at the crossroads inn, Catelyn Stark remembers that when she was a child, Masha always had a smile and a sweet cake for the children.

Masha does not want to get involved when Catelyn arrests Tyrion Lannister, so she lets Catelyn take him.

When the Lannisters invade the riverlands, Tyrion's father, Lord Tywin Lannister, hangs Masha on a gibbet in the inn's yard as punishment. Tyrion sees her body when he arrives at the inn to meet with his father. Ravens have eaten her lips and eyes and most of her cheeks, baring her stained red teeth in a hideous smile.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Masha's nephew takes over the inn.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Masha's nephew is killed by an unspecified nobleman. Afterwards, his daughters, Masha's great-nieces Willow and Jeyne, manage the inn, and become associates of the brotherhood without banners.
