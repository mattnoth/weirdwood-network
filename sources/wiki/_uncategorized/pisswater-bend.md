Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser Jacelyn Bywater reports to Tyrion Lannister that the bloody flux is spreading in the stews along Pisswater Bend.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Young Griff explains how he survived the Sack of King's Landing to Tyrion. The story involves a baby swap with a tanner's son from Pisswater Bend.

Ser Kevan Lannister reflects on the walk of atonement of his niece, Cersei Lannister, and he is irritated that people from Pisswater Bend saw her naked.
