Leo Tyrell is the name of several members of House Tyrell:

Leo Tyrell, known as Leo Longthorn, the Lord of Highgarden and head of House Tyrell during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen

Leo Tyrell (son of Gareth), head of House Tyrell and the second High Steward of the Reach

Leo Tyrell (son of Moryn), known as Lazy Leo, a novice at the Citadel

Leo Tyrell (son of Victor), a knight of House Tyrell
