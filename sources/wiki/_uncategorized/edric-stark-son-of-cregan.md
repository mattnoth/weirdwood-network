## Summary

Edric Stark was the third son of Lord Cregan Stark, second by his last wife Lynara Stark. He was married to Serena Stark, the daughter of his half-brother Rickon Stark. They had four children; twin sons Torrhen and Cregard, and daughters Arrana and Argelle Stark.
