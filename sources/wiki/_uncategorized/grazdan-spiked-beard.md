Grazdan is a Good Master of Astapor. He was presumably named after Grazdan the Great, who founded Old Ghis. He has a spiked beard, and speaks the Common Tongue, though not well.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Grazdan is among the Good Masters who bargain with Daenerys Targaryen over how many Unsullied she could purchase. He offers her two thousand. He is present when Daenerys buys her Unsullied, and is the one who demands Drogon to be turned over. He is also present during the Sack of Astapor.
