Argos Sevenstar was an Andal warlord.

History[ ]

Argos was one of many warlords during the coming of the Andals to Westeros. He led the great of the Andal threats to the north, but was smashed by an alliance between King Theon Stark and House Bolton at the Battle of the Weeping Water.

In the aftermath of victory, King Theon raised his own fleet and crossed the narrow sea to the shores of Andalos in western Essos, with Argos's corpse lashed to the prow of his flagship. Theon's northmen burned a score of villages, captured three tower houses and a fortified sept, and put hundreds to the sword. When he returned to the north, the Hungry Wolf planted the heads of the slain on spikes along his coasts as a warning for other potential invaders.
