Lord of Mistwood is a title held by the head of House Mertyns, the ruler of Mistwood in the stormlands.

History[ ]

Lord Mertyns and his whole household were poisoned by a cask of Dornish wine as a reprisal by the Dornishmen for the Dragon's Wroth.

Lord Jon Mertyns was a guest at the Golden Wedding in 49 AC.
