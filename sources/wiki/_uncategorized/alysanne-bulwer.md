History[ ]

Born in 291 AC, Alysanne is the daughter of Lord Jon Bulwer and Lady Victaria Tyrell, and she is a distant cousin of Lord Mace Tyrell. She succeeded her father after he died of summer fever.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Alysanne is a lady attendant of Margaery Tyrell in King's Landing.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lady Alysanne and Queen Margaery play come-into-my-castle together. It is one of the things Ser Osney Kettleblack claims is preventing him from seducing Margaery.
