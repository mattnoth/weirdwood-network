Silvario Pendaerys was a Lyseni magister during the Lysene Spring.

History[ ]

Silvario was stabbed through the eye leaving the Temple of Trade in 135 AC during the bloody power struggle in the city after the downfall of the Rogare family. His brother Pereno was also murdered.
