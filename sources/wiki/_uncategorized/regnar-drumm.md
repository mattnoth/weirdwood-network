Regnar Drumm, known as Raven-feeder, was a High King of the Iron Islands from House Drumm.

History[ ]

Erich I Greyiron seized the crown of his father, Urras Greyiron, upon Ironfoot's death and declared himself High King of the Iron Islands. Galon Whitestaff commanded that kings may only be made by kingsmoot, however, and the ironborn captains and kings cast Erich down from power. Regnar, the rock king of Old Wyk, was chosen the new High King at the kingsmoot.
