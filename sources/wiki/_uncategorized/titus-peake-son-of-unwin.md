History[ ]

Titus was one of Lord Unwin Peake's seven children, being his third son. He was the only one of Unwin's sons to grow to manhood.

After the Battle of the Honeywine, Titus was knighted by Ser Jon Roxton for his valor, but Ser Titus died only six days later in a meaningless skirmish with a band of broken men he stumbled upon whilst scouting. His sister, Myrielle, was the only sibling to outlive him.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

House Peake is a reference by George R. R. Martin to the books of Mervyn Peake, which are among his reading recommendations. Titus Groan is the main character of the Gormenghast series. Their seat, Starpike, is a reference to Steerpike, another character in the series.
