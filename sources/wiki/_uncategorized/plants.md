Notes[ ]

↑ The description indicates that this is specifically maize-corn, the New World crop, and not just using "corn" in the general sense of "grain". From Bran's dream: "When he pulled his hand out, golden kernels slid from between his fingers into the air." And from a description of the Vale: "wheat and corn and barley grew high in its fields".

↑ Also maize-corn. When Arya travels with Yoren: "They roasted the sweetcorn in the husk that night, turning the ears with long forked sticks, and ate it hot right off the cob."

↑ Dornish "peppers", by their description, are specifically New World crop "bell peppers", not "black pepper" the Old World spice.  Dornish peppers are described as being stuffed with things like cheese.
