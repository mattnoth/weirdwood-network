Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Ulf is the leader of the Moon Brothers who accompany Tyrion Lannister to his father Tywin Lannister's encampment on the promise of weapons and armor. Lord Leo Lefford is displeased when Tyrion requests arms and armor from him for Ulf's men.

Ulf demands blood money when three Stone Crows kill a Moon Brother, but Conn and Shagga refuse to pay.

Ulf is slain during the battle on the Green Fork. After the battle, Tyrion finds Ulf with his arm gone at the elbow and a dozen Moon Brothers dead around him.
