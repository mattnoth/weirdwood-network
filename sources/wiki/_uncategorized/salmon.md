Range[ ]

Salmon can be found in the rivers of Sea Dragon Point, in the Shivering Sea, in the White Knife near White Harbor, and near Maidenpool and Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.

Culture[ ]

A red salmon is included in the arms of House Mooton of Maidenpool. Lord William Mooton pins his cloak with a red-gold brooch in the shape of a salmon.

History[ ]

Lord Torrhen Manderly, Hand of the King, suggested that King Aegon III Targaryen could fish for salmon in the White Knife if he were to visit the north. Aegon cancelled the planned royal progress when he came of age, however, and dismissed Lord Manderly from his service.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Salmon is among the seafood brought by Lord Wyman Manderly from White Harbor to Winterfell. Bran Stark sends salmon to Lady Donella Hornwood during the harvest feast.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Salmon is served at Eastwatch-by-the-Sea the night before Salladhor Saan's fleet departs for White Harbor.

The stores of Castle Black have planks of dry smoked salmon.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Pike, trout, and salmon are served at the Gates of the Moon.
