## Full Text

Martyn Hightower was a squire from House Hightower during the reign of King Aegon III Targaryen.

History[ ]

Martyn was born to Lord Ormund Hightower and his first wife, who died in childbed at some point. He had an elder brother, Lyonel, a younger brother, Garmund, and a sister, Bethany. During the Dance of the Dragons, his father married again to Samantha Tarly, known as Lady Sam.

Martyn was sent to be a squire to Lord Redwyne on the Arbor.
