Apperance[ ]

Duram is a plump boy who wears purple velvet trimmed with white seal.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord Duram supports Stannis Baratheon's claim to the throne by pledging himself to be Stannis's vassal. He attends the feast in which Maester Cressen dies from poison. Duram's face turns a splotchy grey when Melisandre burns statues of the Seven from the sept at Dragonstone.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

After Stannis's defeat in the Battle of the Blackwater, Duram remains loyal to him. However, his support is disparaged by both Stannis and Lord Alester Florent, for the Lord of Sharp Point is a feeble, fat boy of fifteen.

The Bar Emmon banners are seen in Castle Black after Stannis's arrival, as Lord Duram sent men with Stannis on his journey to the Wall.
