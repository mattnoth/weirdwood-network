Kingsgrave

Dorne and the location of Kingsgrave

Kingsgrave is the seat of House Manwoody in Dorne. The castle is located in the Red Mountains approximately halfway through the Prince's Pass, north of Skyreach and south of the tower of joy.

History[ ]

Kingsgrave takes its name from the founder of House Manwoody having slain a king.
