## Full Text

The Round Hall is the main hall of Storm's End.

Layout[ ]

The Round Hall is found in the single massive tower of Storm's End, located below bedchambers, the maester's cell, and the rookery. Nearby is the castle yard.

The throne of the old Storm Kings from House Durrandon, now used by the Lords Paramount of the Stormlands from House Baratheon, is located on a dais in the Round Hall.

History[ ]

Rogar Baratheon, Lord of Storm's End and Lord Paramount of the Stormlands, was in the Round Hall with his young son Boremund on his lap when Princess Rhaena Targaryen blamed him for the death of her mother, Alyssa Velaryon.

During the Dance of the Dragons, the blacks and the greens both sought the support of Lord Borros Baratheon. Prince Aemond Targaryen and Prince Lucerys Velaryon were sent to represent their respective factions and were received by Borros in the Round Hall.
