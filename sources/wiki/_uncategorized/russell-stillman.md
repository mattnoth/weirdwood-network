Russell Stillman was the former squire of Lord Alyn Velaryon and the author of Hard as Oak.

Character and Appearance[ ]

Russell lost a leg.

History[ ]

During Oakenfist's fifth voyage, Russell was knighted by Lord Alyn before losing a leg. He later wrote a book about Lord Alyn Velaryon's life called Hard as Oak. Archmaester Gyldayn considers this account to be colorful, but unreliable.
