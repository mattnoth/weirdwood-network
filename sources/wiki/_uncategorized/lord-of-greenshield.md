History[ ]

In 89 AC, Lord Chester hosted King Jaehaerys I Targaryen during one of his royal progress.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

The ironborn led by King Euron Greyjoy capture Greenshield during the taking of the Shields. Lord Moribald Chester dies in the fighting. Euron names the young Lord Maron Volmark as the new Lord of Greenshield.
