Recent Events[ ]

  

Foamdrinker - by Thomas Denmark © Fantasy Flight Games

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Dagmer and the Foamdrinker are part of the small force under the command of Theon Greyjoy during the harrying of the Stony Shore. Deviating from the plan of his father Balon, King of the Iron Islands, Theon grants six longships to Aeron Greyjoy to continue raiding the shore but keeps the Foamdrinker and the Sea Bitch.

Dagmer threatens Torrhen's Square, but Ser Rodrik Cassel defeats him in the fight at Torrhen's Square. Dagmer's distraction allows Theon to capture Winterfell, however.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

After the sack of Winterfell, Dagmer and his men capture Torrhen's Square.
