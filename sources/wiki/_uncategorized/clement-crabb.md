History[ ]

In the great tourney of King's Landing in 98 AC, to celebrate the fiftieth year of King Jaehaerys I's reign, Ser Clement and Ser Ryam Redwyne broke thirty lances against each other, before Jaehaerys proclaimed them co-champions. Their competition is often called the finest display of jousting in the history of Westeros.
