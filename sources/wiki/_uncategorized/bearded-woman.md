Appearance and Character[ ]

The woman has a beard and speaks a Ghiscari dialect.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Along with the rest of Yezzan's special treasures, the bearded woman accompanies her master to the Yunkai encampment outside of Meereen. 

Tyrion Lannister meets the bearded woman after he's bought into Yezzan's grotesquerie. He finds her Ghiscari incomprehensible. 

When Yezzan contracts the pale mare, it falls to Yezzan's special treasures to attend to him as he is dying. Tyrion Lannister and Penny escape before they can learn of the fate of Yezzan's grotesquerie. The fate of the bearded woman is unknown.
