## Full Text

Lord Peake was the Lord of Starpike, Dunstonbury, Whitegrove, and the head of House Peake during the late reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

After the death of Prince Baelon Targaryen, Prince of Dragonstone and Hand of the King, King Jaehaerys I Targaryen summoned the nobility of Westeros at Harrenhal to form a Great Council and choose the new heir to the Iron Throne. Lord Peake came and voted for Prince Viserys Targaryen instead of Princess Rhaenys Targaryen or her son Laenor Velaryon, as he did not believe woman should rule over men.

He was eventually succeeded by his eldest son, Unwin.
