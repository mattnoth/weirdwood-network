## Full Text

Rodrik is a popular male name in Westeros. It may refer to:

Rodrik Arryn, Lord of the Eyrie during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen

Rodrik Cassel, the master-at-arms at Winterfell

Rodrik Flint, a Lord Commander of the Night's Watch

Rodrik Freeborn, an ironborn raider

Rodrik Greyjoy, a salt son of Lord Dalton Greyjoy

Rodrik Greyjoy, eldest son of Lord Balon Greyjoy

Rodrik Harlaw, Lord of Harlaw, Lord of the Ten Towers and head of House Harlaw

Rodrik Ryswell, Lord of the Rills and head of House Ryswell

Rodrik Sparr, known as "The Vole", an ironborn captain

Rodrik Stark, a King of Winter

Rodrik Stark, known as the "Wandering Wolf", maternal grandfather of Eddard Stark

Rodrik Forrester, a character created for the the video game Game of Thrones - A Telltale Games Series
