Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser Gerris Drinkwater does not speak Volantene as well as Prince Quentyn Martell. Maester Kedry is fluent in the tongues of all of the Free Cities.

Haldon Halfmaester teaches the Volantene dialect to Young Griff and Tyrion Lannister. Tyrion understands only some of the Volantene he hears in Selhorys and Volantis.

When Daenerys Targaryen, Queen of Meereen, meets with refugees from Astapor, she hears Volantene among the various peoples all saying their word for "Mother".
