## Summary

Brandon of the Bloody Blade is a legendary son of Garth Greenhand. He is credited with driving giants away from the Reach and warring against children of the forest, slaying so many at Blue Lake it became known as Red Lake. In some tales he is mentioned as the ancestor or father of Bran the Builder, making him a possible ancestor of House Stark.
