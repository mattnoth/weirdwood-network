A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord Dagon is among the ships that makes the voyage to the kingsmoot on Old Wyk. After the taking of the Shields, Lord Captain Victarion Greyjoy keeps Lord Dagon, Grief, Iron Wind and Maiden's Bane as part of his rearguard, commanding from his own ship, Iron Victory.
