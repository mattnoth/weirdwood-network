Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Poxy Tym is hired as a guard at Winterfell after both Lord Eddard and Robb Stark go south.

Tym weeps after the capture of Winterfell by ironborn. Theon Greyjoy orders him to accompany him on his search for Bran and Rickon Stark. Following the sack of Winterfell, Bran finds that Tym was killed by an axe to his face.
