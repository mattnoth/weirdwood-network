Culture[ ]

The First Sword commands the Sealord's guardsmen and protects him in public. The First Sword is often the deadliest of the bravos and water dancers of Braavos.

History[ ]

First Swords have participated in a dozen wars and protected numerous Sealords. Some of the most famous duels have involved First Swords.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Syrio Forel, a former first sword, tells Arya Stark that he had been chosen by the Sealord of Braavos because he was the most observant of the bravos, and most able to protect the Sealord from threats seen and unseen.
