## Summary

Asabhad is a city at the western boundary of Yi Ti in Essos along the shores of the Jade Sea, east of the Bone Mountains. It is located at a forested river mouth. Situated on a trade route connecting Qarth and Yin, Asabhad is where many caravans begin their journey to the Mountains of the Morn.
