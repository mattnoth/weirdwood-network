History[ ]

The order was founded by House Gardener, the Kings of the Reach, whose sigil was a green hand. Only the most virtuous and skilled in arms were offered membership after proving themselves in tourneys of love and beauty held at Highgarden.

The last members of the order died with King Mern IX Gardener at the Field of Fire during the Aegon's Conquest, but their traditions are still recalled in the Reach, which is now ruled by House Tyrell. Additionally, knights from House Manderly in the north still claim membership in the order, as the Manderlys had been exiled from the Reach centuries before the Conquest.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]
