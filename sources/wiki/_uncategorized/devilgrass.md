## Summary

Devilgrass or devil grass is a tough brown grass that grows in clumps and can survive in harsh climates with little water. It has been found near Standfast in the Reach, near Shandystone in Dorne, in the red waste, near Volantis, and among the plains of the Jogos Nhai. Zorses eat the plant.
