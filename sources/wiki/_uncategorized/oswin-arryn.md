Oswin Arryn, nicknamed the Talon, was a King of Mountain and Vale from House Arryn.

History[ ]

During the wars between House Stark of the North and House Arryn of the Vale of Arryn, King Osgood Arryn laid siege to the Wolf's Den. His son, King Oswin the Talon, later captured and burned the castle.
