## Summary

Septon Barre protests when men sworn to Queen Selyse Florent sack his castle sept, and he is thrown into Dragonstone's jails. Also imprisoned are the two surviving sons of Ser Hubard Rambton, who defended the sept from the queen's men by force, and Lord Guncer Sunglass, who renounces his support for Stannis Baratheon.
