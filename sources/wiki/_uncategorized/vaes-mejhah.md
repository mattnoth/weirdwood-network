## Summary

Vaes Mejhah is a ruined Ghiscari city in Essos between the southeastern Dothraki sea and Lhazar. It is located on a tributary of the eastern Skahazadhan, between Vaes Efe and Krazaaj Has. It was ruined after the Doom of Valyria, and its Dothraki language name means City of Whores.
