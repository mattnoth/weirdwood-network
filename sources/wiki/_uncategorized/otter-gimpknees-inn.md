History[ ]

Most of Lordsport was destroyed during Greyjoy's Rebellion by the host of King Robert I Baratheon in a battle at Lordsport. Rebuilt after the war, the waterside inn of Otter Gimpknee is twice the size of its predecessor. Its lower story is made of cut stone, while the two upper floors are timber.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

When Theon Greyjoy returns to the Iron Islands, he sees a prostitute call to Ibbenese sailors from the inn. Asha Greyjoy stays overnight at the inn after her journey to Great Wyk. With Lord Balon Greyjoy calling his bannermen to Pyke, the inn is crowded and boys from House Goodbrother patronize the inn's prostitutes.
