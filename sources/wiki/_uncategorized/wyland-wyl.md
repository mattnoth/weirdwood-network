## Full Text

Wyland Wyl was a nobleman of House Wyl during the regency of King Aegon III Targaryen.

Appearance[ ]

Wyland was one-armed.

History[ ]

The young Princess Aliandra Martell encouraged Dornish noblemen to raid the stormlands. In 133 AC, Ser Steffon Connington pursued a small band of Dornish raiders across the Dornish Marches, but rode too far. Ignorant of what lied ahead, Steffon was ambushed and slain by Wyland Wyl.
