Culture[ ]

  

Meereen, by Martina Pilcerova © Fantasy Flight Games

The Good Masters of Astapor light silk lanterns on the terraces of their pyramids at night.

The coins of Yunkai are stamped with a stepped pyramid on one side and the harpy of Ghis on the other.

Tree, vines, and flowers can be grown on the terraces of pyramids, including figs, dates, and olives on the terraces of Meereen's buildings.

History[ ]

The Great Pyramid of Ghis was destroyed by the Valyrian Freehold during the Ghiscari wars.

The stepped pyramids of Ghozai were drowned by the Doom of Valyria.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Daenerys Targaryen sees stepped pyramids among the buildings of Vaes Dothrak.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Daenerys takes the Great Pyramid as her abode after the successful siege of Meereen. The master of the Indigo Star informs her that each pyramid in Astapor hosts an armed camp in the aftermath of that city's fall.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The Sons of the Harpy plot against Daenerys from within Meereen's pyramids in response to her abolition of slavery. Daenerys, now Queen of Meereen, imposes a tax of a hundred pieces of gold from every pyramid for every freedman killed in the city, and she demands that each pyramid send her a boy and a girl to serve as her cupbearers.

Many pyramids in Astapor are despoiled amid the city's capture by Yunkai'i.
