## Summary

Draconys is a city founded by the Valyrian Freehold. Although rich and glorious, it did not have self-rule and was instead governed by men and women sent from Valyria to rule in the name of the Freehold. Draconys was presumably ruined during the Doom of Valyria.

## Full Text

Draconys is a city founded by the Valyrian Freehold. Although rich and glorious, it did not have self-rule and was instead governed by men and women sent from Valyria to rule in the name of the Freehold. Draconys was presumably ruined during the Doom of Valyria.

The exact location of Draconys has not been revealed yet. Draconys may be one of the unnamed ruined cities seen in the Valyrian peninsula in The Lands of Ice and Fire.
