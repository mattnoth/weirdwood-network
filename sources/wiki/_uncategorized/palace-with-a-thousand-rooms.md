The Palace With a Thousand Rooms was the residence of the High King of Sarnor in the city of Sarnath.

History[ ]

Lomas Longstrider included the Palace With a Thousand Rooms among the Wonders Made by Man. The building was put to the torch by Khal Loso after the Field of Crows.
