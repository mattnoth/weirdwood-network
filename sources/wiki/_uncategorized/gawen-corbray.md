## Full Text

Gawen Corbray was a knight of House Corbray during the reign of King Aegon I Targaryen.

Character[ ]

Gawen Corbray was reputed as one of the deadliest knights in the Seven Kingdoms.

History[ ]

On the account of his prowess at arms and reputation, Ser Gawen was chosen by Queen Visenya Targaryen as the first master-at-arms for her son, Prince Maegor Targaryen, on Dragonstone. Under Corbray's tutelage, Prince Maegor took to swordplay at once.
