## Full Text

Quent is a household guard sworn to House Stark of Winterfell.

Other characters named with a variant of Quent exist. They include:

Quenten Banefort, the current Lord of Banefort.

Quentin Tyrell, a knight who died at the Battle of Ashford.

Quenton Corbray, the Lord of Heart's Home during the regency of King Aegon III Targaryen.

Quenton Greyjoy, the second son of Lord Quellon Greyjoy.

Quenton Greyjoy, a distant cousin to Asha Greyjoy.

Quenton Hightower, the Lord of the Hightower during the reign of King Maekar I Targaryen.

Quenton Qoherys, the head of House Qoherys and the first Lord of Harrenhal.

Quentyn Ball, called "Fireball", a famous knight and commander during the First Blackfyre Rebellion.

Quentyn Blackwood, a Lord of Raventree Hall during the reign of Daeron II Targaryen.

Quentyn Martell, a POV character and the second child and eldest son of Prince Doran Martell.

Quentyn Qorgyle, the current Lord of Sandstone.

Quentyn Tarth, a knight from House Tarth during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen.
