## Full Text

Amanda Arryn was a noblewoman from House Arryn during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

Amanda was the daughter of Lord Rodrik Arryn and his first wife. She had three siblings, including an elder sister, Elys. After her mother died, her father married again to Princess Daella Targaryen. Amanda largely wrote letters on behalf of her step-mother to Queen Alysanne Targaryen.

Daella died in childbirth, giving Amanda a younger half-sister, Aemma Arryn.
