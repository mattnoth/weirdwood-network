## Full Text

Sam Stoops was the steward of Standfast for House Osgrey during the reign of King Aerys I Targaryen.

Character[ ]

By 211 AC, Sam was an old man and had a wife. He distrusted Rohanne Webber, Lady of Coldmoat, and considered her a witch and a poisoner.

History[ ]

When Ser Duncan the Tall trained smallfolk, Sam stuffed some old sacks with soiled straw for them to practice on. he also provided straw pallets in the undercroft of Standfast for the recruits to rest upon. He began praying when he saw Wat's Wood afire. While Ser Eustace Osgrey was away, Ser Bennis of the Brown Shield tied up Sam and his wife, and stole the remaining valuables from Standfast.
