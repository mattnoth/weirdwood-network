## Summary

Jocelyn Stark was the only daughter of Lord Willam Stark and Lady Melantha Blackwood. She was married to Benedict Royce and had children. According to Catelyn Stark, she had three daughters; one married a Waynwood, another a Corbray, and the third might have married a Templeton.
