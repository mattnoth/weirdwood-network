## Full Text

Broken Branch

The north and the location of the Broken Branch

The Broken Branch is a river in the southeastern lands of the north. The headwaters of the Broken Branch lie in the Sheepshead Hills, while Ramsgate sits at its mouth, near where the Bite meets the Shivering Sea and the narrow sea.

According to a semi-canon source, the Broken Branch is within the dominion of House Manderly. There is ice fishing on the northern rivers.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Wyman Manderly, Lord of White Harbor, informs Davos Seaworth that he can deliver the allegiance of the river's length to King Stannis Baratheon.
