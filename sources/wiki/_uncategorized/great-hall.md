Great Hall or great hall is a common designation for the largest halls of castles in Westeros. These include:

Great Hall of Ashford

Great Hall of Bitterbridge

great hall of Casterly Rock

great timbered hall of Castle Black

great hall of Darry

Great Hall of Dragonstone

great hall of the Dreadfort

great hall of Faircastle

Great Hall of the Gates of the Moon

Great Hall of Ghost Hill

great hall of Griffin's Roost

great hall of the Nightfort

great hall of Oakenshield

Great Hall of Pyke

Great Hall of the Red Keep

Great Hall of Riverrun

great hall of Storm's End, known as the "Round Hall"

great hall of Sunspear

great hall of the east castle of the Twins

great hall of Whitewalls

Great Hall of Winterfell
