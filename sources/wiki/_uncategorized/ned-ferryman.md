## Full Text

Ned was the ferryman at the old inn by the lakeshore near Whitewalls during the reign of King Aerys I Targaryen.

Character[ ]

Ned did not like crossing the lake at night.

History[ ]

In 212 AC, Ned ferried several guests across the lake for the wedding tourney at Whitewalls, including Ser John the Fiddler, Lord Shawney, Lord Costayne, Lord Smallwood, and various hedge knights.
