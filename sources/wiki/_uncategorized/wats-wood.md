## Full Text

Wat's Wood is a forest located in House Osgrey lands in the Reach. It is made up of oaks and pines. The west way, which runs from Standfast to Coldmoat, passes through the wood.

History[ ]

Wat's Wood once extended all the way to Coldmoat, but the forest was harvested to make way for grazing land and fields.

Wat's Wood burned during the long drought of King Aerys I Targaryen's reign, though the fire could have been started by members of House Webber, who had issues with House Osgrey. Ser Eustace Osgrey was surprised to find the carcass of a tree cat in the forest.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

While in the published version of The Sworn Sword, the cause of the fire in Wat's Wood remains unexplained, possibly being accidental, in a draft of the novella, Rohanne Webber suggests that Ser Lucas Inchfield may be responsible.
