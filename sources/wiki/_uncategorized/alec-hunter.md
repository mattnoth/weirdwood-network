## Full Text

Alec Hunter was the Lord of Longbow Hall and the head of House Hunter during the early reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

Alec travelled to King's Landing to attend the Golden Wedding in 49 AC. King Jaehaerys I Targaryen took the opportunity to entertain the assembled lords in the intimacy of his solar, encouraging them to speak freely and share their views on the problems of the realm and how they might best be overcome. Alec thought the young king to be stern.
