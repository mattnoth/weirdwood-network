Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

During the siege of Meereen, Oznak zo Pahl is slain outside the city by Strong Belwas. Oznak's father, the commander of the city guard, dies defending the gates.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Daenerys Targaryen, now Queen of Meereen, disbands the city watch and replaces them with the Brazen Beasts, who are made up of freedmen and shavepates.
