Biology[ ]

These animals are a similar to both rodents and pigs. They are also hairy and of a size comparable to cows.

History[ ]

These mouse-pigs are one of the many queer animals brought from distant lands for the menagerie of the Sealord of Braavos.

Recent events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Syrio Forel tells Arya Stark of the queer animals he's seen in the Sealord's menagerie.

Behind the scenes[ ]

The real-life counterpart of the mouse-pig has not yet been identified. Fans have theorized the capybara, the tapir as well as the extinct diprotodon as possibilities.
