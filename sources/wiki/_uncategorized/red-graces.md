Duties[ ]

At nighttime, the Red Graces wait in little snuggeries in the pleasure gardens every night until a man chooses them. Those who are not chosen must remain until the sun comes up.

Girls of noble birth who are too young to serve their year in the pleasure gardens of the Temple of the Graces serve instead as White Graces.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser Gerris Drinkwater suggests to Prince Quentyn Martell that they go to the Temple of the Graces to find a girl for Quentyn, but he refuses. Gerris feels that the septas of Westeros should emulate the Red Graces, pointing out that old septas always look like prunes, as a result of a boring life of chastity.
