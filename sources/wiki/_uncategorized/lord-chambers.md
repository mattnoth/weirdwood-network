## Full Text

Lord Chambers was the Lord and the head of House Chambers during the civil war known as the Dance of the Dragons.

History[ ]

During the Dance of the Dragons, Lord Chambers sided with the blacks and Rhaenyra Targaryen.

Lord Chambers, alongside with Lord Bigglestone and Lord Perryn, joined Ser Pate of Longleaf and his ragged band of survivors from earlier battles and fought together in the Battle by the Lakeshore.

It is unknown if he survived the war.
