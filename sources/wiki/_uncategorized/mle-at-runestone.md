Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Yohn Royce, Lord of Runestone, hosts a small mêlée with squires being the only participants. Bronze Yohn intends for Harrold Hardyng, the ward of Lady Anya Waynwood and the heir of Lord Robert Arryn, to be the victor. After Harry the Heir wins the competition, he receives knighthood from Lord Royce.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Ser Harrold upsets Alayne Stone when they meet at the Gates of the Moon. When Alayne encounters Ser Lothor Brune, Lothor refers to Harry as "some upjumped squire".
