Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Albett is in the same group of recruits as Jon Snow when he comes to the Wall. Albett is given the nickname "Pimple" by Ser Alliser Thorne. After Jon intervenes during a practice session between Halder and Samwell Tarly, Alliser sends Albett and Rast to help Halder beat Jon. However, Pypar and Grenn aid Jon, and after a relatively quick fight, Albett yields after watching his two comrades fall.

After swearing his vows, Albett is assigned to the builders.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Albett is stationed at Castle Black.
