Character[ ]

According to her sister, Lady Sam, Sansara was "spirited and strong" and "read more books than half the maesters in the Citadel."

History[ ]

Sansara was the daughter of Lord Donald Tarly and Lady Jeyne Rowan. Her sister, Samantha, married Lord Ormund Hightower and then his son, Lord Lyonel.

After the death of Queen Jaehaera Targaryen in 133 AC, Lady Sam boldly wrote a letter proposing that King Aegon III Targaryen should marry both her sister Sansara and her step-daughter, Bethany Hightower.
