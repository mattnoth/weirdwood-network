Womb of the World

Essos west of the Bone Mountains and the location of the Womb of the World

The Womb of the World is a large lake that lies just west of Vaes Dothrak and the Mother of Mountains within the Dothraki sea of central Essos. It is a holy place to the Dothraki people.

Geography[ ]

  

The Womb of the World, by  Zach Graves © Fantasy Flight Games

The Womb of the World has cool and clean waters and is surrounded by a fringe of tall reeds. The Dothraki believe it has no bottom, though the lake bottom is actually muddy. A river runs from the lake north through the Kingdom of the Ifequevron to the Shivering Sea.

Culture[ ]

According to Dothraki legends, the first man emerged from the depths of the Womb of the World, riding on the back of the first horse a thousand thousand years ago. Merchant caravans are allowed to visit the Western Market of Vaes Dothrak as long as they do not profane the Mother of Mountains or the Womb of the World.

A Game of Thrones[ ]

  

Daenerys Targaryen at the Womb of the World, by Xia Taptara © Fantasy Flight Games

After eating the heart of a stallion as part of a ceremony for her child, Daenerys Targaryen rides to the lake and bathes naked to remove the blood. Afterwards she has sex with Khal Drogo on the grassy shore.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Within the House of the Undying in Qarth, Daenerys has a vision of a line of naked crones creeping from a great lake beneath the Mother of Mountains and kneeling before her.
