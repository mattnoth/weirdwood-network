## Full Text

The Land of the Shrykes is a plain in the Further East of Essos. It is bordered by the Bleeding Sea to the west, the Five Forts and the Mountains of the Morn to the south, Bonetown and the Dry Deep to the east, and K'Dath to the north.

The land is said to be a dangerous place populated by shrieking monsters.
