## Full Text

Gawen Wylde was a knight from House Wylde and the master-at-arms at Storm's End during Robert's Rebellion.

History[ ]

During Robert's Rebellion, when the siege of Storm's End was at its worst, Ser Gawen and three other knights tried to sneak out of Storm's End through a postern gate to surrender. However, the four knights were intercepted and captured. Stannis Baratheon, who had command of the castle, ordered them flung from the walls using the catapults. As they were being strapped down, Maester Cressen suggested that they not send them to their deaths, as the remaining rations were low and there was no need to waste good meat.

Gawen was sent to the cells where he later died shortly before Storm's End received food from the smuggler Davos.
