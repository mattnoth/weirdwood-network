## Full Text

Eysen is a place in the Seven Kingdoms; its location has not been revealed. The far-traveled singer Alaric comes from Eysen.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

Eysen is a reference by George R. R. Martin to a friend, writer Phyllis Eisenstein. A Storm of Swords is dedicated to Phyllis, "who made [George] put the dragons in."
