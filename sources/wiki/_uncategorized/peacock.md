Culture[ ]

A peacock is included in the arms of House Serrett.

Salladhor Saan has a green cap decorated with a fan of peacock feathers.

History[ ]

A peacock roasted in its plumage was served at the wedding tourney at Whitewalls.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Roast peacocks stuffed with dates are served at the wedding of King Joffrey I Baratheon and Margaery Tyrell.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Peacocks in their plumage are served at the Gates of the Moon.
