Appearance[ ]

Dick wears his worldly wealth upon his person and a lord's ransom in golden arm rings. 

History[ ]

Dick is a Westerosi exile. His surname Cole is of a house that was once prominent in the histories of the Seven Kingdoms. House Cole served as stewards to House Dondarrion in the years preceding the Dance of the Dragons, and their most prominent member was Ser Criston Cole, Lord Commander of the Kingsguard under Viserys I Targaryen and Aegon II Targaryen. By the present day, their arms are obscure, and they may be extinct. Dick may or may not be of House Cole, as in the free companies a man can call himself whatever he chooses, and not all are genuine. He may be related to another serjeant in the company, Will Cole.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Dick is present during Jon Connington’s rendezvous with the Golden Company three miles south of Volon Therys. Like the rest of the company, he swears his allegiance to Aegon Targaryen before they invade Westeros. 

He is one of the nine men present during Jon Connington and the Golden Company's war council at Griffin's Roost.
