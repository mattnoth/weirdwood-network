## Full Text

Harren is a male name in Westeros, especially common among the ironborn. It may refer to:

Harren Botley, eldest son of Lord Sawane Botley.

Harren Half-Hoare, an ironborn raider.

Harren Hoare, called Harren the Black, King of the Iron Islands and founder of Harrenhal.

Harren the Red, an outlaw and bandit roaming the riverlands during the reign of King Aenys I Targaryen, who claimed to be a grandson of Harren the Black.
