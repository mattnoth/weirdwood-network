Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Maddy is one of the servants assigned to tend to Lord Robert Arryn's needs. According to Maddy, Mord took three fingers from Marillion's hands.

When the Lords Declarant visit the Eyrie, Maddy prepares Lord Petyr Baelish's solar for the newcomers.

Maddy stays behind to help prepare Lord Robert for his descent to the Gates of the Moon after all the other servants have departed. Sansa Stark learns from Maddy that Mya Stone is the bastard daughter of the late King Robert I Baratheon and is not a maid.
