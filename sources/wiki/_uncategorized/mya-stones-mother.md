History[ ]

Robert Baratheon fathered Mya Stone on a common woman while he and Eddard Stark were living at the Eyrie as wards of Lord Jon Arryn. Robert made daily visits to the babe Mya, sometimes with Eddard, even after he lost interest in her mother. Robert never publicly acknowledged Mya, however.

Mya has been told stories about the Giant's Lance by her mother.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

When they reach Snow while traveling to the Eyrie, Mya Stone mentions her mother to Catelyn Stark.

Quotes[ ]

My mother says that hundreds of years ago, this was where the snow began. It was always white above here, and the ice never melted. I can't remember ever seeing snow this far down the mountain, but maybe it was that way once, in the olden times.

—Mya Stone to Catelyn Stark
