Appearance and Character[ ]

Monford is handsome with long, fair hair. He sometimes wears sea-green silk with a white gold seahorse brooch.

Monford favors aggressive maneuvers in warfare. The lowborn Ser Davos Seaworth believes that the highborn Lord Velaryon would never confide in him.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

As a vassal of Dragonstone, Monford supports Stannis Baratheon's claim to the Iron Throne. However, he threatens to take his forces home if they do not strike soon. Lord Monford is present when Melisandre burns statues of the Seven taken from the sept at Dragonstone; Ser Davos Seaworth notices that Monford watches Stannis rather than the conflagration.

Monford wants Stannis to sail from Dragonstone to King's Landing to challenge King Joffrey I Baratheon, but Stannis instead sails to Shipbreaker Bay against his brother, Renly Baratheon. During the siege of Storm's End, Stannis thinks that Lord Velaryon wants to assault the castle instead of starving it.

During the Battle of the Blackwater, Lord Velaryon's Pride of Driftmark crashes between two Lannister river runners, but is later set ablaze with wildfire. Aurane Waters, Monford's half brother, kneels to Joffrey after the battle.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

It is reported by Lord Alester Florent that Lord Monford burned with Pride of Driftmark on the Blackwater Rush. He is succeeded by his young son, Monterys Velaryon.
