Brandon Stark, known as Brandon the Boisterous, was the Lord of Winterfell and the head of House Stark during the first half of the first century after Aegon's Conquest.

History[ ]

Brandon succeeded Torrhen Stark as Lord of Winterfell and Warden of the North, making him the second Stark to hold these titles.

Family[ ]

His relation to his predecessor Lord Torrhen Stark is unknown. However, Torrhen was known to have an unknown number of sons making it likely for Brandon to be one of Torrhen's sons.

Lynara Stark, the third and final wife of Lord Cregan Stark, was a descendant of a younger son of Brandon.
