Range[ ]

Great schools of cod and black cod are found in Ironman's Bay, and the fish is common in Lorath Bay. The western Shivering Sea has rich fishing grounds for cod and herring,  and Ibbenese hunt for cod in the eastern reaches of the sea. 

Culture[ ]

Salt cod and pickled cod are common but not especially popular meals, with fried cod being preferred instead. Chunks of cod are included in sister's stew.

House Codd wears brooches in the shape of silver codfish.

Shoals of herring and great codfish are painted between the tall arched windows of the Merman's Court in the New Castle of White Harbor.

History[ ]

When Lord Franklyn Farman attempted to prevent his sister Elissa from sailing away from Fair Isle, onlookers threw him into a ship's hold filled with fresh-caught cod.

Corlys Velaryon sailed the Cod Queen from Driftmark to Dragonstone when he was sixteen.

During the Dance of the Dragons, Lord Larys Strong helped King Aegon II Targaryen escape the fall of King's Landing. Ser Marston Waters smuggled the king to Dragonstone on a fishing skiff loaded with codfish.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

The fool Patchface waves a cod like a scepter during Stannis Baratheon's feast at Dragonstone.

Cod is among the seafood brought to the harvest feast at Winterfell by Wyman Manderly, Lord of White Harbor.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Salted cod is served at the feast hosted by Victarion Greyjoy during the kingsmoot on Old Wyk.

Arya Stark sees cod wives selling the fish at the fishmarket of Braavos. When Quill offers to teach her how a woman kisses, Tagganaro smacks him with a codfish.

Orphans living at the inn at the crossroads quarrel over codfish.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Prince Quentyn Martell sees cod sold at Fishermonger's Square in Volantis.

Lord Manderly brings a monstrous codfish for the wedding of Ramsay Bolton and Arya Stark. Cod cakes are served during the wedding feast in the Great Hall of Winterfell.

The great cog Noble Lady, carrying salt cod, whale oil, and pickled herring, is captured by Iron Victory in the Stepstones.
