## Summary

Branda Stark was a noblewoman from House Stark during the first half of the third century after Aegon's Conquest. She was the eldest daughter of Rodrik Stark and his wife, Arya Flint. She had a younger sister, Lyarra Stark, the mother of Lord Eddard Stark.
