## Full Text

Donald Tarly was a Lord of Horn Hill and head of House Tarly during the reign of King Viserys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

Lord Donald was married to Lady Jeyne Rowan. They had at least two daughters, Samantha and Sansara.

As Samantha and Sansara lived during the Dance of the Dragons and shortly after, Lord Donald may have been the father of Alan Tarly, who was head of House Tarly during the Dance.
