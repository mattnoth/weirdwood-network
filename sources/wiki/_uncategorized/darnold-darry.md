## Full Text

Darnold Darry was a Lord of Darry and head of House Darry during the reign of King Maegor I Targaryen.

History[ ]

After King Maegor I exterminated House Harroway in 44 AC, he divided up the Harroway holdings among his remaining supporters. Harrenhal was granted to Lord Walton Towers, Lord Harroway's Town was given to Lord Alton Butterwell, and the rest of the Harroway holdings were given to Lord Darnold.

Lord Darry, possibly Darnold, attended the Golden Wedding in 49 AC and approved of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

Notes[ ]

↑ The Sons of the Dragon named him Dormand Darry. In Fire & Blood, he is renamed to Darnold Darry.
