## Full Text

Lady Fell was the Lady of Felwood and the head of House Fell during the reigns of kings Viserys I and Aegon II Targaryen. Her given name is unknown.

In the television adaptation House of the Dragon, she is portrayed by Miriam Lucia.

History[ ]

At the start of the Dance of the Dragons, Lady Fell was arrested and put in the Red Keep's dungeons by Aegon II Targaryen's supporters for supporting his half-sister, Princess Rhaenyra Targaryen. When given the choice between swearing her allegiance to Aegon II or being killed by the King's Justice, Lady Fell was one of the nobles who chose to keep their sworn words and were beheaded for it. Their heads were mounted on spikes above the city’s gates.

Family[ ]

Lady Fell's relation to Ser Willis Fell, a Kingsguard loyal to Aegon II Targaryen, is unknown.

Blood of Dragons[ ]

In the approved on-line roleplaying game Blood of Dragons created by Elio García and Linda Antonsson, she is given the name "Alora Fell", and she is confirmed to be the mother of Willis Fell. However, these details are not considered canonical, and are provided on this article for informational purposes only.
