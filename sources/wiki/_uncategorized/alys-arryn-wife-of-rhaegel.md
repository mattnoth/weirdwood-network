Alys Arryn was the wife of Prince Rhaegel Targaryen, the third son of King Daeron II Targaryen.

History[ ]

Lady Alys was the third match to be made between House Targaryen and House Arryn, following the past unions of Rodrik Arryn to Princess Daella Targaryen and Daella's daughter Aemma Arryn to Viserys I Targaryen. She married Prince Rhaegel and bore him three children; the twins Aelor and Aelora, and daughter  Daenora.
