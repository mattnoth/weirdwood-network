Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When Deepwood Motte comes under attack by King Stannis Baratheon's army, Asha Greyjoy and her men flee towards their longships anchored at sea. They are overwhelmed in the wolfswood by northmen in the fight by Deepwood Motte, however, causing Asha to surrender and plead Stannis to spare her men. Stannis ransoms or uses the captains, knights, notable warriors, and nobles, but he hangs the rest of the survivors.

Asha is made Stannis's personal prisoner for his army's march on Winterfell, leaving the last nine ironborn prisoners behind at Deepwood in the care of Lady Sybelle Glover. Cromm dies of his wounds in prison and two others are too weak to travel. Tycho Nestoris ransoms the last six and uses them as an escort as he searches for Stannis.
