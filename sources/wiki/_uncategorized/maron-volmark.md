Appearance and Character[ ]

Young Lord Volmark is beardless. Victarion Greyjoy considers Maron to be callow.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Tarle the Thrice-Drowned claims that Maron, as heir to the "black line" of House Hoare, has the best claim to the Seastone Chair after the passing of King Balon IX Greyjoy. Lord Volmark, however, is one of Victarion Greyjoy's supporters at the kingsmoot on Old Wyk.

After Euron Greyjoy is chosen king, he commands the ironborn to attack the Shield Islands. During the feast celebrating the taking of the Shields, a hesitant Maron is named the new Lord of Greenshield by King Euron.
