Appearance[ ]

Overweight and with heavy legs, Marghaz tries to hide his physique behind a muscled breastplate. He also wears a lion's mask as commander of the Brazen Beasts.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Soon after Hizdahr zo Loraq marries Daenerys Targaryen, Queen of Meereen, he replaces Skahaz mo Kandaq as commander of the Brazen Beasts with his own cousin, Marghaz.

When Skahaz and Ser Barristan Selmy plot against Hizdahr, Skahaz reassures the knight that Marghaz will not be be a threat to their plans.
