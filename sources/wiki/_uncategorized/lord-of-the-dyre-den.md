## Full Text

Lord of the Dyre Den is a title held by the head of House Brune of the Dyre Den, the ruler of the Dyre Den in Crackclaw Point in the crownlands.

History[ ]

Lord Brune marched to King's Landing with Lord Manfryd Mooton, Lord Leowyn Corbray, and Ser Rennifer Crabb at the end of the Dance of the Dragons.

Known Lords of the Dyre Den[ ]

Lord Brune, a supporter of Rhaenyra Targaryen during the Dance of the Dragons.

Lord Eustace Brune, the current Lord of the Dyre Den.
