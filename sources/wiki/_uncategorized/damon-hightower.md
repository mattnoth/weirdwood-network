## Full Text

Damon Hightower, known as Damon the Devout, was a head of House Hightower and Lord of the Hightower.

History[ ]

Damon was a grandson of Lord Dorian Hightower, the first Hightower to wed an Andal princess. Damon was the first Hightower to convert to the Faith of the Seven, building the first sept in Oldtown and six elsewhere in the Kingdom of the Reach. When Damon prematurely died of a bad belly, Septon Robeson became regent for Damon's newborn son, Triston.
