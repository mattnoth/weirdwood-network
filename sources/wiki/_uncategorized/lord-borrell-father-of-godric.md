Lord Borrell was the Lord of Sweetsister, Shield of Sisterton, Master of Breakwater Castle, Keeper of the Night Lamp, and the head of House Borrell during Robert's Rebellion. 

History[ ]

During Robert's Rebellion, Lord Eddard Stark washed up on Sweetsister while trying to sail to the north to raise his banners. Lord Borrell could easily have turned Eddard over to King Aerys II Targaryen for a reward, but he let him go.
