A-D[ ]

Aidan Gillen, Petyr Baelish[ ]

Aidan Gillen is an Irish actor born April 24, 1968 in Dublin. He plays Petyr Baelish. Gillen is best known for his roles as Stuart Jones in the UK series of Queer as Folk and Tommy Carcetti in the American series The Wire. (IMDb)

Aimee Richardson, Myrcella Baratheon[ ]

Aimee Richardson is an Irish actress born on 29 December 1997 in Bangor, County Down, Northern Ireland. She plays Myrcella Baratheon in Seasons 1 and 2. Richardson has gone on to play Althea in The Sparticle Mystery, Gemma in Storyland, and Minnie in My Mother and Other Strangers. (IMDb) 

Aisling Franciosi, Lyanna Stark[ ]

Aisling Franciosi is an Irish and Italian actress born on 6 June 1993 in Italy. She plays Lyanna Stark. Franciosi has played Katie Benedetto in The Fall and Clare in The Nightingale. (IMDb)

Alexander Siddig, Doran Martell[ ]

Alexander Siddig is an English actor born November 21, 1965 in Sudan. He plays Prince Doran Martell. Siddig is best known for his role as Doctor Bashir in Star Trek: Deep Space Nine. (IMDb)

Alfie Allen, Theon Greyjoy[ ]

Alfie Allen is a English actor born September 12, 1986 in London. He plays Theon Greyjoy. Allen is the younger brother of singer Lily Allen. He has already played several minor roles in television series or films. (IMDb)

Amrita Acharia, Irri[ ]

Amrita Acharia is a Nepalese-born actress living in Norway. She plays Irri. Acharia has a small number of stage and screen roles to her name, most notably an appearance in the play Elevator, a role in the movie In the Company of Wolves and a brief role in the British drama series Casualty. (IMDb)

Andrew Wilde, Tobho Mott[ ]

Andrew Wilde is an English actor. He plays Tobho Mott. Wilde is perhaps best known for his work in the 1984 film Nineteen Eighty-Four. He also appeared in the Pete Townshend short film, White City: A Novel. (IMDb)

Andy Beckwith, Rorge[ ]

Andy Beckwith is an English actor. He plays Rorge. Beckwith is best-known for portraying the characters of Clanker in the second and third Pirates of the Caribbean films and Errol in the Guy Ritchie movie Snatch. (IMDb)

Ania Bukstein, Kinvara[ ]

Ania Bukstein is an Israeli actress, born 7 June, 1982 in Moscow, USSR (now Russia). She plays Kinvara, replacing the role of Benerro. Bukstein is known for her roles in Genius and Madam Yankelova's Fine Literature Club. (IMDb)

Annette Tierney, Nan[ ]

Annette Tierney is an actress. She plays a younger Nan. (IMDb)

Anthony Morris, Tickler[ ]

Anthony Morris is an Irish actor. He plays the Tickler. Morris has appeared in a number of TV shows, including Rough Diamond and Killinaskully. (IMDb)

Anton Lesser, Qyburn[ ]

Anton Lesser is an English actor, born 14 February 1952 in Birmingham. He plays Qyburn. Lesser is known for Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides and Miss Potter. (IMDb)

Art Parkinson, Rickon Stark[ ]

Art Parkinson is an Irish actor born in 2001 in Moville, County Dongegal. He plays Rickon Stark. (IMDb)

Bella Ramsey, Lyanna Mormont[ ]

Bella Ramsey is a British actor born in 2004 in the UK. They play Lyanna Mormont. Ramsey is known for playing Mildred Hubble in The Worst Witch. (IMDb)

Ben Crompton, Eddison Tollett[ ]

Ben Crompton is a English actor born in 1974 in Manchester. He plays Eddison Tollett. Crompton has starred in the BBC sketch comedy Man Stroke Woman, the sitcom Ideal and the films All or Nothing and 102 Dalmatians. (IMDb)

Ben Hawkey, Hot Pie[ ]

Ben Hawkey is an English actor. He plays Hot Pie. (IMDb)

Brendan McCormack, Vardis Egen[ ]

Brendan McCormack is an Irish actor. He plays Vardis Egen. McCormack appeared appeared in several theatre productions, a number of TV series, including a minor role in season one of The Tudors, and a couple of movies. (IMDb)

Brian Fortune, Othell Yarwyck[ ]

Brian Fortune is an Irish actor born in Dublin. He plays Othell Yarwyck. Fortune was trained at the Gaiety School of Acting in Dublin and played a few minor roles since. (IMDb)

Bronson Webb, Will[ ]

Bronson Webb is an English actor, born in 20 February 1983 in London. He plays Will. Webb has had minor roles in Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban and The Dark Knight. (IMDb)

Burn Gorman, Karl Tanner[ ]

Burn Gorman was born on September 1, 1974 in Hollywood, Los Angeles, California. He plays Karl Tanner, a character based on Clubfoot Karl from the novels. (IMDb)

Callum Wharry, Tommen Baratheon[ ]

Callum Wharry is a child actor from Northern Ireland. He plays Tommen Baratheon in Seasons 1 and 2. This is Wharry's first role on television. (IMDb)

Carice van Houten, Melisandre[ ]

Carice van Houten is a Dutch actress, born 5 September 1976 in Leiderdorp. She plays Melisandre. Van Houten is best known to English speaking audiences for her roles in Repo Men, Valkyrie and Black Death. She has won three Golden Calf awards for acting. (IMDb)

Charles Dance, Tywin Lannister[ ]

Charles Dance is an English actor, born 10 October 1946 in Redditch, Worcestershire. He plays Tywin Lannister. Dance is best-known for playing villains, most notably in the films The Golden Child and Last Action Hero (where he acted against Eddie Murphy and Arnold Schwarznegger respectively). He also played a henchmen of the main villain in the James Bond movie For Your Eyes Only, where he met and acted alongside fellow Thrones actor Julian Glover. (IMDb)

Charlotte Hope, Myranda[ ]

Charlotte Hope is an English actress, born 15 October 1991 in Salisbury. She plays Myranda, an original character with some similarities to the Bastard's Boys from the novels. Hope is known for playing Catherine of Aragon in The Spanish Princess. (IMDb)

Ciarán Hinds, Mance Rayder[ ]

Ciarán Hinds is a Northern Irish actor, born 9 February 1953 in Belfast. He plays Mance Rayder. Hinds is known for playing Aberforth Dumbledore in the Harry Potter film series, Sam Daily in The Woman in Black, and Firmin in The Phantom of the Opera. (IMDb)

Clive Russell, Brynden Tully[ ]

Clive Russell is an English and Scottish actor born 7 December 1945 in Hampshire, England. He plays Brynden Tully. (IMDb)

Conleth Hill, Varys[ ]

Conleth Hill is an Northern Irish actor, born 24 November 1964 in Ballycastle, County Antrim. He plays Lord Varys. Hill is a well known stage actor nominated for a Tony Award and won two Olivier awards. (IMDb)

Cordelia Hill, Young Lyanna Stark[ ]

Cordelia Hill is an actress. She plays a young Lyanna Stark. (IMDb)

Daniel Portman, Podrick Payne[ ]

Daniel Portman is a Scottish actor, born 13 February 1992 in Glasgow. He plays Podrick Payne. Portman is known for Outcast and River City. (IMDb)

David Bradley, Walder Frey[ ]

David Bradley is an English actor, born on 17 April 1942 in York, North Yorkshire. He plays Walder Frey. Bradley is known for playing Argus Filch in the Harry Potter films, Jack Marshall in Broadchurch and the First Doctor in Doctor Who. (IMDb)

David Rintoul, Aerys II Targaryen[ ]

David Rintoul is a Scottish actor born on 29 November 1948 in Aberdeen. He plays King Aerys II Targaryen. Rintoul is a film and television actor known for his roles in Dirty Dancing and Agatha Christie's Poirot. (IMDb)

Dean-Charles Chapman, Martyn Lannister and Tommen Baratheon[ ]

Dean-Charles Chapman is an English actor, born on 7 September 1997 in Essex. He plays Martyn Lannister, as well as Tommen Baratheon in Seasons 4, 5, and 6. Chapman is known for portraying Billie Elliot in the West End production of Billy Elliot the Musical and for playing the leading role in CBBC sitcom The Revolting World of Stanley Brown. (IMDb)

DeObia Oparei, Areo Hotah[ ]

DeObia Oparei is an English actor, born on 7 December 1971 in Hackney, London. He plays Areo Hotah. Oparei is best known for his roles as the Gunner in Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides and Thundarian in Your Highness. (IMDb)

Diana Rigg, Olenna Tyrell[ ]

Dame Diana Rigg was an English actress born on 20 July 1938 in Doncaster, South Yorkshire. She passed away on 10 September 2020. She played Olenna Tyrell. Rigg had an extensive career in film and theater, and won the 1994 Tony Award for Best Actress in a Play for her role as the titular character in Medea. She was known for playing Emma Peel in The Avengers and Countess Teresa di Vicenzo in On Her Majesty's Secret Service. (IMDb)

Donald Sumpter, Maester Luwin[ ]

Donald Sumpter is an English actor, born 13 February 1943 in Brixworth, Northamptonshire. He plays Maester Luwin. Sumpter is known for The Constant Gardener, Being Human, and Black Mirror. (IMDb)

E-I[ ]

Elizabeth Cadwallader, Lollys Stokeworth[ ]

Elizabeth Cadwallader is an actress. She plays Lollys Stokeworth. Cadwallader has appeared in Doctors and Holby City. (IMDb)

Ellie Kendrick, Meera Reed[ ]

Ellie Kendrick is an English actress, born 6 June 1990 in Greenwich, London. She plays Meera Reed. Kendrick is known for An Education and Cheerful Weather for the Wedding. She is also a writer. (IMDb)

Elyes Gabel, Rakharo[ ]

Elyes Gabel is an English actor, born 8 May 1983 in London. He plays Rakharo. Gabel starred as PE teacher Rob Cleaver in BBC drama, Waterloo Road and appeared in the ITV Drama Identity. (IMDb)

Emilia Clarke, Daenerys Targaryen[ ]

Emilia Clarke is an English television actress, born in 1987 in London. She plays Daenerys Targaryen. Clarke graduated from the Drama Centre in London. She had turned in a few commercials and two episodes of series before landing the role of Daenerys Targaryen, replacing Tamzin Merchant who portrayed Daenerys in the unaired pilot. She is also known for playing Qi'ra in Solo: A Star Wars Story. (IMDb)

Emun Elliott, Marillion[ ]

Emun Elliott is a Scottish actor born 28 November 1983 in Edinburgh. He plays Marillion. Elliott most recently appeared in the film Black Death. (IMDb)

Eros Vlahos, Lommy Greenhands[ ]

Eros Vlahos is an English actor, born 1995, in London. He plays Lommy Greenhands. Vlahos is best-known for his role as Cyril in Nanny McPhee and the Big Bang. (IMDb)

Esme Bianco, Ros[ ]

Esmé Bianco is an English actress, born 25 May 1982 in St. Albans, Hertfordshire. She plays Ros, an original character with some similarities to Chataya from the novels. Bianco is a model and burlesque/cabaret performer, who has appeared in a number of short films and music videos, as well as performing at live events including the Brit Awards. (IMDb)

Essie Davis, Lady Crane[ ]

Essie Davis is an Australian actress, born 19 January 1970 in Hobart, Tasmania. She plays Lady Crane, renamed from Lady Stork in the novels. Davis is known for Miss Fisher's Murder Mysteries and The Babadook. (IMDb)

Eugene Simon, Lancel Lannister[ ]

Eugene Simon is an English actor, born 11 June 1992. He plays Lancel Lannister. Simon is best-known for playing the younger version of Ben-Hur in the 2010 mini-series and the younger version of Casanova in the 2005 TV series. (IMDb)

Faye Marsay, the Waif[ ]

Faye Marsay is an English actress, born 30 December 1986 in Middlesbrough, Cleveland. She plays the Waif. Marsay is known for playing Queen Anne Neville in The White Queen, Steph in Pride and Sybil in Darkest Hour. (IMDb)

Finn Jones, Loras Tyrell[ ]

Finn Jones is an English actor, born 24 March 1988. He plays Loras Tyrell. Jones is best-known for the recurring role of Jamie in the successful British soap opera Hollyoaks and its spin-off, Hollyoaks Later. He has appeared in Doctors and The Bill and Doctor Who spin-off The Sarah-Jane Adventures as Santiago Jones. He currently plays the titular character Iron Fist in the Netflix and Marvel show. (IMDb)

Fintan McKeown, Amory Lorch[ ]

Fintan McKeown is an Irish actor. He plays Amory Lorch. McKeown is noted for appearing in episodes of Merlin and Star. (IMDb)

Forbes Keir-Bucknall, Lorren[ ]

Forbes Keir-Bucknall is Scottish character actor, born 29 May 1965 in Dundee. He plays Lorren. Keir-Bucknall has appeared in numerous movies and TV series, including the Michael Caine movie Harry Brown, where he appeared alongside fellow Game of Thrones actors Iain Glen, Liam Cunningham and David Bradley. (IMDb)

Francis Magee, Yoren[ ]

Francis Magee is an Irish actor born in Dublin. He plays Yoren. Magee is best-known for his role as Liam in British soap opera EastEnders. (IMDb)

Gary Oliver, Ternesio Terys[ ]

Gary Oliver is an English actor, born 19 November 1966 in London. He plays Ternesio Terys. Oliver is known for The Bible and New Blood. (IMDb)

Gemma Whelan, Yara Greyjoy[ ]

Gemma Whelan is an English actress. She plays Yara Greyjoy, renamed from Asha in the novels. Whelan is a comedian who has appeared in films such as Gulliver's Travels and The Wolfman. In 2010 she won the Funny Women Variety Act at the Leicester Square Theatre for her character of Chastity Butterworth. She has gone on to star in other television series, such as playing Marian Lister in Gentleman Jack and Eunice in The End of the F***ing World. (IMDb)

Gethin Anthony, Renly Baratheon[ ]

Gethin Anthony is an English actor, born 9 October 1983 in Stratford-upon-Avon. He plays Renly Baratheon. Anthony has recently appeared in Ten Days to War and Doctors. (IMDb)

Gwendoline Christie, Brienne of Tarth[ ]

Gwendoline Christie is an English actress. She plays Brienne of Tarth. Christie has played several minor roles in films such as The Imaginarium of Doctor Parnassus. Game of Thrones is her first major role. She has gone onto play other major roles since her success in Game of Thrones, such as Captain Phasma in Star Wars: Episode VII - The Force Awakens and Star Wars: Episode VIII - The Last Jedi. (IMDb)

Hafþór Júlíus Björnsson, Gregor Clegane[ ]

Hafþór Júlíus Björnsson is a Strongman from Iceland. He plays Gregor Clegane. Hafþór was cast as Gregor Clegane for the fourth season of Game of Thrones in August 2013, replacing Ian Whyte in the role. He is the third actor to take up the role, after Conan Stevens and Whyte (both of whom are 7' 1" tall) appeared in the first and second seasons of the show respectively, but this is his first ever acting job. (IMDb)
