Prelude[ ]

After Ser Jaime Lannister defeats the Pipers and Vances in a battle in the hills below the Golden Tooth, Lord Clement Piper falls back to Riverrun. Jaime and  his father, Lord Tywin Lannister, invade the riverlands with separate hosts. Jaime marches directly on Riverrun, where Ser Edmure Tully has assembled many of his father Hoster Tully's bannermen and knights. Edmure's force is diminished on account of the men he has sent to guard the borders and the Golden Tooth in response to Lannister raiding. The forces of Lord Walder Frey are also not present.

Battle[ ]

Jaime meets the Tullys under the walls of Riverrun. Securing a victory, Jaime puts the rivermen to rout. Edmure and many of his knights and bannermen are taken captive. Lord Tytos Blackwood manages to lead some of the surviving men back within Riverrun, where Jaime places them under siege.

Aftermath[ ]

Tywin leads a second, larger Lannister army against the companies of men Edmure had dispersed along his borders to guard against Ser Gregor Clegane's raiding. Tywin and Ser Kevan Lannister are able to destroy them piecemeal before they can regroup. They then attack several castles of the river lords, including Raventree Hall, Pinkmaiden Castle, Stone Hedge, Darry, and Harrenhal. The westermen assume a position at the ruby ford, where they can both block any Stark or Arryn attempt to relieve Riverrun and can maintain contact with King's Landing.
