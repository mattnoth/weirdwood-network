## Full Text

This Archon of Tyrosh, was the ruler of Tyrosh in the time preceding the War of the Ninepenny Kings.

History[ ]

The ruling Archon of Tyrosh was deposed when the Band of Nine and its armies sacked Tyrosh at the behest of one of its members, the merchant prince Alequo Adarys, was then installed as the ruler of the city. Adarys reigned until 266 AC, when he was poisoned by his queen. Afterward, the legitimate Archon was restored to power.
