Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lady Shyra supports Renly Baratheon during the War of the Five Kings. Ser Cortnay Penrose acknowledges a "Lord Errol" at the parley beneath the walls of Storm's End who supports Stannis Baratheon, implying Shyra has died before this event.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Sebastion Errol has become Lord of Haystack Hall after the death of Shyra.
