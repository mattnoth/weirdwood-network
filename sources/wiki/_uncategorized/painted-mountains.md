Painted Mountains

Slaver's Bay and the location of the Painted Mountains

The Painted Mountains are a long range of low, rounded mountains to the north of the Valyrian peninsula. The mountains are naturally banded in various hues of stones, thus leading to the name.

Geography[ ]

The Painted Mountains separate the Dothraki sea from Slaver's Bay and the Sea of Sighs in central Essos. The Demon Road runs through the southern stretch of the mountains. West of the Painted Mountains are the Rhoyne and its tributaries.
