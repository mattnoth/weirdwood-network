Appearance[ ]

Webber is short and muscular, with tattoos of spiders across his head, chest, and arms. 

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Webber fights in the siege of Astapor as a member of the Windblown. Webber and the other Westerosi members of the company are then told by their leader, the Tattered Prince, to go over to Daenerys Targaryen, the Queen of Meereen. The Tattered Prince tells Webber that when he meets Daenerys, the excuse he should give for deserting is that he has some claims to lost lands in Westeros.
