## Full Text

The Battle of the Borderland was a major battle involving four of the nine Free Cities of Essos in 96 AC. Lys, Myr and Tyrosh put their differences aside and made alliance to drove Volantis from the Disputed Lands. The alliance defeated the Volantenes in the Battle of the Borderland.

Aftermath[ ]

After the battle, the three victorious cities forged an "eternal alliance", and formed a strong new power in Essos: the Triarchy.
