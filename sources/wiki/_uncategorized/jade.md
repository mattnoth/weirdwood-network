Culture[ ]

The Jade Gates separate the Jade Sea from the Summer Sea. Jade Compendium is a book about the lands of the Jade Sea. Only the god-emperor is allowed to wear the traditional gowns of cloth-of-gold, green pearls, and jade in Yi Ti.

The massive chairs of the Pureborn of Qarth are decorated with goldwork and studded with amber, onyx, lapis lazuli, and jade. A bronze arch in the city depicts two snakes mating, and their scales are made of delicate flakes of jade, obsidian, and lapis.

Within the Great Sept of Baelor, a chamber has carvings of the Seven with eyes of onyx, jade, malachite, and yellow moonstone.

The buttons on Salladhor Saan's coat are carved jade monkeys. Xaro Xhoan Daxos decorates his nose with rubies, opals, and flakes of jade, and he owns goblets of jade and gold. Leo Tyrell pins his half cape with a jade rose. Garin of the orphans wears a jade stud in his ear. Illyrio Mopatis has jade among his various rings. The beard of the merling king on the helm of Ser Marlon Manderly is decorated in jet and jade.

Squares on cyvasse boards can be made of jade, carnelian, and lapis lazuli, and game pieces can be made of ivory and jade.

Wildfire is also known as the jade demon.

History[ ]

The Jade Emperor was a legendary ruler of the Great Empire of the Dawn. The jade-green emperors were a dynasty in Yi Ti.

The High Septon presented Aenys I Targaryen with a golden crown inlaid in jade and pearl with the faces of the Seven.

House Velaryon became rich when Corlys Velaryon returned from the Jade Sea with jade, spice, and silk.

Prince Daemon Targaryen gave to Princess Rhaenyra Targaryen a jade tiara alleged to have belonged to the Empress of Leng.

Jade from Qarth was popular in King's Landing during the Lysene Spring.

Ser Eustace Osgrey owned an old silver cup which displayed the lion of House Osgrey in flakes of gold and jade.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Daenerys Targaryen has a vision of ghosts with eyes of amethyst, jade, opal, and tourmaline.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

  

The crown of Renly Baratheon, by David Griffith © Fantasy Flight Games

Renly Baratheon's crown is a ring of gold roses with a stag's head made of jade.

The Tourmaline Brotherhood gives Daenerys a crown with three dragon heads carved from ivory, jade, and onyx. Xaro Xhoan Daxos gives her a thousand miniature knights carved of amber, amethyst, beryl, jade, onyx, opal, and tourmaline, with armor of silver and gold.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser Loras Tyrell, having joined the Kingsguard, clasps his cloak with a golden rose brooch with jade leaves.

Tyrion Lannister sends Alayaya a necklace of silver and jade and a pair of matching bracelets to make amends for the girl's beating by order of Cersei Lannister.

Twelve jade monkeys are among the trade goods that Daenerys Targaryen exchanges with the Good Masters for the Unsullied in Astapor.

The seven-sided golden chalice given by Lord Mace Tyrell to King Joffrey I Baratheon has a blue jade falcon to represent House Arryn.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Young Ermesande Hayford's cloth-of-gold gown is decorated with the sigil of House Hayford done in tiny beads of jade.

Jade manticores are among the plundered items used by Euron Greyjoy to bribe ironborn.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

  

An ornate jade fan is a gift for the widow of the waterfront. By Preston Stone © FFG

When Ser Jorah Mormont and Tyrion Lannister visit the widow of the waterfront at the Merchant's House in Volantis, they see she has been given a gift of an ornate fan carved of jade leaves so thin they are translucent.

Among the tributes collected from the free folk passing through the Wall is a green jade comb.

One of the Wise Masters of Yunkai that meets with Hizdahr zo Loraq in Meereen has an ornate breastplate inlaid with erotic scenes done in jet and jade and mother-of-pearl.
