Scenes[ ]

In one popular scene a fat merchant defecates on the head of the Sealord of Braavos as his gold-and-purple barge passes underneath.

Izembaro loves to give the Sealord's speech from the play, part of which goes, "here the last Titan yet stands, astride the stony shoulders of his brothers."
