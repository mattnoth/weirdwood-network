Distribution[ ]

Cave bears dwell on caverns in the Mountains of the Moon.

Biology[ ]

Cave bears are carnivorous. Like shadowcats, they also attack travelers passing through the mountains.

Recent events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Sandor Clegane is warned by the elder of a village at the feet of the Mountains of the Moon of the many dangers of trying to cross the mountains, including cave bears.

Behind the scenes[ ]

The cave bear of the A Song of Ice and Fire novels appears to be based on the Ursus spelaeus, an extinct genus of bear.
