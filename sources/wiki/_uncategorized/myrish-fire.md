Usage[ ]

Myrish fire is often used to clean a wound and burns on contact, and it can be used along with other healing herbs. Myrish fire wines are drunk to help with digestion.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Syrio Forel treats the cat scratch wounds of Arya Stark with Myrish fire.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Brown Bernarr carries Myrish fire among his bags of healing herbs during the great ranging.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Magister Illyrio Mopatis has jars of Myrish fire wine to aid with digestion.

Quotes[ ]
