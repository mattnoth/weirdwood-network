Harry is a name and a nickname in Westeros.

Characters named Harry include:

Harry Merrell, one of Jaime Lannister's men

Harry Rivers, the bastard of Jonos Bracken

Harry Sawyer, a supporter of Renly Baratheon

Harry Strickland, the captain-general in command of the Golden Company

Characters nicknamed Harry include:

Harrold Hardyng, the heir of Robert Arryn

Harrion Karstark, son of Rickard Karstark
