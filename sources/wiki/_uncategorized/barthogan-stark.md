## Full Text

Barthogan Stark, also called Barth Blacksword, was the Lord of Winterfell and the head of House Stark during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen. He was the fourthborn son of Lord Cregan Stark, the third by his last wife, Lady Lynara Stark.

History[ ]

Barthogan became Lord of Winterfell after the death of his brother Jonnel, who had no issue. Barthogan was killed when Skagosi rose up against Winterfell during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen.

Barthogan is entombed in the crypts of Winterfell. He was succeeded as Lord of Winterfell by his younger brother, Brandon.
