## Summary

Lyarra Stark was a noblewoman from House Stark during the reign of Kings Aegon V, Jaehaerys II and Aerys II Targaryen. She was a daughter of Rodrik Stark and Arya Flint, and was the younger sister of Branda Stark.

History[ ]

Lyarra married her first cousin once removed, Rickard Stark, the Lord of Winterfell and Warden of the North. Together they had four children: Brandon, Eddard, Lyanna, and Benjen Stark.
