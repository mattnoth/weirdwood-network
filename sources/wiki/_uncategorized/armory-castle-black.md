Layout[ ]

The armory is warmed by chunks of coal which burn in iron braziers at each end of the long room. Equipment like swords and scabbards can be hung from hooks in the stone wall, and spears are stored on racks. The room also contains benches. The armory has a whetstone and bellows for the armorer, Donal Noye.

A steep flight of stone steps descends from the back of the armory to the wormways, subterranean tunnels which connect the towers and keeps of Castle Black.

Below the armory is one of the vaults of Castle Black, a cavernous stone cellar divided by two rows of square stone pillars. The hall has barrel-vaulted ceilings, tables, and great casks of wine and ale along the walls. A potbellied kettle can be found by a hearth in the corner.

Donel has a pair of modest rooms behind the armory, past the forge.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Fellow recruits of the Night's Watch, angry at Jon Snow's treatment of them, attack him in the armory. Donal Noye, the armorer of Castle Black, breaks up the fighting and then explains to Jon that he had been acting like a bully. Jon then sometimes assists one-armed Donal in the armory, spinning the whetstone or pumping the bellows.

When Samwell Tarly arrives at Castle Black, Ser Alliser Thorne, the master-at-arms, has Sam don black equipment from the armory.

After Alliser allows his recruits to become brothers of the Night's Watch, Dareon retrieves a skin of sour red wine so the youths can celebrate.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser Endrew Tarth, the new master-at-arms, trains recruits outside of the armory, with Donal watching from the doorway. 

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Seeing smoke rising from the armory's chimney when he returns to Castle Black from the great ranging, Jon meets with Donal in the building. In preparation for the attack on Castle Black by Styr and his Thenns, Donal empties the armory to arm the people of Mole's Town. Jon sees wildlings atop the armory's roof during the fighting. Donal is slain fighting Mag Mar Tun Doh Weg during the battle beneath the Wall.

Jon spends most of his time at the armory and in the practice yard. In the aftermath of the fighting, the election of a new Lord Commander of the Night's Watch is held in the vault below the armory.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Jon, the new Lord Commander, takes up residence in Donal's old chambers.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Since the common hall was burned by wildlings, the Night's Watch take their meals in the cellar below the armory.

Jon realizes that Stannis Baratheon intends to clean out the armory for his campaigns. Jon offers some older equipment, but mentions that Castle Black has lost its armorer.

Jon meets with his officers and then with Tormund Giantsbane in his chambers.
