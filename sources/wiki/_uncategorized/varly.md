## Full Text

Varly is a member of the household guard of Lord Eddard Stark at Winterfell.

In the television adaptation Game of Thrones, Varly is portrayed by Graham Charles.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Varly accompanies Lord Eddard Stark to King's Landing when Eddard is named Hand of the King to Robert I Baratheon.

Varlys and Jacks make up the escort for Eddard when he visits the armorer Tobho Mott and discovers Gendry, one of King Robert's bastard children.

Varly's throat is slashed by Janos Slynt when Eddard is arrested in the throne room.
