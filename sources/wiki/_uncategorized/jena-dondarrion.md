## Full Text

Jena Dondarrion was a member of House Dondarrion during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen.

History[ ]

Although the Marcher lords mistrusted King Daeron II and his heir, Jena married the Prince of Dragonstone, Baelor Targaryen. Together they had two sons: Valarr and Matarys Targaryen.
