Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

  

Catelyn Stark on the prow of Storm Dancer, by Katherine Dinger © Fantasy Flight Games

The comatose Bran Stark has a dream of his mother, Catelyn Stark, and a seasick Ser Rodrik Cassel sailing through the Bite.

Catelyn and Rodrik take the Storm Dancer from White Harbor to King's Landing, but Rodrik becomes seasick during the journey. During a storm near Dragonstone, the knight clings to a rope when nearly swept overboard and has to be rescued by three crewmembers.

When the galley arrives in the capital, Catelyn rewards each of the oarmen with a silver stag. Moreo Tumitis offers to hold onto his crew's silver until they return to Tyrosh, but Catelyn insists on personally paying each oarman. She also gives a copper to two crewmembers who bring the travelers' chests to an old inn on Eel Alley.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Storm Dancer is in White Harbor when Lord Davos Seaworth arrives on Merry Midwife. Davos sees one of the ship's oarsmen drinking in the Lazy Eel.
