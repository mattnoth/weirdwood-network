## Full Text

"The Burning Mill" is the third episode of season two of the HBO medieval fantasy television series House of the Dragon. Running 67 minutes long, it premiered on June 30, 2024. The episode was written by writer and executive producer David Hancock and directed by returning director Geeta Vasant Patel. Music was composed by Ramin Djawadi.

The title of the episode takes its name from the Battle of the Burning Mill.
