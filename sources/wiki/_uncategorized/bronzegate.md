History[ ]

Monfryd V Durrandon, a Storm King, defeated the Holy Brotherhood of the Andals in the Battle of Bronzegate.

During Aegon's Conquest, the Last Storm was fought in the hills south of Bronzegate.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Brienne of Tarth passes Bronzegate while traveling from Tarth to Highgarden.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Ralph Buckler hosts the Dornish retinue of Oberyn Martell during their journey north to King's Landing.
