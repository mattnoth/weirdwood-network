Appearance and Character[ ]

Mebble is called Pinkeye because of his runny eyes. He is an alcoholic who drinks ale at breakfast and wine in the evening. The bellicose servant threatens to hit everyone, but he never follows through on his threats. The older man dislikes steps.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

After Weese's death at Harrenhal, Pinkeye is put in charge of the servants, one of whom is Arya Stark under the name "Nan". When captured northmen and rivermen are brought to Harrenhal by Vargo Hoat, Pinkeye emerges from a stairwell to observe the newcomers. Arya evades Pinkeye before the fall of Harrenhal. The next morning he reports to his servants under the Kingspyre that Ser Amory Lorch's garrison was killed by the Brave Companions.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

On his way to lifting the siege of Riverrun, Ser Jaime Lannister returns to Harrenhal and puts the castle back to order. Ben Blackthumb, Pia, and a cook are the only servants from Lady Shella Whent's time who are still around. Pinkeye's fate is unknown.
