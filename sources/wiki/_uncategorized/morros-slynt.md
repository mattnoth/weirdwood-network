Appearance[ ]

Morros is frog-faced.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

When Janos Slynt is named Lord of Harrenhal, two of his three sons accompany him before the Iron Throne.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Morros, a new-made squire, wears a checkered black-and-gold cloak over black armor inlaid with golden scrollwork. He rides in the tourney on King Joffrey's name day, but he is unhorsed by Ser Balon Swann in the second tilt of the day.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Upon Janos's banishment to the Wall, Morros has become a lord.

Sansa Stark spies Morros getting beaten in the yard by Osfryd Kettleblack. Lord Varys warns Tyrion Lannister that Morros and his brothers would gladly inform on Tyrion to avenge their father. 

Morros testifies at the trial of Tyrion for the death of King Joffrey I Baratheon that he had seen Tyrion gather the poisoned chalice off the floor and poured the remaining contents on the floor.
