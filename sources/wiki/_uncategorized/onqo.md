## Full Text

Onqo was a Dothraki khal who lived during the Century of Blood.

History[ ]

Onqo led a particularly huge khalasar.

Onqo began to make incursions into the Ibbenese territories in Essos, only to be killed by the Ibbenese in an epic battle that destroyed his khalasar. His grandson was Khal Dhako.
