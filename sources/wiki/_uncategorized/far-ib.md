Far Ib or Far Ibben is an island settled by the Ibbenese. The islands lies north of the Krazaaj Zasqa and more than a hundred leagues southeast of Ib.

Geography[ ]

Far Ib is the second largest of the isles of Ibben, a bleaker and poorer place than Ib, but similary forested and mountainous. Ib Sar is the only town on the island.
