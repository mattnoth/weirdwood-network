Appearance and Character[ ]

Wallace is long-faced and lantern-jawed, and has stringy brown hair and a pinched nose. He talks with a stutter. Wallace is not as muscular or tall as his nephew, Ser Roland Waynwood. 

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lady Anya Waynwood is present in the High Hall of the Eyrie, surrounded by her sons, when Tyrion Lannister demands a trial by battle to prove his innocence of the claims against him.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Wallace is knighted in 300 AC.

Ser Wallace arrives with his mother, Lady Anya, his nephew, Ser Roland Waynwood, and Ser Harrold Hardyng, at the Gates of the Moon for the tourney held to decide on the first eight members of the Brotherhood of Winged Knights. Upon arrival, Wallace is met by Alayne Stone and Myranda Royce. Wallace and Roland joke about each other, and Alayne courteously helps Wallace when the newly-made knight stutters.

Alayne dances with Wallace at the feast that evening. Wallace stutters again when trying to comment on other guests, so Alayne takes over the conversation to spare him from embarrassment.
