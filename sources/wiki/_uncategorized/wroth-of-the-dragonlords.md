## Summary

Wroth of the Dragonlords is a mummer's play written by Phario Forel. It is the first play Forel ever wrote. The play presumably deals with the history of the Second Spice War between the Valyrian Freehold and the Rhoynar, in which the dragonlords crushed Garin the Great's army with their dragons. In the play, Prince Garin gives a speech on the eve of battle.
