History[ ]

Beron became Lord of Winterfell after his older brother, Rodwell, died without issue.

Lord Beron gathered swords to throw Lord Dagon Greyjoy's ironmen back into the sea and made common cause with the Lord of Casterly Rock for it. According to a semi-canon source, he was mortally wounded in the ensuing clash, however, and House Stark faced a crisis of succession involving several Stark widows and possibly their children. Despite the crisis, Beron's eldest son, Donnor, succeeded him as Lord of Winterfell and Warden of the North.

Beron is buried in the crypts of Winterfell.

Family[ ]

Beron was the middle child of Lord Brandon Stark and Lady Alys Karstark. Beron had an older brother, Rodwell, and a younger sister, Arsa Stark, as well as an older bastard half-brother, Lonnel Snow. Lord Beron was married to Lady Lorra Royce, with whom he had seven children; Donnor, Willam, Artos, Berena, Alysanne, Errold and Rodrik Stark.
