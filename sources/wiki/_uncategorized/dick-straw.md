Appearance[ ]

Dick has cornflower blue eyes, an unsettling smile and hair as white as flax. His back bears numerous cris-crossed scars from the whippings he receives for poor discipline.

History[ ]

The Tattered Prince has had Dick whipped more than any man in the Windblown. Dick has had sex with Pretty Meris a few times.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Dick Straw was part of the Siege of Astapor and was wary of later marching on Meereen and facing Daenerys Targaryens real Unsullied and her Dragons. He was later sent by the Tattered Prince along with the other Westeros Windblown to go over to Daenerys Targaryen. He is currently languishing in the dungeons of the Great Pyramid with some of the Westerosi Windblown.
