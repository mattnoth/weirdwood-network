Alyssa is a popular female name in Westeros. 

Characters[ ]

It may refer to the following characters:

Alyssa Arryn, a legendary figure of House Arryn

Alyssa Blackwood, fourth wife of Walder Frey

Alyssa Royce, a possible marriage candidate for King Aegon III Targaryen

Alyssa Targaryen, second daughter and fifth child of King Jaehaerys I and Queen Alysanne Targaryen, and wife of her brother, Prince Baelon

Alyssa Velaryon, wife of King Aenys I Targaryen, and second wife of Lord Rogar Baratheon

Locations[ ]
