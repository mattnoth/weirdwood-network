Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Harle is among the wildling leaders who accompany Tormund to the Wall and declare a truce with the Night's Watch. As agreed upon in the truce, his son and his nephew become hostages of the Night's Watch. He is present in the Shieldhall when Jon Snow reads aloud the letter purportedly from Ramsay Bolton entitled 'Bastard'. When Jon is done reading the letter, Harle is among those who stand up to show Jon his support.
