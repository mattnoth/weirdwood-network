Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Dennet is present when Sandor Clegane is given a trial by combat by Lord Beric Dondarrion in the hollow hill. Dennet, Anguy, and Kyle are ordered to feather the Hound at the first sign of treachery, and only after all three bowmen have their shafts notched does Thoros of Myr hand Sandor a belt.
