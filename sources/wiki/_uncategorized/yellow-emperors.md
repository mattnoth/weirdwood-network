## Summary

The yellow emperors are an ancient ruling dynasty of the Golden Empire of Yi Ti. The dynasty fell a thousand years ago. However, the sorcerer lord of Carcosa claims to be the sixty-ninth yellow emperor. The family name of the yellow emperors is Chai.
