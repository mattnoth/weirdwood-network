## Summary

Ser Raymond joins Renly Baratheon's host when the latter declares himself King. Brienne of Tarth recalls that Raymond participated in a bet beneath the walls of Highgarden to see who would be the first to claim her maidenhead. After the bet was discovered and ended, Brienne sought out and defeated Raymond during the melee at Bitterbridge. His whereabouts afterwards are unknown.
