## Full Text

Durran Durrandon is the name of several Storm Kings of House Durrandon of Storm's End, founded by Durran Godsgrief. These include:

Durran II the Devout, Godsgrief's son and successor.

Durran Bronze-Axe, who reigned a century after Durran the Devout.

Durran the Dour, who slew the Lun the Last, King of the Giants.

Durran the Ravenfriend, who saw the founding of Lord Maldon Massey's castle Stonedance.

Durran the Young, also known as the Butcher Boy.

Durran the Fair, who annexed the Kingdom of Tarth by marrying Edwyn Evenstar's daughter.

Durran X, who extended the Kingdom of the Storm to the Blackwater Rush.

Durran XI the Dim, grandson of Durran X.

Durran XXI, who reigned during the Andal Invasion.

Durran the Third, who invaded Dorne twice but was defeated by Nymeria.

Durran XXIV, also known as Durran Half-Blood, whose mother was Andal.
