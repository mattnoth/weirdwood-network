## Full Text

Richard Roote was a knight from House Roote during the reign of King Aegon I Targaryen who served in his Kingsguard.

Character[ ]

Richard is remembered for being quick, strong, observant, and famed for his prowess with sword and shield. He was devoted to his king.

History[ ]

Richard was chosen by Queen Visenya Targaryen to be one of the first sworn brothers of the Kingsguard when the order was founded in 10 AC by King Aegon I Targaryen.
