Steffon is a common male name in Westeros. It may refer to:

Characters[ ]

Steffon Baratheon, a previous Lord of Storm's End and father of Robert I Baratheon

Steffon Connington, a knight during the regency of Aegon III Targaryen

Steffon Darklyn, a member of the Kingsguard during the reign of Viserys I Targaryen

Steffon Fossoway, a knight at the tourney at Ashford Meadow

Steffon Frey, called "Steffon the Sweet", a grandson of Ser Stevron Frey

Steffon Hollard, father of Ser Dontos Hollard

Steffon Seaworth, seventh son of Ser Davos Seaworth

Steffon Stackspear, son and heir of Lord Selmond Stackspear

Steffon Sunderland, a Lord of the Three Sisters

Steffon Swyft, son and heir of Ser Harys Swyft

Steffon Varner, head of House Varner in the Reach

Ships[ ]

Lord Steffon, a warship in Lord Stannis Baratheon's fleet, named for Lord Steffon Baratheon
