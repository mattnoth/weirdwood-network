Culture[ ]

A vulture carrying away an infant is depicted in the arms of House Blackmont.

Several outlaws from the Red Mountains have styled themselves as Vulture Kings.

History[ ]

King Benedict Blackmont is said to have been been able to change into a vulture of enormous size.

Singers claim that the first Vulture King was torn apart by vultures after being caught by Lord Samwell Tarly, though the outlaw actually died of thirst and exposure before being eaten.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Theon Greyjoy thinks the nose of his sister Asha resembles the beak of a vulture.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

During the wedding of Ramsay Bolton and Arya Stark, Theon thinks that Lord Ondrew Locke resembles a vulture in the mists of the godswood of Winterfell.
