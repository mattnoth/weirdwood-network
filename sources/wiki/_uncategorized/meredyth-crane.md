Appearance and Character[ ]

Meredyth is a boisterous plump woman. She always has a funny tale to tell.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Meredyth is one of Margaery Tyrell's lady attendants in King's Landing.

Merry dances with Jalabhar Xho during the wedding of Tyrion Lannister and Sansa Stark.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Meredyth is part of Margaery's retinue. She often goes hawking with Janna Fossoway and Queen Margaery. Merry and Janna are escorted by the twins Ser Horas and Ser Hobber Redwyne during one of Margaery's rides, returning with flowers in their hair.
