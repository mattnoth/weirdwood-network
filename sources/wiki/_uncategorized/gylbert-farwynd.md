Appearance[ ]

Gylbert is tall and spare with a clean-shaven lantern jaw. To Aeron Greyjoy, Gylbert's eyes appear to change from grey to blue like the sea.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

During the kingsmoot on Old Wyk to succeed Balon Greyjoy, King of the Isles and the North, Lord Farwynd puts his name forth with his sons Gyles, Ygon, and Yohn as his champions. Gylbert promises to lead the ironborn to a land without winter beyond the Sunset Sea where death holds no sway and every man will be a king and his wife a queen. His gifts of sealskins, walrus tusks, whalebone arm rings, and bronze-banded warhorns are considered poor by the captains, however, and are left for lesser men. Gylbert's claim is dismissed quickly, with not even all of the Farwynds supporting him.
