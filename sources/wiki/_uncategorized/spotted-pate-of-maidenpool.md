Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Halder informs Jon Snow that he helped Pate carve the stone for the new pommel of Longclaw.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Spotted Pate is among the men that Bowen Marsh leaves behind to defend Castle Black. After the attack on Castle Black, Spotted Pate, Kegs, and some of the other builders argue that the debris of the burned stairs and broken ice should be left where it is, thereby creating a new obstacle for Mance Rayder. As the suggestion would mean abandoning the defense of the tunnel, Donal Noye decides against it. Pate fights in the battle beneath the Wall, where he accompanies Noye down into the tunnel. There, he is killed by a giant, Mag Mar Tun Doh Weg, who twists the builder's head off.
