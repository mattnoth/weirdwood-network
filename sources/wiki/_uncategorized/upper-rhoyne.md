Upper Rhoyne

Western Essos and the location of the Upper Rhoyne

The Upper Rhoyne is a river in western Essos whose headwaters lie in the hills south of Braavos. Flowing southeast, it meets the Little Rhoyne east of Ghoyan Drohe, forming the Rhoyne. The Upper Rhoyne is full of snags and sawyers.

History[ ]

Qarlon the Great, an Andal king at Lorath, once controlled land as far west as the Braavosi lagoon and the headwaters of the Upper Rhoyne. The domains of Norvos now reach the Upper Rhoyne.
