## Full Text

Roland Darry was a Lord of Darry and head of House Darry during the Dance of the Dragons.

History[ ]

A Lord Darry and his heir were slain during the Dance when Prince Aemond Targaryen burned Darry with the dragonflame of Vhagar, but Lady Darry and her younger children survived in the castle's vaults.

Lord Derrick Darry gathered men for Ser Addam Velaryon's host before the Second Battle of Tumbleton. Derrick may have died during the battle, as Lord Roland later joined the host of Lord Elmo Tully. Roland was slain by Lord Borros Baratheon during the Battle of the Kingsroad.
