Appearance and Character[ ]

Ser Florian was an old knight, grey and grizzled, and stern.

History[ ]

Lord Manfryd received a raven from Queen Rhaenyra Targaryen ordering him to execute Nettles. As she was his guest, Lord Manfryd was dubious of what to do. He assembled his Maester, Norren, his brother, his captain of the guard and his champion, Ser Florian, and listened to their advices. Ser Florian was shocked the queen would demand him to do such unhonorable act.
