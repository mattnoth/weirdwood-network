Alton is a given name in Westeros and may refer to:

Alton Beesbury, a maester from House Beesbury

Alton Butterwell, master of coin under Maegor I Targaryen

Alton Celtigar, Lord of Claw Isle during the reign of Aegon I Targaryen

Alton Greyjoy, a Lord of the Iron Islands

Alton Lannister, a character in Season 2 of Game of Thrones who took the role of Cleos Frey
