## Full Text

Gowen Baratheon was the third son of a reigning Lord of Storm's End.

In the television adaptation A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms, Gowen is portrayed by Connan Aaron.

History[ ]

Gowen was married to Lady Tya Lannister. Their wedding, circa 208 AC, was the last between houses Lannister and Baratheon until the marriage of King Robert I Baratheon and Queen Cersei Lannister some seventy-six years later. Gowen and Tya had one son, a "large and lusty lad" born with a full head of black hair, who died in his infancy.

Family[ ]

Gowen was the third son of a Lord of Storm's End. Lyonel Baratheon was known to be the heir to Storm's End in 209 AC. Thus, Gowen and Lyonel might have been brothers.
