## Full Text

Lord Perryn was the head of House Perryn during the civil war known as the Dance of the Dragons.

History[ ]

During the Dance of the Dragons, Lord Perryn sided with the blacks and Rhaenyra Targaryen.

Lord Perryn, alongside with Lord Bigglestone and Lord Chambers, joined Ser Pate of Longleaf and his ragged band of survivors from earlier battles and fought together in the Battle by the Lakeshore.

It is unknown if he survived the war.
