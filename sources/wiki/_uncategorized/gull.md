Culture[ ]

Gulltown is a port city found in the Vale of Arryn. According to a semi-canon source, House Shett of Gulltown include nine white seagulls in their arms. Ser Damon Shett is the Knight of Gull Tower.

One of the priests of the Drowned God is called the Old Grey Gull.

History[ ]

  

The Summer Sea, by Juan Carlos Barquet © Fantasy Flight Games

The remains of Arrax were feasted upon by crabs and seagulls after washing up beneath Storm's End during the Dance of the Dragons. The butchered people of Spicetown were left for gulls, rats, and carrion crows after the Battle of the Gullet.

Theon Greyjoy once put an arrow through a seagull on the wing while training with Dagmer Cleftjaw in his youth.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Salladhor Saan has roasted gull stuffed with mushrooms and fennel and onion prepared for him on Valyrian.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser Davos Seaworth sees gulls at the spears of the Merling King.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Prince Doran Martell's breakfast at the Water Gardens includes a plate of gull's eggs diced with bits of ham and fiery peppers.

A raucous seagull watches the kingsmoot on Old Wyk from one of Nagga's ribs.

Samwell Tarly sees seagulls on the statue of King Daeron I Targaryen in Oldtown.
