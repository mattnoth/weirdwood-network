Uthor was a maester of the Citadel who served Lord Roose Bolton at the Dreadfort. Uthor's fate is unknown, with Maester Tybald now serving as the Dreadfort's maester.

History[ ]

The old maester insisted that Reek's stench was a sign of sickness.

When Domeric Bolton died, Uthor claimed it was a sickness of the bowels. Lord Roose Bolton, however, suspected his son had been poisoned by Ramsay Snow.
