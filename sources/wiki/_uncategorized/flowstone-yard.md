Appearance[ ]

Located near Hunter's Hall and a covered bridge, the Flowstone Yard has a lumpy surface. To scour them clean, mail shirts can be tossed in barrels of sand and rolled across the uneven ground. A covered gallery overlooks the yard. Also located near the Flowstone Yard is the bear pit of Harrenhal and the Wailing Tower.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Arya Stark sees three House Frey knights and their bastard brother (Jared, Hosteen, Danwell, and Ronel), all captives of Lord Tywin Lannister, practice with staves and wooden shields in the Flowstone Yard. The four are soon ransomed by other kin from House Frey.

After the fall of Harrenhal, Brave Companions bring the slain to the Flowstone Yard and burn them bodies in a pyre. Elmar Frey asks Arya for help rolling a barrel across the yard.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser Jaime Lannister gallops through the Flowstone Yard to rescue Brienne of Tarth from the bear pit.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

After having supper with Ser Bonifer Hasty in Hunter's Hall, Jaime passes through the Flowstone Yard and meets Ser Ronnet Connington by the bear pit.
