## Full Text

Jon Umber was the Lord of the Last Hearth and the head of House Umber during the latter half of the second century after Aegon's Conquest.

History[ ]

Jon married Serena Stark, the daughter of Rickon Stark, the heir to Winterfell, and the granddaughter of Lord Cregan Stark, but they had no issue. After his death, Serena married again; her father's half-brother, Edric Stark.
