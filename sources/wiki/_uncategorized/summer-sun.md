Appearance[ ]

Summer Sun has two hundred oars.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Magister Illyrio Mopatis sends Saduleon, Joso's Prank, and Summer Sun to Qarth to bring Daenerys Targaryen back to Pentos in preparation for her return to Westeros. Daenerys accepts, but renaming the ships as Balerion, Meraxes, and Vhagar in homage of the three powerful dragons of Aegon's Conquest. She tasks Arstan Whitebeard with having the new names painted on the hulls in golden letters three feet high.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Daenerys orders her small fleet to head for Slaver's Bay. At one point, she offers all three ships as payment to the Good Masters of Astapor for her Unsullied.

On the shores of Slaver's Bay, Daenerys orders her fleet broken up to provide the wood needed by her army for instruments of war for the siege of Meereen.
