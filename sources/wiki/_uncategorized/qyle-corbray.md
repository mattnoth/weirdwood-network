## Full Text

Qyle Corbray was the King of the Fingers and a monarch of House Corbray during the Andal invasion.

Character[ ]

Qyle was the wielder of House Corbray's famous blade Lady Forlorn.

History[ ]

King Qyle was the first of the Andal kings to fall to Robar II Royce, after uniting the First Men of the Vale. According to legend, King Robar II personally slew King Qyle after striking Lady Forlorn from his hand. Afterwards, Robar claimed Lady Forlorn for himself.
