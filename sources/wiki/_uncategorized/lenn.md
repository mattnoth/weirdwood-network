Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lenn is one of the raiders who is ordered by Mance Rayder to climb the Wall and attack Castle Black from the south. He survives the climb and arrives at Queenscrown with the others. Lenn dies during the attack on Castle Black.
