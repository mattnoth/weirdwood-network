## Summary

The great-grandfather of Petyr Baelish was a Braavosi sellsword that came to the Vale of Arryn in the service of Lord Corbray. His line was continued by his son, who became a hedge knight and took the head of the Titan of Braavos as his sigil. His first name is unknown.
