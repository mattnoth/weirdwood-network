## Full Text

Lily was the second of the four bastard children of King Aegon IV Targaryen and his second mistress, Megette.

History[ ]

Lily's mother Megette was the mistress of Prince Aegon for four years. Lily was the second of four daughters. After Prince Viserys discovered this, Megette was returned to her husband in the riverlands, while Lily and her three sisters were given to the Faith to be trained as a septa.
