History[ ]

Ruling from Yronwood in the southeastern Red Mountains, the Kings of Yronwood once controlled half of Dorne. 
Their last king was Yorick V Yronwood, whose vassals included, among others, the Blackmonts of Blackmont, the Jordaynes of the Tor, the Qorgyles of Sandstone, and the Wyls of Wyl. The Yronwoods were defeated by House Martell during Nymeria's War, however, with Yorick forced to join the Night's Watch.
