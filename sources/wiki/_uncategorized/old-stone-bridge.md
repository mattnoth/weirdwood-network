Layout[ ]

The inn is a tall, timbered building next to an old stone bridge. It has diamond-shaped window panes. The plank floor of the inn's common room is covered in sawdust. One of the rooms for guests has a low ceiling and a wide bed. Nearby are stables.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Brienne of Tarth stays here during her search for Sansa Stark, as one of her traveling companions, Ser Creighton Longbough, is friends with the innkeep. Goat, goat's milk, bread, and tankards of ale are served in the common room. Brienne pays for a room for herself and another for Creighton and Ser Illifer the Penniless to share. The merchant Hibald pays for a room for himself and Ser Shadrich, while his serving men sleep in the stables. Brienne departs in the middle of the night, leaving Creighton and Illifer behind.
