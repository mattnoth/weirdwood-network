## Full Text

It has been theorized that Urrathon Night-Walker, a character supposedly living in Qarth mentioned in A Clash of Kings, is actually Euron Greyjoy using an alias.

Supporting Evidence[ ]

Urrathon and similar names (such as Urragon) are common among the ironborn.

Euron has been to Qarth.

Another character named Urrathon, High King Urrathon IV Goodbrother, is known as "Badbrother". Some readers have taken this as a hint by George R. R. Martin, as Euron is a bad brother to Balon, Victarion, and Aeron.

Urrathon Badbrother was known for his arrogance and impiety, and was opposed by the priests in the kingsmoot. The exact same can be said for Euron Greyjoy.
