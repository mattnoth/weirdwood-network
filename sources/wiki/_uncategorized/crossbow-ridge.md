## Summary

Crossbow Ridge is a place in the riverlands on the east bank of the Widow's Wash. It is disputed land between House Bracken and House Blackwood. It is currently held by the Blackwoods.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Jonos Bracken wants the east bank of the Widow's Wash from Crossbow Ridge to Rutting Meadow for subduing Lord Tytos Blackwood. Tytos surrenders Crossbow Ridge as part of the price to submit to the Iron Throne.
