## Full Text

Manfred Hightower was Lord of the Hightower and head of House Hightower during the reign of Kings Aegon I Targaryen and Aenys I Targaryen. He had two sons, Martyn and Morgan, and a daughter, Ceryse.

History[ ]

In 23 AC, when Queen Visenya Targaryen suggested a betrothal between her son Maegor and her newborn niece Rhaena, the High Septon objected to the match and suggested instead his own niece, Manfred's daughter Ceryse. She and Maegor were wed in 25 AC.

In 39 AC, when Maegor took a second bride by wedding Alys Harroway, Lord Manfred made protest to King Aenys I Targaryen, demanding that Lady Alys be put aside.

Manfred died in 41 AC and was succeeded by his son Martyn.
