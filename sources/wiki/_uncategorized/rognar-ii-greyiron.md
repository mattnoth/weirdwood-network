## Full Text

Rognar II Greyiron was the last King of the Iron Islands from House Greyiron.

History[ ]

Rognar II was brought down by pirates, sellswords, and warlords, Andals who came to the Iron Islands and allied with Orkwoods, Drumms, Hoares, and Greyjoys. He was succeeded by Harras Hoare, who, according to the tale, won the crown in a contest of the finger dance.
