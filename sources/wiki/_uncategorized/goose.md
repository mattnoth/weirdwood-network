Culture[ ]

A goose was included in the arms of House Cargyll.

Goose feathers can be used as fletchings for arrows. Goose down can be used in pillows, mattresses, cushions, and quills.

The Stinking Goose is a tavern found in Maidenpool.

History[ ]

Dunk and Egg had goose eggs for breakfast on the first day of the tourney at Ashford Meadow.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

During the Hand's tourney, Eddard Stark sees a serving man carrying a goose which honks and and pecks at the man's fingers. Boiled goose eggs are served at King Robert I Baratheon's breakfast.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Yoren sees a goose running loose in an abandoned Gods Eye town, so Koss retrieves the bird.

Lady Tanda Stokeworth invites Tyrion Lannister to a dinner which includes a brace of stuffed geese sauced with mulberries.

Goose-in-berries is served during the harvest feast at Winterfell.

Roast geese is served at Bitterbridge.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Anguy brings down a goose for the brotherhood without banners to eat. Tom of Sevenstreams sings a silly song about Big Belly Ben and the High Septon's goose.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Tyrion is served goose livers drowned in wine at Illyrio's manse in Pentos.

Quotes[ ]
