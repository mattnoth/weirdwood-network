Known distances[ ]

Westeros[ ]

The Wall is a hundred league long.

Mole's Town is half a league south of Castle Black.

The Gift is composed of Brandon's Gift and the New Gift, each streching twenty five leagues.

Hammerhorn is six leagues inland.

Three leagues separate Sunspear from the Water Gardens.

According to Rodrik Harlaw, the Dornish coast is four hundred leagues long.

Ryman Frey died two leagues south of Fairmarket.

Forty leagues separate Castle Black from Greyguard.

Deepwood Motte is five leagues inland.

A hundred leagues separate Deepwood Motte from Winterfell.

On her way to the Wall, Alys Karstark first meets Ty and Dannel of the Night's Watch two leagues south of Mole's Town, then she is escorted to meet Lord Commander Jon Snow on the kingsroad half a league south of Mole's Town.

There is a mill controled by House Webber six leagues away from their seat of Coldmoat.

In the aftermath of the battle at Stonebridge, it is reported that the Mander ran red with blood for twenty leagues.

During the First Dornish War, Ser Joffrey Dayne burned fields, farms, and villages for twenty leagues around Oldtown.

During the Battle at Rook's Rest, Sunfyre and Meleys crashed to the ground half a league away from Rook's Rest.

Longtable is located some thirty leagues to the southwest of Bitterbridge.

Tumbleton is located sixty leagues to the southwest of King's Landing, and one hundred and sixty leagues to the south of Maidenpool.

During the invasion of the riverlands by the ironborn, the forces of King Harwyn Hoare landed forty leagues south of Seagard.

Casterly Rock has been measured as almost two leagues long from west to east.

Essos[ ]

The Great Masters of Meereen burnt every tree within twenty leagues of their city before the arrival of Daenerys Targaryen.

Five hundred leagues separate Meereen from Volantis.

The Ghiscari cities are linked by the old Ghiscari coast road; a hundred leagues from Astapor to Yunkai and fifty leagues from Yunkai to Meereen.

The Warden of the River is in charge of all the ferries, dredges, and irrigation ditches along the Skahazadhan for fifty leagues from Meereen.

The largest of the Summer Isles, Jhala, stretches two hundred leagues from tip to tip.

The Dothraki Sea stretches more than seven hundred leagues from the Forest of Qohor in the west to the Bone Mountains in the east.

The Bone Mountains stretches more than five hundred leagues from north to south and a hundred leagues from east to west.

Far Ib lies more than a hundred leagues southeast of Ib.

Notes[ ]

↑ The distance between Tumbleton and King's Landing is cited three times in the books: "sixty leagues" (in The World of Ice and Fire, The Targaryen Kings: Aegon II), "some fifty leagues" (in Fire and Blood, The Dying of the Dragons - Rhaenyra Triumphant), and "sixty leagues" (in Fire and Blood, The Dying of the Dragons - Rhaenyra Triumphant). It can be reasonably assumed the distance is actually sixty leagues and the passage referencing fifty leagues was a broad estimation.
