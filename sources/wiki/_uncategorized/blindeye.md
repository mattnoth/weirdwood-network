A Clash of Kings[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

During Tyrion Lannister's trial for the death of King Joffrey I Baratheon, Pycelle shows a number of poisons and medicines from his collection and claims that Tyrion stole a several of them, including a sample of blindeye, during the time Tyrion had him imprisoned.
