Lamentation was a famed Valyrian steel sword of House Royce.

History[ ]

Ser Willam Royce wielded the blade during the Dance of the Dragons. He was one of the Seven Who Rode to rescue Prince Joffrey Velaryon from the riot of King's Landing, but was killed when a man leaped down from a rooftop to land on his back. Lamentation was torn from Willam's hand and carried off, never to be found again. 

However, some rumors claim that Ser Warrick Wheaton slashed a wing off of Syrax with a Valyrian steel sword, likely Lamentation, during the Storming of the Dragonpit.
