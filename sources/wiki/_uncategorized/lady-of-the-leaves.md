Appearance[ ]

The Lady of the Leaves is an old, thin white-haired woman. She wears roughspun.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

The Lady of the Leaves is known to the maester of Lymond Lychester, who directs the brotherhood without banners to meet with her in order to get information on the whereabouts of Lord Beric Dondarrion.

When the brotherhood finds the Lady of the Leaves, she tells them she heard that Beric was killed by Gregor Clegane. She rejoices when they tell her that Beric is still alive. The woman tells the outlaws that once winter comes, her people will have to abandon their hidden village as they will become otherwise too exposed. Arya Stark thinks the lady sounds sick.
