Culture[ ]

Xaro Xhoan Daxos decorates his nose with rubies, opals, and flakes of jade.

Illyrio Mopatis has opal among his various rings.

History[ ]

The Opal Emperor was a ruler of the Great Empire of the Dawn.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Daenerys Targaryen has a vision of ghosts with eyes of amethyst, jade, opal, and tourmaline.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

In a ruined city within the red waste, Aggo finds an iron bracelet set with a large uncut fire opal.

Xaro Xhoan Daxos gives Daenerys a thousand miniature knights carved of amber, amethyst, beryl, jade, onyx, opal, and tourmaline, with armor of silver and gold.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

The seven-sided golden chalice given by Lord Mace Tyrell to King Joffrey I Baratheon has an opal sun to represent House Martell.

A girl claimed to be Arya Stark wears a clasp in the shape of a wolf's head with slitted opal eyes.
