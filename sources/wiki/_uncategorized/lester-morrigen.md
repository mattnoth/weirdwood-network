Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

House Morrigen supports Renly Baratheon during the War of the Five Kings. After Renly's death, Lester allies with Stannis Baratheon. Lester's youngest brother, Ser Guyard Morrigen, is slain in the Battle of the Blackwater.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Stannis tells Ser Davos Seaworth that Lester is among the stormlords who still remain loyal to him following his loss at the Blackwater.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

According to a semi-canon source, Lester has followed Stannis to the north.
