## Full Text

Lord of Grandview is a title held by the head of House Grandison, the ruler of Grandview in the stormlands.

History[ ]

Lord Lorent Grandison was one of the three lords chosen by lot to become King Aegon III Targaryen's regents in 136 AC.

At the beginning of Robert's Rebellion, Lord Grandison remained loyal to the Iron Throne and together with Lords Fell and Cafferen planned to join their forces to bring Lord Robert Baratheon's head to King Aerys II Targaryen. Robert defeated them in three battles at Summerhall, however. Lord Grandison became Robert's ally thereafter and later died of wounds received at the Battle of the Trident.

Lord Hugh Grandison was among the possible matches offered to Princess Arianne Martell.
