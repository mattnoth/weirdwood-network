History[ ]

Skylarks were part of the immense pie at the wedding at Whitewalls.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

A begging brother in King's Landing preaches against Tyrion Lannister, claiming the Hand of the King has grown fat on lark and lamprey while the smallfolk starve.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Tyrion has roasted larks stuffed with garlic and onions while traveling in the litter of Illyrio Mopatis. Tyrion later hears river larks along the Rhoyne.
