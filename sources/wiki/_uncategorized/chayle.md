Character[ ]

Chayle is young and cheerful. He believes that Hodor has a gentle spirit. He wears the crystal of his order on a silver chain.

History[ ]

Chayle grew up on the shores of the White Knife and is a very good swimmer.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Septon Chayle falls asleep reading about Grand Maester Aethelmure while Tyrion Lannister explores Winterfell's library. The library tower is set afire when the catspaw tries to assassinate Bran Stark.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

While they are sorting through some scrolls from Winterfell's library fire, Chayle tells Bran that the red comet "is the sword that slays the season." Bran thinks he probably is right since a white raven has arrived from Oldtown bringing word of autumn.

Jojen Reed foresees the death of Chayle in his green dreams. When Bran tells Chayle of his nearing death, Chayle holds to his faith. After Winterfell is taken by the ironborn, Chayle is struck when he tries to assist Palla, who has been raped. Theon Greyjoy has Chayle thrown into a well to honor the Drowned God.

A dripping Chayle appears in Theon's nightmare of King Robert I Baratheon's feast of the dead at Winterfell. Lady Catelyn's sept is burned during the sack of Winterfell.
