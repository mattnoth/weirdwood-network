Culture[ ]

The she-dragon ridden by Prince Daeron Targaryen, Tessarion, had dark cobalt wings, while the scales of her claws, crest, and belly were a bright beaten copper. Her flames were cobalt blue in color as well.

Brienne of Tarth owns a dented deep blue cobalt suit of armor which earns her the sobriquet of "Brienne the Blue".
