History[ ]

Larra Rogare, the Lysene wife of Viserys Targaryen, was fluent in High Valyrian and the tongues of Myr, Tyrosh, Volantis, and her native Lys. She made no effort to learn the Common Tongue of Westeros while residing at the Red Keep, however.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Maester Kedry is fluent in the tongues of all of the Free Cities.

Young Griff is fluent in High Valyrian, the dialects of Pentos, Tyrosh, Myr, and Lys, and the trade talk of sailors.

When Daenerys Targaryen, Queen of Meereen, meets with refugees from Astapor, she hears Lysene among the various peoples all saying their word for "Mother".

The kindly man insists that Arya Stark learn the tongues of Lys and Pentos.
