## Full Text

Denys Oakheart was a knight of House Oakheart during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen. He was the eldest son and heir of Lord Oakheart.

History[ ]

In 73 AC, Denys took part in a tourney held by his father to celebrate the birth of another son. Denys was defeated by a mystery knight called the Silver Fool, who was later revealed to be Prince Baelon Targaryen.
