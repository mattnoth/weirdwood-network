Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

When Garse's father, Garth Tyrell, travels to Oldtown to take ship to King's Landing to serve on the small council as the new master of coin, Garse and his brother, Garrett Flowers, accompany him, as Lord Tywin Lannister had promised to find a place for them. When informing Queen Regent Cersei Lannister about this, Lord Mace Tyrell suggests that Garse and his brother be named to the City Watch of King's Landing. However, Cersei, wishing to prevent the presence of another Tyrell on the small council, prevents Garth's promotion by quickly naming Lord Gyles Rosby as the new master of coin.
