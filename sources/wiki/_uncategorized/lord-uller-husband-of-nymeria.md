Character[ ]

By the time of Nymeria's War, Lord Uller was an aged man.

History[ ]

During Nymeria's War, Lord Uller supported Princess Nymeria and her husband Lord Mors Martell against King Yorick V Yronwood and his bannermen.

At some point after the death of Lord Martell at the Third Battle of the Boneway, Lord Uller married Nymeria and served her as a counselor and consort since Nymeria remained the unquestioned ruler of Dorne. Their marriage remained childless and Nymeria married Ser Davos Dayne, the Sword of the Morning, after Uller's death.
