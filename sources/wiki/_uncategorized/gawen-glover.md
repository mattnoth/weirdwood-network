## Summary

Gawen Glover is a member of House Glover, the son of Robett and Sybelle Glover. Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Gawen is captured, along with his sister Erena and mother Sybelle, when the ironborn take Deepwood Motte.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Gawen, his sister and mother are held captive at Deepwood Motte by Asha Greyjoy.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

The Glovers are taken to Ten Towers in the Iron Islands by Asha.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Gawen and his sister are left captive at Ten Towers by Asha under the care of her aunt Gwynesse Harlaw.
