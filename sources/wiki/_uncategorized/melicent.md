Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Septas Melicent, Moelle, Aglantine, and Nysterica examine Margaery Tyrell and discover that her maidenhead is not intact, giving grounds for Margaery's arrest by the Faith on charges of infidelity.

In the appendices, she was erroneously called "Septa Helicent". This error is corrected in latest kindle version.
