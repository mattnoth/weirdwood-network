History[ ]

Rhaena Targaryen and her dragon, Dreamfyre, briefly stayed at Ashemark with the Marbrands in 51 AC. King Jaehaerys I Targaryen visited the castle in 55 AC.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Willem Frey serves as a page at Ashemark.

During the War of the Five Kings, Robb Stark, the King in the North, defeats Ser Stafford Lannister at the Battle of Oxcross. Robb then takes Ashemark, along with other castles of the westermen.

Robb leaves Ashemark to march on the Crag. After learning that Storm's End has yielded to Stannis Baratheon, Catelyn Stark has Maester Vyman send a raven from Riverrun to Ashemark, hoping that a rider can then be sent to inform Robb.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Abandoning castles taken in the westerlands, Robb returns to Riverrun.
