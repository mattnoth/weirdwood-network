Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Eddard accompanies his father and his two brothers Harrion and Torrhen to Winterfell when Robb Stark calls his banners. He is a member of Robb's personal guard during the battle in the Whispering Wood. Eddard and Torrhen are both killed by Ser Jaime Lannister during the battle.
