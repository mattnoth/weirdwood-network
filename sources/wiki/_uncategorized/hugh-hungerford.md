History[ ]

Hugh was the Windblown's paymaster until he was caught stealing from the war chest. The Tattered Prince had three of his fingers removed for theft.

Appearance and Character[ ]

Hugh is slim and saturnine, long-faced, long-legged, and clothed in faded finery. He is sour.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Hugh is sent by the Tattered Prince to appear to defect to Queen Daenerys Targaryen, along with the other Westerosi Windblown. However, once Quentyn Martell reveals the ruse to her, Hugh and the other Windblown defectors are imprisoned. Meris is eventually freed by Daenerys but Hugh and the others are still prisoners at the start of the second siege of Meereen.
