## Full Text

Lord Follard was the head of House Follard during the regency of King Aegon III Targaryen.

History[ ]

Lord Follard was one of the six lords who accompanied Lord Manfryd Mooton to Braavos in 133 AC. As the Crown's envoy, Manfryd was tasked to make peace with the Sealord of Braavos after the recent conflict between the Free City and Lord Alyn Velaryon. While Manfryd treated with the Sealord, Lord Follard spread bribes among the Braavosi keyholders, magisters, and other notables. Rather than return to Westeros, he chose to remain in Braavos with a courtesan he had become enamored with.
