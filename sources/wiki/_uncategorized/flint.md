## Summary

Flint is a hard sedimentary rock found in the known world. Flint creates sparks when struck against steel, so flints are often carried by travelers as fire starters. Flint can be grey or black.

Description[ ]

West of the kingsroad, the foothills of the northern mountains are made of flint. There are flint hills and ridges in the haunted forest.

Flintstones are found in the western riverlands.

In the Basilisk Isles, the Isle of Tears has rugged flint hills.

Culture[ ]

Polished flint shines dark. Some free folk warriors wield flint axes.

There are multiple branches of Flints in the north. The Flint Cliffs are named after House Flint of Flint's Finger.

The Flint Barracks is an old building at Castle Black.

The eyes of Lord Gormon Peake, Lord Balon Greyjoy, Ser Alliser Thorne, Hother Umber, and the High Sparrow have been compared to flint. The hard Lord Alaric Stark was known for having a flinty reputation.
