Geography[ ]

Battle Island sits in the area where the Honeywine widens and empties into Whispering Sound. The Hightower sits atop the isle's bluffs. It is located at the center of the city.

History[ ]

It is unknown how the island came to be named. Some tales claim dragons once roosted on Battle Isle until the first member of House Hightower put an end to them.

A large square fortress of black stone was constructed on Battle Island by unknown builders. The Hightowers then ruled as kings from the isle, using the stone fortress as a foundation as they built the Hightower atop it. Gyles I Gardener, King of the Reach, once sacked Oldtown but was unable to conquer the Hightower on Battle Isle.
