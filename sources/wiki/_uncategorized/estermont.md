Geography[ ]

Small and mountainous, Estermont is "damp, windswept, and poor" according to Archmaester Gyldayn.

History[ ]

Estermont and the islands to its south were added to the realm of the Storm Kings of House Durrandon during the reign of Erich the Sailmaker.

Estermont was conquered shortly after Tarth during the coming of the Andals to the stormlands. Peace in the region was eventually achieved when the Durrandons and their First Men bannermen intermarried with the Andals.

Some Storm Kings kept war fleets at Estermont.

Near the end of 55 AC, Rhaena Targaryen spent time at Greenstone with her cousin and childhood friend, Larissa Velaryon.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

During the landing of the Golden Company in the stormlands, five hundred men are put ashore on Estermont by the Volantene fleet transporting the sellswords. Ser Marq Mandrake leads the taking of Greenstone.
