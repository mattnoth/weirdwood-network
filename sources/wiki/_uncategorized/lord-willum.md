Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

At the start of the War of the Five Kings, Lord Willum declares for Renly Baratheon. He and his two sons are part of Renly's army camped beneath the walls of Bitterbridge.

After the death of Renly, Lord Willum goes over to Stannis Baratheon. He and his sons fight for Stannis at the Battle of the Blackwater, but are captured, and later bend the knee to King Joffrey I Baratheon.
