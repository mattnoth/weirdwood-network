## Full Text

Cerelle Lannister was a member of House Lannister during the reign of King Aegon III Targaryen.

History[ ]

Cerelle was the daughter of Lord Jason Lannister and his wife, Lady Johanna Westerling. She had four sisters, including Tyshara, and one brother, Lord Loreon.

After the death of Queen Jaehaera Targaryen in 133 AC, Lady Johanna set aside her war with the ironmen long enough to write the Hand that her daughters Cerelle and Tyshara were maidens of noble birth and marriageable age.
