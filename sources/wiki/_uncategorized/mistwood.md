Layout[ ]

Mistwood is protected by walls, a gatehouse, and a keep. The lord's solar is found high in the Tower of Owls.

History[ ]

Lord Mertyns and his household at Mistwood were poisoned by a cask of wine during the First Dornish War.

Recent Events[ ]

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Arianne Martell visits Mistwood while traveling to find Aegon Targaryen. They find it held by John Mudd and Chain of the Golden Company, who keep the dowager Lady Mary Mertyns under house arrest. John has the castle's maester send a raven to Haldon at Storm's End.
