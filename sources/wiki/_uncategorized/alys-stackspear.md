## Full Text

Alys Stackspear was a noblewoman of House Stackspear, and the first wife of Jason Lannister. She was the mother of Damon Lannister.

History[ ]

According to a semi-canon source, the fifteen-year-old Jason Lannister deflowered the maiden Alys in 244 AC, getting her pregnant. Jason and Alys were married because of this, but Alys would eventually die giving birth to the child, named Damon.
