Gerold Templeton was a squire from House Templeton during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

Gerold was among the group of squires who kept company with Princess Daella Targaryen. By the end of 78 AC the princess fell out with Gerold and the other squires, as she decided she hated them.
