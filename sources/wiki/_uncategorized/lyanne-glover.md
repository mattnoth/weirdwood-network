## Full Text

Lyanne Glover was a noblewoman from House Glover during the early part of the third century after Aegon's Conquest.

History[ ]

Lyanne married Willam Stark, the Lord of Winterfell and Warden of the North. She gave him a son, Brandon, but died during his birth. After Lyanne's death, Lord Willam remarried to Melantha Blackwood.
