Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Russ sails aboard his Storm Crow to rescue the wildlings at Hardhome. During the voyage, his crew sees the wreckage of a galley along the coast of Skagos. Russ arrives at destination but the desperate wildlings try to take his ship by force. They are successfully repelled at the cost of the lives of six black brothers, however. It is unknown if Russ is among the dead.
