## Full Text

Bloodstone is a type of jasper or chalcedony which often appears green with red spots which resemble blood.

Culture[ ]

Bloodstone is the largest island in the Stepstones.

History[ ]

The Bloodstone Emperor was a legendary ruler of the Great Empire of the Dawn. He is said to have worshiped a black stone that fell from the sky.
