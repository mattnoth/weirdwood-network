## Full Text

Hardhand is an ironborn longship and part of the Iron Fleet. It is presumably named after King Harwyn Hoare who was known as "Harwyn Hardhand" and "the Hardhand".

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Hardhand is among the ships which sail to Old Wyk for the kingsmoot. Hardhand later sinks after the taking of the Shields when it is dragged under by a ship it had rammed.
