## Full Text

Gerold is a Westerosi given name. It can refer to several people:

Archmaester Gerold, an Archmaester of the Citadel during the reign of King Aegon V Targaryen.

Ser Gerold Dayne, the head of House Dayne of High Hermitage.

Lord Gerold Grafton, the Lord of Gulltown.

King Gerold Grafton, an Andal knight who established the rule of House Grafton over Gulltown and ruled as its king.

Ser Gerold Hightower, also known as the White Bull, the Lord Commander of the Kingsguard for Kings Jaehaerys II and Aerys II Targaryen.

Lord Gerold Lannister, also known as the Golden, the Lord of Casterly Rock during the reigns of Kings Aerys I, Maekar I, and Aegon V Targaryen.

King Gerold Lannister, a King of the Rock who sailed his fleet against the ironborn.

King Gerold II Lannister, a King of the Rock.

King Gerold III Lannister, a King of the Rock, whose son-in-law inherited the rule of the Kingdom of the Rock.

Gerold Templeton, a member of House Templeton during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.
