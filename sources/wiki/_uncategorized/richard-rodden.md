## Full Text

Richard Rodden was the head of House Rodden during the civil war known as the Dance of the Dragons.

History[ ]

Richard was loyal to King Aegon II Targaryen and the greens. A member of the Caltrops, he took an arrow during the Second Battle of Tumbleton and died the following day.
