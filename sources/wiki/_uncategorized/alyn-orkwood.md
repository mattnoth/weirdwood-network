Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Alyn supports Euron Greyjoy with twenty longships, and he accompanies Euron to a feast tent on the shores of Nagga's Cradle. During the kingsmoot on Old Wyk, Alyn whispers in Euron's ear and holds up Cragorn after the crewman blows the dragon horn.
