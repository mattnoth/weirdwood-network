## Full Text

The Defenestration of Sunspear was an event during the First Dornish War which took place at Sunspear in 5 AC. 

History[ ]

Soon after the Targaryens returned to King's Landing, the Dornish swarmed from the shadow city to retake Sunspear. Lord Jon Rosby, who had been named Castellan of Sunspear and Warden of the Sands by King Aegon I Targaryen, was bound hand and foot, dragged to the top of the Spear Tower and defenestrated from a window by none other than the blind Princess Meria Martell herself.
