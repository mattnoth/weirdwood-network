Appearance and Character[ ]

Dorcas is a big woman. Cersei Lannister thinks she is stronger than Senelle, though also clumsier than her.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

After Cersei gives Senelle to Qyburn to be a subject in his experiments, Dorcas becomes Cersei's chief handmaid. She is present when three lowborn men present to the Cersei the head of a dwarf, intending to pass it as the head of Tyrion Lannister. After seeing the rotting head crawling with maggots, Dorcas gasps.

Dorcas blushes when Cersei orders her to fetch Ser Osney Kettleblack. Lady Taena Merryweather asks Cersei why Dorcas reacted that way, and Cersei replies that Dorcas fancies Osney, and that she believes Dorcas likes his scars. Taena agrees that scars make a man look dangerous, and that danger excites women. 

To amuse young King Tommen I Baratheon's kittens, Dorcas makes a toy mouse from scraps of fur, tied to a fishing pole.
