Bennifer Blackwood was a member of House Blackwood and the heir of Raventree Hall during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen.

History[ ]

Bennifer participated in the tourney at Ashford Meadow in 209 AC.

Notes[ ]

↑ Bennifer is not listed as a knight, per the Blackwood tradition of worshiping the old gods. Although the tourney was reserved for knights only (The Hedge Knight), according to George R. R. Martin and Elio García allowances were made for the Blackwoods because of their high birth.
