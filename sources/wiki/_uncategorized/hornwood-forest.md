Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Following the death of Lady Donella Hornwood, there is fighting in the Hornwood between knights from House Manderly and men from House Bolton.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Tywin Lannister requires that Lord Wyman Manderly give up his claim to Hornwood lands as a condition for peace with the Iron Throne. When Wyman is at Winterfell, Ramsay Bolton is introduced as Lord of the Hornwood.
