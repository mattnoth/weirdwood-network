Fingers is an ironman and one of the crew on Asha Greyjoy's ship, Black Wind. 

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Fingers sails to Deepwood Motte with Asha Greyjoy as she contemplates her next move after the kingsmoot on Old Wyk. He is captured along with Asha, Tristifer Botley, and several other ironborn during the fight by Deepwood Motte by the army of Stannis Baratheon. 

Fingers is later freed and ransomed along with the other surviving ironborn by Tycho Nestoris. The Braavosi banker needs an escort to get him to Stannis, whose army is marching on Winterfell.
