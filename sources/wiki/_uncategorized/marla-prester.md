## Full Text

Marla Prester was a member of House Prester, and the second wife of Ser Jason Lannister.

History[ ]

According to semi-canon sources, after the death of Alys Stackspear in childbirth, Jason Lannister was quickly married to Marla Prester, who was twice his age, to prevent him from fathering more bastards. She gave birth to Joanna, Stafford, two more sons and two more daughters.
