## Full Text

The "giant ape" is a species of ape native to the continent of Sothoryos, said to inhabit the forests south of Yeen. They supposed to be larger than the largest giants and being to powerful they can slay elephants with a single blow.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

Giant apes appears to be based on the fictitious Megaprimatus kong, the species of King Kong, the famed giant ape of the King Kong media franchise.
