Geography[ ]

The Three Sisters and Pebble are islands within the Bite.

To the west the Bite reaches the Neck, and the landscape between the kingsroad and the bay is a bleak and barren shore. The cold salt sea has strong winds and opens onto the narrow sea and the Shivering Sea to the east. To the south are the Mountains of the Moon and the Fingers.

White Harbor is located at the mouth of the White Knife in the bay's north, and Oldcastle is then found to the southeast along the northern coast. Farther to the northeast are Ramsgate and the Broken Branch, near where the Bite meets the deeper seas.

Culture[ ]

Fisherfolk from White Harbor sail the Bite, and Sistermen have a reputation for smuggling and wrecking.

History[ ]

Pirate kings of the Three Sisters controlled the Bite and neighboring waters in antiquity. House Arryn, the Kings of Mountain and Vale, gained control of the Sisters through the War Across the Water.

During the Dance of the Dragons, Prince Jacaerys Velaryon flew on Vermax over the Bite to Sisterton and White Harbor. In 132 AC, Winter Fever crossed the Bite from the Sisters to White Harbor.

After the outbreak of Robert's Rebellion, Lord Eddard Stark attempted to return to the north with the aid of a fisherman from the Fingers. The fisherman drowned during a storm, but his daughter was able to transport Ned to the Three Sisters. Some say Lord Stark impregnated the daughter, who gave birth to Jon Snow.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Catelyn Stark and Ser Rodrik Cassel sail on Storm Dancer from White Harbor to King's Landing. Strong winds in the Bite contribute to Rodrik becoming seasick. During his coma, Bran Stark sees his mother and Rodrik sailing across the Bite during one of his dreams.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Stannis Baratheon tasks Ser Davos Seaworth with sailing on Black Betha north from Dragonstone to the Bite, delivering the king's letters to settlements along the way. 

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

While sailing for Braavos, Sloe-Eyed Maid is blown by a gale into the Bite, and the ship is wrecked at Sweetsister. 

The remnants of Salladhor Saan's fleet sails toward White Harbor with Davos. Tired of storms and Stannis's inability to pay, Salladhor sets Davos loose in a rowboat near Sisterton and sails for the Stepstones.

Lionstar transports three Freys to Sisterton and then White Harbor. Ser Wylis Manderly returns to White Harbor from Maidenpool.
