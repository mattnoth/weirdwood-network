## Summary

The Dragon's Peace was the term the maesters of the Citadel employ to refer to the last two decades of the reign of Aegon I Targaryen over the Seven Kingdoms of Westeros. The term was chosen in opposition to the Dragon's wars that marked Aegon's early reign.
