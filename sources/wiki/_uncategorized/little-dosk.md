## Summary

Little Dosk was a village in the Reach. During the reign of King Aerys I Targaryen, it was raided by ironmen and half of the village was destroyed. It was once controlled by House Osgrey, but the power of the house diminished over the years, and the village eventually came under the power of someone else.
