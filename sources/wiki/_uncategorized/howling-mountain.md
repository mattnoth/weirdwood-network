## Full Text

The Howling Mountain is one of the Basilisk Isles, which are off the northern coast of Sothoryos.

History[ ]

Some of Nymeria's ten thousand ships were attacked by corsairs from the Howling Mountain. Afterwards, the corsairs offered the Rhoynar to settle upon the Isle of Toads, on the condition that they give up their boats and that they'd send each king thirty virgin girls and pretty boys per year, but Nymeria refused.
