Recent Events[ ]

The Winds of Winter[ ]

When Ser Harrold Hardyng arrives at the Gates of the Moon for the tourney which will determine the Brotherhood of Winged Knights, he is given chambers in the Falcon Tower, far from those of Lord Robert Arryn. Alayne Stone offers to escort Harrold to his rooms, but he coldly refuses the girl.

Quotes[ ]

Alayne: You are in the Falcon Tower, Ser Harrold. If it please you, I will show you to your chambers myself.

Harrold: Why should it please me to be escorted anywhere by Littlefinger's bastard?

—Alayne Stone and Harrold Hardyng
