## Summary

Addam Rivers was a knight and a bastard of the Riverlands who claimed the title of River King or King of the Trident in the generations after the riverlands were conquered by the Storm King Arlan III Durrandon. Addam had a brief, inglorious reign, before being put down by the might of Storm's End.
