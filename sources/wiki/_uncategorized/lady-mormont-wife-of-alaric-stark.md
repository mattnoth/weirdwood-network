## Full Text

Lady Mormont was the wife of Lord Alaric Stark and the Lady of Winterfell during the early reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen. Her given name is unknown.

History[ ]

When she was twelve, she took an axe to a pack of wolves, killed two of them, and sewed a cloak from their skins. At some point, she married Alaric Stark, the second son of Lord Brandon Stark. They had three children together, two sons and a daughter, Alarra.

In 50 AC, she became the Lady of Winterfell after the death of her brother-in-law, Lord Walton Stark. She died five years later.
