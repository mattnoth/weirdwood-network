## Full Text

Lord of the Five Fingers was a title assumed by Ser Corwyn Corbray during the coming of the Andals to Westeros.

History[ ]

The Andal knight Ser Corwyn Corbray claimed the Fingers after the deaths of Dywen Shell and Jon Brightstone, rival Kings of the Fingers of First Men origin. Corwyn took Jon's daughter for his bride and Dywin's wife for his bedwarmer. Unlike many of his fellow Andal warlords, Corbray did not style himself as a king, instead preferring the more modest Lord of the Five Fingers.

At some point in history, the title was abandoned and House Corbray began to style themselves Kings of the Fingers.
