## Full Text

Soldier pine is a type of pine tree found in much of Westeros. The tall, green pine is plentiful in the north and beyond the Wall. Soldier pines grow thickly in the haunted forest, the wolfswood, and in the godswood of Winterfell. Scrawny soldier pines grow in the northern mountains.

Green soldier pines grow in the Whispering Wood in the northern riverlands, along the Rosby road, and throughout Crackclaw Point in the crownlands. Blue soldier pines can be found near the Mountains of the Moon. Soldier pines also grow as far south as the rainwood in the stormlands. Blue-green soldier pines cover the mountains of Great Wyk in the Iron Islands.

Soldier pines also grow along the steep ridges guarding the lagoon of Braavos in Essos, such as Sellagoro's Shield.
