History[ ]

In 206 AC the Lords Dondarrion and Caron joined forces and defeated the Vulture King, burning him out of the Red Mountains. Whether he was related by blood to other "Vulture Kings" is unknown.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Speaking with Davos Seaworth about traitors, Stannis Baratheon mentions the Vulture King among a series of people who paid for treason with their lives. Stannis does not specify which Vulture King he means.
