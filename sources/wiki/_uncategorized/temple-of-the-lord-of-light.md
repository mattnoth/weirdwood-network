Layout[ ]

The Temple of the Lord of Light is found along the river road on the eastern shore of the Rhoyne, beneath the Black Walls. The enormous temple has numerous pillars, steps, buttresses, bridges, domes, and towers which flow into one another, and its walls are colored in hues of red, yellow, gold, and orange. According to Archmaester Gramyon in Remnants of the Dragonlords, the temple is three times the size of the Great Sept of Baelor in King's Landing.

The High Priest preaches from a red stone pillar in the great plaza found before the temple. A slender stone bridge connects the pillar to a lofty terrace of the temple. Huge nightfires burn besides the temple's steps, with the pillar standing between them. Crowds can gather in the torchlit plaza to listen to the priests.

History[ ]

Triarch Malaquo Maegyr tried to hire the Golden Company to clean out the red temple and have Benerro put to the sword, but the sellswords refused.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

In Volantis, thousands of slaves and freedmen crowd the temple plaza to hear Benerro shriek of bleeding stars and a sword of fire that will cleanse the world.

Tyrion Lannister and Ser Jorah Mormont pass through the crowded temple plaza as Benerro preaches one night. Shouts erupt from the crowd as Benerro speaks, with women weeping and men shaking their fists. The atmosphere reminds Tyrion of the day his niece, Princess Myrcella Baratheon, sailed for Dorne and the riot that boiled up as the royal retinue made their way back to the Red Keep.
