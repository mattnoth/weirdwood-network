Appearance[ ]

Falia is buxom and roughly seventeen or eighteen years old. She is described as pretty.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

  

Falia in the lap of Euron Greyjoy, her father Humfrey Hewett bound besides them - by Mathia Arkoniel©

When King Euron Greyjoy comes with the ironborn after the taking of the Shields, Lord Humfrey Hewett is tied to his chair and made to watch his wife and daughters serve the raiders. Falia, who becomes Euron's lover, suggests the idea that the women serve naked. Lord Humfrey's wife and daughters have their clothes removed.

Euron later takes Falia upstairs to Lord Hewett's chambers. When Victarion Greyjoy is summoned to Euron, he spots Falia sleeping naked on the bed.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

When Aeron Greyjoy is brought to Oakenshield he is fed by Falia. Aeron asks her to tell Victarion to rescue him, but she says he has already departed the island. She reveals that she is pregnant and claims Euron will make her his salt wife and her sons will be before his other bastard sons. Falia states that Daenerys Targaryen will be Euron's rock wife and that Falia will be like a sister to her. Aeron warns her to flee, saying Euron will hurt her and kill her, but Falia refuses in disbelief, stating Euron loves her and gives her silks and jewels.

Later, when Aeron is lashed to the prow of the Silence, Falia is tied next to him. She is naked, pregnant, her tongue has been cut out, and her face is red from crying.
