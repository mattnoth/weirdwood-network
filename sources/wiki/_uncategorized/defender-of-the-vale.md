History[ ]

House Arryn ruled as Kings of Mountain and Vale after the Battle of the Seven Stars, which was part of the coming of the Andals to Westeros. The Arryns submitted to House Targaryen during Aegon's Conquest, however, and have since served as Defenders of the Vale and Wardens of the East.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Young Lord Robert Arryn serves as Defender of the Vale after the death of his father, Lord Jon Arryn.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Jon's widow, Lady Lysa Arryn, weds Lord Petyr Baelish and names him Lord Protector of the Vale.
