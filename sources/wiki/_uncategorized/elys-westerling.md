Elys Westerling was a knight from House Westerling during the reign of Aerys II Targaryen. His relation to Gawen Westerling, the Lord of the Crag, is unknown.

History[ ]

Elys participated in the Sack of King's Landing. He and Lord Roland Crakehall were the first into the throne room where Ser Jaime Lannister slew King Aerys II Targaryen.
