Joss is a brewer in the town of Sherrer in the riverlands.

In the television adaptation Game of Thrones, Joss is portrayed by Niall Cusack.

Appearance and Character[ ]

Joss is plump and balding. He claims to have the finest ale south of the Neck.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Joss is one of the witnesses who has seen the raiders from the westerlands when they destroyed his town. Together with other survivors, he is sent to King's Landing to describe the events before the Iron Throne.
