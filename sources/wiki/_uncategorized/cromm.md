Character[ ]

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Cromm sails to Deepwood Motte with Asha Greyjoy as she contemplates her next move after the kingsmoot on Old Wyk. He stands watch at the castle with Hagen the Horn.

Cromm flees with Asha toward the Bay of Ice, but is wounded in the fight by Deepwood Motte. Cromm is taken captive by Stannis Baratheon along with several other ironborn, whom he calls the ragged nine. 

Cromm dies of his wounds in captivity at Deepwood.
