Character[ ]

Eddard Stark considers Porther brave but headstrong.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Porther accompanies Lord Eddard to King's Landing when Ned is named Hand of the King. While in the capital, Porther is known to make the rounds of the brothels.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Porther is listed as dead by Bran Stark.

Theon has a nightmare of eating with the dead, including Porther, at Winterfell.

Quotes[ ]

Porther was a brave man, but headstrong. He wanted someone more solid and sensible to keep watch over his daughters.
