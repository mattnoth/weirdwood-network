History[ ]

During Aegon's Conquest, Lord Piper summoned his levies and joined King Aegon I Targaryen against Harren Hoare, King of the Isles and the Rivers.

Lord Jon Piper fought for Prince Aegon the Uncrowned against King Maegor I Targaryen.

Lord Petyr Piper declared for the blacks and Rhaenyra Targaryen during the Dance of the Dragons. After the Battle at the Red Fork, he knighted Pate of Longleaf for mortally wounding Jason Lannister, Lord of Casterly Rock. Petyr died soon after in a battle at Acorn Hall and was succeeded by Lord Stanton Piper. Stanton and Lord Lyonel Deddings were burned by Vermithor during the Second Battle of Tumbleton.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lord Clement Piper and Lord Vance are ordered by Ser Edmure Tully to guard the pass below the Golden Tooth when the Lannisters start to mass troops there. Ser Jaime Lannister defeats them in a battle in the hills below the Golden Tooth, forcing Clement to flee.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord Clement sits on the war council of the royal forces at the siege of Riverrun.
