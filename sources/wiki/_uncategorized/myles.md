Appearance and Character[ ]

The young Myles has a silky, perfumed beard and a servile smile. Myles wears the grey robes of a maester.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

While Maester Caleotte is with Doran Martell, Prince of Dorne, at the Water Gardens, Maester Myles remains at Sunspear to deal with letters not requiring Doran's attention. Myles and the rest of the court greet the prince when he returns from the Water Gardens.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Myles is present when Ser Balon Swann arrives in Sunspear to deliver the Mountain's skull. For the feast welcoming Balon, Myles prepares a wine laced with poppy juice to alleviate the pain in Doran's joints.
