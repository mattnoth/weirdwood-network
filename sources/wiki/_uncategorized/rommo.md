Appearance[ ]

Rommo is wizened, bowlegged and squint-eyed.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When Daenerys Targaryen's bloodriders, Aggo, Jhogo, and Rakharo, are unavailable, Rommo represents her Dothraki at the council in Meereen.

At the council called by Ser Barristan Selmy, Rommo agrees that the hostage Jhogo must be freed.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Rommo gathers twenty Dothraki riders and is their captain in Barristan's host.
