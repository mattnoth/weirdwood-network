Distribution[ ]

Spotted panthers larger than lions have been found in the rain forests of the Summer Isles, where they prowl beneath the canopies of trees.

Culture[ ]

Panthers have been depicted on Westerosi banners.

History[ ]

After actions taken by Malthar Xaq and other princes of the Summer Isles began a new era of exploration and trade for their people, a fashion developed amongst the dragonlords of Valyria for panther cubs from the islands.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

When first laying eyes on Khal Drogo, Daenerys Targaryen thinks him as graceful as the panther in the menagerie of Illyrio Mopatis. Similarly, Catelyn Stark thinks Bronn moves like a panther during a fight.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

When Tywin Lannister takes his leave of Harrenhal, one of the banners Arya Stark spies traveling with his lords and captains bears a panther on it.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Daenerys thinks Barsena Blackhair, a pit fighter in Meereen, moves with the feral grace of a panther.
