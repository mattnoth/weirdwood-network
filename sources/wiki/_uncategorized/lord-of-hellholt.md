History[ ]

After the death of Mors Martell, Princess Nymeria took the aged Lord Uller as her second husband.

During the First Dornish War, Lord Harlan Tyrell was sent by King Aegon I Targaryen to the Hellholt, seat of Lord Uthor Uller. However, by the time Harlan's host arrived, the castle was abandoned. During the Dragon's Wroth, four consecutive Lords Uller (Uthor was possibly amongst them) were murdered for the Iron Throne's ransom on their head.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Harmen Uller is among the Dornish nobles that accompany Oberyn Martell to King's Landing.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Harmen is present at the feast when Balon Swann arrives in Sunspear to deliver the head of Ser Gregor Clegane. He refuses to drink when Ricasso raises a toast to King Tommen Baratheon.
