The sigil of House Greyiron.

The sea king is a figure depicted on the arms of House Greyiron, according to a semi-canon source. He has dark green seaweed beard and hair.

The sea king may be related to the Drowned God, since his priests use dried seaweed which they braid through their long hair and untrimmed beard.
