## Full Text

Griffith Goode was a knight from House Goode during the reign of King Aegon I Targaryen. He had a brother, Ser Gregor Goode.

Character[ ]

Griffith is remembered for being quick, strong, observant, and famed for his prowess with sword and shield. He was devoted to his king.

History[ ]

Ser Griffith was chosen by Queen Visenya Targaryen, alongside his brother Ser Gregor, to be one of the first sworn brothers of the Kingsguard when the order was founded in 10 AC by King Aegon I Targaryen.
