A Feast For Crows[ ]

Lord Rodrik Harlaw summons his banners to support the claim of his niece, Asha Greyjoy, to the Seastone Chair. The Myres are divided in their answer as some Myres have answered Rodrik's call and came to Ten Towers, whilst Pinchface Jon Myre sailed to Pyke with a dozen longships to support Euron Greyjoy. It is unknown which claimant Lord Myre supports.
