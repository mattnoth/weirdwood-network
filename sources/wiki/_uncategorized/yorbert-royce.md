History[ ]

Lord Yorbert Royce was the regent and the Lord Protector of the Vale during the minority of Lady Jeyne Arryn, after her lord father and elder brothers were slain by the Stone Crows. In that capacity, he attended the Great Council of 101 AC in her stead. The text does not state the candidate Yorbert supported in the final vote at the council, however.

Rhea Royce, the heir to Runestone, was married to Prince Daemon Targaryen in 97 AC. By the time of the accession of King Viserys I Targaryen in 103 AC, Rhea had succeeded Yorbert as Lady of Runestone, indicating that he had passed away.
