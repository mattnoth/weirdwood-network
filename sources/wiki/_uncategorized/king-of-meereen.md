History[ ]

According to Ser Barristan Selmy, it has been "a thousand years" since Meereen last had a king.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

  

Queen Daenerys Targaryen in the audience chamber of the Great Pyramid, by Marc Simonetti © LeYa Brasil

In 300 AC, Daenerys Targaryen takes Meereen from the Great Masters, becoming the city's new ruler.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Daenerys is introduced as "Queen of Meereen" as she holds court in the Great Pyramid.

Daenerys weds Hizdahr zo Loraq, through which he becomes Meereen's king. After Daenerys disappears from Daznak's Pit on Drogon, Hizdahr rules over the city as King of Meereen.

Skahaz mo Kandaq convinces Ser Barristan Selmy that Hizdahr played a part in the attempt on Daenerys's life. Together with Grey Worm, Barristan and Skahaz arrest Hizdahr and install a council to rule the city in Daenerys's absence.

Known monarchs of Meereen[ ]

 Hizdahr zo Loraq, Fourteenth of That Noble Name, King of Meereen, Scion of Ghis, Octarch of the Old Empire, Master of the Skahazadhan, Consort to Dragons and Blood of the Harpy
