Archmaester Edgerran was one of the archmaesters at the Citadel. 

Appearance and Character[ ]

Edgerran's ring, rod, and mask were made of silver, which meant he was an expert in medicine.

History[ ]

Edgerran was the Seneschal of the Citadel in 272 AC. He wrote a treatise on the swaddling of infants and tested his theories on the infant Maester Yandel.
