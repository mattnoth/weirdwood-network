## Full Text

Not to be confused with Halys Hornwood.

Hallis Hornwood, known as Mad Hal, was a member of House Hornwood during the Dance of the Dragons.

History[ ]

Hallis accompanied Lord Cregan Stark south to King's Landing during the Dance of the Dragons. After war's end, Hal and Timotty Snow founded the Wolf Pack, a free company which sailed from Westeros for the Free Cities.
