The Tarth triplets were members of House Tarth during the reign of King Aegon I Targaryen. Their given names have not been revealed.

History[ ]

During the early reign of King Aegon I Targaryen, the wife of the Evenstar of Tarth gave birth to triplets daughters. Aegon's sister-wife, Queen Rhaenys Targaryen, arranged betrothals for the girls with members of Houses Corbray, Hightower, and Harlaw.
