Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Eleyna becomes part of King Robb Stark's retinue when he marries her elder sister, Jeyne. The sisters stay at Riverrun with their mother, Sybell Spicer, and their younger brother, Rollam, when Robb goes to the Twins for the marriage of Lord Edmure Tully.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Eleyna is confined in Riverrun during its siege. She is freed and reunited with her father, Lord Gawen, when Ser Jaime Lannister successfully ends the siege. For her actions that undermined Robb’s cause, Lady Sybell receives the promise of good marriage prospects for her two daughters by the Lannister regime. Eleyna returns home with her family among the column led by Ser Forley Prester.
