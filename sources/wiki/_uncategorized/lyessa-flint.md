Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lady Lyessa does not attend the harvest feast at Winterfell. She is pregnant and there is a sickness at Widow's Watch.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lyessa's son, Robin Flint, is killed at the Red Wedding by members of House Frey.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lady Lyessa survived the sickness at Widow's Watch and she is still the head of the house. However, it is unknown if her baby survived.

According to Lord Wyman Manderly, should he declared for King Stannis Baratheon, the Flints of Widow's Watch will take their lead from him.
