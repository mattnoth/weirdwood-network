## Full Text

Raymun and its variants are a common male name in Westeros. It may refer to:

Raymun Darry, a knight and the head of House Darry

Raymun Fossoway, founding member of House Fossoway of New Barrel

Raymun Redbeard, a former King-Beyond-the-Wall

Raymund Connington, younger brother of Ser Ronnet Connington

Raymund Frey, a knight and the eleventh son of Lord Walder Frey

Raymund Mallery, a knight of the Kingsguard for King Maegor I Targaryen

Raymund Tyrell, a cousin of Margaery Tyrell

Raymond Nayland, a knight in Renly Baratheon's camp

Raymond Rychard, a knight present at the Ashford tourney in 209 AC, he is named after George R. R. Martin

Raymont Baratheon, a knight of the Kingsguard for Aenys I Targaryen (dubious canonicity)
