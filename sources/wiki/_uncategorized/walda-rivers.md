Walda Rivers is the daughter of a lady of House Charlton and Ser Walder Rivers, the eldest natural son of Lord Walder Frey.

Family[ ]

Walder Rivers's daughter Walda Rivers only appears in the appendices. Meanwhile, Walder Rivers's son, Aemon Rivers, has a four-year-old daughter also named Walda Rivers, who appears in A Storm of Swords but not in any appendices. It is unclear whether they are two different Waldas or they are the same person, with Aemon being Walda Rivers's actual father.
