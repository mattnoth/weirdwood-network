History[ ]

According to tales, some Warrior's Sons were dragonslayers.

In 10 AC at the Hellholt, an iron bolt from a scorpion was shot through Meraxes's eye. The dragon fell from the sky with her rider, Queen Rhaenys Targaryen, destroying half the castle.

In 130 AC, several dragons were slain during the storming of the Dragonpit, some of them by the Shepherd's "lambs". Following the Second Battle of Tumbleton, Tessarion was greatly wounded from her fight with other dragons. Lord Benjicot Blackwood ordered his best archer, Billy Burley, to mercy kill the dragon, and Billy fired three shafts through her eye.

In 135 AC, the cradle egg of the infant Laena Velaryon hatched into a blind, wingless wyrm that immediately attacked her, and was in return killed by her father, Lord Alyn Velaryon.
