The Witch Isle is one of the threescore islands off the eastern and northern coasts of the Vale of Arryn. It is the seat of House Upcliff, who have a sinister reputation.

History[ ]

The Witch Isle was brought under the rule of House Arryn by the marriage of Alester II Arryn, King of Mountain and Vale, to Arwen Upcliff.
