Ocean Road

The Reach and the location of the Ocean Road

The Ocean Road, ocean road, or Searoad, is a road in southern Westeros that connects the westerlands and the Reach. Most of its course is along the coast of the Sunset Sea.

Route[ ]

  

The Ocean Road by Yoann Boissonnet © Fantasy Flight Games

The Ocean Road begins at Lannisport and runs south along the western coast of Westeros overlooking the Sunset Sea. It passes through the forest around Crakehall and then to Old Oak. It continues southeast along the coast, opposite the Shield Islands. It then moves inland to Highgarden on the Mander.

History[ ]

During Aegon's Conquest, Mern IX Gardener, King of the Reach, took the ocean road to Casterly Rock to meet with Loren I Lannister, King of the Rock.

The coastal road was remade during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen, who was aided by his master of coin, Lord Martyn Tyrell.
