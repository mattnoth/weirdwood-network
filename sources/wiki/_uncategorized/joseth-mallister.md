## Full Text

Joseth Mallister was a knight from House Mallister during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen.

Appearance[ ]

Joseth wore a winged helm. His shield bore a silver eagle flying across an indigo sky, the arms of House Mallister.

History[ ]

In 209 AC, Ser Joseth participated in the tourney at Ashford Meadow. He challenged Ser Humfrey Hardyng in the second tilt, but was defeated and carried from the field unconscious.
