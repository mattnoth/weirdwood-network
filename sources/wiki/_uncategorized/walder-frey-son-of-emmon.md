Character[ ]

Red Walder is a page or squire at Casterly Rock, the seat of House Lannister. His nickname might come from the crimson field in the Lannister coat of arms, since his mother Genna comes from that family, or he might have red hair.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Red Walder is mentioned by his cousins, Big Walder and Little Walder, when they discuss the line of succession of House Frey.
