Layout[ ]

The Small Hall is a long room with a high-vaulted ceiling and bench space for two hundred. It has trestled tables and great wooden doors.

The Queen's Ballroom is half as big as the Small Hall.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lord Eddard Stark resides in the Tower of the Hand after becoming Hand of the King to Robert I Baratheon. Eddard, his daughters Sansa and Arya, and his retinue take their meals in the Small Hall.

Syrio Forel trains Arya in the water dance in the cleared Small Hall. Ser Meryn Trant and House Lannister guards attempt to seize Arya during the arrest of Eddard Stark, but Syrio defends the girl and allows her to escape the hall.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Tyrion Lannister, acting Hand of the King to Joffrey I Baratheon, has Lord Janos Slynt seized by gold cloaks during dinner in the Small Hall. Tyrion then meets with Lord Varys in the hall. 

Tyrion takes a meal in the Small Hall with some of his protectors, Stone Crows and Moon Brothers.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

After Tyrion marries Sansa in the royal sept, their wedding feast is held in the Small Hall.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

The wedding feast for King Tommen I Baratheon and Margaery Tyrell is held in the Small Hall. Afterward, Queen Regent Cersei Lannister has the Tower of the Hand ruined with wildfire.

Quotes[ ]
