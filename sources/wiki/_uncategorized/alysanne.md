## Full Text

Alysanne is a popular female name in Westeros. 

Characters[ ]

It may refer to the following characters:

Alysanne, eldest bastard daughter of King Aegon IV Targaryen by his second mistress, Megette

Alysanne Blackwood, second wife of Lord Cregan Stark

Alysanne Bracken, fifth daughter of Lord Jonos Bracken

Alysanne Bulwer, the head of House Bulwer

Alysanne Farman, first wife of Lord Gerold Lannister

Alysanne Hightower, fifth daughter of Lord Leyton Hightower

Alysanne Lefford, the head of House Lefford

Alysanne Osgrey, only daughter of Ser Eustace Osgrey

Alysanne Stark, youngest daughter of Lord Beron Stark

Alysanne Targaryen, also known as Good Queen Alysanne, second daughter of King Aenys I Targaryen and Queen Alyssa Velaryon, and later the wife of her brother, King Jaehaerys I Targaryen

Alysanne of Tarth, one of the daughters of Lord Selwyn of Tarth

Culture[ ]

"Alysanne", a sad song in the Seven Kingdoms
