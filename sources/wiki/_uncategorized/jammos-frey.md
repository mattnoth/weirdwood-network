## Summary

Jammos Frey is a knight of House Frey and the thirteenth son of Lord Walder Frey, the second by his fourth wife, Lady Alyssa Blackwood. Jammos is married to Sallei Paege and has three children: Big Walder, Dickon, and Mathis Frey.
