Layout[ ]

The Library Tower is located across the bailey from the Great Keep. Snug and warm, the Library Tower has high windows. The tower contains shelves, benches, and a table. Winterfell's library contains more than a hundred books and includes rare volumes, including a complete copy of Engines of War by Ayrmidon. Curling around the tower's exterior are steep stone steps. 

History[ ]

The library of Winterfell has been collected by House Stark for centuries. When Queen Alysanne Targaryen visited Winterfell in 58 AC, Lord Alaric Stark allowed her to browse the modest castle library.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Tyrion Lannister explores the library during his visit to Winterfell and reads a book about the changing of the seasons. Meanwhile, Septon Chayle falls asleep while reading a history of Grand Maester Aethelmure. Lord Eddard Stark allows Tyrion to borrow a few rare volumes for his journey north to the Wall.

An assassin sets the Library Tower on fire as a distraction for his attempt to kill Bran Stark.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Bran and Chayle sort through scrolls rescued from the library fire.

After the sack of Winterfell, Bran sees a shallow steaming lake beneath the Library Tower and a crack in its side.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Sansa Stark includes the Library Tower while making a snow castle of Winterfell while at the Eyrie.
