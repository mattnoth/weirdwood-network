For other characters with the same name, see Walda Frey.

Walda Frey, known as Fat Walda, is the second daughter of Merrett Frey and Mariya Darry, and a granddaughter of Lord Walder Frey, the head of House Frey.

In the television adaptation Game of Thrones, Walda is portrayed by Elizabeth Webster.

Appearance[ ]

Walda is a short, large girl with watery blue eyes, three chins, a huge bosom, and blonde hair. Her voice is fluttering and squeaky.

Walda wears a sable hood.

Recent Events[ ]

  

Walda and her new husband, Roose Bolton.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Walda weds Roose Bolton, Lord of the Dreadfort, in a ceremony at the Twins. While Roose holds Harrenhal, Walda frequently sends letters to him which he reads and then burns without reply. Roose allows his cupbearer "Nan", actually an incognito Arya Stark, to read him the letters.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Roose reveals to Ser Jaime Lannister that Walder Frey, Lord of the Crossing, had offered Roose his prospective bride's weight in silver for her dowry. Lord Bolton chose the fattest available female member of House Frey.

During the feast at the Twins after the wedding of Lord Edmure Tully and Roslin Frey, Walda tells Ser Wendel Manderly she is glad to have been chosen by Roose. She leaves the hall during the bedding custom and is not present for the Red Wedding massacre.

In the aftermath of the Red Wedding, Walda's father Merrett is hanged by the brotherhood without banners at Oldstones.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Walda travels north with her husband along with his army and her kin, Ser Aenys and Ser Hosteen Frey. She meets her stepson, Ramsay Bolton, and Theon Greyjoy, after the siege of Moat Cailin is resolved. At Barrowton, Roose tells Theon he expects Ramsay will kill any sons birthed by Walda.

Walda is present at the wedding of Ramsay and "Arya Stark", actually Jeyne Poole, in the godswood of Winterfell. During the wedding feast in the Great Hall of Winterfell, she has three slices of pork pie.

As Winterfell becomes snowbound by a blizzard, Theon notices that Walda is pregnant. Her younger brother, Little Walder Frey, is found murdered at Winterfell.
