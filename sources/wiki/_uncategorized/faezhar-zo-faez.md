Appearance[ ]

Faezhar is a small man with a receding chin. Two of his front teeth are too big to fit in his mouth, so he is compared to a rabbit.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The men of the Windblown, a sellsword company hired by Yunkai, mock Faezhar as "the Rabbit". 

Faezhar, along with his other two supreme commanders and Bloodbeard, presents the head of captain Groleo to Hizdahr zo Loraq and demands the deaths of Daenerys Targaryen's dragons in return for the release of the other hostages, Hero, Daario Naharis, and Jhogo.

Following the death of Yurkhaz zo Yunzak in the fighting pits of Meereen, the Yunkai'i started daily rotating the duty of supreme commander. Faezhar is among the men who take part in the rotation.
