## Full Text

Harmon of the Reeds, called Iron-Banger, was a knight loyal to the blacks during the civil war known as the Dance of the Dragons.

History[ ]

During the Storming of the Dragonpit, Ser Harmon was one of the Seven Who Rode, men sent by Queen Rhaenyra Targaryen to recover her son, Prince Joffrey Velaryon, as he tried to save the dragons. Joffrey was killed after falling off Syrax, however, and a mob tore apart his body. Iron-Banger survived the battle with smallfolk over the prince's remains.

Harmon and twenty other knights loyal to Rhaenyra were executed by Ser Perkin the Flea after the city gates were opened by Ser Garth the Harelip.
