Bonetown

Location of Bonetown in the known world

Bonetown is a rumored town in the Further East of Essos. 

Geography[ ]

Bonetown is situated just north of the Dry Deep, southwest of the Cannibal Sands, and east of the Land of the Shrykes. The city of K'Dath is to the northwest.

History[ ]

Bonetown may be named after its trade in strange, aged bones found in the nearby Dry Deep. It is also told that the town is made entirely of bones.
