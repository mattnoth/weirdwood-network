Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Kromm is part of the force that accompanies Theon Greyjoy, first in the harrying of the Stony Shore and then in the capture of Winterfell. Theon uses Kromm as a guard within Winterfell.

Kromm remains with Theon after Ser Rodrik Cassel comes to retake Winterfell, and he informs Theon about the battle outside the gates of Winterfell. Kromm is presumably slain during the sack of Winterfell by House Bolton.

Theon: Is it Asha?

Kromm: No. These are northmen, I tell you. With a bloody man on their banner.
