## Full Text

Elaena Targaryen was a member of House Targaryen during the Century of Blood, the eldest daughter of Lord Gaemon and Lady Daenys Targaryen of Dragonstone.

History[ ]

Elaena married her brother, Aegon, and together they had two sons: Maegon and Aerys Targaryen. Elaena and Aegon also had a younger sister who married a petty lord.

Elaena and Aegon inherited the lordship of Dragonstone from their father and mother, they jointly ruled Dragonstone and were succeeded by Maegon.
