Appearance[ ]

Flame was a blood bay with a bright eye and a long fiery mane. She was a big, swift courser, and had some Dornish sand steed blood in her for endurance. Flame was a magnificent horse, with long, clean limbs. She had been bred for beauty and speed.

History[ ]

Lady Rohanne Webber attempted to give Flame to Ser Duncan the Tall as a gift for his service and as an apology. The hedge knight refused, however, saying Flame was too good for him.

Quotes[ ]
