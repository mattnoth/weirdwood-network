Biology[ ]

Spotted whales are carnivorous and hunt in packs, which leads some to compare them with wolves.

Range[ ]

Spotted whales inhabit the Sunset Sea as well as the Shivering Sea.

In culture[ ]

Members of House Farwynd of the Lonely Light are believed by some ironborn to be skinchangers capable of taking the form of spotted whales.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

The description of the animal indicates it is the equivalent of the real life orca.
