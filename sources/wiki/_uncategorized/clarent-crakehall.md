## Full Text

Clarent Crakehall was a knight from House Crakehall during the Dance of the Dragons.

History[ ]

Ser Clarent joined Lord Jason Lannister's host in support of King Aegon II Targaryen during the Dance of the Dragons. Clarent later participated in the Battle by the Lakeshore under Lord Humfrey Lefford, where he was slain in 130 AC.
