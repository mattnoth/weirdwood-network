## Summary

Edwyle Stark was the Lord of Winterfell and the head of House Stark during the late reign of King Maekar I Targaryen and the reign of King Aegon V Targaryen. He was the only son of Lord Willam Stark and his second wife, Lady Melantha Blackwood.

History[ ]

Edwyle married Marna Locke and had only one child with her, Rickard.
Edwyle was buried in the crypt of Winterfell. There is a statue of him placed with his tomb. Edwyle was succeeded as Lord of Winterfell by his son, Rickard Stark.
