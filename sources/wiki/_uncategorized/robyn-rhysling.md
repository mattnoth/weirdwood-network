Appearance[ ]

Robyn was a grizzled old knight with a salt-and-pepper beard and a missing eye.

History[ ]

Ser Robyn lost an eye to a splinter from a broken lance in 204 AC.

During the tourney at Ashford Meadow in 209 AC, Robyn challenged Lord Leo Tyrell to a tilt. Robyn lost his helm to Leo Longthorn's lance in their first course, but refused to yield. Three times more they rode at each other, yet Leo was too chivalrous to aim at Robyn's head. Finally, the Lord of Highgarden struck Robyn's breastplate and sent him to the ground.

Prince Aegon Targaryen brought Robyn to Ser Duncan the Tall's side during the trial of seven, having known him from previous tourneys. Robyn knocked Prince Daeron Targaryen from his horse and also fought one of the Kingsguard.
