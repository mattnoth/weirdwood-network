## Summary

Turrani is a city on the island Leng in the eastern Jade Sea. It is located on the southern shore of the forested island, near the mouth of a river and north of the Manticore Isles. Unlike Leng Yi and Leng Ma in the north, which were founded by men of Yi Ti, Turrani was founded by the original inhabitants of the island, the Lengii. It is a wealthy city rich in spices and gems.
