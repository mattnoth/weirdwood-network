## Full Text

Huzhor Amai, called the Amazing, is a legendary hero and ancestor of the Tall Men, the nation that founded the Kingdom of Sarnor.

History[ ]

According to legend, he was born of the last of the Fisher Queens and took to wife the daughters of the greatest lords and kings of the Gipps, the Cymmeri, and the Zoqora. His Zoqora wife drove his chariot, and his Cymmeri wife made his armor. He also wore about his shoulders a great cloak made from the pelt of a king of the Hairy Men.
