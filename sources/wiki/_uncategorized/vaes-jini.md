## Full Text

Vaes Jini

Essos west of the Bone Mountains and the location of Vaes Jini

Vaes Jini is a ruined city in Essos, located on the Stone Road in the foothills of the western Bone Mountains. It was originally called Yinishar and was inhabited by people related to those of Samyriana, but it is now known by its Dothraki name, Vaes Jini, meaning "City of Goats". It was once located along the Silk Road.

Southwest of Vaes Jini is the ruined city of Adakhakileki and south is the Poison Sea. To the northwest is the edge of the Dothraki sea.
