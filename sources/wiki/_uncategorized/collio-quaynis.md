Character[ ]

Collio's Tyroshi accent is thick, making it sometimes hard to understand him when he sings in the Common Tongue.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Collio is one of the seven singers chosen to compete in the singers tourney at the wedding of King Joffrey I Baratheon and Lady Margaery Tyrell. He sings "The Dance of the Dragons", "Bessa the Barmaid", "The Rains of Castamere", and an unnamed ballad in High Valyrian about two lovers dying in the Doom of Valyria.
