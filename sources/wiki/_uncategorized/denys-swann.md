Denys Swann was a squire from House Swann during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

Denys was among the group of squires who kept company with Princess Daella Targaryen. By the end of 78 AC the princess fell out with Denys and the other squires, as she decided she hated them.
