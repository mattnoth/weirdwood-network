Character[ ]

Jeremy was a dashing young knight.

History[ ]

In his youth, Jeremy was a squire at Highgarden, where he befriended Prince Daeron Targaryen, third son of King Aegon V Targaryen. The two became constant companions, to the point that Daeron broke his betrothal to Olenna Redwyne in 246 AC.

In 251 AC, Prince Daeron led an army against the rebels known as the Rat, the Hawk, and the Pig. Though they managed to quash the rebellion, Daeron was killed in battle, as was Jeremy, who died by his side.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser Barristan Selmy recalls how all three sons of Aegon V Targaryen had married for love (interestingly including Daeron), and how their father had let them have their way.
