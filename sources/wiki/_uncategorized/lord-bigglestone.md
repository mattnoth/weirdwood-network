## Full Text

Lord Bigglestone was the head of House Bigglestone during the civil war known as the Dance of the Dragons.

History[ ]

During the Dance of the Dragons, Lord Bigglestone sided with the blacks and Rhaenyra Targaryen.

Lord Bigglestone, alongside with Lord Chambers and Lord Perryn, joined Ser Pate of Longleaf and his ragged band of survivors from earlier battles and fought together in the Battle by the Lakeshore. He did not survive the battle.
