Character[ ]

Yorko knows the Common Tongue well. He is less friendly than his younger brother Denyo Terys. Denyo helps Yorko do his sums.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

After the Titan's Daughter arrives in Braavos, Captain Ternesio Terys has his son Yorko row Arya Stark ashore in one of the ship's boats. Yorko rows through the canals, describing some of the notable sights of the city to Arya. When the boat arrives at the dock for the House of Black and White, Yorko confirms that Arya knows his name, and replies "Valar dohaeris", before leaving her there.
