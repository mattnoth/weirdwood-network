## Summary

The Shrinking Sea is in eastern Essos. It is east of the Great Sand Sea, north of Yi Ti, and south of the plains of the Jogos Nhai. Trader Town is to the west of the Shrinking Sea. The landlocked Shrinking Sea has drained over time and now is only a few lakes.
