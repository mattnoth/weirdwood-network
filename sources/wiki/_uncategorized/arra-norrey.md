## Full Text

Arra Norrey was a noblewoman of House Norrey during the second century after Aegon's Conquest, and the first wife of Lord Cregan Stark of Winterfell.

History[ ]

Arra was a childhood friend of Cregan Stark. Shortly after Cregan overthrew his regent uncle, Bennard Stark, and took control of the north in 126 AC, she married him. In 128 AC, Arra died while giving birth to their only son together, Rickon Stark.
