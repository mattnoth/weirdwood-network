## Summary

Ryon Allyrion is a knight from House Allyrion. He is the son and heir of Delonne Allyrion, Lady of Godsgrace. Ryon is married to Ynys Yronwood, with whom he has children, at least two sons. He also has a bastard son, Ser Daemon Sand.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser Ryon is among the group of nobles that accompanies Prince Oberyn Martell to King's Landing for the wedding of King Joffrey I Baratheon.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ryon is present in Dorne, along with his bastard son, Ser Daemon Sand, at the feast when Ser Balon Swann arrives in Sunspear to deliver the Mountain's skull. He drinks when Ricasso raises a toast to King Tommen I Baratheon.
