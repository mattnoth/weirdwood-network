Range[ ]

Treecats have been seen in the wolfswood in the north, Wat's Wood in the Reach, and the Forest of Qohor near Qohor.

Culture[ ]

A yellow and black spotted treecat is included in the arms of House Myatt.

History[ ]

Ser Eustace Osgrey was surprised to find the carcass of a great brown tree cat in Wat's Wood, having believed them all gone from his forest.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Summer and Shaggydog run down an elk in the wolfswood and have to fend off a tan-and-brown treecat snarling at them from an oak tree.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Outside the Twins, Arya Stark sees a man with a treecat sigil on his doublet exit a pale blue tent and chase after a laughing girl.
