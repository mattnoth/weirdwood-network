## Full Text

Osric Stark was a member of House Stark who became Lord Commander of the Night's Watch around 400 BC.

History[ ]

Osric was only ten years old when he was elected Lord Commander of the Night's Watch, making him the youngest chosen for that position. He was a son or brother of a Stark King in the North. Maester Balder wrote The Edge of the World during Osric's rule of sixty years.
