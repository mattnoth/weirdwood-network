Timotty Snow, known as the Bastard of Flint's Finger, was a member of House Flint of Flint's Finger.

History[ ]

Timotty accompanied Lord Cregan Stark south to King's Landing during the Dance of the Dragons. After war's end, Timotty and Hallis Hornwood founded the Wolf Pack, a free company which sailed from Westeros for the Free Cities.
