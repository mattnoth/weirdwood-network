## Full Text

Irri is a Dothraki handmaid to Daenerys Targaryen.

In the television adaptation Game of Thrones, Irri is portrayed by Amrita Acharia.

Appearance[ ]

See also: Images of Irri

Irri has skin the color of copper, with black hair and "almond-shaped eyes". She and Jhiqui are of an age with Daenerys Targaryen. She is slimmer than Jhiqui.

History[ ]

Irri was born to a khal of a khalasar. It was destroyed by Khal Drogo, and she was enslaved.

Recent Events[ ]

  

Irri and Jhiqui, by Rae Lavergne ©

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Irri, Doreah, and Jhiqui are given to Daenerys Targaryen by Viserys Targaryen as wedding gifts to serve as her handmaids. She is given to Daenerys to teach her how to ride horses in the Dothraki style. She stays with Daenerys, even after the death of Khal Drogo and the disintegration of his khalasar.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

After struggling through the red waste, Irri and Jhiqui attempt to convince Daenerys not to stay in Vaes Tolorro, believing it haunted by ghosts.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Irri and Jhiqui are seasick during the voyage to Astapor. Irri becomes a paramour to the lonely Daenerys. Daenerys feels guilt for this. Irri is present when Daenerys purchases the Unsullied. Irri and Jhiqui free Rhaegal and Viserion to attack the Astapori slavers when Dany has the Unsullied attack their former masters.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Irri and Jhiqui both develop a crush on Rakharo who has grown more tall and muscular. They argue over which of them he would prefer.

Irri accompanies Daenerys to Daznak's Pit as part of the royal procession. She and Jhiqui both depart Meereen as part of the search parties dispatched across the Skahazadhan to locate Daenerys after she disappears on Drogon's back.
