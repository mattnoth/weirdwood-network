Layout[ ]

The sept is a small building with fragrant incense. Septon Chayle hangs carved masks of the Seven on the sept's seven walls. Septa Mordane tutors Sansa and Arya Stark.

History[ ]

Eddard Stark, Lord of Winterfell, had the sept built after Robert's Rebellion so that his new southron wife, Catelyn Tully, could pray to the Seven after moving to the north.

Arya Stark has prayed Arya with her mother in the sept and with her father in the godswood of Winterfell.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

After Lord Eddard Stark decides to bring Bran Stark with him to King's Landing, Lady Catelyn Stark prays seven times that Ned would change his mind and leave their son at Winterfell. When Bran becomes crippled and comatose after falling from the First Keep, Catelyn wishes that it had instead been Jon Snow, Eddard's bastard son.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Chayle is drowned in a well after the capture of Winterfell by ironborn. The sept is burned to an ashen shell during the sack of Winterfell by House Bolton.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Theon Greyjoy visits the ruins of Mikken's forge and Lady Catelyn's sept.
