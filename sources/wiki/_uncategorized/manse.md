## Summary

A manse is a large house similar to a mansion or manor. Manses are frequently owned by the wealthy, such as by merchants and lords, in cities, though they can also be found in the countryside. Manses can be fortified and found within walled estates.

History[ ]

King Aenys I Targaryen resided at a manse atop Visenya's Hill during construction of the Red Keep. Rego Draz owned a manse on the Street of Silk near the Dragonpit.

Prince Daemon Targaryen and Lady Laena Velaryon settled in a manse outside Pentos owned by a magister while waiting for Laena to give birth to Baela and Rhaena Targaryen.

Lord Bartimos Celtigar was slain in his manse amid the riot of King's Landing during the Dance of the Dragons. Prince Viserys Targaryen stayed at the manse of Bambarro Bazanne while a captive in Lys. Marilda of Hull built a palatial manse at Hull which Mushroom dubbed the Mouse House.

After the passing of Vogarro, his widow had to sell his manse inside the Black Wall of Volantis because freedmen are not allowed inside.

While Ser Jorah Mormont was fighting as a sellsword on the Rhoyne, his wife, Lynesse Hightower, moved into Tregar Ormollen's manse in Lys. 

Ghiscari inter their honored dead in crypts below their manses.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

  

Shae's manse in King's Landing, by Franz Miklis © Fantasy Flight Games

Viserys and Daenerys Targaryen are guests at the manse of Magister Illyrio Mopatis in Pentos. The siblings then reside at Drogo's manse before Daenerys's wedding.

Daenerys sees manses of woven grass as large as castles in Vaes Dothrak.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Tyrion Lannister settles his mistress, Shae, in the manse near the Iron Gate. A manse owned by a goldsmith is burned down during the riots in King's Landing.

Daenerys stays at the manse of Xaro Xhoan Daxos in Qarth, finding it vastly larger than Illyrio's manse.

A Dance with Dragon[ ]

Lord Varys has Tyrion smuggled from King's Landing to Illyrio's home in Pentos. Tyrion sees the ruins of what might have been a huge stone manse while traveling in the countryside between Pentos and Ghoyan Drohe.
