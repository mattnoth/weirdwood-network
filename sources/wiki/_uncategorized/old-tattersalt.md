Appearance and Character[ ]

The Old Tattersalt earned his nickname for the stained and faded black clothes he wears. He has a large belly and seldom talks.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Tattersalt captains the Blackbird on its voyage to Braavos.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Again captaining the Blackbird, Tattersalt is second-in-command during Cotter Pyke's expedition to Hardhome. Maester Harmune reports to Lord Commander Jon Snow from Hardhome that the Blackbird and all hands have been lost.
