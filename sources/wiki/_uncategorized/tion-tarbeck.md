Appearance and Character[ ]

According to a semi-canon source, Tion had red hair.

History[ ]

According to a semi-canon source, Ellyn Reyne named her son after the late Tion Lannister, Ellyn's first husband, as an attack against Gerold Lannister, Lord of Casterly Rock.

Tion Tarbeck died in the collapse of Tarbeck Hall, during Tywin Lannister's suppression of Houses Reyne and Tarbeck.

Family[ ]

Tion had two sisters and several half-siblings.
