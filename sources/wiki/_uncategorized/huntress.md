## Summary

Huntress is a war galley in service to House Hightower. Her figurehead is a slender maiden clad in leaves and carrying a spear. Her captain is a tall man who wears a smoke-grey cloak with a border of red satin flames.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Huntress intercepts and inspects the Cinnamon Wind as it enters Whispering Sound. This is to ensure the ironmen are not using the crew as decoys to slip into Oldtown.
