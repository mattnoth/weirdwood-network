## Full Text

Year 128 After the Conquest (128 AC) is a year in the Westerosi calendar, the 128th after Aegon's crowning in Oldtown that maesters and educated people use as a reference to date the years.

During 128, King Viserys's health deteriorated. He could no longer climb up steps, and had to be carried about the Red Keep in a chair. The governance of the realm was thus left in the hands of his Hand and the small council.
