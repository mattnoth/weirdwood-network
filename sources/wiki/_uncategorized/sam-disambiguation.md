## Full Text

Sam is a Westerosi name. It may refer to:

Sam Salt, a pretender of House Hoare

Sam Stoops, a steward of House Osgrey

Variants of the name Sam exist. They include:

Samantha Tarly, called Lady Sam, the Lady of the Hightower

Samantha Stokeworth, a companion of Princess Rhaena Targaryen

Samgood of Sour Hill, called Sour Sam, a knight of the Kingsguard

Samwell Blackwood, Lord of Raventree Hall

Samwell Dayne, King of the Torrentine

Samwell Rivers, a bastard of House Tully

Samwell Spicer, a cousin of Ser Rolph Spicer

Samwell Stokeworth, a knight of House Stokeworth

Samwell Stone, called Strong Sam Stone, the master-at-arms of Runestone

Samwell Tarly, called Sam the Slayer, a steward of the Night's Watch

Samwell Tarly, called Savage Sam, Lord of Horn Hill

Samwyle Tarly, a knight of House Tarly
