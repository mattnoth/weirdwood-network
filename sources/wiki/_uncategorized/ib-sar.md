## Summary

Ib Sar is the only Ibbenese town on Far Ib. It was originally a place of exile and punishment where the Ibbenese of old sent their most notorious criminals, often after mutilating them so they might never return to Ib itself. Even though this practice ended with the fall of the God-Kings, Ib Sar retains an unsavory reputation.
