Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Ser Garth is part of the Lannister host besieging Riverrun. He is taken captive during the battle in the Whispering Wood.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser Garth is held captive at Raventree Hall.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser Jaime Lannister goes to Raventree Hall and negotiates the surrender of Lord Tytos Blackwood. Garth is presumably freed from captivity.
