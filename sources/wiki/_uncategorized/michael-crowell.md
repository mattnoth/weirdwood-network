Ser Michael Crowell was a knight during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen.

History[ ]

Michael participated in the tourney at Ashford Meadow in 209 AC.

Behind the scenes[ ]

The character is named after Michael Crowell, the inker for the graphic novel adaptations of The Hedge Knight and The Sworn Sword.
