## Summary

King of the Dornish was one of the titles held by King Yorick V Yronwood, the last independent Yronwood king, who resisted the unification of Dorne under House Martell in Nymeria's War. The title boasts of authority over the Dornish people, likely in the face of Rhoynar invasion.
