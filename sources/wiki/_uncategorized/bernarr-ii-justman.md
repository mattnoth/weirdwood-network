## Full Text

Bernarr II Justman was the last King of the Trident from the line of House Justman.

History[ ]

Bernarr's three sons were taken as hostages after the river lords were defeated by Qhored Hoare, King of the Iron Islands. Three years later Qhored murdered Bernarr's sons, whilst they were held captive in a keep of Pyke, known afterward as the Bloody Keep, because Bernarr was slow to pay tribute. Qhored's ironborn crushed Bernarr's hopeless war for vengeance, and Bernarr was drowned as a sacrifice to the Drowned God. The fall of the Justmans led the riverlands into a century of anarchy.
