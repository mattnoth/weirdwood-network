Appearance and Character[ ]

Mollander has thick arms and broad shoulders. He has a clubfoot, which prevents him from becoming a knight, like his father.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

After Mollander heard his father was killed in the Battle of the Blackwater, he began drinking heavily. Because of his drinking, he is not a very good student.

Mollander is one of the group that discusses the discovery of dragons. He gets very drunk and Roone has to help him walk back to the Citadel.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

In an early draft of the prologue from A Feast for Crows, Mollander is named Meribald. While writing the novel, George R. R. Martin also considered making Mollander the POV character for the prologue.
