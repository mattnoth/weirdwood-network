Appearance[ ]

Germund wears a gilded breastplate that he took off a Lannister captain during Greyjoy's Rebellion.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Germund's brother Sawane, Lord of Lordsport, is drowned for defying Euron Greyjoy. Instead of Sawane's heir, Tristifer Botley, Germund is awarded half of House Botley's lands in return for swearing allegiance to Euron, with the other half granted to House Wynch. Lord Germund is listed as one of Euron's champions at the kingsmoot.
