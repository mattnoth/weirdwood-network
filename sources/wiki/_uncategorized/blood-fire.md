## Full Text

For the fictional book, see Blood and Fire.

Blood & Fire is the planned title of the sequel of Fire & Blood, written by George R.R. Martin. It will cover the history of House Targaryen from the reign of Aegon III to Robert's Rebellion.

By September 2024, Martin had written "some new pages" of Blood & Fire, working on it alongside The Winds of Winter.
