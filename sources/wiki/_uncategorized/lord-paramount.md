## Full Text

Lord Paramount is a title granted by King Aegon I Targaryen to some lords of the Seven Kingdoms during Aegon's Conquest, seemingly to those Great Houses who had not previously ruled their respective territories as kings. These are:

Lord Paramount of the Mander (also called Lord Paramount of the Reach)

Lord Paramount of the Stormlands

Lord Paramount of the Trident

Aegon also allowed the ironborn to choose their own lord paramount, rather than make them vassals to House Lannister or House Tully. They chose Vickon Greyjoy, Lord Reaper of Pyke.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord Petyr Baelish is named Lord Paramount of the Trident by King Joffrey I Baratheon, shifting the title from House Tully of Riverrun to House Baelish, the newly-minted lords of Harrenhal.
