Layout[ ]

The godsway is a broad grassy road which begins at the bronze Horse Gate and ends at the Mother of Mountains. It is lined with artifacts and monuments plundered by the Dothraki, including statues of gods, kings, heroes, maidens, and monsters.

Those displayed include:

deities with broken thunderbolts

stone kings on thrones

maidens dancing on marble plinths

maidens draped in flowers

maidens pouring from empty jars

dragons of black iron with jeweled eyes

roaring griffins

manticores ready to strike with barbed tails

a moss-covered stone monolith fifty feet tall

a woman with six breasts and the head of a ferret

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

The khalasar of Khal Drogo rides the godsway after arriving at Vaes Dothrak. Viserys Targaryen is dismissive of most of the statues. Daenerys Targaryen finds some beautiful and others ugly; Ser Jorah Mormont assumes the latter came from the Shadow Lands beyond Asshai.

After Daenerys ceremonially eats a stallion heart, a procession takes the road to the Womb of the World so that she can cleanse herself in its waters. She and Khal Drogo have sex by the lakeside, and they then ride on their horses along the godsway to Drogo's hall in Vaes Dothrak.

Daenerys takes the godsway when she visits the Western Market.
