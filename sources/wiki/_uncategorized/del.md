Appearance[ ]

Del is a horse-faced youth about Jon Snow's age.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Del is part of Jarl's group of raiders who climb the Wall to attack Castle Black from behind. After Jarl's death, Del follows Styr's commands. The youth tells Jon Snow that he intends to 'steal' a wildling girl with red hair.

Together with Errok, Del goes ahead to scout the ruins of Queenscrown. He returns almost at once to inform the raiders that there is someone in the town. He is killed by Summer, the direwolf of Bran Stark, at Queenscrown.
