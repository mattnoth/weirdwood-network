History[ ]

For siding against King Daeron II Targaryen in the First Blackfyre Rebellion, House Peake was stripped of two of their three castles, Dunstonbury and Whitegrove, leaving them only Starpike. It is unknown to which houses the Iron Throne granted the stripped castles.
