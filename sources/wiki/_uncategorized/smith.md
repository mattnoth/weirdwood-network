Description[ ]

Smiths heat and work metals in a forge or a smithy. Smiths are distinguished by the metals they mainly work blacksmith (iron and steel), coppersmith (copper), goldsmith (gold), silversmith (silver), etc. Some smiths are regarded as masters at their craft and superior to common smiths. They wear leather apron.

Culture[ ]

Smithyton is the seat of House Shermer in the Reach. House Ironsmith is a noble house from the north.

Coppersmith's Wynd is a street in King's Landing. Most smiths of King's Landing have their forges on the Street of Steel.

Lannisport is known for its goldsmiths while Bear Island have none. White Harbor is known for its silversmiths.

Goldsmiths and jewelers of Braavos shower the courtesans of the city with gifts.

The goldsmiths on the Long Bridge of Volantis have guards at their door.

History[ ]

The Three Sage Kings sought after Andal craftsmen. Blacksmiths and stonemasons in particular were handsomely rewarded.

Blacksmiths, goldsmiths and silversmiths benefited from the prosperity and the developping trade under the Old King.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Tyrion Lannister meets with the master smiths of the capital to build a monstrous chain in preparation for the Battle of the Blackwater. They considered this work beneath them at first but obliged the acting Hand of the King in the end.

The silversmiths of Qarth gift rings and chains to Daenerys Targaryen.
A wealthy goldsmmith is killed during the riots in King's Landing and his manse is burned.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Just like Queen Cersei Lannister before her, Queen Margaery Tyrell is well-known amongst the city's goldsmiths.
