Cossomo is a conjurer who performs sleight of hand tricks and calls it magic.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Cossomo teaches sleight of hand tricks to Arya Stark, who is disguised as "Cat of the Canals", pretending to swallow mice and then pulling them from her ears.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Prior to the Gate's performance of The Bloody Hand, Mercy takes a quick peek out into the house and sees Cossomo the Conjurer with Yna on his arm in the pit.
