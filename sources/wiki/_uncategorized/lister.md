A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lister's brother Jack-Be-Lucky recalls that Lister had been killed by the Lannisters, and how losing his eye is nothing compared to that. He later blames Sandor Clegane for the deaths of Lister and another one of their brothers, Lennocks.
