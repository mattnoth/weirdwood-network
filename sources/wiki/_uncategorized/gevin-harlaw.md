Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Gevin is among the ironborn who participate in the harrying of the Stony Shore. He kneels on the chest of a dead northman and saws a finger off to get a ring. 

Four Harlaws remain with Theon Greyjoy after the capture of Winterfell and are slain during the sack of Winterfell by House Bolton; it is unknown if Dykk and Gevin are among them or if they are among the ironborn who abandon Theon beforehand.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Gevin is listed among the crew of Sea Bitch.
