## Full Text

The Driftwood Throne is the ancient high seat of House Velaryon of Driftmark.

History[ ]

According to legend, the throne was given by the Merling King as part of a pact with the Velaryons, who had settled in Westeros before the Targaryens. Lord Corlys Velaryon moved the Driftwood Throne from Driftmark to High Tide prior to the Dance of the Dragons. It is unknown if the throne survived the burning of High Tide which followed the Battle in the Gullet.
