## Full Text

Torrhen is a northern given name.

It may refer to the following characters:

Torrhen Karstark, youngest son of Lord Rickard Karstark

Torrhen Manderly, Lord Regent and Hand of the King during the minority of King Aegon III Targaryen

Torrhen Stark, known as the "King Who Knelt", last King in the North

Torrhen Stark, younger son of Serena and Edric Stark, twin to Cregard Stark

Torrhen Whitehill, a character created for the video game Game of Thrones - A Telltale Games Series

Additionally, Torrhen may refer to:

Torrhen's Square, a castle in the north and the seat of House Tallhart
