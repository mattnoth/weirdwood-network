## Summary

The Dragon Twins was the nickname used by Mushroom in his Testimony to refer to princesses Baela and Rhaena Targayren, the twin daughters of Prince Daemon Targaryen and Lady Laena Velaryon.

The Dragon Twins was the nickname used by Mushroom in his Testimony to refer to princesses Baela and Rhaena Targayren, the twin daughters of Prince Daemon Targaryen and Lady Laena Velaryon.
