## Full Text

Lord of Cider Hall was the title held by the head of House Fossoway, the ruler of Cider Hall in the Reach.

Currently the Fossoways of Cider Hall are a house of landed knights.

Cider Hall

The Reach and the location of Cider Hall

History[ ]

Lord Owen Fossoway declared for the greens at the start of the Dance of the Dragons. He was one of the thirteen Caltrops, a group of nobles from the reach who conspired against the Two Betrayers. Lord Owen died in the Second Battle of Tumbleton in 130 AC.

In an unknown time between 130 AC and 209 AC, the house lost the lordship.
