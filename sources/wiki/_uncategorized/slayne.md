Slayne

The stormlands and the location of the Slayne

The Slayne is a river in Cape Wrath in the stormlands. Known for its rapids, pools, and waterfalls, the Slayne flows south from the northeastern Red Mountains to the Sea of Dorne.

History[ ]

In the Battle by the Bloody Pool, King Durran the Young is said to have dammed the Slayne with Dornish corpses. During the coming of the Andals, King Cleoden I Durrandon allied with three Dornish kings and defeated the Andal warlord Drox the Corpse-Maker on the river.
