Species and range[ ]

Parrots are known to come from the Summer Isles. Parrot Bay is south of the island of Jhala.

Known colors of parrots include red and green-and-red. Parrots can be taught to repeat words.

History[ ]

Monkeys, apes, panther cubs, and parrots from the Summer Isles were popular among the dragonlords of the Valyrian Freehold.

Roggerio Rogare filled the Mermaid, his pillow house in King's Landing, with parrots from the Summer Isles and monkeys from Sothoryos. After Roggerio was sentenced to seven lashes by Lord Torrhen Manderly, he sold everything in the Mermaid so he could purchase a cog which he named Mermaid's Daughter.

A great red parrot was part of the immense pie at the wedding at Whitewalls.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Sandor Clegane mocks Sansa Stark by comparing her to the talking birds of the Summer Isles.

In the Western Market of Vaes Dothrak, a birdseller teaches a green-and-red parrot to say the name of Daenerys Targaryen, but she refuses to buy the bird.

Quotes[ ]

Some septa trained you well. You're like one of those birds from the Summer Isles, aren't you? A pretty little talking bird, repeating all the pretty little words they taught you to recite.

—Sandor Clegane to Sansa Stark
