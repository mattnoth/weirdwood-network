Culture[ ]

Smallfolk in the Seven Kingdoms sometimes name girls after the flower.

A strong dose of tansy can cause a pregnant woman to lose her child, so it is one of the key ingredients in moon tea, also called tansy tea. 

History[ ]

Grand Maester Orwyle, disguised as "Old Wyl", mixed potions of tansy and pennyroyal for the prostitutes from Mother's, a brothel in King's Landing, to abort unwanted children.

Among the flowers picked by Chett for Bessa were tansy.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

As Lord Hoster Tully lies dying, he repeats the word "Tansy" and asks for forgiveness. Catelyn Stark initially thinks her father is referring to a woman with that name, but eventually realizes that Hoster is recalling the moon tea he forced his other daughter Lysa to drink to abort her child with Petyr Baelish.

Brown Bernarr brings bags of tansy and other herbs during the great ranging.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

Tansy is a real life natural abortifacient.
