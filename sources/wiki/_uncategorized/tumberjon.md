Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Tumberjon is part of the great ranging that leaves Castle Black to search for wildlings. He is one of the scouts commanded by Thoren Smallwood who are sent up the Frostfangs to find the wildlings. Tumberjon wants to put an arrow into Harma the Dogshead, but is ordered not to by Thoren.

Tumberjon possibly returns to Castle Black and then departs with Bowen Marsh to confront the Weeper. The brothers of the Night's Watch left to defend the Wall from the wildling assault name straw dummies after missing brothers, including Eddison Tollett, Othell Yarwyck, Watt of Long Lake, and Tumberjon.
