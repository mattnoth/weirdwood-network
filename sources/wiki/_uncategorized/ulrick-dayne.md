Ulrick Dayne was a knight from House Dayne and a Sword of the Morning at some point prior to 211 AC. His exact era is unknown, however according to a semi-canon source, he was active somewhere in the days of Eustace Osgrey.

Character[ ]

He was considered one of the greatest knights of his time, though Eustace Osgrey believed Daemon I Blackfyre was the superior warrior.
