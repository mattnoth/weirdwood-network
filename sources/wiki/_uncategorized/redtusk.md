## Full Text

Redtusk was a supporter of Daemon I Blackfyre during the First Blackfyre Rebellion.

History[ ]

At the battle of the Redgrass Field, Daemon ordered Redtusk to bring Ser Gwayne Corbray of the Kingsguard to the rear to see to his wounds after their fight. He was considered one of the finest knights of his time.

He may be a member of House Crakehall.
