Culture[ ]

The Mother of Mountains is considered a holy place for the Dothraki people. Only men can set foot on it, which some do to make sacrifices to their gods. Feuding khalasars cease their fighting when near the Mother, and merchant caravans are allowed to visit Vaes Dothrak as long as they do not profane the Mother of Mountains or the Womb of the World.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

  

The Mother of Mountains in Game of Thrones

Cohollo informs Daenerys Targaryen that her husband, Khal Drogo, will ascend the Mother of Mountains.

After a wineseller attempts to poison Daenerys, Drogo swears before the Mother of Mountains that he will invade the Seven Kingdoms.

After learning of Eroeh's death at the hands of Mago and Jhaqo, Daenerys swears by the Mother of Mountains that she will avenge the girl.

When Drogo's head is placed on a funeral pyre in the Dothraki sea, his head is oriented toward the Mother of Mountains.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Among her visions in the House of the Undying, Daenerys sees a line of naked crones creeping from a great lake beneath the Mother of Mountains and kneeling before her.
