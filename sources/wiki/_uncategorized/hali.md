Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Hali travels south of the Wall with Osha, two unnamed wildling men, and Stiv and Wallen, two deserters of the Night's Watch. When they encounter Bran Stark in the wolfswood, Hali insists Bran give them his horse. When Bran threatens them, Hali suggests killing the boy, which is protested by Osha. Hali then mocks Robb Stark when he arrives on the scene. During the fighting that ensues, Summer bites Hali's calf. She manages to free herself from the direwolf for a second, but when she stumbles and falls, Summer slams her backwards and tears at her belly, killing her.
