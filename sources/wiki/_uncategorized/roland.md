## Full Text

Roland is a Westerosi given name. It may refer to:

Roland I Arryn, the fourth King of Mountain and Vale

Roland II Arryn, King of Mountain and Vale, the great-grandson of Roland I

Roland Blackwood, a nobleman of House Blackwood

Roland Crakehall, a knight of the Kingsguard

Roland Crakehall, Lord of Crakehall during the War of the Five Kings

Roland Darry, Lord of Darry at the end of the Dance of the Dragons

Roland of the Horn, a legendary knight of the Reach

Roland Waynwood, a knight and grandson of Lady Anya Waynwood

Roland Westerling, Lord of the Crag and regent to King Aegon III Targaryen
