Appearance and Character[ ]

The girl is feebleminded. One head is no bigger than an orange and does not speak at all, the other has filed teeth and growls at anyone that comes near her cage.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Along with the rest of Yezzan's special treasures, the two-headed girl accompanies her master to the Yunkai encampment outside of Meereen. 

When Yezzan contracts the pale mare, it falls to Yezzan's special treasures to attend to him as he is dying. Tyrion Lannister and Penny escape before they can learn of the fate of Yezzan's grotesquerie. The fate of the two-headed girl is unknown.
