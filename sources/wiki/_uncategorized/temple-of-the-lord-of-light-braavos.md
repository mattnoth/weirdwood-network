## Full Text

The Temple of the Lord of Light is a great red temple in Braavos dedicated to the god R'hllor, the Lord of Light. It is located on an island near the junction of the Canal of Heroes with the Long Canal, and is connected to the rest of Braavos by a large bridge. Nearby is the Isle of the Gods.

The temple is built from red stone. On top of its great square tower is an iron brazier twenty feet across to contain a great fire. There are also smaller fires in iron braziers near its doors. It is smaller than the Temple of the Lord of Light in Volantis.
