## Summary

Meereenese is a form of bastard Valyrian spoken in the city of Meereen. Like the mongrel Ghiscari of the other Slaver Cities, the tongue of Meereen is a form of High Valyrian influenced by Old Ghiscari. Meereenes has the speaker roll zzzs.
