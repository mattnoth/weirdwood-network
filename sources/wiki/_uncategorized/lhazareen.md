About[ ]

Established in Lhazar, south-west of Vaes Dothrak, the Lhazareen's camps are frequently raided for slaves by the Dothraki, who refer to them as haesh rakhi or Lamb Men. Mirroring the importance of sheep herding in Lhazareen culture, their deity is known as the Great Shepherd.

The Lhazareen have copper skin and almond-shaped eyes, similar to the Dothraki, but they are more squat and flat-faced and have black hair cropped short. They herd sheep and farm. Their language has a sing-song quality. 
The Dothraki believe they lay with sheep.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thones[ ]

A Lhazarene town is encountered in the Dothraki sea by both Khal Ogo and Khal Drogo. Their town is destroyed by Drogo, and its people are taken as slaves.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Under Queen Daenerys Targaryen, Meereen makes an alliance with Lhazar. Fighters from Lhazar are among those who come to Meereen to battle in Daznak's Pit.

A former Lhazarene slave, the Red Lamb, goes into training in the Great Pyramid to become a knight, and is a squire to Ser Barristan Selmy. By the end of the novel he is a newly-made knight.
