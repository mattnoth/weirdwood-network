## Full Text

Robin Darklyn, known as Darkrobin, was a knight from House Darklyn during the reign of King Aegon I Targaryen.

Character[ ]

Ser Robin is remembered for being quick, strong, observant, and famed for his prowess with sword and shield. He was devoted to his king.

History[ ]

Robin was chosen by Queen Visenya Targaryen to be one of the first knights of the Kingsguard when the order was founded in 10 AC by King Aegon I Targaryen. Robin was the first of the eventual seven Darklyns who served in the Kingsguard.
