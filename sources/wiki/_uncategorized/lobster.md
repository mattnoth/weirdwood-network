Range[ ]

Lobsters are found near White Harbor and at the shores of the Iron Islands and the Shivering Sea, with the latter region having some lobsters of monstrous size.

Culture[ ]

A lobster holding a harpoon is depicted in the arms of House Magnar of Skagos.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lobster is among the seafood brought by Lord Wyman Manderly to the harvest feast at Winterfell. Bran Stark sends a huge lobster to Joseth to thank him for training Dancer and making it possible for the crippled boy to ride the horse.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Roast kid, salted cod, and lobster are served at the feast held by Victarion Greyjoy at the kingsmoot on Old Wyk.
