## Full Text

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Maester Vyman provides dreamwine and milk of the poppy to the ailing Lord Hoster Tully.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Maester Frenken gives dreamwine to Sansa Stark to help her sleep after she is beaten by the Kingsguard.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Tywin Lannister orders that King Joffrey I Baratheon be given dreamwine, and Lady Tanda Stokeworth gives her daughter Lollys dreamwine to help her sleep.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Maester Colemon mixes dreamwine and milk of the poppy for Lord Robert Arryn. Samwell Tarly is only able to afford half a flask of dreamwine for Maester Aemon when he visits the House of the Red Hands.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Dreamwine from Qarth is consumed in Meereen when Queen Daenerys Targaryen and King Hizdahr zo Loraq host the Yunkai'i.

Game of Thrones: Ascent[ ]

In Game of Thrones: Ascent, there is an item called vinsonge with the same properties. ("Vinsonge" is the French translation of "dreamwine".)
