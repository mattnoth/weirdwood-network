Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Pinchface Jon brings a dozen longships in support of Euron Greyjoy after he declares himself King of the Isles and the North. He supports Euron at the kingsmoot on Old Wyk, where he trades insults with Asha Greyjoy.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Jon feasts in the ruins of a castle near the Arbor with some of Euron's men, and he takes part in mocking Aeron Greyjoy when the latter is brought up from the hold of the Silence.
