## Full Text

Lynara Stark was a noblewoman of House Stark during the second century after Aegon's Conquest, and the third wife of Lord Cregan Stark of Winterfell.

History[ ]

Lynara was a descendant of a younger son of Lord Brandon "the Boisterous" Stark. Lynara married her distant cousin, Cregan Stark, Lord of Winterfell and Warden of the North, becoming his third wife. They had five children together; Jonnel, Edric, Lyanna, Barthogan and Brandon Stark. The main line of House Stark's lineage descends from her.
