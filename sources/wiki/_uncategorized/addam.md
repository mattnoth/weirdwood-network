## Full Text

Addam is a common given name in Westeros. Characters named Addam include:

Addam of Duskendale, a merchant who visited Bayasabhad, Samyriana, and Kayakayanaya

Addam Frey, a knight who attended the Whitewalls tourney

Addam Hightower, Lord of the Hightower during the reign of King Aegon I Targaryen

Addam Marbrand, a knight and the heir to Ashemark

Addam Osgrey, who died at the Battle of the Redgrass Field

Addam Rivers, a knight and a bastard of the Riverlands who claimed the title of river king or King of the Trident

Addam Velaryon, a legitimized bastard heir to Driftmark and a dragonseed

Addam Whitehead, son and heir to House Whitehead of Weeping Town

Addam, a character created for the TV series House of the Dragon

Addam Flowers, a character created for the video game Game of Thrones
