Vorian Vypren was a member of House Vypren and a warlord of the Andals.

History[ ]

During the Andal Invasion, he was one of the many warlords that conquered the Riverlands. At the Widow's Ford, Vypren and his knights were held back by three sons of Lord Darry for a day and a night before being victorious.
