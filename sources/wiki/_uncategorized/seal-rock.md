Geography[ ]

Grey-green in color, Seal Rock extends fifty feet above the harbor of White Harbor. The ruins of a ringfort are found atop the sea stone. Seals bask on the rocks below.

History[ ]

The First Men constructed an ancient ringfort atop Seal Rock. The ruins have been abandoned for centuries.

Whenever Cobblecat departed White Harbor, Roro Uhoris would have Davos count the seals below Seal Rock.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When Davos Seaworth returns to White Harbor as an envoy of Stannis Baratheon, he observes that the previously-abandoned Seal Rock has been occupied because of the War of the Five Kings. He sees scorpions, spitfires, and crossbowmen amidst the ringfort's ruins, and peat smoke rises from the rock.
