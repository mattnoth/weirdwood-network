Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

While waiting for Lanna at the Happy Port, Red Roggo teaches Cat of the Canals (Arya Stark) how to use a finger knife.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

While planning her assassination of an old man for the Faceless Men, Arya practices her finger knife the way she had been taught by Red Roggo. When she cuts the purse of one of the old man's clients, Arya thinks that Red Roggo would be impressed.
