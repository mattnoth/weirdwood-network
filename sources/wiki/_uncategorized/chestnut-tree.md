History[ ]

Chestnut was the name of a horse owned by first Ser Arlan of Pennytree and then by Ser Duncan the Tall. Ser Glendon Ball was sitting against a chestnut tree when he first met Duncan. Roasted chestnuts were served during the wedding tourney at Whitewalls.

Three-Finger Hobb once chopped up prunes, carrots, and chestnuts and cooked them in a hen.

Theon Greyjoy found secret hiding places among the chestnuts, elms, and soldier pines of the godswood of Winterfell.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Bronn hones the edge of his sword beneath a chestnut tree prior to the battle on the Green Fork.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

During the great ranging, Jon Snow sees a lightning-blasted chestnut tree overgrown with wild white roses in the haunted forest.

Sansa Stark finds Dontos Hollard by a chestnut tree in the godswood of the Red Keep.

A creamy chestnut soup is part of the meal enjoyed by Tyrion Lannister, acting Hand of the King, and Queen Regent Cersei Lannister at the Red Keep.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser Jaime Lannister sees stands of beech, oak, and chestnut on bluffs overlooking the Red Fork. 

While sacking a small sept, the Brave Companions hang a septon upside down from a spreading chestnut tree and use him for target practice.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Leo Tyrell has suckling pig in plum sauce, stuffed with chestnuts and white truffles, at the Checkered Hazard.

Queen Margaery Tyrell and her retinue roast chestnuts in the kingswood.

Stately chestnuts are among the trees near the crossroads inn from which the brotherhood without banners hang their victims. While Brienne Tarth is being brought by the brotherhood to meet with Lady Stoneheart, Brienne sees a horse rooting for chestnuts.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Bran Stark sees bare brown chestnut trees in the haunted forest.

Illyrio Mopatis snacks on a bag of roasted chestnuts.

Bags of chestnuts are among the stores of Castle Black. Jon sees a chestnut tree turned into a heart tree by free folk while traveling to Mole's Town.

Asha Greyjoy sees chestnut trees in the wolfswood.

Venison with roasted chestnuts is included in the feast welcoming Ser Wylis Manderly back to White Harbor.

A peddler offers hot roasted chestnuts during Cersei's walk of atonement through the streets of King's Landing.

Quotes[ ]

The chestnut was leafless and skeletal, but its bare brown limbs were not empty. On a low branch overhanging the stream a raven sat hunched, its feathers ruffled up against the cold.

—thoughts of Jon Snow
