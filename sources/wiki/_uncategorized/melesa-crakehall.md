## Summary

Melesa Crakehall is a member of House Crakehall and the wife of Ser Lyonel Frey, the second son of Ser Emmon Frey, himself the second son of Lord Walder Frey. They have no progeny. Her relation to the main Crakehalls is currently unknown.
