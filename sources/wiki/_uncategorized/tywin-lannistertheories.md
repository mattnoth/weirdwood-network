## Full Text

It is theorized Tywin Lannister is the Hand of the King "whose honor would not allow him to enter [a brothel] openly" so a tunnel was dug for him to enter Chataya brothel.

Supporting evidence[ ]

The following pieces of information can be seen as evidence:

Tywin Lannister did get involved with whores, albeit in secret, as he did with Shae.

The tunnel leads to a room that includes "a narrow window of leaded glass in a pattern of red and yellow diamonds", the colors of House Lannister.

Over the entrance of the brothel, swings a lamp described as "a globe of gilded metal and scarlet glass", also the colors of House Lannister.

One of the whores at Chataya's, Marei, is theorized to be Tywin's bastard daughter, on account of her features, demeanor and age.
