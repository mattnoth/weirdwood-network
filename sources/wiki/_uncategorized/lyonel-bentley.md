## Full Text

Lyonel Bentley was a knight and member of Rhaenyra Targaryen's Queensguard.

In the television adaptation House of the Dragon, Lyonel Bentley will be portrayed by Joe Gaminara.

Character[ ]

At the time of the Dance of the Dragons, Lyonel was a young knight.

History[ ]

Ser Lyonel Bentley was inducted into the Queensguard after Queen Rhaenyra seized King's Landing and commanded Ser Lorent Marbrand to recruit six knights to serve as members.

Lyonel accompanied Rhaenyra as her party escaped King's Landing after the storming of the Dragonpit. On the road between Stokeworth and Duskendale, they were attacked by broken men and Lyonel suffered a blow that cracked his helmet. He died raving the following day.
