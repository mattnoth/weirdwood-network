## Full Text

Mother's was a brothel on the lower end of the Street of Silk in King's Landing during the reign of King Aegon III Targaryen. The prostitutes employed there were referred to as Mother's "daughters".

History[ ]

After escaping imprisonment, Orwyle hid and worked at Mother's during the regency of Aegon III under the alias of Old Wyl. The former Grand Maester helped cleaned the brothel, inspected patrons for pox, and provided moon tea to the prostitutes. Orwyle even began teaching younger girls how to read, but his identity was revealed when one of his students revealed her literacy to a gold cloak.
