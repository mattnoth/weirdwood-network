## Full Text

Hukko is the name given by Pentoshi singers to a legendary Andal hero.

History[ ]

According to legend he slew the swan maidens, who lured travelers to their deaths in the Velvet Hills; though not for their crimes but as sacrifices to the Seven. Some maesters note "Hukko" may be a rendering of the name of Hugor, the first king of the Andals.
