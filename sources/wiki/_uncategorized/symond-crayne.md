Symond Crayne was a knight from House Crayne and a member of the Kingsguard during the reign of King Maegor I Targaryen.

History[ ]

Ser Symond served in the Kingsguard during Maegor's reign. After Maegor's mysterious death, the new young king Jaehaerys I Targaryen offered Maegor's surviving Kingsguard a choice between execution or taking the black. Symond chose the latter.
