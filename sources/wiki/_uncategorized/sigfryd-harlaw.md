Character[ ]

Sigfryd Silverhair is an old man. Asha Greyjoy believes that Sigfryd intends to live forever.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Asha Greyjoy spots the banner of Sigfryd among those that have assembled for her at the Ten Towers. When Lord Rodrik Harlaw offers her the Ten Towers, Asha believes that Sigfryd would be opposed for he intends to follow his great nephew as Lord of the Ten Towers.

Family[ ]

A grandson of Lord Theomore Harlaw, Sigfryd is the great uncle of Rodrik Harlaw, Lord of the Ten Towers. He holds lands from Harlaw Hall as a bannerman to Lord Rodrik. He has several grandsons to follow him.
