## Full Text

Year 10 After the Conquest (10 AC) is a year in the Westerosi calendar, the 10th after Aegon's crowning in Oldtown that maesters and educated people use as a reference to date the years.

In 10 AC the Dornish War took a darker turn when Rhaenys Targaryen and her dragon Meraxes were shot down by a scorpion at Hellholt. This would begin a period of indiscriminate revenges, and the war escalated to a series of castle burnings and targeted murders of nobles known as The Dragon's Wroth. After a failed attempt on the life of King Aegon I, Visenya convinced him to establish a body of seven sworn bodyguards that would be named the Kingsguard.

Events[ ]

King's Landing has become a true city.

King Aegon I and Queen Visenya Targaryen are attacked in the streets of King’s Landing, leading to Aegon ordering Visenya to choose the members of the first Seven Sworn Brothers of the Kingsguard.

The Kingsguard is formed.

A Dornish army under Lord Fowler crosses the Prince's Pass, burns a dozen villages and captures Nightsong. Lord Caron, his wife and his children are made captives.

A Dornish army under Ser Joffrey Dayne burns fields, farms and villages for twenty leagues around Oldtown.

Aegon attacks Skyreach with Balerion, and Visenya attacks Starfall with Vhagar.

Deaths[ ]

Queen Rhaenys Targaryen supposedly dies in Dorne.

Meraxes, Rhaenys's dragon.

Garmon Hightower, dies in Oldtown defending the city against the forces of Ser Joffrey Dayne.

Titles and Positions[ ]

The Kingsguard is created and its first seven knights named:

Ser Corlys Velaryon is named to the Kingsguard, becoming the first Lord Commander of the Kingsguard.

Ser Robin Darklyn, known as "Darkrobin", is named to the Kingsguard.

Ser Richard Roote is named to the Kingsguard.

Ser Gregor Goode, brother to Griffith, is named to the Kingsguard.

Ser Griffith Goode, brother to Gregor, is named to the Kingsguard.

Ser Humfrey, "the Mummer", a hedge knight, is named to the Kingsguard.

Ser Addison Hill, the "Bastard of Cornfield", is named to the Kingsguard.

Ser Addam Hightower becomes the new Lord of Oldtown after his father's death.
