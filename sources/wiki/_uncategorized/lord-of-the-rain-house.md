History[ ]

Lord Morgan Wylde gelded a stableboy who had deflowered his daughter, Coryanne Wylde.

As master of laws, Lord Jasper Wylde was part of the green council in support of King Aegon II Targaryen during the Dance of the Dragons. Jasper was executed as a traitor by Rhaenyra Targaryen after the fall of King's Landing.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

According to a semi-canon source, Lord Casper Wylde supports first Renly Baratheon and then Stannis Baratheon during the War of the Five Kings.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser Laswell Peake leads the taking of Rain House by the Golden Company.
