Appearance[ ]

Catelyn Stark considers Walda to be one of the more nubile Frey maidens.

History[ ]

It is common knowledge around the Twins that Walda has occasionally slept with her first cousin, Black Walder.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

When Lord Roose Bolton has to choose a bride from the maidens of House Frey, everyone is convinced he will choose Fair Walda. When Lord Walder Frey offers Roose his bride's weight in silver as a dowry, Roose chooses Walda's cousin, Fat Walda, instead.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

At the wedding feast of Lord Edmure Tully's and Lady Roslin Frey's wedding, Walda is seated between Robb Stark and Smalljon Umber.
