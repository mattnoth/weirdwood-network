Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

A wineseller at the Western Market in Vaes Dothrak has firewine among his offerings.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Strong Belwas reluctantly has his wounded chest bound with strips of linen soaked in fire wine after his duel with Oznak zo Pahl.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Arya Stark receives thimble cups of fire wine as Titan's Daughter sails to Braavos. Arya learns the Braavosi words for fire wine during the voyage.

Old Tattersalt opens a cask of firewine for the crew of Blackbird as it sails to Braavos.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Jars of Myrish fire wine are served at Illyrio's manse in Pentos.

Quotes[ ]

The captain broached a cask of firewine to fortify the oarsmen. Sam tried a cup and sighed as hot snakes wriggled down his throat and through his chest. Dareon took a liking to the drink as well, and was seldom sober thereafter.

If I drink enough fire wine, he told himself, perhaps I'll dream of dragons.
