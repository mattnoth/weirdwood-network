## Full Text

North Grove is a place beyond the Wall, and is known only to House Forrester south of the Wall. It has only appeared in Game of Thrones - A Telltale Games Series.

History[ ]

Lord Gregor Forrester sent away his bastard children, Josera and Elsera Snow, and they came to the North Grove to cultivate their gifts as a skinchanger and a bloodmage, respectively. Josera thought that their father sent them away out of shame, but Elsera believed that they had been sent away for their own protection.

When Lord Gregor was mortally wounded and dying, he asked Gared Tuttle to see that the North Grove would not be lost. Gared's uncle Duncan had heard of the place, but few others had. Eventually, Gared, Cotter, and Sylvi reached North Grove after venturing north of the Wall, before wights attacked. Based on player choice, Gared would either choose to stay and protect the North Grove or return to the Seven Kingdoms with Josera and Elsera.
