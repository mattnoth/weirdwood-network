## Full Text

Runcel Hightower was a nobleman of House Hightower and a former Lord Commander of the Night's Watch.

Character[ ]

Benjen Stark called Runcel Hightower ambitious and proud, rather than wise.

History[ ]

Some five hundred years ago, Lord Commander Runcel forgot his vows and attempted to bequeath Castle Black and the Night's Watch to his bastard son. Runcel's ambitions almost destroyed the Night's Watch, but his efforts to subvert the traditional electoral system of the Night's Watch ultimately failed.
