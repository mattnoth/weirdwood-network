Appearance and Character[ ]

The always calm Minisa had soft hands and a warm smile. Brynden Tully states that his niece Catelyn, believed by many to be pretty, resembles Minisa in her cheekbones and jaw. Catelyn's elder daughter, Sansa, has high cheekbones as well.

History[ ]

Minisa was born into House Whent, the Lords of Harrenhal, and she wed their liege, Hoster Tully, the Lord of Riverrun and Lord Paramount of the Trident. Minisa's first two children were sons who died in infancy, but her later children Catelyn, Lysa, and Edmure grew to adulthood. Minisa died in childbirth with her fourth son, who also died shortly after.

The sept of Riverrun is located in Minisa's gardens.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord Hoster Tully still misses his late wife. Ailing and feverish, Hoster mistakenly calls his daughter Catelyn by Minisa's name.
