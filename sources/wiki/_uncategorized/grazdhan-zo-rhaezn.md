A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Grazdhan zo Rhaezn, along with his brothers Chezdhar zo Rhaezn and Maezon zo Rhaezn, are mocked by the sellsword companies as the Clanker Lords, since they came up with the idea of chaining all the slave soldiers together so they cannot flee when facing the Unsullied of Daenerys Targaryen (as their previous soldiers did). The soldiers march at a snail's pace due to the weight of the chains.

The Tattered Prince points out that these slave soldiers, due to all the chains attached to them, will be slowed and not be able to fight effectively.
