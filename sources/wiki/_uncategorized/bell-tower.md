Layout[ ]

A covered bridge connects the fourth floor of the bell tower with the second floor of the rookery.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Bran Stark sees the red comet in the sky above the Bell Tower and the Guards Hall.

After his capture of Winterfell, Theon Greyjoy sees a half-moon above the Bell Tower.

The bridge between the Bell Tower and the rookery collapses into the yard as a result of the sack of Winterfell by House Bolton.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Sansa Stark includes the bell tower and its covered bridge while making a snow castle of Winterfell at the Eyrie.
