History[ ]

  

Brandon Stark, by Mike Hallstein ©

The strong-willed Lyanna Stark had a touch of wildness and enjoyed horse riding and tilting at rings. The girl defended Howland Reed at the tourney of Harrenhal, protecting the small crannogman from three squires.

Lyanna's eldest brother, Brandon Stark, had wild rages. After hearing that Lyanna had been abducted by Prince Rhaegar Targaryen, Brandon rode to the Red Keep and demanded that the prince come out and die.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lord Eddard Stark tells his tomboy daughter Arya about his more impetuous siblings, Brandon and Lyanna.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Old Nan tells Bran Stark that House Stark has wolf blood, warning it is stronger in some than others.

Arya recalls her late father's words when preparing to escape from Harrenhal.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

At the House of Black and White in Braavos, plague face disbelieves Arya's denial that she kills for her own pleasure.
