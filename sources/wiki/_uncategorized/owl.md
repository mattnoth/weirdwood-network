Biology[ ]

Owls are known for their eyesight and can attack ravens. Species of owls include snow owl and horned owl.

In culture[ ]

  

Owls in the arms of House Garner

The arms of House Mertyns depict a white great horned owl on grey, and Ser Michael Mertyns was known as the White Owl. Three grey owls are included in the arms of House Garner.

One of the named hours is called the hour of the owl.

Skinchangers who have entered the minds of owls can lose their connection to the earth and instead only want to fly.

The sharp-eyed Mya Stone likes to say her mother was an owl.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Will hears the hoot of a snow owl before Others attack Ser Waymar Royce in the haunted forest.

Bran Stark is familiar with an ancient owl which sleeps in the dusty loft above the old armory of Winterfell.

While on the kingsroad south of Mole's Town, Jon Snow notices an owl taking flight.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

As they travel through the northern mountains, Bran and his companions hear owls hoot and observe them fly silently through pine trees.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Masks worn by shavepates in Meereen depict owls, jackals, and other beasts.

Quotes[ ]

On a clear night like this, the moon and the stars are enough. Mychel says I have the eyes of the owl.

—Mya Stone to Catelyn Stark
