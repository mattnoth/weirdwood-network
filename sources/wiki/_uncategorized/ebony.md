Material[ ]

The house of Tobho Mott in King's Landing has double doors decorated with a hunting scene in ebony and weirwood.

The doors of the House of Black and White in Braavos are made of ebony and weirwood. A statue of the Lion of Night is located within the temple on an ebony throne. Faceless Men gather beneath the temple in a chamber housing tall chairs of ebony and weirwood.

Some inhabitants of Asshai ride in palanquins of ebony and iron.

History[ ]

Ebony from the Summer Isles was popular in the Valyrian Freehold.

According to Mushroom, Sandoq the Shadow played a stringed instrument of ebony and goldenheart for Larra Rogare.

Maester Caleotte designed a rolling chair with wheels of ebony and iron for Doran Martell, Prince of Dorne.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Xaro Xhoan Daxos gives Daenerys Targaryen a palanquin of ebony and gold. Within the House of the Undying, Daenerys sees doors of ebony and weirwood. Drogon flies to the top of a door and bites at the carved wood.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Following the siege of Meereen, Daenerys replaces the harpy throne in the Great Pyramid's audience chamber with a simple bench of polished ebony.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser Balon Swann delivers the Mountain's skull to Sunspear in a box of carved ebony.
