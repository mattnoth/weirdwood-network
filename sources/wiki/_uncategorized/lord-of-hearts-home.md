## Full Text

Lord of Heart's Home is a title held by the head of House Corbray and the ruler of Heart's Home, in the Vale of Arryn.

History[ ]

In 131 AC, during the final months of the Dance of the Dragons, Lord Leowyn Corbray led the ten thousand men of Lady Jeyne Arryn from the Vale to King's Landing, together with his brother, Ser Corwyn Corbray. At the start of the regency of Aegon III, Leowyn was named Protector of the Realm, a position he held until his death in 133 AC, when he succumbed to the Winter Fever.

Lords of Heart's Home[ ]

The known Lords of Heart's Home are:

Lord Qarl Corbray, Lord of Heart's Home during the reigns of the kings Maegor I Targaryen and Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

Lord Leowyn Corbray, Lord of Heart's Home during the Dance of the Dragons and Protector of the Realm during the regency of King Aegon III Targaryen.

Lord Quenton Corbray, Leowyn's son, Lord of Heart's Home during the reign of King Aegon III.

Lord Corbray, who hired Petyr Baelish's great-grandfather.

Lord Corbray, Lord of Heart's Home during Robert's Rebellion.

Lord Lyonel Corbray, his son and the current Lord of Heart's Home.
