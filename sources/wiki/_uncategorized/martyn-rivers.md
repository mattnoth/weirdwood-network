Appearance[ ]

The baseborn Martyn is ruddy, unlike his relatives from House Frey.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Martyn attends the Hand's tourney in King's Landing with six of his kin—Ser Jared, Ser Hosteen, Ser Danwell, Ser Emmon, Ser Theo, and Ser Perwyn Frey.

Martyn is at the Twins when Robb Stark's host arrives at the seat of the Freys.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

While Ser Brynden Tully is raiding in the westerlands with Robb, the new King in the North, Martyn commands the outriders in the riverlands. Martyn escorts Catelyn Stark when her party nears Riverrun, telling her of Robb's victory in the Battle of Oxcross.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Catelyn spots Martyn among the Freys who angrily leave Riverrun after news reaches the castle of Robb's marriage with Jeyne Westerling instead of a Frey.
