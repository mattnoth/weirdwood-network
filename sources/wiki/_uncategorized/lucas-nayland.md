## Full Text

Lucas Nayland was a knight from House Nayland during the reign of King Aerys I Targaryen.

Character[ ]

Lucas was a good jouster.

History[ ]

In 212 AC, Lucas came from Hag's Mire to Whitewalls to attend the wedding of Lord Ambrose Butterwell and the daughter of Lord Frey. He participated in the wedding tourney, and was considered one of the better jousters. During the event, he also played a drinking game with Ser Kirby Pimm.
