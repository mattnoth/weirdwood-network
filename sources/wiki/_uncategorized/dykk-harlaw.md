Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Dykk is among the ironborn who participate in the capture of Winterfell by Theon Greyjoy. Ser Rodrik Cassel leads a northern host to retake the castle. When Theon asks the assembled ironmen to remain and fight alongside him, Dykk spits at the ground. Four Harlaws remain with Theon and are slain during the sack of Winterfell by House Bolton; it is unknown if Gevin and Dykk are among them or if they are among the ironborn who abandon Theon beforehand.
