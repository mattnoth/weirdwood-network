Appearance[ ]

Symond has cold blue eyes, a large beak nose, and a black and pointed beard.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser Symond joins with Lord Yohn Royce in becoming one of the Lords Declarant. He attends the parley between the Lords Declarant and Lord Petyr Baelish, arriving with Bronze Yohn. Petyr intends to befriend Symond after the meeting.

To everyone's surprise, Symond attends the wedding of Lord Lyonel Corbray in Gulltown, an event masterminded by Petyr.
