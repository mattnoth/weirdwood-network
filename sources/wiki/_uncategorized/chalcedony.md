Chalcedony is a translucent quartz mineral and moganite used for ornamentation. It can appear in various colors. Bloodstone can appear as chalcedony or jasper.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

While at the port of Qarth, Daenerys Targaryen is given a wooden box with a mother-of-pearl lid inlaid with jasper and chalcedony by a Sorrowful Man. A manticore attacks the girl when she opens the box.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

When the Brave Companions attack a small sept in the riverlands, Zollo pries out the chalcedony eyes from a statue of the Mother.
