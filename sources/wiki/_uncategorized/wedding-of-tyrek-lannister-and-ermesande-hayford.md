Prelude[ ]

The thirteen-year-old Tyrek Lannister, once a squire of King Robert I Baratheon, is betrothed to Ermesande Hayford, the infant Lady of Hayford, so that House Lannister can claim her lands.

When hungry smallfolk hear about preparations for the wedding feast, they gather at the gates of the Red Keep to demand food. King Joffrey I Baratheon leads a sortie of Kingsguard and red cloaks which disperses the crowd.

Wedding[ ]

Tyrek wears a bridegroom's mantle of miniver and velvet for the wedding.

Aftermath[ ]

Tyrek is mocked as "Wet Nurse" by other squires for having married Ermesande. Tyrek goes missing during the riots in King's Landing.
