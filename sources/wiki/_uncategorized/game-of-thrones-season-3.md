## Full Text

Season 3 of Game of Thrones is the third season of the series. Filming began on Summer 2012. The series consists of ten episodes. 

Season 3 is closely based on the first half or thereabouts of A Storm of Swords, the third  novel in the A Song of Ice and Fire novel series by George R. R. Martin. 

George R. R. Martin wrote the seventh episode, formerly tentatively titled "Autumn Storms", now "The Bear and the Maiden Fair".
