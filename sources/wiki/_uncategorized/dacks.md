Dacks was the squire of a Redfort knight, possibly Lord Horton Redfort, during the time when Robert Baratheon and Eddard Stark were fostering at the Eyrie with Lord Jon Arryn. Robert once threw a rotten orange at Dacks, beginning a food fight in the castle's High Hall.
