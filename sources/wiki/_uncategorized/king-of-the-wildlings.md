Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

In the aftermath of the battle beneath the Wall, King Stannis Baratheon and later his wife, Selyse Florent, attempt to integrate defeated free folk, also called wildings, into the Seven Kingdoms. Since Gerrick Kingsblood claims descent from Raymun Redbeard, a historical King-Beyond-the-Wall, Ser Axell Florent introduces Gerrick as the King of the Wildlings and Selyse agrees to have Gerrick's daughters marry her queen's men. Jon Snow, Lord Commander of the Night's Watch, thinks the title is meaningless to the free folk, however, as Gerrick actually descends from Raymun's younger brother.
