## Summary

Arya Stark, tasked by the Faceless Men with assassinating an old man, is disguised with the face of an ugly little girl. After the man is killed at the Purple Harbor, the city watch of Braavos hunt for the ugly girl. Arya, however, returns to the House of Black and White and has her original face restored.
