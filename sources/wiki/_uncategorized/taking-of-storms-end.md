Prelude[ ]

During the War of the Five Kings, King Stannis Baratheon takes Storm's End after the death of its castellan, Ser Cortnay Penrose. He appoints Ser Gilbert Farring as the new castellan, with Lord Elwood Meadows as Gilbert's second. After his defeat in the Battle of the Blackwater, Stannis sails for the Wall to aid the Night's Watch against Mance Rayder, leaving Storm's End and Dragonstone lightly held in the south.

  

Storm's End by Marc Simonetti ©

After the wedding of King Tommen I Baratheon and Margaery Tyrell in King's Landing, the bride's father, Lord Mace Tyrell, travels south with half of House Tyrell's strength to besiege Storm's End. After learning of Margaery's imprisonment by the Faith, however, Mace abandons his siege and returns to the capital. Mace leaves a token force under the leadership of Lord Mathis Rowan to maintain their siege of Storm's End.

The Golden Company sails on Volantene ships from Essos to Westeros as part of their plan to crown Prince Aegon Targaryen, widely thought to have been killed during Robert's Rebellion, in opposition to Tommen. At Griffin's Roost, Haldon Halfmaester informs Lord Jon Connington that Lord Tyrell has returned to King's Landing. Jon tells the captains of the company that capturing Storm's End would give them a secure base and boost Aegon's prestige. When Jon informs Aegon four days later, the prince agrees with the plan but surprises Jon by stating he wants to lead the attack himself.

The small council of King Tommen I Baratheon hears reports of the Golden Company marching on the castle with Jon and the pretender Aegon.

Battle[ ]

While Princess Arianne Martell travels from Mistwood to Griffin's Roost, she overhears Chain talking about Storm's End. Once at Griffin's Roost, she is told by Haldon that Storm's End has fallen and that Lord Connington awaits her there.'

Aftermath[ ]

An army from King's Landing, assumed by Ser Daemon Sand to be led by Lord Tyrell, is descending on Storm's End. Haldon claims that Aegon means to meet this army in open battle. Daemon privately advises Arianne against traveling to Storm's End to meet with Jon, but she insists upon going.
