Wedding of Jaehaerys and Alysanne by Thomas Denmark for The Rise of the Dragon

The wedding of Jaehaerys I Targaryen and Alysanne Targaryen occurred in 49 AC at Fair Isle. It was the last of three royal weddings that earned the year the name the Year of the Three Brides.

Wedding[ ]

King Jaehaerys I Targaryen and his younger sister Alysanne Targaryen wed in secret on Dragonstone in late 49 AC. The wedding was officiating by Dragonstone's Septon Oswyck.
