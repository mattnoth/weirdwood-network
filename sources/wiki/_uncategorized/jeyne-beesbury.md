Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Jeyne dies of a wasting illness.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Jeyne's widower, Rhaegar Frey, is betrothed to Wynafryd Manderly, granddaughter of Wyman Manderly, the Lord of White Harbor. Rhaegar mysteriously disappears on his way to Barrowton and is presumed dead, however. Thus making Jeyne's three children orphans.

Family[ ]

Jeyne's relation to Lord Warryn Beesbury, the head of the House, is unknown.
