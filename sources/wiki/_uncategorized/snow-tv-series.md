Conception and development[ ]

A sequel series to Game of Thrones, with Kit Harington returning as Jon Snow, was reported by James Hibberd via The Hollywood Reporter on June 16, 2022. In a BBC interview with Emilia Clarke (Daenerys Targaryen in Game of Thrones) less than a week later, Clarke confirmed that she had spoken to Harington about the project, and that it was essentially his creation. The following day, George R. R. Martin confirmed that the series was in development, and had been considered around the same time as A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms, The Sea Snake, and 10,000 Ships, but that it had not yet been greenlit. 

In an interview with Screen Rant in April 2024, Kit Harington confirmed that the Jon Snow spinoff series is no longer in development.
