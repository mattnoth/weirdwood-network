## Summary

The night lands, or nightlands, are the afterlife of the Dothraki religion. Tradition dictates that when a khal dies, his bloodriders must die with him so they can ride at his side in the night lands. The Dothraki believe that the night lands are visible in the night sky, and the stars are the honored dead riding fiery steeds; the more fiercely a man burned in life, the brighter his star will shine in the darkness.
