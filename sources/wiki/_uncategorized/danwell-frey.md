History[ ]

Ser Danwell rode in the tourney on Prince Joffrey's name day held in King's Landing in 298 AC. He was unhorsed by a hedge knight, which embarrassed his father, Lord Walder Frey.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Ser Danwell attends the Hand's tourney in King's Landing with six of his kin—Ser Hosteen, Ser Jared, Ser Emmon, Ser Theo, Ser Perwyn, and Martyn Rivers.

During the parley with Lady Catelyn Stark at the Twins, Danwell and Ser Whalen Frey help their father, Lord Walder Frey, to his chair.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Danwell is a captive of Lord Tywin Lannister, together with his brothers Ser Jared and Ser Hosteen and his half-brother Ronel Rivers. Tywin holds captives from the battle on the Green Fork at the Tower of Dread in Harrenhal. Arya Stark observes the four Freys exercising in the Flowstone Yard of Harrenhal and then being ransomed by their kin.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser Danwell is among the Freys who meet Ser Jaime Lannister when the maimed Kingslayer arrives at Harrenhal with the Brave Companions. He urges Lord Roose Bolton to ransom Jaime back to Riverrun. 

Danwell attends the Red Wedding at the Twins. He plays a drinking game with Marq Piper, who is later held captive by the Freys.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Danwell travels to Darry, where his niece Amerei lives with her new husband, Lord Lancel Lannister. Danwell informs Ser Jaime Lannister that packs of wolves attacked his baggage train as they traveled south from the Twins. Danwell thinks it possible that the Reeds gave shelter to Lord Beric Dondarrion and his outlaws.
