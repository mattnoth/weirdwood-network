Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Merrit o' Moontown takes part in the battle at the burning septry against the Brave Companions. Afterward, Merrit carries a weakened brown brother from the building.

After Merrit's horse throws a shoe, Gendry offers to replace it. Merrit catches a cold from heavy rainfall.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

After Beric's final death, a part of Beric's band, including Merrit, apparently go their separate way from those who chose to follow the resurrected, vengeful Lady Stoneheart.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

Merrit o' Moontown may be a reference to A. Merrit's novel The Moon Pool.
