## Full Text

Lo Han was a God-Emperor of the Golden Empire of Yi Ti and the forty second ruler of his dynasty.

History[ ]

Three times he led armies into the Plains of the Jogos Nhai with the goal of bringing the Jogos Nhai to heel, even compeling handfuls of jhats to sweat fealty to him and forswear raiding forever. Yet by the time of his death the Jogos Nhai had become bolder and more rapacious than when he had first donned the imperial regalia. He was succeeded by his son Lo Bu.
