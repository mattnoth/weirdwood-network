History[ ]

Walys was born a bastard son of a Hightower girl and an archmaester of the Citadel. After forging his chain, he served at Winterfell when Lord Rickard Stark was head of House Stark.

Recent History[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lady Barbrey Dustin believes that Walys instigated Lord Rickard Stark's "southron ambitions", including the marriage of his son Brandon to Catelyn Tully, the daughter of the Lord Paramount of the Trident.

Theories[ ]

Because of the personal items in Archmaester Walgrave's strongbox, and the similarity of Walgrave's and Walys's names, some suspect he was the archmaester who fathered Walys.
