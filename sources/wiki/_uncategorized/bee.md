Culture[ ]

Three beehives are included in the arms of House Beesbury of Honeyholt.

History[ ]

Ellyn Ever Sweet, the legendary founder of House Beesbury, is said to have loved honey so much that she sought out the King of the Bees in his vast mountain hive, and made a pact to care for his children and his children's children for all time, becoming the first beekeeper.

Princess Daella Targaryen loved flowers, but was afraid of gardens after a bee almost stung her once. Daella's sister, Princess Saera, once played a prank on her by filling her chamberpot with bees.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

A septry in the riverlands has a hundred beehives, but their honey is taken by Lannisters during the War of the Five Kings.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

While staying at the Quiet Isle, Brienne of Tarth and her companions are served honey from the septry's hives.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Jonos Bracken asks for Honeytree and its hives as part of negotiations during the siege of Raventree.
