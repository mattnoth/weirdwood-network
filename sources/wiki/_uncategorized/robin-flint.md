Character[ ]

Robin is of mature age.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Robin joins the host of Robb Stark after he calls the northern banners to rescue his father, Lord Eddard Stark, and Robin remains with Robb after the split of the northern host at the Twins. Robin is named to Robb's personal guard before the battle in the Whispering Wood. He is one of the older members of the group, along with Ser Wendel Manderly.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Robin is one of the lordlings who go south with Lady Catelyn Stark to meet with Renly Baratheon. When a half-day's ride from Renly's camp near Bitterbridge, Robin ranges ahead to scout and spots a lookout on a distant windmill. By the time, Catelyn's party arrives at the mill, the scout is gone and Renly's outriders escort them to his camp. After Renly's sudden death near Storm's End, Robin and the rest of Catelyn's party return to Riverrun.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Robin, one of Robb's sworn swords, commands the rearguard for the King in the North as his column marches to the Twins for Lord Edmure Tully's wedding, and he attends Robb's meeting with Lord Roose Bolton.

Robin remains sober during the wedding feast as he is serving as one of Robb's guards, and he dances with Alyx Frey. During the Red Wedding, however, Robin is stabbed to death by a group of Freys.
