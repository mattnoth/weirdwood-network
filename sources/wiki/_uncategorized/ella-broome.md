Character[ ]

Ella was a modest maid.

History[ ]

Ella was briefly offered as a match for Jaehaerys I Targaryen. Later on, she became a lady-in-waiting to his wife, Queen Alysanne Targaryen.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

It is possible she is a reference to Cinderella, due to the name, "Ella," coming from House Broome (Cinderella was made to sweep for her sisters), being a modest sister, and her proposed marriage to King Jaehaerys mirroring Prince Charming.
