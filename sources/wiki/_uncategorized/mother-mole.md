Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After the defeat of Mance Rayder at the battle beneath the Wall, Mother Mole claims to have had a vision of a fleet of ships coming to carry the wildlings away to safety across the narrow sea. Thousands of those who fled the battle are desperate enough to believe her.  She and her followers go east. Mother Mole is heard to preach that the free folk will find salvation where once they found damnation. 

Mother Mole leads thousands of wildlings to Hardhome, to pray and await salvation from across the sea.
