Character[ ]

Medger is soft-spoken.

History[ ]

Medger and his son Cley have often visited Winterfell.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lord Cerwyn comes to Winterfell when Robb Stark calls his banners. He is accompanied by his daughter, Jonelle. One of Lord Roose Bolton's men knifes one of Medger's at the Smoking Log. Lord Jon Umber objects to being placed behind the Cerwyns or the Hornwoods in the order of march.

Medger is placed under the command of Lord Bolton when the northern army splits at the Twins. He is captured at the battle on the Green Fork.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Tyrion Lannister, acting Hand of the King, unsuccessfully offers to trade Lord Medger and Ser Donnel Locke for Tion Frey in an exchange of prisoners.

Lord Tywin Lannister holds captives from the Green Fork at the Tower of Dread in Harrenhal. Arya Stark, disguised as a servant in the castle, hopes to sneak past the guards and ask Lord Cerwyn for assistance. Medger suffers from a serious wound taken at the battle, however, and never leaves his tower cell. Medger eventually dies of his wounds. Arya sees silent sisters take his remains from Harrenhal, with his body in a cloak of silk decorated with the battle-axe of House Cerwyn.
