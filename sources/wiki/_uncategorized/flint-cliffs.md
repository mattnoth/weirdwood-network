## Full Text

Flint Cliffs

The north and the location of the Flint Cliffs

The Flint Cliffs are a series of cliffs in the north along the Sunset Sea, east of Cape Kraken, west of the Neck, and north of the Iron Islands. The cliffs are named after House Flint of Flint's Finger.

History[ ]

At the age of ten, Balon Greyjoy climbed the Flint Cliffs to the Blind Lord's haunted tower.
