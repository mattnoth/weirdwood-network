## Full Text

Warden of the Sands is a defunct warden title devised by Aegon I Targaryen during the brief occupation of Dorne by the Iron Throne in the First Dornish War.

History[ ]

When King Aegon I Targaryen left Dorne to return to King's Landing, he named Lord Jon Rosby the Castellan of Sunspear and the Warden of the Sands. The Dornishmen soon rebelled, however, and Rosby was killed in the Defenestration of Sunspear.
