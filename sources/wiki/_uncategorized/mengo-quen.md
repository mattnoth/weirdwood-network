## Full Text

Mengo Quen, known as the Glittering God, was a God-Emperor of the Golden Empire of Yi Ti and the third member of his dynasty.

History[ ]

Quen earned his moniker because he ruled from a palace where the floors, walls, and columns were covered in gold leaf, and all furnishings were made of gold, even the chamber pots.
