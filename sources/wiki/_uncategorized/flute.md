Recent events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

The flute is played, mixed with pipes and a harp, at the wedding ceremony of Tyrion Lannister and Sansa Stark.

Flutes are played, along with pipes, fiddles, horns, skins, and drums, at the Red Wedding.

During breakfast in the Queen's Ballroom, musicians stroll among the tables, piping and fluting and fiddling.

A flute accompanies Galyeon of Cuy's performance of his song about the Battle of the Blackwater.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

An ensemble of pipes, fiddles, a flute, and a high harp perform during the wedding feast of Tommen Baratheon and Margaery Tyrell.

Robert Arryn is soothed by the high harp, but frightened by flutes and fiddles.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Flutes and drums are played whilst Xaro Xhoan Daxos's naked slaves dance for Daenerys Targaryen.

At the port of Selhorys, Tyrion Lannister hears queer music close by, a thin high fluting accompanied by drums.

At the Yunkish camp near Meereen, one of Yurkhaz zo Yunzak's slaves plays a bone flute whilst his grotesquerie performs the evening's frolics.
