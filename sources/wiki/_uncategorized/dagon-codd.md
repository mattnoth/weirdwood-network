Appearance[ ]

Dagon is a big man, wide mouthed and pop eyed with pale skin.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

According to a guard at the siege of Moat Cailin, Dagon found only two ironborn left alive in the Children's Tower. Because they were eating corpses, he killed them both.

When Ramsay Bolton sends Theon Greyjoy to give peace terms to the ironborn, a drunken Dagon is the only one of the garrison who does not wish to surrender. Because of Dagon's resistance, Adrack Humble throws an axe into Dagon's forehead. Despite Ramsay's promise of safe passage, however, the remaining ironborn who surrender, including Adrack, are flayed after they yield Moat Cailin.
