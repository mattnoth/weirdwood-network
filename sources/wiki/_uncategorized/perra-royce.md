## Full Text

Perra Royce was a noblewoman from House Royce. She married Lord Walder Frey of the Twins, and was the first of his eight wives.

Family[ ]

Perra's relationship to the rest of House Royce is unknown. Perra and Walder had four children together – Ser Stevron, Ser Emmon, Ser Aenys, and Perriane Frey – who all had descendants. Perra has fourteen grandchildren, fifteen great-grandchildren, and two great-great-grandchildren, one of which is named Perra, probably in her honor.
