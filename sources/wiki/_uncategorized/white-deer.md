Culture[ ]

White harts are said to be rare and magical.

White Hart is a galley in the royal fleet.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

After a white hart is sighted in the kingswood, a royal hunt is organized, with King Robert I Baratheon being accompanied by Lord Renly Baratheon, Ser Barristan Selmy, Prince Joffrey Baratheon, Sandor Clegane, Ser Balon Swann, and half the court. Wolves get to the white hart first, however, which angers Robert. The king then ventures deeper into the kingswood after hearing word of a monstrous boar.
