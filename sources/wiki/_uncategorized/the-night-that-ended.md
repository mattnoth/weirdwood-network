Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

The song is sung during the harvest feast at Winterfell. When the singer begins the part where the Night's Watch rides to meet the Others in the Battle for the Dawn, Hother Umber blows his horn and scares all the dogs.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After the marriage of Ramsay Bolton and "Arya Stark" (actually Jeyne Poole), a banquet is held in the Great Hall of Winterfell. Lord Wyman Manderly is in a jolly mood and calls out to the musicians for various tunes, one of which is "The Night That Ended". Wyman remarks that the bride will like that song.
