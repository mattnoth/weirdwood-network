The Street of Steel begins at the southwest corner of Fishmonger's Square and curves up the western slope of Visenya's Hill to the Great Sept of Baelor. Most smiths of the capital have their forges there. The higher up one goes, the more expensive the shops. At its top is the shop of Tobho Mott.

Geography[ ]

The Street of Steel begins at the southwest corner of Fishmonger's Square and curves up the western slope of Visenya's Hill to the Great Sept of Baelor. Most smiths of the capital have their forges there. The higher up one goes, the more expensive the shops. At its top is the shop of Tobho Mott.

History[ ]

The young Duncan the Tall once saw Lord Brynden Rivers and his Raven's Teeth up the Street of Steel.

Lord Jon Arryn, the Hand of the King, and Lord Stannis Baratheon visited the shop of Tobho Mott, where they met Gendry, one of King Robert I Baratheon's bastards.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Investigating Lord Jon Arryn's death, Lord Eddard Stark visits Tobho's shop like his predecessor.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Tyrion Lannister commands the blacksmiths of the Street of Steel to build him a great chain he will later use in the Battle of the Blackwater.
