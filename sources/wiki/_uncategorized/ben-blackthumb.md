Appearance[ ]

Ben is old and hunched.

History[ ]

Ben worked for Lady Shella Whent's father and grandfather, as well as for the last lord of House Lothston.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

After Harrenhal is taken by Lord Tywin Lannister, Ben Blackthumb enters the service of House Lannister. He keeps the swords of the armory locked up.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ben, Pia, and a cook are the only servants from the time of Lady Shella Whent who remain at Harrenhal when Ser Jaime Lannister returns on his way to lift the siege of Riverrun.

Quotes[ ]

You know old Ben Blackthumb? He came here as a boy. Smithed for Lady Whent and her father before her and his father before him, and even for Lord Lothston who held Harrenhal before the Whents. Now he smiths for Lord Tywin, and you know what he says? A sword's a sword, a helm's a helm, and if you reach in the fire you get burned, no matter who you're serving.

—Gendry to Arya Stark
