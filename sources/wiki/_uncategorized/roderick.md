Roderick is a Westerosi given name. It may refer to:

Roderick Blackwood, Lord of Raventree Hall during the reign of King Arlan III Durrandon

Roderick Dustin, known as "Roddy the Ruin", Lord of Barrowton and head of House Dustin during the Dance of the Dragons

Roderick Stark, a Lord of Winterfell and head of House Stark
