Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser Philip Foote slays Bryce Caron, Lord of Nightsong, during the Battle of the Blackwater. The Iron Throne rewards him with Bryce's lands and titles, and Philip founds House Foote of Nightsong.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Bryce's bastard half-brother, Ser Rolland Storm, is a pretender to Nightsong, laying claim under Stannis Baratheon's banner.
