## Full Text

Alys is a Westerosi given name. It may refer to:

Alys Arryn, wife of Prince Rhaegel Targaryen

Alys Arryn, sister to Lord Jon Arryn

Alys Beesbury, wife to Ser Leo Tyrell

Alys Frey, wife to her cousin Ser Jared Frey, the fourth son of Lord Walder Frey

Alys Harroway, wife to Maegor I Targaryen

Alys Karstark, wife of Lord Brandon Stark

Alys Karstark, the only daughter of Lord Rickard Karstark

Alys Oakheart, wife to Ser Jon Cafferen

Alys Rivers, bedmate to Prince Aemond Targaryen

Alys Stackspear, the first wife of Ser Jason Lannister

Alys Stark, the second daughter of Lord Cregan Stark

Alys Stone, the eldest bastard daughter of Ser Harrold Hardyng

Alys Turnberry, a lady companion to Princess Saera Targaryen
