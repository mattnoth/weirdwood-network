History[ ]

The Band of Nine were a group of merchants, sellswords and pirates who joined together to carve kingdoms out for themselves. After conquering the Disputed Lands, they took over Tyrosh. Next, they seized the Stepstones, from where they stood poised to attack Westeros. The Band of Nine was defeated during the War of the Ninepenny Kings.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Jaime Lannister recalls visiting Riverrun as a boy, ignoring Lysa Tully while begging her uncle Brynden for tales of Maelys the Monstrous and the Ebon Prince.
