## Summary

In Astapor, when Daenerys Targaryen is inspecting the Unsullied soldiers that she is considering purchasing, Kraznys mo Nakloz asks her if she wishes to view their fighting pits. Kraznys tells her that Douquor's Pit has a "fine folly" scheduled for the evening: a bear and three small boys - one boy is to be rolled in honey, one in blood and one in rotting fish, with the audience wagering on which boy the bear will eat first. Dany declines the invitation.
