## Full Text

The Bawdy Badger was an inn within the town of Tumbleton during the Dance of the Dragons. It is unknown if the inn survived until the reign of King Robert I Baratheon.

History[ ]

During the Dance of the Dragons, Ser Ulf White took the inn as his own after the Treasons of Tumbleton. During the Second Battle of Tumbleton, Ulf the Sot remained at the inn, sleeping under a table in a drunken slumber and snoring through the entire battle.
