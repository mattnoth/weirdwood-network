Character[ ]

A younger man, Harlan and his brothers are quarrelsome, each more grasping than the last.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

After Lord Eon Hunter dies suddenly, Harlan and his brother Ser Eustace accuse their eldest brother, Gilwood, of murder.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

According to Petyr Baelish, Harlan was responsible for the death of his father and believes Lord Gilwood will soon be murdered by his brothers, most likely Ser Harlan.
