Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

As one of the queen's sworn swords, Ser Narbert travels with Queen Selyse Florent from Eastwatch-by-the-Sea to Castle Black.

Narbert yawns during the wedding of Sigorn and Alys Karstark, and he dances with each of Selyse's lady companions at the feast afterwards.

The knight is present when Selyse refuses Jon Snow, Lord Commander of the Night's Watch, aid in rescuing the wildlings stranded at Hardhome. When Jon informs the Night's Watch and the free folk about his plans for Hardhome and the letter he has received from Ramsay Bolton, Narbet and Ser Benethon Scales stand near the hall's door.
