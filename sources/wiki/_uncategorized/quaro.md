Appearance and Character[ ]

Quaro wears bells in his hair. He has dark, almond-shaped eyes.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Quaro is one of four young warriors assigned to guard Daenerys Targaryen. When Viserys Targaryen attacks his sister in the Dothraki sea, Quaro advises Daenerys to take an ear from Viserys to teach him respect. He later accompanies Daenerys during her visit to the Western Market in Vaes Dothrak, challenging Rakharo to a friendly eating contest. Quaro helps stop an assassination attempt on Daenerys's life by a wineseller.

During the raid on the Lhazareen settlement, Quaro is commanded by Daenerys to stop the Dothraki riders raping the captured Eroeh. Though he initially protests against stopping it, he complies with her command.

When Daenerys intends to have Khal Drogo healed by Mirri Maz Duur using blood magic, Drogo's bloodriders try to stop it from happening. In the ensuing fight, Quaro is killed by Qotho.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Quaro is remembered by Daenerys when she thinks back to the followers who have died under her service.
