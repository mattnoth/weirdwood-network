Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Men-at-arms in the host of Lord Tywin Lannister take their meals in the Barracks Hall while staying at Harrenhal. Weese has the disguised Arya Stark sometimes serve at the hall's tables. While Jaqen H'ghar takes his meal one night, Arya whispers Chiswyck's name into his ear as a target for the assassin.

On the night of the fall of Harrenhal, Arya hears the Brave Companions and the men of Ser Amory Lorch's garrison feasting in the Barracks Hall. When fighting breaks out, Arya sees a wounded man stagger out of the hall and then be cut down by three others.
