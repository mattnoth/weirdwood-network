Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Morrec accompanies Tyrion Lannister north to Winterfell and then to the Wall, and he prepares a stew during the journey. At Castle Black he prepares the fire in Tyrion's chamber. On the return journey south, Morrec and Jyck are taken captive with Tyrion at the crossroads inn and forced to ride into the Mountains of the Moon, with Mohor watching over them. After a second attack by the Vale mountain clans, Morrec dies from a fever caused by a festering wound.
