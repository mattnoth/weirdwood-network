Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Torrhen accompanies his father and his two brothers Harrion and Eddard to Winterfell when Robb Stark calls his banners. He is a member of Robb's personal guard during the battle in the Whispering Wood. Torrhen and Eddard are both slain by Ser Jaime Lannister during the battle.
