## Summary

Port Yhos is a town in Essos located southeast of Slaver's Bay on the shores of the Summer Sea. The westernmost settlement controlled by Qarth, it is south of Vaes Orvik and southwest of the red waste. Qarkash is to the east of Port Yhos.
