History[ ]

When King Maegor I Targaryen and his army approached Oldtown during the uprising of the Faith Militant, Ser Morgan's elder brother, Lord Martyn Hightower, called the banners. Morgan led the Warrior's Sons of Oldtown in surrounding the Starry Sept to protect the High Septon. By morning, however, the High Septon was dead.

Morgan and his sworn brothers were arrested under orders of Lord Martyn and put on trial by Maegor the Cruel. Morgan was the only one pardoned, while the rest were given the choice between death or taking the black. Morgan's pardon led to the suspicion that he had murdered the High Septon at the behest of his lordly brother, as the knight was seen entering and leaving the privy chambers of His High Holiness that night. Patrice Hightower, Visenya Targaryen, and the archmaesters of the Citadel have also been suspected of killing the High Septon.

Morgan was later cut down and butchered on the road to Honeyholt by the Warrior's Sons who remained loyal to Ser Joffrey Doggett.
