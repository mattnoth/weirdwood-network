Appearance[ ]

See also: Images of Harma

Squat and round, Harma Dogshead is a huge woman with white cheeks.

Harma hates dogs, and she kills one every fortnight to create a new totem for her banner. She owns pigs.

History[ ]

  

Harma and Rattleshirt, by Jon Neimeister © Fantasy Flight Games

Before Mance Rayder made peace between them, Harma once warred with Rattleshirt.

Harma has become a well known raider among the brothers of the Night's Watch.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Harma the Dogshead is among the leaders of Mance Rayder's army that the Night's Watch are wary of.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Harma leads the van of the free folk army that marches south down the Milkwater. Goady creeps up on her camp and spots her with five hundred horsemen. Thoren Smallwood convinces Tumberjon not to shoot Harma with an arrow.

Harma is present when the free folk discover the remains of the camp of the Night's Watch following the fight at the Fist. She is skeptical when Jon Snow reveals that the great ranging is led by Jeor Mormont, Lord Commander of the Night's Watch, and she smirks when Ygritte says she has been sleeping with Jon.

During the wildling assault on the Wall, Harma is spotted near Woodswatch-by-the-Pool.

Harma returns from her raids and feints to the main encampment of Mance's army by Castle Black. When Jon arrives at Mance's tent, she urges Mance to kill him. When rangers from Eastwatch-by-the-Sea appear, Mance has Harma ride against them with thirty raiders. The rangers are followed by the southron host of Stannis Baratheon, however, and one column of armored knights smashes Harma. Later in the battle beneath the Wall, Jon sees someone waving Harma's head on a pole.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Harma's brother Halleck kneels before Stannis and yields her pigs.
