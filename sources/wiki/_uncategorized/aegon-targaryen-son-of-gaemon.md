Aegon Targaryen was a member of House Targaryen during the Century of Blood, the son of Lord Gaemon and Lady Daenys Targaryen of Dragonstone.

History[ ]

Aegon married his sister, Elaena, and together they had two sons: Maegon and Aerys Targaryen. Aegon and Elaena also had a younger sister who married a petty lord.

Aegon and Elaena inherited the lordship of Dragonstone from their father and mother, they jointly ruled Dragonstone and were succeeded by Maegon.
