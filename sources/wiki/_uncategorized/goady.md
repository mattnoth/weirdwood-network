Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

During the great ranging beyond the Wall by the Night's Watch, Goady is among the scouts under the command of Jarman Buckwell. He is then a member of Thoren Smallwood's scouting party that finds the wildling masses along the Milkwater. He creeps up to the fire to see who leads the van and spots Harma Dogshead.

Goady stays loyal to the Watch during the mutiny at Craster's Keep and is one of the few survivors of the ranging to make it all the way back to Castle Black.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Goady is stationed at Castle Black.
