Rolland is a Westerosi given name. It may refer to:

Rolland Darklyn, a historical knight of the King's Guard

Rolland Longthorpe, the Lord of Longsister and head of House Longthorpe

Rolland Storm, known as the "Bastard of Nightsong", a knight in service to Stannis Baratheon

Rolland Uffering, a knight during the reign of Daeron II
