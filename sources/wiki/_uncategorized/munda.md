Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Jon Snow becomes attached to the war band of Munda's father, Tormund. She sings along during the song "The Last of the Giants".

Munda fights back when Longspear Ryk comes to "steal" her.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Tormund mentions to Jon that Munda and Ryk are now married, and she is well treated.
