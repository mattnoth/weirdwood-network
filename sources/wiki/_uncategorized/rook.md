Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Rook sails to Deepwood Motte with Asha Greyjoy as she contemplates her next move. He is captured along with Asha, Tristifer Botley, and several other ironborn by Stannis Baratheon after the fight by Deepwood Motte. 

Rook is later freed and ransomed along with the other ironborn by Tycho Nestoris, who needs an escort to get him to Stannis during his march on Winterfell.
