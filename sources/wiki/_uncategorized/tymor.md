Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Tymor and his brothers participate in the harrying of the Stony Shore, gathering all the horses that survive Theon Greyjoy's battle with the Wild Hares. 

Tymor and his brothers take part in Theon's capture of Winterfell. They remain with Theon after northmen come to retake the castle, and they are presumably killed when the Bolton men sack the castle.
