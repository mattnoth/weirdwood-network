## Summary

Black Pearl of Braavos is a traditional title or name borne by a descendant of Bellegere Otherys, the first Black Pearl, a pirate queen who became a mistress of Aegon IV Targaryen. The eldest bastard daughter of King Aegon IV and Bellegere, Bellenora Otherys, became a courtesan under the same name. Bellenora's descendants became courtesans as well, each eventually bearing the name "Black Pearl".

Known Black Pearls (chronological order)[ ]

Bellegere Otherys, the first Black Pearl, pirate, smuggler, and mistress of Aegon the Unworthy.

Bellenora Otherys, the second Black Pearl, Bellegere's daughter, a courtesan.

Bellonara Otherys, the previous Black Pearl, also a courtesan.

Bellegere Otherys, daughter of Bellonara, the current Black Pearl, also a courtesan.
