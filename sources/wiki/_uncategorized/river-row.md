Layout[ ]

The River Row runs from the King's Gate past the River Gate toward Aegon's High Hill and Eel Alley, with the Blackwater Rush nearby.

Inns can be found on the River Row, and sea captains, fishmongers, and others with interest in the harbor live along the street. 

History[ ]

Princess Viserra Targaryen visited the inns of Eel Alley and River Row with her companions for "one last night of laughter" before being sent to White Harbor to marry old Lord Theomore Manderly. She died later that night at the foot of Aegon's High Hill in a drunken horse race.

During the Dance of the Dragons, violent riots occurred in the capital. Ser Torrhen Manderly led a sortie from the Red Keep down the Hook with his northmen to try to stop the rioters. When they arrived at Fishmonger's Square and the River Row, the place was swarming with Ser Perkin the Flea's gutter knights. Torrhen lost a quarter of his men fighting his way back to the Red Keep.
