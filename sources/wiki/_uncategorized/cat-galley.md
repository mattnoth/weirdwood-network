Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Cat is part of Stannis Baratheon's fleet commanded by Ser Imry Florent during the Battle of the Blackwater. While the Courageous is sinking, Cat takes on her survivors. The Cat is engulfed by wildfire after the explosion of the Swordfish and destroyed.
