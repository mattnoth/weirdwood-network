Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

When Ser Jaime Lannister, Lord Commander of the Kingsguard, returns to King's Landing after months of captivity, he interrogates the new members of the Kingsguard regarding their experience at arms. Ser Osmund Kettleblack tells Jaime he was knighted by Ser Robert on the battlefield, and that he is now dead. Jaime does not recognize this knight, and is dubious about Ser Robert's actual existence.
