## Summary

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Aeron Greyjoy calls for a kingsmoot after the death of his eldest brother, Balon Greyjoy, King of the Isles and the North.

Lord Captain Victarion Greyjoy leads the Iron Fleet into Nagga's Cradle and sets anchor in the bay. Feasting tents are raised between the tideline and Nagga's Hill in preparation of the kingsmoot on Old Wyk. Euron Greyjoy is chosen by the assembled ironborn as Balon's successor.

King Euron has the Iron Fleet close the bay after being crowned. When Lord Baelor Blacktyde tries to sail back to Blacktyde, his Nightflyer is seized and Euron has him executed.
