Range[ ]

Trout has been found in the Trident, including the Red Fork and the Green Fork, in the Blackwater Rush, in the wolfswood, and in the Vale of Arryn.

Culture[ ]

  

Stoney Sept by Marc Scheff © Fantasy Flight Games

A leaping silver trout is included in the arms of House Tully of Riverrun, the Lords Paramount of the Trident. The pillars of Lord Hoster Tully's bed are carved in the shapes of leaping trout. A trout scaled in silver and bronze crowns the crest of Hoster's greathelm, and a silver trout ornaments the crest of the greathelm of his son, Ser Edmure Tully. Ser Brynden Tully, known as the Blackfish, has a black trout on his own helm. Ser Robin Ryger, the captain of Riverrun's guards, clasps his cloak with a silver trout.

A statue of a leaping trout spouts water into a shallow pool in the market square of Stoney Sept.

History[ ]

Lord Medgar Tully clasped his striped blue-and-red cloak with a silver trout at each shoulder.

Jory Cassel once brought Robb Stark, Bran Stark, and Jon Snow to the wolfswood to fish for trout.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Prince Joffrey Baratheon and Sansa Stark dine on fresh trout from the Trident while visiting a holdfast near the river.

Trout fresh from the Blackwater Rush is baked in clay and then served during the Hand's tourney.

Bronn catches trout while Lord Tywin Lannister's host marches north along the Green Fork.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Catelyn Stark eats trout wrapped in bacon at Riverrun.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser Jaime Lannister sees trout in the Red Fork.

Meera Reed catches two small silvery trout and six fat green frogs in the wolfswood.

Trout cooked in a crust of crushed almonds is served during the wedding feast of King Joffrey I Baratheon and Queen Margaery Tyrell.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser Creighton Longbough and Ser Illifer the Penniless offer grilled trout to Brienne of Tarth.

Jaime is served trout while staying at Hayford.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Pike, salmon, and trout are served at the Gates of the Moon.
