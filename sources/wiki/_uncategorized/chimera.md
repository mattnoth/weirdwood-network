## Full Text

The chimera is a mythological creature, believed to be composed of different parts from other animals.

According to Septon Barth, the sorcerers of Valyria practiced dark blood magic, allegedly using it to twist and fashion the flesh of beasts and men to create monstrous and unnatural chimeras.
