Language[ ]

Old Ghiscari is a guttural-sounding language, described by Tyrion Lannister as harsh and ugly and by Victarion Greyjoy as full of growls and hisses. Mhysa is a word meaning "mother" in the old pure tongue.

Modern Ghiscari writing uses glyphs; it is unknown if glyphs were used in Old Ghiscari as well. 

History[ ]

Old Ghiscari developed from the ancient city of Ghis in Ghiscar. The region was conquered by the Valyrian Freehold in five Ghiscari wars, however, and the Ghiscari people became slaves of Valyria. The Slaver Cities instead came to speak bastard Valyrian, High Valyrian corrupted and flavored with Ghiscari to form a mongrel tongue.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Mhysa, an Old Ghiscari word meaning "mother", is used by many of the freed people of Yunkai to refer to Daenerys Targaryen when she enters the city after the battle near Yunkai.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When Hizdahr zo Loraq holds a feast in the Great Pyramid of Meereen, singers from Yunkai sing love songs in Old Ghiscari.

Skahaz mo Kandaq, Hizdahr zo Loraq, and Yurkhaz zo Yunzak speak "Ghiscari" at times, though it is unclear if this refers to Old Ghiscari or mongrel Ghiscari.
