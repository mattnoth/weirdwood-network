Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

King Stannis Baratheon tells Davos Seaworth that Ser Gilbert is one of the few men who remained loyal to him after his loss at the Blackwater. Gilbert still holds Storm's End for Stannis, commanding a garrison of two hundred men.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Gilbert continues to hold Storm's End  for Stannis as his castellan, with Lord Elwood Meadows as his second in command, and Maester Jurne as his counselor and healer.

While Stannis is in the north, an army from the Reach under Lord Mace Tyrell and Lord Mathis Rowan besiege Storm's End. However, when Mace receives news that his daughter Margaery has been arrested by the Faith of the Seven, he leaves the siege and marches his army back to King's Landing, leaving Lord Rowan to continue the siege with fewer men. 

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After Jon Connington and Aegon Targaryen take Griffin's Roost with the Golden Company, they plan to attack Storm's End next.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Haldon Halfmaester informs Arianne Martell that the Golden Company has taken Storm's End, and that Lord Mace Tyrell's army is descending on the castle from King's Landing.
