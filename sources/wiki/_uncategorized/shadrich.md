Appearance and Character[ ]

See also: Images of Shadrich

Ser Shadrich is a wiry, fox-faced man, with a sharp nose and bristly orange hair. Though he is not more than five foot two, short enough to be mistaken for a boy, he has the face of an older man, with wrinkles by his mouth, and hard eyes. He also has a scar beneath his ear.

Shadrich is not a tourney knight, instead saving his valor for the battlefield. He has a cocksure manner, and is sharp-witted.

Shadrich's shield depicts his sigil, a large white mouse with red eyes on bendy brown and blue. The hedge knight rides a rangy chestnut courser.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

  

Shadrich by TheMico ©

Ser Shadrich fought for Stannis Baratheon in the Battle of the Blackwater. After Stannis's defeat, Shadrich was forced to pay a ransom he could not afford, meaning that he was ruined financially. Shadrich becomes a hired knight for the merchant Hibald and his serving men.

While Shadrich is escorting Hibald's party to Duskendale, they are joined by Brienne of Tarth, Ser Creighton Longbough, and Ser Illifer the Penniless. When Brienne says she is searching for her lost sister, Shadrich realizes that it is Sansa Stark whom Brienne seeks. Shadrich explains Varys is offering a plump bag of gold for Sansa, which would return him to the status he enjoyed before the Blackwater. Shadrich offers to split the reward, but Brienne carries on pretending she is seeking her sister. Shadrich becomes sarcastic, as he knows she is lying.

After Brienne abandons her traveling companions at the Old Stone Bridge, she worries that Shadrich might be following her. It is actually Podrick Payne who follows Brienne after Duskendale, however.

Ser Shadrich eventually turns up at the Gates of the Moon, as a newly-hired hedge knight in service to Lord Petyr Baelish. Along with Ser Byron the Beautiful and Ser Morgarth the Merry, Shadrich is introduced to Alayne Stone (a disguised Sansa Stark) in the west tower of the Gates.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

When Alayne is greeting guests the day before the tourney for the Brotherhood of Winged Knights is to be held at the Gates of the Moon, she bumps into Ser Shadrich, but he prevents her from falling. He tells her and Myranda Royce that he does not intend to participate in the joust. When Alayne asks if he might try the melee instead, he replies that hedge knights hope for melees, if they can't find a bag of dragons. Later, at the feast, Alayne dances with Shadrich, among others.
