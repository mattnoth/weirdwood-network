Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser Harras Harlaw is one of Asha Greyjoy's champions at the kingsmoot on Old Wyk.

During the taking of the Shields, Harras plants his standard beneath Grimston, the castle of House Grimm on Greyshield, and defeats seven men, slaying five in the process. The septon of the castle believes the gods have spoken and surrenders Grimston to the knight.

Harras is named the new Lord of Greyshield by Euron Greyjoy, King of the Isles and the North.
