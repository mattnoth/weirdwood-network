Dancy has a pug nose and freckles. She has thick red hair, long enough to reach past her waist. Tyrion Lannister believes she would be a lively handful. 

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Tyrion Lannister visits Chataya's brothel to cover his secret visits to his mistress, Shae, going upstairs with Alayaya to a room with a secret passage. Dancy wagers her black pearls to Marei, another whore at the brothel, that she can bed Tyrion within a fortnight. Dancy attempts to convince Tyrion to take her upstairs, but he is faithful to Shae and refuses her advances.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Dancy changes the color of her hair to honey blond.

When Prince Oberyn Martell arrives at King's Landing, and suggests that he is looking for a blonde woman to share with his paramour Ellaria Sand, Tyrion recommends Dancy to him.

Anguy reveals he spent the small fortune he had won at the Hand's tourney on girls from Chataya's brothel—Jayde, Alayaya, and Dancy—roast swan, and Arbor wine.
