Appearance and Character[ ]

Layna is a fat young girl. She has a brother.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Chiswyck tells a story at Harrenhal about how Layna was gang raped by Ser Gregor Clegane and his men after the Hand's tourney. Her brother tried to stop the assault but he was stabbed in the belly by Raff the Sweetling. Chiswyck estimated her to have been 18-years-old at the time of the event. Raff corrected him, stating that she was 13-years-old.
