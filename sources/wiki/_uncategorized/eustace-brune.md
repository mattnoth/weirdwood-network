Character[ ]

Eustace is an old man with sons and grandsons. He is the cousin of Ser Bennard Brune, the Knight of Brownhollow.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Dick Crabb claims to have some knowledge of Lord Eustace as he guides Brienne of Tarth and Podrick Payne past the Dyre Den. Dick claims Lord Eustace's sons and grandsons had gone off to war.
