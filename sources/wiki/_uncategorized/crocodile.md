Geography[ ]

Crocodiles dwell in the green waters of the Zamoyos, lurking beneath the surface of the river and are known to overturn boats, swimming up from below to devour the navigators.

The reptiles are also found on the rivers of Jhala in the Summer Isles.

In culture[ ]

Crocodile meat is consumed by the Ghiscari of Slaver's Bay.

Recent events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Crocodile meat is served in the feast celebrating the wedding between Daenerys Targaryen and Hizdahr zo Loraq.

Crocodiles are one of the animals depicted in the masks of the Brazen Beasts.
