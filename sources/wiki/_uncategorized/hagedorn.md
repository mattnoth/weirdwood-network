## Summary

Archmaester Hagedorn is an Archmaester of the Citadel. He has put forth the theory that the centaurs, who in ancient tales lived in the eastern grasslands of Central Essos, were no more than mounted warriors, as perceived by tribes who had not yet learned to tame and ride horses. His views are widely accepted at the Citadel, despite purported "centaur skeletons" that occasionally appear in grotesqueries.
