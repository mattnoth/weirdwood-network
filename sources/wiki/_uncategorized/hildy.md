Appearance and Character[ ]

Hildy has a pug nose and a shaggy mane of hair, and Jaime Lannister thinks her nipples are darker and thrice the size of his sister Cersei Lannister's. She has a playful personality.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Jonos Bracken of Stone Hedge claims Hildy for himself after killing her lover during the siege of Raventree. Hildy and Jonos are caught abed by Ser Jaime Lannister when he strolls unannounced into Bracken's tent outside Raventree Hall. She flirts with and teases Jaime before Jonos yells at her to leave, which she does gladly.
