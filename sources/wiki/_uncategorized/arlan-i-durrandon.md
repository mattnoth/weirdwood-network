## Full Text

Arlan I Durrandon, also known as Arlan the Avenger, was a Storm King of House Durrandon who ruled during the Age of the Hundred Kingdoms.

History[ ]

Arlan extended the borders of the Kingdom of the Storm as far as the Blackwater Rush and the headwaters of the Mander. His great-grandson, Arlan III Durrandon, annexed the riverlands to the kingdom.
