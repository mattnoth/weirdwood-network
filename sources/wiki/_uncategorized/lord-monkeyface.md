Appearance[ ]

Lord Monkeyface was hirsute and a hunchback.

History[ ]

Archmaester Gyldayn records that Lord Monkeyface's antics greatly amused Queen Visenya. After he choked to death on a peach pit, Visenya replaced him with an ape, which she had dressed in the late Lord Monkeyface's clothing.

Quotes[ ]

The new one is cleverer.

—Visenya Targaryen, about the ape that replaced Lord Monkeyface

Notes[ ]

↑ Since the Goodwife is known to have been the first fool at the royal court in the Red Keep in the beginning of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen's reign (Fire & Blood, A Time of Testing - The Realm Remade) and Queen Visenya Targaryen is known to have resided most of her life at Dragonstone (Fire & Blood, The Sons of the Dragon), it is likely Lord Monkeyface was a fool at Dragonstone.
