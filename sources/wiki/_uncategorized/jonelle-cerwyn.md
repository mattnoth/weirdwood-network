Appearance[ ]

Jonelle is plump and homely.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Jonelle accompanies her father to Winterfell when Robb Stark calls his banners. Robb tells his brother Bran that Lord Medger plans to take her south with Robb's host. Although Medger claims this is so she can cook for him, Theon Greyjoy believes that Robb will one night find her in his bed.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

After the deaths of her father, Medger, and her brother, Cley, Jonelle has become the Lady of Cerwyn.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Jonelle swears fealty to the Iron Throne. She is at Barrowton after Moat Cailin is recovered from the ironborn, and puts her signature on a letter from Ramsay Bolton to Asha Greyjoy at Deepwood Motte and a letter from Ramsay to Jon Snow at Castle Black. Although Cerwyn men later are at Winterfell for Ramsay Bolton's wedding to "Arya Stark", it is unknown if Jonelle accompanied them. Maester Rhodry of Castle Cerwyn is at Winterfell, however.
