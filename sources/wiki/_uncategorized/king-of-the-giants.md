History[ ]

Maester Yandel writes that the giants of the Dawn Age had no kings or lords.

Tales of the north claim  the Great Barrow at Barrowton contains the grave of the First King of the First Men or, because of the hill's size, the grave of a King of the Giants.

Songs claim that Durran the Dour slew Lun the Last, King of the Giants, at the Battle of Crookwater in the stormlands.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

When Jon Snow asks if Mag Mar Tun Doh Weg is a king, Tormund explains that giants have no kings. After Mag the Mighty and Donal Noye slay each other during the battle beneath the Wall, Jon continues to think of Mag as the king of the giants.

Quotes[ ]

Giants have no kings, no more'n mammoths do, nor snow bears, nor the great whales o' the grey sea.

—Tormund to Jon Snow
