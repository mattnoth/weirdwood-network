## Full Text

Lord Swyft was the Lord of Cornfield and head of House Swyft during the Dance of the Dragons.

History[ ]

Lord Swyft declared for the greens during the Dance of the Dragons. He was part of the westermen host led by Lord Jason Lannister then by Ser Adrian Tarbeck and Lord Humfrey Lefford. He was one of the two thousand men who died in the battle by the Lakeshore.

Notes[ ]

↑ At the start of the series, House Swyft is a knightly house holding the title of "Knight of Cornfield". As this character is described as a "Lord", it is possible the Swyfts were once a lordly house who held the title of "Lord of Cornfield".
