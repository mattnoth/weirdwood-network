## Summary

The Feast of Skane is said to have occurred in ancient days when a Skagosi war fleet descended upon the smaller nearby isle of Skane. While the women of Skane were raped and carried off by the Skagosi, the Skanish men were consumed by the cannibalistic Skagosi in a feast which lasted a fortnight on a pebbled beach. The Feast of Skane is described in Maester Balder's The Edge of the World. Skane is said to be uninhabited to this day.
