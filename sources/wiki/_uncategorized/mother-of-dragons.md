Recent events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Following the hatching of her dragons—Drogon, Rhaegal, and Viserion—Daenerys Targaryen is known as Mother of Dragons by her khalasar and the people of Qarth. Quhuru Mo refers to her as Dragonmother.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Daenerys uses the title when she meets with Kraznys mo Nakloz in Astapor.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Daenerys uses Mother of Dragons as part of her style as Queen of Meereen.
