## Summary

Arwyn Frey is the sixth daughter of Lord Walder Frey. She is the first born and eldest daughter of Lord Walder and his seventh spouse, Lady Annara Farring. However according to Merrett Frey, Black Walder Frey was rumored to be sleeping with Lady Annara casting doubts on the parentage of her children.
