## Full Text

Torreo Haen was a Lysene magister who briefly became First Magister of Lys.

History[ ]

In 133 AC, Torreo sent his daughter Myrmadora to King's Landing to be presented to King Aegon III Targaryen at the Maiden's Day Ball.

After the fall of the Rogare family in 135 AC, a bloody struggle ensued in the resulting power vacuum. Torreo was elevated to first magister, but was poisoned with his wife, mistress, daughters (including Myrmadora), siblings, and supporters at the feast he held to celebrate his new position.
