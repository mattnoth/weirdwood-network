History[ ]

Mantarys was founded by the Valyrian Freehold. Although rich and glorious, it did not have self-rule and instead men and women sent from Valyria ruled it in the name of the Freehold - thus it is not counted among the Free Cities. Mantarys remained inhabited after the Doom of Valyria, but its people are said to be twisted and monstrous.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Daenerys Targaryen, Queen of Meereen, sends a mission to Mantarys hoping to find new friends to the west to balance the enmity of Yunkai to the south. Mantarys's answer comes by way of caravan in a cedar chest - inside she finds the pickled heads of three of her envoys. Instead, the city agrees to an alliance with Yunkai.

Tyrion Lannister sees a two-headed slave girl of Mantarys in Yezzan zo Qaggaz's grotesquerie.
