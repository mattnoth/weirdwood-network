## Full Text

The Evenstar, or the Evenstar of Tarth, is a title used by Lords of House Tarth of Tarth.

History[ ]

The Lords of Tarth claim that the title goes back until the dawn of days. A previous Evenstar was Edwyn Tarth, last of the Kings of Tarth, whose daughter married Durran the Fair, a Storm King of House Durrandon, joining Tarth to his kingdom.

The current Evenstar is Lord Selwyn Tarth.
