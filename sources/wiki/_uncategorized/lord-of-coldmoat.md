## Summary

Lord of Coldmoat was a title held by the ruler of the castle Coldmoat and its adjacent lands. The title was held by House Osgrey, which raised the castle, until it was stripped as punishment for Lord Ormond Osgrey opposing King Maegor's laws. At some point House Webber became Lords of Coldmoat after the marriage of Lady Rohanne Webber to Ser Eustace Osgrey, Ser Eustace is called Lord of Coldmoat by Maester Cerrick but whether he is co-ruler or merely Lord-Consort is unknown.
