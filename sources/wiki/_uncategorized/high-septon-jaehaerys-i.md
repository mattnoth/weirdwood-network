## Full Text

The High Septon was High Septon of the Faith of the Seven during the late reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

In the television adaptation House of the Dragon, he is portrayed by Gary Raymond.

History[ ]

The High Septon travelled to Harrenhal in 101 AC to bless the lords assembled in a Great Council to choose a new heir for King Jaehaerys I.
