## Full Text

Lord Vaith was the Lord of the Red Dunes and the head of House Vaith during the Conquest of Dorne.

History[ ]

Lord Vaith was among the forty powerful Dornish lords who bent their knees with their liege lord, the Prince of Dorne, to King Daeron I Targaryen in the Submission of Sunspear.

In order to insure their loyalties, Daeron took fourteen highborn as hostages, including Lord Vaith's daughter, Cassella.
