## Summary

Brienne of Tarth recalls that Ser Will was a part of King Renly Baratheon's host encamped beneath the walls of Highgarden. He was one of the knights that participated in the wager on who would claim Brienne's maidenhead. In his efforts to win the bet, Will offered to rub her shoulders to get the knots out. Lord Randyll Tarly found out about the bet and put an end to it. After the bet was discovered and ended, Brienne sought out and defeated Will during the melee at Bitterbridge. Will was killed at the Battle of the Blackwater.
