## Summary

King of the High Tower is an ancient title held by the head of House Hightower dating back to the time they ruled their own independent kingdom. The realm ruled from Oldtown was bounded by the headwaters of the Honeywine in the north and the Red Mountains in the east. The title was abandoned when House Hightower was brought under the rule of the King of the Reach as part of a marriage alliance.

Known Kings of the High Tower[ ]

Uthor of the High Tower, the mythical first king of House Hightower and founder of the house.

King Urrigon Hightower, his son and successor.

King Otho II Hightower

King Lymond Hightower, dubbed "the Sea Lion", the last King of the High Tower.
