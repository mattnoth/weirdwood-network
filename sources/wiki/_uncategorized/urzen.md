Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Urzen is among the ironborn who raid the Stony Shore. He and Black Lorren silence animals too badly wounded to be saved. 

Urzen is part of Theon Greyjoy's force that captures Winterfell. Theon has him investigate the godswood of Winterfell when Summer and Shaggydog are no longer heard. When the disemboweled body of Squint is found, Theon mocks and shoves Urzen for not understanding the dangers of direwolves.

Urzen serves as one of Theon's guards at Winterfell.

When northmen led by Ser Rodrik Cassel arrive to retake Winterfell, Theon gives a speech asking ironborn to fight beside him. Urzen shuffles his feet and chooses to depart instead.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Urzen is a member of the crew of the Sea Bitch.
