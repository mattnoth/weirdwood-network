## Summary

A Sworn Brother, or sworn brother, is a member of the Kingsguard or the Night's Watch, two Westerosi orders that require to swear an solemn oath of service for life. Members formally refer to each other as Sworn Brothers, or Brothers for short. Night's Watch men are also called "black brothers" for the color of their clothes.
