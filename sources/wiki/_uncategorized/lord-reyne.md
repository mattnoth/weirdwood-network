## Full Text

Lord Reyne was the Lord of Castamere and head of House Reyne during the civil war known as the Dance of the Dragons.

History[ ]

Lord Reyne declared for the greens during the Dance of the Dragons. He was part of the westermen host led by Lord Jason Lannister then by Ser Adrian Tarbeck and Lord Humfrey Lefford. He was one of the two thousand men who died in the battle by the Lakeshore.
