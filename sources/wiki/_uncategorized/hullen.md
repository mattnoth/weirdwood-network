Character[ ]

Arya Stark considers him to be an old man.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Hullen is a member of the party that discovers the direwolves in the snow. He does not like the fact that direwolves are loose south of the Wall.

Hullen is a part of the entourage accompanying Lord Eddard Stark south when he is named Hand of the King by King Robert I Baratheon. He helps Sansa Stark mounting her horse before they leave for King's Landing. Upon his departure, he gives up the position of master of horse. He is killed when Eddard is arrested. While escaping the Lannister guards, Arya Stark founds him dying in the Red Keep's stables.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

The news of Hullen's death has made its way to Winterfell.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Arya Stark tells Harwin about his father's death.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Theon Greyjoy recalls the people of Winterfell who are now dead, including Hullen.

—Harwin after learning of Hullen's death
