Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Rejecting the authority of the Iron Throne, Lord Balon Greyjoy declares himself King of the Iron Islands. Since Robb Stark, the King in the North, is fighting south of the Neck, the ironborn invade the north during the War of the Five Kings. Balon claims the region by right of conquest and styles himself King of the Isles and the North.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Balon styles himself King of the Iron Islands and the North, King of Salt and Rock, Son of the Sea Wind, and Lord Reaper of Pyke. He dies after falling from Pyke, however.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Most of the ironborn withdraw from the north to attend a kingsmoot on Old Wyk to choose Balon's successor. His brother, Euron Crow's Eye, is chosen after announcing he intends to conquer all of Westeros.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Euron styles himself King of the Isles and the North. Lord Roose Bolton, the new Warden of the North, recovers Moat Cailin from its ironborn garrison and Deepwood Motte is freed by Stannis Baratheon, leaving the Dagmer Cleftjaw-held Torrhen's Square as the only remaining ironborn possession in the north.
