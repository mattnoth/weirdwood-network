Layout[ ]

The Queen's Ballroom, which can seat a hundred, is much smaller than the Great Hall and the Small Hall. Its walls are paneled in richly carved wood, and every wall sconce has beaten silver mirrors which reflect torchlight. Musicians can perform in the gallery above the floor, which is covered with sweet-smelling rushes. Arched windows on the south wall can be covered with thick velvet draperies. The ballroom has long trestle tables and a dais.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

During the Battle of the Blackwater, highborn women of King's Landing and some older men and young boys seek refuge in the Queen's Ballroom. Sansa Stark accompanies Queen Regent Cersei Lannister to the hall.

Maester Ballabar treats Tyrion Lannister, wounded during the battle, in a chamber over the Queen's Ballroom. Podrick Payne, Tyrion's squire, is reluctant to enter the sickroom.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

On the wedding day of King Joffrey I Baratheon and Margaery Tyrell, breakfast is held in the Queen's Ballroom, and guests from the Reach give gifts to the young king.
