Behind the Scenes[ ]

Given the typical High Valyrian spelling of Daeryssa's name, it is possible George R. R. Martin had intended for Daeryssa to be a Targaryen princess when he first introduced Serwyn as a member of the Kingsguard in A Game of Thrones. However, The World of Ice & Fire revealed that Serwyn was actually a legendary hero from the Age of Heroes that singers claim to be a Kingsguard despite the fact that the institution was not created until 10 AC. Daeryssa's historical status has not been mentioned.
