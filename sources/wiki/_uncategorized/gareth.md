The name Gareth is a common first name in Westeros. It can refer to the following characters:

Maester Gareth, the author of Red Sands on the Conquest of Dorne.

Ser Gareth Clifton, the husband to Lady Jeyne Farman.

King Gareth II Gardener, a King of the Reach.

Ser Gareth the Grey, a supporter of Daemon I Blackfyre during the First Blackfyre Rebellion.

Ser Gareth Long

Gareth Tyrell, the first High Steward of the Reach.
