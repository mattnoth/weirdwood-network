## Summary

"The Iron Throne" is the sixth and final episode of the eighth season of HBO's fantasy television series Game of Thrones, and the 73rd overall. The episode was written and directed by David Benioff & D.B. Weiss. It first aired on May 19, 2019, and had 13.61 million viewers.
