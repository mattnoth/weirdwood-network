Culture[ ]

Freedmen are not allowed within the Black Walls of Volantis unless invited by its denizens.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Daenerys Targaryen frees the slaves of Astapor, with thousands of them following her north during her campaign in Slaver's Bay. Thousands more former slaves join her host after the battle near Yunkai. Ser Jorah Mormont organizes the fighting men into four companies. Freedmen aid in Daenerys's siege of Meereen, and half of the city's fighting slaves rise in rebellion against the Great Masters.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Half of Daenerys's freedmen are from Yunkai. The Sons of the Harpy attack unarmed freedmen Meereen, so Daenerys orders the creation of the Brazen Beasts, a new city watch composed of shavepates and freedmen. She also has Grey Worm turn companies of freedmen into soldiers, including the Stalwart Shields, the Free Brothers, and the Mother's Men. The bloody flux spreads among the freedmen at Meereen.

The four mates of the Selaesori Qhoran are all freedmen.
