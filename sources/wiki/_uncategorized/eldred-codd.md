Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

During the kingsmoot on Old Wyk, Eldred dances the finger dance with Qarl the Maid. One of his fingers is cut off and lands in Ralf the Limper's drink. After Victarion and Euron Greyjoy confront each other, Eldred departs the tent with his bloodied hand.
