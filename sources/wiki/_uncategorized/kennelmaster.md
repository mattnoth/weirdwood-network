## Full Text

Kennelmaster is a position in a castle household, usually occupied by a lowborn man. The kennelmaster is charged with the training and care of a castle's hunting hounds.

History[ ]

After reaching majority and beggining to rule in his own right, King Jaehaerys I Targaryen replaced the Red Keep's kennelmaster.

The kennelmaster of Casterly Rock saved the life of his lord, Tytos Lannister, from a lioness. Tytos rewarded the man by knighting him, granting him lands and a towerhouse, and taking his son as his personal squire.

Known kennelmasters[ ]

Clegane, late kennelmaster of Casterly Rock and founder of House Clegane.

Farlen, kennelmaster of Winterfell.

Bass, a steward at Castle Black.

Chett, in charge of the dogs during the great ranging beyond the Wall.

Ben Bones, kennelmaster of the Dreadfort.
