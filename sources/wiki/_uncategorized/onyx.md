Culture[ ]

Altars in septs can be inlaid with onyx, mother-of-pearl, and lapis lazuli. Within the Great Sept of Baelor, a chamber has carvings of the Seven with eyes of onyx, jade, malachite, and yellow moonstone.

The massive chairs of the Pureborn of Qarth are decorated with goldwork and studded with amber, onyx, lapis, and jade. Carved onyx is available at the port of Qarth.

Cyvasse pieces can be carved of ivory and onyx.

Illyrio Mopatis has onyx among his various rings.

The scarlet armor of Lord Tytos Blackwood depicts a flock of ravens with onyx.

People with dark eyes can often be considered as having onyx eyes.

History[ ]

The Onyx Emperor was a ruler of the Great Empire of the Dawn.

The helm of Ser Lucas Inchfield was decorated with an onyx spider to represent House Webber. While disguised as Egg, Prince Aegon Targarye kept hidden a gold and onyx signet depicting the arms of his father, Prince Maekar Targaryen.

When King Robert I Baratheon wed Cersei Lannister, the cloak draped upon Cersei's back was decorated with the Baratheon stag in beads of onyx.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

The Tourmaline Brotherhood gives Daenerys Targaryen a dragon crown with three heads carved from ivory, jade, and onyx. Xaro Xhoan Daxos gives her a thousand miniature knights carved of amber, amethyst, beryl, jade, onyx, opal, and tourmaline, with armor of silver and gold.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

The seven-sided golden chalice given by Lord Mace Tyrell to King Joffrey I Baratheon has an onyx stag to represent House Baratheon. During his wedding feast, Joffrey wears a doublet with onyx studs.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser Balon Swann clasps his Kingsguard cloak with a silver brooch with battling ivory and onyx swans.
