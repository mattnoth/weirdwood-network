## Summary

Benfrey Frey is a knight of House Frey. He is the sixteenth son of Lord Walder Frey, the second by his sixth wife, Lady Bethany Rosby. Benfrey married his cousin Jyanna Frey with whom he has two children, Della and Osmund Frey.

Appearance[ ]

Benfrey resembles his sister Roslin. He is a younger knight.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

When Lord Edmure Tully arrives at the Twins for his wedding, Benfrey is ordered by Lord Walder Frey to lead his sister Roslin into the hall. After Roslin and Edmure meet for the first time, Walder has Benfrey return his sister to her chambers.

During the Red Wedding, Benfrey grabs Dacey Mormont by the arm, but she smashes a flagon full of wine in his face and wrenches free.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Benfrey dies of a wound he receives at the wedding.
