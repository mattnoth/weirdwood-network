## Summary

Red Rolfe takes part in the harrying of the Stony Shore and is among the chosen ironborn whom Theon Greyjoy takes with him to capture Winterfell. He remains with Theon when the northmen led by Ser Rodrik Cassel arrive to retake the castle. Rolfe is killed by Bolton men during the sack of Winterfell.
