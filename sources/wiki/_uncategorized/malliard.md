Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Hallyne informs Tyrion Lannister that Malliard expects the Alchemists' Guild will be able to deliver ten thousand jars of wildfire.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

Malliard may be a reference by George R. R. Martin to the chemist Louis Camille Maillard, the namesake of the Maillard reaction.
