## Full Text

The Blue Pearl is a brothel in King's Landing on the Street of Silk.

History[ ]

According to Alys Turnberry, Ser Braxton Beesbury had fathered a bastard daughter on a  whore at the Blue Pearl.

In 84 AC, two men of the City Watch of King's Landing found Tom Turnip, the fool of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen, surrounded by whores at the Blue Pearl, as well as Ser Braxton Beesbury, Lord Roy Connington, and Jonah Mooton, the male favorites of Princess Saera Targaryen. Lord Roy admitted they thought it would be funny to see the fool do the deed, and Jonah Mooton said the prank had been Saera's idea.
