## Full Text

Lord Cressey was the head of House Cressey during the regency of King Aegon III Targaryen.

History[ ]

Lord Cressey was one of the six lords who accompanied Lord Manfryd Mooton to Braavos in 133 AC. As the Crown's envoy, Manfryd was tasked to make peace with the Sealord of Braavos after the recent conflict between the Free City and Lord Alyn Velaryon. While Manfryd treated with the Sealord, Lord Cressey spread bribes among the Braavosi keyholders, magisters, and other notables.
