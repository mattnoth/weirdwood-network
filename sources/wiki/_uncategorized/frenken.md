Appearance[ ]

Frenken is plump with red hair.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Maester Frenken is one of the first to receive the letters from Stannis Baratheon declaring Cersei Lannister's children with Robert I Baratheon illegitimate. He sends his copy of the letter from Stokeworth on to King's Landing.

Later at the Red Keep, Frenken treats Sansa Stark's wounds after she is beaten by Ser Boros Blount and when she is injured during the riot of King's Landing. He also brings messages to Tyrion Lannister, the acting Hand of the King.

During the Battle of the Blackwater, Frenken treats a scared lady and the wounded Ser Lancel Lannister. After the battle, when the wounded Tyrion dismisses Maester Ballabar on suspicions of trying to kill him, Frenken takes up the task of seeing to Tyrion's recovery.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

After the death of the King Joffrey I Baratheon at his royal wedding, Frenken and Ballabar perform the autopsy that refutes the theory of a suffocation with the pigeon pie. During the trial of Tyrion for the death of the king, Frenken is called as a witness for the prosecution, and he agrees that poison was to blame.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Maester Frenken remains busy at Stokeworth caring for Lollys and her mother, Lady Tanda Stokeworth, who fell and broke her hip. During a duel, Ser Bronn of the Blackwater kills Ser Balman Byrch before the wounded Balman can be treated by Frenken.
