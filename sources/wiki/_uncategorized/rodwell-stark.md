## Full Text

Rodwell Stark was the Lord of Winterfell and the head of House Stark during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen.

History[ ]

Rodwell was the eldest son of Lord Brandon Stark and his lady wife, Alys Karstark, and he had two younger siblings, Beron and Arsa, as well as an older bastard half-brother, Lonnel Snow. 

Lord Rodwell married Lady Myriame Manderly, but had no issue. His brother Beron followed him as Lord of Winterfell.

Rodwell is buried in the crypts of Winterfell.
