Appearance and Character[ ]

See also: Images of Alerie Hightower

Alerie is tall and handsome, with long silvery hair which she braids with jeweled rings. She is younger than her husband, Lord Mace Tyrell. Ser Jaime Lannister thinks she always has a pinched expression.

Sansa Stark finds Alerie to be dignified, but Cersei Lannister thinks her vapid.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

  

Alerie, by Christine Mitzuk ©

Lady Alerie is part of the retinue of her daughter, Margaery Tyrell, when they travel to King's Landing.

Lady Tyrell sedately dances with her husband, Lord Mace Tyrell during the wedding of Tyrion Lannister and Sansa Stark.

Alerie proudly attends the wedding of King Joffrey I Baratheon and Margaery. When Joffrey chokes to death during the feast, Alerie comforts her daughter by explaining that the king choked on pie. Queen Regent Cersei Lannister, however, insists that her son was poisoned and accuses her brother, Tyrion.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lady Alerie attends the funeral of Lord Tywin Lannister with her family.

Alerie echoes her good-mother, Lady Olenna Tyrell, that King Tommen I Baratheon should share a bed with Margaery after they are wed; Cersei acquiesces to the couple spending only their wedding night together, however. Alerie makes the arrangements for the modest wedding feast held in the Small Hall.

After the wedding, Alerie leaves King's Landing for the Reach with Olenna and her second son, Ser Garlan.
