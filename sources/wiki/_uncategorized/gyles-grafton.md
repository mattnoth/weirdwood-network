Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Petyr Baelish, Lord Protector of the Vale, asks Lord Grafton to send him one of his sons to ward at the Eyrie. Gerold sends his youngest son Gyles, who becomes Lord Robert Arryn's squire. On the day of the descent to the Gates of the Moon, Gyles is ordered to clean up the contents of the chamberpot that Robert had thrown at Maester Colemon.
