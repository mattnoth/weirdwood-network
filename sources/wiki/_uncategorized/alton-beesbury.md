## Full Text

Alton, born Alton Beesbury, is a maester of the Citadel hailing from House Beesbury in the Reach.

History[ ]

According to semi-canon sources, Alton was the only son of Ben Beesbury, the Lord of Honeyholt. Alton joined the Citadel to become a maester and renounced his inheritance despite his father's protests. Upon Ben's death, he was succeeded by Alton's cousin, Warryn Beesbury.
