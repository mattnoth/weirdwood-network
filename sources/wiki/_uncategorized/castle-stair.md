## Summary

The broad street is pebbled with white stones and has steps that lead up from the Wolf's Den by the harbors to the New Castle on top of the city's hill. Marble mermaids light the way, bowls of burning whale oil cradled in their arms. From the top there is a view down to both the inner and outer harbor. There is a long tunnel that secretly runs below the Castle Stair connecting the Wolf's Den and the New Castle.
