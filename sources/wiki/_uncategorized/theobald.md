Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Theobald was named Seneschal after Archmaester Walgrave's name was chosen. Walgrave is no longer of sound mind, so Theobald offered to take his place.

Samwell Tarly arrives at the Seneschal's Court in hopes of an audience with the Seneschal, but he is forced to wait in the hall by Lorcas.
