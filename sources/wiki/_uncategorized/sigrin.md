Appearance[ ]

Sigrin is a thick-bodied man with flaxen hair. He has a receding hairline with a pimpled brow.

History[ ]

The first ship Sigrin built was called Esgred, named after his mother. He also built Sea Bitch.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Calling herself Esgred, Asha Greyjoy claims to be Sigrin's wife when she first encounters her brother, Theon Greyjoy, at Lordsport.
