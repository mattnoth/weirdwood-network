## Full Text

Armistead Vance was a member of House Vance and the mightiest of the Andal warlords that invaded the riverlands.

History[ ]

Armistead was presumably one of the seven Andal kings who defeated Tristifer IV Mudd, King of the Rivers and the Hills. Afterward he gained the allegiance of Edmure Tully and his sons. Armistead granted Edmure's son Axel lands at the juncture of the Red Fork and the Tumblestone, where Lord Axel raised Riverrun.
