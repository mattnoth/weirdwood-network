## Full Text

Robert Reyne was the Lord of Castamere and head of House Reyne during the time Gerold the Golden was Lord of Casterly Rock. He was the father of Roger, Reynard, and Ellyn.

History[ ]

Lord Robert arranged the betrothal of his daughter Ellyn to Lord Gerold Lannister's son and heir, Tywald. Tywald served Robert as a squire.

During the Peake Uprising both Robert and Tywald were killed during the Storming of Starpike. Upon Robert's death, his son Roger became the new Lord of Castamere. In the aftermath of Starpike, the vengeful Roger executed seven captive Peakes before Prince Aegon Targaryen prevented him from harming others.
