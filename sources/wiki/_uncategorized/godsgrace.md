History[ ]

During Aegon's Conquest, Rhaenys Targaryen found Godsgrace to be deserted during her flight on Meraxes toward Sunspear.

According to Archmaester Gyldayn, each Dornish castle, with the exception of Sunspear, was burned in the Dragon's Wroth during the First Dornish War.

Recent Events[ ]

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Ser Daemon Sand departs Godsgrace to accompany Princess Arianne Martell in her mission to meet Aegon Targaryen.
