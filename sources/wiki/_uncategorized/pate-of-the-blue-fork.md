Character[ ]

Pate was brave, and wanted to make a great name for himself. However, he was also stingy,and some considered him an oaf.

History[ ]

Pate the hedge knight married Amerei Frey, the eldest daughter of Merrett Frey, after she was caught in the stables of the Twins having sex with three grooms at the same time. The bard Tom of Sevenstreams, Pate's cousin, sang at his wedding to Amerei, but afterwards Pate did not want to pay for his singing.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Amerei Frey is widowed because of Pate's death.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Walder Frey tells Robb Stark he had married off his granddaughter Amerei to Pate, but Pate was then killed by Ser Gregor Clegane during the War of the Five Kings.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

When Jaime Lannister visits Castle Darry, Lady Amerei tells how Pate had sworn to kill Gregor Clegane when House Frey was still supporting Robb Stark.
