## Summary

Fighting pits are arenas in the Slaver Cities of Astapor, Meereen, and Yunkai. The fighting pits of Meereen, the largest and most extravagant of Slaver's Bay, are great red circles. Within the pits, beasts are pitted against beasts, pit fighters against fellow fighters, beasts against fighters, and even beasts against slave children, dwarfs, or crones, all for the entertainment of onlookers, which includes nobility and the general populace. Combatants wear no armor in the fighting pits since spectators want to see blood. In addition to the grand public pits, establishments like the Purple Lotus can have their own small pits as well.
