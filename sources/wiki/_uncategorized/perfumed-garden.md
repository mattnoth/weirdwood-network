History[ ]

The Perfumed Garden was one of the many holdings owned by House Rogare under Lysandro "the Magnificent". In 135 AC, Lysandro drowned when his opulent barge sank whilst carrying him from the Perfumed Garden back to his palace. 

A younger son of Lysandro, Fredo, inherited the Perfumed Garden. When the Rogare Bank ran dry, Fredo was put on trial, and the Rogare Bank was seized, along with the bed-slaves there. When the Rogare's assets proved insufficient to cover their debts, the Rogares were also sold into slavery, with the daughters of Lysaro and Fredo Rogare becoming bed-slaves in the Perfumed Garden.
