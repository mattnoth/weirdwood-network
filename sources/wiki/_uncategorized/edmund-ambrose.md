A Feast for Crows[ ]

While Brienne of Tarth waits for Dick Crabb, she reflects on the events at Highgarden when a group of knights made a wager on who would be the first to take her maidenhead. To woo her Edmund brought flowers and offered to ride with her. After the bet was discovered Brienne sought out and defeated Edmund during the melee at Bitterbridge.
