## Full Text

This article is about the Lord Dondarrion who is unnamed in the books and called Beric in the adaptation House of the Dragon. For the leader of the brotherhood without banners, see Beric Dondarrion.

Lord Dondarrion was the Lord of Blackhaven and head of House Dondarrion during the late reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

In the television adaptation House of the Dragon, he is named Beric Dondarrion and portrayed by Paul Leonard. He also mentions Simon Dondarrion as his father.

History[ ]

He was the Lord of Blackhaven when Criston Cole was born in 82 AC. Criston's father served as his steward.
