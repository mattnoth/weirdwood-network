A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lucos becomes the new head of his House when his father, Lord Chyttering, burns during the Battle of the Blackwater. Lord Lucos remains loyal to Stannis Baratheon. Despite his youth, Stannis considers him among the few good men he has left but says he trusts none of them as much as he does Davos Seaworth, who wants as his new Hand of the King.
