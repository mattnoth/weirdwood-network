Pate of Fairmarket was a lowborn king from the the town of Fairmarket in the riverlands.

History[ ]

Pate claimed the title of river king or King of the Trident in the generations after the riverlands were conquered by the Storm King Arlan III Durrandon. Pate even had a brief, inglorious reign, presumably ruling from Fairmarket, before being put down by the might of Storm's End.
