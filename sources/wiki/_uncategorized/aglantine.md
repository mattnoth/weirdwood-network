Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Septas  Aglantine, Moelle, Melicent, and Nysterica examine Margaery Tyrell to see if her maidenhead is still intact, but they find it is not. This is the grounds for Margaery's arrest by the Faith on charges of infidelity.
