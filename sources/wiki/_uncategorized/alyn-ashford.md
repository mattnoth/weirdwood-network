## Full Text

Alyn Ashford was a knight of House Ashford during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

In 73 AC, Alyn took part in a tourney at Old Oak, held by Lord Oakheart. Alyn was defeated by a mystery knight known as the Silver Fool, who was later revealed to be Prince Baelon Targaryen.
