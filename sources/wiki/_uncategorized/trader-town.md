## Full Text

Trader Town

The known world and the location of Trader Town

Trader Town is a rude, sprawling garrison city of northern Yi Ti in eastern Essos. The city lies east of the Great Sand Sea, and west of the Shrinking Sea. The vast plains of the Jogos Nhai are north of Trader Town. From Kayakayanaya, the Steel Road skirts the topmost northern edge of the Great Sand Sea, and turns south to Trader Town. The city is connected to Tiqui and Bayasabhad by the Sand Road.

Trader Town is the seat of General Pol Qo, Hammer of the Jogos Nhai, the self-proclaimed first orange emperor.
