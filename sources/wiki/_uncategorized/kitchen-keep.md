Layout[ ]

The Kitchen Keep is located across the courtyard from the main kitchen of the Red Keep, and occupants can see and smell food being prepared. Below the Kitchen Keep is a gloomy cellar with a vaulted stone ceiling, and the keep connects to other buildings through underground passageways.

Lord Gyles Rosby has spacious apartments atop the Kitchen Keep, including a large bedchamber and solar, a bath and dressing room, and small adjoining chambers for servants.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Gyles Rosby is dispossessed of his apartments in the Kitchen Keep by Lord Tywin Lannister so that Tywin's son Tyrion can move in with his household.
