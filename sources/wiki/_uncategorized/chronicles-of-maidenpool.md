The Chronicles of Maidenpool is a history of Maidenpool in the riverlands.

History[ ]

During the Dance of the Dragons, Maester Norren was the keeper of the text. He recorded the time Prince Daemon Targaryen and his alleged paramour, the dragonseed Nettles, spent at Maidenpool as guests of Lord Manfryd Mooton.
