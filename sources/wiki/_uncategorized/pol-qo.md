## Full Text

Pol Qo, called Hammer of the Jogos Nhai, is a YiTish general who has claimed the title of God-Emperor.

History[ ]

Pol Qo is the first of the orange emperors of the Golden Empire of Yi Ti, in defiance of Bu Gai, the seventeenth azure emperor, enthroned in the capital city of Yin. Qo's seat is located in the garrison city of Trader Town. Another pretender is a sorcerer lord, claiming to be the sixty-ninth yellow emperor despite the fall of that dynasty a thousand years ago, who is exiled to the city of Carcosa, far to the east of Yi Ti. It is unknown which of the three emperors will prevail.
