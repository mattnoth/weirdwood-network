Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser Jaime Lannister, newly Lord Commander, looks at the seat of the Lord Commander of the Kingsguard in the Round Room and wonders how he, the Kingslayer, could belong in the company of Lords Commander past, including Alyn Connington.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Jaime speaks with Ser Loras Tyrell and tells him that he would do well to learn about the Kingsguard that came before then. Alyn Connington is among those he expect Loras to have already heard of.
