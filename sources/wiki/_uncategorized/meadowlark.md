Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After arriving in Lys from the Planky Town, Quentyn Martell and his party decide to switch ships to ensure that anyone following them would find it harder to keep track of their movements. They hire the Meadowlark to take them as far as Volantis.

Off the coast of the Disputed Lands, the Meadowlark is attacked by corsairs. The pirates are fended off, but at the cost of nine Meadowlarks, Maester Kedry, Ser Cletus Yronwood, and Ser Willam Wells.
