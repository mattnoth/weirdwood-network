## Full Text

An octopod, or octopus, is a sea creature that dwells in the oceans off Westeros and Essos, probably similar to real-life octopodes.

Ghiscari are fond of rich food, including octopus stew. The Merman's Court inside New Castle in White Harbor features paintings of octopods slithering amongst rocks and sunken ships.
