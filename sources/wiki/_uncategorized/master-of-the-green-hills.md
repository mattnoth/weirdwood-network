## Summary

Master of the Green Hills is a defunct title once held by the head of House Yronwood, dating back to the time they held sway over the north of Dorne and ruled in their own right as High Kings of Dorne, Lords of the Stone Way, and Lords of Yronwood. There are high meadows north of Yronwood.
