Language[ ]

Braavosi retains some words and phrases from High Valyrian in its pure form, including valar morghulis (all men must die) and valar dohaeris (all men must serve).

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Arya Stark learns some Braavosi words while sailing on the Titan's Daughter from Saltpans to Braavos, such as the words for please, thank you, sea, star, and fire wine.

The waif teaches Arya the tongue of Braavos at the House of Black and White, while Arya teaches the waif the Common Tongue. Arya learns more of the Braavosi tongue while acting as Cat of the Canals.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Tyrion Lannister knows some Braavosi.

Maester Kedry is fluent in the tongues of all of the Free Cities.

Arya hears priests of the Faceless Men speaking Braavosi and High Valyrian during their meeting at the House of Black and White.
