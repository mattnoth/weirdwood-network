Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

When war breaks out, Lord Lewys Lydden supports King Joffrey I Baratheon. He is part of the army of his liege, Tywin Lannister, the Lord of Casterly Rock. Lewys is positioned at the center of the Lannister host during the battle on the Green Fork, where he fights under the command of Ser Kevan Lannister.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lewys is one of the great western lords honored at King's Landing after the Battle of the Blackwater.
