History[ ]

The Lockes ruled as First Men kings but were eventually reduced to vassals by the Kings of Winter from House Stark.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord Ondrew Locke does not come to the harvest feast at Winterfell due to his old age.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ondrew attends the wedding of Ramsay Bolton and "Arya Stark" (actually Jeyne Poole) at Winterfell. When Ser Hosteen Frey attacks Lord Wyman Manderly at breakfeast with a longsword, Lord Locke starts shouting for a maester to tend to the wounded man.
