Plummer was the steward of Ashford during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen.

In the television adaptation A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms, Plummer is portrayed by Tom Vaughan-Lawlor.

Appearance[ ]

Plummer had a narrow, pinched face and thin grey hair.

History[ ]

Plummer was the master of the games for the tourney at Ashford Meadow. He initially rejected Ser Duncan the Tall's entry into the tourney on the grounds that no one knew who he was, until Prince Baelor Targaryen vouched for him.

Quotes[ ]

Duncan: Are you Plummer the steward? I came for the tourney. To enter the lists.

Plummer: My lord's tourney is a contest for knights. Are you a knight?
