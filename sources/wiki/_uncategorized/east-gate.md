Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

In the aftermath of the sack of Winterfell, Osha and Rickon Stark take the East Gate to the kingsroad when they depart the castle.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Since the Kingsroad Gate has not been used recently, ice freezes its drawbridge chains. When Lord Roose Bolton, Warden of the North, decides to send the feuding Freys and Manderlys against Stannis Baratheon, he orders the Freys to gather by Winterfell's main gates and the Manderlys by the east gate.
