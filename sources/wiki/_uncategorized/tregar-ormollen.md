History[ ]

A native of Lys, Tregar is a wealthy merchant prince with a wife and several concubines.

In 293 AC, Ser Jorah Mormont and his wife, Lynesse Hightower, arrived in Lys, seeking an escape from Jorah's crimes in Westeros. In order to keep up with his wife's demands of wealth and luxury, Jorah sold his service as a sellsword. However, while Jorah was fighting the Braavosi on the Rhoyne, Lynesse met Tregar. Soon, he had taken her as one of his concubines and moved her into his manse.

When Jorah returned to Lys, Tregar informed him that he would be enslaved for his debts, unless he gave up Lynesse and left Lys. Heartbroken, Jorah went to Volantis. As Tregar's chief concubine, it is rumored that even his wife goes in fear of Lynesse.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

After Euron Greyjoy's ironborn attack the Reach, Ser Humfrey Hightower travels to Lys in hopes of hiring sellsails with the aid of his sister, Lynesse.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser Jorah Mormont tells Tyrion Lannister of Tregar's threats of enslavement if he did not give up Lynesse.
