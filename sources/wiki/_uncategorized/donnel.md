## Full Text

The name Donnel can refer to the following characters:

Ser Donnel of Duskendale, a knight of King Daeron II Targaryen's Kingsguard

Lord Donnel Arryn, the Lord of the Eyrie during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen

Donnel Drumm, a son of Lord Dunstan Drumm

Donnel Flint, also known as "Black Donnel Flint", the son and heir of Lord Torghen Flint

Ser Donnel Haigh, the second son of Ser Leslyn Haigh and Lady Perriane Frey

Donnel Hightower, called "Donnel the Delayer", the Lord of the Hightower during the early reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen

Donnel Hill, also known as "Sweet Donnel", a steward of the Night's Watch

Ser Donnel Locke, a knight of House Locke who fights with Robb Stark in the War of the Five Kings

Ser Donnel Swann, the eldest son and heir of Lord Gulian Swann of Stonehelm

Donnel Waynwood, the second son of Lady Anya Waynwood and successor of Brynden Tully as the Knight of the Gate in the Vale
