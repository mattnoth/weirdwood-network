Culture[ ]

Three black scorpions are included in the arms of House Qorgyle of Sandstone.

The harpies of Slaver's Bay have the curled and venomous tails of scorpions.

A type of siege weapon is known as a scorpion.

History[ ]

After the Submission of Sunspear during his conquest of Dorne, King Daeron I Targaryen appointed Lord Lyonel Tyrell to rule Dorne in his absence. Lyonel was killed at Sandstone when a hundred red scorpions were dropped on him from a canopy above his bed, and his assassination was the signal for Dorne to rise in rebellion against the Young Dragon.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

While the khalasar of Daenerys Targaryen is staying in Vaes Tolorro, a woman dies after being stung by a red scorpion.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Prince Oberyn Martell give King Joffrey I Baratheon a red gold brooch in the shape of a scorpion as a wedding gift.
