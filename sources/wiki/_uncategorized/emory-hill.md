## Full Text

Emory Hill, called the Bastard of Lannisport, was a knight from the westerlands, likely a bastard of the Lannisters of Lannisport.

History[ ]

Emory joined Lord Jason Lannister's host during the Dance of the Dragons, in support of King Aegon II Targaryen and the greens. Emory was among the notable casualties during the Battle by the Lakeshore in 130 AC, where the westermen host was defeated by the riverlords.

Notes[ ]

↑ The Princess and the Queen named him Tyler Hill. In Fire & Blood, he is renamed to Emory Hill and Ser Tyler Hill is identified as a bastard of the main branch of House Lannister during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.
