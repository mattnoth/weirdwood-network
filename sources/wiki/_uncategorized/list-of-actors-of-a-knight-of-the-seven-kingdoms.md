A-O[ ]

Bertie Carvel, Baelor Targaryen[ ]

Robert Hugh Carvel is an English actor born 6 September 1977 in Marleybone, London. He plays Baelor Targaryen. Carvel is known for Jonathan Strange & Mr Norrell, Doctor Foster, Matilda the Musical, and Ink. (IMDb)

Chloe Lea, Rafe[ ]

Chloe Lea is a British actress born 4 November 2005 in Blackburn, England, who grew up in Swinton. She plays Rafe in flashback scenes. Lea is known for Dune: Prophecy and Foundation. (IMDb)

Connan Aaron, Gowen Baratheon[ ]

Connan Aaron is an Irish actor. He plays Gowen Baratheon. Aaron was a stand-in for Kit Harington (Jon Snow) in the final two seasons of Game of Thrones.

Daniel Ings, Lyonel Baratheon[ ]

Daniel Ings is an English actor born 30 November 1985. He plays Lyonel Baratheon. Ings is known for Lovesick, Black Mirror, and The Crown. (IMDb)

Daniel Monks, Manfred Dondarrion[ ]

Daniel Monks is an Australian actor and screenwriter. He plays Manfred Dondarrion. He is known for Pulse, Dead Hot, and In the Room Where He Waits. (IMDb)

Danny Webb, Arlan of Pennytree[ ]

Daniel "Danny" Webb is an English actor born 6 June 1958 in London. He plays Arlan of Pennytree. Webb is known for Alien 3, Henry VIII, and Doctor Who. (IMDb)

Dexter Sol Ansell, Egg[ ]

Dexter Sol Ansell is an English child actor born 16 September 2014. He plays Egg. Ansell is known for The Hunger Games: The Ballad of Songbirds & Snakes. (IMDb)

Edward Ashley, Steffon Fossoway[ ]

Edward Ashley is an English actor. He plays Steffon Fossoway. Ashley is known for The Lost City of Z, The Terror, and Masters of the Air. (IMDb)

Finn Bennett, Aerion Targaryen[ ]

Finn Bennett is an Irish-British actor born 1999. He plays Aerion Targaryen. Bennett is known for True Detective: Night Country. (IMDb)

Henry Ashton, Daeron Targaryen[ ]

Henry Ashton is an actor. He plays Daeron Targaryen. Ashton is known for A Good Girl's Guide to Murder, Outlander, and My Lady Jane. (IMDb)

P-Z[ ]

Peter Claffey, Duncan the Tall[ ]

Peter Claffey is an Irish actor and former rugby union player born 28 July 1996 in Portumna, County Galway. He plays Duncan the Tall. Claffey is known for Harry Wild, Wreck, and Bad Sisters. (IMDb)

Ross Anderson, Humfrey Hardyng[ ]

Ross Anderson is a Scottish actor born 15 July 1987 in Paisley, Renfrewshire. He plays Humfrey Hardyng. Anderson is known for The Last Kingdom, The King's Man, and Crawl. (IMDb)

Sam Spruell, Maekar Targaryen[ ]

Sam Spruell is an English actor born 1 January 1977 in Southwark, London. He plays Maekar Targaryen. Spruell is known for Snow White and the Huntsman, Legend, and Taken 3. (IMDb)

Shaun Thomas, Raymun Fossoway[ ]

Shaun Thomas is an English actor born 30 May 1997 in Bradford, West Yorkshire. He plays Raymun Fossoway. He is known for The Selfish Giant, The Brothers Grimsby, and How to Have Sex. (IMDb)

Steve Wall, Leo Tyrell[ ]

Stephen Patrick "Steve" Wall is an Irish musician and actor born in Kingsbury, London, England. He plays Leo Tyrell. Wall is known for Dune: Part Two, The Witcher, and The English. (IMDb)

Tanzyn Crawford, Tanselle[ ]

Tanzyn Crawford is an Australian actress born in Perth. She plays Tanselle. Crawford is known for Servant, Tiny Beautiful Things, and Swift Street. (IMDb)

Tom Vaughan-Lawlor, Plummer[ ]

Tom Vaughan-Lawlor is an Irish actor born 4 November 1977 in Dundrum, Dublin. He plays Plummer. Vaughan-Lawlor is known for Love/Hate, Avengers: Infinity War, and Avengers: Endgame. (IMDb)

Wade Briggs, Roland Crakehall[ ]

Wade Briggs is an Australian actor born in Melbourne, Victoria. He plays Roland Crakehall. Briggs is known for Foundation and His Dark Materials. (IMDb)

Youssef Kerkour, Steely Pate[ ]

Youssef Kerkour is a Moroccan-English actor. He plays Steely Pate. Kerkour is known for Home. (IMDb)
