Appearance[ ]

Roggon has a huge red beard. He and Rolfe the Dwarf are Asha Greyjoy's best horse riders.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Roggon sails to Deepwood Motte with Asha Greyjoy, along with the rest of her supporters, as she contemplates her next move after the kingsmoot on Old Wyk chose Euron Greyjoy.

Asha sends Rogon and Rolfe the Dwarf ahead to scout for northmen as her crew flees Deepwood for the Bay of Ice. Roggon is captured along with Asha, Tristifer Botley, and several other ironborn by Stannis Baratheon during the fight by Deepwood Motte. 

Roggon is later freed and ransomed, along with the other ironborn, by Tycho Nestoris, who needs an escort to get to Stannis during his march on Winterfell.
