Rhaena Targaryen is the name of several members of House Targaryen:

Rhaena Targaryen, also known as Queen in the West and Queen in the East, eldest daughter and child of King Aenys I Targaryen, one of King Maegor I Targaryen's Black Brides, elder sister to King Jaehaerys I and Queen Alysanne Targaryen

Rhaena Targaryen, also known as Rhaena of Pentos, younger daughter of Prince Daemon Targaryen and Lady Laena Velaryon

Rhaena Targaryen, second daughter of King Aegon III Targaryen and Queen Daenaera Velaryon
