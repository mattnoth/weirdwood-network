History[ ]

A black tomcat once snatched a roast quail from the hands of Lord Tywin Lannister during dinner at the Red Keep.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Tyrion Lannister takes the finest cook in King's Landing into his service after becoming Hand of the King, and he enjoys quails drowned in butter during his meeting with Janos Slynt.

Koss and Kurz hunt deer and quail for Yoren's band of recruits.

Ser Wendel Manderly offers to hunt quail for Catelyn Stark near Bitterbridge.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Quail is served at the wedding of Petyr Baelish and Lysa Arryn.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Tyrion is served quails in honey at Illyrio's manse in Pentos. Ser Kevan Lannister has a brace of quail at the Red Keep.
