## Full Text

Lorimar Peake was the Lord of Starpike and Dunstonbury, and the head of House Peake during the reign of Perceon III Gardener, King of the Reach.

History[ ]

Lord Lorimar drove House Manderly into exile at the behest of King Perceon III Gardener, who feared their swelling power in the Reach. This allowed House Peake to acquire the Manderly seat of Dunstonbury. Lorimar's daughter married Perceon's son, Gwayne, and she became the seventh Peake maiden to become Queen of All the Reach.
