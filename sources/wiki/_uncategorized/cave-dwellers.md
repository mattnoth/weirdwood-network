Culture[ ]

The cave dwellers dye and paint their faces blue, green and purple, and they file their teeth. Some accounts state the cave dwellers worship dark underground gods.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Cave dwellers are among Mance Rayder's army. They scatter when Stannis Baratheon attacks them in the battle beneath the Wall.
