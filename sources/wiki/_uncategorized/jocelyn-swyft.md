Character[ ]

Jocelyn follows the Faith of the Seven.

Cersei Lannister considers Jocelyn to be timid and slow.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

A frightened Jocelyn is present when Dowager Queen Cersei Lannister receives news of the death of her father, Lord Tywin Lannister. 

As Jocelyn helps Queen Regent Cersei prepare for the wedding of her son, King Tommen I Baratheon, to Lady Margaery Tyrell, Cersei takes out her anger on her companion.

Cersei is presented with the head of a dwarf falsely claimed to be that of her disgraced brother, Tyrion Lannister, and Jocelyn throws up when she sees the head covered with maggots.

Jocelyn spends time in the queen's apartments with Lady Taena Merryweather and a handmaid, Dorcas, while Cersei is away for a small council meeting.

Jocelyn and Dorcas continue to serve Cersei. When Cersei dismisses Tommen from her presence, she often sends the boy off with Jocelyn. The girl snores at night.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When Cersei completes her walk of atonement through the streets of King's Landing and enters the Red Keep, Jocelyn provides a blanket when the Lord Regent, Ser Kevan Lannister, commands the naked Cersei to be covered up.

Family[ ]

It is unclear how Jocelyn is related to the other members of House Swyft. 

Quotes[ ]

Cersei: Jocelyn Swyft is a fool. If the gods could weep, they would have wept for your brother. Rain is rain.
