## Full Text

Defender of the Citadel, also called Protector of the Citadel, is one of the hereditary titles held by the head of House Hightower. The Citadel, the center of the order of maesters, was established on a tract of land granted by King Urrigon Hightower and ever since, House Hightower has been a patron of the order.
