Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

When war breaks out, Lord Regenard Estren supports King Joffrey I Baratheon. He is a part of Ser Jaime Lannister's force besieging Riverrun, but is captured at the battle in the Whispering Wood.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Estren is held captive at the Twins.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Regenard is released following the Red Wedding.

Known Lords of Wyndhall[ ]

Lord Regenard Estren, the current Lord of Wyndhall.
