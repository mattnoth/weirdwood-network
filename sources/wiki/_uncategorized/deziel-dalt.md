Character[ ]

Princess Arianne Martell believes that Ser Deziel is dutiful to his liege, Prince Doran Martell.

History[ ]

Deziel had once aspired to marry Princess Arianne Martell, as had his younger brother, Ser Andrey Dalt.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Deziel is one of the Dornish nobles who are part of the entourage of Prince Oberyn Martell when he comes to King's Landing to claim the seat Tyrion Lannister had offered the Prince of Dorne, Doran Martell.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

While imprisoned by her father, Doran, Princess Arianne Martell considers contacting Deziel for aid. She decides against it, however, considering him too dutiful to his liege lord to be willing to act against him, and not nearly powerful enough to be successful.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Deziel is present at the feast when Ser Balon Swann arrives in Sunspear to deliver the Mountain's skull. He drinks when Ricasso raises a toast to King Tommen I Baratheon.
