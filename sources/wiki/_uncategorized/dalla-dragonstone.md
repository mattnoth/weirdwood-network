Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Dalla and Matrice talk about the red comet, and Dalla shares that she had heard Melisandre tell Queen Selyse Florent that the comet was "dragonsbreath". Princess Shireen Baratheon overhears their discussion, and when she worriedly informs Maester Cressen, he thinks he must have a word with Dalla, to warn her not to spread such rumors.
