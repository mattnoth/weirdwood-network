## Summary

The Sealord's Palace is the seat of Ferrego Antaryon, the Sealord of Braavos. It is located on a small peninsula in the northeast of the Free City of Braavos, at the eastern end of the Purple Harbor. It is north of the Moon Pool, the headquarters of the Iron Bank, and the end of the sweetwater river.

Layout[ ]

The Sealord's Palace has domes and towers. A golden thunderbolt turns on a spire atop the palace. The Sealord's Palace contains the Sealord's menagerie. Water dancers duel at the Moon Pool near the sprawling building.

History[ ]

Before Elissa Farman departed Braavos on Sun Chaser, the Sealord called her to his palace and advised her to sail away quickly and cautiously.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Deserting from the Night's Watch, Dareon intends to one day perform at the Sealord's Palace. He is killed by Arya Stark, however.

Quotes[ ]

Soon I will be playing in the Purple, and after that the Sealord's Palace.
