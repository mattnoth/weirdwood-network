Character[ ]

Unlike his elder brother, Gareth was of a more bookish bent and never achieved knighthood, choosing to serve as a royal steward instead.

History[ ]

Gareth and his son Leo performed their duties so well that the Gardeners made the office of High Steward hereditary.
