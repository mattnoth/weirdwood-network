Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ollidor is among the leading possibilities to replace the deceased High Septon according to Grand Maester Pycelle. He is on the verge of being elected High Septon when the sparrows follow him to a brothel and drag him naked into the street. Septon Luceon replaces him as the leading candidate.
