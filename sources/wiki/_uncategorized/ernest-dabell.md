## Full Text

Ser Ernest Dabell was a knight during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen. He had two brothers: Ser Lester and Ser Pascal Dabell.

History[ ]

He participated in the tourney at Ashford Meadow in 209 AC.

Behind the scenes[ ]

The character is named after Ernest Dabel, a producer of the graphic novel adaptations of The Hedge Knight and The Sworn Sword.
