Apperance and Character[ ]

Morna wears a white weirwood mask. She has at least one son.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Morna White Mask is among the free folk leaders who accompany Tormund Giantsbane to the Wall in declaring a truce with the Night's Watch. She is given the abandoned castle Queensgate by Jon Snow, Lord Commander of the Night's Watch, to garrison and settle her own people.
