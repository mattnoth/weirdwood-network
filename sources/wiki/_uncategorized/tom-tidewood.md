A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Tom accompanies Victarion Greyjoy when the Iron Fleet sails to Slaver's Bay. Victarion has Tom sew new robes in the colors of House Greyjoy for Moqorro. After Victarion kills the master of a fishing ketch, Tom and Longwater Pyke toss the body into the sea.
