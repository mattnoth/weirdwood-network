History[ ]

The daughter of the Archon of Tyrosh during the early reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen was considered to have a delightful accent when she attended the Golden Wedding.

Larra Rogare, the wife of Viserys Targaryen, was fluent in High Valyrian and the tongues of Tyrosh, Myr, Volantis, and her native Lys.

Daenerys Targaryen learned to speak Free Cities Valyrian with a Tyroshi accent from her time living in Tyrosh as a child.

Tyrion Lannister learned a few Tyroshi phrases from a sellsword he knew at Casterly Rock.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Chett considers Tyroshi to be a wet girly tongue.

Symon Silver Tongue believes that Collio Quaynis's Tyroshi accent is absurd in the Common Tongue, and Tyrion Lannister finds Collio's singing to be ludicrous.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Near Oldtown, the captain of the Huntress tells Samwell Tarly that ironborn captured a Tyroshi merchantman in the Redwyne Straits. They killed the ship's crew and disguised themselves as Tyroshi sailors intending to sneak into Oldtown. Their plot was foiled when the oarsmaster of Lady of the Tower hailed the ironmen in the Tyroshi language and they failed to reply to his greeting.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Maester Kedry is fluent in the tongues of all of the Free Cities.

Young Griff is fluent in High Valyrian, the dialects of Tyrosh, Pentos, Myr, and Lys, as well as the trade talk of sailors.
