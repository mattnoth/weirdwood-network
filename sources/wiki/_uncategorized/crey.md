## Full Text

Crey was an archmaester of the Citadel during the late reign of Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

Archmaester Crey is dismissive of the story told by Coryanne Wylde in a A Caution for Young Girls that she was ordered by Lord Rogar Baratheon to try and seduce King Jaehaerys while he was in his minority on Dragonstone. Crey insists that it was a calumny to try and blacken Lord Rogar's name and suspects that Rogar's traitorous brother, Ser Borys Baratheon was the one who started the lie.
