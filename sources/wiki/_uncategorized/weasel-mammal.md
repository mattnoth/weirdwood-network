A weasel is small carnivorous mammal native to Westeros. It is related to the stoat.

Biology[ ]

Weasels are quick and lithe.

In culture[ ]

A white weasel is included in the arms of House Varner.

Members of House Frey are often compared to weasels on account of their facial features.

Pain caused by the tears of Lys has been compared to having a weasel in one's guts.

Women enthusiastic in bed have been compared to squirming, lithe weasels.

Weasel Alley is located in King's Landing.

History[ ]

Haggon advised Varamyr against skinchanging into weasels.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lommy Greenhands gives the name Weasel to a crying girl because of her appearance. While forced to serve at Harrenhal by House Lannister, Arya Stark takes the name "Weasel" for her own. The people at Harrenhal make jokes about weasel soup because of Arya's participation in the castle's fall.
