History[ ]

Lady Corbray is the sixteen-year-old daughter of a rich Gulltown merchant of lowborn birth.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord Petyr Baelish, a former customs officer at the port of Gulltown, brokers a marriage between the merchant's daughter and the childless Lyonel Corbray, Lord of Heart's Home, after the passing of Lyonel's first wife. Lyonel, the head of impoverished House Corbray, receives a significant dowry for marrying a commoner. It is a well-attended wedding, with Lord Benedar Belmore, Lord Gerold Grafton, Lord Jon Lynderly, Ser Symond Templeton, Lord Waxley, and Lady Anya Waynwood present.

Petyr reports that Lyonel is pleased with his wife and with her dowry.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Lord Lyonel and the new Lady Corbray do not attend the tourney for the Brotherhood of Winged Knights at the Gates of the Moon, remaining at Heart's Home instead where they are expecting a baby. The prospect of a child angers Lyonel's brother and heir, Ser Lyn Corbray, as this pushes him down in the line of succession.
