The Hooded Wayfarer is a god. There is a statue of it in the House of Black and White in Braavos. The statue is most commonly visited by poor men.

Recent events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

After entering the House of Black and White for the first time, Arya Stark notices the statue of a hooded man leaning on a staff. She later learns the statue is a favorite of poor men, and that the Hooded Wayfarer is considered by the Faceless Men to be an aspect of the Many-Faced God.
