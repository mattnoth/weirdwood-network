Recent Events[ ]

A Feast For Crows[ ]

Following Princess Arianne Martell's failed attempt to name Princess Myrcella Baratheon as Queen of Westeros, her co-conspirators are sent to Ghaston Grey to await their sentences from Doran Martell, Prince of Dorne. Sylva Santagar is ordered by her father, Ser Symon Santagar, to wed Lord Eldon Estermont, and Garin and Andrey Dalt are eventually sent by Doran to serve for a time in Tyrosh and Norvos, respectively.
