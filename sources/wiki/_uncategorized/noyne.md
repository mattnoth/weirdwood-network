The Noyne is a river in northwestern Essos. The Rhoynar considered it a daughter of the Rhoyne and nicknamed it the Wild Daughter. Its headwaters lie in the Hills of Norvos and it flows south into the Rhoyne at the ruins of Ny Sar. The Free City of Norvos sits along its banks. Norvoshi galleys control the river as far south as Ny Sar.

History[ ]

The Andal king Qarlon the Great extended his control from Lorath to the headwaters of the Noyne, but he was eventually defeated by the Valyrian Freehold.
