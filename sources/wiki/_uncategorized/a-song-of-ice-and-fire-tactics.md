## Summary

A Song of Ice and Fire: Tactics is an upcoming tabletop skirmish game produced by CMON Limited and Dark Swords Miniatures, based on the A Song of Ice and Fire novels by George R. R. Martin. The first Skirmish Set, Battle of the Trident, will be published in 2025.
