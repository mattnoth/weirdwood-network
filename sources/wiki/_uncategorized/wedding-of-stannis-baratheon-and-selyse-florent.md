History[ ]

Wedding[ ]

While Lord Stannis Baratheon and Lady Selyse Florent were dancing at their wedding feast, Stannis's elder brother, King Robert, deflowered Delena Florent, Selyse's cousin and one of her bedmaids, in the wedding bed. Robert later told Stannis he had not recognized the bedchamber because he was drunk, and had not meant to cause shame. However, Stannis felt his brother's actions were an insult to House Florent.

Aftermath[ ]

  

Shireen Baratheon, by Sara Biddle © Fantasy Flight Games

When Delena gave birth to Robert's bastard, Edric Storm, Stannis had the infant sent to Storm's End to be fostered by his younger brother, Lord Renly Baratheon.

Lord Tywin Lannister offered to betroth his son Tyrion to Delena, but her father, Ser Colin Florent, refused, and instead married her to one of his household knights, Ser Hosman Norcross.

Stannis only does his duty in the marriage bed once or twice a year, taking no joy in it. Though he once hoped for sons, Stannis has only had a daughter, Shireen, with Selyse. Selyse believes that Robert and Delena's dalliance cursed her marriage and made her unable to birth a son.

Some years before the start of A Song of Ice and Fire, Lord Varys informed Robert's small council that Selyse had taken up with a red priestess, Melisandre of Asshai, at Dragonstone.

Recent Events[ ]

  

Edric Storm, by Roman Papusev ©

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Stannis Baratheon, Lord of Dragonstone, announces his claim for the Iron Throne during the War of the Five Kings, with Selyse Florent as his queen.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Melisandre of Asshai advises Stannis to sacrifice Edric Storm, his bastard nephew, so that his king's blood can be used to awaken a stone dragon at Dragonstone. To save Edric, Lord Davos Seaworth has the boy smuggled across the narrow sea.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Selyse has often asked Stannis that they marry again, this time in a ceremony under the Lord of Light. Jon Snow believes that Melisandre is Stannis's true queen, and that the queen's men might attend Selyse but actually serve the red priestess.
