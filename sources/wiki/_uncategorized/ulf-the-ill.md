## Summary

Ulf is among the ironborn who raid the Stony Shore, and is part of Theon Greyjoy's force that takes Winterfell. Ulf remains with Theon after the northmen come to retake the castle. Ulf is killed by Bolton men during the sack of Winterfell, shot in the belly with a crossbow bolt while running for the Great Hall.
