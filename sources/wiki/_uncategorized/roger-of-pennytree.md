Roger of Pennytree was the squire and nephew of Ser Arlan of Pennytree.

History[ ]

Roger died at the Battle of the Redgrass Field after Lord Gormon Peake smashed his head with a mace. Ser Duncan the Tall served as Arlan's squire after the war until the old knight's death. As Duncan played a role in events that led to Gormon's execution, he felt he had avenged Roger's death.
