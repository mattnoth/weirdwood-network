Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

When Samwell Tarly is appointed to aid Maester Aemon, Chett is reassigned to the kennels to help Bass.

Ghost brings a severed hand to Jon Snow when he swears his vows in a weirwood grove in the haunted forest. Bass tries to have his pack of hounds track the scent of the hand, but they fight to get away. Ghost instead leads the search party, which finds the corpses of Othor and Jafer Flowers. The dogs continue to be frightened of the bodies.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Chett handles the hounds in the great ranging.
