History[ ]

The Lord of the Tor died shortly before King Aegon I Targaryen captured the Tor during the First Dornish War.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lady Jordayne  delays Ser Balon Swann for Prince Doran Martell.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lady Jordayne is present at the feast when Balon arrives in Sunspear to deliver the Mountain's skull. She drinks when Ricasso raises a toast to King Tommen I Baratheon.

Known Lords of the Tor[ ]

Lord Jordayne during the First Dornish War.

Lord Trebor Jordayne, the current Lord of the Tor.
