Culture[ ]

The arms of House Caron of Nightsong depict a field of black nightingales on yellow.

The hour of the nightingale is one of the named hours.

The Nightingale is a courtesan in Braavos.

History[ ]

  

Lord Pearse Caron by Amok ©

Nightingales were part of the immense pie at the wedding at Whitewalls.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Pate hears the sweet songs of a nightingale in an apple tree by the Quill and Tankard in Oldtown.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Tyrion Lannister hears nightingales as the Shy Maid travels down the Rhoyne.
