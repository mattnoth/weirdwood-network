## Full Text

Skahazadhan

Slaver's Bay and the location of the Skahazadhan

The Skahazadhan is a major river of Essos. It begins in the southeastern Dothraki sea and flows southwest through Meereen into Slaver's Bay. South of the river are Lhazar and the red waste. Cities on its tributaries include Vaes Mejhah, Krazaaj Has, and Kosrak. A tributary also flows through the Khyzai Pass near Meereen.

The Skahazadhan is a slow river with brown water. The Meereenese use it for waste and instead drink from deep wells. Pike are found in the river. Warden of the River is a title granted to officials monitoring the Skahazadhan.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

To resolve the siege of Meereen, Daenerys Targaryen sends a group into the the city by infiltrating the sewers.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

During the second siege of Meereen, galleys from Qarth and New Ghis and a carrack from Tolos seal off the Skahazadhan, and two iron legions are to land north of the river to block Meereenese access to the Dothraki sea.

The Wise Masters of Yunkai set up slave pens and an auction block on the southern shore of the river, where it meets Slaver's Bay.

After Daenerys, now Queen of Meereen, disappears from Daznak's Pit, her bloodriders are sent in search of her north of the Skahazadhan. King Hizdahr zo Loraq styles himself Master of the Skahazadhan. Skahaz mo Kandaq declines the ancient office of Warden of the River.
