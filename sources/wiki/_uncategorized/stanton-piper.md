For the noble house from the crownlands, see House Staunton.

Stanton Piper was the Lord of Pinkmaiden and the head of House Piper during the civil war known as the Dance of the Dragons.

History[ ]

Lord Stanton succeeded Lord Petyr Piper after he died in battle against westermen at Acorn Hall in 130 AC.

During the Second Battle of Tumbleton, Lord Stanton scraped together fresh levies of greybeards and green boys, though had suffered grievous losses in the autumn battles. Seated atop a low rise, Lord Stanton and Lord Lyonel Deddings were burned to death by the riderless dragon Vermithor along with their squires, servants, and sworn shields.
