## Summary

Krazaaj Has is a ruined city in Essos between the southeastern Dothraki sea and Lhazar. It is located on a tributary of the eastern Skahazadhan, west of Vaes Mejhah and east of Meereen. It was originally called Ghardaq, but was ruined by the Dothraki after the Doom of Valyria. It is now known by its Dothraki language name, Krazaaj Has, meaning "Sharp Mountains" after its Ghiscari pyramids.
