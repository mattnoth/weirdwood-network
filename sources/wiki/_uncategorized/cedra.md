## Summary

Cedra is among the servants who are allowed access to Princess Arianne Martell during her imprisonment at Sunspear. She has a crush on Garin, which Arianne uses to get Cedra to smuggle a letter to Lord Franklyn Fowler out of the castle. She is caught by the spies of Doran Martell, Prince of Dorne, and is sent to work at the Water Gardens instead.
