Appearance[ ]

Bluetooth dresses in a bearskin vest and wears a helmet with raven wings.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Theon and Asha Greyjoy meet Bluetooth in Lordsport while riding towards Pyke. He tells them that his wife is about to give birth to twins. Theon thinks about offering Bluetooth a job on his new longship, Sea Bitch, but he reconsiders when Asha tells him that Bluetooth has his own longship and that it would only insult him.
