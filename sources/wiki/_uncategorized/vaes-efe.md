## Summary

Vaes Efe is a ruined Ghiscari city in Essos in the southeastern Dothraki sea. It is located south of Vaes Dothrak and north of the Skahazadhan. Southwest of Vaes Efe are Vaes Mejhah and Krazaaj Has. Its Dothraki language name means City of Shackles.
