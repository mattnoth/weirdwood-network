## Full Text

The scarlet emperors were a dynasty that ruled the Golden Empire of Yi Ti. They founded the city of Si Qo, which served as the capital of the Empire during their reign. The family name of the scarlet emperors is Lo.

The dynasty fell when Emperor Lo Bu was killed in battle by Zhea, a jhatar of the Jogos Nhai.

Known scarlet emperors[ ]

Emperor Lo Tho, called Lo Longspoon and Lo the Terrible, the twenty-second scarlet emperor

Emperor Lo Doq, called Lo Lackwit, the thirty-fourth scarlet emperor

Emperor Lo Han, the forty-second scarlet emperor

Emperor Lo Bu, the forty-third scarlet emperor and last of the dynasty; known as the Boy Too Bold By Half among the Jogos Nhai
