Lemonwood

Dorne and the location of Lemonwood

Lemonwood is the seat of House Dalt in Dorne. It lies just south of the mouth of the Greenblood, near Planky Town.

History[ ]

Prior to the coming of the Andals, some High Kings of Dorne resided at a wooden motte-and-bailey castle near Lemonwood, but their river kingdom eventually splintered.

During the First Dornish War, Lemonwood was captured by Rhaenys Targaryen. Her sister, Visenya Targaryen, unleashed the dragonflame of Vhagar on Lemonwood after Dornishmen attacked Cape Wrath.
