## Full Text

Ser William Tortoll was a knight during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen.

History[ ]

William participated in the tourney at Ashford Meadow in 209 AC.

Behind the scenes[ ]

The character is named after Bill Tortolini, the letterer for the graphic novel adaptations of The Hedge Knight, The Sworn Sword, and The Mystery Knight.
