Character[ ]

According to his grandfather, Lord Walder, Aemon never has anything sensible to say.

Like his father, Aemon also has a personal blazon.

Family[ ]

Aemon's mother is a member of House Charlton. He has a daughter named Walda. It is currently unknown if Aemon is married and thus Walda is a legitimate child, or if she is a bastard.
