Garth is a given name in Westeros, most common in the Reach. Characters named Garth include:

Garth, a gaoler, headsman and torturer in the Wolf's Den

Garth of Greenaway, a member of the Night's Watch

Garth of Oldtown,  a member of the Night's Watch

Garth the Harelip, a captain of the City Watch of King's Landing during the Dance of the Dragons

Garth the Gardener, mythical founder of House Gardener and first King of the Reach

Garth III Gardener, called Garth the Great, a King of the Reach

Garth V Gardener, called Hammer of the Dornish, a King of the Reach

Garth VI Gardener, called Garth the Morningstar, a King of the Reach

Garth VII Gardener, called Garth Goldenhand and The Goldenhand, a King of the Reach

Garth IX Gardener, a King of the Reach

Garth X Gardener, called Garth Greybeard, a King of the Reach

Garth XI Gardener, called Garth the Painter, a King of the Reach

Garth XII Gardener, a King of the Reach

Garth Greenfield, a knight of House Greenfield

Garth Greenhand, the mythical ancestor of numerous houses of the Reach

Garth Greyfeather,  a member of the Night's Watch

Garth Hightower, a knight of House Hightower

Garth Oldflowers, a knight of House Oldflowers

Garth Tyrell, Lord Seneschal of Highgarden
