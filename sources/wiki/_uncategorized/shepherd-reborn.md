## Full Text

The Shepherd reborn was one of the men claiming to be the reincarnation of the Shepherd during the Hour of the Wolf.

Appearance[ ]

Despite claiming to be the reincarnation of the Shepherd, he did not ressemble him and still had his two arms unlike the late one-armed prophet.

History[ ]

After the arrest and execution of the prophet known as the Shepherd on the last day of 130 AC, several individuals in King's Landing claimed to be the reincarnation of the late prophet. One such preached against the northmen in Lord Cregan Stark's army, as they believed in the old gods rather than following the Faith of the Seven. Hundreds flocked to hear him but the gold cloaks of the City Watch captured him and removed his tongue.
