History[ ]

Mylenda married Petyr Frey, called "Petyr Pimple", when he was ten and she was three times his age.

Rumors at the Twins claim that Mylenda is sleeping with Petyr's older brother, Black Walder.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Petyr, Mylenda's husband, wanders off with a camp follower, and is captured by the brotherhood without banners. Merrett Frey believes he had gone whoring because Mylenda was sleeping with his brother, Black Walder. Mylenda becomes a widow when Petyr is hanged by the brotherhood.

Family[ ]

It is unknown how Mylenda is related to the other members of House Caron.
