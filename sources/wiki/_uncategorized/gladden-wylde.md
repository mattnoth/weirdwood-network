Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Ser Gladden is chosen by Lord Eddard Stark in King's Landing to be part of a force sent into the riverlands to apprehend Ser Gregor Clegane and bring him to justice. In the battle at the Mummer's Ford, however, that force is ambushed and Gladden is amongst those slain.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

The brotherhood without banners include Gladden among the victims of the Lannisters.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

The name of the character may be a reference to the Gladden, a river mentioned in The Lord of the Rings by J.R.R. Tolkien, who is a known influence to George R. R. Martin. Isildur is slain during an ambush by the river, a similar fate to Gladden Wylde.
