Harrold Osgrey was a knight of House Osgrey during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen. He was the second son of Ser Eustace Osgrey.

History[ ]

Harrold joined his father in supporting Daemon Blackfyre in his rebellion in 196 AC and was slain at the Battle of the Redgrass Field. He is buried at Standfast.
