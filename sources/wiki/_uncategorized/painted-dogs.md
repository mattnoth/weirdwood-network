## Full Text

The Painted Dogs, by Ignacio Lazcano © Fantasy Flight Games

The Painted Dogs are one of the Vale mountain clans of the Mountains of the Moon, according to Archmaester Arnel's Mountain and Vale.

History[ ]

Roland I Arryn, the fourth King of Mountain and Vale, was slain by Painted Dogs during winter.

Some maesters believe that the Burned Men began as an offshoot clan of the Painted Dogs.
