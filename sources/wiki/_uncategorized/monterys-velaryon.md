Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

After the death of his father at the Battle of the Blackwater, Monterys becomes the head of House Velaryon at the age of six.

When King Stannis Baratheon makes Davos Seaworth his new Hand, Davos protests, saying there is surely someone better, and Stannis points out that the new Lord Velaryon is a boy of six.
