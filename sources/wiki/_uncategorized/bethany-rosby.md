Appearance[ ]

According to Maester Brenett, Bethany was small, and had narrow hips, much like her daughter Roslin Frey.

History[ ]

Although Rosbys are not known for their robustness, Bethany was healthy enough to have many children, one each year she was married to Walder. Of these, five survived to adulthood: Ser Perwyn, Ser Benfrey, Maester Willamen, Olyvar and Roslin Frey.

Quotes[ ]

Brenett: Your lord brother need have no fear, Lady Catelyn. She's small, I'll grant you, and narrow in the hips, but her mother was the same, and Lady Bethany gave Lord Walder a child every year.

Catelyn: How many lived past infancy?

Brenett: Five. Ser Perwyn. Ser Benfrey. Maester Willamen, who took his vows last year and now serves Lord Hunter in the Vale. Olyvar, who squired for your son. And Lady Roslin, the youngest. Four boys to one girl. Lord Edmure will have more
sons than he knows what to do with.

—Maester Brenett and Lady Catelyn Stark
