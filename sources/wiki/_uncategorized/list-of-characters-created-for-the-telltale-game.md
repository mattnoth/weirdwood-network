Season 1[ ]

Episode 1: Iron From Ice[ ]

 Gared Tuttle, played by Daniel Kendrick. He is the squire of Gregor Forrester.

Norren, played by Geoffrey Leesley. He is a Forrester man-at-arms.

Bowen, played by Matt Mercer. He is Norren's squire.

Thermund, played by JB Blanc. He is the Lord's Sentinel to Gregor Forrester.

Errold, a Forrester man-at-arms.

 Lord Gregor Forrester, played by Robin Atkin-Downes. He is the Lord of Ironrath and head of House Forrester.

 Talia Forrester, played by Molly Stone. She is the daughter of Gregor Forrester.

 Ryon Forrester, played by Louis Suc. He is the son of Gregor Forrester.

 Mira Forrester, played by Martha Mackintosh. She is the daughter of Gregor Forrester.

 Rodrik Forrester, played by Russ Bain. He is the son of Gregor Forrester.

 Lady Elissa Forrester, née Branfield, played by Lara Pulver. She is the wife of Gregor Forrester.

 Ethan Forrester, played by Christopher Nelson. He is the son of Gregor Forrester.

Malcolm Branfield, played by JB Blanc. He is the brother of Elissa Forrester.

Duncan Tuttle, played by Robin Atkin-Downes. He is the uncle of Gared Tuttle and the castellan of Ironrath.

Ser Royland Degore, played by Brian George. He is the master-at-arms at Ironrath.

Erik, played by Yuri Lowenthal. He is a thief at Ironrath.

 Maester Ortengryn, played by David Franklin. He is the maester at Ironrath.

 Lord Ludd Whitehill, played by Geoffrey Leesley. He is the Lord of Highpoint and head of House Whitehill.

 Gryff Whitehill, played by Sacha Dhawan. He is the son of Ludd Whitehill.

Britt Warrick, played by Alistair James. He is a Whitehill man-at-arms.

Sera Durwell, played by Natasha Loring. She is a bastard from the Reach, and her real name is Sera Flowers.

Tom, played by Yuri Lowenthal. He is a servant in the Red Keep.

 Lord Gerhard Forrester, known as Gerhard the Tall. He was the founder of House Forrester. (mentioned only)

 Lord Cedric Forrester, the builder of Ironrath. (mentioned only)

 Lord Thorren Forrester, known as Thorren the Bold. He was the father of Gregor Forrester. (mentioned only)

Episode 2: The Lost Lords[ ]

 Asher Forrester, played by Alex Jordan. He is the exiled son of Gregor Forrester.

 Beskha the Basilisk, played by Toks Olagundoye. She is a sellsword in Meereen.

Tazal, played by Brian George. He is a commander of the Lost Legion.

 Gwyn Whitehill, played by Laura Bailey. She is the daughter of Ludd Whitehill.

Elaena Glenmore, played by Amy Pemberton. She is the sister of Arthur Glenmore.

Arthur Glenmore, played by Matt Littler. He is the brother of Elaena Glenmore.

Lucan, played by Fabio Tassone and later Matt Wolf. He is a Lannister man-at-arms.

Damien, played by Owen Thomas. He is a Lannister man-at-arms.

Lord Rickard Morgryn, played by Oliver Vaquer and later Nick Afka Thomas. He is a merchant lord in King's Landing.

Lord Andros, played by Robin Atkin Downes. He is a merchant lord in King's Landing.

 Denner Frostfinger, played by Jeremy Crutchley. He is an officer of the Night's Watch.

 Finn, played by Yuri Lowenthal. He is a ranger of the Night's Watch.

 Owyn Cotter, played by Joseph Balderamma. He is a builder of the Night's Watch.

Lord Glenmore, the head of House Glenmore. (mentioned only)

Lady Glenmore, Lord Glenmore's wife. (mentioned only)

Lord Branfield, the father of Elissa Forrester. (mentioned only)

Lady Talia Branfield, Lord Branfield's wife. (mentioned only)

 Casper, played by JB Blanc. He is a recruit of the Night's Watch.

 Bezzaq, one of the Great Masters of Meereen.

Episode 3: The Sword in the Darkness[ ]

Harys, played by Ron Bottitta. He is a Whitehill man-at-arms.

Lord Garibald Tarwick, played by Rob Rackstraw. He is the head of House Tarwick.

Croft, played by Adam Leadbeater. He is the leader of the Hellhounds.

Old Rob, a farmer in the wolfswood. (mentioned only)

Namond, an inhabitant of Old Rob's farm. (mentioned only)

Meghan, an inhabitant of Old Rob's farm. (mentioned only)

Fegg, played by Matthew Mercer. He is a wagon driver in the wolfswood.

 Hugh, a ranger of the Night's Watch.

 Lobor, a steward of the Night's Watch.

 Errold Holt, a recruit of the Night's Watch.

  Corlys Wells, a recruit of the Night's Watch.

 Wendel, a member of the Night's Watch.

 Duff, a member of the Night's Watch.

 Jodan, a sellsword of the Second Sons.

 Therrond, a sellsword of the Second Sons.

 Kieran, a sellsword of the Second Sons.

 Penn, a sellsword of the Second Sons.

Rhogar, a member of the Lost Legion.

Episode 4: Sons of Winter[ ]

 Lyman Lannister, played by Carl Prekopp. He is a third cousin to Cersei, Jaime, and Tyrion Lannister.

Sylvi, played by Clare Louise Connolly. She is a wildling and the sister of Owyn Cotter.

Nancy, a wildling spearwife.

Barbara, a wildling spearwife.

Jane, a wildling spearwife.

Rose, a wildling spearwife.

  Ebbert Whitehill, a novice or acolyte at the Citadel. (mentioned only)

 Torrhen Whitehill, Ludd Whitehill's heir. (mentioned only)

 Lady Whitehill, Ludd Whitehill's wife. (mentioned only)

 Karl Whitehill, a son of Ludd Whitehill who died of greyscale. (mentioned only)

 Dezhor zo Raza, one of the Great Masters of Meereen.

Episode 5: A Nest of Vipers[ ]

 Bloodsong, played by Paul Nakauchi. He is a pit fighter in Meereen.

 Amaya, played by Jaye Jacobs. She is a pit fighter in Meereen.

 The Beast, played by Samoa Joe. He is a pit fighter in Meereen.

Episode 6: The Ice Dragon[ ]

Josera Snow, played by Joe Tandberg. He is a skinchanger and bastard son of Gregor Forrester.

Elsera Snow, played by Nina Yndis. She is a bloodmage and bastard daughter of Gregor Forrester.

Shadow, a snow bear bonded with Josera Snow.
