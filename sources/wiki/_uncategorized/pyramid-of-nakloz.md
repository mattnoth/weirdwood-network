Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Kraznys mo Nakloz agrees to trade Unsullied for one of the dragons of Daenerys Targaryen, but he is killed by Drogon during the fall of Astapor.

A Dace with Dragons[ ]

During the siege of Astapor by Yunkai, the pyramid of Nakloz is sacked and burned by those blaming Astapor's troubles on the late Kraznys.
