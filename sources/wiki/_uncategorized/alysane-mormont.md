Appearance and Character[ ]

See also: Images of Alysane Mormont

Alysane is short, chunky, and muscular, with big thighs, breasts, and hands. Her hands are heavily callused. She has crooked teeth, but an ingratiating smile. Her snores are reportedly worse than any man's.

Aly is gruff and taciturn.

Alysane wears ringmail under her furs, boiled leather under that, and sheepskin under the leather, turned inside out for warmth, even while sleeping. The layers make her look almost as wide as she is tall, and also ferocious.

History[ ]

Alysane is in her mid-twenties and has two children, a nine-year-old daughter and a two-year-old son. She is unwed, and claims her children were fathered by a bear. Though Alysanne started having children at an age she feels was too young, she also feels that it was better than to wait too long.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

After the death of her elder sister, Dacey, at the Red Wedding, Alysane is the new heir of House Mormont.  

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

  

Alysane and Asha Greyjoy, by Shebsart ©

Alysane and men-at-arms of House Mormont launch a surprise attack on longships off the strand of Deepwood Motte, taking them unawares by hiding fighters inside a gaggle of fishing sloops. She burns and captures all the longships, securing several ironborn captains, knights, warriors, and other nobility for ransom.

After Stannis Baratheon is victorious in the fight by Deepwood Motte and restores it to House Glover, Alysane joins his army for the march on Winterfell. Aly insists that Lord Roose Bolton cannot be allowed to hold Winterfell, and that "Arya Stark" must be rescued from the Boltons. She also wants Theon Greyjoy to be killed for his prior capture of Winterfell. Despite their alliance, Alysane frustrates Stannis. 

She is assigned as one of the guards of the captive Asha Greyjoy, Theon's sister, and watches her day and night. Alysane and Asha share sleeping furs, and Asha grows used to her snores while on the march. On the third day, while riding in the baggage wayn, Asha has a nearly pleasant conversation with Alysane about her family, but the She-Bear's smile fades when she reminds Asha that the Mormont women are warriors because they are frequently attacked by ironborn. 

When Stannis and his army are camped at the crofters' village west of Winterfell, Alysane and Asha go ice fishing on the frozen lakes, but after several days, they have little luck finding any fish. Alysane protects Asha when Clayton Suggs wishes to burn her as a sacrifice to R'hllor.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Stannis commands Ser Justin Massey to bring "Arya Stark" (actually Jeyne Poole) to Castle Black, with Alysane as her female companion.
