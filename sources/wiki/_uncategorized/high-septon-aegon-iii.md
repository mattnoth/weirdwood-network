## Full Text

The High Septon was High Septon of the Faith of the Seven during the reign of King Aegon III Targaryen.

History[ ]

The High Septon reversed the ruling of his predecessor, who forbade Lord Lyonel Hightower to marry his father's widow, Samantha Tarly.

It is unknown if the six children of Lyonel and Samantha born before their marriage were legitimized afterward.
