## Full Text

Lo Tho, called Lo Longspoon and Lo the Terrible, was a God-Emperor of the Golden Empire of Yi Ti and the twenty-second ruler of his dynasty.

History[ ]

Tho was a reputed sorcerer and cannibal, said to have supped upon the living brains of his enemies with a long, pearl-handled spoon, after the tops of their skulls had been removed, earning his monikers.
