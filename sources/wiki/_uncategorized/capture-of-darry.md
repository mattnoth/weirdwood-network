Battle[ ]

Many of the riverlords return to their lands after the battle under the walls of Riverrun. The Lannister army led by Lord Tywin Lannister and Ser Kevan Lannister marches on each stronghold in turn. Darry is captured by Lannister soldiers at some point.

Aftermath[ ]

Following the Battle of the Camps at Riverrun, Darry troops recapture their castle.

Notes[ ]

↑ Houses Brax, Clegane, Crakehall, Lefford, Lydden, Marbrand, Payne, Serrett, and Swyft are not directly mentioned at Darry. However, as men from these houses are part of Tywin Lannister's host in the riverlands, they may be present at Darry.
