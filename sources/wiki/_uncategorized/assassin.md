## Full Text

An assassin attacks a sleeping man. Art by Ian Kirkpatrick © Fantasy Flight Games

An assassin is one who commits a targeted murder or assassination, mostly for political reasons. They may act alone or in organised groups, or may even be created by magic. 

Some believe the infighting within the great houses of the Valyrian Freehold led to the assassinations of too many mages who controlled the Fourteen Flames, causing the Doom of Valyria.

Known Assassins[ ]

  

The assassins known as Blood and Cheese. Illustrated by Will Owen ©

Organizations[ ]

The Faceless Men, a religious society of assassins based in Braavos.

The Sorrowful Men, an ancient guild of assassins from Qarth.

Historical[ ]

Dornish cutthroats, who twice tried to kill King Aegon I Targaryen.

Two Poor Fellows, who attempted to kill King Aenys I Targaryen.

Members of the order of holy sisters at Jonquil's Pool, who tried to kill Queen Alysanne Targaryen.

Blood and Cheese, who were hired by Daemon Targaryen to kill the young son of Aegon II, Prince Jaehaerys.

Lord Larys Strong, Lord Corlys Velaryon, Ser Perkin the Flea, Grand Maester Orwyle, and others, accused of conspiring to poison King Aegon II.

Tessario the Tiger, a sellsword in the service of Unwin Peake, who may have murdered young Queen Jaehaera Targaryen.

The brothers Toyne, who attempted to kill King Aegon IV Targaryen, but were themselves slain by the Kingsguard Prince Aemon the Dragonknight, though they managed to kill him in the process.

Current Events[ ]

A catspaw, hired to murder Bran Stark while he lay in a coma.

A wineseller, who tried to kill Daenerys Targaryen and her unborn son with poisoned wine.

A shadow assassin, a form of magic used by the shadowbinder Melisandre of Asshai.
