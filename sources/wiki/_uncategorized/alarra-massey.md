Appearance[ ]

Alarra was esteemed as a great beauty.

History[ ]

Alarra was married to Lord Aethan Velaryon of Driftmark. During the marriage, she gave birth to several sons, including Daemon, and at least one daughter, Alyssa, in 7 AC.

Family[ ]

Behind the scenes[ ]

In The World of Ice and Fire, Alyssa Velaryon is mentioned to be Targaryen on her mother's side. In Fire & Blood, her mother was changed to be Alarra Massey.
