Character[ ]

According to his son, Ser Manfred, Lord Dondarrion loved to tell the origin story of their House.

History[ ]

In 206 AC, Lord Dondarrion and Lord Pearse Caron raised a host and defeated the Vulture King, burning him out of the Red Mountains. Ser Arlan of Pennytree and his squire, Duncan, were in his service during this campaign.

Family[ ]

Lord Dondarrion had at least one son, Ser Manfred. His relation with Jena Dondarrion, wife of Prince Baelor Targaryen, is unknown.
