Appearance[ ]

Notch is a stooped, thin man with grey hair.

History[ ]

Notch was born near the lands of House Goodbrook.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

During the battle at the burning septry, Notch fires arrows as the Brave Companions.

Notch tells Arya Stark and Gendry how House Goodbrook suffered under Lord Hoster Tully for supporting the Targaryens during Robert's Rebellion.

After the Red Wedding, Notch is among the outlaws who help kill Merrett Frey at Oldstones.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Notch is listed as a member of Lady Stoneheart's band of outlaws.
