History[ ]

During the Targaryen reign of the Seven Kingdoms, Lord Prentys Tully, Lord Albin Massey, Lord Rodrik Arryn, Prince Aemon Targaryen, and Lord Thaddeus Rowan all served as justiciar as well as master of laws.
