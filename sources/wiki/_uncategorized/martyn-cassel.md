Character[ ]

Martyn was a proud man.

History[ ]

Martyn was one of Lord Eddard Stark's companions, fighting alongside him during Robert's Rebellion. He was slain at the tower of joy in the Red Mountains of Dorne during a fight with three of King Aerys II Targaryen's Kingsguard. Afterwards, Eddard had the tower pulled down, and used its stones for cairns for the fallen.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

During a fever dream, Lord Eddard Stark recalls Martyn and his other former companions at the tower of joy.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Rodrik Cassel mentions his brother Martyn when explaining to Bran Stark that nothing in the future is certain.
