## Full Text

Randor the Exile was a knight during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen.

History[ ]

Randor participated in the tourney at Ashford Meadow in 209 AC.

Behind the scenes[ ]

The character is named after Elio "Ran" García, co-founder of Westeros.org, co-author of The World of Ice & Fire, and thematic consultant for the The Hedge Knight Graphic Novel.
