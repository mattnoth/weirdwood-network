Appearance[ ]

She is a gawky girl, all skin and bones and elbows, and always cold. She does not like to wake early.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

She and her older sister, Brea, share their bed with Cat of the Canals when Cat is staying at Brusco's home. When her father's boat leaves the dock she catches the rope that Cat throws to her. She confirms Brea's statement that Cat growls in her sleep. She sells shellfish from a wheelbarrow in the allies around the Moon Pool or on the Isle of the Gods by the temples.
