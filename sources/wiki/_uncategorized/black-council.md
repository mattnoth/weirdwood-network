## Full Text

The black council was a version of the small council which advised Rhaenyra Targaryen during the Dance of the Dragons. Formed at the start of the civil war, the black council's main composition was:

 Queen Rhaenyra Targaryen

 Prince Daemon Targaryen

 Lord Corlys Velaryon

 Princess Rhaenys Targaryen

 Prince Jacaerys Velaryon

 Prince Lucerys Velaryon

 Prince Joffrey Velaryon

 Ser Erryk Cargyll

 Ser Lorent Marbrand

There were also a dozen lesser lords, bannermen and vassals of Dragonstone, including:

 Lord Bartimos Celtigar of Claw Isle

 Lord Staunton of Rook's Rest

 Lord Gormon Massey of Stonedance

 Lord Bar Emmon of Sharp Point

 Lord Gunthor Darklyn of Duskendale

After the Fall of King's Landing, Rhaenyra took the Iron Throne and named several members to her small council.

 Lord Corlys Velaryon was named the Hand of the Queen.

 Maester Gerardys was named her Grand Maester.

 Lord Bartimos Celtigar was named master of coin.

 Ser Luthor Largent, the Commander of the City Watch.

 Ser Lorent Marbrand, the Lord Commander of the Queensguard.

 Ser Medrick Manderly, the heir to White Harbor, an advisor.

 Ser Torrhen Manderly, younger brother to Medrick, an advisor.

 Mysaria, an advisor and unofficially the mistress of whisperers.
