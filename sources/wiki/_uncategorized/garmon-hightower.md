Character[ ]

During the First Dornish War, Archmaester Gyldayn describes Garmon Hightower as a young boy.

History[ ]

In 10 AC during the First Dornish War, Lord Manfred Hightower dispatched his eldest son, Ser Addam, to lead a host against Lord Fowler at Nightsong. With most of the Hightower strength away, Ser Joffrey Dayne led a second army against Oldtown. The young Garmon led a sortie against the Dornishmen, but he was slain in battle by Ser Joffrey.

Family[ ]

Garmon was the younger son of Lord Manfred Hightower. He had several brothers and sisters, including Ser Addam, who succeeded their father as the Lord of the Hightower.
