Fishermonger's Square or Fishmonger's Square is a market square in Volantis where fishmongers sell their catches.

Layout[ ]

Fishermonger's Square is found near the western end of the Long Bridge, with the Merchant's House overlooking the market. The nearby waterside of the Rhoyne has warehouses, brothels, and taverns.

A cracked and headless statue of a dead triarch of Volantis is found in the center of the square.

There is a tangle of wayns, palanquins, and foot traffic in Fishermonger's Square, which can also be busy at night with sailors, whores, and merchants.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Prince Quentyn Martell, Ser Gerris Drinkwater, and Ser Archibald Yronwood, travel on a hathay through Fishermonger's Square while journeying to the Merchant's House. They see a pair of dwarfs putting on a show in the square, with one on a dog and the other on a pig.

Ser Jorah Mormont brings the captive Tyrion Lannister through the square to the Merchant's House. While chained to a wall in the inn, Tyrion looks out a window at the crowded square. A crowd has gathered about a pair of jugglers throwing flaming torches at each other, and he notices a cyvasse game and hears a woman winging a sad song.
