Dick is a common male name and nickname in Westeros. It may refer to:

Beardless Dick, a member of the brotherhood without banners

Fletcher Dick, an outlaw in the Kingswood Brotherhood

Yellow Dick, one of the Bastard's Boys

Dick Bean, a man-at-arms in service of House Targaryen in 42 AC

Dick Cole, a sellsword in the Golden Company

Dick Crabb, called "Nimble Dick" a former soldier from Cracklaw Point

Dick Follard, called "Deaf Dick Follard", a ranger of the Night's Watch

Dick Straw, a sellsword in the Windblown
