Culture[ ]

A heron is included in the arms of House Erenford.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

When Margaery Tyrell goes hawking along Blackwater Bay, her peregrine catches a heron in full flight.

Roast heron is one of the dishes served at the wedding feast of King Joffrey I Baratheon.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

A heron stuffed with figs is served to Tyrion Lannister at Illyrio's manse in Pentos. Tyrion sees egrets and herons along the Rhoyne.

The Windblown mock Paezhar zo Myraq as the "Little Pigeon" and refer to his long-legged, stilt-using warrirs as Herons.

In Meereen, the animal-headed brass masks of the Brazen Beasts include the heads of herons.

Quotes[ ]

Bill: Some say that herons are majestic.

Gerris: If your king eats frogs while standing on one leg.

Archibald: Herons are craven. One time me and Drink and Cletus were hunting, and we came on these herons wading in the shallows, feasting on tadpoles and small fish. They made a pretty sight, aye, but then a hawk passed overhead, and they all took to the wing like they'd seen a dragon. Kicked up so much wind it blew me off my horse, but Cletus nocked an arrow to his string and brought one down. Tasted like duck, but not so greasy.

—Old Bill Bone, Gerris Drinkwater, and Archibald Yronwood
