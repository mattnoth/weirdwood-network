Layout[ ]

The Book Tower is made of large blocks of stone and is octagonal in shape. It is the fattest of the ten towers of the castle. Its staircase is enclosed within the thick walls.

The fifth story of the Book Tower contains the reading room for Rodrik Harlaw, Lord of Harlaw. Rodrik sits at a table by a window with the aid of beeswax candles in ornate iron holders. He does not allow food or drink in his library.

There is a well in the yard beneath the tower.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

  

Rodrik Harlaw by Mustamirri ©

Asha Greyjoy visits her uncle, Lord Rodrik Harlaw, in his reading room when she returns to Ten Towers after the death of her father, Lord Balon Greyjoy. Instead of hosting ironborn in his hall, Rodrik reads a copy of Archmaester Marwyn's Book of Lost Books which had been brought by Hotho Harlaw.
