## Full Text

The Lord of the Three Sisters is a title given to the head of House Sunderland.

Known Lords of the Three Sisters[ ]

Marla Sunderland, who proclamed herself Queen during the Sistermen's Rebellion.

Steffon Sunderland, Marla's younger brother.

Unnamed Lord Sunderland, who supported the Blackfyre Pretenders during the first and second Blackfyre Rebellion.

Triston Sunderland, the current Lord of the Three Sisters.
