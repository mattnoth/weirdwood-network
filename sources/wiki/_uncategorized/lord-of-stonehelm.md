History[ ]

When Johanna Swann was captured by Lysene captains of the Triarchy, her niggardly uncle, the Lord of Stonehelm, refused to pay her ransom.

Lord Gawen Swann competed in the tourney at Ashford Meadow in 209 AC.

Before becoming a lord, Ser Manfred Swann had a young Barristan Selmy as his squire.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord Gulian Swann agrees to meet Ser Davos Seaworth but refuses to join the cause of Stannis Baratheon. During the War of the Five Kings, Gulian remains in his castle pleading illness.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Clifford Swann is listed as Lord of Stonehelm. It is unknown if this is a mistake or whether Clifford has succeeded Gulian over Gulian's eldest son, Ser Donnel.

Known Lords of Stonehelm[ ]
