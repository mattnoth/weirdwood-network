## Full Text

Viserys Targaryen is the name of several members of House Targaryen. These include: 

Prince Viserys Targaryen, second son of King Aenys I Targaryen and Queen Alyssa Velaryon.

King Viserys I Targaryen, fifth king of the Seven Kingdoms succeeding his grandfather, King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

King Viserys II Targaryen, tenth king of the Seven Kingdoms. Prior to being king he was the Hand of the King to his brother Aegon III and to his nephews Daeron I and Baelor I.

Prince Viserys Targaryen, styling himself as Viserys, the third of his name, called the Beggar King, second son of Aerys II, who was forced into exile after Robert's Rebellion.

Feminine equivalent[ ]

Viserra Targaryen, tenth child and sixth daughter of King Jaehaerys I and Queen Alysanne Targaryen.
