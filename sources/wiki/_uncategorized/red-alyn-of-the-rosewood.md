Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Red Alyn is among the men that Bowen Marsh leaves at Castle Black. He serves as a sentry atop the Wall during the battle beneath the Wall. After being struck in the leg by a wildling arrow, he falls from the Wall to his death.
