The three Jeynes was the nickname used by Archmaester Gyldayn to refer to Jeyne Merryweather, Jeyne Mooton and Jeyne Smallwood.

History[ ]

The three Jeynes were among the many maidens who suffered what was called the "Maiden's Day curse" prior to the Maiden's Day Ball of 133 AC. Rumors spread saying they liked to dress in squire's garb, visit brothels along the Street of Silk and kiss and fondle women as if they were boys.
