## Full Text

Stag of the Sea is a war galley of the royal fleet in service to Stannis Baratheon. It has two hundred oars. It is named after House Baratheon, whose sigil includes a stag.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Stag of the Sea is seen berthed alongside the Fury at Dragonstone by Ser Davos Seaworth. Stag of the Sea is part of Stannis's fleet commanded by Ser Imry Florent during the Battle of the Blackwater. It flanks Fury with Lord Steffon in the first line of battle. It splits one of the enemy galleys in two, but later catches fire and is boarded during the fighting.
