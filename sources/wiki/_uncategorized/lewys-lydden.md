Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lewys is part of Lord Tywin Lannister's army invading the riverlands, riding alongside Ser Kevan Lannister in the center of Tywin's army during the battle of the Green Fork.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lewys is with Lord Tywin's army when they take Harrenhal. During their time there, one of Lewys's archers is hanged on Tywin's orders for starting an argument over the Brave Companions's inability to kill Beric Dondarrion which led to a fight with some Lannister men.
Lewys is among the lords present for King Joffrey I Baratheon's first audience after the battle of the Blackwater.

Family[ ]

It is currently unknown how Jeyne Lydden, the second wife of Ser Stevron Frey, is related to Lord Lewys.
