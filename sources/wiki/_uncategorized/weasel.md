Appearance and Character[ ]

No older than two, Weasel is a young child with matted hair. Lommy Greenhands gives her the name because he thinks she looks like the animal.

Weasel often cries, and she never speaks a word after she is rescued. She is stubborn and resilient, but has a bad habit of eating mud.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

While Yoren and his band of Night's Watch recruits travel north from King's Landing, they come across a village and holdfast which have been raided during the War of the Five Kings. Yoren, Murch, and Cutjack investigate the ruins and emerge with a little girl and a one-armed woman who dies soon after. Arya Stark hopes that Shella Whent, Lady of Harrenhal, might be able to care for the child.

At an unnamed Gods Eye town, the girl eagerly eats goose given to her by Hot Pie. The settlement comes under attack by Ser Amory Lorch and his band of westermen, and Arya and Gendry help the traumatized girl through a secret tunnel to escape the fight at the holdfast.

The crying girl stops whimpering, so Lommy Greenhands starts calling her Weasel because of her appearance. The girl is unafraid of eating bugs, and she kicks Hot Pie. When the youths are discovered by the Mountain's men, Weasel hears the soldiers coming and runs off into the woods. She is not found again.

While imprisoned and forced to serve at Harrenhal by House Lannister, Arya takes the name "Weasel" for her own.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

At the House of Black and White in Braavos, the big eyes and hollow face of the waif remind Arya of Weasel.
