Geography[ ]

The Street of the Sisters is one of the four straight streets which starts at the unnamed great square in the center of King's Landing (the three others being: the Gods' Way, the King's Way and the Muddy Way). The street connects the Great Sept of Baelor atop Visenya's Hill to the Dragonpit atop the Hill of Rhaenys and was named after these two sisters. One can find the Guildhall of the Alchemists upon it. The brothel known as the House of Kisses was located at the end of the Street of the Sisters atop Visenya's Hill.

History[ ]

Development of the Street of the Sisters began in 52 AC during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen. A great central square was made in the center of King's Landing, and the Street of the Sisters was one of four long and wide streets which sprung from it.

Lady Baela Targaryen took part in drunken horse races along the Street of the Sisters. 

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

During the frenzy preceding the Hand's tourney, a drunken horse race takes place in the Street of the Sisters.

Arya Stark is in the crowd marching up the Street of Sisters to the Great Sept of Baelor to go see Lord Eddard Stark's confession and unexpected execution.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Tyrion Lannister rides along the Street of the Sisters to go see Hallyne in the Guildhall of the Alchemists.
