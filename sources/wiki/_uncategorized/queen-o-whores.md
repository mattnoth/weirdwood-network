Appearance and Character[ ]

The prostitute has large, heavy breasts with big brown nipples, ample hips, and straw-colored hair. She is crudely spoken.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

After the war council called by Ser Jaime Lannister at the siege of Riverrun, Jaime visits the captive Edmure Tully on the gallows passing Ser Ryman Frey's camp pavilion, who had sent his son Edwyn Frey to the council in his stead claiming to be indisposed, within enjoying some entertainment from the sounds of a woodharp and a singer with a woman's drunken laughter. Ryman shortly arrives at the gallows with his whore, both are drunk and the woman's breasts are exposed. Ryman has crowned her with the crown of Robb Stark, taken by House Frey after the Red Wedding. When Jaime strikes Ryman in the face with his golden hand he falls back into the queen of whores arms. While dismissing Ryman from the siege, Jaime tells him he may take his whore, but not her crown.

Ryman is hanged by outlaws while returning to the Twins. After being captured by the brotherhood without banners, Brienne of Tarth sees Lady Stoneheart holding Robb's crown. The fate of Ryman's prostitute is unknown.
