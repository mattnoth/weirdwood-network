## Full Text

Maiden's Fancy was the name of a small ship captained by Elissa Farman during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

When Lord Marq Farman of Fair Isle died in 50 AC, his eldest son Franklyn Farman became the new lord. He ordered Rhaena Targaryen, who had been a guest of his late father, to leave Faircastle. Rhaena and her companions left on the Maiden's Fancy.
