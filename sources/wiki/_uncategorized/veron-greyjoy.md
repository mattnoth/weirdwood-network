## Full Text

Veron Greyjoy was a member of House Greyjoy and the brother of Lord Dalton Greyjoy.

History[ ]

When Lord Dalton Greyjoy took Fair Isle and Faircastle during the Dance of the Dragons, the Red Kraken claimed four daughters of Lord Farman as salt wives, including Lysa. A fifth daughter, whom Dalton considered "homely", was given to Veron.

Archmaester Gyldayn does not list Veron as one of his brother's heirs after the Red Kraken's death in 133 AC, indicating that Veron had predeceased his elder brother.
