Range[ ]

The western Shivering Sea has rich fishing grounds for cod and herring,  and Ibbenese hunt for herring in the eastern reaches of the sea. 

Culture[ ]

Shoals of herring and great codfish are among the painted decorations of the Merman's Court at the New Castle of White Harbor.

History[ ]

The Sealord of Braavos served ale and herring to Elissa Farman before she departed Braavos.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Herring from White Harbor is served at the harvest feast at Winterfell.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Arya Stark, acting at Cat of the Canals, sees herring being sold at the fishmarket in Braavos.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Tyrion Lannister is served creamed herring while at Illyrio's manse in Pentos.

Lord Davos Seaworth sees a woman offering herring with chopped onions at Fishfoot Yard in White Harbor.

Pickled herring is among the stores of Castle Black.

The great cog Noble Lady, carrying salt cod, whale oil, and pickled herring, is captured by Iron Victory in the Stepstones.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Herring is served at the Gates of the Moon.

Quotes[ ]

Yesterday I ate herring with the whores, but within the year I'll be having emperor crab with courtesans.

—Dareon to Cat of the Canals
