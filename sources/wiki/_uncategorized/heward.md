Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Heward accompanies Lord Eddard Stark south to King's Landing when Eddard is made the new Hand of the King to Robert I Baratheon. Jory Cassel, Wyl and Heward escort Eddard to the brothel that one of King Robert's bastards resides in and plays forfeits with a brothel wench. The three guards are slain by Ser Jaime Lannister's men in the streets of the capital as a lesson to Eddard.
