## Full Text

Adrian Redfort was a knight from House Redfort and a member of Rhaenyra Targaryen's Queensguard.

In the television adaptation House of the Dragon, Adrian is portrayed by Barry Sloane.

History[ ]

Ser Adrian was inducted into the Queensguard after Rhaenyra seized King's Landing and commanded Lord Commander Lorent Marbrand to recruit six knights to serve alongside him.

Ser Adrian was killed in defense of Rhaenyra by the men of Ser Alfred Broome at Dragonstone. He was stabbed in the back by a spear.
