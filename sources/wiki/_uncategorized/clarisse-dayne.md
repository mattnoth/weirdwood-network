## Full Text

Clarisse Dayne was the Lady of Starfall and the head of House Dayne during the reign of King Maegor I Targaryen.

Appearance and Character[ ]

Clarisse was considered to be proud and beautiful.

History[ ]

When King Maegor I Targaryen decided to take another wife, Grand Maester Benifer suggested Clarisse as a possible match in the hopes of detaching House Dayne and their lands from Dorne. Maegor dismissed his advice, however.
