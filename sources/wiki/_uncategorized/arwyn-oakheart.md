Appearance and Character[ ]

Arwyn is tiny and delicate. She is a widow.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Although one of her sons, Ser Arys Oakheart, is a member of the Kingsguard of Joffrey I Baratheon in King's Landing, Lady Arwyn follows her liege lord, Mace Tyrell, in supporting King Renly Baratheon. Arwyn is present with the forces of House Oakheart in Renly's army that slowly progresses along the roseroad toward the capital, and she attends the melee at Bitterbridge. 

After the death of Renly at Storm's End, Arwyn does not support Stannis Baratheon. Instead, the Oakhearts follow the Tyrells in supporting Joffrey in the Battle of the Blackwater.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

The small council of King Joffrey grants Lady Oakheart land as a reward for her support in the war.
