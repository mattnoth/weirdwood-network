Appearance and Character[ ]

Dennis is a big man with a thick neck and a red face.

Like his brother Peter, Dennis is boisterous, quick to laugh, quick to anger, and quick to forgive.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Dennis and his brothers, Ser Peter and Ser Harwyn, are among the men sent by Lord Tywin Lannister to the riverlands in search of his son, after Ser Jaime Lannister has been released from Riverrun by Lady Catelyn Stark.
