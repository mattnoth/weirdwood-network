Appearance and Character[ ]

Peter is a big man with a thick neck and a red face.

Like his older brother Dennis, Peter is boisterous, quick to laugh, quick to anger, and quick to forgive.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Peter and his brothers, Ser Dennis and Ser Harwyn, are among the men sent by Lord Tywin Lannister to the riverlands in search of his son, after Ser Jaime Lannister has been released from Riverrun by Lady Catelyn Stark.
