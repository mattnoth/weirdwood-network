This article is about the King in the North before the Conquest. For the POV character, see Jon Snow.

Jon Stark was a King in the North and head of House Stark before the War of Conquest. There is a statue of Jon within the crypt of Winterfell.

History[ ]

When sea raiders landed in the east, Jon drove them out and built a castle, the Wolf's Den, at the mouth of the White Knife, so as to be able to defend the mouth of the river. His son, Rickard, followed him on the throne and annexed the Neck to the north.
