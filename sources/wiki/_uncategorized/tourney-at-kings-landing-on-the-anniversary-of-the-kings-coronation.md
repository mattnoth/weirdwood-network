History[ ]

The tourney was held at King's Landing in celebration of the first decade of Jaehaerys I Targaryen's reign. King Jaehaerys watched the jousts in the royal box with his wife, Queen Alysanne Targaryen, and their children, Daenerys, Aemon and the newborn Baelon. The young knight, Ser Ryam Redwyne, was the champion, and crowned Queen Alysanne as the queen of love and beauty.

The Joust[ ]

  Ser Ryam Redwyne defeated Ser Ronnal Baratheon.

  Ser Ryam Redwyne defeated Ser Lorence Roxton of the Kingsguard.

  Ser Ryam Redwyne defeated Ser Lucamore Strong of the Kingsguard.
