Valyrian can refer to:

Valyria, a ruined city in Essos

Valyrian peninsula, a ruined peninsula in Essos

Valyrian Freehold, a former realm ruled from Valyria

Valyrians, the ethnic group that inhabited the Valyrian peninsula and founded the Valyrian Freehold

High Valyrian, the language of Valyria

Bastard Valyrian, the language spoken in the Free Cities and in the cities from Slaver's Bay, though all have different dialects, derived from High Valyrian
