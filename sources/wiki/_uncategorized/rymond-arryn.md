## Full Text

Rymond Arryn was a knight from House Arryn during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

In 54 AC, Ser Rymond and his brother, Lord Darnold, were killed at the hands of mountain clan raiders they had unwisely pursued into the Mountains of the Moon. After their deaths, Rymond's ten-year-old son Rodrik became Lord of the Eyrie.

Family[ ]

The relation between Rymond and Darnold and Lord Hubert Arryn has not been stated in the text. Timeline wise, they likely were two of his six sons with his Royce wife, however.
