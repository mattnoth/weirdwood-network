Appearance and Character[ ]

The Nightingale is renowned for her beauty. Like every Braavosi courtesan, she has her own barge and servants to pole her to trysts.

Men can be challenged to a duel by bravos if they do not say that the Nightingale is the most beautiful woman in the world when asked.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

While performing at the House of Seven Lamps, Dareon, a deserter from the Night's Watch, tells the Nightingale he would never be able to leave her to return to the Wall.

Cat of the Canals advises Samwell Tarly to say that the Nightingale is the most beautiful woman in the world if asked by bravos, claiming that her father was killed when he said her mother was more beautiful than the courtesan.

Quotes[ ]

If they ask who is the most beautiful woman in the world, say the Nightingale or else they'll challenge you.

—Cat of the Canals to Samwell Tarly
