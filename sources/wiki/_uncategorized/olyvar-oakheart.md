History[ ]

Olyvar died at the side of his king, Daeron I Targaryen, as depicted in a tapestry at Old Oak. King Daeron was slain under a banner of peace when the Dornishmen betrayed and attacked him during the Conquest of Dorne in 161 AC. Three knights of his Kingsguard died in his defense, and Olyvar Oakheart may have been of these three men.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser Arys Oakheart thinks of Ser Olyvar and the long history of conflict and bloodshed between the Oakhearts of the Reach and their ancient Dornish foes while walking the streets of the shadow city of Sunspear.
