Appearance and Character[ ]

Ben Bushy was one of the few men in service of Renly Baratheon whose height exceeded Brienne's.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Brienne of Tarth recalls that Big Ben courted her when Renly Baratheon's army was encamped beneath Highgarden. He sent his squire to clean her mail and gifted her with a silver drinking horn. Lord Randyll Tarly later discovered that there was a wager on who could take Brienne's maidenhead, a wager started by Big Ben, Ser Hyle Hunt and Ser Edmund Ambrose. After the bet was discovered and ended, Brienne sought out and defeated Big Ben during the melee at Bitterbridge.

Hyle informs Brienne that Big Ben was killed at the Battle of the Blackwater. He does not mention which side Ben fought for.

Quote[ ]

It was only a game to pass the time. We meant no harm. Ben died, you know. Cut down on the Blackwater.

—Hyle Hunt to Brienne of Tarth
