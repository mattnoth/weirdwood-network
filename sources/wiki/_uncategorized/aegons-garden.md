Layout[ ]

Aegon's Garden is located near the arch of the Dragon's Tail. Tall pine trees grow in the garden, which also has wild roses, towering thorny hedges, and a small cranberry bog.

History[ ]

Rhaena Targaryen unsuccessfully searched in Aegon's Garden for her husband, Androw Farman, after being accused of poisoning her allies.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Upon returning to Dragonstone after the Battle of the Blackwater, Ser Davos Seaworth is escorted Aegon's Garden instead of the castle's Stone Drum. The Onion Knight finds Princess Shireen Baratheon at play in the garden with Patchface and Edric Storm. Ser Axell Florent then has Davos arrested for plotting against Melisandre.

Shireen continues to play with Edric in Aegon's Garden every day.
