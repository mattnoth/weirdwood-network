Saltcliffe among the Iron Islands

Saltcliffe is one of the islands that make up the Iron Islands. Noble families from the isle include Houses Saltcliffe and Sunderly. According to a semi-canon source, House Saltcliffe rules the island and is named after it.

Geography[ ]

Saltcliffe is located south of Great Wyk and west of Pyke. There is a castle on the northwestern coast of the isle.

Saltcliffe and Blacktyde are less notable than most of the other islands in the archipelago.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Members of Houses Saltcliffe and Sunderly travel from Saltcliffe to Pyke when Balon Greyjoy, Lord of the Iron Islands, summons his bannermen for the War of the Five Kings.
