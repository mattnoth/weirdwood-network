History[ ]

The Lord Commander of the Night's Watch tasked Ser Olyver Bracken and Ser Raymund Mallery with restoring the crumbling Rimegate and Sable Hall, respectively, but the two former Kingsguard rebelled from the Night's Watch in 50 AC with the intention to make the castles their seats as lords. Aided by Winterfell, loyal brothers of the Night's Watch recaptured Rimegate and hanged Olyver's rebels. Lord Walton Stark beheaded Olyver with Ice.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Commander Jon Snow intends to garrison the abandoned castles of Rimegate and Hoarfrost Hill, and consults with Lord Steward Bowen Marsh and First Builder Othell Yarwyck for their opinion of which wildling chieftains would be best suited to hold the castles. Both men strongly reject his suggestions, with Marsh urging they be hanged for their deeds and Yarwyck claiming it was a lethal choice between "bad" and "worse."
