## Full Text

Hunnimore was a maester who served at Dragonstone during the Dance of the Dragons. 

History[ ]

During the fall of Dragonstone, Ser Marston Waters seized the rookery to prevent Grand Maester Gerardys or Hunnimore from sending ravens. After Marston prevented Ser Alfred Broome from killing Baela Targaryen, Tom Tangletongue brought the girl to the maester for treatment.
