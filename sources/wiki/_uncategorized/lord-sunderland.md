## Full Text

Lord Sunderland was the Lord of the Three Sisters and the head of House Sunderland during the reigns of Kings Daeron II Targaryen and Aerys I Targaryen. His given name is unknown.

History[ ]

Lord Sunderland fought for Daemon I Blackfyre during the First Blackfyre Rebellion.

In 212 AC, Lord Sunderland came to Whitewalls to attend the wedding of the daughter of Lord Frey to Lord Ambrose Butterwell. Ser Glendon Flowers was imprisoned after being accused of stealing the prize of the wedding's tourney, Ambrose's treasured dragon egg. When Ser Duncan the Tall publicly declared that Glendon had been falsely accused, Daemon II Blackfyre ordered Lords Sunderland, Vyrwel, and Smallwood to fetch Glendon from the dungeons. It is unknown if Lord Sunderland suffered any punishment for the part he played in the Second Blackfyre Rebellion, which was suppressed by Lord Brynden Rivers.
