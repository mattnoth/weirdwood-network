## Summary

A Song of Ice and Fire Campaign Guide is a supplemental role-playing game book for use with Green Ronin Publishing's A Song of Ice and Fire Roleplaying, a role-playing game based on A Song of Ice and Fire, the epic fantasy series by George R. R. Martin. A Song of Ice and Fire Campaign Guide describes Martin's Westeros in lavish detail, providing full details on all the major regions and principal players of the game of thrones.
