## Full Text

Serwyn of the Mirror Shield is a legendary figure from the Age of Heroes, although singers now claim he was a knight of the Kingsguard. He is a favorite of the smallfolk. Serwyn served the ancient Kings of the Reach from House Gardener.

Legends[ ]

According to songs, Serwyn once saved Princess Daeryssa from giants. He slew the dragon Urrax by approaching the beast behind his polished shield so the dragon only saw its own reflection, then spearing Urrax through the eye. Serwyn was haunted by ghosts of all the knights he killed.

Despite the legends that have him as a member of the Kingsguard, an institution created in 10 AC during the reign of King Aegon I Targaryen, Serwyn lived in the time of the First Men. His reputation remains so great that Tyrion Lannister uses Serwyn as comparison when describing the fame of Ser Barristan Selmy among smallfolk.
