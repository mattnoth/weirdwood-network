Cultures[ ]

  

Margaery Tyrell in a garden in Highgarden, by Cristi Balanescu © Fantasy Flight Games

All sectors of society are known to keep gardens, such as the smallfolk who keep homely and functional cottage gardens, and nobles who may have more extravagant gardens. Some gardens are kept for pleasure, sustenance, or both, with a mixture of ornamental and edible plants. Attractive fruit trees, such as cherry and lemon, are also sometimes a feature of gardens. Food can be grown in a glass garden (real-world greenhouse) in winter.

Many individuals draw comfort from spending time in gardens. 

Godswoods have garden features. Many godswoods still found in the south now serve as secular gardens.

Gardens of Westeros[ ]

Riverlands[ ]

The sept at Riverrun is a seven-sided sandstone building amidst the gardens of the late Minisa Tully. The godswood at Riverrun is a garden, bright and airy, where tall redwoods spread dappled shadows across tinkling streams, birds sing from hidden nests, and the air is spicy with the scent of flowers.

There is a vegetable garden behind the sept on the Quiet Isle.

Little villages in the eastern riverlands can have flourishing gardens during times of peace.  

Vale of Arryn[ ]

The Eyrie's seven towers encircle a garden, which had been meant as a godswood, but no weirwood heart tree would take root in the stony soil. The garden, which is overlooked by Lady Lysa Arryn's apartments, instead contains grass, shrubbery, and a woman's statue, located at its center. The statue depicts Alyssa Arryn.

Crownlands[ ]

The Great Sept of Baelor in King's Landing has large gardens capable of holding hundreds surrounding it. Myrcella Baratheon has a garden at the Red Keep.

The garden in Dragonstone is called Aegon's Garden. Within, grow tall dark trees, wild roses, towering thorny hedges, and cranberries. The garden has a pleasant piney scent.

North[ ]

In Winterfell, the Glass Gardens are greenhouses heated by natural hot springs, turning them into a place of moist warmth, thereby keeping the earth from freezing. They are used to grow fruits, vegetables and flowers. Inside the glass gardens, it is always like the hottest day of summer. The rare winter rose is said to be the most beautiful of the flowers grown in the glass gardens of Winterfell. The glass gardens have a warm earthy smell.

At the Wall, Castle Black has a vegetable garden. Some free folk also keep vegetable gardens beyond the Wall.

Westerlands[ ]

Casterly Rock contains gardens.

Reach[ ]

Garth Greenhand, the mythical High King of the First Men, supposedly made the land bloom.

Gardens, arbors, pools, fountains, courtyards, and man-made waterfalls surround the keeps of Highgarden.

The Seven Shrines in Oldtown have gardens.

Dorne[ ]

The Water Gardens are a retreat for House Martell; they are Prince Doran Martell's favorite place in the world. 

Gardens of Essos and Beyond[ ]

Pentos[ ]

  

Blooming cherry trees at Illyrio's manse in Pentos, by Mariusz Gandzel © Fantasy Flight Games

The gardens behind Illyrio's manse are extensive. Six cherry trees surround a marble pool in the front. Garlic grows in his gardens, as well as a poisonous variety of mushroom found in a garden by Tyrion Lannister.

Braavos[ ]

There are no trees to be found within Braavos except in the courts and gardens of the mighty. The Gardens of Gelenei is a temple in the city; though it is not stated if there are gardens there, there is a gilded tree a hundred feet high with leaves of hammered silver.

Lys[ ]

Enslaved prostitutes serve at the pleasure gardens of Lys.

Rhoynar[ ]

In Ghoyan Drohe, the ruined city in the Velvet Hills, a few people still remain amidst the squalor, tending little gardens amongst the weeds.

Sarhoy had saltwater gardens before its destruction in the Second Spice War.

The Palace of Love in Chroyane, now called the Palace of Sorrow, once had gardens bright with flowers and fountains sparkling golden in the sun. 

Meereen[ ]

Private chambers are in the lofty apex of the Great Pyramid on the highest step. The chambers are surrounded by terrace garden with greenery and fragrant pools. The audience chamber of the Great Pyramid has its own terrace. The lower terrace and its garden includes lemon trees and night-blooming flowers.

Qarth[ ]

In Qarth, the palace of Xaro Xhoan Daxos has gardens full of fragrant lavender and mint, a marble bathing pool stocked with tiny golden fish, a scrying tower, and warlock's maze. The city also has the Garden of Gehane.

Naath[ ]

The family of Missandei had a garden in Naath.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

In Vaes Tolorro, Daenerys Targaryen puts Rakharo in charge of pulling up the plaza in an effort to turn it into a garden. Later at the House of the Undying in Qarth, Pyat Pree falsely tells Daenerys her visit is over, and to follow him into a garden. After the destruction of the House of the Undying, ghost grass is found growing in the Garden of Gehane.

North along the eastern side of the river running south from the Gods Eye, Yoren and his men come across an abandoned cottage. Tarber finds a garden in the backyard where they pull some onions and radishes.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Arya Stark, Gendry, and Hot Pie encounter the brotherhood without banners at the cottage of dead Old Pate. His cottage is burned and his garden overgrown.

Edric Storm plays monsters-and-maidens with Shireen Baratheon and Patchface at Aegon's Garden in Dragonstone.

Missandei finds Daenerys in the terrace garden of the Great Pyramid in Meereen. Dany has the girl promise to never lie to her, to never betray her. Daenerys draws great solace from her garden.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Cersei Lannister states that after the war she means to build a new palace beyond the Blackwater Rush; in a dream she sees the palace surrounded by woods and gardens.

Cersei is appalled to hear and see that hundreds of sparrows are encamped in the gardens of the Great Sept of Baelor.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Varamyr Sixskins recalls that a dozen villages beyond the Wall paid him a kind of homage; offering, among other things, fruit from their orchards and vegetables from their gardens. 

Daenerys entertains the representatives from Yunkai in the audience chamber of the Great Pyramid and its terrace.
