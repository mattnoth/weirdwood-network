History[ ]

Larra Rogare, the wife of Viserys Targaryen, was fluent in High Valyrian and the tongues of Myr, Tyrosh, Volantis, and her native Lys.

"The Seasons of My Love" is a Myrish love song popular in Westeros. It is considered sweet and sad if you can understand its words.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

A Myrish crossbowmen with a thick accent is hanged by the brotherhood without banners after the battle at the burning septry.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Queen Regent Cersei Lannister thinks that Taena of Myr speaks the Common Tongue with a sultry tone.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Tyrion Lannister knows a smattering of Myrish.

Maester Kedry is fluent in the tongues of all of the Free Cities.

Young Griff is fluent in High Valyrian, the dialects of Myr, Pentos, Tyrosh, and Lys, as well as the trade talk of sailors.
