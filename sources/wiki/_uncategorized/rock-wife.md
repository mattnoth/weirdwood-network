## Full Text

A rock wife is the senior wife in ironborn culture, traditionally a freeborn woman from the Iron Islands. A man may also have multiple salt wives, women captured during raids on foreign lands.

Traditions[ ]

While an ironman can have many salt wives, he is allowed to have only one rock wife. This true bride is expected to be a freeborn woman of the Iron Islands, and she is by her husband's side in board and bed. Although both kinds of marriages are performed by a priest of the Drowned God, the ceremony of a rock wife is more solemn than that of a salt wife. A man may remarry if his rock wife passes away.

The children by a rock wife and by a salt wife are all considered legitimate, though the trueborn sons of a rock wife come before salt sons in the order of succession. A rock wife can serve as a regent when needed.

History[ ]

Dalton Greyjoy, Lord of the Iron Islands, never took a rock wife, and a struggle for power in the Iron Islands broke out after his death.

While his first two marriages were to women from the Iron Islands, Lord Quellon Greyjoy took as his third wife a Piper from the green lands.
