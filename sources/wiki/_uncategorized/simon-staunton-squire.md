For the third husband of Lady Rohanne Webber, see Simon Staunton. For the Lord Staunton who is unnamed in the books and called Simon in the adaptation House of the Dragon, see Lord Staunton.

Simon Staunton was a squire from House Staunton during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

Simon was among the group of squires who kept company with Princess Daella Targaryen. The princess fell out with Simon after he tried to make her drink wine.
