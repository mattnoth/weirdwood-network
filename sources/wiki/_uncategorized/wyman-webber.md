Wyman Webber was the Lord of Coldmoat and the head of House Webber during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen.

History[ ]

Wyman was good friends with Ser Eustace Osgrey until the First Blackfyre Rebellion. He fought at the Battle of the Redgrass Field for House Targaryen.

Lord Wyman died in the Great Spring Sickness. He dictated, in his will, that his daughter Rohanne would have to be married by the second anniversary of his death, or else the lands and income of Coldmoat would pass to his cousin, Ser Wendell Webber.
