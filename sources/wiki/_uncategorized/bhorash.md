## Summary

Bhorash is a ruined city on the northern coast of Slaver's Bay in Essos. It lies south of the Painted Mountains and northeast of the Black Cliffs. Bhorash is connected by Valyrian roads to Mantarys and Tolos. It is unknown if Bhorash was founded by the Valyrian Freehold.
