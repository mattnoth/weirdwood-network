Paymaster is a high ranking position within the free companies of Essos. The paymaster is in charge with the accounting, drawing up contracts, collecting wages, dividing resources to ensure provisions, and payments of the company.

Paymaster is a high ranking position within the free companies of Essos. The paymaster is in charge with the accounting, drawing up contracts, collecting wages, dividing resources to ensure provisions, and payments of the company.
