Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Spare Boot is one of the men that Bowen Marsh leaves behind to defend Castle Black. He uses scorpions from atop the Wall during the battle beneath the Wall, and a wildling's arrow strikes his wooden leg. Spare Boot congratulates Jon Snow after he is chosen Lord Commander of the Night's Watch.

Quotes[ ]

The bloody buggers got my leg. The wooden one!

—Spare Boot to Jon Snow
