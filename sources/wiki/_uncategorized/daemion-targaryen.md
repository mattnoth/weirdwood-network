Daemion Targaryen was a member of House Targaryen during the Century of Blood, the third son of Lord Aerys Targaryen of Dragonstone, and the father of Aerion Targaryen.

History[ ]

Daemion became Lord of Dragonstone after his two brothers. He was succeeded by his only son, Aerion Targaryen.

Lord Daemion kept a maester on Dragonstone.
