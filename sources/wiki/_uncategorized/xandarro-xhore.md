## Full Text

Xandarro Xhore was a Qartheen pirate who lived nearly two centuries after the Doom of Valyria and one century after the  Red Death killed most people in the Basilisk Isles.

History[ ]

Xhore was the first to raise his banner on the Basilisk Isles since the Red Death, a century after the plague swept across the Basilisk Isles. He raised a grim black fort above his anchorage on Ax Isle, using the stones he found on the islands.
