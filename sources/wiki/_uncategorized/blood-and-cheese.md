Blood and Cheese are two individuals who lived during the Dance of the Dragons. Their real names are not known.

History[ ]

Blood was a butcher. He was once a serjeant in the City Watch of King's Landing and lost his gold cloak for beating a whore to death whilst in a drunken rage. Cheese was a ratcatcher familiar with the hidden tunnels of the Red Keep. Together they slew six-year-old Prince Jaehaerys, King Aegon II Targaryen's firstborn son and the heir to the Iron Throne, during the Dance of the Dragons.
