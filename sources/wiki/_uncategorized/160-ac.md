## Full Text

Year 160 After the Conquest (160 AC) is a year in the Westerosi calendar, the 160th after Aegon's crowning in Oldtown that maesters and educated people use as a reference to date the years.

In 160 AC the regent of Dorne, Lord Lyonel Tyrell, was murdered at Sandstone. The Dornish insurgence reemerged and forced King Daeron to return to Dorne, where he obtained a few victories.

Events[ ]

Marriage of Prince Baelor to Princess Daena. The marriage is not consummated.

King Daeron and Lord Alyn Velaryon return to Dorne to put down the emboldening rebels.

Deaths[ ]

Lord Lyonel Tyrell dies, murdered at Sandstone on a bed of scorpions.
