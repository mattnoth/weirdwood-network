Appearance[ ]

Steelskin is a lean and wiry youth whose cheeks, brow, chest, and arms are covered with intricate tattoos in green and black. These sorcerer signs of ancient Valyria supposedly make his skin and flesh as hard as steel.

The pit fighter uses a longaxe.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Steelskin is guarding the door to King Hizdahr zo Loraq's chambers when Ser Barristan Selmy comes to take him. Steelskin is taken into custody by the Brazen Beasts.
