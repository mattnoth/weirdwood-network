A Storm of Swords[ ]

When Robb Stark comes to the Twins for the wedding of Lord Edmure Tully to Roslin Frey, Lord Walder Frey has Robb apologize to the women of House Frey for having married Jeyne Westerling instead of a Frey. Walda sneaks in among the trueborn children and curtsies when Walder asks her name.
