## Full Text

Ser Benjamin Avery was a knight during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen.

History[ ]

Benjamin participated in the tourney at Ashford Meadow in 209 AC.

Behind the scenes[ ]

The character is named after Ben Avery, the adapter (scriptwriter) of the graphic novel adaptations of The Hedge Knight, The Sworn Sword, and The Mystery Knight.
