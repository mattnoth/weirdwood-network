## Full Text

Jessamyn Redfort was a noblewoman from House Redfort during the reign of King Aegon III Targaryen.

History[ ]

Jessamyn was a "dear companion" of Jeyne Arryn, the Lady of the Eyrie. Jeyne, who never married, died in Jessamyn's arms at the Motherhouse of Maris in 134 AC.

Quotes about Jessamyn[ ]

Forty years of age, [Jeyne Arryn] perished in the Motherhouse of Maris on its stony island in the harbor of Gulltown, wrapped in the arms of Jessamyn Redfort, her "dear companion."

—Gyldayn writing in Fire & Blood, Being a History of the Targaryen Kings of Westeros
