Appearance[ ]

See also: Images of Borroq

Borroq is hulking, black browed, with a flat nose, heavy jowls dark with stubble, and small black close-set eyes. Reflecting on an encounter with Borroq in his youth, Varamyr describes him as having looked so much like his boar, that all he lacked were tusks.

Borroq's boar is as large as a bull, covered in coarse black hair, with tusks as long as a man's arm or a sword.

History[ ]

  

Borroq by Yapattack ©

Borroq was one of those present at a meeting of skinchangers that Haggon took Varamyr to in his youth.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Borroq and his boar are amongst Tormund's group which crosses the Wall. Because his boar does not get along with the Ghost, the direwolf of Jon Snow, Borroq takes up residence in an ancient tomb near Castle Black's lichyard, where the boar roots amongst the graves. Jon, the Lord Commander of the Night's Watch, intends to have Borroq eventually accompany Soren Shieldbreaker to Stonedoor. Borroq attends Jon's speech in the Shieldhall.
