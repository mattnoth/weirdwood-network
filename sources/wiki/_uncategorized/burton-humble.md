Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Burton accompanies Victarion Greyjoy when the Iron Fleet sails to Slaver's Bay. When the fleet sails past the Stepstones, Burton knocks out four of Maester Kerwin's teeth for smiling at him. When Moqorro is brought aboard the Iron Victory, Burton suggests sacrificing the red priest to the Drowned God before he can call a curse down on them.
