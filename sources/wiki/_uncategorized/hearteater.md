Appearance[ ]

The pommel of Hearteater is a ruby cut in the shape of a heart, set between a lion's jaws. Three fullers are incised deeply in the blade.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

King Joffrey I Baratheon has Sansa Stark kiss Hearteater before the Battle of the Blackwater.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Joffrey receives Widow's Wail, a Valyrian steel sword, from his grandfather, Lord Tywin Lannister, before his marriage to Margaery Tyrell.

Quotes[ ]

I'll deal with your brother after I'm done with my traitor uncle. I'll gut him with Hearteater, you'll see.

—Joffrey I Baratheon to Sansa Stark
