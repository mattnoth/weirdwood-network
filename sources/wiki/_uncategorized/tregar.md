Appearance[ ]

Tregar owns a helm with a lion crest.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Tregar is the captain of the Lannister guards that are with Ser Jaime Lannister when Jaime confronts Lord Eddard Stark in the streets of King's Landing. Eddard severely injures Tregar in the ensuing fight, and he dies five days after the fight.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Vylarr has succeeded Tregar as captain of the red cloaks in the capital.
