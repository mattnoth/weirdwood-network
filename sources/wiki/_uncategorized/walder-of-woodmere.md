## Full Text

Ser Walder of Woodmere was a knight from Woodmere during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen.

History[ ]

Walder was listed as a participant of the tourney at Ashford Meadow of 209 AC.

Arms[ ]

The roll of arms in the first edition of the The Hedge Knight graphic novel displayed Walder's sigil as a blue plate on white, similar to the arms of House Poole. However, in later editions, his sigil was changed to a grey castle on blue beneath a red bend sinister (same as the arms of Walder Rivers), suggesting that Walder of Woodmere was a bastard of House Frey.
