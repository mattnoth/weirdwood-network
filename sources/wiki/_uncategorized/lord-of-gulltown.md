History[ ]

In 52 AC, Lord Grafton hosted King Jaehaerys I Targaryen and his sister-wife, Queen Alysanne Targaryen, during their royal progress through the Vale. During their stay at Gulltown, Queen Alysanne arranged a betrothal between Lord Grafton and one of her ladies, Lady Prudence Celtigar.

At the start of Robert's Rebellion, when Lord Jon Arryn called his banners and rebelled against King Aerys II Targaryen, Lord Grafton remained loyal to the crown and opposed his liege lord.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord Gerold Grafton is a good friend of Lord Petyr Baelish. Lord Gerold sends his youngest son, Gyles, at the Eyrie to be Lord Robert Arryn's squire.

Lord Gerold attends Lord Lyonel Corbray's wedding at Gulltown.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Lord Gerold travels to the Gates of the Moon.
