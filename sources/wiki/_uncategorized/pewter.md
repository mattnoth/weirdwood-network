## Full Text

Pewter is a gray malleable alloy mostly composed of tin.

Usage[ ]

Pewter objects, such as plates, platters, cups, and tankards, are used to contain liquids and food.

Culture[ ]

Pewter is one of the metals used by maesters for their chains. Armen the Acolyte's chain is composed of links of tin, lead, copper and pewter.
