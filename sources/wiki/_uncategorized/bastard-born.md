## Full Text

Bastard Born is an account of Lord Alyn Velaryon's life written by Rue, who may or may not have been a septa, and may or may not have been one of the Oakenfist's paramours. According to Bastard Born, Lord Alyn accepted the sexual advances made by Princess Aliandra Martell, which Hard as Oak refuted.

Archmaester Gyldayn considers this account to be colorful, but unreliable.
