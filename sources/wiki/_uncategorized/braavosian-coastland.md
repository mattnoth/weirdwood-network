Braavosian Coastland

Western Essos and the location of the Braavosian Coastland

The Braavosian Coastland, Braavosian Coastlands, or Braavosian Coastline is hilly and coastal land claimed by Braavos on the northwestern coast of Essos, opposite the Vale of Arryn along the narrow sea.

History[ ]

The Braavosian Coastlands were historically part of the realm of Andalos.

Prior to the assault on Dragonstone by Stannis Baratheon, Ser Willem Darry fled with Viserys and Daenerys Targaryen for the safety of the Braavosian coast.
