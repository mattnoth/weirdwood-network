## Summary

Ironoaks, also known as Ironoaks Castle, is the seat of House Waynwood in the Vale of Arryn. It is located east of the Eyrie and west of Old Anchor and is situated by a lake. A river runs east from the lake to the narrow sea.

History[ ]

During the coming of the Andals, King Robar II Royce defeated the Hammer of the Hills beneath the walls of Ironoaks.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Using the name Alayne Stone, Sansa Stark stops at Ironoaks while traveling with Lord Petyr Baelish and Lady Lysa Arryn from the Fingers to the Eyrie.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Harrold Hardyng is a ward of Lady Anya Waynwood at Ironoaks.
