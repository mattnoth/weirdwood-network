## Full Text

Raylon Rivers was a knight from House Bracken during the Dance of the Dragons. He was the bastard half-brother of Ser Amos Bracken and the son of Lord Humfrey Bracken of Stone Hedge.

In the television adaptation House of the Dragon, Raylon Rivers is not present. However, a son of Lord Amos Bracken is named Ser Raylon Bracken, who is portrayed by Aedan Day. 

History[ ]

House Bracken declared for the greens during the Dance of the Dragons. Ser Amos Bracken was slain during the Battle of the Burning Mill, after which his half-brother Raylon led survivors back toward Stone Hedge. Prince Daemon Targaryen had taken Stone Hedge and Lord Humfrey Bracken in the meantime, however, so Raylon surrendered to spare his kin from harm.
