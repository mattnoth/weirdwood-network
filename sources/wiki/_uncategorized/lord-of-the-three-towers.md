History[ ]

Lord Owen Costayne declared for the blacks during the Dance of the Dragons. He was killed by Bold Jon Roxton at the Battle of the Honeywine in 130 AC.

An unnamed Lord Costayne fought for Daemon I Blackfyre in the First Blackfyre Rebellion, while his son and successor supported Daemon II Blackfyre in the second rebellion.
