Appearance[ ]

See also: Images of Summer (direwolf)

Summer has silver and smoke grey fur and golden eyes. Bran Stark thinks Summer is smarter than his littermates.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

  

Summer rescues Catelyn Stark and defends Bran, by Chris Dien © Fantasy Flight Games

Summer is found with his litter-mates in the snow near their dead mother by Jon Snow and Robb Stark. Bran has difficulty choosing a name for his new direwolf pup, going through a thousand names without settling on one. 

After Bran's fall from the First Keep, Summer spends every moment he can howling beneath his window. Even when chased away, Summer returns and continues howling. An intruder enters Bran's room with the intent to kill him. Summer sneaks behind the man while he is occupied with Catelyn Stark, and tears out his throat. When Bran awakes from his coma-like state, he gives the name "Summer" to his wolf.

During Tyrion Lannister's return to Winterfell, Rickon arrives with the direwolves Summer, Grey Wind, and Shaggydog in tow. When they catch the scent of Tyrion, all three direwolves move to corner him, and Tyrion is only saved when the Starks call off their wolves. 

During Bran's first ride on a horse since his fall, Robb, Grey Wind and Summer go in search of deer, leaving Bran alone. A small party of wildlings come upon Bran and promptly try to rob him. Robb, Grey Wind and Summer return and a fight ensues. Summer kills Hali, taking a knife wound in the process. All of the wildlings are killed except for Osha, who surrenders and is taken captive for further questioning.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

  

Summer, Bran, and Hodor by Amok

Bran Stark has many wolf dreams where he sees through Summer's eyes while he himself is asleep. Shaggydog and Summer are confined to the godswood of Winterfell by Ser Rodrik Cassel after Shaggy bites Little Walder Frey.

Meera Reed spars with Summer in the godswood, with the girl trapping the direwolf in her net. When Bran becomes agitated by questions asked by Jojen Reed, the Reed siblings are chased up the heart tree by Summer and Shaggy.

During the capture of Winterfell by Theon Greyjoy, Summer hears the ironmen coming in. He tries to climb a sentinel tree to get out of the godswood, but falls to the ground. Theon claims the castle as Prince of Winterfell.

The two wolves escape the castle with their masters, but remain in the forest while the boys return to the castle and hide in the crypt of Winterfell. The direwolves evade Theon Greyjoy's hunting party and witness the sack of Winterfell from outside the castle. After the Boltons depart, leaving Winterfell deserted, the wolves pass through the ruins of the winter town and reunite with their masters near the First Keep.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Bran opens his third eye as a warg and often enters into Summer's mind as they travel north toward the Wall through the wolfswood and the northern mountains. Summer encounters a pack of wolves eating a fresh kill, and fights the wolf pack for the carcass. It is only due to the interference of Hodor, acting on Jojen's command, that Bran is forcibly made to vacate Summer's mind. 

At Queenscrown, Summer attacks Styr's wildlings, killing at least three of them and giving Jon Snow an opening to escape them. Summer's back leg is wounded by an arrow, however, and Bran has a hard time entering his mind while the wolf is in pain.

Through Summer, Bran dreams of the deaths of Robb and Grey Wind. Bran, Summer, and the Reeds cross beyond the Wall through the Black Gate of the Nightfort.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

In Jon Snow's wolf dream, Ghost can no longer sense Summer, who is now beyond the Wall. Summer accompanies Bran in his trip beyond the Wall, limping from a wounded leg. Bran can feel the pain when he is in Summer. The direwolf is able to smell that Coldhands is dead. When they arrive at a deserted wildling village, Bran sends Summer to hunt, and Summer meets Varamyr's wolves—One Eye, Stalker, and Sly—eating the remains of the dead Night's Watch deserters. Summer sees that the head wolf, One Eye, is not afraid of him, even though he is twice the wolf's size. He realizes that One Eye has a warg (Varamyr) within him, and they fight. After defeating One Eye and making him submit, Summer takes over leadership of the pack, and they eat the bodies of the deserters.

Summer accompanies Bran to the hill of the cave of the last greenseer. When the party is attacked by wights, Summer defends Bran while he attempts to enter Hodor's mind. After the wights are scared away by the fire of Leaf's torch, Summer enters the cave and is by Bran's side when he awakes. 

While Summer sleeps within the cave, he continues to hunt outside it, along with Varamyr's former pack, and grows gaunt as living game is hard to find. Summer and the pack find a wight snow bear, and tear it to pieces and devour it, even though the rotten meat still moves while they eat it. Summer eats a wight's severed arm as well.
