Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Sallor is one of the senior captains of the Stormcrows when they take a contract to defend Yunkai against the forces of Daenerys Targaryen. He is among those who attend the parley with Daenerys where she attempts to bribe the captains to come to her side but is rebuffed by Prendahl na Ghezn. However, the Stormcrows are free men and the captains return to camp to put the question to their men. Daario Naharis, a fellow captain, has already been won over, however. Daario kills Sallor and Prendahl and delivers their heads to Daenerys in order to bring the Stormcrows over to her. Daenerys's dragons eat the heads.
