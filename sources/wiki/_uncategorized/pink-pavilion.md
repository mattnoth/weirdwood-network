## Summary

According to The Chronicles of Longsister, the Pink Pavilion was the personal pavilion of Belthasar Bolton, a member of House Bolton who took part in the Rape of the Three Sisters. It was allegedly made from the flayed skins of one hundred Sistermen. Northern accounts of the war do not mention this, nor other numerous atrocities alleged in accounts of the war written by men from the Vale.
