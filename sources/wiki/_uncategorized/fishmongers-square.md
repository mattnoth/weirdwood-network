Layout[ ]

Fishmonger's Square is located inside the River Gate in the south of King's Landing. The Street of Steel begins at the market square beside the gate and runs west to Visenya's Hill. The Muddy Way heads north from the square to the center of King's Landing, while the River Row runs along the southern wall of the city.

The fish market is located nearby outside of the walls.

History[ ]

During the Dance of the Dragons, violent riots occurred in the capital. Ser Torrhen Manderly led a sortie from the Red Keep down the Hook with his northmen to try to stop the rioters. When they arrived at the Fishermonger's Square and the River Row, the place was swarming with the gutter knights of Ser Perkin the Flea. Torrhen lost a quarter of his men fighting his way back to the Red Keep.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

On the way back to the Red Keep from the docks, the party of King Joffrey I Baratheon crosses Fishmonger's Square.

Three catapults are built in the market square by the Mud Gate in preparation for the Battle of the Blackwater.
