## Full Text

Raynard Ruskyn was a knight from House Ruskyn. He became Lord Commander of the Kingsguard during the reign of King Aegon III Targaryen.

History[ ]

By 135 AC, Ser Raynard was a sworn brother of the Kingsguard. King Aegon III Targaryen defied an attempted coup against the Rogare family and the Hand of the King, Lord Thaddeus Rowan, beginning a secret siege. Raynard was imprisoned in Maegor's Holdfast after being overwhelmed and wounded by the Lysene guards of Larra Rogare.

In the aftermath of the coup, Raynard was released from captivity, and Ser Marston Waters, Lord Commander of the Kingsguard, was mortally wounded when he confronted Ser Mervyn Flowers, a fellow Kingsguard and one of the conspirators. At some point, Marston was heard to say to Raynard about Mervyn, "We are the both of us bastards and Sworn Brothers besides".

Several royal offices were filled at the Great Council of 136 AC, including the promotion of Ser Raynard to Lord Commander, left vacant by Marston's death.
