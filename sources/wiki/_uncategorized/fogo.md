## Summary

Fogo is with his father in the procession in Vaes Dothrak that proclaims Daenerys Targaryen's son the stallion who mounts the world. Upon his father's death at the hands of Khal Drogo, Fogo becomes khal, but he is in turn killed by Drogo in the Dothraki sea near a Lhazareen settlement.
