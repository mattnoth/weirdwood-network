## Summary

Vojjor Samvi or Vojjor Samui is a ruined city located in Essos, in the southeastern Kingdom of Sarnor near the Dothraki sea. Vaes Khewo is to the west, Yalli Qamayi is to the southeast, and Vaes Leqse is to the northeast. It was originally called Kasath and the Sarnori called it the City of Caravans, but it is now known by its Dothraki name, Vojjor Samvi, meaning "The Broken Gods".

History[ ]

During the Century of Blood, the kings of Kasath and Gornath fought over the plunder of Sarnath. The khalasar of Khal Moro eventually razed Kasath.

A Game of Thrones[ ]
