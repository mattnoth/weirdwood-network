## Full Text

History of the Ironborn is a book written by Archmaester Haereg about the history of the ironborn people from the Iron Islands. According to the book, there has not been a kingsmoot in four thousand years. Only once has a kingsmoot decision been overturned.

The book also has an account on the great and tragic friendship between Loron Greyjoy, Lord of the Iron Islands, and Ser Desmond Mallister.
