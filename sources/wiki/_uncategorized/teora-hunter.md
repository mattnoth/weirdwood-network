Teora Hunter was a lady of House Hunter who became the queen consort of Roland I Arryn, King of Mountain and Vale.

History[ ]

According to legend, Teora reminded her future husband of how his grandfather, Artys I Arryn, the first King of Mountain and Vale, had defeated Robar II Royce, High King of the Vale, by attacking from the high ground in the Battle of the Seven Stars. Thanks to Teora's words, King Roland I Arryn decreed that the Eyrie should be built on the Giant's Lance high above the Gates of the Moon.
