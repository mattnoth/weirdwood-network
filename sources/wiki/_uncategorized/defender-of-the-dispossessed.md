## Summary

Defender of the Dispossessed is an honorary title claimed by the Lord of House Manderly of White Harbor. It most likely relates to the Manderlys' status as refugees from the Reach, from where House Gardener sent them into exile in the north (where they were welcomed by House Stark), and House Peake occupied their castle Dunstonbury.
