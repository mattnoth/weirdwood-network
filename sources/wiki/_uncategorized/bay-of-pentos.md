## Full Text

Bay of Pentos

Western Essos and the location of the Bay of Pentos

The Bay of Pentos is a bay of the eastern narrow sea, along the western coast of Essos. There is a narrow entrance to the bay, which contains a few islands. The Free City of Pentos is located on the eastern shore. Drogo's manse sits by the Bay of Pentos, and Illyrio's manse also has a view of the bay.

There are fishing boats on the bay and larger ships along the shore. Pentos is limited to a fleet of twenty warships according to a peace treaty with Braavos.
