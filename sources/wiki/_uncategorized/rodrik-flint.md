Rodrik Flint was a member of House Flint who became Lord Commander of the Night's Watch. It is unknown from which branch of House Flint he came from.

History[ ]

Lord Commander Flint attempted to make himself King-Beyond-the-Wall. His efforts almost destroyed the Night's Watch. He is considered one of the worst commanders the Night's Watch has ever had.
