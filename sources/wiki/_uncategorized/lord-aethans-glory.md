Lord Aethan's Glory was a warship belonging to House Velaryon.

History[ ]

When Lord Alyn Velaryon escorted King Aegon II Targaryen from Dragonstone back to King's Landing at the end of the Dance of the Dragons, Alyn chose to sail the king aboard Mouse, an old cog named after his mother, Marilda, instead of a more prestigious warship like Lord Aethan's Glory.
