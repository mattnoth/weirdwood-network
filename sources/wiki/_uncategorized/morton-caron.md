## Full Text

Morton Caron was the Lord of Nightsong and the head of House Caron early in the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

Lord Morton attended the Golden Wedding, where the king entertained the lords in the intimacy of his solar, encouraging them to speak freely and share their views on the problems of the realm and how they might best be overcome. Lord Morton thought the king to be cautious and shrewd.
