Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

While the retinue of King Robert I Baratheon travels south through the Neck along the causeway, Arya Stark receives a rash from poison kisses after gathering flowers for her father, Lord Eddard Stark. Mycah tells her that mud will stop the itching.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Poison kisses are spotted in the rainwood by Arianne Martell.
