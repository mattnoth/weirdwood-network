## Summary

New Ibbish is an Ibbenese town on the mainland of Essos, on a forested peninsula north of the Kingdom of the Ifequevron. The Bay of Whales is to the north of the colony, while to the southeast are the ruins of Ibbish, now known by its Dothraki name, Vaes Aresak.
