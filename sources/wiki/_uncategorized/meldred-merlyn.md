Appearance and Character[ ]

Meldred is a round and fleshy man, bald and bearded. He has a fat white face which turns red when he shouts. He dresses in furs and velvets and has a fox-fur mantle.

Meldred uses the title "lord" in the manner of the green lands, but Aeron Greyjoy thinks of him as "the Merlyn".

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

At Pebbleton, Lord Meldred is blessed with seawater by Aeron Greyjoy, and he is among the first to hear the Damphair call for a kingsmoot.

Lord Merlyn attends the kingsmoot on Old Wyk, where he is among the supporters of Asha Greyjoy.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

Meldred may have been named after Meldred and Merlin, two characters from the Arthurian legend.
