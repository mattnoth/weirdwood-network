Character[ ]

Gwynesse is seven years older than her brother Rodrik. She still grieves the loss of her husband in Greyjoy's Rebellion, and has become forgetful. 

History[ ]

Gwynesse's husband died in the sea battle off Fair Isle during Greyjoy's Rebellion in 289 AC. Following his death, Gwynesse returned to Ten Towers, where she has remained since. One of the towers of the castle was renamed the Widow's Tower in her honor. She believes that the castle should be hers since she is older than Lord Rodrik Harlaw, and constantly reminds her brother of this.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Alannys Harlaw, wife of Lord Balon Greyjoy, has moved to Ten Towers to live with her sister Gwynesse, as Harlaw is less raw than Pyke.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord Rodrik Harlaw tells his niece, Asha Greyjoy, that his sister Gwynesse continues to mourn her husband but has become forgetful.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After fleeing the kingsmoot on Old Wyk, Asha briefly visits Gwynesse and her mother, Alannys Harlaw, at Ten Towers before sailing for Deepwood Motte. Gwynesse maintains that Ten Towers should belong to her.
