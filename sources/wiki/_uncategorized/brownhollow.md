## Full Text

Brownhollow is the seat of House Brune of Brownhollow in Crackclaw Point within the crownlands.

History[ ]

Tales of Sunfyre taking refuge in Crackclaw Point were told in the halls of the Crabbs and Brunes.

Lothor Brune, distant kin to the Brunes of Brownhollow, visited the family after his father died. The Brunes sent Lothor away, however, claiming he was not of their blood.
