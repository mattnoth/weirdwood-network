Appearance and Character[ ]

Conn is a handsome man. He is bold and friends with Shagga.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Conn is a member of the Stone Crows whom Tyrion Lannister and Bronn first encounter in the Mountains of the Moon. Tyrion offers to give his hauberk to Conn to secure passage through the mountains, but the Stone Crows insist that all he possesses is already theirs. Tyrion gains them as allies by promising them the Vale of Arryn. 

Conn is one of the clansmen who leaves the mountains with Tyrion, and he accompanies Tyrion to the war council held by Lord Tywin Lannister.

Ulf demands blood money when three Stone Crows kill a Moon Brother, but Conn and Shagga refuse to pay. Ulf later drinks ale and offers to share oxmeat with Tyrion.

During the battle on the Green Fork, Tyrion sees Conn's horse kick a man in the ribs. Conn is slain by a spear thrust during the battle, with Tyrion finding Shagga cradling his friend's head by a tree after the fighting.
