Appearance and Character[ ]

Gunthor owns a cloth-of-silver cloak and a suit of grey enameled scales.

History[ ]

Ser Gunthor studied at the Citadel for several years, during which time he learned to speak the Summer Tongue.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

After the ironborn attack on the Reach, Ser Gunthor is given command of the harbor at Oldtown, and prepares its defenses.

Approaching Oldtown, the Cinnamon Wind is inspected in Whispering Sound by the captain of the Huntress, and then inspected a second time in the harbor of Oldtown by Gunthor himself. Because Gunthor knows the Summer Tongue, he and Captain Quhuru Mo speak privately in his cabin for a while. Afterwards, Gunthor orders the harbor's chain boom to be opened so the swan ship can dock.
