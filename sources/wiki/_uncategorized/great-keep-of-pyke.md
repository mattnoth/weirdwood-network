Layout[ ]

The Great Keep is a massive building which dominates the largest islet of Pyke. A wide stone bridge connects it to the clifftop gatehouse on the mainland, and other bridges lead to the Kitchen Keep and the Bloody Keep on their own islands.

The Great Hall is long and smoky. The Seastone Chair is located on a dais in the hall.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Balon Greyjoy, Lord of the Iron Islands, hosts a feast in the Great Hall for the captains and bannermen who have mustered for his invasion of the north. The gathered guests laugh at Theon Greyjoy after he is mocked by his sister, Asha.
