Appearance and Character[ ]

Hop-Robin has a clubfoot and he is a poor fighter.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

The wandering crow Conwy arrives at Castle Black with new recruits for the Night's Watch, including "a raggy man with a clubfoot". This recruit may be Hop-Robin.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Hop-Robin is trained by Jon Snow due to the lack of a capable replacement for Ser Endrew Tarth as master-at-arms at Castle Black.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Hop-Robin is trained by Iron Emmett after his appointment as the new master-at-arms. Jon spots him training against Horse in the yard. Jon wonders if Hop-Robin would be better as a steward.
