Appearance and Character[ ]

Ardrian is a sour old man with a wrinkled face. 

Ardrian is known for his avarice and his wealth. He looks down upon the lowborn Ser Davos Seaworth, though Lord Stannis Baratheon is dismissive of Ardrian's own opinions.

Lord Celtigar owns a mantle patterned with red crabs picked out in garnets and a square of linen embroidered in red crabs. His ship is the ornate Red Claw. Ardrian's treasures at Claw Isle are said to include a Valyrian steel axe and a horn capable of summoning krakens.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord Ardrian wants to know which storm lords will support Lord Stannis Baratheon when he lays claim to the Iron Throne during the War of the Five Kings. Ardrian attends Stannis's feast at Dragonstone. He has a coughing fit when Melisandre burns the statues of the Seven taken from the sept at Dragonstone.

Lord Celtigar participates in Stannis's siege of Storm's End.

The Red Crab is captured at the Battle of the Blackwater and publicly bends the knee to King Joffrey I Baratheon.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ardrian stays in King's Landing after the Blackwater, with Ser Axell Florent thinking that Lord Celtigar fears reprisals from Stannis. Axell suggests that Stannis sack Claw Isle in Ardrian's absence, but Ser Davos Seaworth objects to the plan. Stannis sides with Davos the Onion Knight, explaining that he will instead deal with Lord Celtigar personally after becoming king.

Ardrian is present at the wedding of King Joffrey I to Lady Margaery Tyrell, and later testifies against Tyrion Lannister at his trial for regicide.
