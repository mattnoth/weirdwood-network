## Full Text

Year 60 After the Conquest (260 AC) is a year in the Westerosi calendar, the 260th after Aegon's crowning in Oldtown that maesters and educated people use as a reference to date the years.

In 260 AC the Seven Kingdoms got involved in the War of the Ninepenny Kings. The Westerosi army drove out the Band of Nine of the Stepstones, and after Ser Barristan Selmy slew Maelys the Monstruous in single combat, the Blackfyre line was extinguished in the male line.
