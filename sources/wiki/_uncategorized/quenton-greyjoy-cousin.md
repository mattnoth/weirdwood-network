Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Along with the rest of Asha Greyjoy's supporters, Quenton sails to Deepwood Motte where Asha contemplates her next move after the kingsmoot.

Quenton accompanies Asha on her flight to the sea, and he slits his mare's throat after the horse shatters a leg in a pit. Quenton sends three men up trees as guards when Asha's men make camp. Quenton kills a spearman during the fight by Deepwood Motte, but a northern mountain clansman then kills Quenton with his axe.
