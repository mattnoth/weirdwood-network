Synopsis[ ]

Davos has washed up on the shore of a small island among the spears of the Merling King in Blackwater Bay. He remembers swimming beneath the chain boom during the Battle of the Blackwater, and then getting caught by the current and gulping seawater. But, somehow, he is alive, and believes the Seven still have some use for him. Despairing over the loss of four of his sons in the sea battle, he contemplates dying on the island, but decides instead to live. A Lysene ship rescues him, one of Salladhor Saan's fleet.
