Daenora Targaryen was the younger daughter of Prince Rhaegel Targaryen and Lady Alys Arryn. She had an older brother and sister, the twins Aelor and Aelora. Daenora was married to her cousin, Prince Aerion.

History[ ]

Daenora married her cousin, Prince Aerion Targaryen, at an unknown point in time. Together, they had one son, Prince Maegor, who was born in 232 AC, and whose claim to the throne was disputed by the Great Council of 233 AC.
