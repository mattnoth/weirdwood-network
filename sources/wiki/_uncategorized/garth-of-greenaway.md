Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

  

Garth and Ollo Lophand attacking Jeor Mormont, by Victor Garcia © Fantasy Flight Games

Garth is among the members of the Night's Watch who accompany Jeor Mormont, Lord Commander of the Night's Watch, on the great ranging. He survives the fight at the Fist and is among the survivors who make it to Craster's Keep. Garth is skeptical about Samwell Tarly having slain an Other. Garth becomes concerned when Craster beats one of his younger wives, and he thinks that Craster is hoarding apples. The blade-wielding Garth and Ollo Lophand prevent Jeor from interceding during the mutiny at Craster's Keep, and Ollo mortally stabs the Lord Commander. During the turmoil, Garth of Greenaway kills Garth of Oldtown.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Garth remains at Craster's Keep after the betrayal, along with a few other former members of the Night's Watch.

A Dance With Dragons[ ]

Five betrayers wander in the haunted forest, planning to head south over the Wall. However, the traitors are killed by Coldhands and his ravens. Summer, Bran Stark's direwolf, finds the bodies and eats parts of the meat. It is unknown if Garth is among them.

According to a semi-canon source, Garth is killed by Others and turned into a wight.
