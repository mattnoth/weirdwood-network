## Full Text

Jeyne is a common given name in Westeros, and occasionally the Free Cities.

It may refer to the following characters:

Canker Jeyne, a dockside prostitute of uncertain gender in Braavos

Squinty Jeyne, a peasant from the Reach

Jeyne Arryn, the Lady of the Eyrie during the reigns of Jaehaerys I and Viserys I Targaryen

Jeyne Beesbury, the wife of Rhaegar Frey

Jeyne Darry, the wife of Cleos Frey

Jeyne Farman, a childhood friend of Queen Cersei Lannister

Jeyne Fossoway, of the green-apple Fossoways, the wife of Gunthor Hightower

Jeyne Fowler, one of the daughters of Lord Franklyn Fowler

Jeyne Goodbrook, only daughter of Ser Garse Goodbrook and Lady Kyra Frey

Jeyne Harroway, the sister of Queen Alys Harroway

Jeyne Heddle, the niece of Masha Heddle

Jeyne Lothston, King Aegon IV Targaryen's eighth mistress

Jeyne Lydden, second wife of Stevron Frey

Jeyne Manderly, the wife of Rodrik Stark

Jeyne Marbrand, the mother of Lord Tywin Lannister

Jeyne Merryweather, a possible marriage candidate for King Aegon III Targaryen

Jeyne Mooton, a possible marriage candidate for King Aegon III Targaryen

Jeyne Nutt, who briefly reigned as River Queen or Queen of the Trident long after the riverlands had been conquered by King Arlan III Durrandon

Jeyne Poole, Vayon Poole's daughter and a friend to Sansa Stark

Jeyne Poore, known as Poxy Jeyne Poore, a leader of the Poor Fellows during the Faith Militant Uprising

Jeyne Rivers, a bastard-daughter of Walder Frey

Jeyne Rowan, the wife of Lord Donald Tarly

Jeyne Royce, first wife of Lord Jon Arryn

Jeyne Smallwood, a possible marriage candidate for King Aegon III Targaryen

Jeyne Swann, rescued by Ser Barristan Selmy from the Kingswood Brotherhood

Jeyne Waters, the bastard daughter of Princess Elaena Targaryen and Lord Alyn Velaryon

Jeyne Westerling, one of King Maegor I Targaryen's Black Brides

Jeyne Westerling, the wife of King Robb Stark

Additionally, Jeyne may refer to:

Grey Jeyne, one of Ramsay Bolton's hunting hounds

Red Jeyne, one of Ramsay Bolton's hunting hounds

Headless Jeyne, an ironborn longship
