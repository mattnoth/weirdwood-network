Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

When the Lords Declarant of the Vale met at the Eyrie intending to extract the young Lord Robert Arryn from the influence of his deceased mother's husband Lord Petyr Baelish, Lady Anya Waynwood cited Septon Lucos' presence at Runestone as a reason why Robert should be sent there to become a ward. In this plan, he would serve as an instructor in spiritual matters to the boy lord.
