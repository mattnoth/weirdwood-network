History[ ]

Supporters of Prince Aegon Targaryen gathered at Pinkmaiden Castle prior to the Battle Beneath the Gods Eye. After Aegon's death, Rhaena Targaryen flew on Dreamfyre from Pinkmaiden to the westerlands to escape King Maegor I Targaryen. Rhaena briefly returned to Pinkmaiden during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen. The king visited Pinkmaiden atop Vermithor in 53 AC.

Quellon Greyjoy, Lord of the Iron Islands, was married to his third wife, a daughter of Lord Piper, by a septon in Pinkmaiden's hall.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Ser Kevan Lannister states the Pipers were burnt out by Ser Gregor Clegane.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Several high-born captives, such as Ser Lyle Crakehall and Lord Antario Jast, are held at Pinkmaiden Castle during the War of the Five Kings.
