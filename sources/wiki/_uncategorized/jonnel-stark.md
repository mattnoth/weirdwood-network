Appearance[ ]

Jonnel had only one eye.

History[ ]

Jonnel became the heir to Winterfell after the death of his older brother Rickon in the conquest of Dorne, passing over Rickon's daughters Sansa and Serena Stark. Jonnel also married Sansa, his half-niece. Jonnel became the Lord of Winterfell after the death of his father, Cregan. The north lamented the death of Rickon, for the reigns of Jonnel and his brothers after him were troublesome.

Jonnel's marriage with Sansa was childless. After her death, he married Robyn Ryswell, but also had no issue. He was succeeded by his brother, Barthogan Stark, as Lord of Winterfell.

Jonnel was interred in the crypts of Winterfell after his death.
