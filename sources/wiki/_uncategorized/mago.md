## Full Text

Mago is a dothraki rider in the khas of Ko Jhaqo.

In the television adaptation Game of Thrones, Mago is portrayed by Ivailo Dimitrov.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

When Khal Drogo's khalasar destroys a Lhazareen settlement, Mago begins raping a woman, Eroeh, until she is rescued by Daenerys Targaryen.

After Drogo falls ill, Jhaqo declares himself khal and names Mago a bloodrider. Mago then steals Eroeh back and rapes her. He gives her to Jhaqo and she is raped again by him and his other bloodriders. When they are done, they cut her throat. Upon hearing of Eroeh's fate, Daenerys promises that Mago's fate will be more cruel, and swears that Mago and Jhaqo will plead for the mercy they showed Eroeh before she is done with them.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Daenerys Targaryen recalls how Mago raped and killed Eroeh after Khal Drogo's death.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

While in the Dothraki sea, Daenerys recalls her promise to kill both Jhaqo and Mago before being discovered by their khalasar.
