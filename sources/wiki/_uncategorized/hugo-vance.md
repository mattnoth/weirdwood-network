Character[ ]

Hugo is a friend and companion to Ser Edmure Tully.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser Hugo is a member of Ser Jaime Lannister's party as he rides north to end the siege of Riverrun. At Castle Darry he carries Jaime's Kingsguard standard. Hugo falls asleep during the dinner celebrating Jaime's arrival at Darry.

Family[ ]

The second son of Lord Norbert Vance of Atranta, Hugo has four brothers; Ser Ronald, Ser Ellery, Kirth and Maester Jon.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

Hugo is a reference by George R. R. Martin to the Hugo Award-winning author, Jack Vance.
