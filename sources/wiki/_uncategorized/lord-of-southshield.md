Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

The ironborn led by King Euron Greyjoy capture Southshield during the taking of the Shields. Lord Osbert Serry is reported to have fled the Shield Islands for the safety of Highgarden. Euron names Andrik the Unsmiling as the new Lord of Southshield.
