Appearance and Character[ ]

Jon is beardless. According to Jaime Lannister, Jon's homely wife is famous for having the beard he lacks.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Beardless Jon was spotted by Ser Jaime Lannister riding at a quintain in the yard of the Red Keep. He was part of the force that marched north to aid in the siege of Riverrun under the command of Ser Jaime. After the fall of Riverrun, Ser Jon joined Ser Lyle Crakehall in setting out for Darry to begin hunting outlaws.
