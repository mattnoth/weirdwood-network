Northern Sothoryos and the Basilisk Isles

The corsair's road is a sea route through the Summer Sea off the northern coast of Sothoryos. It is frequented by corsairs and pirates from the Basilisk Isles.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Victarion Greyjoy divides the Iron Fleet into three squadrons during their journey to Slaver's Bay. Red Ralf Stonehouse leads the fastest ships on the corsair's road. Only nine of Red Ralf's ships rejoin Victarion at the Isle of Cedars, with Ralf himself among the missing.

The swiftest ships he gave to Red Ralf Stonehouse to sail the corsair's road along the northern coast of Sothoryos.
