Culture[ ]

Altars in septs can be inlaid with onyx, mother-of-pearl, and lapis lazuli.

The massive chairs of the Pureborn of Qarth are decorated with goldwork and studded with amber, onyx, lapis, and jade. A bronze arch in the city depicts two snakes mating, and their scales are made of delicate flakes of jade, obsidian, and lapis.

Lord Alester Florent has inlaid lapis flowers on his breastplate. The doublet of Ser Harys Swyft has a bantom rooster depicted in lapis beads.

Some women of Astapor wear blue linen decorated with flakes of lapis.

Cyvasse boards can have squares of carnelian, jade, and lapis.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Competitors for the tourney of the Brotherhood of Winged Knights receive a lapis brooch in the shape of a pair of falcon's wings.
