Layout[ ]

The subterranean vaults are accessible from the wormwalks of Castle Black. One with barrel-vaulted ceilings contains narrow aisles of tall wooden shelves filled with thousands of books and bins of scrolls and ledgers. Tables can be found in niches carved into stone walls with lamps hanging overheard. Texts in the dusty vaults are disorganized.

Items in the vault include drawings of heart trees; a book about the language of the children of the forest; copies of Annals of the Black Centaur, the Jade Compendium, and Dragonkin; and an account of a journey taken by Redwyn, a ranger of the Night's Watch. The collection also includes scrolls from Valyria, accounts of seasons written by maesters, and inventories of the Watch. There are four chests full of loose pages separated from books.

Below the armory of Castle Black is a cavernous stone cellar divided by two rows of square stone pillars. The hall has barrel-vaulted ceilings, tables, and great casks of wine and ale along the walls. A potbellied kettle can be found by a hearth in the corner.

The Night's Watch stores food in some of the vaults, including in storerooms found underneath the Wall.

History[ ]

Maester Aemon has examined the archives of the Night's Watch at Castle Black.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

In preparation for the great ranging, Maester Aemon sends Samwell Tarly to the vaults to search for maps for Jeor Mormont, Lord Commander of the Night's Watch. Sam finds at least a dozen maps of lands beyond the Wall, and he is intrigued by an account written by Redwyn.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Since the common hall was burned during the attack on Castle Black, the Night's Watch take their meals in a cavernous stone vault below the armory.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Jon Snow, the new Lord Commander, has Sam research the Others in the vaults; Sam sees a mouse during his hours of reading. Aemon has Sam find the copy of the Jade Compendium so the elderly maester can give it to Jon.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Commander Snow and Bowen Marsh, Lord Steward, review food stored in vaults.

Jon researches books in the vaults and determines that they will need to be reorganized once Sam returns from Oldtown.
