## Full Text

The liberation of Raventree Hall occurs after the coronation of Robb Stark as King in the North and King of the Trident during the War of the Five Kings.

With the Battle of the Camps saving Riverrun from the Lannister army besieging it, King Robb gives the riverlords leave to liberate their own seats and lands from Lannister occupation. Lord Tytos Blackwood leaves Riverrun with the intent to recover Raventree Hall, which had been seized by Tywin Lannister and was occupied by a Lannister garrison. Tytos is successful but his lands have been despoiled by the occupiers.
