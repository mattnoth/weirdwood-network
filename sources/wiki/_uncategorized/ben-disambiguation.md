## Full Text

Ben is an orphaned boy at the crossroads inn.

Other characters named Ben include:

Bearded Ben, a ranger of the Night's Watch

Big Belly Ben, an outlaw of the Kingswood Brotherhood

Ben Beesbury, a previous Lord of Honeyholt

Ben Blackthumb, an old blacksmith at Harrenhal

Bloody Ben Blackwood, the nickname of Benjicot Blackwood, Lord of Raventree Hall during the Dance of the Dragons

Ben Blackwood, the nickname of Edmund Blackwood, fourth son and child of Lord Tytos Blackwood

Ben Bones, a kennelmaster at the Dreadfort

Ben Bushy, called "Big Ben", a knight in Renly Baratheon's camp

Ben Buttercakes, an innkeeper during the Dance of the Dragons

Ben Coldwater, a member of House Coldwater

Ben Plumm, called "Brown Ben Plumm", a sellsword of Westerosi descent and commander of the Second Sons

Ben Stark, the nickname of Benjen Stark, younger brother of Lord Eddard Stark
