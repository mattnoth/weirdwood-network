A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser Roger Hogg tells Ser Jaime Lannister of the deprivations and attacks he had suffered during the War of the Five Kings. Jaime advises him to plant and pray for one last harvest.

Ser Roger is questioned by Ser Lyle Crakehall on his ancestry to make sure he is not a long lost Crakehall.
