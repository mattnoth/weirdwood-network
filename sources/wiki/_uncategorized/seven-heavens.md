Seven heavens are possible afterlives preached by septons in the Faith of the Seven. The religion also believes in seven hells.

Belief[ ]

The Seven Above rule all men from their heaven. The Father created a glowing crown for Hugor of the Hill by pulling down seven stars from the heavens.

Those who repent of their sins can taste the sweet milk of the merciful Mother in the afterlife.
