## Full Text

Gerold III Lannister was a King of the Rock of House Lannister of Casterly Rock.

History[ ]

Gerold died without male issues, thus a council crowned his only daughter's husband, Ser Joffrey Lydden, who took the Lannister name and became the first Andal to rule Casterly Rock.
