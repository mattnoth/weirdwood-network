Red Flower Vale

The Summer Isles and the location of the Red Flower Vale

The Red Flower Vale is a settled river valley located in eastern Jhala in the Summer Isles.

History[ ]

The Red Flower Vale was the seat of Prince Jalabhar Xho before he went into exile. He petitions the Iron Throne every so often for the men to retake it, but is always put off.
