Mountains of the Morn

The known world and the location of the Mountains of the Morn

The Mountains of the Morn are a mountain range in the Further East of Essos.

Geography[ ]

The Mountains of the Morn run roughly in a north-to-southeast curve. Northwest of the mountains are the Five Forts and the Bleeding Sea, while to the northeast are the Dry Deep and the Cities of the Bloodless Men. The Hidden Sea and its two cities, the City of the Winged Men and Carcosa, are located in the southeastern stretch of the mountains. West of the Mountains of the Morn is Yi Ti, while to the south are the Shadow Lands. The Ash runs from the Mountains of the Morn to the Jade Sea.

Culture[ ]

The Mountains of the Morn are considered legendary by Maester Yandel.
