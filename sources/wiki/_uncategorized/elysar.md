Elysar was sharp tongued, and never had a thought he didn't share.

History[ ]

After the Shivers ended in 60 AC, the conclave elected Elysar as Grand Maester to succeed Benifer who had passed away from the disease the previous year. Elysar was twenty years younger than Benifer had been when he first took the office. He was known to be very opinionated, with some suspecting the Citadel named him to the office to be rid of him from Oldtown.

Elysar and Septon Barth were present when King Jaehaerys and Queen Alysanne Targaryen confronted their daughter, Princess Saera, about her indiscretions. Elysar passed away in 97 AC and was succeeded by Allar.
