## Full Text

Stinkwater is a noble seat in Dorne. It appears to be in eastern Dorne, near the Planky Town, Lemonwood, Spottswood, and the shadow city of Sunspear.  

History[ ]

During the First Dornish War, after burning Planky Town, Queen Rhaenys Targaryen descended upon Stinkwater with her dragon, Meraxes, but as with all other Dornish seats of power great and small, the Targaryens found only old women and children. Nevertheless, those present at Stinkwater paid nominal obeisance to the queen.
