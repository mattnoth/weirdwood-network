## Full Text

Alyssa Royce was a member of House Royce during the reign of King Aegon III Targaryen.

History[ ]

In 133 AC, the Maiden's Day Ball was held at the Red Keep to find a second wife and new queen for King Aegon III Targaryen. Many maidens across Westeros came to King's Landing in hopes of marrying the young king. However, the event was not a true ball, but merely a procession of maidens, each woman curtsying before the king while a herald announced her name and lineage. Alyssa told King Aegon III that she had come all the way from Runestone to be with him.
