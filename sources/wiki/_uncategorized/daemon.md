## Full Text

Daemon is a popular male name of Valyrian origin. It may refer to:

Daemon Targaryen, the younger brother of King Viserys I Targaryen, and the uncle-husband of Princess Rhaenyra Targaryen

Daemon I Blackfyre, the bastard son of Princess Daena Targaryen and King Aegon IV Targaryen; founder of House Blackfyre

Daemon II Blackfyre, the third born son of Daemon II Blackfyre

Daemon III Blackfyre, the grandson of Daemon I Blackfyre through his fourth born son Haegon

Daemon Blackfyre, a member of House Blackfyre, whose exact ancestry is unknown; a one-time Captain of the Golden Company

Daemon Sand, known as the Bastard of Godsgrace

Daemon Velaryon, master of ships under Aegon I Targaryen

Daemon Velaryon, admiral of the king's fleet under Maegor I Targaryen
