## Full Text

Barra's mother  is a prostitute from Chataya's brothel in King's Landing, whose infant daughter is one of King Robert I Baratheon's bastard children.  

In the television adaptation Game of Thrones, she is named Mhaegen and portrayed by Antonia Christophers.

Appearance[ ]

The mother is a young girl with light red hair and a powdering of freckles across the bridge of her nose and on her breast.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Thanks to Lord Petyr Baelish, Lord Eddard Stark finds Barra and her mother in his search for the bastards of King Robert I Baratheon. The mother asks to see Robert again, just to show him how beautiful his daughter is. Eddard promises to talk about it with the king.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Barra and her mother are killed by Allar Deem, an officer of the City Watch, on orders from Commander Janos Slynt, who is carrying out a purge of King Robert's bastards at the command of Queen Cersei Lannister. Tyrion Lannister later orders Allar to be tossed overboard from Summer's Dream.
