## Full Text

The City Watch of Oldtown is the formal policing and protection unit of Oldtown. They are commanded by Ser Moryn Tyrell. They are not as well-trained as the City Watch of Lannisport.

History[ ]

During the Faith Militant uprising the men of the City Watch reinforce Oldtown's defenses and prepared to face the fire from the Targaryen dragons.

After losing to Uthor Underleaf in the Whitewalls tourney, Ser Duncan the Tall briefly considered joining the City Watch of Lannisport or Oldtown.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

In an early draft of the prologue from A Feast for Crows, the City Watch of Oldtown is known as the "Cobblestone Guard".
