## Full Text

Lady Vyrwel was the wife of Lord Vyrwel during the reign of King Aerys I Targaryen. Her given name is unknown.

History[ ]

In 212 AC, Lord Vyrwel came with his lady wife to Whitewalls to attend the wedding of the daughter of Lord Frey to Lord Ambrose Butterwell. During the wedding feast, the Vyrwels were seated at the top of the hall in a place of high honor. Lady Vyrwel began weeping for an unknown reason during the celebration, though she recovered later and helped pull Ambrose from his chair when it came time for the bedding custom.
