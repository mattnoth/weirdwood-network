Appearance[ ]

See also: Images of Galazza Galare

Galazza is a tall, graceful-looking woman, despite being past eighty years old. Her hair is white and her skin parchment thin, but the years have not dimmed her piercing green eyes. Daenerys Targaryen thinks they are sad eyes, full of wisdom.

As the Green Grace, Galazza Galare wears a shimmering green silk veil that covers her whole face, except for her eyes. She has a soft and kindly voice.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Galazza's counsel proves invaluable to Daenerys Targaryen, Queen of Meereen, as she is a voice for peace and tolerance.

Two of Galazza's cousins, Grazhar and Qezza, are made the queen's cupbearers and hostages. The Green Grace is accompanied by a dozen White Graces when she arrives at the Great Pyramid for a private supper with the queen. Galazza suggests Daenerys marry Hizdahr zo Loraq to achieve the peace she desires.

Three Blue Graces accompany the Green Grace to inform Daenerys about a rider bringing disease from Astapor to Meereen.

While discussing Daenerys's upcoming wedding to Hizdahr, the Green Grace says that the queen should re-open the fighting pits of Meereen, which would be pleasing to the gods. On the day of the wedding, Galazza and her fellow Graces wait for the bride and groom outside their temple.

Galazza and many of her Graces attend the reopening of Daznak's Pit, but Daenerys disappears from the pit on Drogon. Ser Barristan Selmy, Lord Commander of the Queensguard, seizes control of Meereen in his queen's absence, and he sends Galazza to the Yunkai'i to offer his peace terms and gold for the three hostages held by the Yunkai'i. Skahaz mo Kandaq distrusts Galazza.

Galaza returns to inform Barristan that the Yunkai'i have refused his offer, and that the only price they will accept is the death of the dragons. She tells him that Daenerys is dead, and that he should let her dragons die as well.
