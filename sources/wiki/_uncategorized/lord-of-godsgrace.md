Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The Lord of Godsgrace is present at the feast in Sunspear held when Ser Balon Swann arrives to deliver the Mountain's skull. He drinks when Ricasso raises a toast to King Tommen I Baratheon.

Notes[ ]

↑ It is unclear who this Lord of Godsgrace is. If the reference is an error, Lady Delonne Allyrion might have been intended. Possibly, the "Lord of Godsgrace" is Lady Delonne's husband. A third option is that this is a reference to Delonne's son and heir, Ser Ryon Allyrion, in which case Lady Delonne died during the span of A Dance with Dragons, making Ryon the new Lord of Godsgrace.
