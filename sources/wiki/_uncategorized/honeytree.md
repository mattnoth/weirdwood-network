## Summary

Honeytree is a village in the riverlands known for its hives that produce honey. It is disputed land between House Bracken and House Blackwood. It is currently held by the Blackwoods.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Jonos Bracken wants Honeytree for subduing Lord Tytos Blackwood. Tytos surrenders Honeytree and its hives as part of the price to submit to the Iron Throne.
