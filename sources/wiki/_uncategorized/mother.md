History[ ]

Mother Maris taught generations of orphaned girls at a motherhouse in Gulltown that  was later renamed in her honor.

During the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen, Queen Elinor Costayne gave up all claims to nobility, join the Faith, and eventually became Mother Elinor at the great motherhouse in Lannisport.
