The Patternmaker's Maze is a religious labyrinth in Braavos. The priests of the Pattern claim that only those who learn to walk it properly will find wisdom.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

The Patternmaker's Maze may be a reference by George R. R. Martin to The Pattern in the works of Roger Zelazny. House Rogers is also inspired by Zelazny.
