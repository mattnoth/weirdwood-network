## Full Text

Captain-general is the highest rank in the Golden Company.

Known incumbents[ ]

Ser Aegor "Bittersteel" Rivers, founder of the Golden Company. One of the Great Bastards of King Aegon the Unworthy.

Daemon Blackfyre, collateral descendant of Bittersteel through his namesake Daemon I Blackfyre.

Maelys "the Monstrous" Blackfyre, Daemon's cousin and successor. One of the Ninepenny Kings who attempted to conquer the Stepstones and the Seven Kingdoms.

Ser Myles "Blackheart" Toyne, an exiled knight of House Toyne with an erroneous cruel reputation.

Ser "Homeless" Harry Strickland, Toyne's successor. Served as paymaster before he was made captain-general after the death of Blackheart.
