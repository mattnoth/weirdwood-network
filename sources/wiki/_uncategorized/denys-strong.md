Appearance[ ]

Denys wears his worldly wealth upon his person and a lord's ransom in golden arm rings. Each ring signifies one year's service with the Golden Company.

History[ ]

Denys is a Westerosi exile. His surname Strong is of a house that had once loomed large in the histories of the Seven Kingdoms. House Strong once held the infamous castle of Harrenhal until they were extinguished during the Dance of the Dragons. He may or may not be of House Strong, as in the free companies a man can call himself whatever he chooses, though not all are genuine. He may be related to another serjeant in the company, Duncan Strong.  

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Denys, like the rest of the company, swears his allegiance to Aegon Targaryen in Volon Therys before they invade Westeros.
