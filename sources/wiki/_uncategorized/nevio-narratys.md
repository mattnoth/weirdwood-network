## Full Text

Nevio Narratys was a Prince of Pentos during the reign of King Aerys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

Nevio was chosen as Prince during the latest war between Pentos and Braavos over slavery, which ended in 209 AC. He was the fifth prince chosen in the span of that single year, for the war had been a disaster for Pentos, where Magisters sacrifice the Prince should a crop be lost or a war be lost. Nevio convinced the magisters to sue for peace after a rare victory - one that is rumored to have been purchased by Nevio himself through bribery. In these peace accords Pentos was forced to abolish slavery and withdraw from the slave trade.
