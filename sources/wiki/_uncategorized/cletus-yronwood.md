Appearance and Character[ ]

Despite his lazy eye, Cletus is considered handsome, randy, and always laughing.

Cletus is one of Prince Quentyn Martell's closest friends. 

Cletus gave the nickname big man to his cousin, Ser Archibald Yronwood.

History[ ]

When Prince Quentyn Martell admitted to having kissed one of the Drinkwater twins, Cletus did not mind and suggested that his friend could take one or both of the girls as paramours.

Cletus once shot down a heron while hunting with Ser Archibald Yronwood and Ser Gerris Drinkwater.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord Anders Yronwood sends Cletus and three of his best knights to accompany Prince Quentyn Martell on a dangerous journey.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Quentyn travels to Essos on a mission to marry Daenerys Targaryen, Queen of Meereen. To hide the true purpose of the journey, Cletus presents himself as a merchant with Quentyn as his servant.

On the way to Volantis, the Meadowlark is attacked by pirates near the Disputed Lands. Although the crew and the Dornishmen manage to win the fight, Cletus and Ser Willam Wells are killed in the battle. The sailors give them an honorable funeral, but when Quentyn and his companions are asked to say few words about the deeds of their friends, Ser Gerris Drinkwater is forced to lie in order not to reveal their identity.

Having been rejected by Daenerys, Quentyn mentions Cletus and Willam while trying to convince Gerris and Ser Archibald Yronwood to aid him in claiming one of Daenerys's dragons after the queen has disappeared from Meereen. Gerris responds that doing so will not bring their fallen friends back.
