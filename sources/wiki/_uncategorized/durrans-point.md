Geography[ ]

  

Durran and Elenei at Durran's Point, from Game of Thrones Blu-ray

Storm's End, the seat of House Baratheon, is located at Durran's Point. There is no safe anchorage below the great cliffs, which are named after Durran Godsgrief, the founder of House Durrandon. There is a cavern with a landing beneath the castle, however.

Great storms which begin in the Summer Sea travel north and slam into the point.

History[ ]

Amid Robert's Rebellion, the smuggler Davos navigated the tunnel below Durran's Point and delivered onions and salt fish to the starving garrison during the siege of Storm's End.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser Davos Seaworth rows Melisandre into the tunnel beneath Storm's End, and the shadowbinder births a living shadow which assassinates the castle's castellan, Ser Cortnay Penrose.
