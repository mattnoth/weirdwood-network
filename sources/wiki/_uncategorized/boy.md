Appearance and Character[ ]

History[ ]

Husband and his wife Sharna took in Boy after he lost his mother to the Bloody Mummers.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Boy is present when Brienne of Tarth, Ser Jaime Lannister, and Ser Cleos Frey arrive at the Inn of the Kneeling Man. He points a crossbow at Jaime until the three of them drop their weapons.

He is present when the brotherhood without banners arrives at the inn with the captive Arya Stark, Hot Pie, and Gendry. He serves food and drink to them, and laughs at Arya when she offers to sell her three horses for a skiff and a lesson in how to sail it. He witnesses Arya's attempt to escape, and her subsequent revelation of her true identity to Harwin.
