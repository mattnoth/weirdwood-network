Appearance[ ]

Kennos is chunky.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Kennos is seen training with other knights in the small inner yard of the Red Keep by Sansa Stark.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser Jaime Lannister spots Ser Kennos riding at a quintain in the yard of the Red Keep. Kennos is part of the force that leaves King's Landing to end the siege of Riverrun under the command of Jaime. At the gates of King's Landing he urges Jaime to wear his golden hand and wave at the smallfolk. He blows the Horn of Herrock to announce the westermen's arrival at Harrenhal, Darry, and Riverrun. During the dinner held at Darry for Jaime, Kennos pulls a serving girl into his lap.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Kennos accompanies Jaime to the siege of Raventree. He recommends that they attack the holdfast at Pennytree.

Behind the scenes[ ]

George R. R. Martin named the character after his longtime friend Ken Keller, who grew up in the west coast of the United States and lives in Kansas City. George also dedicated the first volume of Wild Cards to Ken.
