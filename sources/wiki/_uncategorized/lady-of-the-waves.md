## Full Text

The Lady of the Waves was a goddess of old worshiped by the people of the Three Sisters. Sacred storms were the result of the Lady mating with the Lord of the Skies.

Worship of the Lady of the Waves ended with the arrival of the Andals, who brought with them the Faith of the Seven.
