Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Daenerys Targaryen weds Khal Drogo, becoming his khaleesi. After the khal dies and dragons are hatched amidst his funeral pyre, Dany becomes the leader of a remnant of his Dothraki khalasar.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Daenerys thinks of the Dothraki sea of central Essos as a great grass sea. 

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Missandei includes Khaleesi of Great Grass Sea among the various titles of Dany, who now rules the city of Meereen along Slaver's Bay.
