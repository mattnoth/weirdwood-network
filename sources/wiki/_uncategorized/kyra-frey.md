Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

During the Red Wedding, Sandor Clegane kills Kyra's brother, Ser Tytos Frey, by putting an axe through his head. Kyra becomes a widow that night as her husband, Ser Garse Goodbrook, dies outside of the Twins during the fighting. Sandor may be Garse's killer as well.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Kyra loses her last remaining parent when her father, Ser Jared Frey, disappears in the North under mysterious circumstances on his way from White Harbor to the wedding of "Arya Stark" to Ramsay Bolton.
