## Full Text

Yndros of the Twilight by Francesa Baerald for The Rise of the Dragon

Yndros of the Twilight is a deity worshipped in Lys. Yndros is male by day and female by night, and their acolytes can supposedly transform themselves from male to female and female to male in the act of love.

History[ ]

Larra Rogare worshipped the manifold gods of Lys, including Yndros of the Twilight. Dark rumors arose that she oft used the powers of an acolyte of Yndros at twilight orgies, so she might visit the brothels of the Street of Silk as a man.

After the Rogare Bank ran dry, Marra Rogare fled to the temple of Yndros in man's garb and there claimed sanctuary.

Lotho Rogare also worshipped Yndros.
