Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Along with Mohor and Kurleket, Lharys is one of three Bracken men-at-arms at the crossroads inn when Lady Catelyn Stark takes Tyrion Lannister as a prisoner. He is among those who escort her into the Mountains of the Moon. He is the only Bracken man-at-arms to survive the first attack from the Vale mountain clans. Lharys claims Jyck's horse as his own in the aftermath. He is slain in the party's second clash with clansmen.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

Lharys is a reference to Larry of the Three Stooges.
