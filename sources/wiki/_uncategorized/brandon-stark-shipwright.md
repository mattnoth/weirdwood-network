History[ ]

King Brandon was known as Brandon the Shipwright due to his love of the sea. Thousands of years before Aegon's Conquest, King Brandon attempted to sail across the Sunset Sea, but was never seen again. It is known that he never visited Asshai. His tomb in the crypts of Winterfell is empty. His son burned the remaining ships in his grief, and is thus known as King Brandon the Burner.

Bran Stark tells the story of Brandon the Shipwright to Hodor and Meera and Jojen Reed.
