Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

When war breaks out, Lord Quenten Banefort supports King Joffrey I Baratheon. He is part of the Lannister host led by Ser Jaime Lannister which besieges Riverrun, but he is taken prisoner in the battle in the Whispering Wood.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Banefort is a captive of Lord Jonos Bracken at Stone Hedge.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Freed from his captivity, Quenten is among the gathered nobility at King's Landing which escort the corpse of Lord Tywin Lannister back to Casterly Rock.

Known Lords of Banefort[ ]

Lord Quenten Banefort, the current Lord of Banefort.
