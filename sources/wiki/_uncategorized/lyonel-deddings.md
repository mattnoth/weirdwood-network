## Full Text

Lyonel Deddings was the head of House Deddings during the civil war known as the Dance of the Dragons.

History[ ]

During the Dance of the Dragons, Lord Lyonel Deddings sided with the blacks and Rhaenyra Targaryen.

At the Second Battle of Tumbleton, Lord Deddings joined the host led by Ser Addam Velaryon and scraped together fresh levies of greybeards and green boys, despite the grievous losses he had suffered in the autumn battles. During the battle, Lyonel and Lord Stanton Piper seated together atop a low rise and burned with their squires, servants, and sworn shields when the riderless Vermithor took note of them.
