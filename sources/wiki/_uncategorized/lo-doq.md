## Full Text

Lo Doq, called Lo Lackwit, was a God-Emperor of the Golden Empire of Yi Ti and the thirty-fourth ruler of his dynasty.

History[ ]

Doq was a simpleton cursed with an affliction that caused him to jerk and stagger when he walked, and drool when he tried to speak. Nonetheless he ruled wisely for more than thirty years. There are those, however, that suggest the true ruler was his formidable wife, Empress Bathi Ma Lo.
