Appearance and Character[ ]

The lanky Lucifer bears a grudge against Caggo, who took a slave girl from him and instead of returning her after he was done, Caggo killed her.

Lucifer may be a member of House Long, however, men of the Free Companies may take whatever name they desire.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lucifer was part of the Siege of Astapor. He is later sent by the Tattered Prince along with the other Westerosi Windblown to go over to Daenerys Targaryen.
