## Full Text

Ormond Osgrey was the Lord of Coldmoat and the head of House Osgrey during the reign of King Maegor I Targaryen.

History[ ]

During the Faith Militant uprising, Lord Ormond spoke out against King Maegor's laws that suppressed the Poor Fellows and the Warrior's Sons, the militant orders of the Faith of the Seven. Maegor took Coldmoat away from House Osgrey in retaliation for the outburst.
