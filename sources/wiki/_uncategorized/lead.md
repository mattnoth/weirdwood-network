Culture[ ]

Northwestern Essos and the Iron Islands are rich in lead ores.

Lead is one of the metals used by maesters for their chains; the subject it represents is unknown. The chains of Armen the Acolyte, Maester Aemon and Grand Maester Pycelle have links of lead. The ring, mask, and rod of Archmaester Theobald are also made of lead.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Syrio Forel trains Arya Stark using wooden swords filled with lead.

The pommel of Longclaw is weighted with lead to give better balance.
