Appearance and Character[ ]

Wendell was a short, bald man with a goiter on his neck. He had a problem with flatulence.

According to Septon Sefton, Wendell was grasping and stupid.

History[ ]

Lord Wyman Webber dictated in his will that his daughter, Lady Rohanne Webber, must have married before the second anniversary of his death or Coldmoat and its lands would go to his cousin, Ser Wendell.
