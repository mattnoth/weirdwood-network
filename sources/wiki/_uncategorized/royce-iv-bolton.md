## Full Text

Royce IV Bolton was a Red King of the Dreadfort from the dynasty of House Bolton.

Character[ ]

Royce was known as "Royce Redarm" because of his habits of tearing out the entrails of his prisoners with his bare hands.

History[ ]

During one of the wars between Houses Bolton and Stark, King Royce succeeded in taking and burning Winterfell just like his namesake King Royce II Bolton had done three hundred years earlier.
