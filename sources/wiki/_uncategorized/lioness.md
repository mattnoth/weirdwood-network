## Full Text

Lioness  is one of Cersei's dromonds in service to House Baratheon of King's Landing. It is named after the lion of House Lannister, possibly Queen Cersei Lannister in particular. 

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lioness is among the ships being built as part of Queen Regent Cersei Lannister's plan to increase the size of the royal fleet instead of relying on the traditional sea powers to protect the crown's interests.

During her imprisonment by the Faith of the Seven, Cersei is visited by Qyburn, who tells the queen that Aurane Waters fled King's Landing with the new ships after her arrest.
