A Feast for Crows[ ]

While she helps Brienne of Tarth with her bath, Brienne asks her about the Darklyns. She explains that the lordly Darklyns are all gone since the Defiance of Duskendale, but Darkwoods, Dargoods and Darkes still live in the town, such as herself.
