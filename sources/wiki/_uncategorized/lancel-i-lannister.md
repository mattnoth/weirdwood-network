## Full Text

Lancel I Lannister, known as Lancel the Lion, was a King of the Rock of House Lannister of Casterly Rock.

History[ ]

Lancel rode to war against the Gardener Kings of the Reach and conquered as far south as Old Oak but was later slain in combat. His conquest were lost by his son, King Loreon III Lannister, however.
