## Summary

Lanna Lannister is a noblewoman of House Lannister and a distant cousin to the main branch, being a first cousin twice removed of Tywin Lannister, the Lord of Casterly Rock. She is the daughter of Ser Damion Lannister and Shiera Crakehall and the sister of Ser Lucion Lannister. She is married to the head of House Jast, Lord Antario Jast; it is unconfirmed whether Antario's sons are from Lanna or an earlier wife.
