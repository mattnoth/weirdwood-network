## Summary

When Harys Swyft, the Knight of Cornfield, is trapped beneath his dying horse during the Battle of the Blackwater, Willit pulls him from under the horse and defends him against a dozen attackers. Willit's wounds are great. For his efforts he is awarded a spear with a silver-banded haft, a new suit of ringmail, and a full helm with visor. His sons are to be taken to Casterly Rock to serve House Lannister, the eldest as a squire and the younger as a page with the opportunity to rise to knighthood.
