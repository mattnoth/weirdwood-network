Appareance and Character[ ]

Moreo has a forked green beard. Hailing from Tyrosh, he can speak the Common Tongue fluently.

History[ ]

Moreo has sailed the narrow sea for roughly thirty years, first as oarman, then as quartermaster, and now as captain of trading galleys. Storm Dancer is his fourth ship and the fastest of them.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Moreo is at White Harbor with his ship when Catelyn Stark and Ser Rodrik Cassel arrive to take passage south to King's Landing. They book passage on Storm Dancer, which is the swiftest available in the harbor.

When the galley arrives at the capital, Moreo suggests the travelers stay at an old inn on Eel Alley. Catelyn rewards each of his oarmen with a silver stag. Moreo offers to hold onto his crew's silver until they return to Tyrosh, but Catelyn insists on personally paying each oarman.

The City Watch of King's Landing arrive at the old inn with orders to escort Catelyn to Lord Petyr Baelish at the Red Keep; Catelyn assumes that Moreo sold her whereabouts for a price. When Catelyn asks Petyr how he knew she was in the city, her childhood friend replies that Lord Varys knows all.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Storm Dancer is in White Harbor when Lord Davos Seaworth arrives on Merry Midwife.
