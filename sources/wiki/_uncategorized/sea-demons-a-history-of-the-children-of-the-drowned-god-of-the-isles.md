Sea Demons: A History of the Children of the Drowned God of the Isles is a work written by Archmaester Mancaster, concerning the history of the Ironborn. Archmaester Gyldayn finds the chapters about the conflict between Dalton Greyjoy's Ironborn and the Westerlands to be especially good.

Contents[ ]

This book describes the history of the Ironborn, including the details of the war between Ironborn and the Westerlands covering from the end of the Dance of the Dragons and the beginning of King Aegon III Targaryen's regency.
