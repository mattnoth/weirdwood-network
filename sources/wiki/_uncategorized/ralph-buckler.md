Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Buckler hosts Prince Oberyn Martell and his entourage when they are on their way to King's Landing for the wedding of King Joffrey I Baratheon and Margaery Tyrell.

Ralph offers a toast to the royal couple during the wedding feast at the Red Keep. However, Joffrey is poisoned moments later.
