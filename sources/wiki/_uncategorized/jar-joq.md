## Full Text

Jar Joq was a God-Emperor of the Golden Empire of Yi Ti and the seventh member of his dynasty.

History[ ]

Under Joq's rule, the Golden Empire continued the apex of its power with the conquest of Great Moraq. He succeeded his father, Jar Har, and was in turn succeeded by his younger brother, Jar Han.
