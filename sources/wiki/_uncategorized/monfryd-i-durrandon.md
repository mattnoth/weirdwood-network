## Full Text

Monfryd I Durrandon, also known as Monfryd the Mighty, was a Storm King of House Durrandon.

History[ ]

Monfryd crossed the Blackwater Rush and defeated the petty kings of House Darklyn and House Mooton in a series of wars and seized the port towns of Duskendale and Maidenpool. He was succeeded by Durran XI Durrandon.
