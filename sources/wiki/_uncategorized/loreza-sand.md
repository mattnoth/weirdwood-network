Appearance and Character[ ]

Loreza idolizes her older sisters, Elia Sand and Obella Sand.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

After the death of her father, Prince Oberyn Martell, Loreza's uncle Doran Martell places her and all her elder sisters into custody out of concern for their reactions to their father's death at the hands of Gregor Clegane, despite Loreza being only six years old at the time.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Loreza has just turned seven and is accompanying her mother to Hellholt, seat of Loreza's grandfather, Lord Harmen Uller.
