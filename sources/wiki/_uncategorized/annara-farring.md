History[ ]

Though invited to the wedding of Annara and Lord Walder Frey, Lord Hoster Tully of Riverrun did not attend.

Rumors around the Twins claim that Annara may have been sleeping with Walder's great-grandson, Black Walder Frey, casting doubts on her children's parentage.
