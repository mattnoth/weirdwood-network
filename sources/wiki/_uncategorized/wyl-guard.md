Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Wyl is one of the guards that go south with Lord Eddard Stark when he is named Hand of the King. Jory Cassel, Heward and Wyl escort Eddard to a brothel to find one of Robert I Baratheon's bastards, Barra. On the return trip to the Red Keep, the three guards are slain by House Lannister guards under orders from Ser Jaime Lannister as a lesson to Eddard.
