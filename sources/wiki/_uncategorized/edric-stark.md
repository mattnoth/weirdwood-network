Edric Stark was a Lord of Winterfell and head of House Stark during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

Edric was a grandson of Lord Alaric Stark. Edric became Lord of Winterfell after Alaric's death in 72 AC, as Alaric's two sons had died previously.
