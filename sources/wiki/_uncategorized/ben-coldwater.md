## Summary

Ben attends the tourney to select the members of the newly-created Brotherhood of Winged Knights. During the feast at the Gates of the Moon, after musicians begin playing for the dance, he inquires into the lack of singers and is the first of the knights to ask Alayne Stone (Sansa Stark) to dance with him.
