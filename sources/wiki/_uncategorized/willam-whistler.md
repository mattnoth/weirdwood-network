## Full Text

Willam Whistler was a knight during the reign of King Maegor I Targaryen.

History[ ]

Willam supported Prince Aegon the Uncrowned when he rebelled against his uncle, Maegor the Cruel. Aegon and Willam both died in the Battle Beneath the Gods Eye in 43 AC.
