## Summary

Supreme Commander of the Armies and Allies of Yunkai is the title held by the Yunkai'i noble Yurkhaz zo Yunzak during the yellow city's campaign against Daenerys Targaryen, indicating his authority in military matters over the rest of the Wise Masters, the forces commanded by the allies cities of Mantarys, Tolos, Elyria, New Ghis, Qarth and the captains of the sellsword companies hired by Yunkai.

## Full Text

Supreme Commander of the Armies and Allies of Yunkai is the title held by the Yunkai'i noble Yurkhaz zo Yunzak during the yellow city's campaign against Daenerys Targaryen, indicating his authority in military matters over the rest of the Wise Masters, the forces commanded by the allies cities of Mantarys, Tolos, Elyria, New Ghis, Qarth and the captains of the sellsword companies hired by Yunkai.

After Yurkhaz's death at Daznak's Pit, supreme command is shared by the Yunkish generals and rotated on a daily basis.
