Prince Aegon Targaryen was the youngest child of Prince Baelon Targaryen and Princess Alyssa Targaryen. He was named after Aegon the Conqueror. His older brothers were King Viserys I and Prince Daemon Targaryen.

History[ ]

Alyssa never recovered from the difficult labor, and perished the same year of Aegon's birth. Aegon died the following year, just short of his first nameday.
