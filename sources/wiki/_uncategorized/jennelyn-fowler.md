Appearance[ ]

Jennelyn is a tall woman with wispy yellow hair.

History[ ]

In her youth, Princess Arianne Martell played in the pools of the Water Gardens with Jeyne and Jennelyn, riding one of the twins' shoulders.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Nymeria Sand was abed with Jennelyn and her sister Jeyne when news of Prince Oberyn Martell's death reached them. Doran Martell, Prince of Dorne, feels Nymeria is too close to the Fowler twins.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Jennelyn and her sister are present at the feast at Sunspear when Ser Balon Swann of the Kingsguard arrives to deliver the Mountain's skull. The Fowler twins refuse to drink when Ricasso raises a toast to King Tommen I Baratheon.
