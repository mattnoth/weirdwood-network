History[ ]

Ser Gwayne fought during the First Blackfyre Rebellion in 196 AC, at the Battle of the Redgrass Field in Lord Donnel Arryn's vanguard. Lord Arryn's lines were shattered and nearly overrun by Daemon I Blackfyre, until Gwayne appeared with reinforcements.

Gwayne came across Daemon and the two dueled ahorsed for nearly an hour. The duel was considered a sight to behold, partly because of the clashing of two Valyrian steel swords against each other, with Gwayne wielding Lady Forlorn and Daemon with Blackfyre. Ultimately, Daemon cut through Gwayne's helm and left him blind and bleeding. Daemon dismounted to ensure the wounded knight was not trampled and ordered Redtusk to bring Gwayne to the maesters in the rear to see to his wounds.

Daemon's duel and subsequent compassion to Gwayne was a fatal error, as it allowed for Lord Brynden Rivers to position his Raven's Teeth atop a ridge and rain arrows upon Daemon and his twin sons, Aegon and Aemon Blackfyre.
