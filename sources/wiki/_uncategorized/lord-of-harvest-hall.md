History[ ]

Lord Selmy was present at the Golden Wedding between Lady Alyssa Velaryon and Lord Rogar Baratheon at King's Landing in 49 AC.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord Arstan Selmy sends men to support King Renly Baratheon during the War of the Five Kings. It is unknown if he has bent the knee to the Iron Throne after Renly's death or if he now supports Renly's brother, Stannis Baratheon.

Known Lords of Harvest Hall[ ]

Lord Selmy, ruling during the early reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

Lord Arstan Selmy, the current Lord of Harvest Hall.
