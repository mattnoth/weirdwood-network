## Summary

The Silver Shore is a lake in the eastern Kingdom of Sarnor. A river from the lake runs north to the Bay of Tusks. The ruined city of Vaes Athjikhari was known as Sallosh by the Silver Shore when it was inhabited by Tall Men.
