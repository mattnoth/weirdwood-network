Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

After his father followed first Renly Baratheon and later Stannis Baratheon in the War of the Five Kings, the Florent heir Alekyne remained safe at the family's seat of Brightwater Keep in the wake of the Battle of the Blackwater. Lord Alester Florent and the rest of House Florent are attainted by the Iron Throne for pledging allegiance to Baratheon claimants in the war, and their castle, Brightwater Keep, is granted to Ser Garlan Tyrell.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Alekyne becomes the new Lord of Brightwater after the death of his father, Alester. Because Garlan is preparing an army to claim Brightwater, Alekyne flees to Oldtown to seek refuge at the Hightower, where his sister Rhea is married to Lord Leyton Hightower, Garlan's maternal grandfather.
