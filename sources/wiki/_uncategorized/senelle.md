Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Senelle is present when the news of Lord Tywin Lannister's death is brought to Cersei Lannister.

Taena Merryweather informs Cersei that Senelle is spying for Margaery Tyrell, so Cersei gives her to Qyburn for his experiments on the dying Ser Gregor Clegane. When he later requests more subjects, Cersei points out that she'd given him Senelle, to which he responds: 

Alas. The poor girl is quite … exhausted.
