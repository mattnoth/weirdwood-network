History[ ]

Aemon was the secondborn of the children of Daemon Blackfyre and Rohanne of Tyrosh. He had an older twin brother, Aegon, and five younger brothers: Daemon, Haegon, Aenys, and two whose names are currently unknown. He also had at least two sisters.

The twins often bullied their younger brother Daemon, and Daemon's friend, Alyn Cockshaw. Daemon once had a prophetic dream about the deaths of Aemon and Aegon, but the disbelieving twins only laughed at their brother.

  

The deaths of Daemon, Aegon, and Aemon Blackfyre at the battle of the Redgrass Field. By Jota Saraiva ©

During the First Blackfyre Rebellion, when Aemon was twelve years old, he served as a squire to his father. At the Battle of the Redgrass Field, Aemon's twin, Aegon, was killed by the arrows of Brynden Rivers and his Raven's Teeth. Unwilling to leave him, Daemon remained by his fallen son's side, and was also struck down by arrows, supposedly driven by Bloodraven's magic. Aemon took up the sword Blackfyre from his dying father, but was then slain by the archers as well.
