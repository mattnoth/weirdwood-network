Known menageries[ ]

Braavos[ ]

The Sealord's menagerie, located in the Sealord's Palace in Braavos, is renowned throughout the known world. Braavosi ship captains bring queer animals from around the world to the Sealord's menagerie.

Lannisport[ ]

Lannisport's menagerie used to include a grey elephant, but the animal died when Tyrion Lannister was seven years old (either 280 AC or 281 AC).

Pentos[ ]

Magister Illyrio Mopatis has a menagerie in his manse that includes a panther.

The menagerie of the Prince of Pentos includes a pride of lions.

Behind the scenes[ ]

Real-world menageries are considered to be the ancestors of modern zoos.
