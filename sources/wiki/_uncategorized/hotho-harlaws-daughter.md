Appearance[ ]

According to Hotho Humpback, his daughter is a fair girl, newly flowered. She has blonde hair, small breasts, and good hips. Unlike her father, she does not have a hump.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Hotho Harlaw goes to Ten Towers hoping to persuade his widowed cousin and liege lord, Rodrik Harlaw, to marry his daughter, but Rodrik refuses.

Before the kingsmoot on Old Wyk, Hotho offers his support to Victarion Greyjoy and offers the Lord Captain his daughter for his queen. Victarion promises Hotho he will take a look at the girl after he has been crowned. Euron Greyjoy wins the election, however.
