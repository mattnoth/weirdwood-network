Cider Hall

The Reach and the location of Cider Hall

Cider Hall is the seat of House Fossoway of Cider Hall in the Reach. It is located near the confluence of the Cockleswhent and the Mander. The castle is named after the alcoholic beverage.

History[ ]

Florence Fossoway, the wife of Lord Martyn Tyrell, became skilled at numbers by counting apples as a child.

According to Ser Franklyn Flowers, a bastard, his mother was a washerwoman at Cider Hall who was raped by one of Ser Fossoway's sons.
