## Summary

King of the Brimstone was one of the hereditary titles held by the lords of House Dryland prior to Nymeria's War. Ruling the sulphurous river Brimstone from their seat at Hellgate Hall, these Dornish petty kings were among those who fought against the warrior queen Nymeria of the Rhoynar and her consort Mors Martell. In the end, Nymeria won, although her spouse had been slain, and Lucifer Dryland, the last King of the Brimstone and the last member of his line, was dethroned. Together with five other kings, he was exiled to the Wall, ending his days as a member of the Night's Watch.
