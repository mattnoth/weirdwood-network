## Full Text

Owen is a male name in Westeros. It may refer to:

Owen, deceased brother of Septon Meribald

Owen, a knight from the Vale of Arryn

Owen the Oaf, a steward of the Night's Watch

Owen Bush, a Kingsguard for King Maegor I Targaryen

Owen Costayne, Lord of Three Towers during the Dance of the Dragons

Owen Fossoway, Lord of Cider Hall during the Dance of the Dragons

Owen Inchfield, a knight in Renly Baratheon's camp

Owen Merryweather, Lord of Longtable and a Hand of the King to Aerys II Targaryen

Owen Norrey, a northern mountain clansman

Owen Oakenshield, a legendary son of Garth Greenhand
