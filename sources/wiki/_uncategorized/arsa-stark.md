## Summary

Arsa Stark was a noblewoman from House Stark during the latter half of the second century after Aegon's Conquest. She was the only daughter of Lord Brandon Stark and his wife, Lady Alys Karstark. Arsa had two full older brothers, Rodwell and Beron and an older bastard brother as well, Lonnel Snow.
