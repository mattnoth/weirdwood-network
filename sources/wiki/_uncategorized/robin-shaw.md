## Full Text

Robin Shaw was a knight of the Kingsguard during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

In 59 AC the winter disease known as the Shivers killed many including Ser Samgood of Sour Hill and Ser Victor the Valiant of the Kingsguard. Once the disease passed, the Lord Commander of the Kingsguard, Ser Gyles Morrigen presented Ser Robin and Ser Ryam Redwyne before King Jaehaerys and both men joined the sworn brotherhood.
