## Full Text

Lord Bracken was the Lord of the Stone Hedge and the head of House Bracken during the reign of kings Daeron II and Aerys I Targaryen. His given name is unknown.

History[ ]

During the First Blackfyre Rebellion, a Lord Bracken (presumably, the father of Ser Otho) declared for House Blackfyre. He sailed to Myr to hire crossbowmen to aid Daemon I Blackfyre and Bittersteel, but was delayed by storms.

In 211 AC, Lord Bracken is dying. As he outlived his eldest son, who died during the Great Spring Sickness, his second son, the belligerent Ser Otho, was set to inherit Stone Hedge.

It is unclear if he died by 212 AC or not. The conspirators of the Second Blackfyre Rebellion hoped for Otho to join the forces of House Bracken to their own, which may imply Otho had succeeded his father.

Family[ ]

His relation to Lord Bracken who was executed for treason in 178 AC is unknown.
