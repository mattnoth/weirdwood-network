Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser Dermot fights for Stannis Baratheon at the Battle of the Blackwater. He is captured and later bends the knee to King Joffrey I Baratheon.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Dermot is spotted by Ser Jaime Lannister when he is riding at a quintain in the yard of the Red Keep. When he is practicing with other knights, King Tommen I Baratheon asks to take a turn. Dermot rides with Jaime to Riverrun and is assigned to carry the banner of Tommen. While dining at Darry, Dermot tells stories about being a hedge knight in the stormlands. Dermot commands the search north of the Red Fork after Ser Brynden Tully escapes from Riverrun, but returns without Brynden after being attacked by wolves, losing two sentries to them.
