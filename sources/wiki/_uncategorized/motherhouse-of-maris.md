## Summary

Mother Maris, one of the Seven Speakers, taught generations of orphaned girls at a motherhouse located on an island in the harbor of Gulltown, outside the city proper. Many nobles sent their bastard daughters to the motherhouse to be raised, such as the daughter of Alys Turnberry. Lady Jeyne Arryn died in the presence of Jessamyn Redfort at the Motherhouse of Maris in 134 AC.

History[ ]

Mother Maris, one of the Seven Speakers, taught generations of orphaned girls at a motherhouse located on an island in the harbor of Gulltown, outside the city proper. Many nobles sent their bastard daughters to the motherhouse to be raised, such as the daughter of Alys Turnberry. Lady Jeyne Arryn died in the presence of Jessamyn Redfort at the Motherhouse of Maris in 134 AC.
