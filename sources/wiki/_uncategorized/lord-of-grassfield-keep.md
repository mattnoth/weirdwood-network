History[ ]

Lord Meadows had the command of King Maegor I Targaryen's army at the start of the Faith Militant uprising. He was killed in battle by Wat the Hewer in 42 AC.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord Elwood Meadows is Ser Cortnay Penrose's second-in-command at Storm's End. After the death of Ser Cortnay, Elwood names himself as the new castellan and surrenders the castle to King Stannis Baratheon.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Elwood is confirmed as second-in-command by Stannis but the position of castellan of Storm's End is given to Ser Gilbert Farring.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Elwood remains loyal to Stannis as the seneschal of Storm's End.

Known Lords of Grassfield Keep[ ]

Lord Meadows, killed in 42 AC at the Battle at Stonebridge.

Lord Elwood Meadows, the current Lord of Grassfield Keep.
