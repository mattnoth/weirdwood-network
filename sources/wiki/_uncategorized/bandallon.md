## Full Text

Bandallon

The Reach and the location of Bandallon

Bandallon is the seat of House Blackbar in the Reach. It is located on the shores of the Sunset Sea, west of Brightwater Keep, northwest of Oldtown, and southwest of the Shield Islands.

History[ ]

After the election of Septon Alfyn to the seat of High Septon, King Jaehaerys I Targaryen visited Bandallon, Three Towers, Uplands, and Honeyholt while awaiting the arrival of the ailing Alfyn to Oldtown.

In 89 AC, King Jaehaerys I stopped at Bandallon during one of his progress around the Reach. As Jaehaerys was estranged from his sister-wife, Queen Alysanne Targaryen, at the time, Lord Blackbar's youngest daughter boldly tried to seduce the king. Jaehaerys refused her advances, however.

While sailing against Lord Dalton Greyjoy in the westerlands in 133 AC, Lord Alyn Velaryon's fleet passed Bandallon.
