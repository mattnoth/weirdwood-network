## Full Text

Aegon is a given name of Valyrian origin. It may refer to:

Characters[ ]

Aegon Ambrose, a member of the Warrior's Sons.

Aegon Blackfyre, eldest son of Daemon I Blackfyre

Aegon Frey (son of Aenys), also known as Bloodborn, eldest son of Aenys Frey and Tyana Wylde

Aegon Frey (son of Stevron), also known as Jinglebell, the son of Stevron Frey and Jeyne Lydden

Aegon I Targaryen, first king of the Targaryen dynasty

Aegon II Targaryen, sixth Targaryen king

Aegon III Targaryen, seventh Targaryen king

Aegon IV Targaryen, eleventh Targaryen king

Aegon V Targaryen, fifteenth Targaryen king

Aegon Targaryen (son of Gaemon), eldest son of Daenys and Gaemon Targaryen

Aegon Targaryen (son of Aenys I), eldest son and heir of King Aenys I Targaryen and Queen Alyssa Velaryon

Aegon Targaryen (son of Jaehaerys I), eldest son and firstborn child of King Jaehaerys I and Queen Alysanne Targaryen

Aegon Targaryen (son of Baelon), youngest son of Prince Baelon and Princess Alyssa Targaryen

Aegon Targaryen (son of Aerys II), third son of King Aerys II and Queen Rhaella Targaryen

Aegon Targaryen (son of Rhaegar), only son of Prince Rhaegar Targaryen and Elia Martell

Aegon Targaryen (Young Griff), a young man of Valyrian origin who claims to be Rhaegar and Elia's son

Locations[ ]

Aegon, an island west of Lonely Light and south of the Summer Isles, in the Sunset Sea

Aegon's High Hill, in King's Landing

Aegonfort, the predecessor of the Red Keep
