## Full Text

Robin Hollard was a member of House Hollard and a squire during the Defiance of Duskendale.

History[ ]

When King Aerys II Targaryen was a captive in the Defiance of Duskendale, Robin would dance around the king and pull on his beard. After the Defiance had been put down, Robin died on the rack.

Family[ ]

Robin may have been Ser Jon Hollard's unnamed son.
