Maegor Targaryen has been the name of several members of House Targaryen:

King Maegor I Targaryen, also known as Maegor the Cruel, third Targaryen king

Prince Maegor Targaryen, only son of Prince Aerion Targaryen and Princess Daenora Targaryen.

King Maegor III Targaryen, a character created for and mentioned briefly in the fifth season of Game of Thrones.
