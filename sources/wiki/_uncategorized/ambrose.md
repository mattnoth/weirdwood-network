Ambrose Butterwell, the Lord of Whitewalls during the reigns of King Daeron II and Aerys I Targaryen

House Ambrose

Ser Aegon Ambrose, a knight of the Warrior's Sons in at the start of the reign of Maegor I Targaryen

Alyn Ambrose, the son of Lord Arthur Ambrose, the head of House Ambrose during the reigns of Kings Joffrey I and Tommen I Baratheon

Lord Arthur Ambrose, the head of House Ambrose during the reigns of Kings Joffrey I and Tommen I Baratheon

Ser Aubrey Ambrose, a knight who supported Daemon I Blackfyre during the First Blackfyre Rebellion

Ser Edmund Ambrose, a knight from Lord Randyll Tarly's household

Marq Ambrose, loyal to King Aegon II Targaryen during the Dance of the Dragons
