## Full Text

Damon is a given name in Westeros. Characters named Damon include:

Damon Dance-for-Me, a man-at-arms sworn to House Bolton, one of the Bastard's Boys

Damon Darry, a knight during the regency of Aegon III Targaryen

Damon Hightower, known as Damon the Devout, lord of the Hightower and the first Hightower to convert to the Faith of the Seven

Damon Lannister, the Grey Lion, Lord of Casterly Rock during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen

Damon Lannister, son of Ser Jason Lannister

Damon Marbrand, Lord of Ashemark

Damon Morrigen, known as Damon the Devout, Grand Captain of the Warrior's Sons early in Faith Militant uprising

Damon Paege, a knight

Damon Shett, the Knight of Gull Tower

Damon Teague, King of the Rivers and the Hills, the last monarch of House Teague who died in the Battle of Six Kings

Damon Vypren, a knight and heir of Lord Lucias Vypren
