## Summary

The eastern gate, sometimes called the hill gate or the Khyzai gate, is a gate in Meereen. Travelers bound for Lhazar via the Khyzai Pass always leave through the eastern gate.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Joso's Cock, a battering ram made from the mainmast of the galley formerly called Joso's Prank, is used by Daenerys Targaryen's forces to break down the eastern gate during the siege of Meereen.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Tal Toraq and his Stalwart Shields gather by the eastern gate during the second siege of Meereen.
