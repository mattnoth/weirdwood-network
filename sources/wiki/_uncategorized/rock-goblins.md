Behind the Scenes[ ]

In a draft of A Dance with Dragons, Tyrion II, Tyrion Lannister hears stories of rock goblins and giants warring while he and Illyrio Mopatis travel east from Pentos. The goblins won but were seduced by swan maidens from the lakes and were made thralls. The Andals then rose up and killed them all.
