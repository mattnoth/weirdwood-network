Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Having been tortured, the Blue Bard includes Hugh in the names of those who spent time with Queen Margaery Tyrell. Queen Cersei Lannister has an arrest warrant signed for Hugh as part of her plans to remove Margaery. He is sent to the dungeons after his arrest.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Hugh is held with the rest of Margaery's accused lovers in a dungeon under the charge of Qyburn.
