Brandon Stark was the Lord of Winterfell and head of House Stark during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen. He was the youngest son of Lord Cregan Stark and Lady Lynara Stark.

History[ ]

Brandon succeeded his brother Barthogan as Lord of Winterfell at some time during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen.

Brandon married Lady Alys Karstark, with whom he had three children: Rodwell, Beron and Arsa Stark. Before his marriage, he had an affair with Wylla Fenn which resulted in a bastard son, Lonnel Snow.

Brandon is buried in the crypts of Winterfell.
