## Full Text

Julian Wormwood was a knight from House Wormwood during the reign of King Aegon II Targaryen.

History[ ]

Julian fought for the greens during the Dance of the Dragons. After King's Landing was taken back by the greens in 130 AC, Ser Tyland Lannister, Ser Marston Waters and Ser Julian were sent to the Free Cities of Pentos, Tyrosh, and Myr to hire sellswords to fight for King Aegon II Targaryen.

By the time Ser Tyland's party returned empty handed to King's Landing, Aegon II was dead.
