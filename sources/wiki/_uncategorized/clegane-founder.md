History[ ]

  

The kennelmaster saves Tytos Lannister from the lioness. © tumcolho

Clegane was originally the kennelmaster at Casterly Rock. One autumn year, Lord Tytos Lannister came between a lioness and her prey. The lioness killed Tytos's horse and was about to attack him as well, when the kennelmaster came to his rescue with his hounds, losing his leg and three dogs in the effort. As a reward, Tytos granted the kennelmaster a knighthood, lands and a towerhouse, and took his son as his squire, thereby creating House Clegane. For his sigil, Ser Clegane chose the three dogs he lost saving Tytos, on a yellow field like autumn grass.
