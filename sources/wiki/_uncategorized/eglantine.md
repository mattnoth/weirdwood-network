Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Septa Eglantine accompanies Princess Myrcella Baratheon when she is sent to Dorne to be a ward of Prince Doran Martell and betrothed to Prince Trystane Martell. Because of the risk of the Seaswift being taken by Stannis Baratheon's forces on the way to Braavos, Septa Eglantine puts brown dye in Myrcella's hair to disguise her, and has the princess's handmaid Rosamund Lannister wear her clothes. The septa tells Myrcella it is a game, but Myrcella understands the truth.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Septa Eglantine is in Dorne with Princess Myrcella.
