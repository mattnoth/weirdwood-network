## Full Text

Clarence Charlton was a knight from House Charlton during the reign of King Aerys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

In 212 AC, Ser Clarence took part in the tourney held by Lord Ambrose Butterwell at his castle of Whitewalls to celebrate his second marriage to the daughter of Lord Frey. Clarence was soundly defeated in the lists by Ambrose's commander and son-in-law, Ser Tommard Heddle.
