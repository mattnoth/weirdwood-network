History[ ]

A great pike crusted with crushed almonds was served to the high table during the wedding tourney at Whitewalls.

River pike is enjoyed by lowbown Norvoshi.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

River pike poached in almond milk is served at the Twins during the Red Wedding.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Herb-crusted pike is served to Queen Regent Cersei Lannister at the Red Keep. Ser Jaime Lannister has river pike baked in a crust of herbs and crushed nuts while at Darry.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When a huge pike is caught in the Skahazadhan, the fisherman who caught the fish presents it to Daenerys Targaryen, Queen of Meereen.

Pike is served during the wedding of Sigorn and Alys Karstark at Castle Black.

King Tommen I Baratheon serves morsels of pike to his kittens, Boots, Lady Whiskers, and Ser Pounce.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Pike, trout, and salmon from the lakes and rivers of the Vale of Arryn are served at the Gates of the Moon.

Quotes[ ]

A pike of unprecedented size had been caught in the Skahazadhan, and the fisherman wished to give it to the queen. She admired the fish extravagantly, rewarded the fisherman with a purse of silver, and sent the pike to her kitchens.
