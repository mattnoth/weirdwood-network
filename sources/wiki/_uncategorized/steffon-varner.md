Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord Steffon is part of Renly's force beneath the walls of Bitterbridge. He takes part in the feast. After Renly's death he goes over to Stannis Baratheon and is present beneath the walls of Storm's End for the parley with Ser Cortnay Penrose.

He fights in the Battle of the Blackwater, having his knee shattered and getting captured by forces loyal to King Joffrey I. He bends the knee to Joffrey.
