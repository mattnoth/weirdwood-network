Recent events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Brother Clement of Quiet Isle had taken a vow of silence. However, during the raid on Saltpans, one of the Brave Companions cut his tongue out.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When Qyburn presents the new member of the Kingsguard, Ser Robert Strong, he states that the massive knight has taken a holy vow of silence, swearing not to speak until all of King Tommen's enemies are dead and evil been driven from the realm.
