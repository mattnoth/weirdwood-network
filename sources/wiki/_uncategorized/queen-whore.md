Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

She claimed the crown along with King Cutthroat when Cleon the Second was slain. She is killed along with most of the population of Astapor when the city is besieged by the Yunkai. She reportedly died fighting against the enemies, while King Cutthroat surrendered.
