History[ ]

When the host of blacks led by Ser Garibald Grey and Lord Roderick Dustin marched south from the riverlands to Tumbleton, they were joined by men from the Blackwater Rush, including Moslander. It is unknown if he survived the First Battle of Tumbleton.
