Description[ ]

A ko can be protected by a khas. If a khal dies without a strong successor, his kos might fight amongst themselves and form their own khalasars.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

The kos of Khal Drogo lead their own khalasars after Drogo's death. Daenerys Targaryen, Drogo's khaleesi, names Aggo, Jhogo, and Rakharo as kos of her small band of remaining Dothraki.

Known kos[ ]

Aggo, Jhogo and Rakharo, kos to Daenerys Targaryen.

Jhaqo and Pono, kos to Drogo.

Quotes[ ]

Daenerys: I name you ko, and ask your oath, that you should live and die as blood of my blood, riding at my side to keep me safe from harm.

Aggo: I cannot say these words. Only a man can lead a khalasar or name a ko.

—Daenerys Targaryen and Aggo

Daenerys: Why should I fear Pono? He was Drogo's ko, and always spoke me gently.

Jorah: Ko Pono spoke you gently. Khal Pono will kill you. He was the first to abandon Drogo. Ten thousand warriors went with him. You have a hundred.

—Daenerys Targaryen and Jorah Mormont
