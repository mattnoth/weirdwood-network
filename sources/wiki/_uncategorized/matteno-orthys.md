## Full Text

Matteno Orthys was a nobleman from House Orthys during the Lysene Spring.

Character[ ]

Matteno was a fervent worshipper of the cat-goddess Pantera. He kept a prized shadowcat.

History[ ]

During the bloody power struggle in Lys after the downfall of the Rogare family, the cage of Matteno's shadowcat was unaccountably left open one night, and the cat mauled and partly devoured him.
