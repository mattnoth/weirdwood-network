## Full Text

Lyonce was an archmaester of the Citadel who became Grand Maester under King Aegon I Targaryen. The ring, rod, and mask he wore were gold.

History[ ]

Lyonce was appointed Grand Maester during the reign of King Aegon I following the death of Grand Maester Ollidar, the first Grand Maester of the Seven Kingdoms. Lyonce held the office for seven years, until he slipped, broke his hip, and died.
