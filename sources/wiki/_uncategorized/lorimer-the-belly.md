Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Podrick Payne tells Brienne of Tarth that he once served his cousin, Ser Cedric Payne. When Cedric died during the War of the Five Kings, Podrick attached himself to Lorimer, who was under the command of Lord Leo Lefford and tasked to protect the baggage train. Lorimer was found to have stolen a ham and was hanged as a thief. Ser Kevan Lannister then sent Podrick, whose family name saved him from sharing Lorimer's fate, to Tyrion Lannister to be his squire.
