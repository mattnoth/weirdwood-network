Location[ ]

The Moon Pool is located south of the Sealord's Palace and before the headquarters of the Iron Bank, in the northeastern section of Braavos. The sweetwater river ends at the Moon Pool.

Several of the best alehouses, inns, and brothels of Braavos are located nearby. Bravos often duel at the Moon Pool at night.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

The Moonshadow, a Braavosi courtesan, rewards the Westerosi singer Dareon with a kiss when she hears him perform at the Moon Pool.

Brusco's daughter, Talea, sometimes sells shellfish at the Moon Pool, as does Cat of the Canals.
