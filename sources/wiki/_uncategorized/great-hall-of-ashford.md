Layout[ ]

The Great Hall contains the high seat of House Ashford. Ashford Castle and its Great Hall are rather small. The hall has a side door and a dais. Wool tapestries of fruits and flowers hung on its walls.

According to Ser Duncan the Tall, the Great Hall is dim.

History[ ]

Ser Duncan the Tall was redirected to the Great Hall by an Ashford guard when he was looking for Plummer. Ducan was later brought to the Great Hall when Prince Aerion Targaryen demanded a trial of seven.

The Great Hall was not so great, as halls went, but Ashford was a small castle.
