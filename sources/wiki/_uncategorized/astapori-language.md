## Summary

Astapori is a form of bastard Valyrian spoken in the city of Astapor. Like the mongrel Ghiscari of the other Slaver Cities, the tongue of Astapor is a form of High Valyrian influenced by Old Ghiscari. The dialect of Astapor is close enough to that of Yunkai to be mutually intelligible.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Kraznys mo Nakloz has Missandei translate for him when he meets with Daenerys Targaryen in Astapor.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Maester Kedry is fluent in the mongrel tongue of Slaver's Bay.

When Daenerys, now Queen of Meereen, meets with refugees from the siege of Astapor, she hears Astapori among the various peoples all saying their word for "Mother".
