## Full Text

The High Septon was High Septon of the Faith of the Seven during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen.

Character[ ]

He distrusted bastards even those who have been legitimized, such as the Great Bastards.

History[ ]

Prince Maekar Targaryen once spoke with the High Septon who told him that no man can truly understand the will of the gods.

The High Septon was one of the casualties of the Great Spring Sickness when the disease hit King's Landing. Sefton Staunton served him in the Great Sept of Baelor before moving to Coldmoat.
