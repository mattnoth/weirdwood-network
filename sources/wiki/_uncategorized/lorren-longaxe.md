Appearance[ ]

Lorren wields a longaxe and wears mail.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lorren sails to Deepwood Motte with Asha Greyjoy as she contemplates her next move after the kingsmoot on Old Wyk.

When five northmen try to infiltrate Deepwood, Lorren kills one of them with his longaxe. Asha has Lorren and Six-Toed Harl fetch Lady Sybelle Glover and her maester; Lorren strikes the maester with a mailed fist when he protests, breaking his nose. During the fight by Deepwood Motte, Asha spots Lorren on the ground of the wolfswood with one leg twisted beneath him.
