Garris, called Garris the Grey, was a legendary Rhoynish prince of Ny Sar.

History[ ]

Garris drove the hairy men away from the land where Norvos stands today. However, the Rhoynar did not linger in those lands, as they preferred the more temperate climes of the lower river to the dark skies and cold winds of the hills.
