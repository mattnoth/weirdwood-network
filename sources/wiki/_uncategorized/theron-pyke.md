## Full Text

Theron, born Theron Pyke, was a maester of the Citadel who lived a century ago.

History[ ]

Theron Pyke was born a bastard of the Iron Islands. In his manuscript Strange Stone, Maester Theron postulated the existence of Deep Ones, which he described as a "queer, misshapen race of half men sired by creatures of the salt seas upon human women". Theron suggested they inspired the legend of merlings and that the sea creatures who fathered them inspired the religion of the Drowned God.

Theron also noted a certain likeness between the black stone of the ancient fortress on Battle Isle that serves as foundation to the Hightower and that of the Seastone Chair, both of unknown origin. He postulated they had been constructed by the Deep Ones.
