Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

At the Inn of the Kneeling Man, Husband explains that Ser Warren has died, and he does not know who is now holding his towerhouse during the War of the Five Kings. Brienne of Tarth, Ser Jaime Lannister, and Ser Cleos Frey see lights in the towerhouse as they travel, so they keep their distance.
