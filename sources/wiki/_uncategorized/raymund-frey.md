A Storm of Swords[ ]

  

Raymund approaching Catelyn Stark, by nejna ©

Ser Raymund is introduced to Lady Catelyn Stark at the Twins when he is sitting in the great hall, drinking with his half-brother Lame Lothar, his brother Ser Hosteen, his goodbrother Lord Lucias Vypren, nephew Ser Damon Vypren, and the Haighs.

The next day, Raymund attends the wedding feast of Lord Edmure Tully and Roslin Frey. Once the Red Wedding begins, the Smalljon defends himself by striking Raymund in the face with a leg of mutton. After Lord Roose Bolton stabs King Robb Stark, Catelyn saws the neck of Raymund's half-nephew, Jinglebell. When the mad Catelyn then begins clawing her own face, Raymund slits her throat from ear to ear.
