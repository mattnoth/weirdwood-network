Colen of Greenpools is a knight from Greenpools.

In the television adaptation Game of Thrones, Colen is portrayed by Darren Killeen.

Appearance and Character[ ]

Colen is grizzled, with a grey beard. His coat-of-arms has bluejays.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser Colen meets Lady Catelyn Tully's party at the head of Renly Baratheon's outriders. He escorts Catelyn to Renly's camp at Bitterbridge, where they see the end of a great melee.
