## Summary

Morosh is a city in northern Essos along the shore of the Shivering Sea. A fishing and mining colony of Lorath, Morosh is situated at the northern end of the Sarne river delta. It is northeast of its trading partner Saath and northwest of the ruined Vaes Graddakh. Braavos allows fisherfolk from Morosh to fish the Shivering Sea.
