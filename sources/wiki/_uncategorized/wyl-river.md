## Full Text

Wyl

Dorne and the location of the Wyl

The Wyl or River Wyl is a river in the Red Mountains of northern Dorne. The ruins of Vulture's Roost are near the source of the river, which flows eastward past the Boneway to the castle of Wyl and the Sea of Dorne.

History[ ]

During the First Dornish War, Lord Orys Baratheon was captured by the Wyl of Wyl when his column was ambushed by Dornish archers where the Boneway crosses the Wyl.
