Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Donnor is a bannerman of Balon Greyjoy, Lord of the Iron Islands. Balon declares himself King of the Isles and the North during the War of the Five Kings.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Donnor is a bannerman of Euron Greyjoy, who was chosen as Balon's successor at the kingsmoot on Old Wyk.
