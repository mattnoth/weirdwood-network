## Full Text

Sylas the Grim was a free folk raider during King Aegon III Targaryen's regency.

History[ ]

In 133 AC, Sylas led three thousand free folk against the Wall, overwhelming the black brothers at Queensgate and spreading out across the Gift. Lord Cregan Stark rode forth from Winterfell and joined with the Glovers, the Flints, the Norreys, and a hundred rangers from the Night's Watch to hunt Sylas's men and put an end to them.
