## Full Text

The Fossoway twins were members of House Fossoway of Cider Hall during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen. Their given names have not been revealed.

History[ ]

In 73 AC, the Fossoway twins entered the list in a tourney at Old Oak hosted by Lord Oakheart in honor of the birth of a son. Both of them were defeated by a mystery knight known as the "Silver Fool", which was later revealed to be Prince Baelon Targaryen.
