Denys Harte was a knight from House Harte during the reign of King Aegon III Targaryen.

History[ ]

Denys was part of Lord Manfryd Mooton's diplomatic mission to Braavos in 133 AC. According to Mushroom, Denys hired the Faceless Men to assassinate a rival in King's Landing.
