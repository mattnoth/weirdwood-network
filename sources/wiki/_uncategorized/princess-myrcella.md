## Full Text

Princess Myrcella is one of Cersei's dromonds in service to House Baratheon of King's Landing. It is named after Princess Myrcella Baratheon by King Tommen I Bartheon.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Princess Myrcella is among the ships being built as part of Queen Regent Cersei Lannister's plan to increase the size of the royal fleet instead of relying on the traditional sea powers to protect the crown's interests. King Tommen I Baratheon intends to name it Moon Boy after the fool of the Red Keep, but Aurane Waters convinces the king to name the ship after his sister.

During her imprisonment by the Faith of the Seven, Cersei is visited by Qyburn, who tells the queen that Aurane fled King's Landing with the new ships after her arrest.
