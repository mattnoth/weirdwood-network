Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Quentyn Martell sees sardines being sold at Fishermonger's Square in Volantis, and the city's widow of the waterfront has sardines as part of her breakfeast. Sardines fried crisp in pepper oil is part of a breakfast enjoyed by Arya Stark at the House of Black and White in Braavos.
