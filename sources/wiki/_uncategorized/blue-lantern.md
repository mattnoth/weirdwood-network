About[ ]

The Blue Lantern's neighborhood is more fashionable than that of the Gate, and the offerings of the Blue Lantern are considered subtler and more poetic than the Gate's.  When Izembaro first dubbed himself the King of the Mummers, the Blue Lantern's troupe of mummers were outraged. Quence and Allaquo are not good enough for the Blue Lantern.
