Supporting evidence[ ]

He himself claims to be a bastard of House Lannister.

Tywin is known to have sex with prostitutes, as with Shae.

Additionally, Varys states that a Hand of the King ordered the construction of a secret passageway to Chataya's brothel, the same brothel that the yellow-haired and green-eyed prostitute Marei works at.

He has physical and characteristic similarities to other Lannisters of Tywin's line.

He has yellow hair that he wears down in a tumble, a common trait of the Lannisters of Casterly Rock.

He has fat lips, a trait he shares with Joffrey Baratheon.

He makes easy japes, a trait he shares with Tyrion and Jaime Lannister.

When asked if Tywin had any bastard offspring, George R. R. Martin did not deny this possibility, merely answering that "[Tywin]'d have you flogged for the mere suggestion."

Evidence against[ ]

No character in the books ever mentions the existence of an acknowledged bastard son of Tywin.

Given the above-quoted answer by George R. R. Martin, it is likely that Tywin would never acknowledge a bastard child as his own.

It is possible that Donnel Hill is a son of another member of House Lannister.
