For the dragon, see Shrykos.

Shrykes are a race of legendary creatures said to inhabit the land beyond the Five Forts, east of the southern half of the Bleeding Sea and south of K'Dath.

Shrykes are said to be half-human beings with green-scaled skin and venomous bites. They supposedly fear the city of K'Dath.
