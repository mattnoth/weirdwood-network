Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

When Ramsay Bolton hosts Arnolf Karstark and Hother Umber at the Dreadfort, the Bastard's Boys occupy the best seats near the dais. They accompany Ramsay to the siege of Moat Cailin and help him search for the missing Frey, Rhaegar, and Symond Frey near Barrowton. Although Ramsay favors them, Lord Roose Bolton claims to Theon Greyjoy that they in fact report to him. 

The Bastard's Boys follow Ramsay around at Winterfell, and they toss a freerider from the castle's battlements.
