## Summary

The Stone Road is a mountain pass in central Essos. Originating in Vaes Dothrak, it runs from the ruined city Vaes Jini (Yinishar) through the northern Bone Mountains to Samyriana, which lies on the eastern side of the mountain range. It then possibly heads north to Kayakayanaya and south to Bayasabhad between the Bone Mountains and the Great Sand Sea.
