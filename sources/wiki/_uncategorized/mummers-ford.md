## Summary

The Mummer's Ford is a crossing of the river Trident, along the Red Fork, in the western riverlands. Upriver of Sherrer, it has a town along its banks. The location of the Mummer's Ford has not yet been published on maps, although it may be north of Pinkmaiden Castle.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

The town is destroyed at the beginning of the War of the Five Kings by Ser Gregor Clegane's raiders from the westerlands, who have already attacked Sherrer. All of the inhabitants are killed. Ser Karyl Vance, Ser Raymun Darry, and Ser Marq Piper ride to King's Landing to inform Lord Eddard Stark, the Hand of the King.

Eddard sends Lord Beric Dondarrion to arrest Gregor, but Beric's detachment is ambushed by the Mountain's men in a battle at the Mummer's Ford.
