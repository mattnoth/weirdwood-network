Appearance[ ]

Lamprey is stooped and has yellowish skin. He has greasy unwashed hair and pebbled skin. He is a brusque gaoler.

Lamprey wears a doublet of white velvet with a ring of golden stars upon it. He may thus have been a servant of House Sunglass or took it from someone affiliated with that House, as Lord Guncer Sunglass was burned by Melisandre.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

While Ser Davos Seaworth is imprisoned beneath Dragonstone, one of his gaolers is Lamprey. Though not his real name, he receives the nickname Lamprey because he once brings Davos half a lamprey pie. Davos finds the other gaoler, Porridge, to be more friendly than Lamprey.
