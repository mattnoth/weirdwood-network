## Summary

Lorra Royce was a noblewoman from House Royce who married Lord Beron Stark, the second son of Lord Brandon Stark. Lorra and Beron had seven children: Donnor, Willam, Artos, Berena, Alysanne, Errold, and Rodrik Stark.
