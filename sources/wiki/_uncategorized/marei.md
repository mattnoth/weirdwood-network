Appearance and Character[ ]

Marei has green eyes and porcelain skin. Tyrion Lannister describes her long straight hair as being silvery on one occasion and pale white-gold on another. Marei is older than Shae.

The solemn Marei is literate.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Tyrion Lannister notices Marei once or twice during his visits to Chataya's brothel. Marei bets that Dancy, a fellow whore at Chataya's, can not convince Tyrion to come upstairs with her. If she had won, she would have received Dancy's black pearls. Marei teaches some of the other prostitutes how to read.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

After receiving his knighthood, Ser Bronn of the Blackwater visits Chataya's brothel and sleeps with Marei and Alayaya simultaneously. Marei is suggested by Tyrion as a possible partner for Prince Oberyn Martell when he comes to the capital, but the Red Viper sleeps with Alayaya instead.
