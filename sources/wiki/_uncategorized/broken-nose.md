Appearance[ ]

The big guard, who has a broken nose, wears black ringmail and a golden cloak.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Six gold cloaks search for Gendry at a village in the crownlands. One of them, a big lout with a broken nose, bares his steel but is challenged by a number of Yoren's recruits. Broken Nose laughs at Arya Stark when she enters a water dancer's pose with Needle. Yoren disarms the officer leading the gold cloaks and sends them into retreat down the kingsroad.
