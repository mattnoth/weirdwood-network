Character[ ]

The gallant Mychel is considered one of the best young swordsmen in the Vale of Arryn.

Mychel has a relationship with Mya Stone, a guide on the Giant's Lance. He has said that her father must have been a goat, and that she has the eyes of an owl.

History[ ]

Mychel took Mya Stone's virginity.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Mya Stone tells Lady Catelyn Stark that she will wed Mychel, the squire of Ser Lyn Corbray, when he is given his knighthood. However, Catelyn doubts a member of House Redfort would ever marry a bastard.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Mychel cares for Mya until his father gives him no choice and marries the newly anointed knight to one of Bronze Yohn Royce's daughters, Ysilla Royce.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Mychel arrives at the Gates of the Moon to compete in Lord Robert Arryn's tourney for a place in the Brotherhood of Winged Knights. He is one of the three competitors who are wed. According to Alayne Stone, he is one of the favorites to win. Mychel's presence in the castle causes sorrow to his former lover, Mya Stone.
