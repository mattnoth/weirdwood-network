The arms of House Tallhart include three sentinels.

Sentinel is a type of tall coniferous evergreen tree in Westeros. It has grey-green needles and produces a sweet, sticky sap from its thick trunk. Sentinels have a sharp piney smell.

Sentinel trees are common in the north, including the wolfswood and the northern mountains, and in the haunted forest in the lands beyond the Wall. Sentinels are also found in the crownlands, the riverlands, the rainwood in the stormlands, and the slopes of the Giant's Lance in the Vale of Arryn.

Culture[ ]

Three green sentinel trees are used on the coat-of-arms of House Tallhart. Sentinel Stand is an abandoned castle of the Night's Watch along the Wall; it is unknown if it is named after the tree.

History[ ]

Bran Stark once slept overnight in the tallest sentinel in the godswood of Winterfell. Theon Greyjoy lost his virginity when he slept with a woman beneath the tree.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

While ranging beyond the Wall, Will climbs a sentinel and observes Ser Waymar Royce being slain by Others.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

During the capture of Winterfell, Summer climbs a tall sentinel in a failed attempt to escape the castle's godswood. Theon Greyjoy passes sentinels in the wolfswood during his search for the missing Bran and Rickon Stark.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Jarl begins his attempted ascent of the Wall by climbing a sentinel.
