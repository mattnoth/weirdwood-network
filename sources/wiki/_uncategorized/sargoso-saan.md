## Full Text

Sargoso Saan was a  Lysene pirate king in the Stepstones during the reign of Aegon I Targaryen. Sargoso was a member of the Saan family.

History[ ]

In 29 AC and 30 AC, Prince Maegor Targaryen, Lord Aethan Velaryon, and Ser Osmund Strong led punitive campaigns to drive out Sargoso from the Stepstones.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

Sargoso's name may be a reference by George R. R. Martin to the Sargasso Sea.
