## Full Text

Balon Blackskin is a legendary ironborn raider who supposedly lived during the Age of Heroes.

History[ ]

According to legend, Balon fought with an axe in his left hand and a hammer in his right. He was impervious to injuries from any man-made weapon: swords glanced off leaving no mark, while axes shattered against his skin.
