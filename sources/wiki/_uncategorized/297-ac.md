## Full Text

Year 297 After the Conquest (297 AC) is a year in the Westerosi calendar, the 297th after Aegon's crowning in Oldtown that maesters and educated people use as a reference to date the years.

297 AC was a quiet year, but is marked by the first recorded return of the Others in Westeros since the Long Night. This was further marked by rangers from the Night's Watch disappearing more and more. Further, 297 AC saw the fortunes of Viserys Targaryen and his sister Daenerys going up, as magister Illyrio Mopatis took them in his care.

The prologue from A Game of Thrones takes place in 297 AC.

Events[ ]

Euron Greyjoy sleeps with the third wife of his brother Victarion and is exiled from the Iron Islands by his eldest brother, Lord Balon Greyjoy.

The city of Meereen suffers from a plague and many slaves die.

Daenerys Targaryen and her brother Viserys Targaryen are taken in by Magister Illyrio Mopatis in Pentos.

Births[ ]

Osmund Frey, son of Ser Benfrey Frey and Jyanna Frey.

Deaths[ ]

Ser Waymar Royce, slain by Others in the haunted forest.

Domeric Bolton, due to a "bad belly". It has been speculated by his father, Roose Bolton, that Domeric's bastard half-brother Ramsay Snow poisoned Domeric.

Will, slain by the wight of Waymar Royce in the haunted forest.
