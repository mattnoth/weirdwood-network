Range[ ]

Lampreys have been found in the narrow sea, near White Harbor, in the lagoon of Braavos, and in the Shivering Sea.

Culture[ ]

White Harbor has an inn famous for its lamprey pies. According to Mors Umber, Lord Wyman Manderly is mocked by some of his own people as Lord Lamprey.

History[ ]

According to Septon Eustace, Queen Rhaenyra Targaryen grew stout from eating sweetmeats, cakes, and lamprey pie while ruling King's Landing during the Dance of the Dragons.

Rhaegel Targaryen choked to death on lamprey pie.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lady Tanda Stokeworth serves lamprey pie and roast suckling pig to Lord Petyr Baelish.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Tanda repeatedly offers lamprey pie to Tyrion Lannister. A begging brother in King's Landing preaches against Tyrion, claiming the Hand of the King has grown fat on lark and lamprey while the smallfolk starve. Lamprey pie is one of the courses in a supper enjoyed by Tyrion and his sister, Queen Regent Cersei Lannister.

Lampreys are included in the seafood brought by Lord Wyman Manderly to Winterfell for the harvest feast.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

After he is rescued from Blackwater Bay, Ser Davos Seaworth's stomach cannot tolerate the rich lampreys and snails provided by Khorane Sathmantes. While imprisoned in Dragonstone's dungeon, Davos is served half a lamprey pie by one of his unnamed gaolers, whom he names Lamprey.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Disguised in Braavos, Arya Stark helps Umma prepare lampreys taken from the city's lagoon. Some nights Umma cooks the eels with chopped garlic.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lamprey pie is served at the feast welcoming Ser Wylis Manderly back to White Harbor. Lord Wyman brings barrels of fresh-caught lampreys with him to Barrowton in preparation for the wedding of Ramsay Bolton and Arya Stark.

Lamprey pies are served at the feast welcoming Ser Balon Swann to Sunspear.
