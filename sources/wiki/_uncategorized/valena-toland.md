The Winds of Winter[ ]

Valena greets Princess Arianne Martell and her companions when they arrive at Ghost Hill. At the welcoming feast, when her younger sister, Teora, says dragons are coming, Valena scornfully asks if Teora knows that from one of her "little dreams."
