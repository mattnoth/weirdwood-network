## Full Text

Addison Hill, known as the Bastard of Cornfield, was a bastard knight from House Swyft and a Lord Commander of the Kingsguard.

Character[ ]

Addison is remembered for being quick, strong, observant, and famed for his prowess with sword and shield. He was devoted to his king.

History[ ]

In 10 AC, Ser Addison was chosen by Queen Visenya Targaryen to be one of the first knights of the Kingsguard when the order was founded by King Aegon I Targaryen. He would serve his king with honor and valor until the end of his life.

Despite his bastard origin, Addison eventually rose to become Lord Commander of the Kingsguard. It is unknown when it happened, however. It could have been during the reign of Aegon I or during one of his sons's, Aenys I and Maegor I Targaryen.
