Appearance and Character[ ]

A big man, Ollo wraps the stump of his missing hand in leather. Chett thinks the Tyroshi dialect of High Valyrian spoken by Ollo is a wet, girly tongue.

Ollo is not remorseful about his crimes.

History[ ]

Ollo is from Tyrosh. He lost a hand after being caught for thievery and was sent to the Wall after being found abed with a knight's wife.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

  

Garth of Greenaway and Ollo attacking Jeor Mormont, by Victor Garcia © Fantasy Flight Games

Ollo Lophand is part of the great ranging, the force that leaves Castle Black in search of the wildlings. He is one of the conspirators who intend to kill Jeor Mormont, Lord Commander of the Night's Watch, at the Fist of the First Men. If the conspiracy is successful, he plans to return to Tyrosh. Their scheme is interrupted when wights attack the Fist, however.

Ollo survives the fight at the Fist and is among the men who make it back to Craster's Keep. He and Tim Stone feed and water the surviving horses, and he thinks that Craster is hoarding food. Garth of Greenaway and Ollo wield blades and prevent Jeor from interceding during the mutiny at Craster's Keep. When Jeor reaches for his own dagger, Ollo stabs Jeor, mortally wounding the Lord Commander. Ollo then rapes one of Craster's wives and searches the walls for food.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ollo remains at Craster's Keep after the betrayal, along with a few other former members of the Night's Watch.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Whilst escorting Bran Stark beyond the Wall, Coldhands tracks the Night's Watch traitors and kills them in the haunted forest. Ollo Lophand's one-handed corpse, the largest of them, is found with its throat slit in the snow by Summer, who eats part of the meat.
