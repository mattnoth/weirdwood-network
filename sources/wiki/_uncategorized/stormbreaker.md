## Summary

The character was named in homage to Ser Sarmion Baratheon, the Stormbreaker, a character originated by Benjamin Swierczek on Blood of Dragons MUSH. In the game, Sarmion is one of Lord Borros Baratheon's nephews. Swierczek was an original member of the game's staff, and a friend to Elio García and Linda Antonsson for over twenty years. He passed away on May 13, 2016.
