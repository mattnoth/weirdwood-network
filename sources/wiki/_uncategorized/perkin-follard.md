Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser Perkin stays with Queen Selyse at Eastwatch-by-the-Sea when King Stannis Baratheon goes to take Deepwood Motte. When Selyse leaves Eastwatch, Perkin accompanies her to Castle Black, where she introduces him to Jon Snow, Lord Commander of the Night's Watch.
