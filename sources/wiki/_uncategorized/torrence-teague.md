## Summary

Torrence Teague was an adventurer of uncertain birth who was the eventual victor in the century-long conflict that arose in the riverlands after the death of King Bernarr II Justman. He was the founder of House Teague of the riverlands. Having seized a fortune in gold in an attack upon the westerlands, he used this wealth to bring large numbers of sellswords from across the Narrow Sea. After six years of war, Torrence was crowned King of the Trident at Maidenpool. However, he was so little loved he was forced to keep sons and daughters of the riverlords as hostages to ensure their loyalty.
