About[ ]

The sentence "Khalakka dothrae" in the Dothraki language can be translated in the Common Tongue as "The prince is riding".

Unlike in the feudal Westeros, when a khal dies his heir does not automatically succeed him. He has to prove his strength to the khalasar in order to be accepted by them as their new leader.

Known Khalakkas[ ]

Drogo, heir to Khal Bharbo. He eventually succeeded his father as khal.

Fogo, heir to Khal Ogo. He becomes khal when his father is killed, but is himself slain shortly after.

Rhaego, heir to Khal Drogo, proclaimed to be the stallion who mounts the world, but stillborn following Mirri Maz Duur's bloodmagic ritual.

Rhogoro, heir to Khal Moro.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

According to David J. Peterson, the female child of a khal would be called a khalakki.
