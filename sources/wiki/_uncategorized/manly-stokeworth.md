## Full Text

Manly Stokeworth was a member of House Stokeworth who served as Commander of the City Watch of King's Landing.

History[ ]

Manly once led the gold cloaks. After Manly's death, Lord Jon Arryn, the Hand of the King to Robert I Baratheon, named Janos Slynt—then captain of the Iron Gate—as the new commander. Whether Manly perished in the Sack of King's Landing during Robert's Rebellion, or later on during Robert's reign, is unknown.
