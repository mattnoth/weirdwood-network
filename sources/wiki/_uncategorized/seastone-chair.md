Appearance[ ]

See also: Images of the Seastone Chair

The Seastone Chair is made of an immense block of oily black stone carved into the shape of a great kraken.

History[ ]

Legend says the Seastone Chair was found on the shores of Old Wyk by First Men when they came to the Iron Islands. Archmaester Haereg pondered that the chair was created by a people who inhabited the isles before the First Men, though he eventually dismissed the idea. Maester Kirth speculated in Songs the Drowned Men Sing that the chair was left by a people from across the Sunset Sea. Noting similarities between the Seastone Chair and the fused black stone at the base of the Hightower, Maester Theron suggested in Strange Stone that they were the work of so-called Deep Ones.

House Greyjoy of Pyke has ruled the Iron Islands since Aegon's Conquest. It is unknown if extinct iron king dynasties, such as the Greyirons or Hoares, used the Seastone Chair as their throne.

After Lord Dalton Greyjoy was killed by Tess at Faircastle without an heir, the Seastone Chair passed by law to his oldest salt son, Toron Greyjoy.

The Seastone Chair is now located on a dais in the Great Hall of the Great Keep of Pyke, the seat of Lord Balon Greyjoy. His younger brother Aeron Damphair, a priest of the Drowned God, claims that only a pious ironman can sit the throne, not an ungodly man or a woman.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

  

Balon Greyjoy by Amok ©

Lord Balon Greyjoy declares himself King of the Isles and the North during the War of the Five Kings.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

The day after Balon falls to his death while crossing Pyke, his brother Euron returns to the Iron Islands and claims the Seastone Chair. When Lord Sawane Botley objects, Euron Crow's Eye has him drowned.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Opposed to his hated brother Euron succeeding Balon, Aeron Greyjoy insists that a kingsmoot be held to determine the new king, but the gathering on Old Wyk confirms Euron as king.

Euron offers to give the Seastone Chair to his brother Victarion, Lord Captain of the Iron Fleet, once Euron claims the Iron Throne.
