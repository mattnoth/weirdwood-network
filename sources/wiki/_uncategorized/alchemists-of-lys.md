## Summary

The alchemists of Lys are master poisoners renowned for preparing lethal substances, such as the tears of Lys. The Lysene practitioners of alchemy are also familiar with the strangler, though they refer to the crystal form of the poison by a different name.

## Full Text

The alchemists of Lys are master poisoners renowned for preparing lethal substances, such as the tears of Lys. The Lysene practitioners of alchemy are also familiar with the strangler, though they refer to the crystal form of the poison by a different name.

Malwyn Frey, second son of Ser Raymund Frey and one of Lord Walder Frey's grandsons, is an apprentice to an alchemist of Lys.
