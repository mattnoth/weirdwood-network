Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Jeor Mormont, Lord Commander of the Night's Watch, plans to re-garrison Greyguard, but Qhorin Halfhand advises Stonedoor instead, due to Greyguard's poor condition.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Jon Snow is with a group of wildlings who attempt to climb the Wall between Stonedoor and Greyguard.

A Dance for Dragons[ ]

Now Lord Commander, Jon gives Stonedoor to Soren Shieldbreaker to garrison and settle his own people. Borroq is scheduled to join them.
