Layout[ ]

The immense longhall is drafty even when a fire burns in its great hearth. Crows nest in the lofty ceiling timbers. Hobb prepares meals for the members of the Night's Watch, and black brothers often drink mulled wine in the hall.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Jon Snow takes his meals in the common hall. Jon introduces Samwell Tarly to Ghost in the hall.

Jon learns from Pypar about the death of King Robert I Baratheon. When Ser Alliser Thorne mocks Jon about the arrest of Eddard Stark, Jon leaps onto a table and has to be restrained from attacking Alliser by his friends.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

During the attack on Castle Black by wildlings, Jon sees the common hall's doors on fire, and then the timbered keep itself burns.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Since the common hall has burned to the ground, the Night's Watch take their meals in one of the vaults of Castle Black, a stone cellar below the armory.
