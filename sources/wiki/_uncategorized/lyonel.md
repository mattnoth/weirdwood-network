## Full Text

Lyonel is a popular male name in Westeros. It may refer to:

Ser Lyonel, a knight in the Lannister camp at Harrenhal

Ser Lyonel Baratheon, called "the Laughing Storm", a competitor at the Ashford tourney

Ser Lyonel Bentley, Queensguard to Rhaenyra Targaryen

Lord Lyonel Corbray, Lord of Heart's Home and head of House Corbray

Lord Lyonel Deddings, Lord and head of House Deddings during the Dance of the Dragons

Ser Lyonel Frey, second son of Ser Emmon Frey and Lady Genna Lannister

Lord Lyonel Hightower, Lord of Oldtown during King Aegon III's regency

Ser Lyonel Lorch, a knight during the Faith Militant uprising

Ser Lyonel Selmy, father of Ser Barristan Selmy

Lord Lyonel Strong, former Lord of Harrenhal

Lord Lyonel Tyrell, Lord of Highgarden during the Conquest of Dorne

Lyonel Tyrell, a cousin of Queen Margaery Tyrell
