Household[ ]

Runestone's household includes Maester Helliweg, Septon Lucos, and Ser Samwell Stone.

History[ ]

  

Corwyn Corbray is slain at Runestone by Ertaç Altinöz for The Rise of the Dragon

Runestone was the ancient seat of the Bronze Kings of old from House Royce. After defeating seven Andal longships during the coming of the Andals, King Yorwyck VI Royce placed the heads of his slain foes along the walls of his castle. The Royces submitted to Artys Arryn after the death of King Robar II Royce in the Battle of the Seven Stars.

During the reign of King Aenys I Targaryen, Princess Rhaena Targaryen was seen flying Dreamfyre as far as Runestone. King Jaehaerys I Targaryen visited Runestone during a royal progress in 52 AC. Ser Victor the Valiant was part of the household of Runestone before being named to the Kingsguard through the War for the White Cloaks.

Prince Daemon Targaryen disliked living at Runestone with his first wife, Lady Rhea Royce. He tried to claim Runestone after Rhea's death, however, but his claim was rejected by Lady Jeyne Arryn. Daemon instead married Lady Laena Velaryon and fled with her to the Free Cities.

Alyssa Royce traveled from Runestone to King's Landing for the Maiden's Day Cattle Show. Ser Arnold Arryn, who had squired at Runestone in his youth, fled to the castle when war broke out in the Vale of Arryn after the passing of Lady Jeyne Arryn. Ser Corwyn Corbray, one of the regents of Aegon III, was killed by a crossbowman on the walls of Runestone in 134 AC.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

At Runestone, the Lords Declarant write and sign a document containing their demands for the removal of Lord Petyr Baelish as Lord Protector of the Vale.

During a parley at the Eyrie, the Lords Declarant attempt to have Lord Robert Arryn become Lord Yohn Royce's ward at Runestone. Petyr manipulates them into granting him a year to prove his worth, however.

Bronze Yohn holds a small mêlée for squires only at Runestone. Harrold Hardyng wins and is knighted by Yohn as his reward.
