Jonquil's Tower is a tower at Maidenpool. It is named after Jonquil, a figure from the Age of Heroes.

History[ ]

When Nettles and Sheepstealer parted from Prince Daemon Targaryen and Caraxes at Maidenpool during the Dance of the Dragons, Caraxes let out a scream which shattered every window in Jonquil's Tower.
