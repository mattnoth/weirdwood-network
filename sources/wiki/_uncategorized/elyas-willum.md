Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Elyas is part of Renly Baratheon's army encamped beneath the walls of Bitterbridge. During the feast he argues with his brother, Josua, over who will be the first to scale the walls of King's Landing.

House Willum goes over to Stannis Baratheon after Renly's death. Lord Willum and both his sons fight for Stannis at the Battle of the Blackwater, but are captured, and later bend the knee to King Joffrey I Baratheon.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

House Willum is a tribute by George R. R. Martin to the Memory, Sorrow, and Thorn series of his colleague Tad Williams. The story includes the feuding royal brothers, Josua and Elias.
