Appearance and Character[ ]

Gerold is a wide man with thick arms and shoulders, but he is not tall. His hair is a dirty blond mop and he has a booming voice.

Lord Grafton is a courteous man.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord Petyr Baelish asks Lord Gerold to send him one of his sons to ward at the Eyrie with Lord Robert Arryn. Gerold sends his youngest son, Gyles.

Gerold attends the wedding of Lord Lyonel Corbray at Gulltown.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Gerold and Lord Benedar Belmore tour the granaries of Lord Nestor Royce at the Gates of the Moon. Petyr instructs him to keep grain in reserve instead of selling to Gulltown merchants.
