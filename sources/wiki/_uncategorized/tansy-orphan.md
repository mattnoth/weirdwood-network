Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Tansy is among the orphans taken in by Jeyne and Willow Heddle at the crossroads inn. When Brienne of Tarth and her party are allowed to stay at the inn, Tansy is sent alongside Pate to get wood for the inn's fire.
