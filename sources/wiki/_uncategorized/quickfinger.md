## Full Text

Quickfinger was a the nickname of a thief who lived during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen. 

History[ ]

During the First Blackfyre Rebellion, Quickfinger sided with the rebels, supporting Daemon Blackfyre. Quickfinger was caught with stolen dragon eggs. Later, a former rebel lamented that if Quickfinger had not been caught, it might have changed the tide of the war.
