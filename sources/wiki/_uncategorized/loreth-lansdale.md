## Full Text

Loreth Lansdale was a knight from House Lansdale and a member of Rhaenyra Targaryen's Queensguard.

History[ ]

During the Dance of the Dragons, Ser Loreth sided with the blacks. After Rhaenyra seized King's Landing, Ser Loreth was made one of her Queensguard. He was one of the Seven Who Rode, seven knights that went after Prince Joffrey Velaryon when the prince mounted the dragon Syrax to fly to the Dragonpit, during the Riot of King's Landing.

Ser Loreth was killed in defense of Rhaenyra by the men of Ser Alfred Broome at Dragonstone. He managed to kill two of his opponents before falling. He was the last of Rhaenyra's Queensguard.
