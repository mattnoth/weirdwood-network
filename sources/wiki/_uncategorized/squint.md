## Summary

Squint is among Theon Greyjoy's force that participates in the harrying of the Stony Shore and the capture of Winterfell. While he and Drennan are on guard duty at the Hunter's Gate, Squint is killed on the wallwalk by the direwolves Summer and Shaggydog. Since Squint's body is found in the moat, Theon conjectures that Osha pushed his remains into the water.
