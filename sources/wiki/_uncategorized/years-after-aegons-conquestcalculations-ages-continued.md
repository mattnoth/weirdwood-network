## Full Text

Due to editing issues caused by the length of the calculations, the list is divided into four pages. Use the navigation box, or navigate here to page 1, page 3 or page 4. You are currently on page 2.

In the main series and novellas, George R.R. Martin on most occasions does not state specifically in which year a character has been born or has died. This page lists the calculations to determine such years, years of birth mostly, but also years of death at times, as specific as is possible, for all characters for all this is possible.
This information then can be used in articles and serves to give a more complete image of the world of A Song of Ice and Fire.

The following guidelines are used for the calculations:

A character, both male and female, will be at least 12 years old at the birth of his or her first born child, for the following reason: a girl who has had her first flowering (i.e. first menstruation) is considered to be fit for both marriage and consummation of the marriage. Most highborn girls have their first flowering at the age of twelve or thirteen, indicating twelve as the youngest a girl can be for getting pregnant, and, as such, give birth. For boys, the same minimum age is used. Furthermore, we are given the precedence of the age of twelve for both males and females: the daughter of Unwin Peake died in childbirth at the age of twelve, while Viserys II Targaryen impregnated his wife at the age of twelve.

Siblings are assumed to be at least one year apart in age, taking into consideration that a woman who has just given birth will not immediately become pregnant again. This is only when discussing full-blood siblings. For siblings with the same father, but with a different mother, no such recovery period is required.

Where historic works are concerned which have been written by maesters, or sources recited from works written by maesters, it can be assumed that the age of a character stated in a year is the age that character would be turning that year. For example, Maegor I Targaryen was born in 12 AC. In historic works, his age in the year 25 AC will be stated as thirteen (the age he would be turning that year), without the author taking into account whether or not the described event took place before or after Maegor's nameday. This goes for historical works (i.e. The Sons of the Dragon, The Rogue Prince, The Princess and the Queen, Fire & Blood, and The World of Ice & Fire), not for POV-information, like A Game of Thrones and its sequels, or The Hedge Knight, and other tales of Dunk & Egg. Do note that on some occasions, this rule might not apply.

The following minimum ages for pages, squires, and knights are used, based on an overview of known ages:

Boys are considered to be at least six years old when becoming a page.

Boys are considered to be at least eight years old when becoming a squire.

Men are considered to be at least fifteen years old when becoming a knight.

A mother will be no older than ~52 years old at the earliest possibility for the birth of her youngest child, for the following reason: the Westerosi consider the age of forty-two to be "past [a woman's] childbearing years", and forty-four to be "well past her childbearing years", although women of forty-four have been known to give birth to a child. In real-life, the end of a woman's fertile cycle (menopause) occurs at an age of 49-52 on average, giving ~52 as a maximum age for giving birth to a child.

The characters are listed alphabetically, using full names. Maesters (like Aemon), will also be listed alphabetically under their birth names, unless those are not known.

Names that belong to several characters (for example, the name Walder Frey) will be listed alphabetically, but will have their nickname or relations listed in parentheses in accordance with their page name, so they can be distinguished from one another.

For calculations on placings of events, see Calculations of Events.

Eddara Tallhart was nine years old in 299 AC.

8 turning 9 in 299 AC, placing her birth in 290 AC

9 turning 10 in 299 AC, placing her birth in 289 AC

No further specifications can be given. Eddara was thus born in either 289 AC or 290 AC.

Eddard Stark[ ]

Birth[ ]

Eddard Stark is thirty-five years old in 298 AC. That gives Ned two options:

34 turning 35, placing his birth in 263 AC

35 turning 36, placing his birth in 262 AC

In addition, Eddard Stark is known to have been eighteen years old in 281 AC, the Year of the False Spring, giving Eddard the same options as above:

17 turning 18, placing his birth in 263 AC

18 turning 19, placing his birth in 262 AC

Eddard Stark was born one year after the birth of his brother, Brandon, who was born in 262 AC. This places Eddard’s birth in 263 AC.

Eddard was thus born in 263 AC.

Death[ ]

Eddard Stark was still alive when the Battle of the Whispering Wood was being fought. This battle occurred in 299 AC (see The War of the Five Kings). Since Eddard was still alive during this battle, his death occurred after.

Eddard thus died in 299 AC.

Edgar Yronwood[ ]

Death[ ]

Edgar Yronwood died shortly after a duel he fought against Prince Oberyn Martell, who, at the time, was sixteen years old. Oberyn Martell was born in either 257 AC or 258 AC (see Oberyn Martell). That gives Oberyn the following possible years to have been sixteen years old:

If born in 257 AC, Oberyn would be 15 turning 16 in 273 AC

If born in 257 AC, Oberyn would be 16 turning 17 in 274 AC

If born in 258 AC, Oberyn would be 15 turning 16 in 274 AC

If born in 258 AC, Oberyn would be 16 turning 17 in 275 AC

No further specifications can be given. Edgar thus died in either 273 AC, 274 AC or 275 AC.

Edmure Tully[ ]

Birth[ ]

Edmure Tully is the youngest child of Hoster Tully. He is younger than both his sisters, Catelyn and Lysa. Lysa was younger than Catelyn, and was born in 266 AC the earliest (see Lysa Tully). This means that Edmure, being younger than Lysa, cannot have been born before 267 AC. 

According to Walder Frey, he had approached Hoster Tully offering one of her daughters as a bride for Edmure, adding he had in a mind a specific daughter only a few years older than Edmure. Catelyn later repeats that tale, and Edmure himself supports that Walder had wanted him to marry one of his daughters. There are two Walder's daughters who fit the description: Morya, born in 268 AC or 269 AC (see Morya Frey), and Tyta, born in 269 AC or 270 AC (see Tyta Frey). 270 AC is thus Edmure's earliest possible year of birth, as he would have been at least two years younger than the earliest possible year of birth of these two girls (268 AC, Morya's first possible year of birth).

In 282 AC, Edmure was a squire to Brandon Stark in his duel against Petyr Baelish. It is known that, while squire's are usually somewhat older, even boys as young as eight can be a squire. This shows that Edmure would have been at least eight years old in 282 AC, placing his birth no later than 274 AC. 

No further specifications can be given. Edmure was thus born in or between 270 AC and 274 AC.

Edric Dayne[ ]

Birth[ ]

Edric Dayne is estimated by Arya Stark to be ten or twelve in 299 AC. Edric admits that he is indeed twelve years old in 299 AC. That gives Edric the following options:

11 turning 12 in 299 AC, placing his birth in 287 AC

12 turning 13 in 299 AC, placing his birth in 286 AC

In 300 AC, Edric is still twelve years old. That shows that Edric cannot have been twelve turning thirteen in 299 AC, eliminating option 286 AC, and thus confirms that Edric was eleven turning twelve in 299 AC, placing his birth in 287 AC.

Edric was thus born in 287 AC.

Edric Stark[ ]

Birth[ ]

Edric Stark's father was not yet married in 58 AC. This means that Edric could not have been born before 58 AC.

In 72 AC, Edric inherited the title of Lord of Winterfell after his grandfather's death.

No further specifications can be given. Edric was thus born in or between 58 AC and 72 AC.

Death[ ]

In 72 AC, Edric Stark inherited the title of Lord of Winterfell after his grandfather's death, demonstrating he could not have died any earlier than 72 AC.

Ellard Stark was the Lord of Winterfell in 101 AC, meaning that Edric had died by then.

No further specifications can be given. Edric thus died in or between 72 AC and 101 AC.

Edric Stark (son of Cregan)[ ]

Birth[ ]

Edric Stark was the second child of Cregan Stark by his third wife, Lady Lynara Stark. He would have been at least one year younger than his older brother Jonnel. As Jonnel was born no earlier than 136 AC (see Jonnel Stark), Edric had been born no earlier than one year later, meaning that Edric could not have been born before 137 AC.

Edric would have been at least one year older than his younger sister, Lyanna Stark. As the latest possible year of birth for Lyanna is 181 AC (see Lyanna Stark (daughter of Cregan)), Edric would have been born no later than 180 AC.

No further specifications can be given. Edric was thus born in or between 137 AC and 180 AC.

Death[ ]

Edric Stark died before his brother Jonnel did, as Jonnel was succeeded as Lord of Winterfell by their younger brother Barthogan. Jonnel died no later than 209 AC (see Jonnel Stark), meaning that Edric would have died no later than 209 AC as well.

Edric cannot cannot have died earlier than the year before his youngest child, Argelle Stark, was born. Argelle was born no earlier than 154 AC (see Argelle Stark), so Edric died no earlier than 153 AC.

No further specifications can be given. Edric thus died in or between 153 AC and 209 AC.

Edric Storm[ ]

Birth[ ]

Edric Storm is said to be "around Bran's age" in 298 AC, when Bran has already turned eight. In 299 AC, Edric is said to be seven or eight years old. 

Edric Storm was conceived during the wedding of Stannis Baratheon and Selyse Florent. Since Shireen Baratheon was born during the marriage, Edric would thus be at least Shireen’s age, if not older. Shireen Baratheon was born in 289 AC (see Shireen Baratheon). This means that Edric was born no later than 289 AC.

Edric is stated to have been twelve years old in 299 AC. That would give him the following options:

11 turning 12 in 299 AC, placing his birth in 287 AC

12 turning 13 in 299 AC, placing his birth in 286 AC

Devan Seaworth is older than Edric Storm. Devan is known to be eleven years old in late 299 AC, and twelve years old in 300 AC, placing his birth in either 287 AC or 288 AC. As Devan is older than Edric, Edric cannot have been born in 286 AC, as that would make him older than Devan. Thus, 286 AC is eliminated, leaving only 287 AC as a possible option.

Edric Storm was thus born in 287 AC.

Note: Due to Edric's birth having occurred in 287 AC, Devan's option of 288 AC is eliminated, placing Devan's birth in 287 AC as well (see Devan Seaworth). As Devan is older than Edric, this means that Devan was born earlier in the year 287 AC than Edric.

Edwyle Stark[ ]

Birth[ ]

Edwyle Stark would have been at least one year older than his younger sister, Jocelyn. As Jocelyn had been born no later than 227 AC (see Jocelyn Stark), Edwyle thus was born no later than 226 AC.

Edwyle's older half-brother, Brandon Stark, was born no earlier than 197 AC (see Brandon Stark (son of Willam)). Half siblings with different mothers may share a birth year, therefore Edwyle was also born no earlier than 197 AC.

No further specifications can be given. Edwyle was thus born in or between 197 AC and 226 AC.

Death[ ]

By 262 AC, Edwyle's son Rickard was the Lord of Winterfell, indicating that Edwyle had died by 262 AC.

Rickard was born no earlier than 230 AC (see Rickard Stark), indicating that Edwyle cannot have died no earlier than late during the previous year, in 229 AC.

No further specifications can be given. Edwyle thus died in or between 229 AC and 262 AC.

Edwyn Frey[ ]

Birth[ ]

Edwyn Frey's father Ryman Frey was born no earlier than 245 AC (see Ryman Frey). Ryman would have been at least twelve years old when Edwyn was born. With 245 AC as the earliest possible year of birth for Ryman, Edwyn was born no earlier than twelve years later, in 257 AC.

Edwyn himself was at least twelve years old when his daughter was born. His daughter Walda was born in either 290 AC or 291 AC (see Walda Frey (daughter of Edwyn)). With 291 AC as the last possible year of birth for Walda, Edwyn would have been born at least twelve years prior, in 279 AC.

No further specifications can be given. Edwyn Frey was thus born in or between 257 AC and 279 AC.

Elaena Targaryen was said to be more beautiful at seventy than seventeen, so she must have lived to at least 220 AC.

No further specifications can be given. Elaena thus died in 220 AC or after.

Elaena Targaryen (daughter of Gaemon)[ ]

Birth[ ]

Elaena Targaryen's eldest child, Maegon Targaryen, was born no later than 68 BC (see Maegon Targaryen). Elaena would have been at least twelve years old at the birth, she would have been born no later than 80 BC.

When Elaena's grandfather, Aenar Targaryen, fled Valyria for Dragonstone in 114 BC, he took with him all his kin, including his children. Elaena was not yet born at the time, therefore she would have been born no earlier than 114 BC.

No further specifications can be given. Elaena was thus born in or between 114 BC and 80 BC.

No further specifications can be given. Elinor was thus born in or between 103 AC and 121 AC.

As Jon ruled in his own right, this means that by 281 AC at least, Jon had turned sixteen, indicating that Jon could not have been born after 265 AC.

Tywin Lannister was appointed as Hand of the King at the age of twenty, and was thereby the youngest man ever to become Hand in the history of the Seven Kingdoms. This means that Jon was at least twenty-one years old when he was appointed Hand of the Kind in 282 AC, placing his birth no later than 261 AC.

No further specifications can be given. Jon Connington was thus born in either 260 AC or 261 AC.

Note: Rhaegar Targaryen visits Griffin's Roost on his way back from Dorne, when Jon's father is still alive. Though there are plenty of reasons why Rhaegar could have been in Dorne, it is possible Rhaegar had gone to Sunspear to meet with the Martells regarding his betrothal or upcoming wedding to Elia Martell (betrothal announced in early 279 AC, wedding in early 280 AC). In this case, Rhaegar's visit to Griffin's Roost would have taken place in either 278 AC (after the failure of Steffon Baratheon's mission) or 279 AC. This would suggest that Jon's father died in either 278 AC, 279 AC, 280 AC or 281 AC, as Jon was already ruling Griffin's Roost by the tourney at Harrenhal in 281 AC. Jon mentions Rhaegar had been young, and he himself even younger, at Rhaegar's visit to Griffin's Roost. If the meeting indeed took place in 278/279 AC, Jon Connington would have been between the ages of sixteen and nineteen, based on the two possible years of birth for Jon.

Rumoured death[ ]

Jon Connington is mentioned to have left the Golden Company a dozen years ago in 300 AC. Later, this is specified as to actually have been twelve years, placing Jon's "exile" from the Golden Company in 288 AC, five years after the Rebellion ended.

Jon Connnigton had spent five years with the Golden Company. For him to have spent five years with the Golden Company during his exile, with his exile beginning only in 283 AC, this year is the first possible moment he could have joined. Since 288 AC is the year he certainly left the company, it shows that Jon joined the Golden Company within the first year of his exile, in 283 AC.

Jon Fossoway[ ]

Birth[ ]

Jon Fossoway was a knight in 299 AC, meaning he was at least 15 years old. Therefore Jon was born in or before 284 AC.

Jon Snow[ ]

Birth[ ]

Jon Snow is fourteen years old when the direwolves are found and during the welcoming feast in Winterfell. Both events occur in 298 AC. After he has turned fifteen, Tyrion Lannister and Yoren start going south. When Yoren arrives at King's Landing, Arya has just witnessed a conversation between Varys and Illyrio Mopatis, concerning, amongst others, Margaery Tyrell, who is mentioned to still be fourteen years old. This means that this event still takes place in 298 AC, since it is known that Margaery, having been born in 283 AC, is fourteen turning fifteen in 298 AC (see Margaery Tyrell). This means that Jon is fourteen turning fifteen in 298 AC, meaning that he was born in 283 AC as well.

Jon Snow was thus born in 283 AC.

Jon Umber (husband of Serena)[ ]

Death[ ]

Jon Umber was the first husband of Serena Stark. Jon cannot have died before the birth of his wife. Serena was born no earlier than 141 AC (see Serena Stark), so Jon could have died no earlier than 141 AC.

Jon would have died before his wife remarried to Edric Stark. Serena and Edric would have married no later than the year their first child, Cregard Stark, was born. Cregard was born no later than 208 AC (see Cregard Stark), so Jon died no later than 208 AC.

No further specifications can be given. Jon thus died in or between 141 AC and 208 AC.

Jon Waters[ ]

Birth[ ]

Jon Waters was born the first, together with his twin, Jeyne, of the children of Princess Elaena Targaryen. Elaena gave birth to her twins after having been freed from the Maidenvault, which occured in 171 AC. Thus, 171 AC is the first possible year of Jon's birth.

Jon was born before his mother's first marriage took place in 176 AC. Thus, 176 AC is the last possible year of birth for Jon.

No further specifications can be given. Jon was thus born in or between 171 AC and 176 AC.

Jonelle Cerwyn[ ]

Birth[ ]

Jonelle Cerwyn is thirty years old in 298 AC. This gives her the following options:

29 turning 30 in 298 AC, placing her birth in 268 AC

30 turning 31 in 298 AC, placing her birth in 267 AC

In 300 AC, she is thirty-two years old. This gives her the following options:

31 turning 32 in 300 AC, placing her birth in 268 AC

32 turning 33 in 300 AC, placing her birth in 267 AC

However, Jonelle was also thirty-two years old in late 299 AC. Being thirty-two years old in both 299 AC and 300 AC shows that she was thirty-one turning thirty-two in 299 AC, and thirty-two turning thirty-three in 300 AC.

Jonelle was thus born in 267 AC.

Jonnel Stark[ ]

Birth[ ]

Jonnel Stark was the eldest son of Cregan Stark by his third wife, Lady Lynara Stark. He could have been born the same year as his youngest half-sibling by Cregan's second marriage, Myriah Stark. Myriah was born no earlier than 136 AC (see Myriah Stark), meaning that Jonnel would have been born no earlier than 136 AC.

Jonnel would have been at least one year older than his younger brother, Edric Stark. As the latest possible year of birth for Edric is 180 AC (see Edric Stark), Jonnel would have been born no later than 179 AC.

No further specifications can be given. Jonnel was thus born in or between 136 AC and 179 AC.

Death[ ]

Jonnel Stark succeeded his father Cregan Stark as Lord of Winterfell. Cregan died no earlier than 157 AC (see Cregan Stark), meaning that Jonnel could not have died before 157 AC either.
Moreover, Jonnel was himself succeeded by his brother Barthogan, who died no later than 209 AC (see Barthogan Stark), meaning that Jonnel could not have died after 209 AC.

No further specifications can be given. Jonnel thus died in or between 157 AC and 209 AC.

Jonos Arryn[ ]

Birth[ ]

Jonos Arryn's older brother, Ronnel Arryn, was born no earlier than 17 BC (see Ronnel Arryn (king)). Jonos would have been born at least a year later, therefore he was born no earlier than 16 BC.

Jonos Frey[ ]

Birth[ ]

Jonos Frey is mentioned to be eight years old in 299 AC and in 300 AC. Being eight years old in both 299 AC and 300 AC means he was seven turning eight in 299 AC, and eight turning nine in 300 AC, placing his birth in 291 AC.

Jorah Mormont[ ]

Birth[ ]

Jorah Mormont notes that he is thrice the age of Daenerys Targaryen, in 299 AC. Daenerys, at that moment, is fifteen years old. That means that Jorah's age is somewhere close to forty-five. This aligns with Daenerys thinking Jorah to have passed forty in either 297 AC or 298 AC, and with Tyrion guessing Jorah had certainly passed forty in 300 AC.

Jorah had left Westeros in exile five years prior to 298 AC, thus in 293 AC. Jorah had been married to Lynesse Hightower after the Greyjoy Rebellion in 289 AC. Prior to his marriage to Lynesse Hightower, Jorah had been married to another woman for ten years. This would put his age during his first marriage at approximately twenty-five, a normal age for a highborn man, and heir to an house, to marry, in peace time.

Though Jorah most likely does not mean that he is exactly three times as old as Daenerys, it is an indication. It can be concluded that Jorah is around forty-five years old in 299 AC, meaning that he was born around 254 AC.

Jorelle Mormont[ ]

Birth[ ]

Jorelle Mormont's younger sister Lyanna was born in 290 AC (see Lyanna Mormont). As such, Jorelle would have been born no later than 289 AC, being minimally a year older. 

Alysane Mormont, Jorelle's older sister, was born in 273 AC the earliest (see Alysane Mormont). With 273 AC as Alysane's first possible moment of birth, Jorelle's first possible moment of birth would be two years later (as another sister was born in between Alysane and Jorelle). This means that Jorelle was born in 275 AC the earliest.

No further specifications can be given. Jorelle was thus born in or between 275 AC and 289 AC.

Jorgen Velaryon[ ]

Birth[ ]

Jorgen Velaryon's father, Daemon Velaryon, was born no earlier than 11 BC (see Daemon Velaryon (son of Aethan)). He would have been at least 12 years old at Jorgen's birth, therefore Jorgen was born no earlier than 2 AC.

Jorgen was alive in 50 AC, so he cannot have been born later than then.

No further specifications can be given. Jorgen was thus born in or between 2 AC and 50 AC.

Jory Cassel[ ]

Birth[ ]

Jory Cassel attended the wedding of Catelyn Tully and Eddard Stark in 283 AC, and at the bedding tore Catelyn's gown in his haste to get her out of it. Jory must have been an adolescent or older at this time, estimate ten years old at the very youngest. Therefore Jory must have been born before 273 AC. 

Josmyn Peckledon[ ]

Birth[ ]

Josmyn Peckledon is said to have been no older than fourteen in 299 AC, after the Battle of the Blackwater. 
Josmyn was fifteen years old in 300 AC.

Being fourteen years old in 299 AC and fifteen in 300 AC, gives the following options:

13 turning 14 in 299 AC, and 14 turning 15 in 300 AC, placing his birth in 285 AC

14 turning 15 in 299 AC, and 15 turning 16 in 300 AC, placing his birth in 284 AC

No further specifications can be given.  Josmyn was thus born in either 284 AC or 285 AC.

Joy Hill[ ]

Birth[ ]

Joy Hill was nine or ten when Jaime Lannister had last seen her, as stated in 300 AC. The last time Jaime could have seen Joy was when he fled to Casterly Rock in 298 AC. Joy is ten years old in 298 AC. In 299 AC, Joy is eleven years old. In 300 AC, Joy is still eleven years old.

Being eleven years old in both 299 AC and 300 AC, shows that Joy was ten turning eleven in 299 AC, and is eleven turning twelve in 300 AC. This places Joy's birth in 288 AC.

Joy Penrose[ ]

Birth[ ]

Joy Penrose was the fourth child of Ronnel Penrose and Elaena Targaryen. Joy would have been born at least one year after her older sibling, Jocelyn. Jocelyn was born in 186 AC the earliest (see Jocelyn Penrose), indicating that Joy was born no earlier than 187 AC.

As Joy's mother Elaena was born in 150 AC (see Elaena Targaryen), she would have reached the end of her child-bearing years near 200 AC, when she turned 50. Joy was the youngest of the four children of Ronnel and Elaena, indicating that she was born no later than 200 AC, the year Elaena turned fifty.

No further specifications can be given. Joy was thus born in or between 187 AC and ~200 AC.

Joyeuse Erenford[ ]

Birth[ ]

Joyeuse Erenford is known to have been sixteen years old in 299 AC. This gives Joyeuse the following options:

15 turning 16 in 299 AC, placing her birth in 283 AC

16 turning 17 in 299 AC, placing her birth in 282 AC

No further specifications can be given. Joyeuse was thus born in either 282 AC or 283 AC.
