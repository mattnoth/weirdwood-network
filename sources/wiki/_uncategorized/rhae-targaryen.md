History[ ]

Rhae was the youngest child of Prince Maekar Targaryen and his wife, Lady Dyanna Dayne. She had four older brothers, Daeron, Aerion, Aemon and Aegon, and one older sister, Daella.

  

Shiera Seastar gives Rhae a love potion for her brother Aegon, by Jota Saraiva ©

Once, in her youth, Rhae gave a love potion to her youngest brother, Aegon, so he would marry her instead of Daella, to whom Aegon may have been betrothed.

Later in life, Rhae was married, and had children.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

While on his deathbed on a voyage from Braavos to Oldtown, Maester Aemon wonders if, when he dies, he will go to a heaven where he will hear his sisters Rhae and Daella singing to their children.
