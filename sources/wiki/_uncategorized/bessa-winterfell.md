Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

During a ride through the winter town, Theon Greyjoy starts telling a story of a sexual nature involving Bessa, Kyra and himself to Robb Stark, only to be cut off before Bran Stark could overhear the details.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

The winter town is burned and sacked by Bolton men led by Ramsay Snow. The women and children survivors of the sack are carried off to the Dreadfort. The fate of Bessa is unknown, however.
