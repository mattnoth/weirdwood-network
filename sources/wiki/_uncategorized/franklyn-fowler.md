Character[ ]

Franklyn is one of the most powerful lords in Dorne. He shares his family's ancestral enmity against House Yronwood and dislikes their current ruler, Lord Anders Yronwood.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Princess Arianne Martell decides to contact Lord Franklyn for aid, for he is strong enough to threaten her father, Prince Doran, and dislikes House Yronwood. She sends a letter with Cedra, but it is intercepted before it can get to Franklyn. 

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Lord Franklyn commands the Dornish host assembled at the Prince's Pass.
