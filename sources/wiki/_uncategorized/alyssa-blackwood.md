History[ ]

Alyssa married Walder Frey after his third wife, Lady Amarei Crakehall, died in 264 AC. Alyssa and Walder had five children together: Lothar, Ser Jammos, Ser Whalen, Morya, and Tyta Frey.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Tytos Blackwood mentioned to Ser Jaime Lannister that his son Lucas was slain at the Red Wedding, even though Lord Walder's fourth wife had been a Blackwood. He notes that kinship counts for nothing at the Twins, no more than guest right.

Family[ ]

It is unknown how Alyssa is related to the other known Blackwoods.
