Hubert Arryn was the Lord of the Eyrie and the head of House Arryn during the reign of King Aenys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

After the death of Lord Ronnel Arryn at the hands of his younger brother, Jonos, and the subsequent death of Jonos and surrender of Jonos's supporters, Hubert was named the new Lord of the Eyrie.

The main line of House Arryn continued through him and his six sons.

Family[ ]

Hubert was a cousin of Lord Ronnel Arryn, the last King of Mountain and Vale, and his brother, Jonos. By 37 AC, Hubert had six sons with his wife from House Royce of Runestone.

Lord Darnold Arryn and his brother Ser Rymond Arryn are likely two of Hubert's sons.
