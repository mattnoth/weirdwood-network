Responsibilities[ ]

Since wardens defend their region from invaders, the Fowlers would have military seniority over other local lords in the defense of the Prince's Pass.

History[ ]

Prior to bending the knee to House Martell, House Fowler held the title of Lord of the Wide Way, referring to the older name of the Prince's Pass.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

The current Warden of the Prince's Pass is Franklyn Fowler, Lord of Skyreach.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Lord Franklyn commands the Dornish host assembled at the Prince's Pass, and Anders Yronwood, Lord of Yronwood and Warden of the Stone Way, commands a second Dornish host assembled at the Boneway.
