Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

House Morrigen supports Renly Baratheon during the War of the Five Kings.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Richard and his elder brother, Lord Lester Morrigen, continue to support Stannis Baratheon after the Battle of the Blackwater. According to a semi-canon source, Lester has accompanied Stannis to the north. The current location of Richard is unknown, however. Ser Tristan Rivers leads the taking of Crow's Nest.
