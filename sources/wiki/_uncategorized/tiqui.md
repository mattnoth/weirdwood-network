## Full Text

Tiqui is a city in Yi Ti, located in the western hills of the region.

Geography[ ]

The city is southeast of the Great Sand Sea and is connected by a trade route, possibly the Sand Road, to Bayasabhad to the west and Trader Town to the north.

History[ ]

Tiqui is one of the ancient capitals of Yi Ti, and was the origin of some of the nation's past god-emperors. The dynasty of the purple emperors had their seat located at Tiqui.

Tiqui is currently a market city, and is connected to Qarth by the Sand Road.
