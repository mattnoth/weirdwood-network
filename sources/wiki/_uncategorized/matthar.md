Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Matthar is in the same class of recruits as Jon Snow. When he learns he is to join the Night's Watch, he celebrates with Jon and the others, drinking wine and throwing snowballs with Jeren. Matt is named to the rangers when he is considered ready to man the Wall.

After the arrest of Lord Eddard Stark in King's Landing, Matthar asks Septon Cellador to light a candle for Jon's father.

Matthar is present when Jon Snow reveals his new sword Longclaw to his friends.

Matt is among the group of friends who stop Jon from abandoning the Night's Watch and forswearing himself.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Matthar is stationed at Castle Black.
