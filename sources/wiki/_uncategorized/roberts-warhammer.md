Appearance[ ]

  

Robert Baratheon carrying his warhammer, by Smirtouille © Fantasy Flight Games

The spiked iron warhammer is a heavy weapon which Eddard Stark can barely lift.

History[ ]

The warhammer was forged for Robert Baratheon by Donal Noye at Storm's End.

Robert wielded his warhammer during Robert's Rebellion. The Lord of Storm's End used its spike to deal a crushing blow against Rhaegar Targaryen, Prince of Dragonstone, in the Battle of the Trident, smashing his chest, sending rubies from Rhaegar's armor into the river.

King Robert used his hammer during the siege of Pyke.

On Edric Storm's last name day, Robert sent his bastard son a smaller version of his warhammer.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

During the Hand's tourney, Lord Eddard Stark sees the warhammer alongside an immense iron shield outside of King Robert I Baratheon's pavilion.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

King Robert's Hammer is a war galley in the royal fleet of Robert's son, King Joffrey I Baratheon.
