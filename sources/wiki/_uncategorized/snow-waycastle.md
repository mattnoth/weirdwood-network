Layout[ ]

Snow consists of a single fortified tower, a timber keep, and a stable placed behind a low wall of unmortared rock. It is nestled into the Giant's Lance so as to command the entire pathway to it from Stone below, and the trail to Snow is steeper than the trail from the Gates of the Moon to Stone. The trail then continues up the mountain from Snow to Sky.

History[ ]

According to Mya Stone's mother, the waycastle Snow was where snow began on the Giant's Lance hundreds of years ago.

Queen Sharra Arryn tripled the size of the garrisons in Stone, Snow, and Sky in preparation for the imminent march of the Targaryen forces against the Vale of Arryn during Aegon's Conquest. Queen Visenya Targaryen evaded them, however, by flying Vhagar to the Eyrie's inner courtyard.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Catelyn Stark stops at Snow while traveling to the Eyrie. The waycastle is commanded by a young knight with a pockmarked face who offers bread and cheese, but Mya Stone has them continue on to Sky. Catelyn rides Whitey from Snow.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Sansa Stark and Lord Robert Arryn stop at Snow while descending from the Eyrie to the Gates of the Moon. They enjoy a hot meal of stewed goat and onions before receiving fresh mules for the journey to Stone.
