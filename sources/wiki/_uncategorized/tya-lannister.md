## Summary

Tya was married to Gowen Baratheon, the third son of a reigning Lord of Storm's End. Their wedding, circa 208 AC, was the last between houses Lannister and Baratheon until the marriage of King Robert I Baratheon and Queen Cersei Lannister some seventy-six years later. Tya and Gowen had one son, a "large and lusty lad" born with a full head of black hair, who died in his infancy.
