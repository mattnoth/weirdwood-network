History[ ]

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Arianne Martell's embassy to Jon Connington and the Golden Company lands at the Weeping Town, heading north to meet them at Griffin's Roost. While staying there, Ser Joss Hood overhears at the Loon inn that half a hundred men and boys from the Weeping Town already left to join Jon at Griffin's Roost, including Ser Addam, old Lord Whitehead's son and heir.

Behind the scenes[ ]

George R. R. Martin apparently named the character after longtime A Song of Ice and Fire blogger Adam Whitehead. Apart from being a moderator on the Westeros.org forums, Adam also runs his own award-winning Scifi and Fantasy review blog, The Wertzone.
