Layout[ ]

The Foghouse is a small, cramped, dirty establishment with barely enough space to fit ten people.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Samwell Tarly unsuccessfully searches for Dareon at the Foghouse. He asks polemen if they have seen a singer dressed all in black, but they either do not understand his High Valyrian or ignore it.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The blind girl has begged at the Foghouse.
