Appearance and Character[ ]

Rollam Westerling was a dutiful squire to Robb Stark, and his mother Catelyn Stark believed her own son Bran Stark would have been much the same. The boy was respectful, initially kneeling before Catelyn when first introduced before realizing his family members merely bowed. He was hesitant to leave Robb's side to settle into his accommodations after the Westerlings' arrival at Riverrun and complained bitterly when left behind for the wedding of Lord Edmure Tully at the Twins.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Rollam replaces Olyvar Frey as the squire of Robb Stark, King in the North, after Robb marries Jeyne Westerling and House Frey withdraws their forces from Robb's. Rollam accompanies Robb to Riverrun when Robb returns from the westerlands. Rollam remains at Riverrun with his sisters, Jeyne and Eleyna, and his mother, Sybell Spicer, when Robb goes to the wedding of Lord Edmure Tully at the Twins. Robb is then slain in the massacre dubbed the Red Wedding.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Rollam's elder brother, Ser Raynald Westerling, is presumed to have died in the Red Wedding. After Ser Jaime Lannister resolves the siege of Riverrun, Lord Gawen Westerling departs for the Crag with his surviving family.
