Lucas is a common male name in Westeros.

Lucas Blackwood, the second son of Lord Tytos Blackwood of Raventree Hall.

Lucas Codd, an ironborn raider known as Left-Hand Lucas Codd.

Lucas Corbray, the youngest brother of Lord Lyonel Corbray.

Lucas Harroway, the Lord of Harrenhal during the reign of King Maegor I Targaryen.

Lucas Inchfield,the castellan of Coldmoat for Lady Rohanne Webber.

Lucas Leygood, the Commander of the City Watch during the regency of King Aegon III Targaryen.

Lucas Lothston (father of Manfryd), a Lord of Harrenhal.

Lucas Lothston (husband of Falena), the first Lothston Lord of Harrenhal.

Lucas Nayland, a knight during the reign of King Aerys I Targaryen.

Lucas Roote, a squire.

Lucas Tyrell, a distant cousin of the ruling branch of House Tyrell.
