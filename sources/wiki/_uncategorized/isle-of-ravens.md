Geography[ ]

A weathered wooden drawbridge links the Isle of Ravens to the eastern bank of the Honeywine. The Ravenry, the oldest building in the Citadel, is a castle on the isle.

History[ ]

In the Age of Heroes, the Ravenry was supposedly the stronghold of a pirate lord who sat there robbing ships as they came down the Honeywine. The drawbridge has not been raised in living memory.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

When Samwell Tarly arrives at the Citadel, Alleras intercepts him in the Seneschal's Court and brings him to the Ravenry on the Isle of Ravens.
