## Full Text

Durran Durrandon, also known as Durran the Young and the Butcher Boy, was a Storm King of House Durrandon.

History[ ]

Durran dammed the river Slayne with Dornish corpses after turning back Yoren Yronwood and the warrior maid Wylla of Wyl in the Battle by the Bloody Pool. Scholars also debate whether he was the same king who became besotted with his own niece later in life and died at the hands of his brother Erich Kin-Killer.
