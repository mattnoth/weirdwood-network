History[ ]

After the death of her father in the Conquest of Dorne, Serena's sister Sansa was set to inherit Winterfell after her grandfather, Lord Cregan. However, the lordship of Winterfell instead passed to Cregan's second son, Jonnel Stark, who also married Sansa. The north lamented the death of Rickon, for the reigns of Jonnel and his brothers after him were troublesome.

Serena married twice in her life. She first married Lord Jon Umber with whom she did not have issue, and then she married her father's half-brother, Edric Stark, with whom she had four children: first the twin sons Torrhen and Cregard, then daughters Arrana and Argelle Stark.
