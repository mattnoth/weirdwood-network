## Full Text

Kyle Connington was the Lord of Griffin's Roost and the head of House Connington during the early reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

Lord Kyle attended the Golden Wedding, where the king entertained the lords in the intimacy of his solar, encouraging them to speak freely and share their views on the problems of the realm and how they might best be overcome. Lord Kyle thought the king to be witty and good-humored.
