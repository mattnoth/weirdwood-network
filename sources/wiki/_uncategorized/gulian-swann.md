Character[ ]

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Lord Gulian agrees to meet Ser Davos Seaworth but refuses to join the cause of Stannis Baratheon. During the War of the Five Kings, Lord Gulian remains in his castle pleading illness.

Gulian's eldest son, Ser Donnel, supports first Renly Baratheon and then Stannis. He is captured during the Battle of the Blackwater and kneels to Joffrey Baratheon. Gulian's second son, Ser Balon, joins Joffrey's Kingsguard after the death of Ser Preston Greenfield.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Clifford Swann is listed as Lord of Stonehelm. It is unknown if this is a mistake, if George R. R. Martin has changed Gulian's name, or if Clifford has succeeded Gulian over Gulian's eldest son Donnel.
