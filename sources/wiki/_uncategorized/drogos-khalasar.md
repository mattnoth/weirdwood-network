Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

After the wedding of Khal Drogo and Daenerys Targaryen, the khalasar leaves Pentos for Vaes Dothrak. Drogo's khalasar breaks up into separate new khalasars after the death of Khal Drogo.
