## Full Text

The name Tytos can refer to one of the following characters:

Lord Tytos Blackwood, the Lord of Raventree Hall.

Ser Tytos Brax, the eldest son of Lord Andros Brax.

Ser Tytos Frey, the eldest son of Ser Jared Frey and Lady Alys Frey.

Lord Tytos Lannister, the Lord of Casterly Rock, Shield of Lannisport and Warden of the West during the reign of Kings Aegon V Targaryen, Jaehaerys II Targaryen and Aerys II Targaryen. He is the father of Tywin, Kevan,  Genna,  Tygett and  Gerion.
