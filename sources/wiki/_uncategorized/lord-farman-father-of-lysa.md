## Full Text

Lord Farman was the Lord of Fair Isle and the head of House Farman during the Dance of the Dragons. His given name is unknown.

History[ ]

During the Dance of the Dragons, Lord Dalton Greyjoy landed longships on Fair Isle. Lord Farman was unable to repel the ironborn attack and retreated to his castle, Faircastle, where he sent out pleas for help. No one came, however, and Faircastle eventually fell to the ironborn in 130 AC. The Red Kraken claimed four of Lord Farman's daughters as salt wives, including Lysa, while giving the fifth "homely one" to his brother, Veron. Lord Farman and his sons were ransomed back to Casterly Rock for their weights in silver. After the fall of Faircastle, Lord Dalton took Lord Farman's bedchamber as his own.

By 133 AC, Lord Farman and two of his daughters had perished.
