Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser Lambert stays with Queen Selyse at Eastwatch-by-the-Sea when Stannis Baratheon goes to retake Deepwood Motte. When Selyse leaves Eastwatch to go to the Nightfort, the knight accompanies her to Castle Black. Lambert attends the wedding of Sigorn and Alys Karstark.
