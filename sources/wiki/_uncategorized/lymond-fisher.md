## Full Text

Lymond Fisher was a Knight of Oldstones four centuries prior to Aegon's Conquest.

History[ ]

Lymond claimed the title of river king or King of the Trident in the generations after the riverlands were conquered by the Storm King Arlan III Durrandon. Lymond had a brief, inglorious reign, presumably ruling from Oldstones, before being put down by the might of Storm's End. His connection with the extinct Fisher dynasty is unconfirmed.
