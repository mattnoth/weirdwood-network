## Summary

The Battle of Bronzegate was one of the greatest victories of House Durrandon during the coming of the Andals to the stormlands. At Bronzegate, King Monfryd V Durrandon defeated the Holy Brotherhood of the Andals, an alliance of seven Andal warlords and petty kings, but at the cost of his own life.
