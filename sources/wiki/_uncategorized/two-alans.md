## Full Text

The two Alans is the historical reference given to Alan Beesbury and Alan Tarly, the Lord of Honeyholt and the Lord of Horn Hill respectively, during the Dance of the Dragons.

History[ ]

The two Alans supported Queen Rhaenyra Targaryen and the blacks during the Dance of the Dragons. Together with Lord Owen Costayne of Three Towers, they harrassed the host of Lord Ormund Hightower of the greens on his march north. Their actions culmulated in the Battle of the Honeywine where the two Alans were captured.
