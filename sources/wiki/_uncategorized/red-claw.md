A Clash of Kings[ ]

Red Claw is spotted by Davos Seaworth in the harbor of Dragonstone when Stannis Baratheon gathers his sworn swords in preparation for the War of the Five Kings. It is part of Stannis's royal fleet commanded by Ser Imry Florent during the Battle of the Blackwater.
