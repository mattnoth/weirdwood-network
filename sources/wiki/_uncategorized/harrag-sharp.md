Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Harrag Sheepstealer participates in the harrying of the Stony Shore and the capture of Winterfell. He chooses to remain with Theon Greyjoy when northmen led by Ser Rodrik Cassel come to retake the castle.

After the battle outside the gates of Winterfell, Theon orders Harrag to find Palla and bring the girl to "Red Helm". The slayer of Rodrik reveals himself to be Ramsay Snow, however, and instead demands Theon's lover, Kyra. Harrag is killed by Bolton men during the ensuing sack of Winterfell.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

During the kingsmoot on Old Wyk, Asha Greyjoy mentions Harrag's death to his father, Harmund Sharp.
