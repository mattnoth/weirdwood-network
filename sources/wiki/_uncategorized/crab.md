Range[ ]

Crabs are found throughout Westeros, including Duskendale, Fair Isle, the Iron Islands, the Shield Islands, Skagos, the Three Sisters, Shipbreaker Bay, and the eastern shore of Dorne. The Trident flows into the Bay of Crabs, and the islands off the coast of the Vale of Arryn are infested with crabs. Stonecrab Cay is found near the Arbor.

Mud crabs, leopard crabs, and climber crabs abound by Braavos. Crabs are found in the Bay of Pentos and in Lorath Bay. Crabs live throughout the shores of the Shivering Sea, and Bitterweed Bay is renowned for its crabs. Spider crabs and emperor crabs are found everywhere in the lands of the Shivering Sea east of Ib. Crabs can be found at the markets of Volantis.

Culture[ ]

  

A spider crab in the arms of House Borrell

Red crabs are depicted in the arms of House Celtigar of Claw Isle, with Lord Ardrian Celtigar known as the Red Crab. A white spider crab is included in the arms of House Borrell, and varieties of crab are included in the sister's stew popular at the Three Sisters. House Crabb is a noble family from Crackclaw Point.

The Crab King is a lesser god of the Rhoyne. Crab gods are said to be worshipped by free folk at Storrold's Point in the lands beyond the Wall.

History[ ]

After suppressing a rebellion by Gulltown, King Gerold Grafton threw the dead into the city's bay for the crabs.

The crab-eaten bodies of the followers of Lodos washed up on the shores of Old Wyk after walking into the sea.

Craghas Drahar, an admiral of Myr, was known as Craghas Crabfeeder for his habit of staking captured pirates on the shore to drown.

Crabs and seagulls feasted on the head and neck of Arrax after the dragon died near Storm's End.

Victarion Greyjoy gave the body of his third wife to the crabs after killing her.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Tyrion Lannister enjoys crabs delivered from Eastwatch-by-the-Sea while dining with the high officers of the Night's Watch at Castle Black.

The ailing Lord Hoster Tully refers to his stomach pain as crabs pinching in his belly.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Hot crab pie is one of the courses served during Tyrion's meal with Lord Janos Slynt.

Crabs are among the seafood brought by Lord Wyman Manderly to the harvest feast at Winterfell.

Crabclaw pies are served in the Queen's Ballroom during the Battle of the Blackwater.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser Davos Seaworth eats tiny crabs while shipwrecked on a spear of the Merling King after the battle.

Crabs reportedly eat King Balon Greyjoy's eyes after he falls to his death from Pyke.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Samwell Tarly sees a wrecked galley on the rocks of Skagos, with bodies of crewmen being consumed by rooks and crabs.

Brienne of Tarth buys hot crab stew for a pious dwarf at the Seven Swords in Duskendale. Crabs are included in the thick seafood stew served at the Quiet Isle. Dog catches a crab in a tidal pool near the isle's septry.

Cat of the Canals sells crabs in Braavos.

Ironborn feast on buckets of fresh crabs at Lord Hewett's castle after the taking of the Shields.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Manderly brings crates of crabs to Barrowton for the wedding of Ramsay Bolton and Arya Stark.

Umma serves salt-crusted crabs for supper at the House of Black and White.
