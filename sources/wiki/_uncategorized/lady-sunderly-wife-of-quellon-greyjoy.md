History[ ]

Lady Sunderly was wed by a priest of the Drowned God to Lord Quellon Greyjoy, after the death of his first rock wife.

She was the mother of Balon, Euron, Victarion, Urrigon and Aeron Greyjoy. After her death, Lord Quellon took a new rock wife, a daughter of Lord Piper of Pinkmaiden Castle.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Aeron recalls his mother after the death of his eldest brother Balon Greyjoy, King of the Isles and the North.
