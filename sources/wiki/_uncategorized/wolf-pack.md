## Summary

The Wolf Pack was founded by Hallis Hornwood and Timotty Snow in the aftermath of the Dance of the Dragons and the collapse of the Triarchy, when the Free Cities of Lys, Myr, and Tyrosh were hiring sellswords in anticipation of the Daughters' War. At the time, the new free company consisted only of northmen, who are often dubbed "wolves" on account of the direwolf sigil of the rulers of the north, House Stark.

History[ ]
