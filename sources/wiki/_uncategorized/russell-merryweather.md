History[ ]

Russell was born at Longtable.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

While Russell remains at Longtable, his father, Lord Orton Merryweather, becomes justiciar on the small council of King Tommen I Baratheon in King's Landing. Russell's mother, Taena of Myr, becomes a confidante of Tommen's mother, Queen Regent Cersei Lannister.

Cersei invites Taena to bring Russell to court as the queen's fosterling. Taena wishes to think about the offer before committing herself to it.

Cersei appoints Orton as Hand of the King after demoting Ser Harys Swyft. After Cersei is arrested by the Faith of the Seven, Orton resigns and departs with Taena for Longtable.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After her release from imprisonment in the Great Sept of Baelor, Cersei asks her uncle, Ser Kevan Lannister, Tommen's new regent, to send word to Longtable asking for Taena and Russell to come to court. Kevan, who wants to send his niece to Casterly Rock, agrees, thinking he still can send Taena to the Rock with Cersei and foster young Russell himself at King's Landing. Kevan is assassinated before he can send a letter to Longtable, however.
