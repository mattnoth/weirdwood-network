Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

At Chataya's brothel, Heward plays a strip game of tiles against a buxom wench.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Timett admits to Tyrion Lannister to cheat at a game of tiles against a wineseller's son in a gambling den on the Street of Silver.

Bran Stark plays a three-sided game of tiles with Meera and Jojen Reed.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Sansa Stark plays tiles with Margaery Tyrell's cousins in the evening.

During their stay at Acorn Hall, Gendry and Lem play tiles with their hosts at night.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lazy Leo lost money at a game of tiles at the Checkered Hazard.

Samwell Tarly sees Meralyn and Xhondo Dhoru playing tiles when he enters the Happy Port.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Jon Snow sees four builders of the Night's Watch playing at tiles at a table when he enters the stone cellar below the armory of Castle Black.
