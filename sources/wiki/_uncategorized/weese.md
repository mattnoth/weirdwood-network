## Full Text

Weese is the understeward of the Wailing Tower at Harrenhal.

In the television adaptation Game of Thrones, he is renamed Weasel and portrayed by Paul Kealyn.

Appearance and Character[ ]

Weese is a squat man with boils near one corner of his lips and a fleshy carbuncle nose. He is an abusive man, resorting to violence at the slightest provocation. He keeps a spotted dog.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Arya Stark, who calls herself Weasel, is assigned to Weese when she is brought to Harrenhal by the Mountain's men. Weese abuses Arya hitting her for being slow and for looking at him. Arya eventually whispers his name to Jaqen H'ghar. Weese is later found with his throat torn out and his face being eaten by his spotted dog, who is then shot with a crossbow. Arya later asks Jaqen how he made the dog to attack her master, but he does not elaborate.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Arya learns from the waif while training at the House of Black and White in Braavos. Faceless Men have access to a paste which, when spiced with basilisk blood, makes cooked meat smell savory but induces violent madness when consumed. This may have been how Jaqen manipulated the dog into killing Weese.
