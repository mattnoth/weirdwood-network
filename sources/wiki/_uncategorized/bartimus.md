Appearance and Character[ ]

Bartimus is a descendant of the First Men who lived at the mouth of the White Knife before the coming of the Manderlys. He is often drunk. He has one leg and one eye. Though he is a knight he worships the old gods.

History[ ]

Ser Bartimus lost his leg to an unmanned horse falling on it and the Maester's saw. Wyman Manderly gave the Wolf's Den to him as a reward for saving his life at the Battle of the Trident.
