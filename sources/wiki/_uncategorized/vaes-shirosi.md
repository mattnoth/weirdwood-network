## Summary

Vaes Shirosi is a ruined town located in Essos, north of Qarkash and Port Yhos, in the southwestern portion of the red waste. The Qaathi town was destroyed by the Dothraki during the Century of Blood. Its Dothraki language name means City of Scorpions.
