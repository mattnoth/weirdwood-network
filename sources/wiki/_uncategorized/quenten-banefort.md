Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lord Quenten is a member of the Lannister host besieging Riverrun. He is taken prisoner at the Whispering Wood.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Banefort is a captive of Lord Jonos Bracken at Stone Hedge.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

At some point Lord Quenten has been released from captivity since he is among the gathered nobility at King's Landing that escort the corpse of Lord Tywin Lannister back to Casterly Rock. Unlike Lord Antario Jast, Quenten dealt with his imprisonment well and looks ready to return to battle. Ser Jaime Lannister thinks Lord Banefort could deal with the outlaws Sandor Clegane and Beric Dondarrion, but he would not be a good Hand of the King.
