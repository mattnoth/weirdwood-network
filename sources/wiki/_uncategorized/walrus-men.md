## Full Text

Walrus-men are a mythical species in legends of Lorath, and they presumably looked similar to walruses. Some free folk of the Frozen Shore are also known as walrus men, but it is unknown if there is a connection.

History[ ]

Some stories claim the mazemakers of ancient Lorath were destroyed by maritime foes, such as merlings, selkies, or walrus-men.
