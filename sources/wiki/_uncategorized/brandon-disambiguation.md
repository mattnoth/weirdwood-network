Characters[ ]

Brandon of the Bloody Blade, son of Garth Greenhand, legends say he may have been the father of Bran the Builder.

Brandon Norrey, called the Norrey, a clan chief of House Norrey.

Brandon Norrey, eldest son and heir of Brandon Norrey.

Brandon Snow, bastard brother of King Torrhen Stark.

Brandon the Builder, legendary first King of Winter and builder of Winterfell and the Wall.

Brandon Stark, a son of Brandon the Builder, who might have designed the Hightower of Oldtown.

King Brandon Stark, also known as Brandon the Breaker, a King of Winter who defeated the Night's King in an alliance with King-beyond-the-Wall Joramun.

King Brandon Stark, also known as Brandon the Shipwright, a King in the North who was lost sailing the Sunset Sea.

King Brandon Stark, also known as Brandon the Burner, the son of Brandon the Shipwright, a King in the North who burned the northern ships following his father's death.

King Brandon Stark, also known as Brandon Ice Eyes, a King in the North who recaptured the Wolf's Den from slavers originating from the Stepstones.

King Brandon Stark, also known as Brandon the Bad, a King in the North.

King Brandon IX Stark, a King in the North who stopped Skagosi raids on the Bay of Seals.

Lord Brandon Stark, called Brandon the Daughterless, a Lord of Winterfell in the legend of Bael the Bard.

Lord Brandon Stark, called Brandon the Boisterous, second Lord of Winterfell after the Conquest.

Lord Brandon Stark, called Brandon the Boastful, the father of Lords Walton and Alaric Stark, a Lord of Winterfell.

Brandon Stark, second son of Bennard Stark and Margaret Karstark.

Lord Brandon Stark, fourth son of Cregan Stark and his third wife Lynara Stark, a Lord of Winterfell.

Brandon Stark, only son of Lord Willam Stark and his first wife Lyanne Glover, who was nursed by Old Nan following his mother's death in childbirth.

Brandon Stark, eldest son of Artos the Implacable and Lysara Karstark.

Brandon Stark, eldest son of Lord Rickard and Lady Lyarra Stark, who was killed by King Aerys II Targaryen prior to Robert's Rebellion.

Brandon Stark, better known as Bran, second son of Lord Eddard Stark and Lady Catelyn Tully.

Brandon Tallhart, eldest son of Leobald Tallhart and Berena Hornwood.
