Layout[ ]

A hundred feet tall, the King's Tower is a round tower with merlons atop it. It has an oak door studded with iron and spiral steps.

The building overlooks the gate of Castle Black and the foot of the wooden stair along the Wall.

History[ ]

Though named for kings, no king has visited the tower in over a hundred years.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Tyrion Lannister stays in the King's Tower when he visits Castle Black.

Jeor Mormont, Lord Commander of the Night's Watch, moves his quarters to the King's Tower after the Lord Commander's Tower burns.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

From atop the King's Tower, Jon Snow sees smoke from the burning of Mole's Town by Styr.

Jon, Satin, and Dick Follard defend the tower against Styr's wildlings during the attack on Castle Black. Jon rests in the King's Tower amid Mance Rayder's assault on the Wall.

King Stannis Baratheon occupies the King's Tower after his victory over the wildlings in the battle beneath the Wall.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Since Stannis has claimed the King's Tower, Jon, the new Lord Commander, makes his residence in Donal Noye's modest chambers behind the armory. 

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

  

Val, by Rosenroot ©

Stannis has Val placed in rooms above his own in the King's Tower, though he allows the captive to walk the battlements. Jon sees Val staring at the Wall from the tower's roof.

Stannis watches the beheading of Janos Slynt from the steps of the King's Tower. Stannis meets with his captains in the tower's solar.

After Stannis departs for the northern mountains, Bowen Marsh urges Jon to move into Jeor's old chambers in the King's Tower. Jon declines, however, and keeps his chambers near the armory.

Melisandre invites Jon to her chambers in the King's Tower, where "Rattleshirt" is revealed to be Mance Rayder.

The royal chambers in the King's Tower are prepared for Stannis's wife, Selyse Baratheon, when she arrives from Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.

When Val returns to Castle Black after finding Tormund in the haunted forest, Lord Commander Snow has her quarters moved from the King's Tower to Hardin's Tower.
