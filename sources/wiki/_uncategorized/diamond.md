Culture[ ]

Magister Illyrio Mopatis has diamonds among his various rings.

Black diamonds are among the gems adorning the nose of Xaro Xhoan Daxos.

History[ ]

Many women at court dazzled in pearls and diamonds during the era of King Aenys I Targaryen and his wife, Queen Alyssa Velaryon.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

After the death of King Robert I Baratheon, his heir, King Joffrey I Baratheon, wears a golden crown crusted with rubies and black diamonds. Joffrey's mother, Queen Regent Cersei Lannister, accompanies her black mourning gown with a veil of diamonds in her hair.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

King Renly Baratheon wears a swordbelt studded with jagged chunks of black diamond.

After the capture of Winterfell, Theon Greyjoy claims the title Prince of Winterfell and wears an iron crown with chunks of black diamond and nuggets of gold.

Cersei wears a necklace of diamonds and emeralds during the Battle of the Blackwater.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser Jaime Lannister dons a golden chain studded with black diamonds for the feast at Darry.
