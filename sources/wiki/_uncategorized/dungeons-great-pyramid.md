## Full Text

The dungeons of the Great Pyramid of Meereen are in proximity to the pyramid's pit. Slaves are tortured in the nearby torture chambers.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Westerosi members of the Windblown, the feigned deserters, are imprisoned in the dungeons. Among the known prisoners are:

Meris, later released by Daenerys Targaryen and sent back to the Tattered Prince.

Orson Stone

Dick Straw

Hugh Hungerford

Will of the Woods

Archibald Yronwood and Gerris Drinkwater are later confined to the Great Pyramid's dungeons due to their role in Quentyn Martell's attempted theft of and the loosing of Rhaegal and Viserion.

When he becomes the Queen's Hand, Ser Barristan Selmy visits Archibald and Geris in the dungeons. He questions them and then offers them their freedom; so long as they carry out a mission for him, he intends to send them back to the Tattered Prince. Barristan wants them to deliver a secret message to the Tattered Prince. In exchange for the Windblown coming over to the queen, he will give the Tattered Prince what he desires: Pentos.
