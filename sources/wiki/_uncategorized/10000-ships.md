Plot[ ]

10,000 Ships takes place a millennium before the War of the Five Kings, focusing on Nymeria and her exodus from Essos to Dorne, leading the survivors of the Rhoynar after they were defeated in a massive war with the Valyrians.

Production[ ]

Conception and development[ ]

10,000 ships was first announced in March 2021, with Amanda Segel enlisted as writer later that year in May. While Segel's involvement was announced on May 25, 2021, the project has not yet been greenlit to full production, nor did Segel receive a formal pilot order: the announcement was that Segel had been hired to write a formal pitch script.

Writer Brian Helgeland revealed he had work on the project in 2017, prior to Amanda Segel. He was unsuccessful to pass the development phase of the project, however.

As of June 2024, the project is still in development at HBO. George R. R. Martin revealed that playwright and Pulitzer winner Eboni Booth is now writing a new pilot for the project.
