History[ ]

Lord George Graceford declared for the greens at the start of the Dance of the Dragons. He was one of the thirteen Caltrops, a group of nobles from the Reach who conspired against the Two Betrayers. George survived the war and was later named Lord Confessor by King Aegon III Targaryen's Hand, Lord Unwin Peake.

In the aftermath of the Lysene Spring, Lord George was found guilty of attempted poisoning and torture. George choose to take the black, rather than be executed.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

The pregnant Lady Alyce Graceford comes to King's Landing in the entourage of Margaery Tyrell.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lady Alyce asks Cersei Lannister for leave to name her child Tywin if it is a boy or Lanna if it is a girl. She is granted leave.
