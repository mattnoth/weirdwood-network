## Summary

Janna Fossoway, née Tyrell, is the wife of Ser Jon Fossoway of the green-apple Fossoways. She is the youngest daughter of Lord Luthor Tyrell and Lady Olenna Tyrell. Janna has two siblings: Mace Tyrell, the current Lord of Highgarden, and an older sister, Mina.

Appearance and Character[ ]

Janna is buxom and enjoys gossip.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Janna Fossoway is among the retinue of her niece, Margaery Tyrell, when Margaery comes to King's Landing to marry King Joffrey I Baratheon. Janna shares gossip with Sansa Stark.

During Sansa's wedding to Tyrion Lannister, Ser Kevan Lannister dances with Janna.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

After the wedding of Margaery to King Tommen I Baratheon, and the departure of most of the lords and ladies from the Reach, Lady Janna remains in King's Landing as one of Margaery's ladies-in-waiting. She often hawks with Queen Margaery and Meredyth Crane.

When Queen Regent Cersei Lannister returns to the Red Keep from the Great Sept of Baelor, she encounters Margaery and her ladies returning from picking autumn flowers in the Kingswood. Janna and Meredyth wear flowers in their hair, and are escorted by Janna's nephews, Ser Horas and Ser Hobber Redwyne.
