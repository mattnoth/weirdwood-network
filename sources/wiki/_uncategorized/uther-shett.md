Appearance[ ]

Uther is a skinny, ginger-haired young man no more than eighteen years old. His face is covered in pimples, which he attempts to hide behind his whiskers.

Recent Events[ ]

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Ser Uther Shett arrives at the Gates of the Moon to compete in Lord Robert Arryn's tourney for a place in the Brotherhood of Winged Knights. While flirting with Myranda Royce along Ser Ossifer Lipps, he is interrupted by Alayne Stone, who rescues Myranda from their unwanted advances. During the feast on the eve of the tourney, Uther treads upon Alayne's feet when the two dances.
