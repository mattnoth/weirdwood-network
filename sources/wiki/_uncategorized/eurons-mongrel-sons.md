## Full Text

Euron's mongrel sons is the term used by the ironborn to refer to King Euron Greyjoy's baseborn offspring born of salt wives taken from across the world. One of them is a boy of ten with woolly hair and skin the color of mud.

They are not to be confused with the crew of the Silence, Euron's ship, which is refered by the ironborn as "Euron's mutes and mongrels".
