History[ ]

When Lord Denys Darklyn defied King Aerys II Targaryen and captured him, imprisoning him within the Dun Fort, Ser Symon slew Ser Gwayne Gaunt of the Kingsguard during the taking of the King. When Ser Barristan Selmy, another knight of the Kingsguard, entered the Dun Fort and escaped with the King, he avenged his sworn brother by killing Ser Symon.

Family[ ]

Symon had a twin brother, Ser Steffon Hollard. Ser Jon Hollard was also his brother.

Notes[ ]

↑ It is said that both Jon and Symon Hollard were Denys Darklyn's good-brothers. Since Lord Darklyn had only one sister, that means they were also brothers to each other, together with Steffon Hollard.
