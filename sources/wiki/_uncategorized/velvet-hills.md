## Summary

The Velvet Hills are a series of low hills in Andalos in western Essos. They lie to the east of Pentos. The headwaters of the Little Rhoyne are found among the hills. Along the Valyrian road to Ghoyan Drohe, which sits among the Velvet Hills, there is a deep lake, a circle of standing stones some claim was raised by giants, and a female Valyrian sphinx, the male having been taken away by the Dothraki.

History[ ]

A Pentoshi legend says that the hero Hukko killed seven swan maidens, mythical maidens that lured travelers in the Velvet Hills to their deaths.

Ser Willam the Wasp and his party searched for Balerion in the Velvet Hills, but they were slain by outlaws.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Illyrio Mopatis and Tyrion Lannister pass through the Velvet Hills on their way to Ghoyan Drohe.
