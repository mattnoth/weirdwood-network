## Summary

The Graces are the priestesses of the Ghiscari religion. They are housed in the Temple of the Graces of each Ghiscari city. They wear different colored robes to denote different functions.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Graces of various robe colors are present with Galazza Galare at the Temple of the Graces in Meereen for the wedding of Hizdahr zo Loraq and Daenerys Targaryen.

When Ser Barristan Selmy refers to King Hizdahr as "His Grace" in the Westerosi fashion, Reznak mo Reznak corrects him by referring to Hizdahr as "His Magnificence", "His Radiance", and "His Worship".
