Baelon Targaryen was a member of House Targaryen during the Century of Blood, the second son of Lord Aerys Targaryen of Dragonstone. He had one older brother, Aelyx and one younger brother Daemion Targaryen.

History[ ]

Baelon inherited the lordship of Dragonstone from Aelyx and was himself succeeded by Daemion. King Jaehaerys I Targaryen named Prince Baelon Targaryen, the second of his sons who survived past infancy, after Lord Baelon, who was himself a second son.
