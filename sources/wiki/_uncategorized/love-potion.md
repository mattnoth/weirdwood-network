## Full Text

Love potion for Egg, by Jota Saraiva ©

Love potions and philtres are concoctions believed to create feelings of love or attraction in the one who drinks it.

History[ ]

Mushroom suggests that Alys Rivers used love potions and philtres on Prince Aemond Targaryen and Ser Criston Cole, causing them to be rivals for her affections.

Prince Aegon Targaryen spit out a love potion put in his drink by Princess Rhae Targaryen, who wanted her brother to marry her instead of their sibling, Princess Daella Targaryen.

Smallfolk of Lannisport went to Maggy the Frog for fortunetelling, charms, cures, love potions, and the like.

Quotes[ ]

Aegon: And once my sister Rhae put a love potion in my drink, so I'd marry her instead of my sister Daella.

Duncan: Did the potion work?

Aegon: It would have, but I spit it out.

—Aegon Targaryen and Duncan the Tall
