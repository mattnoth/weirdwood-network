## Full Text

The Feast Day of Our Father Above is a holy day in the calendar of the Faith of the Seven. It celebrates the Father Above. According to the septons, it is a most auspicious day for making judgements.

History[ ]

On the Feast Day of Our Father Above in 133 AC, Lord Unwin Peake, the Hand of the King of King Aegon III Targaryen, had the dungeons of the Red Keep emptied and had all the offenders punished publicly.
