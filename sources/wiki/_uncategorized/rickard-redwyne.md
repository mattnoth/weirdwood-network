History[ ]

While still a squire, he came to King's Landing with his father and brothers, Robert and Ryam, when Lord Manfryd was appointed to the small council in 50 AC.

In 55 AC Rickard took part in the squire's tourney held as part of the celebrations for the completion of the Dragonpit. He was subsequently knighted on the field by King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

In 73 AC, Rickard took part at a tourney at Old Oak, where he unhorsed the mystery knight called the Silver Fool. Rickard unmasked the mystery knight and revealed him to be Prince Baelon Targaryen. He then knighted Prince Baelon.
