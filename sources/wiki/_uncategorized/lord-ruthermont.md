Lord Ruthermont was an Andal lord during the Andal invasion of the Vale.

History[ ]

During the Andal Invasion of the Vale, Lord Ruthermont was part of the Andal coalition led by Ser Artys Arryn. He fought in the Battle of the Seven Stars and some named him as the killer of King Robar II Royce, the leader of the First Men coalition.
