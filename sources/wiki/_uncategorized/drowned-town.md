## Full Text

The Drowned Town is the oldest area of Braavos, located northwest of Ragman's Harbor.

Layout[ ]

The Drowned Town is an area mostly submerged in water, although some people still live in the high towers and upper floors of buildings there. Only the tops of half-sunken towers and domes are visible as the rest is submerged in the lagoon. Below the Drowned Town are a series of wharves. Residents of the Drowned Town may use the Purple Harbor, as locals of Braavos.

The Spotted Cellar, the Gate, and Pynto's are located east of the Drowned Town.
