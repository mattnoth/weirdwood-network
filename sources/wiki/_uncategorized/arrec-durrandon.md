## Full Text

Arrec Durrandon was a Storm King of the stormlands from House Durrandon who lost the wealthy riverlands to the Iron Islands. He was also the grandsire of the last Storm King, Argilac Durrandon. 

History[ ]

Arrec was the last Storm King to rule the riverlands, an area conquered by his ancestor Arlan III Durrandon some three hundred years earlier. Harwyn Hardhand, King of the Iron Islands, used the mobility of ironborn longships to defeat the stormlanders in the riverlands. Arrec's greatest defeat was in the Battle at Fairmarket. Later in life, Arrec tried twice to cross the Blackwater and take back the lost lands, but failed. Arrec was succeeded by his eldest son, Arlan V.
