Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

After Tyrion Lannister sends Janos Slynt to the Wall, Varys reveals to Tyrion that the captain is plotting to slip anchor three days hence in order to offer his sword and ship to Stannis Baratheon. Tyrion contemplates having Ser Jacelyn Bywater arrange for his dissappereance but concludes that a trial before King Joffrey would assure the loyalty of the other captains.
