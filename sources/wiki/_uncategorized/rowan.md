Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

  

Rowan © Fantasy Flight Games

Rowan accompanies Mance Rayder and five other spearwives to Winterfell on a mission to free "Arya Stark" (actually Jeyne Poole), who is to wed Ramsay Bolton. Mance disguises himself as "Abel", a singer, and the six spearwives pretend to be his mother, wife, two daughters, and two sisters, who perform the instruments with him. Abel and the spearwives play at the wedding feast. Rowan is the first of the washerwomen to contact Theon Greyjoy about how he took Winterfell.

After several men-at-arms and squires are found dead at Winterfell, Theon disbelieves the spearwives' claims of innocence. Rowan explains that Yellow Dick stank and was a pig of a man, but denies responsibility for the murder of Little Walder Frey.  The spearwives enlist the help of a reluctant and fearful Theon in freeing Jeyne. The six spearwives, disguised as maids and accompanied by Theon, walk into Jeyne's room under the pretext of bringing hot water so Jeyne can have a bath.  As they fight their way out of the castle, Rowan and the other spearwives are taken down one by one by the Bolton men.

According to a letter received by Jon Snow at Castle Black, Ramsay has captured Mance and has him wearing a cloak made from the skins of the six women who accompanied him to Winterfell.
