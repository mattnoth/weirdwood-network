Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

When the brotherhood without banners see that the people of Stoney Sept are keeping captured northmen in crow cages, Tom of Sevenstreams asks if this was done on the orders of Ser Wilbert. A resident of the town replies that Ser Wilbert was killed by forces of House Lannister the year before, during the War of the Five Kings. His sons are away in the westerlands with Robb Stark, King in the North and King of the Trident, and the people of Stoney Sept believe that they do not care about the town. The Mad Huntsman has been serving justice in their absence.
