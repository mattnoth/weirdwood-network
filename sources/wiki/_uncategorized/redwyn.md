## Summary

Redwyn lived during the reign of Dorren Stark, a King in the North. He wrote an account of a ranging from the Shadow Tower to Lorn Point on the Frozen Shore. During that ranging they fought giants and traded with the children of the forest. Maester Aemon sent a letter to the Citadel, early in the reign of King Aegon V Targaryen, reporting of Redwyn's account.
