A council of magisters is the governing body of the Free City of Lorath. It is composed of nobles, priests and merchants.

Even though the city is nominally ruled by a council of three princes formed by the Harvest Prince, the Fisher Prince and the Prince of the Streets, true power lies in the council of magisters.
