## Full Text

Platinum is a silvery white precious metal.

Culture[ ]

Platinum is one of the metals used by maesters for their chains; the subject it represents is unknown. The chain of Grand Maester Pycelle has more links of precious metals such as gold, silver, and platinum than baser metals.

People of Valyrian descendant, such as the Targaryens, usually have silver-gold or platinum white hair.
