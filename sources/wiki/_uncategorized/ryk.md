Appearance and Character[ ]

See also: Images of Ryk

Ryk is a short, homely man with a narrow head. According to Tormund, Ryk is called Longspear because he is well endowed.

Longspear has a friendly demeanor.

History[ ]

Ryk is from the same village as Ygritte, who thinks of him as a brother. He broke the arm of Ygritte's first lover after he tried to take her a second time; both of them deemed him a weak partner for her.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

After Jon Snow kills Qhorin Halfhand in the Skirling Pass, Rattleshirt orders Longspear Ryk and Ygritte to guard Jon after he proves he is a turncloak. Ryk claims Qhorin's helm, but he gives it to Ygritte because it does not fit him.

After Jon is told by Mance Rayder to join Tormund's force, Ryk and Ygritte abandon Rattleshirt and join Tormund as well. Jon grows fond of Ryk as they travel. Longspear sings "The Last of the Giants" with Ygritte, Tormund, and Tormund's family.

During Jon's parley amid Mance's attack on the Wall, Tormund informs Jon that Ryk stole his daughter, Munda, after fighting off his younger sons, Dryn and Dormund.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Tormund informs Jon that Ryk has married Munda.
