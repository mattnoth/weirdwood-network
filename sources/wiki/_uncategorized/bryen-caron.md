Bryen Caron was the Lord of Nightsong and the head of House Caron during the early reign of King Robert I Baratheon.

History[ ]

Lord Bryen was the father of Bryce Caron, a second, younger legitimate son, as well as a bastard son, Rolland Storm. He also had several daughters. He, along with his wife, second son, and all his daughters died of a chill in 289 AC. Bryen's second son was twelve and had been engaged to Brienne of Tarth for two years at his time of death.

Family[ ]

It is unclear how Bryen is related to Mylenda Caron, the wife of Petyr Frey.
