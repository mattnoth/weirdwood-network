Appearance[ ]

Younger than the other whores in the brothel, the girl is slim and pretty, with long silvery hair. Tyrion guesses to himself that she is Lyseni.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After finishing with the freckled sunset girl in a Selhorys brothel, a drunken Tyrion Lannister stumbles downstairs and is recognized by Ser Jorah Mormont who has engaged the services of the silvery haired whore. Upon seeing the girl squirming in the knight's lap, Tyrion thinks to himself that he never saw her and if he had he would have taken her upstairs instead of the freckled girl. After exchanging words with Tyrion, Jorah shoves the silvery-haired whore aside and gets to his feet to capture Tyrion.
