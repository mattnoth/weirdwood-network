Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Luton is present at the wedding of Ramsay Bolton to "Arya Stark". When several men-at-arms in the castle are murdered, suspicion eventually falls upon White Harbor knights. This leads to a confrontation and skirmish between the Freys and Manderlys, during which Luton is severely wounded. As he tries to push his entrails back into his wound, crying noisily for his mother, Ramsay loses patience and drives a spear through Luton's chest.
