## Summary

Lord of the Stone Way is an ancient and now defunct title held by the head of House Yronwood, dating to the time they ruled in their own right and styled themselves High Kings of Dorne.. The title seems to have been eventually abandoned in favor of that of Warden of the Stone Way.
