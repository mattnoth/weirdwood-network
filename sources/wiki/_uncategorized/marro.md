Recent Events[ ]

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Marro plays the part of the Stranger in The Bloody Hand. As the troupe prepares for the start of the play, he tells Mercy to bring paste as his horn is coming loose. Mercy fetches the sticky paste and fastens the Stranger’s left horn back onto his forehead. Marro is in the opening lichyard scene with Bobono.

Quotes[ ]

By then Marro had appeared behind him, gaunt and terrible in the Stranger's long black robes. His face was black as well, his teeth red and shiny with blood, while ivory horns jutted upwards from his brow. Bobono could not see him, but the balconies could, and now the pit as well. The Gate grew deathly quiet. Marro moved forward silently.
