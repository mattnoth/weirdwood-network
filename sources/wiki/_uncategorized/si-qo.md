## Summary

Si Qo, known as Si Qo the Glorious, was a city of the Golden Empire of Yi Ti, located in the heart of Yi Ti's jungle. Though now long fallen and overgrown, Si Qo was the capital of the Empire during the rule of the scarlet emperors, who raised it to be their capital.
