Location[ ]

Yronwood is near the mouth of a river whose source is to the west near Skyreach in the foothills of the Red Mountains, which are along the Sea of Dorne. The formidable castle is at the southern end of the Boneway as a guardian against intruders. 

The land protected by the castle is fertile and forested, with deposits of iron, tin, and silver. Yronwood has clean and sweet mountain air, and the high meadows north of the castle have crisp air and cool nights.

History[ ]

Yronwood was the seat of the powerful kings from House Yronwood in antiquity. The Kings of Yronwood eventually submitted to House Martell during Nymeria's War.

During the First Dornish War, King Aegon I Targaryen led a brief siege of Yronwood.  The castle was defended by old men, boys, and women, however, as the fighting men had fled according to the strategy of Meria Martell, Princess of Dorne. Yronwood was quickly recaptured by Dornishmen after Aegon returned to King's Landing. 

Prince Quentyn Martell has been fostered at Yronwood by Lord Anders Yronwood from a young age. Quentyn was smitten with Ynys Yronwood when he first arrived at the castle, but the girl eventually wed Ryon Allyrion. Ynys's younger sister Gwyneth is interested in the prince, however. Cletus Yronwood became Quentyn's closest friend.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Anders Yronwood, Lord of Yronwood, feasts Ser Balon Swann for a fortnight at Yronwood during the Kingsguard's journey from King's Landing to Sunspear.
