Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

After the death of Renly Baratheon beneath Storm's End, Ser Erren and Ser Parmen Crane were sent by Stannis Baratheon to Bitterbridge to retrieve the better part of Renly's army, made up of foot soldiers. Ser Erren and Ser Parmen were captured by the Tyrells at Bitterbridge.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser Erren was imprisoned at Highgarden after his capture.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser Erren continues to be imprisoned at Highgarden.
