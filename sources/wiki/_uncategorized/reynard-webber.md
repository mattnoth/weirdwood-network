Reynard Webber was the Lord of Coldmoat and the head of House Webber during the middle of the second century.

History[ ]

Lord Reynard was the lord of Coldmoat during the time of Ser Eustace Osgrey's father. When Eustace Osgrey's father took him to call on old Reynard Webber, he showed his son the chequy lion of Osgrey carved above the gates of the castle. His relation to Lord Wyman Webber is unknown.
