Character and Appearance[ ]

The cautious Ormund is a cadaverous old man.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser Ormund supports Stannis Baratheon during the War of the Five Kings, and he accompanies King Stannis to the Wall. In a betting pool organized by lower-born men-at-arms, Ormund is the heavy favorite to die next from the cold during Stannis's march on Winterfell.

During a discussion among Stannis's lords and knights in the longhall of the crofters' village the army has stopped at, Ormund suggests that they remain there until the snowy weather breaks since they have some shelter and can fish from the nearby lake.
