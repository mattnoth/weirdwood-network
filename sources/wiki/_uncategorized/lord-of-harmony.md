Beliefs[ ]

The Lord of Harmony, regarded as the only true god by the Naathi, is often represented as a laughing giant, naked and bearded and attended by swarms of slender maidens with butterfly wings. He is said to have made the moon, the stars, and the earth, as well as all creatures.

It is believed that the sacred butterfly spirits, handmaids of the Lord of Harmony, protect Naath against those who would do the isle harm. Many conquerors have sailed to Naath, but all of them sicken and die from butterfly fever if they stay overlong. Maesters theorize that the sickness is caused by actual butterflies on the island, however.

There is a wooden hall dedicated to the Lord of Harmony in Braavos which has windows of leaded glass, showing half a hundred kinds of butterflies in all their bright colors.
