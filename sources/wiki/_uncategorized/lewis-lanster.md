Appearance[ ]

Lewis is of Westerosi origin. He has golden hair.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lewis is among the sellswords of Westerosi origin in the Windblown who are sent by the Tattered Prince to go over to Queen Daenerys Targaryen. In order to gain the trust of Daenerys, Lewis is to tell her that the Tattered Prince killed a boy that he was fond of. After Prince Quentyn Martell reveals this plot to Queen Daenerys after the group arrives at Meereen, Lewis and the other Windblown are imprisoned beneath the Great Pyramid.
