Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Iggo and three other Brave Companions—a pale Lyseni, an Ibbenese axeman, and a Dornishman—search for Nan (actually Arya Stark), Gendry, and Hot Pie after they escape from Harrenhal. While sleeping, Arya dreams of a wolf hunting the sellswords as they pursue the youths in the rain. The three other Brave Companions die quickly when her wolfpack attacks, but Iggo's horse kicks in the head of one wolf and the Dothraki uses his arakh to kill another. The great wolf leading them leaps onto Iggo's back to knock him from the saddle. Tearing off his arm, she shakes it and sends blood flying through the rain.
