Appearance[ ]

Mollono looks more like a scribe than a warrior, with a heavy paunch and inky hands. However, he is very clever.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Mollono Yos Dob is freed by Daenerys Targaryen during her efforts to end slavery in Slaver's Bay.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Mollono is named commander of the newly formed company of freedmen, Stalwart Shields. He is present at several war council meetings, and gives good advice. However, he dies from the pale mare infection that sweeps Meereen, and is replaced as commander by Tal Toraq.
