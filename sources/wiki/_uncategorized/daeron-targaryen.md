## Full Text

Daeron Targaryen is the name of several members of House Targaryen. These include:

Prince Daeron Targaryen, also known as Daeron the Daring, third born son of King Viserys I Targaryen and his second wife, Queen Alicent Hightower.

King Daeron I Targaryen, also known as the Young Dragon, the eighth King to sit upon the Iron Throne. Son of King Aegon III Targaryen and Queen Daenaera Velaryon.

King Daeron II Targaryen, also known as Daeron the Good, the twelfth King to sit upon the Iron Throne.

Prince Daeron Targaryen, also known as Daeron the Drunken, firstborn son of King Maekar I Targaryen.

Prince Daeron Targaryen, youngest son of King Aegon V Targaryen and Queen Betha Blackwood.

Prince Daeron Targaryen, son of King Aerys II Targaryen and Queen Rhaella Targaryen, who lived for only about half a year.
