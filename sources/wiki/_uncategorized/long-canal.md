## Summary

The Long Canal is a large canal in Braavos. Beginning in the south of the city's lagoon near the fishmarket, it extends north under the sweetwater river and several bridges with the Silty Town to the east. It then reaches the heart of the city, with the Palace of Truth to the northeast and the Sept-Beyond-the-Sea, the House of Black and White, the Isle of the Gods, and the Temple of the Lord of Light to the northwest. The Canal of Heroes flows into this city center from the west. The towers of the Antaryons and Prestayns are located near the Long Canal.
