## Full Text

Ser Michael Miller was a knight during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen.

History[ ]

Michael participated in the tourney at Ashford Meadow in 209 AC.

Behind the scenes[ ]

The character is named after Mike S. Miller, the artist for the graphic novel adaptations of The Hedge Knight, The Sworn Sword, and The Mystery Knight. He also is the artist for the covers of the George R.R. Martin's A Game of Thrones and A Clash of Kings graphic novel adaptations.
