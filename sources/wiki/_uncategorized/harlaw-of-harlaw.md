## Summary

Harlaw of Harlaw is an honorary title claimed by the head of House Harlaw. It is most likely used to differentiate the head of the main branch of the house, currently seated at Ten Towers, to the heads of lesser branches seated at Grey Garden, Harlaw Hall, Harridan Hill, and the Tower of Glimmering.
