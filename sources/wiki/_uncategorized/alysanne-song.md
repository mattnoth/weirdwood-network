Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

It is sung during the Red Wedding, though in a performance so odd that Catelyn Stark thought it may as well have been "The Bear and the Maiden Fair." Aegon "Jinglebell" Frey danced along as the players performed the tune.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

It is sung by Marillion in his sky cell at the Eyrie, along with other sad songs such as "Six Sorrows" and "Fallen Leaves."
