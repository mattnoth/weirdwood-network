## Summary

The Gates of Fate are located at the entrance of the Great Pit of Daznak in Meereen. The archway is formed by two bronze warriors, one wielding an axe and the other a sword, killing each other in mortal combat. The names of fallen fighters are engraved at the gate. Animals slain in the fighting pit are used for stew which is then made available in bowls at the Gates of Fate.
