Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Theon Greyjoy is given command of a newly-built longship at Lordsport after he returns to Pyke. He names it Sea Bitch after Esgred (actually his sister Asha) claims the longship is a sea bitch instead of a fair maiden. Victarion Greyjoy loans his own steersman, Rymolf Stormdrunk, to his nephew, and Aeron Greyjoy, another of Theon's uncles, blesses the new ship.

Theon sails the Sea Bitch as the flagship of his force during his harrying of the Stony Shore. Deviating from the plan of his father Balon, King of the Iron Islands, Theon grants six longships to Aeron Greyjoy to continue raiding the shore but keeps the Sea Bitch and Dagmer's Foamdrinker.

While Dagmer threatens Torrhen's Square and distracts Ser Rodrik Cassel, Theon is able to capture capture Winterfell. Crewmembers Stygg and Urzen are among those who abandon Theon before battle outside the gates of Winterfell and the sack of Winterfell by House Bolton.
