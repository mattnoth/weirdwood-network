Appearance and Character[ ]

Denyo is twelve years old, plump and cheerful. He keeps his father's cabin neat. Denyo helps his brother Yorko Terys do his sums. He is more friendly than Yorko.

Denyo speaks the Common Tongue well. He loves to talk about the Titan's Daughter, and loves to talk about Braavos, of which he is very proud.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Denyo befriends Arya Stark on the journey of the Titan's Daughter from Saltpans to Braavos. At some point, he takes her up in the ship's crow's nest. 

As they approach the city, Denyo points out the distant stars that are the fiery eyes of the  Titan of Braavos. He tells Arya about the gods of Braavos, though he seems unnerved by the Many-Faced God. When the Titan roars, he laughs, and tells Arya not to be afraid. As they pass the Arsenal, Denyo proudly explains that it can build a war galley in a day.

As Arya leaves the ship aboard one of its boats, rowed by Yorko, she realizes she never said farewell to Denyo, and wonders if she will ever see him again.
