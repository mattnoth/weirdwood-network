Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Mirri Maz Duur, who studied in Asshai, sings in a foreign tongue while being burned by Daenerys Targaryen, her voice described as "shrill" and "ululating".

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Before burning statues of the Seven taken from the sept at Dragonstone, Melisandre prays to R'hllor "once in the speech of Asshai, once in High Valyrian, and once in the Common Tongue." The red priestess then sings in the tongue of Asshai while the statues burn.
