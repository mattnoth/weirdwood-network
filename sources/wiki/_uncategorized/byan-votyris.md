Appearance and Character[ ]

Byan is a small man with weather-beaten skin, and he wears a splendid blue-dyed mustache extending from one ear to the other.

Byan's caravan is composed of four hundred horses.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Byan's caravan travelled from Pentos to Vaes Dothrak by way of Norvos and Qohor, just like Princess Daenerys Targaryen did with Khal Drogo's Khalasar. Once arrived, they encampe in the Western Market. Byan is the bearer of a letter from Magister Illyrio Mopatis addressed to Ser Jorah Mormont. Daenerys is trading in the market when she is nearly poisoned by a wine trader of the caravan. Aghast, Byan gives the poisoner into the custody of the princess's guards and his goods to Daenerys as a token of regret.
