Sam Salt was a pretender Lord of the Iron Islands who claimed descent from the black line, House Hoare.

History[ ]

In 133 AC, Lord Dalton Greyjoy was murdered at Faircastle by his salt wife, Tess. In the bloody struggle for succession that ensued on the Iron Islands between his salt sons, sisters, and cousins, Sam Salt emerged as a pretender.
