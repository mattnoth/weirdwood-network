Appearance[ ]

Joyeuse is a pale and wispy young woman.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

The sixteen-year-old Joyeuse marries Walder Frey, Lord of the Crossing and seven times a widower, on his ninetieth name day. Though invited to Joyeuse and Walder's wedding, the ailing Lord Hoster Tully, Lord of Riverrun, does not attend.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Joyeuse is present when Robb Stark, King in the North and King of the Trident, presents himself at the Twins before Lord Edmure Tully's wedding to Roslin Frey, Walder's fifth daughter. When Lord Walder has his kinswomen present themselves so that Robb can make amends after having married Jeyne Westerling instead of a Frey, Joyeuse prevents Jinglebell from joining the women.

Joyeuse dances with Robb during the wedding feast. The Freys then betray House Stark with the Red Wedding.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Joyeuse is pregnant.
