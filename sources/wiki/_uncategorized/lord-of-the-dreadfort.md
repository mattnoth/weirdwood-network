History[ ]

The Red Kings of the Dreadfort from House Bolton eventually bent the knee to the Kings of Winter, House Stark of Winterfell.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Lord Roose Bolton leads the northern foot in the battle on the Green Fork.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Roose helps orchestrate the fall of Harrenhal during the War of the Five Kings.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Roose betrays Robb Stark, King in the North, in the Red Wedding. The small council, on the authority of King Tommen I Baratheon, grants Roose the title of Warden of the North.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Roose's heir, Ramsay Bolton, claims the titles of Lord of the Hornwood and Lord of Winterfell.

Lords of the Dreadfort[ ]

The known Lords of the Dreadfort are:

Rogar Bolton, known as the Huntsman, the last Red King (presumed).

Lord Roose Bolton, known as the Leech Lord, Warden of the North.
