Kraznys has an oiled red and black beard, and is so fat that Daenerys Targaryen thinks that he has bigger breasts than her.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Kraznys shows Daenerys Targaryen the Unsullied when she comes to Astapor. Daenerys feigns ignorance and pretends she does not speak High Valyrian so Kraznys speaks to her via a slave girl named Missandei. He insults Daenerys several times during the buying unaware she understands every word he says.

When she wants to buy them, Kraznys is the one who barters the deal that she be sold every Unsullied in Astapor in exchange for a dragon. The exchange is done on the Plaza of Punishment, but Drogon (the dragon chosen by the slavers) refuses to obey him. Daenerys answers that a dragon is no slave, and orders Drogon to unleash a jet of flame into Kraznys' face. The Unsullied, who now belong to her, do not lift a finger to help their former masters and are quickly ordered to eliminate all slavers.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Survivors of the siege of Astapor mention that the pyramid of Kraznys mo Nakloz is set aflame by the population of Astapor when the Yunkai'i besiege the city, because they blame the late Kraznys for their woes.
