Appearance[ ]

Gilly's mother is an old woman.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Amid the mutiny at Craster's Keep, Ferny and Gilly's mother approach Samwell Tarly and ask him to flee with Gilly and her newborn son.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

When Samwell is upset after breaking his Night's Watch vows by sleeping with Gilly on the Cinnamon Wind, Kojja Mo reminds him that Gilly has recently lost all of her family.
