Appearance and Character[ ]

Willam has freckles and crooked teeth. He is fearless with a lance. Will is looking forward to his wedding.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord Anders Yronwood sends Ser Cletus Yronwood and three of his best young knights, including Willam, to accompany Prince Quentyn Martell on a dangerous journey.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Quentyn travels to Essos on a mission to marry Daenerys Targaryen, Queen of Meereen.  While traveling to Volantis disguised as merchants, their ship, the Meadowlark, is attacked by corsairs off the coast of the Disputed Lands and Willam and Cletus are among those killed.

Having been rejected by Daenerys, Quentyn mentions Willam and Cletus while trying to convince Ser Gerris Drinkwater and Ser Archibald Yronwood to aid him in claiming one of Daenerys's dragons after the queen has disappeared from Meereen. Gerris responds that doing so will not bring their fallen friends back.
