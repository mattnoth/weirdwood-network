Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Benfred Tallhart raises a company of young lancers during the War of the Five Kings. After Leobald Tallhart calls them young rabbits, they dub themselves the Wild Hares and tie rabbitskins to the ends of their lances.

King Joffrey I Baratheon shoots at hares with a crossbow in the Red Keep; some are killed but others escape.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Summer, the direwolf of Bran Stark, hunts squirrels and hares as Bran's party travels toward the northern mountains.

Sandor Clegane kills and cooks a hare near the Trident.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Three crossbowmen at the siege of Riverrun offer a share of a hare they caught to Ser Jaime Lannister.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Varamyr skinchanges into a hare in the haunted forest.

Potted hare is found in one of the storerooms of Castle Black.
