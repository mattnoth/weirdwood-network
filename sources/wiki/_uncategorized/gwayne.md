## Full Text

Gwayne is a Westerosi given name. It can refer to:

Gwayne Corbray, a knight and member of the Kingsguard during the reign of Daeron II Targaryen

Gwayne I Gardener, known as Gwayne the Gallant, a King of the Reach

Gwayne III Gardener, known as Gwayne the Fat, a King of the Reach

Gwayne IV Gardener, known as the Gods-fearing, a King of the Reach

Gwayne V Gardener, a King of the Reach

Gwayne Gardener, the son and successor of King Perceon III Gardener

Gwayne Gaunt, a knight and member of the Kingsguard during the reign of Aerys II Targaryen

Gwayne Hightower, a knight and second in command of the gold cloaks during the Dance of the Dragons

Gwayne Oakheart, a knight during the reign of King Daeron II Targaryen.
