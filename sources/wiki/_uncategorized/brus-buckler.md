Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Brus is one of the queen's men in the service of King Stannis Baratheon. When Stannis rides to Castle Black and then Deepwood Motte, Brus stays with Queen Selyse at Eastwatch-by-the-Sea as one of her protectors. When Selyse leaves Eastwatch to go to the Nightfort, he accompanies her to Castle Black. Brus is nervous after hearing that Wun Wun is a guest of the Night's Watch.

Brus appears half drunk during the wedding of Sigorn and Alys Karstark, and he gives Shireen Baratheon her first dance during the celebration. 

Selyse arranges for Brus to wed the second daughter of Gerrick Kingsblood.
