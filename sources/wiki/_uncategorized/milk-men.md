## Summary

Milk Men is the name for the Qartheen in the Dothraki language. They are called this due to their pale complexion. "Milk men" is also the Dothraki term for other non-Dothraki pale-skinned peoples who live in houses.

## Full Text

Milk Men is the name for the Qartheen in the Dothraki language. They are called this due to their pale complexion. "Milk men" is also the Dothraki term for other non-Dothraki pale-skinned peoples who live in houses.
