Culture[ ]

Lead, iron, and tin are abundant on Great Wyk, Harlaw, and Orkmont in the Iron Islands. The Forest of Qohor has silver, tin, and amber, and the mainland near Braavos has iron, tin, lead, and slate. Gold, iron, and tin are abundant on mountain Ib.

Tin is one of the metals used by maesters for their chain links.

Thenns mine tin and copper to make bronze.

History[ ]

The Valyrians of antiquity used slaves to unearth copper and tin from the Fourteen Flames.

House Yronwood grew rich from mining iron, silver, and tin from their lands.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

  

Patchface by M. Luisa Giliberti ©

The fool Patchface wears a mock helm fashioned from an old tin bucket.

During Renly Baratheon's feast at Bitterbridge, a fool wears gold-painted tin with a cloth lion's head.

Ser Dontos Hollard wears tin armor after being named a fool by King Joffrey I Baratheon.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Tin is among the cargo carried by Selaesori Qhoran as it sails from Volantis for Qarth.
