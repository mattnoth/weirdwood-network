Culture[ ]

According to The Seven-Pointed Star, a pious man cannot be harmed by revenants.

History[ ]

Septon Sefton considered the kin of Lady Rohanne Webber's late husbands who remained at Coldmoat to be revenants.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Depressed after having fled to Essos, Tyrion Lannister thinks only his revenant remains.

Jon Connington thinks of the Golden Company as revenants from forgotten wars, lost causes, and failed rebellions.
