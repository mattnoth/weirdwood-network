History[ ]

When King Jaehaerys was on Dragonstone during his regency, he spent every morning until midday in the castle yard training with sword, shield, and any other armament. Ser Alyn, along with his father and brother, Ser Elyas Scales, and knights of the Kingsguard would spar with the young king.
