Josua Massey, also known as the Softspear, was the last King of House Massey.

History[ ]

Josua was slain by the Storm King Qarlton II Durrandon. His daughter was taken as wife by Togarion Bar Emmon, while his son was installed as a puppet lord, after Togarion drove the stormlanders away from Stonedance.
