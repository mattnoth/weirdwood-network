## Full Text

A giant ice spider, by Kevin Catalan ©

Giant ice spiders are creatures of legend used by the Others for manhunting or riding.

It is unclear if they are giant spiders who live in ice-filled frozen wastes, or if they are magical constructs made of living ice, like the mythical ice dragon.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

  

The last hero escaping ice spiders, by Roman Papsuev ©

Old Nan tells Bran about the long night, which includes mention of the Others hunting the last hero with packs of spiders as big as hounds.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

During the grim march back to Craster's Keep after the fight at the Fist, Samwell Tarly recalls the tales of giant ice spiders which scared him when he was young.

A Feast For Crows[ ]

Sam mentions reading about giant ice spiders to Jon Snow.
