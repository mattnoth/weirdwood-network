## Full Text

Ravos Hoare, known as Ravos the Raper, was an ironborn raider and the son of Harrag Hoare, an Iron King.

History[ ]

Ravos ruled Bear Island during the ironborn invasion of the western coast of the north. Theon Stark, a King of Winter, personally slew Ravos, but ironborn raids continued under Harrag's grandson, Erich the Eagle.
