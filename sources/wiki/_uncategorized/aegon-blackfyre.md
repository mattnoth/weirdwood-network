According to Alyn Cockshaw, Aegon was a "witless bully".

History[ ]

Aegon was the oldest of the children of Daemon Blackfyre and Rohanne of Tyrosh. He had a younger twin brother, Aemon, and five other younger brothers: Daemon, Haegon, Aenys, and two whose names are currently unknown. He also had at least two sisters.

The twins often bullied their younger brother Daemon, and Daemon's friend, Alyn Cockshaw. Daemon once had a prophetic dream about the deaths of Aegon and Aemon, but the disbelieving twins only laughed at their brother.

  

The deaths of Aemon, and Aegon Blackfyre at the Battle of the Redgrass Field. By Lilith-Krueger ©

During the First Blackfyre Rebellion, when Aegon was twelve years old, he served as a squire to his father. Aegon was killed at the Battle of the Redgrass Field by the arrows of Brynden Rivers and his Raven's Teeth. Daemon remained by his fallen son's side, and was also struck down by arrows. Aegon's twin, Aemon, took up the sword Blackfyre from his dying father, but was then slain by the archers as well.
