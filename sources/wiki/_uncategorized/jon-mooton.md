## Full Text

Jon Mooton was the Lord of Maidenpool and the head of House Mooton during Aegon's Conquest. 

History[ ]

Jon became Lord of Maidenpool when his older brother was killed in combat against Aegon I Targaryen. Jon yielded Maidenpool to House Targaryen after the battle, becoming one of the first adversaries to join Aegon's cause. He commanded the Targaryen army during the Field of Fire, as Aegon took to the skies on Balerion.
