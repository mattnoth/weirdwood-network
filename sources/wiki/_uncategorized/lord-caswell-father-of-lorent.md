## Full Text

Lord Caswell was the Lord of Bitterbridge and the head of House Caswell during the reign of King Aerys II Targaryen. His given name is unknown.

History[ ]

Old Lord Caswell was Lord of Bitterbridge when his son, Lorent, took a longsword made by the castle's armorer for his own son, Rolly. After taking revenge by wounding Lorent, Rolly fled the Reach for the exiles of the Golden Company. After his death, Lorent succeeded him as Lord of Bitterbridge.
