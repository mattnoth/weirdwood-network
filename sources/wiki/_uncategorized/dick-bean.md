History[ ]

Dick served House Targaryen since he was a boy.

When King Maegor was challenged to a trial of seven by Ser Damon Morrigen, Grand Captain of the Warrior's Sons, Dick was the first to answer Maegor's call to those who would stand beside him, shaming many knights and noble lords into answering Maegor's summons. One account claimed that Dick was the first to die during the trial, cut down by Ser Lyle Bracken.
