Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Big Boil is one of the wildlings under Styr's command who passes the Wall to attack Castle Black from behind. During Summer's attack at Queenscrown, Big Boil stumbles backward and knocks down three men.

During the attack on Castle Black, Big Boil tries to break down the door of Hardin's Tower with his axe. Mully puts an arrow through his leg from atop the Flint Barracks, however, and Big Boil crawls away in retreat. The weakened wildling is taken into custody by the Night's Watch after the battle.
