Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Left-Hand Lucas joins with Euron Greyjoy when Euron declares himself King of the Isles and the North. Lucas and Asha Greyjoy trade insults at the kingsmoot on Old Wyk, and he takes the dragon horn from Cragorn after the horn is blown.

While feasting in the hall of the castle overlooking Lord Hewett's Town, Left-Hand Lucas tears tapestries down from the walls and wears one when Victarion Greyjoy enters the hall. After the feast, Lucas rapes one of Lord Humfrey Hewett's daughters.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

After learning she has been wed by proxy to Erik Ironmaker, Asha thinks Erik is at least better than Lucas or the Red Oarsman.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

Lucas feasts in the ruins of a castle near the Arbor with some of Euron's men, and he takes part in mocking Aeron Greyjoy when the latter is brought up from the hold of the Silence.
