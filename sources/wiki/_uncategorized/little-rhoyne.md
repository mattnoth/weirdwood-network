## Summary

The Little Rhoyne is a river in western Essos. Its headwaters lie in the Velvet Hills, and it is one of the tributaries of the river Rhoyne. Ghoyan Drohe lies along its banks. The Little Rhoyne is smaller and slower than each of the three forks of the Trident.
