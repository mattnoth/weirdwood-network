The Vale and the location of Redfort

Redfort, also called the Redfort, is the ancient seat of House Redfort in the Vale of Arryn. It is located in the south of the valley. It is southeast of the Eyrie, south of Ironoaks, and north of Wickenden.

History[ ]

King Jaehaerys I Targaryen visited Redfort during a royal progress in 52 AC.
