Culture[ ]

Rather than the Titan's head used in the traditional arms of House Baelish, Lord Petyr Baelish has adopted mockingbird imagery for himself. Petyr uses a mockingbird seal, and he clasps his cloak with a silver mockingbird. Petyr owns a plum-colored doublet with a mockingbird embroidered on the breast in black thread, and he also has a silvery cape patterned with mockingbirds. According to a semi-canon source, Petyr's arms are a field of silver mockingbirds on green.
