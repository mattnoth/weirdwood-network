Culture[ ]

Cider Hall is the seat of the elder branch of House Fossoway, while the younger branch resides at New Barrel.

The Quill and Tankard in Oldtown is renowned for its fearsomely strong cider. The septry on the Quiet Isle is also far famed for its sweet cider.

History[ ]

A dozen free folk villages paid homage to Varamyr Sixskins with bread, salt, and cider.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Jon Snow informs Samwell Tarly that hot cider is available at Castle Black.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Brienne of Tarth drinks a cup of cider at the Inn of the Kneeling Man. When Arya Stark visits the inn, Sharna states they have run out of cider.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Pate and his companions drink tankards of strong cider at the Quill and Tankard.

Brienne and Podrick Payne enjoy sweet cider at the Quiet Isle.
