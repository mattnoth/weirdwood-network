Appearance[ ]

Torman wears his worldly wealth upon his person and a lord’s ransom in golden arm rings. Each ring signifies one year's service with the Golden Company.

History[ ]

Torman and his brothers are related to the Peakes of Starpike.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Torman, like the rest of the company, swears his allegiance to Aegon Targaryen in Volon Therys before they invade Westeros.
