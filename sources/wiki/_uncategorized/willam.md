Willam was the brother of Septon Meribald. He died in the War of the Ninepenny Kings.

History[ ]

William and his brothers, Meribald, Robin, Owen and Owen's friend Jon Pox all went off to fight in the War of the Ninepenny Kings. During the War, Willam and Robin died from a fever. Owen died in battle after receiving a blow from a mace and Jon was hanged for rape. Meribald survived to become a septon.
