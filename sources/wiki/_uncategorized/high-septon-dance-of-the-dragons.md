## Full Text

The High Septon was High Septon of the Faith of the Seven during the reign of kings Viserys I and Aegon II Targaryen.

Character[ ]

By 129 AC, he was an old and frail man.

History[ ]

Septon Eustace anointed King Aegon II Targaryen instead of the High Septon, as he was unable to make the travel to King's Landing due to his age and health.

At the begining of the Dance of the Dragons, Ser Otto Hightower, Aegon's Hand, sent the men suspected of supporting Princess Rhaenyra Targaryen's claim on the Iron Throne over Aegon's to the Red Keep's dungeons. The High Septon sent word from the Starry Sept in Oldtown asking after some of the missing.
