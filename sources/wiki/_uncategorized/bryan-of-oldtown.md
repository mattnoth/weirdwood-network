## Full Text

Bryan of Oldtown was a merchant-adventurer and a ship captain who once journeyed through the Shivering Sea.

History[ ]

Hailing from Oldtown in the Reach, Bryan was the captain of the cog Spearshaker.

History[ ]

Bryan reported that the Dothraki language name for the lost race that inhabited the Kingdom of the Ifequevron means "those who walk in the woods". He also reported that the Ibbenese he met claimed these woods walkers bless households that leave offerings of leaf, stone, and water.
