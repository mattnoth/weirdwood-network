History[ ]

Lord Farwynd of the Lonely Light was present at the Great Council of 101 AC at Harrenhal.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

During the kingsmoot, Lord Gylbert Farwynd puts his name forth for King of the Iron Islands, supported by three champions who are his sons. His claim is dismissed quickly, with not even all of the Farwynds supporting him.

Known Lords of the Lonely Light[ ]

Lord Farwynd, present at the Great Council of 101 AC.

Lord Gylbert Farwynd, the current Lord of the Lonely Light.
