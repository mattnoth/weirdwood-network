A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The Windblown, a sellsword company hired by Yunkai, mock him as "Lord Wobblecheeks". Following the death of Yurkhaz zo Yunzak in the fighting pits of Meereen, the Yunkai'i have been daily rotating the duty of Supreme Commander; Ghazdor is among the Yunkish nobles taking part in the rotation.
