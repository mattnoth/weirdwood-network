## Summary

King of the True Men was an ancient, vainglorious title held by House Shett, the former rulers of Gulltown, which supposedly went back ten thousand years to the Dawn Age. After the death of King Osgood III Shett, the Shett crown was claimed by Ser Gerold Grafton, and the Graftons henceforth ruled the city.
