For the family, see House Slate.

Slate is a rock often grey in color. It is frequently used for roofing or flooring.

Culture[ ]

Iron, tin, lead, and slate can be found on the mainland near Braavos. The Ibbenese roof their homes with slate or thatch.

House Slate of Blackpool is a noble house in the north. The houses of White Harbor have steeply pitched roofs of dark grey slate. A weirwood has burst through the slate floor of the Nightfort.

The roofs of the Maidenvault and the Inn of the Kneeling Man are made of slate. A septry in the riverlands and its brewhouse also have slate roofs.

The Hall of the Hundred Hearths in Harrenhal has a smooth slate floor, and the castle's stables have a long slate roof.

The eyes of Lord Yohn Royce and Ser Marlon Manderly are compared to slate grey.

History[ ]

The kitchen of the Aegonfort had a slate roof in case of fire.

Prince Joffrey Velaryon is said to have fallen from Syrax onto a steep roof during the Storming of the Dragonpit, with shards of slate then raining down upon the boy.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Arya Stark sees a long building with a slate roof in a village near the Gods Eye.

Jon Snow sees a slanting ridge of slate during the great ranging into the haunted forest.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord Randyll Tarly's soldiers build a new slate roof for the sept of Maidenpool.
