Appearance and Character[ ]

Khorane has blue eyes and a bony face worn from weather. The captain is a kindly Lyseni who enjoys rich foods like snails and lampreys.

History[ ]

Khorane has spent many years trading with the Seven Kingdoms.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Khorane takes Shayala's Dance into the Spears of the Merling King to rescue Ser Davos Seaworth. The captain gives Davos the use of his own cabin and his own clothes, as well as a dirk and a new pair of boots. Khorane tells Davos about the Battle of the Blackwater, and he delivers Davos to Salladhor Saan at Dragonstone.
