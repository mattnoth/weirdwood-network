History[ ]

Pate is a pig boy, a good-hearted, empty-headed lunkhead, who always manages to succeed, often when his stupidity turns out to be a clumsy sort of cunning. He usually bests fat lords, haughty knights, and pompous septons. The stories all end well for Spotted Pate, including sitting on a high lord's seat or bedding a knight's daughter.

Because of his legendary status, "Pate" is a popular name among the smallfolk of Westeros.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Pate, a novice at the Citadel, hates when people compare him to Pate the pig boy. When Leo Tyrell mocks Pate by calling him "our spotted pig boy", he considers hitting Leo in the mouth with his tankard and knocking out his teeth.

Months later, a man who has killed Pate and replaced him introduces himself to Samwell Tarly as "I'm Pate, like the pig boy."
