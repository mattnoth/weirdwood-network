History[ ]

Lord Drumm was killed by westermen during Lady Johanna Lannister's invasion of the Iron Islands in 134 AC.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord Dunstan Drumm puts forth his name for consideration at the kingsmoot. His two sons, Denys and Donnel, as well as Andrik the Unsmiling, a famed ironborn warrior in his service, champion him. Euron Greyjoy is proclaimed King of the Isles and the North instead. 

After the taking of the Shields, Lord Dunstan along side with Lord Rodrik Harlaw and Lord Gorold Goodbrother discuss their fears that the gains from the battle are little compared to the enmity of the Reach they have earned.
