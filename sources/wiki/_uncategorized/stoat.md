Ermine in the arms of House Rosby

Stoats are small carnivorous mammals found in the known world. The fur from its winter coat is referred to as ermine. The stoat is related to the weasel.

Culture[ ]

Members of House Frey are often compared to stoats on account of their facial features.

Ermine fur is often included in luxurious garments. Ermine spots can be a heraldic tincture; ermine is depicted in the arms of Houses Florent, Gower, Prester, Rosby, Ruthermont, and Varner, as well as the arms of Ser Illifer the Penniless.

History[ ]

Prince Maekar Targaryen wore a heavy black cloak trimmed with ermine during the tourney at Ashford Meadow.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Grand Maester Pycelle's red velvet robe has an ermine collar.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Queen Regent Cersei Lannister wears an ermine cloak when touring the gates of King's Landing.

Rickon Stark wears an ermine mantle during the harvest feast at Winterfell.

Lord Tywin Lannister wears a thick ermine cloak when departing Harrenhal.

While in the House of the Undying, Daenerys Targaryen sees wizards wearing robes of ermine, ruby velvet, and cloth of gold.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

  

Tycho Nestoris wearing robes trimmed with ermine, by Preston Stone © Fantasy Flight Games

Lord Walder Frey's lap is covered with an ermine robe at the Twins.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Cersei wears an old gown of black velvet lined with ermine for the wake of her father, Lord Tywin. Lord Garrison Prester wears ermine when the lords of the westerlands depart King's Landing with Tywin's body.

Lord William Mooton pins his ermine cloak with a salmon-shaped brooch, and Lord Gilwood Hunter uses a brooch with five arrows for his own ermine cloak.

Ser Jaime Lannister and his cousin, Ser Daven Lannister, mock House Frey for resembling stoats.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Val wears an ermine cloak at Castle Black. Selyse Baratheon dons an ermine mantle during the wedding of Sigorn and Alys Karstark, and Gerrick Kingsblood is given an ermine half-cape by Selyse's retinue.

Jeyne Poole, pretending to be Arya Stark, wears an ermine cloak clasped with a silver wolf's head.

Lord Wyman Manderly pins his ermine mantle with a golden trident.

Tycho Nestoris has robes trimmed with ermine.
