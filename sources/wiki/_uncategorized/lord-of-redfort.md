History[ ]

During the Andal invasion, King Robar II Royce formed an alliance of First Men Houses from the Vale to repel the Andals. Robar's forces were defeated in the Battle of the Seven Stars, however, and Torgold Tollett chopped off Lord Redfort's arm during the battle.

A recent Lord Redfort, presumably Horton, had Dacks and later Domeric Bolton as a squire.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Horton Redfort is a suitor to Lady Lysa Arryn.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord Horton is one of the six Lords Declarant at Runestone that demand Lord Petyr Baelish's resignation as Lord Protector of the Vale. Horton forces his youngest son, Ser Mychel, to marry Ysilla Royce.

Known Lords of Redfort[ ]

Lord Redfort, who fought in the Battle of the Seven Stars.

Lord Horton Redfort, the current Lord of Redfort.
