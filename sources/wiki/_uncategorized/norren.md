Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Samwell Tarly goes to the Citadel to talk with Norren, who he thinks is the Seneschal, except Norren was the Seneschal of the Citadel until two months before his arrival. Archmaester Theobald holds the office now.
