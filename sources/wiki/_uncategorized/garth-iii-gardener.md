Garth III Gardener, called Garth the Great, was a King of the Reach and head of House Gardener.

History[ ]

Garth extended the borders of the Reach northward, winning Old Oak, Red Lake, and Goldengrove with pacts of friendship and mutual defense. In the annuals of history the name of Garth III is writ large.
