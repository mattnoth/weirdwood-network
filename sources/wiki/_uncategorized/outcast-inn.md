## Full Text

The Outcast Inn is an inn in Braavos. It is located in the midst of Ragman's Harbor, south of Moroggo's and north of the Mummer's Ship and the Happy Port.

Instead of hosting professional musicians like Moroggo's and the Inn of the Green Eel, the Outcast Inn has its patrons sing instead in drunken voices and half a hundred tongues.
