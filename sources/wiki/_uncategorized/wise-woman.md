## Full Text

The Wise Woman was a trading galley during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

On the seventh day of the second moon of 50 AC, the Wise Woman carried the women chosen to become Queen Alysanne Targaryen's ladies and companions across Blackwater Bay from King's Landing to Dragonstone. These women would later be collectively known as the Wise Women.
