## Summary

Slaver Cities or slaver cities refers to the cities of Astapor, Yunkai, and Meereen, located on the coast of Slaver's Bay on the continent of Essos. Drawing on the culture of the Old Empire of Ghis, the Slaver Cities have been ruled by aristocrats who call themselves "masters". Their wealth derives from engaging in slavery. They are distinct from the Free Cities located to the west, which are either former Valyrian colonies or colonies formed by Valyrian refugees.

History[ ]

The Old Empire of Ghis, one of the first great civilizations of the known world, turned their conquered foes into slaves. The Valyrian Freehold eventually conquered the Ghiscari over the course of five wars, with the Valyrians learning slavery from their defeated foes and incorporating the practice into their own empire.

The remaining Ghiscari cities regained their independence from Valyria in the aftermath of the Doom. They reestablished their own slave trade, now acquiring new slaves by purchasing and breeding them. The Dothraki and corsairs from the Basilisk Isles sell their captives to the Slaver Cities. Tolos and Elyria have developed close ties with the Ghiscari cities.
