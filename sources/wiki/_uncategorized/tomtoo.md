Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

TomToo's father, Tomard, is killed by a gold cloak in King's Landing.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

TomToo plays Lord of the crossing at Winterfell with the Frey wards, Big Walder and Little Walder. 

A Storm of Swords[ ]

The women and children of Winterfell are said to have been taken by Ramsay Snow to the Dreadfort. It is unknown if TomToo is with them or if he died in the sack of Winterfell.
