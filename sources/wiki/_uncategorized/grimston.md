Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

During the taking of the Shields, Grimston falls to Ser Harras Harlaw, who planted his standard beneath its walls and challenged the defenders to face him in single combat. After defeating seven men, two of whom yielded, a septon declared the gods had spoken and surrendered the castle to Harras. Its previous lord, Guthor Grimm, is kept prisoner in Grimston. King Euron Greyjoy names Harras Lord of Greyshield.
