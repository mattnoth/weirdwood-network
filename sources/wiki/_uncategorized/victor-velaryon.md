## Full Text

Victor Velaryon was a member of House Velaryon during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen.

History[ ]

Victor and his brothers Jorgen and Corwyn escorted King Jaehaerys I Targaryen and Queen Alysanne Targaryen when they flew their dragons to Driftmark in 50 AC and toured the shipyards of the island.

Family[ ]

Victor was one of the children (possibly the youngest) of Lord Daemon Velaryon, the Lord of the Tides and the head of House Velaryon during the reign of kings Maegor I and Jaehaerys I Targaryen.
