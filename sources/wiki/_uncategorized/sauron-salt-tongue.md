History[ ]

Sauron declared the ironborn to have originated in the Drowned God's watery halls, made in the god's image, emerging from beneath the seas with dominion over all the waters of the earth.

Quotes[ ]

We did not come to these holy islands from godless lands across the seas. We came from beneath those seas, from the watery halls of the Drowned God who made us in his likeness and gave to us dominion over all the waters of the earth.

—Sauron Salt-Tongue

Behind the Scenes[ ]

Sauron Salt-Tongue's name is probably a reference to the homonymous character from the novel The Lord of the Rings by J. R. R. Tolkien.
