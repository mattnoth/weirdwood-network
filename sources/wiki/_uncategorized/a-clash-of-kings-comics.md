Production[ ]

The comic book series is scripted by comic book writer Landry Walker and drawn by Mel Rubi, with covers by Rubi and by Mike S. Miller. It is intended to follow the story and atmosphere of the novel closely, at a rate of about a page of art for each page of text. It covered 32 issues of 29 pages each.

Publication history[ ]

The initial issue was published by Dynamite Entertainment in June 2017, almost 3 years after the conclusion of the previous adaptation, George R.R. Martin's A Game of Thrones. New issues were published at a rate of one per month. Sixteen issues were released until March 2019, when the series went on hiatus. In January 2020, the adaptation continued in a new volume, marketed as A Clash of Kings, Part II. The series concluded with the 16th issue of the second volume on November 24, 2021. 

Collections[ ]

Four sets of eight issues each were published by Bantam Books as trade paperbacks, marketed as a graphic novel.
