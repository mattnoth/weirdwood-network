Layout[ ]

Thee Great Hall of Bitterbridge is modest in size compared to the halls of larger castles like Winterfell or Riverrun. A stair tower with a low door leads to the keep's roof.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

After the melee at Bitterbridge, Lord Lorent Caswell hosts a farewell feast in the Great Hall for the departing Renly Baratheon. Catelyn Stark is seated on the dais between Lord Mathis Rowan and Ser Jon Fossoway, and Catelyn's retinue are squeezed onto the crowded benches with Renly's knights. A plump fool and a dwarf entertain the guests by pretending to be Ser Jaime Lannister and Tyrion Lannister.
