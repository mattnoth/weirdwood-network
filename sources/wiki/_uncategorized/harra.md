## Summary

After Lord Tywin Lannister takes over Harrenhal, Harra continues in service to the Lannisters. When a captive Arya Stark arrives at Harrenhal, Harra and Amabel inspect her as they do the other captives, and eventually assign her to Weese. After Lord Tywin departs Harrenhal and the castle falls to Lord Roose Bolton, Harra is executed for telling the servants of Lady Shella Whent to serve the Lannisters.
