Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

The Pride of Driftmark is spotted by Davos Seaworth in the harbor of Dragonstone when Stannis Baratheon gathers his sworn swords in preparation for the War of the Five Kings. It is part of Stannis's royal fleet commanded by Ser Imry Florent during the Battle of the Blackwater, and the ship crashes through two Lannister river runners. Pride of Driftmark tries to flee from wildfire during the battle, but green flame races up its oars.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Monford Velaryon is reported to have died with his ship during the battle.
