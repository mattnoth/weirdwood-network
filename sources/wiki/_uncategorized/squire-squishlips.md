## Full Text

Squire Squishlips was the nickname Prince Oberyn Martell gave to an unidentified suitor of his sister Elia Martell during the trip the siblings took in 273 AC, during which their mother hoped to betroth either one of them, or both.

As the Martells stopped at Starfall, the Arbor, Oldtown, the Shield Islands and Crakehall before arriving to Casterly Rock, Squire Squishlips was either a Dayne, a Redwyne, a Hightower, a Grimm, a Chester, a Hewett, a Serry or a Crakehall.
