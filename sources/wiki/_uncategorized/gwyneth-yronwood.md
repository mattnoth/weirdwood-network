Appearance and Character[ ]

At twelve years old, she is a small, scrawny girl whose dark eyes and brown hair set her apart in a house of blue-eyed blondes. She is clever and as quick with words as with her hands.

History[ ]

While Prince Quentyn Martell was fostered at Yronwood, Gwyneth became fond of him and hoped to marry him after she had flowered.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Her older brother, Ser Cletus, has recently passed while traveling with Prince Quentyn Martell on his mission to meet with Daenerys Targaryen in the Free Cities.
