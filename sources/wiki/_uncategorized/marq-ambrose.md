## Full Text

Marq Ambrose was the head of House Ambrose during the civil war known as the Dance of the Dragons.

History[ ]

During the civil war known as the Dance of the Dragons, Marq was loyal to the greens. When the Caltrops assembled to conspire against the Two Betrayers, Marq was of the opinion to take King's Landing before killing them. Marq was slain two days later during the Second Battle of Tumbleton.

Notes[ ]
