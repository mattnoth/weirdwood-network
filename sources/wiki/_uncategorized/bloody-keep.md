Layout[ ]

Located on its own island, the Bloody Keep is one of the larger buildings of Pyke. Although its halls are larger and better furnished than those of the Sea Tower, it is still cold and damp. The gloomy suite which inspires the Bloody Keep's name has high ceilings. A covered stone walkway connects the Bloody Keep and the Great Keep.

History[ ]

King Qhored I Hoare defeated King Bernarr II Justman in battle and took his three sons back to the Iron Islands as hostages. When Bernarr was slow in paying tribute three years later, Qhored hacked the boys to pieces in their beds at Pyke and cut their hearts out. The keep where the boys were slaughtered has since been known as the Bloody Keep.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Theon Greyjoy stays in a gloomy suite of the Bloody Keep after returning to Pyke, and Helya attends to him.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

In the US edition of A Clash of Kings, the Bloody Keep is also called "Guest Keep" in an instance. That is likely an error, since in the UK edition only the name "Bloody Keep" is used.
