History[ ]

Bran Stark likes to climb a tall sentinel in the godswood of Winterfell and then cross the roofs of the armory and the Guards Hall to reach the blind side of the First Keep.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Bran Stark sees the red comet in the sky above the Bell Tower and the Guards Hall.

The guards speak to each other in their hall about Bran's shouting and sweating in the night.

During the capture of Winterfell by the ironborn, Bran hears shouting beyond the Guards Hall.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Sansa Stark includes the guardshall while making a snow castle of Winterfell at the Eyrie.
