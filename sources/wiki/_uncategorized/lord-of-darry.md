Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Ser Raymun Darry hosts King Robert I Baratheon at Darry. Raymun is not formally mentioned as "Lord of Darry", but Lord Eddard Stark thinks of him as the castle's lord. It is possible that Raymun's father was still alive, having retired from active rule in the years after the rebellion, with Raymun being the de facto lord.

Raymun is killed by Ser Gregor Clegane in the battle at the Mummer's Ford. According to a semi-canon source, his father, the current lord, dies in the battle as well. His son, Lyman Darry, is among the nobles assembled at Riverrun.

A Clash of Kings[ ]

The child lord Lyman is killed by Gregor in the sack of Darry.

After the Battle of the Blackwater, Darry is granted to Ser Lancel Lannister.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

When the brotherhood without banners lists victims of House Lannister in the riverlands, Tom of Sevenstreams mentions "Ser Raymun Darry, Lord Darry, young Lord Darry."  It is possible that "Lord Darry" and "young Lord Darry" refer respectively to Raymun's father and son.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lancel renounces Darry to join the Warrior's Sons.

Known Lords and Ladies of Darry[ ]

Unnamed Lord Darry, during the time of Vorian Vypren.

Unnamed Lord Darry, during the reign of Humfrey I Teague who died at the Battle of Six Kings.

Lord Darnold Darry, head of the House during the reign of King Maegor I Targaryen.

Unnamed Lord Darry, one of the blacks during the Dance of the Dragons. He died on the battlements of Darry burned by Vhagar.

Lord Derrick Darry, one of the blacks during the Dance of the Dragons.

Lord Roland Darry, one of the blacks during the Dance.

Unnamed Lord Darry, during the First Blackfyre Rebellion who cut down the black dragon sign at the inn at the crossroads.

Lord Deremond was presumably a Darry. There is a song about his exploits at the Bloody Meadow. Some of its verses are: "And there he stood with sword in hand, the last of Darry's ten...".

Unnamed Lord Darry. Brynden Tully and Norbert Vance were his squires in their youth.

Unnamed Lord Darry, Raymun, Mariya and Jeyne Darry's grandfather.

Unnamed Lord Darry, Raymun, Mariya and Jeyne's father.

Lyman Darry, Raymun's son and heir.

Lancel Lannister, founder of House Lannister of Darry. He later renounces his lordship and joins the Warrior's Sons.

Amerei Frey, Lancel's former wife and daughter of Mariya, the current Lady of Darry.
