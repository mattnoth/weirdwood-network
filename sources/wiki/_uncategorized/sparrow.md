Culture[ ]

The captain's sister included two sparrows on a branch when she painted the double doors of her home near the Seven Swords in Duskendale.

History[ ]

Small brown sparrows were part of the immense pie at the wedding at Whitewalls.

Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

Bran Stark likes the tiny sparrows which nest between the cracks of Winterfell.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

When Arya Stark tries to escape Harwin on horseback in the riverlands, sparrows fly from the branches of an alder.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lowly members of the Faith of the Seven begin calling themselves sparrows as they march toward King's Landing, and they force the Most Devout to choose their leader, the so-called High Sparrow, as the next High Septon.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Varamyr, a skinchanger, briefly takes possession of a sparrow before ending up in One Eye.

Quotes[ ]

The sparrow is the humblest and most common of birds, as we are the humblest and most common of men.

—A septon to Creighton Longbough
