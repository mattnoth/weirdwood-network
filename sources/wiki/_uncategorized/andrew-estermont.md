Appearance[ ]

Andrew has a long pointy beard, and bushy brown eyebrows. He is very tall, standing a head above Davos Seaworth.

History[ ]

The only son of Ser Lomas Estermont, Andrew is a former squire of Lord Stannis Baratheon.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser Andrew is listed as supporting King Stannis Baratheon at the beginning of the War of the Five Kings.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

One of the king's men, Andrew is considered one of the few men Stannis can still rely on after the Battle of the Blackwater. Andrew supports Lord Davos Seaworth, and escorts Edric Storm when Davos arranges for the boy to leave Dragonstone on the Mad Prendos, in order to protect him from Melisandre's fires.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Andrew sails across the narrow sea to Essos, on the Mad Prendos commanding Edric's escort.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Andrew hides in Lys with Edric and his protectors.
