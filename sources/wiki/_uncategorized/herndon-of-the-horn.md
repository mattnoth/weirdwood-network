History[ ]

According to legend, Herndon and his twin brother Harlon the Hunter built their castle atop Horn Hill and took to wife the beautiful woods witch who dwelled there, sharing her favors for a hundred years. The brothers did not age so long as they had intercourse with her whenever the moon was full.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

The twin brothers Herndon and Harlon are possibly inspired by King Herla and Herne the Hunter, two mythological figures related to the Wild Hunt, an European folklore motif.
