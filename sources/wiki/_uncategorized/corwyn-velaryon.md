History[ ]

When King Jaehaerys I Targaryen and Queen Alysanne Targaryen flew their dragons to Driftmark and toured the shipyards, they were escorted by Corwyn and his brothers Jorgen and Victor.

As Corwyn predeceased his father, his eldest son, Ser Corlys, succeeded Lord Daemon as the Lord of the Tides and Master of Driftmark.
