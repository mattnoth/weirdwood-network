History[ ]

After giving birth to a bastard boy in 48 AC, Coryanne Wylde was closely watched within the walls of the Rain House. She was eventually summoned to King's Landing to serve Queen Alysanne Targaryen.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The stormlands are invaded with the landing of the Golden Company. While Harry Strickland and Jon Connington head for Griffin's Roost and Tristan Rivers for Crow's Nest, Laswell Peake marches on Rain House. Harry believes that if Tristan and Laswell are successful the Golden Company will control most of Cape Wrath. Rain House falls quickly.
