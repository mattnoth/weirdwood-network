Culture[ ]

Some Westerosi consider ghouls to be rotting corpses; it is unknown if they are similar to wights.

According to Old Nan, wildlings consort with giants and ghouls beyond the Wall.

Maesters consider Sothoryi who feast on their own dead to be ghouls.

History[ ]

Yeen in Sothoryos is threatened by ghouls. When Nymeria led the Rhoynar to Sothoryos, the Rhoynish colony at Yeen was attacked by brindled ghouls.

Rangers of the Night's Watch claim that the ruins of Hardhome are haunted by ghouls, demons, and bloodthirsty burning ghosts.
