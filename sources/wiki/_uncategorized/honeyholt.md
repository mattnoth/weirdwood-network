## Full Text

Honeyholt

The Reach and the location of Honeyholt

Honeyholt is the seat of House Beesbury in the Reach. It is situated along the eastern bank of the Honeywine river between Brightwater Keep and Oldtown.

In 44 AC, Ser Morgan Hightower was slain by a group of Warrior's Sons  led by Ser Joffrey Doggett on the road to Honeyholt.

After the election of Septon Alfyn to the seat of High Septon, King Jaehaerys I Targaryen visited Bandallon, Three Towers, Uplands, and Honeyholt while awaiting the arrival of the injured Alfyn to Oldtown.
