Culture[ ]

  

Jet decorates the armor of Tytos Blackwood, by Wibben © Fantasy Flight Games

Lord Tytos Blackwood owns bright yellow armor inlaid with jet in elaborate vine-and-leaf patterns. The helm of Ser Marlon Manderly depicts the merling king with a beard of jade and jet.

The broken wheel of House Waynwood is picked out in beads of jet on the green mantle of Lady Anya Waynwood.

Bran Stark has a wolf's-head brooch of silver and polished jet. One of the brooches of Ser Brynden Tully is a black fish wrought in jet and gold.

Illyrio Mopatis has jet among his various rings.

Oznak zo Pahl wears armor with copper and jet scales.

The dark hair of characters like Boremund Baratheon, Renly Baratheon, Edric Storm, Alleras, and Arianne Martell can be called jet black.

Some Westerosi compare the dark skin of Summer Islanders like Alayaya, Quhuru Mo, and Kojja Mo to polished jet.

History[ ]

Jet and jade were among the goods on the Treasure when it reached Pentos after a voyage to the Jade Sea.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Renly Baratheon wears a cloth-of-gold cape with the black stag of House Baratheon picked out in flakes of jet.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Victarion Greyjoy thinks of the skin of Moqorro as even blacker than jet.
