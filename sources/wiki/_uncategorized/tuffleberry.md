Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Weese sends Arya Stark to give him a message concerning some ale that Weese has for him. Tuffleberry sends her back with a nastier message.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

On his way to lifting the siege of Riverrun, Ser Jaime Lannister returns to Harrenhal and puts the castle back to order. Ben Blackthumb, Pia, and a cook are the only servants from Lady Shella Whent's time who are still around. Tuffleberry's fate is unknown.
