Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Pate is among the orphans taken in by Jeyne and Willow Heddle at the crossroads inn. When Brienne of Tarth and her party are allowed to stay at the inn, Pate is sent alongside Tansy to get wood for the inn's fire.
