## Full Text

The Adventurers were a sellsword company in Essos.

History[ ]

In 56 AC, the Adventurers were caught between a great fire that swept across the Disputed Lands, fanned by strong winds and fed by dry grasses on one side, and a Tyroshi host under the command of the Archon of Tyrosh, whom they were fighting, on the other side. Most of the Adventurers ultimately chose to die fighting the Tyroshi rather than be burned alive. Not a single man of the Adventurers survived.
