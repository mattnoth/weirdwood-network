Pink Graces are priestesses in the religion of the Ghiscari, though their specialty among the various Graces is as yet unknown. They can attend the Green Grace, the high priestess at the Temple of the Graces.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The Green Grace of Meereen, Galazza Galare, is attended by four Pink Graces when she meets with Ser Barristan Selmy in the Great Pyramid after Daenerys Targaryen's disappearance on Drogon. The Pink Graces array themselves behind her and lower their eyes and clasp their hands before them when Galazza sits down to talk with Barristan.
