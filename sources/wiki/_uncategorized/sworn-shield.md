Duties[ ]

Many lords and lordlings keep a retinue of knights and sworn swords in their service, including at court. Sworn swords can fight in their lord's host during wartime.

The Kingsguard are a specialized and most prestigious type of sworn shield, sworn to the King of the Andals, the Rhoynar, and the First Men. However, an individual Kingsguard may be appointed the sworn shield of another member of the royal family, and a non-Kingsguard may also serve as the king's sworn shield.
