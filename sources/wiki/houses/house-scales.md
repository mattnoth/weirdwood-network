History[ ]

Ser Elyas Scales was the master-at-arms at Dragonstone during the regency of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen. Ser Elyas was known to spar with the young king during his training.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser Benethon Scales is in service to King Stannis Baratheon, and is among the knights known as the queen's men. When Stannis goes to take Deepwood Motte, Benethon remains with Queen Selyse, later accompanying her to the Nightfort when she travels from Eastwatch to Castle Black. Benethon is present when Selyse refuses Lord Commander Jon Snow aid in rescuing the wildlings, and later appears in the Shieldhall when Jon Snow informs his men about his plans for Hardhome and the letter he has received from Ramsay Bolton.
