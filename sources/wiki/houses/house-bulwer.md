## Full Text

House Bulwer of Blackcrown is a noble house from the Reach, sworn to the Hightowers of Oldtown. Blackcrown is located on the northern coast of Whispering Sound.

According to semi-canon sources their arms depict a bull's skull, bone over blood. Their words do not appear in the books, but according to semi-canon sources they are Death Before Disgrace.

History[ ]

  

Blackcrown knights. © FFG

According to legend, House Bulwer descends from Bors the Breaker, a son of Garth Greenhand.

According to semi-canon source, Ser Humphrey Bulwer attended the tourney at Ashford Meadow in 209 AC.

Ser Buford Bulwer fought at the Battle of the Redgrass Field in 196 AC, where he slew forty men, or so he claimed. In 212 AC, he participated in the wedding tourney at Whitewalls where he was defeated by Ser Uthor Underleaf in five tilts.

In more recent years, Lord Jon Bulwer, the father of the current lady of the house, died of a summer fever.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lady Alysanne Bulwer, a child of eight, is among Margaery Tyrell's companions at King's Landing.

Black Jack Bulwer stays at Castle Black and survives the battle against the wildlings.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Black Jack and twelve seasoned rangers escort Maester Aemon, Samwell Tarly and Gilly to Eastwatch-by-the-Sea.

Margaery plays come-into-my-castle with little Alysanne.

Lady Merryweather reports to Queen Cersei rumors that Megga Tyrell is planning to wed Lady Bulwer's brother. It is unknown if this Lady Bulwer refers to Alysanne, and if so why did he not inherit the lordship.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Lord Commander Jon Snow makes Black Jack First Ranger and sends him ranging beyond the Wall, but he is slain by the Weeper.

House Bulwer at the end of the third century[ ]

The known Bulwers during the timespan of the events described in A Song of Ice and Fire are:

Lady Alysanne Bulwer, the Lady of Blackcrown. A young girl.

Lord {Jon Bulwer}, her late father. The previous lord of the house, died of a summer fever.

Victaria Tyrell, her mother and Jon's widow.

Her brother.

With unspecified familiar relationship with the lord of the house there are also:

Jack Bulwer, known as Black Jack Bulwer, one of the rangers at Castle Black.

Household[ ]

Maester Normund, counselor, healer, and tutor. Formerly of House Tyrell.
