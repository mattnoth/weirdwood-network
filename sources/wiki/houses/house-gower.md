Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser Gerald Gower remains in the service of Stannis Baratheon after the Battle of the Blackwater. One of the king's men, Gerald questions Stannis's new faith in R'hllor after the defeat.

Gerald is among the conspirators who smuggle Edric Storm out of Dragonstone on behalf of Lord Davos Seaworth.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Gerald helps protect Edric as the boy hides in Lys.

House Gower at the end of the third century[ ]

The known Gowers during the timespan of the events described in A Song of Ice and Fire are:

Ser Gerald Gower, a knight in service at Dragonstone.
