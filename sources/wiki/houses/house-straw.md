History[ ]

In 50 AC, Ser Casper Straw and thirty armed septons defended Princess Rhaella Targaryen, a novice of the Faith, when Ser Orryn Baratheon tried to take her into custody at the motherhouse attached to the Starry Sept in Oldtown.
