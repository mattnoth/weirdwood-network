House Foxglove at the end of the third century[ ]

The known Foxgloves during the timespan of the events described in A Song of Ice and Fire are:

Ser Willam Foxglove, a knight sworn to King Stannis Baratheon. He may or may not be Lord Foxglove, who is also in Stannis's army in the north.
