History[ ]

Ser Harry Penny, a hedge knight from the riverlands, and Ser Adrian Tarbeck died fighting each other during the Dance of the Dragons.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser Corliss Penny accompanies Stannis Baratheon to the Wall. He is one of the queen's men who support Melisandre and Selyse Baratheon.

Jon Penny is a young orphan at the inn at the crossroads. It is unknown if he is related to Corliss.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Corliss commands Stannis's guards at Castle Black and participates in the march on Winterfell.

House Penny at the end of the third century[ ]

The known Pennys during the timespan of the events described in A Song of Ice and Fire are:

Ser Corliss Penny, a knight sworn to Stannis Baratheon and one of the queen's men.

Unconfirmed:

Jon Penny, an orphan taken in by Willow Heddle at the crossroads inn.
