History[ ]

House Broome was among the pious noble houses who openly sided with the Faith Militant uprising against King Maegor I Targaryen. Eventually Maegor the Cruel used his dragon Balerion to burn out their castle, along with the castles of other houses in the westerlands such as the Falwells, Lorches, and Doggetts.

Prentys Tully, who was Lord of Riverrun during the uprising, was married to Lucinda Broome, who was noted for her piety.

House of the Dragon[ ]

In the television series House of the Dragon, Ser Alfred Broome has a sigil on his armor that resembles House Broom's  coat of arms. It could mean that, at least in the show canon, the Brooms and the Broomes are the same house spelled in different ways.
