History[ ]

Ser Symond Crayne was a knight in the Kingsguard of King Maegor I Targaryen. After the ascension of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen, Symond was sentenced to take the black.

When a war of succession ensued in the Vale after the death of Lady Jeyne Arryn, House Crayne supported her chosen successor, Ser Joffrey Arryn.
