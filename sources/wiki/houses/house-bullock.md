History[ ]

The Bullocks served House Targaryen. Ser Merrell Bullock was a knight who was the commander of the castle garrison at Dragonstone during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen. He had two sons, Ser Alyn and Ser Howard.

Historical Members[ ]

Ser Merrell Bullock, commander of the garrison of Dragonstone, dismissed by Rhaena Targaryen for failing to prevent the theft of dragon eggs by Elissa Farman. 

Ser Alyn Bullock, his eldest son.

Ser Howard Bullock, his younger son, left his wife and fled with Lady Coryanne Wylde to Pentos.
