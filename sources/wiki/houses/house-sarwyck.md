## Full Text

House Sarwyck of Riverspring is a noble house from Riverspring in the westerlands. The family also owns a manse in King's Landing. They have thus far only been mentioned in the video game Game of Thrones.

Game of Thrones[ ]

The known Sarwycks appearing in Game of Thrones are:

Ser {Alester Sarwyck}, Raynald's eldest son, a red priest recently living in Braavos. His cause of death is determined by the player's actions.

{Elyana Sarwyck}, Raynald's daughter, beheaded by Ser Valarr Hill.

{Gawen Sarwyck}, Raynald's second son, slain by Endrew.

Ser {Valarr Hill}, Raynald's bastard son and leader of the Bloodseekers, impaled on the Iron Throne during a fight with Alester Sarwyck and Mors Westford.

Ryman Sarwyck, Alester's cousin and captain of the guard.
