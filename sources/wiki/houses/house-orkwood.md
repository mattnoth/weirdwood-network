History[ ]

With the exception of House Goodbrother, all of the great houses of the Iron Islands claim descent from the Grey King.

Rognar II Greyiron, King of the Iron Islands, was brought down by an alliance of ironborn lords and Andals which included the Orkwoods.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

  

House Orkwood reaver, by Aaron Riley © Fantasy Flight Games

The Orkwoods support Euron Greyjoy with twenty longships. Some Orkwoods gather to wish Victarion Greyjoy well on the shores of Nagga's Cradle. Aeron Greyjoy spots the banners of House Orkwood among those gathered for the kingsmoot on Old Wyk. Lord Alyn Orkwood, called Orkwood of Orkmont, supports Euron instead of Victarion during the kingsmoot.
