House Fisher of the Stony Shore was a noble house who ruled the Stony Shore along the western coast of what is now the north. It is unknown if they had any connection with House Fisher of the Misty Isle in the riverlands.

History[ ]

The Fishers of the Stony Shore were First Men kings after the Long Night who were eventually defeated and reduced to vassals by the Kings of Winter, House Stark of Winterfell. The Fishers have not been mentioned in A Song of Ice and Fire and are possibly extinct.
