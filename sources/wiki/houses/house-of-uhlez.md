Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The House is hostile to the rule of Daenerys Targaryen and their household are suspected of being members of the Sons of the Harpy.

Since the escape of the wild dragons Rhaegal and Viserion, the pyramid of Uhlez has become the lair of Viserion.
