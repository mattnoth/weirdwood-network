## Summary

Uthero Zalyne was a Sealord of Braavos, in the century after the founding of Braavos. He  dispatched envoys of the Iron Bank to Valyria to pay settlements to the grandchildren of the owners of the ships Braavos' founders had seized - but not the value of the escaped slaves. The dragonlords turned out to have little interest in the descendants of slaves who had escaped a century before. Several years later, Uthero sent forth ships throughout the world, announcing the existence of Braavos, and inviting the men of all nations to celebrate the 111th anniversary of the city's founding. However, even after it unveiled itself, Braavos continued to be known as the Secret City, with its exact location hidden for another two centuries. The anniversary of the Uncloaking of Uthero is celebrated every year.
