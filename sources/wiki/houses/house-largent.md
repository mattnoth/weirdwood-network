History[ ]

Ser Luthor Largent was the Commander of the gold cloaks during the Dance of the Dragons. During the fall of King's Landing in 130 AC, Luthor and his gold cloaks turned on the greens, as they were loyal to Prince Daemon Targaryen, their former commander. Luthor personally killed his second-in-command, Ser Gwayne Hightower. After Queen Rhaenyra Targaryen took the Iron Throne, she ennobled Luthor as a reward for his loyalty, forming House Largent.

Later in 130 AC, Luthor was killed during the riots in the city; it is unknown if he was the last of his line.
