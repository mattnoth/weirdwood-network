Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Harry Strickland is the captain-general of the Golden Company. The landing of the Golden Company begins their effort to place Aegon Targaryen on the Iron Throne.

House Strickland at the end of the third century[ ]

The known Stricklands during the timespan of the events described in A Song of Ice and Fire are:

Ser Harry Strickland, the current captain-general and former paymaster of the Golden Company.

Historical Members[ ]

Unnamed Strickland, the great-grandsire of Harry. He supported Daemon I Blackfyre during his rebellion and lost his lands when he went into exile in Essos. He was part of the Golden Company from its founding by Ser Aegor Rivers in 212 AC.

His son, Harry's grandsire. He was part of the Golden Company.

His son, Harry's father. He was part of the Golden Company.
