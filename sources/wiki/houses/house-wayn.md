House Wayn is a noble house from the riverlands. According to semi-canon sources, they blazon their arms with four wheels countercharged on quartered field blue and white.

It is unknown whether the Wayns are related to House Waynwood.

History[ ]

Lord Tristifer Wayn participated in the battle at the Great Fork during the Faith Militant uprising. Visenya Targaryen used Vhagar to set aflame in a single night the seats of Houses Wayn, Blanetree, Deddings, Lychester, and Terrick.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Utherydes Wayn is the steward of Riverrun and is of an age with Lord Hoster Tully.

House Wayn at the end of the third century[ ]

The known Wayns during the timespan of the events described in A Song of Ice and Fire are:
