Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Brienne of Tarth recalls that Robin Potter participated in a bet beneath the walls of Highgarden to see who would be the first to claim her maidenhead. After the bet was discovered and ended, Brienne sought out and defeated Robin during the melee at Bitterbridge, leaving him with a scar on his head. He survived the Battle of the Blackwater.
