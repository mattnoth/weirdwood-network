## Full Text

There are two branches of House Shett in the Vale:

 House Shett of Gull Tower

 House Shett of Gulltown

It is unknown which is the original line of House Shett, once holding the title of King of the True Men and ruling the city of Gulltown, and which is the cadet branch. Currently both branches are described as houses of landed knights.

Notes[ ]

↑ The kings' line of the Shetts may be House Shett of Gull Tower, since they blazon their arms with golden wings, a color commonly associated with royalty, and reside in Gull Tower, a seat separated from the other noble families of Gulltown, including the other branch of House Shett.
