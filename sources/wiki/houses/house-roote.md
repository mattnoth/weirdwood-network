## Summary

House Roote of Lord Harroway's Town is a noble house from Harroway in the riverlands. According to semi-canon sources, they blazon their arms with a two-headed horse, brown, on a field of wavy green and green. They may have taken their sigil from Old King Andahar, as the ferry boat at Harroway is referred to as the king's two-headed water horse.

House Roote of Lord Harroway's Town is a noble house from Harroway in the riverlands. According to semi-canon sources, they blazon their arms with a two-headed horse, brown, on a field of wavy green and green. They may have taken their sigil from Old King Andahar, as the ferry boat at Harroway is referred to as the king's two-headed water horse.

History[ ]

Ser Richard Roote was one of the first knights of the Kingsguard when the order was founded in 10 AC.

Lord Roote supported Prince Aegon Targaryen in his unsuccessful war against King Maegor I Targaryen.

House Roote had acquired Lord Harroway's Town by the start of the Dance of the Dragons, and they supported Rhaenyra Targaryen during the civil war. The Rootes helped to capture Stone Hedge, but Ser Criston Cole later defeated Lord Roote, Lord Darry, and Ser Oswald Wode along the Gods Eye and Prince Aemond Targaryen burned Harroway with Vhagar.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser Andrey Charlton and his squire, Lucas Roote, are killed by the forces of House Lannister during the War of the Five Kings.

Sandor Clegane and Arya Stark visit Lord Harroway's Town and find it washed out by the overflowing Trident.

 Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser Jaime Lannister spots the banner of House Roote among those of the river lords who have bent the knee and now aid House Lannister in the siege of Riverrun.
