## Summary

House Melcolm of Old Anchor is a noble house from Old Anchor in the Vale of Arryn. It is one of the principal houses sworn to House Arryn. According to semi-canon sources, they blazon their arms with a rusty anchor on turquoise beneath yellow chief with three blue plates.
