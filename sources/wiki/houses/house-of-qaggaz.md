Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Tyrion Lannister, Penny, and Ser Jorah Mormont are purchased by Yezzan zo Qaggaz at a slave auction outside Meereen. One of Yezzan's nephews is killed by sellswords of House Targaryen, and Yezzan himself dies of the pale mare.

House of Qaggaz at the end of the third century[ ]

Yezzan zo Qaggaz, mocked as the Yellow Whale and Lord Yellowbelly, dies of the pale mare.

{Yezzan's nephew}, slain by Targaryen sellswords.

Yezzan's three other nephews, his heirs who command his slave soldiers.

Household[ ]

{Nurse}, a slave overseer, poisoned by Tyrion Lannister.

Slaves: Bearded woman, Goat boy, Tyrion Lannister, Jorah Mormont, Penny, Scar, Sweets, and the Two-headed girl.
