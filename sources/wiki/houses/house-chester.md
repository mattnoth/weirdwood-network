## Full Text

House Chester of Greenshield rules over Greenshield, one of the Shield Islands of the Reach.

According to semi-canon sources they blazon their arms with a green hand on a gold escutcheon on a green field, a border of red rayonne. Their words do not appear in the books.

History[ ]

The Shield Islands are a considerable naval power and were the first line of defense during the days when the ironborn pirates tried to sail up the Mander to sack the wealth of the Reach. 

In 89 AC, King Jaehaerys I Targaryen approved of his granddaughter's, Princess Rhaenys Targaryen, choice to marry Lord Corlys Velaryon while being the host of Lord Chester in Greenshield.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lord Chester is slain during the attack of the ironborn lead by Euron Greyjoy. After the taking of the Shields, King Euron gives Greenshield to Maron Volmark, a young boy of six-and-ten years.

House Chester at the end of the third century[ ]

The known Chesters during the timespan of the events described in A Song of Ice and Fire are:

Lord {Moribald Chester}, Lord of Greenshield. Slain in the taking of the Shields.

Historical Members[ ]

Lord Chester, host of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen during his royal progress in the Reach in 89 AC.
