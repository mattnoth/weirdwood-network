History[ ]

Jonquil Darke, known as the Scarlet Shadow, was the first known Darke. Being the bastard daughter of Lord Darklyn, it is possible she was the founder of the house; alternatively, the house may have been founded by a potential sibling of hers.

Jonquil became a sworn shield of Queen Alysanne Targaryen and dueled a wildling woman at the court of Lord Theomore Manderly when Alysanne visited White Harbor.

The Darkes sided with the blacks in the Dance of the Dragons. Ser Harrold Darke was killed at Dragonstone in defense of Queen Rhaenyra Targaryen.

Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

The servant with a liver-colored birthmark who assists Brienne of Tarth at the Seven Swords in Duskendale claims she is a Darke by birth.
