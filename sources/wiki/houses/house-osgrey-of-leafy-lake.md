## Full Text

House Osgrey of Leafy Lake was a noble house from Leafy Lake in the Reach and was a cadet branch of House Osgrey of Standfast.

History[ ]

Its last known head of house was the uncle of Ser Eustace Osgrey who had at least two daughters. It is unknown if he was a maternal or paternal uncle, and whether he was an Osgrey of Leafy Lake by birth or by marriage. By the time of The Sworn Sword (211 AC), the house had become extinct.
