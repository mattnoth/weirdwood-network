## Full Text

House Waterman is a noble house from the north. According to semi-canon sources they blazon their arms with brown crossed oars on white, between a pair of blue flaunches.

Their motto and seat have not been mentioned in the books. Given their name and their sigil depicting oars on white, their seat may be located along the White Knife.
