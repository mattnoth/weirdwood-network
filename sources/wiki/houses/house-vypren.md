History[ ]

During the coming of the Andals to the riverlands, the Andal warlord Vorian Vypren and his knights were held at bay at the Widow's Ford for a day and a night by three sons of Lord Darry before finally slaying the Darrys.

Lord Vypren supported Harwyn Hardhand, King of the Iron Islands, when he warred against the Storm King Arrec Durrandon.

Lady Sabitha Vypren was married to the gallant Lord Forrest Frey and had at least one son with him. Her father and brothers led the Vyprens in supporting the blacks against the greens during the Dance of the Dragons, as did the widowed Sabitha herself with House Frey.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord Lucias Vypren is married to Lythene Frey, the daughter of Lord Walder Frey and his third wife, Amarei Crakehall. Lucias and his son, Ser Damon, attend the wedding at the Twins of Lord Edmure Tully and Roslin Frey, which becomes the Red Wedding.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Lucias tracks a band of outlaws who may have been part of the group that hanged Merrett Frey all the way to Fairmarket before losing them.

House Vypren at the end of the third century[ ]

The known Vyprens during the timespan of the events described in A Song of Ice and Fire are:

Lord Lucias Vypren, the head of the house.

Lythene Frey, his wife.

Elyana Vypren, his eldest child. Married to Ser Jon Wylde.

Ser Damon Vypren, his son and heir.

Historical Vyprens[ ]

Vorian Vypren, an Andal warlord.

Lord Vypren, ruling during the reign of the Storm King Arrec Durrandon.

Lady Sabitha Vypren, married to Lord Forrest Frey.

Sabitha's father and brothers, who fought alongside her at the Second Battle of Tumbleton.
