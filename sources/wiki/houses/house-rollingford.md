History[ ]

When the realm began to turn against King Maegor I Targaryen for Prince Jaehaerys Targaryen, Lord Rollingford was among the score of lesser lords who gathered at his command. However, when Maegor mysteriously died upon the Iron Throne, Lords Rollingford and Buckwell sailed for Pentos. When the young King Jaehaerys I resided at Dragonstone, Lord Rollingford was one of the several lords who traveled to the island to speak with the king.

Ser Herman Rollingford was slain dueling a bravo in Braavos in 133 AC.
