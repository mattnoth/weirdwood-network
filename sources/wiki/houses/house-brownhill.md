House Brownhill is an extinct noble house from Dorne. Their arms are not yet given.

History[ ]

The Brownhills were among the dozen First Men houses of the Greenblood who chose a High King of Dorne from amongst their number. Three of the houses were destroyed after a generation of warfare following a disputed election, however. The remaining houses were conquered during the coming of the Andals.
