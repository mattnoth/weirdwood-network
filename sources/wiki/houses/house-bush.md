## Summary

Ser Owen Bush was a knight of the Kingsguard in the reign of Maegor I Targaryen. It was widely rumored that he had been ordered to cut out Queen Ceryse Hightower's tongue, though most historians consider this slander by Maegor's enemies. Ser Owen was found dead outside a brothel in 48 AC, with his member cut off and stuffed in his mouth.
