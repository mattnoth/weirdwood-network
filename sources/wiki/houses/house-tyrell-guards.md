Recent Events[ ]

A Game of Thrones[ ]

  

Lady Olenna's Guard, by Rafal Hrynkiewicz © FFG

Igon Vyrwel is the captain of the guard for Mace Tyrell, Lord of Highgarden.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lady Olenna Tyrell is protected by Arryk and Erryk, tall guards in gilded halfhelms and green cloaks edged in gold satin.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser Willam Wythers serves as the captain of the guard in King's Landing for the new queen, Margaery Tyrell. Queen Regent Cersei Lannister has one of his men, Hugh Clifton, arrested for allegedly sleeping with Margaery.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Green-cloaked spearmen protect Mace, the new Hand of the King to Tommen I Baratheon, in the Red Keep.
