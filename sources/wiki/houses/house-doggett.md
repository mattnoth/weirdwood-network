History[ ]

During the Faith Militant uprising, Ser Joffrey Doggett was chosen as the new Grand Captain of the Warrior's Sons, becoming a prominent rebel commander in the campaign against King Maegor I Targaryen. As a result, Maegor the Cruel later used Balerion to set aflame the seat of the Doggetts and reducing it to ash, burning alive the father, mother, and young sister of Joffrey, alongside their sworn swords, serving men, and chattel.

Joffrey Doggett survived Maegor's reign and made peace with his nephew, King Jaehaerys I Targaryen. With the Faith Militant abolished, Jaehaerys granted him a place in his Kingsguard.
