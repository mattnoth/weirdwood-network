History[ ]

In 42 AC, during the Faith Militant uprising, Lord Jon Lychester was among the pious lords who fought King Maegor I Targaryen in the battle at the Great Fork of the Blackwater. The following year, Visenya Targaryen mounted Vhagar to set aflame several keeps and castles, including the seat of House Lychester.

Lord Lymond Lychester took six wounds defending a bridge from Ser Maynard. All of Lymond's sons died during Robert's Rebellion, some fighting with the Targaryens, some with Lord Robert Baratheon.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

The brotherhood without banners visit Lord Lychester's keep, but the old knight has wandering wits, and only repeats the story of his battle against Ser Maynard. Maester Roone gives them information on Lord Beric Dondarrion's supposed whereabouts, and directs them to find the Lady of the Leaves.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser Jaime Lannister spots the banner of House Lychester among those of the riverlords who have bent the knee and now aid House Lannister in the siege of Riverrun.
