## Full Text

House Westford is a minor house of landed knights from the westerlands. They have thus far only been mentioned in the video game Game of Thrones.

Game of Thrones[ ]

The known Westfords appearing in Game of Thrones are:

Ser Mors Westford, a ranger of the Night's Watch and a skinchanger. His fate is determined by the player's choices.

His wife, {Cerenna}, killed by Ser Alester Sarwyck.

Their daughter, {Tya}, killed by Ser Valarr Hill.

Lord Daven Westford, the former Lord of the Westford family and father to Mors.

Lord Amory Westford, who survived the Field of Fire after praying over a statuette of the Warrior.
