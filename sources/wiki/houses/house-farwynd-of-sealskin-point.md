## Full Text

House Farwynd of Sealskin Point is a branch of House Farwynd from the Iron Islands. Their seat is Sealskin Point on the northwestern coast of Great Wyk. Neither their arms nor their motto have yet appeared in the books.

Another branch of the family, House Farwynd of the Lonely Light, holds lands on small isles beyond Great Wyk, including the namesake seat.
