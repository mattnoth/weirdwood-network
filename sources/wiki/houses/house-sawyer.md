Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Brienne of Tarth recalls that Harry Sawyer participated in a bet beneath the walls of Highgarden to see who would be the first to claim her maidenhead. After the bet was discovered and ended, Brienne sought out and defeated Harry during the melee at Bitterbridge. He survived the Battle of the Blackwater.
