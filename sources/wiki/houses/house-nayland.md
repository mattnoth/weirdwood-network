## Summary

House Nayland of Hag's Mire is a noble house from Hag's Mire in the riverlands. Neither their arms nor words have appeared in the books. They may be a house of landed knights sworn to House Frey of the Twins, since Hag's Mire appears to be within Frey territory.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Brienne of Tarth recalls that Ser Raymond Nayland was one of her suitors who participated in a bet to see who could bed her first. As revenge, she defeated Raymond during the melee at Bitterbridge hosted by Renly Baratheon.
