History[ ]

At the time of the regency of King Aegon III Targaryen, House Haen was led by Magister Torreo Haen. In 133 AC, Torreo sent his daughter Myrmadora to King's Landing to be presented as a potential wife to King Aegon III in the Maiden's Day Ball.

After the fall of the Rogare family in 135 AC, a bloody struggle ensued in the resulting power vacuum. Torreo was elevated to First Magister. However, at the feast he held to celebrate his new position, he was poisoned, along with his wife, daughters (including Myrmadora), siblings, and supporters.
