## Summary

House Wendwater is a noble house from the Crownlands. According to semi-canon sources their seat is along the Wendwater in the kingswood south of King's Landing. They blazon their arms with a green engrailed pall, inverted on white, dividing three trees, green, red and bare. Their words are "For All Seasons."
