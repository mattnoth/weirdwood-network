History[ ]

Lord Lorent Grandison served in the regency of Aegon III; Mushroom observed that he loved to sleep.

Ser Harlan Grandison was a knight in the Kingsguard of Aerys II Targaryen. After dying in his sleep, Harlan was replaced in the White Swords by Ser Jaime Lannister.

Along with Lord Cafferen, Lord Grandison was captured by Lord Robert Baratheon at the battles at Summerhall during Robert's Rebellion. Robert then managed to turn the two Targaryen loyalists into allies. Lord Grandison was wounded at the Battle of the Trident fighting for Robert, and died of his wounds a year later.

The elderly Lord Hugh Grandison was among the possible matches offered to Princess Arianne Martell. When he traveled to Dorne, however, he fell asleep between courses at the welcoming feast.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

The aged Lord Hugh Grandison is sworn to Storm's End.

Ser Narbert Grandison serves Stannis Baratheon, and he is one of the queen's men who protect Selyse Florent. Narbert yawns during the wedding of Sigorn and Alys Karstark.

House Grandison at the end of the third century[ ]

The known Grandisons during the timespan of the events described in A Song of Ice and Fire are:

Lord Hugh Grandison, Lord of Grandview. Called Greybeard.

With unknown relation to the Lord of the house, there is also:

Ser Narbert Grandison, a knight.

Ser {Harlan Grandison}, a member of Aerys II Targaryen's Kingsguard.

During the time of Robert's Rebellion

{Lord Grandison} who fought for House Targaryen at the battles at Summerhall. Became a loyal ally and friend to Robert Baratheon after the defeat, but later perished of wounds sustained at the Battle of the Trident.

Household[ ]

Ser Humfrey Wagstaff was once the castellan for House Grandison.

Historical Members[ ]

Lord Lorent Grandison, who became one of the regents of King Aegon III Targaryen in 136 AC.
