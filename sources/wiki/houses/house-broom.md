House Broom is a noble house from the westerlands. It is one of the principal houses sworn to House Lannister.

According to semi-canon sources, they blazon their arms with a silver helm, crested with a sprig of broom, on black and green checks.

History[ ]

The Brooms are an ancient line, dating back to the days of the First Men.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

House Broom's name and coat of arms may be a reference to the House of Plantagenet, since the epithet "Plantagenet" supposedly comes from the yellow sprig of broom that Geoffrey Plantagenet, Count of Anjou wore in his hat. George R. R. Martin has cited Thomas B. Costain's non-fiction books on the Plantagenet dynasty as an influence for Fire & Blood.

House of the Dragon[ ]

In the television series House of the Dragon, Ser Alfred Broome has a sigil on his armor that resembles House Broom's  coat of arms. It could mean that, at least in the show canon, the Brooms and the Broomes are the same house spelled in different ways.
