## Full Text

Two noble houses are called House Towers:

 House Towers (north), a house of First Men eliminated by House Stark during their struggle to establish their rule over the north

 House Towers of Harrenhal, a house that succeeded House Qoherys as Lords of Harrenhal and proved similarly short-lived
