History[ ]

Coming of the Andals[ ]

The Qorgyles were adventuring Andals who established their domains in the unexplored deserts of Dorne, settling in the deep dunes and sands. They fortified the only well for fifty leagues around, raising Sandstone as their castle.

By the time of Nymeria's War, the Qorgyles were bannermen of House Yronwood. The Qorgyles supported King Yorick V Yronwood in his unsuccessful war against Lord Mors Martell and Princess Nymeria. Following the war, the Qorgyles became vassals of House Martell.

Targaryen Era[ ]

When King Aegon I Targaryen attempted to conquer Dorne in the First Dornish War, House Qorgyle never submitted to House Targaryen and Qorgyle spearmen cut down Lord Harlan Tyrell's foragers whenever they ventured to far west. In 9 AC, Sandstone was among the Dornish castles burned by Balerion and Vhagar, the dragons of Aegon the Conqueror and Queen Visenya Targaryen.

During the Maiden's Day Ball in 133 AC, Moriah Qorgyle was presented to King Aegon III Targaryen as a potential bride, boldly requesting for the king to kiss her, though Aegon did not respond.

During the conquest of Dorne by King Daeron I Targaryen, Lord Lyonel Tyrell was tasked by the king with ensuring the submission of the Dornishmen. Lord Qorgyle proved loyal by quelling the revolts of one of the more notorious rebel lords. However, while staying at Sandstone, Lord Tyrell was murdered in a bed of scorpions. Maester Gareth suggests in his Red Sands that Lord Qorgyle arranged Lyonel's murder, though his motives were the subject of speculation. Some say Lord Qorgyle was slighted that his earlier show of loyalty was given little consideration by Lord Tyrell, while others claim his initial support was part of a treacherous plan he made with his castellan to earn the trust of Daeron and Lyonel.

Recent History[ ]

Lord Commander Qorgyle served as the 996th Lord Commander of the Night's Watch and was succeeded by Jeor Mormont.

Prince Oberyn Martell was fostered at Sandstone as a child.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Ser Arron Qorgyle is part of the escort of distinguished Dornish nobles who accompany Prince Oberyn Martell to King's Landing.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Princess Arianne Martell believes that House Qorgyle will aid her attempt to crown Princess Myrcella Baratheon as queen due to their relationship with Prince Oberyn.

House Qorgyle at the end of the third century[ ]

The known Qorgyles during the timespan of the events described in A Song of Ice and Fire are:

Lord Quentyn Qorgyle, Lord of Sandstone.

Ser Gulian Qorgyle, his eldest son and heir to Sandstone.

Ser Arron Qorgyle, his second son.

Historical Members[ ]

Moriah Qorgyle, a potential bride for King Aegon III Targaryen.

Lord Qorgyle, Lord of Sandstone during the conquest of Dorne.

Lord Commander Qorgyle, the 996th Lord Commander of the Night's Watch.
