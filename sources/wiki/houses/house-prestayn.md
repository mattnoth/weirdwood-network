Recent Events[ ]

A Feast for Crows[ ]

The kindly man offers to find Arya Stark passage on Luco Prestayn's ship, Lady Bright, which is leaving for Gulltown, Duskendale, and King's Landing in Westeros the next day, but she refuses.

Arya learns that Lotho Lornel sleeps in Moredo Prestayn's home when the captain is away.

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Arya learns that the thirteen-year-old daughter of a poor Prestayn serving maid has become the Merling Queen's new mermaid.

The Winds of Winter[ ]

An ancient Prestayn sits alone in a private box at the Gate during a performance of  The Bloody Hand.

House Prestayn at the end of the third century[ ]

The known Prestayns during the timespan of the events described in A Song of Ice and Fire are:

Luco Prestayn, Captain of the Lady Bright 

Moredo Prestayn, Merchant and Captain of the Vixen
