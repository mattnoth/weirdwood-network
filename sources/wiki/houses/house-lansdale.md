History[ ]

During the Dance of the Dragons, Ser Loreth Lansdale served in the Queensguard of Rhaenyra Targaryen. He was killed by Ser Alfred Broome's men when Rhaenyra returned to Dragonstone after the Storming of the Dragonpit.
