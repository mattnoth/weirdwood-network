Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

Ser Ottyn Wythers, a commander in the Night's Watch, is part of the great ranging which explores beyond the Wall.

A Storm of Swords[ ]

During the fight at the Fist, Ottyn is killed when a horse kicks him in the face.

A Feast for Crows[ ]

Ser Willam Wythers is the captain of the guard for Queen Margaery Tyrell in King's Landing.

House Wythers at the end of the third century[ ]

The known Wythers during the timespan of the events described in A Song of Ice and Fire are:

Ser {Ottyn Wythers}, a ranger of the Night's Watch.

Ser Willam Wythers, captain of the guard for Queen Margaery Tyrell.
