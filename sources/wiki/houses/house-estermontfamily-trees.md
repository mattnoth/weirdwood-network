Option 1[ ]

Text reference[ ]

Information in A Clash of Kings, A Storm of Swords and A Feast for Crows gives the following family tree:

{Lord Estermont}, former Lord of Greenstone. An ancient man. Old-fashioned and cautious. Died in 300 AC.

Lord Eldon Estermont, Lord of Greenstone, a man of 70, recently married to Sylva Santagar.

Ser Aemon Estermont, Eldon's son and heir.

Ser Alyn Estermont, Aemon's son.

Ser Lomas Estermont, his second son.

Ser Andrew Estermont, Lomas's son.

Lady {Cassana Estermont}, his daughter. Deceased in a shipwreck with her husband, Lord Steffon Baratheon.

King {Robert I}, her eldest son. Slain by a boar.

Stannis I, her second son. Claimant to the Iron Throne.

{Renly I}, her third son. Claimant to the Iron Throne, slain under mysterious circumstances.

With unspecified familiar relationship to the main branch of the house there is also:

An unnamed female cousin of King Robert I.

Her father and husband who both died during the siege of Storm's End.

It is unknown to which degree Robert and this cousin were related. If they were first cousins, then the former Lord Estermont had another son in addition to Eldon and Lomas, as both survived Robert's Rebellion.

Option 2[ ]

Text reference[ ]

Information from A Dance with Dragons and the semi-canon app A World of Ice and Fire shows that the current Lord of Estermont, Eldon, is Stannis' great-uncle. Integrating this with the previous information, it would give another possible version of the family's relationships:

{Lord Estermont}, former Lord of Greenstone. An ancient man. Old-fashioned and cautious. Died in 300 AC

Lady {Cassana Estermont}, his daughter. Deceased in a shipwreck with her husband, Lord Steffon Baratheon.

King {Robert I}, her eldest son. Slain by a boar.

Stannis I, her second son. Claimant to the Iron Throne.

{Renly I}, her third son. Claimant to the Iron Throne, slain under mysterious circumstances.

Two unnamed sons, apparently deceased after the time of King Robert I and Queen Cersei Lannister's marriage.

Lord Eldon Estermont, Lord of Greenstone, a man of 70, recently married to Sylva Santagar.

Ser Aemon Estermont, Eldon's son and heir.

Ser Alyn Estermont, Aemon's son.

Ser Lomas Estermont, his second son.

Ser Andrew Estermont, Lomas's son.

With unspecified familiar relationship to the main branch of the house there is also:

An unnamed female cousin of King Robert I.

Her father and husband who both died during the siege of Storm's End.

It is unknown to which degree Robert and this cousin were related. If they were first cousins, then she could be a daughter of one of the unnamed Cassana's brothers.
