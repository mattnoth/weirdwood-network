History[ ]

During the Faith Militant uprising, Visenya Targaryen used Vhagar to set aflame in a single night the seats of Houses Deddings, Blanetree, Lychester, Terrick, and Wayn.

During the Dance of the Dragons, Davos Deddings was stabbed in the eye while trying to protect his sister, Lady Darla, from rapers in the riots of King's landing. Lords Stanton Piper and Lyonel Deddings were burned by Vermithor during the Second Battle of Tumbleton.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

Lord and Lady Deddings are slain by the forces of House Lannister during the War of the Five Kings.

House Deddings at the end of the third century[ ]

The known Deddings during the timespan of the events described in A Song of Ice and Fire are:

{Lord Deddings}, head of the house. He was slain by the forces of House Lannister.

{Lady Deddings}, his wife. She was slain by the forces of House Lannister.

Historical Deddings[ ]

Lord Lyonel Deddings, burned alive by Vermithor at the Second Battle of Tumbleton.

Lady Darla Deddings

Davos Deddings, her brother.
