Recent Events[ ]

The Winds of Winter[ ]

While in the Weeping Town, Arianne Martell hears that Ser Addam Whitehead has left to join Jon Connington after the taking of Griffin's Roost.

House Whitehead at the end of the third century[ ]

The known Whiteheads during the timespan of the events described in A Song of Ice and Fire are:

Lord Whitehead, an aged lord.

Ser Addam Whitehead, his young heir.

Behind the Scenes[ ]

House Whitehead is apparently named  by George R. R. Martin after longtime A Song of Ice and Fire blogger Adam Whitehead. Apart from being a moderator on the Westeros.org forums, Adam also runs his own award-winning Scifi and Fantasy review blog, The Wertzone.
