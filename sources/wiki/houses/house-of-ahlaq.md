## Summary

Ghazdor zo Ahlaq, a Yunkish noble and slaver, is present at the siege of Astapor. After the death of Yurkhaz zo Yunzak in the fighting pits of Meereen, the Yunkai'i start to rotate the duty of supreme commander amongst them. Ghazdor is among the Yunkish nobles who take part in this rotation.
