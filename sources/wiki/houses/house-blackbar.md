History[ ]

During his reign, King Jaehaerys I Targaryen visited the Blackbars at Bandallon in 54 AC, and again in 89 AC. During his second visit at Bandallon, the king's estrangement with Queen Alysanne Targaryen had been noticed. Lord Blackbar's youngest daughter boldy tried to seduce the king by seating herself in his lap and attempting he feed him a grape, but Jaehaerys rebuffed her.

Ser Elwood Blackbar was among the knights that participated at the tourney at Ashford Meadow in 209 AC.

Recent Events[ ]

A Clash of Kings[ ]

House Blackbar declares for Renly Baratheon during the War of the Five Kings.
