## Full Text

House Cupps is a noble house from Westeros. The words and blazon of House Cupps are currently unknown.

Given their name and the marriage of Ser Jon to a daughter of Lord Leyton Hightower, the Cupps lands may be located near Oldtown and the Arbor.

House Cupps at the end of the third century[ ]

The only known member of House Cupps during the timespan of the events described in A Song of Ice and Fire is:

Ser Jon Cupps, married to Leyla Hightower.
