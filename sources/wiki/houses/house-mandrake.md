History[ ]

According to Jon Connington, the Mandrakes once loomed large in the histories of the Seven Kingdoms.

Recent Events[ ]

A Dance with Dragons[ ]

Ser Marq Mandrake, like the rest of the Golden Company, swears his allegiance to Prince Aegon Targaryen before they invade Westeros. Marq may not be a genuine Mandrake, as a man in the free companies can call himself whatever he chooses.
