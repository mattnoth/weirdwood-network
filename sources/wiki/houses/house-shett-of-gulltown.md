## Full Text

House Shett of Gulltown is a branch of House Shett from Gulltown in the Vale of Arryn. The Shetts of Gulltown are probably landed knights, since House Grafton holds the title of Lord of Gulltown.

According to semi-canon sources they blazon their arms with nine white seagulls on a field of brown.
