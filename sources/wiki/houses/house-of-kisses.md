## Full Text

The House of Kisses was a brothel in King's Landing, located atop of Visenya's Hill.

History[ ]

The House of Kisses existed during the Dance of the Dragons, and became the seat of King Gaemon Palehair, as it presumably was where his mother Essie and her reputed paramour Sylvenna Sand worked.
