## Summary

House Wensington is a noble house from the stormlands. According to semi-canon sources, their arms are two golden trumpets crossed on blue, a gold chief with three black stags. Their motto is "Sound The Charge".

History[ ]

As the upper part of the Wensington coat of arms shows the stag of House Baratheon and House Durrandon, the house may have been founded by a Baratheon or Durrandon.

Recent Events[ ]

A Storm of Swords[ ]

The Wensington banners are seen at the Wall during Stannis Baratheon's expedition to help the Night's Watch. Thus, most likely the Wensingtons declared first for Renly Baratheon, then for Stannis, and some or all of the house remained loyal after the Battle of the Blackwater.
